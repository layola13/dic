noob:/nuːb/| n.|新手，菜鸟|A noob is a person who is new to something, especially a video game or online community, and lacks experience or skill.  They are often considered unskilled or ignorant of the rules or conventions.|例句：He's a total noob at this game; he keeps dying. 他玩这个游戏太菜了，一直死。|例句：Don't be a noob and read the instructions before starting. 不要像个新手一样，开始前先阅读说明。|近义词：beginner, novice, newbie, amateur|反义词：expert, pro, veteran


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a noob at coding. = 他是个编程新手。
She is a noob in this online game. = 她在这个网络游戏中是个新手。

一般过去时:
He was a noob when he first started playing. = 他刚开始玩的时候是个新手。
She was a noob, but she learned quickly. = 她以前是个新手，但她学得很快。

现在完成时:
I have been a noob at many things in my life. = 我一生中在很多事情上都是个新手。
He has become less of a noob after many hours of practice. = 他经过许多小时的练习后，已经不再那么菜了。

过去完成时:
Before he started practicing, he had been a noob at guitar. = 在他开始练习之前，他是个吉他新手。
She had been a noob at skiing, but she improved rapidly. = 她以前滑雪很菜，但她进步很快。

将来时:
He will be a noob no more after this training. = 经过这次训练后，他将不再是新手。
She will still be a noob, even after the tutorial. = 即使看完教程，她仍然是个新手。
