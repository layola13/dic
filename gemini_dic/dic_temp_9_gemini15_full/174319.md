quick: /kwɪk/ | adj. | 快速的，敏捷的 |  Quick means happening or done with great speed. It can also describe someone who is able to understand or react quickly. |例句：He gave a quick answer. 他迅速地回答了。

He's a quick learner. 他是一个学习很快的人。|近义词：fast, rapid, speedy, swift |反义词：slow, sluggish, gradual
