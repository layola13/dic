overcontracting: /ˌoʊvərˈkɑːntræktɪŋ/ | n. |过度承包；超额承包 |The act of a company or individual taking on more work or projects than they can realistically handle. This can lead to stress, missed deadlines, and ultimately, poor quality of work.  |例句：The construction company's overcontracting led to significant delays and cost overruns.  这家建筑公司的过度承包导致了严重的延误和成本超支。

The company's overcontracting resulted in low employee morale and burnout. 这家公司的过度承包导致员工士气低落和倦怠。|近义词：overcommitment, overextension, overloading |反义词：undertaking less, delegating, prioritizing
