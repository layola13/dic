caperer:/kəˈpɪərər/| n. |轻快活泼的人；嬉戏者；（尤指）轻浮的人|A caperer is someone who behaves in a lively and playful, often frivolous, manner. They might be described as someone who skips about, engages in playful antics, or generally exhibits a lighthearted and sometimes irresponsible attitude.  The term often carries a slightly negative connotation, suggesting a lack of seriousness or a tendency towards superficiality.|The children were caperers, running and laughing in the park.孩子们在公园里蹦蹦跳跳，嬉戏玩耍。| 近义词：prankster, scamp, wag, buffoon| 反义词：prude, stick-in-the-mud
