enc: /ɛnk/ | vt. | 包裹；密封；隐藏 | To enclose something means to surround or cover it completely.  It can also mean to put something inside a container or covering. |  He enclosed the letter in an envelope. 他把信装进信封。 |  He enclosed the garden with a high fence. 他用高高的栅栏围住了花园。 |  envelop, surround, contain |  open, reveal, expose | vt.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He encloses the package carefully. = 他仔细地包裹包裹。
They enclose the documents in a folder. = 他们把文件装进文件夹里。


一般过去时:
She enclosed the check with the letter. = 她把支票和信一起寄出。
They enclosed the area with barbed wire. = 他们用铁丝网围住了该区域。


现在完成时:
He has enclosed the documents in the email. = 他已将文件添加到电子邮件中。
She has enclosed the gift in a beautiful box. = 她已将礼物装在一个精美的盒子里。


过去完成时:
He had enclosed the money in a secret compartment. = 他已将钱藏在一个秘密隔间里。
They had enclosed the building with a wall. = 他们已经用墙把建筑物围了起来。


将来时:
She will enclose the documents with her application. = 她将把文件与申请一起提交。
They will enclose the yard with a new fence. = 他们将用新的栅栏围住院子。


虚拟语气:
If he enclosed the package properly, it would arrive safely. = 如果他正确地包裹包裹，它就会安全到达。
I wish she had enclosed the instructions. = 我希望她能附上说明书。


被动语态:
The package was enclosed in a sturdy box. = 包裹被装在一个结实的盒子里。
The field was enclosed by a high stone wall. = 田地被高高的石墙围起来。


疑问句:
Did he enclose the map with the directions? = 他是否将地图与路线一起附上？
Is the letter enclosed in the package? = 信件是否包含在包裹中？


否定句:
He did not enclose the correct documents. = 他没有附上正确的文件。
They did not enclose the garden. = 他们没有围住花园。
