patterning:/ˈpætərnɪŋ/| n. |模式化；图案形成；规律性；模式设计|The process of creating or identifying patterns.  It can refer to the formation of patterns in nature, the design of patterns in art or design, or the identification of patterns in data or behavior.|例句：The patterning of the fabric was intricate and beautiful.  = 这块布料的图案复杂而美丽。

例句：The researcher studied the patterning of migratory bird flights. = 研究人员研究了候鸟迁徙的规律。


近义词：structuring, organizing, designing, modeling


反义词：randomness, chaos, disorder


词性:名词
