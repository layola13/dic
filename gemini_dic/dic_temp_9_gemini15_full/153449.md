penniform:/ˈpɛnɪfɔːrm/| adj.|羽状的，羽形的|Resembling a feather; having parallel barbs or branches on either side of a central axis, like a feather.|例句：The penniform muscles in the wings of birds enable powerful flight.  鸟类翅膀上的羽状肌能够实现强劲的飞行。|例句：The leaves of this plant have a distinct penniform venation.  这种植物的叶子具有明显的羽状脉序。|近义词：pinnate, feather-shaped|反义词：bipinnate
