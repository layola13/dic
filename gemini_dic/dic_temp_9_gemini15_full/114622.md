maleic: /məˈleɪɪk/ | adj. | 马来酸的；反丁烯二酸的 | Relating to or denoting maleic acid or its derivatives.  Maleic acid is an unsaturated dicarboxylic acid.  It is often used in the production of polymers and resins. | The maleic anhydride is used in the production of unsaturated polyester resins.  马来酸酐用于生产不饱和聚酯树脂。|  fumaric (isomer)|  malonic, succinic (related)|  adj.
