nondefensive:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈfɛnsɪv/| adj. |不设防的；不戒备的；不反驳的；平和的|Not defensive or hostile; not intended to defend or protect.  Characterized by a lack of defensiveness; receptive to criticism or feedback without becoming angry or resentful. |例句：He offered a nondefensive explanation of his actions. 他对自己的行为做出了不带防御性的解释。

例句：Her nondefensive attitude allowed for a productive conversation. 她不设防的态度使谈话富有成效。


近义词：unaggressive, receptive, open-minded, accommodating

反义词：defensive, hostile, aggressive, antagonistic, combative
