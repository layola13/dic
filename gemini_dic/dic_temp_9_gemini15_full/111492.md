loafer:/ˈloʊfər/| n.|懒汉；乐福鞋；|A loafer is a type of shoe, typically without laces, that is often associated with a relaxed or informal style. It can also refer to a person who is idle or avoids work. |例句：He wore loafers with his chinos. 他穿着乐福鞋和卡其裤。|例句：Don't be such a loafer; get to work! 不要那么懒，快工作！|近义词：idler, sluggard, moccasin (for the shoe)|反义词：hard worker, diligent
