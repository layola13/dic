corcule: /ˈkɔːr.kjuːl/ | n. |胚芽; 小胚|The corcule is the rudimentary embryo of a seed, the part that will develop into the plant. It is typically located within the seed and is composed of the embryonic axis (radicle and plumule) and one or more cotyledons.|例句：The corcule of the bean seed is clearly visible after soaking.|近义词：embryo, germ|反义词:|
