re-matriculate:/ˌriːmæˈtrɪkjuleɪt/ | v. |重新注册入学；复学|To re-enroll as a student at a college or university, especially after a period of absence.|例句：After a year off, she decided to re-matriculate at the university.  一年后，她决定重新回到大学注册入学。

例句：He re-matriculated at Oxford after a brief career in finance. 他在金融领域短暂工作后，重新回到牛津大学注册入学。


近义词：reenroll, re-register, reinstate


反义词：withdraw, leave, drop out


词性:动词

USAGE->
