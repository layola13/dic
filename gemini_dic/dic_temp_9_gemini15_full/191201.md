scariose:/ˈskæriəs/| adj.|膜质的；干燥的；枯萎的|Having a thin, dry, membranous texture; resembling parchment.  The term often describes the texture of certain plant parts, particularly bracts or leaves.|例句：The bracts of this plant are scarious and brown. 这植物的苞片是膜质的，呈棕色。

例句：The dry, scarious leaves crunched underfoot. 干燥的膜质叶子在脚下嘎吱作响。


近义词：membranous, papery, thin, dry

反义词：succulent, fleshy, juicy

词性：形容词
