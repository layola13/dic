clock-in: /ˈklɒk ɪn/ | v. |打卡，上班签到 |To record one's arrival at work, typically by using a time clock.  |例句：Employees are required to clock in before 9 a.m.  员工必须在上午9点之前打卡。

例句：I forgot to clock in this morning, so I had to ask my manager to correct my time sheet. 今天早上我忘记打卡了，所以我不得不请我的经理更正我的时间表。


近义词：sign in, punch in, register


反义词：clock out, sign out, punch out


词性：动词
