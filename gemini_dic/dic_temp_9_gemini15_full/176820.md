readorned:/ˌriːˈɔːrn/| v. |重新装饰；重新打扮|To decorate or adorn again or anew.  The verb implies a previous state of decoration or adornment that is being enhanced or changed. |例句：She readorned the Christmas tree with new ornaments. 她用新的装饰品重新装饰了圣诞树。

例句：The old house was readorned with antique furniture.  这栋老房子用古董家具重新装饰了。


近义词：redecorate, refurbish, redo, embellish


反义词：strip, dismantle, despoil


词性：动词
