obliterator: /əˈblɪtəreɪtər/ | n. | 毁灭者；磨灭者；消除器 | A person or thing that obliterates something.  An obliterator is something that completely destroys or erases something else, leaving no trace. This could be a physical object, a process, or even an abstract concept.  | The laser was a powerful obliterator of the enemy's defenses. 激光器是摧毁敌人防御的强大武器。 The storm acted as an obliterator of the landscape.  暴风雨摧毁了这片景观。 | destroyer, eraser, annihilator | preserver, creator, restorer | 名词
