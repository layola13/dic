practiser:/ˈpræktɪsə(r)/| n.|实践者，练习者，从业者|Someone who regularly practices a particular profession, skill, or activity.|She's a practising lawyer. 她是一位执业律师。|近义词：practitioner, exponent, professional|反义词：theoretician, amateur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor practises medicine in a busy clinic. = 这名医生在一个繁忙的诊所行医。
He practises yoga every morning. = 他每天早上练习瑜伽。


一般过去时:
She practised the piano for hours yesterday. = 她昨天练琴练了几个小时。
They practised their dance routine before the competition. = 他们在比赛前练习了他们的舞蹈套路。


现在完成时:
He has practised law for over twenty years. = 他从事法律工作已有二十多年了。
She has practised meditation daily for years and feels much calmer. = 她多年来每天都练习冥想，感觉平静多了。


过去完成时:
I had practised my speech many times before the presentation. = 我在演讲之前已经练习了很多次了。
They had practised the song for weeks before the concert. = 他们在音乐会之前已经练习这首歌好几个星期了。


将来时:
She will practise her French pronunciation before the meeting. = 她将在会议前练习她的法语发音。
They will practise the play again tomorrow. = 他们明天将再次排练这出戏。


虚拟语气:
If I practised more, I would be a better musician. = 如果我练习更多，我会成为更好的音乐家。
I wish I had practised harder for the exam. = 我真希望我为考试更努力地练习过。


被动语态:
The new technique is being practised by many doctors. = 许多医生正在实践这种新技术。
The song was practised repeatedly by the choir. = 合唱团反复练习了这首歌。


疑问句:
Does she practise regularly? = 她经常练习吗？
What musical instrument do you practise? = 你练习什么乐器？


否定句:
He doesn't practise enough. = 他练习不够。
They didn't practise before the performance. = 他们在演出前没有练习。
