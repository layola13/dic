peters:/ˈpiːtərz/ | v. |逐渐减少；衰退；逐渐消失 | To decrease gradually; to dwindle; to fade away.  | The excitement peters out after a while.  兴奋过了一会儿就逐渐消失了。|The wind peters out as it reaches the valley. 风到达山谷时逐渐减弱。| diminish, decline, fade | increase, grow, strengthen | 动词

The enthusiasm peters out slowly. 热情慢慢消退。

一般过去时:
