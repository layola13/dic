rebuses:/ˈriːbʌsɪz/| n.|字谜；谜图|Pictures or diagrams representing words or phrases, often using a combination of pictures and letters.  Rebuses are puzzles that require solving by interpreting a visual representation of words or phrases.|例句：The children enjoyed solving the rebuses in the newspaper.孩子们喜欢解报纸上的字谜。


例句：She created a series of rebuses to tell the story. 她创作了一系列字谜来讲述故事。


近义词：enigmas, puzzles, riddles|反义词：answers, solutions|词性:noun
