dreary:/ˈdrɪəri/| adj.|阴沉的；沉闷的；乏味的 | Describing something that is depressing or monotonous, lacking in interest or excitement.  It often refers to weather, atmosphere, or a general feeling. |例句：The dreary weather made me feel depressed.  阴沉的天气让我感到沮丧。

例句：The dreary lecture put everyone to sleep.  沉闷的演讲让每个人都睡着了。

近义词：gloomy, bleak, dismal, dull, monotonous

反义词：cheerful, bright, sunny, lively, exciting
