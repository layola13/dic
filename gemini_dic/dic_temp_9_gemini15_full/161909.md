pragmatist:/ˈpræɡməˌtɪst/| n. |实用主义者，实用论者 |A pragmatist is a person who is guided by practical considerations rather than by ideals.  They prioritize what works in the real world over theoretical or abstract principles. | Example:  She's a pragmatist; she'll choose the solution that gets the job done, even if it's not the most elegant. 她是个实用主义者；她会选择能完成工作的解决方案，即使它不是最优雅的。 |近义词：realist, practical person |反义词：idealist, utopian
