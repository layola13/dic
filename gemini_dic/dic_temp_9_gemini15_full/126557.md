nadir:/ˈneɪdər/| n. |最低点；最恶劣的时期 |The lowest point of something.  It often refers to a period of time or a situation that is at its worst. |例句：The company reached its nadir after the scandal broke. 公司在丑闻爆发后达到了最低谷。
The nadir of his career was when he lost his job. 他职业生涯的最低点是他失业的时候。|近义词：low point, bottom, rock bottom |反义词：zenith, peak, apex
