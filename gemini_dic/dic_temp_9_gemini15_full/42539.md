countermeasures: /ˈkaʊntərmɛʒərz/ | n. | 对策；反制措施 | Actions taken to counteract a threat or problem.  | The government implemented countermeasures to combat the spread of the virus. 政府采取了反制措施来对抗病毒的传播。| preventive measures, safeguards, defenses | none (antonyms depend on the specific threat being countered) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What countermeasures are they using? = 他们正在采取什么对策？
The company implements new countermeasures every year. = 公司每年都实施新的对策。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:

将来时:
What countermeasures will they deploy in the future? = 他们将来会部署什么对策？

虚拟语气:
