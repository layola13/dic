buzzing:/ˈbʌzɪŋ/ | adj. | 嗡嗡作响的；充满活力的；兴奋的 |  Characterized by a continuous low humming sound; full of energy and excitement. |例句：The city was buzzing with activity.  这座城市充满了活力。 |例句：The bees were buzzing around the flowers. 蜜蜂在花朵周围嗡嗡作响。|近义词：humming, vibrant, lively, exciting |反义词：quiet, still, dull, inactive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The city is buzzing with energy. = 城市充满活力。
The bees are buzzing around the hive. = 蜜蜂在蜂巢周围嗡嗡叫。

一般过去时:
The crowd was buzzing with excitement. = 人群兴奋地嗡嗡作响。
The engine was buzzing loudly. = 发动机嗡嗡作响。

现在完成时:
The cafe has been buzzing all afternoon. = 咖啡馆一下午都很热闹。
The news has been buzzing online. = 这个新闻在网上疯传。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
