ems: /ɛmz/ | n. |快递，邮政特快专递 | Emergency Medical Services (EMS) refers to a system that provides emergency medical care outside of a hospital.  It typically includes ambulances, paramedics, and other emergency medical personnel.  EMS systems vary in organization and availability across different regions. |  例句：The EMS arrived quickly and took the injured person to the hospital.  紧急医疗服务迅速到达并将伤者送往医院。


例句：We sent the package via EMS for faster delivery. 我们通过EMS发送包裹以便更快送达。


近义词：express mail, courier


反义词：regular mail, standard mail
