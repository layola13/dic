monodelphic:/ˌmɒnəˈdɛlfɪk/| adj.|单子宫的，单室的|Having a single uterine horn or chamber.  This term is primarily used in zoology, specifically in describing the reproductive system of certain mammals, particularly marsupials and some placental mammals.| Example: The wombat is a monodelphic marsupial. 袋熊是一种单子宫的有袋动物。|近义词：unilocular (if referring to a single chamber)|反义词： didelphic (having two uterine horns)
