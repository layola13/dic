grasslands:/ˈɡræslændz/| n.|草原，牧场|Extensive areas of land covered chiefly with grass, typically without trees or with only scattered trees.|例句：The African grasslands are home to many diverse animal species. 非洲草原是许多不同动物物种的家园。  The cattle grazed peacefully in the lush grasslands. 牛群在茂盛的草原上平静地吃草。|近义词：prairies, steppes, savannas|反义词：forests, jungles
