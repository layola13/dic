rejustifies:/ˌriːˈdʒʌstɪfaɪz/| v. |重新证明合理；再次为…辩护 |To justify again; to provide a new justification for something previously justified or to re-examine and defend a previous justification.  |例句：The new evidence rejustifies the initial decision to proceed with the project. = 新证据再次证明了继续进行该项目的最初决定是合理的。 The government attempted to rejustify its unpopular policies. = 政府试图为其不受欢迎的政策重新辩护。|近义词：vindicate, validate, defend, support |反义词：condemn, refute, invalidate, discredit |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rejustifies his actions every time someone questions them. = 他每次有人质疑他的行为时都会重新为其辩护。
The company rejustifies its pricing strategy annually. = 公司每年都会重新为其定价策略辩护。

一般过去时:
She rejustified her decision after receiving new information. = 她收到新信息后重新为她的决定辩护。
The court rejustified its ruling based on fresh evidence. = 法院根据新的证据重新为其裁决辩护。

现在完成时:
They have rejustified their investment in the failing project. = 他们已经为其在失败项目中的投资重新进行了辩护。
The scientist has rejustified his theory with further research. = 科学家通过进一步的研究为他的理论重新进行了辩护。

过去完成时:
He had rejustified his actions before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，他已经为他自己的行为重新进行了辩护。
The lawyer had rejustified the defendant's claims before the trial. = 在审判之前，律师已经为被告的主张重新进行了辩护。

将来时:
She will rejustify her position in the upcoming debate. = 她将在即将到来的辩论中重新为自己的立场辩护。
The company will rejustify its environmental record next quarter. = 公司将在下个季度重新为其环保记录辩护。

虚拟语气:
If he had more evidence, he would rejustify his claims. = 如果他有更多证据，他就会重新为他的说法辩护。
If they were to rejustify their actions, they would need strong evidence. = 如果他们要重新为他们的行为辩护，他们需要强有力的证据。

被动语态:
His actions were rejustified by the subsequent events. = 他的行为随后发生的事件证明是合理的。
The company's decision was rejustified by a recent market analysis. = 公司的决定得到了最近一次市场分析的支持。

疑问句:

否定句:
