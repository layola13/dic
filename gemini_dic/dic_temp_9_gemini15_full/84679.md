handed:/ˈhændɪd/| adj.|  handed | 递交的；遗传的；熟练的 |  Handed describes something that is given or passed to someone. It can also refer to the way something is inherited or the skill with which something is done.  | Example:  He handed me the package. 他把包裹递给了我。 The vase was a handed-down heirloom.  这个花瓶是祖传的传家宝。 | 近义词：passed, given, transferred, skillful, adept | 反义词：unskilled, clumsy, received


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He handed me the letter. = 他把信递给了我。
She hands out flyers every Saturday. = 她每个星期六都分发传单。

一般过去时:
They handed in their assignments on time. = 他们按时提交了作业。
I handed the book to the librarian. = 我把书递给了图书管理员。

现在完成时:
He has handed in his resignation. = 他已经递交了辞呈。
She has handed out all the prizes. = 她已经分发了所有奖品。

过去完成时:
They had handed over the evidence before the trial began. = 审判开始前，他们已经移交了证据。
She had handed the baby to its mother. = 她已经把婴儿交给了它的母亲。

将来时:
I will hand you the document later. = 我稍后会把文件交给你。
They will hand out the questionnaires tomorrow. = 他们明天将分发问卷。

虚拟语气:
If he had handed in his work earlier, he would have passed. = 如果他早点交作业，他就及格了。
I wish she had handed me the keys. = 我希望她把钥匙交给了我。

被动语态:
The package was handed to the wrong person. = 包裹交给了错误的人。
The awards will be handed out at the ceremony. = 奖项将在仪式上颁发。

疑问句:
Who handed you that note? = 谁把那张纸条递给你的？
Have you handed in your application yet? = 你提交申请了吗？

否定句:
He didn't hand in his homework. = 他没有交作业。
She hasn't handed out the invitations yet. = 她还没有分发请柬。
