remission:/rɪˈmɪʃən/| n. |缓解；宽恕；赦免；(疾病的)缓解期|The temporary or permanent disappearance of symptoms of a disease.|例句：The doctor is pleased with the remission of her symptoms. 医生对她的症状缓解感到欣慰。|例句：He asked for remission of his sentence. 他请求减刑。|近义词：abatement, alleviation, pardon, forgiveness |反义词：aggravation, exacerbation, worsening


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cancer is in remission. = 癌症处于缓解期。
Remission of the debt is granted. = 债务得到豁免。

一般过去时:
The symptoms went into remission after treatment. = 治疗后症状得到缓解。
The king granted a remission of taxes. = 国王减免了税款。

现在完成时:
She has been in remission for five years. = 她已经缓解五年了。
The prisoner has received a remission of his punishment. = 囚犯已经获得了减刑。

过去完成时:
The pain had gone into remission before the surgery. = 手术前疼痛已经缓解了。
The judge had already granted a remission of the fine. = 法官已经减免了罚款。

将来时:
The doctor hopes the disease will go into remission soon. = 医生希望这种疾病很快就会缓解。
We expect a remission of the charges next week. = 我们预计下周将撤销指控。


虚拟语气:
If only there were a remission of suffering. = 如果能减轻痛苦就好了。
I wish there were a remission of the debt. = 我希望债务能得到豁免。

被动语态:
The debt was remitted by the bank. = 债务被银行免除了。
His sentence was remitted by the governor. = 他的刑期被州长减免了。

疑问句:
Is the patient in remission? = 病人病情缓解了吗？
Will there be a remission of the fees? = 费用会得到减免吗？

否定句:
The remission of the disease was not complete. = 病情的缓解并不完全。
There was no remission of the sentence. = 刑期没有得到减免。
