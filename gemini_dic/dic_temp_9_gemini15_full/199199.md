silk:/sɪlk/| n. |丝绸，丝织品|A fine, soft, lustrous fiber produced by certain insect larvae, especially the silkworm, and used to make cloth.|The dress was made of pure silk. 这件裙子是用纯丝绸做的。| 近义词：satin, rayon | 反义词：cotton, wool
