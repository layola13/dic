mentored:/ˈmɛntɔːrd/| v.|指导，教导，培养 |To guide, instruct, and train a less experienced person.|例句：She mentored young artists in her studio.  她在她工作室指导年轻艺术家。|例句：He was mentored by a successful businessman. 他受到一位成功商人的指导。|近义词：guided, coached, trained, tutored|反义词：ignored, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mentors aspiring writers. = 他指导有抱负的作家。
She mentors young professionals. = 她指导年轻的专业人士。

一般过去时:
The CEO mentored many junior employees. = 首席执行官指导了许多初级员工。
My professor mentored me through my thesis. = 我的教授指导我完成了我的论文。

现在完成时:
She has mentored countless students throughout her career. = 她在职业生涯中指导了无数学生。
He has mentored several individuals to success. = 他已经指导了几个人走向成功。

过去完成时:
They had mentored him for years before he started his own company. = 在他创建自己的公司之前，他们已经指导了他好几年了。
She had mentored numerous interns prior to her promotion. = 在她晋升之前，她已经指导了许多实习生。

将来时:
He will mentor a new team next quarter. = 下个季度他将指导一个新的团队。
She will mentor several students in the upcoming program. = 她将在即将到来的项目中指导几位学生。

虚拟语气:
If he had mentored me earlier, I would have been more successful. = 如果他早点指导我，我会更成功。
I wish I had been mentored by someone more experienced. = 我希望我能得到一位更有经验的人的指导。

被动语态:
Many young people are mentored by experienced professionals. = 许多年轻人受到经验丰富的专业人士的指导。
He was mentored throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中都受到指导。

疑问句:
Are you being mentored by anyone? = 你正在接受任何人的指导吗？
Was he mentored by a famous artist? = 他是由一位著名的艺术家指导的吗？

否定句:
He didn't mentor anyone that year. = 那一年他没有指导任何人。
She hasn't mentored anyone in this field before. = 她以前从未在这个领域指导过任何人。
