disrupters:/dɪsˈrʌptərz/ | n. |颠覆者，破坏者 |Those who cause disruption or significant change, often in a positive sense by challenging the status quo and introducing innovation.  They can be individuals, companies, or technologies. |Example:  Tech disrupters are constantly changing the landscape of the business world. 科技颠覆者不断改变着商业世界的格局。
The rise of social media marked the emergence of new disrupters in the advertising industry. 社交媒体的兴起标志着广告业中新兴颠覆者的出现。|近义词：innovators, revolutionaries, game-changers |反义词：conservatives, traditionalists, status quo maintainers |名词
