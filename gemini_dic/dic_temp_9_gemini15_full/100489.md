intervene:/ˌɪntərˈviːn/| v.|干涉；介入；调停|To come between disputing people, groups, or things; to interfere.  |例句：The police had to intervene to stop the fight.  警察不得不介入制止这场打斗。
例句：The government intervened in the economy to prevent a recession. 政府介入经济以防止经济衰退。|近义词：interfere, mediate, arbitrate|反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He intervenes in every family dispute. = 他干涉每一场家庭纠纷。
They often intervene to protect the vulnerable. = 他们经常介入以保护弱势群体。

一般过去时:
The teacher intervened when the students started arguing. = 当学生们开始争吵时，老师进行了干预。
She intervened to prevent a serious accident. = 她介入以防止严重事故发生。

现在完成时:
The United Nations has intervened in several conflicts. = 联合国已经介入了几次冲突。
He has intervened on several occasions to help his friends. = 他曾多次介入帮助他的朋友。

过去完成时:
Before the police arrived, the neighbours had intervened to separate the fighters. = 在警察到达之前，邻居们已经介入将斗殴者分开。
The mediator had intervened several times before the agreement was reached. = 在达成协议之前，调解员已经介入过几次。

将来时:
The army will intervene if the situation deteriorates further. = 如果局势进一步恶化，军队将进行干预。
I will intervene if necessary. = 必要时我会介入。

虚拟语气:
If he had intervened earlier, the situation might have been different. = 如果他早些介入，情况可能会有所不同。
It would have been better if she hadn't intervened. = 如果她没有介入就好了。

被动语态:
The situation was intervened by the government. = 局势受到了政府的干预。
The dispute was intervened in by a neutral party. = 纠纷由一个中立方进行了调解。

疑问句:
Will the international community intervene? = 国际社会会介入吗？
Should we intervene in this matter? = 我们应该介入这件事吗？

否定句:
They did not intervene in the conflict. = 他们没有介入冲突。
The government chose not to intervene in the economic crisis. = 政府选择不干预经济危机。


PHRASE->
intervene in a dispute = 干涉一场争端
intervene on behalf of someone = 代表某人进行干预
intervene militarily =  军事干预
intervene surgically = 外科介入
intervene to prevent = 介入以防止
