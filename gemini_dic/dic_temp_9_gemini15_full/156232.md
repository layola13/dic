phthisis:/ˈθaɪsɪs/| n. |肺痨；消耗；衰竭|A wasting away of the body, especially from a chronic disease, such as tuberculosis.  The term is now largely obsolete, often replaced by more specific medical terms. |The phthisis slowly consumed him, leaving him weak and frail.|近义词：consumption, wasting, decline, atrophy|反义词：growth, thriving, flourishing|名词
