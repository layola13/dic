loris:/ˈlɔːrɪs/| n.|懒猴|A loris is a slow-moving nocturnal primate with large eyes, found in Southeast Asia.|例句：The slow loris is a fascinating creature with unique adaptations for its nocturnal lifestyle.  懒猴是一种迷人的生物，具有适应其夜行性生活方式的独特适应性。
