marketability:/ˌmɑːrkɪtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 市场销售能力；适销性 | The ability of a product or service to be sold successfully in a market.  It encompasses factors like price, demand, competition, and the overall appeal to consumers. |  The high marketability of the new phone resulted in record sales.  这款新手机的极高市场销售能力导致了创纪录的销量。 |  salability, vendibility | unsalability, unprofitability | 名词
