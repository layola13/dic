impalatable:/ɪmˈpælətəbl/| adj.|令人作呕的；难以下咽的；令人厌恶的|Unpleasant to the taste; distasteful.  Something impalatable is not enjoyable or agreeable, often because it is unpleasant to the senses, especially taste, or because it is offensive or morally objectionable.|例句：The food was completely impalatable. = 这食物完全难以下咽。The proposed solution was impalatable to both sides. = 双方都无法接受这个提议的解决方案。|近义词：unappetizing, distasteful, repulsive, offensive, disagreeable|反义词：delicious, palatable, appealing, agreeable
