puts:/pʊts/| vt.|放置；摆放；表达；打印|To put something in a particular place.  To express something. In programming, to display output.|例句：He puts his keys on the table. 他把钥匙放在桌子上。|例句：She puts her feelings into her art. 她把感情融入她的艺术作品中。|近义词：places, sets, expresses, prints|反义词：removes, takes away, suppresses

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He puts the book on the shelf. = 他把书放在书架上。
She puts her coat on. = 她穿上外套。

一般过去时:
I put the milk in the fridge. = 我把牛奶放进冰箱。
They put the chairs around the table. = 他们把椅子放在桌子周围。

现在完成时:
He has put his toys away. = 他已经把玩具收起来了。
She has put the cake in the oven. = 她已经把蛋糕放进烤箱了。

过去完成时:
I had put the letter in the mailbox before he called. = 他打电话之前我已经把信放进邮箱了。
They had put the presents under the tree before the children woke up. = 孩子们醒来之前，他们已经把礼物放在树下了。

将来时:
He will put the flowers in a vase. = 他将会把花插进花瓶里。
She will put the dishes in the dishwasher. = 她将会把盘子放进洗碗机。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would put more effort into it. = 如果我有更多时间，我会投入更多精力。
I wish I could put my worries aside. = 我希望我能把我的担忧放在一边。

被动语态:
The books were put on the shelves. = 书被放在书架上。
The table was put in the corner. = 桌子被放在角落里。

疑问句:
Where did you put the remote control? = 你把遥控器放在哪里了？
Where will they put the new sofa? = 他们会把新的沙发放在哪里？

否定句:
He didn't put the sugar in the coffee. = 他没有在咖啡里放糖。
She doesn't put much effort into her work. = 她对工作不太努力。


PHRASE->

puts up with: 忍受
puts forward: 提出
puts off: 推迟
