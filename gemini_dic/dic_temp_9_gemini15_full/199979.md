skateboarded:/ˈskeɪtˌbɔːrdɪd/ | v. |滑板运动；乘坐滑板 | To ride on a skateboard.  |例句：He skateboarded down the hill. 他从山上滑板下来了。
He has been skateboarding since he was a child. 他从小就一直在玩滑板。|近义词：skated, rode (a skateboard) |反义词：walked, cycled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I skateboard every weekend. = 我每个周末都玩滑板。
She skateboards to school. = 她滑板去上学。

一般过去时:
They skateboarded in the park yesterday. = 他们昨天在公园里玩滑板。
I skateboarded to the store. = 我滑板去商店。

现在完成时:
He has skateboarded all over the city. = 他已经在城里到处玩滑板了。
She has never skateboarded before. = 她以前从未玩过滑板。

过去完成时:
We had skateboarded for hours before it started to rain. = 下雨之前我们已经玩了几个小时的滑板了。
They had never skateboarded on such a steep hill before. = 他们以前从未在如此陡峭的山坡上玩过滑板。

将来时:
I will skateboard tomorrow if the weather is nice. = 如果天气好的话，我明天会去玩滑板。
They will have skateboarded for ten years next month. = 下个月他们玩滑板就十年了。

虚拟语气:
If I had a skateboard, I would skateboard every day. = 如果我有滑板，我每天都会玩滑板。
I wish I could skateboard like a professional. = 我希望我能像专业人士一样玩滑板。

被动语态:
The skateboard was skateboarded by many people today. = 今天很多人玩了这块滑板。(This sentence is grammatically correct but sounds unnatural.  Passive voice is rarely used with this verb.)
The ramp was designed for skateboarding. = 这个坡道是为滑板设计的。(Passive voice used to describe the ramp, not the action of skateboarding itself)

疑问句:
Did you skateboard today? = 你今天玩滑板了吗？
Have you ever skateboarded before? = 你以前玩过滑板吗？

否定句:
I didn't skateboard yesterday. = 我昨天没玩滑板。
She doesn't skateboard often. = 她不经常玩滑板。


PHRASE->
skateboard park = 滑板公园
skateboard ramp = 滑板坡道
skateboard deck = 滑板板面
skateboard wheels = 滑板轮子
longboard skateboarding = 长板滑板


词源:
The word "skateboarded" is derived from "skateboard" + "-ed" (past tense suffix).  "Skateboard" itself is a combination of "skate" (referencing ice skates and the gliding motion) and "board" (the wooden platform).  The "-ed" suffix indicates past tense or past participle.
