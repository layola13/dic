self-focused:/ˈsɛlf ˈfoʊkəst/| adj. |以自我为中心的；自我专注的 |Self-focused individuals tend to prioritize their own needs and perspectives above others'.  以自我为中心的个人往往优先考虑自己的需求和观点，而不是别人的。|近义词：egocentric, narcissistic, self-absorbed, self-centered |反义词：other-focused, altruistic, selfless


例句：

He's become increasingly self-focused since his promotion. 自从升职以来，他变得越来越以自我为中心。

Her self-focused behavior alienated her friends. 她以自我为中心的举动疏远了她的朋友们。
