pulleys:/ˈpʊliz/| n.|滑轮，滑轮组|A pulley is a wheel with a grooved rim around which a rope, cable, or belt passes. It is used to change the direction of a force or to gain a mechanical advantage.|例句：The system of pulleys made it easier to lift the heavy weight.  这个滑轮系统使得举起重物更容易了。|例句：We used pulleys to hoist the sail. 我们用滑轮升起帆。|近义词：block, sheave|反义词:None.
