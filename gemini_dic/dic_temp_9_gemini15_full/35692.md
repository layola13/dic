cobras:/ˈkoʊbrəz/ | n. |眼镜蛇复数 |Highly venomous snakes known for their ability to raise the front part of their bodies and spread a hood.  They are found in Africa, Asia, and parts of the Middle East. |例句：Cobras are known for their deadly venom. 眼镜蛇以其剧毒而闻名。
例句：Several cobras were spotted near the village. 村庄附近发现了数条眼镜蛇。|近义词：asp, viper |反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑用non-venomous snakes) harmless snakes|名词
