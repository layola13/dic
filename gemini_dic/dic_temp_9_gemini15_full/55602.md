disuse:/dɪsˈjuːs/| n.|不用，废弃，不用状态|The state of not being used; lack of use; cessation of use.  It often refers to something that was once used but is no longer. |例句：The old railway line fell into disuse. 这条旧铁路线废弃不用了。

例句：The disuse of the traditional method led to a decline in craftsmanship.  传统方法的废弃导致工艺水平下降。


近义词：abandonment, neglect, obsolescence, discontinuance

反义词：use, employment, utilization

词性: 名词
