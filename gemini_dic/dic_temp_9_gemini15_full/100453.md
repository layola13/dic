intertwined:/ˌɪntərˈtwɪnd/| adj.| 交织的；纠缠的；相互关联的 | Closely connected or related; interwoven.  |例句：Their fates were intertwined. 他们的命运交织在一起。

例句：The stories of the two families were intricately intertwined. 这两家人的故事错综复杂地交织在一起。


近义词：interwoven, interconnected, interwoven, entangled, linked, related

反义词：separate, distinct, unconnected, unrelated


词性：形容词
