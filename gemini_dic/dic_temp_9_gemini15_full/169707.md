proprietarily:/prəˈpraɪətɛrɪli/ | adv. | 专有地；所有权地；独家地 | In a proprietary manner; belonging exclusively to one person or group.  This often refers to something owned or controlled exclusively, such as a brand, technology, or process. |例句：The company uses a proprietarily developed software. 这家公司使用自行开发的软件。

The new drug is protected by a proprietarily held patent.  这种新药受一项专有专利保护。


近义词：exclusively, privately, uniquely, solely


反义词：publicly, commonly, openly, sharedly

词性：副词
