dixieland:/ˈdɪksiˌlænd/| n. |迪克西兰爵士乐；迪克西兰乐队|A style of jazz music originating in New Orleans in the late 19th and early 20th centuries, characterized by collective improvisation, a strong rhythmic drive, and a generally upbeat tempo.  It often features instruments like cornet, trombone, clarinet, and tuba.|The Dixieland band played a lively rendition of "When the Saints Go Marching In." 迪克西兰乐队演奏了一首欢快的《圣徒进行曲》。|jazz, swing|classical music, opera|名词
