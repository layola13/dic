co-owners:/ˈkoʊˌoʊnərz/| n. |共同所有人；共有者 |People who jointly own something.  Co-owners share ownership and responsibilities. |例句：The brothers are co-owners of a successful bakery. 兄弟俩是那家成功面包店的共同所有人。|例句：The apartment building has multiple co-owners who share the maintenance costs. 这栋公寓楼有多个共同所有人，他们共同承担维护费用。|近义词：joint owners, partners, shareholders |反义词：sole owner, individual owner
