nontheatrically:/ˌnɒnθiˈætrɪkli/| adj. |非戏剧性地，不夸张地|In a manner that is not theatrical or dramatic; in a straightforward, unpretentious way.  It implies a lack of artifice or showiness.|The speaker delivered the news nontheatrically, avoiding any unnecessary drama.|近义词：undramatically, plainly, simply, matter-of-factly|反义词：theatrically, dramatically, ostentatiously
