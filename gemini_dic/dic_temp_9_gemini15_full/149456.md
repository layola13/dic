paddleboarding:/ˈpædəlˌbɔːrdɪŋ/ | n. |桨板运动，立式划桨 |The activity of standing on a board and propelling oneself through water using a paddle.  It's a popular watersport enjoyed in calm waters like lakes and oceans. | 例句：She enjoys paddleboarding on weekends. = 她周末喜欢玩桨板运动。

例句：He's taking a paddleboarding lesson this afternoon. = 他今天下午正在上桨板课。


近义词：stand-up paddleboarding (SUP)


反义词：无


词性：名词


USAGE->

They paddleboard on the lake. = 他们在湖上玩桨板运动。
