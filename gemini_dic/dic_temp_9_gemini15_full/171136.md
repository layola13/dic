psychoanalyst:/ˌsaɪkoʊˈænəlɪst/| n. |精神分析学家|A psychoanalyst is a mental health professional trained in psychoanalysis, a specific type of psychotherapy that focuses on unconscious processes and past experiences to understand and treat mental disorders.  They use techniques like free association and dream analysis to help patients gain insight into their behavior and emotions.|例句：The psychoanalyst helped her understand the root of her anxiety.  这位精神分析学家帮助她理解了她焦虑的根源。|近义词：psychiatrist, therapist, psychotherapist|反义词：N/A
