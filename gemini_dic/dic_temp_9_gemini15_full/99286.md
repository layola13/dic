interconvertible: /ˌɪntər kənˈvɜːrtəbəl/ | adj. | 可相互转换的，可互相转化的 | Able to be changed from one form to another and back again.  This term often refers to chemical substances, energy forms, or even concepts that can readily switch between states. |

例句：

These two chemical compounds are interconvertible under certain conditions.  这些两种化合物在特定条件下可以相互转换。

The potential and kinetic energy of a moving object are interconvertible.  运动物体的势能和动能是可相互转换的。


近义词：mutually convertible, interchangeable, reversible

反义词：irreversible, immutable, fixed


词性: adjective
