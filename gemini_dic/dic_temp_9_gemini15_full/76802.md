It can also refer to nonsense or boasting, especially in the context of card games like bridge.|例句：He cooked a delicious gammon for dinner. 他做了一顿美味的咸肉晚餐。|  例句：Don't pay attention to his gammon, he's just trying to bluff. 不要理他的胡说八道，他只是想虚张声势。|近义词：ham, hog, bluster, boast |反义词：truth, honesty, sincerity
