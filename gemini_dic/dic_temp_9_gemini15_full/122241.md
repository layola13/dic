miszealous:/ˌmɪsˈzɛləs/| adj.|过分热心的；狂热的；盲目热情的|excessively zealous;  characterized by excessive zeal, often to the point of being misguided or harmful.  It implies a misguided enthusiasm that leads to negative consequences.|例句：His miszealous efforts to impress his boss backfired, leading to a reprimand.  他的过分热心的努力想给老板留下好印象适得其反，导致了他受到训斥。

例句：The miszealous cleaning of the antique vase damaged its delicate surface.  过分热心地清洁古董花瓶损坏其精美的表面。


近义词：overzealous, fanatical, obsessive, overenthusiastic|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unconcerned, apathetic|词性：形容词

USAGE->
