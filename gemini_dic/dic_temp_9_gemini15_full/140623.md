ologist:/əˈlɒdʒɪst/| /ɑːˈlɑːdʒɪst/| n.|学家，专家|A person who studies or is an expert in a particular branch of knowledge.|例句：He is a renowned geologist. 他是一位著名的地质学家。|例句：She's a leading biologist specializing in marine life. 她是一位专门研究海洋生物的顶尖生物学家。|近义词：expert, specialist, scholar|反义词：layperson, amateur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The biologist studies the behavior of birds. = 生物学家研究鸟类的行为。
The geologist analyzes rock samples. = 地质学家分析岩石样本。


一般过去时:
The archaeologist discovered an ancient artifact. = 考古学家发现了一件古代文物。
The paleontologist examined the dinosaur bones. = 古生物学家检查了恐龙骨骼。


现在完成时:
The climatologist has published numerous papers on climate change. = 气候学家已经发表了许多关于气候变化的论文。
The anthropologist has traveled extensively to study different cultures. = 人类学家为了研究不同的文化而广泛旅行。


过去完成时:
The linguist had already completed his research before the conference. = 语言学家在会议之前已经完成了他的研究。
The oceanographer had never seen such a diverse ecosystem before. = 海洋学家以前从未见过如此多样化的生态系统。


将来时:
The astronomer will observe the lunar eclipse tonight. = 天文学家今晚将观测月食。
The sociologist will present her findings at the seminar. = 社会学家将在研讨会上展示她的研究结果。


虚拟语气:
If I were a zoologist, I would study endangered species. = 如果我是一个动物学家，我会研究濒危物种。
I wish I were a volcanologist to understand these eruptions better. = 我希望我是一位火山学家，以便更好地理解这些火山喷发。


被动语态:
The new species was discovered by a botanist. = 这种新物种是由一位植物学家发现的。
The research was conducted by a team of experienced psychologists. = 这项研究是由一支经验丰富的临床心理学团队进行的。


疑问句:
Is he a cardiologist? = 他是心脏病专家吗？
Is she a dermatologist? = 她是一位皮肤科医生吗？


否定句:
He is not a meteorologist. = 他不是气象学家。
She is not an entomologist. = 她不是昆虫学家。
