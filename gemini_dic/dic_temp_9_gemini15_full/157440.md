/pɪˈrænə/ | n. |食人鱼|A piranha is a type of fish known for its sharp teeth and aggressive feeding habits.  They are found in freshwater habitats of South America.  They are often portrayed as extremely dangerous, but in reality, most attacks on humans are rare and often provoked. |The piranhas attacked the carcass immediately. 食人鱼立刻袭击了尸体。| carnivorous fish, killer fish | peaceful fish, herbivore
