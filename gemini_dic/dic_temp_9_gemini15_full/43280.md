craftsmanship:/ˈkræftsmənʃɪp/| n. |工艺，技艺；精湛的技艺|The skill in making something, especially something by hand; the quality of the skill that is shown in making something.|The craftsmanship of the antique chair was evident in its intricate details. 这把古董椅的精湛工艺在其复杂的细节中显而易见。| artistry, skill, expertise | clumsiness, ineptitude | 名词
