misoccupied:/ˌmɪsˈɑːkjupaɪd/| adj.|被误占用的；被错误使用；时间被浪费的|Incorrectly or wrongly occupied or used;  often implies a wasteful or inefficient use of time, space, or resources.|The misoccupied office space sat empty for months.|近义词：unused, wasted, idle, misapplied|反义词：occupied, utilized, employed, productive

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The office is misoccupied by the sales team. = 销售团队错误地占据了办公室。
My time is misoccupied with unnecessary meetings. = 我的时间被不必要的会议浪费掉了。


一般过去时:
The land was misoccupied by squatters. = 这块土地被棚户区居民非法占据了。
He misoccupied his talent on trivial pursuits. = 他把自己的才能浪费在琐碎的事情上了。


现在完成时:
The building has been misoccupied for years. = 这座建筑物已经被错误地占用多年了。
She has misoccupied her potential by avoiding challenges. = 她因为逃避挑战而浪费了自己的潜能。


过去完成时:

They had misoccupied their time with unproductive activities. = 他们之前把时间浪费在了没有成效的活动上。

He will misoccupy his evenings with meaningless TV shows. = 他将把晚上时间浪费在毫无意义的电视节目上。


虚拟语气:
