mash:/mæʃ/ | vt. n. |捣碎；糊状物；混合物 |to crush something into a soft mass |The potatoes were mashed until smooth. 土豆被捣成泥状。|近义词：crush, puree, pulverize |反义词：separate, divide |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mashes the potatoes every Sunday. = 他每周日都捣土豆泥。
She mashes the bananas for the baby. = 她把香蕉捣碎给婴儿吃。

一般过去时:
I mashed the garlic before adding it to the sauce. = 我在把蒜加到酱汁里之前先把它捣碎了。
He mashed the avocados for guacamole. = 他把鳄梨捣碎做鳄梨酱。

现在完成时:
She has mashed the sweet potatoes for the pie. = 她已经把红薯捣碎做馅饼了。
They have mashed the peas for the toddler. = 他们已经把豌豆捣碎给幼儿吃了。

过去完成时:
He had mashed the potatoes before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经把土豆捣碎了。
She had mashed the bananas earlier that day. = 那天早些时候，她已经把香蕉捣碎了。

将来时:
I will mash the potatoes for dinner tonight. = 今晚我将为晚餐捣土豆泥。
She will mash the avocados for the toast. = 她将把鳄梨捣碎涂在吐司上。

虚拟语气:
If I had a potato masher, I would mash the potatoes more easily. = 如果我有一个土豆捣碎器，我会更容易地把土豆捣碎。
I wish I had mashed the garlic before cooking the onions. = 我希望我在炒洋葱之前就把大蒜捣碎了。

被动语态:
The potatoes were mashed by the chef. = 土豆是由厨师捣碎的。

疑问句:
Did you mash the potatoes? = 你捣土豆泥了吗？
Have they mashed the sweet potatoes yet? = 他们已经把红薯捣碎了吗？

否定句:
