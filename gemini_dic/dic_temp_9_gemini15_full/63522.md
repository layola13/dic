enunciating: /ɪˈnʌnsieɪtɪŋ/ | v. |清晰发音，清楚表达|The act of pronouncing words clearly and distinctly.  It emphasizes the articulation of sounds and the precise formation of words. |例句：She enunciated each word carefully so that everyone could understand. 她仔细地清晰地发出每一个字，以便每个人都能理解。


例句：The speaker enunciated his points with great clarity. 演讲者清晰地阐述了他的观点。


近义词：articulating, pronouncing, expressing, uttering


反义词：mumbling, slurring, muttering


词性:动词
