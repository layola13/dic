cavilled:/ˈkævəld/| v.|吹毛求疵，挑剔，故意找茬 | To raise trivial objections; find fault with.  |例句：He cavilled at the small details of the contract. 他对合同的细枝末节吹毛求疵。

例句：The critics cavilled at every minor imperfection in the painting. 批评家们对这幅画的每一个细微缺陷都吹毛求疵。


近义词：criticize, carp, quibble, nitpick

反义词：praise, commend, approve


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cavils at everything. = 他对什么都吹毛求疵。
She constantly cavils at my work. = 她不断地挑剔我的工作。

一般过去时:
He cavilled at the price. = 他对价格吹毛求疵。
They cavilled at the terms of the agreement. = 他们对协议条款吹毛求疵。

现在完成时:
He has cavilled at my suggestions for weeks. = 他几周来一直在对我的建议吹毛求疵。
She has cavilled at every aspect of the project. = 她对项目的各个方面都吹毛求疵。

过去完成时:
He had cavilled at the plan before the meeting. = 他在会议前就对计划吹毛求疵。
They had cavilled at the design for months. = 他们几个月来一直在对设计吹毛求疵。

将来时:
He will cavil at the details again. = 他会再次对细节吹毛求疵。
They will cavil at any attempt to compromise. = 他们会对任何妥协的尝试都吹毛求疵。

虚拟语气:
If he cavilled at everything, nothing would ever get done. = 如果他事事都吹毛求疵，什么事也做不成。
I wish he wouldn't cavil at my work so much. = 我希望他不要总是那么挑剔我的工作。

被动语态:
His ideas were cavilled at by the committee. = 他的想法受到了委员会的批评。
The proposal was cavilled at for its lack of detail. = 该提案因缺乏细节而受到批评。

疑问句:
Why does he always cavil at my ideas? = 他为什么总是挑剔我的想法？
Does she often cavil at the work of others? = 她经常挑剔别人的工作吗？

否定句:
He did not cavil at the terms of the contract. = 他没有对合同条款吹毛求疵。
She did not cavil at the suggestions made. = 她没有对提出的建议吹毛求疵。


PHRASE->
cavil at something: 对某事吹毛求疵
cavil about something:  对某事挑剔
cavil over something: 对某事争论
cavil with someone: 与某人争论
cavil and quibble: 吹毛求疵和争论


词源:
源自拉丁语 *cavillare,  意思是“嘲笑，戏弄”。
