housing:/ˈhaʊzɪŋ/| n.|住房，房屋，住宅|The provision of houses or accommodation.  It can refer to individual dwellings or the overall system of providing homes. |例句：The government is investing heavily in social housing. 政府正在大力投资社会保障性住房。

例句：The cost of housing in this city is extremely high.  这个城市的住房成本非常高。

近义词：accommodation, lodging, shelter

反义词：homelessness


USAGE->

The government provides affordable housing. = 政府提供经济适用房。
This area lacks sufficient housing. = 这个地区缺乏足够的住房。
