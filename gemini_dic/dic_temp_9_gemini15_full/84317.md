half-womanly: /ˈhæf ˈwʊmənli/ | adj. | 半女性的；部分女性化的 |  Describes something that possesses some, but not all, of the qualities typically associated with women.  It suggests an incompleteness or a blend of masculine and feminine characteristics. The term often carries a connotation of being underdeveloped or insufficiently feminine.  |例句：Her demeanor, though assertive, had a certain half-womanly quality.  她的举止虽然果断，却带有一些半女性化的特质。

The character in the play exhibited a half-womanly strength, a mixture of fragility and resilience. 剧中人物展现出一种半女性化的力量，脆弱与韧性并存。| 近义词：partly feminine, androgynous (in some contexts), effeminate (though with a negative connotation)| 反义词：fully womanly, masculine, completely unfeminine
