sausage:/ˈsɔːsɪdʒ/ | n. |香肠|A cylindrical food made from ground meat (usually pork, beef, or a mixture), seasoned and often encased in a skin (natural or artificial).  Sausages can be cooked in various ways, such as grilling, frying, or boiling. They are a popular food item worldwide, with many regional variations.|例句：I grilled some sausages for dinner last night.  昨晚我烤了一些香肠当晚餐。

例句：The breakfast included eggs, bacon, and sausages. 早餐包括鸡蛋，培根和香肠。
