saunterer:/ˈsɔːntərər/| n. |闲逛者，漫步者 |A saunterer is someone who walks at a leisurely pace, without any particular destination or purpose. They enjoy the act of walking itself.  |Example: The elderly gentleman was a familiar sight, a gentle saunterer along the beach each afternoon.  这位年长的绅士是一个熟悉的身影，每天下午都会在海滩上悠闲地散步。|近义词：stroller, rambler, wanderer |反义词：hurrier, rusher


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He saunters down the street every morning. = 他每天早上都在街上闲逛。
The children saunter through the park. = 孩子们在公园里漫步。


一般过去时:
She sauntered along the beach, enjoying the sunset. = 她沿着海滩漫步，欣赏着日落。
They sauntered home after the party. = 他们派对后慢悠悠地回家了。


现在完成时:
He has sauntered through life without a care. = 他一生悠闲自在地度过。
We have sauntered along this path many times. = 我们已经在这条路上漫步过很多次了。


过去完成时:
She had sauntered into the room before anyone noticed her. = 她在任何人注意到她之前就漫步进了房间。
They had sauntered through the forest for hours. = 他们在森林里漫步了几个小时。


将来时:
I will saunter through the market tomorrow. = 我明天将在市场上闲逛。
They will saunter along the riverbank after lunch. = 午餐后他们将在河边漫步。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would saunter through the museum. = 如果我有更多时间，我会在博物馆里闲逛。
I wish I could saunter through the countryside. = 我希望我能漫步在乡间。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The path was often sauntered upon by tourists. = 游客经常在这条路上漫步。 (This is awkward and less common)
The park is often sauntered through by locals. = 当地人经常在公园里漫步。(This is awkward and less common)


疑问句:
Does he often saunter through the town? = 他经常在镇上闲逛吗？
Will you saunter with me this evening? = 你今晚会和我一起漫步吗？


否定句:
He did not saunter; he hurried. = 他没有闲逛；他匆匆忙忙的。
They will not saunter; they have a tight schedule. = 他们不会闲逛；他们时间紧迫。


PHRASE->
saunter along = 沿着...漫步
saunter through = 漫步穿过
saunter into = 漫步进入
saunter off = 闲逛离开
saunter down = 沿着...往下走
