joins:/dʒɔɪnz/ | /dʒɔɪnz/ | v. | 加入，连接，结合 | To come together to form a single unit or group; to connect or link together. | 例句：Many rivers join to form the Mississippi River.  许多河流汇合形成密西西比河。


The club members join together for a fundraising event.  俱乐部成员们一起参加筹款活动。


近义词：unite, connect, combine, merge | 反义词：separate, disconnect, divide, sever


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He joins the gym every Monday. 他每周一都去健身房。
They join hands and sing a song. 他们手拉手唱歌。

一般过去时:
She joined the company last year. 她去年加入了这家公司。
We joined the parade and marched down Main Street. 我们参加了游行，沿着主街行进。

现在完成时:
I have joined the local singing group. 我已经加入了当地的歌唱小组。
They have joined forces to fight against the common enemy. 他们联手对抗共同的敌人。

过去完成时:
He had joined the army before the war broke out.  战争爆发前他已经参军了。
She had already joined the club before we met her. 我们认识她之前，她已经加入了俱乐部。

将来时:
I will join you for dinner tomorrow. 我明天和你一起吃晚饭。
They will join the meeting via video conference. 他们将通过视频会议参加会议。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would join you. 如果我有更多时间，我会加入你们。
I wish I could join the party, but I have other plans. 我真希望我能参加派对，但我已经有其他计划了。

被动语态:
The two companies were joined together by a merger. 两家公司通过合并结合在一起。
The two pieces of wood were joined with glue. 两块木头用胶水粘在一起。

疑问句:
Will you join us for lunch? 你要和我们一起吃午饭吗？
Does he join the debate club? 他参加辩论社吗？

否定句:
I did not join the protest. 我没有参加抗议活动。
He won't join the team. 他不会加入球队。

词源:
The word "joins" originates from the Old English verb "joinan," meaning "to join together, unite."  This, in turn, has roots in the Proto-Germanic verb "*jungōną," ultimately sharing ancestry with words in other Germanic languages related to joining or uniting.
