janthina:/dʒænˈθaɪnə/| n. |紫螺,紫海螺|A genus of small, pelagic (open-ocean) sea snails, marine gastropod mollusks in the family Janthinidae.  They are characterized by their violet-blue color and the ability to float on the surface of the ocean using a raft of bubbles.|Example:  The *Janthina* snail uses a raft of bubbles to stay afloat.  紫螺利用气泡筏漂浮在水面上。|近义词：sea snail, pelagic gastropod |反义词：N/A
