dripped:/drɪpt/| v.|滴下；渗出|To fall or let fall in drops.  It implies a slow, steady flow of liquid.|例句：Water dripped from the leaky faucet. 水从漏水的龙头滴落。

The rain dripped from the leaves. 雨水从树叶上滴落。


近义词：trickled, drizzled, oozed|反义词：poured, gushed|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The faucet drips constantly. = 水龙头不断地滴水。
Honey drips from the spoon. = 蜂蜜从勺子上滴下来。

一般过去时:
The melting snow dripped from the roof. = 融化的雪从屋顶滴落。
He dripped sweat after the workout. = 锻炼后他汗流浃背。

现在完成时:
The paint has dripped onto the carpet. = 油漆滴到了地毯上。
Water has dripped through the ceiling. = 水已经渗透了天花板。

过去完成时:
The roof had dripped all night. = 屋顶整夜都在滴水。
The candle wax had dripped onto the tablecloth. = 蜡烛油已经滴到了桌布上。

将来时:
The melting ice will drip onto the floor. = 融化的冰会滴到地板上。
I think the tap will drip again tonight. = 我想今晚水龙头还会滴水。

虚拟语气:
If the roof dripped, we would need repairs. = 如果屋顶漏水，我们就需要修理。
I wish the faucet wouldn't drip. = 我希望水龙头不要滴水。

被动语态:
The paint was dripped onto the canvas. = 油漆被滴到画布上。
The floor was dripped with water. = 地板被水滴湿了。

疑问句:
Is the roof dripping? = 屋顶在滴水吗？
Does the tap drip often? = 水龙头经常滴水吗？

否定句:
The water didn't drip from the pipe. = 水没有从管道里滴下来。
Nothing dripped from the broken bottle. = 破瓶子里什么也没有滴出来。

词源:
The word "drip" comes from the Old English word "dryppan," meaning "to drop."  This word is related to other Germanic words with similar meanings.
