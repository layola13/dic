lenten: /ˈlɛntən/ | adj. | 四旬斋期的；与四旬斋有关的 | Relating to Lent, the period of 40 days before Easter observed by many Christians as a time of fasting, repentance, and spiritual preparation.  |  The church holds special Lenten services.  教堂举行特别的四旬斋礼拜仪式。


The Lenten season is a time for reflection. 四旬斋季节是反思的时期。


近义词: penitential (忏悔的), fast-day (禁食日的)


反义词:  None directly opposite, but could be considered the opposite of celebratory or festive (庆祝的，节日的) during this period.


词性: 形容词


USAGE->

They participated in Lenten activities last year. = 他们去年参加了四旬斋的活动。

过去完成时:
They had completed their Lenten devotions before Easter. = 他们在复活节前完成了他们的四旬斋祈祷。

词源:  From Middle English *lentyn, from Old English lencten, from lenct,  meaning "spring".  The connection is that Lent falls in the spring and was originally associated with the lengthening of the days.  The association with fasting and repentance developed later.
