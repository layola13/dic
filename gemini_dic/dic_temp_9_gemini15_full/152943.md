pedicab:/ˈpɛdɪkæb/| n.|人力车，三轮脚踏车|A pedicab is a type of three-wheeled cycle taxi that is pedaled by a person.  They are often found in tourist areas.|例句：Many tourists enjoy riding pedicabs around the city. 许多游客喜欢乘坐人力车游览城市。  We hired a pedicab to get to the beach faster. 我们租了一辆人力车以便更快地到达海滩。|近义词：rickshaw, cycle rickshaw|反义词：car, taxi (in the sense of motorized transport)|名词
