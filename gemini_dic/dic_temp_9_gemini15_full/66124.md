eccentricity:/ˌɛkˌsɛnˈtrɪsəti/ | n. |古怪；偏心率；离心率 |The quality of being eccentric; strangeness; oddity.  It can also refer to the degree to which a curve or orbit deviates from being circular. |例句：His eccentricity amused his friends.  他的古怪行为逗乐了他的朋友们。

例句：The eccentricity of the Earth's orbit causes the seasons. 地球轨道的偏心率导致了四季的变化。


近义词：oddity, peculiarity, quirk, anomaly | 反义词：normality, regularity, conventionality


词源:  From Middle French *excentricité, from Latin *excentricus, from Greek ἔκκεντρος (ékkentros) "out of the center," from ἐκ- (ek-) "out of" + κέντρον (kentron) "center".
