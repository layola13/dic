endogenesis:/ˌɛndoʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs/| n. |内源性；内生作用|The origination or production of something within an organism, system, or process.  It refers to factors or processes that originate internally rather than externally.|Example:  The study focused on the endogenesis of the disease, exploring the internal factors contributing to its development.  这项研究侧重于该疾病的内源性，探索导致其发展的内部因素。|近义词：intrinsic origin, internal generation|反义词：exogenesis, exogenous
