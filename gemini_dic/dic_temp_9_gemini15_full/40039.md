consummated:/ˈkɒnsəmeɪtɪd/ /ˈkɑːnsəmeɪtɪd/ | vt. adj. | 完成的；使完美；完成（婚姻） | To bring to completion or perfection; to fulfill; to complete a marriage through sexual intercourse. |例句：They consummated their love with marriage. 他们通过结婚完成了他们的爱情。

The deal was finally consummated after months of negotiations. 这笔交易在几个月的谈判后终于完成了。|近义词：complete, fulfill, perfect |反义词：incomplete, unfulfilled, imperfect
