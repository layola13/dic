imburse:/ɪmˈbɜːrs/ | vt. |偿还；报销；支付|To repay or reimburse someone for an expense or loss.|The company will reimburse you for your travel expenses. 公司将报销你的差旅费。|refund, repay, compensate|  | vt.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company imburses employees for business-related travel. = 公司报销员工的公务出差费用。
They regularly reimburse their clients for any damages. = 他们定期向客户赔偿任何损失。


一般过去时:
The insurance company reimbursed me for the damage to my car. = 保险公司赔偿了我汽车的损失。
My employer reimbursed my travel expenses last week. = 我的雇主上周报销了我的差旅费。


现在完成时:
She has been reimbursed for the cost of the repairs. = 她已经收到了修理费用的赔偿。
The government has reimbursed the victims of the flood. = 政府已经赔偿了洪灾的受害者。


过去完成时:
They had already been reimbursed for the lost goods before they even filed a claim. = 他们在提出索赔之前就已经收到了丢失货物的赔偿。
The company had reimbursed the employee before the end of the month. = 公司在月底之前已经报销了员工的费用。


将来时:
The company will reimburse you once you submit the receipts. = 你提交收据后，公司会报销你的费用。
They will reimburse the participants for their travel and accommodation costs. = 他们将报销参与者的差旅和住宿费用。


虚拟语气:
If they had submitted the proper paperwork, they would have been reimbursed. = 如果他们提交了正确的文件，他们本来会得到报销。
I wish they would reimburse me promptly for the damage. = 我希望他们能尽快赔偿我的损失。


被动语态:
The expenses were reimbursed by the company. = 费用由公司报销。
The damage will be reimbursed by the insurance company. = 损失将由保险公司赔偿。


疑问句:

否定句:
The company did not reimburse him for his expenses. = 公司没有报销他的费用。
They refused to reimburse me for the faulty product. = 他们拒绝赔偿我因产品故障造成的损失。
