frogmarching:/ˈfrɒɡmɑːrtʃɪŋ/| v.|青蛙式押送；强行押送|The act of carrying or dragging a person, usually against their will, by holding their arms and legs.  This method resembles a frog's posture. |例句：The protesters were frogmarched out of the building by the police.  抗议者被警察强行押送出了大楼。


例句：They frogmarched the suspect to the police car. 他们强行将嫌疑犯押送上警车。


近义词：dragging, hauling, escorting forcefully, forcibly removing


反义词： escorting politely, assisting, guiding


词性：动词
