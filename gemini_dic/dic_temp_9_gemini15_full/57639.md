drave:/dreɪv/| v.|驾驶，驱赶|To drive a vehicle or animal.  It is an archaic or dialectal past tense of "drive".|例句：He drave the cattle to market. 他赶着牛去市场。|近义词：drove, propelled|反义词:None specifically.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drives a car. = 他开车。
She drives the children to school. = 她开车送孩子们去学校。


一般过去时:
He drove to work yesterday. = 他昨天开车去上班。
They drove a hard bargain. = 他们进行了艰难的讨价还价。(引申义)


现在完成时:
I have driven this car for ten years. = 我开这辆车十年了。
She has driven many miles today. = 她今天开了很多英里。


过去完成时:
He had driven for hours before he stopped. = 他开了几个小时的车才停下来。
They had driven all night to get there. = 为了到达那里，他们开了个通宵。


将来时:
I will drive you to the airport. = 我会开车送你去机场。
She will drive the new car tomorrow. = 她明天将驾驶新车。


虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I would drive to the beach. = 如果我有一辆车，我会开车去海滩。
I wish I could drive a sports car. = 我真希望我能开一辆跑车。


被动语态:
The car was driven recklessly. = 这辆车驾驶得很鲁莽。
The cattle were driven into the pen. = 牛被赶进畜栏。


疑问句:
Do you drive? = 你会开车吗？
Who drove the car? = 谁开的车？


否定句:
He didn't drive to work today. = 他今天没开车上班。
She doesn't drive fast. = 她开车不快。



PHRASE->
drive away: 驱赶；开车离开
drive somebody crazy/mad: 把某人逼疯
drive somebody to something: 迫使某人做某事
drive at something: 暗指；意指
drive home: 把...讲清楚；使人彻底明白
