gaiter:/ˈɡeɪtə(r)/| n.|盖腿套；护腿套|A gaiter is a covering for the leg and ankle, often made of fabric or leather, worn over shoes or boots for protection from the elements or for support.|例句：He wore gaiters to keep his ankles warm and dry during the hike. 他在远足时穿着盖腿套以保持脚踝温暖干燥。|近义词：spat, legging|反义词:None
