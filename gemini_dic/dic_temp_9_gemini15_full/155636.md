phishing:/ˈfɪʃɪŋ/| n. v.|网络钓鱼，诈骗|The act of fraudulently acquiring sensitive information, such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details, by disguising oneself as a trustworthy entity in electronic communication.|Example:  He was a victim of phishing, losing thousands of dollars to online scammers. 他是网络钓鱼的受害者，损失了数千美元给网络骗子。|近义词：fraud, scam, deception|反义词：security, safety|名词，动词
