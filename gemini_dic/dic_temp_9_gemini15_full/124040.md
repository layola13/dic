morphemes:/ˈmɔːrfiːmz/|/ˈmɔːrfiːmz/| n. |语素|The smallest units of meaning in a language.  Morphemes can be words (like "cat") or parts of words (like the "-s" in "cats," which indicates plurality).|Example: The word "unbreakable" contains three morphemes: "un-", "break," and "-able".|近义词：units of meaning |反义词：N/A
