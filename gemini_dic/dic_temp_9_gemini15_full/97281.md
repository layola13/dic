influentially: /ˌɪnflʊˈɛnʃəli/ | adj. | 有影响力地；有势力地 |  Having a strong influence or effect.  This describes something that is done or happens in a way that significantly impacts others or a situation. |例句：He influentially advocated for the new policy. 他有影响力地倡导了这项新政策。


例句：Her influentially written article changed the public's perception.  她一篇极具影响力的文章改变了公众的看法。


近义词：powerfully, effectively, persuasively, significantly


反义词：ineffectually, weakly, insignificantly


词性：形容词
