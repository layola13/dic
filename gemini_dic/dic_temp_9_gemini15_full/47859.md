deceits:/dɪˈsiːts/ | n. |欺骗；骗局 |The plural form of "deceit," referring to multiple instances of deception or trickery.  It implies a deliberate and often malicious intent to mislead or betray trust.  |例句：His business dealings were riddled with deceits, leading to his downfall.  他的生意往来充满了欺骗，导致了他的垮台。

His numerous deceits finally caught up with him.  他无数的欺骗行为最终使他自食其果。|近义词：frauds, tricks, deceptions, schemes, swindles |反义词：truth, honesty, integrity


USAGE->
There is no verb form of "deceits".  "Deceits" is a plural noun and therefore does not conjugate through tenses.  The verb form is "deceive".

一般现在时:
He deceives his clients regularly. = 他经常欺骗他的客户。
She deceives her friends with false promises. = 她用虚假的承诺欺骗她的朋友。

一般过去时:
He deceived his partner in the business deal. = 他在生意交易中欺骗了他的伙伴。
She deceived her parents about her whereabouts. = 她欺骗了她的父母关于她的行踪。

现在完成时:
He has deceived many people throughout his career. = 他在他的职业生涯中欺骗了许多人。
She has deceived me several times. = 她欺骗了我好几次。

过去完成时:
He had deceived her for years before she found out. = 在她发现之前，他已经欺骗了她多年。
She had deceived everyone before the truth came to light. = 在真相大白之前，她欺骗了所有人。

将来时:
He will deceive them again if given the chance. = 如果有机会，他会再次欺骗他们。
She will deceive no one again. = 她不会再欺骗任何人。

虚拟语气:
If he deceived her, I would be furious. = 如果他欺骗了她，我会非常愤怒。
If she had deceived me, I would have left. = 如果她欺骗了我，我就离开了。


被动语态:
Many people have been deceived by his charisma. = 许多人被他的人格魅力所欺骗。
She was deceived by his lies. = 她被他的谎言欺骗了。

疑问句:
Were they deceived by his story? = 他们被他的故事欺骗了吗？
Has she ever been deceived before? = 她以前曾经被欺骗过吗？

否定句:
He did not deceive anyone intentionally. = 他并没有故意欺骗任何人。
She would never deceive her best friend. = 她永远不会欺骗她最好的朋友。
