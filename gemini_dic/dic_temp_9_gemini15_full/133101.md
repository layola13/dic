nonerrant:/ˌnɒnˈɛrənt/ | adj. | 无谬的；准确无误的 |  Free from error; completely accurate.  The nonerrant guidance of the GPS led them safely to their destination.  | 近义词：infallible, faultless, flawless, impeccable | 反义词：erroneous, faulty, flawed, imperfect |


例句：

The scientist's nonerrant calculations predicted the eclipse with astonishing accuracy. 科学家的精确计算惊人地准确地预测了日食。

His nonerrant judgment was highly valued by his colleagues. 他的准确判断深受同事的重视。


词源：

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not"
* **errant**: adjective meaning "making mistakes," "wandering," or "deviating from a course."  It derives from the Latin verb *errare*, meaning "to wander" or "to stray."


用法说明：

nonerrant 主要用作形容词，修饰名词，表示某事物或某人没有错误，是完全准确的或可靠的。它通常用于描述判断、计算、信息、指导等方面。  它比简单的 "accurate"  更强调一种绝对的准确性和可靠性，暗示着不会有任何错误。
