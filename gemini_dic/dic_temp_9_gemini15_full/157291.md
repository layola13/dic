pintado:/pɪnˈtɑːdoʊ/| n. |彩绘的；油漆的；(尤指)斑点，花纹；一种鸟类(圭亚那彩鹑)|A pintado is something that is painted or has a painted design, especially with spots or blotches.  It can also refer to a specific type of bird, the painted quail. |例句：The wall had a beautiful pintado finish. 这面墙有着美丽的彩绘效果。

The artist created a striking pintado effect on the canvas.艺术家在画布上创造了一种引人注目的斑点效果。


近义词：painted, decorated, speckled, mottled|反义词：plain, unpainted, monochrome|词性：名词，形容词
