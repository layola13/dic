/praɪˈɔːrətiz/ | n. | 优先事项，优先考虑的事物 | Priorities are things that are considered more important than other things and therefore need to be dealt with first.  |  My priorities are my family and my health. 我的首要任务是我的家人和健康。 | 近义词: preferences, necessities, urgencies | 反义词: non-essentials, trivialities | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are your priorities for this project? = 你这个项目的优先事项是什么？
What is his top priority right now? = 他现在最优先考虑的事情是什么？


一般过去时:
What were your priorities last year? = 你去年最优先考虑的事情是什么？
What did she prioritize during the crisis? = 危机期间她优先考虑了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you prioritized in your life so far? = 到目前为止，你在生活中优先考虑了什么？
What has the company prioritized in its strategic plan? = 公司在其战略规划中优先考虑了什么？


过去完成时:
What had she prioritized before the emergency? = 紧急情况发生之前，她优先考虑了什么？
What had they prioritized in the budget before the cuts? = 预算削减之前，他们优先考虑了什么？


将来时:
What will your priorities be next year? = 你明年的优先事项是什么？
What will the government prioritize in the upcoming budget? = 政府将在即将到来的预算中优先考虑什么？


虚拟语气:
If I had known my priorities, I would have managed my time better. = 如果我知道我的优先事项，我会更好地管理我的时间。
I wish I had prioritized my health earlier. = 我真希望我早点把健康放在首位。


被动语态:
What was prioritized in the meeting? = 会议上优先考虑了什么？
What is being prioritized by the team? = 团队正在优先考虑什么？


疑问句:
What are your main priorities? = 你主要的优先事项是什么？
What needs to be prioritized urgently? = 什么需要紧急处理？


否定句:
I don't know what to prioritize. = 我不知道该优先考虑什么。
They didn't prioritize customer satisfaction. = 他们没有优先考虑客户满意度。


PHRASE->
set priorities:  设定优先级
prioritize tasks: 优先处理任务
re-prioritize:重新设定优先级
a list of priorities: 优先事项清单
shift priorities:  改变优先级
