keychain:/ˈkiːtʃeɪn/| n. |钥匙链，钥匙圈 |A keychain is a small ring or chain used to hold keys together.  It can also be decorative and personalized.|  I keep my house keys and car keys on a keychain. 我把家门钥匙和车钥匙放在一个钥匙链上。 I bought a keychain with my favorite cartoon character on it. 我买了一个印有我最喜欢的卡通人物的钥匙链。| key ring, key holder |  (无明确反义词)
