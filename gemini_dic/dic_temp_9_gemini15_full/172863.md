quadrupling:/kwɒˈdruːplɪŋ/ | v. | 四倍于；增长到四倍 | The act or process of becoming four times as great or numerous.  |例句：The company's profits are quadrupling this year. = 今年公司利润增长到四倍。|例句：The population of the city is quadrupling over the next decade. = 未来十年，该城市人口将增长四倍。|近义词：multiplying by four, increasing fourfold |反义词：halving, quartering


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price is quadrupling. = 价格正在增长到四倍。
Profits are quadrupling this quarter. = 本季度利润增长到四倍。

一般过去时:
The value quadrupled last year. = 去年价值增长到四倍。
The population quadrupled in a short time. = 人口在短时间内增长到四倍。

现在完成时:
The company's revenue has quadrupled since its founding. = 自公司成立以来，收入增长到四倍。
The number of users has quadrupled in the past five years. = 在过去的五年里，用户数量增长到四倍。

过去完成时:
The investment had quadrupled by the time he retired. = 等他退休时，投资已经增长到四倍。
The debt had quadrupled before they could intervene. = 在他们能够干预之前，债务已经增长到四倍。

将来时:
The cost will quadruple next year. = 成本明年将增长到四倍。
Production is expected to quadruple by 2030. = 预计到2030年产量将增长到四倍。

虚拟语气:
If the investment quadrupled, we would be rich. = 如果投资增长到四倍，我们就会富有。
I wish my savings had quadrupled. = 我希望我的存款已经增长到四倍。

被动语态:
The effect was quadrupled by the new technology. = 新技术使效果增长到四倍。
The data was quadrupled unintentionally. = 数据无意中增长到四倍。

疑问句:
Will the production quadruple next year? = 明年产量会增长到四倍吗？
Has the price quadrupled yet? = 价格已经增长到四倍了吗？

否定句:
The number of visitors did not quadruple. = 游客数量并没有增长到四倍。
The output hasn't quadrupled as expected. = 产量并没有像预期的那样增长到四倍。
