lear:/lɜːrn/| /lɝːn/ | v. |学习；获悉；记住|to acquire knowledge or skill through experience or being taught.  |例句：I am learning English. 我正在学习英语。|例句：He learned to ride a bike when he was five. 他五岁时学会了骑自行车。|近义词：study, learn, acquire|反义词：forget, unlearn


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I learn English every day. = 我每天学习英语。
She learns to play the piano. = 她学习弹钢琴。


一般过去时:
I learned a lot from that experience. = 我从那次经历中学到了很多。
They learned to swim last summer. = 他们去年夏天学会了游泳。


现在完成时:
I have learned a lot about history. = 我已经学习了很多历史知识。
She has learned to speak French fluently. = 她已经学会了流利地说法语。


过去完成时:
I had learned the rules before the game started. = 在比赛开始之前，我已经学会了规则。
They had learned the lesson by the time the teacher arrived. = 等老师到达时，他们已经吸取了教训。


将来时:
I will learn to drive next year. = 我明年将学习开车。
She will learn how to use the software. = 她将学习如何使用该软件。


虚拟语气:
If I learned harder, I would pass the exam. = 如果我学习更努力，我就能通过考试。
I wish I had learned more about computers. = 我希望我以前学习更多关于电脑的知识。


被动语态:
The lesson was learned quickly. = 这节课很快就被学会了。
New words are learned more easily through repetition. = 通过重复，新单词更容易被记住。


疑问句:
What did you learn today? = 你今天学到了什么？
Do you learn anything useful? = 你学到什么有用的东西了吗？


否定句:
I didn't learn anything new today. = 我今天没有学到任何新东西。
She doesn't learn easily. = 她不容易学东西。


PHRASE->
learn by heart = 死记硬背
learn from = 向…学习
learn about = 了解
learn to = 学习…
learn of = 听说
