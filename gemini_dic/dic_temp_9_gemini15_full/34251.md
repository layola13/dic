The name likely derives from the sound made when slapping the dough onto the cooking surface.  There is no extensive historical record detailing the specific origins of clapbread, as it is a type of bread that may have evolved independently in various cultures, with similar preparations existing across regions.  The term itself is relatively informal and regional in usage.|Example:  "She made some delicious clapbread for the picnic." = 她为野餐做了一些美味的拍饼。|近义词：flatbread, griddlecake, pancake|反义词：loaf (of bread)
