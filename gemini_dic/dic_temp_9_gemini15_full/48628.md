defencing:/dɪˈfɛnsɪŋ/| v.| 防御；辩护 | The act of defending someone or something.  It involves protecting from attack or criticism. |例句：The soldiers were defending their country against the invasion.  士兵们正在保卫他们的国家免受侵略。

例句：The lawyer was defending her client against the charges. 律师正在为她的当事人辩护，以对抗指控。|近义词：protecting, guarding, shielding, advocating |反义词：attacking, assaulting, accusing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is defending his thesis tomorrow. = 他明天正在答辩他的论文。
They are defending their city against the enemy. = 他们正在保卫他们的城市免受敌人攻击。


一般过去时:
She defended her ideas passionately. = 她热情地捍卫了自己的想法。
The team defended well throughout the match. = 这支球队在整场比赛中都表现出色。


现在完成时:
We have been defending our position for years. = 我们多年来一直坚持我们的立场。
The army has successfully defended the border. = 军队成功保卫了边境。


过去完成时:
They had defended the castle valiantly before surrendering. = 在投降之前，他们英勇地保卫了城堡。
The lawyer had already defended this client in a previous case. = 这位律师之前已经代理过这位客户的案件。


将来时:
He will defend his title next month. = 他下个月将卫冕他的冠军头衔。
They will be defending their home turf. = 他们将在自己的地盘上进行防守。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would defend myself more strongly. = 如果我是你，我会更强硬地为自己辩护。
I wish I had defended my beliefs more effectively. = 我希望我之前能更有效地捍卫我的信仰。


被动语态:
The city was defended bravely by the soldiers. =这座城市被士兵们英勇地保卫着。
His actions were defended by his supporters. = 他的行为得到了支持者的辩护。


疑问句:
Are you defending your friend? = 你在为你的朋友辩护吗？
How will they defend against such a powerful attack? = 他们将如何抵御如此强大的攻击？


否定句:
He didn't defend his actions adequately. = 他没有充分为自己行为辩护。
She wouldn't defend him, even if he were innocent. = 她不会为他辩护，即使他是无辜的。
