internalizing: /ɪnˈtɜːrnəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 内化；吸收；使成为自己的一部分 |  The process of accepting and integrating an idea, belief, or behavior into one's own personality or worldview.  It involves making something internal or personal rather than external or objective. |


例句：

He is internalizing the lessons from his mistakes. = 他正在从错误中吸取教训。

She is internalizing her grief after the loss of her pet. = 她正在消化失去宠物后的悲伤。


近义词：assimilating, absorbing, integrating, incorporating


反义词：externalizing, rejecting, ignoring


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They internalize new information quickly. = 他们很快就能吸收新信息。
He internalizes his emotions, rarely expressing them openly. = 他将自己的情绪内化，很少公开表达。


一般过去时:
She internalized the company's values during her training. = 在培训期间，她内化了公司的价值观。
The children internalized the rules of the game after several rounds. = 孩子们在几轮游戏后就内化了游戏规则。


现在完成时:
He has internalized the principles of non-violent communication. = 他已经内化了非暴力沟通的原则。
She has internalized so much stress that she's become unwell. = 她承受了过多的压力，以至于身体不适。


过去完成时:
Before the presentation, he had internalized all the key points. = 在演示之前，他已经内化了所有要点。
Having internalized the criticism, she worked to improve her performance. = 在内化了批评之后，她努力改进自己的表现。


将来时:
She will internalize the new software's features by the end of the week. = 她将在本周末之前掌握新软件的功能。
They will internalize the lessons learned from this project. = 他们将从这个项目中学到的教训内化。


虚拟语气:
If he internalized the feedback, he could improve significantly. = 如果他能内化反馈，他就能显著提高。
I wish I had internalized the warnings sooner. = 我希望我早点能吸取教训。


被动语态:
The company's culture is internalized by new employees through intensive training. = 新员工通过强化培训将公司的文化内化。
These values should be internalized by all members of the team. = 这些价值观应该被团队的所有成员内化。


疑问句:

Have they internalized the importance of teamwork? = 他们是否已经内化了团队合作的重要性？


否定句:
He didn't internalize the lesson, and made the same mistake again. = 他没有吸取教训，又犯了同样的错误。
She failed to internalize the complex instructions. = 她未能理解复杂的指示。
