knickknack:/ˈnɪkˌnæk/ | n. |小摆设；小玩意儿 | A small, usually decorative object; a trinket.  Knickknacks are often found in collections or displayed on shelves or mantles.  |例句：The shelves were covered in knickknacks.  架子上摆满了小摆设。

例句：Grandma's house was full of knickknacks she'd collected over the years. 奶奶的房子里摆满了她多年来收集的小玩意儿。

近义词：trinket, gadget, gewgaw, ornament

反义词：无
