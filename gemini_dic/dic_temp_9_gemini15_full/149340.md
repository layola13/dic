pachinkos:/pəˈtʃɪŋkoʊz/| n.|弹珠机（复数）|Japanese game of chance played on a vertically oriented machine with many pins and numerous small holes, similar to a pinball machine but with more complex mechanics and payouts.|例句：Many pachinkos are located near train stations in Japan.  许多弹珠游戏厅位于日本火车站附近。
例句：The sound of pachinkos was deafening in the arcade. 游戏厅里弹珠机的声音震耳欲聋。|近义词： pinball machines (less complex)|反义词：无|词性：名词
