rethunder:/riːˈθʌndər/|v.|再打雷，再次雷鸣|To thunder again; to thunder repeatedly.|例句：The storm rethundered, shaking the house.暴风雨再次雷鸣，震动了房子。|近义词：re-roar, redouble|反义词：silence, hush.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm rethunders throughout the night. = 暴风雨整夜都在隆隆作响。
It rethunders occasionally during the day. = 白天偶尔会再次打雷。


一般过去时:
The storm rethundered after a brief lull. = 暴风雨在短暂的平静后再次雷鸣。
It rethundered loudly, startling the birds. = 它响亮地再次打雷，吓到了鸟儿。


现在完成时:
The storm has rethundered several times this afternoon. = 今天下午暴风雨已经响了几次了。
It has rethundered with increasing intensity. = 雷声越来越响。


过去完成时:
The storm had rethundered before we even reached shelter. = 我们甚至还没到达避难所，暴风雨就再次雷鸣了。
It had rethundered all night, keeping us awake. = 它整夜都在隆隆作响，让我们睡不着。


将来时:
The storm will rethunder later tonight. = 暴风雨今晚晚些时候还会再次雷鸣。
It will rethunder with greater force. = 它将会以更大的力量再次雷鸣。


虚拟语气:
If the storm rethundered, we would seek shelter immediately. = 如果暴风雨再次雷鸣，我们会立即寻找避难所。
I wish the storm hadn't rethundered so loudly. = 我希望暴风雨不要响得那么大声。


被动语态:
(The verb "rethunder" is rarely used in the passive voice because it is intransitive.)


疑问句:
Will the storm rethunder again soon? = 暴风雨很快还会再次雷鸣吗？
Did the storm rethunder throughout the night? = 暴风雨整夜都在隆隆作响吗？


否定句:
The storm did not rethunder after the initial clap. = 暴风雨在第一次雷声后没有再次雷鸣。
It will not rethunder during the daytime. = 白天不会再次打雷。
