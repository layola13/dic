iuds:/ˈaɪjuːdz/ | n. |宫内节育器（子宫环）|Intrauterine devices (IUDs) are small, T-shaped devices inserted into the uterus to prevent pregnancy. They work by preventing sperm from reaching the egg, or by preventing a fertilized egg from implanting in the uterus.  |Example:  Many women choose IUDs as a long-term birth control method. 许多女性选择宫内节育器作为一种长期的避孕方法。|近义词：intrauterine contraceptive, coil |反义词：无
