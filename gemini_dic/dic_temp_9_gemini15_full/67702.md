exultantly: /ɪɡˈzʌltəntli/ | adv. | 欢欣鼓舞地；得意洋洋地 |  expressing or feeling great happiness and triumph.  | 例句：She laughed exultantly. 她欢欣鼓舞地大笑起来。


She accepted the award exultantly. 她得意洋洋地接受了奖项。


近义词：triumphantly, joyfully, jubilantly, gleefully | 反义词：sadly, dejectedly, sorrowfully


词源:  The word "exultantly" derives from the verb "exult," which comes from the Latin word "exultare," meaning "to leap for joy," "to rejoice greatly."  The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective "exultant" into the adverb "exultantly."
