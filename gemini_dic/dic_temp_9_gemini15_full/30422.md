centralises:/ˈsɛntrəlaɪzɪz/| v.| 集中化；中央集权化 | To bring or concentrate (control, power, administration, etc.) under a single authority; to concentrate in or at a center.  |例句：The company centralises its operations in London. 公司将其业务集中在伦敦。  The government is centralising power. 政府正在集权。|近义词：concentrate, consolidate, unify |反义词：decentralise, disperse
