foxhound:/ˈfɒkshaʊnd/|/ˈfɑːkshaʊnd/| n. |猎狐犬|A foxhound is a type of hound dog specifically bred for hunting foxes.  They are known for their stamina, keen sense of smell, and pack hunting abilities. |The foxhounds followed the scent of the fox across the fields.  猎狐犬穿过田野追寻狐狸的气味。  The pack of foxhounds bayed loudly as they closed in on their prey.  一群猎狐犬在逼近猎物时大声吠叫。|hound, beagle| (no direct antonym, but perhaps "domestic dog" in a contrasting sense)|名词
