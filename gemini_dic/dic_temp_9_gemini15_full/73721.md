fork-out:/fɔːk aʊt/| vt.|付清；拿出（尤指一大笔钱）|to pay out (especially a large sum of money), often reluctantly.|He had to fork out a large sum of money to repair the car. 他不得不支付一大笔钱来修理汽车。|近义词：pay, shell out, cough up|反义词：receive, gain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They fork out a lot of money on their holidays every year. = 他们每年在假期上花很多钱。
We fork out for our children's education. = 我们为孩子的教育付钱。


一般过去时:
She forked out $1000 for the concert tickets. = 她为演唱会门票付了1000美元。
He forked out a significant amount for the house renovations. = 他为房屋装修支付了一大笔钱。


现在完成时:
I've forked out more than I expected for this project. = 我为这个项目支付的钱比预期的多。
They've forked out a fortune on their new house. = 他们为新房子花了一大笔钱。


过去完成时:
We had forked out all our savings before the trip. = 旅行前我们已经花光了所有的积蓄。
She had forked out a considerable sum before realizing the mistake. = 她意识到错误之前已经支付了一大笔钱。


将来时:
I will fork out the money as soon as I receive my salary. = 我一收到工资就付钱。
They will fork out for the repairs next week. = 他们下周将支付维修费用。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would fork out for a new car. = 如果我有更多钱，我会买一辆新车。
I wish I didn't have to fork out so much for rent. = 我希望我不必为租金支付这么多钱。


被动语态:
A large sum of money was forked out for the charity event. = 为慈善活动支付了一大笔钱。
The repair costs will be forked out by the insurance company. = 保险公司将支付维修费用。


疑问句:
Will you fork out for dinner tonight? = 你今晚付晚餐的钱吗？
How much did they fork out for the painting? = 他们为那幅画花了多少钱？


否定句:
I won't fork out for something I don't need. = 我不会为我不需要的东西花钱。
They refused to fork out for the extra services. = 他们拒绝为额外服务付费。


PHRASE->
fork out for = 为…付钱
fork out a lot of money = 花很多钱
have to fork out = 不得不付钱
fork out reluctantly = 不情愿地付钱
fork out a fortune = 花一大笔钱
