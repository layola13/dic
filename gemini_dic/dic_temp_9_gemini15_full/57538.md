drained:/dreɪnd/|/ˈdreɪnd/| adj., v. |耗尽的；疲惫的；排干；抽干|  Emptied of liquid; exhausted; lacking energy.  |例句：The drained pool needed refilling.  =  泳池的水被排空了，需要重新注水。

例句：After the marathon, I felt completely drained. = 马拉松之后，我感到精疲力尽。

近义词：exhausted, depleted, emptied, tired, weary|反义词：energized, refreshed, full, invigorated

词性:形容词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drains the bathtub. = 他把浴缸里的水放掉。
The plant drains the soil of nutrients. = 植物消耗土壤中的养分。
