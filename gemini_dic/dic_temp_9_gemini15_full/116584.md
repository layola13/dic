matric:/ˈmætɹɪk/| n. |入学，注册；入学考试；（南非）高中毕业证书|The act of enrolling in a college or university; an examination for entrance to a college or university; (South Africa) a matriculation certificate.|例句：He successfully completed his matriculation examinations and gained admission to the university. 他成功完成了入学考试并获得了大学入学资格。|例句：She is preparing for her matric exams next month. 她正在准备下个月的入学考试。|近义词：enrollment, registration, admission|反义词：graduation, expulsion

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He matriculates at Oxford University. 他在牛津大学注册入学。
She matriculates at the prestigious school. 她在那所名校注册入学。


一般过去时:
He matriculated from high school last year. 他去年高中毕业。
She matriculated at Harvard University in 2020. 她2020年在哈佛大学入学。


现在完成时:
He has matriculated at a top university. 他已在顶尖大学入学。
She has matriculated and is now a freshman. 她已经入学，现在是大一新生。


过去完成时:
He had matriculated before the war broke out.  战争爆发前他已经入学了。
She had matriculated from a private school. 她毕业于一所私立学校。


将来时:
He will matriculate next year. 他明年将入学。
She will matriculate at the medical school. 她将在医学院入学。


虚拟语气:
If he had matriculated earlier, he would have a better job now. 如果他早点入学，他现在会有一份更好的工作。
I wish I had matriculated at a different university. 我希望我当初选择不同的大学入学。


被动语态:
Many students are matriculated every year. 每年都有许多学生入学。
The new students were matriculated last week. 新生在上周入学。


疑问句:
When did he matriculate? 他什么时候入学的？
Has she matriculated yet? 她入学了吗？


否定句:
He did not matriculate this year. 他今年没有入学。
She did not matriculate at the university of her choice. 她没有进入她理想的大学入学。
