pungent:/ˈpʌndʒənt/| adj.|辛辣的；刺激的；尖刻的 |Having a strong, sharp taste or smell; causing a sharp or stinging sensation.  The word often describes something that is sharp and intense, whether it's a flavor, smell, or even a remark. |例句：The pungent smell of garlic filled the kitchen. 大蒜的辛辣气味充满了厨房。

例句：Her pungent criticism stung him deeply. 她尖刻的批评深深刺痛了他。

近义词：sharp, acrid, biting, spicy, caustic

反义词：mild, bland, gentle, subtle

词源:
The word "pungent" comes from the Latin word "pungens," the present participle of "pungere," meaning "to prick" or "to pierce."  This reflects the sensation of sharpness associated with pungent smells and tastes.
