oxalate:/ˈɒksəleɪt/| n. |草酸盐|Oxalate is a salt or ester of oxalic acid.  It's a naturally occurring compound found in many plants.  Some oxalates are soluble in water, while others are insoluble.  The solubility and concentration of oxalates can affect their toxicity.| Example: Spinach contains a significant amount of oxalate, which can affect calcium absorption. 菠菜含有大量的草酸盐，这会影响钙的吸收。|近义词：oxalic acid salt|反义词：无
