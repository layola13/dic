pipevine:/ˈpaɪpvaɪn/ | n. | 管状藤本植物 | A climbing plant with hollow stems, often belonging to the genus *Aristolochia*, known for its unusual flower shapes and sometimes toxic properties.  These plants are often host plants for pipevine swallowtail butterflies. | Example:  The pipevine twined around the trellis, its strange flowers attracting hummingbirds.  管状藤本植物缠绕在凉棚上，它奇特的花朵吸引着蜂鸟。|近义词：Aristolochia (genus name) |反义词：无
