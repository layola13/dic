conciliatingly:/kənˈsɪlieɪtɪŋli/| adv. |劝慰地；抚慰地；调解地 |In a way that seeks to reconcile or appease.  It suggests a gentle and appeasing manner intended to soothe or pacify someone. |He spoke conciliatingly to the angry customer, offering a refund. 他温和地对生气的顾客说话，并提出退款。|近义词：appeasingly, soothingly, placatingly |反义词：aggressively, antagonistically, belligerently
