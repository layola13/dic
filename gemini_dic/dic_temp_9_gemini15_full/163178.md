precompensate:/ˌpriːkɒmˈpɛnseɪt/| v.|预先补偿，提前补偿|To compensate beforehand; to pay or give something in advance as compensation for something expected or anticipated in the future.|例句：The company precompensated its employees for the upcoming holiday shutdown. 公司提前支付了员工在即将到来的假期停工期间的工资。
例句：They precompensated for potential losses by investing in a diversified portfolio. 他们通过投资多元化投资组合来预先弥补潜在的损失。|近义词：anticipate, prepay, offset|反义词：undercompensate, shortchange
