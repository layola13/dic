fades:/feɪdz/ | v. | 褪色；逐渐消失；衰弱 | To lose brightness or intensity gradually; to disappear slowly; to weaken or decline.  |例句：The paint on the wall is fading. 墙上的油漆正在褪色。

The sound of the music faded into the distance. 音乐声逐渐消失在远处。


近义词：dim, weaken, vanish, decline | 反义词：brighten, intensify, strengthen


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The color of the shirt fades in the sun. 这件衬衫的颜色在阳光下褪色。
His memory slowly fades with age. 他的记忆力随着年龄的增长慢慢衰退。


一般过去时:
The flowers faded quickly after being picked. 花被摘下后很快就枯萎了。
Her anger faded as she calmed down. 她平静下来后怒气消退了。


现在完成时:
The photograph has faded over the years. 这张照片多年来已经褪色了。
His enthusiasm has faded since the project failed. 项目失败后，他的热情消退了。


过去完成时:
The light had faded before we reached the summit. 我们到达山顶之前天色已经暗了。
His confidence had faded after several setbacks. 经历几次挫折后，他的信心减弱了。


将来时:
The paint will fade if it's not protected. 如果不加以保护，油漆会褪色。
His influence will fade as time goes on. 随着时间的推移，他的影响力将会减弱。


虚拟语气:
If the sun were not so strong, the colors wouldn't fade so quickly. 如果阳光没有那么强烈，颜色就不会褪色这么快。
I wish my memories didn't fade so easily. 我希望我的记忆不会这么容易消退。


被动语态:
The photograph was faded by the sun. 照片被阳光晒褪色了。
His hopes were faded by the repeated failures. 他屡屡失败使他的希望破灭了。


疑问句:
Is the color of the fabric fading? 布料的颜色是不是正在褪色？
Are his ambitions fading? 他的雄心壮志是不是正在消退？


否定句:
The colors don't fade easily. 这些颜色不容易褪色。
His determination didn't fade in the face of adversity. 面对逆境，他的决心没有动摇。


PHRASE->
fade away: 逐渐消失
fade out: 逐渐消失；淡出
fade in: 逐渐出现；淡入
fade to black: 逐渐变黑
fade into: 逐渐变成
