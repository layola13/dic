self-preferences:/ˌsɛlfˈprɛfərənsɪz/| n. |自我偏好，个人喜好 |Self-preferences refer to the individual's own choices and inclinations, often reflecting their values, tastes, and needs.  These preferences can influence various aspects of their life, including their career, relationships, and lifestyle. |Example:  Her self-preferences led her to pursue a career in the arts, despite pressure to enter a more traditional field.  她的自我偏好让她追求艺术事业，尽管有压力让她从事更传统的工作。|近义词：personal preferences, individual choices, personal tastes |反义词：external pressures, societal expectations |名词
