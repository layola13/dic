sisals:/ˈsaɪzəl/| n.|龙舌兰纤维；剑麻|A strong, coarse fiber obtained from the leaves of the sisal plant, used in making rope, twine, and other products.  |例句：The rope was made from sisal. 这根绳子是用剑麻做的。|例句：Sisal is a durable and inexpensive material. 剑麻是一种耐用且廉价的材料。|近义词：hemp, jute, agave fiber|反义词：None specifically;  could consider fine fabrics as a contrast.
