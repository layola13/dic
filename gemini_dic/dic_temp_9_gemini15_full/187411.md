rogatory:/ˈrɒɡətəri/| adj.|恳求的；请求的；询问的|Relating to or of the nature of a request or entreaty; asking a question, especially in a formal or official setting.  This word often appears in legal contexts.|例句：The court issued a rogatory commission to obtain evidence from a foreign country. 法院发布了一项请求书，以从外国获取证据。  The judge responded to the rogatory letter promptly.  法官迅速回应了请求信。|近义词：supplicatory, petitive, interrogative|反义词：imperative, mandatory, peremptory
