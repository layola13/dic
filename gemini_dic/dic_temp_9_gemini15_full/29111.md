cascade's:/kæˈskeɪdz/| n.|级联，瀑布；倾泻而下 |The word "cascade's" is the possessive form of the noun "cascade," indicating belonging or possession.  A cascade is a series of things, such as waterfalls or events, arranged in a way that resembles a waterfall. It can refer to a waterfall itself, or to something that resembles a waterfall in its appearance or the way it flows or unfolds.  |例句：The water cascaded down the mountainside.  水从山坡上倾泻而下。

The cascades of the Niagara Falls are breathtaking. 尼亚加拉瀑布的瀑布令人叹为观止。|近义词：waterfall, torrent, deluge, shower|反义词：trickle, drip
