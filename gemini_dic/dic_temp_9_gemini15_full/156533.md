pick-me-up: /ˈpɪk miː ʌp/ | n. |提神的东西；令人振奋的事物 |Something that improves your mood or energy levels.  A small treat or activity that temporarily lifts your spirits. |例句：A cup of coffee is my morning pick-me-up.  一杯咖啡是我早晨的提神饮品。

A short walk in the park provided a much-needed pick-me-up after a stressful day at work.  在工作压力大的一天后，在公园里短暂散步给了我急需的提神。


近义词：boost, stimulant, tonic, energizer, refresher

反义词：depressant, downer

词源:  The term "pick-me-up" is a compound word.  "Pick" in this context means to lift or raise, and "me up" refers to lifting one's spirits or energy.  The phrase developed as a colloquialism in the early 20th century.
