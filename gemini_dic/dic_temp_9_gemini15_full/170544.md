pseudepigraphic:/ˌsjuːdəˈpɪɡrəfɪk/| adj.|伪经的；伪托的；|Relating to or characteristic of pseudepigrapha, writings falsely attributed to an author or period.  The term often refers to ancient Jewish and early Christian texts attributed to biblical figures or other historical personalities but actually written later.|例句：The discovery of new pseudepigraphic texts has shed light on the religious beliefs of the time.  发现新的伪经文本阐明了当时人们的宗教信仰。 The scholars debated the authenticity of the pseudepigraphic gospel.  学者们就伪经福音的真实性展开了辩论。|近义词：apocryphal, spurious, fictitious|反义词：authentic, genuine, canonical
