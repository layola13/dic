preerecting: /ˌpriːɪˈrɛktɪŋ/ | v. | 预先竖立；预先搭建 |  The act of erecting something beforehand; preparing something in advance for an upright position.  This word is less common and often implies a preparatory stage before the main erection. | 例句：The construction crew was preerecting the steel framework before pouring the concrete foundation.  施工队在浇筑混凝土基础之前预先竖起了钢结构框架。


The construction company preerected the walls of the building off-site to save time. 建筑公司在工地外预先搭建了建筑物的墙壁以节省时间。 | 近义词：prefabricating, assembling, preparing | 反义词：dismantling, demolishing, tearing down | 动词
