dignitary:/ˈdɪɡnɪtəri/| n.|要人，高官|A person who holds a high rank or position, especially in government or the church.  A dignitary is someone important and respected because of their position or achievements.|例句：The dignitary was greeted with a 21-gun salute.  这位要人受到了21响礼炮的迎接。|例句：Many dignitaries attended the international summit. 许多要人出席了国际峰会。|近义词：VIP, official, leader, personage|反义词：nobody, unknown person
