shucked:/ʃʌkt/| adj.|去壳的；剥皮的；|Removed from its shell or husk.  The term often refers to corn, oysters, or other shellfish.|例句：The corn on the cob was already shucked and ready to grill.  玉米棒子已经剥好了，准备烤。

The oysters were shucked and served on the half shell.  牡蛎已经剥壳了，放在半壳上供应。|近义词：shelled, peeled, husked|反义词：unshelled, in-the-shell
