record:/ˈrɛkɔːrd/ /ˈrɛkərd/| n., v.|记录，录音，唱片|

英文解析:
As a noun, "record" refers to a written account of something that has happened, a thing that has been achieved, or a disc or tape on which sound is stored. As a verb, it means to write down or store information, or to make a sound recording.  The word carries connotations of permanence and preservation.


例句:
(Noun):
The police kept a detailed record of the incident.  = 警方对事件做了详细记录。

She set a new world record in the marathon. = 她打破了马拉松的世界纪录。


(Verb):
I need to record my lecture for my students. = 我需要为我的学生录制我的讲座。

The temperature was recorded at 35 degrees Celsius. = 气温记录在摄氏35度。


近义词:
(Noun): document, account, chronicle, registration, disc, album
(Verb): document, register, note, chronicle, tape, film


反义词:
(Noun):  forgetfulness, oblivion (in the sense of something not being recorded)
(Verb): erase, delete, forget


词性:
Noun, Verb
