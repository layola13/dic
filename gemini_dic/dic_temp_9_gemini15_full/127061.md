natalities:/neɪˈtælɪtiz/| n. |出生率，出生数|The number of births in a particular place or during a particular period.|例句：The natalities in rural areas are declining. 农村地区的出生率正在下降。|近义词：birth rate, births|反义词：mortalities, death rate


USAGE->

The natality rate is high in this region. = 这个地区的出生率很高。
Natalities are increasing in the city. = 城市的出生率正在上升。

Natalities were low last year. = 去年出生率很低。
The natality rate decreased significantly in that country last decade. = 上个十年那个国家的出生率显著下降。
