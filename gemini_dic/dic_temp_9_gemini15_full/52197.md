diaspore:/ˈdaɪəspɔːr/| n. |芽胞；孢子；散居；侨民；|A diaspore is a seed or other reproductive structure that is dispersed from the parent plant.  It can also refer to a scattered population of people, especially one that has migrated from their original homeland.|例句：The wind dispersed the diaspores across the landscape. 风将芽胞散播到整个景观中。|例句：The large diaspora of Irish immigrants in the United States contributed significantly to American culture.  美国的大量爱尔兰移民侨民对美国文化做出了重大贡献。|近义词：spore, seed, propagule, migrant community |反义词：native population
