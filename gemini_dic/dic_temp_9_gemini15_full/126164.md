mutton:/ˈmʌtən/| n. |羊肉|Meat from a mature sheep.  Mutton is generally tougher and stronger flavored than lamb. |The roast mutton was delicious.  这道烤羊肉非常美味。|  The stew was made with mutton and vegetables. 这道炖菜是用羊肉和蔬菜做的。| lamb, lamb meat | beef, pork |名词
