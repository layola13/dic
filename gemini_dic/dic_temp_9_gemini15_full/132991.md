nonenergy:/ˌnɒnˈɛnədʒi/ | adj. |非能量的，非能源的 |Not related to or involving energy.  This term often refers to aspects of a system or process that do not directly involve the production, consumption, or transfer of energy. For example, in a manufacturing process, nonenergy costs might include labor, materials, and overhead. |例句：The nonenergy costs of the project significantly exceeded the budget.  这个项目的非能源成本大大超过了预算。

The report analyzed both the energy and nonenergy components of the national economy.  该报告分析了国民经济的能源和非能源构成部分。


近义词：non-energetic (less common)


反义词：energetic


词性：形容词
