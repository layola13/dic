gracing:/ˈɡreɪsɪŋ/| v.|装饰，使优美，荣幸，赐予 | to add beauty or elegance to something; to honor or dignify something or someone |例句：The sun graced the morning with its golden light.  阳光用它金色的光芒装点着早晨。|例句：The singer's performance graced the concert. 歌手的表演为音乐会增色不少。|近义词：adorn, embellish, honor, dignify |反义词：mar, spoil, dishonor, disgrace


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist is gracing the canvas with delicate brushstrokes. = 艺术家正在用精细的笔触装饰画布。
She graces every room she enters. = 她所进入的每个房间都因她而增色。


一般过去时:
The beautiful flowers graced the garden. = 美丽的花朵装饰着花园。
His presence graced the occasion. = 他的出席为这次活动增光添彩。


现在完成时:
Her kindness has graced many lives. = 她的善良已经惠及许多人的生活。
The old house has been graced with many happy memories. = 这座老房子里充满了许多美好的回忆。


过去完成时:
The room had been graced with a stunning chandelier before the party. = 派对前，房间里已经装饰了一盏令人惊叹的枝形吊灯。
The family had been graced with good fortune for many years. = 这个家庭多年来一直很幸运。


将来时:
The new sculpture will grace the city square. = 新雕塑将装饰城市广场。
The renowned chef will grace the event with his culinary skills. = 知名厨师将用他的烹饪技巧为活动增光。


虚拟语气:
If only the weather would grace us with sunshine. = 但愿天气能赐予我们阳光。
I wish his presence would grace the ceremony. = 我希望他的出席能使仪式增光。


被动语态:
The ceremony was graced by the presence of royalty. = 典礼因皇室成员的出席而增光。
The hall was graced with beautiful paintings. = 礼堂里装饰着美丽的绘画作品。


疑问句:
Will your attendance grace the meeting? = 你会出席会议吗？（你的出席会使会议增光吗？）
Does the new design grace the building? = 新的设计使这栋建筑增色了吗？


否定句:
The harsh weather did not grace the outdoor event. = 恶劣的天气并没有使户外活动增色。
His rudeness did not grace the situation. = 他的粗鲁举止并没有使情况好转。
