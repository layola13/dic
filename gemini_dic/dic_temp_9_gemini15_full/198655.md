shutting: /ˈʃʌtɪŋ/ | v. | 关闭；停止；禁闭 | The act of closing something; the process of bringing something to an end; confining or enclosing. |

例句：

He is shutting the door. = 他正在关门。

The factory is shutting down. = 工厂正在关闭。


近义词：closing, stopping, ceasing, terminating

反义词：opening, starting, beginning


词性：动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:

The dog is shutting the gate. = 狗正在关门。

They are shutting the windows. = 他们在关窗户。


一般过去时:

She shut the book. = 她合上了书。

We shut the door behind us. = 我们关上了身后的门。


现在完成时:

I have shut the window. = 我已经关上窗户了。

They have shut down the business. = 他们已经关闭了生意。


过去完成时:

She had shut the door before he arrived. = 他到达之前，她已经关上门了。

They had shut off the water supply. = 他们已经切断了供水。


将来时:

I will shut the door. = 我将关上门。

He will shut down the computer. = 他将关掉电脑。


虚拟语气:

If I shut the door, it would be quieter. = 如果我关上门，就会安静一些。

I wish I had shut the window earlier. = 我真希望我早点关上窗户。


被动语态:

The door was shut. = 门被关上了。

The factory is being shut down. = 工厂正在被关闭。


疑问句:

Is he shutting the window? = 他正在关窗户吗？

Are they shutting the shop? = 他们正在关店吗？


否定句:

He isn't shutting the door. = 他没有关门。

They didn't shut the gate. = 他们没有关门。


PHRASE->

shut down = 关闭；停止运转

shut off = 关闭；切断

shut up = 闭嘴

shut out = 排斥；挡住

shut in = 围住；困住
