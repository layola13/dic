sjambok:/ˈdʒæmbɒk/| n. |鞭子；皮鞭|A sjambok is a type of whip, typically made of hippopotamus hide or other tough leather, with a long, tapering handle and a short, braided lash. It is used for riding and controlling animals, particularly in southern Africa.|例句：The farmer used a sjambok to guide his oxen.  农夫用皮鞭赶他的牛。|例句：The sound of the sjambok cracked through the air. 皮鞭的声响划破空气。|近义词：whip, lash, crop|反义词：None specifically, but words implying gentleness or kindness could be considered opposites.|名词
