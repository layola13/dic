salicylate:/ˈsælɪsɪleɪt/| n.|水杨酸盐|A salt or ester of salicylic acid. Salicylates are used medicinally to reduce fever and relieve pain and inflammation.  They are found naturally in some plants, such as willow bark.|例句：Aspirin is a common salicylate used to treat headaches and fever. 阿司匹林是一种常用的水杨酸盐，用于治疗头痛和发烧。

The doctor prescribed a salicylate to reduce the inflammation in my knee. 医生开了水杨酸盐来减轻我膝盖的炎症。|近义词：aspirin (a specific type of salicylate)|反义词：无
