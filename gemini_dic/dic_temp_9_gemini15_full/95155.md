inarching:/ˈɪnɑːrtʃɪŋ/| n.|  弓状嫁接；(树枝的)弯曲生长 |The process of grafting a branch onto a tree trunk by bending it down and burying a portion of it in the ground until it takes root.  Inarching is a type of grafting used to join two plants together so they grow as one.  |Example: The gardener used inarching to join the two branches.|近义词：grafting, layering|反义词：separation, severance|名词
