degust:/dɛˈɡʌst/| v.|品尝，细细品味|To taste and savor something, especially wine or food, with care and attention to its qualities.|例句：The sommelier expertly degustated the wine, noting its subtle nuances.  侍酒师熟练地品尝着葡萄酒，注意到其细微的差别。

例句：We spent the evening degustating the various cheeses and wines. 我们整个晚上都在细细品味各种奶酪和葡萄酒。


近义词：taste, savor, relish, sample|反义词：gulp, guzzle


词性：动词

词源:
The word "degustation" comes from the Latin word "gustare," meaning "to taste." The prefix "de-" intensifies the meaning, suggesting a thorough and careful tasting.  The verb "degust" is a less common but perfectly valid back-formation from the noun "degustation".
