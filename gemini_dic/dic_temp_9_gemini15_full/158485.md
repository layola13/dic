plenaries:/ˈplɛnəriːz/| n.|全体会议；全体会议记录|A plenary session is a meeting of all the members of a group, especially a parliament or a committee.  It refers to a meeting where everyone with voting rights is present.|例句：The plenary session of the conference will be held tomorrow.  本次会议的全会将于明天举行。
The committee published the plenary minutes. 委员会公布了全会记录。|近义词：full session, general assembly, convention|反义词：partial session, subcommittee meeting
