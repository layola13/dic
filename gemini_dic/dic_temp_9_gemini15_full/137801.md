nonverbalized:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːbəlaɪzd/| adj. |未用言语表达的，非语言化的|Not expressed in words; communicated through nonverbal means such as body language, facial expressions, or tone of voice.  |例句：Her nonverbalized anger was evident in her clenched fists and sharp tone.  她的未言明的愤怒从她紧握的拳头和尖锐的语气中显而易见。

例句：The nonverbalized message conveyed through his posture suggested discomfort. 他通过姿势传达的非语言信息暗示了他感到不舒服。


近义词：unsaid, unspoken, implicit, unexpressed, tacit

反义词：verbalized, expressed, articulated, stated


词性：形容词
