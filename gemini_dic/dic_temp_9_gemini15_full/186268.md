rhizoma:/raɪˈzoʊmə/| n. |根茎|A rhizome is a continuously growing horizontal underground stem which puts out lateral shoots and roots at intervals.  It is often thick and fleshy, and serves as a means of vegetative reproduction. Rhizomes are characteristic of many plants, including ferns, grasses, and lilies.| Example: Ginger is a rhizome that is used as a spice and a medicine. 姜是一种用作香料和药物的根茎。|近义词：rootstock, underground stem |反义词：aerial stem
