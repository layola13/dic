potshot:/ˈpɑːtʃɒt/ /ˈpɑːtʃɑːt/| n., v.|随意射击；轻率的批评；|A potshot is a shot fired without aiming carefully, or a sudden, casual, and often unfair criticism.|例句：He took a potshot at the bird from his car. 他从车里随意朝那只鸟开了一枪。|例句：The columnist took a potshot at the mayor's new policy.专栏作家对市长的新政策进行了轻率的批评。|近义词：random shot, casual criticism, gibe, jibe|反义词：deliberate shot, well-aimed criticism
