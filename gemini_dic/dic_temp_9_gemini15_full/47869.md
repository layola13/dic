deceives:/dɪˈsiːvz/| vt.|欺骗；蒙蔽；使误信|To cause someone to believe something that is not true.  It involves intentionally misleading someone.|例句：He deceived his friends into believing his story. 他欺骗了他的朋友们，让他们相信了他的故事。
The magician deceived the audience with his clever tricks. 魔术师用他精巧的技巧欺骗了观众。|近义词：mislead, trick, fool, cheat|反义词：reveal, enlighten, inform


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He deceives his clients regularly. = 他经常欺骗他的客户。
She deceives herself about her abilities. = 她对自己能力的认知存在欺骗。

一般过去时:
The salesman deceived me into buying that car. = 销售员骗我买了那辆车。
They deceived the authorities about their intentions. = 他们欺骗了当局关于他们的意图。

现在完成时:
He has deceived many people throughout his career. = 他在他的职业生涯中欺骗了许多人。
She has deceived herself into believing she is invincible. = 她欺骗自己相信自己是无敌的。

过去完成时:
I realized that he had deceived me from the start. = 我意识到他从一开始就欺骗了我。
They discovered that the company had deceived investors for years. = 他们发现该公司多年来一直在欺骗投资者。

将来时:
He will deceive them again if given the opportunity. = 如果有机会，他会再次欺骗他们。
She will likely deceive her parents about her whereabouts. = 她很可能会欺骗她的父母关于她的行踪。

虚拟语气:
If he had not deceived her, she would have trusted him. = 如果他没有欺骗她，她就会信任他。
I wish he hadn't deceived me about his true feelings. = 我希望他没有欺骗我关于他的真实感受。

被动语态:
Many people were deceived by his charming personality. = 许多人被他的迷人性格所欺骗。
The public was deceived by the false advertising campaign. = 公众被虚假的广告宣传所欺骗。

疑问句:

否定句:
He did not deceive anyone intentionally. = 他没有故意欺骗任何人。
She would never deceive her best friend. = 她永远不会欺骗她最好的朋友。
