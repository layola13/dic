manorialization: /ˌmænəriəlɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |庄园化；领主化 | The process by which land becomes organized and administered as a manor, typically involving the consolidation of land under the control of a lord and the establishment of serfdom or other forms of dependent labor.  It refers to the historical process of transforming a region or society into a system dominated by manors and their lords. | Example: The manorialization of England was a gradual process spanning centuries.  英格兰的庄园化是一个历经几个世纪的渐进过程。 | 近义词: feudalization,  enfeoffment | 反义词:  privatization, decentralization
