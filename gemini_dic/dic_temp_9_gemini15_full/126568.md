naevus:/ˈniːvəs/ | n. |痣；胎记 |A naevus is a birthmark or a mole, a usually benign growth on the skin.  It can be present at birth or develop later in life.  Naevi can vary greatly in size, color, and shape. Some are flat, while others are raised.  |例句：The child had a small, dark naevus on her cheek.  这个孩子脸上有一颗小而深的痣。

The doctor examined the suspicious-looking naevus on his back. 医生检查了他背上那颗看起来可疑的痣。


近义词：mole, birthmark, nevus (alternative spelling) | 反义词：无 | 词性：名词
