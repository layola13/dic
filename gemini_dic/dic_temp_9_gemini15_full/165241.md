preinspections: /ˌpriːɪnˈspɛkʃənz/ | n. | 预检；预验；预先检查 |  The act of inspecting something before the main inspection.  It often involves a preliminary assessment to identify potential issues or problems before a more thorough examination.  | 例句：Preinspections of the building revealed several structural flaws that needed to be addressed before the final approval.  预检建筑物发现了一些结构缺陷，需要在最终批准前解决。

The preinspections of the aircraft engines identified a potential malfunction.  飞机引擎的预检发现了一个潜在的故障。| 近义词：preliminary inspections, prior examinations, advance checks | 反义词：final inspections, post-inspections |  名词

词源:
