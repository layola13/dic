inanely:/ɪˈneɪnli/ | adv. |愚蠢地；空洞地；无聊地 | acting in a silly or foolish way; without intelligence or meaning.  |例句：He inanely grinned at the serious situation. 他在严肃的场合傻笑。|例句：She inanely chattered on about nothing in particular. 她空洞地喋喋不休地说着一些无关紧要的事情。|近义词：foolishly, stupidly, senselessly, vacuously |反义词：intelligently, wisely, meaningfully
