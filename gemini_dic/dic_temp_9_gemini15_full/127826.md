nematodes:/ˈnɛmətoʊdz/ | n. |线虫；线形动物 | Nematodes are unsegmented worms with elongated, cylindrical bodies. They are found in various environments, from soil and water to plants and animals.  Many are free-living, but some are parasitic. | 例句：The soil sample contained a high number of nematodes.  土壤样本中含有大量的线虫。 | 例句： Some nematodes are plant parasites causing significant crop damage. 一些线虫是植物寄生虫，会导致严重的作物损害。| 近义词：roundworms, threadworms | 反义词： (无明确反义词，可考虑与其他动物类群对比) | 名词
