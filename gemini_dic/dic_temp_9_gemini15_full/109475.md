leprechaun: /ˈlɛprəˌkɔːn/ | n. |小妖精，爱尔兰民间传说中的妖精 | A leprechaun is a small, mischievous sprite from Irish folklore, often depicted as an old man with a red coat, who is said to guard a pot of gold at the end of a rainbow.  Leprechauns are known for their trickery and their ability to make themselves invisible. | Example:  Many children believe in the existence of leprechauns and their hidden treasure.  许多孩子相信小妖精及其隐藏的宝藏的存在。


| Example: According to legend, if you catch a leprechaun, he will reveal the location of his hidden pot of gold.  根据传说，如果你抓住一个小妖精，他会告诉你他隐藏的金锅的位置。| 近义词：fairy, sprite, elf | 反义词：giant, ogre
