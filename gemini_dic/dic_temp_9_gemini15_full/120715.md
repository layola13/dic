misalleged:/ˌmɪsˈælɪdʒd/| adj.|错误指控的，被错误指控的| wrongly accused or stated; falsely alleged.  The defendant claimed he was misalleged.|例句：The newspaper article misalleged the politician's involvement in the scandal. 报纸文章错误地指控了这位政治家参与了丑闻。

例句：He was misalleged of theft, but later cleared of all charges. 他被错误地指控犯有盗窃罪，但后来被撤销了所有指控。


近义词：falsely accused, wrongly charged, slandered, defamed|反义词：correctly accused, justly charged, vindicated


词源：mis- (错误的) + alleged (被指控的)  The prefix "mis-" indicates a wrong or bad action, while "alleged" means stated to be true, but not yet proven.
