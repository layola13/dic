coresidents:/ˌkoʊˈrezɪdənts/| n.|共同居住者，同事居住者|People who live in the same residence, especially in a shared living arrangement.|例句：The coresidents of the apartment building organized a community garden.  共同居住在这个公寓楼的人们组织了一个社区花园。

例句：The coresidents decided to share the cost of utilities. 共同居住者决定分摊水电费。


近义词：roommates, housemates, fellow residents|反义词：None specifically.  The antonym would depend on the context, for example, someone living alone.
