close-up:/ˈkloʊs ʌp/| n. adj.|特写镜头；近距离的；仔细观察|A close-up is a photograph or film shot taken at close range.  It emphasizes detail.|例句：The movie ended with a dramatic close-up of the actor's face.  这部电影以演员面部的戏剧性特写镜头结尾。  The police gave the crime scene a close-up inspection. 警方对犯罪现场进行了仔细检查。|近义词：closeup, detail, magnification|反义词：long shot, wide shot|名词，形容词
