dingoes:/ˈdɪŋɡoʊz/ | n. |澳洲野犬 | Australian wild dogs, known for their distinctive howl and independent nature. They are considered an apex predator in some Australian ecosystems.  |Example:  Dingoes are found throughout much of Australia. = 澳洲野犬广泛分布在澳大利亚各地。

The dingoes' howling echoed through the night. = 澳洲野犬的嚎叫声回荡在夜空中。|近义词：wild dog (general term) |反义词：domestic dog
