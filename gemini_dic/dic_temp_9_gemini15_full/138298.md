notches:/ˈnɑːtʃɪz/ | n. |刻痕；凹口；缺口 |A notch is a V-shaped cut, indentation, or groove in an edge or surface.  It can also refer to a step or degree in a scale or ranking.|The belt had notches to adjust its size. 这条皮带上有用来调节尺寸的刻痕。|The mountain range had deep notches carved by glaciers. 山脉上有冰川刻出的深深的凹口。|近义词：indentation, groove, nick |反义词：smooth surface
