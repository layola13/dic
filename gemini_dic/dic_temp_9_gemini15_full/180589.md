registrability's:/ˌrɛdʒɪstrəˈbɪlətiz/| n.| 可注册性；注册能力 | The state or quality of being registrable; the capability of being registered.  This refers to the eligibility of a trademark, brand name, or other item to be officially registered.  | Example: The company investigated the registrability of its new logo before launching the product. 公司在推出产品之前调查了其新标志的可注册性。 |近义词：registerability | 反义词：unregistrability
