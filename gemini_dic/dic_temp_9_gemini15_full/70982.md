fireside:/ˈfaɪərsaɪd/| n. |炉边；壁炉边|The area around a fireplace, often associated with warmth, comfort, and family gatherings.  It can refer to the physical space or the feeling of coziness associated with it.|例句：We gathered around the fireside to tell stories. 我们围坐在炉边讲故事。


例句：The fireside was the heart of the home in those days. 在那个年代，炉边是家的中心。


近义词：hearth, fireplace


反义词：None directly, but concepts like "outdoors," "cold," or "uncomfortable" could be considered opposites of the feeling evoked by "fireside."


词性:名词
