go-bag:/ˈɡoʊˌbæɡ/ | n. |逃生包，急救包| A bag containing essential items for use in an emergency, such as a natural disaster or evacuation.  It typically includes water, food, first-aid supplies, and other necessities.| Example:  We keep a go-bag in the closet, ready for any emergency. 我们在壁橱里放着一个逃生包，以备不时之需。|近义词：emergency kit, survival kit |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, standard verb tenses are not applicable.  The usage focuses on the contents and preparation of the go-bag.

Example 1 (Present Simple):  My go-bag contains a flashlight, radio, and extra batteries. 我的逃生包里装有手电筒、收音机和备用电池。

Example 2 (Past Simple):  I packed my go-bag last night before the hurricane warning. 我昨晚在飓风警报发出之前收拾好了我的逃生包。
