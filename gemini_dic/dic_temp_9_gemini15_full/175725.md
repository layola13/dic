rapped:/ræpt/ | v. |  说唱，敲击，厉声斥责 | To speak or recite rhythmically, typically to a musical accompaniment; to strike (something) sharply and repeatedly; to rebuke sharply. | 例句：He rapped about his life in the streets. 他说唱了他街头的生活。 He angrily rapped his fist on the table. 他生气地用拳头猛敲桌子。The teacher rapped the student on the knuckles.老师厉声斥责学生。 | 近义词：recited, knocked, scolded, reprimanded | 反义词：whispered, praised, complimented | 动词
