larch:/lɑːrtʃ/| n. |落叶松|A deciduous coniferous tree of the genus Larix, having needle-like leaves that fall in the autumn.  Larch trees are known for their resilience and adaptability to various climates and soil conditions.|例句：The larch trees turned a stunning golden yellow in the autumn. 秋天落叶松变成了令人惊艳的金黄色。|例句：We planted several larch saplings in the garden last spring. 去年春天我们在花园里种了几棵落叶松树苗。|近义词：conifer|反义词:broadleaf tree|名词
