gadget:/ˈɡædʒɪt/| n. |小工具，巧妙装置 |A gadget is a small tool or device that is useful for a particular task.  It often implies a degree of ingenuity or cleverness in its design. |Example: My new kitchen gadget makes chopping vegetables much easier.  我的新厨房小工具让切菜容易多了。|近义词：device, contraption, tool, gizmo |反义词：无
