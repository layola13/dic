rollability:/ˌroʊləˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可滚动性，易于滚动 | The ability of something to roll easily.  This refers to how easily something can be rolled or moved by rolling.  It often implies a smooth surface and a suitable shape for rolling. | 例句：The rollability of the new tire design is significantly improved. 新轮胎设计的可滚动性得到了显著提高。
The high rollability of the ball bearings ensures smooth operation.  滚珠轴承的高可滚动性确保了平稳运行。| 近义词：mobility, maneuverability | 反义词：immobility, unmaneuverability
