donated:/ˈdoʊneɪtɪd/ | v. | 捐赠；捐献 | to give something, especially money or goods, to a person or an organization that needs them |  例句：Many people donated money to the victims of the earthquake. 许多人向地震灾民捐款。

例句：The company donated computers to the school.  公司向学校捐赠了电脑。


近义词：contributed, gave, offered


反义词：received, kept, withheld


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He donates blood regularly. = 他定期献血。
She donates to charity every year. = 她每年都向慈善机构捐款。

一般过去时:
They donated a large sum of money to the hospital. = 他们向医院捐赠了一大笔钱。
I donated my old clothes to a homeless shelter. = 我把旧衣服捐给了收容所。

现在完成时:
We have donated over $10,000 to the cause. = 我们已经为这项事业捐款超过一万美元。
She has donated her time to volunteering at the animal shelter. = 她把时间捐献给了动物收容所的志愿者工作。

过去完成时:
They had donated all their savings before the appeal ended. = 在募捐活动结束之前，他们已经捐出了所有的积蓄。
He had donated blood several times before the accident. = 在事故发生之前，他已经献过几次血了。

将来时:
I will donate to the Red Cross after I get paid. = 我领到工资后将向红十字会捐款。
They will donate their old furniture to a local charity. = 他们将把旧家具捐给当地一家慈善机构。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would donate to more charities. = 如果我有更多钱，我会向更多的慈善机构捐款。
I wish I could donate more to help the victims. = 我希望我能捐更多钱来帮助受害者。

被动语态:
A large amount of money was donated to the relief effort. = 大笔资金被捐献给了救灾工作。
Thousands of books were donated to the library. = 数千本书被捐赠给了图书馆。

疑问句:
Did you donate to the fundraiser? = 你向募捐活动捐款了吗？
How much did they donate? = 他们捐了多少钱？

否定句:
He didn't donate anything to the campaign. = 他没有向竞选活动捐款。
They didn't donate their time to the project. = 他们没有把时间捐献给这个项目。


PHRASE->

donate organs = 捐献器官

donate money = 捐款
donate to charity = 向慈善机构捐款
