herbarize:/ˈhɜːrbəraɪz/ | vt. |制成腊叶标本；收藏植物标本|To collect and preserve plant specimens, typically by pressing and drying them.|The botanist spent hours herbarizing the newly discovered species. 植物学家花了几个小时制作新发现物种的腊叶标本。|preserve, collect, dry|
