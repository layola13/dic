overlead:/ˈoʊvərliːd/ | vt. |超负荷；过载；|To overload something means to put too much weight or pressure on it, often to the point of causing damage or failure.  It can also refer to giving someone too much work or responsibility.|例句：The circuit overloaded and blew a fuse.  电路超载并烧断了保险丝。|例句：Don't overload the washing machine; it can damage the motor. 不要超载洗衣机；这会损坏马达。|近义词：overburden, overtax, overwhelm |反义词：underload
