practices:/ˈpræktɪsɪz/ | n. |实践；练习；惯例；业务；（医生、律师等的）业务；诊疗；开业 |The habitual or customary performance of an act or acts.  It can refer to the actual doing of something, the methods used, or the established ways of doing something.|例句：Regular practices are essential for success.  经常练习对成功至关重要。|例句：The doctor's practices are located downtown.  这位医生的诊所位于市中心。|近义词：exercises, drills, routines, customs, procedures |反义词：theories, ideals, neglect |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His practices are quite effective. = 他的方法非常有效。
Their daily practices include meditation and yoga. = 他们的日常练习包括冥想和瑜伽。

一般过去时:
Her practices were different from ours. = 她的做法与我们的不同。
The company's practices were investigated by the government. = 该公司的经营行为受到了政府的调查。

现在完成时:
We have changed our business practices. = 我们已经改变了我们的经营方式。
They have abandoned their harmful practices. = 他们已经放弃了有害的习俗。

过去完成时:
Before the accident, his safety practices had been lax. = 事故发生前，他的安全措施一直很松懈。
The company had changed its practices several times before finding success. = 那家公司在找到成功之前已经几次改变了其经营方式。


将来时:
We will adopt new practices next year. = 我们明年将采用新的方法。
They will continue their dishonest practices despite the warnings. = 尽管受到了警告，他们仍将继续其不正当的行为。


虚拟语气:
If they adopted better practices, they would be more successful. = 如果他们采取更好的方法，他们会更成功。
I wish they would change their harmful practices. = 我希望他们能改变他们有害的习俗。


被动语态:
New practices are being implemented by the company. = 公司正在实施新的规章制度。
The old practices were replaced with modern ones. = 旧的规章制度被现代的规章制度取代了。


疑问句:
What are your teaching practices? = 你的教学方法是什么？
What practices are used in your company? = 你们公司采用什么规章制度？


否定句:
Their practices are not sustainable. = 他们的做法不可持续。
The company does not condone unethical practices. = 公司不纵容不道德的行为。
