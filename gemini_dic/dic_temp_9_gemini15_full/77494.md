gazes:/ɡeɪz/| v.|凝视，注视|To look steadily and intently, often with a fixed gaze.|例句：She gazed longingly at the sunset. 她深情地凝视着日落。  例句：He gazed out the window, lost in thought. 他凝视着窗外，陷入沉思。|近义词：stare, contemplate, gaze upon|反义词：ignore, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gazes at the stars every night. = 他每晚都凝视着星星。
She gazes out of the window, lost in thought. = 她凝视着窗外，陷入沉思。

一般过去时:
He gazed at the painting for a long time. = 他长时间地凝视着那幅画。
She gazed at her reflection in the mirror. = 她凝视着镜子里的自己。

现在完成时:
He has gazed at that mountain for hours. = 他已经凝视那座山好几个小时了。
She has gazed at the ocean since morning. = 她从早上起就一直凝视着大海。

过去完成时:
He had gazed at the sunset before he left. = 他离开之前凝视过日落。
She had gazed at the painting many times before she bought it. = 在买下这幅画之前，她已经多次凝视过它了。

将来时:
He will gaze at the moon tonight. = 他今晚将凝视月亮。
She will gaze at the sea from the balcony. = 她将从阳台凝视大海。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would gaze at the stars. = 如果我有时间，我会凝视星星。
I wish I could gaze at the ocean every day. = 我希望我每天都能凝视大海。

被动语态:  (Gaze is rarely used passively)
The painting was gazed at by many people. = 那幅画被许多人凝视。
The sunset was gazed upon by the tourists. = 游客们凝视着日落。

疑问句:
Does he gaze at the sea often? = 他经常凝视大海吗？
What does she gaze at so intently? = 她如此专注地凝视着什么？

否定句:
He didn't gaze at the accident. = 他没有凝视那场事故。
She doesn't gaze at strangers. = 她不会凝视陌生人。


PHRASE->
gaze at = 凝视
gaze upon = 凝视
gaze into = 凝视（通常指深情地凝视）
fixed gaze = 凝视
intense gaze = 锐利的目光
