nonrival:/ˈnɒnˈraɪvəl/| adj.|非竞争的，非排他的|Not diminished in value or utility by being shared or used by others.  This is a key characteristic of public goods.|例句：Clean air is a nonrival good because one person's breathing it doesn't diminish the amount available to others.  清洁的空气是一种非竞争性商品，因为一个人呼吸它不会减少其他人可用的数量。|例句：The knowledge gained from scientific research is often considered a nonrival good. 从科学研究中获得的知识通常被认为是非竞争性商品。|近义词：shared, common, public|反义词：rivalrous, exclusive, scarce
