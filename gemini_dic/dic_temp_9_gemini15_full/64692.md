escapes:/ɪˈskeɪps/ | v. n. |逃跑；逃脱；避免；泄漏 | to avoid or get free from a dangerous, unpleasant, or restricting situation or place. |例句：The prisoner escaped from jail. 犯人越狱了。
The balloon escaped into the sky. 气球飞上了天。|近义词： flee, evade, avoid |反义词： capture, apprehend, remain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He escapes the police regularly. 他经常逃脱警方的追捕。
The gas escapes from the pipe.  煤气从管道泄漏。


一般过去时:
She escaped from the burning building. 她从燃烧的建筑物中逃了出来。
The secret escaped his lips. 秘密从他口中泄露了。


现在完成时:
He has escaped prison three times. 他已经三次越狱了。
The news has escaped to the press. 消息已经泄露给了媒体。


过去完成时:
They had escaped before the police arrived. 警察到达之前，他们已经逃跑了。
The information had escaped my memory.  这些信息我已经忘记了。


将来时:
He will escape punishment. 他将逃脱惩罚。
The truth will eventually escape. 真相最终会大白。


虚拟语气:
If he had escaped, he would have been free. 如果他逃脱了，他就自由了。
I wish I could escape this feeling. 我希望我能摆脱这种感觉。


被动语态:
The prisoner was escaped from jail. 犯人越狱了。(被动语态强调越狱成功)
The secret was escaped to the media. 秘密泄露给了媒体。(被动语态更强调秘密被泄露)


疑问句:
Did he escape? 他逃脱了吗？
How did they escape? 他们是如何逃脱的？


否定句:
He didn't escape the consequences. 他没有逃避后果。
She couldn't escape her feelings. 她无法逃避自己的感情。
