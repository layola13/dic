cartwheeler:/ˈkɑːrtˌwiːlər/ | n. |翻滚者，侧手翻者 | A person who performs cartwheels.  Someone skilled in performing cartwheels. |例句：The gymnast was a skilled cartwheeler, effortlessly executing multiple cartwheels in a row.  体操运动员是一位熟练的侧手翻者，轻松自如地连续完成多个侧手翻。

The little girl, a natural cartwheeler, delighted everyone with her performance.  那个小女孩天生就会侧手翻，她的表演让每个人都非常高兴。


近义词：tumbler, acrobat (in the context of performing cartwheels) | 反义词：None specifically, but words implying clumsiness or lack of skill could be considered antonyms in context.


词源:  The word is a compound word.  "Cartwheel" refers to the acrobatic movement resembling a wheel, and "-er" is a suffix forming nouns that denote agents or doers of an action.  The origin of "cartwheel" itself is debated, with some suggesting a resemblance to the movement of a wheelbarrow, while others point to the spinning action resembling a wheel.
