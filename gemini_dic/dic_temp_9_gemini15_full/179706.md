reenthroned:/ˌriːɪnˈθroʊnd/| v.|重新加冕；复位；恢复王位|To restore to a throne or position of power; to reinstate.|例句：The deposed king was reenthroned after the revolution was quelled.  革命平息后，被废黜的国王重新登上了王位。

例句：After a period of exile, the rightful heir was reenthroned.  流亡一段时间后，合法继承人复位了。
