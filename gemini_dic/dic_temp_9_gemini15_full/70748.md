fined:/faɪnd/| vt.|罚款；使精细；澄清| to impose a penalty of a sum of money as punishment for an offense.  The verb "fine" can also mean to make something more refined or pure, or to clarify something. |例句：The driver was fined for speeding. 司机因超速被罚款。

The judge fined him $500 for contempt of court.  法官因他藐视法庭而罚款500美元。


近义词：penalty, sanction, mulct| 反义词：reward, compensate

词性：动词
