disableness:/dɪsˈeɪbəlnəs/ | n. |残疾；无能；无力 |The state of being disabled; lacking the ability to do something.  It encompasses physical, mental, cognitive, or sensory impairments that substantially limit one or more major life activities. |例句：His disableness prevented him from participating in the marathon.  他的残疾使他无法参加马拉松比赛。

例句：The disableness of the equipment delayed the project. 设备的故障延误了项目。


近义词：disability, incapacity, impairment, handicap |反义词：ability, capability, competence


词源：dis- (否定前缀) + able (能够的) + -ness (名词后缀)  The word evolved from the concept of "not being able," signifying a lack of ability or capacity.
