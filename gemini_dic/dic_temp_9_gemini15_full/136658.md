nonseasonable:/ˌnɒnˈsiːzənəbəl/ | adj. |不合季节的，不适时的|Out of keeping with the season; untimely.  It refers to something that is inappropriate or unusual for a particular time of year, or for a particular occasion.|例句：The warm weather in December was nonseasonable.  十二月温暖的天气是不合时宜的。
The nonseasonable rain ruined the harvest.  不合时宜的雨水毁了收成。|近义词：untimely, inappropriate, untimely, unsuitable|反义词：seasonal, timely, appropriate
