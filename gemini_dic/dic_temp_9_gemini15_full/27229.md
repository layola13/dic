calmed:/kɑːmd/| adj.|平静的；镇静的|The verb "calm" means to make or become quiet and peaceful.  The adjective "calmed" describes a state of being quiet and peaceful, often after a period of agitation or stress. |例句：The storm calmed, and the sea became tranquil.  暴风雨平静下来了，大海变得平静。

The children were calmed by their mother's soothing voice. 孩子们被母亲温柔的声音安抚了。|近义词：peaceful, tranquil, serene, composed |反义词：agitated, disturbed, frantic, stressed
