multi-tiered: /ˌmʌltiˈtɪərd/ | adj. | 多层的；多层次的 | Having or involving several levels or layers.  It describes a system, structure, or organization with multiple levels of hierarchy or complexity.  |例句：The company uses a multi-tiered marketing strategy to reach a wider audience. 这家公司采用多层次的营销策略来接触更广泛的受众。

例句：The cake was multi-tiered, with each layer a different flavor. 这个蛋糕是多层的，每一层都有不同的口味。


近义词：layered, stratified, multilevel, hierarchical | 反义词：single-tiered, single-level, flat
