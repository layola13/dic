photophone:/ˈfoʊtoʊfoʊn/|/ˈfəʊtəʊfəʊn/| n. |光电电话；光声电话|A device that transmits speech by means of light.  It uses a beam of light, modulated by sound waves, to transmit speech over long distances.  This was an early form of optical communication.  Alexander Graham Bell invented a version in 1880.|The photophone, invented by Alexander Graham Bell, was a remarkable feat of engineering for its time.|近义词：optical telephone|反义词：None
