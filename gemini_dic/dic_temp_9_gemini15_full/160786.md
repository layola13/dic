positively:/ˈpɒzətɪvli/ /ˈpɑːzətɪvli/ | adv. |肯定地；积极地；绝对地 |  In a positive manner; definitely; to a great extent.  It expresses certainty, enthusiasm, or a strong degree. |

例句：

He positively glowed with pride. 他骄傲得容光焕发。

She positively refused to go. 她坚决拒绝去。


近义词：certainly, definitely, absolutely, emphatically, assuredly


反义词：negatively, doubtfully, uncertainly


词性: 副词
