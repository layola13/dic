dedicators:/ˈdɛdɪkeɪtərz/| n. |题词者；献词者；奉献者|Those who write dedications in books or other works.  They are individuals or groups who formally dedicate something, such as a book, building, or monument, to a person, cause, or event. |例句：The dedicators of the new library were honored at a special ceremony. 新图书馆的题词者们在一个特别的仪式上受到了表彰。

The dedicators of this monument are unknown. 这座纪念碑的题词者们不为人知。


近义词：donors, contributors, patrons|反义词：recipients, beneficiaries|词性：noun
