psychoanalysed:/ˌsaɪkoʊˈænəlaɪzd/ | v. |精神分析的 | To analyze (someone's behavior or thoughts) using the methods of psychoanalysis.  |例句：The therapist psychoanalysed the patient's dreams to understand their subconscious anxieties.  治疗师对病人的梦进行了精神分析，以了解他们的潜意识焦虑。|近义词：analyzed, interpreted, explored |反义词：ignored, overlooked


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The therapist psychoanalyses her patients' dreams. = 治疗师分析她病人的梦。
He psychoanalyses the behaviour of famous figures. = 他分析名人的行为。


一般过去时:
The doctor psychoanalysed my childhood experiences. = 医生分析了我童年的经历。
Freud psychoanalysed many patients during his career. = 弗洛伊德在他职业生涯中分析了许多病人。


现在完成时:
