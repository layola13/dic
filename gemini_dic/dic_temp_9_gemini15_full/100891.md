intuited:/ɪnˈtjuːɪtɪd/| v.|直觉地知道，凭直觉领悟|To understand something instinctively, without the need for conscious reasoning.|She intuited that something was wrong.|近义词：perceived, sensed, grasped|反义词：ignored, overlooked


USAGE->

I intuit his intentions. = 我直觉地知道他的意图。
She intuits the solution to the problem. = 她凭直觉找到了问题的解决方法。
