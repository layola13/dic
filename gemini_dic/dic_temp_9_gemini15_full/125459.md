multiplication:/ˌmʌltɪplɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |乘法；倍增；繁殖|The process of adding a number to itself a certain number of times.  It is a fundamental arithmetic operation. |Example:  The multiplication of 5 and 3 equals 15.  5乘以3等于15。|近义词：multiplication, increase, expansion |反义词：division
