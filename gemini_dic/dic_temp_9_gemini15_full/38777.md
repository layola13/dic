conducive:/kənˈduːsɪv/ | adj. | 有利的；促进的；有益的 |  Conducive describes something that creates an environment or situation likely to result in a positive outcome. It suggests that something makes it easier or more likely for something else to happen or be achieved.  |  A quiet environment is conducive to studying.  安静的环境有利于学习。
