crunch's:/krʌntʃ/ | n. |嘎吱声；紧要关头；艰难时期 |  a sound like something being crushed or chewed; a period of difficulty or crisis |The crunch of gravel under his boots.  他的靴子踩在碎石上发出嘎吱声。  |  The company is facing a financial crunch. 这家公司正面临金融危机。| crackle, grind | boom, prosperity | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The leaves crunch underfoot. = 树叶在脚下发出嘎吱声。
The machine crunches data efficiently. = 机器高效地处理数据。

一般过去时:
The ice crunched under his weight. = 冰在他体重下发出嘎吱声。
She crunched the numbers to find the answer. = 她计算数据以找到答案。

现在完成时:
The economy has been crunching for months. = 经济已经萎靡了几个月。
He has crunched all the data for the report. = 他已经完成了报告的所有数据处理。

过去完成时:
The bear had crunched the bones before we arrived. = 在我们到达之前，熊已经啃碎了骨头。
They had crunched the numbers before making the decision. = 他们在做决定之前已经计算过数据了。


将来时:
The market will crunch under pressure. = 市场将在压力下崩溃。
She will crunch the data next week. = 她下周将处理数据。


虚拟语气:
If the economy crunched, many people would lose their jobs. = 如果经济崩溃，许多人会失业。
I wish the market hadn't crunched so badly last year. = 我希望去年市场没有那么糟糕地崩溃。


被动语态:
The data is crunched by the computer. = 数据由计算机处理。
The bones were crunched by the animal. = 骨头被动物啃碎了。


疑问句:
Does the snow crunch under your feet? = 雪在你脚下嘎吱作响吗？
Will the deal crunch under the weight of negotiations? = 这笔交易会在谈判的压力下破裂吗？


否定句:
The ice didn't crunch under her skates. = 冰在她滑冰鞋下没有发出嘎吱声。
The project didn't crunch under pressure this time. = 项目这次没有在压力下崩溃。
