retractability:/ˌriːtrækˈtæbɪləti/| n. |可收回性，可撤回性 |The ability to be retracted or withdrawn.  This refers to the capability of something to be pulled back, taken back, or reversed.  It can apply to physical objects, statements, or agreements. |例句：The retractability of the landing gear is crucial for a safe landing.  起落架的可收回性对于安全着陆至关重要。


The high retractability of the statement allowed for damage control.  声明的可撤回性使得损害控制成为可能。|近义词：revocability, withdrawability |反义词：irrevocability, finality
