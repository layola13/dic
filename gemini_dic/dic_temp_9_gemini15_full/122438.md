mobber:/ˈmɒbə(r)/| n. |暴徒；攻击者；抢劫者|A mobber is a member of a mob, typically involved in violence, rioting, or looting.  They are often part of a larger group acting aggressively and unlawfully.|例句：The mobber threw stones at the police officers.  暴徒向警察扔石头。|例句：A group of mobber ransacked the store. 一群暴徒洗劫了商店。|近义词：rioter, thug, hooligan, vandal|反义词：peacemaker, protector
