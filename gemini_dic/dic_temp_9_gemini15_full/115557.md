maqui:/ˈmɑːki/ | n. |玛奇浆果；玛奇树 | A small evergreen shrub native to Chile, known for its dark purple berries, rich in antioxidants.  The berries are used to make juice, jams, and other products. | Example:  The maqui berry is gaining popularity due to its purported health benefits. 玛奇浆果因其声称的健康益处而越来越受欢迎。|近义词: Chilean wine berry ( less common)|反义词: N/A
