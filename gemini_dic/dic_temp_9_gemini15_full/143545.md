outhammering: /ˌaʊtˈhæmərɪŋ/ | v. | 超过…的敲击声；胜过；压倒 |  The act of exceeding someone or something in hammering or striking; to surpass or overwhelm.  |  例句：Their relentless marketing outhammered the competition. 他们不懈的营销策略胜过了竞争对手。  | 近义词：outshine, outdo, surpass, overwhelm | 反义词：underwhelm, be outdone


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The blacksmith outhammers the apprentice in skill. = 这位铁匠的技巧胜过学徒。
Their constant noise outhammers the peace and quiet. = 他们不断的噪音盖过了宁静。


一般过去时:
His powerful argument outhammered his opponent's. = 他有力的论点胜过了对手的论点。
The storm outhammered the fragile roof. = 暴风雨击垮了脆弱的屋顶。


现在完成时:
The company has outhammered its rivals in sales this year. = 今年这家公司在销售额上已超过了竞争对手。
His consistent effort has outhammered his initial setbacks. = 他坚持不懈的努力已经克服了最初的挫折。


过去完成时:
The seasoned debater had outhammered his novice opponent before the final round. = 经验丰富的辩手在决赛前就已经胜过了他的新手对手。
The heavy rain had outhammered the city's drainage system. = 大雨已经超过了城市排水系统的处理能力。


将来时:
Their superior technology will outhammer the competition in the marketplace. = 他们的先进技术将在市场上胜过竞争对手。

虚拟语气:

If only she had spoken up, her voice might have outhammered the doubters. = 如果她能大胆地说出来，她的声音或许就能盖过那些怀疑者。


被动语态:

疑问句:
