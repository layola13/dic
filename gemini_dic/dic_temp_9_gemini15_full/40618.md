convenience foods:/kənˈviːniəns fuːdz/ | n. | 方便食品 | Pre-prepared or processed foods that are easy and quick to prepare.  These foods often require minimal cooking or preparation time and are designed to save consumers time and effort.  Examples include frozen dinners, canned soups, and microwaveable meals. |  例句：Convenience foods like frozen pizzas are popular among busy professionals. 方便食品，例如冷冻披萨，在忙碌的专业人士中很受欢迎。

例句：Many convenience foods are high in sodium and unhealthy fats. 许多方便食品钠含量高且不健康脂肪含量高。


近义词：prepared foods, processed foods, ready-to-eat meals, fast foods

反义词：homemade food, home-cooked meals, fresh food


词性:名词
