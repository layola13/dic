patriarchy:/ˈpeɪtriɑːrki/| n. |父权制；家长统治；父系社会|A social system in which men hold primary power and predominate in roles of political leadership, moral authority, social privilege, and control of property.  It is a system of social structures and practices in which men dominate, oppress, and exploit women.|例句：Many feminists argue that patriarchy is the root cause of gender inequality.  许多女权主义者认为父权制是性别不平等的根本原因。|例句：The company's culture was deeply rooted in patriarchy, with few women in senior positions. 这家公司的文化根深蒂固地植根于父权制，高级职位上几乎没有女性。|近义词：male dominance, paternalism|反义词：matriarchy, egalitarianism
