popping:/ˈpɒpɪŋ/ /ˈpɑːpɪŋ/| v. n. adj.|砰的一声；突然出现；流行；(食物)爆裂声|  To make or cause to make a short, sharp explosive sound; to suddenly appear; to be very popular; (of food) making a popping sound. |例句：I heard a popping sound. 我听到一声爆裂声。  The balloon popped. 气球爆了。|近义词：bursting, exploding, cracking, popular, trendy |反义词：quiet, silent, unpopular


USAGE->
