get the jump on: /ɡɛt ðə dʒʌmp ɑn/ | phrasal verb |抢先;先发制人;占先机 | To gain an advantage over someone or something by acting before them.  |例句：We got the jump on our competitors by launching our product earlier. 我们抢先于竞争对手，提前推出产品。

例句：The police got the jump on the robbers, arresting them before they could escape.  警方抢先一步抓获了劫匪，阻止了他们的逃脱。


近义词：preempt, anticipate, outmaneuver,  get ahead of

反义词：be outmaneuvered, be caught off guard, be behind


PHRASE->
get the jump on someone = 抢在某人前面
get the jump on a project = 先下手为强，抢先完成项目
get the jump on the market = 抢占市场先机
get the jump on the competition = 抢先竞争对手
jump the gun = 操之过急


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They get the jump on their rivals by investing early. = 他们通过提前投资抢先于对手。
We get the jump on the competition with our innovative marketing. = 我们凭借创新的营销策略抢先于竞争对手。


一般过去时:
She got the jump on the other applicants by submitting her application first. = 她通过最先提交申请抢先于其他申请者。
The team got the jump on the deadline by working extra hours. =  团队通过加班提前完成了最后期限。


现在完成时:
They have gotten the jump on the new technology, securing a market advantage. = 他们已经掌握了新技术，从而获得了市场优势。
We have gotten the jump on the problem before it escalated. = 我们在问题升级之前就抢先解决了它。


过去完成时:
He had gotten the jump on the news, spreading the information before anyone else. = 他抢先得到了消息，在其他人之前传播了信息。
The company had gotten the jump on the market trend, capitalizing on the opportunity. = 公司抢先把握住了市场趋势，并利用了这个机会。


将来时:
They will get the jump on the opposition by releasing their product next month. = 他们将于下个月发布产品，从而抢先于对手。
We will get the jump on the project by starting the planning phase early. = 我们将通过提前开始计划阶段来抢先完成项目。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:
