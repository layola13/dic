montem:/ˈmɒntɛm/ | n. |蒙特姆节(牛津大学的传统节日) |A ceremony and procession at Oxford University, especially associated with the expulsion of a student(s) by the undergraduates.  Historically, it involved a mock battle and was a significant event in the university calendar.  Modern celebrations are considerably less boisterous. |例句：The students participated enthusiastically in the annual Montem procession.  = 学生们热情地参加了年度蒙特姆游行。

The Montem celebration was a highlight of the academic year. = 蒙特姆庆祝活动是学年的一大亮点。|近义词：ceremony, festival, procession|反义词：N/A
