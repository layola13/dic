inculcators: /ɪnˈkʌlkˌeɪtərz/ | n. |灌输者；教唆者 |Those who instill or implant beliefs, principles, or habits.  People or things that persistently introduce ideas or behaviors. |例句：The teachers acted as inculcators of good moral values.  老师们充当了良好道德价值观的灌输者。

The strict religious upbringing made the parents powerful inculcators of faith.  严格的宗教教育使父母成为强大的信仰灌输者。


近义词：implanters, instillers, indoctrinators, teachers, trainers |反义词：eradicator, destroyer, un-teachers (informal)


词性: 名词
