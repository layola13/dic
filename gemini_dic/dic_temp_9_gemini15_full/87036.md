hepatocystic: /ˌhepətoʊˈsɪstɪk/ | adj. |肝胆的，肝囊的| Relating to both the liver and the gallbladder.  The term combines "hepato-" (liver) and "-cystic" (gallbladder or cyst).| The hepatocystic duct is a short tube connecting the cystic duct to the common hepatic duct.|近义词：biliary (referring to bile), cholecystohepatic|反义词：None specifically, but terms relating to other organs could be considered antonyms in context.
