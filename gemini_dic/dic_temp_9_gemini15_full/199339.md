similarly:/ˈsɪmɪləli/| adv.|同样地，类似地|In a similar way; likewise.  It expresses a resemblance or correspondence between two things. |例句：He behaved similarly to his brother. 他和他的兄弟行为相似。|例句：Similarly, we need to consider the environmental impact. 同样地，我们需要考虑环境影响。|近义词：likewise, equally, in the same way|反义词：differently, conversely


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He acts similarly to his father. = 他行为举止像他父亲。
They dress similarly every day. = 他们每天穿著打扮都很相似。

一般过去时:
She responded similarly to the previous question. = 她对前一个问题的回答也类似。
They behaved similarly during the crisis. = 他们在危机期间表现得也很相似。

现在完成时:
We have approached the problem similarly on multiple occasions. = 我们多次以类似的方式处理这个问题。
They have reacted similarly in all past situations. = 在所有过去的情况中，他们的反应都类似。

过去完成时:
They had acted similarly in the past. = 过去他们也曾有过类似的行为。
She had responded similarly to the earlier requests. = 她对之前的请求也有过类似的回应。

将来时:
He will likely act similarly in the future. = 他将来很可能会采取类似的行动。
They will probably respond similarly to the new policy. = 他们可能会对新政策做出类似的反应。

虚拟语气:
If he were in the same situation, he would act similarly. = 如果他处于同样的境地，他会采取类似的行动。
I wish she would have responded similarly. = 我希望她能给出类似的回应。

被动语态:
The two cases were treated similarly by the court. = 法院对这两个案件的处理方式类似。
The issues were addressed similarly in both reports. = 两份报告都以类似的方式处理了这些问题。

疑问句:
Did they handle the situation similarly? = 他们处理情况的方式相似吗？
Would they react similarly in the future? = 他们将来会做出类似的反应吗？

否定句:
They did not respond similarly to the complaints. = 他们对投诉的回应并不相同。
He did not handle the situation similarly to his colleague. = 他处理情况的方式与他的同事不同。


PHRASE->
similarly to = 与…相似
similarly important = 同样重要
similarly situated = 处境相似
act similarly = 行为相似
think similarly = 想法相似
