desperates: /ˈdɛspərɪts/ | adj. |绝望的，拼命的|extremely desperate; showing or feeling despair.|例句：She made a desperate attempt to save her drowning child. 她拼命试图救她溺水的孩子。|近义词：hopeless, frantic, urgent|反义词：calm, peaceful, hopeful|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are desperate for money. = 他们急需钱。
He is desperate to find a job. = 他急于找到一份工作。


一般过去时:
She was desperate after losing her job. = 她失业后非常绝望。
They were desperate to escape the burning building. = 他们拼命想逃离燃烧的建筑物。


现在完成时:
I have been desperate for a solution to this problem. = 我一直在苦苦寻找这个问题的解决方案。
They have become desperate because of the drought. = 由于干旱，他们已经绝望了。


过去完成时:
He had been desperate for help before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，他已经绝望地需要帮助。
She had become desperate after waiting for hours. = 等了几个小时后，她变得绝望了。


将来时:
They will be desperate if they don't find a new supplier. = 如果他们找不到新的供应商，他们将会绝望。
We will be desperate for water if the rain doesn't come soon. = 如果雨水不尽快到来，我们将缺水。


虚拟语气:
If I were desperate, I would try anything. = 如果我绝望了，我会尝试任何事情。
I wish I weren't so desperate for approval. = 我希望我不那么渴望得到认可。


被动语态:
The situation was made desperate by the lack of resources. = 资源匮乏使情况变得绝望。
The people were driven desperate by the famine. = 饥荒使人们陷入绝望。


疑问句:
Are they desperate for help? = 他们很绝望地需要帮助吗？
Is he desperate to leave? = 他很绝望地想要离开吗？


否定句:
They are not desperate yet. = 他们还没有绝望。
He is not desperate for attention. = 他并不渴望得到关注。


PHRASE->
