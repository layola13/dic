predesigning:/ˌpriːdɪˈzaɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 预先设计；事前设计 | The act of planning or designing something in advance.  It often involves anticipating future needs and challenges to create a more robust and effective final design. |例句：Predesigning the software architecture saved them significant time during development. = 预先设计软件架构为他们在开发过程中节省了大量时间。

例句：The architects spent months predesigning the building's structural elements to ensure stability. = 建筑师们花了几个月的时间预先设计建筑物的结构元素以确保稳定性。


近义词：preplanning, pre-engineering, preliminary design


反义词：retrofitting, redesigning


词性：动词
