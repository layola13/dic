riffled:/ˈrɪf(ə)ld/| v.| 快速翻动；翻阅；弄乱；（头发）蓬松 |To turn over and over quickly; to shuffle (cards); to rumple or disarrange; to make (hair) fluffy.|例句：He riffled through the papers on his desk. 他快速翻阅了他桌上的文件。
He riffled the cards before dealing. 他洗牌前快速翻动牌。|近义词：shuffle, rustle, rummage |反义词：arrange, smooth, straighten|
