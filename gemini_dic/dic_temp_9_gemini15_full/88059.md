highhats:/ˈhaɪhæt/| n.|高音钹|A high-hat is a type of cymbal in a drum kit, typically consisting of two cymbals mounted on a stand that allows the drummer to control their position and sound.  They are played with a pedal that opens and closes the cymbals, creating a characteristic 'chick' sound when closed and a more sustained 'crash' when open.|例句：The drummer's precise high-hat work provided a steady beat for the song.  鼓手的精准的高音钹演奏为歌曲提供了稳定的节奏。

The drummer used the high-hat to accentuate the off-beats. 鼓手用高音钹来强调弱拍。|近义词：cymbals, ride cymbal|反义词:None
