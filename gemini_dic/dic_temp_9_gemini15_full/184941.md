retargeting:/ˌriːˈtɑːɡɪtɪŋ/| n. |重新定位，再定向营销|The process of showing ads to people who have previously interacted with your website or brand but didn't complete a desired action (e.g., purchase).  It involves using data to identify and re-engage these users with tailored messaging. |Example:  The company used retargeting ads on social media to remind potential customers about products they had viewed.  该公司使用社交媒体上的再营销广告来提醒潜在客户他们查看过的产品。|近义词：remarketing, re-engagement|反义词：initial targeting, first-time targeting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We retarget our website visitors who abandoned their shopping carts. = 我们对放弃购物车访问我们网站的访客进行重新定位。
The marketing team retargets users based on their online behavior. = 营销团队根据用户的在线行为对用户进行重新定位。

一般过去时:
They retargeted their previous customers with a special offer. = 他们向之前的客户重新定位，提供了一个特价优惠。
The campaign retargeted users who had shown interest in a specific product. = 该活动重新定位了对特定产品感兴趣的用户。

现在完成时:
We have retargeted thousands of users this month. = 这个月我们已经对数千用户进行了重新定位。
The company has retargeted its audience with great success. = 公司已经成功地对其受众进行了重新定位。

过去完成时:
They had retargeted the audience before launching the new product. = 在推出新产品之前，他们已经对受众进行了重新定位。
The marketing team had retargeted the users who had visited the landing page. = 营销团队已经对访问过登录页面的用户进行了重新定位。

将来时:
We will retarget our inactive subscribers next week. = 我们下周将对不活跃的订阅者进行重新定位。
The company will retarget its audience using a new algorithm. = 公司将使用新的算法对其受众进行重新定位。

虚拟语气:
If we had more budget, we would retarget a wider audience. = 如果我们有更多预算，我们会对更广泛的受众进行重新定位。
It is important that we retarget users who showed interest but didn't convert. = 重要的是，我们要重新定位那些表现出兴趣但没有转化的用户。
