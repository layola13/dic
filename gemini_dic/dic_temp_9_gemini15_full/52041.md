dialight:/ˈdaɪəlaɪt/| n.|指示灯，信号灯|A dialight is a small lamp, often used as an indicator light on a control panel or instrument.  It is typically illuminated to signal a specific status or condition.|例句：The dialight on the machine flashed red, indicating an error.  机器上的指示灯闪烁着红色，表示有错误。 The dialight on the router indicated that the internet connection was active. 路由器上的指示灯显示互联网连接已激活。|近义词：indicator light, pilot light|反义词：None.
