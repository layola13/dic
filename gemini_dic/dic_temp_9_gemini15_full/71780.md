flecked:/flekt/| adj.| 有斑点的；带有杂色的 |Marked with small spots or flecks of a different color or substance.|The granite countertop was flecked with black and white.|近义词：speckled, spotted, mottled|反义词：unblemished, plain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fabric is flecked with gold. = 这块布料带有金色的斑点。
Her hair is flecked with grey. = 她的头发夹杂着灰色。

一般过去时:
The painter flecked the canvas with crimson. = 画家在画布上点缀了胭脂红。
The sky was flecked with pink at sunset. = 日落时分，天空布满了粉色斑点。

现在完成时:

The wall has been flecked with paint. = 墙壁上溅上了颜料。

过去完成时:

The dress had been flecked with mud after the fall. = 她摔倒后，裙子上溅上了泥巴。
