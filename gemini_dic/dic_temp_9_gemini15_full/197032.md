shallop:/ˈʃæləp/| n.|小船，轻舟|A small, light sailboat or rowboat, often with a single mast and sail.  |例句：The fishermen used a small shallop to navigate the shallow waters. 渔民们用一艘小轻舟在浅水区航行。

例句：A tiny shallop bobbed gently on the waves. 一艘小小的轻舟在波浪上轻轻地颠簸。


近义词：boat, dinghy, skiff|反义词：ship, galleon
