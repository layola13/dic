fakirs:/ˈfækɪrz/| n. |苦行僧，瑜伽行者|A fakir is a Muslim or Hindu religious ascetic who practices extreme self-denial and often performs feats of endurance or apparent magic.  They are often associated with India and other parts of South Asia.|例句：The fakir sat cross-legged, seemingly oblivious to the scorching sun.  这位苦行僧盘腿而坐，似乎对灼热的阳光毫不在意。


The fakir amazed the crowd with his seemingly impossible feats of strength.  这位苦行僧以他看似不可能完成的壮举震惊了人群。|近义词：ascetic, hermit, yogi|反义词：hedonist, materialist
