ceratohyal:/ˌsɛrətoʊˈhaɪəl/| n. |角舌骨|The ceratohyal is a paired cartilage or bone in the hyoid apparatus of many vertebrates, contributing to the support of the tongue and throat.  It is located posterior to the epihyal and anterior to the hypohyal. The specific morphology and function of the ceratohyal can vary significantly across different species.|The ceratohyal bone in this fish species is particularly robust.|近义词:hyal,  branchial element|反义词:None
