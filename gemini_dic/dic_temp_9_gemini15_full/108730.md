leaped:/liːpt/ | v. |跳跃；猛增；突然行动|To move a long distance through the air by jumping.  It can also describe a sudden increase or action.|例句：The cat leaped onto the fence.  那只猫跳到了围栏上。

例句： The company's profits leaped dramatically last quarter.  公司上季度的利润大幅增长。


近义词：jumped, sprang, bounded| 反义词：faltered, stumbled

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He leaps over the hurdles. = 他跳过障碍物。
She leaps to conclusions. = 她总是轻易下结论。


一般过去时:
The frog leaped into the pond. = 青蛙跳进了池塘。
He leaped to his feet. = 他猛地站了起来。


现在完成时:
The rabbit has leaped across the road. = 兔子已经跳过了马路。
The price of oil has leaped significantly. = 油价大幅上涨。


过去完成时:
The athlete had leaped before the starting gun fired. = 运动员在发令枪响之前就已经跳了出去。
She had leaped to the wrong conclusion. = 她之前就得出了错误的结论。


将来时:
He will leap into action when the time comes. = 到时候他会立即行动。
The stock price is expected to leap next week. = 股价预计下周会猛涨。


虚拟语气:
If he had leaped higher, he would have won the competition. = 如果他跳得更高，他就会赢得比赛。
I wish I had leaped at the opportunity. = 我真希望我抓住了那个机会。


被动语态:
The hurdle was leaped with ease. = 障碍物很容易就被跳过去了。
The conclusion was leaped to too quickly. = 结论下得太仓促了。


疑问句:
Did he leap over the fence? = 他跳过围栏了吗？
How far did the frog leap? = 青蛙跳了多远？


否定句:
He did not leap to his feet. = 他没有猛地站起来。
The horse didn't leap the obstacle. = 马没有跳过障碍物。

词源:
The word "leaped" originates from the Old English word "lēopan," meaning "to run, to leap."  It's related to other Germanic words with similar meanings.
