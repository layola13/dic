nondenominationalism: /ˌnɒnˌdɪˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃənəlɪzəm/ | n. |非宗派主义；无教派主义 | The belief and practice of not being affiliated with any particular religious denomination.  It often involves a more inclusive and ecumenical approach to faith, emphasizing shared spiritual values over specific doctrinal differences. | Example:  The school adopted a policy of nondenominationalism to accommodate students from diverse religious backgrounds. 这所学校采取了非宗派主义的政策，以适应来自不同宗教背景的学生。  Many nondenominational churches emphasize community and shared faith experiences over strict adherence to theological dogma. 许多非宗派教会更强调社区和共同的信仰体验，而不是严格遵守神学教条。 |近义词: ecumenism, interfaith, inclusivity |反义词: sectarianism, denominationalism
