governorships:/ˈɡʌvənərʃɪps/ | n. | 州长职位；总督职位 | The term "governorships" refers to the office or position of a governor, the head of a state or territory.  It can also refer to the period of time someone holds this office. | Example:  The upcoming elections will determine the governorships of several key states.  即将到来的选举将决定几个关键州的州长职位。

The governorships in the southern states are highly contested. 南方各州的州长职位竞争激烈。


近义词：governorship (单数形式),  administration,  rule


反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑与“absence of leadership” 或 “anarchy”  对比


词性：名词
