differentiate:/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃieɪt/| vt. |区别，区分；使…不同；[数]求…的微分|To recognize or ascertain what makes (someone or something) different.|The scientist differentiated between the two types of bacteria based on their genetic makeup. 科学家根据这两种细菌的基因构成区分它们。|distinguish, discriminate, discern|assimilate, homogenize|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Scientists differentiate between various types of cells. = 科学家区分各种类型的细胞。
He differentiates himself from his competitors through innovation. = 他通过创新将自己与竞争对手区分开来。


一般过去时:
She differentiated the twins by their clothing. = 她通过衣服区分了双胞胎。
The teacher differentiated the lesson to suit different learning styles. = 老师对课程进行了区分以适应不同的学习风格。


现在完成时:
Researchers have differentiated several new strains of the virus. = 研究人员已经区分了几种新的病毒株。
We have differentiated our products to appeal to a wider market. = 我们已经区分了我们的产品以吸引更广泛的市场。


过去完成时:
The doctor had differentiated the symptoms before making a diagnosis. = 医生在做出诊断之前已经区分了症状。
They had differentiated their approach based on previous failures. = 他们根据之前的失败区分了他们的方法。


将来时:
The company will differentiate its products with new packaging. = 公司将通过新的包装来区分其产品。
She will differentiate her teaching style to cater to diverse students. = 她将区分她的教学风格以迎合不同的学生。


虚拟语气:
If I could differentiate the sounds, I could understand them better. = 如果我能区分这些声音，我就能更好地理解它们。
I wish I could differentiate between fact and fiction more easily. = 我希望我能更容易地区分事实和虚构。


被动语态:
The two species are easily differentiated by their size. = 这两个物种很容易通过它们的大小来区分。
The tasks were differentiated to ensure fairness. = 任务被区分开来以确保公平。


疑问句:
How do you differentiate between right and wrong? = 你如何区分对与错？
Can you differentiate the various shades of color? = 你能区分各种色调吗？


否定句:
I cannot differentiate the two melodies. = 我无法区分这两首旋律。
He failed to differentiate between cause and effect. = 他未能区分因果关系。



PHRASE->
differentiate between = 区分……和……
differentiate from = 将……与……区分开来
differentiate itself = 使自身与众不同
differentiate according to = 根据……区分
differentiate on the basis of = 基于……区分
