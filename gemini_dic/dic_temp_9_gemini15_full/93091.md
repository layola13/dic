idyllian:/ɪˈdɪliən/| adj.| 田园诗般的；理想化的；宁静的 |  Characterized by idyllic qualities; charmingly simple and serene; suggesting an idealized vision of rural life.  |例句：The painting depicts an idyllic countryside scene with rolling hills and grazing sheep.  这幅画描绘了一幅田园诗般的乡村景色，有连绵起伏的丘陵和吃草的羊。

例句：They spent an idyllic week in the countryside, far from the hustle and bustle of city life. 他们在远离城市喧嚣的乡村度过了宁静的一周。


近义词：pastoral, bucolic, utopian, peaceful, tranquil

反义词：chaotic, turbulent, stressful, harsh, grim

词性:形容词
