The word refers to the process of becoming mature or ripe, whether it's in terms of biological development, psychological development, or the ripening of fruits.|例句：The maturative process of the fruit is influenced by temperature and humidity.  水果的成熟过程受温度和湿度的影响。

The maturative changes in the adolescent brain are significant. 青少年的脑部发育变化意义重大。|近义词：ripening, developing, maturing|反义词：immature, undeveloped, unripe|
