migratorial:/maɪɡrəˈtɔːriəl/| adj.|迁徙的；移居的|Relating to or characterized by migration;  moving from one place to another, especially seasonally.|The migratory birds flew south for the winter.  这些候鸟飞往南方过冬。|The migratory patterns of the whales are being studied. 鲸鱼的迁徙模式正在被研究。|nomadic, itinerant, transient|stationary, sedentary
