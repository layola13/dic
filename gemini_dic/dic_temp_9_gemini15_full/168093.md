prizefighters:/ˈpraɪzfɑɪtərz/| n. |职业拳击手|A prizefighter is a professional boxer who competes for prize money. They are skilled fighters who train rigorously to win matches and earn rewards.  Prizefighting, as a term, often evokes a sense of the older, more brutal style of boxing, before many of the modern safety regulations were in place.| The prizefighter knocked out his opponent in the third round.  这位职业拳击手在第三回合将对手击倒。|boxer, pugilist|amateur boxer, non-professional fighter
