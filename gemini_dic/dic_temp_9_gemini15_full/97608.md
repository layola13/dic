inhalant:/ˈɪnhələnt/| n.|吸入剂，吸入物|A substance that is inhaled, typically a volatile substance such as solvents or gases, often for recreational purposes.|例句：The misuse of inhalants can lead to serious health problems.  滥用吸入剂会导致严重的健康问题。|例句：Many teenagers experiment with inhalants without understanding the risks.许多青少年尝试吸入剂而不了解其风险。|近义词：inhaler, vapor, gas|反义词：exhalant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The patient inhales the medication. = 病人吸入药物。
He inhales deeply before speaking. = 他在说话前深吸一口气。

一般过去时:
She inhaled the sweet scent of flowers. = 她吸入了花朵的芬芳。
He inhaled deeply and then exhaled slowly. = 他深吸一口气，然后慢慢呼气。

现在完成时:
I have inhaled too much dust today. = 我今天吸入了太多的灰尘。
She has inhaled the fumes from the paint. = 她吸入了油漆的烟雾。

过去完成时:
He had inhaled a large amount of smoke before he collapsed. = 他在倒下之前吸入了大量的烟雾。
They had inhaled the toxic gas unknowingly. = 他们不知不觉地吸入了有毒气体。


将来时:
He will inhale the anesthetic before the surgery. = 手术前他会吸入麻醉剂。
She will inhale deeply to calm herself. = 她会深吸一口气来让自己平静下来。

虚拟语气:
If I had known it was toxic, I wouldn't have inhaled it. = 如果我知道它是剧毒的，我就不会吸入了。
I wish I hadn't inhaled that smoke. = 我真希望我没有吸入那烟。

被动语态:
The medication is inhaled through a nebulizer. = 药物通过雾化器吸入。
The fumes were inhaled by the workers. = 工人们吸入了烟雾。

疑问句:
Did he inhale the entire dose? = 他吸入了全部剂量吗？
What did you inhale that caused the allergic reaction? = 你吸入了什么导致过敏反应？

否定句:
He didn't inhale the entire dose. = 他没有吸入全部剂量。
She doesn't inhale cigarette smoke. = 她不吸香烟。
