directions:/dɪˈrɛkʃənz/| n.|方向，路线，指示，说明|Instructions or information about how to get somewhere or how to do something.  It can refer to physical directions (like a map) or instructions on how to perform a task.|例句：I need directions to the airport. 我需要去机场的路线。

例句：Follow the directions carefully.  仔细按照说明操作。


近义词：instructions, guidance, instructions, route, guide|反义词：None (Antonyms are context-dependent, e.g., if "directions" means "guidance," then "misguidance" could be considered an antonym.)
