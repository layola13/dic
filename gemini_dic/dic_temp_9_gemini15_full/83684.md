haggardly:/ˈhæɡərdli/| adv.|憔悴地，疲惫地，|in a haggard manner, appearing exhausted and worn.|例句：He looked haggardly at the overflowing inbox, his shoulders slumped with exhaustion. 他疲惫地望着堆积如山的收件箱，肩膀无力地垂着。|例句：She spoke haggardly, her voice raspy from lack of sleep. 她疲惫地说着话，声音因睡眠不足而嘶哑。|近义词：wearily, gauntly,  emaciatedly|反义词：vigorously, energetically, radiantly
