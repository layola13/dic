imperativeness:/ɪmˈpɛrətɪvnəs/| n. |强制性；命令性；必要性|The imperativeness of the situation demanded immediate action.  情况的紧急性要求立即采取行动。|近义词：urgency, necessity, compulsion|反义词：optionality, permissiveness


英文解析:
Imperativeness refers to the quality of being imperative; the state or quality of being commanding, urgent, or absolutely necessary. It suggests a strong need or demand for something to be done.  It can also describe the style of language or tone that is commanding or authoritative.


例句:

The imperativeness of the deadline spurred them to work overtime.  最后期限的紧迫性促使他们加班工作。

The doctor emphasized the imperativeness of following the treatment plan. 医生强调了遵循治疗计划的重要性。
