popularizes:/ˈpɑːpjʊləraɪzɪz/ | vt. |使普及；推广；宣传 | To make (something) popular or widely known.  The scientist popularized the theory through his lectures and writings. 科学家通过他的讲座和著作推广了这一理论。 |近义词：disseminate, promote, spread |反义词：suppress, obscure, conceal |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He popularizes his new book through social media. = 他通过社交媒体推广他的新书。
The company popularizes its products with attractive advertisements. = 公司通过有吸引力的广告宣传其产品。


一般过去时:
The invention popularized the use of electricity in homes. = 这项发明使家用电得到普及。
She popularized the yoga style through her successful classes. = 她通过成功的课程推广了这种瑜伽风格。


现在完成时:
The author has popularized the concept of mindfulness to a wider audience. = 作者已将正念的概念推广到更广泛的受众。
This movement has popularized the use of sustainable energy sources. = 这场运动普及了可持续能源的使用。


过去完成时:
Before the internet, she had popularized her art through traditional exhibitions. = 在互联网出现之前，她通过传统的展览推广了自己的艺术。
They had popularized the new technology through extensive marketing campaigns. = 他们通过广泛的营销活动推广了这项新技术。


将来时:

He will popularize his research findings at the upcoming conference. = 他将在即将召开的会议上宣传他的研究成果。


虚拟语气:
If he popularized his ideas effectively, he would have gained more recognition. = 如果他能有效地推广他的想法，他就会获得更多认可。

被动语态:
