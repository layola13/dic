godhead:/ˈɡɒdhed/ | /ˈɡɑːdhed/| n. |神性；神格；上帝；神 |The divine nature or essence of God; the state of being God.  It can also refer to a supreme being or deity. |例句：The godhead is beyond human comprehension. 神性超越了人类的理解能力。

例句：The artist attempted to portray the godhead in his painting.  艺术家试图在他的画作中描绘神性。


近义词：divinity, deity, divinity, godhood |反义词：humanity, mortality
