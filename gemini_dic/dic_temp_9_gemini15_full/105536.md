kilter:/ˈkɪltər/| n.|状况，秩序，协调|The state of being in good order or working properly; alignment or balance.|例句：The machine is out of kilter.  这机器失调了。|例句：After the accident, his life was completely out of kilter. 事故之后，他的生活完全乱套了。|近义词：order, balance, alignment|反义词：disorder, imbalance, disarray


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine is in kilter. = 机器运转正常。
Everything is in kilter. = 一切井然有序。

一般过去时:
The engine was out of kilter. = 发动机失调了。
His health was out of kilter. = 他的健康状况不佳。

现在完成时:
My life has been out of kilter since the move. = 自从搬家后，我的生活就乱了套。
The team's performance has been out of kilter lately. = 球队最近表现不佳。

过去完成时:
The car had been out of kilter for weeks before it finally broke down. = 那辆车在最终抛锚之前已经失调了好几个星期了。
His sleep schedule had been out of kilter for months. = 他几个月的睡眠时间都乱了套。

将来时:
If we don't fix this, the system will be out of kilter. = 如果我们不修理这个，系统将会失调。
I think my health will be out of kilter if I continue to work this hard. = 我想如果我继续这么努力地工作，我的健康状况会变差。


虚拟语气:

I wish my life were in kilter. = 我希望我的生活一切正常。

被动语态:

疑问句:
Is everything in kilter? = 一切正常吗？

否定句:

PHRASE->
in kilter = 状况良好
out of kilter = 失调的，紊乱的
get back in kilter = 恢复正常
throw something out of kilter = 使…失调
bring something back into kilter = 使…恢复正常


词源:
The origin of "kilter" is uncertain, but it is likely related to the Old English word "cylt," meaning a "young animal," or possibly to a dialectal word meaning "tilt" or "slope."  Its current meaning likely developed from the idea of something being "out of tilt" or "out of alignment."
