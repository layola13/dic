indeeds:/ɪnˈdiːdz/ | adv. |的确，确实 |Indeed emphasizes the truth of a statement. It can be used to express agreement, confirmation, or to add emphasis.  | Examples: "Indeed, it was a difficult task."  "Indeed, I agree with you." |近义词：truly, certainly, really, actually |反义词：falsely, incorrectly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is indeed a kind man. = 他确实是个善良的人。
The flowers are indeed beautiful. = 这些花确实很漂亮。


一般过去时:
She was indeed surprised by the news. = 她确实对这个消息感到惊讶。
They did indeed work hard. = 他们确实努力工作了。


现在完成时:
We have indeed made progress. = 我们确实取得了进展。
They have indeed finished the project. = 他们确实完成了项目。


过去完成时:
He had indeed warned them about the danger. = 他确实警告过他们危险。
It had indeed been a long journey. = 这确实是一段漫长的旅程。


将来时:
She will indeed be successful. = 她确实会成功的。
It will indeed be a challenge. = 这确实是一个挑战。


虚拟语气:
If it were indeed true, I would be shocked. = 如果这是真的，我会很震惊。
I wish it were indeed possible. = 我希望这确实是可能的。


被动语态:
The problem was indeed solved. = 问题确实得到了解决。
The plan was indeed approved. = 计划确实得到了批准。


疑问句:
Is it indeed true? = 这确实是事实吗？
Was it indeed him? = 那确实是 他吗？


否定句:
It is not indeed easy. = 这确实不容易。
It was not indeed a good idea. = 这确实不是一个好主意。
