lexicographer:/ˌlɛksɪˈkɑːɡrəfər/| n. |词典编纂者；词典学家 | A lexicographer is a person who compiles dictionaries. They research, define, and organize words for inclusion in dictionaries and other lexical resources.  Lexicographers are experts in etymology, lexicography, and linguistics. |  The chief lexicographer for the Oxford English Dictionary spent years researching the etymology of the word.  首席牛津英语词典编纂者花费数年时间研究该词的词源。| dictionary compiler, word expert |  (no direct antonym, but a possible contrast would be someone who actively avoids studying language) | 名词
