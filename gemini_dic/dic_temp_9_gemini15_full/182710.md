repaint:/ˌriːˈpeɪnt/| vt.|重新粉刷，重画|To paint something again.  This often implies covering a previous coat of paint.|例句：We need to repaint the kitchen; the walls are looking dirty. 我们需要重新粉刷厨房；墙看起来很脏了。

例句：The artist decided to repaint the canvas after finding a flaw in his initial work.艺术家发现最初作品中存在缺陷后，决定重新绘制画布。


近义词：redo, touch up, refresh|反义词：leave untouched, preserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repaints houses for a living. = 他靠粉刷房屋为生。
She repaints old furniture. = 她重新粉刷旧家具。

一般过去时:
They repainted the bedroom last weekend. = 他们上周末重新粉刷了卧室。
I repainted the car myself. = 我自己重新喷漆了汽车。

现在完成时:
We have repainted the entire apartment. = 我们已经重新粉刷了整套公寓。
She has repainted the mural several times. = 她已经好几次重新粉刷壁画了。

过去完成时:
They had repainted the house before we moved in. = 我们搬进来之前，他们已经重新粉刷了房子。
He had repainted the car twice before it looked perfect. = 他在汽车看起来完美之前已经重新喷漆了两次。

将来时:
We will repaint the fence next spring. = 我们明年春天将重新粉刷栅栏。
They will repaint the office after the renovations. = 装修后，他们将重新粉刷办公室。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would repaint the whole house. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把整个房子重新粉刷一遍。
I wish I had repainted the walls sooner; the damage is worse now. = 我真希望我早点把墙重新粉刷了；现在损坏更严重了。

被动语态:
The house is being repainted this week. = 这栋房子本周正在重新粉刷。
The car was repainted by a professional. = 汽车是由专业人士重新喷漆的。

疑问句:

否定句:
We didn't repaint the garage this year. = 我们今年没有重新粉刷车库。
He hasn't repainted the shed yet. = 他还没重新粉刷棚屋。
