mousetrapped:/ˈmaʊsˌtræpt/ | adj. | 措手不及的；被困住的；陷入困境的 |  Describes a situation where someone is caught in a difficult or unexpected situation, often likened to being caught in a mousetrap. It implies a feeling of being trapped or helpless. | 例句：He felt mousetrapped by his commitments. 他感到自己被承诺束缚住了。

例句：The unexpected tax increase left many homeowners feeling mousetrapped.  意外的税收增长让许多房主感到措手不及。


近义词：trapped, ensnared, cornered, boxed in, caught

反义词：free, liberated, unconstrained, at liberty


词性:形容词
