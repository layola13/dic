isochronic:/aɪˈsɒkrɒnɪk/ | adj. | 等时的；等时间的 |  Of or relating to equal times; having equal durations.  Isochronic refers to events or processes that occur at regular intervals or have the same duration.  It's often used in the context of brainwave entrainment, where sound frequencies are used to synchronize brain activity. |  例句：The isochronic tones helped her relax and focus. 等时音调帮助她放松和集中注意力。

例句：The experiment involved isochronic presentation of stimuli.  实验涉及刺激的等时呈现。


近义词：rhythmic, regular, synchronous | 反义词：asynchronous, irregular, sporadic | 词性：形容词


词源:

The word "isochronic" is derived from the Greek words:

* **isos (ἴσος):** meaning "equal"
* **chronos (χρόνος):** meaning "time"

Therefore, isochronic literally means "equal time" or "of equal duration".
