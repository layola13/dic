USAGE->
一般现在时:
He works contra to company policy. 他违反公司政策工作。
They always act contra to our wishes.  他们总是违背我们的意愿行事。


一般过去时:
She voted contra to the majority. 她投票反对大多数人。
He spoke contra to the evidence presented. 他所说的话与提交的证据相矛盾。


现在完成时:
They have consistently acted contra to our instructions. 他们一直违背我们的指示行事。
We have worked contra to the odds. 我们逆境而为。


过去完成时:
They had acted contra to the agreement.  他们已经违反了协议。
She had always lived contra to societal norms. 她一直过着违背社会规范的生活。


将来时:
He will likely act contra to expectations. 他很可能会出乎意料地行事。
They will continue to work contra to the current trend. 他们将继续与当前趋势背道而驰。


虚拟语气:
If he acted contra to orders, he would face severe consequences. 如果他违抗命令，他会面临严厉的惩罚。
I wish she hadn't acted contra to my advice. 我真希望她没有违背我的建议。


被动语态:
His actions were seen as contra to the public interest.  他的行为被视为违背公众利益。
The decision was made contra to the recommendations of the committee.  该决定违背了委员会的建议。


疑问句:
Is his behavior contra to company regulations? 他的行为违反公司规章制度吗？
Are these actions contra to the spirit of the agreement? 这些行为违背协议的精神吗？


否定句:
His actions were not contra to the law.  他的行为没有违法。
The evidence is not contra to his claim. 证据并不与他的说法相矛盾。
