groaningly:/ˈɡroʊnɪŋli/ | adv. | 呻吟地；痛苦地；令人厌烦地 |  Describes an action or situation performed or experienced in a way that suggests groaning, either physically (in pain or exhaustion) or figuratively (in boredom or displeasure). | 例句：He groaned, his voice cracking, "This is going to take forever." 他呻吟着，声音嘶哑，“这要花好长时间。”

The old car groaned along the bumpy road.  这辆旧车在颠簸的路上发出咯吱咯吱的声音行驶。| 近义词：painfully, wearily, agonizingly, tediously | 反义词：joyfully, happily, enthusiastically
