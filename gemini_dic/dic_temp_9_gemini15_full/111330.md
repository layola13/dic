live-stream:/ˈlaɪvstriːm/| n. v.| 直播，现场直播 |A live stream is a broadcast of an event that is happening in real time.  It is typically shown via the internet.| 例句：Many people watched the concert live stream online. 许多人在线观看音乐会直播。  They are planning to live stream their wedding. 他们计划直播他们的婚礼。|近义词：broadcast, webcast |反义词：recording, replay


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They live stream their gaming sessions every night. = 他们每晚直播他们的游戏环节。
We live stream our cooking class on YouTube. = 我们在YouTube上直播我们的烹饪课。

一般过去时:
She live streamed the protest march yesterday. = 她昨天直播了抗议游行。
The band live streamed their rehearsal last week. = 乐队上周直播了他们的彩排。

现在完成时:
They have live streamed over 100 events this year. = 今年他们已经直播了超过100个活动。
She has live streamed her daily workouts for months. = 她已经直播了几个月的日常锻炼。

过去完成时:
Before the conference, they had live streamed a Q&A session. = 会议之前，他们已经直播了一个问答环节。
The company had live streamed several product launches before this one. = 在这次之前，该公司已经直播了几次产品发布会。


将来时:
We will live stream the graduation ceremony next month. = 我们下个月将直播毕业典礼。
They will live stream the match from the stadium. = 他们将从体育场直播比赛。


虚拟语气:
If we had better equipment, we would live stream more often. = 如果我们有更好的设备，我们会更频繁地直播。
I wish I could have live streamed the concert last night. = 我真希望昨晚能直播这场音乐会。


被动语态:
The event was live streamed to millions of viewers. = 这次活动直播给了数百万观众观看。

疑问句:

否定句:
