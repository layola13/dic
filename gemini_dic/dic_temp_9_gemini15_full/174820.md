It's a type of polysaccharide with a branched structure, differing from other glucose polymers like cellulose and amylose in its branching pattern and properties.  | Example: The racemulose content of the plant extract contributed to its viscous properties.  植物提取物中的葡萄糖聚合物使其具有粘性。|  近义词：gum, mucilage, polysaccharide | 反义词：无  | 名词
