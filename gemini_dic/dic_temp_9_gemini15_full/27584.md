canary:/ˈkænəri/| n. |金丝雀；鲜黄色|A small yellow songbird, originally from the Canary Islands.|例句：We have a canary in our cage. 我们笼子里养了一只金丝雀。
例句：Her dress was a bright canary yellow. 她的裙子是鲜艳的金丝雀黄。|近义词：songbird,  goldfinch|反义词：无
