flossification:/ˌflɒsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |化石化；变成化石|The process by which organic matter is converted into a fossil.  This involves a complex series of chemical and physical changes, often including the replacement of original organic materials with minerals. |The flossification of the dinosaur bones took millions of years. 恐龙骨骼的化石化过程花了数百万年。|petrification, fossilization|decomposition, decay|名词
