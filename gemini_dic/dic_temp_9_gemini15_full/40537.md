controverser:/ˈkɒntrəvɜːrsər/| n. |争议者，好辩者 |A controversier is someone who enjoys or is skilled in engaging in controversies or debates. They often have strong opinions and are not afraid to express them, even if it means sparking conflict.  | Example: He was a known controversier, always ready to challenge established norms and beliefs. 他是一位著名的争议者，总是乐于挑战既定的规范和信仰。|近义词：debater, disputant, polemicist |反义词：peacemaker, conciliator
