demolishes:/dɪˈmɒlɪʃɪz/| vt.|拆除；破坏；彻底击败|To destroy completely; to pull down or tear down a building.  |例句：The earthquake demolished several buildings. 地震摧毁了几座建筑物。

例句：The team demolished their opponents in the final game.  这个队在决赛中彻底击败了对手。|近义词：destroy, wreck, raze, obliterate|反义词：construct, build, erect, create|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The construction crew demolishes old buildings every week. = 建筑队每周都拆除旧建筑物。
He demolishes his opponents with powerful arguments. = 他用有力的论据击败了他的对手。

一般过去时:
They demolished the old factory last year. = 他们去年拆除了旧工厂。
The boxer demolished his opponent in the first round. = 拳击手在第一回合就击败了他的对手。

现在完成时:
The city has demolished several blocks of run-down houses. = 城市已经拆除了几栋破旧的房屋街区。
The new evidence has demolished his alibi. = 新证据推翻了他的不在场证明。

过去完成时:
They had demolished the building before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，他们已经拆除了那栋建筑物。
The lawyer had demolished the witness's testimony. = 律师已经驳斥了证人的证词。

将来时:
The council will demolish the outdated stadium next year. = 理事会明年将拆除过时的体育场。
He will demolish his opponents with his superior skills. = 他将凭借其高超的技能击败对手。

虚拟语气:
If they demolished that building, they would improve the neighborhood. = 如果他们拆除那栋建筑物，他们会改善社区环境。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
