enlivener:/ɪnˈlaɪvnər/ | n. | 使活跃的人或事物；兴奋剂 | Something or someone that makes something more lively or exciting.  It can refer to a person, event, or substance. |例句：The comedian was a real enlivener at the party.  喜剧演员是派对上真正的活跃气氛的人。

例句：A glass of wine can be an enlivener after a long day.  一杯葡萄酒在漫长的一天后可以让人精神振奋。


近义词：stimulant, energizer, invigorator, excitement

反义词：depressant, duller, damper


词性：名词
