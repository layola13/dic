krait:/kɹeɪt/| n. |眼镜蛇(一种剧毒蛇)|A krait is a venomous snake of the family Elapidae, known for its highly toxic venom.  Kraits are typically nocturnal and often found in Southeast Asia. They are characterized by their slender bodies, smooth scales, and often striking patterns. |Example: The farmer was bitten by a krait while working in his rice paddy.  农民在稻田里干活时被一条眼镜蛇咬了。|近义词：cobra, viper, asp|反义词： (无直接反义词，可考虑 harmless snake)|名词
