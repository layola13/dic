dropbox:/ˈdrɒpˌbɒks/| n.| 滴答箱；(Dropbox) 著名云端存储服务 |A Dropbox is a file hosting service that operates on a freemium model. Users can store files on Dropbox's cloud storage and access them from anywhere.  The service offers synchronization between different computers, mobile devices, and other systems. |I store all my important documents on my Dropbox. = 我把所有重要的文件都存储在我的Dropbox上。 I shared the presentation files via Dropbox. = 我通过Dropbox共享了演示文稿文件。|Google Drive, OneDrive, iCloud|local storage, physical storage|名词
