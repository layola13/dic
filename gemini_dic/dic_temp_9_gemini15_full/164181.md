predistributing: /ˌpriːdɪˈstrɪbjuːtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先分配，预先分发 |  The act of distributing something beforehand.  It implies preparation and planning before the actual distribution takes place. |

例句：

The company is predistributing marketing materials to its sales team. = 公司正在向销售团队预先分发营销材料。

The government is predistributing emergency supplies before the hurricane hits. = 政府在飓风来临之前预先分发紧急物资。


近义词：preallocating, advance distributing, advance allocation

反义词：postdistributing, delayed distribution


词性：动词
