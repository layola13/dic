mormons:/ˈmɔːrmənz/| n. |摩门教徒；摩门教成员|Members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, a Christian religious movement founded in the 19th century in the United States.  Mormons are known for their unique beliefs, including the Book of Mormon, temple worship, and missionary work. |Example: Many Mormons serve missions abroad.  许多摩门教徒在国外传教。|近义词: Latter-day Saints |反义词:  (no direct antonym,  but terms associated with opposing religious beliefs could be used contextually)|名词
