prerequisites:/ˌpriːˈrɛkwɪzɪts/| n.|先决条件，必备条件|Things that are required before something else can happen or be done.|To apply for the job, you must meet all prerequisites.|近义词：requirements, essentials, necessities|反义词：nonessentials, options


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are the prerequisites for this course? = 这门课程的先决条件是什么？
What prerequisites do I need to meet for the job? = 我需要满足什么先决条件才能得到这份工作？


一般过去时:
What were the prerequisites for the grant application? = 拨款申请的先决条件是什么？
What prerequisites did he need to fulfill for graduation? = 他需要满足什么先决条件才能毕业？


现在完成时:
Have you met all the prerequisites for the program? = 你已经满足了该项目的所有先决条件了吗？
Have they completed all the prerequisites for the exam? = 他们已经完成了考试的所有先决条件了吗？


过去完成时:
Had she met all the prerequisites before she applied? = 她申请之前是否已经满足了所有先决条件？
Had they fulfilled all the prerequisites before the project started? = 项目开始之前他们是否已经满足了所有先决条件？


将来时:
What will be the prerequisites for next year's admission? = 明年入学的先决条件是什么？
Will you have met all the prerequisites by the deadline? = 你会在截止日期前满足所有先决条件吗？


虚拟语气:
If I had met all the prerequisites, I would have been accepted. = 如果我满足了所有先决条件，我就已经被录取了。
If I were to apply, I would need to meet all the prerequisites. = 如果我要申请，我需要满足所有先决条件。


被动语态:
All prerequisites were met by the applicant. = 申请人满足了所有先决条件。
The prerequisites for the scholarship are clearly stated. = 奖学金的先决条件已明确说明。


疑问句:
Are there any prerequisites for this position? = 这个职位有什么先决条件吗？
What are the prerequisites for the advanced course? = 高级课程有什么先决条件？


否定句:
The applicant did not meet all the prerequisites. = 申请人没有满足所有先决条件。
He lacked the prerequisites for the advanced class. = 他缺乏高级班的先决条件。
