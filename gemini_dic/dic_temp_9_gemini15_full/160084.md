pomade:/poʊˈmeɪd/| n. |发油，油膏|A hair styling product, typically an ointment or cream, used to keep hair in place.  |例句：He slicked his hair back with pomade. 他用发油把头发梳到后面。

例句：She applied a small amount of pomade to her short hair for extra shine. 她往短发上涂抹少量发油，增加光泽。


近义词：hair cream, hair gel, brilliantine|反义词：无


词源:  From French *pommade*, from *pomme* "apple", referring to the original apple-based preparations.  The association likely stems from the smooth, often fragrant nature of the product.
