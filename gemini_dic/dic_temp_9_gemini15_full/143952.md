outquench:/aʊtˈkwɛntʃ/| vt.|熄灭；压制；盖过|to extinguish or suppress completely; to surpass or excel|例句：The firefighters worked tirelessly to outquench the raging inferno. 消防员们不懈努力地扑灭了猛烈的火灾。
