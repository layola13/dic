pretaster: /ˈpriːteɪstər/ | n. | 预先品尝者，试吃员 | Someone who tastes food or drinks before others to check the quality or identify potential problems.  | Example: The chef hired a pretaster to sample the new soup before serving it to the guests.  厨师雇了一位预先品尝者在把新汤端给客人之前先试吃。|近义词: taster, sampler | 反义词: N/A

She tastes the wine before serving it. = 她上菜前先品尝葡萄酒。
