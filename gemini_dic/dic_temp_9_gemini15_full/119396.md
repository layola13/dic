/ˌmaɪkroʊɪˌlɛkˈtrɑːnɪksɪz/ | n. | 微电子学；微电子器件 | Microelectronics is the study and application of extremely small electronic devices and circuits.  It deals with the design, fabrication, and application of electronic components and systems that have dimensions on the scale of micrometers or even nanometers.  This field has revolutionized many aspects of modern technology, leading to the development of smaller, faster, and more powerful electronic devices. | The advancements in microelectronics have led to the miniaturization of computers. | 近义词：microelectronic technology, integrated circuits | 反义词：macroelectronics
