desecration:/ˌdɛsɪˈkreɪʃən/| n.|亵渎；玷污；破坏|The act of violating or profaning something sacred or revered;treating something with contempt or disrespect.|例句：The vandalism of the church was an act of desecration. 教堂的破坏行为是一种亵渎行为。

例句：The graffiti on the war memorial was a shocking desecration.  战争纪念碑上的涂鸦是一种令人震惊的亵渎行为。

近义词：profanation, violation, defilement, pollution, contamination

反义词：consecration, sanctification, reverence, respect
