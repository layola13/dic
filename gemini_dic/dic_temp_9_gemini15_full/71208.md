fit-to-kill: /ˌfɪt tuː kɪl/ | adj. | 杀伤力极强的，极度危险的 |  Ready or suitable for killing; extremely dangerous or lethal.  The term suggests a state of readiness or preparedness for inflicting death or serious harm. It often implies a deliberate and calculated intention to kill. | 例句：The weapon was fit-to-kill, capable of causing immense damage. 这件武器杀伤力极强，能够造成巨大的破坏。

The newly developed virus is fit-to-kill, posing a serious threat to public health.  这种新研发的病毒杀伤力极强，对公共健康构成严重威胁。| 近义词：lethal, deadly, murderous, deadly, dangerous | 反义词：harmless, safe, innocuous |  词性:形容词
