demurs:/dɪˈmɜːrz/ | v. | 提出异议；反对；犹豫；迟疑 | To raise doubts or objections; to hesitate or object. |  He demurred at the suggestion. 他对这个建议表示异议。 | She demurred, saying she needed more time to consider. 她犹豫了，说她需要更多时间考虑。 | object, protest, hesitate, balk | agree, accept, consent | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He demurs to any changes to the plan. = 他反对对计划做任何更改。
They always demur when asked to help. = 当被要求帮忙时，他们总是犹豫。


一般过去时:
She demurred at the thought of public speaking. = 她一想到公开演讲就犹豫了。
He demurred politely but firmly to the proposal. = 他礼貌但坚定地反对了该提案。


现在完成时:
They have demurred on several occasions to the new policy. = 他们几次反对这项新政策。
She has never demurred to authority before. = 她以前从未反对过权威。


过去完成时:
He had demurred at the outset, but eventually agreed. = 他一开始就反对，但最终还是同意了。
They had demurred to the original plan, which resulted in delays. = 他们反对最初的计划，导致了延误。


将来时:
She will likely demur if asked to take on more responsibility. = 如果被要求承担更多责任，她很可能会反对。
He will demur to the idea unless presented with concrete evidence. = 除非拿出确凿证据，否则他会反对这个想法。


虚拟语气:
I would demur if I thought it was a bad idea. = 如果我认为这是一个坏主意，我会反对。
If she had demurred earlier, the situation might have been different. = 如果她早点反对，情况可能就不同了。


被动语态:
His objections were demurred to by the committee. = 他的反对意见被委员会驳回了。(被动语态中demur使用较少，通常用其名词形式objection)
The proposal was demurred against by several members. =  这项提案遭到几名成员的反对。(被动语态中demur使用较少，通常用其名词形式objection)


疑问句:
Will he demur to the request? = 他会反对这个请求吗？
Does she usually demur to such suggestions? = 她通常会反对这样的建议吗？


否定句:
He did not demur to the judge's ruling. = 他没有反对法官的裁决。
She did not demur and readily agreed to the terms. = 她没有反对，欣然同意了这些条款。


PHRASE->
