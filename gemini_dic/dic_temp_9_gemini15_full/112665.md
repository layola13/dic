lowered:/ˈloʊərd/| /ˈləʊəd/| v.|降低；放下；使沮丧|To move something or someone down to a lower position or level.  To reduce the intensity, amount, or level of something.|例句：He lowered the flag to half-mast. 他把旗子降到半旗。

The heavy rain lowered the temperature. 大雨降低了温度。|近义词：reduced, decreased, depressed|反义词：raised, elevated, lifted|动词
