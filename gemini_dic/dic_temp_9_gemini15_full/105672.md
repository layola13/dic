kingdom: /ˈkɪŋdəm/ | n. | 王国，界 | A kingdom is a country ruled by a king or queen.  A kingdom can also refer to one of the five major biological classifications (Animalia, Plantae, Fungi, Protista, and Bacteria).|例句：The king ruled his kingdom wisely.  国王明智地统治着他的王国。 |例句：The animal kingdom is vast and diverse. 动物界浩瀚而多样。 |近义词：realm, empire, domain |反义词：republic, democracy |名词
