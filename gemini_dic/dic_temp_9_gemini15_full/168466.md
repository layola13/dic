probationally:/prəˈbeɪʃənəli/ | adv. | 缓刑地；试用期地；暂定地 |  Adverbial form of "probation," indicating something is done or exists under a period of probation or trial.  It implies a conditional or temporary state, subject to review or potential termination. | 例句：He was probationally employed for six months before receiving a permanent contract. 他试用期工作了六个月后才获得永久合同。

He was probationally released from prison, meaning he still faced strict conditions. 他被假释出狱，这意味着他仍然面临严格的条件。


近义词：conditionally, tentatively, experimentally, provisionally | 反义词：permanently, unconditionally, definitively
