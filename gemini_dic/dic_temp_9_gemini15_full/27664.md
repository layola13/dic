candidly:/ˈkændɪdli/| adv. |坦率地；直言不讳地 |Honestly and openly, without holding back unpleasant truths.  |例句：Candidly, I think the project is doomed to fail. 坦率地说，我认为这个项目注定要失败。|例句：She candidly admitted her mistakes. 她坦率地承认了自己的错误。|近义词：frankly, openly, honestly |反义词：indirectly, evasively, deceptively

词源:
From Middle English *candid, from Old French *candide, from Latin candidus ("shining white, pure, frank"), from candēre ("to be white, to glow").  The sense evolution is from "pure white" to "pure in intention" to "frank and open."
