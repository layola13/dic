grape:/ɡreɪp/ | n. |葡萄 |A small, round fruit that grows in bunches on a vine and is usually green, red, or purple when ripe. Grapes are often eaten fresh or used to make wine, juice, and jam. |例句：I love to eat fresh grapes in the summer. 夏天我喜欢吃新鲜葡萄。|例句：Wine is made from fermented grapes. 葡萄酒是由发酵的葡萄酿造的。|近义词：berry |反义词:  无


USAGE->

Grapes grow on vines. = 葡萄生长在藤蔓上。
She buys grapes every week. = 她每周都买葡萄。

一般过去时:
