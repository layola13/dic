improbably:/ɪmˈprɒbəbli/| adj. |不太可能地，难以置信地 |  unlikely to be true or to happen; unbelievable.  |The story, while entertaining, seemed improbably far-fetched.  这个故事虽然有趣，但却显得难以置信地牵强。| likely, possible, plausible | certainly, definitely, undoubtedly | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It improbably rains in the desert.  沙漠里不太可能下雨。
He improbably believes that he can fly. 他不太可能相信自己能飞。

一般过去时:
It improbably rained yesterday. 昨天不太可能下雨了。
She improbably finished the marathon in under three hours. 她不太可能在三小时内完成马拉松。

现在完成时:
It has improbably rained all week. 这周一直在下雨，这不太可能。
He has improbably won the lottery twice. 他竟然两次中彩票，这太不可思议了。

过去完成时:
It had improbably rained for days before the drought. 在干旱之前，竟然下了好几天的雨，这太不可思议了。
She had improbably completed the project before the deadline. 她竟然在截止日期前完成了项目，这太不可思议了。

将来时:
It will improbably snow tomorrow. 明天不太可能下雪。
He will improbably be able to attend the meeting. 他不太可能参加会议。

虚拟语气:
If it improbably rained tomorrow, we'd stay inside. 如果明天不太可能下雨，我们就会待在家里。(This example is a bit awkward, as the improbability clashes with the conditional.)
It would be improbably convenient if they arrived on time. 如果他们准时到达，那将是不可思议的方便。

被动语态:
It is improbably believed that aliens exist. 人们不太相信外星人的存在。
The theory is improbably considered to be valid. 这个理论不太可能被认为是有效的。

疑问句:
Is it improbably that he will succeed? 他成功的机会渺茫吗？
Is it improbably cold for this time of year? 对于这个季节来说，天气冷得不可思议吗？

否定句:
It is not improbably that he will win. 他获胜并非不可能。
It's not improbably cold today. 今天并不冷得不可思议。
