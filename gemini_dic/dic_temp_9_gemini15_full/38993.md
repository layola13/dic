confirmatory: /kənˈfɜːrmətəri/ | adj. | 证实的；确认的 |  Serving to confirm or verify; tending to confirm.  This adjective describes something that serves to prove or establish the truth or accuracy of something else. | 例句：The confirmatory tests proved the initial diagnosis was correct.  证实性测试证明了初步诊断是正确的。


The confirmatory evidence was crucial to the case.  确凿的证据对案件至关重要。


近义词：verifying, validating, corroborative, supportive | 反义词：disproving, refuting, contradicting | 词性：形容词


词源:  Derived from the verb "confirm," which comes from the Latin "confirmare" meaning "to strengthen," "to make firm," or "to establish."  The suffix "-atory" indicates something that has the function of or relates to confirming.
