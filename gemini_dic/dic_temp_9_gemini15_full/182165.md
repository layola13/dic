remodels:/ˌriːˈmɒdəlz/| vt.| 改建；翻新；重塑 | To change the form or structure of something, often to improve it.  The architect remodels old buildings into modern apartments. 建筑师将旧建筑改建成现代公寓。|近义词：renovate, refurbish, reconstruct |反义词：destroy, demolish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He remodels houses for a living. = 他以改建房屋为生。
They remodel their kitchen every five years. = 他们每五年翻新一次厨房。

一般过去时:
She remodeled her bathroom last year. = 她去年重新装修了浴室。
The company remodeled the old factory. = 公司对旧工厂进行了改造。

现在完成时:
They have remodeled their entire home. = 他们已经重新装修了整个家。
We have remodeled the office to improve efficiency. = 我们已经改造了办公室以提高效率。

过去完成时:
They had remodeled the building before the new tenants moved in. = 新租户搬进来之前，他们已经对建筑物进行了改造。
She had remodeled the car before selling it. = 她卖车之前已经改装了汽车。

将来时:
We will remodel the garden next spring. = 我们明年春天将改造花园。
He will remodel his basement into a home gym. = 他将把地下室改造成家庭健身房。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would remodel my kitchen. = 如果我有更多钱，我会重新装修我的厨房。
I wish I could remodel my entire house. = 我希望我能改造我的整个房子。

被动语态:
The house is being remodeled by a local contractor. = 这栋房子正在由当地承包商改建。
The old building is being remodeled into a hotel. = 旧建筑正在被改造成酒店。

疑问句:
Are they remodeling the school? = 他们正在改造学校吗？
When will the bathroom be remodeled? = 浴室什么时候会重新装修？

否定句:
They did not remodel the living room. = 他们没有重新装修客厅。
We will not remodel the house this year. = 我们今年不会改造房子。
