longing:/ˈlɒŋɪŋ/| /ˈlɔːŋɪŋ/ | n. |渴望，思念，向往 |A feeling of intense longing for something or someone.  It often involves a sense of sadness or incompleteness because the desired thing is absent. |例句：She felt a deep longing for her homeland. 她深深地渴望回到故乡。

例句：The longing in his eyes betrayed his unspoken desire. 他眼神中的渴望出卖了他未说出口的愿望。


近义词：yearning, yearning, desire, craving, wistfulness | 反义词：satisfaction, contentment, apathy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am longing for a vacation. = 我渴望休假。
He longs for his family. = 他渴望见到家人。


一般过去时:
I longed for a simpler life. = 我渴望过一种更简单的生活。
She longed to see him again. = 她渴望再次见到他。


现在完成时:
I have been longing to visit Italy. = 我一直渴望去意大利旅游。
They have longed for peace for many years. = 他们多年来一直渴望和平。


过去完成时:
I had longed for this moment for years. = 我多年来一直渴望这一刻。
She had longed to be a doctor since she was a child. = 从小她就渴望成为一名医生。


将来时:
I will be longing for your return. = 我将渴望你的归来。
We will long for the summer days. = 我们将会思念夏日时光。


虚拟语气:
I wish I could satisfy my longing for adventure. = 我希望我能满足我渴望冒险的愿望。
If only I could alleviate this longing for home. = 如果我能减轻这种思乡之情就好了。


被动语态:
His longing for freedom was felt by everyone. = 他的渴望自由的心情被每个人都感受到了。
The longing for a better future is being expressed by many. = 许多人都在表达对更美好未来的渴望。


疑问句:
What are you longing for? = 你渴望什么？
Is she longing for anything in particular? = 她渴望什么特别的东西吗？


否定句:
I do not long for fame and fortune. = 我不渴望名利。
He did not long for a life of luxury. = 他不渴望奢华的生活。
