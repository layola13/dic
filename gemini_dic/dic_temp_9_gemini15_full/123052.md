monetarian:/ˌmʌnɪˈtɛəriən/| adj. |货币主义的；货币量的|Relating to or characteristic of monetarism, an economic theory that emphasizes the role of money supply in influencing economic activity.|The monetarian approach suggests controlling inflation by managing the money supply.|近义词：monetary|反义词：Keynesian
