dollishly:/ˈdɒlɪʃli/|/ˈdɑːlɪʃli/| adv.|像娃娃一样地，可爱地，娇柔地|In a way that resembles a doll; charmingly or delicately.  It often implies a slightly artificial or affected sweetness.|She smiled dollishly at the camera. 她对着镜头娃娃般地微笑。|The actress played the role with a dollishly innocent expression.  这位女演员以一种天真可爱的娃娃表情扮演这个角色。|childishly, sweetly, prettily|cruelly, harshly, roughly|副词
