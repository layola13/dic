pentacrostic: /ˌpentəˈkrɒstɪk/ | n. |五角诗| A poem of five lines in which the first letter of each line spells out a word, typically the subject of the poem.  | Example:  The following is a simple pentacrostic poem about LOVE:

Luminous stars above,
Over the moonlit night,
Victorious feelings ignite,
Enchanting, pure, and bright,
Lovely, a celestial light.


|近义词: acrostic (though less specific)|反义词:|  无 |
