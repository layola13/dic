reacidify:/ˌriːəˈsɪdɪfaɪ/| vt.| 再酸化；重新酸化 | To make something acidic again.  This typically involves adding an acid to a substance that has become less acidic or neutralized. | 例句：The soil was reacidified after the lime treatment to restore optimal conditions for the plants.  土壤在石灰处理后重新酸化，以恢复植物的最佳生长条件。

The process of re-acidification was crucial to the experiment's success. 再酸化过程对实验的成功至关重要。| 近义词：re-acidulate,  resour | 反义词：neutralize, alkalinize | 动词
