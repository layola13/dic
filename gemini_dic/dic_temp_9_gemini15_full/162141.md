pre-freeze:/priːˈfriːz/| adj.| 预冷，预冻 |  Describes a process or state before freezing, often involving cooling to a temperature close to freezing point but not yet frozen.  It's often used in food processing and cryogenics. | Example: The pre-freeze process ensures even ice crystal formation.  预冷过程确保冰晶均匀形成。|近义词：pre-chilling, pre-cooling |反义词：post-freeze, thawing
