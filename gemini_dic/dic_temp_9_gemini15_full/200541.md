slasher:/ˈslæʃər/| n. adj. |凶杀狂；砍杀者；（电影）砍杀片；砍杀的|A person who commits murder by slashing.  A film genre featuring graphic violence and murder. |例句：The slasher movie was incredibly gory. 这部砍杀片极其血腥。

The police are hunting for a slasher who has killed three people in the last month. 警方正在追捕一名在上个月杀害了三人的凶杀狂。


近义词：murderer, killer, butcher, hack-and-slash film (for the film genre) |反义词：victim, survivor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slashes the tires of his neighbor's car. 他划破了他邻居的车胎。
The slasher attacks his victims with a knife.  凶手用刀袭击他的受害者。


一般过去时:
The criminal slashed the painting. 罪犯划破了这幅画。
The slasher stalked his prey before killing them. 凶手在杀死猎物之前跟踪了他。


现在完成时:
The vandal has slashed several car tires in the neighborhood.  破坏分子已经划破了附近几辆车的轮胎。
The police have apprehended the slasher responsible for the recent murders. 警方已经逮捕了最近几起谋杀案的凶手。


过去完成时:
