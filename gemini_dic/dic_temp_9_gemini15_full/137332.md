nontariffs:/ˌnɒnˈtærɪfs/| n. |非关税壁垒|Non-tariff barriers are trade restrictions that countries use to limit imports without imposing direct taxes or tariffs on imported goods.  These barriers can include quotas, embargoes, sanctions, and other regulations.|The government implemented new nontariffs to protect domestic industries.|trade barriers, restrictions, quotas|free trade, open markets|名词
