词源:
The word "piercing" comes from the Old French word "percier," meaning "to pierce," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "perforare," meaning "to bore through."  The meaning has evolved to encompass both the physical act of piercing and the sensory experience of something sharp or penetrating.
