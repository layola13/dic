immobilizations:/ˌɪməʊbɪlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 固定；固定化；麻痹；不动 | The process of rendering something immobile or fixed.  It often refers to the act of restricting movement, either physically or chemically.  In medicine, it might refer to immobilizing a broken bone. In chemistry, it might refer to immobilizing enzymes. |例句：The immobilization of the fractured leg was crucial for healing.  腿部骨折的固定对于愈合至关重要。

The immobilization of enzymes on a solid support increased their stability. 将酶固定在固体载体上提高了它们的稳定性。|近义词：fixation, restraint, stabilization |反义词：mobilization, release, freeing
