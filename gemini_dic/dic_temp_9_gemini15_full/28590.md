carefully:/ˈkerfəli/ | adv. |仔细地，谨慎地 |The adverb "carefully" means in a careful manner; with care and attention to detail. It emphasizes the precision and caution taken in performing an action.  |例句：He carefully placed the vase on the table. 他小心地把花瓶放在桌子上。|例句：She carefully read the instructions before starting the experiment. 她仔细阅读了说明书才开始实验。|近义词： cautiously, attentively, meticulously, thoroughly |反义词： carelessly, recklessly, haphazardly |副词
