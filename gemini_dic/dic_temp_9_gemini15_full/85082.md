hard-won:/ˌhɑːrdˈwʌn/| adj. |来之不易的，艰苦取得的 | achieved only after a great deal of effort.  This adjective describes something that has been obtained with significant difficulty and perseverance. |例句：Her hard-won victory was a testament to her dedication.  她来之不易的胜利证明了她的奉献精神。

例句：After years of struggle, he finally achieved his hard-won success.  经过多年的奋斗，他终于取得了来之不易的成功。


近义词：difficultly-obtained, dearly bought, painstakingly achieved, laboriously acquired

反义词：easily obtained, effortlessly achieved


词性：形容词
