shimmery:/ˈʃɪməri/| adj.|闪闪发光的，微光闪烁的|Having a shimmering or glistening quality; slightly shining with a soft, unsteady light.  The fabric had a shimmering quality in the sunlight.|近义词：glittering, sparkling, glistening, lustrous|反义词：dull, matte
