normalization:/ˌnɔːrməlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |标准化，规范化 |The process of making something conform to a standard or norm.  This often involves transforming data to a standard format or range.  For example, in database design, normalization reduces data redundancy and improves data integrity. In image processing, it might involve adjusting brightness and contrast to a standard level. |例句：Data normalization is crucial for efficient database management. 数据规范化对于高效的数据库管理至关重要。

例句：The normalization of the audio track improved its clarity. 音频轨道的标准化提高了其清晰度。


近义词：standardization, regulation, regularization |反义词：randomization, irregularity


词源:  From "norm" (a standard or rule) + "-alization" (the act of making something conform to a norm).  The root "norm" traces back to Latin "norma" meaning "carpenter's square," "rule," or "pattern."
