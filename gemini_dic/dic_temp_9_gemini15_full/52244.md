dibbel:/ˈdɪbəl/|n.|小铲，播种器|A small tool, typically with a pointed end, used for making holes in the ground for planting seeds or seedlings.  It is often used in gardening and agriculture.|例句：He used a dibbel to plant the seeds in neat rows. 他用小铲把种子整整齐齐地种成一行行。|例句：The gardener carefully used the dibbel to create small holes for the tulip bulbs. 园丁小心地用播种器为郁金香球茎挖出小洞。|近义词：planting tool, seed dibbler, planter|反义词：N/A
