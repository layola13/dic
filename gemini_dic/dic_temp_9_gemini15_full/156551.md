pickback:/ˈpɪkbæk/| n. v.|搭便车；背着；(信息)顺带捎带|A ride given on the back of a vehicle, animal, or person.  The act of carrying something on one's back.|例句：He got a pickback on his friend's motorcycle. 他搭了他朋友摩托车的便车。

He gave his daughter a pickback to the playground. 他背着女儿去了操场。|近义词：ride, piggyback, carry|反义词：walk, drive
