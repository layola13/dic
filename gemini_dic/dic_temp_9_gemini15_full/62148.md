encloister:/ɪnˈklɔɪstər/| vt.|使隐居；使与世隔绝；把…围以修道院建筑|To confine or seclude (someone) in a cloister or monastery; to surround with cloisters.  |例句：The nuns were encloistered in the convent for many years. 修女们在修道院隐居多年。

例句：The ancient city was encloistered by high walls.  古城被高墙环绕。


近义词：seclude, isolate, immure, confine|反义词：liberate, release, open


词性：动词
