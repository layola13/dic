reaccelerates:/ˌriːəkˈsɛləreɪts/| v.|重新加速；再次加速|To accelerate again; to increase speed after a decrease.  |例句：The car re-accelerated after slowing down for the corner.  汽车在减速过弯后再次加速。

例句：After a brief pause, the rocket re-accelerated towards its target.  短暂暂停后，火箭再次加速飞向目标。


近义词：speed up, accelerate again, pick up speed


反义词：decelerate, slow down, brake


词性：动词
