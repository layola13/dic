It might be less expensive, less refined, or lack some of the hallmarks of true luxury.  | The hotel offered quasi-luxurious accommodations at a surprisingly affordable price.  这家酒店以令人惊讶的实惠价格提供准奢华的住宿。 | luxurious, upscale, high-end | inexpensive, budget, basic | 形容词

词源:
The word is a combination of "quasi" (from Latin, meaning "as if," "seemingly," or "apparently") and "luxurious" (from Latin "luxus," meaning "luxury").  Therefore, "quasi-luxurious" literally means "seemingly luxurious" or "almost luxurious."
