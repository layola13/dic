events:/ɪˈvɛnts/ | n. |事件，大事，活动 |Things that happen, especially important or significant occurrences.  |例句：The events of the past year have been challenging. 过去一年的事件充满挑战。 |例句：We're planning a series of events to celebrate the anniversary. 我们正在策划一系列活动来庆祝周年纪念。|近义词：occurrences, happenings, incidents |反义词：inaction, stillness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What events are happening this weekend? = 这个周末有什么活动？
What events shape history? = 什么事件塑造了历史？


一般过去时:
What events occurred during the war? = 战争期间发生了什么事件？
What events led to the company's bankruptcy? = 哪些事件导致了公司的破产？


现在完成时:
What important events have taken place this century? = 本世纪发生了哪些重要事件？
What events have you attended recently? = 你最近参加了哪些活动？


过去完成时:
What events had transpired before the police arrived? = 在警察到达之前发生了哪些事件？
What events had led up to the crisis? = 哪些事件导致了这场危机？


将来时:
What events will be held next month? = 下个月将举办哪些活动？
What events will affect the market in the future? = 未来哪些事件会影响市场？


虚拟语气:
If those events hadn't happened, the world would be different. = 如果那些事件没有发生，世界将会不同。
I wish those events hadn't happened. = 我希望那些事件没有发生。


被动语态:
The events were widely reported in the media. = 这些事件被媒体广泛报道。
The events were carefully planned and executed. = 这些事件被精心策划和执行。


疑问句:
What events are you referring to? = 你指的是哪些事件？
What events are most significant in your life? = 你生命中最重大的事件是什么？


否定句:
No significant events occurred during the night. = 夜间没有发生重大事件。
There were no events scheduled for that day. = 那天没有安排任何活动。


PHRASE->
current events = 时事
major events = 重大事件
significant events = 重要事件
future events = 未来事件
special events = 特别活动
