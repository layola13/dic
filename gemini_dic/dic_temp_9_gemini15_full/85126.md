hardiest:/ˈhɑːrdiɪst/ | adj. |最耐寒的；最坚强的；最勇敢的 | Most able to withstand difficult conditions; most resilient; most courageous. |例句：The hardiest plants survived the harsh winter. 最耐寒的植物挺过了严冬。
The hardiest of the soldiers continued to fight despite their injuries. 最勇敢的士兵们尽管受伤了，但仍然继续战斗。 |近义词：most resilient, toughest, most robust, most courageous |反义词：most vulnerable, weakest, most delicate
