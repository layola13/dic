clasps:/klæspz/| n. vt.|扣子，钩子；紧握，拥抱|A clasp is a device used to fasten something, such as a bracelet or a bag.  It can also refer to a firm grip or an embrace.|例句：She fastened the clasp on her necklace. 她扣上了项链上的扣子。

The climber clasped the rock face. 登山者紧紧抓住岩壁。|近义词：fastener, grip, hold, embrace|反义词：unfasten, release, let go
