haemadrometer:/ˌhiːməˈdɒmɪtə(r)/| n. |血细胞计数器|A haemocytometer is a counting chamber used in haematology to count blood cells. It is a specialized microscope slide with a gridded area of known volume. By counting the cells within this known volume, the concentration of cells in the whole blood sample can be calculated.|Example: The technician used a haemocytometer to determine the white blood cell count in the patient's blood sample.  技师使用血细胞计数器测定患者血液样本中的白细胞计数。|近义词：blood cell counter, counting chamber|反义词：无
