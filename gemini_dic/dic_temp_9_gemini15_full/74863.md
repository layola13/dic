fres:/frɛʃ/| adj.| 新鲜的，清新的 | Fresh air and sunshine are good for you. 新鲜的空气和阳光对你有益。 |近义词：new, recent,  |反义词:stale, old, musty


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The bread is fresh. = 面包很新鲜。
The flowers smell fresh. = 花闻起来很清新。


一般过去时:
The air was fresh after the rain. = 雨后空气很清新。
The fish was fresh this morning. = 这条鱼今天早上很新鲜。


现在完成时:
They have bought fresh fruit. = 他们买了新鲜水果。
She has made a fresh start in her career. = 她开始了她事业的新篇章。


过去完成时:
The milk had gone fresh before we could drink it. = 我们还没来得及喝牛奶就变质了。(注意：此句中fresh用反义，表示“没有变质”)
The vegetables had been fresh yesterday. = 蔬菜昨天很新鲜。


将来时:
The flowers will be fresh tomorrow. = 明天这些花会很新鲜。
We will buy fresh produce at the farmer's market. = 我们将在农贸市场购买新鲜农产品。


虚拟语气:
If the air were fresh, we would go for a walk. = 如果空气新鲜的话，我们会去散步。
I wish the vegetables were fresher. = 我希望蔬菜更新鲜一些。


被动语态:
The bread is kept fresh by refrigeration. = 面包通过冷藏保持新鲜。
The salad is made with fresh ingredients. = 沙拉是用新鲜食材制作的。


疑问句:
Is the milk still fresh? = 牛奶还新鲜吗？
Are the vegetables fresh today? = 蔬菜今天新鲜吗？


否定句:
The fish isn't fresh. = 这条鱼不新鲜。
The air isn't fresh in this room. = 这个房间的空气不清新。


PHRASE->

fresh air: 新鲜空气
fresh start: 新开始
fresh water: 淡水
fresh look: 新鲜的视角
fresh produce: 新鲜农产品
