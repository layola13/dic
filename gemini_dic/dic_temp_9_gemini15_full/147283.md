overmobilize: /ˌoʊvərˈmoʊbəlaɪz/ | v. |过度动员；过度调动|To mobilize excessively or to a degree that is counterproductive.  This term implies that the mobilization is not only excessive but also potentially harmful or inefficient. It suggests that the resources or personnel involved are being stretched too thin, leading to potential problems. | 例句：The company overmobilized its resources for the project, leading to burnout among employees and ultimately hindering progress. 公司为这个项目过度动员了资源，导致员工筋疲力尽，最终阻碍了项目的进展。

例句：The government’s overmobilization of troops led to logistical nightmares and weakened its overall defensive capabilities. 政府过度动员军队导致后勤问题重重，削弱了其整体防御能力。


近义词：overextend, overstrain, overtax, exhaust

反义词：undermobilize, underutilize
