papal:/ˈpeɪpəl/| adj.|教皇的；天主教教宗的|Relating to the Pope or the papacy.  The word derives from the Latin word "papa," meaning "father," which was a title given to the Bishop of Rome.|例句：The papal blessing was a highlight of the pilgrimage. 教皇的祝福是朝圣之旅的亮点。


例句：The papal nuncio delivered a powerful message of peace. 教廷大使发表了强有力的和平信息。


近义词：pontifical, pontiffical


反义词：None specifically antonymous, but words suggesting secular or non-Catholic authority could be considered contrastive.


词性：形容词
