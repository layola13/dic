fistuliform:/ˈfɪstjʊləfɔːrm/ | adj. | 廔管状的；管状的 |  Resembling a fistula; tubular or channel-like in form.  The term refers to a structure or organ that is long, narrow, and somewhat cylindrical, reminiscent of a fistula (an abnormal channel or passage). | Example: The fistuliform structure of the worm allowed it to burrow easily through the soil.  廔管状的蠕虫结构使它能够轻松地钻入土壤。|近义词：tubular, cylindrical, channel-like | 反义词：globular, spherical
