outblusters:/ˌaʊtˈblʌstərz/| v.|喧嚣地大声说话；压制；大喊大叫|To speak or shout loudly and aggressively, often to overpower or silence someone else.|The bully outblustered the smaller children, silencing their complaints.  暴徒大声呵斥那些较小的孩子，使他们不敢再抱怨。|The politician tried to outbluster his opponent during the debate.  这位政治家试图在辩论中压制他的对手。|近义词：overpower, shout down, drown out|反义词：whisper, murmur
