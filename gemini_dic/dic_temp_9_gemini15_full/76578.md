gallicanism:/ˈɡælɪkənɪzəm/| n. | 加利肯主义；法国教会独立自主论 | The principles and practices of Gallicanism, advocating for the independence of the French Catholic Church from papal authority.  Gallicanism asserts the superiority of general councils over the Pope and emphasizes the autonomy of national churches. |The rise of Gallicanism in France was a direct challenge to papal authority.  加利肯主义在法国的兴起是对教皇权威的直接挑战。| 近义词：nationalism (in a religious context), conciliarism | 反义词：Ultramontanism, papal infallibility
