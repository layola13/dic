nonrepublican:/ˌnɒnˌrɪˈpʌblɪkən/| adj.|非共和党的；反共和党的|Not belonging to or supporting the Republican Party.  This term describes individuals, groups, or ideologies that are opposed to or outside of the Republican Party.|Example:  The nonrepublican voters overwhelmingly supported the proposed environmental protection bill. 非共和党选民压倒性地支持拟议的环境保护法案。|近义词: anti-Republican, non-GOP|反义词: Republican
