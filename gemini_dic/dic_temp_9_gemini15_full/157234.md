pinnace:/ˈpɪnəs/| n.|小帆船，轻型快艇|A small sailing vessel, typically a light, fast boat used for various purposes, including carrying passengers or goods.  It was often used as a tender to larger ships.  |例句：The admiral's pinnace sped towards the flagship.  海军上将的小帆船快速驶向旗舰。

The captain dispatched a pinnace to shore to fetch supplies. 船长派出一艘小帆船去岸上取补给。


近义词：lighter, shallop, launch|反义词：battleship, galleon


词源：源自古法语 *pinasse, 最终来自意大利语 *pinassa, 可能源自希腊语 πῖνος (pinos) "松树"，指其船体材料。
