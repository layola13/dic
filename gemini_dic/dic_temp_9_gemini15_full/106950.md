lacinia:/ləˈsɪniə/| n.|裂片；瓣；（花冠等的）裂片|A lacinia is a lobe or segment, especially of a petal or sepal.  It refers to a deeply divided or cut part of a larger structure, often found in plants or other biological structures.|例句：The flower's laciniae were delicately fringed. 这朵花的花瓣裂片边缘呈精细的流苏状。|The deeply dissected leaves had numerous laciniae. 深裂的叶子有很多裂片。|近义词：lobe, segment, division|反义词：undivided, entire
