memorizer:/ˈmeməraɪzər/| n. |记忆者；记忆器；助记工具 | A person or thing that memorizes; a device that aids in memorization.  | He's a prodigious memorizer, able to recite entire books from memory. 他是一位惊人的记忆高手，能够背诵整本书籍。  The software acts as a memorizer, helping students learn vocabulary.  这个软件作为一个记忆辅助工具，帮助学生学习词汇。|  learner, student, recorder | forgetter (反义词较为宽泛，没有完全对应的词)|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He memorizes vocabulary every day. = 他每天都记住词汇。
She memorizes poems for her performance. = 她为了表演而记忆诗歌。


一般过去时:
I memorized the speech for the competition. = 我为了比赛而记住了演讲稿。
He memorized the phone number. = 他记住了电话号码。


现在完成时:
She has memorized all the lines for the play. = 她已经记住了戏剧的所有台词。
We have memorized the lyrics to that song. = 我们已经记住了那首歌的歌词。


过去完成时:
I had memorized the poem before the recital. = 我在朗诵之前已经记住了这首诗。
She had memorized the script by the time of the audition. = 在试镜的时候，她已经记住了剧本。


将来时:
I will memorize the address before I go. = 我去之前会记住地址。
They will memorize the parts for the play next week. = 他们下周会记住戏剧的角色。


虚拟语气:
If I had memorized the material, I would have done better on the test. = 如果我记住了材料，考试成绩会更好。
I wish I had memorized the directions. = 我真希望我记住了路线。


被动语态:
The lines were memorized by the actors. = 台词被演员们记住了。
The vocabulary list is often memorized by students. = 词汇表经常被学生们记住。


疑问句:
Did you memorize the password? = 你记住了密码吗？
Have you memorized the capital cities of all the states? = 你记住了所有州的首府吗？


否定句:
I couldn't memorize the long list of names. = 我记不住这么长的名字列表。
She didn't memorize the whole chapter. = 她没有记住整章内容。
