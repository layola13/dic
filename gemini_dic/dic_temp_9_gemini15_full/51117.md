despised:/dɪˈspaɪzd/| v. |鄙视，厌恶，蔑视 |to feel or show contempt or disdain for (someone or something).  |例句：He despised liars and cheats. 他鄙视说谎者和骗子。|例句：She despised the way he treated her. 她鄙视他对待她的方式。|近义词：abhor, detest, scorn, loathe |反义词：adore, admire, cherish, respect |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They despise his arrogance. = 他们鄙视他的傲慢。
I despise dishonesty in all its forms. = 我鄙视一切形式的不诚实。


一般过去时:
She despised him for his betrayal. = 她因为他的背叛而鄙视他。
We despised the dictator's cruelty. = 我们鄙视独裁者的残酷。


现在完成时:
I have despised his behavior for years. = 多年来我一直鄙视他的行为。
They have despised injustice since childhood. = 从小他们就鄙视不公正。


过去完成时:
He had despised her for her snobbishness before they even met. = 甚至在他们见面之前，他就因为她的势利而鄙视她。
We had despised his lies long before the truth came out. = 早在真相大白之前，我们就鄙视他的谎言。


将来时:
They will despise him for his actions. = 他们会因为他的行为而鄙视他。
I will always despise cruelty to animals. = 我将永远鄙视虐待动物的行为。


虚拟语气:
I would despise myself if I did that. = 如果我那样做了，我会鄙视自己。
If she knew the truth, she would despise him. = 如果她知道真相，她会鄙视他。


被动语态:
His actions were despised by everyone. = 他的行为受到所有人的鄙视。
The politician's lies were widely despised. = 这位政治家的谎言受到了广泛的鄙视。


疑问句:
Do you despise him? = 你鄙视他吗？
Why do they despise her? = 他们为什么鄙视她？


否定句:
I do not despise him, I just disagree with him. = 我并不鄙视他，我只是不同意他的观点。
They did not despise her initially, but changed their minds later. = 他们最初并不鄙视她，但后来改变了主意。


PHRASE->
be despised by = 被…所鄙视
feel despised = 感到被鄙视
despised enemy = 令人憎恨的敌人
utterly despised = 极度鄙视
deeply despised = 深深地鄙视
