rarest:/ˈrɛərɪst/ | adj. | 最稀有的，最罕见的 |  Most infrequent or uncommon; exceeding rare.  It describes something that is exceptionally uncommon or infrequent, often to the point of being almost unique. |  The rarest stamp in my collection is worth a fortune.  我收藏中最稀有的邮票价值不菲。


The rarest bird in this region is the ivory-billed woodpecker. 这个地区最稀有的鸟是象牙喙啄木鸟。

近义词：most uncommon, most unusual, most scarce, most exceptional

反义词：commonest, most frequent, most abundant, most ordinary


词性：形容词
