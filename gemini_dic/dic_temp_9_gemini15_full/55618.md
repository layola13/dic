diswarren:/dɪsˈwɒrən/| n. |混乱的兔子窝；混乱不堪的地方| A diswarren is a place that is extremely disorganized and chaotic, often likened to a rabbit warren due to its complexity and confusing nature.  It suggests a sense of being overwhelmed and lost within a tangled mess.| The old attic was a diswarren of forgotten treasures and broken furniture.  旧阁楼里堆满了被遗忘的珍宝和破旧的家具，一片狼藉。|近义词：tangle, mess, jumble, chaos|反义词：order, organization, tidiness|名词
