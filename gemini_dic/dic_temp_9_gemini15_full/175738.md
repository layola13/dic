rapporteur:/ˌræpɔːrˈtɜːr/| n. |报告员，记录员，发言人|A person who makes a report, especially on behalf of a committee or organization.  A rapporteur summarizes discussions and prepares a report. |The rapporteur summarized the key points of the meeting.  报告员总结了会议要点。The committee appointed a rapporteur to draft the final report.  委员会任命了一名报告员来起草最终报告。|reporter, summarizer| n/a


USAGE->

The rapporteur presents the report. = 报告员提交报告。
