inconsequentiality:/ˌɪnkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃiˈælɪti/| n. |不重要；无关紧要；微不足道|The inconsequentiality of his remarks was striking.  他的话语的无关紧要令人吃惊。|lack of importance, insignificance, triviality|importance, significance, relevance|名词


英文解析:The state or quality of being inconsequential; lack of importance or significance.  指不重要的状态或性质；缺乏重要性或意义。


例句：

The inconsequentiality of his actions surprised everyone.  他行为的微不足道让每个人都感到惊讶。

The professor dismissed the student's question due to its inconsequentiality to the lecture. 教授因为这个问题与讲座无关而忽略了学生的提问。


近义词：

unimportance, insignificance, triviality, irrelevance, immateriality


反义词：

importance, significance, relevance, consequence, materiality
