magnums'/ˈmæɡnəm/| n.|大瓶酒，大作，巨作|A magnum is a large bottle of wine, typically holding 1.5 liters.  The term can also refer to a large, impressive, or significant work of art or achievement.|例句：He brought a magnum of champagne to the party. 他带了一大瓶香槟酒去参加派对。|近义词：large bottle, masterpiece, great work|反义词：small bottle, minor work
