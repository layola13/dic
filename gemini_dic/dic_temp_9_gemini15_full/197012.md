shaking: /ˈʃeɪkɪŋ/ | v. | 颤抖，震动；摇动 | The act of shaking involves rapid, involuntary movements or the act of moving something vigorously to and fro.  It can describe a physical tremor or the action of moving something. | 例句：My hands were shaking with fear.我的手害怕得颤抖。| 例句：She was shaking the bottle to mix the ingredients.她在摇晃瓶子以混合成分。| 近义词：trembling, quivering, vibrating, jiggling | 反义词：still, motionless, stable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shakes his head in disbelief. = 他难以置信地摇摇头。
The machine shakes violently. = 机器剧烈震动。


一般过去时:
The earthquake shook the city. = 地震震动了这座城市。
She shook her head sadly. = 她伤心地摇了摇头。


现在完成时:
The building has been shaking all day. = 这座建筑物整天都在震动。
He has shaken my hand many times.  = 他多次和我握手。


过去完成时:
The ground had shaken before the tremors started. = 地震前地面已经震动了。
She had shaken the bottle before opening it. = 她打开瓶子前已经摇晃过了。


将来时:
The building will shake during the storm. = 这座建筑物会在暴风雨中摇晃。
I will shake hands with him tomorrow. = 我明天将和他握手。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't shake his hand. = 如果我是你，我不会和他握手。
I wish I hadn't shaken that bottle so hard. = 我真希望我没有那么用力摇晃那个瓶子。


被动语态:
The picture was shaken off the wall. = 照片被震落了墙。
The drink is shaken, not stirred. = 这饮料是摇晃的，而不是搅拌的。


疑问句:
Is your hand shaking? = 你的手在颤抖吗？
Are you shaking the mixture well? = 你把混合物摇匀了吗？


否定句:
The ground didn't shake. = 地面没有震动。
I don't shake hands with strangers. = 我不和陌生人握手。
