selflessnesses:/ˈsɛlfliːsnɪsɪz/| n. |无私行为（复数）|The plural form of "selflessness," referring to multiple instances or types of selfless acts or behavior characterized by a lack of selfishness and concern for the well-being of others.  It emphasizes the multiplicity of such actions.|例句：Her selflessnesses throughout her life were legendary, from volunteering at the homeless shelter to donating her kidney to a stranger.  她一生中的许多无私行为都成了传奇，从在无家可归者收容所做志愿者到向陌生人捐献肾脏。

例句：The doctor's selflessnesses extended beyond his professional duties, encompassing acts of charity and community service.  这位医生的无私奉献超出了他的职业职责，包括慈善和社区服务行为。


近义词：acts of altruism, sacrifices, charitable deeds, benevolent acts

反义词：selfishness, egotism, greed, self-interest

词性:名词(复数)
