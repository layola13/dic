elamite:/ˈiːleɪmaɪt/| adj. n.|埃兰的；埃兰语；埃兰人|Of or relating to Elam, an ancient kingdom in southwestern Iran.  The term can refer to the people, their language, or their culture. |例句：The museum houses a remarkable collection of Elamite artifacts.  博物馆收藏了大量非凡的埃兰文物。

例句：Elamite cuneiform script is quite different from Babylonian.  埃兰楔形文字与巴比伦楔形文字大相径庭。


近义词：Elamitic (less common)

反义词：无


词性：形容词，名词
