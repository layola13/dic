misunderstandings:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstændɪŋz/| n.|误解，误会|A misunderstanding is a failure to understand something correctly.  It can be caused by a lack of clear communication, different interpretations of words or actions, or cultural differences.|例句：The argument stemmed from a simple misunderstanding.  这场争论源于一个简单的误会。|例句：Their misunderstandings led to a breakdown in their relationship. 他们的误解导致了他们关系的破裂。|近义词：misinterpretations, misconstructions, confusion|反义词：understanding, clarity
