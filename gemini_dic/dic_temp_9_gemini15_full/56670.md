doodle:/ˈduːdəl/ | v. n. |涂鸦；随意乱画； |to draw or scribble absentmindedly | I doodled in my notebook during the boring lecture.  我在无聊的讲座上在笔记本上涂鸦。|近义词：scribble, sketch |反义词：paint meticulously


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He doodles on his notepad while he's on the phone. = 他打电话时在记事本上涂鸦。
She doodles flowers in her notebook. = 她在笔记本上随意画些花。


一般过去时:
I doodled a funny face during the meeting. = 我在会议期间涂鸦了一张滑稽的脸。
He doodled all over his textbook. = 他在他的教科书上到处乱涂乱画。


现在完成时:
She has doodled all over the margins of her report. = 她已经在她报告的页边空白处涂满了画。
He has doodled since he was a child. = 他从小就喜欢涂鸦。


过去完成时:
I had doodled a map of my dream house before I started designing it. = 在我开始设计之前，我已经涂鸦了一张我的梦想之家的地图。
She had doodled numerous pictures before she started painting. = 在她开始绘画之前，她已经涂鸦了很多幅画。


将来时:
I will doodle some designs for the new website. = 我将为新的网站涂鸦一些设计。
He will doodle during the presentation. = 他将在演示期间涂鸦。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would doodle more often. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更经常地涂鸦。
I wish I could doodle as well as she can. = 我希望我的涂鸦技术和她一样好。


被动语态:
The margins of the book were doodled on. = 书的页边空白处被涂鸦了。
The page was doodled with flowers and hearts. = 这页纸上涂满了花朵和心形图案。


疑问句:
What are you doodling? = 你在涂鸦什么？
Did you doodle in class? = 你上课时涂鸦了吗？


否定句:
He doesn't doodle often. = 他不经常涂鸦。
I didn't doodle during the test. = 我考试时没有涂鸦。

doodle around:随意涂鸦
