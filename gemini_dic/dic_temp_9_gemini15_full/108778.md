leases:/ˈliːsiz/| n. |租赁；租约；租期|A lease is a contract granting use or occupation of property during a specified time for a specified payment.|例句：The lease on my apartment expires next month. 我的公寓租约下个月到期。|例句：They signed a lease for a new office space. 他们签署了一份新办公室的租赁合同。|近义词：rental, tenancy, hire|反义词：ownership, purchase

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He leases a car every year. = 他每年租一辆车。
She leases her apartment to a student. = 她把公寓租给了一个学生。

一般过去时:
They leased the land for farming. = 他们租赁了这块土地用于耕种。
We leased a car for our vacation. = 我们租了一辆车度假。

现在完成时:
We have leased this property for five years. = 我们已经租赁了这处房产五年了。
She has leased several apartments in the past. = 她过去租过几套公寓。

过去完成时:
They had leased the building before the company went bankrupt. = 公司破产前，他们已经租赁了那栋楼。
He had leased the equipment for the project. = 他已经为项目租赁了设备。

将来时:
We will lease a new office next year. = 我们明年将租赁一个新的办公室。
They will lease the property to the highest bidder. = 他们将把房产租赁给出价最高的人。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would lease a bigger apartment. = 如果我有更多钱，我会租一套更大的公寓。
I wish I could lease a car without a down payment. = 我希望我能不用首付就租一辆车。

被动语态:
The apartment was leased quickly. = 这套公寓很快就被租出去了。
The land is being leased to a developer. = 这块土地正在被租赁给一个开发商。

疑问句:
Does she lease a car or own one? = 她租车还是自己买车？
Are you leasing this space from the landlord? = 你是从房东那里租用这个空间吗？

否定句:
He doesn't lease a car; he owns one. = 他不租车，他有一辆自己的车。
They didn't lease the property because of the high rent. = 因为租金太高，他们没有租赁这处房产。


PHRASE->
lease agreement = 租赁协议
lease term = 租赁期限
break a lease = 违反租赁合同
lease option = 租赁期权
assign a lease = 转租
