matrons:/ˈmeɪtrənz/| n.|女家长；女看守；中年妇女|A matron is a woman who is in charge of a place, such as a hospital or school, or a married woman who is considered to be respectable and dignified.  It can also refer to an older woman, often one who is married.  The term often carries a slightly formal or old-fashioned connotation.|例句：The matron of the hospital was known for her kindness and efficiency. 医院的女院长以其善良和效率而闻名。

The elderly matron smiled warmly at the children.  这位上了年纪的女家长对孩子们报以温暖的微笑。|近义词：housewife, caretaker, supervisor|反义词：young woman, girl
