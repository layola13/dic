reenlighten:/ˌriːɪnˈlaɪtn/| vt.|重新启发；重新阐明；使…重获光明|To enlighten again; to shed new light on something previously understood or explained.  It implies a deeper or more complete understanding than the initial explanation.|例句：The professor's new research reenlightened the class on the complex subject. 教授的新研究使课堂上对这个复杂主题有了新的认识。
The revised manuscript reenlightened the readers on the author's intentions. 修订后的手稿使读者重新理解了作者的意图。|近义词：reilluminate, clarify, elucidate, illuminate|反义词：obscure, confuse, bewilder|动词
