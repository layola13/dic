plugging:/ˈplʌɡɪŋ/ | n. |插入；堵塞；连接；（非正式）努力工作|The act of inserting something into a hole or socket, or the state of being inserted.  It can also refer to the act of blocking something, or to the connection of something to a system. In informal use, it refers to diligent work or effort.|例句：He was busy plugging away at his computer. 他正埋头苦干地用他的电脑工作。|例句：The plumber carefully plugged the leak in the pipe. 水管工仔细地堵住了管道漏水的地方。|近义词：inserting, connecting, blocking, working hard|反义词：unplugging, disconnecting, opening


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is plugging the gap in the market. = 他正在填补市场空白。
They are plugging away at their studies. = 他们正在努力学习。


一般过去时:
She plugged the hole with a rag. = 她用抹布堵住了洞。

I have plugged all the leaks. = 我已经堵住了所有的漏洞。
They have been plugging away at the project for months. = 几个月来他们一直在努力完成这个项目。


过去完成时:

将来时:
