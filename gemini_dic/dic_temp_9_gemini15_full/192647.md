scutch:/skʌtʃ/| vt. n.| 打（麻、亚麻等）; 粗制滥造| To beat or strike repeatedly; to separate the fibres of (flax or hemp) by beating. |例句：He scutched the flax to prepare it for spinning. 他打麻以准备纺纱。  |例句：The rough-spun cloth was clearly scutched together. 这粗制滥造的布显然是粗制滥造的。|近义词：beat, thrash, hack|反义词：refine, polish|动词
