rebuke:/rɪˈbjuːk/ | vt. | 斥责；谴责；训斥 | To express sharp disapproval or criticism of (someone) because of their behavior or actions.  | 例句：The teacher rebuked the student for his disruptive behavior. 老师因学生的扰乱行为而斥责了他。


例句：Her boss rebuked her for her unprofessional conduct. 她的老板因她的不专业行为而斥责了她。


近义词：reprimand, scold, chide, reproach, admonish


反义词：praise, commend, applaud


词性：动词
