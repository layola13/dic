lunar-days:/ˈluːnər deɪz/| n.|阴历日，农历日 |Lunar days refer to the days in a lunar calendar, which is a calendar based on the monthly cycles of the Moon's phases.  These days are different from the days in a solar calendar (like the Gregorian calendar).|The festival falls on the 15th lunar day of the eighth month.|近义词：moon days|反义词：solar days
