enlister:/ɪnˈlɪstər/ | n. |招募者，应征者 | A person who enlists others, or someone who joins the military.  | Example: The army sergeant was a skilled enlister, attracting many recruits.  军队中士是一位熟练的招募者，吸引了许多新兵。 |近义词: recruiter, recruit, conscript | 反义词: deserter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enlists new members every year. = 他每年都招募新成员。
The company enlists the help of volunteers. = 公司寻求志愿者的帮助。


一般过去时:
She enlisted in the army after college. = 她大学毕业后参军了。
They enlisted the support of local businesses.  = 他们争取到了当地企业的支持。


现在完成时:
He has enlisted in the marines for five years. = 他已经参军五年了（海军陆战队）。
The organization has enlisted the aid of many celebrities. = 该组织已经争取到了许多名人的帮助。


过去完成时:
Before the war began, he had already enlisted. = 在战争开始之前，他已经参军了。
They had enlisted the help of experts before starting the project. = 在开始项目之前，他们已经寻求了专家的帮助。


将来时:
She will enlist in the air force next year. = 她明年将加入空军。
The company will enlist the services of a consultant. = 公司将聘请一位顾问的服务。


虚拟语气:
If he were younger, he would enlist. = 如果他年轻一些，他会参军。
I wish I had enlisted sooner. = 我希望我早点参军。


被动语态:
Many young people are enlisted every year. = 每年都有许多年轻人参军。
The project's success was largely due to the resources that were enlisted. = 项目的成功很大程度上归功于所争取到的资源。


疑问句:
Will he enlist in the navy? = 他会加入海军吗？
Have they enlisted enough volunteers? = 他们招募到足够的志愿者了吗？


否定句:
He did not enlist in the army. = 他没有参军。
The company did not enlist the services of an accountant. = 公司没有聘请会计师的服务。


PHRASE->
enlist the help of  = 寻求…的帮助
enlist support for = 为…争取支持
enlist in the army = 参军
enlist someone's aid = 寻求某人的帮助
enlist the services of = 聘请…的服务
