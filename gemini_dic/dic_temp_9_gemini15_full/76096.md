furrowing:/ˈfɜːroʊɪŋ/| v.| 皱眉；使起皱纹 |The act of making wrinkles or furrows, especially in the brow.  It often indicates displeasure, worry, or concentration. |例句：He sat furrowing his brow in concentration. 他皱着眉头全神贯注。


例句：The farmer was furrowing the field with his plough. 农民正用犁耕地。


近义词：creasing, wrinkling, corrugating |反义词：smoothing, relaxing


词性:动词
