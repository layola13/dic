demarcated:/ˈdiːmɑːrkeɪtɪd/| adj.|划定的，界定的，标明的|Marked or divided by boundaries or limits.  The area was clearly demarcated by a fence.  这个区域由篱笆清晰地划分。|近义词：defined, delimited, bounded, separated |反义词：undefined, unseparated, blended

词源:
From de- + mark + -ate.  The prefix "de-" indicates completion or thoroughness. "Mark" refers to making a visible sign or boundary. "-ate" is a verb suffix indicating to make or cause to become.  Therefore, "demarcate" means to thoroughly mark or establish boundaries.
