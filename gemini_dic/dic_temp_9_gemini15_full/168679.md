procrastination:/prəˌkræstɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |拖延；耽搁 |The act of delaying or postponing something.  It often involves putting off tasks that need to be done, despite knowing that it will likely lead to negative consequences.|例句：His procrastination led to a last-minute rush to complete the project.  他的拖延导致了最后时刻完成项目的匆忙。|例句：She suffers from chronic procrastination and constantly misses deadlines. 她患有慢性拖延症，经常错过截止日期。|近义词：delay, postponement, deferment, putting off |反义词：promptness, diligence, expeditiousness |名词
