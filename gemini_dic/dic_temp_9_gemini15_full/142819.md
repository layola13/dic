out:/aʊt/ | adv. prep. adj. | 出，外面，外出 |  Away from the inside or center; externally. | 例句：The cat is out. 猫在外面。

The sun is out today. 今天太阳出来了。


近义词：outside, exterior, abroad | 反义词：in, inside


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He goes out every evening. 他每天晚上都出去。
She puts the trash out every Tuesday. 她每个星期二都把垃圾扔出去。


一般过去时:
They went out for dinner last night. 他们昨晚出去吃饭了。
I took the books out of the library. 我把书从图书馆借出来了。


现在完成时:
The milk has gone out. 牛奶用完了。
I have been out all day. 我整天都在外面。


过去完成时:
He had gone out before I arrived. 我到的时候他已经出去了。
She had taken the trash out before the rain started. 下雨之前她已经把垃圾扔出去了。


将来时:
We will go out to celebrate tomorrow. 我们明天将出去庆祝。
They will be out all day tomorrow. 他们明天一整天都会在外面。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't go out tonight. 如果我是你，我今晚不会出去。
I wish I could go out this weekend. 我真希望这个周末我能出去。


被动语态:
The lights were put out. 灯被关掉了。
The information was leaked out. 信息被泄露了。


疑问句:
Is the cat out? 猫在外面吗？
Are you going out tonight? 你今晚要出去吗？


否定句:
He didn't go out last night. 他昨晚没出去。
The lights are not out yet. 灯还没关。


PHRASE->
find out: 发现，查明
check out: 检查，结账
work out: 解决，锻炼
figure out: 弄明白，算出
drop out: 退学，退出
