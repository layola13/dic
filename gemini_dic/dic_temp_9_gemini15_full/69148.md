favourite:/ˈfeɪvərɪt/| n. adj.| 最喜欢的；宠儿 | a person or thing that is preferred above all others | My favourite colour is blue.  我的最喜欢的颜色是蓝色。|近义词：favorite, preferred, darling | 反义词：least favourite, disliked


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My favourite food is pizza. = 我最喜欢的食物是披萨。
His favourite hobby is reading. = 他最喜欢的爱好是阅读。


一般过去时:
Her favourite book was "Pride and Prejudice". = 她最喜欢的书是《傲慢与偏见》。
My favourite movie last year was "Inception". = 我去年最喜欢的电影是《盗梦空间》。


现在完成时:
My favourite band has released a new album. = 我最喜欢的乐队发行了一张新专辑。
He has always been his mother's favourite. = 他一直是他母亲最喜欢的孩子。


过去完成时:
Before the trip, my favourite toy had been lost. = 旅行之前，我最喜欢的玩具丢了。
She had already finished her favourite book before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，她已经读完了她最喜欢的书。


将来时:
I think my favourite sport will always be basketball. = 我认为我喜欢的运动将永远是篮球。
Her favourite restaurant will be opening a new branch next month. = 她最喜欢的餐厅下个月将开设新分店。


虚拟语气:
If I had to choose, my favourite dessert would be chocolate cake. = 如果我必须选择，我最喜欢的甜点将是巧克力蛋糕。
I wish my favourite singer would come to my city. = 我希望我最喜欢的歌手能来我的城市。


被动语态:
His painting was chosen as the favourite by the judges. = 他的画被评委选为最喜欢的。
The blue dress was declared her favourite by the fashion critic. = 蓝色连衣裙被时尚评论家宣布为她最喜欢的。


疑问句:
What's your favourite animal? = 你最喜欢的动物是什么？
What is her favourite subject at school? = 她学校最喜欢的科目是什么？


否定句:
That's not my favourite song. = 那不是我最喜欢的歌。
This isn't my favourite colour; I prefer green. = 这不是我最喜欢的颜色；我更喜欢绿色。
