词源:
The word "degerminator" is a combination of "de-" (meaning "to remove" or "to deprive of") and "germ" (meaning "a microorganism, especially one that causes disease"), plus the suffix "-inator" (forming nouns denoting an agent or device).  Therefore, it literally means "a device or agent that removes germs".
