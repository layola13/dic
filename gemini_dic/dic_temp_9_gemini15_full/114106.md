magnolia:/mæɡˈnoʊliə/| n. |木兰花；木兰树|A magnolia is a type of flowering tree or shrub known for its large, fragrant flowers.  They are popular ornamental plants. |例句：The magnolia tree in our garden is in full bloom.  我们花园里的木兰树正盛开着。|例句：The perfume of the magnolia blossoms filled the air. 木兰花的香味充满了空气。|近义词：tulip tree (某些种类), flowering tree|反义词：无
