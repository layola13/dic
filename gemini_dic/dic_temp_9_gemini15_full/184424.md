respooling:/ˌriːˈspuːlɪŋ/| v.|重新卷绕，重新绕线|The act of winding something, typically yarn or cable, onto a spool again.  This often implies a previous unwinding or a need for reorganization.  |例句：The technician is respooling the fiber optic cable after the repair. 技师正在修理后重新绕线光纤电缆。

例句：After the presentation, she respooled the projector cable neatly. 演示结束后，她整齐地重新卷起了投影仪电缆。


近义词：rewinding, recoiling, re-winding

反义词：unwinding, unspooling


词性:动词
