endenizen:/ɪnˈdɛzənən/| v.|使成为居民；使归化；使习惯于|To make someone a citizen of a place; to accustom someone to a place or situation. |例句：The king endenizened the foreign merchants, granting them citizenship and protection. 国王使外国商人成为臣民，给予他们公民身份和保护。  例句：After years of living abroad, she gradually endenizened herself to the local culture. 在国外生活多年后，她逐渐习惯了当地的文化。|近义词：naturalize, acclimatize, acculturate|反义词：expatriate, alienate
