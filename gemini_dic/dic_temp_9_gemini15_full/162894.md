precensuses:/ˈpriːsɛnsəsɪz/| n.| 前期人口普查 |The word "precensuses" refers to censuses taken before a particular census. It's a plural noun formed by adding "-es" to the word "precensus."  A precensus is a census conducted before another, usually larger or more official, census.  They might be smaller-scale surveys or preliminary data gathering efforts used to inform the planning and execution of a main census.| Example:  The researchers analyzed the data from several precensuses to refine their methodology for the national census.  前期人口普查的研究人员分析了几个前期人口普查的数据，以改进他们的人口普查方法。|近义词：prior censuses, preliminary surveys|反义词：postcensuses|名词
