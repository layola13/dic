refortification:/ˌriːˌfɔːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |再强化；再设防；补充营养|The process of strengthening or fortifying something again.  It often refers to adding nutrients back into food after processing or to reinforcing a structure that has been weakened.|例句：The refortification of the city walls was a top priority after the siege.  城市围攻后，城墙的再次加固是重中之重。

例句：The doctor recommended a diet plan including refortification with vitamins and minerals. 医生建议一个包含补充维生素和矿物质的饮食计划。


近义词：reinforcement, strengthening, replenishment|反义词：weakening, depletion, deterioration
