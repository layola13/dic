词性：形容词


Etymology:

The word "manliest" derives from the Old English word "mann," meaning "man."  The suffix "-liest" is a superlative suffix indicating the highest degree of the quality described by the base word.  Therefore, "manliest" signifies "most like a man" in terms of traditional masculine traits.  The evolution of the word reflects the changing societal perceptions of masculinity over time.
