importune:/ɪmˈpɔːrtjuːn/ | vt. |恳求；纠缠；强求 | To ask (someone) pressingly and persistently for or to do something.  |例句：He importuned her to lend him money. 他不断恳求她借给他钱。

例句：She importuned her boss for a raise. 她一再请求老板加薪。


近义词：beseech, implore, solicit, urge, dun | 反义词：refuse, deny, reject | 词性：动词


词源:

From Old French *importuner (13th century), from Latin importunus "unseasonable, inconvenient, troublesome," from in- "not" + portus "harbor, haven."  The sense shifted from "unwelcome" to "insistent."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He importunes his friends for favors. = 他不断麻烦朋友帮忙。
She importunes her parents for a new car. = 她不断向父母要一辆新车。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
He has importuned me for weeks about this. =  他已经就这事纠缠我几周了。
She has importuned her colleagues for support. = 她一直恳求同事的支持。


过去完成时:
He had importuned her for months before she finally agreed. =  在他最终得到同意之前，他已经纠缠了她几个月。
She had importuned the bank for a loan before she finally gave up. = 在她最终放弃之前，她已经向银行恳求贷款很长时间了。
