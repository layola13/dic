refreshing:/rɪˈfreʃɪŋ/| adj.|令人耳目一新的；清爽的；提神的|Having a pleasantly stimulating effect; invigorating.  It suggests a feeling of renewed energy and vitality, often associated with coolness or a change from something dull or tiring.  |例句：The cool breeze was refreshing after the hot day. 炎热的一天过后，凉爽的微风令人神清气爽。

例句：The new marketing strategy is refreshing and innovative. 新的营销策略令人耳目一新且富有创新性。


近义词：invigorating, stimulating, revitalizing, refreshing, bracing, cheering


反义词：dull, boring, tiring, exhausting, dispiriting


词性：形容词
