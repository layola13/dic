phonetist: /fəˈnɛtɪst/ | n. |语音学家|A phonetist is a specialist in phonetics, the scientific study of speech sounds. They study the production, transmission, and perception of speech sounds.  Phonetists might work in areas such as speech therapy, language teaching, or forensic linguistics. |例句：The phonetist analyzed the speaker's pronunciation to identify regional dialects.  语音学家分析了说话人的发音以识别方言。|近义词：phonetician, speech scientist |反义词：N/A
