candying: /ˈkændiɪŋ/ | v. | 糖衣；蜜饯；用糖浆煮 | To coat or preserve (food) with sugar.  The process involves cooking food in sugar syrup until it is coated and preserved. |

例句：

She enjoys candying her own fruit for holiday gifts. = 她喜欢自己做水果蜜饯作为节日礼物。

My grandmother taught me how to candy ginger. = 我奶奶教我如何做姜糖。


近义词：sugaring, glazing, preserving

反义词：unsugaring (not a common word, implies removing sugar coating)


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They candy apples every Halloween. = 他们每年万圣节都做糖苹果。
She candies ginger roots for her business. = 她为她的生意做蜜饯生姜根。


一般过去时:
He candied the pecans before baking the pie. = 他在烤馅饼之前把山核桃裹上了糖。
We candied the citrus peels last weekend. = 我们上周末做了蜜饯柑橘皮。


现在完成时:
I have candied several batches of fruit this year. = 今年我已经做了几批水果蜜饯。
She has candied her own cherries for years. = 她多年来一直自己做樱桃蜜饯。


过去完成时:
They had candied the yams before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经把山药做成了蜜饯。
She had candied the peel long before the cake was finished. = 蛋糕做好很久之前，她就做好了蜜饯果皮。


将来时:
We will candy the oranges for the Christmas market. = 我们将为圣诞市场做橘子蜜饯。
He will candy the nuts for the upcoming holiday. = 他将为即将到来的节日做坚果蜜饯。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would candy the grapefruit. = 如果我有更多时间，我会做柚子蜜饯。
I wish I had candied the sweet potatoes for Thanksgiving. = 我希望感恩节的时候我做了蜜饯红薯。


被动语态:
The apples were candied perfectly. = 苹果被完美地做成了糖衣。
The ginger is being candied slowly over low heat. = 生姜正在小火慢煮成蜜饯。


疑问句:

否定句:

They will not be candying the walnuts for the party. = 他们不会为派对做核桃蜜饯。
