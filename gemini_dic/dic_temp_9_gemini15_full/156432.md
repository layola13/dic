phytophagy:/faɪˈtɒpədʒi/ | n. |植食性；食草性 |The act or practice of feeding on plants.  It refers to the consumption of plant matter by an animal.  This can range from herbivores grazing on grasses to insects feeding on leaves. |Example:  The phytophagy of locusts can devastate entire crops.  蝗虫的植食性会摧毁整个庄稼。|近义词：herbivory, plant eating |反义词：carnivory, zoophagy
