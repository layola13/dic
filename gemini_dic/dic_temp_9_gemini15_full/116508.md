maternity:/məˈtɜːrnəti/| n. |产妇期；母性；产科；孕妇装|The state or period of being a mother, especially the period during and after childbirth.  It can also refer to the care provided to mothers and their babies, or to clothing designed for pregnant women.|例句：She enjoyed her maternity leave after giving birth. 她很享受产假。|例句：The hospital has a dedicated maternity ward. 这家医院设有专门的产科病房。|近义词：motherhood, motherhood,childbirth|反义词：paternity
