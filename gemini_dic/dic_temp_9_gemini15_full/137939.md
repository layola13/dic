nonwalking: /ˈnɑːnˌwɔːkɪŋ/ | adj. | 不走路的；不能走路的 | Not walking; unable to walk.  The term describes a state or condition of not engaging in the activity of walking, often due to inability or choice.  |


例句：

The nonwalking patient required a wheelchair. =  这位不能走路的病人需要轮椅。

The doctor assessed the child's nonwalking condition. = 医生评估了孩子的不能走路的情况。


近义词：immobile, sedentary, stationary, unmoving

反义词：walking, ambulatory

词性：形容词
