refilling:/ˌriːˈfɪlɪŋ/| v. n.| 再装满；补充；续杯 |The act of filling something again.  It can refer to the process of adding more of a substance to something that is already partially full, or to the substance itself that is used to refill something.|例句：I need to get a refilling for my coffee. 我需要续杯咖啡。

The printer needs refilling. 这台打印机需要加墨水。

近义词：replenishing, topping up, replenishment|反义词：emptying, draining

词性:动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refils the water bottle every morning. 他每天早上都会把水瓶装满水。
She regularly refills her prescription medication. 她定期补充处方药。


一般过去时:
I refilled the ink cartridge yesterday. 我昨天加了墨水。
They refilled the gas tank before the long drive. 他们在长途驾驶前加满了油箱。


现在完成时:
I have refilled the bird feeder twice today. 我今天已经给鸟食器添了两次食。
She has refilled her coffee cup several times. 她已经续了好几次咖啡了。


过去完成时:
He had refilled the tank before the engine started. 发动机启动之前，他已经加满了油箱。
They had refilled the water bottles before setting off on the hike. 他们在徒步旅行出发前已经把水瓶装满了水。


将来时:
I will refill the water cooler tomorrow. 我明天会把饮水机加满水。
She will refill the prescription next week. 她下周会重新开药。


虚拟语气:
If I had remembered, I would have refilled the car's gas tank. 如果我记得的话，我会把汽车的油箱加满。
I wish I had refilled my water bottle before the run. 我真希望跑步前我把水瓶装满了水。


被动语态:
The ink cartridge was refilled by the technician. 墨盒由技术人员加墨。
The gas tank is being refilled at the moment. 油箱正在加油。


疑问句:
Have you refilled the printer yet? 你给打印机加墨了吗？
Will they refill the water bottles for the meeting? 他们会为会议准备饮用水吗？


否定句:
I didn't refill the sugar bowl. 我没有给糖罐加糖。
She hasn't refilled her prescription yet. 她还没重新开药。


PHRASE->
refilling a prescription: 续方
refilling a tank: 加油，加水
refilling a bottle: 装满瓶子
refilling an ink cartridge: 加墨水
refilling a coffee cup: 续杯咖啡

词源:
The word "refilling" comes from the verb "refill," which is formed by adding the prefix "re-" (meaning "again") to the verb "fill."  The root "fill" has Old English origins.
