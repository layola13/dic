puppeteer:/ˈpʌpɪtɪər/| n. |木偶戏演员；操纵者；幕后操纵者|A puppeteer is a person who manipulates puppets in a puppet show.  They control the puppets' movements and voices to create a performance. |例句：The puppeteer skillfully manipulated the marionettes, bringing them to life. 木偶戏演员熟练地操纵着木偶，使它们栩栩如生。|例句：Some people believe that powerful corporations are the puppeteers of global politics. 一些人认为强大的公司是全球政治的幕后操纵者。|近义词：manipulator, controller, operator|反义词：puppet (in the sense of being controlled)
