signorina:/ˌsiɲɔːˈriːnɑː/|/siɲəˈriːnə/| n. |小姐；年轻女子 |A polite term of address for a young unmarried woman in Italian. It is equivalent to "Miss" in English.  |Example:  "Buongiorno, signorina." = "Good morning, Miss."

Example: The waiter addressed the young woman as "signorina". = 服务员称呼那位年轻女子为“小姐”。

近义词：miss, young lady, maiden

反义词： signora (Mrs., Madam)
