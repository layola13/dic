plagiarize:/ˈpleɪdʒəraɪz/| vt.|剽窃；抄袭|To steal and pass off (the ideas or words of another) as one's own; use (another's production) without crediting the source.|例句：He was accused of plagiarizing his dissertation. 他被指控剽窃了他的论文。|例句：The student plagiarized a passage from a well-known novel.  这个学生抄袭了一段著名小说中的文字。|近义词：copy, steal, appropriate, crib, pirate|反义词：originate, invent, create


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plagiarizes frequently. = 他经常剽窃。
She plagiarizes from online sources. = 她从网上抄袭资料。

一般过去时:
The student plagiarized the essay. = 这个学生抄袭了这篇论文。
He plagiarized extensively from various authors. = 他大量剽窃了各个作者的作品。

现在完成时:
She has plagiarized several times this semester. = 这学期她已经好几次抄袭了。
He has never plagiarized before. = 他以前从未剽窃过。

过去完成时:
The professor discovered that he had plagiarized the report. = 教授发现他抄袭了报告。
They realized they had plagiarized a significant portion of the book. = 他们意识到他们抄袭了该书的重要部分。

将来时:
He will plagiarize again if he doesn't change his habits. = 如果他不改掉坏习惯，他会再次剽窃。
She will likely plagiarize if she doesn't have enough time to complete the assignment. = 如果她没有足够的时间完成作业，她很可能会抄袭。

虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:
