mailer:/ˈmeɪlər/| n.|邮寄者；邮递员；邮寄广告；邮件发送者|A person or thing that mails something.  It can refer to a person who sends mail, a machine that sends mail, or an advertising piece sent through the mail.|例句：The mailer delivered the package promptly. 邮递员及时送来了包裹。|例句：The company sent out a mailer announcing its new product. 公司寄出了宣传新产品的广告邮件。|近义词：sender, advertiser, distributor|反义词：receiver, recipient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mails the letters every day. = 他每天都邮寄信件。
The company mails out newsletters monthly. = 公司每月邮寄简报。


一般过去时:
She mailed the package yesterday. = 她昨天寄了包裹。
They mailed the invitations last week. = 他们上周寄出了邀请函。


现在完成时:
I have mailed all the applications. = 我已经寄出了所有的申请表。
He has mailed the report to his boss. = 他已将报告寄给了老板。


过去完成时:
She had mailed the letter before he called. = 他打电话之前，她已经寄了信。
They had mailed the package before the deadline. = 截止日期前，他们已经寄出了包裹。


将来时:
I will mail the documents tomorrow. = 我明天将邮寄文件。
She will mail the gift next week. = 她下周会寄送礼物。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would mail the letter today. = 如果我有时间，我今天就会寄信。
I wish I had mailed the package earlier. = 我真希望早点寄出包裹。


被动语态:
The package was mailed yesterday. = 包裹昨天寄出了。
The letters are mailed daily. = 信件每天都邮寄。


疑问句:
Did you mail the letter? = 你寄信了吗？
When will the package be mailed? = 包裹什么时候寄出？


否定句:
I didn't mail the letter. = 我没有寄信。
She hasn't mailed the package yet. = 她还没有寄包裹。
