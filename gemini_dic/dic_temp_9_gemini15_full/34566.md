clearances:/ˈklɪərənsɪz/ | n. |许可；间隙；清除；批准 |The official permission or authorization for something to proceed.  A space between two objects. The act of removing something. |例句：The project requires several clearances before it can begin. 这个项目在开始之前需要几个许可。 The mechanic checked the clearances between the moving parts of the engine. 机械师检查了发动机活动部件之间的间隙。  The crew completed the clearances of the debris after the storm. 暴风雨过后，工作人员完成了碎片的清理工作。|近义词：approvals, permits, gaps, spaces, removals |反义词：obstructions, blockages, denials
