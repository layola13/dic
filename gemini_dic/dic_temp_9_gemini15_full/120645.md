mis-registers:/ˌmɪsˈrɛdʒɪstərz/| v.|错误登记，错误注册|To record or register incorrectly; to incorrectly identify or categorize something.  The term often implies a systematic or repeated error, not just a single mistake.|例句：The software mis-registers the user's location frequently, causing navigation errors. 软件经常错误登记用户的地理位置，导致导航错误。|The system mis-registers the incoming data, leading to inaccurate reports. 系统错误登记传入数据，导致报告不准确。|近义词：misrecord, misidentify, misclassify|反义词：correctly register, accurately record|动词
