diffuses:/dɪˈfjuːz/| vt. vi.|扩散；弥漫；传播；使模糊|to spread or cause to spread over a wide area or among a large number of people.|The scent of roses diffused through the garden. 玫瑰的香味弥漫在花园里。|The news diffused rapidly throughout the city.  这个消息迅速传遍全城。|近义词：spread, disseminate, disperse|反义词：concentrate, condense|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The perfume diffuses a pleasant scent. 香水散发出宜人的香味。
Heat diffuses throughout the room. 热量扩散到整个房间。

一般过去时:
The dye diffused slowly into the fabric. 染料慢慢渗透到织物中。
The rumor diffused quickly among the students. 谣言迅速在学生中传播。

现在完成时:
The news has diffused widely across the internet.  这个消息已经在互联网上广泛传播。
The color has diffused beautifully in the painting. 颜色在绘画中完美地晕染开了。

过去完成时:
The gas had diffused throughout the building before we noticed the leak.  在我们注意到泄漏之前，气体已经扩散到整个建筑物中了。
The information had diffused to such an extent that it was difficult to trace its origin. 信息已经传播到如此广泛的程度，以至于很难追溯其来源。

将来时:
The aroma will diffuse throughout the kitchen. 香味将弥漫在整个厨房中。
The technology will diffuse to other countries soon. 这项技术很快就会传播到其他国家。

虚拟语气:
If the information were to diffuse more rapidly, the response would be quicker. 如果信息传播得更快，反应也会更快。
I wish the paint would diffuse more evenly. 我希望油漆能更均匀地扩散。


被动语态:

疑问句:
Does the perfume diffuse quickly? 这款香水扩散得快吗？

否定句:

The message did not diffuse effectively to the intended audience.  信息未能有效地传达给目标受众。
