concussion:/kənˈkʌʃən/| n.|脑震荡；冲击；剧烈震动|A concussion is a type of traumatic brain injury caused by a bump, blow, or jolt to the head or body that causes the head and brain to move rapidly back and forth. This sudden movement can cause the brain to bounce around or twist in the skull, resulting in temporary disruption of brain function.|例句：He suffered a concussion after falling from his bike. 他从自行车上摔下来后脑震荡了。


例句：The earthquake caused a concussion to the building. 地震对建筑物造成了剧烈震动。|近义词：brain injury, head injury, trauma|反义词：None|词性:名词
