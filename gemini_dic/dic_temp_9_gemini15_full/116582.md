matriarchs:/ˈmeɪtriɑːrks/ | n. |女族长，女家长 | A matriarch is a woman who is the head of a family or tribe.  The term often implies a powerful and influential female figure who holds significant authority and respect within her community.  |例句：The matriarch of the family ruled the household with a firm but fair hand.  家族的女族长以坚定而公平的手段统治着这个家庭。

The matriarchs of the village gathered to discuss the upcoming harvest festival. 村里的女族长们聚集在一起讨论即将到来的丰收节。

近义词：female head, leader, chief, elder

反义词：patriarch
