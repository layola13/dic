non-fictitious:/ˌnɒn fɪkˈtɪʃəs/| adj. |非虚构的，真实的|Not imaginary or fictional; real or actual.  It refers to something based on fact rather than imagination.|例句：The book is based on non-fictitious events. 这本书是基于真实事件的。|例句：His account of the incident was entirely non-fictitious. 他对事件的描述完全是真实的。|近义词：real, factual, true, authentic |反义词：fictional, imaginary, fabricated, untrue
