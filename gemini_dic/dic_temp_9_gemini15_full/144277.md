outsourcing:/ˈaʊtˌsɔːrsɪŋ/| n. |外包；外判 |The act of obtaining goods or services from an outside or foreign supplier, in place of an internal source.  This is often done to reduce costs or to access specialized skills. |例句：Many companies outsource their customer service to call centers in other countries.  许多公司将其客户服务外包给其他国家的呼叫中心。

例句：Our company decided to outsource the software development to a more experienced team.  我们公司决定将软件开发外包给一个更有经验的团队。


近义词：contracting out, subcontracting, delegation


反义词：insourcing


词性: 名词
