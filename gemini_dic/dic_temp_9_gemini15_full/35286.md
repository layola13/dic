clutter:/ˈklʌtər/| n. v.|杂乱，混乱；使杂乱；塞满|To fill or cover with a large number of things in a way that is untidy or disorganized.|The desk was cluttered with papers.  桌子堆满了文件，一片混乱。 |近义词：mess, disorder, jumble |反义词：order, tidiness, neatness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The toys clutter the living room. = 玩具弄乱了客厅。
She clutters her desk with unnecessary items. = 她用不必要的东西弄乱了她的桌子。


一般过去时:
The storm cluttered the beach with debris. = 暴风雨把大量的残骸冲到了海滩上。
He cluttered the garage with old boxes. = 他把旧箱子堆满了车库。


现在完成时:
They have cluttered the attic with years of accumulated junk. = 他们把阁楼塞满了多年积累的垃圾。
We have cluttered the kitchen with dirty dishes. = 我们把厨房弄得乱七八糟，全是脏盘子。


过去完成时:
He had cluttered the room before his parents arrived. = 他在他父母到达之前就把房间弄乱了。
She had cluttered the shelves with countless books. = 她已经把书架塞满了无数的书。


将来时:
They will clutter the house with Christmas decorations. = 他们会用圣诞装饰品把房子弄得乱七八糟。
He will clutter the garden with his tools. = 他会把他的工具堆放在花园里，弄得乱七八糟。


虚拟语气:
If he didn't clutter his desk, he would find his work easier. = 如果他不把桌子弄得乱七八糟，他会发现工作更容易。
I wish I hadn't cluttered the room with so many things. = 我真希望我没有把房间塞满这么多东西。


被动语态:
The room was cluttered with old furniture. = 房间里堆满了旧家具。
The beach was cluttered by the storm. = 海滩被暴风雨弄得乱七八糟。


疑问句:
Is the room cluttered with things? = 房间里是不是堆满了东西？
Does this clutter bother you? = 这种杂乱会让你困扰吗？


否定句:
The room wasn't cluttered at all. = 房间一点也不乱。
He didn't clutter the space with unnecessary objects. = 他没有用不必要的东西弄乱空间。
