gem:/dʒɛm/| n. |宝石，珍宝；精华；优秀的人或事物 |A gem is a precious stone, especially one that has been cut and polished.  It can also refer to something excellent or valuable. |例句：The museum has a stunning collection of gems.  这个博物馆收藏了令人惊叹的宝石。

That novel is a real gem; I highly recommend it. 那部小说真是佳作；我强烈推荐。|近义词：jewel, treasure, masterpiece |反义词：rubbish, trash, worthless thing |名词
