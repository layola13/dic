cosmopolite:/ˈkɒzməpɒlaɪt/|/ˈkɑːzməpɑːlaɪt/| n. adj.|世界主义者；国际化的人；世界各地的人；世界性的；国际性的|A cosmopolite is a person who is at home in many places and cultures, often traveling widely and having a broad perspective on the world.  They are comfortable interacting with people from diverse backgrounds and embracing different ways of life.  Cosmopolitans often appreciate the richness and diversity of global cultures. |例句：She is a true cosmopolite, having lived in five different countries and speaking four languages fluently. 她是一位真正世界主义者，曾在五个不同的国家生活过，并能流利地说四种语言。

例句：The city is known for its cosmopolite atmosphere, attracting people from all over the world. 这座城市以其国际化的氛围而闻名，吸引着来自世界各地的人们。

近义词：internationalist, global citizen, world traveler, cosmopolitan (adj.)

反义词：provincial, insular, parochial, local, narrow-minded

词性: noun, adjective
