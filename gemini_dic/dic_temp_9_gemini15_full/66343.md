exculpatory: /ɪkˈskʌlpətɔːri/ | adj. | 辩白的；开脱罪责的 |  Tending to clear from guilt or blame; justifying.  |  The exculpatory evidence proved his innocence.  辩白的证据证明了他的清白。 |  justificatory, exonerative, vindicator | incriminatory, inculpatory | 形容词
