pinheads:/ˈpɪnˌhɛdz/| n.|蠢人，笨蛋；小脑袋|People with small heads, or figuratively, people considered to be stupid or foolish.  The term is often used informally and can be considered offensive.|例句：He called them pinheads for not understanding the simple instructions. 他称他们为蠢货，因为他们不明白简单的指示。


例句：Don't be such a pinhead; think before you act! 不要这么傻；行动前先思考！


近义词：idiots, fools, dunces, simpletons


反义词：geniuses, intellectuals, brilliant minds


词性：名词
