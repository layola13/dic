gene:/dʒiːn/ | n. |基因|A unit of heredity that is transferred from a parent to offspring and is held to determine some characteristic of the offspring.|例句：Scientists are studying the human gene to understand diseases. 科学家们正在研究人类基因以了解疾病。|近义词：allele, factor|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gene determines eye color. = 基因决定眼睛的颜色。
This gene controls plant height. = 这个基因控制植物的高度。

一般过去时:
Scientists discovered the gene in 2000. = 科学家们在2000年发现了这个基因。
That gene was identified last year. = 那个基因是去年被发现的。

现在完成时:
Researchers have identified several genes related to cancer. = 研究人员已经鉴定了几个与癌症相关的基因。
We have sequenced the entire genome, revealing many new genes. = 我们已经测序了整个基因组，发现了许多新的基因。

过去完成时:
They had already studied the gene before the experiment. = 他们在实验之前就已经研究过这个基因了。
Scientists had discovered the gene's function before its sequence was known. = 科学家们在知道它的序列之前就已经发现了这个基因的功能。


将来时:
Researchers will study the gene's interaction with other genes. = 研究人员将研究该基因与其他基因的相互作用。
We will sequence more genes to understand genetic diseases better. = 我们将测序更多的基因以更好地了解遗传疾病。

虚拟语气:
If this gene were mutated, the plant would be shorter. = 如果这个基因发生突变，植物就会矮一些。
I wish we knew more about the function of this gene. = 我希望我们对这个基因的功能了解更多。

被动语态:
The gene was cloned successfully. = 基因被成功克隆。
The gene's function is being investigated. = 基因的功能正在被研究。

疑问句:
What gene is responsible for this trait? = 哪个基因负责这种性状？
How many genes are involved in this process? = 在这个过程中有多少基因参与？

否定句:
This gene does not affect height. = 这个基因不影响身高。
The study did not find any mutations in this gene. = 这项研究没有发现该基因有任何突变。
