nonreceptively:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈsɛptɪvli/| adv.|非感受性地，无反应地|Not receptively; without receiving or responding.|The patient reacted nonreceptively to the treatment.  病人对治疗没有反应。|receptively,responsively|unresponsively, insensitively|副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  The usage is straightforward: it describes how an action is performed – without receptiveness or response.


例句：

He listened nonreceptively, his mind elsewhere. 他心不在焉地听着，没有反应。

She stared nonreceptively at the speaker. 她无动于衷地盯着演讲者。


PHRASE->

There are no common set phrases using "nonreceptively". Its usage is typically within a sentence to modify a verb.
