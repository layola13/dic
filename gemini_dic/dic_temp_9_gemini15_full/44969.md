crystallic: /krɪˈstælɪk/ | adj. | 晶体的；结晶的 | Relating to or resembling crystal; having the structure and form of a crystal.  The word describes something possessing the characteristics of a crystal, such as a regular, repeating atomic structure.  |


例句：

The crystallic structure of the mineral was clearly visible under the microscope. 显微镜下可以清楚地看到这种矿物的晶体结构。

The chemist analyzed the crystallic compounds found in the sample. 化学家分析了样品中发现的结晶化合物。


近义词：crystalline, crystal-like


反义词：amorphous, non-crystalline


词源：源自于单词"crystal"，加上后缀"-lic"，表示“类似于…的”或“有…性质的”。  "crystal"本身来源于古希腊语的"κρύσταλλος" (krustallos)，意思是“冰”，后来被用来指代透明的石英。
