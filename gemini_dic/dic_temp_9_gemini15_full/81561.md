graystone:/ˈɡreɪstoʊn/| n.|灰石；灰色石头|A graystone is a stone that is gray in color.  It can refer to various types of stone, including granite, limestone, and others, that exhibit a gray hue.  The term is often used descriptively rather than as a specific geological classification.|例句：The old church was built of graystone. 这座古老的教堂是用灰石建造的。|The old wall was made of rough graystone. 这堵旧墙是用粗糙的灰石砌成的。|近义词：greystone, gray rock, stone|反义词：brightly colored stone
