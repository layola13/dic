self-appointment:/ˌsɛlf əˈpɔɪntmənt/| n.|自我任命，自行委任|The act of appointing oneself to a position or role, often without authorization.  This can be seen as arrogant or presumptuous.|例句：His self-appointment as team leader was met with disapproval from other members. 他的自我任命为团队领导受到了其他成员的反对。

例句：The self-appointment of the CEO caused a major scandal within the company.  首席执行官的自我任命在公司内部引起了重大丑闻。


近义词：self-designation, usurpation, arrogation


反义词：appointment by others, election, selection


词性:名词
