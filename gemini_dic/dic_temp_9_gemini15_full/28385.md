The term is less common in modern chemistry, often replaced by more specific names like "methanol" or "alcohol."  It refers to compounds containing a hydroxyl group (-OH) attached to a carbon atom. | Example: Methanol, the simplest carbinol, is highly toxic.|近义词:alcohol, methanol|反义词:  None directly, but could consider ketones or aldehydes as contrasting functional groups.|
