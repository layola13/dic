fruitarianism:/ˌfruːtˈeəriənɪzəm/| n. |水果素食主义；果食主义 |The practice of eating only fruits, nuts, seeds, and other plant products that do not require killing the plant to harvest.  It is a stricter form of vegetarianism.  |Example:  Fruitarianism is a very restrictive diet and can be difficult to maintain due to nutritional deficiencies. 水果素食主义是一种非常严格的饮食方式，由于营养缺乏，很难坚持。|近义词：veganism, vegetarianism |反义词：carnivorism
