over-report:/ˌoʊvər rɪˈpɔːrt/| vt.|过分报道，夸大报道|To report something as being larger, more significant, or more frequent than it actually is.  |例句：The media tends to over-report on sensational news stories.媒体倾向于夸大耸人听闻的新闻事件的报道。
The company over-reported its profits to attract investors.这家公司夸大其利润以吸引投资者。|近义词：exaggerate, inflate, embellish|反义词：underreport, downplay, minimize|动词
