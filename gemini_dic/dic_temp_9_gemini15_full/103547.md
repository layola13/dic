jogging:/ˈdʒɒɡɪŋ/|/ˈdʒɑːɡɪŋ/| n. v.|慢跑；慢跑运动|The act of running at a steady, gentle pace, typically for exercise.|例句：I go jogging every morning. = 我每天早上都去慢跑。

例句：She enjoys jogging in the park. = 她喜欢在公园慢跑。


近义词：running, trotting|反义词：sprinting, racing|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I jog every morning. = 我每天早上慢跑。
He jogs in the park. = 他在公园慢跑。

一般过去时:
I jogged yesterday. = 我昨天慢跑了。
She jogged for an hour. = 她慢跑了一个小时。

现在完成时:
I have jogged five kilometers today. = 我今天已经慢跑了五公里。
She has jogged regularly for years. = 她多年来一直定期慢跑。

过去完成时:
I had jogged three miles before I felt tired. = 我慢跑了三英里后才感到疲倦。
They had jogged for hours before they reached the finish line. = 他们慢跑了几个小时才到达终点线。

将来时:
I will jog tomorrow. = 我明天将去慢跑。
He will jog with his friends. = 他将和朋友们一起去慢跑。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would jog more often. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常地慢跑。
I wish I could jog a marathon. = 我希望我能跑马拉松。

被动语态:  (Jogging itself is rarely used passively.  You'd use a related verb like "be run" instead)  The race was run at a slow pace. = 比赛以慢速进行。
The course was prepared for the joggers. = 跑道已为慢跑者准备好。


疑问句:
Do you jog regularly? = 你定期慢跑吗？
Does he enjoy jogging? = 他喜欢慢跑吗？

否定句:
I don't jog very often. = 我不经常慢跑。
She didn't jog yesterday. = 她昨天没有慢跑。


PHRASE->
go jogging = 去慢跑
morning jogging = 早晨慢跑
jogging track = 慢跑道
jogging shoes = 慢跑鞋
jogging suit = 慢跑服

词源:
The word "jogging" comes from the verb "jog," which has its roots in the Middle English word "joggen," meaning "to shake or jolt."  Over time, the meaning shifted to describe a gentle, steady running pace.
