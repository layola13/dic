orthodoxes:/ˈɔːθədɒksiːz/ | n. | 正统观念；正统信仰；正统派 | The various orthodoxies of the different Christian churches.  不同的基督教教会有着各自的正统观念。|近义词：doctrines, beliefs, traditions |反义词：heresies, heterodoxies


英文解析：The plural form of "orthodoxy," referring to established beliefs, doctrines, or practices, especially in religion or politics.  It often implies a rigid adherence to tradition and resistance to change.  It can also refer to a group of people adhering to such beliefs.


例句：

The political orthodoxies of the time prevented any serious discussion of reform.当时的政治正统观念阻止了任何关于改革的认真讨论。

Several orthodoxies co-exist peacefully in this diverse community.  在这个多元化的社区中，几种正统信仰和平共处。
