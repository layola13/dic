sherberts:/ˈʃɜːrbəts/| n. |雪葩，冰冻甜点 |A sherbet is a frozen dessert that is similar to ice cream but typically contains less fat and is often more tart. It is usually made with fruit juice, sugar, and water, and sometimes contains milk or cream.  | Example:  I love the refreshing taste of lemon sherbet on a hot day.  今天炎热的天气里，我喜欢柠檬雪葩清爽的味道。 |近义词：sorbet, ice, ice cream |反义词:  None specifically, but could be contrasted with rich, creamy desserts.
