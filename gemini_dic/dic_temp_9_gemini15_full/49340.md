delineament:/dɪˈlɪniəmənt/| n. |轮廓；略图；特征；地质构造线|A delineament is a linear feature, typically a topographic or subsurface expression of a geologic structure, often visible in aerial or satellite imagery.  It can also refer to a distinguishing feature or characteristic.  The term is commonly used in geology and remote sensing.|例句：The aerial photograph revealed a distinct delineament that suggested a previously unknown fault line.  航拍照片显示出一条明显的构造线，表明存在一条以前未知的断层线。

The geologist carefully mapped the delineaments visible on the seismic profile. 地质学家仔细绘制了地震剖面上可见的构造线。|近义词：outline, contour, feature, trace, lineament|反义词：obscurity, indistinctness
