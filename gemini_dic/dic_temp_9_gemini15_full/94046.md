impartiality:/ˌɪmpɑːrʃiˈælɪti/ | n. | 公正; 公平; 不偏不倚 | The absence of bias or favoritism; fairness.  It implies a neutral and objective stance, free from prejudice or personal interest. | Example: The judge's impartiality was questioned after his close relationship with the defendant was revealed.  法官与被告关系密切后，其公正性受到了质疑。  The committee prides itself on its impartiality in evaluating grant proposals. 委员会为自己在评估拨款提案时的公正性感到自豪。|近义词：fairness, objectivity, neutrality, equity |反义词：bias, prejudice, partiality, favoritism
