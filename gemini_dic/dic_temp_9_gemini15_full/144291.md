outspending: /ˌaʊtˈspɛndɪŋ/ | v. | 超支；花费超过 |  To spend more money than someone or something else, or than is available. |


例句：

The company is outspending its competitors on advertising. = 这家公司在广告上的支出超过了竞争对手。

The government is outspending its revenue, leading to a growing national debt. = 政府的支出超过了收入，导致国家债务不断增加。


近义词：exceeding, surpassing, outlaying


反义词：underspending, saving, economizing


词性：动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:

The government consistently outspends its projected budget. = 政府持续超支预算。

Our team outspends the others on research and development. = 我们的团队在研发上的支出超过了其他团队。


一般过去时:

Last year, the company outspent its rivals in the marketing campaign. = 去年，该公司在市场营销活动上的支出超过了竞争对手。

They outspent their savings on a luxury vacation. = 他们把积蓄花在了奢侈的假期上。


现在完成时:

The project has outspent its initial allocated funds. = 该项目已超过其最初分配的资金。

We have outspent our budget this month, due to unexpected expenses. = 由于意外支出，我们这个月超支了预算。


过去完成时:

By the end of the year, they had outspent their entire annual budget. = 到年底，他们已经超支了全年的预算。

The company realized they had outspent their competitors on the project, but ultimately it was worth it. = 公司意识到他们在项目上的支出超过了竞争对手，但最终这是值得的。


将来时:

We predict that they will outspend us on this new product launch. = 我们预测他们会在新产品发布上超过我们的支出。

The company expects to outspend its competitors on research next year. = 公司预计明年在研发上的支出将超过竞争对手。


虚拟语气:

If we continued like this, we would outspend our entire budget before the project ends. = 如果我们继续这样下去，项目结束前我们会超支整个预算。

I wish we hadn't outspent our savings on that frivolous purchase. = 我真希望我们没有把积蓄花在那笔毫无意义的购买上。


被动语态:

The budget was outspent due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，预算被超支了。

The competitor's advertising campaign was significantly outspent by our efforts. = 竞争对手的广告活动被我们的努力大幅超过。


疑问句:

Has the marketing department outspent its allocated budget? = 市场部是否超支了其分配的预算？

Will we outspend our competitors in the upcoming quarter? = 我们会在下一个季度超过竞争对手的支出吗？


否定句:

The team did not outspend its budget for the quarter. = 团队在本季度没有超支预算。

We made sure not to outspend our competitors on unnecessary expenses. = 我们确保不会在不必要的开支上超过竞争对手。


PHRASE->

outspending on: 在...方面超支
significantly outspending: 大幅超支
constantly outspending: 持续超支
outspending competitors: 超过竞争对手的支出
outspending the market: 超过市场平均支出
