cercaria:/sərˈkɛəriə/| n. |尾蚴|A free-swimming larval stage in the life cycle of certain parasitic trematodes (flukes), especially those that infect snails and vertebrates.  It has a tail and is capable of infecting a new host.|例句：The cercaria penetrated the skin of the swimmer, initiating the infection.  尾蚴穿透了游泳者的皮肤，开始了感染。|近义词：larva (general term)|反义词：adult (stage)|名词
