dumplings: /ˈdʌmplɪŋz/ | n. |饺子，汤圆 | Small pieces of dough, often filled with meat or vegetables, that are boiled, steamed, or fried.  Dumplings are a popular dish in many cultures, with variations in filling and preparation methods. | Example:  I made a big batch of pork dumplings for dinner. 我做了很多猪肉饺子当晚餐。  My grandmother's recipe for steamed dumplings is the best. 我祖母做的蒸饺最好吃。 |近义词： ravioli, pierogi, wontons | 反义词：无
