Immutability is a key characteristic of certain data structures in computer science, ensuring data integrity and preventing unintended modifications. |Example: The immutability of the laws of physics is a fundamental principle of science.  不变的物理定律是科学的一个基本原理。 The immutability of the contract protected both parties. 合同的不变性保护了双方。|近义词：permanence, constancy,unchangeableness|反义词：mutability,changeability,variability|名词
