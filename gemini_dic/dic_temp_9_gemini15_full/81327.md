graphemes:/ˈɡræfiːmz/ | n. | 语素，音位图 | The smallest unit in a writing system, representing one or more sounds.  Graphemes can be letters, combinations of letters, or symbols. For example, the word "though" has five graphemes but only four phonemes. | 例句：The English language uses a variety of graphemes to represent sounds. = 英语使用各种语素来表示声音。


The study of graphemes is crucial to understanding how writing systems work. = 语素的研究对于理解书写系统的工作原理至关重要。|近义词：letters, symbols, characters |反义词：None  |名词
