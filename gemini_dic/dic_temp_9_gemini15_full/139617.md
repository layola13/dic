occasioning: /əˈkeɪʒənɪŋ/ | v. | 引起；导致；造成 |  causing something to happen; giving rise to something. |  The heavy rain occasioned a flood.  大雨引发了洪水。  The accident occasioned a delay. 事故导致延误。 |  causing, producing, resulting in | preventing, hindering, stopping | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud music is occasioning complaints from the neighbors. = 吵闹的音乐正在引起邻居的抱怨。
His carelessness is occasioning many problems. = 他的粗心正在导致许多问题。

一般过去时:
The storm occasioned widespread power outages. = 暴风雨导致了大范围的停电。
Her sudden illness occasioned a change of plans. = 她突然生病导致了计划的改变。

现在完成时:
The drought has occasioned a severe water shortage. = 干旱已经导致了严重的缺水。
His actions have occasioned much controversy. = 他的行为已经引起了很多争议。

过去完成时:
The faulty wiring had occasioned the fire. = 故障的线路已经导致了火灾。
Her previous mistakes had occasioned her current difficulties. = 她之前的错误已经导致了她目前的困境。

将来时:
The new regulations will occasion some difficulties for businesses. = 新规将给企业带来一些困难。
The upcoming election will occasion much political debate. = 即将到来的选举将引发大量的政治辩论。

虚拟语气:
If the project had been better planned, it would not have occasioned so many problems. = 如果这个项目计划得更好，就不会导致这么多问题了。
If he had been more careful, he would not have occasioned this accident. = 如果他更小心一点，就不会造成这次事故了。

被动语态:
Many problems were occasioned by the lack of communication. = 许多问题都是由于缺乏沟通造成的。
The delay was occasioned by unforeseen circumstances. = 延误是由不可预见的情况造成的。

疑问句:
What occasioned the argument? = 什么导致了这场争论？
What occasioned your late arrival? = 什么导致你迟到了？

否定句:
The good weather did not occasion any delays. = 好天气没有造成任何延误。
His explanation did not occasion any further questions. = 他的解释没有引起任何进一步的问题。
