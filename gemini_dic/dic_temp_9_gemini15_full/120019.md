millionaire:/ˌmɪljəˈneər/| n. |百万富翁，百万富婆 | A person who has assets worth one million or more.  |He hopes to become a millionaire one day. 他希望有一天成为百万富翁。| multimillionaire, tycoon | pauper, beggar |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a millionaire. = 他是一个百万富翁。
She is a self-made millionaire. = 她是一位白手起家的百万富翁。

一般过去时:
He was a millionaire before the market crash. = 在市场崩盘之前，他是一个百万富翁。
She was once a millionaire, but lost her fortune. = 她曾经是一个百万富翁，但后来失去了她的财富。

现在完成时:
He has become a millionaire through hard work. = 他通过努力工作成为了百万富翁。
She has remained a millionaire despite the economic downturn. = 尽管经济低迷，她仍然是一个百万富翁。

过去完成时:
He had been a millionaire for many years before he retired. = 他退休前已经做了多年的百万富翁。
She had already been a millionaire when she started her charity. = 当她开始做慈善事业时，她已经是百万富翁了。

将来时:
He will be a millionaire by the time he is forty. = 到他四十岁的时候，他将成为百万富翁。
She will hopefully become a millionaire with her new business venture. = 她希望通过她的新商业冒险成为百万富翁。

虚拟语气:
If I were a millionaire, I would travel the world. = 如果我是一个百万富翁，我会环游世界。
I wish I were a millionaire so I could help more people. = 我希望我是一个百万富翁，这样我就能帮助更多的人。

被动语态:
(This word is rarely used in passive voice)  He was declared a millionaire by the court. = 法院宣布他为百万富翁。(较为罕见)
The list of millionaires was published yesterday. = 百万富翁名单昨天公布了。(侧重名单而非个人)


疑问句:
Is he a millionaire? = 他是百万富翁吗？
Are they millionaires? = 他们是百万富翁吗？

否定句:
He is not a millionaire. = 他不是百万富翁。
She is not yet a millionaire, but she's working towards it. = 她还不是百万富翁，但她正在努力争取。


PHRASE->
self-made millionaire = 白手起家的百万富翁
dollar millionaire = 美元百万富翁

property millionaire = 房地产百万富翁
celebrity millionaire = 名人百万富翁


词源:
The word "millionaire" is derived from the French word "millionnaire," which itself is a combination of "million" (meaning "million") and "-aire" (a suffix indicating a person connected with or possessing something).  The suffix "-aire" has roots in Latin.
