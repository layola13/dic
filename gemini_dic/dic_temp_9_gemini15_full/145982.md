overdoer: /ˌoʊvərˈduːər/ | n. |做得过分的人；过度者 | A person who does things to excess; someone who overdoes things.  |  He's an overdoer; he always takes on too much work. 他是个做事过分的人，总是承担过多的工作。 |近义词：excess, extremist, fanatic |反义词：minimalist, underachiever
