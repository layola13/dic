decided:/dɪˈsaɪdɪd/| adj. v.|决定了的；坚决的；果断的 |To make a firm decision or choice; to settle or resolve.|例句：He decided to go to college. 他决定上大学。
The jury decided the case. 陪审团对案件作出了裁决。|近义词：determined, resolved, settled|反义词：undecided, irresolute, wavering|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They decide where to eat every day. = 他们每天决定去哪里吃饭。
She decides what to wear each morning. = 她每天早上决定穿什么。

一般过去时:
He decided to quit his job last week. = 他上周决定辞职。
We decided against going to the party. = 我们决定不去参加聚会。

现在完成时:
They have decided to move to a new city. = 他们已经决定搬到一个新的城市。
She has decided on her major for college. = 她已经决定了大学的专业。

过去完成时:
After he had decided to leave, he sold his car. = 在他决定离开后，他卖掉了他的车。
I realized that I had already decided against it. = 我意识到我已经决定反对它了。

将来时:
I will decide on my next vacation destination tomorrow. = 我明天将决定我的下一个度假目的地。
We will decide what to do after consulting with the expert. = 我们将在咨询专家后决定该做什么。

虚拟语气:
If I had decided differently, my life would be completely different. = 如果我当初做了不同的决定，我的生活将会完全不同。
I wish I had decided to study harder when I was younger. = 我希望我年轻的时候能决定更加努力学习。

被动语态:
The date of the meeting was decided yesterday. = 会议日期昨天已确定。
The winner will be decided by a panel of judges. = 获胜者将由评审团决定。

疑问句:
Have you decided what to do? = 你决定好做什么了吗？
What have they decided about the proposal? = 他们对这个提案做了什么决定？

否定句:
I haven't decided yet. = 我还没决定。
They didn't decide to go. = 他们决定不去。


PHRASE->
decide on:  决定…
decide against: 决定反对…
decide to: 决定做…
make up one's mind to decide:下定决心决定
have decided:已经决定
