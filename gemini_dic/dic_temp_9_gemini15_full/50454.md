depreciators:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪtərz/| n.|贬值者；减值物；折旧物|People or things that cause depreciation.  They can refer to assets losing value over time, or individuals or factors contributing to this loss.|The old machines are depreciators, costing the company more in maintenance than they're worth.|近义词：devaluers, diminishers|反义词：appreciators, enhancers|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The old equipment depreciates quickly. = 旧设备贬值很快。
These assets are depreciating at an alarming rate. = 这些资产正在以惊人的速度贬值。

一般过去时:
The value of the currency depreciated sharply last year. = 去年货币价值大幅贬值。
The machinery depreciated faster than we anticipated. = 机器贬值的速度比我们预期的快。

现在完成时:
The car has depreciated significantly since we bought it. = 自从我们买车以来，车价已经大幅贬值。
Property values have depreciated in this area. = 这个地区的房地产价值已经贬值。

过去完成时:
The company had depreciated its assets before the audit. = 公司在审计之前已经对资产进行了减值。
By the end of the year, the investment had depreciated considerably. = 到年底，投资已经大幅贬值。

将来时:
The value of the painting will depreciate if not properly cared for. = 如果不妥善保管，这幅画的价值将会贬值。
We expect the equipment to depreciate over the next five years. = 我们预计设备在未来五年内将会贬值。

虚拟语气:
If the market hadn't crashed, our investments wouldn't have depreciated. = 如果市场没有崩溃，我们的投资就不会贬值。
If the building were better maintained, its value wouldn't have depreciated so much. = 如果建筑物维护得更好，它的价值就不会贬值这么多。

被动语态:
The assets were depreciated according to company policy. = 资产根据公司政策进行了减值。
The building's value is being depreciated due to neglect. = 建筑物的价值由于疏忽而正在贬值。

疑问句:
How much has the equipment depreciated? = 设备贬值了多少？
Has the value of your investment depreciated? = 你的投资价值贬值了吗？

否定句:
The value of gold doesn't depreciate easily. = 黄金的价值不容易贬值。
The antique furniture hasn't depreciated in value. = 这套古董家具的价值没有贬值。
