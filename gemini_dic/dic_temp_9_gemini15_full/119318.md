microbially:/maɪˈkroʊbiəli/| adj.|微生物地，由微生物引起的|Relating to or caused by microbes.  The adjective form of "microbial". It describes something that is done by, or related to, microbes (bacteria, fungi, etc.).|例句：Microbially mediated processes are crucial for nutrient cycling in soil.  微生物介导的过程对土壤养分循环至关重要。

例句：The degradation of the pollutant was found to be microbially driven.  发现污染物的降解是由微生物驱动的。


近义词：microbiologically

反义词：sterilely (in the context of absence of microbial activity)


词性：形容词
