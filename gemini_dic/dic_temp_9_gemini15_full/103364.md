jimson:/ˈdʒɪmsən/| n. |颠茄；曼陀罗|Datura stramonium, a poisonous plant in the nightshade family, also known as jimsonweed.  Its leaves, seeds, and flowers contain tropane alkaloids which have hallucinogenic and deliriant effects.  The name is thought to derive from Jamestown, Virginia, where it was reportedly used to poison soldiers during an early colonial conflict.|The jimsonweed is a common weed found throughout the world. |近义词：datura, Jamestown weed, devil's snare |反义词：无
