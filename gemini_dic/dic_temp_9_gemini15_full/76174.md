fuss:/fʌs/| n., vi.|大惊小怪；忙乱；小题大做；琐碎之事 |to make a needless display of worry, excitement, or activity.  |例句：He made such a fuss about a tiny scratch on his car. 他为汽车上的一点小划痕大惊小怪。|近义词：commotion, ado, bother, trouble |反义词：calm, tranquility, peace


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fusses over his appearance. = 他很在意自己的外表。
She fusses about the house all day. = 她整天在家里忙忙碌碌。


一般过去时:
They fussed over the arrangements for the party. = 他们为派对的安排忙得不亦乐乎。
He fussed about the missing keys. = 他为丢失的钥匙而着急。


现在完成时:
She has fussed over this project for weeks. = 她为这个项目忙活了好几个星期了。
They have fussed too much about nothing. = 他们为一些无关紧要的事情大惊小怪。


过去完成时:
He had fussed over the details for hours before the presentation. = 他在演示前几个小时就为细节操心了。
She had fussed about the children's clothes all morning. = 她整个上午都在为孩子们挑选衣服而忙活。


将来时:
They will fuss over the baby all night. = 他们今晚会一直忙着照顾婴儿。
He will fuss about the smallest things. = 他会为最细微的事情而大惊小怪。


虚拟语气:
If he didn't fuss so much, he would be less stressed. = 如果他不那么大惊小怪，他会不那么紧张。
I wish she wouldn't fuss so much about my clothes. = 我希望她不要再为我的衣服那么操心了。


被动语态:
The matter was fussed over by the committee for days. = 这件事被委员会讨论了好几天。
The arrangements were fussed over by the event planner. =  活动策划人费尽心思安排了这些事宜。


疑问句:
Does she fuss over every little detail? = 她对每一个小细节都大惊小怪吗？
Why are you fussing so much? = 你为什么这么大惊小怪？


否定句:
He didn't fuss about the delay. = 他对延误并不在意。
She doesn't fuss over her appearance. = 她不太在意自己的外表。


PHRASE->
make a fuss = 大惊小怪
fuss about/over = 为...而大惊小怪
fuss with =  摆弄；修补
fuss and bother =  大惊小怪；忙乱
no fuss = 不费吹灰之力
