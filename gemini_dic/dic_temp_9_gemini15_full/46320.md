dabble:/ˈdæbl/| v.|涉猎，浅尝辄止|To take part in an activity in a superficial way; to engage in something playfully or casually.  |例句：He dabbled in painting for a while but never became serious about it. 他曾经涉猎过绘画，但从未认真对待。|例句：She dabbled her fingers in the water. 她用手指轻轻地拨弄着水。|近义词：dip, play, toy, trifle |反义词：master, specialize, delve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dabbles in photography. = 他涉猎摄影。
She dabbles her brush in the paint. = 她用画笔轻蘸颜料。

一般过去时:
He dabbled in politics during his youth. = 他年轻时涉足政治。
She dabbled her toes in the cool water. = 她把脚趾头伸进凉凉的水里。

现在完成时:
I have dabbled in several different writing styles. = 我尝试过几种不同的写作风格。
She has dabbled in various forms of art. = 她涉猎过各种形式的艺术。

过去完成时:
He had dabbled in poetry before turning to prose. = 他转向散文创作之前曾涉猎过诗歌。
She had dabbled in several businesses before finding success. = 她创业之前尝试过好几项生意。

将来时:
I will dabble in coding this summer. = 今年夏天我将尝试编程。
She will dabble in watercolor painting during her vacation. = 她将在假期期间尝试水彩画。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would dabble in astronomy. = 如果我有更多时间，我会尝试一下天文学。
I wish I could dabble in music. = 我真希望我能涉猎音乐。

被动语态:  (Dabble is rarely used passively)
The water was dabbled with paint. = 水被颜料弄脏了。(This uses a different sense of "dabble")
The topic was dabbled in briefly during the meeting. = 会议上简要地提及了这个话题。(This is a less common passive usage)

疑问句:
Does he dabble in anything creative? = 他从事任何有创意的事情吗？
Will you dabble in this new project? = 你会参与这个新项目吗？

否定句:
He doesn't dabble in the stock market. = 他不炒股。
She didn't dabble in that area of study. = 她没有涉猎那个研究领域。


PHRASE->
dabble in = 涉猎
dabble with = 玩弄；尝试
dabble one's toes in = 涉足（某事）
dabble one's fingers in = 轻触；拨弄
dabble in paint = 蘸颜料
