epigraphics:/ˌɛpɪˈɡræfɪks/| n. |铭文学；碑文研究|The study of inscriptions, especially those on ancient monuments and artifacts.  It involves deciphering the writing, understanding the language, and interpreting the historical and cultural context. |Example: Epigraphics played a crucial role in understanding the history of the ancient Maya civilization.  铭文学在了解玛雅文明的历史方面发挥了至关重要的作用。|近义词：inscriptive studies, paleography (partially)|反义词：无
