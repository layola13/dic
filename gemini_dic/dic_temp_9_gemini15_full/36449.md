cold-hearted:/ˈkoʊldˈhɑːtɪd/ | adj. | 冷酷无情的；铁石心肠的 |  Lacking in sympathy and compassion; cruel and unfeeling.  The adjective "cold-hearted" describes someone who shows little or no empathy, kindness, or warmth towards others. They may be indifferent to the suffering of others, or even actively cause it. |  He showed a cold-hearted disregard for the feelings of others. 他表现出对他人感受的冷酷无情。

The boss's cold-hearted decision to lay off workers left many families struggling. 老板冷酷无情地裁员，导致许多家庭陷入困境。 | 近义词：heartless, unfeeling, ruthless, callous, pitiless | 反义词：kind, compassionate, warm-hearted, sympathetic, merciful |  形容词

词源:
The word "cold-hearted" is a compound adjective, combining "cold" and "hearted."  "Cold" here refers to a lack of warmth, emotion, or feeling, while "hearted" relates to the heart as the center of emotions.  The expression thus signifies an absence of emotional warmth and compassion.
