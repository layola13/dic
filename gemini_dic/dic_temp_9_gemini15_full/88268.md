hinny:/ˈhɪni/| n.|骡子（公驴和母马的后代）|A hinny is the offspring of a male donkey (jack) and a female horse (mare).  Hinnies are typically smaller than mules (the offspring of a male horse and a female donkey) and are less common.|Example: The farmer bred a hinny from his jack and mare.  这个农民用他的公驴和母马培育了一匹骡子。|近义词：mule, hybrid|反义词：N/A
