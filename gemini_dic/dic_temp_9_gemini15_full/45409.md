cupolas:/ˈkjuːpələz/| n.|圆屋顶；穹顶；圆形塔楼|A cupola is a small dome-like structure on top of a building, often used for ventilation or as an architectural feature.  It can also refer to a small tower with a dome-shaped roof.|例句：The church was crowned with a beautiful cupola. 这座教堂顶部装饰着一个美丽的圆屋顶。|例句：The cupola provided ventilation for the hot attic. 圆屋顶为炎热的阁楼提供了通风。|近义词：dome, turret, dome-shaped roof|反义词：flat roof
