sipped:/sɪpt/| v.|轻啜，小口喝|To drink in small sips.  It implies a slow, delicate way of drinking.|例句：She sipped her tea slowly, enjoying the warmth.|近义词：drank, tasted, sampled|反义词：gulped, quaffed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sips his coffee every morning. = 他每天早上都小口喝咖啡。
She sips tea while reading a book. = 她看书时小口喝茶。

一般过去时:
I sipped the wine carefully. = 我仔细地小口喝着葡萄酒。
He sipped his beer thoughtfully. = 他若有所思地小口喝着啤酒。

现在完成时:
She has sipped all the juice. = 她已经把所有的果汁都喝完了（小口喝）。
They have sipped several glasses of champagne. = 他们已经小口喝了几杯香槟。

过去完成时:
He had sipped the tea before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他已经小口喝了茶。
She had sipped the wine before she realized it was spoiled. = 她直到意识到酒变质了才小口喝了点酒。

将来时:
I will sip the smoothie after my workout. = 我锻炼后会小口喝这个冰沙。
She will sip her coffee while she watches the sunrise. = 她会在看日出时小口喝咖啡。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would sip the wine slowly to savor its flavor. = 如果我是你，我会慢慢地小口喝葡萄酒以品尝它的味道。
I wish I had sipped the tea more slowly. = 我真希望我当时能更慢地小口喝茶。

被动语态:
The wine was sipped delicately by the connoisseur. = 这葡萄酒被鉴赏家细细品尝。
The tea was sipped slowly throughout the evening. = 茶在整个晚上都被慢慢地小口喝着。

疑问句:
Did you sip the soup before you added the salt? = 你加盐之前有没有小口喝汤？
Will you sip the hot chocolate before bed? = 你睡前会小口喝热巧克力吗？

否定句:
He didn't sip his drink; he gulped it down. = 他没有小口喝他的饮料；他是一饮而尽。
She didn't sip the coffee; she found it too bitter. = 她没有小口喝咖啡；她觉得太苦了。


PHRASE->
sip by sip = 一口一口地
sip on = 小口喝
sip something slowly = 慢慢地小口喝
sip and savor = 小口品尝
sip from = 从...小口喝
