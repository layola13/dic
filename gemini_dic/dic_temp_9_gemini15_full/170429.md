proximately:/ˈprɒksɪmətli/| adv.|大约，近似地，接近地 |Approximately means very close to a particular number, amount, time, etc., but not exactly that number. It suggests a close estimation rather than a precise measurement.  |例句：The meeting will start approximately at 2 p.m.  会议大约在下午两点开始。

例句：The project cost approximately $10,000.  这个项目花费大约一万美元。


近义词：roughly, nearly, about, around, closely

反义词：exactly, precisely, accurately


词性: 副词
