relabelers:/ˌriːˈleɪbələrz/| n. |重新贴标签者，重新标记者 | People or systems that change the labels or tags of items.  Relabelers are often used in data processing or logistics to correct, update, or standardize labels. | Example: The warehouse uses automated relabelers to ensure all packages have the correct shipping information.  重新贴标签的仓库使用自动化系统来确保所有包裹都有正确的运输信息。 The data relabelers corrected inconsistencies in the dataset. 数据重新标记者纠正了数据集中不一致的地方。|近义词：tag changers, label correctors, data annotators|反义词：N/A
