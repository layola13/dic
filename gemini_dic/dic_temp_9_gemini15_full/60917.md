elephantoid:/ˈɛlɪfænˌtɔɪd/| adj.|象形的；类似大象的|Resembling an elephant; having characteristics of an elephant. The term is often used in a metaphorical or descriptive sense, rather than a strictly scientific classification.  It might refer to something large, grey, or possessing a trunk-like appendage.|例句：The massive, grey cloud formation had an elephantoid quality.  那巨大的灰色云层具有象形的特征。 The structure's elephantoid legs supported the heavy roof. 建筑物类似大象腿的支柱支撑着沉重的屋顶。|近义词：elephantine, colossal, massive|反义词： diminutive, tiny, minute|形容词
