disinfection:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛkʃən/| n. |消毒；灭菌|The process of destroying harmful microorganisms to prevent infection.  Disinfection reduces the number of germs on surfaces or objects, but it doesn't necessarily kill all of them. This is different from sterilization, which aims for complete elimination of all microorganisms.|例句：Regular disinfection of surfaces is crucial in preventing the spread of viruses. 定期对表面进行消毒对于预防病毒传播至关重要。
The hospital implemented strict disinfection protocols to combat the outbreak. 医院实施了严格的消毒规程以应对疫情。|近义词：sterilization, sanitization|反义词：contamination, infection
