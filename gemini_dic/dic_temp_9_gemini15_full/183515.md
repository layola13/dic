repunishes:/ˌriːˈpʌnɪʃɪz/| vt.|再次惩罚；重复惩罚|To punish again or repeatedly.  The word is formed by adding the prefix "re-" (meaning again) to the verb "punishes".|例句：The judge repunished the defendant for violating his parole. = 法官因被告违反假释而再次惩罚他。
The school repunishes students who repeatedly break the rules. = 学校再次惩罚那些反复违反规章制度的学生。|近义词：reprimand, chastise, penalize|反义词：reward, pardon
