prerepublican:/ˌpriːˈrɛpʌblɪkən/| adj.| 共和国以前的；共和体制建立之前的 | Existing or occurring before the establishment of a republic.  The term often refers to the period in Roman history before the Roman Republic was established. |例句：Prerepublican Roman society was characterized by a monarchy. = 共和国以前的罗马社会以君主制为特征。

例句：Scholars are studying prerepublican art to understand its influence on later styles. = 学者们正在研究共和体制建立之前的艺术，以了解其对后期风格的影响。


近义词：pre-republican, ante-republican


反义词：post-republican


词性:形容词
