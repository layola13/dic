nicknaming:/ˈnɪkneɪmɪŋ/ | n. | 昵称；起绰号；给…取昵称 | The act of giving someone a nickname.  It refers to the process of creating and using nicknames. | 例句：The nicknaming of players is a common practice in team sports. 球队运动中给队员起绰号是很常见的。

例句：The constant nicknaming annoyed him.  不断地给他起绰号让他很烦。


近义词：dubbing, christening (in a informal sense) | 反义词：using full names, formal addressing


词性：名词 (gerund，动名词)
