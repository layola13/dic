fulminic: /fʊlˈmɪnɪk/ | adj. | 雷鸣般的；爆炸的；猛烈的 | Relating to or denoting fulminic acid or its salts.  Characterized by or expressing vehement denunciation or condemnation. | 例句：The fulminic attack on the government's policies stunned the nation.  = 政府政策遭到的猛烈抨击震惊了全国。  The fulminic sound of the explosion echoed through the valley. = 爆炸的雷鸣般巨响在山谷中回荡。| 近义词：explosive, thunderous, vehement, scathing | 反义词：gentle, mild, calm, measured |
