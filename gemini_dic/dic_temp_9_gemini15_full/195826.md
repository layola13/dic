sends:/sɛndz/| vt.|发送，寄送|To transmit something to someone or somewhere, usually by post or electronically.|例句：He sends emails every day. 他每天都发邮件。|例句：She sends her children to school by bus. 她让孩子们坐公共汽车去上学。|近义词：transmit, dispatch, forward|反义词：receive, collect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sends letters regularly. = 他定期寄信。
She sends her love to you. = 她向你问好。

一般过去时:
I sent a package yesterday. = 我昨天寄了一个包裹。
They sent a message to their friends. = 他们给朋友们发了一条消息。

现在完成时:
He has sent three emails this morning. = 他今天早上已经发了三封邮件。
She has sent the documents to the client. = 她已经把文件寄给了客户。

过去完成时:
I had sent the application before the deadline. = 我在截止日期前已经提交了申请。
They had sent the package before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他们已经寄出包裹了。

将来时:
I will send you the report tomorrow. = 我明天会把报告发给你。
She will send a postcard from her vacation. = 她度假时会寄一张明信片。

虚拟语气:
If I had known his address, I would have sent him a postcard. = 如果我知道他的地址，我会给他寄明信片。
I wish I had sent that email sooner. = 我希望我早点发送那封邮件。

被动语态:
The package was sent last week. = 包裹是上周寄出的。
The email was sent to the wrong address. = 邮件发送到了错误的地址。

疑问句:
Did you send the invitation? = 你寄出邀请函了吗？
When will the goods be sent? = 货物什么时候寄出？

否定句:
I didn't send the email. = 我没有发送邮件。
She hasn't sent the documents yet. = 她还没有寄出文件。


PHRASE->
send for: 派人去请
send off: 送别；寄出
send out: 发送；发出
send in: 提交；送交
send back: 退回
