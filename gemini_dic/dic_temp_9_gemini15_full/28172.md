caporal:/kəˈpɔːrəl/ | n. |下士，伍长 | A corporal is a non-commissioned officer of low rank in the army, navy, or air force.  |Example: He was promoted to corporal after six months of exemplary service. 他在服役六个月后被提升为下士。|近义词：lance corporal, private first class |反义词：officer, sergeant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The corporal leads his squad. = 下士带领他的小队。
A corporal patrols the area. = 一个下士在巡逻该地区。


一般过去时:
The corporal inspected the troops. = 下士视察了部队。
The corporal reported to his sergeant. = 下士向他的中士报告。


现在完成时:
The corporal has completed his training. = 下士已经完成了他的训练。
The corporal has served for five years. = 下士已经服役五年了。


过去完成时:
The corporal had already left when the order arrived. = 命令到达时，下士已经离开了。
The corporal had prepared his report before the meeting. = 会议前，下士已经准备好了他的报告。


将来时:
The corporal will lead the patrol tomorrow. = 下士明天将带领巡逻队。
The corporal will be promoted soon. = 下士很快就会被提升。


虚拟语气:
If he were a corporal, he would have more responsibility. = 如果他是下士，他会更有责任感。
I wish I were a corporal; I could have more influence. = 我希望我是个下士；我可以更有影响力。


被动语态:
The patrol was led by the corporal. = 巡逻队由下士带领。
The report was prepared by the corporal. = 报告是由下士准备的。


疑问句:
Is he a corporal? = 他是下士吗？
Was the corporal present at the meeting? = 下士出席会议了吗？


否定句:
He is not a corporal; he is a private. = 他不是下士；他是个列兵。
The corporal did not attend the briefing. = 下士没有参加简报会。
