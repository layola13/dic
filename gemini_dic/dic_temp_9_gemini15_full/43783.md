cremation:/krɪˈmeɪʃən/ | n. |火化，焚化 |The act of burning a dead body to ashes.  Cremation is a common practice in many cultures as a way to dispose of the deceased.  |例句：His family chose cremation over burial. =他的家人选择火化而不是土葬。

例句：The cremation took place at a local crematorium. =火化仪式在当地火葬场进行。


近义词：incineration, burning | 反义词：burial, interment


词源:  From Latin *crematio, from cremare "to burn".
