quasi-organic:/ˈkwɑːzi ɔːrˈɡænɪk/| adj.|准有机的，似有机的|Seemingly or partly organic; having some but not all of the characteristics of something organic.|The company uses quasi-organic farming methods, meaning they use some organic practices but not all to meet certification standards.|近义词：semi-organic, partially organic|反义词：fully organic, inorganic


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like "what" does.  It describes something.  Therefore, tense, voice, and mood are not applicable.  Examples will focus on its use in sentences.

Example Sentences:

Affirmative Sentence:
The farm produces quasi-organic vegetables, using minimal pesticides. = 这个农场生产准有机蔬菜，使用最少的杀虫剂。


Negative Sentence:
Their products are not truly organic; they're only quasi-organic. = 他们的产品并非真正有机；只是准有机。


Question Sentence:
Is this quasi-organic coffee ethically sourced? = 这款准有机咖啡是道德采购的吗？


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "quasi-organic".  The word is used independently to describe a characteristic.
