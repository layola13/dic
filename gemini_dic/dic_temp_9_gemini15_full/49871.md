demotivation:/ˌdiːˌmoʊtɪˈveɪʃən/ | n. |消极情绪，缺乏动力，士气低落 | The feeling of having lost motivation or enthusiasm.  It's a state of lacking the drive or desire to achieve something.  This can be caused by various factors, including stress, burnout, lack of recognition, or unchallenging work. |例句：His demotivation stemmed from a lack of recognition for his hard work. 他的消极情绪源于他的辛勤工作没有得到认可。

例句：The team's demotivation was evident in their poor performance.  团队士气低落，从他们糟糕的表现中就可以看出来。


近义词：discouragement, apathy, lack of motivation, disillusionment |反义词：motivation, inspiration, enthusiasm |词性：名词
