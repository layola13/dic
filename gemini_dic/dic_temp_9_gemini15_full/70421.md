fighters:/ˈfaɪtərz/| n.|战斗人员，战士，战斗机|People or things that fight.  This word can refer to people engaged in combat,  aircraft designed for air combat, or even metaphorical struggles.|例句：The fighters bravely defended their country.  战士们英勇地保卫了自己的国家。

The fighter jets soared through the sky. 战斗机在空中翱翔。


近义词：combatants, warriors, soldiers, planes (for fighter jets)|反义词：peacemakers, pacifists|名词
