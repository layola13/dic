mauler:/ˈmɔːlər/| n. v.|痛打者；猛击者；粗暴的人；猛烈攻击|A person who mauls; one who attacks violently.  A mauler is someone who inflicts forceful and often brutal physical harm.|The boxer was a real mauler in the ring.  拳击手在拳击台上是个真正的猛击者。|He was a mauler, both physically and verbally. 他既在肢体上，又在言语上很粗暴。|bruiser, brawler, roughneck|gentleman, softy|名词，动词
