motoneurons: /ˈmoʊtəʊˌnʊrɒnz/ | n. | 运动神经元 |  Specialized nerve cells that transmit signals from the central nervous system to muscles, causing muscle contraction.  They are the final common pathway for the control of voluntary movement. | Example:  The degeneration of motoneurons is a hallmark of amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS). 运动神经元的退化是肌萎缩性侧索硬化症（ALS）的标志。


The signals transmitted by motoneurons cause muscles to contract, enabling movement. 运动神经元传递的信号导致肌肉收缩，从而实现运动。| 近义词: motor neurons | 反义词: sensory neurons
