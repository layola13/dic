fed-up:/fed ˈʌp/| adj.|厌倦的，受够了|Extremely annoyed or bored with something.  This adjective describes a state of being extremely irritated or disenchanted with something that has been going on for some time.|例句：I'm fed up with his constant complaining. 我受够了他没完没了的抱怨。  I am fed up with this endless rain. 我受够了这没完没了的雨。|近义词：annoyed, irritated, exasperated, disenchanted|反义词：pleased, content, satisfied|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am fed up with this job. = 我对这份工作感到厌倦。
She is fed up with his behavior. = 她对他这种行为感到厌倦。

一般过去时:
I was fed up with the noise. = 我受够了噪音。
They were fed up with waiting. = 他们等得不耐烦了。


现在完成时:
I have been fed up with his lies for years. = 多年来我一直对他那些谎话感到厌倦。
She has been fed up with her boss's attitude for months. = 她已经对老板的态度不满了好几个月了。


过去完成时:
I had been fed up with the project before it even started. = 在项目开始之前我就已经对它感到厌倦了。
They had been fed up with the situation long before they decided to quit. = 在他们决定辞职很久以前，他们就已经对这种情况感到厌烦了。


将来时:
I will be fed up with this soon. = 我很快就会受不了这个。
She will be fed up with the traffic by the time she gets home. = 等她到家的时候，她会被堵车堵得受不了。


虚拟语气:
If I had known how fed up I would be, I would have quit sooner. = 如果我知道我会这么厌倦，我就会早点辞职。
I wish I wasn't so fed up with everything. = 我希望我不至于对所有事情都这么厌倦。

疑问句:
Are you fed up with your job? = 你对你的工作感到厌倦吗？
Is she fed up with the long commute? = 她对漫长的通勤感到厌倦吗？


否定句:
I'm not fed up with my job yet. = 我还没有对我的工作感到厌倦。
He wasn't fed up with the game until the very end. = 直到最后他才对这场比赛感到厌倦。


PHRASE->
be fed up with = 对...感到厌倦
get fed up = 变得厌倦
fed up to the teeth = 极其厌倦
fed up to here = 非常厌倦 (这里指指着某个高度)
I've had it up to here with... = 我受够了...(类似fed up to here)
