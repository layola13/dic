reoxygenating: /ˌriːˈɒksɪdʒəneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 再充氧，再氧化 | The process of supplying oxygen again to a tissue or organ that has been deprived of it.  This is often used in the context of medicine, particularly cancer treatment. | Example: The tumor was reoxygenating after radiotherapy, making it more susceptible to chemotherapy.  肿瘤在放疗后重新获得氧气供应，使其更容易受到化疗的影响。 |近义词: oxygenating, re-aerating |反义词:deoxygenating,anaerobiosis
