gleanable: /ˈɡliːnəbəl/ | adj. | 可收集的；可 glean 的 |  Able to be gleaned; capable of being gathered or collected gradually.  It implies a process of careful and often painstaking collection of small amounts of information or material.


例句：

There was little gleanable information in the report.  = 报告中几乎没有可收集的信息。

The archaeologist spent months finding gleanable evidence of ancient civilization. = 考古学家花了几个月的时间寻找古代文明的蛛丝马迹。


近义词：collectable, gatherable, collectible

反义词：unobtainable, inaccessible


词性：形容词


词源：源自动词 glean，意为“收集（谷物等少量剩余物）”。 glean 本身来源于古英语 *glēnian，其词源与古高地德语 *klēnōn ("to pluck")和古诺尔斯语 *klyna ("to pluck, pinch")有关。 因此，gleanable 的词源最终指向“采摘”或“摘取”少量东西的含义。
