counterterrorists: /ˌkaʊntərˈtɛrərɪsts/ | n. | 反恐人员 |  People or groups who combat terrorism.  They are trained and equipped to prevent, deter, and respond to terrorist acts.  This term often refers to specialized military or police units. | The counterterrorists successfully neutralized the bomb threat.  反恐人员成功化解了炸弹威胁。


The counterterrorists engaged in a high-speed chase after the suspects. 反恐人员在追捕嫌疑人时进行了高速追逐。

近义词：anti-terrorist forces, counter-terrorism operatives, terrorism fighters

反义词：terrorists
