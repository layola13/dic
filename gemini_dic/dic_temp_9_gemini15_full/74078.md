fossilizes:/ˈfɒsɪlaɪzɪz/| v.| 使成化石；使僵化；使陈腐 | to turn into a fossil; to become fixed or unchanging; to become outdated or old-fashioned | 例句：The ancient city was fossilized by a volcanic eruption.  这座古城被火山爆发变成了化石。

The dinosaur bones fossilized over millions of years. 恐龙骨骼经过数百万年的时间变成了化石。


The old traditions are fossilizing the society.  古老的传统正在使这个社会僵化。

The company's fossilized management practices hindered its growth.  公司僵化的管理模式阻碍了它的发展。


近义词：petrify, mineralize, solidify,  embalm,  (for ideas: ossify)


反义词：modernize, revitalize, update,  renew
