counterargued:/ˌkaʊntərˈɑːrɡjuːd/ | v. | 反驳；对……提出反驳 | To present a contrary argument; to offer opposing reasons or evidence. |  The defense lawyer counterargued the prosecution's claim of premeditation.  辩护律师反驳了检方关于预谋的主张。 | refuted, rebutted, contested | agreed, conceded | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He counterargues every point I make. = 他反驳我提出的每一个观点。
They counterargue that the data is flawed. = 他们反驳说数据有缺陷。

一般过去时:
She counterargued his assertion effectively. = 她有效地反驳了他的说法。
The judge counterargued the defendant's plea. = 法官反驳了被告的答辩。

现在完成时:
They have counterargued the initial proposal numerous times. = 他们已经多次反驳了最初的提议。
The scientists have counterargued the theory with new evidence. = 科学家们用新的证据反驳了该理论。

过去完成时:
He had counterargued the same point several times before. = 他之前已经好几次反驳过同样的论点。
The committee had counterargued the amendment before voting. = 委员会在投票前已经反驳了该修正案。

将来时:
She will counterargue that the contract is unenforceable. = 她将反驳该合同不可执行。
They will counterargue the decision in the appeals court. = 他们将在上诉法院反驳该判决。

虚拟语气:
If he had counterargued more forcefully, he might have won the debate. = 如果他更强有力地反驳，他可能赢得了辩论。
I wish I had counterargued more effectively. = 我希望我当时能更有效地反驳。

被动语态:
His argument was counterargued by several experts. = 他的论点被几位专家反驳了。
The policy was counterargued by the opposition party. = 该政策遭到反对党的反驳。

疑问句:

否定句:
He did not counterargue the accusations. = 他没有反驳这些指控。
They failed to counterargue the opposing viewpoint convincingly. = 他们未能令人信服地反驳对方的观点。
