chirruped:/ˈtʃɜːrəpt/ | vt. |啁啾地叫；吱吱叫 | To make a short, cheerful, high-pitched sound, like a bird.  | 例句：The birds chirruped merrily in the morning sun.  清晨，鸟儿们在阳光下快乐地啁啾着。 | 例句：The crickets chirruped a gentle serenade. 蟋蟀发出轻柔的鸣叫声。 | 近义词：twitter, chirp, peep | 反义词：roar, bellow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds chirp every morning. = 鸟儿每天早上都鸣叫。
He chirrups happily to himself. = 他快乐地自言自语。


一般过去时:
The robin chirped a cheerful song. = 知更鸟唱了一首快乐的歌。
She chirped a greeting to her friend. = 她向朋友发出问候。
