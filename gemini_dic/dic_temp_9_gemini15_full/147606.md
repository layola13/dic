overplaying:/ˌoʊvərˈpleɪɪŋ/ | v. | 过分强调；夸张表演；滥用 | To emphasize or exaggerate something to an excessive degree; to act or perform in an exaggerated or unnatural manner; to overuse or exploit something. | 例句：He was overplaying his role in the project. 他在项目中过分夸大自己的作用。  The actor was overplaying the villain's part, making him seem cartoonish.  这位演员对反派角色进行了夸张的表演，使他看起来像个卡通人物。| 近义词：exaggerating, overemphasizing, overacting | 反义词：underplaying, downplaying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overplays his hand in negotiations. = 他在谈判中过分强硬。
She overplays the minor details in her reports. = 她在报告中过分强调次要细节。


一般过去时:
The comedian overplayed his jokes, and the audience grew restless. = 喜剧演员对笑话进行了夸张表演，观众开始不耐烦了。
They overplayed their welcome at the party and stayed too long. = 他们在派对上待得太久，不受欢迎了。


现在完成时:
He has overplayed his strengths and neglected his weaknesses. = 他过分强调了自己的优势，而忽略了自己的劣势。
She has overplayed her role in the company's success. = 她夸大了自己在公司成功中的作用。


过去完成时:
They had overplayed their cards, and now they were in a difficult situation. = 他们打错了牌，现在陷入了困境。
She realized she had overplayed her hand in the argument. = 她意识到她在争论中过分强硬了。


将来时:
He will overplay his emotions if he isn't careful. = 如果他不小心，他会过度表达情绪。
They will likely overplay the risks involved in the project. = 他们可能会夸大项目中涉及的风险。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't overplayed his hand, he might have won the negotiation. = 如果他没有过分强硬，他可能会赢得谈判。
I wish I hadn't overplayed my role in the team's success. = 我真希望我没有夸大自己在团队成功中的作用。


被动语态:
His role was overplayed by the director. = 他的角色被导演过分夸大了。
The importance of the issue was overplayed in the media. = 媒体过分夸大了这个问题的重要性。


疑问句:
Do you think he's overplaying his part? = 你认为他在演戏吗？（夸张表现）
Is the media overplaying the threat of the virus? = 媒体是否夸大了病毒的威胁？


否定句:
He didn't overplay his emotions during the interview. = 他在采访中没有过度表达情绪。
The report didn't overplay the significance of the findings. = 报告没有夸大研究结果的重要性。
