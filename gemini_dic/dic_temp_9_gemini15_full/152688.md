pearch:/pɜːrtʃ/| n. v.|栖息；鲈鱼；|英文解析：As a noun, "pearch" refers to a type of fish or a place where a bird rests. As a verb, it means to settle or rest on a branch or similar elevated place.|例句：The bird perched on the branch.  这只鸟栖息在树枝上。The river is known for its abundance of perch. 这条河以盛产鲈鱼而闻名。|近义词： (n.) roost, rest; (v.) roost, alight|反义词： (v.) fly, depart|词性:n. v.
