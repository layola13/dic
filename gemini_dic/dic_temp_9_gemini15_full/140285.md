The word "oftens" does not exist in standard English.  There is no entry for it in major dictionaries.  It's likely a misspelling of "often."  Therefore, I will provide the analysis for "often."


often:/ˈɔːfən/ | adv. | 常常；经常 | Frequently; many times. | Example: I often go for walks in the park. 我经常在公园里散步。|近义词:frequently, regularly, commonly |反义词:rarely, seldom, infrequently | 副词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He often visits his grandparents. = 他经常去看望他的祖父母。
They often eat dinner at 7 pm. = 他们经常在晚上7点吃晚饭。


一般过去时:
She often played the piano when she was young. = 她小时候经常弹钢琴。
We often went to the beach in the summer. = 我们夏天经常去海滩。


现在完成时:
I have often wondered about that. = 我常常为此感到疑惑。
They have often helped their neighbors. = 他们经常帮助他们的邻居。


过去完成时:
He had often told me the same story. = 他经常跟我讲同一个故事。
She had often dreamed of traveling to Europe. = 她经常梦想去欧洲旅行。


将来时:
I will often think of you. = 我会经常想起你。
We will often visit them next year. = 我们明年将会经常去看望他们。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would often go to the gym. = 如果我有更多时间，我会经常去健身房。
I wish I could often travel abroad. = 我希望我能经常出国旅行。


被动语态:
This question is often asked. = 这个问题经常被问到。
The park is often visited by tourists. = 公园经常被游客参观。


疑问句:
Do you often exercise? = 你经常锻炼吗？
Does she often call you? = 她经常打电话给你吗？


否定句:
I don't often eat meat. = 我不经常吃肉。
He doesn't often go to parties. = 他不经常参加聚会。



PHRASE->

as often as possible = 尽可能经常地
more often than not = 通常；多半
how often = 多久一次
quite often = 相当频繁地
so often = 如此频繁地
