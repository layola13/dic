quasi-initiated: /ˈkwɑːzaɪɪˈnɪʃieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 准入门的；半入门的；似已入门的 |  Describes someone who has undergone some, but not all, of the processes or rituals necessary for full initiation into a group, organization, or activity.  It suggests a partial or incomplete initiation. |  The quasi-initiated members of the club were allowed to attend some meetings but couldn't vote.  准入门槛的俱乐部成员被允许参加一些会议，但不能投票。 | near-initiated, partially initiated | fully initiated, uninitiated | 形容词
