pasture:/ˈpæstʃər/| n.|牧场，草地|Land covered with grass and other low plants suitable for grazing animals.  Pastures provide food for livestock such as cows, sheep, and horses. |例句：The sheep grazed peacefully in the green pasture.  羊群在青翠的牧场上安详地吃草。

例句：The farmer rotates his cattle between different pastures to ensure sufficient grazing.  这位农民轮换着让他的牛群在不同的牧场上吃草，以确保有足够的草料。


近义词：meadow, grassland, grazing land|反义词：desert, wasteland

词性：名词


USAGE->
