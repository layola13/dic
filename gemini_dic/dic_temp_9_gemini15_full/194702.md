self-willed:/ˌsɛlfˈwɪld/| adj.|任性的；固执的；倔强的|Having a strong will and refusing to be controlled by others; determined to do as one pleases, often regardless of the wishes or advice of others.  |例句：He was a self-willed child, always doing exactly what he wanted. 他是个任性的孩子，总是做自己想做的事。


例句：Her self-willed nature often led to conflicts with her parents. 她的任性性格经常导致她和父母发生冲突。


近义词：headstrong, obstinate, stubborn, willful, intractable


反义词：docile, obedient, compliant, amenable


词性：形容词
