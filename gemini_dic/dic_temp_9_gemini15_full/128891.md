nimbleness:/ˈnɪmblnəs/ | n. |灵活；敏捷；轻快 |The quality or state of being nimble; agility; quickness and lightness of movement.  It can also refer to mental agility or quick-wittedness. |例句：Her nimbleness on the dance floor was captivating.  她的舞姿轻盈迷人。

例句：The problem required a surprising degree of mental nimbleness.  这个问题需要相当程度的心智敏捷。


近义词：agility, dexterity, quickness, lightness, swiftness, spryness |反义词：clumsiness, awkwardness, slowness, sluggishness


词源：源自古英语 *nimble，意思是“快速移动的”。
