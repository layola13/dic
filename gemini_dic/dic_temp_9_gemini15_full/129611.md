non-confidential: /ˌnɑːn ˈkɑːnfɪdɛnʃəl/ | adj. | 非机密的；公开的 | Not considered private or secret; open to public knowledge.  | The meeting's minutes were non-confidential and available to all attendees.  会议记录是非机密的，所有与会者都可以查看。 | confidential, public | secret, private | 形容词
