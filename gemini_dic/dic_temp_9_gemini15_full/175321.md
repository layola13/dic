raising: /ˈreɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 举起；提高；抚养；筹集；提出 |

英文解析:The verb "raising" refers to the act of lifting something to a higher position, increasing the level or amount of something, bringing up children, collecting money for a cause, or bringing up a topic for discussion.


例句:

He was raising his hand to answer the question.  他举手回答问题。

The company is raising prices on its products. 公司正在提高其产品价格。


近义词:lifting, elevating, increasing, fostering, breeding, collecting, bringing up, proposing


反义词:lowering, decreasing, dropping, suppressing


词性:Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are raising funds for the new hospital.  他们在为新医院筹集资金。
She raises her voice when she is angry. 她生气时会提高嗓音。

一般过去时:
He raised his hand in protest. 他举手抗议。
They raised three children. 他们养育了三个孩子。

现在完成时:
They have raised a significant amount of money. 他们已经筹集了一大笔钱。
She has raised her concerns with management. 她已经向管理层提出了她的担忧。

过去完成时:
He had raised the issue several times before.  他之前已经多次提出这个问题了。
They had raised their children to be responsible adults. 他们把孩子们抚养成了负责任的成年人。

将来时:
They will raise the flag at dawn. 他们将在黎明时分升旗。
She will raise the rent next month. 她下个月将提高租金。

虚拟语气:
If they raised more money, they could build a bigger hospital. 如果他们筹集更多资金，他们就能建造更大的医院。
I wish I had raised my concerns earlier. 我真希望我早点提出我的担忧。

被动语态:
The issue was raised in the meeting.  这个问题在会议上被提出。
The flag was raised slowly and solemnly. 旗帜缓缓庄严地升起。

疑问句:
Are they raising money for charity? 他们在为慈善机构筹款吗？
Is she raising her children alone? 她独自抚养孩子吗？

否定句:
They are not raising their voices. 他们没有提高嗓音。
He did not raise his hand. 他没有举手。
