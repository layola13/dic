nonsprouting: /ˈnɒnˈspraʊtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不发芽的；无芽的 | Not producing sprouts or shoots.  The term describes something that doesn't germinate or produce new growth.  | Example: The nonsprouting seeds were likely too old to germinate.  这些不发芽的种子可能太老了，无法发芽。

The nonsprouting potato variety is resistant to disease.  这种不发芽的土豆品种具有抗病性。| 近义词: seedless, sterile, dormant | 反义词: sprouting, germinating
