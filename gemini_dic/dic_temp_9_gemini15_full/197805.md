shingling: /ˈʃɪŋɡlɪŋ/ | n. | 铺盖瓦片；屋面瓦片；(头发的)分层剪法 | The act of covering a roof or other surface with shingles.  It can also refer to a hairstyle. | 例句：The shingling of the roof took several days. = 屋顶的盖瓦工作花了几天时间。

The new shingling gave the house a fresh look. = 新的屋瓦使房子焕然一新。


近义词：roofing, tiling | 反义词：None specifically, but "unroofing" could be considered an antonym in a very broad sense. | 词性:名词

词源:
The word "shingle" comes from the Middle English word "shingle," which itself derives from the Old English word "singele," meaning "small, flat piece of stone or wood."  The usage extended to roofing materials over time.
