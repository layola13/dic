rerigging:/ˌriːˈrɪɡɪŋ/| n. |重新装备；重新装配；(船的)重新装帆|The process of refitting or reinstalling rigging, especially on a ship or aircraft.  It often involves replacing worn or damaged parts and ensuring everything is correctly secured and functioning. |例句：The yacht required extensive rerigging after the storm.  暴风雨过后，游艇需要大规模的重新装配。

例句：The aircraft's rerigging took several weeks to complete.  飞机的重新装配花了几个星期才完成。

近义词：refitting, re-equipping, overhaul

反义词：dismantling, stripping down
