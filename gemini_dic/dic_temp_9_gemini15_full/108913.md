leds:/ledz/| n.|发光二极管，LED灯|Light-emitting diodes are semiconductor devices that emit light when an electric current passes through them. They are used in a wide variety of applications, from indicator lights to high-power lighting.  |例句：The new streetlights are LEDs, saving the city a lot of energy.|近义词：diode, light|反义词:None
