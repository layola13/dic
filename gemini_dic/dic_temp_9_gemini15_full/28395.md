carbon-dates:/ˈkɑːrbən deɪts/| v.|碳测年法；用碳十四测定年代|To determine the age of something, especially an organic object, by measuring the amount of carbon-14 it contains.|例句：Scientists carbon-dated the ancient artifact to determine its age.  科学家用碳十四测定法测定了这件古代文物的年代。|例句：The wood was carbon-dated and found to be over 5000 years old. 这段木头经过碳十四测定，发现已有5000多年历史。|近义词：radiocarbon date|反义词:无
