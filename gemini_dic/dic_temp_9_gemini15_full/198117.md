shop-floor:/ʃɒp flɔːr/| n.|车间；工厂底层；生产现场|The shop floor refers to the area of a factory or workshop where the actual manufacturing or production takes place.  It encompasses the workers, machinery, and processes involved in creating goods. |例句：The new management strategy aims to improve communication between the shop floor and the executive team. 新的管理策略旨在改善车间与高管团队之间的沟通。
