obviable:/ˈɒbviəbl/| adj.|可避免的，可防止的|avoidable; preventable; capable of being prevented or avoided.  The accident was obviable if proper safety precautions had been taken. 如果采取适当的安全预防措施，事故是可以避免的。|近义词：avoidable, preventable, avertable|反义词：inevitable, unavoidable
