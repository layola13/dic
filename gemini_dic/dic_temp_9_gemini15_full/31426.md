charioting: /ˈtʃæriətiŋ/ | v. |乘坐战车；以战车载运|The act of riding in or transporting something via a chariot.  It can also imply a swift or triumphant movement.|例句：The victorious general was charioting through the streets.  凯旋的将军乘坐战车穿过街道。


例句：They were charioting the wounded soldiers back to camp. 他们用战车把受伤的士兵运回营地。


近义词：riding in a chariot, conveying by chariot, transporting


反义词：walking, carrying (on foot)


词性：动词
