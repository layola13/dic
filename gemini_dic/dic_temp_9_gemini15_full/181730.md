relic:/ˈrɛlɪk/| n.|遗物，遗迹；圣物|An object surviving from an earlier time, especially one of historical or cultural interest; a surviving trace or evidence of something.  A relic can be an object, a custom, or a belief that has survived from an earlier time.  |例句：The museum houses a collection of ancient Roman relics.  博物馆收藏着许多古罗马遗物。

The ancient temple is a relic of a bygone era. 这座古老的寺庙是过去时代的遗迹。


近义词：artifact, remnant, vestige, souvenir |反义词：novelty, innovation
