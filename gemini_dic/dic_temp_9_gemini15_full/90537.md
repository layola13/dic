huffing:/ˈhʌfɪŋ/| v.|用力喘气；怒气冲冲地说；|发出呼呼声|The runner was huffing and puffing after the race.  赛跑者赛后气喘吁吁。 He huffed at the injustice. 他对不公正感到愤怒。|近义词：puffing, panting, snorting|反义词：whispering, murmuring


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He huffs and puffs when he climbs stairs. 他爬楼梯时气喘吁吁。
She huffs at every little thing. 她对每一件小事都生气。


一般过去时:
He huffed and puffed his way to the top of the hill. 他气喘吁吁地爬上了山顶。
She huffed at me when I was late. 我迟到的时候她对我生气了。


现在完成时:
He has been huffing and puffing all morning. 他整个上午都在气喘吁吁。
She has huffed and puffed at me all day. 她整天都在对我生气。


过去完成时:
He had huffed and puffed before he reached the summit. 他到达山顶前就已经气喘吁吁了。
She had huffed and puffed at him before he apologized. 他道歉前她已经对他生气了。


将来时:
He will huff and puff when he sees the bill. 他看到账单时会气喘吁吁（或生气）。
She will huff and puff if you don't finish your work on time. 如果你不按时完成工作，她会生气。


虚拟语气:
If I ran a marathon, I would be huffing and puffing. 如果我跑马拉松，我会气喘吁吁。
I wish I hadn't huffed at him; I regret it now. 我真希望我没有对他生气，我现在后悔了。


被动语态:  (Huffing is rarely used passively)
The engine was huffing and puffing, indicating a problem. 发动机发出呼呼声，表明存在问题。(This focuses on the sound, not the action of someone huffing)


疑问句:
Is he huffing and puffing because he's tired? 他是不是因为累了而气喘吁吁？
Was she huffing at you because she was angry? 她是不是因为生气而对你生气？


否定句:
He didn't huff and puff as much as I expected. 他没有像我预期的那样气喘吁吁。
She didn't huff at me this time. 她这次没有对我生气。


PHRASE->
huff and puff 气喘吁吁；生气
huff and puff your way through  费力地完成
huff it out  用力地呼气
huff and blow  吹气；怒气冲冲地说
to huff oneself into a rage  让自己气得发怒
