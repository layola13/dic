empathised:/ˈɛmpəθaɪzd/ | v. | 同情，感同身受 | To understand and share the feelings of another.  | Example: I empathised with her grief after hearing about her loss. 我听到她失去亲人的消息后，对她感到同情。 | 近义词：sympathise, commiserate, understand | 反义词：be indifferent to, be unsympathetic to | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He empathizes with the homeless. = 他同情无家可归的人。
She empathizes with her students' struggles. = 她同情学生的困境。

一般过去时:
I empathised with his situation. = 我同情他的处境。
They empathised with the victims of the accident. = 他们同情事故的受害者。

现在完成时:
She has empathised with many people in her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她同情过很多人。
We have empathised with their pain and suffering. = 我们同情他们的痛苦和磨难。

过去完成时:
I had empathised with him before I knew the whole story. = 在我了解全部故事之前，我就已经同情他了。
They had empathised with her difficulties for years. = 他们多年来一直同情她的困难。

将来时:
I will empathise with you if you are ever in trouble. = 如果你遇到麻烦，我会同情你。
She will empathise with the challenges faced by refugees. = 她将同情难民面临的挑战。

虚拟语气:
If I were in her shoes, I would empathise with her more. = 如果我处在她的位置，我会更加同情她。
I wish I could empathise with his feelings better. = 我希望我能更好地理解他的感受。

被动语态:
His plight was empathised with by many. = 许多人同情他的困境。
The suffering of the refugees was deeply empathised with by the public. = 公众对难民的苦难深表同情。

疑问句:
Do you empathise with their situation? = 你同情他们的处境吗？
Can you empathise with someone you don't know well? = 你能同情你不认识的人吗？


否定句:
I didn't empathise with his actions. = 我不同情他的行为。
They couldn't empathise with her anger. = 他们无法理解她的愤怒。


PHRASE->

deeply empathise = 深切同情
empathise strongly = 强烈同情
empathise completely = 完全感同身受
