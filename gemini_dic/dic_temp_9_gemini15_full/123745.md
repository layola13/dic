moonwalker:/ˈmuːnwɔːkər/| n. |月球行走者，登月者 |A person who has walked on the Moon.  Someone who performs the moonwalk dance move. |Example: Neil Armstrong was the first moonwalker. 尼尔·阿姆斯特朗是第一个月球行走者。  He's a pretty good moonwalker; he can really nail that dance move. 他是一个相当不错的“月球漫步”舞者；他真的可以完美地完成那个舞蹈动作。|近义词：astronaut (if referring to space travel), dancer (if referring to the dance move) |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "earthbound" or "terrestrial".
