muskellunge: /ˈmʌskəlʌndʒ/ | n. |麝香鱼，大梭鱼 | A large, predatory freshwater fish of the pike family, found in North America.  It is known for its size and aggressive nature. |例句：The muskellunge is a prized game fish sought after by anglers. 麝香鱼是一种备受垂钓者追捧的珍贵游钓鱼类。

The muskellunge lurked beneath the surface, waiting to ambush its prey. 麝香鱼潜伏在水面下，等待伏击猎物。|近义词：pike, northern pike |反义词：None (no direct antonym, but smaller fish could be considered opposite in terms of size and predatory nature)
