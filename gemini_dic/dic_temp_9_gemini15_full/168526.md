procainamide:/proʊˈkeɪnæmaɪd/| n. |普鲁卡因酰胺|Procainamide is an antiarrhythmic medication used to treat various types of irregular heartbeats (arrhythmias). It works by slowing down the electrical signals in the heart that cause rapid or irregular heartbeats.  It's often used for ventricular arrhythmias and supraventricular tachycardias.|The doctor prescribed procainamide to regulate his erratic heartbeat.|近义词：quinidine, disopyramide|反义词：无直接反义词，但可考虑与治疗相反效果的药物|名词
