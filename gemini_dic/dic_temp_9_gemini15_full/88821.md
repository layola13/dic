hogfish:/ˈhɒɡfɪʃ/| n. |猪鱼|A hogfish is a type of brightly colored fish, often with a prominent lower jaw, found in warm waters of the Atlantic Ocean and Caribbean Sea. They are known for their distinctive appearance and are popular among divers and snorkelers.  |例句：The diver spotted a beautiful hogfish hiding amongst the coral.潜水员发现一条美丽的猪鱼藏在珊瑚丛中。


例句：Many species of hogfish are commercially important for their meat.许多种类的猪鱼因其肉类而具有重要的商业价值。

近义词：reef fish (礁石鱼),  wrasse (隆头鱼) (a broader category)


反义词：无


词源:The name "hogfish" likely refers to its large, prominent lower jaw, which resembles a pig's snout.  The word is a compound of "hog" (pig) and "fish".
