horns:/hɔːrnz/| n.|角；喇叭；触角|Horns are hard, pointed growths on the heads of some animals, such as cows and goats.  They can also refer to musical instruments or the antenna-like appendages of insects.|The bull lowered its horns and charged. 公牛低下角冲了过来。 The car's horn beeped loudly. 汽车喇叭响得很响。|antlers, tusks|none|名词
