limberness:/ˈlɪmbərnəs/ | n. |柔软；灵活；柔韧性 |The quality or state of being limber; flexibility; suppleness.  It refers to the ability to bend easily and gracefully, often applied to the body or its parts.  |例句：The dancer's limberness was evident in her effortless movements.  舞蹈演员的柔软性体现在她轻松自如的动作中。

The gymnast's incredible limberness allowed her to perform complex routines. 体操运动员令人难以置信的柔韧性使她能够完成复杂的动作。


近义词：flexibility, suppleness, agility, pliancy |反义词：stiffness, rigidity, inflexibility
