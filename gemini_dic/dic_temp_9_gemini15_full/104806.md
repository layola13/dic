kean:/kiːn/| adj.|热切的；敏锐的；渴望的|keen means having or showing eagerness or enthusiasm.|He's keen to start his new job. 他渴望开始他的新工作。|eager, enthusiastic, ardent|indifferent, apathetic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is keen on learning new things. 他热衷于学习新事物。
She is keen to help others. 她渴望帮助他人。


一般过去时:
They were keen to see the movie. 他们渴望去看电影。
He was keen on playing football when he was young. 他年轻时热衷于踢足球。


现在完成时:
I have been keen on photography for many years. 我多年来一直热衷于摄影。
She has been keen to visit Paris since she was a child. 她从小就渴望去巴黎。


过去完成时:
He had been keen to get a promotion before he left the company. 他离开公司之前一直渴望升职。
They had been keen on that project for months. 他们几个月来一直热衷于那个项目。


将来时:
I will be keen to hear your opinion. 我将渴望听到你的意见。
She will be keen to start her own business. 她将渴望开始自己的事业。


虚拟语气:
I wish I had been keener on studying when I was younger. 我希望我年轻时更努力学习。
If I were keener on exercise, I would be healthier. 如果我更热衷于锻炼，我会更健康。
