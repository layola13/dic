nonschizophrenic: /ˌnɒnˌskɪtsəˈfrɛnɪk/ | adj. |非精神分裂症的| Not exhibiting symptoms of schizophrenia; not suffering from schizophrenia.  This adjective describes individuals or groups who do not have schizophrenia. It's primarily used in medical and research contexts to specify a control group or to contrast with schizophrenic individuals in studies.  | Example: The study compared the brain scans of schizophrenic and nonschizophrenic participants. 这项研究比较了精神分裂症患者和非精神分裂症患者的大脑扫描结果。 |近义词: unaffected by schizophrenia, mentally healthy (in this specific context) | 反义词: schizophrenic | 词性: adjective
