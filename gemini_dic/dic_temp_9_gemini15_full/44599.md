crowberry: /ˈkraʊbɛri/ | n. |越橘; 黑果越橘 | A low-growing evergreen shrub, typically found in acidic, boggy areas, with small, dark berries.  Crowberries are often found in high-altitude or high-latitude regions. The berries are edible, though often tart. | Example:  We foraged for crowberries during our hike in the Scottish Highlands. = 我们在苏格兰高地徒步旅行时采集了越橘。  The crowberry bushes provided a stark contrast against the snowy landscape. = 越橘灌木丛与雪景形成了鲜明的对比。| 近义词：blackberry (but smaller and tarter), lingonberry | 反义词：无
