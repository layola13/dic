nominal:/ˈnɑːmɪnəl/| adj.|名义上的，有名无实的；极小的| relating to a name or names;  existing in name only;  very small or insignificant. |例句：He held a nominal position in the company. 他在公司担任一个名义上的职位。|例句：The fee is nominal. 费用极少。|近义词： titular, superficial, trifling |反义词：real, actual, substantial
