lender:/ˈlɛndər/| n. |贷款人，贷方|A person or organization that lends money.  A lender provides funds to a borrower, typically with the expectation of repayment with interest. |例句：The bank is a lender of last resort.  银行是最后的贷款人。|例句：She acted as a lender to her struggling friend. 她充当了她那个陷入困境的朋友的贷款人。|近义词：creditor, loan provider |反义词：borrower


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bank lends money to small businesses. = 银行向小型企业贷款。
He lends his car to his brother every weekend. = 他每个周末都把车借给他的兄弟。


一般过去时:
She lent me her bicycle last week. = 她上周把自行车借给了我。
The company lent money to the struggling startup. = 这家公司向那家苦苦挣扎的初创公司贷款。


现在完成时:
We have lent him a considerable amount of money. = 我们已经借给他一大笔钱。
They have lent their support to the charity. = 他们已经向慈善机构提供了支持。


过去完成时:
I had lent him the book before he asked for it. = 在他问我要书之前，我已经把书借给他了。
The government had lent significant funds to the infrastructure project. = 政府已经向基础设施项目提供了大量资金。


将来时:
They will lend us the equipment for the project. = 他们将把设备借给我们用于该项目。
The bank will lend money at a lower interest rate next year. = 银行明年将以较低的利率贷款。


虚拟语气:
If I had a lot of money, I would lend it to you. = 如果我有很多钱，我会借给你。
I wish I could lend you the money, but I'm broke. = 我真希望我能把钱借给你，但我身无分文。


被动语态:
Money was lent to the farmers by the government. = 政府向农民提供了贷款。
The equipment will be lent to the researchers next week. = 设备将于下周借给研究人员。


疑问句:
Will you lend me your pen? = 你愿意把你的钢笔借给我吗？
Does the bank lend money to students? = 银行向学生贷款吗？


否定句:
I cannot lend you any more money. = 我不能再借给你任何钱了。
He didn't lend his car to anyone that day. = 那天他没有把车借给任何人。
