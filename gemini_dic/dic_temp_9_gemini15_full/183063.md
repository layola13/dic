repolarize:/ˌriːˈpəʊləraɪz/ | vt. | 再极化；重新极化 | To restore the polarization of (something, especially a cell membrane) after depolarization.  This process is crucial for the proper functioning of nerve and muscle cells. | Example: After the action potential, the neuron membrane repolarizes.  神经元膜在动作电位后重新极化。|近义词：restore polarization, re-establish polarization |反义词：depolarize | 动词
