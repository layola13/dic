embank:/ɪmˈbæŋk/| n. v.|筑堤；堤岸；|A bank or wall of earth or stone built to prevent a river or sea from overflowing.|The river was embanked to protect the town from flooding. 河流筑起了堤坝以保护城镇免受洪水侵害。|堤坝，河岸，护岸|enbank, dike, levee|无|

USAGE->

They embank the river every year. = 他们每年都修筑河堤。
The river embankments protect the low-lying areas. = 河堤保护着低洼地区。
