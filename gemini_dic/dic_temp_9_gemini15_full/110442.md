likens:/ˈlaɪkənz/| vt.|比作；比拟；使相似|To represent or describe (someone or something) as similar to someone or something else.|The poet likens the moon to a silver coin.  诗人把月亮比作一枚银币。|近义词：compare, equate, associate|反义词：differentiate, distinguish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He likens his life to a journey. = 他把他的人生比作一次旅程。
She likens the situation to a powder keg. = 她把形势比作火药桶。


一般过去时:
The speaker likened the government to a ship. = 演讲者将政府比作一艘船。
He likened her singing to that of an angel. = 他把她唱歌的声音比作天使的歌声。


现在完成时:
The critics have likened his work to that of Picasso. = 批评家们把他作品比作毕加索的作品。
She has likened the experience to a nightmare. = 她把这次经历比作一场噩梦。


过去完成时:
They had likened the ancient city to a labyrinth. = 他们曾把这座古城比作迷宫。
He had likened the situation to a ticking time bomb. = 他曾把形势比作一颗定时炸弹。


将来时:
She will liken the upcoming election to a battle. = 她将把即将到来的选举比作一场战斗。
He will liken the new technology to a miracle. = 他将把这项新技术比作奇迹。


虚拟语气:
If I were to liken my feelings, I would say they are like a storm. = 如果我要比喻我的感受，我会说它们像暴风雨。
I wish I could liken the experience to something more positive. = 我希望我能把这段经历比作一些更积极的东西。


被动语态:
His writing style is often likened to Hemingway's. = 他的写作风格经常被比作海明威的。
The painting was likened to a masterpiece. = 这幅画被比作杰作。


疑问句:
How would you liken the two candidates? = 你会如何将这两位候选人进行比较？
To what does the author liken the protagonist's journey? = 作者将主人公的旅程比作什么？


否定句:
He did not liken the situation to a crisis. = 他没有把形势比作危机。
She does not liken her work to art. = 她不认为她的作品是艺术。



PHRASE->
liken A to B = 把A比作B
liken something to something else = 把某事物比作其他事物
be likened to = 被比作
often likened to = 经常被比作
liken oneself to = 自比为
