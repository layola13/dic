domesticated:/dəˈmɛstɪkeɪtɪd/| adj.|驯化的；家养的；国内的；家庭的 |  Having been brought from a wild state to live with and be controlled by humans; relating to the home or family; relating to or occurring within a country's own borders. | 例句：Cats are domesticated animals. 猫是家养动物。  The domesticated dog is man's best friend. 家养的狗是人类最好的朋友。 | 近义词：tamed, cultured, civilized | 反义词：wild, untamed, feral
