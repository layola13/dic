premeasuring: /ˌpriːˈmɛʒərɪŋ/ | v. | 预先测量，预先计量 | The act of measuring something before it is used or processed.  It involves determining the dimensions, quantity, or other properties of an object or substance in advance. | Example:  Premeasuring the ingredients ensured the recipe's success. 预先测量配料确保了食谱的成功。| 近义词：pre-measuring, advance measuring | 反义词：post-measuring
