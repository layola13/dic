hospitalize:/ˈhɑːspɪtəlaɪz/| vt.|送…进医院；住院治疗|To place someone in a hospital for treatment or observation.  |例句：The doctor decided to hospitalize him after the accident. 医生决定事故后让他住院治疗。|例句：She was hospitalized for a week due to pneumonia.  她因肺炎住院一周。|近义词：admit to hospital, confine to hospital |反义词：discharge, release from hospital


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hospital hospitalizes many patients every day. = 医院每天收治许多病人。
They hospitalize children with serious illnesses. = 他们收治患有严重疾病的儿童。

一般过去时:
The surgeon hospitalized the patient immediately after the operation. = 手术后，外科医生立即将病人送往医院。
They hospitalized her for observation. = 他们将她送院观察。

现在完成时:
They have hospitalized over 100 patients this month. = 这个月他们已经收治了100多名病人。
The hospital has hospitalized many victims of the earthquake. = 医院收治了许多地震的受害者。

过去完成时:
He had been hospitalized twice before this incident. = 在这次事件之前，他已经住院两次了。
She had already been hospitalized when her family arrived. = 当她家人到达时，她已经住院了。

将来时:
The doctor thinks he will be hospitalized for at least a week. = 医生认为他至少要住院一周。
They will hospitalize her for further tests. = 他们会将她送院进行进一步检查。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't been hospitalized, he might have died. = 如果他没有住院，他可能已经死了。
It would have been better if she had been hospitalized earlier. = 如果她早点住院就好了。

被动语态:
Many patients are hospitalized due to heart problems. = 许多病人因心脏问题住院。
He was hospitalized against his will. = 他被强迫住院。

疑问句:
Will she be hospitalized? = 她会被送进医院吗？
Was he hospitalized after the car crash? = 车祸后他住院了吗？

否定句:
They did not hospitalize the patient because his condition was stable. = 因为他的病情稳定，他们没有让他住院。
The doctors decided not to hospitalize her. = 医生决定不让她住院。
