deficients:/dɪˈfɪʃənts/| n.|缺陷者；不足之处|People or things lacking something essential.|The report highlighted the deficiencies in the company's safety procedures.  这份报告强调了公司安全程序中的缺陷。|近义词：shortcomings, flaws, defects|反义词：perfections, strengths|名词

These deficiencies in the system need to be addressed immediately. = 系统中的这些缺陷需要立即解决。
