quasi-mechanically: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ mɪˈkænɪkli/ | adj. | 准机械地，似机械地 |  Acting or functioning in a manner resembling a machine, often implying a lack of spontaneity or flexibility.  It suggests a process that is systematic and predictable, but not entirely automatic or devoid of some degree of control or influence. |

例句：

The assembly line operated quasi-mechanically, with workers performing repetitive tasks with precision. =  装配线准机械化地运行，工人们精确地执行着重复性的任务。

The politician's responses to questions were so formulaic that they seemed quasi-mechanically generated. = 这位政治家对问题的回答是如此公式化，以至于看起来像是准机械化生成的。


近义词：automatically, robotically, systematically, routinely, mechanically


反义词：spontaneously, organically, freely, creatively


词性：形容词


词源:

* **quasi-**:  from Latin, meaning "as if," "seemingly," or "almost."  It indicates a resemblance or approximation, but not a complete identity.

* **mechanically**: from "mechanism," ultimately derived from Greek words relating to machines and tools.
