reproducibility:/ˌriːprədʌsɪˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可重复性，可复制性 | The ability of a study or experiment to be repeated by others with the same results.  It's a crucial aspect of scientific rigor, ensuring the validity and reliability of findings.  | Example: The high reproducibility of the experiment confirmed the accuracy of the initial findings.  高可重复性的实验证实了最初发现的准确性。 |近义词：repeatability, replicability | 反义词：unreliability, irreproducibility |
