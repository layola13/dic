hypnotics:/hɪpˈnɒtɪks/| n. |催眠药；安眠药 |Substances that induce sleep.  They are often used to treat insomnia and other sleep disorders.  However, they can be habit-forming and should only be used under the supervision of a doctor. |例句：The doctor prescribed hypnotics to help him sleep.  医生开了催眠药帮助他睡觉。

例句：Long-term use of hypnotics can lead to dependence. 长期服用催眠药会导致依赖。


近义词：sedatives, sleeping pills, somnifics

反义词：stimulants


词性：名词
