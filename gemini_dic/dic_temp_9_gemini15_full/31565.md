chased:/tʃeɪst/| vt.|追赶；追捕；追求；驱逐|To pursue or follow someone or something quickly in order to catch them.  It can also refer to pursuing a goal or objective.|例句：The police chased the robbers through the streets.  警察在街上追捕劫匪。

例句：She chased her dream of becoming a doctor. 她追求成为一名医生的梦想。


近义词：pursue, hunt, follow, track|反义词：escape, evade, abandon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chases the ball. 他追球。
She chases her dreams. 她追逐她的梦想。

一般过去时:
The dog chased the cat. 狗追赶猫。
They chased the thief down the street. 他们追捕小偷跑下街。

现在完成时:
I have chased my goals for many years. 我多年来一直在追求我的目标。
She has chased away all the bad memories. 她已经驱散了所有不好的回忆。


过去完成时:
He had chased the rabbit for hours before catching it. 他追了兔子几个小时才抓住它。
They had chased after success for years before they finally achieved it. 他们追逐成功多年后才最终实现它。


将来时:
