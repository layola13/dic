/ˌriːəˈfɒrɪstɪŋ/ | v. | 再造林；重新造林 | The act of planting trees in an area that has been deforested.  It aims to restore forests to their former glory, improving biodiversity, mitigating climate change, and providing various ecological and economic benefits. | Example:  The government is investing heavily in reafforesting the deforested hillsides. = 政府正在大力投资对砍伐森林的山坡进行重新造林。

The country is committed to reafforesting millions of acres of land. = 该国致力于对数百万英亩土地进行重新造林。


| 近义词: reforestation, afforestation, replanting | 反义词: deforestation, deforesting |  动词
