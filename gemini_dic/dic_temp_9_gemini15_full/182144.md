remix:/ˈriːmɪks/| n. vt.|混音；重混；重新组合|A remix is a new version of a song or piece of music, created by combining or altering the original recording.|例句：The DJ played a remix of the popular song.  DJ播放了这首流行歌曲的混音版。

例句：She remixed the track with a faster beat. 她把这首歌的节奏加快重新混音了。|近义词：remake, re-edit,  remaster|反义词：original
