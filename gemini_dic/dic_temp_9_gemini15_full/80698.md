gorgonian:/ˈɡɔːrɡənɪən/| n. | Gorgonian, 海扇 | A gorgonian is a type of colonial coral, belonging to the order Gorgonacea, characterized by a hard, often branched skeleton made of gorgonin, a horny protein. They are found in a wide range of marine environments and come in various colors and shapes.  They are important components of coral reef ecosystems. | Example: The divers marveled at the vibrant colors of the gorgonians swaying in the ocean current.  潜水员惊叹于在洋流中摇曳的海扇的鲜艳色彩。|近义词: sea fan, coral |反义词:  None specifically, but could be contrasted with soft corals. | 名词
