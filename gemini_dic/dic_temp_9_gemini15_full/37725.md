compares:/kəmˈpeərz/| v.|比较，对照|To examine or judge the similarities and differences between two or more things.|例句：She compares apples and oranges. 她比较苹果和橘子。|例句：He compares his current job to his previous one. 他将他目前的工作与之前的工作进行比较。|近义词：contrast, liken, differentiate|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He compares his work to a marathon. = 他把他的工作比作马拉松。
She compares prices before buying anything. = 她买东西之前都会比较价格。

一般过去时:
They compared notes after the exam. = 考试后他们互相比较了答案。
The teacher compared the two essays. = 老师比较了两篇论文。

现在完成时:
I have compared several different plans. = 我已经比较了几种不同的方案。
She has compared the two products and found one to be superior. = 她已经比较了两种产品，发现其中一种更好。

过去完成时:
He had compared the data before drawing his conclusions. = 他在得出结论之前已经比较了数据。
They had compared their results with others before submitting their report. = 他们在提交报告之前已经将他们的结果与其他人的结果进行了比较。


将来时:
We will compare the two designs next week. = 我们下周将比较这两种设计。
She will compare the offers before making a decision. = 她会在做决定之前比较这些报价。


虚拟语气:
If I were to compare the two, I would choose the latter. = 如果让我比较两者，我会选择后者。
I wish I had compared the prices earlier. = 我真希望我早点比较一下价格。

被动语态:
The two paintings were compared by the art critic. = 两幅画被艺术评论家进行了比较。
The results will be compared by an independent auditor. = 结果将由独立审计师进行比较。

疑问句:
How do you compare these two approaches? = 你如何比较这两种方法？
Can you compare the features of these two cars? = 你能比较一下这两款车的功能吗？

否定句:
He didn't compare the options carefully. = 他没有仔细比较这些选择。
They didn't compare their notes after the meeting. = 会议后他们没有互相比较笔记。


PHRASE->
compare notes = 互相交换意见
compare and contrast = 比较和对比
compare to = 比作
compare with = 与…相比
in comparison with = 与…相比
