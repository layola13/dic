mouldier:/ˈmoʊldiər/| adj.| 更发霉的；更潮湿的 | More moldy; more damp.  The bread is mouldier than I thought. 这面包比我想象的更发霉了。|近义词：more moldy, damper |反义词：cleaner, drier


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The basement is mouldier than usual. = 地下室比平时更潮湿。
This old book smells mouldier than that one. = 这本旧书比那本闻起来更霉。


一般过去时:
The room was mouldier after the rain. = 雨后房间更潮湿了。
The food became mouldier overnight. = 食物一夜之间更发霉了。


现在完成时:
The clothes have become mouldier since being left in the damp. = 这些衣服自从放在潮湿的地方后就更发霉了。
The walls have gotten mouldier over the years. = 多年来，墙壁越来越潮湿了。


过去完成时:

将来时:
