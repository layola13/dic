hollered:/ˈhɒləd/ /ˈhɑːlərd/| v.| 大喊；叫喊 | To shout or yell loudly.  |例句：He hollered for help. 他大声呼救。

例句：The children hollered with laughter. 孩子们大声笑着。


近义词：yell, shout, scream, bellow, cry out

反义词：whisper, murmur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hollers at the top of his lungs. = 他扯着嗓子大喊。
She hollers instructions from the sidelines. = 她从场边大声喊出指令。


一般过去时:
He hollered at the referee. = 他朝裁判大喊。
She hollered when she saw the spider. = 她看到蜘蛛时尖叫起来。


现在完成时:
He has hollered at me several times today. = 他今天已经对我大喊了几次了。
She has hollered her disapproval from the balcony. = 她从阳台上大声表达了她的不满。


过去完成时:
He had hollered for help before he fainted. = 他晕倒之前曾大声呼救。
She had hollered warnings to the others before the accident. = 事故发生前，她曾向其他人大声发出警告。


将来时:
He will holler if he doesn't get his way. = 如果他不遂心愿，他会大喊大叫。
She will holler when she sees the surprise. = 当她看到惊喜时，她会大声喊叫。


虚拟语气:
If he hollered louder, someone might hear him. = 如果他喊得更大声一些，可能会有人听到他。
I wish he hadn't hollered so loud. = 我真希望他不要喊那么大声。


被动语态:
His name was hollered across the field. = 他的名字在田野上传来传去。
The instructions were hollered repeatedly. = 指令被反复大声喊叫。


疑问句:
Did he holler at you? = 他对你大喊大叫了吗？
Why did she holler? = 她为什么大喊？


否定句:
He didn't holler this time. = 这次他没有大喊。
She didn't holler even though she was scared. = 尽管她很害怕，但她并没有大喊。


PHRASE->
holler back = 回喊
holler at = 对...大喊
holler down = 大声喝斥
holler out = 大声喊出
holler for = 大声呼喊（求救等）
