fuddler:/ˈfʌd(ə)lər/| n. |笨蛋，傻瓜，业余爱好者|A fuddler is someone who is unskilled or inept at a particular task or activity, often implying a lack of seriousness or expertise.  It can also refer to someone who is foolish or muddle-headed.|Example: He's a complete fuddler when it comes to fixing cars. 他修车完全是个笨手笨脚的人。|近义词：amateur, novice, bungler, dilettante |反义词：expert, professional, master
