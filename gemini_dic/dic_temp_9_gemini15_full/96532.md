indoles:/ˈɪndoʊlz/ | n. |吲哚类化合物|Indoles are a group of organic compounds containing the indole ring, a bicyclic structure consisting of a benzene ring fused to a pyrrole ring.  They are found widely in nature and have various biological activities. | Example:  Tryptophan, a crucial amino acid, is an indole derivative.  色氨酸，一种重要的氨基酸，是一种吲哚衍生物。 |近义词:  (no direct synonyms, but related terms include:  heterocycles, aromatic compounds) |反义词: (no direct antonyms) |名词
