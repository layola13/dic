cause:/kɔːz/|ˈkɑːz/| n./vt.|原因；事业；理由；引起；导致|  A factor that contributes to a result or outcome; to make something happen.|例句：The cause of the fire was a faulty electrical wire. = 火灾的原因是电线故障。

例句： Heavy rain caused the flood. = 大雨导致了洪水。


近义词：reason, reason, origin, effect, lead to, bring about|反义词：effect, result


词性:名词和动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wind causes the leaves to fall. = 风使树叶落下。
This medicine causes drowsiness. = 这药会引起嗜睡。


一般过去时:
The storm caused widespread damage. = 暴风雨造成了广泛的破坏。
His carelessness caused the accident. = 他的粗心导致了事故。


现在完成时:
The drought has caused severe crop failures. = 干旱已经导致了严重的农作物歉收。
Pollution has caused significant environmental damage. = 污染已经造成了严重的生态破坏。


过去完成时:
The heavy snow had caused the train to be delayed. = 大雪导致火车延误了。
His poor planning had caused the project to fail. = 他糟糕的计划导致了项目的失败。


将来时:
The new law will cause many problems. = 新法将引起许多问题。
The storm is expected to cause power outages. = 预计暴风雨将导致停电。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I wouldn't have caused so much trouble. = 如果我知道的话，我就不会造成这么大的麻烦。
I wish I hadn't caused him so much pain. = 我真希望我没有给他造成那么多的痛苦。


被动语态:
The accident was caused by a reckless driver. = 事故是由一名鲁莽的司机造成的。
The damage was caused by the earthquake. = 损害是由地震造成的。


疑问句:
What caused the explosion? = 什么导致了爆炸？
What caused the delay? = 什么导致了延误？


否定句:
The rain didn't cause any damage. = 雨没有造成任何损失。
His actions didn't cause any harm. = 他的行为没有造成任何伤害。



PHRASE->
cause and effect = 因果关系
root cause = 根源
just cause = 正当理由
common cause = 共同原因
contributory cause = 促成原因
