mysteriarch:/ˈmɪstəriˌɑːrk/| n.|神秘的首领，女族长|A mysteriarch is a female leader or matriarch who is mysterious, powerful, and often associated with secrets or occult practices. The term blends "mystery" and "matriarch," highlighting the enigmatic nature of the character.|例句：The old woman, a mysteriarch of the secluded village, held the key to ancient secrets.  那位隐秘村庄的神秘女族长掌握着古代秘密的钥匙。|近义词：matriarch, sorceress, enchantress|反义词：follower, subordinate
