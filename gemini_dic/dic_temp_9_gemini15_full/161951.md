prances:/ˈprænsɪz/| v.| 腾跃，欢快地小跑 | To move with quick, light steps, especially in a lively or proud way.  |例句：The horse pranced proudly around the ring. 这匹马在场地里骄傲地腾跃。
The children pranced excitedly as they waited for the show to begin.孩子们兴奋地蹦跳着等待演出开始。|近义词：caper, skip, prance about|反义词：trudge, lumber


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prances around the stage. = 他在舞台上蹦跳。
The foal prances in the pasture. = 小马驹在牧场上欢快地跳跃。

一般过去时:
The dancer pranced gracefully across the floor. = 舞者优雅地在舞池里跳跃。
She pranced happily down the street. = 她高兴地在街上蹦跳着走。


现在完成时:
The colt has pranced all afternoon. = 小马驹整个下午都在跳跃。
He has pranced through life with great enthusiasm. = 他一直以极大的热情地生活。


过去完成时:
The pony had pranced before the crowd many times. = 这匹小马以前在人群面前跳跃过很多次。
Before the performance, she had pranced nervously backstage. = 演出之前，她在后台紧张地蹦跳着。


将来时:
The horses will prance during the parade. = 马会在游行时腾跃。
She will prance with joy when she hears the news. = 当她听到这个消息时，她会高兴地跳跃。


虚拟语气:
If I could prance like that, I would. = 如果我能像那样跳跃，我会的。
I wish I could prance with such confidence. = 我希望我能如此自信地跳跃。


被动语态:
(The verb "prance" is rarely used in the passive voice because it implies a voluntary action.)


疑问句:
Does the horse prance when it's happy? = 马高兴的时候会腾跃吗？
Will the children prance at the party? = 孩子们会在派对上蹦跳吗？


否定句:
The horse didn't prance; it walked calmly. = 马没有腾跃；它平静地走着。
He didn't prance; he just walked. = 他没有蹦跳；他只是走着。
