permitting: /pərˈmɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 允许；许可；容许 |  Allowing or consenting to something.  The act of granting permission. |


例句：
The city is permitting the construction of a new park. = 城市允许建造一个新的公园。

Permitting the use of cell phones in class is a controversial issue. = 允许在课堂上使用手机是一个有争议的问题。


近义词：allowing, authorizing, enabling, sanctioning


反义词：prohibiting, forbidding, preventing


词性：动词


词源：源自动词permit，加上-ing构成现在分词，用作动名词或形容词。permit来源于古法语"permettre"，最终来源于拉丁语"permittere"，由"per-" (through, thoroughly) 和 "mittere" (to send, let go) 组合而成。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Permitting this behavior is a mistake. = 允许这种行为是错误的。
The school is permitting students to use laptops. = 学校允许学生使用笔记本电脑。

一般过去时:
Permitting the late submission was a one-time exception. = 允许延迟提交是一次性的例外。
They were permitting visitors until late evening. = 他们允许访客直到深夜。

现在完成时:
Having permitted the changes, we now see the positive impact. = 允许这些改变后，我们现在看到了积极的影响。
The company has been permitting remote work since the pandemic. = 自从疫情以来，公司一直允许远程办公。

过去完成时:
Having permitted the earlier requests, they felt obligated to permit this one too. = 因为之前允许了请求，所以他们觉得有义务也允许这个请求。
They had permitted the use of the facility until the incident. = 在事件发生之前，他们允许使用该设施。

将来时:
The council will be permitting the parade to proceed down Main Street. = 理事会将允许游行队伍沿着主要街道前进。
We will not be permitting any unauthorized access. = 我们将不允许任何未经授权的访问。

虚拟语气:
If they permitted it, I would attend. = 如果他们允许的话，我会参加。
It would be better if they permitted more flexibility. = 如果他们允许更大的灵活性就好了。

被动语态:
The construction was permitted by the council. = 建设得到了理事会的允许。
The event was not permitted due to safety concerns. = 由于安全问题，该活动未被允许。

疑问句:
Is permitting dogs in the park allowed? = 公园允许带狗吗？
Will they be permitting late entries? = 他们会允许迟到参赛吗？

否定句:
They are not permitting any exceptions to the rule. = 他们不允许任何例外情况。
We did not permit the use of outside materials. = 我们不允许使用外部材料。
