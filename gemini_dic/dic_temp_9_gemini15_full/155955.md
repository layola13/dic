photogenic:/ˌfoʊtoʊˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. |上镜的，照相好看的|  photogenic describes someone or something that looks attractive in photographs. It suggests that the subject has qualities that translate well to a photographic image, such as good features, expressive eyes, or a naturally pleasing composition.  |Example: She is incredibly photogenic; every picture of her is stunning. 她非常上镜，每张照片都美极了。|近义词：attractive, picturesque, appealing|反义词：unattractive, unflattering
