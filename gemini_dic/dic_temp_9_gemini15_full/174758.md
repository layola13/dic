rabbitish:/ˈræbɪtɪʃ/| adj.|像兔子的；胆怯的；慌张的；|Resembling a rabbit; timid; nervous; quick and jerky in movement.|The dog displayed rabbitish fear when faced with the large dog.|近义词：timid, fearful, skittish, nervous|反义词：bold, brave, courageous


USAGE->

The kitten moves in a rabbitish way. = 这只小猫的动作很像兔子。
