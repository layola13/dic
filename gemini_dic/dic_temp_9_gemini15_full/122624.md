modicum:/ˈmɒdɪkəm/| /ˈmɑːdɪkəm/ | n. |少量，一点儿 |A small or moderate amount; a limited quantity.  It suggests a small but sufficient amount, often implying just enough to satisfy a basic need or requirement. |She showed a modicum of grace under pressure. 她展现出一点承受压力的优雅。 |a little, a bit, slightly,  |abundance, profusion, plethora, excess |名词
