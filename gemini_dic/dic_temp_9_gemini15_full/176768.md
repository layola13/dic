reader:/ˈriːdər/ | n. |读者，阅读者 |A person who reads.  |He is a voracious reader.|近义词：peruser, bookworm |反义词：writer, author |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a careful reader. = 他是一位细心的读者。
She reads novels every night. = 她每晚都读小说。


一般过去时:
He was a fast reader in his youth. = 他年轻的时候阅读速度很快。
She read the entire book in one sitting. = 她一口气读完了整本书。


现在完成时:
I have read that book twice. = 我读过那本书两次了。
She has read many classic novels. = 她读过许多经典小说。


过去完成时:
I had read the instructions before I started the project. = 我在开始项目之前已经阅读了说明。
They had read the report before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经阅读了报告。


将来时:
I will read that new bestseller next week. = 我下周会读那本畅销书。
She will have finished reading the book by tomorrow. = 她明天之前就会读完那本书。


虚拟语气:
If I were a better reader, I would understand this more easily. = 如果我是一个更好的读者，我会更容易理解这一点。
I wish I had read that book sooner. = 我真希望我早点读那本书。


被动语态:
The book was read by many people. = 这本书被很多人读过。
The manuscript is being read by the editor. = 手稿正在被编辑阅读。


疑问句:
Are you a frequent reader? = 你是一个经常阅读的人吗？
Who is the intended reader of this book? = 这本书的目标读者是谁？


否定句:
He is not a fast reader. = 他不是一个快速的读者。
She didn't read the email. = 她没有读那封邮件。
