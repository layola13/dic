oxalis:/ˈɒksəlɪs/| n. |酢浆草属植物|Oxalis is a genus of flowering plants in the wood sorrel family Oxalidaceae.  They are known for their characteristic clover-like leaves and often brightly colored flowers. Many species are cultivated as ornamentals. |The oxalis in my garden are blooming beautifully.  我花园里的酢浆草开得真漂亮。| clover, shamrock|  (no direct antonym)|名词
