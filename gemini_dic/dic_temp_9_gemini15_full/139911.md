modeling:/ˈmɒdəlɪŋ/ | /ˈmɑːdəlɪŋ/ | n. | 建模；造型；模仿；模特行业 | The act of creating a model or representation of something.  This can refer to creating a physical model, a mathematical model, or a fashion model.  | Example:  The architect presented a detailed modeling of the new building. 建筑师展示了新建筑的详细模型。

The fashion industry relies heavily on modeling. 时尚产业严重依赖模特行业。


近义词：modelling, simulation, representation, emulation


反义词：deconstruction, demolition, destruction


词性：名词
