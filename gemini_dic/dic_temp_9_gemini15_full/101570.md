ironwork:/ˈaɪərnwɜːrk/| n.|铁器；铁艺制品；铁工技艺|The art or practice of making objects from wrought iron, often decorative.  It encompasses a wide range of items, from gates and railings to furniture and sculptures.|例句：The intricate ironwork on the balcony was a stunning example of the craftsman's skill.  阳台上的精巧铁艺是工匠技艺的绝佳范例。

例句：The blacksmith was renowned for his exquisite ironwork.  这位铁匠以其精美的铁器而闻名。


近义词：metalwork, wrought iron, smithwork|反义词：无

词源:
The word "ironwork" is a compound word combining "iron" (Old English īsern, from Proto-Germanic *īsarnaz) and "work" (Old English weorc, from Proto-Germanic *werkō).  It refers to objects or structures made of iron, particularly those involving skilled craftsmanship.
