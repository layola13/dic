rootle:/ˈruːtəl/ | v. | 翻找；搜寻；刨；挖掘 | To search for something by moving things around carelessly or roughly.  |例句：The dog was rootling around in the garden.  这只狗在花园里乱翻。
例句：She rootled around in her bag for her keys. 她翻遍了她的包找钥匙。|近义词：rummage, search, ferret, hunt |反义词：organize, tidy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rootles around in the attic every weekend. 他每个周末都在阁楼里翻找东西。
They rootle through the trash looking for recyclables. 他们翻找垃圾寻找可回收物。

一般过去时:
She rootled in the drawer until she found her passport. 她翻找抽屉直到找到她的护照。
The pigs rootled in the mud looking for food. 猪在泥里拱来拱去寻找食物。

现在完成时:
He has rootled through all his old papers and found some interesting letters. 他翻遍了他所有的旧文件，找到了一些有趣的信件。
They have rootled around in the garden but haven't found the missing toy. 他们在花园里翻找了，但是没有找到丢失的玩具。

过去完成时:
The dog had rootled up the entire flower bed before I could stop him.  在我阻止他之前，狗已经把整个花坛都翻了个底朝天。
She had rootled through the box several times before she finally found what she was looking for. 她已经翻找了好几次那个箱子，才最终找到她要找的东西。

将来时:
I will rootle around in the cupboard for some old photos. 我会在橱柜里翻找一些旧照片。
They will rootle through the archives for information about the family history.他们会在档案中翻找有关家族历史的信息。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rootle through the library archives. 如果我有更多的时间，我会翻找图书馆的档案。
I wish I could rootle around in my grandmother's attic; I bet there are treasures there. 我真希望我能翻翻我祖母的阁楼；我敢打赌那里有宝贝。

被动语态:
The garden was rootled up by the pigs. 花园被猪拱翻了。
The old chest was rootled through by the curious children.  那个旧箱子被好奇的孩子们翻找过了。

疑问句:
Was the dog rootling in the trash again?  那只狗又在翻垃圾了吗？
Have you been rootling in my drawers? 你是不是一直在翻我的抽屉？

否定句:
He didn't rootle around in the dirt. 他没有在泥土里翻找。
She didn't rootle through my belongings without asking. 她没有未经允许翻找我的东西。


PHRASE->
