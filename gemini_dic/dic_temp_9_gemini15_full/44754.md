crumbling: /ˈkrʌmbəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 崩塌的；破败的；逐渐崩溃的 |  Describing something that is breaking into small pieces or falling apart gradually.  It can refer to physical structures or systems that are deteriorating. |

例句：

The old bridge was crumbling, posing a danger to drivers. = 那座旧桥正在崩塌，对驾驶员构成危险。

His empire was crumbling under the weight of its own corruption. = 他的帝国在其自身腐败的重压下正在崩溃。


近义词：disintegrating, decaying, collapsing, deteriorating, falling apart

反义词：solid, stable, intact, strong, flourishing


词性：形容词


USAGE->

The building is crumbling. = 这座建筑物正在崩塌。
The system is crumbling under pressure. = 系统在压力下正在崩溃。
