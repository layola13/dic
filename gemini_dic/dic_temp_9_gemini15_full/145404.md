overcensor: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsər/ | vt. |过度审查；过度删减| To censor excessively; to remove or suppress more content than is necessary or justifiable.  The act of excessively censoring something. |例句：The government's overcensoring of the news led to public outrage.  政府对新闻的过度审查导致了公众的愤怒。 The film was overcensored, removing all traces of political dissent.  这部电影被过度审查，删除了所有政治异见的痕迹。|近义词：suppress, expurgate, bowdlerize |反义词：unfetter, liberalize |动词
