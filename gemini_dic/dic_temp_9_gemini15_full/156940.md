pilferage:/ˈpɪlfərɪdʒ/| n.|偷窃；少量盗窃|The act of stealing things of little value.  It often refers to small-scale thefts, usually from a workplace or other setting.|例句：The company suffered significant losses due to pilferage. 公司因盗窃而遭受了重大损失。

例句：Police are investigating a case of pilferage from the warehouse. 警方正在调查仓库失窃案。


近义词：theft, larceny, petty theft, shoplifting|反义词：donation, contribution|词性:名词
