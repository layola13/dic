fattish:/ˈfætiʃ/| adj.|稍胖的，有点胖的|Slightly fat; somewhat overweight.  The adjective "fattish" is a less harsh way to describe someone as fat, implying a moderate degree of overweight rather than extreme obesity.  It is often used informally.|例句：He's a bit fattish, but still quite healthy. 他有点胖，但身体还是很健康。

例句：She's a fattish woman, but very kind. 她有点胖，但是人很好。


近义词：plump, chubby, stout, overweight|反义词：slim, slender, thin, skinny


词性：形容词
