eject:/ɪˈdʒɛkt/ | vt. |弹出，喷射，驱逐 | To force or throw something or someone out, typically forcefully.  |例句：The pilot ejected from the plane.  飞行员从飞机上弹射出来。|例句：The CD player ejected the disc.  CD播放器弹出了光盘。|近义词：expel, oust, dismiss |反义词：insert, admit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ejects the disc from the player. = 他从播放器中弹出光盘。
The volcano ejects lava. = 火山喷射熔岩。


一般过去时:
The passenger was ejected from the vehicle. = 乘客被从车辆中弹出。
The machine ejected the faulty part. = 机器弹出了故障部件。


现在完成时:
The system has ejected the user. = 系统已将用户注销。
The rocket has ejected its payload. = 火箭已经弹射出有效载荷。


过去完成时:
The pilot had ejected before the plane crashed. = 飞机坠毁前飞行员已经弹射出来。
The machine had ejected all the components before shutting down. = 机器在关机前已经弹出了所有组件。


将来时:
The capsule will eject the astronauts. = 胶囊将弹射出宇航员。
The mechanism will eject the waste material. = 机制将排出废料。


虚拟语气:
If the pilot had ejected earlier, he might have survived. = 如果飞行员早点弹射出来，他可能就活下来了。
I wish the system hadn't ejected me. = 我真希望系统没有把我注销。


被动语态:
The passenger was ejected from the car during the accident. = 乘客在事故中被从车里弹出。
The waste products are ejected into the atmosphere. = 废物被排放到大气中。


疑问句:
Does the computer eject the disc automatically? = 电脑会自动弹出光盘吗？

否定句:
The button didn't eject the tray. = 按钮没有弹出托盘。
The system failed to eject the user. = 系统未能注销用户。
