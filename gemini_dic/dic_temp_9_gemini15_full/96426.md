indissolubly:/ˌɪndɪˈsɒljʊbli/ | adj. |不可分割地；牢不可破地 | Inseparably; incapable of being dissolved or separated.  The word implies a strong and permanent connection that cannot be broken or undone. |例句：Their fates were indissolubly linked. 他们的命运不可分割地联系在一起。

Their friendship was indissolubly bound by shared experiences. 他们的友谊因共同的经历而牢不可破地维系着。|近义词： inseparably, inextricably, permanently, unbreakably |反义词：separably, dissolvably, breakably
