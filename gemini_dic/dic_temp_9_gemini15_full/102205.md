italicize:/ɪˈtælɪsaɪz/ | vt. | 斜体书写；强调 | To set (text) in italic type.  This is often done to emphasize a word or phrase, or to indicate a title of a work. | Example: Please italicize the book titles in your bibliography. 请在你的参考文献中将书名斜体化。|近义词：emphasize, highlight | 反义词：boldface


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He italicizes important words. = 他将重要的单词斜体化。
She italicizes the names of books. = 她将书名斜体化。

一般过去时:
I italicized the title of the essay. = 我将文章标题斜体化了。
They italicized all the foreign words. = 他们将所有外来词都斜体化了。

现在完成时:
I have italicized all the keywords. = 我已经将所有关键词都斜体化了。
She has italicized every proper noun. = 她已经将每个专有名词都斜体化了。

过去完成时:
He had italicized the key terms before submitting the paper. = 他在提交论文之前已经将关键词斜体化了。
They had italicized the quotes before printing the book. = 他们在印刷书籍之前已经将引语斜体化了。


将来时:
I will italicize the names of the authors. = 我将把作者的名字斜体化。
She will italicize all the Latin phrases. = 她将把所有拉丁语短语斜体化。


虚拟语气:
If I were writing a paper, I would italicize the titles. = 如果我正在写论文，我会将标题斜体化。
I wish I had italicized the names earlier. = 我希望我早点把名字斜体化。


被动语态:
The names were italicized by the editor. = 编辑将姓名斜体化了。
The titles should be italicized in the index. = 在索引中应将标题斜体化。


疑问句:
Should I italicize this word? = 我应该将这个词斜体化吗？
Are the book titles italicized in this style guide? = 在这个风格指南中，书名是否斜体化？


否定句:
I didn't italicize the proper nouns. = 我没有将专有名词斜体化。
He does not italicize words unnecessarily. = 他不会不必要地将单词斜体化。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases directly using "italicize".  The verb is used straightforwardly to describe the action of applying italic formatting.  Instead, phrases related to emphasis or formatting might be used:


* emphasize the importance of... = 强调...的重要性
* highlight the key points...= 突出要点...
* put...in italics = 把...用斜体
* use italics for...= 对...使用斜体
* draw attention to... = 引起对...的注意


Word Origin:  From Italian *italico*, literally "Italian," referring to the italic style of typeface, which originated in Italy.
