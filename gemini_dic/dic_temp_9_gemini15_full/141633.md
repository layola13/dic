optimality:/ˌɒptɪˈmælɪti/| n. |最优性，最佳状态 |The state of being optimal; the best possible result under given conditions.  It refers to a solution or outcome that is the best or most favorable among all possible alternatives.  |例句：The algorithm strives for optimality in resource allocation.  该算法力求在资源分配方面达到最优。

The company's new strategy aims for operational optimality. 公司的新战略旨在实现运营最优。


近义词：optimum, perfection, excellence |反义词：suboptimality, imperfection, inferiority |词性：名词
