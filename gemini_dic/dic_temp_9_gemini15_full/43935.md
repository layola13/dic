crewmen:/ˈkruːmən/| n.|船员，全体船员|Members of a ship's crew.  The term often refers to the enlisted personnel, as opposed to officers.|例句：The crewmen worked tirelessly to repair the damage to the ship. 船员们夜以继日地努力修复船只的损坏。

例句：All the crewmen were mustered on deck for the emergency drill. 全体船员都被召集到甲板上进行紧急演习。


近义词：sailors, seamen, hands


反义词：passengers, officers


词性:名词
