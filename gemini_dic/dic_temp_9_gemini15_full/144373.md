outstinks:/ˌaʊtˈstɪŋks/| v.|胜过…的气味，臭味盖过|To surpass in bad odor; to smell worse than.  This is a relatively informal and humorous word.|例句：That old cheese outstinks the gym socks. 那块旧奶酪的气味比臭袜子还难闻。  The garbage outstinks the entire neighborhood. 垃圾的气味熏遍了整个街区。|近义词：overshadow (in smell), outdo (in unpleasantness)|反义词：outsmell (implying a pleasant smell)
