prefigures:/ˈpriːfɪɡərz/| vt.|预示，预兆；先于…而存在|To indicate or foreshadow something that will happen or exist later; to exist or occur before something else.|The dark clouds prefigured a storm.  乌云预示着一场暴风雨。

The ancient myths prefigure many of the themes found in modern literature.  古代神话预示了现代文学中许多主题。

|近义词：foreshadow, foretell, anticipate, precede|反义词：follow, succeed|动词
