sequestrable: /sɪˈkwɛstrəbl/ | adj. | 可扣押的，可没收的 | Capable of being sequestered; subject to sequestration.  This typically refers to assets, property, or funds that can be legally taken and held by a court or other authority during a legal proceeding. |  The judge ruled that the defendant's assets were sequestrable pending the outcome of the lawsuit. 法官裁定被告的资产可在诉讼结果出来之前被扣押。|近义词：confiscatable, attachable |反义词：unattachable, inalienable
