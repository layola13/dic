rooms:/ruːmz/| n.|房间，室；场所；空间|Rooms are spaces within a building, typically enclosed by walls and a ceiling.|例句：Our house has five rooms.  我们的房子有五个房间。|例句：The hotel offers rooms with a view. 这家酒店提供带景致的房间。|近义词：chambers, apartments, spaces|反义词：outside, outdoors


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The hotel has many rooms. = 这家酒店有很多房间。
This room needs cleaning. = 这个房间需要打扫。


一般过去时:
