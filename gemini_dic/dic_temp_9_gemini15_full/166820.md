press:/prɛs/| /ˈprɛs/| n./vt./vi.| 按；压；印刷；新闻；出版社；压力；紧迫；|To exert steady force on; to compress.  To print (something) using a printing press.  A company that publishes newspapers, magazines, or books. The act of pressing or state of being pressed.|例句：He pressed the button to start the machine. 他按下按钮启动机器。
The newspaper press reported on the accident. 报纸媒体报道了这起事故。|近义词：push, squeeze, print, publish, urge|反义词：release, loosen, relax
