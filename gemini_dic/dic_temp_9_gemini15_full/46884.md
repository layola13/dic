daringness:/ˈdɛərɪŋnəs/| n. |大胆；勇敢；冒险精神 |The quality of being daring; willingness to take risks.  It often implies a certain boldness and courage in the face of danger or uncertainty. |例句：Her daringness in tackling the difficult project impressed everyone.  她的大胆尝试那个困难的项目给每个人留下了深刻的印象。

例句：The climber's daringness was evident in his ascent of the treacherous peak. 登山者的勇敢在他的攀登险峻山峰的过程中显而易见。


近义词：audacity, bravery, courage, boldness, intrepidity
