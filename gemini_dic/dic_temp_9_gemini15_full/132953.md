nonemotionally: /ˌnɒnɪˈmoʊʃənəli/ | adj. |冷静地；不感情用事地；不动声色地 |Without showing or expressing emotion; in a calm and unemotional way.  |例句：He described the accident nonemotionally, focusing on the facts. 他冷静地描述了事故，重点关注事实。|例句：She delivered the bad news nonemotionally, her voice steady and calm. 她不动声色地带来了坏消息，声音稳定而平静。|近义词：calmly, dispassionately, impassively, objectively |反义词：emotionally, passionately, dramatically
