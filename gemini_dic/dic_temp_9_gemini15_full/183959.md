resewn:/riːˈsoʊn/| v. |缝合过的；重新缝合的 |To sew again.  This implies that something was previously sewn and then unsewn or needed further sewing.  |例句：The seam had come undone, so I had to resewn the dress.  这条缝线开了，所以我不得不重新缝合这条裙子。
