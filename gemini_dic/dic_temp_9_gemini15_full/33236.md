chronography:/krɒˈnɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 年代记；编年史；时间记录 | The detailed recording of events in chronological order, often including historical accounts and descriptions.  | Example:  The historian compiled a meticulous chronography of the reign of Queen Elizabeth I.  历史学家编纂了一部关于伊丽莎白一世女王统治时期详尽的编年史。

The author's chronography covered the major political and social changes of the century.  作者的年代记涵盖了本世纪主要的政治和社会变革。|近义词：chronicle, annals, record |反义词：None directly opposite, but could be contrasted with accounts lacking chronological order.
