on's:/ɒnz/| pron.| 某人的；他的，她的，它们的 |  A possessive pronoun indicating belonging to someone or something.  It's a contraction of "one's".|例句：One should always respect one's elders.  = 人们应该总是尊敬他们的长辈。
|例句：He lost his temper and forgot his manners, injuring one's reputation. = 他发脾气，忘了礼貌，损害了自己的名声。|近义词：his, her, its, their|反义词：no one's
