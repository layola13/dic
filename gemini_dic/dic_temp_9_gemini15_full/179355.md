redrafted:/ˌriːˈdræftɪd/| adj. |重新起草的；修改后的|  Having been drafted again; revised.  This refers to a document or piece of writing that has been rewritten or amended. |例句：The contract was redrafted to include the new clauses. 合同已被重新起草以包含新的条款。

例句：The redrafted proposal addressed all of the committee's concerns. 修改后的提案解决了委员会的所有担忧。


近义词：rewritten, revised, amended, re-drafted


反义词：original (in its first form), unchanged


词性：形容词
