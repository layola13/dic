preprohibition: /ˌpriːˌproʊɪˈbɪʃən/ | adj. | 禁酒令前的 |  Relating to the period before the Prohibition era in the United States (1920-1933), when the manufacture, sale, and transportation of alcoholic beverages were illegal.  | 例句：Preprohibition saloons were often lively and boisterous places. 禁酒令前的酒馆通常是热闹而喧嚣的地方。


The preprohibition era saw a flourishing of speakeasies and bootlegging. 禁酒令前的时代见证了地下酒吧和走私酒的兴盛。


近义词：anteprohibition (less common)


反义词：postprohibition


词性：形容词
