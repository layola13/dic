emerg:/ˈɜːmɜːrdʒ/| v.|出现，浮现；(问题、疾病等)发生|to become apparent or prominent; to come forth into view; to arise or develop (as a problem or disease)|例句：The sun emerged from behind the clouds. 太阳从云后出现。|例句：A new problem has emerged in the negotiations. 谈判中出现了一个新的问题。|近义词：appear, arise, develop|反义词：disappear, vanish, subside


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun emerges from behind the clouds every morning. = 太阳每天早上都从云后出现。
A new problem emerges in every project. = 每个项目都会出现新问题。


一般过去时:
The truth emerged after a long investigation. = 长期调查后真相大白。
Several new diseases emerged in the past decade. = 在过去的十年里出现了几种新的疾病。


现在完成时:
Several new problems have emerged since the beginning of the project. = 自项目开始以来，出现了一些新问题。
The full impact of the decision has yet to emerge. = 该决定的全部影响尚未显现。


过去完成时:
It had emerged that he was a liar before the trial began. = 在审判开始之前，他已经露出了谎言的马脚。
After a long investigation, it had emerged that the company was guilty. =经过长时间的调查，该公司有罪的事实已经浮出水面。


将来时:
New challenges will emerge as the technology advances. = 随着技术的进步，新的挑战将会出现。
Scientists predict that new viruses will emerge in the future. = 科学家预测未来会出现新的病毒。


虚拟语气:
If the truth emerged, everyone would be shocked. = 如果真相大白，每个人都会感到震惊。
If a new crisis emerged, our plan would be in jeopardy. = 如果出现新的危机，我们的计划将处于危险之中。


被动语态:
The problem was later emerged as a serious mistake. = 后来这个问题被发现是一个严重的错误。
The details of the scandal are gradually emerging.  = 这场丑闻的细节正在逐渐浮出水面。


疑问句:
When will the truth emerge? = 真相何时会大白？
What new challenges will emerge in the next five years? = 未来五年将会出现什么新的挑战？


否定句:
The truth didn't emerge until years later. = 真相直到数年后才大白。
No new problems emerged during the testing phase. = 在测试阶段没有出现新的问题。


PHRASE->
emerge from = 从……出现
emerge victorious = 获胜
emerge unscathed = 安然无恙
emerge triumphant = 凯旋
emerge into view = 映入眼帘
