exchangeable: /ɪkˈstʃeɪndʒəbəl/ | adj. | 可交换的，可兑换的 |  capable of being exchanged or interchanged.  Something that is exchangeable can be traded or substituted for something else of equal value or usefulness. |

例句：

These coupons are exchangeable for goods in the store.  这些优惠券可以在商店兑换商品。

The two toys are exchangeable; they are of equal value. 这两个玩具是可以互换的；它们价值相等。


近义词：interchangeable, substitutable, convertible

反义词：irreplaceable, unique, non-transferable

词性：形容词
