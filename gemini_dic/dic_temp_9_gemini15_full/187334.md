rocking: /ˈrɑːkɪŋ/ /ˈrɒkɪŋ/ | adj. v. n. |摇摆的；极好的；令人兴奋的；摇滚乐；摇晃；摇动 |

英文解析:
As an adjective, "rocking" describes something that is moving back and forth rhythmically, or something that is exceptionally good or exciting. As a verb, it means to move back and forth rhythmically or to shake or sway.  As a noun, it can refer to a style of popular music, or the act of rocking something.

例句:
The rocking chair gently swayed back and forth. = 摇椅轻轻地来回摇晃。

She gave a rocking performance. = 她表演得非常精彩。


近义词:
(adj.) swaying, swinging, excellent, superb, amazing
(v.) swaying, swinging, shaking, jiggling
(n.)  rock music, swaying


反义词:
(adj.) still, stationary, terrible, awful
(v.)  stopping, halting


词性:
adjective, verb, noun
