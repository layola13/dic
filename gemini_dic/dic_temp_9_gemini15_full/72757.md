foggily:/ˈfɒɡɪli/| adj.| 模糊地；朦胧地；迷迷糊糊地 | In a foggy or unclear manner; indistinctly.  The distant mountains were visible only foggily.  | 近义词： hazily, dimly, unclearly | 反义词：clearly, distinctly

英文解析:  The adverb "foggily" describes something done or perceived in a way that is unclear, hazy, or indistinct, like looking through a fog. It suggests a lack of clarity or precision.

例句：

He spoke foggily, as if he were still half asleep. 他说话含糊不清，好像还在半睡半醒之间。

The future looked foggily uncertain to her.  对她来说，未来显得一片模糊不定。
