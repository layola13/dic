countering:/ˈkaʊntərɪŋ/| v.|反驳；对抗；抵制|The act of opposing or resisting something.  It can refer to a physical act of fighting back, a verbal argument, or a strategic move to counteract something.|例句：He countered her argument with a strong rebuttal. 他以强有力的反驳来反驳她的论点。|例句：The company is countering the negative publicity with a new advertising campaign. 公司正在通过新的广告宣传活动来对抗负面宣传。|近义词：opposing, resisting, combating, refuting|反义词：supporting, agreeing, yielding, succumbing|动词
