evanesce:/ˌɛvəˈnɛs/|/ɪˈveɪnəs/| |v.| |逐渐消失；逐渐消散；逐渐衰退|To disappear gradually; to fade away; to vanish slowly.  The evidence gradually evanesced. The memory of the event will eventually evanesce. |近义词：fade, vanish, disappear, dissolve, dissipate|反义词：appear, materialize, emerge


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mist evanesces slowly in the morning sun. = 薄雾在清晨的阳光下慢慢消散。
The scent of flowers evanesces as the day grows old. = 花香随着时间的推移逐渐消散。


一般过去时:
The pain evanesced after he took the medicine. = 他服药后疼痛逐渐消失了。
Her anger evanesced as she calmed down. = 她平静下来后怒气消散了。


现在完成时:
The excitement has evanesced since the game ended. = 比赛结束后，兴奋之情已经消退了。
The feeling of sadness has evanesced over time. = 悲伤的感觉随着时间的推移逐渐消失了。


过去完成时:
The fear had evanesced before he reached the stage. = 他到达舞台前，恐惧感已经消失了。
The initial shock had evanesced, leaving only weariness. = 最初的震惊已经消退，只留下疲惫。


将来时:
The snow will evanesce as the temperature rises. = 随着温度上升，积雪将会消融。
The effects of the medicine will evanesce in a few hours. = 药效将在几小时后消失。


虚拟语气:
If the problem evanesced, everything would be fine. = 如果问题消失了，一切都会好起来的。
I wish my worries would evanesce. = 我希望我的担忧能够消失。


被动语态:
The evidence was gradually evanesced by the passage of time. = 证据随着时间的推移逐渐消失。
The memory will be evanesced by the passing years. = 这段回忆将会被流逝的岁月所淡忘。


疑问句:
Will the pain evanesce soon? = 疼痛会很快消失吗？
Does the evidence ever truly evanesce? = 证据真的会完全消失吗？


否定句:
The smell didn't evanesce easily. = 这气味不容易散去。
The problem didn't evanesce; it got worse. = 问题并没有消失；它变得更糟了。
