chop:/tʃɒp/| /tʃɑːp/| vt. n.|砍，剁，切；排骨；一块|To cut something into pieces with a sharp tool, especially an axe or knife.  A piece of meat cut from a larger piece, especially a rib.|例句：He chopped the wood for the fire. 他砍柴生火。

例句：We had lamb chops for dinner. 晚饭我们吃了羊排。


近义词：hack, cut, mince, slice| 反义词：join, unite


词性:动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chops vegetables every day. 他每天都切菜。
She chops the meat into small pieces. 她把肉切成小块。


一般过去时:
The butcher chopped the meat into steaks.  屠夫把肉切成牛排。
I chopped down the old tree in the garden. 我砍倒了花园里那棵老树。


现在完成时:
He has chopped all the wood for the winter. 他已经砍好了过冬的柴火。
She has chopped the onions very finely. 她把洋葱切得很细。


过去完成时:
They had chopped the vegetables before I arrived. 我到的时候他们已经把菜切好了。
He had chopped down the tree before the storm hit.  暴风雨来临之前他已经把树砍倒了。


将来时:
I will chop the vegetables for the stir-fry. 我会把蔬菜切碎做炒菜。
She will chop the herbs before adding them to the soup. 她会在把香草加到汤里之前先切碎它们。


虚拟语气:
If I had a sharper knife, I would chop the meat more easily. 如果我有把更锋利的刀，我会更容易把肉切好。
I wish I had chopped the vegetables earlier. 我真希望我早点把蔬菜切好。


被动语态:
The wood was chopped by the lumberjack. 木材被伐木工砍倒了。
The vegetables are being chopped for the salad. 蔬菜正在被切碎做沙拉。


疑问句:
Have you chopped the carrots yet? 你切胡萝卜了吗？
Will he chop the onions for the soup? 他会为汤切洋葱吗？


否定句:
I didn't chop the wood because it was raining.  因为下雨，我没有砍柴。
She doesn't chop her own vegetables. 她不自己切菜。


PHRASE->
chop down (砍倒):  Chop down that dead tree. 砍掉那棵枯树。
chop up (切碎): Chop up the onions and garlic. 把洋葱和大蒜切碎。
chop off (砍掉): He chopped off the branch with an axe. 他用斧头砍断了树枝。
chop and change (反复改变):  Stop chopping and changing your mind! 别再反复改变主意了！
a chop shop (非法车辆修理厂): The police raided a chop shop last night. 警方昨晚突袭了一个非法车辆修理厂。
