peaceably:/ˈpiːsəbli/ | adj. | 和平地；平静地；安详地 | In a peaceful manner; calmly; quietly.  The protestors demonstrated peaceably. | The meeting proceeded peaceably. | 近义词：calmly, quietly, serenely, tranquilly | 反义词：violently, aggressively, belligerently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They peaceably assemble every Sunday. = 他们每星期天都和平地集会。
He peaceably coexists with his neighbors. = 他与邻居和平相处。


一般过去时:
The crowd dispersed peaceably after the rally. = 集会后人群平静地散去了。
The two countries peaceably resolved their dispute. = 两国和平地解决了他们的争端。


现在完成时:
They have peaceably lived together for many years. = 他们和平地生活在一起很多年了。
The community has peaceably resisted the development project. = 社区一直和平地抵制这个开发项目。


过去完成时:
The villagers had peaceably occupied the land for generations. = 村民世世代代和平地居住在这片土地上。
The two families had peaceably settled their differences before the trial. = 两家人在审判前已经和平地解决了他们的分歧。


将来时:
We hope they will peaceably settle their differences. = 我们希望他们能够和平地解决分歧。
The government expects the protesters to peaceably demonstrate. = 政府期望抗议者和平示威。


虚拟语气:
If only they would peaceably resolve their conflict! = 但愿他们能和平解决冲突！
It would be best if they could peaceably negotiate a solution. = 如果他们能和平协商出一个解决方案，那就最好了。


被动语态:
The situation was peaceably handled by the police. = 警方和平地处理了这种情况。
The land dispute was peaceably resolved through mediation. = 土地纠纷通过调解和平解决。


疑问句:
Did they resolve the issue peaceably? = 他们是否和平地解决了这个问题？
Was the protest conducted peaceably? = 抗议活动是否和平进行？


否定句:
The demonstration was not peaceably conducted; violence broke out. = 示威活动并非和平进行；爆发了暴力事件。
They did not peaceably accept the decision; they protested loudly. = 他们并不平静地接受这个决定；他们大声抗议。


PHRASE->
peaceably assemble = 和平集会
peaceably coexist = 和平共处
peaceably negotiate = 和平谈判
peaceably resolve = 和平解决
peaceably protest = 和平抗议


词源:
peaceably derives from the Old French word *paisible*, meaning "peaceful," which in turn comes from the Latin word *pacificus*, meaning "making peace."  The suffix "-ably" is an adverbial suffix indicating "in a ... manner."
