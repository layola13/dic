copycats: /ˈkɒpikæts/ | n. |模仿者，抄袭者 |A person who imitates or copies someone else's work or ideas, often without acknowledgement.  |例句：The band was accused of being copycats, blatantly ripping off another group's style.乐队被指控是模仿者，公然抄袭另一支乐队的风格。

例句：He's nothing but a copycat, always following trends instead of creating his own style. 他只不过是个模仿者，总是追随潮流而不是创造自己的风格。

近义词：imitator, plagiarist, imitative,  follower

反义词：innovator, originator, pioneer


USAGE->

The copycat follows the trend. = 模仿者追随潮流。
She copies her classmates' homework. = 她抄袭同学的作业。

He copied the design from a famous artist. = 他抄袭了一位著名艺术家的设计。

将来时:
