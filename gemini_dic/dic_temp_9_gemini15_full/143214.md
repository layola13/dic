outcry:/ˈaʊtkraɪ/| n.|强烈抗议，大声疾呼|A strong expression of protest or disapproval.|The government's decision sparked a public outcry.  政府的决定引发了公众的强烈抗议。|There was a huge outcry over the proposed cuts to public services.  针对削减公共服务的提议，出现了巨大的抗议声浪。|protest, clamor, objection|acceptance, approval|名词
