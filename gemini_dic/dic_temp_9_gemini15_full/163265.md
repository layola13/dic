They are often based on biases, stereotypes, or assumptions.  They can hinder objective judgment and critical thinking. |Example:  His preconclusions about the new employee prevented him from giving her a fair chance.  他的对新员工的先入为主的观念让他无法给她一个公平的机会。|近义词: prejudices, biases, assumptions, predispositions |反义词: open-mindedness, objectivity, impartiality
