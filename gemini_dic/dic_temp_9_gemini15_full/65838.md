eviscerate:/ɪˈvɪsərˌeɪt/| vt.|掏空内脏；使…失去活力；严厉批评|To remove the internal organs of (a body).  To remove the essential parts of something; to severely criticize or weaken something.|例句：The surgeon eviscerated the animal to study its organs. 外科医生剖开动物的内脏以研究其器官。 The critic eviscerated the new play in his review. 批评家在他的评论中严厉批评了这部新剧。|近义词： disembowel, gut,  destroy,  critique,  demolish|反义词：reinforce, strengthen, support
