hypoplastron: /ˌhaɪpoʊˈplæstrən/ | n. |  reducido plastrón |  The hypoplastron is a bone in the plastron of some turtles and tortoises, specifically the posterior part of the plastron. It is often paired with the epiplastron.  Its size and shape can vary significantly between species.  The term is primarily used in zoology and comparative anatomy. |The fossil shows a well-preserved hypoplastron, indicating the turtle's size and species.|近义词: posterior plastral bone (less precise)|反义词: epiplastron (in the context of plastral bones)|名词
