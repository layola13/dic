poohing:/ˈpuːɪŋ/| v.|轻蔑地表示不屑，嗤之以鼻|To express contempt or scorn by pooh-poohing; to dismiss something as unimportant or insignificant.  |例句：He pooh-poohed my suggestions, saying they were unrealistic. 他对我的建议嗤之以鼻，说它们不切实际。

例句：She pooh-poohed the idea of going to the party, claiming it would be boring. 她对参加派对的想法不屑一顾，声称那会很无聊。


近义词：scoffing, sneering, dismissing, disparaging, belittling

反义词：approving, praising, admiring, respecting


词源:  The word "pooh-pooh" (from which "poohing" is derived) is an onomatopoeic word, imitating the sound of expressing disdain or contempt. Its usage dates back to the late 19th century.  The  "pooh" element itself reflects a sound expressing disapproval or dismissal.
