ladies'/ˈleɪdiz/| n.|女士们|The plural form of "lady," referring to multiple women, often used as a polite form of address.  It can also be used to refer to women in general or a group of women of a particular type.|例句：Ladies and gentlemen, welcome to the show! 女士们先生们，欢迎来到节目！
例句：The ladies' restroom is down the hall. 女士洗手间在走廊尽头。|近义词：women, females|反义词：gentlemen, men
