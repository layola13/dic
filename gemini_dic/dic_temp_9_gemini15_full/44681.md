cruciformity:/ˈkruːsɪfɔːrməti/| n. |十字形；十字形结构|The state or quality of being cruciform;  resemblance to a cross in form or arrangement.  Cruciformity is often used to describe the shape of structures or objects.|The cruciformity of the church's design is evident in its floor plan.|近义词：cross-shape,  cross-like structure|反义词：circularity, linearity
