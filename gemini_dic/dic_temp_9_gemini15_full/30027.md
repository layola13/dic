cayo:/ˈkaɪoʊ/ | n. |珊瑚礁，小岛 | A cay is a small, low-lying island, typically composed of coral or sand, formed on a coral reef.  They are often found in tropical and subtropical regions. | Example:  The turquoise waters lapped gently against the white sands of the cay.  这片绿松石般的水轻轻地拍打着珊瑚礁小岛的白沙。 |近义词：islet, key, reef |反义词：continent, mainland
