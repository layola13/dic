charco:/ˈtʃɑːrkoʊ/| n.|炭,木炭|Charcoal is a lightweight black carbon material obtained by strongly heating wood or other organic matter in the absence of oxygen.  It is porous and has a high surface area, making it useful in various applications.|例句：The artist sketched the portrait using charcoal.  艺术家用木炭素描了这幅肖像。


例句：We used charcoal to grill the steaks. 我们用木炭烤牛排。

近义词：coal, coke

反义词：None directly opposite, but could consider "brightly colored pigments" as a contrast.
