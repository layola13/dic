imaging:/ˈɪmɪdʒɪŋ/| n. v.|成像；图像处理；想象|The creation of images, especially by means of a computer or other electronic device.  It also refers to the process of forming a mental image. |例句：Medical imaging techniques, such as MRI and CT scans, provide detailed views of internal organs.医学成像技术，如核磁共振和CT扫描，提供了内部器官的详细视图。
