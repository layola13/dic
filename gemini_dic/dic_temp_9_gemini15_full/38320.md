conceded:/kənˈsiːdəd/| v.|承认，让步，同意|To admit that something is true or to allow something to happen.|The government conceded that the new policy had failed. 政府承认新政策失败了。|承认, 让步, 同意|deny, refuse, oppose|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He concedes defeat. = 他承认失败。
She concedes the point. = 她承认这一点。

一般过去时:
They conceded the game in the last minute. = 他们在最后一分钟输掉了比赛。
The company conceded to the workers' demands. = 公司同意了工人们的要求。

现在完成时:
He has conceded that he made a mistake. = 他已经承认自己犯了一个错误。
She has conceded many points in the debate. = 在辩论中她已经让步很多了。

过去完成时:
They had conceded defeat before the final whistle. = 在终场哨响之前他们就已经承认失败了。
She had conceded the argument before he even finished speaking. = 他还没说完话她就承认自己输了这场争论。

将来时:
He will concede the election if the results are against him. = 如果结果对他不利，他会承认选举失败。
They will concede to the pressure and change their plans. = 他们会屈服于压力并改变他们的计划。

虚拟语气:
If he conceded the error, the situation might improve. = 如果他承认错误，情况可能会好转。
I wish he would concede that he was wrong. = 我希望他会承认自己错了。

被动语态:
The point was conceded by everyone. = 每个人都承认了这一点。
The election was conceded by the losing candidate. = 落选的候选人承认了选举结果。

疑问句:
Will he concede the match? = 他会认输吗？
Did she concede to his request? = 她同意他的请求了吗？

否定句:
He didn't concede his mistake. = 他没有承认自己的错误。
She didn't concede any ground in the negotiations. = 在谈判中她寸步不让。
