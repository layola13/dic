outcried:/ˈaʊtkraɪd/| vt.|大声喊叫；大声哭喊；公开谴责|to cry out loudly; to protest strongly and publicly against something.|例句：The crowd outcried their disapproval of the decision.  人群大声喊叫表示他们对这个决定的不满。|例句：The opposition party outcried the government's new policies. 反对党公开谴责政府的新政策。|近义词：shout, yell, protest, denounce, condemn|反义词：endorse, approve, support|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They outcry the injustice every day. = 他们每天都大声疾呼这种不公正。
The protesters outcry against the new law. = 抗议者大声抗议这项新法律。

一般过去时:
She outcried her anger at the unfair treatment. = 她大声喊出她对不公平待遇的愤怒。
The audience outcried their excitement during the concert. = 音乐会期间，观众们大声喊叫表达他们的兴奋。

现在完成时:
They have outcried against the corruption for years. = 他们多年来一直在大声疾呼反对腐败。
The activists have outcried for social justice. = 活动家们一直在为社会正义大声疾呼。

过去完成时:
He had outcried his concerns before the meeting started. = 会议开始前，他已经大声表达了他的担忧。
The villagers had outcried their grievances to the authorities. = 村民们已经向当局大声表达了他们的不满。

将来时:
They will outcry their support for the candidate. = 他们将大声表达他们对候选人的支持。
The students will outcry against the tuition increase. = 学生们将大声抗议学费上涨。

虚拟语气:
If they knew the truth, they would outcry against it. = 如果他们知道真相，他们就会大声反对。
I wish they would outcry against this injustice. = 我希望他们能大声疾呼反对这种不公正。

被动语态:
The government's decision was outcried by the public. = 政府的决定受到了公众的强烈谴责。
The new policy was outcried as unfair. = 新政策被斥为不公平。

疑问句:
Did anyone outcry during the speech? = 有人在演讲期间大声喊叫吗？
Will they outcry against the proposed changes? = 他们会大声抗议拟议中的改变吗？

否定句:
They did not outcry during the meeting. = 他们在会议期间没有大声喊叫。
He didn't outcry his opinion, fearing reprisal. = 他没有大声说出自己的意见，因为他害怕报复。
