pluralism:/ˈplʊərəlɪzəm/| n. |多元主义；多元论|The acceptance and tolerance of different opinions and beliefs.  Pluralism is a political philosophy that emphasizes the existence of a variety of groups and ideas in society. It suggests that these different groups and ideas should coexist peacefully and that no single group or idea should dominate.|例句：The university embraces pluralism in its student body and faculty. 这所大学在其学生和教师队伍中包容多元化。|例句：Religious pluralism is a key feature of modern society. 宗教多元化是现代社会的一个重要特征。|近义词：diversity, inclusiveness, multiculturalism |反义词：monism, uniformity, homogeneity |名词
