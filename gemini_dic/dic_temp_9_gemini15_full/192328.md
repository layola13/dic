scriber:/ˈskraɪbər/| n. |抄写员，记录者，刻写器|A person who makes copies of documents, especially by hand; a person who writes or records events.  Also, a tool used for scratching or engraving. |例句：The scribe carefully copied the ancient scrolls. 抄写员仔细地抄写了古代卷轴。 The architect used a scribe to mark the precise measurements. 建筑师用刻写器标出精确的尺寸。|近义词：copyist, writer, recorder, engraver|反义词：reader, interpreter
