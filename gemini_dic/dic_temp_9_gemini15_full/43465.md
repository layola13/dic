crashing:/ˈkræʃɪŋ/|/ˈkræʃɪŋ/| adj. v. n.| 撞击的；坠毁的；猛烈的；失败的；|  Making a loud noise as it breaks or collides;  sudden and violent. Also refers to a sudden and complete failure.|例句：The plane went down in a crashing sound.飞机坠毁时发出巨响。  The stock market experienced a crashing decline.股市经历了暴跌。|近义词：collapsing, plummeting, smashing, failing|反义词：stable, successful, gentle, gradual


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The waves are crashing against the shore. = 海浪拍打着海岸。
The server is constantly crashing. = 服务器不断崩溃。


一般过去时:
The car crashed into a tree. = 汽车撞到了一棵树上。
The system crashed during the update. = 系统在更新期间崩溃了。


现在完成时:
The stock market has crashed several times this year. = 股市今年已经暴跌了好几次。
The website has crashed because of the high traffic. = 由于访问量过大，网站崩溃了。


过去完成时:
The economy had crashed before the new government took office. = 新政府上任前，经济已经崩溃了。
The computer had crashed several times before I finally fixed it. = 在我最终修复之前，电脑已经崩溃了好几次。


将来时:
The plane will crash if the pilot doesn't regain control. = 如果飞行员不能重新控制飞机，飞机就会坠毁。
If the system is overloaded, it will crash. = 如果系统超载，它就会崩溃。


虚拟语气:
If the bridge had been stronger, it wouldn't have crashed. = 如果桥更坚固，它就不会倒塌了。
I wish the computer hadn't crashed during my presentation. = 我真希望我的电脑在演示期间没有崩溃。


被动语态:
The car was crashed by a drunk driver. = 汽车被一名醉酒司机撞坏了。
The system was crashed by a virus. = 系统被病毒搞崩溃了。


疑问句:
Did the plane crash? = 飞机坠毁了吗？
Has the computer crashed again? = 电脑又崩溃了吗？


否定句:
The building didn't crash. = 建筑物没有倒塌。
The program didn't crash during the test. = 程序在测试期间没有崩溃。
