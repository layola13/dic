dehydrates:/ˌdiːˈhaɪdreɪts/ | vt. |脱水；使脱水| To remove water from (something), especially food, to preserve it or to make it easier to store or transport.  It can also refer to the process of losing water from the body, often resulting in a deficiency of water. |例句：The sun dehydrates the land.  烈日使土地干涸。  The long hike dehydrated me. 长途跋涉让我脱水了。|近义词：dry out, desiccate|反义词：hydrate, rehydrate

The hot sun dehydrates the plants. = 炎热的太阳使植物脱水。

一般过去时:
