siege:/siːdʒ/| n. |围攻，包围，长期困扰|The prolonged siege of the city led to widespread starvation.  长期的城市围攻导致了大范围的饥饿。|近义词：besiegement, blockade, assault|反义词：relief, liberation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army besieges the castle. = 军队围攻城堡。
She is under siege from the media. = 她正承受着媒体的围攻。


一般过去时:
The city was besieged for months. = 这座城市被围困了几个月。
They besieged the enemy's position. = 他们围攻了敌人的阵地。


现在完成时:
The town has been under siege since last week. = 这个城镇从上周起就一直处于围攻状态。
They have besieged the fort for three days. = 他们已经围攻要塞三天了。


过去完成时:
The city had been besieged for years before it fell. = 这座城市在陷落之前已经被围困多年了。
They had besieged the castle before reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达之前，他们已经围攻了城堡。


将来时:
The rebels will siege the capital city. = 叛军将围攻首都。
The army will soon besiege the enemy stronghold. = 军队很快就会围攻敌人的据点。


虚拟语气:
If they had besieged the city earlier, they might have won. = 如果他们早点围攻这座城市，他们也许就赢了。
I wish they hadn't besieged the town. = 我希望他们没有围攻这座城镇。


被动语态:
The castle was besieged by the enemy. = 城堡被敌人包围了。
The city is being besieged by the rebels. = 城市正被叛军围攻。


疑问句:
Was the city besieged? = 这座城市被围攻了吗？
Is the fortress under siege? = 堡垒正在被围攻吗？


否定句:
The army did not siege the city. = 军队没有围攻这座城市。
The rebels haven't besieged the town yet. = 叛军还没有围攻这座城镇。


PHRASE->
lay siege to = 围攻
lift the siege = 解围
under siege = 处于围攻状态
a siege of illness = 一场疾病的困扰
the siege mentality = 围城心态


词源:
From Old French *siège, from Vulgar Latin *sediare ("to sit"), from Latin sedēre ("to sit").  The meaning evolved from "a sitting" (as in a formal assembly) to "a sitting down" (as in a prolonged encampment) and finally to "a siege".
