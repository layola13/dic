dragons:/ˈdræɡənz/ | n. |龙，巨大的爬行动物|Mythical creatures, often depicted as large, winged, fire-breathing reptiles. They appear in many cultures' folklore and legends. |Example:  Many children are fascinated by stories about dragons. 许多孩子对关于龙的故事着迷。|近义词：serpents, monsters, wyverns|反义词：none
