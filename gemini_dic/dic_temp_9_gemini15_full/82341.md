grow:/ɡroʊ/ /ɡrəʊ/ | v. |生长；成长；种植；变得；发展 | To increase in size or amount; to cultivate plants; to develop or become. |例句：The plant grew quickly in the sunlight.  这植物在阳光下长得很快。

例句：He grew taller over the summer. 他夏天长高了。


近义词：increase, develop, cultivate, expand | 反义词：shrink, decrease, decline | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tree grows tall. = 这棵树长得高。
She grows vegetables in her garden. = 她在花园里种菜。

一般过去时:
The plants grew well last year. = 这些植物去年长得很好。
He grew a beard. = 他留起了胡子。

现在完成时:
The child has grown significantly. = 这个孩子长得很大了。
We have grown accustomed to the noise. = 我们已经习惯了噪音。

过去完成时:
The tree had grown to a considerable height. = 这棵树已经长到相当的高度了。
She had grown tired of waiting. = 她等得不耐烦了。

将来时:
The company will grow its market share. = 公司将扩大其市场份额。
The population will grow rapidly. = 人口将迅速增长。

虚拟语气:
If I had grown taller, I could have played basketball. = 如果我长得更高，我就能打篮球了。
I wish I had grown up in a different era. = 我希望我在不同的时代长大。

被动语态:
The plants are grown in greenhouses. = 这些植物是在温室里种植的。
The business is slowly being grown. = 这项业务正在慢慢发展壮大。

疑问句:
Does the plant grow well? = 这植物长得好吗？
How quickly does your business grow? = 你的生意增长速度有多快？

否定句:
The flowers didn't grow well this year. = 今年花长得不好。
He hasn't grown much since last year. = 他自去年以来长得不多。


PHRASE->
grow up: 长大成人
grow out of: 脱离；长大到不再需要
grow into:逐渐适应；发展成为
grow on someone: 越来越喜欢
grow old: 变老
