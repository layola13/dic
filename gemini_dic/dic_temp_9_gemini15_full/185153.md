retiree:/rɪˈtaɪəriː/ | n. |退休人员 | A person who has retired from their job.  |Many retirees enjoy traveling the world. 许多退休人员喜欢环游世界。| pensioner, senior citizen | employee, worker | 名词


USAGE->
