inearth:/ɪnˈɜːrθ/ | v. | 发现；挖出；发掘 | to find or discover something, especially something hidden or buried | Example:  They unearthed a Roman statue in the garden. = 他们在花园里挖出了一尊罗马雕像。  The police unearthed evidence of a conspiracy. = 警方发现了阴谋的证据。 | 近义词：discover, uncover, excavate | 反义词：bury, conceal
