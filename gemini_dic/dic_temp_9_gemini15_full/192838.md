searching:/ˈsɜːrtʃɪŋ/ | v. |搜索，寻找，探究 |  The act of seeking or looking for something.  It can refer to a physical search or a more abstract intellectual search. | 例句：I am searching for my keys. = 我正在找我的钥匙。

He is searching for the truth. = 他正在探求真相。|近义词：seeking, looking for, exploring, investigating |反义词：finding (in the sense of completing the search)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am searching for a new job. = 我正在找一份新工作。
They are searching the database for information. = 他们正在数据库中搜索信息。

一般过去时:
She searched her bag for her phone. = 她翻找她的包找手机。
We searched the entire house but couldn't find it. = 我们搜遍了整座房子，但还是没找到。

现在完成时:
I have been searching for hours but haven't found anything. = 我已经找了好几个小时了，但什么也没找到。
They have searched the area thoroughly. = 他们已经彻底搜查了该区域。

过去完成时:
I had searched everywhere before I realized it was in my pocket. = 我到处都找过了才意识到它在我的口袋里。
They had searched for days before they finally found the missing child. = 他们在寻找失踪的孩子几天后终于找到了。


将来时:
I will be searching online for the best price. = 我将在线搜索最佳价格。
We will search the archives tomorrow. = 我们明天将搜索档案。


虚拟语气:
If I were searching for a new car, I would look at reviews first. = 如果我要买一辆新车，我会先看评论。
I wish I had searched more thoroughly before making my decision. = 我希望在做决定之前我更彻底地搜索过。


被动语态:
The area was searched by the police. = 该地区被警方搜查了。
The database is being searched for relevant information. = 正在搜索数据库以查找相关信息。


疑问句:
Are you searching for something specific? = 你在寻找什么具体的东西吗？
What are they searching for? = 他们在寻找什么？


否定句:
I am not searching for anything in particular. = 我没有在寻找任何特别的东西。
They were not searching in the right place. = 他们没有在正确的地方搜索。
