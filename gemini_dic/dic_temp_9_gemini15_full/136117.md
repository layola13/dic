nonreductional: /ˌnɒnˌrɪˈdʌkʃənəl/ | adj. |非还原的，非简化的|Not involving or characterized by reduction;  preserving complexity or detail.  It often describes processes or systems that do not simplify or reduce complex entities to simpler components, maintaining the integrity of the original system. |例句：The nonreductional approach to understanding consciousness considers the whole system, rather than just its parts.  非还原论的意识理解方法考虑的是整个系统，而不仅仅是它的各个部分。


The nonreductional model of climate change takes into account complex interactions between various factors.  非简化气候变化模型考虑了各种因素之间复杂的相互作用。


近义词：holistic, comprehensive, integrated, multifactorial |反义词：reductionist, simplistic, oversimplified
