multi-institutional:/ˌmʌltiɪnstɪˈtuːʃənəl/ | adj. | 多机构的，跨机构的 |  Relating to or involving more than one institution.  This term typically refers to collaborations, projects, or initiatives that span multiple organizations, such as universities, hospitals, or research centers. | Example: The multi-institutional study involved researchers from five different universities.  这个跨机构的研究涉及来自五所不同大学的研究人员。


|近义词: inter-institutional, collaborative, cross-institutional |反义词: single-institutional, uni-institutional |
