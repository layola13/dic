/ˈlaɪkənɪst/| n. |地衣学家|A lichenologist is a scientist who studies lichens.  Lichenologists are experts in the biology, ecology, and taxonomy of lichens. They often work in fields like botany, mycology, and ecology.|Example: The lichenologist meticulously documented the diverse lichen species found in the rainforest.|近义词: lichenologist (synonym)|反义词: N/A
