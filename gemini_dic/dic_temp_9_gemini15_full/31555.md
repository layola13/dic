chartulary:/ˈtʃɑːrtʃʊləri/| n. |抄本簿；文献汇编|A chartulary is a collection of charters and other documents relating to a particular monastery, institution, or family, often kept in a bound book.  These documents typically concern land ownership, privileges, and other legal matters.|Example: The monastery's chartulary provided invaluable evidence of its history and landholdings.  这个修道院的抄本簿提供了其历史和土地所有权的宝贵证据。|近义词：register, archive, record |反义词：None.
