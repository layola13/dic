damsons:/ˈdæmzənz/| n.|梅子(一种小而酸的李子)|A damson is a small, dark-purple plum, typically tart in flavor.  They are commonly used in jams, jellies, and pies.|The damson plum harvest is always a highlight of the autumn.|近义词：plum, prune|反义词: None specifically.

词源:
The word "damson" ultimately derives from the Old French word *damasquin*, referring to plums from Damascus.  The fruit itself likely originated in the Middle East and spread throughout Europe.
