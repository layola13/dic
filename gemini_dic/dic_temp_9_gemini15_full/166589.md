presearches:/ˈpriːsɜːrtʃɪz/| n.|预先搜索；前期调研|Preliminary searches or investigations conducted before a more extensive study or project.  It involves gathering initial information to define the scope, identify key areas, and formulate a research plan.|例句：The presearches revealed a significant gap in existing literature on this topic. 预先搜索揭示了现有文献在这个主题上的一个重大差距。

例句：Thorough presearches are crucial for successful grant applications. 彻底的预先搜索对于成功的拨款申请至关重要。
