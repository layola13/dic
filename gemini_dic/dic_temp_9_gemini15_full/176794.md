readjuster: /ˌriːəˈdʒʌstər/ | n. | 调整者；重调者 | A person or thing that readjusts something.  Someone or something that makes adjustments or alterations to restore something to a proper state. | The readjuster quickly fixed the broken machine. 调整者迅速修理好了坏掉的机器。 |近义词：adjuster, repairer, modifier |反义词：disruptor, destroyer


USAGE->

The adjuster checks the damaged car. = 调整员检查受损的汽车。
The machine readjusts the settings automatically. = 机器自动重新调整设置。


一般过去时:
