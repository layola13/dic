carelessnesses:/ˈkerləsnəsiz/| n. |粗心大意；疏忽；马虎|The plural form of carelessness, referring to multiple instances of negligence or lack of attention to detail. It emphasizes a pattern of inattention rather than a single act.|例句：His carelessnesses led to numerous mistakes in the report. 他的粗心大意导致报告中出现许多错误。  His repeated carelessnesses eventually cost him his job. 他反复的粗心大意最终让他丢了工作。|近义词：negligences, oversights, slips, lapses|反义词：attentiveness, carefulness, diligence
