finalize:/ˈfaɪnəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使最终确定；完成；定稿 | To make something final or complete.  |例句：We need to finalize the contract before the end of the week. 我们需要在本周末前敲定合同。

例句：The committee finalized its report after several weeks of deliberation.  委员会经过几周的审议后最终完成了报告。


近义词：complete, conclude, perfect, settle


反义词：initiate, begin, start, postpone


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They finalize the details of the project every Friday. = 他们每周五敲定项目的细节。
He finalizes his work before leaving the office. = 他下班前完成他的工作。


一般过去时:
She finalized the design yesterday. = 她昨天完成了设计。
We finalized the agreement last night. = 我们昨晚敲定了协议。


现在完成时:
They have finalized the budget for the next fiscal year. = 他们已经完成了下一财政年度的预算。
She has finalized all her reports. = 她已经完成了所有报告。


过去完成时:
He had finalized the report before the meeting. = 他在会议前完成了报告。
They had finalized the plans before the construction began. = 施工开始前他们已经完成了计划。


将来时:
We will finalize the contract tomorrow. = 我们明天将敲定合同。
They will finalize the arrangements next week. = 他们下周将完成安排。


虚拟语气:
If we finalized the deal, we would celebrate. = 如果我们完成了交易，我们会庆祝。
I wish I had finalized the application earlier. = 我希望我早点完成申请。


被动语态:
The contract will be finalized next week. = 合同将于下周最终确定。
The plans were finalized before the project started. = 在项目开始之前，计划已经确定了。


疑问句:
Have they finalized the arrangements yet? = 他们已经完成安排了吗？
When will the deal be finalized? = 交易什么时候敲定？


否定句:
They haven't finalized the agreement yet. = 他们还没有敲定协议。
He didn't finalize the report on time. = 他没有按时完成报告。


PHRASE->
