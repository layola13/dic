concordancer:/kənˈkɔːrdənsər/ | n. | 检索词一致性程序；索引程序；语料库检索工具 | A concordancer is a computer program that creates concordances.  It allows users to search a text corpus for specific words and see their occurrences within their surrounding context. This helps researchers analyze word usage, grammar, and style. | Example:  The linguist used a concordancer to study the frequency and collocations of the verb "to be" in Shakespeare's plays.  语言学家使用检索词一致性程序来研究莎士比亚戏剧中动词“to be”的频率和搭配。| 近义词：text analysis tool, corpus tool, keyword-in-context (KWIC) program | 反义词：N/A
