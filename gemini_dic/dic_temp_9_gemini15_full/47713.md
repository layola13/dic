decad:/ˈdɛkæd/| n.|十年；十年期|A period of ten years.  The word is often used to refer to a specific decade, such as the "roaring twenties" or the "swinging sixties".|Example: The 1920s were a decad of significant social and cultural change.  二十世纪二十年代是一个社会和文化变革显著的十年。|近义词：decade, ten years|反义词：无

This decade is marked by rapid technological advancements. = 这个十年以快速的科技进步为标志。
A decade consists of ten years. = 一个十年包含十年。


一般过去时:
The last decade saw significant economic growth. = 上个十年见证了显著的经济增长。
That decade was known for its fashion trends. = 那十年以其时尚潮流而闻名。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

疑问句:
