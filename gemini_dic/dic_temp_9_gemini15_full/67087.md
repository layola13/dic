exploiting:/ɪkˈsplɔɪtɪŋ/| v.|利用；剥削；开发；充分利用 | To make full use of a resource or situation, sometimes in a way that is considered unfair or unethical.  |例句：The company is exploiting its workers by paying them low wages.  这家公司通过支付低工资来剥削其工人。

例句：They are exploiting a new market for their product.  他们正在为他们的产品开发一个新的市场。


近义词：using, utilizing, taking advantage of, abusing,  leveraging

反义词：conserving, protecting, neglecting, ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are exploiting the natural resources of the country. = 他们正在利用该国的自然资源。
The company exploits its employees. = 这家公司剥削其员工。


一般过去时:
They exploited the weakness of their opponents. = 他们利用了对手的弱点。
The miners exploited the coal seam. = 矿工们开采了煤层。


现在完成时:
They have exploited the situation to their advantage. = 他们已经利用了这种情况来达到自己的优势。
The company has exploited its workers for years. = 这家公司多年来一直在剥削其工人。


过去完成时:
They had exploited the loophole in the law. = 他们已经利用了法律中的漏洞。
The farmers had exploited the fertile land for generations. = 几代农民都利用了这块肥沃的土地。


将来时:
They will exploit the new technology to improve efficiency. = 他们将利用新技术来提高效率。
The company will exploit the market opportunity. = 公司将抓住市场机遇。


虚拟语气:
If they exploited the opportunity, they would succeed. = 如果他们抓住机会，他们就会成功。
I wish they hadn't exploited the workers. = 我希望他们没有剥削工人。


被动语态:
The resources are being exploited at an unsustainable rate. = 资源正以不可持续的速度被利用。
The workers were exploited by the factory owner. = 工人们被工厂老板剥削了。


疑问句:
Are they exploiting the workers? = 他们正在剥削工人吗？
How are they exploiting the situation? = 他们如何利用这种情况？


否定句:
They are not exploiting the situation. = 他们没有利用这种情况。
The company doesn't exploit its employees. = 这家公司没有剥削其员工。


PHRASE->
exploit a weakness = 利用弱点
exploit a loophole = 利用漏洞
exploit resources = 利用资源
sexually exploit = 性剥削
exploit the situation = 利用形势

词源:
源自古法语 *exploiter，源于拉丁语 *expluere，意为“从…中驱逐出去”。  其含义演变经历了从“驱逐”到“利用”的过程，这反映了利用资源或情况的过程，有时也带有不公平或不道德的意味。
