isostatic:/ˌaɪsoʊˈstætɪk/| adj.|静力学的，均衡的|Relating to or denoting the equilibrium between parts of the earth's crust, whereby areas of lower density are raised to higher elevations than areas of higher density.|The isostatic adjustment of the Earth's crust is a slow process. 地球地壳的均衡调整是一个缓慢的过程。|近义词：balanced, equilibrium|反义词：unbalanced, imbalanced
