interpledges:/ˈɪntərˈplɛdʒɪz/| n. |相互质押；互保；共同抵押|A mutual pledge or agreement, especially one involving the mutual pledging of property as security.  It often involves multiple parties agreeing to jointly guarantee a debt or obligation.|The banks interpledged their assets to secure the loan.  银行相互抵押资产以担保贷款。

The company's interpledges were insufficient to cover the debt. 该公司的相互质押不足以偿还债务。|近义词：mutual guarantee, reciprocal pledge, joint security|反义词：individual guarantee, separate security|名词
