dwarflike:/ˈdwɔːrflaɪk/| adj. |像侏儒的；矮小的；|Resembling a dwarf; short and stout.  The term often carries connotations of being somewhat deformed or misshapen, in addition to simply being small.|例句：The garden gnome had a dwarflike appearance. 花园里的侏儒看起来像个小矮人。

例句：The bonsai tree, though healthy, had a surprisingly dwarflike stature. 这棵盆栽树虽然健康，但身材却出奇地矮小。


近义词：dwarfish, stunted, undersized, pygmy


反义词：giantlike, colossal, gigantic
