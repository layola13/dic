femineity: /ˌfɛməˈnɪnəti/ | n. |女性气质；女人味 | The quality or nature of being feminine; the aggregate of qualities characteristic of women.  It encompasses a range of attributes, including physical characteristics, behaviors, and social roles traditionally associated with women.  The concept of femininity is complex and culturally influenced, varying across time periods and different societies. |  例句：Her dress and demeanor exuded femininity.  她的穿着和举止散发着女性气质。

例句：The novel explores the complexities of femininity in modern society. 这部小说探讨了现代社会女性气质的复杂性。


近义词：womanliness, womanhood, girlishness

反义词：masculinity
