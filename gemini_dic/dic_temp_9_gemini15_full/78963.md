girlfriends:/ˈɡɜːrlfrɛndz/| n. |女朋友们，女性朋友们|The word "girlfriends" refers to a plural noun, indicating more than one girlfriend.  It can refer to a group of female friends or a group of romantic partners.  The context usually clarifies the meaning. |例句：My girlfriends and I went shopping yesterday. 我的女朋友们和我昨天去购物了。


例句：She's close friends with all her girlfriends from college. 她和大学里所有的女朋友都很亲近。


近义词：female friends, female companions


反义词：boyfriends (in the context of romantic relationships)


词性:Noun
