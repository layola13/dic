paragliding:/ˈpærəɡlaɪdɪŋ/| n. |滑翔伞运动|The activity of flying in a paraglider.  Paragliding is a recreational activity that involves flying a paraglider, a type of wing made of fabric, to soar through the air.  It is a popular sport enjoyed by people of all ages and skill levels.|例句：He took up paragliding last year and loves the thrill. 他去年开始玩滑翔伞运动，并且非常喜欢这种刺激。

例句：Many people enjoy paragliding as a way to experience the beauty of nature from a unique perspective.  许多人享受滑翔伞运动，以此从独特的视角体验自然之美。


近义词：hang gliding (though hang gliding uses a different type of wing)


反义词：无


词性:名词
