词源:

* **engagement:**  From Old French *engagement*, from *engager* "to pledge, guarantee," from *en-* "in" + *gage* "pledge, security."  The sense of a promise to marry developed later.

* **ring:** From Old English *hring* "ring, circle," of Germanic origin; related to Dutch *ring* and German *Ring*.
