half-barrels:/ˈhæf ˈbærəlz/| n.|半桶；半桶装的量|A half-barrel is a unit of volume equal to half of a barrel, typically used for liquids like beer or oil.  The exact volume varies depending on the type of barrel.|例句：The brewery shipped the beer in half-barrels. 这家啤酒厂用半桶装啤酒运送。|例句：We need to order at least five half-barrels of oil for the machine. 我们需要至少订购五桶半的油用于机器。|近义词：half a barrel, half cask|反义词：full barrel, whole barrel|名词
