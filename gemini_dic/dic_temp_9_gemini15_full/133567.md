nonflagellated: /ˌnɒnˈflædʒəleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 无鞭毛的 | Lacking flagella; referring to organisms or cells that do not possess flagella, the whip-like appendages used for locomotion.  | Example:  Many bacteria are nonflagellated and rely on other means of movement.  许多细菌是无鞭毛的，依靠其他方式移动。 | 近义词: aflagellate, ciliated (if using cilia for movement) | 反义词: flagellated
