/ˌfoʊnˈɑːlədʒɪsts/ | n. |语音学家|Experts who study the sounds of language, including their production, perception, and acoustic properties. They analyze how sounds are organized and used in different languages, exploring aspects like phonetics, phonology, and the relationship between sounds and meaning.  | Phonologists study the sound systems of languages, examining how sounds are combined to form words and how those combinations affect meaning. For example, they might analyze the different sounds in a language and how they contrast to create different words. |  近义词：linguists (with a focus on phonetics and phonology) | 反义词：无
