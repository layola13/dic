degradation:/ˌdeɡrəˈdeɪʃən/| n. |退化；降级；恶化；降解|The process of being reduced in rank or status; the deterioration of something into a worse state.  It can refer to a decline in quality, value, or moral character, or to the breakdown of a substance into simpler components.|例句：The degradation of the environment is a serious concern. 环境恶化是一个严重的问题。|例句：His degradation from captain to private was a harsh punishment.  他从上尉降为列兵是一种严厉的惩罚。|近义词：deterioration, decline, decay, degeneration |反义词：improvement, enhancement, elevation, upgrade
