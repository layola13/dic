harbourer: /ˈhɑːbərər/ | n. |窝藏者；庇护者 |  A person who harbors or shelters someone or something, often in a secretive or illegal manner.  | Example: The harbourer of the fugitive was eventually caught by the police. 窝藏逃犯的人最终被警方抓获。  |近义词:shelterer, protector, concealer | 反义词: betrayer, informer |

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harbours resentment towards his former boss. 他对他的前任老板怀恨在心。
She harbours a secret ambition to become a writer. 她怀有一个成为作家的秘密愿望。


一般过去时:
They harboured the escaped prisoner in their barn. 他们把逃犯藏在他们的谷仓里。
The old house harboured many secrets. 这座老房子藏着许多秘密。


现在完成时:
He has harboured doubts about the project's feasibility. 他一直对该项目的可行性表示怀疑。
She has harboured a deep-seated fear of failure. 她一直深藏着对失败的恐惧。


过去完成时:
They had harboured suspicions about his true identity for months. 他们几个月来一直对他的真实身份心存怀疑。
She had harboured a secret hope that he would return. 她一直怀着秘密的希望，希望他会回来。


将来时:
They will harbour no ill will towards their rivals. 他们不会对他们的竞争对手怀有任何恶意。
She will harbour the thought for a long time. 她会长时间地思考这件事。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't harbour such negative feelings. 如果我是你，我就不会怀有如此负面的情绪。
I wish I hadn't harboured such a naive belief. 我真希望我没有抱有如此天真的想法。


被动语态:
The refugees were harboured by a local family. 难民们得到当地一个家庭的收留。
Suspicions were harboured by the police. 警方心存怀疑。


疑问句:
Does she harbour any doubts about the plan? 她对这个计划有任何怀疑吗？
Do they harbour any resentment towards me? 他们对我有什么怨恨吗？


否定句:
He does not harbour any ill intentions towards her. 他对她没有任何恶意。
They did not harbour any secrets from each other. 他们彼此之间没有任何秘密。
