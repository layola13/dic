reamalgamating: /ˌriːæˈɡælɡəmeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 再合并；重新合并 | The act of combining or uniting again, typically after a period of separation or division.  It implies a process of re-integration or reconstitution. |例句：The company is reamalgamating its subsidiaries after a period of restructuring.  公司在经历了一段时间的重组后正在重新合并其子公司。

The city is reamalgamating its previously separated boroughs.  这座城市正在重新合并之前被分开的自治市。


近义词：reuniting, recombining, reconsolidating, merging (again), integrating (anew)

反义词：separating, dividing, fragmenting, disintegrating


词性：动词 (现在分词形式)

词源:
The word is formed by combining "re-" (again), "amalgamate" (to combine or unite), and "-ing" (present participle).  "Amalgamate" itself originates from the Latin "amalgama," referring to a mixture or alloy, particularly of mercury.
