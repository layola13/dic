re-urge:/ˌriːˈɜːrdʒ/| vt.|再三催促；再次强烈要求|To urge again or repeatedly; to insist on again.|例句：I had to re-urge him to finish the report by the deadline. 我不得不再次催促他按时完成报告。
例句：The committee re-urged the government to reconsider its decision. 委员会再次强烈要求政府重新考虑其决定。|近义词：reinforce, reiterate, press, insist|反义词：neglect, ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-urges his employees to meet the quota every month. 他每个月都再三催促他的员工完成配额。
They re-urge the importance of safety regulations. 他们再次强调安全规章的重要性。


一般过去时:
She re-urged her son to study harder for the exam. 她再次催促儿子为考试努力学习。
The manager re-urged the team to complete the project on time. 经理再次敦促团队按时完成项目。

虚拟语气:
If I re-urged him, I think he might listen. 如果我再三催促他，我想他会听的。

否定句:
