mis:/mɪs/| pref.|错误，坏，错，不好|表示错误、坏、误解或不正确的含义的前缀。它通常加在词根前面，构成新的单词，表示与词根相反或不好的含义。|例句：misunderstand (误解), misspell (拼错), misinform (误导), misjudge (误判), miscalculate (计算错误)|近义词：wrong, bad, incorrect, faulty|反义词：correct, right, accurate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I misunderstand your instructions. = 我误解了你的指示。
He misspells many words. = 他拼错很多单词。


一般过去时:
She misjudged the situation. = 她误判了形势。
They misinformed the public. = 他们误导了公众。


现在完成时:
I have miscalculated the budget. = 我预算计算错误了。
We have misunderstood the agreement. = 我们误解了协议。


过去完成时:
He had misrepresented the facts. = 他歪曲了事实。
They had mismanaged the company. = 他们管理不善。


将来时:
I will misinterpret his words. = 我将会误解他的话。
She will misplace her keys again. = 她又会把钥匙放错地方。


虚拟语气:
If I had not misunderstood you, I would have acted differently. = 如果我没有误解你，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I hadn't misjudged him. = 我真希望我没有误判他。


被动语态:
The information was misinterpreted. = 信息被误解了。
The instructions were misconstrued. = 指示被曲解了。


疑问句:
Did I misspeak? = 我说错话了吗？
Did you mishear me? = 你听错了吗？


否定句:
I didn't misinterpret the signal. = 我没有误解信号。
He didn't misplace the document. = 他没有把文件放错地方。


PHRASE->
misunderstandings  = 误解
misinformation = 虚假信息
mismanagement = 管理不善
misbehavior = 不良行为
misfortune = 不幸
