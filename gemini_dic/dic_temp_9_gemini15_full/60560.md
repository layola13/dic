elasmobranchiate:/ɪˌlæzməˈbræŋkieɪt/ | adj. | 板鳃类的；软骨鱼类的 | Relating to or characteristic of elasmobranchs, a group of cartilaginous fishes including sharks, rays, and skates.  These fishes have five to seven gill slits that open separately on the sides of the head, rather than being covered by an operculum (gill cover) as in bony fishes. | Example:  The elasmobranch fauna of the region is diverse, including several species of sharks and rays.  该地区的板鳃类动物区系多样，包括几种鲨鱼和鳐鱼。| 近义词：chondrichthyan (more inclusive term) | 反义词：teleost (bony fish)
