miniaturize:/ˌmɪniˈætʃəraɪz/ | vt. | 使小型化；缩小 | To make something smaller, especially in size or scale.  |例句：The company is working to miniaturize its new product for better portability. 公司正在努力将其新产品小型化，以提高便携性。
例句：They miniaturized the circuit boards to fit into smaller devices. 他们将电路板小型化以适应更小的设备。 | 近义词：reduce, shrink, downsize | 反义词：enlarge, magnify, amplify | 词性:动词
