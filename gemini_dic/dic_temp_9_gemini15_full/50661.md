derivatively:/dɪˈrɪvətɪvli/ | adv. | 派生地；衍生地 | Derived from or based on something else; indirectly.  It indicates something is not original but comes from or is based on something else. | 例句：The meaning of the word is used derivatively.  这个词的含义是派生使用的。

The theory was developed derivatively from earlier research. 这个理论是从早期的研究中衍生出来的。

近义词：indirectly, secondarily, consequentially | 反义词：originally, primarily, directly
