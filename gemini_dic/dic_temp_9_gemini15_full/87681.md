hexahedron:/ˈhɛksəˌhiːdrən/| n. |六面体|A solid geometric figure with six faces.  It is a polyhedron with six faces.  Examples include a cube and a rectangular prism. |例句：A cube is a regular hexahedron.  一个立方体是一个正六面体。

例句：Many crystals form as hexahedrons. 许多晶体形成六面体。


近义词：six-sided solid, polyhedron (more general term)
反义词：None (antonyms relate to specific shapes, not the number of faces)
词性：noun
