prescreenings:/ˈpriːˌskriːnɪŋz/| n.|预选，预审，初步筛选 |The process of screening something or someone before a full screening.  It often involves a preliminary assessment to determine suitability or eligibility.|例句：The prescreenings for the film festival were rigorous, ensuring only the highest-quality films were selected.  电影节的预选非常严格，确保只有最高质量的电影才能入选。


The prescreenings for the job application involved a written test and an interview.  工作的预选包括笔试和面试。|近义词：preliminary screenings, initial selections, preliminary evaluations|反义词：final screenings, main selections
