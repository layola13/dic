midlives:/ˈmɪdlaɪvz/| n.|中年时期，中年生活|The period of life between youth and old age;  the years of a person's life that are neither young nor old.  It often refers to the challenges and transitions associated with this stage of life.|例句：Many people experience significant life changes during their midlives.  许多人在中年时期经历了重大的生活变化。|例句：She wrote a book about navigating the emotional complexities of midlives. 她写了一本书，讲述了如何应对中年时期复杂的情感。|近义词：middle age, middle years, adulthood (in part)|反义词：youth, adolescence, old age
