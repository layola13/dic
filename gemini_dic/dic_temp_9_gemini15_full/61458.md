embrace:/ɪmˈbreɪs/| vt. n.|拥抱；欣然接受；包含；围绕|To hold (someone) closely in one's arms, especially as a sign of affection. To accept (something wholeheartedly). To include or contain (something).|例句：She embraced her son tightly. 她紧紧地拥抱了她的儿子。
She embraced the opportunity to travel. 她欣然接受了旅行的机会。

近义词：hug, clasp, accept, welcome, encompass|反义词：reject, refuse, shun, spurn|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He embraces his daughter every night. 他每晚都拥抱他的女儿。
They embrace new challenges with enthusiasm. 他们热情地迎接新的挑战。

一般过去时:
She embraced her friend after the long separation. 分离很久后，她拥抱了她的朋友。
The company embraced a new marketing strategy. 公司采用了新的营销策略。

现在完成时:
I have embraced a healthier lifestyle recently. 我最近过上了更健康的生活方式。
She has embraced the challenges of motherhood. 她欣然接受了为人母的挑战。

过去完成时:
He had embraced Buddhism before he moved to Japan. 他在搬到日本之前就已经皈依佛教了。
They had embraced the idea long before the meeting. 他们在会议之前很久就接受了这个想法。

将来时:
She will embrace the future with open arms. 她将张开双臂拥抱未来。
The company will embrace technological advancements. 公司将拥抱技术的进步。

虚拟语气:
If I embraced the opportunity, I would have achieved more. 如果我抓住机会，我会取得更大的成就。
I wish I had embraced the chance to study abroad. 我真希望我当初抓住了出国留学的机会。

被动语态:
The new policy was embraced by the majority of people.  这项新政策得到了大多数人的支持。
The changes were gradually embraced by the community.  这些变化逐渐被社区所接受。

疑问句:
Does he embrace his responsibilities? 他承担责任吗？（这里 embrace 指的是承担责任）
Will they embrace the new technology?  他们会采用这项新技术吗？


否定句:
He didn't embrace the criticism. 他没有接受批评。
She refused to embrace the changes. 她拒绝接受改变。


PHRASE->
embrace change 拥抱改变
embrace opportunity 抓住机会
embrace challenges 迎接挑战
embrace life 热爱生活
embrace diversity 拥抱多元化


词源:
From Old French embracer, from en- ("in, into") + bracer ("to clasp, embrace").  Ultimately derived from Latin bracchium ("arm").
