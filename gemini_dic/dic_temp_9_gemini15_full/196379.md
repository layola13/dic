sermonish: /ˈsɜːrmənɪʃ/ | adj. | 说教的；教训人的；冗长的 |

英文解析:  Characterized by or resembling a sermon; preachy or didactic in tone.  Suggesting a long, tedious, and moralizing lecture.

例句:

His sermonish tone irritated the audience. = 他说教的语气激怒了听众。

Her sermonish remarks about my lifestyle were unwelcome. = 她对我生活方式的说教般的评论不受欢迎。


近义词: preachy, didactic, moralistic, lecturing, dogmatic

反义词:  unassuming, modest, humble, tolerant
