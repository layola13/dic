emerged:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒd/ | v. |出现；浮现；兴起| To become apparent, known, or prominent.  It can also refer to something coming out of something else, or someone rising to a position of prominence.|

例句：

The sun emerged from behind the clouds.  太阳从云层后面出来了。

A new political party emerged after the election.  选举后出现了一个新的政党。


近义词：appeared, arose, surfaced, developed, evolved

反义词：disappeared, vanished, subsided, receded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun emerges from behind the clouds every morning. 太阳每天早上从云层后面升起。
A new trend emerges in the fashion industry each season. 时尚行业每个季度都会出现新的潮流。

一般过去时:
The truth emerged during the investigation. 真相在调查中浮出水面。
He emerged as a leader in the community. 他成为社区的领导者。

现在完成时:
Several new problems have emerged since the project began. 自从项目开始以来，出现了一些新的问题。
The company has emerged as a major player in the market. 这家公司已成为市场上的主要参与者。

过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, the winner had already emerged. 当我到达时，获胜者已经产生了。
It had emerged that the documents were forged. 文件是伪造的这一事实已经浮出水面。

将来时:
New challenges will emerge as we move forward.  随着我们的前进，新的挑战将会出现。
The results of the experiment will emerge next week. 实验结果将于下周公布。

虚拟语气:
If a better solution emerged, we would adopt it. 如果出现更好的解决方案，我们会采纳它。
I wish a new opportunity would emerge for me. 我希望会有新的机会出现。

被动语态:
The truth was eventually emerged. 真相最终大白于天下。(注意：emerge本身很少用被动语态)
The problem was emerged during the meeting. 问题在会议期间被提出。(更常见的表达是The problem was raised/identified during the meeting)

疑问句:
When will the results emerge? 结果什么时候出来？
Has a solution emerged yet?  目前已经找到解决方案了吗？

否定句:
No new evidence has emerged in the case.  此案中没有出现新的证据。
The details of the incident have not yet emerged.  事件的细节尚未浮出水面。


PHRASE->

emerge from: 从...出现
emerge victorious: 胜利出现；取得胜利
emerge unscathed: 安然无恙地出现
emerge as: 成为...；显现为...
emerge into: 进入；来到...


词源:

Emerged comes from the Old French word *emerger*, which itself derives from the Latin *emergere*, meaning "to rise out of, to come forth."  The Latin verb is formed from the prefix *e-* (out) and *mergere* (to plunge, to dip).
