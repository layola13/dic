pseudogenuses: /ˌsuːdoʊˈdʒɛnəsɪz/ | n. | 假属；伪属 |  The plural of pseudogenus.  It refers to a group of organisms that appear to be a genus based on superficial similarities, but which are actually not closely related, often due to convergent evolution.  These are taxonomic groupings that are considered artificial or invalid upon further study.  |  Further research revealed that the previously identified pseudogenuses were actually composed of distantly related species. = 进一步的研究表明，先前鉴定的假属实际上是由远缘物种组成的。 | 近义词：paraphyletic group, artificial taxon | 反义词：monophyletic group, natural taxon
