exported:/ɪkˈspɔːtɪd/ | adj. | 出口的；已出口的 |  Something that has been exported has been sent to another country for sale.  It describes goods or services that are sent from one country to another. | Example: Exported goods accounted for a significant portion of the country's economy.  出口商品占该国经济的很大一部分。  The company's exported products are highly sought after in the international market. 该公司的出口产品在国际市场上非常抢手。|近义词：shipped, sent abroad, conveyed overseas |反义词：imported


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company exports high-quality products. = 这家公司出口高质量的产品。
We export agricultural goods to many countries. = 我们向许多国家出口农产品。

一般过去时:
They exported a large quantity of rice last year. = 他们去年出口了大量的稻米。
China exported a significant amount of textiles to the US. = 中国向美国出口了大量的纺织品。

现在完成时:
We have exported over 1000 units this year. = 我们今年已经出口了1000多台。
The company has exported its products to over 50 countries. = 这家公司已将其产品出口到50多个国家。

过去完成时:
They had exported all the goods before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经出口了所有货物。
The company had exported millions of dollars worth of goods before the economic crisis hit. = 在经济危机爆发之前，该公司已经出口了价值数百万美元的商品。

将来时:
We will export more goods to Europe next year. = 我们明年将向欧洲出口更多商品。
The government plans to export more high-tech products in the future. = 政府计划将来出口更多高科技产品。

虚拟语气:
If we exported more, we would make more profit. = 如果我们出口更多，我们将获得更多利润。
I wish we had exported more goods last year. = 我希望我们去年出口更多商品。

被动语态:
Many goods are exported from this port. = 许多货物从这个港口出口。
A large quantity of tea was exported to England. = 大量的茶叶被出口到英国。

疑问句:
Are these goods exported to other countries? = 这些货物出口到其他国家吗？
How many products were exported last month? = 上个月出口了多少产品？

否定句:
They did not export any goods last week. = 他们上周没有出口任何货物。
We don't export to countries with trade sanctions. = 我们不向有贸易制裁的国家出口。
