rubrician:/ˈruːbrɪʃən/ | n. |红字注记者；(尤指)礼仪书的编纂者；(书中)用红色书写的部分|A rubrician is someone who writes or is skilled in the use of rubrics, especially in liturgical books.  The term can also refer to a section of text written in red. |Example: The rubrician meticulously checked the liturgical books for accuracy.  红字注记者一丝不苟地检查礼仪书籍的准确性。|近义词：commentator, annotator, liturgist |反义词：None specifically applicable.
