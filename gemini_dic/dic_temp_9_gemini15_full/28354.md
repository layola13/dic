carats:/ˈkærəts/| n.|克拉，开罗(古埃及城市)|A carat is a unit of mass equal to 200 milligrams, used for weighing gemstones.  It is also sometimes used as a measure of the purity of gold (karat).  The word "carat" can also refer to the ancient Egyptian city of Cairo. |例句：The diamond ring has a 1-carat diamond. 这枚钻戒上有一颗1克拉的钻石。

例句：The gold is 18 carats. 这金子是18k的。


近义词：karat (when referring to gold purity)


反义词：无


词性:名词
