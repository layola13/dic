cineria:/sɪˈnɛəriə/| n.|灰烬；骨灰；残骸|The powdery residue left after the incineration of organic matter, often associated with death and remembrance.  It can also refer to the remains of something destroyed or decayed.|例句：The cineria of the deceased were scattered at sea. 死者的骨灰撒在了海里。|例句：The cineria of the burned building were all that remained. 烧毁建筑物的残骸是唯一剩下的东西。|近义词：ashes, remains, debris|反义词:  living matter, intact structure
