skyman:/ˈskaɪmæn/| n. |天空人，天人；（非正式）飞行员|A skyman is an informal term for a pilot, or sometimes a person who seems to be exceptionally skilled or capable in a particular area, suggesting they are almost superhuman. It can also refer to a mythical or fictional being associated with the sky.|例句：He's a real skyman; he can fix any airplane engine. 他真是个高手；他能修理任何飞机引擎。


例句：The children imagined the skyman was a friendly god who lived in the clouds.孩子们想象天空人是一位居住在云层里的友善的神。


近义词：pilot, aviator, airman


反义词：earthbound, terrestrial

词性：名词
