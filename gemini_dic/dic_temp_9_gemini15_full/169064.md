programmatist:/proʊˈɡræmətɪst/| n. |程序员，编程员 |A person who writes computer programs.  This term is less common than "programmer" but emphasizes the act of creating programs. |Example:  He is a skilled programmatist, able to write efficient and elegant code. 他是一位熟练的程序员，能够编写高效且优雅的代码。|近义词：programmer, coder, developer |反义词：None.
