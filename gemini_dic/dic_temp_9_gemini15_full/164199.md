predivert:/ˌpriːdɪˈvɜːrt/| v.|预先转移，预先转向|To divert beforehand; to anticipate and change the course of something.  The term is rare and mostly found in specialized contexts, often implying a proactive measure to prevent something undesirable.|例句：The company prediverted its resources to mitigate the potential impact of the impending storm. 公司预先转移了资源以减轻即将到来的风暴的潜在影响。The pilot prediverted the aircraft to avoid the turbulent weather.  飞行员预先改变了飞机航向以避开湍流天气。|近义词：preempt, redirect, reroute|反义词：allow, permit
