closefisted:/ˈkloʊsfɪstɪd/ | adj. | 吝啬的；小气的 | Stingy; unwilling to spend money.  The adjective "closefisted" describes someone who is unwilling to spend money or give anything away, even when they can easily afford to. It implies a miserly or tightfisted nature. | He's known to be closefisted with his money, rarely treating himself or others. 他以吝啬著称，很少请自己或别人。|近义词：stingy, miserly, tightfisted, parsimonious |反义词：generous, openhanded, munificent |形容词

词源:
