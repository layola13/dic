cheapos:/ˈtʃiːpoʊz/| n.|廉价货，便宜货 | A cheapo is a person who is unwilling to spend money, or something that is very cheap and of poor quality.  The plural form, "cheapies," is also commonly used.|例句：Don't buy those cheapos; they'll break down in a week.  别买那些廉价货，它们一周内就会坏掉。  Those cheapos at the flea market were surprisingly good quality.  跳蚤市场上的那些廉价货质量出奇的好。|近义词：bargains, cheapskates,  budget items |反义词：luxuries, high-end items,  expensive goods |名词
