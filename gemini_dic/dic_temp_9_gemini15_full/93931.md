immunize:/ˈɪmjʊnaɪz/ | vt. | 使免疫；使免于；预防接种 | To protect someone or something from a disease by giving them a vaccine. |例句：The doctor immunized the child against measles.医生给孩子接种了麻疹疫苗。|例句：We need to immunize ourselves against negative thinking.我们需要使自己免受消极思想的影响。|近义词：inoculate, vaccinate, protect |反义词：infect, endanger


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor immunizes children against polio. = 医生给孩子们接种小儿麻痹症疫苗。
She immunizes her dog against rabies annually. = 她每年都给她的狗接种狂犬病疫苗。


一般过去时:
They immunized the entire population against the flu. = 他们为整个人口接种了流感疫苗。
The nurse immunized me against tetanus. = 护士给我接种了破伤风疫苗。


现在完成时:
I have immunized my cat against several diseases. = 我已经为我的猫接种了几种疾病的疫苗。
She has been immunized against the flu for years. = 她多年来一直接种流感疫苗。


过去完成时:
The doctors had immunized the patients before the outbreak. = 在疫情爆发前，医生们已经为病人接种了疫苗。
They had immunized the livestock against foot-and-mouth disease. = 他们已经为牲畜接种了口蹄疫疫苗。


将来时:
We will immunize all the students before the school year begins. = 我们将在学年开始前为所有学生接种疫苗。
The government will immunize the entire nation against the new virus. = 政府将为全国人民接种新型病毒疫苗。


虚拟语气:
If we had immunized the population earlier, fewer people would have gotten sick. = 如果我们早点为人口接种疫苗，就不会有那么多人患病。
It would be wise to immunize our pets against common diseases. = 为我们的宠物接种常见疾病的疫苗是明智之举。


被动语态:
The children were immunized against diphtheria. = 孩子们接种了白喉疫苗。
The vaccine was designed to immunize against a wide range of viruses. = 该疫苗旨在预防多种病毒。


疑问句:
Have you been immunized against hepatitis B? = 你接种过乙肝疫苗吗？
Is it necessary to immunize against this particular disease? = 有必要针对这种特定疾病进行免疫接种吗？


否定句:
They did not immunize their child against chicken pox. = 他们没有为他们的孩子接种水痘疫苗。
The hospital could not immunize everyone due to vaccine shortage. = 由于疫苗短缺，医院无法为每个人接种疫苗。
