purposer:/ˈpɜːrpəsə(r)/| n.|目的;意图者|A purposer is someone who has a specific purpose or intention.  It refers to someone driven by a goal. The word is rarely used in modern English and is considered somewhat archaic or overly formal.|例句：He was a purposer of great ambition, always striving for something more. 他是一个雄心勃勃的人，总是努力追求更多。  She, as a dedicated purposer, achieved her goal despite many obstacles.  她作为一名坚定目标的人，尽管遇到许多障碍，最终还是实现了她的目标。|近义词：intentionalist, goal-setter,  planner|反义词：aimless, purposeless, indecisive|名词
