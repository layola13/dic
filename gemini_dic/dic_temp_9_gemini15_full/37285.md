一般现在时:
The president serves as the commander-in-chief. = 总统担任总司令。
He acts as commander-in-chief during wartime. = 他在战时担任总司令。


一般过去时:
General Eisenhower was the commander-in-chief of the Allied forces in Europe. = 艾森豪威尔将军是盟军在欧洲的总司令。
She served as commander-in-chief during the conflict. = 她在冲突期间担任总司令。


现在完成时:
The general has been commander-in-chief for over five years. = 该将军担任总司令已超过五年。
He has served as commander-in-chief throughout this challenging period. = 在这段充满挑战的时期，他一直担任总司令。


过去完成时:
Before the war, he had been commander-in-chief of a smaller division. = 战争爆发前，他曾是较小师的总司令。
By the time the battle ended, she had already been commander-in-chief for many months. = 在战斗结束时，她已经担任总司令好几个月了。


将来时:
The newly elected president will be the commander-in-chief. = 新当选的总统将担任总司令。
He will assume the role of commander-in-chief next month. = 他将于下个月担任总司令一职。


虚拟语气:
If he were the commander-in-chief, he would handle the situation differently. = 如果他是总司令，他会以不同的方式处理这种情况。
I wish I were the commander-in-chief; I could solve this crisis. = 我希望我是总司令；我可以解决这场危机。
