self-dissolution: /ˌsɛlf dɪˈsɒljuːʃən/ | n. | 自我解散；自行瓦解 | The process by which something dissolves or disintegrates itself.  This term often refers to the voluntary termination of an organization, group, or legal entity.  It can also describe a process of internal breakdown or decay. |

例句：

The company announced its self-dissolution after years of financial struggles. = 公司在经历多年的财务困境后宣布自行解散。

The rebel group's self-dissolution was a surprising turn of events. = 反叛组织的自发解散是一个令人惊讶的转折。


近义词：disbandment, dissolution, disintegration, collapse

反义词：formation, establishment, consolidation
