careened:/kəˈriːnd/| v.| 倾斜，摇晃，急速行驶 | To lean or sway to one side; to move swiftly and recklessly.  |例句：The car careened around the corner, narrowly avoiding a collision.  汽车急速转弯，险些相撞。  |例句：The drunken sailor careened down the street.  喝醉的船员摇摇晃晃地走在街上。|近义词：lurch, swerve, tilt, veer |反义词：straighten, stabilize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ship careens dangerously in the storm. = 船在暴风雨中危险地倾斜。
He careens his bike down the hill. = 他骑着自行车飞驰下山。


一般过去时:
The bus careened violently and crashed into a tree. = 公共汽车猛烈摇晃并撞到了一棵树上。
The drunkard careened into the lamppost. = 醉汉撞到了路灯杆上。


现在完成时:
The old house has careened noticeably since the last earthquake. = 自上次地震以来，这座老房子明显倾斜了。
The car has careened wildly across the road many times. = 这辆车多次在路上疯狂地摇晃。


过去完成时:
The ship had careened sharply before finally capsizing. = 船在最终倾覆之前已经剧烈倾斜了。
The driver had careened through the streets before the police caught him. = 司机在警察抓住他之前已经在街上疯狂行驶。


将来时:
The runaway train will careen off the tracks. = 失控的火车将脱轨。
I predict that the economy will careen toward a recession. = 我预测经济将走向衰退。


虚拟语气:
If he had not careened, he would not have crashed. = 如果他没有倾斜，他就不会撞车。
If the ship had not careened, it wouldn't have sunk. = 如果船没有倾斜，它就不会沉没。


被动语态:
The car was careened into a ditch by the reckless driver. = 这辆车被鲁莽的司机撞进了沟里。(被动语态不太常用)
The boat was careened dangerously by the strong winds. = 小船被强风吹得危险地倾斜。(被动语态不太常用)


疑问句:
Did the car careen out of control? = 汽车是不是失控地摇晃？
Why did the ship careen so violently? = 为什么船会这么剧烈地倾斜？


否定句:
The driver did not careen, he drove steadily. = 司机没有摇晃，他稳稳地开车。
The ship did not careen; it sailed smoothly. = 船没有倾斜；它航行得很顺利。


PHRASE->

careen down a hill = 飞驰下山
careen through the streets = 在街上飞驰
