outhustle: /aʊtˈhʌsəl/ | vt. |胜过，技高一筹，在竞争中超过|To outperform or surpass someone in skill, effort, or determination, especially in a competitive context.  |例句：He outhustled his opponent to win the championship. 他通过比对手更努力地工作赢得了冠军。

例句：The small startup outhustled the established corporation to secure the lucrative contract. 这家小型初创公司通过比老牌公司更努力地工作获得了这份利润丰厚的合同。


近义词：outperform, outwork, outdo, surpass, exceed


反义词：underperform, fail, lose to


词性：动词
