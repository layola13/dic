remounting: /ˌriːˈmaʊntɪŋ/ | v. |重新安装；重新装备；再次骑乘 |  The act of mounting something again; to mount again.  This verb implies a previous mounting, and refers to the process of putting something back in place or onto something else, often after it has been removed or dismounted. It can refer to physical objects or abstract concepts. |

例句：

The museum is remounting its dinosaur exhibit after renovations. =  博物馆在装修后正在重新安装它的恐龙展览。

After the storm, they were busy remounting the satellite dish. =  暴风雨过后，他们忙着重新安装卫星天线。
