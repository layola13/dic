non-vedic:/ˌnɒnˈveɪdɪk/|/ˌnɑːnˈveɪdɪk/| adj. |非吠陀的；非婆罗门的|Not related to or derived from the Vedas, the ancient Hindu scriptures.  The term often implies a contrast with traditional or orthodox Hindu beliefs and practices.|例句：Many aspects of Indian culture are non-vedic, showing influences from various other sources.  许多印度文化方面是非吠陀的，显示出来自各种其他来源的影响。

例句：The non-vedic traditions of South India are rich and diverse. 南印度的非吠陀传统丰富多样。


近义词：unvedic, heterodox (in the context of Hinduism)


反义词：vedic, orthodox (in the context of Hinduism)


词性：形容词
