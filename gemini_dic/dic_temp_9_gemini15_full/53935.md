discourtesy:/dɪsˈkɜːtəsi/| n.|无礼；失礼；粗鲁|Lack of courtesy; rudeness.  Discourtesy is often shown through impolite behavior or a lack of consideration for others' feelings.  |例句：His discourtesy towards the elderly woman was shocking. 他对这位老妇人的无礼行为令人震惊。

例句：The discourtesy of interrupting someone while they are speaking is unacceptable.  打断别人说话的无礼行为是不可接受的。

近义词：impoliteness, rudeness, incivility, disrespect, bad manners

反义词：courtesy, politeness, civility, respect, consideration

词性:名词
