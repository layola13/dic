prevailing:/prɪˈveɪlɪŋ/| adj.|流行的；盛行的；占优势的；获胜的 | prevailing means existing and being accepted or used by most people.  It suggests something is dominant or widespread. |例句：The prevailing opinion is that the war is unwinnable.  普遍的观点是这场战争无法取胜。

例句：The prevailing wind direction is westerly. 盛行的风向是西风。

近义词：dominant, prevalent, current, widespread, predominant

反义词：uncommon, rare, exceptional, minor, insignificant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The prevailing wind is from the west. = 盛行的风是从西方吹来的。
What is the prevailing fashion this season? = 这个季节流行的时尚是什么？


一般过去时:
The prevailing attitude was one of caution. = 那时盛行的态度是谨慎。
What was the prevailing mood before the announcement? = 宣布之前大家普遍的情绪是什么？


现在完成时:
The prevailing economic conditions have impacted many businesses. = 盛行的经济环境影响了许多企业。
What has been the prevailing trend in technology lately? = 最近科技领域的总体趋势是什么？


过去完成时:
The prevailing belief had been that the earth was flat. = 之前普遍的观点是地球是平的。
What had been the prevailing political ideology before the revolution? = 革命之前主要的政治意识形态是什么？


将来时:
The prevailing weather forecast predicts rain. = 盛行的天气预报预测会有雨。
What will be the prevailing sentiment after the election? = 选后普遍的民意会是什么？


虚拟语气:
If a prevailing south wind were to blow, the fire would spread rapidly. = 如果盛行南风，大火会迅速蔓延。
I wish the prevailing opinion were more optimistic. = 我希望普遍的观点更乐观一些。


被动语态:
The prevailing view is often dictated by the media. = 普遍的观点往往是由媒体决定的。
The prevailing theme is subtly conveyed throughout the novel. = 主导主题在小说中被巧妙地表达出来。


疑问句:
What is the prevailing wisdom on this issue? = 关于这个问题，普遍的看法是什么？
What's the prevailing price for this product? = 这个产品的普遍价格是多少？


否定句:
The prevailing view is not necessarily the correct one. = 普遍的观点并不一定正确。
The prevailing opinion was not in favor of the proposal. = 普遍的观点不支持该提案。
