colander:/ˈkɒləndər/| n. |漏勺，滤器 |A colander is a bowl-shaped kitchen utensil with many holes used for draining liquids from food, such as pasta or vegetables.  |Example: I used a colander to drain the pasta after it was cooked. 我用漏勺沥干煮熟的面条。|近义词：strainer, sieve |反义词：None

词源:
