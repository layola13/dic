half-numb:/ˈhæf nʌmb/| adj.| 半麻木的，部分麻木的 | Partially numb; experiencing a loss of feeling in part of the body.  |例句：My fingers were half-numb from the cold. 我的手指冻得半麻木了。 |例句：After the surgery, her leg felt half-numb for several hours. 手术后，她的腿几个小时都感觉半麻木。|近义词：partially numb, somewhat numb|反义词：fully sensitive, acutely feeling|词性：形容词
