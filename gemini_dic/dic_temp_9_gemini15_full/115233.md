manihoc:/ˈmænɪhɒk/ | n. |木薯 |A starchy tuberous root, Manihot esculenta, that is a major food crop in many tropical regions.  It is also known as cassava. The root is rich in carbohydrates and is used to make various foods such as bread, flour, and tapioca. | Example:  Many people in tropical countries rely on manihoc as a staple food.  许多热带国家的人民依赖木薯作为主食。|近义词：cassava, yuca |反义词：无
