cockchafers:/ˈkɒkˌtʃeɪfəz/| n. |金龟子，甲虫|A cockchafer is a large beetle, especially the common European species (Melolontha melolontha), whose larvae feed on the roots of plants and whose adult form is a pest of trees and crops.|例句：The farmer was worried about the cockchafers damaging his crops.  农民担心金龟子会破坏他的庄稼。|例句：The children collected cockchafers in jars.孩子们用罐子收集金龟子。|近义词：May bug, June bug|反义词：无
