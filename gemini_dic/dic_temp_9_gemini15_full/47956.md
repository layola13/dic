decider:/dɪˈsaɪdər/| n.|决定者，裁决者 | a person or thing that decides something.  Someone or something that makes a decision, especially an important one. |例句：The final decider in the competition was the head judge.  最终的比赛决定者是主裁判。

例句：The jury acted as the decider of the defendant's fate. 陪审团充当了决定被告命运的人。


近义词：judge, arbitrator, determiner, authority |反义词：follower, subordinate


词源：derive from the verb "decide," ultimately from the Latin "decidere," meaning "to cut off," "to determine."  The suffix "-er" indicates an agent or doer of the action.
