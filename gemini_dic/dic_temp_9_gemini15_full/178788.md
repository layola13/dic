recuperating:/rɪˈkuːpəreɪtɪŋ/| v. |恢复健康；复原；重新获得| recovering from illness or injury; regaining something lost or spent.  |例句：He is recuperating from a serious illness. 他正在从一场严重的疾病中恢复。

例句：She's recuperating well after her surgery. 她手术后恢复得很好。

近义词：recovering, convalescing, healing

反义词：worsening, deteriorating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is recuperating well. = 他恢复得很好。
They are recuperating at a spa. = 他们在水疗中心休养。

一般过去时:
She recuperated quickly after the operation. = 她手术后恢复得很快。
He recuperated at his family's home. = 他在他家的休养。

现在完成时:
He has recuperated sufficiently to return to work. = 他已经恢复得足够好，可以重返工作岗位了。
She has recuperated from her injuries. = 她已经从伤病中恢复过来了。


过去完成时:
He had recuperated before the next competition. = 他在下一场比赛前已经恢复了。
She had recuperated completely by the time she returned home. = 她回家时已经完全康复了。

将来时:
He will recuperate quickly with proper rest. = 如果好好休息，他会很快恢复的。
She will recuperate at a seaside resort. = 她将在海滨度假胜地休养。


虚拟语气:
