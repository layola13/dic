copy-the-mail: /ˈkɑːpi ðə meɪl/ | v. | 复制邮件 | To make an exact duplicate of an email. This involves creating a new email with the same content as the original.  This action is often done for various purposes, such as forwarding, archiving, or sending the same email to multiple recipients. | Example:  Please copy-the-mail and send it to the entire team. 请复制邮件并发送给整个团队。 | 近义词: duplicate, replicate, forward | 反义词: delete, erase |  动词
