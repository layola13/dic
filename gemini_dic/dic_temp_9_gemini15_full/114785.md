maltoses:/ˈmɔːltoʊsiːz/| n. |麦芽糖（复数）|Maltose is a disaccharide sugar composed of two glucose units. It is formed by the breakdown of starch and glycogen. It is commonly found in germinating seeds and honey.  |例句：The enzyme amylase breaks down starch into maltoses. 淀粉酶将淀粉分解成麦芽糖。|近义词：glucose, sugars|反义词:  None (no direct antonym)|名词
