hitchers:/ˈhɪtʃərz/| n. |搭便车的人，免费乘客|People who travel by getting free rides in vehicles.|The hitchhikers thumbed a ride on the highway. 搭便车的人在高速公路上竖起了拇指搭车。|Passengers, riders|drivers, pedestrians|名词

一般现在时:
The hitchhikers wait patiently by the roadside. = 搭便车的人耐心地在路边等待。
Many hitchhikers rely on the kindness of strangers. = 许多搭便车的人依靠陌生人的好心。

一般过去时:
The hitchhikers hitched a ride to the next town. = 搭便车的人搭车去了下一个城镇。
We saw several hitchhikers on our journey. = 在我们的旅途中，我们看到几个搭便车的人。

现在完成时:
They have hitchhiked across the country. = 他们已经搭便车穿越了全国。
She has never hitchhiked before. = 她以前从未搭过便车。

过去完成时:
The hitchhikers had already left before we arrived. = 在我们到达之前，搭便车的人已经离开了。
They had hitchhiked for days before finding a ride. = 他们在找到车之前已经搭便车好几天了。

将来时:
We will hitchhike to the festival next week. = 我们下周将搭便车去参加节日。
He will be hitchhiking across Europe this summer. = 他今年夏天将搭便车穿越欧洲。

虚拟语气:
If I were a hitchhiker, I would be more cautious. = 如果我是个搭便车的人，我会更加谨慎。
I wish I hadn't hitchhiked alone that night. = 我真希望那天晚上我没有独自搭便车。

被动语态:
Rides are rarely given to hitchhikers in this area. = 在这个地区，很少有人给搭便车的人搭车。
Hitchhikers are often viewed with suspicion. = 搭便车的人经常受到怀疑。

疑问句:
Are those hitchhikers waiting for a ride? = 那些搭便车的人在等车吗？
How many hitchhikers have you seen today? = 你今天看到多少个搭便车的人了？

否定句:
They are not hitchhikers; they are tourists. = 他们不是搭便车的人；他们是游客。
She did not hitchhike; she took a bus. = 她没有搭便车；她坐公共汽车。
