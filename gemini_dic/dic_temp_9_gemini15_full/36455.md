cold-stores:/ˈkoʊld stɔːrz/| n.|冷藏库，冷藏室|Facilities used for storing perishable goods at low temperatures to maintain freshness and prevent spoilage.|例句：The company invested in new cold-stores to expand its capacity for fresh produce. 这家公司投资建设新的冷藏库，以扩大其新鲜农产品的储存能力。|例句：Many supermarkets rely on extensive cold-store networks to ensure the quality of their products.许多超市依靠庞大的冷藏库网络来保证其产品质量。|近义词：refrigerated warehouses, chill rooms|反义词：ambient storage
