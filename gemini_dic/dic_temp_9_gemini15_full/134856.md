nonmodifying: /ˌnɒnˈmɒdɪfaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 非修饰的 |  Describes a word, phrase, or clause that does not modify or describe another element in a sentence.  It functions independently, often adding extra information or an aside.  | Example:  The cat, *a fluffy Persian*, sat on the mat. (The phrase "a fluffy Persian" is nonmodifying; it adds information about the cat but doesn't directly modify another word.) | 近义词: supplementary, additional, parenthetical | 反义词: modifying, restrictive
