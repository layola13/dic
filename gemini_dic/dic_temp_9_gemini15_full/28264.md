captious:/ˈkæpʃəs/ | adj. |吹毛求疵的，爱挑剔的 | tending to find fault or raise objections; given to petty criticism.  |例句：He's a captious critic, always finding fault with everything. 他是一个吹毛求疵的评论家，总是对一切挑剔。

例句：Her captious remarks spoiled the party atmosphere. 她吹毛求疵的言论破坏了聚会的氛围。


近义词：critical, faultfinding, censorious, carping, caviling | 反义词：uncritical, tolerant, lenient, forgiving


词源:  From Middle English *capcios, from Latin captiosus "able to trap, ensnaring," from captiō "a taking, seizure," from capere "to take, seize."  The sense development is from "able to trap" (in argument) to "tending to find fault."
