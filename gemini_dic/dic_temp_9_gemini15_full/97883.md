innateness:/ˈɪneɪtnəs/| n. |天赋；先天性；固有性 |The inherent quality of something, especially a characteristic or ability present from birth.  It refers to the idea that certain traits, behaviors, or knowledge are present at birth rather than learned through experience. |Example: The innateness of language acquisition is a subject of ongoing debate among linguists.  语言习得的天赋性是语言学家们持续争论的话题。|近义词: inbornness, inherent nature, nativism |反义词: acquiredness, learnedness
