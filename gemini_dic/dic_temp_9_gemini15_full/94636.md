impresses:/ɪmˈprɛsɪz/| vt.| 给…留下深刻印象；使钦佩；强迫接受 |to have a strong effect on someone's mind; to cause someone to feel admiration and respect |The grand scale of the project impressed everyone.  这个项目的宏伟规模给每个人留下了深刻印象。|Her intelligence and determination impressed me deeply. 她的智慧和决心给我留下了深刻的印象。|affect, strike, overwhelm |disappoint, bore |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His work impresses his boss. = 他的工作给老板留下了深刻的印象。
The painting impresses me greatly. = 这幅画给我留下了深刻的印象。

一般过去时:
The movie impressed me last night. = 我昨晚对那部电影印象深刻。
His speech impressed the audience. = 他的演讲给观众留下了深刻的印象。

现在完成时:
His achievements have impressed everyone in the company. = 他的成就给公司里的每个人都留下了深刻的印象。
The beauty of the scenery has impressed me deeply. = 这风景的美景给我留下了深刻的印象。

过去完成时:
I had been impressed by his previous work. = 我对他以前的作品印象深刻。
She had already impressed the interviewer with her qualifications. = 她已经凭借自己的资格给面试官留下了深刻的印象。

将来时:
This new technology will impress the customers. = 这项新技术将给客户留下深刻的印象。
I believe his presentation will impress the judges. = 我相信他的陈述会给评委留下深刻的印象。

虚拟语气:
If he had worked harder, he would have impressed his teacher. = 如果他更努力工作，他就会给老师留下深刻的印象。
I wish I could impress her with my skills. = 我希望我能用我的技能给她留下深刻的印象。

被动语态:
The audience was impressed by the singer's performance. = 观众对歌手的表演印象深刻。
He was impressed by her kindness. = 他被她的善良所感动。

疑问句:
Does the new design impress you? = 新的设计给你留下了深刻的印象吗？
Will the presentation impress the investors? = 演示会给投资者留下深刻印象吗？

否定句:
The movie didn't impress me at all. = 那部电影根本没有给我留下什么印象。
His explanation didn't impress the judge. = 他的解释没有给法官留下印象。


PHRASE->

impress somebody with something = 用某物给某人留下深刻印象
be impressed by/with = 对…印象深刻
make an impression on = 给…留下印象
impress oneself on = 使自己对…留下印象
