packed:/pækt/ | adj. v. | 包装好的；塞满的；挤满的；（时间）紧凑的 |  Describes something filled to capacity or tightly compressed; also describes a schedule that is full.  | 例句：The suitcase was packed with clothes.  = 行李箱塞满了衣服。


The box was packed full of books. = 箱子里装满了书。


近义词：crowded, crammed, full, tight, busy, compressed | 反义词：empty, sparse, loose, unfilled, leisurely

词性：形容词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He packs his lunch every day. = 他每天都打包午餐。
They pack the boxes carefully. = 他们仔细地打包箱子。


一般过去时:
She packed her bags before the trip. = 她旅行前收拾好行李。
We packed the car for the camping trip. = 我们为露营旅行打包了汽车。


现在完成时:
He has packed his things and is ready to go. = 他已经收拾好东西，准备出发了。
They have packed all the presents for the party. = 他们已经把派对的所有礼物都打包好了。


过去完成时:
She had packed her suitcase the night before. = 她前一天晚上就已经打包好行李了。
They had packed the truck before the sun rose. = 太阳升起之前他们就已经把卡车装满了。


将来时:
I will pack my bags tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上会打包行李。
We will pack the food for the picnic. = 我们将为野餐打包食物。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pack more carefully. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更仔细地打包。
I wish I had packed a warmer jacket. = 我真希望我打包一件更暖和的夹克。


被动语态:
The boxes were packed yesterday. = 箱子昨天被打包了。
The car is being packed for the journey. = 汽车正在为旅程打包。


疑问句:
Have you packed your lunch? = 你打包午餐了吗？
Are they packed for the trip yet? = 他们为旅行打包好了吗？


否定句:
I haven't packed my suitcase yet. = 我还没有打包行李。
They didn't pack enough food for the hike. = 他们没有为徒步旅行打包足够的食物。


PHRASE->
packed lunch: 便当
packed schedule: 紧凑的日程
packed house: 座无虚席
packed earth: 压实的泥土
packed snow: 压实的雪
