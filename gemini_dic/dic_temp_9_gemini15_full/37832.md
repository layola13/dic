competence:/ˈkɒmpɪtəns/ /ˈkɑːmpɪtəns/| n. |能力；胜任；权限|The ability to do something successfully or efficiently.  It often refers to the skills and knowledge needed to perform a particular task or job.  It can also refer to the authority to do something.|例句：He demonstrated his competence in handling difficult situations. 他展现了他处理棘手情况的能力。

例句：The company questioned her competence to manage such a large project. 公司质疑她管理如此大型项目的能力。


近义词：ability, capability, proficiency, expertise, skill|反义词：incompetence, inability, incapability
