huntress:/ˈhʌntres/| n. |女猎手；女猎人|A woman who hunts.  The term often implies skill and prowess in hunting, potentially with a connotation of independence and strength.|例句：The huntress stalked her prey with quiet determination. 这名女猎手以平静的决心跟踪猎物。

例句：The skilled huntress brought down a magnificent stag.  这位技艺高超的女猎手射倒了一只雄伟的雄鹿。


近义词：female hunter, huntswoman


反义词：prey


词性：名词

词源:
The word "huntress" is derived from the Old English word "huntere," meaning "hunter." The suffix "-ess" is a feminine suffix added to nouns, indicating a female version of the word.  Therefore, "huntress" literally means "female hunter."
