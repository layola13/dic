nitrocellulose:/ˌnaɪtroʊˈsɛljuloʊs/ | n. |硝化纤维素|A highly flammable compound produced by treating cellulose with nitric acid; used in explosives, lacquers, and photographic film.|例句：Nitrocellulose is a key ingredient in some types of gunpowder.硝化纤维素是某些类型火药的关键成分。|近义词：guncotton, cellulose nitrate |反义词：无
