purveys:/pərˈveɪz/| vt.|供应，提供，传播|To supply or provide (something, especially goods, services, or information).  The restaurant purveys delicious Italian food. 这家餐厅供应美味的意大利菜。|近义词：supply, provide, furnish, dispense|反义词： withhold, deprive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company purveys high-quality products. = 这家公司供应高质量的产品。
This website purveys false information. = 这个网站传播虚假信息。


一般过去时:
The merchant purveyed spices from the East. = 商人供应来自东方的香料。
The newspaper purveyed sensational news stories. = 这家报纸传播耸人听闻的新闻报道。


现在完成时:
They have purveyed food to the homeless shelter for years. = 他们多年来一直向收容所提供食物。
The media has purveyed a distorted image of the situation. = 媒体传播了对局势的歪曲形象。


过去完成时:
The shop had purveyed groceries to the community for decades. = 这家商店几十年来一直为社区提供杂货。
Before the scandal, the politician had purveyed a clean image. = 在丑闻发生之前，这位政客一直保持着清廉的形象。


将来时:
The farmer will purvey his produce at the market. = 农民将在市场上出售他的农产品。
The government will purvey aid to the disaster-stricken area. = 政府将向灾区提供援助。


虚拟语气:
If the store purveyed organic products, I would shop there more often. = 如果这家商店供应有机产品，我会更经常去那里购物。
I wish the company purveyed more sustainable options. = 我希望这家公司提供更多可持续发展的选择。


被动语态:
Fresh produce was purveyed to the hotel daily. = 新鲜农产品每天都被供应到酒店。
False news was purveyed through social media. = 虚假新闻通过社交媒体传播。


疑问句:
Does this restaurant purvey vegetarian options? = 这家餐厅提供素食选择吗？
What kind of information does this website purvey? = 这个网站传播什么信息？


否定句:
The company does not purvey substandard goods. = 这家公司不供应劣质商品。
The teacher did not purvey misleading information to her students. = 老师没有向她的学生传播误导性信息。


PHRASE->
purvey to: 向…供应
purvey for: 为…供应
purvey of:  …的供应者
purvey from: 来自…的供应
purvey throughout: 遍布…供应


词源:
源自古法语 *purveier，意为“提供者”，最终源自古法语 purveir，意为“提供”。  This in turn has roots in the Latin word *providere, meaning "to foresee, provide."
