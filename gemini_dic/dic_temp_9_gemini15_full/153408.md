penitent:/ˈpɛnɪtənt/| adj. n.|后悔的；忏悔的；悔罪者|Feeling or showing sorrow and regret for having done wrong; repentant.  A person who repents their sins or wrongdoing.|The penitent criminal asked for forgiveness.  这位悔罪的罪犯请求宽恕。|He showed true penitence for his actions. 他为自己的行为表现出真正的悔恨。|repentant, remorseful, contrite|unrepentant, remorseless, impenitent|形容词，名词
