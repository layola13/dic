scratchier:/ˈskræʧəriər/| adj.| 粗糙的；刺耳的；令人不快的 | More rough or harsh to the touch or ear.  It implies a greater degree of roughness or harshness than "scratchy".|例句：The wool sweater felt scratchier than I expected. 这羊毛衫比我想象的要粗糙。

The singer's voice sounded scratchier after the long concert.  演唱会结束后，歌手的声音听起来更沙哑了。|近义词：rough, harsh, abrasive, grating |反义词：smooth, soft, gentle
