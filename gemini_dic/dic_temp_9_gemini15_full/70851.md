fink:/fɪŋk/| n. v.|告密者；出卖者；罢工破坏者；（非正式）无用的人；|英文解析：A fink is a person who informs on others, especially to those in authority.  It can also refer to someone who is considered unreliable or incompetent, or a strikebreaker.  The word carries a strong negative connotation.|例句：He was branded a fink for telling the police about the robbery. 他因为向警方举报抢劫案而被贴上了告密者的标签。

He's a real fink; he never finishes what he starts. 他真是个没用的人；他从来不完成他开始的事情。|近义词：informer, snitch, rat, stool pigeon, scab|反义词：loyal, trustworthy, reliable
