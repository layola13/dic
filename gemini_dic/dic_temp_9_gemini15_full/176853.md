reaffiliating: /ˌriːəˈfɪlieɪtɪŋ/ | v. |重新加入；重新联系；重新加入组织 | The act of re-establishing an affiliation or connection.  It implies a previous affiliation that was broken or discontinued, and now is being restored. | 例句：The university is  reaffiliating with the national athletic association after a period of independence.  大学在一段时间的独立后正重新加入全国体育协会。|The company is  reaffiliating with its former distributor to expand its market reach. 这家公司正在与之前的经销商重新合作以扩大其市场份额。|近义词：rejoin, reconnect, reassociate |反义词：disaffiliate, sever ties, disconnect |动词
