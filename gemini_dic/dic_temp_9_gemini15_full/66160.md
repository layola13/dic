excerpting: /ɪkˈsɜːrptɪŋ/ | v. | 摘录；节录 | The act of selecting and extracting portions of a text or other work.  It implies a careful choice of parts to represent the whole, often for a specific purpose. | Example:  She is excerpting passages from the novel for her literary analysis. = 她正在为她的文学分析摘录小说中的段落。|近义词: extracting, quoting, selecting |反义词: summarizing (in the sense of condensing rather than selecting specific parts)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is excerpting key findings from the report. = 他正在从报告中摘录关键发现。
They excerpt relevant information for their presentation. = 他们为演示文稿摘录相关信息。


一般过去时:
She excerpted the most interesting parts of the book. = 她摘录了书中最有趣的部分。
He excerpted several chapters for his dissertation. = 他为他的论文摘录了几章。


现在完成时:
I have excerpted the main arguments for my essay. = 我已经为我的论文摘录了主要论点。
They have excerpted passages from various sources. = 他们已经从各种来源摘录了段落。


过去完成时:
She had excerpted the relevant data before the meeting. = 她已经在会议前摘录了相关数据。
He had excerpted the poems before he started his analysis. = 他在开始分析之前已经摘录了这些诗歌。


将来时:
I will excerpt the best parts of the speech for the newsletter. = 我将为简报摘录演讲的最佳部分。
They will excerpt the most important statistics for the report. = 他们将为报告摘录最重要的统计数据。


虚拟语气:
If I were writing a summary, I would excerpt more carefully. = 如果我写一篇摘要，我会更仔细地摘录。
I wish I had excerpted more information from the original text. = 我希望我从原文中摘录了更多信息。


被动语态:
Key passages were excerpted from the book for the anthology. = 书中的关键段落被摘录到选集中。
The most important quotes were excerpted and presented in the paper. = 最重要的引语被摘录并呈现在论文中。


疑问句:
Will you excerpt the entire chapter or just the introduction? = 你会摘录整章还是仅仅摘录引言？
Where did you excerpt that passage from? = 你从哪里摘录的那段话？


否定句:
I did not excerpt anything from that unreliable source. = 我没有从那个不可靠的来源摘录任何东西。
They did not excerpt enough evidence to support their claim. = 他们没有摘录足够的证据来支持他们的说法。
