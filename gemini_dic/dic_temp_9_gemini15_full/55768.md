diversifies:/daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪz/| v.|使多样化；使多样化；发展多样化|To make something more varied or diverse; to expand into different areas of business or activity.|The company diversifies its product line to reduce risk.  公司将其产品线多样化以降低风险。|The university is diversifying its student body. 这所大学正在使学生群体多样化。|specializes, expands, broadens|unifies, concentrates, homogenizes|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company diversifies its investments. = 公司使投资多样化。
She diversifies her portfolio with different stocks. = 她用不同的股票使她的投资组合多样化。

一般过去时:
The farmer diversified his crops last year. = 农民去年使他的作物多样化。
They diversified their business into new markets. = 他们将业务扩展到新的市场。

现在完成时:
The company has diversified its operations significantly. = 公司已经显著地使运营多样化。
He has diversified his income streams by starting a side business. = 他通过开办副业使他的收入来源多样化。

过去完成时:
The company had already diversified its product line before the market crash. = 在市场崩盘之前，公司已经使产品线多样化。
She had diversified her investments before the economic downturn. = 在经济低迷之前，她已经使投资多样化。

将来时:
The company will diversify its offerings next year. = 公司明年将使产品多样化。
We will diversify our marketing strategies to reach a wider audience. = 我们将使营销策略多样化以接触更广泛的受众。

虚拟语气:
If the company diversified its products, it would be less vulnerable to market fluctuations. = 如果公司使产品多样化，它将不太容易受到市场波动的影响。
I wish they had diversified their investments earlier. = 我希望他们早些时候就使投资多样化。

被动语态:
The company's operations are being diversified. = 公司的运营正在多样化。
The portfolio was diversified by a professional financial advisor. = 投资组合由专业的理财顾问进行了多样化处理。

疑问句:
Does the company diversify its products? = 公司是否使产品多样化？
How will they diversify their income? = 他们将如何使收入多样化？

否定句:
The company does not diversify its investments enough. = 公司的投资多样化程度不够。
They didn't diversify their business quickly enough. = 他们没有及时地使业务多样化。
