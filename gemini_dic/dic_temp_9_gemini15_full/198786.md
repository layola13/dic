sickness:/ˈsɪknɪs/| n. |疾病；恶心；呕吐；病态|The state of being ill; a disease.  Sickness can also refer to nausea or vomiting.  It can also describe a morally or mentally unhealthy state.|例句：His sickness kept him from attending the meeting.他的病使他无法参加会议。

例句：The smell caused a wave of sickness.  气味引起一阵恶心。


近义词：illness, disease, malady, ailment, affliction|反义词：health, wellness, well-being|词性:名词
