reindicate:/ˌriːˈɪndɪkeɪt/| vt.|重新表明；再次指出；重新指示|To indicate again or anew; to show or point out again.|The doctor reindicated the importance of a healthy diet. 医生再次强调了健康饮食的重要性。|近义词：reinforce, reiterate, restate|反义词：obscure, conceal|动词
