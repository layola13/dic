phosphorescent: /ˌfɒsfəˈrɛsnt/ /ˌfɑːsfəˈrɛsnt/ | adj. |磷光的；发磷光的 |  Emitting light without appreciable heat.  This light is emitted after the source of excitation has been removed. Phosphorescence is a type of luminescence. Unlike fluorescence, phosphorescence persists for a longer time after the excitation source is removed. |


例句：

The glow-in-the-dark stickers are phosphorescent. = 这些夜光贴纸是磷光的。

The phosphorescent paint on the ceiling glowed softly in the dark. = 天花板上的磷光涂料在黑暗中柔和地发光。


近义词：luminescent, glowing


反义词：non-luminescent, dark


词源:

From phospho- + -escent.  "Phospho-" derives from phosphorus, referring to the element phosphorus, which exhibits this property. "-escent" is a suffix indicating a gradual process or state.  The word ultimately relates to the Greek word "phos," meaning "light."
