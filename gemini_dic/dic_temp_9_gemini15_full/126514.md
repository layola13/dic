na:/nɑː/ | /næ/ | pron.| 那，那个；（用于强调）正是；（用于反驳）才不；|  This word is a demonstrative pronoun indicating something previously mentioned or easily identified.  It can also be used for emphasis or contrast. | Examples:  That's my car.  那是我的车。 He said he wouldn't go, and na, he didn't. 他说他不去，而且，他真的没去。|近义词：that, this those|反义词：无|pronoun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
That is my book. = 那是我的书。
That is a beautiful flower. = 那是一朵漂亮的花。


一般过去时:
That was a great movie. = 那是一部很棒的电影。
That happened yesterday. = 那件事发生在昨天。


现在完成时:
That has been a long day. = 今天过得很漫长。
That has already been decided. = 那已经决定了。


过去完成时:
That had already happened before I arrived. = 那在我到达之前就已经发生了。
That had been his plan all along. = 那一直都是他的计划。


将来时:
That will be a great party. = 那将是一个盛大的派对。
That will happen next week. = 那将会在下周发生。


虚拟语气:
If that were true, I would be surprised. = 如果那是真的，我会很惊讶。
I wish that were possible. = 我希望那是可能的。


被动语态:
That was decided by the committee. = 那由委员会决定。
That is being investigated by the police. = 那正在由警方调查。


疑问句:
Is that your bag? = 那是你的包吗？
Is that what you want? = 那是你想要的吗？


否定句:
That is not my problem. = 那不是我的问题。
That wasn't what I meant. = 那不是我的意思。
