costa-ricas: /ˈkɔːstə ˈriːkəs/ | n. |哥斯达黎加（的）| Costa Ricas refers to things relating to or originating from Costa Rica, a country in Central America.  It can be used as an adjective or, less commonly, as a noun referring to the people or culture of Costa Rica.|

例句：
Costa Rican coffee is renowned worldwide.  = 哥斯达黎加咖啡享誉世界。

The vibrant culture of Costa Ricas is captivating. = 哥斯达黎加的活力文化令人着迷。


近义词：Costa Rican (adjective)


反义词：无


词性:  名词(有时作形容词)
