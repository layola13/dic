harmonics:/hɑːrˈmɒnɪks/ /hɑːrˈmɑːnɪks/| n.|泛音；谐波；和声；和谐|The branch of acoustics that deals with the production and properties of musical sounds, specifically the relationship between fundamental and overtones.|例句：The harmonics of the guitar added richness to the sound. 吉他泛音增加了声音的丰富度。

The singer's voice was full of beautiful harmonics. 歌唱家的声音充满了美丽的泛音。|近义词：overtones, resonance|反义词：discord, dissonance
