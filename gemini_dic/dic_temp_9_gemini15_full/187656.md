rood:/ruːd/ | n. | 罗德（面积单位）；十字架；残酷的行为 | A rood is an old unit of area, equal to one-quarter of an acre.  It also refers to a cross, especially a large one in a church.  Figuratively, it can mean a cruel or wicked deed. | Example: The church boasted a magnificent rood screen. 这座教堂拥有一个宏伟的圣像屏风。|近义词：cross, crucifix (for the cross meaning); acre (in relation to area)|反义词：kindness, mercy (for the figurative meaning)
