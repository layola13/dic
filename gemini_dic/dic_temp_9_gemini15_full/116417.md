matadors:/ˌmætəˈdɔːrz/| n.|斗牛士|A matador is a bullfighter, especially the chief bullfighter in a Spanish bullfight.  Matadors are known for their skill, bravery, and grace in facing the bull. They use a red cape and a sword to subdue and kill the animal.|The matador expertly maneuvered the cape, distracting the bull.  斗牛士熟练地挥舞着斗篷，分散了公牛的注意力。|近义词：bullfighter, torero|反义词：无
