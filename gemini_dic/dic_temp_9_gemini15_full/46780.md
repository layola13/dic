danging:/ˈdæŋɪŋ/| adj. | 悬荡的；摇摇欲坠的 | Dangling refers to something hanging loosely or insecurely, often suggesting a sense of instability or precariousness.  It can describe physical objects or metaphorical situations. |例句：The sign was danging precariously from the broken post.  招牌摇摇欲坠地挂在断裂的柱子上。 The unresolved issue left the future of the project danging. 未解决的问题使项目的未来岌岌可危。|近义词：hanging, swaying, pendulous, precarious |反义词：secure, stable, firm, fixed |形容词
