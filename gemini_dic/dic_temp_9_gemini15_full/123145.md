monitors:/ˈmɒnɪtərz/ /ˈmɑːnɪtərz/ | n. |监视器；监控器；监听器；监督者 |A device used to display information from a computer or other electronic device.  It can also refer to a person who monitors or supervises something. |例句：The nurse monitors the patient's vital signs. 护士监测病人的生命体征。

例句：The security cameras monitor the building 24/7. 安全摄像头全天候监控大楼。


近义词：supervisors, observers, controllers, screens, displays

反义词：ignore, neglect


词性: 名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system monitors the temperature constantly. = 系统持续监测温度。
He monitors his employees' work closely. = 他密切监督员工的工作。


一般过去时:
She monitored the progress of the project daily. = 她每天监测项目的进展。
They monitored the situation carefully. = 他们仔细地监视局势。


现在完成时:
We have monitored the market trends for months. = 我们已经监测市场趋势好几个月了。
The scientists have monitored the volcano's activity since last year. = 科学家们自去年以来一直在监测火山的活动。


过去完成时:
They had monitored the patient's heart rate before the surgery. = 他们在手术前已经监测了病人的心率。
The company had monitored the sales figures for the previous quarter. = 公司已经监测了上一季度的销售数据。


将来时:
We will monitor the experiment closely. = 我们将密切关注实验。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
