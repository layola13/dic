chippable:/ˈtʃɪpəbəl/ | adj. |易碎的；可凿的；可削的 | Easily broken or damaged; able to be chipped.  |例句：The paint on the car is very chippable.  这辆车的油漆很容易剥落。
The ice was chippable with a hammer.  冰可以用锤子凿碎。

近义词：fragile, brittle, breakable
反义词：durable, strong, sturdy
