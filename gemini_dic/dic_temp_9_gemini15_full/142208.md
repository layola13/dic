ornitholite:/ˈɔːrnɪθəlaɪt/| n. |鸟粪石|A sedimentary rock composed largely of fossilized bird droppings (guano).  Ornitholites are valuable sources of phosphate. | Example:  The discovery of ornitholites in this region suggests a significant ancient bird population.  这个地区的鸟粪石发现表明古代鸟类数量众多。|近义词：guano (as a substance), phosphatic rock |反义词：无
