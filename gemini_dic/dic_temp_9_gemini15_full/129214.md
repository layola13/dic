nobody's: /ˈnoʊbɑːdiz/ | pron. | 无人所有；不属于任何人 |  The possessive form of "nobody," indicating something belonging to no one. It's a contraction of "nobody is" or "nobody has."  | 例句：Nobody's car is parked here.  = 这里没有人的车停着。

Nobody's fault is apparent. = 看不出是谁的错。


近义词：no one's, none's

反义词：everyone's, somebody's
