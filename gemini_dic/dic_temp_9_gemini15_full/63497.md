entwining:/ɪnˈtwaɪnɪŋ/| v. n. adj.| 缠绕；交织；（关系等的）密切联系 |The act of twisting or intertwining things together; a close relationship or connection.  |例句：The trees' branches were entwining overhead. 树枝在头顶缠绕在一起。

The vines were entwining around the fence.藤蔓缠绕在栅栏上。


近义词：intertwining, interweaving, twisting, braiding|反义词：separating, untangling|动词，名词，形容词
