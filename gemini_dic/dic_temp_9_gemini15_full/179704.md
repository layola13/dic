reenters:/ˌriːˈɛntərz/| v.|重新进入，再进入|To enter again; to return to a place or situation.|例句：The astronaut reenters the spacecraft after his spacewalk. 宇航员太空行走后重新进入宇宙飞船。

例句：After a brief intermission, the actors reentered the stage.  短暂的幕间休息后，演员们重新回到舞台上。


近义词：return, re-enter, come back, go back|反义词：exit, leave, depart|动词
