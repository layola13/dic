revaccinations:/ˌriːvæksɪˈneɪʃənz/| n. |再次接种疫苗；追加接种疫苗|The act of vaccinating again, typically to boost immunity or to address a new variant of a virus.  Revaccinations are recommended for some individuals to maintain protection against diseases.| 例句：The government is urging people to get their flu revaccinations this year. = 政府正在敦促人们今年接种流感疫苗加强针。

The hospital is offering free revaccinations for the measles. = 医院正在提供免费的麻疹疫苗加强针。


近义词：booster shots, repeat vaccinations, second vaccinations|反义词：initial vaccination, first vaccination
