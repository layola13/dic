It is a relatively uncommon medical term, primarily used in a clinical setting. |Example:  The patient presented with melanorrhoea, a symptom suggesting internal bleeding.  = 病人出现黑便，这预示着体内出血。

The patient's melanorrhoea was a serious indication requiring immediate medical attention. = 病人的黑便是一个严重的迹象，需要立即就医。

近义词:  black stool, melena (specifically for dark, tarry stool)  | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with normal bowel movement or clear nasal discharge) | 词性: n.
