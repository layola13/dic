lancasterian:/ˌlæŋˈkæstəriən/ | adj. | 兰开斯特式的；兰开夏郡的 | Relating to Lancaster or Lancashire, especially to the educational system developed by Joseph Lancaster.  The term often refers to a system of monitorial education where older students teach younger ones. | Example: The school employed a Lancasterian system of teaching, with older pupils assisting the master.  兰开斯特学校采用了一种兰开斯特式的教学制度，由高年级的学生协助老师。| 近义词：monitorial (监视的，指导的) | 反义词：individualized (个别化的)
