dean-ship's: /ˈdiːnʃɪps/ | n. | 系主任的职位；院长职位；（学院的）院长职务 | The position or office of a dean.  It often refers to the authority and responsibilities associated with the role. The possessive 's indicates that something belongs to or is related to the deanship. |  例句：The dean-ship's responsibilities include overseeing the academic programs. 系主任的职责包括监督学术项目。  The new dean-ship's focus is on student success. 新任院长关注学生的成功。 | 近义词：deanship, headship | 反义词：无  | 词性：名词
