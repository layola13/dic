generalise:/ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪz/| vt.|概括；推广；普遍化|To make a general statement about something; to form a general opinion about something based on limited information.  To extend or apply to a wider range of people or things.|例句：Don't generalise about all men based on your experiences with one.  不要根据你与一个男人的经历来概括所有男人。

例句：The research findings can be generalised to other populations.  研究结果可以推广到其他人群。|近义词：summarize, generalize, broaden, extend|反义词：specify, particularize, detail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He generalises about politics. = 他对政治进行概括。
She generalises her findings to a larger sample. = 她将她的研究结果推广到更大的样本。

一般过去时:
They generalised the theory to different contexts. = 他们将该理论推广到不同的语境中。
He generalised from a small sample size. = 他根据小样本量进行了概括。

现在完成时:
Scientists have generalised the principles of physics. = 科学家们已经概括了物理学的原理。
She has generalised her experience to other situations. = 她已将她的经验推广到其他情况。

过去完成时:
They had generalised the problem before they found the solution. = 他们找到解决方案之前已经对问题进行了概括。
He had generalised the pattern before analysing the data in detail. = 在详细分析数据之前，他已经概括了模式。

将来时:
We will generalise the model to other applications. = 我们将把该模型推广到其他应用中。
She will generalise her research findings in the next publication. = 她将在下一份出版物中概括她的研究结果。

虚拟语气:
If I understood better, I wouldn't generalise so much. = 如果我理解得更好，我就不会这么概括了。
If he had more data, he wouldn't have generalised so quickly. = 如果他有更多的数据，他就不会这么快就概括了。

被动语态:
The results were generalised to a wider population. = 结果被推广到更广泛的人群。
The theory was generalised by several researchers. = 该理论被几位研究人员推广。

疑问句:
Can we generalise these findings to other cultures? = 我们可以将这些发现推广到其他文化吗？
How did he generalise from such limited evidence? = 他是如何从如此有限的证据中进行概括的？

否定句:
You shouldn't generalise about people based on their appearance. = 你不应该根据人的外表来概括他们。
We cannot generalise this particular case to all similar situations. = 我们不能将这个特例推广到所有类似的情况。
