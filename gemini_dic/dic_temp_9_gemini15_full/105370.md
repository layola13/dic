kickstand:/ˈkɪkˌstænd/| n. |撑脚架；支架|A stand, typically hinged or folding, used to support a bicycle or motorcycle when it is not in motion.|例句：I put down the kickstand before I got off my bike. 我下车前放下了自行车撑脚架。|例句：The motorcycle's kickstand was broken, so it kept falling over. 电动车的撑脚架坏了，所以它一直倒下。|近义词：stand, support, prop|反义词:None.
