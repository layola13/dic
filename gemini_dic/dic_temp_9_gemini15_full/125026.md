mujahideen:/ˌmuːdʒəˈhiːdɪn/| n. |圣战者；穆加希德（复数）|Participants in a jihad, often referring to Islamic holy warriors engaged in armed struggle. The term is often used to describe fighters involved in various conflicts, sometimes with varying degrees of religious motivation.  |例句：The mujahideen fought against the Soviet occupation of Afghanistan.  圣战者们对抗苏联对阿富汗的占领。|近义词：guerrillas, fighters, combatants, insurgents|反义词：collaborators, peacekeepers|名词
