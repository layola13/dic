placatingly:/ˈplækɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 安抚地；讨好地 |  In a way intended to soothe or appease.  The adverbial form of "placating," indicating a manner or action performed to calm someone down. |


例句：

He spoke placatingly to the angry customer. = 他安抚地对生气顾客说话。

She offered a placating smile. = 她报以一个安抚的笑容。


近义词： soothingly, appeasingly, conciliatorily, mollifyingly

反义词： antagonistically, aggressively, irritably, provokingly

词性：副词
