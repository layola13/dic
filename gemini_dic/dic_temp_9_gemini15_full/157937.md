planet:/ˈplænɪt/| n.|行星|A large, round object in space that moves around a star.  Planets do not produce light of their own; they shine by reflecting the light of their star.|Example: Earth is a planet in the solar system. 地球是太阳系中的一颗行星。|近义词：world, celestial body|反义词：star, sun
