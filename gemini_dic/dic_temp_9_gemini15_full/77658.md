gelding:/ˈɡɛldɪŋ/| n. |阉割的马；阉马|A male horse that has been castrated.  Gelding is also used to refer to the act of castrating a male horse.|例句：He owns a beautiful gelding that won several races. 他拥有一匹赢得过几次比赛的漂亮的阉马。
The vet performed the gelding procedure quickly and efficiently. 兽医快速有效地进行了阉割手术。|近义词：castrated horse, stallion (before castration)|反义词：stallion
