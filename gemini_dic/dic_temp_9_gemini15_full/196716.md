sewed:/soʊd/| v.|缝，缝合|To join or unite (pieces of fabric or other material) with stitches using a needle and thread.  It implies a completed action of sewing.|例句：She sewed a button onto her shirt. 她把纽扣缝在了衬衫上。
She sewed the two pieces of fabric together. 她把两块布缝在一起了。|近义词：stitched, darned, patched|反义词：unsew, rip


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I sew every Saturday. = 我每个星期六都缝纫。
She sews beautiful dresses. = 她缝制美丽的衣服。


一般过去时:
I sewed a dress yesterday. = 我昨天缝了一件衣服。
He sewed the tear in his pants. = 他缝补了他裤子上的破洞。


现在完成时:
I have sewed all the buttons on. = 我已经缝上了所有的纽扣。
She has sewed many quilts in her lifetime. = 她一生缝制了许多棉被。


过去完成时:
I had sewed the curtains before the party started. = 聚会开始前我已经缝好了窗帘。
She had sewed all the costumes before the play began. = 在戏剧开始之前，她已经缝好了所有的戏服。


将来时:
I will sew a new dress for the wedding. = 我将为婚礼缝制一件新衣服。
She will sew the buttons on tomorrow. = 她明天会缝上纽扣。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would sew a quilt. = 如果我有更多时间，我会缝一床被子。
I wish I had sewed that dress sooner. = 我真希望我早点缝好那件衣服。


被动语态:
The button was sewed on securely. = 纽扣被牢固地缝上了。
The quilt was sewed by my grandmother. = 这床被子是我祖母缝的。


疑问句:
Did you sew this dress yourself? = 这件衣服是你自己缝的吗？
Have you ever sewed a garment before? = 你以前缝过衣服吗？


否定句:
I didn't sew the dress; I bought it. = 我没有缝这件衣服；我是买的。
She hasn't sewed anything today. = 她今天什么都没缝。


PHRASE->
sew on: 缝上
sew up: 缝合，解决（问题）
sew together: 缝合在一起
hand-sewed: 手工缝制的
machine-sewed: 机器缝制的
