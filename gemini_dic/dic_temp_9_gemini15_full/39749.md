consolator:/ˈkɒnsəleɪtə(r)/|/ˈkɑːnsəleɪtər/| n. |安慰者，慰藉者 |A consolator is a person who offers comfort or solace to someone who is suffering.  They provide emotional support during difficult times. |例句：She was a true consolator to her grieving friend. 她是她悲伤的朋友真正的安慰者。|例句：The priest acted as a consolator, offering words of hope and peace. 牧师扮演安慰者的角色，提供希望与和平的话语。|近义词：comforter, soother, sympathizer |反义词：tormentor, abuser, antagonist
