masculinization: /ˌmæskjʊlɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 男性化；男子气概化 | The process of becoming or making something more masculine.  This can refer to physical changes, behavioral changes, or social roles.  It often involves the development or enhancement of traits traditionally associated with men. |


例句：

The masculinization of his features was striking.  | 他面容的男性化特征非常明显。

Hormonal changes can lead to masculinization in women. | 荷尔蒙变化会导致女性男性化。


近义词：virility, manliness, maleness

反义词：feminization
