rabbits:/ˈræbɪts/ | n. |兔子 | Rabbits are small mammals with long ears and a fluffy tail.  兔子是长着长耳朵和蓬松尾巴的小型哺乳动物。|  近义词：hares, bunnies | 反义词：None | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Rabbits eat carrots. = 兔子吃胡萝卜。
Many rabbits live in burrows. = 许多兔子住在洞穴里。

一般过去时:
The rabbit hopped away quickly. = 兔子迅速跳走了。
We saw three rabbits in the field yesterday. = 昨天我们在田里看见了三只兔子。

现在完成时:
I have seen many rabbits in my garden this year. = 今年我在我的花园里见过许多兔子。
The farmer has caught several rabbits. = 农民已经抓了几只兔子。

过去完成时:
The dog had chased the rabbit before I arrived. = 我到达之前，狗已经追过兔子了。
The hunter had already shot the rabbit before it ran into the bushes. = 兔子跑到灌木丛里之前，猎人已经射杀了它。

将来时:
I will feed the rabbits tomorrow. = 我明天将喂兔子。
We will go rabbit hunting next weekend. = 我们下周末将去打猎兔子。

虚拟语气:
If I were a rabbit, I would live in a burrow. = 如果我是兔子，我会住在洞穴里。
I wish I had a pet rabbit. = 我希望我有一只宠物兔。

被动语态:
The rabbits were chased by a fox. = 兔子被狐狸追赶。
The carrots were eaten by the rabbits. = 胡萝卜被兔子吃掉了。

疑问句:
Are those rabbits wild or domestic? = 那些兔子是野生的还是家养的？
How many rabbits do you have? = 你有多少只兔子？

否定句:

I don't have any rabbits. = 我没有兔子。
