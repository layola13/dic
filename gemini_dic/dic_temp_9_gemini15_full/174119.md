quayful: /ˈkeɪfʊl/ | n. | 码头装满的货物；码头装载量 |  A quayful refers to the amount of goods that can be loaded or unloaded at a quay (a wharf or dock).  It is a relatively uncommon word, used primarily in contexts relating to shipping and trade. |  The quayful of oranges was destined for markets across the country.  码头装满的橙子将运往全国各地的市场。  The ship unloaded its quayful of timber. 这艘船卸下了码头装满的木材。 | 近义词：cargo, load, shipment | 反义词：None specifically;  antonyms would relate to the absence of goods, such as emptiness or void. | 名词
