preantiquity:/ˌpriːænˈtɪkwəti/| adj.|史前时代前的；远古以前的|  Relating to the period before antiquity, typically referring to a very remote past beyond the reach of historical records.  It emphasizes a time even earlier than the conventionally defined period of antiquity (roughly the Bronze Age and Classical Antiquity).|例句：The discovery of these tools suggests a preantiquity human presence in the region.  这些工具的发现表明该地区在史前时代前就有人类活动。
