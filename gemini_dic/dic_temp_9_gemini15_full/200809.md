slippery:/ˈslɪpəri/| adj.|滑的，湿滑的；狡猾的，不可靠的|adj.  Difficult to hold or walk on because it is wet or smooth;  characterized by a lack of firm footing. Also, describes someone who is not trustworthy or reliable.|例句：The road was slippery after the rain. 雨后路面很滑。The businessman had a slippery reputation. 那个商人名声不好。|近义词：slippery, smooth, slimy, treacherous, unreliable |反义词：grippy, secure, trustworthy, reliable
