eliciting:/ɪˈlɪsɪtɪŋ/| v.|引出，探出，诱导|To evoke or draw out (a response, answer, or fact) from someone.  The process of eliciting information often involves careful questioning or prompting.|例句：The teacher used a series of questions to elicit the students' understanding of the concept. 老师用一系列问题来引出学生对这个概念的理解。

例句：The psychologist used various techniques to elicit the patient's hidden memories. 心理学家使用各种技术来引出病人的隐藏记忆。


近义词：evoke, extract, draw out, obtain, bring out|反义词：suppress, conceal, hide, repress|动词
