prepartnership: /ˌpriːˈpɑːrtnərʃɪp/ | n. | 合伙前期；合伙前的阶段 |  The period of time before a partnership is officially formed.  It often involves discussions, negotiations, and planning related to the partnership agreement and the business venture. | Example: The prepartnership phase involved extensive due diligence and legal review.  合伙前期包括了广泛的尽职调查和法律审查。


The prepartnership negotiations were protracted and complex.  合伙前的谈判冗长而复杂。


近义词: preliminary partnership stage, pre-formation phase


反义词: post-partnership, partnership operation
