fertilised:/ˈfɜːrtɪlaɪzd/| v. |受精的；施肥的| To make (an egg, ovum, or female reproductive cell) capable of developing into a new organism by combining it with a male reproductive cell, or to enrich (soil) with nutritive materials.|例句：The egg was fertilised successfully.  这枚卵子成功受精了。|例句：The farmer fertilised the field with manure. 农民用肥料给田地施肥。|近义词：impregnated, inseminated, fertilized|反义词：sterilised, barren
