rantings:/ˈræntɪŋz/| n.| 狂言；胡言乱语；唠叨 |  The incoherent and often angry expressions of opinions or complaints.  It often implies a lack of rationality or control. |例句：His rantings about the government were completely unfounded.  他的关于政府的咆哮完全没有根据。

例句：Ignoring the rantings of the angry mob, the police proceeded with their duty. 忽略愤怒暴民的叫嚣，警察继续执行他们的职责。


近义词：ramblings, ravings, diatribes, fulminations, tirades

反义词：reasoned arguments, calm discourse, logical explanations


词性:名词
