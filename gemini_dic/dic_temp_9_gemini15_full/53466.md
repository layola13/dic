disarmature: /ˌdɪsˈɑːrmətʃər/ | n. | 解除武装；拆卸电枢 | The process of removing the armature from a machine, or the state of being unarmed.  | Example: The disarmature of the weapon was necessary for safety. 解除武器的武装对于安全来说是必要的。 | 近义词：disarmament, demilitarization | 反义词：armament, arming


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mechanic disarms the bomb. = 机械师拆除了炸弹。
They disarm the suspect. = 他们解除嫌疑人的武装。


一般过去时:
The soldiers disarmed the enemy. = 士兵们缴了敌人的械。
The technician disarmed the device. = 技术员拆除了该装置。


现在完成时:
The country has disarmed its nuclear weapons. = 这个国家已经销毁了它的核武器。
The police have disarmed the criminal. = 警察已经缴了罪犯的械。


过去完成时:
The rebels had disarmed before the negotiations. = 叛军在谈判前已经缴械投降了。
The team had disarmed the explosive before it detonated. = 团队在爆炸物爆炸前已经将其拆除了。


将来时:
