crochets:/krəˈʃeɪz/ | n. |钩针编织物；钩针编织工艺|The art of creating fabric from yarn using a hooked needle.  Crochets are often used to make clothing, blankets, and other items.|例句：She showed me the beautiful crochets she had made. 她给我看她做的漂亮钩针编织物。|例句：The shop sells a wide variety of crochets, from scarves to sweaters. 这家商店出售各种各样的钩针编织品，从围巾到毛衣。|近义词：knitting, needlework|反义词：machine knitting
