cabining: /ˈkæbɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 限制；禁锢；困住 |  The act of confining or restricting; the state of being confined or restricted.  It often implies a feeling of being cramped or limited. | 例句：The small cabin felt like cabining his spirit. 小木屋让他感觉精神受限。

The constant pressure of deadlines was cabining his creativity.  不断逼近的截止日期限制了他的创造力。| 近义词：confining, restricting, cramping, imprisoning | 反义词：liberating, freeing, unconstraining | 动词
