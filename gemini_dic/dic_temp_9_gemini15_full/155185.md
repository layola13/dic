pewit:/ˈpiːwɪt/| n. |鸻鹬；一种小型涉禽|A small wading bird, typically with a distinctive cry.  The term often refers to several species of plovers or lapwings.|例句：The pewit’s distinctive call echoed across the marsh.  沼泽地上回荡着鸻鹬独特的叫声。|例句：We saw several pe-wits foraging in the mudflats. 我们看到几只鸻鹬在泥滩上觅食。|近义词：plover, lapwing|反义词：无|名词
