irked:/ɜːrkt/ | vt. |使恼怒；使烦躁 |To annoy or irritate someone.  |例句：His constant complaining irked me. 他不断的抱怨让我很烦。|例句：The noise irked her throughout the meeting.  会议期间的噪音让她很烦躁。|近义词：annoy, irritate, bother, exasperate |反义词：please, soothe, delight


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His constant chatter irks me. = 他没完没了的唠叨让我烦。
The traffic noise irks the neighbours. = 交通噪音让邻居们很烦。

一般过去时:
The delay irked the passengers. = 延误让乘客们很恼火。
Her rudeness irked him greatly. = 她的粗鲁让他非常生气。

现在完成时:
His behaviour has irked me for years. = 他的行为多年来一直让我很烦。
The constant interruptions have irked the speaker. = 持续不断的打断让演讲者很烦躁。

过去完成时:
The long wait had irked the audience before the show began. = 演出开始前漫长的等待已经让观众们感到不耐烦了。
I had been irked by his attitude for a long time before I finally confronted him. = 在我最终和他对质之前，他的态度已经让我烦扰很久了。

将来时:
The new rules will irk many employees. = 新规会让许多员工不满。
I know this extra work will irk you, but it needs doing. = 我知道这额外的工作会让你不爽，但它必须完成。

虚拟语气:
If only he wouldn't interrupt, it wouldn't irk me so much. = 要是他别打断，我就不会这么烦了。
I wish that his constant complaining wouldn't irk me so much. = 我希望他不断的抱怨不会让我这么烦。

被动语态:
Many people were irked by the government's decision. = 许多人对政府的决定感到不满。
He was irked by the disrespectful comments. = 他被那些不尊重的评论惹恼了。

疑问句:
Does his attitude irk you? = 他的态度让你生气吗？
Does the repetitive nature of the work irk you? = 这工作的重复性让你感到厌烦吗？

否定句:
The news didn't irk him at all. = 这消息一点也没让他生气。
The quiet atmosphere didn't irk her. = 安静的环境并没有让她感到烦躁。
