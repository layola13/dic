netless:/ˈnɛtlɪs/| adj. |无网的，无网络的 | Without a net; lacking a network.  The term often refers to a lack of internet access or a network connection. |例句：The remote island had a netless existence, cut off from the outside world.  偏远的岛屿与世隔绝，过着与网络无关的生活。

例句：His netless computer couldn't access the company's intranet. 他的电脑无法连接公司内网。


近义词： unconnected, offline, off-grid

反义词： networked, online, connected

词性：形容词
