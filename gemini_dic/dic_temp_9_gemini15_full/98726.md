insurgent:/ˈɪnsɜːrdʒənt/| n. adj.|叛乱者；反抗者；造反的；起义的|A person who rises in opposition to authority; a rebel.  An insurgent movement or group is one that fights against the established government or authority. |例句：The insurgents launched a surprise attack on the capital.  叛乱分子对首都发动了突然袭击。

例句：The government is struggling to suppress the insurgent forces. 政府正在努力压制叛乱武装。


近义词：rebel, revolutionary, mutineer, dissident|反义词：loyalist, government supporter, conformist|词性：名词，形容词
