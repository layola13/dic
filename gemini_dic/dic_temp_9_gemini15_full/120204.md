mineralize:/ˈmɪnərəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使矿化；使成矿物；使钙化 | To convert (organic matter) into a mineral substance or to become mineralized.  The process by which minerals are formed in a rock or sediment. |例句：The bones mineralize as the child grows. = 孩子长大后骨骼会矿化。

The process of mineralizing the waste water helped reduce environmental pollution. = 矿化污水有助于减少环境污染。

近义词：calcify, petrify | 反义词：demineralize
