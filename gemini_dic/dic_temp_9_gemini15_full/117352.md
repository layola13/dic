medially:/ˈmiːdiəli/| adj.|中间地；正中地；内侧地|Situated in the middle or center; toward the middle or center.|The artery was damaged medially.|近义词：centrally, centrally located, middling|反义词：laterally, peripherally

例句：

The tumor was located medially in the brain.  肿瘤位于大脑中间。

The incision was made medially along the thigh. 切口沿着大腿内侧进行。


词源:

From Medieval Latin *medialiter, from Latin medius ("middle").  源于中古拉丁语 *medialiter，来自拉丁语 medius（“中间”）。
