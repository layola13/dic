overbroaden:/ˌoʊvərˈbrɔːdn/| vt.|过度扩大；过分延伸|To make something broader or wider than is necessary or appropriate; to extend the scope or meaning of something excessively.|例句：The company's marketing campaign overbroadened its target audience, leading to wasted resources. 公司的营销活动目标受众过于广泛，导致资源浪费。例句：The judge criticized the lawyer for overbroadening the definition of the crime.  法官批评律师对罪行的定义过于宽泛。|近义词：extend excessively, exaggerate, amplify, generalize excessively|反义词：narrow, restrict, limit, specify

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overbroaden their research scope. = 他们过度扩大了他们的研究范围。
The company overbroadens its marketing strategy. = 公司过度扩展了它的营销策略。

一般过去时:
He overbroadened his interpretation of the data. = 他对数据的解释过于宽泛。
The government overbroadened the scope of the new law. = 政府过度扩大了新法律的范围。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

The committee had overbroadened its authority before the chairman intervened. = 在主席干预之前，委员会已经滥用了权力。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
