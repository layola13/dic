USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".  It describes how an action is performed, not an action itself.  The following examples illustrate its use in different sentence structures:

肯定句:
He spoke noncreditably about his achievements. 他不可信地谈论了他的成就。
She answered the question noncreditably. 她不可信地回答了这个问题。

否定句:
He did not speak noncreditably; his evidence was compelling. 他并没有不可信地说话；他的证据很有说服力。
The report was not noncreditably written; it was meticulously researched. 这份报告写得并非不可信；它是经过细致研究的。

疑问句:
Did he testify noncreditably?  他的证词不可信吗？
Was her explanation noncreditably given? 她的解释不可信吗？
