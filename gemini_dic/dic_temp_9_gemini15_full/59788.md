ecotourism: /ˈekoʊˌtʊərɪzəm/ | n. | 生态旅游 |  Environmentally responsible travel to natural areas, conserving the environment, and improving the well-being of local people.  Ecotourism focuses on minimizing the negative impact of tourism on the environment and local communities while maximizing the positive benefits. | Example:  We went on an ecotourism trip to the Amazon rainforest, staying in eco-lodges and learning about sustainable practices. 我们去亚马逊雨林进行了一次生态旅游，住在生态旅馆里，学习可持续发展实践。


Example: Many countries are promoting ecotourism to boost their economies while protecting their natural resources. 许多国家正在推广生态旅游，以促进经济发展，同时保护自然资源。


近义词：sustainable tourism, responsible travel, green tourism


反义词：mass tourism, irresponsible tourism, destructive tourism
