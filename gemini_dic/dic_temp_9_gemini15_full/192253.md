screeching:/ˈskriːtʃɪŋ/| adj. n. v.|尖锐刺耳的叫声；刺耳的摩擦声；发出尖锐刺耳的声音|Making a loud, harsh, high-pitched sound.|The brakes screeched as the car came to a sudden stop.  刹车发出尖锐刺耳的声音，汽车突然停了下来。The bird's screeching woke me up. 鸟的尖锐叫声把我吵醒了。|shrieking, squawking, grating|silent, soft, gentle|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tires screech on the wet road. 轮胎在湿滑的道路上发出刺耳的摩擦声。
The birds screech loudly in the morning. 鸟儿早上大声尖叫。


一般过去时:
The train screeched to a halt. 火车嘎吱一声停了下来。
The rusty door screeched open. 生锈的门发出刺耳的声音打开了。


现在完成时:
The children have been screeching all afternoon. 孩子们整个下午都在尖叫。
The brakes have screeched several times today. 刹车今天已经尖叫了几次。


过去完成时:
The car had screeched before it crashed. 汽车在撞毁之前发出了刺耳的声音。
The owl had screeched all night. 猫头鹰整夜都在尖叫。


将来时:
The tires will screech when I brake hard. 当我猛踩刹车时，轮胎会发出尖锐的摩擦声。
The audience will screech with excitement. 观众会兴奋地尖叫。


虚拟语气:
If the brakes screeched, I would know something was wrong. 如果刹车发出尖锐的声音，我就知道出问题了。
I wish the birds wouldn't screech so early. 我希望鸟儿不要这么早叫。


被动语态:
A loud screech was heard from the engine. 从引擎里传来一声尖锐的叫声。
A terrifying screech was made by the animal. 动物发出一声可怕的尖叫。


疑问句:
Did you hear that screech? 你听到那尖锐的声音了吗？
What made that screeching sound?  是什么发出那种刺耳的声音？


否定句:
The car didn't screech when it stopped. 车停下来时没有发出刺耳的声音。
The birds weren't screeching this morning. 鸟儿今天早上没有尖叫。
