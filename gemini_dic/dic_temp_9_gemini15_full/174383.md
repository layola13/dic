quiesce:/kwɪˈɛs/| vi.|  静止；平静；默然；顺从 | to become quiet, still, or inactive; to submit or comply passively |例句：The storm finally quiesced, leaving behind a calm sea.  暴风雨最终平息了，留下平静的海面。
例句：He chose to quiesce rather than argue. 他选择顺从而不是争论。|近义词：calm down, cease, subside, submit |反义词：agitate, excite, rebel, resist |动词
