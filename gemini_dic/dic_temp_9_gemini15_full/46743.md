dandruff: /ˈdændrʌf/ | n. |头皮屑|A condition of the scalp that is characterized by the excessive shedding of dead skin cells, appearing as small, white flakes.|Example: He suffers from severe dandruff and needs to use a medicated shampoo.|近义词:scurf|反义词:None.
