creative:/krɪˈeɪtɪv/ | adj. | 有创造力的；创造性的 |  relating to or involving the imagination or original ideas, especially in the production of an artistic work.  |  She's a very creative writer. 她是一位很有创造力的作家。  |  imaginative, inventive, original | uncreative, unimaginative | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is creative in his approach to problem-solving. = 他解决问题的方法很有创造力。
The artist is creative in her use of color. =这位艺术家在色彩运用方面很有创造力。

一般过去时:
She was creative when she designed the new logo. =她设计新标志时很有创造力。
The team was creative during the brainstorming session. =团队在头脑风暴会议期间很有创造力。

现在完成时:
They have been creative with their marketing campaigns. = 他们的市场营销活动很有创意。
The company has been creative in its product development. =该公司在产品开发方面很有创意。

过去完成时:
He had been creative in his previous role. = 他在他之前的角色中很有创造力。
The writer had been creative in her previous novels. =这位作家在她之前的几部小说中很有创造力。

将来时:
She will be creative in her next project. =她将在下一个项目中发挥创造力。
The designers will be creative with the new website design. =设计师们将在新的网站设计中发挥创造力。

虚拟语气:
If I were more creative, I would design my own clothes. =如果我更有创造力，我会自己设计衣服。
I wish I were more creative in my writing. =我希望我的写作更有创造力。

被动语态:
The design was creatively executed. =该设计得到了富有创意的执行。
The problem was solved creatively. =这个问题得到了富有创造力的解决。

疑问句:

否定句:
