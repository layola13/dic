reinstruct:/ˌriːɪnˈstrʌkt/| vt.|重新指导；重新指示；重新教授|To instruct again or differently; to give new or revised instructions to.|例句：The teacher had to reinstruct the students after they misunderstood the initial directions.  老师不得不重新指导学生，因为他们误解了最初的指示。

例句：The manager reinstructed the team on the new safety procedures.  经理就新的安全程序重新指导了团队。


近义词：re-teach, re-educate, retrain, re-explain|反义词：misdirect, confuse|动词


USAGE->

The teacher reinstructs the students every week. = 老师每周都重新指导学生。
He reinstructs his employees on the new policy. = 他就新政策重新指导他的员工。
