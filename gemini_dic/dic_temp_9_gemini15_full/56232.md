doff:/dɒf/| vt.|脱掉（衣帽等）|To take off (something, especially a hat or piece of clothing).  |He doffed his hat to the lady. 他向那位女士脱帽致意。|近义词：remove, take off, shed|反义词：don


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He doffs his hat to the queen. = 他向女王脱帽致敬。
She doffs her gloves before entering the building. = 她在进入大楼前脱下手套。


一般过去时:
He doffed his cap and bowed. = 他脱下帽子鞠躬。
She doffed her coat and sat down. = 她脱下外套坐了下来。


现在完成时:
He has doffed his disguise. = 他已经脱掉了伪装。
She has doffed her winter clothes. = 她已经脱掉了冬衣。


过去完成时:
He had doffed his uniform before entering the party. = 他在参加聚会前已经脱掉了制服。
She had doffed her jewels before going to bed. = 她睡觉前已经脱掉了珠宝。


将来时:
He will doff his mask at the end of the play. = 他将在剧终时摘下面具。
She will doff her apron after finishing cooking. = 她做完饭后会脱下围裙。


虚拟语气:
If he were to doff his armor, he would be vulnerable. = 如果他脱掉盔甲，他就会变得脆弱。
I wish I could doff these worries. = 我希望我能摆脱这些担忧。


被动语态:
His hat was doffed in respect. = 他的帽子被脱下以示敬意。
The disguise was doffed quickly and secretly. = 伪装被迅速而秘密地脱掉。


疑问句:
Will he doff his hat to the judge? = 他会向法官脱帽吗？
Does she usually doff her shoes before entering the house? = 她通常进屋前会脱鞋吗？


否定句:
He did not doff his hat to the stranger. = 他没有向陌生人脱帽。
She will not doff her mask until the game is over. = 在游戏结束之前，她不会摘下面具。


PHRASE->
doff one's hat to someone = 向某人脱帽致敬

doff one's armour = 脱掉盔甲
doff one's responsibilities = 摆脱责任
