defiant:/dɪˈfaɪənt/| adj.| 违抗的；反抗的；挑衅的 |Showing a bold resistance to authority or opposition.  A defiant attitude or action shows a refusal to obey or be controlled. |例句：He gave a defiant stare at the police officer. 他向警官投去了挑衅的目光。

例句：Her defiant refusal to cooperate angered the judge. 她拒不合作的对抗态度激怒了法官。


近义词：rebellious, insubordinate, resistant, challenging

反义词：obedient, submissive, compliant, docile

词性：形容词
