hanging-around:/ˌhæŋɪŋ əˈraʊnd/ | v. |闲逛，游荡 | To spend time idly; to loiter.  It often implies a lack of purpose or direction. |例句：They were just hanging around the mall, not really buying anything.  他们在商场里闲逛，并没有真正买东西。

例句：I spent the afternoon hanging around with my friends. 我下午和朋友们一起闲逛。


近义词：loitering, loafing, idling, dawdling | 反义词：working, hustling, bustling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are hanging around the park every afternoon. = 他们每天下午都在公园里闲逛。
We hang around the cafe after school. = 我们放学后在咖啡馆闲逛。


一般过去时:
He hung around the library for hours. = 他在图书馆闲逛了好几个小时。
They hung around the city center all day. = 他们整天在市中心闲逛。


现在完成时:
I have been hanging around here all morning. = 我整个上午都在这里闲逛。
They have hung around waiting for the bus for an hour. = 他们已经等公交车等了一个小时了。


过去完成时:
We had been hanging around for a while before she finally arrived. = 她最终到达之前，我们已经闲逛了一段时间了。
They had hung around the corner, waiting for their opportunity. = 他们在拐角处闲逛，等待机会。


将来时:
We will be hanging around the beach tomorrow. = 我们明天将在海滩上闲逛。
They will hang around the town square after the parade. = 游行结束后，他们将在城镇广场闲逛。


虚拟语气:
If I had nothing better to do, I would be hanging around. = 如果我没有更好的事做，我会去闲逛。
I wish I could be hanging around with my friends right now. = 我真希望现在能和朋友们一起闲逛。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)


疑问句:
Are they hanging around again? = 他们又在闲逛了吗？
What are you doing, just hanging around? = 你在干什么，只是闲逛吗？


否定句:
I wasn't hanging around; I was waiting for the bus. = 我不是在闲逛；我在等公共汽车。
They didn't hang around long; they left quickly. = 他们没有闲逛很久；他们很快就离开了。


PHRASE->
hang around with friends = 和朋友们一起闲逛
hang around the town = 在镇上闲逛
hang around the corner = 在拐角处闲逛
hang around all day = 整天闲逛
hang around waiting = 闲逛等待
