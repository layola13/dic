drawlink:/ˈdrɔːlɪŋk/| n.|牵引装置，拉索|A drawlink is a mechanical device used to connect or disconnect components, often under tension or load. It's typically a pin or bar that can be inserted or removed to join two parts.  |例句：The drawlink on the trailer failed, causing the load to detach.  拖车的牵引装置失效，导致货物脱落。|近义词：coupling, connector, linkage |反义词：disconnector, separator.
