nonbeneficently:/ˌnɒnbenˈefɪsntli/ | adv. | 不利地，有害地 | Acting in a way that does not benefit or is harmful.  | The doctor acted nonbeneficently by prescribing medication with known harmful side effects.  医生开了有已知有害副作用的药，其行为是不利的。| beneficently, helpfully | malevolently, harmfully | 副词


USAGE->
