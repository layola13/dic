decryption:/dɪˈkrɪpʃən/ | n. | 解密；译解 | The process of converting encrypted data back into its original, readable form.  It involves reversing the encryption process to reveal the original message or information. | Example: The decryption of the intercepted message revealed the enemy's plans.  解密截获的消息揭示了敌人的计划。 |近义词：decoding, decipherment | 反义词：encryption
