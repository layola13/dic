scrutinize:/ˈskruːtənaɪz/ | vt. |仔细检查；细察；审查 |To examine or inspect closely and thoroughly. |The police scrutinized his passport carefully. 警方仔细检查了他的护照。|examine, inspect, investigate | overlook, ignore |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They scrutinize every detail of the contract. = 他们仔细审查合同的每一个细节。
The professor scrutinizes the students' essays. = 教授仔细检查学生的论文。

一般过去时:
She scrutinized the painting for any signs of damage. = 她仔细检查画作是否有任何损坏迹象。
The detective scrutinized the crime scene for clues. = 侦探仔细检查犯罪现场寻找线索。

现在完成时:
The auditors have scrutinized the company's financial records. = 审计员已经仔细审查了公司的财务记录。
He has scrutinized the evidence and found no inconsistencies. = 他仔细审查了证据，没有发现任何不一致之处。

过去完成时:
They had scrutinized the map before setting out on their journey. = 他们在出发旅行之前仔细研究过地图。
The judge had scrutinized all the witness testimonies before making a decision. = 法官在做出判决之前仔细审查了所有证人的证词。

将来时:
The committee will scrutinize the proposal before making a decision. = 委员会将在做出决定之前仔细审查该提案。
She will scrutinize the data to ensure its accuracy. = 她将仔细审查数据以确保其准确性。

虚拟语气:
If they had scrutinized the documents more carefully, they would have found the mistake. = 如果他们更仔细地审查文件，他们就会发现错误。
I wish I had scrutinized his resume more thoroughly before hiring him. = 我希望在雇用他之前更彻底地审查了他的简历。

被动语态:
The documents were scrutinized by the committee. = 文件由委员会仔细审查。

疑问句:

否定句:
