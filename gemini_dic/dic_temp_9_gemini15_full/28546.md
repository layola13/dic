cardiography:/ˌkɑːdiˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. |心电图描记术，心动描记术 |The process of recording the electrical activity of the heart.  It involves attaching electrodes to the skin to detect and record the heart's electrical signals.|例句：The cardiography results showed an irregular heartbeat. 心电图结果显示心跳不规则。|例句：The doctor ordered a cardiography to assess the patient's heart condition. 医生为了评估病人的心脏状况，开了心电图检查。|近义词：electrocardiography (ECG)|反义词：无
