crunchiest:/ˈkrʌntʃiɪst/ | adj. | 最脆的，最硬的 | Most crisp or brittle.  It is the superlative form of "crunchy".  Describes something that makes a crunching sound when bitten or crushed. | Example: "These potato chips are the crunchiest I've ever tasted."  这些薯片是我吃过的最脆的。 |近义词：crispest, crispest, most brittle | 反义词：softest, flabbiest, mushiest | 词性：形容词
