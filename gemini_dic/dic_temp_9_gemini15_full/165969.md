prepares:/prɪˈpɛərz/ | v. |准备，预备 | To make something ready for use or action; to get oneself ready. |例句：She prepares her lessons carefully. 她认真准备她的课程。

例句：He prepares breakfast every morning. 他每天早上准备早餐。


近义词： gets ready, arranges, makes ready | 反义词： neglects, ignores, delays


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prepares his lunch every day. = 他每天都准备午餐。
She prepares the documents for the meeting. = 她为会议准备文件。


一般过去时:
They prepared a delicious meal for the guests. = 他们为客人准备了一顿美味的饭菜。
He prepared himself for the marathon. = 他为马拉松做好了准备。


现在完成时:
She has prepared everything for the party. = 她已经为派对准备好了所有东西。
They have prepared the report for submission. = 他们已经准备好报告提交了。


过去完成时:
He had prepared a speech before the conference. = 他在会议之前已经准备好了演讲。
She had prepared the ingredients before starting to cook. =  她在开始烹饪之前已经准备好了食材。


将来时:
I will prepare the presentation tomorrow. = 我明天将准备演示文稿。
We will prepare the room for the meeting. = 我们将准备房间开会。


虚拟语气:
If I had prepared better, I would have done better on the test. = 如果我准备得更好，考试成绩就会更好。
I wish I had prepared more for the interview. = 我真希望我为面试做了更多准备。


被动语态:
The documents were prepared by the secretary. = 文件是由秘书准备的。
The food was prepared carefully by the chef. = 这食物是由厨师精心准备的。


疑问句:
Are you prepared for the exam? = 你准备好考试了吗？
Has she prepared the presentation yet? = 她准备好演示文稿了吗？


否定句:
I didn't prepare for the quiz. = 我没有准备测验。
They haven't prepared the budget yet. = 他们还没有准备好预算。


PHRASE->
prepare for = 为...做准备
prepare to = 准备...
prepare a meal = 准备一顿饭
prepare a speech = 准备一场演讲
prepare oneself =  做好准备
