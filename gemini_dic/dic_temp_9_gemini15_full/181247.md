reinstalled:/ˌriːɪnˈstɔːld/| v.|重新安装|To install something again.  This typically refers to software, hardware, or applications that were previously removed or uninstalled.|例句：I had to reinstall the operating system after the virus attack.  病毒攻击后我不得不重新安装操作系统。

例句：The IT department reinstalled the printer drivers for all employees. IT部门为所有员工重新安装了打印机驱动程序。


近义词：re-establish, reinstate, put back


反义词：uninstall, remove


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reinstalls software regularly. = 他定期重新安装软件。
She reinstalls the application after every update. = 她每次更新后都会重新安装应用程序。


一般过去时:
I reinstalled the game after deleting it accidentally. = 我不小心删除游戏后又重新安装了。
They reinstalled the windows system yesterday. = 他们昨天重新安装了Windows系统。


现在完成时:
I have reinstalled the program three times today. = 我今天已经重新安装了这个程序三次。
She has reinstalled the operating system successfully. = 她已经成功重新安装了操作系统。


过去完成时:
I had reinstalled the drivers before the technician arrived. = 技术人员到达之前我已经重新安装了驱动程序。
They had reinstalled the application several times before finding the solution. = 他们在找到解决方案之前已经重新安装了应用程序好几次。


将来时:
I will reinstall the software tomorrow. = 我明天会重新安装软件。
He will reinstall the operating system after the backup is complete. = 备份完成后，他会重新安装操作系统。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would reinstall the entire system. = 如果我有时间，我会重新安装整个系统。
I wish I had reinstalled the backup before the crash. = 我真希望在崩溃之前重新安装了备份。


被动语态:
The software was reinstalled by the technician. = 该软件由技术人员重新安装。

疑问句:

否定句:
