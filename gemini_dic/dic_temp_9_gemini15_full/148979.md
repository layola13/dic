oviposition:/ˌoʊvɪˈpɒzɪʃən/| n. |产卵；卵的放置|The act of laying eggs, especially by an insect or other invertebrate.  It encompasses the process of selecting a suitable site, attaching the eggs, and ensuring their survival.|Example: The butterflies completed their oviposition on the underside of the leaves. 蝴蝶在叶子的底部完成了产卵。The study focused on the oviposition behavior of the species. 这项研究的重点是该物种的产卵行为。|近义词：egg-laying, spawning (for fish)|反义词：None (antonym is not directly applicable)|名词
