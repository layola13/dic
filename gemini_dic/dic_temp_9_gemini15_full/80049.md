goal:/ɡoʊl/ /ɡəʊl/ | n. |目标；球门；得分 |A goal is something that you are trying to achieve.  It can be a specific aim, a long-term objective, or a point scored in a game. |例句：My goal is to graduate from university next year. 我的目标是明年大学毕业。

例句：He scored a goal in the final minutes of the game.  他在比赛的最后几分钟进了一个球。|近义词：aim, objective, target |反义词：failure, setback


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My goal is to become a doctor. = 我的目标是成为一名医生。
What is your main goal this year? = 你今年的主要目标是什么？


一般过去时:
Her goal was to win the race. = 她的目标是赢得比赛。
What was your goal in that project? = 你在那个项目中的目标是什么？


现在完成时:
We have achieved our goal. = 我们已经实现了我们的目标。
She has not yet reached her goal. = 她还没有实现她的目标。


过去完成时:
I had already achieved my goal before the deadline. = 我在截止日期前就已经实现了我的目标。
They had set their goal before they started the project. = 他们开始项目之前就已经设定了目标。


将来时:
I will achieve my goal one day. = 我总有一天会实现我的目标。
What will your goal be next year? = 你明年的目标是什么？


虚拟语气:
If I had worked harder, I would have achieved my goal. = 如果我更努力工作，我就会实现我的目标。
I wish I had set a clearer goal. = 我希望我设定了一个更明确的目标。


被动语态:
The goal was easily achieved. = 这个目标很容易实现。
The goal is being pursued vigorously. = 这个目标正在积极地追求。


疑问句:
What is your goal? = 你的目标是什么？
What are the goals of this meeting? = 此次会议的目标是什么？


否定句:
That is not my goal. = 那不是我的目标。
He did not achieve his goal. = 他没有实现他的目标。


PHRASE->
set a goal = 设定目标
reach a goal = 达成目标
achieve a goal = 实现目标
work towards a goal = 为目标而努力
long-term goal = 长期目标
