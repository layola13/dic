containers:/kənˈteɪnərz/ | n. |容器，集装箱 | A container is something that holds or contains something else.  Containers can be made of various materials, such as metal, plastic, glass, or cardboard. They are used for storage, transportation, and packaging. | Example:  The shipping containers were stacked high on the dock.  这些集装箱高高地堆放在码头上。  The refrigerator contains many different foods. 这台冰箱里装着许多不同的食物。|近义词：receptacles, vessels, holders |反义词：contents (反义词是其内容物，并非直接反义) |名词
