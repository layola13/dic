oversaturate: /ˌoʊvərˈsætʃəreɪt/ | vt. | 使过饱和；使过度饱和 | To saturate to excess; to fill or supply with more than can be absorbed or contained.  |  例句：The market has been oversaturated with cheap smartphones. 市场已经被廉价智能手机充斥。

例句：They oversaturated the soil with fertilizer, damaging the plants.  他们用过量的肥料使土壤过饱和，损害了植物。


近义词：flood, inundate, deluge


反义词：undersaturate, deplete


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company oversaturates the market with its new product. = 公司用其新产品使市场饱和。
He oversaturates his paintings with color. = 他用过多的颜色使他的画过饱和。


一般过去时:
The heavy rain oversaturated the ground. = 大雨使地面过饱和。
The market was oversaturated last year. = 去年市场已经饱和。


现在完成时:
The advertising campaign has oversaturated the media. = 广告宣传活动已经使媒体过饱和。
They have oversaturated the consumer market with similar products. = 他们已经用类似的产品使消费者市场过饱和。


过去完成时:
The soil had been oversaturated before the planting. = 在种植之前，土壤就已经过饱和了。
The market had been oversaturated by the time they launched their product. = 当他们推出产品时，市场已经饱和了。


将来时:
The new technology will oversaturate the current communication systems. = 新技术将使当前的通信系统过饱和。
They predict that the market will be oversaturated next year. = 他们预测明年市场将饱和。


被动语态:
The market was oversaturated by the influx of cheap imports. = 市场因大量廉价进口商品涌入而饱和。
The soil is being oversaturated by the continuous rainfall. = 土壤因持续降雨而变得过饱和。


疑问句:
Has the market been oversaturated by the new product? = 新产品是否使市场饱和？
Will this new strategy oversaturate the market? = 这个新策略会使市场饱和吗？


否定句:
The company did not oversaturate the market with its product. = 公司的产品并没有使市场饱和。
The campaign did not oversaturate the audience with information. = 这场活动并没有用信息使观众过饱和。


PHRASE->
oversaturate the market = 使市场饱和
oversaturate with information = 信息过载
oversaturate the soil = 使土壤过饱和
oversaturate the media = 使媒体过饱和
oversaturate the demand = 使需求过饱和


词源:
over- (表示“过度”) + saturate (使饱和)  The word "saturate" comes from the Latin word "saturare," meaning "to fill," "to stuff," or "to satisfy."
