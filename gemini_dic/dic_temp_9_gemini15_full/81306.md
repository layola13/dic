granulous: /ˈɡrænjʊləs/ | adj. | 粒状的，具颗粒的 | Having a granular structure or appearance; consisting of or resembling grains.  The rock sample exhibited a coarse, granulous texture.  |近义词： grainy, gritty, particulate | 反义词：smooth, seamless


例句:

The sugar had a fine, granulous texture. =  糖的质地细腻，呈颗粒状。

The photo was slightly granulous due to low resolution. = 由于分辨率低，照片略显颗粒状。


词源:

From Latin *granulosus, from granulum ("small grain"), diminutive of granum ("grain").  The suffix "-ous" indicates possessing the quality of.
