hesperian:/hɛˈspɪəriən/| adj.|西方的，晚间的，傍晚的|Relating to Hesperus, the evening star (Venus); hence, western, evening.  The term often evokes a sense of romance, beauty, and the golden age.|例句：The painting depicted a Hesperian landscape, bathed in the golden light of the setting sun. 这幅画描绘了沐浴在夕阳金光中的西方风景。

例句：The poet described the Hesperian breezes carrying the scent of orange blossoms. 诗人描写了带着橙花香味的西方微风。


近义词：western, evening, occidental|反义词：eastern, oriental, matutinal


词性:形容词
