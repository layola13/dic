dyspnea:/ˈdɪspniə/| n. |呼吸困难，气促|Shortness of breath; difficulty or labored breathing.|例句：The patient complained of dyspnea and chest pain.  病人抱怨呼吸困难和胸痛。|例句：  Exercise induced dyspnea is a common symptom in heart failure patients. 运动诱发的呼吸困难是心力衰竭患者的常见症状。|近义词：shortness of breath, breathlessness, labored breathing|反义词：easy breathing

一般现在时:
He experiences dyspnea during strenuous activity. 他在剧烈运动时呼吸困难。
She often has dyspnea when climbing stairs. 她爬楼梯时经常呼吸困难。

虚拟语气:
