liquidy: /ˈlɪkwɪdi/ | adj. | 液态的；流动性的；易变现的 |  Describes something that is like a liquid, or that is easily changed into cash.  It often implies a lack of solidity or firmness. |


例句：

The paint was very liquidy and difficult to apply.  =  油漆非常稀薄，难以涂抹。

The market is currently quite liquidy, making it easy to buy and sell assets. = 目前市场流动性很好，买卖资产很容易。


近义词：fluid, runny, watery, liquid, readily marketable


反义词：solid, thick, viscous, illiquid


词性: 形容词
