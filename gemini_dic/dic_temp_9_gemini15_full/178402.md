reconnoissances:/rɪˈkɒnɪs(ə)nsz/ | n. |侦察；勘察；探测 |The act of reconnoitering; exploration of enemy territory to gain information.  It often involves surveying the terrain, identifying enemy positions, and assessing their strength. Reconnaissance missions are crucial for military planning and strategic decision-making. |例句：The army conducted several reconnaissances before launching their attack.  军队在发动攻击之前进行了多次侦察。

例句：Aerial reconnaissance revealed the enemy's troop movements.  空中侦察揭示了敌军的部队调动。


近义词：exploration, survey, scouting, investigation | 反义词：retreat, withdrawal
