persister:/pərˈsɪstər/| n. |坚持者；执着的人|A persister is someone who continues to do something despite difficulties or opposition.  They show determination and perseverance. |Example: He was a true persister, refusing to give up on his dream even after numerous setbacks. 他是一位真正的坚持者，即使在多次挫折后也拒绝放弃他的梦想。|近义词：perseverer, adherent, proponent |反义词：quitter, deserter
