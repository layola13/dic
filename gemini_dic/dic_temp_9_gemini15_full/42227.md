count:/kaʊnt/| /kaʊnt/| n./v.|计算；计数；数量；伯爵|To determine the total number of; to name one by one;  a total or number; a nobleman.|例句：Please count the apples. 请数一下苹果。|例句：The count of attendees was impressive. 出席人数令人印象深刻。|近义词：number, enumerate, tally, earl|反义词：estimate (in the sense of approximation)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I count my blessings every day. = 我每天都数算我的福气。
They count the money carefully. = 他们仔细地数钱。

一般过去时:
She counted the sheep to fall asleep. = 她数绵羊来入睡。
We counted the votes after the election. = 选举后我们清点选票。

现在完成时:
I have counted to ten. = 我已经数到十了。
They have counted all the books in the library. = 他们已经数清了图书馆所有的书。

过去完成时:
She had counted the money before she left. = 她离开前已经数过钱了。
They had counted the stars before they went to bed. = 他们睡觉前已经数过星星了。

将来时:
I will count the votes tomorrow. = 我明天将清点选票。
We will count the number of participants next week. = 我们下周将统计参与人数。

虚拟语气:
If I counted correctly, there are twenty. = 如果我数得没错，有二十个。
I wish I had counted the cost beforehand. = 我真希望我事先算过成本。

被动语态:
The votes are being counted now. = 选票正在被清点。
The money was counted by the bank teller. = 钱被银行出纳员数过了。

疑问句:
How many people are you counting? = 你要数多少人？
Can you count the number of errors? = 你能数一下错误的数量吗？

否定句:
I cannot count that high. = 我数不到那么高。
He didn't count the cost of the project. = 他没有计算项目的成本。
