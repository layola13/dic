dramedies:/ˈdræmədiz/| n.|戏谑剧，喜剧性悲剧|A dramedy is a genre of film or television that blends elements of drama and comedy.  It often deals with serious or sensitive topics but uses humor to lighten the mood or offer a different perspective.  Dramedies can explore complex characters and relationships while still providing moments of laughter and levity.|Example:  "The movie was a dramedy; it made me laugh and cry at the same time."|"comedy-drama, tragicomedy"|serious drama, pure comedy|名词
