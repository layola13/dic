hogs:/hɒɡz/| n.|猪；(尤指)肥猪；贪婪的人|A hog is a pig, especially a large one raised for meat.  The term can also be used figuratively to describe someone greedy or selfish.|例句：The farmer raises hogs for market. 这个农民养猪卖钱。

例句：He's such a hog; he ate all the cake. 他真是个贪婪的人，把所有的蛋糕都吃光了。|近义词：pig, swine, glutton|反义词：vegetarian, ascetic
