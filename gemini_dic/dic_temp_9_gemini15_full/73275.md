forebear:/ˈfɔːrbeər/ | n. |祖先，祖宗 | A forebear is a person who is an ancestor of someone.  It refers to a person in one's ancestry, typically more distant than a parent or grandparent. | My forebears were farmers for generations. 我的祖先世世代代都是农民。 | ancestor, predecessor | descendant | 名词
