prosperous:/ˈprɒspərəs/| adj. |繁荣的，兴旺的，成功的 | Flourishing and successful.  A prosperous nation or business is rich and successful. |例句：The country experienced a prosperous period after the war. = 战后，这个国家经历了一段繁荣时期。

例句：His business has become incredibly prosperous in recent years. = 近年来，他的生意变得非常兴隆。


近义词：thriving, flourishing, successful, affluent, wealthy | 反义词：poor, impoverished, destitute, failing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is prosperous. = 这家公司很繁荣。
This region is prosperous. = 这个地区很繁荣。


一般过去时:
The city was prosperous in the 18th century. = 这个城市在18世纪很繁荣。
The farm was prosperous before the drought. = 干旱之前，这个农场很兴旺。


现在完成时:
Our business has become prosperous. = 我们的生意已经变得兴隆了。
The nation has been prosperous for many decades. = 这个国家繁荣了几十年。


过去完成时:
The kingdom had been prosperous until the invasion. = 在入侵之前，王国一直很繁荣。
The family had been prosperous for generations. = 这个家族几代人都很富裕。


将来时:
With this new strategy, the company will be prosperous. = 采用这个新战略，公司将会兴旺发达。
We expect the region to become prosperous in the future. = 我们预计该地区将来会繁荣起来。


虚拟语气:
If we worked harder, our business would be prosperous. = 如果我们更努力工作，我们的生意就会兴隆。
I wish my family were prosperous. = 我希望我的家人能过上富裕的生活。


被动语态:
The city is known to be prosperous. = 这个城市以繁荣著称。（被动语态中，prosperous本身不发生变化）
The region was considered prosperous. = 这个地区被认为是繁荣的。（被动语态中，prosperous本身不发生变化）


疑问句:
Is the company prosperous? = 这家公司繁荣吗？
Are these farmers prosperous? = 这些农民富裕吗？


否定句:
The country is not prosperous. = 这个国家不繁荣。
The business is not currently prosperous. = 这家公司目前并不兴隆。



PHRASE->
a prosperous nation = 一个繁荣的国家
a prosperous economy = 一个繁荣的经济
a prosperous future = 一个光明的未来
a prosperous family = 一个富裕的家庭
a prosperous career = 一份成功的事业


词源:
From Old French *prosperer, from Latin prosperare "to succeed, to fare well," from prosperus "successful, fortunate," from pro "forward" + sperare "to hope."
