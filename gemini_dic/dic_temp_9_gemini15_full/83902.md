half-baked:/ˌhɑːfˈbeɪkt/| adj.|半生不熟的；不成熟的；不完善的 |Imperfectly cooked or developed; incomplete or insufficiently developed.|例句：His plan was half-baked and needed more thought.他的计划很不成熟，需要多考虑一下。|例句：The cake is half-baked, we need to put it back in the oven. 蛋糕没烤熟，我们需要把它放回烤箱。|近义词：incomplete, undeveloped, immature, sketchy |反义词：complete, fully developed, mature, thorough
