premiere:/prɪˈmɪər/| n. v.|首映式；首演；首次公演；首发|The first public performance or showing of a film, play, or other work.|例句：The film's premiere was a huge success. 这部电影的首映式非常成功。The play had its premiere in London. 这部戏剧在伦敦首演。|近义词：debut, opening, launch|反义词：re-release, encore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The film premieres next week. = 这部电影下周首映。
The theatre premieres a new play every month. = 剧院每个月都会首演一部新剧。


一般过去时:
The movie premiered last night. = 电影昨晚首映了。
The band premiered their new album at a sold-out concert. = 乐队在爆满的演唱会上首发了他们的新专辑。


现在完成时:
The director has premiered three films this year. = 这位导演今年已经首映了三部电影。
The company has premiered several new products recently. = 公司最近首发了数款新产品。


过去完成时:
The play had premiered in New York before it came to London. = 这部戏在来伦敦之前已在纽约首演。
They had premiered the song at a small club before releasing the album. = 他们在发行专辑之前，在一个小俱乐部首发了这首歌。


将来时:
The show will premiere next spring. = 这部剧将在明年春天首映。
The new game will premiere at E3. = 新游戏将在E3展会上首发。


虚拟语气:
If the film premiered earlier, it would have made more money. = 如果电影早点首映，就能赚更多钱。
I wish the play had premiered to a bigger audience. = 我希望这部戏能有更多观众观看首演。


被动语态:
The movie was premiered at the Cannes Film Festival. = 这部电影在戛纳电影节首映。
The new software will be premiered at the technology conference. = 新软件将在科技大会上首发。


疑问句:
When does the new season premiere? = 新剧季什么时候首映？
Where will the play premiere? = 这部戏将在哪里首演？


否定句:
The play did not premiere as planned. = 这部戏没有按计划首映。
The album hasn't premiered yet. = 专辑还没有首发。
