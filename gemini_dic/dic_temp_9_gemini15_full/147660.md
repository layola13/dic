overpopulated: /ˌoʊvərˈpɑːpjuleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 人口过多的，过度拥挤的 |  Having a population that is too large for the available resources.  It describes a situation where the number of people in a particular area exceeds the capacity of the environment to support them adequately, leading to strain on resources like food, water, housing, and infrastructure. |

例句：

The city is overpopulated, leading to traffic congestion and housing shortages. = 这个城市人口过多，导致交通拥堵和住房短缺。

Many developing countries are facing the challenge of overpopulated cities and rural areas. = 许多发展中国家正面临着城市和农村人口过多的挑战。


近义词：crowded, congested, densely populated


反义词：underpopulated, sparsely populated


词性：形容词
