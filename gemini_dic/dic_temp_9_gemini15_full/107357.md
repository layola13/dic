lamellibranch: /ˌlæməˈlɪbræŋk/ | n. |瓣鳃类动物；双壳类动物 | A lamellibranch is a mollusk of the class Bivalvia, having a laterally compressed body enclosed by a shell consisting of two hinged parts.  They are filter feeders, often found in aquatic environments. | Example:  Clams, oysters, and mussels are all examples of lamellibranchs.  瓣鳃类动物的例子包括蛤、牡蛎和贻贝。| 近义词: bivalve | 反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑cephalopod 头足类动物作为对比)| 名词
