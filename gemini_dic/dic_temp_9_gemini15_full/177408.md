rebaited:/ˌriːˈbeɪtɪd/| v. |重新装饵；重新设置诱饵 |To bait again; to put new bait on a hook or in a trap.  |例句：He rebaited his hook after a fish took the bait and broke the line. 他在鱼咬钩并断线后重新装了饵。

例句：The fisherman rebaited his trap with fresh fish to attract more crabs. 渔夫用新鲜的鱼重新设置了捕蟹笼以吸引更多的螃蟹。


近义词：re-equipped, replenished


反义词：unbaited


词性：动词
