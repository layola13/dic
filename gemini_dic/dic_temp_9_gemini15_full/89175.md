homeopathy:/ˌhoʊmiˈɒpəθi/| n. |顺势疗法 |A system of alternative medicine that uses highly diluted substances to treat patients.  It is based on the principle of "like cures like," meaning a substance that causes symptoms in a healthy person can cure those same symptoms in a sick person.  The effectiveness of homeopathy is highly debated and considered by many to be pseudoscience. | Example:  She sought treatment through homeopathy for her allergies. 她用顺势疗法治疗过敏。 |近义词：alternative medicine, complementary medicine |反义词：allopathy, conventional medicine
