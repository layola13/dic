drugstore:/ˈdrʌɡˌstɔːr/| n. |药店，杂货店|A store that sells medicines, toiletries, and other household goods.  Drugstores in the US often also sell snacks, drinks, and other convenience items.|例句：I need to pick up some aspirin from the drugstore. 我需要去药店买一些阿司匹林。|例句：The local drugstore carries a wide variety of beauty products. 当地的药店销售各种各样的美容产品。|近义词：pharmacy, chemist's (UK)|反义词：无
