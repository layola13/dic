prereconciled:/ˌpriːˈrɛkənsaɪld/| adj.|预先协调的；预先对账的 | Already reconciled in advance; prepared in advance for reconciliation.  This term often refers to financial accounts or data that have been matched and balanced before a formal reconciliation process. |The prereconciled data significantly reduced the time needed for the final reconciliation. 预先对账的数据大大缩短了最终对账所需的时间。 |近义词：pre-matched, pre-balanced |反义词：unreconciled
