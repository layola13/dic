doxology:/dɒkˈsɒlədʒi/| n. |颂赞诗；感恩歌；赞美诗 |A doxology is a hymn or other form of liturgical expression of praise to God.  It often incorporates a declaration of praise or glory to God. |Example: The service concluded with a traditional doxology.  仪式以一首传统的颂赞诗结束。|近义词：hymn, anthem, canticle |反义词：blasphemy, curse

词源:
From Late Latin *doxologia, from Greek δοξολογία (doxología), from δοξα (dóxa, "glory, opinion") + -λογία (-logía, "-logy").  The Greek word itself is a compound combining "glory" and the suffix "-logy" meaning "study of" or "speech about," resulting in "speech of glory" or "praise."
