overcriticism:/ˌoʊvərˈkrɪtɪsɪzəm/| n. |过度批评；吹毛求疵|The act of criticizing excessively or unfairly; fault-finding to an excessive degree.  It often implies a lack of fairness or objectivity.  |例句：His overcriticism of her work discouraged her.  他的过度批评让她泄气了。

例句：The project suffered from overcriticism, leading to delays and frustration.  这个项目因为过度批评而导致延误和沮丧。


近义词：hypercriticism, excessive criticism, faultfinding, nitpicking


反义词：praise, commendation, approval


词性：名词
