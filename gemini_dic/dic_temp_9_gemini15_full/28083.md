capillariness:/ˌkæpɪˈlærɪnɪs/| n. |毛细现象；毛细作用|The property of a liquid to rise in a narrow tube or porous material due to surface tension and adhesion. This phenomenon is crucial in various natural processes like water absorption by plants and blood circulation in the human body.|例句：The capillariness of the soil allows water to reach the roots of plants. 土壤的毛细作用使水分能够到达植物的根部。

The capillariness of the wick draws the oil up to the flame. 灯芯的毛细作用将油吸到火焰上。|近义词：capillary action, capillary attraction|反义词:N/A
