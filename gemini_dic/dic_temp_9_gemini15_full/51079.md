despatch:/dɪˈspætʃ/|/ˈdɪspætʃ/| n. vt.|派遣；发送；急件；|The act of sending something, especially goods or messages, quickly; an urgent message, especially one sent in a time of war or crisis.|The company will despatch the goods within 24 hours. 公司将在24小时内发货。|They received a despatch from the front lines. 他们收到了前线的急件。|send, dispatch, transmit, forward|receive, collect|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company despatches goods daily. = 公司每天都发货。
He despatches emails every morning. = 他每天早上都发送邮件。

一般过去时:
She despatched the letter yesterday. = 她昨天寄出了信。
They despatched troops to the border. = 他们向边境派遣了军队。

现在完成时:
We have despatched all the orders. = 我们已经完成了所有的订单。
The government has despatched aid to the disaster area. = 政府已经向灾区派遣了援助。

过去完成时:
They had despatched the package before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经寄出了包裹。
The general had despatched reinforcements before the battle. = 将军在战斗前已经派遣了增援部队。

将来时:
We will despatch the shipment next week. = 我们下周将发货。
They will despatch a team to investigate. = 他们将派遣一个团队进行调查。

虚拟语气:
If they had despatched the order sooner, it would have arrived on time. = 如果他们早点发货，它就能准时到达。
I wish they had despatched the troops earlier. = 我希望他们早点派遣军队。

被动语态:
The goods were despatched promptly. = 货物迅速发出。
The message was despatched by courier. = 消息由信使发出。

疑问句:
When will the goods be despatched? = 货物什么时候发出？
Have the troops been despatched yet? = 部队已经派遣了吗？

否定句:
They didn't despatch the goods on time. = 他们没有按时发货。
The company hasn't yet despatched my order. = 公司还没有寄出我的订单。
