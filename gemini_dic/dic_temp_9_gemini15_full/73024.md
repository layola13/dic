It's often used to measure the success of a business or event.|例句：The shop's footfall has increased significantly since the new advertising campaign started. 这家店的客流量自从新的广告宣传活动开始以来显著增加了。

例句：The low footfall at the museum was disappointing.  博物馆的低人流量令人失望。
