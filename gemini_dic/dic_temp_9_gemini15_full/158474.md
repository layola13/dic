pledgor:/ˈplɛdʒɔːr/| n. |保证人；抵押人|A pledgor is a person who gives something as security for a loan or debt.  They are legally obligated to fulfill the terms of the pledge. |Example: The pledgor defaulted on the loan, resulting in the bank seizing the collateral.  保证人未能偿还贷款，导致银行没收了抵押品。|近义词：guarantor, mortgagor|反义词：pledgee
