profoundest:/prəˈfaʊndɪst/| adj. |最深奥的；最深刻的；极大的 |Most deeply felt or experienced; of great depth or intensity.  It is the superlative form of "profound".|例句：His profoundest regret was his inability to spend more time with his family. 他最深的遗憾是他无法花更多时间陪伴家人。

例句：The book explores the profoundest mysteries of the universe. 这本书探讨了宇宙中最深奥的奥秘。


近义词：deepest, most intense, most significant, most serious |反义词：superficial, shallow, insignificant, trivial


词源:  From Old French *profund, from Latin profundus, from pro- "forth" + fundus "bottom".  The superlative suffix "-est" is added to indicate the highest degree.
