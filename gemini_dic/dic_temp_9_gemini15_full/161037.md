postcoding:/ˈpoʊstˌkoʊdɪŋ/| n. |邮政编码系统；邮政编码化|The process of assigning and using postcode/zip codes to addresses.  This includes the system itself and the act of applying codes.  It often involves databases and software for efficient sorting and delivery of mail.|Example:  The improved postcoding system significantly reduced mail delivery times.  改进后的邮政编码系统大大缩短了邮件投递时间。|近义词：zip coding, postal coding|反义词：无
