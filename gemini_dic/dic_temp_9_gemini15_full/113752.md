macronutrients:/ˈmækroʊˌnuːtrɪənts/| n. |宏量营养素|Macronutrients are the nutrients that are required in relatively large amounts in a diet.  They provide energy and are essential for building and maintaining body tissues. The three main macronutrients are carbohydrates, proteins, and fats.|Example:  A balanced diet includes sufficient macronutrients to support overall health.  一个均衡的饮食包括足够的宏量营养素来支持整体健康。|近义词：essential nutrients, major nutrients|反义词：micronutrients
