embryonic: /ˌɛmbrɪˈɒnɪk/ | adj. |胚胎的；初期的；萌芽期的 | Relating to an embryo or the embryonic stage of development.  The term often implies something is in its early stages and not yet fully formed or developed.  | 例句：The embryonic cells are rapidly dividing. = 胚胎细胞正在迅速分裂。


The study is in its embryonic stage. = 这项研究处于起步阶段。| 近义词：fetal, rudimentary, nascent, incipient | 反义词：mature, developed, advanced

词源:
From the Old French *embryon, from Latin embryō, from Greek ἔμβρυον (embruon), from ἐν (en, "in") + βρύω (bruō, "to swell").  The suffix "-ic" is a common adjective-forming suffix indicating relating to or characteristic of.
