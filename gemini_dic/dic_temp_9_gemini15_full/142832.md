out-cold:/ˌaʊtˈkoʊld/| adj.|昏迷的，不省人事的|Completely unconscious; knocked out.  The phrase implies a sudden and forceful loss of consciousness, often due to a blow or injury.|例句：He was hit on the head and knocked out cold. 他被击中头部，当场昏迷。|例句：After the accident, she lay out cold on the pavement. 事故后，她昏迷不醒地躺在人行道上。|近义词：unconscious, knocked out, passed out|反义词：conscious, awake
