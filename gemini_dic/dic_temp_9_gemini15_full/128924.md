nine:/naɪn/| num.|九|The number nine is the natural number following eight and preceding ten.|例句：There are nine planets in our solar system.  我们的太阳系中有九个行星。|例句：She counted nine birds in the tree.  她数到树上有九只鸟。|近义词：IX, 9|反义词:N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There are nine cats. = 有九只猫。
Nine plus one equals ten. = 九加一等于十。


一般过去时:
