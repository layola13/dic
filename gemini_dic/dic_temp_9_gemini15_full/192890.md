seat:/siːt/ | n. | 座位，席位 | A seat is a place for one person to sit, typically in a chair, vehicle, or other structure.  | Example:  Please take a seat. 请坐。 |  chair, place, position | standing,  (no direct antonym, context dependent) | 名词
