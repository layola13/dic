self-troubling: /ˌsɛlfˈtraʊblɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我困扰的，自寻烦恼的 |  Describes someone who is prone to worrying excessively or causing themselves problems through their own actions or thoughts.  It suggests a tendency towards negative self-talk and self-sabotage. |例句：He's a self-troubling individual, always finding reasons to worry. 他是一个自我困扰的人，总是找到理由担心。

He engages in self-troubling behaviors, constantly second-guessing his decisions. 他从事自我困扰的行为，不断地质疑自己的决定。


近义词：anxious, worried, apprehensive, self-destructive, neurotic | 反义词：calm, peaceful, relaxed, composed, serene | 词性：形容词
