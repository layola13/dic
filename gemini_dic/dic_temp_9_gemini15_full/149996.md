palmist:/ˈpɑːlmɪst/|/ˈpælmist/| n. |算命者（尤指通过看手掌算命的人）|A person who practices palmistry, telling fortunes or interpreting character from the lines on a person's palm.|The palmist predicted a long and prosperous life for me.  这位算命先生预言我将拥有漫长而富足的人生。|fortune teller, chiromancer|—|名词
