evokes:/ɪˈvoʊks/| vt.|唤起，引起，使人想起|to bring or recall something to someone's mind|The music evoked memories of her childhood. 这音乐唤起了她童年的回忆。|suggests, brings to mind, reminds of|suppresses, forgets|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The painting evokes a sense of peace. = 这幅画唤起了一种平静的感觉。
That smell evokes memories of my grandmother's kitchen. = 那种气味使我想起了祖母的厨房。


一般过去时:
The movie evoked strong emotions in the audience. = 这部电影激起了观众强烈的感情。
Her words evoked a feeling of sympathy in me. = 她的话语引起了我的同情。


现在完成时:
The author's descriptions have evoked vivid images in my mind. = 作者的描写在我的脑海中唤起了生动的画面。
This song has evoked many happy memories. = 这首歌唤起了许多美好的回忆。


过去完成时:
The old house evoked memories of a happier time before the war. = 这座老房子唤起了战争前更快乐时光的回忆。
Having read the book, I realized it evoked a feeling of nostalgia I hadn't expected. = 读完这本书后，我意识到它唤起了一种我意想不到的怀旧之情。


将来时:
The new museum will evoke a sense of wonder and excitement. = 新博物馆将唤起一种惊奇和兴奋的感觉。
I hope the play will evoke strong reactions from the audience. = 我希望这部戏剧能引起观众强烈的反应。


虚拟语气:
If the painting evoked more emotion, it would be a masterpiece. = 如果这幅画能唤起更多的情感，它将成为杰作。
I wish the speech had evoked a stronger response from the crowd. = 我希望演讲能引起人群更强烈的反应。


被动语态:
Strong emotions were evoked by the speaker's words. = 演讲者的言语激起了强烈的感情。
A sense of nostalgia is evoked by the old photographs. = 老照片唤起了怀旧之情。


疑问句:
Does this music evoke any particular memories for you? = 这音乐是否唤起了你任何特别的回忆？
What emotions does this artwork evoke in you? = 这件艺术品在你心中唤起了什么情感？


否定句:
The film didn't evoke much of a response from the critics. = 这部电影并没有引起评论家太大的反响。
The story failed to evoke any empathy from the readers. = 这故事未能引起读者的任何同情。
