go-fer:/ˈɡoʊfər/| n. |跑腿的人，杂役|A go-fer is someone whose job is to run errands and do small tasks for someone else.  They are typically low-level employees who assist others with various minor duties.| Example:  He started as a go-fer in the office, making coffee and fetching documents. 他一开始在办公室做跑腿的，负责煮咖啡和取文件。|近义词：errand boy/girl, messenger, assistant|反义词：boss, manager


USAGE->

He is a go-fer for the senior partner. = 他是高级合伙人的跑腿。
She often acts as a go-fer for her boss. = 她经常充当老板的跑腿。
