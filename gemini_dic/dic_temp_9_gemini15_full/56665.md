doo:/duː/| interj.|哎呀，表示轻微的惊叹或抱怨| An exclamation expressing mild surprise, annoyance, or concern.  It's often used informally.|例句：Doo, I spilled my coffee! 哎呀，我把咖啡洒了！|近义词：oops, oh, well, gosh|反义词:N/A
