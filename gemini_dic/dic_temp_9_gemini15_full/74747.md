freewares:/ˈfriːˌwɛrz/| n. |免费软件|Freeware refers to software that is available for use at no monetary cost.  It's often distributed under a license that permits free use but may restrict modification, redistribution, or commercial use.  Unlike open-source software, the source code of freeware is typically not available to the public. |Example: Many antivirus programs offer freeware versions with limited features. 许多杀毒软件提供具有有限功能的免费版本。|近义词：free software, shareware (with limitations)|反义词：commercial software, paid software|名词
