figulate:/ˈfɪɡjuleɪt/| vt.|塑形；捏塑；制作陶器|To shape or mold (clay, etc.), especially in the making of pottery.  The word implies a shaping or molding process, often with hands, to create a specific form.|例句：The potter carefully figulated the clay into a graceful vase.  陶工仔细地将粘土塑造成一个优雅的花瓶。


例句：She figulated the dough into small rolls before baking. 她把面团捏成小卷再烘烤。

近义词：model, shape, mold, form

反义词：destroy, break, demolish

词性:动词
