incultivation:/ˌɪnkʌltɪˈveɪʃən/ | n. |  耕种；栽培；教化；培养 | The state or process of bringing land into cultivation; the act of cultivating or improving something.  It can refer to the cultivation of land for agriculture, or the cultivation of skills, knowledge, or character. |例句：The incultivation of the land led to a bountiful harvest.  土地的耕作带来了丰收。

The incultivation of their musical talents was evident in their breathtaking performance.  他们音乐才能的培养在他们令人惊叹的表演中显而易见。|近义词：cultivation, improvement, development, refinement |反义词：neglect, abandonment, deterioration
