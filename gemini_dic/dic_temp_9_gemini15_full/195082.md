semichemically:/ˌsɛmɪˈkɛmɪkli/| adj.|半化学的；半化学处理的|Relating to or denoting a process or product that is partly chemical and partly mechanical or physical.  It often refers to pulping processes in papermaking where both chemical and mechanical methods are used.|例句：The paper was produced using a semichemical pulping process. 这张纸是用半化学制浆法生产的。 The semichemical treatment improved the fiber's properties. 半化学处理改善了纤维的性能。|近义词：partially chemical,  mechanochemical (depending on the context)|反义词：fully chemical, purely mechanical|词性:形容词
