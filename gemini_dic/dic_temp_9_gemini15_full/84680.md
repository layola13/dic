handed-in: /ˈhændɪd ɪn/ | v. | 交上；提交 | To give something to someone in authority, such as a teacher or employer.  | 例句：I handed in my assignment on time. 我按时提交了我的作业。

例句：He handed in his resignation letter yesterday. 他昨天递交了辞职信。


近义词：submitted, delivered, presented, turned in | 反义词：withheld, retained, kept


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She hands in her homework every Friday. = 她每个星期五都交作业。
They hand in their reports at the end of the month. = 他们月底提交报告。


一般过去时:
I handed in my application last week. = 我上周提交了我的申请。
He handed in the completed forms to the receptionist. = 他把填好的表格交给了接待员。


现在完成时:
She has handed in all her assignments. = 她已经提交了所有作业。
They have handed in their proposals for review. = 他们已经提交了提案以供审查。


过去完成时:
I had handed in my report before the deadline. = 我在截止日期前提交了报告。
She had handed in her notice before she left for vacation. = 她休假前已经递交了辞职通知。


将来时:
I will hand in my project next Monday. = 我下周一提交我的项目。
He will hand in the documents as soon as he finishes them. = 他一完成文件就提交。


虚拟语气:
If I had handed in my work earlier, I wouldn't have received a lower grade. = 如果我早点交作业，就不会得低分了。
I wish I had handed in my application sooner. = 我真希望我早点提交申请。


被动语态:
The assignments were handed in on time. = 作业按时提交了。
The report will be handed in by Friday. = 报告将在星期五前提交。


疑问句:
Have you handed in your essay yet? = 你提交你的论文了吗？
When will the documents be handed in? = 文件什么时候提交？


否定句:
He didn't hand in his homework. = 他没有交作业。
She hasn't handed in her report yet. = 她还没有提交报告。
