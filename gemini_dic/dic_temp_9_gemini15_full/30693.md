certificate:/sərˈtɪfɪkət/| n. |证书，证明 |A document that provides evidence or proof of something, such as completion of a course, achievement of a skill, or verification of a fact.  |例句：He received a certificate of completion after finishing the course. 他完成课程后收到了结业证书。|例句：The bank requires a certificate of deposit before granting the loan. 银行在发放贷款前需要存款证明。|近义词：diploma, license, credential, attestation |反义词：disproof, refutation
