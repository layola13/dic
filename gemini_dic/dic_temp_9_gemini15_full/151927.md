pasteurizing: /ˌpæs.tər.aɪz.ɪŋ/ | v. |巴氏消毒法 |The process of heating a liquid, such as milk or juice, to a specific temperature for a specific length of time to kill harmful bacteria and other microorganisms without significantly altering the taste or nutritional value.  |  例句：The milk is pasteurized to ensure its safety. 这牛奶经过巴氏消毒以确保安全。

例句：We pasteurize all our juices to prevent spoilage. 我们对所有果汁进行巴氏消毒以防止变质。


近义词：sterilizing (although sterilization is typically a more extreme process) | 反义词：contaminating
