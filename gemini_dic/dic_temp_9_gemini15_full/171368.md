publishes:/ˈpʌblɪʃɪz/| vt.|出版；发表；公布|To make (a book, article, etc.) available to the public by printing or other means; to make known or announce officially.|例句：The author publishes his novels annually. 作者每年出版他的小说。|例句：The government publishes its economic forecasts each month. 政府每月公布其经济预测。|近义词：issue, release, disseminate|反义词：suppress, withhold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company publishes several books every year. = 这家公司每年出版几本书。
She publishes her research findings in scientific journals. = 她将她的研究成果发表在科学期刊上。

一般过去时:
They published the news immediately. = 他们立即发布了这条新闻。
He published his first novel when he was 25. = 他25岁时出版了他的第一部小说。

现在完成时:
The newspaper has published several articles on the subject. = 这家报纸已经发表了几篇关于这个主题的文章。
She has published extensively in the field of linguistics. = 她在这个语言学领域发表了大量的作品。

过去完成时:
They had published the report before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经发表了报告。
The company had published three books before it went bankrupt. = 这家公司破产前已经出版了三本书。

将来时:
The university will publish the results of the study next year. = 大学将于明年公布这项研究的结果。
We will publish a new edition of the textbook next month. = 我们下个月将出版新版本的教科书。

虚拟语气:
If they published the truth, many people would be shocked. = 如果他们公布真相，许多人都会感到震惊。
I wish they had published my article sooner. = 我希望他们早点发表我的文章。

被动语态:
The article was published in the New York Times. = 这篇文章发表在《纽约时报》上。
The book is being published next spring. = 这本书将于明年春天出版。

疑问句:
Does your company publish academic journals? = 你的公司出版学术期刊吗？
When was the book first published? = 这本书第一次出版是什么时候？

否定句:
They did not publish the findings of the study. = 他们没有公布这项研究的结果。
The publisher refused to publish the controversial novel. = 出版商拒绝出版这本有争议的小说。


PHRASE->
