missionary:/ˈmɪʃənəri/ | n. |传教士；从事慈善事业的人 | A person sent on a religious mission, especially one sent to promote Christianity in a foreign country.  |例句：Many missionaries devoted their lives to serving others in remote areas.  许多传教士将毕生奉献给在偏远地区服务他人。|例句：The missionary work focused on education and healthcare, as well as religious instruction. 传教工作侧重于教育和医疗保健，以及宗教教育。|近义词：evangelist, proselytizer |反义词：N/A |名词


USAGE->

He is a missionary in Africa. = 他是位在非洲的传教士。
Missionaries work tirelessly to help the poor. = 传教士们不知疲倦地帮助穷人。
