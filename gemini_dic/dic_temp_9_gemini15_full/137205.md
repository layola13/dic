nonsupplication:/ˌnɒnsʌplɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |非恳求；不祈求；不乞求 |The act or instance of not begging or pleading.  It implies a sense of self-reliance and dignity, a refusal to lower oneself to supplication. |例句：His nonsupplication in the face of adversity surprised everyone.  他的逆境中不卑不亢的态度让每个人都感到惊讶。  His response was characterized by a quiet nonsupplication, a refusal to plead for mercy.他的回应以一种平静的不祈求为特征，拒绝恳求怜悯。|近义词：self-reliance, independence, dignity, assertion |反义词：supplication, pleading, begging, entreaty |名词
