riting:/ˈraɪtɪŋ/| n. |写作；书写；作品 |The act of writing or the process of producing written text.  It can also refer to the style or quality of a person's writing, or to a particular piece of written work.|Example: Her writing is elegant and precise.她的文笔优雅而精确。|Example: The novel is a work of exceptional writing. 这部小说是一部杰出的作品。|近义词：composition, authorship, script |反义词：reading, speaking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His writing improves every day. = 他的写作水平每天都在提高。
She is writing a novel. = 她正在写一部小说。


一般过去时:
He wrote a letter to his friend. = 他给朋友写了一封信。
She wrote a poem about nature. = 她写了一首关于自然的诗。


现在完成时:
I have written three essays this week. = 我本周已经写了三篇论文。
She has written many articles for the newspaper. = 她已经为报纸写了很多文章。


过去完成时:
He had written the report before the meeting. = 他在会议之前已经写好了报告。
She had written several drafts before she was satisfied. = 在她满意之前，她已经写了好几个草稿。


将来时:
I will write to you soon. = 我很快就会写信给你。
She will be writing her dissertation next year. = 她明年将要写她的论文。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would write a book. = 如果我有更多时间，我会写一本书。
I wish I had written to him sooner. = 我希望我早点给他写信。


被动语态:
The letter was written in haste. = 这封信是仓促写成的。
The essay is being written by a well-known author. = 这篇文章是一位著名的作家写的。


疑问句:
What are you writing? = 你在写什么？
Who wrote this book? = 谁写的这本书？


否定句:
I didn't write that email. = 我没有写那封邮件。
She hasn't written to me yet. = 她还没给我写信。


PHRASE->
creative writing: 创意写作
technical writing: 技术写作
handwriting: 书法，手写体
writing style: 写作风格
writing skills: 写作技巧
