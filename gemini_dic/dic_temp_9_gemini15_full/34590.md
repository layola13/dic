clearwater:/ˈklɪrwɔːtər/| n. |清水；清澈的水；地名 |Clearwater refers to water that is transparent and free from impurities.  It can also be a proper noun referring to various places. |Example: The lake's clearwater reflected the blue sky.  这湖的清水映照着蓝天。The Clearwater River flows through several states. 克利尔沃特河流经数个州。|近义词：pure water, limpid water|反义词：muddy water, polluted water


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lake water is clear. = 湖水清澈。
The river always flows clear. = 这条河的水总是清澈的。


一般过去时:
The water was clear yesterday. = 昨天水很清澈。
The stream ran clear after the rain stopped. = 雨停后，溪水变得清澈。


现在完成时:
The water has been clear all week. = 这周水一直很清澈。
The pollution has cleared, and the water is clear now. = 污染已经清除，水现在很清澈了。


过去完成时:
The water had been clear until the factory started dumping waste. = 直到工厂开始倾倒废物之前，水一直很清澈。
They had cleared the debris, and the water was clear again. = 他们清理了碎片，水又清澈了。


将来时:
The water will be clear after the cleanup. = 清理之后，水会很清澈。
The lake will likely remain clear throughout the summer. = 湖水很可能在整个夏天都保持清澈。


虚拟语气:
If the water were clear, we could swim in it. = 如果水清澈的话，我们就可以游泳了。
I wish the river were clear again. = 我希望这条河能再次清澈。


被动语态:
The water was cleared by the filtration system. = 水被过滤系统净化了。
The lake is being cleared of pollutants. = 湖泊正在清除污染物。


疑问句:
Is the water clear enough to drink? = 水够干净可以喝吗？
Was the water clear before the storm? = 暴风雨前水清澈吗？


否定句:
The water isn't clear enough for swimming. = 水不够清澈，不能游泳。
The river wasn't clear after the heavy rain. = 大雨过后，河水不清澈。
