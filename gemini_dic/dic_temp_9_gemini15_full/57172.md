dowelling: /ˈdaʊəlɪŋ/ | n. |木销；榫头；木钉；|The act of joining pieces of wood using dowels.  Dowelling refers to both the process of using dowels to join wood and the dowels themselves. Dowels are cylindrical pins used to join pieces of wood, often used in furniture making and woodworking projects.|例句：The cabinetmaker used dowelling to create strong joints.  = 制柜工人用木销制作牢固的接合处。

The chair's legs were joined with strong dowelling. = 椅子的腿是用牢固的木销连接起来的。|近义词：jointing, pinning |反义词：separation, disjoining |名词
