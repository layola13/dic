renegade:/ˈrɛnɪɡeɪd/ | n., adj., v. |叛徒；背叛者；脱离正轨的；背信弃义的 | A person who deserts a cause, country, or group.  Someone who rejects the accepted beliefs or standards of a group.  To abandon or desert one's principles, allegiance, or cause. |例句：He was branded a renegade after betraying his comrades. 他背叛了他的同志后被贴上了叛徒的标签。

The general became a renegade and joined the opposition.  将军叛变并加入了反对派。


近义词：deserter, traitor, defector, apostate, turncoat |反义词：loyalist, conformist
