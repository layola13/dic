近义词：clergyman, minister, priest|反义词：layperson, layperson


词源:  The surname Parsons is an occupational name for a parson, derived from the Old French word *person*, meaning "person," and ultimately from the Latin *persona*.  The term "parson" originally referred to the rector of a parish church.


用法说明:

As a surname:  Parsons is used as a last name, and has no further grammatical function.

As a noun: "Parsons" (plural of parson) refers to multiple ministers of a Christian church.  It is used in the same way as "clergymen," "ministers," or "priests".
