leanings:/ˈliːnɪŋz/| n. |倾向，爱好，偏见 | inclinations or tendencies, especially of a political or ideological nature; a disposition or tendency to behave in a particular way.|例句：His political leanings are clearly left-wing.  他的政治倾向明显是左翼的。|例句：She has artistic leanings and spends most of her free time painting. 她有艺术爱好，大部分空闲时间都用来绘画。|近义词：tendencies, inclinations, predispositions, biases |反义词：opposites, antipathies


USAGE->

一般现在时:
His political leanings are conservative. = 他的政治倾向是保守的。
Her artistic leanings are evident in her home decor. = 她的艺术爱好在她家的装饰中显而易见。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

We will see how her leanings will develop over time. = 我们将看到她的倾向如何随着时间的推移而发展。


虚拟语气:
