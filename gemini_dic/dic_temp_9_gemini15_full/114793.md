maltreats:/mælˈtriːts/| v.|虐待；粗暴对待 |To treat (a person or animal) roughly or cruelly.  |例句：The animals were maltreated and neglected.  这些动物受到了虐待和忽视。
The child was maltreated by their caregiver. 这个孩子被他们的看护者虐待了。|近义词：abuse, ill-treat, mistreat |反义词：cherish, care for, nurture|动词
