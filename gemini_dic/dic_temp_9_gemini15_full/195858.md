senior:/ˈsiːniər/| adj., n.|资深的；高级的；年长的；（大学）四年级的学生|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "senior" means holding a higher rank or position, having more experience, or being older.  As a noun, it refers to a person who holds a senior position or is older, or a student in their final year of high school or college.


例句:
He is a senior manager in the company. 他是公司的高级经理。

She's a senior citizen and enjoys her retirement. 她是老年人，享受着退休生活。


近义词:
elder, older, superior, advanced, veteran


反义词:
junior, younger, subordinate, lower, novice


词性:
adjective, noun
