prolificity:/ˌprɒlɪˈfɪsəti/| n. |多产；丰富；丰饶 |The ability to produce a large amount of something, especially works of art, scientific discoveries, or offspring.  It refers to the state or quality of being prolific. |例句：The prolificity of the artist's output is astounding.  这位艺术家的创作量惊人。

例句：Her prolificity as a writer is evident in her vast body of work. 她作为一名作家的高产在其大量的作品中可见一斑。


近义词：fertility, fecundity, abundance, productivity |反义词：scarcity, barrenness, sterility
