glor:/ˈɡlɔːr/| n.|光荣，荣耀，辉煌|glory means great praise and admiration given to someone for something they have done; the state of being admired for something.  It can also refer to something that brings great honor or praise. |例句：Her victory brought her great glory. 她的胜利给她带来了巨大的荣耀。|例句：The glory of the setting sun filled the sky. 落日的辉煌照亮了天空。|近义词：honor, fame, renown|反义词：shame, disgrace, ignominy|名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The athlete basks in the glory of his victory. = 运动员沉浸在胜利的荣耀中。
Their hard work brings glory to the company. = 他们的辛勤工作为公司带来了荣耀。

一般过去时:
The general received much glory after the war. = 战争结束后，将军获得了许多荣誉。
The artist's masterpiece brought him lasting glory. = 艺术家的杰作给他带来了持久的荣耀。

现在完成时:
She has achieved great glory in her career. = 她在事业上取得了巨大的成就。
The team has brought glory to their country. = 这支队伍为他们的国家带来了荣耀。

过去完成时:
By the end of the campaign, he had already earned considerable glory. = 到竞选结束时，他已经获得了相当大的荣誉。
Before his death, he had lived a life of glory and achievement. = 在他去世之前，他过着光荣而成就非凡的生活。

将来时:
Future generations will remember his glory. = 子孙后代将铭记他的功绩。
Her upcoming performance promises to bring her more glory. = 她即将举行的演出有望给她带来更多荣耀。

虚拟语气:
If he had won, he would have enjoyed the glory. = 如果他赢了，他就会享受到荣耀。
I wish I could share in their glory. = 我希望我能分享他们的荣耀。

被动语态:
His achievements were celebrated in a blaze of glory. = 他的成就以辉煌的方式被庆祝。
The award was given to him for his glorious service. = 为了表彰他光荣的服务，授予了他这个奖项。

疑问句:
What is the glory of this moment? = 这时刻的荣耀是什么？
Does he seek glory or genuine achievement? = 他追求的是荣耀还是真正的成就？

否定句:
He sought not glory, but justice. = 他追求的不是荣耀，而是正义。
The campaign lacked glory and was ultimately a failure. = 这场战役缺乏荣耀，最终以失败告终。
