downrange:/ˈdaʊnreɪndʒ/| adj. adv. n.|下游的；下风处的；目标区；射程内；|英文解析：Referring to the direction or area beyond the launch point of a projectile, especially a missile or rocket, along its trajectory.  It can also refer to the area affected by something, such as the fallout from a nuclear explosion. |例句：The downrange sensors detected the missile's impact. 下游传感器探测到导弹的撞击。

The effects of the explosion were significant downrange. 爆炸的影响在下游地区相当显著。


近义词：downfield, downstream (in some contexts)|反义词：uprange|词性：形容词，副词，名词
