inspires:/ɪnˈspaɪrz/ | vt. | 激励；鼓舞；启发 | To fill (someone) with the urge or ability to do or feel something, especially to do something creative.  | 例句：His speech inspired us to work harder.他的演讲激励我们更加努力工作。

例句：The beautiful scenery inspired the artist to paint a masterpiece.美丽的风景启发了艺术家创作出一幅杰作。


近义词：motivate, encourage, stimulate, influence

反义词：discourage, depress, demotivate

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music inspires me. = 这音乐激励着我。
Her courage inspires others. = 她的勇气激励着他人。

一般过去时:
The teacher inspired her students. = 老师激励了她的学生们。
His words inspired hope. = 他的话带来了希望。

现在完成时:
This book has inspired many people. = 这本书激励了许多人。
Her work has inspired generations of artists. = 她的作品激励了一代又一代的艺术家。

过去完成时:
His speech had inspired them to act. = 他的演讲激励他们采取行动。
They had been inspired by his example. = 他们受到他的榜样的鼓舞。

将来时:
This experience will inspire me in the future. = 这段经历将来会激励我。
The new technology will inspire innovation. = 新技术将激发创新。

虚拟语气:
If only I could inspire others as he does! = 但愿我也能像他一样激励他人！
I wish I could inspire more confidence in myself. = 我希望我能增强更多的自信。

被动语态:
We were inspired by her story. = 我们被她的故事激励了。
The audience was inspired by his performance. = 观众被他精彩的表演所激励。

疑问句:
What inspires you to keep going? = 什么激励你继续前进？
What inspired this painting? = 什么启发了这幅画？

否定句:
Nothing inspired him to change his mind. = 没有什么能够激励他改变主意。
The project didn't inspire much enthusiasm. = 这个项目并没有激发多少热情。


PHRASE->
inspire confidence:树立信心
inspire hope: 赋予希望
inspire creativity: 激发创造力
draw inspiration from: 从…汲取灵感
be inspired by:  受到…的启发

词源:
The word "inspire" comes from the Latin word "inspirare," which means "to breathe into."  The original meaning referred to the act of breathing life or spirit into something. Over time, the meaning evolved to encompass the act of filling someone with creative or emotional energy.
