floatier:/ˈfloʊtiər/ | adj. | 漂浮的；轻飘飘的；不切实际的 | More floaty; having or showing a light, airy quality.  It suggests a greater degree of floatiness than "floaty." |例句：The clouds looked floatier than usual in the soft sunlight.  在柔和的阳光下，云朵看起来比平时更轻盈飘逸。

The fabric felt floatier after being washed. 洗涤后，面料感觉更轻盈了。

近义词：buoyant, airy, ethereal, weightless | 反义词：dense, heavy, grounded, substantial | 词性：形容词
