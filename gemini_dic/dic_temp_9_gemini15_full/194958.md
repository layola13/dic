semi-opaque: /ˌsɛmiˈoʊpeɪk/ | adj. | 半透明的，半不透明的 |  Partially transparent; allowing some light to pass through but diffusing it so that objects beyond are not clearly visible.  | The stained glass window was semi-opaque, allowing a soft, diffused light to enter the room.  半透明的彩色玻璃窗让柔和的散射光进入房间。 | translucent, diaphanous | opaque, solid |
