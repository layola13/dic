second-handed:/ˌsekəndˈhændɪd/ | adj. | 二手的，间接的 |  Describes something that has been owned or used by someone else before being sold or given to another person. It can also describe information received indirectly, not firsthand. | 例句：I bought a second-handed car for a good price. 我以好价钱买了一辆二手车。 |近义词：used, pre-owned, secondhand (same spelling)|反义词：new, unused, firsthand
