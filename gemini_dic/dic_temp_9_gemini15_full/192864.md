seasickness:/ˈsiːˌsɪknɪs/| n. |晕船(车、船等)|A feeling of nausea and dizziness caused by the motion of a ship or other vehicle.  This is a condition affecting people who are traveling by sea or other forms of transportation, causing them to feel sick.  It is typically characterized by nausea, vomiting, dizziness, and general discomfort.|例句：The rough seas caused severe seasickness among the passengers.  颠簸的海浪导致乘客严重晕船。

例句：She took medication to prevent seasickness on the ferry ride.  她在渡轮旅行中服用药物以预防晕船。


近义词：motion sickness, nausea, queasiness |反义词：wellness, good health|词性：noun
