palisade:/ˈpælɪseɪd/| n.|栅栏；防御工事；（植物的）栅栏状结构 |A palisade is a fence made of strong, pointed wooden stakes, often used for defensive purposes.  It can also refer to a row of closely set objects resembling a fence, or a vertical row of cells in a plant. |例句：The castle was protected by a strong palisade.  城堡由坚固的栅栏保护着。

The ancient city was surrounded by a tall palisade of sharpened stakes. 古城被高高的尖桩栅栏包围着。|近义词：fence, barricade, stockade |反义词：open space, gap
