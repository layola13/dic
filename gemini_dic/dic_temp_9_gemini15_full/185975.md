rewarned:/ˌriːˈwɔːrnd/| v.|再次警告，重新警告|To warn someone again about something.  It implies a previous warning was given but ignored or was insufficient.|The police rewarned the protesters about the dangers of blocking the highway.  警方再次警告抗议者堵塞高速公路的危险。|近义词：remonstrate,admonish|反义词:encourage,permit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rewarns his son about the dangers of speeding. 他再次警告儿子超速的危险。
They rewarn the public about the approaching storm. 他们再次提醒公众即将到来的风暴。


一般过去时:
The teacher rewarned the students about the upcoming exam.老师再次提醒学生即将到来的考试。
The captain rewarned the crew about the approaching iceberg. 船长再次警告船员即将到来的冰山。


现在完成时:
I have rewarned him several times, but he still ignores me. 我已经多次警告他了，但他仍然不理会我。

过去完成时:

They had rewarned the residents about the potential flood risk before the heavy rain. 大雨来临之前，他们已经再次提醒居民潜在的洪水风险。


将来时:

The authorities will rewarn the citizens about the potential health hazards. 当局将再次警告市民潜在的健康危害。


虚拟语气:
