overcook:/ˌoʊvərˈkʊk/| vt.|烹调过度；煮过头|To cook (something) for too long, resulting in it being dry, hard, or otherwise unpalatable.|例句：I overcooked the chicken, and now it's dry and tough. 我把鸡肉煮过头了，现在又干又硬。例句：Don't overcook the vegetables; they should be tender-crisp. 不要把蔬菜煮过头；它们应该嫩脆。|近义词：burn, scorch, char|反义词：undercook
