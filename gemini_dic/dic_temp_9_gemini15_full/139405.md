observe:/əbˈzɜːrv/| /ɑːbˈzɜːrv/ | vt. & vi. |观察；遵守；庆祝；注意到| to watch carefully the way something happens or the way someone behaves; to notice or pay attention to something; to comply with a rule, law, etc.; to celebrate a religious or national holiday |例句：The scientist observed the experiment carefully. 科学家仔细观察了实验。|例句：We must observe the traffic laws. 我们必须遵守交通法规。|近义词：watch, notice, monitor, heed, celebrate |反义词：ignore, disregard, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I observe the birds in my garden every morning. = 我每天早上都观察花园里的鸟。
She observes the speed limit carefully. = 她仔细遵守限速规定。


一般过去时:
He observed a strange phenomenon during the experiment. = 他在实验中观察到了一种奇怪的现象。
They observed a minute of silence to remember the victims. = 他们默哀一分钟来悼念受害者。


现在完成时:
I have observed a significant change in his behavior recently. = 我最近观察到他行为上的显著变化。
Scientists have observed many new species in the Amazon rainforest. = 科学家们在亚马逊雨林中观察到了许多新物种。


过去完成时:
They had observed the star for several nights before making a discovery. = 他们在发现之前已经观察了那颗星星好几个晚上。
She had observed the pattern before but hadn't understood it. = 她以前观察到过这种模式，但没有理解。


将来时:
We will observe the total lunar eclipse tonight. = 我们今晚将观察月全食。
The astronomers will observe the comet's trajectory. = 天文学家们将观测彗星的轨迹。


虚拟语气:
If I had observed the warning signs, I would have avoided the accident. = 如果我注意到了警告标志，我就能避免事故。
I wish I had observed the details more closely. = 我希望我当时更仔细地观察了细节。


被动语态:
The experiment was observed by several scientists. = 实验由几位科学家观察。
The speed limit must be observed. = 必须遵守限速规定。


疑问句:
Did you observe anything unusual? = 你观察到什么异常情况了吗？
What did the scientists observe during the experiment? = 科学家们在实验中观察到了什么？


否定句:
He did not observe the rules of the game. = 他没有遵守比赛规则。
I didn't observe any significant differences between the two samples. = 我没有观察到这两个样本之间有任何显著差异。
