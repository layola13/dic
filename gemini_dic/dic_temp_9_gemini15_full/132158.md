/nɑːnˈkjʊəriəsli/ | adj. | 不好奇地，无好奇心地 | Not showing curiosity; without curiosity.  The word is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the adverb "curiously". |  He listened to the story noncuriously, his eyes glazed over. 他漫不经心地听着故事，眼睛都直了。  She examined the evidence noncuriously, showing no interest in its implications. 她漫不经心地检查证据，对它的含义毫无兴趣。 | curiously, interestedly |  interested, curious |  adj.
