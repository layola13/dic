regeared:/ˌriːˈɡɪər/| v.|重新配置齿轮；改变传动比；重新调整|To change the gears or gear ratios of (a machine, especially an engine or transmission) .  It often implies a significant adjustment to improve performance or efficiency.|例句：The car's engine was regeared to improve its acceleration. 这辆车的发动机重新配置了齿轮以提高其加速性能。The company regeared its marketing strategy to target a younger demographic. 公司调整了其营销策略以瞄准更年轻的人群。|近义词：re-equip, refit, readjust|反义词：leave unchanged
