shiftiest:/ˈʃɪftiɪst/| adj.|最狡猾的，最不可靠的|Most untrustworthy or suspicious;  characterized by evasiveness and deception.  It is the superlative form of "shifty".|例句：He gave me the shiftiest look I've ever seen. 他给了我我见过的最狡猾的眼神。

例句：The shiftiest character in the whole town was finally caught.  整个镇上最狡猾的角色最终被抓住了。


近义词：dodgiest, shadiest, trickiest, most deceitful, most unreliable|反义词：most honest, most trustworthy, most reliable

词性：形容词
