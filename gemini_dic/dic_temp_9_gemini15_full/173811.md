It suggests a lyrical or evocative quality, perhaps in language or style, but not necessarily adhering to poetic conventions.  | Example: He described the sunset quasi-poetically, using vivid imagery and figurative language, though it wasn't a formal poem. 他几乎诗意地描述了日落，使用了生动的意象和比喻，尽管那不是一首正式的诗。 | 近义词：lyrically, evocatively, figuratively, poetically (to a lesser extent) | 反义词：prosaically, plainly, bluntly

Example 1 (Simple Present):  She speaks quasi-poetically about her childhood memories. 她几乎诗意地谈论着她童年的回忆。

Example 2 (Past Perfect): He had written quasi-poetically in his journal for years before anyone discovered it.  多年来他一直在日记里几乎诗意地写作，直到有人发现了它。
