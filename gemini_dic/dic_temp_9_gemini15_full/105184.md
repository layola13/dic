keyboarded:/ˈkiːbɔːrdɪd/ | v. |键盘输入的；用键盘打字的 |  Past tense and past participle of keyboard.  It describes something that has been inputted or typed using a keyboard. |  He keyboarded the entire report in one sitting. 他一次性地用键盘输入了整份报告。  She keyboarded the email quickly. 她快速地用键盘打出邮件。| typed, entered, input | handwritten, hand-written | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He keyboards his notes every day. = 他每天用键盘记录笔记。
She keyboards the data into the spreadsheet. = 她将数据输入电子表格。

一般过去时:
I keyboarded the document last night. = 我昨晚用键盘输入了文件。
