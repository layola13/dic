gangplank:/ˈɡæŋˌplæŋk/| n.|跳板；舷梯|A gangplank is a movable bridge used to connect a ship to the shore or to another ship.  It is typically made of planks of wood or metal and is often equipped with railings for safety.|例句：The passengers walked down the gangplank and onto the dock.  乘客们走下舷梯，来到码头上。

The sailors carefully secured the gangplank to the ship. 水手们小心地将舷梯固定在船上。|近义词：gangway, bridge, walkway|反义词:None.

The gangplank connects the ship to the pier. = 舷梯连接着船和码头。
The crew secures the gangplank after each passenger disembarks. = 每次乘客下船后，船员都会固定舷梯。
