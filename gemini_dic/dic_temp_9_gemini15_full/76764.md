gamepad:/ˈɡeɪmpæd/| n. |游戏手柄，游戏控制器|A handheld device used to control video games, typically with buttons, directional pads, and analog sticks.|例句：He plugged his gamepad into the console and started playing. 他把游戏手柄插进游戏机开始玩游戏。

例句：The new gamepad features improved ergonomics and haptic feedback.  新的游戏手柄具有改进的人体工程学设计和触觉反馈。


近义词：controller, joystick


反义词：keyboard (in the context of game input)
