outlearn:/ˌaʊtˈlɜːrn/| vt.|胜过…的学习；超过…的学习|To surpass someone in learning or knowledge; to learn more than someone else.|例句：He outlearned all his classmates in mathematics. 他在数学方面胜过所有同学。|例句：She completely outlearned her tutor in just a few months.  她仅仅几个月就完全超过了她的导师。|近义词：surpass, exceed, excel|反义词：be outlearned by


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outlearns his brother in science. = 他在科学方面胜过他的兄弟。
She outlearns her peers in history. = 她在历史方面胜过她的同龄人。

一般过去时:
He outlearned his classmates in physics last year. = 去年他在物理方面胜过他的同学们。
She outlearned her tutor in ancient literature. = 她胜过了她的古代文学导师。

现在完成时:
He has outlearned everyone in his class in math. = 他在数学方面已经胜过班里所有人了。
She has outlearned her former professor. = 她已经超过了她之前的教授。

过去完成时:
By the time of the exam, he had outlearned all his competitors. = 到考试的时候，他已经超过了所有竞争对手。
She had outlearned her brother in languages before she turned eighteen. = 在她十八岁之前，她在语言方面就已经超过了她的哥哥。

将来时:
He will outlearn his rivals in this competition. = 他将在这次比赛中胜过他的对手。
She will outlearn her current tutor by next year. = 到明年她将超过她现在的导师。

虚拟语气:
If he studied harder, he would outlearn everyone. = 如果他学习更努力，他会胜过所有人。
If she had applied herself, she would have outlearned her opponent. = 如果她更加努力，她本可以超过她的对手。

被动语态:
He was outlearned by his younger sister in chess. = 他在下棋方面被他的妹妹超过了。
She was outlearned by her rival in debate. = 她在辩论方面被她的对手超过了。

疑问句:
Did he outlearn his opponent in the debate? = 他在辩论中胜过他的对手了吗？
Will she outlearn her classmates in this subject? = 她会在这个科目上胜过她的同学们吗？

否定句:
He didn't outlearn his rival in the competition. = 他在比赛中没有胜过他的对手。
She did not outlearn her tutor in that specific area. = 她在那方面没有胜过她的导师。
