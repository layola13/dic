hyperperfection:/ˌhaɪpər pərˈfɛkʃən/| n. |完美主义，过度追求完美|The relentless pursuit of flawlessness, often to the detriment of other important aspects of life.  It's characterized by excessive attention to detail, an inability to accept anything less than perfect, and often leads to procrastination, anxiety, and low self-esteem. |He suffers from hyperperfection, spending hours on tasks that could be completed in minutes. 他患有完美主义，花数小时完成几分钟就能完成的任务。|perfectionism, meticulousness| imperfection, sloppiness|名词
