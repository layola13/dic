popple:/ˈpɒpəl/ | n. |轻微的爆裂声；（尤指树叶的）沙沙作响声；小水泡|a slight popping sound, especially of leaves rustling; a small bubble|The leaves poppled in the gentle breeze.  树叶在微风中发出轻微的爆裂声。|The soda water had tiny popples on the surface. 苏打水表面有许多小气泡。|rustle, crackle, fizz| (近义词缺乏精确的反义词，可考虑silent, still)|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The leaves popple gently in the wind. = 树叶在风中轻轻地沙沙作响。
The bubbles popple on the surface of the drink. = 饮料表面上的气泡发出轻微的爆裂声。


一般过去时:
The leaves poppled as the wind picked up. = 风加大时，树叶发出沙沙声。
The champagne poppled merrily in the glass. = 香槟酒在酒杯里欢快地冒着泡。


现在完成时:
The leaves have poppled all afternoon. = 树叶整个下午都在沙沙作响。
The soda has poppled over, making a mess. = 苏打水溢出来了，弄得一团糟。


过去完成时:
The leaves had poppled all morning before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临前，树叶整个上午都在沙沙作响。
The drink had poppled over before I could even taste it. = 我还没来得及尝，饮料就溢出来了。


将来时:
The leaves will popple in the coming wind. = 树叶将在即将到来的风中沙沙作响。
The soda will popple if you shake it too hard. = 如果你用力摇晃苏打水，它就会冒泡。


虚拟语气:
If the wind blew harder, the leaves would popple more loudly. = 如果风吹得更猛烈，树叶就会发出更大的沙沙声。
I wish the bubbles in my drink would popple more. = 我希望我饮料里的气泡能冒得更多一些。


被动语态: (popple本身很少用被动语态)
The sound of poppling leaves was heard throughout the park. = 公园里到处都能听到树叶沙沙作响的声音。(此处是“沙沙声”被听到，而非叶子被“沙沙响”)
The overflowing of the drink was caused by the poppling bubbles. = 饮料溢出是由气泡冒泡引起的。(此处是溢出被解释，而非气泡被“冒泡”)


疑问句:
Do the leaves popple in the wind? = 树叶在风中沙沙作响吗？
Are the bubbles still poppling in the glass? = 酒杯里的气泡还在冒泡吗？


否定句:
The leaves did not popple in the calm air. = 在平静的空气中，树叶没有发出沙沙声。
The drink did not popple at all. = 饮料根本没有冒泡。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "popple".  The word is quite uncommon.
