nonsharing: /ˈnɒnˈʃɛərɪŋ/ | adj. | 不共享的，非共享的 | Not involving or characterized by sharing.  This word describes a situation or system where resources, information, or responsibilities are not shared among individuals or groups. | Example: The nonsharing of resources led to conflict among the team members.  不共享资源导致团队成员之间发生冲突。 The company adopted a nonsharing policy regarding its proprietary software.  公司采用了关于其专有软件的不共享政策。|近义词: unshared, exclusive, private | 反义词: sharing, collaborative, communal |
