filching: /ˈfɪltʃɪŋ/ | v. | 偷偷摸摸地偷窃；窃取 | To steal something in a sneaky or petty way.  |例句：He was caught filching stationery from the office. 他被抓到从办公室偷文具。

例句：They were accused of filching funds from the charity. 他们被指控从慈善机构窃取资金。


近义词：stealing, pilfering, thieving, purloining | 反义词：giving, donating, contributing


词源:  The word "filch" originates from Middle English *filchen,  ultimately from a now-lost Old English word.  Its exact etymology is uncertain, but it's possibly related to words suggesting stealth or cunning.
