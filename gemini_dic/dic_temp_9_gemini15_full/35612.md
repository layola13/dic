coaster:/ˈkoʊstər/| n.|杯垫；过山车；沿海地区居民；沿海地区；|A coaster is a small, usually flat object placed under a glass or cup to protect a surface from spills or scratches. It can also refer to a thrilling amusement park ride.|例句：I need a coaster to protect my table from the condensation on my iced tea. 我需要一个杯垫来保护我的桌子不受冰茶冷凝水的侵蚀。

例句：The roller coaster was a wild ride! 过山车真刺激！
