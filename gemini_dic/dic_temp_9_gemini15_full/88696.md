hobbit:/ˈhɒbɪt/| n. |霍比特人，矮人 | A fictional race of small, furry-footed humanoids in J.R.R. Tolkien's fantasy writings, inhabiting Middle-earth.  They are known for their love of simple pleasures, such as food, drink, and a comfortable home. | Example: Bilbo Baggins is a famous hobbit from The Hobbit. 比尔博·巴金斯是《霍比特人》中著名的霍比特人。   |近义词：dwarf (in some contexts), little person|反义词：giant, giantess
