disclosure's:/dɪˈskloʊʒərz/| n. |公开；揭露；泄露|The act of making something known that was previously secret or hidden. It often implies revealing something potentially embarrassing or damaging.|The disclosure of the company's financial problems led to a sharp drop in its stock price. 公司财务问题的公开导致其股价暴跌。| revelation, exposure, unveiling | concealment, secrecy, suppression |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company makes regular disclosures about its financial performance. = 公司定期公开其财务业绩。
This disclosure reveals a previously unknown fact. = 此次公开揭露了一个以前未知的事实。


一般过去时:
The whistleblower made a crucial disclosure to the authorities. = 举报人向当局作出了至关重要的披露。
The government's disclosure of the classified documents caused a scandal. = 政府泄露机密文件导致了一场丑闻。


现在完成时:
The investigation has revealed several important disclosures. = 调查已经揭露了一些重要的信息。
Recent disclosures have shaken public confidence in the government. = 最近的公开信息动摇了公众对政府的信心。


过去完成时:
Before the trial, the defense had made no disclosures about their strategy. = 在审判之前，被告方没有披露其策略。
It turned out that they had made misleading disclosures earlier. = 事实证明，他们之前做出了误导性的披露。


将来时:
The company will make a full disclosure of its findings next week. = 公司将在下周公布其调查结果。
We will await the disclosure of further details with bated breath. = 我们将屏息等待更多细节的披露。


虚拟语气:
If the company had made an earlier disclosure, the damage might have been less severe. = 如果公司早些时候就公开信息，损失可能就不会那么严重。
I wish they had made a more transparent disclosure of the risks involved. = 我希望他们能更透明地披露所涉及的风险。


被动语态:
The information was disclosed to the press. = 信息被泄露给了媒体。
The details of the agreement were disclosed in a press conference. = 协议的细节在新闻发布会上被披露。


疑问句:
What disclosures have been made so far? = 到目前为止有哪些公开信息？
When will the full disclosure be made? = 全面公开将在何时进行？


否定句:
The company did not disclose the extent of its losses. = 公司没有披露其损失的程度。
There was no disclosure of the conflict of interest. = 没有披露利益冲突。
