claiming:/ˈkleɪmɪŋ/| v.| 声称；索赔；要求 |The act of stating that something is true or is a fact, typically without providing evidence.  Also, the act of demanding something as one's right. |例句：He was claiming to be a doctor. 他声称自己是一名医生。

例句：She is claiming compensation for her injuries. 她正在索赔她的伤亡赔偿。


近义词：asserting, alleging, demanding, requesting

反义词：denying, disclaiming, conceding

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is claiming his lost bag. = 他正在索要他丢失的包。
She claims she saw a ghost. = 她声称她看到鬼了。


一般过去时:
They claimed victory. = 他们宣布胜利。
He claimed to have seen the thief. = 他声称自己看到了小偷。


现在完成时:
She has claimed her prize. = 她已经领取了奖品。
They have claimed responsibility for the attack. = 他们已经对袭击事件承担责任。


过去完成时:
He had claimed the reward before anyone else. = 他在其他人之前就领取了奖励。
She had claimed to know the answer but was wrong. = 她曾声称知道答案，但错了。


将来时:
They will claim their inheritance next week. = 他们下周将继承遗产。
He will claim his right to a fair trial. = 他将要求获得公平审判的权利。


虚拟语气:
If he claimed to be innocent, he would have to prove it. = 如果他声称无罪，他就必须证明这一点。
I wish he hadn't claimed to know everything. = 我真希望他不要自称无所不知。


被动语态:
The prize was claimed by the winner. = 奖品被获胜者领取了。
Responsibility for the accident was claimed by the driver. = 司机承担了事故的责任。


疑问句:
Is he claiming damages? = 他在索赔吗？
What are they claiming? = 他们在声称什么？


否定句:
He didn't claim to be an expert. = 他并没有声称自己是专家。
She didn't claim any responsibility for the mess. = 她没有为这烂摊子承担任何责任。


PHRASE->
lay claim to = 声称拥有
stake a claim = 提出要求
false claim = 虚假主张
insurance claim = 保险索赔
land claim = 土地索赔

词源:
源自古法语 *clamer, 源自拉丁语 *clamare “to shout, cry out”，与“call”同源。  最初的意思是“大声喊叫”，后来引申为“宣布，声明”，最终演变为现代英语中“声称”的意思。
