pirouettes:/ˌpɪruˈɛts/| n. v.|旋转跳跃；(尤指芭蕾舞中的)旋转|A pirouette is a ballet term referring to a complete turn of the body on one leg, typically performed by dancers.  It can also refer to any quick, graceful spin. |例句：The ballerina executed a flawless pirouette.  那位芭蕾舞演员完美地完成了一个旋转跳跃。

The dancer pirouetted across the stage.  舞者在舞台上旋转着跳跃。|近义词：spin, twirl, whirl|反义词：None specifically, but actions implying a lack of grace or speed could be considered antonyms.
