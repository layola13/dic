phyllopoda:/faɪˈlɒpədə/| n. |叶足类动物|Leaf-footed crustaceans, characterized by their flattened, leaf-like appendages.  They are primarily aquatic, inhabiting various freshwater and saltwater environments.  Many are filter feeders, while others are scavengers or predators.  The group includes fairy shrimp, tadpole shrimp, and clam shrimp.|The phyllopoda are a diverse group of crustaceans found in various aquatic habitats.|近义词：branchiopoda (a broader term)|反义词：N/A
