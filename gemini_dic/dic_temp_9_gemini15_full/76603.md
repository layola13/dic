gallivant:/ˈɡælɪvænt/| v.|四处游荡；寻欢作乐|To travel around from place to place, especially in search of pleasure or amusement.|例句：They gallivanted around Europe for three months.  他们游荡在欧洲三个月。|例句：She gallivanted around town with her friends. 她和朋友们在城里四处寻欢作乐。|近义词：roam, wander, travel, revel, carouse|反义词：stay home, remain sedentary


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They gallivant around the world. = 他们周游世界。
He gallivants around town every weekend. = 他每个周末都在城里四处闲逛。

一般过去时:
We gallivanted through the countryside last summer. = 我们去年夏天在乡下四处游荡。
She gallivanted with her friends all night. = 她整晚和朋友们寻欢作乐。

现在完成时:
They have gallivanted across several continents. = 他们已经游历了几个大洲。
He has gallivanted all over the country. = 他已经在全国各地游荡。

过去完成时:
They had gallivanted for years before settling down. = 他们在定居之前已经游荡了很多年。
She had gallivanted extensively before she met her husband. = 在她遇到丈夫之前，她已经广泛地周游各地。

将来时:
We will gallivant around Europe next year. = 我们明年将在欧洲四处游荡。
They will gallivant through South America for six months. = 他们将在南美洲游荡六个月。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would gallivant around the world. = 如果我有钱，我就会周游世界。
I wish I could gallivant carefree like that. = 我真希望我能像那样无忧无虑地四处游荡。

被动语态:
(Gallivant is rarely used in the passive voice.)  (gallivant很少用被动语态。)


疑问句:
Do they often gallivant? = 他们经常四处游荡吗？
Will you gallivant this summer? = 你今年夏天会四处游荡吗？

否定句:
They don't gallivant much anymore. = 他们不再经常四处游荡了。
He didn't gallivant when he was younger. = 他年轻的时候不怎么四处游荡。


PHRASE->

gallivant with =与...一起寻欢作乐
