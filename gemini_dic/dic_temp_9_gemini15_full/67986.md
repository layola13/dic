factfinder:/ˈfæktˌfaɪndər/| n. |事实调查员；事实调查机构|A person or organization that investigates and establishes facts, often in a legal or investigative context.  It can refer to a software tool or a person. |例句：The factfinder meticulously gathered evidence to support their claim. 事实调查员一丝不苟地收集证据来支持他们的说法。The independent factfinder issued a report detailing their findings. 独立的事实调查机构发布了一份详细说明其调查结果的报告。|近义词：investigator, researcher, examiner |反义词：none specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who deliberately misrepresents facts.
