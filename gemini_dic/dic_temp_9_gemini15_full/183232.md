represses:/rɪˈprɛsɪz/ | vt. |压制；抑制；压抑；阻止…的表达|To subdue or restrain; to keep under control.  It often refers to suppressing emotions, thoughts, or desires.|例句：The government represses dissent. = 政府压制异见。

The dictator represses his people's freedom of speech. = 独裁者压制人民的言论自由。


近义词：suppress, subdue, stifle, inhibit, curb | 反义词：express, unleash, release, encourage
