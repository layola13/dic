entraps: /ɪnˈtræps/ | vt. | 诱捕；使陷入困境 | To catch or trap (someone or something).  To involve someone in a difficult or compromising situation from which it is hard to escape. | 例句：The police entrapped the thief with a sting operation. = 警方用钓鱼行动诱捕了小偷。

The clever con artist entrapped his victims with promises of easy money. = 那个狡猾的骗子用轻松赚钱的承诺诱捕了他的受害者。


近义词： ensnare, snare, trap, ensnatch | 反义词： liberate, free, release | 词性: 动词
