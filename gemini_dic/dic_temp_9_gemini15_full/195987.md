sentry: /ˈsɛntri/ | n. |哨兵，岗哨 | A soldier or guard stationed at a place to keep watch.  | Example: The sentry stood guard at the gate.  哨兵在门口站岗。 |近义词：guard, watchman, sentinel |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with terms like "attacker" or "intruder". |名词

词源:
The word "sentry" comes from the Old French word "sentinel," which ultimately derives from the Latin "sentire," meaning "to feel" or "to perceive."  This reflects the sentry's role in perceiving and responding to potential threats.
