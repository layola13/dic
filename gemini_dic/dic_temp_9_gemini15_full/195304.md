semihumanized: /ˌsɛmiˈhjuːmənaɪzd/ | adj. | 半人化的；部分人化的 |  Describes something that has been partially modified or adapted to resemble human characteristics, often in the context of technology or artificial intelligence.  It suggests a blend of human and non-human elements, falling short of full humanization. | 例句：The semihumanized robot displayed surprisingly realistic emotional responses.  这个半人化的机器人展现出令人惊讶的逼真情感反应。


The semihumanized AI assistant could understand and respond to complex queries.  这个半人化的AI助手能够理解并回应复杂的查询。| 近义词：partially humanized, anthropomorphized (in certain contexts) | 反义词：fully humanized, dehumanized, mechanized
