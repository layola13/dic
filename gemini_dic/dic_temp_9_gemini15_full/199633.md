sinocentrism:/ˌsaɪnoʊˈsɛntrɪzəm/| n. |中国中心论；以中国为中心的观点|The belief that China is the center of the world and that other cultures are inferior.  This perspective often involves viewing the world through a lens that prioritizes Chinese culture, history, and values while diminishing or ignoring the contributions of other civilizations.|Example:  His historical analysis suffers from a strong dose of sinocentrism, neglecting the influence of other Asian cultures.  他的历史分析带有浓厚的中国中心主义色彩，忽略了其他亚洲文化的影响。|近义词：ethnocentrism,chauvinism|反义词：cosmopolitanism,multiculturalism
