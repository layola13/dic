nightsticks: /ˈnaɪtstiks/ | n. |警棍|A nightstick is a short, heavy stick, typically made of wood or plastic, carried by police officers as a weapon.  It is used primarily for defense and crowd control.  The term is often used interchangeably with "billy club" or "truncheon."|The officer used his nightstick to break up the fight. 警官用警棍制止了这场打斗。The protesters were met by a line of police officers wielding nightsticks. 抗议者遭到了一排挥舞着警棍的警察的阻拦。|billy club, truncheon, club|—|名词
