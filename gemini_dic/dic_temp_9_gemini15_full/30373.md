centerless:/ˈsɛntərləs/| adj.|无中心的，无核心的|Lacking a center or core; without a central point or focus.  It can also describe something lacking direction or purpose.|例句：The argument was centerless, drifting from one irrelevant point to another.  论点不着边际，从一个无关紧要的点飘到另一个点。

The organization lacked a strong leader and felt centerless. 这个组织缺乏强有力的领导，感觉毫无中心。|近义词：unfocused, disorganized, aimless|反义词：focused, centralized, organized
