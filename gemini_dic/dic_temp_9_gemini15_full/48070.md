declaratives:/dɪˈklærətɪvz/| adj.| 陈述句的；声明的 | Relating to or characterized by declarative sentences; stating something directly.  Declarative sentences make statements. | Example:  Declarative sentences are the most common type of sentence. 陈述句是最常见的句子类型。 | 近义词：assertive, affirmative | 反义词：interrogative, imperative


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a doctor. = 他是一名医生。
The sun rises in the east. = 太阳从东方升起。

一般过去时:
She went to the store. = 她去了商店。
They played soccer yesterday. = 他们昨天踢足球。

现在完成时:
I have finished my work. = 我已经完成了我的工作。
We have lived here for ten years. = 我们在这里住了十年了。

过去完成时:
The movie had already started when we arrived. = 我们到达的时候电影已经开始了。
She had eaten dinner before she went to bed. = 她睡觉前已经吃过晚饭了。

将来时:
He will travel to Europe next year. = 他明年将去欧洲旅行。
They will meet us at the airport. = 他们将在机场接我们。

虚拟语气:
If I were rich, I would travel the world. = 如果我有钱，我会周游世界。
It's time we went home. = 我们该回家了。

被动语态:
The house was built last year. = 这房子是去年建的。
The problem is being discussed now. = 这个问题现在正在讨论中。

疑问句:
(Declaratives cannot be directly converted to questions.  Declarative sentences make statements, not questions.)
