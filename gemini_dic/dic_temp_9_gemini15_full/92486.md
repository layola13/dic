hypothec:/ˈhaɪpəθɛk/| n.|抵押；动产抵押|A hypothec is a right granted by law to a creditor to retain possession of property belonging to a debtor until a debt is paid.  It's a type of security interest, often used in civil law systems.|例句：The bank holds a hypothec on the farmer's crops until the loan is repaid.  银行持有对农民农作物的抵押权，直到贷款偿还。|例句：The landlord has a hypothec on the tenant's belongings for unpaid rent. 房东对租客的物品拥有抵押权，以偿还未付租金。|近义词：lien, pledge, security, mortgage|反义词：unsecured loan|名词
