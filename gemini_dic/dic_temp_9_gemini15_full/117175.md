measles:/ˈmiːzəlz/| n.|麻疹|A highly contagious infectious disease, especially of children, caused by a paramyxovirus and characterized by fever, cough, and a distinctive red rash.|例句：The measles outbreak was quickly contained by the health authorities.  卫生部门迅速控制了麻疹疫情。|例句：He was diagnosed with measles and had to stay home from school. 他被诊断出患有麻疹，不得不从学校回家。|近义词：rubella, rubeola|反义词：无
