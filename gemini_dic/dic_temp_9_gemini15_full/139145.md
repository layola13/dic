obeyed:/oʊˈbeɪd/ | v. | 服从；遵守 | Past tense of "obey".  It means to comply with a command, request, or law. |  The children obeyed their parents. 孩子们听从了父母的话。 | The soldiers obeyed orders without question. 士兵们毫无疑问地服从命令。 | follow, comply with | disobey, defy, rebel | 动词

He obeys the rules. 他遵守规则。
She obeys her doctor's orders. 她遵从医生的医嘱。

They obeyed the law. 他们遵守法律。
