re-inflated:/ˌriːɪnˈfleɪtɪd/| v. |重新充气；使重新膨胀；使重新充满活力 |To inflate something again after it has been deflated.  This implies a return to a previous state of inflation. |例句：The tire was re-inflated after the puncture was repaired. 轮胎在修补好扎破的地方后重新充气了。The economy was re-inflated after a period of recession. 经济在一段时间的衰退后重新恢复了活力。|近义词：re-inflate, refill,  replenish |反义词：deflate, deplete
