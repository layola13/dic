palometa:/ˌpæləˈmɛtə/| n. |小鲹鱼，飞鱼|A small, silvery fish, often found in schools,  known for its ability to leap from the water.  It is a type of jackfish.  The term is predominantly used in Spanish and Portuguese speaking regions.| 例句：The fishermen caught a large school of palometa.  渔民捕到了一大群小鲹鱼。|  例句：We saw several palometa leaping out of the water. 我们看到几条小鲹鱼跃出水面。|近义词：jackfish, skipjack (depending on the specific species)|反义词：无|名词
