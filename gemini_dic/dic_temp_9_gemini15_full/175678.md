rapacious:/rəˈpeɪʃəs/| adj.|贪婪的；掠夺成性的；强夺的|Excessively greedy or grasping; predatory.  Characterized by or tending toward aggressive self-seeking, often at the expense of others.|例句：The rapacious corporation swallowed up smaller companies to expand its market share.  这家贪婪的公司吞并了规模较小的公司以扩大其市场份额。

例句：The rapacious general led his army on a campaign of plunder and destruction. 这名贪婪的将军率领他的军队进行了一场掠夺和破坏的战役。

近义词：greedy, avaricious, predatory, voracious, covetous|反义词：generous, altruistic, benevolent, magnanimous


词源:
The word "rapacious" comes from the Latin word "rapāx," meaning "seizing," "greedy," or "ravenous."  This in turn derives from the verb "rapere," meaning "to seize," "to snatch," or "to carry off."  The suffix "-acious" is a common adjective-forming suffix in English, indicating a full or marked degree of a particular quality.
