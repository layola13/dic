self-promoter:/ˈsɛlf prəˈmoʊtər/ | n. |自我推销者，自我宣传者 | A person who actively promotes themselves and their achievements, often excessively.  They may seek publicity or recognition for their accomplishments, sometimes at the expense of others. |例句：He's a notorious self-promoter, always boasting about his successes. 他是个臭名昭著的自我推销者，总是吹嘘自己的成功。|例句：The company hired a self-promoter to head their marketing campaign.  这家公司聘请了一位自我推销者来领导他们的营销活动。|近义词：egotist, braggart, self-advertiser |反义词：self-effacer, humble person
