misdated:/ˌmɪsˈdeɪtɪd/| adj.| 日期错误的，误记日期的 | Having the wrong date; incorrectly dated.  |例句：The letter was misdated by several years. 这封信的日期记错了好几年。

例句：The check was misdated; it was written in 2022 but dated 2023. 这张支票的日期写错了；它是2022年写的，但日期却是2023年。


近义词：incorrectly dated, wrongly dated, dated incorrectly

反义词：correctly dated, accurately dated


词性:形容词
