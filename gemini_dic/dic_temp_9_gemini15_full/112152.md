look down on:/lʊk daʊn ɒn/| v.|轻视，瞧不起|To regard someone or something as inferior or unimportant.  This verb phrase implies a feeling of superiority or disdain towards the object of the action.|He looks down on anyone who hasn't had a university education. 他瞧不起任何没有上过大学的人。|despise, scorn, disdain|respect, admire, esteem|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They look down on people from poorer backgrounds. = 他们看不起来自贫困背景的人。
She looks down on those who don't share her interests. = 她看不起那些不和她有共同兴趣的人。


一般过去时:
He looked down on his colleagues for their lack of experience. = 他因为同事缺乏经验而看不起他们。
The manager looked down on employees who arrived late. = 经理看不起迟到的员工。


现在完成时:
They have always looked down on immigrants. = 他们一直看不起移民。
Society has looked down on single mothers for too long. = 社会长期以来一直看不起单亲母亲。


过去完成时:
She had always looked down on him, even before he lost his job. = 她在他失业之前就一直看不起他。
The aristocracy had looked down on the newly rich for generations. = 上流社会几代人都看不起暴发户。


将来时:
They will continue to look down on anyone different from themselves. = 他们将继续看不起任何与他们不同的人。
People will always look down on those who are less fortunate. = 人们总是会看不起那些比较不幸的人。


虚拟语气:
If I were rich, I wouldn't look down on the poor. = 如果我有钱，我就不会看不起穷人。
I wish I didn't look down on people who are different from me. = 我希望我不看不起与我不同的人。


被动语态:
Immigrants are often looked down on in this country. = 在这个国家，移民经常受到轻视。
The underprivileged are often looked down upon by the wealthy. = 弱势群体经常被富人轻视。


疑问句:
Do you look down on people who have different beliefs? = 你看不起信仰不同的人吗？
Why do some people look down on others? = 为什么有些人看不起其他人？


否定句:
We don't look down on anyone, regardless of their background. = 无论其背景如何，我们都不会看不起任何人。
He doesn't look down on people who make mistakes. = 他不会看不起犯错的人。


PHRASE->
