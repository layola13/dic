cumbent:/ˈkʌmbənt/| adj.|卧着的；俯卧的；就任的；在位的|Lying down; reclining; incumbent (in office).  The word describes something that is lying down or reclining, often in a position of rest or submission. It also refers to someone who holds an office or position. |例句：The lion lay cumbent in the tall grass.  狮子卧在高高的草丛中。The cumbent president faced a tough reelection campaign.  现任总统面临一场艰难的竞选连任。|近义词：reclining, prostrate, incumbent (in the sense of holding office)|反义词：erect, upright, standing
