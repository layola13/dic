hyperlinks:/ˈhaɪpərˌlɪŋks/| n.|超链接，外部链接|A hyperlink is a link from a hypertext document to another location, typically activated by clicking on a highlighted word or image.|Example:  This sentence contains a hyperlink to Wikipedia. 这句话包含一个指向维基百科的超链接。|近义词：link, URL|反义词：None
