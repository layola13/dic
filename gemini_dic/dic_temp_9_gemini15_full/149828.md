paleontologist:/ˌpeɪliɒnˈtɒlədʒɪst/| n. |古生物学家|A scientist who studies fossils to learn about life in prehistoric times.  |例句：Mary Anning was a renowned paleontologist who made significant discoveries in the early 19th century. 玛丽·安宁是一位著名的古生物学家，在19世纪早期取得了重大发现。|例句：The paleontologist carefully examined the dinosaur bones. 古生物学家仔细检查了恐龙骨骼。|近义词：fossil expert, paleozoologist (if specializing in animals), paleobotanist (if specializing in plants)|反义词：None
