remixture: /ˈriːmɪkstʃər/ | n. |混音，重新混音的版本 | A remixed version of a piece of music; the act of remixing.  A remixture involves taking existing audio recordings and manipulating them to create a new musical work. This often involves changing the tempo, adding or removing instruments, and rearranging the song's structure. | Example: The DJ played a killer remixture of that old disco track.  |近义词：remix,  re-edit | 反义词：original version
