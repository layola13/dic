eager-beaver: /ˈiːɡər ˈbiːvər/ | n. | 积极分子，拼命工作的人 | An eager beaver is someone who is very enthusiastic and works extremely hard, often to an excessive degree.  They are typically diligent and proactive, but sometimes their eagerness can be seen as overly zealous or annoying. |  He's a real eager beaver; he always volunteers for extra work. 他是个十足的积极分子；他总是自愿承担额外的工作。  She's an eager beaver, always the first to arrive and the last to leave. 她是个拼命工作的人，总是第一个到，最后一个走。 | hard worker, go-getter, overachiever | lazy person, slacker
