housekeepers:/ˈhaʊskiːpərz/| n.|管家；女管家；清洁工|A housekeeper is a person employed to manage a household, typically including cleaning, cooking, and other domestic tasks.  They may work in private homes or commercial settings like hotels.|例句：The housekeeper cleaned the entire house before the guests arrived.  管家在客人到达之前打扫了整个房子。|近义词：maid, domestic, cleaner|反义词：N/A
