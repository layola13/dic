seafarers:/ˈsiːˌfɛərərz/| n.| 海员，水手 |People who work on ships as their profession.  They are skilled in navigation, sailing, and other maritime tasks.|例句：Many seafarers risked their lives during the war. 许多海员在战争期间冒着生命危险。|例句：The life of a seafarer is often challenging and unpredictable. 海员的生活通常充满挑战且难以预测。|近义词：sailors, mariners, seamen |反义词：landlubber
