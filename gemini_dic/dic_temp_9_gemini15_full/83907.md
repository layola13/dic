half-bath:/ˈhæfˌbæθ/| n. |半个浴室；简易卫生间|A half-bath is a bathroom with a toilet and a sink, but no shower or bathtub.  It's often found in smaller homes or as a powder room for guests.|例句：The guest house only has a half-bath. 客房只有一个简易卫生间。|The master bathroom has a full bath, while the downstairs has a half-bath. 主卧浴室是完整的浴室，而楼下只有一个简易卫生间。|近义词：powder room, restroom (informal)|反义词：full bath
