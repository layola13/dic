faints:/feɪnts/| v.|昏倒；变得虚弱；(光线等)变弱 | to lose consciousness for a short time because of illness, weakness, or shock.  |例句：She fainted from the heat. 她热晕过去了。|例句：The light fainted as the sun went down. 随着太阳下山，光线变弱了。|近义词：swoons, collapses, blacks out |反义词：remains conscious, revives


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He faints easily in hot weather. 他在炎热的天气里很容易晕倒。
The light faints gradually as the sun sets.  随着太阳下山，光线逐渐变弱。


一般过去时:
She fainted when she heard the news.  听到这个消息她晕倒了。
The colors in the painting fainted with age. 画中的颜色因年代久远而褪色。


现在完成时:
He has fainted twice this week. 他本周已经晕倒两次了。
The once-bright colors have fainted considerably.  曾经鲜艳的颜色已经褪色不少。


过去完成时:
She had fainted before anyone could help her.  在她还没来得及得到帮助之前就晕倒了。
The painting had fainted significantly before it was restored.  这幅画在修复之前已经严重褪色了。


将来时:
He thinks he will faint if he sees blood. 他认为如果他看到血就会晕倒。
The light will faint as the storm clouds gather.  随着暴风雨云的聚集，光线将会变弱。


虚拟语气:
If I saw a snake, I think I would faint. 如果我看到一条蛇，我想我会晕倒。
I wish the colors hadn't fainted so much.  我希望颜色不会褪色那么多。


被动语态:
He was fainted by the sight of blood. 他被血的景象吓晕了。(此用法较少见，通常用主动语态)
The design was fainted by repeated washings.  图案因反复清洗而褪色。(此用法更常见于指颜色或图案的褪色)


疑问句:
Did she faint during the ceremony?  她在仪式上晕倒了吗？
Has the color of the fabric fainted? 布料的颜色褪色了吗？


否定句:
He didn't faint, despite feeling dizzy. 尽管感到头晕，他却没有晕倒。
The dye didn't faint even after many washes.  即使经过多次清洗，染料也没有褪色。

faint scent: 淡香
