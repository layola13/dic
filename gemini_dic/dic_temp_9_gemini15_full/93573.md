imago:/ˈɪmæɡoʊ/| n. |成虫，影像，理想形象 |The adult stage of an insect, especially after metamorphosis.  Also refers to a mental image or idealized representation.|例句：The butterfly is the imago of the caterpillar. 蝴蝶是毛虫的成虫阶段。  The artist struggled to capture the imago of his muse on canvas.艺术家努力在画布上捕捉他缪斯的形象。|近义词：adult, image, ideal|反义词：larva, pupa
