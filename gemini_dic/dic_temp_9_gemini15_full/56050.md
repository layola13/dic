docent:/ˈdoʊsnt/| n. |讲解员，教师 |A docent is a person who acts as a guide, especially in a museum, art gallery, or zoo, and provides information and explanations to visitors.  |The docent led us through the museum, explaining the history of each exhibit.  讲解员带领我们参观了博物馆，解释了每个展品的历史。|guide, lecturer, instructor |student, learner


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The docent guides the tour group through the gallery. = 讲解员带领旅游团参观画廊。
He explains the artwork to visitors. = 他向游客解释艺术品。


一般过去时:
The docent guided the group yesterday. = 讲解员昨天带领了这个团队。
She explained the painting's symbolism. = 她解释了这幅画的象征意义。


现在完成时:
The docent has guided hundreds of tours. = 这位讲解员已经带领了数百次旅游。
She has explained this exhibit many times. = 她已经多次解释过这个展览。


过去完成时:
The docent had already finished the tour before the museum closed. = 在博物馆关闭之前，讲解员已经完成了讲解。
She had explained the key concepts before the questions began. = 在提问开始之前，她已经解释了关键概念。


将来时:
The docent will lead the tour tomorrow. = 讲解员明天将带领参观。
He will explain the process of creating the sculpture. = 他将解释雕塑创作的过程。


虚拟语气:
If the docent were here, she would answer your questions. = 如果讲解员在这里，她会回答你的问题。
I wish the docent had explained the artist's background more fully. = 我希望讲解员能更全面地解释一下这位艺术家的背景。


被动语态:
The tour was guided by a knowledgeable docent. =  这次旅行由一位知识渊博的讲解员带领。
The artwork was explained clearly by the docent. = 讲解员清楚地解释了这件艺术品。


疑问句:
Is the docent available to answer questions? = 讲解员有空回答问题吗？
Did the docent explain the significance of this artifact? = 讲解员解释了这件文物的意义吗？


否定句:
The docent did not explain the controversial aspects of the artwork. = 讲解员没有解释这件艺术品的有争议的方面。
She wasn't able to answer all the visitors' questions. = 她无法回答所有游客的问题。
