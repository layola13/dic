levying:/ˈleviɪŋ/| v.|征收；课税；强加|The act of imposing or collecting a tax, fee, or levy.  It can also refer to imposing something forcefully. |例句：The government is levying a new tax on sugary drinks. 政府正在对含糖饮料征收新税。

例句：The school is levying a fee for extracurricular activities. 学校对课外活动收取费用。


近义词：imposing, exacting, collecting, assessing|反义词：remitting, refunding, abolishing|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government levies taxes on income. = 政府对收入征税。
They levy a surcharge on late payments. = 他们对逾期付款收取附加费。


一般过去时:
The king levied heavy taxes on his subjects. = 国王向他的臣民征收重税。
They levied a fine for the traffic violation. = 他们因交通违规而处以罚款。


现在完成时:
The government has levied new tariffs on imported goods. = 政府对进口商品征收了新的关税。
They have levied a significant amount of money for charity. = 他们为慈善机构筹集了大量资金。


过去完成时:
The empire had levied taxes for centuries before its collapse. = 这个帝国在其崩溃之前已经征税几个世纪了。
They had levied a tax on land ownership before the war. = 他们曾在战争前对土地所有权征税。


将来时:
The council will levy a new charge for parking. = 理事会将对停车征收新的费用。
The company will levy fines for non-compliance. = 公司将对违规行为处以罚款。


虚拟语气:
If they levied a higher tax, they might have more revenue. = 如果他们征收更高的税，他们可能会获得更多收入。
I wish they hadn't levied such a harsh penalty. = 我希望他们没有处以如此严厉的处罚。


被动语态:
Taxes are levied by the government. = 税收由政府征收。
A fee was levied for the service. = 该项服务收取费用。


疑问句:
Are they levying a new tax this year? = 他们今年要征收新税吗？
What fees are being levied for this event? = 这次活动收取什么费用？


否定句:
The government is not levying any new taxes this year. = 政府今年没有征收任何新税。
They did not levy a fine for the minor infraction. = 他们没有因轻微违规而处以罚款。
