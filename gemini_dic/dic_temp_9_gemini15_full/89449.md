honors:/ˈɑːnərz/ | n. |荣誉，尊敬，优等成绩 | Honors are given to those who deserve them. 荣誉给予那些应得的人。 |近义词：awards, distinctions, accolades |反义词：dishonor, disgrace, shame


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He honors his commitments. 他信守承诺。
She honors her parents. 她孝敬父母。


一般过去时:
They honored the agreement. 他们遵守了协议。

过去完成时:
