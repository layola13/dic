overawe:/ˌoʊvərˈɔː/|/ˌəʊvəˈɔː/| vt. |使敬畏；使惊叹；威慑 |To inspire awe or admiration in (someone), often to the point of overwhelming them.  It often implies a sense of being overpowered or humbled by something impressive or powerful. |例句：The sheer scale of the mountain range overawed the climbers.  群山的巨大规模使登山者敬畏不已。

The grandeur of the ancient temple overawed the tourists. 古庙的宏伟景象使游客们惊叹不已。|近义词：impress, overwhelm, awe, intimidate |反义词：underwhelm, bore, disappoint |动词
