ozonification:/ˌoʊzəʊnɪˈfeɪʃən/| n. |臭氧化；用臭氧处理|The process of treating something with ozone, typically to disinfect or purify it.  This process involves the application of ozone gas (O3) to achieve desired effects such as disinfection, oxidation, or deodorization.|例句：The water treatment plant uses ozonification to kill harmful bacteria.  该水处理厂使用臭氧处理来杀死有害细菌。|近义词：ozonation, sterilization, disinfection|反义词：contamination, pollution|
