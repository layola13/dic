hidability:/ˌhɪdəˈbɪləti/ | n. |隐藏性，隐蔽性 |The ability to be hidden or concealed; the degree to which something can be hidden.  This refers to the ease with which something can be hidden, and the effectiveness of its concealment.  |例句：The low hidability of the base made it vulnerable to attack. 低隐蔽性使得基地容易受到攻击。

The high hidability of the chameleon in its natural environment allows it to evade predators. 变色龙在其自然环境中的高隐蔽性使其能够躲避捕食者。

|近义词：concealment, secretiveness, obscurity |反义词：visibility, exposure, openness |名词
