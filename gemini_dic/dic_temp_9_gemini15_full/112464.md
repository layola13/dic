lough:/lɒx/| n.|湖泊，海湾|A lough is a lake, especially one in Ireland or Scotland.  The term often refers to an inlet of the sea, similar to a fjord or loch. |例句：We spent the afternoon swimming in the lough. 我们在湖里游泳度过了下午。  The picturesque lough was surrounded by rolling hills.  风景如画的湖泊被连绵起伏的丘陵环绕着。|近义词：lake, loch, loch, fjord|反义词：N/A|名词
