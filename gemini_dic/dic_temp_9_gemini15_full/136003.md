nonrationalistic:/ˌnɒnˌræʃənəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. |非理性主义的|Not based on or conforming to rational principles or methods; opposed to rationalism.  It describes something that rejects or ignores reason and logic as the primary basis for belief or action.  The term often implies a reliance on intuition, emotion, or faith instead.|The nonrationalistic approach to decision-making prioritized gut feeling over data analysis.  非理性主义的决策方法优先考虑直觉而非数据分析。|近义词：irrational, illogical, unreasonable, intuitive, emotional |反义词：rational, logical, reasonable, reasoned
