munificent:/mjuˈnɪfɪsnt/| adj.|慷慨的， щедрый | generous or lavish in giving; characterized by great generosity |The munificent donation allowed the charity to build a new hospital.  这位慷慨的捐赠使慈善机构得以建造一所新医院。|近义词：generous, bountiful, lavish, liberal |反义词：stingy, miserly, parsimonious

词源:
Middle English: from Old French *munificent, from Latin munificus, from munus ‘gift’ + facere ‘to make’.

用法说明:

例句：
The king was known for his munificent gifts to the poor. 国王以其对穷人的慷慨捐赠而闻名。

His munificent hospitality made everyone feel welcome. 他慷慨的款待使每个人都感到宾至如归。


近义词：
generous, bountiful, lavish, liberal, magnanimous


反义词：
stingy, miserly, parsimonious, frugal, niggardly


词性：
形容词
