legatura:/ləˈɡɑːtʃʊərə/| n. |连音，连字|A legatura is a musical notation indicating that two or more notes should be played or sung smoothly and connectedly, without a break between them.  It often involves slurring notes together.  This creates a legato style of performance.|Example: The composer used many legaturas to create a flowing melodic line.  作曲家使用了许多连音来创造流畅的旋律线条。|近义词：slur, tie, connection|反义词：staccato
