demystifying: /ˌdiːˈmɪstɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 解除神秘感；使易于理解 | Making something easier to understand by explaining it clearly and removing any mystery or confusion.  | Example:  The professor's lecture on quantum physics was surprisingly demystifying.  教授关于量子物理学的讲座令人惊讶地易于理解。 | 近义词：clarifying, elucidating, explaining, simplifying | 反义词：obscuring, mystifying, confusing
