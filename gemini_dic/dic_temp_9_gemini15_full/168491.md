probiotics:/ˌproʊbaɪˈɒtɪks/| n.|益生菌|Live microorganisms that, when administered in adequate amounts, confer a health benefit on the host.  益生菌是指当以足够的量给予时，能对宿主产生健康益处的活微生物。|Example:  Taking probiotics can improve gut health.服用益生菌可以改善肠道健康。

|近义词：beneficial bacteria,  good bacteria|反义词：pathogens, harmful bacteria|词性:noun
