dabber:/ˈdæbər/ | n. |轻拍者；涂抹器；印章|A person or thing that dabs.  A small pad or sponge for applying something, such as ink or paint. |例句：She used a dabber to apply the paint to the canvas. 她用涂抹器将颜料涂在画布上。  He is a skilled dabber of ink. 他是一位熟练的印章工。|近义词：applicator, sponge, pad |反义词：N/A
