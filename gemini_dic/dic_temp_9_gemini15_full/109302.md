lend-a-hand: /lɛnd ə hænd/ | v. | 帮忙，协助 | To give assistance or support.  | Example:  I'll lend a hand with the moving if you need it. 我需要的话我会帮忙搬家的。  He decided to lend a hand to the homeless shelter. 他决定帮助收容所。 | 近义词：assist, help, aid | 反义词：hinder, obstruct | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I lend a hand to my neighbors whenever they need it. = 我在邻居需要的时候都会帮忙。
She lends a hand with the cooking every Sunday. = 她每个星期天都帮忙做饭。

一般过去时:
He lent a hand to his friend who was fixing his car. = 他帮助他的朋友修理汽车。
They lent a hand at the community cleanup. = 他们在社区清洁活动中帮忙。

现在完成时:
I have lent a hand to many people in need over the years. = 多年来，我帮助过许多需要帮助的人。
She has lent a hand to various charities. = 她已经帮助过各种慈善机构。

过去完成时:
I had lent a hand to him before he even asked. = 在他开口之前我就已经帮过他了。
They had lent a hand to the project before the deadline. = 他们在大限之前就帮助了这个项目。

将来时:
I will lend a hand with your project next week. = 我下周会帮你做项目。
He will lend a hand at the school fundraiser. = 他将在学校筹款活动中帮忙。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would lend a hand. = 如果我有时间，我会帮忙的。
I wish I could lend a hand, but I'm busy. = 我真希望我能帮忙，但我太忙了。

被动语态:
A hand was lent to the victims of the flood. = 洪水的受害者得到了帮助。
Assistance was lent to the struggling business. = 陷入困境的企业得到了援助。

疑问句:
Will you lend a hand with the dishes? = 你能帮忙洗碗吗？
Can anyone lend a hand with this heavy box? = 有谁能帮忙搬这个沉重的箱子吗？

否定句:
I couldn't lend a hand because I was ill. = 我因为生病所以无法帮忙。
She didn't lend a hand because she was too tired. = 她因为太累了所以没有帮忙。
