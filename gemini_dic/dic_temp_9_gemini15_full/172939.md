qualifier:/ˈkwɒlɪfaɪər/ /ˈkwɑːlɪfaɪər/| n.|限定词，修饰语，资格赛选手|A word or phrase that limits or modifies the meaning of another word or phrase.  It can be an adjective, adverb, or phrase that specifies or restricts the scope of something.|例句：The adjective "small" is a qualifier in the phrase "a small car".  形容词“小”在短语“一辆小汽车”中是一个限定词。

例句：In the sentence "He is a highly qualified surgeon," "highly" is a qualifier, modifying "qualified". 在句子“他是一位高素质的外科医生”中，“高度”是一个限定词，修饰“合格”。


近义词：modifier, restrictor, specifier, determinant|反义词：generalizer, broadener|词性：noun
