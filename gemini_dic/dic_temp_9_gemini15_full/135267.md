nonpareil:/ˌnɒnˈpærəl/| adj. n.|无与伦比的；无比的；一流的；|Unequalled; surpassing all others.  It is often used to describe something exceptional or unique. |例句：Her beauty is nonpareil.  她的美貌无与伦比。

例句：His performance was a nonpareil display of skill. 他的表演是技艺的无与伦比的展示。


近义词：unrivaled, unparalleled, unsurpassed, matchless, peerless
