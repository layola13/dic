quincuncially:/kwɪnˈkʌnsɪəli/| adv.|成梅花形地，成斜列地| Arranged in a quincunx pattern; diagonally or in a staggered arrangement.  The term refers to the arrangement of five objects with four at the corners of a square and one in the center, often used to describe planting patterns or arrangements. |例句：The trees were planted quincuncially across the field.  树木成梅花形地种植在田野里。 The chess pieces were arranged quincuncially on the board.  棋子斜列地摆放在棋盘上。|近义词：diagonally, staggered, obliquely|反义词：linearly, squarely
