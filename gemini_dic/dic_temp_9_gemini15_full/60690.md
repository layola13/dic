electrifications:/ɪˌlɛktrɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n.|电气化；通电|The process of converting to electric power.  It refers to the widespread adoption of electric power in various sectors, such as transportation, industry, and homes.|例句：The government is investing heavily in the electrification of the railway network.  政府正在大力投资铁路网络的电气化建设。

例句：The electrification of rural areas has improved the quality of life for many people.  农村地区的电气化提高了许多人的生活质量。

近义词：electrification, energization

反义词：de-electrification, de-energization

词性：名词
