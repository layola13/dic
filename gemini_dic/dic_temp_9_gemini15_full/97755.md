initiates:/ɪˈnɪʃieɪts/ | vt. n.| 开始；发起；介绍；使入门 | To begin or start something; to introduce someone to something; to admit someone into a group or organization. |例句：The company initiated a new marketing campaign. 这家公司发起了一场新的营销活动。  |例句：She initiated the conversation by asking about his family. 她以询问他的家人开始了谈话。|近义词：begin, commence, start, introduce |反义词：conclude, end, finish |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He initiates new projects every year. 他每年都会启动新的项目。
The teacher initiates the students into the mysteries of algebra. 老师向学生们介绍代数的奥秘。


一般过去时:
The government initiated reforms to the healthcare system. 政府启动了医疗体系改革。
They initiated me into the secret society. 他们把我介绍进了这个秘密社团。


现在完成时:
She has initiated several successful businesses. 她已经创办了几家成功的企业。
They have initiated a dialogue with the opposing party. 他们已经和对方展开了对话。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, he had initiated contact with the other delegates. 会议之前，他已经与其他代表取得了联系。
The team had initiated the project months ago.  团队几个月前就启动了这个项目。


将来时:
The company will initiate legal proceedings. 公司将启动法律程序。
They will initiate a new research program next year. 他们明年将启动一个新的研究项目。


虚拟语气:
If he initiated the plan, it would have been a success. 如果他启动了这个计划，它就会成功。
I wish they had initiated the project earlier. 我希望他们早点启动这个项目。


被动语态:
The project was initiated last month. 该项目在上个月启动。
He was initiated into the club last year. 他去年加入了这个俱乐部。


疑问句:
When did they initiate the negotiations? 他们什么时候开始谈判的？
Who initiated the discussion? 谁发起了讨论？


否定句:
The company did not initiate the merger.  公司没有启动合并。
He has not initiated any contact with them. 他没有与他们联系。
