onward:/ˈɒnwəd/ /ˈɑːnwɚd/| adv.| 向前；继续；以后| Moving or directed toward a point further on; further on or ahead in time or space.  |例句：The army marched onward.  军队继续前进。

例句：We must press onward despite the difficulties.  尽管困难重重，我们必须继续前进。


近义词：forward, ahead, forth, onwards| 反义词：backward, back


PHRASE->
onward and upward:不断进步
from now onward:从现在开始
onward journey:继续旅程
press onward:继续前进
look onward:展望未来
