networking: /ˈnɛtwɜːrkɪŋ/ | n. | 网络；人际关系网；人脉 | The process of building and maintaining professional relationships, often with the goal of career advancement or business development.  It involves connecting with people in your field or industry to exchange information, build rapport, and create opportunities. |  例句：She attended the networking event to make connections with potential clients. = 她参加了这个社交活动，目的是与潜在客户建立联系。

例句：Networking is crucial for career success in many fields. = 在许多领域，人脉关系网对于职业成功至关重要。


近义词：socializing, connecting, fraternizing | 反义词：isolation, seclusion

词源:
The word "networking" emerged in the late 20th century as a combination of "network" and "-ing."  "Network" itself has roots in the Old English "net," referring to a woven fabric, evolving through Middle English and Old French to describe interconnected systems, and eventually encompassing social and professional connections.
