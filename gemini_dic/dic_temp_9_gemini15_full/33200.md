chromosphere:/ˈkroʊməˌsfɪr/| n. |色球层|The chromosphere is the relatively thin layer of the sun's atmosphere that lies just above the photosphere and below the corona. It is characterized by a reddish glow and is only visible during a total solar eclipse or with specialized instruments.  |Example: During a total solar eclipse, the chromosphere can be seen as a thin, reddish ring around the moon.  在日全食期间，色球层可以被看到像围绕月亮的一圈薄薄的红色环。|近义词：solar atmosphere (部分)|反义词：None|
