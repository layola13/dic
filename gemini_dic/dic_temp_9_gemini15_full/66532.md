exhaustions:/ɪɡˈzɔːʃənz/ | n. | 精疲力竭；耗尽；衰竭 | The state of being extremely tired and having no energy left.  It can also refer to the act of using something up completely. |例句：After running the marathon, he experienced complete exhaustion.  马拉松跑完后，他精疲力竭。

例句：The exhaustion of natural resources is a serious problem. 天然资源的枯竭是一个严重的问题。


近义词：fatigue, tiredness, depletion, drain

反义词：energy, vigor, revitalization

词性: 名词
