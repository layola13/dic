siphoning: /ˈsaɪfənɪŋ/ | v. | 抽吸；窃取；转移 | The act of transferring liquid from one container to another using a siphon;  the act of secretly or illegally taking something, especially money. |例句：The thieves were siphoning money from the company's accounts.  窃贼正在从公司的账户中窃取资金。

They were siphoning petrol from the tanker. 他们在从油罐车里偷油。


近义词：drawing off, transferring, pilfering, embezzling | 反义词：depositing, contributing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He siphons gas from his neighbor's car. 他从邻居的车里偷汽油。
They siphon money from the charity. 他们挪用慈善机构的钱。


一般过去时:
She siphoned the water from the tank. 她把水从水箱里抽了出来。
The criminals siphoned millions from the bank. 罪犯们从银行盗取了数百万美元。


现在完成时:
He has siphoned off all the profits. 他已经把所有的利润都抽走了。
They have been siphoning money for years. 他们多年来一直在挪用资金。


过去完成时:
They had siphoned the fuel before the police arrived. 警察到达之前，他们已经把燃料抽走了。
The accountant had siphoned off a large sum of money. 会计已经挪用了一大笔钱。


将来时:
They will siphon the water into the new container. 他们会把水抽到新的容器里。
He will siphon money to his offshore account. 他会把钱转移到他的海外账户。


虚拟语气:
If I had a siphon, I would siphon the wine. 如果我有一个虹吸管，我会把酒抽出来。
If they had not siphoned the funds, the company would still be in business. 如果他们没有挪用资金，公司仍然会运营。


被动语态:
The water was siphoned from the pool. 水是从游泳池里抽出来的。
The money was siphoned out of the account. 钱从账户中被转移走了。


疑问句:
Are they siphoning the oil? 他们在抽油吗？
Was the money siphoned illegally? 钱是非法转移的吗？


否定句:
They did not siphon the gasoline. 他们没有偷汽油。
He didn't siphon the funds from the account. 他没有从账户中挪用资金。


PHRASE->
siphon off: 抽走，转移
siphon out: 抽出，汲取
siphon away: 逐渐转移，慢慢抽走
siphon from: 从...抽取
secretly siphon: 秘密转移


词源:
From the Greek word "siphon," meaning a tube or pipe used to convey liquid.  The modern English word retains this original meaning, and it has developed to also include the figurative sense of secretly transferring something.
