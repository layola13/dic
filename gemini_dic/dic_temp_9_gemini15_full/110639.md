limps:/lɪmps/| v.|跛行，蹒跚|To walk with difficulty because of a leg injury or weakness.|例句：He limps because of his injured ankle. 他因为脚踝受伤而跛行。|例句：The old dog limps noticeably after long walks. 老狗长途跋涉后明显地跛行。|近义词： hobble, limp along, shuffle|反义词：stride, walk normally


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He limps every day. = 他每天都跛着走。
She limps when she walks fast. = 她快走时会跛行。

一般过去时:
He limped home after the accident. = 事故后他跛着回家了。
She limped across the finish line. = 她一瘸一拐地穿过终点线。

现在完成时:
He has limped since the operation. = 他手术后就一直跛着走。
She has limped for years because of arthritis. = 多年来她一直因为关节炎而跛行。

过去完成时:
He had limped for miles before he found help. = 他走了几英里才找到帮助。
She had limped all day before collapsing from exhaustion. = 她跛行了一整天后才因疲惫而倒下。

将来时:
He will limp for the rest of his life. = 他余生都将跛行。
She will probably limp if she continues to run on that injury. = 如果她继续带着伤跑步，她可能会跛行。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't injured his knee, he wouldn't limp. = 如果他没有伤到膝盖，他就不会跛行。
I wish I didn't limp so much. = 我希望我不那么跛行。

被动语态: (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice.)  N/A

疑问句:
Does he limp? = 他跛行吗？
Why does she limp? = 她为什么跛行？


否定句:
He doesn't limp anymore. = 他不再跛行了。
She didn't limp before the fall. = 她摔倒之前不跛行。
