hypothetic:/ˌhaɪpəˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 假设的，假设性的 |  Relating to or involving a hypothesis; based on or involving a hypothesis.  It suggests something that is assumed or supposed to be true for the sake of argument or investigation, but it is not necessarily true in reality.  | Example:  The hypothetic scenario explored the potential consequences of climate change.  假设的情况探讨了气候变化的潜在后果。


Example: We discussed the hypothetic possibility of life on other planets. 我们讨论了其他星球上存在生命的假设可能性。


Near synonyms:  theoretical, conjectural, supposed, assumed


Antonyms:  actual, real, factual, certain
