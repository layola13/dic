kick-about:/ˈkɪkəbaʊt/| n. v.|随便踢球；闲逛；临时比赛；非正式的讨论|A casual game of football; an informal gathering; a discussion that is not planned or formal.|例句：We had a kick-about in the park after school.放学后我们在公园里踢了一场球。|例句：They had a kick-about to decide who would captain the team.他们进行了一场随便踢踢的比赛来决定谁当队长。|近义词：scrimmage, jamboree,  (for discussion) chat,  (for gathering) hangout|反义词：formal meeting, planned event
