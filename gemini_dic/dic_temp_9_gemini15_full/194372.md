self-presented:/ˌsɛlf prɪˈzɛntɪd/| adj.| 自我陈述的；自行呈报的 |  Describes something presented or offered by someone themselves, without external assistance or prompting.  It often implies a certain level of initiative or self-advocacy. |例句：The self-presented evidence was insufficient to convict him. 自行提交的证据不足以定他的罪。


The candidate's self-presented portfolio was impressive.  求职者自行提供的作品集令人印象深刻。|近义词：self-reported, volunteered, submitted |反义词： externally presented, imposed, mandated
