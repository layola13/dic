carouser:/kəˈraʊzər/| n. |狂饮者，寻欢作乐者 |A carouser is someone who drinks a lot of alcohol and enjoys a lively social life, often involving boisterous behaviour.  They are typically associated with late nights and merrymaking. |The carouser stumbled home at dawn, singing loudly.  这个狂饮者黎明时分摇摇晃晃地回家，大声唱歌。|reveler, merrymaker, drinker |homebody, hermit
