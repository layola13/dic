slopped:/slɒpt/| /slɑːpt/ | v. | 洒，泼，溅；邋遢地走，拖着脚走 | To spill or splash a liquid; to walk or move in a careless or sloppy manner. |例句：He slopped the paint onto the canvas. 他把油漆泼到画布上。


例句：He slopped through the mud. 他在泥里拖着脚走。|近义词：splashed, spilled, splashed, sloshed |反义词：carefully poured, neatly walked
