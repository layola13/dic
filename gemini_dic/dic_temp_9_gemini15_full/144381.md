outstream:/ˈaʊtˌstriːm/| adj. n.|流出的；外流的；外部数据流|Relating to or denoting the delivery of advertisements or other content outside of the main stream of a website or app, typically through a separate player or window.|例句：Many brands are now using outstream video advertising to reach wider audiences.  许多品牌现在正在使用外部视频广告来吸引更广泛的受众。  |例句：The outstream video ad played seamlessly within the article. 这则外部视频广告在文章中流畅播放。|近义词：external, peripheral|反义词：instream
