repromised:/ˈriːˈprɑːmɪst/| v.| 再承诺，重新承诺 | To promise something again; to reaffirm a promise.  |例句：He repromised to help us finish the project. 他再次承诺帮助我们完成项目。|例句：After the initial delay, the company repromised delivery within a week. 在最初的延误之后，公司再次承诺在一周内交货。|近义词：reassure, reaffirm, reiterate |反义词：break a promise, renege


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repromises to pay back the loan. = 他再次承诺偿还贷款。
She repromises to attend the meeting. = 她再次承诺参加会议。

一般过去时:
He repromised to call me later. = 他再次承诺稍后给我打电话。
They repromised to deliver the goods on time. = 他们再次承诺准时交付货物。

现在完成时:
He has repromised to support our cause. = 他已经再次承诺支持我们的事业。
She has repromised to finish the report by Friday. = 她已经再次承诺在星期五之前完成报告。

过去完成时:
He had repromised to help before the deadline. = 他在截止日期之前再次承诺提供帮助。
They had repromised to attend the conference. = 他们之前已经再次承诺参加会议。

将来时:
He will repromise to donate to the charity. = 他将再次承诺向慈善机构捐款。
She will repromise to complete the assignment. = 她将再次承诺完成作业。

虚拟语气:
If he repromised, I would believe him. = 如果他再次承诺，我会相信他。
I wish he had repromised to be there. = 我希望他之前已经再次承诺会在那里。

被动语态:
The promise was repromised by the government. = 政府再次承诺了这项承诺。(This is less common usage)
The delivery date was repromised by the supplier. = 供应商再次承诺了交货日期。(This is less common usage)

疑问句:

否定句:
