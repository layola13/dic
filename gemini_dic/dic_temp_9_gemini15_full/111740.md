locust:/ˈloʊkəst/| n. |蝗虫|A locust is a type of grasshopper that travels in large swarms and can cause significant damage to crops.  Locusts are known for their devastating ability to consume vast amounts of vegetation in short periods.|Example:  A swarm of locusts descended upon the fields, devouring everything in their path.  一大群蝗虫飞落田间，吞噬着沿途的一切。|近义词：grasshopper (in some contexts),  insect|反义词：N/A
