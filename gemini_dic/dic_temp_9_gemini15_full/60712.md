electro-hydraulics: /ˌɪˌlektrəʊhaɪˈdrɔːlɪks/ | n. | 电液控制；电液系统 |  The branch of engineering dealing with systems that combine electrical and hydraulic power.  Electro-hydraulics are used in a wide variety of applications, from aircraft control systems to industrial machinery. | Example: The aircraft's flight controls are powered by an electro-hydraulic system.  这架飞机的飞行控制系统由电液系统驱动。


近义词：electromechanical systems (在某些特定应用中)

反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑与纯机械系统或纯电气系统相对比
