separ:/ˈsɛpər/| n.|分离器，隔板| A device or structure that separates or divides something.|The separ in the factory helps to separate the different types of materials. 工厂里的分离器有助于分离不同类型的材料。|近义词：divider, partition|反义词：unifier, combiner

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The separ separates the wheat from the chaff. = 分离器将麦粒与谷壳分开。
This machine separates liquids of different densities. = 这台机器分离不同密度的液体。


一般过去时:
The technician separated the wires carefully. = 技术人员小心地分离了电线。
The storm separated the ships. = 暴风雨使船只分散开来。


现在完成时:
They have separated the recyclable materials from the waste. = 他们已经将可回收材料与垃圾分离。
The company has separated its marketing and sales departments. = 公司已将市场营销部门和销售部门分开。


过去完成时:
The workers had separated the components before the assembly. = 工人们在组装之前已经将组件分离。
The police had separated the fighting crowds before it escalated. = 在冲突人群升级之前，警察已经将他们隔开。


将来时:
We will separate the eggs before baking the cake. = 我们将在烘焙蛋糕之前分离蛋清和蛋黄。
The scientists will separate the isotopes using a centrifuge. = 科学家们将使用离心机分离同位素。


虚拟语气:
If they separated the issues, it would be easier to resolve them. = 如果他们将问题分开，解决起来就会更容易。
I wish they had separated the recycling earlier. = 我希望他们早点把可回收物分开。


被动语态:
The mixture was separated into its constituent parts. = 混合物被分离成其组成部分。
The oil and water are being separated by a filter. = 油和水正在被过滤器分离。


疑问句:
How does this machine separate the materials? = 这台机器是如何分离这些材料的？
Are the two groups separated by any significant differences? = 这两个群体之间是否存在任何显著差异？


否定句:
The two substances did not separate easily. = 这两种物质不容易分离。
They haven't separated the wheat from the chaff yet. = 他们还没有将麦粒与谷壳分开。
