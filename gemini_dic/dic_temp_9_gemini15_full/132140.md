noncultivated: /ˌnɒnkʌlˈtɪveɪtɪd/ | adj. | 未开垦的；未耕种的；野生的 | Not cultivated or tilled; uncultivated land is land that has not been prepared for planting crops.  It can also refer to something that hasn't been refined or developed. |

例句：

The land was noncultivated for centuries, resulting in a rich ecosystem.  这片土地几个世纪以来都没有耕种，因此形成了一个丰富的生态系统。

The noncultivated plants thrived in the untamed wilderness.  这些未经耕种的植物在未驯服的荒野中茁壮成长。


近义词：wild, uncultivated, untamed, undeveloped, primitive

反义词：cultivated, tilled, developed, refined, domesticated

The word "noncultivated" is a combination of the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "cultivated."  "Cultivated" comes from the Latin word "cultivatus," the past participle of "cultivare," meaning "to cultivate," "to till," or "to care for."  This, in turn, is related to the Latin word "colere," meaning "to cultivate," "to inhabit," or "to till."
