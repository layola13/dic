grouching:/ˈɡraʊtʃɪŋ/| n.|抱怨，牢骚|The act of complaining or grumbling; a grumpy or complaining mood or disposition.|例句：His constant grouching about the weather was getting on everyone's nerves. 他不断抱怨天气，让每个人都很烦躁。|近义词：complaining, grumbling, grumbling, whining, complaining|反义词：cheerfulness, contentment, satisfaction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is grouching about his job. = 他在抱怨他的工作。
She grouches about the traffic every morning. = 她每天早上都抱怨交通堵塞。


一般过去时:
He grouched about the food last night. = 他昨晚抱怨食物。
They grouched all the way home. = 他们一路抱怨着回家。


现在完成时:
She has been grouching all day. = 她整天都在抱怨。
He has grouched about this problem for weeks. = 他已经抱怨这个问题几周了。


过去完成时:
He had grouched about the delay before the meeting even started. = 会议还没开始他就抱怨延误了。
She had been grouching for hours before she finally calmed down. = 她抱怨了好几个小时才最终平静下来。


将来时:
I think he will be grouching again tomorrow. = 我想他明天还会抱怨。
They will probably grouch about the long wait. = 他们可能会抱怨漫长的等待。


虚拟语气:
If he didn't have so many problems, he wouldn't be grouching so much. = 如果他没有那么多问题，他就不会抱怨这么多。
I wish he wouldn't grouch all the time. = 我希望他不要一直抱怨。


被动语态:  (Grouching is less commonly used in the passive voice)
His complaints were often described as grouching. = 他的抱怨经常被描述为牢骚。
The situation was causing a lot of grouching among the employees. = 这种情况导致员工们有很多抱怨。


疑问句:
Is he still grouching about the project? = 他还在抱怨那个项目吗？
Why is she grouching today? = 她今天为什么抱怨？


否定句:
He wasn't grouching this morning. = 他今天早上没有抱怨。
They didn't grouch at all during the trip. = 他们在旅途中一点也没有抱怨。


PHRASE->
a fit of grouching = 一阵抱怨

petty grouching = 小题大做的抱怨
endless grouching = 无休止的抱怨
general grouching =  普遍的抱怨
