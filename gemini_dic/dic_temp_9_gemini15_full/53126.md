diplograph:/ˈdɪpləɡræf/| n.|双字母组合，双字母字|A diplograph is a combination of two letters representing a single sound or phoneme.  This often occurs in languages where a single letter does not adequately represent the sound.  It can also refer to a writing system that uses such combinations extensively.|Example: The letters "ch" in "chair" represent a single sound, making "ch" a diplograph.|近义词： digraph|反义词：monograph
