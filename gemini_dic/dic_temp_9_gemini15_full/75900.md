fundi:/ˈfʌndi/| n. |基层民众；当地人；（尤指非洲）农村居民；基础知识；基本技能|A fundi is a person who is skilled or knowledgeable in a particular area, especially in a practical or technical field.  The term is often used in East Africa to refer to someone with expertise in a particular trade or craft.  It can also refer to someone who is well-versed in the ways of a particular community or region.|例句：He's a fundi when it comes to fixing cars. 他是修车高手。|近义词：expert, specialist, artisan, local, native |反义词：amateur, novice, unskilled, outsider
