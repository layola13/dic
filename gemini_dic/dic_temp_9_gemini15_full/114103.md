magnitude:/ˈmæɡnɪtuːd/|/ˈmæɡnɪtjuːd/| n. |大小；数量；震级；重要性 |The magnitude of the problem is far greater than we initially thought.  这个问题的严重程度远超我们最初的设想。|The earthquake measured 7.0 on the Richter magnitude scale.  地震的里氏震级为7.0。|近义词：size, extent, importance, significance |反义词：insignificance, triviality


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The magnitude of the task is immense. = 这项任务的规模巨大。
The magnitude of his achievement is undeniable. = 他成就的重要性不容否认。


一般过去时:
The magnitude of the explosion surprised everyone. = 爆炸的规模令每个人都感到惊讶。
The magnitude of the damage was assessed after the storm. = 暴风雨过后评估了损失的程度。


现在完成时:
Scientists have determined the magnitude of the asteroid. = 科学家已经确定了小行星的大小。
We have underestimated the magnitude of the challenge. = 我们低估了挑战的规模。


过去完成时:
They had not realized the magnitude of the consequences before it was too late. = 在为时已晚之前，他们没有意识到后果的严重性。
The full magnitude of the crisis had only become apparent recently. = 危机的全部严重程度直到最近才显现出来。


将来时:
Future research will determine the precise magnitude of the effect. = 未来的研究将确定这种影响的确切程度。
We will assess the magnitude of the damage tomorrow. = 我们明天将评估损失的程度。


虚拟语气:
If we had known the magnitude of the risk, we would have acted differently. = 如果我们知道风险的严重性，我们会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had appreciated the magnitude of his contribution. = 我希望我当时能认识到他贡献的重要性。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
