preadvocating: /ˌpriːˈædvəkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先倡导；提前宣传 |  To advocate or promote something before it is officially proposed or implemented.  This often involves preparing the ground for acceptance of an idea or policy. | 例句：The company was preadvocating for a new environmental policy before presenting it to the board. 公司在向董事会提交新的环保政策之前就进行了预先倡导。

The government is preadvocating for stricter gun control laws through public awareness campaigns. 政府正在通过公众意识宣传活动预先倡导更严格的枪支管制法。


近义词：pre-campaigning, pre-promoting, preparing the ground for, laying the groundwork for | 反义词：opposing, resisting, hindering


词性:动词
