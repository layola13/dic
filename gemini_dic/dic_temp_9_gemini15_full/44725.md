cruised:/kruːzd/| v.|巡航，漫游，轻松地度过 |to travel at a steady speed, especially in a ship or car, often for pleasure.|例句：We cruised along the coast. 我们沿着海岸巡航。|例句：They cruised around town in their new car.他们开着新车在城里四处兜风。|近义词：sailed, travelled, sped|反义词：hurried, rushed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cruises the streets every evening. = 他每天晚上都在街上巡航。
She cruises the internet for hours. = 她会在网上闲逛好几个小时。


一般过去时:
We cruised the Caribbean last summer. = 我们去年夏天在加勒比海巡航。
They cruised down the highway. = 他们沿着高速公路行驶。


现在完成时:
I have cruised extensively in the Mediterranean. = 我在地中海广泛地巡航过。
She has cruised through her exams. = 她轻松地通过了考试。


过去完成时:
They had cruised to several islands before reaching their final destination. = 在到达最终目的地之前，他们已经巡航过好几个岛屿。
He had cruised along that road many times before. = 他以前多次沿着那条路行驶。


将来时:
We will cruise to Alaska next year. = 我们明年将前往阿拉斯加巡航。
They will cruise the streets looking for trouble. = 他们将巡逻街道寻找麻烦。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would cruise the world. = 如果我有钱，我会环游世界。
I wish I could cruise to the Bahamas next week. = 我希望下周可以去巴哈马群岛巡航。


被动语态:
The ship was cruised by many tourists last year. = 去年这艘船被许多游客乘坐。
The city streets are often cruised by police cars. = 城市街道经常有警车巡逻。


疑问句:
Did you cruise around town yesterday? = 你昨天在城里兜风了吗？
Will they cruise to Hawaii this summer? = 他们今年夏天会去夏威夷巡航吗？


否定句:
We didn't cruise to Europe this year. = 我们今年没有去欧洲巡航。
He hasn't cruised the internet today. = 他今天没有上网闲逛。
