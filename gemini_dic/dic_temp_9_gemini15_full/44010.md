crimination:/krɪmɪˈneɪʃən/| n. | 判罪；指责；歧视 | The act of accusing or condemning someone of wrongdoing; the act of making a distinction in favor of or against a person or thing based on the group, class, or category to which that person or thing belongs. |例句：The discrimination against minorities is unacceptable. 对少数民族的歧视是不可接受的。 |例句：His discrimination of the facts led to a wrong judgment. 他对事实的曲解导致了错误的判断。|近义词：accusation, condemnation, prejudice, bias |反义词：acquittal, exoneration, fairness, impartiality |名词
