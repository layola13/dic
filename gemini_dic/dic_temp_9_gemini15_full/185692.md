reversers:/rɪˈvɜːrsərz/| n. |反向器，逆转器|A device or mechanism that reverses the direction of something, such as a motor or a train.  It can also refer to a person or thing that reverses a process or trend.|例句：The train's reversers allowed the engineer to quickly change direction.|近义词：inverter,  reverser gear|反义词：forwarder, driver


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine reverses smoothly. = 机器平稳地反转。
The engineer reverses the train. = 工程师使火车倒车。


一般过去时:
He reversed the car into the garage. = 他把车倒进车库。
The company reversed its decision. = 公司撤销了它的决定。


现在完成时:
They have reversed the engine's polarity. = 他们已经反转了发动机的极性。
The market has reversed its downward trend. = 市场已经扭转了其下跌趋势。


过去完成时:
The technician had reversed the wires before the test. = 技术员在测试前已经反转了电线。
She had reversed her opinion after hearing new evidence. = 在听到新的证据后，她改变了主意。


将来时:
The pilot will reverse the thrusters for landing. = 飞行员将反转推进器以便着陆。
They will reverse the charges on my credit card. = 他们将取消我信用卡上的费用。


虚拟语气:
If the switch were reversed, the motor would run in the opposite direction. = 如果开关反转，马达将朝相反的方向运转。
I wish I had reversed my investment sooner. = 我真希望我早点撤回投资。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
