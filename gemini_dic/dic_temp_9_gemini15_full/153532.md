pentacrinoid:/ˈpentəkrɪnɔɪd/| n. |五辐射的，海百合幼虫|A pentacrinoid is the larval stage of a crinoid (sea lily), characterized by a five-rayed body plan.  It is a free-swimming, planktonic stage before settling to the seafloor and developing into a mature crinoid.|Example: The pentacrinoid larva of the sea lily settled on the coral reef and began its metamorphosis.|近义词: crinoid larva,  sea lily larva|反义词: None.
