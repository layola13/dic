outplanning: /ˌaʊtˈplænɪŋ/ | v. |  超越计划；预先计划；计划得比…好 | To surpass in planning; to plan better than.  It implies a superior level of planning compared to someone or something else.  |  例句：The competitor's outplanning of our marketing strategy led to their success.  竞争对手的营销策略比我们计划得更好，导致了他们的成功。


例句：Their meticulous outplanning ensured the project's timely completion.  他们细致的预先计划确保了项目的按时完成。


近义词：outmaneuvering, outsmarting, surpassing, excelling


反义词：underplanning, poor planning, neglecting


词性：动词
