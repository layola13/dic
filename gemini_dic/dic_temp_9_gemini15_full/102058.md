isometric-projection:/ˌaɪsoʊˈmɛtrɪk prəˈdʒɛkʃən/| n. |等轴测投影|A method of visual representation where three sides of an object are shown in a single drawing, with all three axes equally foreshortened.  It's a type of axonometric projection used to create a pictorial drawing of a three-dimensional object.  The resulting image is not a true perspective view, but it's useful for conveying the overall shape and proportions of the object. |Example: The architect used an isometric projection to show the client the building's design.  建筑师用等轴测投影向客户展示了建筑的设计。The engineering drawing utilized an isometric projection to illustrate the complex assembly. 工程图纸利用等轴测投影来说明复杂的装配。|近义词：axonometric projection, pictorial drawing |反义词：perspective projection
