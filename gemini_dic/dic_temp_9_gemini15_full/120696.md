misadvises:/ˌmɪs ədˈvaɪzɪz/| vt.|误导，提供错误建议 |To give someone bad or wrong advice.|例句：He misadvised me about the investment, and I lost a lot of money. 他在投资方面误导了我，我损失了很多钱。|  The lawyer misadvised his client, leading to a costly mistake. 律师误导了他的当事人，导致了一个代价高昂的错误。|近义词：misguide, mislead, ill-advise|反义词：advise well, counsel wisely|动词
