flueless:/ˈfluːləs/| adj.| 无烟道的；无烟囱的 | Without a flue or chimney.  A flueless appliance is designed to vent combustion products directly to the outside air without requiring a traditional chimney or flue system. | Example:  The new heater is flueless, so we don't need to worry about installing a chimney.  新的加热器是无烟道的，所以我们不用担心安装烟囱。|近义词：chimneyless, ventless |反义词：flued, chimneyed
