cender:/ˈsɛndər/| n.|发送者，寄件人|The sender of the email was unknown.  邮件发送者未知。|近义词：transmitter, originator |反义词：recipient, receiver


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sender sends the package everyday. = 发送者每天都发送包裹。
Who sends this email? = 谁发送了这封邮件？


一般过去时:
The sender sent the package yesterday. = 发送者昨天寄送了包裹。
Who sent this letter? = 谁寄了这封信？


现在完成时:
The sender has sent many packages this month. = 发送者这个月已经发送了很多包裹。
Have you sent the email yet? = 你已经发送邮件了吗？


过去完成时:
The sender had sent the package before the deadline. = 发送者在截止日期之前已经寄送了包裹。
He had sent the report before the meeting started. = 他在会议开始前就已发送了报告。


将来时:
The sender will send the package tomorrow. = 发送者明天将寄送包裹。
When will you send the document? = 你什么时候会发送文件？


虚拟语气:
If the sender were here, he would explain. = 如果发送者在这里，他会解释。
I wish the sender had sent the instructions earlier. = 我希望发送者早点发送说明。


被动语态:
The package was sent by the sender. = 包裹是由发送者寄送的。
The email was sent yesterday. = 邮件昨天被发送了。


疑问句:
Who is the sender? = 发送者是谁？
Where is the sender located? = 发送者在哪里？


否定句:
The sender did not send the package. = 发送者没有寄送包裹。
I am not the sender of this message. = 我不是这条消息的发送者。
