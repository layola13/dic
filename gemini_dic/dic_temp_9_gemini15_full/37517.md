common eras:/ˈkɑːmən ˈɪərəz/| n. | 公元纪年；常用纪元 | Refers to the system of dating years from the traditionally reckoned birth of Jesus Christ, using BC (Before Christ) and AD (Anno Domini, "in the year of the Lord") or BCE (Before Common Era) and CE (Common Era).  This system is widely used for historical dating. |Example: The Roman Empire flourished during the common eras. 罗马帝国在公元纪年时期繁荣昌盛。The construction of the Great Wall began in earlier common eras. 长城在更早的公元纪年时期开始建造。|近义词：AD/CE system, Anno Domini system |反义词：None, it's a dating system, not an adjective.|名词
