locus's:/ˈloʊkəsɪz/| n. |基因座，轨迹，地点|The plural of locus, referring to a specific position or point, especially on a chromosome or in a genome.  It signifies the location of a gene or a specific DNA sequence. |The loci of the genes responsible for eye color are on chromosome 15. 基因决定眼睛颜色的基因座位于15号染色体上。|location, site, point, position |  |n.
