graphically:/ˈɡræfɪkli/ | adj. |生动地；形象地；用图表表示地 | In a vivid and detailed manner, often suggesting a shocking or horrifying effect.  It can also refer to the use of graphs or charts to represent data. |

例句：The accident was graphically described in the newspaper. 报纸上生动地描述了这场事故。

The data was graphically presented in a bar chart. 数据以柱状图的形式形象地呈现出来。


近义词：vividly, vividly, pictorially, visually


反义词：vaguely, abstractly, implicitly


词性：形容词
