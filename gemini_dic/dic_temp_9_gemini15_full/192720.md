USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sea crashes against the shore. = 海浪拍打着海岸。
We often swim in the sea during summer. = 我们夏天经常在海里游泳。


一般过去时:
The ship sailed across the sea. = 船航行在大海上。
They saw dolphins playing in the sea yesterday. = 他们昨天看到海豚在海里玩耍。


现在完成时:
I have swum in the sea many times. = 我多次在海里游泳。
She has never seen such a beautiful sea. = 她从未见过如此美丽的大海。


过去完成时:
The storm had calmed before they reached the sea. = 在他们到达大海之前，暴风雨已经平息了。
They had sailed for days before they spotted land.  = 他们在发现陆地之前已经航行了好几天了。


将来时:
We will go to the seaside tomorrow. = 我们明天要去海边。
They will sail across the sea next week. = 他们下周将横渡大海。


虚拟语气:
If I were at the sea, I would swim. = 如果我在海边，我会游泳。
I wish I were at the sea right now. = 我真希望我现在在海边。


被动语态:
The coast is often affected by the sea. = 海岸经常受到大海的影响。
The ship was tossed about by the stormy sea. = 船只被暴风雨的海浪抛来抛去。


疑问句:
Is the sea calm today? = 今天海面平静吗？
Have you ever been to the sea? = 你去过海边吗？


否定句:
The sea wasn't calm yesterday. = 昨天海面并不平静。
I haven't seen the sea for a long time. = 我很久没见过大海了。
