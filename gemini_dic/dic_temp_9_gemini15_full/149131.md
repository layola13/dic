oxidic:/ˈɒksɪdɪk/| adj. |氧化性的，含氧化物的|Relating to or containing oxides.  The term describes substances that are composed of, or characterized by, the presence of oxides.  These oxides can be metal oxides, non-metal oxides, or a combination of both. The properties of oxidic materials are largely determined by the type and bonding nature of the oxides present.|例句：The oxidic layer on the metal surface protects it from further corrosion. 金属表面的氧化层保护它免受进一步腐蚀。

The sample exhibited distinct oxidic characteristics after prolonged exposure to high temperatures. 样品在长时间暴露于高温后表现出明显的氧化特性。|近义词：oxygenic, oxide-containing |反义词：reduced (in the chemical sense)
