point-off: /pɔɪnt ɒf/ | phrasal verb | 指出；挑出；批评；使…显得不重要 | To point out a fault or mistake; to criticize; to dismiss something as unimportant. |例句：The teacher pointed off several errors in my essay. 老师指出了我论文中的几个错误。|近义词：criticize, indicate, highlight, dismiss |反义词：praise, approve, endorse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He points off the flaws in the design. = 他指出了设计中的缺陷。
She points off the inconsistencies in the report. = 她指出了报告中前后矛盾之处。


一般过去时:
The manager pointed off several aspects of the proposal that needed improvement. = 经理指出了提案中几个需要改进的地方。
He pointed off my careless mistake during the presentation. = 他在演示过程中指出了我的粗心错误。


现在完成时:
They have pointed off many problems with the new software. = 他们已经指出了新软件的许多问题。
She has pointed off the need for more funding. = 她已经指出了对更多资金的需求。


过去完成时:
The reviewer had pointed off several stylistic issues before submitting his review. = 审稿人在提交评论之前已经指出了几个风格问题。
We had pointed off the risks associated with the project before we started. = 我们在开始项目之前就已经指出了与项目相关的风险。


将来时:
The consultant will point off areas for improvement in the business plan. = 顾问将指出商业计划中需要改进的地方。
They will point off any safety hazards they find during the inspection. = 他们在检查过程中会指出他们发现的任何安全隐患。


虚拟语气:
If he had pointed off the problem earlier, we could have avoided it. = 如果他早点指出问题，我们就能避免它了。
I wish he had pointed off the errors in my report before submitting it. = 我希望他在提交我的报告之前能指出其中的错误。


被动语态:
Several errors were pointed off in the student's paper. = 学生的论文中指出了几个错误。
The need for greater efficiency was pointed off by the audit. = 审计指出了提高效率的必要性。


疑问句:
What aspects of the proposal did the committee point off? = 委员会指出了提案的哪些方面？
Did the instructor point off any grammatical errors in the assignment? = 指导老师在作业中指出了任何语法错误吗？


否定句:
He didn't point off any significant problems with the project. = 他没有指出该项目任何重大问题。
She didn't point off the potential risks involved. = 她没有指出所涉及的潜在风险。


PHRASE->
point off a flaw = 指出缺陷
point off an error = 指出错误
point off a problem = 指出问题
point off a solution = 指出解决方法
point off the main points = 指出要点
