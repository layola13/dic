exhibitionist:/ˌɛksɪˈbɪʃənɪst/| n. |展览主义者，爱炫耀的人|A person who behaves in a way designed to attract attention to themselves.  An exhibitionist enjoys showing off and seeking attention.|例句：He's such an exhibitionist; he always has to be the center of attention. 他是个十足的展览主义者；他总是要成为众人关注的焦点。

例句：Her flamboyant dress and loud laughter made her seem like an exhibitionist. 她艳丽的服装和响亮的 laughter 使她看起来像个爱炫耀的人。


近义词：show-off, attention-seeker, narcissist|反义词：shy person, introvert|词性:noun
