sadists:/ˈseɪdɪsts/| n. |虐待狂；施虐者 |A sadist is a person who derives pleasure from inflicting pain, suffering, or humiliation on others.  Sadists often engage in acts of cruelty and violence, and their behavior is considered to be a mental disorder. |例句：He exhibited sadist tendencies in his treatment of animals. 他在对待动物时表现出虐待狂倾向。


例句：The serial killer was identified as a sadist who enjoyed torturing his victims. 连环杀手被认定为一个喜欢折磨受害者的虐待狂。


近义词：brute, tormentor, abuser, tyrant |反义词：masochist, altruist
