chewinesses:/ˈtʃuːinɪsɪz/| n. |耐嚼性（复数）|The plural form of chewiness, referring to multiple instances or types of the quality of being chewy.  It describes the texture of food that requires considerable chewing before it can be swallowed.  |例句：The chewinesses of the different breads were significantly different.  不同的面包的耐嚼性差异很大。 The various chewinesses of the gummy candies made them interesting to explore.  各种软糖的耐嚼性使它们很有探索的乐趣。|近义词：chewy textures, masticatory qualities|反义词：crumbliness, crispness
