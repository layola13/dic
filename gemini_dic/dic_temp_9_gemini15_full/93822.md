immigration:/ˌɪmɪˈɡreɪʃən/ | n. | 移民；移居 | The process of coming to live permanently in a foreign country.  It also refers to the people who immigrate. | Example:  Illegal immigration is a serious problem in many countries.  非法移民在许多国家都是一个严重的问题。  The large-scale immigration to the city put a strain on resources.  大规模的移民涌入城市给资源带来了压力。 |近义词：migration, influx | 反义词：emigration


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Immigration affects the social structure of a country. = 移民影响着一个国家的社会结构。
The government is addressing the issue of illegal immigration. = 政府正在处理非法移民的问题。


一般过去时:
Many immigrants arrived in the US during the 19th century. = 许多移民在19世纪到达美国。
The country experienced a wave of immigration after the war. = 战后，这个国家经历了一波移民浪潮。


现在完成时:
Millions of people have immigrated to the United States. = 数百万的人移民到了美国。
Immigration has significantly changed the demographics of many cities. = 移民已经显著地改变了许多城市的社会人口统计。


过去完成时:
By the time the new laws were enacted, many immigrants had already settled. = 在新法律颁布之前，许多移民已经定居下来了。
They had immigrated before the outbreak of the war. = 他们在战争爆发之前就移民了。


将来时:
More people will immigrate to urban areas in the coming decades. = 未来几十年，将会有更多的人移民到城市地区。
The government predicts a surge in immigration next year. = 政府预测明年移民人数将会激增。


虚拟语气:
If immigration were easier, more people would move. = 如果移民更容易，更多的人会搬迁。
