/ɛlt/ |abbr.|  英语教学 | 英语教学 (缩写，通常指English Language Teaching) |ELT refers to English Language Teaching, encompassing the methods, materials, and practices used to teach English as a second or foreign language. It's a broad field covering various aspects of language acquisition and pedagogy. |The ELT industry is constantly evolving to meet the needs of diverse learners. |近义词: ESL (English as a Second Language), EFL (English as a Foreign Language), TESOL (Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages) |反义词:N/A
