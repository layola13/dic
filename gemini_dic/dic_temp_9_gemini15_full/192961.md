secess:/səˈsɛs/| v.|脱离，退出，分离|To withdraw formally from membership of a federation or body, especially a political state.|The southern states seceded from the Union in 1861, leading to the American Civil War.  南方各州于1861年脱离联邦，导致了美国内战。|近义词：withdraw, separate, depart|反义词：join, unite, adhere|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The small town secedes from the larger country.  这个小镇脱离了更大的国家。
He secedes from the group. 他脱离了这个组织。


一般过去时:
The colony seceded from British rule.  殖民地脱离了英国的统治。
Many members seceded from the party. 许多成员退出了政党。


现在完成时:
The region has seceded from the empire. 这个地区已经脱离了帝国。
They have seceded from the union. 他们已经脱离了工会。


过去完成时:
