methinks:/ˈmɛθɪŋks/| v.|我想，我认为 |It is an archaic verb meaning "I think" or "it seems to me."  It is a contraction of "me thinks,"  where "me" is the dative case of the pronoun "I" and "thinks" is the third-person singular present tense of the verb "to think."|例句：Methinks he doth protest too much. = 我想他太矫揉造作了。  Methinks it is time to go. = 我想是时候走了。|近义词：I think, I believe, it seems to me|反义词：I know, I am certain
