comported:/kəmˈpɔːrtɪd/ | v. |举止，行为；相符；适应 | behaved or conducted oneself in a particular way; to be in agreement or harmony with something; to behave in a way that is appropriate or expected in a particular situation. |例句：He comported himself with dignity throughout the trial. 他在整个审判过程中都保持着尊严。  She comported herself gracefully at the formal dinner.  她在正式晚宴上举止优雅。 |近义词：behaved, conducted, acted, conformed |反义词：misbehaved, misconducted
