erase:/ɪˈreɪs/| vt.|擦除，抹去，消除|To remove something written, printed, or drawn, typically by rubbing it.|例句：Please erase the mistake. 请擦掉错误。|例句：He tried to erase the memory of that day. 他试图抹去那天记忆。|近义词：rub out, delete, wipe out|反义词：write, draw, create

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He erases the board every day. = 他每天擦黑板。
She erases her mistakes with an eraser. = 她用橡皮擦去错误。

一般过去时:
I erased the data from the hard drive. = 我从硬盘中删除了数据。
He erased the drawing with a tissue. = 他用纸巾擦掉了图画。

现在完成时:
She has erased all the files. = 她已经删除了所有文件。
They have erased the evidence. = 他们已经销毁了证据。

过去完成时:
He had erased the message before I saw it. = 我看到之前，他已经删除了信息。
She had erased the whiteboard before the meeting started. = 会议开始前，她已经擦掉了白板。

将来时:
I will erase the file tomorrow. = 我明天将删除该文件。
He will erase the graffiti from the wall. = 他将清除墙上的涂鸦。

虚拟语气:
If I erased the data, I wouldn't be able to recover it. = 如果我删除了数据，我就无法恢复它。
I wish I had erased that email. = 我真希望我没有发送那封邮件。

被动语态:
The data was erased from the computer. = 数据从电脑中被删除了。
The message will be erased automatically. = 信息将被自动删除。

疑问句:
Did you erase the file? = 你删除了文件吗？
Have you erased the board? = 你擦黑板了吗？

否定句:
I didn't erase the file. = 我没有删除文件。
She hasn't erased the whiteboard yet. = 她还没擦黑板。
