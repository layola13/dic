contraindicants:/ˌkɒntrəɪnˈdɪkənts/ | n. |禁忌症， противопоказания | Things that advise against a particular action or treatment; factors that make a particular treatment or procedure inadvisable.  |Example:  The patient's history of heart disease is a contraindicant to this surgery.  病人的心脏病史是这项手术的禁忌症。|近义词： contraindications, warnings, cautions |反义词：indications, recommendations


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This medication has several contraindicants. = 这药有几种禁忌症。
What are the contraindicants for this procedure? = 这个手术的禁忌症是什么？


一般过去时:
The doctor listed the contraindicants for the treatment. = 医生列出了治疗的禁忌症。
The patient's allergies were a contraindicant to the prescribed medication. = 病人的过敏症是服用处方药的禁忌症。


现在完成时:
The research has revealed several new contraindicants for the drug. = 研究已经揭示了该药物的几种新的禁忌症。
Doctors have identified several contraindicants associated with long-term use of the medication. = 医生已经确定了几种与长期服用该药物相关的禁忌症。


过去完成时:
They had overlooked several key contraindicants before starting the treatment. = 他们在开始治疗之前忽略了几项关键的禁忌症。
It turned out that the patient had several contraindicants that had not been previously identified. = 事实证明，病人有几项以前没有被发现的禁忌症。


将来时:
Future studies will likely identify additional contraindicants. = 未来的研究可能会发现更多的禁忌症。
The doctor will explain all the contraindicants before the procedure. = 医生会在手术前解释所有的禁忌症。


虚拟语气:
If there were no contraindicants, the treatment would be straightforward. = 如果没有禁忌症，治疗就会很简单。
I wish there were fewer contraindicants associated with this medication. = 我希望这种药物相关的禁忌症少一些。


被动语态:
Several contraindicants were identified during the pre-operative assessment. = 在术前评估中发现了几项禁忌症。
The contraindicants to the procedure are carefully explained to the patient. = 手术的禁忌症会仔细地向病人解释。


疑问句:
Are there any contraindicants I should be aware of? = 我应该注意任何禁忌症吗？
What are the most common contraindicants for this type of therapy? = 这种疗法的最常见禁忌症是什么？


否定句:
There were no contraindicants to the treatment. = 治疗没有禁忌症。
The patient had no known contraindicants to the planned surgery. = 病人对计划中的手术没有已知的禁忌症。
