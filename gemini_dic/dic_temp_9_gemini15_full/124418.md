motorboats:/ˈmoʊtərboʊts/ | n. |摩托艇；汽艇 | A motorboat is a boat propelled by a motor, typically an internal combustion engine. They are commonly used for recreation, fishing, and transportation.  |例句：We rented a motorboat and spent the afternoon exploring the coastline. 我们租了一艘摩托艇，下午用来探索海岸线。

例句：The motorboat sped across the lake, leaving a white wake. 摩托艇快速驶过湖面，留下白色的尾迹。


近义词：powerboat, speedboat | 反义词：sailboat, rowboat

词源:
The word "motorboat" is a compound word, combining "motor" (from Latin "movēre," meaning "to move") and "boat."  It reflects the primary function of the vessel: a boat powered by a motor.
