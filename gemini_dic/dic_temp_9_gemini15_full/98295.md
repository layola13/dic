insider: /ˈɪnsaɪdər/ | n. |内部人士，知情人士 | A person who has special knowledge or information about a particular organization, situation, etc., especially one that is not available to the general public.  |例句：The insider leaked the company's financial secrets to the press.  内部人士向媒体泄露了公司的财务秘密。

例句：She is an insider in the fashion industry, having worked for many top designers. 她在时尚行业是位内部人士，曾为许多顶级设计师工作过。


近义词：informer, confidant, source, informant | 反义词：outsider
