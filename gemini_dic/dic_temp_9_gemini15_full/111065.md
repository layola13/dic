listeners:/ˈlɪsənərz/| n. |听众，收听者|People who listen to something, such as a speaker, a performance, or a broadcast.  It refers to a group of people paying attention to an auditory input.|The listeners were captivated by the musician's performance. 听众们被音乐家的表演迷住了。|The audience attentively followed the speaker's words.  听众们专心致志地听着演讲者的讲话。|audience, audience members, hearers|speakers, performers
