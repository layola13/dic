self-loves:/selfˈlʌvz/| v.|自恋，自我陶醉|This word is a verb formed by combining "self" and "loves," indicating a state of being deeply in love with oneself. It often implies excessive admiration or preoccupation with one's own appearance, abilities, or accomplishments.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting narcissism or a lack of empathy.|例句：He self-loves excessively, constantly posting selfies and boasting about his achievements. 他非常自恋，不断发布自拍照并夸耀自己的成就。

例句：Her self-loves blinded her to the needs of others.  她的自恋让她看不到别人的需求。


近义词：narcissism, self-obsession, vanity, egotism|反义词：humility, selflessness, modesty|词性：verb
