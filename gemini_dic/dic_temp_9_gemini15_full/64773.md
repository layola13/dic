asexual:/ˌeɪˈsɛkʃuəl/| adj.|无性别的，无性繁殖的| Relating to or characterized by the absence of sexual reproduction or differentiation.  Asexual reproduction is a type of reproduction that does not involve the fusion of gametes or sex cells.  |Example: Many plants reproduce asexually.  许多植物进行无性繁殖。|  近义词：nonsexual, agamic | 反义词：sexual
