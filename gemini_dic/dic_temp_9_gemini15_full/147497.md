overoxidized: /ˌoʊvərˈɑːksɪdaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度氧化的 |  Beyond the normal or desired level of oxidation; excessively oxidized.  This term describes a substance that has undergone more oxidation than is considered ideal or beneficial.  The specific meaning depends heavily on the context (e.g., in chemistry, materials science, or biology).|


例句：The overoxidized polymer showed reduced strength and flexibility. 过度氧化的聚合物显示出降低的强度和柔韧性。

The overoxidized oil became rancid and unusable. 过度氧化的油变得腐败且无法使用。


近义词：hyperoxidized, excessively oxidized


反义词：underoxidized, reduced


词性: adjective
