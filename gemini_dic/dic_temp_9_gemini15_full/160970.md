postaccident: /ˌpoʊstˈæksɪdənt/ | adj. | 事故后的；事故之后的 | Relating to the period of time after an accident.  This term is used to describe events, actions, or conditions that occur after an accident has taken place.  It implies a consequence or a reaction to the accident itself. |


例句：

Postaccident investigation revealed a critical design flaw. 事故后的调查揭示了一个严重的設計缺陷。

The postaccident report detailed the causes and effects of the collision. 事故后的报告详细说明了碰撞的原因和后果。


近义词：post-crash, after-accident,  following the accident


反义词：pre-accident, before the accident


词性: adjective (形容词)
