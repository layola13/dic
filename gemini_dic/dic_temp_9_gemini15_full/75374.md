frowned:/fraʊnd/| v.|皱眉，不赞成|To draw the eyebrows together, typically in displeasure or concentration.  It can also express disapproval or displeasure.|He frowned at the messy room. 他对凌乱的房间皱起了眉头。She frowned in disapproval. 她不赞成地皱起了眉头。|smiled, grinned|smiled, laughed|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He frowns at the news. = 他对这个消息皱起了眉头。
She often frowns when she's concentrating. = 她专注的时候经常皱眉。


一般过去时:
He frowned when he heard the bad news. = 他听到坏消息时皱起了眉头。
She frowned at the rude customer. = 她对那个粗鲁的顾客皱起了眉头。


现在完成时:
He has frowned all day. = 他整天都愁眉苦脸的。
She has frowned ever since she received the letter. = 从收到那封信以来，她就一直愁眉苦脸。


过去完成时:
He had frowned before he spoke. = 他说话前皱起了眉头。
She had frowned throughout the entire meeting. = 整个会议期间她都一直皱着眉头。


将来时:
He will frown if he sees the mess. = 如果他看到这乱七八糟的样子，他会皱眉头的。
She will probably frown at my suggestion. = 她可能会对我的建议皱眉头。


虚拟语气:
I wish he wouldn't frown so much. = 我希望他不要总是皱眉。
If he knew the truth, he would frown. = 如果他知道真相，他会皱眉的。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
His actions were frowned upon by the community. = 他的行为受到社区的谴责。(这里 "frowned upon" 作形容词短语)
The suggestion was frowned upon by the board. = 董事会对这个建议表示反对。(这里 "frowned upon" 作形容词短语)


疑问句:
Does he often frown? = 他经常皱眉吗？
Why did she frown at me? = 她为什么对我皱眉？


否定句:
He didn't frown at all. = 他根本没有皱眉。
She didn't frown, instead she smiled. = 她没有皱眉，而是笑了。


PHRASE->
frown upon = 不赞成，反对
frown at = 对…皱眉
frown on = 不赞成
wear a frown = 愁眉苦脸
frown with displeasure = 不悦地皱眉
