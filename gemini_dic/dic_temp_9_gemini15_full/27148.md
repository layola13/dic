call-in:/ˈkɔːlɪn/| n. v.|电话参与；来电；电话会议|A call-in is a telephone call made to a radio or television program, or a telephone conference.  It can also refer to the act of making such a call or the total number of calls received.|例句：The radio show had a large number of call-ins about the controversial topic.  这个广播节目收到了大量关于有争议话题的来电。
The company held a call-in to discuss the new policy. 这家公司举行了一次电话会议来讨论新政策。|近义词：phone-in, telephone conference, hotline|反义词：call-out
