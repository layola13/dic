expiation:/ˌɛkspɪˈeɪʃən/| n. |赎罪；补偿；弥补 |The act of making amends or reparation for guilt or wrongdoing; atonement.  Expiation often involves a ritual or symbolic act to cleanse oneself of sin or wrongdoing. |例句：He sought expiation for his sins through years of charitable work. 他通过多年的慈善工作来寻求赎罪。

例句：The priest offered a ritual of expiation for the community's sins.  牧师为社区的罪行提供了一个赎罪仪式。


近义词：atonement, redemption, reparation, penance |反义词：condemnation, punishment, retribution
