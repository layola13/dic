despisingly: /dɪˈspaɪzɪŋli/ | adv. | 轻蔑地，鄙视地 |  Expressing or showing contempt or disdain.  | 例句：He looked at her despisingly. 他轻蔑地望着她。

例句：She spoke despisingly of her former colleagues. 她轻蔑地谈论她以前同事。


近义词：scornfully, contemptuously, disdainfully, sneeringly

反义词：respectfully, admiringly, approvingly


词源:  Derived from the verb "despise" (to look down on with contempt), adding the adverbial suffix "-ingly".  "Despise" itself comes from Old French *despiser, from Latin *despicere (to look down upon), from de- (down) + specere (to look).
