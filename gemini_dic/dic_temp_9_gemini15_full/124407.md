motorbike:/ˈməʊtəbaɪk/| n. |摩托车|A two-wheeled vehicle powered by a motor.  It's typically ridden by one person, but some models can accommodate a passenger.|例句：He rode his motorbike to work this morning. 他今天早上骑摩托车上班了。|例句：My motorbike broke down on the highway. 我的摩托车在高速公路上抛锚了。|近义词：motorcycle, motor cycle|反义词：bicycle, car
