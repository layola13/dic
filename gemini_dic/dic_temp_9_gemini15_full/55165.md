dissembles:/dɪˈsɛmbəl/| vt.|掩饰；伪装；假装|To conceal one's true feelings or intentions.|He dissembled his anger and spoke calmly. 他掩饰了怒气，平静地说话。|近义词：conceal, disguise, feign, mask|反义词：reveal, expose, confess|动词

词源:
From Old French *dessembler, from des- (from Latin dis-) + sembler ("to seem," from Latin similis "like").  The word ultimately derives from the Latin root "similis," meaning "like" or "similar," reflecting the act of making something appear different from what it is.
