glacial:/ˈɡleɪʃəl/| adj.|冰河的；冰川的；冰冷的；缓慢的|Relating to or caused by glaciers.  Characterized by slowness or lack of progress.|例句：The glacial pace of the negotiations frustrated everyone.  谈判的进展缓慢令人沮丧。

例句：The glacial lake was stunningly beautiful. 冰川湖泊美得令人惊叹。


近义词：icy, frigid, frozen, slow, sluggish, tardy|反义词：rapid, swift, fast, warm, tropical
