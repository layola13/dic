forthwith:/fɔːrθˈwɪð/ | adv. | 立刻；马上 | Immediately; at once; without delay.  The word implies a sense of urgency or promptness.  |例句：The order was given, and the soldiers marched forthwith. 命令一下达，士兵们立即出发了。 |例句：Upon receiving the news, she acted forthwith. 收到消息后，她立即采取了行动。|近义词：immediately, instantly, directly, at once |反义词：later, afterward, subsequently
