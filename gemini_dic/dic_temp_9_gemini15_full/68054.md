facture:/ˈfækʃər/| n.|发票；账单；骨折；（尤指建筑物的）裂缝|An invoice detailing goods or services provided,  a bill for payment.  In some contexts, it can also refer to a fracture or crack.|例句：The facture for the repair work was quite high. 修理工程的发票相当高。
