matures:/məˈtʃʊr/| v. adj. n.|成熟；使成熟；到期|To become fully developed or complete; to reach a point of full development or perfection;  to become due for payment.|例句：The fruit matures in the fall.  水果在秋天成熟。The wine will mature nicely in the oak barrels. 这葡萄酒在橡木桶中会陈酿得很好。|近义词：ripen, develop, age|反义词：immature, undeveloped, unripe


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cheese matures slowly. =奶酪慢慢成熟。
He matures gracefully with age. =他优雅地老去成熟了。


一般过去时:
The plan matured quickly. = 计划迅速成熟。
The fruit matured last week. = 水果上周成熟了。


现在完成时:
The wine has matured beautifully. = 这葡萄酒酿造得非常漂亮。
The project has matured over the years. = 这个项目经过多年的发展。


过去完成时:
The deal had matured before the deadline. = 这笔交易在截止日期前已完成。
They had matured their business plan before seeking investment. = 他们在寻求投资之前已经完善了他们的商业计划。


将来时:
The bonds will mature next year. = 债券将于明年到期。
The fruit will mature in a few weeks. = 水果几周后就会成熟。


虚拟语气:
If the plan had matured earlier, we would have succeeded. = 如果计划早点成熟，我们就成功了。
I wish my understanding of the subject had matured sooner. = 我希望我对这个主题的理解早点成熟。


被动语态:
The wine is matured in oak barrels. = 这葡萄酒在橡木桶中陈酿。
The fruit was matured in the sun. = 水果在阳光下成熟。


疑问句:
Has the plan matured yet? = 计划成熟了吗？
When will the bonds mature? = 债券什么时候到期？


否定句:
The plan hasn't matured yet. = 计划还没有成熟。
The fruit didn't mature properly. = 水果没有成熟好。
