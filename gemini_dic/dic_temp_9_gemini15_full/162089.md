pre-completion:/priːkəmˈpliːʃən/| n. | 预先完成；提前完成 | The act or process of completing something before the scheduled or expected time.  It often implies preparation or anticipation of a future event or task. |例句：The pre-completion of the project allowed us to start the next phase early.  提前完成项目使我们能够提前开始下一阶段。

The pre-completion of her studies gave her a head start in her career. 她提前完成学业让她在职业生涯中领先一步。|近义词：advance completion, early completion, prior completion |反义词：post-completion, delayed completion
