/ˈpriːtærɪfs/| n. |关税预告，预先征收的关税|A pretariff is a preliminary announcement or indication of tariffs to be imposed, often used to give businesses time to adjust before the tariffs officially take effect.|例句：The government released a pretariff on imported steel, giving manufacturers a month to prepare.  政府发布了进口钢铁的关税预告，给制造商一个月的时间准备。|近义词：preliminary tariff announcement, advance tariff notice|反义词：final tariff, actual tariff|名词
