disforest:/dɪsˈfɒrɪst/| v.|毁林，使…失去森林|To clear a forest or area of trees.  The process of deforestation.|例句：The company was criticized for disforesting the area to build a new factory.  该公司因砍伐该地区森林以建造新工厂而受到批评。

例句：Illegal logging is disforesting vast tracts of rainforest. 非法伐木正在毁掉大片雨林。


近义词：deforest, clear-cut, deforest


反义词：reforest, afforest


词性:动词
