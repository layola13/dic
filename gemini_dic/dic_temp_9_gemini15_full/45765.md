cushiony:/ˈkʊʃəni/ | adj. |柔软舒适的，像垫子一样的 | Having the softness and yielding quality of a cushion.  The cushioning effect absorbs shocks and vibrations.  | 例句：The sofa has a luxuriously cushiony seat.  = 这张沙发有一个非常柔软舒适的座位。

The mattress felt delightfully cushiony. = 床垫摸起来令人愉快地柔软舒适。


近义词：soft, plush, padded, yielding, comfortable | 反义词：hard, firm, rigid


词性:形容词
