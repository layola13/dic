hymenomycetes:/ˌhaɪmənəˈmaɪsiːts/| n. |担子菌类|A class of fungi characterized by their exposed spore-bearing surfaces (hymenia) on gills, pores, teeth, or smooth surfaces.  They include many familiar mushrooms, bracket fungi, and puffballs.|Example: Many delicious edible mushrooms belong to the class Hymenomycetes.|近义词：basidiomycetes (broader term)|反义词：ascomycetes (a different class of fungi)
