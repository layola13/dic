loaming:/ˈləʊmɪŋ/ | v. | 漫游；闲逛；徘徊 | to wander aimlessly or idly; to move slowly and lazily. |例句：The children were loaming through the fields. 孩子们在田野里漫无目的地闲逛。  |例句：He spent the afternoon loaming along the beach. 他下午在海滩上漫步。|近义词：wandering, rambling, strolling, sauntering |反义词：hurrying, rushing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He loams around the park every evening. = 他每天晚上都在公园里闲逛。
They loam through the streets looking for something to do. = 他们漫无目的地在街上游荡，寻找事情做。

一般过去时:
She loamed along the riverbank yesterday. = 她昨天沿着河岸漫步。
We loamed through the forest, enjoying the scenery. = 我们漫步穿过森林，欣赏风景。

现在完成时:
I have been loaming around all day. = 我今天一直在闲逛。
They have loamed through several towns on their journey. = 他们在旅途中漫步穿过几个城镇。

过去完成时:
She had loamed through the countryside before she found her way. = 她漫步穿过乡村后才找到路。
We had loamed for hours before we realised we were lost. = 我们漫步了好几个小时才意识到自己迷路了。


将来时:
I will loam through the antique shops tomorrow. = 我明天将在古董店闲逛。
They will loam along the coastline during their holiday. = 他们将在假期期间沿着海岸线漫步。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would loam through the art galleries. = 如果我有更多时间，我会在美术馆闲逛。
I wish I could loam through the streets of Paris. = 我希望我能漫步在巴黎的街道上。

被动语态:
(Loaming is rarely used in the passive voice.)  被动语态很少使用。


疑问句:
Were they loaming around when you saw them? = 你看到他们时，他们在闲逛吗？
Will you be loaming around town this weekend? = 这个周末你打算在镇上闲逛吗？

否定句:
He didn't loam through the woods; he took a direct route. = 他没有在树林里闲逛；他走的是直路。
They didn't loam around waiting for the bus; they caught the earlier one. = 他们没有在等公共汽车时闲逛；他们赶上了早班车。
