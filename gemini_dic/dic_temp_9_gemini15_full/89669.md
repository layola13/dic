hopes:/hoʊps/| n.|希望，期望|The plural form of "hope," referring to multiple aspirations or desires.  It can also refer to the things that are hoped for.|例句：My hopes for the future are bright.  我的未来充满希望。

例句：Her hopes of winning the competition were dashed. 她赢得比赛的希望破灭了。
