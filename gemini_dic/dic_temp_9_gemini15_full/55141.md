dissected:/dɪˈsɛktɪd/ | v. | 解剖；仔细分析 | To cut something, especially a body, into parts in order to study it; to analyze something in detail. |例句：The frog was dissected in biology class. = 青蛙在生物课上被解剖了。
The scientist dissected the data to find patterns. = 科学家仔细分析了数据以寻找规律。|近义词：analyzed, examined, scrutinized |反义词：synthesized, combined


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The surgeon dissects the tissue carefully. = 外科医生仔细地解剖组织。
She dissects the arguments in her essay. = 她在论文中仔细分析了论点。


一般过去时:
The students dissected the worms in the lab. = 学生们在实验室里解剖了蠕虫。
