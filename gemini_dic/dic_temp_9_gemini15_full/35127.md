cloudland:/ˈklaʊdlænd/| n.|云地，云中仙境|A region or expanse of clouds; a land or realm seemingly composed of or existing among clouds.  It often evokes a sense of fantasy or unreality.|例句：The sunset painted the cloudland with vibrant hues.  日落给云层涂上了鲜艳的色彩。

The distant mountains seemed to float in the ethereal cloudland.  远山似乎漂浮在虚无缥缈的云海之中。|近义词：cloudscape, sky, heaven|反义词：earth, ground
