reservoir:/ˈrezəvwɑːr/|/ˈrezəvwɔːr/| n. |水库；蓄水池；储藏；贮存器 |A large artificial lake used to store water for later use, especially to supply a town or factory.  A place or container where something is stored. |例句：The city's water supply comes from a large reservoir in the mountains.  城市的供水来自山区的一个大型水库。

The reservoir is almost full after the recent heavy rains.  最近的大雨过后，水库几乎满了。


近义词：lake, tank, store, container |反义词：outlet, drain, depletion


USAGE->

The reservoir holds millions of gallons of water. = 水库蓄水数百万加仑。
This reservoir supplies water to the entire city. = 这个水库为整个城市供水。


一般过去时:
The reservoir overflowed during the storm. = 暴风雨期间，水库水位超过了警戒线。
