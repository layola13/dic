incomes:/ˈɪnkʌmz/ | n. |收入，收益 |The money received, especially on a regular basis, for work or through investments.  |例句：His annual incomes are quite high.  他的年收入相当高。|例句：The company's incomes have increased by 10% this year.  今年公司收入增长了10%。|近义词：earnings, revenue, proceeds |反义词：expenditures, outgoings
