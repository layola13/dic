nonruminative: /ˌnɒnˈruːmɪneɪtɪv/ | adj. | 不反刍的；不沉思的；不反复思考的 | Not characterized by rumination or repetitive thought; tending to let go of negative thoughts and feelings easily. |  例句：She had a nonruminative approach to problems, addressing them directly and moving on. 她处理问题的方式不拖泥带水，直接解决并继续前进。  例句：His nonruminative nature allowed him to quickly recover from setbacks. 他不爱沉思的性格使他能迅速从挫折中恢复过来。 | 近义词：unreflective, unconcerned, detached | 反义词：ruminative, pensive, brooding
