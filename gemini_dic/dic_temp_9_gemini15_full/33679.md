cinnamene:/ˈsɪnəmiːn/| n. |肉桂烯，苯乙烯|A colorless, oily liquid hydrocarbon, C8H8, that is an isomer of styrene and is found in cinnamon oil. It is also known as styrolene.|例句：Cinnamene is a constituent of cinnamon oil. 肉桂烯是肉桂油的成分之一。|近义词：styrene, styrolene|反义词:  无
