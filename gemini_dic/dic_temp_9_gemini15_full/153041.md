peen:/piːn/ | n., vt. |  锤头；(用锤子)捶打；钉牢 |  A peen is the end of a hammerhead opposite the striking face, often used for shaping metal.  As a verb, it means to strike or shape metal with a hammer. | 例句：The blacksmith used the peen of his hammer to shape the horseshoe.  铁匠用锤子的锤头来塑造马蹄铁。

The mechanic peened the rivet to secure the metal plate. 技工用锤子捶打铆钉以固定金属板。 | 近义词：(n.) hammerhead, head (of a hammer); (vt.) hammer, forge, rivet | 反义词：无
