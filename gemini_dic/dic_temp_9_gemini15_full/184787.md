resurfacing:/ˌriːˈsɜːrfɪsɪŋ/| n. |重新铺面；重新出现；浮出水面 |The process of applying a new surface to something, or the act of something reappearing or becoming visible again.  This word often refers to road repair, but can also be used figuratively. |例句：The road is closed for resurfacing. 这条路因重新铺面而关闭。  The old rivalry resurfaced during the debate. 在辩论中，旧有的竞争关系再次出现。|近义词：repaving, reappearance, revival |反义词：destruction, disappearance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The road crew is resurfacing the highway. = 路政人员正在重新铺设高速公路。
He resurfs his skis every winter. = 他每年冬天都重新磨他的滑雪板。

一般过去时:
They resurfaced the tennis court last week. = 他们上周重新铺设了网球场。
The issue resurfaced during the negotiations. = 这个问题在谈判中再次出现。

现在完成时:
The company has resurfaced several parking lots this year. = 公司今年已经重新铺设了几个停车场。
His anger has resurfaced after the incident. = 事故发生后，他的怒火再次爆发。

过去完成时:
The problem had resurfaced before they found a solution. = 他们找到解决方案之前，问题就再次出现了。
She had resurfaced her memories after years of repression. = 她多年压抑之后，再次回忆起往事。

将来时:
They will resurface the driveway next month. = 他们下个月将重新铺设车道。
The topic will likely resurface in future discussions. = 这个话题很可能会在未来的讨论中再次出现。

虚拟语气:
If they resurfaced the road, traffic would improve. = 如果他们重新铺设道路，交通状况就会改善。
I wish the issue hadn't resurfaced at the meeting. = 我希望这个问题没有在会议上再次出现。

被动语态:
The road is being resurfaced at the moment. = 道路正在重新铺设中。
The old memories were resurfaced by the photograph. = 照片唤起了旧日的回忆。

疑问句:
Is the road being resurfaced? = 道路正在重新铺设吗？
Will the problem resurface again? = 问题还会再次出现吗？

否定句:
They didn't resurface the pool this year. = 他们今年没有重新粉刷游泳池。
The old wounds did not resurface during the reunion. = 在团聚期间，旧伤没有再次浮现。
