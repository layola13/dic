preparedly:/prɪˈpɛərɪdli/ | adv. | 预备地，准备充分地 | In a prepared manner; showing readiness.  This adverb describes an action performed with prior preparation or readiness.  | He answered the question preparedly, having studied the topic extensively. 他准备充分地回答了这个问题，因为他已经广泛学习了这个话题。 |  readily, thoughtfully, deliberately | unpreparedly, haphazardly, carelessly | 副词
