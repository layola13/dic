fiat:/ˈfiːæt/| n.|法令；命令；许可；批准|A fiat is an authoritative decree or pronouncement; an official order.  It can also refer to something established by order, without consultation or discussion.  In finance, it refers to a currency issued by government decree.|例句：The king issued a fiat declaring war. 国王发布了一道宣战的法令。 The new law was enacted by fiat. 新法律是通过法令颁布的。|近义词：decree, edict, order, mandate |反义词：negotiation, consultation
