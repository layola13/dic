geopolitics:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈpɑːlətɪks/| n. |地缘政治学|The study of international relations in terms of geography and material resources.  It examines how geographical factors, such as location, resources, and terrain, influence political power and international relations.  Geopolitics considers the strategic implications of geography for political decision-making. |例句：The ongoing conflict is deeply rooted in geopolitics and the struggle for control of vital resources.  这场持续的冲突深深植根于地缘政治以及争夺重要资源的斗争。

The rise of China has significantly altered the global geopolitical landscape. 中国的崛起已经显著改变了全球地缘政治格局。|近义词：international relations, political geography, power politics |反义词：N/A
