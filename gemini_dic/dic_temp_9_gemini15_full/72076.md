flo:/floʊ/| n. |洪水；泛滥；大量涌现|A large amount of water covering an area that is usually dry.|The recent heavy rains caused severe flooding in many areas. 最近的大雨导致许多地区严重洪涝。|近义词：deluge, inundation, overflow |反义词：drought, dryness


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Flooding occurs frequently in this region. = 这个地区经常发生洪涝。
The river flows steadily. = 河水稳定地流淌。


一般过去时:
The dam broke and flooded the town. = 水坝决堤，淹没了小镇。
The river flowed rapidly after the rain. = 雨后河水流速很快。


现在完成时:
Floods have devastated the crops. = 洪水摧毁了庄稼。
The river has overflowed its banks several times this year. = 今年这条河已经多次决堤泛滥。


过去完成时:
The area had flooded before the rescue teams arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，该地区就已经被洪水淹没了。
The river had been flowing swiftly for days before the bridge collapsed. = 大桥倒塌前几天，河水一直流得很快。


将来时:
Heavy rain is forecast, and we expect flooding. = 预报有大雨，我们预计会发生洪涝。
The river will flow faster if it rains heavily. = 如果下大雨，河水将会流得更快。


虚拟语气:
If the dam had not broken, the town would not have flooded. = 如果水坝没有决堤，小镇就不会被淹了。
If it had rained less, the river wouldn't have overflowed. = 如果雨下得小一些，河水就不会泛滥了。


被动语态:
The streets were flooded after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，街道被淹了。
The area is being flooded by the rising tide. = 正在上涨的潮水正在淹没该地区。


疑问句:
Has the river flooded yet? = 河水已经泛滥了吗？
Is the water flowing too fast? = 水流得太快了吗？


否定句:
The river didn't flood this year. = 今年河水没有泛滥。
The water wasn't flowing very quickly. = 水流得并不很快。


PHRASE->

flooding rains = 暴雨

coastal flooding = 沿海洪涝
severe flooding = 严重洪涝
flood damage = 水灾损失
