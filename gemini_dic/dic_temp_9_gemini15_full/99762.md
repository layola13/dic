intermediaries:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiˌɛəriz/| n. |中间商，媒介，调解人 |People or organizations that act as a link between two other parties, often to facilitate a transaction or communication.  |例句：Real estate agents act as intermediaries between buyers and sellers.  房地产经纪人充当买卖双方之间的中间人。

例句：The UN acted as intermediaries in the peace negotiations. 联合国在和平谈判中充当调解人。|近义词：mediators, agents, go-betweens, facilitators |反义词： principals, direct parties


USAGE->

The bank acts as an intermediary in many financial transactions. = 银行在许多金融交易中充当中间人。
Several companies serve as intermediaries between manufacturers and retailers. = 几家公司充当制造商和零售商之间的中间商。
