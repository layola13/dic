grime:/ɡraɪm/| n.|污垢，尘垢| ingrained dirt that is difficult to remove; a layer of dirt that has become ingrained.  |例句：The grime on the window obscured the view. 窗户上的污垢遮挡了视线。

例句：Years of neglect had left the house covered in grime. 多年的忽视使房子布满了污垢。


近义词：dirt, filth, soil, smut, muck|反义词：cleanliness, purity|词性:名词


词源:  The word "grime" originates from the Middle English word "grim," meaning "dirt" or "filth."  This, in turn, likely derives from Old English or Proto-Germanic roots related to concepts of "dirt" or "darkness."
