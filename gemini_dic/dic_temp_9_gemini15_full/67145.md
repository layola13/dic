expose's:/ɪkˈspoʊz/ | v. | 揭露；使暴露；使曝光 | To reveal or uncover something that was previously hidden or unknown.  It can also refer to making something vulnerable or subject to something. | 例句：The journalist exposed the politician's corruption.  记者揭露了这位政治家的腐败行为。  The film exposed the harsh realities of poverty. 这部电影揭露了贫困的残酷现实。 | 近义词：reveal, unveil, disclose, uncover | 反义词：conceal, hide, cover up | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun exposes the land to its rays. = 太阳把土地暴露在阳光下。
He exposes his vulnerabilities to his therapist. = 他向他的治疗师暴露了他的弱点。


一般过去时:
The investigation exposed a major fraud. = 调查揭露了一起重大诈骗案。
She exposed the dangerous chemicals to the air. = 她将危险的化学物质暴露在空气中。


现在完成时:
The media has exposed several scandals this year. = 媒体今年已经曝光了几起丑闻。
They have exposed the children to various cultures. = 他们让孩子们接触了各种文化。


过去完成时:
The documents had exposed the company's illegal activities. = 这些文件已经揭露了这家公司的非法活动。
She had exposed the truth before the trial. = 在审判之前，她已经揭露了真相。


将来时:
The report will expose the environmental damage. = 这份报告将揭露环境破坏。
We will expose the students to new learning methods. = 我们将让学生接触新的学习方法。


虚拟语气:
If I exposed the truth, I would face severe consequences. = 如果我揭露真相，我将面临严重的处罚。
I wish I had exposed the problem sooner. = 我希望我早点发现这个问题。


被动语态:
The scandal was exposed by the press. = 这起丑闻被媒体曝光了。
The children were exposed to harmful substances. = 孩子们接触到了有害物质。


疑问句:
Will the investigation expose the truth? = 调查会揭露真相吗？
Has the company exposed its financial problems? = 公司是否已经公开了其财务问题？


否定句:
The government did not expose the full extent of the disaster. = 政府没有公开灾难的全部情况。
They did not expose the children to any danger. = 他们没有让孩子们接触到任何危险。
