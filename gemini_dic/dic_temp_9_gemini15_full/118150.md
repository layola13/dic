menstruate:/ˈmɛnstruˌeɪt/| v.|月经来潮；行经|To experience menstruation; the regular discharge of blood from the uterus of women of reproductive age.|例句：She started menstruating at the age of twelve. 她十二岁开始来月经。|例句：Many women experience discomfort during menstruation. 许多女性在月经期间感到不适。|近义词：menstruate, have one's period, bleed|反义词:无

词源:
