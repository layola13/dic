consignature: /kənˈsɪɡnətʃər/ | n. | 共同签署；连署；共同签字 | The act of signing a document together with another person or persons; joint signature.  It often implies a shared responsibility or agreement. | Example: The consignature of the CEO and CFO was required to authorize the large expenditure.  共同签署CEO和CFO的签名是批准这笔巨额支出的必要条件。 | 近义词：joint signature, co-signature | 反义词：sole signature, individual signature | 名词
