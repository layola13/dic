prefinishing: /ˌpriːˈfɪnɪʃɪŋ/ | n. | 预加工；预处理 | The process of applying a finish to a surface before the final finish is applied.  It often involves steps like sanding, priming, or applying a base coat to improve the quality of the final finish.  | Example:  The prefinishing process ensured a smooth and even surface for the final paint application.  预处理过程确保了最终涂漆表面的光滑和平整。 |近义词：preparation, pretreatment | 反义词：finishing (final finishing)
