pourtray:/pɔːrˈtreɪ/ | vt. | 描绘；描写；扮演 | To represent or describe someone or something in a particular way, especially in a work of art.  |例句：The painting portrays a young woman in a red dress. 这幅画描绘了一位穿着红裙子的年轻女子。

The novel portrays the struggles of a working-class family. 这部小说描绘了一个工人阶级家庭的挣扎。|近义词：depict, represent, depict, illustrate |反义词：misrepresent, distort

一般现在时:
He portrays the character with great skill. = 他以高超的技巧扮演这个角色。
The artist portrays nature's beauty in his paintings. = 艺术家在他的画作中描绘了大自然的美丽。

一般过去时:
