gushers:/ˈɡʌʃərz/| n.|喷涌者；涌出物；（尤指）油井喷油 | A gusher is something that flows or pours out rapidly and abundantly, especially a well that produces a large amount of oil or gas.  It can also refer to a person who expresses their feelings or opinions excessively. |例句：The newly discovered oil well was a gusher, producing thousands of barrels a day.  这口新发现的油井是个喷油井，每天产油数千桶。

例句：He's such a gusher; he never stops talking about his accomplishments. 他真是个夸夸其谈的人；他没完没了地谈论自己的成就。


近义词：spouter, fountain, torrent, outburst | 反义词：trickle, drip, meager flow| 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The oil gushes from the well. = 石油从油井中喷涌而出。
He gushes about his new car. = 他滔滔不绝地谈论他的新车。


一般过去时:
The geyser gushed water high into the air. = 间歇泉喷出水柱，高高地冲向空中。
She gushed with excitement when she saw the gift. = 当她看到礼物时，她激动地叫了起来。


现在完成时:
The volcano has gushed lava for weeks. = 火山已经喷发了数周的熔岩。
He has gushed about his girlfriend to everyone he knows. = 他向他认识的每一个人夸耀他的女朋友。


过去完成时:
The pipe had gushed water before the plumber arrived. = 水管工到达之前，管道已经喷水了。
She had gushed about the movie to her friends before they saw it. = 在朋友们看电影之前，她已经向他们大肆吹嘘这部电影了。


将来时:
The geyser will gush again tomorrow. = 间歇泉明天还会再次喷发。
I'm sure he will gush with pride when he sees his son graduate. = 我确信当他看到儿子毕业时，他会非常自豪。


虚拟语气:
If the dam broke, the water would gush into the valley. = 如果大坝决堤，水就会涌入山谷。
I wish he wouldn't gush so much. = 我希望他不那么夸夸其谈。


被动语态:
The oil was gushed from the well. = 石油从油井中喷涌而出。(被动语态在此略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
Her talent was gushed over by critics. = 评论家们对她的才华赞不绝口。(被动语态在此更强调被赞美)


疑问句:
Does the fountain still gush water? = 喷泉还在喷水吗？
Will he gush about his trip again? = 他还会再次夸耀他的旅行吗？


否定句:
The water didn't gush out of the tap. = 水没有从水龙头里喷出来。
She didn't gush over the new painting. = 她对这幅新画并没有赞不绝口。
