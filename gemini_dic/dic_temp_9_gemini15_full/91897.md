hyperlink:/ˈhaɪpərˌlɪŋk/| n. |超链接，网页链接 |A hyperlink is a link from a hypertext document to another location, typically activated by clicking on a highlighted word or image.  It allows users to quickly navigate between different parts of a website or to other websites.| Example:  Click on this hyperlink to visit our homepage. 点击此超链接访问我们的主页。|近义词：link, URL, reference |反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This website contains many hyperlinks to external resources. = 这个网站包含许多指向外部资源的超链接。
The document uses hyperlinks to navigate between sections. = 该文档使用超链接在各个部分之间导航。
