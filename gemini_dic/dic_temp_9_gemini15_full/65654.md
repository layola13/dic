evaporative:/ɪˈvæpəreɪtɪv/| adj.|蒸发的，汽化的|Relating to or involving evaporation.  The process of turning from a liquid into a gas.|The evaporative cooler helped lower the temperature in the room. 蒸发冷却器帮助降低了房间的温度。|近义词：vaporizing, vaporous|反义词：condensing
