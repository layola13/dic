drying: /ˈdraɪɪŋ/ | v. adj. n. | 干燥；使干燥；晾干 | The process of removing moisture from something.  |近义词：desiccation, dehydration | 反义词：wetting, moistening


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun is drying the clothes. = 太阳正在晒干衣服。
She is drying her hair with a towel. = 她正用毛巾擦干头发。


一般过去时:
I dried the dishes after dinner. = 晚饭后我擦干了盘子。
He dried his hands on a napkin. = 他用餐巾纸擦干了手。


现在完成时:
I have dried all the laundry. = 我已经把所有的衣服都晾干了。
The paint has dried completely. = 油漆已经完全干了。


过去完成时:
The clothes had dried before we left. = 我们离开之前衣服已经干了。
The food had dried out by the time we got home. = 我们到家的时候食物已经干了。


将来时:
The clothes will dry quickly in this sun. = 在这样的阳光下，衣服会很快干。
I will dry the dishes after I finish cooking. = 我做完饭后会洗碗。


虚拟语气:
If it were not raining, the laundry would dry quickly. = 如果不下雨，衣服会很快干。
I wish the paint had dried faster. = 我希望油漆干得更快。


被动语态:
The clothes are being dried in the dryer. = 衣服正在烘干机里烘干。
The dishes were dried by the dishwasher. = 盘子是洗碗机洗干的。


疑问句:
Is the laundry drying properly? = 衣服晾得干吗？
Are you drying the dishes by hand? = 你用手擦干盘子吗？


否定句:
The paint isn't drying fast enough. = 油漆干得不够快。
She didn't dry her hair before going out. = 她出门前没有把头发擦干。


PHRASE->
air drying: 风干
freeze-drying: 冷冻干燥
heat drying: 热风干燥
spray drying: 喷雾干燥
