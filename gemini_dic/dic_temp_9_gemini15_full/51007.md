desists:/dɪˈzɪsts/| v.|停止；终止；放弃|To stop doing something; to cease.|He desisted from his bad habits after the accident. 他在事故后改掉了坏习惯。|cease, stop, discontinue|continue, persist
USAGE->
一般现在时:
He desists from smoking. = 他戒烟了。
She desists from criticizing others. = 她不再批评别人了。

一般过去时:
He desisted from his plan. = 他放弃了他的计划。
They desisted from further argument. = 他们停止了进一步的争论。

现在完成时:
He has desisted from gambling. = 他已经戒赌了。
She has desisted from her unhealthy diet. = 她已经停止了不健康的饮食。

过去完成时:
He had desisted from drinking before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经戒酒了。
They had desisted from their protests before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他们已经停止了抗议。

将来时:
He will desist from interfering in their affairs. = 他将不再干涉他们的事务。
She will desist from making such foolish remarks. = 她将不再发表这种愚蠢的言论。

虚拟语气:
If he desisted from his bad habits, he would be healthier. = 如果他改掉坏习惯，他会更健康。
I wish he would desist from complaining all the time. = 我希望他能停止一直抱怨。

被动语态:
His bad behavior was desisted from. = 他的不良行为被制止了。(This is less common)
Further action was desisted from. = 进一步的行动被停止了。(This is less common)

疑问句:
Will he desist from his actions? = 他会停止他的行动吗？
Did she desist from her attempts? = 她放弃尝试了吗？

否定句:
He did not desist from his efforts. = 他没有放弃努力。
She did not desist from her pursuit of knowledge. = 她没有放弃对知识的追求。


PHRASE->
desist from =停止；避免
desist in = (较少见，通常指在某事上停止)
desist entirely = 完全停止
desist completely = 完全停止
desist immediately = 立即停止
