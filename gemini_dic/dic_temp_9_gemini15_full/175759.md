rapturing: /ˈræptʃərɪŋ/ | v. | 使狂喜；使着迷 |  The exhilarating experience of rapturing filled her heart.  | 近义词：enthralling, captivating, enchanting | 反义词：disappointing, boring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music rapturing the audience. = 音乐使观众着迷。
The beautiful scenery rapturing her. =美丽的景色使她着迷。

一般过去时:
The news raptured the whole family. = 这个消息使全家人都欣喜若狂。
The performance raptured the critics. = 这场演出使评论家们赞叹不已。

现在完成时:
The artist's work has raptured many people. = 艺术家的作品使许多人着迷。
His kindness has raptured her heart. = 他的善良俘获了她的芳心。

过去完成时:
They had been raptured by the sunset before the storm came. =暴风雨来临之前，他们已被日落迷住了。
She had been raptured by his words before she realized the truth. = 在她意识到真相之前，她已经被他的话语迷住了。


将来时:
The concert will rapture the crowd. = 音乐会将使人群欣喜若狂。
The new technology will rapture the world. = 新技术将使世界着迷。


虚拟语气:

I wish the painting could rapture me as much as it did before. = 我希望这幅画能像以前一样让我着迷。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
