laths:/læðz/| n.|木条，细木条|Thin strips of wood used as a base for plaster or for other building purposes.  They are typically narrow and relatively lightweight.|例句：The plasterer carefully applied the plaster to the laths. 石膏匠小心翼翼地将石膏涂抹在木条上。

例句：The old house's walls were made of laths and plaster.  这栋老房子的墙壁是用木条和石膏做的。


近义词：strips, slats, battens|反义词：None specifically|词性:名词


词源:  The word "lath" comes from the Old English word "læþe," meaning "a thin strip of wood."  This word is related to other Germanic words with similar meanings.
