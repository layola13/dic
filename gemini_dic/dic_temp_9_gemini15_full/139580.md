obviated:/ˈɒbvieɪtɪd/ /ˈɑːbvieɪtɪd/ | vt. |消除，避免，使…不必要 | To remove a need or difficulty.  To anticipate and prevent (a difficulty or inconvenience).|

The new bridge obviated the need to take the long, winding road. = 新桥消除了走那条漫长而蜿蜒的道路的必要。

The improved design obviated many of the previous problems. = 改进的设计避免了许多以前的问题。


近义词：avoid, prevent, eliminate, preclude, remove

反义词：cause, necessitate, require


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new system obviates many problems. = 新系统消除了许多问题。
This solution obviates the need for further action. = 此解决方案消除了进一步行动的必要。

一般过去时:
The invention of the telephone obviated the need for telegrams. = 电话的发明消除了对电报的需求。
The recent changes obviated the need for extra staff. = 最近的变革消除了对额外员工的需求。

现在完成时:
Technological advancements have obviated many manual tasks. = 技术进步已经消除了许多手工任务。
The new software has obviated the need for multiple programs. = 新软件消除了对多个程序的需求。

过去完成时:
They had obviated the potential risk by taking precautions. = 他们通过采取预防措施消除了潜在的风险。
The engineers had obviated the structural flaws before construction. = 工程师们在施工前消除了结构缺陷。

将来时:
The new policy will obviate the need for lengthy negotiations. = 新政策将消除冗长的谈判的必要。
This technology will obviate the need for human intervention. = 这项技术将消除人工干预的必要。

虚拟语气:
If we had planned better, we could have obviated many difficulties. = 如果我们计划得更好，我们本可以避免许多困难。
I wish we had obviated the problem earlier. = 我希望我们早点解决这个问题。

被动语态:
Many problems were obviated by the new software. = 新软件消除了许多问题。
The need for extra resources was obviated by the efficient planning. = 高效的规划消除了对额外资源的需求。

疑问句:
Can this new technology obviate the current challenges? = 这项新技术能否消除当前的挑战？
Will the proposed solution obviate the risks involved? = 提出的解决方案能否消除所涉及的风险？

否定句:
The current system does not obviate the need for manual input. = 当前系统并不能消除手动输入的必要。
The new policy did not obviate all the concerns. = 新政策并没有消除所有担忧。


PHRASE->
obviate the need for = 消除…的必要
obviate the problem of = 消除…的问题
obviate the risk of = 消除…的风险
obviate the difficulty of = 消除…的困难
obviate the necessity of = 消除…的必要性

词源:
源自拉丁语 *obviare，意为“to meet, encounter, get in the way of”，前缀ob-表示“against, toward”，词根via表示“way, road”。  字面意思为“to get in the way of, to meet”，后来演变为“to anticipate and prevent”。
