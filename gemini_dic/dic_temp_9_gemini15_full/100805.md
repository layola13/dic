USAGE->

一般现在时:
He intrigues against his rivals. 他密谋对付他的对手。
The mystery of the lost treasure intrigues many people. 失落的宝藏之谜吸引了许多人。


一般过去时:
The nobles intrigued to overthrow the king. 贵族们密谋推翻国王。
The story intrigued me so much that I couldn't put down the book.  这个故事太吸引我了，我根本放不下书。


现在完成时:
They have intrigued for years to gain control. 他们多年来一直在暗中策划以获得控制权。
The unusual circumstances have intrigued investigators for months.  这不同寻常的情况已经让调查人员困惑了几个月。


过去完成时:

将来时:
