rethinks:/ˌriːˈθɪŋks/ | vt. |重新考虑；再思考 | To think again about (something), reconsider.  |例句：He rethinks his decision after hearing new evidence. 他在听到新的证据后重新考虑了他的决定。
例句：The company is rethinking its marketing strategy. 公司正在重新考虑其营销策略。|近义词： reconsider, re-evaluate, review | 反义词： confirm, endorse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rethinks his approach every day. = 他每天都在重新思考他的方法。
They rethink their strategies regularly. = 他们定期重新思考他们的策略。


一般过去时:
She rethought her answer after a moment's pause. = 她停顿了一下后重新考虑了她的回答。
They rethought their business plan after the market crash. = 市场崩盘后，他们重新考虑了他们的商业计划。


现在完成时:
He has rethought his position on the issue. = 他已经重新考虑了他在这个问题上的立场。
The government has rethought its economic policies. = 政府已经重新考虑了它的经济政策。


过去完成时:
She had rethought her decision before she announced it. = 在宣布决定之前，她已经重新考虑过了。
They had rethought their strategy many times before they finally succeeded. = 在他们最终成功之前，他们已经多次重新考虑了他们的策略。


将来时:
He will rethink his plans if the situation changes. = 如果情况发生变化，他会重新考虑他的计划。
The company will rethink its approach to customer service. = 公司将重新考虑其客户服务方法。


虚拟语气:
If he had rethought his actions, he might have avoided the consequences. = 如果他重新考虑了他的行为，他可能就能避免后果。
I wish I had rethought my decision before I committed to it. = 我真希望我在做出承诺之前重新考虑过我的决定。


被动语态:
His decision was rethought by the committee. = 他的决定被委员会重新考虑了。
The proposal was rethought and revised several times. = 该提案被重新考虑并修改了几次。


疑问句:
Will she rethink her decision? = 她会重新考虑她的决定吗？
Have they rethought their strategy yet? = 他们已经重新考虑他们的策略了吗？


否定句:
He didn't rethink his approach, and it cost him dearly. = 他没有重新考虑他的方法，这让他付出了巨大的代价。
The company did not rethink its marketing campaign despite poor results. = 尽管结果不佳，公司并没有重新考虑其营销活动。
