ellipsoidal:/ɪˌlɪpsˈɔɪdəl/| adj.|椭球形的，椭圆形的|Having the shape of an ellipsoid.  An ellipsoid is a three-dimensional shape that is like a stretched-out sphere.  |例句：The planet's ellipsoidal shape is due to its rotation.  这个行星的椭球形状是由于它的自转造成的。

The Earth is not perfectly spherical, but rather ellipsoidal. 地球并非完美的球形，而是椭球形的。|近义词：oval, oblong, spheroidal|反义词：spherical (perfectly round)
