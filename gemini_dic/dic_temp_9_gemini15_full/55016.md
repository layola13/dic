disputer:/dɪˈspjuːtər/| n. |争论者；辩论者 |A disputer is someone who frequently engages in arguments or debates. They may be skilled debaters, but their focus might be on winning rather than finding common ground.  |Example: He's a notorious disputer, always ready to pick a fight over the smallest detail. 他是个臭名昭著的争论者，总是为最小的细节而争吵。|近义词：arguers, debaters, controversialist |反义词：peacemaker, conciliator |名词

词源:
From the verb "dispute," meaning to argue or contend, ultimately derived from the Old French "disputer" and the Latin "disputare," meaning "to argue, debate, discuss."  The "-er" suffix indicates an agent or doer of the action.
