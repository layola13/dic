deployment:/dɪˈplɔɪmənt/ | n. | 部署；展开；配置 | The act of deploying; the state of being deployed.  Deployment refers to the act of putting something into action or use, especially military forces, software, or other resources.  It can also describe the arrangement or distribution of something. | 例句：The military deployment involved thousands of troops.  军事部署涉及数千名士兵。

The software deployment was completed successfully. 软件部署成功完成。

| 近义词：distribution, implementation, installation,  rollout | 反义词：retreat, withdrawal |  名词
