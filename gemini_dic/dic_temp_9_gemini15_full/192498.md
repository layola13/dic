scruple:/ˈskruːpəl/| n. |顾虑；不安；一丝不苟；犹豫 |A feeling of doubt or hesitation with regard to the morality or propriety of a course of action.  |例句：He had no scruples about cheating on the exam. 他对考试作弊毫无顾虑。|例句：She acted with scrupulous care. 她一丝不苟地行动。|近义词：hesitation, qualm, doubt, misgiving |反义词：recklessness, ruthlessness, unconcern
