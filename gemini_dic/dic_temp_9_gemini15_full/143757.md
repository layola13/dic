outman:/ˌaʊtˈmæn/| vt.|胜过，智胜|To surpass or defeat in skill, cleverness, or strategy.|例句：The experienced strategist outmanned his opponent.  经验丰富的战略家智胜了他的对手。|例句：The smaller team outmanned the larger one through superior tactics.  规模较小的团队凭借更优越的战术击败了规模较大的团队。|近义词：outsmart, outwit, outdo, surpass|反义词：be outmaneuvered, be defeated|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outmans his opponents in every chess game. = 他在每一盘象棋游戏中都胜过对手。
She consistently outmans her rivals in negotiations. = 她总是在谈判中胜过对手。

一般过去时:
The general outmanned the enemy forces through clever strategy. = 将军凭借巧妙的策略战胜了敌军。
Our team outmanned the opposing team in the final match. = 我们队在决赛中胜过了对方球队。

现在完成时:
He has outmanned every competitor he has faced this year. = 他今年已经战胜了他遇到的每一个竞争对手。
She has outmanned her rivals in the industry for many years. = 多年来，她在业界一直胜过竞争对手。

过去完成时:
They had outmanned their rivals before the final round of negotiations. = 在谈判的最后一轮之前，他们就已经胜过了竞争对手。
The company had outmanned its competitors in the market before the new product launch. = 在新产品发布之前，该公司已经在市场上胜过了竞争对手。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
