coniferous:/kəˈnɪfərəs/| adj.|针叶的；松柏科的|Of or relating to the Coniferae, an order of mostly evergreen trees and shrubs that bear cones. Coniferous trees are characterized by their needle-like or scale-like leaves and their cone-shaped seeds.|例句：Pine trees are coniferous trees. 松树是针叶树。|例句：The coniferous forest stretched for miles. 针叶林绵延数英里。|近义词：needle-leaved, evergreen (in the context of trees)|反义词：deciduous
