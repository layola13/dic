misshipping:/mɪsˈʃɪpɪŋ/| n.|错运，寄错|The act of sending something to the wrong address or recipient.  It often involves errors in labeling, addressing, or handling during the shipping process. |例句：The misshipping of the package caused a significant delay in the project.  包裹寄错导致项目严重延误。|近义词：misdelivery, misdirection, wrong delivery|反义词：correct shipping, accurate delivery|名词
