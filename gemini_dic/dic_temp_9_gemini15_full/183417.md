reprosecuting: /ˌriːˈprɒsɪkjuːtɪŋ/ | v. | 再起诉，重新提起公诉 | The act of prosecuting someone again for the same crime after a previous prosecution has ended.  This often happens if new evidence is found or if there were procedural errors in the initial trial. | Example: The case was dismissed, but the prosecutor is considering reprosecuting. = 该案已被驳回，但检察官正在考虑重新起诉。  The defendant was acquitted, but the prosecution is exploring options for reprosecuting. = 被告已被宣告无罪，但检方正在探索重新起诉的可能性。 | 近义词：reindict, retry | 反义词：dismiss, acquit
