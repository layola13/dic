scientifically:/ˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪkli/| adv.|科学地，根据科学地|In a way that is based on or agrees with the principles of science.|The experiment was conducted scientifically.|近义词：systematically, methodically|反义词：unscientifically, haphazardly
