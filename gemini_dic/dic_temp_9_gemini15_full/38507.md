conciliates:/ˈkɒnsɪlieɪts/ /ˈkɑːnsɪlieɪts/ | vt. | 安抚；调解；劝诱；使和解 | To appease or pacify someone, especially by making concessions. To make someone less angry or hostile. To bring (opposing parties) into a harmonious relationship. |  例句：The mediator worked hard to conciliate the warring factions. 调解员努力安抚交战的各派。

The government attempted to conciliate the protestors with promises of reform.  政府试图通过承诺改革来安抚抗议者。


近义词：appease, placate, pacify, reconcile, mollify


反义词：antagonize, alienate, exacerbate, inflame


词性：动词
