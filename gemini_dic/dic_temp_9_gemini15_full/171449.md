puerperous:/pjuːˈpɜːrəs/| adj.| 产后的；分娩后的；产褥期的 | Relating to or characteristic of the period after childbirth.  The term often refers to the time immediately following childbirth, encompassing physical and emotional changes in the mother. | Example:  The puerperal period is a time of significant hormonal shifts. = 产褥期是激素发生重大变化的时期。

The puerperous woman experienced fatigue and emotional lability. = 产后妇女感到疲劳和情绪波动。|近义词：postpartum, postnatal |反义词：antenatal, prenatal
