honeypots:/ˈhʌniˌpɑːts/ | n. |蜜罐；诱饵系统 |A honeypot is a cybersecurity decoy designed to lure and trap attackers, allowing security professionals to study their techniques and prevent future attacks.  It mimics a valuable system or data, attracting malicious actors while being closely monitored.  |Example: The company deployed several honeypots to identify and analyze the attack patterns of hackers.|近义词：decoy, lure, trap |反义词：firewall, security system (in the sense of protection, not attraction)
