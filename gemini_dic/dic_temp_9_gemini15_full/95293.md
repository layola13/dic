incarn:/ɪnˈkɑːrn/| v.|化身，体现|To embody or represent (a quality, idea, etc.) in human or physical form.  |例句：The movie incarnates the spirit of rebellion. 这部电影体现了叛逆的精神。|例句：He incarnated the role of Hamlet perfectly. 他完美地诠释了哈姆雷特的角色。|近义词：embody, exemplify, personify|反义词：de-embody
