fox:/fɒks/ /fɑːks/ | n. |狐狸 |A fox is a small, wild canine mammal with reddish-brown fur, a pointed muzzle, and a bushy tail. Foxes are known for their cunning and intelligence. They are omnivores, feeding on small mammals, birds, fruits, and insects. |例句：The fox cleverly escaped from the hunter.  狐狸巧妙地逃脱了猎人的追捕。

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.  那只敏捷的棕色狐狸跳过了懒惰的狗。

近义词：vixen (female fox), Reynard (literary name for a fox)

反义词：无直接反义词，可根据语境选择如：sheep (象征无害)
