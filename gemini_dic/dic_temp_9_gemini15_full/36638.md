collectivizes:/kəˈlɛktaɪzaɪz/| vt.| 集体化；使集体所有 | To bring under collective ownership or control.  The process of bringing land, industries, or other resources under collective ownership and control, typically by a government. | 例句：The Soviet Union collectivized agriculture in the 1930s.  = 20世纪30年代，苏联对农业进行了集体化。 The government attempted to collectivize the fishing industry. = 政府试图将渔业集体化。|近义词：nationalize, socialize|反义词：privatize, individualize|动词
