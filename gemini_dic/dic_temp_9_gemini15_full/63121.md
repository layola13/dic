enslaved: /ɪnˈsleɪvd/ | adj. | 被奴役的，受奴役的 |  Describes a person or group who are forced to work for another person without pay and against their will.  It implies a state of captivity and oppression. | Example: The enslaved people worked tirelessly in the fields.  被奴役的人们在田里不知疲倦地工作。  The enslaved population suffered greatly under the harsh conditions. 被奴役的人口在恶劣的条件下遭受了巨大的痛苦。| 近义词：captive, subjugated, bonded,  imprisoned | 反义词：free, liberated, emancipated |  形容词
