disproves:/ˌdɪsˈpruːvz/| vt.|证明…是错误的；驳斥|To show something to be false or incorrect.|The experiment disproves the scientist's theory.  实验驳斥了科学家的理论。|The new evidence disproves his alibi. 新证据证明了他的不在场证明是假的。|refutes, contradicts, invalidates|proves, confirms, validates|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disproves the rumor every day. 他每天都驳斥这个谣言。
The data disproves the initial hypothesis. 数据驳斥了最初的假设。

一般过去时:
The experiment disproved the existing theory.  实验驳斥了现有的理论。
She disproved the accusations against him. 她驳斥了针对他的指控。

现在完成时:
Recent research has disproved many traditional beliefs. 最近的研究已经驳斥了许多传统观念。
Scientific advancements have disproved previous assumptions. 科学进步已经证伪了先前的假设。

过去完成时:
They had disproved the myth long before the publication. 他们在出版物之前很久就驳斥了这个神话。
The evidence had disproved the defendant's claim. 证据已经驳斥了被告的主张。

将来时:
Further testing will disprove the current model. 进一步的测试将推翻目前的模型。
The upcoming study will disprove the prevailing idea.  即将进行的研究将驳斥流行的观点。

虚拟语气:
If we had more data, we could disprove their claim. 如果我们有更多数据，我们就能驳斥他们的说法。
I wish I could disprove the conspiracy theories. 我希望我能驳斥阴谋论。

被动语态:
His theory was disproved by new evidence. 他的理论被新的证据驳斥了。
The hypothesis was disproved by the experimental results. 假设被实验结果驳斥了。

疑问句:
Does this evidence disprove the hypothesis? 这证据是否驳斥了假设？
Can we disprove the prevailing theory? 我们能驳斥流行的理论吗？

否定句:
The study did not disprove the hypothesis. 这项研究并没有驳斥该假设。
He couldn't disprove the allegations against him. 他无法驳斥针对他的指控。
