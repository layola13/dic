pseudo-mongolian:/ˌsuːdoʊmɒŋˈɡoʊliən/|/ˌsjuːdəʊmɒŋˈɡoʊliən/| adj.|伪蒙古的；貌似蒙古的 |Appearing to be or resembling something from Mongolia, but not genuinely so.  Often used to describe things that are stylistically reminiscent of Mongolian art, culture, or design, but lack authentic Mongolian origins or characteristics.  |例句：The museum curator discovered that the purportedly "ancient Mongolian artifact" was actually a pseudo-mongolian imitation. 博物馆馆长发现所谓的“古代蒙古文物”实际上是伪蒙古仿制品。

例句：His "Mongolian-inspired" painting, while visually appealing, is considered pseudo-mongolian by true art historians.  他的“蒙古风格”绘画虽然赏心悦目，但在真正的艺术史学家看来却是伪蒙古的。


近义词：fake, imitation, spurious, sham, artificial


反义词：genuine, authentic, real, true


词性:形容词
