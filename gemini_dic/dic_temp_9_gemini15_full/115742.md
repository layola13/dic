marigold:/ˈmærɪɡoʊld/|/ˈmæriɡəʊld/| n. |金盏花；万寿菊 |A marigold is a plant with bright yellow or orange flowers.  They are often grown in gardens for their vibrant color and are sometimes used in cooking and medicine. |The marigolds in her garden were in full bloom.  她花园里的金盏花盛开了。

The vibrant marigolds added a splash of color to the otherwise drab landscape.  鲜艳的金盏花为原本单调的景色增添了一抹色彩。|calendula, pot marigold |  | noun
