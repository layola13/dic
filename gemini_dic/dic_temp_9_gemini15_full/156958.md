pilgrimer:/ˈpɪlɡrɪmər/| n. |朝圣者，香客|A pilgrim is a person who journeys to a sacred place for religious reasons.|例句：Many pilgrims travel to Mecca each year.  每年都有许多朝圣者前往麦加。|例句：The pilgrims walked for days to reach the holy city. 朝圣者们走了几天才到达圣城。|近义词：traveler, devotee, worshiper |反义词：nonbeliever


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pilgrim walks to the shrine. = 朝圣者步行前往神殿。
Pilgrims visit holy sites. = 朝圣者参观圣地。

一般过去时:
The pilgrim walked for miles. = 朝圣者走了数英里。
Pilgrims visited the temple last year. = 朝圣者去年参观了寺庙。

现在完成时:
The pilgrim has completed his journey. = 朝圣者已经完成了他的旅程。
Many pilgrims have already arrived. = 许多朝圣者已经到达。

过去完成时:
The pilgrim had prayed before starting his journey. = 朝圣者在开始旅程之前祈祷了。

将来时:
The pilgrim will travel to Jerusalem next year. = 朝圣者明年将前往耶路撒冷。

虚拟语气:
If I were a pilgrim, I would visit Rome. = 如果我是朝圣者，我会去罗马。
I wish I could be a pilgrim and visit the holy land. = 我希望我能成为朝圣者并参观圣地。

被动语态:

The pilgrimage was completed successfully. = 朝圣成功完成了。

疑问句:
Is he a pilgrim? = 他是朝圣者吗？
Are these pilgrims going to the same place? = 这些朝圣者要去同一个地方吗？

否定句:
He is not a pilgrim. = 他不是朝圣者。
