chinned:/tʃɪnd/| v.| 抬起下巴；用下巴支撑；|to raise one's chin, often in a defiant or proud manner; to support something with one's chin.|例句：She chinned her head defiantly. 她倔强地抬起了下巴。 He chinned the book while he read. 他看书时下巴抵着书。|近义词：lifted, raised, supported|反义词：lowered, dropped
