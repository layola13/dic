hanky's:/ˈhæŋkiːz/| n. |手帕（复数）；亨基的（所有格）|The word "hanky's" is a plural possessive form.  It refers to more than one handkerchief belonging to someone named Hanky, or it could be a colloquial shortening of "handkerchiefs". |例句：Those are Hanky's hankies. 那些是亨基的手帕。  The hanky's stained.  手帕弄脏了。(This usage is less common and relies on context to understand "hanky" as a handkerchief.) |近义词: handkerchiefs, tissues (in some contexts) |反义词: N/A |名词
