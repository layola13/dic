multigym: /ˈmʌltiˌdʒɪm/ | n. |多功能健身器材| A piece of exercise equipment that combines several different weight training machines into one unit.  It allows users to perform a variety of exercises targeting different muscle groups. |例句：He bought a multigym to work out at home. 他买了一台多功能健身器材在家锻炼。

例句：The gym offers a wide range of equipment, including a multigym and free weights. 这个健身房提供各种各样的器材，包括多功能健身器材和自由重量。


近义词：home gym, weight machine combination, all-in-one gym


反义词：single-function exercise machine
