pop's:/pɑːps/| n.|爸爸的；爸爸的店|A shortened form of "pop's place" or "dad's place", referring to a place owned or frequented by one's father, often a small business like a shop or diner.  It can also refer to things belonging to one's father.|例句：I'm going to pop's for a burger. 我要去爸爸开的店吃汉堡。|近义词：dad's, father's|反义词：无
