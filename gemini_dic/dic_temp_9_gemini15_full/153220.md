pelt:/pɛlt/ | n. v. |兽皮；皮毛；猛击；投掷 | The outer skin of an animal, especially when removed as a whole;  to strike with something thrown. |例句：The hunter carefully removed the pelt from the deer.  猎人小心翼翼地剥下鹿皮。

The children pelted the snowman with snowballs. 孩子们用雪球猛击雪人。|近义词：hide, skin (n.), strike, throw (v.) |反义词：  (n.) none  (v.)  receive, avoid |词性: 名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pelts the dog with stones. = 他用石头砸狗。
She regularly pelts her husband with questions. = 她经常向她丈夫提出一连串问题。

一般过去时:
They pelted the teacher with apples. = 他们用苹果砸老师。
The protesters pelted the police with eggs. = 抗议者用鸡蛋砸警察。

现在完成时:
The children have pelted the poor cat with pebbles all afternoon. = 孩子们整个下午都在用鹅卵石砸可怜的猫。
He has pelted me with insults for weeks. = 他几周来一直辱骂我。

过去完成时:
They had pelted the car with mud before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们已经往汽车上扔泥巴了。
She had pelted him with accusations before he could explain himself. = 在他能够解释自己之前，她已经对他进行了指控。

将来时:
They will pelt the rival team with tomatoes. = 他们将用西红柿砸对手队。
The fans will pelt the stage with flowers. = 粉丝们将向舞台上投掷鲜花。

虚拟语气:
If they pelted him with rotten fruit, he would be angry. = 如果他们用烂水果砸他，他会生气的。
I wish they hadn't pelted me with those insults. = 我真希望他们没有那样侮辱我。

被动语态:
The car was pelted with stones. = 汽车被石头砸了。
The politician was pelted with tomatoes. = 这位政治家被西红柿砸了。

疑问句:
Were the demonstrators pelted with tear gas? = 示威者是否遭到催泪瓦斯的袭击？
Did anyone pelt the speaker with questions? = 有人向演讲者提问了吗？

否定句:
He didn't pelt the window with a rock. = 他没有用石头砸窗户。
They didn't pelt the band with boos. = 他们没有对乐队嘘声。

词源:
From Old English *pel, meaning "skin," related to Old High German *fel, meaning "skin, hide".  The verb sense of "to throw repeatedly" developed later.
