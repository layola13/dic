rectilineous: /ˌrɛktɪˈlɪniəs/ | adj. | 直线的；直线形的 |  Characterized by straight lines; consisting of or bounded by straight lines.  The term refers to something having a straight or linear form, lacking curves or bends. |

例句：

The rectilineous path was the shortest distance between the two points.  = 直线路径是两点之间的最短距离。

The architect designed a rectilineous building with sharp angles and straight lines. = 建筑师设计了一座棱角分明、线条直线形的建筑。


近义词：straight, linear, direct

反义词：curvilinear, curved, winding


词性：形容词


词源：

From Latin *rectus ("straight") + līnea ("line").  The word combines the concept of "straight" and "line" to describe a characteristic of being straight-lined.
