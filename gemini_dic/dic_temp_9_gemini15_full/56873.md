dosa:/ˈdoʊsə/| n.|印度薄饼|A dosa is a thin crepe made from a fermented batter of rice and lentils. It is a popular South Indian breakfast food, often served with sambar (a vegetable stew) and chutney.|Example:  For breakfast, I had a delicious masala dosa.  早餐我吃了一份美味的马萨拉薄饼。|近义词：crepe, pancake|反义词：无
