outfloated:/ˌaʊtˈfloʊtɪd/ | v. | 漂浮出去；超越；胜过 |  To float out or away; to surpass or exceed in excellence or achievement.  | The balloons outfloated on the gentle breeze. 气球随微风飘走了。 | The new design outfloated all previous attempts. 新设计超越了所有之前的尝试。 | surpassed, exceeded, excelled |  sunk, failed | 动词
