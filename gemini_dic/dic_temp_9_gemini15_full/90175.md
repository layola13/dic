hotplate:/ˈhɒtpleɪt/ /ˈhɑːtpleɪt/| n. |电热板，电炉 |A hotplate is a portable electric cooking appliance with one or more heating elements.  It is often used for cooking small amounts of food or for keeping food warm. |例句：I cooked the eggs on a small hotplate. 我用一个小电热板煮鸡蛋。

例句：She used the hotplate to keep the soup warm. 她用电热板保温汤。


近义词：electric burner, heating element, cooker (in certain contexts) |反义词：refrigerator, icebox
