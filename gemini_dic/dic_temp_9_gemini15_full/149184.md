oxycymene:/ˈɒksɪsaɪmiːn/| n. |氧化伞素|A monoterpene hydrocarbon found in many essential oils, particularly those from plants in the Myrtaceae family (e.g., eucalyptus, tea tree). It has a woody, camphoraceous odor.  |例句：The analysis of the essential oil revealed a significant presence of oxycymene.  氧化伞素的分析显示其存在量很大。|近义词：terpene, hydrocarbon|反义词：无|名词
