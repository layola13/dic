erupted:/ɪˈrʌptɪd/| v.|喷发；爆发；突然发生|To break out suddenly and violently, especially with regard to a volcano or other natural phenomenon, or a conflict or other disruptive event.  |例句：The volcano erupted with a deafening roar. 火山爆发了，发出震耳欲聋的轰鸣声。

例句：Violence erupted in the streets after the election results were announced. 选举结果公布后，街头爆发了暴力事件。


近义词：burst forth, exploded, broke out, flared up|反义词：subsided, calmed down, quieted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The volcano erupts every few years. = 这座火山每隔几年就会喷发一次。
He erupts in anger at the slightest provocation. = 他稍受激怒就会勃然大怒。


一般过去时:
The geyser erupted spectacularly. = 那口间歇泉喷发得非常壮观。
A fight erupted between the two brothers. = 两个兄弟之间爆发了一场争吵。


现在完成时:
The volcano has erupted several times this century. = 这座火山在本世纪已经喷发过几次了。
Tension has erupted into open conflict. = 紧张局势已演变成公开冲突。


过去完成时:
The volcano had erupted before the villagers could evacuate. = 村民们还没来得及撤离，火山就喷发了。
A major argument had erupted before I even arrived at the party. = 我到聚会之前就已经爆发了一场大争论。


将来时:
Scientists predict the volcano will erupt soon. = 科学家预测火山很快就会喷发。
If you continue to provoke him, a fight will erupt. = 如果你继续激怒他，就会爆发一场争斗。


虚拟语气:
If the volcano had erupted earlier, more people would have been affected. = 如果火山早些喷发，受影响的人会更多。
If he hadn't lost his temper, the argument wouldn't have erupted. = 如果他不发脾气，争吵就不会爆发了。


被动语态:
The city was erupted by the sudden storm. = 城市被突如其来的暴风雨袭击了。(注意：此句被动语态使用略显不自然，通常用其他更贴切的表达)
The conflict was erupted by a misunderstanding. = 冲突是由误解引起的。(同样，此句被动语态也略显不自然)


疑问句:
When did the volcano last erupt? = 火山上次喷发是什么时候？
Did a fight erupt at the party? = 聚会上爆发了争吵吗？


否定句:
The volcano did not erupt that year. = 火山那一年没有喷发。
No fight erupted during the meeting. = 会议期间没有发生任何争吵。
