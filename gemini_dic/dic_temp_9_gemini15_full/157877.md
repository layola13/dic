plainest: /ˈpleɪnɪst/ | adj. | 最简单的；最朴素的；最清楚的 |  The simplest or most basic; lacking ornamentation or complexity;  easily understood or seen. |例句：Her dress was the plainest in the room.  她的衣服是房间里最朴素的。

例句：The instructions were so plainest that even a child could understand them. 说明书非常简单，即使小孩子也能理解。


近义词：simplest, clearest, most basic, unadorned, undecorated


反义词：most ornate, most elaborate, most complex, most embellished


词性：形容词


词源：源自古英语 *plæne，意思是“平坦的，平滑的”，后来引申出“简单，朴素”的意思。  This word's meaning developed from the physical sense of "flat" to the figurative sense of "simple" or "unadorned."
