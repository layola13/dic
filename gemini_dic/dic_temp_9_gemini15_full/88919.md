hold-to:/hoʊld tuː/ | phrasal verb | 坚持；遵守；墨守；依附 |  To hold-to means to adhere to something, typically a promise, agreement, or standard. It emphasizes the steadfastness and refusal to deviate from a commitment. |  The company held-to its promise to deliver the product on time. 公司信守承诺，按时交付产品。


The team held-to the original plan despite the unexpected challenges. 团队尽管面临意想不到的挑战，仍坚持最初的计划。| adhere to, stick to, abide by | deviate from, disregard, abandon | phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hold to their principles. = 他们坚持他们的原则。
We hold to the highest standards. = 我们坚持最高的标准。


一般过去时:
She held to her beliefs despite the pressure. = 尽管有压力，她还是坚持自己的信念。
He held to his promise, even though it was difficult. = 他信守承诺，即使这很困难。


现在完成时:
They have held to their agreement for many years. = 多年来他们一直遵守他们的协议。
We have held to our budget despite rising costs. = 尽管成本上升，我们仍然坚持我们的预算。


过去完成时:
She had held to her decision for a long time before changing her mind. = 她坚持她的决定很久才改变主意。
They had held to the old ways for generations. = 他们世世代代都坚持旧的习俗。


将来时:
We will hold to our schedule, regardless of any delays. = 无论遇到什么延误，我们都将坚持我们的时间表。
They will hold to their commitment to help the community. = 他们将信守对帮助社区的承诺。


虚拟语气:
If they held to their promises, things would be better. = 如果他们信守诺言，情况就会好转。
I wish I had held to my original plan. = 我真希望我坚持我的最初计划。


被动语态:
The agreement was held to by both parties. = 双方都遵守了协议。(This is less common usage)
The rules should be held to by everyone. = 每个人都应该遵守规则。(This is less common usage)


疑问句:
Will they hold to their word? = 他们会信守诺言吗？
Do they hold to the same standards? = 他们坚持同样的标准吗？


否定句:
They did not hold to the original plan. = 他们没有坚持最初的计划。
She didn't hold to her promise. = 她没有遵守诺言。



PHRASE->

hold-to a commitment  坚持承诺
