postdiagnostic: /ˌpoʊstˌdaɪəɡˈnɒstɪk/ | adj. | 诊断后的；诊断之后 |  Relating to the period of time after a diagnosis has been made.  This term is used in medical contexts to describe treatments, care, or studies that occur after a disease or condition has been identified. | Example: Postdiagnostic care is crucial for managing the long-term effects of the illness. 诊断后的护理对于控制疾病的长期影响至关重要。 | 近义词: post-diagnosis, after-diagnosis | 反义词: prediagnostic
