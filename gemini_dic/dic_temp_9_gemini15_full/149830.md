paleontology:/ˌpeɪliɒnˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. |古生物学|The scientific study of life that existed prior to, and sometimes including, the start of the Holocene Epoch (roughly 11,700 years before present).  It includes the study of fossils to classify organisms and study their evolution and interactions with their environments. |The paleontology department at the museum houses a vast collection of dinosaur fossils.  博物馆的古生物学系收藏了大量的恐龙化石。


The study of paleontology involves analyzing fossil evidence to understand extinct organisms.  古生物学的研究涉及分析化石证据以了解已灭绝的生物。|近义词：paleozoology (古动物学), paleobotany (古植物学)|反义词：暂无
