niggardship:/ˈnɪɡərdʃɪp/| n. |吝啬；小气；守财奴的行为|The extreme unwillingness to spend money; stinginess.  It refers to the actions and characteristics of a niggard, emphasizing the miserly and ungenerous nature of those actions.|His niggardship prevented him from donating to the charity, even though he could easily afford it.  他的吝啬阻止了他向慈善机构捐款，即使他很容易就能负担得起。|stinginess, miserliness, parsimony|generosity, extravagance, munificence|名词
