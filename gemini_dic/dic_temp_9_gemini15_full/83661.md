haemotachometer:/ˌhiːməʊtæˈkɒmɪtər/ | n. |血流速度计 |A haemotachometer is an instrument used to measure the velocity of blood flow.  It is often used in medical research and clinical settings to assess blood flow dynamics in various parts of the circulatory system. | Example: The researchers used a haemotachometer to measure blood flow velocity in the coronary arteries. 研究人员使用血流速度计测量冠状动脉的血流速度。 |近义词: blood flow meter, flowmeter |反义词:  (no direct antonym, as it measures a quantity rather than having an opposite)
