shivered:/ˈʃɪvərd/| v.|颤抖，哆嗦|To shake slightly because of cold, fear, or excitement.  |例句：She shivered in the cold wind.  她在寒风中瑟瑟发抖。|例句：He shivered with fear when he heard the noise.  听到那个声音，他吓得发抖。|近义词：tremble, shake, quiver|反义词：remain still, be calm


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shivers when he's cold. = 他冷的时候会发抖。
The leaves shiver in the breeze. = 树叶在微风中颤抖。

一般过去时:
I shivered all night. = 我整夜都在发抖。
She shivered at the thought. = 她一想到就发抖。

现在完成时:
He has shivered all day. = 他整天都在发抖。
She has never shivered so much before. = 她以前从未如此颤抖过。

过去完成时:
I had shivered for hours before help arrived. = 在得到帮助之前，我已经颤抖了几个小时。
They had shivered all night in the snow. = 他们在雪地里颤抖了一整夜。

将来时:
I will shiver if it rains. = 如果下雨我会发抖。
The dog will shiver if it gets wet. = 狗如果弄湿了会发抖。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would shiver in that cold. = 如果我是你，我会在那样的寒冷中发抖。
I wish I hadn't shivered so much last night. = 我真希望我昨晚没有抖那么厉害。

被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)
His body was shivered by the cold. = 他的身体被冻得发抖。(不自然，少用)
The poor dog was shivered by the sudden storm. = 可怜的狗被突如其来的暴风雨冻得发抖。(不自然，少用)


疑问句:
Are you shivering? = 你在发抖吗？
Does he shiver easily? = 他容易发抖吗？

否定句:
I didn't shiver at all. = 我一点也不发抖。
She didn't shiver in the slightest. = 她一点也不颤抖。


PHRASE->
shiver with cold = 冻得发抖
shiver with fear = 害怕得发抖
shiver with excitement = 激动得发抖
give someone the shivers = 让某人毛骨悚然
a shiver down one's spine = 一阵寒意
