rethreaded:/ˌriːˈθredɪd/| v. |重新缝合；重新拧上；重新穿线| Past participle of rethread.  It means to thread (a needle, screw, etc.) again, or to sew something again using thread.  It can also refer to a discussion or conversation that has been revisited and restarted. |例句：The tailor rethreaded the needle and continued sewing.  裁缝重新穿针引线，继续缝纫。

The surgeon rethreaded the suture to secure the wound. 外科医生重新缝合伤口以使其牢固。


近义词：resewn, re-stitched, restarted(in context of conversation) |反义词：untreaded(not a direct opposite, but implies the opposite action)
