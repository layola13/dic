milkmen:/ˈmɪlkmæn/| n. |送牛奶的人（复数）|Milkmen are people who deliver milk to homes and businesses.  This term is largely historical, as milk delivery is less common now.|例句：In the past, milkmen would deliver fresh milk to doorsteps every morning.  过去，送牛奶的人每天早上都会把鲜奶送到家门口。

The milkmen used to leave bottles on the porch. 送牛奶的人过去常常把瓶子放在门廊上。|近义词：milk deliverers, dairymen |反义词：无
