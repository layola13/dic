词源:
From Mediterranean + -ans.  "Mediterranean" itself derives from Latin *mediterraneus, from medius ("middle") + terra ("earth").  Thus, literally meaning "inland" or "in the middle of the land".  The "-ans" suffix is a common way to form plural nouns referring to people or inhabitants of a place.
