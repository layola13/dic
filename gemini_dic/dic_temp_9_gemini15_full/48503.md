deer:/dɪər/ | n. |鹿 |A hoofed herbivorous mammal with antlers in the male. Deer are known for their grace and agility. They are found in various habitats across the world. |例句：The deer gracefully leaped over the fence.  那只鹿优雅地跳过了栅栏。|例句：We saw a herd of deer grazing in the meadow. 我们看到一群鹿在草地上吃草。|近义词：stag, doe, fawn |反义词：无
