nilotic:/naɪˈlɒtɪk/ | adj. |尼罗河的；尼罗河居民的；尼罗特人的 | Relating to the Nile River or its people.  The term often refers to a group of ethnicities in East Africa speaking Nilotic languages. | Example:  The Nilotic peoples are known for their pastoral traditions.  尼罗特人以其畜牧传统而闻名。|近义词：Nilotic-speaking,  Nilote |反义词：无
