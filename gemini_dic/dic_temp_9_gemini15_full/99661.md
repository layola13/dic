interlibel:/ˌɪntərˈlaɪbəl/| n. |相互诽谤，互相中伤|The act of two or more parties engaging in mutual defamation or libel.  It involves reciprocal accusations of false and damaging statements.|例句：The two companies engaged in a bitter interlibel, each accusing the other of false advertising.  两个公司互相诽谤，互相指责对方虚假广告。|近义词：mutual defamation, reciprocal slander|反义词：praise, commendation|名词
