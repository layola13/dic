gropers:/ˈɡroʊpərz/| n. |摸索者，探索者，笨手笨脚的人|A groper is someone who feels around blindly or clumsily, often in search of something.  It can also refer to someone who inappropriately touches others. |例句：The blind man used his cane as a groper to navigate the busy street.  盲人用拐杖作为探路工具在繁忙的街道上行走。

The drunkard was a groper, stumbling around the bar.  那个醉汉像个笨手笨脚的人，在酒吧里跌跌撞撞。|近义词：explorer, searcher, fumbler|反义词：expert, skilled, adroit|
