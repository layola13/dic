glaucomatous:/ˌɡlɔːkəˈmeɪtəs/| adj.|青光眼的；青光眼引起的 | Relating to or characteristic of glaucoma.  Glaucoma is a condition of increased pressure within the eyeball, causing gradual loss of sight. |例句：The patient presented with glaucomatous optic nerve damage.  这位病人出现了青光眼性视神经损伤。

例句：He is suffering from glaucomatous visual field loss. 他正遭受青光眼引起的视野缺损。


近义词：glaucomatous, glaucomatic (less common)


反义词：None directly opposite, but terms describing healthy vision could be considered contrasting.  例如：healthy vision, normal vision


词性：形容词
