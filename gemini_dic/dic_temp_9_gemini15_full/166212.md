prepurposive: /ˌpriːˈpɜːrpəsɪv/ | adj. | 预先有目的的；有预谋的 |  Relating to or characterized by premeditation or forethought; done or made with a prior purpose or plan.  The word implies a deliberate intention or planning before an action takes place. |


例句：

The prepurposive actions of the attacker suggested a carefully planned assault.  = 袭击者的预谋行为表明这是一次精心策划的袭击。

Her prepurposive visit to the library indicated she needed to research something specific. = 她事先有计划地去图书馆表明她需要研究一些具体的东西。


近义词：intentional, deliberate, premeditated, planned, purposeful

反义词：accidental, unintentional, unplanned, spontaneous, impulsive

词性：形容词
