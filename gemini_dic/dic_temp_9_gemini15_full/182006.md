remeasuring:/ˌriːˈmɛʒərɪŋ/| v.|重新测量，再测量|The act of measuring something again, often to verify previous measurements or to account for changes.|例句：The surveyor is remeasuring the property lines to ensure accuracy.  测量员正在重新测量地界以确保精度。

例句：After the earthquake, engineers were remeasuring the structural integrity of the building. 地震后，工程师们正在重新测量建筑物的结构完整性。


近义词：re-measuring, measuring again


反义词：initial measuring, first measuring


词性：动词
