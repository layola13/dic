词源:

The word "sanitariness" derives from the word "sanitary," which comes from the Latin word "sanitas," meaning "health" or "wholesomeness."  The suffix "-ness" is added to form the noun, indicating a state or quality.


用法说明:

Sanitariness is primarily used as a noun to describe the condition of being clean and free from dirt, germs, or other harmful substances. It often refers to public health and environmental cleanliness.  The word is typically used in formal contexts, such as health reports or discussions about public infrastructure.
