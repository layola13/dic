commemorated:/kəˈmɛməreɪtɪd/| v.|纪念，庆祝 |To remember and celebrate an important event or person, especially with a ceremony.|例句：A ceremony was held to commemorate the 50th anniversary of the company.  一场仪式是为了纪念公司成立50周年而举行的。|例句：We commemorated the victims of the war with a minute of silence. 我们以默哀一分钟的方式纪念战争受害者。|近义词：celebrate, remember, honor|反义词：forget, ignore
