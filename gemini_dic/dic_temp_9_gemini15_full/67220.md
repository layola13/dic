expressway: /ˈɛkspresˌweɪ/ | n. | 高速公路 | A highway designed for high-speed traffic, typically with multiple lanes and controlled access.  Expressways are characterized by limited or no access points along their route, reducing congestion and enhancing safety. They often bypass urban areas, offering a faster route for long-distance travel. | Example:  We took the expressway to get to the airport faster. 我们走高速公路去机场更快。|近义词：freeway, motorway, highway | 反义词：local road, back road
