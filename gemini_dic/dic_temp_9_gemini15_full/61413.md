embody:/ɪmˈbɒdi/ | vt. | 体现；包含；使具体化 | To represent or be a perfect example of a quality, idea, or feeling.  |  Her work embodies the spirit of the 1960s.  她的作品体现了20世纪60年代的精神。|  represent, exemplify, personify |  abstract, formless | v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He embodies the qualities of a great leader. = 他体现了伟大领导者的品质。
The painting embodies the artist's vision. = 这幅画体现了艺术家的视野。


一般过去时:
The sculpture embodied the artist's grief. = 这座雕塑体现了艺术家的悲伤。
The novel embodied the social issues of the time. = 这部小说体现了当时的社会问题。


现在完成时:
Her performance has embodied grace and power. = 她的表演体现了优雅和力量。
This building has embodied innovation in architecture. = 这座建筑体现了建筑的创新。


过去完成时:
The ancient ritual had embodied the community's beliefs. = 远古的仪式体现了社区的信仰。
The earlier poems had embodied a sense of loss. = 早期的诗歌体现了一种失落感。


将来时:
The new law will embody the government's commitment to environmental protection. = 新法将体现政府对环境保护的承诺。
The next film will embody a unique storytelling style. = 下一部电影将体现一种独特的讲故事风格。


虚拟语气:
If the design embodied more creativity, it would be more successful. = 如果设计更具创意，它将会更成功。
I wish the project embodied our original goals. = 我希望这个项目能体现我们的最初目标。


被动语态:
The principles of democracy are embodied in the constitution. = 民主原则体现在宪法中。
The company's values are embodied in its mission statement. = 公司的价值观体现在其使命宣言中。


疑问句:
Does this statue embody the spirit of freedom? = 这座雕像是否体现了自由的精神？
Does the character truly embody the author's intentions? = 这个角色是否真正体现了作者的意图？


否定句:
The film failed to embody the complexity of the characters. = 这部电影未能体现人物的复杂性。
The design does not embody the latest technological advances. = 这个设计没有体现最新的技术进步。


PHRASE->
embody in = 体现在…中
embody the spirit of = 体现…的精神
embody the principle of = 体现…的原则
embody a concept = 体现一个概念
embody an idea = 体现一个想法

词源:
Old French *enbodier (from en- + *bodier, from Late Latin *corporare, to give a body to)
