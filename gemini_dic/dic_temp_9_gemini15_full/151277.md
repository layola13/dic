parfaits:/pɑːrˈfeɪ/| n. |冻糕，圣代|A parfait is a dessert typically made of layers of ice cream, fruit, and/or other sweet ingredients, often served in a tall glass.  It's known for its visually appealing layered presentation. |Example:  She ordered a strawberry parfait for dessert. 她点了一份草莓圣代作为甜点。

|近义词：sundae, dessert, ice cream,  |反义词：N/A|
