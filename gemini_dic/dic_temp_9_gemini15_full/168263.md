pro-Jewish: /ˌproʊˈdʒuːɪʃ/ | adj. | 亲犹太的，支持犹太人的 |  Supporting or favorable to Jewish people and their interests.  It indicates a positive attitude towards Judaism and Jewish people, often implying advocacy for their rights and well-being. | Example:  He's known for his pro-Jewish stance on political issues. 他以其在政治问题上的亲犹太立场而闻名。 | Near synonyms: pro-Israel (often used interchangeably, but with a focus on the state of Israel), philosemitic (more academic and nuanced) | Antonyms: antisemitic, anti-Jewish |  词性:形容词
