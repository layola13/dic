decompartmentalization: /ˌdiːkəmˈpɑːtmɛntaɪzeɪʃən/ | n. |去隔间化；打破部门界限；整合 | The process of breaking down or eliminating compartmentalization, typically referring to organizational structures, information, or thinking.  It involves integrating previously separate parts or systems to achieve greater efficiency, collaboration, and understanding. | Example: The company's decompartmentalization initiative improved communication between departments. 公司的去隔间化举措改善了部门之间的沟通。 |近义词：integration, unification, interconnectivity |反义词：compartmentalization, segregation, isolation
