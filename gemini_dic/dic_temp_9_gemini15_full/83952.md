half-conscious: /ˈhæf ˈkɒnʃəs/ | adj. | 半清醒的，迷迷糊糊的 |  Partially conscious; in a state between being awake and asleep, or between full consciousness and unconsciousness.  It suggests a dulled or impaired awareness. |


例句：

She woke with a half-conscious feeling that something was wrong. = 她半梦半醒地感觉到有什么不对劲。

He mumbled something half-consciously before falling back asleep. = 他迷迷糊糊地咕哝了几句，然后又睡着了。


近义词：semi-conscious, drowsy, lethargic, dazed,  unresponsive


反义词：fully conscious, alert, awake, aware


词性：形容词
