jellyfish:/ˈdʒɛliˌfɪʃ/ | n. |水母|A gelatinous, free-swimming marine animal with a bell-shaped body and tentacles that sting.  They are invertebrates belonging to the phylum Cnidaria. |例句：We saw many jellyfish while swimming in the ocean.  我们在海里游泳时看到了许多水母。

例句：The jellyfish washed ashore after the storm.  暴风雨后，水母被冲上了岸。


近义词：sea jelly


反义词：无


词性：名词
