chewing:/ˈtʃuːɪŋ/| v.|咀嚼，嚼碎|The act of grinding food with the teeth.|例句：He was chewing his food thoroughly. 他正在仔细咀嚼食物。

例句：The cow was chewing its cud.  那头牛正在反刍。


近义词：masticating, munching, gnawing|反义词：swallowing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chews his food slowly. = 他慢慢地咀嚼食物。
She chews gum all day. = 她整天嚼口香糖。


一般过去时:
I chewed my nails nervously. = 我紧张地咬指甲。
They chewed the cud after lunch. = 午餐后他们反刍。


现在完成时:
He has chewed through the rope. = 他已经咬断了绳子。
She has been chewing on that problem all day. = 她整天都在思考那个问题。


过去完成时:
The dog had chewed up my shoes before I got home. = 我回家前，狗已经把我的鞋子咬坏了。
They had chewed over the proposal for hours. = 他们已经讨论这个提案好几个小时了。


将来时:
I will chew on this idea for a while. = 我会考虑一下这个想法。
She will chew the food carefully. = 她会仔细地咀嚼食物。


虚拟语气:
If I chewed my food properly, I wouldn't have indigestion. = 如果我好好咀嚼食物，我就不会消化不良。
I wish I hadn't chewed that gum all day. = 我真希望我今天不要嚼口香糖嚼了一整天。


被动语态:
The food must be chewed thoroughly before swallowing. = 食物必须彻底咀嚼后再吞咽。
The bone was chewed clean by the dog. = 骨头被狗啃得干干净净。


疑问句:
Are you chewing gum? = 你在嚼口香糖吗？
What are you chewing on? = 你在嚼什么？


否定句:
He doesn't chew his food properly. = 他没有好好咀嚼食物。
She didn't chew the gum because it was stale. = 她没嚼口香糖因为口香糖不新鲜了。


PHRASE->
chew over: 仔细考虑
chew the fat: 闲聊
chew the cud: 反刍；沉思
chew someone out: 严厉斥责
chew on: 思考


词源:
源自古英语 *ceowan，与古高地德语 *kiuwan, 古诺尔斯语 *kjova 相关，意为“咀嚼”。
