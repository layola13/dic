panacea:/pænəˈsiːə/| n. |万能药；灵丹妙药；解决一切问题的办法 |A panacea is a solution or remedy for all difficulties or diseases.  It often implies something that is too good to be true and that promises a cure for all ills.  |Example: There is no panacea for the problems of modern society.  没有解决现代社会问题的万能药。

There is no single panacea for climate change.  应对气候变化没有单一的灵丹妙药。|近义词：cure-all, remedy, solution |反义词：ineffective treatment, useless solution |名词
