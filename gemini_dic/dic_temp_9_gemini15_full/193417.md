seismoscope:/ˈsaɪzməskoʊp/ | n. |地震仪；测震器 | An instrument for detecting and recording the occurrence of an earthquake.  It doesn't measure the magnitude or intensity, only the fact that an earthquake occurred. | Example:  The ancient Chinese seismoscope, invented by Zhang Heng, was a remarkable feat of engineering.  古代中国人张衡发明的地动仪是一项了不起的工程成就。| 近义词：seismometer (measures magnitude)| 反义词：无
