semi-fabricated: /ˈsɛmiˌfæbrɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 半制成的，半成品的 | Partially manufactured or processed; not completely finished.  It describes materials or products that have undergone some processing but are not yet in their final form. |  例句：The semi-fabricated parts arrived at the factory ready for assembly. 半制成的零件已运抵工厂，准备组装。

The company specializes in semi-fabricated metal components for the automotive industry. 这家公司专门为汽车工业生产半制成的金属部件。


近义词：partially finished, partly processed, unfinished, incomplete | 反义词：finished, complete, fully processed


词源：

* **semi-**:  From Latin "semi-", meaning "half".
* **fabricated**: From Latin "fabrica" meaning "workshop, structure, building",  and ultimately related to "facere" meaning "to make" or "to do".  The word "fabricate" evolved to mean "to construct" or "to manufacture".
