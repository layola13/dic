rectifying:/ˈrɛktɪfaɪɪŋ/| v. |矫正；纠正；整流|The act of making something right or better; correcting a fault or error;  in electronics, converting alternating current to direct current.|例句：The government is rectifying the economic imbalances. 政府正在纠正经济失衡。|例句：The engineer is rectifying the faulty wiring.工程师正在纠正有故障的线路。|近义词：correcting, amending, adjusting |反义词：worsening, exacerbating
