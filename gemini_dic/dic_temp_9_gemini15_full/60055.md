eductor:/ɪˈdʒʊktər/ | n. |吸出器；喷射器；提升器 |A device that uses a jet of fluid to draw another fluid into itself.  It's often used to remove materials from a container or system. |Example: The eductor efficiently removed the sludge from the tank.  吸出器有效地从罐中清除了污泥。|近义词：injector, ejector |反义词：injector (in some contexts, as an injector can introduce while an eductor removes)|名词
