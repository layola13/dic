maintaining:/meɪnˈteɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 维持；保持；保养；坚持 | To continue or preserve (something) in existing state. To keep something in good condition by regularly repairing, servicing, or renewing it. To assert or support a belief or claim. |例句：We need to maintain a positive attitude. 我们需要保持积极的态度。  She maintains a strict diet. 她坚持严格的饮食。 |近义词：preserve, conserve, uphold, sustain |反义词：neglect, abandon, destroy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He maintains a healthy lifestyle. 他保持健康的生活方式。
They maintain regular contact. 他们保持定期联系。

一般过去时:
The mechanic maintained my car. 修理工保养了我的车。
She maintained her innocence. 她坚持自己的清白。

现在完成时:
We have maintained a high standard of work. 我们一直保持着高标准的工作。
They have maintained their position.  他们一直坚持自己的立场。

过去完成时:
He had maintained his composure throughout the ordeal. 在整个磨难中，他一直保持着镇静。
She had maintained her silence for weeks. 她保持沉默好几个星期了。

将来时:
We will maintain the current policy. 我们将维持现行政策。
They will maintain order. 他们将维持秩序。

虚拟语气:
If only I could maintain my weight! 要是我能保持我的体重就好了！
If he maintained his focus, he would succeed. 如果他保持专注，他就会成功。

被动语态:
The building is maintained by a professional team. 这栋建筑由一支专业的团队负责维护。
Order was maintained by the police. 秩序由警察维持。

疑问句:
How do you maintain such a healthy lifestyle? 你是如何保持如此健康的生活方式的？
Can they maintain their lead in the race? 他们能保持比赛领先地位吗？

否定句:
He didn't maintain his promise. 他没有遵守诺言。
They failed to maintain the equipment properly. 他们未能妥善维护设备。
