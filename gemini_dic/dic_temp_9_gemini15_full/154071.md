pericardiac:/ˌpɛrɪˈkɑːrdiæk/| adj.| 心包的；心包周围的 | Relating to the pericardium, the membranous sac enclosing the heart.  | Example: The pericardiac fluid lubricates the heart's movements. 心包液润滑心脏的运动。 The doctor detected pericardiac inflammation.医生发现了心包炎症。| 近义词：pericardial | 反义词：None
