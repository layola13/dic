distractednesses:/dɪˈstræktɪdnɪsɪz/| n. |分心状态；注意力不集中状态|The plural form of distractedness, referring to multiple instances or types of being distracted.  It describes a state of mental disengagement or lack of focus, often resulting from various internal or external stimuli. |例句：Her distractednesses prevented her from completing the project on time.  她的注意力不集中导致她没能按时完成项目。  The professor noticed the students' various distractednesses during the lecture. 教授注意到学生们在讲座期间各种分心的情况。|近义词：inattentiveness, preoccupation, lack of focus|反义词：focus, concentration, attentiveness|名词
