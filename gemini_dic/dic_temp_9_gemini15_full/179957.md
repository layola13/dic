referred:/rɪˈfɜːrd/| v. |提及的；参考的；被推荐的|To mention or speak about (someone or something).  To direct or send someone or something to a person or place for help, information, or further action. |例句：The doctor referred me to a specialist.  医生把我转诊给了一位专家。

例句：The report referred to several important issues.  报告中提到了几个重要的问题。


近义词：mentioned, cited, recommended, directed

反义词：ignored, overlooked


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refers to the book often. = 他经常参考这本书。
She refers her patients to a physical therapist. = 她把她的病人转诊给物理治疗师。


一般过去时:
The teacher referred the student to the principal. = 老师把学生转介给了校长。
He referred to the incident in his speech. = 他在演讲中提到了这件事。


现在完成时:
They have referred me to several other doctors. = 他们已经把我转诊给了其他几位医生。
She has referred to that study in all of her publications. = 她在所有出版物中都引用了那项研究。


过去完成时:
He had already referred to the matter in his previous email. = 他在之前的邮件中已经提到了这件事。
The committee had referred the proposal to a subcommittee. = 委员会已将提案提交给小组委员会。


将来时:
I will refer you to our website for more information. = 我会引导您访问我们的网站以获取更多信息。
The judge will refer the case to a higher court. = 法官将把此案提交给上级法院。


虚拟语气:
If I needed help, I would refer to the manual. = 如果我需要帮助，我会参考手册。
I wish I had referred to the dictionary earlier. = 我真希望我早点查字典。


被动语态:
The patient was referred to a cardiologist. = 病人被转诊给心脏病专家。
The article was frequently referred to by other researchers. = 这篇文章经常被其他研究人员引用。


疑问句:
Did he refer to any specific examples? = 他提到任何具体的例子了吗？
To whom should I refer this matter? = 我应该把这件事转交给谁？


否定句:
He didn't refer to the previous agreement. = 他没有提及之前的协议。
The document doesn't refer to that specific issue. = 该文件没有提到那个具体问题。


PHRASE->
refer to = 参考；提及
refer back to = 回到；回顾
refer oneself to = 自我介绍
cross-refer = 交叉参考
refer a case = 转介案件

词源:
源自于拉丁语 *referre，由 re-（再次）和 ferre（携带）构成。  最初的意思是“带回”，后来演变为“提及”、“参考”等含义。
