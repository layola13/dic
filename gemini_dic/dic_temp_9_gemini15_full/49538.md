demarkations:/ˌdiːmɑːrˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. |界限，分界线，区分 |The act of setting boundaries or limits; the process of distinguishing or separating something.  It refers to the lines or marks that separate different areas or things. |例句：The map showed clear demarcations between the different countries. 这张地图清晰地显示了不同国家之间的界限。

例句：The demarcations between the two political ideologies were increasingly blurred. 两种政治意识形态之间的界限越来越模糊。


近义词：divisions, boundaries, separations, distinctions, limits


反义词：unifications, mergers, combinations, integrations
