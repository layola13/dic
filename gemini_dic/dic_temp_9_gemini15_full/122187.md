misunderstandable: /ˌmɪsʌndərˈstændəbəl/ | adj. | 易误解的，含糊不清的 |  Likely to be misunderstood; ambiguous.  The instructions were misunderstandable, leading to several mistakes.  | 近义词：ambiguous, unclear, confusing, obscure | 反义词：clear, unambiguous, lucid


例句：

The contract's wording is misunderstandable; we need to clarify it. = 合同措辞含糊不清，我们需要澄清一下。

His explanation was so misunderstandable that nobody understood what he meant. = 他的解释太令人费解了，没有人理解他的意思。


USAGE->
(由于misunderstandable是形容词，没有动词变化，故不适用时态变化。以下列举形容词的用法)

比较级：more misunderstandable
最高级：most misunderstandable

例句：

The second explanation was more misunderstandable than the first. = 第二个解释比第一个更令人费解。

This instruction manual is the most misunderstandable I have ever seen. = 这本说明书是我见过的最令人费解的。


PHRASE->
(misunderstandable本身就是一个完整的词，没有常用搭配短语)
