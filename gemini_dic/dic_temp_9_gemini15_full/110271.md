lifting: /ˈlɪftɪŋ/ | n. v. |举起；提升；电梯；情绪高涨 | The act of raising something to a higher position;  an upward movement; also refers to the act of improving something or someone's spirits.  It can also be a noun referring to an elevator or the sport of weightlifting. |

英文解析: Lifting can refer to the physical act of raising something heavy, such as lifting weights in a gym.  It can also describe an upward movement, like a helicopter lifting off the ground.  Figuratively, it can mean improving something, such as "lifting someone's spirits." As a noun, it can refer to an elevator ("take the lift to the top floor") or the act of weightlifting as a sport ("He's into competitive lifting").

例句:

He was lifting heavy weights at the gym. = 他在健身房举重。

The crane lifted the container onto the ship. = 起重机将集装箱吊到船上。


The news lifted her spirits. = 这个消息使她精神振奋。

The company is lifting its prices. = 公司正在提高价格。


The lift is out of order. = 电梯坏了。


He's a professional weight lifting champion. = 他是职业举重冠军。


近义词: raising, hoisting, elevating, boosting, improving

反义词: lowering, depressing, dropping, reducing


词性: noun, verb
