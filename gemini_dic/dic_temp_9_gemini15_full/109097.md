legendary:/ˈlɛdʒənˌdɛri/| adj.|传奇的，著名的，极好的|  of, or relating to, a legend; extremely famous; exceptionally good.  |例句：The band's legendary guitarist died last year.  乐队的传奇吉他手去年去世了。|例句：That restaurant is legendary for its delicious seafood. 那家餐厅以其美味的海鲜而闻名。|近义词：famous, mythical, renowned, celebrated|反义词：obscure, unknown, insignificant
