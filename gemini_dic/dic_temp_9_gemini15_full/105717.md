kinships:/ˈkɪnʃɪps/| n.|血缘关系；亲属关系；家族关系|The state of being related to someone; a system of social relationships based on family ties.  It can refer to blood relationships, marriage, or adoption, and often involves shared responsibilities, obligations, and emotional connections.|例句：The strong kinships within the family helped them overcome hardship.  强大的家族关系帮助他们克服了困难。|例句：He explored the complex kinships of the tribal society. 他探究了该部落社会复杂的血缘关系。|近义词：relationship, family ties, blood ties, kindred|反义词：estrangement, alienation|名词
