conversed:/kənˈvɜːrsd/| v.|交谈，谈话|To engage in conversation; to talk together.|They conversed for hours about their childhood memories.  他们聊了几个小时关于他们童年记忆的事情。|They spent the evening conversing about politics. 他们整个晚上都在谈论政治。|talked, chatted, discussed|quarreled, argued|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They converse easily in English. = 他们用英语轻松交谈。
We converse regularly about our work. = 我们定期讨论我们的工作。

一般过去时:
They conversed about the latest news. = 他们谈论了最新的新闻。
We conversed for hours at the party. = 我们在派对上聊了几个小时。

现在完成时:
They have conversed on the phone several times. = 他们已经通过电话交谈过几次了。
We have conversed with the manager about the project. = 我们已经和经理讨论过这个项目了。

过去完成时:
They had conversed before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他们已经谈过了。
We had conversed extensively before reaching a decision. = 我们在做出决定之前已经广泛地讨论过了。

将来时:
They will converse with the client tomorrow. = 他们明天将与客户交谈。
We will converse further on this topic next week. = 我们下周将进一步讨论这个话题。

虚拟语气:
If they conversed more openly, they would understand each other better. = 如果他们更坦诚地交谈，他们会更好地理解彼此。
I wish they had conversed before making the decision. = 我希望他们在做决定之前能谈谈。

被动语态:
The matter was conversed about extensively in the meeting. = 会议上对这件事进行了广泛的讨论。(注意：被动语态用词略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The topic was conversed upon by the experts. = 专家们讨论了这个话题。(注意：被动语态用词略显不自然，主动语态更常用)

疑问句:
Did they converse much at the dinner party? = 他们在晚宴上聊得很多吗？
Have they conversed about the problem yet? = 他们已经讨论过这个问题了吗？

否定句:
They did not converse much during the meeting. = 他们在会议期间交流不多。
We haven't conversed with him since last week. = 自从上周以来，我们没有和他谈过话。


PHRASE->
converse with someone = 与某人交谈
converse on a topic = 讨论一个话题
converse freely = 自由交谈
converse at length = 长时间交谈
engage in conversation = 开始谈话


词源:
Comes from the Old French word "converser," meaning "to live together, converse, associate."  This in turn comes from the Latin word "conversari," which had a similar meaning, relating to living with or associating with someone.
