scrap:/skræp/| n., v.|碎片；废料；争吵；丢弃；取消|a small piece of something that is left over; a fight or argument; to remove or cancel something|The dog found a scrap of meat.  这只狗找到了一小块肉。|They had a scrap over the last piece of cake. 他们因为最后一块蛋糕吵了一架。|近义词：fragment, remnant, quarrel, dispute|反义词：whole, complete, agreement


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He scraps the old car. 他报废了旧车。
She scraps the leftover food. 她把剩菜扔掉了。

一般过去时:
They scrapped their plans for the weekend. 他们取消了周末的计划。
He scrapped the metal for recycling. 他把金属废料送去回收。

现在完成时:
They have scrapped the old building. 他们已经拆除了旧建筑。
We have scrapped our original design. 我们已经放弃了最初的设计。

过去完成时:
They had scrapped the project before the funding ran out. 在资金耗尽之前，他们已经取消了项目。
She had scrapped her plans to go abroad. 她已经取消了出国的计划。

将来时:
They will scrap the outdated equipment. 他们将淘汰过时的设备。
We will scrap the idea if it doesn't work. 如果这个主意行不通，我们将放弃它。

虚拟语气:
If they scrapped the project, they would save money. 如果他们取消项目，他们就能省钱。
I wish they had scrapped that terrible idea earlier. 我真希望他们早点放弃那个糟糕的主意。

被动语态:
The old car was scrapped last week. 旧车是上周报废的。
The project was scrapped due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目被取消了。

疑问句:
Will they scrap the plan? 他们会取消计划吗？
Did they scrap the meeting? 他们取消会议了吗？

否定句:
They didn't scrap the car. 他们没有报废那辆车。
She won't scrap her plans. 她不会取消她的计划。
