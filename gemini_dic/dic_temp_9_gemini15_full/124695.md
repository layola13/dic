mow:/moʊ/| vt.|割草，修剪|To cut down (grass, grain, etc.) with a mowing machine or scythe.  |例句：He mows the lawn every Saturday. 他每个星期六都割草。|例句：The farmer mowed the wheat field. 农民收割了小麦田。|近义词：cut, trim, scythe|反义词：grow, cultivate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mows the lawn every week. = 他每周都割草。
They mow the fields in the summer. = 他们在夏天收割庄稼。


一般过去时:
I mowed the lawn yesterday. = 我昨天割了草。
She mowed the grass before the picnic. = 野餐前她割了草。


现在完成时:
He has mowed the lawn already. = 他已经割了草。
They have mowed all the wheat. = 他们已经收割了所有的麦子。


过去完成时:
I had mowed the lawn before the rain started. = 下雨前我已经割了草。
They had mowed the field before the sun set. = 太阳下山前他们已经收割了田地。


将来时:
I will mow the lawn tomorrow. = 我明天将要割草。
We will mow the wheat next week. = 我们下周将收割小麦。


虚拟语气:
If I had a lawnmower, I would mow the lawn myself. = 如果我有割草机，我会自己割草。
If he had time, he would mow the grass today. = 如果他有时间，他今天会割草。


被动语态:
The lawn is mowed every week. = 草坪每周都被修剪。
The fields were mowed by the farmer. = 田地被农民收割了。


疑问句:
Do you mow your own lawn? = 你自己割草吗？
Will they mow the field today? = 他们今天会收割田地吗？


否定句:
I didn't mow the lawn last week. = 我上周没有割草。
He doesn't mow the lawn often. = 他不经常割草。


PHRASE->
mow down:  击倒，摧毁 (e.g., The soldiers mowed down the enemy.)  士兵们消灭了敌人。
mow the lawn: 割草 (e.g., I need to mow the lawn this weekend.) 我这个周末需要割草。
to mow the grass: 修剪草坪 (e.g., He uses a riding mower to mow the grass.) 他用骑式割草机修剪草坪。
give it a mow: 给它修剪一下 (e.g.,  The garden needs a mow.) 花园需要修剪一下。
make a clean mow: 干净利落地割倒 (e.g.,  The scythe made a clean mow through the tall grass.) 镰刀干净利落地割倒了高高的草。
