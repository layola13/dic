pilgrimaging:/ˈpɪlɡrɪmɪdʒɪŋ/| v.|朝圣；旅行；艰难跋涉|The act of making a pilgrimage; a journey, especially one made to a sacred place as an act of religious devotion.|例句：Many people are making pilgrimages to Mecca this year.  今年许多人正在前往麦加朝圣。|例句：Their journey across the desert was a grueling pilgrimage. 他们穿越沙漠的旅程是一次艰苦的跋涉。|近义词：journey, travel, expedition|反义词：staying, remaining

They are pilgrimaging to the holy shrine. = 他们正在去圣地朝圣。
We pilgrimage to the mountain every year. = 我们每年都去那座山朝圣。

一般过去时:
She pilgrimaged to Santiago de Compostela. = 她去圣地亚哥德孔波斯特拉朝圣了。
He pilgrimaged across the country to see his family. = 他穿越全国去探望家人。

现在完成时:
They have pilgrimaged to Lourdes several times. = 他们已经去卢尔德朝圣过好几次了。
We have pilgrimaged to many sacred sites. = 我们已经去过许多圣地朝圣了。

过去完成时:
Before he died, he had pilgrimaged to all the important Buddhist temples in Tibet. = 在他死之前，他已经朝拜了西藏所有重要的佛教寺庙。
She had pilgrimaged to Jerusalem before settling in Israel. = 在定居以色列之前，她已经去耶路撒冷朝圣了。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I could pilgrimage to Varanasi next month. = 我希望下个月我能去瓦拉纳西朝圣。
