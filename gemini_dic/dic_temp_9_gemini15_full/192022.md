scorpioidal:/ˌskɔːpiˈɔɪdəl/| adj.|蝎形；蝎状的|Resembling a scorpion in form or arrangement, especially referring to the arrangement of inflorescences (flower clusters) in certain plants.  The term describes a type of cymose inflorescence where the main axis ends in a flower, and subsequent flowers arise from the lateral branches, creating a curved, often somewhat scorpion-like, arrangement.|例句：The plant displayed a distinctive scorpioidal inflorescence, curving gracefully to one side. 这植物展现出独特的蝎形花序，优雅地弯向一侧。

The scorpioidal cyme of this species is easily distinguishable from the racemose inflorescence of related plants.  该物种的蝎形聚伞花序很容易与相关植物的总状花序区分开来。|近义词：helicoid, spiral, circinate|反义词：straight, linear
