inc:/ɪŋk/|abbr.|包含；包括；公司；股份有限公司|inclusion, incorporation, company|The price includes tax. (价格包含税款。)|近义词：include, comprise|反义词：exclude


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The price includes tax. = 价格包含税款。
This project includes many challenges. = 这个项目包含许多挑战。


一般过去时:
The package included a free gift. = 包裹里包含一份免费礼物。
Her speech included many interesting anecdotes. = 她的演讲包含许多有趣的轶事。


现在完成时:
The final cost has included all expenses. =最终成本已包含所有费用。
This book has included many revisions. = 这本书已经包含了许多修订。


过去完成时:
The contract had included a non-compete clause. = 合同中包含一项竞业禁止条款。
The report had included all the necessary data. = 报告包含了所有必要的数据。


将来时:
The next update will include new features. = 下次更新将包含新功能。
The itinerary will include a visit to the museum. = 行程将包括参观博物馆。


虚拟语气:
If the contract included that clause, I wouldn't have signed it. = 如果合同包含那条条款，我不会签字的。
I wish the proposal included a more detailed budget. = 我希望提案包含更详细的预算。


被动语态:
Tax is included in the price. = 价格包含税款。
Many challenges are included in this project. = 这个项目包含许多挑战。


疑问句:
Does the price include shipping? = 价格包含运费吗？
What is included in the package? = 包裹里包含什么？


否定句:
The price does not include insurance. = 价格不包含保险。
The list did not include his name. = 名单上没有他的名字。


PHRASE->

inc. = incorporated (股份有限公司)
incidental = 附带的；偶然的
including = 包括
incident to = 附属于
incorporated = 合并的；注册的
