husbandman:/ˈhʌzbəndmən/| n. |农夫，耕作者|A husbandman is a farmer who cultivates land and raises crops or livestock.  The term often implies a more traditional or self-sufficient farming style than "farmer".|例句：The husbandman tilled the soil from sunrise to sunset.  这位农夫从日出到日落都在耕作土地。|例句：A skilled husbandman can coax a bountiful harvest even from poor land. 一位熟练的农夫即使在贫瘠的土地上也能获得丰收。|近义词：farmer, cultivator, tiller, agriculturalist|反义词：landowner (in the sense of someone who owns land but doesn't farm it)|词性:名词
