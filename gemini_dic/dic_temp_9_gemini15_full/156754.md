piercingly:/ˈpɪrsɪŋli/ | adv. | 刺耳地；尖锐地；深刻地 | In a way that is sharp, intense, or deeply felt.  It describes something that affects the senses or emotions intensely. |例句：The wind howled piercingly through the trees.  风呼啸着穿过树林，声音尖锐刺耳。

The singer's voice pierced the air piercingly. 歌手的歌声尖锐地刺破了空气。

近义词：sharply, intensely, acutely, keenly | 反义词：gently, softly, mildly
