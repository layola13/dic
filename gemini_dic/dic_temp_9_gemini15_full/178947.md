redeclarations:/ˌriːˌdekleraˈʃənz/| n. |重新声明；重复声明|The act of declaring something again, often to correct a previous declaration or to emphasize a point.  It often implies a previous declaration existed and is now being modified or superseded.|例句：The repeated redeclarations of his innocence failed to convince the jury.  他反复申明自己的清白未能说服陪审团。

例句：The programmer made several redeclarations in the code to fix a bug. 程序员在代码中进行了多次重新声明以修复错误。


近义词：restatements, reiterations, reaffirmations|反义词：retractions, denials|词性:noun
