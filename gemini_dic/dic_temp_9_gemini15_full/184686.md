resty:/ˈrɛsti/| adj.| 安静的；休息的；宁静的 |Resty describes something that is quiet, calm, and peaceful, often suggesting a state of rest or relaxation.  It implies a sense of stillness and tranquility. |The resty forest was a welcome escape from the city's noise.  安静的森林是逃离城市喧嚣的理想去处。|peaceful, tranquil, serene, calm |noisy, chaotic, turbulent, restless |形容词
