nonmobile:/ˈnɑːnˈmoʊbəl/ | adj. |非移动的，不可移动的 | Not able to move; stationary.  The definition emphasizes the inability to move independently or easily.  This contrasts with things that are mobile or portable. |例句：The nonmobile sculpture was a striking centerpiece in the gallery.  这尊不可移动的雕塑是画廊中引人注目的中心作品。


例句：The nonmobile equipment required a crane for transport. 这台不可移动的设备需要起重机运输。


近义词：stationary, immobile, fixed, unmovable | 反义词：mobile, portable, movable
