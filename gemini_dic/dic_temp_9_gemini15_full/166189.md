prepubescent: /ˌpriːˈpjuːbəsənt/ | adj. | 青春期前的；未到青春期的 |  Referring to the period of time before puberty, characterized by the absence of secondary sexual characteristics.  | Example:  The prepubescent children were still quite naive about the complexities of adult relationships. 青春期前的孩子们对成年人复杂的关系还很天真。  |近义词：preteen, preadolescent | 反义词：pubescent, adolescent |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is prepubescent. 他还没到青春期。
She is currently prepubescent. 她目前还没到青春期。


一般过去时:
He was prepubescent at that time. 那时候他还没到青春期。
She was prepubescent when she moved. 她搬家的时候还没到青春期。


现在完成时:
He has remained prepubescent. 他一直没到青春期。（这句比较少用，因为青春期会到来）
She has always been prepubescent until recently. 她直到最近才到青春期。


过去完成时:
He had been prepubescent for many years. 他好几年都没到青春期。
She had already been prepubescent before the incident. 事件发生前她就已经过了青春期。（逻辑上有点矛盾，因为过去完成时强调在过去某个时间点之前已经发生的事情，而prepubescent本身描述一个时间段）


将来时:
He will be prepubescent for a few more years. 他再过几年才会到青春期。
She will likely remain prepubescent for some time. 她很可能还会过一段时间才到青春期。


虚拟语气:
If he were prepubescent, he wouldn't understand. 如果他还没到青春期，他就不会理解。
I wish I were still prepubescent. 我真希望我还处于青春期前。


被动语态: (This adjective is rarely used passively.)  被动语态很少使用这个形容词


疑问句:
Is he prepubescent? 他还没到青春期吗？
Are the children prepubescent? 这些孩子们还没到青春期吗？


否定句:
He is not prepubescent. 他已经到了青春期。
She is no longer prepubescent. 她已经过了青春期。


PHRASE->
prepubescent children: 青春期前的儿童
prepubescent development: 青春期前的发育
prepubescent stage: 青春期前的阶段
prepubescent girl: 青春期前的女孩
prepubescent boy: 青春期前的男孩
