cut-it:/kʌt ɪt/| phrasal verb|  切断它；停止；结束它 |  This phrase is an informal imperative, meaning a command. "Cut it" means to stop doing something, often something annoying or inappropriate. It can also refer to literally cutting something. |例句：Cut it out!  别闹了！
Cut it with the jokes.  别再讲笑话了。|近义词：stop it, cease, quit, finish |反义词：continue, proceed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cuts it every day. = 他每天都切它。
They cut it carefully. = 他们仔细地切它。

一般过去时:
She cut it yesterday. = 她昨天切了它。
We cut it into pieces. = 我们把它切成碎片。

现在完成时:
I have cut it already. = 我已经切好了。
They have cut it several times. = 他们已经切了好几次了。

过去完成时:
He had cut it before she arrived. = 在她到达之前，他已经切好了。
We had cut it into small pieces before cooking. = 我们在烹饪之前已经把它切成小块了。


将来时:
I will cut it tomorrow. = 我明天会切它。
She will cut it with a sharp knife. = 她会用一把锋利的刀切它。

虚拟语气:
If I had a knife, I would cut it. = 如果我有一把刀，我会切它。
I wish I could cut it easily. = 我希望我能轻松地切它。

被动语态:
It was cut by her yesterday. = 它昨天被她切了。
It will be cut into slices. = 它会被切成薄片。

疑问句:
Did you cut it? = 你切了吗？
Will you cut it for me? = 你会帮我切吗？

否定句:
I didn't cut it. = 我没有切它。
She won't cut it. = 她不会切它。

PHRASE->
Cut it out! = 停止！别闹了！
Cut it short! = 简短些！
Cut it fine! = 精细地切！
Cut it up! = 把它切碎！
Cut it down! = 把它砍倒/缩减！
