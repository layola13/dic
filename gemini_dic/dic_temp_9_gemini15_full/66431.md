exegetics:/ˌɛksɪˈdʒiːtɪks/| n. |释经学；解经学|The critical explanation or interpretation of a text, especially of the Bible.  Exegetics involves careful study of the text's historical, literary, and cultural context to understand its original meaning.|例句：His lectures were renowned for their insightful exegetics of biblical passages. 他的讲座以对圣经章节的深刻解经而闻名。

例句：Modern exegetics often employs various critical methods to interpret religious texts. 现代解经学经常采用各种批判性方法来解释宗教文本。


近义词：hermeneutics, biblical interpretation, textual criticism |反义词：misinterpretation, distortion


词性：名词
