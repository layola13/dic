hin:/hɪn/| interj.|嘿；喂|A casual greeting.  It's a shorter, more informal version of "hello".|例句：Hin, what's up? 嘿，怎么了？|近义词：hi, hello, hey|反义词:无


USAGE->
This word is an interjection and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  It's used as a greeting.


PHRASE->
Hin there! = 嘿！
See you hin! = 再见！
Hin, buddy! = 嘿，朋友！
How's it hin? = 嘿，你好吗？
Hin, whatcha doin'? = 嘿，你在干嘛？
