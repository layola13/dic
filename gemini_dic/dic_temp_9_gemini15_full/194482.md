self-retired:/ˌsɛlf rɪˈtaɪrd/| adj.| 自己退休的；自行退休的 |  Describes someone who has retired from their job or career without being formally retired by an employer. This often implies a voluntary retirement, perhaps before the traditional retirement age or without the benefits associated with a formal retirement plan. | Example:  After years of dedication, she chose to become self-retired and pursue her passion for painting.  她多年奉献后，选择自己退休，追求她对绘画的热情。 | Near synonyms: independently retired, early retired | Antonyms: employed, working, still employed
