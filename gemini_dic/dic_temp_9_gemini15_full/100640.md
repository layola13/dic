intolerance:/ɪnˈtɒlərəns/ | n. |不容忍；不耐受；偏执 | a lack of tolerance; intolerance towards others' beliefs or opinions.  The inability to endure something. |例句：His intolerance of other cultures led to many conflicts. 他的不容忍其他文化导致了许多冲突。

例句：She suffers from lactose intolerance. 她患有乳糖不耐症。


近义词：bigotry, prejudice, bias, aversion | 反义词：tolerance, acceptance, open-mindedness | 词性：名词
