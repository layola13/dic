eschewed:/ɪsˈtʃuːd/ | vt. | 避免；避开；放弃 | To avoid or shun (something, typically something wrong or unpleasant).  |例句：He eschewed the use of violence. 他避免使用暴力。|例句：She eschewed luxury and lived a simple life. 她避开奢侈的生活，过着简朴的生活。|近义词：avoid, shun, shun, forgo, abstain from |反义词： embrace, adopt, welcome


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eschews all forms of social media. = 他避免使用任何形式的社交媒体。
She eschews rich foods. = 她避免食用油腻的食物。

一般过去时:
They eschewed the temptation to cheat. = 他们避免了作弊的诱惑。
He eschewed a lucrative job offer. = 他放弃了一份高薪的工作机会。

现在完成时:
She has eschewed meat for many years. = 她多年来一直不吃肉。
We have eschewed political involvement. = 我们一直避免卷入政治。

过去完成时:
Having eschewed violence in the past, he found it difficult to resort to it now. = 过去他一直避免暴力，现在发现很难诉诸暴力。
They had eschewed all contact with their former associates. = 他们之前一直避免与以前的同事联系。

将来时:
She will eschew any attempts at compromise. = 她将避免任何妥协的尝试。
He will eschew the use of harsh language. = 他将避免使用粗暴的语言。

虚拟语气:
If he had eschewed his bad habits, he would be healthier now. = 如果他过去避免不良习惯，他现在会更健康。
I wish I had eschewed that risky investment. = 我真希望我当时避免了那项高风险的投资。

被动语态:
Violence was eschewed by the protesters. = 抗议者避免了暴力。
Luxury was eschewed in favor of simplicity. = 奢侈被简朴所取代。

疑问句:
Does he eschew alcohol? = 他戒酒吗？
Why did they eschew the easy solution? = 为什么他们避免了简单的解决方案？

否定句:
He didn't eschew the opportunity to travel. = 他没有放弃旅行的机会。
They didn't eschew the use of force. = 他们没有避免使用武力。


PHRASE->

词源:
From Old French eschiver "to avoid, evade," from Frankish *skiuhan, from Proto-Germanic *skiuhan "to shun, avoid," from PIE *skeu- "to cover, protect."  The modern sense developed from the earlier meaning of "to avoid, evade."
