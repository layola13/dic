favorites:/ˈfeɪvərɪts/| n.| 最爱的事物，喜爱的人或物 |Things or people that are especially liked. |例句：My favorites are chocolate and ice cream. 我的最爱是巧克力和冰淇淋。

例句：Her favorite color is blue. 她最喜欢的颜色是蓝色。


近义词：favorites,darlings,beloveds| 反义词：dislikes, least favorites| 词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are your favorites? = 你最喜欢什么？
What are his favorite foods? = 他最喜欢的食物是什么？


一般过去时:
What were your favorites as a child? = 你小时候最喜欢什么？
What were her favorite books? = 她以前最喜欢的书是什么？


现在完成时:
What have been your favorite movies this year? = 今年你最喜欢的电影是什么？
What have been her favorite songs lately? = 她最近最喜欢的歌是什么？


过去完成时:
What had been his favorite hobbies before he retired? = 他退休前最喜欢的爱好是什么？
What had been their favorite restaurant before it closed? = 那家餐馆关门前，他们最喜欢去哪家？


将来时:
What will be your favorites next year? = 明年你最喜欢什么？
What will be her favorite subject in college? = 她大学最喜欢的科目将会是什么？


虚拟语气:
If I had known what her favorites were, I would have bought her a gift. = 如果我知道她喜欢什么，我就给她买礼物了。
I wish I knew what his favorites were. = 我真希望知道他喜欢什么。


被动语态:
His favorites were announced yesterday. = 昨天公布了他的最爱。
Her favorites are always being asked about. = 人们总是问起她最喜欢的东西。


疑问句:
What are your favorites? = 你最喜欢什么？
What are their favorites this season? = 他们这个季节最喜欢什么？


否定句:
Those aren't my favorites. = 那些不是我最喜欢的。
These aren't her favorites colors. = 这些不是她最喜欢的颜色。


PHRASE->
favorite color = 最喜欢的颜色
