cotillion:/kəˈtɪljən/| n. |社交舞会；(尤指)隆重的舞会|A formal ball, especially one with a set of dances.  |例句：The debutantes attended a grand cotillion.  这些初次参加社交活动的年轻女子参加了一个盛大的社交舞会。|例句：The cotillion was a glittering affair, with elegant gowns and sparkling conversation. 这场社交舞会是一场光彩夺目的盛会，穿着优雅的礼服，谈话闪闪发光。|近义词：ball, dance, formal, reception|反义词：informal gathering, casual party|名词
