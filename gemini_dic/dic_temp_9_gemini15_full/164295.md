/priːd/| v. | 夸耀；炫耀；自夸 | To boast or brag about something.  |He preed about his new car. 他炫耀他的新车。| boast, brag, show off | humble, modest | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preens himself constantly. = 他不断地自我吹嘘。
She preens about her achievements. = 她炫耀自己的成就。


一般过去时:
He preened himself after winning the race. = 他在赢得比赛后自我吹嘘。
She preened about her new dress. = 她炫耀她的新裙子。


现在完成时:
He has preened himself all day. = 他整天都在自我吹嘘。
She has preened about her success. = 她一直在炫耀她的成功。


过去完成时:
He had preened himself before the interview. = 他在面试前就已自我吹嘘。
She had preened about her trip to Europe. = 她已经炫耀过她去欧洲的旅行。


将来时:
He will preen himself if he wins the award. = 如果他获奖，他会自我吹嘘。
She will preen about her promotion. = 她会炫耀她的晋升。


虚拟语气:
If he won, he would preen himself endlessly. = 如果他赢了，他会没完没了地自我吹嘘。
I wish she wouldn't preen so much about her looks. = 我希望她不要那么炫耀自己的外表。


被动语态:
His accomplishments were preened about by the media. = 他的成就被媒体大肆宣扬。
Her success was preened by her colleagues. = 她的成功被她的同事们夸耀。


疑问句:
Does he often preen about his wealth? = 他经常炫耀他的财富吗？
Does she preen herself before going out? = 她出门前会自我吹嘘吗？


否定句:
He didn't preen himself after the defeat. = 他在失败后没有自我吹嘘。
She didn't preen about her failures. = 她没有炫耀她的失败。


PHRASE->
preen oneself = 自我吹嘘
preen one's feathers =  整理羽毛；打扮自己 (比喻意义上指自我陶醉)
preen and pose =  摆姿势炫耀
preen one's appearance = 打扮仪容
preen for the camera =  在镜头前摆POSE
