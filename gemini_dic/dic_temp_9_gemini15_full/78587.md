giantism:/ˈdʒaɪəntɪzəm/| n. |巨人症，巨大症|A condition characterized by excessive growth of the body or its parts, typically caused by an overproduction of growth hormone.|例句：Acromegaly is a form of giantism that affects adults.  肢端肥大症是一种影响成年人的巨人症。|例句：The child's giantism was attributed to a pituitary tumor.  孩子的巨人症归因于垂体肿瘤。|近义词：gigantism, gigantism|反义词：dwarfism
