overfunding:/ˌoʊvərˈfʌndɪŋ/ | n. | 资金过剩，过度融资 | The provision of more funding than is needed or requested.  This can lead to inefficient use of resources or even wasteful spending. | Example: The project suffered from overfunding, leading to a bloated budget and unnecessary expenses.  The company received overfunding from investors, resulting in a surplus of cash. | 近义词：excess funding, surplus funding | 反义词：underfunding
