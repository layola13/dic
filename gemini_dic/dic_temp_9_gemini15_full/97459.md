infusorian:/ɪnˈfjuːzəriən/| n. |纤毛虫，草履虫|A single-celled microscopic organism belonging to the phylum Ciliophora, characterized by the presence of cilia used for locomotion and feeding.  Infusorians are found in various aquatic environments. |Example: Paramecium is a well-known example of an infusorian.  草履虫是纤毛虫的一个著名例子。|近义词：ciliate, protozoan|反义词：None.
