outshowed:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊd/| v.|胜过，超过|To surpass or exceed in excellence, achievement, or performance.|例句：His latest novel outshowed all his previous works. 他的最新小说胜过他以前的所有作品。|近义词：surpassed, excelled, outdid |反义词：underperformed, failed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outshows his rivals in the competition. = 他在比赛中胜过他的对手。
The new design outshows the old one in terms of efficiency. = 新设计在效率方面胜过旧设计。


一般过去时:
Our team outshowed the opponent's team in the final match. = 我们队在决赛中胜过了对方队。
Her performance outshowed everyone else's at the concert. = 她在音乐会上的表演胜过了其他所有人。


现在完成时:
He has outshowed all expectations in his career. = 他在事业上已经超出了所有人的预期。
The company has outshowed its competitors in the market share. = 这家公司在市场份额方面已经超过了竞争对手。


过去完成时:
By the end of the project, she had outshowed all her previous achievements. = 到项目结束时，她已经超越了她之前的所有成就。
They had outshowed their rivals in terms of innovation before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们在创新方面已经胜过了竞争对手。


将来时:
We expect that the new technology will outshow the current one in the future. = 我们预计新技术将来会胜过目前的。
His next book is expected to outshow his previous works in terms of sales. = 预计他的下一本书在销量方面会胜过他之前的作品。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he would have outshowed his opponent. = 如果他训练更刻苦，他就会胜过对手。
If only she had prepared better, she would have outshowed her anxiety. = 如果她准备得更好，她就不会那么焦虑。


被动语态:
His talent was outshowed by none in the group. = 在小组中，没有人比他的天赋更出色。
The importance of sustainability is being outshowed by short-term gains. = 可持续性的重要性正被短期利益所掩盖。（out showed 在此略有不自然，需考虑其他更佳表达）


疑问句:
Did his work outshow the others? = 他的作品是否胜过其他作品？
Does this product outshow its competitors? = 这个产品是否胜过它的竞争对手？


否定句:
His performance did not outshow the expectations. = 他的表现没有超出预期。
The plan did not outshow the original idea. = 这个计划没有比原来的想法更好。
