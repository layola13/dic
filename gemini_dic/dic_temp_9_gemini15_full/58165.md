droven: /ˈdroʊvən/ | adj. |  驱赶的；受驱使的；被强迫的 | driven is the past participle of the verb "to drive". It can be used as an adjective to describe something that is motivated or compelled by a strong force. It can also describe something that is forcefully propelled or moved. | 例句：He was a driven man, always striving for success. 他是一个有强烈进取心的人，总是努力追求成功。


The storm drove the boat towards the rocks. 风暴把船向礁石推去。 | 近义词：motivated, compelled, impelled, forced | 反义词：unmotivated, passive, lethargic |  形容词


USAGE->
