retroactivities:/ˌrɛtroæktɪvɪtiz/| n.|追溯效力；反向效力；溯及既往|The application of laws or rules to events that occurred before the laws or rules were enacted.|例句：The new regulations have retroactivities, affecting contracts signed last year. 新规具有追溯力，影响去年签订的合同。|近义词：backdating,  ex post facto application|反义词：prospectivity
