morpho:/ˈmɔːrfəʊ/| n. |形态；形态学；变体|The study of the form and structure of organisms.  It encompasses various aspects of biological form, including the shape, size, color, and other physical attributes of organisms.  In linguistics, it refers to the forms of words. |例句：The morpho of the butterfly is striking.  蝴蝶的形态很醒目。|例句：Morphological analysis is crucial in linguistics. 形态学分析在语言学中至关重要。|近义词：form, shape, structure, morphology |反义词：无
