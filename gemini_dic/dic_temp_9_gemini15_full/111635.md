localizers:/ˈloʊkəlaɪzərz/ | n. |本地化人员，本地化专家 | People or companies that adapt products or content to a specific local market.  They translate text, adjust images and cultural references, and ensure the product resonates with the target audience. | Example: The game developers hired localizers to translate the game into Spanish and Portuguese.  游戏开发者雇佣本地化人员将游戏翻译成西班牙语和葡萄牙语。|近义词：translators, adaptors |反义词：globalizers
