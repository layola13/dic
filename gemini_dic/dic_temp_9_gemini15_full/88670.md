hoarstone:/ˈhɔːrˌstoʊn/| n. |霜冻石，白霜覆盖的石头|A hoarstone is a stone covered with hoar frost.  The term is primarily used in a poetic or descriptive context, referring to the beautiful, ephemeral effect of frost on a stone.  It evokes a sense of cold, stillness, and winter. |例句：The hoarstone glistened in the pale morning sun.  晨曦中，霜冻石闪闪发光。 The ancient hoarstone stood silent witness to the passing centuries. 古老的霜冻石静静地见证着岁月的流逝。|近义词：frosty stone, frost-covered rock |反义词：sun-baked stone
