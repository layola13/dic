iridescently:/ˌɪrɪˈdɛsntli/ | adv. | 彩虹般地，闪耀地 | Showing luminous colours that seem to change when seen from different angles.  | The soap bubbles shimmered iridescently in the sunlight.肥皂泡在阳光下闪耀着彩虹般的光泽。| shimmeringly, glitteringly | dullly, flatly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and as such it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It describes actions or states of being. There are no tense variations for "iridescently".


例句1：The oil slick spread across the water, iridescently reflecting the sunset.  油污在水面上蔓延，彩虹般地反射着夕阳。

例句2：The hummingbird's feathers shimmered iridescently as it hovered near the flower.  蜂鸟的羽毛在它盘旋在花朵附近时闪耀着彩虹般的光泽。
