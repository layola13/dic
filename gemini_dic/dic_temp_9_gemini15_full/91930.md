hypermedication:/ˌhaɪpərˌmɛdɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |过度用药|The administration of medication in excessive amounts or at excessive frequency.  This can lead to serious side effects and health complications.  It's often associated with polypharmacy (the use of multiple drugs simultaneously).|例句：The patient experienced hypermedication leading to liver damage.  病人因过度用药导致肝损伤。

例句：His hypermedication resulted in unexpected side effects, requiring immediate medical attention. 他的过度用药导致了意想不到的副作用，需要立即就医。


近义词：overmedication, polypharmacy, drug overdose (in severe cases) |反义词：undermedication, appropriate medication
