It describes something that has drooped again after perhaps having been revived or straightened.  There is no established dictionary entry or common usage.|例句：The flowers, though watered, redrooped in the intense afternoon heat.  花朵尽管浇了水，但在强烈的午后阳光下再次下垂了。  |近义词：sagged again, wilted again, slumped again|反义词：perked up, revived, straightened|形容词
