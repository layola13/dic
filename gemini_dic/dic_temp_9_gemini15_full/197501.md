sheiks:/ʃeɪks/| n. |酋长，族长；富有的阿拉伯人|A sheik is the male head of an Arab tribe or family, often possessing significant wealth and influence.  The term is sometimes used loosely to refer to wealthy Arab men.|例句：The sheik owned vast tracts of land and many camels.  这位酋长拥有大片土地和许多骆驼。

例句：He met a sheik who was known for his generosity. 他遇到一位以慷慨著称的酋长。


近义词：chieftain, emir, potentate|反义词：subject, commoner|词性:名词
