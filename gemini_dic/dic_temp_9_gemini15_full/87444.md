hesitation:/ˌhɛzɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. |犹豫，迟疑，踌躇 |The act or an instance of hesitating; a pause or delay before acting or speaking.  It often indicates uncertainty, doubt, or reluctance. |例句：After some hesitation, she agreed to go.  犹豫了一会儿，她同意去了。|例句：His hesitation betrayed his nervousness. 他的犹豫暴露了他的紧张。|近义词：delay, pause, reluctance, wavering, vacillation |反义词：certainty, decisiveness, confidence
