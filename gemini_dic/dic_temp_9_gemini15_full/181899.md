remaining:/rɪˈmeɪnɪŋ/| adj.|剩余的，剩下的|The remaining food was thrown away. 剩下的食物被扔掉了。|例句：There are only a few remaining tickets.  只剩下几张票了。

The remaining chapters are the most difficult.  剩下的章节是最难的。|近义词：left-over, residual, surplus|反义词：departed, gone, used

词源:
The word "remaining" comes from the present participle of the verb "remain," which ultimately derives from the Old French word "remaner" and further back to the Latin word "manere," meaning "to stay" or "to remain."  The prefix "re-" intensifies the meaning.
