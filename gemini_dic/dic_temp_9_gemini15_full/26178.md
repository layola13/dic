butchery:/ˈbʊtʃəri/| n. |屠宰；屠夫店；残杀；拙劣的技艺 |The act of slaughtering animals for meat; a shop where meat is sold; brutal killing; clumsy or unskilled work. |例句：The butchery of the animals was swift and efficient.  = 动物的屠宰又快又有效率。

例句：The butcher shop offered a wide selection of meats. = 这家肉铺提供各种各样的肉类。


近义词：slaughter, carnage, massacre, butchering | 反义词：preservation, protection, skillful work |  词性: Noun
