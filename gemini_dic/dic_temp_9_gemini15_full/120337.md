minimalistic:/ˌmɪnɪˈmælɪstɪk/| adj.|极简主义的；简约的| characterized by a minimalist style; simple and uncluttered.  It emphasizes simplicity, functionality, and the reduction of unnecessary elements. |例句：Her apartment has a minimalistic design, with only essential furniture and décor.|近义词：simple, understated, spare, unadorned|反义词：ornate, elaborate, maximalist
