paraders:/pəˈreɪdərz/| n.|游行者，参加游行的人|People who are taking part in a parade.  They are often marching or walking in a procession to celebrate something or to make a statement.|例句：The paraders marched down Main Street, waving flags and banners. 游行者沿着主街行进，挥舞着旗帜和横幅。|例句：Thousands of paraders participated in the annual Mardi Gras parade.数千名游行者参加了年度狂欢节游行。|近义词：marchers, demonstrators, protesters|反义词：spectators, onlookers
