damn-well:/ˈdæm wel/| adv.|的确，确实，当然|Emphasizes the truth or certainty of a statement.  It is an informal and often emphatic way of saying "absolutely" or "certainly."  The word "damn" is a mild expletive.|例句：I damn-well told you so!  我早就告诉过你了！（语气强烈）

例句：She damn-well deserves the promotion. 她确实应该得到晋升。


近义词：certainly, absolutely, definitely, surely, indeed


反义词：not at all, never, by no means


词性: 副词
