secularise:/ˈsɛkjʊləraɪz/|/ˈsɛkjʊləraɪz/| vt.|世俗化；将…脱离宗教控制|To remove (something) from religious or ecclesiastical influence or control; to make worldly or nonreligious.  The process of secularization involves separating something from religious or spiritual influence or control, making it worldly or nonreligious.|例句：The government secularised education by removing religious instruction from schools. 政府通过取消学校的宗教教育来世俗化教育。

例句：The movement aimed to secularise the law, removing religious influence from legal decisions.  这个运动旨在使法律世俗化，消除宗教对法律判决的影响。


近义词：desacralize, profane, de-church


反义词：sacralize, sanctify


词性：动词
