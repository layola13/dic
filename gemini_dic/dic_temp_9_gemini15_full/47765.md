decamping:/dɪˈkæmpɪŋ/| v.| 突然离开；撤营；逃走 | To leave a place suddenly, especially a campsite; to abandon a position or activity. |例句：The soldiers decamped in the middle of the night. 士兵们在半夜撤离了。

例句：After the picnic, we decamped back to the car.野餐后，我们迅速回到车里。


近义词： depart, leave, abscond, flee, evacuate

反义词： arrive, settle, encamp

词源:
The word "decamp" comes from the French "décamper," which itself is a combination of the prefix "dé-" (meaning "away from" or "off") and "camper" (meaning "to camp").  Therefore, its original meaning directly relates to breaking camp and moving away.
