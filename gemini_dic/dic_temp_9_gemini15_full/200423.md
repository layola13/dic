slackener: /ˈslækənər/ | n. | 松弛剂，缓和剂 | A substance or agent that causes something to slacken or become less tight.  It can refer to a chemical used to loosen or relax something, or a device that reduces tension. | Example: The gardener used a chemical slackener to loosen the tightly packed soil. 园丁使用化学松弛剂来松开紧实的土壤。|近义词：relaxant, emollient | 反义词：tightener, constrictor
