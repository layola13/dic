pregalvanized: /ˌpriːˈɡæl vənaɪzd/ | adj. | 预镀锌的 |  Pre-galvanized refers to steel or iron that has been galvanized (coated with zinc) before being further processed or used in manufacturing. This process protects the metal from corrosion. | Example: The pregalvanized steel sheets were used to construct the building's frame. 预镀锌钢板被用来建造建筑物的框架。 | 近义词: zinc-coated | 反义词: ungalvanized
