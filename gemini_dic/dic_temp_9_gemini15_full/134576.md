nonlogically:/ˌnɒnˈlɒdʒɪkli/| adv.|非逻辑地，不合逻辑地|In a manner that is not logical or rational.  It implies a lack of coherence or consistency in reasoning or argument.|例句：He argued nonlogically, jumping from one unrelated point to another. 他论证得不合逻辑，从一个不相干的论点跳到另一个论点。


例句：She reached a conclusion nonlogically, ignoring crucial evidence. 她得出的结论不合逻辑，忽略了关键证据。


近义词：illogically, irrationally, inconsistently, unreasonably


反义词：logically, rationally, consistently


词性：副词
