forecaster:/ˈfɔːrˌkæstər/ | n. |预报员，预测者 |A person who predicts future events, especially weather.  |例句：The weather forecaster predicted rain for tomorrow.  天气预报员预测明天会下雨。|例句：The economic forecaster believes the market will rebound next year. 经济预测者认为市场明年将反弹。|近义词：predictor, prognosticator |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The forecaster predicts sunny weather. = 预报员预测天气晴朗。
She forecasts the stock market's trends. = 她预测股市的趋势。

一般过去时:
The forecaster predicted a storm. = 预报员预测了一场风暴。
He accurately forecast the election results. = 他准确地预测了选举结果。

现在完成时:
The forecaster has already given the report. = 预报员已经发布了报告。
She has consistently forecast market volatility. = 她一直预测市场波动。

过去完成时:
They had forecast a heatwave before the summer. = 他们在夏天之前预测了热浪。
The analyst had forecast lower profits. = 分析师曾预测利润下降。

将来时:
The forecaster will announce the forecast later. = 预报员稍后将公布预报。
He will forecast the weather for the next week. = 他将预测未来一周的天气。

虚拟语气:
If the forecaster were right, we would have prepared. = 如果预报员是对的，我们就会做好准备。
I wish the forecaster had been more accurate. = 我希望预报员更准确一些。

被动语态:
The weather was forecast to be stormy. = 天气预报说会有暴风雨。
The economic forecast was revised downwards. = 经济预测被下调了。

疑问句:
What does the forecaster say about the weekend? = 预报员对周末有什么说法？
What has the forecaster predicted for the next month? = 预报员对下个月预测了什么？

否定句:
The forecaster did not predict the heavy snowfall. = 预报员没有预测到这场大雪。
She doesn't forecast the market perfectly. = 她不能完美地预测市场。
