eye:/aɪ/| n. |眼睛|The eye is the organ of sight.  It allows us to see the world around us.|例句：The baby opened its eyes and looked at me. 婴儿睁开眼睛看着我。|例句：He has beautiful blue eyes. 他有一双漂亮湛蓝的眼睛。|近义词：optic, eyeball|反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I see a bird. = 我看见一只鸟。
She looks at the painting. = 她看着这幅画。


一般过去时:
I saw a movie last night. = 我昨晚看了一部电影。
They looked for their keys. = 他们找他们的钥匙。


现在完成时:
I have seen that movie before. = 我以前看过那部电影。
She has looked at many paintings. = 她看过很多画。


过去完成时:
I had seen him before. = 我以前见过他。
They had looked everywhere for it. = 他们到处都找过了。


将来时:
I will see you tomorrow. = 我明天见你。
They will look into the matter. = 他们会调查此事。


虚拟语气:
If I had eyes, I would see better. = 如果我有眼睛，我会看得更好。
I wish I could see the future. = 我希望我能看到未来。


被动语态:
The painting was looked at by many people. = 这幅画被很多人观赏。
The problem is being looked into. = 这个问题正在调查中。


疑问句:
Do you see what I see? = 你看见我看见的东西了吗？
Can you see the mountains? = 你能看见山吗？


否定句:
I don't see anything. = 我什么也看不见。
She didn't see the accident. = 她没看到事故。


PHRASE->
keep an eye on = 注意；留心
catch someone's eye = 吸引某人的注意
in the eye of = 在…看来；从…的观点来看
have an eye for = 对…有鉴赏力
with one's own eyes = 亲眼所见
