kingbird: /ˈkɪŋbɜːrd/ | n. |  捕蝇鸟 | A bird of the family Tyrannidae, characterized by its large size and often aggressive behavior towards other birds. Many kingbirds are insectivores, known for their aerial acrobatics in catching insects.  |  The kingbird perched on the branch, surveying its territory.  这只捕蝇鸟栖息在树枝上，观察着它的领地。|  tyrant flycatcher |  (no direct antonym) | 名词
