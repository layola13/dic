requisitionary:/ˌrɛkwɪˈzɪʃənəri/ | adj. | 征用的；征募的；供给的 | Relating to or of the nature of a requisition;  demanding or requiring something as a right or necessity.  It often implies an official or authoritative demand. | The requisitionary process for office supplies was slow and cumbersome.  征用办公用品的流程缓慢而繁琐。  |  mandatory, compulsory, obligatory | voluntary, optional |
