rotunda:/roʊˈtʌndə/| n. |圆形大厅；圆形建筑|A rotunda is a round building or room, usually with a dome.  It is often characterized by its circular shape and impressive architecture.  |The museum's grand rotunda housed a stunning collection of ancient artifacts.|近义词：dome, rotunda, cupola|反义词：rectangle, square
