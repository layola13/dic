grown:/ɡroʊn/| adj. v. |成长的；成熟的；长大的；种植的；已长成的| Grown describes something that has developed or matured, either naturally or through cultivation.  It can be used as an adjective or a past participle of the verb "grow".|例句：The grown man refused to admit his mistake.  这个成年男子拒绝承认自己的错误。


The grown plants need more water.  这些长大的植物需要更多水。|近义词：adult, mature, developed, cultivated |反义词：young, immature, undeveloped, uncultivated|词性：adj./v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grows tomatoes in his garden. = 他在花园里种西红柿。
The plant grows quickly. = 植物长得很快。


一般过去时:
The tree grew taller last year. = 这棵树去年长得更高了。
She grew up in a small town. = 她在一个小镇长大。


现在完成时:
The children have grown so much since last year. = 孩子们自从去年以来长大了不少。
The company has grown significantly in the past decade. = 这家公司在过去十年中发展壮大。


过去完成时:
The plants had grown considerably before the frost. = 霜冻来临之前，植物已经长得相当大了。
He had grown accustomed to the noise. = 他已经习惯了噪音。


将来时:
The sapling will grow into a tall tree. = 这棵树苗将会长成一棵高大的树。
She will grow to be a strong woman. = 她将会成长为一个坚强的女人。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would grow my own vegetables. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会自己种植蔬菜。
I wish I had grown taller. = 我希望我长得更高。


被动语态:
The plants were grown in a greenhouse. = 这些植物是在温室里种植的。
The vegetables are grown organically. = 这些蔬菜是有机种植的。


疑问句:
Has the plant grown much? = 植物长大了很多吗？
Did the company grow last year? = 公司去年发展壮大了吗？


否定句:
The plant didn't grow well this year. = 今年植物长得不好。
He hasn't grown much since he was a child. = 他自从孩提时代以来就没怎么长高。
