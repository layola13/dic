myositis:/ˌmaɪəˈsaɪtɪs/| n. |肌炎|Inflammation of muscle tissue.  Myositis can affect a single muscle or multiple muscle groups, causing pain, swelling, and weakness.  The cause can vary, ranging from viral infections to autoimmune disorders.|例句：He was diagnosed with polymyositis, a chronic inflammatory disease affecting multiple muscles. 他被诊断出患有多发性肌炎，这是一种影响多块肌肉的慢性炎症性疾病。|近义词：myopathy, muscle inflammation|反义词：muscle health, muscle normalcy
