photoheliographic:/ˌfoʊtoʊˌhiːliəˈɡræfɪk/| adj.| 日像摄影的 | Relating to the photography of the sun.  This term combines "photo" (light/photography), "helio" (sun), and "graphic" (writing/recording). It describes techniques and images created by photographing the sun, often to study solar features like sunspots. | Example: The photoheliographic observations revealed a significant increase in sunspot activity. 日像摄影观测显示太阳黑子活动显著增加。|近义词：solar photographic |反义词：无
