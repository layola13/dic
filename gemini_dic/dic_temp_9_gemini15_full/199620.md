sinnable:/ˈsɪnəbl/| adj.| 可犯罪的；有罪的 | Capable of sinning; subject to sin.  It implies a susceptibility or predisposition to committing sins, rather than the act of sinning itself. | Example:  Human beings are sinnable creatures. = 人类是容易犯罪的生物。  The sinnable nature of humanity has been a subject of theological debate for centuries. = 人类的罪性已成为几个世纪以来神学辩论的主题。 | 近义词：fallible, errant, peccable | 反义词：sinless, impeccable, faultless
