pealed:/piːld/| v.|剥落，脱落；响亮地发出声音|To peel off or away; to ring loudly.|The paint peeled from the old house. 老房子的油漆剥落了。The bells pealed joyfully. 钟声响亮而欢快地鸣响。|shed, flake, ring, sound|stick, adhere|


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The paint peels from the wall. 油漆从墙上剥落。
The church bells peel every Sunday. 教堂的钟声每个星期天都会响起。


一般过去时:
The bark peeled easily from the tree. 树皮很容易从树上剥落。
The choir pealed out a joyous hymn. 合唱团响亮地唱出一首欢快的赞美诗。


现在完成时:
The sun has peeled the paint on the car. 太阳晒掉了汽车上的油漆。
The bells have pealed throughout the city. 钟声响彻全城。


过去完成时:
The paint had peeled before the renovation. 装修前油漆就已经剥落了。
The bells had pealed before the ceremony began. 典礼开始前钟声就已经响过了。


将来时:
The paint will peel if it's not protected. 如果不保护的话，油漆会剥落。
The bells will peel to announce the wedding. 钟声将敲响以宣布婚礼。


虚拟语气:
If the paint peeled, we would have to repaint the house. 如果油漆剥落了，我们就得重新粉刷房子。
If the bells pealed louder, more people would hear it. 如果钟声响得更响亮，更多的人会听到。


被动语态:

The news was pealed out on the radio.  这个消息通过广播大声宣布了。（此例句中peel指“大声宣布”，与pealed含义略有不同，但语法结构适用）


疑问句:

否定句:
The paint didn't peel easily. 油漆不容易剥落。
