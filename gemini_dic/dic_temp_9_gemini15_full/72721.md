foddering:/ˈfɒdərɪŋ/| n.|饲料；养料；（尤指牲畜的）饲养|The act of providing fodder; the state of being fed fodder.  It can also refer to material used for encouraging or stimulating something, often in a negative context, such as fueling conflict or gossip.|例句：The farmer spent the morning preparing fodder for his cattle. = 农民花了整个上午准备牲畜的饲料。
The constant negative news was fodder for the tabloids. =  不断涌现的负面新闻成为小报的素材。|近义词：feed, forage, provender|反义词：starvation, famine
