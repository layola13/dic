mig:/maɪɡ/| n.| 迁移；移动；候鸟迁徙|The mass migration of people from rural areas to cities is a major social change.  大规模的人口从农村地区迁移到城市是一项重大的社会变革。|近义词：movement, relocation, emigration |反义词：immigration, settlement


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Birds migrate south for the winter. = 鸟类在冬天向南迁徙。
Many people migrate to find better job opportunities. = 许多人为了寻找更好的工作机会而迁移。

一般过去时:
The family migrated to Canada in 1990. = 这家人在1990年移居加拿大。
Our ancestors migrated across the continents. = 我们的祖先迁徙过各大洲。

现在完成时:
Millions of people have migrated from the countryside to the city. = 数百万的人口已经从农村迁移到城市。
They have migrated to several different countries. = 他们已经迁移到几个不同的国家。

过去完成时:
The birds had already migrated before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，鸟类已经迁徙了。
The population had migrated to higher ground before the flood. = 在洪水到来之前，人们已经迁移到高地。

将来时:
We will migrate to a warmer climate next year. = 我们明年将迁移到气候更温暖的地方。
The company plans to migrate its data to the cloud. = 公司计划将其数据迁移到云端。

虚拟语气:
If we had migrated earlier, we would have avoided the worst of the storm. = 如果我们早点迁移，就能避开最严重的暴风雨。
I wish I had migrated sooner. = 我希望我早点迁移。

被动语态:
The data was migrated successfully to the new server. = 数据成功迁移到新的服务器。
The refugees were migrated to safety. = 难民被迁移到安全的地方。

疑问句:
When do the birds usually migrate? = 鸟类通常什么时候迁徙？
Why did your family migrate? = 你家为什么迁移？

否定句:
The birds did not migrate this year. = 鸟类今年没有迁徙。
They haven't migrated yet. = 他们还没有迁移。


PHRASE->
mass migration = 大规模迁移
seasonal migration = 季节性迁徙
bird migration = 鸟类迁徙
human migration = 人口迁移
migration patterns = 迁徙模式
