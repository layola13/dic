chicha:/ˈtʃiːtʃə/| n.|奇恰酒（一种南美玉米酒）|An alcoholic beverage made from fermented maize (corn), common in several South American countries.  It is often prepared and consumed communally, and its preparation and consumption are often associated with indigenous cultures and traditions.  The specific recipe and alcohol content can vary greatly depending on the region and the specific preparation method.|例句：The villagers gathered to share stories and drink chicha after the harvest. 村民们在收割后聚在一起分享故事和饮用奇恰酒。|近义词：corn beer, maize beer |反义词：N/A
