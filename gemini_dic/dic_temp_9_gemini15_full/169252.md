promenade:/ˌprɒməˈneɪd/ /ˌprɑːməˈneɪd/| n. v.|散步；漫步；（海滨）长廊；（音乐会等的）序曲|A leisurely walk, especially one taken for pleasure in a public place; a public walk or area for strolling.|例句：We took a leisurely promenade along the beach. 我们沿着海滩悠闲地散步。|例句：The town's promenade is a popular spot for evening strolls.  这个镇的海滨长廊是傍晚散步的热门场所。|近义词：stroll, walk, stroll, parade|反义词：hurry, rush|名词，动词
