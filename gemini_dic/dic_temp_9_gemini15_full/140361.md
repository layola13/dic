oil-bearing: /ˈɔɪlˌbɛərɪŋ/ | adj. | 含油的，产油的 | Containing or yielding oil.  | Example: The oil-bearing shale is a potential source of energy.  这层含油页岩是潜在的能源。 The oil-bearing region attracted many investors.  产油地区吸引了许多投资者。 | 近义词: oil-rich, petroliferous | 反义词: oil-free, barren


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The oil-bearing region produces a significant amount of crude oil. = 产油区生产大量的原油。
This soil is oil-bearing and needs special treatment. = 这土壤含油，需要特殊处理。


一般过去时:
That area was an oil-bearing region until the wells dried up. = 那片地区曾经是产油区，直到油井枯竭。
Geologists confirmed that the rock formation was oil-bearing. = 地质学家证实该岩层含油。


现在完成时:
Scientists have discovered several new oil-bearing formations. = 科学家们已经发现了几个新的含油地层。
The company has invested heavily in exploring oil-bearing lands. = 该公司已大力投资勘探含油土地。


过去完成时:
Before the discovery, nobody knew the land was oil-bearing. = 在发现之前，没有人知道这片土地是产油的。
Geologists had already identified the area as potentially oil-bearing. = 地质学家们已经确定该地区可能有石油。
