prebake:/ˈpriːbeɪk/| vt.|预烤|To bake (food) partially before completing the baking process, often to prevent burning or ensure even cooking.|例句：We prebake the pie crust for 10 minutes before adding the filling. 我们先将派皮预烤10分钟，然后再添加馅料。|例句：The chef prebaked the bread rolls to ensure they were crispy. 厨师预烤面包卷以确保它们酥脆。|近义词：partially bake, pre-cook|反义词：fully bake, completely bake
