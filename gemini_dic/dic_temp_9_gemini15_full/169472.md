propagable:/ˈprɒpəɡəbl/| adj.| 可传播的，可繁殖的 | Capable of being propagated or spread; able to be transmitted or replicated.  |Example: The disease is highly propagable, requiring strict quarantine measures. 这疾病具有高度传染性，需要严格的隔离措施。The plant is easily propagable by cuttings.  这种植物很容易通过扦插繁殖。|近义词：transmissible, communicable, reproducible, replicable|反义词：incurable, untransmittable
