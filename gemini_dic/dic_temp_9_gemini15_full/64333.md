eradicated:/ɪˈrædɪkeɪtɪd/| v.|根除；消灭；杜绝|To completely destroy or get rid of something harmful or unwanted.|The government is committed to eradicating poverty. 政府致力于消除贫困。|近义词：eliminate, destroy, exterminate, abolish|反义词：establish, cultivate, foster


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We eradicate weeds in the garden every week. = 我们每周都在花园里除草。
The company eradicates harmful substances from its products. = 这家公司从其产品中清除有害物质。

一般过去时:
The disease was eradicated in the 1950s. = 这疾病在20世纪50年代被根除了。
They eradicated the pests from the crops. = 他们根除了农作物上的害虫。

现在完成时:
Scientists have eradicated many diseases. = 科学家们已经根除了许多疾病。
We have eradicated all the bugs in the system. = 我们已经消除了系统中的所有漏洞。

过去完成时:
They had eradicated the problem before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前解决了这个问题。
The team had eradicated the virus before it spread widely. = 该团队在病毒广泛传播之前就将其根除了。

将来时:
We will eradicate hunger in our community. = 我们将消除我们社区的饥饿问题。
They will eradicate the invasive species from the area. = 他们将从该地区清除入侵物种。

虚拟语气:
If we acted quickly, we could eradicate the problem. = 如果我们迅速行动，就能解决这个问题。
I wish we could eradicate all forms of discrimination. = 我希望我们能够消除所有形式的歧视。

被动语态:
The disease was eradicated by the new vaccine. = 这种疾病被新疫苗根除了。
The problem will be eradicated by the new policy. = 新政策将解决这个问题。

疑问句:
Can we eradicate poverty in our lifetime? = 我们有生之年能消除贫困吗？
Has the government eradicated corruption successfully? = 政府成功地根除了腐败吗？

否定句:
We haven't eradicated the problem completely yet. = 我们还没有完全解决这个问题。
They didn't eradicate the weeds effectively. = 他们没有有效地除掉杂草。
