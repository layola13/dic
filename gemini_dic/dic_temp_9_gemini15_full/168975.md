profiters:/ˈprɑːfɪtərz/| n.|牟利者，获利者|Those who benefit unfairly or excessively from a situation, often at the expense of others.  People who make money from something that is wrong or unpleasant. |例句：Many saw the rising food prices as an opportunity for profiters. 许多人认为食品价格上涨是投机者获利的机会。

例句：The government cracked down on profiters who were exploiting the shortage of essential goods. 政府打击了那些利用生活必需品短缺牟利的投机者。


近义词：speculators,exploiters,opportunists|反义词：benefactors,altruists|名词
