designer:/dɪˈzaɪnər/| n.|设计师；设计者|A person who designs something, typically clothing, jewelry, or interiors.|例句：She's a renowned fashion designer.  她是位著名的服装设计师。|例句：The interior designer transformed the space. 室内设计师改变了空间的面貌。|近义词：creator, architect, stylist|反义词：consumer, user


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The designer creates beautiful dresses. = 设计师设计美丽的衣服。
He designs innovative software. = 他设计创新的软件。


一般过去时:
The designer created a stunning collection. = 设计师创作了一个令人惊叹的系列。
She designed the logo for the company. = 她为公司设计了标志。


现在完成时:
The designer has designed many award-winning projects. = 该设计师已设计了许多屡获殊荣的项目。
He has completed the design for the new building. = 他已经完成了新建筑的设计。


过去完成时:
The designer had already finished the sketches before the meeting. = 在会议之前，设计师已经完成了素描。
They had designed the website before launching the product. = 他们在发布产品之前就已经设计好了网站。


将来时:
The designer will present her new collection next week. = 设计师将于下周展示她的新系列。
He will design the set for the play. = 他将为戏剧设计布景。


虚拟语气:
If I were a designer, I would design sustainable clothing. = 如果我是设计师，我会设计可持续的服装。
I wish I had designed that amazing building. = 我真希望我设计了那座令人惊叹的建筑。


被动语态:
The dress was designed by a famous designer. = 这件衣服是一位著名设计师设计的。
The new software is being designed by a team of experts. = 新软件正由专家团队设计。


疑问句:
Who designed this beautiful house? = 谁设计了这座美丽的房子？
Where did you find this designer bag? = 你在哪里找到这个设计师包？


否定句:
She didn't design the website herself. = 她自己并没有设计这个网站。
He is not a well-known designer. = 他不是一位知名的设计师。
