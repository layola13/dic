impeachments:/ɪmˈpiːtʃmənts/ | n. | 弹劾；指控 | The process of accusing a public official of misconduct.  Impeachments are formal accusations, not convictions.  | Example: The president faced several impeachments throughout his career.  总统在他的职业生涯中面临了几次弹劾。|近义词：accusations, indictments |反义词：acquittals, exonerations
