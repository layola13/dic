panga:/ˈpæŋɡə/| n.| 庞加，一种船|A panga is a small, fast motorboat, often used in coastal waters for fishing or transportation.  Pangas are commonly found in Latin America and the Caribbean.|例句：The fishermen used their panga to reach the fishing grounds. 渔民们用他们的庞加到达渔场。|近义词：small boat, skiff, dinghy|反义词：large ship, ocean liner|名词
