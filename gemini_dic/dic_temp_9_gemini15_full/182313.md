renailing: /ˌriːˈneɪlɪŋ/ | n. | 重新钉牢；再次钉钉 | The act of nailing something again, often to repair or reinforce.  This usually involves removing old nails and replacing them with new ones, or adding more nails to existing ones. |例句：The loose floorboard required renailing.  重新钉牢松动的地板。


The roof needed renailing after the storm.暴风雨过后屋顶需要重新钉牢。

近义词：refastening, resecuring | 反义词：removing, unfastening | 词性:名词
