complimental:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmɛntəl/ /ˌkɑːmplɪˈmɛntəl/ | adj. |赞美的；免费赠送的；补充的 |Expressing praise or admiration; given free as a bonus or courtesy.|The complimentary remarks boosted her confidence. 赞美的言论增强了她的信心。|The hotel offered a complimentary breakfast.  酒店提供免费早餐。|近义词：praiseworthy, flattering, gratuitous|反义词：critical, insulting, charged


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The article is complimentary to the author's work. = 这篇文章对作者的作品给予了赞扬。
The hotel offers a complimentary shuttle service. = 酒店提供免费班车服务。

一般过去时:
Her comments were complimentary about his performance. = 她的评论对他的表现赞赏有加。
The restaurant gave us complimentary desserts. = 餐厅给了我们免费甜点。


现在完成时:
They have received many complimentary emails about their new product. = 他们收到了很多关于他们新产品的赞扬邮件。
The airline has provided complimentary drinks on this flight. = 这趟航班上航空公司提供了免费饮料。


过去完成时:
She had already received several complimentary reviews before the book was even published. = 这本书出版之前，她已经收到了几篇好评。
The company had offered complimentary samples at the trade show. = 公司在贸易展会上提供了免费样品。


将来时:
The chef will provide complimentary appetizers for VIP guests. = 主厨将为贵宾提供免费开胃菜。
We will receive complimentary tickets to the concert. = 我们将收到音乐会的免费门票。


虚拟语气:
If the review were complimentary, it would boost sales. = 如果评论是赞扬的，就会促进销售。
I wish the feedback had been more complimentary. = 我希望反馈更正面一些。


被动语态:
The service was complimented by many customers. = 这项服务受到了许多顾客的称赞。
Free samples were offered as a complimentary gift. = 免费样品作为赠品提供。


疑问句:
Is the service complimentary? = 服务是免费的吗？
Were the drinks complimentary? = 饮料是免费的吗？


否定句:
His remarks were not complimentary at all. = 他的话语一点也不赞美。
The upgrade was not complimentary; we had to pay extra. = 升级不是免费的；我们不得不额外付费。
