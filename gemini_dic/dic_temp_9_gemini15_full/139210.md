objectivity:/ˌɒbdʒɛkˈtɪvɪti/ /ˌɑːbdʒɛkˈtɪvɪti/ | n. |客观性；客观现实|The quality or state of being objective; unbiasedness, fairness, and lack of prejudice in considering facts.  It refers to the ability to judge fairly, without letting personal feelings or opinions influence your decisions. |The judge's objectivity was questioned due to his prior relationship with the defendant.  法官的客观性因其与被告之前的关系而受到质疑。|subjectivity, impartiality|subjectivity, bias


例句：

The scientist maintained strict objectivity in their research, avoiding any personal bias. 科学家在研究中保持严格的客观性，避免任何个人偏见。

Her report lacked objectivity, showing a clear preference for one side of the argument.  她的报告缺乏客观性，明显偏袒一方的论点。


近义词：impartiality, fairness, neutrality, unbiasedness

反义词：subjectivity, bias, prejudice, partiality
