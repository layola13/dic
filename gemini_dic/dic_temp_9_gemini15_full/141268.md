opaqued:/oʊˈpeɪkt/ | adj. | 不透明的；使不透明 | Made unclear, dull, or opaque.  To make something opaque is to obscure it or make it difficult to see through, either literally or figuratively. | The frosted glass opaqued the view into the office.  磨砂玻璃使办公室的景色变得模糊不清。| The explanation opaqued rather than clarified the issue. 解释使问题更加模糊，而不是更清晰。| translucent, cloudy | transparent, clear, lucid
