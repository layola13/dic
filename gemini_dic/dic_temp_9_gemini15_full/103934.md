judging:/ˈdʒʌdʒɪŋ/ | v. | 判断；裁决；评价 | To form an opinion or conclusion about something or someone.  |例句：Judging by his expression, he was unhappy.  根据他的表情判断，他不高兴。|例句：The judge is judging the case. 法官正在审理此案。|近义词：assessing, evaluating, appraising |反义词：ignoring, overlooking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is judging the contestants' performance. = 他正在评判选手的表现。
I am judging the quality of the wine. = 我正在评判葡萄酒的质量。

一般过去时:
She judged the competition fairly. = 她公平地评判了比赛。
The committee judged the entries carefully. = 委员会仔细地评判了参赛作品。

现在完成时:
They have judged the winner of the contest. = 他们已经评判出了比赛的获胜者。
We have judged the situation to be critical. = 我们已经判断形势危急。

过去完成时:
He had judged the book by its cover. = 他以貌取人，凭封面来判断这本书。
She had judged the distance incorrectly. = 她判断距离不准确。

将来时:
They will judge the merits of the proposal. = 他们将评判这项提案的优点。
The panel will judge the applicants' qualifications. = 专家小组将评判申请人的资格。

虚拟语气:
If I were judging, I would choose differently. = 如果我来评判，我会做出不同的选择。
I wish I had judged the situation more accurately. = 我希望我当时更准确地判断了形势。

被动语态:
The painting was judged to be a masterpiece. = 这幅画被认为是杰作。
The case is being judged by a panel of experts. = 此案由专家小组正在审理。

疑问句:
How are you judging the situation? = 你是如何判断局势的？
What criteria are you using to judge the quality? = 你用什么标准来判断质量？

否定句:
Don't judge a book by its cover. = 不要以貌取人。
I don't judge people based on their appearance. = 我不以貌取人。


PHRASE->
Judging from... = 从……来看
Judging by... = 根据……判断
Judging by appearances = 从外表看来
Judging from the evidence = 从证据来看
Judging from what I've seen = 从我所见来看
