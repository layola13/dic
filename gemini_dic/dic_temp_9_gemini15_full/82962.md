gunged:/ɡʌŋd/| adj.| 脏兮兮的；堵塞的；厌倦的 | Covered in mud or dirt; blocked or clogged; bored or fed up. |例句：The car's engine was completely gunged up with oil. = 汽车引擎完全被油堵塞了。
The kids were gunged up after playing in the mud. = 孩子们在泥地里玩耍后变得脏兮兮的。|近义词：dirty, clogged, grimy, fed up, bored |反义词：clean, clear, unblocked, enthusiastic
