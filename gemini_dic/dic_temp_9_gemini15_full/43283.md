craftsmen:/ˈkræftsmən/| n. |工匠，技工 |A craftsman is a skilled worker who makes things by hand.  They are typically highly proficient in their chosen craft, often possessing years of experience and expertise. |例句：The craftsmen meticulously carved the intricate details of the wooden sculpture.  这些工匠一丝不苟地雕刻着木雕的复杂细节。

The antique furniture was made by skilled craftsmen. 这件古董家具是由熟练的工匠制作的。|近义词：artisan, artisan, handcraftsman |反义词：amateur, novice
