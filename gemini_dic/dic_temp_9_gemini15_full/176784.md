readjourning:/ˌriːˈdʒɜːrnɪŋ/| v.| 重新休会；再次休会 |  The act of resuming a meeting or session after a recess or adjournment.  It implies a prior adjournment and a subsequent resumption. | Example: After a brief recess for lunch, the committee readjourned to continue their deliberations.  重新休会后，委员会继续他们的讨论。|近义词：resuming, reconvening |反义词：adjourning (in the sense of ending a meeting permanently)

The court readjourns after lunch. = 法院午饭后重新休会。
They readjourn the meeting to tomorrow. = 他们把会议重新推迟到明天。
