dissipated:/ˈdɪsɪpeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 浪费的；消散的；放荡的 |  Scattered or dispersed; wasted or squandered; showing a lack of restraint.  | The dissipated fog revealed the landscape.  消散的雾露出了风景。 | The dissipated heir squandered his inheritance.  挥霍无度的继承人挥霍了他的遗产。 | concentrated, focused, thrifty | frugal, temperate | adj.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dissipates his energy on trivial pursuits. 他把精力浪费在琐事上。
The sun dissipates the morning mist. 太阳驱散了早晨的雾气。


一般过去时:
The party dissipated as the night wore on.  随着夜晚的推移，聚会逐渐散去。
He dissipated his fortune in gambling. 他把财产都赌光了。


现在完成时:
The rumors have dissipated. 谣言已经消散了。
He has dissipated all his savings. 他已挥霍光了他的积蓄。


过去完成时:
The evidence had dissipated by the time the police arrived.  警察到达时，证据已经散失了。
He had dissipated his inheritance before he turned thirty.  他不到三十岁就挥霍了他的遗产。


将来时:
The storm will dissipate by tomorrow.  暴风雨明天将会消散。
He will likely dissipate his new fortune as quickly as he earned it. 他可能会像赚到一样快地挥霍掉他的新财富。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't dissipated his resources, he would be wealthy now. 如果他没有挥霍他的资源，他现在就会富有。
I wish I hadn't dissipated my time on unproductive activities. 我真希望我没有把时间浪费在没有成效的活动上。


被动语态:
The fog was dissipated by the rising sun.  雾被升起的太阳驱散了。
His energy was dissipated by endless worries. 他精力被无尽的忧虑消耗殆尽。


疑问句:
Has the crowd dissipated yet? 人群散了吗？
Will the tension dissipate soon? 紧张局势很快就会缓解吗？


否定句:
The problem hasn't dissipated despite our efforts.  尽管我们努力了，问题并没有解决。
He didn't dissipate his inheritance; he invested wisely. 他没有挥霍他的遗产，而是明智地进行了投资。

词源:
From Latin *dissipare, from dis- ("apart") + spargere ("to scatter").  意思是“分散”，“驱散”。
