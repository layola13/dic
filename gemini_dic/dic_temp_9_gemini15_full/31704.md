chavish:/ˈtʃeɪvɪʃ/| adj.| 粗俗的，不雅观的；炫耀财富的 |  Referring to a person or their behaviour as loud, flashy, and considered lower-class or uncouth, often associated with ostentatious displays of wealth.  The term carries a strong negative connotation. | Example:  He drives a chavish car, all chrome and spoilers. 他开着一辆俗气的车，全是镀铬和扰流板。  |近义词：vulgar, loutish, tacky, common, gaudy | 反义词：refined, classy, elegant, sophisticated |
