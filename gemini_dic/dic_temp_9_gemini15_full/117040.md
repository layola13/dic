mcman:/ˈmækmæn/| n. |麦曼式住宅；郊区大型住宅|A McMansion is a large, often ostentatious, house built in a relatively short period of time, typically in a suburban area.  These homes are characterized by their size, mass-produced features, and sometimes a lack of architectural distinction compared to more custom-built homes. They are often criticized for their environmental impact and contribution to urban sprawl.|例句：The new development was filled with McMansions, all nearly identical.|近义词：superhouse, large house, suburban home|反义词：cottage, bungalow, modest home
