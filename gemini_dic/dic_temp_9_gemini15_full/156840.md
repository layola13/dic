piglike:/ˈpɪɡlaɪk/| adj. |像猪一样的；贪婪的；邋遢的 |Resembling a pig in appearance, behavior, or character; often implying dirtiness, greed, or slovenliness.  |例句：He had a piglike appetite, devouring everything on the plate. 他有一个像猪一样的胃口，把盘子里的东西都吃光了。
例句：The piglike conditions in the slum were shocking. 贫民窟里像猪圈一样的环境令人震惊。|近义词：swinish, hoggish, gluttonous, slovenly |反义词：neat, tidy, refined, dainty
