reoriented:/ˌriːˈɔːriɛntɪd/ | v. |重新定向；重新定位；改变方向 | To change the direction or focus of something or someone.  |例句：The company reoriented its marketing strategy to target a younger demographic. 公司重新调整了其营销策略，以瞄准更年轻的人群。

例句：After the accident, he needed therapy to reorient himself to everyday life. 事故之后，他需要进行治疗以重新适应日常生活。


近义词：redirect, refocus, readjust, realign | 反义词：misdirect, disorient


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reorients its marketing strategy annually. = 公司每年都会调整其营销策略。
He reorients himself to the new environment quickly. = 他很快适应了新的环境。


一般过去时:
They reoriented their business model after the market crash. = 市场崩盘后，他们重新调整了商业模式。
She reoriented her priorities after having children. = 她生孩子后重新调整了优先级。


现在完成时:
The government has reoriented its foreign policy. = 政府已经调整了其外交政策。
He has reoriented his life goals since graduating. = 他毕业后重新规划了自己的生活目标。


过去完成时:
They had reoriented their plans before the unexpected delay. = 他们因为意外的延误而提前调整了计划。
She had reoriented her career path before accepting the new position. = 在接受新职位之前，她已经调整了自己的职业道路。


将来时:
We will reorient our efforts towards sustainable development. = 我们将把工作重点转向可持续发展。
The company will reorient its product line next year. = 公司明年将调整其产品线。


虚拟语气:
If they had reoriented their strategy earlier, they would have avoided bankruptcy. = 如果他们早点调整策略，就能避免破产。
I wish I had reoriented my career path sooner. = 我真希望我早点调整职业道路。


被动语态:
The company's marketing strategy was reoriented to focus on online sales. = 公司的营销策略已重新调整，重点放在在线销售上。
His life was reoriented by a profound spiritual experience. = 他的人生因一次深刻的精神体验而发生了改变。


疑问句:
Has the project been reoriented to meet the new deadline? = 项目是否已重新调整以满足新的截止日期？
Will the curriculum be reoriented to include more technology? = 课程是否会重新调整以包含更多技术？


否定句:
The company did not reorient its strategy despite the declining sales. = 尽管销售额下降，但公司并没有调整其策略。
He refused to reorient his beliefs, even in the face of overwhelming evidence. = 即使面对压倒性的证据，他也不愿改变自己的信仰。
