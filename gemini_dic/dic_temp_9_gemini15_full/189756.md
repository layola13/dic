saltpeter:/ˈsɔːltpiːtər/ | n. |硝石，硝酸钾 |Saltpeter is a naturally occurring mineral consisting primarily of potassium nitrate (KNO3). It has been known and used for centuries for various purposes, including preserving food (as a curing agent), making gunpowder, and as a fertilizer.  |Example:  The ancient Egyptians used saltpeter in their mummification process. 古埃及人用硝石进行木乃伊制作。

The earliest known use of saltpeter was as a food preservative.  硝石最早的已知用途是作为食品防腐剂。|近义词：potassium nitrate, niter |反义词：无
