noncompulsions:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpʌlʃənz/| n. |非强迫行为|The absence of compulsive behaviors; actions performed without feeling driven or obligated.|Example:  He exhibited noncompulsions in his daily routine, a stark contrast to his previous struggles. 他在日常生活中表现出非强迫行为，这与他之前的挣扎形成了鲜明对比。|近义词: voluntary actions, free choices|反义词: compulsions, obsessions|名词
