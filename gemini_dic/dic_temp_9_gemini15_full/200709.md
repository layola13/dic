sleepwalked:/ˈsliːpwɔːkt/ | v. |梦游(过去式和过去分词)|To walk around while asleep.  This is an involuntary action that occurs during sleep. |例句：He sleepwalked down the stairs and into the kitchen last night.  昨晚他梦游着下楼走进了厨房。|例句：She didn't remember sleepwalking, but her husband found her in the garden. 她不记得自己梦游了，但是她丈夫在花园里找到了她。|近义词：somnambulated |反义词：N/A


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He sleepwalks every night. = 他每晚都梦游。
She often sleepwalks when stressed. = 她压力大的时候经常梦游。

一般过去时:
I sleepwalked last night. = 我昨晚梦游了。
The child sleepwalked into the street. = 那孩子梦游着走到了街上。

现在完成时:
He has sleepwalked several times this month. = 这个月他已经梦游过好几次了。
She has never sleepwalked before. = 她以前从未梦游过。

过去完成时:
I had sleepwalked before, so I wasn't surprised. = 我以前梦游过，所以我不感到惊讶。
They had sleepwalked to the edge of the cliff. = 他们梦游着走到悬崖边。

将来时:
He will likely sleepwalk again if he doesn't get enough sleep. = 如果他不睡够觉，很可能还会梦游。
She thinks she will sleepwalk tonight. = 她认为她今晚会梦游。

虚拟语气:
If I sleepwalked, I'd be terrified. = 如果我梦游，我会很害怕。
I wish I hadn't sleepwalked and woken everyone up. = 我真希望我没有梦游并吵醒所有人。
