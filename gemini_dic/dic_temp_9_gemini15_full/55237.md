dissimilar:/dɪˈsɪmɪlər/ | adj. | 不同的，相异的 | Unlike; not similar.  Dissimilar things have different characteristics or qualities. | The two paintings were dissimilar in style and subject matter. 这两幅画在风格和主题上都不同。 The twins, though similar in appearance, had dissimilar personalities.  虽然这对双胞胎外表相似，但性格却大相径庭。 | similar, unlike, different | identical, alike, same
