miniaturising: /ˌmɪnɪətʃəˈraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 小型化，微型化 | The process of making something smaller, especially in scale or size.  It often involves technological advancements to reduce the size of devices or systems while maintaining functionality. |例句：The company is focusing on miniaturising its new phone to make it more portable.  该公司正致力于将其新款手机小型化，以使其更便携。

例句：Scientists are constantly working on miniaturising electronic components for better performance in smaller spaces. 科学家们不断致力于小型化电子元件，以在更小的空间内获得更好的性能。


近义词：downsizing, reducing, shrinking


反义词：enlarging, magnifying, expanding


词性：动词
