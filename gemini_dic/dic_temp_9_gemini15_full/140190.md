office:/ˈɒfɪs/ /ˈɑːfɪs/| n.|办公室；办事处；职务；公职|A room or set of rooms used as a place of business.  The place where a particular kind of work is done. A position of employment; a job.|例句：He works in a small office in the city center. 他在市中心的一间小办公室工作。|例句：She holds a senior office in the government.她在政府担任要职。|近义词：bureau, department, agency|反义词：home, residence
