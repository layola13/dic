discarnate:/dɪsˈkɑːrneɪt/| adj.|脱离肉体的；无形的；灵魂的| Separated from the body; disembodied; spiritual.  The term often refers to a spirit or soul after death, existing independently of a physical form. |例句：Many religions believe in a discarnate existence after death. 许多宗教相信死后存在脱离肉体的存在。

例句：The medium claimed to communicate with discarnate spirits. 那个灵媒声称可以与脱离肉体的灵魂沟通。


近义词：disembodied, incorporeal, ethereal, spiritual| 反义词：incarnate, embodied, corporeal| 词性: adjective
