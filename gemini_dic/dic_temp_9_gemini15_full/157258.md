pinnock:/ˈpɪnək/| n. |小木桩；钉子；（尤指）木制销子|A small peg, pin, or dowel, especially one made of wood.  It is often used in carpentry or other crafts. |例句：He used a pinnock to secure the loose board. 他用一个小木桩固定松动的木板。 He hammered the pinnock firmly into place. 他用力把木销钉牢固地钉好。|近义词：peg, dowel, pin|反义词：None specifically
