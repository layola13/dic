piteous:/ˈpɪtiəs/| adj.|可怜的；令人怜悯的；卑鄙的 | Evoking or deserving pity; pathetic.  The word often implies a degree of weakness or helplessness.  It can also, less commonly, suggest something contemptible or mean. |例句：The piteous cries of the wounded animal filled the air.  受伤动物可怜的叫声充满了空气。

The piteous state of the abandoned building was clear to see.  被遗弃建筑物可怜的状态一目了然。


近义词：pathetic, mournful, wretched, lamentable, sorry, pitiful |反义词：joyful, happy, fortunate, blessed


词源：Middle English: from Old French piteus, from pite ‘pity’.
