genitor:/ˈdʒɛnɪtər/ | n. | 生父，父亲 | A male parent; a father.  The word often implies a biological father, rather than a stepfather or adoptive father.  It is a more formal and less common word than "father". | Example: He was estranged from his genitor for many years. 他与生父多年疏远。 |近义词：father, sire, begetter | 反义词：mother, offspring
