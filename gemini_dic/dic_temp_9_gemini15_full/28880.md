carpool:/ˈkɑːrpuːl/|/ˈkɑːrpuːl/| n. v.|拼车；合用汽车|The act of sharing a car journey with others, typically to commute to work or school.  |例句：We carpool to work every day to save money on gas. 我们每天拼车上班以节省汽油费。

例句：I'm looking for someone to carpool with to the concert. 我正在寻找一起去演唱会拼车的人。


词源：源于"car"（汽车）和"pool"（共用，合用），表示共享汽车资源。


用法说明：

名词用法:  a carpool arrangement; a carpool lane


动词用法: to carpool to work;  to carpool with friends


近义词：rideshare, share a ride


反义词：drive alone, solo commute


词性：名词，动词
