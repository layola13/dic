impactor:/ˈɪmpæktər/| n. |撞击物；陨石；冲压器| An object that impacts something else, especially a meteorite or a tool used for shaping metal by impact.  |例句：The crater was formed by a massive impactor.  陨石坑是由巨大的撞击物形成的。

The researchers used a high-velocity impactor to test the material's strength. 研究人员使用高速撞击器来测试材料的强度。

|近义词：projectile, meteorite, ram|反义词：N/A|
