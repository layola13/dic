nonlubriciously:/ˌnɒnluːˈbrɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 非润滑地；不润滑地 | Not lubricated; lacking lubrication.  The gears ground together nonlubriciously, causing a loud screech.  | 近义词：dryly, unlubricatedly | 反义词：lubricously


例句：

The machine parts moved nonlubriciously, leading to increased friction and wear.  机器零件非润滑地移动，导致摩擦和磨损增加。


The engine seized due to the nonlubriciously operation of its parts.  由于零件的非润滑运行，发动机卡住了。
