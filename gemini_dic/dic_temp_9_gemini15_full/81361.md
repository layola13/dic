grapnel:/ˈɡræpnəl/| n.|抓锚，抓钩|A grapnel is a small anchor with several flukes or claws, used for holding a small boat or for grappling. It's often used in situations where a regular anchor is too large or unnecessary.|例句：The sailors used a grapnel to secure the small boat to the rocks. 水手们用抓锚把小船固定在岩石上。

例句：The divers deployed a grapnel to retrieve a lost piece of equipment from the seabed. 潜水员放下抓钩从海底打捞丢失的设备。|近义词：anchor, hook, claw|反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting release or letting go could be considered opposites in context.
