ilia:/ˈɪliə/| n.|髂骨；骼；肠骨|The ilia are two large, flaring bones that form part of the pelvis. They provide attachment points for numerous muscles and ligaments, contributing to hip stability and movement.  |例句：The fracture affected his left ilium.  他的左髂骨骨折了。|例句：The ilia articulate with the sacrum to form the sacroiliac joint. 髂骨与骶骨连接形成骶髂关节。|近义词：hip bone, pelvic bone|反义词：无
