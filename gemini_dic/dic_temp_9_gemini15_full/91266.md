hydrogen:/ˈhaɪdrədʒən/ | n. |氢 |A colorless, odorless, tasteless, nonmetallic, highly flammable diatomic gas, the simplest and lightest element, which is a major constituent of water, acids, and alkalis, and is used in the synthesis of ammonia and methanol and as a rocket fuel.  |Example: Hydrogen is the most abundant element in the universe. 氢是宇宙中最丰富的元素。

Example:  Water is made up of hydrogen and oxygen. 水是由氢和氧组成的。|近义词:None |反义词:None |词性:名词
