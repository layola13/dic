recession's:/rɪˈseʃənz/| n. |经济衰退的；衰退的 |The possessive form of "recession," referring to something belonging to or associated with a recession.  It often modifies nouns. |Example: The recession's impact on the housing market was significant.  经济衰退对房地产市场的影响是巨大的。|近义词：depression's, downturn's |反义词：boom's, expansion's |名词
