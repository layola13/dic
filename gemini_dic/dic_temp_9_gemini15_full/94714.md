improperness:/ɪmˈprɒpənəs/| n. |不当；不合适；无礼|The improperness of his remarks offended many people.  他的言论不当，冒犯了许多人。|近义词：inappropriateness, unsuitability, indecency|反义词：propriety, appropriateness, decency|

英文解析:The state or quality of being improper; lack of propriety or decorum; unsuitability.  指不当的行为、状态或品质；缺乏得体或礼仪；不合适。

例句：

The improperness of his behaviour was evident to everyone. 他行为的不当之处显而易见。

The improperness of the attire caused a stir at the formal event. 不合适的穿着在正式场合引起了骚动。


词源:

From improper + -ness.  源自 improper (不当的) + -ness (名词后缀)。  Improper itself comes from the prefix "im-" (not) and "proper" (suitable, appropriate).  "Improper" 本身来自前缀 "im-" (不) 和 "proper" (合适的，适当的)。
