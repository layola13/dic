religionless: /rɪˈlɪdʒənles/ | adj. | 无宗教信仰的，非宗教的 | Lacking religious belief or affiliation.  |  例句：He led a religionless life, finding meaning in secular pursuits. 他过着无宗教信仰的生活，在世俗追求中找到了意义。

例句：The survey revealed a growing number of religionless individuals in the younger generation.  调查显示，年轻一代中无宗教信仰的人越来越多。


近义词：secular, irreligious, atheistic (if specifically lacking belief in a god)

反义词：religious, devout, pious

词性：形容词
