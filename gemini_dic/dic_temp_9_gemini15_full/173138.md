quasi-alternative:/ˈkwɑːziːˈɔːltərnətɪv/| adj. |准替代的，类似替代的 |  Describes something that is almost but not quite an alternative; it shares some characteristics with an alternative but lacks complete equivalence.  It suggests a partial or imperfect substitution. | Example:  The proposed solution is a quasi-alternative to the existing system; it addresses some of the issues but not all.  提议的解决方案是现有系统的一个准替代方案；它解决了一些问题，但不是全部。|近义词：near-alternative, partial alternative, substitute (in a limited sense)|反义词：true alternative, complete replacement|形容词
