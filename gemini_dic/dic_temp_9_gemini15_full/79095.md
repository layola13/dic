glacis:/ˈɡlæsɪs/| n.|缓坡；护坡；釉面；|A glacis is a gently sloping surface, especially one used as a military defense or a decorative feature on a building.  It can also refer to a smooth, glassy surface, such as a glaze on pottery.|例句：The fortress was protected by a wide glacis.  这座堡垒受到一个宽阔的缓坡的保护。

The artist applied a beautiful glacis to the ceramic vase.艺术家在陶瓷花瓶上涂上了一层美丽的釉面。|近义词：slope, rampart, embankment, glaze|反义词：steep incline, cliff
