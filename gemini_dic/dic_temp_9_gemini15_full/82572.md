guard against:/ɡɑːrd əˈɡenst/ | v. | 预防；提防；警惕 | To protect or defend against; to be cautious or wary of.  | 例句：We must guard against complacency. 我们必须防止自满。

We need to guard against the spread of misinformation. 我们需要警惕虚假信息的传播。

近义词：prevent, protect, avoid, shield, safeguard | 反义词：neglect, encourage, promote


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They guard against theft by installing security cameras. = 他们通过安装安全摄像头来防止盗窃。
We guard against making hasty decisions. = 我们警惕做出草率的决定。


一般过去时:
She guarded against making the same mistake twice. = 她警惕不再犯同样的错误两次。
They guarded against the dangers of the wilderness. = 他们提防着荒野的危险。


现在完成时:
We have guarded against potential risks throughout the project. = 在整个项目中，我们一直提防着潜在的风险。
They have guarded against any form of corruption. = 他们一直警惕任何形式的腐败。


过去完成时:
He had guarded against betrayal by keeping his secrets close. = 他通过保守秘密来防止背叛。
She had guarded against the cold by wearing many layers of clothing. = 她通过穿很多层衣服来抵御寒冷。


将来时:
We will guard against future errors by implementing better controls. = 我们将通过实施更好的控制来防止未来的错误。
They will guard against any attempts to sabotage the mission. = 他们将警惕任何破坏任务的企图。


虚拟语气:
If we had guarded against the risks, the outcome would have been different. = 如果我们当时提防了风险，结果就会不一样。
I wish we had guarded against the potential for conflict. = 我希望我们当时已经警惕了冲突的可能性。


被动语态:
The system is designed to guard against unauthorized access. = 该系统旨在防止未经授权的访问。
Precautions are being taken to guard against the spread of disease. = 正在采取预防措施以防止疾病的传播。


疑问句:
How can we best guard against cyberattacks? = 我们如何才能最好地防止网络攻击？
What measures are in place to guard against financial fraud? = 有哪些措施来防止财务欺诈？


否定句:
They failed to guard against the unforeseen consequences. = 他们没有防范到不可预见的后果。
We did not guard against the possibility of failure. = 我们没有提防失败的可能性。
