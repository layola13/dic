outsets:/ˈaʊtˌsɛts/| n.|开端；起始；开场白|The early stages or beginning of something.|例句：The outset of the project was plagued with delays. 项目的开端充满了延误。|例句：From the outset, it was clear that the plan was flawed. 从一开始，很明显这个计划是有缺陷的。|近义词：beginning, commencement, start, initiation|反义词：conclusion, end, finish
