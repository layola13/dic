gouts:/ɡaʊts/| n.|少量；一阵；少量；少量|A gout is a sudden, sharp attack of a disease or a sudden appearance of something unpleasant.  It often refers to a small amount of something, especially a liquid.|例句：A gout of blood spurted from the wound.  一股鲜血从伤口喷涌而出。
例句：He suffered a gout of pain in his shoulder. 他肩膀一阵剧痛。|近义词：spurt, outburst, attack, fit|反义词：abundance, plenty, flood
