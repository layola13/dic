omnium:/ˈɒmniəm/| n.|全部，全体；综合；总计|From Latin *omnium, literally "of all things", a neuter plural of omnis "all".  It originally referred to the aggregate of securities issued by a joint-stock company. In modern usage, it generally refers to a collection or totality of something. |例句：The omnium of his debts was staggering. 他所有债务的总额令人震惊。|近义词：aggregate, totality, sum, whole|反义词：none, individual, part
