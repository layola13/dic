outmarched:/ˌaʊtˈmɑːtʃt/ | v. |  超过（行军速度）；胜过（在行军中） | To march further or faster than; to surpass in a march.  | The experienced troops outmarched the enemy, reaching the strategic point first.  经验丰富的部队行军速度超过了敌人，率先到达战略要地。 | surpassed, excelled, outstripped |  retreated, lagged behind | 动词
