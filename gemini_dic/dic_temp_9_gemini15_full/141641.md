optimises:/ˈɒptɪmaɪzɪz/ | v. | 优化；使最优化 | To make the best or most effective use of (a resource).  | The company optimised its production process to reduce costs. 公司优化了生产流程以降低成本。 | improve, enhance, refine | worsen, deteriorate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The software automatically optimises the settings. = 软件自动优化设置。
He optimises his workout routine for maximum results. = 他优化他的锻炼计划以获得最大效果。


一般过去时:
She optimised her website for better search engine ranking. = 她优化了她的网站以获得更好的搜索引擎排名。
They optimised the supply chain to improve efficiency. = 他们优化了供应链以提高效率。


现在完成时:
We have optimised our processes to improve customer satisfaction. = 我们已经优化了流程以提高客户满意度。
The engineers have optimised the engine for better fuel economy. = 工程师们已经优化了发动机以提高燃油经济性。


过去完成时:
They had optimised the algorithm before releasing the software. = 他们已经在发布软件之前优化了算法。
The team had optimised the design to meet the client's needs. = 团队在满足客户需求之前已经优化了设计。


将来时:
We will optimise the system for faster performance. = 我们将优化系统以提高性能。
They will optimise the marketing campaign to reach a wider audience. = 他们将优化营销活动以接触更广泛的受众。


虚拟语气:
If we optimised the code, the program would run faster. = 如果我们优化代码，程序运行速度会更快。
I wish we had optimised the strategy earlier. = 我希望我们早点优化策略。


被动语态:
The system is optimised for maximum efficiency. = 系统经过优化，以达到最大效率。
The design was optimised to reduce weight. = 设计经过优化以减轻重量。


疑问句:
Has the software been optimised for mobile devices? = 软件是否针对移动设备进行了优化？
How can we optimise the workflow? = 我们如何优化工作流程？


否定句:
They did not optimise the database for performance. = 他们没有针对性能优化数据库。
The company has not yet optimised its marketing strategy. = 公司尚未优化其营销策略。


PHRASE->
optimise for: 为…优化
optimise performance: 优化性能
optimise resources: 优化资源
optimise the process: 优化流程
optimise the design: 优化设计

词源:
源自拉丁语 "optimus" (最好的) + "-ise" (动词后缀)。  表示使达到最佳状态。
