polite:/pəˈlaɪt/ | adj. |有礼貌的，客气的 | showing behaviour that is respectful and considerate of other people.  |例句：It's polite to say "please" and "thank you".  说“请”和“谢谢”是有礼貌的。

例句：He was always polite and courteous to everyone. 他总是对每个人都很客气和有礼貌。


近义词：courteous, civil, respectful, well-mannered | 反义词：rude, impolite, discourteous, uncivil


词性：形容词
