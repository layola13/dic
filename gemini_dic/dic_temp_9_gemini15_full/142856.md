out-group:/ˈaʊtɡruːp/| n. |外群体，非我族类|A social group toward which an individual feels a sense of opposition, rivalry, or competition.  It is the group to which one does not belong.|例句：The study examined the prejudice and discrimination experienced by the out-group. 这项研究调查了外群体所经历的偏见和歧视。

例句：Members of the out-group were often stereotyped and marginalized. 外群体成员经常被贴上标签和边缘化。


近义词：other group, outsider, external group

反义词：in-group
