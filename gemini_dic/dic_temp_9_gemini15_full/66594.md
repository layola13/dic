exhumed:/ɪɡˈzjuːmd/ | vt. | 挖掘出（尸体）；发掘出；使重见天日 | To remove (something, especially a body) from a grave.  |例句：The archaeologists exhumed the ancient tomb. 考古学家挖掘了古代墓葬。

例句：They exhumed the body to perform a second autopsy.  他们挖掘出尸体以进行第二次尸检。


近义词：unearth, disinter, dig up | 反义词：bury, inter
