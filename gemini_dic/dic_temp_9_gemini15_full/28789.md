caroling:/ˈkærəlɪŋ/| n. |颂歌，报佳音|The act of singing carols, typically at Christmas.  Caroling is a cherished Christmas tradition in many cultures.|例句：The children went caroling through the neighborhood on Christmas Eve. 圣诞节前夕孩子们在街区报佳音。
例句：We heard beautiful caroling coming from the church. 我们听到教堂传来优美的颂歌声。|近义词：carol singing, Christmas singing|反义词:无
