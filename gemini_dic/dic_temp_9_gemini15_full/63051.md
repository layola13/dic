enrichment:/ɪnˈrɪtʃmənt/| n. | 丰富；充实；增值 | The act of improving or enhancing something, making it better or more valuable.  It can refer to adding value, improving quality, or making something more interesting or stimulating. | 例句：The enrichment program for the students included field trips and workshops. 学生的充实计划包括实地考察和讲习班。

例句：The soil enrichment improved the crop yield significantly. 土壤改良显著提高了作物产量。


近义词：improvement, enhancement, betterment, augmentation

反义词：depletion, impoverishment, degradation


词性:名词
