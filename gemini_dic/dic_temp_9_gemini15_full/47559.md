deb:/deb/| n.| 争论，辩论 | A discussion or argument. |Example: The deb over the new policy lasted for hours.  这场关于新政策的辩论持续了几个小时。|近义词：argument, discussion, dispute |反义词：agreement, accord


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team debates the issue every week. = 这个团队每周都讨论这个问题。
They frequently debate politics. = 他们经常讨论政治。


一般过去时:
We debated the merits of the proposal. = 我们讨论了这项提案的优缺点。
The two candidates debated fiercely. = 两位候选人激烈地辩论。


现在完成时:
They have debated this topic many times. = 他们已经多次讨论过这个话题。
We have debated the pros and cons of the plan. = 我们已经讨论了这个计划的利弊。


过去完成时:
They had debated the matter before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就讨论过这件事。
She had debated the implications of her decision. = 她已经仔细考虑过她决定的影响。


将来时:
We will debate the motion tomorrow. = 我们明天将对这项动议进行辩论。
They will debate the issue next week. = 他们下周将讨论这个问题。


虚拟语气:
If we debated the issue more openly, we might find a solution. = 如果我们更公开地讨论这个问题，我们可能会找到解决办法。
I wish we had debated the risks before proceeding. = 我希望我们在继续之前已经讨论过风险。


被动语态:
The issue was debated extensively. = 这个问题被广泛讨论。
The proposal is being debated by the committee. = 这项提案正在由委员会讨论。


疑问句:
Will they debate the subject tonight? = 他们今晚会讨论这个话题吗？
Have they debated this before? = 他们以前讨论过这个吗？


否定句:
They did not debate the issue thoroughly. = 他们没有彻底讨论这个问题。
We do not usually debate such sensitive topics. = 我们通常不讨论如此敏感的话题。


PHRASE->
deb over sth =关于某事的辩论
deb with sb =与某人辩论
engage in deb =参与辩论
heated deb =激烈的辩论
public deb =公开辩论
