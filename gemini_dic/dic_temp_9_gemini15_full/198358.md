showdowns:/ˈʃoʊdaʊnz/| n. |摊牌；决战；最终对决 |A showdown is a final confrontation or decisive battle, often implying a tense and potentially violent resolution. It can refer to a situation where opposing parties finally reveal their intentions or strengths.  |例句：The two rivals finally had a showdown at the championship game.  两个对手最终在冠军赛中进行了决战。

The long-standing dispute between the two companies culminated in a showdown in court.  两家公司之间长期存在的纠纷最终在法庭上摊牌。

近义词：confrontation, clash, conflict, battle, crisis |反义词：agreement, compromise, reconciliation | 名词
