impersonates:/ɪmˈpɜːrsəneɪts/| vt.|模仿；扮演；假冒|To represent oneself as another person; to act the part of someone else.  It can also refer to mimicking someone's mannerisms or voice.|例句：He impersonates famous actors on stage. 他在舞台上模仿著名演员。|例句：The con artist impersonated a police officer to gain access to the building.  这个骗子假扮警察以进入大楼。|近义词：mimic, imitate, enact, portray|反义词：be oneself, be authentic|动词
