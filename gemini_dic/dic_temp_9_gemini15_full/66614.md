exile:/ˈɛɡzaɪl/| n., v.|流放；放逐；被流放者；流亡者|The state of being sent away from one's country or home;  banishment;  a person who has been forced to leave their country or home.|例句：He was sentenced to exile for his political activities. 他因政治活动被判处流放。

例句：Many intellectuals went into exile during the war. 许多知识分子在战争期间流亡。


近义词：banishment, deportation, expulsion, ostracism|反义词：return, repatriation, welcome


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exiles his enemies. 他放逐他的敌人。
The government exiles dissidents. 政府流放持不同政见者。

一般过去时:
They exiled him to a remote island. 他们把他流放到一个偏远的岛屿。
The king exiled his brother. 国王流放了他的兄弟。

现在完成时:
They have exiled him for his crimes. 他们因他的罪行而将他流放。
The dictator has exiled thousands of people. 独裁者已经流放了成千上万的人。

过去完成时:
He had been exiled for many years before his pardon. 在获得赦免之前，他已经流放多年了。
The family had already been exiled when the revolution broke out.  革命爆发时，这个家庭已经被流放了。


将来时:
They will exile him if he continues his subversive activities. 如果他继续从事颠覆活动，他们将会流放他。
The court will likely exile the convicted criminal. 法院很可能会将该罪犯流放。

虚拟语气:
If he were to speak out again, he would be exiled. 如果他再次公开讲话，他将被流放。
I wish I hadn't been exiled from my homeland. 我真希望我没有被流放出我的祖国。

被动语态:
He was exiled for treason. 他因叛国罪被流放。
Many were exiled during the political upheaval. 在政治动荡期间，许多人被流放。

疑问句:
Was he exiled for his beliefs? 他是因为他的信仰而被流放的吗？
Will she be exiled from the country? 她会被驱逐出境吗？

否定句:
They did not exile him, despite his opposition to the regime. 尽管他反对政权，他们也没有流放他。
He was not exiled; he left voluntarily. 他没有被流放；他是自愿离开的。


PHRASE->
in exile: 流亡中
go into exile: 流亡
a life in exile: 流亡生活
forced exile: 被迫流放
self-imposed exile: 自我放逐

词源:
源自古法语 exiler，最终源自拉丁语 exilium，意思是"离开家园"。  ex-  表示“出”，-ilium 与“地方”有关。
