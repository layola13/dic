horsetails:/ˈhɔːrsˌteɪlz/| n.|木贼属植物；马尾；(头发)马尾辫|A type of plant, typically growing in damp places, with hollow, jointed stems and whorls of scale-like leaves.  The name refers to the resemblance of the plant's stem to a horse's tail.|例句：The horsetails by the stream were swaying gently in the breeze. 溪边的小木贼在微风中轻轻摇曳。

例句：She wore her hair in a neat horsetail. 她把头发梳成一个整齐的马尾辫。|近义词：Equisetum (scientific name)|反义词: None.
