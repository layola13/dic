exemplifies:/ɪɡˈzɛmplɪfaɪz/| vt.|例证；是…的典范|To serve as a typical example of; illustrate.|This painting exemplifies the artist's early style. 这幅画是艺术家早期风格的典范。|illustrates, demonstrates, shows, epitomizes|contradicts, refutes, belies|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This action exemplifies the company's commitment to sustainability.  这个行动例证了公司对可持续发展的承诺。
His kindness exemplifies the spirit of giving. 他的善良是乐于助人的典范。


一般过去时:
Her work exemplified the principles of modern art. 她的作品是现代艺术原则的典范。
The case exemplified the dangers of reckless driving. 这个案例例证了鲁莽驾驶的危险性。


现在完成时:
His career has exemplified dedication and hard work. 他的职业生涯体现了奉献和努力。
This project has exemplified the power of collaboration.  这个项目充分体现了合作的力量。


过去完成时:
The previous administration's policies had exemplified a lack of foresight.  前任政府的政策体现了缺乏远见。
The earlier experiments had exemplified the challenges of the project. 早期的实验已经例证了该项目的挑战。


将来时:
This new technology will exemplify the future of communication. 这项新技术将成为未来通讯的典范。
His upcoming book will exemplify his unique writing style. 他即将出版的书将体现他独特的写作风格。


虚拟语气:
If his actions exemplified true leadership, we would all follow him. 如果他的行为是真正领导力的典范，我们都会追随他。
I wish his work exemplified a higher standard of excellence. 我希望他的作品能达到更高的优秀标准。


被动语态:
The company's success is exemplified by its innovative products. 公司的成功体现在其创新的产品上。
The principles of democracy are exemplified by the country's fair elections. 民主原则体现在该国的公平选举中。


疑问句:
Does this example exemplify the concept clearly? 这个例子是否清晰地例证了这个概念？
Which of these paintings best exemplifies Impressionism? 这些画作中哪一幅最能体现印象派风格？


否定句:
His behavior did not exemplify the values of the organization. 他的行为并没有体现该组织的价值观。
The film does not exemplify the director's best work. 这部电影并非导演的最佳作品。


PHRASE->
exemplify something by something 用……来例证……
exemplify a point 举例说明
exemplify the importance of…  例证……的重要性
exemplify a principle 例证一个原则
exemplify a trend  例证一种趋势
