re-fire:/ˌriːˈfaɪər/| vt.|重新点燃；解雇后重新雇用|To ignite or set on fire again; to dismiss someone from a job and then rehire them. |例句：The firefighters had to re-fire the boiler after the power outage. 消防员必须在停电后重新点燃锅炉。|例句：After being unfairly dismissed, she was eventually re-fired by the company. 在被不公平解雇后，她最终被公司重新雇用。|近义词：reignite, re-employ|反义词：extinguish, dismiss permanently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-fire the furnace every morning. = 他们每天早上都重新点燃熔炉。
The company re-fires its best employees after a short break. = 公司在短暂休息后会重新雇用其最好的员工。


一般过去时:
The chef re-fired the oven after cleaning it. = 厨师清洁烤箱后重新点燃了它。
He was re-fired after a successful appeal. = 在成功上诉后，他被重新雇用。
