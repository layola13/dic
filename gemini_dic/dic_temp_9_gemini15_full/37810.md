compendium:/kəmˈpendiəm/| n. |简编，摘要，概略|A compendium is a concise but relatively complete summary of a larger work or field of study.  It typically gathers together information from multiple sources and presents it in a compact form.  |例句：This book is a compendium of information on ancient civilizations. 这本书是关于古代文明信息的简编。

例句：The professor assigned us to write a compendium of our research findings. 教授让我们撰写一份研究成果的摘要。


近义词：summary, abstract, digest, synopsis, epitome

反义词：expansion, elaboration, detail
