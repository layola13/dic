knickknacks:/ˈnɪkˌnæks/| n. |小摆设；小玩意儿 |Small, usually decorative objects; trinkets.  Knickknacks are typically small items that are kept for their aesthetic value rather than their practical use. They often add a personal touch to a space. |例句：Her shelves were filled with knickknacks she'd collected over the years.  她的架子上摆满了她多年来收集的小玩意儿。

例句：The antique shop was overflowing with fascinating knickknacks. 这家古董店里摆满了引人入胜的小摆设。

近义词：trinkets, gadgets, gewgaws, baubles

反义词：essentials, necessities

词性: 名词
