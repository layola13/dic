restitute:/ˈrɛstɪtuːt/| vt.|恢复；归还；赔偿|To restore something to its former state or position; to return something to its rightful owner; to compensate for a loss or injury.|例句：The government promised to restitute the stolen artifacts to the museum.  政府承诺将被盗文物归还博物馆。|例句：The company was ordered to restitute the customer for the damaged goods. 公司被命令赔偿顾客损坏的货物。|近义词：restore, repay, compensate, reimburse|反义词：destroy, damage, confiscate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government restitutes stolen property to its rightful owners. = 政府将失窃的财产归还给其合法所有者。
He restitutes the library books every week. = 他每周都归还图书馆的书籍。


一般过去时:
They restituted the damaged painting to its original glory. = 他们将受损的画作修复到原来的辉煌状态。
The court ordered the company to restitute the victim's losses. = 法院命令公司赔偿受害者的损失。


现在完成时:
The museum has restituted several stolen artifacts to their countries of origin. = 博物馆已将几件被盗文物归还给其原产国。
She has restituted the money she borrowed from her friend. = 她已经归还了从朋友那里借的钱。


过去完成时:
They had restituted the land to its original owners before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已将土地归还给原业主。
The company had restituted the customer before the lawsuit was filed. = 在提起诉讼之前，公司已经对顾客进行了赔偿。


将来时:
The insurance company will restitute the full value of the damaged car. = 保险公司将赔偿受损汽车的全部价值。
He will restitute the borrowed tools tomorrow. = 他明天将归还借来的工具。


虚拟语气:
If the thief had been caught, he would have been forced to restitute the stolen goods. = 如果小偷被抓住了，他就会被迫归还赃物。
I wish they would restitute the money they owe me. = 我希望他们能还我欠我的钱。


被动语态:
The stolen painting was restituted to the gallery. = 被盗的画作被归还给了画廊。
The damaged property will be restituted by the insurance company. = 受损的财产将由保险公司赔偿。


疑问句:
Will the company restitute the customer's losses? = 公司会赔偿顾客的损失吗？
Has the government restituted all the stolen artifacts? = 政府是否已经归还所有被盗文物？


否定句:
The company did not restitute the customer's money. = 公司没有归还顾客的钱。
He refused to restitute the damaged equipment. = 他拒绝赔偿损坏的设备。


PHRASE->
restitute to = 归还给…
restitute for = 赔偿…
restitute oneself = 恢复元气
restitute the status quo = 恢复现状
restitution of property = 财产赔偿
