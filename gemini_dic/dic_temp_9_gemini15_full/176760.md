readdresses:/ˌriːˈædrɛsɪz/| v.|重新处理；再次提出；再谈；重新解决|To address something again, often after a previous attempt has failed or been unsatisfactory.  It implies a renewed effort or a different approach.|例句：The committee will readdress the issue of funding next week.  委员会下周将重新讨论资金问题。
例句：The company readdressed the problem by hiring a new consultant. 公司通过聘请一位新的顾问来重新解决这个问题。|近义词：reconsider, review, revisit|反义词：ignore, neglect|动词
