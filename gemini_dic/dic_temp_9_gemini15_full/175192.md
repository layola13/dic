ragouting:/ræɡˈaʊtɪŋ/| n.|炖菜，杂烩|A ragout is a type of stew, typically made with meat and vegetables, often in a rich sauce.  It's characterized by its slow cooking process, which allows the flavors to meld together.|Example:  The chef prepared a delicious beef ragout with mushrooms and red wine.  厨师准备了一道美味的蘑菇红酒牛肉炖菜。|近义词：stew, casserole, hotpot|反义词：salad (generally),  raw ingredients|名词
