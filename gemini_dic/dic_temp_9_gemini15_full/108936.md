leeks:/liːks/| n.|韭葱，韭菜|A leek is a vegetable that is a member of the onion family, having long, flat, green leaves and a white bulb at the base. It has a milder flavor than onions and is often used in soups, stews, and other dishes.|例句：I added some leeks to the soup for extra flavor. 我在汤里加了一些韭葱以增加风味。  The farmer grows leeks and other vegetables. 这位农民种植韭葱和其他蔬菜。|近义词：scallions, onions, shallots|反义词:None.
