invigorates:/ɪnˈvɪɡəreɪts/| vt.|使精力充沛；使振奋；使活跃|To give strength, energy, or vitality to someone or something; to stimulate or refresh.|The morning sun invigorated me after a long night's sleep.  早晨的阳光让我在长时间的睡眠后精力充沛。|The news invigorated the team and spurred them on to victory. 这个消息让球队士气大振，激励他们取得胜利。|enliven, energize, stimulate|debilitate, weaken, depress|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The fresh air invigorates me. = 新鲜的空气使我精力充沛。
Regular exercise invigorates the body. = 定期锻炼使身体充满活力。

一般过去时:
The coffee invigorated him after a long drive. = 长途驾驶后，咖啡使他精神焕发。
The good news invigorated her spirits. = 好消息使她精神振奋。

现在完成时:
The vacation has invigorated me completely. = 假期让我完全恢复了活力。
This new strategy has invigorated the company's performance. = 这项新战略使公司的业绩焕发生机。

过去完成时:
A good night's sleep had invigorated him for the challenging day ahead. = 良好的睡眠让他为即将到来的挑战性的一天注入了活力。
The team had been invigorated by their recent success. = 团队最近的成功使他们士气大振。


将来时:
A long walk will invigorate you. = 一次长途步行会让你精神焕发。
The new marketing campaign will invigorate sales. = 新的营销活动将刺激销售。


虚拟语气:
If I had a good night's sleep, I would feel invigorated. = 如果我睡个好觉，我会感到精力充沛。
I wish this medicine would invigorate me. = 我希望这种药能让我精神焕发。


被动语态:
He was invigorated by the success of his project. = 他的项目成功让他精力充沛。
The team was invigorated by the coach's inspiring speech. = 球员们被教练的鼓舞人心的演讲激励了。


疑问句:
Does fresh air invigorate you? = 新鲜的空气能让你精力充沛吗？
Will this new plan invigorate our sales? = 这个新计划会振兴我们的销售吗？


否定句:
The heat didn't invigorate him; it made him tired. = 炎热的天气并没有让他精力充沛；反而让他感到疲倦。
The boring meeting didn't invigorate the participants. = 枯燥的会议没有让与会者感到振奋。
