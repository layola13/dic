essence:/ˈɛsəns/ | n. |本质，精髓，精华 |The essence of his argument was that we should act now.  他的论点核心是我们应该立即行动。|近义词：core, quintessence, substance |反义词：superficiality, exterior


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The essence of this plan is efficiency. = 这项计划的精髓是效率。
What is the essence of your argument? = 你论点的核心是什么？


一般过去时:
The essence of her presentation was clear. = 她演讲的要点很清楚。
What was the essence of the meeting? = 会议的重点是什么？


现在完成时:
The essence of their work has been revealed. = 他们工作的精髓已经被揭示。
What has been the essence of your studies? = 你学习的重点是什么？


过去完成时:
The essence of the matter had been misunderstood. = 事情的本质已经被误解了。
What had been the essence of their previous discussions? = 他们之前的讨论重点是什么？


将来时:
The essence of the problem will be clear soon. = 问题的本质很快就会清楚。
What will be the essence of the next chapter? = 下一章的重点是什么？


虚拟语气:
If I knew the essence of the problem, I could solve it. = 如果我知道问题的本质，我就能解决它。
I wish I knew the essence of his theory. = 我真希望我知道他理论的精髓。


被动语态:
The essence of the message was misunderstood. = 信息的本质被误解了。
The essence of the contract is being reviewed. = 合同的精髓正在被审查。


疑问句:
What is the essence of happiness? = 幸福的本质是什么？
What's the essence of this painting? = 这幅画的精髓是什么？


否定句:
The essence of his argument wasn't clear. = 他的论点的精髓并不清晰。
She didn't grasp the essence of the situation. = 她没有抓住情况的本质。


PHRASE->
in essence = 本质上
the essence of = ...的精髓
of the essence = 至关重要的
capture the essence = 抓住精髓
lose the essence = 失去精髓


词源:
源自古法语 *essence,  最终源自拉丁语 *essentia,  意为“being, existence”。
