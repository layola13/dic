memetic:/mɪˈmɛtɪk/| adj.| 模因的， 模仿的 | Relating to or denoting memes or their spread.  The term refers to the spread of ideas, behaviors, or styles through a population, similar to the way genes are passed down biologically.  It emphasizes the transmission and replication of cultural information.|例句：The rapid memetic spread of the video across social media platforms was remarkable.  这个视频在社交媒体平台上的迅速传播令人瞩目。|近义词：viral, contagious, infectious |反义词：unique, original, isolated
