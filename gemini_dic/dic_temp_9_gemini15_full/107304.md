/ˌlɑːməˈsɛriːz/ | n. |喇嘛庙；寺院 | A lamasery is a monastery inhabited by lamas (Tibetan Buddhist monks).  It is typically a complex of buildings dedicated to religious practices, study, and community life. Lamaseries often house libraries containing sacred texts, shrines, and meditation halls.  They play a vital role in preserving Tibetan Buddhist culture and tradition. | Example: The ancient lamasery nestled high in the Himalayas was a breathtaking sight.  古老的喇嘛庙依偎在喜马拉雅山的高处，景色令人叹为观止。|近义词：monastery, temple, abbey | 反义词：None  |名词
