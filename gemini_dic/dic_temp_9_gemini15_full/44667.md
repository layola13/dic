crucifer:/ˈkruːsɪfər/| n. |十字架持有者；十字花科植物|A crucifer is a person who carries or holds a crucifix, or it can refer to any plant belonging to the family Cruciferae (now generally known as Brassicaceae), characterized by four-petaled flowers arranged in a cross-like pattern.  The term often refers to plants like cabbage, broccoli, and cauliflower.|Example:  Many gardeners grow cruciferous vegetables in their gardens.  许多园丁在自家菜园里种植十字花科蔬菜。  The ancient symbol of the crucifer was a powerful religious image. 古代十字架持有者的象征是一个强大的宗教形象。|近义词：brassica (for the plant)|反义词：N/A
