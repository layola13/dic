siltage:/ˈsɪltɪdʒ/| n. |淤泥；泥沙沉积物 |Siltage refers to the accumulation of silt, a fine-grained sediment, typically deposited by rivers, streams, or other bodies of water.  It often forms layers of mud or clay-like material at the bottom of water bodies or on the banks. |The siltage in the harbor was causing navigation problems.  港口淤积的泥沙造成了航行困难。|sediment, deposit, sludge |clean water, clear water |名词
