self-amusement: /ˌsɛlf əˈmjuːzmənt/ | n. | 自娱自乐 | The act of amusing oneself; entertainment or diversion provided for oneself.  |  例句：He found self-amusement in solving complex puzzles. 他从解决复杂的难题中获得乐趣。

例句：Her self-amusement consisted of reading and gardening. 她的自娱自乐包括阅读和园艺。


近义词：self-entertainment, diversion, recreation, pastime


反义词：boredom, tedium


词源：self- (self) + amusement (amusement)  "Self" is from Old English *seolf, "self, same," from Proto-Germanic *selbaz, from Proto-Indo-European *selbʰ- "self." "Amusement" comes from amuse (verb), which originates from Old French amuser "to entertain, divert, amuse," from a- "to" + user "to use."
