nonrevolutionaries:/ˌnɒnˌrevəˈluːʃənəriːz/| n. |非革命者，反革命分子|People who do not participate in or support a revolution.  They may be passive observers, active opponents, or simply uninterested in revolutionary activities.  |例句：The nonrevolutionaries watched from the sidelines as the city erupted in protest.  非革命者在城市爆发抗议时在旁观望。

The government suppressed the voices of the nonrevolutionaries. 政府压制了非革命者的声音。

|近义词：counterrevolutionaries, reactionaries, conservatives |反义词：revolutionaries, radicals, rebels |名词
