shrivelled:/ˈʃrɪvəld/| adj.| 枯萎的；干瘪的 | Dried up and wrinkled, especially due to loss of moisture.  |例句：The leaves were shrivelled and brown from the heat.  叶子因高温而枯萎发黄。

The grapes had shrivelled on the vine. 葡萄藤上的葡萄都干瘪了。|近义词：withered, wrinkled, dried-up |反义词：plump, full, turgid |词性:形容词

词源:
The word "shrivel" comes from the Middle English word "shryvelen," which is possibly related to the Old Norse word "skrifla," meaning "to wrinkle."  The suffix "-ed" indicates the past participle form, creating the adjective "shrivelled."
