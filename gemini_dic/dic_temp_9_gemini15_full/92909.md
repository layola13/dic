ideated:/aɪˈdiːeɪtɪd/| v. |构思出的；想出的；设想出的 |Past participle of "ideate," meaning to form or conceive an idea.  It describes something that has been created or generated through the process of thinking and imagining. |The team ideated a new marketing campaign that targeted a younger demographic. = 团队构思了一个针对年轻群体的新的营销活动。

The project was built on ideas that had been carefully ideated by the project leader. = 这个项目建立在项目负责人精心构思的想法之上。|conceived, envisioned, imagined, formulated |unrealized, unplanned |动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They ideate new products every year. = 他们每年都构思新产品。
We ideate solutions to complex problems. = 我们构思解决复杂问题的方案。


一般过去时:
The architect ideated a stunning design for the new building. = 建筑师构思了一个令人惊叹的新建筑设计。
They ideated a plan to increase sales. = 他们构思了一个增加销售额的计划。


现在完成时:
The company has ideated several innovative strategies. = 公司已经构思了几种创新策略。
We have ideated a solution and are ready to implement it. = 我们已经构思出一个解决方案，并准备实施它。


过去完成时:
They had ideated a marketing plan before the meeting. = 他们会议之前已经构思了一个营销计划。
She had ideated a novel approach to the problem. = 她之前已经构思了一种新颖的解决问题的方法。


将来时:
We will ideate new features for the software next month. = 我们下个月将为软件构思新的功能。
They will ideate a response to the customer's complaint. = 他们将构思对客户投诉的回应。


虚拟语气:
If we had ideated a better plan, the project would have been more successful. = 如果我们当时构思了一个更好的计划，项目就会更成功。
I wish I had ideated a more creative solution. = 我希望我当时构思了一个更有创意的解决方案。


被动语态:
A new marketing strategy was ideated by the team. = 一个新的营销策略是由团队构思的。
The design was carefully ideated and refined. = 该设计经过精心构思和改进。


疑问句:

What ideas were ideated during the brainstorming session? = 在头脑风暴会议中构思出了哪些想法？


否定句:
They didn't ideate a comprehensive plan. = 他们没有构思出一个全面的计划。
We haven't ideated a suitable alternative yet. = 我们还没有构思出一个合适的替代方案。


PHRASE->

词源:
The verb "ideate" is derived from the noun "idea," ultimately tracing back to the Greek word "ἰδέα" (idea), meaning "form," "shape," or "appearance."  The "-ate" suffix transforms the noun into a verb, indicating the action of forming or creating an idea.
