quasi-scientifically:/ˈkwɑːzaɪˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪkli/| adv.|似是而非地，貌似科学地 | appearing to be scientific but not truly so; based on or resembling scientific method but lacking rigor or validity. |例句：The claims were presented quasi-scientifically, using charts and graphs but lacking substantial evidence.  这些说法貌似科学，使用了图表，但却缺乏实质证据。
The theory, while interesting, was developed quasi-scientifically and lacks empirical support.  这个理论虽然有趣，但其发展方式似是而非，缺乏经验证据。|近义词：pseudoscientifically, superficially scientifically, speciously scientifically |反义词：scientifically, rigorously, accurately
