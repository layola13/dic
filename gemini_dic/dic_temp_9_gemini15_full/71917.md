flexors:/ˈflɛksərz/| n.|屈肌，弯曲肌|Muscles that bend a joint.  They are the opposite of extensors, which straighten a joint.|例句：The biceps are flexors that bend the elbow.肱二头肌是弯曲肘部的屈肌。

例句：The hamstrings are powerful flexors in the knee joint. 腘绳肌是膝关节强有力的屈肌。


近义词：benders, flexor muscles|反义词：extensors
