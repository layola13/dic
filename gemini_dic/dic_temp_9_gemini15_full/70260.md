fictitiously:/fɪkˈtɪʃəsli/| adj. |虚构地；假装地 |In a fictitious manner; not genuinely or truthfully.  It implies a deliberate creation of something unreal or false. |例句：The characters in the novel are fictitiously portrayed. 小说中的人物是虚构的。

例句：He fictitiously claimed to be a doctor. 他假装是医生。


近义词：falsely, artificially, imaginarily, pretendly

反义词：truthfully, genuinely, really, authentically

词性：形容词
