sensorium:/senˈsɔːriəm/| n. |感觉器官；感觉中枢；感觉世界 |The sensorium is the entire system of sensory organs in a living being; or the part of the brain that receives and interprets sensory information. It can also refer to the subjective experience of sensory input.  |例句：The artist's sensorium was highly developed, allowing him to perceive subtle nuances of color and texture.  这位艺术家的感觉器官高度发达，使他能够感知色彩和纹理的细微差别。|例句：The patient's damaged sensorium impaired his ability to understand his surroundings. 病人的受损感觉中枢影响了他理解周围环境的能力。|近义词：sensory system, perceptive faculty |反义词：insensibility, numbness
