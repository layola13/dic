heterochromia:/ˌhɛtərəˈkroʊmiə/| n. | 异色症，虹膜异色症 | A condition in which a person has two differently colored eyes, or two differently colored sections within the same iris.  This difference in coloration is due to variations in the amount or distribution of melanin. | Example:  The cat's striking heterochromia made it instantly recognizable.  这只猫醒目的异色症使它立刻被人认出来。|近义词：iris heterochromia, ocular heterochromia |反义词：homochromia
