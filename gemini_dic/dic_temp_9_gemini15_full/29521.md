catatonic: /ˌkætəˈtɒnɪk/ | adj. | 紧张症的；紧张症状态的 | Relating to or affected by catatonia, a state of immobility and unresponsiveness sometimes associated with schizophrenia or other mental illnesses.  It is characterized by a marked reduction in reactivity to external stimuli. |  The patient exhibited catatonic symptoms, including mutism and rigidity.  这位病人表现出紧张症症状，包括缄默和僵硬。 |  Stuporous, rigid, unresponsive |  Active, responsive
