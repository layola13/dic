ironically:/aɪˈrɒnɪkli/| adv.|具有讽刺意味地；令人啼笑皆非地|In a way that is contrary to what is expected, and often amusing as a result.  It often implies a contrast between expectation and reality.|例句：Ironically, the fire station burned down.  讽刺的是，消防站烧毁了。

例句：Ironically, his attempts to help only made things worse.  具有讽刺意味的是，他试图帮忙反而让事情变得更糟了。


近义词：paradoxically, contradictorily, unexpectedly, surprisingly|反义词：expectedly, predictably


PHRASE->
ironically enough = 说来讽刺的是
ironically speaking = 说句讽刺的话
ironically enough, ... = 说来讽刺的是...
It's ironic that... =  ...真是讽刺
ironically, however,... =  然而，具有讽刺意味的是...
