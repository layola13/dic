engineed:/ˌɛnˈdʒɪnɪrd/ | adj. | 工程的；设计的；精密的 | Engineered products are designed and manufactured with precision.  工程产品的设计和制造都非常精密。|近义词：designed, manufactured, constructed | 反义词：natural, unplanned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engineers are designing a new engine. =工程师们正在设计一个新发动机。
This system is engineered for efficiency. =这个系统的设计是为了提高效率。

一般过去时:
They engineered a solution to the problem. =他们设计出了一个解决问题的方案。
The bridge was engineered to withstand earthquakes. =这座桥的设计能够承受地震。

现在完成时:
They have engineered many innovative products. =他们已经设计出许多创新产品。
The company has engineered a significant turnaround. =该公司已经实现了显著的扭转。

过去完成时:
The plans had been engineered before the construction began. =在施工开始之前，计划就已经设计好了。
The software had been engineered to be user-friendly. =该软件的设计目标是易于用户使用。

将来时:
They will engineer a new type of aircraft. =他们将设计一种新型飞机。
The project will be engineered to meet strict environmental standards. =该项目的设计将符合严格的环境标准。

虚拟语气:
If they had engineered the system differently, it would have worked better. =如果他们当初的设计方案有所不同，系统就会运行得更好。
I wish they had engineered a more reliable system. =我希望他们设计一个更可靠的系统。

被动语态:
The software was engineered by a team of experts. =该软件是由一个专家团队设计的。
The building is being engineered to meet modern safety codes. =该建筑正在按照现代安全规范进行设计。

疑问句:
Was the system engineered correctly? =这个系统设计正确了吗？
How was the bridge engineered to withstand such stress? =这座桥是如何设计来承受如此大的压力的？

否定句:
The product was not engineered for mass production. =该产品并非为大规模生产而设计。
The system wasn't engineered properly. =系统没有正确设计。
