conquistador:/ˌkɒŋkɪsˈtɑːdɔːr/|/ˌkɑːŋkɪsˈteɪdɔːr/| n. |征服者；西班牙征服者 |A conquistador was a Spanish soldier and explorer who conquered much of Central and South America in the 16th century.  Conquistadors were driven by a desire for gold, glory, and the spread of Christianity. They often engaged in brutal tactics against the indigenous populations. |The conquistadors' ruthless methods led to the decimation of many native American civilizations.  西班牙征服者残酷的手段导致许多美洲原住民文明的灭亡。|explorer, conqueror |pacifist, liberator |名词
