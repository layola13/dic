periastral: /ˌpɛriˈæstrəl/ | adj. | 近拱点的；围绕拱点的 | Relating to or located near the periastron (the point in the orbit of a celestial body around another at which it is nearest to the latter).  | The periastral distance of the comet was measured.  彗星的近拱点距离被测量了。 |  near-periastron,  periapsis-related |  (no direct antonym,  but "apoastral" could be considered an antonym in the context of orbital points) |  形容词

词源:
peri- (around, near) + astre (star) + -al (relating to)  源于希腊语，peri-意为“周围，附近”，astre意为“恒星”，-al为形容词后缀。  虽然词根包含“星”，但其应用已扩展到任何围绕中心天体的轨道运动。
