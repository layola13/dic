overdistorts:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪstɔːrts/| v.|过度扭曲，歪曲；夸大|To distort something beyond its normal or intended shape, meaning, or proportion.  It implies an exaggeration or misrepresentation that goes beyond simple distortion.|The media overdistorted the politician's statement, making him seem far more radical than he actually was.  媒体过度扭曲了这位政治家的声明，使他看起来比实际情况激进得多。|distort, warp, exaggerate, misrepresent|represent accurately, clarify, simplify|动词
