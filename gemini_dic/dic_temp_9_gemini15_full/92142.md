hypersensitivity:/ˌhaɪpərˌsensɪˈtɪvɪti/ | n. |过敏性；高度敏感；过敏反应 |  An excessive reaction to a stimulus.  It can refer to physical reactions like allergies or psychological reactions like extreme emotional responses.  | Example:  Her hypersensitivity to criticism made it difficult for her to receive constructive feedback.  她的批评敏感性让她很难接受建设性反馈。


Example: He suffers from hypersensitivity to pollen. 他对花粉过敏。


近义词：allergy, sensitivity, oversensitivity, reactivity


反义词：hyposensitivity, insensitivity, tolerance
