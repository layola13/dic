overpump:/ˌoʊvərˈpʌmp/| vt.|过度抽取，抽取过多|To pump (something) excessively or beyond a safe or recommended limit.  This often refers to extracting resources like groundwater or oil faster than they can replenish.|例句：Overpumping the aquifer has led to land subsidence. 过度抽取地下水导致了地表下沉。

例句：The oil company was fined for overpumping its wells.  这家石油公司因过度抽油而被罚款。


近义词：overextract, deplete, exhaust


反义词：conserve, replenish, preserve


词性:动词
