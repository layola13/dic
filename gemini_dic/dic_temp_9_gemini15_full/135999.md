nonrational: /ˌnɒnˈræʃənəl/ | adj. | 非理性的，不合理的 | Not based on or guided by reason; illogical.  |  例句：His decision to quit his job without another lined up was a nonrational act.  他的决定在没有找到另一份工作的情况下辞职，是一个非理性的行为。

例句：Her fear of spiders, despite knowing they are harmless, is a nonrational response. 她害怕蜘蛛，尽管知道它们无害，这是一种非理性的反应。


近义词：irrational, illogical, unreasonable, absurd, senseless

反义词：rational, logical, reasonable, sensible


词源：non- (否定前缀) + rational (理性的)  源于拉丁语 *ratio ("reason," "calculation")
