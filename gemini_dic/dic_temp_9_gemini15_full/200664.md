sledgehammer:/ˈslɛdʒˌhæmər/| n.|大锤，重锤|A sledgehammer is a large hammer with a heavy head, used for breaking things or driving in stakes.  It's typically used for demolition work or heavy-duty tasks requiring significant force.|例句：He used a sledgehammer to break down the door. 他用大锤砸开了门。

例句：The construction workers used sledgehammers to break up the concrete. 建筑工人用大锤砸碎了混凝土。


近义词：heavy hammer, maul|反义词：small hammer, light hammer|名词
