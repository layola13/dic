felicities:/fəˈlɪsɪtiz/ | n. | 巧妙；精彩之处；幸福；优点 | Successes, advantages, or pleasing aspects.  The felicitous phrasing added to the overall impact of the speech.  | 例句：The felicities of her prose style are widely admired.  她的散文风格的巧妙之处广受赞赏。

The felicities of their marriage were evident to all. 他们婚姻的幸福对所有人来说都是显而易见的。


近义词：pleasures, delights, charms, graces, excellencies | 反义词：misfortunes, misfortunes, flaws, defects | 词性：名词


词源：源于拉丁语 *felix, felicis,  意为“幸运的，幸福的”。  通过法语 félicité 演变而来。
