/priːˈpɜːrtʃəsɪŋ/ | v. | 预购，提前购买 | The act of purchasing something before it is officially released or available.  It often involves placing an order or making a down payment in advance. |  例句：Prepurchasing concert tickets often guarantees you a seat. 预购演唱会门票通常可以保证你有一个座位。

例句：The limited edition sneakers were available for prepurchasing online a month before the official release. 限量版运动鞋在正式发售前一个月就可以在网上预购。


近义词：advance purchase, pre-ordering, reserving


反义词：postponing purchase, delaying purchase


词性：动词
