cliffs:/klɪfs/| n.|悬崖峭壁|High, steep rocks, often at the edge of the sea or a river.  Cliffs are formed by erosion and weathering.|例句：The waves crashed against the cliffs. 海浪拍打着悬崖峭壁。

例句：We hiked along the dramatic cliffs overlooking the ocean. 我们沿着俯瞰大海的壮丽悬崖远足。|近义词：precipice, crag, bluff|反义词：plain, lowland
