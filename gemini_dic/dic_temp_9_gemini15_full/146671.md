overhauling: /ˌoʊvərˈhɔːlɪŋ/ | v. | 大修；彻底检修；彻底改革 |  The act of thoroughly repairing or renovating something, often a machine or system. It implies a complete and comprehensive examination and repair, going beyond simple maintenance.  | 例句：The car needs an overhaul. 这辆车需要大修。 The company is undergoing a major overhaul of its operations. 这家公司正在对其运营进行大规模改革。 | 近义词：repair, renovate, revamp, rebuild, refit | 反义词：neglect, maintain (in a minimal way)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are overhauling the engine. = 他们正在大修发动机。
The government is overhauling the tax system. = 政府正在彻底改革税收制度。

一般过去时:
We overhauled the old machinery last year. = 我们去年大修了旧机器。
The mechanic overhauled the engine completely. = 技工彻底地检修了发动机。

现在完成时:
They have overhauled the entire production line. = 他们已经彻底检修了整条生产线。
The company has overhauled its marketing strategy. = 公司已经彻底改革了其营销策略。

过去完成时:
They had overhauled the system before the launch. = 他们在发布之前已经彻底检修了系统。
The team had overhauled the software before the deadline. = 团队在截止日期前已经对软件进行了大修。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

It would be better if they overhauled their approach. = 如果他们彻底改变方法会更好。

被动语态:
