outwrestling: /ˌaʊtˈrɛslɪŋ/ | v. |  摔跤战胜，技高一筹 | To defeat someone in wrestling; to surpass someone in skill or strength, especially in a competitive context. | 例句：He outwrestled his opponent with a surprising display of strength. 他以令人惊讶的力量战胜了他的对手。

The champion outwrestled all his challengers. 冠军战胜了所有挑战者。


近义词：defeat, overcome, surpass, outmaneuver | 反义词：lose to, be defeated by | 词性:动词

词源:  The word "outwrestling" is a combination of the prefix "out-" (meaning "to a greater extent than") and the verb "wrestling" (the act of engaging in the sport of wrestling).  Therefore, it directly signifies surpassing someone in wrestling.
