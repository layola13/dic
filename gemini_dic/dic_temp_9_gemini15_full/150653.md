pappoose:/pæˈpuːs/| n. |印第安婴儿；小孩|A baby, especially a Native American baby, carried on the back in a cradleboard.  The term is sometimes considered offensive due to its historical association with colonial depictions of Native Americans.|例句：The mother carried her pappoose on her back in a woven sling. 母亲用编织的吊带背着她的孩子。|例句： The old photograph showed a Native American woman carrying her pappoose. 老照片上显示一位美洲原住民妇女背着她的孩子。|近义词：baby, infant, child|反义词：adult
