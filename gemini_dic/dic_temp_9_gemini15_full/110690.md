lineality: /ˌlaɪniˈæləti/ | n. | 直系血统；线性；直线性 | The state or quality of being lineal; descent in a direct line from an ancestor.  It also refers to the property of being linear, characterized by straight lines or a one-dimensional arrangement. |例句：The family boasted a long lineality tracing back to the Norman Conquest.  这个家族自诺曼征服以来就拥有悠久的直系血统。

例句：The lineality of the design made it easy to reproduce.  这个设计的一维线性使其易于复制。


近义词：lineage, ancestry, heredity, linearity | 反义词：collaterality, nonlinearity
