sanctity:/ˈsæŋktɪti/| n. |神圣；圣洁；尊严；不可侵犯性 |The quality of being holy, sacred, or inviolable.  It often refers to something considered sacred or deserving of reverence, respect, or protection.  |例句：The sanctity of marriage is important to many people.  婚姻的神圣对许多人来说很重要。

例句：The sanctity of human life must be upheld. 人的生命神圣必须得到维护。


近义词：holiness, sacredness, inviolability, purity, sacredness


反义词：profane, desecration, violation, impurity


词性：名词
