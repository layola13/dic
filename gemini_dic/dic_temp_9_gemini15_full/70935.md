firecracker:/ˈfaɪəˌkrækər/| n. |鞭炮，爆竹 |A small explosive device that makes a loud noise when lit.  Firecrackers are often used in celebrations and festivals. |例句：The children lit the firecrackers to celebrate the New Year. 孩子们点燃鞭炮庆祝新年。|例句：The loud bangs of the firecrackers startled the dog. 鞭炮的巨响吓到了狗。|近义词：cracker, firework |反义词：None specifically.
