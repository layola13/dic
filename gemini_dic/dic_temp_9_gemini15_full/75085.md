friller:/ˈfrɪlə(r)/| n.|褶饰；装饰褶边|A friller is a decorative frill or ruffle, often used on clothing or other textiles.  It can also refer to a person or thing that adds frills or ornamentation.|例句：The dress had a pretty friller around the neckline. 这件裙子领口有一圈漂亮的褶边。 The baker added a friller to the top of the cake. 面包师在蛋糕顶部加了一圈褶边。|近义词：ruffle, flounce, trimming|反义词：plainness, simplicity|名词
