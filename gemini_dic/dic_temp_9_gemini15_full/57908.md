driftwood:/ˈdrɪftˌwʊd/ | n. |漂流木，浮木 | Wood that has been carried by water and deposited on a bank.  It is often weathered and bleached by the sun and sea. |例句：The beach was littered with driftwood after the storm.  暴风雨过后，海滩上散落着许多漂流木。

例句：We used pieces of driftwood to build a bonfire. 我们用一些漂流木生了一堆篝火。


近义词：flotsam, jetsam | 反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with freshly cut wood. | 词性：名词
