propheticality: /ˌprɒfɪˈtɛtɪkælɪti/ | /ˌprɑːfəˈtɛtɪkælɪti/ | n. | 预言性；预言能力 | The quality or state of being prophetical; the ability to prophesy.  It refers to the characteristics or attributes associated with prophecy, including the accuracy, insight, and divine inspiration often attributed to prophetic utterances. |


例句：

The propheticality of the ancient oracles is still debated by scholars. 古代神谕的预言性仍然被学者们争论不休。

The book explores the propheticality of various religious texts. 这本书探讨了各种宗教文本的预言性。


近义词：prescience, foreknowledge, divination

反义词：unpredictability, randomness


词性：名词
