slipskin:/ˈslɪpskɪn/| n.|蛇蜕皮；薄而易撕的皮肤|A slipskin is the shed skin of a snake or similar reptile.  It is also used to describe thin, easily torn skin.|例句：The snake left a perfect slipskin behind.  这条蛇留下了一张完整的蜕皮。|例句：His skin was so thin, almost like a slipskin. 他的皮肤很薄，几乎像蛇蜕的皮一样。|近义词：exuviae (plural), slough|反义词：thick skin, tough skin|名词
