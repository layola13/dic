portentous:/pɔːˈtɛntəs/| adj.|预兆的；不祥的；自命不凡的；庄严的；夸张的；|

英文解析：Giving the impression that something important or calamitous will happen; ominous.  Also, pompous and self-important.


例句：

The dark clouds gathering on the horizon were portentous.  = 天边聚集的乌云预示着不祥。

His portentous pronouncements bored everyone. = 他自以为是的声明让每个人都厌烦了。


近义词：ominous, ominous, foreboding, threatening, pretentious, pompous


反义词：auspicious, propitious, humble, modest


词性：形容词
