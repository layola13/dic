daisies:/ˈdeɪziːz/| n.|雏菊，（复数）|The word "daisies" refers to the plural of "daisy," a type of flowering plant known for its bright, usually white petals surrounding a yellow center.  Daisies are often associated with innocence and purity. |例句：The field was covered in a carpet of white daisies.  田野里铺满了白色的雏菊。

There were dozens of daisies blooming in the garden. 花园里盛开着几十朵雏菊。|近义词：day's eyes, wildflowers|反义词：无
