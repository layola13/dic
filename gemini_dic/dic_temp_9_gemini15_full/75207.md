frogbit:/ˈfrɒɡbɪt/ | n. |浮萍；青萍 | A small, free-floating aquatic plant that grows on the surface of still or slow-moving water.  It has small, round or oval leaves and tiny white flowers. | Example:  The pond was covered in a thick mat of frogbit.  池塘上覆盖着一层厚厚的浮萍。

The frogbit is a common weed in many ponds. 许多池塘里浮萍很常见。|近义词：duckweed, watermeal |反义词：None.
