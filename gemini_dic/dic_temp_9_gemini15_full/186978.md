risk:/rɪsk/| n. v.|风险；危险；冒险|The possibility of something bad happening.|例句：There is a risk of fire.  存在着火灾的危险。

例句：He risked his life to save the child. 他冒着生命危险救了那个孩子。|hazard, peril, danger|safety, security|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He risks his health by smoking. = 他吸烟，危害健康。
We assess the risks involved. = 我们评估所涉及的风险。


一般过去时:
She risked everything for her career. = 她为了事业冒险一切。
They risked their lives crossing the border. = 他们冒着生命危险越过边境。


现在完成时:
He has risked his reputation by lying. = 他撒谎，已经损害了自己的名誉。
We have assessed all the potential risks. = 我们已经评估了所有潜在的风险。


过去完成时:
They had risked their savings on the venture. = 他们曾为这项事业冒险投资所有的积蓄。
She had risked her job by speaking out. = 她因为直言不讳而冒着丢掉工作的风险。


将来时:
They will risk everything to win. = 他们将为胜利而冒险一切。
We will assess the risks before we proceed. = 我们将在继续之前评估风险。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn’t risk it. = 如果我是你，我不会冒险。
I wish I hadn’t risked my money on that investment. = 我真希望我没有把钱投资在那项投资上。


被动语态:
The project was considered too risky. = 该项目被认为风险太大。
His reputation was risked by his actions. = 他的行为使他的名誉受到威胁。


疑问句:
What are the risks involved? = 其中有哪些风险？
Do you want to risk it? = 你想冒险吗？


否定句:
We don't want to risk failure. = 我们不想冒险失败。
They didn't risk their lives unnecessarily. = 他们没有不必要地冒生命危险。



PHRASE->
at risk:  处于危险中
take a risk: 冒险
run a risk: 冒风险
high risk: 高风险
low risk: 低风险
