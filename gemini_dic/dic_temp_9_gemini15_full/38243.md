computerized:/ˌkəmˈpjʊtəraɪzd/ | adj. | 电脑化的；计算机化的 | Using or controlled by a computer.  This adjective describes something that has been automated or made more efficient through the use of computers.  |例句：The factory has become fully computerized.  工厂已经完全实现了电脑化。

The company uses a computerized system for managing its inventory.  这家公司使用计算机系统管理库存。


近义词：automated, automated, mechanized

反义词：manual, hand-operated


词源: The word "computerized" is derived from the noun "computer" and the suffix "-ized," which means "to make or become."  "Computer," in turn, has roots in the Latin word "computare," meaning "to compute" or "to reckon."  The suffix "-ized" is of Greek origin.
