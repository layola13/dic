monocotyle:/ˈmɒnəʊkaɪtɪl/| n. |单子叶植物|A monocotyledon; a flowering plant with one cotyledon (embryonic leaf) in the seed.|  Monocots are characterized by their parallel leaf venation, fibrous root systems, and flower parts in multiples of three.  |近义词：monocotyledon|反义词：dicotyledon
