exhedra:/ɛkˈsɛdrə/| n. |凹室；休息室；讲台 |A recess or alcove, especially one in a building used for conversation or seating.  It often refers to a semi-circular or rectangular alcove, usually found in ancient Greek and Roman architecture, often used as a place for conversation or relaxation, or sometimes as a niche for a statue. |The exhedra was a popular feature in Roman villas, providing a quiet space for private discussions.| 近义词：alcove, recess, niche | 反义词：center, middle
