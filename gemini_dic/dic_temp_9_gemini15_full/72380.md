flubbers:/ˈflʌbərz/| v.|笨手笨脚地做事，搞砸|to make a mistake, especially in a clumsy or nervous way; to botch or bungle something. |例句：He flubbed his lines during the play. 他在剧中把台词说错了。|例句：She flubbed the interview and didn't get the job. 她面试搞砸了，没有得到这份工作。|近义词：bungle, botch, mess up, screw up|反义词：succeed, excel, master
