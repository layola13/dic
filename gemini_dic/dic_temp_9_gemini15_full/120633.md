mis-evaluating: /ˌmɪsɪˈvæljueɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 错误评估，误判 | To judge the value or importance of something incorrectly. |  例句：The company mis-evaluated the market demand, leading to significant losses.  公司错误评估了市场需求，导致了巨大的损失。

The board mis-evaluated the risk associated with the new project.  董事会错误评估了新项目相关的风险。 |近义词：underestimate, misjudge, overestimate (depending on the direction of the error)| 反义词：accurately assess, correctly evaluate | 动词


USAGE->
