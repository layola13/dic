pooh-poohs:/ˈpuːpuːz/| v.|嗤之以鼻，不屑一顾 |To express contempt or disapproval of something.  |例句：He pooh-poohed my suggestion. 他对我的建议嗤之以鼻。|例句：She pooh-poohed the idea of going to the party. 她对参加聚会不屑一顾。|近义词：scoff at, dismiss, scorn, disdain |反义词：approve, endorse, praise
