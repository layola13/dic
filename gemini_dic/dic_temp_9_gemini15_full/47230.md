dazzle:/ˈdæzəl/ | vt. & n. |使眼花缭乱；使惊叹；耀眼的光芒 | to impress greatly; to shine intensely; to confuse or overwhelm with brilliance. |例句：The fireworks dazzled the crowd.  烟花使人群眼花缭乱。

The singer's performance dazzled the audience. 歌手的表演让观众惊叹不已。


近义词：amaze, impress, astound, blind,  shine

反义词：bore, disappoint, dull


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun dazzles me. 太阳晃得我睁不开眼。
The bright lights dazzle the driver.  明亮的灯光晃得司机睁不开眼。


一般过去时:
The magician's trick dazzled the children. 魔术师的戏法让孩子们眼花缭乱。
The performance dazzled the audience last night. 昨晚的演出让观众们惊叹不已。


现在完成时:
The city lights have dazzled me since I arrived. 自从我到达以来，城市的灯光一直让我眼花缭乱。
Her beauty has dazzled many. 她的美貌迷住了许多人。


过去完成时:
The bright sunlight had dazzled him before he reached the top of the mountain.  强烈的阳光在他到达山顶之前就已把他晃得睁不开眼了。
I had been dazzled by his charm before I realized his true nature.  在我意识到他的真实本性之前，我一直被他迷人的魅力所迷惑。


将来时:
The new technology will dazzle the consumers. 新技术将使消费者惊叹不已。
The fireworks display will dazzle the viewers tonight. 今晚的烟火表演将让观众们眼花缭乱。


虚拟语气:
If only I had the money, I would buy a car that would dazzle everyone. 如果我有钱的话，我会买一辆让每个人都惊叹不已的车。
I wish the sunset would dazzle me every day. 我希望每天日落都能让我感到惊艳。


被动语态:
The audience was dazzled by the spectacular show. 观众被精彩的演出迷住了。
He was dazzled by her beauty. 他被她的美貌迷住了。


疑问句:
Does the bright sunshine dazzle you? 刺眼的阳光是否让你眼花缭乱？
Will the new product dazzle the market?  新产品会让市场惊艳吗？


否定句:
The presentation didn't dazzle the investors.  演示并没有让投资者们印象深刻。
The movie didn't dazzle me as much as I expected.  这部电影并没有像我预期的那样让我惊艳。
