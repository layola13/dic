ketua:/ˈkɛtuə/| n.|主席，领导，负责人|The word "ketua" is of Malay origin, meaning "chairman," "leader," or "head."  It refers to the person who presides over a meeting or organization.  It is a common term used in many Southeast Asian countries.|例句：The ketua of the committee announced the decision.  委员会主席宣布了决定。

The ketua led the team to victory.  领导带领团队取得了胜利。|近义词：chairman, president, leader, head|反义词：follower, subordinate
