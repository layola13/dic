nonexistentially:/ˌnɒnˌɛɡzɪˈstɛnʃəli/ | adv. |非存在地，非本质地 |In a way that does not relate to existence or essence;  in a manner that ignores or downplays existential concerns.  |例句：He approached the problem nonexistentially, focusing solely on its practical aspects. 他以非存在的方式处理这个问题，只关注其实际方面。


例句：The argument was treated nonexistentially, neglecting the philosophical implications.  这场争论被非存在地对待，忽略了其哲学含义。


近义词：immaterially, superficially, pragmatically


反义词：existentially, essentially, fundamentally


词性: 副词
