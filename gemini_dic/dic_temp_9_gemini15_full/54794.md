dispensator:/dɪˈspɛnseɪtər/ | n. |分配器；发放者；自动售货机 |A device or container that dispenses something, such as a liquid, medicine, or tickets.  |例句：The hospital uses a new type of medication dispenser. 医院使用了一种新型的药物分配器。|例句：The automatic ticket dispenser was out of order. 自动售票机坏了。 |近义词：dispenser, distributor, vendor |反义词：collector, receiver
