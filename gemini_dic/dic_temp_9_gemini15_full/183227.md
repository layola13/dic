representment:/ˌreprɛzɛntmənt/| n. |再呈递；再提出；再申述|The act of presenting something again, especially a legal document or claim.  It often implies a previous attempt that was unsuccessful.|The insurance company rejected the initial claim, but the representment was successful.|近义词：re-submission, resubmission, restatement|反义词：withdrawal, abandonment|名词
