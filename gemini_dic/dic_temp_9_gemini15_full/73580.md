foreswart:/fɔːrˈswɔːrt/| v.|阻止；阻碍；使受挫折|To prevent or hinder; to thwart.|The heavy rain foreswarted our plans for a picnic.  大雨阻碍了我们的野餐计划。|The unexpected snowstorm foreswarted their journey.  突如其来的暴风雪阻碍了他们的旅程。|hinder, impede, thwart, obstruct|aid, assist, facilitate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He foreswears his ambition. 他放弃了他的抱负。
The law foreswears discrimination. 法律禁止歧视。


一般过去时:
They foreswore their allegiance to the king. 他们放弃了对国王的效忠。
The witness foreswore telling a lie. 证人发誓不说谎。


现在完成时:

He has foresworn smoking for years. 他戒烟多年了。


过去完成时:

将来时:
