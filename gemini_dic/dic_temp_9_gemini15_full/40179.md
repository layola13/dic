contentions:/kənˈtɛnʃənz/ | n. | 争论；论点；争辩 | Strong disagreement or argument.  A contentious issue is one that is likely to cause disagreement or argument. |The main contention of his argument was that the government was inefficient.他的论点主要是政府效率低下。  There was much contention over the best way to solve the problem. 关于解决这个问题的最佳方法，有很多争论。| disputes, arguments, disagreements | agreement, harmony | 名词
