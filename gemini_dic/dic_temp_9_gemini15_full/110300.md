light-at-the-end-of-the-tunnel: /laɪt æt ði ɛnd əv ðə ˈtʌnəl/ | n. phr. |隧道尽头的光明；希望的曙光 | A sign of hope or improvement after a difficult period.  It refers to a glimmer of hope that appears after a prolonged period of hardship or difficulty.  The metaphor comes from the experience of being in a dark tunnel, where the light at the end represents a positive outcome or solution. | Example: After months of job hunting, she finally saw the light at the end of the tunnel when she received a job offer.  几个月的工作寻找后，当她收到一份工作邀请时，她终于看到了隧道尽头的光明。 | 近义词：glimmer of hope, ray of hope, promise, sign of improvement | 反义词：despair, hopelessness, bleak outlook
