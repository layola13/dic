complimenting: /ˌkɒmplɪˈmɛntɪŋ/ | /ˌkɑːmplɪˈmɛntɪŋ/ | v. | 赞美；恭维；补充 |  The act of expressing praise or admiration.  It can also refer to something that enhances or completes something else. |

例句：

She spent the evening complimenting her friend's new dress. = 她花了整个晚上赞美她朋友的新裙子。

He complimented her on her excellent presentation skills. = 他称赞她优秀的演示技巧。


近义词：praising, commending, flattering, enhancing

反义词：criticizing, insulting, belittling


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is constantly complimenting his colleagues' work. = 他不断地赞美同事的工作。
She compliments her friends on their achievements. = 她赞美朋友们的成就。


一般过去时:
He complimented her on her beautiful singing voice. = 他赞美她优美的歌声。
They complimented each other on their performance. = 他们互相称赞彼此的表演。


现在完成时:
I have complimented her many times on her cooking. = 我已经多次赞美她的厨艺。
She has complimented him on his new haircut. = 她已经赞美了他的新发型。


过去完成时:
He had complimented her dress before he noticed the stain. = 他在她注意到污渍之前就赞美了她的裙子。
They had complimented each other's work before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就互相赞美了彼此的工作。


将来时:
I will compliment her on her bravery. = 我将赞美她的勇敢。
He will compliment her on her new job. = 他将祝贺她找到新工作。


虚拟语气:
If I saw her, I would compliment her on her dress. = 如果我见到她，我会赞美她的裙子。
I wish I had complimented her on her performance. = 我希望我当时赞美了她的表演。


被动语态:
Her work was complimented by the judges. = 她的作品受到了评委的赞扬。
His cooking was complimented by all the guests. = 他的厨艺受到了所有客人的赞扬。


疑问句:
Did he compliment her on her writing? = 他赞美了她的写作吗？
Has she ever complimented your work? = 她曾经赞美过你的作品吗？


否定句:
He didn't compliment her on her efforts. = 他没有赞美她的努力。
I didn't compliment him on his speech. = 我没有赞美他的演讲。
