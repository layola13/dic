outscout:/aʊtˈskaʊt/ | vt. |侦察；超过；胜过 |To scout beyond the main body of troops; to surpass in scouting or exploration.  |例句：The scouts outscouted the enemy, discovering their hidden camp.  侦察兵们侦察到了敌人的藏匿营地，超过了敌人。|近义词：outmaneuver, outwit, surpass |反义词：be outscouted by, be surpassed by |动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team outscouts its opponents every game. = 这个队每场比赛都侦察得比对手好。
He outscouts his rivals in the business world. = 他在商场上比竞争对手更善于侦察。

一般过去时:
Our scouts outscouted the enemy forces. = 我们的侦察兵超过了敌军。
The explorers outscouted the previous expeditions. = 探险家们超过了之前的探险队。

现在完成时:
They have outscouted their competitors in the market research. = 他们在市场调研方面已经超过了竞争对手。
She has outscouted all her classmates in this project. = 在这个项目中，她已经超过了所有同学。

过去完成时:

We had outscouted the rival company in securing the contract. = 我们在获得合同方面已经超过了竞争对手公司。

虚拟语气:
If we had outscouted them earlier, we would have won. = 如果我们早点侦察到他们，我们就赢了。

被动语态:
