captioning:/ˈkæpʃənɪŋ/| n.|字幕制作；加字幕|The process of adding captions to audio or video content, making it accessible to people who are deaf or hard of hearing, or who prefer to read along.|例句：The movie's captioning was excellent, making it easy to follow even with the loud background music. 这部电影的字幕制作非常好，即使背景音乐很大声也很容易理解。|近义词：subtitling|反义词：None
