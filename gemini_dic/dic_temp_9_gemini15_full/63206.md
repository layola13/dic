ententes:/ɑ̃ˈtɑːnt/| n. |协定，协议；(尤指非正式的)谅解|An entente is a formal agreement between countries, often informal and less binding than a treaty.  It usually involves cooperation on specific issues or areas of mutual interest.  The term often implies a degree of mutual trust and understanding, even if not formally codified. |Example:  The two nations signed an entente to cooperate on environmental protection.  两个国家签署了一项关于环境保护合作的协定。


The entente between the warring factions was short-lived.  交战各派之间的谅解是短暂的。|近义词：agreement, understanding, accord, treaty (more formal)|反义词：disagreement, conflict, discord|名词
