parakeet:/ˈpærəkiːt/ | n. |小鹦鹉 |A small, often brightly colored parrot.  Parakeets are known for their ability to mimic sounds and are popular as pets. |Example:  My aunt owns a green parakeet that can whistle a tune.  我阿姨养了一只绿色的鹦鹉，它会吹口哨。


Example: The vibrant colors of the parakeet attracted many birdwatchers.  这只鹦鹉鲜艳的颜色吸引了许多观鸟者。|近义词：budgerigar, lovebird |反义词：None.  |词性:名词

词源:
The word "parakeet" comes from the Dutch word *parakeet*, which in turn may derive from the Portuguese *periquito*, ultimately from the Tupi word *periquito*.  The Tupi language is indigenous to Brazil.
