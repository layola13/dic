lithotomy:/lɪˈθɑːtəmi/ | n. | 膀胱切开术；截石术；(妇科检查或手术时)仰卧位 | The surgical procedure of cutting into the bladder; also, the supine position with the hips and knees flexed and abducted, used for surgical procedures involving the pelvis and lower abdomen. | Example: The patient was positioned in lithotomy for the gynecological examination.  病人被摆成截石位进行妇科检查。 The surgeon performed a lithotomy to remove a bladder stone. 外科医生进行了膀胱切开术以取出膀胱结石。| 近义词：cystotomy (膀胱切开术),  pelvic examination (盆腔检查) | 反义词：N/A | 名词
