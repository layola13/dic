relegation:/ˌrɛləˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |降级；贬黜；降职|The act of relegating someone or something to a lower rank or position.  It often refers to the demotion of a sports team to a lower league or the lowering of someone's status or importance.|例句：The team suffered relegation to the second division after a poor season.  这个队因为赛季表现糟糕而降级到第二级别联赛。|例句：His relegation to a minor role in the company was a blow to his pride. 他被降职到公司一个次要角色，这对他的自尊心是一个打击。|近义词：demotion, degradation, downgrading|反义词：promotion, advancement, elevation|名词
