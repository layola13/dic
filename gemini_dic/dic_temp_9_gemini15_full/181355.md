reinvents:/ˌriːɪnˈvɛnts/| vt.|重新发明；彻底改变；改头换面 |To create or develop something again in a new or different way.|The company reinvented itself as a technology firm. 这家公司将自己改造成了一家科技公司。|近义词：reimagine, transform, revolutionize |反义词：destroy, abolish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reinvents his strategies every year. 他每年都重新制定策略。
They reinvent their product line regularly. 他们定期更新产品线。


一般过去时:
The company reinvented its image after the scandal.  丑闻过后，公司重塑了形象。
She reinvented herself as a successful businesswoman. 她把自己改造成一个成功的女商人。


现在完成时:
He has reinvented the wheel, again. 他又重新发明了轮子。(比喻：做了无用功)
The artist has reinvented himself several times throughout his career.  这位艺术家在他的职业生涯中多次改变了自己的风格。


过去完成时:
They had reinvented their marketing plan before launching the new product. 他们在推出新产品之前已经重新制定了营销计划。
She had reinvented her life after a difficult divorce.  经历了一场艰难的离婚后，她重塑了自己的生活。


将来时:
They will reinvent the way we communicate. 他们将彻底改变我们的沟通方式。
The company will reinvent its manufacturing process next year. 公司明年将改造其生产流程。


虚拟语气:
If they reinvented their business model, they might succeed. 如果他们重塑商业模式，他们可能会成功。
I wish I could reinvent myself and start over. 我希望我能重新开始，改头换面。


被动语态:
The product was reinvented to appeal to a younger audience.  产品经过重新设计以吸引更年轻的受众。
The old system has been reinvented to improve efficiency. 旧系统已被改造以提高效率。


疑问句:
Will they reinvent the technology?  他们会重新发明这项技术吗？
How can we reinvent our approach to the problem? 我们如何才能改变我们解决问题的方法？


否定句:
They did not reinvent the wheel; they simply improved it. 他们并没有重新发明轮子；他们只是改进了一下。
The company failed to reinvent itself and eventually went bankrupt. 这家公司未能重塑自我，最终破产了。


PHRASE->
reinvents itself 重塑自我

reinvents the future 重新创造未来
reinvents the rules 改变规则
reinvents the narrative 改变叙述
