complete:/kəmˈpliːt/ | adj., v. |完整的；完成 |  complete means having all the necessary parts; finished or ended.  |例句：The project is now complete.  这个项目现在完成了。 |例句：She completed her degree last year. 她去年完成了她的学位。 |近义词：finished, concluded, entire, whole |反义词：incomplete, unfinished, partial


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The task is complete. = 任务完成了。
This story is complete. = 这个故事是完整的。


一般过去时:
He completed the marathon. = 他完成了马拉松比赛。
She completed her homework. = 她完成了她的家庭作业。


现在完成时:
I have completed all my assignments. = 我已经完成了所有的作业。
They have completed the construction. = 他们已经完成了建筑工程。


过去完成时:
He had completed the painting before the exhibition. = 他在展览前已经完成了这幅画。
She had completed her studies before she got married. = 她结婚前已经完成了学业。


将来时:
I will complete the report by tomorrow. = 我将在明天完成报告。
We will complete the project next month. = 我们将在下个月完成项目。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would complete the project. = 如果我有更多时间，我会完成这个项目。
It would be great if we completed it on time. = 如果我们按时完成就好了。


被动语态:
The work was completed on time. = 工作按时完成了。
The form must be completed before submission. = 表格必须在提交前填写完整。


疑问句:
Is the report complete? = 报告完整吗？
Are all the requirements complete? = 所有要求都齐全吗？


否定句:
The document is not complete. = 文件不完整。
He didn't complete the task. = 他没有完成任务。


PHRASE->
