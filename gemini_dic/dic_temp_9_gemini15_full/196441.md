serrations:/sɛˈreɪʃənz/| n.|锯齿，齿状物|The indentations or notches on an edge, resembling the teeth of a saw.  They can be found on various objects, from leaves to knives.|例句：The serrated edge of the knife cut cleanly through the bread.  这把刀的锯齿状刀刃干净利落地切开了面包。

The leaves of the holly bush have sharp serrations.  冬青树的叶子有锋利的锯齿。|近义词：indentations, notches, teeth, jags|反义词：smoothness
