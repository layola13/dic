misasserted:/ˌmɪsæˈsɜːtɪd/| adj.|错误地断言的，错误地声明的|Incorrectly or wrongly stated or asserted.  The witness misasserted the defendant's whereabouts.|例句：He misasserted the facts of the case, leading to a mistrial. 他错误地陈述了案件的事实，导致审判无效。  The article misasserted the company's financial stability. 这篇文章错误地断言了公司的财务稳定性。|近义词：misstated, misrepresented, falsely claimed|反义词：correctly asserted, accurately stated|形容词
