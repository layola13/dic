greenswarded:/ˈɡriːnzˈwɔːrdɪd/| adj.|绿草如茵的|Covered with grass.  It describes an area, typically a lawn or field, that is richly covered with green grass.|例句：The greenswarded park was perfect for a picnic.  绿草如茵的公园非常适合野餐。

例句：The greenswarded campus offered a tranquil setting for studying.  绿草如茵的校园为学习提供了宁静的环境。|近义词：grassy, turfed, verdant|反义词：barren, desolate, paved
