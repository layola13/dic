reconfined:/ˌriːkənˈfaɪnd/| v. |重新限制，再次限制 |To confine again; to restrict or imprison once more.  |例句：The escaped prisoner was reconfined after being caught.  逃犯被抓获后再次被监禁。 |例句：The government reconfined the city due to a surge in COVID-19 cases. 由于新冠肺炎病例激增，政府再次封锁了该城市。|近义词：reimprison, restrict, contain |反义词：release, liberate, free
