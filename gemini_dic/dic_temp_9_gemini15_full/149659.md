pairer:/ˈpɛərər/| n. |配对者，配对器|A person or thing that pairs things together.  It can also refer to a tool or device used for pairing. |例句：The computer program acted as a pairer, matching similar items.  电脑程序充当配对器，匹配类似的项目。
例句：He is a skilled pairer of wines with food. 他是一个擅长将葡萄酒与食物搭配的行家。|近义词：matcher, coupler, joiner|反义词：separator, divider|名词
