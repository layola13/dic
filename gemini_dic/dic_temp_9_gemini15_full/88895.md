hokum:/ˈhoʊkəm/ | n. | 废话；空话；胡说 | Unsubstantiated or unbelievable talk; nonsense; bunk.  It often refers to something designed to deceive or impress, but ultimately lacking substance. | Example: The politician's speech was full of hokum.  那位政治家的演讲充满了空话。 | 近义词：nonsense, bunk, balderdash, poppycock, twaddle | 反义词：truth, fact, reality
