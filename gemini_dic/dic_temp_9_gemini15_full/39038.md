conflictive:/kənˈflɪktɪv/| adj.|冲突的；矛盾的；好争吵的 |Characterized by conflict or disagreement; tending to cause conflict.  The situation is highly conflictive, with both sides unwilling to compromise.|近义词：contentious, discordant, antagonistic, problematic |反义词：harmonious, peaceful, agreeable, compatible


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The situation is conflictive. = 情况充满冲突。
His behavior is conflictive. = 他的行为充满冲突。


一般过去时:
The meeting was conflictive. = 会议充满了冲突。
Their relationship was conflictive. = 他们的关系很冲突。


现在完成时:
The negotiations have been conflictive. = 谈判一直很冲突。
The situation has become increasingly conflictive. = 情况变得越来越冲突。


过去完成时:
The issue had already become conflictive before the meeting. = 在会议之前，这个问题已经变得很冲突了。
The relationship had been conflictive for years. = 他们的关系多年来一直很冲突。


将来时:
The upcoming election will likely be conflictive. = 即将到来的选举可能会充满冲突。
The talks will probably be conflictive. = 会谈可能会很冲突。


虚拟语气:
If the situation were less conflictive, we could reach an agreement. = 如果情况不那么冲突，我们就能达成协议。
I wish the discussion hadn't been so conflictive. = 我希望讨论不那么冲突。


被动语态:
The issue was perceived as conflictive by many. = 许多人认为这个问题很冲突。
The project was deemed conflictive and was halted. = 该项目被认为充满冲突，因此被叫停。


疑问句:
Is the situation conflictive? = 情况很冲突吗？
Do you find this issue conflictive? = 你觉得这个问题很冲突吗？


否定句:
The relationship is not conflictive. = 关系并不冲突。
The atmosphere was not conflictive. = 气氛并不冲突。
