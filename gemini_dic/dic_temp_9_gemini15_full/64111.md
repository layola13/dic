epoxying:/ɪˈpɒksɪŋ/ | v. |环氧树脂涂层，使用环氧树脂 |The process of applying epoxy resin to a surface, often for protective or adhesive purposes.  It involves spreading, smoothing, and curing the epoxy to create a durable coating. |例句：He spent the afternoon epoxying the boat's hull to protect it from the water. 他花了一下午的时间给船体涂上环氧树脂以防水。


例句：We are epoxying the cracks in the concrete floor to strengthen it. 我们正在用环氧树脂填充混凝土地板上的裂缝以增强其强度。


近义词：coating, sealing, resinating


反义词：removing, stripping (referring to removing an epoxy coating)


词性:动词
