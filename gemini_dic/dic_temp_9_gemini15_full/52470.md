diet:/ˈdaɪət/| n./ v.| 饮食；节食；规定食物| a course of food and drink regularly consumed| She is on a strict diet to lose weight.  她正在严格节食以减肥。| 近义词：regime, regimen, food plan | 反义词：gorge, gluttony


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He follows a healthy diet. = 他遵循健康的饮食。
They eat a balanced diet. = 他们吃均衡的饮食。

一般过去时:
She followed a low-carb diet last year. = 去年她遵循低碳水化合物饮食。
We ate a vegetarian diet for a month. = 我们吃了一个月的素食。


现在完成时:
I have been on this diet for three months. = 我已经坚持这个饮食三个月了。
They have changed their diet significantly. = 他们已经显著地改变了他们的饮食。


过去完成时:
She had already started a diet before the doctor's appointment. = 在看医生之前，她已经开始节食了。
He had never tried such a restrictive diet before. = 他以前从未尝试过如此严格的节食。


将来时:
I will start a new diet next week. = 我下周将开始新的节食。
They will maintain their current diet. = 他们将保持目前的饮食。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would adopt a healthier diet. = 如果我是你，我会采取更健康的饮食。
I wish I had started a diet sooner. = 我希望我早点开始节食。


被动语态:
A strict diet was prescribed by the doctor. = 医生开了严格的饮食处方。
The diet is being closely monitored. = 饮食正在被密切监控。


疑问句:
What kind of diet are you on? = 你在进行什么类型的节食？
What is a healthy diet? = 什么是健康的饮食？


否定句:
He doesn't follow a regular diet. = 他没有遵循规律的饮食。
She didn't stick to her diet. = 她没有坚持她的节食。


PHRASE->
go on a diet = 开始节食
be on a diet = 正在节食
diet plan = 饮食计划
balanced diet = 均衡饮食
healthy diet = 健康饮食
