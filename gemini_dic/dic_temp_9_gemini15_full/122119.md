mistral:/mɪˈstrɑːl/ | n. | 弥斯特拉尔风（一种强烈的西北风） | A strong, cold, dry northerly wind that blows from the Rhône valley down the coast of southern France.  It is particularly common in the autumn and winter. | The mistral whipped through the narrow streets, scattering leaves and dust.  弥斯特拉尔风呼啸着穿过狭窄的街道，吹散了树叶和尘土。

The mistral forced us to cancel our boat trip.  弥斯特拉尔风迫使我们取消了我们的乘船旅行。| 近义词：northerly, gale, wind | 反义词：calm, breeze
