overnighting:/ˌoʊvərˈnaɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 隔夜；通宵；留宿 |  To spend the night somewhere other than one's usual place of residence.  |  例句：We are overnighting at a hotel near the airport. 我们在机场附近的一家酒店过夜。
例句：They decided to overnight in a small town along the way. 他们决定在路途中一个小镇过夜。 | 近义词：staying, lodging | 反义词：day-tripping


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overnights at his friend's house every Friday. = 他每个星期五都睡在他朋友家。
They overnight in different hotels every week during their tour. = 他们在巡演期间每周都在不同的酒店过夜。

一般过去时:
We overnighted at a cozy cabin in the woods. = 我们在森林里舒适的小屋里过夜。
She overnighted at her sister's apartment after the concert. = 音乐会后，她在姐姐的公寓里过夜。

现在完成时:
I have overnighted in many different cities during my travels. = 在我的旅行中，我已经在许多不同的城市过夜了。
They have overnighted at that same resort for the past three years. = 在过去的三年里，他们一直在那家度假村过夜。

过去完成时:
Before the flight, they had overnighted at a hotel near the airport. = 在飞行之前，他们在机场附近的一家酒店过夜了。
We had overnighted at a friend's place before driving to the coast. = 我们在开车去海边之前，在一个朋友家过夜了。

将来时:
We will overnight at a bed and breakfast tomorrow. = 我们明天将在一家住宿加早餐旅馆过夜。
They will overnight in a hotel near the conference center. = 他们将在会议中心附近的一家酒店过夜。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would overnight in a more luxurious hotel. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会住在一间更豪华的酒店。
I wish we could overnight on the beach. = 我希望我们能在海滩上过夜。

被动语态:
The guests were overnighted in the hotel's best suite. = 客人们被安排住在酒店最好的套房里。(此用法较少见，通常用被动语态表达住宿安排)
Many bags were overnighted to the destination. = 许多行李被快递到目的地。(此处"overnighting"指快递隔夜送达，非住宿)

疑问句:
Where will you be overnighting tonight? = 你今晚会在哪里过夜？
Are they overnighting at your house? = 他们会在你家过夜吗？

否定句:
We did not overnight at a hotel; we camped instead. = 我们没有在酒店过夜；我们而是露营了。
They couldn't overnight because the hotel was fully booked. = 他们不能过夜，因为酒店客满了。
