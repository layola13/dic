overhunts: /ˌoʊvərˈhʌnts/ | v. |过度狩猎|To hunt to excess; to hunt beyond sustainable levels, leading to depletion of the animal population.  |例句：The overhunting of whales nearly drove several species to extinction.  过度捕鲸几乎导致几种鲸鱼灭绝。 The government implemented stricter regulations to prevent the overhunting of endangered species. 政府实施了更严格的法规以防止对濒危物种的过度捕猎。|近义词：overexploit, deplete, exhaust |反义词：conserve, protect, preserve |动词
