munificate:/mjuːˈnɪfɪkeɪt/ | vt. |慷慨给予，施舍 |To give or bestow generously; to be munificent.  The act of giving generously is often associated with charity or philanthropy. |例句：The wealthy philanthropist munificated large sums of money to various charities.  这位富有的慈善家慷慨地向各种慈善机构捐赠了巨款。

The king munificated land and titles to his loyal subjects. 国王慷慨地将土地和爵位赏赐给他的忠臣。|近义词：bestow, donate, grant, endow |反义词：stint, withhold, deprive
