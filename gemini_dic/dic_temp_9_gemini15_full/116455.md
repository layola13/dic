matchups:/ˈmætʃʌps/| n.|比赛组合；对阵；配对|A matchup refers to a pairing of competitors, often in a sporting event or a competition.  It can also refer to the pairing of things in a more general sense.|例句：The much-anticipated matchup between the two teams will determine the champion.  这场备受期待的两队比赛将决定冠军。

The marketing team analyzed the different product matchups to maximize sales. 市场团队分析了不同的产品组合以最大化销售额。|近义词：pairings, contests, encounters|反义词：separation, disjunction|
