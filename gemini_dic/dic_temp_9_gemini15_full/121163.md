misdiagnoses:/ˌmɪsdaɪəɡˈnoʊsiːz/| n.|误诊|The plural form of the noun "misdiagnosis," referring to instances of incorrect diagnoses. It implies a series of errors in medical judgment.  | Example: The hospital is facing lawsuits due to numerous misdiagnoses of serious illnesses. 医院因多次误诊严重疾病而面临诉讼。|近义词：incorrect diagnoses, wrong diagnoses|反义词：accurate diagnoses, correct diagnoses|名词

The doctor frequently misdiagnoses patients. = 医生经常误诊病人。
His misdiagnoses often lead to delayed treatment. = 他的误诊往往导致治疗延误。

一般过去时:
They misdiagnosed the disease initially. = 他们最初误诊了这种疾病。
The patient's condition worsened due to past misdiagnoses. = 由于过去的误诊，病人的病情恶化了。

现在完成时:
The clinic has had several misdiagnoses this year. = 诊所今年已经发生了好几起误诊。
They have reported many misdiagnoses to the medical board. = 他们已经向医疗委员会报告了许多误诊案例。

过去完成时:

The patient's death was linked to misdiagnoses that had occurred months earlier. = 病人的死亡与几个月前发生的误诊有关。

将来时:
Without better training, more misdiagnoses will occur in the future. = 如果没有更好的培训，将来会发生更多误诊。
We predict that improved technology will reduce the number of misdiagnoses. = 我们预测，改进的技术将减少误诊的数量。

虚拟语气:
