junkets:/ˈdʒʌŋkɪts/| n.| 宴席；公款旅游；|A junket is an expensive trip, often paid for by public funds, usually taken by officials or other important people.  It often implies a lack of accountability or a misuse of public money.|例句：The congressman was criticized for taking lavish junkets paid for by taxpayers.  这位国会议员因乘坐纳税人支付的豪华公款旅游而受到批评。|例句：The company organized a junket to celebrate its anniversary. 公司组织了一次公款旅游来庆祝周年纪念。|近义词：excursion, trip, outing, feast|反义词：stay-at-home, frugal trip|名词
