词源:
The word is formed from the combination of:

* **pseudo-**:  (from Greek *pseudēs*) meaning "false," "sham," or "spurious."
* **socially**:  an adverb derived from the adjective "social," referring to society or social interaction.


Therefore, "pseudosocially" literally means "in a false or sham social manner."
