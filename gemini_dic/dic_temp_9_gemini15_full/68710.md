farfalle:/fɑːrˈfælɪ/| n. |蝴蝶面|Bow-tie pasta; a type of pasta shaped like a bow tie or butterfly.  It's known for its unique shape and ability to hold sauces well. |Example:  I'm making a delicious pesto sauce for the farfalle tonight. 今晚我要为蝴蝶面做美味的罗勒酱。|近义词: bow tie pasta,  butterfly pasta |反义词:None
