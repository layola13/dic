sarcina:/ˈsɑːrsɪnə/| n.|沙雷菌属；(细菌)方形群体|A sarcina is a genus of bacteria that are arranged in cube-like packets of eight cells.  These bacteria are often found in the environment and can sometimes cause infections.|Example:  The researchers identified *Sarcina ventriculi* in the contaminated water sample. 研究人员在受污染的水样中发现了*腹腔沙雷菌*。|近义词:coccus (球菌) (in the context of bacterial shape)|反义词:None specifically, but terms referring to other bacterial shapes could be considered contrastive. |名词
