fevering:/ˈfiːvərɪŋ/| adj.|发烧的；兴奋的；狂热的|Characterized by or exhibiting fever; exhibiting or characterized by intense excitement or enthusiasm.|例句：The child was feverish and restless.  孩子发烧且烦躁不安。|例句：The feverish activity in the office suggested an important deadline was approaching.  办公室里紧张忙碌的景象表明即将迎来重要的截止日期。|近义词：feverish, excited, enthusiastic|反义词：calm, apathetic, unemotional
