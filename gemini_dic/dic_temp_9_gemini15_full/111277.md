litter:/ˈlɪtər/ | n. v. |垃圾；杂乱；弄乱；产仔 |

英文解析:
As a noun, "litter" refers to scattered rubbish or waste material, often carelessly discarded. It can also refer to a group of young animals born at the same time (e.g., a litter of puppies). As a verb, "litter" means to cover or scatter with rubbish or to make untidy.


例句:
The park was littered with empty bottles and cans.  = 公园里到处都是空瓶子和易拉罐。

Please don't litter the streets. = 请不要乱扔垃圾。


近义词:
rubbish, trash, garbage, debris, mess, offspring, brood


反义词:
cleanliness, order, tidiness


词性:
Noun, Verb
