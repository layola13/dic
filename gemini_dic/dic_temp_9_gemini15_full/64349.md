erases:/ɪˈreɪzɪz/ | vt. |擦除；抹去；消除；删除 | To remove something written or drawn, or to remove information from a computer. |例句：He erased the mistake with an eraser. 他用橡皮擦掉了错误。

例句：The teacher erased the words from the board. 老师从黑板上擦掉了字。


近义词：delete, remove, wipe out | 反义词：write, draw, add | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He erases the board every day. 他每天擦黑板。
She erases her mistakes carefully. 她仔细地擦掉她的错误。


一般过去时:
I erased the file by mistake. 我不小心删除了文件。
He erased the drawing from the paper. 他从纸上擦掉了图画。


现在完成时:
She has erased all the data from the hard drive. 她已经从硬盘中删除了所有数据。
He has erased the evidence. 他已经销毁了证据。


过去完成时:
I had erased the message before she saw it.  在她看到之前我已经删除了信息。
They had erased the graffiti before the police arrived. 在警察到达之前，他们已经擦掉了涂鸦。


将来时:
I will erase this file later. 我稍后会删除这个文件。
She will erase the whiteboard after the lesson. 课后她会擦掉白板。


虚拟语气:
If I had a magic eraser, I would erase all my problems. 如果我有一块神奇的橡皮擦，我会擦掉我所有的烦恼。
I wish I could erase that embarrassing moment from my memory. 我真希望我能从记忆中抹去那段尴尬的时刻。


被动语态:
The message was erased from the server.  信息从服务器上被删除了。
The board is erased after each class. 每次课后黑板都会被擦干净。


疑问句:
Did you erase the file? 你删除了文件吗？
Has she erased the data yet? 她已经删除数据了吗？


否定句:
I didn't erase the document. 我没有删除文档。
He hasn't erased the whiteboard. 他还没有擦黑板。


PHRASE->
erase a memory: 忘记一段记忆
erase a hard drive: 清空硬盘
erase a mistake: 纠正错误
erase from existence: 消灭
erase the line between: 打破界限


词源:
来自古法语 *eraser,  源自拉丁语 *radere "to scrape, shave".  "erase" 的含义演变与擦除的动作相关，从最初的刮擦、刮除演变为现代意义上的删除、抹去。
