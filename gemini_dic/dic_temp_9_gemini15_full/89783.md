horizonless: /həˈraɪzən lɪs/ | adj. | 无边无际的，广阔无垠的 |  Without a horizon; vast and unending.  It implies a lack of boundaries or limits, often in a metaphorical sense. | Example: The endless, horizonless expanse of the ocean filled her with both awe and terror.  无尽的、无边无际的大海让她既敬畏又恐惧。| 近义词：boundless, limitless, endless, immeasurable | 反义词：limited, bounded, confined
