cartridges:/ˈkɑːtrɪdʒɪz/ | n. |墨盒；弹药筒；盒式磁带|A cartridge is a case containing a charge of gunpowder and a bullet or shot, or a container holding ink or toner for a printer.  It can also refer to a container for magnetic tape in older audio or video recording systems. |例句：The printer is out of cartridges. 这台打印机墨盒用完了。

例句：The soldiers loaded their cartridges before the battle. 士兵们在战斗前装填了弹药筒。


近义词：cassette (for tape),  ink container, toner container

反义词：无
