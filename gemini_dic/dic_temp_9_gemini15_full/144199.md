outsight:/ˈaʊtsaɪt/| n. |视野，眼界，见识 |A broad outlook or perspective; the extent of one's knowledge or experience.  It refers to the scope of one's understanding and perception, often implying a wider perspective than immediate surroundings. |例句：His travels broadened his outsight considerably.他的旅行大大开阔了他的视野。

例句：The summit offered a magnificent outsight of the valley below. 山顶提供了壮丽的山谷景色。(This example uses "outsight" in a less common, more figurative sense, emphasizing the visual scope.)


近义词：outlook, perspective, viewpoint, vision, scope

反义词：narrow-mindedness, ignorance, shortsightedness
