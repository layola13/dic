self-ruined: /ˌsɛlfˈruːɪnd/ | adj. | 自毁的；自我毁灭的 |  Describes someone or something that has been destroyed or ruined by their own actions or choices.  It emphasizes the internal cause of the destruction. |


例句：

His self-ruined reputation made it hard to find a new job. = 他自毁的名声使他很难找到新工作。

The once-great empire fell into self-ruined decay. =  曾经伟大的帝国衰落成了一片自毁的废墟。


近义词：self-destructive, ruined, destroyed, undone


反义词：successful, thriving, flourishing


词性: 形容词
