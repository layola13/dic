chequer:/ˈtʃɛkər/| n. |棋盘；方格图案；财政部；支票|A chequer is a board for playing games like chess, draughts, or backgammon, marked with squares of contrasting colours.  It can also refer to a pattern of squares of different colours, or, historically, to the British Exchequer, a government department responsible for finances. |例句：The chessboard is a beautiful example of a chequer.  这个棋盘是方格图案的漂亮例子。 The chequer pattern on the tablecloth was very striking. 桌布上的方格图案非常醒目。|近义词：checkered, grid, tessellation |反义词：uniform, plain
