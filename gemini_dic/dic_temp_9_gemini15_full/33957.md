cirsotomy:/sɜːrˈsɒtəmi/| n. | 血管瘤切除术 | Surgical operation to remove a cirsoid aneurysm (a type of arteriovenous malformation).  The procedure involves excising the dilated, tortuous vessels characteristic of the aneurysm. | Example: The surgeon recommended a cirsotomy to treat the patient's facial arteriovenous malformation.  外科医生建议进行血管瘤切除术来治疗患者的面部动静脉畸形。 |近义词：aneurysmectomy (if the focus is on the aneurysm itself), arteriovenous malformation resection |反义词：N/A
