clenched:/klɛntʃt/| v. adj.| 紧握；咬紧；（肌肉）紧张的 |To close or press (something) tightly; to hold something tightly.  It can also describe a tense or rigid state, particularly of muscles or jaws.|例句：He clenched his fists in anger. 他愤怒地握紧了拳头。
She clenched her jaw, determined not to cry. 她咬紧牙关，决心不哭。|近义词：grasped, gripped, squeezed, tightened|反义词：relaxed, loosened, opened
