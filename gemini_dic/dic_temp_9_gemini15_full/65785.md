/ˈiːvzdrɒp/| vt.|偷听；窃听|To listen secretly to a private conversation.|例句：I accidentally eavesdropped on their conversation. 我不小心偷听了他们的谈话。

例句：It's rude to eavesdrop on other people's conversations. 偷听别人的谈话是不礼貌的。|近义词：listen in, overhear, spy|反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eavesdrops on his neighbors' conversations every day. 他每天都偷听邻居的谈话。
They often eavesdrop on the phone calls of others. 他们经常窃听别人的电话。

一般过去时:
She eavesdropped on the meeting between the boss and his secretary. 她偷听了老板和秘书之间的谈话。
I eavesdropped on their argument last night. 昨晚我偷听了他们的争吵。


现在完成时:
He has eavesdropped on our conversations many times. 他多次偷听我们的谈话。

过去完成时:
She had eavesdropped on their plans before they revealed them. 在他们透露计划之前，她就偷听了他们的计划。
They had eavesdropped on the secret meeting for weeks. 他们已经偷听了几个星期的秘密会议。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I hadn't eavesdropped on their private conversation. 我真希望我没有偷听他们的私人谈话。


被动语态:
