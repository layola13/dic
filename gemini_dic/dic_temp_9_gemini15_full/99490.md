interfiltrates:/ˌɪntərˈfɪltreɪts/| v.|渗透，弥漫，互相渗透|To penetrate and spread between or through the parts of (something).  It often implies a gradual or subtle process of mixing or mingling.|例句：The two cultures interfiltrated over centuries, resulting in a unique blend of traditions.  几个世纪以来，这两种文化相互渗透，形成了独特的传统融合。

例句：The dye interfiltrated the fabric, creating a mottled effect. 染料渗透到织物中，产生斑驳的效果。


近义词：permeate, infiltrate, intermingle, blend, diffuse|反义词：separate, segregate, isolate|词性:动词
