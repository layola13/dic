initiated:/ɪˈnɪʃieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 开始的；创始的；入门的 |  Having been started; begun; introduced.  | The initiated members of the club understood the secret handshake.  俱乐部里那些入门会员懂得秘密握手。 | begun, commenced, started | finished, concluded | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The program is initiated by the government. = 这个项目由政府启动。
He initiates many new projects every year. = 他每年启动许多新项目。


一般过去时:
The war was initiated in 1914. = 战争始于1914年。
She initiated a conversation with him. = 她和他开始了对话。


现在完成时:
They have initiated a new marketing strategy. = 他们已经启动了一个新的营销策略。
The company has initiated several cost-cutting measures. = 公司已经启动了几项削减成本的措施。


过去完成时:
The project had been initiated before he joined the team. = 他加入团队之前，项目就已经启动了。
Before the meeting, they had initiated several discussions. = 会议之前，他们已经进行了多次讨论。


将来时:
The company will initiate a new product line next year. = 公司明年将启动一条新的产品线。
They will initiate legal proceedings against the company. = 他们将对该公司提起诉讼。


虚拟语气:
If they initiated the project earlier, it would have been completed by now. = 如果他们早点启动项目，现在应该已经完成了。
I wish they had initiated the conversation sooner. = 我希望他们早点开始对话。


被动语态:
The project was initiated last month. = 该项目是上个月启动的。
The changes were initiated by the management team. = 这些改变是由管理团队启动的。


疑问句:
When was the program initiated? = 该项目是什么时候启动的？
Who initiated the investigation? = 谁发起了调查？


否定句:
The project was not initiated on time. = 项目没有按时启动。
He didn't initiate any contact with her. = 他没有和她联系。


PHRASE->
initiated into = 被介绍到…；被吸纳入…
initiated a discussion/conversation = 开始讨论/对话
initiated a process/procedure = 启动流程/程序
initiated a project/plan = 启动项目/计划
initiated a lawsuit/legal proceedings = 提起诉讼


词源:
From Latin *initiātus, past participle of initiāre "to begin," from in- "in, into" + initium "beginning."
