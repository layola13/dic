samovars:/ˈsɑːməvɑːrz/ | n. | 茶炊；保温茶壶 | A samovar is a large metal container, traditionally made of silver or brass, used to boil water for tea.  It typically has a spigot for dispensing the hot water.  Samovars are a cultural symbol, particularly associated with Russia. | Example: The grandmother placed a steaming samovar on the table, ready to serve tea to her guests.  祖母把一个冒着热气的茶炊放在桌子上，准备为客人沏茶。 | 近义词：urn, kettle (less culturally specific) | 反义词：N/A
