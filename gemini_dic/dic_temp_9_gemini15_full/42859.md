courter:/ˈkɔːrtər/ | n. |求爱者，追求者 | A person who courts someone, especially a man who is courting a woman.  | Example: He was a devoted courter, showering her with gifts and attention. 他是一位忠实的追求者，用礼物和关爱来讨好她。 |近义词：suitor, wooer, admirer |反义词：None specifically, but could be considered opposite of someone who rejects courtship.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He courts her relentlessly. = 他锲而不舍地追求她。
She courts popularity with her kindness. = 她以她的善良博得人气。


一般过去时:
He courted her for a year before proposing. = 他追求了她一年才求婚。
They courted each other for months before getting married. = 他们在结婚前互相追求了几个月。


现在完成时:
He has courted several women since his divorce. = 自从离婚以来，他已经追求过好几个女人了。
She has courted controversy with her outspoken views. = 她因直言不讳的观点而引起争议。


过去完成时:
He had courted her for two years before she finally accepted his proposal. = 他追求了她两年，她才最终接受了他的求婚。
They had courted for a long time before deciding to get married. = 他们恋爱很久之后才决定结婚。


将来时:
He will court her with flowers and romantic dinners. = 他将用鲜花和浪漫的晚餐来追求她。
She will court investors for her new startup. = 她将为她的新创业公司争取投资者。


虚拟语气:
If he had courted her more patiently, she might have accepted him. = 如果他更耐心地追求她，她也许会接受他。
If I were to court someone, I would be honest and upfront. = 如果我要追求某人，我会诚实和坦率。


被动语态:
She was courted by many suitors. = 她被许多追求者追求。
His ideas were courted by the media. = 他的想法受到媒体的追捧。


疑问句:
Who is he courting? = 他在追求谁？
Is she still courting him? = 她还在追求他吗？


否定句:
He didn't court her in a traditional way. = 他没有以传统的方式追求她。
She didn't court attention; it found her. = 她并没有刻意去吸引注意，而是注意找到了她。
