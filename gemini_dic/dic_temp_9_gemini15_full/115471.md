manufactures:/ˌmænjuˈfæktʃərz/| v. |制造；生产|To make products, especially on a large scale using machinery.  The verb form of "manufacture".|例句：This company manufactures cars.这家公司生产汽车。

例句：Many factories manufacture clothes for export.许多工厂生产服装出口。


近义词：produces, makes, fabricates, constructs|反义词：consumes, destroys, uses up|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They manufacture high-quality furniture. = 他们生产高质量的家具。
The factory manufactures electronics components. = 这家工厂生产电子元件。


一般过去时:
Ford manufactured its first car in 1908. = 福特公司在1908年制造了它的第一辆汽车。
The company manufactured toys for many years. = 这家公司生产玩具很多年了。


现在完成时:
They have manufactured over a million units this year. = 他们今年已经生产了超过一百万台。
The plant has manufactured various products since its opening. = 自工厂开业以来，它已经生产了各种产品。


过去完成时:
They had manufactured all the parts before the assembly began. = 在组装开始之前，他们已经制造了所有零件。
The company had manufactured thousands of items before the new regulations were introduced. = 在新的规章制度出台之前，该公司已经生产了数千件商品。


将来时:
We will manufacture more sustainable products in the future. = 我们将来会生产更环保的产品。
The factory will manufacture a new line of smartphones next year. = 该工厂明年将生产一款新型智能手机。


虚拟语气:
If they manufactured more efficiently, they would be more profitable. = 如果他们生产效率更高，他们就会更有利可图。
I wish they manufactured a version with better battery life. = 我希望他们生产一个电池续航时间更长的版本。


被动语态:
Thousands of cars are manufactured in this factory every year. = 每年在这个工厂生产数千辆汽车。
The product is manufactured using cutting-edge technology. = 该产品采用尖端技术制造。


疑问句:
Where are these products manufactured? = 这些产品在哪里生产？
What kind of materials are manufactured here? = 这里生产什么类型的材料？


否定句:
They do not manufacture any hazardous materials. = 他们不生产任何危险材料。
The company did not manufacture that particular model anymore. = 该公司不再生产该特定型号。
