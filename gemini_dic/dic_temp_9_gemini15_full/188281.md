rsvped:/ˌɑːrɛsviːˈpiːd/| adj. |已回复的；已回应的|The abbreviation of "Responded"  It indicates that someone has replied to an invitation or request.  This is commonly used in the context of event invitations.|Example: "We're pleased to announce that over 100 people have RSVPed to the event." = 我们很高兴地宣布，已有超过100人回复了此次活动的邀请。|近义词：replied, answered, confirmed|反义词：unresponded, ignored


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He RSVPs to every invitation he receives. = 他会回复收到的每一个邀请。
They always RSVP promptly. = 他们总是迅速回复。


一般过去时:
She RSVPed to the wedding invitation last week. = 她上周回复了婚礼邀请。
He RSVPed "yes" to the dinner party. = 他回复参加晚宴。


现在完成时:
Have you RSVPed to the conference yet? = 你已经回复会议邀请了吗？
They have RSVPed and will be attending. = 他们已经回复了并且将会参加。


过去完成时:
She had RSVPed before the deadline. = 她在截止日期前已经回复了。
We had RSVPed to the event months ago. = 我们几个月前就回复了该活动的邀请。


将来时:
I will RSVP as soon as I can. = 我会尽快回复。
They will RSVP by the end of the week. = 他们会在周末前回复。


虚拟语气:
If I had known earlier, I would have RSVPed sooner. = 如果我早点知道，我会早点回复。
I wish I had RSVPed to the party. = 我希望我回复了派对邀请。


被动语态:
The invitations were RSVPed to by most attendees. = 大多数与会者都回复了邀请函。
The event was fully RSVPed to within a week. = 活动在一周内全部收到了回复。


疑问句:
Have you RSVPed to Sarah's birthday party? = 你回复莎拉的生日派对邀请了吗？
Did everyone RSVP to the meeting? = 每个人都回复了会议邀请吗？


否定句:
I haven't RSVPed yet. = 我还没回复。
They didn't RSVP to the event. = 他们没有回复该活动的邀请。


PHRASE->
RSVP by [date] = 请在[日期]前回复
Please RSVP = 请回复
RSVP required = 必须回复
Kindly RSVP = 请回复
RSVP your attendance = 请回复您是否参加
