proctors:/ˈprɒktərz/| n. v.|监考员；监督者；| a person who supervises or oversees an exam or test, ensuring fairness and preventing cheating.  Also, someone who acts as a guardian or representative.|例句：The proctors watched carefully to ensure there was no cheating during the exam.  监考人员仔细观察以确保考试期间没有作弊。

The university appointed proctors to oversee the student elections. 大学委派监考人员监督学生选举。


近义词：supervisor, invigilator, examiner, monitor|反义词：examinee, test-taker|词性:noun, verb
