in the event of: /ɪn ðiː ɪˈvɛnt əv/ | prep. |万一；如果发生… |This phrase introduces a hypothetical situation or contingency. It signifies that the following clause describes what should be done or will happen if a particular event occurs.  |例句：In the event of fire, please use the nearest exit. 万一发生火灾，请使用最近的出口。

In the event of a power outage, we'll switch to the backup generator.  万一停电，我们将切换到备用发电机。


近义词：in case of, should, if, supposing


反义词：in the absence of, regardless of


词性：介词短语
