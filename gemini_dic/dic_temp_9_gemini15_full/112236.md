loosens: /ˈluːsən(z)/ | vt. |使松动；放松；变松 | To make or become less tight or firm.  |例句：He loosened his tie. 他松了松领带。 |例句：The heat loosened the soil. 热使土壤松动。|近义词：relax, slacken, loosen up |反义词：tighten, firm up |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gardener loosens the soil before planting. = 园丁在种植前松土。
He loosens his grip on the rope. = 他放松了对绳子的抓握。


一般过去时:
She loosened her hair after the long day. = 长长的一天后，她松开了头发。
The earthquake loosened the rocks on the mountainside. = 地震使山坡上的岩石松动。


现在完成时:
The screws have loosened over time. = 螺丝随着时间的推移松动了。
The tension has loosened considerably since the agreement. = 自从达成协议以来，紧张局势已大大缓和。


过去完成时:
The rope had loosened, causing the bucket to fall. = 绳子已经松了，导致桶掉了下来。
The screws had loosened before the machine malfunctioned. = 机器出现故障之前，螺丝已经松动了。


将来时:
The cold weather will loosen the frozen ground. = 冷天气会使冻土松动。
I will loosen my belt after this big meal. = 这顿大餐后我会解开我的腰带。


虚拟语气:
If you loosened your grip, you might be able to reach it. = 如果你放松一下你的抓握，你也许就能拿到它。
I wish the knot would loosen so I could untie it. = 我希望这个结能松开，这样我就能解开了。


被动语态:
The soil was loosened by the rain. = 土壤被雨水弄松了。
The bolts were loosened with a wrench. = 螺栓用扳手松开了。


疑问句:
Does this nut loosen easily? = 这个螺母容易松动吗？
Will the warm weather loosen the snow? = 暖和的天气会融化积雪吗？


否定句:
The door doesn't loosen easily; it's stuck. = 门不容易松动；它卡住了。
He didn't loosen his grip on the steering wheel. = 他没有放松对方向盘的抓握。
