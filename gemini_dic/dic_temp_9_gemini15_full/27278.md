calumniator:/kəˈlʌmnieɪtər/| n. |诽谤者，中伤者 | A person who makes false and defamatory statements about someone.  |例句：He was branded a calumniator by his rivals. 他被他的对手斥为诽谤者。|例句：The newspaper article portrayed him as a calumniator, spreading false accusations. 那篇报纸文章将他描绘成一个诽谤者，散布虚假指控。|近义词：slanderer, defamer, libeler |反义词：truth-teller, defender
