dizzy:/ˈdɪzi/| adj.|头晕的，眼花的；令人头晕目眩的|Feeling a sensation of spinning or whirling; causing such a sensation.  It can also describe something that is confusing or overwhelming.|例句：The fast ride made me dizzy.  快速的骑乘让我头晕。

例句：The complex instructions left me feeling dizzy. 复杂的指令让我感到头晕目眩。


近义词：giddy, lightheaded, faint, woozy|反义词：steady, stable, clearheaded


词性:形容词
