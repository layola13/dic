exhale:/ɛkˈheɪl/| vt. vi.|呼气，吐气|To breathe out.|She exhaled slowly, trying to calm herself. 她慢慢地呼气，试图让自己平静下来。|inhale|inhale|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exhales deeply after each yoga pose. = 他在每个瑜伽姿势之后深呼吸。
She exhales quietly while meditating. = 她冥想时静静地呼气。

一般过去时:
The runner exhaled sharply as he crossed the finish line. = 跑步者到达终点线时用力呼气。
He exhaled a long sigh of relief. = 他长长地舒了一口气。

现在完成时:
She has exhaled the smoke from her cigarette. = 她已经把香烟的烟吐了出来。
They have exhaled deeply to relax their minds. = 他们已经深呼吸来放松身心。

过去完成时:
He had exhaled all the air from his lungs before he dived into the water. = 他在潜入水中之前已经呼出了肺部所有的空气。
She had exhaled a cloud of steam before she spoke. = 她说话前已经呼出一团水汽。


将来时:
He will exhale the smoke when he finishes his cigar. = 他抽完雪茄后会把烟吐出来。
She will exhale deeply to calm her nerves before the performance. = 演出前她会深呼吸来平复紧张情绪。

虚拟语气:
If I could exhale my worries, I would feel better. = 如果我能把我的担忧呼出去，我会感觉更好。
I wish I could exhale all this stress. = 我希望我能把所有的压力都释放出来。

被动语态:
(Exhale is rarely used in the passive voice,  as it's an action the subject performs on itself.)  The air was exhaled from his lungs. = 空气从他的肺部呼出。(This is an unnatural sentence)

疑问句:
Do you exhale deeply during your yoga practice? = 你在练习瑜伽时会深呼吸吗？
Does he exhale the smoke in the direction of the wind? = 他是否顺着风向呼气？


否定句:
He didn't exhale the smoke properly. = 他没有正确地呼出烟。
She couldn't exhale due to the tight chest. = 由于胸闷，她无法呼气。


PHRASE->

exhale forcefully = 用力呼气
exhale steadily = 平稳地呼气
