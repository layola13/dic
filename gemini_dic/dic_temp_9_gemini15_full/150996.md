paramour:/ˈpærəmʊr/| n. |情妇，情人 |A paramour is a person who is having a sexual relationship with someone, typically a secret one.  This usually implies that one or both of the people involved are already in a committed relationship with someone else. |例句：He carried on a secret affair with his paramour. 他与他的情妇私通。|例句：The king's paramour wielded considerable influence over him. 国王的的情妇对他有很大的影响力。|近义词：lover, mistress, concubine |反义词：spouse, husband, wife
