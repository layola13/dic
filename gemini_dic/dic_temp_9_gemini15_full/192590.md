scumbags:/ˈskʌmˌbæɡz/| n.|卑鄙小人，无赖，渣滓 | A scumbag is a person who is considered to be extremely unpleasant, immoral, or dishonest.  The term is highly offensive and should be used with extreme caution. | Example: He's a scumbag who cheated his own mother out of her money. 他是个卑鄙小人，骗了他母亲的钱。  |近义词：villain, rat, creep, lowlife, bastard |反义词：saint, angel, hero, paragon
