etherize:/ˈiːθəraɪz/| vt.|用乙醚麻醉|To administer ether to (someone) as an anesthetic.  The term often implies a historical context, as ether is less commonly used as an anesthetic now.|例句：The surgeon prepared to etherize the patient before the operation. = 外科医生准备在手术前用乙醚麻醉病人。

例句：They etherized him to alleviate the pain during the procedure. = 他们在手术过程中用乙醚麻醉他以减轻疼痛。


近义词：anesthetize, narcotize|反义词：None specifically, but words implying awakening or consciousness could be considered antonyms in context.|词性:动词
