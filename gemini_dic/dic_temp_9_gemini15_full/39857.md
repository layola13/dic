constant: /ˈkɒnstənt/ /ˈkɑːnstənt/ | adj., n. |不变的；常数；恒量 | unchanging in nature, amount, or degree |近义词：consistent, invariable, perpetual |反义词：variable, changeable, fluctuating

英文解析:
As an adjective, "constant" describes something that stays the same or continues without changing.  It implies a lack of variation or fluctuation.  As a noun, "constant" refers to a value or quantity that remains unchanged throughout a process or equation, often used in mathematics and science.


例句:
The speed of light is a constant.  光速是一个常数。

He remained constant in his support for the project. 他一直坚定地支持这个项目。


近义词:
consistent, invariable, perpetual, steady, unchanging


反义词:
variable, changeable, fluctuating, inconsistent, unsteady


词性:
adjective, noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The temperature remains constant. 温度保持不变。
The pressure is constant throughout the experiment. 实验过程中压力保持恒定。


一般过去时:
The price remained constant last year. 去年价格保持不变。
The engine ran at a constant speed. 发动机以恒定的速度运转。


现在完成时:
The machine has run constantly for 24 hours. 这台机器已经连续运行了24小时。
The value has remained constant since the beginning. 自始至终该值保持不变。


过去完成时:
The temperature had remained constant until the experiment started. 直到实验开始，温度才保持不变。
The signal had been constant before the interruption. 中断前信号一直很稳定。


将来时:
The flow rate will remain constant. 流量将保持不变。
The system will maintain a constant temperature. 系统将保持恒温。


虚拟语气:
If the pressure were constant, the results would be different. 如果压力恒定，结果就会不同。
If the supply were constant, there would be no shortage. 如果供应稳定，就不会短缺。


被动语态:
The temperature is kept constant in the incubator. 恒温箱中的温度保持恒定。
The speed was maintained constant by the control system. 速度由控制系统保持恒定。


疑问句:
Is the voltage constant? 电压恒定吗？
Does the rate of change remain constant? 变化率保持恒定吗？


否定句:
The quality is not constant. 质量不稳定。
The signal was not constant throughout the transmission. 信号在整个传输过程中并不稳定。

词源:
From Old French *constant, from Latin *constans, present participle of constare "to stand together, be firm," from com- "together" + stare "to stand."
