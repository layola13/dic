electroluminescent:/ˌɪˌlɛktrəˈlumɪnɛsnt/| adj.|电致发光的|Emitting light in response to the passage of an electric current.  This phenomenon is used in various displays and lighting applications.  |例句：The electroluminescent panel glowed softly in the dark. 电致发光面板在黑暗中柔和地发光。  The display uses electroluminescent technology.  该显示器使用电致发光技术。|近义词：phosphorescent, luminescent|反义词：non-luminescent

词源:

The word "electroluminescent" is a combination of "electro-" (from Greek  ἤλεκτρον (ēlektron) meaning "amber," the source of static electricity), "luminescence" (from Latin *lūmen, meaning "light," and -escence, meaning "becoming").  It describes the emission of light caused by an electric current.
