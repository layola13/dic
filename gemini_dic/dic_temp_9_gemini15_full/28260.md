captioner: /ˈkæpʃənər/ | n. |字幕员，解说员 | A person who adds captions to videos or other media.  This often involves transcribing spoken words into text and synchronizing it with the video.  They may also add descriptions of non-speech audio elements. | Example: The captioner worked quickly to ensure the video was accessible to deaf and hard-of-hearing viewers.  字幕员快速工作以确保聋哑和听力障碍的观众可以观看视频。|近义词：subtitler, transcriber |反义词：None specifically.
