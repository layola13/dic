lipgloss:/ˈlɪpɡlɒs/| n.|唇彩，唇蜜|A cosmetic applied to the lips to add shine and color.|例句：She applied a shimmering lipgloss before going out. 她出门前涂了闪亮的唇彩。|例句：The lipgloss made her lips look fuller. 这款唇蜜让她的嘴唇看起来更丰满了。|近义词：lip shine, lip gloss, lip lacquer|反义词：lipstick (in terms of texture and finish)
