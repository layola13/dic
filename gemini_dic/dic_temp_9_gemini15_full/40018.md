consumed:/kənˈsuːmd/ | v. | 消耗；吃完；吞噬；使沉迷 | To use up (resources, time, etc.), to eat or drink something, to completely absorb someone's attention or interest. |例句：He consumed a large amount of coffee. 他喝掉了大量的咖啡。

例句：She was consumed by guilt after the accident. 事故后她被负罪感吞噬。


近义词：used up, eaten, devoured, absorbed | 反义词：produced, created, saved


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He consumes a lot of energy. = 他消耗很多能量。
She consumes a whole pizza every week. = 她每周吃掉一个披萨。


一般过去时:
They consumed all the food at the party. = 他们在派对上吃光了所有的食物。
He consumed the entire book in one sitting. = 他一口气读完了整本书。


现在完成时:
We have consumed all the milk. = 我们已经喝光了所有的牛奶。
She has consumed a great deal of knowledge in her studies. = 通过学习，她已经吸收了大量的知识。


过去完成时:
They had consumed all the supplies before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，他们已经消耗了所有的物资。
She had consumed a large amount of alcohol before driving. = 她在开车前喝了很多酒。


将来时:
He will consume all his savings on the trip. = 他将在旅行中花光所有的积蓄。
They will consume a vast amount of resources to build the project. = 他们将消耗大量资源来建设这个项目。


虚拟语气:
If he consumed less sugar, he would be healthier. = 如果他少吃糖，他会更健康。
I wish I hadn't consumed so much chocolate yesterday. = 我真希望昨天我没有吃那么多巧克力。


被动语态:
All the resources were consumed in the fire. = 所有的资源都被大火吞噬了。
A large amount of energy is consumed by the factory. = 工厂消耗了大量的能量。


疑问句:
How much water do you consume daily? = 你每天喝多少水？
How many calories did you consume today? = 你今天摄入了多少卡路里？


否定句:
He didn't consume any alcohol at the party. = 他在派对上没有喝酒。
They haven't consumed all the supplies yet. = 他们还没有用完所有的物资。


PHRASE->
be consumed with = 被…所吞噬
consumed by = 被…消耗
consume oneself with =  为…而苦恼
consume time = 消耗时间
consume resources = 消耗资源
