oleaginosity:/ˌoʊliədʒɪˈnɒsəti/| n. |油性；油脂；油腻|The quality or state of being oleaginous; oiliness.  It refers to the characteristic of being oily or greasy.  This can describe a substance's physical properties or, figuratively, a person's overly smooth or suave manner. |例句：The oleaginosity of the olive oil made it perfect for frying.  橄榄油的油性使其非常适合煎炸。

The high oleaginosity of the seeds made them valuable for oil production.  种子的高油性使其成为有价值的油料作物。


近义词：oiliness, greasiness, unctuousness|反义词：dryness, aridity


词源:From Latin *oleaginosus, from oleum ("oil") + -ginosus ("producing").  The suffix "-osity" indicates a state or quality.
