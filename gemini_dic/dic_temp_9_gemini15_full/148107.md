oversecretions: /ˌoʊvərsiˈkriːʃənz/ | n. |分泌过多，过度分泌 | Excessive secretion of a bodily substance, often indicating a medical condition.  The term usually refers to mucus, sweat, or other fluids. |例句：Excessive coughing may be a symptom of oversecretions in the lungs.  咳嗽过多可能是肺部分泌过多的一种症状。

The patient's oversecretions led to respiratory distress. 病人的分泌过多导致呼吸窘迫。|近义词：hypersecretion, excessive secretion |反义词：hyposecretion, insufficient secretion |
