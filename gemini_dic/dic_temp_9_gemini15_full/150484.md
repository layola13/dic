pantingly:/ˈpæn(t)ɪŋli/| adv.|喘着气地，气喘吁吁地|Breathing heavily, usually due to exertion or excitement.|例句：He arrived pantingly at the finish line. 他气喘吁吁地到达了终点线。

例句：The dog ran pantingly after the ball. 那条狗喘着粗气追逐着球。


近义词：breathlessly, gaspingly, puffingly|反义词：calmly, serenely


词源：源自动词pant，意为“喘气”，加上副词后缀-ingly构成。pant本身源自古法语pantan，意为“to stretch out, to spread”。
