repainting:/ˌriːˈpeɪntɪŋ/| v. n.|重新粉刷；再绘画|The act of painting something again.  It can refer to painting a surface again to refresh its appearance or to correct flaws, or to creating a new artwork based on a previous one.|例句：The house needs repainting. 这房子需要重新粉刷。

The artist is considering a repainting of his famous landscape.  这位艺术家正在考虑重新创作他著名的风景画。|近义词：redecorating, renovating, restoring|反义词：painting (for the first time)


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He repaints the fence every spring. 他每年春天都重新粉刷栅栏。
They are repainting the kitchen. 他们正在重新粉刷厨房。

一般过去时:
She repainted the bedroom last weekend. 她上周末重新粉刷了卧室。
We repainted the car after the accident. 事故后我们把车重新喷漆了。


现在完成时:
They have repainted the whole building. 他们已经重新粉刷了整栋楼。
I have repainted the door twice this year. 今年我已经两次重新粉刷了门。


过去完成时:
He had repainted the furniture before we moved in. 我们搬进来之前，他已经重新粉刷了家具。
She had repainted the mural several times before it was finally displayed. 在壁画最终展出之前，她已经重新粉刷了好几次。


将来时:
We will repaint the living room next month. 下个月我们将重新粉刷客厅。
They will be repainting the exterior of the house soon. 他们很快就会重新粉刷房子的外部。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would repaint the bathroom. 如果我有时间，我会重新粉刷浴室。
It would be nice if they repainted the whole house. 如果他们能重新粉刷整栋房子就好了。


被动语态:
The walls were repainted last year. 墙壁是去年重新粉刷的。
The car is being repainted at the moment. 汽车正在重新喷漆。


疑问句:
Will you be repainting the fence this year? 你今年会重新粉刷栅栏吗？
Have they repainted the kitchen yet? 他们已经重新粉刷厨房了吗？


否定句:
I didn't repaint the kitchen this year. 今年我没有重新粉刷厨房。
They haven't repainted the shed yet. 他们还没有重新粉刷棚屋。
