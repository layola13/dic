refracture:/ˌriːˈfrækʧər/| vt.|再骨折；再次断裂|To fracture (a bone, etc.) again.  This term implies a previous fracture that has either not healed properly or has been re-injured.|例句：He refractured his leg during a skiing accident. 他在一次滑雪事故中再次摔断了腿。

例句：The already weakened bone refractured under the strain.  这块已经脆弱的骨头在压力下再次断裂。


近义词：refracture, break again, re-injure


反义词：heal, mend, fuse


词性:动词
