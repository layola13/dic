inter-quarter:/ˌɪntərˈkwɔːrtər/ | adj. | 季度间的，季度之间的 | Relating to the period between two consecutive financial quarters.  This term is primarily used in finance and accounting contexts. | Example: The company experienced significant inter-quarter growth. 这家公司经历了显著的季度间增长。 | 近义词：between-quarters | 反义词：intra-quarter (within a quarter) |  形容词

Simple Sentence:

WORD ORIGIN->

The word "inter-quarter" is a compound word formed from:

* **inter-**:  A prefix meaning "between" or "among".  It comes from Latin *inter*.
* **quarter**: Refers to a period of three months, often used in financial reporting.  It's derived from the Old French *quart*, meaning "fourth".


Therefore, "inter-quarter" literally means "between quarters" in a financial context.
