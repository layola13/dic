overtaken:/ˌoʊvərˈteɪkən/| v.|超过，赶上，突然袭击|To surpass or go beyond in speed, amount, or achievement.  It can also mean to unexpectedly affect or overcome someone.|例句：The faster car overtaken the slower one.  更快的那辆车超过了较慢的那辆车。

The news of his death overtaken me with sadness.  他去世的消息让我悲伤不已。|近义词：surpass, outstrip, overtake, catch up with|反义词：lag behind, fall behind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overtakes me on the highway every morning. 他每天早上都在高速公路上超我。
The company overtakes its competitors in sales. 这家公司在销售额上超过了竞争对手。

一般过去时:
She was overtaken by a wave of emotion. 她被一阵情绪冲昏了头脑。
The police overtaken the thief. 警察抓住了小偷。

现在完成时:
The new technology has overtaken the old methods. 新技术已经取代了旧方法。
We have been overtaken by events. 我们被事态的发展所超越了。

过去完成时:
By the time I reached the finish line, I had been overtaken by two runners. 当我到达终点线时，我已经被两名跑步者超过了。
They had been overtaken by a sudden storm. 他们遭遇了突如其来的暴风雨。

将来时:
The young company expects to overtake its rivals soon. 这家年轻的公司预计很快就会超过其竞争对手。
I think electric cars will overtake gasoline cars in the future. 我认为电动汽车将来会超过汽油汽车。

虚拟语气:
If he had tried harder, he might have overtaken his opponent. 如果他更努力一些，他可能会超过他的对手。
I wish I hadn't been overtaken by fear. 我真希望自己没有被恐惧所吞噬。


被动语态:
He was overtaken by a feeling of despair. 他被绝望的感觉所淹没。
Our progress was overtaken by unforeseen circumstances. 我们的进展被不可预见的情况所阻碍。

疑问句:
Were you overtaken by the other car? 你被另一辆车超了吗？
Has the company been overtaken by its competitors? 这家公司被竞争对手超越了吗？

否定句:
He didn't overtake the leading runner. 他没有超过领先的跑步者。
The small business didn't overtake the market leader. 这家小企业没有超过市场领导者。
