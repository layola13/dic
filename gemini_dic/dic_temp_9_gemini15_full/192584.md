sculpture:/ˈskʌlptʃər/| n.|雕塑，雕刻|The art or practice of creating three-dimensional forms or figures by carving, modeling, welding, or assembling materials.|The museum boasts a stunning collection of sculptures from various eras.  博物馆拥有来自不同时代的令人惊叹的雕塑收藏。|The ancient statue is a fine example of sculpture.  这尊古代雕像是一个精美的雕塑范例。|carving, carving, molding|destruction, demolition|名词

He creates beautiful sculptures. = 他创作美丽的雕塑。

一般过去时:
Michelangelo sculpted the David. = 米开朗基罗雕刻了大卫像。
