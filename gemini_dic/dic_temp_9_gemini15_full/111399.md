livingness: /ˈlɪvɪŋnəs/ | n. | 活力；生动性；生命力 | The quality or state of being alive; the quality of being vivid or lively.  It refers to the characteristic of something that is alive or full of life and energy. |


例句：

The painting captured the livingness of the subject. = 这幅画捕捉到了人物的活力。

The livingness of the city amazed the visitor. = 城市的活力让游客惊叹不已。


近义词：vitality, vibrancy, animation, liveliness, aliveness

反义词：inertness, lifelessness, stagnation, dormancy
