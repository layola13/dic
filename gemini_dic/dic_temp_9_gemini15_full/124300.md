mothereater:/ˈmʌðərˌiːtər/ | n. |食母者，(尤指)弑母者 | A person who kills their mother.  The term is often used figuratively to describe someone who is extremely destructive or harmful to their own source of support or sustenance. |例句：He was called a mothereater for betraying his own company, which had nurtured his career. 他被称作食母者，因为他背叛了自己的公司，而这家公司培养了他的事业。

例句：The ruthless businessman was seen as a mothereater, exploiting his employees and leaving them with nothing.  这个无情的商人被看作是一个食母者，他剥削他的员工，让他们一无所有。


近义词：parricide (弑亲者), destroyer, ruiner |反义词：benefactor, protector, nurturer |词性：名词
