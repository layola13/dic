deejay:/ˈdiːdʒeɪ/| n. |迪斯科唱片骑师，DJ | A person who plays recorded music (especially at a disco or club).  A deejay selects and plays music, often mixing tracks together to create a continuous flow. They may also use other audio effects. | Example: The deejay spun the latest hits all night long.  这个DJ整晚都在播放最新的热门歌曲。The club hired a famous deejay for the anniversary party.  俱乐部为周年庆典请了一位著名的DJ。|近义词：disc jockey, music mixer, selector |反义词: listener, audience member
