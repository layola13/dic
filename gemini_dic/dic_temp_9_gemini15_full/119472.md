micrometry:/maɪˈkrɒmətri/ | n. |显微测微术；显微测量法 |The science or art of measuring very small objects or distances, typically using a microscope.  It involves the use of specialized instruments like micrometers to obtain precise measurements.  |例句：Micrometry is crucial in fields like biology and materials science for characterizing microscopic structures. 显微测微术在生物学和材料科学等领域对于表征微观结构至关重要。

例句：The researchers used micrometry to determine the exact size of the pollen grains. 研究人员使用显微测微术来确定花粉粒的确切大小。


近义词：microscopic measurement, micro-measurement |反义词：macrometry (measurement of large objects)
