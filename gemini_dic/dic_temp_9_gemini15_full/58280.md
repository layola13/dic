drunkish:/ˈdrʌŋkɪʃ/| adj.| 微醉的；有点醉的 | Slightly drunk; showing the effects of having drunk some alcohol.  |例句：He was feeling a little drunkish after two glasses of wine. 他喝了两杯酒后感觉有点微醉。|例句：She walked with a drunkish stagger. 她走路摇摇晃晃的，像喝醉了一样。|近义词：tipsy, slightly intoxicated, inebriated (less common in this context)|反义词：sober, clear-headed
