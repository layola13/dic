double-checks:/ˈdʌbəl tʃɛks/ | vt. |复查，仔细检查|To check something again to ensure accuracy or completeness.  It implies a more thorough or cautious check than a single check.|例句：The accountant double-checks all the figures before submitting the report. = 会计在提交报告之前仔细核对所有数字。

例句：  She double-checked the flight schedule to make sure she hadn't missed anything. = 她仔细核对航班时刻表，确保自己没有错过任何信息。


近义词：recheck, verify, review, confirm | 反义词：neglect, overlook


USAGE->

She double-checks the spelling before sending emails. = 她发邮件前都会仔细检查拼写。
