dimensionality:/ˌdaɪmɛnʃənˈælɪti/| n. |维度；维数|The number of dimensions something has.  It refers to the number of independent variables needed to specify a point in a space. In mathematics and physics, it often refers to the number of coordinates needed to specify a position. In data analysis, it refers to the number of features or variables in a dataset.|例句：The dimensionality of the problem makes it difficult to solve.  这个问题的维度使其难以解决。

The high dimensionality of the data set required advanced dimensionality reduction techniques. 数据集的高维度需要先进的降维技术。|近义词：dimension, size, extent|反义词：point, singularity
