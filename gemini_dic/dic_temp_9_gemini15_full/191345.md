scepters:/ˈsɛptərz/| n. |权杖；统治的象征|A scepter is a staff or rod carried by a monarch as a symbol of royal authority and power. It is often ornately decorated and made of precious materials.  |Example: The queen held her scepter aloft as she addressed the crowd. 女王高举权杖向人群致辞。|近义词：staff, rod, wand |反义词：无
