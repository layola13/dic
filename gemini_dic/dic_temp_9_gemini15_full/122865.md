mollified:/ˈmɑːlɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 安抚的；平息的；缓和的 | Soothed in temper or disposition; appeased.  |例句：The angry customer was mollified by the manager's apology.  经理的道歉使愤怒的顾客平静下来了。 |例句：Her fears were mollified by the doctor's reassuring words. 医生令人安心的言语减轻了她的恐惧。|近义词： appeased, pacified, soothed, calmed |反义词：agitated, angered, provoked, inflamed

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mollifies his clients with gifts. = 他用礼物安抚他的客户。
The sweet music mollifies my anxiety. = 甜美的音乐抚平了我的焦虑。

一般过去时:
She mollified her angry boss with a sincere apology. = 她以真诚的道歉平息了愤怒的老板。
The teacher mollified the upset student with a kind explanation. = 老师用善意的解释安抚了沮丧的学生。

现在完成时:
The government has mollified the protestors with new policies. = 政府已通过新政策安抚了抗议者。
He has mollified his wife with flowers and chocolates. = 他用鲜花和巧克力安抚了他的妻子。

过去完成时:
She had mollified the situation with a thoughtful gesture. = 她曾用体贴的举动缓和了局势。
They had mollified their differences through careful negotiation. = 他们通过仔细协商化解了分歧。

将来时:
I hope to mollify the situation before it escalates. = 我希望在情况恶化之前能够缓和局势。
He will mollify his neighbors by fixing the fence. = 他将通过修理围栏来安抚他的邻居。

虚拟语气:
If I had apologized earlier, I could have mollified him. = 如果我早点道歉，我就能安抚他了。
If she were to offer a compromise, she might mollify the opposition. = 如果她提出妥协，她可能会安抚反对派。

被动语态:
The angry crowd was mollified by the police's intervention. = 愤怒的人群被警方的干预平息了。
The situation was mollified by his timely intervention. = 局势因他的及时干预而得到缓和。

疑问句:
How can we mollify the situation? = 我们如何才能缓和局势？
What can be done to mollify the stakeholders? = 可以采取什么措施来安抚利益相关者？

否定句:
His apology did not mollify her. = 他的道歉并没有安抚她。
The company's response failed to mollify the public. = 公司的回应未能安抚公众。

词源:
From Middle French *mollifier, from Latin mollire "to soften," from mollis "soft."  The -fy suffix adds the sense of making something soft or gentle.
