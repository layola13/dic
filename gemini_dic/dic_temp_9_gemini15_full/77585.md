geddit:/ˈɡɛdɪt/| v.|明白了，懂了|  A shortened, informal version of "got it," indicating understanding. It's primarily used in spoken English and informal writing, often among younger generations or in online communication.  | Example: "I explained the instructions again, and finally, he geddit."  "我再次解释了说明，最后他明白了。" |近义词: got it, understand, grasped|反义词: don't understand, confused
