finds:/faɪndz/| v.|发现，找到；查明；认为；判决|To discover something or someone unexpectedly or during a search; to determine or establish something; to consider or judge something in a particular way.|例句：I found my keys under the couch. 我在沙发底下找到了我的钥匙。

例句：The jury found him guilty. 陪审团判定他有罪。


近义词：discover, locate, determine, conclude|反义词：lose, miss


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He finds his work challenging. = 他觉得他的工作很有挑战性。
She finds happiness in simple things. = 她从简单的事情中找到快乐。

一般过去时:
They found a lost puppy in the park. = 他们在公园里发现了一只走失的小狗。
