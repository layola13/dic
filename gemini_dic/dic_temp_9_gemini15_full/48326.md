decried:/dɪˈkraɪd/| vt.|谴责；批评；贬低|To express strong disapproval of; condemn.|The protesters decried the government's new policies.  抗议者谴责政府的新政策。|The critics decried the film as tasteless and offensive. 批评家谴责这部电影庸俗和令人反感。| denounced, condemned, criticized|praised, lauded, commended|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They decry the injustice of the system. = 他们谴责这个制度的不公正。
She decries the lack of opportunities for young people. = 她谴责年轻人缺乏机会。


一般过去时:
He decried the violence in the streets. = 他谴责街头的暴力事件。
The newspaper decried the politician's actions. = 报纸谴责了这位政治家的行为。


现在完成时:
Activists have decried the environmental damage for years. = 多年来，活动家们一直在谴责环境破坏。
The public has decried the company's unethical practices. = 公众谴责了这家公司的违反道德的行为。


过去完成时:
They had decried the decision before the meeting even started. = 甚至在会议开始之前，他们就已经谴责了这个决定。
The community had decried the proposed development for months. = 社区几个月来一直在谴责拟议的开发项目。


将来时:

He will decry the government's handling of the crisis. = 他将谴责政府处理危机的做法。


虚拟语气:
If they knew the truth, they would decry the actions taken. = 如果他们知道真相，他们就会谴责所采取的行动。
I wish they would decry the unfair treatment of workers. = 我希望他们能谴责对工人的不公平待遇。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
They did not decry the decision. = 他们没有谴责这个决定。
