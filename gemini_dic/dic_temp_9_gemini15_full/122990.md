monandrous:/mɒˈnændrəs/ | adj. | 单雄蕊的；单雄蕊植物的 | Having only one stamen.  This term is primarily used in botany to describe flowers or plants with a single stamen.  | Example:  The *monandrous* flower had a single prominent stamen.  单雄蕊的花有一根突出的雄蕊。| 近义词:  uniandrous (less common) | 反义词: polyandrous (many stamens)
