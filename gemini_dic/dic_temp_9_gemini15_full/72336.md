flowchart:/ˈfloʊtʃɑːrt/ | n. |流程图，流程表 | A diagram showing the steps in a process or system.  It uses standardized symbols to represent actions, decisions, and data. | Example:  We created a flowchart to visualize the software development process. 我们制作了一个流程图来形象化软件开发流程。|近义词：diagram, process chart, workflow diagram |反义词：None

词源:
The word "flowchart" is a compound word.  "Flow" refers to the movement or progression of something, while "chart" refers to a diagram or graphical representation.  Therefore, a flowchart literally means a diagram showing the flow of a process.
