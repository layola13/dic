liman:/ˈlaɪmən/| n.|海湾；港湾；避风港；|A liman is a sheltered body of water, typically a bay or inlet, where ships can find protection from storms and rough seas.  It often refers to a coastal lagoon or estuary.  The term is primarily used in the Black Sea region and other parts of Eastern Europe.|例句：The ships sought refuge in the liman during the storm.  暴风雨期间，船只在海湾中寻求庇护。|例句：The calm waters of the liman provided a safe anchorage for the fishing fleet. 海湾平静的水域为渔船提供了安全的停泊地。|近义词：bay, inlet, harbor, cove, estuary |反义词：open sea, ocean
