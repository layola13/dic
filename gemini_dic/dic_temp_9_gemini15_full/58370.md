dualism:/ˈduːəlɪzəm/| n. |二元论，二元性 |The philosophical theory that reality consists of two fundamental entities, such as mind and matter, or good and evil.  It often involves a contrast between two opposing forces or principles.  |例句：Many religions embrace a form of dualism, contrasting good and evil.  许多宗教都包含某种形式的二元论，对比善与恶。

例句： Cartesian dualism posits a fundamental distinction between mind and body.  笛卡尔二元论认为精神和物质之间存在根本的区别。


近义词：dichotomy, duality, bipolarity |反义词：monism, unity, oneness
