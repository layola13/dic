helicotrema:/ˌhɛlɪˈkoʊtrɪmə/| n. |蜗牛孔；耳蜗顶端|The helicotrema is the apex of the cochlea, where the scala vestibuli and scala tympani meet.  It is a small opening that connects the two fluid-filled spaces within the cochlea.  Sound vibrations traveling through the fluid can pass through the helicotrema.|The helicotrema plays a crucial role in the transmission of sound vibrations within the inner ear.|近义词：cochlear apex|反义词：无
