centrism:/ˈsɛntrɪzəm/| n. |中间立场；中间主义|The political doctrine of centrism emphasizes moderation and pragmatism, seeking compromise between extreme viewpoints.|例句：His political views are characterized by centrism, avoiding the extremes of both left and right wing ideologies. 他的政治观点以中间立场为特征，避免了左右两翼意识形态的极端。|近义词：moderation, middle-of-the-road, centrist politics|反义词：extremism, radicalism
