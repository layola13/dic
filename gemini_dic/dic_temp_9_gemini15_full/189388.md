sagoin:/səˈɡɔɪn/ | n. | 僧帽猴 | A small monkey of the family Callitrichidae, characterized by their small size, prehensile tails, and often brightly colored fur.  They are found in the tropical forests of Central and South America. | Example: The sagoin leaped nimbly through the branches.  这只僧帽猴灵活地跳跃在树枝之间。 | 近义词: marmoset, monkey | 反义词: None
