prelecturing: /ˌpriːˈlɛktʃərɪŋ/ | v. | 预先讲课；事先训斥 |  The act of lecturing beforehand; giving a lecture or instruction in advance.  It implies a somewhat didactic or condescending tone. |例句：He was prelecturing the students on the importance of punctuality before the exam. 他在考试前预先告诫学生准时到场的重要性。


例句：The professor was prelecturing his students about the upcoming assignment, ensuring they understood the expectations. 教授正在预先向学生讲解即将到来的作业，确保他们理解要求。


近义词：lecturing beforehand, pre-teaching, instructing beforehand, admonishing in advance


反义词：post-lecturing, explaining afterward


词性：动词
