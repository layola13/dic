lodestones: /ˈloʊdstəʊnz/ | n. | 指南针石，吸铁石 | A naturally magnetized piece of iron ore (magnetite) that attracts iron.  Lodestones were historically used as the basis for early compasses. | Example: The ancient mariners relied on lodestones for navigation.  古代水手依靠指南针石进行导航。 | 近义词：magnet, magnetite | 反义词： (no direct antonym, but could consider something that repels iron) | 名词
