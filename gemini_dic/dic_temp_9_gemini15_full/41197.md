coquelicot:/ˌkoʊklɪˈkoʊ/|/ˈkɒklɪkɒt/| n. |虞美人；罂粟花 |A coquelicot is a common name for the corn poppy (Papaver rhoeas), a bright red flower often found in fields of grain.  It is known for its delicate, crepe-like petals and vibrant color. |The field was ablaze with coquelicots.|近义词：corn poppy, red poppy |反义词：N/A
