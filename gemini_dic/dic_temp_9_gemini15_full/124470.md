mouflon:/ˈmuːflɒn/| n. |欧穆弗隆羊，欧洲盘羊|A mouflon is a wild sheep, typically with a reddish-brown coat and curved horns.  Mouflons are found in various parts of Europe and Asia. They are considered the ancestor of many domestic sheep breeds.|Example: The Corsican mouflon is a subspecies found on the island of Corsica.|近义词：wild sheep,  ram|反义词：domestic sheep
