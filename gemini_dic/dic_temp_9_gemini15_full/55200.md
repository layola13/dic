dissert:/dɪˈsɜːrt/ | vt. |详述；论述；撰写论文 | To discuss or write about a topic in detail, especially in a formal way.  |He will dissert on the topic of artificial intelligence. 他将详细论述人工智能这个话题。 | discuss, expound, elaborate |  condense, summarize |动词

词源:
源自拉丁语 *dissertare，意思是“讨论，谈论”。  It's related to the Latin word *sermo, meaning "speech" or "conversation."
