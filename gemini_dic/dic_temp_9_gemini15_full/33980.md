cisterns:/ˈsɪstərnz/| n.|蓄水池；水箱|A cistern is a tank for storing water, especially one built underground.  They are often used to collect rainwater or to store water for later use. |例句：The old house had a large cistern in the basement to collect rainwater. 这栋老房子地下室有一个大型蓄水池用来收集雨水。


例句：The city relies on a network of cisterns for its water supply. 这个城市依靠一个蓄水池网络来供水。


近义词：reservoir, tank, container|反义词：N/A

词源:  The word "cistern" comes from the Old French "cistérne," which ultimately derives from the Latin "cisterna," meaning "a tank" or "reservoir."  The Latin word is likely related to the Latin word "cista," meaning "box" or "chest."
