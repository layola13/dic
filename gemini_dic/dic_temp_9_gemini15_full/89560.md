honors:/ˈɑːnərz/ | n. |荣誉，尊敬，优等成绩 | Honors are given to those who deserve them. 荣誉给予那些应得的人。  |近义词：awards, distinctions, accolades |反义词：dishonor, disgrace, shame |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He honors his commitments. = 他信守承诺。
She honors her parents. = 她孝敬父母。


一般过去时:
They honored the agreement. = 他们遵守了协议。
The company honored its debt. = 公司偿还了债务。


现在完成时:
We have honored our guests. = 我们已经款待了我们的客人。
She has been honored with an award. = 她获得了奖项的殊荣。


过去完成时:
They had honored their promise. = 他们已经履行了诺言。
The school had already honored the student's achievements. = 学校已经表彰了这位学生取得的成就。


将来时:
They will honor the contract. = 他们将履行合同。
The university will honor the graduates. = 大学将表彰毕业生。


虚拟语气:
If they honored their word, things would be different. = 如果他们遵守诺言，情况就会不一样。
I wish they honored my request. = 我希望他们能满足我的请求。


被动语态:

The soldiers were honored for their bravery. = 这些士兵因其勇敢而受到表彰。


疑问句:
Will they honor the invitation? = 他们会接受邀请吗？
Does she honor her commitments? = 她信守承诺吗？


否定句:
He didn't honor his promise. = 他没有遵守诺言。
