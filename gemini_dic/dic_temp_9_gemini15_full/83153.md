gutted:/ˈɡʌtɪd/| adj. v.|掏空的；极度失望的；彻底破坏的|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "gutted" means deeply disappointed or devastated.  It can also describe something that has been emptied of its contents, or severely damaged or destroyed. The verb form refers to the act of removing the internal organs of something (usually an animal) or the act of completely destroying or ruining something.

例句:
I was gutted when I didn't get the job. = 我没得到那份工作时非常沮丧。

He was gutted to hear about the accident. = 他听到事故的消息后非常难过。


近义词:
devastated, heartbroken, destroyed, ruined, emptied, eviscerated

反义词:
elated, joyful, thrilled, intact, undamaged


词性:
adjective, verb
