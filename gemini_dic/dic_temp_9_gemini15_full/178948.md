redeclare:/ˌriːˈdɪklɛər/| vt.|重新声明，再次声明|To declare something again, especially after it has been previously declared or defined.  This often involves overriding a previous declaration.|The programmer had to redeclare the variable after encountering a compilation error.  程序员在遇到编译错误后不得不重新声明变量。

The function was redeclared with a different return type.  该函数被重新声明为不同的返回类型。|redefine, restate|undefine|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He redeclares his love for her every day. 他每天都重新表达他对她的爱意。
The company redeclares its commitment to sustainability. 公司再次声明其对可持续发展的承诺。

一般过去时:
She redeclared her innocence to the police. 她向警方再次声明自己的清白。
The government redeclared a state of emergency. 政府再次宣布进入紧急状态。

现在完成时:
He has redeclared his candidacy for mayor. 他已经再次宣布竞选市长。
The court has redeclared the law unconstitutional. 法院已再次宣布该法违宪。

过去完成时:
They had redeclared their support for the project before the funding was secured. 在资金到位之前，他们已经再次表示支持该项目。
The committee had redeclared its position on the matter after further investigation.  委员会在进一步调查后再次声明其立场。

将来时:

The company will redeclare its profits next quarter. 公司将在下个季度重新申报其利润。

虚拟语气:
If he redeclared his intentions, she might reconsider. 如果他再次表明他的意图，她可能会重新考虑。
I wish he had redeclared his love for me before he passed away. 我希望他在去世前能再次向我表达爱意。

被动语态:

否定句:
He did not redeclare his assets. 他没有重新申报他的资产。
