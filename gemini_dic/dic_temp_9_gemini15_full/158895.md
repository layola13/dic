plundered:/ˈplʌndərd/| v.|洗劫，掠夺，抢劫 | to steal goods violently from a place, especially during a war or other disorder |例句：The invaders plundered the city, stealing everything of value. 侵略者洗劫了这座城市，偷走了所有值钱的东西。|近义词：rob, loot, ransack, pillage |反义词：protect, preserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pirates plunder coastal towns. = 海盗掠夺沿海城镇。
They plunder the resources of the poor. = 他们掠夺穷人的资源。

一般过去时:
The Vikings plundered many villages. = 维京人洗劫了许多村庄。
The army plundered the defeated city. = 军队洗劫了战败的城市。

现在完成时:
The thieves have plundered the museum. = 小偷已经洗劫了博物馆。
They have plundered the natural resources of the region. = 他们已经掠夺了该地区的自然资源。

过去完成时:
The bandits had plundered the caravan before the soldiers arrived. = 在士兵到达之前，强盗已经洗劫了商队。
The invaders had plundered the capital before the king surrendered. = 在国王投降之前，侵略者已经洗劫了首都。

将来时:
They will plunder the next town they come across. = 他们将会掠夺下一个遇到的城镇。
The rebels will plunder the wealthy districts. = 反叛者将会洗劫富人区。

虚拟语气:
If the army had not arrived, the rebels would have plundered the village. = 如果军队没有到达，叛乱分子就会洗劫村庄。
I wish they hadn't plundered my home. = 我真希望他们没有洗劫我的家。

被动语态:
The city was plundered by the enemy. = 城市被敌人洗劫了。
The ancient tomb was plundered centuries ago. = 古墓几个世纪前就被洗劫了。

疑问句:
Was the village plundered? = 村庄被洗劫了吗？
Have the treasures been plundered? = 宝藏被洗劫了吗？

否定句:
The soldiers did not plunder the town. = 士兵没有洗劫城镇。
They did not plunder the civilians. = 他们没有掠夺平民。
