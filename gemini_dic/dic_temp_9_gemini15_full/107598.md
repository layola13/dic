landslide:/ˈlændˌslaɪd/| n.|山崩，滑坡；压倒性胜利|A landslide is a mass of rock and earth moving down a slope.  It can also refer to a victory by a very large margin.|例句：The heavy rain caused a devastating landslide.  大雨引发了破坏性山崩。

例句：The candidate won the election in a landslide victory.  这位候选人在选举中以压倒性优势获胜。|近义词：landslip, avalanche, rout,  sweep |反义词：narrow victory, defeat
