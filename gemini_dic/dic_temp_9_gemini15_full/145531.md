overcoming: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌmɪŋ/ | v. | 克服，战胜 |  The act of successfully dealing with and controlling a problem or difficulty.  It emphasizes the process of conquering a challenge. | 例句：Overcoming her fear of public speaking was a major accomplishment. 克服她对公开演讲的恐惧是一项重大成就。

She is overcoming her shyness gradually. 她正在逐渐克服她的害羞。


近义词：conquering, mastering, surmounting, triumphing over | 反义词：succumbing to, yielding to, giving in to | 动词
