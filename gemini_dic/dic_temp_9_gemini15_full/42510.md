counterintelligence:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns/| n. |反间谍情报；反情报工作 |The activities undertaken to protect an organization from espionage, sabotage, subversion, and other forms of hostile intelligence activities.  Counterintelligence involves detecting, identifying, and neutralizing threats to national security posed by foreign intelligence services and other adversaries. |例句：The agency's counterintelligence efforts thwarted a major terrorist plot.  该机构的反情报工作挫败了一场重大的恐怖袭击阴谋。

例句：The counterintelligence operation successfully exposed a network of spies.  反情报行动成功地揭露了一个间谍网络。


近义词：espionage countermeasures, intelligence security, anti-espionage


反义词：espionage, intelligence gathering


词性: 名词
