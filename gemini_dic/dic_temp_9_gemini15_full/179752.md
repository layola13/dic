reescalation: /ˌriːˌɛskəˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 重新升级；再次升级；(问题的)再次恶化 | The act of escalating something again, typically a conflict or problem, after a previous attempt at de-escalation has failed.  It implies a renewed intensification or worsening of the situation. | 例句：The reescalation of the conflict led to further casualties. 冲突的再次升级导致了更多伤亡。

例句：After a period of relative calm, the reescalation of tensions between the two countries was alarming.  一段相对平静的时期之后，两国之间紧张局势的再次升级令人担忧。


近义词：re-intensification, exacerbation, resurgence, renewal


反义词：de-escalation, resolution, settlement


词性：名词
