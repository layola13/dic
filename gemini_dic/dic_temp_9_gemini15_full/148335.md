overspan:/ˈoʊvərspæn/| vt. |超过；跨越；胜过；(桥梁等)跨越（一定距离）|To extend or reach beyond the limits of; to exceed.  Also refers to a structure (like a bridge) extending over a certain distance.|例句：The bridge overspans the river. 这座桥跨越了这条河。
例句：His ambition overspans his abilities. 他的雄心壮志超过了他的能力。|近义词：extend, exceed, surpass, span|反义词：undershoot, fall short of|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bridge overspans the river. = 这座桥跨越了这条河。
The project overspans our current budget. = 这个项目超出了我们目前的预算。


一般过去时:
The old bridge overspanned the narrow creek. = 那座旧桥跨越了狭窄的小溪。
His influence overspanned several generations. = 他的影响力跨越了好几代人。


现在完成时:
The company has overspanned its resources in this project. = 公司在这个项目中过度消耗了资源。
The effects of the policy have overspanned the initial expectations. = 政策的影响超出了最初的预期。


过去完成时:
The architect had overspanned the design, leading to structural problems. = 建筑师的设计过于夸张，导致了结构问题。
The team had overspanned their capabilities, resulting in missed deadlines. = 团队能力不足，导致错过截止日期。


将来时:
The new bridge will overspan the entire bay. = 新桥将跨越整个海湾。
The demands of the job will overspan his free time. = 这份工作要求会占用他的空闲时间。


虚拟语气:
If the bridge overspanned the entire valley, it would be a magnificent sight. = 如果桥梁跨越整个山谷，那将是一道壮丽的景象。
If he had not overspanned his budget, he wouldn't be in debt. = 如果他没有超出预算，他就不会负债。


被动语态:
The river is overspanned by a magnificent bridge. = 这条河被一座宏伟的桥梁跨越。
The available resources were overspanned by the ambitious project. = 雄心勃勃的项目超出了可用资源。


疑问句:
Does the bridge overspan the entire canyon? = 这座桥跨越了整个峡谷吗？
Does the project overspan the allocated budget? = 这个项目是否超支了？


否定句:
The bridge doesn't overspan the entire river. = 这座桥没有跨越整条河。
The project did not overspan the deadline. = 这个项目没有超过截止日期。
