decommissions:/ˌdiːkəˈmɪʃən/ | vt. | 使退役；使停止服役；报废 | To officially remove something, especially a piece of military equipment or a nuclear power plant, from active service.  |例句：The navy decommissioned the old warship last year.  海军去年退役了那艘旧军舰。

The government plans to decommission the aging nuclear power plant. 政府计划关闭这座老化的核电站。| 近义词：retire, disband, scrap | 反义词：commission
