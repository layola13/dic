hypogeous:/haɪˈpɒdʒiəs/ | adj. | 地下的；土中的 |  Growing or occurring beneath the surface of the ground.  This term is typically used to describe plants whose seeds germinate and develop underground. | Example:  Many types of fungi have hypogeous fruiting bodies.  许多种类的真菌具有地下子实体。


The peanut plant is a common example of a hypogeous plant. 花生植物是地下植物的常见例子。

近义词: subterranean, underground

反义词: epigeous, aboveground
