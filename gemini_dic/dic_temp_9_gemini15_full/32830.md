choky:/ˈtʃoʊki/ | adj. | 令人窒息的；哽咽的；令人作呕的 |  Feeling as if the throat is blocked; causing a feeling of tightness in the throat.  Also, figuratively, something unpleasant or overwhelming. | 例句：The smoke was choky and made it hard to breathe.  烟雾令人窒息，难以呼吸。 The air in the room was thick and choky with the smell of paint.  房间里的空气又浓又闷，充满了油漆味。 | 近义词：suffocating, stifling, choking, gagging | 反义词：refreshing, breathable
