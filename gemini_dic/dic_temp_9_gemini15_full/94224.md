imperialistically:/ˌɪmpɪəriˈælɪstɪkli/| adv. |帝国主义地，专制地|In a manner characteristic of imperialism; aggressively seeking to extend power and dominion.|例句：The country was ruled imperialistically, with little regard for the people's wishes.  这个国家实行帝国主义统治，很少顾及人民的意愿。|例句：They expanded their empire imperialistically, conquering neighboring territories.他们以帝国主义的方式扩张他们的帝国，征服了邻近的领土。|近义词：dominatingly, autocratically, aggressively |反义词：democratically, peacefully, cooperatively
