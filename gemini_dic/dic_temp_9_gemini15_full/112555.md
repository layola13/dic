lovelier:/ˈlʌvliər/| adj.|更可爱的，更美丽的，更令人愉快的|More lovely; more beautiful; more delightful.  It is the comparative form of "lovely".|例句：The sunset was lovelier than I had imagined.  日落比我想象的更美丽。

例句：Her singing voice is lovelier than ever before. 她的歌声比以往任何时候都更动听。


近义词：more beautiful, more charming, more attractive, prettier

反义词：uglier, less attractive, less charming

词性：形容词
