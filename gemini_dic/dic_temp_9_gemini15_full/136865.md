nonskeptics: /ˈnɑːnˈskɛptɪks/ | n. |非怀疑论者，相信者|People who do not exhibit skepticism; those who readily accept claims without sufficient evidence or critical analysis. They tend to believe things without questioning their validity. | Example:  The nonskeptics readily embraced the new theory without considering the counterarguments.  非怀疑论者欣然接受了新理论，而没有考虑反驳意见。|近义词：believers, credulous people|反义词：skeptics, doubters
