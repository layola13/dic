/ˌpɒlɪsɪˈtɛəriə/| n. |多室菌属|Polycyttaria is a genus of fungi in the family Polyporaceae.  Members of this genus are characterized by their polyporous (many-pored) fruiting bodies, typically found on decaying wood. They are important decomposers in forest ecosystems.
