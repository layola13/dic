miner:/ˈmaɪnər/| n. |矿工，采矿者 |A miner is a person who works in a mine, extracting minerals or other valuable materials from the earth.  |例句：The miners went on strike demanding better wages. 矿工们举行罢工，要求提高工资。

例句：Many miners lost their lives in the mine collapse. 许多矿工在矿难中丧生。


近义词：excavator, digger, quarryman |反义词：None directly applicable.  |词性：名词

词源:
The word "miner" comes from the Old French word "minier," meaning "to mine," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "minera," meaning "mine" or "ore."  The suffix "-er" is a common agent noun suffix in English, indicating the person who performs the action.
