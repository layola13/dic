sippers:/ˈsɪpərz/ | n. | 啜饮者；慢慢饮用的人 | People who sip drinks slowly and delicately.  They often enjoy the taste and experience of the beverage. | Example: The sippers at the wine tasting event carefully evaluated each vintage.  啜饮者在葡萄酒品鉴会上仔细品尝了每一个年份的酒。|近义词：drinkers, tasters | 反义词：gulpers, chuggers

USAGE->

The sippers savor each drop. = 啜饮者细细品味每一滴。
Those sippers enjoy the quiet atmosphere. = 那些啜饮者享受着宁静的氛围。
