numerologies:/nuːˈmɛrɑːlədʒiz/ | n. |数字命理学，数理学 | The study of the mystical significance of numbers.  It involves assigning numerical values to letters and words and interpreting the resulting numbers to reveal hidden meanings or predict future events. | Example:  She was fascinated by numerologies and consulted a numerologist to get insights into her future. 她对数字命理学很着迷，并咨询了一位数字命理学家来了解她的未来。 |近义词：numerical divination, number symbolism | 反义词：无
