semite:/ˈsɛmaɪt/| n. |闪米特人；闪族人 |A member of any of the Semitic peoples, whose languages include Arabic, Hebrew, and Aramaic.  The term is often used in a derogatory and inaccurate manner, especially in reference to Jewish people.| 例句：The professor is an expert in Semitic languages.  教授是闪米特语专家。  |  近义词：Semite (no true synonyms, as it's a broad ethnic group) | 反义词：None (Not an antonym-based word) | 名词
