puffing:/ˈpʌfɪŋ/| v.| 喘气；喷出；夸张地吹嘘 |  The act of breathing heavily, often due to exertion; the act of emitting something in puffs or short bursts; to exaggerate or boast excessively. | 例句：He was puffing from the climb. 他爬山后喘着粗气。  The engine was puffing out black smoke. 发动机喷出黑烟。 He was puffing about his achievements. 他夸夸其谈自己的成就。|近义词：panting, blowing, boasting, bragging | 反义词：whispering, murmuring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He puffs on his pipe. = 他抽着他的烟斗。
She puffs out her chest with pride. = 她骄傲地挺起胸膛。

一般过去时:
The train puffed into the station. = 火车噗噗地驶进车站。
He puffed his cheeks out and blew. = 他鼓起腮帮子吹气。

现在完成时:
They have been puffing away at their work all day. = 他们一整天都在努力工作。
He has puffed himself up to impress her. = 他为了给她留下好印象而吹嘘自己。

过去完成时:

将来时:
The volcano will puff out more ash. = 火山将会喷出更多的火山灰。
He will puff himself up if he wins the prize. = 如果他赢得奖品，他会自吹自擂。

虚拟语气:
