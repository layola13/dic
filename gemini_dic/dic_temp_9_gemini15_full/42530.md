countermanding:/ˌkaʊntəˈmændɪŋ/| adj.|取消的；撤销的|The act of countermanding an order or instruction;  overruling or contradicting a previous order or command.|例句：The general issued a countermanding order, canceling the previous advance.将军发布了一道取消先前推进命令的相反命令。The countermanding instructions caused confusion among the troops. 反复的指示使部队感到混乱。|近义词：revoking, rescinding, annulling|反义词：confirming, ratifying, approving|


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The general is countermanding the previous order. = 将军正在取消先前的命令。
He is countermanding his earlier instructions. = 他正在取消他早些时候的指示。


一般过去时:
The captain countermanded the order to attack. = 船长取消了进攻的命令。
She countermanded the previous instructions. = 她取消了先前的指示。


现在完成时:
They have countermanded the original plan. = 他们已经取消了最初的计划。
The government has countermanded its earlier decision. = 政府已经取消了之前的决定。


过去完成时:
He had countermanded the order before the troops moved. = 部队行动之前，他已经取消了命令。
The manager had countermanded the instructions before the mistake was made. = 在错误发生之前，经理已经取消了指示。


将来时:
The CEO will countermand the project if necessary. = 必要时，首席执行官将取消该项目。
They will countermand the order if the situation changes. = 如果情况发生变化，他们将取消命令。


虚拟语气:
If the situation were different, I would countermand the order. = 如果情况不同，我会取消命令。
I wish I had countermanded the order earlier. = 我希望我早点取消命令。


被动语态:
The order was countermanded by the higher authority. = 命令被上级取消了。
The instructions were countermanded due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，指示被取消了。


疑问句:
Was the order countermanded? = 命令被取消了吗？
Have the instructions been countermanded? = 指示被取消了吗？


否定句:
He did not countermand the order. = 他没有取消命令。
The officer did not countermand the instructions. = 军官没有取消指示。
