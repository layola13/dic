linking:/ˈlɪŋkɪŋ/ | adj. |连接的；联系的；相关的| Connecting, joining, or uniting.  It describes something that creates a connection or relationship between things.|The linking verb "is" connects the subject and predicate.  连接动词“is”连接主语和谓语。|近义词：connecting, joining, uniting, associating|反义词：separating, disconnecting, disjoining
