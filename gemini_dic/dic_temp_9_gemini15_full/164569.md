preexamines:/ˌpriːɪɡˈzæmɪnz/| vt.|预先检查；预先审查|To examine something beforehand; to review something in advance.  This often involves a thorough inspection or analysis to ensure accuracy, completeness, or compliance with standards before a final decision or action is taken.|例句：The committee preexamines all proposals before submitting them to the full board. 委员会在向全体董事会提交提案之前会预先审查所有提案。


The auditors preexamined the company's financial records before the official audit. 审计员在正式审计之前预先检查了公司的财务记录。|近义词：inspect, review, scrutinize, survey|反义词：neglect, overlook
