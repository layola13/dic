perspires:/pərˈspaɪrz/| v.|出汗，流汗|To secrete sweat through the pores of the skin.  It often indicates exertion, nervousness, or illness.|例句：He perspired heavily after the run. 他跑步后汗流浃背。|例句：She perspired nervously before the interview.  她在面试前紧张地冒汗。|近义词：sweat|反义词：dehydrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He perspires easily in hot weather. = 他在炎热的天气里很容易出汗。
She perspires a lot during exercise. = 她运动时出很多汗。

一般过去时:
I perspired profusely during the marathon. = 我在马拉松比赛中汗流浃背。
He perspired after a strenuous workout. = 他剧烈运动后出汗了。

现在完成时:
I have perspired so much; I need a shower. = 我出了一身汗，需要洗个澡。
She has perspired throughout the entire meeting. = 她在整个会议期间一直出汗。


过去完成时:
I had perspired before I even started the race. = 我甚至还没开始比赛就已出汗。
They had perspired heavily before reaching the summit. = 他们在到达山顶之前已经汗流浃背了。


将来时:
I will perspire if I wear that heavy coat. = 如果我穿那件厚外套，我会出汗的。
He will perspire during the intense yoga session. = 他在激烈的瑜伽课程中会出汗。


虚拟语气:
If I were to run a marathon, I would perspire a great deal. = 如果我要跑马拉松，我会出很多汗。
I wish I didn't perspire so much under pressure. = 我希望我不那么容易在压力下出汗。


被动语态:
(The verb "perspire" is rarely used in the passive voice because it is an intransitive verb)


疑问句:
Do you perspire easily? = 你容易出汗吗？
Does she perspire when she's nervous? = 她紧张的时候会出汗吗？


否定句:
He doesn't perspire much, even during intense exercise. = 他即使剧烈运动也不怎么出汗。
She didn't perspire at all during the presentation. = 她在演讲过程中一点汗都没出。
