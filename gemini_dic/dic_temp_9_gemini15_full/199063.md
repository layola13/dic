signifier:/ˈsɪɡnɪfaɪər/| n. |能指；标志物|The signifier is the form of a sign, such as a word, image, or sound, which is used to represent something else.  In semiotics, it's the physical form that conveys meaning, distinct from the signified (the concept or meaning it represents). For example, the word "tree" (the signifier) represents the concept of a tree (the signified).|The word "tree" is a signifier for the concept of a tree.  The image of a red rose is a signifier for love and romance.|近义词：symbol, indicator, emblem|反义词：signified
