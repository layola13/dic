hot-dog:/ˈhɑːtˌdɔːɡ/|/ˈhɑːtˌdɒɡ/| n. |热狗；热狗肠|A hot dog is a cooked sausage served in a sliced bun.  It's a popular street food and fast food item. |例句：I bought a hot dog from the street vendor. 我从街边小贩那里买了一根热狗。

例句： He ate a hot dog with mustard and relish. 他吃了一根加了芥末酱和酸黄瓜的热狗。


近义词：frankfurter, sausage, wiener
