outinfluence:/ˌaʊtˈɪnflʊəns/| v.|  影响，施加影响 | To exert influence beyond or despite existing power structures or expectations.  It suggests a significant and possibly unexpected impact. | Example: The small protest unexpectedly outinfluenced the government's decision.  小规模抗议出乎意料地影响了政府的决定。|近义词：overshadow, surpass, outweigh|反义词：be influenced by, be subject to
