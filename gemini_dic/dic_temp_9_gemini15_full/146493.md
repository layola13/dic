overgarnish:/ˌoʊvərˈɡɑːrnɪʃ/| vt.|装饰过度；过度装饰|To decorate or garnish excessively; to add more decoration than is necessary or desirable.|例句：The cake was overgarnished with frosting, making it look messy.  蛋糕上糖霜装饰过度，看起来很乱。|例句：She overgarnished the salad with herbs, obscuring the vegetables. 她用香草过度装饰沙拉，遮住了蔬菜。|近义词：overdecorate, overdo, embellish excessively|反义词：undergarnish, simplify, minimalize|动词
