scrubbery:/ˈskrʌbəri/| n.|灌木丛；矮树丛|A scrubbery is a place where shrubs grow thickly. It is often used to describe an area of tangled, low-growing bushes.|The children played hide-and-seek in the dense scrubbery.|近义词：undergrowth, thicket, bush|反义词：open field, grassland
