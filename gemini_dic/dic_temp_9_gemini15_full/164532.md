preestimate:/ˌpriːˈɛstɪmeɪt/| vt.|预估，预先估计|To make a preliminary estimate of (something); to estimate in advance.|例句：We preestimated the project cost at $100,000. 我们预估该项目成本为10万美元。例句：The engineers preestimated the time needed to complete the bridge. 工程师们预估了完成桥梁建设所需的时间。|近义词：forecast, project, anticipate|反义词：underestimate, overestimate
