reprioritizing:/ˌriːpraɪˈɔːrətaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |重新排序优先级，重新确定优先顺序 |The act of reconsidering and changing the order of priorities.  It involves reassessing the importance of different tasks or goals and adjusting their ranking accordingly. This often happens when new information emerges, circumstances change, or more pressing needs arise.|例句：The team is reprioritizing its tasks to focus on the most urgent deadlines.  团队正在重新安排任务优先级，以集中精力处理最紧急的截止日期。

The company is reprioritizing its investments to support its new strategic direction. 公司正在重新调整投资优先级，以支持其新的战略方向。|近义词：reordering, readjusting, reranking |反义词：maintaining, preserving the status quo
