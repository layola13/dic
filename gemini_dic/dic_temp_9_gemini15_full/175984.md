rattles:/ˈrætlz/| n. v.|嘎啦嘎啦声；摇动；使发出嘎啦嘎啦声|to make a series of short, sharp knocking sounds;  to shake something so that it makes a rattling sound; to cause to shake or vibrate with a rattling sound|The old car rattled down the road. 这辆旧车沿着路嘎啦嘎啦地响着。The wind rattled the windows. 风把窗户摇得嘎啦嘎啦响。|近义词：clatter, clattering, shake, jiggle|反义词：silence, stillness|名词，动词
