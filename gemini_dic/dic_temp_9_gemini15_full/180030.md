refitting:/ˌriːˈfɪtɪŋ/ | n. |重新装备；改装；翻新 | The process of repairing, renovating, or equipping something again.  It often involves replacing old or worn-out parts with new ones to improve functionality or appearance. |例句：The refitting of the old ship cost a fortune.  = 这艘旧船的改装花费了一大笔钱。

The refitting of the kitchen is almost complete. = 厨房的翻新工作几乎完成了。


近义词：renovation, repair, refurbishment, overhaul | 反义词：dismantling, demolition


词性：名词
