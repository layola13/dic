outsummed:/ˌaʊtˈsʌmd/| v.|胜过，超过|to exceed in amount, number, or importance|例句：His achievements outsummed those of his contemporaries.他的成就超过了他的同时代人。例句：The benefits of the project outsummed its costs.该项目的益处超过了它的成本。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, outweigh|反义词：underestimated, be less than

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team outsums the opponent in skill. = 这个队的技术胜过对手。
Her kindness outsums her anger. = 她的善良胜过她的愤怒。


一般过去时:
The army outsummed the rebels in numbers. = 军队的数量超过了叛军。
Last year's profits outsummed this year's. = 去年的利润超过了今年的。


现在完成时:
Her contributions have outsummed everyone else's. = 她的贡献已经超过了其他所有人。
The evidence has outsummed the initial suspicion. = 证据已经超过了最初的怀疑。


过去完成时:
By the end of the game, their points had outsummed ours. = 到比赛结束时，他们的分数已经超过了我们。
The damage had outsummed our expectations. = 损失已经超过了我们的预期。


将来时:
The demand will outsummed the supply. = 需求将超过供应。
His influence will outsummed his father's. = 他的影响力将超过他父亲的。


虚拟语气:
If they had worked harder, their results would have outsummed their rivals. = 如果他们更努力工作，他们的成绩就会超过竞争对手。
I wish my efforts had outsummed my shortcomings. = 我希望我的努力能够超过我的缺点。


被动语态:
The project's benefits were outsummed by its costs. = 该项目的益处被其成本所超过。
Their achievements were outsummed by the challenges they faced. = 他们的成就被他们面临的挑战所超过。


疑问句:
Does the value of the asset outsummed the debt? = 资产的价值是否超过了债务？
Does her talent outsummed her experience? = 她的天赋是否超过了她的经验？


否定句:
The advantages didn't outsummed the disadvantages. = 优势并没有超过劣势。
His efforts didn't outsummed the obstacles. = 他的努力没有克服障碍。


PHRASE->
outsummed in quality = 在质量上胜过
outsummed in importance = 在重要性上胜过
outsummed in quantity = 在数量上胜过
outsummed in value = 在价值上胜过
outsummed in impact = 在影响上胜过
