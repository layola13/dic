occasional:/əˈkeɪʒənəl/ | adj. | 偶然的；间或的；临时的 |  Something that is occasional happens only sometimes, not regularly or frequently. It can also describe something that is intended for special occasions or is suitable for such occasions. |例句：We only have occasional visitors. 我们只有偶尔的访客。

例句：She wears that beautiful dress only on occasional special events. 她只在偶尔的特殊场合才穿那件漂亮的裙子。

近义词：infrequent, sporadic, irregular, intermittent, rare | 反义词：frequent, regular, constant, habitual
