importunator:/ɪmˈpɔːrtʃənˌeɪtər/ | n. |纠缠者；强求者；喋喋不休的人 | A person who makes repeated and unwelcome requests or demands.  They are persistent and annoying in their pursuit. | Example: The importunator wouldn't leave her alone, constantly asking for money.  那个纠缠者不愿离开她，不断地向她要钱。 |近义词：pest, nuisance, bore, nag |反义词：benefactor, helper
