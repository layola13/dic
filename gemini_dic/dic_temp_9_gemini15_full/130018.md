non-onerous:/ˌnɒnˈɒnərəs/| adj. |不繁重的；轻松的；不费力的|Not burdensome or oppressive; easy or light.  The new regulations are designed to be non-onerous for small businesses.|近义词：easy, light, undemanding, effortless |反义词：burdensome, oppressive, demanding, strenuous


例句：

The job was non-onerous, allowing me plenty of time for other pursuits. = 这份工作轻松愉快，让我有充足的时间去做其他的事情。

The new tax laws are intended to be non-onerous to the average citizen. = 新的税法旨在对普通公民来说不会造成负担。
