nonemanating: /ˌnɒnɪˈmeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不散发；不发出的 | Not emitting or radiating anything; not emanating.  The word describes something that doesn't produce or give off something, such as light, heat, or a smell. | 例句：The nonemanating heater was a disappointment; it barely warmed the room. = 不散发热量的加热器令人失望；它几乎没有暖和房间。


The nonemanating source of the strange smell baffled the investigators. =  奇怪气味的非散发源让调查人员感到困惑。 | 近义词：unemitting, nonradiating, inert | 反义词：emanating, radiating, emitting |
