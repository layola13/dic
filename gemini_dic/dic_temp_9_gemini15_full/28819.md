carousel:/ˌkærəˈsɛl/| n. |旋转木马，旋转展台|A carousel is a type of amusement ride consisting of a rotating circular platform with seats for riders.  It can also refer to a device used to display items in a continuous loop, like images or advertisements.|例句：The children loved the carousel at the amusement park. 孩子们喜欢游乐园里的旋转木马。|例句：The online store uses a carousel to showcase its latest products.这家网店使用旋转展台展示其最新产品。|近义词：merry-go-round, roundabout, rotating display|反义词：static display
