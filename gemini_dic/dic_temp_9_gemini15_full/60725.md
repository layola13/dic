electro-silver: /ɪˈlɛktrəʊˌsɪlvər/ | adj. | 电镀银的；电解银的 |  Describes something plated or treated with silver using an electrolytic process. This typically results in a thin layer of silver coating a base metal, enhancing its appearance and/or conductivity.  | 例句：The electro-silver cutlery looked elegant on the table. 电镀银的餐具摆在桌子上看起来很优雅。

The electro-silver contacts ensured a reliable electrical connection. 电镀银的触点确保了可靠的电气连接。


近义词：silver-plated, silvered | 反义词：unplated,  (no direct antonym, but implies lack of silver plating)


词性: adjective
