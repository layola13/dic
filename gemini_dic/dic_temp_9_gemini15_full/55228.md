dissident:/ˈdɪsɪdənt/| n. adj.|持不同政见者；异议者；反对者|a person who opposes official policy, especially that of an authoritarian state.  A dissident is someone who disagrees with the established political system or government, often expressing their opposition publicly. They may advocate for change through peaceful means or engage in more active forms of resistance.|例句：Many dissidents were imprisoned for their beliefs. 许多持不同政见者因其信仰而被监禁。

The government cracked down on political dissidents.政府镇压政治异见人士。

近义词：rebel, protester, opponent, nonconformist |反义词：conformist, supporter, loyalist|名词，形容词
