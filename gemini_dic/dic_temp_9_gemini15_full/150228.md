pandemicity:/ˌpændɪˈmɪsəti/| n. |大流行性，泛流行性 |The state or quality of being pandemic; the widespread prevalence of a disease.  It refers to the geographical extent and severity of a disease outbreak.  |例句：The pandemicity of the flu was unprecedented in modern history.  这场流感的泛流行在现代史上是史无前例的。
The high pandemicity of the disease necessitated a global response.  该疾病的高度流行性需要全球共同应对。


近义词：widespread prevalence, global spread, pandemic extent |反义词：localized outbreak, regional epidemic |词性：名词
