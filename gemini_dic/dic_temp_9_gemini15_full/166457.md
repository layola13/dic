prereviews:/ˌpriːˈrjuːz/| n.| 预审；预评；提前评论 | Preliminary reviews or assessments before a formal review.  These might be internal reviews within a company before a public release, or early reviews by a select group of people. | Example: The prereviews of the movie were overwhelmingly positive, suggesting a successful box office run.  预映的电影评论非常正面，预示着票房将大卖。 |近义词：previews, advance reviews, early assessments |反义词：post-reviews, final reviews|名词
