non-therapeutic:/ˌnɒnˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪk/ | adj. |非治疗性的，无效的 | Not related to or intended for the treatment of illness or injury.  It describes something that does not have a healing or curative effect. | Example:  The non-therapeutic activities, such as watching TV, were distracting him from his recovery.  非治疗性的活动，例如看电视，分散了他的注意力，妨碍了他的康复。 |近义词：ineffective, unhelpful, unproductive, remedial |反义词：therapeutic, curative, healing
