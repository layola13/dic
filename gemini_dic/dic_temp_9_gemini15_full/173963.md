quasi-Spanish: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈspænɪʃ/ | adj. | 类似西班牙语的；具有西班牙语特征的 |  Approximating or resembling the characteristics of Spanish; having a quasi-Spanish style or quality.  This term is used to describe something that has some, but not all, of the features of the Spanish language or culture. It often implies an incomplete or imperfect imitation. |


例句：

The restaurant's menu featured a quasi-Spanish paella, with some unusual ingredients.  这家餐馆的菜单上有一道类似西班牙海鲜饭的菜，里面有一些不寻常的配料。


Her accent, though not perfectly Spanish, had a distinctly quasi-Spanish flavour. 她的口音虽然不是标准的西班牙语，却带有明显的西班牙语特色。


近义词：Spanish-like, pseudo-Spanish,  resembling Spanish


反义词： authentically Spanish, purely Spanish


词性：形容词
