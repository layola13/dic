nuzzles:/ˈnʌzəlz/| v. |轻嗅；蹭；依偎|To rub or press one's nose or face against something affectionately.  |例句：The puppy nuzzled against my leg. 小狗依偎在我的腿上。

例句：The kitten nuzzled its head against its mother. 小猫把头蹭在它妈妈身上。


近义词：cuddle, snuggle, rub|反义词：push away, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nuzzles his teddy bear every night. 他每晚都抱着他的泰迪熊轻蹭。
The cat nuzzles against the warm sunbeam. 猫咪蹭在温暖的阳光下。


一般过去时:
She nuzzled her baby close. 她把婴儿紧紧抱在怀里轻蹭。
The dog nuzzled my hand for attention. 狗用鼻子蹭我的手寻求关注。


现在完成时:
The child has nuzzled his blanket until it's completely flat.  孩子一直蹭他的毯子，直到它完全平了。
The puppy has nuzzled its way into my heart. 这只小狗已经融化了我的心。


过去完成时:
The kitten had nuzzled itself to sleep against its mother's fur. 小猫已经依偎在妈妈的毛皮上睡着了。
He had nuzzled his face into the soft pillow before falling asleep. 他睡着前把脸埋在柔软的枕头上轻轻蹭了蹭。


将来时:
She will nuzzle her new puppy tonight. 她今晚会抱着她新的小狗轻蹭。
The baby will nuzzle its way into its mother's arms for comfort. 宝宝会依偎到妈妈的怀里寻求安慰。


虚拟语气:
If I had a pet, I would nuzzle it all day long. 如果我有一只宠物，我会整天抱着它轻蹭。
I wish I could nuzzle my favorite stuffed animal right now. 我真希望现在能抱着我最喜欢的毛绒玩具轻蹭。


被动语态:
(Nuzzle is rarely used in the passive voice because it implies active affection)  (nuzzle很少用于被动语态，因为它暗示着主动的亲昵)


疑问句:
Does your dog often nuzzle you? 你的狗经常蹭你吗？
Does the baby nuzzle its teddy bear before bed? 宝宝睡觉前会蹭它的泰迪熊吗？


否定句:
The cat didn't nuzzle me today. 猫今天没有蹭我。
He didn't nuzzle his mother for comfort. 他没有依偎着母亲寻求安慰。


PHRASE->
nuzzle up to: 靠近；讨好
nuzzle into: 偎依进…
nuzzle against: 蹭着…
nuzzle one's way: 蹭着前进
tenderly nuzzle: 温柔地轻蹭


词源:
The word "nuzzle" comes from the Middle English word "nuzelen," meaning "to push with the nose."  This, in turn, likely derives from a combination of the Old French word "nez" (nose) and a diminutive suffix.
