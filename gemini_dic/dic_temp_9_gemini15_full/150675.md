papyrograph:/pəˈpɪrəɡræf/| n.|纸上描图，描图纸|A papyrograph is a type of stencil used to create multiple copies of a drawing or design.  It involves creating a stencil by cutting away parts of a sheet of specially prepared paper, then using it with a duplicating machine or ink to produce copies.|例句：The teacher used a papyrograph to make copies of the worksheet for the class. 老师用描图纸为班级制作了工作表的副本。  The detailed papyrograph produced crisp, clear copies. 精细的描图纸制作出清晰锐利的副本。|近义词：stencil, duplicator|反义词：N/A|名词
