portended:/pɔːˈtɛndɪd/| v.|预示，预兆；表示…的预兆|to be a sign or warning that something, especially something bad or unpleasant, will happen in the future.|The dark clouds portended a storm.  乌云预示着一场暴风雨。 The king's illness portended the end of his reign. 国王的病预示着他统治的结束。|foreshadow, augur, presage, foretell|negate, contradict, belie|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dark clouds portent a storm. = 乌云预示着暴风雨。
These events portent future difficulties. = 这些事件预示着未来的困难。

一般过去时:
The soothsayer portended disaster. = 预言家预言了灾难。
His strange behavior portended trouble. = 他奇怪的行为预示着麻烦。

现在完成时:
Recent events have portended a change in government. = 最近发生的事件预示着政府将要变革。
The rising tensions have portended a conflict. = 紧张局势的加剧预示着冲突。

过去完成时:
The old prophecies had portended the fall of the empire. = 古老的预言已经预示了帝国的灭亡。
Analysts had portended the market crash. = 分析师们已经预言了市场崩盘。

将来时:
The signs portent a significant economic downturn. = 迹象预示着经济将出现大幅下滑。
Experts portent a rise in inflation next year. = 专家们预言明年通货膨胀将会上升。

虚拟语气:
If the signs had been correctly interpreted, they would have portended the disaster. = 如果这些迹象被正确解读，它们本可以预示这场灾难。
If the data were analyzed properly, it would portent a different outcome. = 如果数据被正确分析，它将预示不同的结果。

被动语态:
The coming storm was portended by the falling barometer. = 即将到来的暴风雨是由下降的气压预示的。
The crisis was portended by several warning signs. = 危机是由几个警告信号预示的。

疑问句:
What did the oracle portent? = 预言家预言了什么？
Does this event portent anything significant? = 这件事预示着什么重要的事情吗？

否定句:
The calm weather did not portent a hurricane. = 平静的天气并没有预示着飓风。
The initial reports did not portent any serious problems. = 初步报告并没有预示任何严重的问题。
