dressmaking:/ˈdrɛsmˌeɪkɪŋ/ | n. |制衣，服装制作 |The art or practice of making dresses and other garments.  It involves pattern making, cutting, and sewing fabric to create clothing. |例句：She's taking a dressmaking class to learn how to make her own clothes. = 她正在参加一个制衣课程，学习如何制作自己的衣服。|例句：Her dressmaking skills are exceptional; her creations are always stunning. = 她的制衣技巧非凡；她的作品总是令人惊叹。|近义词：sewing, tailoring, garment making |反义词：None
