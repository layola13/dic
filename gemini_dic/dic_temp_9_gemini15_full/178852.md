red-eye:/ˌredˈaɪ/| n. adj.|红眼航班；红眼病|A red-eye is a flight that departs late at night and arrives early in the morning, often requiring passengers to travel overnight.  It can also refer to conjunctivitis (pink eye).|例句：I took a red-eye flight from New York to Los Angeles. 我乘坐了从纽约到洛杉矶的红眼航班。


The term "red-eye" in reference to a flight likely originates from the exhaustion passengers experience after a long overnight journey, making their eyes appear bloodshot or red.  The term for conjunctivitis, "red-eye", describes the redness in the eyes caused by the inflammation.


近义词：overnight flight (for the flight meaning), conjunctivitis, pink eye (for the medical meaning)|反义词：daytime flight (for the flight meaning),  healthy eyes (for the medical meaning)|词性:n. adj.
