decision-making: /dɪˈsɪʒənˌmeɪkɪŋ/ | n. | 决策；做决定 | The process of identifying, evaluating, and choosing among several alternatives.  It involves critical thinking, problem-solving, and considering various factors to arrive at a course of action. |  例句：The company's decision-making process is notoriously slow. 公司的决策过程出了名的慢。 | 例句：Effective decision-making requires careful consideration of all options. 有效的决策需要仔细考虑所有选择。| 近义词：problem-solving, choice-making, judgment | 反义词：indecision, procrastination | 名词
