probator:/ˈproʊbeɪtər/| n. | 检验者；鉴定人；试用期员工 | A person appointed by a court to examine and verify the validity of a will or the accounts of an executor.  Someone who is undergoing a probationary period, often in a job. | Example: The probator carefully examined the will to ensure its authenticity. 检验者仔细检查遗嘱以确保其真实性。 The new employee is currently a probator, and their performance is being evaluated. 新员工目前处于试用期，正在对其表现进行评估。 |近义词：examiner, tester, probationer | 反义词：N/A
