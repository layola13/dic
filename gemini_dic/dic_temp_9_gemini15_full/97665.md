inhibitive: /ɪnˈhɪbətɪv/ | adj. |抑制的；阻止的；妨碍的 |  Having the power or tendency to inhibit or restrain; acting as a deterrent.  It describes something that discourages or prevents an action or process. |


例句：

The high cost of the project had an inhibitive effect on its development. 高昂的项目成本对其发展起到了抑制作用。

The government's new regulations have an inhibitive effect on small businesses. 政府的新规定对小型企业具有抑制作用。


近义词：restrictive, deterrent, repressive, restraining, inhibitory

反义词：stimulative, encouraging, promoting, facilitating


词性：形容词
