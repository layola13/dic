kinglike:/ˈkɪŋlaɪk/| adj.|国王似的；威严的；有王者风范的|Resembling a king in appearance, manner, or bearing; majestic; regal.|例句：He carried himself with a kinglike air of authority. 他举止威严，像个国王一样。


例句：The kinglike lion surveyed his pride from atop the rock.  那只像国王一样的狮子从岩石顶上俯视着它的狮群。


近义词：regal, majestic, royal, imperial, sovereign


反义词：common, humble, meek, lowly


词性:形容词
