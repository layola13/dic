controller:/kənˈtroʊlər/|/kənˈtrəʊlər/| n. |控制器；管理者；控制装置 |A controller is a person or thing that controls something.  It can refer to a person who manages or directs something, or a device that regulates or governs a system. | Examples:  The flight controller guided the plane safely to the ground.  飞行控制器引导飞机安全着陆。 The game controller wasn't working properly. 游戏控制器无法正常工作。|近义词：regulator, governor, manager, supervisor |反义词：subordinate, follower
