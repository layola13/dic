pooper:/ˈpuːpər/| n.|大便；糟糕的人或事；|a person or thing that is regarded as unpleasant or worthless;  excrement.|例句：The dog left a pooper in the garden.  狗狗在花园里拉了一泡屎。|例句：That new policy is a real pooper.  那个新政策真是一团糟。|近义词：mess, failure, excrement|反义词：success, triumph


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The dog often poops in the garden. = 狗经常在花园里大便。
He poops three times a day. = 他一天大便三次。


一般过去时:
The baby pooped all over his diaper. = 婴儿把尿布弄脏了。
The dog pooped on the carpet yesterday. = 狗昨天在毯子上拉屎了。


现在完成时:
The dog has pooped on the rug again! = 狗又跑到地毯上拉屎了！
He hasn't pooped yet today. = 他今天还没大便。


过去完成时:
He had pooped before we left the house. = 我们离开家之前他已经大便了。
She had already pooped before her mom found out. = 她妈妈发现之前她已经大便了。


将来时:
I think the baby will poop soon. = 我想婴儿很快就要大便了。
The dog will probably poop later. = 狗可能过一会儿会大便。


虚拟语气:
If the dog pooped outside, I wouldn't mind. = 如果狗在外面大便，我不介意。
I wish the dog hadn't pooped on the floor. = 我真希望狗没在地板上拉屎。

疑问句:

否定句:
