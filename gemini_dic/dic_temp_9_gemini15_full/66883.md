expects:/ɪkˈspɛkts/ | vt. | 预期；期望；认为 | To believe or think that something will happen or that something is likely to be true.  | 例句：I expect him to arrive soon. 我预计他很快就会到。  | 例句：She expects good news from the doctor. 她期待着医生带来好消息。 | 近义词：anticipates, hopes, believes | 反义词：doubts, questions, disbelieves | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He expects a promotion next year. 他期待明年升职。
They expect the project to be completed by Friday. 他们预计项目将在周五前完成。


一般过去时:
She expected a difficult exam. 她预料到考试会很难。
We expected more people to attend the meeting. 我们预计会有更多人参加会议。


现在完成时:
I have expected this outcome for a long time. 我早就预料到这个结果了。
They have expected the delay, so they are not surprised. 他们预料到了延误，所以并不惊讶。


过去完成时:
I had expected him to call, but he didn't. 我原以为他会打电话，但他没有。
They had expected the weather to be better, but it rained all day. 他们原以为天气会更好，但结果一整天都在下雨。


将来时:
We expect to finish the work by next week. 我们预计下周完成这项工作。
She expects that she will pass the test. 她预计自己会通过考试。


虚拟语气:
I expect that he would have finished the project by now, if he hadn't been ill.  如果他不生病的话，我估计他现在已经完成项目了。
It is expected that he would apologize if he knew what he had done.  如果他知道自己做了什么，人们期望他会道歉。


被动语态:
The result was expected to be announced this week. 预计结果本周公布。
A lot of trouble was expected during the construction.  施工期间预计会遇到很多麻烦。


疑问句:
Do you expect rain tomorrow? 你预计明天会下雨吗？
What do they expect from this new policy? 他们对这项新政策有什么期望？


否定句:
I don't expect him to agree. 我不指望他会同意。
They didn't expect such a strong reaction. 他们没有预料到如此强烈的反应。


PHRASE->
expect to: 预计会
expect from: 期望从…得到
expect of: 对…期望
expect something of someone: 对某人抱有某种期望
expect the unexpected: 预料到意外
