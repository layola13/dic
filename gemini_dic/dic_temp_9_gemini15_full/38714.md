condiment:/ˈkɑːndɪmənt/| n. |调味品，佐料 |A condiment is a substance such as mustard, sauce, or relish that is added to food to improve its flavor.  |Example: Ketchup is a popular condiment for fries.  番茄酱是炸薯条很受欢迎的调味品。  |近义词：sauce, relish, seasoning |反义词：None
