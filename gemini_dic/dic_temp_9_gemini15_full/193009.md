secondaries:/ˈsekəndɛriz/| n.| 次要事物；附属物；副产品；中学；（电力）二次绕组| Things of secondary importance; supplementary items or products; high schools; secondary windings in electricity.|例句：The report highlighted the primary findings,  but also mentioned some secondary observations. 这份报告强调了主要发现，但也提到了一些次要观察结果。

例句：Many students attend secondary schools before going to university. 许多学生在大学之前上中学。


近义词：derivatives, byproducts, secondaries, high schools, subsidiaries|反义词： primaries, principals, main
