frontier:/frʌnˈtɪər/| n.|边疆；前沿；新领域|The edge or limit of settled or explored territory.  A field of study or activity that is new and developing. |例句：The Wild West was a dangerous frontier.  西部荒野是一片危险的边疆。

The researchers are exploring the frontiers of medical science.  研究人员正在探索医学科学的前沿领域。|近义词：border, boundary, edge, forefront|反义词：interior, hinterland


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The frontier separates two countries. = 边界分隔了两个国家。
This company is expanding into new frontiers. = 这家公司正在拓展新的领域。


一般过去时:
The pioneers pushed back the frontier. = 拓荒者们向边疆推进。
The scientist crossed the frontier of knowledge. = 科学家突破了知识的界限。


现在完成时:
They have reached the frontier of space exploration. = 他们已经到达了太空探索的前沿。
We have pushed back the frontiers of what's possible. = 我们已经突破了可能性的界限。


过去完成时:
Before the expedition, they had mapped the frontier. = 探险队出发前，他们已经绘制了边疆地图。
By 1850, they had already crossed many frontiers. = 到1850年，他们已经越过了许多边疆。


将来时:
They will explore the new frontiers of artificial intelligence. = 他们将探索人工智能的新领域。
The company will expand its frontiers into new markets. = 公司将把业务拓展到新的市场。


虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we could push the frontier further. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们就能进一步推进前沿。
I wish I could explore the frontiers of my own potential. = 我希望我能探索自身潜能的边界。


被动语态:
The frontier was defended by brave soldiers. = 边疆由勇敢的士兵保卫。
The frontiers of knowledge are constantly being pushed. = 知识的边界不断被突破。


疑问句:
Where does the frontier begin? = 边界从哪里开始？
What are the frontiers of this research project? = 这个研究项目的边界是什么？


否定句:
The explorers did not reach the frontier. = 探险家们没有到达边疆。
They haven't yet crossed the frontier of understanding. = 他们尚未跨越理解的界限。
