offending:/əˈfɛndɪŋ/ | adj. |冒犯的；令人反感的；犯罪的 | causing someone to feel upset, annoyed, or insulted;  relating to or constituting an offense or crime. |例句：His remarks were deeply offending to many people.  他的话深深地冒犯了许多人。


例句：The offending item was quickly removed from the shelf.  令人反感的商品很快就被从货架上拿掉了。


近义词：insulting, offensive, objectionable, disagreeable,  scandalous


反义词：pleasing, agreeable, acceptable, inoffensive


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The smell is offending my nostrils. = 这气味熏着我的鼻子。
That remark is offending many people. = 那句话冒犯了许多人。


一般过去时:
His behavior offended everyone at the party. = 他的行为冒犯了聚会上的每个人。
The loud music offended the neighbors. = 吵闹的音乐惹恼了邻居们。


现在完成时:
His actions have offended many people. = 他的行为已经冒犯了许多人。
That comment has offended me deeply. = 那句话深深地冒犯了我。


过去完成时:
I had offended her without realizing it. = 我在不知情的情况下冒犯了她。
They had offended the local customs. = 他们冒犯了当地的习俗。


将来时:
His words will offend many people. = 他的话会冒犯很多人。
I think this action will offend her sensibilities. = 我认为这个行为会冒犯她的情感。


虚拟语气:
If I had known it would offend him, I wouldn't have said it. = 如果我知道这会冒犯他，我就不会说了。
I wish I hadn't offended her. = 我真希望我没有冒犯她。


被动语态:
Many people were offended by his behavior. = 许多人被他粗鲁的行为冒犯了。
She was offended by the insensitive comment. = 她被那句不敏感的话语冒犯了。


疑问句:
Is anything I said offending you? = 我说的有什么冒犯你的吗？
Are these actions offending anyone? = 这些行为有冒犯到任何人吗？


否定句:
His words were not offending, just misunderstood. = 他的话并非冒犯，只是被误解了。
I didn't intend to offend anyone with my joke. = 我无意用我的笑话冒犯任何人。


PHRASE->
the offending party = 肇事方
the offending article = 违规物品
the offending substance = 有害物质
the offending behavior = 违规行为
the offending weapon = 作案武器


词源:
From Old French *offendre, from Latin offendere "to strike against, stumble, attack," from ob- "against" + fendere "to strike, cleave."  The sense shifted to "to cause offense" in Middle English.
