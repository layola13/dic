counteruniversities: /ˌkaʊntərjuːnɪˈvɜːrsətiz/ | n. | 反大学；另类大学 |  Organizations or movements that challenge traditional university structures, values, or methods of knowledge production. They often emphasize alternative pedagogies, community engagement, and social justice.  These are not necessarily physical institutions, but rather networks or philosophies of education. | Example: The rise of online learning and open educational resources has led to the emergence of various counteruniversities.  另类学习和开放教育资源的兴起导致了各种反大学的出现。 | 近义词: alternative educational institutions, unconventional learning communities | 反义词: traditional universities, established educational systems |  词性: noun
