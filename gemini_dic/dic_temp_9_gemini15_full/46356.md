dactylitis:/ˌdækˈtɪlaɪtɪs/| n. |指骨炎|Inflammation of the bones of the fingers or toes.  This condition is often associated with underlying diseases like sickle cell anemia or psoriatic arthritis.  The inflammation can cause pain, swelling, and tenderness in the affected digits.|The patient presented with dactylitis in multiple fingers, a classic symptom of his sickle cell disease.|近义词：finger inflammation, toe inflammation|反义词：无
