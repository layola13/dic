butterfats:/ˈbʌtərˌfæts/| n.|奶油脂肪|Butterfats refers to the fat content found in butter.  It is primarily composed of triglycerides, with varying proportions of saturated and unsaturated fatty acids.  The specific composition can vary depending on factors such as the breed of cow, diet, and processing methods.  |Example: The high butterfats content in this butter contributes to its rich flavor and texture.  这个黄油的高奶油脂肪含量使其具有浓郁的风味和质地。|近义词: butterfat, milkfat|反义词:  None specifically, but could be contrasted with low-fat dairy products.|名词
