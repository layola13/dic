manichord:/ˈmænɪkɔːrd/| n. |古钢琴|A plucked keyboard instrument, a precursor to the piano, in which small metal plectra (plectrums) pluck the strings when the keys are depressed.|The manichord was a popular instrument in the 17th and 18th centuries.|近义词：clavichord, harpsichord |反义词：None
