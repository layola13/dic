psychopharmacological: /ˌsaɪkoʊˌfɑːrməˈkɑːlədʒɪkəl/ | adj. |精神药理学的| Relating to psychopharmacology, the study of the effects of drugs on the mind and behavior.  This adjective describes things related to the branch of medicine focusing on the use of medication to treat mental disorders. |例句：The psychopharmacological effects of the new drug are still being studied.  新的药物的精神药理学效应仍在研究中。


The psychopharmacological treatment approach proved effective in managing his anxiety.  精神药理学治疗方法被证明在控制他的焦虑方面是有效的。

近义词：neuropharmacological (神经药理学的), pharmacopsychiatric (药物精神病学的)

反义词：N/A (没有直接的反义词，只能用相反的领域来表达，例如：psychotherapeutic (心理治疗的))

词性：形容词
