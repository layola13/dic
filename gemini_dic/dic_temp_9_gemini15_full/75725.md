full-time:/fʊlˈtaɪm/| adj.|全职的，全时间的|The adjective "full-time" describes something that is done for the whole working day or week, as opposed to part-time.|例句：He works full-time at a bank. 他在银行全职工作。|例句：She has a full-time job as a teacher. 她有一份全职教师的工作。|近义词：permanent, whole-time |反义词:part-time


USAGE->

词源:
The word "full-time" is a compound adjective formed by combining "full" and "time."  "Full" means complete or entire, while "time" refers to the period of work.  The combination signifies that the work or activity occupies the complete working period.
