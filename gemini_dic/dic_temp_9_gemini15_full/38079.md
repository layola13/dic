composits:/ˈkɒmpəzɪts/ | n. |复合材料，合成物|A composite material is a material made from two or more constituent materials with significantly different physical or chemical properties that, when combined, produce a material with characteristics different from the individual components.|例句：Fiber-reinforced polymers are a type of composite material commonly used in aerospace engineering.  纤维增强聚合物是一种常用在航空航天工程中的复合材料。|例句：The artist created a striking composite image from several photographs.  艺术家从几张照片中创作出一幅引人注目的合成图像。|近义词：compound, mixture, amalgam|反义词：element, single material
