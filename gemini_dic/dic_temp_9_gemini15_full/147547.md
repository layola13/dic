overpedalling: /ˌoʊvərˈpɛdəlɪŋ/ | v. | 过度踩踏板；用力过猛 | The act of pedalling too hard or too frequently, often resulting in inefficient movement or loss of control.  This term is commonly used in cycling and other activities involving pedals. |例句：He was overpedalling on the uphill climb, wasting energy and losing momentum. 他在上坡时用力过猛，浪费了能量并失去了动力。

例句：Overpedalling can lead to muscle fatigue and reduced efficiency.  过度踩踏板会导致肌肉疲劳和效率降低。

近义词：overexerting, straining, forcing

反义词：underpedalling, coasting
