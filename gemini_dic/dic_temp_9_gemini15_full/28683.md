carjacking:/ˈkɑːrdʒækɪŋ/|/ˈkɑːrˌdʒækɪŋ/| n. |劫车；汽车劫持|The act of stealing a car from someone by force or threat of force.  It involves the use of violence or intimidation to take control of a vehicle from its driver or occupant.|例句：The police are investigating a recent carjacking in the city center.  警方正在调查市中心最近发生的汽车劫持案。

例句：He was a victim of a carjacking last night and lost all his belongings. 他昨晚遭遇了汽车劫持，损失了所有财物。


近义词：vehicle theft, car robbery, hijacking (though hijacking often implies larger vehicles or aircraft)|反义词：car donation, car sharing
