jettisoning:/ˈdʒɛtɪsənɪŋ/ | v. |抛弃；丢弃；放弃 | The act of jettisoning something, typically cargo from a ship or aircraft to lighten the load in an emergency, or discarding something unwanted or burdensome.  | Example: The crew jettisoned the cargo to save the ship. 船员抛弃货物以挽救船只。

The pilot jettisoned the fuel tanks to reduce weight.  飞行员抛弃油箱以减轻重量。


| 近义词：discarding, abandoning, relinquishing, dumping | 反义词： retaining, keeping, preserving | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are jettisoning the old equipment. = 他们正在抛弃旧设备。
We jettison unnecessary information. = 我们抛弃不必要的信息。

一般过去时:
The captain jettisoned the ballast. = 船长抛弃了压舱物。
She jettisoned her old beliefs. = 她放弃了她旧有的信仰。

现在完成时:
They have jettisoned their original plan. = 他们已经放弃了最初的计划。
The company has jettisoned several projects this year. = 公司今年已经放弃了好几个项目。

过去完成时:
The pilot had jettisoned the fuel before the crash. = 飞行员在坠机前已经抛弃了燃料。
They had jettisoned their hopes of success. = 他们已经放弃了成功的希望。

将来时:
We will jettison the damaged parts. = 我们将抛弃损坏的部件。
The airline will jettison the excess baggage. = 航空公司将抛弃超重的行李。

虚拟语气:
If they jettisoned their prejudices, they would see things differently. = 如果他们抛弃偏见，他们会以不同的眼光看待事物。
I wish I had jettisoned that bad habit years ago. = 我真希望几年前我就已经改掉那个坏习惯了。

被动语态:
The cargo was jettisoned to save the ship. = 货物被抛弃以挽救船只。
The outdated technology is being jettisoned by the company. = 公司正在抛弃过时的技术。

疑问句:
Will they jettison the project? = 他们会放弃这个项目吗？
Have they jettisoned their old car? = 他们已经抛弃他们的旧车了吗？

否定句:
They did not jettison the plan. = 他们没有放弃计划。
We will not jettison our principles. = 我们不会放弃我们的原则。


PHRASE->
jettison a plan = 放弃一个计划
jettison a relationship = 放弃一段关系
jettison a burden = 卸下负担
jettison one's fears = 抛弃恐惧
jettison an idea = 放弃一个想法


词源:
From Middle French *jeter, from Old French jeter ("to throw"), from Vulgar Latin *iactare ("to throw, hurl"), from Latin iactare, frequentative of iacere ("to throw").  The nautical sense ("to throw overboard") developed in the 16th century.
