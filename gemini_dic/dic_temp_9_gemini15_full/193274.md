seeing:/ˈsiːɪŋ/ | v. |看见；理解；拜访 |The act of perceiving with the eyes; understanding; a visit. |例句：I'm seeing the doctor tomorrow. 我明天去看医生。  I'm seeing a pattern here. 我在这里看到一种模式。|近义词：observing, perceiving, understanding, visiting |反义词：ignoring, misunderstanding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I see a bird. = 我看见一只鸟。
He sees the problem. = 他看到了问题。

一般过去时:
I saw a movie last night. = 我昨晚看了一部电影。
She saw her friend at the mall. = 她在商场看到了她的朋友。

现在完成时:
I have seen that movie before. = 我以前看过那部电影。
They have seen the doctor several times. = 他们已经看过几次医生了。

过去完成时:
I had seen him once before. = 我以前见过他一次。
She had seen the signs, but ignored them. = 她曾看到过那些迹象，但忽略了它们。

将来时:
I will see you tomorrow. = 我明天见你。
We will see what happens. = 我们等着瞧吧。

虚拟语气:
If I saw a bear, I would run away. = 如果我看到熊，我会跑掉。
I wish I could see the future. = 我希望我能看到未来。

被动语态:
The problem was seen immediately. = 问题立刻被发现了。
The movie is being seen by many people. = 这部电影正在被很多人观看。

疑问句:
Do you see what I mean? = 你明白我的意思吗？
Can you see the bird in the tree? = 你能看到树上的鸟吗？

否定句:
I don't see any problems. = 我没看到任何问题。
He didn't see the car coming. = 他没看到车来了。


PHRASE->
Seeing is believing. = 眼见为实。
Seeing as... = 鉴于...
Seeing red = 勃然大怒
Seeing eye to eye = 完全一致
For seeing = 为了看
