polacre:/ˈpɒləkr/ | n. |波拉克帆船 | A polacre is a type of sailing vessel, typically a three-masted ship with the foremast and mizzenmast rigged as fore-and-aft sails and the mainmast rigged square.  It was common in the Mediterranean. | Example: The old captain recounted tales of his voyages on a polacre in the Aegean Sea.  | 近义词:xebec, schooner (depending on specific rigging) | 反义词: N/A
