quateron:/kwɑːˈtɛrən/| n. |四分之一黑人血统的人 | A person who is one-quarter Black, having one Black great-grandparent and three white great-grandparents.  The term reflects a historical system of racial classification based on ancestry. | Example: In some historical contexts, a quateron was considered to have a different social status than someone with a higher percentage of Black ancestry. 在某些历史语境中，四分之一黑人血统的人被认为拥有与黑人血统比例更高的人不同的社会地位。| 近义词:quadroon (less common)| 反义词:  None directly applicable, as it describes a degree of ancestry.  |名词
