invile:/ɪnˈvaɪt/ | v. | 邀请，恳请 | To request someone to come to a place or event; to formally ask someone to participate in something. | 例句：I invited my friends to my birthday party.  我邀请我的朋友参加我的生日派对。| 例句：She invited him to dinner. 她邀请他共进晚餐。|近义词：ask, summon, bid | 反义词：exclude, reject


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He invites his friends over every weekend. = 他每个周末都邀请朋友来玩。
She invites us to her house for dinner. = 她邀请我们去她家吃晚饭。

一般过去时:
They invited us to their wedding last year. = 他们去年邀请我们参加他们的婚礼。
I invited her to the party, but she couldn't make it. = 我邀请她参加派对，但她没来。

现在完成时:
We have invited all the guests to the ceremony. = 我们已经邀请所有来宾参加典礼了。
She has invited him to join the project several times. = 她已经多次邀请他加入这个项目了。

过去完成时:
They had invited all their relatives before the announcement. = 他们在宣布之前已经邀请了所有亲戚。
I had already invited them before realizing the conflict of schedule. = 我在意识到时间冲突之前就已经邀请他们了。

将来时:
We will invite them to the new housewarming party. = 我们将邀请他们参加新居入伙派对。
She will invite her colleagues to celebrate her promotion. = 她将邀请她的同事庆祝她的晋升。

虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have invited him. = 如果我知道的话，我就邀请他了。
I wish I had invited more people. = 我真希望我邀请更多的人。

被动语态:
We were invited to the conference. = 我们被邀请参加会议。
He was invited to give a speech. = 他被邀请作演讲。

疑问句:
Did they invite you to the party? = 他们邀请你参加派对了吗？
Were you invited to the meeting? = 你被邀请参加会议了吗？

否定句:
I didn't invite her to the wedding. = 我没有邀请她参加婚礼。
They didn't invite him to the dinner. = 他们没有邀请他参加晚餐。


PHRASE->
invite someone to dinner = 邀请某人共进晚餐
invite someone over = 邀请某人过来
invite someone out = 邀请某人出去
a formal invitation = 正式邀请
an open invitation = 随时欢迎
