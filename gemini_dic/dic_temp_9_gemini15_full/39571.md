consciousnesses: /ˈkɒnʃəsnɪsɪz/ /ˈkɑːnʃəsnɪsɪz/ | n. |意识状态；意识的总和；认知能力|The state of being aware of and responsive to one's surroundings.  It often refers to the totality of a being's subjective experiences, thoughts, feelings, and sensations.  The word also encompasses the awareness of oneself as an individual.|例句：The drug affected her consciousnesses, making her disoriented and confused.  药物影响了她的意识，使她迷失方向并感到困惑。

例句：  Exploring different states of consciousnesses is a key aspect of many spiritual practices.  探索不同的意识状态是许多灵性实践的关键方面。


近义词：awareness, sentience, perception, mindfulness

反义词：unconsciousness, insensibility, unawareness


词性：名词
