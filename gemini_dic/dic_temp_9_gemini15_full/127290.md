navigates:/ˈnævɪɡeɪts/| vt.| 导航；驾驶；顺利通过；设法应对 |To find one's way or guide a vehicle through (a place, course, or system).  It implies skillful maneuvering and overcoming obstacles. |例句：The captain skillfully navigates the ship through the stormy seas. 船长熟练地驾驶着船只穿过暴风雨的海面。

例句：She navigates the complex bureaucracy with ease. 她轻松地应对了复杂的官僚机构。


近义词：steer, guide, pilot, manage, negotiate


反义词：founder, flounder, stray


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The captain navigates the ship expertly. = 船长熟练地驾驶着船只。
He navigates the city streets with ease. = 他轻松地穿梭于城市街道。


一般过去时:
The sailor navigated the treacherous waters. = 水手航行了险峻的水域。
They navigated the difficult negotiation successfully. = 他们成功地完成了艰难的谈判。


现在完成时:
We have navigated many challenges in our business. = 我们在生意中克服了许多挑战。
She has navigated the complexities of the legal system. = 她已经应对了法律体系的复杂性。


过去完成时:
He had navigated the maze before, so he knew the way. = 他以前走过这个迷宫，所以他知道路。
They had navigated the political landscape for years. = 他们多年来一直在应对政治局势。


将来时:
We will navigate the river tomorrow. = 我们明天将航行这条河。
They will navigate the upcoming merger carefully. = 他们将谨慎地应对即将到来的合并。


虚拟语气:
If I had a map, I could navigate this forest. = 如果我有一张地图，我就能穿越这片森林。
I wish I could navigate this software better. = 我希望我能更好地使用这个软件。


被动语态:
The ship was navigated by an experienced captain. = 这艘船由一位经验丰富的船长驾驶。
The project was successfully navigated through difficult times. = 这个项目在艰难时期成功地渡过了难关。


疑问句:
How do you navigate this software? = 你如何使用这个软件？
Can you navigate this complicated situation? = 你能应对这种复杂的局面吗？


否定句:
He couldn't navigate the unfamiliar roads. = 他无法驾驭不熟悉的道路。
The company failed to navigate the economic downturn. = 这家公司未能渡过经济低迷时期。
