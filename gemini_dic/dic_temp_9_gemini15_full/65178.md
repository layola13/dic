ethnological:/ˌɛθnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 人种学的；民族学的 | Relating to ethnology; the study of the origins and cultures of different races and peoples.  | Example: The museum houses a fascinating ethnological collection of artifacts from around the world. 这个博物馆收藏了来自世界各地引人入胜的人类学文物。 |近义词：anthropological, cultural | 反义词：N/A
