lorries:/ˈlɒriz/| n.|卡车，货车|A lorry is a large motor vehicle used for transporting goods.  It's the British English equivalent of the American English word "truck".|例句：The lorry was carrying a heavy load of bricks. 这辆卡车载着大量的砖头。|例句：Several lorries were parked outside the factory.几辆卡车停在工厂外面。|近义词：truck, van (smaller)|反义词：None specifically, but  "car" or "passenger vehicle" could be considered opposites in terms of function.|名词
