英文解析：In entomology, the metasternum is the ventral (bottom) sclerite of the metathorax, the third thoracic segment of an insect.  It's part of the insect's exoskeleton and often plays a role in muscle attachment and overall body support. The metasternum's structure and characteristics can be important for insect identification and classification.

The researchers carefully examined the metasternum of the beetle specimen to determine its species.  研究人员仔细检查了甲虫标本的中胸骨以确定其物种。
