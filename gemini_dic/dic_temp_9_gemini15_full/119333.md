microbreweries:/ˈmaɪkroʊˈbruːəriːz/| n.|小型啤酒厂；精酿啤酒厂|Small breweries that produce beer on a small scale, often emphasizing unique or local ingredients and brewing techniques.|例句：Many microbreweries have opened in the city recently, offering a wide variety of craft beers.  最近很多小型啤酒厂在城里开业，提供各种各样的精酿啤酒。|例句：The success of local microbreweries has boosted the city's tourism. 当地小型啤酒厂的成功推动了该市的旅游业发展。|近义词：craft breweries, small breweries|反义词：large-scale breweries, mass-production breweries
