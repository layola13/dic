musashi:/muˈʃɑːʃi/ | n. | 武藏；宫本武藏 |  Musashi refers to a Japanese province (now part of Tokyo and Saitama Prefectures), a famous swordsman Miyamoto Musashi, and can also be used as a given name.  It can also refer to works inspired by Miyamoto Musashi, such as video games and anime. | Example:  Miyamoto Musashi was a legendary swordsman. 宫本武藏是一位传奇剑客。 The video game "Musashi" is based on his life.  游戏《武藏》是根据他的生平改编的。| 近义词: (depending on context) swordsman, warrior, samurai | 反义词: (no direct antonyms) | 名词
