malfunctioning:/ˌmælˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ/ | adj. | 故障的；功能失常的 |  Describes something that is not working properly or as intended. It implies a temporary or intermittent failure, rather than a complete breakdown. |


例句：

The computer system is malfunctioning, causing delays in processing orders.  = 计算机系统出现故障，导致订单处理延误。

Due to malfunctioning brakes, the car crashed into a tree. = 由于刹车失灵，汽车撞上了树。


近义词：faulty, defective, broken, failing, out of order


反义词：functioning, working, operational


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine is malfunctioning. = 引擎正在发生故障。
The printer is malfunctioning again. = 打印机又出故障了。


一般过去时:
The machine malfunctioned during the test. = 机器在测试过程中发生故障。
The software malfunctioned yesterday. = 软件昨天出现故障了。


现在完成时:
The system has malfunctioned several times this week. = 本周系统已经出现过几次故障。
The car has been malfunctioning since last month. = 自从上个月以来，汽车就一直出现故障。


过去完成时:
The plane had malfunctioned before it crashed. = 飞机坠毁前就已经出现故障了。
The equipment had malfunctioned repeatedly, leading to production delays. = 设备反复出现故障，导致生产延误。


将来时:
The air conditioner will likely malfunction in the summer heat. = 空调很可能在夏季高温下发生故障。
I think the washing machine will malfunction soon. = 我认为洗衣机很快就会出现故障。


虚拟语气:
If the brakes had not malfunctioned, the accident would not have happened. = 如果刹车没有失灵，事故就不会发生了。
If the computer were malfunctioning, we would have to call for help. = 如果电脑出现故障，我们就得打电话求助。


被动语态:
The problem was caused by a malfunctioning component. = 问题是由一个故障部件引起的。
The flight was delayed because the plane's engine was malfunctioning. = 由于飞机引擎故障，航班延误了。


疑问句:
Is the refrigerator malfunctioning? = 冰箱是不是出故障了？
Are there any malfunctioning parts in the machine? = 机器里有没有什么部件出故障了？


否定句:
The equipment is not malfunctioning; it's simply turned off. = 设备没有故障；它只是被关掉了。
The system wasn't malfunctioning; it was overloaded. = 系统没有故障；它是过载了。


PHRASE->
malfunctioning equipment = 故障设备
malfunctioning system = 故障系统
malfunctioning component = 故障组件
malfunctioning parts = 故障部件
malfunctioning appliance = 故障家电
