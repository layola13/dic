dumpings:/ˈdʌmpɪŋz/ | n. |倾倒物；倾销；廉价倾销 |The act of dumping something, especially waste or goods at a lower price than the cost of production.  Dumpings can refer to the disposal of waste materials or the practice of selling goods in a foreign market at a price below their domestic price. |例句：The illegal dumpings of toxic waste polluted the river.  非法倾倒有毒废物污染了河流。

The large-scale dumpings of cheap imported steel damaged the domestic industry.  大量倾销廉价进口钢材损害了国内产业。|近义词：disposal, discharge,  underselling,  |反义词：conservation, preservation, fair trade.
