phoenixlike:/ˈfiːnɪkslaɪk/| adj. |像凤凰一样的；死而复生的；复兴的 |Resembling a phoenix;  characterized by or suggesting rebirth or resurrection.  The term evokes the mythical bird's ability to rise from ashes, symbolizing renewal, immortality, and cyclical regeneration. |例句：The city, after the devastating earthquake, showed a phoenixlike recovery.  地震过后，这座城市展现了凤凰涅槃般的复苏。

The company, despite facing bankruptcy, demonstrated a phoenixlike resurgence in the market.  这家公司尽管面临破产，却在市场上展现了凤凰涅槃般的复兴。


近义词：reborn, resurrected, revived, renewed, immortal

反义词：destroyed, extinguished, annihilated, defunct, dead


词性:形容词
