priapism:/ˈpraɪəpɪzəm/| n. |阴茎持续勃起|A persistent and usually painful erection of the penis that is not associated with sexual arousal.  It's a medical condition that requires treatment because it can damage the penis if left untreated.  The condition can be caused by various factors, including injury, certain medications, blood disorders, and neurological problems.|例句：He experienced priapism after taking a new medication. 他服用新药后出现了阴茎持续勃起。

例句：The persistent priapism led to significant pain and required immediate medical intervention.  持续的阴茎持续勃起导致剧烈疼痛，需要立即进行医疗干预。


近义词：prolonged erection, persistent erection|反义词：flaccidity, detumescence|词性：名词
