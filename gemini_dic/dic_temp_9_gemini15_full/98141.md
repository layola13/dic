insanities:/ɪnˈsænɪtiz/| n.|疯狂；精神错乱；愚蠢的行为|The plural form of "insanity," referring to multiple instances of madness, foolishness, or extreme unreasonableness.  It can refer to actual mental illness or to wildly irrational behavior.|例句：His actions were a series of insanities that ultimately led to his downfall.他的行为是一连串的疯狂举动，最终导致了他的垮台。
例句：The insanities of war are often overlooked in the pursuit of victory.战争的疯狂之处在追求胜利的过程中常常被忽视。|近义词：madness, craziness, lunacy, folly|反义词：sanity, reasonableness, sense
