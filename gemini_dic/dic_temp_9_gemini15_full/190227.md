saplings:/ˈsæplɪŋz/| n.|树苗，幼树|Young trees, especially those recently planted.  |例句：The saplings were carefully planted in the newly prepared soil.  树苗被小心地种在刚准备好的土壤里。
例句：We need to protect the saplings from strong winds. 我们需要保护树苗免受强风的侵害。|近义词：seedlings, young trees|反义词：mature trees, old trees
