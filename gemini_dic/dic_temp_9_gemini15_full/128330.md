neuterer:/ˈnjuːtərər/| n.|使中性者；中和剂|One who or that which makes neutral or neutralizes something.  A neuterer is someone or something that removes the sexual characteristics of an animal, typically through castration or spaying. The term can also refer to something that neutralizes or counteracts the effect of something else.|例句：The farmer acted as the neuterer for his flock of sheep.  这个农民充当了他羊群的阉割者。|近义词：castrator, neutralizer|反义词：activator, stimulator|名词
