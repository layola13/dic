silverize:/ˈsɪlvəraɪz/| vt.|镀银，使具有银的特性|To coat (something) with silver or a silver-like substance.  The process often involves chemical or electrochemical methods to deposit a thin layer of silver onto a surface.  This improves the appearance, conductivity, or reflectivity of the object.|例句：They silverized the Christmas tree ornaments to give them a shiny finish. 他们给圣诞树装饰品镀银以使其具有闪亮的表面。

They silverized the mirror to improve its reflectivity. 他们给镜子镀银以提高其反射率。


近义词：plate, coat, silver-plate|反义词：oxidize, tarnish
