hurricane:/ˈhʌrɪkeɪn/| n.|飓风|A hurricane is a storm with a violent wind, in particular a tropical cyclone in the Atlantic Ocean.|例句：The hurricane caused widespread devastation. 飓风造成了大范围的破坏。|近义词：typhoon, cyclone, tempest|反义词：calm, breeze
