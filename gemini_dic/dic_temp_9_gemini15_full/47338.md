de-shelling:/ˌdiːˈʃɛlɪŋ/| v.|去壳；去除外壳 |The process of removing the shell or husk from something, typically seeds, nuts, or shellfish.|例句：The de-shelling of the peanuts is a labor-intensive process. 花生去壳是一个劳动密集型的过程。|例句：The machine efficiently de-shells the corn, separating the kernels from the cob. 这台机器有效地去除玉米的壳，将玉米粒与玉米棒分开。|近义词：hulling, shelling, peeling|反义词：shelling (in the sense of adding a shell)
