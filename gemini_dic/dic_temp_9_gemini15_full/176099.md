ray:/reɪ/ | n. |光线；射线；一丝；少量 | A ray is a beam of light, heat, or other radiation.  It can also refer to a small amount or trace of something. |例句：A ray of sunshine broke through the clouds.  阳光透过云层射了下来。
The x-rays showed a fracture in his leg. X光显示他的腿骨折了。|近义词：beam, gleam, streak |反义词：darkness, shadow
