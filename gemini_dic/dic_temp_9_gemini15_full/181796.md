relinquishing: /rɪˈlɪŋkwɪʃɪŋ/ | v. | 放弃；交出；让渡 | The act of giving up something.  It implies a voluntary surrender, often of something valued or important. |例句：He is relinquishing his claim to the throne. 他正在放弃他对王位的要求。


例句：After years of struggle, she relinquished her dream of becoming a doctor.  多年奋斗后，她放弃了成为医生的梦想。


近义词：abandon, surrender, cede, forgo, forsake


反义词：retain, keep, hold, claim, acquire


词性：动词 (现在分词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He relinquishes his right to the inheritance. 他放弃了他继承权。
She relinquishes control of the company. 她放弃了对公司的控制。


一般过去时:
He relinquished his position as CEO. 他辞去了首席执行官的职务。
The government relinquished its claim to the land. 政府放弃了对这块土地的所有权。


现在完成时:
She has relinquished all hope of finding him. 她已经放弃了找到他的所有希望。
They have relinquished their efforts to reach an agreement. 他们已经放弃了达成协议的努力。


过去完成时:
He had relinquished his pursuit of fame and fortune. 他已经放弃了他追求名利。
The company had relinquished its market share to its competitors. 这家公司已经将其市场份额拱手让给了竞争对手。


将来时:
She will relinquish her duties next month. 她下个月将卸任。
He will relinquish his grip on the steering wheel. 他将松开方向盘。


虚拟语气:
If he relinquished his control, the company might survive. 如果他放弃控制，公司或许能生存下来。
I wish he had relinquished his stubbornness earlier. 我希望他早点放弃他的固执。


被动语态:
His claim was relinquished by the court. 他的要求被法院驳回了。
Control of the project was relinquished to the new team. 项目的控制权被移交给新的团队。


疑问句:
Will he relinquish his seat on the board? 他会放弃他在董事会的席位吗？
Is she willing to relinquish her position? 她愿意放弃自己的职位吗？


否定句:
He will not relinquish his belief in justice. 他不会放弃他对正义的信念。
She refused to relinquish her hold on the bag. 她拒绝松开对包的抓握。


PHRASE->
relinquish control 放弃控制

relinquish possession 放弃所有权
relinquish one's claim 放弃索赔
relinquish hope 放弃希望

词源:
Relinquishing comes from the Old French word "relinquisher," which itself derives from the Latin word "relinquere," meaning "to leave behind," "to abandon," or "to forsake."  The prefix "re-" intensifies the meaning, implying a complete and final giving up.
