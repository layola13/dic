enumerative:/ɪˈnjuːmərətɪv/ | adj. | 列举的；枚举的 | Relating to or involving the process of enumerating or listing items.  It emphasizes the act of systematically counting or listing individual items. | The enumerative approach to the problem proved to be inefficient.  列举法解决这个问题被证明效率低下。 | descriptive, listing, cataloging | comprehensive, holistic | adjective


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The report enumerates the company's assets. = 报告列举了公司的资产。
She enumerates the advantages of the new system. = 她列举了新系统的优点。

一般过去时:
The teacher enumerated the rules of the game. = 老师列举了游戏的规则。
He enumerated the reasons for his resignation. = 他列举了他辞职的原因。

现在完成时:
She has enumerated all the possibilities. = 她已经列举了所有可能性。
We have enumerated the items in the inventory. = 我们已经清点并列出了库存清单中的物品。

过去完成时:
They had enumerated the risks before making the decision. = 他们在做决定之前已经列举了风险。
She had enumerated the steps in the process before starting. = 在开始之前，她已经列举了流程中的步骤。

将来时:
He will enumerate the points in his presentation. = 他将在他的演示文稿中列举要点。
They will enumerate the challenges facing the project. = 他们将列举该项目面临的挑战。

虚拟语气:
If he enumerated all the facts, the situation would be clearer. = 如果他列举所有事实，情况就会更清楚。
I wish she had enumerated her concerns more clearly. = 我希望她更清楚地列举了她的担忧。

被动语态:
The items were enumerated in the report. = 报告中列举了这些项目。
The advantages of the program were enumerated by the speaker. = 演讲者列举了该项目的优点。

疑问句:
Does the contract enumerate all the terms and conditions? = 合同是否列明了所有条款和条件？
Have they enumerated all the potential hazards? = 他们是否列举了所有潜在的危害？

否定句:
The report did not enumerate all the relevant factors. = 报告没有列举所有相关因素。
He failed to enumerate all the problems encountered. = 他未能列举遇到的所有问题。
