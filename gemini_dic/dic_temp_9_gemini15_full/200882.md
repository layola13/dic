It can also imply slyness or deceitfulness. |例句：The slinkiness of the silk dress made her look elegant.  丝绸衣服的柔软使她看起来很优雅。 The slinkiness of his movements betrayed his guilt. 他偷偷摸摸的动作暴露了他的罪行。|近义词：suppleness, sinuosity, slyness, sneakiness |反义词：stiffness, rigidity, honesty, openness
