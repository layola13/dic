haunting:/ˈhɔːntɪŋ/| /ˈhɑːntɪŋ/ | adj. |令人难忘的；萦绕心头的；鬼怪出没的 |  Evoking a feeling of mystery, dread, or fascination, often due to its intense emotional impact or persistent presence in memory.  It suggests something unforgettable, often in a slightly unsettling or melancholic way. | 例句：The haunting melody stayed with me long after the concert ended.  演唱会结束后，那令人难忘的旋律还萦绕在我的心头。


The haunting image of the abandoned house filled my dreams.  废弃房屋的令人难忘的景象充满了我的梦境。


近义词：memorable, unforgettable, lingering, pervasive, spectral, ghostly | 反义词：forgettable, fleeting, transient
