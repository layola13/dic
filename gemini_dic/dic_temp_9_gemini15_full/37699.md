companions:/kəmˈpænɪənz/| n.|同伴，伙伴，同事|A companion is a person who spends a lot of time with another person, often as a friend.  It can also refer to something that accompanies or goes along with something else.|例句：She and her companions went on a hiking trip. 她和她的同伴们去了一次徒步旅行。|例句：The book is a companion to the film. 这本书是电影的配套读物。|近义词：friends, associates, colleagues, mates|反义词：enemies, rivals, adversaries|名词
