rapid-fire:/ˈræpɪd faɪər/| adj.|快速射击的；连续不断的；|快速的，连续不断的，指说话或行动速度极快，像机关枪一样。 It describes something that happens very quickly and repeatedly, like a machine gun firing bullets.|例句：The comedian delivered a rapid-fire series of jokes.  这位喜剧演员快速地讲了一连串笑话。|例句：He answered the questions in rapid-fire succession. 他连续不断地回答了这些问题。|近义词：fast, quick, incessant, continuous |反义词：slow, gradual, deliberate
