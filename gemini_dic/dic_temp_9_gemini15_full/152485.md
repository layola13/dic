payed:/peɪd/| v.|支付，偿付|Past tense of "pay".  It indicates that payment has already been made.|例句：I payed for the groceries yesterday. 我昨天付了杂货的钱。|例句：She payed her bills on time. 她按时付了账单。|近义词：paid, settled, discharged|反义词：owe, receive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I pay my bills every month. = 我每个月都付账单。
He pays his employees fairly. = 他公平地支付员工薪水。

一般过去时:
I payed the rent last week. = 我上周付了房租。
They payed for the dinner. = 他们付了晚餐的钱。

现在完成时:
I have payed all my debts. = 我已经还清了所有债务。
She has payed the tuition fees. = 她已经支付了学费。

过去完成时:
I had payed the deposit before the deadline. = 我在截止日期前已经支付了押金。
They had payed the contractor before the work started. = 他们在工作开始前已经付了钱给承包商。


将来时:
I will pay you back tomorrow. = 我明天会还钱给你。
She will pay the fine next week. = 她下周会缴纳罚款。

虚拟语气:
If I had payed attention, I would have understood. = 如果我当时注意听，我就明白了。
I wish I had payed more attention in class. = 我希望我上课时更专心一点。

被动语态:
The bills were payed promptly. = 账单被迅速支付了。
The workers will be payed on Friday. = 工人们将在星期五领到工资。

疑问句:
Have you payed the bill yet? = 你付账了吗？
Did he pay for the tickets? = 他买了票了吗？

否定句:
I haven't payed the electricity bill yet. = 我还没付电费。
He didn't pay for the damage. = 他没有赔偿损失。
