condensed milk:/kənˈdɛnst mɪlk/| n.|炼乳|Sweetened milk from which water has been removed.  It is often used in desserts and confectionery.  |例句：She used condensed milk to make her famous caramel sauce. 她用炼乳制作了她著名的焦糖酱。

例句：The recipe called for a can of sweetened condensed milk. 食谱需要一罐加糖炼乳。


近义词：sweetened milk, evaporated milk (though evaporated milk has less sugar)|反义词：None


词源:  The word "condensed" comes from the Latin word "condensare," meaning "to thicken" or "to make dense."  "Milk" has its origins in Proto-Germanic and Proto-Indo-European roots.  The term "condensed milk" thus describes milk that has been thickened by the removal of water.
