filers:/ˈfaɪlər/| n.|提交者；归档者；（尤指）纳税申报者|A filer is a person or entity that submits something, especially a document or form, for official record or processing.  This often refers to individuals or businesses filing tax returns, but it can also apply to other types of filings, such as legal documents or applications.|例句：The tax filers are required to submit their returns by April 15th.  纳税申报者必须在4月15日之前提交他们的申报表。


The company is a major filer of environmental impact reports. 这家公司是环境影响报告的主要提交者。|近义词：applicant, submitter, registrant |反义词：recipient, receiver
