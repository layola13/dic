prev:/prɛv/| adj. |先前的；以前的| preceding in time or order.  |例句：The prev chapter explained the background.  上一章解释了背景。|例句：The prev owner left some tools in the shed.  之前的主人在棚子里留下了一些工具。|近义词：prior, former, previous, antecedent |反义词：following, subsequent, next


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The previous chapter is more difficult than this one. = 前一章比这一章更难。
His previous job was in marketing. = 他以前的工作是在市场营销部门。


一般过去时:
The previous government made many mistakes. = 前一届政府犯了许多错误。
I met him at a previous conference. = 我在之前的会议上见过他。


现在完成时:
I have read all his previous books. = 我读过他以前的所有书。
She has never encountered such a problem in her previous work. = 她以前的工作中从未遇到过这样的问题。


过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, the previous speaker had already finished. = 我到达时，之前的演讲者已经完成了演讲。
They had completed the previous project successfully. = 他们已经成功完成了之前的项目。


将来时:
The previous promises will be kept. = 之前的承诺将会兑现。
I will refer to previous examples to illustrate my point. = 我将参考之前的例子来说明我的观点。
