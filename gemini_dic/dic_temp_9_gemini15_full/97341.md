informs:/ɪnˈfɔːrmz/ | vt. | 通知，告知；使了解 | To give someone facts or information. |例句：The doctor informed me of the test results.医生通知了我检查结果。

例句：He informed his boss that he would be late. 他通知他的老板他将迟到。


近义词：tell, notify, advise, apprise | 反义词：conceal, hide, suppress | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He informs me of the latest news daily. 他每天都通知我最新的消息。
The teacher informs the students of the upcoming exam. 老师通知学生即将到来的考试。


一般过去时:
She informed her parents about her travel plans. 她告诉她的父母她的旅行计划。
The police informed the public about the crime. 警察向公众通报了这起犯罪事件。


现在完成时:
They have informed us of the changes in the schedule. 他们已经通知我们日程安排的变更。
The company has informed its employees of the new policy. 公司已经通知其员工新的政策。


过去完成时:
He had informed his colleagues before leaving for the conference. 他在去参加会议之前已经通知了他的同事。
She had informed the bank of her account details before the transfer.  她在转账之前已经通知了银行她的账户详情。


将来时:
I will inform you as soon as I have the results. 我一拿到结果就会通知你。
The school will inform parents of the school closure. 学校将通知家长学校关闭的消息。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would inform the authorities immediately. 如果我是你，我会立即通知当局。
I wish I had informed him earlier. 我真希望我早点告诉他。


被动语态:
We were informed of the delay by the airline. 我们被航空公司告知了延误的消息。
The students were informed about the change in the curriculum. 学生们被告知了课程的变更。


疑问句:
Did he inform you of his decision? 他是否通知你他的决定？
Has she been informed of the cancellation? 她是否已经被告知取消的消息？


否定句:
I didn't inform them about the meeting. 我没有通知他们关于会议的事情。
He hasn't informed his family of his whereabouts. 他没有告诉他的家人他的下落。


PHRASE->
inform sb of sth = 通知某人某事
keep sb informed = 让某人随时了解情况
well-informed = 消息灵通的
misinform = 误导
be informed by = 受…影响
