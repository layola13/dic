needlepointer:/ˈniːdlˌpɔɪntər/| n. |针尖画者；针线画作品 |A needlepointer is a person who creates artwork using the needlepoint technique.  Needlepoint is a form of embroidery that uses stitches to create a design on canvas.  A needlepoint piece is also referred to as a needlepointer. |Example: My grandmother is a skilled needlepointer, and her creations are truly beautiful.  我的祖母是一位熟练的针尖画者，她的作品非常漂亮。|近义词：needlepoint artist, embroiderer |反义词：N/A
