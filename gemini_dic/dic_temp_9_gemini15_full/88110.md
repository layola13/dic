hijab:/hɪˈdʒɑːb/ | n. | 头巾；面纱 | A veil or headscarf worn by some Muslim women.  It is a religious symbol and a form of modesty. |  She wore a hijab to cover her hair. 她戴着头巾遮盖头发。 |  headscarf, veil |  (None explicitly, but some might consider revealing clothing to be an antonym in the context of modesty.) | 名词
