overaggravate:/ˌoʊvərˈæɡrəveɪt/| v.|加剧；使恶化；过度加重|To aggravate or worsen something to an excessive degree; to make something already bad even worse.|例句：His constant complaining overaggravated the already tense situation. 他不断的抱怨加剧了本已紧张的局势。
例句：Don't overaggravate the injury by continuing to use it. 不要继续使用受伤的部位，以免加重伤势。|近义词：exacerbate, worsen, intensify|反义词：alleviate, improve, soothe|词性:动词
