liquor:/ˈlɪkər/| n. |酒，烈酒 |alcoholic beverages, especially distilled ones.  Liquor is often used to refer to spirits such as whiskey, vodka, gin, and rum, as opposed to wine or beer.|例句：He poured himself a glass of liquor. 他给自己倒了一杯酒。|例句：The store has a wide selection of liquors. 这家商店有很多种酒。|近义词：alcohol, spirits, strong drink |反义词：water, juice
