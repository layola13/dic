celery:/ˈsɛləri/| n. |芹菜|A long, slender vegetable with crisp stalks and a slightly salty flavor. It's commonly used in salads, soups, and as a snack.  |例句：I added some chopped celery to the stew.我把一些切碎的芹菜加进了炖菜里。

例句：She enjoys eating celery sticks with peanut butter. 她喜欢吃花生酱芹菜条。


近义词：None (no perfect synonym, but similar vegetables include fennel and kohlrabi) |反义词：None |词性：noun

词源:

The word "celery" comes from the  Latin word *apium graveolens*, meaning "strong-smelling parsley."  The word evolved through Old French *celeri* and Middle English *selyry*.
