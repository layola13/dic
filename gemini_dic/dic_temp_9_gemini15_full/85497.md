hastelessness: /ˈheɪstləsnəs/ | n. | 从容；不慌不忙；悠闲 | The quality or state of being without haste; calmness; deliberation.  It suggests a peaceful and unhurried approach to life or a task. |  例句：The artist approached her work with a remarkable hastelessness, taking her time to perfect every detail.  艺术家以非凡的从容态度对待她的工作，花时间完善每一个细节。


例句：He demonstrated hastelessness in his response to the crisis, carefully considering all options before acting. 他在应对危机时表现出从容不迫，在采取行动之前仔细考虑了所有选择。


近义词：calmness, composure, serenity, tranquility, leisureliness, unhurriedness


反义词：hurry, rush, haste, urgency, precipitateness


词性: 名词
