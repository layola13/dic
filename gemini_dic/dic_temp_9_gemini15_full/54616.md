dismember:/dɪsˈmɛmbər/ | vt. |肢解；使解体；使分裂 | To cut or tear the body of (a person or animal) into pieces.  The term often implies violence or a forceful separation.  |例句：The body was found dismembered in the woods.  尸体在树林里被发现肢解了。

The serial killer dismembered his victims. 连环杀手肢解了他的受害者。

近义词：mutilate, sever, cut up, dissect | 反义词：assemble, unite, join, reconstruct | 词性:动词

词源:
