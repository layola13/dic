nonthermoplastic:/ˌnɒnˌθɜːrməʊˈplæstɪk/| adj.|非热塑性的|Not capable of being repeatedly softened by heating and hardened by cooling.  Such materials permanently change shape when heated.  |例句：Epoxy resins are nonthermoplastic materials, meaning they set permanently after curing.环氧树脂是非热塑性材料，这意味着它们固化后会永久固化。

例句：The nonthermoplastic nature of the composite material made it unsuitable for recycling.这种复合材料的非热塑性使其不适合回收。


近义词：thermosetting, non-meltable, infusible

反义词：thermoplastic


词性：形容词
