chants:/tʃænts/| n. v.| 吟唱；圣歌；反复诵读| Repeated rhythmic chanting or singing, often of a religious or ceremonial nature.  It can also refer to the act of repeatedly saying something. |例句：The monks chanted prayers for hours.  僧侣们吟唱祈祷数小时。|例句：The crowd chanted the team's name.  人群反复高喊着球队的名字。|近义词：singing, reciting, intoning, calls|反义词：whispering, murmuring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They chant prayers every morning. = 他们每天早上吟诵祈祷文。
The children chant rhymes during playtime. = 孩子们在玩耍时吟唱儿歌。

一般过去时:
The choir chanted a beautiful hymn. = 合唱团吟唱了一首优美的圣歌。
The protesters chanted slogans in the street. = 抗议者在街上高喊口号。

现在完成时:
The team has chanted its victory song many times. = 球队已经多次吟唱他们的胜利之歌。
She has chanted mantras for years to find inner peace. = 她多年来一直诵念咒语以寻求内心的平静。

过去完成时:
They had chanted the anthem before the game started. = 在比赛开始之前，他们已经唱完了国歌。
The crowd had chanted for hours before the speaker arrived. = 在演讲者到达之前，人群已经高喊了数小时。

将来时:
The choir will chant a new piece at the concert. = 合唱团将在音乐会上吟唱一首新的作品。
We will chant slogans to support our cause. = 我们将高喊口号来支持我们的事业。

虚拟语气:
If they chanted louder, they would be heard. = 如果他们喊得更大声，他们就会被听到。
I wish they chanted less loudly. = 我希望他们不要喊那么大声。

被动语态:
The anthem was chanted with great fervor. = 国歌被充满热情地吟唱。
The slogans were chanted by the protesters. = 口号被抗议者高喊。

疑问句:
What did they chant at the rally? = 他们在集会上喊了什么？
Are they chanting a religious song? = 他们在吟唱宗教歌曲吗？

否定句:
They did not chant during the ceremony. = 他们在仪式上没有吟唱。
The students did not chant against the policy. = 学生们没有高喊反对这项政策。
