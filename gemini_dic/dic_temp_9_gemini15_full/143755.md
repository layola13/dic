outmanoeuvre: /ˌaʊtˈmænəvər/ | vt. |  智胜；以策略战胜 | To outwit or defeat (someone) by using clever tactics.  | 例句：The general outmanoeuvred the enemy by using a flanking maneuver. 将军用侧翼机动战术智胜了敌人。
The chess player skillfully outmanoeuvred his opponent, forcing a checkmate.  象棋选手巧妙地击败了他的对手，迫使其将死。| 近义词：outwit, outsmart, outplay,  surpass,  best | 反义词：be outmanoeuvred, be defeated
