hypersthene:/ˈhaɪpərsθin/| n. |紫苏辉石|A dark-colored, often bronze or greenish-brown, rock-forming mineral belonging to the pyroxene group.  It's a silicate mineral containing iron, magnesium, and calcium. Hypersthene is commonly found in igneous and metamorphic rocks.|The geologist identified hypersthene in the sample collected from the volcanic eruption.|近义词：enstatite (when magnesium-rich)|反义词：无|名词
