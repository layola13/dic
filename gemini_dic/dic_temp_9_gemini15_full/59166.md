dysenteric:/ˌdɪsənˈtɛrɪk/| adj.|痢疾的，患痢疾的| Relating to or characterized by dysentery.  The adjective describes something connected with or suffering from dysentery, a severe form of diarrhea.|The dysenteric patient was severely dehydrated. 这位痢疾患者严重脱水。|The doctor diagnosed a dysenteric infection. 医生诊断为痢疾感染。|diarrheal, diarrhoeic|healthy, well
