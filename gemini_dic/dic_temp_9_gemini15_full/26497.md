caballed:/kəˈbæld/| v.|密谋；结党；阴谋策划 |To secretly plan with others to do something bad or illegal.  |例句：The politicians were accused of having cabalbed to overthrow the government.  政客们被指控密谋推翻政府。
例句：They cabalbed together to ruin their rival's business. 他们密谋在一起毁掉竞争对手的生意。|近义词：conspire, plot, intrigue|反义词：cooperate, collaborate
