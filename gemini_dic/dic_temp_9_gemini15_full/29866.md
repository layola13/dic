causerie:/ˈkɔːzəri/| n. |闲谈；聊天；随意谈话 |A causerie is a light, informal conversation or chat, often on a particular topic. It's typically characterized by its relaxed and friendly atmosphere, unlike a formal debate or lecture.  | Example:  We enjoyed a delightful causerie over coffee about our travels. 我们一边喝咖啡一边愉快地聊着旅行的事。|近义词：chat, conversation, discussion, colloquy |反义词：lecture, debate, sermon
