polo:/ˈpoʊloʊ/ | n. |马球；马球衫；一种针织衫 |A polo is a sport played on horseback, with teams of players trying to score by hitting a ball through goals.  It can also refer to a type of short-sleeved shirt, typically with a collar and buttons. |例句：He played polo professionally for many years. 他多年来一直从事马球运动。

He wore a crisp white polo shirt to the party. 他穿着干净利落的白色马球衫参加派对。|近义词： (for the sport) equestrian sport; (for the shirt) T-shirt, shirt |反义词：无
