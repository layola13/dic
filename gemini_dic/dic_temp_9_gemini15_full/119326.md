microbiologists:/ˌmaɪkroʊbaɪˈɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. |微生物学家 | Scientists who study microorganisms (bacteria, viruses, fungi, etc.).  They research the growth, structure, function, and classification of microorganisms, and their roles in various environments. | Example: Microbiologists are working to develop new antibiotics to combat drug-resistant bacteria. 微生物学家正在努力开发新的抗生素来对抗耐药细菌。  |近义词: microbial scientists, bacteriologists (if focusing on bacteria), virologists (if focusing on viruses), mycologists (if focusing on fungi) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
