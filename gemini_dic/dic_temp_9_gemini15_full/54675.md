disorder:/dɪsˈɔːrdər/ | n. |紊乱；疾病；骚乱 | A lack of order or regularity; a state of confusion.  A disturbance of the peace; riot. A mental or physical illness. |例句：The room was in a state of disorder.  房间一片混乱。  The political disorder led to violence. 政治动乱导致暴力。 The doctor diagnosed a serious disorder. 医生诊断出一种严重的疾病。 |近义词：chaos, turmoil, confusion, illness, disease |反义词：order, peace, calm, health
