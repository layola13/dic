muscle-in: /ˈmʌsəl ɪn/ | v. |强行介入；插手；强行加入 | To intrude or force one's way into a situation, group, or activity, often aggressively or without invitation.  |例句：Several companies tried to muscle in on the lucrative new market.  几家公司试图强行进入这个利润丰厚的新市场。

例句：The new kid muscled in on their game of basketball. 新来的孩子强行加入他们的篮球游戏。


近义词：intrude, barge in, butt in, horn in, crash, interlope


反义词：withdraw, retreat, abstain, stand aside


词性:动词
