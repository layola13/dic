presuspended:/ˌpriːsəˈspɛndɪd/| adj.| 预先暂停的，提前中止的 | Pre-suspended refers to something that has been suspended or stopped before its scheduled or usual time.  It implies a proactive or preventative measure, rather than a reaction to an event. | Example: The presuspended bridge project was due to budget cuts.  预先暂停的桥梁项目是由于预算削减。 The presuspended sentence was later overturned.  预先暂停的判决后来被推翻了。|近义词：preempted, postponed, deferred, halted |反义词：continued, resumed, commenced|
