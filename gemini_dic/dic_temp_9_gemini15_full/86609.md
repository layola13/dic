heliosphere:/ˈhiːliəʊsfɪər/| n. |日球层|The heliosphere is the region of space dominated by the Sun's magnetic field and solar wind.  It extends far beyond the orbit of Pluto, forming a protective bubble around our solar system. |Example: The Voyager 1 spacecraft has reached the edge of the heliosphere. 旅行者1号探测器已经到达日球层的边缘。|近义词：heliospheric bubble|反义词:  interstellar space
