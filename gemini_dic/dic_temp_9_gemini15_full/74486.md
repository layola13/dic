franklin:/ˈfræŋklɪn/| n. |富兰克林；法兰克林|  A surname, also the name of several places and things, most famously associated with Benjamin Franklin.  The name itself derives from Old French or Old German origins, possibly meaning "freeholder" or relating to a place name. |例句：The Franklin Institute in Philadelphia is a renowned science museum.  费城富兰克林研究所是一个著名的科学博物馆。|近义词：无直接近义词|反义词：无直接反义词|名词
