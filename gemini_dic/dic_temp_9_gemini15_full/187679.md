rookeries:/ˈrʊkəriːz/| n.|鸟类群居地；（海豹等的）繁殖地|A rookery is a large colony of birds, especially rooks, nesting together.  It can also refer to a breeding ground for seals or other animals.|例句：The island is a major rookery for several species of seabirds.  这个岛屿是几种海鸟的主要繁殖地。|例句：The researchers observed the rookery's complex social structure. 研究人员观察了鸟类群居地的复杂社会结构。|近义词：colony, breeding ground|反义词：solitary habitat
