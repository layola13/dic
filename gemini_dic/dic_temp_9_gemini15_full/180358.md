refunds: /ˈriːfʌndz/ | n. |退款；偿还 |  Money returned to a buyer after they have returned a faulty product or cancelled a service.  Refunds are typically processed through the same method of payment as the original purchase.  |


例句：

The store offered a full refund after I returned the damaged shoes. = 我退回损坏的鞋子后，商店提供了全额退款。

I requested a refund for the flight that was canceled due to bad weather. = 我要求退款，因为航班由于恶劣天气被取消。


近义词：reimbursements, repayments


反义词：charges, costs


词性：名词
