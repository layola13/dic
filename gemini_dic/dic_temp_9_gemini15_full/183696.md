rerouting:/ˌriːˈraʊtɪŋ/| n.|重新布线；改道；路线变更 |The act of changing a route, especially of a network or transportation system.  It involves redirecting something, like data packets in a network or vehicles on a road, to a new path.|例句：The network administrator performed a rerouting of the data packets to avoid congestion. 网络管理员对数据包进行了重新路由以避免拥塞。

例句：Due to the road closure, the traffic was rerouted through a nearby residential area. 由于道路封闭，交通被改道到附近的住宅区。


近义词：redirection, diversion, realignment |反义词：direct routing, straight path


词性:名词
