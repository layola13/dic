hyperarid:/ˈhaɪpərærɪd/| adj.|极度干旱的|Extremely arid; characterized by an extremely low level of precipitation, typically less than 50 mm (2 inches) per year.  Such regions often experience intense solar radiation and large diurnal temperature ranges.|例句：The Atacama Desert is a hyperarid region known for its extreme dryness.  阿塔卡马沙漠是一个以极度干燥而闻名的极度干旱地区。

The hyperarid conditions in this area make plant life almost impossible.  这个地区的极度干旱条件使得植物几乎无法生存。|近义词：extremely arid, desert, parched|反义词：humid, wet, lush
