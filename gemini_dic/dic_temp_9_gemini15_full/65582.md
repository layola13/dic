evacuees:/ˌɛvækjuˈiːz/| n. |疏散者，撤离者 |People who have been evacuated from a place of danger.  Evacuees often find themselves in temporary shelters or with host families until they can return home. |例句：The evacuees from the wildfire were given temporary shelter in the community center.  这场野火中的疏散人员在社区中心得到了临时住所。  The government provided food and clothing for the earthquake evacuees. 政府为地震疏散人员提供了食物和衣物。|近义词：refugees, displaced persons |反义词：settlers, residents |名词
