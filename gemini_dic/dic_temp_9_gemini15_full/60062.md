edutainment:/ˌɛdjuːˈteɪnmənt/ | n. | 教育娱乐；寓教于乐 |  The combination of education and entertainment; activities or programs designed to be both educational and entertaining.  | Example:  Many museums now offer edutainment programs for children. 许多博物馆现在为儿童提供寓教于乐的节目。


近义词：educational entertainment, learning entertainment, instructive amusement

反义词：None directly opposite, but antonyms could include  pure entertainment (lacking educational value) or pure instruction (lacking entertainment value).


词源:  A portmanteau word formed by combining "education" and "entertainment".  The term emerged in the late 20th century to describe media and activities that blend learning and fun.
