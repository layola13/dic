preresolutions:/ˌpriːˌrezəˈluːʃənz/| n. |预决议；预先决议| Resolutions made in advance of a formal meeting or decision-making process.  These are typically preliminary discussions or agreements that lay the groundwork for a final resolution.  They might be proposals, suggestions, or tentative decisions that are subject to later confirmation or modification. | Example: The preresolutions drafted by the committee were presented to the board for review and final approval.  预决议委员会起草的预决议提交董事会审查和最终批准。|近义词：preliminary proposals, advance preparations, tentative agreements|反义词：final resolutions, conclusive decisions|名词
