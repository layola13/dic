gents:/dʒɛnts/| n.|男士们，先生们|A shortened form of "gentlemen".  It is often used as a polite form of address for a group of men, similar to "ladies" for women. It can also be used as a label for a men's restroom.|Example: "Gents' toilets are down the hall."  男士洗手间在走廊尽头。|近义词：gentlemen, men, fellows|反义词：ladies
