porta:/ˈpɔːrtə/ | n. | 门, 门户, 入口 | A door or gate; an opening, passage, or means of access.  | Example: The heavy oak porta creaked open. 这扇沉重的橡木门吱呀一声开了。 | 近义词：door, gate, entrance | 反义词：exit, outlet
