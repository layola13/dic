cofinancing:/ˌkoʊˈfaɪnænsɪŋ/| n. |共同融资；联贷|The act of two or more parties providing funding for a single project or venture.  It often involves sharing the financial risk and responsibilities. |例句：The new stadium project relied heavily on cofinancing from both the public and private sectors.  新的体育场项目严重依赖公共和私营部门的共同融资。|例句：The small business secured cofinancing from a bank and a venture capital firm. 这家小企业获得了银行和风险投资公司的共同融资。|近义词：joint financing, shared funding |反义词：sole financing, individual funding|名词
