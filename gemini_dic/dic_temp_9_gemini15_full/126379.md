myrmicine:/ˈmɜːrmɪsaɪn/| adj. |蚁亚科的；猛蚁亚科的 |Relating to the Myrmicinae, a subfamily of ants characterized by their distinctive features, often including a two-segmented node on their petiole (the narrow waist between the thorax and abdomen).  Many species in this subfamily are known for their aggressive behavior and powerful stings. | Example:  The myrmicine ant *Solenopsis invicta*, also known as the red imported fire ant, is a highly invasive species.  猛蚁亚科蚂蚁，例如入侵红火蚁（Solenopsis invicta），是一种具有高度入侵性的物种。| 近义词：formicine (referring to a different ant subfamily) | 反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with terms describing other ant subfamilies. | adj.
