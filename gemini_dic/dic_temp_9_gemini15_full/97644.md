inheritor:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtər/ | n. |继承人，承受者 | A person who inherits something, especially property or a title.  |例句：He is the sole inheritor of his father's vast estate. 他是他父亲巨额遗产的唯一继承人。|例句：As the inheritor of a long tradition, she felt a strong sense of responsibility. 作为悠久传统的继承者，她感到强烈的责任感。|近义词：heir, successor, recipient |反义词：donor, testator (in the context of wills)


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The inheritor manages the family business. = 继承人管理着家族企业。
She is the inheritor of a great legacy. = 她是一位伟大遗产的继承人。

一般过去时:
He became the inheritor of his uncle's fortune. = 他成为了他叔叔财产的继承人。
They were the inheritors of a rich cultural heritage. = 他们是丰富的文化遗产的继承者。

现在完成时:
She has become the inheritor of her grandmother's jewelry collection. = 她已经成为她祖母珠宝收藏的继承人。
He has inherited his father's business and has successfully expanded it. = 他继承了他父亲的事业，并成功地将其扩张了。

过去完成时:
Before his death, he had named his daughter the inheritor of his estate. = 他去世前，已指定他的女儿为他的遗产继承人。
They had already identified the rightful inheritor before the dispute arose. = 在争议出现之前，他们已经确定了合法的继承人。

将来时:
She will be the inheritor of a significant sum of money. = 她将成为一笔巨款的继承人。
He will likely be the inheritor of his family's prestigious reputation. = 他很可能会继承家族的显赫声誉。

虚拟语气:
If he were the inheritor, he would restore the old house. = 如果他是继承人，他会修复老房子。
I wish I were the inheritor of that fortune. = 我真希望我是那笔财富的继承人。

被动语态:
The property was inherited by the rightful inheritor. = 财产被合法的继承人继承了。
The throne was inherited by the eldest inheritor. = 王位由最年长的继承人继承。


疑问句:
Who is the inheritor of this property? = 谁是这处房产的继承人？
Are you the inheritor of your family's business? = 你是你家族企业的继承人吗？


否定句:
He is not the inheritor of the throne. = 他不是王位的继承人。
She was not the inheritor of her father's will. = 她不是她父亲遗嘱的继承人。


PHRASE->

sole inheritor = 唯一继承人
legal inheritor = 法定继承人
heirs and inheritors = 继承人和继承人（常用于法律文件）
potential inheritor = 潜在继承人
designated inheritor = 指定继承人


词源:

inherit (v.) + -or (noun suffix)

inherit 源自古法语 heriter，最终源自拉丁语 heres “继承人”。 -or 是一个名词后缀，表示人或事物。
