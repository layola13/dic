mesencephalic:/ˌmɛsɛnˈsɛfəlɪk/| adj.|中脑的；中脑的|Of or relating to the mesencephalon (midbrain).  The mesencephalon is a part of the brain located between the diencephalon and the pons.|例句：The study focused on mesencephalic dopamine neurons. 这项研究集中在中脑多巴胺神经元上。


例句：Mesencephalic structures play a crucial role in visual and auditory reflexes. 中脑结构在视觉和听觉反射中起着至关重要的作用。


近义词：midbrain (as a noun, not an adjective)


反义词：无


词性:形容词
