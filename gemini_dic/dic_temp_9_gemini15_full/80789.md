gotchas:/ˈɡɑːtʃəz/ | n. | 陷阱；未预料到的问题；隐患 | Unexpected problems or difficulties that are not immediately obvious.  These are often subtle details that can cause significant issues if overlooked. | Example: "The software seemed easy to use at first, but we quickly encountered several gotchas."  = “这个软件一开始看起来很容易使用，但我们很快就遇到了几个陷阱。”  | 近义词：pitfalls, snags, complications, hidden problems | 反义词：easy solutions, straightforward processes
