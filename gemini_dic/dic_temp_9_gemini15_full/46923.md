darling: /ˈdɑːrlɪŋ/ | n. adj. |亲爱的；宝贝儿；可爱的 |

英文解析：Darling is a term of endearment used to address someone loved or cherished. It can be used as a noun or an adjective, and its usage varies depending on context and relationship.  It can sound affectionate and intimate, but also somewhat old-fashioned or overly familiar depending on the speaker and listener.


例句：

As a noun:

My darling, I love you so much.  = 亲爱的，我非常爱你。

He called his wife his darling. = 他称他的妻子为宝贝。


As an adjective:

She has a darling little dog. = 她有一只可爱的狗狗。

That's a darling dress! = 那件衣服真可爱！



近义词：sweetheart, honey, love, dear, beloved

反义词： (No direct antonyms, as it expresses affection, the opposite would be terms of dislike or indifference)


词性：Noun, Adjective
