pre-revision:/priːriˈvɪʒən/ | n. | 预修，预先修改 | The process of reviewing or modifying something before the final version is created.  It often involves checking for errors, making improvements, and ensuring accuracy and clarity. | 例句：The pre-revision of the manuscript took several weeks.  预修稿件花了几个星期。  |  例句：The editor requested a pre-revision of the article before publication. 编辑要求在发表前预先修改这篇文章。 |  近义词：preliminary review, preliminary edit, draft revision | 反义词：final revision, post-revision | 名词
