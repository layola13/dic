nondiscounted:/ˌnɒnˈdɪskaʊntɪd/| adj. |未打折的，原价的 |Not reduced in price; at the original price.  |The nondiscounted price of the dress was $100.  这件裙子的原价是100美元。|discounted, reduced |discounted, sale price|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The nondiscounted price is higher than the discounted price. = 未打折的价格高于打折的价格。
This item remains nondiscounted until the end of the month. = 本商品在本月底前保持原价。


一般过去时:
The tickets were nondiscounted before the sale began. = 在促销开始之前，票价是原价。
The product remained nondiscounted throughout last year. = 该产品去年全年都保持原价。


现在完成时:
The store has kept many items nondiscounted. = 商店里许多商品一直保持原价。
The airline has not nondiscounted these tickets.  = 这家航空公司没有对这些机票进行原价销售。


过去完成时:
The item had been nondiscounted until the recent promotion. = 在最近的促销活动之前，该商品一直是原价。
They had nondiscounted the goods for a long time before the clearance sale. = 在清仓甩卖之前，他们很长时间都保持商品原价。


将来时:
The price will remain nondiscounted until next month. = 价格将维持到下个月不变。
This product will likely be nondiscounted during peak season. = 此产品在旺季可能不会打折。


虚拟语气:
If the item were nondiscounted, I would not buy it. = 如果该商品不打折，我不会买它。
I wish the tickets were nondiscounted so I could afford them. = 我希望票价是原价，这样我就能买得起了。


被动语态:

疑问句:
Are these items nondiscounted? = 这些商品是原价吗？

否定句:
The shoes are not nondiscounted; they're on sale. = 这双鞋不是原价的；它们正在打折。
The hotel rooms were not nondiscounted for that event. = 宾馆房间在那次活动中没有保持原价。
