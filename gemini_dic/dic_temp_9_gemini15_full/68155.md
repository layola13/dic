英文解析：Failingnesses refers to the state or quality of having many failures or shortcomings.  It implies a multitude of weaknesses or deficiencies, often in a system, plan, or person.  It's a more formal and less frequently used word than its synonyms.

例句：

His many failingnesses eventually led to his downfall.  他的许多缺点最终导致了他的垮台。


The company's failingnesses were exposed during the investigation.  在调查期间，该公司暴露了其不足之处。


词源：源自fail (失败) + -ing (动名词后缀) + -ness (名词后缀).  Essentially, it's the noun form of "being full of failings."
