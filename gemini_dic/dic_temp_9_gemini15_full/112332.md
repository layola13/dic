lores:/ˈlɔːriz/| n.|传说；民间故事；知识；信息|Traditional stories, especially those of a region or community, passed down through generations.  These stories often explain natural phenomena or cultural practices.  They can also refer to a body of knowledge or information.|例句：The old woman told us fascinating lores about the history of the village.  老妇人给我们讲述了关于村庄历史的迷人传说。|例句：The ancient lores of the tribe were passed down orally for centuries.  部落古老的传说几个世纪以来都是口头传承的。|近义词：legends, myths, traditions, folklore|反义词：facts, reality
