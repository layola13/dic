gatekeeper:/ˈɡeɪtkiːpər/| n. |守门人；看门人；门卫；（尤指）控制进入某事物的人或组织|A gatekeeper is a person who controls access to something, often literally a gate, but also figuratively a position, information, or opportunity.  They act as a filter, deciding who or what is allowed to pass. |例句：The gatekeeper checked our tickets before letting us into the concert. 门卫检查了我们的票，然后才让我们进入音乐会。

例句：The editor acts as a gatekeeper, deciding which articles are published. 编辑充当守门人的角色，决定发表哪些文章。


近义词：guard, warden, custodian, controller, filter

反义词：facilitator, enabler
