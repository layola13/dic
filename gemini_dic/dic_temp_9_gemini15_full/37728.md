comparisons:/kəmˈpærɪzənz/ | n. |比较，对比 |The essay relies heavily on comparisons between different cultures. 这篇文章大量依赖不同文化之间的比较。|近义词：contrasts, likenesses, parallels |反义词：differences, dissimilarities


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He makes comparisons between the two paintings. 他比较这两幅画。
She often draws comparisons between her childhood and her adult life. 她经常将她童年和成年生活进行比较。


一般过去时:
The teacher made comparisons of the two poems in class. 老师在课堂上比较了两首诗歌。
They made several comparisons to support their argument. 他们进行了几次比较来支持他们的论点。


现在完成时:
Scientists have made many comparisons of the genomes of different species. 科学家们已经对不同物种的基因组进行了许多比较。
She has made numerous comparisons between the two candidates' policies. 她已经对两位候选人的政策进行了多次比较。


过去完成时:
The historian had already made many comparisons of ancient civilizations before writing his book. 这位历史学家在写书之前已经对古代文明进行了许多比较。
Before concluding his presentation, he had made a series of comparisons to highlight the key differences. 在结束他的演讲之前，他做了一系列的比较来突出关键差异。


将来时:
The researchers will make further comparisons with the new data. 研究人员将用新的数据进行进一步的比较。
We will make a detailed comparison of the two proposals next week. 我们下周将对这两个提案进行详细比较。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would make more detailed comparisons. 如果我有更多时间，我会进行更详细的比较。
I wish I could have made more comparisons before submitting the report. 我希望在提交报告之前我能进行更多比较。


被动语态:
Several comparisons were made to illustrate the point. 做了几次比较来说明这一点。
The data will be compared and analyzed in detail. 数据将被详细比较和分析。


疑问句:
What comparisons can you make between these two approaches? 你能在这两种方法之间作什么比较？
Are there any significant comparisons to be drawn from this research? 从这项研究中可以得出什么重要的比较？


否定句:
The study did not make any valid comparisons. 这项研究没有进行任何有效的比较。
He avoided making direct comparisons between the two products. 他避免直接比较这两种产品。
