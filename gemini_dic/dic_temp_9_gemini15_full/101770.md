irretrievable: /ˌɪrɪˈtriːvəbl/ | adj. | 不可挽回的，无法补救的 |  Impossible to recover or regain; beyond recall or repair.  |例句：The damage to the antique vase was irretrievable.  古董花瓶的损坏是无法挽回的。

例句：The lost data was irretrievable due to a hard drive failure. 由于硬盘故障，丢失的数据无法找回。


近义词：irreparable, unrecoverable, irreversible, irreparable, lost


反义词：recoverable, retrievable, repairable


词源:

* **ir-:**  prefix meaning "not" or "without"
* **re-:** prefix meaning "again" or "back"
* **triev-:** from Old French *treuver* "to find" (related to the English word "to trove")
* **-able:** suffix meaning "capable of being"


用法说明:

irretrievable 通常修饰名词，表示某种损失、损坏或情况是无法挽回的。它强调的是一种永久性的、无法逆转的状态。
