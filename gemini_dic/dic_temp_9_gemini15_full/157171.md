pingan:/pɪŋˈæn/| n. adj.|平安，安全；平静，安宁 |Peace, safety, tranquility; calm, peaceful.|例句：I wish you peace and safety. 我祝你平安。|近义词：safety, security, peace, calm |反义词：danger, peril, unrest, chaos |名词，形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is safe and sound. = 他平安无事。
We are at peace with our neighbors. = 我们和邻居相处得很平静。


一般过去时:
Everything went smoothly and peacefully. = 一切都平安顺利地过去了。
They arrived home safely. = 他们平安到家了。


现在完成时:
We have lived peacefully for many years. = 我们已经和平地生活了许多年。
Nothing bad has happened to me; I'm safe and sound. = 我什么事都没发生，我平安无事。


过去完成时:
They had arrived safely before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前他们已经平安到达了。
We had lived a peaceful life until the war began. = 直到战争爆发之前，我们一直过着平静的生活。


将来时:
I hope everything will be alright and safe. = 我希望一切都会平安无事。
We will live peacefully in this village. = 我们将在这个村庄里过平静的生活。


虚拟语气:
If only I could live a peaceful life. = 但愿我能过上平静的生活。
I wish I had been safe during the accident. = 我真希望我在事故中能平安无事。


被动语态:
The city was kept safe by the police. = 这个城市由警察维持着安全。
Peace was maintained through negotiation. = 通过谈判维持了和平。


疑问句:
Are you safe? = 你安全吗？
Is everything alright? = 一切都好吗？


否定句:
We were not safe in that area. = 我们在那片区域不安全。
The situation was not peaceful at all. = 情况一点也不平静。
