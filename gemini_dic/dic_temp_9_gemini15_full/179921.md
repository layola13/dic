refederates:/ˌriːˈfedəreɪts/| v.|重新联合，重新结盟|To unite or form an alliance again; to reunite into a federation.|The states refederated after a period of civil strife.  各州在一段时间的内乱后重新联合。|近义词：reunite, recombine, realign|反义词：disunite, separate, divide


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The states refederate annually to discuss common issues. = 各州每年重新联合以讨论共同问题。
They refederate their efforts to achieve a common goal. = 他们重新联合他们的努力以实现一个共同的目标。


一般过去时:
The warring factions refederated after the treaty was signed. = 在条约签署后，交战各派重新联合。
The two companies refederated to create a larger entity. = 两家公司重新联合，创建了一个更大的实体。


现在完成时:
The countries have refederated after decades of conflict. = 这些国家在几十年的冲突后已经重新联合。

虚拟语气:
