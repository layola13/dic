narrowband:/ˈnæroʊbænd/| adj.|窄带的，窄频带的|Relating to or denoting a communication system using a relatively small range of frequencies.|例句：The narrowband system offered limited data transmission capabilities.  窄带系统提供的 数据传输能力有限。|近义词：limited-bandwidth, restricted-bandwidth|反义词：broadband, wideband|形容词
