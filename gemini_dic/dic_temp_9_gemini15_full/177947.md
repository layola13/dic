recidivist:/ˈrɛsɪdɪvɪst/| n. |累犯，惯犯|A person who repeatedly commits crimes, especially after having been convicted and punished for previous crimes.  A recidivist is someone who relapses into criminal behavior.|例句：He was a recidivist, having been arrested multiple times for theft. 他是个累犯，多次因盗窃被捕。|例句：The high recidivism rate suggests that the current rehabilitation programs are ineffective. 高度的累犯率表明目前的改造计划无效。|近义词：repeat offender, habitual offender, criminal, delinquent|反义词：first-time offender, law-abiding citizen
