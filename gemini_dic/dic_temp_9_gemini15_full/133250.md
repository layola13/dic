nonexempts:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzɛmpt/ | adj. | 免除...的；非豁免的 | Not exempt; not excluded or excepted.  This word describes things or people that are not given special permission or freedom from a rule, law, or obligation.  | 例句：The nonexempt employees are subject to overtime pay.  非豁免员工需要支付加班费。

The nonexempt employees' salaries are calculated based on hours worked. 非豁免员工的薪水是根据工作时间计算的。

近义词：subject, liable, included

反义词：exempt, excluded, excepted

词性: adjective
