hoopla:/ˈhuːplə/| n.|喧嚣；大肆宣传；热闹；夸张的表演|A commotion; a fuss; exaggerated publicity or excitement.|例句：The media created a huge hoopla over the celebrity's wedding.媒体对这位名人的婚礼大肆宣传。|例句：There was a lot of hoopla surrounding the product launch.产品发布会周围有很多喧嚣。|近义词：commotion, fuss, fanfare, excitement, ado|反义词：calm, quiet, silence
