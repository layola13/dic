harmonize:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪz/|/ˈhɑːrmənaɪz/| vt. vi. n.|使协调；使和谐；和睦相处；调和|To bring into harmony or agreement; to be in agreement or harmony.|The colors in the room harmonize beautifully.  房间里的颜色搭配得非常漂亮。|The two countries are trying to harmonize their economic policies.  两国正在努力协调它们的经济政策。|Synonyms: accord, agree, blend, match|Antonyms: clash, conflict, discord, disharmony|verb
