gerfalcon: /ˈdʒɜːrfɔːlkən/ | n. |猎隼| A large falcon, Falco rusticolus, of northern regions, having a variable plumage ranging from white to dark brown.  It is a powerful bird of prey known for its speed and hunting prowess.  |例句：The gerfalcon swooped down and snatched the ptarmigan from the snowy ground.  猎隼俯冲下来，从雪地里抓走了岩雷鸟。


The gerfalcon, a majestic bird of prey, is renowned for its exceptional hunting skills.  雄伟的猎隼以其非凡的狩猎技巧而闻名。|近义词：gyrfalcon, (less precise) falcon |反义词：None directly applicable.  (Perhaps "prey" as an antonym to its role as a predator, but that's not a direct opposite.)
