hermitages:/ˈhɜːrmɪtɪdʒɪz/ | n. |隐居处；隐修院；隐士住所 |Places of seclusion or retreat, often associated with religious hermits or solitary individuals.  These can range from simple dwellings to elaborate monasteries. |例句：Many ancient hermitages are nestled in remote mountain areas. 许多古老的隐居处都坐落在偏远的地区。

例句：The hermit spent years in quiet contemplation at his hermitage.  这位隐士在他的隐居处度过了多年平静的沉思时光。


近义词：retreat, seclusion, monastery, abbey


反义词：city, metropolis, bustling place
