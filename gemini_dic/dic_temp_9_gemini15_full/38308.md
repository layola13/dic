concealed: /kənˈsiːld/ | adj. |隐藏的；秘密的；隐蔽的 | Hidden from view; kept secret. |The treasure was concealed in a secret chamber. 宝藏藏在一个秘密的房间里。 | hidden, secret, disguised, obscured | revealed, exposed, open, evident |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist conceals his true feelings in his paintings.  艺术家在他的绘画中隐藏了他的真实情感。
They conceal their weapons before entering the building. 他们进入大楼前藏起了武器。


一般过去时:
She concealed the truth from her parents. 她向父母隐瞒了真相。
He concealed the evidence from the police. 他向警方隐瞒了证据。


现在完成时:
They have concealed their intentions for months. 他们几个月来一直隐瞒着他们的意图。
She has concealed her identity for years. 她多年来一直隐瞒着自己的身份。


过去完成时:
He had concealed the letter before his wife came home. 他在他妻子回家之前藏起了信。
They had concealed their relationship from their families. 他们向各自的家人隐瞒了他们的关系。


将来时:
They will conceal the documents in a safe. 他们将把文件藏在保险箱里。
She will conceal her disappointment from everyone. 她将向所有人隐瞒她的失望。


虚拟语气:
If I had concealed the evidence, I would have avoided arrest. 如果我隐瞒了证据，我就能避免被捕。
I wish I had concealed my feelings better. 我希望我当时能更好地掩饰我的感情。


被动语态:
The truth was concealed from the public. 真相被公众隐瞒了。
The documents were concealed in a vault. 文件被藏在一个地窖里。


疑问句:
Was the evidence concealed effectively? 证据是否被有效地隐藏了？
Are their intentions concealed from us? 他们的意图是否对我们隐瞒了？


否定句:
He did not conceal his anger. 他没有掩饰他的愤怒。
She did not conceal her surprise. 她没有掩饰她的惊讶。
