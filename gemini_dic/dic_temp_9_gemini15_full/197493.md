sheik:/ʃeɪk/| n.|酋长，谢赫，富有的阿拉伯人|A sheik is a male leader of an Arab tribe or family, often wealthy and influential.  The term is also used to refer to a man of high social standing, especially in the Middle East.|例句：The sheik welcomed the guests to his opulent palace.  这位酋长欢迎客人们来到他富丽堂皇的宫殿。

例句：He was known as a generous sheik who supported many charitable causes. 他以慷慨的酋长身份闻名，支持许多慈善事业。


近义词：chief, emir, leader|反义词：follower, subject|词性:名词
