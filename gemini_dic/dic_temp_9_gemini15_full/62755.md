engorges:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒ/| vt.|使膨胀；使充血；使充满|To swell or become swollen with blood or other fluid; to fill to excess.|例句：The river engorged after the heavy rainfall. 大雨过后，河水暴涨。|例句：His face engorged with rage. 他气得满脸通红。|近义词：swell, distend, inflate|反义词：deflate, shrink, contract|动词
