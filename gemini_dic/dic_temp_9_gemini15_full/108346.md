lavender:/ˈlævəndər/| n. adj.|薰衣草，淡紫色|a plant with purple flowers that smell sweet, often used in perfumes and aromatherapy.  It can also describe a pale purple color.|The lavender fields in Provence are breathtaking. 普罗旺斯的薰衣草田令人叹为观止。|The walls were painted a delicate lavender. 墙壁漆成淡紫色。|purple, lilac|  (no direct antonym, but could be described as the opposite of a strong color like crimson)|noun, adjective
