compute:/kəmˈpjuːt/ | vt. |计算，估算，推断 |To determine (a result, answer, or solution) by mathematical methods; to calculate.  |Example:  The computer computed the answer in milliseconds.  电脑在几毫秒内计算出了答案。
The scientists computed the trajectory of the rocket. 科学家们计算了火箭的轨迹。|  calculate, reckon, figure | estimate, guess | verb
