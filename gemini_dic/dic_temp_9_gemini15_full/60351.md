egotistically:/ˌɛɡoʊˈtɪstɪkli/| adv. |自负地；自我中心地 | In a self-centered or conceited manner; showing excessive self-importance.  |例句：He egotistically claimed all the credit for the project's success. 他自负地将项目成功的全部功劳都归于自己。|例句：She spoke egotistically about her achievements, ignoring the contributions of others. 她自负地谈论着自己的成就，忽略了其他人的贡献。|近义词：conceitedly, narcissistically, self-importantly |反义词：humbly, modestly, selflessly


USAGE->
