evincing: /ɪˈvɪnsɪŋ/ | v. | 显示；表明；证明 |  To show or demonstrate clearly; to be evidence of.  | The evidence evinced a clear pattern of negligence.  证据清楚地表明存在明显的疏忽。 | demonstrate, show, indicate, reveal | conceal, hide, obscure | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He evinces a strong interest in art. = 他对艺术表现出浓厚的兴趣。
Her behavior evinces a lack of respect. = 她的行为表明缺乏尊重。

一般过去时:
The study evinced a correlation between diet and disease. = 这项研究表明饮食与疾病之间存在关联。
His smile evinced his happiness. = 他的笑容表明了他的快乐。

现在完成时:
The results have evinced the effectiveness of the new treatment. = 结果已经证明了新疗法的有效性。
Her actions have evinced a commitment to social justice. = 她的行为已经表明了她对社会正义的承诺。

过去完成时:
The preliminary findings had evinced a need for further research. = 初步调查结果表明需要进一步研究。
His previous work had evinced his talent as a writer. = 他以前的作品已经证明了他作为一名作家的才能。

将来时:
Further investigation will evince the truth. = 进一步的调查将证明真相。
The upcoming report will evince the company's financial difficulties. = 即将来临的报告将显示出该公司的财务困难。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence evinced guilt, he would be convicted. = 如果证据表明有罪，他将被定罪。
I wish the results evinced a clearer trend. = 我希望结果能显示出更清晰的趋势。


被动语态:
The truth was evinced by the witness testimony. = 真相由证人证词证明。
His innocence was evinced by the lack of evidence. = 缺乏证据证明了他的清白。


疑问句:
Does this behavior evince a problem? = 这种行为是否表明存在问题？
What does the data evince about the market? = 数据表明了关于市场哪些信息？


否定句:
The study did not evince any significant differences. = 这项研究没有显示出任何显著差异。
His words did not evince any remorse. = 他的话语没有表现出任何悔恨。


PHRASE->
evince a desire = 表达愿望
evince interest in = 对...表示兴趣
evince support for = 表示支持
evince concern about = 对...表示担忧
evince a willingness to = 表达愿意
