ocular:/ˈɑːkjələr/| adj.|视觉的；眼的；目视的|relating to the eye or vision.  The ocular muscles control eye movement.  |近义词：visual, optic|反义词：auditory


例句：

He suffered an ocular injury in the accident. 他在事故中眼睛受伤了。

The doctor performed an ocular examination. 医生进行了眼科检查。


词源:

From Latin *ocularis, from oculus "eye".
