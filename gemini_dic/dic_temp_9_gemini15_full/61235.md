emailed:/iˈmeɪld/| v.|发送电子邮件|To send a message electronically using email.|I emailed him the report yesterday. 我昨天给他发了报告的邮件。|sent, messaged, mailed|received, collected|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I email my friends every day. = 我每天都给我的朋友们发邮件。
She emails her boss regularly. = 她定期给老板发邮件。


一般过去时:
He emailed me last night. = 他昨晚给我发了邮件。
They emailed the client the contract. = 他们给客户发了合同的邮件。


现在完成时:
I have emailed him three times. = 我已经给他发了三次邮件了。
She has emailed the application. = 她已经发了申请邮件。


过去完成时:
I had emailed the professor before the class. = 我在上课前已经给教授发了邮件。
They had emailed the documents before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经发了文件的邮件。


将来时:
I will email you the details tomorrow. = 我明天会把细节发邮件给你。
He will email her when he arrives. = 他到达后会给她发邮件。


虚拟语气:
If I had emailed him sooner, he would have known. = 如果我早点给他发邮件，他就知道了。
I wish I had emailed her earlier. = 我真希望我早点给她发邮件。


被动语态:
The report was emailed to everyone. = 报告已经发邮件给每个人了。
The invitation was emailed to all the guests. = 请柬已经发邮件给所有客人了。


疑问句:
Did you email him? = 你给他发邮件了吗？
Have you emailed the documents yet? = 你已经发了文件邮件了吗？


否定句:
I didn't email him yesterday. = 我昨天没有给他发邮件。
She hasn't emailed me back yet. = 她还没回复我的邮件。



PHRASE->
email address = 电子邮件地址
email attachment = 邮件附件
email server = 邮件服务器
email marketing = 电邮营销
email client = 电子邮件客户端
