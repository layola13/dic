embeddable:/ɪmˈbɛdəbl/ | adj. | 可嵌入的，可嵌套的 | Capable of being embedded; able to be inserted into something else.  | Example: This widget is embeddable on any website. 这个小部件可以嵌入任何网站。  |近义词:insertable, integrable | 反义词:uninsertable, fixed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This video is embeddable on social media platforms. = 这个视频可以嵌入社交媒体平台。
The map is embeddable in our presentation. = 这张地图可以嵌入我们的演示文稿中。


一般过去时:
That plugin was embeddable in older versions of the software. = 那个插件在旧版本的软件中是可以嵌入的。
The code was easily embeddable into the website. = 代码很容易嵌入到网站中。


现在完成时:
We have made the form embeddable on our website. = 我们已经将表单嵌入到我们的网站上了。
