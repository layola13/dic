dreader:/ˈdriːdər/| n.|惧怕者，害怕的人|A person who experiences dread or fear, often excessively or habitually.  Someone who is prone to worrying and anticipating negative outcomes.|例句：He was a dreader of public speaking, his heart pounding at the mere thought of it. 他是一个害怕公开演讲的人，一想到它就心跳加速。He is a dreader of failure, always second-guessing his decisions. 他是一个害怕失败的人，总是反复琢磨自己的决定。|近义词：worrier, fearmonger, coward (in some contexts)|反义词：daredevil, adventurer, risk-taker|名词
