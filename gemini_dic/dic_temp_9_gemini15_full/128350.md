neutralizers:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪzərz/ | n. | 中和剂，抵消因素 | Substances or processes that counteract or neutralize the effects of something else.  Neutralizers are often used in chemistry to balance acidity or alkalinity, but can also refer to things that mitigate negative effects in other contexts. | Example: The farmer used a soil neutralizer to balance the pH level.  这个农民使用土壤中和剂来平衡pH值。|近义词：counteragents, antidotes, inhibitors |反义词：accelerators, catalysts |名词
