pillowing: /ˈpɪloʊɪŋ/ | adj. | 枕头的；柔软如枕的 |  Resembling a pillow in softness or support; soft and yielding like a pillow.  | 例句：The clouds drifted lazily, pillowing the landscape in a soft, white blanket.  云朵懒洋洋地飘动，像柔软的白色毯子一样覆盖着大地。

The soft, pillowing earth cushioned his fall.  柔软的土地像枕头一样缓冲了他的摔倒。| 近义词：cushiony, soft, yielding | 反义词：hard, rigid, firm |  词性:形容词
