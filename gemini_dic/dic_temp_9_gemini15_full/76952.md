garages:/ˈɡærɪdʒɪz/| n.|车库；修车厂|A garage is a building for storing or repairing motor vehicles.  It can be attached to a house or a separate structure.|例句：My car is in the garage.我的车在车库里。|例句：He works at a garage.他在一家修车厂工作。|近义词：carport, shed|反义词：N/A|名词
