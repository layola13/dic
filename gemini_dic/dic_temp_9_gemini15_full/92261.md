hyperventilate:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɛntɪleɪt/| v.|过度换气，呼吸急促|To breathe very quickly and deeply, often as a result of anxiety or panic.  This can lead to a decrease in blood carbon dioxide levels.|例句：She started to hyperventilate when she saw the accident. 她看到事故后开始过度换气。|例句：He hyperventilated during the stressful exam.  他在压力巨大的考试中呼吸急促。|近义词：overbreathe, pant, gasp|反义词：hypoventilate
