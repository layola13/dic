skimping:/ˈskɪmpɪŋ/| v.|吝啬；节省；敷衍；偷工减料 |To be unwilling to spend money or use resources; to do something badly or carelessly to save money or time.|例句：They're skimping on quality to cut costs. 他们为了降低成本而降低了质量。|例句：He was skimping on the details of the report. 他在报告的细节上敷衍了事。|近义词：stinting, scrimping, cheeseparing, economizing|反义词：splurging, lavishing, indulging


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are skimping on the ingredients. = 他们在偷工减料。
We often skimp on sleep. = 我们经常睡眠不足。


一般过去时:
She skimped on the decorations for the party. = 她对派对的装饰很吝啬。
He skimped on his homework. = 他敷衍了事地完成了作业。


现在完成时:
They have skimped on maintenance for years. = 他们多年来一直吝啬维护费用。
We have skimped and saved to buy a house. = 我们省吃俭用地攒钱买房。


过去完成时:
They had skimped on safety precautions, resulting in an accident. = 他们之前对安全预防措施吝啬，导致了事故。
She had skimped on practice, which affected her performance. = 她之前练习不足，影响了她的表现。


将来时:
We will skimp on our vacation this year. = 今年我们的假期会很节省。
They will probably skimp on the food if they are short of money. = 如果他们缺钱，他们可能会在食物上节省开支。


虚拟语气:
If they skimped on materials, the product would be substandard. = 如果他们偷工减料，产品质量就会不合格。
I wish I hadn't skimped on my studies. = 我真希望我以前学习不用那么节省。


被动语态:
The project was skimped on, leading to poor results. = 项目偷工减料，导致结果不佳。
Quality is often skimped on to meet tight deadlines. = 为了赶上最后期限，质量经常被忽视。


疑问句:
Are they skimping on the project? = 他们在项目上偷工减料吗？
Were they skimping on the food? = 他们当时在食物上吝啬吗？


否定句:
They didn't skimp on the materials. = 他们没有在材料上吝啬。
We won't skimp on the details this time. = 这次我们不会忽略细节。


PHRASE->
skimp on something: 在...方面吝啬
skimp and save: 省吃俭用
skimp out on something: 逃避...责任；敷衍...
skimp on quality: 降低质量
skimp on details: 忽略细节
