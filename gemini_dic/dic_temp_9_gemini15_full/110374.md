lignify:/ˈlɪɡnɪfaɪ/ | v. |木质化；使木质化 | To become woody or to convert into wood; to make woody.  The process involves the deposition of lignin in plant cell walls, making them rigid and strong. |例句：The stems of herbaceous plants lignify as they mature.  草本植物的茎在成熟时会木质化。

The process of lignification strengthens and supports the plant. 木质化过程增强并支撑植物。


近义词：woody, harden, petrify |反义词：soften, decay
