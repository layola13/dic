over-reduction: /ˌoʊvər rɪˈdʌkʃən/ | n. |过度简化；简化过度 | The simplification of something to an excessive degree, often resulting in the loss of important details or nuances.  It can refer to a process, a result, or a representation. | 例句：The over-reduction of complex historical events in the textbook led to a misunderstanding of the causes of the war. 教材对复杂历史事件的过度简化导致对战争起因的误解。

The over-reduction of data resulted in a flawed analysis. 数据的过度简化导致分析存在缺陷。| 近义词：simplification, oversimplification, abbreviation, condensation | 反义词：elaboration, expansion, explication, detailed analysis |
