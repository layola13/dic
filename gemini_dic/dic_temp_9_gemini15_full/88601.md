hitting-up:/ˈhɪtɪŋ ʌp/| v.|拜访；搭讪；索要；袭击；|to visit someone unexpectedly or informally; to ask someone for something, often money; to attack or assault someone or something.|例句：I'm hitting up my friend's house later. 我晚点要去朋友家拜访。 I had to hit him up for a loan. 我不得不向他借钱。|近义词：visit, accost, solicit, attack, assault|反义词：avoid, ignore, donate, defend


USAGE->

I hit up my friends every weekend. = 我每个周末都去看我的朋友们。
He hits up strangers for money on the street. = 他在街上向陌生人要钱。
