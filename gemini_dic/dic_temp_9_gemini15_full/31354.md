characterizer:/ˈkærəktəraɪzər/ | n. |表征者；特性描述者 | A person or thing that characterizes something else; something that serves to characterize.  It can also refer to a device or method used for characterization. | Example: The film's fast-paced editing was a key characterizer of its action genre. 这部电影快节奏的剪辑是其动作类型的一个关键特征。 |近义词: describer, identifier, indicator, delineator |反义词:  obscurer, misrepresenter


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The artist's brushstrokes characterize her style. = 艺术家的笔触体现了她的风格。
His quick wit characterizes his personality. = 他敏捷的才智体现了他的个性。


一般过去时:
The historian characterized the era as one of great upheaval. = 历史学家将那个时代描述为一个动荡的时代。
Her laughter characterized her joyful nature. = 她的笑声体现了她快乐的天性。


现在完成时:
The discovery has characterized the new scientific field. = 这一发现已经成为这个新科学领域的特点。
His actions have characterized his commitment to the cause. = 他的行为已经体现了他对事业的奉献。


过去完成时:
The previous research had characterized the compound as unstable. = 先前的研究已将该化合物描述为不稳定的。
The historical context had characterized the event as a rebellion. = 历史背景已将这一事件描述为一场叛乱。


将来时:
Future research will characterize the effects of climate change. = 未来的研究将描述气候变化的影响。
The new technology will characterize the next generation of computers. = 新技术将决定下一代计算机的特性。


虚拟语气:
If the data characterized the problem accurately, we would have a solution. = 如果数据准确地描述了问题，我们就会有解决方案。
I wish the analysis characterized the situation more clearly. = 我希望分析能更清晰地描述情况。


被动语态:
The painting is characterized by its bold colors. = 这幅画的特点是色彩大胆。
The era was characterized by political instability. = 那个时代的特点是政治不稳定。


疑问句:
What characterizes this particular species of bird? = 什么是这种特定鸟类的特征？
How would you characterize the current economic climate? = 你将如何描述当前的经济环境？


否定句:
His behavior didn't characterize his usual politeness. = 他的行为并不符合他通常的礼貌。
The report failed to characterize the severity of the situation. = 报告未能描述情况的严重性。


PHRASE->

be characterized by = 以...为特征
characterized as = 被描述为
characterized by its = 以其...为特征
well-characterized = 特征鲜明的
poorly characterized = 特征不明确
