preventability:/ˌprɪˌvɛntəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可预防性，可避免性 | The ability to be prevented or avoided.  The extent to which something can be prevented. | Example:  The high preventability of many diseases emphasizes the importance of public health initiatives.  许多疾病的高度可预防性突显了公共卫生倡议的重要性。

Example: The study highlighted the preventability of workplace accidents through improved safety training.  这项研究强调了通过改进安全培训来预防工作场所事故的可行性。


近义词：avoidability,  preventibility

反义词：inevitability, unavoidability
