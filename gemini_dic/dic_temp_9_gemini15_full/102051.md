isomeric:/ˌaɪsəˈmɛrɪk/| adj.| 同分异构的 | Relating to or denoting isomers. Isomeric compounds have the same number and kinds of atoms but differ in the arrangement of atoms.  | Example:  Butane and isobutane are isomeric hydrocarbons. 丁烷和异丁烷是同分异构的烃。  |近义词：isomerous|反义词：identical


USAGE->
There is no verb conjugation for adjectives.  Adjectives like "isomeric" describe nouns.  Therefore, grammatical tenses, moods, and voices do not apply.
