satirised:/ˈsætəraɪzd/| v.|讽刺；挖苦|To ridicule or make fun of someone or something, often using irony, sarcasm, or humor to expose or criticize folly, vice, or stupidity.|例句：The cartoon satirised the politician's recent gaffe.  这幅漫画讽刺了这位政治家最近的失误。

例句：The writer satirised the hypocrisy of the wealthy elite.  这位作家讽刺了富裕精英的虚伪。


近义词：ridiculed, mocked, lampooned, caricatured


反义词：praised, complimented, lauded
