prelecture:/ˈpriːˌlɛktʃər/| n. |预讲座，讲座前的介绍| A prelecture is a short presentation or introduction given before a main lecture to provide background information or context.  It might summarize key concepts, introduce the lecturer, or set the stage for the upcoming lecture. |Example: The professor gave a brief prelecture outlining the main themes of the upcoming lecture on quantum physics.  教授在即将到来的量子物理学讲座之前做了一个简短的预讲座，概述了主要主题。|近义词：introduction, prelude, preview |反义词：postlecture (this word is not commonly used)|名词
