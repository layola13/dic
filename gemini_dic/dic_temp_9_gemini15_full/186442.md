riblike:/ˈrɪblaɪk/| adj.|肋骨似的，肋状的|Resembling a rib or ribs in form or structure.  The term often describes something with a series of parallel ridges or grooves.|例句：The fossilized remains showed a riblike structure.  化石遗骸显示出肋骨状的结构。
The ribbed texture of the fabric felt riblike against her skin.  织物的肋纹质地感觉像肋骨一样贴在她的皮肤上。|近义词：ridged, grooved, furrowed|反义词：smooth, flat
