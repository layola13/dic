greased:/ɡriːst/ | adj. v. | 涂油的；贿赂的；顺利进行的 | Covered with grease; bribed; proceeding smoothly. |例句：The gears were greased to reduce friction.  齿轮上涂了油以减少摩擦。The politician was accused of greasing the wheels to get the bill passed.  这位政客被指控为了让法案通过而行贿。The negotiations greased along smoothly.  谈判顺利进行。 |近义词：lubricated, oiled, bribed, greased the wheels |反义词：dry, rusty, obstructed


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He greases the car engine regularly. = 他定期给汽车发动机加油。
She greases the baking pan to prevent sticking. = 她给烤盘涂油以防止粘连。


一般过去时:
They greased the rusty hinges. = 他们给生锈的铰链上油了。
The mechanic greased the car's chassis. = 机械师给汽车底盘加了润滑油。


现在完成时:
I have greased the wheels to get the project approved. = 我已经活动了关系以获得项目的批准。
She has greased her hair for the event. = 她为了参加活动而给头发抹了油。


过去完成时:

He had greased the palms of several officials. = 他已经贿赂了几位官员。


将来时:
