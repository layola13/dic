cacuminal:/ˈkækjʊmɪnəl/| adj.|卷舌的，舌根音的|Relating to or denoting sounds produced with the tongue curled back against the hard palate.  These sounds are articulated with the tongue curled back towards the hard palate, often producing a retroflex sound.|The pronunciation of the 'r' in some Indian languages is a cacuminal sound.|近义词：retroflex|反义词：apical
