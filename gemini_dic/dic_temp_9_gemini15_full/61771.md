empathizing: /ˈɛmpəθaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 移情，感同身受 |  The act of understanding and sharing the feelings of another.  It involves putting yourself in someone else's shoes and experiencing the world from their perspective. | 例句：Empathizing with her grief, I offered my condolences.  = 同情她的悲伤，我表示了慰问。

She was empathizing with the character's struggles in the book. = 她正在设身处地地体会书中人物的挣扎。


近义词：sympathizing, understanding, relating to, feeling for, identifying with | 反义词：being indifferent to, being uncaring, ignoring, disregarding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He empathizes with his friend's problems. = 他同情他朋友的困难。
She empathizes with the suffering of the refugees. = 她同情难民的苦难。

一般过去时:
I empathized with her when she lost her job. = 当她失业时，我感同身受。
He empathized deeply with the victims of the disaster. = 他对灾难的受害者深感同情。

现在完成时:
I have empathized with many people throughout my life. = 我一生中同情过很多人。
She has empathized with the struggles of single mothers. = 她体会过单亲母亲的艰辛。

过去完成时:
I had empathized with his situation before he even spoke. = 他还没开口我就已经体会到他的处境了。
She had empathized with his feelings long before the argument started. = 在争吵开始很久以前，她就体会到了他的感受。

将来时:
I will empathize with you when you face similar challenges. = 当你面临类似挑战时，我会感同身受。
She will empathize more with others as she matures. = 随着她逐渐成熟，她会更加善解人意。

虚拟语气:
If I were in his shoes, I would empathize with his anger. = 如果我处在他的位置，我会理解他的愤怒。
I wish I could empathize more effectively with people's pain. = 我希望我能更有效地体会人们的痛苦。

被动语态:
His suffering was empathized with by many. = 许多人都同情他的痛苦。
Her plight was empathized with by the entire community. = 她的困境得到了整个社区的同情。

疑问句:
Do you empathize with the character's actions in the story? = 你同情故事中人物的行为吗？
Does he empathize with the difficulties faced by the homeless? = 他同情无家可归者面临的困难吗？

否定句:
He did not empathize with her situation at all. = 他一点也不同情她的处境。
She couldn't empathize with his joy, as she was too upset. = 她无法体会他的快乐，因为她太沮丧了。
