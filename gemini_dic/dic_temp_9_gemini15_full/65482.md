Europe:/ˈjʊərəp/|/ˈjʊərəʊp/| n. |欧洲|The continent of Europe, encompassing a vast area and numerous countries with diverse cultures and histories.  Europe is known for its rich history, art, and culture.|Example:  He spent his summer traveling through Europe. 他夏天在欧洲旅行。|近义词：the European continent|反义词：无


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Europe is a continent of diverse cultures.  = 欧洲是一个文化多样化的洲。
Many people travel to Europe every year. = 每年有很多人去欧洲旅行。


一般过去时:
The Romans ruled much of Europe. = 罗马人统治着欧洲大部分地区。
My family visited Europe last year. = 我的家人去年访问了欧洲。


现在完成时:
I have never been to Europe. = 我从未去过欧洲。
She has lived in Europe for five years. = 她已经在欧洲住了五年了。


过去完成时:
Before the war, he had lived in Europe for many years. = 在战争爆发前，他在欧洲住了很多年。
They had already visited many countries in Europe before they reached France. = 在到达法国之前，他们已经游览了欧洲的许多国家。


将来时:
We will travel to Europe next year. = 我们明年将去欧洲旅行。
The conference will be held in Europe. = 会议将在欧洲举行。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would travel to Europe. = 如果我有更多钱，我会去欧洲旅行。
I wish I were in Europe right now. = 我希望我现在在欧洲。


被动语态:
Europe is often visited by tourists. = 欧洲经常被游客参观。
The conference will be held in Europe. = 会议将在欧洲举行。(This example works for both future and passive.)


疑问句:
