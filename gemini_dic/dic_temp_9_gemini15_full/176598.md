reaccentuate: /ˌriːˈæksənˌtjuːeɪt/ | vt. | 重新强调；再次强调 | To emphasize or stress something again.  |例句：The professor reaccentuated the importance of regular study. 教授再次强调了定期学习的重要性。

例句：The politician reaccentuated his commitment to the environment during the debate.  这位政治家在辩论中再次强调了他对环境的承诺。


近义词：reiterate, emphasize, stress, underscore | 反义词：downplay, de-emphasize
