megampere:/ˈmɛɡæmˌpɪər/| n. |兆安培|A megampere (MA) is a unit of electric current equal to one million amperes. It's used to measure extremely large currents, often encountered in high-power applications like lightning strikes or large-scale power transmission. |例句：The lightning strike generated a megampere surge. 这道闪电产生了兆安培的电流冲击。

例句：The power grid experienced a brief megampere overload. 电网经历了短暂的兆安培过载。


近义词：million amperes


反义词：microampere (极小电流单位)


词性:名词
