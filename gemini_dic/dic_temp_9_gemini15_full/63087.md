enseal:/ɪnˈsiːl/| vt.|封；密封；盖章|To close and secure (something) with a seal; to make (something) legally binding by affixing a seal.|例句：He carefully ensealed the envelope before mailing it. 他在邮寄信封前仔细地封好了它。  He ensealed the document with his official stamp. 他用他的官印盖章在文件上。|近义词：seal, close, fasten|反义词：open, unseal
