pulping:/ˈpʌlpɪŋ/ | n. | 纸浆制造；制浆 | The process of reducing wood or other fibrous material to pulp, usually for making paper.  | Example: The mill specializes in pulping techniques for recycled paper.  这家工厂专门从事再生纸的制浆技术。 | 近义词：maceration, reduction to pulp | 反义词：papermaking (in the sense of the opposite stage of the process)
