queues:/kjuːz/ | n. | 队列；行列；(计算机)队列 | A queue is a line of people or things waiting for something.  It can also refer to a data structure in computer science. | 例句：There's a long queue at the cinema.  电影院排着长队。

There is a queue of jobs waiting to be processed.  有一堆工作在等待处理。

|近义词：line, row, file |反义词：disorder, chaos |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The queue moves slowly. = 队伍移动缓慢。
People queue for hours to buy tickets. = 人们排队数小时购买门票。

一般过去时:
The queue snaked around the block. = 队伍蜿蜒绕过街区。
We queued for over an hour. = 我们排队超过一个小时。

现在完成时:
They have queued for the concert all morning. = 他们整个上午都在为音乐会排队。
I have never queued so long before. = 我以前从未排过这么长的队。

过去完成时:
They had queued for hours before the shop opened. = 商店开门前他们已经排了几个小时的队。
We had already queued when it started to rain. = 开始下雨时我们已经在排队了。

将来时:
We will queue up for the new phone. = 我们将排队购买新手机。
They will queue for hours to get the best seats. = 他们将排队数小时以获得最佳座位。

虚拟语气:
If I had known there would be such a long queue, I wouldn't have come. = 如果我知道会有这么长的队伍，我就不会来了。
I wish I hadn't queued for so long. = 我真希望我没有排队这么久。

被动语态:
The customers were queued efficiently. = 顾客们被有效地安排了队伍。
The tickets are being distributed by a queued system. = 门票正在通过排队系统分发。

疑问句:
How long is the queue? = 队伍有多长？
Is there a queue for the bus? = 等公共汽车要排队吗？

否定句:
There wasn't a queue when I arrived. = 我到达时没有队伍。
We didn't have to queue at all. = 我们根本不需要排队。
