genuinely:/ˈdʒɛnjuɪnli/| adv.| 真正地；真诚地 | truly and sincerely; in a genuine manner.  |例句：He genuinely cares about his students. 他真心关心他的学生们。
例句：She genuinely believed in his innocence. 她真心相信他是无辜的。|近义词：authentically, sincerely, truly, honestly |反义词：falsely, insincerely, superficially

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He genuinely enjoys his work. = 他真心喜欢他的工作。
She genuinely believes in what she's doing. = 她真心相信自己在做的事情。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
