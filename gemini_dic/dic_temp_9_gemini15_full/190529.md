satieties:/səˈtaɪətiz/| n. | 满足感；饱足感；厌倦 | A feeling of being satisfied or full, often to the point of boredom or weariness.  It can refer to a feeling of having enough of something, whether it's food, experiences, or even emotions. |例句：After a huge Thanksgiving dinner, a feeling of satiety settled over everyone. 感恩节大餐后，每个人都感到饱了。


例句：The endless parade of similar movies led to a sense of satiety in the audience.  电影的无尽重复让观众感到厌倦。


近义词：satisfaction, fullness, repletion, surfeit


反义词：hunger, emptiness, dissatisfaction, craving


词性:名词
