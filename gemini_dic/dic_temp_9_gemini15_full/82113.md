grogginess:/ˈɡrɒɡɪnəs/| /ˈɡrɑːɡɪnəs/ | n. |困倦；昏沉；头昏脑胀 | A state of being drowsy and sluggish, often after waking up from sleep or due to illness or medication.  It is characterized by a feeling of mental and physical sluggishness. |例句：The grogginess from the late night kept me from focusing on my work.  一夜没睡导致的困倦让我无法集中精力工作。

例句：After the surgery, he experienced grogginess and nausea. 手术后，他感到昏昏沉沉，还恶心。


近义词：drowsiness, sleepiness, lethargy, sluggishness |反义词：alertness, wakefulness, vivacity
