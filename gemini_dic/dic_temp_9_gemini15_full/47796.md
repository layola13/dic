decaploid:/ˈdɛkəplɔɪd/| adj.|十倍体|Having ten sets of chromosomes in each cell.  A decaploid organism or cell possesses ten times the haploid number of chromosomes.|Example:  Some plants, like certain strawberries, can be decaploid.  某些植物，例如某些草莓，可能是十倍体的。|近义词：polyploid (a more general term)|反义词：haploid, diploid, triploid, tetraploid etc.
