penitentness:/ˈpɛnɪtənts/| n. |悔恨，悔过；痛悔|The feeling of regret and sorrow for having done wrong; repentance.  It often involves a desire to make amends for one's actions. |例句：Her penitentness was evident in her tearful apology.  她的悔恨在她泪流满面的道歉中显而易见。

例句：His penitentness led him to confess his crime to the authorities.  他的悔恨使他向当局自首。


近义词：repentance, remorse, contrition, regret

反义词：unrepentance, remorselessness, callousness


词性:名词
