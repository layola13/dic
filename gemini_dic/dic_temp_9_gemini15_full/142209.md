ornithologist:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɒlədʒɪst/| n. |鸟类学家|A scientist who studies birds.  Ornithologists study the behavior, physiology, and ecology of birds.|例句：The ornithologist spent months observing the nesting habits of the rare bird.  这位鸟类学家花了几个月的时间观察这种稀有鸟类的筑巢习性。|近义词：bird expert, avian biologist|反义词：N/A

The ornithologist studies bird migration patterns. = 鸟类学家研究鸟类的迁徙模式。
He observes birds in their natural habitat. = 他在鸟类的自然栖息地观察它们。
