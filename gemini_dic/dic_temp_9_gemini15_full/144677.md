outyells:/ˌaʊtˈjɛlz/| vt.|大声喊过；比…喊得大声|To shout louder than someone else.  This is a verb formed by adding the prefix "out-" to the verb "yell". The prefix "out-" indicates exceeding or surpassing.|例句：He outyelled the other protesters, making his voice heard above the crowd. 他大声喊叫，盖过了其他抗议者的声音。|例句：The singer outyelled the band during the final chorus, showcasing her powerful voice.  在最后的合唱中，歌手的声音盖过了乐队，展现了她强大的嗓音。|近义词：overshout, drown out|反义词：whisper, murmur
