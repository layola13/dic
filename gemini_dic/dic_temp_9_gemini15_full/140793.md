on-a-string: /ˌɒn ə strɪŋ/ | adj. | 受控制的；完全受他人摆布的 | Easily manipulated or controlled; completely under someone else's influence.  |例句：He had the whole town on a string. 他完全控制了整个城镇。  |近义词：controlled, manipulated, dominated |反义词：independent, autonomous, free


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The puppeteer has his puppets on a string. = 木偶戏演员操控着他的木偶。
She keeps her employees on a string. = 她把她的员工牢牢控制住。


一般过去时:
The dictator kept his people on a string. = 独裁者控制着他的百姓。
He had her on a string for years. = 他多年来一直控制着她。


现在完成时:
They have had the media on a string for decades. = 几十年来他们一直操控着媒体。
She has kept her rivals on a string throughout the campaign. = 在整个竞选过程中，她一直控制着她的对手。


过去完成时:
The con artist had had his victim on a string for months. = 骗子已经控制了他的受害者好几个月了。
Before the scandal, he had had the board on a string. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他一直控制着董事会。


将来时:
The new CEO will have the company on a string in no time. = 新任CEO很快就会掌控公司。
I suspect he will have her on a string before long.  = 我怀疑他很快就会控制住她。


虚拟语气:
If he were a good leader, he wouldn't have his people on a string. = 如果他是一位好领导，他不会控制他的百姓。
I wish the government wouldn't have the public on a string. = 我希望政府不要控制公众。


被动语态:
The citizens were kept on a string by the authoritarian regime. = 公民被专制政权控制着。
The witnesses were easily kept on a string by the defense lawyer. = 证人们很容易被辩护律师控制。


疑问句:
Is the government keeping the public on a string? = 政府是否在控制公众？
Are you sure he doesn't have her on a string? = 你确定他没有控制着她吗？


否定句:
The protesters were not easily kept on a string. = 抗议者不容易被控制。
The company is not on a string; it's independent. = 这家公司不受控制，它是独立的。


PHRASE->
have someone on a string = 控制某人
keep someone on a string = 操控某人
pull the strings = 幕后操纵
dance to someone's tune = 听从某人的摆布
at someone's beck and call = 随时听候某人吩咐
