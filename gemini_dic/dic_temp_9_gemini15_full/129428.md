nominees:/ˌnɑːmɪˈniz/ | n. |被提名者，候选人 |People or things that have been nominated for an award or position.  |The nominees for Best Actor were all incredibly talented.|近义词：candidates, contenders |反义词：winners, losers


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee considers the nominees carefully. = 委员会仔细考虑候选人。
Who are the nominees for this year's award? = 今年奖项的候选人是谁？


一般过去时:
The academy announced the nominees last night. = 学院昨晚公布了提名名单。
They carefully reviewed the nominees before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前仔细审查了候选人。


现在完成时:
The organization has already selected the nominees. = 该组织已经选定了候选人。
Have they announced the nominees for the upcoming election? = 他们是否已经公布了即将举行的选举的候选人？


过去完成时:
The judges had already reviewed the nominees' work before the final decision. = 在最终决定之前，评委们已经审查了候选人的作品。
We had considered many nominees before settling on the final three. = 在确定最终三名候选人之前，我们已经考虑了许多候选人。


将来时:
They will announce the nominees next week. = 他们将在下周公布提名名单。
The committee will interview all the nominees before making a choice. = 委员会将在做出选择之前采访所有候选人。


虚拟语气:
If only these nominees were more qualified. = 但愿这些候选人更有资格。
I wish the nominees had more experience in this field. = 我希望候选人有更多这方面的经验。


被动语态:
The nominees were chosen by a panel of experts. = 候选人是由专家小组选出的。
The nominees are being interviewed this afternoon. = 候选人今天下午正在接受采访。


疑问句:
Are there any other nominees besides these? = 除此之外还有其他候选人吗？
Who are the leading nominees in this category? = 在这一类别中，领先的候选人是谁？


否定句:

The committee did not include those nominees in the final selection. = 委员会没有将这些候选人列入最终名单。
