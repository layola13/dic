furzes:/ˈfɜːzɪz/| n.|金雀花丛,荆棘丛|A furze is a thorny shrub of the genus Ulex, especially Ulex europaeus, which is common in Europe.  It has bright yellow flowers and is often found in heathlands and other open areas.  The word is often used to refer to a thicket or mass of furze bushes.|The hillside was covered in a dense mass of furzes.|近义词：gorse, whin|反义词：无
