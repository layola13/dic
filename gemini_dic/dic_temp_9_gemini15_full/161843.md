practise:/ˈpræktɪs/ |/ˈprækɪs/| v. |练习，实践|To perform or carry out repeatedly in order to improve skill or ability; to put into regular use.|例句：I practise the piano every day. = 我每天练习钢琴。
I practise what I preach. = 我言行一致。|近义词：exercise, rehearse, train |反义词：neglect, ignore |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I practise yoga every morning. = 我每天早上练习瑜伽。
She practises speaking English with her tutor. = 她和她的导师练习说英语。

一般过去时:
He practised the violin for hours yesterday. = 他昨天练习拉小提琴几个小时。
They practised their dance routine before the performance. = 他们在演出前练习了他们的舞蹈套路。

现在完成时:
I have practised playing the guitar for five years. = 我练习弹吉他五年了。
She has practised diligently for the upcoming exam. = 她为即将到来的考试勤奋练习。

过去完成时:
He had practised his speech many times before the presentation. = 他在演讲之前练习了很多次。
They had practised for weeks before the competition. = 比赛前他们已经练习了几周。

将来时:
I will practise my driving skills before the test. = 我将在考试前练习我的驾驶技能。
She will practise her presentation tomorrow. = 她明天将练习她的演示文稿。

虚拟语气:
If I practised more, I would be a better musician. = 如果我多练习，我会成为一个更好的音乐家。
I wish I had practised harder for the exam. = 我希望我为考试更努力地练习。

被动语态:
The technique is practised by many athletes. = 这种技术被许多运动员所练习。
The song was practised repeatedly by the choir. = 这首歌被合唱团反复练习。

疑问句:
Do you practise any sports? = 你练习任何运动吗？
Does she practise the piano regularly? = 她定期练习钢琴吗？

否定句:
I don't practise enough. = 我练习得不够。
He didn't practise for the test. = 他没有为考试练习。
