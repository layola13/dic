recontemplates:/ˌriːkənˈtɛmpleɪt/ | vt. |重新思考，再考虑 | To think again about something; to reconsider.  |例句：He recontemplated his decision after receiving new information. 他在收到新的信息后重新考虑了他的决定。 |近义词：reconsider, rethink, review |反义词：decide, conclude
