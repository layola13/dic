forward:/ˈfɔːrwərd/| /ˈfɔːrwɚd/| adv., adj., n., v.|向前，前进；未来的；前锋；促进|To or toward the front; in or to a more advanced state or position.|例句：He stepped forward to greet her. 他向前一步迎接她。|例句：The company is looking forward to a bright future. 公司期待着光明的未来。|近义词：ahead, onward, advanced|反义词：backward, behind, reverse|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He forwards the email to his boss. = 他把邮件转发给他的老板。
She forwards her mail to another address. = 她将邮件转发到另一个地址。


一般过去时:
They forwarded the package to the new address. = 他们将包裹转发到新地址。
He forwarded the complaint to the manager. = 他将投诉转交给经理。


现在完成时:
I have forwarded all the necessary documents. = 我已经转发了所有必要的文档。
She has forwarded the application to the relevant department. = 她已将申请转交给相关部门。


过去完成时:
He had forwarded the email before leaving the office. = 他在离开办公室之前已经转发了邮件。
They had forwarded the information to the team before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已将信息转发给团队。


将来时:
I will forward the report to you tomorrow. = 我明天会把报告转发给你。
She will forward the request to the appropriate person. = 她会将请求转交给合适的人。


虚拟语气:
If I had known his email address, I would have forwarded the message. = 如果我知道他的邮箱地址，我就会转发邮件了。
If she were here, she would forward the document. = 如果她在，她会转发文件的。


被动语态:
The email was forwarded to the wrong address. = 邮件被转发到错误的地址。
The application is being forwarded to the review committee. = 申请正在被转交给审查委员会。


疑问句:
Did you forward the email? = 你转发邮件了吗？
Has she forwarded the documents yet? = 她已经转发文件了吗？


否定句:
I didn't forward the email. = 我没有转发邮件。
He hasn't forwarded the report yet. = 他还没转发报告。


PHRASE->
look forward to: 期待
move forward: 向前推进
go forward: 前进
forward-looking: 有远见的
forward momentum: 向前动力
