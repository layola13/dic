preconcerns: /ˌpriːkənˈsɜːrnz/ | n. | 预先的担忧；事先的顾虑 |  Concerns that exist before a particular event or action.  Preconcerns often involve anticipating potential problems or challenges. |  例句：The preconcerns about the project's feasibility led to extensive planning.  项目可行性的预先担忧导致了广泛的规划。


例句：Her preconcerns regarding the trip were unfounded; everything went smoothly. 她对这次旅行的预先担忧是毫无根据的；一切都很顺利。


近义词：prior worries, anticipatory anxieties, advance apprehensions | 反义词：后顾之忧 (lack of preconcerns, afterthoughts) | 词性: 名词
