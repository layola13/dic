ectoplasm:/ˈɛktəʊˌplæzəm/| n. |外质；(超自然现象中的)灵体物质 |A supposedly gelatinous substance secreted by mediums during a séance, claimed to be the physical manifestation of a spirit.  It is a concept primarily associated with spiritualism and parapsychology and lacks scientific evidence. |例句：The medium claimed to have produced ectoplasm during the séance.  灵媒声称在通灵会上产生了外质。 The photograph purportedly showing ectoplasm was later revealed to be a hoax. 那张据称显示外质的照片后来被揭露是骗局。|近义词：protoplasm (in a biological context, but conceptually similar)|反义词：N/A (no direct antonym)|名词
