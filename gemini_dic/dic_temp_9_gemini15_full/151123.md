parastatal: /ˈpærəˌsteɪtəl/ | n. | 半官方机构；准国有企业 | A parastatal is a company or organization that is owned or controlled by a government, but is not part of the government itself.  It often operates independently but still receives some government funding or direction.  These entities usually have a social or economic role, often in areas like utilities, transportation, or communications. | Example: Many developing countries use parastatals to provide essential services like electricity and water.  许多发展中国家利用半官方机构提供电力和水等基本服务。| 近义词: semi-governmental organization, quasi-public entity | 反义词: private company, privately owned business | 名词
