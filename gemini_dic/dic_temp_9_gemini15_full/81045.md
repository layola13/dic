graftage:/ˈɡræftɪdʒ/| n.|嫁接；接穗；移植|The process of grafting one plant onto another.  It involves joining parts of different plants together so that they grow as one.  This technique is commonly used in horticulture to combine desirable traits from different varieties.|例句：The successful graftage of the apple tree resulted in a bountiful harvest.  成功的苹果树嫁接导致了丰收。|近义词：grafting, implantation, inoculation|反义词：removal, excision
