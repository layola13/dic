nonrelation:/ˌnɒnˈreɪʃən/| n. |非亲属关系；不相干的人或事物 |A nonrelation is someone who is not related to you by blood or marriage.  It can also refer to something that is unrelated or irrelevant. |Example: He's a nonrelation, but we've been close friends for years. 他并非亲戚，但我们已经做了多年的好朋友了。|近义词：stranger, unrelated person, irrelevant thing |反义词：relative, kin, relevant thing
