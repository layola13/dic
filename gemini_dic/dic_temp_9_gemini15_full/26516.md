cabbaged:/ˈkæbɪdʒd/| adj.| 卷心菜的；像卷心菜一样的；大量的；拥挤的 |Covered or filled with cabbages; resembling a cabbage in appearance;  in large quantities or amounts; crowded. |例句：The garden was cabbaged with heads of lettuce and cabbage. 花园里种满了莴苣和卷心菜。

The market was cabbaged with shoppers. 市场挤满了购物者。|近义词：packed, crowded, abundant, plentiful |反义词：sparse, empty, scarce


USAGE->

The garden is cabbaged with vegetables. = 花园里种满了蔬菜。
The room is cabbaged with furniture. = 房间里塞满了家具。
