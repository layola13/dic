shimmering: /ˈʃɪmə(r)ɪŋ/ | adj. | 微光闪烁的；闪耀的 |  Having a shimmering quality; seeming to shine with a soft, unsteady light.  It describes something that appears to shine with a soft, wavering light, often due to reflection or refraction. |


例句：

The shimmering heat haze distorted the distant landscape.  微光闪烁的热浪扭曲了远处的景色。

The lake shimmered under the moonlight. 月光下，湖面波光粼粼。


近义词：glistening, glittering, sparkling, twinkling, gleaming


反义词：dull, matte, opaque


词性：形容词
