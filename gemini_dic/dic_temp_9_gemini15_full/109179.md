legitimizations:/ləˌdʒɪtɪməˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |合法化；赋予合法性；正统化 |The act of making something legitimate or acceptable.  This often involves granting authority or official recognition.  It can also refer to the process of justifying or rationalizing something that was previously questionable. |例句：The government's legitimizations of its actions were unconvincing to many citizens.  政府为其行为辩护的说法未能让许多公民信服。

The widespread legitimization of same-sex marriage is a significant social change. 同性婚姻的广泛合法化是一项重大的社会变革。|近义词：justification, validation, authorization, ratification |反义词：illegitimation, invalidation, condemnation
