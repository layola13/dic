permeator: /ˈpɜːrmieɪtər/ | n. |渗透器，透过器 | A device or substance that allows a substance to pass through it.  It often refers to a membrane used in separation processes like reverse osmosis or dialysis. | Example: The permeator efficiently separated the salt from the water.  渗透器有效地将盐与水分离开。 |近义词：membrane, filter, separator |反义词：barrier, obstruction |
