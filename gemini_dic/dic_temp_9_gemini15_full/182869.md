repenters:/rɪˈpɛntərz/| n.|悔过的人，忏悔者|People who feel regret for their past actions and seek forgiveness.  They are individuals who have experienced a change of heart and are actively seeking to amend their ways. |例句：The repenters gathered at the church to confess their sins. 悔过的人聚集在教堂忏悔他们的罪过。

The repenters sought solace in prayer and acts of charity. 悔过的人在祈祷和慈善行动中寻求慰藉。|近义词：penitents, converts, the contrite|反义词：unrepentant, hardened sinners|名词
