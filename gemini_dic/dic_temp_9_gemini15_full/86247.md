heave-out:/hiːvˈaʊt/| vt.|用力抛出，抛弃，呕吐|To throw or cast something with great force; to eject or expel forcefully; to vomit.|例句：The sailors heaved out the anchor. 水手们用力抛出锚。 The ship heaved out tons of garbage. 船抛弃了几吨垃圾。|近义词：fling, cast, eject, expel|反义词：retrieve, keep, retain
