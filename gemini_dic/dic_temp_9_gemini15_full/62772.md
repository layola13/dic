engrave:/ɪnˈɡreɪv/| vt.|雕刻，铭刻|To cut or carve words or designs into a hard surface, such as stone or metal.|例句：The artist engraved his initials on the silver plate.  艺术家在他的银盘上刻上了他的姓名首字母。|例句：They engraved a message on the tombstone. 他们在墓碑上刻了一段话。|近义词：carve, etch, inscribe|反义词：erase, obliterate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He engraves beautiful designs on wood. = 他在木头上雕刻精美的图案。
The company engraves trophies for winners. = 这家公司为优胜者雕刻奖杯。


一般过去时:
She engraved her name on the ring. = 她把自己的名字刻在戒指上。
They engraved the date of their wedding on a locket. = 他们在锁匙上刻下了他们的结婚日期。


现在完成时:
He has engraved hundreds of plates. = 他已经雕刻了数百块版。
The artist has engraved several portraits this year. = 这位艺术家今年已经雕刻了几幅肖像画。


过去完成时:
They had engraved the inscription before the ceremony. = 他们在仪式之前已经刻好了铭文。
She had engraved the message before she gave him the gift. = 她送给他礼物之前已经刻好了留言。


将来时:
We will engrave your name on the plaque. = 我们会在牌匾上刻上你的名字。
The artist will engrave a special design for you. = 艺术家会为你雕刻一个特别的图案。


虚拟语气:
If I had the tools, I would engrave a picture on the stone. = 如果我有工具，我会在石头上雕刻一幅画。
I wish I could engrave my own jewelry. = 我希望我能自己雕刻首饰。


被动语态:
The names were engraved on the monument. = 名字被刻在纪念碑上。
The trophy will be engraved with the winner's name. = 奖杯将刻上优胜者的名字。


疑问句:
Will you engrave the date on the watch? = 你会在手表上刻上日期吗？
Can they engrave a photo onto metal? = 他们可以把照片刻在金属上吗？


否定句:
I cannot engrave the design precisely. = 我无法精确地雕刻图案。
They did not engrave the message deeply enough. = 他们没有把信息刻得足够深。
