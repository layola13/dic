creedlessness:/ˈkriːd.ləs.nəs/| n. |缺乏信仰；不信教；无原则 |The absence of a creed or belief system; lack of strong convictions or principles.  It often implies a moral or spiritual emptiness, rather than simply a lack of religious belief.  |例句：His creedlessness was evident in his opportunistic behavior.他的行为机会主义十足，显然缺乏信仰。

例句：The artist's work reflects a deep sense of creedlessness and alienation.  这位艺术家的作品反映出一种深刻的缺乏信仰和疏离感。


近义词：unbelief, faithlessness, skepticism, agnosticism, secularism


反义词：faith, belief, conviction, piety, orthodoxy


词性：名词
