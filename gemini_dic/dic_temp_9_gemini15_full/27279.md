calumniatory: /kəˈlʌmnɪeɪtəri/ | adj. |诽谤的；中伤的 |  Characterized by or containing calumny; slanderous.  It refers to statements or actions intended to harm someone's reputation by making false accusations. | 例句：He launched a calumniatory attack against his opponent. 他对他的对手发起了诽谤攻击。

例句：The article contained several calumniatory remarks about the politician's personal life. 这篇文章包含了几条关于这位政治家私生活的诽谤言论。


近义词：defamatory, slanderous, libelous, malicious, vituperative

反义词：laudatory, complimentary, flattering, praising

词性: adjective
