papilla:/pəˈpɪlə/| n. |乳头；突起；味蕾|A small, nipple-like projection or bump, especially on a surface.  The term is used in various contexts, referring to anatomical structures in different parts of the body.|The papillae on the tongue contain taste buds.  舌头上的乳头含有味蕾。|  The dermal papillae contribute to the texture of the skin.  真皮乳头有助于皮肤的纹理。| protuberance, projection |  (no direct antonym, depends on context)|名词
