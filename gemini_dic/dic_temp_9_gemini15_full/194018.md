self-fearing: /ˈsɛlf ˈfɪərɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我恐惧的，害怕自己的 |  Describes someone who is afraid of themselves, their own actions, or their own potential. This can manifest as a fear of failure, a fear of one's own power, or a fear of one's own emotions or thoughts.  It's often a symptom of underlying psychological issues. |  He was self-fearing, constantly worried about making mistakes and ruining his reputation. 他很害怕自己，总是担心犯错并毁掉自己的名声。|近义词: self-doubting, apprehensive, fearful, anxious | 反义词: self-assured, confident, fearless
