nonforest:/ˈnɑːnfɔːrɪst/ | adj. | 非森林的；非林地的 | Not covered with forest; relating to areas that are not forested.  | Example: The nonforest areas are experiencing rapid urbanization.  非森林地区正在经历快速的城市化。 The study focused on the impact of deforestation on both forest and nonforest ecosystems. 这项研究的重点是森林砍伐对森林和非森林生态系统的影响。|近义词：woodless, treeless | 反义词：forested, wooded
