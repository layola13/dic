shotmaker:/ˈʃɒtˌmeɪkər/| n. |射手, 制弹者,  (尤指)能准确投篮的球员|A shotmaker is someone skilled at shooting, especially in sports like basketball or shooting games.  They are known for their accuracy and ability to consistently make shots.  The term often implies a high level of skill and precision. |例句：He's a renowned shotmaker; his three-pointers are legendary. 他是一位著名的射手；他的三分球是传奇。

例句：The skilled shotmaker sank the ball with ease.  这位技术娴熟的射手轻松地将球投中。


近义词：marksman, sharpshooter, shooter|反义词：inept shooter, poor shot
