domesticism: /dəˈmɛstɪsɪzəm/ | n. | 家庭生活；家务事；重家庭生活；国内事务 | The state or quality of being domestic; devotion to domestic life; homeliness.  It can also refer to the policies or concerns relating to a particular country's internal affairs. | Example:  Her paintings depict scenes of quiet domesticism, showcasing the everyday life of a family. 她画的画描绘了宁静的家庭生活场景，展现了一个家庭的日常生活。

Example: The government's focus shifted from international relations to domesticism after the economic crisis. 经济危机后，政府的重点从国际关系转向国内事务。

近义词: homebody, family life, home life, domesticity

反义词: cosmopolitanism, internationalism
