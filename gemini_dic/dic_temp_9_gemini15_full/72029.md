flippancy:/ˈflɪpənsi/ | n. |轻率；轻浮；不尊重 |Lack of seriousness or respect; frivolousness.  It often involves a lack of consideration for the feelings or opinions of others, or for the seriousness of a situation.  |例句：Her flippancy in the face of such tragedy was shocking.  她面对如此悲剧却如此轻浮，令人震惊。


例句：His flippancy about the exam made his teacher angry. 他对考试的轻率态度惹怒了他的老师。


近义词：frivolity, levity, glibness, flippantness


反义词：seriousness, gravity, solemnity, respectfulness


词性：名词
