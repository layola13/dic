gaggled:/ˈɡæɡld/| v.|嘎嘎叫，唠叨|To talk in a noisy, confused way; to make the sound of geese.  It can also imply gossiping or chattering incessantly.|例句：The geese gaggled loudly as they flew overhead.  = 大雁在头顶飞过时大声嘎嘎叫。

The children gaggled excitedly about their trip to the zoo. = 孩子们兴奋地唠叨着他们去动物园的旅行。|近义词：chattered, babbled, cackled, gossiped|反义词：whispered, mumbled
