participative: /pɑːrˈtɪsɪpeɪtɪv/ | adj. | 参与的；参与式的；合作的 |  Relating to or characterized by participation; involving the participation of many people.  |  例句：The company fosters a participative management style, encouraging employees to share their ideas.  这家公司提倡参与式管理风格，鼓励员工分享他们的想法。


例句：The participative budgeting process ensures that all departments feel heard.  参与式预算流程确保所有部门都能被听到。


近义词：collaborative, inclusive, interactive, joint


反义词：autocratic, dictatorial, exclusive, passive


词性: adjective
