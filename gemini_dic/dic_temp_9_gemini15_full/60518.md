elaboration:/ɪˌlæbəˈreɪʃən/| n. |详细阐述；详尽说明；精心制作 |The act of elaborating or the state of being elaborated; the process of adding details or expanding on something.  It can refer to a detailed explanation or description, or to the act of making something more complex or ornate. |例句：His elaboration of the theory was impressive.  他的理论阐述令人印象深刻。

例句：The architect's elaboration of the building's design included intricate carvings. 建筑师对建筑设计的详细阐述包括复杂的雕刻。


近义词：explanation, explication, detail, expansion, refinement

反义词：simplification, abbreviation, summarization


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The professor elaborates on the topic every lecture. = 教授在每次讲座上都会详细阐述这个主题。
She elaborates her ideas in her writing. = 她在写作中详细阐述了自己的想法。


一般过去时:
He elaborated on his experiences in the army. = 他详细讲述了他参军的经历。
The artist elaborated the painting with delicate brushstrokes. = 艺术家用精细的笔触细致地描绘了这幅画。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
