carotenes:/ˈkærətiːnz/| n. |胡萝卜素|Carotenes are a group of organic pigments that are found in plants and some other organisms. They are responsible for the orange, yellow, and red colors of many fruits and vegetables.  Carotenes are important for human health because they are precursors to vitamin A. |Example: Carrots are rich in carotenes, which are converted into vitamin A in the body. 胡萝卜富含胡萝卜素，人体会将其转化为维生素A。

|Example:  The vibrant orange color of the pumpkin is due to the high concentration of carotenes within it. 南瓜鲜艳的橙色是由于其中含有高浓度的胡萝卜素。|近义词： carotenoids |反义词：无|词性：名词
