civilians:/səˈvɪljənz/ | n. |平民，百姓 |People who are not members of the armed forces or the police.  Civilians are ordinary people who are not involved in military or police work. | Example:  The bombing killed several civilians.  这次轰炸炸死了几名平民。

The attack targeted both military personnel and civilians.  这次袭击的目标既有军人也有平民。


近义词: non-combatants, commoners | 反义词:military personnel, soldiers
