papilionaceous:/ˌpæpɪliˈoʊneɪʃəs/| adj. |蝶形花冠的；蝶形的|Having the characteristic shape of a butterfly's wings, especially referring to flowers with a standard petal, two wing petals, and two keel petals.  This floral structure is common in the pea family (Fabaceae).|The sweet pea has papilionaceous flowers.|近义词：butterfly-shaped, leguminous|反义词：Non-papilionaceous
