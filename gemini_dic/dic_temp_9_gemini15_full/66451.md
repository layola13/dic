exemplify:/ɪɡˈzɛmpləˌfaɪ/ | vt. | 举例说明；例证；是…的典型 | To serve as a typical example of; illustrate.  | The teacher exemplified the rule with several examples. 老师用几个例子来说明这个规则。  | illustrate, demonstrate, show, represent | contradict, refute | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exemplifies patience. = 他是耐心的典范。
These cases exemplify the problem. = 这些案例说明了这个问题。


一般过去时:
The architect exemplified his designs with detailed sketches. = 建筑师用详细的草图举例说明了他的设计。
Her work exemplified the best of modern art. = 她的作品是现代艺术的典范。


现在完成时:
His actions have exemplified his commitment to the cause. = 他的行为已经证明了他对事业的承诺。
Many examples have exemplified the importance of teamwork. = 许多例子都说明了团队合作的重要性。


过去完成时:
The previous examples had exemplified the dangers of reckless driving. = 先前的例子已经说明了鲁莽驾驶的危险。
His early paintings had exemplified a unique style. = 他早期的画作体现了一种独特的风格。


将来时:
This project will exemplify the company's innovative approach. = 这个项目将体现公司创新的方法。
Future studies will exemplify the long-term effects. = 未来的研究将举例说明长期影响。


虚拟语气:
If he were to exemplify his ideas, we would understand better. = 如果他能举例说明他的想法，我们会更容易理解。
If his work exemplified perfection, it would be legendary. = 如果他的作品堪称完美，那将是传奇。


被动语态:
The principles were exemplified by numerous examples. = 这些原则通过许多例子得到了说明。
The importance of diligence is exemplified in the success stories. = 成功的故事体现了勤奋的重要性。


疑问句:
Does this case exemplify the rule? = 这个案例是否说明了这个规则？
Which examples best exemplify the concept? = 哪些例子最能说明这个概念？


否定句:
His behavior did not exemplify good manners. = 他的行为没有体现良好的礼仪。
The data does not exemplify the proposed hypothesis. = 数据并未证实所提出的假设。


PHRASE->
exemplify by = 用…举例说明
exemplify with = 用…举例说明
exemplify the importance of = 举例说明…的重要性
exemplify a point = 证明一个论点
exemplify the concept of = 举例说明…的概念
