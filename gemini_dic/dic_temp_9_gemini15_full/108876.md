lectionary:/lɛkˈʃənəri/| n. |读经书，礼拜书|A book containing the liturgical readings appointed for use in a particular church or tradition throughout the year.  It specifies the scripture passages to be read at services on particular days.|The lectionary for this Sunday includes readings from the Gospel of Matthew.  这个星期日的读经书包括马太福音的经文。| liturgical calendar, service book |  | noun
