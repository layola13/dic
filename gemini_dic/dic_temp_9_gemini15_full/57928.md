drink-driver: /ˈdrɪŋkˌdraɪvər/ | n. |酒后驾车者| A person who drives a vehicle after consuming alcoholic beverages.  This term emphasizes the act of driving while intoxicated. | Example: The police arrested the drink-driver after he caused an accident.  警方在肇事后逮捕了酒后驾车者。  The drink-driver received a lengthy prison sentence. 酒后驾车者受到了漫长的监禁。| 近义词：drunk driver, intoxicated driver | 反义词：sober driver | 名词
