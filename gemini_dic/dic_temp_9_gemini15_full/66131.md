excepting:/ɪkˈsɛptɪŋ/| prep.|除…之外；不包括|excluding; with the exception of. It is often used interchangeably with "except," but can sometimes sound more formal. |例句：Everyone went to the party excepting John. = 除约翰外，大家都去了派对。  例句：She liked all the flowers excepting the roses. = 除玫瑰花外，她喜欢所有其他的花。|近义词：except, excluding, barring|反义词：including


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are going everywhere excepting the park. = 我们去所有地方，除了公园。
They invite everyone excepting the boss. = 他们邀请所有人，除了老板。


一般过去时:
I liked everything excepting the dessert. = 我喜欢所有东西，除了甜点。
He visited every country excepting China. = 他访问了每一个国家，除了中国。


现在完成时:
I have done everything excepting finish my report. = 我已经完成了所有事情，除了完成我的报告。
She has met everyone excepting her aunt. = 她已经见到了每一个人，除了她的阿姨。


过去完成时:
They had tried everything excepting surgery. = 他们尝试了一切，除了手术。
She had read every book excepting that one. = 她读了每一本书，除了那本。


将来时:
We will go everywhere excepting the museum. = 我们将去所有地方，除了博物馆。
He will contact everyone excepting his competitor. = 他将联系所有人，除了他的竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If I did anything, it would be excepting this task. = 如果我做任何事，那将是除了这项任务。
I wish I could visit everywhere excepting those dangerous areas. = 我希望我能访问所有地方，除了那些危险区域。


被动语态:
Everything was considered excepting the cost. = 除成本外，所有内容都被考虑在内。
All the options were evaluated excepting the most expensive one. = 所有选项都经过评估，除了最昂贵的一个。


疑问句:
Is there anything we should do excepting call the police? = 除报警外，我们还有什么事情要做吗？
Are there any places we should avoid excepting the slums? = 除贫民窟外，我们应该避免去哪些地方？


否定句:
I didn't do anything excepting watch TV. = 我什么也没做，除了看电视。
She didn't go anywhere excepting the supermarket. = 她没有去任何地方，除了超市。


PHRASE->
with the exception of = 除…之外
not excepting = 包括…在内
save for = 除…之外
barring = 除…之外
apart from = 除…之外
