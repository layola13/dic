escalators:/ˈɛskəleɪtərz/| n. |自动扶梯 |Moving staircases that transport people vertically or inclined. They are commonly found in shopping malls, subways, and other public places.  |例句：We took the escalators to the second floor. 我们乘坐自动扶梯到了二楼。|例句：The escalators were out of service, so we had to take the stairs. 自动扶梯停运了，所以我们不得不走楼梯。|近义词：moving stairs, conveyor stairs |反义词：stairs, elevator (in some contexts)|名词
