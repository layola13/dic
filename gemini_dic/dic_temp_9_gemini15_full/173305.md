quasi-critical:/ˈkwɑːziˈkrɪtɪkəl/| adj. |准临界的，接近临界的|Approaching or resembling a critical state; almost but not quite critical.  It implies a state close to a critical point, often with significant implications but not quite reaching the threshold of a full-blown crisis or criticality.|The reactor reached a quasi-critical state, causing concern among operators.  反应堆达到了准临界状态，引起操作员的担忧。|近义词：near-critical, subcritical|反义词：supercritical, far from critical
