globbier:/ˈɡlɒbiər/| adj.|更粘稠的，更块状的|More viscous or clumped together; resembling a glob or globs.  The sauce became globbier as it cooled.  |近义词：lumpier, chunkier, thicker, stickier |反义词：runnier, thinner, smoother


例句:

The globbier the paint, the harder it is to spread evenly. =  颜料越粘稠，越难均匀涂抹。

The chocolate became globbier after it melted and then cooled slightly. = 巧克力融化后稍微冷却后变得更粘稠了。
