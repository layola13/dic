deferred:/dɪˈfɜːrd/ | adj. v. | 推迟的；延期的；缓期的 | Delayed or postponed to a later time.  | The meeting has been deferred until next week.  会议推迟到下周举行。 | postponed, delayed, adjourned | immediate, prompt |  形容词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He defers the decision until tomorrow. = 他把决定推迟到明天。
They defer payment for three months. = 他们将付款推迟三个月。

一般过去时:
She deferred her studies to get married. = 她为了结婚推迟了学业。
The judge deferred sentencing until next week. = 法官将判决推迟到下周。

现在完成时:
We have deferred the project due to funding issues. = 由于资金问题，我们已经推迟了项目。
They have deferred making a decision until they have more information. = 他们已经推迟了做决定，直到他们获得更多信息。

过去完成时:
He had deferred the payment several times before they contacted him. = 在他们联系他之前，他已经几次推迟了付款。
The company had deferred the launch of the new product due to unexpected circumstances. = 由于意外情况，公司已经推迟了新产品的发布。

将来时:
They will defer the final decision until all the evidence is presented. = 他们将把最终决定推迟到所有证据提交之后。
The government will defer the tax increase for another year. = 政府将把税收增加推迟一年。

虚拟语气:
If I had the power, I would defer the project indefinitely. = 如果我有权力，我会无限期地推迟这个项目。
I wish they had deferred the decision until we arrived. = 我希望他们把决定推迟到我们到达为止。

被动语态:
The decision was deferred until the next meeting. = 决定被推迟到下次会议。
The project has been deferred due to unforeseen circumstances. = 该项目由于不可预见的情况而被推迟。

疑问句:
Will the payment be deferred? = 付款会推迟吗？
Has the deadline been deferred? = 最后期限是否被推迟了？

否定句:
They did not defer the decision. = 他们没有推迟决定。
The company will not defer the launch date. = 公司不会推迟发布日期。


PHRASE->
deferred maintenance = 推迟的维护
deferred gratification = 延迟满足
deferred compensation = 递延补偿
deferred revenue = 递延收入
deferred assets = 递延资产


词源:
From Middle English *deferren, from Old French deferrer, from Latin differre ("to carry apart, differ, postpone").  The modern sense evolved from the older meaning of "to put off, delay."
