multicentral:/ˌmʌltiˈsɛntrəl/| adj. |多中心的；多核心的|Having or relating to multiple centers or central points.  The term often describes organizations, systems, or processes with decentralized authority and distributed functions.|例句：The company adopted a multicentral structure, empowering regional offices to make key decisions. 这家公司采用了多中心结构，赋予地区办事处做出关键决策的权力。

例句：Multicentral governance is increasingly common in globalized businesses. 在全球化企业中，多中心治理越来越普遍。


近义词：decentralized, polycentric |反义词：centralized, unicentral

词性：形容词
