removeable: /rɪˈmuːvəbl/ | adj. | 可移动的，可拆卸的 | Able to be removed.  Something that is removable can be detached or taken away easily. | Example: The hard drive is removable, so you can easily upgrade it.  硬盘是可移动的，所以你可以很容易地升级它。The battery is removable for easy replacement. 电池是可拆卸的，方便更换。|近义词：detachable, detachable, extractable | 反义词：permanent, fixed, integral
