herpetology:/ˌhɜːrpəˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. |爬虫学|The branch of zoology concerned with the study of amphibians and reptiles.  Herpetology encompasses the study of their biology, ecology, behavior, and conservation.  |例句：Herpetology is a fascinating field that explores the diverse world of amphibians and reptiles. 爬虫学是一个迷人的领域，探索两栖动物和爬行动物多样化的世界。

例句：My interest in herpetology began with a childhood fascination with snakes. 我对爬虫学的兴趣始于孩提时代对蛇的迷恋。


近义词：reptile study, amphibian study, herpetofauna study (These are less common and more specific terms)
反义词：None (There isn't a direct opposite field of study)
词性：名词
