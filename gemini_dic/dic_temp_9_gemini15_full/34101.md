/ˈsɪvɪzəm/| n. |公民精神；公民意识；公民行为|The quality of being a good citizen; a sense of civic duty and responsibility.  It involves active participation in community affairs and upholding the values of democracy and social justice. |Example:  She showed great civicism by volunteering at the local soup kitchen. 她通过在当地施粥站做志愿者表现出了极高的公民精神。|近义词：civic duty, social responsibility, patriotism|反义词：apathy, indifference, selfishness|名词
