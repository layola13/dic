shouted: /ʃaʊtɪd/ | v. | 喊叫；大声说 | To cry out or speak in a loud voice. | 例句：He shouted at the top of his lungs. 他大声喊叫。  | 例句：She shouted instructions to the workers. 她大声向工人们喊叫指示。 | 近义词：yelled, screamed, roared | 反义词：whispered, murmured


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shouts instructions every day. = 他每天都大声喊叫指示。
She shouts at the children frequently. = 她经常对孩子们大喊大叫。

一般过去时:
He shouted his name across the field. = 他在田野里大声喊叫他的名字。
They shouted with excitement. = 他们兴奋地大喊大叫。

现在完成时:
He has shouted at me several times this week. = 这周他已经对我大喊了几次。
She has shouted her opinions at every meeting. = 她在每次会议上都大声表达她的意见。

过去完成时:
He had shouted himself hoarse before the performance. = 演出前他已经喊哑了嗓子。
They had shouted warnings, but nobody listened. = 他们曾大声警告过，但没有人听。

将来时:
He will shout if he sees a bear. = 如果他看到熊，他会大喊大叫。
She will shout for help if she gets lost. = 如果她迷路了，她会大声呼救。

虚拟语气:
If he shouted louder, they might hear him. = 如果他喊得更大声，他们可能会听到他。
I wish he hadn't shouted at me. = 我希望他没有对我大喊大叫。

被动语态:
His name was shouted from the rooftops. = 他的名字被从屋顶上传了出去（大声喊叫）。
Instructions were shouted across the room. = 指令被大声喊叫传遍了整个房间。

疑问句:
Did he shout at you? = 他对你大喊大叫了吗？
Why did she shout? = 她为什么大喊大叫？

否定句:
He didn't shout at all. = 他根本没有喊叫。
She didn't shout, she whispered. = 她没有喊叫，她低声细语。


PHRASE->
shout at sb. = 对某人大喊大叫
shout for help = 呼救
shout with laughter = 哈哈大笑
shout down sb. = 大声喝止某人
shout out = 大声喊出


词源:
The word "shout" comes from the Old English word "scūtan," meaning "to shoot, to thrust."  This reflects the forceful expulsion of air involved in shouting.  The evolution of the meaning from a physical thrust to a vocal outburst is a semantic shift common in language development.
