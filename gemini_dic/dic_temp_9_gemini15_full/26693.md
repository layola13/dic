caddies:/ˈkædiz/ | n. |球童；行李箱；茶叶罐|A caddie is a person who carries a golfer's clubs during a round of golf.  It can also refer to a small container, often for tea or other small items. |例句：He hired a caddie to carry his clubs. 他雇了一个球童帮他提球杆。  The tea is kept in a small caddie. 茶叶放在一个小茶叶罐里。|近义词：golf attendant, carrier, container|反义词：N/A
