outpromised:/ˌaʊtˈprɑːmɪst/ | vt. |超过承诺；承诺过多 |To promise more than one can deliver; to exceed in promises. |例句：The government outpromised itself on tax cuts.  政府在减税问题上承诺过多。|例句：He outpromised his abilities and couldn't meet the deadline. 他承诺过多，无法按时完成。|近义词：overpromise, exaggerate promises |反义词：underpromise, keep one's promises |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outpromises his capabilities every year. = 他每年都夸大自己的能力。
They outpromise their ability to deliver. = 他们承诺的能力超过了他们实际交付的能力。

一般过去时:
The company outpromised its potential investors. = 公司向潜在投资者许诺过多。
The candidate outpromised what he could realistically achieve. = 候选人承诺了他实际上无法实现的目标。

现在完成时:
The government has outpromised on several key initiatives. = 政府在几个关键项目上承诺过多。
The sales team has outpromised the production capacity. = 销售团队的承诺超过了生产能力。

过去完成时:
They had outpromised their resources before the project even began. = 项目开始之前，他们就已经承诺了超出他们资源范围的事情。
The manager had outpromised the team, leading to burnout. = 经理对团队承诺过多，导致团队精疲力尽。

将来时:
I fear he will outpromise himself again in the next election. = 我担心他在下次选举中还会夸大其词。
The company will outpromise what it can realistically deliver. = 公司将承诺超出其实际交付能力的事情。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't outpromised, the project might have been successful. = 如果他没有承诺过多，项目也许会成功。
If they hadn't outpromised, the team wouldn't be so stressed. = 如果他们没有承诺过多，团队就不会这么紧张。

被动语态:
The promises were outpromised by the company's poor performance. = 公司糟糕的表现使得之前的承诺显得言过其实。
His claims were outpromised by the actual results. = 他的说法与实际结果相比显得言过其实。

疑问句:

否定句:
The company did not outpromise its resources this time. = 公司这次没有承诺超出其资源范围的事情。
He did not outpromise himself during the negotiations. = 他在谈判中没有夸大其词。
