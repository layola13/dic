shrieks:/ʃriːks/| v.|尖声喊叫；发出尖锐的声音|to utter a high-pitched piercing cry.|The child shrieked in terror when she saw the spider.  孩子看到蜘蛛时吓得尖叫起来。|The brakes shrieked as the car came to a sudden stop.  汽车突然刹车时，刹车发出尖锐的响声。|scream, yell, shout|whisper, murmur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shrieks with laughter. = 他笑着尖叫。
The audience shrieks their approval. = 观众们尖叫着表示赞同。

一般过去时:
She shrieked when she saw the mouse. = 她看到老鼠时尖叫起来。
The car tires shrieked as it rounded the corner. = 汽车转弯时轮胎发出尖锐的响声。

现在完成时:
The baby has shrieked all night. = 婴儿整夜都在尖叫。
The audience has shrieked its approval many times during the performance. = 演出期间，观众多次尖叫表示赞同。

过去完成时:
She had shrieked before anyone could react. = 在任何人反应过来之前，她就尖叫了。
The audience had shrieked with delight before the curtain even rose. = 在幕布升起之前，观众就已经兴奋地尖叫了。

将来时:
He will shriek if he sees a snake. = 如果他看到蛇，他会尖叫。
The crowd will shriek with excitement when the band comes on stage. = 当乐队上台时，人群会兴奋地尖叫。

虚拟语气:
If I saw a ghost, I would shriek. = 如果我看到鬼，我会尖叫。
I wish I hadn't shrieked so loudly. = 我真希望我当时没有尖叫那么大声。

被动语态:
(Shriek is rarely used in the passive voice)  尖叫很少用被动语态。
A high-pitched shriek was heard from the next room. = 从隔壁房间传来一声尖锐的尖叫。

疑问句:
Did he shriek when he saw the accident? = 他看到事故时尖叫了吗？
Why did the children shriek? = 为什么孩子们尖叫？


否定句:
He didn't shriek; he just cried silently. = 他没有尖叫；他只是默默地哭泣。
She didn't shriek, but she did jump. = 她没有尖叫，但她确实跳了起来。


PHRASE->
shriek with laughter = 笑着尖叫
shriek in terror = 恐惧地尖叫
shriek in pain = 痛苦地尖叫
let out a shriek = 发出一阵尖叫
a blood-curdling shriek = 一声令人毛骨悚然的尖叫
