hero-worshipping: /ˈhɪərəʊ ˈwɜːʃɪpɪŋ/ | n. adj. | 英雄崇拜；盲目崇拜 | The act of excessively admiring and imitating a hero or role model, often to the point of idolatry.  It can involve unquestioning acceptance of their views and actions, even if flawed. |

例句：His hero-worshipping of the rock star led him to make some questionable life choices.  他的英雄崇拜导致他做出了一些有问题的选择。

例句：The hero-worshipping culture in some societies can be detrimental to individual growth.  某些社会中的英雄崇拜文化可能会阻碍个人发展。


近义词：idolization, veneration, admiration, emulation

反义词：criticism, disdain, contempt, rejection


词性: 名词和形容词
