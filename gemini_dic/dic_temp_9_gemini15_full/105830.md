kitbag:/ˈkɪtbæɡ/| n. | 旅行包，背包 | A kitbag is a strong bag, typically made of canvas or similar material, used for carrying clothes and other personal belongings, especially when traveling or going on an expedition.  It is often smaller and more robust than a suitcase. | He packed his kitbag with his essentials for the hiking trip. 他把远足旅行的必需品装进了他的旅行包。|  rucksack, duffel bag, backpack | suitcase, handbag | 名词
