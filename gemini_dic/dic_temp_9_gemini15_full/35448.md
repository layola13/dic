co-star:/ˈkoʊˌstɑːr/ | n. | 配角演员，联合主演 | A co-star is an actor who plays a main role in a film or play, but not the leading role.  It also refers to someone who stars alongside another, often equally prominent, actor. | Example:  She co-starred with Tom Hanks in the movie. 她和汤姆·汉克斯一起主演了这部电影。 | 近义词: supporting actor, leading player (in the context of sharing the lead) | 反义词: lead actor, star (sole lead)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He co-stars in the new action movie. 他在新的动作电影中担任联合主演。
They co-star in a popular TV series. 他们在一部受欢迎的电视剧中联合主演。


一般过去时:
She co-starred with Leonardo DiCaprio in that film.  她在那部电影中与莱昂纳多·迪卡普里奥联合主演。
They co-starred in a Broadway production last year. 他们去年在百老汇演出中联合主演。


现在完成时:
He has co-starred in several successful films. 他已经参演了几部成功的电影。
She has co-starred with many famous actors. 她已经与许多著名演员合作演出。


过去完成时:
Before this role, he had co-starred in a few independent films. 在这个角色之前，他曾在几部独立电影中担任联合主演。
They had co-starred together many times before this project. 在这个项目之前，他们已经多次合作演出。


将来时:
She will co-star with a rising star in the upcoming romantic comedy. 她将在即将上映的浪漫喜剧中与一位冉冉升起的新星合作。
They will co-star in the new adaptation of the classic novel. 他们将在经典小说改编的新作中联合主演。


虚拟语气:
If he had co-starred in that movie, his career would have taken off earlier. 如果他参演那部电影，他的事业就会更早起飞。
I wish I could co-star with my favorite actress. 我希望我能与我最喜欢的女演员合作。


被动语态:
He was co-starred with a newcomer in the film. 他在这部电影中与一位新人联合主演。（被动语态用法较少见）
The roles were co-starred by two talented actors. 这两个角色由两位才华横溢的演员共同饰演。（被动语态用法较少见）


疑问句:
Who will co-star with him in the sequel? 谁将在续集中与他合作？
Did they co-star in any other movies? 他们还合作过其他电影吗？


否定句:
He did not co-star in that movie, although he was offered the role. 他没有参演那部电影，尽管他得到了这个角色。
She has never co-starred with that particular actor. 她从未与那位演员合作过。


PHRASE->

to co-star in:  在……中联合主演
co-starring cast:  联合主演阵容
a strong co-starring performance:  精彩的联合主演表演
