cowboy-hats:/ˈkaʊˌbɔɪ hæts/| n. |牛仔帽|A cowboy hat is a type of hat typically worn by cowboys and cowgirls, characterized by a wide brim and a high crown.  They are often made of felt or straw. |Example: He tipped his cowboy hat as he rode past.|近义词：ten-gallon hat, Stetson|反义词：None specifically, but perhaps a bowler hat or top hat as stylistic opposites.
