/ˌoʊvərˈlɪbərəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 过度自由化 | The act of making something excessively liberal or free; going beyond the reasonable limits of liberalization.  This often refers to economic policies, social reforms, or political systems.  It implies negative consequences resulting from excessive liberalization. | The government's overliberalizing of the financial markets led to a major economic crisis.  政府过度自由化金融市场导致了严重的经济危机。 | liberalizing, deregulating | restricting, controlling | 动词
