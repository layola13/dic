ministry:/ˈmɪnɪstri/| n.|部, 部委, 牧师的职位, 职责, 服务|The action of ministering; the work or function of a minister.  It can refer to a government department, religious service, or act of serving. |例句：The Ministry of Defence is responsible for national security. 国防部负责国家安全。

例句：He devoted his life to the ministry. 他将一生奉献给了牧师的职责。


近义词：department, office, service, vocation|反义词：None directly applicable.

词源:
From Old French *ministre ("servant, minister"), from Latin minister ("servant, attendant, minister"), from minus ("less") + -ter (suffix denoting an agent).  The meaning evolved from a general sense of "servant" to the specific roles of government officials and religious leaders.
