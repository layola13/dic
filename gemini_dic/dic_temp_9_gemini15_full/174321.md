quick-on-the-trigger: /kwɪk ɒn ðə ˈtrɪɡə(r)/ | adj. |反应迅速的，易怒的，轻易开火的|  Ready to act or react very quickly, often impulsively or aggressively.  This idiom originates from the action of quickly pulling the trigger of a gun.  It implies a lack of deliberation or careful consideration before acting. |例句：He's quick on the trigger with a joke, always ready with a witty remark. 他很会开玩笑，总是能很快地说出俏皮话。

He's quick on the trigger; he fired before he even saw the target. 他反应很快，甚至还没看到目标就开火了。|近义词：impulsive, hasty, rash, trigger-happy, reactive |反义词：deliberate, thoughtful, cautious, slow, considered |词性:形容词
