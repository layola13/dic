outflee:/ˌaʊtˈfliː/| v.|逃离，逃逸|To flee from a place or situation, especially in large numbers or in a hurried manner.|例句：Thousands of refugees outfled the war-torn country. 数千名难民逃离了饱受战争蹂躏的国家。|例句：The crowd outfled the burning building in panic. 人群惊慌地逃离了燃烧的建筑物。|近义词：escape, flee, abscond|反义词：approach, confront


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They outflee the approaching storm. = 他们逃离逼近的风暴。
Birds outflee the forest fire. = 鸟儿们逃离森林大火。

一般过去时:
The villagers outfled the advancing army. = 村民们逃离了推进的军队。
We outfled the collapsing building. = 我们逃离了倒塌的建筑物。

现在完成时:
Many people have outfled the violence in their homeland. = 许多人已经逃离了家乡的暴力。
The animals have outfled the wildfire. = 动物们已经逃离了野火。

过去完成时:
The rebels had outfled the city before the government troops arrived. = 政府军到达之前，叛军已经逃离了城市。
They had outfled the flood before the dam broke. = 大坝决堤之前，他们已经逃离了洪水。

将来时:
They will outflee the impending disaster. = 他们将逃离迫在眉睫的灾难。
The citizens will outflee the city if the earthquake strikes. = 如果发生地震，市民们将逃离城市。

虚拟语气:
If they had known about the danger, they would have outfled the area. = 如果他们知道危险，他们就会逃离该地区。
I wish we had outfled the hurricane sooner. = 我希望我们早点逃离飓风。

被动语态: (This verb is rarely used passively)  The city was (effectively) outfled by the inhabitants. = 城市（实际上）被居民们逃离了。(This sounds unnatural and is generally avoided.)


疑问句:
Did the people outflee the riot? = 人们逃离了暴乱吗？
Will the animals outflee the predator? = 动物们会逃离捕食者吗？


否定句:
The soldiers did not outflee the enemy. = 士兵们没有逃离敌人。
They could not outflee the rapidly spreading fire. = 他们无法逃离迅速蔓延的大火。


PHRASE->
outflee the danger = 逃离危险
outflee the enemy = 逃离敌人
outflee the storm = 逃离暴风雨
outflee the disaster = 逃离灾难
outflee persecution = 逃离迫害
