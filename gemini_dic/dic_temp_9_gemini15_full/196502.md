servicers:/ˈsɜːvɪsərz/| n. |服务商，服务机构|Those who provide services, particularly in finance, such as mortgage servicers or loan servicers.  They handle administrative tasks related to loans and other financial products.|例句：Many mortgage servicers are struggling with the increased number of delinquent loans. 许多抵押贷款服务商都在努力应对违约贷款数量的增加。|例句：The loan servicers are responsible for collecting payments and managing the accounts. 贷款服务商负责收取款项和管理账户。|近义词：providers, administrators, managers |反义词：borrowers, clients
