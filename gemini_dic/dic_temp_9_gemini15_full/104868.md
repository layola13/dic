keener:/ˈkiːnər/| adj.|热心的；敏锐的；渴望的| More enthusiastic or intense; having or showing a strong interest or desire.  A keener sense of smell or hearing indicates a heightened ability to detect odors or sounds. |例句：She's a keener student, always eager to learn more. 她是一位非常热心的学生，总是渴望学习更多。

例句：He has a keener eye for detail than most. 他比大多数人更敏锐地观察细节。

近义词：enthusiastic, ardent, eager, sharp, acute |反义词：apathetic, indifferent, dull, obtuse |词性：形容词

词源:
