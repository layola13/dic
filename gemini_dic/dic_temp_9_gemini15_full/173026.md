quarrelled:/ˈkwɒrəld/ /ˈkwɔːrəld/| v.|争吵，吵架|To have an angry argument or disagreement.|They quarrelled loudly in the street. 他们在街上大声争吵。|He quarrelled with his brother over their inheritance. 他和兄弟因为遗产而争吵。| argued, disputed, bickered|agreed, reconciled|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They quarrel frequently. = 他们经常吵架。
We rarely quarrel. = 我们很少吵架。


一般过去时:
They quarrelled about the movie. = 他们因为电影而吵架。
We quarrelled yesterday. = 我们昨天吵架了。


现在完成时:
They have quarrelled several times this week. = 他们本周已经吵了好几次架了。
We haven't quarrelled in a long time. = 我们很久没吵架了。


过去完成时:
They had quarrelled before the party. = 他们在派对之前就吵过架了。
We had never quarrelled before that incident. = 在那件事发生之前，我们从未吵过架。


将来时:
They will quarrel again, I'm sure. = 我肯定他们还会再吵。
We will not quarrel over such a trivial matter. = 我们不会为这么琐碎的事情吵架。


虚拟语气:
If they quarrelled less, they would be happier. = 如果他们少吵架一些，他们会更快乐。
I wish they hadn't quarrelled. = 我希望他们没有吵架。


被动语态:

否定句:
