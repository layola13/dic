deplumate:/ˈdiːpljuːmeɪt/| vt.|拔光…的毛；除去…的羽毛|To remove the feathers from (a bird or similar creature).  The term can also be used figuratively to mean to strip someone of something valuable or important.|例句：The hunter deplumatethe pheasant before cooking it. 猎人宰杀野鸡前先拔光了它的毛。  The scandal deplumatethe politician's reputation. 这场丑闻毁掉了这位政治家的名声。|近义词：pluck, strip, denude|反义词：feather, adorn
