guardfish:/ˈɡɑːrdfɪʃ/| n. |守卫鱼|A type of fish, typically with a long, slender body and a large, prominent mouth, that often lives in holes or crevices in the reef.  They are known for their defensive behavior. |例句：The guardfish darted into its crevice when it sensed danger.  守卫鱼感觉到危险时，迅速钻进了它的缝隙。  |例句：Divers observed a school of guardfish patrolling the coral reef. 潜水员观察到一群守卫鱼在珊瑚礁上巡逻。|近义词：reef fish, sentinel fish |反义词：prey fish
