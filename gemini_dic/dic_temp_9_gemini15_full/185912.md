revolutions:/ˌrevəˈluːʃənz/| n.|革命，变革，旋转|A revolution is a fundamental change in power or organization, a drastic and far-reaching change in ways of thinking or behaving, or a single complete turn around a central point.|例句：The American Revolution led to the creation of a new nation. 美国革命导致了一个新国家的诞生。|例句：The invention of the printing press caused a revolution in communication. 印刷术的发明引发了通讯领域的革命。|近义词：upheavals, transformations, rotations|反义词：stasis, stagnation, stability|名词
