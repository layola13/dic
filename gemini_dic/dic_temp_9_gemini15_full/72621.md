flyer:/ˈflaɪər/ | n. |传单，宣传单，飞行员 |A flyer is a small printed sheet of paper that is used for advertising something.  It can also refer to a person who flies an aircraft. |例句：I picked up a flyer advertising a concert.  我捡到一张广告演唱会的传单。

例句：He's a skilled flyer with many years of experience. 他是一位经验丰富的飞行员。

近义词：handbill, leaflet, pamphlet, pilot

反义词：N/A

词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flyer announces a new product. = 这张传单宣传一款新产品。
He flies the plane every day. = 他每天都驾驶飞机。


一般过去时:
The flyer advertised a sale. = 这张传单宣传了一次促销活动。
He flew the plane to London yesterday. = 他昨天驾驶飞机飞往伦敦。


现在完成时:
They have distributed the flyers. = 他们已经分发了传单。
She has flown to many countries. = 她已经飞往许多国家。


过去完成时:
They had already posted the flyers before the event. = 他们在活动开始前就已经张贴了传单。
He had flown over the mountains before landing. = 他在降落之前已经飞越了山脉。


将来时:
We will distribute the flyers tomorrow. = 我们明天将分发传单。
He will fly to Paris next week. = 他下周将飞往巴黎。


虚拟语气:
If I were a flyer, I would explore the world. = 如果我是一名飞行员，我会去探索世界。
If I had had more flyers, I would have had a better response. = 如果我有更多传单，我会得到更好的回应。


被动语态:
The flyers were distributed by volunteers. = 传单是由志愿者分发的。
The plane was flown expertly. = 飞机驾驶得很熟练。


疑问句:
Is this flyer informative enough? = 这张传单信息量足够吗？
Does he often fly?  = 他经常飞行吗？


否定句:
