skeptical:/ˈskɛptɪkəl/| adj.|怀疑的，持怀疑态度的|Having an attitude of doubt or disbelief; questioning or doubting the truth of something.|He was skeptical of the salesman's promises.|近义词：doubtful, dubious, incredulous|反义词：believing, credulous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is skeptical about the new project. = 他对这个新项目持怀疑态度。
She is skeptical of his claims. = 她对他的说法表示怀疑。

一般过去时:
They were skeptical of the government's explanation. = 他们对政府的解释持怀疑态度。
I was skeptical that he would finish the work on time. = 我怀疑他能否按时完成工作。

现在完成时:
I have been skeptical of his motives for a long time. = 我长期以来一直怀疑他的动机。
Scientists have been skeptical of the new theory. = 科学家们一直对这个新理论持怀疑态度。

过去完成时:
She had been skeptical of his abilities before he proved himself. = 在他证明自己之前，她一直怀疑他的能力。
We had been skeptical of the results until we saw the evidence. = 直到我们看到证据之前，我们一直对结果持怀疑态度。

将来时:
I will be skeptical until I see the proof. = 在我看到证据之前，我将持怀疑态度。
They will likely be skeptical of any changes to the policy. = 他们很可能会对任何政策变化持怀疑态度。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would be skeptical of such promises. = 如果我是你，我会对这样的承诺持怀疑态度。
I wish I hadn't been so skeptical of his advice. = 我真希望我没有那么怀疑他的建议。

被动语态:
His claims were skeptically received by the public. = 他的说法受到了公众的怀疑。
The new theory is being skeptically examined by experts. = 专家们正在对这个新理论进行怀疑的审查。

疑问句:
Are you skeptical of the findings of the study? = 你对这项研究的结果持怀疑态度吗？
Is he skeptical about the future of the company? = 他对公司的未来是否持怀疑态度？

否定句:
I am not skeptical about his honesty. = 我不怀疑他的诚实。
She was not skeptical of the information provided. = 她不怀疑所提供的信息。


PHRASE->
be skeptical of/about = 对……持怀疑态度
a skeptical view = 怀疑的观点
skeptical inquiry = 怀疑的探究
express skepticism = 表达怀疑
cast skepticism on = 对……表示怀疑
