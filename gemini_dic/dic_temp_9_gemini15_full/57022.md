doubleminded:/ˈdʌb(ə)lˌmaɪndɪd/| adj.| 优柔寡断的；犹豫不决的；心怀二意的 | Having or showing contradictory thoughts or feelings; indecisive.  |例句：He was doubleminded about accepting the job offer, fearing both the challenges and the monotony. 他对接受这份工作犹豫不决，既害怕挑战，又害怕单调。

例句：Her doubleminded approach to the problem prevented her from finding a solution. 她对问题的犹豫不决态度阻碍了她找到解决办法。


近义词：indecisive, irresolute, hesitant, wavering, vacillating

反义词：decisive, resolute, determined, unwavering


词源:  The word "doubleminded" is a compound word. It combines "double," meaning "two" or "twice," and "minded," derived from the word "mind,"  referring to one's thoughts, intentions, or opinions.  The combination suggests having two opposing minds or opinions, leading to indecision.  Its roots are in Old English, with "double" tracing back to Proto-Germanic *dubl- and "mind" to Proto-Germanic *munþaz.
