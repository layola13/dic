lab:/læb/ | n. |实验室；研究室；实验室工作；（尤指大学的）实验课 | A lab is a room or building equipped for scientific experiments, research, or teaching.  |例句：The scientists conducted experiments in the lab. 科学家们在实验室里进行实验。|例句：I have a chemistry lab tomorrow. 我明天有化学实验课。|近义词：laboratory, workshop |反义词：field (in the context of research)


USAGE->
