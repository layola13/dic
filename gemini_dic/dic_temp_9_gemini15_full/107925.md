laryngealist:/ˌlærɪnˈdʒiːəlɪst/| n. |喉科学专家，嗓音专家|A laryngealist is a specialist in the larynx (voice box) and its disorders. They often diagnose and treat voice problems, including vocal cord nodules, polyps, and paralysis.|例句：The laryngealist examined the patient's vocal cords and recommended voice therapy.  喉科学专家检查了病人的声带，并建议进行语音治疗。|近义词：laryngologist, phoniatrician|反义词：无
