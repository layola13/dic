nothingness:/ˈnʌθɪŋnəs/ | n. |虚无；不存在；毫无意义 | The state or quality of being nothing; the absence of all things.  It often refers to a philosophical concept of the absence of being, existence, or reality.  It can also describe a feeling of emptiness or meaninglessness. |例句：The vastness of space filled him with a sense of nothingness.  浩瀚的宇宙让他感到虚无。

例句：He stared at the blank page, feeling the crushing weight of nothingness. 他盯着空白的页面，感到虚无的巨大压力。


近义词：emptiness, void, vacuum, nil, naught |反义词：existence, being, reality, fullness
