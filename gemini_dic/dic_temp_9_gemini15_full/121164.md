misdiagnosing:/ˌmɪsˈdaɪəɡnoʊzɪŋ/| v.|误诊|The act of incorrectly identifying a disease or condition.  It often leads to inappropriate or ineffective treatment.  Misdiagnosing a serious illness can have devastating consequences.|The doctor was later criticized for misdiagnosing the patient's condition.|近义词：misidentifying, mistaking|反义词：correctly diagnosing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor is misdiagnosing many patients. = 医生正在误诊许多病人。
He misdiagnoses illnesses frequently. = 他经常误诊疾病。


一般过去时:
They misdiagnosed the patient's illness. = 他们误诊了病人的疾病。
The hospital misdiagnosed her condition last year. = 去年医院误诊了她的病情。


现在完成时:
The clinic has misdiagnosed several cases recently. = 该诊所最近误诊了几例病例。
She has been misdiagnosed with several different conditions. = 她被误诊为几种不同的疾病。


过去完成时:
The doctor realized he had misdiagnosed the patient after further tests. = 医生在进一步检查后意识到他误诊了病人。
They discovered that they had misdiagnosed the disease long ago. = 他们发现很久以前就误诊了这种疾病。


将来时:
They fear that the new technology may misdiagnose some diseases in the future. = 他们担心这项新技术将来可能会误诊某些疾病。
He will likely misdiagnose the patient without further testing. = 没有进一步检查，他很可能会误诊病人。


虚拟语气:
If the doctor had not misdiagnosed the case, the patient would have received timely treatment. = 如果医生没有误诊，病人就会得到及时的治疗。
I wish they hadn't misdiagnosed my condition. = 我希望他们没有误诊我的病情。


被动语态:
Many patients have been misdiagnosed by inexperienced doctors. = 许多病人被缺乏经验的医生误诊。
The case was misdiagnosed due to a lack of proper equipment. = 由于缺乏合适的设备，该病例被误诊。


疑问句:

Was the patient misdiagnosed by the specialist? = 病人是被专家误诊了吗？


否定句:
The doctor didn't misdiagnose the patient; it was a difficult case. = 医生没有误诊病人；这是一个棘手的病例。
She wasn't misdiagnosed, the symptoms were just unusual. = 她没有被误诊，只是症状不寻常。
