cochairpersons:/ˌkoʊˈtʃɛrpɜːrsənz/| n. |共同主席们；联合主席们 |  The word "cochairpersons" refers to two or more people who jointly preside over a meeting, organization, or event.  It combines "co-" (meaning "together" or "jointly") with "chairperson" (the person who presides over a meeting or organization).| The conference will be co-chaired by two prominent figures in the field. 这场会议将由该领域的两位杰出人物共同主持。  The co-chairpersons worked collaboratively to ensure a smooth and productive meeting. 共同主席们通力合作，以确保会议顺利高效地进行。 | co-chairs, joint chairs |  —  | noun
