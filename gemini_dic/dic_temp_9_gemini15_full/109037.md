legal:/ˈliːɡəl/ | adj. | 法律的；合法的；法定的 | Relating to the law; permitted or required by law. |例句：It is legal to drink alcohol at the age of 21 in many countries.  在许多国家，21岁饮酒是合法的。

The contract is legally binding. 这份合同具有法律约束力。|近义词：lawful, legitimate, rightful |反义词：illegal, unlawful, illegitimate
