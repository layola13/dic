intubate:/ˈɪntjuːbeɪt/ | vt. | 插管；气管插管 | To insert a tube into (a body passage, especially the trachea).  | Example: The paramedics had to intubate the patient to help him breathe.  急救人员不得不给病人插管以帮助他呼吸。 |近义词：cannulate | 反义词：extubate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor intubates the patient. = 医生给病人插管。
He routinely intubates patients in emergency situations. = 他通常在紧急情况下为病人插管。


一般过去时:
The surgeon intubated the patient before the operation. = 外科医生在手术前为病人插管。
They intubated him quickly and efficiently. = 他们迅速有效地为他插管。


现在完成时:
The medical team has intubated several patients today. = 医疗队今天已经为几名病人插管。
She has never intubated a patient before. = 她以前从未给病人插管过。


过去完成时:
The patient had been intubated for several hours before they arrived. = 在他们到达之前，病人已经被插管几个小时了。
The doctor had already intubated the child when the ambulance arrived. = 救护车到达时，医生已经为孩子插管了。


将来时:
The anesthesiologist will intubate the patient before surgery. = 麻醉师将在手术前为病人插管。
They will need to intubate him if his breathing worsens. = 如果他的呼吸恶化，他们将需要为他插管。


虚拟语气:
If the patient's condition worsened, they would intubate him immediately. = 如果病人的病情恶化，他们会立即为他插管。
It would have been better if they had intubated him sooner. = 如果他们早点给他插管就好了。


被动语态:
The patient was intubated by the doctor. = 病人被医生插管了。
He was intubated successfully. = 他成功地进行了插管。


疑问句:
Did they intubate the patient? = 他们给病人插管了吗？
Should the patient be intubated? = 病人是否应该插管？


否定句:
The doctor did not intubate the patient. = 医生没有给病人插管。
They decided not to intubate the patient at that time. = 他们当时决定不给病人插管。



PHRASE->
endotracheal intubation = 气管内插管
nasotracheal intubation = 鼻气管插管
orotracheal intubation = 口气管插管
rapid sequence intubation = 快速序列插管
intubation tube = 插管管


词源:
From Latin *intubāre, from in- + tuba "tube".  源于拉丁语*intubāre，由in-和tuba（“管子”）组成。
