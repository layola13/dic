overplots:/ˈoʊvərplɒts/ | v. |叠加绘制，覆盖绘图|To superimpose one plot or map onto another.  This is typically done to compare or analyze the spatial relationships between different datasets.  The term often appears in the context of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and spatial data analysis.|例句：The researchers overplotted the population density map onto the crime rate map to identify potential correlations. 研究人员将人口密度图叠加到犯罪率图上，以识别潜在的相关性。
The geologist overplotted the seismic data onto the geological map to locate fault lines. 地质学家将地震数据叠加到地质图上以定位断层线。|近义词：superimpose, overlay |反义词：separate, distinguish
