fester:/ˈfɛstər/| v.|腐烂；恶化；溃烂 | To decay and generate pus; to become worse.|The wound festered badly.  伤口溃烂得很厉害。|The resentment festered until it erupted into violence.  怨恨积聚直到爆发成暴力。|heal, improve|cleanse, cure|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wound festers. = 伤口溃烂。
Anger festers in his heart. = 怒火在他心中积聚。

一般过去时:
The cut festered for days. = 伤口溃烂了好几天。
The argument festered in their minds. = 争论在他们心中挥之不去。

现在完成时:
The problem has festered for too long. = 这个问题已经存在太久了。
His anger has festered since the incident. = 自那件事以来，他的怒火一直未消。

过去完成时:
The wound had festered before he sought medical attention. = 他就医之前，伤口就已经溃烂了。
The misunderstanding had festered for years before they finally talked it out. = 误解持续多年，直到他们最终谈开了。

将来时:
If we ignore it, the problem will fester. = 如果我们忽视它，问题将会恶化。
The situation will fester unless we take action. = 除非我们采取行动，否则情况将会恶化。

虚拟语气:
If he had cleaned the wound, it wouldn't have festered. = 如果他当时清理了伤口，它就不会溃烂了。
I wish this feeling of resentment wouldn't fester. = 我希望这种怨恨不会持续下去。

被动语态:
The wound was allowed to fester. = 伤口被任其溃烂。
The issue was allowed to fester for months. = 这个问题被搁置了几个月。

疑问句:
Is the wound festering? = 伤口溃烂了吗？
Is this problem festering beneath the surface? = 这个问题是不是潜藏在表面之下？

否定句:
The wound didn't fester. = 伤口没有溃烂。
The conflict didn't fester into a full-blown war. = 冲突没有演变成一场全面战争。


PHRASE->
