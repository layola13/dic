overservilenesses: /ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvələnəsɪz/ | n. | 过分奴颜卑膝；过分谄媚；过度卑躬屈膝 |  The excessive display of servility;  extreme and unnecessary deference or submissiveness.  This word highlights the excessive nature of servileness, implying an unhealthy level of subservience.


英文解析: This word is formed by adding the prefix "over-" to the plural noun "servilenesses," which itself is derived from the noun "servility."  "Over-" indicates an excessive or extreme degree. Thus, "overservilenesses" signifies instances of going far beyond appropriate levels of subservience or deference, often to the point of being fawning or obsequious.

例句:

His overservilenesses towards the new manager were embarrassing to watch. = 他对新经理的过度谄媚令人尴尬。


The court witnessed several instances of overservilenesses from the defendant, damaging his credibility. = 法庭目睹了被告多次过度卑躬屈膝的行为，损害了他的信誉。
