resurrecting:/ˌrezəˈrektɪŋ/| v.|使复活；使复兴；重新启用|The act of bringing something back to life or restoring something to its former state.  It can refer to literal resurrection (bringing the dead back to life) or figurative resurrection (reviving something that was inactive or forgotten).|例句：The company is resurrecting its classic product line. 公司正在重新启用其经典产品线。

例句：They are attempting to resurrect the ancient traditions. 他们试图使古老的传统复兴。


近义词：revive, revitalize, restore, rehabilitate

反义词：destroy, kill, extinguish, obliterate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is resurrecting old family recipes. 他正在重新启用旧的家庭食谱。
They are resurrecting the lost art of glassblowing. 他们正在使失传的玻璃吹制技艺复兴。


一般过去时:
She resurrected her grandmother's antique doll. 她使祖母的古董娃娃“复活”了。
The archaeologists resurrected the ancient city from the sands. 考古学家将古城从沙土中发掘出来。


现在完成时:
They have resurrected the band after a long hiatus. 他们经过长时间的休整后，使乐队重新活跃起来。
The museum has resurrected several forgotten masterpieces. 博物馆使几件被遗忘的杰作重见天日。


过去完成时:
He had resurrected his career after a scandal. 他在丑闻之后使自己的事业重获新生。
The team had resurrected their hopes after a losing streak.  球队在经历连败后重燃希望。


将来时:
They will resurrect the play for a new generation of audiences. 他们将为新一代观众重新上演这部戏剧。
The government will resurrect the old infrastructure project. 政府将重启旧的基础设施项目。


虚拟语气:
If they resurrected the project, it would be a success. 如果他们重启这个项目，它将会成功。
I wish they would resurrect the old festival. 我希望他们能恢复旧的节日。


被动语态:
The old building is being resurrected. 这座旧建筑正在被修复。
The forgotten tradition is being resurrected by a new generation. 被遗忘的传统正被新一代人传承。


疑问句:
Is she resurrecting her old blog? 她正在重新启用她的旧博客吗？
Are they resurrecting the old factory? 他们正在重新启用旧工厂吗？


否定句:
They did not resurrect the project. 他们没有重启这个项目。
He couldn't resurrect his failing business. 他无法使他失败的生意起死回生。
