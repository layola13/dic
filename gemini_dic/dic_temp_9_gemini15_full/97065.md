inferiority:/ɪnˌfɪəriˈɒrɪti/ | n. |自卑，劣势，下级 |The feeling or state of being inferior; low status.  Inferiority can manifest in various ways, from a lack of self-confidence to feelings of inadequacy compared to others. It often stems from negative experiences or comparisons. |例句：He suffered from a deep sense of inferiority. 他有一种深深的自卑感。

例句：Her inferiority complex prevented her from pursuing her dreams. 她的自卑感阻止了她追求梦想。


近义词：lowliness, submissiveness, subservience, inadequacy |反义词：superiority, dominance, excellence


词源:
From Middle French *inferiorité, from Latin inferioritas, from inferior (adjective), from infra ("below") + superior ("higher").  The suffix "-ity" denotes a state or quality.
