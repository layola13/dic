shuttlecocks:/ˈʃʌtəlˌkɒks/| n. | 羽毛球 | A shuttlecock is a small, light object used in the game of badminton.  It consists of a cork base with feathers attached.  | Example:  The shuttlecock flew across the net.  羽毛球飞过球网。  The player skillfully returned the shuttlecock. 选手熟练地回击了羽毛球。|近义词:birdie (informal)|反义词:None
