/neɪər/| n.|邻居|a person living near another|The neighbors are having a party tonight. 今晚邻居们正在举行派对。|近义词：neighbor|反义词：distant person


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My neighbor waters his plants every morning. 我的邻居每天早上都浇花。
The neighbors often play music loudly. 邻居们经常大声放音乐。


一般过去时:
My neighbor helped me move last week. 上周我的邻居帮我搬家。
The neighbors complained about the noise yesterday. 邻居们昨天抱怨噪音。


现在完成时:
We have known our neighbors for five years. 我们认识邻居五年了。
The neighbors have just moved in. 邻居们刚搬进来。


过去完成时:
I had already met my neighbors before I moved in. 我搬进来之前就已经见过邻居了。
They had lived there for ten years before they moved. 他们住了十年才搬走。


将来时:
We will visit our new neighbors next week. 我们下周将拜访新邻居。
The neighbors will be having a barbecue tomorrow. 邻居们明天将举行烧烤。


虚拟语气:
If I were a better neighbor, I would help them more. 如果我是一个更好的邻居，我会更多地帮助他们。
I wish my neighbors were quieter. 我希望我的邻居们安静点。


被动语态:
The fence was damaged by our neighbors' dog. 篱笆被我们邻居的狗弄坏了。
The noise complaint was ignored by the neighbors. 噪音投诉被邻居们忽略了。


疑问句:
Do you know your neighbors? 你认识你的邻居吗？
Have you met the new neighbors yet? 你见过新邻居了吗？


否定句:
I don't know my neighbors very well. 我不太了解我的邻居。
We haven't spoken to our neighbors recently. 我们最近没有和邻居说过话。
