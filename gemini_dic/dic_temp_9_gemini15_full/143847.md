outplan:/ˌaʊtˈplæn/| v.|制订计划；预先计划|To make plans in advance, especially for a large-scale or complex project.|例句：We need to outplan the competition to secure the contract.  我们需要提前计划，以确保赢得合同。|近义词：preplan, strategize, scheme|反义词：improvise, wing it


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They outplan their marketing campaigns meticulously. = 他们一丝不苟地预先策划他们的营销活动。
We outplan every detail of the project. = 我们事先计划项目的每一个细节。


一般过去时:
The team outplanned their opponents' strategies. = 该团队预先计划好了对手的策略。
She carefully outplanned her trip to Europe. = 她仔细地预先计划了她去欧洲的旅行。


现在完成时:
The company has outplanned its expansion into new markets. = 公司已经预先计划了向新市场的扩张。
They have outplanned every possible scenario. = 他们已经预先计划了每一种可能的方案。


过去完成时:
He had outplanned his escape route before the meeting. = 他在会议之前就已经预先计划好了逃跑路线。
They had outplanned the surprise party months in advance. = 几个月前，他们就已经预先计划好了惊喜派对。


将来时:
We will outplan our next product launch comprehensively. = 我们将全面预先计划我们下一次产品的发布。
She will outplan her retirement strategy carefully. = 她将仔细地预先计划她的退休策略。


虚拟语气:
If we had outplanned the crisis, the damage would have been less. = 如果我们预先计划了这场危机，损失就会少一些。
I wish we had outplanned the logistics better. = 我希望我们能更好地预先计划好后勤工作。


被动语态:
The project was outplanned by a team of experts. = 这个项目是由一个专家团队预先计划的。
The entire operation was outplanned with precision. = 整个行动都被精确地预先计划好了。


疑问句:
Has the marketing team outplanned the launch event? = 营销团队是否已经预先计划好了发布活动？
Did they outplan their response to potential problems? = 他们是否预先计划了应对潜在问题的方案？


否定句:
They didn't outplan the potential risks adequately. = 他们没有充分预先计划潜在的风险。
We failed to outplan the unexpected delays. = 我们未能预先计划好意外的延误。


PHRASE->
outplan a strategy: 制定策略
outplan a campaign: 制定战役计划
outplan a project: 制定项目计划
outplan a response: 预先计划应对措施
outplan for success: 为成功做好计划
