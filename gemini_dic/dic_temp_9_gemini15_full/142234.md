orogeny:/əˈrɒdʒəni/| n. |造山运动|The process of mountain formation, typically involving the deformation of the Earth's crust and the uplift of large landmasses.|The Himalayas are a product of ongoing orogeny. 喜马拉雅山脉是持续造山运动的产物。|mountain building, tectonics|erosion|名词
