minimize:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪz/ | vt. | 使最小化；使减到最少 | To reduce something to the smallest possible amount or degree. | 例句：We need to minimize the risk of accidents. 我们需要将事故风险降到最低。 | 例句：The company is trying to minimize its losses. 公司正试图将损失降到最低。 |近义词：reduce, lessen, decrease | 反义词：maximize, amplify, increase | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They minimize their expenses. = 他们尽量减少开支。
We minimize the impact on the environment. = 我们尽量减少对环境的影响。

一般过去时:
He minimized the damage. = 他将损失降到了最低。
She minimized the importance of the problem. = 她轻描淡写了这个问题的重要性。

现在完成时:
They have minimized their debt. = 他们已经减少了债务。
We have minimized the risks involved. = 我们已经将所涉及的风险降到了最低。

过去完成时:
He had minimized his involvement in the project. = 他已经尽量减少了他对项目的参与。
She had minimized her contact with him. = 她已经尽量减少了与他的接触。

将来时:
They will minimize their carbon footprint. = 他们将尽量减少他们的碳足迹。
We will minimize the disruption to services. = 我们将尽量减少对服务的干扰。

虚拟语气:
If we minimized waste, we would save money. = 如果我们减少浪费，我们将节省资金。
I wish they had minimized the noise. = 我希望他们当时能把噪音降到最低。

被动语态:
The risks were minimized by careful planning. = 通过仔细的计划，风险被降到了最低。
The impact on the environment is being minimized by new technologies. = 新技术正在尽量减少对环境的影响。

疑问句:
How can we minimize the cost? = 我们怎样才能降低成本？
What steps can be taken to minimize the damage? = 可以采取什么措施来尽量减少损失？

否定句:
They didn't minimize the problem. = 他们没有轻视这个问题。
We didn't minimize the importance of the situation. = 我们没有低估局势的重要性。


PHRASE->
minimize the risk = 降低风险
minimize damage = 减少损失
minimize disruption = 减少中断
minimize costs = 降低成本
minimize errors = 减少错误
