inning:/ˈɪnɪŋ/| n. |局（棒球）；一段时间|A period of play in baseball or cricket, during which each team has a turn at batting.  In baseball, there are nine innings in a game. |例句：The home team scored three runs in the first inning. 主队在第一局得了三分。

例句：The game went into extra innings.  比赛进入加时赛。


近义词：period, phase, stage |反义词：None

词源:  The word "inning" ultimately derives from the Old English word "innan," meaning "within" or "inside."  Its evolution through Middle English reflects the sense of "a turn at bat," where a team is "within" the game's action for their turn.
