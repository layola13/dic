preimage:/ˈpriːɪmɪdʒ/| n. |原像，逆像|The preimage of a point under a transformation is the point that is mapped to it.|例如：In the context of a function f(x) = x², the preimage of 4 is both 2 and -2, because f(2) = f(-2) = 4.|近义词：inverse image, antecedent|反义词：image
