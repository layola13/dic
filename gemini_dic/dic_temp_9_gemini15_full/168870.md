profaner:/proʊˈfeɪnər/| n. |亵渎者；污蔑者 |One who profanes; one who treats something sacred with contempt or disrespect.  |例句：He was considered a profaner of religious relics. 他被认为是亵渎宗教圣物的亵渎者。|近义词：desecrator, violator, blasphemer |反义词：reverer, sanctifier |名词
