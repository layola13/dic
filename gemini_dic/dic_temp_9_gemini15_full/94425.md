implode:/ɪmˈploʊd/ | v. |内爆；崩溃；瓦解 | To collapse inward violently, as if from internal pressure.  The building imploded after the explosion.  | 近义词：collapse, cave in, crumble | 反义词：explode, expand


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pressure implodes the container. = 压力使容器内爆。
The company is slowly imploding due to mismanagement. = 公司由于管理不善而逐渐崩溃。


一般过去时:
The old building imploded during demolition. = 旧建筑在拆除过程中内爆。
The band imploded after their lead singer left. = 主唱离开后乐队瓦解了。


现在完成时:
The economy has imploded, causing widespread unemployment. = 经济已经崩溃，导致了大范围的失业。
The project has imploded due to lack of funding. = 项目由于资金不足而失败了。


过去完成时:
The stock market had imploded before the government intervened. = 政府干预之前，股市已经崩盘了。
The negotiations had imploded, leaving no possibility of agreement. = 谈判已经破裂，没有达成协议的可能性。


将来时:
If they continue this way, the business will implode next year. = 如果他们继续这样下去，这家企业明年就会倒闭。
The building might implode if the support beams fail. = 如果支撑梁失效，建筑物可能会内爆。


虚拟语气:
If the pressure were increased, the container would implode. = 如果压力增加，容器就会内爆。
I wish the team hadn't imploded during the crucial match. = 我真希望球队在关键比赛中没有崩溃。


被动语态:
The building was imploded using controlled explosives. = 建筑物使用受控炸药进行了内爆拆除。
The company was imploded by a series of scandals. = 公司因一系列丑闻而倒闭。


疑问句:
Will the building implode during demolition? = 建筑物在拆除过程中会内爆吗？
Did the team implode under the pressure of the playoffs? = 球队在季后赛的压力下崩溃了吗？


否定句:
The building did not implode; it simply collapsed. = 建筑物没有内爆；它只是倒塌了。
The project did not implode despite the numerous challenges. = 尽管面临诸多挑战，项目并没有失败。
