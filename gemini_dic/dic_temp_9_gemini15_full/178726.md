recti: /ˈrɛkti/ | v. |矫正，纠正，调整|To make or set right; correct.|The mechanic rectified the problem with the car engine. 机械师纠正了汽车引擎的问题。|近义词：correct, amend, adjust |反义词：worsen, distort


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rectifies mistakes in his work daily. 他每天纠正工作中的错误。
The company rectifies customer complaints promptly. 公司迅速处理客户投诉。

一般过去时:
She rectified the error in the report. 她纠正了报告中的错误。
The judge rectified the unfair verdict. 法官纠正了不公平的判决。

现在完成时:
They have rectified the faulty wiring. 他们已经纠正了错误的线路连接。
We have rectified the situation. 我们已经纠正了局面。

过去完成时:
He had rectified the problem before the meeting. 他在会议前已经解决了这个问题。
She had rectified her mistake before anyone noticed. 她在任何人注意到之前已经改正了她的错误。

将来时:
They will rectify the damage as soon as possible. 他们会尽快修复损坏。
The government will rectify the economic imbalance. 政府将纠正经济失衡。

虚拟语气:
If they rectified their mistakes, they would succeed. 如果他们纠正了错误，他们就会成功。
I wish they had rectified the situation earlier. 我希望他们早点纠正这种情况。

被动语态:
The error was rectified by the editor. 编辑纠正了错误。
The problem will be rectified soon. 问题很快就会得到解决。

疑问句:
Can the damage be rectified? 损坏可以修复吗？
Has the situation been rectified? 局势已经得到纠正了吗？

否定句:
He did not rectify his behaviour. 他没有改正他的行为。
They failed to rectify the situation. 他们未能纠正局面。


PHRASE->
rectify a mistake 纠正错误

rectify an error 纠正错误
rectify a problem 解决问题
rectify defects 纠正缺陷


词源:
From Latin *rectus ("straight, right"), past participle of regere ("to rule, direct, guide").  The meaning evolved from "to make straight" to "to correct."
