earthquake:/ˈɜːθkweɪk/|/ˈɝθkweɪk/| n. |地震|A sudden and violent shaking of the ground, sometimes causing great destruction, as a result of movements within the earth's crust or volcanic action.  |例句：The earthquake caused widespread damage and loss of life. 地震造成大范围破坏和人员伤亡。|例句：A powerful earthquake struck the region last night.  昨晚该地区发生了一次强烈地震。|近义词：tremor, temblor, seismic shock|反义词：calm, stillness
