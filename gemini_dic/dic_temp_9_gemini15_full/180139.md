reforesting:/ˌriːˈfɒrɪstɪŋ/| v.|重新造林；植树造林|The act of planting trees on land that has been deforested.  It aims to restore forests and their ecological functions.|例句：The government is investing heavily in reforesting the mountains. 政府正在大力投资山区重新造林。


例句：The organization is committed to reforesting areas affected by wildfires. 该组织致力于对受野火影响的地区进行重新造林。


近义词：afforestation, replanting, tree planting


反义词：deforestation


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reforesting the hillsides. = 他们在重新造林山坡。
We reforest areas damaged by logging. = 我们重新造林被采伐破坏的地区。


一般过去时:
They reforested the area after the fire. = 大火之后，他们重新造林了该地区。
The company reforested a large tract of land. = 公司重新造林了一大片土地。


现在完成时:
They have reforested a significant portion of the land. = 他们已经重新造林了很大一部分土地。
The government has successfully reforested many areas. = 政府已成功地对许多地区进行了重新造林。


过去完成时:
They had reforested the area before the new housing development began. = 在新的住宅开发项目开始之前，他们已经对该地区进行了重新造林。
The environmental group had already reforested the valley by the time the grant was awarded. = 当拨款发放时，环保组织已经完成了对山谷的重新造林。


将来时:
They will reforest the damaged land next spring. = 他们将在明年春天重新造林受损的土地。
We will be reforesting the slopes over the next five years. = 我们将在未来五年内对山坡进行重新造林。


虚拟语气:
If we had more funding, we could reforest a much larger area. = 如果我们有更多的资金，我们可以重新造林更大的面积。
I wish we could reforest the entire region. = 我希望我们能够对整个地区进行重新造林。


被动语态:
The land is being reforested by the local community. = 这片土地正在由当地社区重新造林。
A large area was reforested last year. = 去年重新造林了一大片地区。


疑问句:
Are they reforesting the national park? = 他们在对国家公园进行重新造林吗？
Will the project successfully reforest the damaged areas? = 该项目能否成功地对受损地区进行重新造林？


否定句:
They are not reforesting the area at the moment. = 他们目前没有对该地区进行重新造林。
The company did not reforest the land as promised. = 公司没有按照承诺对土地进行重新造林。
