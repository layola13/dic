infirms:/ɪnˈfɜːrmz/ | v. |使虚弱；使衰弱 | to weaken or enfeeble; to make less strong or effective | The illness infirms the body.  疾病使身体虚弱。| The long journey infirms the traveler. 漫长的旅程使旅行者虚弱。| weakens, debilitates | strengthens, invigorates | 动词
