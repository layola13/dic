cancer:/ˈkænsər/ | n. |癌症，癌；恶性肿瘤 | A disease in which some of the body's cells grow uncontrollably and spread to other parts of the body.  Cancer can affect many parts of the body and have various forms depending on the origin and spread of the cancerous cells. | Example:  She was diagnosed with lung cancer. 她被诊断出患有肺癌。|近义词：malignancy, tumor (when cancerous)|反义词：health, wellness
