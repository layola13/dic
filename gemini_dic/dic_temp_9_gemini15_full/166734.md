presidents: /ˈprezɪdənts/ | n. | 总统们，总统职位 | The word "presidents" is the plural form of "president," referring to multiple individuals holding the office of president.  It can refer to presidents of countries, companies, organizations, or other entities. |例句：The presidents of several nations met to discuss global issues.  几个国家的总统会面讨论全球性问题。


The presidents of the different clubs all attended the meeting. 各个俱乐部的会长都出席了会议。

近义词：leaders, chief executives, heads of state | 反义词：subjects, subordinates


词源:  The word "president" comes from the Latin word "praesidens," which means "presiding," "sitting before," or "one who presides."  The suffix "-ent" indicates the person who performs the action.
