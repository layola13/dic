rebreeds:/ˌriːˈbriːdz/| n.|再繁殖，重新培育|The act of breeding animals or plants again, often with a focus on specific traits or to improve a breed.|例句：The farmer focused on rebreeds to improve the milk yield of his cows.  这位农民专注于再繁殖以提高奶牛的产奶量。|近义词：reproduction, breeding, propagation|反义词：extinction, eradication
