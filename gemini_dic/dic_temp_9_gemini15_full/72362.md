flowing:/ˈfloʊɪŋ/| adj.| 流动的；流畅的；飘逸的 | flowing describes something that moves smoothly and continuously, like a river or a piece of fabric.  It can also describe something that is expressed in a smooth and easy way, like writing or speech. |例句：The river flowed smoothly and quietly. 这条河平稳而安静地流淌着。The dancer's flowing dress added to her grace. 舞蹈演员飘逸的裙子更添优雅。|近义词：running, fluid, smooth, graceful |反义词：stagnant, stiff, jerky, abrupt


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The river is flowing steadily. = 河水稳定地流淌着。
Her hair is flowing in the wind. = 她的头发在风中飘扬。

一般过去时:
The water flowed rapidly after the rain. = 雨后水流湍急。
The silk flowed beautifully over her shoulders. = 丝绸美丽地滑过她的肩膀。

现在完成时:
The river has been flowing for centuries. = 这条河已经流淌了几个世纪。
The artist has created a flowing masterpiece. = 艺术家创作了一幅流畅的杰作。

过去完成时:
The river had been flowing for days before the dam broke. = 水坝决堤前，河水已经流淌了几天。
The writer had already written a flowing narrative before she started editing. = 作家在开始编辑之前已经写了一个流畅的叙事。

将来时:
The water will flow downhill. = 水会流下山坡。
The fabric will flow beautifully in the breeze. = 布料将在微风中飘逸。

虚拟语气:
If the river were flowing faster, it would be dangerous. = 如果河水流得更快，那就危险了。
If the writing were more flowing, it would be easier to read. = 如果文章更流畅，就更容易阅读了。

被动语态:
The water is being flowed into the reservoir. = 水正被流入水库。(此句被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The fabric is being flowed by the wind. = 布料被风吹动。(此句被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用)

疑问句:
Is the river flowing today? = 河水今天在流动吗？
Is the music flowing smoothly? = 音乐流畅吗？

否定句:
The river isn't flowing today. = 河水今天没有流动。
The writing isn't flowing very well. = 这篇文章写得不太流畅。
