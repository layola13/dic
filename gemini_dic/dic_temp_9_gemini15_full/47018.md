英文解析：Dastardliness refers to the quality or state of being dastardly; it signifies a lack of courage,  moral weakness, and often despicable behavior.  It implies a cowardly and shameful act.


例句：

The dastardliness of his betrayal was evident in his subsequent actions. 他背叛的卑劣行径在他随后的行为中显而易见。

His attack on the defenseless old woman was an act of pure dastardliness. 他袭击手无寸铁的老妇人的行为是纯粹的卑劣行径。


词源：

The word "dastardliness" derives from the Middle English word "dastard," which meant "coward."  "Dastard" itself has uncertain origins, possibly related to Old Norse or Old French roots.  The "-liness" suffix is a common English suffix that creates nouns indicating a quality or state.
