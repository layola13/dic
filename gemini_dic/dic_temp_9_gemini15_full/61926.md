emulating:/ˈɛmjuleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 模仿，模拟，仿真 |  To emulate someone or something means to imitate them, especially by trying to be as good as or better than them.  It also refers to imitating the function or operation of something. |例句：She spent years emulating her favourite artists. 她花了数年时间模仿她最喜欢的艺术家。

例句：The software emulates the behaviour of a real computer. 该软件模拟真实计算机的行为。


近义词：imitate, copy, mimic, simulate, replicate | 反义词：originate, innovate, create


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He emulates his father's work ethic. = 他效仿他父亲的职业道德。
They emulate successful business models. = 他们模仿成功的商业模式。


一般过去时:
She emulated the style of famous painters. = 她模仿著名画家的风格。
The researchers emulated the natural process. = 研究人员模拟了自然过程。


现在完成时:
He has emulated many great leaders. = 他已经模仿了许多伟大的领导者。
The team has emulated the winning strategy. = 该团队已经模拟了获胜策略。


过去完成时:
She had emulated her hero since childhood. = 她从小就模仿她的英雄。
They had emulated the design before production. = 他们在生产前已经模拟了设计。


将来时:
He will emulate his mentor's success. = 他将效仿导师的成功。
The company will emulate the industry's best practices. = 公司将效仿行业最佳实践。


虚拟语气:
If I could emulate his skills, I would be very happy. = 如果我能模仿他的技能，我会非常高兴。
I wish I could emulate his success in business. = 我希望我能模仿他在商业上的成功。


被动语态:
His style was emulated by many young artists. = 他的风格被许多年轻艺术家模仿。

否定句:
