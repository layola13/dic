putted:/ˈpʌtɪd/ | v. | 推杆；轻放；轻击 | To strike (a ball), especially in golf, with a putting stroke.  |例句：He putted the ball into the hole. 他把球推进了洞。|例句：She carefully putted the delicate vase on the table. 她小心地把精致的花瓶轻轻放在桌子上。|近义词： tapped, stroked, knocked | 反义词：threw, hurled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He putts the ball well. = 他推杆技术很好。
She putts every day to improve her skills. = 她每天都练习推杆来提高技术。


一般过去时:
He putted the ball right into the cup. = 他一杆把球推进了洞。
She putted the golf ball gently onto the green. = 她轻轻地把高尔夫球推上果岭。


现在完成时:
He has putted a lot of balls today. = 他今天推了很多球。
She has putted consistently well this tournament. = 在这次比赛中，她的推杆一直很稳定。


过去完成时:
He had putted the ball before the other players arrived. = 在其他球员到达之前，他已经把球推了出去。
She had putted several times before finally sinking the putt. = 她推了几次才最终把球推进洞。


将来时:
He will putt the ball with more confidence tomorrow. = 他明天会更有信心去推杆。
She will putt first on the next hole. = 她将在下一洞率先推杆。


虚拟语气:

I wish I had putted more accurately. = 我希望我的推杆更精准一些。


被动语态:
