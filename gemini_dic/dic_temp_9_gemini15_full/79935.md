gnomonic:/nəˈmɒnɪk/ | adj. | 类似的；透视投影的；有关地晷的 | Relating to or resembling a gnomon;  of or relating to gnomonic projection, a map projection where the projection surface is a plane tangent to the globe at a point.  In the context of sundials, it refers to the style or pin that casts the shadow. | The gnomonic projection is useful for navigation because great circles appear as straight lines. | 近义词： projective (in the context of projection) | 反义词：None specifically, but words describing other map projections could be considered antonyms contextually.
