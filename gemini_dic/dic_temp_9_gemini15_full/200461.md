slalom:/ˈslæləm/| n. v.|  回转滑雪；（其他运动中的）回转滑行 |The act of skiing, snowboarding, or cycling down a slope by making a series of turns around obstacles, typically poles. Also, the course itself. | 例句：The skier executed a perfect slalom down the mountain.  滑雪者完美地完成了下山回转滑雪。

The slalom course was challenging but exhilarating.  回转滑雪道具有挑战性，但也令人兴奋。


近义词：skiing, snowboarding, cycling (in the context of slalom)


反义词：straight run (a run without turns)


词性：名词，动词
