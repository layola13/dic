evoker:/ɪˈvoʊkər/ | n. |唤起者；引起回忆的人或事物 | A person or thing that evokes something.  An evoker is something that brings something else to mind or creates a particular feeling or atmosphere.  |例句：The old photograph was a powerful evoker of happy childhood memories.  那张旧照片强烈地唤起了快乐的童年回忆。

The haunting melody served as an evoker of sadness and longing.  那首萦绕不去的旋律唤起了悲伤和渴望。


近义词：reminder, stimulator, catalyst | 反义词：suppressor, inhibitor


词性: 名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The painting evokes a sense of peace. = 这幅画唤起了一种平静的感觉。
That song evokes memories of my childhood. = 那首歌唤起了我童年的回忆。


一般过去时:
The smell of rain evoked a feeling of nostalgia. = 雨的味道唤起了怀旧的感觉。
The movie evoked strong emotions in the audience. = 这部电影在观众中唤起了强烈的情感。


现在完成时:
This music has evoked many positive emotions in listeners. = 这音乐已在听众中唤起了许多积极的情感。
The speech has evoked a strong response from the public. = 这篇演讲已引起公众的强烈反响。


过去完成时:
The old house had evoked a sense of mystery before its renovation. = 这栋旧房子在翻新之前曾唤起了一种神秘感。
The poem had evoked a deep sadness in me before I understood its meaning. = 在我理解这首诗的意思之前，它曾在我心中唤起深深的悲伤。


将来时:
The new exhibition will evoke strong reactions from art critics. = 新展览将引起艺术评论家的强烈反应。
The upcoming performance will evoke a sense of wonder in the audience. = 即将上演的演出将给观众带来一种奇妙的感觉。


虚拟语气:
If the music evoked stronger emotions, the performance would be more successful. = 如果音乐能唤起更强烈的情感，演出就会更成功。
I wish the painting evoked a sense of joy instead of sadness. = 我希望这幅画能唤起快乐而不是悲伤的感觉。


被动语态:
A sense of longing was evoked by the melancholic tune. = 忧郁的曲调唤起了渴望的感觉。
Strong emotions were evoked by the actor's powerful performance. = 演员精彩的表演唤起了观众强烈的情感。


疑问句:
What feelings does this artwork evoke in you? = 这件艺术品在你心中唤起了什么感觉？
What memories does this scent evoke? = 这种气味唤起了什么回忆？


否定句:
The movie did not evoke any strong emotions in me. = 这部电影并没有在我心中唤起任何强烈的情感。
The book failed to evoke the intended response from readers. = 这本书未能引起读者预期的反应。


PHRASE->
evoke memories = 唤起回忆
evoke emotions = 唤起情感
evoke a response = 引起反应
evoke a sense of = 唤起一种…的感觉
evoke an image = 唤起一幅画面


词源:  From Middle French évoquer, from Latin evocare "to call out, summon," from e- "out" + vocare "to call."
