ectodermal:/ˌɛktoˈdɜːrməl/ | adj. | 外胚层的；外胚叶的 | Relating to the ectoderm, the outermost layer of cells or tissue of a developing embryo, from which the skin, nervous system, and sense organs are formed.  | Example:  Ectodermal cells differentiate into a variety of specialized cell types. 外胚层细胞分化成各种特化的细胞类型。 The ectodermal dysplasia affected his teeth and hair. 外胚层发育不良影响了他的牙齿和头发。|近义词：epidermal (when referring specifically to the skin) |反义词：mesodermal, endodermal
