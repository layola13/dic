nonfelonious: /ˌnɒnˈfɛləniəs/ | adj. | 非重罪的，非刑事犯罪的 | Not involving a felony; not constituting a serious crime.  The act was nonfelonious, merely a misdemeanor. | 近义词：noncriminal, minor, petty | 反义词：felonious, criminal


英文解析:The word "nonfelonious" is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the word "felonious," which describes an act that constitutes a felony.  A felony is a serious crime, typically punishable by imprisonment for more than one year. Therefore, "nonfelonious" signifies that an action or offense is not a felony; it may be a misdemeanor or not a crime at all.


例句:

The investigation concluded that the damage to the property was nonfelonious.  = 调查得出结论，对财产的损害是非重罪行为。

His actions, though reckless, were ultimately deemed nonfelonious. = 他的行为虽然鲁莽，但最终被认定为非重罪行为。


词源:

* **non-**:  A prefix meaning "not," "opposite of," or "lacking."  It's derived from Latin.

* **felonious**: Derived from "felony," which ultimately comes from Old French "felonie" and earlier from Latin "felonia," meaning "treachery," "wickedness," or "evil deed."
