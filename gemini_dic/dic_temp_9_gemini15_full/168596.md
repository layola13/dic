prochemical: /ˌproʊˈkɛmɪkəl/ | adj. | 前化学的；化学前驱的 | Relating to or denoting a substance that is a precursor to a chemical compound or is involved in an early stage of a chemical process.  It often refers to a substance that is converted into another, more active chemical within a biological system. | Example:  The prochemical is metabolized in the liver to form the active drug.  前体药物在肝脏中代谢形成活性药物。 | 近义词: precursor, precusor substance, pro-drug | 反义词: metabolite, product
