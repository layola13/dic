mathematicians:/ˌmæθəməˈtɪʃənz/ | n. | 数学家们 | People who study or work in mathematics. Mathematicians are skilled in using abstract reasoning and problem-solving techniques to explore and develop mathematical theories and applications. They often work in academia, research, or industry. |  Many famous mathematicians have contributed significantly to the field.  许多著名的数学家对该领域做出了重大贡献。

Many mathematicians are working on unsolved problems. 许多数学家正在研究未解决的问题。


近义词:  mathematicians(复数), mathematical experts, mathematical scientists

反义词:  无
