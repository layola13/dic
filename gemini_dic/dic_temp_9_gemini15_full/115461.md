manufacture:/ˌmænjuˈfæktʃər/| vt. n.|制造，生产|To make something on a large scale using machinery.  |例句：This company manufactures cars. 这家公司生产汽车。|例句：The manufacture of these goods requires specialized equipment. 这些商品的生产需要专门的设备。|近义词：produce, fabricate, create|反义词：destroy, demolish|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They manufacture cars in that factory. = 他们在那个工厂制造汽车。
The company manufactures various types of electronics. = 这家公司生产各种电子产品。

一般过去时:
They manufactured thousands of toys last year. = 他们去年生产了数千个玩具。
Ford manufactured its first car in 1908. = 福特汽车公司在1908年制造了它的第一辆汽车。

现在完成时:
They have manufactured over a million units this year. = 他们今年已经生产了超过一百万台设备。
The factory has manufactured high-quality products for decades. = 这家工厂几十年来一直在生产高质量的产品。

过去完成时:
They had manufactured all the parts before the assembly began. = 在组装开始之前，他们已经制造好了所有零件。
The company had manufactured similar products in the past. = 这家公司过去生产过类似的产品。

将来时:
They will manufacture more electric vehicles in the future. = 未来他们将生产更多电动汽车。
The factory will manufacture a new line of products next year. = 该工厂明年将生产一条新的产品线。

虚拟语气:
If they manufactured more efficiently, they would make more profit. = 如果他们生产效率更高，他们就能赚更多钱。
I wish they manufactured a better quality product. = 我希望他们生产更高质量的产品。

被动语态:
Cars are manufactured in many countries around the world. = 汽车在世界许多国家都有生产。
The product is manufactured using advanced technology. = 该产品采用先进技术制造。

疑问句:
Where are these products manufactured? = 这些产品在哪里生产？
Who manufactures this type of equipment? = 谁制造这种设备？

否定句:
They do not manufacture that particular model anymore. = 他们不再生产那种特定型号了。
The company did not manufacture toys before 2010. = 这家公司在2010年之前没有生产玩具。
