jacobites:/ˈdʒeɪkəbaɪts/| n.|雅各布派支持者；詹姆斯派支持者|Supporters of James II and his heirs in their claim to the British throne after the Glorious Revolution of 1688.  They believed in the divine right of kings and opposed the Protestant succession established by the Act of Settlement. |The Jacobites fought several uprisings against the Hanoverian monarchs.  雅各布派发动了几次起义反对汉诺威王朝的君主。|近义词：Jacobite, supporter, loyalist|反义词：Whig, Hanoverian
