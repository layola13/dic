heavyish:/ˈhɛviʃ/| adj.|有点重的，相当重的|Slightly heavy; somewhat heavy.  It describes something that is heavier than average but not excessively so.|例句：The box was heavyish, but I managed to lift it.  这个箱子有点重，但我还是把它抬起来了。

例句：The cake was heavyish, so I only ate a small piece.  蛋糕有点重，所以我只吃了一小块。


近义词：somewhat heavy, fairly heavy, rather heavy|反义词：light, lightweight
