firelock: /ˈfaɪərˌlɒk/ | n. |火绳枪|A firearm that uses a matchlock to ignite the gunpowder.  A firelock is an early type of firearm where a burning match is used to ignite the gunpowder. They were important in warfare for centuries before flintlocks became common. |Example: The musketeers stood shoulder-to-shoulder, their firelocks gleaming in the sun.  火枪手肩并肩地站着，他们的火绳枪在阳光下闪闪发光。  The antique firelock was a prized possession of the collector. 这支古董火绳枪是收藏家的珍藏。|近义词：matchlock musket, firearm |反义词：modern firearm
