resigner:/rɪˈzaɪnər/| n. |辞职者，放弃者 |A resigner is a person who has resigned from a job, position, or membership.  They have formally given up their role or position. | Example:  The resigner left a note on his desk before departing.  这位辞职者在离开前留了一张字条在他的办公桌上。|近义词：departer, leaver |反义词：appointee, incumbent
