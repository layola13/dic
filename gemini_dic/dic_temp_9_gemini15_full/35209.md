clucked:/klʌkt/| v.|咯咯叫，啧啧地说|To make the short, repeated sound typical of a hen.|The hen clucked contentedly as she sat on her eggs. 母鸡满意地咯咯叫着，坐在蛋上。The teacher clucked her tongue disapprovingly.老师不赞成地啧啧舌头。|murmured, muttered|shouted, cheered|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The hen clucks all day. = 母鸡整天咯咯叫。
He often clucks his tongue in disapproval. = 他经常啧啧舌头表示不满。

一般过去时:
The hen clucked loudly when she laid an egg. = 母鸡下蛋时大声咯咯叫。
She clucked her tongue at the mess. = 她对这堆烂摊子啧啧舌头。

现在完成时:
The hen has clucked all morning. = 母鸡叫了一上午了。
She has clucked her disapproval several times. = 她已经多次啧啧表示不满。

过去完成时:
The hen had clucked before she laid her eggs. = 母鸡下蛋前已经咯咯叫过了。
He had clucked his tongue before he started to speak. = 他开口说话前已经啧啧舌头了。

将来时:
The hen will cluck when she sees her chicks. = 母鸡看到小鸡就会咯咯叫。
She will cluck her tongue if you don't clean your room. = 如果你不打扫房间，她会啧啧舌头。

虚拟语气:
If the hen clucked, I would know she was happy. = 如果母鸡咯咯叫，我就知道她很高兴。
I wish she hadn't clucked so loudly. = 我真希望她不要叫那么大声。

被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)
Her disapproval was clucked at the meeting. = 会议上她的不满被啧啧地表达了出来。(不太自然)
The sound was clucked from the hen house. = 鸡舍里传来了咯咯叫的声音。(不太自然)


疑问句:
Does the hen cluck often? = 母鸡经常咯咯叫吗？
Did she cluck when she saw the fox? = 她看到狐狸的时候咯咯叫了吗？


否定句:
The hen didn't cluck this morning. = 母鸡今天早上没有咯咯叫。
He didn't cluck his tongue at my mistake. = 他没有因为我的错误而啧啧舌头。


PHRASE->
cluck at someone = 对某人啧啧不满
cluck of disapproval = 不满的啧啧声
clucking sound = 咯咯叫的声音
softly clucked = 轻声咯咯叫
to cluck one's tongue = 啧啧舌头
