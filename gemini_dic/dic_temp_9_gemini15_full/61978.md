enables:/ɪˈneɪbəlz/ | vt. |使能够；使可能；授权 |To give someone or something the authority or power to do something.  It implies making something possible. |例句：This software enables users to create professional-looking documents. 这款软件使用户能够创建专业外观的文档。

例句：The new law enables the police to search homes without a warrant.  这项新法律使警方能够未经许可搜查房屋。


近义词：empowers, facilitates, permits, allows | 反义词：disables, prevents, hinders, restricts | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new policy enables greater flexibility. = 新政策使工作更有灵活性。
This tool enables efficient data analysis. = 这个工具能够高效地分析数据。


一般过去时:
The invention enabled faster communication. = 这项发明使得通讯速度更快。
The grant enabled the research to continue. = 拨款使研究得以继续进行。


现在完成时:
Technological advancements have enabled incredible progress. = 技术进步带来了令人难以置信的进步。
His hard work has enabled him to achieve his goals. = 他努力工作使他实现了目标。


过去完成时:
The previous agreement had enabled peaceful negotiations. = 先前的协议促成了和平谈判。
Her experience had enabled her to handle the crisis effectively. = 她的经验使她能够有效地处理危机。


将来时:
The new system will enable faster processing of applications. = 新系统将使应用程序处理速度更快。
This technology will enable us to explore new possibilities. = 这项技术将使我们能够探索新的可能性。


虚拟语气:
If the funding were available, it would enable us to expand. = 如果资金到位，它将使我们能够扩张。
If I had the necessary skills, it would enable me to complete the project. = 如果我有必要的技能，它将使我能够完成项目。


被动语态:
Users are enabled to customize their settings. = 用户可以自定义他们的设置。(被动语态，但“enabled”本身不常用被动)
The project was enabled by a generous donation. = 该项目得益于一笔慷慨的捐款。(强调项目被启动)


疑问句:
Does this key enable access to the system? = 这把钥匙可以访问系统吗？
What technologies enable this level of performance? = 哪些技术能够达到这种性能水平？


否定句:
The lack of resources disabled the project. = 缺乏资源导致项目失败。(否定形式，用disable)
His illness prevented him from participating. = 他的病阻止了他参与。(否定形式，用prevent)


PHRASE->
enable someone to do something = 使某人能够做某事
enable access to = 启用访问
enable features = 启用功能
enable security = 启用安全功能
enable a device = 启用设备
