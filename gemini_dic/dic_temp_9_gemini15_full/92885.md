ideality:/ˌaɪdiˈælɪti/| n. |理想性；完美；理想状态 |The state or quality of being ideal; perfection.  It refers to the state of being perfect or excellent, often in a theoretical or abstract sense.  It can also refer to the pursuit or embodiment of ideals. |例句：The artist strived for ideality in his paintings.  艺术家在他的画作中追求完美。

例句：The ideality of the plan was undermined by the lack of resources.  计划的完美性因缺乏资源而受损。


近义词：perfection, flawlessness, excellence, ideality, perfectionism |反义词：imperfection, flaw, defect, reality
