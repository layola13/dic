namby-pamby:/ˈnæmbi ˈpæmbi/ | adj. | 懦弱的；无力的；甜腻的；矫揉造作的 | Weak, effeminate, sentimental, or insipid.  The word suggests a lack of strength or decisiveness, often accompanied by excessive sentimentality or sweetness. | Example: His namby-pamby poetry lacked the strength and passion of true art.  他的矫揉造作的诗歌缺乏真正艺术的力量和激情。|近义词：weak, effeminate, sentimental, insipid, wishy-washy | 反义词：strong, forceful, masculine, robust, decisive
