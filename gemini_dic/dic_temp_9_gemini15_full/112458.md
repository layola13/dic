loudmouths:/ˈlaʊdmaʊθs/| n.|大嘴巴，夸夸其谈的人|A loudmouth is a person who talks loudly and often boastfully or offensively.  They are characterized by their excessive and often unwelcome talkativeness.|例句：He's such a loudmouth; he can't stop talking about himself. 他是个大嘴巴，总是喋喋不休地谈论自己。|例句：The loudmouths at the back of the room disrupted the lecture.  房间后面那些大嘴巴扰乱了讲座。|近义词：boaster, braggart, chatterbox, blowhard|反义词：quiet person, reserved person
