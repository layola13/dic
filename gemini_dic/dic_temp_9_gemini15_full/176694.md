reacted:/riˈæktɪd/| v.|反应，回应|To respond or behave in a particular way to something.|The crowd reacted with cheers.  人群欢呼雀跃地做出反应。|The patient reacted badly to the medication. 病人对药物反应不良。|responded, answered|ignored, disregarded|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reacts quickly in emergencies. 他在紧急情况下反应迅速。
She reacts well to stress. 她能很好地应对压力。

一般过去时:
I reacted angrily to his accusation. 我对他指控的反应很生气。
The chemicals reacted violently when mixed. 这些化学物质混合时剧烈反应。

现在完成时:
They have reacted strongly to the news. 他们对这个消息反应强烈。
She has reacted positively to the treatment. 她对治疗反应积极。

过去完成时:
He had reacted defensively before I could explain.  在我解释之前，他已经做出了防御性的反应。
The market had reacted negatively to the announcement. 市场对公告做出了负面反应。


将来时:
How will she react to the news? 她会如何回应这个消息？
I expect he will react favorably to the proposal. 我预计他会对这个提议做出积极的回应。

虚拟语气:
If he had reacted differently, the outcome might have been better. 如果他的反应不同，结果可能会更好。
I wish I hadn't reacted so impulsively. 我真希望我没有那么冲动地做出反应。

被动语态:
The proposal was reacted to negatively by the board.  董事会对该提案做出了负面反应。
His actions were reacted against by the community. 他的行为遭到了社区的反击。

疑问句:
How did you react to the situation? 你对这种情况的反应如何？
How will the market react to the new policy? 市场将如何应对新政策？

否定句:
He didn't react at all to my comments. 他对我的评论没有任何反应。
She didn't react in the way I expected. 她的反应并非我预料的那样。


PHRASE->
react to: 对...作出反应
react against: 反抗；反对
react with:  与...反应
overreact:  反应过度
underreact:反应不足
