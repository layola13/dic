helminthagogue: /ˌhɛlmɪnˈθæɡoʊɡ/ | n. |驱虫药，驱虫剂 | A substance or agent that expels parasitic worms (helminths) from the body.  |Example:  The doctor prescribed a helminthagogue to treat the patient's intestinal worms.  医生开了驱虫药来治疗病人的肠道寄生虫。 |近义词：anthelmintic, vermifuge |反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting the promotion of parasitic growth could be considered antonyms.
