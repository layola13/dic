emulsions:/ɪˈmʌlʒənz/| n.|乳状液，乳剂|A mixture of two or more liquids that are normally immiscible (not capable of being mixed), such as oil and water, stabilized by an emulsifier.|例句：Mayonnaise is an emulsion of oil and water.  蛋黄酱是油和水的乳状液。|例句：The photographer used an emulsion of silver salts to coat the film. 摄影师用卤化银乳剂涂覆胶片。|近义词：colloid, suspension|反义词：solution


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Milk is an emulsion of fat and water. = 牛奶是脂肪和水的乳状液。
This cream is a stable emulsion. = 这款面霜是一种稳定的乳状液。
