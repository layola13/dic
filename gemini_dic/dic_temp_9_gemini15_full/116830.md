maxillipedary:/ˌmæksɪˈlɪpɪdəri/| adj.|上颚足的；具上颚足的|Relating to or characteristic of maxillipeds.  Maxillipeds are modified appendages near the mouth of crustaceans, used for feeding and manipulating food.|The study focused on the maxillipedary morphology of various crab species. 这项研究侧重于各种螃蟹的上颚足形态。|近义词：mandibular (in some contexts)|反义词：无
