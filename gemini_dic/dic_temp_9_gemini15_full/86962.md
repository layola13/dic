hencoop:/ˈhɛnˌkup/| n. |鸡舍|A small structure, usually made of wood, where chickens are kept.  |Example: The hens were all safely tucked away in their hencoop for the night.  母鸡晚上都安全地待在鸡舍里。|近义词：chicken house, poultry house |反义词：None
