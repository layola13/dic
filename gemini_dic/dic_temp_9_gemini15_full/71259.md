fiveman: /ˈfaɪvmæn/ | n. |五人小组，五人队 | A group of five men.  This term is often used informally to describe a team or group of five men working together, especially in a specific context like a military squad or a sports team.  It's not a formal or widely used word, but rather a descriptive compound word. | Example:  The fiveman team successfully completed the mission.  这个五人小组成功完成了任务。 | 近义词：quintet (if referring to a musical group), team of five, group of five | 反义词:  None directly, but could be contrasted with larger groups (e.g., ten-man team).
