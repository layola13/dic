refuging:/rɛˈfjuːdʒɪŋ/| v. |寻求庇护；逃难；躲避|The act of seeking refuge or shelter, especially from danger or persecution.  It implies a temporary or ongoing state of seeking protection.|例句：Many people are refuging in neighboring countries to escape the war. 许多人逃往邻国以躲避战争。

例句：The family was refuging from the approaching storm. 这家人正在躲避即将到来的暴风雨。

近义词：seeking refuge, taking shelter, fleeing, escaping

反义词：remaining, confronting, facing
