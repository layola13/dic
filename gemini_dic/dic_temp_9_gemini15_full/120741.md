misanalyzes:/ˌmɪsənəˈlaɪzɪz/ | vt. |误解；分析错误 | To interpret or understand incorrectly; to make a wrong analysis of. | 例句：The researcher misanalyzes the data, leading to flawed conclusions. 研究人员误解了数据，导致结论有缺陷。| 例句：He misanalyzes the situation, resulting in a poor decision. 他误判了形势，导致做出了错误的决定。|近义词：misinterpret, misunderstand, misjudge | 反义词：correctly analyze, accurately interpret


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misanalyzes the situation frequently. 他经常误判形势。
