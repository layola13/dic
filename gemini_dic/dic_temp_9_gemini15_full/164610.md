preexempts:/ˌpriːɪɡˈzɛmpt/ | v. | 预先豁免；预先免除 | To exempt beforehand or in advance; to preempt.  | The new law preempts certain aspects of the existing regulations.  新法律预先豁免了现有规章的某些方面。 | exempt, exclude, except | subject, include | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company preempts its employees from certain taxes. = 公司预先免除其员工的某些税款。
This policy preempts potential conflicts. = 这一政策预先避免了潜在的冲突。


一般过去时:
The court preempted the jury's decision. = 法院预先否决了陪审团的决定。
The governor preempted the state legislature's action. = 州长先发制人，阻止了州议会的行动。


现在完成时:
The government has preempted several attempts at rebellion. = 政府已经先发制人地阻止了几次叛乱企图。
The new software has preempted the need for the older version. = 新软件已经取代了旧版本的需要。


过去完成时:
They had preempted the competitor's launch by releasing their product earlier. = 他们通过提前发布产品来抢先于竞争对手的产品发布。
The army had preempted the enemy's attack by launching a surprise offensive. = 军队通过发动突然袭击先发制人，阻止了敌人的进攻。


将来时:
The company will preempt any potential legal challenges. = 公司将预先避免任何潜在的法律挑战。
We will preempt the problem before it escalates. = 我们会在问题升级之前先发制人解决它。


虚拟语气:
If the government had preempted the crisis, the damage would have been less severe. = 如果政府预先阻止了这场危机，损失就不会那么严重。
I wish they had preempted the situation earlier. = 我真希望他们早点预先阻止这种情况。


被动语态:
The decision was preempted by the higher court. = 该决定被上级法院预先否决。
The meeting was preempted by an unexpected emergency. = 会议被突发事件打断。


疑问句:
Did the company preempt the lawsuit? = 公司是否预先避免了诉讼？
Will the new regulations preempt existing laws? = 新规章会取代现行法律吗？


否定句:
The company did not preempt the strike. = 公司没有预先阻止罢工。
The government failed to preempt the economic downturn. = 政府未能预先阻止经济衰退。
