pass-up:/pæs ˈʌp/| vt.|放弃，忽略，错过|To fail to take advantage of an opportunity; to disregard or ignore something.|例句：He passed up the chance to go to college. 他放弃了上大学的机会。|例句：Don't pass up this opportunity to travel. 不要错过这次旅行的机会。|近义词：reject, decline, forgo, miss|反义词：accept, embrace, seize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He passes up many opportunities. = 他错过很多机会。
They pass up the chance to win. = 他们放弃了获胜的机会。


一般过去时:
She passed up a promotion. = 她放弃了一次晋升。
We passed up the dessert. = 我们没有吃甜点。


现在完成时:
I have passed up several job offers. = 我已经放弃了好几个工作机会。
They have passed up the chance to invest. = 他们错过了投资的机会。


过去完成时:
He had passed up many chances before he finally succeeded. = 他在最终成功之前错过了很多机会。
She realized she had passed up a great opportunity. = 她意识到她错过了一个绝佳的机会。


将来时:
I will pass up the offer if the salary is too low. = 如果薪水太低，我会放弃这个提议。
They will pass up the game if the weather is bad. = 如果天气不好，他们将放弃比赛。


虚拟语气:
I would have passed up the job if I had known the conditions. = 如果我知道情况的话，我就不会接受这份工作了。(这里用的是would have passed up,  表示与过去事实相反的假设)
If I were you, I wouldn't pass up this chance. = 如果我是你，我不会错过这个机会。(这里用的是wouldn't pass up, 表示与现在事实相反的假设)


被动语态:
The opportunity was passed up by many people. = 许多人都错过了这个机会。
The offer was passed up because of the low salary. = 由于薪水低，这个提议被拒绝了。


疑问句:
Will you pass up the invitation? = 你会拒绝邀请吗？
Did he pass up the chance to speak? = 他错过了发言的机会吗？


否定句:
I won't pass up this opportunity. = 我不会错过这个机会。
He didn't pass up the chance to see her. = 他没有错过见她的机会。


PHRASE->
pass up a chance = 放弃机会
pass up an opportunity = 放弃机会
pass up a good deal = 放弃一个好交易
pass up the chance to do sth = 放弃做某事的机会
pass up something good = 放弃好东西
