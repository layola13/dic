ploughfoot:/ˈplaʊfʊt/ | n. |犁铧; 犁头 | The ploughfoot is the metal part of a plough that cuts and turns the soil.  It is the crucial element for effective ploughing.  | The farmer sharpened the ploughfoot before beginning to plough the field.  农民在开始耕地之前磨快了犁铧。  The rusty ploughfoot was a relic from a bygone era. 生锈的犁铧是过去时代的遗物。| share, plowshare |  (无明确反义词，可考虑“broken ploughfoot”损坏的犁铧)| 名词
