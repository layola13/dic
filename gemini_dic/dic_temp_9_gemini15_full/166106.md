preposterous:/prɪˈpɒstərəs/| adj.|荒谬的，极其愚蠢的 |extremely absurd or unreasonable.  |例句：The idea that the earth is flat is preposterous.  地球是平的这个想法是荒谬的。

例句：His explanation for his lateness was utterly preposterous. 他解释迟到的理由完全是荒谬的。


近义词：absurd, ridiculous, nonsensical, ludicrous, illogical, unreasonable

反义词：reasonable, sensible, logical, rational, credible


词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The idea seems preposterous. = 这个想法似乎很荒谬。
His claims are preposterous. = 他的说法很荒谬。


一般过去时:
His excuse was preposterous. = 他的借口很荒谬。
The suggestion seemed preposterous at the time. = 那时这个建议似乎很荒谬。


现在完成时:
His behavior has been preposterous lately. = 他最近的行为很荒谬。
The whole situation has become preposterous. = 整件事变得荒谬了。


过去完成时:
I thought his argument had been preposterous. = 我认为他的论点很荒谬。
The decision seemed preposterous even then. = 即使在当时，这个决定也显得荒谬。


将来时:
His actions will seem preposterous in the future. = 他的行为将来会显得荒谬。
The plan will likely prove preposterous. = 这个计划很可能会被证明是荒谬的。


虚拟语气:
If his claims were true, it would be preposterous. = 如果他的说法是真的，那将是荒谬的。
It would be preposterous to assume that. = 假设那样是荒谬的。


被动语态:
His theory was considered preposterous. = 他的理论被认为是荒谬的。
The proposal was deemed preposterous. = 该提案被认为是荒谬的。


疑问句:
Isn't the whole thing preposterous? = 这整件事难道不荒谬吗？
Does that sound preposterous to you? = 那听起来对你来说荒谬吗？


否定句:
The idea isn't preposterous, it's simply unconventional. = 这个想法并不荒谬，它只是不寻常。
His explanation wasn't entirely preposterous. = 他的解释并非完全荒谬。


PHRASE->
a preposterous idea = 一个荒谬的想法
a preposterous claim = 一个荒谬的主张
a preposterous suggestion = 一个荒谬的建议
a preposterous notion = 一个荒谬的概念
utterly preposterous = 完全荒谬的


词源:
From Late Latin praepropere  "headlong, rashly"  + -ous.  The modern meaning developed from the sense of something done rashly and without consideration, thus absurd.
