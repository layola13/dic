preassemblies:/ˌpriːəˈsɛmbliz/| n.|预组装件，预先组装|Partially assembled components or units ready for final assembly.  These are often used in manufacturing to increase efficiency and reduce assembly time.|例句：The car manufacturer uses preassemblies of the engine and transmission to speed up production. 汽车制造商使用发动机和变速箱的预组装件来加快生产速度。|例句：The furniture company ships preassemblies of cabinets to save on shipping costs. 家具公司运送橱柜的预组装件以节省运输成本。|近义词：subassemblies, prefabricated parts|反义词：raw materials, individual components|名词
