open-pit:/ˈoʊpən pɪt/ | n. |露天矿;露天采矿场 | An open-pit mine is a surface mining technique of extracting rock or minerals from the earth by their removal from an open pit or borrow.  Open-pit mining is used when deposits of commercially valuable material are found near the surface. | Example: The copper mine is an enormous open-pit operation. 这座铜矿是一个巨大的露天矿。  The company plans to expand its open-pit coal mine. 公司计划扩大其露天煤矿。|近义词：open-cast mine, quarry |反义词：underground mine
