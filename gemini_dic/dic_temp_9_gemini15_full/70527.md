filialnesses:/ˈfɪliəlˌnɛsɪz/| n. |孝顺的行为；子女应尽的义务|The plural form of "filialness," referring to instances or aspects of filial piety.  It describes the multiple ways in which someone demonstrates filial duty or respect.  The word is less common than "filial piety" or simply "filial duty".|Examples:  His various filialnesses, from helping his parents with chores to supporting them financially, were admirable.  Her many filialnesses showed a deep love and respect for her aging mother.|近义词: filial piety, filial duties,  devotion to parents|反义词:  disrespect, disobedience, neglect
