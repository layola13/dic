cheesemonger:/ˈtʃiːzˌmʌŋɡər/| n. |奶酪商，奶酪贩子 |A cheesemonger is a person who sells cheese, often specializing in a wide variety of cheeses and offering expert knowledge about them.  They may work in a cheese shop, a larger grocery store, or even a market stall.|例句：The cheesemonger recommended a strong cheddar to pair with the red wine. 这家奶酪店老板推荐了一种浓郁的切达干酪来搭配红酒。|例句：Many cheesemongers are passionate about their craft and take pride in their selection.许多奶酪商对自己的手艺充满热情，并以自己的选择为荣。|近义词：cheese seller, cheese dealer |反义词：cheese consumer (反义词比较牵强，更像是一种对比关系)|名词
