plains:/pleɪnz/ | n. |平原，平地 |Extensive areas of flat land with few trees.  Plains are characterized by their relatively level surface and lack of significant elevation changes. They can be found in various climates and are often used for agriculture and grazing. |Example: The vast plains of the Midwest are ideal for farming.  中西部广阔的平原非常适合耕种。

The African savannas are vast, grassy plains. 非洲大草原是广阔的草地平原。

|近义词：flatlands, prairies, meadows |反义词：mountains, hills, highlands |名词
