polyanthus:/ˈpɒliænθəs/| n. |多花报春花；多花樱草|A polyanthus is a type of primrose that produces many flowers on a single stem.  They are known for their vibrant colors and are popular garden plants. |The garden was ablaze with color, particularly the vibrant polyanthus blooming in profusion. 花园里一片鲜艳，特别是盛开的五彩缤纷的多花报春花。|primrose, primula|  |n.
