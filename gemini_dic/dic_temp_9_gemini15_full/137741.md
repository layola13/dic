nonvalues:/ˈnɒnˈvæljuːz/ | n. |非价值观，不重要的事物 |Things considered unimportant or lacking in value;  the opposite of values.  These can include behaviors, beliefs, or goals that are considered detrimental or harmful.  |Example:  The company's focus on short-term profits led to a disregard for environmental nonvalues. 这家公司专注于短期利润，导致忽视了环境非价值观。|近义词：unimportant things, trivialities, irrelevancies |反义词：values, principles, priorities
