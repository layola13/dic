domineer:/ˌdɒmɪˈnɪər/| /ˌdɑːmɪˈnɪr/| vt.| 跋扈；专横；支配； |To rule over someone in a tyrannical or overbearing way. To be bossy or overbearing.|例句：He tried to domineer over his colleagues. 他试图对同事颐指气使。

例句：She domineered her family with her strong will. 她凭借坚强的意志支配着她的家庭。


近义词：rule, control, tyrannize, bully, boss around

反义词：obey, submit, yield
