coactivity:/ˌkoʊækˈtɪvɪti/| n. |协同作用；共同活动；协同性 |The interaction of two or more things, especially in a way that produces a combined effect greater than the sum of their individual effects.  It often refers to the combined action of multiple components or agents working together to achieve a common goal or outcome. |例句：The researchers observed a significant coactivity between the two drugs, resulting in enhanced therapeutic effect. 研究人员观察到这两种药物之间存在显著的协同作用，从而增强了治疗效果。

例句：The success of the project relies heavily on the coactivity of all team members.  项目的成功很大程度上取决于所有团队成员的协同作用。


近义词：synergy, collaboration, cooperation, teamwork |反义词：antagonism, opposition, conflict


词性: 名词
