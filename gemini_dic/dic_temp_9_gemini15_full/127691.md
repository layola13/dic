negativeness:/ˌneɡəˈtɪvnəs/| n. |消极性；负面性；悲观 |The state or quality of being negative; pessimism or a tendency to emphasize the bad aspects of things.  It can refer to an attitude, outlook, or behavior characterized by pessimism, cynicism, or a lack of hope. |例句：His negativeness affected the team's morale. 他的消极情绪影响了团队士气。

例句：The negativeness surrounding the project discouraged potential investors. 项目周围的负面情绪吓退了潜在投资者。


近义词：pessimism, cynicism, gloom, despair, negativity |反义词：optimism, positivity, hopefulness, cheerfulness |词性：名词
