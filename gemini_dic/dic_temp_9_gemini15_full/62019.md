enantiosis:/ˌɛnæntiˈoʊsɪs/| n. |对掌性；对映异构现象；镜像异构现象|The property of existing in two forms that are mirror images of each other, like a left hand and a right hand.  This term is primarily used in chemistry and biology to describe molecules that are mirror images but not superimposable, often having different properties. |例句：The enantiosis of the drug molecule resulted in two isomers with different pharmacological activities.  药物分子的对映异构现象导致了两种具有不同药理活性的异构体。|近义词：chirality, isomerism|反义词：achirality
