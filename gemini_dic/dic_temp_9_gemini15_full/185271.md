retransplant:/ˌriːˈtrænzˌplænt/| vt.| 再移植；重新移植 |To transplant again or anew.  The act of transplanting again. |例句：The gardener had to retransplant the seedlings after the heavy rain damaged the initial planting. 园丁不得不重新移植幼苗，因为大雨破坏了最初的种植。

The surgeon decided to retransplant the kidney after initial rejection.  外科医生决定在第一次排异反应后重新移植肾脏。|近义词：replant, transplant anew|反义词：remove, uproot
