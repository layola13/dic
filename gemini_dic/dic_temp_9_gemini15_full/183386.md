reprojecting:/ˌriːprəˈdʒɛktɪŋ/| v.|重新投影；重新规划|The act of projecting again, often with adjustments or onto a different surface or coordinate system.  This involves transforming coordinates from one projection to another.  It's commonly used in GIS (Geographic Information Systems) and computer graphics.|例句：The cartographer is reprojecting the map from UTM to WGS84. 制图师正在将地图从UTM投影重新投影到WGS84投影。

The engineers are reprojecting the building's shadow onto the adjacent building to assess sun exposure. 工程师们正在将建筑物的阴影重新投影到相邻建筑物上以评估日照情况。|近义词：remapping, transforming, converting|反义词：projecting (in the sense of initial projection)
