imparter: /ɪmˈpɑːrtər/ | n. | 传授者；给予者 | One who imparts something, especially knowledge or information.  A person or thing that gives or communicates something. | The teacher was a skilled imparter of knowledge.  这位老师是一位熟练的知识传授者。  The book served as an imparter of wisdom to its readers. 这本书为读者传授了智慧。 | giver, communicator, dispenser, transmitter | receiver, withholder | Noun


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The teacher imparts knowledge to her students. = 老师向学生传授知识。
He imparts wisdom through his writings. = 他通过写作传授智慧。


一般过去时:
She imparted the secret to her friend. = 她把秘密告诉了她的朋友。
He imparted his experience to the young team. = 他把他的经验传授给了年轻的团队。


现在完成时:
She has imparted valuable lessons to many. = 她已经向许多人传授了宝贵的教训。
He has imparted his knowledge generously. = 他慷慨地传授了他的知识。


过去完成时:
They had imparted the news before the meeting began. = 他们在会议开始之前已经传达了这个消息。
She had imparted her feelings before she left. = 她离开前已经表达了她的感受。


将来时:
He will impart his knowledge to the next generation. = 他将把他的知识传授给下一代。
They will impart the good news to their families. = 他们将把好消息告诉他们的家人。


虚拟语气:
If he imparted his wisdom, they would understand. = 如果他传授他的智慧，他们就会明白。
I wish he had imparted more information earlier. = 我希望他早点提供更多信息。


被动语态:
The knowledge was imparted clearly. = 知识被清晰地传授了。
The information was imparted to the audience. = 信息被传达给了听众。


疑问句:
Who imparted this information? = 谁传达了这个信息？

否定句:
