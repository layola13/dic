over-discuss:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskʌs/ | v. | 过度讨论，反复讨论 | To discuss something excessively or repeatedly; to talk about something more than is necessary or helpful.  |  例句：They over-discussed the minor details, delaying the project. 他们过度讨论了细枝末节，导致项目延期。


例句：We over-discussed the budget until everyone was exhausted. 我们反复讨论预算，直到每个人都筋疲力尽。


近义词：overanalyze, belabor, harp on, dwell on | 反义词：ignore, neglect, overlook


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They over-discuss every single point. = 他们对每一个点都过度讨论。
We over-discuss the plan at every meeting. = 我们每次会议都对计划进行过度讨论。

一般过去时:
They over-discussed the problem last night. = 他们昨晚过度讨论了这个问题。
We over-discussed the details and missed the deadline. = 我们过度讨论细节，错过了最后期限。

现在完成时:
They have over-discussed this issue for weeks. = 他们已经讨论这个问题几周了。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If they had not over-discussed the plan, they would have finished on time. = 如果他们没有过度讨论计划，他们就会按时完成。

否定句:
