preverbal: /ˈpriːvɜːrbəl/ | adj. | 谓词前的；动词前的 |  Relating to or occurring before a verb.  It typically describes an element (like an adverb or particle) that precedes a verb and modifies its meaning or function. | 例句：The preverbal particle "up" in "look up" changes the meaning of "look".  谓词前的粒子“up”在“look up”中改变了“look”的意思。


The preverbal adverb "quickly" modifies the verb "ran".  谓词前的副词“quickly”修饰动词“ran”。


近义词：antecedent (in some contexts), preceding

反义词：postverbal


词性: adjective
