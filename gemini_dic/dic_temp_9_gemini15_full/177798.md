receivables:/rɪˈsiːvəbəlz/| n. |应收款；应收账款 |Money owed to a business by its customers for goods or services sold on credit.  Receivables are a current asset on a company's balance sheet. |Example: The company's receivables increased significantly last quarter.  这家公司的应收账款上个季度大幅增加。|Example:  We need to improve our collection process for outstanding receivables. 我们需要改进我们对未收应收账款的收款流程。|近义词：accounts receivable, outstanding invoices, credit sales |反义词：payables (应付款)
