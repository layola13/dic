detasseling: /ˌdiːˈtæsəlɪŋ/ | n. | 去雄穗 (玉米) | The process of removing the tassels from corn plants to prevent pollination and improve the quality of the kernels.  | The farmers spent weeks detasseling the cornfields.  农民们花了几个星期的时间去除玉米田里的雄穗。 |  – | – | v.
