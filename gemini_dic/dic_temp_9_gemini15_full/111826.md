logger:/ˈlɒɡər/ /ˈlɑːɡər/| n. |记录器，日志记录器|A logger is a system or program that records events or messages, typically for debugging, auditing, or monitoring purposes.  It writes these records to a log file or other storage medium.|例句：The application uses a logger to record errors and warnings.  这个应用程序使用记录器来记录错误和警告。|例句：We configured the logger to send email alerts when critical errors occur. 我们配置了记录器，以便在发生严重错误时发送电子邮件警报。|近义词：recorder, registrar, chronicler|反义词：eraser, deleter|名词
