floppers:/ˈflɒpərz/| n.|  拖鞋；拍打声；笨拙的人|  Loose, often flat, footwear, typically slippers or sandals.  It can also refer to the sound of something flapping or the clumsy movement of something.  |例句：He wore his old floppers to the beach. 他穿着旧拖鞋去海滩。

例句：The bird's wings made loud floppers as it struggled to fly. 鸟翅膀拍打发出很大的声音，因为它挣扎着要飞起来。


近义词：slippers, thongs, sandals,  flops|反义词：boots, shoes (formal)|词性:名词
