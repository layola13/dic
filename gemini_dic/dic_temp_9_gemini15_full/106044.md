kneecapper:/ˈniːkæpər/ | n. v. |膝盖打击者；（尤指）行凶者；使…丧失能力的人或事 | A person who shoots someone in the kneecap; someone or something that cripples or severely weakens something else.  |例句：The kneecapper was caught by the police.  膝盖打击者被警方抓获了。  The economic downturn was a kneecapper for many small businesses. 经济衰退对许多小型企业来说是一场灾难。 |近义词：hitman, assassin, crippler, saboteur |反义词：benefactor, supporter, helper


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He kneecaps his rivals. = 他打击他的竞争对手。
The new policy kneecaps the company's growth. = 新政策扼杀了公司的增长。


一般过去时:
They kneecapped the witness. = 他们袭击了证人（击打膝盖）。
The storm kneecapped the power grid. = 暴风雨摧毁了电网。


现在完成时:
The scandal has kneecapped his career. = 丑闻毁了他的事业。
The drought has kneecapped agricultural production. = 干旱摧毁了农业生产。


过去完成时:
They had kneecapped the opposition before the election. = 他们在选举前就打压了反对派。
The previous recession had kneecapped the local economy. = 此前的经济衰退已经重创了当地经济。


将来时:
The new regulations will kneecap the industry. = 新规将扼杀这个行业。
They will kneecap their competitor with a new marketing campaign. = 他们将通过新的营销活动来打击竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If they kneecapped the project, we would be in trouble. = 如果他们破坏了这个项目，我们将陷入困境。
I wish they hadn't kneecapped the funding. = 我真希望他们没有削减资金。


被动语态:
The project was kneecapped by budget cuts. = 该项目因预算削减而夭折。
His reputation was kneecapped by the media. = 他的名声被媒体毁了。


疑问句:
Did they kneecap the opposition? = 他们打压了反对派吗？
Will the new technology kneecap the existing market? = 新技术会扼杀现有市场吗？


否定句:
They didn't kneecap the deal. = 他们没有破坏这笔交易。
The company didn't kneecap its competitors. = 公司没有打击其竞争对手。


PHRASE->
kneecapper for...= ...的毁灭者
a financial kneecapper = 财务杀手
a political kneecapper = 政治打击

a career kneecapper = 事业终结者
