endorses:/ɪnˈdɔːrsɪz/ | vt. |认可，支持，背书 | To publicly declare one's support for someone or something.  |例句：The celebrity endorsed the new product.  这位名人认可了这款新产品。 |例句：Many experts endorse this new treatment method. 许多专家都认可这种新的治疗方法。|近义词：supports, approves, champions, backs |反义词：opposes, rejects, condemns, denounces |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He endorses the candidate. = 他支持这位候选人。
She endorses the new policy. = 她支持这项新政策。

一般过去时:
The company endorsed the athlete. = 公司赞助了这位运动员。
He endorsed the check. = 他在支票上签字背书。

现在完成时:
They have endorsed the project. = 他们已经批准了这个项目。
She has endorsed several charities. = 她已经支持过几家慈善机构。

过去完成时:
He had endorsed the bill before the vote. = 他在投票前就已经赞成该法案了。
The bank had endorsed the check. = 银行已经在这张支票上进行了背书。

将来时:
They will endorse the agreement. = 他们将认可该协议。
She will endorse the product on television. = 她将在电视上为该产品代言。

虚拟语气:
If he endorsed the plan, it would be more likely to succeed. = 如果他支持这项计划，它成功的可能性就会更大。
I wish the committee would endorse our proposal. = 我希望委员会能批准我们的提案。

被动语态:
The product was endorsed by a famous actor. = 该产品由一位著名演员代言。
The proposal was endorsed by the board. = 该提案得到了董事会的批准。

疑问句:
Does she endorse the new legislation? = 她是否支持这项新法案？
Will the bank endorse this check? = 银行会在这张支票上背书吗？

否定句:
He did not endorse the controversial policy. = 他不支持这项有争议的政策。
The company does not endorse the use of its products in this way. = 公司不认可以这种方式使用其产品。


PHRASE->
endorse a check = 支票背书
endorse a product = 产品代言
endorse a candidate = 支持候选人
endorse a policy = 支持政策
endorse a bill = 赞成法案
