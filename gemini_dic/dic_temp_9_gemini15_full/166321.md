preregnant: /ˌpriːˈreɡnənt/ | adj. | 怀孕前的；妊娠前的 |  Describes the period of time before a pregnancy occurs, often referring to a woman's state of being before conception.  This term is less common than other ways of describing the pre-pregnancy state, and its usage is mostly found in medical or scientific contexts. | Example:  She underwent several preregnant checkups to ensure her health was optimal for conception. 她进行了几次妊娠前检查，以确保她的健康状况适合怀孕。 | 近义词: preconception, antepartum (though antepartum usually refers to the period before childbirth) | 反义词: pregnant, postpartum
