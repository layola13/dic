freedstool:/ˈfriːdstuːl/| n. |免税凳，特权座位|A freedstool is a specific type of stool or seat that historically granted the holder certain privileges or exemptions, often tax-related.  The term is largely archaic and its precise meaning varied depending on context and historical period.  The etymology suggests a connection to freedom from taxation or other obligations.|例句：The merchant guild was granted a freedstool in the marketplace, exempting them from certain levies. 商人公会获得了市场上的免税凳，免除了他们某些税收。|近义词：privileged seat, exempt seat|反义词：taxed seat, ordinary seat
