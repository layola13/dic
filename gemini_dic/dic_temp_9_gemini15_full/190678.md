saunaed:/sɔːˈnɑːd/| v.|桑拿过；去桑拿浴|To spend time in a sauna.  It implies the completion of a sauna session.|例句：I saunaed after my workout to relax my muscles. 我锻炼后去桑拿放松肌肉。

I saunaed for an hour to relieve my stress. 我桑拿了一个小时来缓解压力。|近义词：steamed, sweated|反义词:  chilled, cooled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I sauna regularly to improve my circulation. = 我定期去桑拿以改善血液循环。
He saunas every Sunday morning. = 他每个星期天早上都去桑拿。

一般过去时:
She saunaed after a long hike. = 她在长途徒步旅行后去桑拿。
They saunaed at the spa last night. = 他们昨晚在水疗中心去了桑拿。

现在完成时:
I have saunaed three times this week. = 我这周已经去桑拿三次了。
She has never saunaed before. = 她以前从未去过桑拿。

过去完成时:
He had saunaed before he went to the gym. = 他去健身房之前已经去过桑拿了。
We had saunaed several times that month. = 我们那个月已经去桑拿几次了。

将来时:
I will sauna tomorrow after my run. = 我明天跑步后会去桑拿。
They will sauna at the new health club. = 他们会在新的健康俱乐部去桑拿。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would sauna tonight. = 如果我有时间，我今晚会去桑拿。
I wish I could sauna more often. = 我希望我能更经常地去桑拿。

被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice,  it would sound unnatural.)


疑问句:
Did you sauna yesterday? = 你昨天去桑拿了吗？
Will you sauna before or after dinner? = 你会在晚餐之前还是之后去桑拿？

否定句:
I didn't sauna this morning. = 我今天早上没去桑拿。
They haven't saunaed all week. = 他们整个星期都没去桑拿。
