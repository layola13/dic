fireback:/ˈfaɪrbæk/| n.|防火板；壁炉挡板；（尤指壁炉上的）装饰性金属板|A fireback is a decorative metal plate placed at the back of a fireplace.  It protects the wall behind the fireplace from heat damage and can also be highly decorative.  Firebacks were particularly popular in the 17th and 18th centuries and often featured intricate designs.|The antique fireback was a stunning example of 18th-century craftsmanship.  = 这块古董壁炉挡板是18世纪工艺的惊人典范。

The cast iron fireback was heavy and difficult to move. = 这块铸铁壁炉挡板很重，很难移动。|近义词：hearth plate, fire screen|反义词：无
