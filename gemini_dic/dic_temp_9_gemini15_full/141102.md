onstage:/ɒnˈsteɪdʒ/| adj.|在舞台上的；公开的|Located on or relating to the stage of a theater or other performance venue.  It can also refer to something happening publicly or in the open.|例句：The actors were nervous before going onstage.  演员们上台前很紧张。

The singer's powerful voice filled the entire onstage area. 歌手强大的歌声充满了整个舞台区域。


近义词：on-stage, public, open|反义词：offstage, backstage, private|词性:adj.
