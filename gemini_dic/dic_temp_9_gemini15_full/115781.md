mariolater:/ˌmæriˈoʊleɪtər/| n. |马里奥崇拜者，狂热的马里奥粉丝 |A mariolater is someone who excessively admires and often obsessively dedicates themselves to the video game character, Mario.  This term is typically used in a humorous or slightly ironic way to describe someone with an extreme and perhaps unhealthy devotion to Mario. |Example:  He's a total mariolater; his apartment is completely decked out in Mario merchandise.|近义词：fanatic, enthusiast, devotee |反义词：detractor, critic
