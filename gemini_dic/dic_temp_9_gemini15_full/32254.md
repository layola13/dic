chicky:/ˈtʃɪki/| n.|小鸡；年轻女子；（非正式）女人|A chick is a baby bird, especially a baby chicken.  The word is also used informally to refer to a young woman, or sometimes to a woman in general. |例句：The chick just hatched from its egg.  这只小鸡刚从蛋里孵出来。|例句：She's a really cool chick. 她真是个很酷的女孩。|近义词：bird, pullet, young woman, girl|反义词：old woman, hen
