gloomy:/ˈɡluːmi/ | adj. | 忧郁的；阴暗的；令人沮丧的 |  Feeling or causing a feeling of being sad and without hope.  Characterized by little or no light; dark and depressing. |

例句：

The gloomy weather matched my mood. = 阴沉的天气与我的心情相符。

The gloomy forecast predicted rain for the entire week. = 阴沉的预报预测整个星期都会下雨。


近义词：depressed, dismal, dreary, somber, dark, pessimistic

反义词：cheerful, bright, sunny, optimistic, cheerful


词源:

源自古英语 *glōm,  意为“昏暗，黄昏”。  与"glow"有关，但含义发展为负面。
