defying:/dɪˈfaɪɪŋ/| adj. v. |违抗的；反抗的；挑战的|  To refuse to obey or accept; to challenge (someone or something).  It can describe a person's attitude or action, or an object's characteristics.|


例句：

He was defying the law by refusing to pay his taxes. = 他拒绝纳税，这是在违抗法律。

The climbers were defying the mountain's dangerous conditions. = 登山者们正在挑战这座山危险的环境。


近义词：opposing, resisting, challenging, flouting


反义词：obeying, conforming, accepting, submitting


词性：verb (动词), adjective (形容词)

词源:

The word "defying" comes from the Old French word "defier," meaning "to challenge" or "to refuse."  It's related to the Latin word "fide," meaning "faith" or "trust," suggesting a breaking of trust or a challenge to established norms.
