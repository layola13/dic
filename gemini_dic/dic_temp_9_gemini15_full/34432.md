claustrophobia:/ˌklɔːstrəˈfoʊbiə/| n. |幽闭恐惧症 |An abnormal fear of confined or enclosed spaces.  This fear can manifest in various ways, from mild anxiety to panic attacks.  It's a type of anxiety disorder. |例句：Her claustrophobia made it impossible for her to ride in elevators.  她的幽闭恐惧症使她无法乘坐电梯。

例句：He suffers from claustrophobia and avoids small, enclosed rooms. 他患有幽闭恐惧症，避免进入狭小封闭的房间。


近义词：enclosed space anxiety, confined space phobia |反义词：None (Antonyms describe the absence of the fear, not a contrasting emotion.) |词性：名词
