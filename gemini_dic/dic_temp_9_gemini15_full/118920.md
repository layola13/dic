meted:/ˈmiːtɪd/| v.| 分配；给予；处以（惩罚）|  To distribute or apportion something, especially in a measured or allotted way; to give out as a portion or share.  It can also mean to inflict a punishment or penalty. |例句：The judge meted out a harsh sentence.  法官判处了严厉的刑罚。

The teacher meted out the rewards fairly.老师公平地分配了奖励。|近义词：allocated, distributed, dispensed, dealt, administered |反义词：withheld, retained
