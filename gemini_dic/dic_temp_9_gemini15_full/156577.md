pickle:/ˈpɪkəl/| n. vt.|腌菜；泡菜；困境；棘手的问题 |  to preserve (vegetables, etc.) in brine or vinegar;  a difficult or unpleasant situation | 例句：We made pickles from cucumbers. 我们用黄瓜做了泡菜。  The project turned into a real pickle.  这个项目变成了一个真正的难题。 | 近义词：preserve, conserve, jam, predicament | 反义词：fresh, unpreserved

词源:

The word "pickle" comes from the Dutch word "pekel," meaning "brine" or "salt water," which is used in the pickling process.  The Dutch word ultimately derives from the Middle Dutch word "pekel." The process of pickling has been practiced for centuries as a method of food preservation.
