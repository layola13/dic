escrol:/ɪˈskroʊl/| n.|卷轴；文件；证券|A scroll; a roll of parchment or paper containing a document or writing.  It can also refer to a document or deed, especially one under seal. |例句：The ancient scroll contained a detailed history of the kingdom.  古代卷轴包含了王国详细的历史。

The lawyer presented the escrow agreement. 律师出示了托管协议。|近义词：roll, document, deed|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with modern digital documents.
