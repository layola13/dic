carbon:/ˈkɑːrbən/| n. |碳|A nonmetallic chemical element that occurs in all organic compounds, existing in two forms: diamond and graphite.  It is also a key component of many minerals and fuels.|Example: Carbon is a vital element for all life on Earth.  碳是地球上所有生命的重要元素。|近义词:carbon element, charcoal|反义词:None
