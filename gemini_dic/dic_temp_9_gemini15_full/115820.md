markers:/ˈmɑːrkərz/| n. |标记物，标记；指标；记号|A marker is something that shows the position of something or indicates a limit or boundary.  It can also be a person who marks or grades papers.  Markers are also used in drawing and writing. |例句：The hikers used bright red markers to mark their trail. 登山者用鲜红的标记物来标记他们的路线。

例句：The teacher used a red marker to correct the student's mistakes. 老师用红色记号笔更正学生的错误。

近义词：indicator, sign, signal, tag, label |反义词：eraser
