lutein:/ˈluːtiːn/| n.|叶黄素|A yellow pigment found in plants, especially in the leaves and flowers of many plants. It is also a major component of the macula of the human eye and is an important antioxidant.|例句：Lutein is a powerful antioxidant that protects against age-related macular degeneration.  叶黄素是一种强大的抗氧化剂，可以预防与年龄相关的黄斑变性。|例句：Many fruits and vegetables, such as spinach and kale, are rich sources of lutein. 许多水果和蔬菜，如菠菜和羽衣甘蓝，都是叶黄素的丰富来源。|近义词：carotenoid, xanthophyll|反义词：无
