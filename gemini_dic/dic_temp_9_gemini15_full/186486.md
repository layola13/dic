ricin:/ˈrɪsɪn/| n.|蓖麻毒素|A highly toxic protein found in the seeds of the castor bean plant (Ricinus communis).  It is a potent toxin that can cause serious illness and death if ingested, inhaled, or absorbed through the skin. Ricin is not easily absorbed through intact skin, but can be dangerous if the skin is broken or if it is injected.|例句：Ricin is a dangerous poison that can cause severe health problems. 蓖麻毒素是一种危险的毒药，会导致严重的健康问题。

例句：The terrorist group planned to use ricin to poison the city's water supply.恐怖组织计划使用蓖麻毒素来毒害城市供水。


近义词：abrin (another toxic protein), toxalbumin


反义词：antidote (a remedy for a poison)


词性：名词
