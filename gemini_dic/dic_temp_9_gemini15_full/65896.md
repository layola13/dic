evomit:/ˈɛvəˌmɪt/| vt.|呕吐，吐出|To eject the contents of the stomach through the mouth.|例句：He vomited after eating the spoiled food. 他吃了变质的食物后呕吐了。|例句：The patient vomited blood. 病人呕吐出血。|近义词：throw up, puke, spew|反义词：swallow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He vomits every morning. = 他每天早上都呕吐。
She vomits when she's sick. = 她生病的时候会呕吐。

一般过去时:
The dog vomited on the carpet. = 狗在毯子上呕吐了。
I vomited after the rollercoaster ride. = 过山车之后我呕吐了。

现在完成时:
He has vomited three times today. = 他今天已经呕吐了三次。
She has never vomited blood before. = 她以前从未吐过血。

过去完成时:
He had vomited before the doctor arrived. = 在医生到达之前他已经呕吐了。
She had vomited all night. = 她呕吐了一整夜。

将来时:
I think I will vomit if I eat that. = 我想如果我吃那个我会呕吐。
He will probably vomit on the journey. = 他很可能在旅途中呕吐。

虚拟语气:
If I ate that, I would vomit. = 如果我吃了那个，我会呕吐。
I wish I hadn't eaten so much; I might vomit. = 我真希望我没有吃那么多；我可能会呕吐。

被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)
The food was vomited up. = 食物被吐出来了。(This is less common and sounds unnatural.)
Nothing was vomited. = 什么也没有吐出来。(This is more natural in a passive context.)


疑问句:
Did he vomit after the surgery? = 他手术后呕吐了吗？
Will she vomit on the plane? = 她会在飞机上呕吐吗？


否定句:
He didn't vomit after the meal. = 他饭后没有呕吐。
She didn't vomit despite the rough seas. = 尽管风浪很大，她也没有呕吐。


PHRASE->
vomit blood 吐血
vomit up  吐出
force to vomit 强迫呕吐
violent vomiting 剧烈呕吐
repeated vomiting 反复呕吐
