misrehearse:/ˌmɪsriˈhɜːrs/ | v. | 排练出错，预演不佳 | To rehearse incorrectly or inadequately; to practice something badly or insufficiently.  |例句：The actors misrehearsed the crucial scene, leading to a disastrous performance.  演员们把关键场景排练错了，导致演出灾难性失败。


The band misrehearsed the song, resulting in a sloppy performance at the concert. 乐队把这首歌排练得很糟糕，导致音乐会在演出时很草率。|近义词：rehearse badly, botch, flub |反义词：rehearse well, perfect
