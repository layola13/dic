reconcentrate:/ˌriːˈkɒnsənˌtreɪt/| vt.|重新集中；再浓缩|To concentrate again; to increase the concentration of.|例句：After a break, he tried to reconcentrate on his work.  休息过后，他试图重新集中精力工作。
例句：The factory reconcentrated the juice to make it thicker. 工厂对果汁进行了浓缩使其变稠。|近义词：refocus, concentrate, intensify|反义词：diffuse, disperse, scatter|动词
