meds:/mɛdz/| n.|药物，药品|Medicine or medications, especially those prescribed by a doctor.|例句：I need to take my meds before bed. 我需要睡前服用我的药。|例句：The doctor prescribed some new meds for my headache. 医生为我的头痛开了一些新药。|近义词：medications, drugs, pills|反义词：None specifically, but could consider "placebo" in certain contexts.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He takes his meds every day. = 他每天都吃药。
She needs her meds to sleep. = 她需要吃药才能睡着。

一般过去时:
I took my meds this morning. = 我今天早上吃了药。
He forgot to take his meds yesterday. = 他昨天忘了吃药。

现在完成时:
I have taken my meds already. = 我已经吃过药了。
She has not taken her meds yet. = 她还没吃药。

过去完成时:
He had taken his meds before he went to sleep. = 他睡觉前已经吃过药了。
She had forgotten to take her meds before the appointment. = 她预约前忘了吃药。

将来时:
I will take my meds after dinner. = 我晚饭后会吃药。
He will need to take his meds for a few more weeks. = 他还需要吃几周的药。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would take my meds on time. = 如果我是你，我会按时吃药。
I wish I had taken my meds sooner. = 我真希望我早点吃药。

被动语态:
The meds were prescribed by the doctor. = 这些药是医生开的。
The meds are being dispensed at the pharmacy. = 药正在药房发放。

疑问句:
Have you taken your meds today? = 你今天吃药了吗？
What kind of meds are you taking? = 你正在服用什么药？

否定句:
I didn't take my meds this morning. = 我今天早上没吃药。
She doesn't need to take her meds anymore. = 她不再需要吃药了。
