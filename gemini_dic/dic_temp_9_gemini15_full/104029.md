jujutsu:/dʒuːˈdʒutsʊ/|/ˈdʒuːdʒutsu/| n. |柔术 |A Japanese martial art emphasizing grappling, throws, joint locks, and pressure points.  It's often characterized by its use of an opponent's weight and momentum against them.  |例句：He studied jujutsu for many years and became quite skilled. 他学习柔术多年，技艺精湛。

例句：The jujutsu instructor demonstrated a powerful throw. 柔术教练演示了一个强大的摔技。


近义词：martial art, grappling, aikido (shares some similarities)


反义词：无


词性:名词
