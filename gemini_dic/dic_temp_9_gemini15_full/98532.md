instigates:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪts/| vt.|煽动；激起；唆使|To provoke or incite (someone) to do something, especially something bad.|The rebels instigated a riot in the city center. 叛乱分子在市中心煽动了一场暴动。|近义词：provoke, incite, foment, stir up|反义词：calm, soothe, pacify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He instigates conflicts between his colleagues. 他在同事之间煽风点火。
The government instigates new policies to boost the economy. 政府出台新政策以刺激经济。

一般过去时:
She instigated a fight between the two boys. 她挑拨了两个男孩之间的争斗。
The rumor instigated widespread panic. 这则谣言引发了广泛的恐慌。

现在完成时:
They have instigated several changes within the company. 他们已经在公司内部引发了几项变革。
He has instigated a legal battle against his former employer. 他已经对他之前的雇主发起了法律诉讼。

过去完成时:
She had instigated the whole scheme before anyone realized it. 在任何人意识到之前，她就已经策划了整个计划。
The manager had instigated a meeting to discuss the problem. 经理已经召开了会议来讨论这个问题。

将来时:
They will instigate a new marketing campaign next month. 他们下个月将发起一场新的营销活动。
The protesters will instigate a demonstration against the new law. 抗议者将发起示威活动以反对这项新法律。

虚拟语气:
If he had instigated a rebellion, he would have been punished severely. 如果他煽动叛乱，他将受到严厉的惩罚。
I wish he hadn't instigated that argument. 我真希望他没有挑起那场争论。

被动语态:
A riot was instigated by a small group of extremists. 一场暴动是由一小撮极端分子煽动的。
Changes were instigated to improve efficiency.  为了提高效率，进行了改革。

疑问句:
Who instigated this whole mess?  是谁挑起了这场混乱？
Did he instigate the rumors? 他是否散布了谣言？

否定句:
He did not instigate the violence. 他没有煽动暴力。
The company denies instigating any illegal activity. 公司否认煽动任何非法活动。
