contraltos:/kənˈtræltəʊz/ | n. | 女低音；女低音声部 | Contraltos are female singers with the lowest vocal range in a choir or ensemble.  They provide a rich, deep tone to the harmonies. | The contraltos sang the lowest notes in the beautiful choral piece. 女低音歌手在优美的合唱曲中唱了最低音。  The choir director needed more contraltos to balance the sound. 合唱团指挥需要更多女低音来平衡声音。 | altos, mezzosopranos | sopranos, tenors | 名词
