gloats:/ɡləʊts/| v.|洋洋得意，幸灾乐祸|To express great pleasure or satisfaction at one's own success or another's misfortune, often in a smug or boastful way. |例句：He gloated over his victory. 他洋洋得意地炫耀他的胜利。

例句：She gloated at her rival's failure. 她幸灾乐祸地看着对手的失败。


近义词：brag, boast, exult, revel


反义词：mourn, lament, grieve


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gloats about his achievements every chance he gets. = 他抓住一切机会炫耀自己的成就。
She gloats whenever her opponents make a mistake. = 每当对手犯错时，她都会幸灾乐祸。


一般过去时:
He gloated about his promotion all evening. = 他整个晚上都在炫耀他的升职。
She gloated over her competitor's downfall. = 她为竞争对手的失败而沾沾自喜。


现在完成时:
He has gloated over this win for days. = 他已经为此胜利沾沾自喜好几天了。
She has gloated publicly about her success. = 她公开炫耀了自己的成功。


过去完成时:
He had gloated about his success before the celebration even started. = 庆祝活动甚至还没开始，他就已经开始炫耀自己的成功了。
She had gloated many times over her opponent's previous defeats. = 她曾多次为对手之前的失败而沾沾自喜。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
