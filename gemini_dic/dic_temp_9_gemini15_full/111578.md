lobotomise:/ləˈbɒtəmaɪz/| vt.| 脑叶切除术 | To perform a lobotomy on (someone).  A lobotomy is a surgical procedure that severs connections in the brain's prefrontal cortex. This procedure was once used to treat mental illness, but it is now considered outdated and unethical due to its significant and often irreversible side effects. | Example: The doctor decided to lobotomise the patient to alleviate his severe symptoms.  医生决定对病人进行脑叶切除术以缓解其严重的症状。 | 近义词：prefrontal leucotomy (a more precise term)| 反义词：None specifically, but procedures aimed at restoring brain function could be considered opposites. | 动词
