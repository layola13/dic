overdone:/ˌoʊvərˈdʌn/ | adj. | 做过头了；过分的；矫揉造作的 | Excessively done or prepared; overcooked; exaggerated or excessive in style or expression. | 例句：The steak was overdone and tough.  这块牛排做得太老了，而且很韧。

The movie's plot was overdone and predictable. 这部电影的情节过于夸张且可预测。


近义词：excessive, exaggerated, overcooked, overwrought


反义词：underdone, understated, moderate, subtle

词性：形容词
