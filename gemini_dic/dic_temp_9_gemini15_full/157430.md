pirated:/ˈpaɪrətɪd/ | adj. | 盗版的；海盗的 |  Something that is pirated is illegally copied or obtained.  It refers to things copied without permission from the copyright holder,  often relating to software, movies, music, or books.  It can also relate to acts of piracy committed by seafaring pirates. | 例句：The software is pirated.  这个软件是盗版的。| 例句：The ship was attacked by pirates. 这艘船受到了海盗的袭击。| 近义词：bootlegged, copied illegally, stolen | 反义词：legitimate, authentic, original
