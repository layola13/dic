interspersing: /ˌɪntərˈspɜːrsɪŋ/ | v. |  散布；点缀；穿插 | The act of scattering or placing things at intervals among other things.  | 例句：The author interspersed anecdotes throughout the biography. 作者在传记中穿插了轶事。

例句：The teacher interspersed jokes into her lecture to keep the students engaged. 老师在讲座中穿插笑话以保持学生的参与度。


近义词：intermingling, mixing, scattering, strewing | 反义词：concentrating, grouping, clustering | 词性：动词
