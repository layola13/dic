displaceability:/ˌdɪsˈpleɪsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可取代性，可移动性 | The ability to be displaced or replaced.  The ease with which something can be moved or replaced. | Example: The high displaceability of the workforce made the company vulnerable to downsizing.  劳动力的高度可取代性使公司容易裁员。  The furniture's displaceability allowed for easy rearrangement of the room. 家具的可移动性使得房间很容易重新布置。 | 近义词：replaceability, mobility, removability | 反义词：permanence, fixity, irreplaceability |  名词
