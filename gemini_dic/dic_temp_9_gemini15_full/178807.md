recurs:/rɪˈkɜːrz/| v.|反复出现，再发生|To happen or occur again; to appear again.|The theme of betrayal recurs throughout the novel.  背叛的主题贯穿整部小说。|The problem of overcrowding recurs every year. 人满为患的问题每年都会重现。|reappear, repeat, reiterate|cease, end, stop|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pattern recurs frequently. = 这种模式频繁出现。
The same mistake recurs in his work. = 他工作中同样的错误反复出现。

一般过去时:
The melody recurred throughout the piece. = 这段旋律在乐曲中反复出现。
The symptoms recurred after a few days. = 几天后症状又复发了。

现在完成时:
This problem has recurred several times. = 这个问题已经反复出现几次了。
The error has recurred despite our efforts to fix it. = 尽管我们努力修复，错误还是反复出现。

过去完成时:
The pain had recurred before he sought medical attention. = 他就医前疼痛就反复发作过。
The issue had recurred several times in previous meetings. = 这个问题在之前的几次会议中都反复出现过。

将来时:
I fear the problem will recur next year. = 我担心这个问题明年还会再次出现。
The symptoms may recur if you don't follow the treatment plan. = 如果你不遵循治疗方案，症状可能会复发。

虚拟语气:
If the problem recurred, we would have to find a new solution. = 如果问题再次出现，我们就必须找到一个新的解决方案。
I wish the pain wouldn't recur. = 我希望疼痛不会复发。

被动语态:
The theme is recurred in many of his paintings. = 这个主题在他的许多画作中反复出现。
The error was recurred in the final report. = 错误在最终报告中再次出现。（注意：被动语态使用较少）

疑问句:
Does the motif recur in the final movement? = 主题在最后的乐章中反复出现吗？
Will the problem recur after the fix? = 修复后问题还会再次出现吗？


否定句:
The error did not recur after the correction. = 校正后错误没有再次出现。
The symptoms have not recurred since the treatment. = 自从治疗以来，症状没有复发。


PHRASE->
recur again = 再次发生
recur frequently = 频繁出现
recur periodically = 定期出现
recur relentlessly = 不停地反复出现
recur in dreams = 在梦中反复出现
