misemployed:/ˌmɪsɪmˈplɔɪd/ | adj. | 错用；误用；滥用；失业的 |  Misused; wrongly employed; out of work.  It refers to something or someone being used incorrectly or inefficiently, or to a person being unemployed. |


例句：

The company's resources were misemployed on unprofitable ventures. = 公司的资源被误用于无利可图的风险投资。

Many skilled workers are misemployed due to the economic downturn. = 由于经济低迷，许多熟练工人失业了。


近义词：misused, misapplied, unemployed, underemployed


反义词：employed, well-used, properly used


词性：形容词
