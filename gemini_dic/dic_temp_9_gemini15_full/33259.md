chrysalises:/ˈkrɪsələsiːz/| n. |蛹(复数)|The plural form of chrysalis, referring to multiple pupae of insects, especially butterflies and moths.  Chrysalises are the stage in an insect's life cycle where it undergoes metamorphosis before emerging as an adult.| Example:  The chrysalises hung from the branches, swaying gently in the breeze.  蛹挂在树枝上，在微风中轻轻摇曳。|近义词：pupae|反义词：无
