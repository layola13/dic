jolthead:/ˈdʒɒlˌhed/ | n. | 笨蛋，傻瓜 | A jolthead is a stupid or foolish person.  The term is informal and often used in a humorous or mildly insulting way. | Example: "Don't be such a jolthead, it's obvious what to do!"  别那么傻，很明显该怎么做！ | 近义词：idiot, fool, dunce, moron | 反义词：genius, intellectual, mastermind |  词性：名词
