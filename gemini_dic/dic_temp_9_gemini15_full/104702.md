/kæt/ | n. | 猫 | A small domesticated carnivorous mammal with soft fur, a short snout, and retractible claws. It is widely kept as a pet.  | Example: My cat likes to sleep in sunbeams.  我的猫喜欢在阳光下睡觉。  |近义词：feline, kitty | 反义词：dog


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat sleeps all day. = 猫整天睡觉。
The cats chase mice. = 猫追老鼠。

一般过去时:
The cat sat on the mat. = 猫坐在垫子上。
The cats played in the garden. = 猫在花园里玩耍。

现在完成时:
The cat has slept for hours. = 猫睡了好几个小时了。
The cats have caught many mice. = 猫抓了很多老鼠。

过去完成时:
The cat had already eaten before I arrived. = 我到达之前猫已经吃过了。
The cats had been playing before the storm started. = 暴风雨开始之前猫一直在玩耍。

将来时:
The cat will sleep on the bed tonight. = 猫今晚将睡在床上。
The cats will hunt for mice tomorrow. = 猫明天将去捕猎老鼠。

虚拟语气:
If the cat were bigger, it would catch more mice. = 如果猫更大一些，它就能抓到更多老鼠。
I wish the cat would stop scratching the furniture. = 我希望猫能停止抓挠家具。

被动语态:
The cat was petted by the child. = 猫被孩子抚摸。
The mice were chased by the cats. = 老鼠被猫追赶。

疑问句:
Is the cat hungry? = 猫饿了吗？
Where are the cats? = 猫在哪里？

否定句:
The cat is not sleeping. = 猫没有睡觉。
The cats did not catch any mice. = 猫没有抓到任何老鼠。
