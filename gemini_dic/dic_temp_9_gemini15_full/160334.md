poorer:/ˈpʊərər/ | adj. | 更穷的，更贫困的 |  Describes someone or something that is less wealthy or less fortunate than others. It is the comparative form of "poor." |  The poorer families in the community received extra food assistance.  更贫困的家庭获得了额外的食物援助。|  impoverished, needy, destitute | wealthy, rich, affluent | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The poorer families rely on government aid. = 更贫穷的家庭依靠政府援助。
He feels poorer since losing his job. = 他自从失业后感觉更穷了。

一般过去时:
They were poorer last year than they are now. = 他们去年比现在更穷。
The harvest was poor, making the farmers poorer. = 收成不好，让农民们更穷了。

现在完成时:
They have become poorer due to the economic downturn. = 由于经济衰退，他们变得更穷了。
We have never felt poorer than we do now. = 我们从未像现在这样感觉穷困。


过去完成时:
They had been poorer before they received the inheritance. = 在他们继承遗产之前，他们更穷。
The country had become significantly poorer after the war. = 战争之后，这个国家变得非常贫穷。


将来时:
They will be poorer unless they find new jobs. = 除非他们找到新工作，否则他们会更穷。
The rising cost of living will make many people poorer. = 生活成本的上升会使许多人更穷。


虚拟语气:
If he had worked harder, he wouldn't be poorer now. = 如果他工作更努力，他现在就不会这么穷了。
I wish I weren't poorer so I could travel more. = 我希望我不那么穷，这样我就能多旅行了。


被动语态:
The poorer areas are often neglected by the government. = 贫困地区经常被政府忽视。(此句中poorer作定语，本身不被用作被动语态)
The community's poorer members were disproportionately affected. = 社区的贫困成员受到了不成比例的影响。(此句中poorer作定语，本身不被用作被动语态)


疑问句:
Are the poorer sections of society being adequately supported? = 社会上的贫困阶层是否得到了充分的支持？
Who are the poorer people in this city? = 这个城市里哪些人是更贫穷的人？


否定句:
The situation isn't poorer than we initially thought. = 情况并没有比我们最初想的更糟。
They are not poorer than their neighbours. = 他们并不比他们的邻居穷。
