magnetotherapy:/ˌmæɡnɪtoʊˈθɛrəpi/| n. |磁疗|The therapeutic application of magnetic fields.  Magnetotherapy uses magnetic fields to stimulate healing and reduce pain. It is believed to work by influencing the electrical currents within the body.  | Example:  The patient is undergoing magnetotherapy to alleviate back pain.  = 病人正在接受磁疗以缓解背痛。


|近义词:magnetic therapy, magnetic field therapy|反义词: (no direct antonym, but therapies that oppose magnetic effects could be considered indirect antonyms)|词性:名词
