placental:/pləˈsɛntəl/ | adj. |胎盘的；胎生的| Relating to or characteristic of a placenta.  A placenta is an organ that develops in the uterus during pregnancy. This organ connects the developing fetus to the uterine wall to allow nutrient uptake, thermo-regulation, waste elimination, and gas exchange via the mother's blood supply, but without allowing maternal and fetal blood mixing.  |例句：The placental barrier protects the fetus from many harmful substances. 胎盘屏障保护胎儿免受许多有害物质的侵害。

The placental mammals are characterized by the presence of a placenta during pregnancy. 胎盘哺乳动物的特点是在怀孕期间存在胎盘。|近义词：uterine (related to the uterus), fetal (related to the fetus)|反义词：aplacental (lacking a placenta)
