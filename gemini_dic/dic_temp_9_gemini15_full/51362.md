detergents:/dɪˈtɜːrdʒənts/| n. |洗涤剂，清洁剂|Substances used for washing, cleaning, and removing dirt and stains from surfaces.|Many detergents are formulated to be environmentally friendly.|近义词：cleansers, cleaning agents|反义词：dirt, grime
