finessing:/fɪˈnɛsɪŋ/| v. |巧妙处理；以技巧处理；使技巧化 |To handle (a situation, problem, etc.) skillfully and cleverly, often by using indirect or subtle methods.  It often implies a degree of manipulation or evasion. |例句：He finessed the negotiation, securing a better deal than expected. 他巧妙地进行了谈判，获得了比预期更好的交易。

例句：She finessed the awkward situation by changing the subject. 她巧妙地转移话题，化解了尴尬的局面。


近义词：managing, maneuvering, negotiating, manipulating, outsmarting |反义词：bungling, botching, mishandling


词源:源自法语“finesse”, 指技巧，精致。
