carry-ons:/ˈkæriˌɒnz/ | n. |随身携带的行李；(飞机)随身携带物品 | Items that you are allowed to take on board an aircraft or other form of transportation with you, as opposed to checked baggage.  |Example:  I only packed carry-ons for my weekend trip.  我周末旅行只带了随身行李。
                                                                                                                                   I made sure all my carry-ons were within the airline's size restrictions. 我确保我的所有随身行李都在航空公司的尺寸限制内。|近义词：hand luggage, personal items |反义词：checked baggage
