shelters:/ˈʃɛltərz/| n.| 庇护所；掩蔽处；栖身之所 | A shelter is a structure that provides protection from the elements or danger.  It can be a building, a vehicle, or even a natural formation.  |例句：The homeless shelter provides food and a safe place to sleep.  无家可归者收容所提供食物和安全的睡觉场所。

The hikers sought shelter from the sudden rainstorm.  徒步旅行者躲避突如其来的暴雨。


近义词：refuge, haven, asylum, sanctuary | 反义词：exposure, danger, risk | 词性:名词

一般现在时:
The animal shelter cares for abandoned pets. = 动物收容所照顾被遗弃的宠物。
This building shelters many families from the storm. = 这栋建筑物庇护了许多家庭免受风暴侵害。

一般过去时:
They sheltered in a cave during the earthquake. = 地震期间他们躲在山洞里。
The old church sheltered many refugees during the war. = 在战争期间，古老的教堂庇护了许多难民。

现在完成时:
The government has opened several emergency shelters. = 政府已经开设了几个紧急避难所。
We have sheltered from the rain under the bridge. = 我们在桥下躲雨。

过去完成时:
They had sheltered in the basement before the tornado hit. = 龙卷风来袭之前，他们已经躲在地下室里了。
The villagers had sheltered in their homes when the floodwaters rose. = 当洪水上涨时，村民们已经躲在家里了。

将来时:
We will shelter in the storm cellar if the tornado warning is issued. = 如果发出龙卷风警报，我们将躲在地下室里。
The city plans to build more shelters for the homeless next year. = 该市计划明年为无家可归者建造更多庇护所。

虚拟语气:
If we had sheltered earlier, we wouldn't be so wet. = 如果我们早点找个庇护所，就不会这么湿了。
I wish we had sheltered from the sun. = 我真希望我们能躲躲太阳。

被动语态:
The injured were sheltered in a nearby hospital. = 受伤的人被安置在附近的医院。
Many families were sheltered from the cold by the community. = 许多家庭得到社区的帮助，免受寒冷侵袭。

疑问句:
Where can we shelter from this heavy rain? = 我们在哪里可以躲避这场大雨？
Are there any shelters available for victims of the hurricane? = 有任何可供飓风受害者使用的庇护所吗？

否定句:
They did not shelter from the storm; they stayed outside. = 他们没有躲避风暴；他们待在外面。
We couldn't shelter from the hail; it was too intense. = 我们无法躲避冰雹；它太强烈了。
