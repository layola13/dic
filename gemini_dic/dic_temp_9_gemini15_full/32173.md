chia:/ˈtʃiːə/| n.|奇亚籽，芡欧鼠尾草|Chia seeds are small, oval-shaped seeds from the Salvia hispanica plant. They are a nutritional powerhouse, packed with fiber, protein, and omega-3 fatty acids.  They are often added to foods and drinks for their health benefits and unique texture.|例句：I added chia seeds to my yogurt for extra nutrition. 我在酸奶里加了奇亚籽以增加营养。|近义词：salvia seed (芡欧鼠尾草种子)|反义词：无
