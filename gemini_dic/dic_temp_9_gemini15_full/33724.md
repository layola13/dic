circler:/ˈsɜːrk(ə)lər/| n. v.| 圆圈者；画圆圈的人；环绕者 | A person or thing that circles or moves in a circle.  |例句：The birds circled the mountain before landing. 鸟儿在着陆前绕着山飞了一圈。|近义词：rounder, encircler |反义词：straightener
