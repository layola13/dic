misjoined:/mɪsˈdʒɔɪnd/| adj.|错误连接的，连接错误的|Improperly joined; joined incorrectly.  This usually refers to legal documents or pleadings where parts are incorrectly combined or linked.  |例句：The misjoined claims resulted in the dismissal of the entire lawsuit.  错误连接的索赔导致整个诉讼被驳回。 The appellate court reversed the lower court’s decision because of the misjoined parties. 上诉法院因当事人连接错误而推翻了下级法院的判决。|近义词：incorrectly connected, improperly combined, wrongly linked|反义词：correctly joined, properly connected
