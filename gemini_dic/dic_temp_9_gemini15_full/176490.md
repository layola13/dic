re-practiced:/ˌriːˈpræktɪst/| v.|重新练习，再练习|To practice something again, often after a period of time or with renewed focus.|例句：After the poor performance, the band re-practiced their setlist. 乐队演出失误后，重新练习了他们的歌单。  例句：She re-practiced the piano piece until she could play it flawlessly. 她反复练习钢琴曲，直到能完美演奏。|近义词：rehearse, review, go over|反义词：neglect, abandon

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-practice their lines every evening. = 他们每天晚上都重新练习台词。
We re-practice the dance routine before the competition. = 我们在比赛前重新练习舞蹈。

一般过去时:
The team re-practiced their strategy after their loss. = 球队在输球后重新练习了他们的策略。
She re-practiced the song for the audition. = 她为试镜重新练习了这首歌。

现在完成时:
He has re-practiced the difficult passage several times. = 他已经反复练习了好几次那段难懂的章节。
They have re-practiced the scene to improve their performance. = 他们已经重新排练了这个场景以提高他们的表演。

过去完成时:
They had re-practiced the play multiple times before the opening night. = 他们在首映夜之前已经多次排练了这部戏。
She had re-practiced her speech many times before the presentation. = 她在演讲之前已经多次练习了她的演讲。

将来时:
We will re-practice the choreography tomorrow. = 我们明天将重新练习舞蹈动作。
The orchestra will re-practice the symphony before the concert. = 管弦乐队将在音乐会前重新练习交响乐。


被动语态:
The song was re-practiced by the singer until it was perfect. = 这首歌被歌手反复练习，直到完美无瑕。
The routine was re-practiced by the dancers before the show. = 舞蹈演员在演出前重新练习了舞蹈动作。


疑问句:
Will you re-practice the music before the performance? = 你会在演出前重新练习音乐吗？
Have they re-practiced their lines for the play? = 他们已经为戏剧重新练习台词了吗？

否定句:
They did not re-practice the material before the test. = 他们在考试前没有重新练习材料。
She will not re-practice the piano piece today. = 她今天不会重新练习这首钢琴曲。
