foxes:/ˈfɒksɪz/ /ˈfɑːksɪz/| n.|狐狸（复数）|The plural form of "fox," referring to multiple foxes. Foxes are cunning and intelligent animals known for their sly behavior and adaptability.|例句：We saw several foxes near the woods. 我们在树林附近看到几只狐狸。|例句：The foxes have been raiding the hen house. 狐狸一直在偷鸡。|近义词：vipers, wolves|反义词:None specifically, but could be contrasted with prey animals like rabbits.
