rejudging:/ˌriːˈdʒʌdʒɪŋ/| v.|重新评判，再审理|To judge again; to reconsider a judgment.|例句：The court decided to rejudge the case after new evidence emerged. 法院决定在出现新的证据后重新审理此案。

例句：The athlete's performance was rejudged, leading to a change in the rankings. 运动员的表现被重新评判，导致排名发生变化。


近义词：reassess, reconsider, review|反义词：accept, confirm
