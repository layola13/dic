foldaway: /ˈfoʊldəweɪ/ | adj. | 可折叠的，可收合的 |  Describes something that can be folded and stored away to save space.  It implies a design feature allowing for easy folding and compact storage. |  例句：We bought a foldaway table for the small apartment. 我们为小公寓买了一张折叠桌。


例句：The foldaway chairs were easy to store after the party. 派对结束后，折叠椅很容易收起来。


近义词：collapsible, folding, retractable | 反义词：permanent, fixed, stationary | 词性：形容词
