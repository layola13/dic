embarrasses: /ɪmˈbærəsɪz/ | v. | 使尴尬；使窘迫 | To cause (someone) to feel awkward, ashamed, or uneasy.  | 例句：His clumsy behavior embarrasses him. 他的笨拙行为让他很尴尬。

例句：Her public speaking embarrasses her.  她的公开演讲让她感到尴尬。


近义词：humiliate, shame, mortify, discomfit | 反义词：embolden, encourage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His jokes embarrass me. = 他的笑话让我尴尬。
The situation embarrasses everyone involved. = 这种情况让所有参与者都感到尴尬。


一般过去时:
His mistake embarrassed him greatly. = 他的错误让他非常尴尬。
The news embarrassed the whole family. = 这个消息让全家人都感到难堪。


现在完成时:
That incident has embarrassed me for years. = 那件事多年来一直让我感到尴尬。
This situation has embarrassed the company considerably. = 这种情况严重损害了公司的声誉。


过去完成时:
I had embarrassed myself before I even started speaking. = 我还没开始说话就让自己感到尴尬了。
He had embarrassed her several times before this incident. = 在这次事件之前，他已经让她尴尬了好几次了。


将来时:
This public announcement will embarrass him. = 这则公开声明将会让他难堪。
The scandal will embarrass the government. = 这场丑闻将使政府难堪。


虚拟语气:
I wish I hadn't said that; it would have embarrassed everyone. = 我真希望我没说那些话；那样就不会让大家尴尬了。
If I had known, I wouldn't have done anything that would embarrass her. = 如果我知道的话，我不会做任何让她尴尬的事情。


被动语态:
He was embarrassed by his own actions. = 他为自己所做的感到尴尬。
The politician was embarrassed by the revelations. = 这位政治家因被揭露的真相而感到难堪。


疑问句:
Does this situation embarrass you? = 这种情况让你尴尬吗？
What embarrasses you the most about this? = 关于这件事，什么让你最尴尬？


否定句:
His actions didn't embarrass him at all. = 他的行为一点也没有让他感到尴尬。
The situation didn't embarrass her; she handled it well. = 这种情况并没有让她尴尬；她处理得很好。


PHRASE->
embarrass oneself = 自讨没趣
feel embarrassed = 感到尴尬
be embarrassed to do sth = 不好意思做某事
deeply embarrassed = 深感尴尬
to embarrass someone = 使某人尴尬
