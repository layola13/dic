jackrabbit:/ˈdʒækˌræbɪt/| n.|杰克兔，草原兔|A jackrabbit is a large North American hare, known for its long ears and powerful hind legs, enabling it to run at high speeds.  They are typically found in arid and semi-arid regions.|Example: The jackrabbit darted across the desert road, its long ears twitching.|近义词：hare, cottontail (less accurate, as jackrabbits are hares, not rabbits)|反义词：无
