kitting:/ˈkɪtɪŋ/| n. v.|成套装备；成套配制；装备；|The act of assembling or preparing a kit.  It can refer to the process of putting together components to form a complete unit, or to the preparation of individual kits for distribution.|例句：The factory is responsible for the kitting of the product before shipping.  工厂负责在发货前组装产品。 The company provides kitting services for various industries. 这家公司为各个行业提供成套配制服务。|近义词：assembling, packaging, preparing|反义词：disassembling, unpacking
