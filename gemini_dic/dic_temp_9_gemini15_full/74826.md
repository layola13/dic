frenetic: /frəˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. | 狂热的；极度兴奋的；疯狂的 | Extremely energetic in a wild and uncontrolled way.  |  例句：The frenetic pace of modern life can be overwhelming. 现代生活的快节奏令人难以承受。

例句：He worked with frenetic energy to finish the project before the deadline. 他以狂热的精力在截止日期前完成项目。


近义词：frantic, frantic, hectic, feverish, agitated | 反义词：calm, tranquil, peaceful, relaxed

词源:
