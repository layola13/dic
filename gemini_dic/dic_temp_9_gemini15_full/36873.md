coloration:/ˌkʌləˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 颜色；着色；配色 | The arrangement of colors in an animal or plant.  It can refer to the overall pattern of colors or the specific colors themselves.  Coloration serves various functions, including camouflage, warning signals, and mate attraction. | The chameleon's coloration changed to match the leaves.  变色龙的颜色改变以匹配树叶。

The vibrant coloration of the parrot attracted many onlookers.  鹦鹉鲜艳的颜色吸引了许多旁观者。| pigmentation, coloring, hue | drabness, monochromaticity | 名词
