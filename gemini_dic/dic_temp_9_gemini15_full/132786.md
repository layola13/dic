nonduplicating: /ˌnɒnˈdʒuːplɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不重复的，唯一的 | Not repeating or containing repetitions.  This adjective describes something that avoids duplication or redundancy.  It emphasizes the uniqueness or distinctness of each element within a set or sequence. |


例句：

The algorithm ensures a nonduplicating sequence of numbers. = 该算法确保数字序列不重复。

The database system features a nonduplicating key to prevent data conflicts. = 数据库系统具有唯一键以防止数据冲突。


近义词：unique, distinct, original, unrepeated

反义词：duplicated, repetitive, redundant


词性：形容词
