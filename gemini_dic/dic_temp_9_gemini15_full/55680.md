dittoed:/ˈdɪtoʊd/| adj. v.| 同样的；重复的；一模一样的 |  Used to indicate that something is the same as something previously mentioned.  It can function as an adjective or a verb. As an adjective, it describes something that is identical; as a verb, it means to repeat or copy something. |例句：The second page was dittoed from the first. = 第二页与第一页完全相同。  His speech was a dittoed version of last year's. = 他的演讲是去年演讲的翻版。|近义词：identical, same, duplicated, copied |反义词：different, unique, original
