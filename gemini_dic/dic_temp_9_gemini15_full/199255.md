silty:/ˈsɪlti/| adj.|泥泞的；含泥沙的|Containing or consisting of silt.  Silt is a sedimentary material consisting of fine particles of rock and mineral fragments. Silty soil is often fertile but can be difficult to work with because it can be both heavy and easily compacted.|例句：The riverbed was silty and muddy. 河床泥泞且充满泥沙。

The silty soil made it difficult to plant the seeds.  泥泞的土壤使得播种变得困难。|近义词：muddy, sandy, loamy|反义词：gravelly, sandy (in the context of soil texture)
