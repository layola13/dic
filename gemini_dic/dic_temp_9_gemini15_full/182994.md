replenished:/rɪˈplɛnɪʃt/| v. |补充；重新装满；补充；|to fill something up again:  The replenished water supply ensured the town's survival. 补充的水供应确保了城镇的生存。|近义词：refill, restock, replenish, refill|反义词：deplete, exhaust, empty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store replenishes its stock daily. = 商店每天补充库存。
She replenishes her water bottle after every workout. = 她每次锻炼后都会补充水瓶里的水。

一般过去时:
He replenished the sugar bowl after breakfast. = 早餐后他补充了糖罐里的糖。
The team replenished their energy with a quick snack. = 团队用快速的零食补充了能量。

现在完成时:
They have replenished the emergency fund. = 他们已经补充了应急基金。
We have replenished our supplies for the camping trip. = 我们已经补充了野营旅行的物资。

过去完成时:
She had replenished the coffee pot before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经补充了咖啡壶里的咖啡。
They had replenished their ammunition before the final assault. = 在最后一次攻击之前，他们已经补充了弹药。

将来时:
We will replenish the flowerbeds next spring. = 我们将在明年春天补充花坛里的花。
The company will replenish its inventory before the holiday season. = 公司将在节假日之前补充库存。

虚拟语气:
If we replenished our supplies now, we wouldn't run out later. = 如果我们现在补充物资，我们以后就不会用完了。
I wish I had replenished the oil before the engine seized. = 我真希望发动机卡住之前我已经补充了机油。

被动语态:
The water tank was replenished overnight. = 水箱在夜间被补充满了。
The shelves were replenished with new products. = 货架上补充了新的产品。

疑问句:
Has the water tank been replenished? = 水箱补充了吗？
Will the supplies be replenished in time? = 物资会及时补充吗？

否定句:
They did not replenish the stock before closing. = 他们关门前没有补充库存。
The farmer didn't replenish the hay bales in the barn. = 农民没有补充谷仓里的干草捆。
