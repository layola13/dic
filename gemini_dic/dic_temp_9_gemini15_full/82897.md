gum:/ɡʌm/| n. v.|树胶；口香糖；牙龈；粘合剂|The word "gum" has multiple meanings. As a noun, it can refer to the sticky substance produced by certain plants (e.g., chewing gum, tree sap), the fleshy tissue surrounding the teeth (gums), or a sticky substance used as an adhesive. As a verb, it means to apply gum or a sticky substance to something.  |例句：The children were chewing gum.孩子们正在嚼口香糖。 The carpenter used gum to stick the pieces of wood together. 木匠用胶水把木头粘在一起。|近义词：resin, adhesive, mastic|反义词：None specifically, depending on context.
