pseudosphere:/ˈsjuːdoʊsfɪr/|/ˈsjuːdəʊsfɪə/| n. |伪球面，假球面 |A pseudosphere is a surface with constant negative Gaussian curvature. It's a surface of revolution generated by a tractrix curve.  Unlike a sphere, which curves inward, a pseudosphere curves outward infinitely.  It's an important concept in non-Euclidean geometry. |The pseudosphere is a fascinating example of a non-Euclidean surface. |近义词：tractroid (less common)|反义词：sphere
