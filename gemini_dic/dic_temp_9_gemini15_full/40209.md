contesters:/kənˈtɛstərz/ | n. | 参与者；抗议者；竞争者 | People who participate in a contest, competition, or challenge; those who express strong disapproval or opposition.  | Example: The contesters in the spelling bee were all highly skilled. 拼字比赛的参赛者都很熟练。The environmental contesters organized a protest march.  环保抗议者组织了一场抗议游行。 |近义词：competitors, opponents, protesters, objectors |反义词：supporters,approvers
