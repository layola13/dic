scripturally:/ˈskrɪptʃərəli/| adv.|根据圣经地；从圣经角度来看 |Based on or in accordance with the scriptures or the Bible.  It implies an interpretation or understanding derived from religious texts.|例句：Scripturally, the Sabbath is observed on Saturday.  = 根据圣经，安息日是在星期六遵守。

例句：He argued scripturally against the proposed change. = 他从圣经角度论证反对拟议的变更。


近义词：biblically, religiously, theologically


反义词：unscripturally, secularly, worldly


词性：副词
