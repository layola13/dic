juddered:/ˈdʒʌdəd/| v.|震动；抖动；剧烈摇晃|To shake or vibrate violently and irregularly.|The old car juddered as it climbed the hill.  这辆旧车爬坡时剧烈震动。 The ground juddered beneath our feet. 地面在我们脚下震动。|近义词：shake, vibrate, tremble, rattle|反义词：glide, flow smoothly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine judders constantly. = 发动机持续震动。
The train judders as it starts. = 火车启动时震动。


一般过去时:
The building juddered during the earthquake. = 地震时建筑物剧烈摇晃。
The car juddered to a halt. = 车子猛地停了下来。


现在完成时:
The old machine has juddered its way through many years of service. = 这台旧机器已经运转多年。
