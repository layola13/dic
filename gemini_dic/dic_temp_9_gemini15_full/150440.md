pantagraph:/ˈpæntəɡræf/| n. |万花筒；等比例复制器|A pantagraph is a mechanical device used to reproduce a drawing or design at a different scale.  It consists of a system of hinged rods that allow for proportional enlargement or reduction of the original image.  The user traces the original drawing with a stylus, and the pantagraph simultaneously replicates the motion on a different surface at a different scale.|例句：The architect used a pantagraph to enlarge the blueprint. 建筑师用万花筒放大蓝图。

The engineer employed a pantagraph to reduce the scale of the circuit diagram.工程师用等比例复制器缩小电路图的比例。|近义词：copying machine, reproducer|反义词：original
