marvelously:/ˈmɑːrvələsli/| adj. |极好地；精彩地；奇妙地 |Admirably; wonderfully; in a marvelous manner.  The word expresses a high degree of admiration or wonder.  It is often used to describe something that is exceptionally good, impressive, or delightful. |例句：The magician performed marvelously, leaving the audience spellbound. 魔术师表演得精彩绝伦，观众看得入迷。

The concert was marvelously orchestrated and executed. 音乐会编排和演奏都非常出色。|近义词：wonderfully, splendidly, amazingly, exquisitely, superbly |反义词：poorly, terribly, awfully, badly
