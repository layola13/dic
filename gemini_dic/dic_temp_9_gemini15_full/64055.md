epitomised:/ɪˈpɪtəmʌɪzd/| v.| 成为…的典型；完美地体现；概括；总结 |To be a perfect example of something; to summarize or represent the essential qualities of something.|His work epitomised the spirit of the age. 他的作品完美地体现了那个时代的精神。The building epitomises modern architecture. 这座建筑是现代建筑的典范。|近义词：embody, exemplify, represent, encapsulate|反义词：misrepresent, contradict


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The painting epitomises the artist's style. = 这幅画是这位艺术家风格的典范。
His behaviour epitomises arrogance. = 他的行为是傲慢的典范。


一般过去时:
Her performance epitomised grace and elegance. = 她的表演完美地体现了优雅和高贵。
The novel epitomised the anxieties of the time. = 这部小说概括了那个时代的焦虑。


现在完成时:
His career has epitomised dedication and hard work. = 他的职业生涯体现了奉献和努力。
Their actions have epitomised selfless service. = 他们的行动体现了无私的服务。


过去完成时:
The previous administration's policies had epitomised inefficiency. = 上届政府的政策体现了低效。
The earlier experiments had epitomised the challenges faced. = 早期的实验体现了所面临的挑战。


将来时:
This project will epitomise sustainable development. = 这个项目将成为可持续发展的典范。
Future generations will see this as epitomising a new era. = 后代将把这看作是新时代的象征。


虚拟语气:
If his work epitomised perfection, it would be instantly recognized. = 如果他的作品完美无瑕，它将立即被认可。
I wish his leadership had epitomised fairness. = 我希望他的领导能体现公平。


被动语态:
The event was epitomised by a sense of unity. = 这个事件的特点是团结一致。
The movement is epitomised by its commitment to peace. = 这个运动的特点是致力于和平。


疑问句:
Does this design epitomise modern aesthetics? = 这个设计体现了现代美学吗？
Does his behaviour epitomise professionalism? = 他的行为体现了专业精神吗？


否定句:
His actions did not epitomise courage. = 他的行为没有体现勇气。
The film did not epitomise the director's best work. = 这部电影并非导演最好的作品的代表。


PHRASE->
epitomise something perfectly = 完美地体现某事物
epitomise a particular style = 体现某种特定的风格
epitomise the spirit of = 体现…的精神
epitomise the best of = 体现…的精华
epitomise the problems of = 体现…的问题
