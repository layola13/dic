ethology:/iˈθɒlədʒi/|/ˈɛθɑːlədʒi/| n. |动物行为学|The scientific and objective study of animal behavior, focusing on the evolutionary and ecological basis of behavior.  It observes and analyzes animal behavior in their natural environment, often using observational methods and experimental manipulations. |Ethologists study the adaptive significance of behavior, such as how it helps animals survive and reproduce.  For example, they might investigate the mating rituals of birds or the foraging strategies of primates. |ecology, behavioral biology|psychology, sociology|
例句：
The ethology of chimpanzees reveals a complex social structure.  = 黑猩猩的行为学揭示了一个复杂的社会结构。

Ethologists have observed fascinating communication patterns in dolphin pods. = 动物行为学家观察到海豚群中引人入胜的交流模式。


近义词:behavioral biology, animal behavior
反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑与研究动物心理的学科相对比)
