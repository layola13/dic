preknowledge:/ˈpriːnɒlɪdʒ/|/ˈpriːnɑːlɪdʒ/| n. |预先知识；先验知识|Prior knowledge or information about something.  It often refers to knowledge gained before a specific event or learning experience.|例句：The preknowledge of the test questions gave him an unfair advantage.  预先知道考试题给了他一个不公平的优势。

例句：His preknowledge of the subject made the lecture much easier to understand. 他对这个主题的预先了解使得讲座更容易理解。


近义词：foreknowledge, prior knowledge, advance knowledge

反义词：ignorance, unawareness

词性：名词
