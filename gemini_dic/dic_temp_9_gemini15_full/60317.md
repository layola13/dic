ego-tripping:/ˈiːɡoʊˌtrɪpɪŋ/| n. |自负；自我陶醉；自我膨胀|Engaging in behavior intended to boost one's self-esteem or sense of importance, often to an excessive or boastful degree.  It often involves showing off or bragging about one's accomplishments or possessions. |例句：His constant ego-tripping is tiresome. 他不断的自吹自擂令人厌烦。

例句：She's been on an ego-tripping spree lately, constantly talking about her new job. 她最近一直在自我膨胀，不断谈论她的新工作。


近义词：narcissism, self-aggrandizement, boasting, showing off, grandstanding

反义词：humility, modesty, self-deprecation
