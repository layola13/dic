propagandizers:/ˌprɒpəˈɡændɪzərz/ | n. |宣传者，鼓吹者 |People who spread propaganda.  They disseminate information, often biased or misleading, to promote a particular political cause or point of view. |Example: The government's propagandizers worked tirelessly to shape public opinion. 政府的宣传者们不知疲倦地努力塑造公众舆论。|近义词: propagandists, agitators, promoters |反义词: truth-tellers, fact-checkers
