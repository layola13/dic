corsned:/kɔːrsned/| n. |决斗面包，决斗圣餐|A piece of consecrated bread used in a trial by ordeal in medieval times.  The accused person had to swallow the bread; if they were innocent, it was believed they would be able to swallow it easily; if guilty, it would supposedly stick in their throat.|Example: The accused man was forced to undergo the corsned ordeal to prove his innocence.  被控告的男子被迫接受决斗面包的考验来证明他的清白。|近义词：ordeal (考验)|反义词：N/A|名词
