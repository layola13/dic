imagesetters: /ˈɪmɪdʒˌsɛtərz/ | n. |图像设置器，图像排版系统 | A system or software used to prepare images for printing or display, often involving high-resolution image processing and layout.  It refers to the technology and processes involved in preparing digital images for output, typically for professional printing. | Example:  The imagesetters produced high-quality prints for the brochures. 图像排版系统为宣传册制作了高质量的印刷品。 |近义词: image processor, typesetter, prepress system |反义词:  None directly applicable.
