inventress:/ɪnˈvɛntɹəs/| n. |女发明家|A woman who invents things; a female inventor.|The inventress of the new medical device was awarded a prestigious prize.  这位新型医疗设备的女发明家获得了享有盛誉的奖项。| inventor | non-inventor | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She invents new gadgets every year. = 她每年都发明新的玩意儿。
The company invents innovative solutions. = 这家公司发明创新的解决方案。


一般过去时:
Marie Curie invented radium. = 居里夫人发明了镭。
Edison invented the light bulb. = 爱迪生发明了电灯泡。


现在完成时:
She has invented several groundbreaking technologies. = 她已经发明了几项突破性的技术。
They have invented a new type of battery. = 他们发明了一种新型电池。


过去完成时:
Before the war, they had invented a new weapon. = 战争爆发前，他们已经发明了一种新武器。
By the time she died, she had invented many things. = 到她去世时，她已经发明了许多东西。


将来时:
Scientists will invent new ways to cure diseases. = 科学家们将发明治疗疾病的新方法。
She will invent a new kind of robot. = 她将发明一种新型机器人。


虚拟语气:
If she had invented this earlier, things would be different. = 如果她早些发明这个，情况就会有所不同。
I wish I had invented that technology. = 我真希望我发明了那项技术。


被动语态:
The device was invented by a young scientist. = 该装置是由一位年轻的科学家发明的。
Many useful things have been invented throughout history. = 历史上发明了许多有用的东西。


疑问句:
Who invented the telephone? = 电话是谁发明的？
What did she invent? = 她发明了什么？


否定句:
He didn't invent anything significant. = 他没有发明任何重要东西。
They haven't invented a cure for cancer yet. = 他们还没有发明出治疗癌症的方法。
