misdescribe:/ˌmɪsdɪˈskraɪb/| vt.|错误描述，误述|To give a wrong or inaccurate description of something or someone.|例句：He misdescribed the situation to the police. 他向警方错误描述了情况。|近义词：misrepresent, misinterpret, distort|反义词：accurately describe, correctly portray
