/ˈsleɪɪŋz/ | n. | 杀戮；屠杀 | The plural form of "slaying," referring to multiple acts of killing.  It often implies violence and often a large number of deaths. | Example: The slayings shocked the community.  这起屠杀震惊了整个社区。 The historical slayings were a dark chapter in the nation's history. 历史上这些杀戮是国家历史上黑暗的一章。 | 近义词：massacres, killings, murders | 反义词：rescues, salvations
