multivalence:/ˌmʌltiˈveɪləns/| n. |多价态；多义性；多重含义|The term refers to the capacity of a word, symbol, concept, or other signifying element to have multiple meanings or interpretations.  It emphasizes the inherent ambiguity and potential for varied understanding rather than simply listing multiple meanings.  This contrasts with polysemy, which focuses on a word having related meanings that stem from a single root.  Multivalence highlights the potential for diverse and even conflicting interpretations. |The multivalence of the poem allowed for diverse critical readings.  这首诗的多义性允许多种批判性解读。The multivalence of the symbol made it suitable for different interpretations across cultures.  这个符号的多义性使其适合跨文化的多样化解读。|polysemy, ambiguity, pluralism|univocality, single meaning|名词
