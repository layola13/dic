captcha:/ˈkæptʃə/| n. |验证码|Completely Automated Public Turing test to tell Computers and Humans Apart.  A CAPTCHA is a type of challenge-response test used in computing to determine whether or not a user is human.  It works by presenting the user with a test that is easy for a human to solve but difficult for a computer program to solve.  This is typically done to prevent automated programs, such as bots, from abusing online services.| Example:  I had to solve a CAPTCHA to prove I wasn't a robot. = 我必须解一个验证码来证明我不是机器人。 I failed the CAPTCHA three times before accessing the website. = 我在访问网站之前三次未能通过验证码。|近义词：verification test, security check |反义词：None
