neuronic: /nʊˈrɒnɪk/ | adj. | 神经元的；神经的 | Relating to neurons or the nervous system.  The word is a relatively recent coinage, formed from "neuron" + "-ic". It describes things related to the function or structure of neurons. |例句：Neuronic pathways transmit signals throughout the body. 神经通路在全身传递信号。

例句：The study focused on neuronic activity in the brain during sleep.  该研究侧重于睡眠期间大脑中的神经元活动。


近义词：neural, nerve-related


反义词：None directly opposite, but words implying absence of neural activity could be considered indirect antonyms, such as "inert," "inactive," or "unresponsive" in a neurological context.


词性：形容词
