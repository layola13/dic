readvertisements:/ˌriːˈædvərtaɪzmənts/| n. |再次广告宣传；重新刊登广告|Repeated advertisements; the act of advertising something again.|The company issued readvertisements for the job opening after receiving insufficient applications.  公司在收到申请不足后，再次发布了招聘广告。|近义词：re-advertisements, repeated advertisements|反义词：withdrawal of advertisements


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company readvertises its products regularly. = 公司定期重新刊登其产品广告。
They readvertise the position every week. = 他们每周都重新刊登职位广告。


一般过去时:
They readvertised the concert after it was cancelled. = 音乐会取消后，他们重新刊登了广告。
The shop readvertised the sale items. = 商店重新刊登了促销商品的广告。


现在完成时:
The company has readvertised the job opening three times. = 公司已经三次重新刊登了该职位空缺的广告。
We have readvertised the property since it didn't sell. =  由于房产没有卖出，我们已经重新刊登了广告。


过去完成时:
They had readvertised the position before finding a suitable candidate. = 他们在找到合适的候选人之前已经重新刊登了职位广告。
The store had readvertised the sale before the weekend. = 商店在周末之前已经重新刊登了促销广告。


将来时:
They will readvertise the product next month. = 他们下个月将重新刊登该产品的广告。
The company will readvertise the position if they don't find a suitable candidate. = 如果他们找不到合适的候选人，公司将重新刊登职位广告。


虚拟语气:
If they readvertised the product, they might sell more. = 如果他们重新刊登产品广告，可能会卖出更多。
If I had readvertised my car, I might have sold it sooner. = 如果我重新刊登我的汽车广告，我可能会早点卖掉它。


被动语态:

否定句:
