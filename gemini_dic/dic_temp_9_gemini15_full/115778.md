/ˌmæriˈnɔːrəmʌ/| n. |海洋全景图，海洋景观模型 |A marinorama is a large-scale model or diorama depicting a marine environment, often showcasing diverse marine life and underwater landscapes.  It aims to provide a realistic and immersive experience for viewers, similar to a large aquarium but with a more controlled and curated presentation.| Example: The museum's new marinorama is a breathtaking display of coral reefs and their inhabitants.  博物馆新的海洋全景图令人叹为观止地展示了珊瑚礁及其居民。|近义词：marine diorama, underwater display, aquatic exhibit |反义词：terrestrial diorama, landscape model (non-aquatic)|名词
