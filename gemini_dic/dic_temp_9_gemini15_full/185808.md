revisit:/ˌriːˈvɪzɪt/| vt.|重新访问；重新审视；重温|To visit or go back to a place or situation again.  To reconsider or examine something again.|例句：We decided to revisit the museum. 我们决定再次参观博物馆。

例句：Let's revisit the plan and see if we can make improvements. 让我们重新审视一下计划，看看是否可以改进。

近义词：reexamine, review, reconsider, revisit, return to

反义词：abandon, forsake, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I revisit my childhood home every summer. = 我每年夏天都会重访我的童年故居。
She revisits her notes before the exam. = 她考试前会复习笔记。


一般过去时:
He revisited his decision after careful consideration. = 他经过仔细考虑后重新审视了他的决定。
They revisited the scene of the accident. = 他们重返事故现场。


现在完成时:
I have revisited that old cafe several times. = 我已经几次重访过那家老咖啡馆。
She has revisited her artistic style in recent years. = 她近年来重新审视了自己的艺术风格。


过去完成时:
After he had revisited his old school, he felt nostalgic. = 在他重返母校之后，他感到怀旧。
They had revisited their strategy before launching the product. = 他们在推出产品之前重新审视了他们的策略。


将来时:
We will revisit this topic later in the meeting. = 我们稍后将在会议上重新讨论这个话题。
She will revisit her family in the country next month. = 她下个月将回乡探望家人。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would revisit my options. = 如果我是你，我会重新考虑我的选择。
I wish I could revisit my youth. = 我希望我能重温我的青春。


被动语态:
The decision was revisited by the committee. = 委员会重新审议了这项决定。
The old house was revisited by its former owner. = 这座老房子被它的前主人重访了。


疑问句:
Will you revisit your proposal? = 你会重新考虑你的提议吗？
Have you ever revisited your hometown? = 你曾经回过家乡吗？


否定句:
He did not revisit the issue. = 他没有重新讨论这个问题。
They will not revisit their agreement. = 他们不会重新考虑他们的协议。


PHRASE->
revisit a decision = 重新考虑一个决定
revisit a theme = 重新探讨一个主题
revisit a memory = 回忆往事
revisit a past experience = 回顾过去的经历
revisit a project = 重新审视一个项目
