英文解析:The Missouri River is a major North American river, the longest river system in North America, flowing 2,341 miles (3,767 km) from its headwaters in the Rocky Mountains of Montana through the Great Plains to its confluence with the Mississippi River.  It is known for its significant role in the history of westward expansion in the United States, serving as a major transportation route and influencing the development of numerous settlements along its banks.
