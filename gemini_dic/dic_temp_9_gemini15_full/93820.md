immigrates:/ˈɪmɪɡreɪts/| v.| 移民；移居 |To come to live permanently in a foreign country.|例句：Many people immigrate to the United States in search of better opportunities.  许多人为了寻找更好的机会而移民到美国。|例句：After graduating from university, she immigrated to Canada. 大学毕业后，她移民到了加拿大。|近义词：migrate, emigrate |反义词：emigrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He immigrates to Australia every year. 他每年都移民到澳大利亚。(This example is unusual as immigration is typically a one-time event, but it can refer to repeated actions in certain contexts.)
They immigrate to the US for better education. 他们为了更好的教育而移民到美国。


一般过去时:
My grandparents immigrated to this country in 1950. 我的祖父母在1950年移民到这个国家。
She immigrated to Canada after the war. 战争结束后，她移民到了加拿大。


现在完成时:
Millions have immigrated to the city since the industrial revolution. 自工业革命以来，已有数百万人移民到这座城市。
He has immigrated to several countries throughout his life. 他一生中移民到过好几个国家。


过去完成时:
They had immigrated to the US before the outbreak of the war. 战争爆发前，他们已经移民到美国了。
She had immigrated to France before she met her husband.  她在遇到丈夫之前已经移民到法国了。


将来时:
We will immigrate to New Zealand next year. 我们明年将移民到新西兰。
They plan to immigrate to Spain after they retire. 他们计划退休后移民到西班牙。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would immigrate to Italy. 如果我有钱的话，我会移民到意大利。
I wish I could immigrate to a warmer climate. 我希望我能移民到气候更温暖的地方。


被动语态:
Many refugees are being immigrated to different countries. 许多难民正在被安置到不同的国家。(Note:  The passive voice is less common and often sounds unnatural with "immigrate."  It's better to rephrase using different verbs.)
Thousands were immigrated to the country last year. (Awkward phrasing; better options exist)


疑问句:
Will they immigrate to the UK? 他们会移民到英国吗？
Where did your family immigrate from? 你的家人是从哪里移民过来的？


否定句:
They did not immigrate to Australia. 他们没有移民到澳大利亚。
He chose not to immigrate to the US. 他选择不移民到美国。
