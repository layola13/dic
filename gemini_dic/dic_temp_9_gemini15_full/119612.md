midbrains:/ˈmɪdbreɪnz/ | n. | 中脑 | The midbrain is the part of the brainstem that connects the forebrain to the hindbrain.  It plays a crucial role in vision, hearing, motor control, sleep/wake cycles, arousal (alertness), and temperature regulation. | Example:  Damage to the midbrain can result in a variety of neurological problems. 中脑损伤会导致各种神经系统问题。  The midbrain is a vital part of the brain's complex network. 中脑是大脑复杂网络的重要组成部分。|近义词: mesencephalon |反义词:  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with forebrain or hindbrain)|名词
