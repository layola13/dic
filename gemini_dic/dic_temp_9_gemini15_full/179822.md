reexercising:/ˌriːˈɛksərˌsaɪzɪŋ/| v. |重新锻炼；再次锻炼 |The act of exercising again after a period of inactivity or injury.  It emphasizes the repetition of exercise after a break. |例句：After recovering from his injury, he started reexercising his leg muscles.  在他从伤病中恢复后，他开始重新锻炼腿部肌肉。


例句：She's been reexercising her vocal cords to prepare for the concert. 她一直在重新练习她的声带，为音乐会做准备。


近义词：rehabilitating, retraining, reconditioning


反义词：resting, ceasing exercise


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reexercises his arm every day. 他每天都重新锻炼他的手臂。
They reexercise regularly to maintain fitness. 他们定期重新锻炼以保持健康。


一般过去时:
She reexercised after a long illness. 她大病一场后重新锻炼身体。
He reexercised his injured knee cautiously. 他谨慎地重新锻炼他受伤的膝盖。


现在完成时:
I have reexercised since my surgery. 我手术后一直在重新锻炼。
They have reexercised consistently for months. 他们已经坚持了几个月的重新锻炼。


过去完成时:
He had reexercised for weeks before the marathon. 他在马拉松赛前已经重新锻炼了好几个星期。
She had reexercised her muscles before attempting the lift. 在尝试举重之前，她已经重新锻炼了肌肉。


将来时:
I will reexercise tomorrow after a day of rest. 我将在休息一天后明天重新锻炼。
We will reexercise more intensely next week. 我们下周将进行更剧烈的重新锻炼。


虚拟语气:
If I weren't injured, I would be reexercising. 如果我没有受伤，我就会重新锻炼。
I wish I could reexercise without pain. 我希望我可以不痛地重新锻炼。


被动语态:
The injured muscle is being reexercised slowly and carefully. 受伤的肌肉正在缓慢而谨慎地重新锻炼。
His rehabilitation program involves his leg being reexercised daily. 他的康复计划包括每天重新锻炼他的腿。


疑问句:
