corduroying: /ˌkɔːrdʒəˈrɔɪɪŋ/ | n. | 灯芯绒织造；灯芯绒制作 | The process of making corduroy fabric.  It involves weaving  ribs of pile into the fabric. |例句：The mill specializes in corduroying, producing a wide range of textures and colors. 这家工厂专门从事灯芯绒织造，生产各种纹理和颜色的灯芯绒。|近义词：corduroy making, ribbed fabric production |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with processes making smooth fabrics like satin or silk.
