horizon:/həˈraɪzən/| n. |地平线，视野，眼界 |The line at which the earth's surface and the sky appear to meet.  It represents the limit of one's vision or perception.  |例句：The sun dipped below the horizon.  太阳落到了地平线下。
The horizon of possibilities expanded before her. 她面前展现出无限的可能性。|近义词：skyline, view, prospect |反义词：nadir, zenith

The horizon stretches far on a clear day. = 晴朗的日子里，地平线延伸得很远。
The horizon often appears hazy in the distance. = 远处的视野通常显得朦胧。
