continuants:/kənˈtɪnjuənts/| n. |持续物；持续音|Sounds produced with continuous airflow from the lungs, such as /f/, /s/, /z/, /v/, /l/, /r/, /w/, and /j/.  These sounds can be sustained without interruption.  They are contrasted with stops, which involve a complete closure of the vocal tract.|The sibilant continuants /s/ and /z/ are often difficult for second language learners to master.|近义词：sustained sounds|反义词：stops (in phonetics)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wind continues to blow. = 风继续吹着。
He maintains his innocence. = 他坚持自己的清白。


一般过去时:
The rain continued throughout the night. = 雨整夜不停地下着。
She sustained a serious injury in the accident. = 她在事故中受了重伤。


现在完成时:
The project has continued for several years. = 这个项目已经持续了好几年了。
They have maintained their position on the issue. = 他们在这个问题上一直保持自己的立场。


过去完成时:
The game had continued even after it got dark. = 即使天黑了，比赛也还在继续。
He had sustained numerous injuries before retiring. = 他在退休前受过多次伤。


将来时:
The meeting will continue tomorrow. = 会议明天继续进行。
She will maintain her composure during the interview. = 她在面试中会保持镇静。


虚拟语气:
If the weather continued like this, we would be stuck. = 如果天气继续这样，我们就困住了。
If he had maintained his focus, he would have succeeded. = 如果他保持专注，他就会成功。


被动语态:
The tradition is continued by younger generations. = 这个传统由年轻一代传承下来。
The building’s integrity was maintained by regular inspections. =  建筑的完整性通过定期检查得以维护。


疑问句:
Does the sound continue? = 声音还在继续吗？
Will the support be maintained? = 支持会持续吗？


否定句:
The music didn't continue for long. = 音乐没有持续很久。
He didn't maintain his promise. = 他没有遵守诺言。
