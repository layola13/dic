hallelujahs:/ˌhælɪˈluːjəz/ | n. |哈利路亚（复数）|  Expressions of praise to God; shouts of joy and praise.  The plural form of "hallelujah." |例句：The choir sang hallelujahs at the end of the service.  唱诗班在礼拜结束时唱起了哈利路亚。


The congregation erupted in joyous hallelujahs. 会众爆发出欢欣的哈利路亚声。


近义词：praises, hosannas, amens |反义词：None (as it expresses praise) |词性: noun
