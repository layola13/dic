heterozygoted:/ˌhetərəʊˈzaɪɡəʊtɪd/ | adj. | 杂合的；异型合子的 |  Describes an organism that has two different alleles for a particular gene.  This means it inherited different versions of the gene from each parent.  The contrasting trait is homozygous, which means having two identical alleles for a given gene. | Example: The plant is heterozygoted for flower color, carrying one allele for red and one for white.  这株植物的花色是杂合的，携带一个红色等位基因和一个白色等位基因。


近义词：hybrid (in some contexts)
反义词：homozygous

词性：形容词
