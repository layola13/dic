encounters:/ɪnˈkaʊntərz/ | n. v. | 遭遇；邂逅；遇到 |  An encounter is a brief or unexpected meeting.  It can refer to a meeting with a person, an event, or a situation. As a verb, it means to unexpectedly meet or experience something. |

例句：
I had an unexpected encounter with my old teacher at the mall.  我在商场意外地遇到了我的老老师。

The hikers encountered a bear on the trail.  徒步旅行者在小路上遇到了一只熊。


近义词：meetings, meetings, confrontations, experiences

反义词：avoidances, planned meetings


词性：名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He encounters many challenges in his work. 他在他的工作中遇到许多挑战。
She encounters new people every day. 她每天都遇到新朋友。


一般过去时:
I encountered a problem with my computer yesterday. 我昨天遇到了电脑问题。
They encountered a storm during their journey. 他们在旅途中遇到了暴风雨。


现在完成时:
I have encountered many difficulties in my life. 我一生中遇到过许多困难。
She has encountered similar problems before. 她以前遇到过类似的问题。


过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, they had already encountered several obstacles. 当我到达时，他们已经遇到了几个障碍。
Before the project started, they had encountered significant funding issues.  项目开始前，他们遇到了严重的资金问题。


将来时:
We will encounter more difficulties as the project progresses.  随着项目的进展，我们将遇到更多困难。
They expect to encounter resistance from some stakeholders. 他们预计会遇到一些利益相关者的阻力。


虚拟语气:
If I encountered him again, I would apologize. 如果我再次遇到他，我会道歉。
I wish I hadn't encountered that problem. 我真希望我没有遇到那个问题。


被动语态:
The team was encountered with unexpected technical issues.  团队遇到了意想不到的技术问题。(被动语态不太常用在此词上)
Many difficulties were encountered during the construction.  在施工过程中遇到了许多困难。(被动语态不太常用在此词上)


疑问句:
Did you encounter any problems during your trip? 你在旅途中遇到什么问题了吗？
What kind of challenges will we encounter in the future? 我们将来会遇到什么样的挑战？


否定句:
I didn't encounter any problems with the software. 我没有遇到任何软件问题。
They didn't encounter any opposition to their plan. 他们的计划没有遇到任何反对。


PHRASE->
a chance encounter 偶然相遇
a close encounter 近距离接触
an unexpected encounter 意外相遇

a spiritual encounter 灵性体验
