migrator:/ˈmaɪɡreɪtər/| n. |迁徙者，候鸟|A migrator is an animal that migrates, especially a bird that travels regularly between breeding and wintering grounds.  Migrators often face challenges such as finding food and avoiding predators during their journeys.|例句：Many bird species are migrators, traveling thousands of miles each year. 许多鸟类都是候鸟，每年迁徙数千英里。

例句：The monarch butterfly is a well-known migrator, known for its long-distance flights. 君主蝶是一种著名的迁徙者，以其长途飞行而闻名。


近义词：migrant, traveler, wanderer|反义词：resident, sedentary|词性:noun
