marginality:/ˌmɑːrdʒɪˈnæləti/ | n. |边缘性，边缘地位，非主流 |The state of being marginal; the condition of being on the edge or periphery of a group, society, or system.  It often refers to a position of powerlessness or disadvantage.  Marginality can be economic, social, political, or cultural. |例句：The study explored the marginality experienced by immigrant communities in the city. 这项研究探讨了城市移民社区所经历的边缘化。

例句：Her marginality in the company became increasingly apparent after her project was cancelled.  在她项目被取消后，她在公司中的边缘地位变得越来越明显。


近义词：periphery, outsider status, fringe, insignificance |反义词：centrality, mainstream, prominence, importance |词性:名词
