creamest:/ˈkriːmist/| adj.|最奶油的，最乳脂状的，最浓稠的|Most creamy; having the highest concentration of cream or a cream-like substance.  It's a superlative form of "creamy".|例句：This is the creamest ice cream I've ever tasted. 这是我吃过最奶油的冰淇淋。

例句：The chef used the creamest butter to make the sauce. 厨师用最浓稠的黄油做了酱汁。


近义词：richest, smoothest, thickest |反义词：thinnest, wateriest

词性:形容词
