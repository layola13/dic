laurate:/ˈlɔːreɪt/ | n. |月桂酸酯；月桂酸盐| A laurate is an ester or salt of lauric acid.  Laurates are used in various applications, including cosmetics, soaps, and detergents. | Example: Sodium laurate is a common surfactant found in many cleaning products.  月桂酸钠是许多清洁产品中常见的表面活性剂。|近义词：lauric acid ester (for ester form)|反义词：无
