obstetrician:/ˌɒbstɪˈtrɪʃən/| n. |妇产科医生| A physician specializing in obstetrics, the branch of medicine dealing with pregnancy, childbirth, and the postpartum period.  Obstetricians provide medical care to women during pregnancy, labor, and delivery, and often provide postpartum care as well. |例句：My wife is seeing an obstetrician for her prenatal checkups. 我的妻子正在去看妇产科医生进行产前检查。

例句：The obstetrician performed a successful cesarean section.  这位妇产科医生成功地进行了剖腹产手术。


近义词：gynecologist (if also dealing with women's reproductive health),  midwife (though midwives typically have a different scope of practice)


反义词：无


词性：名词
