distractedly:/dɪˈstræktɪdli/ | adv. |心不在焉地，漫不经心地 | in a way that shows a lack of attention or concentration because one's thoughts are elsewhere.  |例句：He listened distractedly to the lecture, his mind wandering to his upcoming vacation. 他心不在焉地听着讲座，思绪飘到了即将到来的假期。


例句：She walked distractedly down the street, bumping into several people. 她心不在焉地走在街上，撞到了几个人。


近义词：absentmindedly, inattentively, heedlessly


反义词：attentively, carefully, purposefully


词性: 副词
