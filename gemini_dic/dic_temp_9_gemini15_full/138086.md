normand:/ˈnɔːrmænd/| adj. n.|诺曼的；诺曼人|Of or relating to Normandy, its people, or their language.  The term often refers to the descendants of the Vikings who settled in Normandy in the 10th century and their subsequent cultural and political influence.|例句：The Norman Conquest of England profoundly changed the English language and culture.  诺曼征服英格兰深刻地改变了英语和文化。  The architecture of the region shows strong Norman influences.  该地区的建筑风格显示出强烈的诺曼风格影响。|近义词：Norman French（诺曼法语）|反义词：无
