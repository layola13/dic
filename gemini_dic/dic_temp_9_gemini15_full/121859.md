misrepresenting:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪˈzɛntɪŋ/| v.|歪曲；虚报；不实地描述|To misrepresent something means to give a false or misleading account of it.  It involves presenting something in a way that is not accurate or true, often deliberately.|

例句：

He was accused of misrepresenting the company's financial situation. = 他被指控歪曲了公司的财务状况。

The advertisement misrepresented the product's capabilities. = 广告歪曲了产品的性能。


近义词：distort, falsify, misstate, pervert, twist

反义词：represent accurately, truthfully portray


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They misrepresent the facts to the public. = 他们向公众歪曲事实。
The company misrepresents its products' quality. = 这家公司虚报其产品的质量。


一般过去时:
He misrepresented his qualifications on his resume. = 他在简历上虚报了自己的资历。
The politician misrepresented the voters' opinions. = 这位政治家歪曲了选民的意见。


现在完成时:
She has misrepresented herself in several interviews. = 她曾在几次采访中虚报了自己的情况。
They have misrepresented the risks involved in the project. = 他们歪曲了该项目中涉及的风险。


过去完成时:
The defendant had misrepresented his alibi to the police. = 被告曾向警方虚报了自己的不在场证明。
The company had misrepresented its environmental record for years. = 这家公司多年来一直虚报自己的环保记录。


将来时:
He will misrepresent the evidence in court. = 他将在法庭上歪曲证据。
The media will misrepresent the scientist's findings. = 媒体将会歪曲这位科学家的研究结果。


虚拟语气:
If he misrepresented the facts, he would be punished. = 如果他歪曲事实，他将受到惩罚。
I wish they hadn't misrepresented my intentions. = 我真希望他们没有歪曲我的意图。


被动语态:
The situation was misrepresented by the media. = 这种情况被媒体歪曲了。
The company's actions were misrepresented in the report. = 该公司的行为在报告中被歪曲了。


疑问句:
Did he misrepresent the facts deliberately? = 他是故意歪曲事实的吗？
Were the figures misrepresented in the financial statement? = 财务报表中是否歪曲了数字？


否定句:
He did not misrepresent his age. = 他没有虚报年龄。
The witness did not misrepresent what she saw. = 目击者没有歪曲她所看到的情况。


PHRASE->
misrepresent oneself = 虚报身份
misrepresent the truth = 歪曲真相
deliberately misrepresent = 故意歪曲
seriously misrepresent = 严重歪曲
grossly misrepresent = 极度歪曲
