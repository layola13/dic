constrainedly:/kənˈstreɪnɪdli/ | adv. | 受约束地；勉强地；别扭地 | In a constrained manner; showing constraint or restraint.  It implies a lack of freedom or naturalness in behavior or expression.  |例句：She smiled constrainedly, unsure of how to respond. 她勉强地笑了笑，不知道该如何回应。

He spoke constrainedly, clearly uncomfortable with the subject. 他说话吞吞吐吐，显然对这个话题感到不舒服。


近义词：reluctantly, hesitantly, stiffly, awkwardly, unwillingly | 反义词：freely, naturally, spontaneously, openly, willingly | 词性: 副词
