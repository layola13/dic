haystacks:/ˈheɪstæks/| n. |干草堆|A large pile of hay, typically in a field.  Haystacks are often conical or rectangular in shape and are created to store hay for animal feed during the winter months. They are a common sight in rural areas.|The farmer carefully built his haystacks after harvesting.|piles of hay, hay bales|—|
