protestations:/ˌprɒtɛsˈteɪʃənz/ | n. |抗议；坚决否认；强烈声明|The act of protesting or the expression of strong objection or disapproval; a vehement denial.  |例句：His protestations of innocence were unconvincing.他的无辜抗辩令人难以信服。

例句：Despite her protestations, he knew she was lying.  尽管她强烈否认，但他知道她在撒谎。


近义词：objections, complaints, denials, assertions

反义词：acceptance, agreement, approval


词性：名词
