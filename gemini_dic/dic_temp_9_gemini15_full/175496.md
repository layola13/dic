ramps:/ræmps/| n.|斜坡，坡道|A ramp is an inclined plane or surface, usually gentle sloping, used to connect different levels.|例句：The wheelchair ramp made the building accessible to everyone.  轮椅坡道使建筑物对所有人开放。  The car drove up the steep ramp to the parking garage. 汽车驶上陡峭的坡道进入停车场。|近义词：slope, incline, gradient|反义词：stairs, steps
