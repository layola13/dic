USAGE->
This word is not used as a standalone word, but as a prefix.  Therefore, grammatical usage examples are not applicable in the same way as a verb or noun.  The usage is purely contextual, depending on the word it prefixes.  For example:

* **Deutoxide:**  This refers to an oxide containing two oxygen atoms per molecule of the other element.

* **Deuteron:**  This refers to the nucleus of deuterium (a stable isotope of hydrogen).

PHRASE->
There are no common phrases using "deuto-" as a standalone prefix since it only appears as a part of scientific or technical terms.  It's not part of everyday vocabulary.
