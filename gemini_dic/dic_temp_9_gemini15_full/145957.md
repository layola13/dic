overdistend: /ˌoʊvər dɪˈstɛnd/ | vt. |过度膨胀；过度扩张 | To distend or swell excessively.  It implies exceeding the normal or healthy limits of expansion.  |例句：The balloon overdistends and bursts.  气球过度膨胀然后爆裂了。

The constant coughing overdistended his lungs.  持续的咳嗽使他的肺过度膨胀。


近义词：overinflate, overstretch, bloat

反义词：deflate, contract, shrink
