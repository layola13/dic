demarcates:/ˈdiːmɑːrkeɪts/ | vt. | 划定界限；区分；标明…的界限 | To set the boundaries or limits of something; to distinguish or separate something from something else. | The river demarcates the boundary between the two countries. 这条河划分了这两个国家的边界。  The white line demarcates the lanes on the road. 白线标明了道路上的车道。 | delineate, define, distinguish | blur, confuse, merge | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The treaty demarcates the territorial limits of the two nations. = 这项条约划定了这两个国家的领土范围。
This fence demarcates my property from my neighbor's. = 这道栅栏将我的财产与邻居的财产区分开来。

一般过去时:
The ancient Romans demarcated their empire with roads and milestones. = 古罗马人用道路和里程碑来划分他们的帝国。
The map clearly demarcated the areas affected by the flood. = 地图清晰地标明了受洪水影响的地区。

现在完成时:
The committee has demarcated the responsibilities of each member. = 委员会已经划分了每个成员的职责。
They have demarcated the research areas for the different teams. = 他们已经为不同的团队划分了研究领域。


过去完成时:
Before the war, the border had been clearly demarcated by treaty. = 在战争爆发前，边界已由条约明确划定。
The archaeologists had carefully demarcated the excavation site. = 考古学家们已经仔细地划定了发掘地点。

将来时:
The government will demarcate a new wildlife reserve. = 政府将划定一个新的野生动物保护区。
The surveyors will demarcate the property lines next week. = 测量员下周将划定地界。

虚拟语气:
If we demarcated the roles more clearly, there would be less confusion. = 如果我们更清晰地划分角色，就不会有那么多混乱。
I wish they had demarcated the parking areas more effectively. = 我希望他们能更有效地划定停车区域。

被动语态:
The boundaries were demarcated by a team of surveyors. = 边界是由一个测量员小组划定的。
The different zones have been demarcated for safety reasons. = 为了安全起见，不同的区域已经被划分开来。

疑问句:
How will the new park be demarcated? = 新公园将如何划定？
Where exactly is the border demarcated? = 边界究竟在哪里划定？

否定句:
The map didn't demarcate the different types of terrain. = 这张地图没有标明不同类型的地形。
They failed to demarcate their responsibilities properly. = 他们未能恰当地划分他们的职责。

词源:
From Latin *de- ("off, away, from") + *marcare ("to mark").  The word essentially means "to mark off".
