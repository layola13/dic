moved:/muːvd/| /muːvd/ | v. |移动的；感动的；搬迁的 |To have changed position; to have been affected emotionally; to have changed residence. |例句：The box was moved to the corner. 箱子被移到了角落。 |例句：I was deeply moved by her story. 我被她的故事深深打动了。 |近义词：shifted, touched, relocated |反义词：stationary, unaffected, remained


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He moves the table every day. 他每天都移动桌子。
She moves gracefully. 她优雅地移动。


一般过去时:
They moved the furniture yesterday. 他们昨天搬动了家具。
He moved to a new city last year. 他去年搬到了一个新的城市。


现在完成时:
I have moved three times in my life. 我一生中搬过三次家。
She has moved on from that difficult experience. 她已经从那次艰难的经历中走了出来。


过去完成时:
They had moved out before the landlord arrived. 房东来之前他们已经搬走了。
She had moved to London before she met him. 在她遇到他之前，她已经搬到伦敦了。


将来时:
We will move to a bigger house next year. 我们明年将搬到一栋更大的房子。
He will move the car later. 他稍后会挪动汽车。


虚拟语气:
If I had moved sooner, I would have avoided the traffic. 如果我早点动身，我就不会堵车了。
I wish I had moved to the countryside years ago. 我真希望几年前就搬到乡下去了。


被动语态:
The table was moved by the workers. 桌子被工人们搬动了。
The meeting was moved to a larger room. 会议被移到了一个更大的房间。


疑问句:
Did you move the chairs? 你搬动椅子了吗？
Has she moved to a new apartment? 她搬到新公寓了吗？


否定句:
He didn't move the car. 他没有挪动汽车。
They haven't moved yet. 他们还没搬家。


PHRASE->
moved out 搬出
moved in 搬入
be moved 感动
moved to tears 哭泣
moved to action 激励行动
