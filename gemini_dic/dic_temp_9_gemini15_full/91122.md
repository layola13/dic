hybernation:/ˌhaɪbərˈneɪʃən/| n. |冬眠；蛰伏|The state or period of winter sleep in which some animals pass the winter.  It is a state of inactivity and metabolic depression in endotherms. |例句：Bears enter hibernation during the winter months. 熊在冬季进入冬眠。

例句：The groundhog's hibernation cycle is closely tied to the weather. 土拨鼠的冬眠周期与天气密切相关。


近义词：dormancy, torpor, lethargy |反义词：activity, wakefulness
