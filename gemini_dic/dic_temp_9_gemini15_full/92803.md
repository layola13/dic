iconical:/aɪˈkɒnɪkəl/ | adj. |标志性的，偶像的，极具代表性的 | Extremely well known and representative of a particular style, period, or person.  |例句：The Eiffel Tower is an iconic landmark of Paris.  埃菲尔铁塔是巴黎的一个标志性地标。

例句：His rebellious style became iconic for a generation. 他的叛逆风格成为一代人的标志。


近义词：emblematic, symbolic, representative, classic, legendary | 反义词：unremarkable, insignificant, obscure


词源:  From "icon" (itself from Greek εἰκών, eikōn, "image, likeness"), + "-ical" (forming adjectives).  The word evolved from its religious origins (referring to religious images) to its broader modern usage.
