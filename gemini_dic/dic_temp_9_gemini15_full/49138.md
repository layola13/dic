deity:/ˈdeɪəti/| n. |神；神性 |A deity is a god or goddess, a supernatural being worshipped as having power over nature or human fortunes.  The concept of deity varies across cultures and religions. |Example:  In Greek mythology, Zeus is the chief deity.  希腊神话中，宙斯是主神。  Many cultures have a pantheon of deities.  许多文化都有一个神灵万神殿。|近义词：god, goddess, divinity |反义词：mortal, human
