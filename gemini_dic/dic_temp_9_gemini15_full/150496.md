pantographers:/pænˈtɒɡrɑːfəz/| n.|万花筒,复写器|A pantograph is a mechanical linkage that is used to copy a drawing or design at a different scale.  It consists of a parallelogram-shaped framework with four hinged joints.  By tracing the original drawing with one end of the pantograph, a proportionally scaled copy is produced at the other end.  |The architect used a pantograph to enlarge the blueprint.|近义词：copying machine, duplicator|反义词：original
