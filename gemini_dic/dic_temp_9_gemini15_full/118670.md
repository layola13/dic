It includes anabolism (the building up of complex molecules from simpler ones) and catabolism (the breaking down of complex molecules into simpler ones).| Example:  The athlete's high metabolic rate allowed him to burn calories quickly.  这位运动员的高代谢率使他能够快速燃烧卡路里。|近义词：metabolism, transformation|反义词：stasis, inactivity|名词
