reseat:/ˌriːˈsiːt/| vt.|重新安排座位；使重新就座|To seat again; to arrange or assign seats again.|例句：The airline had to reseat several passengers due to overbooking. 航空公司由于超额预订不得不为几位乘客重新安排座位。
例句：Please reseat yourselves after the intermission. 中场休息后请重新入座。|近义词：relocate, reposition|反义词：evacuate, remove
