discomposed:/ˌdɪskəmˈpoʊzd/ | adj. |不安的；心烦意乱的；扰乱的 |  To be discomposed means to be disturbed or upset; to be thrown into a state of disorder or confusion.  It implies a loss of composure or calmness, often due to an unexpected or unpleasant event. |  The unexpected news left her utterly discomposed.  =  这个意外的消息让她完全慌乱了。

The sudden storm discomposed the carefully planned picnic. = 突如其来的暴风雨打乱了精心策划的野餐。

|近义词：perturbed, unsettled, disturbed, agitated, flustered |反义词：calm, composed, serene, tranquil, undisturbed |形容词
