multitubular: /ˌmʌltiˈtjuːbjələr/ | adj. | 多管的，多管状的 | Having or consisting of many tubes. 词源:
The word "multitubular" is a combination of "multi-" (meaning many) and "tubular" (relating to tubes).  "Tubular" derives from the Latin word "tubulus," a diminutive of "tuba" (tube).  Therefore, "multitubular" literally means "having many tubes."
