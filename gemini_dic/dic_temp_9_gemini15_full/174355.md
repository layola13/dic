quicksell:/kwɪkˈsɛl/| n. |快速销售，速卖|A quicksell is a sales strategy focused on rapid transactions, often involving high-volume, low-margin products or services. It prioritizes speed and efficiency over extensive customer engagement or relationship building.  |Example: The online retailer relied on a quicksell model, offering discounted products with limited-time promotions to drive immediate sales.  在线零售商依靠快速销售模式，提供限时促销的打折产品以推动即时销售。|近义词：fast sale, rapid sale, flash sale |反义词：long-term sales strategy, relationship selling |名词
