falling-off: /ˈfɔːlɪŋ ɒf/ | adj. | 下降；衰退；脱落 |  Describes a decline or decrease in something, or the act of something detaching itself.  It can refer to a gradual process or a sudden event. |

例句：
There's been a significant falling-off in sales this quarter. = 本季度销售额大幅下降。

The paint is showing signs of falling-off. = 油漆有脱落的迹象。


近义词：decline, decrease, drop, deterioration, shedding, slump

反义词：increase, rise, growth, improvement, upswing


词性: Adjective (形容词),  though it functions as a gerund (动名词) within a noun phrase.  It's not typically used as a noun, verb, or adverb on its own.
