nimbyism:/ˈnɪmbiɪzəm/| n. |邻避主义|The opposition by residents to the siting of something perceived as undesirable near their homes, such as a power plant, prison, or landfill.  It's characterized by the phrase "Not In My Back Yard".|Example: The proposed new highway faced strong nimbyism from residents concerned about increased traffic and noise.|近义词：localism, parochialism|反义词：universalism, cosmopolitanism
