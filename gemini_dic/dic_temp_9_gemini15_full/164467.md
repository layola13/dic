preentering: /ˌpriːˈɛntəriŋ/ | adj. | 预先进入的；预先录入的 |  Describing something that is done or happens before entering a place, system, or process.  It suggests preparation or prior action before the main event. | 例句：The preentering of data saved us considerable time later. 预先录入数据为我们节省了大量时间。


例句：The preentering of the flight details expedited the check-in process.  预先录入航班详情加快了办理登机手续的速度。


近义词：prior, preliminary, advance, anticipatory


反义词：post-entry, subsequent, following
