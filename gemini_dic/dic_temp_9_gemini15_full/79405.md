glimpsed:/ɡlɪmpt/| v.|瞥见，一瞥|To catch a brief sight of something or someone.  |例句：I glimpsed a figure in the shadows. 我瞥见阴影中一个人影。|例句：She glimpsed her reflection in the window. 她瞥见自己在窗户里的倒影。|近义词：caught sight of, saw briefly, glanced at|反义词：stared at, observed carefully


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I glimpse the ocean from my window. = 我从窗户里瞥见大海。
Birds glimpse the worms under the ground. = 鸟儿瞥见地下的虫子。

一般过去时:
He glimpsed a deer in the forest. = 他在森林里瞥见了一只鹿。
She glimpsed a familiar face in the crowd. = 她瞥见人群中一张熟悉的面孔。

现在完成时:
I have glimpsed the Northern Lights several times. = 我已经几次瞥见过北极光。
We have glimpsed a solution to the problem. = 我们已经瞥见了解决这个问题的方法。

过去完成时:
Before the accident, he had glimpsed the oncoming truck. = 事故发生前，他瞥见了迎面而来的卡车。
They had glimpsed the thief running away. = 他们瞥见小偷跑掉了。

将来时:
I will glimpse the Eiffel Tower when I arrive in Paris. = 我到达巴黎后将会瞥见埃菲尔铁塔。
We will glimpse the comet tonight if the sky is clear. = 如果今晚天空晴朗，我们会瞥见彗星。

虚拟语气:
If I had glimpsed the danger, I would have stopped. = 如果我当时瞥见危险，我就会停下来。
I wish I had glimpsed the opportunity earlier. = 我真希望我早点瞥见这个机会。

被动语态:
A glimpse of the future was seen. = 未来的一瞥被看到了。(被动语态较少用在此处)
The rare bird was glimpsed by the bird watcher. = 观鸟者瞥见了这种稀有的鸟类。

疑问句:
Did you glimpse anything unusual? = 你瞥见什么不寻常的东西了吗？
What did you glimpse in the distance? = 你在远处瞥见了什么？

否定句:
I didn't glimpse anyone near the house. = 我没有瞥见任何人靠近房子。
She didn't glimpse the opportunity to escape. = 她没有瞥见逃跑的机会。


PHRASE->
a fleeting glimpse = 一瞥
a brief glimpse = 短暂的一瞥
a quick glimpse = 快速的一瞥
a sudden glimpse = 突然的一瞥
a distant glimpse = 遥远的一瞥
