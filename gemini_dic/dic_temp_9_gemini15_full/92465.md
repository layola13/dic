hypostatize: /haɪˈpɒstətaɪz/ | vt. | 将…人格化；赋予…实体；具体化 | To regard or represent (an abstract concept) as a concrete entity.  To give concrete form to (an abstraction). |  例句：He hypostatized the concept of justice as a blindfolded woman with scales. 他将正义的概念人格化为一个蒙着眼睛拿着秤的女人。


例句：The poet hypostatized love as a powerful force shaping human destiny. 诗人将爱具体化为一种塑造人类命运的强大力量。


近义词：personify, embody, exemplify, concretize


反义词：abstract, dematerialize


词性：动词


词源：来自希腊语 ὑπόστασις (hypostasis)，意思是“基础”， “本质”， “实体”。  这个词的含义演变为将抽象概念赋予具体形式。
