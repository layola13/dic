ketamine:/ˈkiːteɪmiːn/|/ˈketəmɪn/| n. |氯胺酮，凯他命|A dissociative anesthetic drug used medically as an anesthetic and recreationally as a hallucinogen. It can induce a state of dissociation, where the user feels detached from their body and surroundings.  Ketamine is also used to treat depression and other mental health conditions.|例句：The veterinarian used ketamine to anesthetize the cat.  兽医用氯胺酮麻醉了那只猫。

例句：The illegal use of ketamine as a recreational drug is a growing concern. 滥用氯胺酮作为娱乐性药物日益成为一个令人担忧的问题。|近义词：anesthetic, anesthetic agent, dissociative anesthetic|反义词：stimulant
