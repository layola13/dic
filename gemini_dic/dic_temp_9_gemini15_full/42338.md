counteract:/ˈkaʊntərækt/ | vt. |对抗；抵消；中和|To act against something in order to reduce its force or effects.  |例句：The drug is designed to counteract the effects of the poison.  这种药物旨在对抗毒药的作用。

例句：We need to counteract the negative impact of climate change. 我们需要抵消气候变化的负面影响。


近义词：counter, oppose, neutralize, offset | 反义词：reinforce, support, enhance | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The medicine counteracts the effects of the virus. = 这药能对抗病毒的侵害。
They counteract the opponent's arguments. = 他们反驳对手的论点。


一般过去时:
The antidote counteracted the poison. = 解毒剂抵消了毒药的作用。
He counteracted the rumors by releasing a statement. = 他发表声明以反驳谣言。


现在完成时:
She has counteracted many challenges in her career. = 她已经克服了她职业生涯中的许多挑战。
We have counteracted the negative press with positive publicity. = 我们用积极的宣传来抵消负面新闻的影响。


过去完成时:
They had counteracted the initial attack before reinforcements arrived. = 增援到达之前，他们已经抵御住了最初的攻击。
The government had counteracted the economic crisis with various measures. = 政府已采取各种措施来应对经济危机。


将来时:
We will counteract any attempt to sabotage the project. = 我们将对抗任何破坏该项目的企图。
The new policy will counteract the rising inflation. = 新政策将对抗通货膨胀。


虚拟语气:
If we took action now, we could counteract the damage. = 如果我们现在采取行动，我们就能抵消损失。
I wish I had known how to counteract his influence. = 我真希望我知道如何抵消他的影响。


被动语态:
The effects of the toxin were counteracted by the antidote. = 毒素的作用被解毒剂抵消了。
The negative publicity was counteracted by a strong PR campaign. = 负面宣传被强有力的公关活动所抵消。


疑问句:
How can we counteract this trend? = 我们如何才能对抗这种趋势？
What measures can counteract the spread of misinformation? = 采取什么措施可以对抗虚假信息的传播？


否定句:
The medication did not counteract the allergic reaction. = 药物没有对抗过敏反应。
Their efforts did not counteract the damage already done. = 他们的努力并没有抵消已经造成的损害。


PHRASE->
counteract the effects of = 抵消…的影响
counteract a trend = 逆转一种趋势
counteract negative influences = 抵消负面影响
counteract the impact of = 抵消……的影响
counteract each other = 相互抵消


词源:
The word "counteract" comes from the prefix "counter-" (meaning "against" or "opposite") and the verb "act" (meaning "to do something").  Therefore, it literally means "to act against".
