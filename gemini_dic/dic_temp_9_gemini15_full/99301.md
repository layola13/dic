intercostals: /ˌɪntərˈkɒstəl/ | /ˌɪntərˈkɑːstl/ | adj. |肋间的，肋骨间的| Situated between the ribs.  The term refers to structures, muscles, nerves, or vessels located between the ribs. |例句：The intercostal muscles help with breathing. 肋间肌有助于呼吸。|例句：The doctor examined the patient's intercostal spaces for signs of infection. 医生检查了病人的肋间隙是否有感染迹象。|近义词：intercostalis (less common)|反义词：None specifically, but terms referring to structures *outside* the ribs could be considered antonyms in context.
