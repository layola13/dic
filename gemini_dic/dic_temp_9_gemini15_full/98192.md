insect:/ˈɪnsekt/ | n. |昆虫 |An insect is a small creature with six legs, usually with wings.  Insects belong to the class Insecta and are the largest group within the arthropod phylum. They are found in almost every habitat on Earth.  |Example:  The spider is not an insect; it's an arachnid.  蜘蛛不是昆虫，它是蛛形纲动物。

The butterfly is a beautiful insect. 蝴蝶是一种美丽的昆虫。|近义词：bug, creature, vermin |反义词：N/A
