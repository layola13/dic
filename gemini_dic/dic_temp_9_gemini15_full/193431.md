seizure:/ˈsiːʒər/| n. | 癫痫发作；夺取；没收 | The sudden occurrence of symptoms such as convulsions, loss of consciousness, or sensory disturbances, typically associated with epilepsy.  Also refers to the act of taking something by force or authority. |例句：He suffered a seizure and fell to the ground. 他癫痫发作倒地了。|例句：The police made a seizure of illegal drugs. 警方查获了一批毒品。|近义词：convulsion, fit, attack, confiscation |反义词：release, surrender
