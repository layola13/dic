hung around:/hʌŋ əˈraʊnd/| v. |闲逛，逗留，徘徊|To spend time idly or without purpose; to loiter.  The phrase implies a lack of specific activity or destination.|例句：They hung around the mall for hours. 他们在商场闲逛了好几个小时。

例句：The teenagers hung around the park after school.放学后，孩子们在公园里闲逛。


近义词：loiter, linger, dawdle, hang out


反义词：hurry, rush


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hang around the coffee shop every afternoon. = 他们每天下午都在咖啡店闲逛。
We hang around the park on weekends. = 我们周末在公园里闲逛。


一般过去时:
He hung around the library until closing time. = 他在图书馆待到闭馆。
They hung around the city center all day. = 他们整天在市中心闲逛。


现在完成时:
I've hung around this town for too long. = 我在这个小镇上闲逛太久了。
She's hung around waiting for her friend for an hour. = 她等朋友已经等了一个小时了。


过去完成时:
They had hung around the bar before going to the concert. = 他们去音乐会之前在酒吧里闲逛了一阵。
We had hung around the beach all morning before swimming. = 我们在海滩上闲逛了整个上午才去游泳。


将来时:
I will hang around the bookstore until it closes. = 我会在书店待到它关门。
We will hang around here until the bus arrives. = 我们将在这里等着公共汽车到来。


虚拟语气:
If I had nothing to do, I would hang around the park. = 如果我没事可做，我会在公园里闲逛。
I wish I could hang around with my friends more often. = 我希望我能更经常地和朋友们一起闲逛。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The area was often hung around by loiterers. = 这个区域经常有闲逛的人。 (This is an unusual construction)


疑问句:
Where do you usually hang around? = 你通常在哪里闲逛？
What do you like to do when you hang around? = 你闲逛的时候喜欢做什么？


否定句:
I didn't hang around after the party. = 派对结束后我没有闲逛。
They didn't hang around long at the meeting. = 他们在会议上没有待很久。



PHRASE->
hang around with (someone): 和（某人）一起闲逛
hang around the house: 在家闲逛
hang around waiting: 闲逛等待
hang around doing nothing: 无所事事地闲逛
just hang around: 只是闲逛
