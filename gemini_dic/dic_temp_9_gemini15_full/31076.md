champ-at-the-bit:/tʃæmp æt ðə bɪt/| v. | 跃跃欲试，按捺不住 | To be eager to start something or go somewhere.  This idiom refers to a horse that is eagerly pawing the ground, champing its bit, because it wants to start running. |The kids were champing at the bit to get started on their summer vacation.|近义词：eager, impatient, restless |反义词：calm, patient, relaxed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He champs at the bit to begin his new job. = 他迫不及待地想开始他的新工作。
They champ at the bit to start the race. = 他们跃跃欲试地准备开始比赛。


一般过去时:
She champed at the bit to tell everyone the good news. = 她按捺不住想告诉所有人这个好消息。
The audience champed at the bit while the band tuned their instruments. = 乐队调试乐器的时候，观众们都等不及了。


现在完成时:
The runners have been champing at the bit all morning. = 跑步运动员们整个上午都跃跃欲试。
We have champed at the bit for this opportunity for years. = 多年来，我们一直渴望得到这个机会。


过去完成时:
They had champed at the bit for hours before the announcement. = 在宣布消息之前，他们已经等不及好几个小时了。
The horses had champed at the bit all day, eager for the evening race. = 马儿们一整天都跃跃欲试，渴望参加晚上的比赛。


将来时:
She will champ at the bit to see her family after such a long trip. = 这么长时间的旅行后，她将迫不及待地想见她的家人。
The athletes will champ at the bit until the starting gun fires. = 运动员们将在发令枪响之前跃跃欲试。


虚拟语气:
If I were in her shoes, I would champ at the bit to travel the world. = 如果我是她，我会迫不及待地去环游世界。
I wish I could champ at the bit like that young athlete. = 我真希望自己也能像那个年轻运动员那样充满活力。


被动语态: (This idiom is rarely used in the passive voice.)  The phrase doesn't lend itself to passive voice naturally.


疑问句:
Is he champing at the bit to start the project? = 他是否迫不及待地想开始这个项目？
Are the children champing at the bit for Christmas? = 孩子们是不是都迫不及待地等着过圣诞节？


否定句:
He wasn't champing at the bit; he was quite calm. = 他并不着急；他相当平静。
The students weren't champing at the bit for the exam. = 学生们并不急于参加考试。
