epipedometry:/ˌɛpɪpɪˈdɒmətri/ | n. |表土测量学 |The science or practice of measuring the surface layer of soil.  It involves techniques to determine the thickness, composition, and properties of the topsoil.  This information is crucial for agriculture, environmental science, and archaeology. | Example: Epipedometry is essential for assessing soil health and guiding sustainable land management practices. 表土测量学对于评估土壤健康和指导可持续土地管理实践至关重要。|近义词：soil surveying, topsoil analysis |反义词：无
