interfamilial: /ˌɪntərˈfæmɪliəl/ | adj. | 家庭内部的；家族内部的 | Relating to or occurring within a family.  | Example:  Interfamilial disputes can be damaging to family relationships. 家庭内部的纠纷会损害家庭关系。


Example: The therapist helped the family work through their interfamilial conflicts.  治疗师帮助这个家庭解决他们家庭内部的冲突。


Near synonyms: intrafamilial, familial


Antonyms:  extrafamilial (outside the family)
