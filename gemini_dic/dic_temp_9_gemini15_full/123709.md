moonie:/ˈmuːni/ | n. | 穆尼教徒；迷恋者 | A member of the Unification Church, founded by Sun Myung Moon.  Also, someone who is excessively infatuated or obsessed with someone or something. | Example: He was a devout Moonie, completely dedicated to the church's teachings. 他是一位虔诚的统一教教徒，完全致力于教会的教义。 He became a moonie over his new girlfriend, neglecting all his other responsibilities. 他迷恋上了他的新女朋友，忽略了他所有的其他责任。 | 近义词：Unification Church member, fanatic, devotee, obsessive | 反义词：skeptic, doubter, apathetic
