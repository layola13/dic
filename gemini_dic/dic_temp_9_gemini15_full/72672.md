foamier:/ˈfoʊmiər/| adj.|较多泡沫的，起泡的，泡沫状的 |More full of or covered with foam.  It describes something that has a greater quantity of foam than something else or than is typical.|例句：The soap produced a foamier lather than the previous brand. 这款肥皂比之前的品牌产生更多泡沫。 The sea was foamier after the storm. 暴风雨过后，大海泡沫更多。|近义词：frothier, bubblier, sudsier |反义词：flat, still
