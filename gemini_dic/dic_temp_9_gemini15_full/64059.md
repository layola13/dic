epitomization:/ɪˌpɪtəmaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. | 典型; 缩影; 完美体现 | The epitome of something is a perfect example of it.  Epitomization refers to the act of serving as a perfect example or embodiment of something. It highlights the essence or most representative characteristics. | Example: The building's design is the epitomization of modern architecture. 这座建筑的设计是现代建筑的完美体现。 |近义词：embodiment, quintessence, personification |反义词：opposite, antithesis
