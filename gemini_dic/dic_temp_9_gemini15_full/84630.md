hand-grenades: /ˈhænd ɡrəˈneɪdz/ | n. | 手榴弹 |  A small, explosive device thrown by hand.  Hand grenades are typically used in close-quarters combat. They are designed to detonate after a short delay or when impacted.  |例句：The soldier threw a hand grenade into the enemy trench.  士兵把手榴弹扔进了敌人的战壕。

The enemy soldiers were surprised by the sudden explosion of the hand grenades. 敌军士兵被手榴弹的突然爆炸吓了一跳。| 近义词：explosive device, grenade | 反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with ranged weapons like missiles. | 名词
