overcleans:/ˌoʊvərˈkliːnz/ | v. |过度清洁；清洁过度 |To clean something excessively, often to the point of damage or harm.  |例句：She overcleans her house, leaving it smelling strongly of bleach. 她把房子打扫得太干净了，导致房子里充满了漂白剂的味道。  |例句：Overcleaning the antique furniture stripped away its protective varnish. 过度清洁古董家具破坏了它的保护漆。|近义词：excessively clean, hyper-clean, sanitize excessively |反义词：neglect, underclean
