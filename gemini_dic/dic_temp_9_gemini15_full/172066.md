purloin:/pɜːˈlɔɪn/| vt.| 偷窃；盗取 | To steal something | The thief purloined a valuable painting from the museum.  小偷从博物馆偷走了一幅名贵的画。 |近义词：steal, pilfer, filch | 反义词：return, restore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He purloins valuable items from shops. 他从商店偷窃贵重物品。
They purloin information from their competitors. 他们从竞争对手那里窃取信息。


一般过去时:
She purloined a diamond necklace from the jewelry store. 她从珠宝店偷走了一条钻石项链。
The spies purloined secret documents. 间谍窃取了秘密文件。

过去完成时:

She realized that someone had purloined her wallet. 她意识到有人偷走了她的钱包。


将来时:
