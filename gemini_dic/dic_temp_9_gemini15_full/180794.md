rehabilitate:/ˌriːhəˈbɪlɪteɪt/ | vt. |使康复；修复；恢复名誉 |To restore someone to health or normal life through training and therapy after imprisonment, addiction, or illness.  To repair or restore something to its former condition. |例句：The program aims to rehabilitate offenders and help them reintegrate into society.  该项目旨在使罪犯康复并帮助他们重新融入社会。

The hospital is working to rehabilitate patients after surgery. 医院正在努力使手术后的病人康复。 |近义词：restore, recover, reinstate |反义词：disable, impair, damage |词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rehabilitate prisoners to prepare them for life outside of jail. = 他们让囚犯进行康复训练，为他们出狱后的生活做准备。
The therapist rehabilitates patients with speech impediments. = 治疗师帮助有言语障碍的病人进行康复。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
The program has rehabilitated hundreds of addicts. = 该项目已经帮助数百名瘾君子康复。

过去完成时:
He had rehabilitated himself completely before he applied for the job. = 他在申请这份工作之前已经完全康复了。
The company had rehabilitated its image before launching the new product. = 公司在推出新产品之前已经重塑了其形象。

将来时:
