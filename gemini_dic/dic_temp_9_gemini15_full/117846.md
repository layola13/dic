mellow:/ˈmɛloʊ/| adj. v.|醇厚的；成熟的；温和的；使成熟；使柔和|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "mellow" describes something that is soft, smooth, and rich in flavor or tone.  It can also refer to a person who is calm, relaxed, and easygoing. As a verb, "mellow" means to become or make something softer, smoother, or more mature.

例句:
The mellow tones of the saxophone filled the room. = 萨克斯风的醇厚音色充满了房间。

The wine had mellowed with age. = 这葡萄酒随着时间的推移变得醇厚了。


近义词:
ripe, mature, soft, smooth, gentle, mild, relaxed

反义词:
harsh, sharp, sour, immature, tense, bitter


词性:
adjective, verb
