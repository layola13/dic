ill-bred:/ɪlˈbred/| adj.| 没有教养的，粗鲁的 | Ill-bred refers to someone who lacks good manners, refinement, or polite social behavior.  They may be considered rude, impolite, or uncouth.  It implies a lack of proper upbringing or education in social graces. | Example: He was considered ill-bred for interrupting the speaker constantly. 他因为不断打断说话者而被认为没有教养。  |近义词：rude, impolite, unrefined, uncouth, boorish, vulgar |反义词：well-bred, refined, polite, courteous, cultured
