epipodite:/ɪˈpɪpədaɪt/ | n. | 附肢| A small, often leg-like appendage attached to the base of a crustacean's leg or other appendage; it typically has a respiratory or sensory function.  | Example: The epipodites on the maxillipeds of this shrimp are particularly well-developed for filtering food particles. 这个虾的颚足上的附肢特别发达，用于过滤食物颗粒。 | 近义词: branchial appendage, gill appendage | 反义词:  (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑与没有附肢的生物结构对比) | 名词
