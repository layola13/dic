funguslike: /ˈfʌŋɡəsˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 真菌似的，类似真菌的 | Resembling a fungus in appearance or growth habit.  The term often describes organisms or structures that exhibit characteristics similar to fungi, such as a filamentous structure, or a specific mode of reproduction.  It doesn't necessarily imply a close phylogenetic relationship. | The growth on the log was funguslike in appearance.  = 木头上生长的东西看起来像真菌。 The researcher noted the funguslike morphology of the newly discovered organism. = 研究人员注意到新发现的生物体的形态类似真菌。|fungal, moldy, mycelial| non-fungal, plant-like
