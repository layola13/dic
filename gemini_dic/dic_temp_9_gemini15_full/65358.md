eulogize:/ˈjuːlədʒaɪz/ | vt. |赞扬；颂扬；悼词 |To speak or write in praise of someone or something, especially at a funeral.  |例句：The speaker eulogized the deceased as a great humanitarian.  演讲者称赞死者是一位伟大的人道主义者。
例句：He delivered a heartfelt eulogy at his father's funeral. 他在父亲的葬礼上发表了一篇感人的悼词。|近义词：praise, commend, celebrate, extol |反义词：criticize, condemn, denounce |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eulogizes his friend's accomplishments at every opportunity. = 他抓住每一个机会赞扬他朋友的成就。
They eulogize the virtues of hard work and perseverance. = 他们赞扬勤奋和毅力的美德。
