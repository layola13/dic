prediplomas:/ˌpriːdɪˈpləʊməz/| n. |预科证书；预科课程结业证书|Certificates awarded before full diplomas are granted, often signifying completion of prerequisite coursework or examinations.|Example:  He received his prediplomas in mathematics and physics before enrolling in the engineering program. 他在注册工程项目之前获得了数学和物理的预科证书。|近义词：preliminary certificates, preparatory certificates|反义词：diplomas
