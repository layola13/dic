fabaceous:/feɪˈbeɪʃəs/| adj.|豆科的；蝶形花的| Belonging to the pea family (Fabaceae), characterized by flowers with a typically butterfly-like shape.  The term often refers to plants with pods containing seeds.| Example: Many fabaceous plants are important sources of nitrogen in the soil.  许多豆科植物是土壤中重要的氮源。|近义词：leguminous|反义词：无
