convolvulus: /kɒnˈvɒlvjʊləs/ | n. |旋花属植物，牵牛花|A genus of flowering plants in the family Convolvulaceae, commonly known as bindweeds or morning glories.  They are characterized by their trumpet-shaped flowers and twining stems. |Example: The convolvulus bloomed beautifully in the morning sun.  牵牛花在清晨的阳光下盛开得非常美丽。  The field was covered with a carpet of convolvulus. 田野里铺满了旋花。|近义词：bindweed, morning glory |反义词：无
