depict:/dɪˈpɪkt/| vt.|描绘，描述，刻画|To represent (someone or something) in a picture or story.  To describe (someone or something) in words.|例句：The painting depicts a beautiful landscape. 这幅画描绘了一幅美丽的风景。

例句：The author depicts the character as a kind and gentle soul. 作者将这个人物描绘成一个善良温柔的人。

近义词：portray, represent, illustrate, describe

反义词：misrepresent, distort

She depicts her childhood memories in her novel. = 她的小说中描绘了她童年的回忆。
