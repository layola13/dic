outjinxed: /ˌaʊtˈdʒɪŋkst/ | v. | 坏运气消除；摆脱厄运 | To remove or overcome a jinx or bad luck.  It implies a reversal of bad luck, suggesting that the previous jinx has been broken. |例句：After years of losing, the team finally outjinxed their losing streak and won the championship.  多年来一直输球的球队最终摆脱了霉运，赢得了冠军。|近义词：break a jinx, overcome bad luck, reverse a curse |反义词：jinx, curse, hex

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outjinxes his bad luck by wearing a lucky charm. = 他通过佩戴幸运符来摆脱霉运。
They outjinx their losing streak by changing their strategy. = 他们通过改变策略来摆脱连败的霉运。

一般过去时:

The company outjinxed its financial difficulties by securing a large investment. = 公司通过获得一笔巨额投资摆脱了财务困境。

过去完成时:
