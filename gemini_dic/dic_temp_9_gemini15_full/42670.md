counterstrikes:/ˈkaʊntə(r)straɪks/| n.|反击；报复性打击|A counterstrike is a military attack made in response to an earlier attack.  It is a retaliatory action aimed at weakening or punishing the aggressor.|例句：The rebels launched a counterstrike after the government bombed their village. 反政府武装在政府轰炸了他们的村庄后发起了反击。

例句：The air force executed a series of devastating counterstrikes against the enemy's supply lines. 空军对敌人的供应线进行了多次毁灭性的反击。


近义词：retaliation, reprisal, revenge|反义词：surrender, capitulation|名词
