reburials:/ˌriːˈbɛəriəlz/| n.|重新下葬；二次安葬|The act of burying something, typically a body, a second time.  This often happens due to the need for relocation, exhumation for forensic reasons, or family requests.|例句：The reburials of the victims were conducted with the utmost respect.  受害者的重新安葬以最大的尊重进行。|例句：The ancient artifacts unearthed during the reburials provided valuable historical insights. 在二次安葬期间出土的古代文物提供了宝贵的历史见解。|近义词：reinterment, reburial|反义词：exhumation
