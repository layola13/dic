fluffing:/ˈflʌfɪŋ/| n. |蓬松；调整；(头发等的)整理；闲聊；|The act of making something fluffy or fuller, or the state of being fluffy;  loose talk or padding in writing or speech.|例句：The fluffing of the pillows made them more comfortable.  给枕头弄蓬松使它们更舒服。  The speech was full of fluffing and lacked substance.  演讲充满了空话，缺乏实质内容。|近义词：puffing, plumping, padding|反义词：compressing, flattening

He is fluffing his pillows every morning. = 他每天早上都整理他的枕头。
She fluffs her hair before going out. = 她出门前会整理她的头发。

一般过去时:
I fluffed the cushions to make them look better. = 我把坐垫弄蓬松使其看起来更好。
