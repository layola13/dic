ladders:/ˈlædərz/| n.|梯子，阶梯，等级|A ladder is a structure used for climbing, typically consisting of two long side pieces joined by rungs.|例句：He climbed the ladder to reach the roof. 他爬上梯子到达屋顶。

例句：The company offers a ladder of opportunities for its employees. 这家公司为员工提供晋升机会。|近义词：staircase, steps, scale|反义词：ground, bottom
