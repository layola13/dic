immortification:/ɪˌmɔːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. | 羞辱；懊悔；苦修 | The state of being humiliated or disgraced; a feeling of shame or embarrassment.  It can also refer to the act of mortifying oneself, often through religious asceticism. |例句：The public humiliation brought him great immortification. 公开羞辱给他带来了极大的羞辱。

例句：Her immortification at her mistake was evident in her flushed cheeks.  她犯错后的懊悔从她涨红的脸颊上显而易见。


近义词：humiliation, shame, mortification, chagrin, embarrassment |反义词：pride, elation, gratification
