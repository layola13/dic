shawm:/ʃɔːm/| n. |沙姆（一种管乐器） |A shawm is a type of double-reed woodwind instrument, similar to an oboe but with a conical bore and a louder, rawer sound. It was popular in medieval and Renaissance Europe. |The shawm players filled the square with their lively music. 沙姆演奏家们用他们欢快的音乐充满了广场。| 近义词：oboe, crumhorn | 反义词：无
