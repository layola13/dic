hallucinogenics:/ˌhəˌluːsɪnəˈdʒɛnɪks/ | n. |致幻剂；致幻药 |Substances that induce hallucinations.  These drugs alter perception, thoughts, and emotions, often causing users to experience vivid sensory distortions and unreal imagery.  The effects vary greatly depending on the specific hallucinogen and the dose. |例句：The use of hallucinogenics is illegal in most countries.  大多数国家禁止使用致幻剂。

例句：Research into the effects of hallucinogenics on the brain is ongoing. 对致幻剂对大脑影响的研究正在进行中。

近义词：psychedelics, psychotomimetics |反义词：None (Antonyms are not readily applicable as it describes a class of substances) |词性：名词
