infomercial:/ɪnˈfoʊrmɜːrʃəl/| n. |电视购物广告；信息广告|A long television advertisement that looks like a normal program, giving information about a product and trying to persuade people to buy it.|Example:  I saw an infomercial for a new type of vacuum cleaner.我看过一个关于新型吸尘器的电视购物广告。|近义词：commercial, advertisement|反义词：N/A

That infomercial promotes a miracle weight-loss pill. = 那则电视购物广告推销一种神奇的减肥药。
