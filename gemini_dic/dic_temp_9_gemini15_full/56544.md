dominators:/ˈdɑːmɪneɪtərz/| n.|支配者，统治者，控制者 |Those who exercise control or power over others;  people or things that dominate a particular area or field. |例句：The dominators of the industry set the prices.  行业支配者设定价格。|例句：The large corporations are the dominators of the global market. 大型公司是全球市场的支配者。|近义词：rulers, controllers, masters, leaders|反义词：subjects, subordinates, the ruled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dominators control the resources. = 支配者控制资源。
These companies dominate the market. = 这些公司主导市场。

一般过去时:
The empire's dominators ruled with an iron fist. = 帝国的统治者铁腕统治。
The old dominators lost their power. = 旧的统治者失去了权力。

过去完成时:
