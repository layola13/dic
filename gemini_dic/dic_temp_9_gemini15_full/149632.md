paintballs:/ˈpeɪntbɔːlz/ | n. |彩弹 | Small, gelatin capsules filled with paint, used in paintball games.  Paintballs are shot from a paintball gun and burst on impact, leaving a mark of paint. | Example:  He got hit with three paintballs in the chest during the paintball game. 他在彩弹游戏中胸部被击中了三颗彩弹。  |近义词：paint pellets | 反义词：无
