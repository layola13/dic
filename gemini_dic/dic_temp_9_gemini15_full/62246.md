encrypting: /ɪnˈkrɪptɪŋ/ | v. |加密；将…编码| The process of converting information or data into a code, making it unreadable to anyone without the decryption key.  This protects sensitive information from unauthorized access. | Example: The company is encrypting all customer data to prevent breaches. 公司正在加密所有客户数据以防止泄露。

Example: We are encrypting the email to ensure confidentiality. 我们正在加密邮件以确保机密性。|近义词：encoding, scrambling, securing |反义词：decrypting, decoding
