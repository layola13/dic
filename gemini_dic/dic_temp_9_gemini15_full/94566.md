impoundage:/ɪmˈpaʊndɪdʒ/| n. |扣押；扣押物；蓄水池|A body of water impounded by a dam or other barrier; the act of impounding something.  The term often refers to the artificial storage of water for various purposes, such as irrigation, hydroelectric power generation, or flood control.|例句：The new impoundage provides water for the entire region. 新建的蓄水池为整个地区提供水源。

The large impoundage behind the dam is used for hydroelectric power. 大坝后面的大型蓄水池用于水力发电。|近义词：reservoir, dam, retention pond|反义词：release, discharge
