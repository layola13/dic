cranked:/kræŋkt/ | v. | 曲柄，启动；强行扭转；使兴奋；制造；| to turn a crank; to force something by twisting; to make something work forcefully; to become excited; to produce something quickly or efficiently | 例句：He cranked the engine to start the car. 他摇动曲柄启动汽车。 He cranked out ten articles in a week. 他一周写了十篇文章。|近义词：started, twisted, produced, excited |反义词：stopped, relaxed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cranks the handle to open the window. = 他摇动把手打开窗户。
She cranks out reports every week. = 她每周都完成报告。

一般过去时:
I cranked the old bike to get it going. = 我摇动旧自行车的曲柄让它动起来。
They cranked up the volume of the music. = 他们把音乐音量调大。

现在完成时:
He has cranked out three novels this year. = 他今年已经完成了三本小说。
She has cranked up the pressure on the team. = 她已经给团队施加了压力。

过去完成时:
We had cranked the music up before the party started. = 派对开始前我们已经把音乐音量调大了。
They had cranked the engine several times before it finally started. = 他们在发动机终于启动之前已经摇动了它几次。

将来时:
I will crank the window open when it gets hot. = 天热的时候我会把窗户摇开。
They will crank out a new product line next year. = 他们明年将推出新的产品线。

虚拟语气:
If I had cranked the engine harder, it might have started. = 如果我用力摇动发动机，它可能就启动了。
I wish I had cranked out more work last week. = 我希望上周我能完成更多工作。

被动语态:
The engine was cranked by the mechanic. = 发动机由机械师启动。
The music was cranked up too loud. = 音乐音量开得太大了。

疑问句:
Did you crank the window open? = 你把窗户摇开了吗？
Have they cranked out the report yet? = 他们完成报告了吗？

否定句:
He didn't crank the engine hard enough. = 他没有用力摇动发动机。
They haven't cranked out a new design yet. = 他们还没有设计出一个新的方案。


PHRASE->
crank up (增加，提高): crank up the volume
crank out (快速生产): crank out essays
crank something out (迅速完成): crank out a report
crank something in (强行塞入): crank the ideas in
crank something down (降低，减少): crank down the heat
