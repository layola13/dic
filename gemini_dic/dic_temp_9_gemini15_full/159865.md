polymeric:/ˌpɒlɪˈmɛrɪk/ /ˌpɑːlɪˈmɛrɪk/ | adj. | 聚合的；聚合体的；多聚物的 | Relating to or consisting of polymers.  Polymeric materials are made up of long chains of repeating molecular units. | Example:  The polymeric substance is incredibly strong and flexible.  这种聚合物质非常坚固且灵活。  The research focused on the polymeric properties of the new material. 这项研究侧重于这种新材料的聚合物特性。 |近义词：polymeric;  |反义词：monomeric (单体的)
