misfeeling:/ˌmɪsˈfiːlɪŋ/ | n. | 不良感觉；误会；反感 | A feeling of unease or disquiet; a wrong or mistaken feeling; a feeling of dislike or resentment.  |例句：I have a misfeeling about this deal. 我对这笔交易有一种不好的预感。

例句：There's a misfeeling between the two brothers.  两兄弟之间存在着隔阂。


近义词：unease, apprehension, distrust, resentment, antipathy | 反义词：comfort, reassurance, trust, fondness, affection | 词性：名词
