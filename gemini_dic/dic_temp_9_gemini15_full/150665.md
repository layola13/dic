papula:/ˈpɑpjʊlə/ | n. |丘疹，小丘疹 | A small, raised bump on the skin.  Papules are usually less than 1 centimeter in diameter and may be caused by a variety of factors, including inflammation, infection, or allergic reactions. They are often solid, but can sometimes be filled with fluid. | Example: The rash consisted of numerous small, red papules. 这疹子由许多细小的红色丘疹组成。|近义词：nodule, vesicle (if fluid-filled), pustule (if pus-filled) |反义词：macule (flat lesion) |名词
