/kɑːrpeɪz/| n.|地毯；地席|A carpet is a thick textile floor covering.  It's typically made of wool, synthetic fibers, or other materials.|例句：We need a new carpet for the living room. 我们需要给客厅换一张新地毯。|例句：The carpet was stained with red wine. 地毯被红酒弄脏了。|近义词：rug, floor covering|反义词：bare floor
