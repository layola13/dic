nonfluidly:/ˈnɑːnˈfluːɪdli/| adv.|不流畅地，不灵活地|In a manner lacking smoothness or ease of movement or flow.  It describes something that is not flowing smoothly or easily. This can refer to physical movement, a process, or even a style of writing.|例句：The paint flowed nonfluidly, creating a textured surface.  颜料流动不流畅， tạo ra một bề mặt có kết cấu.|近义词：awkwardly, clumsily, stiffly|反义词：fluidly, smoothly, effortlessly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb,  so it doesn't conjugate like a verb.  It doesn't have tenses. It modifies verbs.

例句1: He moved nonfluidly across the ice. 他在冰上移动得很不流畅。
例句2: The narrative progressed nonfluidly, jumping between timelines.  叙事进展不流畅，在不同时间线之间跳跃。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "nonfluidly."  Its usage is generally standalone to describe the manner of an action.
