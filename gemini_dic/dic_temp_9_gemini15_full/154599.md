persevered:/ˌpɜːrsɪˈvɪərd/| v.|坚持不懈；忍耐；顽强地继续|to continue trying to do something even though it is difficult|The runner persevered despite the pain in his leg.  这位跑步者尽管腿部疼痛，还是坚持了下来。
She persevered in her studies and finally graduated. 她坚持学习，最终毕业了。| persisted, continued, endured | gave up, quit, surrendered | 动词


USAGE->

He perseveres in his efforts to learn Mandarin. 他坚持努力学习普通话。
