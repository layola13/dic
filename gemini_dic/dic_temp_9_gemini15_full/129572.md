non-Christian:/ˌnɑːnˈkrɪʃtʃən/|/ˈnɒnˈkrɪʃtʃən/| adj. n.|非基督教的；非基督教徒|Not belonging to or connected with Christianity.  This word can be used as an adjective to describe something not related to Christianity, or as a noun referring to a person who is not a Christian.|例句：He comes from a non-Christian background. 他来自非基督教背景。

The festival is celebrated by both Christians and non-Christians.  这个节日由基督教徒和非基督教徒共同庆祝。


近义词：un-Christian, pagan (in some contexts), nonreligious


反义词：Christian


词性：adjective, noun
