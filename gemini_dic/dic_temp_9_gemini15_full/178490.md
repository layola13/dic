recontemplate:/ˌriːkənˈtɛmpleɪt/| vt.|重新考虑，再思考|To think again about something; to reconsider.|例句：I need to recontemplate my decision about the job offer. 我需要重新考虑我的工作机会决定。|例句：After careful consideration, she decided to recontemplate her life goals. 经过仔细考虑，她决定重新思考自己的人生目标。|近义词：reconsider, rethink, review|反义词：decide, finalize
