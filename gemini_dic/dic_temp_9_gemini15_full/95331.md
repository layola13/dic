incense:/ˈɪnsɛns/| n., v.|熏香；使生气|A substance that produces a fragrant smoke when burned, often used in religious ceremonies or to create a pleasant atmosphere.  Also, to make someone angry.|例句：The air was thick with the incense of burning sandalwood.  空气中弥漫着檀香的香味。|例句：His constant complaints incensed his colleagues. 他不断的抱怨激怒了他的同事。|近义词：perfume, fragrance (n.); anger, irritate, provoke (v.)|反义词：displease (v.)
