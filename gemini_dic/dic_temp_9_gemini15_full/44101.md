crisp:/krɪsp/| adj., n.| 清脆的；干脆的；（食物）松脆的 |

英文解析:
As an adjective, "crisp" describes something that is firm, fresh, and easily broken.  It can refer to textures (like a crisp apple), sounds (like a crisp sound), or even styles (like a crisp writing style).  It can also mean decisive and efficient, lacking ambiguity.

例句:
The autumn air was crisp and cool.  = 秋天的空气清新凉爽。

The salad had a crisp, refreshing taste. = 沙拉口感清爽脆口。


近义词:
(for texture) crunchy, brittle, snappy
(for sound) clear, sharp, distinct
(for style) concise, succinct, clear-cut


反义词:
(for texture) soggy, soft, mushy
(for sound) muffled, dull, indistinct
(for style) rambling, vague, unclear


词性:
adjective, noun
