presavage:/ˈpriːˌsævɪdʒ/| adj.|未开化的，野蛮的，原始的|  Describes something or someone exhibiting characteristics of savagery or a pre-civilized state; lacking refinement or culture.  It often implies a state before the development of civilization or societal norms. |例句：The presavage tribes lived in harmony with nature.  原始部落与大自然和谐相处。

The presavage rituals involved elaborate dances and animal sacrifices.  原始的仪式包括复杂的舞蹈和祭祀动物。


近义词：uncivilized, barbaric, primitive, untamed, wild


反义词：civilized, cultured, refined, sophisticated


词性:形容词
