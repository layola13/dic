possibility:/ˌpɒsəˈbɪləti/ /ˌpɑːsəˈbɪləti/ | n. |可能性，可能的事|The possibility of success is high.成功的可能性很高。There are many possibilities for the future. 未来有很多可能性。|近义词：chance, probability |反义词：impossibility, certainty |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The possibility exists that he is lying. = 他可能在撒谎。
The possibility of rain is high today. = 今天下雨的可能性很大。

一般过去时:
There was a possibility that she would miss the train. = 她有可能错过火车。
The possibility of a strike was discussed. = 罢工的可能性被讨论了。

现在完成时:
There has been a possibility of a breakthrough in the negotiations. = 谈判中可能取得了突破。
The possibility of success has increased. = 成功的可能性增加了。

过去完成时:
There had been a possibility that the project would fail. = 项目有可能失败。
The possibility of rain had lessened by evening. = 到晚上下雨的可能性减小了。

将来时:
There will be a possibility of a snowstorm tomorrow. = 明天可能有暴风雪。
The possibility of a cure will increase with further research. = 随着进一步的研究，治愈的可能性将会增加。

虚拟语气:
If there were a possibility of peace, we would pursue it. = 如果有和平的可能性，我们会去追求它。
I wish there was a possibility of changing the decision. = 我希望有可能改变这个决定。

被动语态:
The possibility of a merger was considered. = 合并的可能性被考虑了。
The possibility of error was highlighted in the report. = 报告中强调了出错的可能性。

疑问句:
Is there a possibility of getting a refund? = 有可能获得退款吗？
What is the possibility of winning the lottery? = 中彩票的可能性有多大？

否定句:
There is no possibility of escape. = 没有逃脱的可能性。
There was no possibility of finishing the work on time. = 没有可能按时完成工作。
