downed:/daʊnd/| vt. adj.|击落；使倒下；使沮丧；(飞机)坠毁 |To force (an aircraft, for example) to land; to shoot down.  To fell or knock down. To overcome or subdue. To make (someone) feel dejected or depressed.|例句：The pilot downed his plane in a field.  飞行员将飞机降落在田野里。

The strong wind downed many trees. 大风吹倒了许多树木。

近义词：shot down, felled, depressed, subdued |反义词：erected, raised, uplifted, exhilarated |动词
