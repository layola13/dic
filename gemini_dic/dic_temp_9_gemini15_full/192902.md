词源:
The name "Seattle" comes from Chief Sealth (also spelled Seathl), a leader of the Duwamish and Suquamish tribes who inhabited the Puget Sound region.  The city was originally named "Duwamps" but was later renamed in honor of Chief Sealth.  西雅图的名字来源于杜瓦米什族和苏夸米什族的领袖西雅图酋长（也拼写为西雅图）。这座城市最初名为“杜瓦姆斯”，后来为了纪念西雅图酋长而改名。
