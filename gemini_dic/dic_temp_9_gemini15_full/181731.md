relicensed:/ˌriːˈlaɪsnsd/| v. |重新许可，再授权 |To grant a license again; to renew a license.|The software was relicensed to a new company.  软件被重新许可给了一家新公司。|The old license expired, so the software had to be relicensed. 旧许可证过期了，所以软件必须重新获得许可。| licensed, authorized | unauthorized, unlicensed | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They relicense the software annually. = 他们每年都会重新许可该软件。
The company relicenses its patents frequently. = 这家公司经常重新许可其专利。


一般过去时:
We relicensed the music for the film. = 我们为电影重新许可了音乐。
The government relicensed the broadcasting station. = 政府重新许可了该广播电台。


现在完成时:
They have relicensed the application for another five years. = 他们已经将应用程序重新许可了五年。
The developers have relicensed their code under a more permissive license. = 开发人员已将其代码在更宽松的许可下重新授权。


过去完成时:
Before the project started, they had relicensed the necessary software. = 在项目开始之前，他们已经重新许可了必要的软件。
The company had relicensed the technology before the merger. = 在合并之前，该公司已经重新许可了该技术。


将来时:
We will relicense the software next year. = 我们明年将重新许可该软件。
The firm will relicense the patent to a larger corporation. = 该公司将把专利重新许可给一家更大的公司。


虚拟语气:
If we relicensed the software, we could use it for commercial purposes. = 如果我们重新许可该软件，我们就可以将其用于商业目的。
I wish we had relicensed the music earlier. = 我希望我们早点重新许可音乐。


被动语态:
The software was relicensed under a different agreement. = 该软件在不同的协议下被重新许可。
The patent was relicensed to a competitor. = 专利被重新许可给了一个竞争对手。


疑问句:
Will the software be relicensed? = 软件会被重新许可吗？
Has the music been relicensed for the new album? = 新专辑的音乐是否已重新获得许可？


否定句:
They did not relicense the software. = 他们没有重新许可该软件。
The company has not yet relicensed its technology. = 该公司尚未重新许可其技术。


PHRASE->
relicense software = 重新许可软件
relicense a patent = 重新许可一项专利
relicense a trademark = 重新许可一个商标
relicense intellectual property = 重新许可知识产权
relicense the rights = 重新许可权利
