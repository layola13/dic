non-Indo-European:/ˌnɑːn ˈɪndoʊ ˌjʊərəˈpiːən/|/ˌnɒn ˈɪndoʊ ˌjʊərəˈpiːən/| adj. |非印欧语系的|Not belonging to the Indo-European language family.  This term refers to languages that are not related to the Indo-European family, which includes most of the languages spoken in Europe and parts of Asia.  These languages often have very different grammatical structures and vocabularies.|The Basque language is a well-known example of a non-Indo-European language in Europe.|近义词：non-IE|反义词：Indo-European|形容词
