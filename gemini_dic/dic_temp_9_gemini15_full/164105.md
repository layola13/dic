predismissals:/ˌpriːdɪˈsmɪsəlz/| n.|  解雇前；解雇前的程序 | Dismissals that occur before something else, often referring to a series of dismissals before a larger event or change.  It implies a proactive measure to prevent further problems.  |例句：The predismissals were a prelude to the company's restructuring.  解雇前夕是公司重组的前奏。


例句：The predismissals of several key employees signaled an impending crisis.  几名关键员工的解雇预示着即将到来的危机。


近义词：preemptive firings, advance dismissals, prior layoffs


反义词：post-dismissals, subsequent layoffs


词性：名词
