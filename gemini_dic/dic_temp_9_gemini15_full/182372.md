rending:/ˈrendɪŋ/| v.|撕裂；劈开；使痛苦；使分裂|To tear or split violently; to cause great pain or distress; to split or divide.|例句：The storm was rending the trees apart.暴风雨正在撕裂树木。The news of her death was rending to the family.她去世的消息使家人悲痛欲绝。|近义词：tearing, splitting, shattering, agonizing|反义词：mending, repairing, healing, uniting|


USAGE->

The wind is rending the sails. = 风正在撕裂船帆。
He is rending his clothes in grief. = 他悲痛地撕扯着衣服。


一般过去时:
