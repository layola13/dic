call-ups:/ˈkɔːlʌps/| n.|征召；召集；电话通知|The act of summoning someone for military service, or for another purpose.  It can refer to a summons, often unexpected.|例句：The army issued call-ups for reservists. 军队向预备役人员发出了征召令。

例句：He received a call-up to attend a meeting. 他接到通知要参加会议。


近义词：summons, mobilization, draft, requisition


反义词：discharge, release, demobilization


词性：名词
