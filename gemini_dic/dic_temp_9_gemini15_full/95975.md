incumbent:/ɪnˈkʌmbənt/| adj., n.|在职的；现任的；义不容辞的；（尤指）必须履行的|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "incumbent" means holding an office or position.  As a noun, it refers to the person who holds an office or position.  It can also describe something that is necessary or obligatory.

例句:
The incumbent president is seeking re-election. = 现任总统正在寻求连任。

The incumbent mayor faces a tough challenge from the opposition. = 现任市长面临着来自反对派的严峻挑战。


近义词:
present, current, reigning, existing, obligatory, mandatory

反义词:
former, previous, past, optional


词性:
adjective, noun
