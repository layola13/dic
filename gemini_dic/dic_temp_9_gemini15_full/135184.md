词源:
The word "nonorchestral" is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the adjective "orchestral" (relating to an orchestra).  "Orchestral" itself derives from "orchestra," which ultimately has roots in ancient Greek, referring to the dancing place in a theatre.
