populating: /ˈpɒpjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 填充；居住于；构成…的人口 | To fill or occupy a place or area; to constitute the population of. |


例句：
The birds are populating the newly planted trees. = 鸟儿正在栖息于新种植的树木上。

The city is populating rapidly. = 这个城市人口正在迅速增长。


近义词：inhabiting, filling, colonizing

反义词：depopulating, emptying, evacuating


词性：动词
