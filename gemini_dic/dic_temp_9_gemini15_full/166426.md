prerestrict:/ˌpriːrɪˈstrɪkt/| v.|预先限制，事先限制 |To impose restrictions on something in advance; to limit something before it begins.  |例句：The company prerestricted access to the sensitive data. 公司预先限制了对敏感数据的访问。  |例句：They prerestricted the number of participants to ensure a smooth event. 他们预先限制了参与人数以确保活动顺利进行。|近义词：prelimit, predefine, constrain beforehand |反义词：unrestrict, liberalize
