gustation:/ɡʌˈsteɪʃən/| n. |味觉；品尝；尝味|The sense or act of tasting; the ability to perceive flavors.  Gustation involves the complex interaction of taste receptors on the tongue with chemical compounds in food and drink.  |例句：The chef carefully considered the gustation of each dish.  这位厨师仔细考虑了每道菜的味道。|例句：Her impaired gustation made it difficult to enjoy the meal. 她味觉受损，难以享受这顿饭。|近义词：taste, savor, relish |反义词：None specifically opposite, but could be related to terms like  anesthesia (loss of sensation) or ageusia (loss of taste).|名词
