essentialized:/ɪˌsɛnʃəlaɪzd/ | v. | 使本质化；使成为本质 | To reduce someone or something to a set of essential characteristics, often oversimplifying or stereotyping.  This often involves ignoring individual differences and complexities. |  The media often essentializes women as either mothers or career women, neglecting the diversity of female experiences.  媒体常常将女性本质化，要么是母亲，要么是职业女性，忽略了女性经验的多样性。|  simplified, stereotyped, reduced | nuanced, complex, individualistic | 动词

一般现在时:
They essentialize the role of women in society. = 他们将女性在社会中的角色本质化。
He essentializes the problem by focusing only on one aspect. = 他只关注一个方面，从而使问题简单化。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
