porridges:/ˈpɒrɪdʒɪz/ | n. |粥类；粥（porridge的复数） |Porridges are a type of food made by boiling grain in water or milk until it becomes a thick, soft consistency.  They can be made with various grains, such as oats, rice, barley, or wheat, and often include other ingredients like fruits, nuts, or spices. |例句：Many different kinds of porridges are available in the supermarket. 许多不同种类的粥在超市有售。
