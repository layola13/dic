genuflections:/ˌdʒɛnjuˈflɛkʃənz/| n. |跪拜；屈膝礼|The act of bending the knee, especially as a sign of reverence or respect.  It often refers to the act of kneeling in a Catholic church service. |例句：The priest performed the mass, and the congregation responded with genuflections.  牧师做弥撒，会众以跪拜回应。


例句：Her repeated genuflections showed her deep respect for the religious leader.  她反复的跪拜显示了她对宗教领袖的深深敬意。


近义词：kneeling, prostration, obeisance


反义词：standing, defiance


词性:名词
