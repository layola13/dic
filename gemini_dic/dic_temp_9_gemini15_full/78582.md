giantesses:/ˌdʒaɪənˈtɛsɪz/| n.|女巨人；巨大的女人|The word "giantess" refers to a female giant, a woman of exceptionally large size and stature. It can also be used figuratively to describe a woman with immense power, influence, or skill.|例句：In Greek mythology, there are tales of powerful giantesses who fought alongside the giants.|近义词：gigantesque woman, colossal woman|反义词：tiny woman, petite woman
