clang:/klæŋ/| n. v.|铿锵声，金属撞击声；尖锐刺耳的声音|A loud, resonant metallic sound, like that of a bell. It can also refer to a harsh, unpleasant sound.|例句：The clang of the blacksmith's hammer echoed through the village.  铁匠的锤子铿锵声回荡在村庄里。
The falling metal made a loud clang.  掉落的金属发出一声巨响。|近义词：clatter, clangor, clash|反义词：whisper, murmur
