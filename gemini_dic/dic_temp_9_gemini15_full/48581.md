defeaters:/dɪˈfiːtərz/ | n. | 击败者；挫败者；使无效者 | Those who defeat or overcome something, especially an opponent or argument.  It can also refer to things that disprove or invalidate something else. | Example: The champion's defeaters were a formidable team.  击败冠军的队伍是一支强大的队伍。 The new evidence was a defeater of the original theory. 新证据推翻了最初的理论。 | 近义词：conquerors, vanquishers, refuters, disprovers | 反义词：losers, vanquished, supporters


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team celebrates their defeaters. = 球队庆祝他们的胜利者。
He often defeats his opponents. 他经常击败对手。


一般过去时:
The underdog defeated the champion. =  弱者击败了冠军。
She defeated all her competitors. 她击败了所有竞争对手。


现在完成时:
They have defeated all their opponents this season. = 他们在本赛季击败了所有对手。
She has defeated many challenges in her life. 她一生中克服了许多挑战。


过去完成时:
They had defeated their rivals before the final match. = 他们在决赛前就击败了对手。
He had defeated his fears before taking the stage.  他在上台前克服了恐惧。


将来时:
We will defeat our enemies. 我们将打败敌人。
She will defeat her opponent in the next round.  她将在下一轮击败对手。


虚拟语气:
If only I had defeated my procrastination. 要是我能克服拖延就好了。
If they had defeated the other team, they would have won the championship.  如果他们击败了另一支队伍，他们就赢得了冠军。


被动语态:
The champion was defeated by the underdog. = 冠军被弱者击败了。
The argument was defeated by new evidence.  论点被新的证据驳倒了。


疑问句:
Who defeated the champion? = 谁击败了冠军？

否定句:
They did not defeat their opponents.  他们没有击败对手。
The team was not defeated easily. 这支球队并没有轻易被打败。
