runway:/ˈrʌnweɪ/| n.|跑道，飞机跑道|A runway is a long, paved strip of land at an airport used for the takeoff and landing of aircraft. It can also refer to a raised platform used for fashion shows.|例句：The plane took off from the runway.  飞机从跑道起飞了。|例句：The models walked down the runway in stunning gowns. 模特们穿着令人惊艳的长袍走过T台。|近义词： airstrip, catwalk |反义词: N/A
