overedits:/ˈoʊvərˌedɪts/ | n. |过度编辑；修改过多 | Excessive editing of a text or other work, often to the point of obscuring the original meaning or intent.  It implies that too much editing has been done, potentially making the work worse rather than better. | Example: The article suffered from overedits; the original author's voice was lost in the process.  过度编辑使这篇文章面目全非；作者的原意完全消失了。|近义词：over-editing, excessive revision,  overhaul (if referring to a significant restructuring)|反义词：under-editing, insufficient revision | n.
