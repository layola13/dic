minimizing: /ˈmɪnɪmaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 最小化；使减到最小 | To reduce something to the smallest possible amount or degree.  |  例句：The company is minimizing its environmental impact. 公司正在最大限度地减少其对环境的影响。 | 近义词：reducing, lessening, curtailing | 反义词：maximizing, enlarging, increasing | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are minimizing their expenses. = 他们正在尽量减少开支。
We minimize risks by careful planning. = 我们通过仔细的计划来最大限度地减少风险。

一般过去时:
The government minimized the effects of the crisis. = 政府将危机的后果降到了最低。
He minimized the importance of the problem. = 他轻描淡写了这个问题的重要性。

现在完成时:
They have minimized their losses. = 他们已经将损失降到了最低。
We have minimized the number of errors. = 我们已经将错误的数量降到了最低。

过去完成时:
She had minimized her involvement in the project. = 她已经尽量减少了她对项目的参与。
They had minimized the damage before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们已经将损失降到了最低。

将来时:
We will minimize the disruption to services. = 我们将把对服务的干扰降到最低。
They will minimize their carbon footprint. = 他们将尽量减少他们的碳足迹。

虚拟语气:
If we minimized costs, we could invest more. = 如果我们降低成本，我们就能进行更多投资。
I wish we had minimized the risks earlier. = 我希望我们早点就把风险降到最低。

被动语态:
The impact of the storm was minimized by the emergency services. = 暴风雨的影响被紧急服务部门降到了最低。
The risks are minimized through careful planning. = 风险通过仔细的计划被降到了最低。

疑问句:
How can we minimize the damage? = 我们如何才能将损失降到最低？
Are they minimizing their environmental impact? = 他们正在最大限度地减少其对环境的影响吗？

否定句:
They did not minimize the seriousness of the situation. = 他们没有轻视局势的严重性。
We cannot minimize the importance of education. = 我们不能轻视教育的重要性。


PHRASE->
risk minimizing = 风险最小化
cost minimizing = 成本最小化
damage minimizing = 损失最小化
effort minimizing = 努力最小化
time minimizing = 时间最小化

词源:
minimizing 源自 minimize，minimize 来自 mini- (表示“小”) + -ize (动词后缀)。  mini-源于拉丁语 *minimus (最小)
