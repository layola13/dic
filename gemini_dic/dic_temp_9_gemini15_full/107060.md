lacune:/læˈkuːn/| n. |（尤指知识或信息中的）缺口，空白，不足 | A lacuna is a gap or missing part, especially in a piece of writing or information.  It often refers to something missing that is important or needs to be filled. |例句：There are several significant lacunae in our knowledge of the Roman Empire's early history.  我们的罗马帝国早期历史知识中存在一些重大的缺口。|例句：The historian attempted to fill the lacunae in his account of the battle.  这位历史学家试图填补他对这场战役的叙述中的空白。|近义词：gap, void, hiatus, omission |反义词：completeness, fullness
