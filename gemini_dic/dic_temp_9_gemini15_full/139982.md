odorous:/ˈoʊdərəs/| adj.|有气味的，芬芳的，臭的|Having a strong smell; especially, a bad smell.  The word can be used to describe both pleasant and unpleasant smells, but it often implies an unpleasant or strong odor.|例句：The odorous cheese filled the room.  这块臭奶酪充满了整个房间。 The flowers have a sweet, odorous fragrance. 这些花有一种甜美的芬芳。|近义词：fragrant (pleasant smell), aromatic, pungent (strong smell), fetid (foul smell), smelly, malodorous|反义词：odorless, scentless


USAGE->

The garbage smells odorous. = 垃圾闻起来很臭。
These flowers are odorous and beautiful. = 这些花又香又漂亮。
