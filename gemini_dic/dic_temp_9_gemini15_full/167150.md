presystolic:/ˌpriːˈsɪstɑːlɪk/| adj.| 收缩期前的，心房收缩期的 | Relating to the period immediately before the systole of the ventricles of the heart.  It refers to the final phase of diastole, just before ventricular contraction begins. |The presystolic murmur is a significant diagnostic clue.  收缩期前的杂音是一个重要的诊断线索。|近义词：late diastolic|反义词：postsystolic
