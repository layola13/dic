geometer:/dʒiˈɒmɪtər/| n. |几何学家，几何学者|A geometer is a person who studies or works in the field of geometry.  They may be a mathematician specializing in geometry, or someone who applies geometric principles in a practical field like surveying or engineering. |He is a renowned geometer, known for his work on non-Euclidean geometry. 他是一位著名的几何学家，以其在非欧几何方面的研究而闻名。|mathematician, geometrician|  (no direct antonym) |名词
