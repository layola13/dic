shunted:/ˈʃʌntɪd/| v.|转移，调车，岔开|To push or shove something or someone roughly or suddenly to one side.  It often implies a forceful or abrupt movement,  and can be used figuratively to describe the abrupt dismissal or sidelining of something or someone.|例句：The train was shunted onto a siding. 这列火车被调到支线上了。

例句：He felt shunted aside in the company after the merger. 合并后，他感觉在公司里被边缘化了。


近义词：diverted, switched, shoved, sidelined|反义词：advanced, promoted, included


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They shunt the carriages every evening. = 他们每天晚上都调动车厢。
The manager shunts the most difficult tasks to his assistant. = 经理把最困难的任务交给他的助手。


一般过去时:
The old train was shunted to a siding. = 旧火车被调到支线上了。
They shunted the problem onto someone else. = 他们把问题推给了别人。


现在完成时:
They have shunted the blame onto the junior staff. = 他们已经把责任推给了初级员工。
The company has shunted its unprofitable division. = 公司已经放弃了它无利可图的部门。


过去完成时:
The train had been shunted before the accident. = 事故发生前，火车已被调离。
They had shunted the responsibility before the investigation started. = 调查开始前，他们已经推卸了责任。


将来时:
They will shunt the heavy equipment tomorrow. = 他们明天将要调动重型设备。
The government will shunt the issue to another committee. = 政府将把这个问题交给另一个委员会。


虚拟语气:
If they shunted the resources, the project would succeed. = 如果他们调配资源，项目就会成功。
I wish they had not shunted me aside. = 我真希望他们没有把我排挤在外。


被动语态:
The goods were shunted onto the platform. = 货物被转移到站台上。
The responsibility was shunted onto the innocent employee. = 责任被推到了一位无辜员工身上。


疑问句:
Were the goods shunted safely? = 货物是否安全地转移了？
Will the problem be shunted aside again? = 这个问题还会被搁置一边吗？


否定句:
They didn't shunt the train on time. = 他们没有按时调动火车。
The company didn't shunt the unprofitable projects. = 公司没有放弃那些无利可图的项目。


PHRASE->
shunt aside: 搁置一边
shunt off: 转移
shunt onto: 调到...
shunt to: 转向
shunt away: 避开

词源:
源自古英语单词“scuntian”，意思是“跳跃，推挤”。  这个词的含义后来发展到与铁路调车相关的意义。
