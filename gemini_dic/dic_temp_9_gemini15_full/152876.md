pedantic:/pɪˈdæntɪk/| adj.|卖弄学问的；迂腐的；学究气的；|characterized by a ostentatious and arrogant display of learning; excessively concerned with minor details and rules.  It implies an irritating display of knowledge, often in a way that is irrelevant or inappropriate.|例句：His pedantic explanations bored the students. 他卖弄学问的解释让学生们感到厌烦。

例句：She's so pedantic about grammar. 她对语法过于吹毛求疵。


近义词：scholarly (when positive), didactic, doctrinaire, formalistic, fussy, overprecise, precise


反义词：casual, informal, relaxed, unpretentious


词性:形容词
