dysnomy:/ˈdɪznəmi/| n.|命名困难；失名症；词语检索困难|Difficulty in naming things; a condition characterized by difficulty in remembering names or finding the right words.  It can be a symptom of various neurological conditions or cognitive impairments. |Example:  Her dysnomy made it difficult for her to recall the names of common objects.  她的失名症使她难以想起常见物体的名称。|近义词：anomia, word-finding difficulty|反义词：fluent naming, ease of recall|名词
