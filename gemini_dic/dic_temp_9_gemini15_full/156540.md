pickaback:/ˈpɪkəˌbæk/| n. v.|搭便车；背着走；|A ride on someone's back.  Also, to ride on someone's back.|例句：The child asked for a pickaback ride.  孩子请求背着他走。  He gave me a pickaback. 他背着我。|近义词：piggyback, carry|反义词：walk alone


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gives his daughter pickaback rides every day. = 他每天都背着他女儿玩。
They often pickaback each other for fun. = 他们经常互相背着玩。


一般过去时:
I gave my son a pickaback ride to the park. = 我背着我的儿子去公园。
She gave him a pickaback yesterday. = 昨天她背了他。


现在完成时:
I have given many pickaback rides to my niece. = 我已经背过很多次我的侄女了。
They have never given each other pickaback rides before. = 他们以前从未互相背过。


过去完成时:
He had already given her a pickaback before she asked. = 在她提出请求之前，他已经背过她了。
We had never had a pickaback ride before that day. = 在那一天之前，我们从未有过搭便车的经历。


将来时:
I will give you a pickaback ride home. = 我会背你回家。
They will pickaback each other to the top of the hill. = 他们会互相背着爬上山顶。


虚拟语气:
If I were stronger, I would give you a pickaback. = 如果我更强壮一些，我会背你的。
I wish I could give you a pickaback, but I'm too tired. = 我真希望我能背你，但我太累了。


被动语态:
The child was given a pickaback ride by his father. = 孩子被父亲背着走。
The heavy box was pickabacked to the next room. (less common usage) = 重箱子被背到隔壁房间。(用法较少见)


疑问句:
Can you give me a pickaback? = 你能背我吗？
Will you give her a pickaback to the car? = 你会背她到车那里吗？


否定句:
I can't give you a pickaback; I'm too busy. = 我不能背你，我太忙了。
He didn't give her a pickaback because she was too heavy. = 他没有背她，因为她太重了。
