royalization:/ˌrɔɪəlɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |特许权使用费；版税；王室化 |The payment made to an owner of intellectual property for the use of their property.  This often involves things like patents, copyrights, or trademarks.  Royalties are a key revenue stream for creators and innovators. |Example: The author received substantial royalties from the sale of his best-selling novel. 作者从他畅销小说的销售中获得了大量的版税。


The company pays royalties to the inventor for the use of his patented technology.  这家公司因使用他的专利技术而向发明人支付版税。|近义词：royalty, licensing fee |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The author receives royalties from his books every year. = 作者每年都收到他书籍的版税。
The artist receives royalties for the use of his artwork. = 艺术家因其作品的使用而获得版税。


一般过去时:
The musician received a large royalty payment last month. = 音乐家上个月收到了一笔巨额版税。
The inventor received royalties for his groundbreaking invention. = 发明家因其突破性发明而获得版税。


现在完成时:
The company has paid millions in royalties this year. = 今年公司已支付了数百万美元的版税。
She has earned significant royalties from her music career. = 她从她的音乐生涯中赚取了可观的版税。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, they had received all the royalties due to them. = 到年底，他们已经收到了应得的所有版税。
Before the film's release, the studio had already paid out substantial royalties. = 在电影上映之前，制片厂已经支付了大量的版税。


将来时:
He will receive royalties for the next five years. = 他将在未来五年内收到版税。
They will continue to pay royalties for the software license. = 他们将继续支付软件许可证的版税。


虚拟语气:
If the book were a bestseller, he would receive more royalties. = 如果这本书成为畅销书，他将获得更多版税。
I wish I were receiving more royalties for my work. = 我希望我的作品能获得更多版税。


被动语态:
Royalties are paid to the copyright holder. = 版税支付给版权持有者。
Millions of dollars in royalties were paid out to the artists. = 数百万美元的版税支付给了艺术家们。


疑问句:
How much royalties did he earn from his last album? = 他从上一张专辑中赚了多少版税？
What are the royalty rates for this particular license? = 此特定许可证的版税率是多少？


否定句:
He did not receive any royalties this quarter. = 本季度他没有收到任何版税。
The company does not pay royalties for open-source software. = 该公司不支付开源软件的版税。

词源:源自法语“royalté”，意为“王室的；王权”。  随着时间的推移，其含义演变为特指由知识产权所有者获得的付款。
