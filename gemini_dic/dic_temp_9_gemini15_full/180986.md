reimplements:/ˌriːˈɪmplɪmɛnts/| vt.|重新实现，重新执行|To implement (a system, plan, etc.) again, often with modifications or improvements.|The company reimplemented its customer relationship management system to improve efficiency. 公司重新实施了其客户关系管理系统以提高效率。|implement, reinstate|abandon, discontinue|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reimplement the software every year. = 他们每年重新实施该软件。
The team reimplements the strategy regularly. = 团队定期重新实施该战略。

一般过去时:
We reimplemented the security protocols last month. = 我们上个月重新实施了安全协议。
She reimplemented the project after the initial failure. = 初次失败后，她重新实施了该项目。

现在完成时:
They have reimplemented the system twice this year. = 今年他们已经两次重新实施了该系统。
The developers have reimplemented the algorithm for better performance. = 开发人员为了更好的性能已经重新实现了该算法。

过去完成时:
Before the launch, they had reimplemented the core features. = 在发布之前，他们已经重新实现了核心功能。
The engineers had reimplemented the code to fix the bugs. = 工程师们已经重新编写了代码来修复错误。

将来时:
We will reimplement the database next quarter. = 我们将在下个季度重新实施数据库。
They will reimplement the marketing campaign in the spring. = 他们将在春天重新实施营销活动。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would reimplement the entire system. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们将重新实施整个系统。
It's essential that they reimplement the safety measures. = 他们重新实施安全措施是必要的。

被动语态:
The software was reimplemented by a third-party vendor. = 该软件由第三方供应商重新实施。
The new features will be reimplemented in the next update. = 新功能将在下一个更新中重新实施。

疑问句:
Will they reimplement the old system? = 他们会重新实施旧系统吗？
Have they reimplemented the security updates yet? = 他们已经重新实施安全更新了吗？

否定句:
They did not reimplement the changes as planned. = 他们没有按照计划重新实施更改。
We will not reimplement the outdated technology. = 我们不会重新实施过时的技术。
