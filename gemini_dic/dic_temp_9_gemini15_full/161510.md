potently:/ˈpoʊtəntli/ | adv. |有效地；强有力地；有力地 | powerfully; effectively; strongly |例句：The drug acted potently to relieve the pain.  这种药物有效地缓解了疼痛。

例句：The argument was potently presented, leaving no room for doubt. 论点有力地陈述，不容置疑。


近义词：strongly, powerfully, effectively, forcefully

反义词：weakly, ineffectively, feebly


词源:

源自potent (adj.)  "有力的，有效的"，最终源自拉丁语 *potens, potentis  "有能力的，有权势的"，  来自 *pote "能够"。  "-ly" 是副词后缀。
