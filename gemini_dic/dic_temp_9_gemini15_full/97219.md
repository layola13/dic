inflater:/ɪnˈfleɪtər/ | n. |充气机；膨胀物 | An inflater is a device used to inflate something, typically with air or gas.  It can refer to a mechanical device or a system within a larger object. | Example: The car had a built-in tire inflater. 这辆车有一个内置的轮胎充气机。  The airbag inflater deployed rapidly in the accident. 安全气囊充气装置在事故中迅速展开。 |近义词：inflator, pump |反义词：deflator
