disimagine:/dɪsɪˈmædʒɪn/| v.|难以想象，无法构想|To fail to imagine; to be unable to form a mental image of.  The word is a relatively rare neologism,  formed by adding the prefix "dis-" (meaning "not" or "opposite of") to "imagine." It implies a strong inability to conceive of something.|例句：I disimagine a world without music. = 我无法想象一个没有音乐的世界。
I disimagine how anyone could do such a thing. = 我无法想象任何人会做出这样的事。|近义词：cannot imagine, fail to conceive,  find inconceivable|反义词：imagine, envision, conceive
