re-enslave: /ˌriːɪnˈsleɪv/ | vt. |重新奴役；再次使成为奴隶 | To force (someone) back into slavery after a period of freedom.  |例句：The freed slaves feared being re-enslaved.  解放的奴隶害怕再次被奴役。

例句：The oppressive regime attempted to re-enslave the population after a brief period of independence. 压迫性政权试图在短暂的独立时期后再次奴役人民。


近义词：re-subjugate, re-bondage

反义词：emancipate, liberate


词性：动词
