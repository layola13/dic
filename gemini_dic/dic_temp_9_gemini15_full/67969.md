facility:/fəˈsɪləti/|/n.|设施；设备；才能；容易；便利|A facility is a place, piece of equipment, or service that is designed to make a particular activity easier or more efficient.  It can also refer to someone's natural aptitude or skill in doing something.|例句：The new sports facility includes a swimming pool and a gymnasium.  新的体育设施包括一个游泳池和一个体育馆。

例句：She has a facility for languages. 她有语言天赋。


近义词：equipment, installation, aptitude, ease, convenience|反义词：difficulty, hardship, incompetence
