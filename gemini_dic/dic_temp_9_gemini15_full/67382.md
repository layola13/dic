extenuatory: /ɪkˈstɛnjuˌeɪtəri/ | adj. | 减轻罪责的；情有可原的 |  serving to lessen the seriousness or extent of something, especially an offense.  | 例句：The judge considered extenuating circumstances before sentencing.  法官在判刑前考虑了减轻罪责的情节。

例句：There were extenuating circumstances surrounding his lateness; he was involved in a car accident. 他迟到的原因情有可原；他卷入了一场车祸。


近义词：mitigating, excusing, justifying, palliating

反义词：aggravating, exacerbating

词性：形容词


词源：源自拉丁语 *extenuāre (to make thin, weaken, lessen)，由 *ex- (out, away) + *tenuāre (to make thin) 构成。  因此，其核心含义是“使变薄，减轻”。
