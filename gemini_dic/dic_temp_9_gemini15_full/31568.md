chases:/tʃeɪsɪz/| v. |追赶；追求；追捕|To pursue or follow someone or something quickly in order to catch them.|The dog chases its tail.  这只狗追着自己的尾巴。|The police are chasing the robbers. 警察正在追捕强盗。|pursue, follow, hunt|stop, halt|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chases the ball every day. 他每天都追逐球。
She chases her dreams relentlessly. 她坚持不懈地追求梦想。


一般过去时:
The cat chased a mouse yesterday. 猫昨天追了一只老鼠。
They chased the thief down the street. 他们追捕小偷穿过街道。


现在完成时:
I have chased my goals for many years. 我多年来一直追求我的目标。
She has chased away all her doubts. 她已经驱散了所有的疑虑。


过去完成时:
He had chased the rabbit into the woods before it disappeared.  兔子消失之前，他已经追到树林里去了。
They had chased the suspect for hours before apprehending him. 他们在逮捕嫌犯之前已经追捕了几个小时。


将来时:
I will chase after my dreams. 我将追逐我的梦想。
We will chase away the shadows of doubt. 我们将驱散疑虑的阴影。


虚拟语气:
If I chased my dreams, I would be happy. 如果我去追逐我的梦想，我会很快乐。
I wish I had chased that opportunity. 我真希望我当时抓住那个机会。


被动语态:
The thief was chased by the police. 小偷被警察追捕。
The ball was chased by the dog. 球被狗追着。


疑问句:
Does he chase butterflies? 他追蝴蝶吗？

否定句:
