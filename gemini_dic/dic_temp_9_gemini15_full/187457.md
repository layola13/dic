role-play:/ˈroʊlˌpleɪ/ | n. v. | 角色扮演；角色扮演游戏 |  The act of playing a role, typically in a theatrical or educational context. It can also refer to a game or activity where participants assume different roles and interact accordingly.  |例句：The teacher used role-play to help the students understand different perspectives. 老师用角色扮演帮助学生理解不同的观点。

例句：We played a role-play game where we were detectives solving a mystery. 我们玩了一个角色扮演游戏，扮演侦探破案。


近义词：acting, simulation, dramatization | 反义词：reality, observation


词性: noun, verb
