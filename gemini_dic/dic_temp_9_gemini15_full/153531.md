pentacrinite:/ˌpentəˈkraɪnaɪt/| n. |五辐射海百合化石|A fossilized crinoid, especially one with five radiating arms.  Pentacrinites are extinct marine animals related to starfish and sea urchins.  Their fossilized remains are often found in sedimentary rocks. |The limestone contained numerous pentacrinite stems.  这块石灰岩包含许多五辐射海百合茎。  |Fossil, crinoid |  (None readily available)|名词
