counterplayers:/ˈkaʊntərpleɪərz/ | n. | 对手，反方玩家 | Players who oppose or act against another player or team, often in a game or competition.  They actively try to thwart the plans and actions of their opponents.  The term is frequently used in contexts like video games, sports, or strategic negotiations. | Example: In the final chess match, the counterplayers displayed exceptional skill and strategy.  在最后的象棋比赛中，对手们展现了非凡的技巧和策略。


The counterplayers developed a cunning plan to outmaneuver their rivals.  对手们制定了一个巧妙的计划来击败他们的对手。


近义词：opponents, adversaries, antagonists | 反义词：allies, teammates, collaborators |  词性：名词
