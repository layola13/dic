hexameric:/hɛkˈsæmərɪk/| adj.|六聚体的；由六个亚基组成的|Hexameric refers to something composed of six subunits or monomers.  It is commonly used in biochemistry and molecular biology to describe the structure of proteins or other macromolecules.|例句：The protein formed a hexameric structure.  该蛋白质形成了六聚体结构。|近义词：hexametric (in poetry)|反义词：monomeric, dimeric, trimeric
