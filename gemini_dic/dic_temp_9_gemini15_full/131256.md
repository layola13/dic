noncash:/ˈnɒnkæʃ/| adj.|非现金的，非货币的|Not involving or using cash; relating to transactions or payments made without the use of physical currency.|例句：The company offers noncash benefits like health insurance and paid time off.  这家公司提供非现金福利，例如健康保险和带薪休假。

例句：Many businesses are now accepting noncash payments such as credit cards and debit cards.  许多企业现在都接受信用卡和借记卡等非现金支付。


词源:  The word is a combination of "non-" (meaning "not") and "cash".  The meaning is straightforwardly derived from its constituent parts.


近义词：cashless, credit, electronic


反义词：cash, monetary
