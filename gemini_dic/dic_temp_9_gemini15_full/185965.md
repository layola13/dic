rewarding:/rɪˈwɔːrdɪŋ/| adj.|有益的；值得做的；有回报的|Giving a feeling of satisfaction because of being useful or successful.|Teaching is a very rewarding career. 教学是一个非常有益的事业。|近义词：gratifying, fulfilling, satisfying|反义词：unrewarding, fruitless, unrewarding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The job is rewarding. = 这份工作很有成就感。
Volunteering is rewarding. = 做志愿者很有意义。


一般过去时:
The experience was very rewarding. = 这段经历非常有意义。
I found the project rewarding. = 我发现这个项目很有成就感。


现在完成时:
I have found my work incredibly rewarding. = 我发现我的工作令人难以置信地有成就感。
She has had a rewarding career in medicine. = 她从事医学事业，收获颇丰。


过去完成时:
She had already found the job rewarding before she received the promotion. = 在她升职之前，她就已经发现这份工作很有成就感了。
They had completed a rewarding project before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前完成了一个令人满意的项目。


将来时:
This new project will be very rewarding. = 这个新项目将会非常有意义。
I believe that this challenge will be incredibly rewarding. = 我相信这个挑战将非常有意义。


虚拟语气:
If I had known it would be so rewarding, I would have started sooner. = 如果我知道它会这么有意义，我就会早点开始。
I wish I had pursued a more rewarding career path. = 我希望我选择了更有意义的职业道路。


被动语态:
The effort was found to be rewarding. = 努力被证明是有意义的。
The work was considered rewarding by most employees. = 大多数员工认为这项工作很有意义。


疑问句:
Is this job rewarding? = 这份工作有意义吗？
Do you find your work rewarding? = 你觉得你的工作有意义吗？


否定句:
The task was not rewarding at all. = 这项任务一点都不值得。
