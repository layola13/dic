drosky:/ˈdrɒski/| n.|轻便马车，出租马车|A light, usually horse-drawn carriage, especially one used as a public conveyance.  It is typically a two-wheeled vehicle.|例句：He hailed a drosky to take him to the station. 他拦了一辆轻便马车去车站。|例句：The drosky rattled along the cobblestone streets. 轻便马车在鹅卵石街道上嘎吱作响。|近义词：cab, carriage, hackney carriage|反义词：limousine, private car
