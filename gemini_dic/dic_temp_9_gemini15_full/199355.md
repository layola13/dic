simmered:/ˈsɪmərd/| v.|炖，煨|To cook (food) slowly in a liquid just below the boiling point.|例句：The stew simmered gently for hours, developing rich flavors.  这道炖菜慢慢炖了几个小时，味道浓郁。

例句：I simmered the sauce until it thickened. 我把酱汁炖到浓稠为止。


近义词：stew, boil gently, poach|反义词：fry, roast, grill


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The soup simmers on the stove. = 汤在炉子上炖着。
She simmers the vegetables until tender. = 她把蔬菜炖到软烂。


一般过去时:
He simmered the sauce for thirty minutes. = 他把酱汁炖了三十分钟。
They simmered the chicken in a rich broth. = 他们把鸡肉在浓郁的肉汤里炖。


现在完成时:
I have simmered this curry for two hours. = 这道咖喱我已经炖了两个小时了。
She has simmered the apples until they are soft. = 她已经把苹果炖到软烂了。


过去完成时:
The chef had simmered the stock overnight. = 厨师把高汤炖了一整夜。
We had simmered the beans until they were almost falling apart. = 我们把豆子炖到几乎快要散架了。


将来时:
I will simmer the vegetables tomorrow. = 我明天会炖蔬菜。
She will simmer the soup for at least an hour. = 她至少会把汤炖一个小时。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would simmer the stew longer. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会把炖菜炖久一点。
I wish I had simmered the sauce for a longer time. = 我希望我把酱汁炖久一点。


被动语态:
The vegetables were simmered until tender. = 蔬菜被炖到软烂。
The sauce is being simmered slowly over low heat. = 酱汁正在小火慢炖。


疑问句:
Is the stew simmering properly? = 炖菜炖得怎么样？
Were the potatoes simmered before they were mashed? = 土豆在捣碎之前是否炖过？


否定句:
The soup didn't simmer long enough. = 汤没有炖够时间。
I didn't simmer the sauce, I fried it. = 我没有炖酱汁，我是煎的。
