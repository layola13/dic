pre-history:/ˌpriːˈhɪstəri/ | n. | 史前史，史前时代 | The period of time before written records.  It encompasses the entire span of human existence before the invention of writing, including the Paleolithic, Mesolithic, and Neolithic periods.  | Example: The discovery of ancient tools shed new light on pre-history.  史前工具的发现为史前史提供了新的线索。 | 近义词：protohistory, antiquity | 反义词：history
