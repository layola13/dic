depravers: /dɪˈpreɪvərz/ | n. |败坏者；堕落者 |Those who corrupt or debase.  People or things that cause moral corruption or decay. |例句：The depravers of society are often those in positions of power.  社会败坏者往往是那些掌权的人。

例句：The novel explores the actions of the depravers and their impact on the innocent. 这部小说探讨了败坏者的行为及其对无辜者的影响。


近义词：corrupters, perverters, degenerates, debauchers |反义词：reformers, moralists, uplifters


词源:  From "deprave" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Deprave" comes from the Old French "depraver," ultimately from the Latin "depravare," meaning "to make crooked, distort, pervert."
