polystyle:/ˈpɒliˌstaɪl/ /ˈpɑːliˌstaɪl/| adj.|多种风格的；多风格的；多种样式的|Characterized by the use of multiple styles or forms, often in art, architecture, or literature.  It implies a deliberate mixing of styles, not simply inconsistency. |The building exhibited a polystyle design, blending elements of Gothic and Renaissance architecture.|近义词：eclectic, heterogeneous, multifarious|反义词：monostyle, uniform, consistent|形容词
