negated:/ˈneɪɡeɪtɪd/| adj.|否定的，被否定的|The statement was negated by new evidence.  新的证据否定了这一说法。|近义词：denied, refuted, contradicted|反义词：affirmed, confirmed, validated|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The statement is negated by the facts. = 事实否定了这一说法。
His efforts are negated by his lack of planning. = 他缺乏计划，导致他的努力付诸东流。


一般过去时:
The rumor was negated by the official announcement. = 官方声明否定了这个谣言。
The judge negated the defendant's claim. = 法官否定了被告的声明。


现在完成时:
The progress has been negated by recent setbacks. = 最近的挫折抵消了之前的进展。
All our hard work has been negated by this mistake. = 这次错误让我们所有的努力都白费了。


过去完成时:
The previous agreement had been negated by the new contract. = 新合同否定了之前的协议。
Their efforts had been negated by unforeseen circumstances. = 不可预见的情况使他们的努力化为泡影。


将来时:
The new evidence will negate the original findings. = 新证据将否定最初的发现。
This decision will negate the benefits of the previous reforms. = 这个决定将抵消先前改革的好处。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence were negated, the case would collapse. = 如果证据被推翻，案件就会崩溃。
I wish my negative thoughts had been negated. = 我希望我的消极想法能够被消除。


被动语态:
His argument was negated by the opposing counsel. = 他的论点被对方律师驳斥了。
The effects of the medicine were negated by the patient's allergies. = 药物的作用被病人的过敏反应抵消了。


疑问句:
Is the initial hypothesis negated by the results? = 结果是否否定了最初的假设？
Were the claims negated by the subsequent investigation? = 后续调查是否否定了这些说法？


否定句:
The results were not negated by the further testing. = 进一步的测试并没有否定结果。
The treaty was not negated, despite the disagreements. = 尽管存在分歧，条约并没有被否定。


PHRASE->
