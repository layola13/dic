continentals:/ˌkɒntɪˈnentəlz/ /ˌkɑːntɪˈnentəlz/| n.|大陆人；大陆式；大陆货币|People or things from the continental United States, as opposed to those from other areas.  It can also refer to continental currency or a continental breakfast.|例句：Many Americans refer to residents of the continental US as "continentals". 许多美国人将美国大陆的居民称为“大陆人”。|例句：The hotel offered a complimentary continental breakfast. 这家酒店提供免费的欧式早餐。|近义词：mainlanders (in the context of the US)|反义词：insular (in the context of geographically separate regions)
