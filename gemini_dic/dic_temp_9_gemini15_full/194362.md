self-preference:/selfˈprɛfərəns/| n.|自我偏好，偏好自己|The tendency to favor oneself or one's own interests above others.  This can manifest in various ways, from prioritizing personal needs to exhibiting biased decision-making.|例句：Her self-preference led her to choose the project that benefited her most, even if it wasn't the best option for the team.  她的自我偏好让她选择了最有利于自己的项目，即使这对团队来说并非最佳选择。|近义词：egoism, self-interest, selfishness|反义词：altruism, selflessness|名词

词源:

The word "self-preference" is a modern compound word.  "Self" derives from Old English *self, meaning "self, same."  "Preference" comes from the Old French *prefere, from Latin *praeferre, meaning "to carry or bring before, prefer."  The combination creates a term specifically denoting a preference for oneself.
