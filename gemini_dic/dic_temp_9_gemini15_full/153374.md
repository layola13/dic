penetrators:/ˈpɛnɪtreɪtərz/| n.|穿透物；穿透者；渗透者|Objects or devices designed to penetrate a surface or material.  They can be projectiles, tools, or even biological agents.  The term often implies forceful entry.|例句：The military used armor-piercing penetrators to destroy the enemy tanks.  军队使用穿甲弹摧毁敌方坦克。

例句：Researchers developed new types of penetrators for oil extraction. 研究人员开发了用于石油开采的新型穿透器。|近义词：perforators, projectiles, probes|反义词：obstructors, blockers|名词
