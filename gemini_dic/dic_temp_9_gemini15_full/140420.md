okapis:/ˈoʊkəpiːz/ | n. | 奥卡皮 | Okapis are a type of mammal native to the rainforests of the Democratic Republic of the Congo. They are known for their long necks, zebra-like stripes on their hindquarters, and donkey-like ears.  | Example:  The okapis in the sanctuary are thriving thanks to conservation efforts. 奥卡皮保护区里的奥卡皮由于保护工作而蓬勃发展。

The okapis at the zoo were a major attraction for visitors. 动物园里的奥卡皮是游客们的主要吸引力。| 近义词: giraffe (in some contexts, due to shared characteristics) | 反义词: None  | 名词
