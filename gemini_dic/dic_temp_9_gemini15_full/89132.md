homebrew:/ˈhoʊmˌbruː/| adj. n. v.|自制的；家酿的；自己动手制作|Something made at home, especially alcoholic beverages.  It can also refer to the act of making something at home or the software package manager.|例句：He enjoys homebrewing his own beer. 他喜欢自己酿造啤酒。

例句：I installed the software using Homebrew. 我使用Homebrew安装了这个软件。

近义词：homemade, handcrafted, self-made |反义词：commercial, mass-produced
