midmarket:/ˈmɪdˌmɑːrkɪt/ | adj., n. | 中端市场，中等价位 | Relating to or denoting goods, services, or companies that are neither high-end nor low-end in terms of price or quality.  |例句：The company targets the midmarket with its new line of affordable laptops. 这家公司将其新款经济型笔记本电脑的目标市场定位于中端市场。


The midmarket is a strong competitor in the industry. 中端市场是该行业中强大的竞争者。|近义词：moderate, average, mainstream |反义词：high-end, luxury, low-end, budget
