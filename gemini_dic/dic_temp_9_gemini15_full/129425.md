nominators:/ˈnɑːmɪneɪtərz/ | n. |提名者，推荐者 |Those who nominate someone for a position or award.  People who put forward names for consideration. |The nominators for the award were all respected figures in the field.  提名奖项的人都是该领域受人尊敬的人物。


The nominators for the best picture award were very diverse in their backgrounds. 最佳影片奖的提名者背景非常多元化。


近义词：proposers, recommenders
反义词：nominees (反义词更像是对应词，而非真正反义)

词源:
From nominate + -or.  "Nominate" ultimately derives from the Latin "nominare," meaning "to name."  The suffix "-or" indicates the agent or doer of the action.
