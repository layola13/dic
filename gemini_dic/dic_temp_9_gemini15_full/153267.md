penalties:/ˈpɛnəltiz/| n.|处罚，惩罚；不利因素|Penalties are punishments imposed for breaking rules or laws.  Penalties can also refer to disadvantages or drawbacks.|例句：There are heavy penalties for speeding. 超速行驶会受到严厉处罚。

例句：The team faced several penalties during the game.  该队在比赛中面临多次处罚。

近义词：punishments, sanctions, fines, forfeitures

反义词：rewards, benefits
