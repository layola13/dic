self-witnesses:/ˈsɛlf ˈwɪtnɪsɪz/| n.|目击证人；亲历者|People who have personally witnessed an event and can provide firsthand testimony about it.  This term often implies a lack of other witnesses, or that the self-witness is the primary source of information.|例句：The police relied heavily on the self-witness's account of the accident.  警方严重依赖这位目击证人的事故描述。

The only self-witness to the crime was the victim herself.  这起犯罪的唯一目击证人是受害者本人。|近义词：eyewitnesses, firsthand accounts|反义词：indirect witnesses, hearsay accounts
