deduplication:/ˌdiːˌdjuːplɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |数据重复数据删除，去重|The process of identifying and removing duplicate data.  This is often used in databases, data storage, and data backup systems to save space and improve efficiency. |Example:  Data deduplication is crucial for efficient cloud storage.  数据重复数据删除对于高效的云存储至关重要。|近义词：duplicate removal, data reduction|反义词：data duplication, redundancy|名词
