foolish:/ˈfuːlɪʃ/| adj.|愚蠢的，傻的|foolish means lacking good sense or judgment; unwise. It implies a lack of intelligence or common sense, often leading to silly or unwise actions.|例句：It was foolish of him to spend all his money. 他把所有的钱都花光了，真是愚蠢。|例句：That's a foolish idea. 那是个愚蠢的主意。|近义词：silly, stupid, unwise, absurd|反义词：wise, intelligent, sensible, clever|
