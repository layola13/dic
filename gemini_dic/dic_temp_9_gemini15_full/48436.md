deduction:/dɪˈdʌkʃən/ | n. | 推论；演绎；扣除；减除 | The process of reaching a conclusion by reasoning from general principles or evidence.  It can also refer to the amount subtracted or deducted. | Example:  The detective made a deduction based on the evidence.  侦探根据证据做出了推论。

The police made a deduction from his wages for the damage.  警察从他的工资中扣除了损坏赔偿。| 近义词：inference, conclusion, implication, subtraction | 反义词：addition, induction


USAGE->

His deduction is usually accurate. = 他的推论通常很准确。
The company makes deductions from salaries for tax. = 公司从工资中扣除税款。
