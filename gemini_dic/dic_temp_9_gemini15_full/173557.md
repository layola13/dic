quasi-heroically:/ˈkwɑːziˌhɪəˈrɒkɪkli/| adj. |近乎英雄般地，类似英雄般地 |  Describes an action or behavior that possesses some, but not all, of the characteristics associated with heroic deeds. It suggests a level of bravery, selflessness, or determination that is admirable but perhaps falls short of true heroism.  |例句：He acted quasi-heroically, rushing into the burning building to save the cat, despite his fear of fire. 他近乎英雄般地冲进燃烧的建筑物去救猫，尽管他害怕火。

例句：She tackled the problem quasi-heroically, working tirelessly for days to find a solution, even though it was beyond her job description. 她以近乎英雄般的姿态解决了这个问题，日夜不停地工作了好几天以找到解决方案，即使这超出了她的工作范围。


近义词：bravely, valiantly, courageously, gallantly


反义词：cowardly, timidly, fearfully


词性：形容词
