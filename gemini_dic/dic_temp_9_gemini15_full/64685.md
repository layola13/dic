escapee: /ɪˈskeɪpiː/ | n. | 逃亡者，逃犯 | A person who has escaped from a place of confinement or a dangerous situation.  |例句：The escapee was eventually caught and returned to prison.  逃亡者最终被抓住并送回监狱。  The escapee from the burning building was treated for smoke inhalation. 从燃烧的建筑物中逃出来的人接受了吸入烟雾的治疗。|近义词：fugitive, runaway |反义词：captive, prisoner | 名词
