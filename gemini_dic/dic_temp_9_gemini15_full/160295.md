pooh-poohing: /ˌpuːˈpuːɪŋ/ | v. |嗤之以鼻，不屑一顾 | To express contempt or disbelief; to scorn or dismiss something as unimportant or unworthy.  |例句：He pooh-poohed my suggestion, saying it was impractical. 他对我的建议嗤之以鼻，说它不切实际。 He pooh-poohed the idea of climate change. 他不屑一顾气候变化的想法。|近义词：scoff at, sneer at, dismiss, disdain, belittle |反义词：approve of, endorse, praise, admire
