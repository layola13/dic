firepower:/ˈfaɪərpɑːʊər/| n. |火力，战斗力|The destructive power of artillery or other weapons.  It can also refer to the overall offensive capability of a military force or even a political entity. |例句：The army boasted superior firepower. 这支军队拥有强大的火力。

例句：Their firepower overwhelmed the enemy defenses.  他们的火力压倒了敌人的防御。


近义词：offensive capability, striking power, military might|反义词：weakness, vulnerability, defenselessness|词性:名词
