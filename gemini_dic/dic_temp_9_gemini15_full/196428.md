serpette:/sɛrˈpɛt/| n.|小镰刀，弯刀|A small sickle or hooked knife, typically used for harvesting or pruning.  It is often smaller and more delicate than a scythe. |例句：The gardener used a serpette to prune the rose bushes. 园丁用一把小镰刀修剪玫瑰花丛。

例句：He carefully harvested the grapes using a sharp serpette. 他用一把锋利的小镰刀仔细地收割葡萄。


近义词：sickle, hook, billhook|反义词：None specifically, but words describing large cutting tools could be considered antonyms. |词性：n.
