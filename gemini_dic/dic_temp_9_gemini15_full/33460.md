chuting:/ˈtʃuːtɪŋ/| n.|跳伞；滑降；(用溜槽)搬运|The act of descending rapidly, typically from an aircraft or a high place, using a parachute or similar device, or the process of transporting something down a chute.|例句：The parachuting instructor demonstrated the proper techniques. 跳伞教练演示了正确的技巧。

例句：The factory uses a chute system for transporting materials. 工厂使用溜槽系统运输材料。


近义词：parachuting, sliding, rappelling|反义词：ascending, climbing|名词
