It's essentially a type of chloroplast with a distinctive, often hand-like or branched, shape.|Example: The research focused on the unique morphology and function of the chiroplasts in the newly discovered species.  研究的重点是新发现物种中掌状体的独特形态和功能。|近义词：chloroplast (though chiroplast emphasizes specific morphology)|反义词：无|名词
