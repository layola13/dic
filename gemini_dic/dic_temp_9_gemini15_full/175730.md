rappelled:/ræˈpɛld/| v.|绳降；用绳索下降|To descend a steep surface or cliff using ropes.|They rappelled down the cliff face. 他们沿悬崖峭壁绳降而下。|descend, abseil|ascend, climb|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rappels down the mountain every weekend. 他每个周末都从山上绳降而下。
They rappel into the cave to explore. 他们绳降进入洞穴进行探险。


一般过去时:
We rappelled down the canyon yesterday. 我们昨天沿着峡谷绳降而下。
She rappelled from the helicopter to the ground. 她从直升机上绳降到地面。


现在完成时:
He has rappelled many times in his life. 他一生中多次绳降过。
