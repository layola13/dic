dolts:/dɒlts/ | n. |笨蛋，傻瓜 | A dolt is a stupid or foolish person.  They lack intelligence or common sense. | Example: He's such a dolt; he can't even change a tire. 他真是个笨蛋，连换个轮胎都不会。 |近义词：idiot, fool, moron, buffoon |反义词：genius, intellectual, brilliant


USAGE->

He acts like a dolt. = 他表现得像个傻瓜。
That man is a dolt. = 那个男人是个笨蛋。

She was a dolt in that situation. = 在那种情况下，她表现得很傻。
