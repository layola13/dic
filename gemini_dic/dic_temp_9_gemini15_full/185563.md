revealer: /rɪˈviːlə(r)/ | n. |揭露者，显示器| a person or thing that reveals something.  Someone or something that makes something known or visible. |例句：The journalist was a revealer of government corruption.  记者是政府腐败的揭露者。

The new software is a powerful revealer of hidden patterns in the data.  这个新软件是揭示数据中隐藏模式的强大工具。|近义词：discloser, exposer, informer |反义词：concealer, hider
