clatters:/ˈklætərz/| v.|发出铿锵声，发出哗啦声|To make a rattling sound, as of hard objects striking together.|The dishes clattered as she dropped them.  她把盘子掉在地上，发出叮当声。|The stones clattered down the hill. 石头哗啦啦地滚下山坡。|rattle, bang, crash|silence, hush

USAGE->

一般现在时:
The train clatters past the station. = 火车哐当哐当地驶过车站。
The cutlery clatters in the kitchen. = 餐具在厨房里发出叮当声。

一般过去时:
The old clock clattered and then stopped. = 旧钟发出当当声然后停了。
The window clattered shut in the wind. = 窗户在风中砰地关上了。

现在完成时:
The wind has clattered the windows all night. = 风整夜都在拍打窗户。
The storm has clattered the roof tiles. = 暴风雨打碎了屋顶瓦片。

过去完成时:
The horse had clattered down the cobbled street before I saw him. = 我看到他之前，马儿已经哒哒地跑过鹅卵石街道了。
The children had clattered the toys all over the floor before their mother came home. = 孩子们在妈妈回家之前把玩具扔得满地都是。

将来时:
The falling rocks will clatter down the mountainside. = 落石将哗啦啦地滚下山坡。
The thunderstorm will clatter the windows throughout the night. = 雷雨将整夜拍打窗户。


虚拟语气:
If the cups were dropped, they would clatter on the floor. = 如果杯子掉了，它们会在地板上发出叮当声。
I wish the old wagon wouldn't clatter so much. = 我真希望这辆旧马车不要发出那么大的声响。


被动语态:
The windows were clattered by the hail. = 窗户被冰雹打得砰砰响。
The dishes were clattered when the cat jumped on the table. = 猫跳到桌子上时，盘子发出叮当声。


疑问句:
Did you hear the stones clatter down the hill? = 你听到石头滚下山的声音了吗？
Does the old house clatter every time the wind blows? = 每当刮风时，那座老房子都会发出响声吗？


否定句:
The rain didn't clatter against the windows tonight. = 今晚雨水没有拍打窗户。
The birds didn't clatter their beaks against the feeder. = 鸟儿没有用它们的喙敲打喂食器。

The clatter of machinery = 机器轰鸣声
