overinform:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfɔːrm/ | vt. | 提供过多信息；信息过载 | To give someone more information than they want or need, often leading to confusion or annoyance.  | 例句：The marketing materials overinformed the customers, leading to confusion about the product's features.  市场营销材料信息过多，导致顾客对产品功能感到困惑。  |  例句：She overinformed her son about the accident, causing him unnecessary distress. 她向儿子提供了过多关于事故的信息，给他造成了不必要的痛苦。| 近义词：overload, inundate, overwhelm | 反义词：underinform, simplify
