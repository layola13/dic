gymnoblastic:/ˌdʒɪmnəˈblæstɪk/| adj. |裸芽的；无被的| Relating to or characterized by gymnoblastic hydroids, which lack a protective perisarc (a chitinous covering).  These hydroids typically have a naked, unprotected polyp. |The gymnoblastic hydroids are readily distinguished from the calyptoblastic hydroids by the lack of a perisarc.| 裸芽水螅纲很容易通过缺乏围鞘来与被囊水螅纲区分。|近义词：naked, uncovered|反义词：calyptoblastic
