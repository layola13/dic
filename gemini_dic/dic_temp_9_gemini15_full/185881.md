revolt's:/rɪˈvoʊltz/ | n. | 反叛；起义 | The revolt's impact on the country was significant.  这场叛乱对国家的影响是重大的。| 近义词：rebellion, uprising, insurrection | 反义词：obedience, submission | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The revolt causes widespread unrest. = 这场叛乱导致了广泛的不稳定。
His revolt against authority is well-known. = 他反抗权威是众所周知的。


一般过去时:
The peasant revolt ended in failure. = 农民起义以失败告终。
The city witnessed a bloody revolt last year. = 去年这座城市经历了一场血腥的叛乱。


现在完成时:
The people have suffered greatly from the revolt. = 人民在这场叛乱中遭受了巨大的痛苦。
The effects of the revolt are still being felt today. = 叛乱的影响至今仍在持续。


过去完成时:
The king had anticipated the revolt. = 国王预料到了这场叛乱。
They had been planning the revolt for months. = 他们已经策划这场叛乱数月了。


将来时:
Experts predict a further revolt if the government doesn't change its policy. = 如果政府不改变政策，专家预测还会发生进一步的叛乱。
The situation could easily lead to a revolt. = 这种情况很容易导致叛乱。


虚拟语气:
If the people had not revolted, the country would still be under oppressive rule. = 如果人民没有起义，国家现在仍然会处于压迫统治之下。
I wish the revolt hadn't caused so much damage. = 我希望这场叛乱没有造成这么大的破坏。


被动语态:
The revolt was brutally suppressed. = 叛乱被残酷地镇压了。
The causes of the revolt were investigated thoroughly. = 叛乱的原因受到了彻底调查。


疑问句:
When did the revolt begin? = 叛乱是什么时候开始的？
What were the main causes of the revolt? = 叛乱的主要原因是什么？


否定句:
The revolt did not achieve its goals. = 叛乱没有实现其目标。
There was no sign of an imminent revolt. = 没有迹象表明即将发生叛乱。


PHRASE->
revolt against = 反抗…
in revolt = 在反抗中
open revolt = 公开反抗
armed revolt = 武装叛乱
student revolt = 学生运动


词源:
revolt源自于古法语revolter，意思是“反转，旋转”，最终源于拉丁语volvere，意思是“滚动，旋转”。  它的含义逐渐演变为“反抗，造反”。
