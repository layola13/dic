frillers:/ˈfrɪlərz/ | n. |褶饰；(尤指衣物上的)荷叶边；炫耀者 | A friller is something that adds frills, often referring to decorative ruffles or gathers on clothing or other items.  It can also refer to a person who is showy or ostentatious. |例句：The dress had delicate frillers around the neckline. 这件裙子领口周围有精致的荷叶边。 The politician was known for his frillers and grand gestures.  这位政客以其炫耀和夸张的姿态而闻名。|近义词：ruffles, flounces, furbelows, show-offs |反义词：plainness, simplicity
