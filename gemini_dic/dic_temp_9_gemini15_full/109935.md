liberal:/ˈlɪbərəl/| adj., n.|自由主义的；自由的；慷慨的；宽容的|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "liberal" can describe someone or something that is open to new behavior or opinions and willing to discard traditional values.  It can also refer to generous giving or a generous amount. In politics, a liberal typically advocates for social progress and individual rights.

例句:
He has a liberal attitude towards social issues. 他对社会问题持自由主义态度。

She made a liberal donation to the charity. 她向慈善机构慷慨捐款。


近义词:
generous, tolerant, open-minded, progressive, broad-minded

反义词:
conservative, strict, intolerant, narrow-minded, stingy


词性:adjective, noun
