kiss:/kɪs/| n., vt.|亲吻，轻吻|The act of pressing one's lips against another person's lips, cheek, or other body part as a sign of affection, greeting, or farewell.  It can also refer to a light touch or contact.|例句：She gave her mother a kiss on the cheek. 她亲吻了她母亲的脸颊。|例句：He kissed her goodnight. 他吻了她道晚安。|近义词：peck, smooch, osculation|反义词：N/A|动词
