preexperimental: /ˌpriːɪkspɛrɪˈmɛntəl/ | adj. | 预实验的；实验前的 | Relating to or denoting an experiment that is preliminary to a more extensive investigation.  It often involves a small-scale trial run to test the feasibility and design of a larger experiment. |  The preexperimental design was used to refine the methodology before the main study. 预实验设计用于在主要研究之前改进方法。  | 近义词: preliminary, preparatory | 反义词:  post-experimental, conclusive
