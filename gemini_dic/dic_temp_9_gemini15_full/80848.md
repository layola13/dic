gourd:/ɡɔːrd/| n. |葫芦；瓢；瓜类植物 |A gourd is the fruit of any of various plants, typically having a hard rind and a fleshy interior.  Many gourds are used for containers or decoration. |例句：The farmer grew a variety of gourds in his field.  这位农民在他的田里种植了各种葫芦。

例句：She carved a beautiful face into the gourd. 她在一个葫芦上雕刻了一张漂亮的脸。

近义词：bottle gourd, calabash, pumpkin, squash |反义词：无
