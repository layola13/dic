lummox:/ˈlʌməks/| n.|笨手笨脚的人，笨蛋|A clumsy, awkward person, especially a woman.  A lummox is typically characterized by their lack of grace and coordination. They might be perceived as unintelligent or insensitive.|例句：He called her a lummox for tripping over the rug. 他因为她绊倒地毯而称她为笨手笨脚的人。  She felt like a complete lummox trying to assemble the furniture. 她试图组装家具时感觉自己像个十足的笨蛋。|近义词：clutz, oaf, goof, blunderer|反义词：graceful, elegant, dexterous
