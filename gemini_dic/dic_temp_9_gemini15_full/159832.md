polyhalite:/ˈpɒliˌheɪlaɪt/| n. |多卤石|A hydrous potassium magnesium calcium sulfate mineral. It is a naturally occurring evaporite mineral, usually found in layered deposits along with other evaporites such as halite and anhydrite. Polyhalite is an important source of potassium fertilizer. |The discovery of large polyhalite deposits has significantly impacted the potassium fertilizer market. 多卤石大型矿床的发现极大地影响了钾肥市场。|sylvite, kainite|  (no direct antonym)|名词
