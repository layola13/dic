flocculent:/ˈflɒkjʊlənt/| adj.|絮状的，绒毛状的，蓬松的 |  Descriptive of a substance that has a fluffy or woolly appearance, consisting of small, loose tufts or flakes.  It often refers to textures that are light, airy, and somewhat unorganized.  | Example: The clouds were flocculent and white, resembling cotton wool.  云朵蓬松洁白，像棉花糖一样。 The precipitate formed a flocculent mass in the bottom of the beaker. 沉淀物在烧杯底部形成了絮状团块。|近义词：fluffy, woolly, flaky, downy |反义词：compact, dense, solid, smooth
