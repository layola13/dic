horseshoeing:/ˈhɔːrsʃuːɪŋ/ | n. v. | 马蹄铁钉制；钉马蹄 | The act of fitting horseshoes onto a horse's hooves.  It involves shaping the horseshoe to fit the hoof, and then nailing it securely in place.  |例句：The farrier is skilled in horseshoeing.  = 这位铁匠擅长钉马蹄。

例句：He learned the craft of horseshoeing from his father. = 他从他父亲那里学会了钉马蹄的手艺。


近义词：shoeing

反义词：无


词性：名词和动词
