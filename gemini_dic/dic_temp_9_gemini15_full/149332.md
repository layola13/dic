pacesetters:/ˈpeɪsˌsɛtərz/ | n. |领跑者，开拓者，先驱 |People or things that set the pace or standard for others to follow.  They are the leaders in a field, setting the trends and influencing others. |Example:  The company is a pacesetter in the renewable energy sector. 这家公司是可再生能源领域的领跑者。

The innovative start-up is a pacesetter in the tech industry. 这家创新型初创企业是科技行业的先驱。


近义词：leaders, pioneers, innovators, trendsetters, frontrunners

反义词：followers, laggards, stragglers


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company pacesets the market. = 这家公司引领市场。
These athletes are pacesetters in their sport. = 这些运动员是他们运动项目中的领跑者。


一般过去时:
She paced the market last year. = 她去年引领了市场。
That team paced the league in scoring. = 那支球队在得分方面领先联盟。


现在完成时:
They have paced the industry for decades. = 几十年来，他们一直引领着这个行业。
Our company has paced the market in innovation. = 我们公司在创新方面引领了市场。


过去完成时:
