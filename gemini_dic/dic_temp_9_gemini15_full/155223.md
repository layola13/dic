phaenomenon:/fəˈnɒmɪnən/| n.|现象|A fact or situation that is observed to exist or happen, especially one whose cause or explanation is in question.|The aurora borealis is a spectacular phenomenon. 极光是一种壮观的现象。The rise in sea levels is a worrying phenomenon. 海平面上升是一个令人担忧的现象。|近义词：occurrence, event, incident|反义词：non-event


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense changes are not applicable.  The word itself will remain unchanged.


PHRASE->
a natural phenomenon 自然现象
a strange phenomenon 奇怪的现象
a social phenomenon 社会现象
a global phenomenon 全球现象
