coactions:/koʊˈækʃənz/ | n. | 共同作用；协同作用 | The interplay of multiple factors or agents working together.  Coactions are often complex and difficult to predict, as the outcome depends on the interaction of various elements.  For example, the coactions of genes and environment determine an organism's phenotype. |例句：The success of the project depends on the effective coactions of all team members.  项目的成功取决于所有团队成员的有效协同作用。

The complex coactions of climate change, deforestation, and human activity are leading to biodiversity loss. 气候变化、森林砍伐和人类活动的复杂共同作用正在导致生物多样性丧失。|近义词：interactions, synergies, collaborations, joint actions |反义词：independent actions, isolated effects
