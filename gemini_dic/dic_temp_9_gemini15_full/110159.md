liege:/liːdʒ/| n. |封君，领主|A liege is a feudal lord to whom someone owes allegiance and service.  The term emphasizes the reciprocal relationship, implying both obligation and protection.|Example:  The knight swore fealty to his liege lord.  骑士向他的封君宣誓效忠。|近义词：lord, sovereign, master|反义词：vassal, subject


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is the liege of many knights. = 他是许多骑士的封君。
My liege requires my immediate attention. = 我的领主需要我立即的关注。


一般过去时:
The knight served his liege faithfully. = 骑士忠实地侍奉他的封君。
The vassal pledged allegiance to his former liege. = 臣民向他以前的领主宣誓效忠。
