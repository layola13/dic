disgorgement:/dɪsˈɡɔːrdʒmənt/ | n. | 呕吐；吐出；(尤指非法获得的)返还；交出 | The act of disgorging; the ejection of something from the stomach or bowels.  It also refers to the surrender or return of something, especially something illegally obtained. | 例句：The doctor prescribed medication to induce disgorgement. 医生开了药来催吐。

The company was ordered to make disgorgement of its illegally obtained profits. 公司被命令返还其非法获得的利润。|近义词：vomiting, regurgitation, restitution, surrender, repayment |反义词：retention, withholding, keeping
