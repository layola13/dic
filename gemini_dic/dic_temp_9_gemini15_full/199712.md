siphons:/ˈsaɪfən/| n. v.|虹吸管；虹吸；用虹吸管抽取|A siphon is a tube used to convey liquid upwards from a reservoir and then downwards to a lower level of its own accord.  It works by creating a pressure difference between the two ends of the tube.|例句：We used a siphon to transfer the wine from the barrel to the bottle. 我们用虹吸管把酒从桶里转移到瓶子里。

例句：The gardener used a siphon to empty the pond. 园丁用虹吸管清空了池塘。


近义词：tube, pipe | 反义词：None  |词性：n. v.
