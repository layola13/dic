impression:/ɪmˈprɛʃən/| n. |印象，感觉；效果；印记|The overall effect or feeling created by something or someone.  It can also refer to a mark made by pressing something onto a surface. |例句：I had a very positive impression of him after our first meeting.  第一次见面后，我对他的印象非常好。|例句：The stamp left a clear impression on the paper.  邮票在纸上留下了清晰的印记。|近义词：feeling, opinion, effect, impact|反义词：misconception, misunderstanding


USAGE->

一般现在时:
His speech makes a strong impression on the audience. = 他的演讲给听众留下了深刻的印象。
The new design leaves a lasting impression. = 新设计给人留下持久的印象。

一般过去时:
The movie made a deep impression on me. = 这部电影给我留下了深刻的印象。
Her performance left a lasting impression on the judges. = 她的表演给评委留下了深刻的印象。

现在完成时:
This experience has made a profound impression on my life. =  这段经历对我的生活产生了深远的影响。
The book has left a lasting impression on readers. = 这本书给读者留下了持久的印象。

过去完成时:
I had already formed a negative impression of him before we even spoke. =  在我们说话之前，我已经对他形成了负面印象。
She had never made such a strong impression on anyone before. = 她以前从未给任何人留下如此深刻的印象。

将来时:
I think this project will make a good impression on the client. = 我认为这个项目会给客户留下好印象。
His new book is expected to make a big impression. = 他的新书有望引起轰动。

虚拟语气:
If I had been more careful, I could have made a better impression. = 如果我更小心一点，我本可以留下更好的印象。
I wish I had made a better impression at the interview. = 我希望我在面试中能留下更好的印象。

被动语态:
A strong impression was made on the audience by his speech. = 他的演讲给听众留下了深刻的印象。(被动语态)
A favorable impression was created by the new product. = 新产品给人留下了良好的印象。(被动语态)

疑问句:
What impression did you get from the meeting? = 你从会议上得到了什么印象？
What kind of impression do you want to make? = 你想留下什么样的印象？

否定句:
The play failed to make any impression on the audience. = 这部戏未能给观众留下任何印象。
He didn't make a good impression on his boss. = 他没有给老板留下好印象。
