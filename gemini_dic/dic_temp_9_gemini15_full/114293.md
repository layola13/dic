mainprise:/ˈmeɪnpraɪz/| n.| 保释；保证；担保 | The act of becoming surety for someone;  the undertaking of responsibility for another's appearance in court.  It involves a third party guaranteeing the appearance of an accused person in court. |Example: The judge accepted mainprise for the accused, allowing him to be released pending trial.  法官接受了对被告的担保，允许他在审判前获释。|近义词：bail, surety, bond |反义词：imprisonment, confinement |名词
