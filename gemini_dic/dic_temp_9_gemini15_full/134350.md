noninteracting: /ˌnɑːnɪn.təˈræktɪŋ/ | adj. | 不相互作用的，独立的 | Not interacting or influencing each other.  This term often describes systems or particles that do not affect each other's behavior.  | Example:  The particles in an ideal gas are considered noninteracting. = 理想气体中的粒子被认为是不相互作用的。 The two departments operated in a completely noninteracting manner. = 这两个部门以完全不相互影响的方式运作。|近义词：independent, separate, unrelated | 反义词：interacting, interdependent, correlated
