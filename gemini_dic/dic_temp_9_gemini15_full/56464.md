dolphinarium:/ˌdɒlfɪˈneəriəm/| n. |海豚馆 |A dolphinarium is a facility where dolphins are kept and trained for display and entertainment, often including other marine animals.  It's similar to an aquarium but focuses specifically on dolphins and their performances. |Example: The children were thrilled to see the dolphins perform tricks at the dolphinarium. 孩子们在海豚馆看到海豚表演特技时非常兴奋。|近义词：aquarium (if including other marine life), marine park|反义词：None
