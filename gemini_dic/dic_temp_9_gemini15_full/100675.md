intoxicatedness:/ɪnˌtɒksɪˈkeɪtɪdnəs/| n. |醉酒状态；陶醉状态；兴奋状态 |The state of being intoxicated;  a feeling of excitement or elation often associated with alcohol consumption or other intoxicating substances.  It can also refer to a state of being overwhelmed by a strong emotion or experience.|例句：His intoxicatedness was evident in his slurred speech and unsteady gait. 他的醉酒状态从他含糊不清的言语和不稳的步态中显而易见。

例句：The intoxicatedness of the crowd at the concert was infectious. 音乐会上人群的兴奋状态是具有感染力的。|近义词：inebriation, inebriety, drunkenness, exhilaration |反义词：sobriety, temperance
