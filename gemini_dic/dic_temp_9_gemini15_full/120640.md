mis-parsed:/ˌmɪsˈpɑːzd/| adj.| 解析错误的；误解的 | Incorrectly analyzed or interpreted, especially in the context of computer language processing or linguistic analysis.  |Example: The sentence was mis-parsed by the machine translation software, resulting in a nonsensical output.  机器翻译软件错误地解析了这个句子，导致输出结果毫无意义。 |近义词： misinterpreted, wrongly analyzed, incorrectly processed |反义词： correctly parsed, accurately interpreted |形容词
