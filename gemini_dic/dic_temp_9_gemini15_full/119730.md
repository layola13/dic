midtone:/ˈmɪdˌtoʊn/| adj. | 中音的；中等音调的 |  Relating to or denoting a tone or color of medium lightness or darkness.  A midtone is a color that is neither very light nor very dark. In music, it refers to a pitch that is neither high nor low. | 例句：The painting used a range of midtones to create a subtle effect. 这幅画使用了各种中等色调来营造一种微妙的效果。

The singer's voice has a pleasant midtone quality.  这位歌手的声音具有令人愉悦的中音品质。| 近义词：medium, intermediate, average | 反义词：high-pitched, low-pitched, dark, light
