sawing:/ˈsɔːɪŋ/| v.|锯，锯开|The act of cutting something with a saw.  It can also refer to the sound produced by sawing.|例句：He was sawing wood in the backyard. 他在后院锯木头。

例句：The rhythmic sawing of the wood was soothing.  锯木头的有节奏的声音很舒缓。


近义词：cutting, severing |反义词：joining, uniting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He saws wood every Saturday. = 他每个星期六都锯木头。
She saws the bread for breakfast. = 她早餐锯面包。


一般过去时:
I sawed the branch from the tree. = 我把树枝从树上锯掉了。
They sawed the log into pieces. = 他们把原木锯成碎片。


现在完成时:
He has sawed all the wood. = 他已经把所有的木头都锯好了。
She has sawed through the thick plank. = 她已经锯穿了厚厚的木板。


过去完成时:
He had sawed the wood before the rain started. = 下雨前他已经把木头锯好了。
They had sawed through the metal before the welder arrived. = 焊接工来之前，他们已经锯穿了金属。


将来时:
I will saw the board tomorrow. = 我明天将锯这块木板。
We will saw the tree down next week. = 我们下周将把树锯倒。


虚拟语气:
If I had a saw, I would saw the wood. = 如果我有一把锯子，我就会锯木头。
I wish I had sawed the wood yesterday. = 我真希望昨天就把木头锯好了。


被动语态:
The wood was sawed into small pieces. = 木头被锯成小块。
The tree was being sawed down by the workers. = 工人们正在把树锯倒。


疑问句:
Are you sawing the wood now? = 你现在正在锯木头吗？
Is he sawing the tree trunk? = 他正在锯树干吗？


否定句:
He didn't saw the wood. = 他没有锯木头。
She doesn't saw wood often. = 她不经常锯木头。


PHRASE->
sawing a log = 锯木头
sawing wood = 锯木头 (idiomatic for pretending to be busy)

back-sawing = 用背锯锯
