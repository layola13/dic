seaborn:/ˈsiːbɔːrn/| adj.|海生的；海产的；|Derived from "sea" and "born," indicating something originating from or related to the sea.  It describes things that are born or produced in the sea, or have a close connection to the sea.|例句：Seaborn creatures are adapted to survive in saltwater environments. 海生生物适应在咸水环境中生存。

例句：The restaurant specializes in seaborn delicacies. 这家餐馆专门供应海鲜美味。


近义词：marine, aquatic, oceanic|反义词：terrestrial
