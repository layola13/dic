nebulas:/ˈnɛbjələz/| n.|星云|A nebula is a giant cloud of gas and dust in space.  Nebulas are often the birthplaces of stars.|例句：The Hubble Space Telescope has captured stunning images of distant nebulas. 哈勃太空望远镜拍摄到了遥远星云的令人惊叹的图像。|例句：Many nebulas are visible to the naked eye under dark, clear skies. 在黑暗晴朗的天空下，许多星云肉眼可见。|近义词：cloud, cosmic cloud|反义词:star, planet
