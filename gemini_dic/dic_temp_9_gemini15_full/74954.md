friar:/ˈfraɪər/| n. |修士，僧侣 |A friar is a member of a religious order, especially one of the mendicant orders.  Friars typically live in monasteries and dedicate their lives to religious service, often involving preaching, teaching, and charitable work.  |Example: The friar preached a sermon on compassion.  这位修士做了一场关于慈悲的布道。|近义词：monk, religious |反义词：layperson
