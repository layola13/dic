pre-production:/priːprəˈdʌkʃən/ | n. |前期制作，前期准备 | The stage of filmmaking or other creative projects before principal photography or recording begins, involving planning, scripting, casting, location scouting, and other preparatory work.  | Example: The pre-production phase of the movie took six months.  前期制作阶段耗时六个月。|近义词：preparation, planning, development |反义词：post-production
