fingerling:/ˈfɪŋɡərˌlɪŋ/| n.|小指鱼；幼苗；小手指|A small fish, especially a young fish.  Also, a small plant, typically a newly grown one, especially a young potato. |例句：We caught several fingerlings while fishing in the stream. 我们在小溪里钓鱼时钓到几条小鱼。

例句：The gardener planted fingerling potatoes in the spring. 园丁在春天种植了小土豆苗。|近义词：fry, seedling, sapling|反义词：adult fish, mature plant|词性:名词
