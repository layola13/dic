misfocuses:/ˌmɪsˈfoʊkəsɪz/| v.| 焦点错位；失焦；(注意力)不集中 |To fail to focus correctly; to be out of focus.  It implies a lack of clarity or precision, either in a literal sense (like a camera lens) or a figurative sense (like attention or thought).| 例句：The photographer misfocuses his lens, resulting in a blurry image. 摄影师镜头失焦，导致图像模糊。

The student misfocuses on minor details and misses the main point of the lecture.  学生注意力不集中在细节上，错过了讲座的要点。| 近义词：blur, defocus, unfocus,  misdirect (figurative)| 反义词：focus, sharpen, concentrate
