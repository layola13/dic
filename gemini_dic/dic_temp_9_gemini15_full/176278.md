re-defies: /ˌriːdɪˈfaɪz/ | v. |重新定义，再次挑战 | To define or challenge something again; to redefine something previously defined or to challenge a previously accepted definition or concept.  |例句：The artist re-defines the boundaries of traditional painting.  艺术家重新定义了传统绘画的界限。


The scientific breakthrough re-defines our understanding of the universe.  这一科学突破重新定义了我们对宇宙的理解。


近义词：redefine, reinterpret, reconsider, challenge


反义词：accept, confirm, endorse


词性：动词
