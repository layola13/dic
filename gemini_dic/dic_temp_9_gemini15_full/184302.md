resowing:/riːˈsoʊɪŋ/| v.| 再播种；重新播种 |The act of sowing seeds again, typically after a previous crop has failed or to improve the yield.|例句：The farmer is resowing the field after the hailstorm damaged the initial planting.  农民在冰雹破坏了最初的种植后正在重新播种田地。


例句：After the drought, the farmer decided to resow the entire field with a more drought-resistant variety. 干旱过后，农民决定用更抗旱的品种重新播种整个田地。


近义词：replanting, sowing again, reseeding


反义词：harvesting, reaping

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resows his fields every spring. = 他每年春天都重新播种他的田地。
The gardener resows the seeds regularly to maintain a continuous bloom. = 园丁定期播种种子以保持持续开花。


一般过去时:
They resowed the damaged crop last week. = 他们上周重新播种了受损的作物。
The farmer resowed the field after the flood receded. = 洪水退去后，农民重新播种了田地。


现在完成时:
We have resown the lawn several times this year. = 今年我们已经重新播种草坪几次了。
She has resown the flower bed with new seeds. = 她已经用新的种子重新播种了花坛。


过去完成时:

They had resown the seeds twice before they finally got a good yield. = 他们播种了两次之后才最终获得了丰收。
