semicircle:/ˈsemiˌsɜːrkl/ | n. | 半圆形 | A semicircle is a half of a circle.  It is a two-dimensional geometric shape formed by cutting a circle along a diameter. | Example: The students arranged themselves in a semicircle around the teacher. 学生们围着老师排成半圆形。 |近义词：half-circle | 反义词：full circle
