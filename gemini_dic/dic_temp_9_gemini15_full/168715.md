proctorially:/prɒkˈtɔːriəli/ | adv. | 代理地；监督地；监考地 |  In a manner that involves proctoring or supervision;  acting as a proctor.  | 例句：The exam was proctorially administered to ensure fairness.  考试由监考人员监督进行，以确保公平性。

The test was proctorially overseen to prevent cheating.  考试在监考人员的监督下进行，以防止作弊。


近义词：supervisingly,  oversightly,  monitorially | 反义词：unsupervisedly, independently


词性: 副词
