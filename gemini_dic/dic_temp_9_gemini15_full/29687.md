catheterize:/ˈkæθɪtəraɪz/ | vt. |导尿；插管 | To insert a catheter into (a body cavity, duct, or vessel).  This typically involves inserting a thin, flexible tube into a body part to drain fluids, inject substances, or monitor pressure. |例句：The nurse had to catheterize the patient after surgery. 护士手术后不得不为病人导尿。  The doctor catheterized the artery to measure blood pressure. 医生为测量血压而对动脉进行了插管。|近义词：cannulate |反义词：None directly opposite, but the opposite action would be removing a catheter. |词性：动词
