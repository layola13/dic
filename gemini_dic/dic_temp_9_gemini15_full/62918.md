enlivenment: /ɪnˈlaɪvnmənt/ | n. | 活跃；生气；使活跃；使有生气 | The act of enlivening something; the state of being enlivened.  It refers to the process of making something more lively, interesting, or exciting, or the resulting state of increased animation or vitality. |  The enlivenment of the party after the band arrived was remarkable. 晚会乐队到达后变得热闹非凡。 The garden enjoyed a remarkable enlivenment after the spring rains. 春雨过后，花园生机勃勃。 | 近义词：animation, vivification, invigoration | 反义词：dulling, deadening, stagnation | 名词
