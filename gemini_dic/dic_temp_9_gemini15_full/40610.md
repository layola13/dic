convene:/kənˈviːn/ | vt. |召集，召开 |To call people to a formal meeting; to assemble or gather. |The committee will convene next week to discuss the budget.  委员会下周将召开会议讨论预算。|The protesters convened in the town square. 抗议者在市中心广场集会。|assemble, summon, gather |disperse, adjourn


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The board convenes every month. = 董事会每月召开一次会议。
She convenes meetings regularly to keep everyone updated. = 她定期召开会议，让大家了解最新情况。


一般过去时:
The court convened to hear the case. = 法庭开庭审理此案。
They convened a meeting to discuss the problem. = 他们召开会议讨论这个问题。


现在完成时:
The committee has convened three times this year. = 委员会今年已经召开了三次会议。
The group has convened several times to plan the event. = 该团体已经几次集会来策划活动。


过去完成时:
The judge had convened the jury before the evidence was presented. = 在证据提交之前，法官已经召集了陪审团。
The team had convened several times before the final decision was made. = 在做出最终决定之前，团队已经开过几次会。


将来时:
The council will convene next Tuesday. = 理事会将在下周二召开。
The president will convene a press conference tomorrow. = 总统将在明天召开新闻发布会。


虚拟语气:
If the committee convened earlier, we could have avoided this problem. = 如果委员会早点召开会议，我们本可以避免这个问题。
It would be best if the committee convened soon to address the issue. = 最好委员会尽快召开会议来解决这个问题。


被动语态:
A meeting was convened to discuss the new policy. = 召开了一次会议来讨论新的政策。
The meeting was convened by the chairman. = 会议是由主席召集的。


疑问句:
When will the committee convene? = 委员会什么时候召开？
Has the meeting been convened yet? = 会议已经召开了吗？


否定句:
The meeting did not convene because of the storm. = 由于暴风雨，会议没有召开。
They did not convene the meeting on time. = 他们没有按时召开会议。
