overtaxing:/ˌoʊvərˈtæksɪŋ/| adj.| 过度征税；负担过重；使不堪重负的 |  Excessively taxing; placing too great a demand on someone or something.  It implies that something is demanding too much, leading to strain or exhaustion. |例句：The constant demands of her job were overtaxing.她的工作不断地要求使她不堪重负。The overtaxing workload led to burnout among employees.  过重的的工作量导致员工筋疲力尽。|近义词：overburdening, overwhelming, excessive, strenuous|反义词：undemanding, easy, effortless

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The constant demands of the project are overtaxing the team. = 项目持续不断的需求让团队不堪重负。
This exercise is overtaxing my strength. = 这个练习超出了我的体力承受范围。

虚拟语气:
