dacoit:/ˈdækɔɪt/| n. |土匪，强盗 |A dacoit is a member of a band of robbers or bandits, especially in India.  The term often implies a degree of organized crime and violence, operating often in rural areas. |Example:  The dacoits ambushed the travelers and stole their valuables.  土匪伏击了旅行者并偷走了他们的贵重物品。

The dacoit gang terrorized the village for months.  土匪团伙几个月来一直恐吓着这个村庄。|近义词：bandit, robber, brigand, outlaw |反义词：police, law enforcement, peacekeeper
