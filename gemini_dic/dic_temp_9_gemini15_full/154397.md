permeating:/ˈpɜːmieɪtɪŋ/| v.|弥漫，渗透，遍布| to spread throughout (something); to penetrate|The aroma of coffee permeated the air. 咖啡的香味弥漫在空气中。

The influence of social media permeates every aspect of modern life. 社交媒体的影响渗透到现代生活的方方面面。|pervade, penetrate, diffuse, infiltrate|isolate, separate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scent of roses permeates the garden. 玫瑰的香味弥漫在花园里。
Fear permeates the hearts of many. 恐惧弥漫在许多人的心中。

一般过去时:
The rain permeated the soil. 雨水渗透了土壤。
Anxiety permeated her thoughts. 焦虑充满了她的思绪。

现在完成时:
The new ideas have permeated the company culture. 新的理念已经渗透到公司文化中。
Disappointment has permeated his every action. 失望已经渗透到他的每一个行动中。

过去完成时:
The rumors had permeated the town before the truth emerged. 真相出现之前，谣言已经传遍了整个城镇。
Doubt had permeated her mind before the exam. 考试前，怀疑充满了她的思绪。

将来时:
The technology will permeate all aspects of our lives. 这项技术将渗透到我们生活的方方面面。
His influence will permeate the organization for years to come. 他的影响将在未来几年渗透到整个组织。

虚拟语气:
If the news permeated the public, there would be chaos. 如果这个消息传到公众那里，就会造成混乱。
I wish the joy of the festival permeated every home. 我希望节日快乐能弥漫每个家庭。

被动语态:
The building was permeated by the smell of smoke. 建筑物里弥漫着烟味。
The culture was permeated by ancient traditions. 文化中充满了古老的传统。

疑问句:
Does the aroma of baking bread permeate the whole house? 烤面包的香味是否弥漫了整个房子？
How did the rumor permeate the community so quickly? 谣言是如何如此迅速地传遍社区的？

否定句:
The message did not permeate the intended audience. 信息没有传达给目标受众。
The water did not permeate the waterproof fabric. 水没有渗透到防水织物中。
