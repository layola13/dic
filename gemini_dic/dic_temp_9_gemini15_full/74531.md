fraternization:/ˌfrætərnaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |友谊；兄弟情谊；士兵与敌方士兵或平民的非正式交往|The act of associating or interacting in a friendly way, especially between members of different groups who are normally kept apart, particularly soldiers and civilians or members of opposing forces.  This often implies a violation of rules or regulations.|例句：Fraternization between the soldiers and the local population was strictly forbidden. 士兵与当地居民的友好交往是被严格禁止的。

例句：The excessive fraternization between officers and enlisted men led to disciplinary action.军官和士兵之间的过度友好交往导致了纪律处分。


近义词：camaraderie, companionship, fellowship, sociability|反义词：estrangement, alienation, isolation, segregation|词性：名词
