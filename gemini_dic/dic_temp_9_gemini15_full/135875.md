nonprovable: /ˌnɒnˈpruːvəbl/ | adj. | 不可证实的，无法证明的 |  Unable to be proven true or false; incapable of being demonstrated or verified.  It often refers to statements or theorems within a formal system, particularly in mathematics and logic.  |


例句：

Some mathematical statements are nonprovable within a given axiomatic system.  = 在给定的公理系统中，一些数学命题是不可证明的。

The existence of God is a famously nonprovable proposition. = 上帝的存在是一个著名的不可证实的命题。


近义词：unprovable, indemonstrable, unverifiable, irrefutable (in the sense that its opposite cannot be proven)


反义词：provable, demonstrable, verifiable


词性：形容词
