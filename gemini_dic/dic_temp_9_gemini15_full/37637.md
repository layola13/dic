commutation:/ˌkɒmjuˈteɪʃən/| /ˌkɑːmjuˈteɪʃən/ | n. |交换；通勤；减刑；兑换 |The act of commuting or exchanging; the process of substituting one thing for another.  It can also refer to the act of traveling regularly between one's home and place of work, or a reduction in a criminal sentence. |例句：The commutation of his sentence from death to life imprisonment was a surprise.  他的死刑被减为无期徒刑，这令人意外。

例句：His daily commutation to work takes over an hour. 他每天通勤上班要花一个多小时。


近义词：exchange, substitution, commute, reduction |反义词：increase, aggravation, extension (of sentence)


词性：名词
