pleasurist:/ˈplɛʒərɪst/ | n. |享乐主义者 |A person who seeks pleasure as a primary goal in life.  They prioritize immediate gratification and sensual experiences above other considerations such as morality, responsibility, or long-term consequences. | Example:  He was a confirmed pleasurist, dedicating his life to the pursuit of sensual delights. 他是一个坚定的享乐主义者，毕生致力于追求感官享受。|近义词: hedonist, sensualist, epicurean |反义词: ascetic, puritan, stoic |
