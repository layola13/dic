reprovisioning:/ˌriːprəˈvɪʒənɪŋ/| v.|重新供应；重新配置；再配置 |The act of supplying or configuring something again.  This often refers to IT infrastructure,  applications, or services. It involves restoring or replacing resources or capabilities. |例句：The server requires reprovisioning after the security breach.  服务器在安全漏洞后需要重新配置。

例句：We need to reprovision the database after the system failure. 系统故障后我们需要重新配置数据库。


近义词：reconfiguration, redeployment, reinstatement


反义词：decommissioning, decommission, removal


词性：动词
