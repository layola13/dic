extremism:/ˈɛkstrɪmɪzəm/| n. |极端主义；极端行为|The belief in and use of extreme methods;  the holding of extreme political or religious views.  It often involves violence or the threat of violence to achieve political aims.|例句：Religious extremism has led to violence and unrest in many parts of the world.  宗教极端主义导致世界许多地区暴力和动荡。|例句：Political extremism can undermine democracy and stability. 政治极端主义会破坏民主和稳定。|近义词：radicalism, fanaticism, zealotry |反义词：moderation, tolerance, centrism
