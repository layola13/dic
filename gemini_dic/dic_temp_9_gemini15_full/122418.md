moabite:/ˈmoʊbaɪt/| n. |摩押人；摩押语；摩押的|An inhabitant of Moab; the language of the Moabites; of or relating to Moab.|The Moabites were a Semitic people who lived in the ancient land of Moab, east of the Dead Sea.  Their language, Moabite, was closely related to Hebrew.|The Moabite Stone, a significant archaeological discovery, bears an inscription in the Moabite language.|近义词：Moabite (adj.)|反义词：无
