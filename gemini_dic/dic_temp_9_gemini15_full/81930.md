grim:/ɡrɪm/| adj. |严峻的；可怕的；令人沮丧的 |grim describes something unpleasant, depressing, or frightening. It suggests a sense of seriousness and severity, often implying a lack of hope or optimism.  |The grim reality is that many people are still suffering.  严峻的现实是许多人仍在受苦。|The grim face of the judge showed no mercy.  法官严峻的面容毫无怜悯之意。|severe, harsh, bleak | cheerful, optimistic, pleasant
