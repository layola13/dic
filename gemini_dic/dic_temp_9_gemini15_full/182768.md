repasting:/riˈpæs.tɪŋ/ | v. | 再吃，重新进食 | The act of eating again, often after a period of fasting or abstaining from food.  It implies a resumption of eating, not necessarily a large meal. | Example: After a long hike, they repast on energy bars and water.  长途跋涉后，他们又吃了一些能量棒和水。|近义词：refueling,  replenishing |反义词：fasting, starving
