firebare: /ˈfaɪərˌbɛər/ | adj. | 赤裸的，光秃秃的 | Completely bare or exposed; lacking covering or protection.  | 例句：The firebare mountain peak was exposed to the harsh elements.  = 光秃秃的山峰暴露在恶劣的天气中。


The wind whipped across the firebare fields. = 狂风席卷着光秃秃的田野。


近义词：naked, exposed, uncovered, desolate, barren | 反义词：covered, sheltered, protected, lush, verdant

词性:形容词
