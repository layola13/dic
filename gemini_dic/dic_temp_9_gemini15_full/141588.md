oppressors:/əˈprɛsərz/| n. |压迫者，压制者|People or groups who unjustly exercise power or authority over others, often causing suffering and hardship.  They impose their will on others through force, coercion, or exploitation.|例句：Throughout history, many oppressed peoples have fought for their freedom from their oppressors.  纵观历史，许多被压迫的人民一直在为摆脱压迫者的统治而斗争。|例句：The actions of the oppressors led to widespread unrest and rebellion. 压迫者的行为导致了广泛的不满和叛乱。|近义词：tyrants, dictators, persecutors, subjugators|反义词：liberators, emancipators, defenders
