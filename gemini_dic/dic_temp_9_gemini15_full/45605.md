curriculums:/kəˈrɪkjʊləmz/ | n. |课程；课程表 | Curriculum refers to the courses of study offered by an educational institution.  It outlines the subjects, learning objectives, and methods of teaching.  The plural form is curriculums or curricula. | Example: The school's curriculum includes math, science, and English. 这所学校的课程包括数学、科学和英语。  The curriculums offered by different universities vary greatly. 不同大学提供的课程大相径庭。|近义词: syllabus, program, course of study |反义词: None  |名词
