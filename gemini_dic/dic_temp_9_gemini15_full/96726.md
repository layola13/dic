inefficiency:/ˌɪnɪˈfɪʃənsi/ | n. | 无效率；低效率 | Lack of efficiency; the state or quality of being inefficient.  It refers to a failure to make the best use of time, resources, or effort. | The inefficiency of the system led to long delays.  这个系统的低效率导致了长时间的延误。 The inefficiency of the old machine was costing the company money. 老机器的低效率正在让公司赔钱。 | inefficiency, ineffectiveness, unproductivity | efficiency, effectiveness, productivity
