copular:/ˈkɒpjʊlə(r)/| adj.| 系动词的；连接词的 | Relating to or being a copular verb. A copular verb is a verb that connects a subject with a predicate adjective or noun that describes or identifies the subject.  Examples include "be," "seem," "become," "appear," and "remain." |The sentence "The cat is fluffy" uses a copular verb ("is") to link the subject ("cat") with a predicate adjective ("fluffy"). |近义词：linking, connecting |反义词：action, dynamic
