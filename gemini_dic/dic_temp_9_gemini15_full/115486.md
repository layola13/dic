manure:/məˈnʊr/| n. |肥料，粪肥 |Decayed organic matter used as a fertilizer for crops.  It is typically composed of animal excrement and plant matter. |The farmer spread manure on the fields to enrich the soil.  农民在田里施肥以肥沃土壤。|fertilizer, dung, compost |  (无明显反义词，可考虑clean soil as a contextual opposite) |名词

The farmer uses manure to fertilize his crops. = 农民用肥料来给庄稼施肥。
We spread manure on the garden every spring. = 我们每年春天都在花园里施肥。
