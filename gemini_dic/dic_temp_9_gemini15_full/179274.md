redistributional: /ˌriːdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃənəl/ | adj. | 再分配的；重新分配的 | Relating to or involving the redistribution of something, especially wealth or resources.  |例句：The government's redistributional policies aim to reduce income inequality. 政府的再分配政策旨在减少收入不平等。


例句：The proposed tax reforms have significant redistributional effects.  拟议的税收改革具有重大的再分配效应。


近义词：reallocating, reapportioning


反义词：concentrative, centralizing

词性:形容词
