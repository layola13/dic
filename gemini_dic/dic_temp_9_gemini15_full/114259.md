maimer:/ˈmeɪmər/ | vt. |使残废；使受伤；严重损坏 | To injure someone so severely that part of their body is permanently damaged or useless.  |例句：The accident maimed him for life.  事故使他终身残废。|例句：The storm maimed the landscape. 暴风雨严重破坏了景观。|近义词：cripple, disable, injure, wound |反义词：heal, cure, restore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The careless driver maims pedestrians every day. = 粗心的司机每天都会撞伤行人。
He often maims his arguments with irrelevant details. = 他经常用无关紧要的细节来削弱他的论点。


一般过去时:
The explosion maimed several people. = 爆炸使几个人受伤。
The war maimed many soldiers both physically and mentally. = 战争使许多士兵身心受伤。


现在完成时:
The accident has maimed him for life. = 那次事故使他终身残废。
Years of overwork have maimed his health. = 多年的过度工作损害了他的健康。


过去完成时:
He had maimed himself beyond repair before help arrived. = 在得到帮助之前，他已经伤得无法修复了。
The factory had maimed hundreds of workers before safety regulations were implemented. = 在实施安全条例之前，这家工厂已经使数百名工人受伤。


将来时:
The upcoming hurricane will maim coastal communities. = 即将来临的飓风将摧毁沿海社区。
If we don't act now, the drought will maim the harvest. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，干旱将毁掉收成。


虚拟语气:
If he had been more careful, he wouldn't have maimed himself. = 如果他更小心一点，他就不会伤到自己了。
I wish the storm hadn't maimed the old oak tree. = 我真希望暴风雨没有毁坏那棵老橡树。


被动语态:
Many soldiers were maimed in the battle. = 许多士兵在战斗中受伤。
The city was maimed by the earthquake. = 城市在地震中遭到破坏。


疑问句:
Was he maimed in the accident? = 他在事故中受伤了吗？
Were the crops maimed by the frost? = 霜冻毁坏庄稼了吗？


否定句:
The surgery didn't maim her; it saved her life. = 手术没有使她残废；它挽救了她的生命。
The storm didn't maim the town as badly as expected. = 暴风雨并没有像预期的那样严重地破坏小镇。
