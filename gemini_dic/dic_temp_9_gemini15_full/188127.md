roundy:/ˈraʊndi/| adj.| 圆形的；丰满的；圆滚滚的 | Roundy describes something that is round or plump.  It implies a pleasing fullness or roundness. | The child's cheeks were roundy and rosy.  孩子的脸蛋圆润红润。|近义词：round, plump, chubby, rotund |反义词: angular, gaunt, thin
