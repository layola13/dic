lairs:/lerz/| n.|兽穴；藏身处；窝点|a den or shelter, especially of a wild animal; a place where someone hides or conducts illegal activities. |Example: The lion retreated to its lair.  狮子退回到它的巢穴。The police raided the drug lair. 警方突袭了毒品窝点。|近义词：den, hideout, haven|反义词：open, exposed


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The fox sleeps in its lair. = 狐狸睡在它的巢穴里。
Lions often hide in their lairs during the day. = 狮子白天经常躲在它们的巢穴里。

一般过去时:
The bear found a safe lair for winter. = 熊找到了一个安全的冬眠处。
The thieves used an abandoned building as their lair. = 小偷们用一栋废弃的建筑物作为他们的藏身处。

现在完成时:
The wolves have made a new lair in the mountains. = 狼群在山里建造了一个新的巢穴。
The criminals have used this abandoned house as their lair for years. = 罪犯们多年来一直使用这栋废弃的房屋作为他们的窝点。

过去完成时:
The robbers had already escaped to their lair before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，强盗们已经逃到了他们的藏身处。
The animals had prepared their lairs before the harsh winter came. = 动物们在严冬来临之前已经准备好了它们的巢穴。

将来时:
The birds will build their lairs in the trees. = 鸟儿们将在树上筑巢。
The gang will move their lair to a more secure location. = 帮派会把他们的窝点转移到一个更安全的地方。

虚拟语气:
If I were a bear, I would have a cozy lair in the forest. = 如果我是一只熊，我就会在森林里有一个舒适的巢穴。
If the thieves had a better-hidden lair, they wouldn't have been caught. = 如果小偷们有一个更隐蔽的藏身处，他们就不会被抓住了。

被动语态:
The lair was discovered by the police. = 巢穴被警方发现了。
The abandoned building was used as a lair by the criminals. = 废弃的建筑物被罪犯们用作藏身处。

疑问句:
Where is the bear's lair? = 熊的巢穴在哪里？
Do you know where the gang's secret lair is located? = 你知道这个帮派的秘密窝点在哪里吗？

否定句:
The lion did not return to its lair that night. = 那天晚上狮子没有回到它的巢穴。
The police couldn't find the criminals' lair. = 警方找不到罪犯的藏身处。
