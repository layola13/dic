ghostfish:/ˈɡoʊstfɪʃ/ | n. |幽灵鱼，透明鱼|A ghostfish is a type of small, translucent fish that is often difficult to see in the water.  They are typically found in coastal waters and are characterized by their lack of strong coloration and often their small size.  They are not a single species but a collective term for several different types of fish sharing these characteristics.| Example: The divers spotted several ghostfish flitting among the coral.  潜水员发现几条幽灵鱼在珊瑚丛中飞舞。  The researchers observed the ghostfish's unique feeding behavior. 研究人员观察了幽灵鱼独特的觅食行为。|近义词：transparent fish, clear fish|反义词：None specifically, but the antonym would be a brightly colored, easily visible fish. |名词
