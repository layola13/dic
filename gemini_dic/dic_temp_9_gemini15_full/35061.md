closer:/ˈkloʊsər/ | /ˈkləʊsə(r)/| adj., n., adv.| 更近的；关系密切的人；更近地 |  Closer describes something that is nearer in space, time, or relationship.  It can be an adjective, adverb, or noun. |例句：The closer we got, the more nervous I became.  我们越靠近，我就越紧张。


The birds are building a nest closer to the house this year. 今年鸟儿在离房子更近的地方筑巢。


近义词：nearer, nigher | 反义词：farther, further


词性:  adj., adv., n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shop is closer than I thought. = 这家店比我想象的更近。
He is closer to his father than his mother. 他和父亲的关系比和母亲的关系更近。

一般过去时:
The train was closer to the station than I expected. = 火车比我预料的更靠近车站。
They were closer friends in college.  他们在大学时是更亲密的朋友。

现在完成时:
We have moved closer to the city center. = 我们已经搬到更靠近市中心的地方了。
They have become closer since their trip. 自从旅行之后，他们变得更亲密了。

过去完成时:
The car had been closer to the edge of the cliff than I realized. = 汽车比我意识到的更靠近悬崖边。
They had been closer friends before the argument. 争吵之前，他们是很要好的朋友。

将来时:
We will be closer to our goal soon. = 我们很快就会更接近我们的目标。
They will be closer to solving the mystery. 他们将更接近解开这个谜团。

虚拟语气:
If we were closer, we could visit more often. = 如果我们住得更近，我们就能更常拜访。
I wish I were closer to my family. 我希望我能更靠近我的家人。

被动语态:
The bridge was built closer to the river than the original design. = 这座桥建得比原设计更靠近河流。
The issue was discussed in closer detail.  这个问题得到了更详细的讨论。

疑问句:
Is the restaurant closer to the train station or the bus stop? = 餐厅离火车站近还是离汽车站近？
Are they closer than they appear?  他们关系真的像看起来那么好吗？

否定句:
The hotel is not closer to the beach than the last one we stayed in. = 这家酒店不像我们上次住的那家酒店离海滩那么近。
They are not closer friends than they used to be. 他们不像以前那样是好朋友了。


PHRASE->
get closer:  越来越近
draw closer: 靠近；接近
closer look: 仔细看看
closer examination: 仔细检查
closer relationship: 更密切的关系

词源:
The word "closer" comes from the Old English word "clōs," meaning "closed, shut."  Over time, it evolved to also mean "near" and "confined," eventually leading to its modern meaning of "nearer."  The "-er" suffix indicates a comparative form.
