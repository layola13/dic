overshooting:/ˌoʊvərˈʃuːtɪŋ/| v.|超过；越过；瞄得过远|To go beyond a target or limit.  It can refer to exceeding a desired value, missing a target, or going beyond a planned or expected level.|例句：The pilot overshot the runway. = 飞行员飞机跑道跑过头了。

The company overshot its sales targets for the quarter. = 公司本季度的销售目标超额完成。|近义词：exceed, surpass, overreach|反义词：undershoot, fall short


USAGE->
