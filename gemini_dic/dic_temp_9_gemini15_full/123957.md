morin:/ˈmɒrɪn/| n. |染料;  黄烷醇类化合物|A yellow flavonoid dye obtained from various plants, especially the heartwood of the osage orange (Maclura pomifera). It is used as a dye for wool and silk and also has antioxidant properties.  |Example:  Morin is a natural dye used to create beautiful yellow hues in fabrics.|近义词：dye, pigment|反义词：N/A
