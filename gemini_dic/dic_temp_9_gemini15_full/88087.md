highres:/ˈhaɪrez/| adj.|高分辨率的|High-resolution; characterized by a high number of pixels, resulting in a sharp and detailed image or display.  It is commonly used to describe images, videos, and displays.|例句：The highres image showed every detail of the landscape. 高分辨率图像显示了景观的每一个细节。 The monitor boasts a stunning highres display. 这台显示器拥有令人惊叹的高分辨率显示效果。|近义词：high-definition, sharp, detailed|反义词：low-resolution, blurry, pixelated
