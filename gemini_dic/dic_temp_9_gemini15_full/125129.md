multi-lingual: /ˌmʌltiˈlɪŋɡwəl/ | adj. | 多语言的，通晓多种语言的 | Able to speak or use several languages.  |  She's multilingual, speaking fluent English, Spanish, and French. 她精通多种语言，流利地说英语、西班牙语和法语。 | polyglot, plurilingual | monolingual
