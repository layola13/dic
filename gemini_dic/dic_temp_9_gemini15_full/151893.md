passus:/ˈpæsəs/| n. |段落，章节；一段经历|A passage is a section of a written work, or a continuous period of time. It can also refer to a journey or experience.|例句：The passage was long and difficult to read. 这段文字又长又难读。


例句：The passage of time healed her wounds.  时间的流逝治愈了她的伤痛。


近义词：section, paragraph, episode, phase


反义词：whole, entirety


词性:名词
