laparoscopies:/ˌlæpəˈrɒskəpiz/ | n. | 腹腔镜检查（复数） | Laparoscopies are minimally invasive surgical procedures that use small incisions and a camera to view and operate within the abdominal cavity.  | 例句：The doctor recommended laparoscopies to diagnose the cause of her abdominal pain. 医生建议她进行腹腔镜检查以诊断腹痛的原因。
The patient underwent several laparoscopies before the surgeons found the source of the bleeding.  病人进行了几次腹腔镜检查，外科医生才找到出血的根源。


近义词：minimally invasive surgery, endoscopic surgery | 反义词：open surgery, traditional surgery


词源:  The word "laparoscopy" comes from the Greek words "lapara" (meaning flank or abdomen) and "skopein" (meaning to look or examine).  The plural form is "laparoscopies".
