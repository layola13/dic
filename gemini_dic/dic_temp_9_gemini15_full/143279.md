outdoing:/ˌaʊtˈduːɪŋ/| v.|胜过；超越；胜出 |The act of surpassing or excelling someone or something.  It implies a competitive context where one person or thing achieves a better result than another.|例句：He was outdoing everyone in the race. 他在比赛中胜过所有人。
例句：Her latest novel is outdoing all her previous works. 她的最新小说超越了她以前的所有作品。|近义词：surpassing, excelling, exceeding, topping|反义词：underperforming, lagging behind, failing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is outdoing his brother in academics. = 他在学业上胜过他的兄弟。
They are constantly outdoing each other in their attempts to impress their boss. = 他们为了给老板留下好印象，不断地互相竞争超越对方。


一般过去时:
She outdid herself in the final performance. = 她在最后的演出中超越了自己。
The team outdid their rivals in the championship game. = 这支队伍在冠军赛中击败了他们的对手。


现在完成时:
He has outdone all expectations with his remarkable achievement. = 他以其非凡的成就超越了所有人的期望。
She has outdone herself in this project, exceeding all deadlines. = 她在这个项目中超越了自己，提前完成了所有截止日期。


过去完成时:
By the time the race ended, he had outdone all his competitors. = 当比赛结束时，他已经超越了所有竞争对手。
They had outdone their previous best score, setting a new record. = 他们已经超越了他们之前的最好成绩，创造了一个新的纪录。


将来时:
She will outdo her previous best time in the upcoming marathon. = 她将在即将到来的马拉松比赛中超越她之前的最好成绩。
He believes he will outdo his opponents in the upcoming debate. = 他相信他将在即将到来的辩论中胜过他的对手。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he would have outdone his rival. = 如果他训练更努力，他就会胜过他的对手。
I wish I could outdo my own limitations. = 我希望我能超越自身的局限。


被动语态:
His performance was outdone by his younger sister. = 他的表现被他的妹妹超越了。
The previous record was outdone by a new athlete. = 之前的记录被一位新的运动员打破了。


疑问句:
Can he outdo his previous record? = 他能超越他之前的记录吗？
Will they be able to outdo the reigning champions? = 他们能够战胜卫冕冠军吗？


否定句:
He did not outdo his opponent in the chess match. = 他在国际象棋比赛中没有胜过对手。
They failed to outdo their competitors in the market. = 他们在市场上未能超越竞争对手。
