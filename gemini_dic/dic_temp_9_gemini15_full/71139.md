fishs:/fɪʃz/| n.|鱼类(复数)|The word "fishs" is not a standard word in English.  It's an attempt to create a plural form of "fish," but "fish" is one of the few English nouns that uses the same form for both singular and plural.  The standard plural form is "fish."|例句：There are many fish in the sea. = 海里有很多鱼。|近义词：fishes (formal plural), seafood|反义词：无
