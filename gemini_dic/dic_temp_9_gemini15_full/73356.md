foregate:/ˈfɔːrɡeɪt/| n. |前门，城门；（尤指）外城门 |A foregate is an outer gate of a city or town, especially one leading to a suburb.  The term often implies a less important or less secure gate compared to the main city gate.  It is now largely archaic.| Example: The foregate was left unguarded, allowing the enemy to sneak into the town.  外城门没有守卫，使得敌人得以偷偷潜入城镇。|近义词：outer gate, entrance, gateway|反义词：inner gate, main gate|名词
