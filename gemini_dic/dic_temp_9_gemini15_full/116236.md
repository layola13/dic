masquerading:/ˌmæskəˈreɪdɪŋ/| v. |伪装；假扮；掩饰 |To pretend to be someone or something that you are not.  |例句：He was masquerading as a doctor. 他假扮医生。|例句：The thief was masquerading as a police officer. 小偷伪装成警察。|近义词：disguising, pretending, feigning |反义词：revealing, exposing
