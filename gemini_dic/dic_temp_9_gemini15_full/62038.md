encage:/ɪnˈkeɪdʒ/| vt. |关进笼子里；囚禁；限制|To confine or restrict someone or something, typically in a cage or other confined space.  It can also refer to restricting someone's freedom or expression. |例句：They encaged the bird in a small cage. 他们把鸟关在一个小笼子里。

例句：His creativity was encaged by the rigid rules of the company.  他的创造力受到了公司严格规章制度的限制。|近义词：imprison, confine, trap, restrict|反义词：liberate, free, release|动词
