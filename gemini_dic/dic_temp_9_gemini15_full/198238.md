shorthead:/ˈʃɔːrthedz/| n.|短头，短头鱼|A shorthead is a type of fish, often referring to specific species within certain families like the Scorpaenidae (rockfishes) or the Serranidae (groupers), characterized by their relatively short head compared to their body length.  The term is not a formal taxonomic classification but rather a descriptive term used in common language and some scientific publications to distinguish certain fish species based on their morphology.|例句：The fisherman caught a large shorthead grouper. 渔民钓到了一条大型短头石斑鱼。
例句：Several species of shorthead rockfish inhabit the rocky reefs.  几种短头岩鱼栖息在岩石礁上。|近义词：short-headed (adjective),  stubby-headed (informal)|反义词：longhead (in the comparative sense)
