denoting: /dɪˈnoʊtɪŋ/ | v. |指示；表示；意味着 |  Showing or indicating something.  It is the present participle of the verb "denote". | 例句：The red flag denoting danger was clearly visible.  红色的危险标志清晰可见。


例句：The symbol denotes a hazardous material. 这个符号表示危险材料。


近义词：indicating, signifying, representing, showing, implying


反义词：concealing, hiding, obscuring
