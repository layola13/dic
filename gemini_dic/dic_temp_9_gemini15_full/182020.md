remediation:/ˌriːmiˈdiːeɪʃən/| n.|补救；纠正；改进|The process of correcting or improving something, especially environmental problems or flaws in a system or process.  It involves actions taken to address a problem and restore it to a desirable state.|例句：The remediation of the contaminated soil will be expensive and time-consuming.  土壤的修复将是昂贵和费时的。|例句：The school implemented a remediation program for students struggling in math. 学校为在数学方面挣扎的学生实施了一个补救计划。|近义词：correction, improvement, reclamation, rehabilitation|反义词：deterioration, worsening
