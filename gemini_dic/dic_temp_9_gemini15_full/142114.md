orifice:/ˈɔːrɪfɪs/| n. |孔，开口，出口 |An orifice is a natural or artificial opening, especially one in a body part or a mechanical device.  It refers to a mouth or aperture. |例句：The surgeon carefully examined the orifice of the wound. 外科医生仔细检查了伤口的开口。

例句：The small orifice in the pipe restricted the flow of water. 管子上的小孔限制了水流。


近义词：opening, aperture, hole, vent |反义词：closure, blockage

词源:  From Latin *ōrificium, from ōs "mouth" + -ficium "making".  The word reflects the concept of a "mouth-making" or an opening.
