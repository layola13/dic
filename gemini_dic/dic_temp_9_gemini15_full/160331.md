poor:/pʊər/| adj.|贫穷的，可怜的，劣质的|The word "poor" has a multifaceted meaning, primarily referring to a lack of financial resources (poverty) but also encompassing feelings of pity, low quality, or inadequacy.  It can describe a person's economic status, their condition, or the quality of something.|例句：He is a poor man. 他是个穷人。

例句：The quality of the product is poor. 产品质量很差。


近义词：impoverished, needy, destitute, inferior, substandard|反义词：rich, wealthy, affluent, excellent, superior|词性：形容词
