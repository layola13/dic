rewraps:/ˌriːˈræps/| v.|重新包裹；重新包装|To wrap something again.  This usually implies a previous wrapping that has been undone or is inadequate.|例句：He rewraps the presents after the dog tore into the wrapping paper. 他在狗撕碎包装纸后重新包装了礼物。
例句：The store rewraps damaged goods before putting them back on the shelves. 商店在将损坏的商品重新上架之前会重新包装它们。|近义词：repackage, repack, re-cover|反义词：unwrap, unpack


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rewraps the gifts every year. = 他每年都重新包装礼物。
She rewraps the sandwiches for lunch. = 她把三明治重新包装起来当午餐。


一般过去时:
They rewrapped the presents before giving them away. = 他们送礼物之前重新包装了一下。
I rewrapped the leftovers and put them in the fridge. = 我把剩菜重新包装好放进冰箱里。


现在完成时:
She has rewrapped the damaged goods. = 她已经重新包装了损坏的商品。
He has rewrapped the presents several times. = 他已经几次重新包装了礼物。


过去完成时:
They had rewrapped the package before shipping it. = 他们发货前已经重新包装了包裹。
She had rewrapped the present before giving it to her friend. = 在送给朋友之前，她已经重新包装了礼物。


将来时:
I will rewrap the Christmas presents tomorrow. = 我明天将重新包装圣诞礼物。
He will rewrap the damaged items before returning them. = 他将在退回损坏的物品之前重新包装它们。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rewrap the gifts more carefully. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更仔细地重新包装礼物。
I wish I had rewrapped the cake before it got smudged. = 我真希望我在蛋糕弄脏之前已经重新包装了它。


被动语态:
