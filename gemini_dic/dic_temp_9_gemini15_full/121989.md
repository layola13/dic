misspeak:/ˌmɪsˈspiːk/| v.|说错话；讲错；失言|To speak incorrectly or unintentionally; to make a mistake in speaking.  |例句：He misspoke during the interview and said the company was losing money. 他在面试中说错话了，说公司正在亏损。|例句：I misspoke; I meant to say "tomorrow," not "today." 我说错了；我的意思是“明天”，不是“今天”。|近义词：err, blunder, slip up, misstate|反义词：speak correctly, articulate clearly|动词
