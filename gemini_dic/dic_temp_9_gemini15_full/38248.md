computes:/kəmˈpjuːts/ | vt. |计算，估算 |To determine or calculate (something) numerically.  It also refers to the process of using a computer to perform calculations or process data.| 例句：The computer computes the results instantly.  电脑立即计算出结果。

The program computes the trajectory of the projectile. 程序计算弹丸的轨迹。


近义词：calculates, reckons, figures, determines | 反义词：estimates (in the sense of approximation, not exact calculation)
