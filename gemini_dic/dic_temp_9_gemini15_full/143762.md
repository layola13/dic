outmarching: /ˌaʊtˈmɑːrtʃɪŋ/ | v. | 超过；胜过；超过…行军 |  The act of marching further or faster than someone else; surpassing someone in a march or in a metaphorical sense of progress or achievement.  | 例句：Their army was outmarching the enemy, gaining a strategic advantage. = 他们的军队超过了敌人，获得了战略优势。

Their innovative marketing strategy was outmarching the competition. = 他们创新的营销策略胜过了竞争对手。


近义词：outspeeding, outpacing, surpassing, exceeding

反义词：being outmarched, lagging behind, falling behind

词性：动词
