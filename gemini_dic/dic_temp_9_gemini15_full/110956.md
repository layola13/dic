/lɪˈkweɪʃən/| n. |熔析；熔炼；液化|The process of separating substances by melting them at different temperatures.  It is often used in metallurgy to separate metals from their ores or to purify metals. |例句：Liquation is a method used to extract silver from lead.  熔析是一种从铅中提取银的方法。 |例句：The process of liquation involves heating the mixture until one component melts while the others remain solid. 液化过程包括加热混合物，直到一种成分熔化而其他成分保持固态。|近义词：fusion, melting, smelting|反义词：solidification, freezing
