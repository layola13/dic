frowstily:/ˈfraʊstɪli/| adv.|冷淡地，不友善地，严寒地 |  describes an action or manner that is cold, unfriendly, or frosty.  It suggests a reserved and distant attitude, often implying disapproval or displeasure. |例句：She greeted him frowstily and turned away. 她冷淡地向他打招呼然后转身离开了。|  She spoke frowstily, her voice barely a whisper. 她语气冰冷地说着话，声音几乎是耳语。|近义词：coldly, icily, frigidly, unpleasantly|反义词：warmly, cordially, amiably, pleasantly|副词
