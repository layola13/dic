faint-hearted:/ˈfeɪntˌhɑːtɪd/| adj. |胆小的，懦弱的，缺乏勇气的|Lacking courage; timid.  The faint-hearted will likely avoid the haunted house.|例句：The faint-hearted soldier deserted his post during the battle.  这个胆小的士兵在战斗中逃离了岗位。

例句：He's too faint-hearted to ask her out. 他太胆小了，不敢约她出去。


近义词：timid, cowardly, pusillanimous, craven

反义词：brave, courageous, bold, valiant


词源:

* **faint:**  From Old French *feint*, meaning "weak, feeble," ultimately from Latin *fīnīre*, "to finish, end."  This sense reflects the idea of weakening or failing in courage.

* **hearted:**  From Old English *heort*, meaning "heart," referring to the seat of emotions and courage.
