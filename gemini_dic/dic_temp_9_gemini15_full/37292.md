commanding:/kəˈmændɪŋ/| adj.|有权威的；指挥的；令人印象深刻的；引人注目的|Having or showing great authority; impressively large or powerful.|例句：She has a commanding presence. 她有一种有权威的气场。

例句：The general gave a commanding voice.将军用有命令口吻说话。


近义词：authoritative, dominant, impressive, imposing|反义词：submissive, weak, insignificant, unassuming|词性：形容词


USAGE->
