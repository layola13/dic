kumbaya:/kʌmˈbaɪə/ | n. |和平颂歌；和睦相处 | A song of peace and unity, often sung in a circle, typically associated with campfire sing-alongs and spiritual gatherings.  It's often used ironically to suggest naive or overly simplistic approaches to solving complex social problems. | Example:  They sang "Kumbaya" around the campfire.  他们围着篝火唱起了《和平颂》。  The politician's simplistic solutions felt like a "Kumbaya" moment. 这位政治家的简单解决方案让人感觉像是一个“和平颂”时刻。| 近义词：harmony, unity, togetherness | 反义词：discord, conflict, disunity | 名词
