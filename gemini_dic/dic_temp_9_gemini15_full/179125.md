redfish: /ˈrɛdˌfɪʃ/ | n. |红鱼，红鲷鱼 | A type of fish, typically reddish in color, belonging to the family Sciaenidae.  Many species are called redfish, and the term can be ambiguous depending on region and context.  The term often refers to species of the genus Sebastes,  especially in the North Atlantic.  | 例句：We caught several redfish during our fishing trip. 我们在钓鱼旅行中钓了几条红鱼。

The redfish population is declining due to overfishing.  由于过度捕捞，红鱼数量正在下降。


近义词：ocean perch (for some species),  sea bass (depending on species) | 反义词：N/A | 词性：n.
