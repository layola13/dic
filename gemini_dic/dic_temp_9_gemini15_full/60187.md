efficacity:/ˌɛfɪˈkeɪsəti/| n. |功效；有效性 |The efficacity of the new drug is being tested. 新药的疗效正在测试中。|近义词：effectiveness, potency, efficacy |反义词：ineffectiveness, ineffectiveness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The efficacity of this treatment is high. = 这种治疗的疗效很高。
This medicine shows high efficacity in treating the disease. = 这种药在治疗这种疾病方面疗效很高。


一般过去时:
The efficacity of the previous treatment was low. = 之前的治疗疗效很低。
The study showed that the drug's efficacity was significantly higher than expected. = 研究表明，该药的疗效远高于预期。


现在完成时:
Researchers have demonstrated the efficacity of the new vaccine. = 研究人员已经证明了这种新疫苗的疗效。
The long-term efficacity of this method has yet to be determined. = 此方法的长期疗效还有待确定。


过去完成时:
They had already questioned the efficacity of the old method before introducing the new one. = 在引入新方法之前，他们就已经质疑旧方法的疗效了。
It had been proven that the efficacity of the traditional treatment was limited.  =  已经证明传统疗法的疗效有限。


将来时:
The trial will determine the efficacity of the new therapy. = 试验将确定新疗法的疗效。
We expect the efficacity of the new drug to be significantly improved. = 我们预计新药的疗效将得到显著提高。


虚拟语气:
If the efficacity were higher, we would use it more widely. = 如果疗效更高，我们会更广泛地使用它。
I wish the efficacity of this treatment was more predictable. = 我希望这种治疗的疗效更可预测。


被动语态:
The efficacity of the drug is being studied. = 正在研究该药物的疗效。
The efficacity of the program was questioned by the committee. = 该项目的疗效受到了委员会的质疑。


疑问句:
What is the efficacity of this treatment? = 这种治疗的疗效如何？
Does the research support the claimed efficacity of the product? = 研究是否支持该产品声称的疗效？


否定句:
The study did not show significant efficacity. = 该研究没有显示出显著的疗效。
The treatment's efficacity is questionable. = 该治疗的疗效值得怀疑。


PHRASE->
high efficacity = 高效
demonstrate efficacity = 证明疗效
question the efficacity = 质疑疗效
prove efficacity = 证明疗效
assess efficacity = 评估疗效


词源:
From Latin *efficacitas, from efficax "effective," from efficere "to effect, accomplish," from ex- "out" + facere "to do, make."  The suffix -acity is a Latinate suffix forming nouns of quality or state.
