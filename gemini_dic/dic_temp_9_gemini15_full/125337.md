multilateralize:/ˌmʌltiˈlætərəlaɪz/| vt.|使多边化；使多国参与|To make something involve more than two sides or countries; to make a treaty or agreement involve multiple nations.|The government is trying to multilateralize the trade agreement. 政府正试图将贸易协定多边化。|近义词：internationalize, globalize|反义词：bilateralize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They multilateralize the negotiations. = 他们使谈判多边化。
The company multilateralizes its supply chain. = 这家公司使其供应链多边化。


一般过去时:
The treaty was multilateralized last year. = 该条约去年被多边化了。
The conflict was successfully multilateralized. = 冲突成功地实现了多边化。


现在完成时:
They have multilateralized the peace process. = 他们已经使和平进程多边化了。
The organization has multilateralized its operations. = 该组织已经使其运作多边化了。


过去完成时:
The agreement had been multilateralized before the summit. = 在峰会之前，协议已经实现了多边化。
The issue had been successfully multilateralized. = 该问题已成功地实现了多边化。


将来时:
They will multilateralize the trade talks next month. = 他们下个月将使贸易谈判多边化。
The project will be multilateralized in the near future. = 该项目将在不久的将来实现多边化。


虚拟语气:
If they multilateralized the dispute, it might be resolved. = 如果他们使争议多边化，它或许就能得到解决。
It would be beneficial if they multilateralized the efforts. = 如果他们使努力多边化，将是有益的。


被动语态:
The agreement was multilateralized by the participating nations. = 该协议由参与国多边化。
The issue is being multilateralized through international cooperation. = 该问题正在通过国际合作实现多边化。


疑问句:

否定句:
