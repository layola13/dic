eotf:/ˈiːˌoʊtɛf/| n. |电光转换函数|Electro-Optical Transfer Function.  It's a mathematical function that describes the relationship between the light intensity input to a display device (e.g., a monitor or projector) and the resulting luminance output.  This function is crucial for accurate color reproduction and ensuring consistent image quality across different devices and viewing conditions.  It's often used in video production and image processing to manage the color space transformation. |Example:  The eotf of the monitor was calibrated to ensure accurate color representation.  监视器的EOTF经过校准以确保准确的颜色表示。|近义词：transfer function, tone mapping curve |反义词：无|名词
