scaff:/skæf/| n.| 脚手架；支架 | A temporary structure used for supporting workers and materials during the construction or repair of a building.  |例句：The workers erected a scaff around the building. 工人们在建筑物周围搭建了脚手架。|例句：The artist used a scaff to reach the high parts of the canvas.艺术家用支架够到画布的高处。|近义词：scaffolding, staging, framework |反义词：N/A
