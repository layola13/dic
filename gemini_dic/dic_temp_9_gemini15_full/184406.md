responsible: /rɪˈspɑːnsəbl/ | adj. | 负责任的；可靠的；应承担责任的 |  Having a duty to deal with something or of having control over someone.  Someone who is responsible shows good judgment and carefulness. |

例句：
He is responsible for the project's success.  = 他对项目的成功负有责任。

She is a responsible mother. = 她是一位负责任的母亲。


近义词：accountable, liable, dependable, trustworthy

反义词：irresponsible, unreliable, careless, negligent


词源:
The word "responsible" comes from the Old French word "responsable," which itself derives from the Latin word "responsalis." This Latin word is formed from "respondere," meaning "to answer" or "to reply."  The sense of responsibility evolved from the idea of being able to answer for one's actions or choices.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is responsible for managing the team. = 他负责管理团队。
She is responsible for the children's safety. = 她负责孩子们的安全。


一般过去时:
He was responsible for the accident. = 他对事故负有责任。
She was responsible for cleaning the house. = 她负责打扫房子。


现在完成时:
They have been responsible for many successful projects. = 他们已经负责过许多成功的项目。
She has always been responsible and reliable. = 她一直都很负责可靠。


过去完成时:
He had been responsible for the department before his promotion. = 他晋升之前负责该部门。
They had already been responsible for similar tasks. = 他们之前已经负责过类似的任务。


将来时:
She will be responsible for training the new employees. = 她将负责培训新员工。
He will be responsible for the budget next year. = 他明年将负责预算。


虚拟语气:
If he were responsible, he would have apologized. = 如果他负责任的话，他会道歉的。
If she were responsible for the mistake, she would admit it. = 如果她对这个错误负责，她会承认的。


被动语态:
The team is responsible for the task. = 团队负责这项任务。(主动语态更常用)
The damage was considered to be the responsibility of the contractor.  =  损害被认为是承包商的责任。(被动语态强调责任归属)


疑问句:
Who is responsible for this mess? = 谁应该为这烂摊子负责？
Is he responsible enough for the job? = 他够格胜任这份工作吗？


否定句:
He is not responsible for the delay. = 他不应对延误负责。
She was not responsible for the accident. = 她对事故不负责任。
