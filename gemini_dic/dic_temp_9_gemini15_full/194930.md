semi-internal:/ˌsemiɪnˈtɜːrnəl/ | adj. | 半内部的，半内在的 | Partially internal; relating to or situated within something but not completely enclosed or integrated.  The term often implies a degree of access or control, but not full integration. For example, a semi-internal system might be accessible from the outside, but its core functionality remains within a protected environment.  | Example: The company uses a semi-internal communication system, allowing external partners limited access to project updates.  公司使用半内部通信系统，允许外部合作伙伴有限地访问项目更新。 |近义词：partially internal, partially integrated | 反义词：external, fully internal
