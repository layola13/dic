lexicological:/ˌlɛksɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/| adj.| 词汇学的；词典学的 | Relating to lexicology, the branch of linguistics that studies the vocabulary of a language.  It deals with the meaning, history, and use of words and their relationships to each other. | Example: The professor delivered a fascinating lexicological lecture on the evolution of slang.  教授做了一个关于俚语演变的引人入胜的词汇学讲座。 | 近义词：lexical, semantic | 反义词：N/A
