placket:/ˈplækɪt/| n.| 门襟；（衬衫等的）前襟开口 | A slit or opening, typically in a garment, such as a shirt or blouse, often reinforced with a facing.  It allows for easier dressing and may be fastened with buttons or other closures. | Example: The shirt had a placket down the front. 这件衬衫前面有门襟。  The dressmaker carefully stitched the placket.  裁缝仔细地缝好了门襟。|近义词：slit, opening, vent | 反义词：seam, closure
