identically:/aɪˈdɛntɪkli/ | adv. | 完全相同地；完全一样地 | In exactly the same way; exactly alike.  The twins dressed identically. |  近义词：exactly, precisely, similarly | 反义词：differently, dissimilarly


例句：

The two paintings were identically sized. = 这两幅画大小完全相同。

They answered the questions identically. = 他们对问题的回答完全相同。


词源：

From identical + -ly.  "Identical" itself comes from Late Latin *identica, from Latin idem "the same".


用法说明：

identically is an adverb, modifying a verb, adjective, or another adverb to emphasize the exact similarity or sameness of something.  It stresses that the similarity is complete and without any difference.
