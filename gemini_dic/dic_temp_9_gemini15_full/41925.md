cosmolatry: /ˌkɒzməˈleɪtri/ | n. |宇宙崇拜；对宇宙的崇拜 | The worship of the cosmos or universe;  a religious system centered on the veneration of the cosmos.  Cosmolatry often involves the belief that the universe itself is divine or a manifestation of a divine being. | Example:  Some ancient cultures practiced cosmolatry, viewing celestial bodies as gods or sacred entities.  一些古代文化奉行宇宙崇拜，将天体视为神灵或神圣实体。| 近义词:  astrolatry (astrology as a form of worship) | 反义词: atheism, secularism
