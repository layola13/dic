deservedly:/dɪˈzɜːrvɪdli/ | adv. | 应受地；理所当然地 |  In a way that is deserved or justified.  It indicates that something happened or was given because it was earned or rightfully due. | 例句：She deservedly won the award for her outstanding performance. 她凭借杰出的表现理所当然地赢得了奖项。

She deservedly received praise for her hard work. 她因辛勤工作而理所当然地受到赞扬。| 近义词：rightfully, justly, rightfully, properly | 反义词：unjustly, undeservedly, unfairly | 词性：副词
