noncirculatory: /ˌnɒnˈsɜːkjʊlətəri/ | adj. |非循环的；非循环系统的| Not relating to or involving circulation; not circulating.  This adjective describes something that doesn't involve the movement of fluids (like blood or lymph) or the flow of information or materials in a cyclical pattern. | Example: The noncirculatory functions of the liver include detoxification and protein synthesis.  肝脏的非循环功能包括解毒和蛋白质合成。|近义词：non-circulating, non-circulative |反义词：circulatory
