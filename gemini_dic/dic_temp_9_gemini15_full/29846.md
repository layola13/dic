causational:/ˌkɔːzeɪʃənəl/| adj.|因果的；导致的；起因的| Relating to or involving causation; causing something to happen.  It emphasizes the act of causing something. |例句：The causational link between smoking and lung cancer is well-established. 吸烟和肺癌之间的因果关系已得到充分证实。
例句：He explored the causational factors behind the economic crisis. 他探究了经济危机背后的因果因素。|近义词：causal, causative, etiological|反义词：unrelated, accidental, coincidental|词性:形容词
