quavering:/ˈkweɪvərɪŋ/| adj.|颤抖的，发抖的，声音颤抖的|trembling or shaking, especially from fear, weakness, or cold;  characterized by a trembling voice.|例句：Her voice was quavering with emotion.  她的声音因激动而颤抖。|例句：He gave a quavering laugh. 他发出颤抖的笑声。|近义词：trembling, shaking, shivering, vibrating|反义词：steady, firm, unwavering
