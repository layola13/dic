nonconcur:/ˌnɒnkənˈkɜːr/ | /ˌnɑːnkənˈkɜːr/ | v. |不同意；不一致 |To disagree; to not concur.  It implies a lack of agreement, often on a formal matter or decision. | The board members nonconcurred with the CEO's proposal. 董事会成员不同意首席执行官的提议。| concur, agree | concur, agree | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They nonconcur with the proposed changes. = 他们不同意拟议的变更。
We nonconcur on the best approach to the problem. = 我们对解决这个问题的最佳方法意见不一致。


一般过去时:
The committee nonconcurred on the budget allocation. = 委员会不同意预算分配。
He nonconcurred with the judge's ruling. = 他不同意法官的裁决。


现在完成时:
They have nonconcurred on every point so far. = 到目前为止，他们在每一点上都持不同意见。
The parties have nonconcurred on the terms of the settlement. = 各方在和解条款上意见不一致。


过去完成时:
They had nonconcurred on the strategy before the meeting even began. = 会议甚至还没开始，他们就已经对战略意见不一致了。
The negotiators had nonconcurred repeatedly throughout the discussions. = 在整个讨论过程中，谈判代表们多次意见不一致。


将来时:
They will likely nonconcur on the final decision. = 他们很可能会对最终决定持不同意见。
We anticipate that they will nonconcur with our recommendations. = 我们预计他们会不同意我们的建议。


虚拟语气:
If they nonconcurred, we would have to reconsider our options. = 如果他们不同意，我们就必须重新考虑我们的选择。
I wish they had nonconcurred earlier, it would have saved time. = 我希望他们早点表示不同意，这样可以节省时间。


被动语态:
The proposal was nonconcurred with by the majority of shareholders. = 该提案未获得大多数股东的同意。
His assessment was nonconcurred with by the independent auditors. = 他的评估未获得独立审计师的认可。


疑问句:
Did they nonconcur with the plan? = 他们不同意这个计划吗？
Will the committee nonconcur with our request? = 委员会会不同意我们的请求吗？


否定句:
They did not nonconcur with the decision. = 他们同意这个决定。
We did not nonconcur; we actually agreed completely. = 我们并没有不同意；我们实际上完全同意。


PHRASE->
nonconcur with a decision =不同意一项决定
nonconcur in a statement =不同意一份声明
nonconcur on a matter =在某事上意见不一致
nonconcur formally =正式表示不同意
to formally nonconcur = 正式表示不同意
