calvinists:/ˈkælvɪnɪsts/| n. |加尔文教徒；加尔文主义者 | Followers of the theological system of John Calvin, emphasizing God's sovereignty, predestination, and the absolute authority of Scripture.  Calvinists believe in the absolute sovereignty of God in salvation, meaning that God chooses who will be saved and who will not.  They also emphasize the importance of God's grace and the necessity of faith for salvation. |  Many Calvinists are active in missionary work. 许多加尔文教徒积极参与传教工作。 The debate between Arminians and Calvinists continues to this day. 阿米纽派和加尔文派之间的争论至今仍在继续。| 近义词：Protestants (a broader term) | 反义词：Arminians (holding opposing theological views) | 名词
