coverage:/ˈkʌvərɪdʒ/ | n. | 覆盖范围；报道；保险范围 | The extent to which something is covered, dealt with, or included.  It can refer to the physical area covered, the topics included in a report, or the amount of insurance protection. | 例句：The map shows the coverage area of the new cell phone network. 这张地图显示了新的手机网络的覆盖范围。

The news coverage of the hurricane was extensive.  飓风的新闻报道非常广泛。


近义词：extent, scope, breadth, reporting, insurance | 反义词：lack, absence, exclusion


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The report provides comprehensive coverage of the issue. = 这份报告全面报道了这个问题。
Our insurance policy offers good coverage for medical expenses. = 我们的保险政策为医疗费用提供了良好的保障。


一般过去时:
The newspaper's coverage of the election was biased. = 这家报纸对选举的报道有偏见。
The television coverage of the event was interrupted. = 对该事件的电视报道被打断了。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
