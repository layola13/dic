compromiser: /ˈkɒmprəmaɪzər/ | n. |  妥协者；调解人 | A person who makes compromises; someone who helps to settle disputes by finding solutions that satisfy both sides.  |  The mediator acted as a compromiser, finding a solution acceptable to both parties.  调解员充当了调解人的角色，找到了双方都能接受的解决方案。| negotiator, mediator, conciliator |  obstructionist, antagonist |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He compromises often to maintain peace. = 他经常妥协以维持和平。
She compromises her principles occasionally. = 她偶尔会妥协自己的原则。


一般过去时:
They compromised on the price of the house. = 他们就房价达成了妥协。
He compromised his position to get the deal done. = 他为了达成交易而牺牲了自己的立场。


现在完成时:
She has compromised her integrity in the past. = 她过去曾损害过自己的正直。
We have compromised on several issues to reach an agreement. = 为了达成协议，我们在几个问题上都做出了妥协。


过去完成时:
They had compromised their position before the negotiations began. = 在谈判开始之前，他们就已经让步了。
She had compromised her safety by going alone at night. = 她独自一人在晚上出行，危及了自己的安全。


将来时:
He will compromise on some points to reach a consensus. = 他将在某些方面做出让步以达成共识。
They will compromise their principles if necessary. = 必要时，他们将妥协自己的原则。


虚拟语气:
If he compromised more, we could reach an agreement. = 如果他多做些让步，我们就能达成协议。
I wish she hadn't compromised her values. = 我真希望她没有牺牲自己的价值观。


被动语态:
The agreement was compromised by the unexpected circumstances. = 协议因意外情况而受损。
His reputation was compromised by the scandal. = 他的名誉因丑闻而受损。


疑问句:
Will he compromise on the deadline? = 他会就截止日期妥协吗？
Has she compromised her beliefs? = 她有没有放弃自己的信仰？


否定句:
He will not compromise his beliefs under any circumstances.  = 无论如何，他都不会放弃自己的信仰。
They did not compromise on the key terms of the contract. = 他们没有在合同的关键条款上妥协。


PHRASE->
compromise on = 在...上妥协
reach a compromise = 达成妥协
make a compromise = 做出妥协
at a compromise = 以妥协的方式
a compromise solution = 一个折中的方案
