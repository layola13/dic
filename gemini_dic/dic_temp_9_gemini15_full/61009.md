eliminative:/ɪˈlɪmɪneɪtɪv/| adj.|排除的；消除的；旨在消除的 | Relating to or characterized by elimination; tending to eliminate.  This adjective describes something that aims to remove or get rid of something else. It often implies a systematic or thorough process of removal. |Example:  The eliminative process was rigorous, ensuring only the most qualified candidates advanced.  消除过程非常严格，确保只有最合格的候选人才能晋级。


The eliminative approach to the problem proved successful.  采用排除法解决这个问题证明是成功的。|近义词：purging, expunging, eradicative|反义词：additive, inclusive, augmentative
