overinstruct:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌkt/ | vt. | 过度指导，指导过多 | To instruct or give directions to excessively; to give more instruction than is necessary or helpful.  | The teacher overinstructed the students, leading to confusion rather than understanding.  老师对学生指导过度，导致他们更加困惑而不是理解。| instruct, teach, guide | underinstruct, neglect  | 动词
