clovers:/ˈkloʊvərz/ | n. |三叶草，苜蓿 |Three-leaved plants of the genus Trifolium, often considered lucky.  Clovers are commonly found in meadows and pastures, and are known for their three-leaf structure, with a rare four-leaf clover considered a lucky charm. |例句：I found a four-leaf clover in the field! = 我在田里找到了一片四叶草！|例句：The sheep grazed contentedly in the field of clovers. = 羊群心满意足地在苜蓿地里吃草。|近义词：shamrock, trefoil |反义词：无
