predestination:/ˌpriːdɛstɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |预定；命中注定；天命|The doctrine that God has already decided who will be saved and who will be damned.  It implies that human free will is limited or nonexistent in relation to salvation. Predestination is a central theological concept in Calvinism and some other branches of Christianity. |例句：The belief in predestination is a complex theological issue.  相信预定论是一个复杂的神学问题。

例句：Some people believe their entire lives are governed by predestination. 有些人相信他们的一生都受着预定的支配。


近义词：foreordination, fate, destiny|反义词：free will, chance, accident|词性：名词
