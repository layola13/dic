endemic:/ɛnˈdɛmɪk/ | adj. | 地方性的；特有的；（疾病）地方流行的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a particular people or place; native or confined to a particular region or people.  A disease that is endemic is regularly found among particular people or in a certain area. | Example:  Malaria is endemic in many tropical countries. 疟疾在许多热带国家是地方性疾病。  The koala is endemic to Australia. 考拉是澳大利亚特有的动物。 | 近义词：native, indigenous, local | 反义词：exotic, foreign, pandemic
