mambas:/ˈmɑːmbəz/| n.|曼巴蛇 |Highly venomous African snakes known for their speed and aggressiveness.|例句：Mambas are among the most dangerous snakes in Africa.  曼巴蛇是非洲最危险的蛇类之一。
