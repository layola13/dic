eliminates:/ɪˈlɪmɪneɪts/ | vt. |消除；排除；消灭 | To remove or get rid of something completely. |例句：The new policy eliminates many of the previous restrictions. 新政策消除了许多以前的限制。

The new software eliminates the need for manual data entry. 新软件消除了手动数据录入的需要。


近义词：remove, get rid of, eradicate, abolish, delete | 反义词：add, include, introduce, retain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The filter eliminates impurities from the water.  = 过滤器去除水中的杂质。
He eliminates distractions to improve focus. = 他排除干扰以提高专注力。

一般过去时:
The doctor eliminated the possibility of a heart attack. = 医生排除了心脏病发作的可能性。
They eliminated the problem by replacing the faulty part. = 他们通过更换故障部件解决了问题。

现在完成时:
The new treatment has eliminated the infection. = 新疗法已消除了感染。
She has eliminated all traces of the evidence. = 她已经消除了所有证据的痕迹。

过去完成时:
They had eliminated all the competitors before the final round. = 在决赛之前，他们已经淘汰了所有竞争对手。
He had eliminated the risk by taking precautions. = 他通过采取预防措施消除了风险。

将来时:
The new system will eliminate the need for paperwork. = 新系统将消除对文书工作的需求。
This medicine will eliminate the pain quickly. =  这种药会很快消除疼痛。


被动语态:
The problem was eliminated by a quick solution. = 问题被一个快速的解决方案解决了。
Many errors were eliminated during the editing process. =  在编辑过程中消除了许多错误。

疑问句:
How can we eliminate this source of error? = 我们如何消除这个错误来源？
What steps can be taken to eliminate poverty? = 可以采取哪些措施来消除贫困？

否定句:
The medicine did not eliminate the symptoms completely. = 这种药没有完全消除症状。
They failed to eliminate the threat entirely. = 他们未能完全消除威胁。


PHRASE->
eliminate from = 从…中消除
eliminate the possibility of = 排除…的可能性
eliminate competition = 消除竞争
eliminate waste = 消除浪费
eliminate the threat = 消除威胁

词源:
From Latin *ēlimināre, from ex- "out" + līmen "threshold".  The original meaning was "to remove from the threshold," and hence "to remove from consideration," leading to its modern meaning.
