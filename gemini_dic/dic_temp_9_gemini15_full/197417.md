shedload: /ˈʃɛdloʊd/ | n. |  一大堆，许多 | A large amount or quantity of something.  It's informal and emphasizes a large, perhaps overwhelming, quantity. |例句： I've got a shedload of work to do this week.  = 这周我有一大堆工作要做。


例句： There's a shedload of washing up to do after the party. = 派对后有一大堆碗要洗。


近义词：a lot, heaps, tons, loads, masses


反义词：a little, a few, hardly any
