The dog was skeletal from starvation. 那条狗因为饥饿而骨瘦如柴。


例句：The artist created a skeletal drawing of the building.  艺术家创作了一幅建筑物的骨架图。

近义词：bony, skinny, bare-bones, rudimentary

反义词：fleshy, plump, robust, complete
