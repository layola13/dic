part:/pɑːrt/ /pɑːrt/| n.|部分，零件，角色，份额|a portion or section of something larger; a component or element of a whole; a character in a play or film; a share or proportion.|例句：The car part is broken.  汽车零件坏了。|例句：She played a major part in the success of the project.她在项目的成功中扮演了重要角色。|近义词：section, piece, role, share|反义词：whole, entirety


USAGE->
