debunks:/dɪˈbʌŋks/ | vt. |揭穿；驳斥；消除…的谬误| To expose the falseness or hollowness of (a claim, belief, etc.); to disprove. |The scientist debunked the theory with new evidence. 科学家用新的证据驳斥了这一理论。|近义词：refute, disprove, expose, contradict |反义词：confirm, corroborate, substantiate

一般现在时:
He debunks conspiracy theories on his podcast. 他在他的播客上揭穿阴谋论。
The journalist debunks false information regularly.  记者定期揭穿虚假信息。

一般过去时:
She debunked the myth that all cats hate water. 她揭穿了猫都讨厌水的谬论。
The historian debunked the popular legend about the king. 历史学家揭穿了关于这位国王的流行传说。

现在完成时:
Scientists have debunked many old wives' tales. 科学家们已经揭穿了许多老掉牙的传说。
Researchers have debunked the claim that coffee causes heart disease. 研究人员已经驳斥了咖啡会导致心脏病的说法。

过去完成时:
Before the study, they had debunked several similar hypotheses. 在这项研究之前，他们已经驳斥了几个类似的假设。
By the end of the debate, he had debunked all of his opponent's arguments. 辩论结束时，他已经驳斥了对手的所有论点。

将来时:
She will debunk the false claims made by the politician. 她将揭穿这位政治家提出的虚假说法。
The professor will debunk the misconceptions surrounding climate change. 教授将揭穿围绕气候变化的误解。

虚拟语气:
If he had more evidence, he would debunk the conspiracy theory more effectively. 如果他有更多证据，他就能更有效地揭穿阴谋论。
If I were you, I would debunk those rumors immediately. 如果我是你，我会立即揭穿那些谣言。

被动语态:
The theory was debunked by a new study. 这一理论被一项新的研究驳斥了。
Many myths have been debunked over the years. 多年来，许多神话都被揭穿了。

疑问句:
Can we debunk this rumour effectively? 我们能有效地揭穿这个谣言吗？
How can we debunk these false accusations? 我们如何才能揭穿这些不实的指控？

否定句:
He didn't debunk the claims, but he did raise some doubts. 他没有揭穿这些说法，但他确实提出了一些疑问。
They couldn't debunk the evidence completely. 他们无法完全驳斥证据。
