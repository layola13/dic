pseudocultural:/ˌsuːdoʊˈkʌltʃərəl/ | adj. |伪文化的；假文化的 | Relating to or characteristic of a culture that is artificial, superficial, or imitative, often lacking genuine depth or authenticity.  It often refers to cultural elements adopted for commercial purposes or to appear fashionable rather than for genuine cultural reasons.  |例句：The festival was criticized for its pseudocultural displays, lacking any real connection to the local traditions.  这个节日因其缺乏与当地传统真正联系的伪文化展示而受到批评。

The marketing campaign felt pseudocultural, using stereotypical imagery to appeal to a broad audience without genuine understanding.  这场营销活动感觉很假文化，它使用刻板的意象来吸引广大受众，却缺乏真正的理解。|近义词：artificial, superficial, inauthentic, contrived, affected |反义词：authentic, genuine, real, traditional
