i-spy:/ˈaɪ ˌspaɪ/| n. v.| 我找到了；间谍游戏 | A children's game where one person looks at an object and others have to guess what it is. Also, the act of secretly watching or observing someone. |例句：We played i-spy in the car. 我们在车里玩“我找到了”游戏。
|例句：The police were i-spying on the suspect. 警方正在监视嫌疑人。|近义词：hide-and-seek, peek-a-boo, surveillance |反义词：openly, frankly.|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I spy a red car. = 我看到一辆红色的车。
They spy on their neighbors. = 他们监视他们的邻居。


一般过去时:
I spied a bird in the tree. = 我看到一只停在树上的鸟。
The detectives spied on the criminal gang for months. = 探员们监视这个犯罪团伙数月之久。


现在完成时:
I have spied several interesting things today. = 我今天已经发现了几件有趣的事情。
The government has been spying on its citizens for years. = 政府多年来一直在监视其公民。


过去完成时:
I had spied the thief before he escaped. = 在他逃跑之前，我已经发现了小偷。
The agency had been spying on the company before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，该机构一直在监视该公司。


将来时:
I will spy on him tomorrow. = 我明天将监视他。
They will spy on the enemy's movements. = 他们将监视敌人的行动。


虚拟语气:
If I spied a hidden treasure, I would be very happy. = 如果我发现一个隐藏的宝藏，我会非常高兴。
I wish I had spied the opportunity sooner. = 我希望我早点发现这个机会。


被动语态:
The hidden object was spied by the child. = 孩子找到了隐藏的物品。
The suspect was spied upon by the police. = 嫌疑人被警方监视。


疑问句:
What did you spy? = 你发现了什么？
Were they spying on us? = 他们在监视我们吗？


否定句:
I didn't spy anything interesting. = 我没有发现任何有趣的东西。
They didn't spy on her, they just saw her by chance. = 他们没有监视她，只是偶然看到了她。
