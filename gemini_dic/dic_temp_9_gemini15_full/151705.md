partridges:/ˈpɑːtrɪdʒɪz/| n.|鹧鸪 (复数)|Partridges are a group of birds belonging to the pheasant family, known for their plump bodies, short tails, and distinctive calls. They are often hunted for sport and their meat is considered a delicacy.  |例句：The hunters were hoping to bag a few partridges.  猎人们希望打到几只鹧鸪。|例句：We saw a covey of partridges in the field. 我们在田野里看到一群鹧鸪。|近义词：pheasants, quail|反义词：无|名词
