floodwaters: /ˈflʌdˌwɔːtərz/ | n. | 洪水；泛滥的河水 |  The strong currents of the floodwaters swept away everything in their path. 洪水的急流冲走了它前进道路上的所有东西。The floodwaters receded slowly after the storm. 暴风雨过后，洪水慢慢退去。 | 近义词：deluge, inundation | 反义词：drought


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The floodwaters damage the crops every year. 洪水每年都会破坏庄稼。
Floodwaters often rise rapidly in mountainous regions. 山区洪水经常迅速上涨。


一般过去时:
The floodwaters destroyed many homes last year. 去年洪水摧毁了许多房屋。
Floodwaters reached the second floor of many buildings during the storm. 暴风雨期间，洪水到达许多建筑物的二楼。


现在完成时:
The floodwaters have already caused significant damage to the infrastructure. 洪水已经对基础设施造成了重大破坏。
Floodwaters have receded in most areas, but some remain flooded. 大部分地区的洪水已经消退，但有些地区仍然被淹。


过去完成时:
By the time help arrived, the floodwaters had already subsided. 当救援到达时，洪水已经消退了。
The residents had never experienced floodwaters of such magnitude before. 居民们以前从未经历过如此大规模的洪水。


将来时:
Experts predict that the floodwaters will continue to rise overnight. 专家预测洪水将在夜间继续上涨。
If it continues to rain, the floodwaters will overwhelm the levees. 如果继续下雨，洪水将淹没堤坝。


虚拟语气:
If the floodwaters had not receded, many more people would have been displaced. 如果洪水没有消退，将会有更多的人流离失所。
I wish the floodwaters hadn't damaged my house so severely. 我真希望洪水没有严重损坏我的房子。


被动语态:
Many areas were inundated by the floodwaters. 许多地区被洪水淹没。
The roads were rendered impassable by the floodwaters. 道路被洪水阻断。


疑问句:
How high did the floodwaters rise? 洪水涨到多高？
Have the floodwaters caused any casualties? 洪水造成人员伤亡了吗？


否定句:
The floodwaters did not reach the town center. 洪水没有到达市中心。
The floodwaters haven't caused as much damage as initially feared. 洪水造成的破坏并没有最初担心的那么严重。
