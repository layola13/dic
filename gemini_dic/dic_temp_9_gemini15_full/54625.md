dismiss:/dɪsˈmɪs/ | vt. |解雇；驳回；解散；忽视 | to refuse to consider someone's idea or suggestion: to send someone away; to end a meeting or gathering |例句：The company dismissed him for his misconduct. 公司因他的不当行为而解雇了他。|例句：The judge dismissed the case due to lack of evidence. 由于证据不足，法官驳回了此案。|近义词：fire, reject, ignore, disregard |反义词：consider, accept, retain, employ


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dismisses his employees unfairly. = 他不公平地解雇他的员工。
She dismisses the idea as ridiculous. = 她认为这个想法荒谬可笑，不屑一顾。

一般过去时:
The teacher dismissed the class early. = 老师提前下课了。
The court dismissed the charges against him. = 法院驳回了对他的指控。

现在完成时:
They have dismissed several workers this year. = 今年他们已经解雇了几名工人。
The committee has dismissed the proposal. = 委员会已经否决了该提案。

过去完成时:
He had dismissed the warning as insignificant. = 他之前曾认为警告无关紧要而忽略了。
The jury had dismissed the evidence as unreliable. = 陪审团之前已认定证据不可靠而将其驳回。

将来时:
The judge will dismiss the case tomorrow. = 法官明天将驳回此案。
The manager will dismiss the meeting after the presentation. = 演示结束后，经理将解散会议。

虚拟语气:
If the evidence were stronger, the judge would not dismiss the case. = 如果证据更充分，法官就不会驳回此案。
I wish they had not dismissed my application. = 我希望他们没有驳回我的申请。

被动语态:
The employee was dismissed for stealing. = 这位员工因偷窃而被解雇。
The case was dismissed due to insufficient evidence. = 由于证据不足，此案被驳回。

疑问句:
Will the court dismiss the charges? = 法院会驳回指控吗？
Did the manager dismiss the employee? = 经理解雇了员工吗？

否定句:
The judge did not dismiss the case. = 法官没有驳回此案。
They will not dismiss the meeting early. = 他们不会提前结束会议。


PHRASE->
dismiss sb from a job = 解雇某人
dismiss an idea = 否定/摒弃一个想法
dismiss a case = 驳回一个案件
dismiss a meeting = 解散一个会议
dismiss sth as unimportant = 认为某事不重要
