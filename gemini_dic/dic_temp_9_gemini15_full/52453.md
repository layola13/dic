dieing: /ˈdaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 垂死的，将死的 | dying describes something in the process of ceasing to exist or live.  It often refers to a living being nearing death, but can also apply to other things that are fading or ending. | 例句：The dying man gasped for breath.  垂死的男人大口喘气。 The dying embers glowed faintly in the fireplace. 壁炉里将熄灭的余烬发出微弱的光。 | 近义词：fading, expiring, perishing, declining | 反义词：thriving, flourishing, burgeoning


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The plant is dying. = 这棵植物正在枯萎。
He is dying of cancer. = 他死于癌症。

一般过去时:
The flower was dying. = 这朵花快要枯萎了。
The old dog was dying peacefully. = 老狗平静地死去。

现在完成时:
The flowers have been dying since the frost. = 霜冻以来，花儿一直在枯萎。
My grandmother has been dying for months. = 我奶奶已经病危好几个月了。

过去完成时:
The patient had been dying for days before the doctor arrived. = 在医生到达之前，病人已经病危好几天了。
The tree had been dying for years before it finally fell. = 这棵树枯萎了好几年才最终倒下。

将来时:
The old building will be dying soon. = 这座旧建筑很快就要倒塌了。
If he doesn't get treatment, he will be dying soon. = 如果他不接受治疗，他很快就会死了。


虚拟语气:
If the plant were dying, I would water it more often. = 如果植物快要死了，我会更频繁地浇水。
I wish the dying flower could live longer. = 我希望快要枯萎的花能活得更久。


被动语态:
The dying flower was being watered by her. = 快要枯萎的花正在被她浇水。
The dying patient was being cared for by his family. = 病危的病人正由家人照顾。


疑问句:
Is the plant dying? = 这棵植物快要死了吗？
Is he dying? = 他快死了吗？


否定句:
The plant isn't dying. = 这棵植物没有枯萎。
He isn't dying yet. = 他还没死。


PHRASE->
a dying breed:  垂死的物种/行将消失的类型
dying wish:  临终遗愿
dying embers:  将熄灭的余烬
dying light:  将尽的光线
dying gasp: 临死前的喘息
