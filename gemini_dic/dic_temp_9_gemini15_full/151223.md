pared:/pɛrd/| v. |削去…的皮；修剪；准备；削减|To remove the skin or outer covering of something, especially fruit or vegetables; to trim or reduce something.|例句：She pared the apple before eating it. 她吃苹果前削去了苹果皮。|例句：He pared his expenses to save money. 他削减开支以省钱。|近义词：peel, trim, reduce|反义词：add, increase|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pares the potatoes every night. = 他每天晚上都削土豆皮。
She pares her nails carefully. = 她仔细地修剪指甲。


一般过去时:
I pared the carrots for the soup. = 我为汤削了胡萝卜。
The gardener pared back the overgrown bushes. = 园丁修剪了过度生长的灌木丛。


现在完成时:
She has pared the fruit for the pie. = 她已经为馅饼准备好了水果。
They have pared down their budget significantly. = 他们已经大幅削减了预算。


过去完成时:
He had pared the apples before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经削好了苹果。
The company had pared its workforce before the merger. = 公司在合并之前已经裁员了。


将来时:
I will pare the vegetables for the stir-fry. = 我将为炒菜准备蔬菜。
They will pare down the article to meet the deadline. = 他们将缩短文章以赶上截止日期。


虚拟语气:

I wish I had pared the expenses earlier. = 我希望我早点削减开支。


被动语态:
