/muː/| n.|亩|A unit of land area in China, traditionally about 0.067 hectares (0.165 acres), but varying slightly regionally.|例句：The farmer owns ten mu of farmland. 这个农民拥有十亩农田。|近义词：acre, hectare|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farm owns five mu of land. = 这个农场拥有五亩地。
How many mu of rice did they plant? = 他们种了多少亩水稻？


一般过去时:
They harvested ten mu of wheat last year. = 他们去年收获了十亩小麦。
How many mu did they cultivate? = 他们耕种了多少亩地？


现在完成时:
They have already planted all their mu of corn. = 他们已经种完了所有的玉米地。
How many mu have they harvested this season? = 这个季节他们收获了多少亩？


过去完成时:

How many mu had they planted before the drought? = 干旱之前他们种了多少亩？

How many mu will they cultivate next spring? = 明年春天他们将耕种多少亩？

I wish they had more mu to cultivate. = 我希望他们有更多地可以耕种。
