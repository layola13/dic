groining:/ˈɡrɔɪnɪŋ/| n. |腹股沟；拱顶；交叉拱顶|The intersection of two vaulted surfaces, typically forming a diagonal rib.  Groining can refer to the architectural feature itself or the act of constructing it.  It's often used in architecture to support ceilings or roofs. |例句：The cathedral boasts impressive groining in its vaulted ceilings. 这座大教堂以其拱顶天花板上的令人印象深刻的交叉拱顶而自豪。

The ancient Roman aqueducts are known for their sophisticated groining. 古罗马渡槽以其复杂的拱顶结构而闻名。|近义词：vaulting, arching |反义词：flat ceiling, plain ceiling |名词
