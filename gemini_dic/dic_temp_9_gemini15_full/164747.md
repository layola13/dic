prefectorial:/ˌprɛfɛkˈtɔːriəl/| adj.|管理的；监督的；学监的；（尤指）英国公学学监的|Relating to a prefect or prefects; characteristic of a prefect or prefects.  The term often refers to the authority or jurisdiction of a prefect, especially in the context of a British public school. |例句：The prefectorial system at Eton College is a long-standing tradition. 伊顿公学的学监制度是悠久的传统。

The prefectorial duties included maintaining order in the dormitories. 学监的职责包括维持宿舍的秩序。|近义词：supervisory,  administrative, governing|反义词：unsupervised, ungoverned
