revivify:/rɪˈvaɪvɪfaɪ/ | vt. |使复苏；使复活；使重新活跃；使振兴 |To restore to life, consciousness, or vigor.  To renew or refresh. |例句：The rain revivified the wilting plants.  雨水使枯萎的植物恢复了生机。

The doctor's treatment revivified the patient.  医生的治疗使病人恢复了活力。

近义词：reanimate, revitalize, refresh, rejuvenate | 反义词：kill, destroy, extinguish, deplete | 动词
