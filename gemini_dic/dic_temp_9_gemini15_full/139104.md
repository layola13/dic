obdurately:/ˈɑːbdʒʊrətli/ | adv. | 固执地；顽固地 | stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or course of action.  | He obdurately refused to admit his mistake. 他固执地拒绝承认自己的错误。 | stubbornly, inflexibly, persistently | yielding, compliant, amenable
