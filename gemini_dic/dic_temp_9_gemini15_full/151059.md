paraphrast: /ˈpærəfreɪst/ | n. |释义者，解释者，改写者 | A person who paraphrases; someone who restates something in different words.  A paraphrast aims to clarify the meaning of a text or speech without altering its essential message.  | Example: The professor acted as a paraphrast, explaining the complex theory in simpler terms for the students.  | 近义词: interpreter, translator, summarizer, reworder | 反义词:  None directly opposite, but "literal translator" could be considered a contrasting term.
