risings:/ˈraɪzɪŋz/| n.| 上升；兴起；上涨；（太阳、月亮等的）升起 | The rising of the sun.  日出。The rising prices. 物价上涨。 |The rising of the tide.  潮涨。The rising generation.  新一代。|  rising popularity, rising star, rising costs,  sunrise, sunset | falling, setting, decline | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun is rising in the east. = 太阳从东方升起。
The cost of living is rising steadily. = 生活成本稳步上涨。


一般过去时:
The river rose rapidly after the rain. = 雨后河水迅速上涨。
The star rose in the night sky. = 星星在夜空中升起。


现在完成时:
The price of oil has been rising for months. = 油价已经上涨了几个月。
Sea levels have been rising due to global warming. = 由于全球变暖，海平面一直在上升。


过去完成时:
The water had risen significantly before we arrived. = 我们到达之前水位已经显著上升了。
The temperature had risen sharply by noon. = 到中午气温已经急剧上升。


将来时:
The stock market will be rising next year. = 股市明年将会上涨。
The sun will be rising early tomorrow. = 明天太阳会很早升起。


虚拟语气:
If the water were rising, we would evacuate. = 如果水位上涨，我们将撤离。
I wish the sun were rising earlier. = 我希望太阳能早点升起。


被动语态:
The matter was being risen by the committee. = 委员会正在讨论这个问题。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，通常用主动语态)
The issue is being raised by many people. = 这个问题正在被许多人提出。


疑问句:
Are the tides rising today? = 今天潮汐上涨吗？
Is the sun rising yet? = 太阳升起来了吗？


否定句:
The sun isn't rising yet. = 太阳还没升起。
The prices aren't rising as fast this year. = 今年物价上涨速度没有那么快。
