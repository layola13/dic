priced:/praɪst/| adj.|标价的，定价的|Having a price assigned or attached.  The item is priced to sell quickly.|The newly priced menu offers a wider variety of dishes.|近义词：valued, costed|反义词：priceless, unpriced

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The apples are priced at $2 per pound. = 苹果标价每磅2美元。
This hotel is priced reasonably. = 这家酒店价格合理。


一般过去时:
The tickets were priced higher than expected. = 票价比预期高。
The house was priced to sell quickly. = 这房子定价是为了快速出售。


现在完成时:
The goods have been priced for the sale. = 商品已为促销定价。
The products have been priced competitively. = 产品已具有竞争力的价格。


过去完成时:
The items had been priced before the store opened. = 商品在商店开业前就已经定价了。
The tickets had already been priced when I arrived. = 我到达时票价已经定好了。


将来时:
The new model will be priced aggressively. = 新型号将采用激进的定价策略。
The items will be priced according to their quality. = 商品将根据其质量定价。


虚拟语气:
If the product were priced lower, more people would buy it. = 如果产品价格更低，更多人会购买它。
I wish the car were priced more affordably. = 我希望这辆车价格更实惠。


被动语态:
The goods are priced fairly. = 商品价格公平。
The items were priced too high. = 商品价格太高。


疑问句:
How are the items priced? = 商品是如何定价的？
Are the services priced separately? = 服务是分开定价的吗？


否定句:
The items are not yet priced. = 商品尚未定价。
The house was not priced competitively. = 这房子的价格没有竞争力。


PHRASE->
fairly priced = 价格合理的
competitively priced = 价格具有竞争力的
reasonably priced = 价格合理的
overpriced = 价格过高的
underpriced = 价格过低的
