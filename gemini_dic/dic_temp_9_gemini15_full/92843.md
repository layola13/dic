/ɪks/ | n. | 学科；专业；学术；【复数】课程 |  The word "ics" is a suffix forming nouns that denote a field of study, a system, or a practice. It's often used to create names for academic disciplines or technical subjects. It originates from the Greek word "-ικός" (-ikos), which means "relating to" or "pertaining to".  | Examples: Physics is the study of matter and energy.  | 近义词: subject, discipline, field, specialty | 反义词: None directly applicable.
