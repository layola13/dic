lowballer:/ˈloʊˌbɔːlər/| n. v.| 出价过低者；故意压低价格的人；|Someone who makes a very low offer, especially in a negotiation.  A person who consistently offers very low prices.  |例句：He's a known lowballer, so don't expect him to pay full price. 他是个出了名的压价者，所以别指望他能付全价。

The contractor was a lowballer, bidding significantly less than other companies.  承包商故意压低价格，比其他公司报的价低得多。

近义词：underbidder, cheapskate, miser, tightwad |反义词：high roller, overpayer

词性：名词，动词
