messes:/ˈmɛsɪz/| n.| 混乱；脏乱；困境；一团糟 | a state of disorder or untidiness; a difficult or unpleasant situation |  例句：The kitchen is a complete mess after the party. 派对后厨房一片狼藉。

例句：He's gotten himself into a terrible mess with his finances. 他把自己的财务搞得一团糟。


近义词：disorder, chaos, muddle, jumble | 反义词：order, tidiness, cleanliness


词源:源自古英语 *mes,  意为“混合物，糊状物”。  后来演变出“混乱”的含义。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog messes up the house every day. = 这只狗每天都把房子弄得乱七八糟。
My roommate always messes with my things. = 我的室友总是乱动我的东西。


一般过去时:
He messed up the project badly. = 他把项目搞砸了。
She messed her hair up before going out. = 她出门前把头发弄乱了。


现在完成时:
They have messed up the garden with their toys. = 他们用玩具把花园弄乱了。
The children have messed the room up again. = 孩子们又把房间弄乱了。


过去完成时:
He had messed up the presentation before he even began. = 他甚至还没开始就搞砸了演示。
She had messed up her chance for promotion. = 她已经错过了晋升的机会。


将来时:
They will mess up the whole plan if they are not careful. = 如果他们不小心，他们会把整个计划搞砸。
He will mess up his life if he keeps this up. = 如果他继续这样下去，他会毁了自己的生活。


虚拟语气:
If I had known better, I wouldn't have messed up the interview. = 如果我早知道，我不会在面试中搞砸的。
I wish I hadn't messed up the recipe. = 我真希望我没有把食谱搞砸。


被动语态:
The room was messed up by the children. = 房间是被孩子们弄乱的。
The project was messed up due to poor planning. = 由于计划不周，项目被搞砸了。


疑问句:
Did he mess up the report? = 他把报告弄糟了吗？
Have you ever messed up badly at work? = 你在工作中有没有严重犯过错？


否定句:
I didn't mess up the experiment. = 我没有把实验搞砸。
She didn't mean to mess up your plans. = 她不是故意要打乱你的计划的。



PHRASE->
make a mess = 弄得一团糟
mess around = 闲逛；胡闹
mess with = 惹；招惹
mess up = 搞砸；弄乱
in a mess = 处于混乱状态
