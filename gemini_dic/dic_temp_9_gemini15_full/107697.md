lanolin:/ˈlænəlɪn/| n. |羊毛脂|A yellowish waxy substance secreted by the sebaceous glands of sheep, used in cosmetics and ointments.  It's a natural emollient and moisturizer.|例句：The lotion contains lanolin to help soften and moisturize dry skin. 这款乳液含有羊毛脂，有助于软化和滋润干燥的皮肤。|例句：Many lip balms utilize lanolin for its protective and hydrating qualities. 许多润唇膏都利用羊毛脂的保护和保湿特性。|近义词：wool fat, adeps lanae|反义词：N/A|名词
