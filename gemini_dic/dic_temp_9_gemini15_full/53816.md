disconnected: /ˌdɪskəˈnɛktɪd/ | adj. | 脱节的；失去联系的；断开的 |  Not connected; separated.  Lacking connection or continuity. Feeling isolated or alienated. |

例句：

The two parts of the machine are disconnected.  = 这台机器的两个部件脱节了。

He felt disconnected from his family after moving to a new city. = 他搬到新城市后感觉与家人脱节了。


近义词：separated, unconnected, isolated, detached, disjointed, fragmented

反义词：connected, linked, joined, united, integrated, coherent


词源：dis- (否定前缀) + connect (连接) + -ed (过去分词后缀)  The word evolved from the verb "disconnect," meaning to separate or break a connection.
