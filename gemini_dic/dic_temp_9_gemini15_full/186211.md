rheuminess:/ˈruːminɪs/| adj.|多涕的；粘液性的；潮湿的 |  The quality or state of being rheumy; characterized by or resembling rheum.  It suggests an excessive amount of watery mucus, often associated with the eyes or nose,  implying a slightly unpleasant dampness or stickiness. |例句：The old man's rheuminess made it difficult for him to see clearly. 老人的多涕使他难以看清。 The rheuminess of the autumn air clung to everything. 秋天的潮湿空气粘附在一切事物上。|近义词：watery, moist, damp, mucous,  |反义词：dry, arid
