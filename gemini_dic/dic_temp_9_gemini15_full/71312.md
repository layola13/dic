fizzes:/ˈfɪzɪz/| v.|发出嘶嘶声，冒泡|To make a hissing sound, typically because of the escape of gas.|The soda fizzed in the glass. 苏打水在杯子里发出嘶嘶声。The champagne fizzed merrily in the bottle. 香槟酒在瓶子里快乐地冒泡。|近义词：sizzle, bubble, effervesce|反义词：be still, be quiet


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lemonade fizzes when I open the bottle. = 当我打开瓶子时，柠檬水会发出嘶嘶声。
The champagne fizzes constantly. = 香槟酒不断地冒泡。

一般过去时:
The soda fizzed as I poured it. = 当我倒苏打水时，它发出了嘶嘶声。

现在完成时:
The bottle has fizzed over. = 瓶子里的液体溢出来了（因为冒泡）。
The drink has fizzed and gone flat. = 饮料已经冒泡然后变平了。

过去完成时:
The soda had already fizzed before I could taste it. = 在我尝到苏打水之前，它已经冒泡了。
The champagne had fizzed out by the time we got to it. = 等我们拿到香槟酒的时候，它已经停止冒泡了。
