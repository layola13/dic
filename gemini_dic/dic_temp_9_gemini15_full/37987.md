complicates:/ˈkɒmplɪkeɪts/ /ˈkɑːmplɪkeɪts/| vt.|使复杂化；使变得困难|To make (something) more complex or difficult.|The unexpected rain complicated our travel plans.  意外的雨雪使我们的旅行计划复杂化了。
The new regulations complicated the process significantly. 新规章显著地使流程复杂化了。|intricate, complexify|simplify, clarify|动词
