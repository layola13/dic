recompensation:/ˌrɛkɑːmpɛnˈseɪʃən/| n. |补偿；赔偿；报酬| The act of compensating for something; the making of amends for a wrong; payment for services rendered.  It implies a making good for loss or damage, or a reward for service. |例句：The company offered recompensation for the damaged goods. 公司为损坏的货物提供了赔偿。

例句：He received fair recompensation for his hard work. 他得到了与其辛勤工作相称的报酬。


近义词：compensation, indemnity, remuneration, amends, redress

反义词：penalty, punishment, loss


词性：名词
