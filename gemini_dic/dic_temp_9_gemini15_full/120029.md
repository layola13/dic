millionism:/ˈmɪljənɪzəm/| n. |百万富翁统治；百万富翁的思想或行为|The belief that society is or should be controlled by millionaires.  This term is rarely used and lacks widespread acceptance.  It suggests a social or political system where the wealthy elite hold disproportionate power and influence.|例句：The rise of millionism is a concerning trend, leading to increased inequality and social unrest. 百万富翁统治的兴起是一个令人担忧的趋势，导致不平等加剧和社会动荡。|近义词：plutocracy, oligarchy|反义词：democracy, egalitarianism
