nibbing:/ˈnɪbɪŋ/| n.|啄食；细小的一口；|The act of a bird pecking at food or a small amount of something eaten.  |例句：The birds were busy with their nibbing at the scattered seeds.  鸟儿正忙着啄食散落的种子。
The cake was delicious; I only had a small nibbing. 蛋糕很好吃；我只吃了一小口。|近义词：pecking, picking, biting|反义词：gorging, devouring|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds are nibbing at the seeds. = 鸟儿正在啄食种子。
He is nibbing at his sandwich. = 他正在吃他的三明治。

一般过去时:
The chickens nibbed at the corn. = 小鸡啄食玉米。
She nibbed at her salad. = 她吃了一点沙拉。


现在完成时:
The squirrels have nibbed at all the nuts. = 松鼠已经把所有的坚果都啃了。
The dog has nibbed at the bone. = 狗啃了骨头。


过去完成时:
The birds had nibbed at the berries before I arrived. = 我到之前，鸟儿已经啄食了浆果。
She had nibbed at the cake before the party started. = 派对开始前，她已经吃了一点蛋糕。


将来时:
The mice will nibble at the cheese. = 老鼠将啃食奶酪。(Note:  "nibble" is more common than "nib" in the future tense)
He will nib at the apple. = 他会啃苹果。(Note:  "nibble" is more common than "nib" in the future tense)


虚拟语气:
If I were a bird, I would nib at the seeds. = 如果我是一只鸟，我会啄食种子。
I wish I could nib at that delicious chocolate. = 我真希望我能吃一点那美味的巧克力。


被动语态:
The bread was nibbed at by the mice. = 面包被老鼠啃食了。
The cake was nibbed at by the children. = 蛋糕被孩子们吃了一点。


疑问句:
Are the ducks nibbing at the bread? = 鸭子在吃面包吗？
Was he nibbing at his food? = 他在吃东西吗？


否定句:
The birds were not nibbing at the fruit. = 鸟儿没有啄食水果。
She didn't nib at the cookies. = 她没有吃饼干。
