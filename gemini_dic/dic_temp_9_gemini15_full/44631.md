crown:/kraʊn/| n. v.|王冠；顶点；加冕；加冠|A crown is a circular ornament worn on the head, typically by a monarch as a symbol of royalty. It can also refer to the topmost part of something or the act of placing a crown on someone's head.|例句：The queen wore a jeweled crown. 女王戴着一顶珠宝王冠。

The dentist crowned the damaged tooth. 牙医给受损的牙齿戴上了牙冠。|近义词：diadem, coronet|反义词：base, bottom
