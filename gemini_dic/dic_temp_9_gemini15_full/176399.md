re-hammers:/ˌriːˈhæmər/| v.|反复敲打；重新锤炼|To hammer again; to rework or refine something by hammering.|例句：The blacksmith re-hammered the horseshoe to make it fit perfectly.  铁匠反复敲打马蹄铁使其完美契合。

例句：He re-hammered his argument, trying to make it more persuasive. 他反复锤炼自己的论点，试图使其更有说服力。


近义词：rework, refine, reshape, reforge


反义词：leave alone, abandon
