pepperiness:/ˈpɛpəriˌnɛs/ | n. |辛辣；刺激；活力 | The quality of being peppery; a pungent or spicy flavor or quality.  It can also refer to a lively or spirited quality. |例句：The pepperiness of the chili made my mouth water.  辣椒的辛辣味让我垂涎欲滴。

The sauce had a delightful pepperiness that complemented the fish perfectly. 这酱汁带有令人愉悦的辛辣味，与鱼肉完美搭配。

近义词：pungency, spiciness, zing, zest, liveliness |反义词：blandness, mildness
