fitters:/ˈfɪtərz/| n.|安装工；装配工；(衣服)试穿员|People who install or repair machinery, equipment, or fittings.  They might also adjust or modify existing installations.  In the context of clothing, it refers to someone who fits clothes on customers.|例句：The fitters installed the new boiler quickly and efficiently. 安装工快速有效地安装了新锅炉。

The tailor is a skilled fitter, ensuring the perfect fit for each customer.  裁缝是一位熟练的试衣员，确保每位顾客都能完美合身。|近义词：installers, mechanics, adjusters|反义词：dismantlers, removers
