resail:/ˌriːˈseɪl/| v.|再航行，重新启航|To sail again; to set sail again after having been at anchor or in port.|例句：After a brief stop, the ship will resail for the next port.  停靠片刻后，船将再次启航前往下一个港口。
例句：The captain decided to resail despite the stormy weather. 船长决定尽管暴风雨来袭也要再次启航。|近义词：reembark, restart, recommence|反义词：moor, dock, land

He resails every summer. 他每年夏天都重新启航。
