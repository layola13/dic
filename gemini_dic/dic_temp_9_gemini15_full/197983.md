shmoozing:/ʃmuːzɪŋ/| v.|闲聊；套近乎；拉关系 |To engage in casual, often informal conversation, especially for social or professional networking purposes.  It often implies a degree of flattery or ingratiation. |例句：He spent the evening shmoozing with potential investors. 他整个晚上都在和潜在投资者闲聊套近乎。

例句：She was shmoozing with the CEO at the conference.她在会议上与首席执行官套近乎。


近义词：networking, socializing, chatting, hobnobbing,  schmoozing


反义词：isolating, ignoring, shunning


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shmoozes with clients every week. = 他每周都和客户闲聊。
They shmooze at industry events. = 他们在行业活动上互相套近乎。


一般过去时:
She shmoozed with the celebrities at the party. = 她在派对上和名人们闲聊。
