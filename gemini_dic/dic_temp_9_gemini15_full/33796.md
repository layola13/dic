circumduction:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈdʌkʃən/| n. |环绕运动；旋转；环行|The act of moving a limb or body part in a circular motion.  It involves a combination of flexion, extension, abduction, and adduction. |例句：The physical therapist instructed the patient to perform circumduction exercises for shoulder rehabilitation. 物理治疗师指导病人进行肩部康复的环绕运动练习。

例句：The dancer's graceful circumduction of her arm captivated the audience. 舞蹈演员优雅的环绕手臂动作迷住了观众。

近义词：rotation, revolution, gyration|反义词：linear motion, straight movement |词性：名词


词源:

来自拉丁语 *circumducere，由circum-（周围，环绕）和ducere（引导，带领）构成。
