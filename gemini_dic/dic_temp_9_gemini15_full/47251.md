dci:/ˈdiːsiːaɪ/|abbr.|数据中心基础设施|Data Center Infrastructure|DCI refers to the infrastructure that supports the operation of a data center. This includes the physical components like servers, networking equipment, storage, power, cooling, and security systems.  It also encompasses the management and monitoring systems that ensure the data center's reliability and efficiency.  The term is often used in the context of connecting multiple data centers, referring to the network infrastructure and technologies used to link them.|Example: The company invested heavily in upgrading its DCI to handle the increased data traffic.|近义词：data center infrastructure, data center interconnect|反义词:N/A
