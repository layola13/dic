drum-up:/drʌm ʌp/| vt.|鼓动；煽动；筹集|To drum up support for something means to try to get people to support it, often by enthusiastically promoting it.  It implies a somewhat active and possibly forceful effort to gain support.|例句：The campaign is drumming up support for its new initiative. 这场运动正在为其新计划争取支持。
例句：They're drumming up business by offering special discounts. 他们正在通过提供特别折扣来争取生意。|近义词：generate, solicit, rally, promote|反义词：suppress, discourage, dampen
