knead:/niːd/| vt.|揉捏；按摩；充分考虑|To work dough or clay into a uniform mixture with the hands; to massage.  |例句：She kneaded the dough until it was smooth. 她揉捏面团直到它变得光滑。
例句：The therapist kneaded the muscles in my back.理疗师按摩了我的背部肌肉。|近义词：massage, work, manipulate |反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I knead the dough every morning. = 我每天早上都揉面团。
She kneads the clay before sculpting. = 她雕塑前先揉捏黏土。

一般过去时:
He kneaded the bread dough for ten minutes. = 他揉面团揉了十分钟。
The masseuse kneaded my shoulders to relieve tension. = 按摩师按摩我的肩膀以缓解紧张。

现在完成时:
I have kneaded this dough for an hour, and it's still sticky. = 我揉这个面团已经一个小时了，它还是粘乎乎的。
She has kneaded the clay into a beautiful vase. = 她已经把粘土揉捏成一个漂亮的花瓶。

过去完成时:
After I had kneaded the dough, I let it rise. = 我揉好面团后，让它发酵。
He had kneaded the clay for hours before he started shaping it. = 在开始塑形之前，他已经揉捏了粘土几个小时。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would knead the dough more thoroughly. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更彻底地揉面团。
I wish I had kneaded the dough longer; it's a little tough. = 我真希望我揉面团揉得久一点；它有点硬。

被动语态:
The dough was kneaded until it was smooth and elastic. = 面团被揉捏直到光滑有弹性。
The clay is being kneaded by the artist. = 粘土正在被艺术家揉捏。

疑问句:
Do you knead the dough by hand or with a mixer? = 你用手揉面团还是用搅拌机？

否定句:
