electrostatic:/ˌɪˌlɛktrəʊˈstætɪk/| adj.|静电的；静电学的|Relating to or produced by static electricity.  The term describes phenomena and processes involving stationary or slowly moving electric charges.|The electrostatic charge on the balloon caused it to stick to the wall.  气球上的静电荷使它粘在墙上。


The electrostatic field around the charged particle is strong. 带电粒子周围的静电场很强。|近义词：electrified, static|反义词：dynamic, moving

WORD ORIGIN->

The word "electrostatic" is a combination of "electro-" (from "electron," relating to electricity) and "-static" (from "static," meaning stationary or unchanging).  Therefore, "electrostatic" literally means relating to stationary electricity.
