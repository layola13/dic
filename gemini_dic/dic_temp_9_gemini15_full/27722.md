canes:/keɪnz/| n.|手杖；甘蔗；藤条|A cane is a stick used for support when walking, or a plant stem, especially of bamboo or sugar cane.  |例句：He leaned heavily on his cane. 他重重地倚靠着他的手杖。
例句：Sugar cane is grown in many tropical countries. 甘蔗在许多热带国家种植。|近义词：stick, staff, walking stick, pole|反义词：N/A
