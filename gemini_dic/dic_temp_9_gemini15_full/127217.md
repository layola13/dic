naughts:/nɔːts/| n. |零，无价值之物，坏蛋|The word "naughts" is the plural of "naught," meaning zero or nothing. It can also refer to worthless things or mischievous people.  |例句：His efforts amounted to naughts.他的努力等于零。  The children were a pack of little naughts.孩子们是一群小坏蛋。|近义词：zeros, nothings, worthless things, rascals |反义词：somethings, valuables, goodies
