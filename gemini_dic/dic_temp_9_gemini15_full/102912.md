jawless:/ˈdʒɔːləs/| adj.|无颌的，无下巴的|Lacking jaws.  This term is primarily used to describe certain types of fish and other aquatic animals that do not possess jaws.  |例句：Hagfish are jawless fish. = 八目鳗是无颌鱼。|例句：The fossil shows a jawless vertebrate. = 这些化石显示了一种无颌脊椎动物。|近义词：agnathous|反义词：jawed


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "to be," "to have," etc.  The usage is straightforward descriptive.
