patriotisms:/ˈpætrieˌɒtɪzəm/| n.|爱国主义(复数)|The feeling of patriotism is a complex emotion, encompassing love for one's country, a sense of loyalty and duty, and a willingness to defend its interests.  It can manifest in various ways, from quiet acts of civic duty to fervent demonstrations of national pride.|例句：The government's policies are designed to foster patriotism among its citizens.  政府的政策旨在培养公民的爱国主义精神。|近义词：nationalism, loyalty, allegiance|反义词：treason, disloyalty
