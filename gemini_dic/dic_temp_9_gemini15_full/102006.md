isodiametric:/ˌaɪsoʊdaɪəˈmɛtrɪk/| adj.|等轴的，等径的|Equally sized in all dimensions.  Isodiametric crystals are those that have approximately equal dimensions along all three axes.  |例句：The minerals in this rock sample exhibit isodiametric crystal habits.  该岩石样品中的矿物呈现等轴晶习性。

The igneous rock shows an isodiametric texture. 这火成岩显示出等轴结构。|近义词：equidimensional, isometric|反义词：anisometric, inequidimensional

词源:
iso- (from Greek ἴσος, "equal") + dia- (from Greek διά, "through, across") + -meter (from Greek μέτρον, "measure")  字面意思是“在所有方向上具有相等的测量值”。
