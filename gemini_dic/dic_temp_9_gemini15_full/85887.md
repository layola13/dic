headdress:/ˈhedˌdrɛs/| n.| 头饰 | A covering for the head, often decorative and worn as part of a costume or uniform.  |Example: The queen wore a magnificent headdress of jewels and feathers. 女王戴着一顶华丽的珠宝羽毛头饰。|近义词：headgear, head covering, hat, crown |反义词：None specifically, but could be considered opposite to bare head.
