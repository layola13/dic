fraternizes:/ˈfrætərnaɪzɪz/| v.|兄弟般相处；友善交往；|to be friendly and associate with, especially with someone of a different group or social standing.  It often implies a degree of familiarity or intimacy that might be considered inappropriate in certain contexts.|例句：The soldiers fraternized with the local people. 士兵们与当地人友好相处。

例句：The opposing teams fraternized after the game, despite the intense competition.  比赛结束后，尽管竞争激烈，两队队员还是友好地交流。


近义词：associate, socialize, mingle, hobnob

反义词：estrange, alienate, isolate, shun
