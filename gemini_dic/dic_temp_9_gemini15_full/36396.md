coinvestor: /ˌkoʊɪnˈvɛstər/ | n. |共同投资者 | A person who invests money in a project or business along with one or more other people.  A coinvestor shares the risks and rewards of the investment. | Example:  The startup secured funding from a group of angel investors and a coinvestor. 这家初创公司从一群天使投资者和一位共同投资者那里获得了资金。

| 近义词: joint investor, co-investor, partner (in investment) | 反义词: sole investor, single investor | 词性: noun
