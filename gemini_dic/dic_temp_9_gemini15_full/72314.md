flourishment:/ˈflʌrɪʃmənt/| n. |繁荣；兴盛；装饰；华丽的辞藻 |The flourishing of the arts during the Renaissance. 文艺复兴时期艺术的繁荣。|近义词：prosperity, thriving, success |反义词：decline, decay, failure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The business is flourishing. = 这家生意兴隆。
Her career is flourishing. = 她的事业蒸蒸日上。

一般过去时:
The city flourished in the 18th century. = 这座城市在18世纪繁荣昌盛。
The arts flourished under his rule. = 在他的统治下，艺术蓬勃发展。

现在完成时:
The company has flourished since its inception. = 自公司成立以来，它一直蓬勃发展。
The economy has flourished in recent years. = 近年来经济蓬勃发展。

过去完成时:
The empire had flourished for centuries before its decline. = 这个帝国在其衰落之前已经繁荣了几个世纪。
The town had flourished until the plague hit. = 直到瘟疫爆发前，这个小镇都很繁荣。

将来时:
The company will flourish with the new technology. = 公司将凭借新技术而蓬勃发展。
With proper care, the plant will flourish. = 只要精心照料，这种植物就会茁壮成长。

虚拟语气:
If we had invested more, the business might have flourished. = 如果我们投资更多，生意可能会兴隆。
If only the company had flourished, we wouldn't be in this situation. = 如果公司能兴旺发达就好了，我们现在就不会陷入这种境地了。

被动语态:
The city was flourished by the discovery of gold. = 这座城市因发现黄金而繁荣起来。(注意：此句略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The economy is being flourished by government policies. = 政府政策正在促进经济繁荣。(注意：此句略显不自然，主动语态更常用)

疑问句:
Is the project flourishing? = 项目进展顺利吗？
Will the new policy flourish the economy? = 新政策会促进经济发展吗？

否定句:
The business is not flourishing. = 生意不景气。
The plants are not flourishing in this dry weather. = 在这种干燥的天气下，植物生长不良。

词源:
Flourishment comes from the Old French *florissement*, ultimately derived from the Latin *florēre*, meaning "to bloom" or "to flourish".  The noun sense developed from the verb's meaning of thriving or prospering.
