palliatory:/ˈpæliətɔːri/| adj. |缓解的；治标不治本的；姑息的| serving to relieve or lessen pain or distress without curing; offering only temporary relief|The doctor prescribed palliative care to ease the patient's suffering.  医生开了姑息性治疗来减轻病人的痛苦。|The palliative measures only provided temporary relief.  这些姑息措施只提供了暂时的缓解。|curative, remedial | curative, radical
