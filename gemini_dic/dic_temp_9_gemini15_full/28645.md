caribou:/ˈkærɪbuː/ | n. |驯鹿|A large deer of northern North America, Europe, and Asia, with large antlers in both sexes.  Caribou are known for their ability to migrate long distances. |例句：The caribou herd migrated across the tundra.  这群驯鹿迁移穿过苔原。

例句：We saw a large herd of caribou grazing near the lake. 我们看到一大群驯鹿在湖边吃草。


近义词：reindeer (in Eurasia)


反义词：无
