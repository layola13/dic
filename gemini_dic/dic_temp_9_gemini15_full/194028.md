self-focussed:/ˌsɛlfˈfoʊkəst/ | adj. |以自我为中心的，专注于自己的| Self-focussed describes someone who is primarily concerned with their own thoughts, feelings, and experiences, often to the exclusion of others.  It implies a level of preoccupation with oneself and one's own needs. | 例句：He was so self-focussed that he didn't even notice his friend's distress. 他太以自我为中心了，甚至没有注意到朋友的痛苦。


例句：Her self-focussed behavior alienated many of her colleagues. 她的以自我为中心的举动疏远了许多同事。


近义词：self-centered, egocentric, narcissistic, self-absorbed | 反义词：other-centered, selfless, altruistic | 词性：形容词

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
He had been self-focussed for years before he sought therapy. = 在寻求治疗之前，他已经以自我为中心多年了。
She had been so self-focussed that she didn't realize the impact of her actions. = 她太以自我为中心了，没有意识到自己行为的影响。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:  (This adjective is rarely used in the passive voice.  The focus is on the subject's behavior, not on the subject being acted upon.)


疑问句:

否定句:
