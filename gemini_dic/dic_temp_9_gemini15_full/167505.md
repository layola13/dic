previewing: /prɪˈvjuːɪŋ/ | v. | 预览；预先查看 |  The act of viewing something beforehand, typically to assess its quality or suitability.  It implies a brief examination, not a thorough analysis. |例句：I'm previewing the movie before taking the kids to see it.  我正在看电影预告片，然后再带孩子们去看。

例句：She is previewing the presentation slides before the meeting. 她正在会议前预览演示文稿。


近义词：viewing, screening, examining, inspecting | 反义词：ignoring, neglecting


词性: 动词

词源:
The word "previewing" is derived from the verb "preview," which is a combination of the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before") and the noun "view."  Therefore, it literally means "to view before."  The "-ing" suffix indicates the present participle or gerund form of the verb.
