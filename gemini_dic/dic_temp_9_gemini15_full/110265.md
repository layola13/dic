lift-a-finger: /lɪft ə ˈfɪŋɡər/ | phrasal verb | 帮忙；伸出援手 |  To lift a finger means to do even the smallest amount of work or make the slightest effort to help. It is often used negatively to emphasize a lack of help or contribution.  | 例句：He didn't lift a finger to help clean up the mess. 他连一根手指头都没动就帮着打扫了烂摊子。  He wouldn't lift a finger to help his brother. 他不愿帮他兄弟一点忙。 | 近义词：do anything, help, assist | 反义词：do everything, work hard, contribute greatly | 词性: phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He doesn't lift a finger to help around the house. = 他在家务方面一点忙也不帮。
They rarely lift a finger to assist others. = 他们很少伸出援手帮助别人。


一般过去时:
She didn't lift a finger to help with the party preparations. = 她对派对的准备工作一点忙也没帮。
He never lifted a finger to support his family. = 他从来没有尽力支持过他的家庭。


现在完成时:
He hasn't lifted a finger all day. = 他今天一天都没动过手。
They haven't lifted a finger to improve the situation. = 他们没有采取任何措施来改善这种情况。


过去完成时:
They hadn't lifted a finger to stop the argument before it escalated. = 在争吵升级之前，他们没有采取任何措施阻止。
She hadn't lifted a finger to help him even though he needed assistance badly. = 即使他非常需要帮助，她也没有帮他一把。


将来时:
He won't lift a finger to help you. = 他不会帮你任何忙。
I doubt they will lift a finger to resolve the conflict peacefully. = 我怀疑他们会采取和平的方式来解决冲突。


虚拟语气:
If he had lifted a finger, the outcome would have been different. = 如果他肯帮忙，结果就会不一样。
I wish he would lift a finger to help me. = 我希望他会帮我一把。


被动语态:
Nothing was done; no one lifted a finger. = 什么也没做；没有人伸出援手。
A finger wasn't lifted to prevent the accident. = 没有人采取任何措施来防止事故发生。


疑问句:
Did anyone lift a finger to help the victim? = 有人帮助受害者吗？
Will anyone lift a finger to address this problem? = 会有人来解决这个问题吗？


否定句:
They didn't lift a finger to assist the refugees. = 他们没有伸出援手帮助难民。
I refuse to lift a finger until I'm properly compensated. = 在我得到适当的补偿之前，我拒绝帮忙。
