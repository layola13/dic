They rhumba'd across the dance floor with effortless grace. 他们优雅地横跨舞池跳着伦巴舞。


近义词：danced (the rhumba), swayed, moved rhythmically |反义词：None directly applicable.  The opposite would be to remain still or move in a non-rhythmic way.
