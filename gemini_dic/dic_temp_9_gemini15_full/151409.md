parnellism:/ˈpɑːrnəlɪzəm/| n. |帕内尔主义；爱尔兰民族主义的一种形式|Parnellism refers to the political movement and ideology associated with Charles Stewart Parnell, a 19th-century Irish nationalist leader.  It emphasized the pursuit of Irish Home Rule through constitutional means, primarily focusing on practical political action rather than revolutionary violence.  The movement was characterized by its pragmatic approach and its emphasis on unity within the Irish nationalist movement. |The rise of Parnellism marked a significant shift in Irish politics. |近义词：Irish nationalism, Home Rule movement |反义词：Unionism, British imperialism
