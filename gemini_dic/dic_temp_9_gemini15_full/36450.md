cold-shoulder:/ˈkoʊld ˈʃoʊldər/| v. n.|冷落，不理睬；冷肩 | To ignore or treat someone coldly and unsympathetically.  |例句：She cold-shouldered him after their argument. 他们吵架后，她对他冷若冰霜。|例句：He felt the cold shoulder from his colleagues after the incident.  事件发生后，他感到同事们对他冷淡。|近义词：ignore, snub, slight, ostracize |反义词：welcome, embrace, cherish |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cold-shoulder their neighbors. = 他们对邻居冷淡。
He cold-shoulders anyone who disagrees with him. = 他对任何与他意见相左的人冷眼相待。


一般过去时:
She cold-shouldered him at the party. = 她在派对上冷落了他。
They cold-shouldered her application. = 他们对她申请置之不理。


现在完成时:
They have cold-shouldered me since the incident. = 自从那件事发生后，他们就一直冷落我。
She has cold-shouldered her family for years. = 她多年来一直冷落她的家人。


过去完成时:
He had cold-shouldered her for weeks before she finally left. = 他冷落了她几周，她才最终离开。
They had cold-shouldered the proposal before even considering it. = 他们甚至没有考虑就否决了这个提议。


将来时:
I think she will cold-shoulder me if I tell her the truth. = 我想如果我告诉她真相，她会冷落我。
They will cold-shoulder the project if they don't get enough funding. = 如果他们得不到足够的资金，他们会放弃这个项目。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't cold-shoulder him. = 如果我是你，我不会冷落他。
I wish they hadn't cold-shouldered my suggestion. = 我真希望他们没有冷落我的建议。


被动语态:
He felt cold-shouldered by his friends. = 他感到朋友们冷落了他。
The proposal was cold-shouldered by the committee. = 委员会对该提案置之不理。


疑问句:
Were you cold-shouldered by your boss? = 你被老板冷落了吗？
Why are they cold-shouldering us? = 他们为什么冷落我们？


否定句:
They didn't cold-shoulder her request. = 他们没有冷落她的请求。
He wouldn't cold-shoulder his own family. = 他不会冷落自己的家人。


PHRASE->
give someone the cold shoulder = 冷落某人
get the cold shoulder = 受到冷落
a cold shoulder = 冷淡的待遇
turn a cold shoulder to someone = 对某人冷淡
shoulder someone = 甩掉某人(非冷落含义，但与shoulder有关)
