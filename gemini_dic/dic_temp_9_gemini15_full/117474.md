medivacking:/ˈmɛdɪvækɪŋ/| n. |医疗后送；医疗撤离|The act of evacuating someone, typically a wounded soldier or civilian, by medical means for treatment.|例句：The injured soldiers were medivacked to a field hospital. 受伤的士兵被医疗后送至野战医院。|例句：The civilian population needed medivacking after the earthquake. 地震后，平民需要医疗撤离。|近义词：evacuation, airlift, rescue|反义词：abandonment
