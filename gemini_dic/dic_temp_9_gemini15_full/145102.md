overappreciative: /ˌoʊvərəˈpriːʃiətɪv/ | adj. | 过分感激的；过分欣赏的 | Excessively appreciative; showing excessive gratitude or admiration.  |  He was overappreciative of the small gift, making her feel uncomfortable. 他对那件小礼物过于感激，让她感到不舒服。 |近义词：excessively grateful, overly thankful, effusive | 反义词：underappreciative, ungrateful
