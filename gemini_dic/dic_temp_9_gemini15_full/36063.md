coedited:/koʊˈɛdɪtɪd/| v. adj.| 合编的；共同编辑的 | Past participle of coedit.  It describes something that has been edited by two or more people jointly. | 例句：The book was coedited by two leading scholars in the field. 这本书是由该领域两位领先的学者合编的。  The coedited anthology contains essays from diverse perspectives. 这部合编的选集包含来自不同视角的文章。| 近义词： jointly edited, collaboratively edited | 反义词：solely edited, individually edited | 动词的过去分词
