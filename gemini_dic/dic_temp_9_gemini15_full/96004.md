incurs:/ɪnˈkɜːrz/ | vt. |招致；承担；发生 | To bring upon oneself (something undesirable, such as a debt, penalty, or injury).  |例句：His reckless driving incurred a heavy fine.  他鲁莽驾驶招致了巨额罚款。|例句：The company incurred significant losses last year. 公司去年遭受了重大损失。|近义词：suffers, sustains, attracts |反义词：avoids, escapes, prevents


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He incurs debt every month. = 他每个月都欠债。
The company incurs high operating costs. = 公司承担着高昂的运营成本。

一般过去时:
She incurred a serious injury in the accident. = 她在那次事故中受了重伤。
The project incurred unexpected delays. = 项目发生了意料之外的延误。

现在完成时:
We have incurred significant expenses on this project. = 我们在这个项目上已经花费了巨额资金。
They have incurred the wrath of the community. = 他们招致了社区的愤怒。

过去完成时:
He had incurred many debts before he declared bankruptcy. = 他在宣布破产前已经欠了很多债。
The company had incurred substantial losses before the merger. = 在合并之前，公司已经遭受了巨大的损失。

将来时:
The new policy will incur the opposition of many voters. = 新政策将会招致许多选民的反对。
This decision will likely incur additional costs. = 这个决定可能会导致额外的成本。

虚拟语气:
If he continued this behavior, he would incur the consequences. = 如果他继续这种行为，他将会承担后果。
I wish I hadn't incurred that debt. = 我真希望我没有欠下那笔债。

被动语态:
Heavy fines are incurred by those who violate the law. = 违反法律的人将被处以巨额罚款。(被动语态较少使用)
Significant losses were incurred during the economic downturn. = 在经济衰退期间遭受了重大损失。(被动语态较少使用)

疑问句:
What penalties will we incur if we miss the deadline? = 如果我们错过截止日期，我们将面临什么处罚？
What additional costs will this project incur? = 这个项目还会产生哪些额外成本？

否定句:
The company did not incur any losses this quarter. = 本季度公司没有遭受任何损失。
He tried to avoid incurring any further debt. = 他试图避免进一步负债。


PHRASE->
incur losses: 遭受损失
incur debt: 负债
incur expenses: 产生费用
incur penalties: 遭受惩罚
incur criticism: 招致批评
