overadvance:/ˌoʊvərədˈvæns/| v.|预支；提前支付|To pay or receive money before it is due.  It implies paying more than is necessary or appropriate in advance.|例句：He overadvanced his salary by borrowing from a payday lender. 他通过向发薪日贷款机构借款预支了他的薪水。例句：The company overadvanced funds for the project, leading to a cash flow problem later. 公司为该项目预支了资金，后来导致现金流问题。|近义词：prepay, advance, pay in advance|反义词：delay payment, defer payment
