fermes: /fɛʁm/ | n. | 农场；农庄 | A farm is an area of land used for growing crops or raising livestock.  | 例句：He works on a large farm growing wheat. 他在一个种植小麦的大农场工作。 | 例句：The farm is home to many different animals. 农场是许多不同动物的家园。 | 近义词：ranch, plantation, homestead | 反义词：city, metropolis


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer farms the land diligently. = 农民勤奋地耕种土地。
This farm produces a lot of milk. = 这个农场产很多牛奶。

一般过去时:
They farmed this land for generations. = 他们世世代代耕种这片土地。
The old farm was abandoned years ago. = 那座旧农场几年前就被废弃了。


现在完成时:
We have farmed this land since 1980. = 自1980年以来，我们一直在耕种这片土地。
They have successfully farmed organic vegetables for five years. = 他们成功地种植有机蔬菜五年了。


过去完成时:
They had farmed the land before the drought hit. = 干旱来临之前，他们就已经耕种这片土地了。
The family had owned the farm for over a century. = 这个家族拥有这个农场超过一个世纪了。


将来时:
We will farm more sustainably in the future. = 我们将来会更可持续地耕种。
They will expand their farm next year. = 他们明年将扩大他们的农场。


虚拟语气:
If I had a farm, I would grow sunflowers. = 如果我有一个农场，我会种植向日葵。
I wish I could farm my own organic produce. = 我希望我能自己种植有机农产品。


被动语态:
The land was farmed for many years. = 这块土地被耕种了很多年。
The farm is managed by a local family. = 这个农场由当地一个家庭管理。


疑问句:
Do you farm this land? = 你耕种这片土地吗？
Does this farm raise cattle? = 这个农场养牛吗？


否定句:
They don't farm that area anymore. = 他们不再耕种那个地区了。
He didn't farm organically last year. = 他去年没有进行有机耕作。
