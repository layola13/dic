entrances:/ˈɛntrənsɪz/| n.| 入口，进入，开端|The plural form of "entrance," referring to multiple points of entry or the act of entering multiple times.|例句：The building has several entrances on different sides. 这座建筑物在不同的侧面有几个入口。|例句：The entrances to the park were crowded with people. 公园的入口挤满了人。|近义词：accesses, entries, inlets|反义词：exits, departures

一般现在时:
The main entrance is always busy. = 正门总是很忙。
Multiple entrances lead to the museum. = 多个入口通向博物馆。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:

We have entered the building through various entrances at different times. = 我们在不同时间通过不同的入口进入过这座建筑物。


过去完成时:

将来时:
