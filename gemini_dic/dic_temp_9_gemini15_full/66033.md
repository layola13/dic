exanthema:/ˌɛɡzænˈθiːmə/| n. |皮疹；疹子|A widespread eruption of the skin, typically accompanied by fever and inflammation.  It's a general term referring to various skin rashes caused by different conditions. |例句：The child developed an exanthema after contracting a viral infection. 孩子感染病毒后起了皮疹。|例句：The doctor examined the patient's exanthema to determine the cause. 医生检查了病人的皮疹以确定病因。|近义词：rash, eruption, efflorescence|反义词：clear skin
