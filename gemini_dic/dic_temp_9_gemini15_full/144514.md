outtravels:/ˌaʊtˈtrævəlz/| v.|超过…的行程；旅行范围超过|To travel farther or more extensively than.  It implies exceeding someone or something else in the distance or scope of travel.|例句：His adventurous spirit outtravels his physical capabilities.  他的冒险精神超过了他的体力。
例句：The seasoned explorer outtravels any newcomer to the region.  这位经验丰富的探险家旅行的范围超过该地区任何新手。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip|反义词：undertravel


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outtravels his friends every summer. = 他每年夏天旅行的范围都超过他的朋友们。
This car outtravels that one on rough terrain. = 这辆车在崎岖的地形上行驶的距离比那辆车远。


一般过去时:
She outtravelled her colleagues during her sabbatical. = 她休假期间旅行的范围超过了她的同事们。
The expedition outtravelled all previous attempts to reach the summit. =  这次探险的行程超过了以前所有到达山顶的尝试。


现在完成时:
He has outtravelled most people his age. = 他旅行的范围超过了他大多数同龄人。
She has outtravelled her expectations for this trip. = 她这次旅行的行程超过了她的预期。


过去完成时:
They had outtravelled their supplies by the time they reached the oasis. = 当他们到达绿洲时，他们的补给已经不足了（他们的行程超过了补给所能支持的范围）。
The explorers had outtravelled any previous expeditions to that remote island. = 探险家们到达那个偏远岛屿的行程超过了以往任何探险队。


将来时:
We will outtravel our planned itinerary if we have good weather. = 如果天气好，我们将超过计划中的行程。
She will outtravel her family on this year's vacation. = 她今年度假的行程将超过她的家人。


虚拟语气:
If he had a better car, he would outtravel his rivals. = 如果他有一辆更好的车，他就能超过他的竞争对手的行程。
I wish I could outtravel the limitations of my budget. = 我希望我能不受预算限制地旅行。


被动语态:
His journeys were outtravelled by few in the world. = 他旅行的范围在世界上很少有人能超过。
The record for the longest journey was outtravelled last year. = 最长旅程的记录去年被打破了(行程被超过)。


疑问句:
Does his experience outtravel his youth? = 他的经验是否超过了他的年轻？
Will this new model outtravel the old one? = 这种新型号的旅行距离会超过旧型号吗？


否定句:
His ambition did not outtravel his resources. = 他的野心没有超过他的资源。
The team didn't outtravel their planned route. =  团队没有超过他们计划的路线。
