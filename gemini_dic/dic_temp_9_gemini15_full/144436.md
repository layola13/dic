outswear:/ˌaʊtˈswer/| vt.|超过…的穿着；比…穿得更华丽|To surpass in wearing apparel; to dress more richly or finely than.  The verb implies a competition or a display of wealth or status through clothing.|例句：She outswore all the other guests at the ball. =她在舞会上比其他所有客人都穿得华丽。

例句：He tried to outswear his opponent in the debate, but it backfired. = 他试图在辩论中比对手穿得更华丽，但这适得其反。


近义词：outdress, outshine (in clothing)

反义词：underdress


词性:动词
