favicon:/ˈfeɪvˌaɪkɒn/|/ˈfeɪvˌaɪkɑːn/| n. |网站图标|A small icon, usually 16x16 or 32x32 pixels, that represents a website and is displayed in the browser's address bar or tab.  It helps users quickly identify the website.|Example:  The website's favicon was a small green leaf.|近义词：website icon, browser icon|反义词：无
