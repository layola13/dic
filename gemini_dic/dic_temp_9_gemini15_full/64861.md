espouse:/ɪˈspaʊz/ | vt. | 支持，拥护；嫁娶 | To support, advocate, or embrace (a cause, belief, etc.); to marry. |例句：He espoused the cause of human rights. 他拥护人权事业。  |例句：She espoused a new religion. 她皈依了一种新的宗教。|近义词：advocate, champion, support, marry |反义词：oppose, reject, repudiate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He espouses conservative values. = 他支持保守的价值观。
She espouses a vegetarian lifestyle. = 她提倡素食生活方式。

一般过去时:
The politician espoused the new policy. = 这位政治家支持这项新政策。
They espoused each other in a small ceremony. = 他们在一个小仪式上结婚了。

现在完成时:
She has espoused the principles of non-violence. = 她一直拥护非暴力的原则。
They have espoused a new faith. = 他们皈依了一种新的信仰。

过去完成时:
He had espoused that belief for many years. = 他多年来一直信奉那种信念。
They had espoused a simpler way of living before moving to the city. = 在搬到城市之前，他们过着更简朴的生活。

将来时:
She will espouse the cause of environmental protection. = 她将拥护环保事业。
He will espouse her in the spring. = 他将在春天迎娶她。

虚拟语气:
If he espoused a different political party, he might have a better chance of winning. = 如果他支持不同的政党，他可能会有更好的获胜机会。
I wish he would espouse a more moderate stance on this issue. = 我希望他能对这个问题采取更温和的立场。

被动语态:
The policy was espoused by the majority of the voters. = 这项政策得到了大多数选民的支持。
The new technology was quickly espoused by businesses worldwide. = 这项新技术很快就被全世界的企业所采用。

疑问句:
Does he espouse any particular political ideology? = 他是否支持任何特定的政治意识形态？
Will they espouse the same values as their parents? = 他们会像父母一样秉持相同的价值观吗？

否定句:
He does not espouse violence as a means to achieve his goals. = 他不支持暴力作为实现目标的手段。
She did not espouse his views on religion. = 她不同意他对宗教的看法。


PHRASE->
espouse a cause = 支持一项事业
espouse a belief = 信奉一种信仰
espouse a philosophy = 拥护一种哲学
espouse a lifestyle = 采用一种生活方式
espouse a theory = 支持一种理论
