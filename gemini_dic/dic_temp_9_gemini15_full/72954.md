what:/wɒt/ /wʌt/| pron.|什么，什么事情|The word "what" is an interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something. It can refer to things, actions, qualities, or situations.  It can also be used as an exclamation.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？


近义词：which,  (in some contexts) which thing, how


反义词: none (Not applicable in all contexts, as "what" doesn't inherently have an opposite.)


词性:pronoun

USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？

一般过去时:
What did you see? = 你看到了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

现在完成时:
What have you done today? = 你今天做了什么？
What has she achieved recently? = 她最近取得了什么成就？

过去完成时:
What had you eaten before the party? = 聚会前你吃了什么？
What had they decided before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们决定了什么？

将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like? = 天气会怎么样？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to expect. = 我希望我知道会发生什么。

被动语态:
What is being discussed? = 正在讨论什么？
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？

疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
He didn't say what he wanted. = 他没说他想要什么。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What about...? = ……怎么样？
What if...? = 如果……将会怎样？
What for? = 为什么？为了什么？
No matter what = 无论如何
