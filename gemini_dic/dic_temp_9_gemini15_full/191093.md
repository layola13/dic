scantiness:/ˈskæntɪnəs/| n. |稀少；不足；贫乏 |The scantiness of resources hindered the project's progress. 资源的匮乏阻碍了项目的进展。|  近义词：scarcity, dearth, paucity, meagerness | 反义词：abundance, plentifulness, profusion |  名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scantiness of the evidence makes it difficult to reach a conclusion.  证据的稀少使得难以得出结论。
His scantiness of words betrayed his nervousness. 他言语的简略暴露了他的紧张。


一般过去时:
The scantiness of the harvest led to food shortages. 收成稀少导致了粮食短缺。
The scantiness of his explanation left many questions unanswered. 他解释的简略使得许多问题悬而未决。


现在完成时:
The scantiness of rainfall has severely impacted the crops. 降雨稀少严重影响了农作物。
The scantiness of information has hampered our investigation. 信息的匮乏阻碍了我们的调查。


过去完成时:
The scantiness of funding had already caused delays in the project. 资金的匮乏已经导致了项目的延误。
The scantiness of support had left them feeling isolated. 支持的匮乏使他们感到孤立无援。


将来时:
The scantiness of resources will likely lead to budget cuts. 资源的匮乏很可能会导致预算削减。
The scantiness of the evidence will probably result in a mistrial. 证据的稀少可能会导致审判无效。


虚拟语气:
If there were no scantiness of resources, the project would be completed on time. 如果没有资源匮乏，项目就能按时完成。
If there had been no scantiness of information, the decision would have been different. 如果信息不匮乏，决定就会不一样。


被动语态:
The project was delayed due to the scantiness of the budget. 项目由于预算不足而被延误。
The investigation was hampered by the scantiness of evidence. 调查由于证据不足而受阻。


疑问句:
Does the scantiness of the data affect the accuracy of the results? 数据的稀少是否会影响结果的准确性？
Is the scantiness of detail intentional or accidental? 细节的匮乏是故意的还是偶然的？


否定句:
The abundance of resources negated the effects of previous scantiness. 资源的丰富抵消了先前匮乏的影响。
The plentiful harvest made up for the scantiness of the previous year. 丰收弥补了前一年的歉收。

词源:
From Middle English *scantnesse, from scant ("scarce, meager").  Ultimately from Old Norse skamt ("short, meager").
