on:/ɒn/ /ɑːn/| preposition, adverb|在…上，关于，进行中|Indicates position, time, or a state of being; also used to show a continuous action or a topic of discussion. |例句：The book is on the table. 书在桌子上。The meeting is on. 会议正在进行。|近义词：upon, about, concerning|反义词：off, below, under


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat is on the roof. = 猫在屋顶上。
He is on the phone. = 他正在打电话。


一般过去时:
The picture was on the wall. = 图片在墙上。
She was on holiday last week. = 她上周在度假。


现在完成时:
The project has been on for months. = 这个项目已经进行了几个月了。
I have been on a diet since January. = 我从一月份就开始节食了。


过去完成时:
The show had already been on when we arrived. = 我们到达时演出已经开始了。
He had been on the team for five years before he retired. = 他退役前已经在队里待了五年了。


将来时:
The game will be on tomorrow. = 游戏将在明天进行。
We will be on the move soon. = 我们很快就要出发了。


虚拟语气:
If I were on the committee, I would vote against it. = 如果我在委员会上，我会投票反对。
I wish I were on holiday. = 我真希望我在度假。


被动语态:
The lights are on. = 灯是亮着的。（被动语态也可理解为系表结构）
The meeting was on for two hours. = 会议进行了两个小时。（被动语态也可理解为系表结构）


疑问句:
Is the TV on? = 电视开着吗？
Is the meeting still on? = 会议还在进行吗？


否定句:
The light is not on. = 灯没有亮。
The party is not on. = 聚会取消了。


PHRASE->
on time = 准时
go on = 继续
on the other hand = 另一方面
on average = 平均
on sale = 减价销售
