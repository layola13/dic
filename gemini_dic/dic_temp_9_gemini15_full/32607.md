chirped:/tʃɜːrp/|v.|吱吱叫，啁啾；轻声说|To make a short, high-pitched sound, like that of a bird or insect; to speak in a cheerful, high-pitched voice.|例句：The crickets chirped all night. 蟋蟀整夜都在吱吱叫。The girl chirped happily. 女孩高兴地轻声说道。|近义词：tweet, twitter, peep, whisper|反义词：roar, bellow, shout


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds chirp merrily in the morning. = 鸟儿在早晨快乐地啁啾。
The children chirp excitedly about their trip. = 孩子们兴奋地叽叽喳喳地谈论他们的旅行。

一般过去时:
The robin chirped a cheerful song. = 知更鸟唱了一首快乐的歌。
She chirped a greeting as she passed. = 她走过时高兴地打了个招呼。


现在完成时:
The birds have chirped all day. = 鸟儿整天都在鸣叫。
The baby has chirped incessantly since morning. = 宝宝从早上开始就一直在不停地咿呀学语。


过去完成时:
The birds had chirped sweetly before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，鸟儿们婉转地鸣叫过。
She had chirped a warning before the accident. = 事故发生前她曾发出过警告。


将来时:
The birds will chirp at dawn. = 鸟儿将在黎明时分鸣叫。
I expect the baby will chirp a lot today. = 我预计宝宝今天会咿呀学语很多。


虚拟语气:
If the birds chirped louder, we would have heard them. = 如果鸟叫得更响亮，我们就会听到它们。
I wish the birds would chirp more often. = 我希望鸟儿能更频繁地鸣叫。


被动语态:
(Not typically used in passive voice, as it's an intransitive verb.  A forced example would be awkward.)  A cheerful greeting was chirped by the girl.  = 女孩高兴地打了个招呼。(This is unnatural.)


疑问句:
Did the birds chirp this morning? = 今早鸟儿叫了吗？
Does the baby chirp much? = 宝宝经常咿呀学语吗？


否定句:
The birds didn't chirp last night. = 昨晚鸟儿没有叫。
She didn't chirp a word. = 她一句话也没说。


PHRASE->

chirp a greeting: 打招呼
