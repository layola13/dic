dime-a-dozen: /daɪm ə ˈdʌzən/ | adj. | 一分钱一打；非常普通；廉价且常见 | Extremely common and of little value.  |例句：These kinds of jobs are a dime a dozen.  这类工作多如牛毛。


例句：
These mass-produced toys are a dime a dozen; you can find them anywhere. 这些批量生产的玩具多如牛毛；你随处可见。

These ideas are a dime a dozen; nothing original. 这些想法很常见；毫无新意。


近义词：common, plentiful, cheap, inexpensive, ordinary, commonplace

反义词：rare, scarce, valuable, precious, unique

词性:形容词
