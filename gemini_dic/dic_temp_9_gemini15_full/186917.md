ripcord:/ˈrɪpkɔːrd/| n. |拉绳；紧急拉索|A cord or rope designed to be pulled to release a mechanism, especially in a parachute or other safety device.|例句：He pulled the ripcord and the parachute opened. 他拉动拉绳，降落伞打开了。|例句：The pilot pulled the ripcord to eject from the malfunctioning aircraft.  飞行员拉动拉绳弹射出了故障飞机。|近义词：release cord, emergency cord|反义词：None
