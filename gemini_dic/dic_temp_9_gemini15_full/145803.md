overdecorative:/ˌoʊvərˈdɛkərətɪv/| adj. |过度装饰的，装饰过多的 | Excessively decorated; ornamented to an excessive degree.  The room was overdecorative, with too many knick-knacks and fussy details. |近义词：ornate, embellished, flamboyant, gaudy |反义词：plain, simple, understated


例句：

The overdecorative cake was almost too beautiful to eat.  过度装饰的蛋糕几乎美得让人不忍心吃。

Her overdecorative style clashed with the minimalist architecture of the house.  她过度装饰的风格与房子的极简主义建筑格格不入。


词源:

* **over-**:  prefix indicating excess or to a greater degree than is necessary. (表示超过、过度)
* **decorative**: adjective meaning relating to or intended for decoration. (与装饰有关的)


用法说明:

overdecorative是一个形容词，用来描述被过度装饰的事物。它通常带有负面含义，暗示装饰过多，以至于显得俗气或累赘。
