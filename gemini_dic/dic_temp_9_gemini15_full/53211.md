direct-mail: /ˌdaɪˈrɛkt meɪl/ | n. | 直邮广告；直接邮寄 | Direct mail marketing involves sending promotional materials directly to potential customers through the postal service.  |  近义词：junk mail, postal marketing | 反义词：online marketing, digital marketing

The company sends direct mail to its customers regularly. = 公司定期向客户发送直邮广告。
We receive a lot of direct mail every week. = 我们每周收到很多直邮广告。

They sent a direct mail campaign last month. = 他们上个月进行了一次直邮广告活动。
