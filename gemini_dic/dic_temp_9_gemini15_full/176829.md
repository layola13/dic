readvertises:/ˌriːˈædvərtaɪz/| vt.|再次刊登广告；重新做广告|To advertise something again.|The company readvertised the job opening after failing to find a suitable candidate. 公司在未能找到合适的候选人后再次刊登了招聘广告。|re-advertise, advertise again|stop advertising


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They readvertise the product every month. = 他们每个月都重新为产品做广告。
The company readvertises its services on television. = 这家公司在电视上再次宣传它的服务。


一般过去时:
They readvertised the position after receiving few applications. = 他们收到很少的申请后重新发布了职位信息。
The store readvertised the sale after the initial announcement was unsuccessful. = 商店在第一次广告宣传失败后重新宣传了促销活动。


现在完成时:
They have readvertised the job several times this year. = 他们今年已经几次重新发布了这个职位信息。
The company has readvertised its product in different magazines. = 这家公司已经在不同的杂志上重新为其产品做广告。


过去完成时:
They had readvertised the concert before the final tickets sold out. = 在最后门票售罄之前，他们已经为音乐会重新做了广告。
The restaurant had readvertised its special offer before the rush hour. = 餐厅在高峰时段之前重新宣传了它的特价优惠。


将来时:
They will readvertise the property if it doesn't sell quickly. = 如果房产卖不掉，他们会重新发布广告。
The school will readvertise the scholarship opportunity next semester. = 学校将在下学期重新发布奖学金机会的广告。


虚拟语气:
If they readvertised the product, they might get more customers. = 如果他们重新为产品做广告，他们可能会获得更多客户。
