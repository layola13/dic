self-rules:/ˈsɛlf ruːlz/| n.| 自我约束；自我规章；自律规则 |Rules or principles that one sets for oneself. These rules govern one's behavior, work habits, or other aspects of life.  They are often used to improve oneself or achieve specific goals.| 例句：She established strict self-rules regarding her diet and exercise. 她制定了严格的饮食和锻炼自我约束规则。

She lives by a set of self-rules that guide her daily actions. 她遵循一套指导她日常行为的自我规章。|近义词：self-discipline, self-regulation, personal rules|反义词：self-indulgence, lack of discipline, recklessness| noun
