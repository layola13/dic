skittled:/ˈskɪtld/| v.|击倒；使震惊；(保龄球)全中|To knock down (something, especially bowling pins) completely; to overwhelm or shock someone;  to bowl a perfect game (in ten-pin bowling).|例句：The bowler skittled all ten pins.  保龄球运动员击倒了所有十个瓶子。

例句：The news skittled him. 这个消息让他震惊了。

近义词：knocked down, bowled over, stunned, amazed|反义词： erected, built up,  calm, composed

词源:
The word "skittle" ultimately derives from the Middle Dutch word *skuttelen*, meaning "to shuffle" or "to move quickly". This relates to the rapid movement of the bowling balls and pins.  The sense of "to knock down" developed over time.
