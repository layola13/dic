enhanced:/ɪnˈhɑːnst/| adj.|增强的，改进的|Improved; made better or more effective.|The enhanced security measures made the building safer.  增强的安全措施使建筑物更安全。|近义词：improved, bettered, amplified, strengthened|反义词：weakened, diminished, deteriorated|形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The filter enhances the image quality. = 滤镜增强了图像质量。
This software enhances productivity. = 这款软件提高了生产力。


一般过去时:
The new training program enhanced employee skills. = 新的培训项目提高了员工的技能。
He enhanced his presentation with visuals. = 他用视觉效果增强了他的演示文稿。


现在完成时:
We have enhanced our customer service system. = 我们已经改进了我们的客户服务系统。
They have enhanced the security of their website. = 他们已经增强了他们网站的安全性。


过去完成时:
They had enhanced the product before launching it. = 他们在发布产品之前已经对其进行了改进。
She had enhanced her resume before applying for the job. = 她在申请工作之前已经润色了她的简历。


将来时:
We will enhance the functionality of the app next month. = 我们下个月将增强应用程序的功能。
The company will enhance its marketing strategy. = 公司将改进其营销策略。


虚拟语气:
If we enhanced the design, it would be more appealing. = 如果我们改进设计，它会更吸引人。
I wish they had enhanced the sound quality. = 我希望他们能提高音质。


被动语态:
The image was enhanced using special software. = 图像使用特殊的软件进行了增强。
The security system is being enhanced to prevent future breaches. = 安全系统正在改进以防止未来的漏洞。


疑问句:
How can we enhance the user experience? = 我们如何才能增强用户体验？
Does this feature enhance the product's value? = 此功能是否提高了产品的价值？


否定句:
The changes did not enhance the overall effect. = 这些变化并没有增强整体效果。
The update didn't enhance the game's performance. = 更新并没有增强游戏的性能。
