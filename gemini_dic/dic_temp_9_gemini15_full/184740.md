resulted:/rɪˈzʌltɪd/| v.|导致，结果是|To be the consequence or effect of something.  It is the past tense of "result."|例句：His hard work resulted in success. 他的努力导致了成功。
例句：The accident resulted in two deaths. 这场事故导致两人死亡。|近义词：produced, caused, led to, brought about|反义词：prevented, avoided


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His actions result in positive changes. = 他的行为带来积极的改变。
Consistent effort results in improvement. = 持续的努力带来进步。

一般过去时:
The experiment resulted in a surprising discovery. = 实验结果出乎意料。
The rain resulted in the cancellation of the game. = 雨导致比赛取消。

现在完成时:
The changes have resulted in increased efficiency. = 这些改变导致效率提高。
Our efforts have resulted in a successful project. = 我们的努力导致项目成功。

过去完成时:
The storm had resulted in widespread power outages. = 暴风雨导致了大范围的停电。
Her illness had resulted in her missing several weeks of school. = 她的病导致她缺课几周。

将来时:
This decision will result in many benefits. = 这个决定将带来许多好处。
The new policy will result in improved safety. = 新政策将提高安全性。

虚拟语气:
If he had studied harder, he would have resulted in better grades. = 如果他学习更努力，他的成绩就会更好。
If we had planned better, the project would have resulted in a different outcome. = 如果我们计划得更好，项目的结果就会不同。

被动语态:
The damage was resulted from the earthquake. = 损害是由地震造成的。(注意：此用法较少见，更常用的表达是"The damage was caused by the earthquake.")
Success is resulted from hard work and dedication. = 成功源于努力和奉献。(注意：此用法较少见，更常用的表达是"Success is caused by hard work and dedication.")


疑问句:
What resulted from the meeting? = 会议的结果是什么？
What will result from this policy change? = 这一政策变化将导致什么结果？

否定句:
The negotiations did not result in an agreement. = 谈判没有达成协议。
His efforts did not result in any significant progress. = 他的努力没有取得任何重大进展。


PHRASE->
result in = 导致
result from = 源于
as a result = 结果是
result of = …的结果
end result = 最终结果
