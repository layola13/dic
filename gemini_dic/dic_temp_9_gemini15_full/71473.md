flaming:/ˈfleɪmɪŋ/| adj.|燃烧的；激烈的；鲜艳的|burning brightly; characterized by intense emotion; brightly colored|例句：The fireplace was flaming brightly.壁炉里火焰熊熊燃烧。The speech was a flaming attack on the government. 这篇演讲是对政府的猛烈抨击。She wore a flaming red dress. 她穿着一件鲜红色的裙子。|近义词：burning, fiery, passionate, ardent, vivid|反义词：dull, pale, subdued, calm, mild


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The fire is flaming brightly. = 火焰熊熊燃烧。
The debate is flaming with passion. = 辩论充满了激情。


一般过去时:
The wood flamed brightly in the fire. = 木柴在火中熊熊燃烧。
His anger flamed at the injustice. = 他因不公正而怒火中烧。


现在完成时:
The sun has flamed all day. = 太阳一整天都炙热地照耀着。
The controversy has flamed across the nation. = 争议席卷全国。


过去完成时:

The argument had flamed out of control before anyone intervened. = 在任何人介入之前，争论已经失控。
