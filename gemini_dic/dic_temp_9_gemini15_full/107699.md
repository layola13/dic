lanolins: /ˈlænəlɪnz/ | n. |羊毛脂；羊毛蜡| A waxy substance secreted by the sebaceous glands of sheep, used in cosmetics and other products.  It's a complex mixture of esters of sterols and fatty acids. |例句：The cream contains lanolins to moisturize the skin. 这款面霜含有羊毛脂，可以滋润皮肤。  The lotion is enriched with natural lanolins. 这款乳液富含天然羊毛脂。|近义词：wool fat, adeps lanae |反义词：无
