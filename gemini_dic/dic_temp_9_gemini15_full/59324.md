earl:/ɜːl/| n. |伯爵 |A British nobleman ranking below a marquess and above a viscount.  Earls hold a hereditary title and historically held significant power and land.  The title is often associated with specific counties or regions. |Example: He inherited the title of Earl from his father. 他从父亲那里继承了伯爵的头衔。|近义词：nobleman, peer |反义词：commoner
