serotine:/ˈsɛrətiːn/| n. |晚间蝙蝠；鼠耳蝠|A serotine is a bat of the genus Eptesicus, characterized by its relatively large ears and often reddish-brown fur.  These bats are widely distributed across various continents.|The brown long-eared bat (Plecotus auritus) is often confused with the serotine bat.|近义词：brown bat, evening bat|反义词：无
