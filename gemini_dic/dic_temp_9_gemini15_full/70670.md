finagle:/fɪˈneɪɡəl/ | vt. | 巧妙地获得；耍小聪明取得；欺骗；歪曲 | To obtain something by trickery or manipulation; to cleverly or deviously achieve something. | 例句：He finagled his way into a free ticket. 他耍小聪明弄到了一张免费票。

He finagled an invitation to the exclusive party. 他巧妙地获得了参加那个专属派对的邀请。


近义词：manipulate, contrive, scheme, wangle, wheedle | 反义词：earn honestly, obtain legitimately


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He finagles his way out of trouble. = 他总是巧妙地摆脱困境。
She finagles extra vacation days. = 她耍小聪明多请几天假。

一般过去时:
They finagled a discount on the purchase. = 他们通过耍小聪明在购买时获得折扣。
He finagled the information from a reluctant source. = 他从一个不愿透露消息的人那里巧妙地套取了信息。

现在完成时:
He has finagled his way to the top of the company. = 他通过耍小聪明爬到了公司的顶端。
She has finagled a better salary than her colleagues. = 她耍小聪明拿到了比同事更高的薪水。

过去完成时:
They had finagled a meeting with the CEO before the announcement. = 他们在公告发布前巧妙地安排了一次与CEO的会面。
She had finagled a promotion by flattering her boss. = 她通过奉承老板，巧妙地获得了晋升。

将来时:
He will finagle a way to get the tickets. = 他会想办法弄到票的。
She will finagle a meeting with the investor. = 她会想办法和投资者见面的。


虚拟语气:
If I could finagle a meeting with him, I would. = 如果我能巧妙地和他会面，我会的。
I wish I could finagle a better deal. = 我真希望我能谈到一个更好的交易。

被动语态:
A free ticket was finagled by him. = 他耍小聪明弄到了一张免费票。(被动语态略显不自然，此词多用于主动语态)
The information was finagled from him. = 信息被他巧妙地套取了。(被动语态略显不自然，此词多用于主动语态)


疑问句:
How did he finagle that? = 他是怎么耍小聪明做到的？
Can she finagle a better price? = 她能谈到更好的价格吗？


否定句:
He couldn't finagle his way into the concert. = 他没能耍小聪明混进音乐会。
She didn't finagle the results. = 她没有歪曲结果。


PHRASE->
finagle a deal: 讨价还价
finagle an advantage: 巧取优势
finagle a solution: 巧妙解决
finagle a way: 想方设法
finagle something out of someone: 从某人那里巧妙地得到某物


词源:
The origin of "finagle" is uncertain, but it's likely a blend of words, possibly including "fin" (end or finish) and a word suggesting manipulation or trickery.  It appeared in American English in the early 20th century.
