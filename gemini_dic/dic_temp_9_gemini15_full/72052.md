flirter:/ˈflɜːrtər/ | n. |调情者，卖弄风情的人 | A person who flirts.  Someone who behaves in a way that shows romantic interest in someone else, but without serious intentions. | Example: He's a known flirter, always chatting up women. 他是个出了名的调情者，总是跟女人搭讪。|近义词：tease, coquette, charmer |反义词：prude, shy person


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flirts with every girl he meets. = 他见到每个女孩都调情。
She flirts constantly to get attention. = 她不断地调情以引起注意。

一般过去时:
He flirted with her at the party. = 他在派对上和她调情。
She flirted with the bartender all night. = 她整晚都在和调酒师调情。

现在完成时:
He has flirted with several women this week. = 他这周已经和几个女人调情了。
She has flirted with danger her whole life. = 她一生都在玩火。

过去完成时:
He had flirted with the idea of quitting his job. = 他曾考虑过辞职。
She had flirted with several men before she met her husband. = 在遇到她丈夫之前，她曾与好几个男人调情。

将来时:
He will flirt with her again tomorrow. = 他明天还会和她调情。
She will flirt shamelessly at the club tonight. = 她今晚会在俱乐部肆无忌惮地调情。

虚拟语气:
If he flirted less, he might find a serious girlfriend. = 如果他不那么爱调情，或许就能找到一个认真的女朋友。
I wish she wouldn't flirt so much with other guys. = 我希望她不要再和别的男人那么调情了。

被动语态:
He was flirted with by many women at the event. = 在活动中，许多女人都和他调情。
(Note: Passive voice is less common with "flirt".)  这个词用被动语态的情况较少。

疑问句:
Does he flirt a lot? = 他很爱调情吗？
Is she flirting with him? = 她是在和他调情吗？

否定句:
He doesn't flirt; he's too shy. = 他不调情，他太害羞了。
She didn't flirt with him; she was just being friendly. = 她并没有和他调情；她只是表现得很友好。
