crowdfund:/ˈkraʊdfʌnd/| v.|众筹，通过大众募资|To raise funds for a project or venture by obtaining many small amounts of money from a large number of people, typically via the internet.|例句：The artist successfully crowdfunded their new album through an online platform.  这位艺术家通过一个在线平台成功地为他们的新专辑进行了众筹。

例句：They are trying to crowdfund their startup through a Kickstarter campaign. 他们正在尝试通过Kickstarter活动为他们的创业公司众筹资金。


近义词：fundraise, collect donations|反义词：self-fund


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They crowdfund their projects online. = 他们在线为他们的项目进行众筹。
We crowdfund every year for our charity event. = 我们每年为我们的慈善活动进行众筹。


一般过去时:
The company crowdfunded its new product last year. = 这家公司去年为其新产品进行了众筹。
She crowdfunded her education through a crowdfunding platform. = 她通过众筹平台为自己的教育筹集了资金。


现在完成时:
They have crowdfunded over $100,000 for their film. = 他们已经为他们的电影筹集了超过10万美元的资金。
We have successfully crowdfunded our community garden project. = 我们已经成功地为我们的社区花园项目进行了众筹。


过去完成时:
They had crowdfunded a significant amount before the launch. = 他们在发布之前已经筹集了一大笔资金。
She had crowdfunded her trip to Europe before she left. = 她出发去欧洲之前已经为这次旅行筹集了资金。


将来时:
We will crowdfund our next album release. = 我们将为我们下一张专辑的发布进行众筹。
They will crowdfund their research project next year. = 他们明年将为他们的研究项目进行众筹。


虚拟语气:
If they crowdfunded enough money, they would start the business. = 如果他们筹集到足够的资金，他们就会开始创业。
I wish I had crowdfunded my previous project. = 我希望我之前为我的项目进行了众筹。


被动语态:
The project was crowdfunded by thousands of people. = 这个项目是由成千上万的人众筹的。
The film's production was crowdfunded through a popular platform. = 这部电影的制作是通过一个流行的平台进行众筹的。


疑问句:

否定句:
They did not crowdfund their latest project. = 他们没有为他们最新的项目进行众筹。
We will not crowdfund this project without a solid plan. = 没有一个可靠的计划，我们不会为这个项目进行众筹。
