disestablished: /ˌdɪsɪˈstæblɪʃt/ | v. |废除（尤指教会的官方地位）,取消建制 | To officially end the establishment or recognition of something, especially a church or other institution as the official one.  |例句：The Church of England was disestablished in 1869. 英格兰教会在1869年被废除。

例句：The government disestablished the national broadcasting corporation.  政府取消了国家广播公司的建制。

近义词：abolish, dismantle, overthrow, suppress

反义词：establish, institute, found, create

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government disestablishes state-run industries. = 政府取消国营企业的建制。
They disestablish the old system every year. = 他们每年都废除旧制度。

一般过去时:
The monarchy was disestablished after the revolution. = 革命后君主制被废除。
The school disestablished its outdated curriculum. = 学校废除了过时的课程。

现在完成时:
They have disestablished the old order. = 他们已经废除了旧秩序。
The reforms have disestablished the former power structure. = 改革已经废除了原有的权力结构。

过去完成时:
Before the reforms, they had disestablished the old system. = 在改革之前，他们已经废除了旧制度。
The church had been disestablished long before the revolution. = 教会在革命前很久就被废除了。

将来时:
The new government will disestablish the current tax system. = 新政府将废除现行的税制。
They will disestablish the corrupt organization soon. = 他们很快就会取缔这个腐败的组织。

虚拟语气:
If they disestablished the corrupt system, the country would prosper. = 如果他们废除了腐败的制度，国家就会繁荣。
I wish they had disestablished the unfair laws sooner. = 我希望他们早点废除那些不公平的法律。

被动语态:
The old laws were disestablished by the new government. = 旧法律被新政府废除了。
The outdated system is being disestablished gradually. = 过时的制度正在逐步被废除。

疑问句:
Was the church disestablished in that year? = 那一年教会被废除了吗？
Will the old policies be disestablished next year? = 明年旧政策会被废除吗？

否定句:
The government did not disestablish the national bank. = 政府没有取消国家银行的建制。
They have not yet disestablished the outdated regulations. = 他们还没有废除过时的规章制度。


PHRASE->
disestablish a church = 废除教会的官方地位
disestablish a system = 废除一个制度
disestablish a tradition = 废除一种传统
disestablish a government = 推翻政府
disestablish a monopoly = 取消垄断


词源:

dis- (否定前缀) + establish (建立，确立)  The word is formed by adding the negative prefix "dis-" to the verb "establish," reversing its meaning.
