haycock:/ˈheɪkɒk/| n. |干草堆；圆锥形草堆 | A haycock is a small, conical pile of hay, typically made in the field after harvesting.  It's a temporary structure, often left to dry before being moved to a barn or other storage. |The farmer carefully shaped the haycock to allow for even drying.| 近义词：haystack (larger)| 反义词：None.
