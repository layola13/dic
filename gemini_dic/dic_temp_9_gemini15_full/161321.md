postpones:/pəˈspoʊnz/| v. |推迟，延缓 |To delay something until a later time.  |例句：The meeting has been postponed until next week.  会议推迟到下周举行。

The game was postponed due to the rain. 比赛由于下雨而推迟。|近义词：delay, defer, reschedule |反义词：expedite, accelerate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He postpones his trip every year. 他每年都推迟他的旅行。
She postpones making decisions until the last minute. 她总是拖到最后一刻才做决定。


一般过去时:
They postponed the wedding because of the storm. 他们因为暴风雨推迟了婚礼。
He postponed his vacation because of work. 他因为工作推迟了他的假期。


现在完成时:
We have postponed the project several times. 我们已经推迟了这个项目好几次了。
She has postponed her retirement plans. 她已经推迟了她的退休计划。


过去完成时:
They had postponed the launch before the announcement. 他们在宣布之前已经推迟了发射。
She had postponed answering his calls for weeks. 她已经推迟回复他的电话好几个星期了。


将来时:
They will postpone the concert if it rains. 如果下雨，他们将推迟音乐会。
We will postpone our decision until we have more information. 我们将在获得更多信息后再做决定。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have postponed the meeting. 如果我知道的话，我就推迟会议了。
I wish I had postponed my trip. 我真希望我推迟了我的旅行。


被动语态:
The meeting was postponed due to unforeseen circumstances. 会议由于不可预见的情况而被推迟。
The flight was postponed because of bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，航班被推迟。


疑问句:
Has the deadline been postponed? 最后期限是否被推迟了？
Will the game be postponed? 比赛会被推迟吗？


否定句:
They did not postpone the meeting. 他们没有推迟会议。
We will not postpone our plans. 我们不会推迟我们的计划。



PHRASE->
postpone until: 推迟到……
postpone for: 推迟……
postpone indefinitely: 无期限推迟
postpone to a later date: 推迟到以后的日期
postpone the decision: 推迟决定
