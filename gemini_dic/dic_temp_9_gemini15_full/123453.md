This term is primarily used in botany to describe fruits containing a single seed.  |  The olive is a monopyrenous drupe.  橄榄是单核核果。 The fruit is monopyrenous, containing a single large seed.  这个果实是单核的，含有一颗大的种子。 |  unipyrenous | polypyrenous |
