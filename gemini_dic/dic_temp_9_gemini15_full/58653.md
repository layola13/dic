dumbo:/ˈdʌmboʊ/| n. |小飞象，笨蛋|A large-eared, cartoon elephant created by Walt Disney.  The word is also used informally to refer to a clumsy or foolish person.|例句：Dumbo is a classic Disney cartoon. 小飞象是迪士尼的经典动画片。  He's such a dumbo; he tripped over his own feet. 他真是个笨蛋，居然绊倒了自己的脚。|近义词：fool, idiot, simpleton |反义词：genius, clever, smart|名词
