corant:/ˈkɒrənt/|ˈkɔːrənt/| n.|快步舞；早报；电流|A corant is a type of lively dance, particularly popular in the 16th and 17th centuries.  It could also refer to an early form of newspaper, often published daily and containing news from across the country or even internationally. In a more specialized context, it may refer to a current of electricity.|例句：The dancers performed a spirited corant. = 舞蹈演员们表演了一支充满活力的快步舞。

The corant brought news of the King's death. = 早报带来了国王驾崩的消息。|近义词：jig, galliard (dance); news bulletin, gazette (newspaper)|反义词：slow dance (dance);  (no direct antonym for newspaper)|名词
