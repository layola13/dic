feasibly:/ˈfiːzəbli/ | adv. | 可行地；切实地 |  In a way that is possible, practical, or likely.  It suggests that something can be done successfully without significant difficulty or insurmountable obstacles. |


例句：

It's feasibly to complete the project by the deadline. = 按期完成该项目是可行的。

We need to find a feasibly solution to this problem. = 我们需要找到一个可行的解决方案来解决这个问题。


近义词：practically, possibly, realistically, viably

反义词：impossibly, impractically, unrealistically


词性: 副词
