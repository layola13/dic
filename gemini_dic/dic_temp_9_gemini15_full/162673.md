preassume:/ˌpriːəˈsjuːm/ | vt. | 预先假定；预先设想 | To assume something beforehand; to presuppose.  This word is less common than "presume" or "presuppose." | 例句：He preassumed my agreement, which was a mistake. 他预先假定我同意了，这是一个错误。

例句：The committee preassumed the success of the project before any data was collected.  委员会在收集任何数据之前就预先假定了项目的成功。


近义词：presuppose, assume, presume, postulate


反义词：doubt, question, challenge


词性：动词
