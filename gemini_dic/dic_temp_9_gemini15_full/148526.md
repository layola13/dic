oversure:/ˈoʊvərʃʊr/| adj.|过分自信的，自以为是的|excessively or blindly confident; presumptuous.  It implies a confidence that is unwarranted or excessive, often leading to mistakes or misjudgments.|例句：He was oversure of his abilities and underestimated the challenge. 他对自己的能力过于自信，低估了挑战。|例句：Her oversure attitude alienated many of her colleagues. 她过于自信的态度疏远了她许多同事。|近义词：cocky, arrogant, presumptuous, self-assured (in a negative context)|反义词：humble, modest, diffident, self-doubting
