foamers:/ˈfoʊmɜrz/ | n. |发泡剂，起泡剂 |A substance that creates foam or froth.  Foamers are used in various applications, including fire suppression, cleaning, and personal care products. |例句：The fire fighters used a special foamer to extinguish the blaze. 消防员用一种特殊的起泡剂扑灭了大火。|例句：Many shampoos contain foamers to create a rich lather. 许多洗发水都含有起泡剂以产生丰富的泡沫。|近义词：frothers, sudsers |反义词：foam suppressants
