gargle:/ˈɡɑːrɡəl/| vt.| 漱口，含漱 |To rinse the mouth and throat with a liquid, usually to relieve a sore throat or clean the mouth. |例句：I gargle with salt water every morning to prevent sore throats. 我每天早上用盐水漱口以预防喉咙痛。  例句：She gargled with antiseptic mouthwash after her surgery. 她手术后用消毒漱口水漱口。|近义词：rinse, swish |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I gargle with warm salt water. = 我用温盐水漱口。
He gargles every morning. = 他每天早上都漱口。

一般过去时:
I gargled with mouthwash after brushing my teeth. = 我刷牙后用漱口水漱口。
She gargled before she went to bed. = 她睡前漱了口。

现在完成时:
I have gargled several times today because of my sore throat. = 今天因为喉咙痛我已经漱口好几次了。
She has never gargled with anything other than water. = 她除了用水漱口，从未用过其他东西。

过去完成时:
I had gargled before I went to see the doctor. = 我去看医生之前已经漱过口了。
He had gargled with antiseptic liquid, but the infection persisted. = 他用消毒液漱过口，但感染仍然持续存在。

将来时:
I will gargle with this medicine three times a day. = 我一天要用这种药漱口三次。
She will gargle after her next meal. = 她下次吃完饭后会漱口。

虚拟语气:

I wish I had gargled before eating that spicy food. = 我真希望在吃那种辛辣食物之前漱过口。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
I don't usually gargle. = 我通常不漱口。
