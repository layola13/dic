heirloom:/ˈɛrlum/| n.|传家宝，祖传遗物|A heirloom is a valuable object that has belonged to a family for many generations.  It's often associated with sentimental value and history.|例句：My grandmother left me her heirloom necklace, a beautiful piece of jewelry passed down through generations.  我的祖母留给了我她的传家宝项链，一件世代相传的精美首饰。

例句：This antique clock is a cherished heirloom, reminding us of our family's history. 这座古董钟是一件珍爱的传家宝，提醒我们家族的历史。


近义词：family treasure, ancestral possession, legacy |反义词：newly acquired item, disposable item
