philately:/fɪˈlætəli/| n. |集邮；邮票收集|The study of postage stamps and postal history.  Philately involves collecting, studying, and appreciating postage stamps and related materials.  It encompasses various aspects, including the stamps' designs, history, and the technical aspects of their production.|例句：His hobby is philately; he has a vast collection of rare stamps.他的爱好是集邮；他收藏了大量的珍稀邮票。
例句：The philatelic society held an exhibition showcasing stamps from around the world.集邮协会举办了一场展览，展出了来自世界各地的邮票。|近义词：stamp collecting|反义词：无
