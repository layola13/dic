coldish:/ˈkoʊldɪʃ/| adj.|稍冷的，有点冷的|Slightly cold; somewhat chilly.  It describes something that is not extremely cold, but cooler than what is considered comfortable or normal.|例句：The room was a little coldish, so I turned up the thermostat.  房间有点冷，所以我把恒温器调高了。|例句：The weather is coldish today, you should wear a jacket.今天天气有点冷，你应该穿一件夹克。|近义词：chilly, coolish, moderately cold|反义词：hot, warm, sweltering
