impoverishes: /ɪmˈpɑːvərɪʃɪz/ | vt. | 使贫穷；使枯竭；使恶化 | to make someone or something poor or weak |  The war impoverished the nation.  战争使这个国家贫困不堪。| The drought impoverished the soil.  干旱使土壤贫瘠。|近义词: depletes, weakens, ruins | 反义词: enriches, strengthens, improves | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government's policies impoverish many families. = 政府的政策使许多家庭陷入贫困。
His illness impoverishes his quality of life. = 他的疾病降低了他的生活质量。


一般过去时:
The famine impoverished the entire region. = 饥荒使整个地区陷入贫困。
Years of neglect impoverished the once-fertile land. = 多年的忽视使曾经肥沃的土地贫瘠不堪。


现在完成时:
Years of drought have impoverished the land, leaving it barren. = 多年的干旱使这片土地贫瘠，寸草不生。
Excessive logging has impoverished the forest ecosystem. = 过度砍伐使森林生态系统遭到破坏。


过去完成时:
The family had been impoverished by years of bad harvests. = 这个家庭多年歉收已经贫困不堪。
Decades of war had impoverished the entire country. = 数十年的战争使整个国家贫困不堪。


将来时:
Without intervention, the ongoing conflict will impoverish the entire nation. = 如果不采取干预措施，持续的冲突将使整个国家陷入贫困。
Continued pollution will impoverish the river ecosystem. = 持续的污染将使河流生态系统恶化。


虚拟语气:
If the government hadn't implemented those policies, the people wouldn't have been impoverished. = 如果政府没有实施那些政策，人们就不会陷入贫困。
If the company had acted more responsibly, it wouldn't have impoverished its investors. = 如果公司行事更负责任，就不会使投资者蒙受损失。


被动语态:
Many families were impoverished by the economic crisis. = 许多家庭在经济危机中陷入贫困。
The soil was impoverished by years of intensive farming. = 这片土壤由于多年的集约化耕作而贫瘠。


疑问句:
Did the recession impoverish many people? = 经济衰退是否使许多人陷入贫困？
Has the drought impoverished the farmland? = 干旱是否使农田贫瘠？


否定句:
The new policies did not impoverish the citizens. = 新政策并没有使公民贫困。
The investment did not impoverish him; rather, it enriched him. = 这项投资并没有使他贫困，反而使他富有。
