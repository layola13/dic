cough-up: /kɒf ʌp/| /kɔːf ʌp/ | vt. | 勉强拿出；吐出；咳出 | To produce or give something reluctantly or with difficulty; to vomit; to cough something up. | 例句：He finally coughed up the money he owed me. 他最终勉强拿出欠我的钱。  He coughed up a large amount of phlegm. 他咳出了一大口痰。 | 近义词：give up, produce, vomit, expectorate | 反义词：hold on to, keep, retain | 动词
