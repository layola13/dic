crape:/kreɪp/| n. |粗棉布；薄纱； crêpe; 绉纱|A crape is a thin, crinkled fabric, often black, used for mourning clothes.  It can also refer to a type of pancake. |例句：She wore a dress made of black crape. 她穿着一件黑绉纱裙子。

例句：The crepe Suzette was delicious.  苏格兰薄饼真好吃。|近义词：crepe, muslin, chiffon|反义词：thick fabric, sturdy fabric|名词
