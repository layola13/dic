gee-gee:/ˈdʒiːdʒiː/| n. |小马，矮种马|A young horse, especially a pony.  This term is primarily used informally and often affectionately.|例句：The children loved riding the gee-gee in the field. 孩子们喜欢在田野里骑小马。|例句：My little sister calls her pony her gee-gee. 我妹妹称她的矮种马为小马。|近义词：pony, nag (informal)|反义词：stallion (adult male horse)
