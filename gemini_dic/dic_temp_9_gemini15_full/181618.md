relaunch:/ˌriːˈlɔːntʃ/| vt. |重新启动；再次推出|To launch something again, especially after a period of inactivity or following a failure.|The company is planning to relaunch its flagship product next year. 公司计划明年重新推出其旗舰产品。|近义词：restart, relaunch, revive|反义词：terminate, discontinue


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They relaunch the product every year. = 他们每年都重新推出这款产品。
The company relaunches its marketing campaign. = 公司重新启动了它的营销活动。


一般过去时:
We relaunched the website last month. = 我们上个月重新启动了网站。
The government relaunched its economic reform program. = 政府重新启动了经济改革计划。


现在完成时:
They have relaunched their brand several times. = 他们已经多次重新推出他们的品牌。
The company has relaunched its app with new features. = 公司已经重新推出了其具有新功能的应用程序。


过去完成时:
They had relaunched the product before the crisis hit. = 在危机爆发之前，他们已经重新推出了该产品。
The team had relaunched the project after a thorough review. = 团队在彻底审查后重新启动了项目。


将来时:
We will relaunch the campaign next spring. = 我们将在明年春天重新启动活动。
The company will relaunch its product line in the fall. = 公司将在秋季重新推出其产品线。


虚拟语气:
If they relaunched the product, it might be successful. = 如果他们重新推出该产品，它可能会成功。
I wish they had relaunched the website sooner. = 我希望他们早点重新启动网站。


被动语态:
The product was relaunched with great fanfare. = 该产品在盛大的宣传活动中重新推出。
The website is being relaunched next week. = 网站将于下周重新启动。


疑问句:
When will the product be relaunched? = 产品什么时候重新推出？
Has the company relaunched its marketing strategy? = 公司是否重新启动了其营销策略？


否定句:
They did not relaunch the product as planned. = 他们没有按计划重新推出产品。
The company has not yet relaunched its website. = 公司尚未重新启动其网站。


PHRASE->
relaunch a product = 重新推出产品
relaunch a campaign = 重新启动一场活动
relaunch a website = 重新启动一个网站
relaunch a business = 重新启动一个企业
relaunch a career = 重新开始职业生涯
