loathnesses:/ˈloʊθnəsɪz/ | n. |厌恶；嫌恶；反感 |The plural form of "loathness,"  referring to multiple instances or types of reluctance, unwillingness, or aversion. It signifies a strong feeling of dislike or disinclination towards something. |例句：His loathnesses toward hard work and responsibility were well-known.  他厌恶努力工作和承担责任是出了名的。
His numerous loathnesses prevented him from pursuing any meaningful career. 他的诸多厌恶情绪阻止了他追求任何有意义的事业。|近义词：aversions, repugnances, dislikes |反义词：enthusiasms, affections, fondness
