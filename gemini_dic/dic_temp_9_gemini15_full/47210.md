daymare:/ˈdeɪmeər/| n.|噩梦；可怕的经历|A daymare is a bad or unpleasant experience that resembles a nightmare but occurs during the day.  It often refers to a situation that is stressful, frightening, or overwhelming.|例句：The presentation went terribly; it was a real daymare. 演示糟糕透顶；简直是一场噩梦。|例句：The traffic jam was a daymare, making me late for the meeting. 交通堵塞真是一场噩梦，让我开会迟到了。|近义词：nightmare, ordeal, bad experience|反义词：dream, pleasant experience|名词
