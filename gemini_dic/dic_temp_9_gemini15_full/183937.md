reservists:/ˈrɛzərˌvɪsts/| n.|预备役军人；后备人员|Members of a reserve force, especially in the military, who are not on active duty but can be called up in an emergency.  They are trained personnel who are available to be activated when needed.|例句：The reservists were called up to active duty during the war.  预备役军人在战争期间被征召服现役。

例句：Many reservists volunteered their skills to aid in disaster relief.  许多预备役人员自愿提供技能以协助救灾。


近义词：reserve forces, standby personnel, auxiliary troops


反义词：active-duty personnel, regulars


词性:名词
