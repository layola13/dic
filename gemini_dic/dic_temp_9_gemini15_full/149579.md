paid-down: /peɪd daʊn/ | adj. | 已付清的，已偿还的 |  Describes a debt or loan that has been completely repaid.  It emphasizes the completion of the repayment process. | Example:  He finally has a paid-down mortgage. 他终于还清了抵押贷款。  |近义词：repaid, settled, extinguished | 反义词：outstanding, unpaid, overdue |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loan is paid down. = 贷款已还清。
The debt is paid down in full. = 债务已全额还清。


一般过去时:
The mortgage was paid down last year. = 抵押贷款去年还清了。
We paid down the car loan early. = 我们提前还清了汽车贷款。


现在完成时:
They have paid down a significant portion of their student loans. = 他们已经还清了很大一部分学生贷款。
She has paid down her credit card debt. = 她已经还清了信用卡债务。


过去完成时:
Before the sale, he had paid down most of the debt. = 在出售之前，他已经还清了大部分债务。
By the time they retired, they had paid down their house loan. = 到他们退休的时候，他们已经还清了房屋贷款。


将来时:
They will have paid down their loan by next year. = 到明年他们将还清贷款。
We will pay down the debt gradually. = 我们将逐步偿还债务。


虚拟语气:
If he had paid down the debt earlier, he would be in a better financial position now. = 如果他早点还清债务，他现在财务状况就会更好。
If she paid down her debt consistently, she would be stress-free. = 如果她持续还清债务，她就会没有压力。


被动语态:
The debt was paid down by the end of the year. = 债务在年底已还清。
A considerable amount of money has been paid down on the property. = 一大笔钱已用于偿还该房产的债务。


疑问句:
Is the loan paid down? = 贷款还清了吗？
Has the debt been paid down completely? = 债务已完全还清了吗？


否定句:
The loan is not yet paid down. = 贷款尚未还清。
The debt hasn't been fully paid down. = 债务尚未完全还清。


PHRASE->
paid-down debt = 已付清的债务
paid-down loan = 已付清的贷款
paid-down mortgage = 已付清的抵押贷款
fully paid-down = 全额还清的
partially paid-down = 部分还清的
