nibbling:/ˈnɪblɪŋ/| n. v.|轻咬；一点点地吃；|The act of taking small bites of something.  It can refer to eating something in small amounts or gradually, or to gently biting something.|例句：The mouse was nibbling on a piece of cheese.  老鼠正在啃一块奶酪。
The child was nibbling at her cookie. 孩子在慢慢地吃她的饼干。|近义词：gnawing, pecking, munching|反义词：devouring, gobbling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nibbles his food. = 他细嚼慢咽地吃东西。
She nibbles on a carrot. = 她啃着胡萝卜。

一般过去时:
The rabbit nibbled the leaves. = 兔子啃食树叶。
He nibbled at the apple. = 他啃了口苹果。

现在完成时:
The mice have nibbled through the wires. = 老鼠已经啃断了电线。
She has nibbled all the chocolates. = 她把所有的巧克力都吃掉了（少量多次）。

过去完成时:
The dog had nibbled at the bone all night. = 狗整夜都在啃那根骨头。
They had nibbled most of the cake before the party started. = 派对开始前他们已经吃掉了大部分蛋糕（少量多次）。

将来时:
The squirrels will nibble the nuts. = 松鼠会啃食坚果。
I think the dog will nibble at your shoes. = 我想狗会啃你的鞋子。

虚拟语气:
If I were a mouse, I would nibble on cheese. = 如果我是老鼠，我会啃奶酪。
I wish I hadn't nibbled all the cookies. = 我真希望我没有把所有的饼干都吃掉了（少量多次）。

被动语态:
The carrots were nibbled by the rabbit. = 胡萝卜被兔子啃食了。
The bread was nibbled by the birds. = 面包被鸟啄食了。

疑问句:
Is the mouse nibbling the cheese? = 老鼠在啃奶酪吗？
Are they nibbling on popcorn? = 他们在吃爆米花吗？（少量多次）

否定句:
The dog wasn't nibbling the bone. = 狗没有啃骨头。
She didn't nibble the cake. = 她没有吃蛋糕（少量多次）。


PHRASE->
nibbling at:  一点点地吃
nibbling on:  啃着
nibbling away at:  一点一点地啃掉
nibbling through:  啃穿
nibbling around:  在...周围啃咬


词源:
The word "nibbling" comes from the Middle English word "niblen," meaning "to bite gently or repeatedly."  This, in turn, has roots in Old English.  The suffix "-ing" denotes the act of doing something.
