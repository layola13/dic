pediculation:/ˌpɛdɪkjuˈleɪʃən/| n. |阴虱感染；阴虱病|The infestation of lice in the pubic hair.  It is a sexually transmitted infection (STI).|例句：The clinic offers treatment for pediculation and other STIs.  诊所提供阴虱感染和其他性传播感染的治疗。

例句：Untreated pediculation can lead to intense itching and secondary skin infections. 未经治疗的阴虱感染会导致剧烈瘙痒和继发性皮肤感染。


近义词：pubic lice infestation, phthiriasis pubis | 反义词：无


词源：源自拉丁语词根 *pediculus (louse) + -ation (action or process).  表示虱子感染的过程。
