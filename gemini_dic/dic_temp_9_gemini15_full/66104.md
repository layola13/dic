exceedingly:/ɪkˈsiːdɪŋli/| adj. |极其地，非常地 | exceedingly  means to a very great extent; extremely. It is an adverb that intensifies the adjective or verb it modifies. |The task was exceedingly difficult.  这项任务极其困难。

The weather was exceedingly hot and humid. 天气极其炎热潮湿。


近义词：extremely, exceptionally, incredibly, extraordinarily, vastly


反义词：slightly, mildly, barely


词性：副词
