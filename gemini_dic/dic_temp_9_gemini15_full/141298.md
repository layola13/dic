open-minded: /ˌoʊpənˈmaɪndɪd/ | adj. | 开明的，心胸开阔的 | Willing to consider new ideas; unprejudiced.  Someone who is open-minded is receptive to different ideas and perspectives, even if they differ from their own. They are not rigid in their thinking and are willing to challenge their own beliefs. | 例句：He's very open-minded about different cultures. 他对不同的文化非常开明。

She has an open-minded approach to problem-solving. 她对解决问题采取开明的态度。


近义词：tolerant, receptive, broad-minded, liberal, unbiased | 反义词：narrow-minded, bigoted, prejudiced, intolerant, closed-minded


词性: 形容词
