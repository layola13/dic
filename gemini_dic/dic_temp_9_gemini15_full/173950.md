quasi-similarly:/ˈkwɑːziˌsɪmələli/| adv.| 准相似地；近似地 |  The two systems, while not identical, function quasi-similarly.  |  This adverb combines "quasi-" (meaning "seemingly," "apparently," or "to a certain extent") with "similarly" (meaning "in a similar way"). It describes a resemblance or similarity that is not exact or complete, but close enough to be considered analogous. |近义词：approximately similarly, roughly similarly, somewhat similarly | 反义词：dissimilarly, differently, contrarily | 副词
