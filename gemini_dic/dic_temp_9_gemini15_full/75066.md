frighting: /ˈfraɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人害怕的；可怕的 | causing or inspiring fear; terrifying. |例句：The storm was frightening.  暴风雨很可怕。

The frightening noises kept me awake all night.  可怕的声音让我整夜睡不着。


近义词：terrifying, scary, dreadful, alarming, horrifying | 反义词：comforting, reassuring, calming, peaceful


词性: 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The movie is frightening. = 这部电影很吓人。
That noise is frightening me. = 那个噪音让我害怕。

一般过去时:
The story was frightening. = 这个故事很吓人。
The experience was frightening for her. = 那次经历对她来说很可怕。

现在完成时:
The news has been frightening. = 这个消息一直令人害怕。
I have found the situation frightening. = 我发现这种情况很可怕。

过去完成时:
The house had been frightening to live in. = 住在那所房子里曾经很可怕。
We had found the journey frightening. = 我们发现这趟旅程很可怕。

将来时:
The journey will be frightening. = 这趟旅程将会很可怕。
The results will be frightening. = 结果将会令人害怕。

虚拟语气:
If the movie were frightening, I wouldn't watch it. = 如果电影很吓人，我就不看了。
I wish the experience hadn't been so frightening. = 我希望那次经历没有那么可怕。

被动语态:
The children were frightened by the loud thunder. = 孩子们被响雷吓坏了。(注意，这里是被动语态，主语是children，frighten是动词)
The audience was frightened by the monster in the movie. = 观众被电影里的怪物吓坏了。(注意，这里是被动语态，主语是audience，frighten是动词)

疑问句:
Is the story frightening? = 这个故事吓人吗？
Isn't that sound frightening? =  那个声音不吓人吗？

否定句:
The film wasn't frightening at all. = 这部电影一点也不吓人。
The situation is not frightening. = 情况并不吓人。



PHRASE->
a frightening experience = 一次可怕的经历
a frightening prospect = 一个令人恐惧的前景
a frightening thought = 一个令人恐惧的想法
a frightening sight = 一个可怕的景象
frighteningly realistic =  可怕的逼真


词源:  Derived from the Old English word *frēosian*, meaning "to tremble" or "to shiver," evolving through Middle English *frihten* (to frighten) to its modern form.  The -ing suffix creates the present participle, used adjectivally.
