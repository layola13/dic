counterimage:/ˈkaʊntərɪmɪdʒ/| n. |反像；负片；对照图像|A counterimage is a reversed or negative image, often used in photography or art to create a contrasting effect or to reveal hidden details.  It can also refer to a contrasting idea or concept.|Example: The photograph's counterimage revealed a hidden message in the shadows.|近义词：negative, reverse, opposite|反义词：positive image, original image
