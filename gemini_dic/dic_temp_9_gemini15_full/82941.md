gun:/ɡʌn/| n. |枪，炮|A weapon that shoots bullets or other projectiles.|例句：He pointed the gun at the robber. 他用枪指着强盗。  例句：The soldiers fired their guns. 士兵们开枪了。|近义词：firearm, pistol, rifle|反义词：peace


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He carries a gun. = 他携带一把枪。
She owns several guns. = 她拥有几支枪。

一般过去时:
He fired the gun. = 他开枪了。
They hid the gun. = 他们藏起了枪。

现在完成时:
He has never fired a gun. = 他从未开过枪。
She has owned that gun for years. = 她拥有那支枪多年了。

过去完成时:
He had hidden the gun before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他已经藏起了枪。
They had fired all their ammunition before the enemy surrendered. = 在敌人投降之前，他们已经打光了所有弹药。

将来时:
He will use the gun only in self-defense. = 他只会自卫时使用枪支。
They will buy new guns next week. = 他们下周会买新枪。

虚拟语气:
If I had a gun, I would defend myself. = 如果我有一把枪，我会自卫。
I wish I didn't have to carry a gun. = 我希望我不必携带枪支。

被动语态:
The gun was found by the police. = 枪是被警方发现的。
The gun was fired accidentally. = 这枪是意外走火的。

疑问句:

Is that a real gun? = 那是真枪吗？

否定句:

She didn't fire the gun. = 她没有开枪。
