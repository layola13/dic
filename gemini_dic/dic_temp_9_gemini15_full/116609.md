matrimonious:/ˌmætrɪˈmoʊniəs/| adj.|已婚的；婚姻的；适合结婚的|Characterized by or relating to marriage; inclined or suited to marriage.  The word suggests a strong inclination towards or suitability for marriage, often implying a happy and committed marital state.  It doesn't simply mean "married".|例句：He's a very matrimonious type; he's always talking about settling down and starting a family.|近义词：marital, conjugal, wedded|反义词：unmarried, single, celibate
