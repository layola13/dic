embroiders:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdərz/| vt.|绣花；刺绣|To decorate (cloth or other material) with needlework.|She embroiders beautiful patterns on her clothes. 她在她衣服上绣美丽的图案。|近义词：embroider, stitch, sew|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She embroiders a floral pattern on the pillowcase. = 她在一个枕套上绣着花朵图案。
He embroiders his initials onto his shirts. = 他在他的衬衫上绣上他的姓名首字母。

一般过去时:
My grandmother embroidered beautiful tapestries. = 我祖母绣过美丽的挂毯。
