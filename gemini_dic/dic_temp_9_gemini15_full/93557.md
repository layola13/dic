imaginer:/ɪˈmædʒɪnər/| n. |想象者，设想者 | A person who imagines things, especially creatively.  An imaginer is someone with a vivid and active imagination, often used in creative fields. |Example: The renowned architect was a brilliant imaginer, transforming abstract concepts into breathtaking structures.  著名的建筑师是一位杰出的想象者，他将抽象的概念转化为令人惊叹的建筑。 |近义词：dreamer, visionary, inventor |反义词：realist


USAGE->

He imagines a better future. = 他想象着一个更美好的未来。
She imagines herself as a famous actress. = 她想象自己是一位著名的女演员。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
