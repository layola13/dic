drilling:/ˈdrɪlɪŋ/| n./v.|钻孔；训练；反复练习|The act of making a hole using a drill;  repeated practice to improve a skill.|例句：The drilling of the oil well took several weeks.  钻油井花了几个星期。The soldiers underwent rigorous drilling before deployment. 士兵们在部署前进行了严格的训练。|近义词：boring, training, practice|反义词：filling (as in filling a hole)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drills holes in the wall. = 他在墙上钻孔。
They drill new recruits every day. = 他们每天训练新兵。

一般过去时:
She drilled a hole in the wood. = 她在木头上钻了个洞。
The team drilled the play repeatedly. = 球队反复练习了这个战术。

现在完成时:
They have drilled several wells this year. = 他们今年已经钻了几口井。
She has drilled herself on the piano piece. = 她已经反复练习了这首钢琴曲。

过去完成时:
He had drilled many holes before he found the right spot. = 他在找到合适的位置之前已经钻了很多洞。
The team had drilled the play many times before the game. = 球队在比赛前已经多次练习了这个战术。

将来时:
They will drill a new well next month. = 他们下个月将钻一口新井。
She will drill the students on grammar tomorrow. = 她明天将给学生们讲解语法。

虚拟语气:
If I had a drill, I would drill a hole. = 如果我有一把钻头，我会钻个洞。
If they had drilled more, they might have won. = 如果他们练习得更多，他们可能会赢。

被动语态:
The hole was drilled by a professional. = 这个洞是由专业人士钻的。
The recruits were drilled relentlessly. = 新兵们接受了残酷的训练。

疑问句:
Are you drilling a hole? = 你在钻孔吗？
Will they be drilling today? = 他们今天会进行训练吗？

否定句:
He didn't drill the hole correctly. = 他没有正确地钻孔。
They didn't drill enough before the competition. = 他们在比赛前练习不够。
