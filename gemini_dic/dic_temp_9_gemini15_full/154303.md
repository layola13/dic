peritoneally:/ˌpɛrɪtəˈniːəli/| adv.|腹膜的；经腹膜的 | Relating to or affecting the peritoneum.  The administration of a drug or substance via the peritoneum. |例句：The fluid was administered peritoneally.  液体经腹膜途径给药。

The drug was injected peritoneally.  药物经腹膜注射。


近义词：intraperitoneally | 反义词：None specifically opposite, but extraperitoneally could be considered a contrasting term. | 词性: 副词
