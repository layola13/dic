impassible:/ɪmˈpæsəbl/| adj.|不可逾越的；不能通行的；无动于衷的 | incapable of being passed or traversed; incapable of feeling emotion.  |The mountain pass was impassable due to the heavy snow.  由于大雪，山口无法通行。| The river became impassable after the heavy rains.  大雨过后，河水变得无法通行。| passable, accessible | passable, penetrable,  permeable


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The mountain pass is impassable. = 山口无法通行。
This problem is impassable. = 这个问题无法解决。


一般过去时:
The road was impassable after the earthquake. = 地震后道路无法通行。
His heart remained impassable to her pleas. = 他对她的恳求无动于衷。


现在完成时:
The bridge has been impassable since the storm. = 自从暴风雨以来，桥梁一直无法通行。
He has remained impassable in the face of criticism. = 面对批评，他依然无动于衷。


过去完成时:
The path had been impassable for weeks before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，这条小路已经无法通行数周了。
She had been impassable to his advances for years. = 多年来，她对他一直无动于衷。


将来时:
The road will be impassable until the repairs are completed. = 在维修完成之前，道路将无法通行。
His resolve will remain impassable despite the pressure. = 尽管面临压力，他的决心依然坚定。


虚拟语气:
If the road were impassable, we would have to take the train. = 如果道路无法通行，我们就得坐火车。
If he were not so impassable, she might have a chance. = 如果他不那么无动于衷，她或许还有一线希望。


被动语态:
The road was rendered impassable by the flood. = 道路被洪水冲毁，无法通行。
His heart was considered impassable to any feeling. = 他的心被认为对任何感情都无动于衷。


疑问句:
Is the road impassable? = 道路无法通行吗？
Is he truly impassable to all appeals? = 他真的对所有请求都无动于衷吗？


否定句:
The road is not impassable; we can still drive through. = 道路并非无法通行；我们仍然可以开车通过。
He is not entirely impassable; he does show some emotion. = 他并非完全无动于衷；他确实表现出一些情绪。
