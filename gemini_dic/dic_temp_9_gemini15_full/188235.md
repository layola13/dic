royalism:/ˈrɔɪəlɪzəm/ | n. | 皇室主义；君主制拥护 | The belief in or support for a system of government by a monarch.  It often involves a strong attachment to traditional values and the monarchy itself, viewing it as a symbol of national unity and stability.  |  例句：The king's popularity fueled a resurgence of royalism in the country. 国王的受欢迎程度激起了该国皇室主义的复兴。 |  例句：Her deep-seated royalism led her to actively campaign against republican movements. 她根深蒂固的皇室主义促使她积极反对共和运动。 | 近义词：monarchism, loyalism | 反义词：republicanism
