seasoning:/ˈsiːzənɪŋ/ | n. | 调味料；调味品 | Substances used to enhance the flavor of food, such as herbs, spices, salt, and pepper.  | Example:  The key to a good stew is the seasoning.  好的炖菜的关键在于调味。 |近义词：spice, condiment, flavoring | 反义词：blandness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chef adds seasoning to the soup. = 厨师往汤里加调味料。
This dish needs more seasoning. = 这道菜需要更多调味料。

一般过去时:
She seasoned the chicken with herbs. = 她用香草调味鸡肉。
He added extra seasoning after tasting the food. = 他尝过食物后加了额外的调味料。

现在完成时:
I have seasoned the vegetables perfectly. = 我已经完美地调好了蔬菜的味。
They have been experimenting with different seasonings. = 他们一直在尝试不同的调味料。

过去完成时:
She had already seasoned the meat before she put it in the oven. = 她把肉放进烤箱之前就已经调好味了。
We had seasoned the pasta sauce earlier that day. = 我们那天早些时候已经调好意面酱的味了。

虚拟语气:
