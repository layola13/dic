dictations:/dɪkˈteɪʃənz/ | n. |听写；口述；命令|The act of dictating or something dictated; a passage dictated for practice in shorthand or typing; an instance of giving orders or instructions.  |例句：The teacher gave us a dictation on the new vocabulary.老师给我们进行了一次新词汇的听写练习。|例句：The boss's dictations were clear and concise.老板的指示清晰简洁。|近义词： transcription, dictation exercise, instructions |反义词：writing independently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gives dictations every Monday. = 他每周一都进行听写。
She dictates letters to her secretary. = 她向秘书口述信件。


一般过去时:
The teacher dictated a passage from the textbook. = 老师口述了一段课文。
He dictated his will to his lawyer. = 他向律师口述了他的遗嘱。
