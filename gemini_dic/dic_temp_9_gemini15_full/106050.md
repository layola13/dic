kneejerk:/ˈniːdʒɜːrk/| adj. n.|膝跳反射的；下意识的；反应迅速的；|An automatic, unthinking reaction or response.  A reflex action involving an involuntary contraction of the muscles of the leg in response to a sharp tap on the patellar tendon.|例句：His kneejerk reaction was to deny any involvement.他的下意识反应是否认任何参与。  His support for the policy was a kneejerk reaction to the party line. 他对这项政策的支持是对党内路线的本能反应。|近义词：instinctive, automatic, reflex, impulsive|反义词：deliberate, considered, thoughtful
