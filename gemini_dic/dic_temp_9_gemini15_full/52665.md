digitized:/ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪzd/| adj.| 数字化的；已数字化的 | Converted into digital form; expressed as a series of digits.  |例句：The museum has digitized its entire collection of photographs.  博物馆已经将其全部照片收藏数字化了。 |例句：The company digitized its accounting system to improve efficiency. 公司将其会计系统数字化以提高效率。|近义词：digital, electronic |反义词：analog, non-digital
