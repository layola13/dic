conqueror:/ˈkɒŋkərər/ /ˈkɑːŋkərər/| n. |征服者，胜利者|A conqueror is a person who defeats an enemy or opponent, often taking control of their land or people.  They are often associated with military victory and the establishment of power.|Example: Alexander the Great was a renowned conqueror who built a vast empire. 亚历山大大帝是一位著名的征服者，他建立了一个庞大的帝国。|近义词：victor, vanquisher, subduer|反义词：defeated, conquered
