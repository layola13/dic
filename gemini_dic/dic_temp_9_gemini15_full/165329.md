preinvestment:/ˌpriːɪnˈvɛstmənt/| n. |前期投资；预先投资 | Investment made before a larger project or undertaking begins, often used for planning, research, and development.  It aims to reduce risks and increase the likelihood of success in the main investment. | Example: The preinvestment phase involved extensive market research and feasibility studies.  前期投资阶段包括广泛的市场调研和可行性研究。|近义词：advance investment, preliminary investment, upfront investment |反义词：post-investment, follow-up investment
