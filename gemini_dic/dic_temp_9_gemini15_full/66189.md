excide:/ɪkˈsaɪd/| v.|使兴奋，刺激；激发|To excite or stimulate; to arouse or awaken.|The thrilling music excited the crowd.  令人兴奋的音乐激起了人群的热情。|The news excited him greatly. 这个消息让他非常兴奋。|stimulate, arouse, invigorate|calm, depress, soothe|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music excites me. = 这音乐让我兴奋。
The news excites the public. = 这则新闻让公众兴奋。


一般过去时:
The speech excited the audience. = 这演讲让听众兴奋。
The roller coaster excited the children. = 过山车让孩子们兴奋。


现在完成时:
The good news has excited everyone. = 好消息让每个人都兴奋。
His success has excited his family. = 他的成功让他的家人兴奋。


过去完成时:
I had been excited about the trip for weeks. = 我已经对这次旅行兴奋了好几周了。
They had excited themselves with the prospect of victory. = 他们因为胜利的前景而激动不已。


将来时:
The upcoming concert will excite many fans. = 即将来临的演唱会将让许多歌迷兴奋。
The announcement will excite investors. = 这则公告将让投资者兴奋。


虚拟语气:
If the plan excited them, they would support it. = 如果这个计划让他们兴奋，他们就会支持它。
I wish the game had excited me more. = 我希望这场比赛能让我更兴奋。


被动语态:
The audience was excited by the performance. = 观众被演出所打动。
The children were excited by the gift. = 孩子们因为礼物而兴奋。


疑问句:
Does the idea excite you? = 这个主意让你兴奋吗？
What excites you about this project? =  你对这个项目什么感到兴奋？


否定句:
The movie didn't excite me at all. = 这部电影一点也不让我兴奋。
The presentation didn't excite the investors. = 这次演示并没有让投资者兴奋。
