double: /ˈdʌbəl/ | adj., n., v. | 双倍的；两倍的；双重的；两份的；加倍；使加倍 |

英文解析：
As an adjective, "double" means consisting of two identical parts or things; twice as much or as many.  As a noun, it refers to something that is double another or a duplicate. As a verb, it means to make or become twice as much or as many; to multiply by two.

例句：
adj.  The room had double beds. = 这个房间有双人床。

adj.  She earns double what I do. = 她挣的钱是我的两倍。


n.  He ordered a double espresso. = 他点了一杯双份浓缩咖啡。

n.  The police are investigating a possible double murder. = 警方正在调查一起可能的双重谋杀案。


v.  We'll have to double our efforts to meet the deadline. = 我们必须加倍努力才能赶上最后期限。

v.  The recipe instructs you to double the ingredients if you're cooking for six people. = 食谱说明如果为六个人做饭，需要将食材加倍。


近义词：
twin, twofold, duplicate, twice


反义词：
single, half, solitary


词性：
adjective, noun, verb
