hardens:/ˈhɑːrdənz/|/ˈhɑːrdənz/| vt.|变硬；使硬化|To become or make something become firm and solid.  The process often involves a loss of plasticity and an increase in strength and rigidity. |例句：The cement hardens as it dries. 水泥干燥后变硬了。

The sun hardens the clay.  太阳使粘土变硬。


近义词：solidify, stiffen, firm up|反义词：soften


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun hardens the ground every day. = 太阳每天使地面变硬。
The clay hardens as it dries. = 粘土干燥时变硬。


一般过去时:
The mixture hardened overnight. = 混合物一夜之间变硬了。
The concrete hardened quickly in the hot sun. = 烈日下混凝土迅速变硬。


现在完成时:
The paint has hardened. = 油漆已经干了（变硬了）。
The glue has hardened, so I can't use it. = 胶水已经变硬了，所以我不能用了。


过去完成时:
The bread had hardened by the time we got home. = 我们到家时面包已经变硬了。
The plaster had hardened before we could remove the mold. = 我们还没来得及移除模具，石膏就已经变硬了。


将来时:

This material will harden when exposed to air. = 这种材料暴露在空气中会变硬。


虚拟语气:
If the mixture hardened quickly, we could finish the project sooner. = 如果混合物快速变硬，我们就能更快完成项目。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

The clay hasn't hardened enough to be used. = 粘土还没有硬化到可以使用的程度。
