lobbing:/ˈlɒbɪŋ/| /ˈlɑːbɪŋ/ | vt. n.| 投掷；猛击；(尤指政治上的)游说 | To throw (something, especially something large or heavy) in a high arc.  Also, to exert pressure on (someone) especially to influence their opinion or decision. |例句：He was lobbing grenades at the enemy. 他正向敌人投掷手榴弹。

The senator was accused of lobbing for special interests. 参议员被指控为特殊利益集团游说。|近义词：throwing, pitching, hurling, lobbying (n.) |反义词：receiving, catching


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are lobbing the ball across the field. = 他们正在把球投掷过球场。
He is constantly lobbing for stricter gun control laws. = 他一直在游说更严格的枪支管制法。

一般过去时:
She lobbed the heavy package onto the table. = 她把沉重的包裹扔到桌子上。
The company lobbied the government for tax breaks. = 这家公司游说政府给予税收减免。

现在完成时:
They have lobbed several proposals to the committee. = 他们已经向委员会提交了几项提案。
The environmental group has been lobbing for years to protect the rainforest. = 这个环保组织多年来一直在游说保护雨林。

过去完成时:
The pitcher had lobbed a slow curveball before the batter swung. = 投手在击球手挥棒之前投了一个慢速曲线球。
The activists had lobbied extensively before the bill was introduced. = 在法案提出之前，活动家们进行了广泛的游说。

将来时:
She will lob the ball high over the net. = 她将把球高高地抛过网。
They will continue lobbing for changes to the healthcare system. = 他们将继续游说改变医疗体系。

虚拟语气:
If he had lobbed the ball harder, it would have reached the other side. = 如果他把球扔得更用力，它就能到达对面。
If they had lobbied more effectively, they might have achieved their goals. = 如果他们游说更有效，他们可能已经实现了他们的目标。

被动语态:
The ball was lobbed gently into the basket. = 球轻轻地被抛进篮筐。
The proposal was heavily lobbied against by several industries. = 该提案受到了几个行业的强烈反对游说。

疑问句:
Was the ball lobbed or thrown? = 球是抛的还是扔的？
Have they been successfully lobbing for the new policy? = 他们游说新政策成功了吗？

否定句:
He didn't lob the ball far enough. = 他没有把球扔得够远。
The organization didn't lobby the government on that issue. = 该组织没有就该问题游说政府。
