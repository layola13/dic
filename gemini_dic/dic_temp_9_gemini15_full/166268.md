prerecites:/ˌpriːrɪˈsaɪts/| v. |预先背诵；预先朗诵|To recite something beforehand; to rehearse a recitation.|He prerecites his lines before every performance. 他每次演出前都会预先背诵台词。|近义词：rehearse, practice|反义词：improvise


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prerecites his speech every morning. = 他每天早上都会预先背诵演讲稿。
She prerecites the poem before the competition. = 她在比赛前预先背诵这首诗。

一般过去时:
The actor prerecited his lines before the filming. = 演员在拍摄前预先背诵了台词。
They prerecited their speeches for the conference. = 他们为会议预先背诵了演讲稿。

现在完成时:
She has prerecited the poem many times. = 她已经多次预先背诵了这首诗。
He has prerecited his lines for tomorrow's play. = 他已经为明天的戏剧预先背诵了台词。

过去完成时:
The students had prerecited the verses before the test. = 学生们在考试前已经预先背诵了诗句。
She had prerecited the speech several times before delivering it. = 在发表演讲之前，她已经预先背诵了好几次。

将来时:
He will prerecite his poem for the upcoming event. = 他将为即将到来的活动预先背诵他的诗歌。
They will prerecite their parts before the play begins. = 在戏剧开始之前，他们将预先背诵他们的台词。

虚拟语气:
If I had prerecited my speech, I wouldn't have been so nervous. = 如果我预先背诵了我的演讲稿，我就不会那么紧张了。
I wish I had prerecited the script last night. = 我真希望我昨晚预先背诵了剧本。

被动语态:

The poem was prerecited by the student for the class. = 这首诗被学生预先背诵给课堂上的人听。

疑问句:

否定句:
