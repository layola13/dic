crammers:/ˈkræmərz/| n.|临时抱佛脚的人；死记硬背的人|Someone who studies intensively for a short period, especially just before an examination.|The students were all crammers the night before the final exam.|近义词：swot, grinder|反义词: diligent student (长期努力学习的人)
