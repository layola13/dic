romping:/ˈrɒmpɪŋ/| /ˈrɑːmpɪŋ/ | adj., v.|嬉戏的；喧闹的；痛打；|英文解析：Moving or playing in a lively and energetic way; boisterous.  Also, to defeat someone decisively.|例句：The children were romping in the garden.孩子们在花园里嬉戏。

The team romped to victory.  这个队轻松获胜。|近义词：playing, frolicking, cavorting,  thrashing, trouncing |反义词：quiet, subdued, gentle, losing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The children are romping in the park. = 孩子们在公园里嬉戏。
He is romping through his work. = 他正在飞速地完成他的工作。


一般过去时:
They romped through the fields. = 他们在田野里嬉戏。
Our team romped to victory last night. = 我们队昨晚轻松获胜。


现在完成时:
The dog has romped all afternoon. = 这只狗整个下午都在嬉戏。
They have romped their way to the top of the league. = 他们一路高歌猛进，登上了联赛榜首。


过去完成时:
They had romped through the first half of the game. = 他们在比赛的上半场轻松领先。
The children had romped before their nap. = 孩子们在午睡前玩耍了一番。


将来时:
The puppies will be romping all over the house. = 小狗们会在房子里到处乱跑。
We will romp to victory in the next game. = 我们将在下一场比赛中取得压倒性胜利。


虚拟语气:
If they had more time, they would romp in the park. = 如果他们有更多时间，他们会在公园里嬉戏。
I wish I could romp through life without a care. = 我希望我能无忧无虑地度过一生。


被动语态:
The field was romped on by the children. = 田野被孩子们玩耍过了。
(Note:  The passive voice is less common with "romp".)


疑问句:
Are the children romping again? = 孩子们又在嬉戏了吗？
Were they romping in the mud? = 他们在泥地里玩耍吗？


否定句:
The children weren't romping; they were quietly reading. = 孩子们没有嬉戏；他们安静地看书。
He didn't romp through the exam; he struggled. = 他没有轻松通过考试；他很费力。
