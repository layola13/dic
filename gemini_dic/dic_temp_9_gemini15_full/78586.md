giantism:/ˈdʒaɪəntɪzəm/| n.|巨人症，巨大症|A condition characterized by excessive growth of the body or its parts, typically caused by an overproduction of growth hormone.|例：Acromegaly is a form of giantism that affects adults.  肢端肥大症是一种影响成年人的巨人症。|近义词：gigantism, gigantism|反义词：dwarfism
