myops:/ˈmaɪɒps/| n.|近视眼|Myopia, also known as nearsightedness, is a refractive error of the eye in which close objects appear clear but distant objects appear blurry.  This occurs because the eyeball is too long or the cornea (the clear front part of the eye) is too curved.|例句：He suffers from myops and needs glasses to see distant objects. 他患有近视，需要戴眼镜才能看清远处的物体。  Myopia is a common vision problem affecting millions worldwide. 近视是一种常见的视力问题，影响着全球数百万的人。|近义词：nearsightedness, shortsightedness|反义词：hyperopia (farsightedness)|名词
