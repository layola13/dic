inorganity: /ˌɪnɔːrɡˈænɪti/ | n. | 无机性；非有机性 | The lack of organic structure or organization; the state or quality of being inorganic.  It refers to the characteristics of inorganic substances, lacking the complex organization of living organisms. | Example: The inorganity of the rock contrasted sharply with the vibrant life of the surrounding ecosystem.  岩石的无机性与周围生态系统的勃勃生机形成鲜明对比。|近义词: inorganic nature, mineral nature |反义词:organicity, organic nature
