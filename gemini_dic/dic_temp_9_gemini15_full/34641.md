clenbuterol:/ˌklenˈbjuːtəˌrɒl/ | n. |克仑特罗|A synthetic sympathomimetic amine used as a bronchodilator and, illegally, to increase muscle mass in livestock and humans. It is a beta-2 agonist that stimulates beta-2 adrenergic receptors in the lungs and other tissues, causing relaxation of smooth muscle and bronchodilation.  However, its use carries significant health risks. |例句：The athlete tested positive for clenbuterol, resulting in disqualification from the competition.  这位运动员的克仑特罗检测呈阳性，导致其被取消比赛资格。

The presence of clenbuterol in the meat supply raised serious concerns about food safety. 肉类供应中克仑特罗的存在引发了人们对食品安全的严重担忧。|近义词：salbutamol (less potent), terbutaline |反义词：None directly antonymous, but drugs with opposite effects on the respiratory system could be considered indirect antonyms (e.g., bronchoconstrictors).
