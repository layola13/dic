candles:/ˈkændlz/| n.|蜡烛|A cylindrical or tapered mass of wax or other combustible material with a wick in the center, that is burned to give light or scent.|例句：She lit the candles on the birthday cake. 她点燃了生日蛋糕上的蜡烛。|例句：The flickering candles cast a warm glow on the room. 闪烁的烛光给房间投下温暖的光芒。|近义词：lights, tapers|反义词:electric lights, sunlight
