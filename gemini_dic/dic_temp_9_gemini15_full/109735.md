leverages:/ˈlɛvərɪdʒɪz/| vt.|利用，杠杆作用|To use something to maximum advantage.|The company leverages its strong brand image to attract customers.  这家公司利用其强大的品牌形象吸引顾客。|近义词： utilize, exploit, employ |反义词： neglect, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He leverages his skills to get ahead. = 他利用他的技能来获得成功。
The company leverages its resources effectively. = 这家公司有效地利用其资源。


一般过去时:
She leveraged her network to find a job. = 她利用她的人脉找到了工作。
They leveraged their experience to build a successful business. = 他们利用他们的经验建立了一家成功的企业。


现在完成时:
We have leveraged technology to improve our efficiency. = 我们已经利用技术提高了效率。
They have leveraged their knowledge to create innovative products. = 他们已经利用他们的知识创造了创新产品。


过去完成时:
He had leveraged his savings to start a business. = 他已经动用他的积蓄来创业。
The company had leveraged its market position to dominate the industry. = 这家公司已经利用其市场地位主导了该行业。


将来时:
We will leverage the new software to streamline our workflow. = 我们将利用新软件来简化我们的工作流程。
They will leverage their partnership to expand into new markets. = 他们将利用他们的合作关系拓展新的市场。


虚拟语气:
If we leveraged our resources better, we would be more successful. = 如果我们更好地利用资源，我们会更成功。
I wish we had leveraged our opportunities more effectively. = 我希望我们能更有效地利用我们的机会。


被动语态:
The new strategy will be leveraged to increase market share. = 新战略将被用来增加市场份额。
Their expertise is leveraged by many companies. = 他们的专业知识被许多公司所利用。


疑问句:
How can we leverage this technology? = 我们如何利用这项技术？
What resources will be leveraged in this project? = 在这个项目中将利用哪些资源？


否定句:
They did not leverage their strengths effectively. = 他们没有有效地利用他们的优势。
We will not leverage outdated technology. = 我们不会利用过时的技术。
