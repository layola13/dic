skilling:/ˈskɪlɪŋ/| n. |技能培养；技能发展；熟练掌握技能的过程|The process of acquiring or developing skills, often in a specific field or area.  It can refer to individual skill development, or training programs designed to improve skills within a workforce. | Example:  The company invested heavily in skilling its employees in new technologies. 公司大力投资员工在新技术方面的技能培训。The skilling initiative aims to reduce unemployment. 技能发展计划旨在减少失业。|近义词：training, development, upskilling, reskilling |反义词：de-skilling (the reduction of skills)|名词
