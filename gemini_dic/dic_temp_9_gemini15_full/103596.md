joiner:/ˈdʒɔɪnər/ | n. |木匠，细木工；结合物，连接件 | A joiner is a skilled carpenter who makes and fits the interior woodwork of buildings, such as doors, windows, and staircases.  A joiner can also refer to something that joins two things together. | Example: He hired a joiner to install the new kitchen cabinets. 他雇了一个木匠来安装新的厨房橱柜。  The metal bracket acted as a joiner for the two pipes. 金属支架充当了这两根管子的连接件。 |近义词：carpenter, fitter, connector |反义词：separator, divider |名词

词源:
The word "joiner" comes from the Old English word "gegyndra," meaning "one who joins."  It's a direct descendant of the verb "to join," reflecting its core function of connecting things.
