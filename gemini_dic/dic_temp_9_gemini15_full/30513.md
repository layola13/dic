ceo:/ˈsiːiːoʊ/|/ˈsiːiːəʊ/| n. |首席执行官，最高执行长|Chief Executive Officer, the highest-ranking executive in a company or organization, responsible for the overall management and performance.|Example:  The CEO announced a new strategic plan for the company.  首席执行官宣布了公司新的战略计划。

The CEO resigned after the company's stock price plummeted. 公司股价暴跌后，首席执行官辞职了。|近义词：president, managing director, top executive|反义词：employee, subordinate
