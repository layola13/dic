nauseate:/ˈnɔːzieɪt/| vt. |使恶心；使反感 |To cause (someone) to feel nausea; to disgust or sicken.  |The smell of the rotting fish nauseated me.  腐烂鱼的臭味使我恶心。|The speaker's blatant lies nauseated the audience. 演讲者公然的谎言使听众反感。|近义词：disgust, sicken, revolt |反义词：delight, please


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The smell of garbage nauseates me. = 垃圾的气味使我恶心。
Spicy food nauseates many people. = 辛辣的食物使很多人恶心。

一般过去时:
The roller coaster ride nauseated him. = 过山车让他感到恶心。
The sight of blood nauseated her. = 看到血让她感到恶心。

现在完成时:
That movie has nauseated me. = 那部电影让我恶心。
This strong perfume has nauseated her all day. = 这种浓烈的香水让她恶心了一整天。

过去完成时:
The spoiled milk had nauseated him before he even tasted it. = 馊掉的牛奶在他还没尝之前就已经让他恶心了。
She had been nauseated by the strong odor all morning. = 她整个上午都被强烈的臭味弄得恶心。


将来时:
I think that strong medicine will nauseate you. = 我想那种烈性药会让你恶心。
The long journey will likely nauseate her. = 漫长的旅程可能会让她恶心。

虚拟语气:
If I ate that, I think it would nauseate me. = 如果我吃了那个，我想它会让我恶心。
I wish that awful smell wouldn't nauseate me so much. = 我希望那种难闻的气味不会让我这么恶心。

被动语态:
He was nauseated by the motion of the boat. = 他被船的晃动弄得恶心了。
Many passengers were nauseated during the turbulent flight. = 许多乘客在颠簸的飞行中感到恶心。

疑问句:

Does eating raw fish nauseate you? = 吃生鱼会让你恶心吗？

否定句:
The food didn't nauseate me. = 这食物没有让我恶心。
The ride didn't nauseate her. = 这次乘坐并没有让她恶心。
