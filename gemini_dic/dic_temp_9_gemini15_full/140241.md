offloader:/ˌɒfˈləʊdə(r)/|/ˈɒfləʊdər/| n. |卸载机；卸货机；卸载程序|A machine or program that unloads something, typically cargo from a vehicle or data from a computer system.  |例句：The port uses specialized offloaders to quickly unload containers from ships. 港口使用专用卸货机来快速从船上卸下集装箱。

例句：The software offloader efficiently transfers large datasets from the server to the local machine. 该软件卸载程序有效地将大型数据集从服务器传输到本地计算机。|近义词：unloader, discharger|反义词：loader
