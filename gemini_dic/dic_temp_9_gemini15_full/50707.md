dermophyte:/ˈdɜːrməfaɪt/| n. |皮肤真菌|A fungus that causes skin infections. Dermophytes are a type of fungus that thrives in warm, moist environments and feeds on keratin, a protein found in skin, hair, and nails.  They cause infections known as dermatophytoses.|Example:  Athlete's foot is a common dermatophyte infection.|近义词：fungus, dermatophyte (synonymous)|反义词：无
