ninefold:/ˈnaɪnfoʊld/| adj.|九倍的，九重叠的|Ninefold increase means an increase to nine times the original amount.  九倍的增长意味着增长到原来的九倍。|例句：The company's profits showed a ninefold increase this year. 今年公司利润增长了九倍。  The legend is a ninefold tale of love and betrayal. 这个传说是一个关于爱情和背叛的九重故事。|近义词：nine times as much, nonuple|反义词：single, onefold
