frostbit:/ˈfrɔːstbɪt/| n. adj.|冻伤；冻伤的|Frostbite is damage to body tissue caused by freezing.  It most commonly affects the extremities, such as fingers, toes, ears, and nose.|例句：The hiker suffered severe frostbite on his toes after spending a night in the blizzard.  登山者在暴风雪中过夜后，脚趾严重冻伤。|例句：The frostbitten fingers needed immediate medical attention. 冻伤的手指需要立即就医。|近义词：freezing, chillblains|反义词：healing, recovery|
