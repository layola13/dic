cathartin:/kæˈθɑːrtɪn/| n. |泻药；番泻叶提取物|A cathartic is a strong laxative that causes a bowel movement.  It is often derived from plants like senna.  Cathartins are the active compounds responsible for this effect. |Example: The doctor prescribed a cathartic to relieve the patient's constipation.  医生开了泻药来缓解病人的便秘。|近义词：purgative, laxative|反义词：constipant|名词
