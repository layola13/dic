delectably:/dɪˈlɛktəbli/| adj.|令人愉悦地，美味地|Pleasingly; delightfully, especially to the taste.  The word suggests a high degree of pleasure.|例句：The cake was delectably moist and flavorful.  这蛋糕湿润可口，令人愉悦。

例句：The aroma of the freshly baked bread was delectably fragrant. 新鲜烘焙的面包香味令人愉悦地芬芳。


近义词：deliciously, delightfully, appetizingly, pleasingly

反义词：disgustingly, unappetizingly, unpleasantly

词性：形容词
