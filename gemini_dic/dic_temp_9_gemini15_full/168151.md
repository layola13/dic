pro bono:/ˌproʊ ˈboʊnoʊ/| adj.|公益的；免费的|For the public good; done or given without payment.|The lawyer offered pro bono services to the needy family.  律师为这个贫困家庭提供了公益法律服务。|近义词：free, gratis, voluntary|反义词：paid, for-profit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He does pro bono work for the homeless shelter. = 他为无家可归者收容所做公益工作。
She offers pro bono legal advice to small businesses. = 她为小型企业提供免费的法律咨询。


一般过去时:
The law firm completed a pro bono case last month. = 该律师事务所上个月完成了一起公益案件。
They provided pro bono services to victims of the hurricane. = 他们为飓风受害者提供了公益服务。
