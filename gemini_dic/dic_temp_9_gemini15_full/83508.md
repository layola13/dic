词源:
The word "hacksaw" is a compound word.  "Hack" refers to a rough or forceful cut, while "saw" is the tool used for cutting.  The combination suggests a saw used for a rough, forceful cutting action, which is characteristic of a hacksaw.  The "-ed" ending is the past tense and past participle suffix for regular verbs.
