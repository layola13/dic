dipped:/dɪpt/| vt.  |浸，蘸；下降；减少；|to put something quickly into a liquid and take it out again|The bird dipped its beak into the water.  鸟把喙浸到水里。|She dipped a brush into the paint. 她把刷子蘸到油漆里。|近义词：immerse, dunk, submerge|反义词：lift, raise


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dips his bread into the soup. 他把面包蘸到汤里。
She dips her toes in the water. 她把脚趾浸在水里。


一般过去时:
I dipped my finger in the honey. 我把手指蘸了一下蜂蜜。
The temperature dipped below freezing. 温度降到了冰点以下。


现在完成时:
The sales have dipped significantly this quarter. 本季度销售额大幅下降。
The sun has dipped below the horizon. 太阳已经落山了。


过去完成时:
The stock market had dipped sharply before the announcement. 在公告发布之前，股市已经大幅下跌。
The bird had dipped its head before drinking.  鸟在喝水之前已经低下了头。


将来时:
The temperature will dip to -5 degrees tonight. 今晚气温将降至零下5度。
He will dip his pen in ink. 他将把钢笔蘸上墨水。


虚拟语气:
If the temperature dipped below zero, the pipes would freeze. 如果温度降到零度以下，管道就会冻结。
I wish the price hadn't dipped so much.  我希望价格没有下降这么多。


被动语态:
The bread was dipped in oil. 面包被浸在油里。
The cookies were dipped in chocolate. 饼干蘸了巧克力。


疑问句:
Did you dip the fries in ketchup? 你把薯条蘸番茄酱了吗？
Has the value of the dollar dipped lately? 美元汇率最近下降了吗？


否定句:
He didn't dip his biscuit in his tea. 他没有把饼干蘸在茶里。
The plane didn't dip its wings.  飞机没有倾斜机翼。
