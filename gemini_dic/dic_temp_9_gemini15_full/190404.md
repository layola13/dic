sarong:/ˈsæɹɒŋ/| n. |沙龙，纱笼 | A length of cloth, typically brightly coloured, worn wrapped around the body, especially by women in parts of Southeast Asia.  It's often used as a skirt or dress.  | Example: She wore a beautiful batik sarong to the beach. 她穿着一条美丽的蜡染沙龙去海滩。|近义词：wrap, skirt, pareo |反义词：无
