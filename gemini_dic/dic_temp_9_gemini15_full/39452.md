connectome:/ˈkənɛktoʊm/ | n. | 连接组；脑连接图谱 | A connectome is a comprehensive map of neural connections in the brain, showing how different areas are interconnected.  It represents the structural and functional organization of the brain. | Example:  Researchers are working to map the human connectome to better understand brain function and disease.  研究人员正在努力绘制人类连接组图谱，以更好地了解大脑功能和疾病。 |近义词: brain map, neural network | 反义词: None
