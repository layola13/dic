precocious:/prɪˈkoʊʃəs/| adj.|早熟的，过早发育的|Showing unusually early development or maturity, especially in mental abilities.  |例句：She was a precocious child who could read before she was three. 她是一个早熟的孩子，三岁就能阅读。

例句：He showed a precocious talent for music at a very young age. 他很小就表现出了过人的音乐天赋。


近义词：advanced, gifted, talented, mature


反义词：backward, immature, undeveloped

词性:形容词


USAGE->

He is precocious in his understanding of complex topics. 他对复杂主题的理解很早熟。
The child is precocious and already reads Shakespeare. 这个孩子很早熟，已经读莎士比亚了。

She was precocious and excelled in her studies. 她很早熟，学习成绩优异。

过去完成时:
