presubscribe: /ˌpriːsʌbˈskraɪb/ | vt. | 预先订阅；预订 | To subscribe to (something) in advance.  This typically involves committing to a future service or product before it's officially available or released. | 例句：I presubscribed to the new software to get early access. 我预先订阅了新软件以获得早期访问权限。

例句：Many fans presubscribed to the limited edition vinyl record.  许多粉丝预订了限量版黑胶唱片。


近义词：preorder, pre-register, reserve


反义词：cancel (subscription), unsubscribe


词性：动词
