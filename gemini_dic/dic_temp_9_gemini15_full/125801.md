murkiest:/ˈmɜːrkiɪst/ | adj. | 最黑暗的；最模糊不清的 | Most dark; lacking clarity; obscure.  The murkiest depths of the ocean are still largely unexplored.  The politician's answers were deliberately murky, avoiding any direct response to the questions. |近义词：darkest, cloudiest, foggiest, obscurest, most unclear |反义词：clearest, brightest, clearest, most transparent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water is murky. = 水很浑浊。
The future seems murky. = 未来看起来很不明朗。


一般过去时:
The lake was murky after the storm. = 暴风雨过后湖水变得浑浊。
His explanation was murky and unconvincing. = 他的解释含糊不清且令人难以信服。


现在完成时:
The river has become murky due to pollution. = 由于污染，河水已经变得浑浊。
The situation has remained murky despite investigations. = 尽管进行了调查，情况仍然不明朗。


过去完成时:
The water had been murky for days before the cleanup began. = 在清理工作开始前的几天里，水一直很浑浊。
The details of the agreement had remained murky until the final contract was signed. = 在最终合同签署之前，协议的细节一直不明确。


将来时:
The forecast predicts the water will be murky tomorrow. = 预报预测明天的水会很浑浊。
The future of the company will likely remain murky until the new CEO is appointed. = 在任命新首席执行官之前，公司的未来可能会仍然不明朗。


虚拟语气:
If the water were less murky, we could see the fish. = 如果水不那么浑浊，我们就能看到鱼了。
If the situation were clearer, we would be able to make a decision. = 如果情况更清晰，我们就能做出决定。


被动语态:
The lake is often made murky by heavy rains. = 湖泊经常被大雨冲刷得浑浊不堪。
The details were kept murky to protect sensitive information. = 细节被故意模糊以保护敏感信息。


疑问句:
Is the water murky? = 水浑浊吗？
Is the political situation still murky? = 政治局势仍然不明朗吗？


否定句:
The water is not murky; it's quite clear. = 水并不浑浊；它相当清澈。
The future is not necessarily murky; there is hope. = 未来并不一定不明朗；仍然有希望。
