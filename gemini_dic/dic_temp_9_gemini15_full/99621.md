/ˌɪntərˈdʒʌŋkʃənz/ | n. | 交叉点；联结点；连接 | Interjunctions refer to points or areas where things intersect or connect, often implying a joining or merging of different elements or paths.  They can be physical locations (like road intersections) or abstract concepts (like the interjunction of ideas). | Example: The interjunction of the two rivers formed a fertile delta.  交叉的两条河流形成了一个肥沃的三角洲。 The interjunction of art and technology is a fascinating field of study.  艺术与科技的结合是一个引人入胜的研究领域。 | 近义词：junction, intersection, confluence, meeting point | 反义词：separation, divergence, disjunction |
