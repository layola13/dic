retrained:/ˌriːˈtreɪnd/| v. | 再培训，重新训练 | To train (someone or oneself) again, often in a different skill or area of expertise.  |例句：The company retrained its employees in new software. 公司对员工进行了新软件的再培训。|例句：He retrained as a plumber after losing his job as a carpenter.  他失业后重新学习了水管工。|近义词：re-educated, reskilled, upskilled |反义词：untrained, unskilled


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They retrain employees annually. = 他们每年对员工进行再培训。
The company retrains its staff in new technologies. = 公司对员工进行新技术的再培训。


一般过去时:
The factory retrained its workers on safety procedures. = 工厂对工人进行了安全规程的再培训。
She retrained as a teacher after working in finance. = 她从事金融工作后重新培训成为一名教师。


现在完成时:
He has retrained as a software developer. = 他已经重新培训成为一名软件开发人员。
The government has retrained thousands of unemployed workers. = 政府已经对成千上万的失业工人进行了再培训。


过去完成时:
They had retrained the staff before the new system was implemented. = 新系统实施之前，他们已经对员工进行了再培训。
She had retrained several times before finding her niche. = 在找到自己的专长之前，她已经多次进行了再培训。


将来时:
The company will retrain its employees in the new system next month. = 公司下个月将对员工进行新系统的再培训。
I will retrain as a nurse after I finish my current job. = 我完成目前的工作后将重新培训成为一名护士。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would retrain as a chef. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新培训成为一名厨师。
It would be beneficial if the company retrained its employees in customer service. = 如果公司对员工进行客户服务的再培训将是有益的。


被动语态:
The workers were retrained in emergency procedures. = 工人们接受了紧急程序的再培训。
The software will be retrained to improve accuracy. = 该软件将重新训练以提高准确性。


疑问句:
Have you ever been retrained in your career? = 你在职业生涯中是否接受过再培训？
Will the company retrain its staff on the new software? = 公司会对员工进行新软件的再培训吗？


否定句:
He did not retrain after losing his job. = 他失业后没有进行再培训。
The company has not yet decided whether to retrain its employees. = 公司尚未决定是否对员工进行再培训。


PHRASE->

retrain for a new career = 为新的职业进行再培训
retrain in a new skill = 学习新的技能
retrain employees on new technology = 对员工进行新技术的再培训
government retraining programs = 政府再培训项目
