nesters:/ˈnestərz/| n. |筑巢者，（尤指）新婚夫妇|People who are nesting, especially newly married couples who are setting up a home together.  They are focused on creating a comfortable and stable home environment.|例句：The young nesters spent their weekends painting and decorating their new apartment.  年轻的夫妇们周末都在粉刷和装饰他们的新公寓。|例句：Many nesters find that buying a house is a significant step in their lives. 许多筑巢者发现买房是他们生活中一个重要的步骤。|近义词：newlyweds, homemakers, householders|反义词：nomads, wanderers
