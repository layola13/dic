hypostrophe:/haɪˈpɒstrəfi/ | n. |下位置换；词尾同义反复|A rhetorical device where the same word or phrase is repeated at the end of successive clauses or sentences.  It emphasizes the repeated word or phrase and creates a sense of rhythm and impact.|例句：The government of the people, by the people, for the people, shall not perish from the earth.  (林肯葛底斯堡演说中运用到的修辞手法) 人民的政府，由人民，为人民，将不会从地球上消失。|近义词：epistrophe, epiphora|反义词：anaphora
