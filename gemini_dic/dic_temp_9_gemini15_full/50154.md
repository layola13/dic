denutrition: /ˌdiːnuˈtrɪʃən/ | n. |营养不良；营养不足 |  The state of being inadequately nourished; a deficiency of calories or essential nutrients. This can lead to various health problems, including weight loss, muscle wasting, weakened immune system, and impaired growth and development.  | Example:  Prolonged illness can lead to denutrition. 长期生病会导致营养不良。  The child suffered from severe denutrition due to famine.  这个孩子由于饥荒而患有严重的营养不良。 | 近义词：malnutrition, undernutrition | 反义词：overnutrition, overfeeding |  名词
