membership:/ˈmɛmbə(r)ʃɪp/| n.|成员资格；会员身份；会籍|The state of being a member of a group or organization; the rights and privileges of a member.|例句：His membership in the club gives him access to the exclusive facilities.  他的俱乐部会员资格使他可以使用专属设施。
例句：Membership fees are due at the end of the year. 会费在年底到期。|近义词：affiliation, enrolment, subscription|反义词：non-membership


USAGE->
