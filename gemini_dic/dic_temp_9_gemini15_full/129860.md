non-individual: /ˌnɒnɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəl/ | adj. | 非个体的；非个人的 |  Not relating to or characteristic of a single person or thing; collective or general.  The term often implies a lack of individuality or uniqueness, suggesting something is part of a larger group or system, lacking distinct characteristics. | 例句：The non-individual nature of the data made it difficult to identify specific trends.  数据非个体的性质使得难以识别具体的趋势。

The study focused on the non-individual aspects of group behavior.  这项研究关注群体行为的非个体方面。


近义词：collective, general, impersonal, aggregate, unified

反义词：individual, unique, distinct, specific, personalized


词性：形容词
