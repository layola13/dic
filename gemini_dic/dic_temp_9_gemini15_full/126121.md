mutessarif:/ˈmjuːtəsærif/| n. |省长，总督（奥斯曼帝国）|A mutessarif was a high-ranking Ottoman official who governed a vilayet (province) or a smaller administrative division.  They held significant authority and were responsible for the administration of justice, taxation, and public order within their jurisdiction. |The mutessarif of Damascus was known for his strict but fair rule.  达马斯克的省长以其严格但公平的统治而闻名。|governor, prefect, administrator|rebel, subordinate|名词
