mark-time:/mɑːrk taɪm/| v.|原地踏步，虚度光阴|To remain in the same place or condition; to make no progress.|The soldiers marked time while waiting for orders. 士兵们原地踏步等待命令。The project is marking time until more funding is secured.  项目在等待获得更多资金之前一直停滞不前。|近义词：stall, delay, procrastinate|反义词：progress, advance, proceed


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They mark time while waiting for the signal. = 他们在等待信号时原地踏步。
He marks time instead of working on the project. = 他虚度光阴而不是致力于项目。

一般过去时:
The soldiers marked time for hours. = 士兵们原地踏步等候数小时。
She marked time by reading books instead of working. = 她通过读书而不是工作来消磨时间。

现在完成时:
We have marked time for too long on this project. = 我们在这个项目上虚度了太长时间。
They have marked time since their last success. = 自从他们上次成功以来，他们一直停滞不前。

过去完成时:
The team had marked time for weeks before the breakthrough. = 在取得突破之前，团队已经原地踏步了几周。
He had marked time in his career before finally finding a fulfilling job. = 在最终找到一份令人满足的工作之前，他的职业生涯一直停滞不前。

将来时:
They will mark time until the next phase begins. = 他们将在下一阶段开始之前原地踏步。
She will mark time until her boss decides. = 她将等待老板决定。

虚拟语气:
If they had not marked time, they would have finished earlier. = 如果他们没有虚度光阴，他们本可以早点完成。
If I had known better, I wouldn’t have marked time. = 如果我知道得更好，我就不会浪费时间了。

被动语态:
Time was marked by the slow progress of the negotiations. = 时间因谈判的缓慢进展而被拖延。
Progress was hampered because time was marked by inactivity. = 由于时间被浪费，进度受到了阻碍。

疑问句:
Are they marking time again? = 他们又在原地踏步了吗？
Is this project marking time or actually progressing? = 这个项目是停滞不前还是真的在进展？

否定句:
They did not mark time; they worked efficiently. = 他们没有浪费时间；他们工作效率很高。
We won't mark time any longer; we need to finish this. = 我们不会再浪费时间了；我们需要完成这个。
