polynomial:/ˌpɒlɪˈnəʊmiəl/ /ˌpɑːlɪˈnoʊmiəl/| n. |多项式，多项式函数|A polynomial is a mathematical expression involving a sum of powers in one or more variables multiplied by coefficients.  The powers must be non-negative integers.  For example, 3x² + 2x + 1 is a polynomial.|Example: The equation represents a second-degree polynomial.  这个方程式表示一个二次多项式。
