plasterworks:/ˈplæstərwɜːrks/| n. |灰泥制品；灰泥工程；石膏制品|The collective term for decorative or functional items made of plaster. It can refer to the process of creating plasterwork or the resulting creations themselves.  This encompasses a wide range of objects, from architectural moldings and ceilings to sculptures and decorative wall panels. |The intricate plasterworks in the palace were a testament to the artisans' skill. 宫殿里复杂的灰泥制品证明了工匠们的技艺。 The restoration project involved extensive plasterworks repairs. 修复项目包括大量的灰泥工程修复。|stone carving, stucco work | (no direct antonym, as it describes a type of work,  but opposites might be  "plain surfaces", "unadorned walls")|名词
