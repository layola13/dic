eruptions:/ɪˈrʌpʃənz/| n.|喷发；爆发；（疾病的）发作|The sudden or violent throwing out of something, especially lava or steam from a volcano, or any similar forceful ejection of material.|例句：The volcanic eruptions caused widespread devastation. 火山喷发造成了大范围的破坏。

例句：The eruptions of the geyser were spectacular.间歇泉的喷发非常壮观。

近义词：outbursts, explosions, bursts|反义词：calm, subsidence
