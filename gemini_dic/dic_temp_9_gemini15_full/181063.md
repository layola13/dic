reincured:/ˌriːɪnˈkɜːrd/ | v. | 再度遭受；再次承担 | To incur again; to suffer or experience again.  The word is formed from "re-" (again) and "incurred" (past participle of incur). It implies a repetition of a previous negative experience or cost. | Example: The company reincurred significant losses after the unexpected market downturn. 公司在意外的市场低迷后再次遭受了重大损失。 | 近义词：re-experience, suffer again, experience anew | 反义词：avoid, prevent, escape
