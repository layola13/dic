mulch:/mʌltʃ/| n. v.|覆盖物；护根物；覆盖（土壤）|A layer of material (such as decaying leaves, bark, or compost) spread over the surface of the soil to retain moisture, suppress weeds, and enrich the soil. |例句：We spread a thick layer of mulch around the rose bushes. 我们在玫瑰花丛周围铺了一层厚厚的覆盖物。


例句：The gardener mulched the flower beds with wood chips. 园丁用木屑覆盖了花坛。


近义词：ground cover, covering, compost


反义词：bare soil


词性:Noun and Verb
