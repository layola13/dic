half-profile:/ˈhæf ˈproʊfaɪl/| n.|半身侧面像|A portrait or representation of a person showing only one side of the face, usually the side view.  A half-profile is a type of profile view, in contrast to a full-face portrait. |例句：The coin featured a half-profile of the queen. 这枚硬币印有女王的半身侧面像。

The painting showed a half-profile of a young woman, her gaze directed away from the viewer. 这幅画展现了一个年轻女人的半身侧面像，她的目光移开了观者。|近义词：profile, side view|反义词：full-face portrait|名词
