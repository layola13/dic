oxidation:/ˌɒksɪˈdeɪʃən/ /ˌɑːksɪˈdeɪʃən/ | n. | 氧化；氧化作用 | The process of combining a substance chemically with oxygen.  This process often involves the loss of electrons by the substance being oxidized.  Oxidation can be a slow process, like the rusting of iron, or a rapid process, like combustion. | Example: The oxidation of iron produces rust.  铁的氧化产生锈。

The oxidation of glucose provides energy for the body. 葡萄糖的氧化为身体提供能量。| reduction | reduction |名词
