plunder:/ˈplʌndər/| n., vt.|掠夺；抢劫；抢掠物|The act of stealing valuable things from a place, especially during a war or riot; to steal valuable things from a place, especially during a war or riot.|例句：The Vikings plundered the coastal villages. 维京人洗劫了沿海村庄。

例句：The army plundered the city, taking all the gold and jewels.  军队洗劫了这座城市，带走了所有的金银珠宝。


近义词：loot, pillage, ransack, despoil|反义词：protect, preserve, conserve|词性:n., vt.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They plunder the resources of the country. = 他们掠夺国家的资源。
She plunders the shops for bargains. = 她到处搜寻便宜货。


一般过去时:
The pirates plundered the ship. = 海盗洗劫了那艘船。
The soldiers plundered the town after the battle. = 战斗结束后，士兵们洗劫了城镇。


现在完成时:
The invaders have plundered the ancient city. = 侵略者已经洗劫了这座古城。
They have plundered the museum of its artifacts. = 他们已经洗劫了博物馆的文物。


过去完成时:
The thieves had plundered the house before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，小偷已经洗劫了房子。
The army had plundered the villages before they reached the capital. = 军队在到达首都之前已经洗劫了村庄。


将来时:
The rebels will plunder the capital if they win the war. = 如果叛军赢得战争，他们将洗劫首都。
They will plunder the stores if the price goes up. = 如果价格上涨，他们将洗劫商店。


虚拟语气:
If the pirates had plundered the ship, we would have lost everything. = 如果海盗洗劫了那艘船，我们将失去一切。
I wish they hadn't plundered the museum. = 我希望他们没有洗劫博物馆。


被动语态:
The city was plundered by the invaders. = 这个城市被侵略者洗劫了。
The museum was plundered of its priceless treasures. = 博物馆被洗劫了其无价的珍宝。


疑问句:
Did the pirates plunder the ship? = 海盗洗劫了那艘船吗？
Was the town plundered after the battle? = 战斗后城镇被洗劫了吗？


否定句:
The army didn't plunder the village. = 军队没有洗劫村庄。
They didn't plunder the shops. = 他们没有洗劫商店。
