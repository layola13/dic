curdle:/ˈkɜːr(d)l/| vt. vi. n.|使凝结；使凝固；凝结；凝固|To turn or cause to turn into a curdled or clotted mass, typically milk or cream.  The verb can also describe the process of something becoming sour or unpleasant.|The milk curdled in the heat.  牛奶在高温下凝结了。The sour milk curdled into lumps. 酸奶凝结成块状。The sudden silence curdled the atmosphere. 突如其来的沉默使气氛凝固。|coagulate, thicken, solidify|liquid, smooth|
