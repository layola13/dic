hoofless:/ˈhuːfləs/| adj.|无蹄的|Lacking hooves.  The term describes animals that do not possess hooves on their feet.  This is a descriptive adjective primarily used in zoology and comparative anatomy. |例句：The hoofless animal scampered across the plains. 这只无蹄动物在平原上飞奔。

例句：Elephants, despite their size, are considered hoofless mammals.  大象，尽管体型庞大，却被认为是无蹄哺乳动物。


近义词：hooveless (less common)


反义词：hoofed


词性:形容词
