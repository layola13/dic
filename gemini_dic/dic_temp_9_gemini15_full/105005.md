kenogenetic:/ˌkiːnoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|新生成的，后生的；新生的|Relating to or denoting the origin and development of new organs or characters in evolution, particularly those that do not arise by modification of pre-existing structures.|The kenogenetic theory suggests that certain features in organisms arise anew in evolution, rather than being modified from ancestral structures.  新生成的理论表明，生物体中的某些特征是在进化中新产生的，而不是从祖先结构中修改而来的。|近义词：neogenetic, newly evolved|反义词：phylogenetic, ancestral
