inundates:/ˈɪnʌndeɪts/ | vt. | 淹没；泛滥；充斥 | To overwhelm (someone or something) with a large amount of something.  |例句：The heavy rain inundated the town.  大雨淹没了小镇。

例句：The fans inundated the stadium. 粉丝们涌入了体育馆。


近义词：flood, overwhelm, deluge, swamp | 反义词：drain, dry out | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river inundates the valley every spring. = 春天这条河都会淹没山谷。
He inundates his friends with emails. = 他用邮件淹没了他的朋友们。


一般过去时:
The flood inundated the entire city. = 洪水淹没了整个城市。
The news inundated the media. = 新闻充斥着媒体。


现在完成时:
The constant rain has inundated the fields. = 持续的降雨淹没了田地。
His problems have inundated his thoughts. = 他的问题充斥着他的思绪。


过去完成时:
The dam had already broken before the flood inundated the village. = 大坝在洪水淹没村庄之前就已经决堤了。
They had already been inundated with calls before the official announcement. = 在官方宣布之前，他们就已经接到了大量的电话。


将来时:
If the storm continues, it will inundate the coastal areas. = 如果风暴继续，它将淹没沿海地区。
The marketing campaign will inundate the public with advertisements. = 营销活动将用广告充斥公众视野。


虚拟语气:
If the river were to inundate the town, we would need to evacuate. = 如果河水淹没城镇，我们就需要撤离。
I wish the constant noise wouldn't inundate my concentration. = 我希望持续的噪音不会扰乱我的注意力。


被动语态:
The city was inundated by the rising tide. = 城市被上涨的潮水淹没了。
The company was inundated with job applications. = 公司收到了大量的求职申请。


疑问句:
Will the rising sea levels inundate our homes? = 上升的海平面会淹没我们的家园吗？
Is the market inundated with similar products? = 市场是否充斥着类似的产品？


否定句:
The drainage system prevented the area from being inundated. = 排水系统防止了该地区被淹没。
He didn't want to inundate his boss with unnecessary emails. = 他不想用不必要的邮件淹没他的老板。
