remasticated:/ˌriːˈmæstɪkeɪtɪd/ | v. | 再咀嚼；反复思考 |  To chew (food) again.  To think about something again and again. |  He remasticated the tough piece of meat. 他再次咀嚼了那块硬肉。

The dog remasticated its bone. 狗又啃起了它的骨头。


近义词：rechew, reconsider, ruminate, contemplate

反义词：swallow (in the context of food), ignore (in the context of thought)


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He remasticates his food thoroughly. 他仔细地咀嚼食物。
She remasticates her decisions before acting.  她在行动前反复思考她的决定。


一般过去时:
He remasticated the food before swallowing. 他在吞咽之前又咀嚼了食物。
She remasticated the problem all night. 她整夜都在反复思考这个问题。


现在完成时:
He has remasticated the same idea several times. 他已经好几次反复思考同一个想法了。
She has remasticated the evidence carefully. 她已经仔细地重新审视了证据。


过去完成时:
He had remasticated the information before presenting it. 他在陈述信息之前已经反复思考过了。
She had remasticated her options before making a choice.  她在做出选择之前已经反复考虑过她的选择。


将来时:
He will remasticate the proposal before submitting it. 他将在提交提案之前再次仔细考虑。
She will remasticate the data to ensure accuracy. 她将再次仔细检查数据以确保准确性。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would remasticate the entire argument. 如果我有更多时间，我会重新仔细考虑整个论点。
I wish I had remasticated my words before speaking. 我希望我在说话之前能三思而后行。


被动语态:
The arguments were remasticated by the committee. 这些论点被委员会反复讨论。
The evidence needs to be remasticated before a conclusion is reached. 在得出结论之前，需要重新审核证据。


疑问句:
Has he remasticated the contract terms? 他是否重新仔细审阅了合同条款？
Will she remasticate her response before sending it? 她会在发送回复之前重新考虑一下吗？


否定句:
He did not remasticate the information before making a decision. 他在做决定之前没有重新思考信息。
She didn't remasticate her options; she acted impulsively. 她没有重新考虑她的选择，而是冲动行事。
