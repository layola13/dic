gatefolds:/ˈɡeɪtˌfoʊldz/| n. |折叠页，拉页|A gatefold is a page in a book, magazine, or other printed material that folds out to reveal a larger image or text than would normally fit on a single page.  It's often used for maps, posters, or large photographs.|Example: The album cover featured a stunning gatefold revealing a panoramic landscape.|近义词：foldout, insert|反义词:None
