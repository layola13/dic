nonanimate: /ˌnɒnˈænɪmeɪt/ | adj. |非生物的；无生命的| Not possessing life; inanimate.  The term refers to objects, concepts, or entities that are not living organisms.  It contrasts with "animate," which describes living things. |

例句：

Rocks and mountains are nonanimate objects. = 石头和山是无生命的物体。

The research focused on nonanimate objects in the environment. = 这项研究集中在环境中的无生命物体。


近义词：inanimate, lifeless, inert


反义词：animate, living, alive


词性：形容词
