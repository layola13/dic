hygienists:/ˈhaɪdʒiːnɪsts/| n. |卫生学家，保健专家|Professionals who are trained to promote and maintain oral hygiene.  They work in various settings, such as dental offices, schools, and public health clinics.  They provide education, prevention, and treatment services to improve people's oral health.|例句：The hygienist cleaned my teeth and gave me advice on proper brushing techniques.  卫生学家清洁了我的牙齿，并给了我关于正确刷牙技巧的建议。|近义词：dental hygienist, oral hygienist|反义词：N/A
