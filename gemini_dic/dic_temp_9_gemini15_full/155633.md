phished:/fɪʃt/| v.|网络钓鱼；诱骗；|To deceive someone into revealing personal information such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details by disguising oneself as a trustworthy entity in a digital communication.|The scammer phished my login details by pretending to be my bank.|近义词：scammed, defrauded, tricked|反义词：protected, secured


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He often phishes for personal information online. = 他经常在网上钓鱼获取个人信息。
They phish their victims using fake websites. = 他们使用假网站来欺骗受害者。


一般过去时:
I was phished last week and lost my credit card information. = 我上周被钓鱼了，丢失了我的信用卡信息。
The company was phished by a sophisticated cybercriminal. = 这家公司被一个老练的网络罪犯进行了钓鱼攻击。


现在完成时:
Many people have been phished through email scams. = 许多人通过电子邮件诈骗被钓鱼。
The hackers have phished millions of accounts this year. = 黑客今年已经窃取了数百万个账户。


过去完成时:
Before I realised, I had already been phished. = 在我意识到之前，我已经中了钓鱼。
They had been phished multiple times before they finally changed their passwords. = 在他们最终更改密码之前，他们已经被钓鱼多次了。


将来时:
They will phish more people using AI-powered tools. = 他们将使用人工智能工具欺骗更多的人。
Cybersecurity experts predict that phishing attacks will increase next year. = 网络安全专家预测，明年的网络钓鱼攻击将会增加。


虚拟语气:
If I had known it was a phishing scam, I wouldn't have clicked the link. = 如果我知道这是一个钓鱼骗局，我就不应该点击链接。
I wish I hadn't fallen for the phishing attempt. = 我真希望我没有上当受骗。


被动语态:
My account was phished and my money stolen. = 我的账户被钓鱼，我的钱被偷了。
Thousands of accounts are being phished every day. = 每天都有数千个账户被钓鱼。


疑问句:
Have you ever been phished? = 你曾经被钓鱼过吗？
Was your information phished from your social media accounts? = 你的信息是从你的社交媒体账户上被钓鱼的吗？


否定句:

They didn't phish successfully. = 他们的钓鱼攻击没有成功。
