commandos:/kəˈmɑːndoʊz/| n. |突击队队员；突击队员|Highly trained soldiers who operate in small groups behind enemy lines to carry out special missions, such as reconnaissance, sabotage, or raids.|例句：The commandos carried out a daring night raid on the enemy's base.  突击队员对敌人的基地进行了一次大胆的夜间袭击。

例句：A team of commandos was sent to rescue the hostages.  一支突击队被派去营救人质。|近义词：special forces, raiders, saboteurs|反义词：regular troops, conventional forces|名词
