intermarried:/ˌɪntərˈmæriːd/| v.|通婚；异族通婚|To marry between different groups, especially social, racial, or religious groups.  |例句：Many families in this town have intermarried over the generations.  这个镇上的许多家庭几代人都通婚了。 |例句：The two communities have a long history of intermarrying. 这两个社区有长期通婚的历史。|近义词：marry across, interbreed, cross-marry|反义词：marry within (one's own group), remain separate
