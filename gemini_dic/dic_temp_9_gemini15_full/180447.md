regardless:/rɪˈɡɑːrdləs/ | adv. | 不管怎样；无论如何 | Regardless of the weather, we will go hiking.  不管天气如何，我们都会去远足。 | 近义词：irrespective of, notwithstanding, in spite of | 反义词：considering, depending on


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He goes to work regardless of the weather. = 不管天气如何，他都去上班。
She speaks her mind regardless of the consequences. = 她不管后果如何，都会直言不讳。


一般过去时:
They continued the project regardless of the funding issues. = 不管资金问题如何，他们都继续了项目。
He went ahead regardless of my objections. = 不管我的反对，他还是继续进行了。


现在完成时:
We have completed the task regardless of the difficulties. = 不管困难如何，我们都完成了任务。
She has maintained her composure regardless of the pressure. = 不管压力多大，她都保持着镇静。


过去完成时:
They had finished the job regardless of the late hour. = 不管时间已经很晚了，他们还是完成了工作。
She had made the decision regardless of her family's opinions. = 不管家人的意见如何，她都做出了决定。


将来时:
We will go to the party regardless of the traffic. = 不管交通状况如何，我们都会去参加派对。
He will submit the report regardless of the deadline. = 不管截止日期如何，他都会提交报告。


虚拟语气:
I would go regardless of what they say. = 不管他们说什么，我都会去。
She would have done it regardless of the risk. = 不管有多大的风险，她都会去做。


被动语态:
The decision was made regardless of public opinion. = 这个决定是不管公众舆论如何做出的。
The plan was implemented regardless of the opposition. = 计划是不管反对意见如何而实施的。


疑问句:
Will you go regardless of the cost? = 不管成本多少，你都会去吗？
Should we proceed regardless of the potential risks? = 我们应该不顾潜在风险继续进行吗？


否定句:
I will not go regardless of what you say. = 不管你说什么，我都不去。
She did not consider the consequences regardless of her actions. = 她不管后果如何，做了自己的事。(这句略微改变了“regardless”的用法，更强调“不顾后果”)
