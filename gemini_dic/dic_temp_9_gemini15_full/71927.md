flibbertigibbet:/ˈflɪbərtiˈɡɪbɪt/| adj. n.|轻浮的；喋喋不休的人；轻浮的人|Frivolous, flighty, or excessively talkative person.  The word suggests a lack of seriousness and a tendency towards silliness or impertinence. It is often used to describe someone who is giddy and superficial.|例句：She's such a flibbertigibbet, always chattering away about nothing. 她真是个轻浮的人，总是喋喋不休地说着一些无关紧要的事。

例句：He acted like a flibbertigibbet at the important meeting, disrupting the proceedings.  他在重要的会议上表现得像个轻浮的人，扰乱了会议的进程。


近义词：flighty, frivolous, giddy, scatterbrained, irresponsible, giggly, frivolous, airhead


反义词：serious, responsible, sedate, composed, grave


词性: adjective, noun
