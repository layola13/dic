purgatorian:/ˌpɜːrɡəˈtɔːriən/| adj. |炼狱的；赎罪的|Relating to purgatory, a state or place of purification or punishment in some religious beliefs, where souls undergo purification before entering heaven.  It often implies a state of suffering or temporary punishment.|例句：The priest described the purgatorian fires of hell.  牧师描述了地狱炼狱之火。

例句：He felt a purgatorian sense of guilt after his actions. 他在行动之后感到一种炼狱般的罪恶感。


近义词：penitential, expiatory, atoning

反义词：heavenly, blissful, celestial


词性：形容词
