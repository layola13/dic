ferries:/ˈfɛriz/| n.|渡船，渡口|A ferry is a boat or ship used to carry passengers and sometimes vehicles across a body of water, especially between islands or across a river.|例句：The ferry to the island runs every hour.  岛上的渡船每小时一班。|例句：We took the ferry across the lake. 我们乘渡船过了湖。|近义词：boat, ship, barge|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ferry carries passengers across the river. = 渡船载着乘客穿过河流。
Ferries connect many islands in this archipelago. = 渡轮连接着这个群岛中的许多岛屿。
