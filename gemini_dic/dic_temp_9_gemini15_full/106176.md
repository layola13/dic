英文解析:  The verb "knock-up" has several meanings, some of which are considered informal or even vulgar.  It can mean to wake someone up, typically by knocking on their door; to make someone pregnant; to hastily construct something; to hit or strike something; to criticize or reprimand someone. The specific meaning depends heavily on context.

She was knocked up by her boyfriend. = 她被她的男朋友弄怀孕了。(粗俗说法)

The teacher knocked him up for being late. = 老师因为迟到而责备了他。


近义词: wake up (唤醒),  impregnate (使怀孕),  construct (建造), hit (击打),  reprimand (责备)
