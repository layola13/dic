narration:/næˈreɪʃən/ | n. |叙述，讲述，故事 |The narration in the novel is captivating. 小说中的叙述引人入胜。|近义词：account, description, recital |反义词：silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The narrator describes the scene vividly.  = 叙述者生动地描述了场景。
He narrates the story in a captivating way. = 他以引人入胜的方式讲述故事。


一般过去时:
She narrated the events of the day to her friend. = 她向朋友讲述了当天发生的事情。
The author narrated the story from the protagonist's perspective. = 作者以主人公的视角讲述了故事。


现在完成时:
He has narrated many stories throughout his career. = 他在他的职业生涯中讲述了许多故事。
She has narrated the events leading up to the accident. = 她已经讲述了导致事故发生的一系列事件。


过去完成时:
He had narrated the story several times before. = 他之前已经讲述过好几次这个故事了。
She had narrated the whole incident to the police. = 她已经把整个事件向警方讲述了。


将来时:
He will narrate the story at the conference. = 他将在会议上讲述这个故事。
She will narrate her experiences in Africa. = 她将讲述她在非洲的经历。


虚拟语气:
If he narrated the story differently, it would be more engaging. = 如果他以不同的方式讲述故事，它会更引人入胜。
I wish she had narrated the events more clearly. = 我希望她能更清晰地讲述这些事件。


被动语态:
The story was narrated in a compelling manner. = 故事以引人入胜的方式讲述。
The events of the war were narrated by several eyewitnesses. = 战争的事件由几位目击者讲述。


疑问句:
Who narrated the audiobook? = 谁讲述的有声书？
How will the story be narrated? = 故事将如何讲述？


否定句:
He did not narrate the story accurately. = 他没有准确地讲述故事。
The movie did not narrate the events chronologically. = 电影没有按时间顺序讲述事件。


PHRASE->
first-person narration = 第一视角叙述
third-person narration = 第三视角叙述
unreliable narration = 不可靠的叙述
voice-over narration = 画外音叙述
internal narration = 内心独白叙述
