pack-away: /pæk əˈweɪ/ | v. | 收拾；整理；妥善存放 | To put something away neatly and securely, often for storage.  |例句：I need to pack away the Christmas decorations after the holidays.  圣诞节过后，我需要把圣诞装饰品收起来。

例句：Let's pack away these clothes before they get dusty.  让我们把这些衣服收起来，以免落灰。


近义词：put away, store, tidy away, stow away | 反义词：unpack, take out, retrieve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He packs away his clothes every Sunday. = 他每周日都会收拾好他的衣服。
She packs away the groceries after shopping. = 她买完东西后会把杂货收起来。

一般过去时:
We packed away all the camping gear after our trip. = 旅行结束后，我们把所有的露营装备都收了起来。

现在完成时:
I have packed away all the summer clothes. = 我已经把所有的夏装收起来了。

过去完成时:

We had packed away the tent before the rain started. = 下雨之前，我们已经把帐篷收好了。

将来时:
