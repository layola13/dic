glancing:/ˈɡlɑːnsɪŋ/| v.|扫视，一瞥|To look quickly at something.  It implies a brief and incomplete view.|例句：She glanced at the clock. 她看了一眼钟。|例句：He glanced over his shoulder. 他扫了一眼肩膀后面。|近义词：peeking,glimpsing,scanning|反义词：staring, scrutinizing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He glances at his phone every few minutes. = 他每隔几分钟就看一眼手机。
She glances through the newspaper before breakfast. = 她早餐前浏览一下报纸。


一般过去时:
I glanced at the menu and ordered the steak. = 我看了一眼菜单，点了牛排。
He glanced back to see if anyone was following him. = 他回头看是否有人跟踪他。


现在完成时:
She has glanced at the report, but hasn't read it thoroughly. = 她已经看过报告了，但还没仔细读。
I have glanced through the document several times. = 我已经浏览过这份文件好几次了。


过去完成时:
He had glanced at the letter before he threw it away. = 他在扔掉信之前已经看过信了。
Having glanced at the map, she knew where to go. = 扫了一眼地图后，她就知道了该去哪里。


将来时:
I will glance at the email later. = 我稍后会看一眼邮件。
She will glance through the book before buying it. = 她会在买书之前浏览一下这本书。


虚拟语气:
If I had glanced at the map, I wouldn't have gotten lost. = 如果我当时看了一眼地图，就不会迷路了。
I wish I had glanced at the price tag before buying it. = 我真希望我在买之前看了一眼价格标签。


被动语态:
The picture was glanced at by the visitors. = 游客们看了一眼那张照片。(此用法不常见)
The document was only glanced at, not read in detail. = 文件只是被浏览了一下，并没有仔细阅读。(此用法不常见)


疑问句:
Did you glance at the newspaper this morning? = 你今天早上看报纸了吗？
Has he glanced at the report yet? = 他看过报告了吗？


否定句:
I didn't glance at the letter. = 我没有看那封信。
She didn't glance at me when I spoke to her. = 我跟她说话时，她没有看我。


PHRASE->
glance at = 扫视
glance over = 浏览
glance through = 快速查看
take a glance at = 看一眼
at a glance = 一眼就能看出
