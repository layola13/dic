regimentals:/ˌredʒɪˈmentlz/| n.|军服，制服|The clothing worn by members of a regiment, typically a distinctive uniform.  It often includes specific items like jackets, trousers, and headgear. |例句：The soldiers wore their regimentals with pride.  士兵们自豪地穿着他们的军服。|例句：The new regimentals were unveiled at the parade. 新军服在阅兵式上首次亮相。|近义词：uniform, military attire, dress uniform|反义词：civilian clothes
