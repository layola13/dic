pit-a-pats: /pɪt ə pæts/ | v. | 轻轻拍打；怦怦乱跳 |  To strike or pat lightly and repeatedly; to beat rapidly, as the heart.  |例句：The kitten pit-a-patted against my leg. 小猫轻轻地拍打着我的腿。

The rain pit-a-patted against the windowpane. 雨点轻轻地打在窗玻璃上。


近义词：pat, tap, flutter | 反义词：None (the antonym depends on the context,  e.g.,  a strong blow for "pit-a-patting" as a striking action)


词源:  The word is an onomatopoeia, meaning it imitates the sound it describes.  "Pit-a-pat" suggests the soft, repeated sound of light taps or a rapid heartbeat.
