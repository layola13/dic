heuristic:/hjʊˈrɪstɪk/| adj., n.|启发式的；经验法则；试探法| relating to or involving the use of a heuristic.  A heuristic is a method that is not guaranteed to find the optimal solution but is sufficient for the immediate goals.  It's often used when an exhaustive search is impractical. |例句：The scientist used a heuristic approach to solve the complex problem.  这位科学家采用了一种启发式方法来解决这个复杂的问题。  Heuristic programming is often used in artificial intelligence.  启发式编程常用于人工智能。|近义词：rule of thumb, guideline, shortcut, approximation|反义词：optimal, exhaustive, precise
