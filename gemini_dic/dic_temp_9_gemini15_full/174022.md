quasi-truthfully:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈtruːθfʊli/ | adv. | 几乎真实地；似是而非地 |  Approximating truth; partly or seemingly true, but not entirely accurate or honest. It suggests a statement or account that contains elements of truth but also significant omissions or distortions. |例句：He quasi-truthfully described the incident, omitting key details that would have painted a less favorable picture. 他几乎真实地描述了事件，省略了一些关键细节，这些细节本来会描绘出一幅不太有利的画面。


例句：She answered the question quasi-truthfully, evading the most difficult parts. 她似是而非地回答了这个问题，避开了最困难的部分。| 近义词：partially truthfully, seemingly truthfully, approximately truthfully | 反义词：completely truthfully, entirely honestly, frankly, openly


词性:副词
