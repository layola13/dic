estafet:/ɛsˈtæfɪt/| n.|接力赛；接力赛跑；接力赛跑选手|A relay race, in which participants run a certain distance before handing off a baton to the next runner.  It can also refer to a system of relaying messages or information.|例句：The team won the 4x100 meter estafet.  这个队赢得了4x100米接力赛。|例句：The news was passed along by estafet.  消息是通过接力方式传递的。|近义词：relay race, relay, baton pass|反义词：individual race, solo race|名词
