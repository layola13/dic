helicon:/ˈhɛlɪkɒn/|/ˈhɛlɪkɑːn/| n. |赫利孔山；(诗歌)灵感女神缪斯居住的山；(尤指)螺旋形乐器|A mountain in Boeotia, Greece, sacred to the Muses.  The name is also used figuratively to refer to a source of inspiration, especially in poetry, and sometimes to a helical musical instrument.  |例句：The poet found his helicon in the quiet solitude of the countryside.  诗人在宁静的乡村找到了他的灵感源泉。

例句：The band played a lively tune on their helicon. 乐队用他们的螺旋形乐器演奏了一首欢快的曲子。


近义词：inspiration, muse, source


反义词：block, impediment


词性：名词
