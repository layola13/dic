ourselves:/ˌaʊərˈsɛlvz/ | pron. | 我们自己 | Reflexive pronoun referring to the subject of the verb. It indicates that the subject performs the action upon itself. | We hurt ourselves playing football.  我们踢足球时伤到了自己。|  They prepared the meal themselves. 他们自己准备了这顿饭。| themselves, each other | others, them


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We praise ourselves for our achievements. = 我们为自己的成就而赞扬自己。
They often blame themselves for their mistakes. = 他们经常为自己的错误责备自己。


一般过去时:
We taught ourselves how to code. = 我们自学了如何编程。
They dressed themselves quickly. = 他们迅速地穿好了衣服。


现在完成时:
We have surprised ourselves with our progress. = 我们对自己的进步感到惊讶。
They have convinced themselves that they are right. = 他们已经说服自己他们是正确的。


过去完成时:
We had already prepared ourselves for the worst. = 我们已经做好了最坏的打算。
They had exhausted themselves before the race even began. = 比赛还没开始，他们就已经筋疲力尽了。


将来时:
We will reward ourselves with a vacation. = 我们将用假期来奖励自己。
They will motivate themselves to finish the project. = 他们将激励自己完成项目。


虚拟语气:
If we were to help ourselves, we would be more efficient. = 如果我们自己动手，我们会更高效。
I wish I had prepared myself better for the exam. = 我真希望我为考试做了更好的准备。


被动语态:
(Ourselves cannot be used in the passive voice.)  (反身代词不能用于被动语态)


疑问句:
(Ourselves is rarely used in questions.) (反身代词很少用于疑问句)


否定句:
We did not hurt ourselves during the trip. = 我们在旅途中没有受伤。
They didn't blame themselves for the failure. = 他们没有为失败责备自己。
