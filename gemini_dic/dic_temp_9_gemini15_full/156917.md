pile:/paɪl/| n. vt.|堆；摞；大量；绒毛|a collection of things placed one on top of another|例句：There's a pile of books on the table. 桌子上有一堆书。|例句：He piled the wood high. 他把木头堆得很高。|近义词：heap, stack, mass|反义词：scatter, disperse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He piles the papers neatly. = 他整齐地堆放着文件。
She piles her clothes on the chair. = 她把衣服堆在椅子上。

一般过去时:
They piled the sandbags against the wall. = 他们把沙袋堆在墙边。
He piled the dishes in the sink. = 他把盘子堆在水槽里。

现在完成时:
I have piled all the laundry. = 我已经把所有的衣服都叠好了。
She has piled up a lot of work. = 她积压了很多工作。

过去完成时:
They had piled up a huge debt before they went bankrupt. = 他们破产前已经积累了巨额债务。
He had piled the boxes in the corner before the movers arrived. = 在搬运工到来之前，他已经把箱子堆放在角落里了。

将来时:
We will pile the leaves into the compost bin. = 我们将把树叶堆进堆肥箱。
They will pile the books on the shelf. = 他们会把书堆在书架上。

虚拟语气:
If he piled the books more carefully, they wouldn't fall. = 如果他更仔细地堆放书籍，它们就不会掉下来。
I wish I had piled the wood higher; it's damp now. = 我真希望我把木头堆得更高一些；现在它潮湿了。


被动语态:
The books were piled neatly on the shelf. = 书本整齐地堆放在书架上。
The sandbags were piled against the wall to protect it. = 沙袋堆在墙边以保护它。


疑问句:
Where did they pile the garbage? = 他们把垃圾堆在哪里了？
Have you piled all the clothes yet? = 你把所有的衣服都叠好了吗？


否定句:
He didn't pile the wood properly. = 他没有正确地堆放木头。
They didn't pile the boxes carefully, so some fell over. = 他们没有仔细地堆放箱子，所以一些箱子倒了。


PHRASE->
pile up: 堆积
pile on:  大量增加
pile high: 堆得很高
a pile of: 一堆
on the pile: 在堆上
