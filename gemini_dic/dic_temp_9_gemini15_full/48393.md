dedesignated:/ˌdiːˈdɛzɪɡneɪtɪd/ | v. |重新指定；取消指定；撤销指定 |To designate again; to remove the designation of something.  The term often implies a change or reversal of a previous designation. |例句：The area was dedesignated as a wildlife sanctuary after years of neglect.  多年疏于管理后，该区域的野生动物保护区称号被取消了。|例句：The government dedesignated the road as a highway, allowing for increased residential development. 政府取消了该道路的高速公路指定，允许增加住宅开发。|近义词：re-assign, revoke, rescind, cancel |反义词：designate, appoint, assign
