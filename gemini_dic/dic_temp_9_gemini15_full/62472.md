endoskeleton:/ˌɛndoʊˈskɛlətn/| n. |内骨骼|An internal skeleton, such as that found in vertebrates, composed of bone or cartilage, providing support and protection for the body.  It contrasts with an exoskeleton, which is an external skeleton.|例句：Humans possess an endoskeleton made of bones. 人类拥有由骨骼构成的内骨骼。|例句：The endoskeleton provides structural support and protection for internal organs. 内骨骼为内脏器官提供结构支撑和保护。|近义词：internal skeleton|反义词：exoskeleton
