non-reimbursed:/ˌnɒnˌriːɪmˈbɜːrst/| adj. |未报销的；未偿还的 |Not compensated for; not paid back.  This refers to expenses or costs that were incurred but not subsequently covered or refunded by a company, organization, or insurance provider. |例句：The non-reimbursed travel expenses put a strain on his personal finances.  未报销的差旅费给他个人财务带来了压力。


The non-reimbursed medical bills left the family deeply in debt. 未报销的医疗账单使这个家庭深陷债务。|近义词：uncompensated, unpaid, unrecovered |反义词：reimbursed, compensated, repaid
