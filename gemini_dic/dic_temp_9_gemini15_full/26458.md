byte:/baɪt/| n. |字节|A byte is a unit of digital information that consists of eight bits.  It is the fundamental unit of data storage in most computer systems.  A byte can represent a single character in many character encoding systems. |例句：One byte can represent a single character. 一个字节可以表示单个字符。

例句：The file size is 10 kilobytes. 文件大小是10千字节。


近义词：octet (less common)


反义词：无
