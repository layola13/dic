/ɛmˈpiːviː/ | n. |MPV（多用途汽车）|A Multi-Purpose Vehicle (MPV) is a type of car designed to carry a large number of passengers, often with versatile seating arrangements.  They are typically larger than station wagons but smaller than minivans.|Example:  Our family bought a new MPV to accommodate all seven of us comfortably.  我们的家庭买了一辆新的MPV，以便我们七个人都能舒适地乘坐。|近义词：minivan, people carrier|反义词：sports car, roadster|名词
