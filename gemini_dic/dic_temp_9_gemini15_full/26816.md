cages:/keɪdʒɪz/| n.|笼子，鸟笼|A cage is a structure, typically made of bars or wire mesh, used to confine animals or birds.|例句：The birds were singing happily in their cages. 鸟儿们快乐地唱着歌，在它们的笼子里。|例句：He kept his pet hamster in a small cage. 他把他的宠物仓鼠养在一个小笼子里。|近义词：enclosure, coop, pen|反义词：freedom, liberty


USAGE->

The birds sing in their cages every morning. = 鸟儿每天早上都在笼子里唱歌。
He keeps his pet parrot in a large cage. = 他把他的宠物鹦鹉养在一个大笼子里。
