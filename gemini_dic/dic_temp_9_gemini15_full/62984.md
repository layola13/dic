enologists:/ˌiːnɒˈlɒdʒɪsts/|/n.|酿酒师；葡萄酒专家|Experts in the science and technology of winemaking, encompassing all aspects from grape growing to the finished product. They analyze grapes, monitor fermentation, and assess the quality of wine.|例句：Enologists carefully monitor the fermentation process to ensure the wine's quality.  酿酒师仔细监控发酵过程以确保葡萄酒的质量。|例句：Many renowned enologists contribute to the prestigious wine competition. 许多著名的酿酒师都参与了这个著名的葡萄酒大赛。|近义词：winemaker, viticulturist|反义词：无
