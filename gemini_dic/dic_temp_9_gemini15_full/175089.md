radius: /ˈreɪdiəs/ | n. | 半径，半径范围，范围 | The radius of a circle is the distance from the center to any point on the circumference.  半径是圆心到圆周上任意一点的距离。The area of influence of a powerful nation often extends far beyond its territorial radius.  一个强大的国家的势力范围通常远远超出其领土范围。| diameter, range |  diameter (反义词不直接对应，但直径是半径的两倍)
