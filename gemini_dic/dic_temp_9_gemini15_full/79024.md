giv:/ɡɪv/ | vt. |给予，赠送，提供 | To give something to someone means to transfer possession or control of it to them.  It can also mean to provide or supply something. | He gave me a book. 他给了我一本。|She gave a speech. 她做了一个演讲。|近义词：present, offer, donate |反义词：take, receive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I give him money every week. = 我每周给他钱。
They give presentations at the conference. = 他们在会议上做演示。

一般过去时:
She gave him a gift for his birthday. = 她送给他一件生日礼物。
He gave a detailed explanation. = 他做了详细的解释。

现在完成时:
I have given him all the information. = 我已经给他所有信息了。
They have given many donations to charity. = 他们已经向慈善机构捐赠了很多。

过去完成时:
She had given him a warning before the incident. = 事件发生前她已经警告过他了。
They had given their best effort. = 他们已经尽力了。

将来时:
I will give you a call later. = 我稍后会给你打电话。
He will give a lecture tomorrow. = 他明天会做一个讲座。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would give it to you. = 如果我有钱，我会给你。
I wish I could give you more help. = 我希望我能给你更多帮助。

被动语态:
The prize was given to the best student. = 奖品颁给了最好的学生。
The information was given to the press. = 信息被提供给了媒体。

疑问句:
What did you give him? = 你给了他什么？
Will you give me a hand? = 你会帮我一把吗？

否定句:
I didn't give him my address. = 我没有给他我的地址。
They didn't give a clear answer. = 他们没有给出明确的答案。


PHRASE->

give away = 赠送；泄露
