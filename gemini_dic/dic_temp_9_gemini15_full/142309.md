orthodontist:/ˌɔːrθəˈdɒntɪst/| n. |正畸医生|A dentist specializing in the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of dental and facial irregularities.  Orthodontists use braces, retainers, and other appliances to correct misaligned teeth and jaws.|例句：My orthodontist recommended I get braces to correct my overbite. 我的正畸医生建议我戴牙套矫正我的深覆合。|近义词：dental specialist,  orthodontic specialist|反义词：N/A
