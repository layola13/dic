hominy:/ˈhɑːmini/| n.|玉米糁；浸泡去皮的玉米粒|Dried corn kernels that have been treated with lye (or another alkali) to remove the hull and germ.  Hominy is a staple food in many cultures, particularly in the southern United States and Mexico. It can be used in various dishes, including stews, grits, and side dishes.|例句：She made a delicious hominy stew for dinner. 她做了美味的玉米糁炖菜当晚餐。

例句：We enjoyed a hearty breakfast of hominy grits. 我们享用了一顿丰盛的玉米糁粥早餐。

近义词：cornmeal (in some contexts), grits

反义词：None directly applicable, as it refers to a specific processed form of corn.
