reverberations: /ˌrɛvərˈbeɪʃənz/ | n. | 余波；回声；反响 | The continued effects of an event, especially a negative one, or the sound that is repeated after a sound is made.  |例句：The reverberations of the scandal continue to affect the company's reputation.  丑闻的余波仍在影响着公司的声誉。


例句：The explosion sent reverberations through the building. 爆炸的震动传遍了整栋建筑物。


近义词：aftershocks, repercussions, echoes, ramifications


反义词：silence, stillness


词性：名词
