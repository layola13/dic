It often refers to a spiritual or moral state of purity. |Example: The monk dedicated his life to the nondefilement of his mind and spirit.  这位僧侣毕生致力于保持身心纯洁。 The nondefilement of the sacred site was paramount to the community.  对这个圣地的纯洁至关重要。 |近义词：purity, cleanliness, sanctity, incorruptibility |反义词：defilement, corruption, pollution, contamination |名词
