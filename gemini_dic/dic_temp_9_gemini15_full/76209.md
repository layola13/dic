fustiness:/ˈfʌstɪnəs/| n. |霉臭；陈腐；过时 |A stale, musty smell or atmosphere;  a lack of freshness or originality.  The fustiness of the old house was immediately apparent.  |The old book smelled of fustiness.|近义词：mustiness, staleness, moldiness,  datedness |反义词：freshness, modernity, newness


USAGE->
This word is a noun, and as such, does not conjugate through tenses like verbs.  The usage is primarily descriptive.


PHRASE->
a fusty smell: 霉味
a fusty room: 一间霉臭的房间
fusty ideas: 陈腐的思想
a fusty old book:一本陈旧的书
fusty traditions: 陈腐的传统
