contester:/kənˈtɛstər/| n. |抗议者；竞争者；质疑者|A person who contests something, such as a decision, election, or claim.  They may challenge the validity or fairness of something.|例句：The election results were contested by several candidates.  几名候选人对选举结果提出质疑。

例句：She is a strong contester in the debate. 她是辩论中的一位强有力的竞争者。|近义词：opponent, challenger, rival, disputant|反义词：supporter, conformist, accepter


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He contests the election results. 他对选举结果提出质疑。
They contest the validity of the contract. 他们质疑合同的有效性。


一般过去时:
She contested the will. 她对遗嘱提出异议。
He contested the referee's decision. 他对裁判的判决提出异议。


现在完成时:
They have contested the findings of the report. 他们已经对报告的调查结果提出质疑。
She has contested numerous elections. 她参加过多次选举。


过去完成时:
He had contested the decision before appealing it. 在上诉之前，他已经对该决定提出过异议。
They had contested the ownership of the land for years. 多年来，他们一直在争夺土地的所有权。


将来时:
We will contest the case in court. 我们将在法庭上对该案提出异议。
She will contest the upcoming election. 她将参加即将到来的选举。


虚拟语气:
If I were to contest the election, I would need a strong campaign team. 如果我要参加选举，我需要一个强大的竞选团队。
I wish I had contested the decision earlier. 我希望我早点对这个决定提出异议。


被动语态:
The election results were contested. 选举结果受到了质疑。
The validity of the contract is being contested. 合同的有效性正在受到质疑。


疑问句:
Will you contest the charges? 你会对指控提出异议吗？
Who is contesting the championship? 谁在争夺冠军？


否定句:
He did not contest the findings. 他没有对调查结果提出异议。
They will not contest the decision. 他们不会对这个决定提出异议。
