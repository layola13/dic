flouters:/ˈflaʊtərz/ | n. |蔑视者，违反者 |Those who openly disregard rules or laws.  People who flout the law often face consequences. |例句：The flouters of the traffic laws were fined heavily.  交通法规的违反者被处以重罚。

例句：Several flouters of the school dress code were sent to the principal's office.  几个违反学校着装规范的人被送到了校长办公室。


近义词：violators, transgressors, rebels, lawbreakers |反义词：conformists, law-abiders


词源：源自动词"flout"，意为“公开蔑视；嘲笑”，其词源追溯到古法语“flouter”，意为“嘲笑，戏弄”。
