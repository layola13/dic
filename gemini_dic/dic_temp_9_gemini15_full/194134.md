self-infatuated: /ˌsɛlfɪnˈfæʃuˌeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 自恋的，自我陶醉的 | Excessively vain and absorbed in oneself and one's appearance.  Characterized by or showing an excessive admiration of oneself. | 例句：He's become rather self-infatuated since his success. 他自从成功以后就变得相当自恋了。

He delivered a self-infatuated speech filled with boasts about his achievements. 他发表了一篇自吹自擂、自我陶醉的演讲，充满了对自身成就的夸耀。


近义词：narcissistic, vain, conceited, egotistical, self-centered, self-absorbed


反义词：humble, modest, unassuming, self-effacing


词性：形容词
