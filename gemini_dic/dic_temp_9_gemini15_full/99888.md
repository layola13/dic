internalize:/ɪnˈtɜːrnəlaɪz/| vt.|内化，吸收；使成为自己的一部分 |To make (something, such as a belief, idea, or value) part of one's own being or identity.|The students internalized the lesson on empathy. 学生们内化了关于同理心的课程。|近义词：assimilate, integrate, absorb|反义词：externalize, reject


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He internalizes his emotions. 他内化了他的情绪。
She internalizes the company's values. 她将公司的价值观内化了。


一般过去时:
They internalized the new rules quickly. 他们很快地内化了新规章。
He internalized the trauma of his childhood. 他内化了他童年的创伤。


现在完成时:
I have internalized many new concepts this semester. 这学期我已经内化了许多新的概念。
She has internalized the importance of teamwork. 她已经内化了团队合作的重要性。


过去完成时:
They had internalized the company culture before the merger. 在合并之前，他们已经内化了公司文化。
He had internalized the lessons from his mistakes. 他已经从错误中吸取教训并内化了。


将来时:
She will internalize the information gradually. 她将逐渐内化这些信息。
We will internalize this new technology over time. 我们将随着时间的推移内化这项新技术。


虚拟语气:
If I could internalize this knowledge, I would feel much more confident. 如果我能内化这些知识，我会更有信心。
It would be beneficial if he internalized the feedback he received. 如果他能内化他收到的反馈，将会有益。


被动语态:
The information was internalized by the students. 信息被学生们内化了。
The values of the organization are internalized by its employees. 组织的价值观被员工们内化了。


疑问句:
Has he internalized the lesson? 他内化了这节课了吗？
Do you think she will internalize the criticism? 你认为她会内化批评吗？


否定句:
He did not internalize the feedback. 他没有内化反馈。
She failed to internalize the importance of punctuality. 她没能内化准时的重要性。
