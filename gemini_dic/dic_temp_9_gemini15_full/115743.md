marigolds:/ˈmærɪɡoʊldz/ | n. |金盏花 |Marigolds are annual flowering plants in the genus Tagetes, with brightly colored flowers ranging from yellow to orange and red.  They are popular garden plants known for their vibrant colors and pungent odor.  |例句：The garden was ablaze with marigolds. 花园里金盏花盛开。

例句：She planted rows of marigolds along the fence.  她在栅栏边种了一排排的金盏花。


近义词：pot marigold, calendula (though botanically distinct)


反义词：无


词性：名词
