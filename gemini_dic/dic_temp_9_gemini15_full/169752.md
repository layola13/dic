prorate:/ˈproʊreɪt/| vt.|按比例分摊；按比例计算|To divide or assess proportionally.  It often involves adjusting a payment or charge to reflect a partial period of service or usage.|例句：The rent was prorated for the remaining month.  房租按剩余月份比例分摊。

例句：We will prorate your final bill to reflect the time you used the service. 我们将按您使用服务的时间比例计算您的最终账单。


近义词：apportion, allocate, distribute, adjust


反义词：None directly opposite, but "to charge the full amount" could be considered a contrasting action.


词性:动词
