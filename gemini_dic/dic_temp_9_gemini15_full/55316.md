dissolvers:/dɪˈsɒlvərz/| n. |溶解器；解散者|A device or substance that dissolves something.  Dissolvers are used in various industrial processes to break down materials.  They can also refer to people or groups that cause the breakdown or disintegration of something, like an organization or a social structure. |例句：The chemical plant uses powerful dissolvers to break down waste materials.  这家化工厂使用强大的溶解器来分解废料。
