glim:/ɡlɪm/| v.| 闪光；微光 | To shine faintly or intermittently; to gleam faintly.  |例句：The candle glimmed in the darkness.  蜡烛在黑暗中闪烁。  The distant lights glimmered on the horizon. 远处灯光在视野中闪烁。|近义词：gleam, glimmer, shimmer|反义词：blaze, flare

PHRASE->
