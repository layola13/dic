mellifluent:/ˈmɛlɪfluːənt/ | adj. |流畅的；甜美的；娓娓动听的 |Sweetly and smoothly flowing; honeyed.  The word suggests a pleasing quality, often associated with speech or writing that is both smooth and agreeable to the ear. |例句：Her mellifluent voice captivated the audience.  她的甜美流畅的声音吸引了观众。

例句：His mellifluent prose was a delight to read. 他的流畅优美的散文读起来是一种享受。


近义词：fluent, smooth, flowing, honeyed, dulcet

反义词：harsh, rough, jarring, strident, halting


词源：
The word "mellifluent" is derived from the Latin words "mel" (honey) and "fluens" (flowing).  The combination creates a sense of something flowing smoothly and sweetly, like honey.  It is a relatively uncommon word, but its components are readily understood when broken down.
