multichannel:/ˌmʌltiˈtʃænəl/| adj.|多通道的，多频道的|Having or using more than one channel.  For example, a multichannel marketing campaign uses multiple channels to reach its target audience.|例句：Their marketing strategy employs a multichannel approach, utilizing social media, email, and print advertising. 他们的营销策略采用多渠道方法，利用社交媒体、电子邮件和印刷广告。|例句：This sound system is multichannel, offering a truly immersive audio experience. 这个音响系统是多声道系统，提供真正身临其境的音频体验。|近义词：polychannel, multi-channel|反义词：single-channel, monochannel
