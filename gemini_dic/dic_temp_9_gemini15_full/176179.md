re-addresses:/ˌriːˈædrɛsɪz/ | vt. |重新处理；再次讨论；重新考虑；重新寄送 | To address something again, often with a different approach or to correct a previous mistake.  It implies a revisiting of a previous issue or problem. |例句：The committee re-addressed the budget concerns after receiving new data.  委员会在收到新的数据后重新处理了预算问题。

例句：The speaker re-addressed the controversial topic, clarifying his previous statements. 演讲者重新讨论了有争议的话题，澄清了他之前的陈述。


近义词：reconsider, review, revisit, revise

反义词：ignore, neglect


词性：动词


USAGE->

The company re-addresses customer complaints promptly. = 公司迅速处理客户投诉。
