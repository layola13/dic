rubbernecked:/ˈrʌbərˌnekt/| v.| 扭头看，探头观看 | To turn one's head and stare, often at something unusual or an accident.  | 例句：Everyone rubbernecked as the ambulance sped past. 大家都扭头看着救护车飞驰而过。

例句：  Drivers rubbernecked at the accident on the highway. 司机们都扭头看着高速公路上的事故。


近义词： gawked, stared, craned (one's neck)


反义词： ignored, overlooked


词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rubbernecks at every accident he sees. 他看到事故就扭头去看。
They often rubberneck while driving. 他们开车时经常探头观看。


一般过去时:
She rubbernecked at the celebrity's car. 她扭头看着那个名人的车。
We rubbernecked at the burning building. 我们扭头看着燃烧的建筑物。


现在完成时:
I've rubbernecked so much today, my neck is stiff. 我今天看了太多东西，脖子都僵硬了。
They have rubbernecked at every parade this year. 今年他们看了每一场游行的热闹。


过去完成时:
He had rubbernecked at the crash before the police arrived. 在警察到达之前，他已经扭头去看那场车祸了。
She had rubbernecked at the scene for a good five minutes. 她已经看了那个场面足足五分钟了。


将来时:
I will rubberneck at the fireworks display tonight. 今晚我将要观看烟火表演。
They will probably rubberneck at the parade. 他们可能会观看游行。


虚拟语气:
If I were there, I would have rubbernecked, too. 如果我在那里，我也会扭头去看。
I wish I hadn't rubbernecked; I saw something horrible. 我真希望我没有扭头去看；我看到了一些可怕的东西。


被动语态:
The accident was rubbernecked by many onlookers. 那起事故被许多旁观者围观。
The parade was rubbernecked by thousands of people. 游行被成千上万的人围观。


疑问句:
Did you rubberneck at the accident? 你有去看那起事故吗？
Will they rubberneck at the concert? 他们会去看音乐会吗？


否定句:
I didn't rubberneck; I kept my eyes on the road. 我没有扭头去看；我一直看着路。
They didn't rubberneck at the commotion. 他们没有去看那场骚动。
