showcased: /ˈʃoʊkeɪst/ | vt. | 展示，展出，突显 | To exhibit or display prominently; to present or demonstrate something in a way that makes it attractive or impressive.  | 例句：The museum showcased its new collection of ancient artifacts.  博物馆展出了它新收藏的古代文物。

例句：The company showcased its innovative new product at the trade show. 公司在贸易展会上展示了其创新型的新产品。


近义词：displayed, exhibited, presented, highlighted, featured | 反义词：concealed, hidden, obscured


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The museum showcases its collection regularly. = 博物馆定期展出它的收藏。
The artist showcases his paintings in various galleries. =  这位艺术家在不同的画廊展出他的画作。


一般过去时:
They showcased their new technology last year. = 他们去年展示了他们的新技术。
The company showcased its products at the convention. = 公司在大会上展示了它的产品。


现在完成时:
The gallery has showcased many famous artists. = 这个画廊已经展出了许多著名的艺术家。
The company has showcased its commitment to sustainability. = 这家公司已经展示了它对可持续发展的承诺。


过去完成时:
They had showcased the prototype before the official launch. = 他们在正式发布之前已经展示了原型。
The artist had showcased his work in numerous exhibitions. = 这名艺术家曾在许多展览会上展出过他的作品。


将来时:
We will showcase our latest designs at the fashion week. = 我们将在时装周上展示我们最新的设计。
The company will showcase its new software next month. = 公司下个月将展示其新的软件。


虚拟语气:
If they showcased their product more effectively, they would sell more. = 如果他们更有效地展示他们的产品，他们就能卖出更多。
I wish they had showcased the product's features better. = 我希望他们能更好地展示产品的特点。


被动语态:
The new product was showcased to potential investors. = 新产品向潜在投资者进行了展示。
The artwork was showcased in a prominent position in the gallery. = 这幅艺术品在画廊的显眼位置展出。


疑问句:

否定句:

They chose not to showcase the controversial artwork. = 他们选择不展出有争议的艺术品。
