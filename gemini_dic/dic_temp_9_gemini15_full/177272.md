reassign:/ˌriːˈaɪn/| vt.|重新分配；重新指派|To assign (something) again or to someone else.  This typically involves changing the allocation or responsibility of a task, role, or resource.|例句：The manager decided to reassign the project to a more experienced team.  经理决定将项目重新分配给一个更有经验的团队。

例句：They had to reassign the seats after several passengers missed their flight. 由于几名乘客错过了航班，他们不得不重新分配座位。


近义词：reallocate, redistribute, reassign, delegate

反义词：retain, keep, withhold

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reassigns employees regularly. = 公司定期重新分配员工。
He reassigns tasks based on employee skills. = 他根据员工技能重新分配任务。


一般过去时:
She reassigned the case to a different detective. = 她将案件重新分配给了另一位侦探。
They reassigned the funds to a more urgent project. = 他们将资金重新分配给了更紧急的项目。


现在完成时:
Management has reassigned several key personnel. = 管理层已经重新分配了几名关键人员。
The project manager has reassigned all the tasks. = 项目经理已经重新分配了所有任务。


过去完成时:
They had reassigned the resources before the crisis hit. = 危机发生前，他们已经重新分配了资源。
The team leader had reassigned the roles before the meeting. = 团队领导在会议前已经重新分配了角色。


将来时:
They will reassign the staff to different departments. = 他们将把员工重新分配到不同的部门。
The company will reassign the budget next quarter. = 公司将在下个季度重新分配预算。


虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we would reassign the tasks. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们会重新分配任务。
I wish they would reassign the project to someone more competent. = 我希望他们能把项目重新分配给更有能力的人。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
