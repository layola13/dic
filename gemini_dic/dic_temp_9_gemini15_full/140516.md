oleander:/ˌoʊliˈændər/| n. |夹竹桃|An evergreen shrub or small tree with fragrant, typically pink or white flowers, native to the Mediterranean region and widely cultivated as an ornamental plant.  It is highly poisonous. |例句：The oleander bushes bloomed profusely in the summer heat.  盛夏的酷暑中，夹竹桃灌木花开繁茂。|例句：  We had to be careful not to let the children touch the oleander plants. 我们必须小心，不要让孩子们触摸夹竹桃植物。|近义词：nerium (botanical name)|反义词：无|名词
