reverer:/ˈrɛvərər/| v. |尊敬者，敬畏者 |To regard with respect, reverence, or admiration.  To hold in high esteem.|例句：Many people reverer the Dalai Lama for his peaceful teachings. 许多人尊敬达赖喇嘛及其和平教诲。|例句：She reverers her grandmother's wisdom and kindness. 她敬畏祖母的智慧和善良。|近义词：adore, venerate, honor, respect |反义词：despise, scorn, disdain |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reverer their ancestors. = 他们尊敬他们的祖先。
We reverer the wisdom of the ages. = 我们敬畏时间的智慧。


一般过去时:
She reverered her teacher for his dedication. = 她尊敬她的老师的奉献精神。
He reverered the power of nature. = 他敬畏大自然的力量。


现在完成时:
I have reverered this artist's work for many years. = 多年来我一直尊敬这位艺术家的作品。
We have reverered the sanctity of life. = 我们一直敬畏生命的圣洁。


过去完成时:
They had reverered the old traditions for generations. = 几代人以来，他们一直敬畏古老的传统。
She had reverered her father's memory since his passing. = 自父亲去世以来，她一直尊敬父亲的记忆。


将来时:

We will reverer his contributions to science. = 我们将敬畏他对科学的贡献。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would reverer such a great mind. = 如果我是你，我会尊敬这样一位伟大的思想家。
I wish I could reverer the wisdom of the ancients. = 我希望我能敬畏古代人的智慧。
