manicuring:/ˈmænɪkjʊərɪŋ/| v.|修指甲；美甲|The act of caring for and beautifying fingernails and toenails.  It involves cleaning, shaping, polishing, and sometimes decorating the nails. |例句：She enjoys manicuring her own nails at home. 她喜欢在家自己修指甲。|例句：The salon offers a wide range of manicuring services. 这个沙龙提供各种美甲服务。|近义词：nail care, nail treatment|反义词：neglecting nails
