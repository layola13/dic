relooking:/ˌriːˈlʊkɪŋ/| n. |重新审视，重新考虑|The act of looking again at something, often with a fresh perspective or new information.  It implies a more thorough and critical examination than a simple second glance.  |例句：The company is undergoing a relooking of its marketing strategy after poor sales figures. 公司在销售业绩不佳后正在重新审视其营销策略。


例句：After the accident, the police conducted a thorough relooking of the security footage. 事故发生后，警方对安全录像进行了彻底的重新审查。

近义词：review, reconsideration, re-evaluation, reassessment

反义词：ignoring, overlooking, disregarding

词性：名词
