impertinent:/ɪmˈpɜːtɪnənt/| adj.|不恰当的；无礼的；放肆的|Rude and disrespectful; improperly forward or bold.|His impertinent remarks offended everyone present. 他无礼的言辞冒犯了在场的所有人。|近义词：insolent, cheeky, audacious, presumptuous|反义词：respectful, polite, courteous, deferential

USAGE->
一般现在时:
His comments are often impertinent.  他的评论常常不恰当。
She is impertinent to her elders. 她对长辈无礼。

一般过去时:
He was impertinent to the teacher. 他对老师无礼。
Her question was impertinent and unnecessary. 她的问题不恰当且不必要。

现在完成时:
He has been impertinent on several occasions. 他多次表现得无礼。
She has acted impertinently throughout the meeting. 在整个会议期间，她的行为都很放肆。

过去完成时:
He had been impertinent to his boss many times before he was fired. 他被解雇之前多次对老板无礼。
She had already been impertinent once that day. 那天她已经无礼过一次了。

将来时:
He will be impertinent again if you don't correct him. 如果你不纠正他，他还会再次无礼。
She will likely be impertinent to the interviewer. 她可能会对面试官无礼。

虚拟语气:
If he were less impertinent, people would like him more. 如果他不那么无礼，人们会更喜欢他。
I wish he weren't so impertinent to his parents. 我希望他对父母不要那么无礼。

被动语态:
His behavior was considered impertinent by many. 许多人认为他的行为不恰当。
Her questions were deemed impertinent and irrelevant. 她的问题被认为是不恰当且不相关的。

疑问句:
Wasn't his remark impertinent? 他的话难道不无礼吗？
Is it impertinent to ask such a question? 问这样的问题是不是不恰当？

否定句:
His behavior was not impertinent, just a little clumsy. 他的行为并非无礼，只是有点笨拙。
Her actions were not impertinent; she was simply nervous. 她的行为并非无礼；她只是紧张。
