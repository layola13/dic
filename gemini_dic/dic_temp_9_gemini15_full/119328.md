microbiome:/ˈmaɪkroʊbaɪoʊm/| n. |微生物组|The microbiome refers to the collective genomes of the microorganisms in a specific environment, such as the human gut.  It encompasses bacteria, archaea, fungi, protists, and viruses.  Studying the microbiome helps us understand its impact on health and disease. |The human gut microbiome plays a crucial role in digestion and immunity.  人类肠道微生物组在消化和免疫中起着至关重要的作用。| microbiota, microbial community | sterile environment |名词
