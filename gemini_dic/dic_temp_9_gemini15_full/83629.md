haemodialyser:/ˌhiːməʊˈdaɪəlɪzə(r)/| n. |血液透析器|A medical device used to remove waste products and excess fluid from the blood of patients with kidney failure.  It works by using a semipermeable membrane to filter the blood. |The patient is currently undergoing treatment with a haemodialyser.|近义词：dialyzer, artificial kidney|反义词:None
