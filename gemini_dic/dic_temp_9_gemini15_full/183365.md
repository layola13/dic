reproducing:/ˌriːprəˈduːsɪŋ/| v.|复制；繁殖；再现|The act of making a copy or duplicate of something; the process by which organisms create offspring.|例句：The artist is reproducing the painting.  艺术家正在复制这幅画。|例句：The rabbits are reproducing rapidly. 兔子繁殖得很快。|近义词：copying, replicating, breeding, propagating|反义词：destroying, eliminating, annihilating|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reproducing the experiment. = 他们在重复实验。
Plants are reproducing through seeds. = 植物通过种子繁殖。

一般过去时:
She reproduced the image using a copier. = 她用复印机复制了图像。
The dinosaurs reproduced sexually. = 恐龙进行有性繁殖。

现在完成时:
Scientists have reproduced the results of the study. = 科学家们已经复制了该研究的结果。
The species has reproduced successfully in captivity. = 该物种在人工饲养下成功繁殖。

过去完成时:
They had reproduced the painting before the exhibition. = 他们在展览前已经复制了这幅画。
The birds had reproduced several times that year. = 这些鸟那一年已经繁殖了好几次。

将来时:
He will reproduce the data in his presentation. = 他将在他的演示文稿中复制数据。
We will reproduce the plants by cuttings. = 我们将通过扦插繁殖这些植物。

虚拟语气:
If I had a better printer, I could reproduce the image more clearly. = 如果我有一个更好的打印机，我可以更清晰地复制图像。
If they had reproduced more quickly, they would have been more successful. = 如果它们繁殖得更快，它们就会更成功。

被动语态:
The painting was reproduced in several different sizes. = 这幅画被复制成几种不同的尺寸。
The experiment is being reproduced by another research team. = 该实验正在由另一个研究小组复制。

疑问句:
How do these animals reproduce? = 这些动物是如何繁殖的？
Can this software reproduce the sound accurately? = 这个软件能准确地复制声音吗？

否定句:
He couldn't reproduce the results of the experiment. = 他无法复制实验的结果。
The company failed to reproduce the success of its previous product. = 公司未能复制其先前产品的成功。


PHRASE->

reproducing asexually = 无性繁殖
faithfully reproducing = 忠实地复制
reproducing sounds = 复制声音
