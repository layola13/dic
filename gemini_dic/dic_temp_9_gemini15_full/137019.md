nonstampable: /ˌnɒnˈstæmpəbl/ | adj. | 不可盖章的，无法盖章的 |  Unable to be stamped; not suitable for stamping.  This word describes something that cannot be stamped, either because of its physical properties or because it's not allowed to be stamped. | Example:  The fragile package was nonstampable due to its delicate contents.  易碎的包裹由于其易碎的内容而无法盖章。


The irregular shape made the envelope nonstampable.  不规则的形状使信封无法盖章。


近义词: unstamped, unmarkable (in the context of stamping) | 反义词: stampable
