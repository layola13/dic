intentionless:/ɪnˈtɛnʃən(ə)lɪs/| adj.|无意的；非故意的；无心之举的 |Lacking intention or purpose; unintentional.  It describes actions or behaviors performed without a conscious plan or deliberate aim. |例句：He bumped into her, the collision was entirely intentionless. 他撞到了她，这完全是无意的。

例句：Her seemingly careless remarks were actually intentionless, not malicious. 她看似漫不经心的言论实际上是无心的，并非恶意。


近义词：unintentional, accidental, involuntary, unwitting, casual


反义词：intentional, deliberate, purposeful, premeditated, willful


词性：形容词
