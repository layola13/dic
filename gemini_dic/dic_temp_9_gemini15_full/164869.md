prefreezes:/ˌpriːˈfriːzɪz/| v.|预先冷冻，提前冷冻 |To freeze something before it is used or needed. This is typically done to preserve food or other perishable items, or to prepare something for a later process.|例句：We prefreeze the vegetables before packaging them. 我们在包装蔬菜之前先冷冻它们。 The company prefreezes the fish to maintain freshness during transport. 公司预先冷冻鱼类以保持运输过程中的新鲜度。|近义词：freeze beforehand, pre-chill |反义词：thaw, defrost|动词
