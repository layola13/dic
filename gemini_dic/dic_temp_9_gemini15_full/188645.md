rummages:/ˈrʌmɪdʒɪz/| v.|翻找；乱翻；搜寻|To search unsystematically and untidily for something.|例句：She rummaged in her handbag for her keys. 她翻找她的手提包寻找钥匙。|例句：He rummaged through the drawers looking for his passport. 他翻箱倒柜地找他的护照。|近义词：search, hunt, ferret, delve|反义词：organize, tidy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rummages through his pockets every morning. 他每天早上都在翻找他的口袋。
She rummages in her closet for a dress. 她在衣橱里翻找一件衣服。

一般过去时:
I rummaged through the attic yesterday. 我昨天翻找了阁楼。
He rummaged in his bag for his wallet. 他翻找他的包寻找钱包。

现在完成时:
She has rummaged through all the boxes. 她已经翻找了所有箱子。
They have rummaged everywhere but still can't find it. 他们到处都翻找了，但仍然找不到。

过去完成时:
He had rummaged through the entire house before calling the police. 他在报警之前已经翻遍了整个房子。
She had rummaged through her memories before writing the book.  她在写书之前已经翻阅了她的回忆。

将来时:
I will rummage through the old photos tomorrow. 我明天会翻看旧照片。
They will rummage in the basement for the old tools.  他们会在地下室翻找旧工具。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rummage through the library archives. 如果我有更多时间，我会翻阅图书馆的档案。
I wish I could rummage through my grandmother's attic. 我希望我能翻找我祖母的阁楼。

被动语态:
The drawers were rummaged through by the thief. 抽屉被小偷翻找过。
The attic is being rummaged through by the family. 阁楼正在被家人翻找。

疑问句:
Are you rummaging in the cupboard again? 你又在翻找橱柜了吗？
Was he rummaging through the trash? 他在翻找垃圾吗？

否定句:
I didn't rummage through your belongings. 我没有翻找你的东西。
She doesn't usually rummage in other people's things. 她通常不会翻找别人的东西。
