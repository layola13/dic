pteropod:/ˈtɛrəpɒd/|/ˈtɛrəpɑːd/| n. |翼足类动物|A marine gastropod mollusk with wing-like parapodia used for swimming.  They are a type of plankton and an important part of the marine food web.|Example:  Many species of pteropods are threatened by ocean acidification.|近义词：sea butterfly (informal)|反义词：N/A
