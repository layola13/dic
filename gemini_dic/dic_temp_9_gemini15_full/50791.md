descendent:/dɪˈsɛndənt/| n. |后代，子孙|A descendant is a person, plant, or animal that is descended from a particular ancestor.  It refers to someone who comes from a particular ancestor or ancestors. |例句：She is a direct descendant of the royal family.  她是皇室的直系后裔。|例句：Many modern breeds of dog are descendants of wolves. 许多现代犬种都是狼的后代。|近义词：offspring, progeny, heir|反义词：ancestor, forefather
