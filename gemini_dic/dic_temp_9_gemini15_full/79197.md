glanced:/ɡlændst/| v.|瞥了一眼，匆匆一看|To look quickly at something.  |例句：I glanced at the clock and saw it was nearly midnight. 我瞥了一眼钟，发现快到午夜了。
例句：She glanced through the newspaper before going to work.  她上班前匆匆浏览了一下报纸。|近义词：peek, glimpse, peep|反义词：stare, gaze


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He glances at his phone every few minutes. 他每隔几分钟就看一眼他的手机。
They glance at each other shyly. 他们害羞地互相看了一眼。


一般过去时:
I glanced at the menu before ordering. 我点菜前看了一眼菜单。
She glanced nervously at the door. 她紧张地瞥了一眼门。


现在完成时:
He has glanced at the report but hasn't read it properly. 他已经看过报告了，但没有仔细阅读。
She has glanced through several books today. 她今天浏览了好几本书。


过去完成时:
I had glanced at the email before deleting it. 我删除邮件之前已经看过它了。
They had glanced at the map before setting off. 他们出发前已经看过地图了。


将来时:
I will glance at the document when I have time. 我有时间的时候会看一下这份文件。
She will glance at the price tag before buying it. 她买之前会看一下价签。


虚拟语气:
If I had glanced at the instructions, I wouldn't have made that mistake. 如果我看一下说明书，就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I had glanced at the weather forecast before leaving. 我真希望我离开前看过天气预报。


被动语态:
The document was glanced at by the manager. 文件被经理看了一眼。
The picture was only glanced at briefly. 图片只被匆匆看了一眼。


疑问句:
Did you glance at the newspaper this morning? 你今天早上看报纸了吗？
Has he ever glanced at your work? 他看过你的作品吗？


否定句:
I didn't glance at the letter. 我没有看那封信。
He didn't glance at her. 他没有看她。


PHRASE->

at a glance: 一眼就能看出
