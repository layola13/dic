shelved:/ʃɛlvd/| v. |搁置；把…放在架子上 |To put something on a shelf; to postpone or abandon a plan or project. |例句：The project was shelved due to lack of funding.  该项目因缺乏资金而被搁置。

例句：He shelved his books neatly on the bookcase. 他把书整齐地放在书架上。


近义词：postpone, defer, table, suspend, archive |反义词：resume, revive, continue


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shelves his books every night. = 他每天晚上都把书放回书架上。
They shelve the new proposals for further review. = 他们将新的提案搁置以供进一步审查。

一般过去时:
She shelved the idea after careful consideration. = 她经过仔细考虑后放弃了这个想法。
They shelved the project until next quarter. = 他们将项目推迟到下一季度。


现在完成时:
We have shelved the plan indefinitely. = 我们已无限期搁置该计划。
They have shelved several projects this year due to budget constraints. = 他们今年由于预算限制而搁置了几个项目。


过去完成时:
They had shelved the project before the merger was announced. = 在合并宣布之前，他们就已经搁置了该项目。
She had shelved her writing ambitions for many years. = 她多年来一直搁置了自己的写作抱负。


将来时:
We will shelve the discussion until after the meeting. = 我们将把讨论推迟到会议之后。
They will shelve the controversial bill. = 他们将搁置有争议的法案。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we wouldn't have shelved the project. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们就不会搁置这个项目了。
I wish they hadn't shelved the research. = 我真希望他们没有搁置这项研究。


被动语态:
The proposal was shelved due to insufficient data. = 由于数据不足，该提案被搁置。
The plans were shelved indefinitely. = 计划被无限期搁置。


疑问句:
Was the project shelved? = 项目被搁置了吗？
Have they shelved the new initiative? = 他们是否搁置了这项新举措？


否定句:
They didn't shelve the plan. = 他们没有搁置该计划。
We will not shelve this important issue. = 我们不会搁置这个问题。


PHRASE->
shelve a problem = 搁置问题
shelve a decision = 搁置决定
shelve a plan = 搁置计划
shelve a project = 搁置项目
shelve an idea = 搁置一个想法
