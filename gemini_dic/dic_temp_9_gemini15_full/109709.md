leveed:/lɛviːd/| n.|筑堤的；堤防；|An embankment built to prevent the overflow of a river.|The Mississippi River levees protect the surrounding land from floods. 密西西比河的堤防保护着周围的土地免受洪水侵害。|dam, dike, embankment|ravine, breach|名词
