interosseous:/ˌɪntərˈɒsiəs/| adj. |骨间的；骨间隙的| Situated or occurring between bones.  The term refers to structures, such as muscles, ligaments, or nerves, that are located between bones, typically within the spaces between metacarpals or metatarsals. |Example: The interosseous membrane connects the radius and ulna. 骨间膜连接桡骨和尺骨。|近义词：interbony|反义词：extraosseous
