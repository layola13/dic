look-daggers:/ˈlʊkˌdæɡərz/| v. |怒目而视，恶狠狠地瞪|To look at someone with angry or hostile eyes, as if threatening them.  |例句：He looked daggers at his opponent after the unfair tackle. 他在对手犯规后怒目而视。|例句：She looked daggers at the man who cut in front of her in line. 她怒视插队的那个人。|近义词：glare at, scowl at, stare down |反义词：smile at, look kindly at
