metric:/ˈmɛtrɪk/| n. adj.|度量衡；公制的；指标的 |A system of measurement based on the meter, liter, and gram; something used as a standard of measurement.  |例句：The metric system is used in most countries.  = 大多数国家使用公制。

The company uses customer satisfaction as a key metric for success. = 公司将客户满意度作为成功的关键指标。|近义词：system, standard, measure, indicator |反义词：imperial (system)
