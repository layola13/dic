seld:/sɛld/| adv.|很少，难得|Rarely; infrequently; on few occasions.|  He seldom goes to the cinema. 他很少去看电影。|She seldom visits her parents. 她很少去看望父母。|often, frequently|always, frequently|副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He seldom visits his grandparents. = 他很少去看望他的祖父母。
They seldom eat out. = 他们很少外出吃饭。


一般过去时:
She seldom watched television in the past. = 她过去很少看电视。
We seldom traveled abroad when we were young. = 我们年轻的时候很少出国旅行。


现在完成时:
I have seldom seen such a beautiful sunset. = 我很少看到如此美丽的日落。
They have seldom been to the city center. = 他们很少去市中心。


过去完成时:
He had seldom felt so happy before. = 他以前很少感到如此快乐。
She had seldom experienced such a difficult situation. = 她以前很少遇到如此困难的局面。


将来时:
I will seldom have time for hobbies next year. = 明年我很少有时间从事爱好。
They will seldom attend parties in the future. = 他们将来很少参加聚会。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would seldom miss my friends' gatherings. = 如果我有更多的时间，我很少会错过朋友的聚会。
I wish I seldom had to work overtime. = 我希望我很少需要加班。


被动语态:
The museum is seldom visited by tourists. = 这个博物馆很少被游客参观。
This type of car is seldom seen on the road. = 这种类型的汽车很少在路上看到。


疑问句:
Do you seldom go to the library? = 你很少去图书馆吗？
Does he seldom communicate with his colleagues? = 他很少和同事沟通吗？


否定句:
He doesn't seldom go to the gym; he goes quite often. = 他并非很少去健身房；他经常去。
She doesn't seldom call her mother; she calls regularly. = 她并非很少打电话给她的母亲；她定期打电话。
