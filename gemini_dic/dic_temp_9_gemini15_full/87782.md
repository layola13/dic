The word evokes a sense of coldness, dormancy, and the stillness often associated with the winter season. | Example: The hibernal landscape was covered in a thick blanket of snow. 冬天的景色被厚厚的积雪覆盖着。 The bear's hibernal sleep lasted for months.  熊的冬眠持续了几个月。|近义词：wintery, wintry, winter, dormant |反义词：estival, summery
