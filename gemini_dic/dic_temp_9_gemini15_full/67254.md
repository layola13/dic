expurgate:/ˈɛkspərɡeɪt/| vt.|净化；删去（令人反感的部分）|to remove matter considered objectionable or unsuitable from a book or other text.|The publisher expurgated the novel before releasing it to a wider audience. 出版商在向更广泛的读者发布小说之前对其进行了删节。|近义词：censor, purge, sanitize|反义词：include, add


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They expurgate offensive content from the textbook. 他们从教科书中删除了令人反感的內容。
The editor expurgates any inappropriate language. 编辑删去了任何不合适的语言。


一般过去时:
The librarian expurgated the books before they were put on the shelves. 图书管理员在将书籍上架之前对其进行了删节。
He expurgated the manuscript to make it suitable for publication. 他删改了手稿使其适合出版。


现在完成时:
They have expurgated several passages from the original text. 他们已经从原文中删去了几段。
The committee has expurgated the report of any biased information. 委员会已经从报告中删除了任何有偏见的资料。


过去完成时:
The author had expurgated the controversial chapter before submitting the manuscript. 作者在提交手稿之前已经删去了有争议的那一章。
The censors had expurgated the play before its premiere. 审查员在该剧首映之前对其进行了删改。


将来时:
The publisher will expurgate any sexually explicit scenes. 出版商将删除任何性爱场面。
The school will expurgate any inappropriate material from the curriculum. 学校将从课程中删除任何不合适的材料。


虚拟语气:
If they expurgated the offensive language, the book would be more acceptable. 如果他们删去攻击性语言，这本书会更易于接受。
I wish they had expurgated the violent scenes from the movie. 我希望他们能从电影中删去暴力场面。


被动语态:
The offensive passages were expurgated from the book. 书中令人反感的段落被删除了。
The report is being expurgated to remove sensitive information. 报告正在被删改以移除敏感信息。


疑问句:
Will the editor expurgate the inappropriate content? 编辑会删去不合适的內容吗？
Have they expurgated all the offensive jokes from the comedy show? 他们是否已经从喜剧节目中删除了所有令人反感的笑话？


否定句:
The author did not expurgate any of the controversial scenes. 作者没有删去任何有争议的场景。
They will not expurgate the historical context, even if it is uncomfortable. 即使令人不舒服，他们也不会删去历史背景。
