clothiers:/ˈkloʊðɪərz/ | n. | 成衣商；服装店；服装制造商 | Makers and sellers of clothing.  They design, manufacture, or retail clothes. | Example:  Many high-end clothiers are located on Fifth Avenue. 许多高端成衣商都位于第五大道。|近义词:tailors, outfitters, apparel makers|反义词:None (no direct antonym)|名词
