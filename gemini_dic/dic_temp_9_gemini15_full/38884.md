conferments:/kənˈfɜːrmənts/| n.|授予；授予仪式|The act of conferring something, especially a degree, honor, or right.  It refers to the process of officially bestowing something upon someone.|例句：The university conferments were held in the grand hall. 大学的学位授予仪式在大会堂举行。
The conferments of honorary doctorates were a highlight of the ceremony. 荣誉博士学位的授予是典礼的亮点。|近义词：bestowals, grants, awards|反义词：revocations, withdrawals|名词
