sabean:/ˈseɪbiən/| n. |萨拜人；萨巴人；| relating to the Sabeans, an ancient South Arabian people.  The Sabeans were known for their advanced civilization, particularly their expertise in astronomy and trade. Their kingdom flourished for centuries, leaving behind significant archaeological remains and historical records.|The Sabean inscriptions are a valuable source of information about ancient South Arabian history.|近义词:South Arabian|反义词:无
