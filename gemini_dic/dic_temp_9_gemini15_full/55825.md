divided: /dɪˈvaɪdəd/ | adj. v. | 分开的；有分歧的；（使）分裂 |

英文解析:
As an adjective, "divided" means separated into parts or sections, or showing disagreement or conflict. As a verb, it means to separate something into parts or to cause a group to disagree.

例句:
The cake was divided into eight slices. = 蛋糕被切成了八块。

The country is deeply divided on the issue of immigration. = 这个国家在移民问题上存在严重分歧。


近义词:
split, separated, fractured, partitioned (adj.)
split, separate, break up, fracture (v.)

反义词:
united, unified, joined, whole (adj.)
unite, unify, join, combine (v.)


词性:
Adjective, Verb
