lacewing:/ˈleɪsˌwɪŋ/| n. |草蛉|A lacewing is any insect of the order Neuroptera, characterized by delicate, lacy wings.  They are often green or brown and are beneficial insects because their larvae are voracious predators of aphids and other small insects. |例句：The gardener was pleased to see several lacewings in her garden, as they help control the aphid population. 花园主人很高兴在她花园里看到几只草蛉，因为它们有助于控制蚜虫数量。
例句：Many species of lacewing are nocturnal, emerging at dusk to hunt. 许多种类的草蛉是夜行动物，黄昏时出来觅食。|近义词：neuropteran|反义词:  (无明确反义词)|词性:n.
