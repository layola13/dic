pretimely:/ˈpriːtaɪmli/| adj.|及时的，适时的|timely, opportune, well-timed|The word "pretimely" does not exist in standard English dictionaries.  It appears to be a neologism or a misspelling of "timely."  The intended meaning is likely "occurring at a favorable or opportune time,"  "done or occurring at the right time," or "in good time."|例句：The timely intervention prevented a disaster.  及时的干预避免了一场灾难。|近义词：timely, opportune, well-timed, prompt, punctual|反义词：late, untimely, belated, delayed
