provider:/prəˈvaɪdər/| n. |提供者，供应商，服务商 |A provider is a person or organization that supplies something, such as goods or services.  |Example:  Our internet provider is experiencing technical difficulties. 我们的网络服务商正在经历技术故障。|近义词：supplier, vendor, source |反义词：consumer, recipient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company provides excellent customer service. = 这家公司提供优质的客户服务。
He provides for his family. = 他养家糊口。


一般过去时:
She provided a delicious meal for the guests. = 她为客人准备了一顿美味的饭菜。
The government provided aid to the disaster victims. = 政府向灾民提供了援助。


现在完成时:
They have provided all the necessary documents. = 他们已经提供了所有必要的文书。
The organization has provided support to many charities. = 该组织已经为许多慈善机构提供了支持。


过去完成时:
He had provided all the materials before the construction started. = 建设开始之前，他已经准备好了所有材料。
She had provided a detailed explanation before the meeting. = 会议之前，她已经做了详细的解释。


将来时:
They will provide more information next week. = 他们下周将提供更多信息。
The company will provide training to its employees. = 公司将为其员工提供培训。


虚拟语气:
If they provided better service, they would have more customers. = 如果他们提供更好的服务，他们就会有更多的客户。
I wish they provided a refund for the defective product. = 我希望他们能为有缺陷的产品退款。


被动语态:
The information was provided by the government. = 信息由政府提供。
Food and water are being provided to the refugees. = 难民正在得到食物和水的供应。


疑问句:
Who provided the information? = 谁提供了信息？
What services does your provider offer? = 你的服务商提供什么服务？


否定句:
The company did not provide enough support. = 公司没有提供足够的支援。
They haven't provided a satisfactory explanation. = 他们没有给出令人满意的解释。
