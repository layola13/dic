/ˈmɛtsə/| n. |半份；少量；中音|A mezza is a half-portion of food or drink, or a moderate amount of something.  It can also refer to a mezzo-soprano voice in music.| 例句：I'll have a mezza pizza, please.  = 请给我一份半份披萨。

She added a mezza teaspoon of salt. = 她加了半茶匙的盐。


近义词：half-portion, small amount, moderate quantity| 反义词：full portion, large amount, whole| 名词
