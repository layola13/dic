parameter:/pəˈræmɪtər/ | n. | 参数，变量，因素 | A parameter is a measurable factor forming one of a set that defines a system or sets the conditions of its operation.  It can also refer to a limit or boundary. | Example:  The key parameters of the experiment were temperature and pressure.  这个实验的关键参数是温度和压力。

The model's parameters need to be carefully calibrated. 模型的参数需要仔细校准。|近义词：variable, factor, argument, setting | 反义词：constant, invariant


parameter:/pəˈræmɪtər/ | n. | 参数，变量，因素 | A parameter is a measurable factor forming one of a set that defines a system or sets the conditions of its operation.  It can also refer to a limit or boundary. | Example:  The key parameters of the experiment were temperature and pressure.  这个实验的关键参数是温度和压力。

The model's parameters need to be carefully calibrated. 模型的参数需要仔细校准。|近义词：variable, factor, argument, setting | 反义词：constant, invariant

词源:  From Late Latin *parameter, from Greek parámetros "something measured beside," from pará "beside" + metron "measure."
