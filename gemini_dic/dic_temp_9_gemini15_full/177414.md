rebaking:/ˌriːˈbeɪkɪŋ/| n.|再次烘烤；重新烘焙|The act of baking something again, often to improve its texture or flavor, or to finish the cooking process.|例句：The cake needed rebaking after it was slightly undercooked.  再次烘烤蛋糕，因为它有点没烤熟。


The bread required rebaking to achieve a crispier crust.  面包需要重新烘焙才能得到更脆的表皮。|近义词：reheating, second baking|反义词：first baking, initial baking
