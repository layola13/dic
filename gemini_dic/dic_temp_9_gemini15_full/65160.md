ethnicity: /ɛθˈnɪsəti/ | n. | 种族；民族；族裔 | The quality or state of belonging to a particular ethnic group.  Ethnicity refers to shared cultural characteristics, such as language, ancestry, practices, and beliefs, that may not necessarily align with national or political boundaries.  | 例句：His ethnicity is Chinese. = 他的种族是中国人。

His ethnicity is a significant part of his identity. = 他的种族是其身份认同的重要组成部分。


近义词：race, nationality (in some contexts), heritage, background | 反义词：None (it's not a word that has a direct opposite) | 词性：名词
