querulist:/ˈkwɛrəlɪst/| n. |抱怨者，爱发牢骚的人 | A querulist is a person who habitually complains or grumbles. They often express dissatisfaction and are prone to finding fault.  |Example:  He was a querulist, always finding something to complain about. 他是个爱发牢骚的人，总是能找到一些东西抱怨。|近义词：complainer, grumbler, whiner, fault-finder |反义词：optimist, complaisant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a querulist. = 他是个爱抱怨的人。
She constantly querulously complains about the weather. = 她总是爱抱怨天气。


一般过去时:
He was a querulist in his youth. = 他年轻时是个爱抱怨的人。
