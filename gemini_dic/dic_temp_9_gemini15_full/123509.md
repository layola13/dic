monothetic:/ˌmɒnəˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 单类型的；单特征的 |  Relating to or denoting a classification system in which a class is defined by the presence of a single attribute.  A monothetic classification is one where membership in a group is determined by the presence of a single defining characteristic.  This contrasts with polythetic classification, where membership is determined by the presence of multiple characteristics. | Example:  "A monothetic classification of plants might be based solely on the presence of flowers." = “植物的单类型分类可能仅仅基于花的 presence。” | 近义词: single-characteristic, uni-characteristic | 反义词: polythetic
