non-routine:/ˌnɒnruːˈtiːn/| adj.|非例行的，非常规的|not a regular part of a job, process, or system; unusual or unexpected.|例句：The project required a non-routine solution.  这个项目需要一个非常规的解决方案。

例句：Dealing with customer complaints is sometimes a non-routine task for sales staff. 处理客户投诉有时是销售人员的非常规任务。


近义词：irregular, unusual, exceptional, extraordinary|反义词：routine, regular, ordinary, standard


词源：non- (prefix meaning "not") + routine (adjective meaning "regular, ordinary").  The word "routine" itself originates from the French word "routine," meaning "a regular course or track".
