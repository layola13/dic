methylated:/ˈmɛθɪleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 甲基化的 | Treated with or containing a methyl group.  This process often alters the properties of the molecule it's applied to, such as its activity or charge.  | Example:  The methylated DNA showed reduced gene expression.  甲基化的DNA显示基因表达减少。  |近义词：methylated  |反义词：demethylated
