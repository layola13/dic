re-sifting:/ˌriːˈsɪftɪŋ/ | v. | 再筛，重新过筛 | The act of sifting something again; to sift again.  | Example: After the initial sifting, the baker decided to re-sift the flour to remove any remaining lumps.  重新过筛面粉以去除任何剩余的结块。 | 近义词：re-screening, re-bolting | 反义词：None (反义词需根据语境而定，例如：粗筛)

They re-sift the sand to remove impurities. = 他们重新过筛沙子以去除杂质。
