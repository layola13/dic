metrograph:/ˈmɛtrəɡræf/| n. |地铁图，地铁线路图 |A metrograph is a diagram showing the routes and stations of a metro or subway system.  It is typically used by passengers to navigate the system. |Example:  The metrograph clearly indicated the transfer station between Line 1 and Line 3. 地铁线路图清楚地标明了1号线和3号线之间的换乘站。|近义词： subway map, transit map |反义词：无
