词源:
The word "gut-wrenching" is a compound word.  "Gut" refers to the internal organs, particularly the intestines, implying a deep, visceral feeling. "Wrenching" means twisting or pulling violently, suggesting a feeling of intense physical and emotional pain.  The combination creates a powerful image of intense emotional distress.
