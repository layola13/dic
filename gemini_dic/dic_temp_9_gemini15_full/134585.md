nonloyal:/ˌnɒnˈlɔɪəl/| adj. |不忠诚的，不效忠的 |Not loyal; faithless.  This word describes someone or something that lacks loyalty or faithfulness.  It implies a betrayal of trust or commitment. |例句：His nonloyal behavior led to his dismissal from the team. 他的不忠诚行为导致他被逐出球队。

例句：The nonloyal general switched sides during the war.  这位不忠诚的将军在战争期间倒戈。


近义词：disloyal, unfaithful, treacherous, faithless

反义词：loyal, faithful, true, devoted

词性:形容词
