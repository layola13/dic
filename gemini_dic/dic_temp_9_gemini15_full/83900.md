half-asleep: /ˌhɑːf əˈsliːp/ | adj. | 半睡半醒的，昏昏欲睡的 |  Partially asleep; in a state between being awake and being fully asleep.  |例句：I was half-asleep when the phone rang.  电话响的时候我正半睡半醒。

例句：The lecture was so boring that I felt half-asleep. 讲座太无聊了，我感觉昏昏欲睡。


近义词：dozy, drowsy, sleepy, semiconscious

反义词：wide awake, fully awake

词性：形容词
