dime:/daɪm/| n. |十分硬币；少量钱|A dime is a United States coin worth ten cents.  It's a small, silver-colored coin.  |例句：I only have a dime in my pocket. 我口袋里只有一枚十分硬币。

例句：He paid a dime for the candy. 他花了一毛钱买了糖果。


近义词：ten cents, small amount |反义词：large sum, fortune


词源:  The word "dime" comes from the French word "disme," meaning "tenth," referring to its value as one-tenth of a dollar.  This ultimately derives from the Latin word "decima," also meaning "tenth."
