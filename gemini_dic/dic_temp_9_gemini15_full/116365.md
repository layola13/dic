mastications:/ˌmæstɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. |咀嚼；嚼碎的动作 |The act of chewing food.  It involves the mechanical breakdown of food in the mouth using the teeth and jaw muscles, preparing it for swallowing and digestion. | Example:  Thorough mastication aids digestion.  彻底咀嚼有助于消化。 The baby’s mastications were slow and clumsy. 婴儿的咀嚼动作缓慢而笨拙。|近义词：chewing, munching |反义词：swallowing (as a contrasting action)
