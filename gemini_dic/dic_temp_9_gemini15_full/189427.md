sailboat:/ˈseɪlboʊt/ | n. |帆船 | A boat propelled by sails.  It is typically used for recreation or racing. | 例句：The sailboat glided gracefully across the calm water. 这艘帆船优雅地滑过平静的水面。
The children were thrilled to see the brightly colored sailboats. 孩子们看到色彩鲜艳的帆船很兴奋。|近义词：sailing vessel, yacht (often larger and more luxurious)|反义词：motorboat, powerboat
