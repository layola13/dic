disincentive:/ˌdɪsɪnˈsɛntɪv/| n. |阻碍；制约；反激励；不利因素|Something that discourages someone from doing something.|The high cost of fuel is a disincentive to long journeys.|近义词：deterrent, handicap, obstacle|反义词：incentive, motivation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
High taxes act as a disincentive to investment. = 高额税收对投资起到了阻碍作用。
The threat of punishment serves as a disincentive to crime. = 惩罚的威胁可以阻止犯罪。


一般过去时:
The lack of job security was a disincentive to many potential employees. = 就业保障的缺乏对许多潜在员工来说是一种阻碍。
The poor working conditions acted as a disincentive to productivity. = 恶劣的工作条件降低了生产力。


现在完成时:
The new regulations have created a disincentive to small business growth. = 新规对小型企业的发展造成了阻碍。
Years of government mismanagement have acted as a disincentive to foreign investment. = 多年的政府管理不善对外国投资起到了阻碍作用。


过去完成时:
The previous administration's policies had created a disincentive to innovation. = 上届政府的政策已经对创新造成了阻碍。
The high interest rates had acted as a disincentive to borrowing. = 高利率已经对借贷产生了阻碍作用。


将来时:
Increased regulation will create a disincentive to entrepreneurship. = 加强的监管将会对创业产生阻碍。
The proposed tax changes will act as a disincentive to saving. = 拟议的税收变化将会对储蓄产生阻碍作用。


虚拟语气:
If there were fewer disincentives, more people would start their own businesses. = 如果阻碍因素较少，更多的人会自己创业。
If the government removed this disincentive, the economy would improve. = 如果政府取消了这个阻碍因素，经济将会改善。


被动语态:
Many potential investors were discouraged by the disincentives. = 许多潜在投资者被这些阻碍因素劝退了。
Growth was hampered by the disincentives created by the new policy. = 新政策造成的阻碍因素阻碍了增长。


疑问句:
What are the main disincentives to investing in this sector? = 投资这个领域的主要障碍是什么？
Are there any disincentives to taking early retirement? = 提早退休有什么不利因素吗？


否定句:
There were no disincentives to participation in the project. = 参与这个项目没有任何阻碍因素。
The government tried to remove disincentives to home ownership. = 政府试图消除购房的阻碍因素。
