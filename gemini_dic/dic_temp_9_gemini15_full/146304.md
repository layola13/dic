overfearfulness: /ˌoʊvərˈfɪərflənəs/ | n. |过度恐惧；忧虑过度|Excessive fear or anxiety;  an extreme degree of fearfulness.  It implies a level of fear that is disproportionate to the actual threat or danger. |例句：Her overfearfulness prevented her from enjoying the trip.  她的过度恐惧让她无法享受这次旅行。

例句：His overfearfulness about public speaking led him to avoid career opportunities. 他对公开演讲的过度恐惧导致他避免了职业机会。


近义词：apprehension, anxiety, nervousness, phobia, dread

反义词：bravery, courage, calmness, composure


词性：名词
