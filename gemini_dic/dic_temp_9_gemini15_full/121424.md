misinformative:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrmətɪv/| adj.| 误导性的，提供错误信息的 | Giving incorrect information; misleading.  |The article was misinformative and contained several factual errors. 这篇文章误导性很强，包含几个事实错误。|近义词：deceptive, misleading, inaccurate |反义词：informative, accurate, truthful

词源:
mis- (错误地) + informative (提供信息的)  The prefix "mis-" indicates a wrong or bad action.  "Informative" comes from "inform" (to give information) + "-ative" (having the nature of).
