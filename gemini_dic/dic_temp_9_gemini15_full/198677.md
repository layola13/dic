shyest: /ˈʃaɪɪst/ | adj. | 最害羞的 |  The superlative form of "shy," describing someone or something exhibiting the highest degree of shyness. It indicates the utmost reluctance to be with or be seen by other people. | 例句：She is the shyest person I know.  = 她是所有我认识的人中最害羞的一个。  The shyest child in the class hid behind his mother. = 班上最害羞的孩子躲在他母亲的身后。 | 近义词：most timid, most reserved, most bashful | 反义词：most outgoing, most extroverted, most bold
