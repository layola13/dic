larceny:/ˈlɑːrsəni/| n.|盗窃罪；盗窃；偷窃|The unlawful taking, carrying, leading, or riding away of another person's personal property with the intent to permanently deprive the owner of it.|例句：He was arrested for grand larceny. 他因重窃罪被捕。  例句：The larceny of the jewels was never solved. 珠宝失窃案从未侦破。|近义词：theft, robbery, pilfering|反义词：donation, gift
