perishless:/ˈpɛrɪʃləs/| adj.|不朽的；永恒的；不会腐烂的 |  Free from decay or destruction; imperishable; enduring.  The word suggests a quality of lastingness beyond the typical lifespan of mortal things, often implying a divine or exceptional nature. | Example: The Pharaoh hoped for a perishless legacy, his name and deeds etched in stone for eternity.  法老希望留下不朽的遗产，他的名字和事迹永远铭刻在石头上。  |近义词：immortal, eternal, imperishable, everlasting |反义词：mortal, perishable, ephemeral, transient |词性:形容词
