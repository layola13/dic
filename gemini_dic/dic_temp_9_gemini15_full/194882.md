semi-divine:/ˌsɛmiˈdaɪvaɪn/| adj.| 半神性的，似神的 | Possessing qualities or attributes of both a god and a mortal; partly divine.  |例句：The hero, though mortal, possessed semi-divine powers, enabling him to perform incredible feats.  这位英雄虽然凡人，却拥有半神的力量，能够完成不可思议的壮举。

例句：The ancient myths often portrayed kings as semi-divine figures, bridging the gap between humanity and the gods. 古代神话经常将国王描绘成半神的人物，连接着人类和神之间的差距。


近义词：godlike,supernatural,partly divine,deified

Etymology:

The word "semi-divine" is a compound word formed from:

* **semi-**:  A prefix derived from Latin "semi," meaning "half," "partly," or "partially."
* **divine**:  An adjective derived from Latin "divinus," meaning "divine," "godlike," or "of or relating to a god."
