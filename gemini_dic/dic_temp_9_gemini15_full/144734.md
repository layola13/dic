over-and-over:/ˌoʊvər ænd ˈoʊvər/| adj. adv. |反复地，多次地 |Repeatedly; many times.  The phrase emphasizes the repetition and often implies tediousness or lack of variation. |例句：He said it over and over again until I understood. 他一遍遍地说直到我明白为止。

例句：She practiced the piano piece over and over until she got it perfect. 她反复练习这首钢琴曲直到演奏完美。


近义词：repeatedly, again and again, time and again, incessantly


反义词：once, singly, rarely


词性: 副词(主要)，形容词(较少见，用作非正式的形容词)
