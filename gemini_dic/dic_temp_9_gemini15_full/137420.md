nontextually:/ˌnɒnˈtɛkstʃuəli/ | adv. | 非文本地；非文字地 | Not in a textual or literal way; not according to the text.  It refers to something that is implied or suggested but not explicitly stated. | 例句：The meaning is nontextually implied in the author's use of symbolism. 作者运用象征手法间接地表达了其含义。


例句：The illustration, while not explicitly stated in the text, nontextually conveys the character's inner turmoil.  插图虽然没有在文本中明确说明，但却非文本地传达了人物内心的挣扎。


近义词：implicitly, indirectly, figuratively


反义词：textually, literally, explicitly


词性：副词
