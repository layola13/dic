shakingly: /ˈʃeɪkɪŋli/ | adv. |颤抖地，摇晃地 |  Describes an action or state performed in a trembling or shaky manner, often due to fear, weakness, or cold.  | Example: He spoke shakingly, his voice barely a whisper. 他颤抖着说话，声音几乎是耳语。 | 近义词:tremulously, quiveringly, shakily | 反义词:steadily, calmly, firmly |  副词
