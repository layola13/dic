corridors:/ˈkɒrɪdɔːrz/ /ˈkɔːrɪdɔːrz/| n.|走廊，通道|A corridor is a long passage in a building or train, typically with rooms on either side.|例句：The hotel corridors were dimly lit.  酒店走廊灯光昏暗。|例句：We walked down the long corridor to the elevator. 我们沿着长长的走廊走到电梯处。|近义词：passage, hallway, aisle|反义词：room, chamber
