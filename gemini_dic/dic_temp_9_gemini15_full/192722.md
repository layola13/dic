sea-bird:/ˈsiːbɜːrd/|/ˈsiːbɝːd/| n. |海鸟|A bird that lives near or on the sea and often feeds on fish or other marine life.|例句：Gulls are a common type of sea-bird found along coastlines. 海鸥是沿海常见的一种海鸟。|例句：Many sea-birds migrate long distances to breed. 许多海鸟迁徙很长的距离去繁殖。|近义词：seabird, marine bird|反义词:land bird
