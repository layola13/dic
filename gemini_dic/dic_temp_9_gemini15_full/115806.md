mark out:/mɑːk aʊt/ | vt. | 划掉；标出；计划；使显眼；使筋疲力尽 | To draw a line through something, to indicate the boundaries of something, to plan or schedule something, to make something noticeable, to exhaust someone. | 例句：He marked out the area to be fenced. 他划出了需要围栏的区域。

例句：They marked out the playing field.  他们划出了运动场。


近义词：indicate, delineate, plan, schedule, highlight, exhaust, tire out

反义词：obscure, conceal, ignore, rest, relax

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He marks out the important points in the text. 他在文本中标出重点。
They mark out the running track every year. 他们每年都会标出跑道。


一般过去时:
She marked out the paragraphs she didn't understand. 她划掉了她不明白的段落。
We marked out a campsite for the night. 我们规划了一个夜营地。


现在完成时:
They have marked out the entire garden for planting. 他们已经规划好了整个花园的种植区域。
She has marked out the key areas for improvement. 她已经标出了需要改进的关键领域。


过去完成时:
He had marked out the route before the race began. 比赛开始前他已经规划好了路线。
They had marked out the boundaries of the property. 他们已经标明了房产的界限。


将来时:
We will mark out the new parking lot next week. 我们下周将规划新的停车场。
She will mark out the design on the fabric. 她将在布料上勾勒出设计。


虚拟语气:
If I had marked out the plan more carefully, I wouldn't have made so many mistakes. 如果我更仔细地规划了计划，我就不会犯那么多错误了。
I wish I had marked out the key points before the presentation. 我希望在演示之前我已经标出了重点。


被动语态:
The area was marked out for construction. 该区域被划定用于施工。
The route will be marked out by the volunteers. 路线将由志愿者标出。


疑问句:
Have you marked out the areas for the different activities? 你已经规划好不同活动的区域了吗？
Will the path be clearly marked out? 小路会被清晰地标示出来吗？


否定句:
He didn't mark out the important details in his report. 他没有在他的报告中标出重要的细节。
They haven't marked out the area for the new building yet. 他们还没有为新建筑划定区域。
