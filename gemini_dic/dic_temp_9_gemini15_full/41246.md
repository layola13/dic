corban:/ˈkɔːrbæn/| n. |祭品；捐献；供物|A gift or offering, especially one made to God or a religious institution.  The term often carries connotations of sacrifice or dedication.| The priest blessed the corban before placing it on the altar.  祭司在将祭品放在祭坛上之前为其祝福了。|offering, donation, sacrifice |—|名词
