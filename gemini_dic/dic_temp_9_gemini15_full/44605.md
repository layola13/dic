crowd-pulling: /ˈkraʊdˌpʊlɪŋ/ | adj. | 吸引人群的，吸引人的 |  Attractive and exciting enough to draw a large number of people.  | 例句：The concert was a crowd-pulling event, attracting thousands of fans.  这场音乐会是一场吸引人的活动，吸引了成千上万的粉丝。

例句：The new amusement park boasts many crowd-pulling rides and attractions.  新的游乐园拥有许多吸引人的游乐设施和景点。


近义词：attractive, appealing, popular, captivating, engaging


反义词：unattractive, unappealing, unpopular, repellent, boring


词性：形容词
