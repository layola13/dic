predebiting:/ˌpriːˈdɛbɪtɪŋ/| v. |预先借记；预先扣除|The act of debiting an account before a transaction occurs.  This is often used in situations where payment is guaranteed, such as with automatic bill payments or subscription services. |例句：The company practices predebiting for all monthly subscriptions. = 公司对所有月度订阅实行预先扣款。

例句：Predebiting your account ensures that your bills are paid on time. = 预先从您的账户扣款确保您的账单按时支付。


近义词：pre-authorization, advance deduction, prior deduction|反义词：post-debiting|词性:动词
