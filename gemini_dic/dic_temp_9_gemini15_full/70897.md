fir: /fɜːr/ | n. | 冷杉；枞树 | A type of evergreen tree belonging to the genus Abies, characterized by needle-like leaves and upright cones.  The wood is often used for construction and furniture. |例句：The forest was filled with towering fir trees. 这片森林里长满了高耸的冷杉。

例句：We used fir wood to build the cabin. 我们用冷杉木建造了小屋。


近义词：pine, spruce | 反义词：无


词源:  From Old English *fir, from Proto-Germanic *firhō, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰer- ("to bear, carry").  The word's original meaning likely referred to a tree that bore cones.
