skyline:/ˈskaɪlaɪn/| n.|天际线，地平线|The line where the sky seems to meet the earth.  It is often used to describe the outline of buildings or mountains against the horizon.|例句：The city skyline was breathtaking at sunset.  日落时分，城市的天际线令人叹为观止。|例句：The jagged mountain skyline was visible for miles.  连绵起伏的山峦天际线在数英里外清晰可见。|近义词：horizon, outline, profile|反义词：ground level
