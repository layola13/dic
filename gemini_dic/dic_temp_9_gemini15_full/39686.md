considerably:/kənˈsɪdərəbli/ | adv. |相当地，非常地 | To a great extent; significantly.  It implies a noticeable or substantial degree. |例句：The price of gasoline has increased considerably this year. 今年汽油价格大幅上涨。

例句：Her singing improved considerably after she took vocal lessons.  她上了声乐课后，歌声有了相当大的提高。


近义词：significantly, substantially, greatly, markedly, appreciably

反义词：slightly, marginally, insignificantly
