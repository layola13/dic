He led a harum-scarum life, flitting from one adventure to another. 他过着一种鲁莽的生活，从一个冒险跳到另一个冒险。


近义词：reckless, impetuous, irresponsible, haphazard, chaotic | 反义词：cautious, careful, organized, methodical | 词性：形容词
