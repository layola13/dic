hard-working:/ˌhɑːrdˈwɜːrkɪŋ/| adj. |勤奋的，努力工作的 |Diligent and persistent in one's work or studies.  |例句：She is a hard-working student and always gets high marks. 她是一个勤奋的学生，总是取得高分。 |例句：He's a hard-working employee and always meets deadlines. 他是一位勤奋的员工，总是按时完成工作。|近义词：diligent, industrious, assiduous, dedicated |反义词：lazy, indolent, idle, slothful
