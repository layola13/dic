floro:/ˈflɔːroʊ/| n.|佛罗拉（罗马神话中的花神）, 植物区系|Flora, in Roman mythology, is the goddess of flowers and spring.  The term also refers to the plants of a particular region, habitat, or geological period.|例句：The flora of the rainforest is incredibly diverse. 热带雨林的植物区系极其多样化。 The book detailed the flora and fauna of the Galapagos Islands. 这本书详细介绍了加拉帕戈斯群岛的动植物。|近义词：vegetation, plant life|反义词：fauna
