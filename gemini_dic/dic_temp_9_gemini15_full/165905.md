preoppressor:/ˌpriːəˈprɛsər/| n.| 预压迫者；潜在的压迫者 | A person or entity that is likely to become an oppressor in the future, or who exhibits precursory behaviors indicative of future oppression.  This term highlights the potential for oppression before it is fully realized.  It often refers to systems, ideologies, or individuals showing early signs of oppressive tendencies.| 例句：The rise of nationalist rhetoric could be seen as a sign of a preoppressor at work.  潜在的民族主义言论可以被视为预压迫者正在起作用的迹象。  |近义词：potential oppressor, incipient tyrant, would-be dictator|反义词：liberator, emancipator|名词
