frostbite:/ˈfrɒstbaɪt/| n.|冻伤|Frostbite is an injury caused by freezing of body tissues. It most commonly affects the extremities, such as fingers, toes, ears, and nose.|例句：The hiker suffered severe frostbite on his toes after spending a night lost in the blizzard.  登山者在暴风雪中迷路过夜后，脚趾严重冻伤。|例句：Exposure to extreme cold can lead to frostbite and hypothermia.  暴露在极度寒冷的环境中会导致冻伤和体温过低。|近义词：freezing, chilblain|反义词：sunburn
