cloudiness:/ˈklaʊdinəs/| n. |阴天；阴沉；浑浊|The state or quality of being cloudy; the condition of the atmosphere when it is covered with clouds.  It can also refer to a lack of clarity or transparency in something.|例句：The cloudiness prevented us from seeing the stars.  阴天让我们看不到星星。

The high cloudiness obscured the view. 高度的阴云遮挡了视线。|近义词：overcast, haziness, murkiness|反义词：clearness, transparency
