h-bombs:/ˈeɪtʃ ˌbɒmz/| n. |氢弹；氢炸弹|A hydrogen bomb, also known as a thermonuclear bomb, is a tremendously powerful explosive device that derives its destructive force from nuclear fusion reactions involving isotopes of hydrogen, primarily deuterium and tritium.|The detonation of h-bombs releases enormous amounts of energy, causing widespread destruction and radioactive fallout.|近义词：thermonuclear bomb, hydrogen bomb|反义词：conventional weapon
