pattern:/ˈpætərn/ | n. |模式；图案；样板；典范|A repeated or regular way in which something happens or is done.|The wallpaper has a floral pattern.  壁纸上有花卉图案。|The pattern of his life changed dramatically after the accident.  事故后他的生活模式发生了巨大的变化。|design, model, template, archetype|randomness, chaos|名词
