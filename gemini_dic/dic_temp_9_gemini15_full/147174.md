overlubrication:/ˌoʊvərˌluːbrɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |过度润滑|The act of lubricating something excessively, leading to potential problems.  Overlubrication can cause excessive friction, heat build-up, and contamination. |Example: Overlubrication of the engine bearings led to a build-up of oil and eventual engine failure.  过度润滑发动机轴承导致积油，最终导致发动机故障。|近义词: excessive lubrication, superfluity of lubricant |反义词: underlubrication, insufficient lubrication
