darlingly:/ˈdɑːrlɪŋli/ | adv. |可爱地，亲爱的；亲切地 | In a darling or endearing manner.  It suggests a charming and affectionate way of behaving or speaking. |例句：She smiled darlingly at the children. 她可爱地朝孩子们微笑。

She spoke to him darlingly, her voice soft and gentle. 她温柔亲切地和他说话。
