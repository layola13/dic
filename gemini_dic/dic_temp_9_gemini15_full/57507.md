dragoman:/ˈdræɡəmən/| n.|通事，译员|A dragoman is an interpreter, especially one who translates between European languages and those of the Middle East or the East.  Historically, dragomans were often employed by diplomats or merchants to facilitate communication in regions with different languages.  The term is now largely archaic, though it retains a certain romantic and historical flavor.|例句：In the 19th century,  many European travelers relied on dragomans to navigate the complexities of the Ottoman Empire. = 在19世纪，许多欧洲旅行者依靠通事来应对奥斯曼帝国的复杂情况。|近义词：interpreter, translator, guide|反义词：N/A
