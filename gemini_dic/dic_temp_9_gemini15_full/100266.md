interscene: /ˌɪntərˈsiːn/ | n. | 幕间; 场景之间 |  The events or actions occurring between scenes in a play, film, or other narrative work.  It often refers to transitional material or brief sequences that bridge the gap between larger scenes. | Example: The interscene showed the protagonist traveling to a new location.  幕间场景显示主人公前往新的地点。 | 近义词：transition, interlude, bridge | 反义词：scene, action
