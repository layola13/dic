gravedigger:/ˈɡreɪvˌdɪɡər/| n. |掘墓人，殡葬工人 | A gravedigger is a person whose job is to dig graves.  They are often associated with cemeteries and funerals. |The gravedigger worked silently and efficiently.  掘墓人默默地、高效地工作着。
The old gravedigger knew every family buried in the cemetery.  这位年长的掘墓人认识墓地里埋葬的每一个家庭。|undertaker,burial attendant |(反义词无直接对应，可考虑与“life-giver”相对，但意义相差甚远)|名词
