deglorify: /dɪˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 去掉…的光彩；贬低；使…不光彩 | To remove the glory or splendor from something; to diminish the reputation or importance of something. | 例句：The constant criticism deglorified his achievements.  不断的批评使他的成就黯然失色。

The media's sensationalism deglorified the heroic efforts of the rescue workers. 媒体的煽情报道贬低了救援人员的英勇事迹。


近义词：belittle, denigrate, disparage, diminish, discredit

反义词：glorify, exalt, praise, enhance, celebrate

词性：动词


USAGE->
