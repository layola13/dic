dispellers: /dɪˈspɛlər/ | n. |驱散者；消除者 | A person or thing that dispels something, especially something unpleasant.  |例句：The rising sun was a dispeller of the morning mist.  冉冉升起的太阳驱散了早晨的薄雾。|例句：Hope acted as a dispeller of their fears. 希望驱散了他们的恐惧。|近义词：remover, eliminator, banishers |反义词：aggravator, intensifier
