situate:/ˈsɪtʃueɪt/| vt.|使位于；使处于某种境地；安置|To place or fix in a particular position or situation.  It also means to place or establish (someone or something) in a particular place, position, or setting.|例句：The house is situated on a hill overlooking the sea. 这栋房子位于俯瞰大海的山丘上。 The company is well situated to take advantage of the new market. 这家公司的地位很好，可以利用新市场。|近义词：place, locate, position, establish|反义词：remove, displace, dislodge

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They situate the new school near the park. = 他们把新学校建在公园附近。
We situate our marketing strategy on social media. = 我们将我们的营销策略定位在社交媒体上。

一般过去时:
The explorers situated their camp near the river. = 探险家们在河边扎营。
He situated himself comfortably in the armchair. = 他舒服地坐在扶手椅上。

现在完成时:
They have situated the statue in the town square. = 他们已将雕像安放在市中心广场。
She has situated herself as a leader in the industry. = 她已确立自己在业界的领导地位。

过去完成时:
They had situated the furniture before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经摆好了家具。
Before the speech, he had situated himself at the podium. = 演讲前，他已经站在讲台上。


将来时:
We will situate the new office building downtown. = 我们将在市中心建造新的办公大楼。
They will situate the exhibit in the main hall. = 他们将在主厅展出展品。


虚拟语气:
If we situated the factory closer to the port, transportation costs would be lower. = 如果我们将工厂设在离港口更近的地方，运输成本就会降低。
I wish I had situated myself better in that negotiation. = 我真希望我在那次谈判中能更好地定位自己。


被动语态:
The building is situated on a quiet street. = 这栋建筑位于一条安静的街道上。
The problem is situated within the software's algorithm. = 问题出在软件的算法上。


疑问句:
Where is the hotel situated? = 旅馆位于何处？
How is this issue situated within the broader context? = 这个问题在更广泛的背景下是如何定位的？


否定句:
The building is not situated near the highway. = 这栋建筑不在高速公路附近。
The company is not well situated to compete in that market. = 这家公司在这个市场上竞争地位并不理想。


PHRASE->
be situated in/at/on = 位于……
well situated = 位置优越的；情况有利的
strategically situated = 战略性地位于
poorly situated = 位置不好的；情况不利的
ideally situated = 位置极佳的


词源:
源自拉丁语 *situāre ，意思是“放置，安置”。  这个词根也与situation（形势，情况）和site（地点）相关。
