expressionist:/ɪkˈspreʃənɪst/ | adj. | 表现主义的；表现派的 | Relating to or denoting a style of painting, music, literature, etc., in which the artist or writer seeks to express emotional experience rather than to depict external reality.  | Example: The painting is a prime example of expressionist art. 这幅画是表现主义艺术的典型代表。 | 近义词：expressive, emotional | 反义词：realistic, representational
