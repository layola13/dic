reboarding:/ˌriːˈbɔːrdɪŋ/| v.|重新登机，再次登机|The act of getting back on a plane, train, or other form of transportation after having disembarked.|例句：After a long delay, we were finally allowed reboarding.  长时间延误后，我们终于获准重新登机。

例句：The passengers were informed of the reboarding procedure.  乘客们被告知重新登机的程序。


近义词：reembarkation, getting back on


反义词：disembarkation, getting off


词性:动词
