It suggests a close but not exact imitation or a blend of Jacobean elements with others.  | Example: The building's ornamentation displayed a quasi-Jacobean aesthetic, incorporating some Baroque flourishes.  这座建筑的装饰展现了一种准雅各布式的审美，融合了一些巴洛克风格的装饰。|近义词:Jacobean-influenced, near-Jacobean, reminiscent of Jacobean |反义词:  modern, contemporary
