harped-on: /hɑːrpt ˈɒn/ | v. |喋喋不休地谈论；反复强调 | To talk or write about something repeatedly and tediously.  |例句：He kept harping on about his problems. 他不停地唠叨他的问题。|近义词：dwell on, iterate, reiterate, emphasize excessively |反义词：ignore, neglect, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harps on the same topic every day. = 他每天都在喋喋不休地谈论同一个话题。
They harp on the importance of punctuality. = 他们反复强调准时的重要性。


一般过去时:
She harped on her childhood memories all evening. = 她整个晚上都在喋喋不休地谈论她童年的回忆。
He harped on the details of the contract until everyone was bored. = 他反复强调合同细节，直到每个人都感到厌烦。


现在完成时:
They have harped on this issue for months. = 他们已经就这个问题喋喋不休地谈论了好几个月了。
She has harped on the need for change for years, but nothing has happened. = 她多年来一直强调需要改变，但什么也没发生。


过去完成时:
He had harped on the subject so much that everyone had already heard it. = 他已经对这个话题喋喋不休了很久，以至于每个人都听过了。
They had harped on the dangers of smoking before the campaign started. = 在运动开始之前，他们就反复强调吸烟的危害。


将来时:
I'm afraid he will harp on the same old complaints again. = 我担心他还会再次喋喋不休地抱怨那些陈芝麻烂谷子的事情。
She will harp on the benefits of exercise during the presentation. = 在演示中，她将反复强调锻炼的好处。


虚拟语气:
If he harped on the topic less, people would listen better. = 如果他不那么喋喋不休地谈论这个话题，人们会更好地倾听。
I wish he wouldn't harp on about his problems all the time. = 我希望他不要总是喋喋不休地谈论他的问题。


被动语态:
The topic was harped on relentlessly by the speaker. = 演讲者没完没了地反复强调这个话题。
The need for reform was harped on in the media. = 媒体反复强调改革的必要性。


疑问句:
Why does he keep harping on about that incident? = 他为什么一直喋喋不休地谈论那件事？
Is she still harping on the same old grievances? = 她还在喋喋不休地抱怨那些陈芝麻烂谷子的事情吗？


否定句:
He didn't harp on the negative aspects of the situation. = 他没有喋喋不休地谈论情况的消极方面。
She didn't harp on the details, but gave a general overview. = 她没有反复强调细节，而是作了一个总体的概述。
