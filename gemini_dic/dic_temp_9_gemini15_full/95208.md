inbetweeners:/ˌɪntəˈbiːnərz/| n. |夹在两者之间的人；处于过渡阶段的人；（尤指）介于青少年和成年人之间的人| People who are in a transitional stage, especially those between adolescence and adulthood.  They are often characterized by uncertainty and a sense of not quite belonging to either group. | Example:  The inbetweeners struggled to find their place in society.  介于青少年和成年人之间的人努力在社会中找到自己的位置。|近义词：adolescents, young adults, transitional people |反义词：children, adults
