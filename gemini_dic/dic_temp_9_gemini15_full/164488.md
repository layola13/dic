preenumerating: /ˌpriːɪˈnjuːməreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先列举；预先枚举 |  The act of listing or counting something beforehand.  This word is a combination of "pre-" (before) and "enumerating" (listing). It implies a process of systematically listing items before a main action or event. | The researcher spent hours preenumerating the potential variables before conducting the experiment.  研究人员在进行实验之前花了数小时预先列举潜在变量。


近义词:  pre-listing,  pre-cataloging,  advance listing


反义词:  post-enumerating,  retrospectively listing


词性: 动词
