mitring:/ˈmaɪtrɪŋ/| v.|斜接，斜角连接|Joining two pieces of material, typically wood, at a 45-degree angle to form a miter joint.|例句：He was mitring the picture frames to create a seamless look. 他正在斜接相框以 tạo ra vẻ ngoài liền mạch。|近义词：beveling, joining|反义词：butt joining
