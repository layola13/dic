rub: /rʌb/ | vt. n. |摩擦；揉；擦；涂抹 | To move something over the surface of something else with pressure.  |例句：He rubbed his tired eyes. 他揉了揉他疲惫的眼睛。

例句：She rubbed some cream on her face. 她往脸上涂了一些面霜。


近义词：scrape, wipe, smear, polish | 反义词：polish (in the sense of making smooth)


词源:  源自古英语 *rubbian，意思是“擦拭，摩擦”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rubs his hands together before speaking. = 他说话前搓搓手。
She rubs the stain with a cloth. = 她用布擦拭污渍。


一般过去时:
I rubbed my sore shoulder. = 我揉了揉我酸痛的肩膀。
They rubbed the car clean. = 他们把车擦干净了。


现在完成时:
He has rubbed the paint off the door. = 他把门上的油漆擦掉了。
She has rubbed the salt into the meat. = 她把盐搓进了肉里。


过去完成时:
I had rubbed the dirt off my shoes before entering the house. = 我进屋前已经把鞋上的泥土擦掉了。
She had rubbed the candle wax off the table. = 她已经把桌上的蜡烛蜡擦掉了。


将来时:
I will rub the lotion into my skin. = 我将把乳液涂抹到皮肤上。
He will rub the mirror until it shines. = 他会擦拭镜子直到它发亮。


虚拟语气:
If I rubbed the lamp, I might get a wish. = 如果我擦了灯，我可能会实现一个愿望。
I wish I had rubbed the sunscreen on more thoroughly. = 我真希望我更彻底地涂抹防晒霜。


被动语态:
The table was rubbed clean. = 桌子被擦干净了。
The lotion is rubbed into the skin. = 乳液被涂抹到皮肤上。


疑问句:
Are you rubbing the lotion on correctly? = 你涂抹乳液的方式正确吗？
Did you rub the stain before washing? = 你在洗涤前擦拭过污渍吗？


否定句:
Don't rub your eyes! = 不要揉眼睛！
He didn't rub the dirt off his shoes. = 他没有把鞋上的泥土擦掉。


PHRASE->
rub shoulders with: 与……交往
rub someone up the wrong way: 惹恼某人
rub it in: 伤口上撒盐；反复提及
rub along:  勉强过活
rub out: 擦掉；抹去
