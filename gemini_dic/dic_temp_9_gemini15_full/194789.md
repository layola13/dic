selvage:/ˈsɛlvɪdʒ/| n.|纬边，织边|The finished edge of a piece of woven fabric, formed by turning the weft threads back on themselves.  It prevents the fabric from unraveling.|例句：The selvage of the fabric was neatly finished. 这块布料的纬边很整齐。|例句：The tailor carefully examined the selvage before cutting the cloth.裁缝仔细检查了纬边才剪裁布料。|近义词：edge, border, hem|反义词：raw edge
