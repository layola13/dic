equanimity:/ˌekwəˈnɪməti/| n. |平静;沉着;镇静| calmness and composure, especially in a difficult situation.  The ability to remain calm and even-tempered, especially in a difficult situation. |例句：She faced the crisis with remarkable equanimity. 她以非凡的镇静应对危机。|例句：Despite the chaos around him, he maintained his equanimity. 尽管周围一片混乱，但他仍然保持镇静。|近义词：calmness, composure, serenity, tranquility |反义词：agitation, anxiety, panic, turmoil |名词
