/ˌnɒnɪmˈpruːvmənts/ | n. |未改进的事物，没有改进的方面 |Things that have not been improved; aspects that remain unchanged or deteriorated.  The report highlighted several nonimprovements in the company's performance. 这份报告强调了公司业绩中一些没有改进的方面。|近义词：lack of progress, stagnation, deterioration |反义词：improvements, advancements, progress

USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  Therefore, verb tenses are not applicable.  Instead, we'll show example sentences demonstrating its usage in different sentence structures.

The nonimprovements are a source of concern. =  这些未改进的方面令人担忧。
