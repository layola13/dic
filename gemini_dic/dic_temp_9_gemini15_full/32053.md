cherry-pick:/ˈtʃɛriˌpɪk/| vt.|精选；挑拣；选择性地采用|To select only the best or most desirable items from a group, often ignoring or rejecting the rest.  This term often carries a negative connotation, implying bias or unfairness.|例句：The company cherry-picked the most positive customer reviews for their marketing campaign.  这家公司为其营销活动挑选了最积极的客户评价。


例句：He cherry-picked data to support his flawed argument. 他挑选数据来支持他那有缺陷的论点。


近义词：select, choose, handpick, cull


反义词：reject, dismiss, ignore, overlook


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cherry-pick the best fruits from the orchard. = 他们从果园里挑选最好的水果。
She cherry-picks information to fit her narrative. = 她挑选信息以符合她的说法。


一般过去时:
The team cherry-picked the strongest players for the final game. = 球队为决赛挑选了最强的球员。
He cherry-picked the easiest questions on the test. = 他在考试中挑选了最简单的题目。


现在完成时:
They have cherry-picked the most promising candidates. = 他们已经挑选了最有希望的候选人。
She has cherry-picked the best parts of the report. = 她已经挑选了报告中最好的部分。


过去完成时:
The manager had cherry-picked the most experienced workers before the project started. = 项目开始前，经理已经挑选了最有经验的工人。
They had cherry-picked the most relevant evidence before presenting their case. = 他们在陈述案情之前已经挑选了最相关的证据。


将来时:

They will cherry-pick the best ideas from the brainstorming session. = 他们将从头脑风暴会议中挑选最好的想法。


虚拟语气:
If I were to cherry-pick my favorite songs, this would be my playlist. = 如果我要挑选我最喜欢的歌曲，这就是我的播放列表。
I wish I could cherry-pick the best aspects of both jobs. = 我希望我能挑选两份工作的最佳方面。


被动语态:
The best proposals were cherry-picked by the committee. = 最好的提案被委员会挑选出来。

疑问句:

Are they cherry-picking the data to support their biased conclusion? = 他们是否在挑选数据来支持他们有偏见的结论？


否定句:
They did not cherry-pick the data; they used all of it. = 他们没有挑选数据；他们使用了所有数据。
