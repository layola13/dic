of-age: /ˈɒv eɪdʒ/ | adj. | 成年的，法定年龄的 | Legally adult; having reached the legal age.  |例句：She's of age now and can vote. 她现在成年了，可以投票了。|  例句：He's not of age yet, so he can't drink alcohol. 他还没成年，所以不能喝酒。|近义词：adult, grown-up, mature |反义词：minor, underage, immature


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is of age. = 他已成年。
She is of the drinking age. = 她已到法定饮酒年龄。


一般过去时:
He was of age when he joined the army. = 他参军时已成年。
She was not of voting age last year. = 她去年还没到法定投票年龄。


现在完成时:
She has been of age for two years. = 她成年两年了。
He has just become of age. = 他刚刚成年。


过去完成时:
He had been of age for a year before he got married. = 他结婚前已成年一年了。
She had not been of driving age when the accident happened. = 事故发生时她还没到法定驾车年龄。


将来时:
He will be of age next year. = 他明年就成年了。
She will be of drinking age in a few months. = 几个月后她就能喝酒了。


虚拟语气:
If he were of age, he could buy a car. = 如果他成年了，他就能买车了。
I wish I were of age so I could go to that club. = 我希望我已经成年了，这样我就能去那个俱乐部了。


被动语态:
(This adjective is rarely used passively.)


疑问句:
Is she of age? = 她成年了吗？
Are you of the legal drinking age in this state? = 在这个州，你达到了法定饮酒年龄了吗？


否定句:
He is not of age yet. = 他还没成年。
She was not of driving age at the time of the accident. = 事故发生时她还没到法定驾车年龄。


PHRASE->
come of age: 成年；成熟
of marriageable age: 到结婚年龄
of school age: 到入学年龄
of voting age: 到投票年龄
of legal age: 到法定年龄
