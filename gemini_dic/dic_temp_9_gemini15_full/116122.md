marvels:/ˈmɑːrvəlz/| n.|奇迹，令人惊奇的事物|Things that cause great wonder or astonishment; wonders.|The Grand Canyon is one of the natural marvels of the world.  大峡谷是世界自然奇观之一。|The intricate design of a spider's web is a marvel of nature.  蜘蛛网错综复杂的设计是自然界的奇迹。| wonders, miracles, prodigies|ordinary, commonplace|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The scientist marvels at the new discovery. = 科学家对这项新发现感到惊奇。
These marvels of engineering inspire awe. = 这些工程奇迹令人敬畏。


一般过去时:
We marveled at the breathtaking scenery. = 我们对令人叹为观止的景色感到惊叹。
The audience marveled at the magician's tricks. = 观众对魔术师的戏法感到惊奇。


现在完成时:
Scientists have marveled at the complexity of the human brain for centuries. = 科学家几个世纪以来一直对人脑的复杂性感到惊叹。
People have marveled at the pyramids of Egypt for thousands of years. = 人们几千年来一直对埃及的金字塔感到惊叹。


过去完成时:
Before the invention of the internet, people had marveled at the speed of the telegraph. = 在互联网发明之前，人们对电报的速度感到惊叹。
Having never seen anything like it, they had marveled at the spectacle. = 因为从未见过这样的景象，他们对这场奇观感到惊叹。


将来时:
Future generations will marvel at our technological achievements. = 子孙后代将会对我们的科技成就感到惊叹。
We will marvel at the wonders of space exploration. = 我们将对太空探索的奇迹感到惊叹。


虚拟语气:
If I had seen it with my own eyes, I would have marveled at its beauty. = 如果我亲眼看到它，我会对它的美丽感到惊叹。
I wish I could have marveled at the aurora borealis. = 我希望我能看到北极光。


被动语态:
The new technology is marveled at by experts worldwide. = 这项新技术受到世界各地专家的赞叹。
The ancient ruins were marveled at by archaeologists. = 古代遗址受到了考古学家的赞叹。


疑问句:
Do you marvel at the beauty of nature? = 你对大自然的美丽感到惊叹吗？
What marvels of science and technology have you witnessed? = 你见证了哪些科学技术的奇迹？


否定句:
I didn't marvel at his performance; it was quite ordinary. = 我对他（的表演）并不感到惊奇；它相当普通。
They didn't marvel at the view; they were too busy taking pictures. = 他们对景色并不感到惊奇；他们太忙于拍照了。
