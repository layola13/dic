godsib:/ˈɡɒdzɪb/| n.|教母；女教父|A godsib is a person who is the sponsor or godparent of a child at baptism.  The term is now largely archaic.  It's a contraction of "God's sib," meaning "God's relative" or "God's kin."|例句：In the olden days, my godsib played a significant role in my upbringing. = 在过去，我的教母在我的成长中扮演着重要的角色。


例句：She remained close friends with her godsib throughout her life. = 她一生都和她的教母保持着密切的友谊。


近义词：godmother, godparent


反义词：无


词性:名词
