multilamellate: /ˌmʌltiˈlæməleɪt/ | adj. |多层片的；多层板的 | Having many lamellae or thin plates or layers.  The term describes a structure composed of numerous thin, parallel layers or plates.  |例句：The multilamellate structure of the bone provided exceptional strength and flexibility.  多层板状的骨骼结构提供了非凡的强度和柔韧性。


The shell exhibits a strikingly multilamellate structure under the microscope. 在显微镜下，贝壳呈现出引人注目的多层片状结构。|近义词：stratified, layered, laminated |反义词：unilamellate, single-layered


词源:

* **multi-**:  Latin prefix meaning "many" or "much."
* **lamella**: Latin word meaning "a thin plate" or "a scale."  It's related to the Latin word "lamina," also meaning "thin plate."
* **-ate**:  Suffix forming adjectives, indicating "possessing" or "characterized by."

Therefore, "multilamellate" literally means "having many thin plates" or "composed of many layers."
