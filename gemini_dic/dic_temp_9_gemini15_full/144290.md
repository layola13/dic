outspend:/ˌaʊtˈspɛnd/| vt.|超过…的开支；花费多于…|To spend more money than someone or something else.|例句：The government outspent its predecessor on education. 政府在教育上的支出超过了前任政府。|例句：We outspent our budget by $1000. 我们超支了1000美元。|近义词：exceed, surpass, outlay|反义词：underspend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They outspend their neighbours on holidays. = 他们在假期上的花费超过了邻居。
The company outspends its competitors on advertising. = 这家公司在广告上的支出超过了竞争对手。


一般过去时:
Last year, the company outspent its rivals on research and development. = 去年，这家公司在研发上的支出超过了竞争对手。
We outspent our allocated budget for the project. = 我们超出了该项目的预算。


现在完成时:
They have outspent their savings on a new car. = 他们把积蓄都花在新车上。
The government has outspent its projected budget for the year. = 政府今年的支出超过了预算。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, they had outspent their initial investment. = 到年底，他们已经超过了最初的投资额。
The project had outspent its allocated funds before completion. = 项目在完工前就超支了。


将来时:
They will outspend their competitors in the next marketing campaign. = 他们在下一轮营销活动中将超过竞争对手的支出。
The company anticipates that it will outspend its previous year's budget. = 公司预计其支出将超过去年的预算。


虚拟语气:
If they had planned better, they wouldn't have outspent their budget. = 如果他们计划得更好，就不会超支了。
I wish we hadn't outspent our savings on that vacation. = 我真希望我们没有把积蓄都花在那次度假上。


被动语态:
The budget was outspent by the ambitious project. = 雄心勃勃的项目导致预算超支。
The previous year's spending was outspent significantly this year. = 今年的支出大大超过了去年的支出。


疑问句:
Did they outspend their rivals in the last quarter? = 他们上个季度是否超过了竞争对手的支出？
Will the company outspend its competitors on new technology? = 这家公司会在新技术上超过竞争对手的支出吗？


否定句:
They didn't outspend their budget this month. = 他们这个月没有超支。
The project didn't outspend its allocated funds. = 该项目没有超过拨款。
