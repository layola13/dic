prevacating:/ˌpriːˈveɪkeɪtɪŋ/| v.| 提前撤离；预先腾空 | The act of leaving a place or position before the scheduled or expected time.  It often implies a planned or necessary departure, sometimes due to unforeseen circumstances. |例句：The tenants are prevacating their apartment to allow for renovations. = 租户们正在提前搬出他们的公寓以便进行装修。

The company is prevacating its office space in anticipation of a merger. = 公司正在提前腾空办公室空间以应对即将到来的合并。


近义词：evacuating, vacating, leaving early, departing prematurely |反义词：staying, remaining, occupying, settling in |词性:动词
