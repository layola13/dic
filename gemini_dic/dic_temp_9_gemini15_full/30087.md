cease-fire:/ˈsiːsfaɪər/| n. |停火；休战|An agreement to stop fighting a war, temporarily or permanently.  A period of time during which fighting is stopped. |例句：Both sides agreed to a cease-fire after weeks of intense fighting.  双方经过几周激烈的战斗后同意停火。


例句：A temporary cease-fire was declared to allow for humanitarian aid to be delivered.  宣布暂时停火，以便运送人道主义援助。


近义词：truce, armistice, suspension of hostilities|反义词：outbreak of hostilities, resumption of fighting|词性:noun
