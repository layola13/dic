emburse:/ɪmˈbɜːrs/| vt.|偿还；报销；付还|To repay (a person) a sum of money; to reimburse.  |例句：The company will reimburse you for your travel expenses. 公司将报销你的差旅费。|例句：She was reimbursed for the cost of the repairs. 她获得了修理费用的赔偿。|近义词：repay, refund, recompense|反义词：owe, debit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reimburses employees for business travel. = 公司报销员工的公务旅行费用。
He usually reimburses his friends for lunch. = 他通常会偿还朋友们的午餐费用。


一般过去时:
They reimbursed me for the damaged goods. = 他们赔偿了我损坏货物的费用。
The insurance company reimbursed her for the stolen jewelry. = 保险公司赔偿了她被盗珠宝的损失。


现在完成时:
I have been reimbursed for my expenses. = 我的费用已经报销了。
She has not yet been reimbursed for the medical bills. = 她的医疗账单尚未报销。


过去完成时:
He had already been reimbursed before he submitted the claim. = 他在提交索赔之前就已经获得了报销。
They had reimbursed her for the initial investment before the project started. = 在项目开始之前，他们已经赔偿了她最初的投资。


将来时:
The company will reimburse you as soon as possible. = 公司将尽快给你报销。
They will be reimbursed for their time and effort. = 他们的时间和精力将会得到补偿。


虚拟语气:
If the company reimbursed me promptly, I would be very happy. = 如果公司及时报销我的费用，我会非常高兴。
I wish they had reimbursed me earlier for the conference fees. = 我希望他们早点报销我的会议费。


被动语态:
Expenses will be reimbursed at the end of the month. = 费用将在月底报销。
The damaged goods were reimbursed by the manufacturer. = 损坏的货物由制造商赔偿。


疑问句:
Will the company reimburse me for this? = 公司会报销这个吗？
Are you going to be reimbursed for your travel costs? = 你的差旅费会报销吗？


否定句:
The company did not reimburse him for the parking fees. = 公司没有报销他的停车费。
They were not reimbursed for their time spent on the project. = 他们没有得到项目所花费时间的报酬。
