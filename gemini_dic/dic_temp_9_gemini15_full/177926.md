rechoose:/ˌriːˈtʃuːz/ | vt. |重新选择，再选择|To choose again; to make a new selection.|例句：After careful consideration, she decided to rechoose her major.  仔细考虑后，她决定重新选择专业。

例句：The committee will rechoose the chairman next week. 委员会下周将重新选举主席。


近义词：select again, choose anew, reconsider


反义词：finalize, decide finally


词性:动词
