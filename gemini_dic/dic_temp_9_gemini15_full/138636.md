nucleus:/ˈnuːkliəs/ | n. |细胞核；核心；原子核|The central and most important part of an object, institution, or system.  It is the central part of an atom, containing protons and neutrons.|例句：The nucleus of a cell contains its genetic material.  细胞核包含其遗传物质。

The nucleus of the problem is a lack of communication.问题的核心是缺乏沟通。|近义词：core, center, heart |反义词：periphery, surface
