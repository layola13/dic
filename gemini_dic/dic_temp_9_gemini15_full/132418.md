/nɑːn dɪˈpɑːrtʃərz/ | n. | 未离开者；未出发者 |  The term "nondepartures" refers to individuals or things that have not departed or left a particular place or situation. It is a relatively uncommon word, often used in specific contexts like transportation or event attendance.  | Example:  The airline recorded a surprisingly high number of nondepartures due to the severe weather.  航空公司由于恶劣天气记录了数量惊人的未出发旅客。  The concert organizers were concerned about the large number of nondepartures on the guest list.  音乐会组织者担心宾客名单上有大量未到场者。 | 近义词: absentees, those who didn't leave, those who remained | 反义词: departures, those who left
