palmately:/ˈpælmeɪtli/| adj.|掌状的；掌裂的|Having the lobes or parts radiating from a common point, like the fingers of a hand.  This term is often used in botany to describe the arrangement of leaves or veins.|例句：The leaves of the maple tree are palmately lobed.  枫树的叶子是掌状裂的。|例句：The veins of the leaf are arranged palmately.  叶脉呈掌状排列。|近义词：radiate, digitately|反义词：pinnately
