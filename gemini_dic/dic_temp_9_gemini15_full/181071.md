reindex:/ˌriːˈɪndɛks/ | vt. |重新索引 |To create a new index for (a database or other collection of data).  This often involves rebuilding the index from scratch or updating an existing one to reflect changes in the data.  |例句：The database administrator decided to reindex the entire database to improve search performance. 数据库管理员决定重新索引整个数据库以提高搜索性能。


例句：We need to reindex the website after adding new content. 我们需要在添加新内容后重新索引网站。


近义词：rebuild index, update index | 反义词：None (the opposite action would be to remove an index, not to "un-reindex") | 词性: verb
