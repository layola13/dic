resails:/ˌriːˈseɪlz/| n. |再航行，重新启航|The act of setting sail again; a second voyage.|例句：After a brief stop, the ship made resails to complete its journey.  再短暂的停留后，船只重新启航完成旅程。
例句：The captain ordered resails after the storm subsided. 风暴平息后，船长下令重新启航。|近义词：reembarkation, renewed voyage|反义词：arrival, docking
