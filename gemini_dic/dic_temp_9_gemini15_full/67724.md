exuviae:/ɛkˈsuviːaɪ/| n.|蜕皮；蜕壳；残骸|The cast-off skin or outer covering of an insect or other invertebrate.  It refers to the remains or remnants left behind after shedding or molting.  The term can also be used figuratively to describe the remnants of something that has been outgrown or left behind.|例句：The snake left its exuviae behind in the grass.  蛇在草地上留下了它的蜕皮。

例句：The exuviae of the cicada were scattered on the ground. 蝉蜕散落在地上。


近义词：slough, cast-off skin, remains, remnants

反义词：N/A (no direct antonym, as it describes a byproduct)

词性：名词
