chitinous:/ˈkaɪtɪnəs/| adj.|几丁质的，几丁质构成的|Made of or resembling chitin.  Chitin is a tough, protective substance that forms the exoskeletons of many arthropods, such as insects and crustaceans.|The beetle's chitinous shell protected it from predators.  甲虫几丁质的外壳保护它免受捕食者的攻击。|tough, hard, rigid|soft, flexible
