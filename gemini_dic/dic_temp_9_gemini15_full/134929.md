nonmutinousnesses:/ˌnɒnˈmjuːtɪnəsnəsiz/ | n. | 非叛乱状态；非反抗状态 | The absence of mutiny or rebellion; the state of being free from rebellious acts or attitudes.  This word is extremely rare and largely theoretical; it's unlikely to be encountered in everyday speech or writing.  It highlights the absence of rebellious behavior. |  例句：The prolonged period of nonmutinousnesses within the army was surprising given the recent hardships.  军队长期处于非叛乱状态，考虑到最近的困难，这令人惊讶。


The prolonged nonmutinousnesses among the sailors allowed the voyage to proceed smoothly. 水手们长时间的平静状态使航行顺利进行。| 近义词：tranquility, peace, calm, order | 反义词：rebellion, mutiny, insurrection, unrest


由于该词极度罕见且缺乏实际应用，无法提供例句的各种时态、语态等语法范例。词组搭配也几乎不存在。  该词的构成是通过在 "mutinous" (叛乱的) 上添加否定前缀 "non-"，再添加名词后缀 "-ness" 来表示状态，最后再添加 "-es" 来构成复数形式。  其词源可追溯到拉丁语 "mutare" (改变) 和古英语 "tin" (争论)。
