preassuming:/ˌpriːəˈsjuːmɪŋ/| v.|预先假设；预先认定|Assuming something before having sufficient evidence.  It often implies a degree of unwarranted confidence or presumptuousness. |例句：Preassuming his guilt, they refused to hear his defense.  预先认定他有罪，他们拒绝听取他的辩护。

例句：She preassumed the success of her project, leading to inadequate planning. 她预先认定她的项目会成功，导致计划不足。


近义词：presupposing, assuming, taking for granted


反义词：verifying, confirming, questioning


词性：动词
