inconveniencing: /ˌɪnkənˈviːnjənsɪŋ/ | v. | 使...不便；造成...麻烦 |  The act of causing inconvenience or trouble. It implies causing a minor disruption or annoyance, not a major problem. | 例句：I apologize for inconveniencing you.  对不起，给你添麻烦了。  |  近义词：bothering, troubling, disrupting | 反义词：helping, assisting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am inconveniencing my neighbors with my loud music. = 我吵闹的音乐给邻居带来了不便。
This new system inconveniences many users. = 这个新系统给许多用户带来了不便。


一般过去时:
The road closure inconvenienced many drivers. = 道路封闭给许多司机带来了不便。
I’m sorry I inconvenienced you yesterday. = 对不起，我昨天给你添麻烦了。


现在完成时:
I have inconvenienced you enough.  = 我已经给你添够麻烦了。
The changes have inconvenienced many customers. = 这些改变给许多顾客带来了不便。


过去完成时:
I had inconvenienced her before, so I tried to be more considerate. = 我以前给她带来过不便，所以我尽量考虑周到一些。
They had inconvenienced several people with their late arrival. = 他们迟到给几个人带来了不便。


将来时:
The new regulations will inconvenience many businesses. = 新规将给许多企业带来不便。
This change will inconvenience some users, but it is necessary. = 这一改变会给一些用户带来不便，但这是必要的。


虚拟语气:
I would hate to inconvenience you further. = 我不想再给你添麻烦了。
If we leave now, we will inconvenience no one. = 如果我们现在走，就不会给任何人添麻烦。


被动语态:
Many people were inconvenienced by the power outage. = 许多人因停电而感到不便。
Customers are often inconvenienced by long wait times. = 顾客经常因为长时间等待而感到不便。


疑问句:
Will this plan inconvenience anyone? = 这个计划会给任何人带来不便吗？
Does this decision inconvenience anybody? = 这个决定会给任何人带来不便吗？


否定句:
I didn't mean to inconvenience you. = 我不是故意给你添麻烦的。
This system does not inconvenience users. = 这个系统不会给用户带来不便。


PHRASE->

To minimize inconveniencing others. = 尽量减少给他人带来的不便。
It is important to avoid inconveniencing customers. = 避免给客户带来不便非常重要。
