predirector:/ˌpriːdaɪˈrektər/| n. |预导演；预先指导者|A predirector is a person who plans and prepares for a director's work, often involved in pre-production activities such as storyboarding, casting, location scouting, and scheduling.  They may also assist the director in various aspects of the production.|Example: The predirector meticulously planned the shooting schedule, ensuring efficiency and minimizing potential delays.  预导演一丝不苟地计划了拍摄日程，确保效率并最大限度地减少了潜在的延误。|近义词：pre-production coordinator, assistant director (in some contexts)|反义词：director|名词
