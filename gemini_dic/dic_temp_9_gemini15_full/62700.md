engineered:/ˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrd/ | adj. | 工程的；设计的；精巧的 | Designed or produced using engineering; showing skill and ingenuity.  |例句：The bridge is an engineered marvel. 这座桥是工程奇迹。

The system was engineered to maximize efficiency.  这个系统的设计是为了最大限度地提高效率。


近义词：designed, manufactured, constructed, contrived, devised | 反义词：natural, unplanned, accidental


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company engineers new products every year. = 公司每年都设计新产品。
They engineer solutions to complex problems. = 他们设计出解决复杂问题的方案。


一般过去时:
The engineers engineered a new bridge across the river. = 工程师们设计了一座横跨河流的新桥。
They engineered a clever workaround. = 他们设计了一个巧妙的变通方法。


现在完成时:
They have engineered several successful projects this year. = 今年他们已经成功完成了几个项目。
We have engineered a solution to the problem. = 我们已经设计出了解决这个问题的方案。


过去完成时:
They had engineered the system before the launch. = 他们在发布之前就设计好了这个系统。
The company had engineered a successful marketing campaign. = 公司之前策划了一个成功的营销活动。


将来时:
They will engineer a new type of aircraft. = 他们将设计一种新型飞机。
We will engineer a more efficient system. = 我们将设计一个更高效的系统。


虚拟语气:
If they engineered a better system, the problem would be solved. = 如果他们设计一个更好的系统，问题就能解决。
I wish they had engineered a more robust solution. = 我希望他们当时设计了一个更强大的解决方案。


被动语态:
The software was engineered by a team of experts. = 这个软件是由一个专家团队设计的。
The building was engineered to withstand earthquakes. = 这座建筑的设计能够承受地震。


疑问句:
Was the product engineered to meet specific requirements? = 这个产品的设计是否符合特定要求？
How was the system engineered to improve efficiency? = 这个系统是如何设计的以提高效率？


否定句:
The bridge was not engineered properly. = 这座桥的设计不当。
They didn't engineer the system correctly. = 他们没有正确地设计这个系统。
