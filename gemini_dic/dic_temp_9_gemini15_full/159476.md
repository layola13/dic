policewomen:/pəˈliːswʊmɪn/| n.|女警，女警察|The word "policewomen" refers to female members of a police force. It's a plural noun, the plural form of "policewoman".|例句：Several policewomen were investigating the crime scene.  几名女警正在调查犯罪现场。
Several policewomen responded to the emergency call.  几名女警回应了紧急呼叫。


近义词：female police officers, women police officers|反义词：policemen
