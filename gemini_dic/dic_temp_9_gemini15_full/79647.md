glossly:/ˈɡlɒsli/ | adj. |有光泽的；闪亮的|Shining or reflecting light smoothly; having a smooth, shiny surface.  The word implies a smoothness and evenness of reflection, often suggesting a polished or varnished appearance. |例句：The glossy magazine cover attracted my attention.  =  光滑的杂志封面吸引了我的注意。|例句：Her hair had a healthy, glossy sheen. = 她的头发有着健康的光泽。|近义词：shiny, lustrous, polished, gleaming |反义词：dull, matte, rough
