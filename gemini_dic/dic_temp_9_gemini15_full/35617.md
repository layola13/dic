coastlines:/ˈkoʊstlaɪnz/ | n. |海岸线，海岸|The outlines of the land where it meets the sea.  Coastlines are shaped by a variety of factors, including erosion, deposition, and tectonic activity. |例句：The rugged coastlines of the Pacific Northwest are breathtaking.  太平洋西北部的崎岖海岸线令人叹为观止。
The smooth coastlines of the Caribbean are popular tourist destinations. 加勒比海平缓的海岸线是受欢迎的旅游胜地。|近义词：shorelines, shores, seaboards |反义词：inland, interior
