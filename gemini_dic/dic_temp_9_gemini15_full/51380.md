determinant:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnənt/ | n. |行列式，决定因素|A determinant is a scalar value that can be computed from the elements of a square matrix and encodes certain properties of the linear transformation described by the matrix.  It is also a factor that decisively affects the nature or outcome of something.|例句：The determinant of the matrix is zero.  这个矩阵的行列式为零。The economic downturn was a major determinant in the company's failure. 经济衰退是导致该公司倒闭的主要决定因素。|近义词：factor, influence, component|反义词：indeterminate, random
