rumor:/ˈruːmər/| n.|谣言，传闻|A rumor is an unconfirmed story or piece of information that is passed from person to person.  It often lacks evidence and can be untrue.|例句：I heard a rumor that he's leaving the company. 我听说他将要离开公司。|例句：The rumor spread quickly through the town. 这个谣言迅速传遍了整个小镇。|近义词：gossip, hearsay, report, speculation|反义词：fact, truth


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rumor spreads quickly. = 谣言传播很快。
Rumors often lack evidence. = 谣言常常缺乏证据。

一般过去时:
The rumor spread like wildfire. = 谣言像野火一样迅速蔓延。
She denied the rumor. = 她否认了这个谣言。


现在完成时:
The rumor has spread throughout the city. = 谣言已经传遍了整个城市。
Rumors have damaged his reputation. = 谣言损害了他的名誉。

过去完成时:
The rumor had already spread before I heard it. = 我听到这个谣言之前，它已经传开了。
They had dismissed the rumor as untrue. = 他们已经把这个谣言斥为不实之言。


将来时:
The rumor will likely reach the media soon. = 这个谣言很可能会很快传到媒体。
I think the rumor will die down eventually. = 我认为这个谣言最终会平息。

虚拟语气:
If the rumor were true, I would be surprised. = 如果这个谣言是真的，我会很惊讶。
I wish the rumor wasn't true. = 我希望这个谣言不是真的。

被动语态:
The rumor was started by a disgruntled employee. = 这个谣言是由一名不满的员工散布的。
The rumor is being investigated by the authorities. = 这个谣言正在由当局调查。


疑问句:
Is the rumor true? = 这个谣言是真的吗？
What is the latest rumor about the company? = 关于公司最新的谣言是什么？

否定句:
The rumor is not true. = 这个谣言不是真的。
I don't believe the rumor. = 我不相信这个谣言。
