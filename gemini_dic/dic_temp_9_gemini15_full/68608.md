/fænˈtæstɪk/ | adj. | 极好的；奇异的；空想的 |  Fantastic means extraordinarily good; wonderful. It can also describe something that is very strange or unusual, or something that is imaginary or unreal. |例句：The view from the mountaintop was fantastic.  山顶的景色美极了。

例句：He told me a fantastic story about a dragon. 他给我讲了一个关于龙的奇异故事。


近义词：wonderful, amazing, extraordinary, marvelous, incredible, superb, magical, unreal

反义词：terrible, awful, dreadful, ordinary, mundane, realistic


词源：From the Middle English word "fantastical," ultimately derived from the Late Latin "fantasticus," meaning "fanciful, whimsical." This in turn comes from the Greek "fantastikos,"  related to "phantasia," meaning "imagination, appearance."
