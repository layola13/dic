prognathism:/ˈprɒɡnəθɪzəm/| n. |前突（颌）；前颌突出 |Prognathism refers to the positional relationship of the jaws, specifically the projection of the jaw or jaws beyond the proper facial plane.  It is a condition where the lower jaw (mandible) and/or upper jaw (maxilla) protrudes excessively beyond the upper part of the face.
