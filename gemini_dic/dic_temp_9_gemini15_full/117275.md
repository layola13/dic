mechanization:/ˌmɛkənaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |机械化；机械操作|The process of changing from working by hand or with animals to working with machines.  It involves the widespread use of machinery in all aspects of production and daily life.|例句：The mechanization of agriculture led to increased food production.  农业机械化导致粮食产量增加。

The mechanization of the factory increased efficiency and reduced labor costs. 工厂的机械化提高了效率并降低了劳动力成本。|近义词：automation, motorization|反义词：manual labor, handwork
