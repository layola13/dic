nubia:/ˈnuːbiə/| n. |努比亚（古王国）|Nubia refers to a region in northeastern Africa, located along the Nile River, south of Egypt and north of Sudan.  It encompasses a vast area with a rich history and civilization that flourished for millennia. The term often evokes images of ancient kingdoms, pyramids, and cultural exchanges between African and Mediterranean societies. |Example: The Kingdom of Kush, a powerful Nubian state, exerted significant influence over Egypt for centuries. 努比亚的库什王国几个世纪以来对埃及施加了重大影响。|近义词：Kush (Kingdom of Kush) |反义词：无
