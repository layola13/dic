championships:/ˈtʃæmpiənʃɪps/| n. |锦标赛；冠军赛；冠军称号|A competition to find the best player or team in a particular sport or activity.  It often involves a series of games or events. |例句：The World Cup is a major international football championships. 世界杯是一项主要的国际足球锦标赛。

例句：She won the national swimming championships. 她赢得了全国游泳锦标赛。


近义词：competitions, tournaments, contests|反义词：None.


词源:  From "champion" + "-ship".  "Champion" ultimately derives from the Old French *champion*, meaning "battle-champion, combatant", from *camp*, "field". The suffix "-ship" indicates a state, condition, or quality.
