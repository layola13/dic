heroify:/ˈhɪroʊɪfaɪ/ | vt. |使成为英雄；把…英雄化；赞扬… |To represent or treat someone as a hero; to glorify or exaggerate the heroic qualities of someone or something. |例句：The media often heroify athletes, ignoring their flaws.媒体常常把运动员神化，忽略了他们的缺点。 The movie heroified the historical figure, ignoring his less admirable actions.这部电影把这位历史人物英雄化了，忽略了他不那么令人钦佩的行为。|近义词：glorify, idolize, lionize |反义词：vilify, demonize, denigrate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They heroify athletes in the media. = 媒体将运动员视为英雄。
The company heroifies its employees' achievements. = 公司赞扬员工的成就。


一般过去时:
The press heroified the rescuers after the disaster. = 灾难过后，媒体赞扬了救援人员。
The novel heroified a fictional character. = 这部小说将一个虚构人物英雄化。


现在完成时:
Society has heroified many historical figures. = 社会已经将许多历史人物英雄化。
The media has heroified this athlete's comeback story. = 媒体已经将这位运动员的复出故事英雄化。


过去完成时:
They had heroified the general before the war even began. = 战争甚至还没开始，人们就已经将这位将军英雄化了。
Historians had heroified the king's reign long before any critical analysis. = 在任何批判性分析之前，历史学家们就已经将国王的统治时期英雄化了。


将来时:
They will heroify the astronauts upon their return. = 宇航员返回后，人们将他们视为英雄。
Future generations will likely heroify this scientific breakthrough. = 后代很可能会将这一科学突破视为英雄事迹。


虚拟语气:
If they won the game, the media would heroify them. = 如果他们赢得了比赛，媒体就会将他们视为英雄。
I wish they wouldn't heroify such violent characters in movies. = 我希望他们不要在电影中将如此暴力的角色英雄化。


被动语态:
Athletes are often heroified by the media. = 运动员常常被媒体视为英雄。
The historical events were heroified in the documentary. = 纪录片将这些历史事件英雄化了。


疑问句:
Do they always heroify the winners? = 他们总是把获胜者视为英雄吗？
Why does society heroify certain individuals? = 为什么社会会将某些个人视为英雄？


否定句:
They did not heroify the losing team. = 他们没有将输的队伍视为英雄。
The book did not heroify the protagonist; instead, it showed their flaws. = 这本书没有将主角英雄化；相反，它展现了他们的缺点。
