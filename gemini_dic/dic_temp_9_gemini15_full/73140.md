foraminifera:/ˌfɔːræmɪˈnɪfərə/| n. |有孔虫；有孔虫类|Single-celled protists with shells composed of calcium carbonate or other materials. They are abundant in marine environments and their fossils are important in stratigraphy. |例句：The ocean floor is teeming with foraminifera. 海洋底部充满了有孔虫。|例句：The study of foraminifera helps geologists understand past climates.  对有孔虫的研究帮助地质学家了解过去的气候。|近义词：protozoa (a broader term)|反义词：N/A
