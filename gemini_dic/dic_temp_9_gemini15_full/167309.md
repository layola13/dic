pretransact:/ˌpriːtrænˈzækt/| v.|预先交易，预先办理|To conduct a transaction or business before the main event or officially scheduled time.  This often involves preparatory steps or agreements.|例句：The companies pretransacted some key details before the official merger negotiations.  公司在正式合并谈判之前预先商定了几个关键细节。|例句：They pretransacted the land sale to ensure a smooth closing. 他们预先办理了土地买卖手续，以确保顺利过户。|近义词：prearrange, prepare, negotiate beforehand|反义词：postpone, delay, defer|动词
