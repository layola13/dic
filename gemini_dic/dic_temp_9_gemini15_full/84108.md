half-joking: /ˈhæf ˈdʒoʊkɪŋ/ | adj. | 半开玩笑的，开玩笑似的 |  Partially joking; said or done partly in jest.  It implies that the statement or action is not entirely serious, but contains a degree of seriousness as well. |


例句：

He said it half-joking, but I think there's some truth to it. 他半开玩笑地说，但我认为其中有一些道理。

She half-jokingly suggested we should run away together. 她半开玩笑地建议我们一起私奔。


近义词：partly serious, playfully teasing, somewhat facetious


反义词：serious, completely earnest, utterly sincere


词性:形容词
