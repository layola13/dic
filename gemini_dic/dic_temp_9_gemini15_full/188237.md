royalists:/ˈrɔɪəlɪsts/| n. |保皇党；忠君主义者 |Supporters of a monarch or monarchy, especially during a period of revolution or upheaval.  They advocate for the restoration or preservation of the monarchy. |The royalists fought fiercely to restore the king to the throne. 保皇党为了复辟国王而激烈战斗。|The Jacobite royalists in Scotland. 苏格兰的雅各布派保皇党。|revolutionaries, rebels |republicans, democrats
