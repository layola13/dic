parthenogenetically:/ˌpɑːrθɛnoʊdʒɛˈnɛtɪkli/ | adv. | 单性生殖地；孤雌生殖地 |  In a manner characteristic of parthenogenesis; without fertilization.  | 例句：The species reproduces parthenogenetically.  该物种进行单性生殖。

The lizard reproduced parthenogenetically, producing offspring genetically identical to itself.  这只蜥蜴进行了孤雌生殖，产下了基因与其完全相同的后代。


近义词：asexually (无性地)


反义词：sexually (有性地)


词性:副词
