encouragement: /ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒmənt/ | n. | 鼓励；激励 | The act of giving support, confidence, or hope to someone.  It can refer to words of support, actions that inspire, or a general atmosphere that promotes progress. | 例句：Her encouragement helped me overcome my fear and try again.  她的鼓励帮助我克服了恐惧，并再次尝试。

His consistent encouragement of his team led to their success. 他对团队始终如一的鼓励促成了他们的成功。


近义词：inspiration, motivation, support, stimulation, incentive | 反义词：discouragement, dispiritedness, demotivation
