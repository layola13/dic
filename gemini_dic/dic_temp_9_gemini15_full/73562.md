forestations:/ˌfɒrɪsˈteɪʃənz/|/n.|森林化；植树造林；森林覆盖|The process of establishing forests on land that was previously not forested.  It involves planting trees and managing their growth to create a forest ecosystem.  This can be done for various reasons, including conservation, carbon sequestration, and timber production.|例句：The government is investing heavily in forestations projects to combat deforestation. 政府正在大力投资植树造林项目以应对森林砍伐。|例句：Successful forestations requires careful planning and ongoing maintenance.成功的植树造林需要周密的计划和持续的维护。|近义词：afforestation, reforestation|反义词：deforestation
