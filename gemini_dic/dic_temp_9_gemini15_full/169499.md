propagatory: /ˈprɒpəɡətəri/ | adj. | 传播的；繁殖的 | Relating to or promoting propagation or spread.  The word describes something that aids in the spreading or reproduction of something else, be it ideas, plants, or diseases. |例句：The propagatory efforts of the church helped spread Christianity across the continent. 教会的传播努力帮助基督教传遍了整个大陆。 The propagatory system of the plant is quite efficient.  这种植物的繁殖系统非常高效。|近义词：proliferative, spreading, disseminating |反义词：inhibitory, restrictive, suppressive
