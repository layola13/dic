odes:/ˈoʊdz/ | n. |颂歌，诗篇 | A lyric poem, typically one expressing praise of a person or thing.  |Example: Keats' odes are renowned for their beauty and evocative imagery.  济慈的颂歌以其美丽和令人回味的意象而闻名。|近义词：poem, hymn, lyric |反义词：prose (as an antonym of poetic form)
