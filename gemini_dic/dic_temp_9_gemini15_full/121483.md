mislaid:/mɪsˈleɪd/| v.|放错地方；遗失|To have unintentionally put something in a place where one cannot now remember where it is.|I've mislaid my keys; I can't find them anywhere. 我把钥匙放错地方了，到处都找不到。|近义词：misplace, lose|反义词：find, locate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I mislay my glasses frequently.  = 我经常把眼镜放错地方。
He mislays his phone every week. = 他每周都把手机放错地方。


一般过去时:
I mislaid my passport yesterday. = 我昨天把护照放错地方了。
She mislaid her wallet at the market. = 她在市场上把钱包放错地方了。


现在完成时:
I have mislaid my favorite pen. = 我把我最喜欢的钢笔放错地方了。
They have mislaid the important documents. = 他们把重要的文件放错地方了。


过去完成时:
I had mislaid my notes before the exam. = 我考试前就把笔记放错地方了。
She had mislaid her phone before she needed to make a call. = 她需要打电话之前就把手机放错地方了。


将来时:
I think I will mislay my keys again. = 我想我还会再把钥匙放错地方。
He's afraid he will mislay the contract. = 他担心他会把合同放错地方。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't mislaid my book, I would have finished reading it. = 如果我没有把书放错地方，我就已经读完了。
I wish I hadn't mislaid my camera during the trip. = 我真希望旅行期间我没有把相机放错地方。


被动语态:
The documents were mislaid by someone in the office. = 文件被办公室的某人放错地方了。
The report was mislaid, and we couldn't find it. = 报告被放错地方了，我们找不到它。


疑问句:

否定句:

He didn't mislay his keys, he just couldn't find them. = 他没有把钥匙放错地方，只是找不到。
