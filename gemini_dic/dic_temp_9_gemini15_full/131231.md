noncapitalist:/ˌnɑːnˈkæpɪtəlɪst/| adj.|非资本主义的|Not based on or relating to capitalism; characterized by the absence of private ownership of the means of production and distribution.|例句：Many countries adopted noncapitalist economic systems after World War II.  许多国家在二战后采用了非资本主义经济制度。
例句：The debate about the merits of capitalist versus noncapitalist systems continues.  关于资本主义与非资本主义制度优劣的争论仍在继续。|近义词：anticapitalist, socialist, communist|反义词：capitalist|形容词
