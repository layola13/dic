framework:/ˈfreɪmˌwɜːrk/| n.|框架；结构；构架|A basic structure underlying a system, concept, or text.  It is a supporting structure, a set of ideas, or a system of rules that can be used as a basis for something else.|例句：The report provides a framework for future research.  这份报告为未来的研究提供了一个框架。

例句：We need a strong framework to support our new business model. 我们需要一个强大的框架来支撑我们的新商业模式。


近义词：structure, skeleton, system, foundation |反义词：chaos, disorder, randomness|名词
