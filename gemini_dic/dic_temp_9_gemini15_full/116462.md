mateless:/ˈmæt.ləs/| adj.|无伴侣的，孤独的|Without a mate or partner; alone.  The word implies a lack of a romantic partner, but can also be used more broadly to describe a lack of companionship.|例句：He felt mateless and lonely after his wife died. 他妻子去世后感到孤单寂寞。

例句：The cat, mateless and uncared for, wandered the streets.  这只无伴侣且无人照顾的猫在街上游荡。


近义词：lonely, solitary, single, unpaired

反义词：paired, coupled, partnered


词性:形容词
