monarchically:/məˈnɑːrkɪkli/| adv.|君主地；帝王地；专制地|In a manner characteristic of a monarch or monarchy;  with the authority or manner of a king or queen.  It often implies absolute rule or a strong, centralized power structure.|例句：The kingdom was ruled monarchically, with the king holding absolute power.  这个王国实行君主统治，国王拥有绝对权力。

例句：The country's traditions were maintained monarchically, with little input from the people.  这个国家的传统以君主的方式维持，人民很少有发言权。

近义词：royally, imperially, autocratically, despotically

反义词：democratically, republically
