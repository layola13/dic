malware:/ˈmælˌwer/| n.|恶意软件|Malicious software designed to damage or disable computer systems.  It can include viruses, worms, Trojans, spyware, and adware.|例句：The company's network was attacked by malware, causing significant data loss.  这家公司的网络遭到恶意软件攻击，导致大量数据丢失。|近义词：malicious code, virus, spyware, Trojan horse|反义词：antivirus software, beneficial software
