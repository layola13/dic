USAGE->
This word is a noun, and thus doesn't conjugate like verbs.  The usage is primarily descriptive, focusing on how something is presented or understood.  Therefore, tense, voice, and mood are not applicable in the same way as with verbs.  Instead, we can examine how the noun "misframing" is used in different sentence structures.

Example Sentences demonstrating different sentence structures:
