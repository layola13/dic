carbonators:/ˈkɑːrbənˌeɪtərz/| n. |碳酸化器；使碳酸化的装置或物质|A carbonator is a device or substance that adds carbon dioxide to a liquid, making it carbonated.  Carbonators are commonly used in the production of carbonated beverages. |例句：The soda factory uses a large industrial carbonator to add fizz to its drinks.  苏打水厂使用大型工业碳酸化器为饮料添加气泡。

The restaurant uses a small countertop carbonator for making sparkling water. 这家餐厅使用小型台式碳酸化器来制作苏打水。


近义词：carbonation machine, fizz infuser|反义词：decarbonator (removing carbonation)|词性:noun
