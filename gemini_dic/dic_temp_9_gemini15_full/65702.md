event's:/ɪˈvɛnts/ | n. |事件的；活动的 |The possessive form of "event," indicating belonging or possession.  It refers to something that happens, especially something important or significant.  It's often used to describe the qualities or characteristics of an event. |例句：The event's success depended on the weather.  (活动的成功取决于天气。)

The event's impact was significant. (事件的影响是重大的。)
