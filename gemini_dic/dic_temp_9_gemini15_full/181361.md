reinvestigating: /ˌriːɪnˈvɛstɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 重新调查；再调查 |  To investigate something again, typically because of new evidence or doubts about the previous investigation.  | The police are reinvestigating the case due to new evidence.  警方正在重新调查此案，因为出现了新的证据。 The committee decided to reinvestigate the allegations of fraud.  委员会决定重新调查欺诈指控。| investigate, examine, review |  ignore, overlook | 动词
