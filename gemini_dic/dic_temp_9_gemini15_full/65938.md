exactable: /ɪɡˈzæktəbl/ | adj. | 可索取的；可要求的；可强制执行的 |  Capable of being exacted; that can be demanded or enforced.  It implies a right or authority to demand something, often a payment, penalty, or performance. |


例句：

The contract stipulates an exactable penalty for late delivery. = 合同规定了延迟交货的可索取的罚款。

The judge ruled that the debt was exactable. = 法官裁定该债务是可强制执行的。


近义词：demandable, enforceable, collectible, recoverable


反义词：unobtainable, unenforceable, irrecoverable


词性：形容词
