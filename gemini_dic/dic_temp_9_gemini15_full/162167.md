pre-Jewish:/priːˈdʒuːɪʃ/| adj.|犹太人之前的，犹太教之前的|  Describes something that existed or occurred before the establishment or widespread influence of Judaism.  It often refers to historical periods or cultural practices. | Example: Pre-Jewish religious practices in the region are poorly documented.  犹太教之前的该地区宗教习俗记载很少。|近义词：ante-Jewish, pre-Israelite|反义词：post-Jewish
