rhetoric:/ˈretərɪk/| n. |修辞学；修辞手法；雄辩术；空洞的言辞 |The art of effective or persuasive speaking or writing, especially the use of figures of speech and other compositional techniques.  |例句：His speech was full of empty rhetoric.他的演讲充满了空洞的言辞。

例句：The professor's lecture focused on the use of rhetoric in Shakespeare's plays. 教授的讲座重点讲解了莎士比亚戏剧中修辞的运用。


近义词：eloquence, oratory, discourse, persuasion |反义词：silence, honesty(in the context of insincere rhetoric) |词性:名词
