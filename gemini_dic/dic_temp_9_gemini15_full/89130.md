homeboy: /ˈhoʊmˌbɔɪ/ | n. |哥们儿，老乡，朋友 | A male friend or acquaintance, especially one from the same neighborhood or social group.  The term often carries connotations of familiarity, loyalty, and shared background. |例句：He's my homeboy; we grew up together. 他是我的哥们儿，我们一起长大的。


例句： I met my homeboy at the park yesterday.  昨天我在公园遇到了我的哥们儿。


近义词：buddy, pal, friend, dude, man


反义词：stranger, enemy


词性：名词
