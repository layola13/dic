glover:/ˈɡlʌvər/| n. |手套制造者|A glover is a person who makes or sells gloves.  Glovers traditionally work with leather, but now also use other materials such as fabric and synthetics.|例句：My grandfather was a glover, crafting exquisite leather gloves for decades.  我的祖父是一位手套制造商，几十年来一直制作精美的皮手套。|近义词：glovemaker, leatherworker|反义词：无
