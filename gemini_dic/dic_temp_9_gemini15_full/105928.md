klap:/klæp/ | vt. n.|拍，拍手，轻拍|to strike together (as the hands) with a sharp sound; to strike with a flat object|例句：He clapped his hands in delight. 他高兴地拍手。|近义词：slap, pat, clap together|反义词: ignore, neglect.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He claps his hands. = 他拍手。
She claps the child gently. = 她轻轻地拍拍孩子。


一般过去时:
The audience clapped enthusiastically. = 观众热情地鼓掌。
He clapped the dust off his coat. = 他拍掉外套上的灰尘。


现在完成时:
They have clapped their hands for the performance. = 他们为这场演出鼓掌。
She has clapped the baby to sleep. = 她拍着婴儿睡觉。


过去完成时:
He had clapped his hands before the music started. = 音乐开始前他已经鼓掌了。
They had clapped the meeting to an end. = 他们已经宣布会议结束。


将来时:
The audience will clap loudly. = 观众会大声鼓掌。
She will clap her hands if she is happy. = 如果她高兴，她会拍手。


虚拟语气:
If he clapped his hands, the birds might come. = 如果他拍拍手，鸟儿可能会来。
I wish they had clapped more enthusiastically. = 我希望他们能更热情地鼓掌。


被动语态:
The dust was clapped off the table. = 桌子上的灰尘被拍掉了。
His hands were clapped together in prayer. = 他的双手在祈祷中合十。


疑问句:
Did he clap his hands? = 他拍手了吗？
Will they clap at the end of the show? = 节目结束时他们会鼓掌吗？


否定句:
He didn't clap his hands. = 他没有拍手。
She didn't clap the dust off her dress. = 她没有拍掉她裙子上的灰尘。


PHRASE->

round of applause (clap) = 一阵掌声
