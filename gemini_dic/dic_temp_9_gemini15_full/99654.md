interleaving:/ˌɪntərˈliːvɪŋ/ | n. | 交错；穿插；(信息)交织 | The act of arranging or placing things in alternating or overlapping positions.  It often refers to the mixing of different types of information or tasks during learning or study, to improve retention and understanding. | Example: Interleaving different subjects during study sessions can boost learning outcomes.  交错不同的科目进行学习可以提高学习成果。|近义词：mixing, shuffling, alternating |反义词：segregating, separating |名词
