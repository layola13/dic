overwatering: /ˌoʊvərˈwɔːtəriŋ/ | v. |过度浇水 | The act of watering plants excessively, leading to negative consequences such as root rot and plant death.  It involves supplying more water than the plants can absorb and utilize. |例句：Overwatering your seedlings can kill them.  过度浇水会杀死你的幼苗。

例句：The wilting plants were a clear sign of overwatering.  枯萎的植物是过度浇水的明显迹象。


近义词：waterlogging, inundation, drenching


反义词： underwatering, dehydration


词性：动名词 (gerund)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overwaters his plants every week. = 他每周都过度浇水。
They overwater the lawn, leading to fungal growth. = 他们过度浇灌草坪，导致真菌滋生。


一般过去时:
I overwatered my orchids last week, and now they're dying. = 我上周给我的兰花浇了太多的水，现在它们快死了。
She admitted that she overwatered the tomatoes. = 她承认她给西红柿浇了太多的水。


现在完成时:

We have overwatered the potted plants, resulting in soggy soil. = 我们给盆栽植物浇了太多的水，导致土壤过于潮湿。


过去完成时:
The gardener had overwatered the roses before the storm. = 园丁在暴风雨来临之前给玫瑰花浇了太多的水。
Because he had overwatered the seedlings, they rotted. = 因为他给幼苗浇了太多的水，所以它们烂掉了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
