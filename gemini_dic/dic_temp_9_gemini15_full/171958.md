puppyishly:/ˈpʌpiʃli/| adv. |像小狗一样地；天真地；幼稚地 |In a manner characteristic of a puppy; playfully; childishly or immaturely.  |例句：He puppyishly wagged his tail after getting a treat. 他得到奖励后像小狗一样摇着尾巴。

例句：She puppyishly begged her mother for another cookie. 她像个孩子一样央求妈妈再给她一块饼干。


近义词：childishly, playfully, naively, innocently

反义词：maturely, seriously, gravely, solemnly

词性:副词
