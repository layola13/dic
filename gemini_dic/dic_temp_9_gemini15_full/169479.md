propagandism:/ˌprɒpəˈɡændɪzəm/| n. |宣传；宣传活动；鼓吹|The systematic dissemination of information, often biased or misleading, to promote a particular political cause or point of view.  It involves techniques designed to influence public opinion and behavior. |例句：The government's propagandism during the war was effective in rallying public support.  政府在战争期间的宣传有效地争取了公众的支持。|近义词：propaganda, indoctrination, brainwashing |反义词：truth, objectivity, factual reporting
