grounders:/ˈɡraʊndərz/| n.|落地球|A grounder is a batted ball in baseball or softball that stays low to the ground.  It is typically hit softly and does not travel far. |例句：The batter hit a weak grounder to the shortstop.  击球手击出了一个软弱的落地球到游击手那里。

The shortstop fielded a sharp grounder and threw to first for the out.  游击手接到了一个强劲的落地球，然后传到一垒封杀出局。|近义词：ground ball|反义词：fly ball, pop fly|词性：名词
