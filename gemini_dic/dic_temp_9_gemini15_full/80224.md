godson:/ˈɡɒdˌsʌn/| n. |教子，教男|A godson is a male child who has been given a name at a christening ceremony by a person who is not the child’s parent.  This person is known as the godfather.|He is my godson, and I'm very proud of him. 他是我的教子，我为他感到非常骄傲。|He's been a good godfather to his godson.|近义词：spiritual son|反义词：goddaughter


USAGE->

He is my godson. = 他是我的教子。
My godfather gives me gifts every year. = 我的教父每年都送我礼物。


一般过去时:
He became my godson when I was a child. = 我小时候他成为我的教子。
My godfather helped me with my college application. = 我的教父帮我填写大学申请。
