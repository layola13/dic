selectees:/ˌsɪˈlektɪz/| n. |被选中者，当选者 |Those who have been selected, chosen, or picked for something.  The term often implies a group selected from a larger pool of candidates. |例句：The selectees for the scholarship program were notified by email.  被选中参加奖学金项目的学者们已通过电子邮件收到通知。

The selectees for the Olympic team underwent rigorous training. 奥运代表队的入选者接受了严格的训练。


近义词：chosen ones, appointees, candidates (in the context of having been selected), nominees |反义词：rejects, those who were not selected |词性:n.
