inappreciation:/ˌɪnæˌpriːʃiˈeɪʃən/| n. |不领情；不感激；漠视|Lack of appreciation or gratitude; disregard.  The feeling of not being appreciated or valued. |例句：His inappreciation of her efforts led to a bitter argument. 他的不领情导致了一场激烈的争吵。

例句：The company's inappreciation of its employees' hard work resulted in high turnover. 公司对员工辛勤工作的漠视导致了高员工流失率。

近义词：ungratefulness, ingratitude, disregard, unconcern

反义词：appreciation, gratitude, thankfulness, recognition
