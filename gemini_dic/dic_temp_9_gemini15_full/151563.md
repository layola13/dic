part-way:/ˈpɑːrtweɪ/| adj.|部分地，中途|Partially; to some extent but not completely.|例句：The project is only part-way complete.  项目只完成了一部分。|例句：We're part-way through the journey. 我们旅程进行到中途。|近义词：partially, halfway, incompletely|反义词：completely, fully, entirely|形容词


USAGE->
