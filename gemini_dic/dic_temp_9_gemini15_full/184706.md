resubmission:/ˌriːsʌbˈmɪʃən/| n. |重新提交，再次提交 |The act of submitting something again, typically after it has been rejected or found to be incomplete.  This often involves correcting errors or addressing deficiencies in the original submission. |例句：The resubmission of the project proposal is due next week.  下周项目建议书需要重新提交。


例句：After receiving feedback, she made some changes and submitted a resubmission of her application.  在收到反馈后，她做了一些修改并重新提交了申请。


近义词：re-submission, second submission, renewed submission | 反义词：withdrawal, rejection | 词性：名词
