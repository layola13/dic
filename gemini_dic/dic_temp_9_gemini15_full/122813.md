moldy:/ˈmoʊldi/| adj.|发霉的，霉烂的|Covered with or affected by mold; musty.  The bread is moldy and should be thrown away.|近义词：mildewed, mildewy, musty, fusty|反义词：fresh, clean


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The bread is moldy. = 面包发霉了。
The cheese smells moldy. = 奶酪闻起来发霉了。


一般过去时:
The fruit was moldy. = 水果发霉了。
The basement was moldy after the flood. = 洪水过后，地下室发霉了。


现在完成时:
The food has become moldy. = 食物已经发霉了。
The clothes have gone moldy in the damp cupboard. = 湿漉漉的橱柜里的衣服发霉了。


过去完成时:
The bread had already gone moldy before we could eat it. = 我们还没来得及吃，面包就已经发霉了。
The book had been moldy for years. = 那本书已经发霉好多年了。


将来时:
The apples will be moldy if we don't eat them soon. = 如果我们不尽快吃掉苹果，它们就会发霉。
The clothes will become moldy in this humidity. = 在这种潮湿的环境下，衣服会发霉。


虚拟语气:
If the food weren't moldy, we could eat it. = 如果食物没有发霉，我们就可以吃了。
I wish the shoes weren't moldy. = 我真希望鞋子没发霉。


被动语态:
The walls were found to be moldy. = 发现墙壁发霉了。
The old books are being checked for moldy. = 正在检查旧书是否发霉。


疑问句:
Is the bread moldy? = 面包发霉了吗？
Are the apples moldy? = 苹果发霉了吗？


否定句:
The fruit isn't moldy. = 水果没有发霉。
The cake isn't moldy, it's just old. = 蛋糕没发霉，只是放久了。
