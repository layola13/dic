frolicly:/ˈfrɑːləkli/ | adv. |嬉戏地；欢乐地；愉快地 |In a frolicsome or playful manner; merrily.  It describes an action or state characterized by lightheartedness, fun, and playfulness. |例句：The children frolicked merrily in the park. 孩子们在公园里快乐地嬉戏。

The puppies frolicked playfully in the garden. 小狗们在花园里嬉戏玩耍。


近义词：playfully, merrily, joyfully, sportively, gaily

反义词：seriously, solemnly, gravely, sadly, morosely

词性：副词


词源:  The word "frolicly" derives from the Middle English word "frolicken," meaning "to play," which in turn is of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old French words suggesting leaping or dancing.  The suffix "-ly" is a common adverbial suffix in English, indicating manner or way.
