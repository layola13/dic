mellifluently:/ˈmɛlɪfluːəntli/ | adv. |流畅地，甜美地，悦耳地 |  characterized by sweetness or smoothness of sound; sweetly or smoothly flowing. It combines the sense of "honey-sweet" (from "mellifluous") with the adverbial suffix "-ly".  |  He spoke mellifluently, his words flowing like honey. 他说话流畅甜美，字句如蜜般流淌。 | 近义词：smoothly, sweetly, eloquently | 反义词：harshly, roughly, awkwardly
