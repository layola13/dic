phlebotomists:/flɛˈbɑːtəmɪsts/| n. |采血员，静脉切开术者 | A phlebotomist is a healthcare professional who is trained to draw blood from patients. They work in various healthcare settings, such as hospitals, clinics, and blood banks.  |The phlebotomist skillfully drew my blood.  采血员熟练地为我抽血。| blood collector, venipuncturist |  |名词
