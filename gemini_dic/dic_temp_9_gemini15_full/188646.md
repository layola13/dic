rummaging:/ˈrʌmɪdʒɪŋ/| v.|翻找，搜寻|To search unsystematically and thoroughly through something.|例句：She was rummaging in her handbag for her keys. 她正在翻找手提包找钥匙。|例句：He spent the morning rummaging through old boxes in the attic. 他上午花时间在阁楼上翻找旧箱子。|近义词：searching, hunting, exploring|反义词：organizing, tidying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rummages through his pockets every morning. = 他每天早上都在翻找他的口袋。
She rummages in her drawers for a suitable outfit. = 她在抽屉里翻找合适的衣服。

一般过去时:
I rummaged through the cupboard for a snack. = 我翻找橱柜找零食。
They rummaged in the attic for old photographs. = 他们在阁楼上翻找旧照片。

现在完成时:
He has rummaged through all the boxes but hasn't found it yet. = 他已经翻遍了所有箱子，但还没找到。
She has rummaged through her closet and found a dress for the party. = 她翻遍了衣橱，找到一件参加派对的衣服。

过去完成时:
I had rummaged through the entire house before I found my lost keys. = 我翻遍了整个房子才找到我丢失的钥匙。
They had rummaged through their belongings before they moved to the new house. = 他们在搬到新家之前翻找过他们的东西。

将来时:
I will rummage through the garage tomorrow to find the old tools. = 我明天将翻找车库寻找旧工具。
She will rummage through the boxes after she finishes her work. = 她完成工作后会翻找箱子。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rummage through the library archives. = 如果我有更多时间，我会翻找图书馆档案。
I wish I could rummage through the antique shop without spending any money. = 我希望我能翻找古董店而不用花钱。

被动语态:
The drawers were rummaged through by the thief. = 抽屉被小偷翻找过。
The attic is being rummaged through for the missing documents. = 阁楼正在被翻找以寻找丢失的文件。

疑问句:
Are you rummaging through my things again? = 你又在翻找我的东西了吗？
Was he rummaging through the trash can? = 他在翻找垃圾桶吗？

否定句:
I didn't rummage through your suitcase. = 我没有翻找你的手提箱。
He doesn't usually rummage through his desk. = 他通常不翻找他的办公桌。


PHRASE->
rummage through sth: 翻找某物

rummage for sth: 搜寻某物
rummage sale: 旧货出售
rummage in: 在...里翻找
