mckinley:/məˈkɪnli/| n. |麦金利（人名，山名）| McKinley is a common surname and also the name of a mountain in North America.  It can refer to William McKinley, the 25th president of the United States, or Mount McKinley (now Denali), the highest peak in North America. | 例句：He shares his name with President McKinley. 他和麦金利总统同名。

例句：Mount McKinley is a breathtaking sight. 麦金利山景色壮观。
