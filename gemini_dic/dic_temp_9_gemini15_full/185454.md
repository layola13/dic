retweeted:/ˌriːˈtwiːtɪd/| v. |转发（推文）|To share someone else's tweet on your own Twitter feed.  This action spreads the original tweet to a wider audience.  It is a core function of the Twitter platform.|例句：I retweeted the article because it was so informative. 我转发了这篇文章，因为它很有信息量。
例句：She retweeted her friend's post about the concert. 她转发了她朋友关于音乐会的帖子。|近义词：reshared, shared, forwarded|反义词：deleted, ignored


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He retweets interesting articles daily. 他每天都转发有趣的文章。
They retweet news from reliable sources. 他们转发来自可靠来源的新闻。

一般过去时:
I retweeted your post yesterday. 我昨天转发了你的帖子。
She retweeted the announcement about the event. 她转发了关于该活动的公告。

现在完成时:
I have retweeted many posts this week. 我本周已经转发了很多帖子。
He has retweeted all of his favorite tweets. 他已经转发了他所有喜欢的推文。

过去完成时:
She had retweeted the breaking news before I saw it. 我看到之前她就转发了这条突发新闻。
They had retweeted the video multiple times before it went viral.  在视频爆红之前，他们已经转发了多次。


将来时:
I will retweet your tweet if it's relevant. 如果你的推文相关，我会转发。
They will retweet anything related to their business.他们会转发任何与他们业务相关的东西。

虚拟语气:
If I had seen the tweet earlier, I would have retweeted it. 如果我早点看到这条推文，我就会转发它。
I wish I had retweeted that inspiring story. 我真希望我转发了那个励志的故事。

被动语态:
The tweet was retweeted thousands of times. 这条推文被转发了数千次。
His post was retweeted by many influential people. 他的帖子被许多有影响力的人转发。

疑问句:
Have you retweeted that video yet? 你转发那个视频了吗？
Will you retweet my latest post? 你会转发我的最新帖子吗？

否定句:
I didn't retweet the controversial tweet. 我没有转发那条有争议的推文。
They haven't retweeted anything today. 他们今天什么都没转发。
