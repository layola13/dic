leadenly:/ˈlɛdənli/| adv.|迟缓地；沉重地；阴沉地|In a slow, heavy, or dull manner; sluggishly; gloomily.  The word evokes a sense of weight, slowness, and often depression.|例句：He moved leadenly through the mud. 他在泥泞中缓慢地移动。


例句：The atmosphere in the room felt leadenly oppressive.  房间里的气氛令人感到沉重压抑。


近义词：sluggishly, heavily, slowly, gloomily, dully


反义词：briskly, quickly, lightly, brightly, cheerfully


词性：副词
