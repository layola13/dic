igloo:/ˈɪɡluː/| n. |冰屋，雪屋 |A dome-shaped house built of blocks of hard-packed snow.  Igloos are traditionally built by Inuit and other Arctic peoples. |例句：The Inuit built a sturdy igloo to shelter them from the blizzard. 因纽特人建造了一个坚固的冰屋来躲避暴风雪。  They lived in igloos for generations. 他们世世代代住着冰屋。|近义词：snow house, winter dwelling |反义词：tent, house (in a warmer climate)
