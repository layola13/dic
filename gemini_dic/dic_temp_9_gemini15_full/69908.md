fertilizes:/ˈfɜːrtɪlaɪzɪz/| vt.|施肥；使肥沃；受精 |To enrich (soil) with nutrients to promote growth. To make fertile or productive. To unite male and female germ cells, resulting in fertilization.|例句：The farmer fertilizes his fields every spring.  农民每年春天都给他的田地施肥。

The pollen fertilizes the ovules. 花粉使胚珠受精。


近义词：enrich, impregnate, inseminate |反义词：sterilize, deplete
