perform:/pərˈfɔːrm/| vt.  |表演；履行；执行；表现|To carry out, accomplish, or fulfill an action, task, or function.  It can also refer to the act of presenting something, such as a play or musical piece, to an audience.|例句：The magician performed amazing tricks. 魔术师表演了令人惊叹的魔术。
The engineers performed a thorough analysis of the bridge. 工程师们对桥梁进行了彻底的分析。|近义词：execute, accomplish, achieve, enact, present|反义词：fail, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He performs on stage every night. 他每晚都在舞台上表演。
She performs her duties diligently. 她勤勤恳恳地履行职责。

一般过去时:
The band performed a new song last night. 乐队昨晚演奏了一首新歌。

现在完成时:
They have performed many experiments. 他们已经做了很多实验。

词源:
The word "perform" ultimately comes from the Latin word "performare," which is a combination of "per-" (through, thoroughly) and "formare" (to form, shape, or make).  This reflects the original meaning of carrying something out completely or shaping something into its final form.
