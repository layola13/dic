relapsing:/rɪˈlæpsɪŋ/| adj.|复发的；反复的|Recurring or tending to recur after a period of improvement or remission, especially of a disease.|The patient experienced a relapsing fever.  这位病人经历了复发性发热。|She suffered from relapsing depression. 她患有反复发作的抑郁症。|recurring, intermittent, recurrent|remitting, improving, healing|形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The disease is relapsing. = 这种疾病正在复发。
His symptoms are relapsing. = 他的症状正在复发。

一般过去时:
The infection relapses last week. = 感染上周复发了。
Her pain relapses yesterday. = 她的疼痛昨天复发了。

现在完成时:
The patient has experienced several relapsing episodes. = 病人经历了几次复发。
His condition has been relapsing for months. = 他的病情已经复发了几个月。

过去完成时:
He had experienced a relapsing before the treatment. = 他在治疗前曾复发过。
They had several relapsing episodes during the year. = 他们在这一年里经历了几次复发。


将来时:
The doctor fears the disease will relapse. = 医生担心这种疾病会复发。
We are worried that his condition will relapse again. = 我们担心他的病情会再次复发。

虚拟语气:
If the symptoms relapse, we'll adjust the treatment. = 如果症状复发，我们将调整治疗方案。
I wish his condition wouldn't relapse. = 我希望他的病情不会复发。


被动语态:
The disease is often reported as relapsing. = 这种疾病经常被报道为复发性疾病。
The case was identified as a relapsing type. = 此病例被鉴定为复发型。

疑问句:
Is the condition relapsing again? = 病情又复发了？
Are his symptoms relapsing? = 他的症状正在复发吗？

否定句:
The disease is not relapsing. = 这种疾病没有复发。
His pain is not relapsing. = 他的疼痛没有复发。
