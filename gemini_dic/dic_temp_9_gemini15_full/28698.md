carlist:/ˈkɑːrlɪst/ | n. | 汽车清单；车辆目录 | A carlist is a list of cars, often used in a specific context like a dealership's inventory or a rental company's fleet.  It might include details such as make, model, year, and condition. | Example: The dealership provided a carlist showing all available vehicles.  这家经销商提供了一份汽车清单，显示所有可用的车辆。 |近义词：vehicle list, car inventory, automobile catalog | 反义词：无
