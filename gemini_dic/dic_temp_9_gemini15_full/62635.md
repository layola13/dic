enforest:/ɪnˈfɒrɪst/|/ˈɛnfɔːrɪst/| vt.|使…成森林；使…长满树木|To convert (land) into forest; to plant trees on (land) to create a forest.|例句：The government is planning to enforest large areas of the country. 政府计划将该国大片土地改造成森林。

例句：They are enforesting the barren hillsides.  他们正在使荒凉的山坡长满树木。


近义词：afforest, reforest|反义词：deforest


词性：动词
