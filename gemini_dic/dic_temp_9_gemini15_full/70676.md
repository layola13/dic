final:/ˈfaɪnəl/| adj.|最终的；最后的；决定性的| final means coming at the end of a series or process. It can also describe something that is conclusive or decisive.|例句：This is the final chapter of the book. 这是这本书的最后一章。
例句：The final decision rests with the manager. 最终决定权在经理手中。|近义词：ultimate, last, concluding, decisive|反义词：initial, preliminary, first, beginning
