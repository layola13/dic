partitionary:/pɑːrˈtɪʃənəri/ | adj. | 分割的；划分性的；隔间的 | Relating to or characterized by partition or division.  The term often implies a process of dividing something into separate parts, often with the implication of conflict or inequality in the distribution of resources or power.  | Example: The partitionary policies of the colonial government led to widespread resentment.  殖民政府的分裂政策导致了广泛的不满。 The proposed partitionary agreement was rejected by both sides. 提出的分割协议遭到双方拒绝。 |近义词：divisive, separatist, segmental | 反义词：unifying, integrative, holistic
