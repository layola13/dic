plaguer:/ˈpleɪɡər/| n. v.|瘟疫；灾难；折磨；使苦恼|To inflict great suffering or distress on; to cause widespread trouble or distress.|The plague ravaged the city, killing thousands. 瘟疫肆虐城市，造成数千人死亡。The constant noise plagued her all day. 持续的噪音整天折磨着她。|近义词：afflict, torment, beset, scourge|反义词：bless, soothe, comfort


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The drought plagues the farmers. = 干旱折磨着农民。
The mosquitoes plague the tourists. = 蚊子困扰着游客。


一般过去时:
The locusts plagued the crops last year. = 蝗虫去年摧毁了庄稼。
Financial problems plagued the company for years. =  多年来，财务问题一直困扰着这家公司。


现在完成时:
Droughts have plagued this region for decades. = 几十年来，干旱一直困扰着这个地区。
Worries about the future have plagued him since his illness. = 自从生病以来，对未来的担忧一直困扰着他。


过去完成时:
The army had been plagued by disease before the battle. = 在战斗之前，军队一直饱受疾病的困扰。
He had been plagued by self-doubt for many years. = 多年来，他一直被自我怀疑所困扰。


将来时:
Climate change will plague coastal communities. = 气候变化将困扰沿海社区。
I fear that this problem will plague us for years to come. = 我担心这个问题将在未来几年困扰我们。


虚拟语气:
If only this constant noise wouldn't plague me. = 要是这持续的噪音不会困扰我就好了。
If the drought had not plagued the land, we would have had a bountiful harvest. = 如果干旱没有侵害这片土地，我们就会有丰收。


被动语态:
The village was plagued by bandits. = 村庄被强盗骚扰。
The project was plagued by delays. = 项目被延误所困扰。


疑问句:
What plagues you so? = 什么事让你如此苦恼？
What plagues the country's economy? = 什么困扰着国家的经济？


否定句:
The new system wasn't plagued by the same problems as the old one. = 新系统没有像旧系统那样受到同样的问题困扰。
Thankfully, the project wasn't plagued by significant delays. = 谢天谢地，该项目没有受到严重的延误。

plague of rats = 鼠患
plague of insects = 虫害
