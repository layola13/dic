shuttling: /ˈʃʌtəlɪŋ/ | v. | 穿梭；往返运送；频繁往返 |  Moving back and forth repeatedly; transporting something repeatedly between two places.  | 例句：The shuttle bus was constantly shuttling between the airport and the city center. = 机场和市中心之间不断有班车往返穿梭。

The factory workers are shuttling materials between the different production lines. = 工人们在不同的生产线之间来回运送材料。


近义词：transporting, conveying, ferrying, traversing


反义词：staying, remaining, idling

词源:
The word "shuttling" comes from the word "shuttle," which originally referred to a device used in weaving.  The back-and-forth motion of the shuttle in weaving gave rise to the broader meaning of moving back and forth repeatedly.  The ultimate origin is from the Old English word "scyttel."
