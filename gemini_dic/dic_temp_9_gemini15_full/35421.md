co-ordination: /ˌkoʊˌɔːrdɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |协调，配合，调和 |The act or process of coordinating or being coordinated.  It refers to the harmonious functioning of parts or the organization of different elements to achieve a common goal.  This can apply to various contexts, from the physical coordination of movements to the organizational coordination of tasks and people. |例句：The dancers showed excellent coordination in their performance. 舞蹈演员在表演中展现了极佳的协调性。

The successful project required careful coordination between different departments.  项目的成功需要不同部门之间的仔细协调。

近义词：collaboration, cooperation, harmony, integration |反义词：disorganization, chaos, discord


词源:  From the Old French *coordination, from Latin *coordinatio, from *coordinare "to arrange together," from com- "together" + ordinare "to arrange, order."
