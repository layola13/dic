pedalled: /ˈpɛdəld/ | v. |踩踏；骑（自行车）| To operate a pedal or pedals; to ride a bicycle. |例句：He pedalled his bicycle up the hill. 他骑自行车上了山。  She pedalled furiously to escape the storm. 她拼命地踩踏自行车以躲避暴风雨。|近义词：rode, cycled, wheeled|反义词：walked


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pedals his bike to work every day. = 他每天骑自行车上班。
She pedals slowly up the steep incline. = 她慢慢地踩着自行车上陡坡。

一般过去时:
I pedalled as fast as I could. = 我尽可能快地踩踏。
They pedalled through the countryside. = 他们骑车穿过乡下。

现在完成时:
He has pedalled over 100 miles this week. = 他这周已经骑了100多英里。
She has pedalled to the top of the mountain. = 她已经骑车到达山顶。

过去完成时:
I had pedalled for hours before I reached the village. = 我骑了几个小时才到达村庄。
They had pedalled all day and were exhausted. = 他们骑了一整天，筋疲力尽了。

将来时:
I will pedal to the store after lunch. = 我午饭后将骑车去商店。
We will pedal along the coast tomorrow. = 我们明天将沿着海岸骑车。

虚拟语气:
If I had a bike, I would pedal to work. = 如果我有一辆自行车，我会骑车上班。
I wish I could pedal faster. = 我希望我能骑得更快。

被动语态:
The bicycle was pedalled hard. = 自行车被用力地踩踏。
The route was rarely pedalled by tourists. = 这条路线很少被游客骑车经过。

疑问句:
Do you pedal to work? = 你骑车上班吗？
How fast can you pedal? = 你能骑多快？

否定句:
He didn't pedal fast enough to win the race. = 他骑得不够快，所以没有赢得比赛。
She doesn't pedal her bike often. = 她不经常骑自行车。
