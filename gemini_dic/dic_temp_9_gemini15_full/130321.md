non-territorial: /ˌnɒnˌtɛrɪˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. | 非领土的，不占有领地的 | Not associated with or confined to a particular territory or area; not possessive or protective of one's space or belongings.  |  例句：Many animals, such as many bird species, exhibit non-territorial behavior. 许多动物，例如许多鸟类，表现出非领地行为。

例句：The nomadic lifestyle is inherently non-territorial. 游牧的生活方式本质上是非领土的。


近义词：unattached, itinerant, nomadic, wandering


反义词：territorial, possessive, sedentary


词性: adjective
