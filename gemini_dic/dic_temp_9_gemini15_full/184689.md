restyling:/ˌriːˈstaɪlɪŋ/ | n. | 重塑风格；重新设计；改头换面 | The act of giving something a new style or appearance.  It involves making significant changes to the design, look, or feel of something, often to improve its appeal or functionality. |例句：The company is undergoing a restyling of its brand image. 公司正在对其品牌形象进行重新设计。

例句：The car's restyling included a new grille and headlights. 这款车的重新设计包括新的格栅和车灯。


近义词：redesign, makeover, revamp, refurbishment, rebranding | 反义词：leaving as is, maintaining the status quo | 词性：名词
