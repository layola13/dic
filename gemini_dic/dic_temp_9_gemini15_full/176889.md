reagitate: /ˌriˈæɡɪteɪt/ | vt. | 使再次激动；使重新骚动；重新激起 | To stir up again; to excite or agitate anew.  | The news reagitated his already fragile emotions.  这条新闻再次激起了他本已脆弱的情绪。|近义词：re-excite, rekindle, stir up | 反义词：calm, soothe, pacify | 动词
