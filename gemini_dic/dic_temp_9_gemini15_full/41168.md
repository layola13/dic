copycatism: /ˈkɒpikætɪzəm/| n. |模仿行为；抄袭习气 |The act of copying someone else's work or ideas, often without attribution; the tendency to imitate others.  |例句：The rampant copycatism in the fashion industry is stifling creativity.时装界的猖獗模仿行为扼杀了创造力。
例句：His success was attributed to his talent, not to copycatism. 他的成功归功于他的天赋，而不是模仿。 |近义词：imitation, plagiarism, mimicry |反义词：originality, innovation
