magilp:/ˈmædʒɪlp/| n. |油画颜料调和剂|A viscous, oily mixture used by artists to thin oil paints and improve their flow and consistency. Magilp is traditionally made from linseed oil, mastic varnish, and sometimes turpentine.  It enhances the blending of colors and creates a smoother painting surface. However, it can also lead to cracking or yellowing over time. |Example: The artist used magilp to thin the heavy oil paint and achieve a more fluid application.|近义词：oil medium, paint thinner|反义词：None specifically, but thickeners are the opposite effect.
