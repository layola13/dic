sauropoda:/sɔːˈrɒpədə/| n. |蜥脚类恐龙|Sauropoda is a clade of saurischian dinosaurs.  They were quadrupedal herbivores characterized by their extremely long necks and tails, small heads, and pillar-like legs.  They are among the largest animals to have ever lived. |例句：The discovery of new sauropod fossils has significantly advanced our understanding of their evolution.  发现新的蜥脚类恐龙化石极大地提高了我们对其进化过程的理解。

例句：Many sauropods, like Brachiosaurus, were gigantic creatures. 许多蜥脚类恐龙，例如腕龙，都是巨大的生物。
