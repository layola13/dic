declutter:/dɪˈklʌtər/| vt.|整理；清除杂物|To remove unnecessary items from (a place or thing) in order to make it more organized and efficient.  |Example: I spent the weekend decluttering my garage.  我花了整个周末整理车库。|近义词：tidy, organize, clear out, straighten up|反义词：clutter, mess up


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I declutter my closet every spring. = 我每年春天都会整理我的衣橱。
She declutters her desk before starting work. = 她开始工作前会整理她的办公桌。


一般过去时:
He decluttered his apartment last month. = 他上个月整理了他的公寓。
We decluttered the attic over the weekend. = 我们周末整理了阁楼。


现在完成时:
I have decluttered my home several times this year. = 今年我已经整理了几次我的家。
She has decluttered her life by focusing on what's important. = 她通过专注于重要的事情来简化她的生活。


过去完成时:
They had decluttered the house before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经整理了房子。
We had decluttered the storage room before the move. = 我们搬家之前已经整理了储藏室。


将来时:
I will declutter my office tomorrow. = 我明天将整理我的办公室。
They will declutter the garage next weekend. = 他们下周末将整理车库。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would declutter my entire house. = 如果我有更多时间，我会整理我的整个房子。
I wish I could declutter my mind as easily as I declutter my home. = 我希望我能像整理家一样轻松地整理思绪。


被动语态:
The house was decluttered before the open house. = 公开看房之前，房子已经整理过了。
My desk is being decluttered right now. = 我的办公桌现在正在被整理。


疑问句:
Have you decluttered your basement yet? = 你已经整理你的地下室了吗？
Will you declutter the kitchen after dinner? = 晚饭后你会整理厨房吗？


否定句:
I haven't decluttered my room yet. = 我还没整理我的房间。
He didn't declutter his office before the meeting. = 他在会议前没有整理他的办公室。
