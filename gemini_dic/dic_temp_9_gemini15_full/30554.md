cephalotomy:/ˌsɛfəˈlɑːtəmi/| n. |颅脑切开术|Surgical incision of the skull.  This procedure is used in various contexts, including to relieve intracranial pressure, access the brain for surgery, or as part of a difficult delivery. |Example:  The surgeon performed a cephalotomy to remove a blood clot from the patient's brain. = 外科医生进行了颅脑切开术以去除病人脑中的血凝块。


Example: In cases of severe fetal distress, a cephalotomy might be considered as a last resort to deliver the baby. = 在胎儿严重窘迫的情况下，颅脑切开术可能会被考虑作为分娩的最后手段。
