moorage:/ˈmʊərɪdʒ/| n.|系泊；停泊处；泊位费|The act of mooring a vessel; a place for mooring vessels; the fee charged for mooring.|例句：The boat requires secure moorage in the harbor. 这艘船需要在港口安全系泊。

例句：The moorage fees at this marina are quite high. 这个码头的系泊费相当高。


近义词：berthing, docking, anchoring|反义词：unmooring, setting sail|词性：名词
