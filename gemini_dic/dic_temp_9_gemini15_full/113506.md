m-commerce:/ˈɛmˈkɑːmɜːrs/|/ˈɛmˈkɒmɜːs/| n. |移动商务|Mobile commerce, the buying and selling of goods and services through wireless handheld devices such as mobile phones and personal digital assistants (PDAs).  It encompasses a wide range of activities, including online shopping, mobile banking, and mobile payments.|Example: M-commerce is rapidly growing in popularity, with more and more consumers using their smartphones to make purchases.|近义词：mobile shopping, wireless commerce|反义词：e-commerce (in the sense that it's a subset of e-commerce)|名词
