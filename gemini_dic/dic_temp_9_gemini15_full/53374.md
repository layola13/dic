disaggregate: /dɪˈsæɡrɪɡeɪt/ | vt. |分解；细分；分开|To separate or break down into smaller, constituent parts.  The process of separating aggregated data into its component parts. |例句：The researchers disaggregated the data by age and gender. 研究人员按年龄和性别对数据进行了细分。

例句：We need to disaggregate the sales figures by region to understand the market better.我们需要按地区细分销售数据以更好地了解市场。


近义词：decompose, separate, break down, dismember, analyze

反义词：aggregate, combine, unify, consolidate

词性：动词
