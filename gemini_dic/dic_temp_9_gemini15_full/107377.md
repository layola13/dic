lamentation:/ˌlæmənˈteɪʃən/| n.| 悲叹；哀歌；哭诉 | The expression of great sorrow; a mournful utterance.  It can refer to a song, poem, or other expression of grief. |例句：Her lamentation over the loss of her dog was heartbreaking.  她的狗死了，她悲痛欲绝。

例句：The poem is a powerful lamentation for the fallen soldiers. 这首诗是对阵亡士兵的强烈哀悼。


近义词：mourning, wailing, weeping, lament, dirge


反义词：celebration, rejoicing, jubilation


词性:名词
