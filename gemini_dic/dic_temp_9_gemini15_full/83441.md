habitat:/ˈhæbɪtæt/ | n. |栖息地，生境 |The natural environment in which an organism lives.  It includes all the biotic and abiotic factors that influence the organism's survival and reproduction.  |Example: The rainforest is a habitat for many endangered species.  热带雨林是许多濒危物种的栖息地。

The polar bear's natural habitat is the Arctic. 北极熊的自然栖息地是北极。|近义词：environment, dwelling, home |反义词：N/A |名词

一般现在时:
The koala's habitat is the eucalyptus forest. 考拉的栖息地是桉树林。
Their natural habitat is threatened by deforestation. 他们的自然栖息地受到森林砍伐的威胁。

一般过去时:
The wolves' habitat was destroyed by human activity. 狼的栖息地被人类活动破坏了。
Their habitat was once much larger than it is today. 他们的栖息地曾经比现在大得多。

现在完成时:
Humans have significantly altered many animal habitats. 人类已经显著地改变了许多动物的栖息地。
The construction of dams has fragmented the river's habitat. 水坝的建设使河流的栖息地支离破碎。

过去完成时:
Before the fire, the birds' habitat had been lush and green. 在火灾发生之前，鸟类的栖息地曾经郁郁葱葱。
The area had once been a thriving habitat for various species. 这个地区曾经是各种物种繁荣的栖息地。

将来时:
Climate change will drastically alter many habitats. 气候变化将极大地改变许多栖息地。
Their habitat will be protected by a new conservation program. 他们的栖息地将受到一项新的保护计划的保护。

虚拟语气:
If we continue to pollute, many habitats would be destroyed. 如果我们继续污染，许多栖息地将会被破坏。
I wish the destruction of their habitat could be reversed. 我希望他们的栖息地破坏能够得到扭转。

被动语态:
Many habitats are being destroyed by human activities. 许多栖息地正在被人类活动破坏。
The habitat was carefully restored after the oil spill. 石油泄漏后，栖息地得到了仔细的修复。

疑问句:
What is the habitat of the giant panda? 大熊猫的栖息地是什么？
Where is the most suitable habitat for this plant?  这种植物最合适的栖息地在哪里？

否定句:
This area is not a suitable habitat for these animals. 这个地区不适合这些动物居住。
They do not have the resources to protect their habitat. 他们没有资源来保护他们的栖息地。

词源:

The word "habitat" comes from the Latin verb "habitare," meaning "to dwell" or "to inhabit."  It entered English in the 18th century.
