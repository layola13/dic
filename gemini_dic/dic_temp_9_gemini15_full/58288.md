drupelet:/ˈdruːplət/| n. |小核果；核果小单位|A drupelet is a small, fleshy fruit with a hard stone containing a single seed, typically one of many clustered together to form an aggregate fruit.  For example, raspberries and blackberries are composed of many drupelets.|The raspberry is an aggregate fruit made up of numerous drupelets.|近义词：drupe (but a single unit, not a cluster)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a simple fruit like a berry that isn't an aggregate of smaller units.|名词
