inconsistently: /ˌɪnkənˈsɪstəntli/ | adv. | 不一致地；反复无常地 |  Acting or happening in a way that is not consistent; erratically or irregularly.  | 例句：He inconsistently applied the rules, causing confusion. 他前后不一致地适用规则，造成了混乱。  | 例句：The quality of the product was inconsistently high. 产品质量忽高忽低。 | 近义词：erratically, irregularly, capriciously, variably | 反义词：consistently, regularly, uniformly
