monastery:/ˈmɒnəstri/ /ˈmɑːnəstri/ | n. | 修道院；寺院 | A monastery is a building or group of buildings where monks or nuns live, usually following a religious order and dedicated to prayer, study, and religious work.  | Many monks live in the monastery, devoting their lives to prayer and meditation.  许多僧侣住在寺院里，将他们的生命奉献给祈祷和冥想。  | convent, abbey | secular world, worldly life | 名词
