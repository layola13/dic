retold:/ˌriːˈtoʊld/ | v. | 讲述；复述 | To tell (a story, etc.) again, in a different way or to a different audience. | 例句：She retold the story to her grandchildren. 她把这个故事又讲给了她的孙子孙女听。  | 例句：The legend has been retold countless times. 这个传说已经被无数次地讲述。|近义词：recount, narrate, relate | 反义词：conceal, hide


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retells the story every year. = 他每年都复述这个故事。
She retells her childhood experiences often. = 她经常复述她童年的经历。


一般过去时:
He retold the joke to his friends. = 他把笑话又讲给了他的朋友们。
She retold the events of the day to her mother. = 她把当天发生的事情告诉了她母亲。


现在完成时:
She has retold that story many times. = 她已经多次复述过那个故事。
He has retold the tale with added details. = 他已经用增加的细节重新讲述了这个故事。


过去完成时:
He had retold the story before anyone else did. = 他比任何人都先讲述了这个故事。
They had retold the legend to countless generations. = 他们已经把这个传说讲述给了无数代人。


将来时:
She will retell the story in her own words. = 她将用她自己的话来复述这个故事。
He will retell the events of the meeting tomorrow. = 他明天将复述会议的情况。


虚拟语气:
If I knew the story, I would retell it. = 如果我知道这个故事，我会复述它。
I wish I could retell the story more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地复述这个故事。


被动语态:
The story was retold by many authors. = 这个故事被许多作者复述过。

疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't retell the story correctly. = 他没有正确地复述这个故事。
She chose not to retell the embarrassing incident. = 她选择不复述那件尴尬的事件。
