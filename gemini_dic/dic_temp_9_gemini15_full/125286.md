multiflorous:/ˌmʌltiˈflɔːrəs/| adj.|多花的，多朵花的|Having many flowers; producing many flowers.|The multiflorous rose bush was a stunning sight, covered in blooms.|近义词：many-flowered, profuse, prolific|反义词：uniflorous, pauciflorous


Etymology:

The word "multiflorous" is derived from the Latin words "multi" meaning "many" and "florous" which is derived from "flos" meaning "flower".  Therefore, it literally means "many-flowered".
