finder:/ˈfaɪndər/| n. |寻找者；发现者；寻物器；(照相机)取景器| A person or thing that finds something.  A device for locating something, such as a lost item or a specific location. In photography, a device used to compose a shot. |例句：He's a skilled finder of rare books. 他是一位熟练的珍本书籍寻找者。

The police are using a metal finder to search for the weapon. 警方正在使用金属探测器搜寻武器。


近义词：discoverer, inventor, locator, viewer (for camera finder) |反义词：loser, hider |词性：名词
