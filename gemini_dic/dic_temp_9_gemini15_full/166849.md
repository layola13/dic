pression:/prɛʃən/| n.|压力，压迫，表达|Pressure is defined as the force applied per unit area.  压力被定义为单位面积上施加的力。|The pressure of the deadline made him anxious. 截止日期的压力使他焦虑不安。|tension, stress, strain|relaxation, ease, comfort|名词
