foamingly:/ˈfoʊmɪŋli/ | adv. | 起泡地；泡沫般地 |  Describes something happening with or producing foam or froth.  |The beer poured foamingly into the glass.  啤酒泡沫般地倒入玻璃杯中。| frothily, bubblingly |  calmly, smoothly  |副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore does not conjugate like a verb.  It doesn't have tenses like past, present, future etc.  The usage is always the same, modifying a verb to describe how the action is performed.

Example 1: The waves crashed foamingly against the shore.  海浪泡沫般地拍打着海岸。
Example 2: The soap lathered foamingly in his hands. 肥皂在他手中泡沫般地起泡。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "foamingly".  Its use is fairly limited to describing a foaming action.
