finlet:/ˈfɪnlət/| n. |小鳍，小翅状物 |A small fin, especially one of a pair near the tail of a fish.  Finlets are often used for stability and maneuverability.  |例句：The fish has several small finlets near its tail. 这条鱼尾巴附近有几条小鳍。

The mackerel's finlets help it to maintain stability at high speeds. 鲭鱼的小鳍有助于它在高速下保持稳定性。|近义词：small fin, finlet, small appendage |反义词:large fin, main fin.

词源:
The word "finlet" is a combination of "fin" (a flattened appendage used for swimming) and "-let" (a diminutive suffix indicating smallness).  Therefore, it literally means "small fin".
