manors:/ˈmænərz/| n.|庄园；大住宅；风度；礼貌|A manor is a large country house with its surrounding lands, traditionally belonging to a lord or wealthy person.  The term can also refer to the refined behavior and manners of a person.|例句：The old manor house was surrounded by rolling hills and ancient trees.  古老的庄园被连绵起伏的丘陵和古老的树木环绕着。

例句：He showed impeccable manners at the dinner party. 他在晚宴上表现出无可挑剔的礼仪。


近义词：estate, mansion, country house, grace, politeness

反义词：rudeness, impoliteness, vulgarity


词源：源自古法语 manoir，最终源自拉丁语 manere（居住）。
