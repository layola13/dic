directnesses:/dɪˈrektnəsɪz/ | n. | 直接性；坦率；率真 | The quality of being direct or frank in speech or manner; straightforwardness.  It often implies a lack of evasion or ambiguity. |例句：I appreciated the directness of his response. 我欣赏他回答的直接性。

例句：Her directness, while sometimes blunt, was ultimately refreshing.  她的直接，虽然有时有些生硬，但最终令人耳目一新。


近义词：frankness, candor, openness, forthrightness, bluntness


反义词：indirectness, evasiveness, ambiguity, circumlocution


词性: 名词


词源:  Derived from the adjective "direct," ultimately from the Latin word "dirigere" (to direct, to guide). The suffix "-ness" forms nouns of quality or state.
