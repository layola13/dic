incre:/ˈɪŋkriː/| adj.|增加的；增长的；越来越多的 |increasing; growing; becoming greater.  |The increasng demand for skilled workers is putting pressure on wages.  对熟练工人的需求不断增长，导致工资上涨。 |growing, escalating, augmenting |decreasing, diminishing, lessening |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The demand for housing is increasing. = 住房需求正在增加。
The temperature is increasing gradually. = 温度正在逐渐升高。


一般过去时:
The company's profits increased last year. = 公司去年的利润增加了。
The number of students increased significantly. = 学生人数显著增加。


现在完成时:
The population has increased dramatically. = 人口急剧增加。
The cost of living has increased considerably. = 生活成本大幅增加。


过去完成时:
The price had increased before I could buy it. = 在我买之前价格已经上涨了。
The river had increased its flow after the heavy rain. = 大雨过后，河水流量增加了。


将来时:
The interest rates will increase next month. = 利率下个月将会上涨。
The company's market share will increase in the coming year. = 公司的市场份额将在来年增加。


虚拟语气:
If the government increased spending, the economy would improve. = 如果政府增加支出，经济就会好转。
I wish the temperature would increase a little. = 我希望温度能稍微升高一点。


被动语态:
The tax was increased by the government. = 税收被政府提高了。
The price has been increased due to inflation. = 由于通货膨胀，价格上涨了。


疑问句:
Has the price increased? = 价格上涨了吗？
Will the demand increase in the future? = 未来需求会增加吗？


否定句:
The number of accidents did not increase this year. = 今年事故数量没有增加。
The production did not increase as expected. = 生产没有按预期增加。


PHRASE->
incremental increase: 逐步增加
incremental progress: 渐进式进展
incremental cost: 增量成本
increasingly important: 日益重要
increasingly complex: 日益复杂
