reniform:/ˈrɛnɪfɔːrm/| adj.|肾形的|Kidney-shaped; resembling the shape of a kidney.  The term describes an object having a bean-shaped or kidney-shaped form, typically with a smooth, curved outline and a slightly indented area. |Example: The reniform leaf of the plant was easily identifiable. 这株植物的肾形叶子很容易辨认。The tumor was described as reniform in the medical report. 医学报告中将肿瘤描述为肾形的。|近义词：kidney-shaped, bean-shaped|反义词：circular, square, rectangular
