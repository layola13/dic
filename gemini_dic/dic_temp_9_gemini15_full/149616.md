pains:/peɪnz/ | n. | 痛苦，努力，辛劳 | pains refer to physical or mental suffering, or the diligent effort put into something.  It can also be used in the plural to refer to great effort or trouble. |例句：He was in great pain after the accident. 他事故后非常痛苦。  |例句：She took pains to ensure everything was perfect. 她煞费苦心确保一切完美。|近义词：suffering, agony, ache, trouble, effort |反义词：pleasure, ease, comfort |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wound pains him. = 伤口让他疼痛。
My back pains constantly. = 我的背总是痛。


一般过去时:
My head pained me all night. = 我整晚头痛。
The effort pained him. = 这项努力让他感到痛苦。


现在完成时:
My knee has pained me for weeks. = 我的膝盖已经痛了好几个星期了。
She has taken pains to help us.  = 她费尽心思帮助我们。


过去完成时:
My back had pained me all day, so I went to the doctor. = 我的背痛了一整天，所以我去看医生了。
He had taken great pains to prepare for the presentation. = 他为了准备演示做了很大的努力。


将来时:
I think my tooth will pain me tonight. = 我想我今晚牙会痛。
The decision will pain many people. = 这个决定会让很多人痛苦。


虚拟语气:
If my leg pained less, I would go for a walk. = 如果我的腿不那么痛，我会去散步。
I wish the loss didn't pain me so much. = 我希望失去他不会让我如此痛苦。


被动语态:
He was pained by the news. = 他听到这个消息感到痛苦。
Great pains were taken to ensure accuracy. = 为了确保准确性，人们付出了巨大的努力。


疑问句:
Does your head pain you? = 你头痛吗？
Does this decision pain you? = 这个决定让你痛苦吗？


否定句:
My back doesn't pain me today. = 我的背今天不痛了。
He didn't pain himself too much with the work. = 他在工作中并没有太费力。


PHRASE->
take pains = 煞费苦心，费尽心思
go to great pains = 费尽心机
be at pains to do something = 煞费苦心做某事
have pains in one's back/chest/etc = 背/胸等部位疼痛
no pains spared = 不遗余力
