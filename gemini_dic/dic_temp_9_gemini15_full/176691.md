reactances:/riˈæktənsɪz/| n. |电抗；阻抗|The opposition that a circuit presents to the flow of alternating current, due to inductance and capacitance.  It is measured in ohms.|例句：The reactances of the circuit components need to be carefully calculated to ensure optimal performance. 电路的组件电抗需要仔细计算以确保最佳性能。


例句：High reactances can significantly reduce the efficiency of a power transmission line. 高电抗会显著降低输电线的效率。


近义词：impedance|反义词：conductance
