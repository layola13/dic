lumberjack:/ˈlʌmbərdʒæk/| n. |伐木工人|A lumberjack is a person who cuts down trees for a living.  They typically work in forests and use various tools and machinery to fell trees and prepare them for transport.|The lumberjack swung his axe with powerful strokes.  伐木工人用有力的一击挥舞着斧头。|  He's a strong lumberjack with years of experience. 他是一位经验丰富的强壮伐木工人。| logger, woodsman | (none explicitly opposite)| 名词
