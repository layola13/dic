perturbance:/pərˈtɜːrbəns/| n. |扰乱；骚动；不安|A disturbance or disruption of a state of tranquility or order.  It often refers to a deviation from a normal or expected state, particularly in a system or process.  The term implies a temporary disruption, not necessarily a complete breakdown.|例句：The unexpected storm caused a major perturbance in the air traffic schedule.  突如其来的风暴扰乱了空中交通的正常运行。

The economic downturn resulted in a period of social perturbance. 经济衰退导致了一段时间的社会动荡。|近义词：disruption, disturbance, disorder, commotion|反义词：calm, peace, tranquility, order|名词
