mausoleum:/mɔːzəˈliːəm/ | n. |陵墓；大型坟墓 | A mausoleum is a large and impressive tomb, often built for a single person or family.  It is typically elaborate in design and construction, often containing statues, inscriptions, and other decorative elements. | The Taj Mahal is a magnificent mausoleum built by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan for his wife Mumtaz Mahal.  泰姬陵是莫卧儿皇帝沙贾汗为他的妻子蒙塔兹·玛哈尔建造的一座宏伟的陵墓。 | tomb, sepulcher, monument |  (反义词较少，因为其本身含义特殊，与其相对的词更侧重于“不宏伟”、“简陋”等方面，难以精确找到反义词)
