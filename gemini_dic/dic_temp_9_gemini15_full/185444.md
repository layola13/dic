returned:/rɪˈtɜːrnd/| v. |归还；回来；回应；(使)恢复|To give something back to someone; to come back to a place; to reply or respond; to restore something to a former state.|例句：He returned the book to the library. 他把书还给了图书馆。

例句：She returned from her trip last night. 她昨晚从旅行中回来了。


近义词：restored, gave back, replied, came back|反义词：kept, sent, remained, failed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He returns the book every week. 他每周都还书。
They return to their hometown every summer. 他们每年夏天都回故乡。


一般过去时:
I returned the call immediately. 我立即回电了。
The soldiers returned from the battlefield. 士兵们从战场上回来了。


现在完成时:
She has returned from her travels. 她已从旅行中回来了。
Have you returned the borrowed money? 你还了借款了吗？


过去完成时:
He had returned the tools before leaving. 他离开前已归还了工具。
They had returned home by the time I called. 我打电话时他们已经到家了。


将来时:
I will return the favor someday. 我总有一天会回报你的恩情。
They will return to work next week. 他们下周将重返工作岗位。


虚拟语气:
If he returned the money, I would forgive him. 如果他还钱，我会原谅他。
I wish he had returned my call. 我希望他回过我的电话。


被动语态:
The book was returned to the shelf. 书被放回书架上了。
The package was returned to the sender. 包裹被退回寄件人。


疑问句:
Did he return the phone? 他还手机了吗？
Have they returned yet? 他们回来了吗？


否定句:
He did not return my email. 他没有回复我的邮件。
She hasn't returned the keys yet. 她还没把钥匙还回来。
