haemorrhagic:/ˌhɛməˈrædʒɪk/ | adj. | 出血性的，大出血的 | Relating to or characterized by hemorrhage (the escape of blood from a ruptured blood vessel).  It describes conditions, processes, or agents that cause or are associated with bleeding. | The patient presented with haemorrhagic symptoms, including severe nosebleeds.  这位病人出现了出血症状，包括严重的鼻出血。|  hemorrhagic, bleeding | non-haemorrhagic
