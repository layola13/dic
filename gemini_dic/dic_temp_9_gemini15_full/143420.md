例句：The politician outflattered the voters with promises he couldn't keep.  这位政客用他无法兑现的承诺过度奉承选民。


近义词：overpraise,  exaggerate praise,  fawn over,  adulate


反义词：criticize,  underestimate,  belittle


词性：动词
