prettiest:/ˈprɛtiɪst/| adj.|最漂亮的，最可爱的 |Most beautiful; most charming.  It is the superlative form of "pretty".|例句：She has the prettiest smile I've ever seen. 她拥有我见过的最美的笑容。

例句：That's the prettiest dress in the shop. 那是商店里最漂亮的裙子。


近义词：most beautiful, loveliest, most charming, most attractive |反义词：ugliest, least attractive, most unattractive
