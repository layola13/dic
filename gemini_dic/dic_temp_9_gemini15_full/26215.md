butterfat: /ˈbʌtərˌfæt/ | n. | 奶油脂肪 | The fat found in milk and cream.  It is a major component of butter and is used in various food products.  | Example: The butterfat content of this milk is quite high. 这牛奶的奶油脂肪含量很高。 |近义词：milkfat, butter oil | 反义词：skimmed milk (lack of butterfat)
