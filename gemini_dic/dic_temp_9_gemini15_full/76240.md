futuristically: /ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 未来主义地，未来式地 |  In a manner that is characteristic of futurism;  in a way that resembles or anticipates the future.  | 例句：The city was futuristically designed, with towering skyscrapers and flying vehicles.  这座城市的设计极具未来感，拥有高耸的摩天大楼和飞行器。

The building's architecture was futuristically styled, using advanced materials and bold geometric shapes. 这座建筑的建筑风格极具未来感，使用了先进的材料和大胆的几何形状。


近义词：progressively, avant-gardely, modernistically, advancedly

反义词：archaically, traditionally, retroactively


词源:  Derived from "futurism,"  a 20th-century artistic and literary movement that emphasized speed, technology, youth, and violence.  "Futurism" itself is formed from "future" + "-ism" (a suffix indicating a doctrine, system, or practice).
