darkly:/ˈdɑːrkli/| adv.|黑暗地；阴沉地；隐秘地；险恶地|In a dark or gloomy manner; obscurely; secretly; menacingly.  It describes how something is done or happens, emphasizing the lack of light, a somber mood, secrecy, or a sense of threat.|例句：He spoke darkly of impending doom. 他阴沉地谈到即将到来的厄运。|例句：The future looked darkly for the struggling company.  这家苦苦挣扎的公司未来一片黯淡。|近义词：obscurely, gloomily, secretly, menacingly, ominously|反义词：brightly, cheerfully, openly, clearly
