manitou:/ˈmænɪtuː/| n. |神灵；精灵；魔力|A supernatural being or spirit, especially in Algonquian religions.  The term often refers to a powerful spirit or force believed to inhabit natural objects or phenomena.  |例句：In Algonquian traditions, the manitou is a powerful spiritual force that can be benevolent or malevolent.  在阿尔冈昆人的传统中，曼尼图是一种强大的精神力量，它可能是仁慈的，也可能是邪恶的。


例句：Many Native American tribes believe in the existence of manitous that reside in nature. 许多美洲原住民部落相信自然界中存在着曼尼图。


近义词：spirit, deity, god, power |反义词：nothingness, insignificance


词性:名词
