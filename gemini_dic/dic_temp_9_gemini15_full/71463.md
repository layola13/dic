flameproofer:/ˈfleɪmpruːfər/| n. |防火者；防火材料；阻燃剂 |A person or thing that makes something flameproof.  Someone or something that protects against fire.  |例句：The factory hired a specialist flameproofer to treat the curtains. 工厂雇佣了一位专业的防火处理人员来处理窗帘。  The new flameproofer made the fabric resistant to flames. 新型阻燃剂使织物具有抗火焰性。|近义词：fireproofer, fire retardant|反义词：flammable substance
