rhapsodized:/ˈræpsədaɪzd/| v.|狂想；热情洋溢地谈论；|to speak or write about something with great enthusiasm and feeling, often in a rather disorganized way.|The critic rhapsodized about the beauty of the painting. 评论家热情洋溢地谈论这幅画的美。|近义词：rave, enthuse, extol|反义词：criticize, condemn


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rhapsodizes about his travels. = 他滔滔不绝地谈论他的旅行。
She rhapsodizes about the beauty of nature. = 她热情洋溢地赞美大自然的美丽。


一般过去时:
He rhapsodized about the concert last night. = 他昨晚兴高采烈地谈论昨晚的音乐会。
She rhapsodized about her childhood memories. = 她热情地回忆起了童年往事。


现在完成时:
He has rhapsodized about that book for weeks. = 他已经连续几周热情洋溢地谈论那本书了。
She has rhapsodized about her new job to everyone she knows. = 她已经向所有认识的人热情洋溢地讲述了她的新工作。


过去完成时:
He had rhapsodized about the food before he even tasted it. = 他甚至还没尝过食物就已热情洋溢地谈论起来了。
She had rhapsodized about the trip for months before she finally went. = 在她最终出行之前，她已经对这次旅行热情洋溢地谈论了好几个月了。


将来时:
He will rhapsodize about his success to anyone who will listen. = 他会向任何愿意听他说话的人滔滔不绝地谈论他的成功。
She will rhapsodize about her wedding day for years to come. = 在未来的几年里，她将会热情洋溢地回忆她的婚礼。


虚拟语气:
If he had time, he would rhapsodize about his favorite author. = 如果他有时间，他会热情洋溢地谈论他最喜欢的作家。
I wish I could rhapsodize about music like he can. = 我真希望自己也能像他一样热情洋溢地谈论音乐。


被动语态:
His achievements were rhapsodized about in the newspaper. = 报纸上热情地赞扬了他的成就。
The beauty of the scenery was rhapsodized about by the tourists. = 游客们热情地赞美了风景的美丽。


疑问句:
Does he often rhapsodize about his childhood? = 他经常回忆童年吗？（热情地）
Does she rhapsodize about her work very often? = 她经常热情地谈论她的工作吗？


否定句:
He didn't rhapsodize about the film; he found it boring. = 他并没有对这部电影赞不绝口；他觉得它很无聊。
She didn't rhapsodize about her experience; she was quite reserved. = 她并没有热情洋溢地讲述她的经历；她相当含蓄。


PHRASE->
rhapsodize about something = 热情洋溢地谈论某事
rhapsodize on something =  对某事作热情洋溢的描述
rhapsodize in verse = 用诗歌热情地表达
rhapsodize with feeling =  充满感情地热情洋溢地表达
rhapsodize at length =  冗长地热情洋溢地表达
