north-westerly:/ˌnɔːθˈwɛstəli/| adj.|西北的，向西北的|Coming from or facing the northwest.  The word combines "north" and "west" with the suffix "-ly" indicating direction.|例句：The wind was blowing north-westerly.  西北风吹着。 The ship sailed in a north-westerly direction. 船向西北方向航行。|近义词：northwest, north-west|反义词：south-easterly
