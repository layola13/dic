microfossil: /ˈmaɪkroʊˌfɑːsɪl/ | n. |微化石|  A microscopic fossil, typically of a single-celled organism.  These fossils are often studied to understand past environments and the evolution of life. | Example:  The scientists discovered numerous microfossils in the ancient rock samples.  科学家们在古老的岩石样本中发现了许多微化石。 |近义词: microscopic fossil, minute fossil | 反义词: macrofossil |
