semiliberals: /ˌsemiˈlɪbərəl/ | adj. | 半自由主义的；温和自由主义的 |  Relating to or characteristic of semiliberalism; a political ideology that combines some liberal principles with elements of conservatism or other ideologies.  It often represents a moderate stance between outright liberalism and conservatism. | Example:  The semiliberal party platform advocated for market-based reforms while maintaining a strong social safety net.  半自由主义政党的纲领主张市场化改革，同时维持强大的社会保障体系。| 近义词: centrist, moderate, liberal-conservative | 反义词: radical, extremist, reactionary | 词性: adjective
