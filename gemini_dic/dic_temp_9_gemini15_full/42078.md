cotidal:/ˈkoʊtaɪdl/| adj.|同时潮的；等潮的|Of, relating to, or denoting a line on a chart connecting points at which the tide is at the same stage at a given time.|The cotidal chart showed the simultaneous high tide across the ocean.  这张等潮图显示了海洋中同时出现的高潮。|近义词：isochronal|反义词：无
