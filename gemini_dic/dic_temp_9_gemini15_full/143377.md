outfence: /ˈaʊtfɛns/ | n. | 外围栅栏；界外围栏 | An outer fence, especially one surrounding a property or field.  It often serves as a boundary or barrier. | Example: The farmer reinforced the outfence to keep the cattle from escaping.  这个农民加固了外围栅栏以防止牛逃跑。 | 近义词：perimeter fence, boundary fence, outer fence | 反义词：inner fence, interior fence |  名词
