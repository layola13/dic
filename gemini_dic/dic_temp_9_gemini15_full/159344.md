poisonousness: /ˈpɔɪzənəsnəs/ | n. | 毒性；剧毒；有害性 | The inherent capacity of a substance to cause harm or death through its toxic properties.  It refers to the degree or intensity of poisonousness. | Example: The poisonousness of the snake venom was evident in the rapid deterioration of the victim's health.  蛇毒的毒性在受害者健康迅速恶化中显而易见。  The poisonousness of the plant was underestimated.  这种植物的毒性被低估了。| 近义词：toxicity, venomousness, lethality | 反义词：harmlessness, innocuousness |  名词
