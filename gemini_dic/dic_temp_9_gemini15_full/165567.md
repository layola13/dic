premeasures:/ˈpriːmɛʒərz/| n.|预测值，预测试，前测|Measurements taken before an experiment or intervention to establish a baseline.  These measurements are then compared to post-measures to assess the effect of the experiment or intervention.|例句：The premeasures showed a significant difference in blood pressure between the two groups.  前测显示两组的血压存在显著差异。


The researchers collected premeasures on participants' anxiety levels before starting the therapy program. 研究人员在开始治疗项目之前收集了参与者的焦虑水平的预测值。|近义词：baseline measurements, initial measurements, pretreatment assessments|反义词：postmeasures|名词
