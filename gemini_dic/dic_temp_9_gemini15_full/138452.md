novas:/ˈnoʊvəz/ | n. | 新星，新发现 | A nova is a sudden, significant increase in the brightness of a star.  It is caused by a thermonuclear runaway on the surface of a white dwarf star in a binary system. | Example:  The astronomers observed a bright nova in the Andromeda galaxy. 天文学家观测到仙女座星系中出现一颗明亮的新星。 |近义词：supernova (超新星), outburst (爆发) | 反义词：None specifically.  The antonym would describe the opposite state, such as a dim star. | 名词
