reciprocated:/rɪˈsɪprəkeɪtɪd/| v.| 回报；互换；互相给予 | To give or feel something in return; to exchange something mutually.  |例句：He reciprocated her feelings. 他回应了她的感情。
The two countries reciprocated trade agreements. 这两个国家互换了贸易协定。|近义词：return, exchange, repay, requite |反义词：ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reciprocates my kindness. = 他回报我的好意。
They reciprocate ideas regularly. = 他们定期交换想法。


一般过去时:
She reciprocated his gift with a heartfelt thank you. = 她以衷心的感谢来回报他的礼物。
The company reciprocated the employee's loyalty with a promotion. = 公司以晋升来回报员工的忠诚。


现在完成时:
They have reciprocated our efforts with a generous donation. = 他们以慷慨的捐款回报了我们的努力。
We have reciprocated many times in the past. = 我们过去已经多次互相回报了。


过去完成时:
She had reciprocated his love before he even confessed. = 在他表白之前，她就回应了他的爱意。
They had reciprocated the gesture of friendship years ago. = 他们几年前就回应了友谊的表示。


将来时:
I hope she will reciprocate my feelings. = 我希望她会回应我的感情。
They will reciprocate our invitation to their party. = 他们将回应我们对他们聚会的邀请。


虚拟语气:
If he reciprocated my feelings, I would be so happy. = 如果他回应我的感情，我会非常高兴。
I wish she would reciprocate my efforts to help her. = 我希望她能回应我帮助她的努力。


被动语态:
His kindness was reciprocated with a warm smile. = 他的好意得到了温暖笑容的回应。
The favor was reciprocated promptly. = 帮忙很快得到了回报。


疑问句:
Will he reciprocate my invitation? = 他会回应我的邀请吗？
Did they reciprocate your offer? = 他们回应你的提议了吗？


否定句:
He didn't reciprocate her affections. = 他没有回应她的感情。
She didn't reciprocate the help she received. = 她没有回报她收到的帮助。
