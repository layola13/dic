nondependence: /ˌnɑːnˈdɪpɛndəns/ | n. |非依赖性，独立性 | The state or quality of not being dependent; independence.  It emphasizes the absence of reliance or need for something or someone else. This can apply to various contexts, including emotional, financial, or physical independence. | Example: The study highlighted the importance of fostering self-reliance and nondependence in young adults.  这项研究强调了在年轻人中培养自立和非依赖性的重要性。


Example:  Her nondependence on others allowed her to pursue her goals without hesitation. 她对别人的不依赖使她能够毫不犹豫地追求自己的目标。


近义词: independence, self-reliance, autonomy, self-sufficiency


反义词: dependence, reliance, dependency


词性: noun
