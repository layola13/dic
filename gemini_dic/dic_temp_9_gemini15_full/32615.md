chirping:/ˈtʃɜːrpɪŋ/| n. |啁啾声，唧唧喳喳声 |The sound of insects or birds making short, high-pitched sounds.|The birds were chirping merrily in the morning sun.  清晨的阳光下，鸟儿们快乐地啁啾着。|The crickets chirped all night. 蟋蟀整夜都在唧唧喳喳叫。|twittering, tweeting|silence


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The birds are chirping sweetly. = 鸟儿们正甜美地鸣叫着。
The crickets chirp throughout the night. = 蟋蟀整夜都在鸣叫。


一般过去时:
The birds chirped a cheerful song. = 鸟儿们唱着一首快乐的歌。
The crickets chirped loudly last night. = 昨晚蟋蟀叫得很响。


现在完成时:
The birds have chirped all morning. = 鸟儿们整个上午都在鸣叫。
The crickets have chirped since sunset. = 从日落开始，蟋蟀就一直在鸣叫。


过去完成时:
The birds had chirped before the sun rose. = 太阳升起之前，鸟儿们就已经鸣叫了。
The crickets had chirped for hours before I fell asleep. = 我睡着之前，蟋蟀已经叫了几个小时了。


将来时:
The birds will chirp at dawn tomorrow. = 明天黎明鸟儿将会鸣叫。
The crickets will chirp throughout the night. = 蟋蟀将整夜鸣叫。


虚拟语气:
If the birds chirped, I would wake up. = 如果鸟儿鸣叫，我会醒来。
I wish the crickets would stop chirping. = 我希望蟋蟀能停止鸣叫。


被动语态:
(Chirping is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Are the birds chirping? = 鸟儿在鸣叫吗？
Were the crickets chirping last night? = 昨晚蟋蟀在鸣叫吗？


否定句:
The birds aren't chirping today. = 鸟儿今天没有鸣叫。
The crickets didn't chirp last night. = 昨晚蟋蟀没有鸣叫。
