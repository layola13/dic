domains:/dəˈmeɪnz/| n.|领域，范围，域名|Areas of activity or interest;  a sphere of influence or activity; in computing, a name that identifies one or more network addresses.|例句：Her domains of expertise include mathematics and physics.  她的专业领域包括数学和物理。

例句：The company operates in several different domains. 这家公司在几个不同的领域运营。


近义词：fields, areas, realms, spheres, territories|反义词：N/A|词性：n.
