king's:/kɪŋz/| n.|国王的；君主的|The possessive form of "king," indicating ownership or relationship.  It can also refer to things associated with a king.|例句：This is the king's crown. 这是国王的王冠。|例句：The king's speech was inspiring. 国王的演讲鼓舞人心。|近义词：royal, monarchical|反义词：commoner's, subject's
