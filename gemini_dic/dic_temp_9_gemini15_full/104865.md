keenan:/ˈkiːnən/|n. proper noun|基南（人名）|Keenan is a surname of Irish origin. It is a variant of O'Cianain, meaning "descendant of Cianán."  The name Cianán itself is derived from the Irish word "cian," meaning "ancient" or "old," combined with the diminutive suffix "-án."
