flimsier: /ˈflɪmziər/ | adj. | 薄弱的；不结实的；不可靠的 |  More flimsy; lacking strength or solidity; weak and shaky.  It describes something that is easily broken or damaged, or something that is not reliable or convincing. |


例句：

The flimsy chair collapsed under his weight. = 这把薄弱的椅子在他体重下塌了。

His argument was flimsy and unconvincing. = 他的论点薄弱且缺乏说服力。


近义词：weak, frail, delicate, shaky, unsubstantial, tenuous

反义词：strong, sturdy, robust, solid, substantial, reliable


词性：形容词
