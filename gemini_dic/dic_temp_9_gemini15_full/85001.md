harajuku:/hɑːrəˈdʒuːkuː/|/ˈhɑːrɑːdʒuːkuː/| n. |原宿|A district in Tokyo, Japan, known for its unique street style and youth culture.  Harajuku is famous for its vibrant fashion, quirky shops, and colorful atmosphere, particularly around Takeshita Street.  It's a center for youth rebellion and self-expression, influencing global fashion trends.|例句：I visited Harajuku and was amazed by the diverse street style. 我去了原宿，对那里的多元街头风格感到惊叹。|例句：Harajuku fashion is known for its creativity and individuality. 原宿时尚以其创造性和个性而闻名。|近义词：Shibuya (another trendy Tokyo district)|反义词：无|名词
