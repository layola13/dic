pastor:/ˈpæstər/| n. |牧师，牧者 |A pastor is a Christian religious leader who provides spiritual guidance and leadership to a congregation.  They typically lead worship services, preach sermons, and offer pastoral care to members of their church. |例句：Our pastor gave a moving sermon on forgiveness. 我们的牧师做了一个关于宽恕的感人布道。|例句：The pastor visited the sick member of the congregation. 牧师探望了教区里生病的成员。|近义词：clergyman, minister, priest |反义词：None specifically applicable.
