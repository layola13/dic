salpid:/ˈsæl.pɪd/| n. |樽海鞘|A salp is a barrel-shaped, planktonic tunicate.  They are free-swimming, transparent, gelatinous animals that live in the open ocean. They are filter feeders, meaning they strain food particles from the water.|Example:  The ocean current carried a large swarm of salps towards the shore.  洋流把一大群樽海鞘带到了岸边。|近义词: tunicate (a broader term)|反义词: N/A
