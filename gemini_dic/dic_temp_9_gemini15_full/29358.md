castrate:/ˈkæstreɪt/| vt.|阉割；使…丧失活力|To remove the testicles of (a male animal or person).  The term can also be used figuratively to describe the removal or suppression of something's power or effectiveness.|例句：The farmer castrated the bull to prevent it from breeding.  这个农民阉割了公牛以防止其繁殖。

The singer's manager tried to castrate his artistic vision by forcing him to record only pop songs.  这位歌手的经纪人试图通过强迫他只录制流行歌曲来扼杀他的艺术视野。|近义词：emasculate, geld, neuter|反义词：None directly antonymous, but  "to enhance" or "to empower" could be considered contextual opposites.
