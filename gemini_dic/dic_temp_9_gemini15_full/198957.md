sight:/saɪt/| n.|景象；视野；视力；看见|The sight of the mountains was breathtaking.  山景令人叹为观止。|近义词：view, vision, spectacle|反义词：blindness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I see a bird. = 我看见一只鸟。
He sights the target. = 他瞄准目标。


一般过去时:
I saw a beautiful sunset. = 我看到一个美丽的日落。
They sighted land after days at sea. = 他们在海上航行了几天后看到了陆地。


现在完成时:
I have seen that movie before. = 我以前看过那部电影。
The sailors have sighted several ships today. = 水手们今天已经看到了几艘船。


过去完成时:
I had seen her before, but I didn't remember. = 我以前见过她，但我记不起来了。
They had sighted the enemy ship before the battle began. = 战斗开始前他们已经发现了敌舰。


将来时:
I will see you tomorrow. = 我明天见你。
They will sight the comet tonight. = 他们今晚将看到彗星。


虚拟语气:
If I saw a bear, I would run away. = 如果我看到熊，我会跑开。
I wish I could sight the Northern Lights. = 我希望我能看到北极光。


被动语态:
The painting was sighted by many tourists. = 这幅画被许多游客看到。
The ship was sighted on the horizon. = 船只在地平线上被发现。


疑问句:
Did you see anything unusual? = 你看到什么异常了吗？
Have you ever sighted a whale? = 你曾经见过鲸鱼吗？


否定句:
I didn't see him there. = 我没在那里看到他。
They didn't sight the plane. = 他们没有看到飞机。


PHRASE->
at first sight = 乍一看
in sight = 看得见
out of sight = 看不见
lose sight of = 看不见；忽略
catch sight of = 瞥见
