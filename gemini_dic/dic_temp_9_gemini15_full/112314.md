例句：The lordly estate was surrounded by high walls and sprawling gardens.  这片庄严的庄园被高墙和广阔的花园环绕着。


近义词：noble, aristocratic, haughty, arrogant, majestic, stately

反义词：humble, meek, subservient, unassuming
