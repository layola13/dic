calumniation:/ˌkæləmniˈeɪʃən/| n. |诽谤；中伤；污蔑 |The act of making false and malicious statements about someone.  It involves spreading false information with the intent to harm their reputation. |例句：The newspaper article was a blatant calumniation of the politician's character. 这篇报纸文章是对这位政治家品格的公然诽谤。 |例句：He was subjected to years of calumniation and false accusations. 他多年来遭受诽谤和不实指控。|近义词：slander, defamation, vilification, denigration|反义词：praise, commendation, extolment|名词
