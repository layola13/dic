empathetic:/ˌɛmpəˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 同感的，有同理心的 | Able to understand and share the feelings of another.  It implies experiencing the other person's emotions as if they were your own, but without necessarily agreeing with their actions or beliefs. | Example:  The empathetic doctor listened patiently to her patient's concerns.  这位富有同理心的医生耐心地倾听了病人的担忧。

Example: She showed great empathetic understanding towards the grieving family. 她对悲伤的家庭表现出极大的同情理解。


近义词:sympathetic, compassionate, understanding, sensitive | 反义词:apathy,indifferent, uncaring, callous
