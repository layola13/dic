ringtone:/ˈrɪŋtoʊn/| n. |铃声|A sound that a mobile phone or other electronic device makes to indicate an incoming call or message.|例句：I changed my ringtone to my favorite song. 我把我的铃声改成了我最喜欢的歌。|近义词：ring, alert, notification sound|反义词:silence
