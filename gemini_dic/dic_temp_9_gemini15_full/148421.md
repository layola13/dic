oversteer:/ˌoʊvərˈstɪr/| v.|转向过度，过度转向|To steer a vehicle so that it turns more sharply than intended, often resulting in a loss of control.|The driver oversteered on the corner and spun out.|近义词：skid, fishtail|反义词：understeer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He oversteers around corners. = 他在拐弯时转向过度。
The car oversteers easily at high speeds. =  这辆车高速行驶时容易转向过度。

一般过去时:
The driver oversteered and crashed into a tree. = 司机转向过度，撞上了一棵树。
I oversteered on the icy patch. = 我在结冰的路面上转向过度了。

现在完成时:
He has oversteered several times this year. = 他今年已经好几次转向过度了。
She has never oversteered before. = 她以前从未转向过度过。

过去完成时:
The driver had oversteered before the car flipped. = 车子翻之前，司机就已经转向过度了。
I realized I had oversteered just before the impact. = 我在撞击之前才意识到我转向过度了。


将来时:
I think he will oversteer again if he doesn't slow down. = 我认为如果他不减速，他会再次转向过度。
The car will probably oversteer on this wet road. = 这条湿滑的路上，这辆车可能会转向过度。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't oversteered, he wouldn't have lost control. = 如果他没有转向过度，他就不会失去控制。
If I had known the road was so icy, I wouldn't have oversteered. = 如果我知道路面这么滑，我就不会转向过度了。


被动语态:
The car was oversteered into the wall. = 车子转向过度撞到了墙上。(这个被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The accident was caused by oversteering. = 事故是由转向过度引起的。


疑问句:
Did the driver oversteer? = 司机转向过度了吗？
Why did the car oversteer? = 为什么车子会转向过度？


否定句:
He didn't oversteer intentionally. = 他不是故意转向过度的。
The driver didn't oversteer; he simply lost control. = 司机没有转向过度；他只是失去了控制。
