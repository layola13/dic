resecting:/rɪˈsɛktɪŋ/| v.|切除；摘除；去除|The act of cutting away or removing a part, especially surgically.  It implies a precise and deliberate removal, often of diseased or damaged tissue.|例句：The surgeon is resecting the tumor.  外科医生正在切除肿瘤。|例句：The dentist resected the decayed tooth. 牙医切除了龋齿。|近义词：excising, removing, cutting out, ablating|反义词：implanting, grafting, adding
