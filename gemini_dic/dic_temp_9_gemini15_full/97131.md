infiltrated:/ˈɪnfɪltreɪtɪd/| v.|渗透；渗入；潜入；（思想、观点等）逐渐影响|to pass gradually or diffuse into or through a substance or body.|The enemy infiltrated our lines. 敌人渗透了我们的防线。|The new ideas gradually infiltrated the organization. 新思想逐渐渗透到这个组织中。|penetrated, permeated, seeped|isolated, excluded


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The water infiltrates the soil. = 水渗入土壤。
He infiltrates enemy territory. = 他潜入敌方领地。


一般过去时:
The enemy infiltrated our defenses last night. = 敌人昨晚渗透了我们的防御。
The protesters infiltrated the government building. = 抗议者潜入了政府大楼。


现在完成时:
The new technology has infiltrated every aspect of our lives. = 新技术已经渗透到我们生活的方方面面。
Radical ideas have infiltrated the political system. = 激进思想已经渗透到政治体制中。


过去完成时:
The spies had infiltrated the organization before anyone noticed. = 间谍们在任何人注意到之前就已经渗透到该组织中了。
The virus had infiltrated the computer system before the security measures were implemented. = 在安全措施实施之前，病毒已经渗透到计算机系统中了。


将来时:
The army will infiltrate the enemy camp at dawn. = 军队将在黎明时分潜入敌营。
The new marketing strategy will infiltrate the target market. = 新的营销策略将渗透到目标市场。


虚拟语气:
If the enemy had infiltrated our base, we would have been in serious trouble. = 如果敌人渗透了我们的基地，我们将面临严重麻烦。
I wish they hadn't infiltrated our ranks. = 我真希望他们没有渗透到我们的队伍中。


被动语态:
Our defenses were infiltrated by the enemy. = 我们的防线被敌人渗透了。
The computer system was infiltrated by a virus. = 计算机系统被病毒入侵了。


疑问句:
Were the enemy forces infiltrated by our spies? = 敌军是否被我们的间谍渗透了？
Has the new idea infiltrated the public consciousness? = 新思想是否已经渗透到公众意识中？


否定句:
The water did not infiltrate the concrete. = 水没有渗透到混凝土中。
The police were unable to prevent the protesters from infiltrating the building. = 警方未能阻止抗议者潜入大楼。


PHRASE->

infiltrate into: 渗透进入
infiltrate through: 渗透穿过
infiltrate with:  用…渗透
deep infiltration: 深度渗透
slow infiltration: 缓慢渗透
