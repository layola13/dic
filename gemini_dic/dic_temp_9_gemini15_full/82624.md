gubernance:/ˈɡʌbərnəns/| n.|治理；管治；统治；管理 |The exercise of political, economic, and administrative authority to manage a country's affairs at all levels.|例句：Good governance is essential for economic development. 良好的治理对于经济发展至关重要。|近义词：governance, administration, management, rule |反义词：anarchy, chaos
