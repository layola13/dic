overbearingly: /ˌoʊvərˈbɛərɪŋli/ | adv. | 专横地；盛气凌人地 |  In a domineering or overbearing manner; oppressively.  | 例句：He behaved overbearingly towards his subordinates. 他对下属的态度非常专横。

例句：She spoke overbearingly to the waiter, demanding immediate service. 她对服务员说话盛气凌人，要求立即提供服务。


近义词：dominatingly, tyrannically, imperiously, arrogantly

反义词：submissively, humbly, meekly


词性：副词
