intermixing:/ˌɪntərˈmɪksɪŋ/| n. |混合；掺杂|The act of mixing or blending together different elements or things.  It often implies a less controlled or systematic mixing than a deliberate process like combining ingredients.  |例句：The intermixing of cultures led to a rich and diverse society. 文化交融造就了一个丰富多样的社会。

The intermixing of different musical styles created a unique sound. 不同音乐风格的融合创造了一种独特的音效。

|近义词：mixing, blending, mingling, fusion |反义词：separation, segregation |名词
