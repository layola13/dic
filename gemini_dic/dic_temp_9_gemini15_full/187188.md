roaner:/ˈroʊnər/ | adj. | 具有栗色和白色混合毛发的 (指马) |  Describes a horse with a coat that is predominantly chestnut or bay, but with interspersed white hairs, giving a roan appearance.  The white hairs are usually evenly distributed, not in patches.  | Example: The farmer's prize-winning horse was a beautiful roan.  这位农民获奖的马是一匹美丽的栗色白毛马。 | 近义词：skewbald (but skewbald implies more irregular patches of white),  piebald (but piebald usually refers to black and white) | 反义词：solid-colored (a horse of one color without white hairs) |
