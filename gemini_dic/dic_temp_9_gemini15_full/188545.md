rugosa:/ruːˈɡoʊsə/ | adj. | 有皱纹的；褶皱的；粗糙的 |  Having a wrinkled or rough surface.  The word describes a surface texture characterized by numerous folds or wrinkles.  It is often used to describe the appearance of leaves, petals, or fruit. | Example: The rose had rugosa leaves, adding to its unique charm.  这朵玫瑰的叶子有皱纹，增加了它独特的魅力。  The rugosa apples were bumpy and uneven.  这些皱皮苹果坑坑洼洼的。| 近义词：wrinkled, crumpled, rough, uneven | 反义词：smooth, sleek, polished |
