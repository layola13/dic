reviewers:/rɪˈvjuːərz/| n. |审稿人，评论员|People who examine and judge something, such as a manuscript, a performance, or a product, before it is published, released, or sold.  They provide feedback and often make recommendations.|例句：The reviewers praised the author's meticulous research. 审稿人称赞了作者细致的研究。|例句：The film received mixed reviews from the reviewers.  这部电影受到了评论员褒贬不一的评价。|近义词：critics, commentators, assessors, evaluators|反义词：authors (in the context of reviewed works), creators
