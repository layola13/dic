prenotifying: /ˌpriːnəʊˈtɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 预先通知；提前告知 |  The act of giving notice beforehand.  It implies informing someone in advance about something that is going to happen. |例句：The company is prenotifying all customers of the upcoming price increase. 公司正在预先通知所有客户即将到来的价格上涨。

The prenotifying of the staff about the restructuring was essential to minimize disruption.  提前通知员工重组对于最大限度地减少干扰至关重要。| 近义词：forewarning, advance notice, giving prior notice | 反义词：keeping secret, surprising
