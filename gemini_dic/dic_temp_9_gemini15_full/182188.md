remold:/ˌriːˈmoʊld/ | vt. |重塑；改造；重新塑造 | To change the form or nature of something; to reshape or restructure.  |例句：The sculptor will remold the clay into a new shape. 雕塑家将把粘土重新塑造成一个新的形状。

例句：The company is attempting to remold its image after the recent scandal. 公司试图在最近的丑闻之后重塑其形象。

近义词：reshape, reform, reconstruct, remake, remodel

反义词：destroy, demolish, ruin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He remolds the clay every day. = 他每天都重塑粘土。
They remold their business strategies annually. = 他们每年都会重塑他们的商业策略。


一般过去时:
The artist remolded the statue. = 艺术家重塑了雕像。
She remolded her life after the divorce. = 她离婚后重塑了自己的生活。


现在完成时:
He has remolded his entire garden. = 他已经重新设计了他的整个花园。
They have remolded their approach to customer service. = 他们已经改变了他们处理客户服务的方式。


过去完成时:
She had remolded her thinking before she started the project. = 在她开始这个项目之前，她已经改变了自己的想法。
They had remolded the building before the new tenants moved in. = 新租户搬进来之前，他们已经改造了这栋建筑。


将来时:
We will remold the kitchen next month. = 我们下个月将改造厨房。
The company will remold its marketing campaign. = 公司将重塑其营销活动。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would remold the entire house. = 如果我有时间，我会改造整栋房子。
I wish I could remold my personality. = 我希望我能改变我的性格。


被动语态:
The clay was remolded into a beautiful vase. = 粘土被重新塑造成一个美丽的的花瓶。
The company's image is being remolded through a new advertising campaign. = 公司的形象正在通过新的广告宣传活动进行重塑。


疑问句:
Will they remold the old building? = 他们会改造那栋旧建筑吗？
How often do you remold your website design? = 你多久重新设计一次你的网站？


否定句:
He did not remold his opinion. = 他没有改变他的想法。
The government will not remold the current laws. = 政府不会修改现行法律。


PHRASE->
remold oneself = 重塑自我
remold society = 重塑社会
remold the future = 重塑未来
remold the past =  重新诠释过去(通常指从新的角度解读)
remold one's character = 重塑性格


词源:
The word "remold" is a combination of the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "back") and the verb "mold," which originally meant to shape something out of clay or other malleable material.  The meaning has broadened to encompass any reshaping or reformation.
