detractor's:/dɪˈtræktər/| n. |批评者，诋毁者，贬低者 |A detractor is someone who expresses strong disapproval of someone or something.  They often criticize or belittle the subject. |His detractor's comments were unfounded and malicious. 他的批评者们的评论是毫无根据且恶意的。| critic, opponent, censurer |supporter, admirer, praiser|名词
