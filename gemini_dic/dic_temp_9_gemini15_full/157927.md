planed:/plænd/| v. | 计划过的；刨平的 |  Past participle of "plane" (to make smooth or level, or to make plans).  It can refer to a surface that has been smoothed or a plan that has been formulated. |

例句：

The wood was planed smooth. = 木头被刨平了。

We had carefully planed our trip to Europe. = 我们已经仔细计划了去欧洲的旅行。


近义词：smoothed, leveled, designed, prepared, schemed

反义词：roughened, uneven, unplanned, unprepared


词性: Verb (past participle)
