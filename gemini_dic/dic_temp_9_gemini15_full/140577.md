oligoclase:/ˈɒlɪɡəʊkleɪs/ | n. |斜长石|A plagioclase feldspar mineral intermediate in composition between albite and anorthite. It's a relatively common mineral found in igneous and metamorphic rocks.  Its chemical formula is (Na,Ca)(Al,Si)4O8.  It exhibits a pearly to vitreous luster and can range in color from colorless to white, gray, green, or reddish-brown. |The granite contained significant amounts of oligoclase.|近义词：andesine, labradorite|反义词：N/A
