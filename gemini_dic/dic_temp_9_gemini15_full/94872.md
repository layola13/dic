in-base:/ɪnˈbeɪs/| adj.| 基于…的，以…为基础的 | Based on or using a particular base or system.  |例句：The calculation is in-base ten.  这个计算是基于十进制的。|例句：The number system is in-base two. 这个数字系统是基于二进制的。|近义词：based on, founded on |反义词：independent of, unrelated to


USAGE->

This calculation is in-base two. = 此计算基于二进制。
