conacre: /ˈkɒnˌeɪkər/ | n. | 佃地，临时租地 | Land rented for a single crop season, often potatoes.  The tenant pays rent with a share of the crop. | The farmer cultivated a small conacre for potatoes.  这个农民耕种了一小块临时租地种植土豆。| 近义词：leasehold, tenancy | 反义词：freehold
