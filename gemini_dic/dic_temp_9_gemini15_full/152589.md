peacefuller:/ˈpiːsfʊlər/| adj.|更和平的，更加平静的|More peaceful; more tranquil.  This word is a comparative form, implying a greater degree of peacefulness than another thing.|例句：The countryside was peacefuller than the bustling city.  乡村比繁华的城市更宁静。

例句：The atmosphere in the room became peacefuller after the argument ended. 争吵结束后，房间里的气氛变得更加平静。


近义词：calmer, quieter, more tranquil, serener
反义词：more turbulent, more chaotic, more agitated, more restless
词性：形容词
