echoscope:/ˈɛkəskoʊp/| n. |回声探测器，回声机 |An echoscope is a device that uses ultrasound to create images of internal body structures.  It is commonly used in medical diagnosis.  |例句：The doctor used an echoscope to examine the patient's heart. 医生用回声探测器检查病人的心脏。

The echoscope revealed a small tumor in the liver. 回声探测器显示肝脏有一个小肿瘤。|近义词：sonogram, ultrasound machine |反义词：无 |名词
