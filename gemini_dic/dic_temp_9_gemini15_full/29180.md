cashes:/ˈkæʃɪz/| v.|兑换现金；获得现金|To exchange a check or other negotiable instrument for cash.  To receive money in exchange for something.|例句：He cashed his paycheck at the bank. 他在银行兑换了他的工资支票。

He cashed in his chips and retired. 他变卖所有财产退休了。|近义词：redeem, convert, realize|反义词：deposit, invest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cashes his checks every Friday. = 他每星期五都兑现支票。
She cashes in her reward points for a free flight. = 她用奖励积分兑换了一张免费机票。


一般过去时:
I cashed a large check yesterday. = 我昨天兑现了一张大额支票。
They cashed in their savings to buy a house. = 他们用积蓄买了一套房子。


现在完成时:
He has cashed his winnings from the lottery. = 他已经兑现了彩票奖金。
She has cashed in her stocks to pay off her debts. = 她已经卖掉股票来偿还债务。


过去完成时:
He had cashed all his savings before the trip. = 旅行前他已经兑现了所有的积蓄。
They had cashed in their investments before the market crashed. = 市场崩盘前他们已经变卖了投资。


将来时:
I will cash my check tomorrow. = 我明天将兑现我的支票。
She will cash in her frequent flyer miles for a first-class ticket. = 她将用飞行常客里程兑换一张头等舱机票。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would cash in my savings bonds. = 如果我有更多时间，我会兑现我的储蓄债券。
I wish I could cash in my old baseball cards for a fortune. = 我真希望我能把我的旧棒球卡卖个好价钱。


被动语态:
The check was cashed at the local bank. = 支票在当地银行兑现了。
The winnings from the competition will be cashed in soon. = 比赛的奖金很快就会兑现。


疑问句:
Where can I cash this traveler's check? = 我在哪里可以兑换这张旅行支票？
Can you cash a check for me? = 你能帮我兑现支票吗？


否定句:
I couldn't cash the check because it was expired. = 我无法兑现支票，因为它过期了。
The bank refused to cash the check due to insufficient funds. = 由于资金不足，银行拒绝兑现支票。
