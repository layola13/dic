re-encounter:/ˌriːɪnˈkaʊntər/| v.|再次相遇，重逢|To meet someone or something again after a period of time.|例句：I re-encountered my old friend at the reunion. 我在聚会上再次遇到了我的老朋友。|例句：After years apart, they re-encountered in a surprising way. 分开多年后，他们以一种令人惊讶的方式再次相遇。|近义词：meet again, encounter again,  run into again|反义词：part, separate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-encounter their old professor every year at the alumni event. = 他们每年在校友活动上都会再次遇到他们的老教授。
We re-encounter challenges in our work regularly. = 我们经常在工作中再次遇到挑战。


一般过去时:
She re-encountered her childhood sweetheart at a coffee shop. = 她在咖啡店里再次遇到了她的童年恋人。
They re-encountered the same problem during the project. = 他们在项目过程中再次遇到了同样的问题。


现在完成时:
I have re-encountered many old friends since moving back to my hometown. = 自从回到家乡后，我已经再次遇到了许多老朋友。
We have re-encountered similar obstacles in our previous projects. = 我们在之前的项目中遇到过类似的障碍。


过去完成时:
Before the final confrontation, they had re-encountered numerous times in the past. = 在最终对抗之前，他们过去曾多次相遇。
By the time they graduated, they had re-encountered several influential professors. = 到他们毕业的时候，他们已经再次遇到过几位有影响力的教授。


将来时:
We will re-encounter this issue in the next meeting. = 我们将在下一次会议上再次讨论这个问题。
I think we will re-encounter him at the conference next month. = 我想我们下个月在会议上会再次遇到他。


虚拟语气:
If I re-encountered him, I would apologize for my past behavior. = 如果我再次遇到他，我会为我过去的行径道歉。
I wish I had re-encountered her sooner. = 我真希望我早点遇到她。


被动语态:
The same problems were re-encountered in the new system. = 在新系统中再次遇到了同样的问题。
This situation was re-encountered after a decade. = 这种情况十年后再次出现。


疑问句:

Have you re-encountered any difficulties during the process? = 你在这个过程中遇到了什么困难吗？


否定句:
I haven't re-encountered that particular problem since then. = 从那以后，我再也没有遇到过那个特殊的问题。
They did not re-encounter any significant obstacles. = 他们没有遇到任何重大障碍。
