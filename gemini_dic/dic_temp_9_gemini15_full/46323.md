dabbling:/ˈdæblɪŋ/| v.|涉足；浅尝辄止；玩弄；戏水|To take a slight or superficial interest in something; to playfully touch or splash water.|例句：He's dabbling in photography. 他在玩摄影。

例句：She was dabbling her feet in the cool stream. 她正在清凉的小溪里戏水。|近义词：dipping, paddling, trifling, experimenting|反义词：mastering, perfecting, specializing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dapples in various artistic pursuits. = 他涉猎各种艺术追求。
She dapples her toes in the ocean. = 她把脚趾头伸进海里戏水。

一般过去时:
He dabbled in politics during his youth. = 他年轻时涉足政治。
She dabbled her paintbrush in the water. = 她把画笔蘸在水里。

现在完成时:
He has dabbled in several businesses without much success. = 他涉足过几家企业，但都没什么成功。
She has dabbled in watercolor painting for years. = 她多年来一直涉猎水彩画。

过去完成时:
Before focusing on writing, he had dabbled in music. = 在专注于写作之前，他曾涉猎音乐。
She had dabbled in pottery before taking up sculpting. = 在开始雕塑之前，她曾涉猎陶艺。

将来时:
He will dabble in coding during his summer break. = 他将在暑假期间涉猎编程。
She will dabble in the stock market with small investments. = 她将以少量投资涉足股市。

虚拟语气:
If he had more time, he would dabble in astronomy. = 如果他有更多时间，他会涉猎天文学。
I wish I could dabble in painting like her. = 我希望我像她一样能涉猎绘画。

被动语态:

否定句:
