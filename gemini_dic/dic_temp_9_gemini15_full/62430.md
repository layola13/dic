endophyllous:/ˌɛndoʊˈfɪləs/| adj. | 内生的；内生的；内生植物的 | Growing or occurring within a leaf or other plant tissue.  The term specifically refers to structures or organisms that develop inside a leaf.  For example, an endophyllous fungus lives within the leaf tissue. | Example:  The researchers discovered an endophyllous fungus inhabiting the leaves of the rainforest trees.  研究人员发现了一种生活在雨林树叶中的内生真菌。


近义词:intrafolial (less common)


反义词:epiphyllous (growing on the surface of a leaf)


词性:形容词
