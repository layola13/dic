insecurity's:/ˌɪnsɪˈkjʊərɪtiz/| n.|不安全感；缺乏安全感|The feeling of being unsure of oneself or one's abilities; lack of confidence.  It often manifests as anxiety, fear, or self-doubt.|例句：Her insecurities stemmed from a difficult childhood.  她的不安全感源于不幸的童年。|例句：He overcame his insecurities through hard work and self-belief. 他通过努力工作和自信克服了自己的不安全感。|近义词：self-doubt, lack of confidence, anxiety, apprehension|反义词：confidence, self-assurance, security|名词
