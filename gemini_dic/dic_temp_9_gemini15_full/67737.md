eye-opener: /ˈaɪˌoʊpənər/ | n. |令人大开眼界的事物，新奇的体验|Something that reveals surprising and previously unknown facts, making one aware of something previously unknown or unexpected.  It is often used to describe an experience or event that changes one's perspective or understanding. |例句：Visiting the slums was a real eye-opener for me. 参观贫民窟对我来说真是大开眼界。

The trip to the Amazon rainforest was an eye-opener; I had never seen such biodiversity. 亚马逊雨林之旅让我大开眼界；我从未见过如此丰富的生物多样性。


近义词：revelation, revelation,  discovery, surprise|反义词：predictable experience, mundane experience |词性:名词
