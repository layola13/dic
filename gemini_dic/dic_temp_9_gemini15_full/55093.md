disrespectfulness:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspektfʊlnəs/ | n. | 不尊重；无礼；失礼 | The act or state of being disrespectful; lack of respect.  It often involves rudeness or discourtesy towards others. | Example:  His disrespectfulness towards the elders was shocking.  他的不尊重长辈的行为令人震惊。


Example: The disrespectfulness shown by some students towards their teachers is unacceptable. 一些学生对老师的不尊重行为是不可接受的。


近义词：insolence, impoliteness, rudeness, discourtesy, irreverence


反义词：respectfulness, politeness, courtesy, deference


词性: 名词
