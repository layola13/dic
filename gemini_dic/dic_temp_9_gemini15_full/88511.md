/ˈhɪstoʊzaɪm/ | n. |组织酶；(组织中)酶 | Histozyme refers to an enzyme found within tissues or organs.  It is a general term and doesn't refer to a specific enzyme.  The term combines "histo-" (relating to tissue) and "-zyme" (enzyme).  Further specification is needed to identify the particular enzyme in question. |The researchers analyzed the histozyme activity in the liver samples.|近义词: tissue enzyme, organ enzyme |反义词:  (no direct antonym;  the opposite would be the absence of a specific enzyme in the tissue)|名词
