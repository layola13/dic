clawing:/klɔːɪŋ/| v.| 抓，挠，挣扎 |To use one's claws to scratch, tear, or pull something.  It can also describe a desperate struggle or effort to achieve something.| 例句：The cat was clawing at the door.  猫在抓门。 The climber clawed his way up the steep cliff. 登山者费力地攀上了陡峭的悬崖。|近义词：scratching, scraping, tearing, struggling |反义词：smoothing, caressing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat claws at the furniture. = 猫抓家具。
He claws at the wall in frustration. = 他沮丧地抓着墙。

一般过去时:
The bear clawed at the tree trunk. = 熊抓挠树干。
She clawed at her eyes, trying to wake up. = 她抓着眼睛，试图醒来。

现在完成时:
The dog has clawed its way out of the hole. = 狗已经从洞里爬出来了。
He has clawed his way to the top of the company. = 他努力爬到了公司的顶端。

过去完成时:
The thief had clawed his way through the broken window. = 小偷已经从破碎的窗户爬进来了。
She had clawed at the rope, desperately trying to hold on. = 她拼命地抓着绳子，试图抓住。

将来时:
The kitten will claw at the yarn ball. = 小猫会抓毛线球。
He will claw his way to victory. = 他将努力争取胜利。

虚拟语气:
If he clawed at the door, I would hear it. = 如果他抓门，我会听到。
I wish I hadn't clawed at my face, it's now irritated. = 我真希望我没有抓我的脸，现在它很刺激。

被动语态:
The furniture was clawed by the cat. = 家具被猫抓了。
The wall was clawed by the frustrated man. = 墙壁被沮丧的男人抓了。

疑问句:
Is the cat clawing at the curtains again? = 猫又在抓窗帘了吗？
Was the thief clawing at the door? = 小偷在抓门吗？

否定句:
The cat wasn't clawing at the sofa. = 猫没有抓沙发。
He didn't claw at the victim. = 他没有抓受害者。


PHRASE->
