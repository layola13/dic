clapper-board:/ˈklæpərˌbɔːrd/ | n. | 电影拍板；场记板 | A hinged board with a slate used in filmmaking to identify scenes, takes, and other information.  It is typically slapped together to create an audible click that synchronizes audio and video recordings. | Example: The director called for quiet, then the clapper-board snapped shut.  导演要求安静，然后拍板啪的一声合上了。 | 近义词：slate, film slate | 反义词：无
