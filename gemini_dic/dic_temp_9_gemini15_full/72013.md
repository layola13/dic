flintware:/ˈflɪntˌweər/| n.|燧石器；燧石器皿|Flintware refers to tableware or other household items made from flint glass.  This type of glass is known for its high refractive index, making it sparkle and appear clear and brilliant.  It's often used for decorative purposes.|The museum displayed a collection of antique flintware, showcasing its intricate designs.|近义词：glassware, china, pottery|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with earthenware or stoneware in terms of material.|名词
