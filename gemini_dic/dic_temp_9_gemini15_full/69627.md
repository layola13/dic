feminisms: /ˈfɛmɪnɪzəm/ | n. | 女权主义；女权运动 |

英文解析：Feminisms refers to the diverse range of social theories, political movements, and moral philosophies motivated by a concern to achieve greater justice for women. It is not a monolithic ideology but encompasses a variety of perspectives and approaches, often differing in their analyses of gender inequality and their proposed solutions.  Some feminisms focus on achieving legal and political equality, while others address broader social and cultural issues related to gender.


例句：

Different feminisms offer varied perspectives on the root causes of gender inequality.  不同的女权主义对性别不平等的根本原因提供了不同的视角。

The rise of second-wave feminism in the 1960s and 70s significantly impacted social norms and legal frameworks. 20世纪60年代和70年代兴起的第二波女权主义运动对社会规范和法律框架产生了重大影响。


近义词：women's liberation, gender equality movement, women's rights movement


反义词：misogyny, sexism, patriarchy


词性：名词
