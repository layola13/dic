legionary:/ˈliːdʒənɛri/| n. |军团士兵，军人 |A legionary is a soldier who is a member of a legion, especially a Roman legion.  The word often evokes images of disciplined, highly trained Roman soldiers. |Example:  The legionary stood firm against the barbarian horde.  军团士兵坚定地抵抗着野蛮人的冲击。|近义词：soldier, warrior, combatant |反义词：civilian
