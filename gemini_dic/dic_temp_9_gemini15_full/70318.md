fiefs:/fiːfs/| n.|采邑；封地|An estate of land, especially one held in feudal tenure.  A fief was granted by a lord to a vassal in exchange for military service or other forms of loyalty.|Example: The knight received a fief from the king for his loyalty.  这位骑士因其忠诚而从国王那里获得了一块封地。|近义词：estate, manor, holding|反义词：None specifically contrasting, but concepts like "freely owned land" could be considered opposite.|名词
