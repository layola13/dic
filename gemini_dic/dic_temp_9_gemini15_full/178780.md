recumbent:/ˈrɛkəmbənt/| adj.|躺着的；卧着的；斜倚的 |Lying down; reclining.  The adjective describes a posture or position.  It often implies a relaxed or comfortable state. |例句：He was recumbent on the sofa, reading a book. 他斜躺在沙发上看书。

例句：The recumbent figure in the painting seemed peaceful. 画中躺着的人物看起来很平静。


近义词：prostrate, supine, prone, reclining |反义词：upright, erect, standing


词源:  From Latin *recumbere, from re- + *cumbere "to lie down".  The prefix "re-" intensifies the meaning.
