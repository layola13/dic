communal:/ˈkɒmjunəl/ /kəˈmjuːnəl/ | adj. | 公共的；公社的；公有的 | Shared by all members of a community; belonging to or used by a community.  |例句：The communal garden was a source of pride for the residents.  公共花园是居民引以为傲的地方。

例句：We share a communal kitchen in our apartment building. 我们公寓楼里共用一个厨房。


近义词：shared, collective, public | 反义词：private, individual, personal | 词性：adj.

词源:  Comes from the Latin word "communis," meaning "common" or "shared."  The word evolved through Old French ("communal") before entering English.
