nouvelle:/nuˈvɛl/| n.| 新闻；小说；新事物 | A piece of news, especially one that is unusual or exciting; a short story, typically one that is romantic or sentimental. |例句：The nouvelle of the scandal spread rapidly. 这则丑闻的新闻迅速传开。|例句：She is writing a romantic nouvelle. 她正在写一部浪漫小说。|近义词：news, story, tale |反义词：old news, ancient history
