piecer:/ˈpiːsər/| n. |拼缀工；修补工；补缀者|A person who pieces together; a mender; a patcher.  The word often refers to someone who repairs fabrics or other materials by joining pieces together.|例句：The piecer carefully stitched the torn fabric, making the repair almost invisible.  拼缀工仔细缝合了撕破的布料，使修补几乎看不见。


例句：The skilled piecer restored the antique quilt to its former glory. 熟练的补缀者将古董棉被恢复了昔日的辉煌。


近义词：mender, repairer, patcher


反义词：destroyer, ruiner


词性：名词
