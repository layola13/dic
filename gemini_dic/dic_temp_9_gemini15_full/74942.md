freudianism:/ˈfrɔɪdjənɪzəm/| n. |弗洛伊德主义；精神分析学|The theory and therapeutic techniques of psychoanalysis as developed by Sigmund Freud and his followers, emphasizing the role of the unconscious mind, early childhood experiences, and repressed desires in shaping personality and behavior.|例句：His interpretation of the dream was heavily influenced by Freudianism.  他的梦的解释受到了弗洛伊德主义的强烈影响。|近义词：psychoanalysis, psychodynamic theory |反义词：behaviorism (in some aspects)
