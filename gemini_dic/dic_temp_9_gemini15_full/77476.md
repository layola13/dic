/ɡæz/| n.| 瞪眼，凝视 |To look steadily and intently, often with a fixed stare.|例句：He gazed at the painting for a long time. 他长时间地凝视着那幅画。|近义词：stare, gaze, glare |反义词：ignore, overlook.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gazes out the window. = 他凝视着窗外。
She gazes at the stars. = 她凝视着星星。

一般过去时:
He gazed at the sunset. = 他凝视着日落。
She gazed intently at her reflection. = 她专注地凝视着自己的倒影。

现在完成时:
He has gazed at that painting for hours. = 他已经凝视那幅画几个小时了。
She has gazed into the abyss. = 她已经凝视深渊。

过去完成时:
He had gazed at the mountains for many years. = 他已经凝视群山多年了。
She had gazed longingly at the distant shore. = 她已经渴望地凝视着远方海岸。

将来时:
He will gaze at the ocean tomorrow. = 他明天将凝视大海。
She will gaze upon the city from the hilltop. = 她将从山顶凝视这座城市。

虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would gaze at the artwork all day. = 如果我有时间，我会整天凝视这件艺术品。
I wish I could gaze upon the beauty of the world. = 我希望我能凝视世界的美景。

被动语态:
The painting was gazed at by many visitors. = 这幅画被许多游客凝视。
The scenery is gazed upon with awe. = 风景令人敬畏地被凝视。

疑问句:
What are you gazing at? = 你在看什么？
Where did he gaze? = 他往哪里看？

否定句:
He didn't gaze at the accident. = 他没有凝视事故。
She didn't gaze at the screen for too long. = 她没有长时间凝视屏幕。


PHRASE->
gaze intently = 专注地凝视
gaze longingly = 渴望地凝视
gaze steadily = 稳定地凝视
gaze upon = 凝视
a fixed gaze = 一种凝视
