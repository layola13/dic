downwards:/ˈdaʊnwərdz/ | adv. | 向下地，向下 | Moving or directed toward a lower position or level.  |例句：The elevator moved downwards. 电梯向下移动。
The prices are trending downwards. 价格呈下降趋势。|近义词：down, downward,  below |反义词：upwards, upward, above
