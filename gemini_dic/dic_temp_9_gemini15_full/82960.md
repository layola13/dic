gung-ho:/ˈɡʌŋ hoʊ/| adj.| 热衷的；狂热的；积极的 | Enthusiastic; zealous; overly eager.  The term often implies a sometimes reckless enthusiasm. | 例句：He was gung-ho about joining the army. 他对参军非常热衷。

例句：The new recruits were all gung-ho and eager to prove themselves. 新兵们都热情高涨，渴望证明自己。


近义词：enthusiastic, zealous, fervent, ardent, eager, keen

反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic, reluctant, unwilling

词性：形容词
