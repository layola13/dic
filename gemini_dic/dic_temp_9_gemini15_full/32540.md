chipmunks:/ˈtʃɪpmʌŋks/ | n. |花栗鼠（复数）|Small, striped rodents belonging to the family Sciuridae.  They are known for their cheek pouches, which they use to store food.  They are common in North America and Eurasia. |例句：The children enjoyed watching the chipmunks scamper across the lawn.孩子们喜欢看花栗鼠在草坪上跳来跳去。

例句：Several chipmunks were seen foraging for nuts near the oak tree.好几只花栗鼠被看到在橡树附近寻找坚果。


近义词：squirrels (a broader term)|反义词：无
