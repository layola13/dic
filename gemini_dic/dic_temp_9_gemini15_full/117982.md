memorandums:/ˌmeməˈrændəmz/| n. |备忘录（复数）|A memorandum (plural: memorandums or memoranda) is a written message or communication, typically brief, used for internal communication within an organization.  It often summarizes information, requests action, or provides an update.|Example: The office circulated memorandums outlining the new safety procedures.  办公室分发了概述新的安全程序的备忘录。|近义词：note, memo, communication|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company issues memorandums regularly. = 公司定期发布备忘录。
He sends memorandums to his team weekly. = 他每周向他的团队发送备忘录。


一般过去时:
They sent a memorandum to all employees last week. = 他们上周向所有员工发送了一份备忘录。
She received a memorandum regarding the budget cuts. = 她收到了一份关于预算削减的备忘录。
