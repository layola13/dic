furoin:/ˈfjʊroʊɪn/| n. |苯偶姻|A diketone produced by the benzoin condensation reaction; a viscous, yellow liquid with a pleasant odor. It is used in organic synthesis and as a flavoring agent.|The synthesis of furoin involves the reaction of benzaldehyde in the presence of cyanide ion as a catalyst.|近义词:benzoin (less precise)|反义词: None
