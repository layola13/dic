palaeethnologist:/ˌpeɪliːˌɛθˈnɒlədʒɪst/| n. |古人类学家|A palaeethnologist is a scientist who studies the cultures and societies of prehistoric peoples.  They use archaeological evidence, such as tools, artifacts, and human remains, to reconstruct past human behavior and social organization.|  Dr. Jones is a renowned palaeethnologist whose research focuses on the Neolithic period.  琼斯博士是一位著名的古人类学家，他的研究重点是新石器时代。| archaeologist, anthropologist |  (no direct antonym, but perhaps "historian" focusing on documented history rather than prehistory)|名词
