killing:/ˈkɪlɪŋ/| n. v.| 杀戮；谋杀；极度兴奋 |The act of killing someone or something; causing the death of.  |例句：The killing of the president shocked the nation. 总统遇刺震惊了全国。
The thrilling game was a real killing.  这场刺激的游戏真是让人兴奋至极。|近义词：murder, assassination, slaughter, execution |反义词：saving, preserving, protecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kills spiders every day. 他每天都杀蜘蛛。
She kills time by reading. 她通过阅读消磨时间。


一般过去时:
They killed the snake with a stick. 他们用棍子打死了那条蛇。
He killed the engine before getting out of the car. 他下车前熄了火。


现在完成时:
The hunter has killed many deer this season. 这个猎人这个季度已经猎杀了许多鹿。
She has killed two birds with one stone by finding a job that pays well and is close to home. 她找到了一份薪水高且离家近的工作，一举两得。


过去完成时:
They had killed the enemy before the reinforcements arrived. 增援部队到达之前，他们已经消灭了敌人。
The chef had killed the chicken before he started cooking. 大厨在开始烹饪之前已经宰了鸡。


将来时:
He will kill the plants if he doesn't water them. 如果他不浇水，他会把植物弄死的。
They will kill time by playing games. 他们将通过玩游戏来消磨时间。


虚拟语气:
If I killed that spider, would you be happy? 如果我杀了那只蜘蛛，你会高兴吗？
I wish I hadn't killed that plant. 我真希望我没有杀死那株植物。


被动语态:
The lion was killed by the hunter. 那头狮子被猎人杀死了。
The engine was killed accidentally. 发动机意外熄火了。


疑问句:
Who killed the cat? 谁杀了那只猫？
Did he kill the engine? 他熄火了吗？


否定句:
He didn't kill the bird. 他没有杀死那只鸟。
She wouldn't kill a fly. 她连苍蝇都不忍心杀。


PHRASE->

killing joke: 杀人笑话
