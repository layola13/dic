depressurizes:/ˌdiːˈprɛʃəraɪz/ | vt. |减压；使压力降低 | To reduce the pressure of (something), especially a container or a gas.  |例句：The aircraft depressurizes rapidly at high altitude.  飞机在高空迅速减压。|例句：The technician depressurized the system before making repairs. 技术人员在进行维修前对系统进行了减压。|近义词：decompress, relieve pressure |反义词：pressurize, increase pressure
