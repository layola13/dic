cobol:/ˈkoʊbɒl/| n. |通用业务语言|COBOL (Common Business-Oriented Language) is a compiled English-like computer programming language designed for business use.  It is imperative, procedural and, since 2002, object-oriented. It is particularly known for its use in mainframe systems and legacy applications.  |例句：Many large companies still rely on COBOL programs to manage their core business processes. 许多大型公司仍然依靠COBOL程序来管理其核心业务流程。|例句：Learning COBOL can be challenging but offers valuable skills for maintaining legacy systems. 学习COBOL可能具有挑战性，但它为维护遗留系统提供了宝贵的技能。|近义词：programming language, business language|反义词：无
