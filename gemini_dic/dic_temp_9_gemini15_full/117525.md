meet-and-greet:/ˈmiːt ænd ɡriːt/| n. |见面会，会面问候 |A meet-and-greet is an informal event where people can meet and talk to someone, usually a celebrity or public figure.  |例句：The author held a meet-and-greet after his book signing. 作者在他的签售会后举办了一场见面会。|例句：There will be a meet-and-greet with the band after the concert. 音乐会结束后将会有乐队的见面会。|近义词：reception, gathering, introduction |反义词：farewell, separation
