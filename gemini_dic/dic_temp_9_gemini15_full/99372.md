interdetermination: /ˌɪntərˌdɪˌtɜːrmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |相互决定；相互作用；共同决定 | The complex interplay of factors involved in a process where multiple elements influence and are influenced by each other.  It emphasizes the reciprocal and interdependent nature of causality. | Example: The study explored the interdetermination of individual choices and societal structures in shaping economic outcomes.  这项研究探讨了个人选择和社会结构在塑造经济成果中的相互作用。


The concept of interdetermination is distinct from simple cause and effect, highlighting the dynamic feedback loops and reciprocal influences between elements.

近义词：reciprocal determination, mutual influence, interdependence

反义词：unidirectional causality, independent determination

词性：名词
