defibrillators:/dɪˈfɪbrɪleɪtərz/| n.|除颤器|A defibrillator is a medical device that gives a high-energy electric shock to the heart to try to restore a normal heartbeat. It is used in cases of cardiac arrest where the heart has stopped beating effectively.|The paramedics used a defibrillator to try to revive the patient.  急救人员用除颤器试图使病人复苏。|pacemaker, cardioverter|—|名词
