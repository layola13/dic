cretan:/ˈkriːtən/| adj. |克里特岛的；克里特人的；粗鲁的，蛮横的|Of or relating to Crete, its people, or its culture.  The word also carries a secondary, informal meaning implying rudeness or boorishness, stemming from historical stereotypes.|例句：The museum houses a remarkable collection of Cretan pottery.  这个博物馆收藏了大量非凡的克里特陶器。

例句：His cretan behaviour made everyone uncomfortable. 他粗鲁的行为让每个人都感到不舒服。

近义词： (for the geographical sense) Minoan; (for the negative sense) rude, boorish, uncouth, churlish

反义词：(for the negative sense) polite, courteous, refined

词性:形容词
