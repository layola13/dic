dartboards:/ˈdɑːrtbɔːrdz/| n. |飞镖靶 |A dartboard is a board with concentric circles used as a target in the game of darts.  It typically has a bullseye in the center and numbered segments. | Example: He threw the dart and it landed squarely in the bullseye of the dartboard. 他扔出飞镖，正中靶心。|近义词：target, board |反义词:无
