gilgalad: /ˈɡɪlɡəlæd/ | n. | 吉尔伽拉德（指环王人物） | Gilgalad is a fictional character in J.R.R. Tolkien's legendarium, specifically in the appendices of *The Lord of the Rings*. He was the High King of the Noldor in Middle-earth during the Second Age.  He is portrayed as a wise and powerful elf, a skilled warrior and a noble leader. |  Gilgalad, High King of the Noldor, bravely fought against Sauron. 吉尔伽拉德，诺多族的大王，勇敢地与索伦对抗。| 近义词：Elrond (in terms of leadership and wisdom, though from different lineages) | 反义词：Sauron (his archenemy) | 名词
