insured:/ɪnˈʃʊrd/| adj. n. |投保的；被保险人|Having insurance coverage; a person or thing that is insured.  |例句：The car is insured against theft. 这辆车投保了盗窃险。|The insured party filed a claim. 被保险人提交了索赔申请。|近义词：covered, protected |反义词：uninsured, unprotected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The house is insured for $500,000. = 这栋房子投保了50万美元。
He is insured against accidents. = 他购买了意外险。


一般过去时:
The building was insured last year. = 这栋建筑物去年投保了。
My car was insured by a reputable company. = 我的车由一家信誉良好的公司投保。


现在完成时:
The company has insured millions of people. = 这家公司已经为数百万人投保。
She has been insured with this company for ten years. = 她在这家公司投保了十年。


过去完成时:
The policy had been insured before the accident. = 事故发生前已经投保了。
They had insured their property before the hurricane hit. = 飓风来临之前，他们已经为他们的财产投保。


将来时:
We will insure the shipment next week. = 我们下周将为货物投保。
The new car will be insured tomorrow. = 新车明天将投保。


虚拟语气:
If I had insured my phone, I wouldn't be so upset now. = 如果我给我的手机投保了，我现在就不会这么沮丧了。
I wish I had insured my house against floods. = 我希望我给我的房子投了洪水险。


被动语态:
The damage was insured by the insurance company. = 损失由保险公司承保。
The policy is insured against fire and theft. = 保单承保火灾和盗窃。


疑问句:
Is your house insured? = 你的房子投保了吗？
Is the package insured? = 包裹投保了吗？


否定句:
The bicycle wasn't insured. = 这辆自行车没有投保。
He is not insured against illness. = 他没有购买疾病保险。


PHRASE->
fully insured = 全额投保
insure against = 保险...以免受...之害
insure oneself = 为自己投保
life insured = 人寿保险
insured value = 保险价值
