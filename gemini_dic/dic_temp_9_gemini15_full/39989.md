consulships:/ˈkɒnsəlʃɪps/ | n. |领事职位；领事馆 |The position or term of office of a consul.  Consulships are often sought-after diplomatic appointments. 领事职位通常是人们争相争取的外交任命。|例句：He served several consulships in different countries. 他在不同的国家担任过几次领事。  The number of consulships available depends on diplomatic relations.  可获得的领事职位数量取决于外交关系。|近义词：consulate, diplomatic post |反义词：无
