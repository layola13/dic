mope:/moʊp/| v.| 闷闷不乐，意志消沉 | to be unhappy and unwilling to be active or cheerful | He moped around the house all day after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后，整天闷闷不乐地待在家里。|近义词： sulk,  deject, despond | 反义词： rejoice, cheer up


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mopes around all day. = 他整天闷闷不乐。
She mopes whenever it rains. = 每当下雨她都闷闷不乐。

一般过去时:
He moped for days after losing his job. = 他失业后闷闷不乐了好几天。
She moped in her room after the argument. = 争吵后，她在房间里闷闷不乐。

现在完成时:
He has been moping since his girlfriend left him. = 自从女朋友离开他后，他一直闷闷不乐。
She has moped all week because of the bad weather. = 因为天气不好，她闷闷不乐了一周。

过去完成时:
He had been moping for weeks before he finally sought help. = 他闷闷不乐了好几个星期才最终寻求帮助。
She had moped all day before she decided to go for a walk. = 她闷闷不乐了一整天，然后决定去散步。

将来时:
He will probably mope for a while after the breakup. = 分手后，他可能会闷闷不乐一段时间。
She will likely mope if she doesn't get the job. = 如果她没有得到这份工作，她可能会闷闷不乐。

虚拟语气:
If I failed the exam, I would mope for weeks. = 如果我考试不及格，我会闷闷不乐好几个星期。
I wish I hadn't moped so much after the rejection. = 我真希望我没有因为被拒绝而如此闷闷不乐。

被动语态: (Mope is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Is he still moping about the lost game? = 他还在为输掉比赛而闷闷不乐吗？
Why is she moping today? = 她今天为什么闷闷不乐？


否定句:
He didn't mope for long after the initial disappointment. = 他最初的失望并没有持续多久。
She didn't mope; she got right back to work. = 她没有闷闷不乐；她马上又投入工作了。



PHRASE->
mope around:  到处闷闷不乐
mope about:  为…而闷闷不乐
mope along:  无精打采地走
mope it up:  闷闷不乐地度过
stop moping:  别再闷闷不乐了
