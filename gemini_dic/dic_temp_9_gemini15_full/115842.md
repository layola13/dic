marking:/ˈmɑːkɪŋ/ | /ˈmɑːrkɪŋ/ | n. | 标记，做记号；评分；显著特征 | The act of marking something; a mark or sign; the process of assigning grades or scores.  | Example: The teacher is doing the marking of the exam papers. 老师正在批改试卷。 The marking on the map shows the route. 地图上的标记显示了路线。|近义词：labeling, tagging, grading, scoring | 反义词：erasure, removal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher is marking the exams. = 老师正在批改考试试卷。
She marks all her important documents. = 她为所有重要的文件做标记。


一般过去时:
He marked the important passages in the book. = 他在书中标注了重要的段落。
They marked the boundary of the property. = 他们标注了房产的边界。


现在完成时:
She has marked all the essays. = 她已经批改完所有的论文了。
He has marked the map with a red pen. = 他用红笔标注了地图。


过去完成时:
She had marked the papers before the meeting. = 她在会议之前就批改完了试卷。
They had marked the location on the map earlier. = 他们之前已经在地图上标注了位置。


将来时:
He will mark the test papers tomorrow. = 他明天将批改试卷。
She will mark the important points in the presentation. = 她将在演示文稿中标注重点。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would mark the papers carefully. = 如果我有更多时间，我会仔细批改试卷。
I wish I had marked the diagrams more clearly. = 我希望我更清晰地标注了图表。


被动语态:
The exams are being marked by the professor. = 考试正在由教授批改。
The location was marked on the map. = 位置在地图上被标注了。


疑问句:
Are you marking the papers yet? = 你在批改试卷了吗？
Has she finished marking the assignments? = 她批改完作业了吗？


否定句:
He didn't mark the important parts. = 他没有标注重要的部分。
They haven't marked the answers yet. = 他们还没有批改答案。


PHRASE->
