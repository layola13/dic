interoperable:/ˌɪntərˈɒpərəbl/| adj.|可互操作的，互通的|Able to operate with other systems or devices.|The new software is interoperable with our existing systems, allowing for seamless data exchange.|近义词：compatible, compatible, interchangeable|反义词：incompatible


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The systems are interoperable. = 这些系统是互通的。
This software is interoperable with our platform. = 此软件与我们的平台兼容。

一般过去时:
The two systems were interoperable until the recent update. = 这两个系统在最近更新之前是互通的。
The software was not interoperable with older versions. = 该软件与旧版本不兼容。

现在完成时:
The company has made its systems interoperable. = 公司已使其系统互通。
We have ensured that all our products are interoperable. = 我们已确保我们所有产品都是互通的。

过去完成时:
They had made the systems interoperable before the launch. = 他们此前已使系统互通。
The integration had been completed, making the systems interoperable. = 集成已完成，使系统互通。

将来时:
The new update will make the systems interoperable. = 新的更新将使系统互通。
Our aim is to ensure the systems will be fully interoperable by next year. = 我们的目标是确保系统在明年全面互通。

虚拟语气:
If the systems were interoperable, the data transfer would be much easier. = 如果系统互通，数据传输将容易得多。
It is essential that all systems be interoperable. = 所有系统都必须互通。

被动语态:
The systems are being made interoperable. = 系统正在变得互通。
The compatibility issues are being addressed to make the systems interoperable. = 兼容性问题正在得到解决，以使系统互通。

疑问句:
Are the two databases interoperable? = 这两个数据库是否互通？
Is the new hardware interoperable with our current network? = 新硬件与我们当前的网络兼容吗？

否定句:
The systems are not interoperable. = 系统不互通。
The software is not interoperable with that operating system. = 该软件与该操作系统不兼容。


PHRASE->
interoperable systems = 可互操作系统
ensure interoperability = 确保互操作性
promote interoperability = 推广互操作性
achieve interoperability = 实现互操作性
interoperability testing = 互操作性测试


词源:
inter- (between, among) + operable (capable of operating)  组合而成。
