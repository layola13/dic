nontenure: /ˈnɑːntɛnjʊr/ | adj. | 非终身教职的 | Not having tenure; relating to a position without tenure.  This term typically refers to professors or teachers who do not have permanent positions. |  例句：Many universities rely heavily on nontenure-track faculty. 许多大学严重依赖非终身教职的教师。


例句：He was a nontenure professor for five years before securing a tenured position. 他在获得终身教职之前，做了五年非终身教职的教授。


近义词：temporary, provisional, contract, nonpermanent


反义词：tenured, permanent


词性：形容词
