preact:/ˈpriːækt/| n.|预演，预先行动|A preact is a rehearsal or a preliminary action taken in preparation for a larger event or action.|例句：The band held a preact to test the sound system before the concert.乐队在音乐会前进行了一次预演以测试音响系统。|近义词：rehearsal, preview, trial run|反义词：final performance, main event


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The director preacts the scenes before filming. = 导演在拍摄前预演场景。
They preact the emergency procedures every month. = 他们每月预演紧急程序。

一般过去时:
The actors preacted their lines last night. = 演员们昨晚预演了他们的台词。
She preacted her speech before the presentation. = 她在演讲前预演了她的发言。

现在完成时:
They have preacted the play several times. = 他们已经排练了这出戏好几次了。
We have preacted the escape route in case of fire. = 我们已经预演了火灾逃生路线。

过去完成时:
