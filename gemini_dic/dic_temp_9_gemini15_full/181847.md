relocate:/ˌriːˈloʊkeɪt/ | vt. |重新安置；迁移；搬迁 |To move to a new place; to move something to a new place.  |例句：The company relocated its headquarters to Chicago. 公司将其总部迁至芝加哥。

例句：We had to relocate our furniture after the flood. 洪水过后我们不得不搬动家具。

近义词：move, transfer, transplant, shift

反义词：stay, remain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company relocates its offices every five years. = 公司每五年搬迁一次办公室。
They relocate frequently due to their jobs. = 他们因为工作经常搬家。


一般过去时:
We relocated to a smaller house last year. = 我们去年搬到了一套更小的房子。
The factory relocated to a new industrial park. = 工厂搬迁到了一个新的工业园区。


现在完成时:
They have relocated three times in the past decade. = 在过去的十年里，他们已经搬了三次家。
The museum has relocated its ancient artifacts to a more secure location. = 博物馆已将其古代文物搬迁到更安全的地方。


过去完成时:
Before they moved to the city, they had relocated several times in the countryside. = 在他们搬到城市之前，他们在乡下已经搬过几次家。
We had relocated to London before the pandemic began. = 大流行开始之前，我们已经搬到了伦敦。


将来时:
We will relocate to California next spring. = 我们明年春天将搬到加利福尼亚州。
The government plans to relocate the refugees to safer areas. = 政府计划将难民迁移到更安全的地方。


虚拟语气:
If we relocated, we would be closer to our families. = 如果我们搬家，我们会离家人更近。
I wish we had relocated sooner. = 我真希望我们早点搬家。


被动语态:
The office was relocated to a more modern building. = 办公室被搬迁到了一栋更现代化的建筑物里。
The statue is being relocated to a prominent position in the park. = 雕像正在被迁移到公园的一个显眼位置。


疑问句:
Will the company relocate its operations overseas? = 公司会将业务搬迁到海外吗？
Have they relocated yet? = 他们搬家了吗？


否定句:
The family decided not to relocate this year. = 这个家庭决定今年不搬家。
We couldn't relocate due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，我们无法搬迁。



PHRASE->
relocate to = 搬迁到……
relocate within = 在……内部搬迁
relocate oneself = 搬家；自行迁移
relocate employees = 安置员工
relocate a business = 搬迁企业
