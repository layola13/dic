fear:/fɪr/| n. v.|恐惧，害怕；担心|  The feeling of being afraid; a feeling of anxiety and apprehension caused by the belief that someone or something is dangerous, likely to cause pain, or a threat.  |例句：He felt a deep fear of the dark. 他对黑暗感到深深的恐惧。|例句：Her fear prevented her from speaking. 她的恐惧使她无法说话。|近义词：dread, terror, fright, apprehension|反义词：courage, bravery, confidence|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I fear failure. = 我害怕失败。
They fear the unknown. = 他们害怕未知的事物。


一般过去时:
I feared for my life. = 我害怕我的生命。
She feared the consequences. = 她害怕后果。


现在完成时:
I have feared spiders since childhood. = 从小我就害怕蜘蛛。
He has feared speaking in public his whole life. = 他一辈子都害怕在公共场合讲话。


过去完成时:
I had feared the worst. = 我曾害怕最坏的情况。
She had feared telling him the truth. = 她曾害怕告诉他真相。


将来时:
I will fear nothing. = 我将无所畏惧。
They will fear the repercussions. = 他们将害怕后果。


虚拟语气:
I would fear nothing if I were brave. = 如果我勇敢的话，我将无所畏惧。
If I had known, I would have feared the outcome. = 如果我知道的话，我会害怕结果。


被动语态:
His fear was evident to everyone. = 他的恐惧对每个人都很明显。
The unknown is feared by many. = 未知的事物被许多人所恐惧。


疑问句:
Do you fear death? = 你害怕死亡吗？
What do you fear most? = 你最害怕什么？


否定句:
I do not fear death. = 我不怕死。
She does not fear challenges. = 她不害怕挑战。


PHRASE->
