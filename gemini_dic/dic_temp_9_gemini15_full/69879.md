ferrying:/ˈfɛriɪŋ/| v.|摆渡；运送；渡运|The act of transporting people or goods across water, typically in a ferry.|例句：The ferry was ferrying passengers across the river. 渡轮正在运送乘客过河。  例句：They are ferrying supplies to the disaster area.  他们正在向灾区运送物资。|近义词：transporting, conveying, carrying|反义词：receiving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They ferry passengers across the lake every day. = 他们每天都摆渡乘客过湖。
The company ferries goods between the two cities. = 这家公司在两座城市之间运送货物。


一般过去时:
The ferry ferried us safely to the island. = 渡轮安全地把我们送到了岛上。
They ferried the injured to the hospital. = 他们把伤员送到了医院。


现在完成时:
We have ferried thousands of passengers this year. = 今年我们已经运送了数千名乘客。
The company has ferried millions of tons of goods. = 这家公司已经运送了数百万吨的货物。


过去完成时:
The ship had ferried supplies to the troops before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，船只已经向部队运送了物资。
They had ferried the refugees to safety before nightfall. = 他们在夜幕降临之前已经将难民运送到了安全地带。


将来时:
The ferry will ferry us to the other side of the river. = 渡轮将把我们送到河的另一边。
They will ferry the equipment to the construction site tomorrow. = 他们明天将把设备运送到建筑工地。


虚拟语气:
If we had a boat, we could ferry the supplies ourselves. = 如果我们有一艘船，我们就可以自己运送物资了。
If the weather were better, the ferry would be operating. = 如果天气更好，渡轮就会运营了。


被动语态:
Passengers are ferried across the river by the ferry. = 乘客由渡轮摆渡过河。
Supplies were ferried to the island by helicopter. = 物资由直升机运送到岛上。


疑问句:
Does the ferry ferry cars as well? = 渡轮也运送汽车吗？
How often do they ferry goods to the warehouse? = 他们多久向仓库运送一次货物？


否定句:
The ferry doesn't ferry passengers at night. = 渡轮晚上不运送乘客。
They didn't ferry the package on time. = 他们没有按时运送包裹。
