prebought: /ˌpriːˈbɔːt/ | adj. | 预先购买的，事先买好的 |  Already bought before a particular time or event.  |例句：The prebought tickets saved us a lot of time at the box office. 预先购买的票节省了我们在售票处大量时间。
例句：We had prebought all the ingredients for the cake. 我们已经预先买好了蛋糕的所有配料。|近义词：pre-purchased, advance-purchased | 反义词：newly bought, just-bought | 词性：形容词

词源:
The word "prebought" is a combination of the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before") and the past participle "bought" (from the verb "buy").  It's a relatively modern word, formed through the process of compounding.
