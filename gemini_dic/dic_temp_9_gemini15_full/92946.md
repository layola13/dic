ideogrammatic:/ˌaɪdiəʊɡræˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 象形文字的；表意文字的 | Relating to or denoting a system of writing in which symbols represent ideas or concepts rather than sounds.  | Example:  The ancient script was largely ideogrammatic, relying on pictures to convey meaning.  古代文字大多是象形文字，依靠图画来表达意思。|近义词：pictographic, symbolic |反义词：alphabetic, phonetic |
