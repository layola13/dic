refers:/rɪˈfɜːrz/| v.|指的是，涉及，参考|To mention or allude to something.  To have a particular meaning or application.|例句：The study refers to the need for more research. 这项研究指出了需要更多研究。 The law refers to all citizens.  这项法律适用于所有公民。|近义词：mentions, indicates, points to, relates to|反义词：ignores, disregards


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refers to the book often. 他经常参考这本书。
The sign refers to parking regulations.  这个标志指的是停车规定。


一般过去时:
She referred to the incident in her speech.  她在演讲中提到了这件事。
The article referred to several earlier studies. 这篇文章提到了几项早期的研究。


现在完成时:
He has referred to that issue several times. 他已经多次提到那个问题了。
The teacher has referred many students to the school counselor. 老师已经将许多学生推荐给了学校辅导员。


过去完成时:
She had referred to the document before the meeting.  她在会议之前已经参考过这份文件了。
They had referred to the same source in their reports.  他们在报告中都参考了同一个来源。


将来时:
He will refer to the expert's opinion. 他将参考专家的意见。
The court will refer the case to a higher court. 法院将把此案提交更高一级法院。


虚拟语气:
If he referred to the manual, he wouldn't have made that mistake. 如果他参考了手册，他就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish he had referred to the latest data. 我希望他参考了最新的数据。


被动语态:
The matter was referred to the committee.  这件事被提交给了委员会。
The book is often referred to by scholars. 这本书经常被学者们引用。


疑问句:
Does the article refer to this particular study? 这篇文章是否提到了这项具体的研究？
What does this symbol refer to? 这个符号指的是什么？


否定句:
The report doesn't refer to any previous research.  这份报告没有提及任何之前的研究。
He didn't refer to his notes during the presentation. 他在演示过程中没有参考他的笔记。


PHRASE->
refer to as: 称…为
refer back to: 回到…；再次提到…
refer oneself to:  自我参考；自我反省
refer someone to: 将某人转介给…
with reference to: 关于；就…而言
