culter:/ˈkʌltər/| n. |耕犁; 培养; 修养|A culter is a plowshare, the metal blade of a plow that cuts the soil.  The word can also refer to cultivation or refinement of mind, manners, or taste.|例句：The farmer sharpened the culter of his plow before beginning to till the field.  这位农民在开始耕地之前磨快了犁铧。|近义词：plowshare, tiller, cultivator|反义词：neglect, uncultivation
