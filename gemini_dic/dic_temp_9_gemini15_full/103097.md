jerboa:/ˈdʒɜːrboʊ/ | n. |跳鼠 | A small desert rodent with long hind legs, a long tail, and large ears, adapted for jumping.  Jerboas are nocturnal and live in burrows. |例句：The jerboa hopped effortlessly across the sand. 这只跳鼠轻松地跳过沙地。  The researchers observed the jerboa's unique jumping technique. 研究人员观察了跳鼠独特的跳跃技巧。|近义词：desert rodent, jumping mouse |反义词:无
