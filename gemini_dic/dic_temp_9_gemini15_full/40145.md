contempt:/kənˈtɛmpt/ | n. |轻蔑，蔑视，鄙视 |The feeling that a person or a thing is beneath consideration, worthless, or deserving scorn.  It implies a lack of respect and often a feeling of superiority. |例句：He treated her with open contempt. 他对她公开表示轻蔑。

例句：Her contempt for his work was evident in her dismissive remarks. 她轻蔑他的作品，这从她轻蔑的言辞中显而易见。


近义词：scorn, disdain, scorn, disregard, derision

反义词：respect, admiration, esteem


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shows contempt for authority. = 他对权威表现出轻蔑。
She feels contempt for liars. = 她对说谎者感到轻蔑。


一般过去时:
They showed contempt for the law. = 他们对法律表现出蔑视。
He felt contempt for his former friends. = 他对以前的的朋友感到鄙视。


现在完成时:
I have held contempt for that behaviour for years. = 多年来我一直鄙视那种行为。
She has shown contempt for her rivals. = 她对她的竞争对手表现出轻蔑。


过去完成时:
They had held the man in contempt for many years. = 他们多年来一直轻视那个人。
She had felt contempt for his actions. = 她对他的行为感到鄙视。


将来时:
He will show contempt for those who oppose him. = 他将对那些反对他的人表示轻蔑。
They will continue to hold him in contempt. = 他们将继续鄙视他。


虚拟语气:
If I were in her shoes, I would feel contempt for them. = 如果我处在她的位置，我会鄙视他们。
I wish I didn't feel such contempt for him. = 我希望我不那么鄙视他。


被动语态:
His actions were met with contempt. = 他的行为受到了轻蔑。
The proposal was treated with contempt. = 这个提议受到了轻视。


疑问句:
Do you feel any contempt for that person? = 你对那个人有什么轻蔑吗？
Does he show contempt for the rules? = 他对规则表现出轻蔑吗？


否定句:
I don't feel contempt for anyone. = 我不轻视任何人。
She didn't show contempt toward her colleagues. = 她对同事没有表现出轻蔑。



PHRASE->
hold someone in contempt = 轻视某人
show contempt for = 对...表示轻蔑
in contempt of = 蔑视；违反
bring into contempt = 使...受轻蔑
fall into contempt = 陷入轻蔑


词源:
From Old French *contempt, from Latin contemptus, past participle of contemnere "to despise, scorn," from com- "together" + temnere "to touch, handle, disregard."
