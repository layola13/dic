chorusses:/ˈkɔːrəsɪz/ | n. | 合唱队；合唱；齐声|The plural of "chorus".  Refers to multiple choruses, groups of singers, or instances of a chorus.  It can also refer to multiple repetitions of a chorus in a song. | Example: The opera featured several powerful choruses. 这部歌剧有几个强大的合唱队。The song's choruses were incredibly catchy. 这首歌的副歌非常朗朗上口。|近义词：choirs, ensembles |反义词：solo, individual performance
