demographics:/ˌdɛməˈɡræfɪks/| n. |人口统计数据，人口结构|The statistical characteristics of a population (as age, sex, income, education, etc.)|例句：The demographics of the city have changed dramatically in recent years.  近年来，这座城市的人口统计数据发生了巨大变化。|近义词：population statistics, population profile|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The demographics of this area show a high percentage of young adults. = 这个地区的人口统计数据显示，年轻人比例很高。
Marketers analyze demographics to target their advertising effectively. = 市场营销人员分析人口统计数据以有效地定位其广告。


一般过去时:
The demographics of the town changed significantly after the factory closed. = 工厂倒闭后，该镇的人口统计数据发生了显著变化。
Researchers studied the demographics of the 1950s to understand societal shifts. = 研究人员研究了20世纪50年代的人口统计数据以了解社会变迁。


现在完成时:
The company has used demographic data to improve its product offerings. = 该公司已利用人口统计数据来改进其产品供应。
Recent studies have shown a shift in the demographics of the workforce. = 最近的研究表明，劳动力的构成发生了变化。


过去完成时:

By 2000, the demographics of the region had dramatically altered due to migration. = 到2000年，由于人口迁移，该地区的人口统计数据发生了巨大变化。
