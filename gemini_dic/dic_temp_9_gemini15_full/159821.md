polygraphs:/ˈpɒliɡræfs/ /ˈpɑːliɡræfs/ | n. |测谎仪；多笔记录器 |A polygraph is a machine that measures several physiological reactions, such as heart rate, blood pressure, respiration, and skin conductivity, while a person is being questioned.  The goal is to detect deception by identifying physiological responses associated with stress or anxiety that may indicate lying. |The suspect refused to take a polygraph test.  嫌疑犯拒绝接受测谎仪测试。The scientist used a polygraph to record multiple data streams simultaneously.  科学家用测谎仪同时记录多个数据流。 |lie detector, truth verifier |  |n.
