/ˌɪntərˈnɑːlɪdʒ/ | n. | 交叉知识；跨学科知识 | Interknowledge refers to the understanding and integration of knowledge from multiple disciplines or fields of study.  It emphasizes the connections and synergies between seemingly disparate areas of expertise.  It goes beyond simple knowledge accumulation to encompass a deeper comprehension of the interconnectedness of knowledge. | Example: The project required interknowledge in biology, chemistry, and engineering.  这个项目需要生物学、化学和工程学的交叉知识。 | 近义词: transdisciplinary knowledge, cross-disciplinary understanding | 反义词: specialized knowledge, siloed knowledge | 名词
