outbarters: /ˌaʊtˈbɑːrtərz/ | vt. |以物易物；用…交换| To exchange (goods or services) for other goods or services, especially in a way that is considered unfair or disadvantageous.  |例句：He outbartered his old car for a barely functioning motorcycle. 他用他的旧车换了一辆勉强能用的摩托车。|近义词：trade, exchange, barter, swap |反义词：buy, sell |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They outbarter goods regularly at the farmer's market. = 他们在农贸市场定期以物易物。
She outbarters her services for discounted groceries. = 她用服务换取打折的杂货。


一般过去时:
The villagers outbartered their crops for tools. = 村民们用庄稼换取工具。
He outbartered his antique chair for a new television. = 他用他的古董椅子换了一台新电视。


现在完成时:
They have outbartered their way across the country. = 他们一路以物易物走遍了全国。
She has outbartered several items this week at the flea market. = 本周她在跳蚤市场已经用几样东西换了不少东西。


过去完成时:
Before the expedition, they had outbartered supplies for essential equipment. = 在探险之前，他们已经用物资换取了必要的装备。
He had outbartered his entire collection of stamps for a rare coin. = 他已经用他所有的邮票换了一枚稀有硬币。

虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:

PHRASE->
outbarter something for something else = 用某物换取其他东西
outbarter one's way = 以物易物走遍
outbarter goods and services = 以物易物交换商品和服务
outbarter with someone = 与某人以物易物
outbarter unfairly = 不公平地以物易物


词源:
The word "outbarter" is a combination of the prefix "out-" (meaning to surpass or exceed) and the verb "barter" (meaning to exchange goods or services without using money).  Therefore, "outbarter" implies a more extensive or perhaps less advantageous exchange than a typical barter.
