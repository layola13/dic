guzzle:/ˈɡʌzəl/ | vt. | 狂饮，大吃 | To drink large quantities of liquid rapidly.  |例句：He guzzled down the entire bottle of water in one go. 他一口气喝光了整瓶水。|例句：The thirsty hikers guzzled their water bottles after the long trek.  口渴的徒步旅行者在长途跋涉后猛灌水瓶里的水。|近义词：gulp, swig, quaff |反义词：sip, savor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He guzzles his coffee every morning. = 他每天早上都狂饮咖啡。
She guzzles soda throughout the day. = 她整天都在狂饮苏打水。

一般过去时:
He guzzled the beer quickly. = 他很快地喝光了啤酒。
They guzzled all the ice cream. = 他们狼吞虎咽地吃光了所有冰淇淋。

现在完成时:
He has guzzled three bottles of water. = 他已经喝光了三瓶水。
She has guzzled a whole pizza. = 她已经吃掉了一个完整的披萨。

过去完成时:
He had guzzled all the wine before the party started. = 派对开始前他已经喝光了所有的酒。
They had guzzled all the snacks. = 他们已经吃光了所有的零食。

将来时:
He will guzzle the entire milkshake. = 他将会把整杯奶昔喝光。
She will guzzle the whole cake. = 她将会把整个蛋糕吃光。

虚拟语气:
If I were thirsty, I would guzzle that water. = 如果我渴了，我会痛饮那水。
I wish I hadn't guzzled so much coffee. = 我真希望我没有喝那么多咖啡。

被动语态:  (Guzzle is rarely used in the passive voice)
The beer was quickly guzzled. = 啤酒很快就被喝光了。(不太自然)
The food was guzzled by the hungry children. = 饥饿的孩子们狼吞虎咽地吃掉了食物。(不太自然)


疑问句:
Does he guzzle his food? = 他吃饭很快吗？
Will she guzzle the entire drink? = 她会把整杯饮料喝光吗？

否定句:
He doesn't guzzle his food; he eats slowly and deliberately. = 他吃饭不快，他吃得很慢很仔细。
She didn't guzzle the soup; she took small sips. = 她没有猛喝汤，她一小口一小口地喝。


PHRASE->
guzzle down =  猛灌
guzzle up =  大量饮用
guzzle away =  痛饮
guzzle on =  持续大吃大喝
guzzle through =  一口气喝完
