enaliosaurian:/ˌɛnˌeɪliəˈsɔːriən/| n. |鳍龙类 |An enaliosaurian is any member of the extinct order Enaliosauria, a group of marine reptiles that thrived during the Mesozoic Era.  They were highly adapted to aquatic life, with streamlined bodies, flippers, and often powerful tails. |Example:  Many different types of enaliosaurians inhabited the ancient oceans.  许多不同种类的鳍龙类动物生活在古代海洋中。|近义词:marine reptile (a broader term)|反义词:terrestrial reptile (a contrasting term)
