reinitiated:/ˌriːɪˈnɪʃieɪtɪd/| v.|重新开始，再次启动|To start or begin something again after it has been stopped or interrupted.|The project was reinitiated after a period of inactivity.  项目在一段时间的停滞后重新启动了。|The company reinitiated negotiations with the union. 公司重新与工会进行了谈判。|initiated, restarted, recommenced|terminated, stopped, ended|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reinitiate the project every year. = 他们每年都会重启这个项目。
He reinitiates the conversation after a long silence. = 他在长时间沉默后重新开始对话。


一般过去时:
The government reinitiated the peace talks. = 政府重新启动了和平谈判。
She reinitiated contact with her old friend. = 她重新联系了她的老朋友。


现在完成时:
They have reinitiated the investigation into the accident. = 他们已经重新启动了对事故的调查。
We have reinitiated the fundraising campaign. = 我们已经重新启动了筹款活动。


过去完成时:
They had reinitiated the process before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经重新启动了流程。
She had reinitiated the conversation before he could leave. = 他离开之前，她已经重新开始了对话。


将来时:
They will reinitiate the program next month. = 他们下个月将重新启动该项目。
He will reinitiate the discussion at the next meeting. = 他将在下次会议上重新开始讨论。


虚拟语气:
