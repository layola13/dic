dash-off:/dæʃ ˈɔːf/| vt.|匆匆写就；迅速完成；|to write or produce something quickly and without much care or attention to detail|例句：He dashed off a quick email before leaving for work. 他在上班前匆匆写了一封邮件。|例句：She dashed off the painting in a single afternoon. 她一下午就匆匆完成了这幅画。|近义词：scribble, scrawl, jot down|反义词：elaborate, refine, perfect
