overtrouble: /ˌoʊvərˈtrʌbl/ | v. | 使过度烦恼；使忧虑过度 | To worry or bother excessively; to cause undue anxiety or distress. |例句：Don't overtrouble yourself with minor details. 不要为一些小事而过度烦恼。  The news overtroubled him for days. 这个消息让他忧虑了好几天。|近义词：overburden, worry excessively,  distress unduly |反义词：calm, reassure, relax
