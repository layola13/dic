impersonated:/ɪmˈpɜːrsənˌeɪtɪd/| v.|假扮；模仿；扮演 | To assume the character or appearance of; to represent oneself as another person. |例句：He impersonated the president in a hilarious skit. 他在一个滑稽的小品中模仿了总统。  |例句：The actor skillfully impersonated a famous detective.  这位演员巧妙地扮演了一位著名的侦探。|近义词：mimic, emulate, portray, enact|反义词：be oneself, be authentic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He impersonates famous singers. = 他模仿著名的歌手。
She impersonates a variety of characters. = 她扮演各种各样的角色。

一般过去时:
He impersonated Elvis Presley last night. = 他昨晚模仿猫王。

She has impersonated many historical figures. = 她已经模仿了许多历史人物。
He has never impersonated anyone before. = 他以前从未模仿过任何人。

过去完成时:
