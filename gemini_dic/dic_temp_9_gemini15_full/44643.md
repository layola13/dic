crowstep:/ˈkroʊˌstɛp/| n. |乌鸦步，（屋顶的）阶梯状屋脊|A crowstep is a stepped gable, a type of gable roof where the roofline is not a continuous slope but has a series of steps or notches, resembling the steps of a crow.  It is a decorative architectural feature, often seen on older buildings.|The old church boasted a magnificent crowstep gable. 这座古老的教堂拥有一个宏伟的阶梯状屋脊。|stepped gable, stepped roof|continuous slope
