hyperproducer:/ˌhaɪpərprəˈduːsər/ | n. | 高产者，过度生产者 | A hyperproducer is someone or something that produces an unusually large amount of something, often exceeding normal or expected levels.  This term is often used in contexts like music production, business, or biological systems.  | Example:  He's a hyperproducer of electronic music, releasing multiple albums and EPs every year. 他是一位电子音乐的高产者，每年都会发行多张专辑和EP。 |近义词：high-yield producer, prolific producer |反义词：underproducer, low-yield producer |名词
