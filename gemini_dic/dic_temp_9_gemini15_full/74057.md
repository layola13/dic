fossicks:/ˈfɒsɪks/| v.| 寻觅；淘金；翻找|To search thoroughly or casually for something, especially gold or other valuable minerals.  It often implies a somewhat haphazard or unsystematic search.|例句：He spent the afternoon fossicking for gold in the creek. 他花了整个下午在小溪里淘金。例句：She was fossicking around in her handbag looking for her keys.  她在手提包里翻找钥匙。|近义词：search, hunt, rummage, prospect|反义词：organize, tidy
