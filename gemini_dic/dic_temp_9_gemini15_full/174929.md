/ˌreɪdiəˈbələnəs/ | n. | 辐射性；发光性；放射性 | Radiableness refers to the quality or state of being radiant; the ability or capacity to radiate or emit light or heat.  It suggests a bright, glowing, or shining quality.  The word is relatively uncommon in modern English. | The radiableness of the sunset painted the sky in fiery hues.  日落的辐射性将天空染成火红。

The radiableness of the newly polished brass was quite striking. 新抛光的黄铜的发光性非常醒目。
