re-escalate:/ˌriːˈɛskəleɪt/| v.|重新升级；再次加剧；|To increase the intensity or seriousness of something again, after a period of decrease or de-escalation.|例句：The conflict re-escalated after a brief period of peace.  冲突在短暂的和平时期后再次升级。|例句：Tensions re-escalated following the release of the controversial report.  在有争议的报告发布后，紧张局势再次升级。|近义词：intensify, exacerbate, aggravate, heighten|反义词：de-escalate, lessen, reduce, mitigate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The situation re-escalates quickly if we don't intervene. = 如果我们不干预，情况会迅速再次升级。
They re-escalate the argument whenever they disagree. = 他们每当意见不合时就会再次加剧争论。


一般过去时:
The violence re-escalated after a week of relative calm. = 在一周相对平静之后，暴力再次升级。
The company re-escalated the project after a period of delay. = 公司在经历一段时间的延误后重新升级了项目。


现在完成时:
Tensions have re-escalated since the recent election. = 自最近选举以来，紧张局势已经再次升级。
The conflict has re-escalated, threatening regional stability. = 冲突已经再次升级，威胁到地区稳定。


过去完成时:
The dispute had re-escalated before the mediation could take effect. = 在调解生效之前，争端已经再次升级。
The problem had re-escalated by the time the manager arrived. = 当经理到达时，问题已经再次升级。


将来时:
The situation will re-escalate if we don't address the root cause. = 如果我们不解决根本原因，情况将会再次升级。
The conflict will likely re-escalate unless a compromise is reached. = 除非达成妥协，否则冲突很可能会再次升级。


虚拟语气:
If we hadn't intervened, the situation would have re-escalated dramatically. = 如果我们没有干预，情况就会急剧恶化。
I wish the argument hadn't re-escalated to such a degree. = 我希望争论不会恶化到这种程度。


被动语态:
The conflict was re-escalated by the actions of both sides. = 冲突因双方的行为而再次升级。
The issue was re-escalated due to a misunderstanding. = 由于误解，问题再次升级。


疑问句:
Will the conflict re-escalate again? = 冲突还会再次升级吗？
Why did the situation re-escalate so quickly? = 为什么情况会这么快再次升级？


否定句:
The situation did not re-escalate after the initial intervention. = 在初步干预后，情况没有再次升级。
We made sure the argument did not re-escalate. = 我们确保争论没有再次升级。
