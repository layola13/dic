neutral:/ˈnuːtrəl/| adj.| 中性的，中立的 | Not favoring either side in a conflict or dispute; impartial.  |例句：He remained neutral throughout the argument. 他在整个争论中保持中立。|例句：The country declared its neutrality in the war.  这个国家宣布在战争中保持中立。|近义词：impartial, unbiased, objective |反义词：biased, partisan, prejudiced
