nonappealing:/ˌnɒnəˈpiːlɪŋ/ | adj. | 不吸引人的，无魅力的 | Not attractive or interesting; unappealing.  | The movie was nonappealing to a wide audience due to its slow pace and predictable plot. 这部电影由于节奏缓慢和情节可预测，对广大观众没有吸引力。|  attractive, appealing, engaging | appealing, attractive, captivating |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The food is nonappealing. = 这食物不好吃。
This offer is nonappealing to me. = 这个提议对我没有吸引力。


一般过去时:
The presentation was nonappealing. = 这次演示很没吸引力。
Her behaviour was nonappealing to everyone. = 她的行为让每个人都反感。


现在完成时:
The project has proven nonappealing to investors. = 这个项目已被证明对投资者没有吸引力。
The design has been nonappealing to customers. =  这个设计一直不受顾客欢迎。


过去完成时:
The plan had already seemed nonappealing to them. = 这个计划对他们来说已经显得没有吸引力了。
The idea had been nonappealing from the start. = 这个想法从一开始就让人提不起兴趣。


将来时:
The new product will likely be nonappealing to the target market. = 新产品很可能对目标市场没有吸引力。
The campaign will be nonappealing if it lacks originality. = 如果缺乏创意，这场活动将不会吸引人。


虚拟语气:
If the design were more appealing, it would sell better. = 如果设计更吸引人，它就会卖得更好。(反面例子，说明nonappealing的反义用法)
I wish the movie had been more appealing. = 我希望这部电影更吸引人。(反面例子，说明nonappealing的反义用法)


被动语态:
The proposal was considered nonappealing by the committee. = 该提案被委员会认为缺乏吸引力。
The new policy is being found nonappealing by many citizens. = 许多公民发现这项新政策没有吸引力。


疑问句:
Is this proposal nonappealing to you? = 你觉得这个提议不吸引人吗？
Isn't this colour scheme a bit nonappealing? =  这个配色方案是不是有点不吸引人？


否定句:
The restaurant's atmosphere was not nonappealing; it was actually quite charming. = 餐厅的氛围并非不吸引人，实际上相当迷人。（双重否定）
The product isn't necessarily nonappealing, it just needs better marketing. = 产品不一定不吸引人，只是需要更好的营销。
