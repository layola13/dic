candlelight:/ˈkændəlˌlaɪt/| n. |烛光|The soft, warm light produced by a burning candle.|例句：The candlelight flickered in the gentle breeze. 烛光在微风中摇曳。|例句：We dined by candlelight on our anniversary. 我们在结婚纪念日那天烛光晚餐。|近义词：lamplight, firelight|反义词：sunlight, daylight
