dermapteran:/dɜːrˈmæptərən/| n. |革翅目昆虫；蠼螋|A dermapteran is an insect belonging to the order Dermaptera, characterized by cerci (posterior appendages) that are modified into forceps-like structures.  They are also known as earwigs.|例句：The entomologist identified the specimen as a species of dermapteran.  昆虫学家将标本鉴定为一种革翅目昆虫。|例句：Several dermapterans were found under the damp log. 在潮湿的原木下发现了数只蠼螋。|近义词：earwig|反义词：无
