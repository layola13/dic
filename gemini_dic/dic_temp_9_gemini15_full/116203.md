masking:/ˈmæskɪŋ/ | n./v. |掩饰；伪装；遮蔽 |The act of covering or concealing something.  It can refer to the physical act of covering something, or a more metaphorical act of hiding or disguising something.  |例句：The artist used masking tape to create sharp lines in her painting. = 艺术家用遮蔽胶带在她的画中创造出清晰的线条。

The government's attempt at masking the truth failed. = 政府试图掩盖真相的企图失败了。|近义词：concealing, disguising, hiding, covering, veiling |反义词：revealing, uncovering, exposing |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He masks his emotions well. = 他掩饰情绪做得很好。
She masks the flaws in her work. = 她掩盖了她作品中的缺陷。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
