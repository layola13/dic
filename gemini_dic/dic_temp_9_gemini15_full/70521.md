filleting: /fɪˈleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 切片；去骨；剔除鱼骨 | The act of removing the bones from something, typically fish.  |  例句：He's filleting the salmon for dinner. 他正在为晚餐切片三文鱼。  | 近义词：boning, skinning | 反义词：bone-in


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They fillet the fish every morning. = 他们每天早上都切鱼片。
She fillets the chicken breasts before cooking. = 她在烹饪鸡胸肉之前先去骨。

一般过去时:
He filleted the trout skillfully. = 他熟练地将鳟鱼去骨。
We filleted all the fish before we left. = 我们离开之前把所有的鱼都去骨了。

现在完成时:
I have filleted three large salmon already. = 我已经切了三条大鲑鱼了。
She has filleted the fish and is now preparing the sauce. = 她已经把鱼去骨了，现在正在准备酱汁。

过去完成时:
They had filleted the fish before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经把鱼去骨了。
He had filleted all the fish before he started cooking. = 他开始烹饪之前已经把所有的鱼都去骨了。


将来时:
I will fillet the cod for tomorrow's lunch. = 我将为明天的午餐去鳕鱼的骨。
She will fillet the fish before grilling it. = 她将在烤鱼之前将鱼去骨。


虚拟语气:
If I had a sharper knife, I would fillet the fish more quickly. = 如果我有一把更锋利的刀，我会更快地切鱼片。
I wish I had filleted the fish earlier; now it's getting cold. = 我真希望我早点把鱼去骨；现在它变冷了。


被动语态:
The fish were filleted expertly by the chef. = 鱼被厨师熟练地切片了。
The salmon will be filleted before being served. = 三文鱼将在上桌前去骨。


疑问句:
Have you filleted the fish yet? = 你已经把鱼去骨了吗？
Will he fillet the fish himself? = 他会自己把鱼去骨吗？


否定句:
I didn't fillet the fish properly. = 我没有把鱼切好。
She hasn't filleted the fish yet. = 她还没有把鱼去骨。
