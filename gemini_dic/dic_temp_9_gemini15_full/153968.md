perfection:/pərˈfɛkʃən/| n. |完美；尽善尽美；卓越|The state of being free from all flaws or defects; flawlessness; completeness.|例句：He strives for perfection in all that he does. 他力求在做任何事上都做到完美。

例句：The artist's attention to detail brought the painting to a state of near perfection. 艺术家对细节的关注使这幅画达到了近乎完美的境界。


近义词：excellence, flawlessness, impeccability|反义词：imperfection, fault, flaw|词性：名词
