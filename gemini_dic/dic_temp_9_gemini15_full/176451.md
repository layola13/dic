re-loading:/ˌriˈloʊdɪŋ/| v. |重新装填；重新加载|The act of loading something again, typically ammunition or data.  This word is the gerund form of the verb "reload".|例句：The soldier quickly finished re-loading his rifle. =士兵迅速完成了步枪的重新装填。

The hunter spent several minutes re-loading his shotgun after each shot. = 猎人每次射击后都要花几分钟时间重新装填猎枪。

|近义词：recharging, reloading, refilling |反义词：unloading, discharging |动词

USAGE->

He reloads his gun every few minutes. = 他每隔几分钟就重新装填他的枪。
She reloads the software every time she updates her system. = 每次更新系统时，她都会重新加载软件。
