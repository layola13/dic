immemorable:/ɪˈmemərəbl/| adj.|令人难忘的(反语), 不值得纪念的, 难以记住的 |Unremarkable or not worth remembering; forgettable.  Often used ironically to describe something exceptionally bad or unpleasant. |例句：The party was immemorable; I can't even remember what we ate.  这场派对令人难忘（反语，指很糟糕）；我甚至不记得我们吃了什么。

例句：The film was so immemorable that I've already forgotten it. 这部电影太令人难以忘记了（反语，指很糟糕），我已经忘记了。


近义词：forgettable, unremarkable, unmemorable, forgettable


反义词：memorable, unforgettable, remarkable
