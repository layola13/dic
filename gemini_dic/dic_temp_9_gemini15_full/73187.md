forborne:/fɔːˈbɔːrn/| v. |忍耐；克制；容忍|To have patiently endured something difficult or unpleasant; to have refrained from doing something.|例句：She forbore from criticizing his work, despite its flaws. = 她忍住了批评他的作品，尽管它有缺陷。|例句：He forbore to mention the unpleasant truth. = 他忍住没说那令人不快的真相。|近义词：refrained, abstained, endured|反义词：indulged, yielded, acted
