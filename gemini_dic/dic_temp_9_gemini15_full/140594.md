olive-drab:/ˈɑːlɪvˌdræb/| adj. |橄榄绿色的|A dull greenish-brown color.  The term derives from the color of olives and the drabness associated with military uniforms.|例句：The army jeep was painted olive-drab.  军队吉普车涂成了橄榄绿。
The camouflage uniform was a muted olive-drab.  迷彩服是一种暗淡的橄榄绿。|近义词：olive, greenish-brown, khaki|反义词：bright, vivid
