prespecified:/ˌpriːˈspesɪfaɪd/| adj.| 预先指定的；预先确定的 | Already specified or determined in advance.  It implies a plan or decision made before a particular event or process begins. |例句：The study used a prespecified sample size.  这项研究使用了预先确定的样本量。

例句：The prespecified inclusion criteria were strictly adhered to. 预先指定的纳入标准得到了严格遵守。


近义词： predetermined, preordained, prearranged, planned, established

反义词： unspecified, undetermined, unplanned, impromptu

词性：形容词
