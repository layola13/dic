formidable:/ˈfɔːrmɪdəbl/|/ˈfɔːrmɪdəbəl/| adj. |可怕的；令人敬畏的；难以克服的 |inspiring fear or respect through being impressively large, powerful, intense, or capable.  Formidable challenges or opponents are extremely difficult to deal with. |例句：He has a formidable reputation as a negotiator. 他作为一名谈判代表享有盛名。（令人敬畏的）

例句：The task ahead is formidable.  未来的任务是艰巨的。（难以克服的）

近义词：awesome, daunting, intimidating, impressive, challenging

反义词：easy, manageable, insignificant, trivial

词性：形容词
