reconstructing:/ˌriːkənˈstrʌktɪŋ/| v.|重建；重构；复原|The act of building or putting something together again, especially after it has been damaged or destroyed.  It also implies a process of piecing together information or understanding to create a coherent whole. |例句：They are reconstructing the old bridge after the storm. 他们正在暴风雨后重建那座旧桥。

例句： Historians are reconstructing the events leading up to the war. 历史学家们正在重建导致战争爆发的事件。


近义词：rebuilding, restoring, reforming, recreating

反义词：destroying, demolishing, wrecking

词性：动词
