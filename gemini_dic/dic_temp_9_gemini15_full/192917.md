seaweed:/ˈsiːwiːd/| n. |海草，海藻 |Marine algae and plants growing in the sea. Seaweed is a general term encompassing many species of algae and plants that live in saltwater. They are a significant part of marine ecosystems, providing food and habitat for various organisms.  Many types of seaweed are edible and used in various cuisines. |例句：The rocks were covered with seaweed.  岩石上覆盖着海草。

例句：We harvested seaweed for our dinner. 我们收获海藻作为晚餐。


近义词：sea algae, marine algae, kelp (a specific type of seaweed) |反义词：None (it's a broad term, not easily contrasted)|词性:名词
