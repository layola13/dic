paradisical:/ˌpærəˈdaɪsɪkəl/| adj.|天堂似的；极乐的；美好的|Of, relating to, or resembling paradise; idyllically beautiful.  The word evokes a sense of blissful perfection, often associated with tropical settings or utopian ideals. |例句：The island was a paradisical setting, with pristine beaches and lush vegetation.  这个岛屿是一个天堂般的地方，拥有原始的海滩和茂密的植被。

例句：The weather was paradisical; sunny skies and warm breezes made for a perfect day. 天气好极了；阳光明媚，微风习习，是完美的一天。


近义词：heavenly, idyllic, utopian, blissful, enchanting

反义词：hellish, nightmarish, dreadful, awful, terrible

词性：形容词
