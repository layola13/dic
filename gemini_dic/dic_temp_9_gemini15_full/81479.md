gravel:/ˈɡrævəl/ | n. |碎石，砾石 | Small, loose stones, especially those used for making roads or paths.  Gravel is often found in rivers and on beaches. | The path was covered in loose gravel. 那条路覆盖着松散的碎石。
The gardener used gravel to create a decorative pathway. 园丁用碎石铺设了一条装饰性小路。| shingle, pebbles | smooth, paved | noun
