oxides:/ˈɒksaɪdz/ | n. |氧化物 |A compound of oxygen with another element or group, especially a binary compound containing oxygen in its -2 oxidation state.  |Example: Iron oxide is a common rust.  氧化铁是常见的锈。  Water is an oxide of hydrogen. 水是氢的氧化物。|近义词：oxygen compound |反义词：reduction product
