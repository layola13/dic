muslin:/ˈmʌzlɪn/| n. |细棉布，薄棉布 |A lightweight cotton fabric, originally from Mosul.  It's often used for clothing, bedding, and other textiles. |例句：The dress was made of delicate muslin. 这件衣服是用轻柔的细棉布做的。

例句：She used muslin to make curtains for her bedroom. 她用细棉布为她的卧室做窗帘。


近义词：cotton voile, cambric |反义词：无
