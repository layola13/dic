malayan:/məˈleɪən/ | adj. | 马来的；马来亚的 | Relating to Malaya or its people.  | Example: Malayan tapirs are a unique species. 马来貘是一种独特的物种。 |近义词: Malaysian (more commonly used now) | 反义词:  None specifically, but words referencing other regions could be considered contrasting. | 词性:形容词
