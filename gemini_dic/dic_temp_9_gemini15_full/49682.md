demit:/dɪˈmɪt/| vt.|辞职，放弃；终止；|To relinquish or resign from an office or position; to give up or cease.|例句：He decided to demit his post as chairman. 他决定辞去董事长职务。|例句：The company decided to demit its operations in the country. 公司决定停止在该国的运营。|近义词：resign, relinquish, quit, abandon|反义词：assume, accept, begin, commence|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He demists his position every year. = 他每年都辞职。
She demists her claim to the inheritance. = 她放弃了对遗产的索取权。


一般过去时:
He demitted his office last week. = 他上周辞职了。
She demitted her membership in the club. = 她退出了俱乐部的会员资格。


现在完成时:
He has demitted his post to pursue other interests. = 他已辞职去追求其他兴趣。
She has demitted her efforts to save the company.  = 她已经放弃了拯救公司的努力。


过去完成时:
He had demitted his position before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他已经辞职了。
She had demitted her responsibilities before the crisis hit. = 在危机来临之前，她已经卸任了她的职责。


将来时:
He will demit his position at the end of the year. = 他将在年底辞职。
She will demit her efforts if the situation doesn't improve. = 如果情况没有改善，她将放弃努力。


虚拟语气:
If he demitted his position, who would take over? = 如果他辞职了，谁会接替他？
I wish she had demitted her claim earlier. = 我希望她早点放弃她的要求。


被动语态:
His position was demitted by him last month. = 他的职位上个月被他辞去了。(略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The project was demitted due to lack of funding. =  由于资金不足，该项目被终止了。


疑问句:
Will he demit his position soon? = 他会很快辞职吗？
Did she demit her membership? = 她退会了吗？


否定句:
He did not demit his position despite the pressure. = 尽管有压力，他也没有辞职。
She will not demit her efforts until she succeeds. = 在她成功之前，她不会放弃努力。


PHRASE->
demit office = 辞职
demit a commission = 放弃委托
demit a claim = 放弃索赔
demit one's charge = 放弃责任
demit control = 放弃控制
