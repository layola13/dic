sedentary:/ˈsɛdntəri/| adj.|久坐的；定居的；不活动的|Relating to or characterized by much sitting and little physical activity.|例句：A sedentary lifestyle can lead to health problems. 久坐的生活方式会导致健康问题。|近义词：inactive, stationary, settled|反义词：active, mobile


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He leads a sedentary life. = 他过着久坐的生活。
They maintain a sedentary lifestyle. = 他们保持着久坐的生活方式。


一般过去时:
She lived a sedentary life before retirement. = 退休前她过着久坐的生活。
He had a sedentary job in an office. = 他在办公室里有一份久坐的工作。


现在完成时:
He has adopted a more active lifestyle after years of being sedentary. = 在多年久坐不动之后，他过上了更积极的生活方式。
She has realized the negative effects of her sedentary habits. = 她已经意识到了久坐习惯的负面影响。


过去完成时:
He had been sedentary for so long that it was difficult to start exercising. = 他久坐不动的时间太长了，以至于很难开始锻炼。
They had maintained a sedentary lifestyle for decades before making a change. = 在改变之前，他们几十年来一直保持着久坐不动的生活方式。


将来时:
She will continue her sedentary routine unless she makes a change. = 除非她有所改变，否则她将继续她久坐的日常活动。
He will likely experience health problems if he continues his sedentary lifestyle. = 如果他继续久坐的生活方式，他很可能会出现健康问题。


虚拟语气:
If he were not so sedentary, he would be healthier. = 如果他不那么久坐，他会更健康。
I wish I hadn't been so sedentary in my younger years. = 我真希望我年轻时不要那么久坐。


被动语态:
Sedentary lifestyles are often linked to various health issues. = 久坐的生活方式通常与各种健康问题有关。
A sedentary job can be detrimental to one's health if not balanced with physical activity. = 久坐的工作如果不配合体育锻炼，会对健康有害。


疑问句:
Is your job sedentary? = 你的工作是久坐的吗？
Are you concerned about the health implications of a sedentary lifestyle? = 你担心久坐的生活方式对健康的隐患吗？


否定句:
Her lifestyle is not sedentary; she's very active. = 她的生活方式不是久坐的；她非常活跃。
The job description explicitly states that it is not a sedentary position. = 工作描述明确指出这不是一份久坐的工作。
