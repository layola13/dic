commentary:/ˈkɒməntəri/ /ˈkɑːmənˌteri/| n.|解说；评论；注释|A spoken or written explanation or interpretation of something.|例句：The game was shown with expert commentary.  这场比赛配有专家解说。|例句：Her commentary on the political situation was insightful. 她对政治局势的评论很有见地。|近义词：explanation, interpretation, analysis, critique|反义词：silence, absence
