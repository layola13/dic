mean:/miːn/| /min/ | vt. vi. n. adj.| 意思是；意图；平均数；吝啬的；刻薄的 |to express or represent something such as an idea, thought, or fact; to intend or plan to do something; the average of a set of numbers; unwilling to give or share things; unkind or unpleasant |例句：What does this word mean?  这个词是什么意思？  I didn't mean to hurt your feelings. 我并非有意伤害你的感情。|近义词：signify, intend, signify, average, stingy, cruel |反义词：generous, kind, nice,  pleasant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He means well, but he's clumsy. = 他心地善良，但他很笨拙。
What does this symbol mean? = 这个符号是什么意思？


一般过去时:
She meant to call, but she forgot. = 她本来打算打电话，但她忘了。
I didn't mean to offend you. = 我无意冒犯你。


现在完成时:
I have meant to write to you for weeks. = 我几周来一直想给你写信。
What has this experience meant to you? = 这段经历对你意味着什么？


过去完成时:
He had meant to go to the party, but he was sick. = 他本来打算去参加派对，但他病了。
I had meant to finish the report yesterday. = 我本来打算昨天完成报告的。


将来时:
I will mean to be there on time. = 我打算准时到那里。
What will this decision mean for our future? = 这个决定对我们的未来意味着什么？


虚拟语气:
I wish I had meant to be more careful. = 我真希望我当时更小心一些。
If I had meant to hurt you, I would have apologized. = 如果我打算伤害你，我会道歉的。


被动语态:
His words were meant to be encouraging. = 他的话本意是鼓励人的。
The average was meant to be calculated differently. = 平均数本来应该用不同的方法计算。


疑问句:
What do you mean by that? = 你这是什么意思？
What does this passage mean? = 这段话是什么意思？


否定句:
I didn't mean to interrupt. = 我无意打断。
He didn't mean any harm. = 他并无恶意。



PHRASE->
mean to do something = 打算做某事
mean something to someone = 对某人来说很重要
mean business = 说到做到；认真
by all means = 当然；务必
it means a lot to me = 这对我意义重大
