overcredit:/ˈoʊvərkrɛdɪt/ | v. | 过度记入贷方；给予过多的信用 | To credit (someone or something) with more than is due or deserved.  | Example: The bank overcredited my account by $100.  银行将我的账户多记入了100美元。  |近义词: overvalue, exaggerate, inflate | 反义词: undercredit, undervalue


USAGE->

The bank overcredits accounts occasionally. = 银行偶尔会将账户记入过多贷方。
He overcredits his own abilities. = 他过高估计了自己的能力。
