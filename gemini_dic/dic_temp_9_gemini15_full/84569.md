hammers:/ˈhæmərz/| n.|锤子；反复敲打|A hammer is a tool used for driving nails, breaking things, or shaping metal.  It typically consists of a heavy head and a handle.|例句：He used a hammer to drive the nail into the wall. 他用锤子把钉子钉进墙里。


例句：The blacksmith hammered the metal into shape.  铁匠用锤子把金属敲打成型。


近义词：mallet, sledgehammer|反义词：None


词源:  The word "hammer" comes from Old English "hammor," which itself has Proto-Germanic and Proto-Indo-European roots related to striking or hitting.


USAGE->

He hammers nails into the wood. = 他把钉子钉进木头里。
The machine hammers the metal sheet. = 机器敲打金属板。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
He has hammered all the nails in. = 他已经把所有的钉子都钉进去了。

过去完成时:

They had hammered the agreement before the meeting. = 会议之前他们已经敲定了协议。
