noontides:/ˈnuːntaɪdz/ | n. |正午，中午 |The word "noontides" refers to the time of noon, specifically the period around midday.  It evokes a sense of the sun being at its highest point, often associated with warmth and brightness.  The plural form suggests a recurring or extended period of midday. |例句：The noontides were particularly hot and still in the desert.  沙漠中的正午格外炎热而平静。The golden light of the noontides bathed the landscape. 正午的金色阳光沐浴着这片土地。|近义词：midday, noon, high noon |反义词：midnight
