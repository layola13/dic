noncompeting:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpiːtɪŋ/| adj. |非竞争的，不相竞争的|Not competing; not in competition with each other.  This term often refers to groups or products that do not directly vie for the same resources or customers.|The two companies, despite operating in the same industry, offer noncompeting products.|近义词：uncompetitive, separate, distinct|反义词：competitive, rivalrous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
These products are noncompeting in the market. = 这些产品在市场上没有竞争关系。
The two teams are noncompeting for the same trophy. = 这两支队伍没有争夺同一个奖杯。

一般过去时:
Their businesses were noncompeting before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们的企业没有竞争关系。
The two candidates were considered noncompeting in the election. = 在选举中，这两位候选人被认为没有竞争关系。

现在完成时:
These companies have remained noncompeting for many years. = 这些公司多年来一直没有竞争关系。
The two divisions have successfully stayed noncompeting despite sharing resources. = 尽管共享资源，这两个部门成功地保持了不相竞争的状态。

过去完成时:
Before the new regulations, the industries had been largely noncompeting. = 在新的法规出台之前，这些行业基本上没有竞争关系。
It had been clear that their strategies were noncompeting. = 很明显，他们的策略是互不竞争的。

将来时:
These markets will likely remain noncompeting in the foreseeable future. = 在可预见的未来，这些市场可能会保持不相竞争的状态。
The two products will likely continue to be noncompeting. = 这两种产品可能会继续保持不相竞争的状态。

虚拟语气:
If their products were truly noncompeting, there would be no conflict. = 如果他们的产品确实是互不竞争的，就不会有冲突。
It would be ideal if all market segments were noncompeting. = 如果所有市场细分都是互不竞争的，那就最理想了。

被动语态:
The products were considered noncompeting by the regulatory body. = 这些产品被监管机构认为是互不竞争的。
The two companies' strategies were deemed noncompeting. = 这两家公司的策略被认为是互不竞争的。

疑问句:
Are these services noncompeting? = 这些服务是不竞争的吗？
Were the bids noncompeting? = 这些投标是不竞争的吗？

否定句:
The companies are not noncompeting; they are fierce rivals. = 这些公司并非互不竞争；它们是激烈的竞争对手。
The two groups are not noncompeting; they share the same customer base. = 这两组并非互不竞争；它们共享相同的客户群。
