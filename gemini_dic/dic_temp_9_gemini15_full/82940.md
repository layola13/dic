gumshoes:/ˈɡʌmʃuz/| n. |便衣警察，侦探|Informal term for a detective or police officer, especially one working undercover.  The term likely originates from the rubber-soled shoes often worn by such officers.|The gumshoes were tailing the suspect for days.|近义词：detective, investigator, private eye|反义词：none specifically, but could be contrasted with uniformed police officer.
