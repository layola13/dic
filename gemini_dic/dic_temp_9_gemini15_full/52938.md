dine:/daɪn/ | v. | 进餐，吃饭 | To eat a meal, especially a formal or elaborate one.  |  We dined at a Michelin-starred restaurant last night.  我们昨晚在一个米其林星级餐厅用餐。| eat, sup, feast | starve, fast | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We dine at seven o'clock every night. = 我们每晚七点吃饭。
They dine out at least twice a week. = 他们每周至少外出就餐两次。

一般过去时:
I dined with my family yesterday. = 我昨天和家人一起吃饭。
They dined on lobster at the seaside restaurant. = 他们在海边餐厅吃龙虾。

现在完成时:
We have dined at this restaurant several times. = 我们在这家餐厅吃过几次饭了。
She has never dined at such a luxurious place before. = 她以前从未在如此豪华的地方用餐过。

过去完成时:
We had already dined before the theatre performance began. = 在戏剧演出开始之前，我们已经吃过饭了。
They had dined lavishly before attending the ball. = 他们盛宴之后才参加舞会。

将来时:
We will dine at the new Italian restaurant tomorrow. = 我们明天将在新的意大利餐厅吃饭。
They will dine elegantly at the hotel's fine dining establishment. = 他们将在酒店的高级餐厅优雅地用餐。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would dine at a more expensive restaurant. = 如果我有更多的钱，我会去一家更贵的餐厅吃饭。
I wish I could dine at that famous restaurant. = 我希望我能在那家著名的餐厅吃饭。

被动语态:
Dinner will be dined at eight o'clock tonight. = 今晚八点钟将要开始用餐。(较为少见用法)
The food was dined on with great relish. = 这食物吃得很香。(较为少见用法)


疑问句:
Where will you dine tonight? = 你今晚在哪里吃饭？
What time do you usually dine? = 你通常几点吃饭？


否定句:
I didn't dine out last night. = 我昨晚没有外出吃饭。
They do not dine at home very often. = 他们不经常在家吃饭。
