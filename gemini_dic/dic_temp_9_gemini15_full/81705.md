greenhouses:/ˈɡriːnhaʊzɪz/| n.| 温室；暖房 | Structures with walls and roof made chiefly of transparent material, used for growing plants, especially in climates with harsh weather.  |例句：Many farmers use greenhouses to extend the growing season. 许多农民使用温室来延长生长季节。

例句：The university's botany department has several research greenhouses. 大学植物系有几个研究温室。


近义词：hothouses, glasshouses | 反义词：open field, outdoors


词源:  The word "greenhouse" is a compound word. "Green" refers to the plants grown inside, and "house" refers to the structure.  The term evolved over time from earlier terms like "hot house" or "glasshouse," reflecting the technology's development.
