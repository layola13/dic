They may take words out of context, paraphrase inaccurately, or simply remember incorrectly.  |Example:  The misquoters deliberately twisted the politician's words to make him seem like a hypocrite.  误引用者故意曲解这位政治家的言论，使他看起来像个伪君子。|近义词：distorters, misinterpreters |反义词：accurate reporters, faithful paraphrasers |名词
