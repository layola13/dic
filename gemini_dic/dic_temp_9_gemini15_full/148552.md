overswim:/ˈoʊvərswɪm/| v.| 游泳过度；游得太久|To swim for too long or too far, often to the point of exhaustion or danger.  |例句：He overswam in the strong current and nearly drowned. 他在急流中游得太久，差点淹死。|例句：The children overswam in the pool and were too tired to walk home. 孩子们在游泳池里游得太久了，累得走不动路回家了。|近义词：overexert, exhaust|反义词：under swim (not a common word)
