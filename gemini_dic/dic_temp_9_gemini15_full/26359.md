by-chance: /baɪ ˈtʃæns/ | adj. |偶然地；意外地| Happening unexpectedly or by accident.  |例句：I met her by chance at the supermarket.  我在超市偶然遇到了她。 |近义词：accidentally, coincidentally, unexpectedly |反义词：intentionally, deliberately, purposely


USAGE->
