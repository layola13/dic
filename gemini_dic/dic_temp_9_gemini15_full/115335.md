mannitose:/ˈmænɪtoʊs/| n. |甘露醇|A hexahydric alcohol, a sugar alcohol derived from mannose. It is a white, crystalline powder that is soluble in water and slightly soluble in alcohol. Mannitol is used as a sweetener, laxative, and diuretic. It is also used in the manufacture of pharmaceuticals and other products.|Mannitol is a naturally occurring sugar alcohol found in many plants.  The body does not metabolize mannitol efficiently, so it is often used as a diuretic to increase urine output.|近义词：sugar alcohol|反义词：N/A
