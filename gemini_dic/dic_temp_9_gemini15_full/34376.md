classific:/ˈklæsɪfaɪ/| v.|分类，归类|To arrange or organize into categories or classes according to shared qualities or characteristics.|The librarian carefully classified the new books according to subject.|近义词：categorize, sort, group, arrange|反义词：jumble, mix, confuse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She classifies the documents by date. = 她按日期对文件进行分类。
The scientist classifies the species based on their DNA. = 科学家根据它们的DNA对物种进行分类。

一般过去时:
He classified the stamps according to their country of origin. = 他根据邮票的原产国对它们进行了分类。
The teacher classified the students into different ability groups. = 老师把学生分成不同的能力组。

现在完成时:
They have classified all the data for the research paper. = 他们已经将所有数据都分类用于研究论文。
She has classified the images into different categories for easier searching. = 她已将图像分类到不同的类别中，以便于搜索。

过去完成时:
Before the presentation, he had classified the information into a clear structure. = 在演示之前，他已将信息分类成清晰的结构。
The researchers had classified the fossils before publishing their findings. = 研究人员在发表他们的研究结果之前已经对化石进行了分类。

将来时:

The team will classify the data using a new algorithm. = 该团队将使用新算法对数据进行分类。

虚拟语气:
