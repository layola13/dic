petre: /ˈpiːtrə/ | n. |硝石；火药成分| A naturally occurring mineral consisting of potassium nitrate (KNO₃), also known as saltpeter. It was historically important as a source of potassium for fertilizer and as a key ingredient in gunpowder.  | Example:  The alchemist carefully measured the petre for the gunpowder.  炼金术士仔细测量了火药中的硝石。|近义词：saltpeter, potassium nitrate |反义词：无 |词性:名词
