pathogenic:/ˌpæθəˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj.|致病的，病原体的|Causing or able to cause disease.|例句：Many bacteria are pathogenic and can cause various illnesses. 许多细菌是有致病性的，可以引起各种疾病。

例句：The pathogenic fungus caused a widespread crop failure. 致病真菌导致了大范围的作物歉收。


近义词：disease-causing, infectious, virulent|反义词：benign, harmless, innocuous

词源:  From the Greek word "pathos" (suffering, disease) and "genesis" (origin, birth).  Therefore, literally meaning "originating from disease".
