excurse:/ɪkˈskɜːrs(ə)n/| n.| 远足；短途旅行；离题；(谈话或写作中的)离题 | A short journey or trip, especially one taken for pleasure.  It can also refer to a digression in speech or writing. |例句：We took an excursion to the seaside. 我们去海边远足了。
The lecturer's excursion into the history of the subject was fascinating. 讲师对该主题历史的离题讨论很有趣。|近义词：outing, trip, detour, digression |反义词：direct route, main point


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school organizes many excursions every year. = 学校每年组织许多次远足。
This excursion takes us through some beautiful countryside. = 这趟短途旅行将带我们穿过一些美丽的乡村。


一般过去时:
We went on a fascinating excursion to the mountains last weekend. = 上周末我们去山区进行了一次令人着迷的远足。
The teacher's excursion into poetry captivated the students. = 老师对诗歌的离题讨论吸引了学生们。


现在完成时:
They have already planned several excursions for the summer. = 他们已经为夏天计划好几次远足了。
The author has made several excursions from the main argument in this chapter. = 作者在本章中多次偏离了主要论点。


过去完成时:
Before the main lecture, the professor had made a brief excursion into relevant theories. = 在主要讲座之前，教授简要地介绍了相关的理论。
We had completed several excursions before the weather turned bad. = 天气变坏之前，我们已经完成了多次远足。


将来时:
We will take an excursion to the zoo next week. = 我们下周将去动物园远足。
The speaker will likely make an excursion into current events during his presentation. = 演讲者在他的演讲中可能会谈及一些时事。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would take an excursion to the national park. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们将去国家公园远足。
If the writer had avoided the many excursions, the article would have been more concise. = 如果作者避免了多次离题，文章就会更简洁。


被动语态:
Many excursions are organized by the travel agency every year. = 旅行社每年都会组织许多次远足。
The speaker's digressions were often perceived as unnecessary excursions from the topic. = 演讲者的离题讨论常常被认为是不必要的偏离主题。


疑问句:
Will there be any excursions offered during the conference? = 会议期间会提供任何远足活动吗？
Are there any excursions planned for the students this semester? = 这个学期为学生们计划了哪些远足活动？


否定句:
We didn't take any excursions during our vacation. = 我们在假期期间没有进行任何远足。
The essay avoids unnecessary excursions into unrelated topics. = 这篇文章避免了不必要的偏离主题的讨论。
