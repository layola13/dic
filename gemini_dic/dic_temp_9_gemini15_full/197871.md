shipments:/ˈʃɪpmənts/| n.|货物运输；装运；运送的货物|The process of sending goods; the goods sent.|例句：The shipments arrived on time.  货物准时到达了。|例句：We are expecting a large shipment of new products next week. 我们预计下周会有大量新产品到货。|近义词：consignments, deliveries, transports|反义词：receipts


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company makes regular shipments to its customers. = 公司定期向客户发货。
Shipments often arrive late due to bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，货物经常延误。


一般过去时:
They made several large shipments last month. = 他们上个月做了几次大规模的运输。
The shipments arrived damaged. = 货物运送时受损了。


现在完成时:
We have received all the shipments. = 我们已经收到了所有的货物。
The company has made significant improvements in its shipments' efficiency. = 公司在其货运效率方面取得了显著的改进。


过去完成时:
They had already completed the shipments before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经完成了发货。
The problem had been identified before the shipments left the warehouse. = 在货物离开仓库之前，问题就已经被发现了。


将来时:
We will make the shipments next week. = 我们下周发货。
The shipments will arrive by Friday. = 货物将在周五之前到达。


虚拟语气:
If we had more trucks, we could make more shipments. = 如果我们有更多的卡车，我们可以运送更多的货物。
I wish the shipments had arrived earlier. = 我希望货物早点到达。


被动语态:
The shipments were delayed by the storm. = 货物因暴风雨而延误。
The shipments are being processed at the warehouse. = 货物正在仓库里进行处理。


疑问句:
When will the shipments arrive? = 货物什么时候到达？
What is the status of the shipments? = 货物的状态如何？


否定句:
The shipments did not arrive on time. = 货物没有准时到达。
We haven't received the shipments yet. = 我们还没收到货物。
