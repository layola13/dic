liquidizer: /ˈlɪkwɪdaɪzər/ | n. | 液化器；果汁机 | A kitchen appliance used to blend or liquefy foods, such as fruits, vegetables, and ice cream.  It typically consists of a motor-driven blade assembly that rotates at high speed within a container. | Example: I used the liquidizer to make a delicious smoothie this morning.  今早我用果汁机做了一杯美味的奶昔。|近义词：blender, mixer | 反义词：None.
