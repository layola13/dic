dinero:/diˈnɛroʊ/| n. |钱，现金 |Spanish word for money. It refers to cash or currency.  |例句：I don't have enough dinero to buy that car. 我没有足够的钱买那辆车。|近义词：money, cash, funds |反义词：debt, poverty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has a lot of dinero. = 他有很多钱。
How much dinero do you need? = 你需要多少钱？


一般过去时:
She spent all her dinero. = 她花光了所有的钱。
Did he have enough dinero for the trip? = 他有足够的钱去旅行吗？


现在完成时:
I have earned enough dinero for a vacation. = 我已经赚够钱去度假了。
Have they saved enough dinero for a down payment? = 他们已经存够首付的钱了吗？


过去完成时:
He had already spent his dinero before the sale started. = 在促销开始之前他已经花光了钱。
Had she saved enough dinero before the emergency? = 在紧急情况发生之前她存够钱了吗？


将来时:
I will have enough dinero next month. = 下个月我将有足够的钱。
Will they have enough dinero to buy a house? = 他们会有足够的钱买房子吗？


虚拟语气:
If I had more dinero, I would travel the world. = 如果我有更多钱，我会周游世界。
I wish I had more dinero to help my family. = 我希望我有更多钱来帮助我的家人。


被动语态:
The dinero was stolen. = 钱被偷了。
The dinero is being counted. = 钱正在被清点。


疑问句:
How much dinero do you have? = 你有多少现金？
Where did you get the dinero? = 你从哪里弄到钱的？


否定句:
I don't have any dinero. = 我没有钱。
He didn't have enough dinero to pay the bill. = 他没有足够的钱付账单。


PHRASE->
mucho dinero = a lot of money  很多钱
dinero en efectivo = cash  现金
ganar dinero = to make money  赚钱
gastar dinero = to spend money  花钱
falta de dinero = lack of money  缺钱
