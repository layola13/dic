foreguess:/fɔːrˈɡɛs/| vt.|预料，预知|To guess or predict something in advance; to anticipate.|例句：I foreguessed that the meeting would be unproductive. 我预料会议不会有什么成果。|例句：She foreguessed the outcome of the election. 她预料到了选举的结果。|近义词：predict, anticipate, foresee, forecast|反义词：misjudge, underestimate

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He foreguesses the market trends. 他预测市场趋势。
They foreguess the winner of the race. 他们预料比赛的获胜者。

一般过去时:
She foreguessed the problem and prepared a solution. 她预料到了这个问题并准备了解决方案。
I foreguessed his reaction. 我预料到了他的反应。

现在完成时:
We have foreguessed the consequences of our actions. 我们已经预料到了我们行为的后果。
They have foreguessed the difficulties. 他们已经预料到了困难。

过去完成时:
I had foreguessed that he would be late. 我预料到他会迟到。
She had foreguessed the outcome of the negotiation. 她预料到了谈判的结果。

将来时:
He will foreguess the weather conditions for tomorrow. 他将预测明天的天气情况。
They will foreguess the results of the experiment. 他们将预测实验的结果。

虚拟语气:
If I had foreguessed the storm, I would have stayed home. 如果我预料到暴风雨，我就待在家里了。
I wish I had foreguessed the traffic jam. 我希望我预料到了交通堵塞。

被动语态:
The outcome was foreguessed by many experts. 结果被许多专家预料到了。
The winner could not be foreguessed. 赢家无法预料。

疑问句:
Can you foreguess the future? 你能预知未来吗？
Did she foreguess the results correctly? 她是否正确地预料到了结果？

否定句:
I couldn't foreguess what he would do. 我无法预料他会做什么。
They didn't foreguess the market crash. 他们没有预料到市场崩盘。
