foxily:/ˈfɒksɪli/| adj.|狡猾地，像狐狸般地|In a sly or cunning manner, characteristic of a fox.  The adjective is derived from the noun "fox," referring to the animal known for its cunning.|例句：He looked at me foxily, a mischievous glint in his eyes. 他狡猾地望着我，眼中闪着淘气的光芒。
She answered the question foxily, avoiding a direct response. 她狡猾地回答了这个问题，避免了直接回应。|近义词：slyly, cunningly, craftily, artfully|反义词：honestly, openly, frankly
