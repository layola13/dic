realistically:/ˌriːəˈlɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 现实地；实际地 |  In a realistic manner; taking into account the facts and possibilities.  It considers what is actually possible or likely to happen, rather than what is ideal or hoped for. | 例句：Realistically, we need more time to complete the project. 现实地讲，我们需要更多时间来完成这个项目。

例句：Realistically speaking, winning the lottery is highly improbable.  现实地说，中彩票的可能性极低。


近义词：practically, actually, in practice, realistically, truly


反义词：idealistically, unrealistically, impossibly


词性：副词
