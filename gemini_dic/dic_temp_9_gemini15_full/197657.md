sherpa:/ˈʃɜːrpə/| n.|夏尔巴人；夏尔巴向导|A member of a people living in the Himalayas, especially in Nepal, known for their skill in mountaineering.  Sherpas are often employed as guides and porters by mountaineering expeditions.|例句：Many Sherpas guide climbers up Mount Everest.  许多夏尔巴人引导登山者攀登珠穆朗玛峰。

例句：Tenzing Norgay, a Sherpa, was one of the first people to climb Mount Everest. 丹增·诺尔盖，一位夏尔巴人，是首批攀登珠穆朗玛峰的人之一。|近义词：guide, porter, mountaineer|反义词：None specifically.
