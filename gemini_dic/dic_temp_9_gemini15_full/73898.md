forsakers:/fərˈseɪkərz/| n. |抛弃者；遗弃者 |Those who abandon or leave something or someone.  People who forsake something are characterized by their abandonment of it.  The term often carries a connotation of leaving something behind in a negligent or irresponsible manner.|例句：The forsakers of their families faced harsh criticism from the community.  遗弃家庭的人面临着社区的严厉批评。The forsakers of their promises were held accountable for their actions.  那些食言的人要为他们的行为负责。|近义词：abandoners, deserters, relinquishers|反义词：supporters, protectors, keepers|名词
