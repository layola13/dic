expediate:/ɪkˈspiːdieɪt/ | vt. | 加速；促进；加快 | To make something happen faster or more quickly.  |The government is taking steps to expediate the process. 政府正在采取措施来加快这一进程。| accelerate, hasten, expedite | delay, hinder, obstruct | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They expedite the delivery process. = 他们加快了送货流程。
We expedite the application process for qualified candidates. = 我们为合格的候选人加快申请流程。


一般过去时:
The manager expedited the report's completion. = 经理加快了报告的完成。
They expedited the shipment to meet the deadline. = 他们加快了运输速度以赶上最后期限。


现在完成时:
We have expedited the visa applications for all participants. = 我们已经为所有参与者加快了签证申请。
The company has expedited the product launch to gain a market advantage. = 公司已经加快了产品发布速度以获得市场优势。


过去完成时:
They had expedited the project before the crisis hit. = 在危机发生之前，他们已经加快了项目的进度。
She had expedited the paperwork so that we could leave early. = 她已经加快了文书工作，以便我们可以早点离开。


将来时:
We will expedite the repairs as soon as possible. = 我们将尽快加快维修速度。
The company will expedite the order to satisfy the customer's request. = 公司将加快订单处理以满足客户的要求。


虚拟语气:
If we expedited the process, we could finish earlier. = 如果我们加快流程，我们可以早点完成。
I wish they had expedited the delivery; I needed it yesterday. = 我希望他们当时加快了送货速度；我昨天就需要它了。


被动语态:
The process was expedited to meet the deadline. = 为了赶上最后期限，流程得到了加快。
The shipment was expedited by air freight. = 货物通过空运加快了运输速度。


疑问句:
Can we expedite this order? = 我们能加快这个订单的处理吗？
How can we expedite the decision-making process? = 我们如何才能加快决策过程？


否定句:
They did not expedite the application. = 他们没有加快申请的处理。
The company failed to expedite the delivery. = 公司未能加快送货速度。


PHRASE->
expedite a process = 加快流程
expedite delivery = 加快递送
expedite shipping = 加快运输
expedite the approval = 加快批准
expedite a request = 加快请求的处理


词源:
From Latin *expeditāre "to free from entanglements," from ex- "out" + ped-, stem of pes "foot."  The original meaning was to disentangle or free from difficulties, which evolved to the modern sense of speeding up a process.
