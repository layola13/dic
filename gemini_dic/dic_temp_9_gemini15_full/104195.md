junkyards:/ˈdʒʌŋkjɑːrdz/| n.|废旧货场，垃圾场|A junkyard is a place where discarded or unwanted items, especially vehicles, are stored.  It is often characterized by a disorganized collection of scrap metal, broken machinery, and other refuse.|例句：The old car was taken to the junkyard. 这辆旧车被送到了废旧货场。

例句：Many valuable parts can be salvaged from junkyards. 许多有价值的零件可以从废旧货场中抢救出来。


近义词：scrap yard, salvage yard, wrecking yard|反义词：showroom, factory
