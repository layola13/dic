harnessing:/ˈhɑːrnɪsɪŋ/ | v. | 利用；驾驭；控制 | The act of controlling and making use of natural resources, especially to produce energy.  | 例句：The company is exploring new ways of harnessing solar energy.  这家公司正在探索利用太阳能的新方法。 | 例句：The project aims to harness the power of the wind to generate electricity.  该项目旨在利用风力发电。 | 近义词：exploiting, utilizing, employing | 反义词：neglecting, ignoring, wasting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They harness the power of the river. = 他们利用河水的力量。
We harness wind energy to generate electricity. = 我们利用风能发电。

一般过去时:
The farmers harnessed their horses to the plow. = 农民们把他们的马套在犁上。
They harnessed the power of the sun to heat their homes. = 他们利用太阳能来取暖。

现在完成时:
Scientists have harnessed the power of the atom. = 科学家们已经利用了原子的力量。
We have harnessed renewable energy sources to reduce our carbon footprint. = 我们已经利用可再生能源来减少我们的碳足迹。

过去完成时:
Before the invention of the steam engine, people had harnessed animal power for centuries. = 在蒸汽机发明之前，人们已经利用动物动力几个世纪了。
They had harnessed the river for irrigation long before the dam was built. = 在大坝建成之前，他们就利用河水灌溉了很久。

将来时:
We will harness the power of artificial intelligence to improve efficiency. = 我们将利用人工智能的力量来提高效率。
The company plans to harness geothermal energy to provide sustainable heat. = 公司计划利用地热能提供可持续的热能。

虚拟语气:
If we harnessed all available resources, we could solve the energy crisis. = 如果我们利用所有可用的资源，我们就能解决能源危机。
I wish we could harness the power of the ocean currents more effectively. = 我希望我们能够更有效地利用洋流的力量。

被动语态:
The energy of the wind is harnessed to turn turbines. = 风能被用来驱动涡轮机。
The river's potential is being harnessed for hydroelectric power. = 河流的潜力正在被用来发电。

疑问句:
How can we harness the power of the tides? = 我们如何利用潮汐的力量？
Are they harnessing solar energy in this building? = 这栋建筑物是否在利用太阳能？

否定句:
They did not harness the full potential of the technology. = 他们没有充分利用这项技术的潜力。
We cannot harness this energy source without significant investment. = 没有大量的投资，我们无法利用这种能源。
