killings:/ˈkɪlɪŋz/| n. |谋杀；杀戮；屠杀|The plural form of "killing," referring to multiple instances of murder or slaughter. It often implies a significant number of deaths, or deaths that are particularly brutal or systematic.  The word can also refer to the act of killing animals for food or other purposes, although this is less common in this plural form.|例句：The recent killings in the city have shocked the nation. 最近城市发生的谋杀案震惊了全国。

The mass killings during the war were a horrific tragedy. 战争期间的大规模屠杀是一场可怕的悲剧。|近义词：murders, slaughters, massacres, assassinations |反义词：saving, rescuing, preserving
