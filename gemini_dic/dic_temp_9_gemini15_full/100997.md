invalidism:/ˈɪnvælidɪzəm/| n. |伤残；长期病弱；使无效；作废|The state of being an invalid;  a chronic state of ill health preventing normal activity.  It can also refer to the act of making something invalid or the belief that one is incapable of achieving anything. |例句：His invalidism prevented him from working. 他的伤残使他无法工作。
  
His prolonged invalidism led to a sense of isolation. 他长期体弱多病导致了一种孤立感。


近义词：debility, infirmity, disability, sickness|反义词：health, wellness, validity
