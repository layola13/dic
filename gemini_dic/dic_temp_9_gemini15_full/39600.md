consecution:/ˌkɒnsɪˈkjuːʃən/| /ˌkɑːnsɪˈkjuːʃən/| n. |连续；相继；结果|The logical connection or consequence of events or actions following one another. It refers to a series of events or things that follow one another in order.  |例句：The prosecution presented a series of events in close consecution to prove the defendant's guilt. 检方呈上一系列紧密相连的事件来证明被告有罪。|例句：The quick consecution of events left her feeling overwhelmed.  一系列事件的快速发生让她感到不知所措。|近义词：succession, sequence, series, continuity |反义词：discontinuity, interruption
