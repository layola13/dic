outspurn:/aʊtˈspɜːrn/| vt.| 摒弃；抛弃；拒绝；蔑视 | To reject or refuse decisively and contemptuously; to spurn (something) decisively.  |例句：He outspurned their offer of help, believing he could manage alone. 他断然拒绝了他们提供的帮助，因为他相信自己能应付。

例句：The company outspurned the merger proposal, deeming it financially unfavorable. 公司拒绝了合并提案，认为它在经济上不划算。


近义词：reject, refuse, scorn, disdain, repudiate

反义词：accept, embrace, welcome


词性：动词
