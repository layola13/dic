flanging:/ˈflændʒɪŋ/| n. |法兰；凸缘；卷边；（音频效果）镶边效果 |The process of creating a flanging effect in audio, typically by using two identical audio signals and introducing a slight delay to one before mixing them.  This creates a characteristic whooshing or jet plane sound.|例句：The guitarist used a flanging effect to create a swirling sound. 吉他手使用了镶边效果来创造一种旋转的声音。The recording engineer added flanging to the vocals to give them a more dramatic effect.录音工程师在人声中加入了镶边效果，使其更有戏剧性。|近义词：phasing, chorus|反义词：None specifically.
