learns:/lɜːrnz/ | v. |学习；获悉|To gain knowledge or skill in (something) by study, experience, or being taught.|例句：She learns quickly. 她学得很快。|例句：He learns best by doing. 他在实践中学得最好。|近义词：studies, acquires, masters|反义词：forgets, ignores, unlearns


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He learns English every day. = 他每天学习英语。
She learns a new recipe each week. = 她每周学习一个新菜谱。

一般过去时:
I learned to ride a bike when I was five. = 我五岁时学会了骑自行车。
They learned about the accident on the news. = 他们从新闻上得知了事故。

现在完成时:
I have learned a lot from this experience. = 我从这次经历中学到了很多。
She has learned to play the piano. = 她学会了弹钢琴。

过去完成时:
I had learned Spanish before I went to Mexico. = 我去墨西哥之前已经学过西班牙语了。
They had learned the lesson before the test. = 考试前他们已经吸取了教训。

将来时:
I will learn to drive next year. = 我明年将学习开车。
He will learn more about the subject in college. = 他将在大学里学习更多关于这个主题的知识。

虚拟语气:
If I learned harder, I would pass the exam. = 如果我学习更努力，我就能通过考试。
I wish I had learned more about history. = 我希望我以前学过更多历史知识。

被动语态:
The lesson is learned by the students. = 学生们学习了这节课。(被动语态强调课被学习)
New skills are learned through practice. = 新技能是通过练习学习的。

疑问句:
What do you learn in school? = 你在学校学什么？
Does he learn easily? = 他学习容易吗？

否定句:
He doesn't learn easily. = 他学习不容易。
I didn't learn much in that class. = 我在那节课上没学到多少东西。


PHRASE->
learn by heart = 记住；背诵
learn from = 向…学习
learn to = 学习…
learn about = 了解
learn of = 获悉
