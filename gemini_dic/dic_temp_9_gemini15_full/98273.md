insession:/ɪnˈsɛʃən/| adj.| 会议中的；开会的；正在进行的 |  In session means that a meeting, court, or legislative body is currently meeting and conducting business.  | The committee is in session, discussing the new budget.  委员会正在开会，讨论新的预算。|  ongoing, active, underway | adjourned, recessed, concluded |  形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The court is in session. = 法院正在开庭。
The parliament is in session. = 议会正在开会。


一般过去时:
The meeting was in session for three hours. = 会议进行了三个小时。
The committee was in session when the news broke. = 消息传来时，委员会正在开会。


现在完成时:
The conference has been in session for two days. = 会议已经进行了两天。
The jury has been in session all morning. = 陪审团整个上午都在开会。


过去完成时:
The debate had been in session for hours before the vote. = 投票前，辩论已经进行了数小时。
The trial had been in session for a week when the unexpected witness appeared. = 审判进行了一周后，意外的证人出现了。


将来时:
The council will be in session next week. = 理事会下周将开会。
The hearing will be in session until late afternoon. = 听证会将持续到下午晚些时候。


虚拟语气:
If the council were in session, we could present our proposal. = 如果理事会正在开会，我们可以提交我们的提案。
I wish the court were in session so we could get this resolved quickly. = 我希望法院正在开庭，这样我们就能很快解决这个问题。


被动语态:
It was announced that the meeting was in session. = 宣布会议正在进行中。
It is expected that the committee will be in session for several more days. = 预计委员会还将开会几天。


疑问句:
Is the committee in session? = 委员会正在开会吗？
Is the court in session yet? = 法院开庭了吗？


否定句:
The meeting was not in session when I arrived. = 我到达时会议还没有开始。
The court was not in session that day. = 那天法院没有开庭。


PHRASE->
in session now = 现在正在开会
remain in session = 保持开会状态
go into session = 开始开会
out of session = 休会；闭会
during the session = 在会议期间
