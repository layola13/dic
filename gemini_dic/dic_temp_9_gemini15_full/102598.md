jailbreaks:/ˈdʒeɪlbreɪks/ | n. v. |越狱；越狱行为；破解(软件或系统)|The act of breaking out of jail; or the act of circumventing security restrictions on a device, typically a smartphone or tablet, to gain root access.  |例句：The prisoner attempted a daring jailbreak.  囚犯试图进行一次大胆的越狱。


The company released a software update to prevent jailbreaks. 公司发布了软件更新以防止越狱。|近义词：breakout, escape, hack, circumvent |反义词：imprisonment, confinement, security
