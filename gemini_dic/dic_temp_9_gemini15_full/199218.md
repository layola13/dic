silkweed:/ˈsɪlkwid/| n. |丝草，马利筋属植物|A plant of the genus *Asclepias*, having milky juice and usually bearing pods containing silky hairs.  Many species are known as milkweeds.  |Example:  The monarch butterfly lays its eggs on milkweed plants.  帝王蝶将卵产在马利筋属植物上。


The silkweed is a valuable nectar source for pollinators.  马利筋是传粉昆虫宝贵的蜜源植物。|近义词：milkweed|反义词：无
