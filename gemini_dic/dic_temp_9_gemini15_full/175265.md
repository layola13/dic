词源:
The word "rainbowed" is derived from the noun "rainbow," which has Old English roots ("regnboga," meaning "rain-bow").  The "-ed" suffix transforms the noun into an adjective, describing something that possesses the qualities of a rainbow, specifically its vibrant, multicolored appearance.
