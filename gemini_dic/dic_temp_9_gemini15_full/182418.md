renewably:/rɪˈnjuːəbli/| adv.| 可再生地，可持续地 |  Derived from "renewable," meaning capable of being renewed or replaced.  It describes something done or produced in a way that replenishes its resources, often referencing sustainable energy sources. | Example:  The city aims to power its buildings renewably using solar and wind energy.  这座城市的目标是利用太阳能和风能可持续地为其建筑物供电。|近义词：sustainably, sustainably. |反义词：unsustainably, nonrenewably.
