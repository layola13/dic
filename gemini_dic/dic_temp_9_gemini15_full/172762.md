quadrel:/ˈkwɑːdrəl/| n.| 方格；四方形的图案 | A small, usually square, pane of glass.  It is often used in windows or other decorative applications.  |例句：The stained-glass window was composed of hundreds of individual quadrels.  = 彩绘玻璃窗由数百个独立的方格组成。


The church's windows featured intricate designs made from small quadrels. = 教堂的窗户以由小方格组成的复杂设计为特色。


近义词：square, panel, lozenge (if diamond-shaped) | 反义词：无


词源:  The word "quadrel" comes from the Old French word "quadrelle," which itself derives from the Latin word "quadrus," meaning "square."  The suffix "-el" is a diminutive suffix, suggesting something small.
