skedaddled:/skɛˈdædəld/| v.|溜走，逃走，迅速离开|To leave quickly and secretly, often to avoid trouble or responsibility.  The word is informal and often humorous.|例句：The thief skedaddled after the alarm went off. 小偷在警报响起后溜走了。

例句：When the police arrived, the suspects skedaddled into the night. 当警察到达时，嫌疑犯逃进了黑夜。


近义词：absconded, vamoosed, hightailed it, bolted, beat a hasty retreat|反义词：stayed, remained, lingered

词源:
