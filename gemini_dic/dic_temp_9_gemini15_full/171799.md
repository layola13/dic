punctually:/ˈpʌŋktʃuəli/ | adv. | 准时地，按时地 |  Punctually means on time or at the scheduled time.  It emphasizes the precision and exactness of being on time. |  He arrived punctually for the meeting. 他准时到达了会议。|  promptly, precisely, on time | late, tardily

词源:
The word "punctually" derives from the Latin word "punctualis," meaning "point in time," which further originates from "punctum," meaning "point."  The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective "punctual" into an adverb, indicating "in a punctual manner."
