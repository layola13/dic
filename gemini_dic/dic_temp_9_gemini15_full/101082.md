inventory:/ˈɪnvənˌtɔːri/| n. |清单；库存；财产目录 |A detailed list of all the items in stock; the goods or materials that a business has available for sale or use.  |例句：The company conducted a thorough inventory of its assets.  这家公司对其资产进行了彻底的清点。

例句：We need to take inventory before the end of the month. 我们需要在本月底之前盘点库存。


近义词：stock, supplies, stock-taking, list

反义词：None directly opposite, but "depletion" or "lack" could be considered antonyms in context.


词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store takes inventory every week. = 这家商店每周都盘点库存。
Our company regularly updates its inventory records. = 我们公司定期更新库存记录。


一般过去时:
They inventoried the warehouse last night. = 他们昨晚清点了仓库。
The team completed the inventory audit yesterday. = 团队昨天完成了库存审计。


现在完成时:
We have inventoried all the new products. = 我们已经清点完所有新产品了。
The company has not yet completed its inventory count. = 该公司尚未完成库存盘点。


过去完成时:
They had inventoried the supplies before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经清点过物资了。
The accountant had already completed the inventory reconciliation. = 会计师已经完成了库存核对。


将来时:
We will inventory the entire facility next month. = 我们下个月将清点整个设施的库存。
The team will begin the inventory process tomorrow morning. = 团队将于明天早上开始盘点流程。


虚拟语气:
If we had a more efficient inventory system, we would have fewer stockouts. = 如果我们有一个更有效的库存系统，就不会有那么多的缺货了。
It's essential that the inventory be accurate. = 库存准确无误至关重要。


被动语态:
The inventory is checked regularly. = 库存定期检查。
The warehouse was inventoried by the team. = 仓库由团队清点。


疑问句:
What is the current inventory level? = 当前库存水平是多少？
Has the inventory been updated? = 库存是否已更新？


否定句:
We don't have enough inventory to fulfill the order. = 我们没有足够的库存来完成订单。
They didn't complete the inventory check on time. = 他们没有按时完成库存检查。
