externalising: /ɪkˈstɜːrnəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 外化；表现出来 | The act of expressing or manifesting internal feelings, thoughts, or states in an outward or observable way.  This often involves projecting one's inner world onto the external environment or behavior. | 例句：He was externalising his anxieties through excessive drinking. 他通过酗酒来外化他的焦虑。

例句：The child externalized her anger by hitting her toys.  这个孩子通过打她的玩具来表达她的愤怒。


近义词：manifesting, expressing, projecting, vocalizing, exhibiting

反义词：internalizing, suppressing, repressing, concealing, withholding

词性:动词
