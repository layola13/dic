patrimonies:/ˈpætrɪmoʊniːz/ | n. | 世袭财产；遗产 |  Property inherited from one's ancestors; a heritage.  |例句：His patrimony included vast estates and a considerable collection of art.  他的世袭财产包括大片的土地和大量的艺术收藏品。

例句：The family's patrimony was squandered by reckless spending over several generations.  这个家族的遗产在几代人的挥霍下被败光了。


近义词：inheritance, legacy, heritage


反义词：debt, liability


词性：名词
