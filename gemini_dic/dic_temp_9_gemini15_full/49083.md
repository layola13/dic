/deɪ/ | det. pron. | 那，那个；什么 |  "That" or "which one"  or "what kind of". It introduces a noun clause or a noun phrase.  |  例句：What time is it?  = 现在几点了？
What did you say? = 你说什么？

|近义词：which, that |反义词：this, these, this one | 词性:代词,限定词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？

一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 晚会上发生了什么？

现在完成时:
What have you done today? = 你今天做了什么？
What has she achieved in her career? = 她在事业上取得了什么成就？

过去完成时:
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the concert? = 音乐会之前他们吃了什么？

将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天做什么？
What will the weather be like next week? = 下周天气怎么样？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to expect. = 我真希望知道会发生什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done to improve the situation? = 正在采取什么措施来改善这种情况？

疑问句:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are you reading? = 你在读什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道该说什么。
He didn't tell me what happened. = 他没告诉我发生了什么。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What about...? = ……怎么样？
What if...? = 如果……将会怎么样？
What for? = 为什么？为了什么？
No matter what = 无论如何
