multimillionaire:/ˌmʌltiˈmɪljəneər/| n. |亿万富翁，巨富 | A multimillionaire is a person whose net worth is in the multiple millions of dollars or other currency. They have accumulated a significant amount of wealth through various means, often business ventures, investments, or inheritance.  |  He became a multimillionaire after selling his tech company. 他在卖掉他的科技公司后成为了亿万富翁。| billionaire, tycoon | pauper, penniless
