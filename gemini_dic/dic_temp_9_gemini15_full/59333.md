earlet:/ˈɪərlət/| n.|小耳朵；幼小的耳朵 |A small ear, especially of a young animal or plant.  The word is rare and mostly found in older texts. It suggests something small and delicate, like a tiny ear or an ear in its early stage of development.  |Example: The newborn kitten had tiny earlets barely visible in its downy fur.  小猫刚出生，绒毛里的小耳朵几乎看不见。|近义词：small ear, little ear |反义词：large ear, big ear
