infect:/ɪnˈfɛkt/ | vt. | 传染；感染；影响 | To contaminate or affect with a disease-causing organism.  | 例句：The virus infected many people. 病毒感染了许多人。 | 例句：His anger infected the whole room. 他的怒气感染了整个房间。 | 近义词：contaminate, contaminate, affect | 反义词：cure, heal


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The virus infects many people. = 病毒感染许多人。
The bad news infects his mood. = 坏消息影响了他的心情。


一般过去时:
The doctor infected the wound. = 医生感染了伤口。(此处指意外感染)
The flu infected many children last year. = 去年流感感染了许多儿童。


现在完成时:
The disease has infected thousands of people. = 这种疾病已经感染了成千上万的人。
His negativity has infected the entire team. = 他的消极情绪已经影响了整个团队。


过去完成时:
The wound had been infected before he arrived at the hospital. = 他到医院之前伤口就已经感染了。
The rumor had infected the whole town before the truth came out. = 真相大白之前，谣言已经传遍了整个城镇。


将来时:
The bacteria will infect the food if it's not stored properly. = 如果食物保存不当，细菌就会感染食物。
This idea will infect many people if we promote it well. = 如果我们好好推广这个想法，它会影响很多人。


虚拟语气:
If the bacteria infected the patient, it would be very serious. = 如果细菌感染了病人，那就非常严重了。
I wish the negativity hadn't infected my work. = 我真希望消极情绪没有影响到我的工作。


被动语态:
Many people were infected by the virus. = 许多人被病毒感染了。
The whole team was infected by his enthusiasm. = 全队都被他的热情感染了。


疑问句:
Was the wound infected? = 伤口感染了吗？
Is his attitude infecting the atmosphere? = 他的态度正在影响气氛吗？


否定句:
The doctor ensured the wound wasn't infected. = 医生确保伤口没有感染。
The positive message didn't infect the pessimistic crowd. = 积极的信息并没有影响到悲观的人群。


PHRASE->

infect with:  用…传染; 以…感染
infected with: 感染了…
become infected: 感染
highly infectious: 高度传染性的
airborne infection: 空气传播感染
