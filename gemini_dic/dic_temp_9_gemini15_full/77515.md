gazump:/ɡəˈzʌmp/| vt.| 乘虚而入；抬高价格；压价|To unexpectedly increase the price of something, especially property, that has already been agreed upon.  This usually happens when a seller accepts a higher offer from another buyer after already accepting an offer from the original buyer.|例句：We were gazumped on the house we thought we'd bought. 我们以为买下的房子被人抬价抢走了。|近义词：undercut, overbid, outbid |反义词：undersell
