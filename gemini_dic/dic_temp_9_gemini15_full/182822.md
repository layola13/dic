repeatability's:/ˌriːpiːtəˈbɪlətiz/| n.|可重复性，可再現性|The ability of a process or experiment to produce the same results when repeated under the same conditions.  It is a crucial aspect of scientific research and many industrial processes.|例句：The high repeatability of the experiment confirmed the validity of the results.  实验的高可重复性证实了结果的有效性。

The low repeatability of the manufacturing process led to inconsistent product quality.  生产过程的可重复性低导致产品质量不一致。


近义词：reproducibility, consistency, reliability|反义词：inconsistency, unreliability|名词
