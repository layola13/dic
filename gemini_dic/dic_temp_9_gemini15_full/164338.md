preelementary: /priːˈɛləmɛntəri/ | adj. |小学前的，初级的|  Describes something that comes before elementary education or is of a very basic level. It often refers to educational materials or programs designed for children before they enter elementary school.  |Example:  The preschool offers preelementary educational programs. 这所幼儿园提供小学前的教育项目。|近义词: pre-school, preparatory, introductory |反义词: advanced, secondary, post-elementary
