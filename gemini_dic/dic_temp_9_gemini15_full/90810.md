humpty:/ˈhʌmpti/| n.|矮胖的人；驼背的人；(尤指)笨蛋；(尤指)易碎的东西 | A short, fat person; a hunchback; (especially) a clumsy or foolish person; (especially) something easily broken. |例句：Humpty Dumpty sat on a wall.  矮胖的汉普蒂·达普蒂坐在墙上。|例句：He's a bit of a humpty, always dropping things. 他有点笨手笨脚，总是掉东西。|近义词：clumsy, awkward, foolish |反义词：graceful, dexterous, clever
