kinaesthetic:/ˌkaɪnɪsˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 动觉的，动觉感知的 | Relating to a sense of body position, presence, or movement.  It describes the perception of body movements and position without relying on sight or hearing. | Example:  The dancer had an exceptional kinaesthetic sense, allowing her to move with grace and precision.  舞蹈演员具有非凡的动觉感，使她能够优雅而精确地舞动。|近义词：kinesthetic | 反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to a lack of body awareness.
