The crops were meagrely harvested this year due to the drought. 今年由于干旱，收成非常少。|近义词：scarcely, sparingly, thinly, poorly |反义词：abundantly, lavishly, plentifully

词源:
The word "meagrely" is derived from the Old French word "maigre," meaning "lean" or "thin."  This ultimately comes from the Latin word "macer," meaning "to make lean" or "to emaciate."  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective "meagre" into an adverb.
