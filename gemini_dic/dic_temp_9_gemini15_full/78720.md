gigerium:/dʒɪˈdʒɪəriəm/| n. |砂囊；肌胃|The gigerium is a muscular pouch in the digestive tract of birds, reptiles, and some invertebrates, used to grind food.  It's essentially a muscular stomach. |Example: The bird's gigerium contained partially digested seeds and insects.  这只鸟的砂囊里装着部分消化的种子和昆虫。|近义词：muscular stomach, grinding stomach|反义词：无|名词
