comelier:/ˈkʌmliər/ | adj. |更美丽的，更英俊的 | More attractive or beautiful.  It suggests a pleasing and graceful appearance.  |例句：She looked even comelier in her new dress. 她穿上新衣服后显得更加美丽动人。

例句：He is a comelier man than his brother. 他比他哥哥更英俊。

近义词：prettier, more beautiful, more handsome, more attractive

反义词：uglier, less attractive, homely


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flower is comelier than the weed. = 这朵花比这杂草更漂亮。
She is comelier than her sister. = 她比她妹妹更漂亮。


一般过去时:
The princess was comelier than any other lady in the court. = 公主比宫廷里任何其他女士都更漂亮。
He seemed comelier in the sunlight. = 他在阳光下显得更英俊。


现在完成时:
She has become comelier since she lost weight. = 她自从减肥后变得更漂亮了。
The garden has become comelier after the rain. = 雨后花园变得更美了。


过去完成时:
She had been comelier in her youth. = 她年轻时更漂亮。
The building had looked comelier before the renovation. = 翻新之前，这栋建筑物看起来更漂亮。


将来时:
She will be comelier with a better haircut. = 她剪个更好的发型会更漂亮。
The garden will be comelier in spring. = 春天花园会更漂亮。


虚拟语气:
If she were comelier, she would have more suitors. = 如果她更漂亮，就会有更多追求者。
I wish he were comelier. = 我希望他更英俊。


被动语态:
(This adjective is rarely used in the passive voice)


疑问句:
Is she not comelier than her friend? = 她不是比她朋友更漂亮吗？
Isn't he the comelier of the two brothers? = 他不是两个兄弟中更英俊的那个吗？


否定句:
She is not comelier than her sister. = 她不如她妹妹漂亮。
He is not comelier than his brother. = 他不如他哥哥英俊。
