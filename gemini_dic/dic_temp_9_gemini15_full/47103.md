daughterboard:/ˈdɔːtəbɔːrd/| n. |子板，子电路板 |A smaller circuit board that is attached to a larger main circuit board.  It often performs a specific function or provides additional capabilities. |Example: The daughterboard provides extra USB ports for the computer.  这个子板为电脑提供了额外的USB端口。|近义词：sub-board, auxiliary board |反义词：motherboard
