endurances:/ɪnˈdʊərənsɪz/| n. |忍耐力；持久力；耐力|The ability to endure an unpleasant or difficult process or situation without giving way.  It often refers to physical stamina, mental fortitude, or the capacity of something to withstand wear and tear.|例句：His endurance during the marathon was truly remarkable. 他在马拉松比赛中的耐力确实令人瞩目。

例句：The building's endurance is a testament to its sturdy construction. 这座建筑物的持久性证明了其坚固的结构。|近义词：stamina, perseverance, fortitude, resilience|反义词：weakness, fragility, intolerance
