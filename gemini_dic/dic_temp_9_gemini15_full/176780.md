reading-up-on:/ˈriːdɪŋ ʌp ɒn/ | phrasal verb |  学习；研读；查阅 | To study or learn about a subject by reading.  This phrase implies a focused effort to gain knowledge on a specific topic, often in preparation for something. |  I'm reading up on ancient history for my exam. 我正在为考试学习古代历史。  I've been reading up on the latest developments in AI. 我一直在研读人工智能的最新发展。 | studying, researching, reviewing |  ignoring, neglecting |  verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am reading up on the history of the Roman Empire. = 我正在研读罗马帝国的历史。
He is reading up on the latest medical research. = 他正在研读最新的医学研究。

一般过去时:
She read up on the company before the interview. = 她在面试前研读了这家公司的情况。
They read up on the local customs before their trip. = 他们在旅行前研读了当地的风俗习惯。

现在完成时:
I have read up on the subject extensively. = 我已经广泛研读了这个主题。
She has read up on all the candidates before voting. = 她在投票前已经研读了所有候选人的情况。

过去完成时:
He had read up on the topic before the debate. = 他在辩论前已经研读了这个话题。
They had read up on the author's previous works before starting the new novel. = 他们在开始读这本新小说之前已经研读了这位作者之前的作品。

将来时:
I will read up on the project before the meeting. = 我将在会议前研读这个项目。
She will read up on the instructions carefully before starting. = 她将在开始之前仔细研读说明。

虚拟语气:
If I had read up on the instructions, I wouldn't have made so many mistakes. = 如果我之前研读了说明，我就不会犯这么多错误。
I wish I had read up on the company's history before investing. = 我真希望在投资前研读了这家公司历史。

被动语态: (This phrasal verb is rarely used passively)
The subject was being read up on by several researchers. = 这个主题正在被几位研究人员研读。(较为生硬)
The information was read up on prior to the presentation. = 在演示之前，信息已被研读。(较为生硬)


疑问句:
Are you reading up on anything interesting at the moment? = 你现在正在研读什么有趣的东西吗？
Has she read up on the latest news regarding the case? = 她是否研读了关于此案的最新消息？


否定句:
I didn't read up on the topic before the discussion. = 我在讨论前没有研读这个话题。
She hasn't read up on the new regulations yet. = 她还没有研读新的规章制度。
