mistress:/ˈmɪstrəs/| n.|女主人；情妇；女教师；女主人|A woman who is in charge of a house or estate; a woman who has a sexual relationship with a man who is married to someone else; a woman teacher (archaic).|例句：She is the mistress of the house. 她家女主人。|例句：He had a mistress on the side. 他外面养了个情妇。|近义词：governess, mistress, landlady, madam|反义词：master, servant


USAGE->

一般现在时:
She is the mistress of the manor. = 她是庄园的女主人。
He has a mistress in the city. = 他在城里有个情妇。


一般过去时:
She was the mistress of ceremonies. = 她曾是司仪。
He had a mistress for many years. = 他和情妇相处多年了。


现在完成时:
She has been the mistress of her own destiny. = 她一直是自己命运的主宰。
He has had several mistresses throughout his life. = 他一生中有很多情妇。


过去完成时:
She had been mistress of the household for twenty years. = 她当了二十年的家。
He had never had a mistress before he met her. = 在遇到她之前，他从未有过情妇。


将来时:
She will be the mistress of the situation. = 她将掌控局势。
He will likely have another mistress soon. = 他很可能很快就会有另一个情妇。


虚拟语气:
If she were mistress of her own fate, she would travel the world. = 如果她能掌控自己的命运，她就会周游世界。
If he hadn't had a mistress, his marriage might have lasted. = 如果他没有情妇，他的婚姻可能会持续下去。


被动语态:
The position of mistress was filled by a capable woman. = 女主人的职位由一位有能力的女性担任。
(Note:  The word "mistress" itself is rarely used passively in the context of a lover.)


疑问句:
Is she the mistress of the house? = 她家女主人吗？
Does he have a mistress? = 他有情妇吗？


否定句:
She is not the mistress of her emotions. = 她无法控制自己的情绪。
He does not have a mistress, he claims. = 他声称他没有情妇。
