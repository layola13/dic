engrail:/ɪnˈɡreɪl/| vt.|  使成锯齿状；饰以锯齿状边缘 | To make or become notched or indented like a saw.  The verb "engrail" refers to the process of creating a series of small, regular notches along an edge, giving it a serrated or toothed appearance.  This is often done for decorative purposes, or to provide a better grip. | 例句：The blacksmith skillfully engrailed the edge of the sword.  铁匠熟练地将剑刃做成了锯齿状。

The sculptor engrailed the marble border for aesthetic effect. 雕刻家为了美观效果在青石边沿刻出了锯齿纹。| 近义词：indent, notch, serrate | 反义词：smooth, round
