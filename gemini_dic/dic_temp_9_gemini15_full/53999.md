discriminating: /dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 有鉴赏力的；有辨别力的；歧视的 |

英文解析:Showing good taste or judgment; able to recognize differences.  Also, unfairly treating a person or group differently from others.

例句:

He has a discriminating palate for wine.  他具有鉴赏葡萄酒的味觉。

The company was accused of discriminating against women in hiring.  该公司被指控在招聘中歧视女性。


近义词: discerning, perceptive, sophisticated (for the positive sense); prejudiced, biased, bigoted (for the negative sense)

反义词: indiscriminate, uncritical, unbiased


词性: adjective
