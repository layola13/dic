reformulated:/ˌriːˈfɔːrmjuleɪtɪd/| v. adj.|重新表述的；重新制定的 |  To state or express something again in a different way, often to make it clearer or more effective.  |例句：The company reformulated its marketing strategy after a poor sales year.  公司在销售业绩不佳的一年后重新制定了其营销策略。|近义词：rephrased, restated, revised|反义词：unmodified, unchanged


The company reformulated its product to appeal to a wider audience. 公司重新制定了产品以吸引更广泛的受众。
