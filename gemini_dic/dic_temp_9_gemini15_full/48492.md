deeper:/ˈdiːpər/| adj.|更深的；更深的；更深奥的 |More profound or intense; extending further down or inward.|The lake is deeper than it looks.  这个湖比看起来要深。|The meaning of the poem is deeper than you think. 这首诗的含义比你想象的更深奥。|shallow, superficial|shallow, superficial, high|


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The water is deeper here. 这儿的水更深。
This problem is deeper than we thought.  这个问题比我们想的更复杂。

一般过去时:
The hole was deeper than I expected. 洞比我预期的更深。
The discussion became deeper as the meeting went on. 会议进行中，讨论越来越深入。

现在完成时:
The river has become deeper due to recent rains.  由于最近的降雨，河水已变得更深。
My understanding of the subject has deepened significantly. 我对这个主题的理解已经大大加深。

过去完成时:
The well had been deeper before the earthquake. 地震前这口井更深。
Her sadness had deepened after hearing the news.  听到这个消息后，她的悲伤加深了。


将来时:

Our relationship will deepen over time. 我们的关系会随着时间的推移而加深。

被动语态:

否定句:
