coreference:/ˈkɔːrɛfərəns/| n. |共指，指称一致|The phenomenon where two or more expressions in a text refer to the same entity.  Coreference resolution is a key task in natural language processing. 共指现象是指文本中两个或多个表达指向同一实体的现象。共指消解是自然语言处理中的一个关键任务。|Example: In the sentence "John bought a car. He drove it home," "John" and "He" are coreferences, both referring to the same person.  在句子“约翰买了一辆车。他开车回家了”中，“约翰”和“他”是共指，都指同一个人。|近义词：anaphoric relation, identity|反义词：non-coreference, distinct reference|名词
