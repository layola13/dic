delineature:/dɪˈlɪnjətʃər/| n.| 描绘，描述；轮廓；素描 |A delineation is a description or portrayal of something, often in detail and with a focus on its distinctive features. It can also refer to a line drawing or outline. |例句：The artist's delineation of the landscape was breathtaking.  艺术家的风景描绘令人叹为观止。
The map provided a clear delineation of the city's boundaries.  地图清晰地描绘了城市的界限。|近义词：depiction, portrayal, representation, sketch, outline |反义词：obscurity, vagueness, indistinctness |名词
