druggy:/ˈdrʌɡi/| adj. n.|吸毒成瘾者；沉迷于毒品的人；(非正式)药瘾的；|A person who uses drugs excessively.  The term often carries a negative connotation, implying a lack of control and potential social problems.|例句：He became a druggy after losing his job. 他丢了工作后成了个瘾君子。

例句：The police arrested several druggy individuals near the park.  警方在公园附近逮捕了几名吸毒人员。


近义词：junkie, addict, user|反义词：sober, clean


词性: adjective, noun
