self-perpetuation:/ˌsɛlfˌpɜːrpɛtʃuˈeɪʃən/| n. |自我延续；自我维持；永存|The act or process of perpetuating oneself; the ability of something to continue or sustain itself indefinitely.  This often implies a system or process that continues without external intervention.  |例句：The cycle of poverty is a problem of self-perpetuation. 贫困的循环是一个自我延续的问题。

The empire's self-perpetuation relied on a complex system of alliances and military might. 帝国的自我延续依赖于复杂的联盟和军事力量体系。|近义词：self-preservation, perpetuation, continuation |反义词：extinction, termination, cessation
