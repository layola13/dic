semisuccesses:/ˌsɛmiˈsʌksɛsɪz/ | n. | 半成功；部分成功 | Partial successes; instances of achieving some, but not all, of a desired outcome. The company's semisuccesses in the new market indicate a need for adjustments to their strategy.  这家公司在新市场上取得的部分成功表明需要调整其战略。|近义词：partial successes, limited achievements, qualified triumphs |反义词：complete failures, utter defeats
