physopoda:/faɪˈsɒpədə/ | n. |拟足纲动物|  A class of arthropods, characterized by the possession of phyllopods (leaf-like appendages).  They are primarily aquatic, and many species are found in freshwater habitats.  They include fairy shrimp, tadpole shrimp, and clam shrimp. |  Example:  Many species of *Physopoda* are crucial components of aquatic ecosystems.  许多 *拟足纲* 动物是水生生态系统的重要组成部分。|近义词: Branchiopoda (a broader term)| 反义词: N/A |名词
