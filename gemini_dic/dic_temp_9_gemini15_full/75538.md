fubby:/ˈfʌbi/| adj.|胖乎乎的，丰满的|Plump and slightly overweight, often used in a playful or affectionate way.  It suggests a softness and roundness rather than being excessively fat.|例句：The baby is so chubby and adorable!  这个婴儿胖乎乎的，真可爱！例句：The cat has gotten quite chubby since it stopped hunting.|那只猫自从不打猎后就胖了不少。|近义词：plump, chubby, stout, round|反义词：thin, skinny, lean
