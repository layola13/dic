missionate:/ˈmɪʃənˌeɪt/| vt.|传教；从事传教活动|To engage in missionary work; to send on a mission.|例句：The missionaries missionated in the remote villages.  传教士们在偏远的村庄传教。|例句：The church missionated extensively throughout the region. 教堂在整个地区广泛地开展传教活动。|近义词：evangelize, proselytize|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He missionates in Africa. 他在非洲传教。
They missionate to the poor. 他们向穷人传教。


一般过去时:
He missionated in the Amazon for five years. 他在亚马逊传教五年。
The priests missionated throughout the country. 这些牧师在全国各地传教。


现在完成时:
She has missionated in several countries. 她已经在几个国家传教了。
They have missionated extensively in South America. 他们在南美洲广泛地传教了。


过去完成时:
He had missionated for ten years before he retired. 他退休前已经传教十年了。
Before the war, they had missionated peacefully in the village.  战争爆发前，他们在村庄里和平地传教。


将来时:
He will missionate in Nepal next year. 他明年将在尼泊尔传教。
They will missionate in the slums of the city. 他们将在城市贫民窟传教。


虚拟语气:
If I were younger, I would missionate abroad. 如果我还年轻，我会去国外传教。
I wish I could missionate more effectively. 我希望我能更有效地传教。


被动语态:
The gospel was missionated throughout the empire. 福音在整个帝国都被传教了。
The message was missionated effectively to the people. 信息有效地传达给了人们。


疑问句:
Does he still missionate? 他还在传教吗？
Where do they missionate? 他们在哪里传教？


否定句:
He doesn't missionate anymore. 他不再传教了。
They did not missionate in that region. 他们没有在那个地区传教。


PHRASE->
missionate to: 向…传教
missionate among: 在…中传教
missionate for: 为…传教
missionate against: 反对…传教
missionate with: 与…一起传教
