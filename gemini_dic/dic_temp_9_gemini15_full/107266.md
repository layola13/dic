lakesides:/ˈleɪksaɪdz/| n. |湖边，湖畔 |The word "lakesides" refers to the sides or shores of a lake, or areas situated near a lake. It can also refer to multiple lakesides.  |例句：We spent a relaxing afternoon at the lakesides. 我们在湖边度过了一个轻松的下午。

例句：Many beautiful houses are located on the lakesides of the area.  许多美丽的房子都坐落在该地区的湖边。


近义词：shores, lake shores, waterfronts


反义词：inland, center (of a lake)


词性：名词
