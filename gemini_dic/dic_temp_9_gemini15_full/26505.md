cabals:/kəˈbælz/| n.|秘密结社；阴谋集团|A cabal is a small group of people who secretly plan something, especially something harmful or illegal.  The term often carries a negative connotation, implying secrecy, intrigue, and potentially malicious intent. |例句：The king was overthrown by a cabal of his own ministers. 国王被自己大臣中的一个秘密集团推翻了。

例句：Rumors circulated about a cabal plotting to assassinate the president. 有谣言说有一个秘密集团密谋暗杀总统。


近义词：clique, conspiracy, plot, intrigue, faction|反义词：openness, transparency, honesty


词性: 名词
