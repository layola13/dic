retingeing:/ˌriːˈtɪndʒɪŋ/| v.| 再染色；重新着色 |The act of dyeing or coloring something again.  It implies a previous coloring or dyeing process. |例句：The artist was retingeing the canvas to create a richer effect.  艺术家正在重新着色画布以创造更丰富的效果。


The old tapestry needed retingeing to restore its vibrancy. 这幅古老的挂毯需要重新染色才能恢复其鲜艳的色彩。


近义词：redyeing, recoloring, re-staining |反义词：bleaching, fading


词源:  Comes from "retinge," a combination of the prefix "re-" (again) and "tinge," meaning to color slightly.  "Tinge" itself comes from the Old French word "teindre," meaning "to dye."
