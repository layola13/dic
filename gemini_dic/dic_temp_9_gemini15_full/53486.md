disarrayment:/ˌdɪsərˈeɪmənt/| n.|混乱；杂乱；散乱|The state of being in disarray; a lack of order or organization.  It often refers to a state of disorder or confusion, especially in appearance or arrangement.|例句：The room was in a state of complete disarrayment after the party.  派对过后，房间一片狼藉。|例句：The sudden announcement caused disarrayment in the company's plans.  突然的公告使公司的计划陷入混乱。|近义词：disorder, chaos, confusion, disorganization|反义词：order, organization, neatness
