outmoved:/ˌaʊtˈmuːvd/| v.|超过，胜过，比...做得更好|To surpass or exceed in performance, skill, or achievement.  It implies a significant degree of outperformance, often suggesting a sustained effort or advantage.|例句：The new marketing campaign completely outmoved the previous one, resulting in a significant increase in sales. 新的营销活动完全超过了之前的活动，导致销售额大幅增长。

例句：Our team outmoved the competition in terms of innovation and efficiency. 我们的团队在创新和效率方面超过了竞争对手。


近义词：outperform, surpass, exceed, outstrip, overshadow

反义词：underperform, lag behind, be surpassed by


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company outmoves its competitors in customer service. = 这家公司在客户服务方面胜过其竞争对手。
He consistently outmoves his opponents in chess. = 他在国际象棋中总是胜过对手。

一般过去时:
Last year's sales figures outmoved all previous records. = 去年的销售额超过了以往的所有记录。
She outmoved her rivals in the race to the finish line. = 她在冲刺阶段超过了对手。

现在完成时:
Our team has outmoved the opposition in several key areas. = 我们的团队在几个关键领域都超过了对手。
The company has outmoved its rivals in terms of market share. = 这家公司在市场份额方面已经超过了竞争对手。

过去完成时:
By the time the race ended, he had outmoved every other runner. = 到比赛结束时，他已经超过了其他所有跑步者。
She had outmoved her opponent before the final round began. = 在决赛开始之前，她就超过了对手。


将来时:
With this new technology, we will outmove our competitors. = 凭借这项新技术，我们将超越竞争对手。
They believe they will outmove the other teams in the tournament. = 他们相信他们会在锦标赛中超越其他队伍。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
The previous record was outmoved by last year's sales. = 去年的销售额超过了之前的记录。
The smaller company was outmoved by the larger corporation. = 小公司被大公司超越了。

疑问句:

否定句:
The company did not outmove its competitors in the first quarter. = 这家公司第一季度没有超越竞争对手。
They failed to outmove their rivals in the race. = 他们在比赛中未能超越对手。
