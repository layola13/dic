dingo:/ˈdɪŋɡoʊ/| n. |澳洲野犬|A wild dog native to Australia.  It is known for its reddish-brown fur and its role in Australian Aboriginal culture. Dingos are often regarded as a pest due to their predatory behavior towards livestock.|The dingo attacked the sheep.|近义词：wild dog, native dog|反义词：domestic dog
