railroaders:/ˈreɪlˌroʊdərz/| n.|铁路工人，铁路公司职员|People who work for or on railroads.  This can include a wide range of jobs, from engineers and conductors to maintenance workers and office staff.|The railroaders worked tirelessly to clear the tracks after the storm.|近义词：railway workers, trainmen|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with office workers or those in unrelated industries.
