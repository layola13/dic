plum:/plʌm/| n. |李子；深紫色| A plum is a juicy fruit with smooth, purplish skin.  It is sweet and often used in desserts and jams. |例句：I love the taste of ripe plums. 我喜欢成熟李子的味道。

例句：She baked a delicious plum cake. 她烤了一个美味的李子蛋糕。


近义词：prune, damson |反义词：None.


词源：From Old English *plumme, from Latin prūnum.  The Latin word ultimately derives from Greek prūnon.
