directionlessness:/ˌdaɪˈrɛkʃənliːsnəs/| n. |缺乏方向感；茫然；没有目标|The feeling of being lost or without purpose; a lack of direction or aim.  It often describes a state of confusion or uncertainty about one's goals or future. |例句：Her directionlessness after graduation led her to wander aimlessly for several years.  毕业后的迷茫让她漫无目的地游荡了好几年。

例句：The pervasive sense of directionlessness among young people today is a serious societal problem. 当今年轻人普遍存在的迷茫感是一个严重的社会问题。


近义词：aimlessness, purposelessness, rootlessness, disorientation, uncertainty


反义词：purposefulness, direction, focus, clarity
