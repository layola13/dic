charge-out:/tʃɑːrdʒ aʊt/| vt.|记帐收费；把…记入帐单；租借出去|To record a charge for a service or goods provided; to rent or lease something out.|例句：The company charges out its services at a competitive rate. 这家公司以具有竞争力的价格收费。|例句：We charge out our equipment to other businesses. 我们把设备租借给其他企业。|近义词：bill, invoice, rent out, lease|反义词：pay, receive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hotel charges out rooms by the night. = 酒店按夜收费。
They charge out their services at a competitive price. = 他们以具有竞争力的价格收费。


一般过去时:
The mechanic charged out the repair work to the customer. = 技工将修理工作记入客户的账单。
The company charged out all of its available equipment for the project. = 公司将所有可用的设备都租借了出去用于该项目。


现在完成时:
We have charged out all the available vehicles this week. = 我们本周已经把所有可用的车辆都租出去了。
The client has been charged out for the consultation. = 客户已就咨询服务被收费。


过去完成时:
Before the project started, we had charged out all the necessary tools. = 项目开始前，我们已经将所有必要的工具都租出去了。
They had charged out the equipment to the film crew before the shoot. = 在拍摄前，他们已将设备租借给了电影摄制组。


将来时:
We will charge out the new equipment next week. = 我们下周将把新设备租借出去。
The company will charge out its services to clients based on project completion. = 公司将根据项目的完成情况向客户收费。


虚拟语气:
If we charged out our services at a higher rate, we would make more profit. = 如果我们以更高的价格收费，我们将获得更多利润。
I wish we had charged out more for that job. = 我希望我们对那份工作收取更多费用。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
