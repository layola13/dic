mellower:/ˈmɛləʊər/| adj.| 更成熟的；更柔和的；更醇厚的 | More mature; softer; smoother, richer in flavor or tone.  |例句：The wine had mellowed with age, becoming much mellower than it was initially. 这葡萄酒随着时间的推移变得醇厚了，比最初醇厚得多。

例句：His personality became mellower after he retired, less stressed and more relaxed. 他退休后性格变得更温和了，压力更小，也更放松了。


近义词：softer, smoother, gentler, milder, richer


反义词：harsher, sharper, stronger, more intense


词性：形容词
