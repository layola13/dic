muriatic:/ˌmjʊəriˈætɪk/| adj.|盐酸的；酸性的|  relating to or containing muriatic acid (hydrochloric acid).  The term is now largely obsolete in formal scientific contexts, having been replaced by "hydrochloric acid". However, it's still used in some informal settings, particularly in the context of industrial cleaning. | 例句：The old-fashioned cleaning solution contained muriatic acid.  = 这种老式的清洁剂含有盐酸。

The worker used muriatic acid to clean the bricks. = 工人用盐酸清洗砖块。


近义词：hydrochloric, acidic|反义词：alkaline, basic


词源：From Late Latin *muriaticus, from Latin muria "brine, pickle", from the same root as mur "sea".  The name reflects the historical preparation of hydrochloric acid from brine.
