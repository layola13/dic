glisten:/ˈɡlɪsən/| v.|闪光；闪耀|To shine with a soft, reflected light.  The word often implies a wet or oily surface.|例句：The wet leaves glistened in the sunlight.  阳光下，湿漉漉的树叶闪闪发光。 The dew glistened on the grass. 草叶上闪耀着露珠。|近义词：shine, shimmer, gleam, sparkle|反义词：dull, matte


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The diamonds glisten beautifully. = 钻石闪闪发光。
The wet road glistens under the streetlights. = 街灯下，湿漉漉的路面闪闪发光。

一般过去时:
The car glistened after the rain. = 雨后，汽车闪闪发光。
Her eyes glistened with tears. = 她的眼睛闪着泪光。

现在完成时:
The newly polished furniture has glistened all day. = 擦得锃亮的家具一整天都闪闪发光。
The river has glistened under the moonlight for hours. = 河水在月光下闪耀了几个小时。

过去完成时:
The freshly waxed floor had glistened before the party started. = 派对开始前，刚打过蜡的地板闪闪发光。
The silverware had glistened before it was put away. = 餐具在收起来之前闪闪发光。

将来时:
The snow will glisten under the winter sun. = 冬日阳光下，白雪将会闪闪发光。
The polished brass will glisten beautifully in the new house. = 新房子里，擦亮的黄铜将会闪闪发光。

虚拟语气:
If the sun shone, the lake would glisten. = 如果太阳照耀，湖面就会闪闪发光。
I wish my car glistened like that one. = 我希望我的车也能像那辆车一样闪闪发光。

被动语态:
The jewels were glistened under the spotlight. = 珠宝在聚光灯下闪闪发光。（被动语态在此处略显不自然，主动语态更常用）
The surface was glistened with oil. = 表面被油光闪闪地覆盖。（被动语态在此处更常用）

疑问句:
Does the sea glisten in the moonlight? = 月光下大海闪闪发光吗？
Do your eyes glisten when you cry? = 你哭的时候眼睛会闪闪发光吗？

否定句:
The dull paint did not glisten. = 黯淡的油漆没有光泽。
The dry leaves did not glisten in the sun. = 干枯的树叶在阳光下没有闪光。
