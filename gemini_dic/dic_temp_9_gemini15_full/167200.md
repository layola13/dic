pretendant:/prɪˈtɛndənt/| n. |觊觎者；求婚者； претендент | A person who makes a claim to something, especially a throne or title; a person who is courting someone.  |例句：He was a pretender to the throne. 他是一个觊觎王位的篡位者。|例句：She had several pretenders for her hand in marriage.  她有好几个求婚者。|近义词：claimant, suitor, applicant |反义词：legitimate ruler, rightful heir
