scalding:/ˈskɔːldɪŋ/ | adj. v. | 烫伤的；沸腾的；严厉斥责的 | Extremely hot; capable of causing a burn.  The act of scolding severely. |例句：The water was scalding hot. = 水烫得厉害。

The teacher gave him a scalding rebuke for his behavior. = 老师因他的行为严厉斥责了他。


近义词：burning, scorching, searing, blistering (for the hot sense);  reprimanding, berating, upbraiding (for the scolding sense)

反义词：cold, lukewarm (for the hot sense); praising, commending (for the scolding sense)

词性：Adjective, Verb
