nonadjudicative:/ˌnɒnəˈdʒuːdɪkətɪv/| adj.|非裁判性的，非裁决性的|Not involving or relating to the judgment or decision of a court or other judicial body.  It describes processes or actions that aim to resolve disputes outside of formal legal proceedings.|Example:  The mediation was a nonadjudicative process, focusing on finding common ground rather than a legal verdict.  调解是一个非裁判性的过程，侧重于寻找共同点而不是法律判决。|近义词：alternative dispute resolution (ADR), conciliatory, mediating|反义词：adjudicative, judicial, arbitral
