rapaces:/ræˈpeɪsiːz/| n.|猛禽；掠夺者|Birds of prey, characterized by sharp talons and beaks, often hunting other animals.  They are also metaphorically used to describe people who are predatory or exploitative.|例句：Eagles and hawks are examples of rapaces.  鹰和隼是猛禽的例子。

例句：The businessman's rapacious behavior led to his downfall.  这位商人的贪婪行为导致了他的垮台。|近义词：birds of prey, predators, hunters, exploiters|反义词：prey, victims, benefactors
