noncalculating: /ˌnɒnˈkælkjuleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不加计算的；不计较的；不考虑后果的 | Not calculating; not given to calculation or planning; impulsive.  It implies a lack of deliberate planning or forethought, often suggesting spontaneity or a disregard for consequences. |


例句：

His noncalculating generosity surprised everyone.  他的不计较的慷慨让每个人都感到惊讶。

She made a noncalculating decision, one based purely on emotion. 她做了一个不加计算的决定，完全是基于情感。
