detector:/dɪˈtɛktər/ | n. |探测器，检测器 |A device or instrument used to detect the presence of something, often something that is not easily visible or detectable by other means.  |例句：The smoke detector alerted us to the fire.  烟雾探测器提醒我们发生了火灾。|例句：The metal detector beeped as I walked through the airport security. 我走过机场安检时，金属探测器响了。|近义词：sensor, monitor, finder |反义词：N/A
