mitrailleur:/miˌtʁajœʁ/| n. | 机枪；机枪手 | A mitrailleur is a machine gun or, less commonly, a person who operates a machine gun.  The word is primarily used in French, but its use in English is sometimes encountered, particularly in historical contexts. | Example:  The enemy's mitrailleur raked the trench with deadly fire.  敌人的机枪向战壕扫射，火力凶猛。  The young soldier was a skilled mitrailleur. 年轻的士兵是一位熟练的机枪手。|近义词：machine gun, automatic weapon |反义词：None specifically, but words implying hand-to-hand combat could be considered antonyms in a contextual sense.
