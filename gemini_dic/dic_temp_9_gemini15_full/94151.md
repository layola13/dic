impels:/ɪmˈpɛlz/| vt.|驱使；激励；迫使|To drive or urge (someone) to do something.|The feeling of injustice impelled him to act.|近义词：urge, drive, propel, motivate|反义词：deter, discourage, restrain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The strong wind impels the ship forward. = 强风推动着船前进。
His ambition impels him to work harder. = 他的雄心壮志驱使他更加努力工作。

一般过去时:
A sense of duty impelled her to confess. = 责任感驱使她坦白。
The evidence impelled the jury to reach a guilty verdict. = 证据迫使陪审团作出有罪判决。


现在完成时:
What has impelled you to make such a drastic decision? = 什么促使你做出如此激烈的决定？
Nothing has impelled me to change my mind. = 没有什么促使我改变主意。


过去完成时:
A sudden urge had impelled him to leave. = 一种突如其来的冲动促使他离开。
She felt that a higher power had impelled her actions. = 她觉得某种更高的力量促使了她的行为。


将来时:
The upcoming election will impel many changes. = 即将到来的选举将推动许多变化。
I believe that this new technology will impel significant progress. = 我相信这项新技术将推动重大进展。


虚拟语气:
If only a stronger sense of responsibility had impelled him, things might have been different. = 如果他当时有更强的责任感，事情可能就会不一样了。
I wish a desire for justice had impelled me to act sooner. = 我希望正义感能早点促使我采取行动。


被动语态:
He was impelled by a desire for revenge. = 他被复仇的欲望驱使着。
The decision was impelled by financial considerations. = 这个决定是由经济因素驱使的。


疑问句:
What impelled you to say that? = 什么促使你说那样的话？
What factors impelled the company's decision to merge? = 哪些因素促使该公司决定合并？


否定句:
Nothing impelled her to change her plans. = 没有什么促使她改变计划。
He wasn't impelled by greed, but by a desire to help. = 他不是被贪婪驱使，而是被助人的愿望驱使。


PHRASE->
impel to action = 促使采取行动
impel to change = 促使改变
impel to succeed = 驱使成功
impel forward = 推动前进
impel someone to do something = 驱使某人做某事
