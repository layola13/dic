dnce:/dæns/| n. |舞蹈；舞会|The act of dancing; a social gathering where people dance.|例句：She loves to dance. 她喜欢跳舞。|例句：There will be a dance after the ceremony. 典礼结束后将举行舞会。|近义词：ball, hop,  prom|反义词：stillness, inactivity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She dances every Saturday. = 她每个星期六都跳舞。
They dance beautifully. = 他们跳得很漂亮。


一般过去时:
He danced all night. = 他跳了一整夜舞。
We danced at the wedding. = 我们在婚礼上跳舞。


现在完成时:
I have danced before a large audience. = 我曾在大型观众面前跳舞。
She has danced professionally for ten years. = 她专业跳舞十年了。


过去完成时:
They had danced for hours before they got tired. = 他们跳了几个小时才感到疲倦。
He had never danced before that night. = 在那晚之前他从未跳过舞。


将来时:
We will dance at the party. = 我们将在派对上跳舞。
They will be dancing until midnight. = 他们将跳到午夜。


虚拟语气:
If I could dance, I would. = 如果我会跳舞，我会跳。
I wish I could dance like her. = 我希望我能像她一样跳舞。


被动语态:
The dance was enjoyed by everyone. = 舞蹈受到所有人的喜爱。
The waltz is danced gracefully. = 华尔兹跳得很优雅。


疑问句:
Do you dance? = 你跳舞吗？
Can she dance? = 她会跳舞吗？


否定句:
I don't dance. = 我不跳舞。
He didn't dance at the party. = 他在派对上没跳舞。
