luciferian:/ˌluːsɪˈfɪəriən/| adj. n.|路西法式的；与撒旦有关的；堕落的；光明的；| Relating to Lucifer, the fallen angel; characterized by pride, ambition, and a rejection of traditional morality.  Often used to describe something rebellious, arrogant, or evil, but can also refer to bringing light or illumination.  The term carries strong connotations, often negative, due to its association with the devil.|例句：His luciferian ambition led him to betray his friends.  他的路西法式的野心导致他背叛了他的朋友。

The novel explores the luciferian aspects of human nature. 这部小说探讨了人性的路西法式方面。

近义词：diabolical, satanic, demonic, rebellious, arrogant |反义词：angelic, holy, virtuous, humble, obedient
