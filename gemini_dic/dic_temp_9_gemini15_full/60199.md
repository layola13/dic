effigy:/ˈɛfɪdʒi/| n. |肖像；雕像；替身|A crude representation of a person, especially one that is made in order to be attacked or destroyed as a protest.|The protesters burned an effigy of the president.  抗议者焚烧了总统的肖像。|image, likeness, figure|—|
