cypruses:/ˈsaɪprəsɪz/| n. |柏树（复数）|The plural of cypress; refers to multiple cypress trees.  Cypress trees are known for their evergreen foliage and often conical shape. They are frequently used in landscaping and have symbolic meaning in different cultures.|例句：The wind whispered through the tall cypruses. 风在高大的柏树间低语。

例句：Several ancient cypruses stood sentinel over the ruins.几棵古老的柏树守护着废墟。
