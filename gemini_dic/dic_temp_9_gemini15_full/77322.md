gating:/ˈɡeɪtɪŋ/| n. v.|门控；控制；调节|The process of controlling or regulating the flow of something, often involving a gate or barrier, either physical or metaphorical.|例句：The gating of ions through membrane channels is crucial for nerve impulse transmission.  离子通过膜通道的门控对于神经冲动传递至关重要。|例句：The company implemented a new gating system to improve security. 公司实施了新的门禁系统以提高安全性。|近义词：control, regulation, modulation|反义词：opening, freeing
