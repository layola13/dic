heterogeneous: /ˌhɛtərəˈdʒiːniəs/ | adj. | 异质的；不均匀的；多种多样的 | Consisting of dissimilar or diverse elements; not uniform; varied in character or content.  A heterogeneous mixture contains components that are not uniformly mixed.  | 例句：The student body is remarkably heterogeneous, representing a wide range of backgrounds and experiences.  学生群体非常多元化，代表着广泛的背景和经历。

The soil sample revealed a heterogeneous composition of minerals and organic matter. 土壤样本显示出矿物质和有机质的异质成分。


近义词：diverse, varied, mixed, dissimilar, disparate | 反义词：homogeneous, uniform, consistent


词源:  From  Late Latin *heterogeneus, from Greek  ἑτερογενής (heterogenḗs), from ἕτερος (héteros) "other" + γένος (génos) "kind, race, birth".  The word literally means "of different kinds".
