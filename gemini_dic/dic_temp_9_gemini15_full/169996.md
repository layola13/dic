protease:/ˈproʊtiˌeɪs/|/ˈprəʊtiˌeɪs/| n. |蛋白酶；肽酶|An enzyme that breaks down proteins into smaller polypeptide chains or amino acids. Proteases play a crucial role in various biological processes, including digestion, blood clotting, and immune responses.|例句：The pancreas secretes various proteases to aid in digestion. 胰腺分泌各种蛋白酶以帮助消化。|例句：Some proteases are used in detergents to remove protein stains.  一些蛋白酶用于清洁剂中去除蛋白质污渍。|近义词：proteinase, peptidase|反义词：无
