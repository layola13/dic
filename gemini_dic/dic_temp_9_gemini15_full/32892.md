chondroma:/kɒnˈdroʊmə/ | n. |软骨瘤|A benign tumor composed of cartilage.  Chondromas are slow-growing and usually asymptomatic unless they become large enough to cause pressure on surrounding tissues or nerves. They most commonly occur in the bones of the hands and feet. |例句：The X-ray revealed a small chondroma in his finger bone. X光显示他手指骨头里有一个小的软骨瘤。

例句：A biopsy confirmed the presence of a benign chondroma in the patient's knee. 活检证实了病人膝盖里有一个良性软骨瘤。


近义词：cartilaginous tumor | 反义词：chondrosarcoma (malignant cartilage tumor)


词源:  From Greek *chondros* ("cartilage") + *-oma* ("tumor").  The suffix "-oma" is commonly used to denote a tumor or swelling.
