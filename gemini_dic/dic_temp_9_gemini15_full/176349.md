re-explain:/ˌriːɪkˈspleɪn/ | vt. |重新解释，再次解释 |To explain something again, often in a clearer or more detailed way. |例句：Could you re-explain that last point? 你能再解释一下最后一点吗？|例句：The teacher re-explained the difficult concept to the students.老师向学生们重新解释了这个难懂的概念。|近义词：explain again, clarify further, elucidate further |反义词：misunderstand, obfuscate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-explains the rules every week. 他每周都重新解释规则。
She re-explains the assignment to the confused student. 她向困惑的学生重新解释了作业。

一般过去时:
The professor re-explained the theorem after several students asked questions. 教授在几个学生提问后重新解释了定理。
I re-explained my position to my boss. 我向老板重新解释了我的立场。

现在完成时:
He has re-explained the instructions several times. 他已经多次重新解释了说明。
She has re-explained the process to ensure everyone understands. 她已经重新解释了这个过程，以确保每个人都理解。

过去完成时:
They had re-explained the plan before the meeting commenced. 他们在会议开始之前重新解释了计划。
I had re-explained the situation to him several times before he understood. 我在他理解之前已经多次向他重新解释了情况。

将来时:
He will re-explain the concept tomorrow. 他明天将重新解释这个概念。
She will re-explain the instructions to the new employees. 她将向新员工重新解释说明。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would re-explain the situation to them. 如果我是你，我会向他们重新解释情况。
I wish he had re-explained the assignment more clearly. 我希望他更清楚地重新解释作业。

被动语态:
The instructions were re-explained by the manager. 说明是由经理重新解释的。

疑问句:
Will you re-explain the procedure? 你会重新解释一下程序吗？
Did he re-explain the reason for the delay? 他是否重新解释了延误的原因？

否定句:
He did not re-explain the complex formula. 他没有重新解释复杂的公式。
She refused to re-explain her decision. 她拒绝重新解释她的决定。
