overpassionate:/ˌoʊvərˈpæʃənɪt/ | adj. | 过度热情的，过于热烈的 | Excessively passionate; showing or feeling too much passion.  It implies that the passion is excessive to the point of being unreasonable or detrimental. |例句：His overpassionate defense of his client alienated the jury.  他为其当事人所作的过度热情的辩护使陪审团疏远了他。

例句：Her overpassionate speech lost the audience's attention. 她过于热情的演讲失去了听众的注意力。


近义词：excessive, immoderate, fervent, zealous, enthusiastic (in a negative context) |反义词：apathetic, indifferent, calm, composed, reserved


词性：形容词
