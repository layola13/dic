economic: /ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪk/ /ˌɛkəˈnɑːmɪk/ | adj. |经济的；经济学的；有利可图的 | Relating to trade, industry, and money.  |例句：The economic situation is improving. 经济形势正在好转。  The project has strong economic benefits. 这个项目有很强的经济效益。|近义词：financial, monetary, commercial |反义词：uneconomical, unprofitable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The economic outlook is positive. = 经济前景是积极的。
Economic growth is slowing down. = 经济增长正在放缓。

一般过去时:
The economic crisis hit the country hard. = 经济危机重创了这个国家。
Economic policies changed dramatically last year. = 经济政策去年发生了巨大变化。

现在完成时:
The government has implemented several economic reforms. = 政府已经实施了几项经济改革。
Economic inequality has widened in recent years. = 近年来经济不平等加剧了。

过去完成时:
The company had already faced economic difficulties before the pandemic. = 在疫情爆发之前，该公司已经面临经济困难。
The country had experienced several economic downturns before this one. = 在这次经济衰退之前，这个国家经历过几次经济衰退。

将来时:
The economy will likely recover next year. = 经济明年可能会复苏。
Experts predict strong economic growth in the coming decade. = 专家预测未来十年经济将强劲增长。

虚拟语气:
If the economic situation were better, we would invest more. = 如果经济形势更好，我们会投资更多。
I wish the economic conditions were more favorable. = 我希望经济条件更优惠。

被动语态:
Economic policies are being debated in parliament. = 经济政策正在议会中进行辩论。
The economic impact is being assessed. = 经济影响正在评估中。

疑问句:
What are the economic implications of this decision? = 这个决定的经济影响是什么？
How will the economic downturn affect our business? = 经济衰退将如何影响我们的业务？

否定句:
The economic impact was not as severe as predicted. = 经济影响并不像预测的那样严重。
The project is not economically viable. = 这个项目在经济上不可行。
