out-of-humor: /ˌaʊt əvˈhjuːmər/ | adj. | 脾气不好的；心情不好的；没幽默感的 |  Describes someone who is irritable, grumpy, or lacking a sense of humor.  It suggests a temporary state rather than a permanent personality trait. | 例句：He's been out of humor all day;  avoid him if you can. 他今天脾气一直不好，尽量避开他吧。


He was out of humor after the argument. 争吵后，他心情很不好。


近义词：grumpy, irritable, bad-tempered, crotchety, ill-tempered, in a bad mood | 反义词：good-humored, cheerful, jovial, merry, in good spirits


词性: 形容词
