halves:/hɑːvz/| n.|一半，两半|The plural of half.  It refers to two equal parts of something.  It can be used as a noun or a verb. |例句：Cut the apple into halves.  把苹果切成两半。 The cake was divided into four halves. 蛋糕被分成四块。|近义词：sections, parts, pieces|反义词：whole, entirety|名词

We divide the profits into two halves. = 我们把利润分成两半。
