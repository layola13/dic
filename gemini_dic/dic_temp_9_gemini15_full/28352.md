carat:/ˈkærət/| n. |克拉；开；胡萝卜 | A unit of weight for precious stones, equal to 200 milligrams.  Also, a unit of fineness for gold, indicating the proportion of pure gold in an alloy.  Figuratively, it can refer to a degree or amount of something. |例句：The diamond ring has a one-carat diamond. 这枚钻戒有一克拉的钻石。 |例句：The gold is 18 carats fine. 这黄金是18k的。|近义词：unit (of weight), point (in reference to diamonds)|反义词：N/A
