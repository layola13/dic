fibrillar:/fɪˈbrɪlə(r)/| adj.|纤维状的；纤维的|Consisting of or resembling fibers.  It describes a structure or substance that is composed of or resembles fine fibers.|The muscle tissue showed a fibrillar pattern under the microscope. 显微镜下肌肉组织呈现纤维状结构。The protein formed fibrillar aggregates. 蛋白质形成了纤维状聚集体。|fibrous, filamentous|amorphous, non-fibrous|
