photoxylography:/ˌfoʊtoʊksɪˈlɒɡrəfi/| n. |摄影木刻术|A photographic process for producing wood engravings.  It involves using photography to create a reversed image on a wood block, which is then used for printing.| The artist used photoxylography to create detailed prints of the landscape.|近义词：photoengraving|反义词：无
