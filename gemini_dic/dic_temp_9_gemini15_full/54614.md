dismays:/dɪsˈmeɪz/ | vt. |使沮丧；使灰心；使失望 | To cause (someone) to feel dismay; to discourage or disheartened.  |The news of the accident dismayed her.  事故的消息使她沮丧。|近义词：depress, discourage, disappoint |反义词：encourage, uplift, cheer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The news dismays him. = 这个消息让他沮丧。
Her lack of effort dismays her teacher. = 她的不努力让老师很失望。


一般过去时:
The bad weather dismayed the hikers. = 恶劣的天气让徒步旅行者们灰心丧气。
The unexpected failure dismayed the team. = 出乎意料的失败让团队很沮丧。


现在完成时:
The constant criticism has dismayed him. = 持续不断的批评让他灰心了。
Recent events have dismayed many people. = 最近的事件让很多人失望。


过去完成时:
They had been dismayed by the previous failures. = 他们之前多次失败已经让他们灰心了。
I had been dismayed to learn of his departure. = 获悉他离开的消息，我非常沮丧。


将来时:
The announcement will dismay many voters. = 这一声明将会让许多选民失望。
Further delays will dismay the project team. = 进一步的延误将会让项目团队沮丧。


虚拟语气:
I would be dismayed if she failed the exam. = 如果她考试不及格，我会很失望。
If the project failed, it would dismay everyone involved. = 如果项目失败，所有参与者都会很沮丧。


被动语态:
He was dismayed by the results. = 他对结果感到沮丧。
The public was dismayed by the politician's actions. = 公众对这位政治家的行为感到失望。


疑问句:
Would you be dismayed if you lost your job? = 如果你失业了你会沮丧吗？
Is she dismayed by the lack of progress? = 她对缺乏进展感到沮丧吗？


否定句:
The news did not dismay her. = 这个消息并没有让她沮丧。
The challenges did not dismay the team. = 这些挑战并没有让团队灰心。


PHRASE->
to be dismayed at/by sth = 对某事感到沮丧
to feel dismayed = 感到沮丧
to express dismay = 表达沮丧
to cause dismay = 引起沮丧
a look of dismay = 一副沮丧的表情
