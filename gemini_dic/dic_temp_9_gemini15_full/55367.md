distaste:/dɪsˈteɪst/| n. |厌恶，反感 |A feeling of dislike or disgust.  It often implies a strong aversion to something,  whether it's a food, a person, an idea, or a situation. |例句：He expressed his distaste for the bitter medicine. 他表达了他对苦药的厌恶。

例句：Her distaste for politics was evident in her refusal to vote. 她对政治的反感在她拒绝投票的行为中显而易见。


近义词：disgust, aversion, dislike, repugnance, loathing |反义词：liking, fondness, relish, appreciation


词性：名词
