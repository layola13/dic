logicians:/ləˈdʒɪʃənz/| n. |逻辑学家|People who specialize in logic, the study of valid reasoning and argumentation. They analyze arguments, identify fallacies, and develop formal systems for reasoning.  Logicians work in various fields, including philosophy, mathematics, computer science, and linguistics. |Example: Many logicians contributed to the development of symbolic logic.  许多逻辑学家为符号逻辑的发展做出了贡献。|近义词：reasoners, thinkers, philosophers|反义词：irrationalists
