pandals:/ˈpændəl/| n.|临时棚屋，彩棚|A pandal is a temporary structure, usually made of bamboo and cloth, erected for festivals or celebrations, especially in South Asia.  They often provide shade and are decorated elaborately.|Example: The village square was filled with colorful pandals for the Diwali festival.  村庄广场上竖起了许多色彩斑斓的彩棚来庆祝排灯节。|近义词：booth, temporary shelter, marquee|反义词：permanent structure, building|名词
