exploits:/ˈɛkspɔɪts/ | n., v. |  开发；利用；功绩；壮举 | To make full use of a resource or situation, sometimes in a way that is unfair or unethical; a heroic act or deed. | 近义词：uses, utilizes, achievements, feats | 反义词：neglects, ignores


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exploits his workers. = 他剥削他的工人。
The company exploits the market. = 这家公司利用市场。


一般过去时:
They exploited the weakness of their enemy. = 他们利用了敌人的弱点。
She exploited her talents to become a successful artist. = 她利用自己的才能成为一名成功的艺术家。


现在完成时:
They have exploited the natural resources of the region. = 他们已经开发了该地区的自然资源。
He has exploited every opportunity that came his way. = 他抓住了每一个机会。


过去完成时:
The company had exploited the loophole in the law. = 该公司已经利用了法律中的漏洞。
They had exploited the situation to their advantage. = 他们已经利用这种情况为自己谋利。


将来时:
We will exploit this new technology. = 我们将利用这项新技术。
They will exploit the opportunity to expand their business. = 他们将利用这个机会扩大他们的业务。


虚拟语气:
If they exploited the market more effectively, they would make more profit. = 如果他们更有效地利用市场，他们就能获得更多利润。
I wish I had exploited that opportunity. = 我希望我当时抓住了那个机会。


被动语态:
The workers were exploited by their boss. = 工人们被老板剥削了。
The resources are being exploited by the mining company. = 资源正在被采矿公司开采。


疑问句:
How does the company exploit the market? = 这家公司如何利用市场？
Are they exploiting their employees? = 他们是否在剥削他们的员工？


否定句:
They don't exploit their workers. = 他们不剥削他们的工人。
The company didn't exploit the opportunity. = 该公司没有抓住机会。



PHRASE->
