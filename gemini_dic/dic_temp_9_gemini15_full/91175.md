hydrants: /ˈhaɪdrənts/ | n. |消防栓 | A hydrant is a pipe or nozzle attached to a water main, typically found on streets, that provides a readily accessible source of water, primarily for firefighting.  Hydrants are usually equipped with a valve and several outlets for connecting fire hoses. |

例句：

The firefighters quickly connected their hoses to the hydrant. 消防队员迅速将水管连接到消防栓上。

The hydrant was painted red for better visibility. 消防栓被漆成红色以提高可见度。


近义词：fireplug (informal)


反义词：无


词源：源自希腊语“hydro-” (水) 和 “-ant” (表示“有…的”或“属于…的”)，字面意思是“供水的”。
