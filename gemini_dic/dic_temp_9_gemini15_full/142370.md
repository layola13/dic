orthopnoea:/ɔːrˈθɒpniːə/| n. | 端坐呼吸，坐位呼吸 | Difficulty in breathing except when sitting or standing erect.  It is a symptom of several cardiac and respiratory conditions. | Example:  The patient experienced severe orthopnoea, requiring him to sleep propped up on several pillows.  患者经历了严重的端坐呼吸，需要靠着几个枕头才能睡觉。 | 近义词：dyspnea (shortness of breath) | 反义词： eupnea (normal breathing)
