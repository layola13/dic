outfly:/ˌaʊtˈflaɪ/| vt.|超越（在速度或距离上）|To fly faster or farther than (someone or something).|例句：The jet outflew all other aircraft in the race.  喷气式飞机在比赛中超越了所有其他飞机。
例句：The bird outflew the storm. 那只鸟飞越了暴风雨。|近义词：outspeed, surpass, exceed|反义词：underfly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The eagle outflies the hawk. = 老鹰比鹰飞得快。
The plane outflies the helicopter. = 飞机比直升机飞得快。

一般过去时:
The champion outflew his opponent. = 冠军超越了他的对手。
The migrating birds outflew the bad weather. = 迁徙的鸟儿飞越了恶劣天气。

现在完成时:
The new model has outflew all expectations. = 新型号已经超越了所有预期。
She has outflew her previous best time. = 她已经超越了她之前的最好成绩。

过去完成时:
He had outflew the competition by a significant margin. = 他在比赛中已经以显著的优势超越了竞争对手。
The team had outflew their rivals before the final race. = 这支队伍在决赛之前已经超越了他们的对手。

将来时:
The rocket will outfly any other spacecraft. = 火箭将超越任何其他航天器。
I believe he will outfly the current record holder. = 我相信他会超越目前的纪录保持者。

虚拟语气:
If the bird had stronger wings, it would outfly the wind. = 如果那只鸟有更强的翅膀，它就能飞越风。
If only I could outfly my problems. = 但愿我能超越我的困境。

被动语态:
The old plane was outflew by the new model. = 旧飞机被新型号超越了。
The record was outflew last year. = 纪录在去年被打破了。

疑问句:
Can the falcon outfly the eagle? = 猎鹰能比老鹰飞得快吗？
Does the jet outfly all other planes? = 喷气式飞机比所有其他飞机飞得都快吗？

否定句:
The small plane did not outfly the larger one. = 小型飞机没有超越大型飞机。
The tortoise did not outfly the hare. = 乌龟没有跑赢兔子。（虽然outfly指飞行，但可以引申为速度上的超越）


PHRASE->
outfly the competition = 超越竞争对手
outfly the storm = 飞越风暴
outfly the expectations = 超越预期
outfly the record = 超越纪录
outfly the others = 超越其他人
