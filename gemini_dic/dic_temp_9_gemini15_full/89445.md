hone:/hoʊn/| /həʊn/| vt. n.|磨练，锤炼；磨刀石|to sharpen (something, especially a blade) by rubbing it on a stone; to make something perfect or more effective|例句：He honed his skills as a writer over many years. 他多年来磨练了自己的写作技巧。|例句：The carpenter used a whetstone to hone his chisel. 木匠用磨刀石磨他的凿子。|近义词：sharpen, refine, perfect|反义词：dull, blunt


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hones his skills every day. = 他每天磨练他的技能。
She hones her presentation for the meeting. = 她为会议磨练她的演示文稿。

一般过去时:
The blacksmith honed the sword. = 铁匠磨了剑。
They honed their arguments before the debate. = 他们在大辩论之前磨练了自己的论点。

现在完成时:
She has honed her public speaking skills. = 她已经磨练了她的公开演讲技巧。
He has honed his expertise in this field. = 他在这个领域已经磨练了他的专业知识。

过去完成时:
He had honed his skills before the competition. = 他在比赛前已经磨练了他的技能。
She had honed her craft for many years. = 她多年来一直磨练她的技艺。

将来时:
He will hone his skills further. = 他将进一步磨练他的技能。
She will hone her presentation before the big event. = 她将在大型活动之前磨练她的演示文稿。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would hone my skills. = 如果我有更多时间，我会磨练我的技能。
I wish I had honed my writing skills earlier. = 我希望我早点磨练我的写作技巧。

被动语态:

The knives were honed to a razor sharpness. = 刀子被磨得锋利无比。

疑问句:

否定句:
