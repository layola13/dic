buttonbush:/ˈbʌtənˌbuʃ/| n. |纽扣木|A shrub or small tree of the genus Cephalanthus, having spherical heads of small white flowers resembling buttons.|例句：The buttonbush blooms profusely in summer, attracting many pollinators.  夏季纽扣木盛开，吸引了许多传粉者。|例句：We planted a buttonbush near the pond to enhance the wetland habitat. 我们在池塘边种了一棵纽扣木，以改善湿地栖息地。|近义词：globeflower (部分相似之处)|反义词：无|名词
