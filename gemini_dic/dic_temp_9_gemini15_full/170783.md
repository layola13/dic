pseudocubical:/ˌsuːdoʊˈkjuːbɪkəl/ | adj. | 似立方体的；近似立方体的 |  Approximating a cube in shape; nearly cubical.  It refers to something that resembles a cube but is not perfectly cubical, often having slightly irregular dimensions. | Example: The crystal structure exhibited a pseudocubical form.  该晶体结构呈现出近似立方体的形状。|近义词：quasi-cubical,subcubical|反义词：cubical,perfectly cubic|
