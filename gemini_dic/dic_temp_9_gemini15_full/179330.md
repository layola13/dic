redouble:/riːˈdʌbəl/| vt.|加倍；再加倍；加倍努力|To make or become twice as great; to increase twofold; to renew or intensify.|例句：The company redoubled its efforts to meet the deadline. 公司加倍努力以赶上最后期限。例句：He redoubled his efforts to win the race. 他加倍努力赢得比赛。|近义词：multiply, intensify, reinforce|反义词：halve, decrease, lessen|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They redouble their efforts. = 他们加倍努力。
The company redoubles its marketing campaign. = 公司加倍努力进行营销活动。

一般过去时:
He redoubled his vigilance. = 他加倍警惕。
She redoubled her attempts to contact him. = 她加倍尝试联系他。

现在完成时:
We have redoubled our commitment to sustainability. = 我们已经加倍致力于可持续发展。
They have redoubled their security measures. = 他们已经加倍加强了安全措施。

过去完成时:
He had redoubled his efforts before the final exam. = 他在期末考试前加倍努力。
She had redoubled her determination after the setback. = 在遭遇挫折后，她加倍坚定决心。

将来时:
They will redouble their investment in research. = 他们将加倍投资研究。
We will redouble our efforts to resolve the issue. = 我们将加倍努力解决这个问题。

虚拟语气:
If they redoubled their efforts, they might succeed. = 如果他们加倍努力，他们可能会成功。
I wish he had redoubled his attention to detail. = 我希望他以前能更加注意细节。

被动语态:
Efforts were redoubled to prevent further damage. = 加倍努力以防止进一步的损害。
The security measures were redoubled after the incident. = 事件发生后，安全措施得到了加强。

疑问句:
Will the company redouble its investment? = 公司会加倍投资吗？
Did they redouble their efforts to find a solution? = 他们是否加倍努力寻找解决方案？

否定句:
They did not redouble their efforts. = 他们没有加倍努力。
He didn't redouble his commitment to the project. = 他并没有加倍致力于这个项目。


PHRASE->

词源:
re-(again) + double (two fold)  再次加倍。  来自古法语redoubler，源于拉丁语re- (again) + duplare (to double).
