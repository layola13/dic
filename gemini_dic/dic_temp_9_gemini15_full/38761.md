condoners:/kənˈdoʊnərz/ | n. | 宽恕者；容忍者 | Those who condone something accept or allow it, even if it is wrong.  They might not actively participate, but they don't object either.  The term often implies a moral failing in the condoner's inaction. | The condoners of the regime's actions were as culpable as the perpetrators themselves. = 该政权行为的纵容者与作恶者一样有罪。  |  Those who overlook wrongdoing, apologists, tolerators | Condemners, critics, objectors | 名词
