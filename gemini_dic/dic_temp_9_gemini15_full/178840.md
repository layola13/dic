recycled:/ˌriˈsaɪkəld/| adj.| 再生的；循环利用的；回收的 |Something that is recycled has been processed to be used again.  It refers to materials or products that have been reprocessed or reused. | Example:  Many companies now use recycled paper. 许多公司现在使用再生纸。  The city has a program for recycled materials. 这个城市有一个回收利用的项目。|近义词：reprocessed, reused, reclaimed |反义词：new, unused, virgin


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Recycled materials are used in many products. = 再生材料被用于许多产品中。
This company recycles plastic bottles. = 这家公司回收塑料瓶。


一般过去时:
They recycled all the glass bottles. = 他们回收了所有的玻璃瓶。
The old newspapers were recycled. = 旧报纸被回收了。


现在完成时:
We have recycled a lot of waste this year. = 今年我们已经回收了很多废物。
The company has recycled millions of tons of materials. = 这家公司已经回收了数百万吨的材料。


过去完成时:
They had already recycled the paper before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经回收了纸张。
The factory had recycled thousands of tons of waste by the end of the year. = 到年底，这家工厂已经回收了数千吨的废物。


将来时:
We will recycle more plastic in the future. = 我们将来会回收更多塑料。
The new plant will recycle waste water. = 新工厂将回收废水。


虚拟语气:
If we recycled more, we would reduce pollution. = 如果我们回收更多，我们将减少污染。
I wish we had recycled more in the past. = 我希望过去我们回收得更多。


被动语态:
Millions of tons of materials are recycled annually. = 每年都有数百万吨的材料被回收。
The plastic bottles are being recycled at the plant. = 塑料瓶正在工厂里被回收。


疑问句:
Is this paper recycled? = 这张纸是再生纸吗？
Are all the materials recycled? = 所有材料都回收了吗？


否定句:
This product is not made from recycled materials. = 这个产品不是用再生材料制造的。
They didn't recycle the plastic bags. = 他们没有回收塑料袋。
