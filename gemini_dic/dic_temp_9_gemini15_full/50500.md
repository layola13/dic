depressurizing: /ˌdiːˌprɛʃəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 减压；泄压 | The process of reducing pressure, typically in a closed system.  It involves releasing or lowering the pressure within a container or environment. This is often done for safety reasons or to facilitate a process. |

例句：

The aircraft is currently depressurizing. = 飞机目前正在减压。

Engineers are depressurizing the reactor before maintenance. = 工程师们在维护之前正在对反应堆进行泄压。


近义词：decompressing, venting, releasing pressure

反义词：pressurizing

词源:

The word "depressurizing" is derived from "pressure" and the prefix "de-", meaning "to remove or reduce," and the suffix "-izing," indicating the process of doing something.  "Pressure" originates from the Latin word "pressura," meaning "a pressing."
