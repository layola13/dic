guidingly:/ˈɡaɪdɪŋli/| adv.|指导地；引导地；循循善诱地 |In a way that guides or directs; leading or showing the way.  It implies a gentle and helpful manner of guidance.|例句：She guidedly led the lost child back to his parents. 她循循善诱地把迷路的孩子带回了父母身边。


例句：The teacher guidingly explained the complex concept to her students.老师循循善诱地向学生解释了这个复杂的概念。


近义词：directively, instructively, leadingly |反义词：misleadingly, confusingly
