/ˌaʊtˈvɔɪsɪŋ/ | v. |大声说出；盖过…的声音；比…声音大 | To speak louder than someone or something else; to overpower with sound. |例句：The singer outvoiced the orchestra during the powerful crescendo. 歌手在强烈的渐强部分盖过了管弦乐的声音。

例句：The protesters outvoiced the government's attempts to silence them. 抗议者们的声音盖过了政府试图让他们沉默的企图。


近义词：outshout, outcall, overpower (in sound), drown out | 反义词：be outvoiced by, be silenced by


词性:动词


USAGE->

The band outvoices the crowd's cheers. 乐队的演奏声盖过了人群的欢呼声。
