predictor: /prɪˈdɪktər/ | n. | 预报器；预测因子；预测变量 | A predictor is something that can be used to predict something else. It is often a variable in a statistical model that is used to predict the value of another variable.  | Example:  Temperature is a good predictor of ice cream sales.  温度是冰淇淋销量的良好预测指标。

The level of education is a strong predictor of income. 教育程度是收入的有力预测指标。|近义词：indicator, forecaster, prognosticator | 反义词：unforecaster (less common),  something that hinders prediction (no single word antonym) | 名词
