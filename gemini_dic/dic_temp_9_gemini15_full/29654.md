catfighting:/ˈkætˌfaɪtɪŋ/| n.|猫打架；女人间的争吵|A catfight is a fight between women, typically involving scratching, slapping, and hair-pulling. It often implies a petty or spiteful quarrel. |例句：The two women got into a catfight over a man. 这两个女人因为一个男人大打出手。|The two actresses engaged in a vicious catfight during the awards ceremony.|这两位女演员在颁奖典礼上发生了一场恶劣的争吵。|近义词：brawling, squabbling, scuffling|反义词：cooperation, harmony
