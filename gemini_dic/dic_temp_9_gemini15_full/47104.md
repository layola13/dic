daughterless:/ˈdɔːtərləs/| adj. |无女儿的，没有女儿的 |Lacking a daughter or daughters.  The adjective describes a person, family, or situation where there are no daughters. |例句：She remained daughterless, a fact that saddened her deeply. 她终身未育女儿，这让她非常悲伤。

例句：The family line ended with him, leaving the estate daughterless.  这个家族的传承在他这里终结，留下了一个没有女儿继承的产业。
