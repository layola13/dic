re-budget:/ˌriːˈbʌdʒɪt/| vt.|重新编制预算，修订预算 |To revise or adjust a budget.|例句：We need to re-budget for the project due to unforeseen costs.  我们需要因为不可预见的成本而重新编制项目的预算。|例句：The company had to re-budget after experiencing unexpected losses. 公司在经历意外损失后不得不重新编制预算。|近义词：revise, readjust, amend |反义词：finalize, approve (in the context of a final budget)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-budget annually. = 他们每年重新编制预算。
The team re-budgets the project every quarter. = 团队每季度都会重新编制项目预算。

一般过去时:
We re-budgeted the marketing campaign last month. = 我们上个月重新编制了营销活动的预算。
She re-budgeted her personal finances after a large purchase. = 她在进行大笔购买后重新编制了自己的个人财务预算。

现在完成时:
They have re-budgeted several times this year. = 他们今年已经重新编制过几次预算了。
The company has re-budgeted for the new product launch. = 公司已经为新产品的发布重新编制了预算。

过去完成时:
They had re-budgeted before the unexpected expenses arose. = 在出现意外开支之前，他们已经重新编制了预算。
The project manager had re-budgeted twice before the final deadline. = 项目经理在最终截止日期之前已经重新编制了两次预算。

将来时:
We will re-budget next fiscal year. = 我们将在下一个财政年度重新编制预算。
The department will re-budget if the sales figures are lower than expected. = 如果销售额低于预期，该部门将重新编制预算。

虚拟语气:
If we had re-budgeted earlier, we would have avoided the deficit. = 如果我们早点重新编制预算，就能避免赤字。
It would be wise to re-budget if the project scope expands significantly. = 如果项目的范围大幅扩大，重新编制预算将是明智之举。

被动语态:
The budget was re-budgeted after the audit. = 预算在审计后被重新编制。
The project's budget is being re-budgeted due to cost overruns. = 由于成本超支，项目预算正在被重新编制。

疑问句:
Will the budget be re-budgeted before the end of the quarter? = 预算会在本季度结束前重新编制吗？
Has the marketing team re-budgeted their campaign yet? = 营销团队已经重新编制他们的活动预算了吗？

否定句:
We did not re-budget the project, despite the challenges. = 尽管面临挑战，我们并没有重新编制项目的预算。
They will not re-budget unless there are significant changes. = 除非有重大变化，否则他们不会重新编制预算。
