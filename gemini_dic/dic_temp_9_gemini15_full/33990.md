citator: /ˈsaɪteɪtər/ | n. |引证器；引文索引；（法律）判例引用书 | A citator is a legal research tool that lists all the cases that have cited a particular case, statute, or other legal authority.  It helps lawyers and judges track the history and influence of a legal precedent.  | Example: She used a citator to find all the cases that had cited the landmark Supreme Court decision.  | 近义词：case reporter, legal database | 反义词：无
