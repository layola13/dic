off-peak:/ˌɒf piːk/ /ˌɑːf piːk/| adj.|淡季的；非高峰期的；|Not during the busiest time.  This term often refers to times when demand for a service or product is lower, leading to lower prices or less congestion.| 例句：Off-peak train fares are much cheaper.  淡季火车票便宜得多。

例句：We decided to travel during the off-peak season to avoid the crowds. 我们决定在淡季旅行以避开人群。


近义词：low-season, slack, quiet

反义词：peak, rush hour, busy
