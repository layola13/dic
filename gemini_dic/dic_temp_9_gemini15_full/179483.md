reflective:/rɪˈflɛktɪv/ | adj. | 反射的；深思熟虑的；反映的 |  Relating to or characterized by reflection; showing or expressing reflection.  This word has two main meanings: one relating to the physical reflection of light or sound, and the other relating to thoughtful consideration or contemplation. |

例句：

The lake's surface was reflective, mirroring the surrounding mountains.  湖面反射着周围的山峦。

He gave a reflective sigh, pondering the implications of his decision. 他叹了口气，沉思着自己决定的影响。


近义词：thoughtful, contemplative, mirrored, reflecting

反义词：unreflective, impulsive, thoughtless


词源：

From Middle English *reflectryf, from Old French reflectif, from Latin reflectere (to bend back, reflect), from re- (back) + flectere (to bend).
