dehorn:/diːˈhɔːrn/| vt.|去角；除角 |To remove the horns from (an animal).  The practice is often used to prevent animals from injuring themselves or others, or to make them easier to handle.  |例句：The farmer dehorned the calf to prevent it from injuring itself or other animals.  农民给小牛去角，以防止它伤害自己或其他动物。

例句：They dehorned the bull to make it safer to handle. 他们给公牛去角以方便处理。


近义词：disbud, remove horns |反义词：horn,  grow horns
