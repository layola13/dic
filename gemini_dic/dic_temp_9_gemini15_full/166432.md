preretirement: /ˌpriːrɪˈtaɪərmənt/ | n. |退休前；退休准备阶段 | The period of time before retirement, often involving planning and preparation for life after work.  |例句：He's already started his preretirement planning, looking into investment options and travel plans. 他已经开始退休前的计划，研究投资选择和旅行计划。


例句：Many people use their preretirement years to pursue hobbies and interests they neglected during their working lives. 许多人在退休前几年从事他们在工作期间忽视的爱好和兴趣。


近义词：pre-retirement planning, retirement preparation


反义词：postretirement


词性：名词
