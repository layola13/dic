haemocyanin:/ˌhiːməˈsaɪənɪn/ | n. |血蓝蛋白|A blue copper-containing protein that transports oxygen in the blood of many arthropods and mollusks. It is responsible for the blue color of their blood.|例句：Haemocyanin is the respiratory protein found in the blood of many invertebrates. 血蓝蛋白是许多无脊椎动物血液中发现的呼吸蛋白。|例句：The blue color of the octopus's blood is due to the presence of haemocyanin. 章鱼血液的蓝色是由于血蓝蛋白的存在。|近义词：hemocyanin|反义词：hemoglobin (in vertebrates)
