echocardiography:/ˌɛkoʊˌkɑːrdiˈɑːɡrəfi/ | n. |超声心动图检查| The process of using ultrasound to create images of the heart.  It is a non-invasive procedure used to diagnose various heart conditions. |The doctor ordered an echocardiography to assess the extent of the heart damage. 医生开了超声心动图检查来评估心脏损伤的程度。| cardiac ultrasound, ultrasound of the heart |  (no direct antonym, but terms indicating absence of the procedure could be used, e.g.,  no cardiac imaging) | 名词
