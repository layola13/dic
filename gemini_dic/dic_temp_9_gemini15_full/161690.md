poutingly:/ˈpaʊtɪŋli/| adv.|撅嘴地，不高兴地|In a manner that expresses displeasure or sulkiness, typically by pouting.  This adverb describes the way someone acts when they are unhappy or sulky, often involving a pout.|例句：She looked poutingly at her empty plate. 她噘着嘴看着空空的盘子。
She spoke poutingly about her unfair treatment. 她噘着嘴抱怨自己受到了不公平的待遇。|近义词：sulkily, petulantly, sullenly|反义词：happily, cheerfully, joyfully
