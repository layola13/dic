channelings: /ˈtʃænəlɪŋz/ | n. | 通灵；引导；渠道化 | The process of receiving and transmitting information or energy from a source outside of the conscious mind, often attributed to spiritual or supernatural means.  It can also refer to the act of directing something into a particular channel or course. | 例句：Many people believe in the power of channelings to connect with higher beings. 许多人相信通灵的力量可以与高级生命体连接。

The channeling of funds into education is crucial for national development. 将资金引导到教育领域对于国家发展至关重要。 | 近义词：mediumship, guidance, direction, conveyance | 反义词：obstruction, blockage
