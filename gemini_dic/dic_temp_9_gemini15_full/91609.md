hyperanarchy:/ˌhaɪpərˈænərki/| n. |超无政府状态，极端无政府状态 |A state of extreme decentralization and lack of governance, often characterized by a multitude of actors operating independently with limited or no central authority.  It differs from anarchy in that it acknowledges the existence of numerous competing power centers, rather than a complete absence of power. |Example: The internet, with its myriad independent websites and platforms, can be seen as a form of hyperanarchy.  互联网，以其无数的独立网站和平台，可以被看作是一种超无政府状态。|近义词：decentralization, fragmentation, chaos |反义词：centralization, order, governance |名词
