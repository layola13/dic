persuades:/pərˈsweɪdz/| vt.| 说服；劝说 | To convince someone to do something or to believe something.  |例句：He persuaded me to go to the party. 他说服我去参加派对。 |例句：Her arguments persuaded the jury of her innocence. 她的论据使陪审团相信她是无罪的。|近义词：convince, influence, induce |反义词：dissuade


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He persuades his clients to invest wisely. 他劝说他的客户明智地投资。
She persuades her children to eat their vegetables. 她劝说她的孩子们吃蔬菜。

一般过去时:
The salesman persuaded me to buy the car. 销售员说服我买了那辆车。
They persuaded the committee to change its decision. 他们说服委员会改变了决定。

现在完成时:
She has persuaded many people to donate to charity. 她已经说服许多人捐款给慈善机构。
He has persuaded me that I'm wrong. 他已经说服我我错了。

过去完成时:
I had persuaded myself that it was a good idea. 我已经说服自己这是一个好主意。
They had persuaded him to quit his job before the scandal broke. 在丑闻爆发之前，他们已经说服他辞职了。

将来时:
I think I will persuade him to come with us. 我想我会说服他跟我们一起来。
She will persuade her boss to give her a raise. 她将会说服她的老板给她加薪。

虚拟语气:
If only I could persuade him to change his mind! 要是我能说服他改变主意就好了！
I wish I could persuade her to reconsider. 我希望我能说服她重新考虑。

被动语态:
He was persuaded to join the army. 他被说服参军了。
The audience was persuaded by her powerful speech. 观众被她有力的演讲所打动。

疑问句:
Can you persuade him to help us? 你能说服他帮助我们吗？
Will she be persuaded to cooperate? 她会被说服合作吗？

否定句:
I couldn't persuade him to stay. 我无法说服他留下。
They didn't persuade her to change her opinion. 他们没有说服她改变主意。

persuade someone out of doing something 劝阻某人做某事
