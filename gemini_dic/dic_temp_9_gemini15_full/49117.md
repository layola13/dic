deinternationalizes: /ˌdiːˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlaɪzɪz/ | vt. |使不再具有国际性；取消国际化；使脱离国际关系| To remove the international character of something; to make something less international; to withdraw from international relations. | The company deinternationalized its operations, focusing solely on the domestic market.  公司将其业务去国际化，只专注于国内市场。| 近义词:localize, nationalize | 反义词:internationalize, globalize
