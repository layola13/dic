cross-street:/ˈkrɔːsstriːt/| n. |交叉路口；横街|A street that intersects another.  It often refers to a smaller or less important street intersecting a main street.|Example:  Turn left at the next cross-street.  下一个交叉路口左转。|近义词：intersection, side street|反义词：main street, thoroughfare
