inheriting: /ɪnˈhɛrɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 继承；遗传 |  The act of receiving something from someone who has died, or of receiving a quality or characteristic from one's ancestors.  |

例句：

He is inheriting his father's business. = 他正在继承他父亲的事业。

She is inheriting a strong work ethic from her mother. = 她遗传了她母亲强烈的职业道德。


近义词：receiving, succeeding to, acquiring

反义词： bequeathing, leaving (as a legacy)

词性：动词

They are inheriting a large fortune. = 他们正在继承一大笔财产。
He inherits his father's wisdom. = 他继承了他父亲的智慧。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

疑问句:

否定句:
