hymenophore:/ˈhaɪmənəfɔːr/| n. |菌膜, 子实层|The hymenophore is the spore-bearing surface of a fungus.  It can take many forms, depending on the species.  For example, it might be gills, pores, teeth, or smooth. |The mushroom's hymenophore was a series of delicate gills radiating from the stem.  这个蘑菇的菌膜是一系列从菌柄辐射出来的精致菌褶。|近义词： fertile layer, spore-bearing surface|反义词：无
