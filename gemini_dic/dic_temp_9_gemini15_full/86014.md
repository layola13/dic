heady:/ˈhedi/| adj.|令人兴奋的；令人陶醉的；浓烈的；醉人的|intoxicating; strong and exciting;  characterized by or producing exhilaration or intoxication|The heady scent of jasmine filled the air.  茉莉花的浓郁香味充满了空气。|The heady days of youth are soon over. 青春的激情岁月很快就过去了。|近义词：intoxicating, exhilarating, intoxicating, potent, strong|反义词：sober, mild, weak
