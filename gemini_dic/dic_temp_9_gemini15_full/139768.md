octahedral:/ˌɒk.təˈhiː.drəl/ | adj. | 八面体的 | Having eight faces.  An octahedron is a polyhedron with eight faces.  The term is often used to describe the geometry of molecules and crystals.  | Example:  The crystal structure of diamond is octahedral.  钻石的晶体结构是八面体的。  The coordination geometry of many transition metal complexes is octahedral. 许多过渡金属配合物的配位几何形状是八面体的。 | 近义词: eight-sided (when referring to shape only) | 反义词: tetrahedral (four-sided), cubic (six-sided)
