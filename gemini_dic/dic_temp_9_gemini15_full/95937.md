incubating:/ˈɪŋkjuˌbeɪtɪŋ/| v.|孵化；酝酿；培养|The act of incubating involves keeping eggs warm until they hatch, or fostering the development of something.|例句：The hen is incubating her eggs. 母鸡正在孵蛋。

The scientists are incubating a new idea. 科学家们正在酝酿一个新想法。|fostering, hatching, brooding| hindering, neglecting, destroying|动词
