flench:/flɛntʃ/| vt.|  使…畏缩；使…退缩；|to shrink back or recoil, especially in fear or disgust|The smell made him flench.|那气味使他畏缩。|近义词：shrink, recoil, flinch|反义词：advance, approach


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flenches at the sight of blood. = 他看到血就畏缩。
She flenches from difficult conversations. = 她回避艰难的谈话。


一般过去时:
He flenched at the loud noise. = 他被巨大的噪音吓了一跳。
She flenched when she saw the injury. = 她看到伤势时畏缩了。


现在完成时:
He has flenched from responsibility all his life. = 他一生都逃避责任。
She has never flenched in the face of adversity. = 她从未在逆境面前退缩。


过去完成时:
He had flenched before the confrontation, but he faced it bravely. = 他在对抗之前曾畏缩，但他勇敢地面对了。
She had flenched at the thought of public speaking, but she eventually did it. = 她曾害怕公开演讲，但她最终还是做了。


将来时:
He will flench when he hears the bad news. = 他听到坏消息时会畏缩。
She will likely flench at the cost. = 她很可能会对价格退缩。


虚拟语气:
If he had not flenched, he might have won the fight. = 如果他不畏缩，他可能就赢了这场战斗。
I wish I hadn't flenched at the critical moment. = 我真希望我在关键时刻没有畏缩。


被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes a reflexive action.)


疑问句:
Did he flench when he saw the snake? = 他看到蛇时畏缩了吗？
Does she flench at the mention of spiders? = 她一提到蜘蛛就畏缩吗？


否定句:
He did not flench in the face of danger. = 他面对危险时没有畏缩。
She did not flench from her duty. = 她没有逃避她的责任。


PHRASE->
flench away  = 退缩
flench from = 逃避
flench back = 畏缩后退
flench at the sight of = 看到...就畏缩
flench in fear = 害怕地畏缩
