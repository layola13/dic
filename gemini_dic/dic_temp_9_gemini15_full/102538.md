jading:/ˈdʒeɪdɪŋ/| adj.|令人厌倦的；令人疲惫的；| causing weariness, boredom, or dissatisfaction;  making someone feel tired and lacking in energy.|例句：The endless meetings were jading.  冗长的会议令人厌倦。|例句：The repetitive nature of the job was jading her. 这份工作的重复性让她感到厌倦。|近义词：tiring, exhausting, wearisome, tedious, monotonous|反义词：invigorating, stimulating, refreshing, exciting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The repetitive tasks are jading me. = 重复性的任务让我感到厌倦。
This monotonous work is jading him. = 这单调的工作让他感到厌倦。

一般过去时:
The long journey jaded us all. = 漫长的旅程让我们所有人都感到疲惫。
The endless paperwork jaded her spirit. = 无休止的文书工作磨损了她的精神。

现在完成时:
The constant pressure has jaded my enthusiasm. = 持久的压力已经磨损了我的热情。
Years of hard work have jaded him. = 多年的辛勤工作使他感到疲惫。

过去完成时:
She had been jaded by years of disappointment. = 她多年来一直遭受失望的打击，感到厌倦。
The team had been jaded by a series of losses. = 一系列的失利使球队感到疲惫。

将来时:
This endless routine will jade you eventually. = 这无尽的例行公事最终会让你感到厌倦。
I fear that this lifestyle will jade me in the long run. = 我担心这种生活方式从长远来看会让我感到厌倦。

虚拟语气:
If I continued this job, I would be jaded. = 如果我继续这份工作，我会感到厌倦。
I wish I hadn't been jaded by that experience. = 我真希望我没有被那次经历弄得厌倦。

被动语态:
He was jaded by the harsh conditions. = 他被恶劣的条件弄得疲惫不堪。
Her spirit was jaded by the relentless criticism. = 她被无情的批评弄得精神萎靡。

疑问句:
Are you feeling jaded after such a long week? = 经历了这么长的一周，你感到疲惫吗？
Is this project jading you? = 这个项目让你感到厌倦了吗？

否定句:
The holiday was refreshing, not jading. = 假期令人耳目一新，而不是令人厌倦。
I don't feel jaded at all; I'm energized. = 我一点也不觉得厌倦；我精力充沛。
