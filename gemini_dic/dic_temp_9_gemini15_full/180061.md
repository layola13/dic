reflectors:/rɪˈflɛktərz/| n. |反射器，反光镜 |A reflector is a device or surface that reflects light, sound, or other waves.  Reflectors are used in a wide variety of applications, from car headlights to bicycle safety lights to telescopes. |例句：The car's reflectors helped make it visible at night.  这辆车的反光镜有助于在夜间使其可见。
