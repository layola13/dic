chain-smoke:/ˈtʃeɪnˌsmoʊk/ | v. | 狂吸烟，连续不断地抽烟 | To smoke one cigarette immediately after another.  | Example: He chain-smoked throughout the meeting. 他在整个会议期间不停地抽烟。 | 近义词：smoke incessantly, constantly smoke | 反义词：abstain from smoking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chain-smokes every day. = 他每天都狂抽烟。
They chain-smoke during stressful situations. = 他们在压力大的情况下会连续抽烟。

一般过去时:
She chain-smoked during the flight. = 她在飞行期间不停地抽烟。
He chain-smoked all night long. = 他整晚都在不停地抽烟。

现在完成时:
He has chain-smoked for twenty years. = 他抽烟成瘾已经二十年了。
She has chain-smoked since she was a teenager. = 从十几岁起她就一直不停地抽烟。

过去完成时:
He had chain-smoked before the doctor warned him. = 在医生警告他之前，他已经抽了很多烟了。
She had chain-smoked for hours before she finally fell asleep. = 她连续抽烟数小时后才终于睡着。

将来时:
I think he will chain-smoke again tonight. = 我想他今晚还会不停地抽烟。
If he continues to be stressed, he will chain-smoke. = 如果他继续承受压力，他会不停地抽烟。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't chain-smoked, he might be healthier now. = 如果他不一直抽烟，他现在可能更健康。
I wish he wouldn't chain-smoke in my car. = 我希望他不要在我的车里不停地抽烟。

被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Does he chain-smoke? = 他抽烟成瘾吗？
Did she chain-smoke at the party? = 她在派对上不停地抽烟吗？

否定句:
He doesn't chain-smoke anymore. = 他不再不停地抽烟了。
She didn't chain-smoke during the presentation. = 她在演示期间没有不停地抽烟。
