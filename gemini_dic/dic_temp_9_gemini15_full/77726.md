gemmulation:/ˌdʒɛmjuˈleɪʃən/| n. |芽生殖；出芽生殖；(海绵动物的)芽球形成|The asexual reproduction of certain invertebrates, such as sponges, by the formation of buds or gemmules.|例句：Gemmulation is a crucial survival strategy for many freshwater sponges during periods of environmental stress.  芽生殖是许多淡水海绵在环境压力时期的一种重要的生存策略。|例句：The gemmules of freshwater sponges can survive harsh winter conditions and germinate in spring. 淡水海绵的芽球可以挺过严酷的冬季，并在春天发芽。|近义词：budding, gemmation|反义词：sexual reproduction
