lovey:/ˈlʌvi/| n. adj.|亲爱的；爱人；可爱的人；可爱的|A term of endearment, often used for a loved one or something considered cute or charming. It can function as both a noun and an adjective.|例句：My lovey is waiting for me.我的爱人在等我。  She's such a lovey little girl.她真是个可爱的小女孩。|近义词： darling, sweetheart, sweetie, honey, cute |反义词: hateful, unpleasant, unlovable
