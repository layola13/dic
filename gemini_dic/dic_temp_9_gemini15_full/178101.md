recoagulate:/ˌriːkoʊˈæɡjuleɪt/| vt.|重新凝结；使再次凝固|To coagulate again; to cause to clot or thicken again.  The process involves the reformation of a clot or gel-like substance after it has been broken down or disrupted. |例句：The blood recoagulated quickly after the initial injury.  血液在最初的损伤后迅速重新凝结。

The surgeon attempted to recoagulate the bleeding tissue. 外科医生试图使出血组织重新凝固。


近义词：re-clot, thicken, solidify |反义词：liquefy, dissolve, thin
