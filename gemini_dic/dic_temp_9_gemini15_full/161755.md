powerlessness:/ˈpaʊərləsnəs/| n. |无力感，无能为力|The feeling of being unable to control events or influence a situation.  It often involves a sense of helplessness and frustration. |例句：He felt a surge of powerlessness as he watched the fire consume his home. 他看着大火吞噬了他的家，感到一阵无力感。

例句：The powerlessness of the victims in the face of such overwhelming force was heartbreaking.  面对如此强大的力量，受害者的无力感令人心碎。


近义词：helplessness, inability, impotence, inadequacy |反义词：power, control, agency, efficacy
