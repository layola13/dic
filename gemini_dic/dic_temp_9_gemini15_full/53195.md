dipteran:/ˈdɪptərən/| n.|双翅目昆虫|A dipteran is an insect of the order Diptera, characterized by having only two wings, unlike most other flying insects which have four.  Dipterans include flies, mosquitoes, and gnats.|例句：Many dipterans are important pollinators. 许多双翅目昆虫是重要的传粉者。|例句：The study focused on the behavior of a specific dipteran species. 这项研究重点关注一种特定双翅目昆虫的行为。|近义词：fly (informal)|反义词：None specifically, but insects with four wings could be considered the opposite.|名词
