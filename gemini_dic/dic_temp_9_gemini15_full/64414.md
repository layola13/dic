ergoism:/ˈɜːɡoʊɪzəm/ | n. |利己主义；自我中心；唯我论 |The philosophy or practice of egoism, emphasizing self-interest as the foundation of morality.  Ergoism prioritizes individual needs and desires above all else, often disregarding the well-being of others.  | Example: His actions, though seemingly beneficial to himself, were ultimately a manifestation of blatant ergoism.  他的行为虽然表面上对他有利，但最终是赤裸裸的利己主义的表现。|近义词：selfishness, self-centeredness, narcissism, egotism |反义词：altruism, selflessness, benevolence |名词
