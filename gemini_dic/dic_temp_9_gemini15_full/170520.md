psalmography:/sælˈmɒɡrəfi/| n. |诗篇创作；诗篇研究|The study and writing of psalms.  It involves analyzing the structure, themes, and literary devices used in psalms, as well as composing new psalms. |Example:  Her doctoral thesis was a comprehensive study of psalmography in the Hebrew Bible.  她的博士论文是对希伯来圣经中诗篇创作的全面研究。|近义词：psalmody, hymnography |反义词：N/A
