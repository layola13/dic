imperfections:/ˌɪmpərˈfɛkʃənz/| n. |缺陷；缺点；不完美之处 | flaws, defects, shortcomings; the state of being imperfect.  Imperfections are flaws or blemishes that detract from something's perfection. They can be physical, like a crack in a vase, or abstract, like a weakness in a plan. |例句：The painting, despite its imperfections, is a masterpiece. 这幅画尽管有缺陷，但仍是一部杰作。

The product has several imperfections that need to be addressed.  该产品有几个需要解决的缺陷。


近义词：flaws, defects, blemishes, shortcomings, imperfections, faults

反义词：perfection, flawlessness
