parentalism:/ˈpærənˌtælɪzəm/| n. |家长式作风；家长主义；庇护主义 |The act of governing or treating people in the manner of a parent dealing with a child.  This often involves restricting autonomy and decision-making, with the intention of protecting or guiding, but potentially at the expense of individual freedom and responsibility. |例句：The government's paternalistic policies stifled economic growth. 政府的家长式政策扼杀了经济增长。|例句：His paternalistic approach to managing his employees led to resentment. 他家长式的管理方式导致了员工的反感。|近义词：protectionism, guardianship, overprotection |反义词：laissez-faire, autonomy, freedom
