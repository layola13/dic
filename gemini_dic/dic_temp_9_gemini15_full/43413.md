cranny:/ˈkræni/| n. |裂缝；缝隙；隐蔽处 | A narrow opening or fissure, especially in a rock or wall; a secluded or hidden place. |例句：He found a cranny in the wall to hide in. 他找到墙上一个缝隙躲了起来。
例句：The spider spun its web in a cranny of the old building. 蜘蛛在旧建筑的缝隙里结网。|近义词：crevice, fissure, chink, nook |反义词：opening, expanse
