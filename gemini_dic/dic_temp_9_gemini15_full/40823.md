convoying: /kənˈvɔɪɪŋ/ | v. |护航；护送；组成车队| The act of escorting or accompanying, especially ships or vehicles, to protect them from attack or ensure their safe passage.  It implies a group movement for protection or support. |例句：The navy was convoying the merchant ships across the Atlantic.  海军护送商船横渡大西洋。

例句：A long line of trucks were convoying supplies to the disaster zone. 一长队卡车护送物资到灾区。

近义词：escorting, accompanying, shepherding, guarding

反义词：abandoning, deserting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The navy convoys merchant ships across the ocean. 海军护送商船横渡大洋。
They convoy supplies to the remote villages. 他们护送物资到偏远村庄。


一般过去时:
The ships convoyed together for safety.  船只为了安全而结伴而行。
The army convoyed the refugees to safety.  军队护送难民到安全地带。


现在完成时:
The government has convoyed aid to the affected areas. 政府已经护送援助物资到受灾地区。
We have convoyed many shipments safely. 我们已经安全护送了许多货物。


过去完成时:
The captain had convoyed many ships before the war. 船长在战争前护送过许多船只。
They had convoyed the supplies before the storm hit. 他们在暴风雨来临之前护送了物资。


将来时:
The fleet will convoy the aircraft carrier.舰队将护送航空母舰。
We will convoy the goods to the port. 我们将护送货物到港口。


虚拟语气:
If we had more ships, we could convoy the entire fleet. 如果我们有更多船只，我们就能护送整个舰队。
If they had convoyed the supplies earlier, the disaster would have been less severe. 如果他们早点护送物资，灾难就不会那么严重。


被动语态:
The merchant ships were convoyed by the navy. 商船由海军护送。
The refugees were convoyed to safety by the army. 难民由军队护送至安全地带。


疑问句:
Were the ships convoyed safely? 船只安全护送了吗？
Will the goods be convoyed by truck or train? 货物将由卡车还是火车护送？


否定句:
The government did not convoy the aid in time. 政府没有及时护送援助物资。
The supplies were not convoyed properly. 物资没有得到妥善的护送。

词源:
From Old French *convoi, from Medieval Latin *convoiare, from Latin con- ("together") + voiare ("to call, summon, wish").  The original sense was "to call together," evolving to the meaning of accompanying or escorting a group for protection.
