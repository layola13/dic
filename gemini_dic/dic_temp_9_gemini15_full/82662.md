guess:/ɡɛs/| v. n. |猜测，估计；认为 |To form an opinion or make a judgment without sufficient evidence; to suppose.  |I guess it will rain. 我猜会下雨。|近义词：conjecture, speculate, suppose |反义词：know, understand


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I guess the answer is correct. = 我猜答案是对的。
She guesses the meaning of the poem. = 她猜测这首诗的意思。

一般过去时:
He guessed the right number. = 他猜对了数字。
They guessed the ending of the story. = 他们猜到了故事的结尾。


现在完成时:
I have guessed the password several times. = 我已经猜过几次密码了。
She has guessed the riddle correctly. = 她已经正确地猜出了谜语。


过去完成时:
I had guessed that he was lying. = 我当时就猜到他在说谎。
They had guessed the thief's identity before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他们就已经猜到了小偷的身份。


将来时:
I will guess the winner of the game. = 我将猜测这场比赛的获胜者。
We will guess what's in the box. = 我们将猜猜盒子里是什么。


虚拟语气:
If I guessed correctly, I would win a prize. = 如果我猜对了，我会赢得奖品。
I wish I had guessed the lottery numbers. = 我真希望我猜中了彩票号码。


被动语态:
The answer was guessed by many people. = 许多人都猜出了答案。
The murderer's identity has been guessed by the detectives. = 侦探们已经猜出了凶手的身份。


疑问句:
Can you guess what I'm thinking? = 你能猜到我在想什么吗？
Do you guess who will win the election? = 你猜谁会赢得选举？


否定句:
I cannot guess the solution to this problem. = 我猜不出这个问题的答案。
She didn't guess the correct answer. = 她没有猜对答案。


PHRASE->
take a guess: 随便猜猜
wild guess: 瞎猜
educated guess: 有根据的猜测
a good guess: 一个不错的猜测
rough guess: 大概的猜测
