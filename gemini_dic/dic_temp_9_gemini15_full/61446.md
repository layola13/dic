embossments:/ɪmˈbɒsmənts/| n. | 浮雕；凸印；压花| Raised designs or patterns on a surface, created by pressing or stamping.  Embossments are often found on leather goods, stationery, and metalwork. | The leather book cover featured intricate embossments of flowers and vines.  这本皮革书皮上有精美的花藤浮雕图案。

The ancient coins bore beautiful embossments of mythical creatures.  这些古代钱币上有美丽的虚构生物浮雕图案。| raised designs, reliefs | flat surfaces |  n.
