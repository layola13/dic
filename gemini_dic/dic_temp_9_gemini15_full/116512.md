matey:/ˈmeɪti/| adj. n.|伙伴的；朋友的；海员的；|Friendly and approachable; a familiar term of address, especially among sailors.|例句：He's a right matey, always ready to lend a hand. 他是个好伙伴，总是乐于助人。  例句：The old matey spun a yarn about his adventures at sea.  这位老水手讲述了他海上探险的故事。|近义词：pal, buddy, friend, comrade|反义词：enemy, foe
