confabs:/ˈkɒnfæbz/| n. |非正式会谈；秘密会晤|Informal talks or meetings, often secret or private.  The word often implies a discussion of a significant or sensitive matter.|例句：The diplomats held several confabs before reaching an agreement.  外交官们在达成协议之前进行了多次秘密会谈。

例句：The two CEOs engaged in a series of confabs to discuss a potential merger. 两位首席执行官进行了一系列会谈以讨论潜在的合并。


近义词：meeting, discussion, conference, powwow, consultation

反义词：monologue, solo performance


词性：名词
