diapers:/ˈdaɪpərz/ | n. |尿布|Diapers are absorbent pads worn by babies and toddlers to catch urine and feces.  They are usually disposable but can also be reusable cloth diapers.|Example:  The baby needs a fresh diaper.  = 宝宝需要换个新的尿布。|  Example:  We use cloth diapers for our baby. = 我们给宝宝用布尿布。|近义词: nappies (British English) |反义词: None
