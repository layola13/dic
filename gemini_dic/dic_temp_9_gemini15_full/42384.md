counterblockaded:/ˌkaʊntərblɒˈkeɪdəd/ | adj. | 反封锁的；对反封锁的 |  Describes a situation where a blockade is countered or resisted. It implies an active effort to overcome or circumvent an existing blockade.  | Example: The port, though initially counterblockaded, eventually fell under siege.  这个港口虽然最初进行了反封锁，但最终还是被围困了。 | 近义词:  countered, resisted, defied | 反义词: blockaded, besieged,  surrounded
