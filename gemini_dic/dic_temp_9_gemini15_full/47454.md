deal-in:/diːlˈɪn/| v.|经营，买卖，从事|To engage in the business of buying and selling something; to participate in a particular type of activity or business.|例句：The company deals in rare books. 这家公司经营珍本书。|例句：He deals in antiques. 他经营古董。|近义词：trade in, traffic in, handle|反义词：discontinue, cease


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They deal in organic produce. = 他们经营有机农产品。
This shop deals in handmade crafts. = 这家商店经营手工制品。

一般过去时:
The store dealt in vintage clothing. = 这家商店过去经营复古服装。

过去完成时:
