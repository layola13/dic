proplastic:/ˌproʊˈplæstɪk/| adj.|前质体的；可塑的，易成型的|  Relating to or denoting a substance in a precursor stage before becoming plastic.  It often refers to a material that can be easily molded or shaped.| Example:  The proplastic material was easily formed into the desired shape.  前质体材料很容易被塑造成所需形状。 |近义词：malleable, ductile, pliable |反义词：rigid, inflexible, brittle
