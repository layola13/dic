misstruck: /ˌmɪsˈstrʌk/ | adj. | 错误敲击的；击打错误的 |  Describes something that has been struck or hit incorrectly, often referring to a coin or a type key.  This usually implies an accidental or unintended error in the striking process. |

例句：

The coin was misstruck, resulting in a blurred image. 这枚硬币敲击错误，导致图像模糊。

The misstruck key on the typewriter made the letter look strange. 打字机上的错误敲击键使字母看起来很奇怪。


近义词：faulty, flawed, imperfect, incorrectly struck

反义词：correctly struck, accurately struck


词性：形容词
