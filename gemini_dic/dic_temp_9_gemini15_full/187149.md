roaded:/ˈroʊdəd/ | adj. | 道路阻塞的；充满道路的 |  Covered or filled with roads;  characterized by many roads.  |例句：The area is heavily roaded, making navigation difficult. 这个地区道路密布，使得导航困难。
The roaded landscape offered little natural beauty.  这片布满道路的景观几乎没有自然美景。|近义词：networked, gridded | 反义词：unroaded, undeveloped
