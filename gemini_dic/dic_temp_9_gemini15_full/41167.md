copycat:/ˈkɑːpikæt/| n.| 亦步亦趋者；模仿者；抄袭者 | A person who imitates another person's behavior or work, especially in a way that is unoriginal or annoying.  |例句：He's such a copycat; he always copies my homework. 他是个十足的模仿者，总是抄我的作业。|例句：The band was accused of being a copycat of a popular rock group.  乐队被指控模仿一个流行摇滚乐队。|近义词：imitator, imitative, plagiarist, follower|反义词：original, innovator, pioneer|名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a copycat. = 他是个模仿者。
She copies her sister's style. = 她模仿她姐姐的风格。

一般过去时:
He was a copycat in school. = 他上学时是个模仿者。
She copied his design. = 她抄袭了他的设计。

现在完成时:
He has been a copycat all his life. = 他一辈子都是个模仿者。
She has copied several artists' styles. = 她模仿过好几位艺术家的风格。

过去完成时:
He had been a copycat before he found his own style. = 在他找到自己的风格之前，他一直是个模仿者。
She had copied that design before. = 她以前抄袭过那个设计。

将来时:
He will be a copycat if he doesn't develop his own ideas. = 如果他不发展自己的想法，他将继续是个模仿者。
She will copy the trend next season. = 下个季度她会模仿这个潮流。

虚拟语气:
If he were not a copycat, he would be more creative. = 如果他不是个模仿者，他会更有创造力。
I wish she hadn't copied my work. = 我真希望她没有抄袭我的作品。

被动语态:
His work was copied by many students. = 他的作品被许多学生抄袭。
The design was copied from a famous brand. = 这个设计抄袭自一个著名品牌。

疑问句:
Is he a copycat? = 他是个模仿者吗？
Did she copy my idea? = 她抄袭了我的主意吗？

否定句:
He is not a copycat. = 他不是个模仿者。
She did not copy the answer. = 她没有抄答案。
