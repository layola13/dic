multilinear:/ˌmʌltiˈlɪniər/| adj.|多线的；多重线性的；多线性|Relating to or involving multiple lines or variables.  It often describes mathematical functions or transformations that are linear in each variable separately, but not necessarily linear in all variables simultaneously.  The term is frequently used in algebra, particularly in the context of multilinear algebra and tensor analysis.|例句：The multilinear map transforms vectors into a scalar.  这个多线性映射将向量变换成标量。|例句：Multilinear algebra provides a framework for understanding tensors.  多线性代数提供了一个理解张量的框架。|近义词：multivariate, polylinear|反义词：linear (in the sense of single-variable linearity)
