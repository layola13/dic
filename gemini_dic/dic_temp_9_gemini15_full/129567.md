non-challenging: /ˌnɒnˈtʃælɪndʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 不具挑战性的，容易的 | Not presenting a challenge; easy. | 例句：The crossword puzzle was non-challenging for her.  =  这个纵横字谜对她来说很简单。| 例句：He found the job non-challenging and became bored. = 他觉得这份工作毫无挑战性，变得厌倦了。| 近义词：easy, simple, undemanding, effortless | 反义词：challenging, difficult, demanding, strenuous |
