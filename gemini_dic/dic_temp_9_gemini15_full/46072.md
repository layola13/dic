cyclones:/ˈsaɪkloʊnz/| n.|气旋，飓风|A cyclone is a system of winds rotating inward to an area of low atmospheric pressure, typically accompanied by stormy weather.|例句：The cyclone caused widespread flooding and devastation.  气旋造成了大范围的洪水和破坏。|例句：The intensity of the cyclone increased rapidly. 气旋的强度迅速增强。|近义词：hurricane, typhoon, tornado, whirlwind|反义词：anticyclone
