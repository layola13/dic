saltines:/ˈsɔːlˌtiːnz/| n. |咸饼干|Thin, crisp crackers, typically square-shaped and salted.  They are often served with soup or cheese. |例句：I like to eat saltines with my tomato soup. 我喜欢用番茄汤配咸饼干。

例句：We bought a box of saltines for the picnic. 我们为野餐买了一盒咸饼干。


近义词：crackers (饼干),  soda crackers (苏打饼干)


反义词：None (没有直接的反义词)


词源：The name "saltines" derives from the fact that they are salty crackers. The suffix "-ines" is a diminutive suffix, suggesting small size.  The exact origin of the term is difficult to pinpoint precisely, but its usage reflects the cracker's characteristic saltiness and small size.
