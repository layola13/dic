leaflet: /ˈliːflət/ | n. | 传单，小册子 | A leaflet is a single sheet of printed paper, often folded, that gives information or advertises something.  |  I picked up a leaflet about the local museum. 我拿起了一张关于当地博物馆的传单。  The company distributed leaflets advertising their new product. 公司散发传单宣传他们的新产品。| brochure, pamphlet, flyer | poster | noun
