profiting:/ˈprɒfɪtɪŋ/|/ˈprɑːfɪtɪŋ/| v.|获利，受益|Gaining advantage or benefit from something.|The company is profiting from the new technology.  这家公司正在从这项新技术中获利。|He's profiting from his hard work. 他正在从他的努力工作中获益。|gaining, benefiting|losing, suffering


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are profiting from the new market. = 他们正在从新的市场中获利。
The company profits from its efficient operations. = 这家公司因其高效的运营而获利。


一般过去时:
We profited greatly from that investment. = 我们从那项投资中获益良多。
The farmers profited from the good harvest. = 农民们从丰收中获益。


现在完成时:
They have profited immensely from their hard work. = 他们从辛勤工作中获得了巨大的收益。
She has profited significantly from the course. = 她从这门课程中获益匪浅。


过去完成时:
They had profited handsomely before the market crashed. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经赚得盆满钵满。
The investors had profited considerably before selling their shares. = 投资者在出售股票之前已经获得了可观的利润。


将来时:
We will profit from this experience. = 我们将从这次经历中获益。
The company will profit greatly from the new marketing strategy. = 公司将从新的营销策略中获益良多。


虚拟语气:
If we had invested earlier, we would have profited more. = 如果我们早点投资，我们会赚更多。
If they had worked harder, they would have profited more. = 如果他们工作更努力，他们会获利更多。


被动语态:
Little profit was made from the venture. = 这项投资几乎没有盈利。
A considerable profit was gained from the sale of the house. = 出售房屋获得了可观的利润。


疑问句:
Are you profiting from this project? = 你从这个项目中获利了吗？
Is the company profiting from the new product? = 公司从新产品中获利了吗？


否定句:
The business isn't profiting as much as it should. = 这家生意没有赚到它应该赚的那么多。
They did not profit from their risky investment. = 他们没有从他们冒险的投资中获利。


PHRASE->
profit from = 从…获益
profit margin = 利润率
profit sharing = 利润分享
net profit = 净利润
profit and loss statement = 损益表
