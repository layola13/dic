insertional:/ˌɪnˈsɜːrʃənəl/ | adj. | 插入的；嵌入的 | Relating to or involving the insertion of something.  The adjective describes something that is inserted or the process of insertion. | Example: The insertional mutation caused a significant change in the protein structure.  插入突变导致蛋白质结构发生了显著变化。  The insertional inactivation of the gene rendered the bacteria harmless. 基因的插入失活使细菌无害。 | 近义词：inserted, implanted, introduced | 反义词：removed, deleted, extracted
