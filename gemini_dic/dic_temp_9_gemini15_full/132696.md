nondissipative: /ˌnɒnˈdɪsɪpeɪtɪv/ | adj. | 非耗散的；无耗散的 | Not dissipating energy; conserving energy.  Describes a system or process that does not lose energy to its surroundings in the form of heat or other irreversible processes.  | Example:  The nondissipative nature of the superconducting circuit allowed for the persistence of current.  非耗散的超导电路特性使得电流能够持续存在。


近义词: conservative, lossless

反义词: dissipative, lossy

词性: adjective
