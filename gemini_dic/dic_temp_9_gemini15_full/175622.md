ranked:/ræŋkt/ | adj. v. | 排名的；等级的；排列 | Ranked describes something that has been placed in a particular order or position, usually according to importance or merit.  It can also refer to the act of placing something in such an order. | 例句：The university is ranked among the top ten in the country. 这所大学排名全国前十。

The athletes were ranked according to their scores. 运动员们根据他们的分数排名。|近义词：rated, ordered, classified, graded |反义词：unordered, unranked, random


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ranks highly in his field. = 他在他的领域排名很高。
The company ranks its employees annually. = 这家公司每年对员工进行排名。


一般过去时:
The team ranked first in the competition. = 该队在比赛中获得第一名。
She ranked the movies from best to worst. = 她将电影从最好到最坏进行了排名。


现在完成时:
They have ranked consistently high in the surveys. = 他们在调查中一直排名很高。
The restaurant has ranked among the top five for years. = 这家餐馆多年来一直名列前五。


过去完成时:
The book had ranked number one on the bestseller list for months. = 这本书在畅销书排行榜上排名第一已经持续了几个月。
Before the new rankings, they had ranked lower. = 在新的排名之前，他们的排名较低。


将来时:
The committee will rank the proposals next week. = 委员会下周将对这些提案进行排名。
It is likely that she will rank among the top three. = 她很可能进入前三名。


虚拟语气:
If he ranked higher, he would get more opportunities. = 如果他的排名更高，他将获得更多机会。
I wish I had ranked better in the exam. = 我希望我在考试中排名更好。


被动语态:
The candidates were ranked by a panel of judges. = 候选人由评审团进行排名。
The results will be ranked according to the criteria provided. = 结果将根据提供的标准进行排名。


疑问句:
How does this school rank nationally? = 这所学校在全国排名如何？
Where does your team rank in the league? = 你的球队在联赛中排名多少？


否定句:
The project didn't rank highly in the funding applications. = 该项目在资金申请中的排名不高。
He wasn't ranked among the top performers. = 他没有被列入顶级表演者之列。
