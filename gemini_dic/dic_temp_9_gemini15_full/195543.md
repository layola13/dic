semiperspicuous:/ˌsɛmi pərˈspɪkjuəs/| adj. |半透明的，略微透明的 |Slightly transparent; allowing some light to pass through but not completely clear.  The semiperspicuous fabric allowed a soft glow to filter through.  |近义词：translucent, diaphanous |反义词：opaque, impermeable |


例句：

The semiperspicuous curtains diffused the harsh sunlight.
半透明的窗帘使刺眼的阳光变得柔和。

The semiperspicuous glass allowed us to see the silhouettes of the people inside.
半透明的玻璃让我们看到里面人的轮廓。


词源:

* **semi-**:  Latin prefix meaning "half," "partially," or "partly."
* **perspicuous**: Latin "perspicuus," from "perspicere," meaning "to see through," "to discern clearly."


用法说明:

semiperspicuous主要用作形容词，描述物质的透明度介于完全透明和完全不透明之间。它强调的是部分透明的性质，光线可以透过，但不能完全清晰地看到物体。


注意：由于此词较为少见，例句主要依靠推断和上下文，实际使用中需根据具体语境选择更合适的词语。
