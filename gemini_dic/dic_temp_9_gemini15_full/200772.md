sley:/sleɪ/| n.|综纱机；综框|A sley is the part of a loom that holds the heddles, which are used to lift and lower the warp threads to create the shed through which the weft is passed.  It's an essential component in weaving.|例句：The weaver carefully adjusted the sley to ensure even tension on the warp threads. 织工仔细调整综纱机以确保经纱张力均匀。|近义词：heddle frame (部分近义)|反义词：无|名词
