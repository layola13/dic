rediscovering: /ˌriːdɪˈskʌvərɪŋ/ | v. |重新发现；再次发现 | The act of discovering something again, or finding something that was previously unknown or forgotten.  It implies a renewed or refreshed understanding or appreciation. |例句：He rediscovered his love for painting after a long break. 他在长时间的休息后重新燃起了对绘画的热爱。


She is rediscovering her passion for music. 她正在重新发现她对音乐的热情。


近义词：re-exploring,  re-examining, unearthing


反义词：losing, forgetting, abandoning


词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are rediscovering old family recipes. = 他们正在重新发现古老的家庭食谱。
We rediscover the beauty of nature every time we go hiking. = 我们每次徒步旅行时都会重新发现大自然的美丽。

一般过去时:
I rediscovered my old diary yesterday. = 我昨天重新发现了我的旧日记。
She rediscovered her childhood home after many years. = 很多年后，她重新发现了她童年的家。

现在完成时:
He has rediscovered his artistic talent. = 他重新发现了他的艺术天赋。
They have rediscovered the joy of playing together. = 他们重新发现了一起玩耍的乐趣。

过去完成时:
By the end of the trip, they had rediscovered their love for each other. = 到旅行结束时，他们已经重新发现了彼此的爱。
She had rediscovered her passion for writing before she started the novel. = 在她开始写小说之前，她已经重新发现了她对写作的热情。

将来时:
I will rediscover the city's hidden gems on my next visit. = 我下次来访时将重新发现这座城市隐藏的珍宝。
We hope to rediscover the lost art of making traditional bread.  = 我们希望重新发现制作传统面包的失传技艺。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rediscover my love for reading. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新燃起我对阅读的热爱。
I wish I could rediscover the feeling of being a child. = 我真希望我能重新找回儿时的感觉。

被动语态:
The lost city was rediscovered by a team of archaeologists. = 这个失落的城市被一个考古学家团队重新发现。
The ancient manuscript was rediscovered in a dusty attic. = 这份古老的手稿在一个布满灰尘的阁楼里被重新发现。

疑问句:
Will they rediscover the sunken treasure? = 他们会重新发现沉船宝藏吗？
Have you rediscovered any old photos lately? = 你最近重新发现了什么旧照片吗？

否定句:
He hasn't rediscovered his passion for sports. = 他还没有重新燃起他对运动的热情。
They didn't rediscover the missing document. = 他们没有重新找到丢失的文件。
