inspectable:/ɪnˈspektəbl/| adj.| 可检查的，可审查的 | Capable of being inspected; subject to inspection.  Something that is inspectable can be examined or reviewed. |例句：The factory is inspectable by government officials at any time.  政府官员可以随时检查这家工厂。

例句：The documents are inspectable to ensure compliance with regulations.  这些文件是可以检查的，以确保符合规定。


近义词：examinable, reviewable, checkable

反义词：uninspected, unexaminable, uncheckable

词性：形容词
