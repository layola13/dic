dagging:/ˈdæɡɪŋ/ | n. | 粗糙的编织物；粗糙的毛线；（羊毛）打结；去除羊毛上的杂物 | The process of removing burrs, seeds, and other foreign matter from wool fleece.  It can also refer to the resulting tangled mass of wool. | 例句：The farmer spent hours dagging the sheep's fleece before spinning it into yarn.  = 农夫在把羊毛纺成纱线之前花了几个小时清理羊毛上的杂物。


The dagging process is crucial for producing high-quality yarn. = 清理羊毛的过程对于生产高质量的纱线至关重要。
