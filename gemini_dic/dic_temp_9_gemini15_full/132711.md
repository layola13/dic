nondistractingly: /ˌnɒn.dɪˈstræktɪŋli/ | adv. | 不分心；不使人分心 |  In a manner that does not cause distraction.  This adverb describes an action or state that avoids causing distraction or disturbance. It emphasizes the absence of anything that might divert attention. |

例句：

He explained the complex concept nondistractingly, ensuring everyone understood. 他不使人分心，解释了这个复杂的概念，确保每个人都理解。


The teacher presented the material nondistractingly, maintaining the students' focus. 老师不使人分心，讲解了材料，保持了学生的注意力。


近义词：calmly, quietly, peacefully, unobtrusively, serenely


反义词：distractingly, disruptively, disturbingly


词源:

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "without"
* **distract**: verb meaning "to divert attention" (from Latin *di-*, "apart" + *trahere*, "to draw")
* **-ing**: present participle suffix, forming an adjective here ("distracting")
* **-ly**: adverbial suffix, turning the adjective into an adverb ("distractingly")
