masculinized:/ˈmæskjʊlaɪzd/| adj.|男性化的；具有男子气概的|Made to seem more masculine or to have characteristics traditionally associated with men.|The film's portrayal of the female lead was heavily masculinized, stripping her of her vulnerability and independence.|近义词：manly, virile, macho|反义词：feminized, effeminate
