responsib: /rɪˈspɒnsɪbəl/ | adj. | 负责任的；可靠的 | Responsible means being accountable for one's actions and obligations.  It implies a duty to care for something or someone. |  He is a responsible father and always puts his children first. 他是一位负责任的父亲，总是把他的孩子放在第一位。 She is responsible for managing the team. 她负责管理团队。 | reliable, dependable, accountable, trustworthy | irresponsible, unreliable, negligent, careless | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is responsible for the project. 他对这个项目负责。
She is responsible for her own actions. 她对自己行为负责。

一般过去时:
He was responsible for the accident. 他对事故负责。
She was responsible for organizing the event. 她负责组织这次活动。

现在完成时:
He has been responsible for many successful projects. 他已经负责过许多成功的项目。
She has been responsible for the department for five years. 她已经负责这个部门五年了。

过去完成时:
He had been responsible for the company before the merger. 并购之前他一直负责这家公司。
She had been responsible for training new employees. 她一直负责培训新员工。

将来时:
He will be responsible for the new branch. 他将负责新分公司。
She will be responsible for overseeing the project. 她将负责监督这个项目。

虚拟语气:
If he were responsible, he would have apologized. 如果他负责任的话，他会道歉的。
I wish she were more responsible with her work. 我希望她对她的工作更负责一些。

被动语态:
The team was held responsible for the delay. 团队被认为对延误负责。
He was deemed responsible for the damage. 他被认为对损坏负责。

疑问句:
Who is responsible for this mess? 谁应该为这烂摊子负责？
Is he responsible enough for the job? 他胜任这份工作吗？

否定句:
He is not responsible for the mistake. 他对这个错误不负责任。
She was not responsible for the accident. 她对事故不负责任。
