The Seychellois people are known for their friendliness. = 塞舌尔人民以友好著称。|近义词：Seychelles (referring to the country), Islander|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be applied depending on the context (e.g., if discussing nationality, antonyms would be relating to other nationalities).|形容词，名词
