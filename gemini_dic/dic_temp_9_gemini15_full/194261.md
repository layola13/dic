self-neglecting: /ˈsɛlf nɪɡˈlɛktɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我忽视的，疏于照顾自己的 |  Describes a person who fails to take care of their basic needs, such as hygiene, nutrition, or medical care. This can be due to various factors, including mental health issues, physical limitations, or social isolation.  |  The elderly woman was found living in self-neglecting conditions, with piles of trash and spoiled food surrounding her.  | 近义词：neglectful, uncaring, derelict | 反义词：self-caring, attentive, responsible |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is self-neglecting his health. = 他正在忽视自己的健康。
She is self-neglecting her personal hygiene. = 她正在忽视个人卫生。


一般过去时:
He was self-neglecting his responsibilities. = 他过去忽视自己的责任。
She was self-neglecting her appearance. = 她过去忽视自己的外表。


现在完成时:
He has been self-neglecting for months. = 他已经忽视自己好几个月了。
She has been self-neglecting her physical health. = 她一直忽视自己的身体健康。


过去完成时:
He had been self-neglecting before the intervention. = 在干预之前，他一直忽视自己。
She had been self-neglecting for years before anyone noticed. = 在别人注意到之前，她已经忽视自己很多年了。


将来时:
He will continue to be self-neglecting unless he gets help. = 除非他得到帮助，否则他会继续忽视自己。
She will likely become self-neglecting if her support system collapses. = 如果她的支持系统崩溃，她很可能会开始忽视自己。


虚拟语气:
If he were not self-neglecting, he would be much healthier. = 如果他不忽视自己，他会健康得多。
I wish she weren't so self-neglecting. = 我希望她不要那么忽视自己。


被动语态:
His needs were self-neglected for too long. = 他的需求被忽视了太久。
Her condition was self-neglected to a dangerous level. = 她的情况被忽视到了危险的程度。(Note:  While grammatically correct,  "self-neglected" in the passive voice is less common and often sounds awkward.  Active voice is preferred.)


疑问句:
Is he self-neglecting his medication? = 他是否忽视了自己的服药？
Is she self-neglecting her basic needs? = 她是否忽视了自己的基本需求？


否定句:
He is not self-neglecting; he is simply overwhelmed. = 他并没有忽视自己；他只是不堪重负。
She is not self-neglecting, she has a medical condition that limits her abilities. = 她并没有忽视自己，她只是患有某种疾病限制了她的能力。
