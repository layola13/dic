reinvigorators:/ˌriːɪnˈvɪɡəreɪtərz/| n. |复兴者；恢复活力者 |People or things that reinvigorate something;  agents or factors that restore energy, strength, or vitality.  |例句：The new management team were seen as reinvigorators, bringing fresh ideas and energy to the company. 新的管理团队被视为复兴者，他们为公司带来了新的想法和活力。|例句：The mountain air acted as reinvigorators for the weary hikers. 山中的空气让疲惫的徒步旅行者恢复了活力。|近义词：revitalizers, stimulators, energizers|反义词：depressants, drainers, debilitators|名词
