co-payment:/ˈkoʊpeɪmənt/| n. |共同支付；共同付款；自付费用 |A co-payment is a fixed amount you pay for covered healthcare services, like a doctor's visit, after you've paid your deductible.  It's usually a smaller amount than your coinsurance. |Example: My health insurance requires a $25 co-payment for each doctor's visit. 我的健康保险要求每次看病支付25美元的共同支付费用。

Example:  The co-payment for this prescription is $10. 这张处方药的共同支付费用是10美元。|近义词：copay, shared payment |反义词：full payment

词源:
The word "co-payment" is a compound word.  "Co-" is a prefix meaning "together" or "jointly," and "payment" refers to the act of paying money.  Therefore, a co-payment is a payment made jointly, often by both the patient and the insurance company.
