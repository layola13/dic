nonproblematic: /ˌnɒnˌprɒbləˈmætɪk/| adj. |无问题的；不复杂的；容易解决的 |Not causing or likely to cause problems; easily dealt with.  |例句：The project proceeded in a nonproblematic fashion. 项目进展顺利。

例句：The transition to the new system was surprisingly nonproblematic.  向新系统的过渡出乎意料地顺利。


近义词：easy, straightforward, uncomplicated, simple, hassle-free

反义词：problematic, difficult, complicated, complex, troublesome


词源:  The word is a combination of "non-" (meaning "not") and "problematic" (meaning "causing problems"). "Problematic" itself comes from the noun "problem"  + "-atic" (a suffix indicating a tendency or quality).  The ultimate roots lie in Old French "probleme" and Latin "problema," meaning "a question proposed for solution."
