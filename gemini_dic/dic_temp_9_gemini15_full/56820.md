dormancy:/ˈdɔːrmənsi/ | n. |休眠；蛰伏；潜伏期 | A state of inactivity or dormancy, especially in animals or plants.  It refers to a period in an organism's life cycle when growth, development, and physical activity are temporarily suspended. This state is often characterized by reduced metabolic rate and resistance to environmental stresses.  | Example: The seeds remained in a state of dormancy until the spring rains arrived.  种子一直处于休眠状态，直到春雨到来。


The plant entered dormancy during the winter months. 植物在冬季进入休眠期。|近义词：inactivity, latency, quiescence |反义词：activity, growth, flourishing

词源:
