pregnant:/ˈprɛɡnənt/| adj.|怀孕的，充满的|Being in a state of carrying a developing embryo or fetus in the womb;  also figuratively used to describe something full or abundant.|例句：She is pregnant with her first child. 她怀上了她的第一个孩子。  The air was pregnant with expectation. 空气中充满了期待。|近义词：expectant, gravid|反义词：barren, infertile
