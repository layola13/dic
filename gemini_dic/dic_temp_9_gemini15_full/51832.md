dewlap:/ˈduːlæp/| n.| 垂肉；(某些动物的)颈下垂皮|A dewlap is a fold of skin hanging from the neck or throat of certain animals, such as cattle, goats, and some lizards.  It can have various functions, including display, thermoregulation, and fat storage.|例句：The old bull had a large dewlap hanging down to his chest. 这头老公牛有一个很大的垂肉垂到胸前。

The lizard puffed out its dewlap to attract a mate.  这只蜥蜴鼓起它的垂肉来吸引配偶。|近义词：wattles, pendulous skin|反义词:|
