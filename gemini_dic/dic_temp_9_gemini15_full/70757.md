/ˈfaɪnɪsɪz/ | n. | 精细之处；细微之处；优良品质 | The finenesses of the craftsmanship were evident in every detail.  工艺的精细之处在每一个细节中都显而易见。 | 近义词：subtleties, refinements, niceties | 反义词：coarsenesses, crudenesses


The word "finenesses" is the plural form of "fineness,"  referring to multiple instances of delicacy, precision, or high quality.  It highlights the multiple aspects of something being refined, detailed, or superior.  The word implies a degree of intricacy and attention to detail that goes beyond mere quality.
