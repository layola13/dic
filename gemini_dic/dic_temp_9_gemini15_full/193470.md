selection:/səˈlɛkʃən/ | n. |选择，精选，选拔 |The act of selecting or the state of being selected; something that has been selected.|例句：The selection of a new president is a crucial process.  新总统的选举是一个至关重要的过程。|例句：This anthology contains a fine selection of poems. 这部选集包含了一些精选的诗歌。|近义词：choice, option, pick |反义词：rejection


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He makes a careful selection of his words. 他仔细选择他的措辞。
The committee makes the final selection of candidates. 委员会进行最终的候选人遴选。


一般过去时:
She made a poor selection of her outfit for the interview. 她为面试选择了一套不太合适的衣服。
The judges selected the best entries from the competition. 评委们从比赛中挑选出了最好的作品。


现在完成时:
They have already made their selection of gifts. 他们已经选好了礼物。
The company has completed the selection process for new employees. 公司已经完成了新员工的选拔过程。


过去完成时:
He had already made his selection before the others arrived. 在其他人到达之前，他已经做出了选择。
The team had completed the player selection before the season began. 球队在赛季开始前完成了球员选拔。


将来时:
We will make our selection after careful consideration. 我们会在仔细考虑之后做出选择。
The organization will announce the selection of the scholarship winners next week. 该组织将于下周公布奖学金获得者的名单。


虚拟语气:
If I had known better, I would have made a different selection. 如果我早知道，我会做出不同的选择。
I wish I had made a wiser selection of stocks. 我真希望我当初更明智地选择股票。


被动语态:
The winners were selected by a panel of judges. 获奖者是由评审团选出的。
The final selection will be announced tomorrow. 最终入选名单将于明天公布。


疑问句:
What is your selection for dessert? 你要选什么甜点？
What criteria were used for the selection of candidates? 选择候选人使用了什么标准？


否定句:
I didn't make a selection from the menu. 我没有从菜单上点菜。
The committee didn't make a final selection that day. 委员会那天没有做出最终的选择。
