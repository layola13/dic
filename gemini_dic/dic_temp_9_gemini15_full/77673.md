jelly:/ˈdʒɛli/ | n. |果冻，冻状物 |A sweet, usually fruit-flavored, dessert made with gelatin.  It is a soft, trembling mass. |例句：I love strawberry jelly on my toast.  我喜欢在我的吐司上涂草莓果冻。

例句：The artist used jelly to create a unique texture in his sculpture.艺术家用果冻在他的雕塑中创造了一种独特的纹理。


近义词：jellify,  gel | 反义词：solid, firm


词源:  From Middle English *gelee, from Old French gelee, from gelé, past participle of geler "to freeze," from Vulgar Latin *gelare, from Latin gelu "frost, ice."  The modern sense of a sweet, gelatinous dessert developed later.
