catharses:/kəˈθɑːrsɪs/| n.|卡塔西斯；宣泄；净化 |The purging of emotions or relieving of emotional tensions, especially through certain kinds of art, as tragedy or music.  A catharsis is a release of pent-up emotions. It's often associated with a feeling of relief or renewal.|例句：Watching the tragic play provided a catharsis for her grief.  观看这部悲剧让她悲伤的情绪得到了宣泄。  The intense workout was a catharsis after a stressful day. 剧烈的运动让她在压力重重的一天之后得到了身心净化。|近义词：purification, release, relief, expiation|反义词：suppression, repression, containment|名词
