hemoglobin:/ˌhiːməˈɡloʊbɪn/| n. |血红蛋白|A protein in red blood cells that carries oxygen from the lungs to the body's tissues and carbon dioxide from the tissues back to the lungs.|例句：Hemoglobin levels are often checked during routine blood tests.  血红蛋白水平通常在常规血液检查中进行检查。|例句：Low hemoglobin can lead to anemia. 低血红蛋白会导致贫血。|近义词：erythrocruorin|反义词：无

Hemoglobin carries oxygen throughout the body. = 血红蛋白将氧气输送到全身。
The test measures the level of hemoglobin in the blood. = 该测试测量血液中血红蛋白的水平。
