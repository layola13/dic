overmodify:/ˌoʊvərˈmɑːdɪfaɪ/| vt.|过度修饰，矫枉过正|To modify something excessively or to an inappropriate degree, often resulting in a negative effect.  It implies going beyond what is necessary or tasteful, resulting in something that is overly embellished or artificial.|例句：The editor overmodified the manuscript, losing its original charm. 编辑过度修改了手稿，使其失去了原有的魅力。
The excessive use of filters overmodified the photograph, making it look unnatural.过度使用滤镜过度修饰了照片，使其看起来不自然。|近义词：overdecorate, overembellish, overdo, overelaborate|反义词：understate, simplify, streamline
