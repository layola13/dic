downriver:/ˈdaʊnˌrɪvər/| adj. adv.|顺流而下的；向下游的|Going downriver is usually faster than going upriver. |顺流而下通常比逆流而上快。|upstream|upriver|形容词，副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The boat goes downriver quickly. = 船迅速顺流而下。
The current flows downriver. = 水流顺流而下。


一般过去时:
The raft went downriver swiftly. = 木筏迅速顺流而下。
They traveled downriver yesterday. = 他们昨天顺流而下旅行。


现在完成时:
The river has carried debris downriver. = 河流已将碎屑冲向下游。
They have floated downriver for many miles. = 他们已经顺流而下漂流了很多英里。


过去完成时:
The flood had already carried everything downriver. = 洪水已经把所有东西冲向下游了。
They had traveled downriver before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经顺流而下旅行了。


将来时:
We will travel downriver tomorrow. = 我们明天将顺流而下旅行。
The logs will float downriver in the spring. = 原木将在春天顺流而下漂浮。


虚拟语气:
If the river were flowing downriver, we would be able to go faster. = 如果河水顺流而下，我们就能走得更快。
I wish we were already downriver. = 我希望我们已经到达下游了。


被动语态:
The boat was carried downriver by the strong current. = 小船被强劲的水流冲向下游。
The debris is being carried downriver. = 残骸正在被冲向下游。


疑问句:
Does the river flow downriver here? = 这条河在这里是顺流而下的吗？
Are they going downriver? = 他们顺流而下吗？


否定句:
The boat didn't go downriver; it went upstream. = 船没有顺流而下，而是逆流而上。
They are not traveling downriver today. = 他们今天没有顺流而下旅行。
