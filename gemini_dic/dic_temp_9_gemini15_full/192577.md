sculpting: /ˈskʌlptɪŋ/ | n. v. | 雕塑；雕刻 |  The art or process of shaping figures or objects out of a material such as clay, stone, or metal.  |

例句：

The museum boasts a stunning collection of Renaissance sculpting.  = 博物馆拥有令人惊叹的文艺复兴时期雕塑收藏。

He is skilled in sculpting intricate details in wood. = 他擅长在木头上雕刻复杂的细节。


近义词：carving, modeling, molding

反义词：destroying, demolishing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sculpts beautiful figures from clay. = 他用黏土塑造美丽的雕像。
She sculpts every day in her studio. = 她每天都在她的工作室里雕塑。

一般过去时:
Michelangelo sculpted the David. = 米开朗基罗雕刻了大卫像。
They sculpted a magnificent eagle from ice. = 他们用冰雕刻了一只雄伟的鹰。

现在完成时:
She has sculpted numerous works throughout her career. = 她职业生涯中创作了无数作品。
The artist has sculpted a life-size portrait of the queen. = 艺术家雕刻了一尊女王真人大小的肖像。

过去完成时:
He had sculpted many pieces before he achieved recognition. = 在获得认可之前，他已经雕刻了许多作品。
They had sculpted the entire scene before the exhibition. = 展览之前，他们已经雕刻完成了整个场景。

将来时:
She will sculpt a new piece for the upcoming exhibition. = 她将为即将到来的展览创作一件新作品。
They will sculpt a monument to commemorate the event. = 他们将雕刻一座纪念碑来纪念这一事件。

虚拟语气:
If I had the skill, I would sculpt a masterpiece. = 如果我有这项技能，我会雕刻出一件杰作。
I wish I could sculpt as well as Michelangelo. = 我希望我能像米开朗基罗一样雕刻得好。

被动语态:
The statue was sculpted from marble. = 这尊雕像是用大理石雕刻的。
The sculpture is being sculpted by a renowned artist. = 这尊雕塑正在由一位著名的艺术家雕刻。

疑问句:
Who sculpted this amazing piece? = 谁雕刻了这件令人惊叹的作品？

否定句:
