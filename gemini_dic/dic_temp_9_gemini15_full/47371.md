deactivations:/ˌdiːæktɪˈveɪʃənz/| n.|去活化；失活；停用；关闭|The process of making something inactive or unusable.  It refers to the act of deactivating something, often multiple things.  This can apply to various contexts, from chemical reactions to electronic devices.|例句：The deactivations of the security systems allowed the intruders to access the building.  安全系统的停用使得入侵者能够进入大楼。

例句：The study focused on the deactivations of specific genes in cancer cells. 这项研究集中在癌细胞中特定基因的失活。


近义词：disabling, inactivation, shutdowns, suspensions|反义词：activations, enablements
