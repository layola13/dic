colognes:/koʊˈloʊnz/| n. |古龙水；科隆香水|A type of perfume, typically lighter and less concentrated than other perfumes.  Cologne is often used interchangeably with eau de cologne,  a diluted alcoholic solution of fragrant essential oils.|He sprayed on some cologne before going to the party.|近义词：perfume, eau de cologne, scent |反义词：None specifically.
