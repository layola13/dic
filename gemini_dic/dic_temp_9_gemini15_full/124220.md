mosotho:/məˈsoʊθoʊ/| n. |索托人；索托语|The word "mosotho" refers to a member of the Sotho people, a Bantu ethnic group primarily inhabiting Lesotho and parts of South Africa.  It also refers to the Sotho languages spoken by these people.  The term is often used in a broader sense to encompass various Sotho subgroups.|例句：Many Mosotho people are skilled artisans. 许多索托人都是熟练的工匠。 The Mosotho language is part of the Bantu family. 索托语属于班图语系。|近义词：Sotho (often used interchangeably)|反义词：无|名词
