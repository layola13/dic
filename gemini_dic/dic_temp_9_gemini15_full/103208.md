jetlag:/ˈdʒɛtˌlæɡ/| n. |时差反应|The fatigue and disorientation experienced after long-distance air travel, due to a disruption of the body's natural sleep-wake cycle.  It's caused by crossing multiple time zones rapidly.|例句：After flying from London to New York, I suffered from terrible jet lag for several days.  飞越伦敦到纽约后，我好几天都饱受时差反应的折磨。|近义词：time-zone fatigue, travel fatigue |反义词:  None  |名词
