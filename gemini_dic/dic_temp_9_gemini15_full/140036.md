oenologies:/ˌiːnəˈlɒdʒiz/| n. |酿酒学；葡萄酒学|The scientific study of wine and winemaking, encompassing all aspects from grape growing to fermentation and aging.  It includes viticulture (grape cultivation), vinification (winemaking), and the chemical and sensory analysis of wine.|例句：Oenology is a fascinating field that combines science, art, and history. 酿酒学是一个结合了科学、艺术和历史的迷人领域。

例句：She studied oenology at university and now works as a winemaker.  她在大学学习酿酒学，现在是一名酿酒师。

近义词：viticulture, viniculture, ampelography

反义词：无
