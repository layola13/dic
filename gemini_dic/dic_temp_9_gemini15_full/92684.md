iceberg:/ˈaɪsˌbɜːrɡ/| n. |冰山 |A large mass of ice floating in the sea, detached from a glacier or ice sheet.| 例句：The Titanic sank after hitting an iceberg.  泰坦尼克号撞上冰山后沉没了。
The iceberg was a massive, looming presence in the otherwise calm ocean. 这座冰山在平静的海面上显得巨大而逼近。|近义词：ice floe, glacier (partially submerged)|反义词：None specifically, but could consider terms implying warmth or lack of ice, such as "land," "beach."|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Icebergs float in the ocean. 冰山漂浮在海洋中。
The iceberg melts slowly in the sun. 冰山在阳光下慢慢融化。


一般过去时:
An iceberg drifted near the ship. 一座冰山漂浮在船附近。
The iceberg calved from the glacier. 冰山从冰川崩裂下来。


现在完成时:
Several icebergs have been sighted this year. 今年已经发现了几个冰山。
The warming climate has caused many icebergs to melt. 变暖的气候导致许多冰山融化。


过去完成时:
The ship had already struck the iceberg before the alarm sounded. 警报响起之前，船已经撞上了冰山。
They had never seen such a large iceberg before. 他们以前从未见过这么大的冰山。


将来时:
More icebergs will likely break off from the glaciers.  更多的冰山可能会从冰川上崩裂下来。
Scientists predict that the number of icebergs will increase. 科学家预测冰山数量将会增加。


虚拟语气:
If the iceberg had been smaller, the ship would have survived. 如果冰山更小一些，船就能幸存下来。
I wish the iceberg hadn't been in the ship's path. 我希望冰山没有挡在船的航线上。


被动语态:
The iceberg was spotted by the crew. 冰山被船员发现了。
The iceberg is being monitored by scientists. 冰山正在被科学家们监测。


疑问句:
Is that an iceberg? 那是冰山吗？
How large is the iceberg? 冰山有多大？


否定句:
