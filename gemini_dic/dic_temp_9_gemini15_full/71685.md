flaunting: /ˈflɔːntɪŋ/ | v. |炫耀，夸示 | To display ostentatiously or boastfully.  |例句：He was flaunting his wealth by driving a expensive car. 他开着豪车炫耀自己的财富。

例句：She flaunted her new diamond ring in front of her friends. 她在她朋友面前炫耀她新的钻戒。


近义词：showing off, boasting, parading, displaying


反义词：hiding, concealing, obscuring


词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flaunts his success to everyone he meets. = 他向遇到的每一个人炫耀他的成功。
They flaunt their wealth with expensive clothes and cars. = 他们穿着昂贵的衣服和豪车来炫耀他们的财富。


一般过去时:
She flaunted her knowledge of art history at the party. = 她在派对上炫耀她对艺术史的了解。
He flaunted his new girlfriend in front of his ex. = 他在他前女友面前炫耀他的新女朋友。


现在完成时:
They have flaunted their power for too long. = 他们炫耀权力太久了。
She has flaunted her achievements throughout her career. = 她在整个职业生涯中都炫耀过她的成就。


过去完成时:
He had flaunted his wealth before his bankruptcy. = 他破产前曾炫耀过他的财富。
They had flaunted their connections to get ahead. = 他们曾炫耀过他们的人脉来获得成功。


将来时:
She will flaunt her new design at the fashion show. = 她将在时装秀上炫耀她的新设计。
He will flaunt his skills in the upcoming competition. = 他将在即将到来的比赛中炫耀他的技能。


虚拟语气:
If he flaunted his abilities more, he would get promoted. = 如果他更炫耀自己的能力，他就会被提升。
I wish I hadn't flaunted my success so much. = 我真希望我没有那么炫耀我的成功。


被动语态:
His wealth was flaunted by him constantly. = 他的财富被他不断炫耀。
Her talents were flaunted to impress the judges. = 她的才能被炫耀以打动评委。


疑问句:
Is he flaunting his new car? = 他在炫耀他的新车吗？
Are they flaunting their relationship? = 他们在炫耀他们的关系吗？


否定句:
He didn't flaunt his connections to get the job. = 他没有炫耀他的人脉来得到这份工作。
She doesn't flaunt her intelligence; she lets her actions speak for themselves. = 她不炫耀她的智慧；她让她的行动说明一切。


PHRASE->
flaunt one's wealth = 炫耀财富
flaunt one's power = 炫耀权力
flaunt one's knowledge = 炫耀知识
flaunt one's achievements = 炫耀成就
flaunt one's beauty = 炫耀美貌

词源:
源自中古英语 *flaunten，意为“挥舞，飘扬”。  最终来源于古法语 *flaunter，与古法语 *flanc（侧翼）有关，暗示着一种张扬、显眼的方式。
