geometrid:/dʒiˈɑːmɪtrɪd/| n. |尺蛾科昆虫；尺蛾|A geometrid is a moth belonging to the family Geometridae.  These moths are characterized by their slender bodies and often brightly colored wings. The caterpillars, known as inchworms or loopers, move in a characteristic looping motion. |Example: The researcher collected several geometrid specimens for study.  研究人员收集了几只尺蛾标本用于研究。

The larvae of many geometrid species are important defoliators of trees and shrubs. 许多尺蛾物种的幼虫是树木和灌木的重要落叶昆虫。|近义词：inchworm moth (非正式)|反义词：无
