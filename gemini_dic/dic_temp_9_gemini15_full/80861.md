gourmet:/ˈɡʊrmeɪ/| adj. n.|美食家；美味佳肴的；讲究美食的|  A gourmet is a person who enjoys and is knowledgeable about good food and drink.  It can also describe food and drink that is of high quality and prepared with skill. |例句：He is a gourmet who appreciates fine wines. 他是一位懂得欣赏好酒的美食家。

例句：This restaurant offers a gourmet menu with dishes from around the world. 这家餐厅提供一份包含世界各地菜肴的美食菜单。

近义词：epicure, connoisseur, gastronome

反义词：unrefined, commonplace
