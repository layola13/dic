gossipped:/ˈɡɒsɪpt/ /ˈɡɑːsɪpt/| v.|说闲话；传播小道消息|To talk about other people's private lives, often in a way that is unkind or untrue. |例句：They were gossipped about their relationship. 他们之间的关系被人们议论纷纷。

例句：She gossiped with her friends about the latest celebrity scandal. 她和朋友们八卦着最新的名人丑闻。


近义词：whispered, chattered, whispered,rumored


反义词：kept silent, remained discreet


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They gossip about their neighbors. = 他们说邻居的闲话。
She gossips constantly. = 她不停地说闲话。


一般过去时:
We gossiped about the party last night. = 我们昨晚八卦了昨晚的派对。
They gossiped behind her back. = 他们在她背后说她的坏话。


现在完成时:
I have gossiped too much today. = 我今天说太多闲话了。
They have gossiped about this for weeks. = 他们已经八卦这件事几周了。


过去完成时:
She had gossiped with her friends before the meeting. = 她在会议前和朋友们聊过八卦。
They had gossiped about the situation for months. = 几个月来他们一直在议论这个情况。


将来时:
I will gossip with my friends after work. = 我下班后会和朋友们聊八卦。
They will gossip about the new teacher. = 他们会八卦新老师。


虚拟语气:

I wish I hadn't gossiped about her. = 我真希望我没有说她的坏话。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
