disapprovals:/ˌdɪs.əˈpruː.vəl/| n. |反对；不赞成；不认可|The expression of disapproval; a feeling of not approving of something.  It often refers to a collection of negative opinions or objections.|例句：His radical ideas met with widespread disapprovals.他的激进想法遭到了广泛的反对。  The plan received several disapprovals from the board members.该计划受到了董事会成员的多方反对。|近义词：objections, condemnations, censures|反义词：approvals, acceptances
