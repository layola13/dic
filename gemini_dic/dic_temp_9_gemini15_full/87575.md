heteroplastic:/ˌhɛtərəˈplæstɪk/| adj.|异质的；异型发生的；异源的| Relating to or denoting the growth of tissue or an organ from a different kind of tissue or organ;  characterized by the formation of tissues or structures different from the normal type.  It often implies a transplantation or grafting from a different source.| Example: The heteroplastic transplant failed due to immune rejection.  异体移植由于免疫排斥而失败。|近义词：alloplastic, heterogenous|反义词：homoplastic, homogenous
