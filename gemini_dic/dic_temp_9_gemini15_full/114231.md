mail-order:/ˈmeɪl ˈɔːrdər/ | n. adj. |邮购，邮购的 | The system of buying goods from a catalog and having them delivered by mail.  |  I ordered a new computer through mail order. 我通过邮购订购了一台新电脑。 | online ordering, catalog sales | in-store purchase | 名词，形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mail-order their groceries every week. = 他们每周邮购杂货。
She mail-orders books from that online bookstore. = 她从那家网上书店邮购书籍。


一般过去时:
We mail-ordered a new sofa last month. = 我们上个月邮购了一张新沙发。
He mail-ordered a set of tools from a catalog. = 他从目录中邮购了一套工具。


现在完成时:
I have mail-ordered several items from that company. = 我已经从那家公司邮购了几件商品。
They have mail-ordered all their Christmas presents this year. = 今年他们所有的圣诞礼物都是邮购的。


过去完成时:
She had mail-ordered the dress before the party. = 派对之前她就已邮购了那件裙子。
We had mail-ordered the furniture a month before we moved in. = 我们在搬进去一个月前就邮购了家具。


将来时:
I will mail-order a new printer next week. = 我下周将邮购一台新打印机。
They will mail-order the supplies for their project. = 他们将邮购他们项目所需的用品。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would mail-order that expensive camera. = 如果我有更多钱，我会邮购那台昂贵的相机。
If she had known about the sale, she would have mail-ordered the shoes. = 如果她知道促销活动，她就会邮购那双鞋子了。


被动语态:
The books were mail-ordered from a specialized website. = 这些书是从一个专门的网站上邮购的。
The parts for the machine are being mail-ordered. = 机器零件正在邮购中。


疑问句:
Did you mail-order that dress? = 你是邮购那件裙子吗？
Have they ever mail-ordered anything before? = 他们以前有没有邮购过任何东西？


否定句:
I didn't mail-order the shoes; I bought them in the store. = 我没有邮购鞋子；我是在商店买的。
They don't usually mail-order clothes. = 他们通常不邮购衣服。
