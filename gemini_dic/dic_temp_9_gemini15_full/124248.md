most: /moʊst/ | adj., pron., adv. | 最多的；大多数；最；极度 |  The word "most" functions as an adjective, pronoun, and adverb, depending on its usage. As an adjective, it means "greatest in amount, quantity, measure, degree, or extent."  As a pronoun, it refers to the greatest number or amount. As an adverb, it modifies an adjective or another adverb, meaning "to the greatest extent."


例句：

This is the most expensive car I've ever seen. = 这是我见过的最贵的车。

Most people agree that this is a good idea. = 大多数人都认为这是一个好主意。


近义词：greatest, majority, extremely, very


反义词：least, fewest, minimally, slightly


词性:  adj., pron., adv.
