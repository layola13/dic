non-receipt: /ˌnɒn rɪˈsiːpt/ | n. | 未收到；未收到通知 | The absence of receipt; failure to receive something expected or due.  This term often implies a lack of confirmation or acknowledgment of something, such as a payment, delivery, or communication. |例句：The non-receipt of the payment resulted in a late fee. 未收到付款导致滞纳金。

The non-receipt of his application caused a delay in the processing.  未收到他的申请导致处理延误。


近义词：lack of receipt, failure to receive, non-delivery, absence of acknowledgment


反义词：receipt, acknowledgment, confirmation, delivery

词性：名词
