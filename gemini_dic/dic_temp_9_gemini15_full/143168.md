outclass:/ˌaʊtˈklæs/| vt.|胜过，超越|To surpass or excel (someone or something) in quality or performance.|例句：His performance outclassed all the others. 他的表演胜过所有其他人。|例句：The new model outclasses its predecessor in terms of speed and efficiency.新款车型在速度和效率方面都超越了其前代产品。|近义词：surpass, excel, exceed, overshadow|反义词：be outclassed by, be inferior to|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outclasses his opponents in every game. = 他在每一场比赛中都胜过对手。
The new software outclasses the old one in functionality. = 新软件在功能上胜过旧软件。


一般过去时:
She outclassed her rivals in the singing competition. = 她在歌唱比赛中胜过她的对手。
The team outclassed their opponents by a wide margin. = 该队以较大优势战胜了对手。


现在完成时:
He has outclassed all expectations with his recent achievements. = 他最近的成就超出了所有人的预期。
The company has outclassed its competitors in the market. = 这家公司在市场上胜过了竞争对手。


过去完成时:
By the end of the match, they had outclassed the opposing team. = 比赛结束时，他们已经胜过了对方球队。
She had outclassed her previous best time in the race. = 她在比赛中超越了她之前的最好成绩。


将来时:
He will outclass his competitors in the upcoming election. = 他将在即将到来的选举中胜过竞争对手。
The new technology will outclass current methods significantly. = 新技术将显著优于现有方法。


虚拟语气:
If he trained harder, he would outclass his rivals. = 如果他训练更努力，他就能胜过对手。
I wish I could outclass my opponent in the debate. = 我希望我能在辩论中胜过我的对手。


被动语态:
He was outclassed by the champion in the final round. = 在决赛中，他被冠军击败了。
The old system was outclassed by the new one. = 旧系统被新系统超越了。


疑问句:
Does he outclass his teammates in skill? = 他的技术是否胜过他的队友？
Will the new product outclass the existing ones on the market? = 新产品会在市场上胜过现有产品吗？


否定句:
He didn't outclass his opponent in the final match. = 他在决赛中没有胜过对手。
The team was not outclassed, but they lost anyway. = 该队并没有被超越，但他们仍然输了。


PHRASE->
outclass the competition = 超越竞争对手
outclass in performance = 表现优异
outclass others = 胜过其他人
outclass the field = 胜过所有人
significantly outclass = 显著胜过
