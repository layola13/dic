interset:/ˈɪntərɛst/ | n. |兴趣，利益，利息 |Interest refers to a feeling of wanting to learn more about something or someone, a share or stake in something, or the money earned from investments.|例句：He has a keen interest in history. 他对历史有浓厚的兴趣。  |例句：The bank pays 5% interest on savings accounts. 银行活期存款支付5%的利息。|近义词：hobby, fascination, involvement, profit|反义词：disinterest, apathy, boredom|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My interest in art grows daily. = 我对艺术的兴趣与日俱增。
What are your interests? = 你的兴趣爱好是什么？


一般过去时:
His interest in the project waned. = 他对这个项目的兴趣减退了。
I had a strong interest in music when I was younger. = 我年轻的时候对音乐有浓厚的兴趣。


现在完成时:
My interest in coding has developed considerably. = 我对编程的兴趣已经有了相当大的发展。
She has shown a sustained interest in her studies. = 她对学习表现出了持续的兴趣。


过去完成时:
Before the presentation, he had lost his interest in the topic. = 在演示文稿之前，他对这个话题失去了兴趣。
They had developed a mutual interest in each other's work. = 他们对彼此的工作产生了共同的兴趣。


将来时:
I believe my interest in this field will continue to grow. = 我相信我对这个领域的兴趣将会持续增长。
They will show great interest in the new product. = 他们会对新产品表现出极大的兴趣。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pursue my interest in photography. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会追求我对摄影的兴趣。
I wish I had the time to pursue my interest in astronomy. = 我希望我有时间去追求我对天文学的兴趣。


被动语态:
His interest was piqued by the lecturer's remarks. = 演讲者的评论激起了他的兴趣。
The company's interest in the project was eventually lost. = 公司对该项目的兴趣最终消失了。


疑问句:
What are your main interests? = 你的主要兴趣是什么？
Does he have any interest in this matter? = 他对这件事感兴趣吗？


否定句:
I have no interest in politics. = 我对政治没有兴趣。
She showed little interest in the offer. = 她对这个提议不太感兴趣。
