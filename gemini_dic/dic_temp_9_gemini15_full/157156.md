pinenchyma: /paɪˈnɛŋkɪmə/ | n. |松软组织，薄壁组织|Parenchyma composed of thin-walled cells, typically found in the cortex of pine trees and other softwood species.  It's responsible for storage and other metabolic functions. |The pinenchyma in this pine sample is particularly well-developed, indicating a healthy tree.  这棵松树样本中的松软组织特别发达，表明这棵树很健康。|  prosenchyma (厚壁组织)| sclerenchyma (厚角组织) | 名词
