penetrated:/ˈpɛnɪtreɪtɪd/ | v. | 穿透；渗透；刺入；理解；影响 | To force a way into or through (something). To affect or influence (someone or something) deeply.  |例句：The bullet penetrated his chest. 子弹穿透了他的胸膛。

The new ideas penetrated the conservative establishment. 新思想渗透到了保守的体制中。|近义词：pierce, permeate, infiltrate, comprehend |反义词：surface, skim, miss, misunderstand


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain penetrates the soil. 雨水渗透土壤。
His words penetrate my heart. 他的话语触动我的心。


一般过去时:
The knife penetrated the watermelon. 刀子穿透了西瓜。
Her argument penetrated his defenses. 她的论点击破了他的防线。


现在完成时:
The roots have penetrated deep into the ground. 根系已深深扎入地下。
The message has penetrated the public consciousness. 这条信息已深入人心。


过去完成时:
The water had already penetrated the basement before we noticed. 我们注意到之前，水已经渗入了地下室。
The rumor had penetrated every corner of the town before we could stop it. 在我们阻止谣言之前，它已经传遍了小镇的每个角落。


将来时:
The enemy will penetrate our defenses. 敌人将会突破我们的防线。
The fragrance will penetrate the entire room. 香气将会弥漫整个房间。


虚拟语气:
If the bullet had penetrated his skull, he would have died instantly. 如果子弹穿透了他的头骨，他会当场死亡。
If her ideas had penetrated deeper, she might have changed the outcome. 如果她的想法更深入一些，她或许就能改变结果。


被动语态:
The wall was penetrated by a cannonball. 墙壁被炮弹击穿了。
His mind was penetrated by a sudden insight. 他的头脑突然被一个洞察力所穿透。


疑问句:
Did the arrow penetrate the target?  箭射中目标了吗？
Has the truth penetrated the facade of lies? 真相穿透了谎言的伪装吗？


否定句:
The water didn't penetrate the roof. 水没有渗透屋顶。
His plea didn't penetrate the judge's heart. 他的恳求没有打动法官。
