occupiers:/ˈɒkjupaɪəz/ | n. |占领者，居住者 |Those who occupy a place or territory, either temporarily or permanently.  They can be soldiers in a military occupation, tenants in a building, or even people living on a piece of land. |例句：The occupiers of the building were forced to evacuate.  建筑物的居住者被迫撤离。  |例句：The occupying forces imposed strict curfews. 占领军实施了严格的宵禁。|近义词：inhabitants, residents, tenants, occupants |反义词：evacuees, refugees


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The occupiers control the city. = 占领者控制着这座城市。
The tenants occupy the apartments. = 租户居住在公寓里。


一般过去时:
The soldiers occupied the town. = 士兵占领了城镇。
The family occupied the house for ten years. = 这家人住了十年房子。


现在完成时:
The army has occupied the territory for months. = 军队已经占领了该领土数月。
They have occupied the building since last year. = 他们从去年开始就住在这栋楼里。


过去完成时:
The rebels had occupied the capital before the peace talks. = 在和平谈判之前，叛军已经占领了首都。
The family had occupied the farm for generations. = 这家人世世代代居住在这个农场。


将来时:
The invaders will occupy the country. = 侵略者将占领这个国家。
We will occupy the new office next week. = 我们下周将搬进新办公室。


虚拟语气:
If the enemy occupied the town, we would retreat. = 如果敌人占领了城镇，我们将撤退。
I wish the squatters hadn't occupied the building. = 我希望那些非法占用者没有占用这栋建筑。


被动语态:
The city was occupied by the enemy. = 这座城市被敌人占领了。
The apartment is occupied by a young couple. = 这套公寓住着一对年轻夫妇。


疑问句:
Who occupied the house before us? = 我们之前是谁住在这个房子里？
Are the apartments fully occupied? = 公寓住满了人吗？


否定句:
The army did not occupy the city. = 军队没有占领这座城市。
The building is not occupied at the moment. = 这栋建筑目前没有被占用。
