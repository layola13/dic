promotes:/prəˈmoʊts/ | vt. |促进；提升；推销；提拔| to further the progress of something; to raise someone to a higher position or rank; to advertise or sell something.| 例句：The government is promoting the use of renewable energy. 政府正在推广可再生能源的使用。 | 例句：She was promoted to manager. 她被提升为经理。 | 近义词： advances, furthers, elevates, boosts, publicizes | 反义词： hinders, obstructs, demotes, discourages


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company promotes its products through social media. = 公司通过社交媒体推广其产品。
Regular exercise promotes good health. = 定期锻炼促进健康。

一般过去时:
The teacher promoted John to class monitor. = 老师提拔约翰当班长。
They promoted the new film with a massive advertising campaign. = 他们用大规模的广告宣传活动来推广这部新电影。

现在完成时:
The campaign has promoted greater awareness of environmental issues. = 这场运动促进了人们对环境问题的更多关注。
The company has promoted several employees to senior positions this year. = 今年公司已经提拔了几名员工担任高级职位。

过去完成时:
They had promoted the product extensively before the launch. = 他们在产品发布之前已经广泛地推广了该产品。
The company had promoted him twice before he finally resigned. = 在他最终辞职之前，公司已经提拔他两次了。

将来时:
The company will promote its new product next month. = 公司下个月将推广其新产品。
They will promote her to a higher position if she meets the targets. = 如果她达到目标，他们将把她提升到更高的职位。

虚拟语气:
If they promoted me, I would be very happy. = 如果他们提拔我，我会非常高兴。
I wish they promoted me to a higher position. = 我希望他们能把我提升到更高的职位。

被动语态:
The product was promoted through television commercials. = 该产品通过电视广告进行推广。
He was promoted to the position of CEO last year. = 他去年被提升为首席执行官。

疑问句:
Does the company promote its products online? = 公司是否在网上推广其产品？
Will they promote her to a higher position? = 他们会把她提升到更高的职位吗？

否定句:
The company does not promote unethical behavior. = 公司不提倡不道德的行为。
They did not promote him because of his poor performance. = 他们没有提拔他，因为他表现不佳。
