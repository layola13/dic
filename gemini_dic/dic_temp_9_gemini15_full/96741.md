英文解析：The plural form of "ineligibility," meaning the state or quality of being ineligible;  the state of not being qualified or suitable. It refers to multiple instances or aspects of ineligibility.


例句：

The numerous ineligiblenesses discovered during the audit led to the project's cancellation.  审计中发现的诸多不合格之处导致项目取消。

The committee discussed the various ineligiblenesses that prevented some applicants from being considered.  委员会讨论了阻止部分申请人被考虑的各种不合格之处。

* **in-**:  prefix indicating negation or lack of.
* **eligibl-**:  stem from "eligible," meaning qualified or suitable.
* **-ness**: suffix forming abstract nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
* **-es**: plural suffix.


Therefore, "ineligiblenesses" traces its roots back to "eligible," adding prefixes and suffixes to denote the plural state of being unqualified or unsuitable.
