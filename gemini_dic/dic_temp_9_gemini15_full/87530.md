heterogeneousness:/ˌhɛtərədʒɪˈniːəsnəs/| n. |异质性，不均匀性，多样性 |The heterogeneousness of the student body enriched the classroom experience. 学生群体的多样性丰富了课堂体验。|近义词：diversity, variety, disparity |反义词：homogeneity, uniformity


英文解析:Heterogeneousness refers to the state or quality of being heterogeneous;  characterized by a lack of uniformity; composed of different or dissimilar elements. It implies a mixture of different things, often with significant differences between them.


例句:

The heterogeneousness of the soil made it difficult to grow crops uniformly. 土壤的异质性使得作物难以均匀生长。

The city’s population boasts a remarkable heterogeneousness in terms of ethnicity and culture. 这个城市的居民在种族和文化方面拥有显著的多样性。
