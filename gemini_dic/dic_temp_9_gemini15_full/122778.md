molar:/ˈmoʊlər/| adj. n. |磨牙的；摩尔；摩尔浓度的 | Relating to or denoting a molar tooth or the molar region of the jaw.  A unit of measurement for the amount of substance.  A solution containing one mole of solute per litre of solution. |例句：He has a molar toothache. 他牙痛。  The molar mass of water is 18 grams per mole. 水的摩尔质量是每摩尔18克。 The experiment requires a 1 molar solution of hydrochloric acid. 实验需要1摩尔浓度的盐酸溶液。|近义词：grinder (tooth), molarity (for concentration)|反义词：incisor (tooth)

词源:
From Latin *molaris "of or belonging to a millstone," from molāris "of or belonging to a mill," from mola "millstone."  The sense development is from the resemblance of the shape of the molar teeth to a millstone.  The chemical sense of "mole" is a relatively recent development.
