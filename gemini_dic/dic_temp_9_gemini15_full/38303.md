conce: /kənˈsiː/ | v. | 隐藏；隐瞒；遮蔽 | To hide or conceal something is to keep it secret or prevent it from being seen or known.  | He tried to conce his feelings. 他试图隐藏自己的感情。|近义词：hide, secrete, mask | 反义词：reveal, disclose, unveil | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conceals his true intentions. = 他隐藏了他真正的意图。
They conceal the evidence from the police. = 他们向警方隐瞒证据。


一般过去时:
She concealed her disappointment well. = 她很好地掩饰了自己的失望。
The artist concealed the flaw in the painting. = 艺术家掩盖了画作中的瑕疵。


现在完成时:
They have concealed the truth for years. = 他们隐瞒真相多年了。
He has concealed his identity from the press. = 他一直向媒体隐瞒身份。


过去完成时:
She had concealed the letter before her parents arrived. = 她在父母到达之前已经藏起了信。
They had concealed their plans from everyone. = 他们对每个人都隐瞒了他们的计划。


将来时:
He will conceal the documents in a safe place. = 他将把文件藏在一个安全的地方。
They will conceal their activities from the public. = 他们将向公众隐瞒他们的活动。


虚拟语气:
If he concealed the truth, no one would know. = 如果他隐瞒真相，没有人会知道。
I wish I had concealed my feelings better. = 我希望我当时能更好地掩饰我的感情。


被动语态:
The truth was concealed from the public. = 真相被公众隐瞒了。
The documents were concealed in a secret compartment. = 文件被藏在一个秘密隔间里。


疑问句:
Did he conceal the money? = 他把钱藏起来了吗？
Where did she conceal the weapon? = 她把武器藏在哪里了？


否定句:
He did not conceal his anger. = 他没有掩饰他的愤怒。
They did not conceal their involvement in the crime. = 他们没有隐瞒他们参与犯罪的事实。
