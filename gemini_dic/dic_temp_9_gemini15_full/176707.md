reactionisms:/riˈækʃənɪzəm/| n. |反动主义，复古主义|The belief in and advocacy of returning to older, often traditional, social, political, or economic structures or values.  It often involves resistance to progressive or radical change and a desire to maintain or restore a perceived past order.  The term is often used pejoratively.|Example: The rise of reactionism in the country led to increased social unrest.  反动主义在该国的兴起导致社会动荡加剧。|近义词：conservatism, traditionalism, retrogradism|反义词：progressivism, radicalism, liberalism|名词
