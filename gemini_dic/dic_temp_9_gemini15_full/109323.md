lengthening:/ˈlɛŋθənɪŋ/| v. n. |延长；拉长；加长|The act of making something longer; the process of becoming longer.  It can be used as a noun referring to the act or process of lengthening, or as a verb describing the action of making something longer. |例句：The lengthening of the runway improved safety.  跑道的加长提高了安全性。

例句：The days are lengthening as we approach summer.  随着夏天的临近，白天越来越长了。


近义词：prolongation, extension, expansion |反义词：shortening, contraction


词性: Verb (动词), Noun (名词)
