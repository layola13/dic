fluate:/ˈfluːeɪt/| v.|波动，起伏；不稳定；（使）流畅；|to vary or change irregularly; to flow smoothly; to make something flow smoothly |The water level fluctuated throughout the day.  水位整天都在波动。|The stock market fluctuated wildly. 股市剧烈波动。|近义词：vary, waver, undulate |反义词：stabilize, remain constant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price fluctuates daily. = 价格每天都在波动。
The river fluctuates with the tides. = 河水随着潮汐涨落。

一般过去时:
The temperature fluctuated throughout the night. = 温度整夜都在波动。
The economy fluctuated during the recession. = 经济在经济衰退期间波动。

现在完成时:
The market has fluctuated significantly this year. = 今年市场波动很大。
The river has fluctuated less since the dam was built. = 自从建了水坝以来，河水波动较小了。

过去完成时:
The stock price had fluctuated wildly before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，股价波动剧烈。
The water level had fluctuated dramatically before the flood. = 洪水来临之前，水位剧烈波动。

将来时:
The weather is predicted to fluctuate next week. = 预计下周天气将会波动。
The exchange rate will likely fluctuate in the coming months. = 汇率在未来几个月可能会波动。

虚拟语气:
If the temperature fluctuated less, we could have a better harvest. = 如果温度波动小一些，我们就能获得更好的收成。
I wish the market hadn't fluctuated so much. = 我希望市场不要波动这么大。

被动语态:
The data was fluctuated by external factors. = 数据受到外部因素的影响而波动。
The results are fluctuated by several variables. = 结果受几个变量的影响而波动。

疑问句:
Does the level of the sea fluctuate much? = 海平面波动大吗？
How much did the temperature fluctuate yesterday? = 昨天的温度波动有多大？

否定句:
The quality of the product doesn't fluctuate. = 产品质量没有波动。
The production didn't fluctuate significantly. = 生产没有显著波动。


PHRASE->
fluctuate wildly = 剧烈波动
fluctuate between...and... = 在...和...之间波动
fluctuate slightly = 轻微波动
fluctuate seasonally = 季节性波动
fluctuate dramatically = 急剧波动
