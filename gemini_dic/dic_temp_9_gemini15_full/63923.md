epiplastron:/ˌɛpɪˈplæstrɒn/| n. |胸甲(龟类)|The epiplastron is a paired bone in the plastron of a turtle or tortoise, forming the anterior part of the ventral shell.  It is analogous to the clavicle in other vertebrates. |The epiplastron is the most anterior element of the plastron.  |近义词:anterior plastral bone|反义词:hyoplastron, hypoplastron
