cooccurred:/ˌkoʊəˈkɜːrd/| v.|同时发生|To happen or exist at the same time.|The symptoms of the disease often cooccurred with a high fever.  这种疾病的症状通常伴有高烧。|The economic downturn and the rise in unemployment cooccurred. 经济衰退和失业率上升同时发生。|occur simultaneously, coincide|rarely, seldom|动词
