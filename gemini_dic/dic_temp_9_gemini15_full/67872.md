fabricating:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪtɪŋ/| v.|捏造；制造；编造；伪造|To create or manufacture something, especially in a deceptive way.  It can refer to making something up (e.g., a story), constructing something (e.g., a building), or forging something (e.g., a document).|例句：He was accused of fabricating evidence to support his case. 他被指控伪造证据以支持他的说法。

例句：The company was found to be fabricating its safety reports.  这家公司被发现伪造其安全报告。


近义词：invent, concoct, forge, manufacture, construct, create


反义词：disprove, debunk, reveal, expose


USAGE->

They fabricate metal parts for cars. = 他们为汽车制造金属零件。
