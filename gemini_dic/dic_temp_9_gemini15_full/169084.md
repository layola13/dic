progressist:/ˈprɒɡrɛsɪst/| adj. n.|进步主义者；进步的|A person who advocates for social reform and progress.  Someone who believes in progress, especially social and political progress.|例句：Many progressist politicians support social justice initiatives. 许多进步主义政治家支持社会正义倡议。  The progressist movement championed women's suffrage. 进步主义运动大力倡导妇女选举权。|近义词：liberal, reformist, radical|反义词：conservative, reactionary, traditionalist|形容词，名词
