coveting:/ˈkʌvɪtɪŋ/| v.|垂涎；贪求；渴望 | To wish for eagerly; to desire something that belongs to someone else.  It often implies a strong, perhaps even illicit, desire. |例句：He spent years coveting his neighbor's beautiful garden. 他多年来一直垂涎邻居美丽的院子。

例句：She was coveting the diamond necklace she saw in the shop window. 她渴望得到她在商店橱窗里看到的钻石项链。


近义词：craving, yearning, longing, desiring, hankering

反义词：disdaining, rejecting, spurning


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He covets his brother's success. = 他羡慕他哥哥的成功。
They covet the latest technology. = 他们渴望拥有最新的科技。


一般过去时:
She coveted that painting for years. = 她多年来一直渴望得到那幅画。
They coveted the land belonging to their neighbor. = 他们觊觎他们邻居的土地。


现在完成时:
I have coveted that car since I was a child. = 从小我就一直渴望拥有那辆车。
She has coveted a position in that company for a long time. = 她很久以来一直渴望在那家公司谋职。


过去完成时:
He had coveted the throne for many years before he finally seized it. = 在他最终夺取王位之前，他已经觊觎多年了。
They had coveted the prize for a long time before they won it. = 在他们赢得奖项之前，他们已经觊觎很久了。


将来时:
She will covet the title of champion next year.  = 明年她将觊觎冠军头衔。
He will covet that promotion for years to come. = 未来几年他都将渴望得到那次晋升。


虚拟语气:
If I were rich, I would not covet anyone's possessions. = 如果我富有，我就不会觊觎任何人的财产。
I wish I didn't covet my friend's new house. = 我真希望我不羡慕我朋友的新房子。


被动语态:
The prize was coveted by many contestants. = 许多参赛者都渴望获得这个奖项。
The position is coveted by many ambitious individuals. = 许多有抱负的人都渴望得到这个职位。


疑问句:
Does he covet power? = 他渴望权力吗？
Do they covet the attention of the media? = 他们渴望媒体的关注吗？


否定句:
He does not covet material possessions. = 他不贪图物质财富。
She did not covet her sister's beauty. = 她并不羡慕她妹妹的美貌。



PHRASE->
to covet something greatly = 极度渴望某物
to covet another's possessions = 觊觎他人的财产
to covet power/position = 渴望权力/职位
to be consumed by covetousness = 被贪婪吞噬
to openly covet = 公然觊觎


词源:
源自古法语单词 *coveiter*,  最终源自拉丁语单词 *cupiditas*,  意思是“渴望，贪婪”。
