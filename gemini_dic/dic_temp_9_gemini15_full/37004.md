columbite:/ˈkʌlʌmbaɪt/| n. |铌铁矿|A dark-colored mineral consisting of niobium and tantalum oxides, often found together with tin and tungsten ores.  It is an important source of niobium and tantalum, metals used in various high-tech applications.|例句：Columbite is a valuable ore because it contains niobium and tantalum.|近义词：tantalite (often found together and similar in composition)|反义词：无
