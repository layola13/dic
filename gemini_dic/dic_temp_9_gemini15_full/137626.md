nontuned:/ˌnɒnˈtjuːnd/| adj. |未调谐的，未调整的|Not adjusted or tuned; lacking precise adjustment or calibration.  This term often applies to systems or devices that require tuning or calibration to function optimally.  For example, a "nontuned amplifier" means an amplifier that doesn't have a specific resonant frequency. |例句：The nontuned antenna received a wide range of frequencies.  未调谐的天线接收到了各种频率。

The nontuned instrument produced a discordant sound. 未调谐的乐器发出了不和谐的声音。|近义词：unadjusted, uncalibrated, misaligned |反义词：tuned, calibrated, adjusted
