reecho:/ˈriːˌekoʊ/| v.|回响，重复，共鸣|To echo again or repeatedly; to be echoed back.|例句：The sound of the gunshot reechoed through the canyon.  枪声在峡谷中回响。|例句：His words reechoed the sentiments of many.  他的话语反映了许多人的观点。|近义词：resonate, reverberate|反义词：silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The singer's voice reechoes in the concert hall. = 歌手的歌声在音乐厅回荡。
The teacher reechoes the important points. = 老师反复强调重点。


一般过去时:
The thunder reechoed across the valley. = 雷声在山谷中回荡。
She reechoed her friend's opinion. = 她重复了她朋友的意见。


现在完成时:
The news has reechoed around the world. = 这个消息已经在全世界传开了。
His words have reechoed in my mind ever since. = 从那以后，他的话一直在我脑海中回响。


过去完成时:
The applause had reechoed through the stadium before the players left. = 运动员离开之前，掌声已经在体育场回荡。
The rumor had reechoed throughout the town before the truth came out. = 真相大白之前，谣言已经在镇上广为流传。


将来时:
The speech will reecho in the history books. = 这篇演讲将载入史册。
Her laughter will reecho in our memories. = 她的笑声将永远留在我们的记忆中。


虚拟语气:
If he shouted, his voice would reecho across the lake. = 如果他喊叫，他的声音会在湖面上回荡。
I wish my words could reecho in their hearts. = 我希望我的话能引起他们的共鸣。


被动语态:
His ideas were reechoed by many intellectuals. = 他的想法被许多知识分子所认同。
The announcement was reechoed on all major news channels. = 这一公告在所有主要新闻频道上都有报道。


疑问句:
Does the sound reecho in this cave? = 这个洞穴里声音会回响吗？
Will her words reecho throughout the ages? = 她的言论将会流传千古吗？


否定句:
The message did not reecho in the hearts of the people. = 这条信息并没有引起人们的共鸣。
His concerns were not reechoed by the government. = 政府没有回应他的担忧。
