outsulk:/aʊtˈsʌlk/| v.|赌气，生气地不理人|To sulk openly or conspicuously; to display one's sulkiness.  It implies a more overt and dramatic display of sulking than simply "sulking."|例句：He outsulked in his room all afternoon after the argument. 他在争吵后整个下午都生气地待在房间里。  She outsulked at the party, refusing to speak to anyone.  她在派对上赌气，拒绝和任何人说话。|近义词：sulk, pout, mope, brood|反义词：be cheerful, be happy, be pleasant
