restaged:/riːˈsteɪdʒd/| v.|重新上演，重新搬上舞台|To put on a play, performance, or event again, often with changes or in a new setting.|例句：The play was restaged in a modern setting. 这部戏在一个现代化的环境中重新上演。 The director restaged the famous scene with new actors. 导演用新的演员重新排演了那个著名的场景。|近义词：revive, reproduce, recreate|反义词：archive, shelve


USAGE->

They restage the musical every year. = 他们每年都重新上演这部音乐剧。
The theatre company restages classic plays. = 这个剧团重新上演经典戏剧。

The production was restaged with a new cast. = 这部作品由新的演员阵容重新上演。
