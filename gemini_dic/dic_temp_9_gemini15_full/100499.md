interventional:/ˌɪntərˈvɛnʃənəl/| adj.|干预的；介入的；干涉的 | Relating to or involving intervention, especially medical intervention.  It describes actions taken to influence or change a situation, typically a medical one, but can apply to other fields as well. |例句：The doctor recommended interventional radiology to treat the blockage.  医生建议采用介入放射学治疗阻塞。

例句：The government's interventional policies aimed to stabilize the economy. 政府的干预政策旨在稳定经济。


近义词：intrusive, interfering, meddlesome |反义词：non-interventional, passive, laissez-faire
