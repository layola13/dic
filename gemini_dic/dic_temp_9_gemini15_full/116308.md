mastabah: /ˈmæstəbɑː/| n. |马斯塔巴墓|A mastaba is a type of ancient Egyptian tomb, typically rectangular in shape with flat roofs and sloping sides, built of mud-brick or stone.  It's characterized by its simple, rectangular structure and its subterranean burial chamber.  |例句：The archaeologists unearthed several intact mastabas during their excavation. 考古学家在挖掘过程中发现了几个完整的马斯塔巴墓。 The mastaba served as a resting place for the pharaoh's officials. 马斯塔巴墓是法老官员的安息之地。|近义词：tomb, sepulchre|反义词：none|名词
