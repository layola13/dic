checky:/ˈtʃɛki/| adj.| 粗鲁的；厚颜无耻的；放肆的 |  Characterized by impudence or cheeky behavior; showing a lack of respect or politeness in a playful or amusing way.  |例句：He gave a cheeky grin. 他露齿一笑，很无赖。 |例句：Her cheeky remark made everyone laugh.  她无礼的言论让大家哈哈大笑。|近义词：saucy, impudent, impertinent, bold |反义词：respectful, polite, timid
