emigrate:/ˈɛməɡreɪt/| v.| 移民；移居国外 | To leave one's own country to settle permanently in another.  |例句：Many people emigrated from Europe to America in the 19th century.  十九世纪许多人从欧洲移民到美洲。
|例句：  He decided to emigrate to Australia after graduating from university. 他大学毕业后决定移民澳大利亚。|近义词：migrate, expatriate |反义词：immigrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He emigrates to Canada next year. 他明年移民加拿大。
They emigrate to find better opportunities. 他们移民是为了寻找更好的机会。


一般过去时:
My grandparents emigrated to the United States in 1950. 我的祖父母在1950年移民到美国。
She emigrated from Ireland to England. 她从爱尔兰移民到英格兰。


现在完成时:
They have emigrated to Australia and started a new life. 他们已经移民到澳大利亚，并开始了新的生活。
He has emigrated several times in his life. 他一生中多次移民。


过去完成时:
Before he died, he had emigrated to Brazil. 他死前已经移民到巴西。
They had emigrated to France before the war broke out. 战争爆发前他们已经移民到法国。


将来时:
We will emigrate to New Zealand once we sell our house. 我们卖掉房子后就会移民到新西兰。
She will emigrate after she finishes her studies. 她完成学业后就会移民。


虚拟语气:
If I were younger, I would emigrate to a warmer country. 如果我还年轻，我会移民到一个气候温暖的国家。
I wish I had emigrated sooner. 我希望我早点移民。


被动语态:
Many families were emigrated during the war. 许多家庭在战争期间被迫移民。
The policy was changed to encourage people to emigrate. 政策改变是为了鼓励人们移民。


疑问句:
Have you ever considered emigrating? 你有没有考虑过移民？
Why did they emigrate? 他们为什么移民？


否定句:
He didn't emigrate because he loved his country. 他没有移民是因为他热爱自己的国家。
They decided not to emigrate after all. 他们最终决定不移民了。


PHRASE->
emigrate from  从...移民
emigrate to  移民到...
emigrate for  为了...而移民
emigrate because of  因为...而移民
emigrate with  与...一起移民
