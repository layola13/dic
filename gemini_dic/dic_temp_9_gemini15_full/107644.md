languages:/ˈlæŋɡwɪdʒɪz/| n. |语言；语言学| Languages are systems of communication used by humans. They consist of vocabulary, grammar, and pronunciation.  Many languages exist around the world, each with its own unique features. | Example:  The United Nations uses six official languages. 联合国使用六种官方语言。  |The study of languages is called linguistics. 语言的研究被称为语言学。| dialects, tongues |  non-language
