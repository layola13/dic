ice-skates:/ˈaɪsˌskeɪts/| n. |冰鞋 |Ice skates are footwear designed for gliding on ice. They consist of a blade attached to a boot.  |例句：She laced up her ice skates and stepped onto the rink. 她系好冰鞋，走上溜冰场。|例句：The children were thrilled to receive new ice skates for Christmas. 孩子们收到圣诞节的新冰鞋非常兴奋。|近义词：skates |反义词:None.
