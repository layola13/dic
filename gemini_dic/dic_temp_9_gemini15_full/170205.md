prototypical:/ˌproʊtoʊˈtɪpɪkəl/ | adj. | 原型的；典型的；标准的 |  Representing the perfect or most typical example of a particular type of person or thing.  A prototypical example is considered the most representative or quintessential instance of a category. |  The robin is a prototypical example of a songbird.  知更鸟是鸣禽的典型例子。

The dog is a prototypical pet. 狗是典型的宠物。 | typical, exemplary, quintessential | atypical, unusual | adj.

词源:
The word "prototypical" comes from the Greek words "protos" (first, original) and "typos" (type, model, mark).  Therefore, it literally means "first type" or "original model."
