non-fermentable: /ˌnɑːnˈfɜːrmɛntəbl/ | adj. | 不可发酵的 | Not capable of being fermented.  This term typically refers to substances that cannot be broken down by microorganisms (such as bacteria or yeast) into simpler substances through fermentation.  This often relates to the inability of microorganisms to metabolize a substance for energy production in the absence of oxygen. | Example:  "This type of sugar is non-fermentable by most intestinal bacteria." =  "这种类型的糖大多数肠道细菌都无法发酵。" | 近义词: unfermentable, non-fertilizable | 反义词: fermentable
