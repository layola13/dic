look-for:/lʊk fɔːr/| v.|寻找，寻求 |To search for something or someone. |例句：I'm looking for my keys. 我正在找我的钥匙。|例句：They are looking for a new apartment. 他们在寻找一套新公寓。|近义词：search for, hunt for, seek |反义词：find


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I look for my glasses every morning. = 我每天早上都找我的眼镜。
We look for new talent every year. = 我们每年都在寻找新的优秀人才。


一般过去时:
She looked for her lost cat all night. = 她找了她丢失的猫一整夜。
They looked for a solution to the problem. = 他们寻找解决这个问题的方法。


现在完成时:
I have been looking for a job for months. = 我已经找工作好几个月了。
We have looked for a better way to do it. = 我们一直在寻找更好的方法去做这件事。


过去完成时:
He had looked for the book before he asked me. = 他在我问他之前就已经找过那本书了。
They had looked for evidence for weeks. = 他们已经找证据好几个星期了。


将来时:
I will look for you at the airport. = 我将在机场等你。
We will look for a new house next year. = 我们明年将会寻找一栋新房子。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would look for another job. = 如果我是你，我会去找另一份工作。
I wish I could look for a better opportunity. = 我希望我能找到更好的机会。


被动语态:
The missing child is being looked for by the police. = 警察正在寻找失踪的孩子。
The solution is being looked for by the researchers. = 研究人员正在寻找解决方案。


疑问句:
What are you looking for? = 你在找什么？
Where are they looking for the treasure? = 他们在哪里寻找宝藏？


否定句:
I didn't look for trouble. = 我没有惹麻烦。
They didn't look for excuses. = 他们没有找借口。


PHRASE->
look for trouble = 自找麻烦

look for information = 寻找信息
