confinements:/kənˈfaɪnmənts/ | n. |限制；监禁；分娩 |The state of being kept or confined within limits; imprisonment or captivity.  Also refers to the period of childbirth. |例句：The confinement of the prisoners was harsh. 囚犯的监禁很残酷。

例句：The mother's confinement was difficult but ultimately rewarding.  母亲的分娩很艰难，但最终很有意义。


近义词：imprisonment, restriction, incarceration, childbirth |反义词：freedom, liberty, release
