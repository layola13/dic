mulching:/ˈmʌltʃɪŋ/| n. v.|覆盖(土壤),覆盖物|The process of covering the soil surface with a layer of material such as straw, leaves, or plastic.  This helps retain moisture, suppress weeds, and regulate soil temperature.|例句：We're mulching the garden beds with shredded bark. 我们正在用碎树皮覆盖花园的花坛。
例句：The farmer mulched his fields with straw to retain moisture.  这位农民用稻草覆盖他的田地以保持水分。|近义词：ground cover, soil cover|反义词：uncovering, exposing
