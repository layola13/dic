looped:/luːpt/ | adj. |循环的，重复的，卷曲的 | Having a looped or curved shape; repeating in a cycle.  |例句：The recording was looped continuously.  录音一直在循环播放。|The vine looped around the tree. 藤蔓缠绕着树木。|近义词：circular, cyclical, recurrent, coiled |反义词：straight, linear, uncoiled
