hater:/ˈheɪtər/ | n. | 仇恨者；讨厌鬼 | A person who intensely dislikes someone or something.  | Example: He's become a social media hater, constantly criticizing everything online. 他已经成为一个社交媒体上的讨厌鬼，不断地批评网上的所有东西。  |近义词：detractor, critic, opponent | 反义词：admirer, supporter, fan | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He hates Mondays. = 他讨厌星期一。
She hates spicy food. = 她讨厌辛辣的食物。

一般过去时:
I hated that movie. = 我讨厌那部电影。
They hated the weather. = 他们讨厌这个天气。

现在完成时:
I have hated him since childhood. = 从小我就讨厌他。
She has hated public speaking her whole life. = 她一生都讨厌公开演讲。

过去完成时:
I had hated him long before he wronged me. = 在他冤枉我很久之前我就讨厌他了。
They had hated the idea from the start. = 从一开始他们就讨厌这个主意。

将来时:
I will hate this job forever. = 我将永远讨厌这份工作。
They will hate the news when they hear it. = 当他们听到这个消息时，他们会讨厌的。

虚拟语气:
I wish I didn't hate Mondays so much. = 我真希望我不那么讨厌星期一。
If I didn't hate broccoli, I'd eat more vegetables. = 如果我不讨厌西兰花，我会多吃蔬菜。

被动语态:
(Not typically used in passive voice, as "hate" is usually an active verb.)  (很少用被动语态，因为“hate”通常是主动动词)


疑问句:
Do you hate this music? = 你讨厌这音乐吗？
Does he hate cats? = 他讨厌猫吗？

否定句:
I don't hate him, I just don't like him. = 我不讨厌他，我只是不喜欢他。
She doesn't hate her job, but she's not passionate about it. = 她不讨厌她的工作，但她对它并不热情。


PHRASE->

hater of (something): 讨厌...的人
internet hater: 网络喷子
celebrity hater: 名人黑粉
professional hater: 专业黑
full-time hater: 全职黑
