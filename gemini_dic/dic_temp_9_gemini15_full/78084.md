geofencing:/ˈdʒioʊˌfɛnsɪŋ/| n. |地理围栏，地理围墙|The virtual boundary created using GPS or RFID technology to trigger a pre-programmed action when a mobile device or other tagged object enters, exits, or dwells within a defined geographical area.|例句：Many apps use geofencing to send location-based notifications. 许多应用程序使用地理围栏发送基于位置的通知。|例句：Our company uses geofencing to track our delivery vehicles. 我们公司使用地理围栏来追踪我们的送货车辆。|近义词：virtual boundary, location-based service|反义词：open area, unrestricted zone|名词
