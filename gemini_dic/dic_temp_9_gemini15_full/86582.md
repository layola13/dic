heliocentricism:/ˌhiːliəʊˈsɛntrɪzəm/ | n. |日心说 |The astronomical model in which the Earth and planets revolve around the Sun at the center of the solar system.  It is the belief that the sun is the center of the universe, with the earth and other planets revolving around it. |The acceptance of heliocentricism revolutionized astronomy. 日心说的接受彻底改变了天文学。|geocentrism |geocentrism |
