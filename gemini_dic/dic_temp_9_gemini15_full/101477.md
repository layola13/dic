iridescent:/ˌɪrɪˈdɛsənt/ | adj. | 彩虹色的，闪光的 | Showing luminous colours that seem to change when seen from different angles.  The word describes a surface that displays a play of colours, like an oil slick or a soap bubble. |例句：The iridescent hummingbird zipped through the air. 这只闪闪发光的蜂鸟在空中飞舞。

The soap bubble shimmered with iridescent colours.  肥皂泡闪耀着彩虹般的颜色。


近义词：shimmering, lustrous, opalescent, pearlescent | 反义词：dull, matte


词源:  From Middle French *iridescent, from Latin iris "rainbow" + -escent "becoming".  The Latin *iris itself is of Greek origin, from ἶρις (iris), meaning "rainbow".
