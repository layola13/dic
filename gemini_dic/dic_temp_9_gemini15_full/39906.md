constitutively:/kɒnstɪˈtjuːtɪvli/ | adv. | 本质上地；组成性地；构成性地 |  In a constitutive manner; essentially; by virtue of its constitution.  This adverb describes something that is inherent or essential to the nature of something else. It emphasizes the inherent or defining characteristic of something. | Example: The gene is constitutively expressed, meaning it's always active.  这个基因组成性表达，意味着它总是活跃的。
