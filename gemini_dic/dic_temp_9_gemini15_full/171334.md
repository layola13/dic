public-spirited: /ˌpʌblɪkˈspɪrɪtɪd/ | adj. | 热心公益的，具有公共精神的 |  Characterized by or showing a concern for the public good; unselfish; altruistic.  It describes individuals or actions motivated by a desire to benefit society as a whole, rather than personal gain. |

例句：

The public-spirited mayor initiated many programs to improve the city's infrastructure.  = 热心公益的市长启动了许多项目来改善城市的基础设施。

She made a public-spirited donation to the local hospital. = 她向当地医院捐赠了一笔善款，体现了她的公共精神。


近义词：altruistic, benevolent, charitable, philanthropic, unselfish, civic-minded

反义词：selfish, self-centered, egoistic, greedy


词性：形容词
