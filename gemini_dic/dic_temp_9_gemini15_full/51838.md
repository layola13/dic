dewworm:/ˈduːwɔːrm/| n. |蚯蚓|An annelid worm that lives in the soil and feeds on decaying organic matter. Dewworms are important for soil aeration and fertility.  |例句：The dew worm wriggled through the damp earth.  蚯蚓在潮湿的泥土中蠕动。|例句：The gardener welcomed the presence of dew worms in his garden. 园丁欢迎他的花园里有蚯蚓。|近义词：earthworm|反义词：N/A
