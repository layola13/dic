depauperate:/dɪˈpɔːpəreɪt/| adj.|贫乏的；缺乏的；枯竭的|impoverished, reduced to poverty; lacking in essential qualities or resources.  The depauperate soil yielded a meager crop.  |近义词：impoverished, depleted, impoverished, meager, barren |反义词：rich, abundant, fertile


例句:

The depauperate community lacked access to clean water and healthcare.  贫困的社区缺乏干净的水和医疗保健。

The depauperate flora of the region reflected the harsh environmental conditions.  该地区贫乏的植物群反映了恶劣的环境条件。


词源:

From Latin *depauperatus, past participle of depauperare "to make poor," from de- "away, from" + pauper "poor."


用法说明:

depauperate主要用作形容词，描述缺乏资源、贫乏或枯竭的状态。它可以用来形容土地、环境、社区或个人。


(由于depauperate本身是形容词，没有动词或名词用法，因此以下各语法时态部分不适用)


PHRASE->

a depauperate diet  贫乏的饮食

a depauperate ecosystem  贫乏的生态系统

a depauperate environment 贫瘠的环境

a depauperate community  贫困的社区

a depauperate collection  贫乏的收藏
