panoistic:/ˌpænəʊˈɪstɪk/| adj. |泛卵巢的，全卵巢的| Relating to or characterized by panoistic ovaries;  in which all the cells of the ovary develop into oocytes (egg cells), with no nurse cells.  This is in contrast to meroistic ovaries, where only some cells develop into oocytes, and others function as nurse cells. | Example:  "The insect species exhibits a panoistic ovary structure."  "The study focused on the panoistic ovaries of the Drosophila melanogaster." | 近义词：holocytic (in the context of ovarioles)| 反义词：meroistic
