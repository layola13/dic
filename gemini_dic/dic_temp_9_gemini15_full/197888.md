shipwrecked:/ˈʃɪpˌrekt/| adj.|遇难的，失事的；遭受破坏的|suffering the destruction or ruin of a ship at sea;  describing a ship that has been wrecked.  Also, figuratively, describing something that has been ruined or destroyed.|例句：The shipwrecked sailors were rescued after three days at sea.  遇难的水手们在海上漂流三天后获救了。The shipwrecked economy needed significant investment to recover.  遭受破坏的经济需要大量投资才能复苏。|近义词：wrecked, ruined, devastated|反义词：saved, rescued, intact

一般现在时:
The storm shipwrecks many boats every year.  风暴每年使许多船只遇难。
He shipwrecks his own business with bad decision-making. 他因糟糕的决策而毁了自己的生意。

一般过去时:
The ship was shipwrecked during a violent storm. 那艘船在暴风雨中遇难了。
Their plans were shipwrecked by unforeseen circumstances. 他们的计划因不可预见的情况而破产。

现在完成时:
The company has been shipwrecked by its own mismanagement. 这家公司因自身管理不善而破产。
Many dreams have been shipwrecked by the harsh realities of life. 许多梦想都被生活的残酷现实击碎了。

过去完成时:
The ship had been shipwrecked weeks before we found the survivors.  在我们找到幸存者之前，那艘船已经遇难几周了。
By the time the news broke, their reputation had been shipwrecked.  消息传出时，他们的名声已经毁了。

将来时:
If we don't change course, the company will be shipwrecked. 如果我们不改变方向，公司将会破产。
The project will be shipwrecked without further funding.  如果没有进一步的资金，这个项目将会失败。

虚拟语气:
If the captain had been more careful, the ship wouldn't have been shipwrecked. 如果船长更小心一点，船就不会遇难了。
If they had planned better, their careers wouldn't have been shipwrecked. 如果他们计划得更好，他们的职业生涯就不会毁了。

被动语态:
Many ships were shipwrecked on those treacherous rocks.  许多船只在那片危险的礁石上遇难了。
His reputation was shipwrecked by the scandal. 他的名声因丑闻而受损。

疑问句:
Was the ship shipwrecked because of the storm? 船是因为风暴而遇难的吗？
Were their hopes shipwrecked by the failure of the project? 他们的希望因项目的失败而破灭了吗？

否定句:
The ship wasn't shipwrecked; it simply ran aground. 船没有遇难；它只是搁浅了。
His career wasn't shipwrecked by the criticism; he overcame it. 他的职业生涯并没有因为批评而毁掉；他克服了它。
