outvied:/aʊtˈvaɪd/| vt.|胜过；超过|To surpass or exceed in some competition or contest; to outdo.|例句：The small company outvied the larger corporations in innovation. 这家小公司在创新方面胜过了更大的公司。
例句：He outvied his rivals in the election campaign. 他在竞选活动中胜过了他的竞争对手。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, excel|反义词：be outdone, be surpassed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outvies his rivals in every competition. = 他在每一次比赛中都胜过他的对手。
The new product outvies all its competitors in terms of features. = 新产品在功能方面胜过所有竞争对手。

一般过去时:
She outvied her opponent in the debate. = 她在那场辩论中胜过了她的对手。
Our team outvied the other team in the final game. = 我们队在决赛中胜过了另一支队伍。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

The artist had outvied all expectations with her latest masterpiece. = 艺术家用她的最新杰作超越了所有人的期望。

虚拟语气:

If she had a better strategy, she would have outvied the others. = 如果她有更好的策略，她就会胜过其他人。

被动语态:

疑问句:
Did he outvie his opponent in the race? = 他在比赛中胜过他的对手了吗？

否定句:
He did not outvie his rival in the contest. = 他在比赛中没有胜过他的对手。
The company failed to outvie its competitors in the market. = 这家公司未能在这个市场上胜过其竞争对手。

词源:
out- (表示“超过”，类似于outdo, outrun等) + vie (竞争，争夺)  vie来自古法语vier，源于拉丁语vidēre（看，观看），引申为竞争，争夺。
