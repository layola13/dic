newbie:/ˈnuːbiː/| n. |新手，菜鸟 |A newbie is a person who is new to a particular activity or situation.  It is often used in online communities or for people new to a specific field. |Example: He's a newbie to the game, so be patient with him. 他是游戏新手，所以对他耐心点。|近义词：beginner, novice, newcomer |反义词：expert, veteran, pro


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a newbie to online gaming. = 他是网络游戏的菜鸟。
She is a newbie in this company. = 她是这家公司的新人。

一般过去时:
He was a newbie when he first started coding. = 他刚开始编程的时候还是个新手。
She was a newbie at the gym last year. = 她去年还是健身房的新手。


现在完成时:
I have been a newbie at this job for only a week. = 我做这份工作才一周，还是个新手。
They have been newbies in this field for only a month. = 他们在这个领域才一个月，还是新手。


过去完成时:
Before the project, he had been a newbie in project management. = 在这个项目之前，他在项目管理方面还是个新手。
She had been a newbie to public speaking before her presentation. = 在她做演讲之前，她不擅长公开演讲。


将来时:
He will be a newbie at the conference next week. = 他下周参加会议的时候还是个新手。
She will be a newbie in her new role until she gets the hang of it. = 在她掌握新工作之前，她会一直是个新手。


虚拟语气:
If I were a newbie, I would ask for help. = 如果我是新手，我会寻求帮助。
I wish I weren't a newbie in this situation. = 我希望在这种情况下我不是个新手。


被动语态:
Newbies are often overlooked. = 新手经常被忽视。
The newbies were given extra training. = 新手们接受了额外的培训。


疑问句:
Are you a newbie here? = 你是这里的新手吗？
Is he a newbie to this software? = 他不熟悉这个软件吗？


否定句:
He is not a newbie anymore. = 他不再是新手了。
She is not a newbie; she's an expert. = 她不是新手；她是个专家。


PHRASE->
newbie programmer = 新手程序员
newbie mistake = 新手错误
a total newbie = 完全的新手
help the newbie = 帮助新手
newbie question = 新手问题
