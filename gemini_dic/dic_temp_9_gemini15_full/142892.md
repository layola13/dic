out-of-time: /ˌaʊt əv ˈtaɪm/ | adj. | 过时的；超时；不在时间内 |  Describes something that is no longer relevant, current, or fashionable; or an action performed after the deadline.  | 例句：His ideas are out-of-time and impractical. 他的想法过时且不切实际。 The project is out-of-time; the deadline has passed.  这个项目超时了；截止日期已过。 | 近义词：obsolete, outdated, past its prime, overdue | 反义词：timely, current, up-to-date, on time |  形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fashion is out-of-time. = 这个时尚过时了。
His comments are out-of-time. = 他的评论过时了。


一般过去时:
The technology was out-of-time. = 这项技术已经过时了。
The application was out-of-time. = 申请已经过期了。


现在完成时:
The design has become out-of-time. = 这个设计已经过时了。
The information has become out-of-time. = 这条信息已经过时了。


过去完成时:
The strategy had become out-of-time. = 这项策略已经过时了。
The equipment had become out-of-time. = 这套设备已经过时了。


将来时:
This design will be out-of-time in five years. = 这种设计五年后将过时。
The software will be out-of-time next year. = 这软件明年将过时。


虚拟语气:
If the report were submitted out-of-time, there would be a penalty. = 如果报告超时提交，将会有处罚。
If the proposal was out-of-time, it wouldn't be considered. = 如果提案过期了，就不会被考虑。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
