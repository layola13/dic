petiolary:/ˈpiːtiəʊleri/| adj.|叶柄的；有叶柄的| Relating to or resembling a petiole (the stalk that joins a leaf to a stem).  The term often describes structures that are stalk-like or have a petiole-like appearance.| The petiolary stipules of the rose are conspicuous. 玫瑰的叶柄托叶很明显。|近义词：pedunculate, stalked|反义词：sessile
