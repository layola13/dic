gummatous:/ɡʌˈmeɪtəs/| adj. |树胶样的；树胶肿的 | Relating to or characteristic of a gumma.  A gumma is a soft, gummy tumor characteristic of tertiary syphilis.  |例句：The patient presented with gummatous lesions on their skin.  患者皮肤上出现了树胶样病变。

The gummatous infiltration was extensive.  树胶样浸润广泛。|近义词：rubbery,  nodular (in the context of tertiary syphilis)|反义词：hard, firm, solid

词源:
