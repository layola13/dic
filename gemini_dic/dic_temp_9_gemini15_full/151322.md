parishes:/ˈpærɪʃɪz/ | n. |教区；教区居民|The word "parishes" refers to the districts overseen by a particular church or priest, often encompassing a specific geographical area and its associated community.  It can also refer to the people who live within that district.|Example: Many small villages are centered around their local parish church.  许多小型村庄都以当地的教区教堂为中心。

The numerous parishes in the diocese held a joint meeting.  该教区众多教区举行了一次联合会议。|近义词：districts, communities, congregations |反义词：N/A
