findings:/ˈfaɪndɪŋz/| n.|发现，结果，调查结果|The results or conclusions reached after an investigation, study, or experiment.|例句：The findings of the study were surprising.  这项研究的结果令人惊讶。|例句：The police released their findings on the case. 警方公布了此案的调查结果。|近义词：discoveries, conclusions, results|反义词：ignorance, unknowns


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The study's findings are significant. = 这项研究的结果意义重大。
His findings support the hypothesis. = 他的发现支持了这个假设。


一般过去时:
The jury delivered their findings yesterday. = 陪审团昨天公布了他们的裁决。
Researchers published their findings last year. = 研究人员去年发表了他们的研究结果。


现在完成时:
Scientists have released their findings on climate change. = 科学家们已经发布了他们关于气候变化的研究结果。
The committee has presented its findings to the board. = 委员会已向董事会提交了调查结果。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
