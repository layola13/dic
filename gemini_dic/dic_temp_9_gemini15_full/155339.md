pharmacist:/ˈfɑːrməsɪst/ | n. | 药剂师 | A pharmacist is a healthcare professional who is licensed to prepare and dispense medications. They also provide advice on medication usage and patient care. |  The pharmacist filled my prescription quickly and efficiently.  这位药剂师快速有效地完成了我的处方。 | chemist, druggist |  (无明确反义词) | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The pharmacist checks the prescription. = 药剂师检查处方。
Pharmacists advise patients on medication. = 药剂师就药物问题向病人提供建议。


一般过去时:
The pharmacist filled my prescription yesterday. = 药剂师昨天完成了我的处方。
She consulted a pharmacist about her allergies. = 她咨询了一位药剂师关于她的过敏症。


现在完成时:
The pharmacist has already dispensed the medication. = 药剂师已经配发了药物。
He has worked as a pharmacist for ten years. = 他做药剂师已经十年了。


过去完成时:
The pharmacist had already left when I arrived. = 我到达时药剂师已经离开了。
They had discussed the dosage with the pharmacist before administering the drug. = 他们在服用药物前已经与药剂师讨论过剂量。


将来时:
The pharmacist will review the patient's medical history. = 药剂师将审查病人的病史。
She will contact the doctor if there is a problem with the prescription. = 如果处方有问题，她会联系医生。


虚拟语气:
If I were a pharmacist, I would help more people. = 如果我是药剂师，我会帮助更多的人。
I wish I had consulted the pharmacist sooner. = 我真希望我早点咨询药剂师。


被动语态:
The prescription was filled by the pharmacist. = 处方由药剂师完成了。
The medication is dispensed by the pharmacist. = 药物由药剂师配发。


疑问句:
Is this pharmacist available? = 这位药剂师有空吗？
Where can I find a pharmacist? = 我在哪里可以找到药剂师？


否定句:
The pharmacist didn't have the medication in stock. = 药剂师没有这种药的库存。
He is not a qualified pharmacist. = 他不是合格的药剂师。



PHRASE->

词源:
Pharmacist comes from the Greek word "pharmakeutes," meaning "one who prepares drugs."  The suffix "-ist" indicates a person who does something.
