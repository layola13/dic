chomper:/ˈtʃɑːmpər/| n. v.|猛咬者；咬碎东西的人或物|A chomper is someone or something that chews or bites forcefully. It can refer to a person, an animal, or even a machine that bites or chews aggressively. |例句：The shark was a fearsome chomper, its jaws capable of crushing bone.  这条鲨鱼是一个可怕的猛咬者，它的颌骨能够压碎骨头。|例句：The old machine was a chomper, constantly grinding and crushing the materials. 这台旧机器是一个猛咬器，不断地研磨和压碎材料。|近义词：biter, chewer, grinder|反义词：licker (轻柔地舔)|名词，动词
