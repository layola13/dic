scyphus:/ˈsaɪfəs/| n. |天体；酒杯；饮器|A scyphus is a type of ancient Greek drinking cup, typically shallow and wide, with a single handle.  The term can also refer to a shallow, bowl-shaped crater on the moon or other celestial body.|例句：The museum displayed a beautiful example of an Athenian scyphus.  博物馆展示了一个雅典酒杯的精美例子。

The archaeologist discovered a scyphus among the ancient ruins. 考古学家在古遗址中发现了一个饮器。|近义词：cup, bowl, goblet|反义词：N/A
