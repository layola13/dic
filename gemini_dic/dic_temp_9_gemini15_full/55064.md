disregardable: /dɪs.rɪˈɡɑː.də.bəl/ | adj. | 可忽略的，不重要的 |  Something that is disregardable can be ignored or treated as unimportant.  It suggests that the thing in question is not significant enough to warrant attention or action. |

例句：The minor flaws in the report were disregardable.  报告中的一些小瑕疵是可以忽略的。

例句：His complaints are largely disregardable; he's just being difficult.  他的抱怨大多可以忽略；他只是在故意刁难。


近义词：negligible, insignificant, unimportant, trivial, minor, inconsequential

反义词：important, significant, crucial, vital, essential


词性：形容词
