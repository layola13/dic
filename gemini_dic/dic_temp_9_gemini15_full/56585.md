don'ts:/doʊnts/| n. |禁忌，不要做的事情 |Contractions of "do nots," referring to things that should be avoided or are prohibited.  It's often used in lists of instructions or rules. | Examples:  The don'ts of mountain climbing include ignoring weather warnings.  登山的禁忌包括忽略天气预警。The don'ts of public speaking include mumbling.  公开演讲的禁忌包括含糊不清。|近义词：prohibitions, restrictions, taboos |反义词：permissions, allowances


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We don't eat meat. = 我们不吃肉。
They don't go to school on Sundays. = 他们星期天不上学。


一般过去时:
I didn't see him yesterday. = 我昨天没看见他。
She didn't go to the party. = 她没去参加聚会。


现在完成时:
We haven't finished the project yet. = 我们还没完成这个项目。
They haven't called us back. = 他们还没给我们回电话。


过去完成时:
I hadn't eaten before I went to the party. = 我去参加聚会之前还没吃饭。
She hadn't seen him before that day. = 在那一天之前她还没见过他。


将来时:
I won't go to the meeting. = 我不会去参加会议。
He won't be there tomorrow. = 他明天不会在那里。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't have to work, I would travel the world. = 如果我不必工作，我会环游世界。
I wish I didn't have so much work. = 我希望我没有这么多工作。


被动语态:
The rules weren't followed. = 规则没有被遵守。
The problem wasn't solved. = 问题没有被解决。


疑问句:
Don't you like pizza? = 你不喜欢披萨吗？
Doesn't she know the answer? = 她不知道答案吗？


否定句:
I don't want to go. = 我不想去。
He doesn't understand. = 他不明白。


PHRASE->
Don't worry. = 别担心。
Don't be silly. = 别傻了。
Don't mention it. = 不客气。
Don't forget. = 别忘了。
Don't mind me. = 别管我。
