plywood:/ˈplaɪwʊd/| n. |胶合板|A sheet material made of thin layers of wood glued together with the grains of adjacent layers at right angles to one another.  This construction gives plywood greater strength and stability than solid wood of the same thickness.  It's widely used in construction, furniture making, and other applications.|The bookcase is made of plywood.|近义词：layered wood, laminated wood|反义词：solid wood
