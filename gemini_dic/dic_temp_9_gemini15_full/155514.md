philanthropistic: /ˌfɪlænˈθrɒpɪstɪk/ | adj. | 博爱的；慈善的；仁慈的 | Relating to or characteristic of philanthropy; benevolent.  | The organization's philanthropic activities include providing food and shelter to the homeless. 这个组织的慈善活动包括为无家可归者提供食物和住所。 | 近义词：charitable, benevolent, altruistic | 反义词：selfish, egoistic, miserly
