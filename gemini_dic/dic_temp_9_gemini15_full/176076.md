ravishingly:/ˈrævɪʃɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人倾倒地；迷人地；极其美丽地 |  extremely attractive or beautiful in a way that is captivating;  |例句：She looked ravishingly beautiful in her wedding dress. 她穿着婚纱，美得令人倾倒。

例句：The sunset was ravishingly beautiful, painting the sky in vibrant hues. 日落美得令人惊叹，天空被染成鲜艳的色彩。


近义词：stunningly, breathtakingly, captivatingly, enchantingly, divinely


反义词：unattractively, repulsively, uglily
