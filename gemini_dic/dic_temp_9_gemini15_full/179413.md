redubbed:/ˌriːˈdʌbd/| vt.|重新配音，重新命名|To record new dialogue or sound effects for (a film, television program, etc.).  It can also mean to give a new name or title to something.|例句：The film was redubbed in several languages. 这部电影被翻译成几种语言重新配音了。

The old documentary was redubbed with a new narration. 这部旧纪录片用新的旁白重新配音了。|近义词：revoice, rerecord|反义词：leave unaltered
