nonfossil: /ˈnɑːnˈfɑːsɪl/ | adj. |非化石的；非矿物燃料的 | Not derived from fossilized organic matter.  This term is primarily used to describe energy sources and fuels.  |例句：The government is investing heavily in nonfossil energy sources such as solar and wind power. 政府正在大力投资太阳能和风能等非化石能源。

例句：We need to transition to a nonfossil fuel economy to combat climate change.我们需要向非化石燃料经济转型以应对气候变化。


近义词：renewable, alternative (referring to energy sources) | 反义词：fossil, conventional (referring to energy sources)
