preannexing: /ˌpriːæˈnɛksɪŋ/ | v. | 预先兼并；提前吞并 |  The act of annexing something beforehand or in anticipation of a future event.  It implies a proactive and often aggressive seizure of territory or assets before any formal claim or declaration. | 例句：The empire's preannexing of the neighboring territories sparked widespread outrage.  帝国提前吞并邻国领土的行为引发了广泛的愤怒。


The company's preannexing of the smaller firm secured its market dominance. 公司提前兼并小型公司巩固了其市场主导地位。| 近义词：preemptive annexation, prior seizure, advance appropriation | 反义词：ceding, relinquishing, surrendering |  动词
