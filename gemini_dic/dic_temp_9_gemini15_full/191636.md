schoolship:/ˈskuːlʃɪp/| n.|学校船；训练船|A schoolship is a ship used as a school, often for training young people in seafaring skills.  It may also be used for educational purposes more generally. |例句：The old schoolship was a popular tourist attraction. 这艘旧的学校船是一个受欢迎的旅游景点。  The schoolship provided hands-on training for aspiring sailors. 这艘训练船为有抱负的水手提供了实践培训。|近义词：training vessel, training ship|反义词：无
