layabouts:/ˈleɪəbaʊts/| n.|懒汉，游手好闲的人|A layabout is a person who is lazy and avoids work.  They are often idle and unproductive.|例句：He's become a complete layabout since he lost his job. 他自从失业后就变成了一个十足的懒汉。

例句：The layabouts spent their days lounging around the park.  那些懒汉整天在公园里闲逛。|近义词：loafer, idler, sluggard, good-for-nothing|反义词：hard worker, diligent, industrious|名词
