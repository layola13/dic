self-witness:/ˌsɛlfˈwɪtnəs/| n.|自我见证，亲身经历|A self-witness refers to an experience or event that someone has personally witnessed and can attest to from their own perspective.  It emphasizes the firsthand nature of the observation.  The term is often used in legal or religious contexts to denote personal testimony or firsthand knowledge of a fact or event.|例句：Her self-witness to the accident was crucial to the investigation.  她的车祸亲身经历对调查至关重要。  His self-witness of the miracle strengthened his faith. 他对奇迹的亲身经历加强了他的信仰。|近义词：personal testimony, firsthand account, direct experience|反义词：hearsay, secondhand account|名词
