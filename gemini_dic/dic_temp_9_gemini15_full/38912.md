confession:/kənˈfɛʃən/| n.|忏悔，承认，供认|The act of confessing; acknowledgment of a fault, crime, or wrongdoing; something confessed.  A formal statement admitting guilt to a crime.|例句：He made a full confession to the police. 他向警方做了全盘供认。

例句：Her confession shocked everyone in the courtroom.  她的认罪震惊了法庭上的所有人。


近义词：admission, avowal, disclosure, acknowledgment|反义词：denial, disavowal
