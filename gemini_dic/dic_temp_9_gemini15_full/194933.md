semi-intoxicated:/ˌsɛmiˌɪntɒksɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 微醺的，半醉的 | Slightly intoxicated; not completely drunk, but showing some signs of being under the influence of alcohol.  | Example: He was semi-intoxicated after only two glasses of wine. 他只喝了两杯酒就有点微醺了。| 近义词：tipsy, slightly drunk, buzzed | 反义词：sober, completely drunk
