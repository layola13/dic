kibbler:/ˈkɪblər/| n. |挑剔的人，吹毛求疵的人|A kibbler is a person who is excessively critical or fault-finding; someone who finds fault with everything.  They often focus on minor details and are difficult to please. |例句：He's such a kibbler; he complained about the smallest imperfection in the cake. 他真是个挑剔的人；他抱怨蛋糕上最小的瑕疵。|例句：The kibbler refused to sign off on the project until every comma was in its perfect place.  这个挑剔的人拒绝签署项目，直到每个逗号都放在正确的位置。|近义词：critic, fault-finder, nitpicker, quibbler|反义词：uncritical, complacent, easygoing|名词
