forceless:/ˈfɔːrsləs/ | adj. | 无力的；无效的；软弱的 | Lacking force, strength, or effectiveness.  Describes something that is weak, ineffective, or powerless. | 例句：His arguments were forceless and unconvincing.  他的论点无力且缺乏说服力。

例句：The forceless blow barely grazed his skin.  无力的一击几乎没有擦伤他的皮肤。


近义词：powerless, ineffective, weak, feeble, impotent

反义词：powerful, effective, strong, forceful, potent

词性：形容词
