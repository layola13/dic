fucktard:/ˈfʌktɑːrd/| n.|白痴，笨蛋|A highly offensive and insulting term for a stupid or incompetent person.  It combines "fuck" (a vulgar term expressing anger, contempt, or sexual intercourse) and "retard" (an outdated and offensive term for someone with intellectual disabilities). The word is extremely derogatory and should never be used.|Example:  He's such a fucktard; he can't even parallel park.|近义词：idiot, moron, imbecile, retard (offensive)|反义词：genius, intelligent, brilliant|
