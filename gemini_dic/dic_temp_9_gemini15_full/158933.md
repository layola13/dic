pluralizations:/ˌplʊrəlɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 名词复数化；复数形式 | The process of making a word plural; the state of being plural.  It encompasses the rules and variations involved in transforming singular nouns into their plural counterparts. This includes adding -s, -es, -ies, changing the vowel, or utilizing irregular forms. | Example: The pluralizations of "cat" and "child" are "cats" and "children," respectively.  名词“cat”和“child”的复数形式分别是“cats”和“children”。 | 近义词：plural formation, pluralization  | 反义词：singularization
