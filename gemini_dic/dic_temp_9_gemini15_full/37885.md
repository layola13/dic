complainer:/kəmˈpleɪnər/| n. |抱怨者，投诉者 |A complainer is someone who frequently expresses dissatisfaction or complains.  They often focus on negative aspects and rarely express appreciation. |Example: He's become a real complainer since his retirement.  他退休后变得非常爱抱怨。

Example:  She is known as a chronic complainer in the office.  她在办公室里以经常抱怨而闻名。


近义词: grumbler, whiner, critic, fault-finder |反义词:praiser, complimenter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The complainer constantly criticizes the service. = 抱怨者不断批评服务。
That complainer always finds fault with everything. = 那个抱怨者总是对一切吹毛求疵。


一般过去时:
The complainer voiced his dissatisfaction yesterday. = 抱怨者昨天表达了他的不满。
The elderly complainer complained about the noise last night. = 那位上了年纪的抱怨者昨晚抱怨噪音。


现在完成时:
He has been a complainer all his life. = 他一辈子都是个爱抱怨的人。
She has complained to the manager several times. = 她已经向经理投诉了好几次。


过去完成时:
The complainer had already lodged a complaint before I arrived. = 我到达之前，抱怨者已经提出了投诉。
Before the meeting, the complainer had aired his grievances. = 会议之前，抱怨者已经表达了他的不满。


将来时:
He will be a complainer even after he gets what he wants. = 即使得到他想要的东西，他仍然会抱怨。
She will likely complain about the food again tomorrow. = 她明天很可能会再次抱怨食物。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't such a complainer, he would have more friends. = 如果他不那么爱抱怨，他就会有更多朋友。
I wish that complainer would stop bothering everyone. = 我希望那个抱怨者能停止打扰所有人。


被动语态:
The service was criticized by the complainer. = 服务受到了抱怨者的批评。
The problem was brought to light by a persistent complainer. = 一个坚持不懈的抱怨者使问题暴露出来。


疑问句:
Is he a habitual complainer? = 他是一个习惯性抱怨者吗？
Who is the main complainer in this situation? = 在这种情况下，主要的抱怨者是谁？


否定句:
He is not a complainer, he's just expressing his opinion. = 他不是一个爱抱怨的人，他只是在表达他的意见。
She wasn't a complainer before the accident. = 事故发生前，她不是一个爱抱怨的人。
