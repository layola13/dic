pluviometry:/ˌpluːviˈɒmɪtri/ | n. |雨量测量；测雨术 |The measurement of rainfall.  Pluviometry involves the collection and analysis of rainfall data to understand precipitation patterns and amounts. |Example:  The study relied heavily on pluviometry data collected over the past decade.  这项研究很大程度上依赖于过去十年收集的雨量数据。|近义词：rain measurement, rainfall measurement |反义词：None  |名词
