corrigent:/ˈkɒrɪdʒənt/| adj.|改正的；纠正的；矫正的|  Something that is corrigent is capable of correcting or rectifying something else, often an error or fault. It implies a power to amend or improve a situation.|The teacher's corrigent remarks helped the student understand their mistake.  老师的纠正性评语帮助学生理解了他们的错误。|近义词：corrective, remedial, amendatory|反义词：incorrigible, uncorrectable
