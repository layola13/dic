shaman:/ˈʃɑːmən/| n.|萨满，巫师|A shaman is a person regarded as having access to, and influence over, the spiritual world, often through trance or other altered states of consciousness. Shamans are found in many indigenous cultures around the world and perform rituals, healing ceremonies, and other spiritual practices.  |例句：The shaman performed a ritual to cure the sick child. 萨满进行了一场仪式来治愈生病的孩子。  The tribe consulted their shaman before making important decisions. 部落在做出重要决定之前咨询了他们的萨满。|近义词：medicine man, witch doctor, sorcerer, mystic|反义词：priest (in a formal religious context)|名词
