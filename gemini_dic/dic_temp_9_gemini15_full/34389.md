classily:/ˈklæsɪfaɪ/| vt.|分类，分级|To arrange or organize into classes or categories according to shared qualities or characteristics.|The librarian classily the books by author and subject.|近义词：categorize, sort, group, arrange|反义词：jumble, mix, confuse|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She classifies her stamps by country of origin. = 她按原产国对她的邮票进行分类。
The scientist classifies the species based on their DNA. = 科学家根据它们的DNA对物种进行分类。

一般过去时:
He classified the documents according to their importance. = 他根据重要性对文件进行了分类。
The teacher classified the students into different ability groups. = 老师把学生分成不同能力的组。

现在完成时:
She has classified all the data for the report. = 她已经为报告整理好所有数据了。
We have classified the minerals according to their hardness. = 我们已经根据矿物的硬度对它们进行了分类。

过去完成时:
They had classified the evidence before presenting it in court. = 他们在法庭上出示证据之前已经对证据进行了分类。
The researchers had classified the plants before beginning their study. = 研究人员在开始研究之前已经对植物进行了分类。

将来时:
He will classify the fossils he finds on the expedition. = 他将对在探险中发现的化石进行分类。
The team will classify the data using a new algorithm. = 该团队将使用一种新的算法对数据进行分类。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would classify the photographs properly. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会正确地对照片进行分类。
It would be helpful if we classified the problems by severity. = 如果我们按严重程度对问题进行分类，将会有所帮助。

被动语态:
The items were classified according to size and weight. = 这些物品是根据大小和重量进行分类的。
The data is being classified using a sophisticated software program. = 数据正在使用复杂的软件程序进行分类。

疑问句:
How did he classify the different types of rocks? = 他是如何对不同类型的岩石进行分类的？
On what basis will the artifacts be classified? = 文物将根据什么标准进行分类？

否定句:
He didn't classify the information correctly. = 他没有正确地对信息进行分类。
They haven't yet classified all the specimens collected. = 他们还没有对收集到的所有样本进行分类。
