saddleries:/ˈsædləriːz/| n.|马具店；马具制造业|The business or workshop of a saddler;  the goods made by a saddler.  It refers to either the place where saddles and other horse riding equipment are made or sold, or the industry as a whole.|例句：He inherited his father's saddlery business. 他继承了他父亲的马具店生意。|例句：The old saddlery still produces high-quality leather goods. 那家老马具店仍然生产高质量的皮具。|近义词：harness shop, tack shop|反义词：无
