condensing:/kənˈdensɪŋ/ | v. adj. | 凝结；浓缩；简略 | To change from a gas or vapor to a liquid; to make something more concise. |The steam condensed on the cold window.  蒸汽在冰冷的窗户上凝结了。|The professor condensed his lecture into a concise summary. 教授将他冗长的讲座浓缩成简洁的摘要。| liquefying, solidifying | expanding, diluting | 动词，形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The steam condenses on the cold surface. = 蒸汽在冰冷的表面上凝结。
He condenses his ideas into short sentences. = 他将他的想法浓缩成简短的句子。

一般过去时:
The water vapor condensed into rain. = 水蒸气凝结成雨。
She condensed the report for her boss. = 她为老板浓缩了报告。

现在完成时:
The fog has condensed into droplets. = 雾已凝结成水滴。
He has condensed his novel into a screenplay. = 他已将他的小说改编成电影剧本。

过去完成时:
The air had condensed before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前空气已经凝结。
She had condensed the lengthy document before submitting it. = 在提交之前，她已经浓缩了冗长的文件。

将来时:
The gas will condense at lower temperatures. = 气体将在较低的温度下凝结。
He will condense his presentation for better clarity. = 他将浓缩他的演示文稿以提高清晰度。

虚拟语气:
If the temperature dropped, the water vapor would condense. = 如果温度下降，水蒸气就会凝结。
I wish he had condensed his speech; it was too long. = 我希望他缩短了他的演讲；它太长了。

被动语态:
The gas is condensed by cooling. = 气体通过冷却而凝结。
The report was condensed for brevity. = 报告为了简洁而被浓缩。

疑问句:
Does the water vapor condense easily? = 水蒸气容易凝结吗？
Will the meeting be condensed? = 会议会被浓缩吗？

否定句:

He did not condense his argument sufficiently. = 他没有充分浓缩他的论点。
