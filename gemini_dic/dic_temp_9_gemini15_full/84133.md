half-minded:/ˌhæfˈmaɪndɪd/| adj. |优柔寡断的，犹豫不决的 |Hesitant or undecided; lacking firmness of purpose.|例句：He was half-minded about going to the party, unsure if he had the energy. 他对于参加聚会犹豫不决，不确定自己是否有精力。

例句：She gave a half-minded answer, suggesting she wasn't fully committed to her decision. 她给出了一个犹豫不决的答案，暗示她并没有完全致力于她的决定。

近义词：indecisive, irresolute, wavering, hesitant

反义词：decisive, resolute, determined, unwavering
