hier:/ˈhɪər/| adv.|这里，此处|Here indicates a location close to the speaker.|例句：The book is here.  书在这里。|近义词：hereby, hither|反义词：there


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The book is here. = 书在这里。
My keys are here. = 我的钥匙在这里。


一般过去时:
The cat was here a moment ago. = 猫刚才在这里。
We were here yesterday. = 我们昨天在这里。


现在完成时:
The package has been here for hours. = 包裹在这里好几个小时了。
They have been here since morning. = 他们从早上就在这里了。


过去完成时:
The meeting had already finished before I got here. = 我到的时候会议已经结束了。
She had been here many times before. = 她以前来过这里很多次。


将来时:
I will be here tomorrow. = 我明天会在这里。
The meeting will be held here next week. = 会议下周将在这里举行。


虚拟语气:
If I were here, I would help you. = 如果我在这儿，我会帮助你。
I wish I were here with you. = 我希望我和你在一起。


被动语态:
The decision was made here. = 决定在这里做出。
The meeting is being held here. = 会议正在这里举行。


疑问句:
Is the doctor here? = 医生在这里吗？
Are you here? = 你在这里吗？


否定句:
The dog isn't here. = 狗不在那儿。
He wasn't here yesterday. = 他昨天不在那儿。


PHRASE->
be here = 在这里
get here = 到达这里
right here = 就在这里
from here = 从这里
here and there = 到处
