dribbly:/ˈdrɪbli/| adj.| 滴落的；稀薄的；不成形的；不稳定的 |  Describes something that is wet and tends to drip or flow slowly; also can describe something weak, unsteady, or poorly formed.  |例句：The paint was dribbly and needed another coat. 这颜料稀薄，需要再涂一层。

The baby's food was all dribbly. 宝宝的食物都滴得到处都是。


近义词：droopy, sloppy, runny, watery, weak, unsteady |反义词：firm, solid, strong, stable


词性:形容词
