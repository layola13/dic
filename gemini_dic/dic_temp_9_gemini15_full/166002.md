prepended:/prɪˈpɛndɪd/| adj.|前置的，附加在前面的|Added to the beginning of something.|The prepended prefix changed the meaning of the word.  前缀的添加改变了单词的含义。|近义词：added, prefixed, attached|反义词：appended,suffixed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The prefix is prepended to the word. = 前缀加在单词前面。
A new chapter is prepended to the book. = 书籍前面增加了一个新的章节。

一般过去时:
He prepended a note to the document. = 他在文件前面添加了一则说明。
They prepended the address to the email. = 他们在邮件前面添加了地址。


现在完成时:
We have prepended the necessary information. = 我们已经添加了必要的信息。
She has prepended a disclaimer to her article. = 她已在文章开头添加了免责声明。


过去完成时:
He had prepended the data before analysis. = 他在分析之前已经添加了数据。
They had prepended the introduction before the main text. = 他们在正文之前已经添加了引言。


将来时:
I will prepend the date to the file name. = 我将把日期添加到文件名前面。
We will prepend a warning message to the application. = 我们将在应用程序前面添加警告消息。


虚拟语气:
If I were to prepend a comment, I would choose this one. = 如果我要添加注释，我会选择这个。
I wish I had prepended my name to the document. = 我真希望我在文档前面加上了我的名字。


被动语态:
The introduction was prepended to the report. = 引言被添加到报告的开头。
The subject line was prepended to the email message. = 主题行被添加到邮件消息的前面。


疑问句:
Was the preface prepended to the book? =  序言加在了书的前面吗？
Is the date prepended to the filename? = 日期加在了文件名前面吗？


否定句:
The name was not prepended to the list. = 名字没有添加到列表的前面。
The chapter was not prepended to the book. = 章并没有添加到书的前面。


PHRASE->
prepended to = 附加到…前面
prepended with = 以…开头
prepended a note = 添加注释
prepended the file = 在前面添加文件
prepended the data = 在前面添加数据
