fablers:/ˈfeɪblərz/| n.|说谎者；编造故事的人；寓言作者|A fabler is someone who tells fables or makes up stories, often untrue ones.  They can also be writers of fables.|例句：The old man was a renowned fabler, spinning tales of mythical creatures and faraway lands.  这位老人是一位著名的说谎者，编造着关于神话生物和遥远土地的故事。|例句：Many children's books feature fablers who use animals to teach moral lessons. 许多儿童读物都以寓言作者为特色，他们用动物来教导道德教训。|近义词：liar, storyteller, mythmaker, fabulist|反义词：truth-teller, honest person|名词
