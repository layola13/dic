encryptation:/ɪnˌkrɪpteɪʃən/ | n. |加密，加 密法 |The process of converting information or data into a code, especially to prevent unauthorized access.  This involves using an algorithm to transform readable data into an unreadable format. |Example:  The encryptation of sensitive data is crucial for maintaining privacy and security. 加密敏感数据对于维护隐私和安全至关重要。 |近义词：encryption, encoding, scrambling |反义词：decryption, decoding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Data encryption protects sensitive information. = 数据加密保护敏感信息。
The system encrypts all communications automatically. = 系统自动加密所有通信。


一般过去时:
They encrypted the message before sending it. = 他们发送信息前对其进行了加密。

现在完成时:
