Despite our best efforts, she remained impersuadable. = 尽管我们尽了最大努力，她仍然固执己见。


近义词: unpersuadable, intractable, obstinate, stubborn, inflexible, adamant


反义词: persuadable, amenable, compliant, yielding, tractable
