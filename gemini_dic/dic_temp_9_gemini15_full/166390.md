prerequisite:/ˈpriːˌrekwɪzɪt/| n. |先决条件，必备条件 |A prerequisite is something that is required as a prior condition for something else to happen or exist.  It emphasizes the necessity of the condition.  |Example:  A college degree is a prerequisite for many jobs.  大学学位是许多工作的先决条件。

A passing grade in the prerequisite course is required to enroll in this advanced class. 通过先修课程的及格分数是注册这门高级课程的必修条件。|近义词：requirement, necessity, condition, pre-requisite |反义词：optional, unnecessary, dispensable |名词
