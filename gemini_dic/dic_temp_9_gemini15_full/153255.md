pen:/pen/| n.|钢笔，笔；书写工具|A pen is a writing instrument used to apply ink to a surface, usually paper, to create marks.|I wrote the letter with a pen. 我用钢笔写的信。|pencil, stylus|crayon, brush|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I use a pen to write. = 我用钢笔写字。
She writes with a red pen. = 她用红笔写字。

一般过去时:
He wrote a letter with a pen. = 他用钢笔写了一封信。
They signed the contract with a pen. = 他们用钢笔签署了合同。

现在完成时:
I have written many letters with this pen. = 我用这支钢笔写过许多信。
She has used that pen for years. = 她已经用了那支笔好几年了。

过去完成时:
He had written the report before the meeting. = 他在会议之前已经写好了报告。(这里与pen关联性较弱，仅作语法示例)
She had finished writing the essay with her favorite pen. = 她用她最喜欢的钢笔写完了论文。


将来时:
I will write a letter with a new pen. = 我将用一支新钢笔写信。
They will sign the document with a fountain pen. = 他们将用钢笔签署文件。

虚拟语气:
If I had a pen, I would write you a note. = 如果我有一支笔，我会给你写个便条。
I wish I had a better pen for this calligraphy. = 我希望我有更好的笔来写这幅书法。

被动语态:
The letter was written with a blue pen. = 这封信是用蓝笔写的。
The contract will be signed with a pen. = 合同将用钢笔签署。

疑问句:
Do you have a pen? = 你有笔吗？
Which pen do you prefer? = 你更喜欢哪支笔？

否定句:
I don't have a pen. = 我没有笔。
He didn't use a pen to sign the form. = 他没有用笔签署表格。
