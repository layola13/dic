lost:/lɒst/ /lɔːst/| adj., v. (past participle of lose)| 迷路的；失去的；困惑的 |  Describes something that is no longer possessed, or a state of being disoriented or confused.  It can also be used as the past participle of the verb "lose".|例句：I lost my keys. 我丢了钥匙。
The lost dog was eventually found. 那只迷路的狗最终找到了。

例句：I am lost in this city. 我在这个城市迷路了。
He felt lost and alone after his wife died.  在他妻子去世后，他感到迷茫和孤单。


近义词：missing, gone, bewildered, perplexed, confused|反义词：found, recovered, oriented, clear


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I lose my temper easily. = 我很容易发脾气。
We lose the game. = 我们输了比赛。

一般过去时:
I lost my wallet yesterday. = 我昨天丢了钱包。
She lost her job last month. = 她上个月丢了工作。

现在完成时:
I have lost my appetite. = 我食欲不振。
They have lost their way. = 他们迷路了。

过去完成时:
I had lost all hope. = 我已经失去了所有希望。
We had lost our way before we found the map. = 我们在找到地图之前就已经迷路了。

将来时:
I will lose my patience soon. = 我很快就会失去耐心。
They will lose the election. = 他们会输掉选举。

虚拟语气:
If I lost my job, I would be very upset. = 如果我丢了工作，我会非常沮丧。
I wish I hadn't lost my phone. = 我真希望我没有丢我的手机。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
