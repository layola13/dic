dissolution:/ˌdɪsəˈluːʃən/| n. |解散；溶解；终止；瓦解|The breaking up or termination of something; the process of dissolving something into a liquid.  It can refer to the ending of a marriage, a partnership, a company, or an assembly, as well as the chemical process of dissolving a substance. |例句：The dissolution of the marriage was amicable.  婚姻的结束是和平的。

例句：The dissolution of the sugar in water was rapid. 糖在水中的溶解很快。

近义词：disbandment, disintegration, termination, breakdown, liquefaction

反义词：formation, establishment, consolidation, unification
