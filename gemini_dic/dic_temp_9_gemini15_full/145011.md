overadorned:/ˌoʊvərəˈdɔːrnd/| adj. |装饰过度的；过于华丽的 | excessively adorned; ornamented to excess.  The overadorned cake was almost too beautiful to eat.  |近义词：overdecorated, ornate, flamboyant, gaudy |反义词：plain, simple, understated


例句：

The overadorned Christmas tree was a dazzling spectacle, but it was difficult to see the presents underneath. = 装饰过度的圣诞树是一场令人眼花缭乱的景象，但很难看到下面的礼物。

Her overadorned gown, with its excessive lace and embroidery, was more distracting than elegant. = 她装饰过度的礼服，上面装饰着过多的蕾丝和刺绣，与其说是优雅，不如说是分散注意力。


词源：

* **over-**:  prefix indicating "excessive" or "too much"
* **adorn**: verb meaning "to decorate"  (from Old French *adurner, from Latin *ad- "to" + *ornāre "to equip, furnish")
* **-ed**: past participle suffix, forming an adjective


用法说明：

overadorned 作为形容词，主要用来描述装饰过多的东西，通常带有负面含义，暗示装饰过多以至于失去了美感或实用性。  它可以用来形容各种物体，包括服装、建筑、家具、珠宝等等。
