commemoration:/ˌkɒməˈmɔːreɪʃn/| /ˌkɑːməˈmɔːreɪʃn/ | n. |纪念，庆祝 |The act of remembering and showing respect for someone or something, especially through a ceremony or celebration.  It involves the preservation of the memory of a person or event, often with a sense of honor or respect. |例句：The ceremony was a solemn commemoration of the fallen soldiers.  这次仪式庄严地纪念阵亡的士兵。

例句：The city held a commemoration to mark the 50th anniversary of the earthquake.  这个城市举行纪念活动以纪念地震50周年。


近义词：celebration, remembrance, memorial, tribute |反义词：forgetfulness, oblivion


词源：来自拉丁语 *commemoratio,  由 *com- (together) 和 *memorare (to remember) 构成。
