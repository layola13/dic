overdomesticate: /ˌoʊvərˈdoʊmɛstɪkeɪt/ | v. |过度驯化；过度家养|To domesticate to an excessive degree, resulting in negative consequences for the animal or plant. This can lead to health problems, reduced adaptability, and dependence on human intervention.|例句：Overdomesticating chickens has led to breeds that are highly susceptible to disease and require constant human care.  过度驯化鸡导致了一些品种容易患病并且需要持续的人工照料。|近义词：overbreed, overcultivate |反义词：leave wild, let run wild
