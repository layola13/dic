relaxing:/rɪˈlæksɪŋ/ | adj. |令人放松的，轻松的 |  Relaxing describes something that makes you feel calm and less stressed. It can refer to activities, environments, or even people.  | Example:  A relaxing bath after a long day is exactly what I need.  一天辛苦的工作后，泡个轻松的澡正是我需要的。|近义词：calming, soothing, restful |反义词：stressful, tense, anxious |形容词
