pickins:/ˈpɪkɪnz/| n.|少量；可怜的收获；微薄的收入| a small amount or quantity, especially of something harvested or collected; meager earnings.  |例句：After a poor harvest, the pickings were slim.  = 收成不好，收获甚微。

例句：The pickings were scarce in the job market. = 就业市场上的机会很少。


近义词：scraps, remnants, leavings, gleanings

反义词：abundance, plenty, bounty


词源：源自动词"pick"，指收集或拾取少量的东西。  The word evolved from the act of picking up small amounts of something, such as berries or leftover food.
