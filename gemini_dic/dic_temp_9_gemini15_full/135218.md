nonowning: /ˌnɒnˈoʊnɪŋ/ | adj. | 不拥有财产的，非所有权的 |  Describing something or someone that does not own property or a particular asset.  It emphasizes the lack of ownership. |  例句：The nonowning class faced significant challenges in accessing resources. 不拥有财产的阶级在获取资源方面面临着巨大的挑战。

例句：He held a nonowning position in the company, meaning he had no shares. 他在公司里担任的是非所有权职位，这意味着他没有股份。


近义词：propertyless, ownerless, without ownership


反义词：owning, proprietary


词性：形容词
