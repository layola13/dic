ostensory: /ˈɒstɛnsəri/ | adj. | 显示的；示意的；明显的 | Relating to or of the nature of ostension; serving to point out or indicate.  It describes something that demonstrates or points to something else, often non-verbally. | 例句：The teacher used an ostensive definition, pointing to the object while saying the word.  老师用指示定义，指着物体同时说出单词。


The ostensive evidence was undeniable; the fingerprints were right there.  明显的证据不容否认；指纹就在那里。| 近义词：demonstrative, indicative, illustrative, explicit | 反义词：implicit, indirect, ambiguous
