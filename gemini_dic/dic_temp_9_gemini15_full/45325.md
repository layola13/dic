cuminol:/ˈkjuːmɪnɒl/| n. |枯茗醛；孜然醛|Cuminol is an organic compound, an aldehyde derived from cumin. It is a colorless liquid with a strong, characteristic cumin-like odor. It is found in the essential oil of cumin and is responsible for much of its aroma. |The essential oil of cumin contains cuminol, giving it its distinctive aroma.|近义词:cuminaldehyde|反义词: N/A
