reconvert:/ˌriːkənˈvɜːrt/| vt.|重新转换，再转换|To convert something back to its original form or a previous state.|The company decided to reconvert the factory to produce bicycles instead of cars. 公司决定将工厂重新改造成生产自行车而不是汽车。|近义词：revert, transform, change back|反义词：convert
