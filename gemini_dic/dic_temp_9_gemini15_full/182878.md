reperceive:/ˌriːpərˈsiːv/| vt.|重新感知，重新认识|To perceive again; to perceive differently or in a new light.  It implies a shift in understanding or interpretation.|例句：After years of misunderstanding, he had to reperceive his relationship with his father.  多年误解之后，他不得不重新审视与父亲的关系。

例句：I reperceived the painting after learning about the artist's life.  在了解了这位艺术家的生平后，我对这幅画有了新的认识。


近义词：reconsider, reinterpret, re-evaluate|反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret|动词
