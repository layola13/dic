excels:/ɪkˈsɛlz/| v.|擅长，优秀，胜过|To surpass others in ability or achievement; to be outstanding or superior.|She excels in mathematics. 她擅长数学。He excels at playing the piano. 他钢琴弹得非常好。|surpasses, surpasses, outperforms|fails, lags, underperforms|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He excels at sports. = 他擅长运动。
She excels in her studies. = 她在学习方面很优秀。

一般过去时:
He excelled in his career. = 他在事业上取得了优异的成就。
She excelled at the competition. = 她在那次比赛中表现出色。

现在完成时:
He has excelled in his field for many years. = 多年来，他在他的领域一直表现出色。
She has excelled at overcoming challenges. = 她一直很擅长克服挑战。

过去完成时:
He had excelled in school before he went to college. = 上大学之前，他在学校表现出色。
She had excelled at art since childhood. = 她从小就擅长艺术。

将来时:
He will excel in his future endeavors. = 他将在未来的努力中取得成功。
She will excel if she works hard. = 如果她努力工作，她将会表现出色。

虚拟语气:
If he excelled at his job, he would get a promotion. = 如果他工作出色，他就会得到晋升。
I wish I excelled at public speaking. = 我希望我擅长公开演讲。

被动语态:
His work was excelled by few. = 他的作品很少有人能超越。
Her talent is excelled only by her hard work. = 她的天赋只有她的努力才能超越。


疑问句:
Does he excel at anything in particular? = 他有什么特别的擅长之处吗？
Does she excel at any sports? = 她擅长任何运动吗？


否定句:
He doesn't excel at cooking. = 他不擅长烹饪。
She didn't excel in that exam. = 她在那次考试中没有取得好成绩。


PHRASE->
excel at = 擅长于…
excel in = 在…方面表现出色
excel oneself = 超越自我
to excel in one's field = 在自己的领域表现出色
excel beyond expectation = 超出预期地优秀
