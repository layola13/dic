efficacious: /ˌɛfɪˈkeɪʃəs/ | adj. |有效的；灵验的 | Producing the intended result; effective.  |The new drug proved efficacious in treating the disease.  这种新药被证明对治疗这种疾病有效。| effective, potent, effectual | ineffective, futile, useless |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This medicine is efficacious for many patients. = 这种药对许多病人有效。
The new marketing strategy is efficacious in boosting sales. = 新的营销策略有效地促进了销售。


一般过去时:
The treatment was efficacious in relieving his pain. = 治疗有效地缓解了他的疼痛。
The old remedy proved efficacious for her cough. = 这种旧疗法对她的咳嗽很有效。


现在完成时:
This method has proved efficacious in solving the problem. = 这种方法已被证明在解决这个问题上有效。
The new policy has been efficacious in reducing crime rates. = 新政策在降低犯罪率方面卓有成效。


过去完成时:
The surgery had been efficacious in saving his life. = 手术已经有效地挽救了他的生命。
They had found that the previous treatment had been efficacious. = 他们发现之前的治疗是有效的。


将来时:
This new technology will be efficacious in improving efficiency. = 这项新技术将有效提高效率。
We believe this approach will be efficacious in reaching our goals. = 我们相信这种方法将有效帮助我们实现目标。


虚拟语气:
If the treatment were efficacious, he would recover quickly. = 如果治疗有效，他会很快康复。
I wish the medicine were more efficacious. = 我希望这种药更有效。


被动语态:
The efficacy of the drug was demonstrated in the clinical trial. = 该药物的疗效在临床试验中得到了证实。
The problem was efficaciously solved by the team. = 问题被团队有效地解决了。


疑问句:
Is this treatment efficacious for all patients? = 这种治疗对所有病人都有效吗？
Is the new strategy efficacious in achieving our goals? = 新策略在实现我们的目标上有效吗？


否定句:
The treatment was not efficacious for her condition. = 这种治疗对她这种情况无效。
The previous attempts were not efficacious in solving the problem. = 此前的尝试未能有效解决问题。
