caramelise:/ˈkærəməlaɪz/ | v. |焦糖化；使…变成焦糖色 |To heat sugar until it melts and turns brown, often with the addition of butter or cream to create a caramel sauce or coating.  |The chef caramelised the onions until they were golden brown and sweet.  厨师把洋葱焦糖化直到它们变成金棕色且很甜。|  brown, glaze | uncaramelise (not a common antonym, as it's more about reversing the process than having a direct opposite)
