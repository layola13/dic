rattlingly: /ˈrætlɪŋli/ | adv. | 嘎吱嘎吱地；震动地；急速地；喧闹地 | In a rattling manner; with a rattling sound; rapidly and noisily.  | 例句：The old wagon rattled along the bumpy road.  = 那辆旧马车嘎吱嘎吱地沿着颠簸的路行驶。 The train rattled past the station. = 火车轰隆地驶过车站。| 近义词：noisily, rapidly, shakily, clatteringly | 反义词：quietly, smoothly, silently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The old car rattles annoyingly. = 这辆旧车烦人地嘎吱作响。
The wind rattles the windows. = 风使窗户发出嘎吱声。

一般过去时:
The cutlery rattled as he nervously set the table. = 他紧张地摆桌子时，餐具叮当作响。
The train rattled noisily through the countryside. = 火车喧闹地穿过乡村。

现在完成时:
The old house has rattled and creaked for years. = 这座老房子已经吱吱呀呀地响了好几年了。
The news has rattled the stock market. = 这条新闻震动了股市。

过去完成时:
The window had rattled violently before it finally broke. = 窗户剧烈震动后才最终破碎。
The old man had rattled off a list of names before he forgot them. = 老头子在忘记之前快速念出了一串名字。

将来时:
The earthquake will rattle the buildings. = 地震将会震动建筑物。
The engine will rattle if we don't fix it soon. = 如果我们不尽快修理，发动机就会发出嘎吱声。

虚拟语气:
If the car rattled less, I would drive it more often. = 如果这辆车震动小一些，我会更经常开它。
I wish the old clock didn't rattle so loudly. = 我希望旧钟不要响那么大声。

被动语态:
The windows were rattled by the strong wind. = 强风使窗户震动。
The train was rattled by the bumpy tracks. = 火车被颠簸的轨道震动了。

疑问句:
Does the old chair rattle when you sit on it? = 你坐在旧椅子上时它会嘎吱作响吗？
Does the engine rattle excessively? = 发动机是否过度震动？

否定句:
The car didn't rattle at all during the journey. = 在旅途中，汽车一点也没有发出嘎吱声。
The new machine doesn't rattle like the old one. = 新机器不像旧机器那样发出嘎吱声。
