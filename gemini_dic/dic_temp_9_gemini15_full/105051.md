keratitis:/ˌkɛrəˈtaɪtɪs/| n. |角膜炎|Inflammation of the cornea, the transparent outer layer of the eye.  Symptoms can include pain, redness, blurred vision, and light sensitivity.  Various causes exist, including infections (bacterial, viral, fungal), autoimmune disorders, and injuries.|例句：The doctor diagnosed him with keratitis after examining his red and irritated eye.  医生在他检查了他红肿的眼睛后诊断他患有角膜炎。

例句：She experienced severe keratitis following a corneal abrasion. 她角膜擦伤后患上了严重的角膜炎。


近义词：corneal inflammation|反义词：None specifically, but "healthy cornea" could be considered an antonym.
