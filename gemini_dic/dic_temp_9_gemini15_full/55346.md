dist:/dɪst/| n. adj. v.|距离，远方；疏远的；使…疏远；使…烦恼|English: distance, remoteness; far-off; to cause separation or estrangement; to trouble or upset.|例句：The distance between the two cities is 200 miles. 这两座城市相距200英里。  The distant mountains were shrouded in mist. 远山笼罩在雾中。|近义词：distance, remoteness, separation|反义词：proximity, closeness, nearness|名词，形容词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The distance is too far. = 距离太远了。
This event disturbs me. = 这件事让我心烦。


一般过去时:
The journey distanced us from our families. = 这段旅程让我们远离了家人。
The news disturbed him deeply. = 这个消息深深地扰乱了他。


现在完成时:
The constant travel has distanced me from my friends. =  持续的旅行让我疏远了朋友们。
This problem has disturbed me for weeks. =  这个问题已经困扰我几周了。


过去完成时:
They had distanced themselves from the conflict. = 他们已经远离了冲突。
The noise had disturbed her sleep. = 噪音扰乱了她的睡眠。


将来时:
The new policy will distance the government from the people. = 新政策将使政府疏远人民。
The storm will disturb the flight schedule. = 暴风雨将扰乱航班时刻表。


虚拟语气:
If the distance were shorter, I would visit more often. = 如果距离近一些，我会更经常去拜访。
If I hadn't been disturbed, I would have finished the work on time. = 如果我没有被打扰，我就能按时完成工作了。


被动语态:
They were distanced by their different beliefs. = 他们因为不同的信仰而疏远了。
He was disturbed by the loud music. = 他被吵闹的音乐扰乱了。


疑问句:
What is the distance to the next town? = 到下一个镇的距离是多少？
What disturbed you so much? = 什么事让你如此不安？


否定句:
The distance didn't deter them. = 距离并没有阻止他们。
The noise didn't disturb me. = 噪音没有打扰我。
