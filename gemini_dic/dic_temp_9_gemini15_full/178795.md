recuperatory: /rɪˈkjuːpəreɪtəri/ | adj. |恢复的；复原的 |  Relating to or promoting recovery from illness or injury.  It emphasizes the process of regaining health or strength. |

例句：

The recuperatory period after surgery was longer than expected. 手术后的恢复期比预期的要长。

His recuperatory efforts focused on physiotherapy and a healthy diet. 他的恢复努力集中在物理治疗和健康饮食上。


近义词：restorative, rehabilitative, healing, convalescent

反义词：debilitating, weakening, deteriorating


词性: 形容词


词源:  Derived from the verb "recuperate," which comes from the Latin "recuperare," meaning "to recover," composed of "re-" (again) and "capere" (to take).  Thus, "recuperatory" literally means "relating to the act of taking back health or strength."
