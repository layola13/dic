kickstands: /ˈkɪkˌstændz/ | n. |撑脚架；支架 | A retractable support used to keep a bicycle or motorcycle upright when not in motion.  It is typically a metal or plastic leg that folds out from the frame. |例句：My bicycle's kickstand is broken, so I have to lean it against the wall.我的自行车支架坏了，所以我不得不把它靠在墙上。

例句：He carefully lowered the motorcycle onto its kickstand. 他小心地把摩托车放到它的支架上。


近义词：stand, support, prop | 反义词：None (antonyms are not directly applicable as it describes a physical object rather than a concept) |词性:名词
