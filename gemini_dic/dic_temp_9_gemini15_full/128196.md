neuration:/njʊˈreɪʃən/| n. |神经分布；脉络；纹理|The arrangement and distribution of nerves or veins in an organism or structure.  It often refers to the pattern of veins in a leaf.  The study of neuration can be important in plant taxonomy. |例句：The neuration of this leaf is characteristic of the species. 这片叶子的脉络是该物种的特征。|例句：The intricate neuration of the insect's wing was fascinating to observe.  昆虫翅膀上复杂的脉络令人着迷。|近义词：venation, nervature |反义词：无
