chemisette:/ʃɛmɪˈzɛt/| n. | 化米塞特（一种女式衬衫）| A chemisette is a woman's undergarment, typically a close-fitting, short-sleeved, high-necked blouse worn under a dress or bodice.  It's often made of lightweight fabric and can be decorative or purely functional.  In modern usage, it can also refer to a similar-styled blouse worn as outerwear.|She wore a delicate lace chemisette under her gown. 她穿着一件精致的蕾丝化米塞特在她的礼服下面。 The chemisette added a touch of elegance to her simple dress. 这件化米塞特为她简单的连衣裙增添了一丝优雅。|blouse, shirt, camisole|  (no direct antonym, but opposites could be a heavy jacket or sweater)|名词
