parboil:/ˈpɑːrbɔɪl/ | vt. | 半煮；烫；略煮 | To boil (food) partially, usually as a preliminary to further cooking.  | 例句：I parboiled the potatoes before roasting them.  我把土豆略煮后再烤。
                                                                                                  The chef parboiled the vegetables before adding them to the stir-fry.  厨师在把蔬菜加入炒菜之前先将它们略煮。| 近义词：simmer, blanch, scald | 反义词：boil thoroughly, roast, bake |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He parboils the pasta before adding the sauce. = 他在加酱汁之前先把意大利面略煮。
She parboils the vegetables for her salad. = 她为沙拉略煮蔬菜。


一般过去时:
They parboiled the rice until it was slightly soft. = 他们把米饭略煮到有点软。
I parboiled the carrots for the soup. = 我把胡萝卜略煮用于汤中。


现在完成时:
She has parboiled the potatoes for the stew. = 她已经为炖菜略煮了土豆。
He has parboiled the green beans for a quicker cooking time. = 他已经略煮青豆以缩短烹饪时间。


过去完成时:
The cook had parboiled the pasta before adding the pesto. = 厨师在加入青酱之前已经略煮了意大利面。
They had parboiled the chicken before grilling it. = 他们已经略煮了鸡肉然后再烤。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would parboil the vegetables. = 如果我有更多时间，我会先把蔬菜略煮。
I wish I had parboiled the potatoes; they're too hard. = 我真希望我之前把土豆略煮了；它们太硬了。


被动语态:
The potatoes were parboiled before being fried. = 土豆在油炸之前先被略煮。
The vegetables are parboiled to soften them. = 蔬菜被略煮以使其软化。


疑问句:

否定句:
I didn't parboil the pasta; I just boiled it. = 我没有略煮意大利面；我只是煮了它。
She didn't parboil the broccoli, and it was too crunchy. = 她没有略煮西兰花，所以它太脆了。
