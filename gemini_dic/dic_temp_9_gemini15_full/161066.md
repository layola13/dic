postcovid: /ˌpoʊstˈkoʊvɪd/ | adj. |新冠疫情后，疫情后的 | Relating to the period after the COVID-19 pandemic.  This term encompasses the lingering effects, both physical and societal, of the pandemic. |例句：Postcovid fatigue is a common symptom reported by many recovered patients. 新冠后疲劳是许多康复患者报告的常见症状。  The postcovid economic recovery is proving to be more challenging than initially anticipated. 新冠疫情后的经济复苏证明比最初预期的更具挑战性。|近义词：postepidemic, after-COVID |反义词：precovid, during-covid
