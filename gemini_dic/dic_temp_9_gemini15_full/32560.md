chipping-in: /ˈtʃɪpɪŋ ɪn/ | v. | 凑钱；参与；插手 | Contributing a small amount of money or effort to a common cause or project; to participate or become involved. |

例句：
We all chipped in to buy John a birthday present. = 我们大家一起凑钱给约翰买生日礼物。

They're chipping in to help with the renovations. = 他们正在帮忙一起装修。


近义词：contributing, participating, pitching in, joining in

反义词：withdrawing, abstaining, refusing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We chip in for lunch every Friday. = 我们每个星期五都一起凑钱买午餐。
They chip in to help their neighbor. = 他们一起帮助他们的邻居。

一般过去时:
We chipped in to buy a new coffee machine. = 我们一起凑钱买了一台新的咖啡机。
She chipped in with some helpful suggestions. = 她贡献了一些有帮助的建议。

现在完成时:
We have chipped in enough money for the trip. = 我们已经凑够了旅行的钱。
They have chipped in to support the local charity. = 他们一起捐款支持当地的慈善机构。

过去完成时:
They had chipped in all their savings before the project started. = 项目开始之前，他们已经捐出了所有的积蓄。
We had chipped in for the repairs before the landlord arrived. = 房东来之前，我们已经凑钱修理好了。

将来时:
We will chip in to help with the wedding expenses. = 我们将一起支付婚礼费用。
They will chip in for the office party. = 他们将一起为办公室聚会出钱。

虚拟语气:
If everyone chipped in, we could afford a better car. = 如果每个人都出钱，我们就能买一辆更好的车。
I wish they had chipped in earlier to help with the project. = 我希望他们早点参与进来帮助这个项目。

被动语态:
The cost of the repairs was chipped in by the tenants. = 修缮费用由租户们一起承担。
A significant amount of money was chipped in for the disaster relief. = 为灾难救济筹集了大量的资金。


疑问句:
Will you chip in for the party? = 你会为聚会出钱吗？
Are they chipping in for the new equipment? = 他们会为新设备出钱吗？

否定句:
He didn't chip in for the team dinner. = 他没有为团队晚餐出钱。
She refused to chip in for the office supplies. = 她拒绝为办公用品出钱。
