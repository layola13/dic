menopauses:/ˈmenəpɔːzɪz/ | n. | 绝经期（复数） | The natural cessation of menstruation in women, typically occurring between the ages of 45 and 55, marked by a decline in ovarian function and hormonal changes. It refers to the multiple instances or occurrences of the menopause process in different women or in a single woman over time. | Example:  The study examined the effects of different lifestyle factors on the experiences of menopauses in a diverse group of women.  这项研究考察了不同生活方式因素对不同女性绝经期体验的影响。|近义词：climacteric (指女性更年期，但范围更广)|反义词：无
