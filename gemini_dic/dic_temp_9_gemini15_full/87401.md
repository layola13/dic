herringbones:/ˈhɛrɪŋboʊnz/ | n. |人字形纹理，人字形图案| A pattern resembling the skeleton of a herring fish, consisting of parallel diagonal lines that intersect to form a series of V-shapes.  It's often used in textiles, flooring, and other design applications. | Example: The jacket featured a stylish herringbone pattern. 这件夹克衫采用时尚的人字形图案。 The new floor is laid in a herringbone pattern. 新地板采用人字形铺设。|近义词：chevron, zig-zag |反义词：plain, straight
