retaught:/ˌriːˈtɔːt/| v.|重新教；再教|To teach again or anew.  It implies a previous attempt at teaching that may not have been fully successful or complete.|例句：The teacher retaught the difficult concept to the students who hadn't understood it the first time.  老师重新讲解了那些第一次没理解的学生难以理解的概念。

例句：After failing the exam, she decided to retake the course and be retaught the material.  考试不及格后，她决定重修课程并重新学习教材。


近义词：reteach, re-educate, re-instruct

反义词：unlearn, forget

词性：动词
