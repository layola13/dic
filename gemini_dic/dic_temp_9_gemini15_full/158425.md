pleb:/plɛb/| n. |平民，下等人 |A pleb is a commoner, especially one of low social standing.  The term is often used derisively. | Example: He considered himself far superior to the plebs. 他认为自己远比平民高贵。|近义词：commoner, peasant, prole, riff-raff |反义词：aristocrat, noble, elite |名词
