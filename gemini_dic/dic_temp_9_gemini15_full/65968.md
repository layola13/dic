exaggeratingly: /ɪɡˈzædʒəreɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 过分地；夸张地 |  In an exaggerated manner; to an excessive degree.  | 例句：He described the incident exaggeratingly, making it sound far worse than it actually was. 他夸大其词地描述了这件事，使其听起来比实际情况糟糕得多。


例句：She was exaggeratingly enthusiastic about the new project, almost to the point of being annoying. 她对新项目过分热情，几乎到了令人讨厌的地步。


近义词： excessively, immoderately, hyperbolically


反义词：understatedly, minimally, subtly


词性:副词
