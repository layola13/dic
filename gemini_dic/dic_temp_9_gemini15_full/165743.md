prenegotiation: /ˌpriːnɪˌɡoʊʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 预先谈判；谈判准备 | The activities and discussions that occur before formal negotiations begin.  It involves groundwork, information gathering, and strategy development to enhance the chances of a successful outcome in the actual negotiation. | Example:  The prenegotiation phase included extensive research into the competitor's market position.  预先谈判阶段包括对竞争对手市场地位的广泛研究。

| Example:  Thorough prenegotiation is crucial for securing a favorable contract.  彻底的预先谈判对于获得有利的合同至关重要。| 近义词: preparation, groundwork, preliminary discussions | 反义词: final negotiation, conclusion | 名词
