diwali:/dɪˈwɑːliː/ | n. |排灯节，光明节 | The festival of Diwali, also known as Deepavali, is a five-day Hindu festival celebrated every year in autumn. It is celebrated by Hindus, Jains, Sikhs, and some Buddhists.  |  例句：Diwali is a time for family and friends to come together.  排灯节是家人和朋友聚在一起的时候。

Diwali celebrations include lighting up homes and temples with diyas (oil lamps), sharing sweets and gifts, performing prayers, and setting off fireworks.  排灯节的庆祝活动包括用迪亚（油灯）点亮家园和寺庙，分享糖果和礼物，进行祈祷，以及燃放烟花。 | 近义词：Deepavali | 反义词：无
