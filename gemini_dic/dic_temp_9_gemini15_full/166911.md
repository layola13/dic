presternum:/ˈprɛstɚnəm/| n. |前胸骨|The presternum is the anterior segment of the sternum, also known as the manubrium. It's the uppermost part of the breastbone and articulates with the clavicles and first ribs.  It plays a crucial role in protecting the heart and lungs.|例句：The doctor examined the patient's presternum for any signs of fracture.医生检查了病人的前胸骨是否有骨折迹象。|近义词：manubrium sterni|反义词：xiphoid process
