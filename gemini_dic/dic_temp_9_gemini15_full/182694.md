repackage:/ˌriːˈpækɪdʒ/| vt.|重新包装；重新组合；重新发布|To repackage something means to present it in a new or different way, often to make it more attractive or appealing to a target audience.  This can involve changing the packaging, the marketing, or the content itself.|例句：The company decided to repackage its product to attract a younger demographic. 公司决定重新包装其产品以吸引更年轻的人群。|例句：They repackaged the old software with a new interface and more features. 他们用新的界面和更多功能重新包装了旧软件。|近义词：rebrand, revamp, reformulate|反义词：leave as is, keep unchanged|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They repackage their products annually. = 他们每年都会重新包装他们的产品。
We repackage the data for better analysis. = 我们重新整理数据以便更好地分析。

一般过去时:
The company repackaged its image after the scandal. = 丑闻发生后，公司重塑了形象。
She repackaged her resume to highlight her skills. = 她重新包装了自己的简历以突出她的技能。

现在完成时:
They have repackaged the software three times this year. = 他们今年已经三次重新包装了这个软件。
We have repackaged our marketing strategy to reach a wider audience. = 我们已经重新调整了营销策略以接触更广泛的受众。

过去完成时:
They had repackaged the product before launching the new campaign. = 他们在推出新活动之前已经重新包装了产品。
She had repackaged her presentation before delivering it to the clients. = 她在向客户展示之前已经重新包装了她的演示文稿。

将来时:
We will repackage the software next month. = 我们下个月将重新包装该软件。
They will repackage the message to be more clear. = 他们将重新包装信息使其更清晰。

虚拟语气:
If they repackaged the product, it might sell better. = 如果他们重新包装产品，它可能会卖得更好。
I wish they had repackaged the website; it looks outdated. = 我希望他们已经重新设计了网站；它看起来过时了。

被动语态:
The product was repackaged to appeal to a wider market. = 该产品经过重新包装以吸引更广泛的市场。
The message is being repackaged for a more effective delivery. = 信息正在被重新包装以便更有效地传递。

疑问句:
Will they repackage the software before the launch? = 他们会在发布之前重新包装软件吗？
Have they repackaged the marketing materials yet? = 他们已经重新包装营销材料了吗？

否定句:
They did not repackage the product for the new market. = 他们没有为新市场重新包装产品。
We will not repackage the software without testing it first. = 我们不会在未经测试的情况下重新包装软件。
