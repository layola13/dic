envisagements:/ɪnˈvɪzɪdʒmənts/ | n. |设想；展望；想象 |The architect's envisagements for the new building were ambitious and innovative.  建筑师对新建筑的设想雄心勃勃，极具创新性。|近义词：conceptions, visions, imaginings |反义词：realities


envisagements 是envisage的名词形式，表示“设想，展望，想象”。它指对未来或尚未发生的事情进行的构想和规划。


英文解析:The plural form of the noun "envisagement," representing the act or process of forming a mental picture of something, especially something that might happen in the future.  It refers to plans, ideas, or visions conceived in the mind.


例句:

The company's envisagements for the next fiscal year included increased market share and expansion into new territories. 公司对下一财年的设想包括增加市场份额和拓展新领域。

His envisagements of a utopian society were detailed and compelling. 他对乌托邦社会的设想详尽而引人入胜。


近义词:

conceptions: 概念，设想
visions: 远景，景象
imaginings: 想象，幻想
projections: 预测，展望


反义词:

realities: 现实
facts: 事实


词性:名词 (plural noun)
