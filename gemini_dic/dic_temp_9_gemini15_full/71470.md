词源:
The word "flamethrower" is a compound word.  "Flame" comes from the Old French *flamme*, ultimately from the Latin *flamma*, meaning "flame."  "Thrower" comes from the Old English *þrowere*, meaning "one who throws."  Therefore, "flamethrower" literally means "a device that throws flames."
