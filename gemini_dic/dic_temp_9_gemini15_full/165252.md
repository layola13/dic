preinstruction:/ˌpriːɪnˈstrʌkʃən/| n. |预先指令，预先指示|Instructions given before a main task or process begins.  It refers to preparatory commands, guidelines, or information provided in advance to guide subsequent actions.|Example: The preinstruction for the experiment included detailed safety protocols.  实验的预先指示包括详细的安全规程。|近义词：advance instruction, preliminary instruction, prior guidance|反义词：postinstruction, afterthought
