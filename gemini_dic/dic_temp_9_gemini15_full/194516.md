self-schooling:/ˌsɛlfˈskuːlɪŋ/| n. | 自学，在家上学 | The act of educating oneself, often independently of formal schooling.  It involves self-directed learning, utilizing various resources and methods to acquire knowledge and skills. | Example:  He achieved a high level of proficiency through self-schooling in computer programming. 他通过自学计算机编程达到了很高的熟练程度。|近义词：home schooling, independent study, self-education |反义词：formal schooling, institutional learning |


USAGE->

He self-schools in history. = 他自学历史。
She self-schools her children. = 她在家教育她的孩子。


一般过去时:
He self-schooled himself in mathematics. = 他自学了数学。
They self-schooled their children for several years. = 他们在家教育了他们的孩子好几年。
