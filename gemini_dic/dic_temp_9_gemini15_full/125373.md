multimetallic:/ˌmʌltiˈmɛtəlɪk/| adj.|多金属的；多种金属的|Containing or composed of several metals.  The term often refers to alloys or materials with multiple metallic components.|例句：The multimetallic catalyst showed improved performance in the reaction.  多金属催化剂在反应中表现出改进的性能。

例句：This multimetallic ore contains gold, silver, and copper.这种多金属矿石含有金、银和铜。
