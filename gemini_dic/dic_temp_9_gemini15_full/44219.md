crocked:/ˈkrɒkt/| adj.| 弯曲的；破损的；受伤的；醉醺醺的 |Damaged, injured, or bent out of shape;  also, intoxicated.|例句：The old chair was crocked. 这把旧椅子坏了。|例句：He was crocked after drinking too much. 他喝醉了。|近义词：bent, broken, injured, drunk, intoxicated |反义词：straight, undamaged, uninjured, sober
