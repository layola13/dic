dasyurine:/ˌdæzɪˈjʊraɪn/| n.| 鼬科动物；（指）黄鼠狼| A dasyurine is a member of the Dasyurinae subfamily of carnivorous marsupials, which includes quolls and Tasmanian devils.  These animals are characterized by their sharp teeth and claws, and are often nocturnal predators.|例句：The dasyurine population has declined significantly in recent years due to habitat loss. 近年来，由于栖息地丧失，鼬科动物的数量显著下降。|近义词：marsupial carnivore, quoll, Tasmanian devil|反义词：herbivore
