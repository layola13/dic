miscasts:/ˈmɪskæsts/| v.|错误选角，选角失误|To cast (an actor) in a role for which they are unsuitable.  This implies a poor choice of actor for a particular part, often due to a mismatch in talent, personality, or physical appearance. |例句：The director miscast the lead role; the actor lacked the necessary gravitas.  导演选角失误了；这位演员缺乏必要的威严。

例句：They completely miscast the villain; he was too likeable.他们完全选错了反派；他太讨人喜欢了。


近义词：misjudge, mischoose,  improperly cast


反义词：perfectly cast, aptly cast


词性:动词
