destroyed: /dɪˈstrɔɪd/ | v. | 破坏的；毁灭的 |  to damage something so badly that it no longer exists, works, or can be used  | 例句：The earthquake destroyed many buildings. 地震摧毁了许多建筑物。

The fire completely destroyed the forest. 大火彻底摧毁了森林。

近义词：ruined, wrecked, demolished, devastated | 反义词：created, built, constructed, restored | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm destroys coastal towns every year.  暴风雨每年都摧毁沿海城镇。
He destroys his own happiness with his bad habits. 他用自己的坏习惯毁了自己的幸福。


一般过去时:
The hurricane destroyed many homes.  飓风摧毁了许多房屋。
The army destroyed the enemy's base.  军队摧毁了敌人的基地。


现在完成时:
The war has destroyed the country's infrastructure.  战争已经摧毁了该国的基础设施。
They have destroyed all the evidence.  他们已经销毁了所有证据。


过去完成时:
The fire had destroyed the entire building before the firefighters arrived.  消防员到达之前，大火已经摧毁了整栋建筑物。
She had destroyed all her old letters before moving.  她在搬家之前已经销毁了她所有的旧信件。


将来时:
The volcano will destroy the village if it erupts.  如果火山爆发，它将摧毁村庄。
The company's poor management will destroy the business. 公司糟糕的管理将会毁掉这个生意。


虚拟语气:
If the bomb had exploded, it would have destroyed the entire city. 如果炸弹爆炸，它将摧毁整个城市。
I wish I hadn't destroyed that photo. 我真希望我没有毁掉那张照片。


被动语态:
The city was destroyed by the earthquake.  这座城市被地震摧毁了。
The old house is being destroyed to make way for a new building.  这座旧房子正在被拆除，为新建筑腾出空间。


疑问句:
Was the bridge destroyed in the flood?  桥在洪水中被摧毁了吗？
Have the documents been destroyed? 文件被销毁了吗？


否定句:
The fire did not destroy the house completely. 火灾并没有完全摧毁房子。
The government will not allow the historical site to be destroyed. 政府不会允许破坏这个历史遗址。
