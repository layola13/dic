reflexibility:/ˌriːflɛksəˈbɪləti/| n. |反射性；灵活性；适应性|The ability to bend or be bent easily; flexibility.  It also refers to the capacity for quick, instinctive reaction or response; reflexivity.  The term can also be used in a broader sense to describe the capacity of a system to adapt or change in response to its environment. |例句：The reflexibility of the material made it ideal for use in clothing.  这种材料的柔韧性使其成为服装的理想材料。

The remarkable reflexibility of the human brain allows us to adapt to new situations. 人类大脑非凡的适应性使我们能够适应新的情况。


近义词：flexibility, adaptability, suppleness, responsiveness |反义词：rigidity, inflexibility, stiffness
