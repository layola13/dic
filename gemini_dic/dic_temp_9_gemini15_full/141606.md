opt:/ɒpt/| /ɑːpt/| v.|选择，挑选|To make a choice; to decide on something.|He opted to take the early train. 他选择乘坐早班火车。|She opted for the vegetarian meal. 她选择了素食。|choose, select, elect|refuse, reject, decline|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I opt for the red dress. = 我选择红色的裙子。
They opt to stay home. = 他们选择待在家里。


一般过去时:
She opted out of the competition. = 她退出比赛了。
He opted to study abroad. = 他选择出国留学。


现在完成时:
I have opted to pursue a career in medicine. = 我已经选择从事医学事业。
They have opted for a simpler lifestyle. = 他们选择了更简单的生活方式。


过去完成时:
I had opted out before the deadline. = 我在截止日期之前就退出了。
She had opted for a different path in her career. = 她在她职业生涯中选择了不同的道路。


将来时:
I will opt for the cheaper option. = 我将选择更便宜的选择。
They will opt to attend the conference. = 他们将选择参加会议。


虚拟语气:
If I could, I would opt for a quieter life. = 如果可以的话，我会选择更安静的生活。
I wish I had opted for a different major. = 我真希望我选择了不同的专业。


被动语态:
The plan was opted for by the majority. = 大多数人选择了这个计划。
This option is rarely opted for. = 这个选项很少被选择。


疑问句:
Which option did you opt for? = 你选择了哪个选项？
Will they opt to participate? = 他们会选择参与吗？


否定句:
I didn't opt to go. = 我没有选择去。
She opted not to answer. = 她选择不回答。


PHRASE->
opt in = 选择加入
opt out = 选择退出
opt for = 选择
opt to = 选择
the opt-out clause = 选择退出条款
