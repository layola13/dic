pervade:/pərˈveɪd/ | vt. | 弥漫；遍布；充满 | To spread throughout and be perceived in every part of.  It implies a thorough and often subtle permeation. | 例句：The smell of baking bread pervaded the kitchen.  烤面包的香味弥漫在整个厨房。

例句：The feeling of anxiety pervaded the room.  焦虑的情绪充满了整个房间。


近义词：permeate, saturate, imbue, suffuse


反义词：lack, absence, vanish


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The aroma of coffee pervades the air. = 咖啡的香气弥漫在空气中。
Fear pervades the hearts of many. = 恐惧弥漫在许多人的心中。


一般过去时:
A sense of unease pervaded the meeting. = 一种不安的感觉弥漫了整个会议。
The sweet scent of roses pervaded the garden. = 玫瑰的甜香弥漫在花园里。


现在完成时:
Disappointment has pervaded her since the news. = 自从听到这个消息以来，失望一直萦绕着她。
Doubt has pervaded the minds of the public. = 怀疑已经充斥了公众的心灵。


过去完成时:
A sense of foreboding had pervaded the town before the storm. = 在暴风雨来临之前，一种不祥的预感已经弥漫了整个城镇。
Silence had pervaded the room after the announcement. = 公告之后，寂静充满了房间。


将来时:
A sense of calm will pervade the atmosphere after the crisis. = 危机过后，平静的气氛将会弥漫开来。
Optimism will pervade the team after their victory. = 胜利之后，乐观的情绪将充满整个团队。


虚拟语气:
If only peace would pervade the world! = 但愿和平能遍布世界！
If joy pervaded his life, he wouldn't be so unhappy. = 如果快乐充满他的生活，他就不会这么不开心。


被动语态:
The city was pervaded by a sense of excitement. = 这个城市充满了兴奋感。
The air was pervaded by the smell of smoke. = 空气中弥漫着烟味。


疑问句:
Does a feeling of despair pervade your life? = 你的人生充满绝望感吗？
Does negativity pervade this workplace? = 负面情绪在这个工作场所中蔓延吗？


否定句:
The feeling of happiness did not pervade the gathering. = 快乐的情绪并没有弥漫在聚会上。
Calm did not pervade the situation. = 平静并没有弥漫整个局势。



PHRASE->
pervade society = 遍布社会
pervade the culture = 渗透文化
pervade the atmosphere = 弥漫气氛
pervade the media = 充斥媒体
pervade every aspect = 渗透到各个方面


词源:
From Latin *pervadere, from per- "through" + vadere "to go."
