reinstructs:/ˌriːɪnˈstrʌkts/| vt.|重新指导；再次指示|To instruct again or differently; to give new instructions to.|The teacher reinstructed the students on the proper use of the equipment.  老师再次指导学生正确使用设备。|The manager reinstructed the team on the new marketing strategy. 经理再次向团队说明新的营销策略。|instruct, teach, guide|misinstruct, confuse

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reinstructs his students every week. 他每周都重新指导他的学生。
The company reinstructs its employees on safety procedures annually. 公司每年都会对员工进行安全规程的重新指导。

一般过去时:
She reinstructed the class after the confusion.  混乱之后，她重新指导了班级。
The pilot reinstructed the passengers on the emergency procedures.  飞行员再次向乘客讲解了紧急程序。

现在完成时:
The professor has reinstructed the students on several occasions. 教授已经多次重新指导学生。
They have reinstructed the workers on the updated guidelines. 他们已经对工人进行了更新指南的重新指导。

过去完成时:
After the accident, they had reinstructed the workers on safety protocols. 事故发生后，他们对工人们进行了安全规程的重新指导。
He had reinstructed the team before the crucial presentation. 在关键的演示之前，他已经对团队进行了重新指导。

将来时:
The instructor will reinstruct the group on the new techniques.  讲师将对小组进行新技术的重新指导。
They will reinstruct the staff on the revised policies next month. 下个月他们将对员工进行修订后的政策的重新指导。

虚拟语气:
If I were the manager, I would reinstruct the team on their priorities. 如果我是经理，我会重新指导团队的工作重点。
It is important that the trainer reinstruct the athletes on the correct form. 培训师对运动员进行正确的形式重新指导非常重要。

被动语态:
The students were reinstructed on the assignment’s requirements. 学生们接受了作业要求的重新指导。
The employees are being reinstructed on the new software. 员工们正在接受新软件的重新指导。


疑问句:
Will the teacher reinstruct the students tomorrow? 老师明天会重新指导学生吗？
Has the manager reinstructed the team on the new project yet? 经理是否已经对团队进行了关于新项目的新指导？

否定句:
The supervisor did not reinstruct the workers on the new procedures.  主管没有对工人进行新程序的重新指导。
They will not reinstruct the students unless there is a significant error. 除非出现重大错误，否则他们不会重新指导学生。
