genialnesses:/ˈdʒiːniəlˌnɛsɪz/ | n. | 亲切的行为；和蔼可亲的品质 |  The plural form of genialness, referring to multiple instances of friendliness, kindness, and pleasantness.  It describes a collection of cheerful and affable characteristics or actions. |  例句：His genialnesses towards the new employees made them feel welcome.  他的对新员工的友善使他们感到受欢迎。

词源:  Derived from the adjective "genial," which originates from the Latin word "genialis," meaning "belonging to a genus, kind, or race; pertaining to birth; native; relating to marriage; festive, merry, genial."  The suffix "-ness" is added to form the noun, indicating a quality or state.
