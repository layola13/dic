glutens:/ˈɡluːtənz/ | n. |面筋 | A mixture of proteins found in wheat, barley, and rye.  It gives elasticity to dough.  People with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity must avoid foods containing gluten. | Example: Many people are now choosing gluten-free products to improve their health. 许多人现在选择无麸质产品来改善他们的健康状况。 |近义词:gluten proteins |反义词:gluten-free
