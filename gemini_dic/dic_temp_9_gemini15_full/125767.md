murderous:/ˈmɜːrdərəs/| adj.|凶杀的；杀人的；极度残忍的；充满杀意的|Having the nature or quality of murder; characterized by violence and bloodshed; extremely cruel or vicious.  It can also refer to something that is extremely unpleasant or difficult.|


例句：

The murderer showed a murderous rage.  凶手表现出极度的愤怒。

His murderous glare frightened the children. 他充满杀意的眼神吓坏了孩子们。


近义词：killing, deadly, lethal, savage, brutal, ferocious, bloodthirsty


反义词：peaceful, gentle, kind, harmless, benevolent


词性：形容词
