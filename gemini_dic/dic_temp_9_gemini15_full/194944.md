英文解析:  The word "semi-minor" combines "semi-" (meaning half or partly) and "minor" (meaning less important or smaller).  It describes something that is somewhat less important or smaller than something else, but not insignificant.  It's less common than simply using "minor" and often implies a relative scale.


例句:

The semi-minor axis of the ellipse is significantly shorter than the major axis.  椭圆的半短轴比长轴短得多。


The semi-minor concerns raised during the meeting were addressed briefly. 会议上提出的次要问题得到了简短的处理。

反义词:  major, principal, primary, main, significant
