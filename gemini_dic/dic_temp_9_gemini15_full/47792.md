decapitation's:/ˌdɛkæpɪˈteɪʃənz/| n.|斩首；处决|The act of cutting off someone's head.  It often refers to a form of capital punishment or a violent act.|例句：The king ordered the decapitation of his enemies. 国王下令处决他的敌人。

例句：Historical accounts detail the gruesome decapitations during the revolution.  历史记载详细描述了革命期间骇人的斩首事件。


近义词：beheading, execution (if a legal process)


反义词：preservation of life, sparing


词性:名词 (复数形式)
