organizationally:/ˌɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃənəli/ | adv. |  组织上地；在组织方面 |  In a way that relates to the structure and function of an organization.  It describes how something is done or arranged within an organization. | Example:  The company restructured organizationally to improve efficiency.  这家公司进行了组织架构调整以提高效率。 The project was managed organizationally to ensure clear lines of responsibility.  这个项目在组织上得到了管理，以确保责任明确。 | 近义词：structurally, systematically | 反义词：chaotically, haphazardly
