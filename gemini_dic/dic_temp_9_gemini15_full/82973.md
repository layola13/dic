gunlock:/ˈɡʌnlɒk/| n. |枪锁；枪机|A gunlock is a mechanical device that secures a firearm, typically by blocking the firing mechanism.  It prevents accidental discharge or unauthorized use.  |Example: He installed a gunlock on his rifle to keep it safe from children. 他在他的步枪上安装了一个枪锁，以防止孩子们触碰。|近义词：firearm lock, trigger lock|反义词：unlocked firearm
