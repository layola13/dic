overburden:/ˈoʊvərˌbɜːrdn/| n. v.|过重的负担；使负担过重|

英文解析:
Noun:  An excessive or unmanageable amount of work or responsibility.  As a verb, it means to impose an excessive burden on someone or something.  The noun form often refers to a layer of rock or earth above a mineral deposit.


例句:
The company is struggling under the overburden of debt.  公司在沉重的债务负担下苦苦挣扎。

The overburden of paperwork delayed the project. 文件工作的过重负担延误了项目。


近义词:
burden, load, weight, strain, oppress


反义词:
relief, ease, alleviation


词性:
Noun, Verb
