indebted:/ɪnˈdɛtɪd/ | adj. | 负债的；感激的；受惠于…的 | feeling or showing gratitude for a benefit received; owing something to someone |  I am deeply indebted to my parents for their support.  我非常感激父母的支持。 | grateful, obliged, thankful, beholden |  ungrateful, unappreciative, independent

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am indebted to you for your help. = 我感谢你的帮助。
They are indebted to their ancestors for their success. = 他们的成功要归功于他们的祖先。


一般过去时:
I was indebted to him for his kindness. = 我感谢他的好意。
She was deeply indebted to her mentor for guidance. = 她非常感谢她的导师的指导。


现在完成时:
I have been indebted to my teachers for shaping my career. = 我一直感激我的老师们塑造了我的职业生涯。
We have been indebted to the community for their support during difficult times. = 在困难时期，我们一直感激社区的支持。


过去完成时:
I had been indebted to him for many years before I finally repaid the favour. = 在我最终报答他的恩情之前，我已经感激他很多年了。
They had been indebted to the bank for a substantial loan. = 他们之前欠银行一笔巨款。


将来时:
I will be forever indebted to you for your generosity. = 我将永远感激你的慷慨。
They will be indebted to the volunteers for their hard work. = 他们将感激志愿者的辛勤工作。


虚拟语气:
If I had not received his help, I would have been indebted to my family instead. = 如果我没有得到他的帮助，我就会感激我的家人了。
I wish I had not been indebted to the loan shark. = 我真希望我没有欠高利贷。


被动语态:
He was indebted to the company for his promotion.  = 他感谢公司提拔他。
The project was indebted to various sponsors for its funding. = 这个项目感谢各个赞助商的资助。


疑问句:
Are you indebted to anyone for your success? = 你的成功是否感谢任何人？
Was she indebted to the bank for a mortgage? = 她是否因为抵押贷款而欠银行钱？


否定句:
I am not indebted to anyone for this achievement. = 我对这一成就无需感谢任何人。
He was not indebted to anyone for his fortune. = 他不需要感谢任何人就能获得财富。


PHRASE->
be deeply indebted to = 深感感激
feel indebted to = 感到感激
indebted to sb for sth = 因某事感激某人
heavily indebted = 负债累累
remain indebted = 仍然感激
