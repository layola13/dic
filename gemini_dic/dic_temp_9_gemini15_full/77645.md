gelatinize:/dʒɛləˈtaɪniz/| vt.|胶化；凝胶化|To change into a gelatinous or jellylike state; to cause to become gelatinous.  The process involves the formation of a three-dimensional network of protein or polysaccharide molecules. |例句：The mixture gelatinized as it cooled.  混合物冷却后凝固了。

The pectin in the fruit helped to gelatinize the jam. 水果中的果胶有助于果酱凝胶化。


近义词：congeal, solidify, jellify|反义词：liquefy, melt|动词
