deplume:/dɪˈpluːm/| vt.|拔毛；剥夺…的羽毛；使失去光彩|To deprive (a bird) of its feathers; to strip of plumage; to despoil; to deflower.  |例句：The hunters deplumed the birds after they shot them. 猎人射杀鸟类后拔掉了它们的羽毛。
The scandal deplumed the politician's reputation. 这场丑闻损害了这位政治家的名誉。|近义词：plunder, despoil, strip|反义词：feathered, adorn
