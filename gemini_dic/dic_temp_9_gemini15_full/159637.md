pollyannaisms:/ˌpɑːliˈænəɪzəm/ | n. | 过分乐观；盲目乐观；一味乐观的态度 | Excessively or blindly optimistic statements or attitudes;  the tendency to see the best in everything, often ignoring negative aspects.  It's derived from the excessively optimistic character Pollyanna in Eleanor H. Porter's novel. |  Her constant pollyannaisms grated on my nerves.  她的过度乐观让我很烦。  His pollyannaisms about the economy were unrealistic. 他对经济的盲目乐观是不现实的。 |  optimism, rose-tinted glasses, positive thinking | pessimism, cynicism, realism | 名词
