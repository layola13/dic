scoping:/ˈskoʊpɪŋ/ | n. |范围界定，范围确定，勘察 |The process of identifying and defining the boundaries or scope of a project, task, or problem.  It involves determining the objectives, deliverables, constraints, and assumptions. |例句：The initial scoping phase of the project took three weeks. 项目的初步范围界定阶段花了三个星期。|例句：The team conducted a thorough scoping exercise to understand the full extent of the problem. 团队进行了彻底的范围界定练习以了解问题的全部程度。|近义词：defining, delimiting, surveying, investigating |反义词：broadening, expanding
