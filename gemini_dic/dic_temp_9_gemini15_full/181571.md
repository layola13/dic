relapses:/ˈriːlæps/| v. n.|复发；反复；故态复萌|To fall back into a previous condition, especially a bad or undesirable one, such as illness or a bad habit.  A relapse is a recurrence of a disease or undesirable behavior after a period of improvement or remission.|例句：He had a relapse after six months of sobriety. 他戒酒六个月后复发了。

例句：After years of recovery, she experienced a relapse into depression.  多年康复后，她再次陷入抑郁。


近义词：recurrence, backsliding, reversion|反义词：recovery, remission, improvement|词性:Verb, Noun

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He relapses into bad habits. = 他又染上了坏习惯。
She relapses into depression regularly. = 她经常复发抑郁症。

一般过去时:
He relapsed after a period of good health. = 他在一段健康时期后复发了。
She relapsed into her old ways. = 她故态复萌了。

现在完成时:
He has relapsed several times this year. = 他今年已经复发好几次了。
She has relapsed into her addiction. = 她又沉溺于她的瘾了。

过去完成时:
He had relapsed before the doctor could intervene. = 在医生能够干预之前，他已经复发了。
She had relapsed several times before finally seeking help. = 在最终寻求帮助之前，她已经复发过好几次了。

将来时:
He will likely relapse if he doesn't get support. = 如果他不得到支持，他很可能会复发。
She fears she will relapse if stressed. = 她担心如果压力太大，她会复发。


虚拟语气:
If he didn't relapse, he would be much healthier. = 如果他不复发，他会健康得多。
I wish she hadn't relapsed. = 我真希望她没有复发。

被动语态:
The patient was reported to have relapsed. = 据报道，病人复发了。
His relapse was attributed to stress. = 他的复发归因于压力。

疑问句:
Has he relapsed again? = 他又复发了吗？
Did she relapse after treatment? = 治疗后她复发了吗？

否定句:
He didn't relapse after the initial treatment. = 初次治疗后他没有复发。
She hasn't relapsed since she started therapy. = 从她开始治疗以来，她就没有复发过。
