re-inoculate:/ˌriːɪˈnɒkjʊleɪt/ | vt. |再次接种，重新接种 |To inoculate again; to introduce a new inoculum.  This term implies a prior inoculation has taken place, and this action is a repetition or continuation of that process. |例句：The doctor decided to re-inoculate the patient after the initial vaccination proved ineffective.  医生决定在初次接种无效后再次为病人接种。

The patient was re-inoculated with a stronger strain of the vaccine. 病人接种了更强效的疫苗。|近义词：reinoculate, revaccinate|反义词：de-inoculate (not a common word)
