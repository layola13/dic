declassified:/ˌdiːˈklæsɪfaɪd/| adj.|解密的；公开的|Removed from a classification of secrecy.  The information was declassified after many years.|例句：The government declassified the documents related to the event.  政府解密了与该事件相关的文件。

The documents were declassified after a thorough review.  经过彻底审查后，这些文件被解密。|近义词：open, revealed, public, unveiled|反义词：classified, secret, confidential, restricted|词性:形容词
