realises:/ˈriːəlaɪz/ | vt. |意识到，实现，了解 | To understand a situation, fact, or feeling completely; to achieve or fulfill something. | 例句：He realised his mistake. 他意识到了他的错误。 | 例句：She realised her ambition to become a doctor. 她实现了成为一名医生的抱负。 | 近义词：understand, comprehend, achieve, accomplish | 反义词：misunderstand, ignore, fail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He realises his potential. = 他意识到他的潜力。
She realises the importance of education. = 她意识到教育的重要性。

一般过去时:
I realised I was wrong. = 我意识到我错了。
They realised their dream of traveling the world. = 他们实现了环游世界的梦想。

现在完成时:
He has realised his lifelong goal. = 他已经实现了他毕生的目标。
She has realised how much she loves him. = 她已经意识到她有多爱他。

过去完成时:
I realised I had made a terrible mistake. = 我意识到我犯了一个可怕的错误。
They realised they had wasted their time. = 他们意识到他们浪费了时间。

将来时:
He will realise his full potential in time. = 他最终将发挥出他的全部潜力。
She will realise the consequences of her actions. = 她将意识到她行为的后果。

虚拟语气:
If I realised how difficult it was, I wouldn't have started. = 如果我意识到这有多难，我本来就不会开始。
I wish I had realised sooner. = 我希望我早点意识到。

被动语态:
The importance of the project was realised by the team. = 项目的重要性被团队认识到。
His talent was finally realised after years of hard work. = 经过多年的努力，他的才能终于被认可了。

疑问句:
Do you realise the danger? = 你意识到危险了吗？
Does she realise what she's doing? = 她意识到她在做什么吗？

否定句:
He didn't realise the seriousness of the situation. = 他没有意识到情况的严重性。
She doesn't realise how much I care. = 她不知道我有多在乎她。


PHRASE->
