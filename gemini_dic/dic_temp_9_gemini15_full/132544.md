USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it modifies verbs.  There aren't different tenses like with verbs.

Example 1:
He listened nondevoutly to the sermon. = 他不虔诚地听着布道。

Example 2:
She responded nondevoutly to the prayer. = 她对祈祷的回应不虔诚。


PHRASE->
