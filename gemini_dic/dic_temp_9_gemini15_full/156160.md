phototropism:/ˌfoʊtoʊˈtrɒpɪzəm/| n. |向光性 |The directional growth of a plant in response to a light source.  Plants exhibit phototropism by growing towards the light. This is due to the action of plant hormones such as auxins.|例句：The sunflower's phototropism is evident in its constant turning towards the sun. 向日葵的向光性体现在它不断地转向太阳。The seedlings demonstrated positive phototropism, bending towards the window. 幼苗表现出正向光性，弯向窗户。|近义词：heliotropism|反义词：aphototropism
