libertines:/ˈlɪbərtɪnz/| n.|放荡不羁的人，浪荡子|A libertine is a person, especially a man, who behaves without moral restraint.  They often engage in excessive sexual activity and disregard social conventions. |例句：He was known as a libertine, notorious for his numerous affairs and disregard for social norms. 他以放荡不羁而闻名，以其无数的风流韵事和无视社会规范而臭名昭著。

例句：The novel features a charming but ultimately reckless libertine as its protagonist. 这部小说以一个迷人但最终鲁莽的浪荡子为主角。


近义词：rake, debauchee, roué, libertine, profligate|反义词：prude, puritan, ascetic
