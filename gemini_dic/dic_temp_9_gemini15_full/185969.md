rewards:/rɪˈwɔːrdz/| n.|奖赏，报酬|Something given in return for good behavior, work, or achievement.|The company offers generous rewards to its employees.|近义词：prizes, bonuses, compensation|反义词：punishments, penalties|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company rewards its employees for their hard work. = 公司奖励员工的辛勤工作。
Good behavior rewards children with positive attention. = 良好的行为会让孩子们得到积极的关注。


一般过去时:
They rewarded her with a promotion for her excellent performance. = 因为她优异的表现，他们奖励了她一次晋升。
He was rewarded for his bravery. = 他因勇敢而受到奖励。


现在完成时:
She has been rewarded for her years of dedicated service. = 她多年来忠诚的服务得到了回报。
They have rewarded the winners with trophies. = 他们用奖杯奖励了获胜者。


过去完成时:
They had already rewarded the participants before the ceremony ended. = 在仪式结束之前，他们已经奖励了参与者。
She had been rewarded handsomely for her contribution. = 她为自己的贡献获得了丰厚的回报。


将来时:
The company will reward employees with bonuses for exceeding targets. = 公司将奖励超过目标的员工奖金。
He will be rewarded for his honesty. = 他将因诚实而受到奖励。


虚拟语气:
If I had worked harder, I would have been rewarded. = 如果我工作更努力，我就应该得到奖励。
I wish I had been rewarded for my efforts. = 我希望我的努力得到回报。


被动语态:
The winner was rewarded with a gold medal. = 获胜者获得了金牌奖励。
The employees are rewarded for their dedication. = 员工们因他们的奉献精神而受到奖励。


疑问句:
How will you reward your team for their success? = 你将如何奖励你的团队的成功？
What rewards are offered for completing this project? = 完成这个项目有什么奖励？


否定句:
They did not reward him for his efforts. = 他们没有奖励他的努力。
His hard work was not rewarded. = 他的努力没有得到回报。
