drearier:/ˈdriəriər/| adj.|更阴沉的；更沉闷的；更令人沮丧的|More dreary; more depressing.  Drearier describes something that is even more gloomy, dismal, or depressing than something else.|The weather was drearier than forecast, with constant drizzle and low clouds.  天气比预报的还要阴沉，下着毛毛雨，云层低垂。|gloomy, dismal, depressing, bleak|cheerful, bright, sunny, optimistic|形容词
