heh: /hɛh/ | int. | 轻笑声，表示轻微的嘲笑或愉悦 |  A sound expressing a slight chuckle or amusement, often suggesting a bit of mockery or irony.  |  He let out a little "heh" when she tripped. 他在她绊倒时轻笑了一声。 | 近义词：chuckle, snicker | 反义词：sob, wail


USAGE->
This word doesn't have grammatical tenses or voice like verbs or nouns.  It's an interjection. Therefore,  the grammatical categories below are not applicable.

PHRASE->
heh heh:  重复的轻笑声，表达更强烈的嘲笑或愉悦。
heh? : 表示轻微的不解或疑问，类似于"huh?"
heh, yeah: 轻微的同意或认同，带有戏谑的意味。
a little heh:  一个轻微的轻笑。
That's a heh moment:  那是一个令人轻笑或有点讽刺的时刻。
