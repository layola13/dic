gentler:/ˈdʒɛntlər/| adj.| 更温柔的，更温和的 | More gentle; less harsh, severe, or violent.  |例句：He spoke in a gentler tone this time.  这次他说话语气温和多了。 |例句：The gentler slopes were easier to climb.  较为平缓的山坡更容易攀登。|近义词：kinder, milder, softer |反义词：harsher, rougher, severer
