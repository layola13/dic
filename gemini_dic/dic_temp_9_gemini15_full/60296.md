eggnog:/ˈɛɡnɑːɡ/| n. |蛋酒 |A creamy alcoholic beverage made with eggs, milk or cream, sugar, and spirits (usually rum or brandy).  It is traditionally served during the Christmas season. |例句：We're having eggnog at the Christmas party.  我们在圣诞派对上喝蛋酒。

例句：He spiked the eggnog with extra rum. 他在蛋酒里加了额外的朗姆酒。


近义词：holiday punch, festive drink |反义词：无
