nuciferous:/nuːˈsɪfərəs/| adj.|核果的，含坚果的|Bearing or producing nuts.  The term refers to plants that produce nuts, which are technically one-seeded indehiscent fruits with a hard shell.  |例句：The walnut tree is a nuciferous species. 核桃树是一种含坚果的树种。|例句：Many nuciferous plants thrive in temperate climates. 许多含坚果的植物在温带气候下茁壮成长。|近义词：nut-bearing, nut-producing|反义词：nutless, seedless


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations.  The usage focuses on describing plants or their characteristics.


PHRASE->
nuciferous plants: 含坚果的植物
nuciferous trees:  含坚果的树木
nuciferous species: 含坚果的物种
nuciferous forest:  盛产坚果的森林
a nuciferous area: 一个盛产坚果的地区
