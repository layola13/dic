overworking: /ˌoʊvərˈwɜːrkɪŋ/ | v. |过度工作，工作过度 |  The act of working excessively; working beyond reasonable limits.  It often implies exhaustion and potential negative consequences for health and well-being. | 例句：He's been overworking lately and is completely burnt out.  他最近工作过度，完全筋疲力尽了。 |  例句：The company is notorious for overworking its employees. 这家公司以让员工过度工作而臭名昭著。 | 近义词：overexertion, overstrain, excessive work | 反义词：idleness, relaxation, underemployment |
