lonesomenesses:/ˈloʊnsəmnɪsɪz/| n.|孤独感；寂寞；孤寂|The plural form of "loneliness," referring to multiple instances or types of loneliness.  It emphasizes the intensity or variety of feelings of isolation. |例句：The lonesomenesses of old age can be profound and isolating.老年人的孤独感可能是深刻而孤立的。


例句：Her lonesomenesses were exacerbated by her recent move to a new city. 她最近搬到一个新的城市，加剧了她的孤独感。


近义词：solitudes, isolations, desolations


反义词：companionship, togetherness, sociability


词性:名词
