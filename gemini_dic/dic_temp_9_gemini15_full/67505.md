extract:/ˈɛkstrækt/ |/ˈɪkstrækt/ | n., vt. |提取物；摘录；榨取 |to remove or take out something from something else; to obtain something useful from a larger mass of material  |例句：The scientist carefully extracted the DNA from the sample.  科学家小心地从样本中提取了DNA。|例句：I extracted the key information from the lengthy report. 我从冗长的报告中提取了关键信息。|近义词：remove, derive, select |反义词：insert, add, include


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He extracts honey from the beehives. = 他从蜂箱中提取蜂蜜。
She extracts the main points from the article. = 她从文章中提取要点。

一般过去时:
They extracted the data from the database. = 他们从数据库中提取了数据。
