disinfected: /ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktɪd/ | adj. | 消毒的；已消毒的 |  Having been made free from infection, especially by destroying harmful microorganisms.  | 例句：The operating room was thoroughly disinfected before the surgery. 手术室在手术前进行了彻底的消毒。

The kitchen surfaces were disinfected after the food poisoning incident. 食物中毒事件后，厨房表面进行了消毒。


近义词：sterilized, sanitized, purified | 反义词：contaminated, infected
