hypertoxicity: /ˌhaɪpərˌtɒksɪsɪti/ | /ˌhaɪpərˌtɑːksɪsɪti/ | n. | 高毒性，超毒性 |  The excessive toxicity of a substance, significantly beyond a typically expected or safe level. It often refers to the unexpectedly high toxicity of a drug or chemical compound that causes serious harm or death.  | 例句：The hypertoxicity of the new drug led to its immediate recall. 新药的高毒性导致其立即被召回。

The hypertoxicity of the pesticide poses a significant threat to the environment.  这种杀虫剂的高毒性对环境构成重大威胁。

近义词：high toxicity, extreme toxicity, severe toxicity | 反义词：low toxicity, harmlessness
