considers:/kənˈsɪdərz/ | vt. | 考虑；认为；细想 | To think about carefully; to deem or regard in a specified way. |例句：She is considering a career change. 她正在考虑换一份工作。|例句：He considers himself lucky. 他认为自己很幸运。|近义词：thinks about, contemplates, regards, deems | 反义词：ignores, disregards | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He considers his options carefully. = 他仔细考虑了他的选择。
They consider the proposal unacceptable. = 他们认为这个提案不可接受。

一般过去时:
She considered the consequences before acting. = 她行动前考虑了后果。
He considered her request unreasonable. = 他认为她的要求不合理。

现在完成时:
I have considered all the possibilities. = 我已经考虑了所有可能性。
They have considered their options and decided to move. = 他们已经考虑了他们的选择，并决定搬家。

过去完成时:
She had considered quitting her job before the promotion. = 在升职之前，她曾考虑过辞职。
They had considered the risks before investing. = 他们在投资之前已经考虑过风险。

将来时:
He will consider the offer tomorrow. = 他明天会考虑这个提议。
We will consider your suggestion at the next meeting. = 我们将在下次会议上考虑你的建议。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would consider the offer more carefully. = 如果我是你，我会更仔细地考虑这个提议。
I wish I had considered the implications more thoroughly. = 我希望我当时更彻底地考虑了影响。

被动语态:
The proposal was considered by the committee. = 这个提案被委员会考虑了。
His work is considered groundbreaking. = 他的工作被认为是开创性的。

疑问句:
Do you consider this a fair price? = 你认为这是个公平的价格吗？
Does he consider himself qualified for the job? = 他认为自己有资格胜任这份工作吗？

否定句:
He doesn't consider himself a genius. = 他不认为自己是个天才。
They didn't consider the environmental impact. = 他们没有考虑环境影响。
