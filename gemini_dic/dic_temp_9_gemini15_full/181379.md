reinvited:/ˌriːɪnˈvaɪtɪd/| v. |再次邀请，重新邀请 | To invite someone again, especially after a previous invitation was declined or cancelled.  |  They reinvited her to the party after she explained her absence.  他们在她解释了缺席的原因后再次邀请她参加聚会。  | invite again, re-invite | decline, refuse |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reinvite guests who missed the first event. = 他们再次邀请那些错过了第一次活动的客人。
We reinvite him to our annual meeting every year. = 我们每年都重新邀请他参加我们的年度会议。


一般过去时:
The host reinvited the guest after they apologized. = 主人收到客人的道歉后再次邀请了他。
She reinvited me to dinner after I explained my previous conflict. = 我解释了之前的时间冲突后，她再次邀请我共进晚餐。


现在完成时:
They have reinvited several speakers for the conference. = 他们已经为会议重新邀请了几位演讲者。
I have reinvited my friend to the birthday party after he missed the first one. = 我朋友错过了第一次生日派对后，我再次邀请了他。


过去完成时:
After the storm, they had reinvited all the guests for a later date. = 暴风雨过后，他们将所有客人都重新邀请到一个稍后的日期。
Because of the power outage, they had reinvited everyone for the party the next day. = 由于停电，他们将所有人重新邀请到第二天参加派对。


将来时:
We will reinvite them if they are available. = 如果他们有空，我们将再次邀请他们。
I will reinvite them to the concert if they change their minds. = 如果他们改变主意，我会再次邀请他们去音乐会。


虚拟语气:
If I had known they were available, I would have reinvited them. = 如果我知道他们有空，我就会再次邀请他们。
If she had apologized sooner, he would have reinvited her. = 如果她早点道歉，他会再次邀请她的。


被动语态:
Many guests were reinvited to the wedding. = 许多客人被重新邀请参加婚礼。
He was reinvited to speak at the seminar. = 他被再次邀请在研讨会上发言。


疑问句:
Were they reinvited to the meeting? = 他们被再次邀请参加会议了吗？
Will you be reinvited to the party? = 你会被再次邀请参加聚会吗？


否定句:
They did not reinvite her to the party. = 他们没有再次邀请她参加聚会。
He was not reinvited to the conference. = 他没有被再次邀请参加会议。
