initiatively:/ɪˈnɪʃətɪvli/ | adv. |主动地；积极地 |Acting or done in a way that shows initiative.  Showing the ability to act and take charge without being told what to do. |例句：She initiatively took on the extra work. 她主动承担了额外的工作。

例句：He initiatively solved the problem without being asked. 他主动解决了问题，没人让他去做。


近义词：proactively, spontaneously, independently, willingly

反义词：passively, reluctantly, unwillingly


词源:  Derived from the word "initiative," which comes from the Latin word "initiativus," meaning "beginning" or "commencement."  The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective "initiative" into an adverb.
