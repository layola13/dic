chained:/tʃeɪnd/| v. adj.|  束缚的；被束缚的；链接的 |  To be chained means to be fastened or connected with a chain;  to link or connect things together. As an adjective, it describes something or someone restricted or bound. |例句：The dog was chained to the tree.  这条狗被链子拴在树上。

The prisoners were chained together.  犯人们被锁在一起。


近义词：bound, fettered, linked, connected |反义词：free, unbound, liberated |词性：Verb, Adjective
