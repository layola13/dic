feaster:/ˈfiːstər/| n. |盛宴参与者，宴会者|A person who attends or participates in a feast.  Someone who enjoys a large meal or celebration.|例句：The feasters enjoyed the lavish spread of food and drink.  盛宴的参与者们享受着丰盛的食物和饮料。

例句：Many feasters gathered at the king's table for the coronation feast. 许多宴会者聚集在国王的餐桌旁参加加冕盛宴。


近义词：guest, diner, participant, reveler|反义词：None specifically.  A word like "abstainer" could be considered an antonym in the context of avoiding a feast. |词性：名词
