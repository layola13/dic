pantheists:/ˈpænθiɪsts/ | n. |泛神论者 |A pantheist is a person who believes that God is everything and everywhere, that God is identical with the universe and nature.  Pantheists don't believe in a separate, personal God who intervenes in human affairs. |Example:  Many ancient philosophers were considered pantheists, believing the divine was inherent in all things.  许多古代哲学家都被认为是泛神论者，他们相信神性存在于万物之中。|近义词: panentheist (泛神论者，但神也超越宇宙), naturalist (自然主义者) |反义词: theist (有神论者), atheist (无神论者)
