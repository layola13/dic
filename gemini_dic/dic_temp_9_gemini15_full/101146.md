investments:/ɪnˈvɛstmənts/ | n. |投资；投资额 |The act of investing money or capital in something, with the expectation of achieving a profit or other benefit.  It can refer to a single investment or a portfolio of investments. |例句：He made several wise investments in the stock market. 他在股市上进行了几笔明智的投资。
|例句：Her investments generated a substantial return. 她的投资获得了可观的回报。|近义词：placements, capital expenditures |反义词：withdrawals, divestments |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My company makes sound investments in renewable energy. = 我的公司对可再生能源进行着合理的投资。
She invests wisely in various assets. = 她明智地投资于各种资产。


一般过去时:
They invested heavily in the new technology last year. = 他们去年大力投资了这项新技术。
He invested his savings in real estate. = 他把积蓄投资到了房地产。


现在完成时:
We have invested millions of dollars in research and development. = 我们已经投资数百万美元用于研发。
She has invested a significant portion of her income in stocks. = 她已经将很大一部分收入投资于股票。


过去完成时:
Before the crisis, they had invested all their money in a single company. = 在危机爆发前，他们已将所有资金投资于一家公司。
By the end of the year, the company had invested a total of $10 million. = 到年底，公司总共投资了1000万美元。


将来时:
We will invest more in marketing next year. = 我们明年将在市场营销方面投入更多资金。
They will invest in a new factory to expand production. = 他们将投资新建工厂以扩大生产。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would invest it in a diversified portfolio. = 如果我有更多钱，我会把它投资于多元化的投资组合。
I wish I had invested in that tech company years ago. = 我真希望几年前就投资了那家科技公司。


被动语态:

The fund is being invested in a variety of emerging markets. = 该基金正在投资于各种新兴市场。


疑问句:

What kind of investments are you considering? = 你正在考虑哪种类型的投资？


否定句:
They did not invest in that project. = 他们没有投资那个项目。
He hasn't invested in the stock market yet. = 他还没有投资股市。
