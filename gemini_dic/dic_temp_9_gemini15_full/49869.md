demotivates:/ˌdiːˈmoʊtɪveɪts/ | vt. |使失去动力；使泄气；使灰心|To discourage or deprive someone of motivation.|The constant criticism demotivates the team.  持续的批评使团队失去动力。|The boring work demotivated him. 单调的工作让他灰心丧气。|motivate, inspire | motivate, encourage |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The monotonous work demotivates many employees. = 单调的工作让许多员工失去动力。
Negative feedback demotivates the team. = 负面反馈使团队失去动力。

一般过去时:
The unexpected layoff demotivated the entire staff. = 意外的裁员让全体员工都失去了动力。
His harsh words demotivated her from pursuing her dreams. = 他严厉的话语让她放弃了追求梦想的动力。

现在完成时:
Years of setbacks have demotivated him. = 多年的挫折让他失去了动力。
The constant pressure has demotivated her from continuing her studies. = 持续的压力让她失去了继续学习的动力。

过去完成时:
He had already been demotivated by previous failures before starting the project. = 在开始这个项目之前，他已经被之前的失败打击得失去了动力。
The team had been demotivated by the lack of support before the presentation. = 在演示之前，团队因为缺乏支持而失去了动力。

将来时:
The new policy will demotivate the workers. = 新的政策将会让员工失去动力。
The lack of recognition will demotivate future applicants. = 缺乏认可将会让未来的申请者失去动力。

虚拟语气:
If the management were more supportive, it wouldn't demotivate the employees. = 如果管理层更有支持力，就不会让员工失去动力。
I wish the lack of resources wouldn't demotivate the team so much. = 我希望资源匮乏不会让团队如此灰心。

被动语态:
The employees were demotivated by the lack of recognition. = 员工因为缺乏认可而失去了动力。
The project was demotivated by the numerous delays. = 项目因为多次延误而失去了动力。

疑问句:
Does the lack of promotion opportunities demotivate the staff? = 升迁机会的缺乏是否让员工失去动力？
What demotivates your employees the most? = 什么最让你的员工失去动力？

否定句:
The positive feedback did not demotivate the team. = 积极的反馈并没有让团队失去动力。
The manager tried to avoid anything that would demotivate his staff. = 经理试图避免任何可能让员工失去动力的事情。
