nonmelodramatically:/ˌnɒnˌmɛlədrəˈmætɪkli/ | adj. |不夸张地；不煽情地；平静地 |  Not in a melodramatic manner; calmly and without exaggeration.  It describes a way of behaving or speaking that avoids excessive emotion or dramatic flair. |例句：He recounted the story nonmelodramatically, focusing on the facts. 他平静地讲述了这个故事，重点放在事实。
He described the accident nonmelodramatically, avoiding sensationalism. 他不带煽情地描述了事故，避免了耸人听闻。|近义词：calmly, quietly, unemotionally, soberly |反义词：melodramatically, dramatically, emotionally, hysterically
