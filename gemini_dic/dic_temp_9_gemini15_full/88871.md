hoisting:/ˈhɔɪstɪŋ/ | n. |起重，吊装；提升 | The act of lifting something heavy, typically with machinery.  |例句：The hoisting of the flag marked the start of the ceremony.  升旗标志着仪式的开始。|例句：The construction crew used a crane for hoisting the steel beams. 建筑队使用起重机吊起钢梁。|近义词：lifting, raising, elevating |反义词：lowering, dropping


USAGE->

The crane is hoisting the heavy equipment. = 起重机正在吊起重型设备。
They hoist the sails every morning. = 他们每天早上都升起帆。

一般过去时:
The workers hoisted the cargo onto the ship. = 工人们把货物吊上了船。
We hoisted the flag to celebrate the victory. = 我们升起旗帜以庆祝胜利。
