degenerated:/dɪˈdʒɛnəreɪtɪd/| adj.| 退化的；堕落的；衰退的 |  Having declined or deteriorated from a former or original state.  This adjective describes something that has worsened in quality, condition, or function. It often implies a loss of former excellence or a decline into something undesirable. | Example: The once-proud city has degenerated into a slum.  这个曾经繁华的城市已经沦为贫民窟了。|Near synonyms: deteriorated, decayed, corrupt, degraded | Antonyms: improved, enhanced, elevated, refined |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The building is degenerating rapidly. = 这座建筑正在迅速恶化。
His moral character is degenerating. = 他的道德品质正在堕落。

一般过去时:
The empire degenerated after the death of the emperor. = 皇帝死后，帝国衰落了。
The quality of the product degenerated over time. = 产品的质量随着时间的推移而下降。

现在完成时:
The situation has degenerated into a crisis. = 局势已经恶化成危机。
His health has degenerated significantly. = 他的健康状况已经严重恶化。

过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, the situation had degenerated completely. = 我到达时，情况已经完全恶化了。
Their relationship had degenerated to the point of no return. = 他们的关系已经恶化到无法挽回的地步。


将来时:
If we don't act now, the situation will degenerate further. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，情况将会进一步恶化。
The environment will degenerate without conservation efforts. = 没有保护措施，环境将会恶化。

虚拟语气:
If he had not been so careless, his health would not have degenerated so quickly. = 如果他不那么粗心，他的健康状况就不会恶化得这么快。
I wish the situation hadn't degenerated to this point. = 我希望情况没有恶化到这个地步。

被动语态:
The building was allowed to degenerate. = 这座建筑被允许破败。
The social fabric was gradually degenerated by corruption. = 社会结构逐渐被腐败所败坏。

疑问句:

Is the quality of education degenerating? = 教育质量正在下降吗？

否定句:
The situation did not degenerate as quickly as we feared. = 情况并没有像我们担心的那样迅速恶化。
The quality of life has not degenerated in this area. = 这个地区的居民生活质量并没有下降。
