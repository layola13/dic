regina:/rɪˈdʒaɪnə/| n. |雷吉娜（女子名）, 女王|Regina is a Latin word meaning "queen".  It is a popular given name for girls, often shortened to Reggi or Gina. The name evokes a sense of royalty, strength, and elegance. |My friend's name is Regina, and she's a truly remarkable person.|近义词：queen, monarch|反义词：subject, commoner
