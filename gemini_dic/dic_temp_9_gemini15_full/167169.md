pretasters:/prɪˈteɪstərz/| n.|先行品尝者，试吃员|People who taste food or drinks before they are offered to the public to assess their quality and suitability.  They often provide feedback to the producers.|Example: The pretasters were crucial in identifying the subtle off-flavor in the new wine.|近义词：taste testers, food testers|反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pretasters check the quality of the food daily. = 试吃员每天检查食物的质量。
She tastes and rates every batch of ice cream. = 她品尝并评价每一批冰淇淋。

一般过去时:
The pretasters reported a slight bitterness in the chocolate. = 试吃员报告巧克力略带苦味。
He tasted the soup and gave feedback to the chef. = 他尝了汤，并给了厨师反馈。

The company has hired several new pretasters this year. = 公司今年聘请了几名新的试吃员。
