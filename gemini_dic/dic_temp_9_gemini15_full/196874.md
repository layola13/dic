shack:/ʃæk/| n.|简陋的小屋，棚屋|A shack is a small, roughly built hut or cabin.  It is typically made of flimsy materials and is considered temporary or substandard housing.|例句：They lived in a ramshackle shack on the edge of town. 他们住在城郊一座摇摇欲坠的小棚屋里。|例句：The hurricane destroyed many shacks along the coast.飓风摧毁了沿海许多简陋的小屋。|近义词：hut, cabin, shanty|反义词：mansion, palace

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shack stands on the hill. = 这间棚屋矗立在山上。
He lives in a small shack. = 他住在一间简陋的小屋里。

一般过去时:
The shack collapsed during the storm. = 棚屋在暴风雨中倒塌了。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
They had lived in the shack before moving to the city. = 在搬到城市之前，他们住在那个棚屋里。
The storm had already damaged the shack before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，暴风雨已经损坏了棚屋。

将来时:

The shack will likely collapse soon. = 这间棚屋很可能会很快倒塌。

虚拟语气:
