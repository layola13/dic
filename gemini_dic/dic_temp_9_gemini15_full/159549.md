politico:/pəˈlɪtɪkoʊ/| n. |政客，政治家 |A person who is actively involved in politics, especially as a career.  Someone who is professionally involved in politics, often emphasizing strategic maneuvering and power plays. |例句：Many politicos attended the fundraising gala. 许多政客出席了筹款晚会。

例句：He's a seasoned politico with decades of experience in Washington. 他是一位在华盛顿拥有几十年经验的老练政客。


近义词：politician, statesman, stateswoman, political figure


反义词：private citizen, non-political individual


词源:  The word "politico" comes from the Italian word "politico," which means "political." It entered English in the early 20th century.  The Italian word itself derives from "politica," meaning "politics," ultimately from the Greek word "politikos," meaning "of or relating to citizens."
