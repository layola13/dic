misbinding:/ˌmɪsˈbaɪndɪŋ/| n.|装订错误，错误合订|The incorrect binding of pages in a book or document.  It often leads to pages being out of order or missing.|例句：The misbinding in the textbook made it difficult to follow the chapters.  这本教科书的装订错误使得章节难以理解。

例句：There was a significant misbinding in the report, resulting in several missing pages. 报告中存在严重的装订错误，导致几页缺失。


近义词：faulty binding, incorrect binding, bad binding|反义词：correct binding, proper binding|名词
