internships:/ˈɪntɜrnʃɪps/| n. |实习，实习期|A period of work experience offered to students or recent graduates, often as a requirement for a qualification.|例句：Many students seek internships to gain practical experience. 许多学生寻求实习以获得实践经验。|近义词：apprenticeships, traineeships|反义词：full-time employment
