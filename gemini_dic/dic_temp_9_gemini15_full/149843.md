paleozoology:/ˌpeɪliˌoʊzuˈɒlədʒi/ | n. | 古动物学 | The branch of paleontology that studies ancient animals.  It encompasses the study of fossilized remains, tracks, burrows, and other evidence of past animal life to understand their evolution, ecology, and relationships. |  The paleozoology of the Burgess Shale reveals a surprising diversity of Cambrian life. 古 Burgess 页岩的古动物学揭示了寒武纪生命令人惊讶的多样性。  Paleozoological research helps us reconstruct ancient ecosystems. 古动物学研究有助于我们重建古代生态系统。 | 近义词: paleontology (更广泛的学科) | 反义词: (无直接反义词, 可考虑与现代动物学相对)
