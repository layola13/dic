egomism:/ˈiːɡoʊɪzəm/ | n. |自我主义；利己主义 |The excessive preoccupation with oneself and one's own importance.  It's a characteristic marked by self-centeredness and a lack of consideration for others. |例句：His egomism prevented him from seeing the needs of others.  他的自我主义使他看不到别人的需求。

例句：Her egomism was evident in her constant boasting. 她的自我主义表现在她不断的吹嘘中。


近义词：narcissism, self-centeredness, selfishness, conceit

反义词：altruism, selflessness, humility


词源:  From ego (self) + -ism (a characteristic or doctrine).  The word ultimately derives from Latin *ego ("I").
