monkey:/ˈmʌŋki/ | n. |猴子 |A small, agile mammal with a long tail, typically living in trees. Monkeys are characterized by their intelligence and social behavior. They belong to the primate order and are closely related to apes and humans. |例句：The monkey swung effortlessly from branch to branch. 猴子轻松自如地从树枝间荡来荡去。|例句：A troop of monkeys chattered noisily in the trees.一群猴子在树上吵吵闹闹。|近义词：ape, primate |反义词: None
