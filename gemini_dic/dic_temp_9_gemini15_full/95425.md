incinerate: /ˈɪnsɪnəreɪt/ | vt. |焚烧；烧成灰烬 | To burn something completely.  |例句：The city incinerated the waste.  = 城市焚烧了垃圾。
They incinerated the infected remains. = 他们焚烧了受感染的遗体。|近义词：burn, cremate, consume |反义词：preserve, save
