nonubiquitous:/ˌnɒnjuːˈbɪkwɪtəs/| adj. |非普遍的，不普遍存在的|Not found everywhere; not common or widespread.  It is the opposite of ubiquitous.  The adjective describes something that is not omnipresent or universally present.|

例句：The use of this particular software is nonubiquitous, meaning it's not used by everyone or in every field.  这个特定软件的使用并不普遍，这意味着它并非每个人或每个领域都在使用。

例句：Nonubiquitous resources may limit the feasibility of certain projects.  资源的匮乏可能会限制某些项目的可行性。


近义词：rare, uncommon, scarce, infrequent, sporadic

反义词：ubiquitous, common, widespread, prevalent, universal

词性：adjective
