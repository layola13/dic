quat:/kwɑːt/| n.|夸脱；四分之一加仑|A quart is a unit of liquid volume equal to one-fourth of a gallon.|例句：He bought a quart of milk. 他买了一夸脱牛奶。|近义词：liter (approximately)|反义词:gallon
