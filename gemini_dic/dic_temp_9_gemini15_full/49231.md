delegator:/ˈdɛləɡeɪtər/| n.| 委派者；授权者 | A person who delegates tasks or responsibilities to others.  Someone who assigns work to others. |  The project manager acted as a delegator, assigning tasks to team members based on their skills. 项目经理扮演了委派者的角色，根据团队成员的技能分配任务。 | assigner, allocator, entrustor | receiver, subordinate | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The delegator assigns tasks daily. = 委派者每天分配任务。
He delegates responsibilities to his team. = 他将责任委托给他的团队。


一般过去时:
The delegator assigned the project yesterday. = 委派者昨天分配了项目。
She delegated the task to her assistant. = 她把任务委托给了她的助手。


现在完成时:
The delegator has assigned all the tasks. = 委派者已经分配了所有任务。
He has delegated the responsibility effectively. = 他有效地委托了责任。


过去完成时:
The delegator had already assigned the tasks before the meeting. = 会议之前，委派者已经分配了任务。
She had delegated the project before leaving for vacation. = 她休假前已经委托了项目。


将来时:
The delegator will assign the new project next week. = 委派者下周将分配新的项目。
He will delegate more responsibilities to his team. = 他将更多地把责任委托给他的团队。


虚拟语气:
If he were a good delegator, he would assign tasks more efficiently. = 如果他是一位优秀的委派者，他会更有效地分配任务。
I wish the delegator had delegated the tasks more clearly. = 我希望委派者能更清晰地委托任务。


被动语态:
The tasks were delegated by the project manager. = 任务由项目经理委托。
The responsibility was delegated to the junior staff. = 责任委托给了初级员工。


疑问句:
Who is the delegator for this project? = 这个项目的委派者是谁？
Has the delegator assigned all the tasks yet? = 委派者是否已经分配了所有任务？


否定句:
The delegator did not assign the task properly. = 委派者没有正确地分配任务。
He did not delegate enough responsibility to his team. = 他没有将足够的责任委托给他的团队。
