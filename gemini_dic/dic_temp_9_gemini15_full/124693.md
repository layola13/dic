movingly:/ˈmuːvɪŋli/ | adj. | 动人地；令人感动地 | In a way that evokes strong emotion, especially sadness or sympathy.  It describes something that is deeply affecting or touching. |例句：The story was told movingly, bringing tears to many eyes.  这个故事讲述得非常动人，让许多人都感动得流泪。

例句：He spoke movingly about his experience with loss. 他动情地讲述了他失去亲人的经历。


近义词：touchingly, poignantly, affectingly, deeply

反义词：unmovingly, coldly, indifferently

词性：形容词
