incompatibility:/ˌɪnkəmˌpætəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不相容性，不兼容性 | The state or fact of being incompatible.  It refers to the inability of two or more things to exist or work together successfully. This can apply to various contexts, including personalities, ideas, systems, or technologies. | Example: The incompatibility of their personalities led to the breakup.  他们的性格不合导致了分手。The incompatibility of the software with the operating system prevented it from running. 该软件与操作系统不兼容，无法运行。 |近义词：inconsistency, conflict, clash |反义词：compatibility, concordance
