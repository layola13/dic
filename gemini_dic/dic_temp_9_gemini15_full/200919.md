slippery:/ˈslɪpəri/| adj.|滑的，光滑的，难以捉摸的 |adj.  Difficult to hold or grip because it is smooth or wet.  Something that is slippery is difficult to grasp or hold firmly. It can also describe a situation or concept that is hard to understand or manage. |例句：The ice was slippery, and I almost fell. 冰很滑，我差点摔倒了。

The politician gave a slippery answer, avoiding the difficult question.  这位政治家给出了一个模棱两可的答案，避开了那个棘手的问题。


近义词：slippery, smooth, slimy, elusive, tricky |反义词：rough, firm, secure, straightforward
