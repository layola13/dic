extoll:/ɪkˈstoʊl/ | vt. | 赞扬；颂扬；吹捧 | To praise highly; to exalt; to glorify.  |例句：The mayor extolled the virtues of the new community center. 市长盛赞了新的社区中心。

例句： Critics extolled the film's stunning visuals and compelling narrative. 批评家们盛赞这部电影令人惊叹的视觉效果和引人入胜的故事情节。


近义词：praise, laud, commend, acclaim, celebrate | 反义词：criticize, condemn, denounce, belittle


词源:  From Old French *extoller, from Latin extollere, from ex- ("out, up") + tollere ("to lift, raise").  The word's development reflects the sense of "lifting up" something in praise.
