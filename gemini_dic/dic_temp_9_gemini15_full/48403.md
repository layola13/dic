dedicating:/ˈdɛdɪkeɪtɪŋ/| v.|奉献，致力于|The act of devoting time, effort, or oneself to a particular task, purpose, or person.  It implies a strong commitment and dedication.|例句：He is dedicating his life to helping the homeless. 他正致力于帮助无家可归的人。


例句：She's dedicating her free time to painting. 她把业余时间都用来画画了。


近义词：committing, devoting, consecrating


反义词：neglecting, ignoring, abandoning


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dedicates his time to charity. = 他把时间奉献给慈善事业。
They dedicate themselves to their work. = 他们致力于他们的工作。

一般过去时:
She dedicated her book to her mother. = 她把书献给了她的母亲。
He dedicated many years to research. = 他把许多年都献给了研究。

现在完成时:
I have dedicated my career to medicine. = 我已将我的职业生涯奉献给了医学。
They have dedicated their lives to helping others. = 他们已将一生奉献给了帮助他人。

过去完成时:
She had dedicated ten years to the project before it was completed. = 在项目完成之前，她已为其奉献了十年时间。
He had dedicated his whole life to the cause before he passed away. = 他去世前将一生都奉献给了这个事业。

将来时:
I will dedicate my next book to my children. = 我将把我的下一本书献给我孩子们。
She will dedicate the next five years to her studies. = 她将把接下来的五年时间投入到学习中。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would dedicate it to learning a new language. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把它用来学习一门外语。
I wish I could dedicate more time to my family. = 我希望我能多花些时间陪伴家人。

被动语态:
This monument is dedicated to the soldiers who died in the war. = 这座纪念碑是为了纪念在战争中牺牲的士兵而建造的。
The park was dedicated to the memory of the city's founder. = 这座公园是为了纪念城市创始人而建立的。

疑问句:
What are you dedicating your life to? = 你将一生奉献给什么？
To whom is this song dedicated? = 这首歌献给谁？

否定句:
He did not dedicate enough time to the project. = 他没有为这个项目投入足够的时间。
She does not dedicate herself to any single cause. = 她没有将自己完全奉献给任何一个事业。
