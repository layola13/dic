pronouncing: /prəˈnaʊnsɪŋ/ | v. | 发音；宣告 |  The act of pronouncing words or speaking clearly.  It involves the articulation of sounds to produce meaningful speech. The pronunciation of a word can vary depending on accent and dialect. | 例句：He is practicing pronouncing the difficult words. 他正在练习发音困难的单词。

例句：The teacher is helping the students with their pronouncing. 老师正在帮助学生们练习发音。


近义词：articulating, enunciating, uttering | 反义词：mumbling, whispering
