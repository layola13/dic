etudes:/eɪˈtjuːdz/| n.|练习曲|A musical composition, typically for a solo instrument, designed to develop specific technical skills.  Etudes are often challenging and demanding, focusing on particular aspects of playing, such as scales, arpeggios, or specific techniques.| 例句：Chopin's etudes are renowned for their technical brilliance and musicality.  肖邦的练习曲以其精湛的技术和音乐性而闻名。

The pianist practiced his etudes diligently every day.  这位钢琴家每天都勤奋地练习练习曲。|近义词：studies, exercises, drills|反义词：performance, concert
