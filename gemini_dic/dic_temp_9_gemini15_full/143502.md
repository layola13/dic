outglowed:/ˌaʊtˈɡloʊd/| v.|胜过（在光芒上）, 超过（在光亮上）|To surpass in brightness or brilliance.  It implies a significant difference in luminosity.|例句：The diamond outglowed all the other jewels in the crown.  这颗钻石的光芒超过了王冠上所有其他的珠宝。

The supernova outglowed all the stars in the galaxy. 这颗超新星的光芒超过了星系中所有的恒星。


近义词：outshine, outshine, eclipse, surpass


反义词：be dimmed by, be overshadowed by


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun outglows the moon. 太阳的光芒胜过月亮。
This star outglows that one. 这颗星星比那颗星星亮。


一般过去时:
The fire outglowed the candles. 火光比烛光亮。
Her beauty outglowed everyone else's. 她的美貌胜过其他人。


现在完成时:
The new LED light has outglowed the old incandescent bulb. 新的LED灯比旧的白炽灯亮。
His talent has outglowed all his peers. 他的才能已超过所有同龄人。


过去完成时:
The setting sun had outglowed the rising moon. 夕阳的光芒曾超过了升起的月亮。
The diamonds had outglowed the other jewelry before it was stolen.  钻石在被偷之前就比其他珠宝更闪亮。


将来时:
The neon lights will outglow the streetlights. 霓虹灯的光芒将超过路灯。
The new technology will outglow the old. 新技术将超越旧技术。


虚拟语气:
If the sun were closer, it would outglow everything. 如果太阳更近，它将比任何东西都亮。
I wish my work would outglow theirs. 我希望我的作品能超过他们的。


被动语态:
The old lamp was outglowed by the new one. 旧灯的光芒被新灯超过了。
His achievements were outglowed by her groundbreaking discoveries. 他的成就被她的突破性发现的光芒所掩盖。


疑问句:
Does the new model outglow the old one? 新型号的光芒是否超过旧型号？
Will the planned fireworks outglow the city lights? 计划中的烟火会比城市灯光更亮吗？


否定句:
The small candle didn't outglow the large bonfire. 小蜡烛的光芒没有超过大型篝火。
His performance didn't outglow hers. 他的表演没有超过她的。
