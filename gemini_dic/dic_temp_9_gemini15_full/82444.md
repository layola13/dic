grumbles:/ˈɡrʌmbəlz/| v.|抱怨，咕哝|To complain or mutter in a low voice, typically expressing discontent.|He grumbled about the long hours he had to work. 他抱怨不得不长时间工作。|She grumbled under her breath about the unfairness of it all. 她低声抱怨这一切的不公平。|complain, moan, mutter|praise, compliment|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grumbles about the weather every day. = 他每天都抱怨天气。
They often grumble about their jobs. = 他们经常抱怨他们的工作。

一般过去时:
She grumbled about the noise all night. = 她整晚都在抱怨噪音。
He grumbled that the food was cold. = 他抱怨食物冷了。

现在完成时:
He has grumbled about his boss for months. = 他已经抱怨他的老板好几个月了。
She has grumbled repeatedly about the service. = 她反复抱怨服务。

过去完成时:
He had grumbled about the delay before the meeting even started. = 会议还没开始他就已经抱怨延误了。
They had grumbled for years before they finally complained formally. = 在他们最终正式投诉之前，他们已经抱怨了好几年了。

将来时:
He will grumble if the team loses again. = 如果球队再次输球，他会抱怨的。
She will probably grumble about having to work overtime. = 她可能会抱怨不得不加班。

虚拟语气:
I wish he wouldn't grumble so much. = 我希望他不要抱怨那么多。
If he were here, he would grumble about the lack of coffee. = 如果他在这儿，他会抱怨咖啡不够的。

被动语态:
His complaints were often grumbled about by others.  = 他的抱怨经常被别人抱怨。
The poor service was grumbled about by many customers. = 许多顾客抱怨服务差。


疑问句:
Does he often grumble about his work? = 他经常抱怨他的工作吗？
Why are they grumbling? = 他们为什么在抱怨？


否定句:
He didn't grumble about the extra work. = 他没有抱怨额外的工作。
She didn't grumble at all during the trip. = 旅行期间她一点也没有抱怨。


PHRASE->
grumble about = 抱怨...
grumble to oneself = 自言自语地抱怨
grumble and groan = 抱怨和呻吟
grumble under one's breath = 小声抱怨
a grumble in the stomach = 肚子咕噜叫 (饥饿)
