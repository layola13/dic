clapcake:/ˈklæpˌkeɪk/| n. |煎饼，薄烤饼 |A small, thin pancake, often made from a batter of flour, milk, and eggs, and cooked on a hot surface.  |例句：I made a stack of clapcakes for breakfast. = 我做了好多煎饼当早餐。
例句：The clapcakes were light and fluffy. = 煎饼松软可口。|近义词：pancake, griddlecake|反义词：无
