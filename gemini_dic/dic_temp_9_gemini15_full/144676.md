outyelling:/ˌaʊtˈjɛlɪŋ/| v.|大声喊叫，盖过…的声音|The act of yelling louder than someone else; to shout over someone.|例句：The children were outyelling each other, making it hard to hear the teacher.|近义词：overshouting, shouting down|反义词：whispering, murmuring|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outyells everyone in the room. = 他大声喊叫盖过了房间里所有人的声音。
They often outyell each other during arguments. = 他们吵架时经常互相大声喊叫，盖过对方的声音。
