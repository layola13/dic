/lɜːr/| v.|潜伏；窥探；潜行|to lie hidden or in wait, especially in order to attack; to secretly watch someone or something.|例句：The lion lurked in the tall grass, waiting for its prey.  狮子潜伏在高高的草丛中，等待着猎物。
例句：The detective lurked in the shadows, observing the suspect.  侦探潜伏在阴影中，观察着嫌疑人。|近义词：hide, stalk, slink|反义词：reveal, expose|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lurks in the shadows. = 他潜伏在阴影中。
They lurk around the corner. = 他们潜伏在拐角处。

一般过去时:
The cat lurked near the mouse hole. = 猫潜伏在老鼠洞附近。

She has lurked online, watching her ex's profile. = 她一直在网上潜伏，查看她前男友的资料。

过去完成时:
