rajah:/ˈrɑːdʒɑː/ (英式)/ˈrɑːʒɑː/ (美式)| n. |王公；酋长；首领 |A ruler of a region or tribe, especially in India.  The term often implies a hereditary title and significant power, though the exact level of authority varied considerably depending on the specific context and historical period. |例句：The rajah ruled his kingdom with an iron fist.  这位王公铁腕统治他的王国。

例句：The villagers paid tribute to the rajah. 村民们向王公进贡。


近义词：king, prince, chief, sovereign, potentate |反义词：subject, peasant, commoner |词性: 名词
