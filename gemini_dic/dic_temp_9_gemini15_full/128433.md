newlywed:/ˈnuːliˌwɛd/ | adj. n. | 新婚的；新婚夫妇 | Newlywed refers to a person who has recently been married.  It can be used as an adjective to describe someone who is recently married, or as a noun to refer to a recently married couple. | Example 1:  The newlywed couple honeymooned in Hawaii. (新婚夫妇在夏威夷度蜜月。) Example 2: They are newlyweds, full of love and excitement. (他们是新婚夫妇，充满了爱和兴奋。) |近义词： newly married, recently married |反义词：long-married, veteran couple | 词性：adj., n.
