recomplicating:/ˌriːˈkɑːmplɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. |使再次复杂化；使更复杂 | Making something complicated again, or adding more complexity to something that was already complex.  | The new regulations are recomplicating the already burdensome tax system. 新规定使本来就繁重的税收制度更加复杂。  | complicating, intensifying, exacerbating | simplifying, clarifying, easing | 动词
