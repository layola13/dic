lactamide:/ˈlæktæmaɪd/| n. |乳酰胺|Lactamide is an amide derived from lactic acid. It's a naturally occurring compound found in small amounts in various biological systems and is used in various applications, including cosmetics and pharmaceuticals.  It is a colorless crystalline solid that is soluble in water. |Example: Lactamide is a humectant used in some cosmetics to retain moisture. 乳酰胺是一种用于某些化妆品中以保持水分的保湿剂。|近义词: N-acetyl-lactic acid (a related compound)|反义词:  None readily applicable.
