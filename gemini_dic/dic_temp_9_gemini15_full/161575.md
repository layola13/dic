pottering:/ˈpɒtərɪŋ/| v.|无所事事地忙活；慢吞吞地做事；闲逛|To potter is to occupy oneself in a leisurely and unproductive way, often doing small tasks without a particular aim.|例句：I spent the afternoon pottering around in the garden.  我整个下午都在花园里闲逛。
例句：He was pottering about in the kitchen, making a cup of tea. 他在厨房里慢吞吞地做茶。|近义词：fussing, fiddling, dawdling, trifling|反义词：hustling, rushing, working diligently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He potters around the house every Sunday. = 他每个星期天都在房子周围闲逛。
She potters in the garden for hours. = 她会在花园里忙活好几个小时。


一般过去时:
I pottered around the shops yesterday afternoon. = 我昨天一下午都在商店里闲逛。
They pottered about in the park all day. = 他们一整天都在公园里闲逛。

She's pottered away at her hobby for years. = 她多年来一直不紧不慢地从事着她的爱好。


过去完成时:

We had pottered about the town all day, feeling relaxed. = 我们一整天都在镇上闲逛，感觉很放松。
