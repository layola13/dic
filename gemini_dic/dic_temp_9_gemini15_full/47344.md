de-sludging:/diːˈslʌdʒɪŋ/| v.| 除去淤泥；清除沉淀物 | The process of removing sludge or sediment from a system, often industrial equipment or pipelines.  It involves removing accumulated thick, semi-solid material. |例句：The factory needs regular de-sludging of its processing tanks to maintain efficiency. 工厂需要定期清除加工槽中的淤泥以保持效率。
The municipality initiated a de-sludging project to improve the drainage system. 市政当局启动了一个清除淤泥的项目以改善排水系统。|近义词：cleaning, purging, dredging|反义词：sludging, accumulating
