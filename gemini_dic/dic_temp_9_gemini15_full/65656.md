evaporators:/ɪˈvæpəreɪtərz/| n. |蒸发器|A device or apparatus used to evaporate liquids, typically by boiling them.  Evaporators are commonly used in various industrial processes, such as desalination, food processing, and chemical manufacturing, to remove water or other solvents from a solution.|例句：The factory uses large evaporators to concentrate the sugar solution. 工厂使用大型蒸发器浓缩糖溶液。

The chemical plant installed new evaporators to improve efficiency. 化工厂安装了新的蒸发器以提高效率。|近义词：vaporizers, dryers|反义词：condensers
