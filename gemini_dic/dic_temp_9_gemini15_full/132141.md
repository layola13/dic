noncultivation:/ˌnɒnˌkʌltiˈveɪʃən/| n. |非耕作；未开垦；荒废|The absence of cultivation; the state of being uncultivated or unplanted.  It often refers to land left fallow, or areas not used for agriculture or gardening.  This can be due to various reasons, including environmental limitations, economic factors, or deliberate choices.|例句：The noncultivation of the land led to soil erosion.  土地未经耕作导致土壤侵蚀。

The continued noncultivation of this field has resulted in an overgrowth of weeds. 这块田地长期未经耕作，导致杂草丛生。|近义词：neglect, abandonment, fallowness|反义词：cultivation, tillage, planting|词性 n.
