compasser:/ˈkʌmpəsər/| n. |指南针；圆规；制图员| A compasser is a tool used for drawing circles or arcs, or a person who uses such a tool for drawing maps or plans.  It can also refer to a magnetic compass used for navigation.|例句：The architect used a compasser to draw the perfect circle for the building's foundation. 建筑师用圆规为建筑的地基画了一个完美的圆圈。|例句：The navigator relied on his compasser to guide him across the ocean.  领航员依靠他的指南针引导他穿越海洋。|近义词：compass, draftsman, cartographer |反义词：None
