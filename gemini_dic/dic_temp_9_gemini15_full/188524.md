rug:/rʌɡ/| n. |地毯，毛毯|A rug is a piece of thick fabric, usually woven, used to cover part of a floor.  It can be made of various materials such as wool, cotton, or synthetic fibers. Rugs are used for warmth, decoration, and to protect floors.|例句：We placed a thick rug in front of the fireplace to keep our feet warm. 我们在壁炉前铺了一块厚地毯来保暖。|例句：The rug in the living room is hand-woven and very beautiful. 客厅里的地毯是手工编织的，非常漂亮。|近义词：carpet, mat|反义词：bare floor
