preluder: /ˈpreljuːdər/ | n. |序曲，前奏；先兆 | A prelude is an introductory piece of music, especially one that precedes a longer composition. It can also refer to something that serves as an introduction or precursor to something else.  |例句：The concert began with a beautiful piano prelude.  这场音乐会以一首优美的钢琴前奏曲开始。
The unusual calm before the storm felt like a prelude to disaster.暴风雨前的异样平静感觉像是灾难的前兆。|近义词：introduction, preface, precursor, overture |反义词：conclusion, aftermath, sequel
