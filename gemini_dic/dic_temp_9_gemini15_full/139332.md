oboval:/oʊˈboʊvəl/| adj.| 卵形的，倒卵形的 |  Oval-shaped, wider at one end and tapering to a point at the other.  The term is often used in botany to describe the shape of leaves or other plant parts. | Example: The leaf had an oboval shape, broader at the apex than at the base.  叶子的形状是倒卵形的，顶端比基部宽。|近义词：ovate, elliptic, oblong|反义词：lanceolate, linear
