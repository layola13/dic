scrum:/ˈskrʌm/| n.|争球，混乱，敏捷开发方法|a.  Scrum is a framework for developing, delivering, and sustaining complex products. It's an iterative and incremental approach that emphasizes teamwork, accountability, and iterative progress toward a well-defined goal.  |例句：Our team uses scrum methodology to manage our software projects.  我们的团队使用Scrum方法来管理我们的软件项目。|近义词：melee, scrimmage, rugby|反义词：order, organization
