fathers-in-law: /ˈfɑːðərzɪnˈlɔː/ | n. |岳父(们)/公公(们) |  A father-in-law is the father of one's spouse.  The plural form, fathers-in-law, refers to multiple fathers-in-law.  This term encompasses both the father of one's husband and the father of one's wife. |  例句：My fathers-in-law are both retired teachers. 我的岳父和公公都是退休教师。

My relationship with my father-in-law is excellent. 我和我的岳父关系很好。
