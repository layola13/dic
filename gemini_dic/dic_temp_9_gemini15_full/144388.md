outstricken: /ˌaʊtˈstrɪkən/ | adj. | 被击垮的；被击中的；被遗弃的 |  The word "outstricken" is a relatively rare and archaic word, meaning overwhelmed or struck down, often by misfortune or affliction. It suggests a state of being severely affected or defeated.  It implies a sudden and forceful impact. | Example:  He felt outstricken by grief after the loss of his wife.  他妻子去世后，他悲痛欲绝。  The once-proud kingdom was outstricken by war and famine.  曾经骄傲的王国被战争和饥荒击垮了。 | 近义词：overwhelmed, devastated, stricken, afflicted | 反义词：unharmed, unscathed, triumphant
