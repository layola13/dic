demonologist:/ˌdiːməˈnɒlədʒɪst/|/n.|恶魔学家，驱魔师|A demonologist is a person who studies demons, their nature, and their activities.  They may also be involved in exorcism or other practices related to dealing with demons.|例句：The demonologist examined the strange symbols found at the crime scene. 恶魔学家检查了在犯罪现场发现的奇怪符号。

例句：The renowned demonologist claimed he could banish the evil entity from the house. 著名的恶魔学家声称他可以将邪恶实体从房子里驱逐出去。


近义词：exorcist, paranormal investigator|反义词：无
