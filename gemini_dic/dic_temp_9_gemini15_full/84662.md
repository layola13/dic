handbooks:/ˈhændbʊks/| n. |手册；指南|A handbook is a concise book giving information or instructions on a particular subject.  It is often used as a quick reference guide. |Example: The company provided a handbook for new employees explaining company policies and procedures. 公司为新员工提供了一本手册，解释公司政策和规程。|近义词：manual, guide, directory |反义词：None
