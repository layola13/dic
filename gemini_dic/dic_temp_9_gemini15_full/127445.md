nebulae:/ˈnɛbjʊliː/| n.|星云|A nebula is a cloud of gas and dust in outer space, visible as a luminous patch or spot.  Nebulae are often the birthplaces of stars.|例句：The telescope revealed a stunning nebula, swirling with vibrant colors. 望远镜显示出一个令人惊叹的星云，色彩斑斓地旋转着。|例句：Many different types of nebulae exist, each with its own unique characteristics. 存在许多不同类型的星云，每种都有其独特的特性。|近义词：nebular cloud, cosmic cloud|反义词：star (in the sense of a fully formed star, not a star forming in a nebula)|名词
