saskatchewanian: /ˌsæskætʃˈwɑːnɪən/ | adj. n. |萨斯喀彻温人；萨斯喀彻温省的 | A person who lives in or comes from Saskatchewan, Canada.  The term can be used as an adjective to describe something related to Saskatchewan. |

例句：

He is a Saskatchewanian and very proud of his province.  他是一个萨斯喀彻温人，并且非常以他的省份为荣。

The Saskatchewanian landscape is vast and beautiful. 萨斯喀彻温省的风景辽阔而美丽。


近义词：Saskatchewani (less common)


反义词：无


词性：形容词，名词


词源：源于加拿大萨斯喀彻温省（Saskatchewan）的名称，加上后缀“-ian”构成，表示“属于…的；…的人”。  The name "Saskatchewan" itself is derived from the Cree word *kisiskâciwanis*, meaning "the river that flows swiftly".
