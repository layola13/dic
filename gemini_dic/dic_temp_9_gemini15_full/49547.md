dematerialize:/ˌdiːməˈtɪəriəlaɪz/| v.|使消失；使非物质化；|to cease to be material; to become immaterial or intangible; to disappear suddenly and mysteriously.|The magician seemed to dematerialize before our very eyes. 魔术师似乎在我们眼前消失了。The company's assets seem to have dematerialized. 公司的资产似乎消失了。|近义词：vanish, disappear, evaporate|反义词：materialize, appear


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The magician dematerializes the rabbit. = 魔术师让兔子消失了。
He dematerializes his worries by meditation. 他通过冥想消除他的烦恼。


一般过去时:
The object dematerialized in a flash of light. = 物体在一闪光中消失了。
She felt her anxieties dematerialize after a long talk.  一场长谈后，她感到焦虑消失了。


现在完成时:
The old photographs have dematerialized over time. = 旧照片随着时间的推移消失了。
My fears have dematerialized since I found a solution.  自从找到解决方法后，我的恐惧消失了。


过去完成时:
The evidence had dematerialized before the police arrived. = 警员到达之前证据消失了。
He realized his confidence had dematerialized after the setback. 他意识到挫折后他的信心消失了。


将来时:
The illusion will dematerialize at the end of the show. = 幻象将在演出结束时消失。
He hopes his worries will dematerialize with time. 他希望他的担忧会随着时间而消失。


虚拟语气:
If the object dematerialized, we would be amazed. = 如果物体消失了，我们会感到惊讶。
I wish my problems would dematerialize. 我希望我的问题能消失。


被动语态:
The rabbit was dematerialized by the magician. = 兔子被魔术师变没了。
His concerns were dematerialized by the good news. 他的担忧被好消息消除了。


疑问句:
Does the object dematerialize at the end of the experiment? = 实验结束时物体是否会消失？
Will his anxieties dematerialize after this meeting? 这次会议后他的焦虑会消失吗？


否定句:
The evidence did not dematerialize; it was stolen. = 证据没有消失；它是被偷了。
His anger did not dematerialize easily. 他的怒气不容易消散。


PHRASE->
dematerialize into thin air = 消失得无影无踪
dematerialize from sight = 从视线中消失
dematerialize before one's eyes = 在某人眼前消失
suddenly dematerialize = 突然消失
gradually dematerialize = 逐渐消失
