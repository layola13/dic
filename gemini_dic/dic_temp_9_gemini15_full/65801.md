evidences:/ˈɛvɪdənsɪz/| n. |证据(复数)|Proofs or signs that something is true.  It is the plural form of "evidence".  The use of "evidences" as a plural noun is considered less common and often replaced by "pieces of evidence" or simply "evidence".|The evidences presented in court were compelling.  法庭上提出的证据令人信服。

The scientific evidences support the theory. 科学证据支持这一理论。|proof, testimony,  indications |absence of evidence, lack of proof|名词
