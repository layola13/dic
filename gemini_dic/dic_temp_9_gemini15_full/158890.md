plumule:/ˈpluːmjuːl/| n.|胚芽|The plumule is the part of a seed that develops into the shoot of a plant. It is the first part of the seedling to emerge from the seed, and it contains the first leaves of the plant.  The plumule is located above the cotyledons and below the radicle.|The plumule of the bean seed is very small.|近义词：shoot, sprout|反义词：radicle
