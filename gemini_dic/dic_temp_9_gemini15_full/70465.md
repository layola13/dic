figurines:/ˈfɪɡjəˌriːnz/| n.|小雕像，小塑像|Small models of people or animals, often made of plastic, wood, or ceramic.  Figurines are often collected as decorative items or used in games.|例句：She collects porcelain figurines of animals. 她收集动物瓷器小雕像。

例句：The children were playing with toy figurines. 孩子们正在玩玩具小雕像。


近义词：statuettes, miniatures, models|反义词：large sculptures, life-size figures|词性:名词

词源:
The word "figurine" comes from the French word "figurine," which in turn is a diminutive of "figure," meaning "shape" or "form."  The ultimate origin is the Latin word "figura," sharing the same root.
