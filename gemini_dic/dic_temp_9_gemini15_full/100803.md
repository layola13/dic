intriguers:/ɪnˈtriːɡərz/| n. |阴谋者；煽动者|People who intrigue, scheming to gain an advantage, often secretly. They plot and plan, often to the detriment of others.  |The intriguers were finally exposed, their plot foiled by a diligent investigator.|近义词：conspirators, plotters, schemers|反义词：benefactors, altruists|名词
