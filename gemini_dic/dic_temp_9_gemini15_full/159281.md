point-and-shoot: /ˈpɔɪnt ænd ʃuːt/ | n. adj. v. | 指向并拍摄；傻瓜相机 |


英文解析:  Describes a type of camera, typically a compact camera, that is easy to use and requires minimal technical expertise. The user simply points the camera at the subject and presses the shutter button to take a picture.  The term can also be used as a verb, describing the act of taking a photo with such a camera.  As an adjective, it describes something that is simple and straightforward to operate.


例句:

n.  My first camera was a simple point-and-shoot. = 我的第一台相机是一台简单的傻瓜相机。

v.  I just pointed and shoot, I don't worry about settings. = 我只是指向并拍摄，我不担心设置。

adj. This point-and-shoot camera is perfect for beginners. = 这款傻瓜相机非常适合初学者。


近义词:  snapshot camera, compact camera, instant camera (depending on context)


反义词:  professional camera, SLR camera, DSLR camera (implying more complex operation)


词性: noun, verb, adjective
