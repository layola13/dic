shelve:/ʃɛlv/| /ʃelv/| vt. n.|搁置；把…放在架子上；延期；|to put something on a shelf; to postpone something.|例句：I've shelved that project for now. 我暂时搁置了那个项目。

例句：He shelved the books carefully. 他小心地把书放在书架上。


近义词：postpone, delay, defer, table|反义词：proceed, continue, resume|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shelves his books alphabetically. = 他按字母顺序排列他的书。
They shelve the new products in the warehouse. = 他们把新产品放在仓库的架子上。


一般过去时:
She shelved the project due to budget cuts. = 由于预算削减，她搁置了该项目。
We shelved the idea after careful consideration. = 在仔细考虑之后，我们放弃了这个想法。


现在完成时:
They have shelved the controversial bill. = 他们已经搁置了那项有争议的法案。
He has shelved his plans to travel abroad. = 他已经搁置了他出国旅行的计划。


过去完成时:
She had shelved the manuscript for several years. = 她已经搁置了那份手稿好几年了。
They had shelved the proposal before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就已经搁置了那个提案。


将来时:
We will shelve the decision until next week. = 我们将把决定推迟到下周。
They will shelve the project if funding is not secured. = 如果资金没有得到保障，他们将搁置该项目。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would shelve the less important tasks. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们会搁置不太重要的任务。
I wish I could shelve my worries for a while. = 我希望我能暂时放下我的担忧。


被动语态:
The project was shelved due to lack of funding. = 该项目由于缺乏资金而被搁置。
The proposal was shelved after much debate. = 在经过激烈的辩论之后，该提案被搁置。


疑问句:
Will they shelve the plan? = 他们会搁置这个计划吗？
Have they shelved the idea permanently? = 他们永久搁置了这个想法了吗？


否定句:
He didn't shelve his responsibilities. = 他没有逃避他的责任。
They won't shelve the important tasks. = 他们不会搁置重要的任务。


PHRASE->
shelve a plan = 搁置计划
shelve a project = 搁置项目
shelve a decision = 搁置决定
shelve a book = 把书放在书架上
shelve a proposal = 搁置提案
