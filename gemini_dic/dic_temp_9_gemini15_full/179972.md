refigure:/ˌriːˈfɪɡər/| vt.|重新计算；重新考虑；重新塑造；重新构想|To calculate or consider something again; to reshape or reinterpret something.|例句：We need to refigure the budget to accommodate the increased costs. 我们需要重新计算预算以适应增加的成本。

例句：The artist refigured the landscape in a more abstract style.  艺术家以更抽象的风格重新塑造了风景。


近义词：recalculate, reconsider, reshape, reinterpret|反义词：finalize, confirm, accept
