roundworm:/ˈraʊndwɜːrm/| n. |线虫；圆虫|A slender, cylindrical worm, typically parasitic, with a complete digestive tract.|例句：The farmer found many roundworms in the soil of his field.  这位农民在他的田地土壤中发现了许多线虫。|例句：Microscopic roundworms can cause serious diseases in humans. 微小的线虫会引起人类的严重疾病。|近义词：nematode|反义词：无
