nationalist:/ˈnæʃənəlɪst/| n. adj.|民族主义者；民族主义的|A person who strongly identifies with and advocates for their nation's interests, often to the exclusion of others.  A nationalist believes in the importance of national unity and independence. |例句：He is a staunch nationalist who believes in the superiority of his country. 他是一个坚定的民族主义者，相信自己国家的优越性。

例句：The nationalist movement gained momentum after the war. 战后民族主义运动获得了发展势头。


近义词：patriot, jingoist|反义词：internationalist, globalist|名词，形容词
