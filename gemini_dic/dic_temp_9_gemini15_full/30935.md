chairmaning:/ˈtʃeərmænɪŋ/| v.|担任主席；主持会议 |The act of presiding over a meeting or organization as chairman.  It implies actively leading and managing the proceedings. |例句：He was chairmaning the meeting with efficiency and diplomacy. 他以高效和外交手腕主持会议。 He's been chairmaning the board for over a decade. 他担任董事会主席已超过十年。|近义词：presiding, moderating, conducting, leading |反义词：participating (as a member, not chair)
