nonhumanistic:/ˌnɒnˌhjuːməˈnɪstɪk/| adj. |非人道主义的，非人本主义的 |Not emphasizing human values or the human element.  It often implies a disregard for human feelings, needs, or rights, or an approach that prioritizes other factors over human concerns. |Example: The nonhumanistic approach to factory farming prioritizes profit over animal welfare.  非人道主义的工厂化养殖方式将利润置于动物福利之上。The experiment involved a nonhumanistic disregard for the subjects' emotional well-being. 实验中非人道地无视受试者的情绪健康。|近义词：inhuman, brutal, unfeeling, unsympathetic |反义词：humanistic, humane, compassionate, empathetic
