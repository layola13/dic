pathogenesy:/ˌpæθəˈdʒɛnəsi/| n. |发病机制；致病机理|The study of the origination and development of a disease.  It involves the mechanisms by which a disease develops, progresses, and causes symptoms.  This field explores the complex interplay between the causative agent (e.g., bacteria, virus, genetic defect), the host's immune response, and environmental factors.|Example: Researchers are investigating the pathogenesy of Alzheimer's disease to find new treatments.  研究人员正在调查阿尔茨海默病的发病机制以寻找新的治疗方法。|近义词： pathogenesis, etiology, aetiology |反义词：cure, recovery
