knotting:/ˈnɒtɪŋ/ | v. | 打结；结节；纠结 | The act of tying a knot or forming knots.  It can also refer to the state of being knotted or tangled, or a cluster of knots. |例句：She is knotting the rope to secure the boat. = 她正在系绳子固定船只。

She was skilled at knotting various types of knots for sailing. = 她擅长系各种用于航海的绳结。


近义词：tying, fastening, binding | 反义词：untying, unknotting
