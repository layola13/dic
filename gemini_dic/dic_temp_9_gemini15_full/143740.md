outlustre: /ˌaʊtˈlʌstər/ | vt. |胜过；超过；比…更出色|To surpass or excel; to outshine.  This word is a relatively rare and somewhat archaic formation. It combines "out-" (meaning "to a greater extent than") with "lustre" (meaning "brightness, splendor, or excellence").|例句：Her performance outlustred all others.  她的表演胜过所有其他人。


Her talent outlustred that of her peers.  她的才能超过了她的同龄人。


近义词：outshine, outdo, surpass, excel, exceed, eclipse


反义词：underperform, be inferior to, be eclipsed by


词性：动词
