registrary:/ˈrɛdʒɪstrɛri/ | n. | 注册员；注册处；档案馆 | The person or office responsible for keeping official records, especially in a university.  A registrary is typically in charge of maintaining student records, handling admissions, and managing academic administration. | Example: The university registrary confirmed my enrollment. 大学注册员确认了我的入学。 |近义词：registrar, recorder |反义词：N/A
