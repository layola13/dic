resorting:/rɪˈzɔːrtɪŋ/| v.|诉诸；求助；凭借；采取|To resort to something means to do something only because you have tried other things and they have failed. It often implies a less desirable option as a last resort. |例句：He resorted to violence after all other options failed. 他在所有其他选择都失败后诉诸暴力。

例句：Facing bankruptcy, the company resorted to drastic cost-cutting measures. 面对破产，公司采取了严厉的成本削减措施。


近义词：turn to, have recourse to, apply to

反义词：avoiding, shunning

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They resort to violence when they feel threatened. = 当他们感到受到威胁时，他们会诉诸暴力。
We resort to prayer when we face difficulties. = 当我们面临困难时，我们会求助于祈祷。


一般过去时:
She resorted to deception to get what she wanted. = 她为了得到她想要的东西而采取了欺骗手段。
The army resorted to bombing after negotiations failed. = 谈判失败后，军队采取了轰炸行动。


现在完成时:
He has resorted to borrowing money from friends several times this year. = 他今年已经几次向朋友借钱了。
They have resorted to extreme measures to save the company. = 他们已经采取极端措施来挽救公司。


过去完成时:
They had resorted to begging on the streets before finding a job. = 在找到工作之前，他们曾在街上乞讨。
The government had resorted to rationing during the war. = 在战争期间，政府曾采取配给制。


将来时:
If all else fails, I will resort to legal action. = 如果其他一切失败，我将采取法律行动。
We may resort to hiring a consultant if we can't solve the problem ourselves. = 如果我们自己无法解决问题，我们可能会求助于聘请顾问。


虚拟语气:
If I were desperate, I would resort to lying. = 如果我绝望了，我会撒谎。
I wish I had not resorted to such harsh methods. = 我真希望我没有采取如此严厉的方法。


被动语态:
Violence was resorted to as a last measure. = 暴力作为最后手段被使用了。
Legal action was resorted to by the company to protect its interests. = 公司采取法律行动来保护其利益。


疑问句:
Did they resort to any illegal methods? = 他们采取了任何非法手段吗？
What measures will the government resort to? = 政府将采取什么措施？


否定句:
They did not resort to violence, even when provoked. = 即使被激怒，他们也没有诉诸暴力。
She refused to resort to blackmail, even though she was tempted. = 即使她很受诱惑，她也拒绝采取敲诈勒索的手段。
