imin:/ˈaɪmɪn/|pron.|我(我自己的)|I'm in.  意思是“我在里面”或者“我参与了”。 It is a shortened form of "I am in." indicating participation or presence. |例句：I'm in!  我参加！  I'm in, let's do it. 我同意，让我们开始吧。|近义词：I am, me|反义词：I'm out.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I'm in the office. = 我在办公室。
I'm working on the project. = 我正在做这个项目。


一般过去时:
I was in the park yesterday. = 我昨天在公园里。
I was working late last night. = 我昨晚工作到很晚。


现在完成时:
I've been in this city for five years. = 我在这个城市住了五年了。
I've been working hard recently. = 我最近一直在努力工作。


过去完成时:
I had been in London before I moved to Paris. = 我搬到巴黎之前已经在伦敦待过一段时间了。
I had been working on that problem for hours before I found the solution. = 在找到解决方案之前，我已经在这个问题上苦思冥想好几个小时了。


将来时:
I'll be in the meeting tomorrow. = 我明天将在会议上。
I'll be working from home next week. = 下周我将在家里工作。


虚拟语气:
If I were in your shoes, I would do it differently. = 如果我是你，我会采取不同的做法。
I wish I were in Hawaii right now. = 我真希望我现在在夏威夷。


被动语态:
I'm included in the project. = 我被包含在这个项目中。 (This is less common with "imin" due to its informal nature.)
He was mentioned in the report. (This is a similar construct, showing passive voice with a pronoun)


疑问句:
Am I in the right place? = 我在正确的地方吗？
Are you in the team? = 你在团队里吗？


否定句:
I'm not in the mood. = 我没心情。
I'm not in the meeting. = 我不在会议上。

PHRASE->
I'm in! = 我参加！/ 我同意！
I'm in trouble. = 我有麻烦了。
I'm in love. = 我恋爱了。
I'm in charge. = 我负责。
I'm in a hurry. = 我很匆忙。
