decay:/dɪˈkeɪ/ | n., v.| 腐烂；衰退；衰亡 | The process of rotting or decomposition; a gradual decline or deterioration. |例句：The fruit began to decay after a few days. 水果几天后开始腐烂。

The building has fallen into decay. 这座建筑物已经衰败了。|近义词：rot, decompose, deteriorate, decline |反义词：growth, flourish, thrive |词性:n., v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wood decays slowly in dry weather. = 干燥的天气下木材腐烂得很慢。
The empire is decaying rapidly. = 这个帝国正在迅速衰落。


一般过去时:
The apple decayed quickly in the sun. = 苹果在阳光下很快腐烂了。
The civilization decayed after the war. = 战争之后，这个文明衰落了。


现在完成时:
The building has decayed significantly over the years. = 这座建筑物多年来已经严重破败了。
Her health has decayed since the accident. = 自从事故发生后，她的健康状况一直在恶化。


过去完成时:
The ancient city had decayed long before the explorers arrived. = 探险家到达之前，这座古城早已衰败。
His reputation had decayed considerably by then. = 到那时，他的名誉已经大大受损了。


将来时:
The fruit will decay if you don't refrigerate it. = 如果你不冷藏，水果会腐烂。
The bridge will decay further without repairs. = 如果不维修，这座桥梁会进一步腐朽。


虚拟语气:
If the food were not refrigerated, it would decay quickly. = 如果食物不冷藏，它会很快腐烂。
If the government hadn't intervened, the economy would have decayed further. = 如果政府不干预，经济将会进一步衰退。


被动语态:
The old house was decayed beyond repair. = 老房子已经腐朽不堪，无法修理了。
The system is being decayed by corruption. = 这个系统正在被腐败侵蚀。


疑问句:
Is the wood decaying? = 木头在腐烂吗？
Is the moral fabric of society decaying? = 社会的道德结构正在衰败吗？


否定句:
The food didn't decay because it was preserved. = 食物没有腐烂，因为它被保存起来了。
The empire did not decay overnight. = 这个帝国并非一夜之间衰落的。


PHRASE->
radioactive decay: 放射性衰变
moral decay: 道德败坏
decay rate: 衰减率
tooth decay: 龋齿
heat decay: 热衰减

词源:
From Old French *decair, from Latin *dēcadere, from dē- ("down, away, off") + cadere ("to fall").  The sense evolution is from "to fall down" to "to fall into ruin," then to the modern meanings of decay and deterioration.
