nob:/nɒb/ | n. | 乡巴佬，笨蛋，傻瓜 | A person considered to be unrefined, unsophisticated, or stupid.  Often used in a derogatory way. | He's such a nob!  他真是个乡巴佬！ | 近义词：bumpkin, yokel, oaf, chump | 反义词：sophisticate, intellectual
