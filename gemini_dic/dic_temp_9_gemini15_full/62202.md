encountering:/ɪnˈkaʊntərɪŋ/| v. | 遇到；遭遇；偶然碰到 | The act of meeting someone or something unexpectedly or by chance.  It can also refer to facing a problem or difficulty. | 例句：I encountered a problem with my computer. = 我遇到了电脑问题。


例句：She encountered her old friend at the supermarket. = 她在超市偶遇了她的老朋友。


近义词：meeting, facing, experiencing | 反义词：avoiding, escaping, ignoring | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am encountering many challenges in my new job. = 我在我的新工作中遇到了很多挑战。
We encounter new people every day. = 我们每天都会遇到新的人。

一般过去时:
I encountered a bear on my hike yesterday. = 昨天我在徒步旅行时遇到了一只熊。
They encountered several obstacles during their journey. = 他们在旅途中遇到了几个障碍。

现在完成时:
I have encountered this problem before. = 我以前遇到过这个问题。
She has encountered many difficulties in her life. = 她一生中遇到了许多困难。

过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, they had encountered significant delays. = 当我到达时，他们已经遇到了很大的延误。
Before starting the project, we had encountered similar issues. = 在开始项目之前，我们遇到过类似的问题。

将来时:
I will encounter many difficulties in the future. = 我将来会遇到许多困难。
We will likely encounter resistance to our proposal. = 我们可能会遇到对我们提案的抵制。

虚拟语气:
If I encountered him again, I would apologize. = 如果我再次遇到他，我会道歉。
I wish I hadn't encountered such a difficult situation. = 我真希望我没有遇到这么困难的局面。

被动语态:
The problem was encountered by many users. = 这个问题被许多用户遇到。
Several unexpected challenges were encountered during the construction. = 在施工过程中遇到了几个意想不到的挑战。

疑问句:
What difficulties did you encounter during the research? = 在研究过程中你遇到了什么困难？
Have you ever encountered a situation like this before? = 你以前遇到过这种情况吗？

否定句:
I didn't encounter any problems during the presentation. = 我在演示过程中没有遇到任何问题。
They haven't encountered such a serious issue before. = 他们以前没有遇到过这么严重的问题。
