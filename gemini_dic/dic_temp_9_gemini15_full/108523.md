laywoman:/ˈleɪˌwʊmən/| n.|外行女子；非专业人士（女性）|A laywoman is a woman who is not a member of the clergy or a professional in a particular field.  It emphasizes a lack of specialized knowledge or training in a specific area.|Example:  Even a laywoman can understand the basic principles of this theory.  即使是一个外行女子也能理解这个理论的基本原理。|近义词：amateur, non-professional, novice|反义词：expert, professional, specialist
