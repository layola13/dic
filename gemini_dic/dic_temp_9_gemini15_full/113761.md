macrophysical:/ˌmækroʊˈfɪzɪkəl/| adj.|宏观的，巨观的 | Relating to or denoting physical phenomena that are observable on a large scale, as opposed to microscopic phenomena.  It refers to properties and behaviors visible to the naked eye or easily measurable with common instruments.  | 例句：The macrophysical properties of the material, such as its density and color, were easily determined.  该材料的宏观特性，如密度和颜色，很容易确定。

The macrophysical structure of the galaxy is complex and fascinating. 星系的宏观结构复杂而迷人。


近义词：macroscopic, large-scale, gross


反义词：microscopic, submicroscopic, minute
