pinnulated:/ˈpɪnəleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 羽状的；羽裂的 | Having small, pointed lobes or segments along the edge, resembling a feather.  This term is commonly used in botany to describe the shape of leaves or other plant parts. | The pinnulated leaves of the fern unfurled in the spring breeze.  蕨类的羽状叶在春风中舒展开来。 |  pinnate, feathery | entire, undivided | 形容词
