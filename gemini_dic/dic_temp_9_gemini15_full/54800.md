dispensers:/dɪˈspɛnsərz/ | n. |分配器；发放器；售货机 |A dispenser is a device that releases a substance, such as liquid, medicine, or money, in controlled amounts.  It can be a simple machine or a complex system.| Example: The office has a water dispenser for employees. 办公室为员工配备了饮水机。 The pharmacy uses an automated pill dispenser. 药房使用自动药丸分配器。|近义词：dispenser, distributor, vendor |反义词：receiver, collector


USAGE->

The machine dispenses drinks. = 这台机器供应饮料。
The bank dispenses cash. = 银行发放现金。
