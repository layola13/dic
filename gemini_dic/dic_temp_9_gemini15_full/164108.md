predismissing:/ˌpriːdɪˈsmɪsɪŋ/| adj. |预先解雇；提前解雇|Dismissing someone before the officially scheduled time or event.  It implies a preemptive action, often without proper procedure or explanation.|例句：The company engaged in predismissing employees, leading to widespread discontent. 公司提前解雇员工，导致了广泛的不满。

例句：The predismissing of the students before the final bell caused confusion. 学生们在最后铃声响起前被提前解雇造成了混乱。


近义词：preemptive dismissal, early dismissal, premature dismissal|反义词：postponing dismissal, retaining, keeping


词性：形容词
