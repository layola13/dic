leapfrogging:/ˈliːpfˌrɒɡɪŋ/| n. v.|跳跃前进；超越；飞跃式发展|The act of surpassing or overtaking someone or something by a significant margin, often through innovation or rapid progress.  It implies a quick and dramatic advancement that leaves others behind.|例句：China's leapfrogging in technology has surprised many Western nations.  中国的技术飞跃令许多西方国家感到惊讶。

The developing country leapfrogged the developed countries in mobile phone adoption. 发展中国家在移动电话普及方面超越了发达国家。|近义词：surpassing, overtaking, outstripping, bypassing|反义词：lagging behind, falling behind


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Developing countries are leapfrogging developed nations in some technological areas. = 发展中国家在某些技术领域正在超越发达国家。
He leapfrogs over obstacles in his career path. = 他在职业道路上跨越障碍。


一般过去时:
The company leapfrogged its competitors by introducing a revolutionary new product. = 该公司通过推出革命性的新产品超越了竞争对手。
They leapfrogged the traditional marketing methods and adopted digital strategies. = 他们跳过了传统的营销方法，采用了数字策略。


现在完成时:
Many Asian nations have leapfrogged to become major economic players. = 许多亚洲国家已经飞跃成为主要的经济参与者。
Our team has leapfrogged the competition and secured the top spot. = 我们的团队已经超越了竞争对手，获得了第一名。


过去完成时:
By the time the new technology was released, the company had already leapfrogged its rivals. = 当新技术发布时，该公司已经超越了竞争对手。
Having leapfrogged the initial challenges, they were ready to expand. = 克服了最初的挑战后，他们准备扩张。


将来时:
Experts predict that this new technology will leapfrog existing solutions. = 专家预测这项新技术将超越现有解决方案。
The company plans to leapfrog its competitors in the next quarter. = 公司计划在下一季度超越其竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If we adopted this new strategy, we could leapfrog our competitors. = 如果我们采用这个新策略，我们就能超越竞争对手。
I wish we had leapfrogged the market earlier. = 我希望我们早点超越市场。


被动语态:
The smaller company was leapfrogged by its larger rival. = 这家规模较小的公司被其规模更大的竞争对手超越了。
The older technology is being leapfrogged by newer innovations. = 旧技术正在被新的创新所超越。


疑问句:
Can this new technology leapfrog the current limitations? =这项新技术能够克服当前的局限性吗？
Will this strategy allow us to leapfrog the competition? = 这个策略能让咱们超越竞争对手吗？


否定句:
The company failed to leapfrog its competitors in the market. = 这家公司未能超越市场上的竞争对手。
They didn't leapfrog the difficulties, instead, they overcame them step by step. = 他们没有跳过困难，而是逐步克服了困难。

leapfrogging into the future = 向未来飞跃


词源:
The word "leapfrog" itself comes from the children's game of the same name, where children jump over each other.  The figurative use of "leapfrogging" to describe surpassing someone or something developed from this image of surpassing by jumping over.
