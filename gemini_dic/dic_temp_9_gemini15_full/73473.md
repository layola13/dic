foreprize:/ˈfɔːrpraɪz/| n.|预奖，预付奖金|A prize or reward given in advance, typically to encourage or incentivize certain actions or achievements.  It's a less common term, often used in specialized contexts.|例句：The company offered a foreprize to the team that completed the project ahead of schedule.  公司向提前完成项目的团队提供了预付奖金。|近义词：advance payment, prepayment, bonus, incentive|反义词：penalty, fine
