companionship:/kəmˈpænɪənʃɪp/ | n. |友谊；陪伴；伙伴关系 |The state of being a companion; fellowship; friendly association.  Companionship implies a mutual relationship characterized by shared interests, activities, or feelings.  It suggests a sense of belonging and mutual support. |例句：He found companionship in his dog. 他在狗身上找到了陪伴。

例句：The elderly woman craved companionship after her husband passed away.  这位老妇人在丈夫去世后渴望陪伴。


近义词：fellowship, camaraderie, companionship, sociability, friendship | 反义词：loneliness, isolation, solitude | 词性：名词
