plentiest:/ˈplɛntiɪst/| adj.| 最多的，最丰富的 | Most abundant; greatest in quantity or amount.  The word is the superlative form of "plentiful".|例句：The farmer had the plentiest harvest of apples this year.  今年这位农民收获了最多的苹果。

The plentiest resources were allocated to the most crucial project. 最丰富的资源被分配给了最重要的项目。|近义词：most abundant, richest, fullest |反义词：scantest, least, fewest | 词性：形容词
