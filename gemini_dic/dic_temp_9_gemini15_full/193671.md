self-cognitions:/ˌsɛlf ˈkɒɡnɪʃənz/ | n. |自我认知，自我意识 |Understanding and awareness of one's own thoughts, feelings, and behaviors.  It encompasses self-perception, self-esteem, and self-concept.  |例句：Developing strong self-cognitions is crucial for mental well-being.  发展强大的自我认知对于心理健康至关重要。

例句：Her self-cognitions were distorted by her trauma. 她的创伤扭曲了她的自我认知。


近义词：self-awareness, self-perception, self-understanding | 反义词：self-deception, unawareness | 词性：n.
