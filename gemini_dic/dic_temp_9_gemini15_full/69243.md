feasta:/ˈfiːstə/| n.|盛宴，宴会|A large meal, especially one held to celebrate a special occasion.  |例句：The wedding feasta was a lavish affair. 婚礼盛宴是一场奢华的活动。|例句：They threw a huge feasta to celebrate their anniversary. 他们举办了一场盛大的宴会来庆祝他们的结婚纪念日。|近义词：banquet, feast, celebration |反义词：fast, famine
