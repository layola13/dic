potage:/ˈpoʊtɑːʒ/| n. |浓汤；菜汤 |A thick soup, often puréed.  Potage can be made with various vegetables, meats, or legumes. |例句：The chef prepared a delicious potage of roasted tomatoes and basil. 主厨准备了一道美味的烤番茄罗勒浓汤。|例句：For a light lunch, I had a simple vegetable potage. 午餐我吃了一份简单的蔬菜浓汤。|近义词：soup, stew, broth |反义词：solid food
