inherits:/ɪnˈhɛrɪts/| vt.|继承，遗传|To receive (property, a title, etc.) from a predecessor, especially by legal right or descent.  It also means to possess a quality or characteristic genetically from one's parents or ancestors.|例句：He inherits his father's business. 他继承了他父亲的事业。|例句：She inherits her mother's beautiful eyes. 她遗传了她母亲漂亮的眼睛。|近义词：receive, succeed to, acquire|反义词：disinherit, bequeath


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He inherits a large fortune. = 他继承了一大笔财产。
She inherits her mother's artistic talent. = 她继承了她母亲的艺术天赋。

一般过去时:
My grandfather inherited a valuable painting. = 我祖父继承了一幅名贵的画。
They inherited the family home. = 他们继承了祖传的房子。

现在完成时:
He has inherited his uncle's estate. = 他继承了他叔叔的产业。
She has inherited a strong sense of responsibility. = 她遗传了一种强烈的责任感。

过去完成时:
They had inherited the business before the economic downturn. = 经济衰退之前，他们已经继承了这家公司。
He had inherited a significant debt from his father. = 他从他父亲那里继承了一笔巨额债务。

将来时:
She will inherit the family farm. = 她将继承家族农场。
He will inherit a significant sum of money. = 他将继承一大笔钱。

虚拟语气:
If he inherited the company, he would modernize it. = 如果他继承了公司，他会使它现代化。
I wish I had inherited my grandmother's artistic talent. = 我真希望我继承了我祖母的艺术天赋。

被动语态:
The property was inherited by his nephew. = 这处房产由他的侄子继承。
The family business was inherited by the eldest son. = 家族企业由长子继承。

疑问句:
Did she inherit anything from her parents? = 她从父母那里继承了什么吗？
Will he inherit the throne? = 他会继承王位吗？

否定句:
He did not inherit his father's business. = 他没有继承他父亲的事业。
She didn't inherit any money from her aunt. = 她没有从她姑姑那里继承任何钱。
