quasi-managed:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈmænɪdʒd/| adj. |准管理的，半管理的 |Approaching or resembling management but lacking complete or formal control.|The quasi-managed fund invests in a diversified portfolio of assets, offering some degree of oversight but not the strict controls of a fully managed fund.  |近义词：semi-managed, partially managed |反义词：fully managed, unregulated
