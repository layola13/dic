experiencer:/ɪkˈspɪəriənsər/| n. |经历者，体验者 |The experiencer is the one who undergoes the event described by the verb.  An experiencer is typically animate, but not necessarily human. For example, in the sentence “The dog felt the cold,” the dog is the experiencer. |例句：In the sentence "The child felt the cold", the child is the experiencer.  孩子在句子“孩子感到寒冷”中是体验者。


例句：The patient, the experiencer of the surgery, was understandably nervous. 病人，手术的体验者，可以理解地很紧张。


近义词：undergoer, subject


反义词：agent (in grammatical terms)


词性：名词
