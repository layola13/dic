reprize:/ˈriːpraɪz/| v.| 重演；重温；重新获得 |To repeat a performance or an event; to take something back or regain something.  |例句：The band will reprise their hit song at the concert.乐队将在音乐会上重演他们的热门歌曲。

例句：He reprized his role as Hamlet after a long break.  他在长时间休息后重新扮演哈姆雷特的角色。


近义词：repeat, reiterate, reproduce, regain


反义词：abandon, relinquish, forfeit


词性：动词
