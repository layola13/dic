slater:/ˈsleɪtər/| n. |瓦工；批评者；(尤指鸟类的) 石板色 | A slater is a person who lays or repairs slates, typically on roofs.  The word can also refer to someone who criticizes harshly or severely. In ornithology, it can describe a bird with slate-colored plumage. |例句：He's a skilled slater, able to repair even the most complex roof damage. 他是一位熟练的瓦工，能够修理甚至是最复杂的屋顶损坏。

The birdwatcher identified the small, slate-colored bird as a slater.  观鸟者将这只小的石板色的小鸟识别为一种石板色鸟。


近义词：roofer (for the first sense), critic, reviewer (for the second sense) |反义词：praiser, flatterer (for the second sense)


词源:源自古英语单词 *slæter，意思是“铺瓦的人”，来源于slate（石板）。
