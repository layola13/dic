get-around: /ˌɡɛt əˈraʊnd/ | v. | 规避；逃避；四处走动；应付；解决 |

英文解析:  The verb "get around" has several meanings, depending on the context. It can mean to avoid something, to travel from place to place, to manage to cope with a situation, or to find a way to overcome a difficulty.

例句:

He tried to get around the law. 他试图逃避法律。

We got around the city in a taxi. 我们乘坐出租车在城市里四处走动。

She's good at getting around problems. 她擅长解决问题。

She's got a lot of friends and can get around quite easily. 她有很多朋友，所以很容易就能应付各种事情。


近义词:  avoid, circumvent, maneuver, manage, cope, travel, negotiate

反义词:  confront, face, succumb
