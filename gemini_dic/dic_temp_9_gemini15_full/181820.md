relishes:/ˈrɛliʃɪz/| n. |美味佳肴；享受； relish (动词的三单形式) |Something that is enjoyed or that adds flavor to food.  It can also refer to the enjoyment of something.|例句：The chef prepared a variety of relishes to accompany the main course.  主厨准备了各种美味佳肴来搭配主菜。

The guests relished the opportunity to socialize. 客人们享受着社交的机会。|近义词：savory, delicacy, enjoyment, pleasure |反义词：dislike, aversion, distaste


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He relishes spicy food. 他喜欢吃辣的食物。
She relishes the challenge. 她喜欢这个挑战。

一般过去时:
We relished the delicious meal. 我们享受了美味的一餐。
They relished their victory. 他们享受着他们的胜利。

现在完成时:
I have relished every moment of this trip. 我享受了这次旅行的每一刻。
She has relished the opportunity to learn new skills. 她一直很享受学习新技能的机会。

过去完成时:
He had relished the anticipation of the trip before he left. 他在出发前就享受着旅行的期待。
They had relished the quiet evening before the storm hit.暴风雨来临之前，他们享受着宁静的夜晚。

将来时:
We will relish the quiet evenings after the children have gone to bed. 孩子们睡着后，我们将享受宁静的夜晚。
She will relish the chance to finally relax. 她将享受最终放松的机会。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would relish the opportunity to travel more. 如果我有更多时间，我会享受更多旅行的机会。
I wish I could relish the simple pleasures in life more often. 我希望我能更经常地享受生活中简单的快乐。

被动语态:
The meal was relished by everyone.  这顿饭大家都吃得很香。
The opportunity was relished by the participants.  参与者们享受这次机会。

疑问句:
Do you relish the idea of a promotion? 你喜欢升职的想法吗？
Does he relish the prospect of hard work? 他喜欢努力工作的想法吗？

否定句:
I don't relish the thought of public speaking. 我不喜欢公开演讲的想法。
She didn't relish the idea of working overtime. 她不喜欢加班的想法。
