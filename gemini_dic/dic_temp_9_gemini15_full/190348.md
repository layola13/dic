sarcosis:/sɑːrˈkoʊsɪs/| n. |肉僵，死后僵硬|The stiffening of the body after death, due to chemical changes in the muscles.  This process typically begins a few hours after death and is complete within 24-48 hours.|例句：Rigor mortis, or sarcosis, is a crucial indicator in determining the time of death.  死后僵硬，或称肉僵，是确定死亡时间的关键指标。|近义词：rigor mortis, postmortem rigidity |反义词：None (opposite to the state of living flexibility)|名词
