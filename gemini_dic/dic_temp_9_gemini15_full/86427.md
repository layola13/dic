heeds:/hiːdz/| v.|注意，留心，理睬|to pay attention to something or someone|He didn't heed the warnings about the storm.|近义词：attend to, mind, regard|反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He heeds my advice. = 他听从我的建议。
She heeds the traffic signals. = 她遵守交通信号。

一般过去时:
They heeded the captain's orders. = 他们听从了船长的命令。
He heeded the warning signs. = 他注意到了警告标志。

现在完成时:
She has heeded my warnings. = 她已经听从了我的警告。
Have you heeded the doctor's advice? = 你听从了医生的建议了吗？

过去完成时:
They had heeded the call for help. = 他们已经听到了求救的呼唤。
She had heeded the signs of danger. = 她已经注意到了危险的迹象。

将来时:
He will heed the advice of his mentor. = 他将会听从他导师的建议。
They will heed the warnings about the approaching storm. = 他们将会注意即将到来的暴风雨的警告。

虚拟语气:
If he had heeded my warning, he wouldn't be in trouble now. = 如果他听了我的警告，他现在就不会有麻烦了。
I wish she had heeded my advice. = 我希望她听了我的建议。

被动语态:
His warnings were heeded by everyone. = 他的警告被每个人都注意到了。
The advice was heeded by the committee. = 委员会采纳了这个建议。

疑问句:
Did he heed your request? = 他理会你的请求了吗？
Will they heed the call to action? = 他们会响应行动号召吗？

否定句:
He didn't heed the warnings. = 他没有理会警告。
She didn't heed my advice. = 她没有听从我的建议。


PHRASE->
