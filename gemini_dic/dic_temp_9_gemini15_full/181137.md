reinflaming: /ˌriɪnˈfleɪmɪŋ/ | v. | 再点燃；重新激起；使复燃 |  To ignite or excite again; to rekindle; to arouse or stir up again.  |  The politician's speech reinflamed the tensions between the two groups.  政治家的演讲再次激化了这两个群体之间的紧张关系。

The old rivalry was reinflamed by a recent disagreement. 最近的一次争执重新点燃了旧有的竞争。|近义词：rekindle, reignite, reawaken | 反义词：extinguish, quell, suppress | 动词
