mistily:/ˈmɪstɪli/| adv.|朦胧地，模糊地，似有若无地|In a misty or hazy manner; vaguely.  The mountains were mistily visible through the fog.|例句：The distant hills were mistily shrouded in mist. 远处的山峦被薄雾朦胧地笼罩着。

例句：The future seemed mistily uncertain. 未来似乎朦胧而不可知。


近义词： hazily, vaguely, faintly, obscurely


反义词： clearly, distinctly, sharply


词性:副词
