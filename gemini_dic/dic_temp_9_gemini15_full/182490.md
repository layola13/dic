renovated:/ˈrɛnəveɪtɪd/ | adj. | 翻新的；装修过的 |  Having been repaired, renewed, or restored.  The renovated building now boasts modern amenities.  | 近义词：refurbished, restored, modernized | 反义词：dilapidated, run-down, decayed


例句：

The renovated kitchen is stunning.  =  翻新的厨房令人惊艳。

Our renovated apartment is much more spacious. = 我们装修过的公寓宽敞多了。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hotel renovates rooms every year. = 这家酒店每年都翻新房间。
They renovate old houses and sell them. = 他们翻新旧房子然后卖掉。

一般过去时:
They renovated the bathroom last month. = 他们上个月装修了浴室。
The company renovated the office building. = 公司翻新了办公大楼。

现在完成时:
We have renovated our entire house. = 我们已经装修了整栋房子。
They have renovated several buildings this year. = 他们今年已经翻新了几栋建筑。

过去完成时:
They had renovated the house before they moved in. = 他们搬进去之前已经装修了房子。
The owners had renovated the property extensively before selling it. = 业主在出售房产之前对其进行了大规模翻新。

将来时:
We will renovate the kitchen next year. = 我们明年将装修厨房。
The city will renovate the old park. = 城市将翻新旧公园。

虚拟语气:
If we had the money, we would renovate the house. = 如果我们有钱，我们会装修房子。
I wish they had renovated the building sooner. = 我希望他们早点翻新那栋楼。

被动语态:
The house is being renovated at the moment. = 这房子现在正在装修。
The old building was renovated last year. = 这栋旧楼去年被翻新了。

疑问句:
Has the building been renovated? = 这栋楼翻新了吗？
When will the renovations be completed? = 装修什么时候完工？

否定句:
They haven't renovated the bathroom yet. = 他们还没装修浴室。
The house wasn't renovated properly. = 这房子装修得不好。
