evangelize:/ɪˈvændʒəlaɪz/ | vt. | 传福音；使…基督教化 | To preach the gospel or Christian doctrine; to convert someone to Christianity. | 例句：The missionaries evangelized the local people. 传教士向当地人传教。 | 例句：He tried to evangelize his friends. 他试图使他的朋友皈依基督教。 | 近义词：proselytize, preach, convert | 反义词：suppress, secularize | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They evangelize in the streets. = 他们在街上传教。
He evangelizes through his music. = 他通过他的音乐传教。

一般过去时:
The early Christians evangelized throughout the Roman Empire. = 早期的基督徒在整个罗马帝国传教。
She evangelized to her neighbors about the importance of charity. = 她向她的邻居传讲慈善的重要性。

现在完成时:
They have evangelized in many countries. = 他们已经在许多国家传教。
He has evangelized his beliefs for many years. = 他多年来一直宣扬他的信仰。

过去完成时:
Before the arrival of the missionaries, they had never been evangelized. = 在传教士到来之前，他们从未被传教。
The church had evangelized in that region for centuries. = 该教堂在该地区传教了几个世纪。

将来时:
They will evangelize in new territories next year. = 他们明年将在新的地区传教。
He will evangelize the importance of education to the youth. = 他将向年轻人宣扬教育的重要性。

虚拟语气:
If I could, I would evangelize to everyone. = 如果可以的话，我会向所有人传教。
I wish I could evangelize more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地传教。

被动语态:
The villagers were evangelized by the missionaries. = 村民们被传教士传教了。
His beliefs were evangelized through various media. = 他的信仰通过各种媒体传播。

疑问句:
How do they evangelize their faith? = 他们如何传播他们的信仰？
Where will they evangelize next? = 他们接下来会在哪里传教？

否定句:
They do not evangelize aggressively. = 他们不会积极地传教。
He did not evangelize his beliefs to his family. = 他没有向家人宣扬他的信仰。
