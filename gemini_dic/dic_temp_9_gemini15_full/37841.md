competes:/kəmˈpiːts/ | v. |竞争，对抗 |To strive for something against others.|例句：Many companies compete for the same customers. 许多公司争夺相同的客户。
例句：The two athletes competed fiercely in the race. 两名运动员在比赛中激烈竞争。|近义词：rival, contend, vie |反义词：cooperate, collaborate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He competes in marathon races every year. = 他每年都参加马拉松比赛。
The two companies compete fiercely for market share. = 这两家公司为了市场份额而激烈竞争。

一般过去时:
She competed in the Olympics last year. = 她去年参加了奥运会。
They competed for the top prize in the competition. = 他们在比赛中竞争最高奖项。

现在完成时:
He has competed in numerous marathons. = 他已经参加过无数次马拉松比赛。
She has competed against the best athletes in the world. = 她曾与世界上最优秀的运动员竞争过。

过去完成时:
Before the final race, he had competed in several qualifying rounds. = 在决赛之前，他已经参加了几轮资格赛。
They had competed against each other many times before. = 他们之前已经多次互相竞争了。

将来时:
She will compete in the national championships next month. = 她下个月将参加全国锦标赛。
They will compete for a place in the final. = 他们将争夺决赛的席位。

虚拟语气:
If I were younger, I would compete in the triathlon. = 如果我年轻点，我会参加铁人三项赛。
I wish I could compete in the Olympics. = 我希望我能参加奥运会。

被动语态:
The race was competed in challenging weather conditions. = 比赛是在具有挑战性的天气条件下进行的。(注意：被动语态使用较少)
The award was competed for by many talented artists. = 许多有才华的艺术家争夺这个奖项。(注意：被动语态使用较少)

疑问句:
Does he compete in any other sports? = 他参加其他运动吗？
Who will compete in the final round? = 谁将参加决赛？

否定句:
He doesn't compete professionally. = 他不是职业运动员。
She didn't compete in the last tournament. = 她没有参加上次的比赛。


PHRASE->
compete against = 与...竞争
compete for = 争夺
compete with = 与...竞争
compete in = 参加（比赛）
compete to = 竞争以
