shanghainese:/ˌʃæŋhaɪˈniːz/| adj. n.|上海话；上海人；上海的|Of or relating to Shanghai or its people or language.  The term can refer to the language, the people, or the culture of Shanghai.|例句：She speaks fluent Shanghainese. 她能流利地说上海话。

例句：He's a Shanghainese businessman. 他是一位上海商人。


近义词：Shanghai dialect (for the language), Shanghaier (for the person)
反义词：None specifically,  but one could contrast it with "Beijing dialect" or other regional Chinese dialects.
词性: adjective, noun
