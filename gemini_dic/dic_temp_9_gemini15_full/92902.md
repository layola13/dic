idealogue:/ˈaɪdiəlɒɡ/| n. |空想家；思想家；意识形态者 |A person who is excessively concerned with or enthusiastic about abstract ideas and theories, especially in relation to political matters.  They often rigidly adhere to their beliefs and are less interested in practical considerations. | Example: He's such an idealogue; he refuses to compromise on any of his principles.  他是个十足的空想家；他拒绝在任何原则上妥协。|近义词：dogmatist, doctrinaire, fanatic |反义词：pragmatist, realist
