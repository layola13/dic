legume:/ˈliːɡjuːm/|/ˈlɛɡjuːm/| n. |豆类植物；豆荚；豆科植物|A legume is a plant in the family Fabaceae, or a fruit or seed of such a plant.  Legumes are known for their ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. They are a significant source of protein and fiber in many diets.|例句：Peas, beans, and lentils are all legumes.  豌豆、豆类和扁豆都是豆科植物。|例句：The farmer harvested a bountiful crop of legumes this year. 今年农民收获了大量的豆类作物。|近义词：pulse, bean, pod|反义词：无
