longhand:/ˈlɒŋhænd/| /ˈlɔːŋhænd/| n.|手写；长笔迹|Writing done by hand, as opposed to typing or printing.|例句：I prefer longhand to typing. = 我更喜欢手写而不是打字。|例句：She kept a longhand journal of her travels. = 她用手写日记记录她的旅行。|近义词：handwriting, script|反义词：typing, shorthand
