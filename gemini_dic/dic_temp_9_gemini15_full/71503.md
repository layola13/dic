flank:/flæŋk/ | n. v. |侧翼，侧腹；侧击 |The side of a person's body between the ribs and the hip.  A military maneuver involving an attack on the side or rear of an enemy formation. |例句：The general ordered a flanking maneuver to outflank the enemy.将军下令实施侧翼机动以包围敌人。

The steak was cooked to perfection, especially the nicely seared flanks.牛排烹制得完美无缺，特别是煎得恰到好处的侧腹部分。|近义词：side, wing, outflank |反义词：center, front


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The soldiers flank the enemy. = 士兵们从侧翼包抄敌人。
He flanks the argument with supportive evidence. = 他用支持性证据来论证这个论点。

一般过去时:
The cavalry flanked the enemy position. = 骑兵从侧翼迂回敌军阵地。
She flanked the issue with irrelevant details. = 她用无关紧要的细节来回避问题。


现在完成时:
They have flanked the opposition successfully. = 他们成功地从侧翼包抄了对手。
The debate has been flanked by numerous distractions. = 这场辩论一直被许多干扰因素所影响。


过去完成时:
The army had flanked the city before the main attack. = 大军在主要进攻之前已经包围了城市。
She had flanked her original argument with additional data. = 她已经用额外的资料补充了她最初的论点。


将来时:
We will flank the mountain to reach the other side. = 我们将绕过这座山到达另一边。
He will flank his speech with anecdotes. = 他将在演讲中穿插一些轶事。


虚拟语气:
If we flanked the enemy, we would win the battle. = 如果我们从侧翼包抄敌人，我们将赢得战斗。
If she had flanked the topic earlier, the discussion would have been more productive.  = 如果她早点回避这个话题，讨论会更有成效。


被动语态:
The enemy was flanked by the swift attack. = 敌人被迅速的攻击从侧翼包围了。
The main argument was flanked by a lot of unnecessary information. = 主要论点被许多不必要的信息所掩盖。


疑问句:
How did they flank the enemy's defenses? = 他们是如何绕过敌人的防线的？
Will the new strategy flank the market leader? = 新战略会绕过市场领导者吗？


否定句:
We did not flank the enemy during the battle. = 我们在战斗中没有从侧翼包抄敌人。
The company didn't flank the competitor's new product launch. = 这家公司没有绕过竞争对手的新产品发布。



PHRASE->
turn flank = 改变侧翼
expose one's flank =  暴露侧翼
on the flank = 在侧翼
flank attack = 侧翼攻击
outflank = 包抄，迂回

词源:
From Old French flanc, from Frankish *flanka ("side, flank").  Ultimately from a Proto-Germanic root *flanka- ("flat, plain"), cognate with Old High German flanc ("flat, plain").
