/ˌpriːˈdoʊnərz/ | n. | 预捐者，预先捐赠者 |  A predonor is someone who commits to making a donation in advance of a specific event or need.  This often involves a pledge or agreement to donate a certain amount at a future date.  The term is commonly used in fundraising contexts. | Example:  The charity secured several predonors before launching their public fundraising campaign.  预先捐赠者在启动公众募捐活动之前为慈善机构争取到了一些捐款。|近义词：pledger, prospective donor |反义词：recipient
