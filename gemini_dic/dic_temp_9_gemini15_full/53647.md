dischargeable:/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒəbl/| adj.| 可免除的；可偿还的；可释放的 | Capable of being discharged; able to be released from an obligation or debt.  It describes something that can be gotten rid of, released, or paid off. | 例句：The debt is dischargeable in bankruptcy. 破产可以免除这笔债务。

例句：The judge declared the contract dischargeable due to a breach of contract.  法官宣布由于违反合同，合同可解除。


近义词：remissible, releasable, payable, cancellable


反义词：unavoidable, unpayable, inescapable, irredeemable


词性：形容词
