researching:/rɪˈsɜːrtʃɪŋ/| v.|正在研究；调查 |  The act of systematically investigating a subject or problem to discover new facts or to revise accepted theories.  It involves a process of gathering, analyzing, and interpreting information to reach conclusions. | 例句：She is researching the effects of climate change on polar bears. = 她正在研究气候变化对北极熊的影响。

例句：Scientists are researching a new cure for cancer. = 科学家们正在研究治疗癌症的新方法。


近义词：investigating, studying, exploring, inquiring into

反义词：ignoring, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are researching the history of the Roman Empire. = 他们正在研究罗马帝国的历史。
He is researching the impact of social media on teenagers. = 他正在研究社交媒体对青少年的影响。


一般过去时:
I researched the topic thoroughly before writing my essay. = 我在写论文之前彻底研究了这个话题。
We researched different investment options before making a decision. = 我们在做决定之前研究了不同的投资方案。


现在完成时:
She has been researching this subject for five years. = 她研究这个课题已经有五年了。
They have researched various methods to improve efficiency. = 他们已经研究了各种提高效率的方法。


过去完成时:
He had researched the company's history before the interview. = 他在面试前已经研究过这家公司的情况了。
We had researched all the available data before drawing our conclusions. = 我们在得出结论之前已经研究了所有可用的数据。


将来时:
I will be researching the causes of poverty next semester. = 下学期我将研究贫困的原因。
They will research the effects of the new drug on patients. = 他们将研究这种新药对患者的影响。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would research the matter further. = 如果我有更多时间，我会进一步研究这个问题。
I wish I had researched the market trends before investing. = 我希望在投资前我已经研究过市场趋势。


被动语态:
The topic is being researched by a team of experts. = 这个主题正在由一个专家团队研究。
The effects of the new policy are being researched by the government. = 政府正在研究新政策的影响。


疑问句:
Are you researching anything interesting at the moment? = 你现在正在研究什么有趣的东西吗？
What are they researching in the laboratory? = 他们在实验室里研究什么？


否定句:
He is not researching the topic at present. = 他目前没有研究这个课题。
They did not research the market adequately before launching the product. = 他们在推出产品之前没有充分研究市场。
