ferricyanic: /ˌfɛriˈsaɪænɪk/ | adj. | 铁氰化 | Relating to or denoting ferricyanide ions or compounds.  It describes compounds containing the ferricyanide ion, [Fe(CN)₆]³⁻, which is a complex ion of iron(III) and cyanide.  These compounds often have a reddish color. |  The solution contained ferricyanic ions, resulting in a deep red hue.  铁氰化离子溶液呈现深红色。  |近义词：ferricyanide (as a noun referring to the ion or compounds) | 反义词：ferrocyanic (relating to ferrocyanide, [Fe(CN)₆]⁴⁻) | 形容词
