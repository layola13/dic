outbleating: /ˌaʊtˈbliːtɪŋ/ | v. |胜过…的叫声；大声叫喊超过 |  To surpass in loudness of bleating; to outdo in bleating. This word is a relatively rare and often used figuratively. It combines "out-" (meaning "to a greater extent than") and "bleating" (the sound of a sheep or goat).  | 例句：The ram outbleated all the other sheep in the field.  = 公羊的叫声盖过了田里其他所有羊的叫声。 The children's excited chatter outbleated the teacher's attempts to maintain order. = 孩子们兴奋的聊天声盖过了老师维持秩序的尝试。 | 近义词：outshout, outcry, exceed | 反义词：be outbleated by, be silenced | 动词
