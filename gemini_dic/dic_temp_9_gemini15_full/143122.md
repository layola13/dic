outbuying: /ˌaʊtˈbaɪɪŋ/ | v. |超过…的购买量；比…买得多 |To surpass someone in the amount purchased.  It implies a competitive buying situation where one party buys more than another. |例句：The department store outbought its competitors during the holiday sale.  = 在假日促销期间，这家百货公司比竞争对手购买的商品更多。

The small business outbought the large corporation in acquiring rare antique books. = 这家小型企业在收购稀有古籍方面超过了大型公司。

近义词：outshopping, outspending (in a buying context), surpassing, exceeding

反义词：underspending, underbuying

The company outbuys its competitors in raw materials. = 这家公司在原材料采购方面超过了竞争对手。
