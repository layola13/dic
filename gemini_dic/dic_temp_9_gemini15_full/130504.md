nonaccidentally: /ˌnɒnˌæksɪˈdentəli/ | adj. |非偶然地，故意地| Not by accident; intentionally or deliberately.  The event was nonaccidentally caused; it was planned. |近义词：intentionally, deliberately, purposely, knowingly |反义词：accidentally, unintentionally, inadvertently


例句：

The damage to the car was nonaccidentally inflicted.  汽车的损坏并非意外造成。

He nonaccidentally left his wallet at home, knowing he wouldn't need it. 他故意把钱包留在家里，因为他知道用不着。
