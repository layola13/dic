can:/kæn/| modal v.|能够，可以，可能|Can expresses ability, possibility, permission, or request. It is an auxiliary verb and does not conjugate.  |I can swim. 我会游泳。|  She can speak three languages. 她会说三种语言。|may, could, be able to|cannot, must not|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I can speak English. = 我会说英语。
Can you help me with this? = 你能帮我这个忙吗？

一般过去时:
I could swim when I was younger. = 我年轻的时候会游泳。
Could you please pass the salt? = 请你把盐递给我好吗？


现在完成时:
(Can does not typically use perfect tenses.  The meaning is usually expressed differently.)
I have been able to finish the project. = 我已经能够完成这个项目了。
She has always been able to play the piano beautifully. = 她一直以来都弹得一手好钢琴。


过去完成时:
(Can does not typically use perfect tenses. The meaning is usually expressed differently.)
I had been able to finish the work before the deadline. = 我在截止日期前已经完成了工作。
They had been able to reach an agreement. = 他们已经达成了一致。


将来时:
I will be able to go to the party. = 我将能够去参加派对。
We will be able to help you. = 我们将能够帮助你。


虚拟语气:
If I could fly, I would visit every country. = 如果我会飞，我会访问每一个国家。
I wish I could go to the concert. = 我真希望我能去听音乐会。


被动语态:
(Can is not used in the passive voice.  The meaning is usually expressed differently.)
It can be done. = 这可以做到。
The problem can be solved. = 这个问题可以解决。


疑问句:
Can you hear me? = 你能听到我说话吗？
Can she come to the meeting? = 她能来参加会议吗？


否定句:
I cannot go to the party. = 我不能去参加派对。
They can't finish the work on time. = 他们不能按时完成工作。


PHRASE->
can do = 能行；行得通
can't help but = 不禁
can't wait to = 迫不及待地
as you can see = 正如你所看到的
can't stand = 无法忍受


词源:
The word "can" derives from the Old English verb "cunnan," meaning "to know" or "to be able."  Over time, its meaning shifted to express ability and possibility.
