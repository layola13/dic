这个题目要求分析单词“legion”，而不是“legionlidn”。  “legionlidn”似乎是一个拼写错误。  以下是对单词“legion”的分析：

legion: /ˈliːdʒən/ | n. |军团，大批，众多 | A legion is a large military unit, typically consisting of several thousand soldiers.  It can also refer to a large number of people or things. |例句：

The Roman legion was a highly disciplined fighting force.  = 罗马军团是一支纪律严明的军队。

A legion of fans waited outside the stadium. =  一大群粉丝在体育场外等待。

近义词：army, horde, multitude, throng  | 反义词：few, handful
