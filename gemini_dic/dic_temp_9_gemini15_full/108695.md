leaks:/liːks/| n. v.|泄漏；漏洞；泄露；|英文解析：To leak means to escape or allow escape through a hole or crack.  It can refer to liquids, gases, or information. As a noun, it refers to an instance of leaking or the thing that leaks.|例句：The roof leaks during heavy rain.  = 大雨时屋顶漏水。

The news of the merger leaked to the press. = 合并的消息泄露给了媒体。|近义词：drip, seep, divulge, disclose|反义词：seal, plug, conceal, hide|词性:n. v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The roof leaks every time it rains. = 每当下雨屋顶就漏水。
Water leaks from the pipe. = 水管漏水。


一般过去时:
The secret leaked to the rival company. = 秘密泄露给了竞争对手公司。
The dam leaked significantly during the storm. = 暴风雨期间大坝严重漏水。


现在完成时:
The information has leaked to the public. = 信息已经泄露给了公众。
The news of the scandal has leaked all over the internet. = 丑闻的消息已经在互联网上传播开来。


过去完成时:

The details of the plan had leaked to the competitor. = 计划的细节已经泄露给了竞争对手。


将来时:
