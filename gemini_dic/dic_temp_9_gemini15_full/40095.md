contaminants:/kənˈtæmɪnənts/ | n. | 污染物，杂质 | Substances that contaminate something, making it impure or unclean.  Contaminants can be biological, chemical, or physical. | Example: The water sample contained harmful contaminants.  这个水样含有有害污染物。  The air in the city is filled with various contaminants. 城市的空气中充满了各种污染物。 | 近义词：impurities, pollutants, pollutants, adulterants | 反义词：purifiers
