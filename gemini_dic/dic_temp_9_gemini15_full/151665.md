partisan:/ˈpɑːrtɪzən/| n. adj.|党派支持者；偏见的；党派的|A partisan is a strong supporter of a party, cause, or person.  A partisan viewpoint is one that is strongly biased in favor of a particular cause or group. |例句：He was a lifelong partisan of the Republican Party. 他是共和党终生的支持者。

例句：Her partisan comments showed a lack of objectivity. 她的偏激言论显示出缺乏客观性。

近义词：supporter, advocate, follower, biased, prejudiced

反义词：impartial, neutral, objective, unbiased


词性:名词，形容词
