circuitous:/sɜːˈkjuːɪtəs/| adj.|迂回的；绕圈子的；不直接的 |Indirect; roundabout; not straightforward.  The explanation was circuitous and difficult to follow. |近义词：indirect, roundabout, meandering, winding |反义词：direct, straight, straightforward


例句：

He took a circuitous route to avoid the traffic. = 他为了避开交通拥堵，走了一条迂回的路线。

The negotiations were circuitous and protracted. = 谈判冗长而曲折。


词源：

From Latin *circuitus, from circum "around" + ire "to go".  The word reflects the idea of going in a circle or around something.
