rosefinch:/ˈroʊzˌfɪntʃ/| n. |玫瑰雀，红腹灰雀|A small Old World finch, typically having a predominantly reddish-brown plumage.  Many species are found in mountainous regions of Asia and Europe.|The rosefinch is known for its beautiful plumage and melodic song.|近义词：bullfinch (though not always reddish), other finch species|反义词：无
