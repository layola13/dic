handbook:/ˈhændbʊk/| n. |手册，指南|A concise book giving information or instructions on a particular subject.  A handbook typically provides a summary of key information, procedures, or guidelines. |Example:  The employee handbook outlines company policies.  员工手册概述了公司政策。|近义词：manual, guide, directory |反义词：无
