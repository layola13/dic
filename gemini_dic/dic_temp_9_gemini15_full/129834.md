non-Hindu:/ˌnɒnˈhɪnduː/| adj.|非印度教徒的|Not belonging to or relating to Hinduism.  The term describes individuals, groups, or things that are not associated with the Hindu religion or its practices.|例句：Many non-Hindu festivals are celebrated in India.  许多非印度教节日都在印度庆祝。
例句：The museum showcases art from both Hindu and non-Hindu cultures.  博物馆展示了印度教和非印度教文化的艺术品。|近义词：non-Hinduistic|反义词：Hindu
