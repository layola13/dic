millionaires:/ˌmɪljəˈneərz/ | n. |百万富翁们 |  A millionaire is a person who has assets worth at least one million dollars or the equivalent in another currency.  The plural form indicates multiple individuals possessing such wealth. |The Forbes list features many millionaires from different industries.|近义词：multimillionaires, tycoons|反义词：paupers, the impoverished
