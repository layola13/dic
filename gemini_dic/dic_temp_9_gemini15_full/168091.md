prized:/praɪzd/| adj. |珍贵的，宝贵的，高度评价的| highly valued; considered to be of great worth or importance.  |例句：Her prized possession was a porcelain doll given to her by her grandmother.  她最珍贵的财产是一个她祖母送给她的瓷娃娃。

例句：His prized roses won first place at the flower show. 他的获奖玫瑰在花展上获得一等奖。


近义词：valued, cherished, treasured, precious, esteemed|反义词：worthless, valueless, insignificant


词源:源自古法语 *prisier，意思是“评估，评价”。  最终来自拉丁语 *pretiosus，“宝贵的”。
