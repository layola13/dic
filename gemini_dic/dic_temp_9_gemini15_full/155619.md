philosophizing:/fɪˈlɒsəfaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 哲学思考；空谈哲学；推理；思辨 | Engaging in philosophical thought or discussion; speculating about abstract or theoretical matters.  |例句：He spent hours philosophizing about the meaning of life. 他花了数小时思考人生的意义。

例句：The professor encouraged students to philosophize about ethical dilemmas. 教授鼓励学生们对伦理困境进行哲学思考。


近义词：speculating, reflecting, pondering, theorizing | 反义词：acting, doing


USAGE->

He philosophizes about the nature of reality. 他思考现实的本质。
They philosophize daily over coffee. 他们每天喝咖啡时都会进行哲学思考。
