monk:/mʌŋk/| n. |和尚，僧侣 |A monk is a member of a religious community who has taken vows of poverty, chastity, and obedience. Monks typically live in monasteries and dedicate their lives to prayer, meditation, and religious study.  |Example: The monk spent years in silent meditation.  这位和尚花了数年时间进行静坐冥想。|近义词：friar, religious, devotee |反义词：layperson, secular
