explanati:/ˌɛkspləˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 解释，说明 | An explanation is a statement that makes something clear or easy to understand.  It clarifies the reason for something or the meaning of something. |  The teacher provided a clear explanation of the difficult concept.  老师对这个难懂的概念作了清晰的解释。
He gave a detailed explanation of his actions. 他详细解释了他的行为。| clarification, elucidation, interpretation | misunderstanding, confusion | 名词

An explanation is needed. = 需要一个解释。
His explanation is convincing. = 他的解释令人信服。

一般过去时:
