incessantly:/ɪnˈsɛsəntli/ | adv. | 不停地；连续不断地 |  Continuously; without interruption. | The rain fell incessantly for three days. 雨连续下了三天。  The dog barked incessantly.  狗不停地叫。 | constantly, continuously, perpetually | intermittently, occasionally | 副词


USAGE->

He incessantly complains about his job. = 他不停地抱怨他的工作。
She incessantly checks her phone. = 她不停地查看她的手机。


一般过去时:
The machine incessantly whirred throughout the night. = 机器整夜不停地嗡嗡作响。
The birds incessantly sang in the morning. = 鸟儿在早晨不停地歌唱。


现在完成时:

She has incessantly worked on that project. = 她一直不停地从事那个项目。


过去完成时:
