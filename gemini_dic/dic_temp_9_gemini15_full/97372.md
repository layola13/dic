infralittoral: /ˌɪnfrəˈlɪtərəl/ | adj. |潮下带的，浅海的| Relating to the shallow-water region of the sea extending from low-water mark to a depth where the light is insufficient for the growth of benthic algae. This zone is influenced by wave action and tidal currents.  | The infralittoral zone is rich in marine life. = 潮下带生物丰富。|  The distribution of certain species is limited to the infralittoral zone. = 某些物种的分布仅限于潮下带。| supralittoral, littoral |  (No direct antonym, but 'supralittoral' refers to the area above the infralittoral zone.)| adj.
