diarrhoea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/| n. |腹泻|Diarrhoea is the passage of frequent, loose, or watery stools.  It is a common symptom of various illnesses and can be caused by infections, food poisoning, or medication side effects. |例句：He suffered from diarrhoea after eating contaminated food. 他吃了受污染的食物后患上了腹泻。|例句：The doctor prescribed medication to treat her diarrhoea. 医生开了药来治疗她的腹泻。|近义词：loose bowels, loose stools, watery stools |反义词：constipation |名词
