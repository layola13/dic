ghostlily:/ˈɡoʊstˌlɪli/ | n. |幽灵兰花|A rare and unusual orchid, often with translucent white or pale green flowers, that appears to float in the air due to its thin stems and delicate appearance. It is also known as the "phantom orchid" and is prized for its ethereal beauty.|例句：The ghostlily, with its delicate petals, seemed to float in the air.  幽灵兰花，它那纤细的花瓣，仿佛漂浮在空中。|The ghostlily is a prized orchid species, highly sought after by collectors.幽灵兰是一种珍贵的兰花品种，深受收藏家的追捧。|近义词：phantom orchid,  shade plant|反义词：无
