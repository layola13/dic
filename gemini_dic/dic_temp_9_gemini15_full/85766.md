This is not a standard or official ornithological term; it's a descriptive term. | Example:  The wren built its nest using twigs and hay; it's quite a skilled haybird. 这只鹪鹩用树枝和干草筑巢；它是一只相当熟练的“干草鸟”。|近义词：bird (general term) |反义词：无
