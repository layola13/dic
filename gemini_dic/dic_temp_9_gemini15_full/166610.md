/ˌpriːˈsɛmɪnɛriz/ | n. | 预备神学院；预科神学院 |  A preseminary is a preparatory school for those intending to enter a seminary to study for the ministry.  It provides a foundation in religious studies, theological principles, and related subjects before formal seminary education. | Example:  He attended a preseminary before entering the prestigious seminary in Boston. 他在进入波士顿著名的神学院之前就读于一所预备神学院。 | 近义词：preparatory school for ministry, theological preparatory school | 反义词：seminary (in the sense that it's a preparatory step) |  名词
