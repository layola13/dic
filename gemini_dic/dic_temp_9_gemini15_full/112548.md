lovehate:/ˈlʌvheɪt/| n.|爱恨交织|A feeling of both love and hate towards someone or something.  This is typically a complex and ambivalent emotion. |例句：I have a lovehate relationship with my job; I love the challenge but hate the long hours. 我对我的工作又爱又恨；我喜欢挑战，但讨厌长时间工作。|近义词：ambivalence, mixed feelings|反义词:pure love, pure hate|名词
