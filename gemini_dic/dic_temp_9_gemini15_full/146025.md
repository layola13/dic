overdramatizes:/ˌoʊvərˈdræməˌtaɪzɪz/| v. |夸大其词；过分戏剧化 |To exaggerate the importance or seriousness of something.  |例句：He overdramatizes every little problem. 他把每一个小问题都夸大其词。

例句：She overdramatizes her minor inconveniences to gain sympathy. 她夸大她的小麻烦以博取同情。


近义词：exaggerate, sensationalize, overstate, magnify

反义词：downplay, understate, minimize


USAGE->

He overdramatizes his experiences. = 他夸大他的经历。
She overdramatizes minor setbacks. = 她夸大轻微的挫折。
