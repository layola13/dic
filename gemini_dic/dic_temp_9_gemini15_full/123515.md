monotonely:/ˈmɒnəˌtoʊnli/| adv.| 单调地，毫无变化地 | In a monotonous manner; without variation in tone or pitch.  |例句：He spoke monotonously, his voice lacking any inflection. 他单调地说着话，声音毫无抑扬顿挫。

例句：The machine worked monotonously, producing the same product hour after hour. 机器单调地工作着，一小时又一小时地生产着同样的产品。

近义词：dully, tediously, uniformly, unvaryingly

反义词：variedly, excitingly, enthusiastically

词性：副词
