quarts:/kwɔːrts/| n.|夸脱（液量单位）|A quart is a unit of liquid volume equal to one-fourth of a gallon or two pints.  It's commonly used in the US and UK for measuring liquids like milk, juice, and paint.|例句：I bought a quart of milk from the store. = 我从商店买了一夸脱牛奶。

例句：She needs two quarts of paint to finish the wall. = 她需要两夸脱油漆才能完成墙壁的粉刷。

近义词：pint (smaller unit), gallon (larger unit)
反义词：None (as it's a unit of measurement)
词性：名词
