ichthyic: /ɪkˈθɪɪk/ | adj. |鱼类的；与鱼有关的 | Relating to fishes or fish.  The word describes something characteristic of, or relating to, fish.  It can refer to the physical characteristics of fish, their behavior, or their environment. |

例句：

The ichthyic fauna of the lake is surprisingly diverse.  = 湖泊的鱼类动物群落种类惊人地丰富。

He presented an ichthyic study on the migration patterns of salmon. = 他提交了一份关于鲑鱼洄游模式的鱼类学研究。


近义词：piscine, fishlike

反义词：没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与陆地动物相关的形容词，例如terrestrial。

词性：形容词
