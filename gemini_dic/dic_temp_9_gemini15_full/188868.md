rust:/rʌst/| n. v.|锈；腐蚀；生锈；衰败|a reddish-brown coating formed on iron or steel by oxidation, especially when exposed to moist air.|The old fence was covered in rust.  那旧篱笆上布满了锈迹。|The car's body began to rust after years of neglect. 那辆汽车的车身由于多年无人照料而开始生锈。|corrosion, oxidation|shine, polish|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Iron rusts in damp air. = 铁在潮湿的空气中会生锈。
This old pipe is rusting. = 这根旧水管正在生锈。

一般过去时:
The tools rusted in the shed. = 工具在棚子里生锈了。
The car body rusted quickly in the salty air. = 汽车车身在咸空气中迅速生锈。

现在完成时:
The metal has rusted badly. = 金属严重生锈了。
The gate has rusted shut. = 大门已经锈住了。

过去完成时:
The car had rusted before we bought it. = 我们买这辆车之前它就已经生锈了。
The tools had rusted solid by the time we found them. = 我们找到那些工具的时候，它们已经锈得牢不可破了。

将来时:
The iron will rust if you don’t protect it. = 如果你不保护它，铁会生锈。
The car will probably rust if left outside. = 如果放在外面，这辆车可能会生锈。

虚拟语气:

I wish the tools hadn't rusted. = 我真希望工具没有生锈。

被动语态:
The tools were rusted. = 工具生锈了。

疑问句:

否定句:
