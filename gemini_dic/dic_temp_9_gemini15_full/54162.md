disentangles: /ˌdɪsɪnˈtæŋɡəlz/ | vt. | 解开；理清；使摆脱；解决 | To separate or free from something tangled or complicated.  | 例句：He disentangled himself from the complicated legal issues. 他摆脱了复杂的法律纠纷。 | 例句：She carefully disentangled the headphone wires. 她仔细地解开了耳机线。 | 近义词：untangle, unravel, extricate, resolve | 反义词：entangle, complicate, confuse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disentangles the knots in the rope. = 他解开绳子上的结。
She disentangles the complex issues in the report. = 她理清了报告中复杂的问题。


一般过去时:
The detective disentangled the truth from the lies. = 侦探从谎言中理清了真相。
He disentangled himself from the difficult situation. = 他摆脱了困境。


现在完成时:
She has disentangled herself from the toxic relationship. = 她已经摆脱了那段有毒的关系。
They have disentangled the wires and fixed the machine. = 他们已经解开了电线并修理好了机器。


过去完成时:
He had disentangled the problem before the meeting. = 他在会议之前已经解决了这个问题。
She had disentangled the yarn before she began to knit. = 在她开始织毛衣之前，她已经把纱线解开了。


将来时:
We will disentangle the misunderstanding between them. = 我们将会化解他们之间的误会。
The judge will disentangle the facts of the case. = 法官将理清案件的事实。


虚拟语气:
If he disentangled the mess, it would be much better. = 如果他能解决这烂摊子，情况会好得多。
I wish she had disentangled the situation earlier. = 我希望她早点解决这个问题。


被动语态:
The problem was disentangled by the expert. = 这个问题由专家解决了。
The truth will eventually be disentangled. = 真相最终会水落石出。


疑问句:
Can you disentangle this mess of wires? = 你能解开这堆乱糟糟的电线吗？
How will we disentangle ourselves from this predicament? = 我们将如何摆脱这种困境？


否定句:
He couldn't disentangle the complicated plot of the novel. = 他无法理清这本小说复杂的故事情节。
She failed to disentangle herself from the influence of her family. = 她未能摆脱家庭的影响。
