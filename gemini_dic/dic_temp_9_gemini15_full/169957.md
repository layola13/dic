prostyle:/ˈproʊstaɪl/| adj.|柱廊式建筑的；（建筑）前廊式的|Of or relating to a style of ancient Greek architecture in which columns are placed in front of a building, rather than within the structure.  The term can also refer to a similar style in other architectural periods.|The Parthenon is a prime example of prostyle architecture.|近义词：porticoed, colonnaded|反义词：in antis (referring to a different architectural style)
