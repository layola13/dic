putrefies:/ˈpʊtrɪfaɪz/| v.|腐烂；腐败|To decay and give off a foul smell, typically of organic matter.|The meat putrefied in the heat. 肉在高温下腐烂了。 The smell of putrefying flesh filled the air. 腐烂的肉的气味充满了空气。|rot, decay, decompose|preserve, sterilize|动词
