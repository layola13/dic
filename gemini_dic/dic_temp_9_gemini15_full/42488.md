countergovernment:/ˈkaʊntərˈɡʌvənmənt/| n. |反政府组织；对抗政府的势力|A group or movement that opposes the established government, often through illegal or subversive means. It can also refer to actions taken against the government.|例句：The countergovernment movement planned a series of protests to challenge the new laws.  反政府运动计划了一系列抗议活动来挑战新法律。


例句：The police cracked down hard on the countergovernment activities. 警方严厉打击了反政府活动。


近义词：opposition, rebellion, insurgency, resistance|反义词：government, authority, establishment|词性: 名词
