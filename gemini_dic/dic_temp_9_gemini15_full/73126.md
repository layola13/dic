for-the-most-part:/fɔːr ðə ˈmoʊst pɑːrt/| adv. |大部分地，通常，大体上|This phrase means "in most cases" or "generally speaking".  It indicates that something is true or happens in the majority of instances, but not necessarily all. |For the most part, I enjoy my job. |mostly, generally, usually, primarily |rarely, exceptionally, unusually, seldom|副词


例句：

For the most part, the students were well-behaved. = 大部分学生表现良好。

For the most part, the weather has been good this summer. = 今年夏天大部分时间天气都很好。
