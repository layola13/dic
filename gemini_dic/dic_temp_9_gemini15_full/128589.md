niceling:/ˈnaɪslɪŋ/| n. |好孩子，乖孩子|A niceling is a term of endearment for a well-behaved and pleasant child. It's a playful and informal word, combining "nice" and a diminutive suffix "-ling".  The word lacks wide usage and is not found in standard dictionaries.  Its usage is highly contextual and dependent on the speaker's familiarity with the child.|例句：The niceling shared his toys with his friends.  这个乖孩子和他的朋友们分享玩具。|近义词：good child, sweet child, darling, dear|反义词：naughty child, brat, rascal|名词
