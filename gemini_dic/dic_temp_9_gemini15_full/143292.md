outdraw:/ˌaʊtˈdrɔː/ | vt. |胜过；技艺胜过；拔枪速度快于|To surpass in drawing (a weapon), especially in a duel or gunfight; to be quicker on the draw.  |例句：In the showdown, the outlaw outdrew the sheriff and shot him. 在决斗中，歹徒拔枪速度快于警长并将其射杀。

The gunslinger outdrew his opponent, gaining a crucial advantage.  枪手拔枪速度快于对手，获得了至关重要的优势。|近义词：outshoot, outgun, best, surpass |反义词：be outdrawn by, be bested by |动词
