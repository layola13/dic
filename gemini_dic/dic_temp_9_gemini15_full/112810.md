lucernaria:/ˌluːsərˈneəriə/| n. |海葵类动物|A marine invertebrate animal belonging to the phylum Cnidaria, class Staurozoa, typically having a stalked body with eight arms bearing tentacles.  They are sessile, meaning they attach themselves to a substrate and don't move around freely. |Example: The diver observed a beautiful lucernaria clinging to a rock in the coral reef.  潜水员观察到一只美丽的鹿角珊瑚附着在珊瑚礁的岩石上。|近义词: stauromedusa (less common)|反义词:  (no direct antonyms, as it refers to a specific type of animal)|词性:名词
