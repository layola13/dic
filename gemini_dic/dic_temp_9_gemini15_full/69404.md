feel-for:/fiːl fɔːr/|/ˈfiːl fɔːr/| v. |同情，体谅；摸索；寻找|To have sympathy or understanding for someone's difficulties; to search for something by touch.|I feel for her; she's going through a tough time. 我同情她；她正在经历一段艰难的时期。|近义词：sympathize with, empathize with, search for|反义词：be indifferent to, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I feel for the homeless people on the streets. = 我同情街上的无家可归者。
She feels for the lost puppy. = 她同情那只走失的小狗。


一般过去时:
We felt for the victims of the earthquake. = 我们同情地震的受害者。
He felt for the smooth surface of the table. = 他摸索着桌子的光滑表面。


现在完成时:
I have felt for her since I heard about her illness. = 从我听说她生病后，我就一直同情她。
She has felt for the missing keys in her bag. = 她一直在她的包里摸索着丢失的钥匙。


过去完成时:
They had felt for the solution before they gave up. = 他们在放弃之前已经摸索过解决方案了。
I had felt for the right button before I finally found it. = 我在最终找到它之前已经摸索过正确的按钮了。


将来时:
I will feel for him when he faces the challenge. = 当他面临挑战时，我会同情他。
She will feel for the answer in the dark room. = 她会在黑暗的房间里摸索答案。


虚拟语气:
If I were in her shoes, I would feel for her. = 如果我处于她的位置，我会同情她。
I wish I could feel for the lost child's parents. = 我希望我能体会到丢失孩子的父母的感受。


被动语态:
His suffering was felt for by many. = 许多人都同情他的痛苦。
The solution wasn't felt for quickly enough. = 解决方案没有被及时找到。


疑问句:
Do you feel for the people affected by the hurricane? = 你同情受飓风影响的人们吗？
Did he feel for the hidden treasure in the cave? = 他在洞穴里摸索隐藏的宝藏了吗？


否定句:
I don't feel for him; he brought it on himself. = 我不同情他；这是他自己造成的。
She didn't feel for the answer, so she gave up.  = 她没有找到答案，所以她放弃了。
