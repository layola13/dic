concocting:/kənˈkɒktɪŋ/| v.|调制；捏造；策划|The act of concocting something involves creating or preparing it, often in a skillful or inventive way.  It can refer to making a food or drink mixture, but also to inventing a story or plan, often with a deceptive or mischievous intent.|例句：She was concocting a delicious stew in the kitchen. = 她正在厨房里调制美味的炖菜。

例句：He concocted a story to explain his lateness. = 他编造了一个故事来解释他的迟到。

近义词：brew, devise, fabricate, invent, manufacture

反义词：destroy, dismantle, ruin
