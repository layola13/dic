empoisonment:/ɛmˈpɔɪzənmənt/ | n. |中毒；下毒|The act of poisoning or the state of being poisoned;  the introduction of a poisonous substance into the body.  It can refer to both the act of poisoning someone and the condition resulting from it. |例句：The empoisonment of the well was a deliberate act of sabotage. 井水被下毒是蓄意破坏的行为。

The symptoms of empoisonment varied greatly depending on the type of poison. 中毒的症状因毒药种类而异。

|近义词：poisoning, intoxication|反义词：cure, healing|
