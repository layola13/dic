repaid:/rɪˈpeɪd/| v. |偿还；报答|To give back money that has been borrowed or spent; to recompense for a service, kindness, etc.|例句：I have repaid all my debts. 我已经还清了所有的债务。

例句：She repaid his kindness with a generous gift. 她以慷慨的礼物来报答他的好意。


近义词：returned, reimbursed, compensated, requited|反义词：owed, borrowed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repays his loans on time. = 他按时偿还贷款。
She repays her friends' kindnesses. = 她报答朋友的恩情。


一般过去时:
They repaid the money last week. = 他们上周还了钱。
He repaid the favor by helping her move. = 他帮忙她搬家来报答她的好意。


现在完成时:
I have repaid the debt to my parents. = 我已经还清了欠父母的债。
She has repaid all her student loans. = 她已经还清了所有的助学贷款。


过去完成时:
He had repaid the loan before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已还清贷款。
They had repaid their debt of gratitude. = 他们已经报答了他们的感激之情。


将来时:
I will repay you next month. = 我下个月会还你钱。
She will repay the favor in due course. = 她会适时地报答这份恩情。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would repay you immediately. = 如果我有更多钱，我会立即还给你。
I wish I could repay you for your help. = 我真希望我能报答你的帮助。


被动语态:
The loan was repaid in full. = 贷款已全部偿还。
The debt will be repaid eventually. = 这笔债务最终会偿还。


疑问句:
Have you repaid the money yet? = 你还钱了吗？
Will you be able to repay the loan on time? = 你能按时偿还贷款吗？


否定句:
He hasn't repaid the money yet. = 他还没还钱。
She didn't repay the favor. = 她没有报答这份恩情。



PHRASE->
repay a loan = 还清贷款
repay a debt = 还清债务
repay a kindness = 报答恩情
repay a favor = 报答好意
repay trust = 辜负信任
