irritated:/ˈɪrɪteɪtɪd/| adj.|恼怒的；烦躁的；发炎的|Feeling or showing irritation; annoyed.  The word describes a state of being annoyed or frustrated. It can also refer to a physical irritation, such as inflamed skin.|例句：He was irritated by the constant noise. 他被持续的噪音弄烦了。

例句：The rash made her skin irritated. 皮疹使她的皮肤发炎。|近义词：annoyed, frustrated, exasperated, inflamed |反义词：calm, relaxed, peaceful, soothed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am irritated by his behavior. = 我被他行为激怒了。
She is irritated with the slow service. = 她对缓慢的服务感到恼火。


一般过去时:
I was irritated by the traffic jam. = 我被交通堵塞惹恼了。
He was irritated because he missed the train. = 他因为错过火车而感到烦躁。


现在完成时:
I have been irritated by this problem all day. = 我一整天都被这个问题弄得很烦。
She has been irritated by his constant interruptions. = 她一直被他不断的打断而感到恼火。


过去完成时:
I had been irritated by his behavior before he apologized. = 他道歉前，我一直被他行为激怒。
She had been irritated with him for weeks before she finally confronted him. = 在她最终与他对质之前，她已经对他生气了好几个星期了。


将来时:
I will be irritated if he doesn't call me back. = 如果他不回我电话，我会很生气。
She will be irritated if the project is delayed again. = 如果项目再次延误，她会很生气。


虚拟语气:
I would be irritated if I were in her shoes. = 如果我站在她的立场上，我会很生气。
I wish I weren't so easily irritated. = 我希望我不那么容易生气。


被动语态:
He was irritated by the loud music. = 他被大声的音乐激怒了。(被动语态较少用于此词)
The skin was irritated by the chemicals. = 皮肤被化学物质刺激了。


疑问句:
Are you irritated by the heat? = 你热得烦躁吗？
Is she irritated with her boss? = 她对老板生气了吗？


否定句:
I am not irritated by small things. = 我不会因为小事而生气。
He wasn't irritated by the criticism. = 他没有被批评激怒。


PHRASE->
highly irritated = 极度恼怒
become irritated = 开始感到恼火
mildly irritated = 稍微有点恼火
extremely irritated = 非常恼火
feeling irritated = 感到恼火
