lime-green: /ˈlaɪmɡriːn/ | adj. | 黄绿色 |  A color that is a yellowish green, like that of limes.  It's a shade between green and yellow, often described as a bright, cheerful color. | The walls were painted a cheerful lime-green.  墙被漆成了愉快的黄绿色。  Her dress was a vibrant lime-green. 她的裙子是鲜艳的黄绿色。 | chartreuse, yellowish green | dark green, olive green |  形容词
