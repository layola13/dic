misgauged:/mɪsˈɡeɪdʒd/| v. | 误判；估计错误；误解 | To judge or estimate incorrectly; to misunderstand. | The engineer misgauged the strength of the bridge, leading to its collapse. 工程师误判了桥梁的强度，导致桥梁坍塌。|  assess incorrectly, miscalculate, underestimate | accurately gauge, correctly assess | 动词
