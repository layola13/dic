punty:/ˈpʌnti/| n. |吹制玻璃用的铁棒；(尤指)用于支撑玻璃器皿的柄|A punty is a long, slender rod of iron used by glassblowers to gather molten glass and shape it.  It is also used to support the forming glass object.  The punty is especially important for creating the base of a glass object.|例句：The glassblower skillfully manipulated the molten glass on the punty. 玻璃吹制工熟练地用铁棒操作熔化的玻璃。

The punty was used to create a sturdy base for the delicate vase. 铁棒被用来为精美的花瓶制作坚固的底座。


近义词：rod, stick (in this context) |反义词：None specifically applicable.
