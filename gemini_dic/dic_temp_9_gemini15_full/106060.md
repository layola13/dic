kneepan:/ˈniːpæn/| n.|膝盖骨，髌骨|The kneecap, a small, flat bone at the front of the knee joint, which protects the joint and helps in the movement of the leg.  It's crucial for efficient knee extension.|例句：He injured his kneepan during the football game. 他在足球比赛中伤到了膝盖骨。|例句：The doctor examined his kneepan for any signs of fracture. 医生检查了他的膝盖骨是否有骨折迹象。|近义词：patella|反义词：无
