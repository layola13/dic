nonreceivables:/ˌnɑːn rɪˈsiːvəbəlz/ | n. | 应收账款坏账 |  Amounts owed to a business that are unlikely to be collected.  These are debts considered irrecoverable.  | Example:  The company wrote off a significant portion of its nonreceivables due to the economic downturn.  这家公司因经济低迷而注销了大部分应收账款坏账。|近义词：bad debts, uncollectible accounts |反义词：receivables, collectible accounts |名词
