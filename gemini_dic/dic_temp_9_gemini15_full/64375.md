erecting:/ɪˈrektɪŋ/| v.|竖立；建造；建立 |The act of putting something upright or constructing a building or structure.|例句：They are erecting a new skyscraper in the city center. 他们在市中心建造一座新的摩天大楼。  例句：The workers were erecting a scaffold around the building. 工人们正在建筑物周围搭建脚手架。|近义词：constructing, building, raising, establishing |反义词：demolishing, destroying, tearing down

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are erecting a new bridge. = 他们在建造一座新桥。
We erect a monument to honor the heroes. = 我们竖立纪念碑来纪念英雄们。

一般过去时:
They erected a statue in the park. = 他们在公园里竖立了一座雕像。
The company erected a new factory last year. = 公司去年建了一家新工厂。


现在完成时:
They have erected several new buildings this year. = 他们今年已经建了几栋新楼。
The government has erected a new system of social welfare. = 政府已经建立了一个新的社会福利制度。


过去完成时:
They had erected the tent before the rain started. = 下雨之前他们已经搭好了帐篷。
The builders had erected the framework before adding the walls. = 建筑工人砌墙之前已经搭建好了框架。


将来时:
They will erect a new wind turbine next month. = 他们下个月将竖立一座新的风力涡轮机。
The city plans to erect a new museum by the river. = 该市计划在河边建造一座新博物馆。


虚拟语气:
If they had the resources, they would erect a larger building. = 如果他们有资源，他们会建造一座更大的建筑物。
I wish they would erect a playground for the children. = 我希望他们能为孩子们建一个游乐场。


被动语态:
