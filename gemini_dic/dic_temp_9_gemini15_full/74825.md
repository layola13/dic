frenemy:/ˈfrɛnəmi/| n. |假朋友，表面朋友，亦敌亦友|A frenemy is a person with whom one is friendly despite a fundamental dislike or rivalry.|She's a frenemy; she acts friendly but secretly competes with me.  她是个假朋友；她装得很友好，但暗地里却跟我竞争。|rival, adversary|friend, ally|名词
