prewarn:/priːˈwɔːrn/| vt.|预先警告，提前警告|To warn beforehand; to give advance notice of something unpleasant or dangerous that is likely to happen.|例句：The company prewarned its employees about the impending layoffs. 公司提前警告员工即将到来的裁员。

例句：They prewarned us of the potential dangers of the expedition. 他们预先警告我们探险中潜在的危险。


近义词：forewarn, alert, notify


反义词：surprise, ambush


词性：动词
