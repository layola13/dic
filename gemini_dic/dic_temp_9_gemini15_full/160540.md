porkless:/ˈpɔːrkles/| adj. |不含猪肉的；无猪肉的|Not containing pork.  This adjective describes food or products that are free from pork. |例句：Many restaurants offer porkless options for customers who don't eat pork.  许多餐馆为不吃猪肉的顾客提供不含猪肉的菜肴。

例句：This sausage is porkless, made with chicken and vegetables. 这根香肠不含猪肉，是用鸡肉和蔬菜制成的。


近义词：pork-free, without pork |反义词：containing pork, with pork |词性: adjective
