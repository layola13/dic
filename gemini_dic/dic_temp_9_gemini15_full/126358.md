myriapod:/ˈmɪriəpɒd/ | n. | 多足动物 | A myriapod is an invertebrate animal with many legs, belonging to the subphylum Myriapoda, which includes centipedes, millipedes, and others.  | Example:  The scientist studied the diverse species of myriapods found in the rainforest.  这位科学家研究了在热带雨林中发现的各种多足动物。 |近义词: centipede, millipede (depending on the specific myriapod)|反义词: vertebrate |名词
