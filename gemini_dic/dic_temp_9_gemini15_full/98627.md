instrumentary:/ˌɪnstrʊˈmɛntəri/ | n. | 仪器；乐器；文书；法律文件 | The documentary evidence and the instrumental evidence were both considered in the trial. |近义词：apparatus, tools, documents |反义词：none


英文解析：Relating to or consisting of instruments, especially musical instruments or the tools and equipment used for a particular task.  Also refers to the documents or writings forming the legal basis of a transaction.


例句：

The band's instrumental arrangements were complex and innovative. 乐队的器乐编曲复杂而创新。

The treaty's instrumentation was meticulously drafted by legal experts.  条约的法律文件由法律专家精心起草。


词源:

From Middle English *instrumentarie, from Old French *instrumentarie, from Latin instrumentum ("instrument, tool"). The suffix "-ary" indicates a collection or a place for something.
