prewash:/ˈpriːwɒʃ/| v.|预洗|To wash something lightly before washing it properly.  This is often done to remove loose dirt or stains before the main wash cycle.|例句：I always prewash my delicates before putting them in the washing machine. 我总是在把我的精致衣物放进洗衣机之前先预洗一下。|例句：Prewashing the heavily soiled shirt removed most of the grease stains. 预洗这件脏衬衫去除了大部分油渍。|近义词：rinse, pre-treat|反义词：final wash


USAGE->

一般现在时:
She prewashes her clothes before washing them properly. = 她会在正式清洗衣服之前先预洗。
He prewashes his car before waxing it. = 他在打蜡之前会先预洗他的车。

一般过去时:
I prewashed my shirt before I put it in the washing machine. = 我在把衬衫放进洗衣机之前先预洗了。
They prewashed the curtains before hanging them back up. = 他们把窗帘挂回去之前先预洗了。

现在完成时:

He has prewashed the car, ready for a wax. = 他已经预洗了车，准备打蜡了。

过去完成时:
