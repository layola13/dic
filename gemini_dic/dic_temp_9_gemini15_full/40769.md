convincement: /kənˈvɪnsmənt/ | n. | 深信；确信；令人信服的证据 | The state of being convinced; firm belief; the act of convincing someone.  It often implies a strong and unshakeable belief. |例句：Her convincement in her own abilities propelled her to success.  她的自信让她获得了成功。

例句：The lawyer presented irrefutable evidence, leading to the jury's convincement of the defendant's guilt. 律师提供了无可辩驳的证据，导致陪审团确信被告有罪。


近义词：conviction, certainty, assurance, persuasion

反义词：doubt, uncertainty, skepticism


词性：名词
