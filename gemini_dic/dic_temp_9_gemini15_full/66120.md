excelsior:/ɛkˈsɛlsɪɔːr/| interj. |更高；更上一层楼|An exclamation encouraging someone to strive for higher things, or to continue upward.  It is often used as a motto or slogan.| 例句：Onward and Excelsior!  继续前进，更上一层楼！|近义词：higher, onward, upward|反义词：downward, lower


USAGE->
This word is primarily an interjection, so grammatical tenses and voice don't apply in the same way as with verbs or nouns.  It's used as an exclamation.

PHRASE->
Excelsior! = 更上一层楼！
Go forth and Excelsior! = 勇往直前，更上一层楼！
Ever Excelsior! = 永远向上！
Strive for Excelsior! = 为更上一层楼而奋斗！
Aim for Excelsior! = 争取更上一层楼！
