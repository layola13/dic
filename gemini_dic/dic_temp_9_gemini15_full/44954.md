cryptosporidium:/ˌkrɪptəʊˌspɔːrɪdɪəm/ | n. |隐孢子虫 | A genus of single-celled parasitic organisms that cause the diarrheal disease cryptosporidiosis.  They are common in contaminated water sources and can infect humans and animals. | Example:  Contaminated water is a major source of cryptosporidium infection. 污染的水是隐孢子虫感染的主要来源。  The outbreak of cryptosporidiosis was traced back to a contaminated swimming pool. 隐孢子虫病的爆发追溯到一个被污染的游泳池。|近义词:  parasite (as a general term)|反义词:  N/A
