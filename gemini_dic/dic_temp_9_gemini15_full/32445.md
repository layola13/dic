chiming-in: /ˈtʃaɪmɪŋ ɪn/ | v. | 插话；加入；参与 | To interrupt a conversation or activity to add one's opinion or contribution.  |例句：He kept chiming in with irrelevant comments. 他不断插话，说了些无关紧要的话。

例句：Several people chimed in during the discussion to share their ideas.  在讨论中，有几个人插话分享他们的想法。


近义词：interjecting, contributing, joining in, butting in, weighing in


反义词：remaining silent, staying quiet

词源:
The word "chiming" comes from the sound of bells chiming, suggesting a harmonious or rhythmic addition to something already happening.  "In" indicates the act of joining or participating.  Therefore, "chiming in" implies a smooth, but potentially interrupting contribution.
