remobilizing: /ˌriːˈmoʊbɪlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 重新动员；重新部署 |  The act of mobilizing again; putting something or someone back into action or service after a period of inactivity.  | 例句：The army is remobilizing its troops for the upcoming offensive.  军队正在为即将到来的攻势重新部署部队。

The government is remobilizing its efforts to combat the spread of the virus. 政府正在重新努力以对抗病毒的传播。


近义词：reactivate, redeploy, reorganize | 反义词：demobilize, disband
