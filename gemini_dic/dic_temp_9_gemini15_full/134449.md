nonirritating: /ˌnɑːnˈɪrɪteɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不刺激的，温和的 | Not causing irritation or inflammation.  This adjective describes substances or things that do not cause discomfort, redness, or allergic reactions. It is often used in the context of skincare, medical products, and other items that come into contact with sensitive skin or mucous membranes. | 例句：This lotion is nonirritating and suitable for sensitive skin. 这款乳液不刺激，适合敏感肌肤使用。

This cleanser is nonirritating to the eyes.  这款洁面乳对眼睛没有刺激性。


近义词：mild, gentle, soothing, harmless | 反义词：irritating, harsh, abrasive, caustic


词源：

* **non-:**  prefix meaning "not" or "without".
* **irritat-:**  stem from the verb "irritate," meaning "to annoy, vex, or make sore."  This ultimately derives from the Latin word "irritare," meaning "to provoke, excite."
* **-ing:**  present participle suffix, forming an adjective.
