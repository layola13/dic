shove:/ʃʌv/| vt.  |猛推；挤；塞；强迫接受 |to push something or someone forcefully |He shoved the box under the bed. 他把箱子塞到床底下。|push, thrust, ram |pull, draw |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shoves his hands in his pockets. 他把手插进口袋里。
She shoves the food down her throat. 她狼吞虎咽地吃东西。


一般过去时:
He shoved the door open. 他猛地推开了门。
They shoved their way through the crowd. 他们挤过人群。


现在完成时:
He has shoved all his belongings into the suitcase. 他已把所有行李塞进行李箱。
She has shoved the problem under the carpet. 她一直回避这个问题。


过去完成时:
He had shoved the evidence into a drawer before the police arrived. 警察来之前，他已经把证据塞进抽屉里了。
They had shoved the blame onto him. 他们把责任推卸给了他。


将来时:
He will shove the papers into the briefcase. 他会把文件塞进公文包。
She will shove him aside if he gets in her way. 如果他挡道，她会把他推开。


虚拟语气:
If he shoved the door harder, it might open. 如果他用力推门，它可能会开。
I wish he hadn't shoved me. 我真希望他没有推我。


被动语态:
The box was shoved under the table. 箱子被塞到了桌子底下。
The blame was shoved onto him unfairly.  责任被不公平地推卸给了他。


疑问句:
Did he shove you? 他推了你吗？
Why did she shove him? 她为什么要推他？


否定句:
He didn't shove the door. 他没有推门。
She didn't shove the food down her throat. 她没有狼吞虎咽地吃东西。


PHRASE->
shove off: 离开；走开
shove it: 少来这套
shove something down someone's throat: 强迫某人接受某事物
shove aside: 把…推到一边
shove oneself in: 挤进去
