scrat:/skræt/ | n. v. | 抓痕；刮擦声；挠；抓 | A scratch is a mark made by rubbing something sharp across a surface.  To scratch is to rub a surface with something sharp. | 例句：The cat left scratches on the furniture. 猫在家具上留下了抓痕。 The dog scratched at the door. 狗抓着门。|近义词：scrape, abrasion, scratch mark|反义词：polish, smooth


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He scratches the table. = 他抓桌子。
The cat scratches the sofa. = 猫抓沙发。


一般过去时:
I scratched my arm. = 我抓伤了我的胳膊。
She scratched the car. = 她抓伤了汽车。


现在完成时:
The dog has scratched the carpet. = 狗已经抓破了地毯。
He has scratched his head in confusion. = 他困惑地挠了挠头。


过去完成时:
I had scratched the car before I noticed it. = 我在注意到之前已经抓伤了汽车。
The cat had scratched the furniture before we got home. = 我们回家之前，猫已经抓坏了家具。


将来时:
I will scratch the lottery ticket. = 我将刮开彩票。
He will scratch his back. = 他将挠他的背。


虚拟语气:
If I scratched the car, I would pay for the damage. = 如果我抓坏了汽车，我会赔偿损失。
I wish I hadn't scratched the table. = 我真希望我没有抓坏桌子。


被动语态:
The car was scratched by a sharp object. = 汽车被尖锐的物体划伤了。
The table was scratched by the cat. = 桌子被猫抓伤了。


疑问句:
Did you scratch the car? = 你把车抓伤了吗？
Has the cat scratched the furniture again? = 猫又抓坏家具了吗？


否定句:
I didn't scratch the wall. = 我没有抓坏墙。
The dog didn't scratch the door. = 狗没有抓门。



PHRASE->

scratch the surface = 只是触及表面
scratch your head = 挠头（表示困惑）
from scratch = 从零开始
scratch team = 临时拼凑的队伍
scratch card = 刮刮卡
