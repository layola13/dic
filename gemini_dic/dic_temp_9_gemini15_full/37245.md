comfortress:/ˈkʌmfətrəs/| n. |安慰者，慰藉者 | A person who provides comfort or solace, especially to someone in distress.  It is a relatively uncommon word, often used in a literary or archaic context.  | Example: The kind nurse acted as a comfortress to the grieving family.  慈祥的护士成为了悲伤家庭的安慰者。 |近义词：consoler, soother, comforter |反义词：tormentor, persecutor |名词
