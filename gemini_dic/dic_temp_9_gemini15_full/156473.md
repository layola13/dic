piatti:/ˈpjɑːti/| n.|盘子，菜肴|Italian plural of piatto, meaning "dish" or "plate". It can refer to dishes of food or the plates themselves.  |例句：We ordered three piatti of pasta. 我们点了三盘意大利面。  |近义词：dishes, plates, courses|反义词：None.
