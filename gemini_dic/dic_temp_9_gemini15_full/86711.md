helmeting:/ˈhɛlmɪtɪŋ/| v.|戴头盔；装备头盔|The act of putting on or wearing a helmet.  It can also refer to the process of fitting something with a helmet-like structure.|例句：Helmeting up before riding a motorcycle is crucial for safety. 戴上头盔后再骑摩托车对安全至关重要。


例句：The construction workers were all helmeting before starting their work. 建筑工人开始工作前都戴上了头盔。


近义词：helmet-wearing, equipping with helmets


反义词：unhelmeting (removing a helmet)


词性:动词
