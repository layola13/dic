petro:/ˈpetroʊ/| adj.|石油的；岩石的|Relating to petroleum or rock.  The word is derived from the Greek word "petra," meaning "rock" or "stone".  It is used as a combining form in many words related to petroleum and geology.|例句：Petrochemicals are used in the production of plastics. 石油化工产品用于塑料生产。  Petrography is the study of rocks. 岩石学是岩石的研究。|近义词：oil (in the context of petroleum), lithoid (referring to rock-like)|反义词： N/A |词性:形容词
