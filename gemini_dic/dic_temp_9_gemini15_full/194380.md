self-procurement:/ˌsɛlf prəˈkjʊərmənt/| n. |自行采购，自我采购 |The act of obtaining goods or services independently, without relying on external suppliers or procurement departments.  This often involves individuals or smaller organizations. |例句：The small business opted for self-procurement to maintain control over its supply chain.  小型企业选择自行采购以控制其供应链。

例句：The project manager, due to time constraints, resorted to self-procurement of necessary materials. 项目经理由于时间限制，不得不自行采购必要的材料。


近义词：independent procurement, self-supply, direct purchase |反义词：outsourced procurement, centralized procurement |词性:名词
