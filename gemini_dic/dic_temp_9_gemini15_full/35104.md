clotted:/ˈklɒtɪd/| adj.|凝结的；凝块的| thickened and solidified, usually referring to liquids like cream or blood.  |例句：The cream was clotted and thick.  这奶油凝结了，很稠。  |例句：The blood clotted quickly after the injury. 受伤后血液很快凝固了。|近义词：congealed, coagulated, curdled|反义词：liquid, fluid

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The blood clots quickly. = 血液凝固得很快。
The cream clots in cold weather. = 奶油在寒冷的天气里会凝结。

一般过去时:
The milk clotted overnight. = 牛奶一夜之间凝结了。
The blood clotted rapidly. = 血液迅速凝结。

疑问句:

否定句:

词源:
From Middle English *clotted, past participle of clotten "to clot," from Old English *clottan, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch klot "lump," German Klotze "block."  The sense development is from "lump" to "congealed mass."
