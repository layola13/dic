mohawk:/ˈmoʊhɔːk/ | /ˈməʊhɔːk/ | n. | 莫霍克族；莫霍克式发型；莫霍克人 |

英文解析：The word "Mohawk" can refer to several things:  a Native American people of the Iroquois Confederacy; a distinctive hairstyle characterized by a strip of hair left down the center of the head, while the rest is shaved; or, less commonly, a type of motorcycle.  The hairstyle is named after the Mohawk people, who were known for this style of hair.


例句：

The Mohawk Nation has a rich history and culture.  莫霍克族拥有丰富的历史和文化。

He sported a bold Mohawk hairstyle. 他留着大胆的莫霍克式发型。


近义词： (for the hairstyle)  fauxhawk (a less extreme version), spiky hair


反义词：(for the hairstyle)  bald, long hair


词性：名词
