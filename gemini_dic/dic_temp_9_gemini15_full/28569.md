careen:/kəˈriːn/| v.| 倾斜，摇晃，急速奔驰 | To move swiftly and wildly, often in an uncontrolled manner; to lean or sway dangerously to one side.  |例句：The runaway horse careened wildly down the street.  失控的马在街上狂奔。

例句：The old ship careened alarmingly in the storm.  老船在暴风雨中危险地倾斜。

近义词：swerve, lurch, tilt, veer

反义词：stabilize, steady, remain upright


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car careens around the corner. = 汽车在拐角处急转弯。
The drunkard careens down the street. = 醉汉摇摇晃晃地走在街上。


一般过去时:
The ship careened violently in the rough seas. = 船在汹涌的海浪中剧烈倾斜。
The bus careened into a tree. = 公共汽车撞到了一棵树上。


现在完成时:
The car has careened off the road several times. = 汽车已经几次冲出路面。
The boat has careened dangerously close to the rocks. = 船已经危险地靠近礁石了。


过去完成时:
The plane had careened before crashing into the mountain. = 飞机在坠毁到山上之前就已经失控了。
The bus had careened sharply to avoid hitting the pedestrian. = 公共汽车为了避免撞到行人而急转弯。


将来时:
The rollercoaster will careen down the steep hill. = 过山车将从陡峭的山坡上冲下来。
If he continues drinking, he will careen into a wall. = 如果他继续喝酒，他将会撞到墙上。


虚拟语气:
If the driver hadn't been alert, the car would have careened into the ditch. = 如果司机不警觉，汽车就会冲进沟里。
I wish the boat hadn't careened so violently; we were frightened. = 我希望船不会那么剧烈地倾斜；我们当时很害怕。


被动语态:
The car was careened into a lamppost. = 汽车撞到了一根灯柱上。(被动语态用起来较为少见)
The ship was careened onto the shore.  船被倾斜地搁浅在岸上。(被动语态用起来较为少见)


疑问句:
Did the car careen into anything? = 汽车撞到什么东西了吗？
Will the boat careen in the storm? = 船会在暴风雨中倾斜吗？


否定句:
The ship did not careen during the voyage. = 船在航行过程中没有倾斜。
The driver carefully avoided careening. = 司机小心地避免了倾斜。


PHRASE->
careen out of control = 失控地倾斜

careen into something = 撞上某物
careen through the air = 在空中翻滚
