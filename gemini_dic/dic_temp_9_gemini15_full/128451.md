newscasters:/ˈnjuːzkæstərz/| n. |新闻播音员|People who present news on television or radio.  They deliver news reports, interviews, and weather forecasts. |Example: The newscasters announced the breaking news with calm professionalism.  新闻播音员以冷静专业的态度宣布了突发新闻。|近义词：news anchors, broadcasters, reporters|反义词：audience, viewer
