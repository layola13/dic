planer:/ˈpleɪnər/| n. |刨子；平滑机|A planer is a machine or tool used to smooth or flatten a surface, typically wood or metal.  It removes thin layers of material to create a level plane.|例句：The carpenter used a planer to smooth the rough edges of the wood. 木匠用刨子来刨平木头的毛边。

The mechanic used a planer to flatten the metal surface. 技工用平滑机来使金属表面平整。|近义词：shaper, smoother|反义词：roughener
