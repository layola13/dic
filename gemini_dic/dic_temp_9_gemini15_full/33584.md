cilium: /ˈsɪliəm/ | n. |纤毛；(复数cilia) | A hairlike projection on the surface of a cell or organism, often involved in movement or sensing.  | Example: The cilia in the respiratory tract help to remove mucus and debris. 纤毛在呼吸道中帮助清除粘液和碎屑。|近义词：flagellum (鞭毛), hair |反义词：无
