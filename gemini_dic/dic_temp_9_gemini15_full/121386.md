/ˈmɪʃməʃ/| n.|混乱；杂乱；混合物 |A mishmash is a disorganized or chaotic mixture of things. It implies a lack of order or coherence.  The term often suggests a haphazard combination of disparate elements. |例句：The report was a mishmash of unrelated facts and figures. 这份报告是一堆毫无关联的事实和数字的混合体。|例句：His garden was a mishmash of different plants, with no apparent design. 他的花园里杂七杂八地种着各种各样的植物，没有任何明显的规划。|近义词：jumble, muddle, hodgepodge, medley |反义词：order, organization, system
