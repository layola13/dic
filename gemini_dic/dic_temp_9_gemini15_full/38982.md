confinity:/kənˈfɪnɪti/| n.|  边界, 限制,  范围 |The word "confinity" refers to a boundary, limit, or extent. It suggests a shared or common boundary between things.  While it exists in dictionaries, it's rarely used in modern English.  It has a more archaic or literary feel. |例句：The confinity of their estates was marked by a low stone wall.  他们的庄园边界由一堵矮石墙标示。|近义词：boundary, limit, border, confines, perimeter|反义词：infinity, boundlessness, vastness|名词
