reveler:/ˈrɛvələr/| n. |狂欢者，寻欢作乐者 |A reveler is someone who enjoys themselves in a lively and noisy way, especially at a party or celebration.  They are often characterized by their enthusiastic participation in festivities.|例句：The revelers danced until dawn. 狂欢者们一直跳到黎明。|例句：The street was filled with revelers celebrating the victory. 街上挤满了庆祝胜利的狂欢者。|近义词：partygoer, merrymaker, celebrant |反义词：hermit, recluse
