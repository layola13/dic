repristination:/ˌriːprɪstɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. |  恢复原状；复原；生态修复 | The act of restoring something to its original pristine condition, especially an ecosystem.  This often involves removing invasive species and restoring native plants and animals.  It is a more intensive and complete form of restoration than simple rehabilitation. | Example: The ambitious repristination project aimed to return the degraded wetland to its pre-industrial state.  这个雄心勃勃的生态修复项目旨在将退化的湿地恢复到工业化前的状态。 | 近义词：restoration, rehabilitation, renewal | 反义词：degradation, destruction, deterioration |  词性：名词
