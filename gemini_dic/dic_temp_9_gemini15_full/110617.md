limper:/ˈlɪmpər/| v. n. adj.|跛行；跛足的人；瘸的|To walk with difficulty because of a limp.  A limper is someone who limps; something that is limper is weak or feeble.|例句：He limped painfully after the accident. 他事故后痛苦地跛行。

The old horse limped across the field. 老马一瘸一拐地穿过田野。


近义词：hobble, hobble along, shuffle

反义词：stride, walk normally


词性：动词，名词，形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He limps every day. = 他每天都跛行。
The dog limps after its injury. = 这只狗受伤后就瘸了。


一般过去时:
She limped home after the marathon. = 马拉松比赛后她一瘸一拐地回家了。
The injured soldier limped through the forest. = 受伤的士兵一瘸一拐地穿过森林。


现在完成时:
He has limped since the fall. = 他从摔倒后就一直跛着脚。
She has limped for years due to arthritis. = 由于关节炎，她多年来一直跛行。


过去完成时:
He had limped for miles before he found help. = 他走了好几英里才找到帮助。
She had limped all the way to the village before collapsing. = 她一瘸一拐地走到村里才倒下。


将来时:
He will limp for several weeks after the surgery. = 手术后他将跛行几周。
I think he will still limp even after the treatment. = 我认为即使治疗后他仍然会跛行。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't injured his ankle, he wouldn't limp. = 如果他没有伤到脚踝，他就不会跛行。
I wish I didn't limp so much. = 我希望我不那么跛行。


被动语态:
His leg was injured, so he was limping. = 他的腿受伤了，所以他在跛行。
(Note:  "Limp" is rarely used passively.)


疑问句:
Is he limping because of his knee? = 他是因为膝盖才跛行吗？
Why is the horse limping? = 为什么马跛行？


否定句:
He doesn't limp anymore. = 他不再跛行了。
She didn't limp after she received the treatment. = 她接受治疗后不再跛行了。


PHRASE->
limp along: 艰难地前进
limp home: 勉强回家
a limp handshake: 软弱无力地握手
limp wrist: 软弱无力的手腕
limp excuse: 站不住脚的借口


词源:
The word "limper" derives from the Middle English word "limpen," meaning "to be or become lame."  This in turn likely comes from a Proto-Germanic root related to words signifying weakness or flexibility.
