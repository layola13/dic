preparers:/prɪˈpɛərərz/| n. |准备者，制备者 |Those who prepare something.  People or entities that engage in the act of preparing.  This could refer to documents, food, materials, or any other item or process requiring preparation. |例句：The preparers of the financial statements must ensure accuracy.  财务报表的编制者必须确保准确性。

例句：The diligent preparers of the exhibition worked tirelessly. 展览的辛勤准备者们夜以继日地工作。


近义词：makers, creators, assemblers, developers


反义词：consumers, users, destroyers


词性:名词
