purulent:/ˈpjʊrələnt/| adj. |脓性的，化脓性的 |Containing or discharging pus.  Purulent infections are characterized by the accumulation of pus, a thick, yellowish-white fluid containing dead white blood cells, bacteria, and tissue debris.  The presence of pus indicates an active infection. |例句：The wound was purulent and required immediate medical attention.  伤口化脓，需要立即就医。

The purulent discharge from the abscess was foul-smelling.  脓肿流出的脓液恶臭难闻。|近义词：suppurative, infected, festering |反义词：clean, sterile, healthy
