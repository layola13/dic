demystification:/ˌdiːmɪstɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |神秘的消除；去神秘化；解释；阐明|The process of removing mystery or obscurity from something; making something easier to understand.  It involves clarifying complex or confusing concepts, making them accessible to a wider audience. |例句：The demystification of quantum physics is a major goal for many science educators. 量子物理学的去神秘化是许多科学教育家的主要目标。

例句：The book aims at the demystification of modern art. 这本书旨在阐明现代艺术。


近义词：clarification, explanation, elucidation, illumination

反义词：mystification, obfuscation
