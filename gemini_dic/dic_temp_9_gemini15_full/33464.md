chutneys:/ˈtʃʌtniːz/| n. |酸辣酱，辣酱|A chutney is a type of condiment, typically from the Indian subcontinent, made from a mixture of fruits, vegetables, spices, vinegar, and sugar. Chutneys can be sweet, sour, spicy, or a combination of these flavors. They are often served with curries, rice, or other dishes.|例句：The mango chutney was a delicious accompaniment to the lamb curry.  芒果酸辣酱是羊肉咖喱的美味配菜。

例句：She added a spoonful of spicy tomato chutney to her sandwich. 她往三明治里加了一勺辣番茄酱。


近义词：relish, sauce, preserve|反义词：None (as it describes a category of food)|词性：名词
