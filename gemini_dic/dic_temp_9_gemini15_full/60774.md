electrolyzers:/ɪˈlɛktrəlaɪzərz/ | n. |电解槽；电解器 |An electrolyzer is a device that uses electricity to split water into hydrogen and oxygen.  It's a key component in green hydrogen production. |例句：Many companies are investing heavily in the development of more efficient electrolyzers.  许多公司正在大力投资开发更高效的电解槽。

例句：The research team tested various types of electrolyzers to determine their performance under different conditions.  研究小组测试了各种类型的电解槽，以确定它们在不同条件下的性能。
