dining-out:/ˈdaɪnɪŋ aʊt/| n.|外出就餐|The act of eating a meal at a restaurant or other place outside of one's home.  It refers to the experience of eating out, not just the meal itself. |例句：Dining-out is a popular pastime for many people. 外出就餐是许多人的热门消遣活动。|例句：We enjoyed dining-out at that new Italian restaurant. 我们在那家新开的意大利餐厅享用了美味的晚餐。|近义词：eating out, restaurant meal|反义词：dining in, home-cooked meal|名词
