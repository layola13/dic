gherkins:/ˈɡɜːrkɪnz/ | n. |小黄瓜(腌制的小黄瓜)|Small pickled cucumbers.  They are usually small and have a tart, vinegary flavor. |例句：I love adding gherkins to my sandwiches. 我喜欢在我的三明治里加小黄瓜。

例句：The burger was topped with cheese, onions, and gherkins. 汉堡上面放了奶酪、洋葱和小黄瓜。
