precompress:/ˌpriːkəmˈprɛs/ | vt. |预压缩；预先压缩 | To compress (data, files, etc.) before further processing or storage, often to reduce size or improve efficiency.  This typically involves applying a compression algorithm in advance of another operation. | Example: The software precompresses the images before uploading them to the server, saving bandwidth and time. = 该软件在将图像上传到服务器之前对其进行预压缩，从而节省带宽和时间。|近义词：compress beforehand, pre-compress |反义词：decompress, expand
