creditor:/ˈkrɛdɪtər/ | n. |债权人，贷方 | A person or company to whom money is owed.  |Example: The bank is a creditor in this transaction. 这笔交易中银行是债权人。  |Example:  He is a creditor of the company, owed a substantial sum. 他是这家公司的债权人，欠着一笔巨款。 |近义词： lender, obligee |反义词：debtor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bank is my creditor. = 银行是我的债权人。
She is a creditor of the business. = 她是这家企业的债权人。


一般过去时:
He was a creditor to the failing business. = 他是那家即将倒闭的企业的债权人。
The company was a creditor of mine. = 那家公司是我的债权人。


现在完成时:
The bank has been my creditor for many years. = 多年来，银行一直是我的债权人。
They have become creditors to the struggling firm. = 他们已经成为这家苦苦挣扎的公司债权人。


过去完成时:
The bank had been his creditor before the bankruptcy. = 破产前，银行一直是他的债权人。
She had already been a creditor to the company for several months. = 她已经做了这家公司好几个月的债权人了。


将来时:
He will be a creditor to the new startup. = 他将成为这家新公司的债权人。
The investors will become creditors to the project. = 投资者将成为该项目的债权人。


虚拟语气:
If I were a creditor, I would demand payment. = 如果我是债权人，我会要求付款。
I wish I were not a creditor to such an unreliable person. = 我真希望我不是这样一个不可靠的人的债权人。


被动语态:
The company was considered a creditor by the court. = 法院认为该公司是债权人。
The debt was assigned to a new creditor. = 债务已分配给新的债权人。


疑问句:
Who is the creditor in this case? = 在这种情况下，债权人是谁？
Are you a creditor of the company? = 你是这家公司的债权人吗？


否定句:
I am not a creditor to that business. = 我不是那家企业的债权人。
He was not a creditor in the original transaction. = 在最初的交易中，他不是债权人。


PHRASE->
secured creditor  担保债权人
unsecured creditor 无担保债权人
priority creditor 优先债权人
trade creditor 贸易债权人
creditor's claim 债权人债权
