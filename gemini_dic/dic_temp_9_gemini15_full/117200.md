measurement:/ˈmɛʒərmənt/ | n. | 测量；衡量；尺寸；大小 | The process of determining the size, amount, or extent of something.  It can refer to the act of measuring, the result of measuring, or a standard unit of measurement. |例句：The measurement of the room was taken before the furniture was delivered.  房间尺寸是在家具送达前测量的。

The accuracy of the measurement is crucial for the experiment's success.  测量的准确性对实验的成功至关重要。


近义词：measurement, mensuration, quantification, gauging, assessment | 反义词：estimation (in the sense of a rough guess), approximation | 词性：名词

词源:
The word "measurement" comes from the Old French "mesurement," ultimately deriving from the Latin "mensūra," meaning "measure."  The suffix "-ment" is a common English suffix indicating a process or result of an action.
