holing-up: /ˈhoʊlɪŋ ʌp/ | v. | 躲藏；隐蔽；躲起来 | To hide or shelter oneself, typically to avoid danger or unwanted attention.  |例句：The robbers holed up in an abandoned building after the heist. 抢劫犯在抢劫后躲藏在一座废弃的建筑物里。

The family holed up at home during the blizzard.  家人在暴风雪期间躲在家中。| 近义词：hiding, sheltering, concealing, secluding | 反义词：showing oneself, appearing, revealing
