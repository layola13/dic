dogwoods:/ˈdɒɡwʊdz/ | n. |山茱萸属植物；山茱萸树 |The dogwood is a flowering tree or shrub known for its showy bracts.  Dogwoods are prized for their beautiful blooms and are often used in landscaping. |例句：The flowering dogwoods are in full bloom, creating a stunning display of color.  盛开的山茱萸花朵竞相开放，形成令人惊叹的色彩展示。

The vibrant pink dogwoods brightened the garden.  鲜艳粉红色的山茱萸点亮了花园。|近义词：flowering trees, shrubs |反义词：无
