cowered:/ˈkaʊərd/| v.|畏缩，胆怯|To shrink away or crouch down in fear or shame.|例句：The small dog cowered behind its owner when the thunderstorm began. 小狗在雷雨开始时畏缩在主人身后。例句：He cowered in fear as the bully approached.  当恶霸走近时，他吓得畏缩起来。|近义词：cringe, shrink, tremble|反义词：stand tall, confront


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He cowers when he sees his boss. = 他看到老板就畏缩。
The cat cowers under the table during fireworks. =  猫在放烟花的时候躲在桌子底下瑟瑟发抖。

一般过去时:
She cowered in the corner during the argument. = 她在争吵期间蜷缩在角落里。
The children cowered behind their mother when they heard the loud noise. = 孩子们听到巨响后躲在母亲身后。


现在完成时:
He has cowered in fear ever since the accident. = 自从事故发生以来，他一直害怕地畏缩着。
The dog has cowered at the sound of thunder all its life. = 这条狗一辈子都害怕雷声而畏缩。


过去完成时:
She had cowered in fear all night before the police arrived. = 在警察到来之前，她整夜都害怕地畏缩着。
The witness had cowered behind the curtain before testifying. =证人作证前躲在窗帘后面瑟瑟发抖。


将来时:
He will cower before the might of the king. = 他将在国王的强大力量面前畏缩。
The mice will cower when the cat enters the room. = 老鼠进房间时，老鼠会吓得畏缩。


虚拟语气:
If I saw a bear, I would cower in fear. = 如果我看到熊，我会吓得畏缩。
I wish I hadn't cowered when he yelled at me. = 我真希望他对我大喊大叫时我没有畏缩。


被动语态:
(Cower is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes an action performed by the subject, not something done *to* the subject.)  畏缩很少用被动语态，因为它描述的是主语执行的动作，而不是对主语所做的动作。


疑问句:
Did the child cower when the dog barked? = 孩子听到狗叫时害怕了吗？
Why did he cower at the sight of the police? = 他为什么看到警察就畏缩？


否定句:
He did not cower in the face of danger. = 他面对危险时没有畏缩。
She did not cower; instead, she stood her ground. = 她没有畏缩；相反，她坚持了自己的立场。


PHRASE->

cower in fear = 害怕地畏缩
cower in shame = 羞愧地畏缩
cower down = 畏缩
cower before = 在...面前畏缩
cower away = 躲避
