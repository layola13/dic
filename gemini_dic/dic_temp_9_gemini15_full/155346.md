pharmacokinetics:/ˌfɑːrməkəʊˈnɛtɪks/ | n. |药物动力学 |The study of how the body affects a drug, including absorption, distribution, metabolism, and excretion.  It describes what the body does to the drug.  |Example: Understanding the pharmacokinetics of a drug is crucial for determining the appropriate dosage and administration route.  理解药物动力学对于确定合适的剂量和给药途径至关重要。|近义词：drug disposition |反义词：pharmacodynamics
