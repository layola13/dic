overjump:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒʌmp/ | vt. |越过；跳过；超过 | To jump over something; to exceed a limit or expectation.  |例句：The horse easily overjumped the fence.  这匹马轻松地越过了栅栏。
The company overjumped its sales target for the quarter.  这家公司超额完成了本季度的销售目标。|近义词：leap over, surpass, exceed |反义词：underjump, fall short of |动词
