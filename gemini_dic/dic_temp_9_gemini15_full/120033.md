millions:/ˈmɪljənz/| n.|数百万；许多|millions refers to a very large, unspecified number, typically in the range of one million or more.  It can also be used figuratively to mean a great many.|例句：Millions of people watched the game. 数百万人在观看比赛。

例句：The company makes millions of dollars a year. 这家公司每年赚数百万美元。


近义词：thousands, countless, innumerable, a multitude, a vast number

反义词：few, handful, a small number

词性:名词
