leaps:/liːps/| n. v.|跳跃；飞跃；骤增|A leap is a jump, especially a long or high one.  It can also refer to a sudden large increase or improvement. The verb form means to jump or make a sudden large increase. |例句：The dog leaped over the fence.  这只狗跳过了栅栏。

The company's profits leaped this year.  今年公司利润大幅增长。|近义词：jump, bound, spring, surge, soar|反义词：crawl, creep, decline, decrease|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He leaps over hurdles. = 他跳过障碍物。
The company's profits leap every year. = 公司的利润每年都在增长。


一般过去时:
She leaped for joy. = 她高兴地跳了起来。
The sales figures leaped dramatically last quarter. = 上个季度的销售额大幅增长。


现在完成时:
He has leaped over many obstacles in his life. = 他一生中克服了许多障碍。
The value of the stock has leaped significantly. = 股票价值大幅上涨。


过去完成时:
He had leaped to his feet before I could speak. = 我还没来得及说话，他就跳了起来。
The temperature had leaped by ten degrees overnight. = 温度一夜之间骤升了十度。


将来时:
She will leap into action when she hears the news. = 她听到这个消息后会立即行动。
The price of oil is expected to leap next year. = 预计明年的油价将会上涨。


虚拟语气:
If I were a bird, I would leap from the tallest building. = 如果我是一只鸟，我会从最高的建筑物上跳下来。
I wish I could leap across the chasm. = 我希望我能跳过这个峡谷。


被动语态:
The fence was leaped over by the dog. = 栅栏被狗跳过去了。(This is less common usage for "leap")
The record was leaped by a young athlete. = 这项纪录被一位年轻运动员打破了。(This implies exceeding a record)


疑问句:
Can he leap that high? = 他能跳那么高吗？
Will the population leap in the next decade? = 人口在未来十年会激增吗？


否定句:
He didn't leap to conclusions. = 他没有妄下结论。
The business did not leap forward as expected. = 这项业务并没有像预期的那样取得进展。


PHRASE->
leap year = 闰年
leap of faith = 盲目信任；冒险一试
take a leap = 冒险；尝试
giant leap = 巨大的飞跃
leap forward = 飞跃进步
