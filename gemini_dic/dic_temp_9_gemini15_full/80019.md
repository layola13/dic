go-someone-one-better:/ɡoʊ ˈsʌmˌwʌn wʌn ˈbɛtər/ | phrasal verb |  胜过某人 | To surpass someone; to do something better than someone else has done. This phrase implies a competitive context, often involving a previous achievement or offer.  | 例句：He offered me $100, but I went him one better and offered $150. 他给了我100美元，但我更胜一筹，给了150美元。

He built a small shed, but his neighbor went him one better and built a whole garage. 他建了一个小棚子，但他的邻居更胜一筹，建了一个完整的车库。


近义词：outdo, surpass, exceed, better, top


反义词：be outdone by, be surpassed by


词性: phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They go each other one better with extravagant gifts. = 他们互相赠送奢侈的礼物，互相攀比。
We always try to go our competitors one better in terms of product quality. = 我们总是试图在产品质量方面胜过我们的竞争对手。


一般过去时:
She baked a delicious cake, but her sister went her one better with a stunning chocolate masterpiece. = 她烤了一个美味的蛋糕，但是她姐姐更胜一筹，做了一个令人惊艳的巧克力杰作。
The company's new marketing campaign went its previous campaign one better, resulting in a significant increase in sales. = 公司的新营销活动比之前的活动更胜一筹，导致销售额大幅增长。


现在完成时:
They've gone each other one better in a series of increasingly outrageous bids. = 他们在一系列越来越离谱的竞价中互相超过对方。
Our team has gone our rivals one better by securing a major contract. = 我们的团队通过获得一份重要的合同而胜过了我们的竞争对手。


过去完成时:
He had already broken the record, but his rival had gone him one better the previous week. = 他已经打破了纪录，但他的对手在上周更胜一筹。
The artist had painted a beautiful landscape, but his apprentice had gone him one better with a more innovative approach. = 艺术家画了一幅美丽的风景画，但他的学徒用更具创新性的方法更胜一筹。


将来时:
I'm sure she will go my offer one better; she's very competitive. = 我相信她会比我的报价更好；她很有竞争力。
The company plans to go its competitors one better by launching a revolutionary new product. = 公司计划通过推出革命性的新产品来超越竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If I could, I would go his offer one better, but I don't have the resources. = 如果可以的话，我会比他的报价更好，但我没有资源。
If she had wanted to, she could have gone his achievement one better, but she wasn't interested in the competition. = 如果她想的话，她本来可以超越他的成就，但她对竞争不感兴趣。


被动语态: (This phrase is rarely used passively)
His offer was gone one better by a rival bidder. = 他的报价被竞争对手更胜一筹。
The previous record was gone one better by a young athlete. = 之前的记录被一位年轻运动员打破。


疑问句:
Can you go his idea one better? = 你能比他的想法更好吗？
Will they try to go our strategy one better? = 他们会试图比我们的策略更好吗？


否定句:
I couldn't go his performance one better, he was simply amazing. = 我无法超越他的表现，他简直太棒了。
They failed to go their rivals one better in the market share race. = 他们在市场份额竞争中未能超越竞争对手。


PHRASE->
go one better = 更胜一筹
go someone one better = 超过某人
try to go someone one better = 试图超过某人
go him/her one better = 超过他/她
outdo someone by going them one better = 通过超越某人来胜过某人
