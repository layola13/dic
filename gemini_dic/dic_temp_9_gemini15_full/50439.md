deprecation:/ˌdɛprɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |弃用，贬低，不赞成 |The act of deprecating something; expressing disapproval of something.  It can refer to the process of phasing out a feature, software, or technology, or the expression of disapproval or criticism. |Example: The deprecation of the old system led to the adoption of a new, more efficient one.  旧系统的弃用导致了新、更高效系统的采用。  The company's deprecation of its employees' efforts was demoralizing. 公司对员工努力的贬低令人沮丧。 |近义词：disapproval, condemnation, phasing out, obsolescence |反义词：approval, endorsement, support


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company deprecates the use of outdated software. = 公司不赞成使用过时的软件。
He deprecates violence in all its forms. = 他谴责一切形式的暴力。


一般过去时:
They deprecated the old method and introduced a new one. = 他们否定了旧方法并引进了一种新方法。
The engineer deprecated the design flaws in the initial prototype. = 工程师批评了初始原型中的设计缺陷。


现在完成时:
The developers have deprecated several features in the latest update. = 开发人员在最新更新中已弃用了一些功能。
Management has deprecated the old bonus system. = 管理层已经取消了旧的奖金制度。


过去完成时:
They had deprecated the older version before releasing the new one. = 他们在发布新版本之前已经弃用了旧版本。
The committee had deprecated the proposal due to its lack of feasibility. = 委员会由于该提案缺乏可行性而将其否决。


将来时:
They will deprecate the current system next year. = 他们明年将弃用当前系统。
The company will deprecate the old software by the end of the year. = 公司将在年底前弃用旧软件。


虚拟语气:
If they deprecated the old system sooner, we would have fewer problems. = 如果他们早点弃用旧系统，我们就不会有这么多问题了。
I wish they had deprecated that feature months ago. = 我真希望他们几个月前就弃用了那个功能。


被动语态:
The old software was deprecated in favor of a newer version. = 旧软件已被弃用，取而代之的是一个新版本。
The policy was deprecated due to public outcry. = 由于公众的强烈抗议，该政策被废弃了。


疑问句:
Has the feature been deprecated yet? = 该功能已被弃用了吗？
Will the old system be deprecated soon? = 旧系统很快会被弃用吗？


否定句:
The company does not deprecate the use of open-source software. = 公司不反对使用开源软件。
They did not deprecate the importance of teamwork. = 他们没有贬低团队合作的重要性。
