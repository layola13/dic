provokers:/prəˈvoʊkərz/ | n. |挑衅者，煽动者 |Those who incite or stimulate a reaction, often negative.  People or things that cause anger, annoyance, or other strong reactions. |例句：The protesters were considered provokers by the authorities.  抗议者被当局视为挑衅者。

例句：His aggressive comments acted as provokers, leading to a heated argument. 他咄咄逼人的言论起到了煽动作用，导致一场激烈的争吵。


近义词：agitators, instigators, inciters, troublemakers |反义词：peacemakers, conciliators


词性: 名词
