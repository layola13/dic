everse:/ɪˈvɜːrs/| adj.|颠倒的；相反的| reversed means turned in the opposite direction or order. It implies a complete change or reversal of something's state, position, or direction.  |例句：The car was driving in reverse. 这辆车在倒车行驶。|例句：The decision was later reversed.  这个决定后来被推翻了。|近义词：inverted, opposite, contrary |反义词：forward, normal, direct|形容词


USAGE->
