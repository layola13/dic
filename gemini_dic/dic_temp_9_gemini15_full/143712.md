outlined:/ˈaʊtlaɪnd/ | adj. | 概述的；轮廓的；要点突出的 |  Giving the main points of something, without details.  It refers to a summary or a sketch of something, highlighting the key features. |


例句：

The professor outlined the main arguments of the essay.  教授概述了论文的主要论点。

She outlined her plans for the future in her speech.  她在演讲中概述了她未来的计划。


近义词：sketched, summarized, summarized, delineated,  schematized

反义词：detailed, elaborated, comprehensive


词性:形容词
