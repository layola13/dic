pixels:/ˈpɪksəlz/| n.|像素|A pixel is a single point of color in a digital image.  Pixels are arranged in a grid to form the complete image. The more pixels an image has, the higher its resolution and the sharper the detail.|例句：This photo has a high number of pixels, so it looks very sharp. 这张照片像素很高，所以看起来非常清晰。  The image is blurry because it has too few pixels. 这张图片模糊是因为像素太少。|近义词：dot, point|反义词: N/A
