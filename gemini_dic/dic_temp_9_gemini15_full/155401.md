phaseout:/ˈfeɪzaʊt/ | n. v. |逐步淘汰，逐渐取消 | The gradual ending of something.|The company announced a phaseout of its older models. 公司宣布逐步淘汰其旧型号产品。 The government is phasing out the use of fossil fuels. 政府正在逐步淘汰化石燃料的使用。 |近义词：elimination, discontinuation, withdrawal |反义词：introduction, implementation

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They phase out outdated equipment every year. = 他们每年淘汰过时的设备。
The company phases out its products gradually. = 公司逐步淘汰其产品。

一般过去时:
The factory phased out its polluting production line last year. = 该工厂去年淘汰了其污染生产线。
They phased out the old system and introduced a new one. = 他们淘汰了旧系统并引进了新系统。

现在完成时:
The government has phased out lead in gasoline. = 政府已经淘汰了汽油中的铅。
They have phased out the use of harmful chemicals. = 他们已经淘汰了有害化学物质的使用。

过去完成时:
The company had phased out that product before the new regulations came into effect. = 在新规生效之前，该公司已淘汰了该产品。
Before the merger, they had phased out several inefficient departments. = 在合并之前，他们已经淘汰了几个效率低下的部门。

将来时:
The company will phase out its inefficient production lines next year. = 公司明年将淘汰其效率低下的生产线。
They will phase out the old software by the end of the year. = 他们将在年底前淘汰旧软件。

虚拟语气:
If they phased out the old system, they would improve efficiency. = 如果他们淘汰旧系统，他们将提高效率。
I wish they would phase out those outdated regulations. = 我希望他们能淘汰那些过时的规章制度。

被动语态:
The old model was phased out last year. = 旧型号去年被淘汰了。
The inefficient system is being phased out gradually. = 效率低下的系统正在逐步被淘汰。

疑问句:
Will the company phase out its older products soon? = 公司很快就会淘汰其旧产品吗？
Is the government phasing out the use of plastic bags? = 政府正在淘汰塑料袋的使用吗？

否定句:
The company did not phase out its older products quickly enough. = 公司淘汰旧产品的速度不够快。
They haven't yet phased out the use of that chemical. = 他们尚未淘汰该化学物质的使用。


PHRASE->
phase out a product = 淘汰产品
phase out a program = 逐步取消一项计划
phase out a policy = 逐步取消一项政策
phase out an employee = 解雇员工（逐渐停止与某员工的雇佣关系）
phase out of business = 逐渐倒闭
