lingual:/ˈlɪŋɡwəl/| adj.|语言的；舌头的|Relating to language or the tongue.  The term can refer to aspects of language itself, or to the physical tongue.|例句：The child showed impressive lingual skills for their age.  这个孩子展现了与其年龄不相符的令人印象深刻的语言能力。

例句：The doctor examined the patient's lingual frenulum. 医生检查了病人的舌系带。


近义词：linguistic, verbal | 反义词：non-verbal, visual


词源:  From Latin *lingualis, from lingua "tongue".  The connection to language arises from the use of the tongue in speech.
