jealous:/ˈdʒɛləs/ | adj. | 嫉妒的；羡慕的 | Feeling or showing envy or resentment towards someone or their achievements, possessions, or advantages.  | He was jealous of his brother's success. 他嫉妒他哥哥的成功。| envious, covetous | indifferent, unconcerned | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is jealous of her new car. = 他嫉妒她的新车。
She is always jealous of her sister's beauty. = 她总是嫉妒她妹妹的美貌。


一般过去时:
I was jealous when he got the promotion. = 当他得到晋升时，我很嫉妒。
They were jealous of our vacation. = 他们嫉妒我们的假期。


现在完成时:
He has been jealous of his colleague's achievements for years. = 多年来，他一直嫉妒他同事的成就。
She has always been jealous of her friend's popularity. = 她一直嫉妒她朋友的名气。


过去完成时:
I had been jealous of her talent before I met her. = 在我遇到她之前，我一直嫉妒她的才能。
They had been jealous of his success for a long time. = 他们很久以前就嫉妒他的成功了。


将来时:
I will be jealous if he gets the award. = 如果他获得奖项，我会嫉妒的。
She will probably be jealous when she sees your new house. = 当她看到你的新房子时，她可能会嫉妒。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't be so jealous. = 如果我是你，我不会那么嫉妒。
I wish I weren't so jealous of his success. = 我希望我不那么嫉妒他的成功。


被动语态:
His success was often envied (by others).  = 他的成功经常被（别人）羡慕。 (Note:  "jealous" is rarely used passively)
Her beauty was often the subject of others' jealousy. = 她的美貌经常成为别人嫉妒的对象。(Note:  This uses a noun form of jealousy.)


疑问句:
Are you jealous of my new job? = 你嫉妒我的新工作吗？
Is he jealous of her boyfriend? = 他嫉妒她的男朋友吗？


否定句:
I am not jealous of his wealth. = 我不嫉妒他的财富。
She wasn't jealous of her friend's promotion. = 她不嫉妒她朋友的晋升。
