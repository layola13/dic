misappropriation: /ˌmɪsˌəˌproʊpriˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 挪用；盗用；侵吞 | The act of using something in a way that is illegal or unfair, especially money or property that is not yours.  | The misappropriation of company funds led to his arrest. 公司资金的挪用导致了他的逮捕。  The government accused the company of misappropriation of public funds. 政府指控该公司挪用公款。 | embezzlement, theft, fraud | appropriate, rightful use | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misappropriates company funds. 他挪用公司资金。
The manager misappropriates the budget regularly. 经理经常挪用预算。


一般过去时:
They misappropriated the charity's donations. 他们挪用了慈善机构的捐款。
The accountant misappropriated a large sum of money. 会计挪用了一大笔钱。


现在完成时:
He has misappropriated thousands of dollars. 他已经挪用了数千美元。
The company has misappropriated funds for years. 这家公司多年来一直在挪用资金。


过去完成时:
They had misappropriated the evidence before the trial. 他们在审判前就已挪用了证据。
The thief had misappropriated the jewels before the police arrived. 警察到达之前，小偷已经盗走了珠宝。


将来时:
They will misappropriate the funds if given the chance. 如果有机会，他们会挪用资金。
He will misappropriate more money if not stopped. 如果不阻止他，他会挪用更多钱。


虚拟语气:
If he had not misappropriated the money, the company would still be in business. 如果他没有挪用资金，公司现在还会在运营。
I wish the government had not misappropriated the tax money. 我真希望政府没有挪用税款。


被动语态:

Company funds were misappropriated for personal gain. 公司资金被挪用以谋取私利。


疑问句:

否定句:

The investigation found that no funds were misappropriated. 调查发现没有资金被挪用。
