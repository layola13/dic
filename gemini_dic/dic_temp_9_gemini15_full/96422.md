indissoluble: /ˌɪndɪˈsɒljʊbl/ | adj. | 不能溶解的；不可分解的；牢不可破的 |  Incapable of being dissolved, separated, or broken; permanent.  It implies a strong and unbreakable bond, often used to describe relationships, bonds, or agreements. |


例句：

Their love was indissoluble, weathering every storm. = 他们的爱情牢不可破，经受住了每一次风暴的考验。

The contract was deemed indissoluble, binding both parties for ten years. = 这份合同被认为是不可撤销的，将双方约束十年。


近义词：unbreakable, inseparable, permanent, indestructible


反义词：soluble, dissolvable, breakable, separable


词性：形容词
