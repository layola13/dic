preoffensive: /ˌpriːəˈfɛnsɪv/ | adj. | 预先进攻的；先发制人的 |  Describing actions or strategies taken before an anticipated attack or offensive, aiming to gain an advantage or prevent an enemy's attack.  It emphasizes the proactive nature of the action, aiming to strike first. |

例句：

The preoffensive strike destroyed the enemy's supply lines.  = 先发制人的打击摧毁了敌人的补给线。

Their preoffensive maneuvers surprised the opposition. = 他们的先发制人策略让对手措手不及。


近义词：proactive, preventative, anticipatory, preemptive

反义词：reactive, defensive, responsive


词性：形容词
