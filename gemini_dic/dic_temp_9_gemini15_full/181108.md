reindulgences:/ˌriːɪnˈdʌldʒənsɪz/| n.|再次沉溺；再次放纵|The act of indulging again in something previously enjoyed or abandoned; a renewed or repeated act of self-indulgence.|例句：After a period of abstinence, she succumbed to reindulgences in chocolate.  在一段时间的戒断之后，她又沉溺于巧克力之中。

例句：His reindulgences in gambling led to further financial ruin. 他再次沉迷于赌博，导致进一步的经济破产。

近义词：relapse, repetition of indulgence, renewed self-indulgence

反义词：abstinence, self-denial, restraint


词性:名词
