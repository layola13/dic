delimiting:/dɪˈlɪmɪtɪŋ/| v. |限定；界定；划分；确定…的界限 |To define or mark the limits of something.  The process of setting boundaries or limits. |例句：The researcher delimited the study's scope to include only undergraduate students. 研究人员将研究范围限定为仅包括本科生。


例句：The red lines on the map delimit the different geographical zones. 地图上的红线划分不同的地理区域。


近义词：defining, bounding, circumscribing, specifying, restricting


反义词：unlimiting, unrestricted, boundless, limitless


词性：动词
