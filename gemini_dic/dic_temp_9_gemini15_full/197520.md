shelduck:/ˈʃeldʌk/| n. | 潜鸭；鹊鸭 | A shelduck is a type of duck belonging to the genus Tadorna, characterized by their often brightly colored plumage.  They are known for their distinctive appearance and behaviour, including their nesting habits. |例句：The shelduck's bright orange beak is a striking feature.  潜鸭鲜艳的橙色喙是一个显著的特征。

The shelduck is a common sight near coastal areas and wetlands. 潜鸭在沿海地区和湿地很常见。|近义词：sheldrake (less common)|反义词：无

词源:
The word "shelduck" combines "shell" (referencing the often brightly colored, shell-like appearance of its plumage) and "duck".  The specific etymology of "shell" in this context is somewhat debated, but likely relates to the bright, shiny quality of the bird's feathers.
