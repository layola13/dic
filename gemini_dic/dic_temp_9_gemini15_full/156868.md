pigsty:/ˈpɪɡstaɪ/| n.|猪圈；脏乱的地方|A pigsty is a place where pigs are kept, often characterized by dirt and mess. It can also be used figuratively to describe a very dirty or untidy place or situation.  |例句：The farmer cleaned the pigsty every day.  = 那个农民每天都打扫猪圈。  The house was a complete pigsty after the party. = 派对后，房子乱成一团糟。|近义词：sty, pigpen, hogpen, mess, squalor|反义词：cleanliness, order, tidiness|名词
