glowlamp:/ˈɡloʊlæmp/| n. |辉光灯，霓虹灯|A glow lamp is a gas-discharge lamp that produces light by passing an electric current through a gas or vapor, causing it to glow.  It's characterized by its low energy efficiency and often used for decorative purposes due to its unique glow.|Example: The neon glow lamps illuminated the city street at night. 夜晚霓虹灯照亮了城市街道。|近义词：neon light, gas-discharge lamp|反义词：incandescent lamp (for general illumination)|名词
