reforestize:/ˌriːˈfɒrɪstaɪz/ /ˌriːˈfɔːrɪstaɪz/| vt.|重新造林；使重新长满树木|To plant trees again on land that has been deforested.  The process involves planting seedlings or saplings to restore a forest area.|例句：The government is planning to reforestize the barren hills. 政府计划对荒山进行重新造林。

例句：The organization is working to reforestize areas damaged by wildfires. 该组织正在努力对野火损毁的地区进行重新造林。


近义词：replant, afforest, reafforest|反义词：deforest, deforest
