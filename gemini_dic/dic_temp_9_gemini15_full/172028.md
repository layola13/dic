purifiers:/ˈpjʊrɪfaɪərz/| n. |净化器；纯化器|A purifier is a device or substance that removes impurities or contaminants from something.|例句：Air purifiers are becoming increasingly popular in homes and offices. 空气净化器在家庭和办公室中越来越受欢迎。  The water purifier removed all the impurities from the water. 水净化器去除了水中的所有杂质。|近义词：cleaners, filters, purifiers|反义词：polluters, contaminators|名词
