flashgun: /ˈflæʃɡʌn/ | n. |闪光灯，闪光枪 | A device that produces a flash of bright light, typically used in photography to illuminate a subject.  |例句：The photographer used a flashgun to capture the moment. 摄影师用闪光灯捕捉到了那一刻。

例句：He fired the flashgun, illuminating the dark alley. 他开了闪光灯，照亮了黑暗的小巷。


近义词：electronic flash, strobe light, speedlight


反义词：None (no direct antonym,  but words implying absence of light could be considered contextual opposites)


词性: Noun
