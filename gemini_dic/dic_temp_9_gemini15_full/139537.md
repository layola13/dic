obtrude:/əbˈtruːd/| vt.|突出；强加；闯入|To force oneself or something into a situation or place where one is unwelcome or inappropriate.  It implies a forceful or unwelcome intrusion.|例句：He obtruded his opinions into the conversation. 他把自己的意见强加到谈话中。
例句：Don't obtrude yourself on others. 不要强迫自己介入别人的事情。|近义词：impose, intrude, thrust|反义词：withdraw, retreat|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He obtrudes his opinions on everyone. = 他总是把自己的意见强加于人。
They obtrude their problems on their friends. = 他们把自己的问题强加在朋友身上。

一般过去时:
She obtruded her presence at the party. = 她不请自来参加了派对。
He obtruded his views during the meeting. = 他在会议期间强加自己的观点。


现在完成时:
They have obtruded themselves into our lives. = 他们强势地闯入了我们的生活。
He has obtruded his advice on me repeatedly. = 他反复地向我强加建议。


过去完成时:
She had obtruded her opinions before anyone else could speak. = 在其他人发言之前，她就强加了自己的意见。
They had obtruded their presence long enough. = 他们已经不请自来很久了。


将来时:
He will obtrude his views at the next meeting. = 他将在下一次会议上强加自己的观点。
She will obtrude her way into the conversation again. = 她将会再次强行介入谈话。


虚拟语气:
If he obtruded himself any further, I would have left. = 如果他再进一步强迫自己介入，我就走了。
I wish he wouldn't obtrude his opinions on me. = 我希望他不要再把他的意见强加于我。


被动语态:
His opinions were obtruded on the group. = 他的观点被强加于这个群体。
Her presence was obtruded upon the quiet atmosphere. = 她不请自来的出现扰乱了宁静的气氛。


疑问句:
Does he often obtrude his views in meetings? = 他经常在会议上强加自己的观点吗？
Will they obtrude their presence at the ceremony? = 他们会在仪式上不请自来吗？


否定句:
Please don't obtrude your concerns onto others. = 请不要把你的担忧强加给他人。
He didn't obtrude himself into their private conversation. = 他没有介入他们的私人谈话。



PHRASE->
obtrude oneself = 强行介入
obtrude upon = 打扰；侵犯
obtrude into = 闯入
obtrude on = 强加于
obtrude with = 以…强加
