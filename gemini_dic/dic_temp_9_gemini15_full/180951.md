reimaged:/ˌriˈɪmɪdʒd/| v.|重新塑造形象；重新构想；重新设计 | To create a new image or representation of something; to re-imagine or redesign something.  |例句：The company reimaged its brand with a new logo and marketing campaign. 公司通过新的标识和营销活动重新塑造了其品牌形象。

The marketing team reimaged the product's packaging to appeal to a younger demographic. 营销团队重新设计了产品的包装，以吸引更年轻的消费者群体。|近义词：reimagine, redesign, reinvent, rebrand |反义词：maintain, preserve
