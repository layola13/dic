ringback:/ˈrɪŋbæk/| n.|回铃音，来电铃声|A sound heard by a caller while their call is being connected to the recipient.|例句：I set a custom ringback tone for my phone. 我为我的手机设置了自定义回铃音。|例句：The ringback indicated that the person was busy. 回铃音表明对方正忙。|近义词：caller ID, ringtone|反义词:silence
