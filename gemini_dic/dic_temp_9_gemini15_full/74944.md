freudianly:/ˈfrɔɪdɪənli/ | adv. | 佛洛伊德式地；精神分析式地 | In a manner characteristic of or consistent with the theories of Sigmund Freud.  This often implies an interpretation of behavior based on unconscious desires and motivations. | 例句：He interpreted her laughter freudianly, suggesting it masked repressed anxieties. 他用佛洛伊德式的解释方法解读她的笑声，暗示这掩盖了她被压抑的焦虑。


例句：The critic analyzed the novel freudianly, exploring the characters' hidden motivations. 批评家以弗洛伊德式的分析方法解读这部小说，探究人物隐藏的动机。


近义词：psychoanalytically, psychodynamically


反义词：superficially, literally


词性: 副词
