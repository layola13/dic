interdepartmental:/ˌɪntərˌdɪpɑːrtˈmɛntəl/ | adj. | 部门间的，跨部门的 | Relating to or involving more than one department.  | Example: The project requires interdepartmental cooperation.  这个项目需要跨部门合作。  |近义词: cross-departmental, multidepartmental | 反义词: intradepartmental
