prognathic:/prɒɡˈnæθɪk/ | adj. | 前突的；（颌骨）前伸的 | Having or relating to a prognathous jaw.  This refers to a jaw that projects outward, beyond the normal alignment of the face.  | Example:  The fossil shows a prognathic jaw, characteristic of many primate species.  化石显示出一个前突的颌骨，这是许多灵长类物种的特征。 |近义词：protruded, projecting | 反义词：orthognathic
