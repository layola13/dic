playfere:/ˈpleɪfɛər/| n. |普莱费尔密码|A Playfair cipher is a manual symmetric encryption technique that uses a 5x5 matrix to encrypt digraphs (two-letter groups).  It was invented in 1854 by Charles Wheatstone, but named after Lord Playfair who promoted its use.|例句：The Playfair cipher was considered quite secure for its time. 普莱费尔密码在当时被认为相当安全。|近义词：substitution cipher, digraphic cipher|反义词：None|名词
