recommand:/ˌrekəˈmɛnd/| vt. |推荐，建议 |to praise or suggest (someone or something) with approval  |例句：I recommend this restaurant; the food is excellent. 我推荐这家餐馆；菜很好吃。|例句：The doctor recommended complete bed rest. 医生建议完全卧床休息。|近义词：suggest, advise, propose |反义词：discourage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I recommend this book to everyone. = 我向每个人推荐这本书。
She recommends healthy eating habits. = 她推荐健康的饮食习惯。

一般过去时:
He recommended a good lawyer to me. = 他向我推荐了一位好律师。
They recommended that we take a taxi. = 他们建议我们打车。

现在完成时:
I have recommended this product to many clients. = 我已经向许多客户推荐了这款产品。
She has recommended several candidates for the position. = 她已经推荐了几位候选人担任这个职位。

过去完成时:
He had recommended the same solution before. = 他之前已经推荐过同样的方案。
They had recommended the film to us several times.  = 他们之前已经向我们推荐过几次这部电影了。


将来时:
I will recommend you for the job. = 我将推荐你担任这份工作。
She will recommend a new strategy to the team. = 她将向团队推荐一个新的策略。


被动语态:
This restaurant is highly recommended. = 这家餐馆广受好评。
The book was recommended by the professor. = 这本书是教授推荐的。


PHRASE->
recommend oneself = 自荐
recommend doing sth = 建议做某事
recommend sb to do sth = 建议某人做某事
recommend sth to sb = 向某人推荐某物
I highly recommend...= 我强烈推荐...
