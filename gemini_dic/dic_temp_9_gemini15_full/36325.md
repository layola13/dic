cohosting: /ˌkoʊˈhoʊstɪŋ/ | v. |共同主持，联合主持 | To host something (like a show, event, or website) together with someone else.  | Example:  We're cohosting a charity gala next month. 我们下个月将共同主持一场慈善晚会。

Example: They are cohosting a podcast about technology. 他们正在共同主持一个关于技术的播客。


近义词：joint hosting,  hosting together


反义词：solo hosting, single hosting


词性：动词
