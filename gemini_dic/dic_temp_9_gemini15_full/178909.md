reddened: /ˈrɛdn̩d/ | adj. | 变红的；脸红的 |  Past participle of "redden," meaning to become red or reddish.  It describes something that has turned red, often due to blushing, anger, or inflammation. |

例句：
Her cheeks reddened with embarrassment. = 她的脸颊羞红了。

His face reddened with rage. = 他气得脸都红了。


近义词： flushed, blushed, inflamed, rosy

反义词： paled, blanched


词性：形容词
