capitation:/ˌkæpɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |人头税；按人头付费制度|The payment of a fixed amount per person to a provider of healthcare services, covering all services provided to that person during a specified period.|The clinic operates under a capitation system, receiving a fixed fee for each patient enrolled.|近义词：per capita payment, per head payment|反义词：fee-for-service
