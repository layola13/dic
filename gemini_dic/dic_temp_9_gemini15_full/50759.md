dervis:/ˈdɜːrvɪʃ/| n. |苦行僧，托钵僧 |A dervish is a member of a Muslim religious order, especially one that practices whirling as a form of worship.  Dervishes are known for their ascetic lifestyles and devotion to Sufism. |Example: The whirling dervishes' hypnotic dance captivated the audience.  旋转的苦行僧的催眠舞蹈迷住了观众。|近义词：fakir, ascetic, mystic |反义词：materialist, hedonist
