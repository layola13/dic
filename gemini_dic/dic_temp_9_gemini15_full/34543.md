clear:/klɪr/|adj., v.|清晰的；明亮的；清楚的；使…清晰；清除；|The explanation was clear and easy to understand. 解释清晰易懂。  The sky is clear tonight. 今晚天空晴朗。 |近义词：transparent, lucid, obvious, distinct|反义词：cloudy, muddy, obscure, vague|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water clears quickly after the rain. 雨后水很快就清澈了。
She clears her throat before speaking. 她说话前清了清嗓子。


一般过去时:
He cleared the table after dinner. 他晚饭后收拾了桌子。
The storm cleared quickly. 暴风雨很快就过去了。


现在完成时:
The police have cleared the road. 警方已经清理了道路。
They have cleared up the misunderstanding. 他们已经消除误解了。


过去完成时:
The judge had cleared him of all charges. 法官已经将他洗脱了所有指控。
She had cleared the dishes before her guests arrived. 客人到达之前，她已经把碗碟收拾好了。


将来时:
The weather will clear up tomorrow. 明天天气将会放晴。
He will clear his desk before leaving. 他离开前会收拾他的桌子。


虚拟语气:
If the weather were clear, we would go hiking. 如果天气晴朗，我们就去远足。
I wish the air were clearer in the city. 我希望城市里的空气更清新。


被动语态:
The path was cleared by the hikers. 小路已经被远足者清理了。
The misunderstanding was cleared up quickly. 误解很快就被澄清了。


疑问句:
Is the water clear enough to swim in? 水够清澈可以游泳吗？
Is the path clear? 路通畅吗？


否定句:
The instructions were not clear. 说明不够清晰。
He didn't clear the dishes. 他没有收拾碗碟。


PHRASE->
clear up: 清理；澄清
make clear: 讲清楚
clear out: 清空；离开
clear the way: 扫清障碍
clear sailing: 一帆风顺
