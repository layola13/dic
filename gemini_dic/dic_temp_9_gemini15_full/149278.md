ozoned:/ˈoʊzoʊnd/| adj.|臭氧化的；充满臭氧的|Containing or treated with ozone.  Ozone is a gas with a pungent odor, often associated with a fresh, clean smell after a thunderstorm.  The word implies a process of treating something with ozone, often for purification or disinfection.|例句：The air smelled ozoned after the storm.  暴风雨过后，空气中弥漫着臭氧味。

The swimming pool water is ozoned to kill bacteria.  游泳池的水经过臭氧处理以杀死细菌。|近义词：ozone-treated, purified, disinfected|反义词：polluted, contaminated|词性：adj.
