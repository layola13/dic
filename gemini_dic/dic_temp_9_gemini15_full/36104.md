coequality:/ˌkoʊˈiːkwɒləti/| n. |平等；同等地位 |The state of being equal in status, rights, or power.  It emphasizes the equal standing of individuals or groups, often in contrast to hierarchical structures. |Example: The principle of coequality among nations is essential for international cooperation.  国家间的平等原则对国际合作至关重要。|近义词：equality, parity, equivalence |反义词：inequality, disparity, hierarchy
