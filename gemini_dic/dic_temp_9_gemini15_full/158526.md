pleochroic:/ˈpliːoʊkroʊɪk/ | adj. |多色的，多色性的| Exhibiting pleochroism; showing different colors when viewed in different directions.  This property is often observed in anisotropic crystals. | Example: The pleochroic tourmaline showed different colors depending on the viewing angle.  这种多色性电气石根据视角的不同显示出不同的颜色。|近义词：dichroic (if only two colors are shown), polychroic|反义词：monochroic
