semi-closed:/ˈsɛmiˌkloʊzd/| adj.|半封闭的，半关闭的|Partially closed; neither completely open nor completely shut.  It suggests a state between openness and closure, often implying some degree of restriction or limited access. |例句：The semi-closed system allows for some interaction with the environment but maintains a degree of internal control. 半封闭系统允许与环境进行一些交互，但保持一定程度的内部控制。


The garden had a semi-closed design, with high walls but open gates. 花园采用半封闭式设计，有高墙但有开放式大门。|近义词：partially closed, restricted, partially open|反义词：open, fully closed, unrestricted
