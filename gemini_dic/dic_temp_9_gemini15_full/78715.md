gigaton:/ˈɡɪɡətɒn/|/ˈɡɪɡəˌtʌn/| n. |吉加吨；十亿吨|A gigaton is a unit of mass equal to one billion (10<sup>9</sup>) metric tons.  It is often used to measure the explosive yield of nuclear weapons or the amount of greenhouse gases emitted. |The explosion had a yield of several gigatons. 这场爆炸的威力达几吉加吨。
