词源:

The word "jade" comes from the Spanish word "piedra de ijada," meaning "loin stone,"  referring to its supposed medicinal properties in treating kidney ailments.  This Spanish term eventually evolved into the English word "jade."  The stone itself has a long and rich history, with various cultures assigning it significant symbolic meaning.
