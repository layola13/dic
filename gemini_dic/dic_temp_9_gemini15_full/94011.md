impalpability: /ɪmˌpælpəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可触知性，难以捉摸 | The quality or state of being impalpable; inability to be felt or touched; intangibility.  It can also refer to something that is difficult to understand or grasp. |例句：The impalpability of the spiritual realm makes it difficult to comprehend. 灵界的不可捉摸性使得它难以理解。

The impalpability of her fear made it hard to comfort her. 她恐惧的难以捉摸使得很难安慰她。

近义词：intangibility, invisibility, elusiveness, vagueness | 反义词：tangibility, concreteness
