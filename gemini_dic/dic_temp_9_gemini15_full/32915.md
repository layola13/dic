choose:/tʃuːz/ | /tʃuːz/ | v. | 选择，挑选 | To select or make a decision between alternatives.  | I chose the red dress. 我选择了那件红裙子。| select, pick, opt | refuse, reject, decline | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I choose the blue one. = 我选择蓝色的那个。
She chooses her words carefully. = 她谨慎地选择措辞。


一般过去时:
He chose a different path. = 他选择了一条不同的路。
They chose to stay home. = 他们选择待在家里。


现在完成时:
I have chosen my career path. = 我已经选择了我的职业道路。
She has chosen a beautiful wedding dress. = 她已经选择了一件美丽的婚纱。


过去完成时:
I had chosen my outfit before the party started. = 派对开始前我已经选好了衣服。
They had chosen a location for the meeting before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经选好了会议地点。


将来时:
I will choose a gift for my friend. = 我将为我的朋友选择一份礼物。
They will choose a new car next week. = 他们下周将选择一辆新车。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would choose differently. = 如果我是你，我会做出不同的选择。
I wish I had chosen a different major in college. = 我真希望我大学时选择了不同的专业。


被动语态:
The winner will be chosen by a panel of judges. = 获胜者将由评审团选出。
The date for the meeting was chosen carefully. = 会议日期是经过仔细选择的。


疑问句:
Which one did you choose? = 你选哪个？
What will you choose to do? = 你会选择做什么？


否定句:
I didn't choose that option. = 我没有选择那个选项。
She didn't choose to participate. = 她没有选择参与。


PHRASE->
choose from = 从...中选择
choose to = 选择做...
choose between = 在...之间选择
make a choice = 做出选择
have a choice = 有选择权
