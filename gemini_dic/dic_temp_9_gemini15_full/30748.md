cession:/sɪˈʃən/ | n. |割让；转让；让与；暂停；休会 |The act of giving up possession or control of something.  It can refer to the transfer of territory, property, or rights.  |例句：The cession of territory to the neighboring country was a controversial decision.  领土割让给邻国是一个有争议的决定。

The company's cession of its patents to a rival was a strategic move.  该公司将其专利转让给竞争对手是一项战略举措。


近义词：transfer, surrender, relinquishment, yielding | 反义词：acquisition, retention, seizure | 词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government cedes control of the project. = 政府放弃了对该项目的控制。
The company cedes market share to its competitors. = 该公司将其市场份额让给了竞争对手。


一般过去时:
France ceded Alsace-Lorraine to Germany after the Franco-Prussian War. = 法国在普法战争后将阿尔萨斯-洛林割让给了德国。
They ceded the land to the developers. = 他们把土地让给了开发商。


现在完成时:
The country has ceded significant territory in recent years. = 该国近年来已割让了大量领土。
The company has ceded its leading position in the market. = 该公司已失去了其在市场上的领先地位。


过去完成时:
Before the treaty was signed, the country had ceded several islands. = 在条约签署之前，该国已经割让了几个岛屿。
The company had ceded its market share long before the merger. = 在合并之前很久，该公司就已经失去了市场份额。


将来时:
The government will cede control of the airport to the private sector. = 政府将把机场的控制权移交给私营部门。
They will cede their rights to the property. = 他们将放弃对该房产的权利。


虚拟语气:
If they ceded control, the project would be more successful. = 如果他们放弃控制权，项目将会更成功。
I wish the company had not ceded its patents. = 我真希望公司没有放弃它的专利。


被动语态:
The land was ceded to the new owners. = 这块土地被转让给了新的所有者。
Control of the company is being ceded to a foreign investor. = 公司的控制权正在被转让给一位外国投资者。


疑问句:
Will the government cede control of the utilities? = 政府会放弃对公共事业的控制吗？
Has the company ceded its intellectual property? = 该公司是否放弃了其知识产权？


否定句:
The government will not cede to pressure from the opposition. = 政府不会屈服于反对派的压力。
The company refused to cede its market position. = 该公司拒绝放弃其市场地位。
