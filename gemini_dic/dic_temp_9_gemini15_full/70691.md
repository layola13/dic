finalized:/ˈfaɪnəlaɪzd/| v. | 使最终确定；定稿；完成 | To make something final or complete; to put the finishing touches on something.  |例句：The contract was finalized yesterday. 合同昨天最终确定了。
The design has been finalized. 设计已经定稿了。|近义词：completed, concluded, settled |反义词：incompleted, unfinished, tentative


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He finalizes the report every week. 他每周都完成报告。
She finalizes her designs before presenting them. 她在展示设计之前都会将其定稿。


一般过去时:
They finalized the deal last night. 他们昨晚敲定了交易。
We finalized the plans for the party. 我们敲定了派对的计划。


现在完成时:
They have finalized the budget for the next year. 他们已经完成了明年的预算。
She has finalized her thesis and submitted it. 她已经完成了论文并提交了。


过去完成时:
He had finalized his presentation before the meeting started. 会议开始前他已经完成了演示文稿。
They had finalized the agreement before the lawyer arrived. 律师到达之前他们已经完成了协议。


将来时:
We will finalize the contract next week. 我们下周将完成合同。
She will finalize the artwork by Friday. 她将在周五前完成艺术作品。


虚拟语气:
If we finalized the agreement, we could start the project. 如果我们敲定协议，我们就可以启动项目了。
I wish I had finalized my report earlier. 我真希望我早点完成我的报告。


被动语态:
The contract was finalized by the legal team. 合同由法律团队最终确定。
The details will be finalized by the end of the week. 细节将在本周末前敲定。


疑问句:
Has the contract been finalized? 合同已经最终确定了吗？
When will the project be finalized? 项目什么时候完成？


否定句:
They haven't finalized the design yet. 他们还没有最终确定设计。
The agreement wasn't finalized due to unforeseen circumstances. 由于不可预见的情况，协议未能最终确定。


PHRASE->

finalize the details: 确定细节
