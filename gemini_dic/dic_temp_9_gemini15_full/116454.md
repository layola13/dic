matchup:/ˈmætʃʌp/| n. |比赛，对决，组合|A matchup is a contest or game between two specific individuals or teams.  It can also refer to the pairing of two things, often for comparison or competition. |例句：The much-anticipated matchup between the two top-ranked tennis players drew a huge crowd.  这场备受期待的两名顶级网球选手之间的比赛吸引了大批观众。

例句：The marketing team created a matchup of products to highlight their complementary features. 市场团队创建了一个产品组合，以突出其互补功能。

近义词：contest, competition, pairing, encounter, confrontation |反义词：agreement, harmony, accord |名词
