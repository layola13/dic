knitter:/ˈnɪtər/| n. |编织者，织造工|A person who knits.  Someone skilled in creating textiles by interlocking loops of yarn with needles.|She's a skilled knitter, creating beautiful sweaters and scarves. 她是一位技艺精湛的编织者，编织出美丽的毛衣和围巾。|weaver, crocheter|  (no direct antonym)|名词
