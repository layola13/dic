senlac:/ˈsɛnlæk/| n. |森拉克战役(1066年)|The Battle of Senlac, also known as the Battle of Hastings, was a pivotal battle in English history. It marked the Norman conquest of England.  The battle took place near Senlac Hill, which is now part of Hastings, East Sussex, England.|例句：The Battle of Senlac resulted in the death of King Harold Godwinson and the beginning of Norman rule in England.  森拉克战役导致了哈罗德·戈德温逊国王的死亡，并标志着诺曼人统治英格兰的开始。|近义词：Battle of Hastings|反义词:Peace, victory (depending on perspective)|名词
