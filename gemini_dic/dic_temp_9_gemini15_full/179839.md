reexpels:/ˌriːɪkˈspɛlz/ | vt. |重新驱逐，再次驱逐 | To expel again; to force someone or something out again.  |例句：The government reexpelled the journalist after he returned to the country. 政府在他回到国家后再次驱逐了这位记者。| 近义词：expel again, banish repeatedly | 反义词：admit, welcome
