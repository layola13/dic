differential:/ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəl/ | adj., n. | 微分的；差别的；差异的；微分；差额 |  An adjective describing something that involves or relates to a difference between things; also a noun referring to a difference or distinction, or a mathematical process of finding the derivative of a function.  |例句：The differential between the two candidates' platforms was significant.  两个候选人平台之间的差异很大。

The differential gear in a car allows the wheels to rotate at different speeds when cornering. 汽车上的差速器允许车轮在转弯时以不同的速度旋转。|近义词：difference, distinction, variation |反义词：similarity, sameness | 词性: adjective, noun
