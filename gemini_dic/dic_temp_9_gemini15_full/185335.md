retriever:/rɪˈtriːvər/| n. |寻回犬，取回者|A retriever is a type of dog bred for its ability to retrieve game, such as waterfowl, for hunters.  They are known for their intelligence, loyalty, and gentle nature. |例句：Golden Retrievers are popular family pets because of their friendly disposition.  金毛寻回犬因其友好的性格而成为受欢迎的家庭宠物。|例句：Labrador Retrievers are often used as guide dogs for the visually impaired. 拉布拉多寻回犬常被用作视障人士的导盲犬。|近义词：seeker, recoverer |反义词：loser, abandoner
