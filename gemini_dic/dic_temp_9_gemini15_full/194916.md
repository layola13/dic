semi-industrial: /ˌsɛmiɪnˈdʌstriəl/ | adj. | 半工业化的；中型工业的 | Relating to or denoting a scale of industrial production that is intermediate between small-scale and large-scale industrial production.  It often implies a level of automation and production volume that is greater than small-scale operations but less than fully industrialized large-scale operations. |例句：The factory is semi-industrial, employing around 50 workers and using some automated machinery. 这个工厂是半工业化的，雇佣大约50名工人，并使用一些自动化机械。

The town has a semi-industrial economy, combining small businesses with a few larger factories. 这个城镇拥有半工业化的经济，将小型企业与几家大型工厂结合在一起。|近义词：moderately industrialized, intermediate-scale |反义词：fully industrialized, small-scale, artisanal
