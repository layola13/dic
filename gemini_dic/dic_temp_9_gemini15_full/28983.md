carte:/kɑːrt/| n.|菜单，地图，图表；（尤指）权利，特权 |A carte is a menu, a map, a chart, or a document granting rights or privileges.  |例句：The restaurant's carte offered a wide variety of dishes. 这家餐馆的菜单提供各种各样的菜肴。|近义词：menu, bill of fare, map, chart, license |反义词：prohibition, restriction
