resection's:/rɪˈsɛkʃən/| n.|切除（术）；移除；截除|The surgical removal of a part of an organ or tissue.  It is often used in the context of medical procedures.|例句：The surgeon performed a resection of the tumor.  外科医生进行了肿瘤切除手术。

例句：The patient underwent a lung resection to remove the cancerous growth. 病人进行了肺部切除手术以去除癌性生长。


近义词：excision, removal, extirpation

反义词：implantation, addition, augmentation

词性：名词 (noun)
