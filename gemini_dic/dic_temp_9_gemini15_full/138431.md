nourishes:/ˈnɜːrɪʃɪz/ | vt. |滋养；培养；鼓励|To provide with the food or other substances necessary for growth, health, and good condition.|例句：A healthy diet nourishes the body.  健康的饮食滋养身体。|例句：The teacher nourishes her students' creativity. 老师培养学生们的创造力。|近义词：sustain, foster, nurture |反义词：starve, deplete
