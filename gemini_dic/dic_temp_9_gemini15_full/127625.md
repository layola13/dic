needlepointed:/ˈniːdlˌpɔɪntɪd/ | adj. | 针织的，针刺的 |  Describes something made using needlepoint, a type of embroidery done with yarn or thread on canvas.  It often refers to a specific stitch or technique, resulting in a textured and raised surface.  | Example: The needlepointed pillow added a touch of elegance to the sofa.  针织的枕头给沙发增添了一丝优雅。The needlepointed belt was intricately designed. 这条针织的腰带设计精巧。|近义词：embroidered, stitched, worked | 反义词：plain, unadorned
