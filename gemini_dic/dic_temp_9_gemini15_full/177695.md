recanceled:/ˌriːˈkænsəld/ | v. |再次取消|To cancel something that has already been canceled.  This implies a second cancellation, often due to unforeseen circumstances or a change in plans. |例句：The flight was canceled due to bad weather, and then recanceled after the weather improved, only to be canceled again later that day. 航班因恶劣天气而取消，天气好转后又恢复了，但当天晚些时候再次取消。  The meeting was recanceled because the key speaker fell ill. 会议因为主要发言人病倒而再次取消。|近义词：cancel again, revoke again |反义词：confirm, reinstate
