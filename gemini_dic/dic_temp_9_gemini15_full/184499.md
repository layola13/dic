restatement:/ˌriːˈsteɪtmənt/ | n. | 重述；改述；重新声明 | A restatement is an act of stating something again, often in a different or clearer way.  It can also refer to the statement itself.  The purpose is usually to clarify, emphasize, or summarize a previous statement. | Example:  The lawyer offered a restatement of the witness's testimony. 律师对证人的证词进行了重述。

The judge's restatement of the law was crucial to the jury's understanding.  法官对法律的重述对于陪审团的理解至关重要。|近义词：reiteration, paraphrase, summary, reformulation |反义词：misrepresentation, distortion, obfuscation |名词
