imbibing:/ɪmˈbɪbɪŋ/| v.| 吸收；饮用；接受；沉浸|The act of absorbing or drinking in.  It can refer to liquids, knowledge, or experiences.|例句：The old house seemed to be imbibing the evening light. 这栋老房子似乎正在吸收傍晚的光线。
例句：He spent the evening imbibing beer and watching television. 他整个晚上都在喝啤酒看电视。|近义词：absorbing, drinking, soaking up, absorbing, assimilating|反义词：expelling, rejecting, ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He imbibes knowledge eagerly. = 他如饥似渴地吸收知识。
They are imbibing the local culture. = 他们在吸收当地文化。

一般过去时:
She imbibed a large glass of water. = 她喝了一大杯水。
We imbibed the atmosphere of the old city. = 我们沉浸在古城的氛围中。

现在完成时:
I have imbibed much wisdom from my experiences. = 我从经验中吸取了很多智慧。
They have imbibed the spirit of the revolution. = 他们已经吸收了革命的精神。

过去完成时:
He had imbibed too much alcohol before driving. = 他开车前喝了太多酒。
She had imbibed the local customs before her trip. = 在旅行之前，她已经了解了当地的习俗。

将来时:
They will imbibe the beauty of the sunset. = 他们将陶醉于日落的美丽。
We will imbibe freely at the party. = 我们将在派对上尽情享用饮品。

虚拟语气:
If I imbibed more water, I wouldn't feel so dehydrated. = 如果我多喝些水，我就不会感觉这么脱水了。
I wish I had imbibed more knowledge during my studies. = 我真希望我在学习期间能吸收更多知识。

被动语态:
The sponge imbibed the liquid readily. = 海绵很容易就吸收了液体。
The information was imbibed quickly by the students. = 学生们很快地吸收了这些信息。

疑问句:
What are you imbibing? = 你在喝什么？
What wisdom are you imbibing from this book? = 你从这本书中吸收了什么智慧？

否定句:
He did not imbibe any alcohol at the party. = 他在派对上没有喝酒。
The plant did not imbibe enough water. = 植物没有吸收足够的水分。
