drainage:/ˈdreɪnɪdʒ/ | n. | 排水，排水系统；流出 | The removal of excess water from land or buildings.  It involves the process of carrying away water from a particular area. This can be done naturally, through rivers and streams, or artificially, through systems of pipes and channels. |例句：The poor drainage in the basement led to flooding.  地下室排水不良导致洪水。

例句：The city invested heavily in improving its drainage system.  该市大力投资改善其排水系统。


近义词：water removal, outflow, evacuation | 反义词：waterlogging, inundation | 词性：名词
