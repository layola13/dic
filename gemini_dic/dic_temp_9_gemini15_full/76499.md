galaxies:/ˈɡæləksiːz/| n. |星系；银河系 |A galaxy is a gravitationally bound system of stars, stellar remnants, interstellar gas and dust, and dark matter.| 例句：Our solar system is located in the Milky Way galaxy. 我们的太阳系位于银河系。

Our galaxy is just one of billions of galaxies in the observable universe. 我们的星系只是可观测宇宙中数十亿个星系中的一个。


近义词：nebulae (if referring to large, diffuse collections of stars and gas) | 反义词：None (galaxies are a broad category) | 词性: 名词

现在完成时:

Astronomers had theorized about the existence of other galaxies long before they were observed. 在观测到其他星系之前，天文学家就已经对它们的存在进行了理论推测。
