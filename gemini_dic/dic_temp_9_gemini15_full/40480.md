contributiveness:/ˌkɒntrɪˈbjuːtɪvnəs/|/ˌkɑːntrɪˈbjuːtɪvnəs/| n. |贡献度；贡献性；有益性 |The degree to which something or someone contributes to a particular outcome or situation.  It often refers to the positive impact or helpfulness of a contribution. |例句：The study assessed the contributiveness of different factors to economic growth. 这项研究评估了不同因素对经济增长的贡献度。

The high contributiveness of the team led to the project's success.  团队的高贡献度导致了项目的成功。|近义词：usefulness, helpfulness, effectiveness, value |反义词：uselessness, harmfulness, ineffectiveness
