eulogized:/ˈjuːlədʒaɪzd/ | vt. | 颂扬；赞美；悼词 | To praise highly, especially in a speech or written work, often at a funeral.  |例句：The speaker eulogized the deceased as a brilliant scientist and a kind friend.  演讲者称赞死者是一位杰出的科学家和一位善良的朋友。

例句：He delivered a heartfelt eulogy that eulogized his father's achievements and his generous spirit. 他发表了一篇发自肺腑的悼词，赞扬了他父亲的成就和慷慨的精神。


近义词：praise, commend, celebrate, extol, laud, acclaim

反义词：criticize, condemn, denounce, vilify

词源:
