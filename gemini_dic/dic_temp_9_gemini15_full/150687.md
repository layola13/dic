词源:

* **para-**:  From Greek preposition παρά (para), meaning "beside," "alongside," or "near."
* **aortic**: From "aorta," ultimately from Greek ἀορτή (aortē), meaning "the great artery."

Therefore, "para-aortic" literally means "beside the aorta" or "near the aorta."
