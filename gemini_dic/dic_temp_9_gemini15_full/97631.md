inherently:/ɪnˈhɪərəntli/ | adv. |固有地；内在地；本质上地 |inherently means existing in something as a permanent, essential, or characteristic attribute.  It describes something that is an inseparable part of something else's nature or being. |例句：The system is inherently flawed.  这个系统本质上是有缺陷的。|例句：Violence is inherently wrong. 暴力本身就是错误的。|近义词：intrinsically, essentially, fundamentally, naturally |反义词：extrinsically, superficially, accidentally
