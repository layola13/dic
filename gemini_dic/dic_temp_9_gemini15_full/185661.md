reverentiality: /ˌrevəˈrenʃiˈæləti/ | n. | 尊敬；敬畏；虔诚 | The quality or state of being reverential; profound respect and awe.  It often implies a deep feeling of respect and admiration, sometimes bordering on worship. |例句：The artist approached the masterpiece with reverentiality, carefully studying every brushstroke.  艺术家怀着敬畏之心靠近这幅杰作，仔细研究每一笔笔触。

例句：Her reverentiality towards her elders was evident in her polite and respectful behavior. 她对长辈的尊敬在她有礼貌和尊重的行为中显而易见。


近义词：respect, awe, veneration, homage, piety, deference | 反义词：disrespect, irreverence, contempt, scorn


词性:名词
