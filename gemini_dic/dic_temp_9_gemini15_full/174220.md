quenelle:/kənˈɛl/| n. |法式小扁圆面包，小肉丸|A quenelle is a small, oval-shaped dumpling or ball of food, often made from fish, meat, or vegetables, and typically served in a sauce.  It is a classic element of French cuisine.|The chef prepared a delicate quenelle of salmon in a creamy dill sauce.|近义词：dumpling, croquette, ball|反义词：无
