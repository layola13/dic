handedness: /ˈhændɪdnəs/ | n. | 手性；惯用手；左右手习惯 | The preference for using one hand over the other;  a characteristic of individuals, relating to their dominant hand.  It can also refer to the chirality of molecules. | Example:  His handedness is right-handed.  他的惯用手是右手。  The handedness of the molecule affects its properties. 分子的手性影响其特性。|近义词：laterality, dexterity (in the context of skill)|反义词：ambidexterity (ability to use both hands equally well)|
