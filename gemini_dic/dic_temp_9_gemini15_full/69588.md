felting:/ˈfɛltɪŋ/| n. v.|毡化；制作毡；毛毡|The process of matting or pressing fibers together to form a fabric, typically wool, without spinning or weaving.  It can also refer to the resulting fabric.|例句：She learned the art of felting and now makes beautiful hats. 她学习了毡化工艺，现在制作精美的帽子。

例句：The wool was felted to create a warm and durable fabric. 羊毛被毡化成一种温暖耐用的织物。


近义词：fulling, matting|反义词：weaving, knitting|名词，动词
