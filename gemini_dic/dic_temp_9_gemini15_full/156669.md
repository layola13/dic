picturising: /ˈpɪktʃəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. |  图像化；描绘；想象 | The act of forming a mental image or picture of something.  It involves creating a visual representation of something in one's mind, or translating something into a visual form. |


例句：

He was picturising the scene from the novel in his mind. = 他正在脑海中描绘小说中的场景。

The director is picturising the script for the upcoming movie. = 导演正在为即将上映的电影制作分镜头脚本。


近义词：visualizing, imaging, illustrating, depicting

反义词：abstracting, ignoring, neglecting


词性: 动词


词源:  From "picture" + "-ise" (suffix forming verbs).  "Picture" ultimately derives from the Latin word "pictura," meaning "painting."  The "-ise" suffix is of French origin, and indicates the act of making or becoming something.
