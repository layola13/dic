crash-land:/ˈkræʃˌlænd/| vt.|坠毁着陆；紧急迫降|To make (an aircraft, etc.) land in an emergency, often resulting in damage.|例句：The pilot crash-landed the plane in a field. 飞行员将飞机紧急迫降在田野里。

例句：The small plane crash-landed after engine failure.  小型飞机因引擎故障而紧急迫降。


近义词：emergency land, ditch (informal)


反义词：smooth landing, controlled landing


词性:动词
