pathfindings:/ˈpæθˌfaɪndɪŋz/ | n. | 探路；探险；开拓；先驱探索 | The act of finding or creating a new path or route, often involving exploration and discovery.  It can refer to physical journeys or metaphorical processes of innovation and problem-solving. | Example:  The early pathfindings of the explorers led to the discovery of new continents. 早期探险家的探路活动导致了新大陆的发现。

The company's pathfindings in renewable energy have made them a leader in the field.  这家公司在可再生能源领域的开拓性工作使其成为该领域的领导者。|近义词：explorations, pioneering, trailblazing, discoveries |反义词：following, stagnation, imitation
