precensorships:/ˌpriːˈsɛnsərʃɪps/| n.| 事先审查制度；预先审查 | The practice of reviewing and approving material before it is published or broadcast.  This often involves suppressing or altering content deemed objectionable. | Example: The government imposed strict precensorships on all media outlets.  政府对所有媒体机构实施了严格的预先审查制度。|近义词：pre-publication review, prior restraint |反义词：freedom of speech, open access
