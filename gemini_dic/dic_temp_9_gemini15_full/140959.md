one-to-one:/ˌwʌn tə ˈwʌn/| adj.|一对一的；个别的|  Describes a relationship or interaction involving only two people or things, with a direct correspondence between them.  It implies a personalized and focused approach.|例句：The tutor provided one-to-one instruction to each student.  导师为每位学生提供一对一的指导。|例句：The company offers one-to-one customer service. 公司提供一对一的客户服务。|近义词：individual, personal, private, direct|反义词：group, collective, mass
