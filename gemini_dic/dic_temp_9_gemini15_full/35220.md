clued-in: /kluːdˈɪn/ | adj. |  了解情况的；消息灵通的 |  Well-informed; aware of what is happening.  |  He's really clued-in about the latest technology. 他非常了解最新的科技。  The clued-in investors saw the opportunity early. 消息灵通的投资者很早就看到了这个机会。|  informed, knowledgeable, savvy, up-to-date | uninformed, ignorant, naive, clueless

USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is clued-in about the company's financial situation. = 他很了解公司的财务状况。
They are clued-in to the latest industry trends. = 他们了解最新的行业趋势。

一般过去时:
She was clued-in to the secret plan. = 她了解这个秘密计划。
We were clued-in about the upcoming changes. = 我们了解即将到来的变化。


现在完成时:
I have been clued-in about the project's challenges. = 我已经了解了这个项目的挑战。
They have become clued-in on the market's volatility. = 他们已经了解了市场的波动性。


过去完成时:
He had been clued-in about the risks before he invested. = 在他投资之前，他已经了解了风险。
We had already been clued-in to the problem before the meeting. = 在会议之前，我们已经了解了这个问题。


将来时:
She will be clued-in on the details by next week. = 到下周她将了解这些细节。
They will be fully clued-in after the presentation. = 演示之后，他们将完全了解情况。


虚拟语气:
If I were clued-in, I would have invested differently. = 如果我了解情况，我会采取不同的投资方式。
I wish I had been clued-in earlier. = 我希望我早点了解情况。


被动语态:
The team was kept clued-in throughout the process. = 在整个过程中，团队一直被告知情况。
The public needs to be clued-in about the dangers. = 公众需要了解这些危险。


疑问句:
Are you clued-in about the new regulations? = 你了解新的规章制度吗？
Is he clued-in enough to handle this situation? = 他了解情况足以处理这种情况吗？


否定句:
He wasn't clued-in about the changes. = 他不了解这些变化。
They were not clued-in to the implications. = 他们不了解其影响。
