freshwater:/ˈfrɛʃˌwɔːtər/| adj. n.|淡水的；淡水生物|Relating to or living in fresh water, not salty water.  Freshwater habitats include lakes, rivers, streams, and ponds.|例句：The lake is a freshwater ecosystem.  这个湖泊是一个淡水生态系统。|例句：Many freshwater fish cannot survive in saltwater. 许多淡水鱼无法在盐水中生存。|近义词：non-saline|反义词：saltwater, marine
