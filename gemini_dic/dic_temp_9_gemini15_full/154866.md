perversity:/pərˈvɜːrsɪti/| n. |变态；故意作对；邪恶；乖张|The quality of being perverse; a deliberate deviation from what is considered normal, proper, or expected; often with an element of wickedness or malice.  It can also refer to an instance of this behavior.|例句：Her perversity led her to do the exact opposite of what everyone advised.她的乖张让她做了与所有人建议完全相反的事情。

例句：The perversity of fate meant that just as he was about to succeed, everything went wrong.命运的捉弄意味着就在他即将成功的时候，一切都出了差错。


近义词：contrariness, perverseness, waywardness, wilfulness, defiance, wickedness, depravity |反义词：obedience, conformity, righteousness, goodness
