dosser:/ˈdɒsə(r)/| n. |游民；流浪汉；懒汉|A dosser is a person who is homeless and sleeps rough, often characterized by laziness or a lack of ambition.  The term carries a strong negative connotation and is considered derogatory.  |Example: He became a dosser after losing his job and his apartment. 他失业又失去公寓后变成了个游民。  |近义词：tramp, vagrant, bum, hobo |反义词：hard worker, diligent, ambitious |名词
