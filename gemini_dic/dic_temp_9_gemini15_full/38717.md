condiments:/ˈkɑːndɪmənts/| n.|调味品，佐料|Substances such as spices, sauces, and relishes that are added to food to enhance its flavor.  |Example: Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise are common condiments.  番茄酱、芥末酱和蛋黄酱是常见的调味品。|近义词：seasonings, spices, sauces, relish|反义词：无
