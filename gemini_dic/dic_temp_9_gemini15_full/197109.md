shamoy:/ʃəˈmɔɪ/| n. |沙莫伊皮革；软皮革|Shammy leather is a type of soft, suede-like leather traditionally made from chamois skin. It is known for its absorbency and is often used for cleaning and polishing.  |Example: He used a shammy to dry the car after washing it. 他用沙莫伊皮擦干洗过的汽车。|近义词：suede, buckskin|反义词：hard leather, stiff leather|名词
