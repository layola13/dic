localism:/ˈloʊkəlɪzəm/ | n. |地方主义；地方特色；方言；土语 |The belief or practice of emphasizing local interests and values over national or global ones.  It can also refer to features unique to a particular place, such as dialects or customs. |例句：The rise of localism is a response to globalization.  地方主义的兴起是对全球化的回应。

例句：The restaurant's menu showcases the localism of the region. 这家餐馆的菜单展示了该地区的地方特色。


近义词：regionalism, provincialism, nativism |反义词：globalism, cosmopolitanism, internationalism |词性:名词
