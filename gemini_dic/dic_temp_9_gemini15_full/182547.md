renunciate:/rɪˈnʌnsieɪt/| vt.|宣布放弃；正式放弃；声明放弃|To formally declare one's abandonment of a claim, right, or responsibility.  It implies a deliberate and often public act of relinquishing something.|He renounced his claim to the throne. 他放弃了他对王位的继承权。|She renounced her citizenship. 她放弃了她的国籍。|近义词：abandon, relinquish, forsake, forgo|反义词：claim, retain, keep, assert

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He renounces his belief in the old ways. 他放弃了他对旧习俗的信仰。
She renounces her inheritance. 她放弃了她的遗产。


一般过去时:
He renounced his title after the scandal. 丑闻过后，他放弃了他的头衔。
She renounced her membership in the club. 她退出了俱乐部的会员资格。


现在完成时:
He has renounced his violent past. 他已经放弃了他暴力的过去。
She has renounced her former lifestyle. 她已经放弃了她以前的生活方式。


过去完成时:
He had renounced his faith before he joined the army. 他入伍前就已放弃了他的信仰。
She had renounced her position before the accusations became public.  指控公开之前，她就已辞去了她的职务。


将来时:
He will renounce his claim to the property. 他将放弃他对该房产的所有权。
She will renounce her citizenship next year. 她明年将放弃她的国籍。


虚拟语气:
If he renounced his ambition, he might find peace. 如果他放弃了他的野心，他可能会找到平静。
I wish she had renounced her harmful habits sooner. 我希望她早点放弃她那些有害的习惯。


被动语态:
His claim was renounced by the court. 他的主张被法院驳回。
Her membership was renounced by the organization. 她的会员资格被组织取消。


疑问句:
Will he renounce his position? 他会放弃他的职位吗？
Has she renounced her beliefs? 她放弃了她的信仰吗？


否定句:
He did not renounce his right to appeal. 他没有放弃他的上诉权。
She would not renounce her principles. 她不会放弃她的原则。
