lean-to:/ˈliːnˌtuː/ | n. | 倚靠式建筑，简易棚屋 | A lean-to is a simple structure with a roof that leans against an existing wall or building. It's typically a single-sloped roof supported by a single wall.  |例句：The gardener built a lean-to against the barn to store tools. 园丁在谷仓旁建了一个简易棚屋来存放工具。
The children built a lean-to out of branches and leaves for shelter. 孩子们用树枝和树叶搭建了一个简易棚屋作为避难所。|近义词：shed, shelter, outbuilding |反义词：mansion, palace
