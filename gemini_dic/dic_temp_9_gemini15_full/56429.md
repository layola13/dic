dollies:/ˈdɑːliz/| n.|小推车；玩具娃娃；(电影)摄影推车|Small wheeled carts used for moving heavy objects, often found in factories or warehouses.  Also refers to dolls, especially small ones. In filmmaking, it refers to a camera dolly used for smooth camera movement.|例句：The workers used dollies to move the heavy boxes across the warehouse. 工人们用小推车把沉重的箱子搬过仓库。
例句：She loved playing with her collection of antique dollies. 她喜欢玩她收藏的古董娃娃。


近义词：handcart, truck, cart, doll (in the sense of dolls)
反义词：N/A (depends on the context)
