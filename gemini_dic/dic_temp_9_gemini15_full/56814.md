dork:/dɔːrk/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A dork is a person who is considered to be socially awkward, unpopular, or unstylish.  They are often perceived as being clumsy, nerdy, or lacking social skills.|He's a bit of a dork, always tripping over his own feet.|近义词：nerd, geek, klutz, goofball|反义词：cool, popular, stylish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a total dork. = 他是个十足的笨蛋。
She acts like a dork sometimes. = 她有时表现得像个傻瓜。


一般过去时:
