reggae:/ˈrɛɡeɪ/| n. |雷鬼乐|A style of Jamaican music characterized by a strong, syncopated rhythm and often featuring social or political commentary.  Reggae is known for its distinctive off-beat rhythm, and its use of instruments such as the guitar, bass, drums, and keyboard.| Example: Bob Marley is a legendary reggae artist.  鲍勃·马利是一位传奇的雷鬼音乐家。|近义词：ska, rocksteady |反义词:None
