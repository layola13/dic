nazaritic: /ˌnæzəˈrɪtɪk/ | adj. | 纳细耳人的；纳细耳派的；与纳细耳人有关的 | Relating to the Nazirites, a group of ancient Israelites who took vows of abstinence and consecration.  The term often refers to their distinctive lifestyle characterized by abstinence from wine, strong drink, and contact with the dead. |  The Nazaritic vows were a significant aspect of Israelite religious practice. | 近义词: consecrated, dedicated, devoted | 反义词: profane, secular
