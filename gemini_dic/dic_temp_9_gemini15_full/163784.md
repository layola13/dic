predelivery:/ˌpriːdɪˈlɪvəri/| n. |交付前，预先交付|The activities and processes that occur before a product or service is delivered to the customer.  It encompasses all aspects of preparation and planning to ensure a smooth and successful delivery.  This includes things like quality control checks, packaging, and shipping arrangements.|Example: Predelivery inspections are crucial to minimize issues after the product reaches the customer.  交付前的检查对于最大限度地减少产品到达客户后出现的问题至关重要。|近义词：pre-delivery, prior delivery, ante-delivery|反义词：postdelivery, after-delivery|名词
