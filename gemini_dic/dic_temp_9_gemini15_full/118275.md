merchants:/ˈmɜːrtʃənts/ | n. | 商人，店主 | People engaged in commerce, especially those who buy and sell goods in large quantities.  They are involved in trade and buying and selling on a large scale. | 例句：The merchants of the old city were renowned for their hospitality.  老城里的商人以好客而闻名。

例句：Many merchants set up stalls at the market. 许多商人都在市场上摆摊。


近义词：traders, dealers, retailers, wholesalers | 反义词：consumers, buyers (in a relative sense) | 词性:名词
