patronal:/ˈpeɪtrənəl/| adj.|赞助人的；主顾的；老板的；恩主的| Relating to a patron; characteristic of a patron.  It can also refer to something relating to an employer or a person in a position of authority. |例句：The company held a patronal feast to celebrate its anniversary. 公司举办了一场赞助人宴会来庆祝周年纪念。 The patronal address was inspiring.  老板的讲话很鼓舞人心。|近义词：protective, benevolent,  supportive|反义词：uncaring, antagonistic, hostile
