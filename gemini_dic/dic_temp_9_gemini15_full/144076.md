outrunner:/ˈaʊtrʌnər/| n. |领先者，先驱，逃亡者|A person or thing that runs ahead of others; a pioneer or innovator; someone who escapes or flees.|例句：The new technology is an outrunner in the field of renewable energy. 这项新技术是可再生能源领域的先驱。

例句：The criminal was an outrunner, evading capture for months.  罪犯是一个逃亡者，几个月来一直躲避追捕。


近义词：pioneer, innovator, forerunner, fugitive, escapee |反义词：follower, laggard, captive
