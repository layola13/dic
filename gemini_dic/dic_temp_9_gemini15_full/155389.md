/fæz/ | n. |相位，阶段，方面|  A particular point in time, especially in a process or cycle; an aspect or stage.  |The project is entering a new phase. 项目进入了一个新的阶段。|stage, step, aspect |  |noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What phase is the moon in tonight? = 今晚月亮处于哪个阶段？
What phase of the project are we in now? = 我们现在处于项目的哪个阶段？


一般过去时:
What phase was the project in last year? = 去年项目处于哪个阶段？
What phase of the relationship were they in? = 他们当时处于恋爱关系的哪个阶段？


现在完成时:
What phases have we already passed through? = 我们已经经历了哪些阶段？
What phases of the disease has she experienced so far? = 到目前为止她经历了疾病的哪些阶段？


过去完成时:
What phase had the construction project reached before the storm? = 暴风雨来临之前，建筑项目进行到了哪个阶段？
What phase of the negotiation had they completed before the break? = 休息之前他们完成了谈判的哪个阶段？


将来时:
What phase will the moon be in next week? = 下周月亮会处于哪个阶段？
What phase will the research project enter next year? = 研究项目明年将进入哪个阶段？


虚拟语气:
If the project were in a different phase, we could do things differently. = 如果项目处于不同的阶段，我们可以采取不同的方法。
If I had known the phase of the moon, I would have planned better. = 如果我知道月亮的阶段，我会计划得更好。


被动语态:
What phase was the product development plan in when the funding was cut? = 资金被削减时，产品开发计划处于哪个阶段？
What phase is this process currently in? = 这个过程目前处于哪个阶段？


疑问句:
What phase are we in? = 我们现在处于哪个阶段？
What phase of life are you in? = 你现在处于人生的哪个阶段？


否定句:
The project is not yet in the testing phase. = 项目尚未进入测试阶段。
I don't know what phase the negotiations are in. = 我不知道谈判处于哪个阶段。
