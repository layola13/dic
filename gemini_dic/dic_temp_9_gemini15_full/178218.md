recommissioning: /ˌriːkəˈmɪʃənɪŋ/ | v. |重新启用；重新委托；再启用 | The act of putting something back into service after it has been decommissioned or out of service for a period.  This often involves inspections, repairs, and updates to ensure the item is safe and functional. | Example: The bridge underwent extensive recommissioning after the earthquake.  桥梁在地震后进行了大规模的重新启用。 The company decided to recommission the old factory after a thorough renovation. 公司决定在彻底翻新后重新启用旧工厂。 | 近义词:reactivation, reinstatement, restarting | 反义词: decommissioning, retirement, scrapping |
