phyllopod:/ˈfɪləpɒd/ | n. |叶足类动物 |A phyllopod is an aquatic crustacean of the subclass Phyllopoda, characterized by broad, leaf-like appendages used for swimming and respiration.  They are typically found in temporary pools and other freshwater habitats. | Example:  The fairy shrimp is a well-known example of a phyllopod.  叶虾是叶足类动物的一个著名例子。 |近义词: branchiopod (a broader term)| 反义词:  None readily available.  |  词性: 名词
