overeducation:/ˌoʊvərˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃən/| n. |过度教育|The phenomenon of possessing educational qualifications exceeding the requirements of one's job or career path.  It often refers to individuals who have obtained advanced degrees (e.g., master's or doctoral degrees) but are employed in positions that do not fully utilize their skills and knowledge.  This can lead to feelings of underemployment, dissatisfaction, and even financial strain. |Example:  The high unemployment rate among recent PhD graduates highlights the issue of overeducation.  过度教育问题在近期博士毕业生高失业率中体现得淋漓尽致。|近义词:overqualification, over-skilling|反义词:undereducation, underqualification|名词
