oecology:/iˈkɒlədʒi/| n. |生态学|The scientific study of the relationships between organisms and their environment.  This includes the study of how organisms interact with each other and with their physical surroundings.  The field encompasses various levels of organization, from individuals to populations, communities, and ecosystems. |Example:  The oecology of the rainforest is incredibly complex.  = 雨林的生态系统极其复杂。

Example:  She is studying the oecology of the coral reef. = 她正在研究珊瑚礁的生态学。|近义词：ecology|反义词：N/A
