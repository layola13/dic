lawning:/ˈlɔːnɪŋ/ | n. |修剪草坪；割草 |The act of mowing or otherwise maintaining a lawn.  It often involves cutting the grass, edging, and other tasks to keep the lawn neat and tidy. |例句：The lawning was completed before the party.  = 派对之前草坪修剪完成了。 |例句：Lawning is a necessary chore for homeowners. = 修剪草坪是房主必须做的家务。 |近义词：mowing, grass cutting, lawn maintenance |反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be letting the lawn grow wild.
