non-electrified: /ˌnɒnɪˈlɛktrɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 未通电的，非电气化的 | Not electrified; lacking an electrical system.  |  例句：The train line is non-electrified, so the journey will be slower. 这条铁路线未通电，所以行程会比较慢。


例句：The railway system in this region is largely non-electrified, relying instead on diesel engines. 这个地区的铁路系统很大程度上未通电，而是依靠柴油发动机。


近义词：unpowered, unelectrified
