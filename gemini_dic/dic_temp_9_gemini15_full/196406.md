serpent:/ˈsɜːrpənt/| n.|蛇，阴险的人|A serpent is a snake, often a large or dangerous one.  The word can also be used metaphorically to describe someone deceitful or treacherous.|例句：The garden snake is a harmless serpent.  花园里的蛇是无害的。|例句：He was a serpent in the guise of a friend. 他伪装成朋友，实则阴险狡诈。|近义词：snake, viper, reptile|反义词： (无直接反义词，可根据语境用象征美好事物的词语对比，例如：dove, lamb)|名词
