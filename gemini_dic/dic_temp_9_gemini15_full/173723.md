quasi-musical:/ˈkwɑːziˌmjuːzɪkəl/|/ˈkwɑːzaɪˌmjuːzɪkəl/| adj.|准音乐的，似音乐的 |  Something that has qualities resembling music but doesn't fully qualify as music.  It might incorporate musical elements but lack the structure or conventions of traditional music. |The film's soundtrack had a quasi-musical quality, blending ambient sounds with melodic fragments.|近义词：semi-musical, musical-like|反义词：purely musical, non-musical
