psittacosis:/ˌsɪtəˈkoʊsɪs/| n. |鹦鹉热，鸟疫|A zoonotic bacterial disease caused by Chlamydia psittaci, which can be transmitted to humans through contact with infected birds.  Symptoms include fever, headache, cough, and pneumonia.|例句：Psittacosis is a serious disease that requires prompt medical attention. 鹦鹉热是一种严重的疾病，需要及时就医。|例句：The outbreak of psittacosis was traced back to a shipment of infected parrots. 鹦鹉热疫情的爆发追溯到一批受感染的鹦鹉。|近义词：ornithosis|反义词：无
