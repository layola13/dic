nondefamatory:/ˌnɒnˈdɛfəməˌtɔːri/| adj. | 不诽谤的，无损名誉的 | Not damaging to someone's reputation.  This adjective describes statements, publications, or actions that do not harm a person's good name or standing. | Example: The article, while critical, remained nondefamatory in its presentation of facts. 这篇文章虽然很批判，但在呈现事实方面仍然没有诽谤。 | 近义词：inoffensive, harmless, innocuous | 反义词：defamatory, libelous, slanderous
