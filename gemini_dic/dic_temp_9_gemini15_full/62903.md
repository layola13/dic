enlisted:/ɪnˈlɪstɪd/ | v. (过去分词) | 入伍的；征募的； | To enlist means to join the armed forces or to officially register someone as a member of the armed forces.  It can also mean to obtain the support or help of someone. |  He enlisted in the army after graduating from college. 他大学毕业后参军了。  The general enlisted the help of his officers. 将军寻求他军官们的帮助。|  recruited, enrolled |  discharged, demobilized | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They enlist new soldiers every month. = 他们每个月都招募新兵。
She actively enlists the support of her friends. = 她积极争取朋友的支持。

一般过去时:
He enlisted in the navy during World War II. = 他在二战期间加入了海军。
The company enlisted the services of a marketing expert. = 公司聘请了一位市场营销专家。

现在完成时:
Many young people have enlisted in the armed forces this year. = 今年许多年轻人参军了。
She has enlisted the help of several colleagues on this project. = 她在这个项目上获得了几个同事的帮助。

过去完成时:
Before he moved to London, he had enlisted in the air force. = 在他搬到伦敦之前，他已经加入了空军。
They had enlisted the support of the local community before starting the project. = 他们在开始项目之前已经获得了当地社区的支持。


将来时:
He will enlist in the marines next year. = 他明年将加入海军陆战队。
The company plans to enlist the expertise of a renowned consultant. = 公司计划聘请一位著名的顾问。

虚拟语气:
If I were younger, I would enlist in the army. = 如果我还年轻，我会参军。
I wish I had enlisted in the navy instead of the army. = 我真希望当初加入的是海军而不是陆军。

被动语态:
New recruits are enlisted twice a year. = 新兵每年招募两次。
His services were enlisted by a large corporation. = 他的服务被一家大公司聘用。

疑问句:
Did he enlist in the army or the navy? = 他是参军还是海军？
Have you enlisted the help of anyone on your project? = 你在这个项目上寻求过任何人的帮助吗？

否定句:
He did not enlist in any branch of the military. = 他没有加入任何军事部门。
They did not enlist the support of the local government. = 他们没有得到当地政府的支持。


PHRASE->

enlist in: 加入（军队等）
enlist the help of: 寻求...的帮助
enlist the support of: 获得...的支持
be enlisted as: 被列为...
enlist oneself: 自愿参军
