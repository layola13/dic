poohs:/puːz/| n.| pooh pooh的复数形式 | pooh pooh的复数，表示多次轻蔑地拒绝或不屑一顾|The committee poohs at the suggestion.  委员会对这个建议嗤之以鼻。 |近义词：rejections, dismissals|反义词：approvals, acceptances|名词


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations like "is", "are", "was", etc.  It's used as a plural form of "pooh-pooh".  Therefore, tense conjugation examples are not applicable.


PHRASE->
pooh-pooh something: to express contempt or disbelief towards something.  轻蔑地拒绝或不屑一顾某事
give a pooh-pooh to something: to dismiss something contemptuously.  轻蔑地驳回某事
a pooh-pooh attitude: a dismissive and contemptuous attitude.  一种轻蔑不屑的态度
met with poohs: received with scorn and dismissal.  受到轻蔑和驳回
a chorus of poohs: many expressions of contempt and disbelief.  许多轻蔑和怀疑的表达
