closetful: /ˈklɑːzɪtfʊl/ | n. | 壁橱里的东西；满满一壁橱 | A closetful refers to the contents of a closet, implying a full or significant amount.  It's often used figuratively to describe a large quantity of something. |例句：The closetful of clothes was overwhelming.  (衣橱里满满的衣服让人难以招架。)  She had a closetful of shoes. (她有一衣橱的鞋子。) |近义词：wardrobe, collection, hoard |反义词:empty, sparse
