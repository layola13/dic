cote:/koʊt/ | n. | 棚屋；羊圈；小湾；股票报价单；(织物)纬纱 | A small, enclosed shelter, especially one for animals.  | The farmer kept his sheep in a cote.  农夫把羊养在羊圈里。 | pen, shed, stall |  (无明确反义词) | 名词
