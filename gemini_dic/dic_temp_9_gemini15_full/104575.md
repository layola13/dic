kangaroo:/ˌkæŋɡəˈruː/| n. |袋鼠|A marsupial mammal of Australia, having a strong tail and powerful hind legs for leaping.  Kangaroos are herbivores and vary greatly in size depending on the species.| Example: The kangaroo hopped across the field. 袋鼠跳过田野。|近义词：wallaby (较小的袋鼠)|反义词：无
