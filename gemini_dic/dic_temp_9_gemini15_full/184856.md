resynthesizing:/ˌriːˈsɪnθəsaɪzɪŋ/| v.|重新合成，再合成|The process of creating something new by combining existing elements.  This often involves breaking down a substance or concept into its constituent parts and then reassembling them in a different way.  It implies a more complex and deliberate process than simply synthesizing.|例句：The chemist is resynthesizing the compound to improve its stability. 化学家正在重新合成该化合物以提高其稳定性。

例句：The artist is resynthesizing their earlier work to create a new, more cohesive collection. 艺术家正在重新整合他们早期的作品，以创造一个新的、更具凝聚力的系列。


近义词：recompose, reconstruct, reformulate, re-create|反义词：decompose, destroy, dismantle|动词
