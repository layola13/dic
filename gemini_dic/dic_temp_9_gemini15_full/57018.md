doubled:/ˈdʌbld/| v. adj.|加倍的；使加倍|To make or become twice as much or as many.  It can also describe something that has been folded in two.|例句：The company doubled its profits this year. 今年公司利润翻了一番。

The road doubled back on itself. 这条路折返回去。|近义词：increased, multiplied, duplicated|反义词：halved, reduced


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He doubles the recipe to feed more people. = 他把食谱加倍以供更多人食用。
The price doubles every year. = 价格每年翻倍。


一般过去时:
She doubled the amount of sugar in the cake. = 她把蛋糕里的糖量加倍了。
The population doubled in just ten years. = 人口在短短十年内翻了一番。


现在完成时:
The company has doubled its workforce. = 公司的员工人数翻了一番。
Our profits have doubled since last year. = 自去年以来，我们的利润翻了一番。


过去完成时:
They had doubled their efforts before the deadline. = 他们在大限之前加倍努力。
The value of the house had doubled by the time they sold it. = 到他们卖掉房子的时候，房子的价值已经翻了一番。


将来时:
We will double our production next year. = 我们明年将把产量翻一番。
The price of petrol is likely to double in the coming months. = 未来几个月汽油价格可能会翻一番。


虚拟语气:
If we doubled our marketing budget, we might see better results. = 如果我们将营销预算翻一番，我们可能会看到更好的结果。
I wish I had doubled my investment earlier. = 我希望我早点把投资翻一番。


被动语态:
The recipe needs to be doubled to serve more guests. = 食谱需要加倍才能招待更多客人。
The production was doubled to meet the increased demand. = 产量翻了一番以满足日益增长的需求。


疑问句:
Did they double the dosage? = 他们是否加倍了剂量？
Has the size of the city doubled in the last decade? = 在过去的十年里，这座城市的大小是否翻了一番？


否定句:
They didn't double-check their work. = 他们没有仔细检查他们的工作。（double-check是动词短语）
The effort wasn't doubled, so the results were disappointing. =  努力没有加倍，所以结果令人失望。
