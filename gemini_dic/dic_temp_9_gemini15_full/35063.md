closes:/kloʊzɪz/ | v. | 关闭；结束；合拢 | To shut or fasten something.  It can also refer to the ending of something. | 例句：He closes the door. 他关上门。  The store closes at 5 pm. 商店下午五点关门。 | 近义词：shut, terminate, conclude | 反义词：open, begin, start | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shop closes at 6 pm every day. = 这家商店每天晚上6点关门。
She closes her eyes before she sleeps. = 她睡觉前闭上眼睛。


一般过去时:
He closed the window because it was cold. = 他关上了窗户，因为天气很冷。
The meeting closed after two hours. = 会议进行了两个小时后结束。


现在完成时:
They have closed the account. = 他们已经关闭了账户。
The store has closed down permanently. = 这家商店已经永久关闭。


过去完成时:
She had closed the book before she went to bed. = 她睡觉前已经合上了书。
The police had closed the investigation. = 警方已经结案了。


将来时:
I will close the deal tomorrow. = 我明天将完成这笔交易。
The school will close for the summer holidays next week. = 学校下周将放暑假。


虚拟语气:
If I closed my eyes, I couldn't see the beautiful scenery. = 如果我闭上眼睛，就看不到美丽的风景了。
I wish I had closed the window earlier. = 我真希望我早点关上窗户。


被动语态:
The door was closed by someone. = 门被某人关上了。
The case was closed after a long investigation. = 经过长时间的调查后，案件结案了。


疑问句:
Does the library close on Sundays? = 图书馆星期天关门吗？
When does the bank close? = 银行几点关门？


否定句:
The shop doesn't close until 10 pm. = 商店直到晚上10点才关门。
He didn't close the door properly. = 他没有把门关好。


PHRASE->
close down: 关闭 (a business)
close in: 逼近
close off: 封锁
close to: 接近
close ranks: 紧密团结
