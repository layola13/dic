quasi:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ/| adj.|准的；似…的；半…的；类似的| seemingly; apparently but not truly or fully;  The word "quasi" indicates something that resembles or has the characteristics of something else, but is not entirely or completely that thing. It suggests an approximation or partial resemblance.  |例句：The company has a quasi-governmental role. 这家公司扮演着准政府的角色。

例句：He held a quasi-official position. 他担任着一个准官方的职位。


近义词：semi-, pseudo-, seeming, apparent|反义词：true, genuine, complete, real|词性:形容词
