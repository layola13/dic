conservationists:/ˌkɒnsəˈveɪʃənɪsts/| n. |自然资源保护主义者，环保人士|People who actively work to protect and preserve the environment and natural resources. They advocate for sustainable practices and policies to prevent environmental degradation and promote biodiversity.|例句：Many conservationists are fighting to protect endangered species. 许多环保人士正在努力保护濒危物种。

例句：Conservationists are working to restore damaged ecosystems.  环保人士正在努力恢复受损的生态系统。


近义词：environmentalists, preservationists, environmental activists|反义词：destroyers, polluters, environmental degraders|词性:名词
