shrinkability: /ˌʃrɪŋkəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可收缩性，可缩小性 | The ability of something to shrink or be shrunk.  This refers to the capacity of a material, object, or system to reduce in size, volume, or extent.  | Example: The shrinkability of wool is a significant factor in garment manufacturing.  羊毛的可收缩性是服装制造中的一个重要因素。 The high shrinkability of the fabric caused the sweater to become too small.  织物的收缩性很高，导致毛衣变得太小了。 | 近义词：contractibility, reducibility | 反义词：inextensibility, irreducibility
