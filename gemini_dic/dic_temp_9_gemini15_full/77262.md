gastropodous:/ˌɡæstrɒˈpoʊdəs/ | adj. | 腹足类的；具有腹足的 | Having or relating to a gastropod.  The term describes animals belonging to the class Gastropoda, characterized by a single shell (in most cases) and a muscular foot used for locomotion. | Example: The gastropodous snail left a silvery trail across the damp pavement.  腹足类的蜗牛在潮湿的人行道上留下一条银色的痕迹。|近义词: gastropodan |反义词:  cephalopodous (头足类的)
