lunisolar:/ˈluːnɪˌsoʊlər/ | adj. |阴阳的，阴阳历的 | Relating to or denoting a calendar system that combines lunar months with solar years.  A lunisolar calendar attempts to reconcile the lunar month (roughly 29.5 days) with the solar year (roughly 365 days).  This often involves the addition of extra months periodically to keep the calendar aligned with the seasons. | The Chinese calendar is a lunisolar calendar.  中国的历法是阴阳历。 |  lunar, solar |  None (antonyms are implied by the contrasting nature of lunar and solar) | adj.
