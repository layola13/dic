idealists:/aɪˈdiːəlɪsts/| n.|理想主义者|People who are guided more by ideals than by practical considerations.  They are often characterized by their strong beliefs in principles and their pursuit of perfection, sometimes at the expense of practicality. |Example: Many young idealists joined the peace movement, hoping to change the world. 许多年轻的理想主义者加入了和平运动，希望改变世界。|近义词：visionaries, dreamers, utopians|反义词：realists, pragmatists|名词
