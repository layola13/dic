nonpatently:/ˌnɒnˈpeɪtəntli/ | adj. |非专利地，非明显地 |Not obviously or readily apparent; not protected by a patent.  This word describes something that is not clearly evident or obvious, and lacks the protection of a patent. |例句：The innovation, while significant, was nonpatently obvious to the average consumer. = 虽然这项创新意义重大，但对于普通消费者来说并不明显。

The improvement was nonpatently obvious, and thus not patentable. = 改进并不明显，因此不可申请专利。

近义词：unobviously, subtly, implicitly, obscurely |反义词：patently, obviously, clearly, explicitly
