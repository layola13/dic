singe:/sɪndʒ/| vt. n.| 烧焦；烤焦；轻微烧灼 | To burn slightly; scorch.  |例句：I accidentally singed my eyebrows while cooking.  我做饭时不小心把眉毛烧焦了。
The fire singed the edges of the curtains. 火烧焦了窗帘的边缘。|近义词：scorch, burn, char |反义词：quench, extinguish
