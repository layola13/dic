dustbins:/ˈdʌstbɪnz/ | n. |垃圾桶，垃圾箱 |A dustbin is a container, typically made of metal or plastic, used for the disposal of household waste.  They are commonly found outdoors in residential areas.|例句：The dustbins overflowed with rubbish after the party. 派对后垃圾桶溢满了垃圾。

例句：We need to take the dustbins out to the curb on Tuesday. 我们需要在星期二把垃圾桶拿到路边。


近义词：trash cans, garbage cans, refuse bins |反义词：无

词源:
The word "dustbin" is a compound word.  "Dust" refers to the fine particles of dirt and debris commonly found in household waste. "Bin" is a shortened form of "basket,"  a container for storage or transportation.  The combination suggests a container for dust and other waste materials. The word evolved over time, reflecting changes in waste disposal practices.
