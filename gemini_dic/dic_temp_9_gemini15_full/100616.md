intimidated: /ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 害怕的；受恐吓的；胆怯的 | feeling fear or lacking confidence because of threats. |  例句：She felt intimidated by his aggressive manner. 她被他咄咄逼人的态度吓住了。 | 例句：The large dog intimidated the small child. 大狗吓到了那个小孩。| 近义词：frightened, scared, daunted, cowed | 反义词：confident, unafraid, bold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He feels intimidated by the task. 他觉得这项任务让他害怕。
The loud noises intimidate the animals. 嘈杂的声音吓唬动物们。


一般过去时:
I was intimidated by her powerful presence. 我被她强大的气场吓到了。
The bully intimidated the smaller children. 恶霸恐吓那些更小的孩子。


现在完成时:
I have been intimidated by public speaking my whole life. 我一生都被公开演讲吓倒了。
She has never been intimidated by challenges. 她从未被挑战吓倒过。


过去完成时:
He had been intimidated by the previous speaker, so he spoke quietly. 他被之前的发言者吓到了，所以他小声地说话。
The children had been intimidated into silence. 孩子们被吓成了哑巴。


将来时:
I will be intimidated if I have to speak in front of a large crowd. 如果我必须在很多人面前讲话，我会感到害怕。
The new regulations will intimidate small businesses. 新规章将吓唬小型企业。


虚拟语气:
If I weren't intimidated, I would apply for the job. 如果我不害怕，我会申请这份工作。
I wish I weren't intimidated by my boss. 我希望我不害怕我的老板。


被动语态:
He was intimidated into signing the contract. 他被迫签订了合同。(被恐吓)
The witnesses were intimidated into silence. 证人们被恐吓得不敢说话。(被恐吓)


疑问句:
Are you intimidated by the exam? 你害怕考试吗？
Was she intimidated by the difficult problem? 她被这个难题吓倒了吗？


否定句:
He was not intimidated by the criticism. 他没有被批评吓倒。
She is not intimidated by his threats. 她不怕他的威胁。


PHRASE->
feel intimidated 感到害怕
be intimidated by 被…吓倒
become intimidated 变得害怕
seem intimidated 看起来害怕
intimidated silence 害怕的沉默
