chatterer:/ˈtʃætərər/| n. |喋喋不休的人；啁啾的鸟|A chatterer is a person who talks excessively and rapidly, often about unimportant things. It can also refer to a bird known for its chattering sounds.|The old woman was a notorious chatterer, always gossiping with her neighbors.|近义词：gossipmonger, blabbermouth, babbler|反义词：silent person, quiet person
