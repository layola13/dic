enactive:/ˈɛnæktɪv/| adj. |活泼的；生效的；起作用的 |Relating to or denoting a theory of cognition that emphasizes the role of action and experience in shaping perception and understanding.  It suggests that our understanding of the world is not passively received but actively constructed through our interactions with it. |The enactive approach to cognition highlights the importance of embodiment and situatedness in understanding how we know the world.  |近义词：active, dynamic, experiential |反义词：passive, static
