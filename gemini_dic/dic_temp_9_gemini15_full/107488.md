lanceolar:/lænˈsiːələr/| adj.|披针形的；狭长的|Shaped like a lance or spearhead; tapering to a point from a broad base.  The term is often used to describe the shape of leaves, petals, or other plant parts.|例句：The leaves of the willow tree are lanceolate. 柳树的叶子是披针形的。

例句：The petals of the iris flower have a distinct lanceolate shape.鸢尾花的花瓣具有明显的披针形。


近义词：lanceolate, acuminate, oblong


反义词：orbicular, rotund, ovate


词性：形容词
