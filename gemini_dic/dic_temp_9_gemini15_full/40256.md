continents:/ˈkɒntɪnənts/ /ˈkɑːntɪnənts/ | n. |大陆，洲 |Continents are large landmasses separated by oceans. 大陆是由海洋分隔开的广大陆地。|近义词：landmasses, mainlands |反义词：islands, oceans


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The seven continents are Asia, Africa, North America, South America, Antarctica, Europe, and Australia. = 七大洲是亚洲、非洲、北美洲、南美洲、南极洲、欧洲和大洋洲。
This map shows the location of all the continents. = 这张地图显示了所有大陆的位置。


一般过去时:
Scientists believed that the continents were once joined together. = 科学家们相信大陆曾经连在一起。
People used to think that the continents were fixed in place. = 人们过去认为大陆是固定不动的。


现在完成时:
Scientists have discovered much about the movement of continents. = 科学家们已经发现了许多关于大陆移动的知识。
Plate tectonics has explained how continents have drifted apart. = 板块构造学解释了大陆是如何漂移分开的。


过去完成时:
Before the continents separated, there was one large landmass. = 在大陆分离之前，存在着一块巨大的陆地。
Geologists had long suspected that the continents were once connected. = 地质学家们长期以来一直怀疑大陆曾经是相连的。


将来时:
Will the continents continue to drift apart? = 大陆还会继续漂移吗？
Scientists predict that the continents will look different in the future. = 科学家预测未来大陆的面貌将会不同。


虚拟语气:
If the continents were not drifting, the Earth would look very different. = 如果大陆不漂移，地球的面貌将会大相径庭。

疑问句:
