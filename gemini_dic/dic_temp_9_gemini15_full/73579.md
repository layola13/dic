foresuffer:/fɔːrˈsʌfər/| vt.|预先受苦；预先经受|To experience something unpleasant or difficult before others do.  It implies anticipation of hardship and often suggests a sense of sacrifice or bearing the brunt of something for the benefit of others.|例句：The pioneers foresuffered many hardships to establish a new settlement.  拓荒者们为了建立新的定居点而预先经受了许多苦难。

例句：Soldiers often foresuffer the consequences of war before civilians feel the full impact.  士兵们往往在平民感受到战争的全部影响之前就预先承受了战争的后果。

近义词：pre-experience, anticipate, undergo, endure

反义词：avoid, evade, escape
