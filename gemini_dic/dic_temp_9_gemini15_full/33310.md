chuckleheaded:/ˈtʃʌkəlˌhedɪd/| adj.|傻的，愚笨的，笨头笨脑的|Foolish; lacking intelligence or good judgment.  The word implies a somewhat amusing or endearing foolishness, rather than outright stupidity.|例句：His chuckleheaded plan to solve the problem only made things worse.  他的愚蠢计划不仅没有解决问题，反而使情况更加糟糕。

例句：She made a chuckleheaded mistake that cost the company a lot of money. 她犯了一个愚蠢的错误，给公司造成了巨大的经济损失。


近义词：silly, foolish, stupid, daft, imbecilic|反义词：clever, intelligent, wise, shrewd, brilliant|词性：形容词
