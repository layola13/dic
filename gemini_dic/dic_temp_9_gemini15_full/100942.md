inundated:/ˈɪnʌndeɪtɪd/| adj. v.| 淹没的；泛滥的；充满的 | overwhelmed by a large amount of something | The city was inundated by the flood. 城市被洪水淹没了。|近义词：flooded, overwhelmed, deluged | 反义词：drained, depleted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The inbox is inundated with spam emails. = 收件箱里充斥着垃圾邮件。
The small town is inundated with tourists every summer. = 每年夏天，这个小镇都会被游客淹没。


一般过去时:
The coastal areas were inundated by the rising tide. = 沿海地区被涨潮淹没了。
The office was inundated with applications for the job. = 办公室收到了大量的求职申请。


现在完成时:
The hospital has been inundated with patients since the accident. = 事故发生后，医院收治了大量的病人。
My social media feed has been inundated with political ads. = 我的社交媒体信息流充斥着政治广告。


过去完成时:
By the time the rescue team arrived, the village had been inundated by the river. = 当救援队到达时，村庄已经被河水淹没了。
Before the launch, the company had been inundated with pre-orders. = 产品发布前，公司已经收到了大量的预订单。


将来时:
If the dam breaks, the valley will be inundated. = 如果大坝决堤，山谷将被淹没。
We expect the customer service line to be inundated with calls after the advertisement airs. = 我们预计广告播出后，客服热线将会接到大量的电话。


虚拟语气:
If the river rose any higher, the whole town would be inundated. = 如果河水再涨高一些，整个城镇都会被淹没。
If I had known the response would be so overwhelming, I would have prepared better; I was inundated with requests. = 如果我知道反响会如此热烈，我会做好更好的准备；我收到了大量的请求。


被动语态:
The streets were inundated with flood water. = 街道被洪水淹没了。
The server was inundated with requests, causing it to crash. = 服务器被大量的请求淹没，导致其崩溃。


疑问句:
Is the city still inundated by the flood? = 城市是否仍然被洪水淹没？
Was the help desk inundated with calls during the system outage? = 系统中断期间，技术支持热线是否接到大量的电话？


否定句:
The streets were not inundated, only slightly wet. = 街道并没有被淹没，只是稍微湿润了一些。
The website wasn't inundated with traffic despite the marketing campaign. = 尽管进行了营销活动，网站的访问量并没有激增。
