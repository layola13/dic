making:/ˈmeɪkɪŋ/ | n. | 制作；制造；行为；过程 | The act of making something.  It can refer to the process, the creation, or the result. | Example: The making of the film took two years. (这部电影的制作花了两年时间。)  The making of bread is a complex process. (面包制作是一个复杂的过程。) | creation, production, manufacture | destruction, breaking | noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The making of this cake is easy. = 这个蛋糕很容易做。
She is making a beautiful dress. = 她正在做一件漂亮的裙子。

一般过去时:
They were making a lot of noise last night. = 他们昨晚制造了很多噪音。
He made a mistake. = 他犯了一个错误。

现在完成时:
I have been making progress in my studies. = 我的学习取得了进步。
She has made several attempts to contact him. = 她已经尝试几次联系他了。

过去完成时:
They had been making plans for their wedding for months. = 几个月来他们一直在为婚礼做计划。
Before the accident, he had been making good progress. = 事故发生前，他一直在取得良好的进展。

将来时:
We will be making a decision soon. = 我们很快就会做出决定。
They will be making a presentation tomorrow. = 他们明天将做一个演示。

虚拟语气:
If I were making the decision, I would choose differently. = 如果由我来做决定，我会做出不同的选择。
I wish I were making more money. = 我希望我赚更多的钱。

被动语态:
The decision was being made by the committee. = 这个决定是由委员会做出的。
The cake is being made by my mother. = 蛋糕是我妈妈做的。

疑问句:
What are you making? = 你在做什么？
Is she making any progress? = 她有取得任何进展吗？

否定句:
I am not making any excuses. = 我没有任何借口。
They were not making sense. = 他们说的没有道理。


PHRASE->
in the making:  正在形成中；制作中
the making of: ……的形成；……的制作
home making: 家务；持家
sense making: 意义建构；理解意义
decision making: 决策；做决定
