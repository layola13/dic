nonsymmetrical: /ˌnɒn.sɪˈmɛtrɪkəl/| adj. |不对称的，非对称的 |Not symmetrical; lacking symmetry.  Describes something that does not have symmetry, meaning its two halves do not mirror each other.  |例句：The building's design is nonsymmetrical, with one wing significantly larger than the other. 这座建筑的设计是不对称的，其中一个翼比另一个翼大得多。

例句：The artist deliberately created a nonsymmetrical composition to add visual interest.艺术家故意创造了一个不对称的构图来增加视觉趣味。


近义词：asymmetrical, unbalanced, uneven, irregular

反义词：symmetrical, balanced
