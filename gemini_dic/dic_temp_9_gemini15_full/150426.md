panspermia:/pænˈspɜːrmiə/| n. |泛种论，宇宙胚种论 |The hypothesis that life exists throughout the Universe, distributed by meteoroids, asteroids, and comets, and that life on Earth originated in this way.  It suggests that the "seeds" of life exist all over the universe, and that these seeds can be transported from one planet to another through space.  |Example: The theory of panspermia proposes that life on Earth may have originated from extraterrestrial sources. 泛种论认为地球上的生命可能起源于地外来源。|近义词：exogenesis,cosmogenesis|反义词：autogenesis
