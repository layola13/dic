divvying:/ˈdɪviɪŋ/| v.|分配，分摊|To divide something, especially into shares or portions.  |例句：They were busy divvying up the profits. 他们正忙着分配利润。

例句：The inheritance was divvied among the three siblings.  遗产被三个兄弟姐妹分掉了。


近义词：distributing, apportioning, allotting, sharing, allocating

反义词：uniting, combining, consolidating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are divvying up the land. = 他们正在分配土地。
We divvy the work equally. = 我们平均分配工作。


一般过去时:
They divvied up the spoils of war. = 他们瓜分了战争的赃物。
She divvied the cake among the children. = 她把蛋糕分给了孩子们。


现在完成时:
We have divvied up the responsibilities. = 我们已经分担了责任。
They have divvied the profits fairly. = 他们已经公平地分配了利润。


过去完成时:
They had divvied the inheritance before the will was read. = 遗嘱宣读之前，他们就已经分掉了遗产。
She had divvied the cookies before the party started. = 派对开始之前，她已经把饼干分好了。


将来时:

They will divvy the profits according to their investment. = 他们将根据投资比例分配利润。


虚拟语气:
If we divvied the workload fairly, we would finish earlier. = 如果我们公平地分配工作量，我们会早点完成。
I wish we had divvied the costs equally. = 我真希望我们能平均分担费用。


被动语态:

否定句:
