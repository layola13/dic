post-Roman:/ˌpoʊstˈroʊmən/| adj.|后罗马的，罗马帝国灭亡后的|relating to the period after the fall of the Western Roman Empire in 476 AD.  This era is characterized by significant political, social, and economic changes across Europe.|Example: Post-Roman Britain saw the rise of numerous small kingdoms.  后罗马时期的不列颠见证了许多小王国的兴起。The post-Roman migration patterns significantly altered the demographics of Europe. 后罗马时期的迁移模式显著改变了欧洲的人口结构。|近义词：postclassical, late antique|反义词：Roman, pre-Roman
