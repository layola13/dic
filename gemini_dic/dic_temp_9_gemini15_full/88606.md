hive:/haɪv/| n.|蜂巢；蜂箱；一窝；人群；中心；聚集地|A hive is a structure where bees live and store honey.  It can also refer to a place of busy activity or a group of people working together.|例句：The bees were busy in their hive. 蜜蜂在蜂巢里忙碌着。|例句：The office was a hive of activity before the deadline. 截止日期前，办公室一片繁忙景象。|近义词：colony, nest,  den|反义词：solitude, isolation

USAGE->

一般现在时:
The bees live in a hive. = 蜜蜂住在蜂巢里。
The workers are busy in the hive of activity. = 工人们在忙碌的中心忙碌着。

一般过去时:
The bees built a new hive last spring. = 蜜蜂去年春天建造了一个新的蜂巢。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

The team had established a thriving hive of collaboration before the project launched. = 项目启动前，团队已经建立了一个蓬勃发展的合作中心。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

If we had more resources, we could create a more efficient hive of productivity. = 如果我们有更多的资源，我们可以创造一个更高效的生产中心。
