purses:/ˈpɜːrsɪz/| n. |钱包；手提包|A purse is a small bag, typically carried by women, used to hold money, cards, and other small personal items.  It can be made of various materials like leather, fabric, or plastic. |She bought a new leather purse.|近义词：handbag, wallet, pouch|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She carries a small purse. = 她带着一个小钱包。
He always keeps his wallet in his pants pocket, not in a purse. = 他总是把钱包放在裤兜里，而不是钱包里。
