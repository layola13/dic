drip-dry: /ˈdrɪpˌdraɪ/ | adj. | 免熨的；滴干的 |  Describes fabrics or garments that can be hung to dry after washing without needing ironing.  They dry without wrinkles. | Example:  This shirt is drip-dry, so you don't need to iron it. 这件衬衫是免熨的，所以你不需要熨烫它。


Example: I bought some drip-dry shirts for my vacation to save time. 我买了一些免熨衬衫去度假以节省时间。


近义词: wrinkle-free, wash-and-wear


反义词: needs ironing, crease-prone


词性: adjective
