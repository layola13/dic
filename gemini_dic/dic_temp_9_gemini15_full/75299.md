frontiersman:/ˈfrʌntɪərzmən/ | n. |拓荒者，边疆居民 | A frontiersman is a person who lives in or explores the frontier, typically a rugged and undeveloped area.  They are often associated with self-reliance, resourcefulness, and a strong sense of independence. | Example:  The frontiersman skillfully navigated the treacherous mountain passes.  这位拓荒者熟练地穿越了险峻的山路。  The frontiersman built his cabin from logs he felled himself. 这位拓荒者用他自己砍伐的原木建造了他的小屋。|近义词：pioneer, settler, pioneer, explorer | 反义词：city dweller, urbanite | 名词
