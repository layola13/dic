seamster:/ˈsiːmstər/| n.|缝纫工，裁缝|A seamster is a person, typically a man, who sews, especially one who makes or repairs clothes.  The term is less common now than "tailor," which is often used for men's clothing, and "seamstress," which is used for women. |He was a skilled seamster, known for his meticulous stitching.|近义词：tailor, sewer, seamstress|反义词：None.
