rebuying:/ˌriːˈbaɪɪŋ/| v.|再次购买，重复购买|The act of buying something again.  It implies a previous purchase of the same or a similar item.|例句：The high customer satisfaction rate encourages rebuying. 高客户满意度促进了二次购买。|例句：The company's loyalty program incentivizes rebuying. 该公司的忠诚度计划激励了重复购买。|近义词：repurchasing, repeat buying|反义词：selling, disposing of


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are rebuying the same brand of coffee. = 他们正在再次购买同一品牌的咖啡。
Customers are rebuying this popular product. = 客户正在重复购买这款畅销产品。


一般过去时:
He re-bought the book after losing the first one. = 他丢失了第一本后又重新买了这本书。
She re-bought the dress in a different color. = 她又买了一件不同颜色的同款连衣裙。


现在完成时:
We have re-bought that software several times. = 我们已经多次重新购买了该软件。
They have re-bought the same car model. = 他们已经再次购买了同一款汽车。


过去完成时:
I had re-bought the tickets before realizing they were already refunded. = 我在意识到票款已经退还之前已经重新购买了门票。
She had re-bought the ingredients after realizing she was missing some. = 她意识到缺少一些配料后又重新购买了配料。


将来时:
I will rebuy the printer ink next week. = 我下周将再次购买打印机墨盒。
They will rebuy the same furniture for their new house. = 他们将为新家再次购买同样的家具。


虚拟语气:
If I had enough money, I would rebuy that antique vase. = 如果我有足够的钱，我会重新购买那个古董花瓶。
If I were you, I wouldn't rebuy that faulty product. = 如果我是你，我不会再次购买那个有缺陷的产品。


被动语态:
The product is frequently re-bought by satisfied customers. = 该产品经常被满意的客户再次购买。
The software was re-bought by the company after the initial license expired. = 该软件在初始许可证到期后被公司再次购买。


疑问句:
Will you be re-buying the same laptop? = 你会再次购买同一款笔记本电脑吗？
Are they planning on re-buying their old house? = 他们计划再次购买他们的老房子吗？


否定句:
I won't rebuy that broken appliance. = 我不会再次购买那个坏掉的电器。
He didn't rebuy the tickets, even though the concert was sold out. = 尽管音乐会门票售罄，他也没有再次购买门票。
