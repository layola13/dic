pretenders:/prɪˈtɛndərz/ | n. |冒名顶替者； претенденты |People who falsely claim to be someone or something they are not; those who make a false claim to a title, position, or role.  |例句：The pretenders to the throne fought a bloody civil war.  王位觊觎者之间爆发了一场血腥的内战。

例句：Many pretenders emerged after the king's death, each claiming to be the rightful heir. 国王死后出现了许多冒名顶替者，每个人都声称自己是合法的继承人。


近义词：imposters, deceivers, frauds, charlatans, claimants


反义词：legitimate successors, rightful heirs, authentic individuals


词性：名词
