overimported:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈpɔːrtɪd/| adj. | 过度进口的，进口过量的 | Excessively imported;  more imported than is needed or desirable.  It implies a negative consequence, often leading to market saturation or harm to domestic producers. |例句：The overimported textiles flooded the market, driving down prices for domestic manufacturers.  过度进口的纺织品充斥市场，压低了国内制造商的价格。

The current trade deficit is largely due to overimported consumer goods.  目前的贸易逆差很大程度上是由于消费品进口过量造成的。|近义词：excessively imported, surplus imported, flooded market |反义词：underimported, domestically produced
