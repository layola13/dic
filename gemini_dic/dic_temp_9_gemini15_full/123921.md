morendo: /mɔˈrɛndoʊ/ | adv. |渐弱地；逐渐消失地|

英文解析：Morendo is an Italian adverb used in musical notation. It instructs the performer to gradually decrease the volume and intensity of the sound until it fades away completely.  It implies a slow and gentle diminuendo.

例句：

Play the final phrase morendo. = 将最后的乐句渐弱演奏。

The melody faded morendo into silence. =  旋律逐渐消失，归于寂静。


近义词： diminuendo, decrescendo, dying away

反义词： crescendo, fortissimo


词性: 副词
