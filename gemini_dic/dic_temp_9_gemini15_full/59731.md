eco:/ˈekoʊ/| adj. n.|生态的；生态系统；经济的 | Relating to the environment; concerning the relationship between living organisms and their surroundings.  Also can refer to economical aspects, especially in terms of cost-effectiveness. |例句：Eco-friendly products are becoming increasingly popular.  环保产品越来越受欢迎。
例句：The eco-system of the Amazon rainforest is incredibly diverse. 亚马逊雨林的生态系统极其多样化。|近义词：environmental, ecological, economical |反义词：unecological, uneconomical |形容词，名词
