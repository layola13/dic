reelecting: /ˌriːɪˈlɛktɪŋ/ | v. |  重新选举 | The act of electing someone to the same office again.  This word is the present participle of the verb "reelect." |


例句：

The citizens are currently re-electing their mayor. = 市民们目前正在重新选举他们的市长。

The party is focused on re-electing the president. = 该党正专注于让总统再次当选。


近义词：re-choosing, re-appointing


反义词：un-electing, dismissing


词性：动词
