dictatorships:/ˈdɪkteɪtərʃɪps/| n.|独裁统治，专政|The rule of a dictator or a group of dictators.  It often involves the suppression of opposition and the restriction of individual freedoms.  |例句：Many countries have suffered under brutal dictatorships. 许多国家都遭受过残酷的独裁统治。|例句：The fall of the dictatorships marked a new era of democracy. 独裁统治的垮台标志着民主新时代的到来。|近义词：autocracies, tyrannies, totalitarian regimes|反义词：democracies, republics
