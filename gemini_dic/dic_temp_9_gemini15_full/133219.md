nonexcessively:/ˌnɒnɪkˈsesɪvli/ | adv. |不过度地，适度地 |Not excessively; moderately.  The word combines the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the adverb "excessively" (meaning "to an excessive degree"). It indicates a level of something that is within reasonable limits and avoids extremes.|例句：He exercised nonexcessively, aiming for consistent fitness rather than extreme gains. 他适度锻炼，目标是保持持续的健康而不是追求极端的成果。

He ate nonexcessively, avoiding overindulgence. 他饮食适度，避免暴饮暴食。|近义词：moderately, reasonably, temperately |反义词：excessively, immoderately, excessively
