reacquaints:/ˌriːəˈkweɪnts/| vt.|使重新熟悉；使重温旧识|To make someone familiar again with something; to renew acquaintance with someone.|例句：The reunion weekend reacquainted me with many old friends.  周末的聚会让我重新认识了许多老朋友。
例句：The trip reacquainted her with the beauty of the countryside. 这趟旅行让她重温了乡村的美景。|近义词：refamiliarize, reaccustom|反义词：estrange, alienate|动词
