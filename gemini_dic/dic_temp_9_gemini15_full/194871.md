semi-crystalline:/ˌsɛmiˈkrɪstəlɪn/| adj. |半结晶的|Possessing a structure that is partly crystalline and partly amorphous.  Semi-crystalline materials exhibit properties intermediate between fully crystalline and fully amorphous materials, often showing increased toughness and flexibility compared to fully crystalline materials, but less than fully amorphous ones.  They are commonly found in polymers. |例句：Many plastics, like polyethylene, are semi-crystalline polymers. 许多塑料，例如聚乙烯，都是半结晶聚合物。

例句：The semi-crystalline structure of the material contributes to its strength and flexibility.  该材料的半结晶结构有助于其强度和柔韧性。


近义词：partially crystalline| 反义词：fully crystalline, fully amorphous


词源:
* **semi-**:  From Latin *semi*, meaning "half," "partly," or "partially."
* **crystalline**: From  Latin *crystallinus*,  from *crystallus*, meaning "ice," "crystal."  This ultimately derives from Greek *krustallos*, meaning "ice," "crystal."


USAGE->
(由于semi-crystalline是一个形容词，它不以独立的动词形式出现，因此无法按照动词的时态进行例句分析。以下列举不同句式中的用法。)

肯定句:
The material is semi-crystalline.  =  这种材料是半结晶的。
This polymer exhibits a semi-crystalline structure. =  这种聚合物呈现半结晶结构。


否定句:
The material is not fully crystalline; it's semi-crystalline. =  这种材料不是完全结晶的；它是半结晶的。
The sample is not amorphous; it has a semi-crystalline nature. = 样本不是无定形的；它具有半结晶的性质。


疑问句:
Is the polymer semi-crystalline or amorphous? = 这种聚合物是半结晶的还是无定形的？
Is this material's structure primarily semi-crystalline? =  这种材料的结构主要是否为半结晶？


比较句:
Semi-crystalline polymers are generally tougher than fully amorphous polymers. = 半结晶聚合物通常比完全无定形聚合物更坚韧。
The semi-crystalline structure offers better strength compared to a fully amorphous structure. = 与完全无定形结构相比，半结晶结构提供了更好的强度。
