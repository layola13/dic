parking:/ˈpɑːrkɪŋ/ | n. | 停车；停车场 | The act of parking a vehicle, or the area designated for parking.  Parking is often regulated and may require payment. |例句：I found a parking space right in front of the store. 我在商店前面找到一个停车位了。

例句：The parking lot was full, so I had to park on the street. 停车场满了，所以我不得不把车停在街上。


近义词：parking lot, car park  | 反义词：driving, departure


词源:  The word "parking" derives from the verb "park," which entered English in the early 20th century.  It's ultimately from the French word "parc," meaning "enclosure" or "park," referring to an enclosed area, which eventually took on the meaning of a place to leave vehicles.
