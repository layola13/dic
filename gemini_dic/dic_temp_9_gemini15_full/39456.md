connects:/kəˈnektz/| vt.|连接，联系|To join or link together.|The bridge connects the two islands. 这座桥连接着两个岛屿。|The two ideas connect in my mind. 这两个想法在我的脑海中联系在一起。|link, join, unite|separate, disconnect|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The road connects the city to the airport. 这条路连接城市和机场。
This wire connects the computer to the printer. 这根线将电脑连接到打印机。

一般过去时:
He connected the two wires together. 他把两根电线连接在一起。
She connected the dots to reveal the pattern. 她连接这些点以揭示图案。

现在完成时:
They have connected the new building to the water supply. 他们已经将新大楼连接到供水系统。
We have connected all the devices to the network. 我们已经将所有设备连接到网络。

过去完成时:
Before the presentation, he had connected his laptop to the projector. 演示之前，他已经将笔记本电脑连接到投影仪。
After the storm, they had connected the power lines. 暴风雨过后，他们已经连接了电力线。

将来时:
They will connect the new satellite to the existing network. 他们将把新的卫星连接到现有的网络。
The engineers will connect the two pipelines tomorrow. 工程师们明天将连接这两条管道。

虚拟语气:
If we connected the two systems, we could share data more easily. 如果我们将这两个系统连接起来，我们可以更容易地共享数据。
I wish I had connected with my old friend sooner. 我希望我早点与我的老朋友联系。

被动语态:
The two towns are connected by a railway line. 这两个城镇由一条铁路连接。
The system is being connected to the mainframe. 系统正在连接到主机。

疑问句:
Does this cable connect to the internet? 这根电缆连接到互联网吗？
How are these two events connected? 这两件事是如何联系起来的？

否定句:
The device doesn't connect to the Wi-Fi. 该设备无法连接到 Wi-Fi。
This road doesn't connect to the highway. 这条路没有连接到高速公路。
