lotoses: /ˈloʊtəʊsiːz/ | n. | 莲花（复数） | The plural form of "lotus," referring to multiple lotus flowers.  Lotus flowers are aquatic plants with large, showy flowers and broad leaves that float on the water. They are often associated with purity, beauty, and spirituality in various cultures. | Example: The pond was covered with beautiful pink and white lotoses.  池塘里覆盖着美丽粉红色和白色的莲花。|近义词: water lilies (广义) | 反义词: N/A
