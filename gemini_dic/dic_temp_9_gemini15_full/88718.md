hobgoblin:/ˈhɒbɡɒblɪn/| n.|小妖精，妖怪；梦魇；令人恐惧的事物|A hobgoblin is a small, mischievous sprite or goblin, often depicted as a frightening creature in folklore.  It can also refer to something that causes fear or anxiety. |例句：The old house was rumored to be haunted by hobgoblins.  这栋旧房子据说闹鬼。

The children's fear of the dark was a hobgoblin they had to overcome.孩子们对黑暗的恐惧是他们必须克服的一个梦魇。|近义词：goblin, sprite, bugbear, nightmare|反义词：comfort, reassurance
