hust:/hʌst/ | v. | 努力工作；匆忙行动；欺骗；强迫 | To work hard and quickly; to rush; to cheat; to force. |例句：He hustled to finish the report before the deadline. 他努力赶在截止日期前完成报告。|近义词：rush, scramble, bustle, swindle |反义词：dawdle, procrastinate, idle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hustles every day to make ends meet. = 他每天努力工作以维持生计。
They hustle the tourists into buying overpriced souvenirs. = 他们骗游客购买价格过高的纪念品。


一般过去时:
She hustled through the airport to catch her flight. = 她匆忙赶到机场赶飞机。
The con man hustled the old woman out of her savings. = 骗子骗走了老妇人的积蓄。


现在完成时:
He has hustled his way to the top of the company. = 他通过努力奋斗爬到了公司的高层。
They have been hustled by a dishonest salesman. = 他们被一个不诚实的推销员欺骗了。


过去完成时:
She had hustled all day and was completely exhausted. = 她忙活了一整天，完全筋疲力尽了。
They had been hustled out of their money before they realized it. = 他们在意识到之前就被骗走了钱。


将来时:
He will hustle to get the project done on time. = 他将努力按时完成项目。
They will hustle the customers into signing the contract. = 他们将催促顾客签署合同。


虚拟语气:
If he hustled more, he would achieve more. = 如果他更努力，他就会取得更大的成就。
I wish I had hustled harder in my studies. = 我希望我学习更努力。


被动语态:
He was hustled out of the building by security guards. = 他被保安赶出了大楼。
The customers were hustled into buying unnecessary products. = 客户被催促购买不必要的商品。


疑问句:
Does he hustle enough to achieve his goals? = 他是否足够努力以实现他的目标？
Were they hustled by the salespeople? = 他们被销售人员欺骗了吗？


否定句:
He doesn't hustle enough to succeed. = 他不够努力以至于不能成功。
They didn't hustle to meet the deadline. = 他们没有努力赶上截止日期。


PHRASE->
hustle and bustle: 熙熙攘攘
hustle up: 赶紧；迅速准备好
hustle someone out: 把某人赶出去
hustle for: 为…而努力
hustle to: 赶往; 急忙做
