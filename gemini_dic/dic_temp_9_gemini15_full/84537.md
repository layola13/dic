hamite:/ˈhæmaɪt/| n.|哈米特人；哈米特语|A member of any of various peoples of northeastern Africa speaking Hamitic languages.  The term is now considered outdated and potentially offensive due to its colonial origins and imprecise classification.|例句：The study examined the cultural practices of various Hamite groups. 这项研究考察了各个哈米特人群体的文化习俗。|近义词：Afro-Asiatic speaker (more accurate and less offensive)|反义词：无|名词
