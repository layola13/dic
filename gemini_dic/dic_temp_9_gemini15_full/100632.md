into:/ˈɪntuː/ | prep. |进入；到…里面；变成；成为；深入；反对 |进入，到…里面，变成，成为，深入，反对；  |例句：He walked into the room. 他走进房间。|例句：The project developed into a major success. 这个项目发展成一个巨大的成功。|近义词：in, inside, toward, against |反义词：out of, from, away from |介词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird flew into the tree. = 鸟飞进了树里。
She walks into the office every morning. = 她每天早上走进办公室。


一般过去时:
He ran into the burning building. = 他跑进了燃烧的建筑物。
They looked into the matter carefully. = 他们仔细调查了此事。


现在完成时:
I have looked into the problem, but I haven't found a solution yet. = 我已经调查过这个问题了，但我还没找到解决办法。
She has moved into a new apartment. = 她搬进了一套新公寓。


过去完成时:
Before I realized it, I had walked into a trap. = 在我意识到之前，我已经落入了陷阱。
They had already gone into the meeting when I arrived. = 我到达时，他们已经开会了。


将来时:
We will move into our new house next month. = 我们下个月将搬进我们的新房子。
He will dive into the pool. = 他将跳入游泳池。


虚拟语气:
If I were to go into that business, I would need a lot of capital. = 如果我要进入那项业务，我需要大量资金。
I wish I could go into more detail. = 我希望我能更详细地说明。


被动语态:
The ball was thrown into the air. = 球被扔到了空中。
The matter is being looked into by the police. = 警方正在调查此事。


疑问句:
What did you mean by "into the night"? = 你说“进入夜晚”是什么意思？
Did you look into the issue? = 你调查过这个问题吗？


否定句:
The plan didn't go into effect. = 计划没有生效。
He didn't go into details. = 他没有详细说明。


PHRASE->
into account: 考虑到
into being: 形成，产生
into effect: 生效
into pieces: 碎片
into the bargain: 此外，另外
