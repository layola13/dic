elodian:/ɪˈləʊdiən/| n. |水蕴草|An aquatic plant of the genus *Elodea*, often used in aquariums.  Elodea is a genus of aquatic plants commonly known as waterweeds. They are submerged, freshwater plants with long, slender leaves. They are native to the Americas but have become widely distributed as invasive species in other parts of the world.  |Example: The aquarium was beautifully landscaped with various aquatic plants, including elodian.  水族箱里种植着各种水生植物，包括水蕴草，景色优美。|近义词：waterweed, anacharis |反义词：None directly applicable.
