maddest: /ˈmædəst/ | adj. | 最疯狂的，最生气（或恼怒）的 |  The superlative form of "mad," indicating the highest degree of madness or anger.  It can refer to someone exhibiting extreme insanity or someone experiencing extreme anger. |

例句：
He was the maddest man I'd ever met. = 他是我见过的最疯狂的人。

She was maddest at herself for making such a stupid mistake. = 她对自己犯了如此愚蠢的错误而感到最生气。


近义词：most furious, most enraged, most insane, most deranged


反义词：calmest, most composed, most rational


词性:形容词
