islamist:/ˈɪzlɑːmɪst/| n. adj.|伊斯兰主义者；伊斯兰主义的|A person who advocates or supports the political system of Islamism.  An Islamist may believe that Islamic law should govern society, and they may advocate for the implementation of Sharia law. The term can be used broadly to describe individuals and groups with varying degrees of religious and political views.|例句：Some Islamist groups are actively engaged in political processes. 一些伊斯兰主义团体积极参与政治进程。

例句：He was accused of being an Islamist extremist. 他被指控是伊斯兰极端主义者。|近义词：Islamist fundamentalist, Islamic activist|反义词：secularist, non-religious
