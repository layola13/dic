eagle:/ˈiːɡəl/| n. |鹰|A large bird of prey with a heavy body and broad wings, noted for its keen eyesight and powerful talons. Eagles are known for their soaring flight and hunting prowess. They are often considered symbols of power, freedom, and vision.|例句：The eagle soared high above the mountains.  鹰在群山之上翱翔。|例句：The American bald eagle is a national symbol. 美国白头鹰是国家的象征。|近义词：hawk, falcon, vulture|反义词：None specifically, but words like "weakling" or "timid creature" could be considered antonyms in terms of the qualities associated with eagles.
