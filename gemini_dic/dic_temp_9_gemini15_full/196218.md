sequestrum: /sɪˈkwɛstrəm/ | n. | 坏死组织，隔离物 | A piece of dead bone that has become separated from the surrounding living bone.  It is a complication of osteomyelitis (bone infection).| The sequestrum was surgically removed to prevent further infection.  坏死骨片被手术切除以防止进一步感染。| necrotic bone, dead bone | living bone |
