self-working: /ˌsɛlfˈwɜːrkɪŋ/ | adj. | 自动运转的；自动工作的 |  Describes something that operates or functions without requiring external intervention or manual operation.  It implies a degree of autonomy and self-sufficiency in its operation. | 例句：The self-working coffee machine brewed a perfect cup without any user input.  = 这台自动咖啡机无需任何用户输入就能冲泡出一杯完美的咖啡。

The self-working sprinkler system saved the garden during the drought. = 自动洒水系统在干旱期间拯救了花园。


近义词：automatic, automated, self-acting, self-regulating | 反义词：manual, hand-operated


词源:  The word is a compound of "self-" (meaning "by oneself" or "of oneself") and "working" (present participle of the verb "to work," meaning "to operate" or "to function").  Therefore, it literally means "working by itself."
