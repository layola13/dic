nondocumented:/ˌnɑːnˈdɒkjʊmɛntɪd/|/ˈnɑːnˌdɒkjuˌmɛntɪd/ | adj. | 未记录的；无证的；未记载的 | Not officially recorded or documented.  This term often refers to individuals who are in a country without the required legal documentation, such as a visa or green card.  It can also apply to software, code, or processes that lack accompanying documentation. | Example:  The undocumented immigrant sought asylum.  未记录的移民寻求政治庇护。

The undocumented software bug caused a system crash. 未记录的软件错误导致系统崩溃。|近义词：unrecorded, unregistered, unofficial, undocumented workers, undocumented immigrants |反义词：documented, recorded, official
