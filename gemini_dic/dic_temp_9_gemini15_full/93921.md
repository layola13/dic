immunise:/ˈɪmjunaɪz/| vt.|使免疫；预防接种|To protect someone or something against disease by giving them a vaccine.|例句：It's important to immunise children against diseases like measles.  重要的是要给孩子们接种麻疹等疾病的疫苗。|例句：The doctor immunised her against the flu. 医生给她注射了流感疫苗。|近义词：vaccinate, inoculate|反义词：infect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government immunises children against various diseases. = 政府为儿童接种各种疾病的疫苗。
Doctors immunise patients to protect them from illness. = 医生为病人接种疫苗以保护他们免受疾病侵害。

一般过去时:
They immunised the entire population against polio. = 他们为整个人口接种了小儿麻痹症疫苗。
My doctor immunised me against the flu last year. = 去年我的医生给我注射了流感疫苗。


现在完成时:
She has immunised her dog against rabies. = 她已经为她的狗接种了狂犬病疫苗。
Scientists have immunised many animals against various diseases. = 科学家已经为许多动物接种了各种疾病的疫苗。


过去完成时:
The nurses had immunised all the patients before the outbreak. = 在疫情爆发之前，护士们已经为所有病人接种了疫苗。
He had immunised his children before their trip abroad. = 他在孩子们出国旅行之前已经为他们接种了疫苗。


将来时:
They will immunise the new arrivals against typhoid. = 他们将为新来的人接种伤寒疫苗。
The clinic will immunise all the refugees against preventable diseases. = 诊所将为所有难民接种可预防的疾病疫苗。


虚拟语气:
If we had immunised the population earlier, the epidemic would have been less severe. = 如果我们早些为人口接种疫苗，疫情就不会那么严重了。
I wish I had immunised myself against the flu; now I am sick. = 我真希望我当时接种了流感疫苗；现在我病了。


被动语态:
The children were immunised against measles at school. = 孩子们在学校接种了麻疹疫苗。
Many people are immunised against various diseases every year. = 每年都有许多人接种各种疾病的疫苗。


疑问句:
Have you been immunised against tetanus? = 你接种过破伤风疫苗吗？
When will the children be immunised against chickenpox? = 孩子们什么时候接种水痘疫苗？


否定句:
He didn't immunise his child, and the child got sick. = 他没有为他的孩子接种疫苗，结果孩子生病了。
They didn't immunise their pets, and one of them caught a disease. = 他们没有为他们的宠物接种疫苗，其中一只宠物得了病。
