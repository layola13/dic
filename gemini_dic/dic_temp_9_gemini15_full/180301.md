refrigerative:/rɪˈfrɪdʒərətɪv/| adj.|制冷的；冷冻的|Relating to or causing refrigeration.  The word describes something that cools or is related to the process of cooling.|例句：The refrigerative system in the truck kept the food fresh during transport.  卡车上的制冷系统使食品在运输过程中保持新鲜。

例句：The refrigerative capacity of the air conditioner is impressive. 空调的制冷能力令人印象深刻。


近义词：cooling, chilling, freezing, refrigerating

反义词：heating, warming


词性：形容词
