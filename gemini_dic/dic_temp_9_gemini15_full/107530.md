landform:/ˈlændfɔːrm/ | n. |地形，地貌 |A landform is a natural feature of the Earth's surface.  Landforms include mountains, valleys, plains, plateaus, and hills. They are created by a variety of processes, including tectonic activity, erosion, and deposition. | Example: The Grand Canyon is a spectacular landform carved by the Colorado River.  大峡谷是由科罗拉多河雕刻而成的壮丽地形。  The rolling hills of Tuscany are a beautiful example of a gentle landform. 托斯卡纳的连绵起伏的丘陵是温和地形的美丽例证。|近义词：topographical feature, geographical feature |反义词：None (it's a broad category) |
