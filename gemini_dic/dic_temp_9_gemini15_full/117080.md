meadowlarks:/ˈmɛdoʊlɑːrkz/| n. |草地鹨（复数）|A type of songbird found in North America, known for its melodious song.  They are typically brown and buff-colored, with a distinctive black V-shaped marking on their breast.|例句：The meadowlarks were singing sweetly in the field.  草地鹨在田野里婉转地歌唱。|例句：We saw several meadowlarks flitting through the tall grass. 我们看到几只草地鹨在高高的草丛中飞舞。|近义词：songbird, lark (general term)|反义词：无|名词
