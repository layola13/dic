deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbərət/| adj. v.|故意的；深思熟虑的；从容的| done consciously and intentionally; characterized by careful consideration; slow and steady|例句：He made a deliberate attempt to ruin her reputation. 他故意试图毁坏她的名誉。
例句：The committee deliberated for hours before reaching a decision. 委员会经过数小时的仔细讨论才做出决定。|近义词：intentional, purposeful, premeditated|反义词：accidental, unintentional, impulsive|词性：adj. v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He deliberates carefully before making a decision. 他在做决定之前仔细考虑。
They deliberate on the matter every week. 他们每周都仔细讨论这件事。

一般过去时:
She deliberated for a long time before answering.  她在回答之前考虑了很久。
The jury deliberated for several days before reaching a verdict. 陪审团经过几天的讨论才达成裁决。

现在完成时:
He has deliberated on this problem for days. 他已经考虑这个问题好几天了。
They have deliberated the issue thoroughly. 他们已经彻底讨论了这个问题。

过去完成时:
She had deliberated for hours before she finally made up her mind. 她经过几个小时的深思熟虑才最终下定决心。
They had deliberated the matter extensively before the meeting. 会议前他们已经广泛地讨论了这个问题。

将来时:
He will deliberate on the proposal tomorrow. 他明天将考虑这个提案。
They will deliberate the matter further next week. 他们下周将进一步讨论此事。

虚拟语气:
If he had deliberated more carefully, he wouldn't have made such a mistake. 如果他考虑得更仔细一些，就不会犯这样的错误了。
I wish I had deliberated more before speaking. 我希望我在说话之前考虑得更多一些。

被动语态:
The matter was deliberated upon by the committee. 委员会对这个问题进行了讨论。
The proposal will be deliberated at the next meeting.  该提案将在下次会议上进行讨论。

疑问句:
Did they deliberate before voting? 他们在投票前是否进行了讨论？
Have you deliberated the implications of your decision? 你是否仔细考虑了你决定的影响？

否定句:
He did not deliberate his actions. 他没有深思熟虑自己的行为。
She didn't deliberate long enough before signing the contract. 她在签合同之前考虑的时间不够长。
