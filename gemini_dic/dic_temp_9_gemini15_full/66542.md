exhausts:/ɪɡˈzɔːsts/| v.|使筋疲力尽；排气；耗尽|to tire someone out completely; to use something up completely; to emit gas or steam.|例句：The long journey exhausted me. 长途旅行把我累坏了。|例句：The car exhausts harmful fumes. 汽车排出有害烟雾。|近义词：tire, deplete, drain, wear out|反义词：energize, revitalize, replenish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hard work exhausts him. = 繁重的工作使他筋疲力尽。
The engine exhausts fumes into the atmosphere. = 发动机向大气中排放废气。

一般过去时:
The marathon exhausted the runners. = 马拉松比赛使跑步者筋疲力尽。
The factory exhausted its supply of raw materials. = 工厂耗尽了原材料供应。


现在完成时:
The long hike has exhausted us. = 长途徒步旅行让我们筋疲力尽了。
They have exhausted all their options. = 他们已经用尽了所有办法。


过去完成时:
He had exhausted himself before reaching the summit. = 他到达山顶之前就已经筋疲力尽了。
The company had exhausted its funds by the end of the year. = 到年底公司已经用光了资金。


将来时:
This project will exhaust us if we don't get more help. = 如果我们得不到更多帮助，这个项目会让我们精疲力尽。
We will have exhausted our resources by next month. = 到下个月我们将耗尽我们的资源。


虚拟语气:
If I had known it would exhaust me so much, I wouldn't have done it. = 如果我知道这会让我如此疲惫，我就不会做了。
If they had not exhausted their savings, they could have bought a house. = 如果他们没有花光积蓄，他们就能买房子了。


被动语态:
He was exhausted by the strenuous exercise. = 他被剧烈的运动弄得筋疲力尽。
The resources were exhausted by the prolonged drought. = 资源被长时间的干旱耗尽了。


疑问句:
Does this work exhaust you? = 这项工作让你筋疲力尽吗？
Has the project exhausted the budget? = 这个项目是否耗尽了预算？


否定句:
The journey didn't exhaust me as much as I expected. = 旅程并没有像我预期的那样让我筋疲力尽。
The company hasn't exhausted all its options yet. = 公司还没有用尽所有办法。
