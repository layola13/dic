cooperates:/koʊˈɒpəreɪts/ | v. |合作，协同工作 |To work together for a common purpose or goal.  It implies mutual effort and agreement. |The team cooperates well to achieve their objectives.  团队通力合作以实现其目标。| collaborates, works together | competes, opposes | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cooperate on most projects. = 他们在大多数项目上都合作。
The two companies cooperate to develop new products. = 这两家公司合作开发新产品。


一般过去时:
The countries cooperated to resolve the conflict. = 各国合作解决冲突。
We cooperated on the science fair project. = 我们在科学展览会上合作了一个项目。


现在完成时:
They have cooperated on several successful ventures. = 他们已经合作完成了几项成功的项目。
The teams have cooperated effectively to achieve the target. = 各团队有效地合作以实现目标。


过去完成时:
They had cooperated before on similar projects. = 他们之前在类似的项目上合作过。
The artists had cooperated extensively before the exhibition. = 在展览之前，艺术家们进行了广泛的合作。


将来时:
We will cooperate fully with the investigation. = 我们将全力配合调查。
The two nations will cooperate to address climate change. = 这两个国家将合作应对气候变化。


虚拟语气:
If they cooperated, the project would succeed. = 如果他们合作，项目就会成功。
I wish they cooperated more effectively. = 我希望他们合作更有效率。


被动语态:
The plan was cooperated on by many different departments. = 该计划得到了许多不同部门的合作。
The project is being cooperated on by a team of experts. = 该项目正在由一个专家团队合作进行。


疑问句:
Do the teams cooperate well? = 团队合作得好吗？
Will they cooperate with our requests? = 他们会配合我们的要求吗？


否定句:
They did not cooperate with the authorities. = 他们没有与当局合作。
The two sides refused to cooperate on the issue. = 双方拒绝就此事进行合作。


PHRASE->
cooperate with = 与…合作
cooperate on = 就…合作
fully cooperate = 全力合作
refuse to cooperate = 拒绝合作
actively cooperate = 主动合作
