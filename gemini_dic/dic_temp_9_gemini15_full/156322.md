physicalness:/ˈfɪzɪkəlnəs/| n. |物质性；物理性；肉体性 |The quality or state of being physical; the tangible or material aspect of something.  It can also refer to the degree to which something is physical, or the emphasis placed on physical attributes or actions. |例句：The play lacked physicalness; the fight scenes were unconvincing.  这部戏缺乏真实感；打斗场面令人难以信服。  The physicalness of the training regime left her exhausted.  高强度的体能训练使她筋疲力尽。|近义词：materiality, corporeality, tangibility |反义词：immateriality, intangibility, spirituality
