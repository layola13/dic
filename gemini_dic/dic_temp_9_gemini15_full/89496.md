honeystone:/ˈhʌnistəʊn/ | n. |方解石;蜜石|A crystalline variety of calcite, often honey-yellow in color, found in cavities in rocks. It's sometimes used ornamentally. |例句：The honeystone samples displayed a range of hues, from pale yellow to deep amber.  方解石样品呈现出一系列色调，从浅黄色到深琥珀色。  |例句：The geologist identified the honey-colored crystal as a rare form of honeystone. 地质学家将这种蜂蜜色的晶体鉴定为一种罕见的方解石。|近义词：calcite,  crystalline calcite |反义词：无
