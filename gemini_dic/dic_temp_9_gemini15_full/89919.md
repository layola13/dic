horse-shoeing: /ˈhɔːrsʃuːɪŋ/ | n. | 钉马掌；马蹄铁工艺 | The craft of fitting horseshoes to a horse's hooves.  It involves shaping and fitting metal shoes to protect and support the hooves. | The blacksmith skillfully performed the horse-shoeing.  铁匠熟练地完成了钉马掌的工作。  The horse needed horse-shoeing after its long journey. 这匹马长途跋涉后需要钉马掌。 | farriery, horseshoeing | (no direct antonym, perhaps "hoof trimming" if considering the opposite action) | n.
