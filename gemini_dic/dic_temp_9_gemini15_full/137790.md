词源:
The word "nonvenomous" is a combination of the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the word "venomous" (meaning "poisonous"). "Venomous" itself derives from the Old French word "venimeux," ultimately originating from the Latin word "venenum," meaning "poison."
