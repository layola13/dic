pyjamas:/pəˈdʒɑːməz/| n. |睡衣|Plural of pyjama.  Loose-fitting pants and often a matching top, worn for sleeping or relaxing.  Pyjamas are typically made from comfortable materials like cotton or flannel.|例句：I changed into my pyjamas after my shower. 我洗完澡后换上了睡衣。
例句：She wore silk pyjamas to bed. 她穿着丝绸睡衣睡觉。|近义词：sleepwear, nightclothes|反义词：day clothes, outerwear
