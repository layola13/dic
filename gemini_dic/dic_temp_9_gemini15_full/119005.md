methodic:/məˈθɒdɪk/| adj.|有条理的；有系统的；循序渐进的 | characterized by method or order; systematic; orderly.  |例句：She approached the problem in a very methodical way. 她以非常有条理的方式处理这个问题。|例句：His methodical approach to research ensured accurate results. 他有条理的研究方法确保了准确的结果。|近义词：systematic, orderly, organized, disciplined |反义词：chaotic, haphazard, disorganized, random |
