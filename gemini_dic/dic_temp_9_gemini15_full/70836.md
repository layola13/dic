finished:/ˈfɪnɪʃt/| adj. v. | 完成的；结束的 |  Completed; brought to an end.  |例句：The project is finished.  项目完成了。

The race is finished, and the winner is announced. 比赛结束了，冠军也公布了。|近义词：completed, concluded, ended |反义词：unfinished, incomplete, ongoing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He finishes his work early every day. 他每天都早早完成工作。
She finishes her homework before dinner. 她晚饭前完成作业。


一般过去时:
I finished the book last night. 我昨晚读完了那本书。
They finished the race in record time. 他们以创纪录的时间完成了比赛。


现在完成时:
I have finished my assignment. 我已经完成了我的作业。
She has finished reading the novel. 她已经读完了那本小说。


过去完成时:
He had finished his lunch before the meeting started. 会议开始前他已经吃过午饭了。
We had finished packing before the taxi arrived. 出租车到达之前，我们已经打包好了。

I will finish the report by tomorrow. 我明天之前会完成报告。

虚拟语气:
If I had finished my work, I would have gone to the party. 如果我完成了工作，我就去参加派对了。
I wish I had finished my studies earlier. 我真希望我早点完成学业。


被动语态:
The task was finished on time. 任务按时完成了。
The painting is being finished by a famous artist. 这幅画正在由一位著名艺术家完成。


疑问句:

否定句:

词源:
The word "finished" comes from the Old English word "fyndan," meaning "to find" or "to discover."  Over time, the meaning evolved to signify "to bring to an end" or "to complete." The past participle "finished" then became a common adjective describing something that has been completed.
