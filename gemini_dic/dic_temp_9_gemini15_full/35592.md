coarse:/kɔːrs/| adj.|粗糙的，粗俗的，粗略的|  Rough or unrefined in texture, appearance, or sound; lacking in fineness or delicacy.  It can also refer to behavior or language that is vulgar or offensive. |例句：The fabric had a coarse texture.  这布料质地粗糙。|例句：His coarse jokes offended many people. 他粗俗的笑话冒犯了许多人。|近义词：rough, crude, vulgar, grainy |反义词：fine, smooth, refined, delicate
