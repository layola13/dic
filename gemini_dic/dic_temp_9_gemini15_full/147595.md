/ˈoʊvərplæn/| v.| 超额计划，过度计划 | To plan or schedule more than is necessary or possible; to overestimate the resources available for a project.  | The company overplanned the project, leading to resource shortages and delays. 公司对项目计划过度，导致资源短缺和延误。| overestimate, overschedule | underestimate, underschedule | 动词

We overplan our work projects frequently. = 我们经常对工作项目计划过度。
