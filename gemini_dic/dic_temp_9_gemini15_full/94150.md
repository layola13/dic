impelling: /ɪmˈpɛlɪŋ/ | adj. | 推动的；强烈的；令人信服的 | Driving or urging something forward; compelling; forceful.  | The impelling force of the wind pushed the boat forward.  | 近义词：driving, compelling, forceful, urgent | 反义词：repelling, dissuading, deterring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence impels me to believe his story. = 证据迫使我相信他的话。
This strong feeling impels her to act. = 这种强烈的感觉驱使她采取行动。


一般过去时:
The desire for success impelled him to work harder. = 对成功的渴望驱使他更加努力工作。
A sense of duty impelled her to confess. = 责任感促使她坦白。


现在完成时:
What has impelled you to make such a drastic decision? = 什么促使你做出如此 drastic 的决定？
The need for change has impelled many reforms. = 变革的需求促使了许多改革。


过去完成时:
Having been impelled by a sense of justice, she reported the crime. = 出于正义感，她报了案。
Impelled by curiosity, he opened the mysterious box. = 出于好奇，他打开了那个神秘的盒子。


将来时:
The pressure of deadlines will impel them to work overtime. = 截止日期的压力将迫使他们加班。
I believe this new evidence will impel a change in policy. = 我相信这项新的证据将促使政策发生改变。


虚拟语气:
If I were impelled by greed, I would have done it differently. = 如果我受贪婪驱使，我会采取不同的做法。
If she had been impelled by revenge, the consequences would have been worse. = 如果她受复仇心驱使，后果会更糟。


被动语态:
He was impelled to action by the urgency of the situation. = 他被紧急情况逼得不得不采取行动。
The project was impelled forward by the team's dedication. = 项目在团队的奉献下得以推进。


疑问句:
What impelled you to choose this career path? = 什么促使你选择这条职业道路？
What is impelling the current economic growth? = 什么是推动当前经济增长的动力？


否定句:
Nothing impelled him to change his mind. = 没有什么能促使他改变主意。
The lack of evidence did not impel the jury to convict. = 缺乏证据并没有促使陪审团定罪。



PHRASE->
impelling force: 推动力
impelling motive: 促动机
impelling reason: 迫使的原因
impelling need: 迫切的需求
impelling urge: 强烈的冲动


词源:
From Middle English impellen, from Old French impeler, from Latin impellere (to push against, drive on), from in- + pellere (to push, drive).
