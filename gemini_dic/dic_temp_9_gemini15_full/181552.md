relacquered:/ˌriːˈlækərd/| v. |重新涂漆|To lacquer something again. This usually implies a previous layer of lacquer has been removed or is being covered.  The process involves applying a new coat of lacquer to a surface. |The antique table was relacquered to restore its original shine.  = 古董桌子重新涂漆以恢复其原有的光泽。

The old chair was carefully relacquered, bringing back its vibrant color. = 旧椅子被仔细地重新涂漆，恢复了它鲜艳的颜色。|近义词：revarnish, refinish |反义词：unlacquer (to remove lacquer)|动词
