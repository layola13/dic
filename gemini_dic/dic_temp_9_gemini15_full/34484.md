claying:/ˈkleɪɪŋ/| v.| 粘土处理；涂粘土；用粘土覆盖 |The process of applying clay to a surface, often for waterproofing, strengthening, or sculpting. It can also refer to the action of mixing or working with clay. |例句：The potter spent hours claying the vase, smoothing the surface to perfection. 陶工花了数小时给花瓶涂抹粘土，将表面磨得完美无瑕。

例句：They are claying the foundation of the house to make it more stable. 他们正在给房子的地基涂抹粘土以使其更稳定。

近义词：coating, plastering, applying

反义词：removing, scraping
