feminising: /ˈfemɪnaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使女性化；使具有女性特征 | To make or become more feminine in appearance or character.  | 例句：The company is feminising its marketing campaign to appeal to a wider audience. 公司正在将其营销活动女性化，以吸引更广泛的受众。

例句：He's feminising his style by wearing more delicate jewelry. 他通过佩戴更精致的珠宝来使自己的风格女性化。


近义词：effeminating, womanizing (in a specific context),  making more delicate


反义词：masculinizing, making more robust


词性：动词
