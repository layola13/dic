faithfully:/ˈfeɪθfəli/| adv.|忠实地；如实地；真诚地|In a faithful manner; loyally; truthfully.|例句：He faithfully served his country for many years. 他多年来忠实地为国家服务。|例句：She faithfully recorded every detail of the meeting. 她如实地记录了会议的每一个细节。|近义词：loyally, truly, honestly, devotedly|反义词：disloyally, falsely, dishonestly
