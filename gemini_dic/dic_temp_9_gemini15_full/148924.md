overwintered:/ˈoʊvərˌwɪntərd/| v. |安全越冬；度过冬天|To survive the winter.  This usually refers to plants, animals, or people surviving harsh winter conditions.|The plants overwintered well in the greenhouse.  温室里的植物安全越冬了。|The birds overwintered in a warmer climate. 鸟类在温暖的气候中过冬。|近义词：survived the winter, endured the winter |反义词： perished in winter, died during winter
