recapitulator:/rɪˌkæpɪˈtʃuleɪtər/| n. |概括者；总结者；复述者 |A recapitulator is a person or thing that summarizes or repeats something.  It often implies a comprehensive review, bringing together disparate elements into a cohesive whole. |The professor served as a recapitulator, expertly summarizing the complex arguments of the debate.  教授充当了总结者，巧妙地总结了辩论中复杂的论点。|summarizer, summariser, synthesizer, condenser|  (无明确反义词，但可以考虑：detailer, expander)|名词
