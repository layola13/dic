misvalues:/ˌmɪsˈvæljuːz/ | v. | 误估；低估；对…估价过低 | To misjudge the worth or importance of something or someone.  |  例句：He misvalues his own abilities. 他低估了自己的能力。  |  例句：The company misvalues its human capital by not investing in employee training. 公司没有投资员工培训，从而低估了人力资本的价值。 | 近义词：underestimate, undervalue, depreciate | 反义词：overvalue, appreciate | 动词
