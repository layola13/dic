educationally:/ˌɛdʒuˈkeɪʃənəli/ | adj. | 教育上地；在教育方面 | Relating to or connected with education.  This adjective describes something that pertains to the process or system of education. |例句：Educationally, the program is very successful.  教育方面，这个项目非常成功。

例句：The school implemented educationally sound methods. 学校实施了教育上合理的教学方法。


近义词：pedagogically, scholastically, didactically | 反义词：ignorantly, unscholastically


词性：形容词
