dramatizer: /ˈdræməˌtaɪzər/ | n. |戏剧化者，使戏剧化的人或事物 | A person or thing that dramatizes something; someone who makes something seem more dramatic or exciting than it really is.  |The news report was criticized for being too dramatizer, exaggerating the events to create more impact. 新闻报道因过于戏剧化而受到批评，夸大了事件以产生更大的影响。|近义词：exaggerator, sensationalist |反义词：understater, minimalist

一般现在时:
He dramatizes every little incident. 他把每一件小事都戏剧化。
She dramatizes her problems to gain sympathy. 她戏剧化地描述自己的问题以博取同情。


一般过去时:
