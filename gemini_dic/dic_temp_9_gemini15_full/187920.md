rotation: /roʊˈteɪʃən/ | /rəʊˈteɪʃən/ | n. |旋转；轮换；循环 | The act or process of rotating; a single turn or complete cycle around an axis.  It can also refer to a system of rotation or the assignment of tasks or positions in a regular sequence. |例句：The Earth's rotation causes day and night. 地球的自转导致昼夜交替。

The doctor recommended a rotation of antibiotics to combat drug-resistant bacteria. 医生建议轮换使用抗生素来对抗耐药细菌。

近义词：revolution, spin, turn, cycle, shift, round | 反义词：stillness, stagnation
