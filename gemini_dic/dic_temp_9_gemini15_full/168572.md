processor: /ˈprɑːsɛsər/ /ˈprəʊsɛsə(r)/ | n. |处理器，处理机 | A device that performs the calculations and controls the operations of a computer.  It is the central processing unit (CPU) of a computer system. | Example: My computer's processor is quite fast. 我的电脑处理器很快。 The new processor significantly improved the phone's performance. 新的处理器显著提升了手机的性能。 |近义词：CPU, central processing unit, microchip | 反义词：N/A | 名词
