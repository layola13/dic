loquat:/ˈloʊkwɑːt/|/ˈlɒkwɒt/| n. |枇杷|The loquat is a small, round fruit with a sweet, slightly acidic taste. It is native to eastern Asia and is cultivated in many warm regions around the world.  The fruit is often eaten fresh, but it can also be used to make jams, jellies, and other preserves.  |例句：The loquat tree is laden with ripe, golden fruit.  = 枇杷树上挂满了成熟的金黄色的果实。|例句：We made a delicious loquat jam from the fruit we picked in the orchard. = 我们用在果园里摘的果子做了一批美味的枇杷酱。|近义词：Japanese plum (though botanically different)|反义词:|词性:名词
