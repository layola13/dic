overevaluation:/ˌoʊvərˌvæljuˈeɪʃən/ | n. |过高评价，高估 |The act of placing too high a value on something; an assessment of something's worth that is excessively positive.  This can apply to assets, skills, or even personal characteristics. | Example: The overevaluation of the company's stock led to a significant market correction. 公司股票的过高估值导致了市场大幅调整。The overevaluation of his abilities resulted in his being given a task beyond his capabilities. 对他能力的过高评价导致他被分配了一项超出他能力范围的任务。|近义词：overestimation, exaggeration, inflation |反义词：undervaluation, underestimation
