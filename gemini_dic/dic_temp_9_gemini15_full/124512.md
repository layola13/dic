mounds:/maʊndz/| n.|土堆；坟堆；大量；堆积物|A mound is a raised mass of earth, typically without a regular shape.|例句：The archaeologists discovered ancient burial mounds in the valley. 考古学家在山谷中发现了古代的坟冢。

例句：There were mounds of laundry waiting to be folded. 有大量的衣服等着叠。|近义词：hill, heap, pile|反义词：depression, hollow
