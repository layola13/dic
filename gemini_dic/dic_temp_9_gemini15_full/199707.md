siphonophore: /ˈsaɪfənəfɔːr/ | n. | 僧帽水母；管水母 | A siphonophore is a marine hydrozoan in the order Siphonophora.  It is a colony of specialized zooids, each performing a specific function, that function together as a single organism.  They are often mistaken for jellyfish, but are fundamentally different in their structure and organization. | Example: The Portuguese man o' war is a well-known example of a siphonophore.  僧帽水母是管水母的一个著名例子。| 近义词：hydrozoan (though not a perfect synonym as it's a broader category) | 反义词：N/A
