exudes:/ɪɡˈzuːdz/ | v. |散发，流露，渗出 | to discharge or emit gradually; to give off or out; to be redolent or suggestive of something | The flower exudes a sweet fragrance. 这朵花散发着甜美的香味。  The painting exudes a sense of serenity. 这幅画流露出宁静的感觉。|近义词：emit, radiate, ooze, emanate |反义词：absorb, retain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exudes confidence. = 他散发着自信。
The plant exudes a sticky sap. = 这植物渗出一种粘稠的树液。

一般过去时:
The cake exuded a delicious aroma. = 蛋糕散发着美味的香气。
The wound exuded pus. = 伤口流出了脓。

现在完成时:
She has exuded charm and grace throughout her career. = 她在整个职业生涯中都散发着魅力和优雅。
The situation has exuded tension for weeks. =  这种情况已经持续几周，充满了紧张气氛。

过去完成时:
The old book had exuded a musty smell. = 那本旧书散发出一股霉味。
The event had exuded an air of mystery. =  这件事曾经笼罩着一层神秘的氛围。

将来时:
The perfume will exude a pleasant scent. = 香水将散发宜人的香味。
The project will exude professionalism. = 这个项目将展现专业性。

虚拟语气:
If he exuded more confidence, he would be more successful. = 如果他更自信一些，他会更成功。
I wish the meeting exuded less tension. = 我希望会议的气氛不那么紧张。

被动语态:
A sweet fragrance was exuded by the flowers. = 花朵散发着甜美的香味。（被动语态较少用）
Tension was exuded by the situation. = 局势充满了紧张感。（被动语态较少用）

疑问句:
Does the perfume exude a strong scent? = 这香水散发浓郁的香味吗？
What kind of feeling does this music exude? = 这音乐散发着什么样的感觉？

否定句:
The meeting didn't exude any sense of urgency. = 会议没有流露出任何紧迫感。
His words didn't exude warmth. = 他的话语并不温暖。
