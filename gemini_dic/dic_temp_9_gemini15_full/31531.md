charterable: /ˈtʃɑːrtərəbl/ | adj. | 可特许的，可包租的 | Capable of being chartered; able to be granted a charter.  This refers to the ability to be officially granted a charter, a document that establishes a corporation, organization, or institution, or to be rented or leased, especially a vessel. |


例句：

The company's new project is charterable under the current regulations.  这家公司的新项目根据现行法规是可以特许的。

That yacht is charterable for private parties. 那艘游艇可以包租用于私人聚会。


近义词：licensable, leasable, rentable


反义词：uncharterable


词性：形容词
