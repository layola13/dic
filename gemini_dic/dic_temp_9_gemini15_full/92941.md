identity: /aɪˈdentɪti/ | n. |身份；特性；一致性 |The state or fact of being a particular person or thing; the qualities or characteristics that make someone or something unique.  It refers to who or what a person or thing is.  This can encompass a person's self-perception, social roles, and the way they are perceived by others. |

例句：

He had to prove his identity to the police. 他必须向警方证明自己的身份。

The identity of the killer remains a mystery. 杀手的身份仍然是个谜。


近义词：personality, individuality, character, self, nature

反义词：anonymity, impersonation


词源:  From Old French *identité, from Latin identitas "sameness," from idem "the same."
