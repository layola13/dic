prelabels:/ˈpriːleɪbəlz/ | n. |预标签；预先标记 |  Prelabels are tags or labels applied to data or items before they are processed or analyzed.  They are often used to categorize or organize data for efficient handling. For example, in machine learning, prelabels might be used to initially classify images before a more sophisticated algorithm is applied. | Example: The researchers used prelabels to speed up the image recognition process.  研究人员使用预标签来加快图像识别过程。 The system automatically assigns prelabels based on initial data analysis. 系统根据初步数据分析自动分配预标签。|近义词: preliminary tags, advance labels |反义词: postlabels, final labels |名词
