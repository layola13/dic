dialogues:/ˈdaɪəlɒɡz/ /ˈdaɪəlɔːɡz/| n. |对话，对白 |Conversations between two or more people.  They can be fictional, as in a play or novel, or real, as in a recorded conversation.  |例句：The dialogues in the play were witty and engaging.  这部戏剧中的对话机智而引人入胜。|例句：The police recorded the dialogues between the suspects. 警方录下了嫌疑人之间的对话。|近义词：conversations, exchanges, talks |反义词：monologue, soliloquy
