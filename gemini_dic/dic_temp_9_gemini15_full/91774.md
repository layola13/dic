hyperenthusiasm: /ˌhaɪpərɪnˈθuːziæzəm/ | n. |极度热情；过度热情 | Excessive enthusiasm; an overabundance of zeal.  It often implies a degree of inappropriateness or unmanageability. |例句：Her hyperenthusiasm for the project led to burnout.  她的项目热情过度导致了倦怠。

例句：The team's hyperenthusiasm was ultimately counterproductive.  团队的过度热情最终适得其反。


近义词：overzealousness, fervor, passion (in an excessive sense), mania


反义词：apathy, indifference, unconcern, lack of interest


词性：名词
