disavowance:/ˌdɪsəˈvaʊəns/| n. |否认；否定；弃绝|The act of disavowing or denying something; repudiation.  It often involves rejecting or disowning something previously acknowledged or accepted.  |例句：His disavowance of the accusations surprised everyone.  他的否认指控让每个人都感到惊讶。


例句：The company issued a formal disavowance of the fraudulent activities. 公司正式否认了欺诈行为。


近义词：denial, repudiation, rejection, renunciation


反义词：acceptance, affirmation, confirmation, endorsement


词性:名词
