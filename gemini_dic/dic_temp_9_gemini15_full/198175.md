shorling: /ˈʃɔːrlɪŋ/ | n. | 短毛羊毛；剪下的短毛 | The process of shearing sheep to obtain short wool fibers.  Shorling is often used in the manufacture of felt and other textiles. | Example: The farmer collected the shorling after shearing the flock.  农民在剪羊毛后收集了短毛。 | 近义词: short wool, clipped wool | 反义词: long wool
