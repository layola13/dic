fronton:/ˈfrɒntən/| n.|壁球场；前厅|A fronton is an indoor or outdoor court for playing pelota (a Basque ball game).  It typically has a smooth, usually cement or stone, wall at one end against which the ball is struck. |The players were warming up on the fronton before the pelota match.|近义词：court (in the context of a game)|反义词：none.
