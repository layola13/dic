just-in-time:/ˌdʒʌstɪnˈtaɪm/| adj.|准时; 及时; 适时 |Just-in-time (JIT) is a manufacturing management system where materials and products arrive only when they are needed in the production process, minimizing waste and inventory costs.  |Example: The just-in-time delivery of the parts prevented a production shutdown. 及时交付的零件避免了生产中断。The company implemented a just-in-time inventory system to reduce storage costs. 公司实施了准时制库存系统以降低存储成本。|近义词：timely, opportune, prompt |反义词：late, delayed, untimely
