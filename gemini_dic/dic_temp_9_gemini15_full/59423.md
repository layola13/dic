earthlike:/ˈɜːrθlaɪk/ | adj. |类地(的)；像地球的 |Resembling the Earth, especially in having conditions suitable for life.  It often refers to planets or celestial bodies that share similar characteristics to Earth, such as size, composition, temperature, and the presence of liquid water.| 例句：Scientists are searching for earthlike planets outside our solar system. 科学家们正在寻找太阳系外的类地行星。

The discovery of an earthlike planet orbiting a nearby star has generated considerable excitement. 发现一颗围绕附近恒星运行的类地行星引起了极大的兴奋。


近义词：terrestrial, planet-like

反义词：alien, unlike Earth
