fencer:/ˈfɛnsər/| n. |击剑运动员；剑客|A person who fences.  Fencer refers to someone who practices or competes in the sport of fencing. They are skilled in using swords or foils. |Example: The fencer executed a flawless riposte.  这位击剑运动员完美地进行了反击。|近义词：swordsman, duelist |反义词：None specifically.
