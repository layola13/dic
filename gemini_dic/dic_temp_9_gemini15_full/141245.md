opabinia:/ˌoʊpəˈbɪniə/| n. |欧巴宾海蝎 |An extinct genus of large, unusual Cambrian arthropod.  It possessed five eyes, a frontal proboscis, and a segmented body with lateral flaps.  It is considered a significant find in understanding the early evolution of arthropods. | Example:  The discovery of Opabinia revolutionized our understanding of Cambrian life.  欧巴宾海蝎的发现彻底改变了我们对寒武纪生命的理解。|近义词：none (unique genus)|反义词：none
