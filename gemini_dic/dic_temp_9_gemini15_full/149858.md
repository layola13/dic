palette:/ˈpælɪt/ | n. |调色板；调色盘；（艺术家的）颜料盒；（选择的）范围；（食物的）色调；|A palette is a thin board or tablet with a range of colors (paints) used by an artist for mixing colors. It can also refer to a range of colors or choices available.  In the context of food, it refers to the range of flavors or colors.|例句：The artist arranged her paints neatly on her palette.  艺术家把颜料整齐地摆放在调色板上。

The chef created a vibrant autumnal palette for the dish.  厨师为这道菜创造了一个充满活力的秋季色调。


近义词：range, spectrum, array, selection |反义词：monotony, uniformity


词源：源自古法语 *palette，最终源自拉丁语 *palea，意为“铲子”或“小木板”。  其演变与艺术家的调色板形状相似有关。
