flammable:/ˈflæməbəl/| adj.|易燃的；可燃的|Easily ignited and capable of burning rapidly.  Flammable materials should be stored carefully to prevent fires.|近义词：inflammable, combustible|反义词：nonflammable, fireproof


例句：

Gasoline is a highly flammable liquid.  = 汽油是一种高度易燃的液体。

That fabric is flammable, so be careful when you use it near open flames. = 那块布料易燃，所以靠近明火时要小心。


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The gas is flammable. = 这种气体易燃。
This material is highly flammable. = 这种材料极易燃烧。

一般过去时:
The old house was flammable. = 那栋旧房子易燃。
The paint was flammable and caused a fire. = 油漆易燃，引发了火灾。

现在完成时:
The chemicals have been flammable since they were manufactured. = 这些化学品自生产以来一直易燃。
The building materials have proven to be flammable. = 建筑材料已被证明是易燃的。

过去完成时:
The wood had been flammable before the treatment. = 木材在处理之前是易燃的。
The storage facility had been deemed flammable and unsafe. = 仓库被认为易燃且不安全。

将来时:
The new building will be flammable if not properly treated. = 如果不进行适当处理，新建筑将是易燃的。
The factory will use flammable materials in the production process. = 工厂将在生产过程中使用易燃材料。

虚拟语气:
If the material were flammable, it would be dangerous. = 如果这种材料易燃，它将很危险。
If the substance were more flammable, the fire would have spread faster. = 如果物质更易燃，火势蔓延得更快。


被动语态:

The furniture is considered flammable and should be replaced. = 家具被认为易燃，应更换。


疑问句:

否定句:
