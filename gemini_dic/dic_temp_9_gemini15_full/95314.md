incautiously:/ɪnˈkɔːʃəsli/| adj.|不谨慎地；轻率地 | acting without caution; rashly; carelessly.  |例句：He incautiously revealed the secret to his friend. 他不谨慎地向朋友透露了秘密。|例句：She incautiously stepped onto the ice and fell. 她不小心踩到冰上摔倒了。|近义词：recklessly, rashly, carelessly, heedlessly |反义词：carefully, cautiously, prudently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He incautiously speaks his mind. = 他不加思索地说出自己的想法。
They incautiously share personal information online. = 他们不谨慎地在网上分享个人信息。

一般过去时:
She incautiously revealed her plans to her rivals. = 她轻率地向竞争对手透露了她的计划。
He incautiously drove through the red light. = 他鲁莽地闯红灯。

现在完成时:
He has incautiously burned his bridges with his former employer. = 他已经鲁莽地和他的前雇主断绝了关系。
They have incautiously invested their savings in a risky venture. = 他们不谨慎地将积蓄投资于一项风险投资。

过去完成时:
She had incautiously trusted him with her most valuable possession.  = 她曾经不谨慎地把最珍贵的财产托付给他。
They had incautiously left the door unlocked. = 他们曾经不谨慎地没有锁门。

将来时:
He will incautiously make the same mistake again. = 他将会再次鲁莽地犯同样的错误。
They will incautiously spend all their money on frivolous things. = 他们将会不谨慎地把所有的钱都花在琐事上。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't acted so incautiously, he wouldn't have gotten into trouble. = 如果他不那么鲁莽行事，他就不会惹上麻烦。
I wish I hadn't spoken so incautiously. = 我真希望我没有那么轻率地说话。

被动语态:
The information was incautiously disclosed to the press. = 信息被不谨慎地泄露给了媒体。
His reputation was incautiously damaged by his own actions. = 他的名誉因他自己的行为而被不谨慎地损害。

疑问句:
Was he incautiously driving when the accident happened? = 事故发生时他是否在鲁莽驾驶？
Did she incautiously reveal the company's secrets? = 她是否不谨慎地泄露了公司的秘密？

否定句:
He did not act incautiously; he was very careful. = 他没有鲁莽行事；他非常小心。
She did not incautiously give away her position in the negotiation. = 她并没有在谈判中不谨慎地放弃自己的立场。
