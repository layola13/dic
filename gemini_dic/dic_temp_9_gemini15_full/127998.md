nephews:/ˈnefjuːz/| n. |侄子（们）；外甥（们）|The plural of nephew.  A nephew is the son of one's brother or sister, or the son of one's spouse's brother or sister.|例句：My nephews are coming to visit next week.  我的侄子们下周要来拜访。
例句：He has two nephews and one niece. 他有两个侄子和一个侄女。|近义词：grandson (if referring to a more distant relative), kinsman|反义词：N/A
