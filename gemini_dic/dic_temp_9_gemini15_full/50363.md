depopularizing: /ˌdiːˈpɒpjʊləraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使不受欢迎；使不再流行；使衰落 |  The act of causing something to become less popular or fashionable; to diminish the popularity of something. |

例句：

The government's new policies are depopularizing the once-beloved national park. = 政府的新政策正在使曾经深受喜爱的国家公园不受欢迎。

The rise of streaming services is depopularizing traditional cable television. = 流媒体服务的兴起正在使传统的付费电视不受欢迎。


近义词：disfavoring, discrediting, undermining,  undermining the popularity of

反义词：popularizing, promoting, boosting


词性：动词
