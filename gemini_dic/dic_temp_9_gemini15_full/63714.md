ephemeron:/ɪˈfɛmərɒn/ | n. |短暂的存在，朝生暮死之物 |A short-lived thing; something existing only for a brief period.  Ephemera are often associated with insects that live only for a day, but the term can refer to any short-lived phenomenon or thing. |例句：Mayflies are a classic example of an ephemeron.  蜉蝣是短暂存在的经典例子。

The fleeting beauty of a summer day is an ephemeron. 夏日短暂的美好是转瞬即逝的。|近义词：fleeting thing, transient, ephemeral |反义词：permanent, enduring, lasting
