non-deleterious:/ˌnɒnˌdɛlɪˈtɪəriəs/ | adj. |无害的，非有害的 | Not causing harm or damage; harmless.  |Example: The study showed the drug had non-deleterious effects on the heart. 这项研究表明该药物对心脏没有有害影响。


The new policy has non-deleterious effects on the environment.这项新政策对环境没有危害。|近义词：harmless, innocuous, safe |反义词：harmful, deleterious, detrimental


USAGE->

This treatment is non-deleterious to the environment. = 这种疗法对环境无害。

虚拟语气:
If the procedure were non-deleterious, we would use it more widely. = 如果该程序是无害的，我们将更广泛地使用它。
