scrunching:/ˈskrʌntʃɪŋ/ | v. | 揉皱；挤压；发出摩擦声 | To crush or squeeze something together.  |例句：She was scrunching the newspaper into a ball. 她把报纸揉成一团。|例句：The car tires were scrunching on the gravel. 汽车轮胎在碎石路上发出摩擦声。|近义词：crumpling, crushing, squeezing, crunching |反义词：smoothing, unfolding


USAGE->

He scrunches his face when he's concentrating. = 他专注的时候会皱起脸。

一般过去时:
