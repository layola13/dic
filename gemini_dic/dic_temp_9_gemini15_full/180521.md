regerminates:/ˌriːˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪts/| v.| 再发芽；重新萌芽 | To begin to grow again; to sprout anew.  The word implies a previous period of dormancy or inactivity before the renewed growth. |例句：The seeds regerminated after the spring rains. 春雨过后，种子重新发芽了。|例句：After a period of drought, the plants regerminated, showing signs of new life. 经历一段旱期后，植物重新发芽，展现出新的生命迹象。|近义词：resprout, sprout again, re-emerge|反义词：wither, die, decay|动词
