plush:/plʌʃ/| adj. n.|柔软的；豪华的；毛绒的 |extremely soft and luxurious; often refers to materials like velvet or fabrics with a thick, soft pile.  |例句：The plush carpet felt wonderful under my feet.  柔软的地毯踩上去感觉很棒。

例句：She stayed at a plush hotel in the city center.她在市中心一家豪华的酒店住了下来。|近义词：soft, luxurious, velvety, opulent |反义词：rough, coarse, harsh, austere |形容词，名词
