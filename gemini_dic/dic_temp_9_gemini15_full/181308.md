reinterrogate:/ˌriːɪnˈtɛrəɡeɪt/| vt.|再次盘问，重新审问|To question someone again, often in more detail or with a different approach.  This implies a previous interrogation has already taken place.|例句：The police decided to reinterrogate the suspect after new evidence emerged.  警方决定在出现新的证据后再次审问嫌疑人。

例句：The witness was reinterrogate about his alibi. 目击证人被再次询问他的不在场证明。


近义词：re-examine, question again, cross-examine, interview again

反义词：release, free


词性:动词
