ibices:/ˈaɪbɪsiːz/| n.|岩羊群，崖羊群|The plural of ibex.  A group of ibices is often referred to as a flock or herd.| 例句：A flock of ibices grazed on the steep mountainside.一群岩羊在陡峭的山坡上吃草。  The herd of ibices was difficult to count from such a distance. 从这么远的距离很难数清那群岩羊的数量。|近义词：herd, flock (of goats/sheep)|反义词：无
