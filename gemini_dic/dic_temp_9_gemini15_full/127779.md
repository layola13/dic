neighbor:/ˈneɪbər/| n. |邻居|A person who lives near you.|My neighbor is very friendly.我的邻居很友好。|近义词：resident,dweller|反义词：stranger


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My neighbor often helps me with my garden. = 我的邻居经常帮我打理花园。
They greet their neighbors every morning. = 他们每天早上都向邻居打招呼。


一般过去时:
My neighbor helped me move last week. = 我的邻居上周帮我搬家。
We visited our neighbors for dinner last night. = 昨晚我们去邻居家吃晚饭了。


现在完成时:
I have known my neighbors for five years. = 我认识我的邻居五年了。
They have always been kind to their neighbors. = 他们一直对邻居都很友善。


过去完成时:
I had already met my neighbors before I moved in. = 我搬进来之前就已经认识我的邻居了。
They had lived there for ten years before we moved next door. = 我们搬到隔壁之前，他们已经在那里住了十年了。


将来时:
I will ask my neighbor to water my plants while I'm away. = 我不在家的时候会请邻居帮我浇花。
We will introduce ourselves to our new neighbors next week. = 我们下周会向新邻居介绍自己。


虚拟语气:
If I had a good neighbor, I wouldn't feel so lonely. = 如果我有一个好邻居，我就不会感到那么孤独。
I wish my neighbor would turn down their music. = 我希望我的邻居能把音乐调小点声。


被动语态:
Our garden was damaged by our neighbor's dog. = 我们的花园被邻居的狗弄坏了。
The fence was repaired by the neighbors. = 篱笆由邻居们修理好了。


疑问句:
Do you know your neighbors? = 你认识你的邻居吗？
Have you met the new neighbors yet? = 你见过新邻居了吗？


否定句:
I don't know my neighbors very well. = 我不太了解我的邻居。
We haven't spoken to our neighbors recently. = 我们最近没有和邻居说过话。
