repopulate:/ˌriːˈpɒpjuleɪt/| /ˌriːˈpɑːpjuleɪt/ | vt. |重新填充；使重新居住；重新繁殖 |To populate again; to re-inhabit; to replenish a population.  The verb implies restoring a population to a previous level or filling an area that was previously empty or sparsely populated.|例句：The government is trying to repopulate the rural areas. 政府正试图使农村地区重新居住起来。

例句：After the devastating fire, the wildlife began to slowly repopulate the forest.  灾难性的火灾过后，野生动物开始慢慢地重新繁衍，使森林恢复生机。


近义词：replenish, restock, reestablish


反义词：depopulate, deplete


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They repopulate the forest with new trees. = 他们用新树重新种植森林。
We repopulate the database with updated information. = 我们用更新的信息重新填充数据库。


一般过去时:
The farmers repopulated the field with new crops. = 农民们用新的作物重新种植了田地。
They repopulated the village after the war. = 战争之后，他们使村庄重新居住起来。


现在完成时:
The conservationists have repopulated the endangered species. = 保育主义者已经使濒危物种重新繁衍。
The company has repopulated its inventory. = 公司已经重新补充了库存。


过去完成时:
Before the flood, the area had been repopulated with new residents. = 在洪水泛滥之前，该地区已经重新居住了一些新的居民。
They had repopulated the island after the tsunami. = 海啸之后，他们已经使岛屿重新居住起来。


将来时:
The government will repopulate the abandoned town. = 政府将使被遗弃的小镇重新居住起来。
They will repopulate the aquarium with new fish. = 他们将用新的鱼重新填充水族馆。


虚拟语气:
If we repopulated the area, it would attract more tourists. = 如果我们使该地区重新居住起来，它将吸引更多游客。
I wish they had repopulated the park with more trees. = 我希望他们用更多的树木重新种植公园。


被动语态:
The city was repopulated after the earthquake. = 地震之后，这座城市重新居住起来。
The lake is being repopulated with fish. = 湖泊正在被重新放养鱼类。


疑问句:
Will they repopulate the old building? = 他们会重新使用那栋旧建筑吗？
How will they repopulate the damaged ecosystem? = 他们将如何使受损的生态系统恢复生机？


否定句:
They did not repopulate the area after the disaster. = 灾难之后，他们并没有使该地区重新居住起来。
The company has not yet repopulated its stocks. = 公司尚未重新补充其库存。

词源:
re- (表示“再次”、“重新”) + populate (填充，居住)  来自拉丁语 *populāre ("to people, to populate")，源于populus ("people")。
