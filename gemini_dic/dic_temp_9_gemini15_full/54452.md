disinheriting: /ˌdɪsɪnˈhɛrɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 剥夺继承权；取消继承权 | The act of depriving someone of their inheritance or rightful share of an estate.  | Example: The father was furious and decided to disinherit his wayward son.  父亲很生气，决定剥夺他那不争气的儿子的继承权。 | 近义词：disowning, depriving, excluding | 反义词：inheriting, bequeathing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is disinheriting his nephew. = 他正在剥夺他侄子的继承权。
The will disinherits the second son. = 这份遗嘱剥夺了次子的继承权。


一般过去时:
She disinherited her daughter after a bitter argument. = 她和女儿大吵一架后，剥夺了女儿的继承权。
The tycoon disinherited his only child. = 那位大亨剥夺了他唯一孩子的继承权。


现在完成时:
He has disinherited his entire family. = 他已经剥夺了他全家人的继承权。
The court has disinherited the heir. = 法院已经剥夺了继承人的继承权。


过去完成时:
The father had disinherited his son before his death. = 父亲在他死前已经剥夺了他儿子的继承权。
It was revealed that she had disinherited her grandson years ago. = 有人透露她几年前就剥夺了她孙子的继承权。


将来时:
The estranged husband plans to disinherit his wife. = 疏远的丈夫计划剥夺妻子的继承权。
They will disinherit their irresponsible child. = 他们将剥夺他们不负责任孩子的继承权。


虚拟语气:
If I were the testator, I would disinherit my rebellious child. = 如果我是遗嘱人，我会剥夺我叛逆孩子的继承权。
I wish he hadn't disinherited his only daughter. = 我真希望他没有剥夺他唯一女儿的继承权。


被动语态:
The son was disinherited by his father. = 儿子被父亲剥夺了继承权。
The property was disinherited from the family. = 这处房产被剥夺了家族的继承权。


疑问句:

否定句:

They chose not to disinherit their prodigal son. = 他们选择不剥夺他们浪子儿子的继承权。
