coproduced:/ˌkoʊprəˈdjuːst/| v. |联合制作，合作制作|The film was coproduced by two different studios.  这部电影是由两家不同的工作室联合制作的。|近义词：jointly produced, collaborated on|反义词：solely produced, independently produced|动词
