monomaniacs:/ˌmɒnəˈmeɪniæks/ | n. |偏执狂；着魔的人 |A monomaniac is someone who is obsessed with a single idea or purpose, often to the exclusion of all others.  They may exhibit obsessive behavior and disregard for other aspects of their life. |例句：He became a monomaniac, fixated on his invention and neglecting his family and health. 他变成一个偏执狂，沉迷于他的发明，而忽略了他的家庭和健康。

例句：The artist was a monomaniac, dedicated solely to his craft.这位艺术家是个偏执狂，只致力于他的技艺。


近义词：obsessive, fanatic, maniac, zealot |反义词：balanced, well-rounded, moderate


词性：名词
