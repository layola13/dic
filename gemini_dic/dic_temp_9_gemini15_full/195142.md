semidangerous:/ˌsɛmiˈdeɪndʒərəs/| adj.|略微危险的，有点危险的|Somewhat dangerous; presenting a moderate degree of risk or harm.  It combines the prefix "semi-" (meaning half or partly) with the adjective "dangerous."  This word suggests a level of danger that is less than extreme but still present and should be considered.|例句：The hike was semidangerous due to the loose rocks and steep cliffs.这次远足有点危险，因为有松散的岩石和陡峭的悬崖。

The activity, while semidangerous, was ultimately rewarding.这项活动虽然有点危险，但最终还是很有回报的。|近义词：risky, hazardous, perilous|反义词：safe, secure, harmless
