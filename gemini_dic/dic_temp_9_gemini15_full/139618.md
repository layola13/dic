occasions:/əˈkeɪʒənz/ | n. | 场合；时机；庆典 | An occasion is a particular time or event.  It can refer to a specific event, a general time, or a special celebration.  The word often implies some significance or importance. | 例句：The wedding was a joyous occasion. 婚礼是一个快乐的场合。


The party was a great occasion to celebrate her birthday.  派对是一个庆祝她生日的盛大场合。


近义词：events, instances, celebrations, times | 反义词：routine, normalcy
