rugous:/ˈruːɡəs/| adj.|有皱纹的，粗糙的，褶皱的 |  Having a wrinkled or rough surface; ridged.  The word often describes the texture of a surface, similar to being bumpy or uneven.  | Example: The old man's face was rugous with age.  这位老人的脸因年龄而布满皱纹。  The rugous bark of the oak tree was deeply furrowed.  橡树粗糙的树皮布满了深深的沟壑。|近义词：wrinkled, furrowed, corrugated, creased |反义词：smooth, unblemished, sleek
