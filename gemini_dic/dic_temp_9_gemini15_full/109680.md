leucorrhoea:/ˌluːkəˈriːə/| n. |白带；阴道分泌物 |A whitish or yellowish vaginal discharge.  The appearance and amount of leucorrhoea can vary depending on the woman's hormonal cycle and overall health.  Excessive or unusual leucorrhoea can be a symptom of various medical conditions.|例句：The doctor examined her for excessive leucorrhoea.医生检查了她过多的白带。

The patient presented with a complaint of abnormal leucorrhoea. 病人主诉异常白带。|近义词：vaginal discharge, cervical mucus|反义词：无
