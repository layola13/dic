overinstruction:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌkʃən/| n. |过度指导，过多的指导|The act of giving excessive instruction or guidance;  teaching or explaining something in too much detail or for too long, often to the point of being counterproductive.  It can lead to confusion, decreased motivation, and dependence on the instructor. | Example: The overinstruction in the early stages of the project led to confusion among the team members.  过度指导导致项目早期团队成员感到困惑。|近义词：overteaching, excessive guidance, superfluous instruction|反义词：underinstruction, insufficient guidance
