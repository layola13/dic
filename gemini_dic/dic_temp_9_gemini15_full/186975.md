rising:/ˈraɪzɪŋ/| adj.|上升的；兴起的；新兴的|Increasing in amount, number, or strength; becoming more prominent.|The rising sun cast a warm glow over the landscape.  旭日东升，给大地披上了一层温暖的光芒。 The rising cost of living is a major concern.  生活成本的上升是一个主要问题。|increasing, growing, escalating, burgeoning|falling, declining, decreasing, diminishing|形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sun is rising in the east. = 太阳正在东边升起。
The cost of fuel is rising steadily. = 燃料成本正在稳步上涨。


一般过去时:
The river was rising rapidly after the heavy rain. = 大雨过后，河水迅速上涨。
The temperature rose sharply in the afternoon. = 下午气温骤然升高。


现在完成时:
The sea level has been rising for decades. = 海平面几十年来一直在上升。
The company's profits have risen significantly this year. = 公司的利润今年显著增长。


过去完成时:
The water level had risen considerably before the dam broke. = 水位在水坝决堤之前已经涨得很高了。
The stock market had risen dramatically before the crash. = 股市在崩盘前曾大幅上涨。


将来时:
The price of oil will be rising next year. = 明年油价将会上涨。
The population of the city is expected to rise dramatically. = 预计该城市人口将急剧增长。


虚拟语气:
If the temperature were rising, we would go inside. = 如果气温在升高，我们会进去。
I wish the sun were rising sooner. = 我希望太阳早点升起。


被动语态:
The issue is being rising by the public. = 这个问题正被公众关注。 (Note:  "rising" is not typically used passively in this way.  A more natural phrasing would be "The issue is being raised by the public.")
The matter was being risen frequently. = 这件事经常被提起。(Note: Similar to the above, a more natural phrasing would be needed)


疑问句:
Is the water level rising? = 水位在上升吗？
Are the prices rising too fast? = 物价上涨得太快了吗？


否定句:
The sun isn't rising yet. = 太阳还没升起。
The unemployment rate isn't rising. = 失业率没有上升。


PHRASE->

rising action:  情节发展 (in literature)
rising star:  冉冉升起的新星
rising tide:  上涨的潮水
rising demand:  不断增长的需求
rising generation:  新一代
