ods:/ɒdz/| n.|几率，可能性；赔率|Odds are, he'll be late.  可能性是他会迟到。|近义词：probability, chance, likelihood|反义词：certainty


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The odds are good that we will win. = 我们获胜的几率很大。
What are the odds of that happening? = 那件事发生的几率有多大？


一般过去时:
The odds were against us. = 胜算对我们不利。
What were the odds of him winning the lottery? = 他赢得彩票的几率有多大？


现在完成时:
The odds have changed since yesterday. = 自从昨天以来，几率已经改变了。
What have the odds been like recently? = 最近几率如何？


过去完成时:
The odds had already been stacked against her. = 胜算早就对她不利了。
The odds had improved significantly before the game started. = 在比赛开始之前，胜算已经大大提高了。


将来时:
The odds will be in our favor. = 胜算将对我们有利。
What will the odds be like tomorrow? = 明天的几率会怎样？


虚拟语气:
If the odds were different, we might have won. = 如果情况不同，我们也许会赢。
I wish the odds were in my favor. = 我希望胜算对我有利。


被动语态:
The odds were calculated carefully. = 几率被仔细计算过。
The odds are being constantly monitored. = 几率正在不断地被监控。


疑问句:
What are the odds? = 几率是多少？
What are the odds of success? = 成功几率有多大？


否定句:
The odds are not in our favor. = 胜算对我们不利。
The odds were against her winning the competition. = 她赢得比赛的几率很小。


PHRASE->

stacked the odds = 操纵几率
beat the odds = 击败几率
long odds = 赔率很低，获胜几率很小
against all odds = 尽管困难重重
odds and ends = 零碎的东西
