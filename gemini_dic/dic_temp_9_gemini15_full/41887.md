cosied:/ˈkəʊziːd/| v.| 使舒适；使温暖舒适；偎依；蜷缩 | To make (something) comfortable or warm; to cuddle or snuggle. | 例句：She cosied herself up in front of the fire. 她舒服地坐在火炉前。  She cosied the baby in a warm blanket. 她用暖和的毯子把婴儿裹得暖暖的。| 近义词： snuggled, nestled, cuddled | 反义词：exposed, uncomfortable

词源:
The verb "cosied" is derived from the adjective "cosy," which ultimately comes from the Dutch word "koosij," meaning "little cage" or "small enclosure."  The sense of warmth and comfort evolved from the image of being safely enclosed.
