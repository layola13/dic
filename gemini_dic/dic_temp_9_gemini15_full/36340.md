coil:/kɔɪl/| n., v.|线圈；卷；盘绕|A coil is a length of wire wound into a series of loops.  It can also refer to something that is curled or wound into a spiral shape. As a verb, it means to wind something into a coil.|例句：The electrician carefully coiled the wire. 电工小心地把电线盘起来。

The spring was tightly coiled. 弹簧紧紧地盘绕着。|近义词：spiral, helix, wind|反义词：uncoil, straighten
