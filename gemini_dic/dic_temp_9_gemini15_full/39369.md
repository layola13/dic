conjugator:/ˈkɒndʒʊɡeɪtər/| n. |词形变化器，缀合器 |A conjugator is a tool or program that automatically inflects verbs (changes their form to indicate tense, mood, person, number, etc.). It aids in learning languages by providing various forms of a verb.  |Example:  Many language learning apps include a verb conjugator to help users practice verb tenses.  许多语言学习应用程序包含一个动词变位器来帮助用户练习动词时态。|近义词：inflector, verb conjugater |反义词：N/A
