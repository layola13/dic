jiffy's:/ˈdʒɪfiːz/| n.|瞬间，片刻|The possessive form of "jiffy," referring to a very short period of time.  It implies belonging to or associated with a jiffy.|例句：I'll be back in a jiffy's time. 我一会儿就回来。|近义词：moment, second, instant|反义词：eon, eternity, age


USAGE->
This word is the possessive form of "jiffy" and doesn't have verb conjugations.  It's used to show possession or association with a jiffy.  Therefore, tense forms are not applicable.


PHRASE->
in a jiffy = 立刻, 马上
a jiffy's worth = 一瞬间的价值
within a jiffy = 在瞬间之内
a jiffy's notice = 瞬间的通知
the jiffy's pace = 瞬间的速度
