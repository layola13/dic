skeptics:/ˈskɛptɪks/| n. |怀疑论者，持怀疑态度的人|People who doubt or question the truth or validity of something. Skeptics often demand evidence before accepting claims as true.  They are critical thinkers who are not easily persuaded.|例句：Many skeptics remain unconvinced by the government's explanation of the incident. 许多怀疑论者仍然不相信政府对这一事件的解释。

例句：The new scientific theory faced strong skepticism from many established scientists.  新的科学理论受到了许多资深科学家的强烈质疑。|近义词：doubters, doubters, questioners, cynics|反义词：believers, true believers
