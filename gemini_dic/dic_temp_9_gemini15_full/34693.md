clerkships:/ˈklɑːrkʃɪps/| n. |实习律师职位；书记员职位|A clerkship is a period of time spent working as a clerk, typically in a law firm or court, often as part of professional training.  It provides practical experience and insights into the legal profession.|例句：Many law students seek clerkships with judges to gain valuable experience. 许多法学院学生寻求法官的实习职位以获得宝贵的经验。


例句：He completed a clerkship at a prestigious law firm before starting his own practice. 他在一个著名的律师事务所完成了实习期后才开始自己执业。


近义词：internship, apprenticeship, traineeship


反义词：无


词性：名词
