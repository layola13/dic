nonendowments:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdaʊmənts/ | n. |非捐赠；非捐赠资产 |Things that are not endowments; assets or resources that are not provided as gifts or grants.  This term often appears in financial contexts, particularly in relation to institutional funding. | Example: The university relied heavily on tuition fees to supplement its nonendowments. 这所大学严重依赖学费来补充其非捐赠资产。|近义词：non-gifts, unearned assets, own resources |反义词：endowments, grants, gifts
