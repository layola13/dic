paste:/peɪst/| n. v.|粘贴；浆糊；糊状物|To stick or attach something, usually with an adhesive substance.  It can also refer to the action of copying and inserting text or images.|例句：I pasted the picture into my scrapbook.我把图片粘贴到我的剪贴簿里了。|例句：The chef used a paste made from flour and water to thicken the sauce.厨师用面粉和水做的糊来使酱汁变稠。|近义词：stick, affix, glue|反义词：detach, remove


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He pastes the poster on the wall. = 他把海报贴在墙上。
She pastes stamps on the envelopes. = 她把邮票贴在信封上。

一般过去时:
I pasted the photo in my album yesterday. = 我昨天把照片贴在我的相册里了。
They pasted the flyer to the notice board. = 他们把传单贴在了布告栏上。

现在完成时:
He has pasted all the pictures into the album. = 他已经把所有的照片都贴到相册里了。
She has pasted the label onto the jar. = 她已经把标签贴到罐子上。


过去完成时:
I had pasted the address label before I mailed the package. = 我在寄包裹之前已经贴好了地址标签。
They had pasted the wallpaper before painting the room. = 他们在粉刷房间之前已经贴好了壁纸。

将来时:
I will paste the document into the report. = 我将把文档粘贴到报告中。
She will paste the drawing on the refrigerator. = 她将会把图画贴在冰箱上。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would paste all the photos. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把所有照片都贴上。
I wish I had pasted the correct label on the box. = 我真希望我把正确的标签贴在了盒子上。

被动语态:
The poster was pasted on the wall. = 海报被贴在了墙上。
The picture will be pasted into the book. = 图片将会被粘贴到书里。

疑问句:
Did you paste the picture correctly? = 你把图片贴正确了吗？
Where did he paste the announcement? = 他把公告贴在哪里了？

否定句:
I didn't paste the label on the package. = 我没有把标签贴在包裹上。
She didn't paste the picture straight. = 她没有把图片贴直。
