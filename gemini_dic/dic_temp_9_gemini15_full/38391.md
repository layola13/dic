conceptualistic: /ˌkɒnsɛptʃuˈælɪstɪk/ | /ˌkɑːnsɛptʃuˈælɪstɪk/ | adj. | 概念主义的；观念主义的 | Relating to or characteristic of Conceptualism, an artistic movement that emphasizes concepts or ideas over traditional aesthetic concerns.  The movement prioritizes the artist's idea or concept as the primary focus of the artwork, often minimizing or rejecting traditional notions of craftsmanship or skill.  |  The artist's work is highly conceptualistic, focusing on the idea behind the piece rather than its visual appeal.  | 近义词：idealistic, theoretical, abstract | 反义词：realistic, concrete, representational
