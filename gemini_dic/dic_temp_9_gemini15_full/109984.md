liberty:/ˈlɪbərti/| n. |自由，自主，特权|Freedom from control, interference, or oppression; the right to act, speak, or think as one wants without hindrances.|例句：They fought for liberty and justice for all. 他们为所有人的自由和正义而战。|例句：The birds enjoyed their liberty in the wide open sky.  鸟儿们在广阔的天空中享受着自由。|近义词：freedom, independence, autonomy|反义词：slavery, oppression, captivity|名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enjoys liberty. = 他享受自由。
We value our liberty. = 我们珍视我们的自由。

一般过去时:
They lost their liberty. = 他们失去了自由。
She regained her liberty. = 她恢复了自由。

现在完成时:
They have fought for liberty for many years. = 他们为自由奋斗多年了。
We have achieved a great degree of liberty. = 我们已经取得了相当程度的自由。

过去完成时:
They had lost their liberty before the revolution. = 他们在革命前就失去了自由。
She had never known true liberty until she escaped. = 直到逃脱后，她才真正体会到自由。

将来时:
They will fight for their liberty. = 他们将为他们的自由而战。
We will cherish our liberty. = 我们将珍惜我们的自由。

虚拟语气:
If I had my liberty, I would travel the world. = 如果我自由了，我会环游世界。
I wish I had the liberty to speak my mind. = 我希望我有自由表达我的想法。

被动语态:
Liberty was denied to them. = 自由被剥夺了。
Their liberty is often restricted. = 他们的自由经常受到限制。

疑问句:
Do you have the liberty to leave? = 你有离开的自由吗？
What liberty do we have in this situation? = 在这种情况下我们有什么自由？

否定句:
They do not have liberty of speech. = 他们没有言论自由。
She was not granted liberty. = 她没有被给予自由。

词源:
The word "liberty" comes from the Old French word "liberte," which in turn derives from the Latin word "libertas."  "Libertas" is related to the Latin word "liber," meaning "free."  The root traces back to Proto-Indo-European *leudh-, meaning "to grow, to become free."
