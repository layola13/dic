lowbrowism:/ˈloʊˌbraʊɪzəm/| n. |低俗趣味，庸俗化|The pursuit and appreciation of lowbrow culture, often characterized by a lack of intellectual or artistic merit and a preference for mass-produced entertainment. It can also refer to the attitude or ideology that values such culture.  Lowbrowism is often contrasted with highbrowism (the appreciation of high culture) and midbrowism (an appreciation of a middle ground between high and low culture).|Example: The rise of reality television is often cited as an example of lowbrowism in modern entertainment.|近义词：vulgarity, philistinism, popularism|反义词：highbrowism, intellectualism
