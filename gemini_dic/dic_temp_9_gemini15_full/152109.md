pathogenetic:/ˌpæθədʒəˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|致病的；病原的 | Relating to or causing the origination or development of a disease.  Pathogenetic mechanisms are those processes that lead to the development of a disease.  The term often refers to the specific biological mechanisms by which a pathogen causes disease. | Example: The study focused on the pathogenetic mechanisms of the new virus.  这项研究重点关注这种新型病毒的致病机制。


The pathogenetic effects of smoking are well documented. 吸烟的致病作用已被充分证明。


近义词：disease-causing, pathogenic, etiologic

反义词：benign, harmless, innocuous

词性：形容词
