intervisits:/ˌɪntərˈvɪzɪts/ | n. |互访，互相拜访 |The word "intervisits" refers to reciprocal visits, where two or more individuals or groups visit each other. It emphasizes the mutual nature of the visits.  |例句：The two research teams engaged in a series of intervisits to share their findings and collaborate on future projects.  两个研究团队进行了一系列互访，以分享他们的研究成果并在未来的项目中进行合作。|近义词：mutual visits, reciprocal visits, exchanges |反义词：one-way visits, unilateral visits |名词
