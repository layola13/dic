emulsifying:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪɪŋ/| v. |乳化；使乳化|The process of transforming two immiscible liquids (like oil and water) into an emulsion, a stable mixture of fine droplets of one liquid dispersed throughout the other. This is typically achieved using an emulsifier.|例句：The chef is emulsifying the oil and vinegar to make a vinaigrette.  厨师正在将油和醋乳化以制作调味汁。

例句：The machine is designed for efficiently emulsifying large volumes of liquid. 这台机器的设计是为了高效地乳化大量的液体。

近义词：emulsification, mixing

反义词：separating,stratifying
