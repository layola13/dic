mystifies: /ˈmɪstɪfaɪz/ | vt. | 使困惑；使费解 | To bewilder or puzzle someone completely; to make something difficult to understand. | 例句：The magician's trick mystified the audience. 魔术师的戏法使观众困惑不解。  The complexity of the problem mystifies even experts.  这个问题的复杂性甚至让专家也感到困惑。| 近义词：confuses, puzzles, baffles, perplexes | 反义词： clarifies, explains, elucidates | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The strange symbols mystify me. = 这些奇怪的符号让我困惑。
This complex equation mystifies many students. = 这个复杂的方程式让许多学生困惑。

一般过去时:
The sudden disappearance mystified the police. = 突然的消失让警方困惑不解。
The ancient ruins mystified the archaeologists. = 古代遗迹让考古学家困惑不解。


现在完成时:
The scientist's discovery has mystified the scientific community. = 科学家的发现让科学界困惑不解。
This phenomenon has mystified researchers for decades. = 这种现象几十年来一直让研究人员困惑不解。


过去完成时:
The unexpected results had mystified the researchers before they found the explanation. = 意想不到的结果在他们找到解释之前让研究人员困惑不解。
The clues had mystified the detectives until a breakthrough occurred. = 线索让侦探困惑不解，直到取得突破。


将来时:
The new technology will mystify many people at first. = 新技术一开始会让很多人困惑。
This advanced concept will mystify those without a strong background in physics. = 对于物理学基础薄弱的人来说，这个高级概念会让他们困惑。


虚拟语气:
If only I knew the answer, I wouldn't be so mystified. = 如果我知道答案就好了，我就不会这么困惑了。
I wish the instructions weren't so mystified. = 我希望说明书不要这么令人费解。


被动语态:
Many people are mystified by the government's decision. = 许多人对政府的决定感到困惑。
The audience was mystified by the illusionist's performance. = 观众被魔术师的表演弄糊涂了。


疑问句:
What mystifies you about this problem? = 你对这个问题什么感到困惑？
What aspects of the theory mystify you the most? = 理论的哪些方面让你最困惑？


否定句:
The explanation didn't mystify me; it was quite clear. = 解释并没有让我困惑；它很清楚。
The simple instructions didn't mystify the children. = 简洁的说明没有让孩子们困惑。
