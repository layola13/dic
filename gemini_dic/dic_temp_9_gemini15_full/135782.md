nonprogressive:/ˌnɒnprəˈɡresɪv/ | adj. | 非持续性的，非进行式的 |  Describes verbs that do not typically occur in progressive (continuous) tenses. These verbs often describe states of being, having, or believing, rather than actions in progress.  They usually express a state, condition, or relationship that exists rather than an activity in progress. |The house is nonprogressive in its decay. 这房子正在缓慢地腐朽。|  static, unchanging | progressive, continuous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He understands the problem. 他理解这个问题。
She owns a car. 她拥有一辆车。


一般过去时:
I knew the answer. 我知道答案。
They believed him. 他们相信他。


现在完成时:
I have known her for years. 我认识她很多年了。
He has owned that house since 2000. 他自2000年以来就拥有那栋房子。


过去完成时:
I had known him before the incident. 我在事故发生前就认识他了。
She had owned several businesses before retiring. 她退休前拥有过几家公司。


将来时:
I will understand eventually. 我最终会理解的。
She will own her own business one day.  有一天她将拥有自己的公司。


虚拟语气:
If I knew the answer, I would tell you. 如果我知道答案，我会告诉你的。
If she owned a car, she would drive there. 如果她有一辆车，她就会开车去那里。


被动语态:
The problem is understood. 这个问题被理解了。
The car is owned by her. 这辆车是她的。


疑问句:
Do you understand? 你明白吗？
Does she own a property? 她拥有房产吗？


否定句:
I don't understand. 我不明白。
She doesn't own a car. 她没有车。
