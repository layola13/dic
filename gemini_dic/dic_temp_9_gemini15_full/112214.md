loopiness:/ˈluːpinis/| adj.|古怪；不稳定；反复无常；混乱|The quality of being loopy; characterized by eccentricity, instability, or erratic behavior. It suggests a lack of seriousness or a whimsical, unpredictable nature.  |例句：His loopiness made him a fascinating, if unreliable, friend.  他的古怪使他成为一个迷人但不可靠的朋友。  The loopiness of the plot made the movie difficult to follow.  剧情的混乱使这部电影难以理解。|近义词：eccentricity, craziness, oddity, whimsicality |反义词：seriousness, stability, predictability, rationality
