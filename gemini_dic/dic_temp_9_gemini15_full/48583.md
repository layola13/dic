defeatism: /ˈdiːfiːtɪzəm/ | n. |失败主义；认输主义| The belief that defeat is inevitable; a feeling of hopelessness and resignation in the face of difficulty.  It often involves a lack of effort or initiative due to a pessimistic outlook. | Example:  The team's defeatism was evident in their lackluster performance.  球队在他们无精打采的表现中显露出了失败主义。


The general feeling of defeatism among the soldiers was palpable. 士兵们普遍存在的失败主义情绪是显而易见的。

|近义词：pessimism, hopelessness, despair, resignation|反义词：optimism, hopefulness, confidence, determination|
