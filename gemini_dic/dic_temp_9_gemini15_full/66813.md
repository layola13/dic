expansion:/ɪkˈspænʃən/ | n. |扩张；膨胀；扩展；详述 |The process of becoming or making something larger or more extensive.  It can refer to physical growth, increase in size, or the development of something into a more complex or detailed form. |例句：The rapid expansion of cities has led to many environmental problems. 城市的快速扩张导致了许多环境问题。

The expansion of the universe is a fascinating topic in astrophysics. 宇宙的膨胀是天体物理学中一个引人入胜的话题。


近义词：growth, enlargement, extension, development, elaboration |反义词：contraction, reduction, shrinkage

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is planning an expansion into new markets. = 公司正计划向新市场扩张。
The balloon expands as it is filled with air. = 气球充气后膨胀。

一般过去时:
The metal expanded when heated. = 金属受热膨胀。
The business experienced rapid expansion in the 1990s. = 这家企业在20世纪90年代经历了快速扩张。

现在完成时:
The city has expanded significantly in recent years. = 近年来，这座城市已经显著扩张。
My understanding of the subject has expanded considerably. = 我对这个主题的理解已经大大扩展。

过去完成时:
The bridge had expanded due to the intense heat. = 由于酷暑，桥梁已经膨胀了。
By the time the meeting ended, the discussion had expanded to include many new points. = 到会议结束时，讨论已经扩展到包括许多新的观点。

将来时:
The company will expand its operations to include online sales. = 公司将扩大其业务，包括在线销售。
The population is expected to expand dramatically in the coming decades. = 预计未来几十年人口将急剧增长。

虚拟语气:
If the economy expanded more rapidly, unemployment would fall. = 如果经济增长更快，失业率就会下降。
I wish my knowledge of the subject had expanded more quickly. = 我希望我对这个主题的知识增长得更快一些。

被动语态:
The factory was expanded to increase production capacity. = 工厂扩建是为了提高生产能力。
The theory has been expanded upon by several other researchers. = 该理论已被其他几位研究人员进一步阐述。

疑问句:
How much will the expansion of the factory cost? = 工厂扩建要花多少钱？
What are the potential drawbacks of this expansion? = 这次扩张的潜在缺点是什么？

否定句:
The company did not expand its operations last year. = 公司去年没有扩大业务。
The metal did not expand as much as expected. = 金属的膨胀没有预期那么多。
