gawker:/ˈɡɔːkər/| n. |爱看热闹的人，看客 | A gawker is a person who stares openly and rudely at something or someone. They are often curious and sometimes intrusive.  |Example: The gawkers lined the street, eager to catch a glimpse of the celebrity.  看热闹的人挤满了街道，渴望瞥见这位名人的一面。|近义词：onlooker, rubbernecker, sightseer |反义词：participant, insider


USAGE->

He gawks at the accident scene. 他盯着事故现场看。
She gawks at the beautiful painting. 她盯着那幅美丽的画看。
