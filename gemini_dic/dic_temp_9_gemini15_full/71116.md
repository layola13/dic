fishery:/ˈfɪʃəri/| n.|渔业；渔场|The practice or industry of catching fish, or the places where fish are caught.  It encompasses various aspects, from fishing itself to processing and selling the fish.|例句：The local fishery is struggling due to overfishing. 当地渔业因过度捕捞而苦苦挣扎。|例句：The government is investing in sustainable fishery practices. 政府正在投资可持续渔业实践。|近义词：fishing industry, fish farm|反义词：N/A
