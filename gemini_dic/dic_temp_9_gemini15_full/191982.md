scorecards:/ˈskɔːrkɑːrdz/ | n. |记分牌；成绩单；评估报告 |A scorecard is a record of points scored in a game or competition, or a summary of performance or achievements. It can be used in various contexts, from sports to business. |例句：The scorecard showed the home team was winning by a significant margin. 记分牌显示主队以显著优势领先。

The teacher used a scorecard to track each student's progress.老师用记分卡追踪每个学生的进步。

近义词：report card, performance evaluation, assessment, summary |反义词：None
