papule:/ˈpɑpjʊl/| n. |丘疹，小疹|A small, raised bump on the skin.  Papules are usually less than one centimeter in diameter and may be red, pink, or flesh-colored. They are often caused by inflammation or infection. |Example: The rash consisted of numerous small, red papules.  疹子由许多细小的红色丘疹组成。|近义词：nodule, pimple, vesicle|反义词：macule (flat lesion)
