non-explanatory: /ˌnɒnɪkˈsplænətəri/ | adj. | 不解释的；不明确的；无解释的 | Not providing an explanation; unclear or not readily understood.  | Example: The instructions were non-explanatory, leaving me confused about how to proceed.  指令含糊不清，让我不知道该如何继续。 | 近义词：unclear, ambiguous, obscure, vague, cryptic | 反义词：explanatory, clear, explicit, lucid
