extenuation:/ɪkˌstɛnjuˈeɪʃən/| n. |减轻；情有可原；宽恕；辩解|The act of making something seem less serious or less bad; a partial excuse.  It refers to lessening the seriousness or gravity of an offense or fault. |例句：The judge considered extenuating circumstances before sentencing.  法官在判刑前考虑了减轻处罚的情节。

例句：Her youth was offered as an extenuation for her crime.  她的年轻被作为她犯罪的减轻处罚理由。


近义词：mitigation, palliation, excuse, justification


反义词：aggravation, exacerbation


词性：名词
