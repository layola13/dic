preexamining:/ˌpriːɪɡˈzæmɪnɪŋ/| v.|预先审查；预先检查|The process of examining something before a formal examination or assessment.  It involves a preliminary review to identify potential issues or areas needing further attention.|例句：The committee is preexamining the proposals before the final vote. 委员会在最终投票前正在预先审查这些提案。


例句：The professor preexamined the students' papers to identify any major flaws. 教授预先检查了学生的论文，以找出任何主要缺陷。


近义词：pre-screening, preliminary review, initial assessment


反义词：post-examining, final review


词性：动词
