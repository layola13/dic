dowery:/ˈdaʊəri/| n. |嫁妆；陪嫁物 |Property or money brought by a bride to her husband on their marriage.  |例句：In some cultures, a large dowry is expected. 在某些文化中，人们期望有丰厚的嫁妆。|例句：Her dowry included a substantial sum of money and several valuable paintings. 她的嫁妆包括一大笔钱和几幅珍贵的画作。|近义词：bridewealth (反义词), marriage portion |反义词：bridewealth (男方给女方的彩礼)


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The family provides a generous dowry for their daughter. = 这家人为他们的女儿提供了丰厚的嫁妆。
A large dowry is still expected in some parts of the world. = 在世界某些地区仍然期望有丰厚的嫁妆。


一般过去时:
Her dowry consisted of land and livestock. = 她的嫁妆包括土地和牲畜。
The bride's family gave a significant dowry to the groom's family. = 新娘的家人给了新郎的家人一大笔嫁妆。


现在完成时:
They have received a substantial dowry from the bride's family. = 他们已经收到了新娘家丰厚的嫁妆。
The dowry has been used to purchase a new home. = 嫁妆已被用来购买新房。


过去完成时:
The dowry had already been transferred before the wedding. = 婚礼前嫁妆就已经转移了。
It had been agreed that the dowry would include several valuable jewels. = 已经约定嫁妆将包括几件珍贵的珠宝。


将来时:
Her dowry will include a house and a car. = 她的嫁妆将包括一套房子和一辆车。
The family will provide a sufficient dowry for their daughter's marriage. = 这家人将为女儿的婚姻提供足够的嫁妆。


虚拟语气:
If she had a larger dowry, she might have married someone richer. = 如果她有更多的嫁妆，她可能会嫁给一个更富有的人。
I wish I had received a dowry when I got married. = 我希望在我结婚时收到嫁妆。


被动语态:

否定句:
