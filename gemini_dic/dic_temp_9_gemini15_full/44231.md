crocodile:/ˈkrɒkədaɪl/|/ˈkrɑːkədaɪl/| n. |鳄鱼|A large reptile that lives in rivers and lakes in warm countries.  They have long powerful jaws and sharp teeth.|例句：The crocodile lurked in the muddy water, patiently waiting for its prey.  鳄鱼潜伏在泥泞的水中，耐心地等待着猎物。

例句：We saw a huge crocodile basking in the sun on the riverbank. 我们看到一条巨大的鳄鱼在河岸上晒太阳。


近义词：alligator (although there are differences)|反义词：None
