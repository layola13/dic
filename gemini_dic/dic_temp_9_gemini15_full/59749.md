ecology:/ɪˈkɒlədʒi/| /iˈkɑːlədʒi/ | n. |生态学；生态环境|The scientific study of the relationships between organisms and their environment.  It encompasses the interactions between living things and their physical surroundings, including the flow of energy and nutrients. |例句：The ecology of the rainforest is incredibly complex and diverse.  热带雨林的生态系统极其复杂多样。

例句：We are studying the ecology of the coral reef. 我们正在研究珊瑚礁的生态。


近义词：environmental science, ecosystem studies| 反义词：N/A | 词性：名词

Ecology studies the interactions between organisms and their environment. 生态学研究生物与其环境之间的相互作用。
The ecology of this region is fragile. 这个地区的生态环境很脆弱。

过去完成时:
