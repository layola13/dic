exporters:/ɪkˈspɔːrtərz/| n.|出口商，出口公司|Those who export goods or services to other countries.  They play a vital role in international trade.|例句：Many small exporters rely on government support to access foreign markets. 许多小型出口商依赖政府支持进入海外市场。|例句：The association of exporters is lobbying for lower tariffs. 出口商协会正在游说降低关税。|近义词：vendors, traders, merchants|反义词：importers
