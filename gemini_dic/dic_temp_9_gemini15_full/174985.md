radicate:/ˈrædɪkeɪt/| vt.|根除；铲除；消灭|To remove or destroy completely; to extirpate.|The government is trying to eradicate poverty. 政府正在努力根除贫困。  We need to eradicate this disease. 我们需要根除这种疾病。|extirpate, eliminate, abolish, remove|tolerate, foster, cultivate, encourage|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government eradicates poverty through various programs. = 政府通过各种计划来根除贫困。
We eradicate weeds regularly in our garden. = 我们定期清除花园里的杂草。


一般过去时:
They eradicated the disease from the region. = 他们从该地区根除了这种疾病。
The farmer eradicated the pests from his field. = 农民根除了田里的害虫。


现在完成时:
Scientists have eradicated smallpox. = 科学家已经根除了天花。
They have eradicated most of the weeds from the garden. = 他们已经从花园里除去了大部分杂草。


过去完成时:
They had eradicated the problem before it spread. = 他们在问题蔓延之前就已经根除了它。
The team had eradicated the virus from the sample. = 团队已经从样本中清除掉了病毒。


将来时:
We will eradicate this problem with new technology. = 我们将用新技术根除这个问题。
They will eradicate the invasive species from the lake. = 他们将从湖中清除入侵物种。


虚拟语气:
If we acted decisively, we could eradicate hunger. = 如果我们果断行动，就能根除饥饿。
I wish we could eradicate all forms of discrimination. = 我希望我们能够根除一切形式的歧视。


被动语态:
Poverty is being eradicated through education and job training. = 贫困正在通过教育和职业培训得到根除。
