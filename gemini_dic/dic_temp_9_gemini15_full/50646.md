derisory:/dɪˈraɪzəri/| adj.|可笑的；微不足道的；不值得重视的|Something that is derisory is so small, unimportant, or bad as to be laughable.  It suggests contempt or scorn for the thing being described. |例句：His salary was derisory. 他的薪水微不足道。

例句：The punishment was derisory and did nothing to deter future offenses.  惩罚微不足道，根本无法阻止今后的违法行为。


近义词：ridiculous, laughable, paltry, trifling, insignificant, contemptible

反义词：significant, substantial, considerable, impressive, important


词性:形容词
