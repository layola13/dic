deerskin:/ˈdɪərs kɪn/| n. |鹿皮|Deer skin is a type of leather made from the hide of a deer. It is known for its softness, suppleness, and durability.  It's often used in clothing, footwear, and accessories. |例句：The gloves were made of soft deerskin. 这副手套是用柔软的鹿皮制成的。

The boots were crafted from supple deerskin. 这双靴子是用柔软的鹿皮制作的。|近义词：leather, hide, buckskin|反义词：无
