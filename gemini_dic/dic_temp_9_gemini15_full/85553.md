hatchettite:/ˈhætʃɪtaɪt/| n.|斧状沥青|A rare, naturally occurring hydrocarbon mineral, dark brown to black in color, with a resinous luster and a hardness of 1-2 on the Mohs scale. It is found in association with other minerals in coal seams and is thought to be formed by the alteration of plant material.  It is brittle and easily fractured.|The hatchettite sample was analyzed for its chemical composition.|近义词：mineral pitch, albertite|反义词：无
