pulverizers:/ˈpʌlvəraɪzərz/| n.|粉碎机；研磨机|Machines used to pulverize or grind materials into a fine powder.  They are used in various industries for processing different materials.|例句：The factory uses powerful pulverizers to grind the raw materials into a fine powder.  这家工厂使用强大的粉碎机将原材料研磨成细粉。

例句：These pulverizers are designed for high-volume processing of grains. 这些粉碎机设计用于大批量加工谷物。


近义词：grinders, crushers, mills|反义词：agglomerators, consolidators|名词
