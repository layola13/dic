coombe:/kuːm/| n.|小谷，峡谷 | A narrow, secluded valley, typically one with a curving or winding shape.  |例句：We walked through a picturesque coombe in the Cotswolds. 我们走过科茨沃尔德风景如画的小谷。
例句：The village nestled in the bottom of the deep coombe. 村庄依偎在深深的小谷底部。|近义词：valley, dell, combe (same word)|反义词：peak, summit, highland
