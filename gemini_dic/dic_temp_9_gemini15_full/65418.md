euphorbia:/juːˈfɔːrbiə/| n. |大戟属植物|Euphorbia is a large and diverse genus of flowering plants in the spurge family, Euphorbiaceae.  They are characterized by their milky sap, often containing toxins, and diverse growth habits, ranging from small herbs to large trees and cacti-like succulents. Many species are cultivated as ornamentals, while others have traditional medicinal uses. However, caution is advised due to the toxic sap.|Example:  Many species of euphorbia are popular garden plants due to their unique and interesting foliage. = 许多大戟属植物因其独特而有趣的叶子而成为受欢迎的花园植物。
