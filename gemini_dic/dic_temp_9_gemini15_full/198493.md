shrimplike:/ˈʃrɪmpˌlaɪk/| adj. |像虾的；虾似的|Resembling a shrimp; having characteristics of a shrimp.  The word combines "shrimp" with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance.|例句：The tiny crustacean had shrimplike legs and a translucent body.  这只微小的甲壳类动物长着像虾一样的腿和半透明的身体。  The shrimplike creature scurried across the seabed.  那个像虾一样的生物在海底快速爬行。|近义词：shrimp-shaped, crustacean-like|反义词：whale-like, gigantic
