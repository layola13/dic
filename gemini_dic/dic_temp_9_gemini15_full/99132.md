intercepters:/ˌɪntərˈsɛptərz/| n.|拦截器，截取器|A device or system that intercepts and stops something, such as a missile or a signal.|例句：The air defense system used interceptors to shoot down the incoming missiles.  防空系统使用拦截器击落来袭导弹。
例句：The police installed network interceptors to monitor suspicious activities. 警方安装了网络拦截器来监控可疑活动。|近义词：interceptors, blockers, traps|反义词：transmitters, senders|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system intercepts incoming missiles. = 系统拦截来袭导弹。
Network interceptors monitor online activity. = 网络拦截器监控在线活动。

一般过去时:
The interceptor successfully intercepted the drone. = 拦截器成功拦截了无人机。
They intercepted a coded message. = 他们拦截了一条密码信息。

现在完成时:
The authorities have intercepted several suspicious packages. = 当局已经拦截了几个可疑包裹。
We have intercepted communications between the two groups. = 我们已经拦截了这两个团体之间的通讯。

过去完成时:
The police had intercepted the criminal before he reached the border. = 警方在他到达边境前就将他拦截了。
The intelligence agency had intercepted the communication before it was decoded. = 情报机构在信息被解码前就将其拦截了。

将来时:
The new system will intercept all incoming emails. = 新系统将拦截所有收到的电子邮件。
They will intercept the signal and analyze its content. = 他们将拦截信号并分析其内容。

虚拟语气:
If we had a better interceptor, we could have stopped the attack. = 如果我们有更好的拦截器，我们就能阻止攻击。
It is crucial that the system intercept any unauthorized access attempts. = 至关重要的是，系统必须拦截任何未经授权的访问尝试。

被动语态:
The message was intercepted by the enemy. = 信息被敌人拦截了。
Several suspicious packages are being intercepted by customs officials. = 海关官员正在拦截几个可疑包裹。

疑问句:
Did they intercept the communication? = 他们拦截了通讯吗？

否定句:
The system did not intercept the virus. = 系统没有拦截病毒。
They failed to intercept the package. = 他们未能拦截包裹。
