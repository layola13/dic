hinders:/ˈhɪndərz/| v.|阻碍，妨碍|to create difficulties for (someone or something), resulting in slower progress or less success.|例句：The bad weather hindered our progress. 恶劣的天气阻碍了我们的进展。
例句：Constant interruptions hinder his work.  不断的打断妨碍了他的工作。|近义词：hamper, impede, obstruct, inhibit|反义词：aid, assist, facilitate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The heavy rain hinders our travel plans. = 大雨阻碍了我们的旅行计划。
Noise hinders concentration. = 噪音妨碍集中注意力。

一般过去时:
The storm hindered the rescue operation. = 暴风雨阻碍了救援行动。
Technical problems hindered the launch of the project. = 技术问题阻碍了项目的启动。

现在完成时:
Unexpected delays have hindered the project's completion. = 意想不到的延误已经阻碍了项目的完成。
Numerous obstacles have hindered our efforts. = 许多障碍阻碍了我们的努力。

过去完成时:
A lack of funding had hindered the research team's progress. = 资金不足已经阻碍了研究团队的进展。
Previous failures had hindered their confidence. = 之前的失败已经损害了他们的信心。

将来时:
Further complications will hinder the negotiations. = 进一步的复杂情况将阻碍谈判。
The strike will hinder production. = 罢工将阻碍生产。

虚拟语气:
If the government hadn't hindered the development, the city would be much better now. = 如果政府没有阻碍发展，这个城市现在会好得多。
I wish the bureaucracy didn't hinder my work so much. = 我希望官僚作风不要这么阻碍我的工作。

被动语态:
The project was hindered by unforeseen circumstances. = 该项目因不可预见的情况而受阻。
His career was hindered by his lack of experience. = 他缺乏经验阻碍了他的职业生涯。

疑问句:
What is hindering the progress of the project? = 什么在阻碍项目的进展？

否定句:
Nothing hindered her determination. = 没有什么能阻碍她的决心。
The smooth road did not hinder their journey. = 平坦的道路并没有阻碍他们的旅程。
