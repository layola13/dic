in respect of: /ɪn rɪˈspekt ɒv/ | preposition | 关于；至于；就…而言 |  This preposition indicates the subject or matter being discussed or considered. It is a more formal alternative to "regarding," "concerning," or "with regard to." |

例句：

The company is expanding in respect of its product line.  = 公司正在扩展其产品线。

We need to discuss the contract in respect of its clauses. = 我们需要讨论合同的条款。


近义词：regarding, concerning, with regard to, respecting, as regards


反义词：ignoring, disregarding, neglecting


词性：介词
