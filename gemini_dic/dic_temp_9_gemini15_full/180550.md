regime:/reɪˈʒiːm/| n. |政权；制度；管理方式 |A regime is a system or method of government.  It can also refer to a period of rule or a particular way of organizing something.|例句：The new regime promised to improve the economy. 新政权承诺改善经济。|例句：The hospital has a strict hygiene regime. 这家医院有一套严格的卫生管理制度。|近义词：government, system, administration, rule |反义词：anarchy, chaos


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The current regime implements strict regulations. =  现行政权实施严格的法规。
This regime prioritizes economic growth. = 这个政权优先考虑经济增长。

一般过去时:
The previous regime collapsed after the revolution. =  革命后，之前的政权垮台了。
The old regime was overthrown by the people. = 旧政权被人民推翻了。

现在完成时:
The regime has implemented several reforms. =  该政权已经实施了几项改革。
The regime has been in power for many years. =  该政权执政多年了。

过去完成时:
Before the coup, the regime had enjoyed widespread support. = 政变前，该政权曾享有广泛的支持。
The people had suffered under the oppressive regime for decades. =  人民在压迫性政权下受苦数十年。

将来时:
The new regime will face many challenges. = 新政权将面临许多挑战。
The upcoming elections will determine the future of the regime. =  即将到来的选举将决定该政权的未来。

虚拟语气:

疑问句:
