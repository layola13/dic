scoutmastering:/ˈskaʊtˌmɑːstərɪŋ/| n. |童子军指导员工作；童子军活动|The act or process of being a scoutmaster; the activities and responsibilities of a scoutmaster.  It encompasses training, leading, and mentoring young scouts in various outdoor and life skills.|Example:  His dedication to scoutmastering was evident in the well-trained and confident troop.|近义词：youth leadership, mentoring, guiding|反义词：None specifically; antonyms would relate to the opposite of the actions involved (e.g., neglect, abandonment).|名词
