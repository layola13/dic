mirroring: /ˈmɪrərɪŋ/ | n. |镜像；反映；模仿 | The act of reflecting or imitating something.  It can refer to a literal reflection in a mirror, or a figurative reflection of something in another thing, such as mirroring someone's behavior. |

例句：

The lake provided a perfect mirroring of the surrounding mountains.  湖泊完美地反映了周围的山峦。

His aggressive behavior was a mirroring of his own insecurities. 他的侵略性行为反映了他自身的不安全感。


近义词：reflecting, reflecting, imitating, replicating, copying

反义词：differing, contrasting, diverging
