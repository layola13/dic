misnomer:/ˈmɪsˌnoʊmər/ | n. |误称，名字不当 | A wrong or inaccurate name or designation.  It implies that the name does not accurately reflect the true nature or characteristics of something. |例句：Calling that a "luxury hotel" is a misnomer; the rooms are cramped and the service is terrible.  把那家酒店称为“豪华酒店”是名不副实；房间狭小，服务也很糟糕。|近义词：misnaming, misapplication, misidentification |反义词：accurate designation, apt name
