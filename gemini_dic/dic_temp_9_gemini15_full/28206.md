caprifoliaceous:/ˌkæprifoʊliˈeɪʃəs/| adj.|忍冬科的|Of or relating to the Caprifoliaceae, a family of flowering plants that includes honeysuckle, viburnum, and elderberry.  The plants in this family are characterized by their opposite leaves, often showy flowers, and berries or drupes as fruits.|The honeysuckle is a caprifoliaceous plant known for its fragrant flowers.|近义词：honeysuckle-like (less formal)|反义词：None
