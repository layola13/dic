keystrokes:/ˈkiːstroʊks/ | n. |击键；按键次数 |The number of times a key is pressed on a keyboard.  Keystrokes are often used to measure typing speed or the amount of user interaction with a computer system.  |Example:  The program recorded 1000 keystrokes during the test.  该程序在测试期间记录了1000次击键。


近义词：key presses, button presses
反义词：None (no direct antonym)
