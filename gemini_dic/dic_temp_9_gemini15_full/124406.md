motorbicycling:/ˈmoʊtərbɑɪsɪklɪŋ/| n. |摩托车骑行；摩托车运动|The activity of riding a motorbike.  It can refer to recreational riding, competitive racing, or simply using a motorbike as a mode of transportation.|例句：He enjoys motorbicycling through the countryside. 他喜欢在乡间骑摩托车。|例句：Motorbicycling is a popular sport in many countries. 摩托车运动在许多国家都很流行。|近义词：motorcycling, biking (on a motorbike)|反义词：walking, cycling (on a bicycle)
