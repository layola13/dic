/ˈɛti/ | n. | 礼仪；规矩；礼节 | Etiquette refers to the customary code of polite behavior in society or among members of a particular profession or group.  It encompasses the norms and expectations governing social interactions, including manners, decorum, and proper conduct. |  Good etiquette dictates that you should offer your seat to an elderly person.  良好的礼仪规定你应该把座位让给老人。|  Manners, decorum, protocol | rudeness, impoliteness | 名词
