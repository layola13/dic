rogueing:/ˈroʊɡɪŋ/ | v. | 除去杂草；剔除不良植株 | The process of removing undesirable plants from a crop, typically by hand.  This is done to improve the quality and yield of the main crop. | Example: The farmer spent the afternoon rogueing the wheat field, removing weeds and diseased plants. = 农民花了整个下午在小麦田中除草，清除杂草和病株。|近义词： weeding, thinning, culling |反义词：planting, cultivating
