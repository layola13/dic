rehabilitative: /ˌriːhəˈbɪlɪteɪtɪv/ | adj. | 康复的；矫正的 | Relating to or involving rehabilitation.  The process aims to restore someone to health or normal life through training and therapy after imprisonment, addiction, or illness. |

例句：

The rehabilitative program helped him regain his independence after his accident.  = 康复计划帮助他在事故后恢复了独立生活。

She underwent extensive rehabilitative therapy to recover from her stroke. = 她接受了广泛的康复治疗以从中风中恢复。


近义词： restorative, remedial, recuperative, therapeutic

反义词：destructive, debilitating, worsening


词性：形容词
