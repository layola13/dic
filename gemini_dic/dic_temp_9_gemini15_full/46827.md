daoism:/ˈdaʊɪzəm/| n. |道教，道家哲学|A philosophical and religious tradition of Chinese origin which emphasizes living in harmony with the Tao (道), the natural order of the universe.  It focuses on concepts such as simplicity, naturalness, spontaneity, and living in accordance with the flow of the universe.  Daoism encompasses a wide range of practices, including meditation, qigong, and internal alchemy.|Example:  Daoism emphasizes living in harmony with nature. 道教强调与自然和谐相处。|近义词：Taoism|反义词：无
