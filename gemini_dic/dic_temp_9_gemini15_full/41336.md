corgis:/ˈkɔːrɡiz/ | n. |柯基犬 | A breed of small dog with short legs and a long body.  Corgis are known for their fox-like appearance and playful personalities. They are popular as family pets. |  例句：We have two corgis, one red and one black and white. 我们有两条柯基犬，一条红色的，一条黑白相间的。 |  例句：My neighbor's corgis are always barking at the mailman. 我邻居的柯基犬总是对着邮递员叫。| 近义词：Welsh Corgi (更具体的品种名称) | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑体型更大的犬种，如Great Dane)
