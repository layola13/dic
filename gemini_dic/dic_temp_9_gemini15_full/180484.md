regelation:/ˌriːdʒɛˈleɪʃən/| n. |再冻结；重结冰|The process by which ice, when subjected to pressure, melts and then refreezes when the pressure is released.  This phenomenon is responsible for the movement of glaciers. |例句：Regelation is responsible for the movement of glaciers down slopes. 再冻结是冰川向下坡移动的原因。
例句：The experiment demonstrated the principle of regelation.  实验演示了再冻结的原理。|近义词：refreezing, re-freezing|反义词：melting, thawing|
