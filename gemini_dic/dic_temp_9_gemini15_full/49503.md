demagogies:/ˈdɛməɡɑːdʒiz/| n. |煽动，蛊惑政治|The practice or art of a demagogue; the use of popular prejudices and false claims and promises to gain power.  Demagogies often involve exploiting people's fears and anxieties to rally support.|例句：His speeches were filled with demagogies designed to win over the undecided voters. 他的演讲充满了旨在赢得未决选民的煽动言论。|例句：The rise of demagogies in the country led to political instability.  该国煽动政治的兴起导致政治不稳定。|近义词：incitement, agitation, rabble-rousing, rabble-rouser |反义词：statesmanship, leadership (responsible), reasoned discourse |名词
