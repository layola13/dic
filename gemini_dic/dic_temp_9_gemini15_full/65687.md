evening:/ˈiːvnɪŋ/| n.|夜晚，傍晚|The period of time between sunset and nightfall.|例句：We had a lovely evening together. 我们一起度过了一个美好的夜晚。|近义词：night, dusk, nightfall|反义词：morning, day
