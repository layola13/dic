graveclothes:/ˈɡreɪvˌkloʊðz/| n. |寿衣，裹尸布 |Grave clothes are the garments in which a deceased person is buried.  They often reflect the cultural and religious beliefs of the community. |The ancient Egyptians meticulously prepared elaborate grave clothes for their pharaohs.|近义词：shroud, burial clothes|反义词：everyday clothes
