gutling:/ˈɡʌtlɪŋ/| n.|肠鸣音，咕噜声|The sound of the bowels rumbling.  It is an onomatopoeia,  imitating the sound.|例句：I could hear the gutling in my stomach. 我能听到我肚子里的咕噜声。|近义词：rumbling, gurgling|反义词：silence

The old pipes gutled throughout the night. = 那些老旧的管道整夜都在咕噜咕噜响。
