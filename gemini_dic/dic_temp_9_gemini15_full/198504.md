shrink: /ʃrɪŋk/ | v., n. | 收缩；退缩；精神科医生 |

英文解析:As a verb, to shrink means to become or make something smaller in size, amount, or value. As a noun, it refers to a psychiatrist or psychoanalyst.


例句:

The wool sweater shrank after I washed it. = 洗完之后羊毛衫缩水了。

The company shrank its workforce by 10%. = 公司裁员10%。


近义词: contract, diminish, decrease, reduce, psychiatrist, psychoanalyst


反义词: expand, enlarge, increase, grow


词性: Verb, Noun


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The wool shrinks when washed. = 羊毛洗后会缩水。
He shrinks from responsibility. = 他逃避责任。

一般过去时:
The clothes shrank in the dryer. = 衣服在烘干机里缩水了。
She shrank away from the sight. = 她看到那个景象吓了一跳。


现在完成时:
The economy has shrunk significantly. = 经济已经大幅萎缩。
He has shrunk from his earlier promises. = 他食言了。


过去完成时:
The fabric had shrunk before I could iron it. = 我还没来得及熨烫，布料就已经缩水了。
He had shrunk from the challenge. = 他已经回避了这个挑战。


将来时:
The ice will shrink as the weather warms. = 天气变暖后，冰会融化。
The market share will shrink if we don't innovate. = 如果我们不创新，市场份额将会缩小。


虚拟语气:
If the cloth shrank, I would return it. = 如果布料缩水了，我会退货。
If I shrank from the task, I would regret it. = 如果我逃避这项任务，我会后悔的。


被动语态:
The sweater was shrunk in the wash. = 这件毛衣洗后缩水了。
The image was shrunk to fit the screen. = 图片被缩小以适应屏幕。


疑问句:
Does this fabric shrink? = 这布料会缩水吗？
Will the economy shrink next year? = 经济明年会萎缩吗？


否定句:
The material doesn't shrink. = 材料不会缩水。
He didn't shrink from the difficult task. = 他没有逃避这项艰巨的任务。


PHRASE->

shrink from: 逃避；回避
shrink back: 退缩
shrink to: 缩小到
shrink wrap: 热缩包装
shrinkage: 收缩；缩水
