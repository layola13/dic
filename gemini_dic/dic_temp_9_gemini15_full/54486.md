disinterments:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜːrmənts/| n. |挖掘出土；重新下葬|The act of digging up something that has been buried, especially a body.  This often happens in forensic investigations or when a body needs to be moved to a new location.|例句：The disinterments were conducted under the supervision of a forensic anthropologist.  挖掘工作是在一位法医人类学家的监督下进行的。

The recent disinterments at the cemetery caused a great deal of controversy.  墓地最近的挖掘工作引起了很大的争议。|近义词：exhumations, reburials (in the context of reburial after disinterment)|反义词：burial, interment|词性:名词
