readers:/ˈriːdərz/ | n. |读者，阅读者 |People who read, especially of a particular type of publication or material.  |Example: The magazine has a wide range of readers. 这本杂志有很多不同类型的读者。|近义词：perusers, patrons (of a library)|反义词：writers, authors


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The readers enjoy this author's books. = 读者们喜欢这位作者的书。
Many readers subscribe to this magazine. = 许多读者订阅这本杂志。


一般过去时:
The readers appreciated the author's clear writing style. = 读者们欣赏作者清晰的写作风格。
The readers were captivated by the story. = 读者们被故事迷住了。


现在完成时:
Many readers have finished reading the book. = 许多读者已经读完了这本书。
The readers have expressed their satisfaction with the new format. = 读者们表达了对新格式的满意。


过去完成时:
The readers had already expressed their opinions before the author's response. = 在作者回应之前，读者们已经表达了他们的意见。
By the time the sequel was released, the readers had eagerly anticipated it for years. = 到续集发行的时候，读者们已经期待了好几年了。


将来时:
The readers will appreciate the author's new work. = 读者们将会欣赏作者的新作品。
We believe that the readers will be fascinated by the upcoming chapters. = 我们相信读者们将会被接下来的章节所吸引。


虚拟语气:
If the readers were more critical, the author would improve. = 如果读者们更批判性，作者就会改进。
I wish the readers understood the deeper meaning of the story. = 我希望读者们能理解故事更深层次的含义。


被动语态:
The author's work was well-received by the readers. = 作者的作品受到了读者的欢迎。
The new book is being eagerly awaited by readers worldwide. = 这本新书正受到世界各地读者的热切期待。


疑问句:
Who are the target readers for this publication? = 这份出版物的目标读者是谁？
What kind of readers does this book appeal to? = 这本书吸引什么样的读者？


否定句:
Not all readers agreed with the author's viewpoint. = 并非所有读者都同意作者的观点。
The author failed to engage a wider range of readers. = 作者未能吸引更广泛的读者。
