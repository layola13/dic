词源:
rheumat-  (from Greek *rheuma, "a flow, discharge, flux") + -ological (from Greek -logikos, "relating to the study of")
The term reflects the historical (and sometimes still relevant) association of rheumatic conditions with inflammation and fluid accumulation in joints.
