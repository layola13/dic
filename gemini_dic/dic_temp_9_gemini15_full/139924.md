odinophagia: /ˌoʊdɪnəˈfeɪdʒə/ | n. |吞咽困难| Difficulty in swallowing.  This can be caused by various factors, including neurological disorders, esophageal obstructions, or muscle problems. The condition can range from mild discomfort to severe pain and inability to swallow.  | The patient presented with odynophagia and difficulty breathing.  患者出现吞咽困难和呼吸困难。  | dysphagia, aphagia | easy swallowing  |名词
