semicellulose:/ˌsɛmiˈsɛljuloʊs/| n. |半纤维素|A complex carbohydrate that is a major component of plant cell walls, along with cellulose and pectin. It is less easily digested than cellulose and consists of various pentose and hexose sugars.  |Example:  The researchers analyzed the semicellulose content in different types of wood. 研究人员分析了不同类型木材中的半纤维素含量。


The study focused on the extraction of semicellulose from agricultural residues.  这项研究的重点是从农业残留物中提取半纤维素。|近义词: hemicellulose |反义词:  None (It's a specific type of carbohydrate)
