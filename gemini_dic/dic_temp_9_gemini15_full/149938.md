palliator: /ˈpæliˌeɪtər/ | n. |缓解剂；减轻痛苦者 |  A person or thing that palliates; something that relieves or mitigates pain or distress.  |例句：The medication served as a palliator for her chronic pain.  这种药物减轻了她长期的疼痛。

例句：He was a palliator, offering comfort to the dying. 他是一位缓解痛苦的人，为垂死之人提供安慰。


近义词：mitigant, alleviator, soother, reliever | 反义词：aggravator, intensifier


词源:  From Latin *palliāre "to cloak, cover," from pallium "cloak." The sense development is from "covering" to "covering up" (a problem or symptom) to "alleviating" or "relieving."
