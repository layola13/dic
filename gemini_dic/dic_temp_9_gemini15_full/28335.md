caramelize:/ˈkærəməlaɪz/| vt.|焦糖化；使…变成焦糖色|To heat (sugar) until it melts and turns brown, often with a distinctive flavor.|例句：I caramelized the onions until they were golden brown.我把洋葱焦糖化直到它们变成金棕色。

例句：The chef caramelized the apples to create a delicious dessert.  厨师将苹果焦糖化以制作美味的甜点。


近义词：糖化, 焙糖|反义词：无|词性：动词
