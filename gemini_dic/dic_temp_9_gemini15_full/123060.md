monetization: /ˌmʌnɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |货币化；获利；赚钱 |The process of converting something into a source of income or profit.  It involves finding ways to generate revenue from an asset, product, service, or platform. | 例句：The monetization of the app through in-app purchases was very successful.  应用程式透过应用程式内购而实现货币化非常成功。| 例句：They are exploring different monetization strategies for their new platform. 他们正在探索其新平台的不同获利策略。| 近义词：commercialization, commercial exploitation, profit generation | 反义词：donation, charity, philanthropy


USAGE->

Their strategy focuses on monetizing user data. = 他们的策略侧重于用户数据的货币化。
