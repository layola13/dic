course:/kɔːrs/|kɔːrs/| n.  v.|课程；过程；一道菜；方向；航向|A series of lectures or lessons on a particular subject; the progress or development of something; a dish served as part of a meal; a direction or route.|例句：He's taking a course in computer programming. 他正在学习计算机编程课程。

例句：The course of the river changed after the flood. 洪水过后，河道改变了。|近义词：class, lesson, program, route, direction|反义词：detour, interruption


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The course runs for ten weeks. = 这门课程持续十周。
This course focuses on writing skills. = 这门课程侧重于写作技巧。

一般过去时:
The course covered a wide range of topics. = 这门课程涵盖了广泛的主题。
I took a cooking course last summer. = 我去年夏天参加了一个烹饪课程。


现在完成时:
I have completed the online course. = 我已经完成了在线课程。
She has taken several courses in marketing. = 她已经参加了几门市场营销课程。


过去完成时:
By the time I finished the course, I had improved my skills. = 当我完成课程时，我的技能已经提高了。
They had completed the course before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前完成了课程。


将来时:
I will take a photography course next semester. = 下个学期我将参加摄影课程。
He will start the course next month. = 他下个月将开始这门课程。


虚拟语气:
If I had taken that course, I would be better at coding. = 如果我上了那门课，我的编码能力就会更好。
I wish I had taken more courses in my college years. = 我真希望我在大学期间上了更多课程。


被动语态:
The course is taught by Professor Smith. = 这门课由史密斯教授教授。
The course materials are available online. = 课程资料可在网上获取。


疑问句:
What course are you taking this semester? = 这个学期你上什么课？
Which course is more difficult? = 哪门课更难？


否定句:
He didn't finish the course. = 他没有完成这门课程。
I haven't taken that course yet. = 我还没上过那门课。


PHRASE->
of course = 当然
in the course of = 在...过程中
in due course = 适时；到时
take a course = 参加课程
follow a course = 遵循路线/方向
