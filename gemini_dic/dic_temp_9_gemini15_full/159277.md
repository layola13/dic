poinsettia:/ˌpɔɪnˈsɛtiə/| n. |一品红，圣诞花 |A poinsettia is a flowering plant with bright red (or sometimes white, pink, or other colors) bracts that are often used as decorations during the Christmas season.  It is native to Mexico. |The poinsettia is a popular Christmas decoration. 一品红是一种流行的圣诞装饰品。|  The bright red poinsettias brightened the room.  鲜红色的圣诞花照亮了房间。|  Christmas flower,  winter rose |  (no direct antonym)
