cotquean:/kɒtˈkwiːən/| n.|絮絮叨叨的女人，爱管闲事的人|A cotquean is a man who meddles in women's affairs, or a woman who is excessively gossipy and interfering.  The word is now largely archaic or used humorously.|例句：He was considered a cotquean for his constant interference in the women's knitting circle. 他因为不断干涉妇女的编织圈而被认为是一个爱管闲事的人。  She, however, was a notorious cotquean, always spreading gossip and meddling in other people's business. 然而，她是一个臭名昭著的絮絮叨叨的女人，总是传播流言蜚语，干涉别人的事。|近义词： busybody, meddler, gossip, chatterbox|反义词：unobtrusive, uninvolved, reserved
