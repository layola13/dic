culturation:/kʌlˈtʃəreɪʃən/ | n. | 文化熏陶；栽培；教化 | The process by which a person or group becomes integrated into a different culture.  It involves learning and adopting the values, beliefs, customs, and behaviors of that culture.  This can be a voluntary process, such as when someone moves to a new country, or it can be imposed, such as during colonization. | Example:  The culturation of immigrant children into the mainstream society often takes several generations.  移民儿童融入主流社会的过程通常需要几代人。 |近义词：enculturation, acculturation, socialization | 反义词：isolation, alienation
