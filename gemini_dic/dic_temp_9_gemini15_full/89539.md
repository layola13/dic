honorably:/ˈɑːnərəbli/ | adj. | 光荣地；荣誉地；体面地 | In a way that brings honor; with honor.  The adjective form of "honor," suggesting something done with integrity, respect, and dignity. It implies a high moral standard and often refers to actions that deserve praise or recognition. | 例句：He served his country honorably. 他光荣地为国效力。

He was honorably discharged from the army. 他光荣地退伍了。


近义词：respectably, respectably, creditably, commendably, laudably | 反义词：dishonorably, disgracefully, shamefully
