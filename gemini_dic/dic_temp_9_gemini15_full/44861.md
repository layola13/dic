crutches:/ˈkrʌtʃɪz/| n.|拐杖；依靠；支撑物|Supports used to aid walking, typically a pair of sticks with a crossbar at the top.  It can also refer to something one relies on excessively. |例句：He used crutches after his leg injury. 他腿受伤后使用了拐杖。

例句：She relied on crutches, both literally and figuratively, to get through the difficult time.  她在艰难时期依靠拐杖，字面意思和比喻意思都有。


近义词：supports, aids, props|反义词：None directly opposite, but  "independence" or "self-reliance" could be considered antonyms in the figurative sense.|名词
