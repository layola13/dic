prescribe:/prɪˈskraɪb/ | vt. | 规定；开处方；指示；推荐 | to order or recommend the use of a medicine, treatment, or course of action | The doctor prescribed antibiotics for the infection. 医生开了抗生素来治疗感染。 | recommend, order, direct | forbid, countermand | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor prescribes medication for her high blood pressure. = 医生给她开了高血压的药。
He prescribes regular exercise to improve his health. = 他建议经常锻炼来改善健康。


一般过去时:
The doctor prescribed bed rest for the patient. = 医生为病人开了卧床休息的处方。
She prescribed a strict diet for weight loss. = 她规定了严格的节食减肥方案。


现在完成时:
The doctor has prescribed this medicine many times before. = 医生之前多次开过这种药。
They have prescribed a new treatment plan for the patient. = 他们为病人制定了一个新的治疗方案。


过去完成时:
The doctor had prescribed the medication before the patient's symptoms worsened. = 在病人症状恶化之前，医生已经开了药。
She had prescribed a similar treatment for other patients. = 她之前也为其他病人开过类似的治疗方案。


将来时:
The doctor will prescribe stronger medication if the condition doesn't improve. = 如果病情没有好转，医生将会开更强的药。
He will prescribe a course of physiotherapy after the surgery. = 手术后他会开物理治疗的疗程。


虚拟语气:
If the patient were more compliant, the doctor would prescribe less medication. = 如果病人更配合，医生就会少开药。
I wish the doctor had prescribed a different treatment. = 我希望医生开的不是这种治疗方案。


被动语态:
Medication was prescribed to manage his chronic pain. = 开药是为了控制他的慢性疼痛。
A new treatment plan was prescribed by the specialist. = 专家的新治疗方案被制定出来了。


疑问句:
What medication did the doctor prescribe for the patient? = 医生给病人开了什么药？
What treatment will be prescribed for this condition? = 这种疾病会采取什么治疗方案？


否定句:
The doctor did not prescribe any medication at this time. = 医生目前没有开任何药。
He did not prescribe the recommended dosage. = 他没有开出推荐的剂量。


PHRASE->
prescribe to:  对…开处方;  为…开处方  (This phrase emphasizes the recipient of the prescription)
prescribe for:  为…开处方;  为…规定 (This phrase is very similar to "prescribe to" and often interchangeable)
prescribe against:  禁止;  反对
prescribe a course of:  开…疗程
prescribe a diet: 开饮食疗法
