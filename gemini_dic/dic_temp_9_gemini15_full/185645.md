reverence:/ˈrevərəns/ | n. | 尊敬，敬畏 |  A feeling or attitude of deep respect tinged with awe; veneration.  It often involves a sense of humility and a recognition of something greater than oneself. |  例句：The children showed reverence for their elders. 孩子们对长辈很尊敬。

例句：She felt a deep reverence for the ancient temple. 她对那座古老的寺庙怀有深深的敬畏之情。


近义词：respect, awe, veneration, homage, adoration | 反义词：contempt, scorn, disdain, disrespect | 词性：名词

否定句:

PHRASE->

词源:
From Old French *reverence (modern French révérence), from Latin reverentia, from revereri "to stand in awe, to fear," from re- "again, back" + vereri "to fear, to reverence."  The root ultimately connects to the Proto-Indo-European *wer- "to speak with respect, reverence."
