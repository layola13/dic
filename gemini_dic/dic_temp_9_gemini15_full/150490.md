panto:/ˈpæntəʊ/| n.|哑剧，童话剧|A pantomime is a type of British musical comedy play, usually performed during the Christmas season, that features slapstick comedy, music, dance, and audience participation. It's often based on a well-known fairy tale or story.|Example:  The children loved the panto; the dame was particularly hilarious. 孩子们很喜欢这部哑剧；那个老妇人的角色尤其搞笑。|近义词：dramatic performance, theatrical production|反义词：serious drama
