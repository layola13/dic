conquers:/ˈkɒŋkərz/ | /ˈkɑːŋkərz/| vt. |征服；战胜；克服|To gain or achieve by overcoming opposition.  It implies a complete victory and often suggests the establishment of control or dominance.  It can refer to physical battles, but also to overcoming challenges, difficulties, or emotions.|例句：The Roman army conquered Gaul. = 罗马军队征服了高卢。

The athlete conquered her fear and competed in the Olympics. = 运动员克服了恐惧，参加了奥运会。

近义词：defeat, overcome, subdue, vanquish, master

反义词：surrender, yield, succumb, fail

词性：动词
