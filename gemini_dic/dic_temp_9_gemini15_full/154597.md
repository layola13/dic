perseverating:/ˌpɜːrsɪvəˈreɪtɪŋ/| adj.|固执的；反复做的；持续不断的|Persisting in or repeatedly performing an action, often to an excessive or undesirable degree.  This usually refers to a repetitive behavior, thought, or speech pattern, often associated with mental health conditions.|例句：His perseverating questions about the meeting frustrated everyone. 他关于会议的反复提问让每个人都很沮丧。

例句：Her perseverating focus on minor details prevented her from completing the project. 她对细枝末节的持续关注妨碍了她完成项目。


近义词：persistent, insistent, repetitive, obsessive|反义词：yielding, compliant, flexible|词性：形容词
