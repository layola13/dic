espousing: /ɪˈspaʊzɪŋ/ | v. | 支持；拥护；提倡 |  To espouse a cause or belief is to publicly support or advocate for it.  It implies a strong commitment and often involves active promotion. | 例句：He's been espousing the benefits of vegetarianism for years. 他多年来一直提倡素食的好处。

She espoused a more liberal approach to education. 她提倡一种更为自由的教育方法。


近义词：advocating, championing, supporting, upholding, promoting | 反义词：opposing, rejecting, condemning, denouncing | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He espouses environmental protection. = 他拥护环境保护。
They espouse a non-violent approach to conflict resolution. = 他们提倡非暴力解决冲突的方法。


一般过去时:
She espoused the cause of women's rights. = 她拥护妇女权利事业。
The politician espoused a populist platform. = 这位政治家支持民粹主义纲领。


现在完成时:
He has espoused this philosophy throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中一直信奉这种哲学。
They have consistently espoused the principles of fairness and justice. = 他们始终坚持公平与正义的原则。


过去完成时:
Before the election, he had espoused different views. = 在选举之前，他曾持有不同的观点。
She had long espoused the idea of world peace. = 她长期以来一直倡导世界和平的理念。


将来时:
He will continue to espouse these ideals. = 他将继续坚持这些理想。
They will likely espouse similar policies in the future. = 他们未来可能会支持类似的政策。


虚拟语气:
If he espoused those beliefs, he would face strong opposition. = 如果他支持那些信仰，他会面临强烈的反对。
I wish he hadn't espoused such a controversial opinion. = 我真希望他不要发表如此有争议的意见。


被动语态:
These ideas were espoused by many intellectuals. = 这些观点被许多知识分子所支持。
A more moderate stance is being espoused by the government. = 政府正在采取更为温和的立场。


疑问句:
What beliefs does he espouse? = 他信仰什么？
Which political party are you espousing? = 你支持哪个政党？


否定句:
He doesn't espouse violence as a solution. = 他不支持暴力作为解决办法。
She didn't espouse the views of the extremist group. = 她不支持极端组织的观点。


PHRASE->
espouse a cause = 支持一项事业
espouse a belief = 信奉一种信仰
espouse a philosophy = 秉持一种哲学
espouse a theory = 支持一种理论
espouse a political ideology = 支持一种政治意识形态
