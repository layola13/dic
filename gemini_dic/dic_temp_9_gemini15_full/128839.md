nightwatchman:/ˈnaɪtwɒtʃmən/| n. |夜警，看守人 |A nightwatchman is a person employed to guard a building or area at night.  They patrol and monitor the premises to prevent theft, vandalism, or other incidents. |Example: The nightwatchman made his rounds every hour. 夜警每小时巡逻一次。 The factory employed a nightwatchman to protect its valuable equipment. 工厂雇佣了一名夜警来保护其宝贵的设备。|近义词：guard, watchman, security guard |反义词：day worker
