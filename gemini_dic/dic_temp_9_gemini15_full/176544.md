re-secure:/ˌriːsɪˈkjʊr/| vt.|重新固定；再次确保；重新获得|To secure again; to make something safe or secure once more.  It implies a previous state of insecurity that has been addressed.|例句：The climber re-secured his rope before ascending the steeper section. 登山者在攀登更陡峭的路段之前重新固定了他的绳索。

例句：The company re-secured the loan after negotiating better terms.  该公司在协商出更好的条件后重新获得了贷款。|近义词：reinforce, safeguard, reaffirm|反义词：jeopardize, endanger, compromise|词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-secure the package before shipping. = 他们在发货前重新固定包裹。
We re-secure the data every night. = 我们每晚都重新确保数据安全。

一般过去时:
He re-secured the door after hearing a noise. = 他听到噪音后重新锁上了门。
She re-secured the funding for her project. = 她为她的项目重新获得了资金。

现在完成时:

We have re-secured our position in the market. = 我们已经重新巩固了我们在市场上的地位。

过去完成时:
He had re-secured the documents before leaving the office. = 他在离开办公室之前已经重新锁好了文件。
She had re-secured the loan before the deadline. = 她在截止日期前已经重新获得了贷款。

虚拟语气:
