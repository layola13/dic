mutters:/ˈmʌtərz/| v.|低声抱怨，咕哝|to speak in a low, indistinct tone, often complaining or grumbling|He muttered something under his breath. 他低声咕哝着什么。|She muttered her discontent to herself. 她低声抱怨着自己的不满。|whispers, grumbles, murmurs|shouts, yells, speaks clearly|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mutters to himself every morning. 他每天早上都自言自语。
She mutters complaints about her job. 她低声抱怨她的工作。


一般过去时:
He muttered a curse under his breath. 他低声咒骂了一句。
She muttered an apology before leaving. 离开前她低声说了句道歉。


现在完成时:
He has muttered about this problem for weeks.  他已经抱怨这个问题好几个星期了。
She has muttered her disapproval of the plan. 她一直在低声表示对这个计划的不赞成。


过去完成时:
He had muttered his objections before the meeting started. 会议开始前他已经低声表达了他的反对意见。
She had muttered something about being tired before falling asleep. 她睡着前低声说了些什么关于自己很累的话。


将来时:
He will mutter his displeasure when he hears the news. 当他听到这个消息时，他会低声表达他的不满。
She will mutter complaints about the long wait. 她会低声抱怨漫长的等待。


虚拟语气:
If he muttered his concerns, someone might have listened. 如果他低声说出他的担忧，或许有人会听。
I wish he hadn't muttered those hurtful words. 我真希望他没有低声说出那些伤人的话。


被动语态:
His complaints were muttered throughout the meeting. 他的抱怨在整个会议期间都被低声表达出来。
The concerns were muttered under their breath by the participants.与会者低声表达了他们的担忧。


疑问句:
What was he muttering about? 他在咕哝些什么？
What is she muttering under her breath? 她低声咕哝着什么？


否定句:
He didn't mutter a word. 他一个字也没说。
She didn't mutter any complaints. 她没有低声抱怨任何事情。
