quasi-Japanese:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˌdʒæpəˈniːz/| adj.|准日本式的，似日本式的|Approximating or resembling Japanese style or characteristics, but not genuinely or fully Japanese.|The restaurant's decor had a quasi-Japanese feel, with paper lanterns and tatami mats, but the menu was entirely Italian.|近义词：pseudo-Japanese, Japanesque|反义词：authentically Japanese


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, and therefore doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".  Its usage is descriptive.

Example 1:  The architecture of the building displayed a quasi-Japanese aesthetic.  这栋建筑的建筑风格展现出一种准日本式的审美。

Example 2:  He attempted a quasi-Japanese garden design, but it lacked the subtlety of true Japanese landscaping. 他尝试设计一个准日本式花园，但它缺乏真正日本园林的精妙之处。
