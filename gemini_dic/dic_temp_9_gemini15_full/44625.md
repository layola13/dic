crowers:/ˈkraʊərz/ | n. |啼叫的动物（尤指公鸡）|The word "crowers" refers to animals, especially roosters, that crow.  It is a collective noun, referring to a group of crowing animals. |例句：The early morning crowers woke the farmer.清晨的啼叫声吵醒了农民。|The crowers filled the air with their noisy calls. 啼叫声充满了空气。|近义词：cockrels, roosters, birds (that crow)|反义词：silent animals, quiet creatures|名词
