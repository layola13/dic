prurigo:/ˈprʊrɪɡoʊ/| n. |皮肤瘙痒症，丘疹性皮肤病| A skin condition characterized by intensely itchy papules (small, raised bumps) without a known cause.  It can be a primary condition or secondary to other dermatological issues. The itching is often severe and can lead to skin damage from scratching. |Example:  The patient presented with prurigo nodularis, characterized by intensely itchy nodules on the arms and legs.  患者出现结节性皮肤瘙痒症，其特征是手臂和腿部出现剧烈瘙痒的结节。|近义词：pruritus, eczema (in some cases)|反义词：None specifically, but conditions without itching could be considered antonyms in a broad sense.
