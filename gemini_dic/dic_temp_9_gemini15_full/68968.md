fathomed:/ˈfæðəmd/ | vt. | 理解；领悟；测量（深度） | to understand something, especially something difficult |  例句：I finally fathomed the meaning of the poem. 我终于理解了这首诗的意思。  例句：The divers fathomed the depth of the lake. 潜水员测量了湖的深度。 |近义词：understand, comprehend, grasp, figure out |反义词：misunderstand, misconceive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I fathom your concerns. = 我理解你的担忧。
She fathoms the complexities of the situation. = 她理解情况的复杂性。

一般过去时:
They fathomed the mystery. = 他们解开了谜团。
He fathomed the depth of the ocean. = 他测量了海洋的深度。

现在完成时:
I have fathomed the solution to the problem. = 我已经找到了问题的解决办法。
We have fathomed the secrets of the universe. = 我们已经理解了宇宙的奥秘。


过去完成时:
She had fathomed the trick before anyone else. = 在其他人之前，她就看穿了这个把戏。
They had fathomed the meaning of the ancient inscription. = 他们已经理解了古代铭文的含义。

将来时:
I will fathom the secrets of this code. = 我将破解这个密码的秘密。
Scientists will fathom the mysteries of space. = 科学家们将解开宇宙的奥秘。


虚拟语气:
If I could fathom her motives, I would. = 如果我能理解她的动机就好了。
I wish I could fathom the meaning of life. = 我希望我能理解生命的意义。

被动语态:
The problem was finally fathomed. = 这个问题最终得到了解决。
The depth of the well was fathomed by the explorers. = 探险家们测量了井的深度。

疑问句:
Can you fathom the reason for his behavior? = 你能理解他行为的原因吗？
Have they fathomed the cause of the accident? = 他们已经弄清楚事故的原因了吗？

否定句:
I cannot fathom his sudden anger. = 我无法理解他突然发怒的原因。
She could not fathom the complexity of the situation. = 她无法理解情况的复杂性。


PHRASE->
fathom out = 弄清楚，理解
fathom something out = 理解...
fathom the depth of = 测量...的深度
beyond my ability to fathom = 我无法理解
