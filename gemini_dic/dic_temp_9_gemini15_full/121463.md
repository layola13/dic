misjudges:/ˌmɪsˈdʒʌdʒɪz/| v.|误判，错估|To make a wrong judgment about; to misunderstand the character or qualities of (someone or something).|例句：He misjudged the distance and crashed his car. 他误判了距离，导致车祸。|例句：The manager misjudged the employee's abilities, leading to his demotion. 经理误判了员工的能力，导致他被降职。|近义词：miscalculate, underestimate, misinterpret|反义词：judge correctly, assess accurately


USAGE->
