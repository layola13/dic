handicapped's: /ˈhændɪkæptz/ | n. | 残疾人的；残障人士的 |  The possessive form of "handicapped," referring to something belonging to or associated with handicapped people.  It indicates ownership or relationship.  The term "handicapped" itself is increasingly considered outdated and potentially offensive;  more appropriate and respectful terms include "person with a disability" or "disabled person". | Example: The handicapped's needs were carefully considered in the building's design.  残疾人的需求在建筑设计中得到了仔细考虑。|近义词:  disabled person's, person with a disability's | 反义词:abled person's | 名词
