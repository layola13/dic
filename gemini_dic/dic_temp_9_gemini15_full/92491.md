hypothecium:/ˌhaɪpəˈθiːʃəm/| n. |菌盖下层，子囊下层 |The hypothecium is the lower layer of a fungal ascoma, typically composed of sterile hyphae. It provides support and protection for the developing asci and ascospores.  |Example: The hypothecium of this species is dark brown and densely packed. 这个物种的子囊下层是深棕色的，并且非常密集。|近义词：subhymenium (有时可互换使用，但含义略有不同)|反义词：N/A
