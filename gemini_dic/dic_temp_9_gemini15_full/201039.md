slopshop:/ˈslɒpʃɒp/ | n. |廉价服装店；旧货店 |A slopshop is a shop that sells cheap or second-hand clothing and other goods, often of poor quality.  It's often associated with a place where discarded or unwanted items are sold. |例句：The sailors frequented the slopshop near the docks for inexpensive clothing. 水手们经常光顾码头附近的廉价服装店购买便宜的衣服。

The slopshop offered a wide variety of used clothes at bargain prices. 这家旧货店以低廉的价格提供各种各样的旧衣服。|近义词：second-hand shop, thrift store, junk shop |反义词：boutiques, high-end stores |名词
