congregationalist:/ˌkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪʃənəlɪst/| n. adj.|公理会教徒；公理会派的|A member or adherent of a Congregational church.  A Congregationalist believes in the autonomy of the local church and the authority of the Bible.  |例句：Many Congregationalist churches are known for their emphasis on community involvement. 许多公理会教堂以其对社区参与的重视而闻名。


例句：He is a devout Congregationalist and attends services every Sunday. 他是一位虔诚的公理会教徒，每周日都参加礼拜。


近义词：Nonconformist (in a historical British context), Independent (in reference to church governance)


反义词：None directly antonymous, but terms representing opposing church governance structures could be considered (e.g., hierarchical, episcopal).


词性:名词，形容词
