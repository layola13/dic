pupilship:/ˈpjuːpəlʃɪp/ | n. |学生身份；学徒身份；受教育时期|The state or period of being a pupil; apprenticeship.  It refers to the time spent as a student learning under a teacher or master, often involving practical training.|例句：His pupilship under the renowned sculptor shaped his artistic style.  他的学徒生涯在著名雕刻家的指导下塑造了他的艺术风格。

例句：Her pupilship at the prestigious music academy lasted for five years.  她在著名的音乐学院学习了五年。


近义词：apprenticeship, studentship, traineeship | 反义词：mastership, instructorship


词性：名词
