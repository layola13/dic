matchmaker:/ˈmætʃˌmeɪkər/ | n. |媒人；促成者 | A person who arranges marriages or other relationships, often romantically.  It can also refer to something that brings things together or facilitates a connection. |例句：She's a very successful matchmaker, having arranged numerous marriages. 她是一位非常成功的媒人，促成了许多婚姻。

例句：The online dating site acted as a matchmaker, connecting singles with compatible partners. 这个在线约会网站充当了媒人的角色，将单身人士与合适的伴侣联系起来。


近义词：marriage broker, intermediary, go-between, facilitator


反义词：obstructor, disrupter
