hyperspherical:/ˌhaɪpərˈsfɪərɪkəl/| adj. |超球面的，多维球面的|  Relating to or having the properties of a hypersphere; existing in or involving more than three dimensions.  A hypersphere is a generalization of a sphere to higher dimensions.  For example, a circle is a 1-dimensional hypersphere, a sphere is a 2-dimensional hypersphere, and a hypersphere in four dimensions is a 3-dimensional hypersphere.|The researchers used hyperspherical coordinates to model the system.  研究人员使用超球坐标对系统进行建模。| n-dimensional, multidimensional |planar, linear
