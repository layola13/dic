litchi:/ˈliːtʃiː/| n. |荔枝|A small, round fruit with a rough, red skin and sweet, white flesh.  It is native to southern China and is grown in many tropical and subtropical regions. |例句：I bought some fresh litchis at the market. 我在市场上买了一些新鲜的荔枝。|例句：The litchi tree produced a bountiful harvest this year. 今年荔枝树结了丰收。|近义词：lychee (alternative spelling)|反义词:  (No direct antonym,  perhaps "apple" or another fruit as a contrasting example)|名词
