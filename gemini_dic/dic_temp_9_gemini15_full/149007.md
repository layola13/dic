ovules:/ˈɒvjuːlz/| n.|胚珠|A small structure within the ovary of a seed plant that contains the female gametophyte (the egg cell) and develops into a seed after fertilization.  Ovules are essential for sexual reproduction in plants.|Example: The flower's ovules were fertilized, resulting in the development of seeds.  这朵花的胚珠受精后，长成了种子。|近义词：seed primordia|反义词：pollen (though not a direct antonym, pollen is the male gamete involved in fertilization, contrasting with the female ovule)|名词
