crossword:/ˈkrɒsˌwɜːrd/ /ˈkrɑːsˌwɜːrd/| n. |纵横字谜；填字游戏 |A crossword puzzle is a word puzzle where you fill in the blanks of a grid with words that fit the clues.  |例句：I enjoy solving crossword puzzles in the newspaper. 我喜欢解报纸上的纵横字谜。

例句：She completed the crossword in under ten minutes. 她十分钟内完成了填字游戏。


近义词：word puzzle, word game |反义词：无


词源:
The word "crossword" is a compound word, combining "cross" and "word".  "Cross" refers to the intersecting nature of the words in the grid, while "word" refers to the words themselves.  The first known use of the term dates back to the late 19th century.
