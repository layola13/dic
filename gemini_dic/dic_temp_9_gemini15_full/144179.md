outshrilling: /ˌaʊtˈʃrɪlɪŋ/ | v. |  大声喧哗超过；盖过…的声音 | To surpass in shrillness or loudness; to outdo in shrieking or screaming. | 例句：The child's laughter outshrilled the birdsong.  孩子的笑声盖过了鸟鸣声。  The singer outshrilled her rival in the final crescendo.  在最后的强音部分，这位歌手的声音盖过了她的对手。 | 近义词：outcry, exceed, surpass | 反义词：subdue, quieten
