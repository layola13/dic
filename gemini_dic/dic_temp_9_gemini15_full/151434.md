parodying:/ˈpærədiɪŋ/| v.|模仿，戏仿|Imitating the style of someone or something else, typically in a humorous or satirical way.  It involves copying the characteristics of the original work, but with a twist or exaggeration to create a comedic or critical effect.|例句：He was parodying the president's speech. 他在模仿总统的演讲。|例句：The comedian's act consisted of parodying popular movies.  这位喜剧演员的表演包括模仿热门电影。|近义词：mimicking, satirizing, lampooning |反义词：copying seriously, honoring

疑问句:
