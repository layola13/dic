redisinfect:/ˌriːdɪsɪnˈfɛkt/| vt.|再次消毒；重新消毒|To disinfect again or anew.  This involves the process of cleaning and sterilizing a surface or area a second time, typically after a prior disinfection attempt has been deemed insufficient or to maintain a high level of hygiene.|例句：The operating room was redisinfected after the surgery. 手术后，手术室进行了再次消毒。

例句：We had to redisinfect the entire kitchen after the leak.  漏水之后，我们不得不重新消毒整个厨房。

近义词：re sterilize, sanitize again, disinfect anew
反义词：contaminate, pollute
