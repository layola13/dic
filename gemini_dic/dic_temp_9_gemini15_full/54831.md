dispetal:/dɪsˈpetəl/ | n. |  离瓣的；散瓣的 |  Referring to a flower with petals that are separate and not fused together.  This term is primarily used in botany and describes a characteristic of flower morphology. | Example:  The rose has dispetalous flowers, while the lily has separate petals.  玫瑰花是离瓣花的，而百合花的花瓣是分开的。|近义词: polypetalous, free-petalled | 反义词: gamopetalous, sympetalous | 名词
