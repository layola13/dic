lacquerer:/ˈlækərər/| n. |漆工，漆器制作人 |A lacquerer is a person who applies lacquer to objects, often for decorative or protective purposes. They may work with various types of lacquer and techniques, creating finished pieces like furniture, bowls, or other items.  |The skilled lacquerer carefully applied several coats of vibrant red to the wooden bowl.  这位熟练的漆工小心地为木碗涂上了几层鲜艳的红色。|painter, finisher |(no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with someone who damages lacquer)|名词
