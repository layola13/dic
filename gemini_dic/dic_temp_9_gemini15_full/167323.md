pretranslates:/ˌpriːˈtrænzleɪts/| v.|预先翻译|To translate something before it is needed or used.  This often involves translating something ahead of time for a future project or event.  The act of pre-translating ensures that materials are ready when required, reducing delays.|例句：The conference organizers pretranslated all the presentations to ensure smooth proceedings.  会议组织者预先翻译了所有演示文稿，以确保会议顺利进行。


例句：The team pretranslated the software interface for the upcoming international launch. 团队预先翻译了软件界面，以便即将到来的国际发布。


近义词：translate in advance, advance translate


反义词：post-translate


词性:动词


USAGE->
