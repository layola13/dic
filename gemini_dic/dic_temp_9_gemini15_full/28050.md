capelin:/ˈkæpəlɪn/ | n. |  北极鱼，青鱼 | A small, silvery fish of the smelt family, found in the North Atlantic and Arctic Oceans, important as a food source for many seabirds and marine mammals, and also commercially harvested. | Example: Capelin are a crucial part of the food web in the North Atlantic.  北极鱼是大西洋北部食物链的重要组成部分。  |近义词：smelt (though not a perfect synonym as capelin is a specific type of smelt) |反义词：无
