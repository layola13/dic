pontoon:/ˈpɒntuːn/ /pɒnˈtuːn/ | n. | 浮桥； pontoon bridge 浮桥 | A floating structure used as a support, especially for a bridge or pier.  Pontoons are often used to create temporary bridges or floating docks. They can be made of various materials, including wood, metal, or concrete. | Example: The army built a pontoon bridge across the river.  军队在河上架起了一座浮桥。  The lake was dotted with pontoons used for fishing. 湖面上点缀着用于钓鱼的浮桥。|近义词：float, raft, ferry |反义词：bridge (in the sense of a permanent structure)
