preferring: /prɪˈfɜːrɪŋ/ | v. | 更喜欢；偏爱 |  present participle of prefer |

英文解析:The present participle of the verb "prefer," indicating a continuous or ongoing action of liking something better than something else.  It describes a state of favoring one option over another.

例句:

He is preferring to work from home these days. = 这些天他更喜欢在家工作。

She is preferring tea to coffee this afternoon. = 今天下午她更喜欢喝茶而不是咖啡。


近义词:favoring, liking better, opting for

反义词:disliking, rejecting, avoiding


词性:Verb (present participle)
