pseudoprincely: /ˌsuːdoʊˈprɪnsli/ | adj. | 虚假的王子般的；冒牌王子的；装腔作势的 |  Having the appearance or manner of a prince but lacking genuine princely qualities; affectedly princely.  | 例句：He affected a pseudoprincely air, complete with extravagant gestures and pronouncements. 他装腔作势地摆出一副王子的架子，伴随着夸张的手势和声明。


His pseudoprincely lifestyle was funded by dubious dealings. 他的伪王子般的生活方式是由可疑的交易资助的。| 近义词：pretentious, affected, pompous, ostentatious | 反义词：genuine, authentic, humble, unpretentious |


词源:

* **pseudo-**:  From Greek *pseudo-* meaning "false," "sham," or "pretended."
* **princely**: From "prince," ultimately from Old French *prince*, from Latin *princeps* meaning "first man," "chief," or "leader."  This evolved into the meaning of "royal" and "noble."

用法说明：

该词主要用作形容词，用来描述那些行为举止或外表看似王子般高贵，但实际上并非如此的人或事物。它通常带有贬义，暗示着虚伪、做作或炫耀。


例句补充:

* The pseudoprincely decree was quickly forgotten.  那道虚张声势的命令很快就被遗忘了。
* Her pseudoprincely mannerisms grated on the nerves of the other guests. 她装腔作势的举止让其他宾客感到反感。


由于该词较为生僻，没有常见的语法用法范例（如一般现在时、过去时等）。其用法完全取决于形容词的常规使用方式，与其他形容词用法相同。
