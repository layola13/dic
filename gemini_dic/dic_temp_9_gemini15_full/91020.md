hushed:/hʌʃt/ | adj. |寂静的；肃穆的；压抑的 | quiet and still, often because of a serious event or atmosphere.  The hushed tones of the mourners filled the chapel.  |近义词：silent, quiet, still, muted |反义词：noisy, loud, boisterous |形容词


例句：

The library was hushed, everyone absorbed in their books. 图书馆里一片寂静，每个人都全神贯注地看书。

A hushed reverence filled the cathedral during the service.  弥撒期间，一种肃穆的敬畏感充满了教堂。


词源:

源自古英语 *hūsian，意思是“保持安静”。


USAGE->

一般过去时:
