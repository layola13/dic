predesigns:/ˌpriːdɪˈzaɪnz/| n.|预先设计，预先规划|Pre-designed plans or schemes; things planned in advance.|The company's predesigns for the new product line were impressive.|近义词：preplanning, advance planning|反义词：improvisation, spontaneity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The architect predesigns the building's layout. = 建筑师预先设计了建筑物的布局。
They predesign the marketing campaign for the new product. = 他们为新产品预先设计了营销活动。

一般过去时:
The team predesigned the software interface last year. = 团队去年预先设计了软件界面。
She predesigned the wedding decorations months in advance. = 她提前几个月预先设计了婚礼装饰。
