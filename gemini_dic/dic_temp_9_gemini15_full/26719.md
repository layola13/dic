cadetships:/kəˈdɛtʃɪps/| n.|学员身份；学员职位；军校学习机会|The position or status of a cadet;  an opportunity to train as a cadet, often within a military or similar organization.  It refers to the period of training and the associated rank or role.|例句：He earned a cadetship at the Royal Military Academy. 他获得了皇家军事学院的学员职位。|例句：The government offered several cadetships to promising young students. 政府为有前途的年轻学生提供了几个军校学习机会。|近义词：apprenticeship, traineeship, scholarship (in some contexts)|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept would be a position of established authority or seniority.
