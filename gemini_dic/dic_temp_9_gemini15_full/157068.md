例句：Her pimpish attitude alienated many of her colleagues. 她傲慢的态度疏远了她许多同事。


近义词：flashy, showy, ostentatious, pretentious, swaggering, arrogant

反义词：humble, modest, unassuming, understated


词源:  The word derives from "pimp," a person who procures clients for prostitutes. The "-ish" suffix transforms the noun into an adjective, describing something having the qualities or characteristics of a pimp.  The association with pimps and their often flamboyant and boastful style explains the connotations of the word.
