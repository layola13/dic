carney:/ˈkɑːrni/| n. |嘉年华；狂欢节；骗子；(尤指)街头骗子 |A carnival; a festive celebration, often involving amusement rides, games, and food stalls.  It can also refer to a swindler or con artist, especially one operating in a public place.|例句：The town held a lively carney every year.  这个镇每年都举行一个热闹的嘉年华。  He was a smooth-talking carney, always ready with a new scam. 他是个油嘴滑舌的骗子，总是准备着新的骗局。|近义词：festival, fair, swindler, con man |反义词： honesty, truthfulness
