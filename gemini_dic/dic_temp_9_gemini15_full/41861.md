cosa:/ˈkɔːzə/| n. |事情，东西；原因；[意]事物|a thing; a matter; a cause; something;  [Italian] thing |例句：What's the cosa? = 什么事？ What is the cosa? =这是什么？|近义词：thing, matter, affair, item|反义词：nothing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is the cosa? = 这是什么？
This cosa is important. = 这件事很重要。


一般过去时:
What was the cosa yesterday? = 昨天发生了什么事？
That cosa happened quickly. = 那件事发生得很快。


现在完成时:
What cosa have you done? = 你做了什么事？
I have finished that cosa. = 我已经完成了那件事。


过去完成时:
What cosa had happened before I arrived? = 我到达之前发生了什么事？
The cosa had already been done. = 那件事已经做完了。


将来时:
What cosa will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
That cosa will be difficult. = 那件事将会很难。


虚拟语气:
If I knew the cosa, I would tell you. = 如果我知道那件事，我会告诉你的。
I wish I knew what the cosa was. = 我真希望知道那是什么事。


被动语态:
What cosa was decided? = 决定了什么？
The cosa is being investigated. = 那件事正在调查中。


疑问句:
What cosa is that? = 那是什么？
What cosa are you doing? = 你在做什么？


否定句:
I don't know the cosa. = 我不知道那是什么。
She didn't do the cosa. = 她没有做那件事。
