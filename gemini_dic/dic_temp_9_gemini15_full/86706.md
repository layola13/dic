helmer:/ˈhɛlmər/| n. |海尔默（人名）,舵手|A surname, and sometimes used as a given name;  also, a person who steers a ship or boat (archaic or literary).|例句：He is a Helmer by birth. 他是海尔默家族的后裔。|近义词：skipper, captain (in the sense of a ship's captain), steersman|反义词：passenger


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He helms the ship skillfully. = 他熟练地掌舵。
The captain helms the vessel through the storm. = 船长带领船只度过暴风雨。


一般过去时:
He helmed the boat expertly. = 他熟练地掌舵小船。
The old sailor helmed the ship for many years. = 那位老水手掌舵多年。


现在完成时:
He has helmed many vessels in his career. = 他在他的职业生涯中掌舵过许多船只。
She has helmed the company through difficult times. = 她带领公司度过了艰难时期。


过去完成时:
He had helmed the ship before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他掌舵过那艘船。
They had helmed the project for several months before it was completed. = 在项目完成之前，他们已经负责项目管理好几个月了。


将来时:
He will helm the new project next year. = 他明年将负责领导新项目。
She will helm the ship on its maiden voyage. = 她将在处女航中掌舵。


虚拟语气:
If he helmed the ship, the accident might have been avoided. = 如果他掌舵，事故可能就可以避免。
I wish I had helmed the project earlier. = 我希望我早点负责这个项目。


被动语态:
The ship was helmed by an experienced captain. = 这艘船由一位经验丰富的船长掌舵。
The company is helmed by a strong CEO. = 这家公司由一位强势的首席执行官领导。


疑问句:
Who helmed the ship during the crisis? = 危机期间是谁掌舵的？
Who is helming the project now? = 现在谁负责这个项目？


否定句:
He did not helm the ship during the storm. = 暴风雨期间他并没有掌舵。
She will not helm the project alone. = 她不会独自负责这个项目。


PHRASE->
