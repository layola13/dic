comprise:/kəmˈpraɪz/ | vt. | 包含；构成；组成 | To include or contain; to consist of.  The course comprises three main modules. | The team comprises players from several countries.  | include, constitute, consist of |  separate, divide | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee comprises five members. = 委员会由五名成员组成。
This book comprises ten chapters. = 这本书包含十章。


一般过去时:
The collection comprised antique furniture and paintings. = 这个收藏包括古董家具和绘画。
The army comprised soldiers from many different backgrounds. = 这支军队由来自不同背景的士兵组成。


现在完成时:
The exhibition has comprised a wide range of artistic styles. = 这个展览包含了各种各样的艺术风格。
The project has comprised many long hours of work. = 这个项目包含了大量的长时间工作。


过去完成时:
The report had comprised detailed analysis of the market trends. = 报告已经包含了对市场趋势的详细分析。
The presentation had comprised several key findings. = 陈述包含了几项主要发现。


将来时:
The new curriculum will comprise several new subjects. = 新的课程将包含几门新科目。
The team will comprise experienced professionals. = 这个团队将由经验丰富的专业人士组成。


虚拟语气:
If the team comprised more experienced players, they would win more games. = 如果球队由更有经验的球员组成，他们会赢得更多比赛。
I wish the report comprised more data. = 我希望报告包含更多数据。


被动语态:
The team is comprised of experienced professionals. = 这个团队由经验丰富的专业人士组成。(被动语态更常用)
The book is comprised of ten chapters. = 这本书由十章组成。(被动语态更常用)


疑问句:
What does the package comprise? = 包裹里包含什么？
What will the new team comprise? = 新团队将由谁组成？


否定句:
The course does not comprise any practical work. = 这门课程不包含任何实践工作。
The team was not comprised solely of local players. = 这个团队并非完全由当地球员组成。


PHRASE->
comprise of:  (虽然常用，但语法上不严谨，建议使用comprise直接)
be comprised of:  (被动语态，更常用)
comprise something of: 包含某种…成分
comprise in: 包含于
comprise itself of:  自身由...组成
