episodal:/ˈɛpɪˈsoʊdəl/ | adj. | 插曲的；片段的；一系列事件的 | Relating to or consisting of a series of separate events or episodes.  The adjective describes something that happens in distinct, separate parts or installments, rather than continuously.  | 例句：The show has an episodic structure, with each episode focusing on a different case.  这部剧采用片段式结构，每集都关注一个不同的案例。

The patient experienced episodic memory loss after the accident. 事故后，病人经历了片段性记忆丧失。


近义词：serial, intermittent, sporadic | 反义词：continuous, uninterrupted, constant | 词性：形容词

词源:
From Late Middle English *episodical, from Old French episodical, from Medieval Latin episodicālis, from Late Greek ἐπεισοδιακός (epeisodiakós), from ἐπεισόδιον (epeisodion) "episode," from ἐπί (epí) "upon, on" + εἰσόδιον (eisódion) "entrance, entry," from εἰσιέναι (eisiénai) "to go in."  Ultimately derived from Greek roots meaning "to enter upon."
