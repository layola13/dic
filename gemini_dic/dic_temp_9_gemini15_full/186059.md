rewrite:/ˌriːˈraɪt/ | vt. |改写，重写|To write something again, typically in order to improve it or to make it clearer or more accurate.  |例句：I had to rewrite the essay because it was too long. 我不得不重写这篇文章，因为它太长了。|例句：The editor asked him to rewrite the article to make it more concise. 编辑要求他改写这篇文章，使其更简洁。|近义词：revise, redraft, edit |反义词：originate, compose (in the first instance)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rewrites his emails before sending them. 他在发送电子邮件之前会重写它们。
She rewrites her stories many times before publishing them. 她会在发表故事之前多次重写它们。


一般过去时:
I rewrote my application letter yesterday. 我昨天重写了我的申请信。
They rewrote the script after the first rehearsal. 他们在第一次排练后重写了剧本。


现在完成时:
She has rewritten the chapter three times. 她已经重写了这章三次了。
He has rewritten the entire book. 他已经重写了整本书。


过去完成时:
I had rewritten the report before the meeting. 我在会议之前已经重写了报告。
They had rewritten the song several times before recording it. 他们在录音之前已经重写了这首歌好几次了。


将来时:
I will rewrite the contract tomorrow. 我明天将重写合同。
She will rewrite the article after she gets feedback. 她会在收到反馈后重写文章。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rewrite the whole thing. 如果我有更多时间，我会重写全部内容。
I wish I had rewritten my essay before submitting it. 我真希望我在提交论文前重写过它。


被动语态:
The essay was rewritten by the student. 这篇文章是由学生重写的。
The script is being rewritten for the new production. 剧本正在为新的制作而被重写。


疑问句:
Will you rewrite the letter for me? 你会帮我重写这封信吗？
Has she rewritten the proposal yet? 她已经重写了提案了吗？


否定句:
I did not rewrite the document. 我没有重写这份文件。
He will not rewrite the article again. 他不会再重写这篇文章了。


PHRASE->
rewrite history = 改写历史
rewrite the rules = 改变规则
rewrite the narrative = 改变叙事
