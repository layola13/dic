low-lying: /ˈloʊˌlaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 低洼的；地势低的 | Lying close to the ground or sea level; situated in a low place.  | The low-lying coastal areas are vulnerable to flooding. 低洼的沿海地区容易发生洪水。  The village is situated in a low-lying valley.  这个村庄坐落在一个低洼的山谷里。 | low-level,  depressed | high-lying, elevated | 形容词


USAGE->

The coastal area is low-lying. = 沿海地区地势低洼。
This land is low-lying and prone to flooding. = 这块地势低洼，容易被淹。


一般过去时:
The low-lying fields were flooded after the heavy rain. = 大雨过后，低洼的田地被淹了。

将来时:
The low-lying regions will be most affected by the rising tides. = 低洼地区将受到涨潮的影响最大。
More land will become low-lying due to coastal erosion. = 由于海岸侵蚀，更多的土地将变成低洼地。


虚拟语气:

I wish this house weren't so low-lying, we'd have less problems with flooding. = 我希望这房子不要这么低洼，我们就会少一些洪水问题。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
