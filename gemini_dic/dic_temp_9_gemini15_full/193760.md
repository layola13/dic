self-cutting:/ˈsɛlf ˈkʌtɪŋ/| n.|自残|The act of intentionally injuring oneself, typically by cutting.  This is often a coping mechanism for individuals dealing with emotional distress, trauma, or mental health challenges.|例句：Self-cutting is a serious issue that requires professional help. 自残是一个需要专业帮助的严重问题。
|例句：He admitted to self-cutting after a particularly difficult period in his life. 他承认在他生命中一段特别艰难的时期后有过自残行为。|近义词：self-harm, self-mutilation|反义词：self-care, self-preservation|名词
