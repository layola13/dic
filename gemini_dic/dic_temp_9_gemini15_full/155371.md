pharos:/ˈfærəs/| n. |灯塔；灯台|A pharos is a lighthouse, especially a tall, prominent one.  The word is derived from the name of the famous lighthouse on the island of Pharos near Alexandria, Egypt.|Example: The pharos guided ships safely into the harbor.|近义词：lighthouse, beacon|反义词：None
