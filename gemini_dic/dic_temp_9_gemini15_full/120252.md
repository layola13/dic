minhaj:/ˈmɪnˌhɑːʒ/| n. |方法；模式；习惯；典礼；仪式|A minhaj is a method, way, or mode of doing something, often referring to a traditional or customary practice, especially a religious ritual or ceremony.  It can also refer to a system or code of conduct.|例句：The minhaj of the Sufi order involved daily meditation and prayer. 苏菲教派的仪式包括每日冥想和祈祷。

The minhaj for preparing the traditional dish was passed down through generations. 制作这道传统菜肴的方法代代相传。|近义词：method, system, procedure, practice, ritual |反义词：chaos, disorder, randomness
