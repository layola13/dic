proofread:/ˈpruːfˌriːd/| vt.|校对，校阅|To read and correct the errors in (a text, etc.) before publication or printing.|例句：I need to proofread this essay before submitting it. 我需要在提交这篇论文之前校对一下。|近义词：edit, revise, check, correct|反义词:  None specifically, but antonyms relate to the action's opposite, like "leave uncorrected", "publish without checking".
