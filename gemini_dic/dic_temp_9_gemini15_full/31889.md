例句：The cheeseboard was overflowing with delicious cheeses and accompaniments.  奶酪板上摆满了美味的奶酪和配菜。

词源:
The word "cheeseboard" is a simple compound word, combining "cheese" (from Old English *cēse) and "board" (from Old English bord).  It's a relatively modern word reflecting the increasing popularity of cheese as a food item presented in a specific and aesthetically pleasing manner.
