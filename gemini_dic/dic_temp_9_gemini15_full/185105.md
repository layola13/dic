retiling:/ˌriːˈtaɪlɪŋ/| n.| 重新铺设瓷砖 | The act of replacing existing tiles with new ones.  It involves removing old tiles, preparing the surface, and installing new tiles. This process is often undertaken to repair damaged tiles, update a room's aesthetic, or improve functionality. |Example: The bathroom needs retiling; the grout is cracked and some tiles are loose.  浴室需要重新铺设瓷砖；水泥已经开裂，有些瓷砖松动了。|近义词：tile replacement, re-tiling|反义词：tile installation (if referring to initial installation)
