daisy:/ˈdeɪzi/| n. |雏菊，戴西（女子名）|A daisy is a small flower with white petals and a yellow center.  Daisies are often associated with innocence and purity.  The name is also used as a given name, typically for females.|例句：The field was covered in a carpet of daisies.  那片田野上铺满了雏菊。

The daisies in the garden are blooming beautifully. 花园里的雏菊开得真漂亮。|近义词：wildflower, flower|反义词：无
