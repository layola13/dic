reproach:/rɪˈproʊtʃ/| vt. n.|责备；谴责；耻辱；羞愧|to express disapproval or criticism of someone|例句：He reproached himself for his carelessness. 他责备自己粗心大意。|例句：Her silence was a reproach to them. 她的沉默是对他们的谴责。|近义词：blame, rebuke, reprimand, censure|反义词：praise, commend, applaud|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reproaches her for her mistakes. = 他因她的错误而责备她。
They reproach him constantly. = 他们不断地责备他。


一般过去时:
She reproached him for his lies. = 她因他的谎言而责备他。
He reproached himself for his inaction. = 他因自己的不作为而自责。


现在完成时:
I have reproached him several times. = 我已经责备他好几次了。
She has reproached herself for not helping. = 她为没有帮忙而自责。


过去完成时:
He had reproached her before she apologized. = 在她道歉之前，他已经责备过她了。
They had reproached him for years. = 他们已经责备他多年了。


将来时:
I will reproach him for his behavior. = 我将因他的行为而责备他。
She will reproach herself later. = 她以后会自责的。


虚拟语气:
I would reproach him if I were you. = 如果我是你，我会责备他。
If she had known, she would have reproached him. = 如果她知道的话，她就会责备他了。


被动语态:
He was reproached for his actions. = 他因自己的行为而受到责备。
She was often reproached by her parents. = 她经常受到父母的责备。


疑问句:
Will you reproach me for this? = 你会因为这个责备我吗？
Why did they reproach her? = 他们为什么责备她？


否定句:
I won't reproach you for this. = 我不会因为这个责备你。
They did not reproach him for his lateness. = 他们没有因为他的迟到而责备他。


PHRASE->
cast reproach on = 使蒙受责备
a subject of reproach = 令人谴责的事
beyond reproach = 无可指责的
bring reproach upon = 使蒙受耻辱
under reproach = 受谴责的

词源:
From Old French reprochier, from Latin reprobare "to disapprove," from re- "again" + probare "to test, prove."  The sense shifted from "disproving" to "expressing disapproval."
