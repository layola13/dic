loincloths: /ˈlɔɪnklɔːθz/ | n. | 缠腰布；遮羞布 | A loincloth is a garment worn around the loins, typically by men in some cultures. It is often made of a single piece of cloth and serves as a minimal form of clothing.  | Example: The ancient warriors wore loincloths into battle.  古代战士穿着缠腰布上战场。 |近义词：wrapper, wrap,  girdle (in some contexts)| 反义词：trousers, pants, full clothing
