congealing:/kənˈdʒiːlɪŋ/| v.|凝固；凝结|The process of changing from a liquid to a solid state, typically due to cooling.  It often refers to substances like liquids becoming thicker or solidifying.|例句：The gravy was congealing on the plate. 肉汁在盘子上凝固了。
The blood congealed quickly in the cold air.  血液在寒冷的空气中迅速凝固。|近义词：solidifying, thickening, setting, clotting|反义词：melting, liquefying, thawing|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The soup congeals quickly in the refrigerator. = 汤在冰箱里很快凝固。
The jelly congeals as it cools. = 果冻冷却时凝固。

一般过去时:
The jam congealed overnight. = 果酱一夜之间凝固了。
The custard congealed in the mold. = 蛋羹在模具中凝固了。

现在完成时:
The fat has congealed on top of the broth. = 油脂已经凝固在肉汤的上面。
The blood has congealed around the wound. = 血液已经凝固在伤口周围。

过去完成时:
The sauce had congealed before we could serve it. = 在我们能上菜之前，酱汁已经凝固了。
The gelatin had congealed completely by morning. = 到早上明胶已经完全凝固了。

将来时:
The ice cream will congeal in the freezer. = 冰淇淋会在冰箱里凝固。
The mixture will congeal as it cools. = 混合物冷却后会凝固。

虚拟语气:
If the temperature dropped further, the liquid would congeal. = 如果温度进一步下降，液体就会凝固。
I wish the chocolate had not congealed so quickly. = 我希望巧克力没有凝固得那么快。

被动语态:
The mixture is being congealed by the cold. = 混合物正在被寒冷凝固。
The soup was congealed before we could eat it. = 汤在我们能吃之前就已经凝固了。

疑问句:
Is the jelly congealing properly? = 果冻凝固得正常吗？
Has the custard congealed yet? = 蛋羹凝固了吗？

否定句:
The sauce didn't congeal properly. = 酱汁没有凝固好。
The gelatin hasn't congealed yet. = 明胶还没有凝固。


PHRASE->
congealing point = 凝固点
rapid congealing = 快速凝固
slow congealing = 缓慢凝固
congealing process = 凝固过程
congealing agent = 凝固剂


词源:
From Old French *congealer, from Latin congelare, from con- + gelare "to freeze."  The sense evolution is from "freeze" to "thicken" and then to the more general "to solidify" or "coagulate."
