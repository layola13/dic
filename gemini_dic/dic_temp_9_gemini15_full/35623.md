coated:/ˈkoʊtɪd/|/ˈkəʊtɪd/| adj.|涂层的；覆盖的；包衣的 |Covered with a layer of something.  The term implies a surface treatment. |例句：The chocolate-coated almonds are delicious.  巧克力包衣的杏仁很好吃。

The car's surface is coated with a protective layer of wax. 汽车表面涂有一层保护蜡。


近义词：covered, layered, sheathed, encrusted

反义词：uncoated, bare, uncovered


词性:形容词
