simplifying: /ˈsɪmplɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 简化；使单纯 |  The act of making something simpler or easier to understand.  This word is the present participle form of the verb "simplify". It describes an ongoing process of simplification. |

例句：
Simplifying the instructions made the task much easier.  = 简化说明使任务容易多了。

Simplifying the complex equation took hours of work. = 简化复杂的方程式花费了数小时的工作。


近义词：streamlining, easing, reducing, clarifying

反义词：complicating, elaborating, intensifying


词性：动词（现在分词）
