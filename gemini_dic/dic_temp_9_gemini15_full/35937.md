codable:/ˈkoʊdəbəl/ | adj. | 可编码的，可编程的 | Able to be encoded or programmed.  Something codable can be translated into a code or set of instructions that a computer or other system can understand and execute. | Example:  The data is codable into a binary format.  数据可以编码成二进制格式。 The algorithm is highly codable and efficient.  该算法高度可编码且高效。 | 近义词: encodable, programmable | 反义词: uncodable, unprogrammable
