palpatory: /ˈpælpeɪtəri/ | adj. | 触诊的；触觉的 | Relating to or performed by palpation (the act of feeling with the hands).  | Example:  The doctor performed a palpatory examination of the abdomen.  医生进行了腹部触诊检查。 |近义词: tactile, exploratory | 反义词:visual, auditory


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor palpates the patient's abdomen. = 医生触诊病人的腹部。
She palpates her neck for swollen glands. = 她摸她的脖子检查是否有肿大的腺体。


一般过去时:
The nurse palpated the patient's pulse. = 护士触诊了病人的脉搏。
He palpated the lump to assess its size and consistency. = 他触摸肿块以评估其大小和硬度。


现在完成时:
The surgeon has palpated the tumor several times. = 外科医生已经多次触诊了肿瘤。
She has palpated her breast regularly for years. = 她多年来定期进行乳房自检。


过去完成时:
The physician had palpated the patient's chest before listening with a stethoscope. = 医生在用听诊器听诊之前已经触诊了病人的胸部。
Before the operation, the doctor had palpated the area for any abnormalities. = 在手术前，医生已经触诊该区域是否有任何异常。


将来时:
The doctor will palpate the patient's injured leg tomorrow. = 医生明天将触诊病人的受伤腿。
They will palpate the patient's abdomen again after surgery. = 他们将在手术后再次触诊病人的腹部。


虚拟语气:
If I were the doctor, I would palpate the patient's thyroid. = 如果我是医生，我会触诊病人的甲状腺。
I wish I had palpated the area more carefully. = 我真希望我当时更仔细地触诊了该区域。


被动语态:
The patient's abdomen was palpated by the doctor. = 病人的腹部被医生触诊了。
The lump was palpated for signs of tenderness. = 触诊肿块以检查是否有压痛。


疑问句:
Did the doctor palpate the patient's spleen? = 医生触诊了病人的脾脏吗？
Have you ever palpated a swollen lymph node? = 你曾经触诊过肿大的淋巴结吗？


否定句:
The doctor did not palpate the patient's heart. = 医生没有触诊病人的心脏。
She did not palpate any masses in the abdomen. = 她没有触诊到腹部有任何肿块。
