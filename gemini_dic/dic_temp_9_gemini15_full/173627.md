quasi-intelligent:/ˈkwɑːzaɪɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənt/| adj. |准智能的，似智能的 |Seeming to possess intelligence, but not fully or genuinely intelligent; exhibiting characteristics of intelligence but lacking true understanding or consciousness.  It often describes systems or processes that mimic intelligent behavior but lack the underlying cognitive abilities of a truly intelligent being.|例句：The chatbot displayed quasi-intelligent behavior, responding to questions in a surprisingly coherent manner, but it ultimately lacked genuine understanding.  该聊天机器人表现出准智能的行为，以令人惊讶的连贯方式回答问题，但它最终缺乏真正的理解。


例句：Some researchers believe that certain animal behaviors might be considered quasi-intelligent, demonstrating problem-solving abilities without conscious thought. 一些研究人员认为，某些动物行为可能被认为是准智能的，它们在没有意识思考的情况下表现出解决问题的能力。|近义词：pseudo-intelligent, seemingly intelligent, artificially intelligent (in a limited sense)|反义词：truly intelligent, genuinely intelligent, conscious, sentient
