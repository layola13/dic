saivism:/ˈʃaɪvɪzəm/| n. |赛瓦教，赛维教 |Saivism is a major tradition within Hinduism that centers on the worship of Shiva.  It encompasses a wide range of beliefs and practices, varying significantly across different regions and sects.  The term "Saivism" itself is a relatively modern construct, used to broadly categorize this diverse group of traditions. |Example:  Many temples in South India are dedicated to Saivism.  许多南印度的寺庙都供奉赛瓦教。|近义词:Shaivism (alternative spelling)|反义词:None specifically, but could be contrasted with other major Hindu traditions like Vaishnavism.
