re-market:/ˌriːˈmɑːrkɪt/| vt.|再上市，重新推销|To market (a product or service) again, often after making changes or improvements, or to a different target market.|例句：The company decided to re-market their product with a new advertising campaign. 公司决定通过新的广告宣传活动重新推销他们的产品。|例句：After some modifications, the old product was successfully re-marketed to a younger demographic.  经过一些修改后，该旧产品成功地重新推销给了更年轻的人群。|近义词：reintroduce, relaunch, reposition|反义词：withdraw, discontinue|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-market their products every year. = 他们每年都重新推销他们的产品。
The company re-markets its services to a wider audience. = 公司将其服务重新推销给更广泛的受众。

一般过去时:
They re-marketed the product with a new design. = 他们用新的设计重新推销了该产品。
The company re-marketed its older models to a niche market. = 公司将其旧型号产品重新推销给利基市场。

现在完成时:
They have re-marketed their flagship product several times. = 他们已经多次重新推销了他们的旗舰产品。
The company has re-marketed its software with improved features. = 公司已经重新推销了其软件，并增加了改进的功能。

过去完成时:
They had re-marketed the product before the competitor launched a similar one. = 在竞争对手推出类似产品之前，他们已经重新推销了该产品。
The company had re-marketed the service several times before achieving success. = 在取得成功之前，该公司已经多次重新推销了这项服务。


将来时:
They will re-market the product next quarter. = 他们将在下个季度重新推销该产品。
The company will re-market its brand with a new logo. = 公司将用新的标识重新推销其品牌。

虚拟语气:
If they re-marketed the product differently, it might sell better. = 如果他们以不同的方式重新推销该产品，它可能会卖得更好。
I wish they had re-marketed the product earlier. = 我希望他们早点重新推销该产品。

被动语态:
The product was re-marketed with a new slogan. = 该产品以新的口号重新推销。
The service is being re-marketed to a new customer base. = 该服务正在重新推销给新的客户群。

疑问句:
Will they re-market the product soon? = 他们很快会重新推销该产品吗？
How will the company re-market its services? = 公司将如何重新推销其服务？

否定句:
They did not re-market the product this year. = 他们今年没有重新推销该产品。
The company will not re-market the outdated product. = 公司不会重新推销过时的产品。


PHRASE->
re-market a product = 重新推销产品
re-market a service = 重新推销服务
re-market to a new audience = 向新的受众重新推销
re-market with a new strategy = 用新的策略重新推销
re-market at a higher price = 以更高的价格重新推销
