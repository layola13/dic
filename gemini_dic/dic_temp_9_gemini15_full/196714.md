sewages:/ˈsuːɪdʒɪz/| n.|污水；下水道|Sewages are the waste water and human waste that are carried away from houses and buildings through pipes.|The city's sewage system needs upgrading.  这个城市的污水系统需要升级。|近义词：wastewater, drainage, effluent|反义词：clean water, purified water
