hypergenetical:/ˌhaɪpərˌdʒɛˈnɛtɪkəl/| adj.| 超遗传的；超越遗传的 | Relating to or denoting the influence of factors beyond the genetic code in determining the characteristics of an organism.  This includes epigenetic effects and environmental factors influencing gene expression.  | Example:  Hypergenetical research explores how environmental stimuli can alter gene expression patterns.  超遗传研究探索了环境刺激如何改变基因表达模式。|近义词：epigenetic, environmental, extrinsic | 反义词：intrinsic, genetic (in a purely genetic sense)
