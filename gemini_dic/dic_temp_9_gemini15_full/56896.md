dossiers:/ˈdɒsieɪz/| n.|档案；卷宗|A dossier is a collection of documents containing information about a person, event, or subject.  It often contains confidential or sensitive information.|例句：The police compiled a dossier on the suspected criminal. 警方收集了关于嫌疑犯的档案。|例句：The journalist spent months gathering information for her dossier on the political scandal.  记者花了几个月的时间收集有关政治丑闻的资料。|近义词：file, record, report|反义词：summary(in the sense of a concise overview)
