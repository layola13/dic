pro-Freudians: /ˌproʊˈfrɔɪdiən/ | adj. | 倾向弗洛伊德学说的；新弗洛伊德主义的 |  Those who generally agree with Freud's theories but modify or extend them in certain ways.  Pro-Freudians often emphasize the social and cultural factors influencing psychological development, rather than solely focusing on biological drives as Freud did.  They might also refine or reinterpret some of Freud's concepts. |  Many pro-Freudian thinkers expanded on Freud's theories of the unconscious and its influence on behavior. | 近义词：neo-Freudian, revisionist Freudian | 反义词：anti-Freudian
