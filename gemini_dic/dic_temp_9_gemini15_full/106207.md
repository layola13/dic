knoppern:/ˈnɒpən/| n.|橡子瘤, 瘿瘤|A knoppern is a gall, a growth induced by insects, found on the acorns of oak trees.  These galls are typically hard, woody, and have a distinctive shape. They are sometimes used in tanning or dyeing.|The oak tree was covered in knopperns.|近义词：gall, oak apple|反义词:None.
