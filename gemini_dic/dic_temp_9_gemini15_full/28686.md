cark:/kɑːrk/| v.| 烦扰；使焦虑；发出嘎嘎声 | To worry or trouble someone; to make a harsh, grating sound. | The constant noise from the construction site is really carking me.  持续的建筑噪音真让我烦躁。|近义词：bother, annoy, irritate | 反义词：soothe, calm, relax

USAGE->

The constant noise carks me. = 持续的噪音烦扰着我。
This problem carks him. = 这个问题让他很烦。

The news carked her. = 这个消息让她很焦虑。
The rough road carked the car's suspension. = 崎岖的路面使汽车的悬挂系统发出嘎吱声。
