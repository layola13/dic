quasi-fascinating: /ˈkwɑːziˌfæsɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 准迷人的，有点迷人的 |  Almost but not quite fascinating; possessing some but not all the qualities of something fascinating.  It suggests a degree of intrigue or interest, but lacking the complete captivating power of something truly fascinating. |

例句：

The lecture, while containing some interesting points, was ultimately quasi-fascinating.  =  虽然演讲包含一些有趣的观点，但最终只是有点迷人。


The museum exhibit, with its strange collection of artifacts, had a quasi-fascinating quality. = 博物馆的展览，以其奇怪的文物收藏，具有某种程度的迷人之处。


近义词：intriguing, mildly interesting, somewhat captivating


反义词：boring, dull, uninteresting


词性：形容词
