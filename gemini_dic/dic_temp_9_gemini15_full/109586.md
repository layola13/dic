lethality:/liːˈtæləti/| n. |致死性；致命性；死亡率 |The capacity to cause death; the degree to which something is likely to cause death.  It can refer to the inherent danger of something, or the statistical likelihood of death resulting from exposure to it. |例句：The lethality of the virus was a major concern.  病毒的致死性是主要的担忧。

The high lethality of the snake venom required immediate medical attention.  蛇毒的高致死性需要立即就医。


近义词：deadliness, mortality, fatality rate, virulence (in the context of diseases) |反义词：harmlessness, innocuousness, safety
