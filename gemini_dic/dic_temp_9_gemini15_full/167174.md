/ˌpriːˈtiːtʃɪz/ | v. | 预先教导；提前教授 | To teach beforehand or in advance.  The term is not common and often considered informal or non-standard.  It implies teaching something before it's officially needed or expected. | 例句：The professor preteaches key concepts to ensure students are prepared for the upcoming lab. = 教授预先教授关键概念，以确保学生为即将到来的实验做好准备。

The teacher preteaches the vocabulary before reading the chapter. = 教师在阅读章节之前预先教授词汇。


近义词：pre-teach,  instruct beforehand,  prepare, brief | 反义词：post-teach (not a common word)
