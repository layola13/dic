myography:/maɪˈɒɡrəfi/| n. |肌动描记术；肌电描记术 |The study of the graphic representation of muscle action.  It involves recording the electrical activity of muscles.  |例句：The myography showed significant muscle weakness in the patient's left leg. 肌动描记术显示患者左腿肌肉明显无力。

例句：Researchers used myography to analyze the effects of the new drug on muscle function. 研究人员用肌动描记术分析了新药对肌肉功能的影响。


近义词：electromyography (EMG)


反义词：无


词性:名词
