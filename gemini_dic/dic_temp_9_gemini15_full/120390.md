minishment: /ˈmɪnɪʃmənt/ | n. | 减少；减损；贬低 | The act of diminishing or lessening something; a reduction in size, amount, or importance.  It often implies a gradual or subtle decrease, rather than a sudden or complete removal. |  例句：The minishment of his power was gradual but undeniable. 他的权力逐渐削弱，但这是不可否认的。  例句：The minishment of natural resources is a serious concern. 自然资源的减少是一个严重的问题。 | 近义词：reduction, decrease, diminution, lessening | 反义词：increase, augmentation, enhancement |  名词
