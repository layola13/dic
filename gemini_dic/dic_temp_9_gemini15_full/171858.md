punition:/pjuːˈnɪʃən/ | n. |惩罚，处罚 |The act of punishing someone for a wrongdoing.  It often involves consequences intended to deter future misbehavior. |例句：The punition for stealing was a harsh sentence. 偷窃的惩罚是严厉的判决。
例句：The child received a punition for his bad behavior. 这个孩子因为不良行为受到了惩罚。|近义词：penalty, punishment, chastisement |反义词：reward, recompense
