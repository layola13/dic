sanguivorous:/ˌsæŋˈɡwɪvərəs/| adj.|食血的，吸食血液的|Feeding on blood; bloodsucking.  The term is often used to describe animals that primarily or exclusively consume blood, such as vampire bats.|Example: Vampire bats are a well-known example of sanguivorous creatures.  吸血蝙蝠是食血生物的一个著名例子。  Many insects are also sanguivorous, feeding on the blood of animals.  许多昆虫也是食血的，以动物的血液为食。|近义词：hematophagous|反义词：herbivorous, carnivorous
