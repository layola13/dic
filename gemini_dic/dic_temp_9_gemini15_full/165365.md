prejudicate:/ˈprɛdʒʊdɪkeɪt/ | vt. | 预先判断；预先决定；对…有偏见 | To influence or affect (someone or something) beforehand; to pre-judge; to bias.  | The judge's prior knowledge of the defendant might prejudicate the case.  法官对被告的先前了解可能会影响案件的判决。| influence, bias, predispose |  unbias,  fair |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prejudicates every situation based on his past experiences. 他根据过去的经验预先判断每种情况。
The media prejudicates public opinion with biased reporting. 媒体通过有偏见的报道预先影响公众舆论。


一般过去时:
The lawyer argued that the judge's comments prejudicated the jury. 律师辩称法官的评论预先影响了陪审团。
Her childhood experiences prejudicated her view of relationships. 她的童年经历影响了她对人际关系的看法。


现在完成时:
Years of negative media coverage have prejudicated the public's perception of the company. 多年的负面媒体报道已经影响了公众对公司的看法。
His early failures have prejudicated his confidence in his abilities. 他早期的失败已经影响了他对自己能力的信心。


过去完成时:
The witness's testimony had prejudicated the outcome of the trial before the defense could present their case.  证人的证词在辩方陈述案情之前就已经影响了审判的结果。
The rumors had prejudicated the meeting before it even began.  谣言在会议开始之前就已经影响了会议的结果。


将来时:
His aggressive tone will prejudicate the negotiations. 他咄咄逼人的语气将会影响谈判。
The leaked information will prejudicate the election results.  泄露的信息将会影响选举结果。


虚拟语气:
If the judge had not prejudicated the case, the outcome might have been different. 如果法官没有预先判断此案，结果可能会有所不同。
If only he hadn't prejudicated her character based on hearsay.  如果他当初没有根据道听途说就预先判断她的性格就好了。


被动语态:
The jury was prejudicated by the media coverage. 陪审团受到了媒体报道的影响。
The decision was prejudicated by the previous ruling.  这个决定受到了先前裁决的影响。


疑问句:
Could the witness's testimony have prejudicated the jury? 证人的证词是否可能影响了陪审团？
Does his personal bias prejudicate his judgment?  他的个人偏见是否影响了他的判断？


否定句:
The judge tried to remain impartial and not prejudicate the case.  法官试图保持公正，不预先判断案件。
We must avoid prejudicating the situation before we have all the facts.  在掌握所有事实之前，我们必须避免预先判断情况。
