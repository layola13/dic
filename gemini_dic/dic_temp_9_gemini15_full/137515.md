nontrading: /ˌnɑːnˈtreɪdɪŋ/ | adj. | 非交易的；不进行交易的 | Not involved in trading or commerce; not actively bought or sold in the market.  | Example: The nontrading assets of the company included its office building and company cars. 这家公司的非交易资产包括其办公楼和公司汽车。| 近义词：inactive, dormant, idle | 反义词：trading, active
