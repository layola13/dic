restaurants: /ˈrɛstrɑːnts/ | n. | 餐馆，饭店 | Places where meals are served to the public.  They vary widely in style, price, and cuisine. | 例句：We went to a fancy restaurant for our anniversary. 我们去了一家高档餐厅庆祝周年纪念。  There are many restaurants near the university. 大学附近有很多餐馆。| 近义词：eateries, diners, cafes, bistros | 反义词：home cooking (反义词较为宽泛，表示在家自己做饭)
