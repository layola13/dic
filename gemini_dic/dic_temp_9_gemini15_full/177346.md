reattempts:/ˌriːəˈtempt/| n.|再次尝试，反复尝试|The act of attempting something again, often after a previous failure.|例句：After several reattempts, he finally succeeded.  多次尝试后，他最终成功了。|近义词：retry, repeated attempts|反义词：giving up, abandonment|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His reattempts at the exam are frequent. = 他经常重考。
She makes many reattempts at the painting. = 她反复尝试绘画。


一般过去时:
His reattempts at the project failed. = 他再次尝试这个项目失败了。
Their reattempts to contact him were unsuccessful. = 他们再次尝试联系他未果。


现在完成时:
She has made several reattempts to climb the mountain. = 她已经多次尝试攀登这座山。
He has had numerous reattempts at solving the puzzle. = 他已经多次尝试解决这个难题。


过去完成时:
They had made several reattempts before they finally gave up. = 在他们最终放弃之前，他们已经尝试过好几次了。
Before the final attempt, she had made several reattempts. = 在最后一次尝试之前，她已经尝试过好几次了。


将来时:
He will make further reattempts next week. = 他下周将再次尝试。
