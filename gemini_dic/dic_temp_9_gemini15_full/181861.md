relook:/ˌriːˈlʊk/| vt.|重新审视；再看一遍|To look at or consider something again.|例句：I need to relook at my budget. 我需要重新审视我的预算。|例句：Let's relook at the contract before signing. 签字前让我们再看看合同。|近义词：review, reconsider, reexamine|反义词：ignore, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He relooks at the data every morning. 他每天早上都会重新审视数据。
She relooks her work before submitting it. 她在提交作业之前会重新检查一遍。

一般过去时:
They relooked the proposal after the meeting. 会议后他们重新审视了提案。
He relooked his decision after speaking to his wife.  他和妻子谈话后重新考虑了他的决定。

现在完成时:
I have relooked the document several times. 我已经多次重新审视了这份文件。

过去完成时:
