poisoning:/ˈpɔɪzənɪŋ/ | n. |中毒；投毒|The act of poisoning someone or something, or the state of being poisoned.  It refers to the harmful effect of a poisonous substance on a living organism. |例句：The accidental poisoning of the children was a terrible tragedy.  孩子们意外中毒是一场可怕的悲剧。

例句：She was suspected of poisoning her husband. 她涉嫌毒死了她的丈夫。


近义词：intoxication, envenomation

反义词：cure, healing
