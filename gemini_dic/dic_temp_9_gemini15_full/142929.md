out-toil:/ˌaʊtˈtɔɪl/| v.| 胜过；超过；比…干得更好 |To surpass in toil; to excel in labor or effort.  It's a relatively rare and archaic word, often found in older literature.|例句：He out-toiled all his rivals in the race to finish the project. 他在完成项目的竞赛中胜过了所有竞争对手。 He completely out-toiled his opponent, demonstrating superior stamina and skill. 他完全胜过对手，展现出更强的耐力和技巧。|近义词：outshine, outstrip, surpass, exceed |反义词：underperform, fail
