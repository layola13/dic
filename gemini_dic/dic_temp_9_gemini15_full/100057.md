interpluvial:/ˌɪntərˈpluːviəl/ | adj. |雨季之间的；雨季间歇的 |  Relating to or denoting the period between rainy seasons or periods of heavy rainfall.  It refers to a dry period that occurs between wetter periods, typically within a larger pattern of seasonal or cyclical rainfall.  | Example: The interpluvial period saw a decline in plant growth due to lack of water. 雨季间歇期植物生长因缺水而减少。 |近义词：interseasonal, dry, arid | 反义词：pluvial, rainy, wet | 词性：形容词
