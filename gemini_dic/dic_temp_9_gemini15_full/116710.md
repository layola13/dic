matzos:/ˈmɑːtsoʊz/| n. |无酵饼；薄脆饼| Unleavened bread eaten by Jews during Passover.  It is made from flour and water, and is baked until crisp.|例句：We ate matzos and gefilte fish for the Seder. 我们在逾越节晚餐吃了无酵饼和鱼冻。|例句：The matzos were a bit dry this year. 今年的无酵饼有点干。|近义词：cracker (general term)|反义词：leavened bread|名词
