rugs:/rʌɡz/| n.|地毯，毛毯|A rug is a piece of thick fabric, typically used to cover part of a floor.  Rugs can be made from various materials, such as wool, cotton, or synthetic fibers, and come in many different sizes, colors, and patterns.|例句：The rug in the living room is very soft. 客厅里的地毯很柔软。

例句：We need a new rug for the kitchen. 我们需要一张新的厨房地毯。


近义词：carpet, mat, floor covering|反义词：bare floor
