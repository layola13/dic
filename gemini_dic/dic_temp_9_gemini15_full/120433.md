minkes:/ˈmɪŋkiːz/| n. |小须鲸；小鳁鲸|The word "minkes" is the plural of "minke whale," a small baleen whale found in all oceans.  They are known for their relatively small size and distinctive V-shaped blow.  They are a common species and are a target for some whaling operations, though their population is currently considered sustainable in most areas.|例句：Many minke whales were sighted during the research expedition. = 在研究考察期间，许多小须鲸被发现。

例句：The minke whale population in this region seems to be stable. = 这个地区的小须鲸种群数量似乎很稳定。|近义词：baleen whale, small whale|反义词：N/A
