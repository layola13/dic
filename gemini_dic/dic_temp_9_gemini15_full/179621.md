reencountering:/ˌriːɪnˈkaʊntərɪŋ/ | v. |再次相遇；重新遇到 | The act of meeting again after a period of time.  It implies a previous encounter. |例句：Reencountering my old friend after twenty years was a truly emotional experience. 二十年后再次遇到我的老朋友，真是令人激动。

例句：Reencountering the challenges of starting a new business felt both daunting and exhilarating.  再次面对创业的挑战，既令人畏惧又令人兴奋。


近义词：meeting again, reuniting, running into


反义词：parting, separating, leaving


词性：动词
