salamandrina: /ˌsæləˈmæn.drɪ.nə/ | n. |火螈属（一种小型两栖动物）| Salamandrina is a genus of small salamanders found in Italy.  The genus includes two species, Salamandrina perspicillata and Salamandrina terdigitata.  They are characterized by their relatively small size, vibrant coloration, and preference for moist, mountainous habitats. | The  Salamandrina perspicillata is also known as the spectacled salamander. |近义词：salamander (更广义的称呼)|反义词：无
