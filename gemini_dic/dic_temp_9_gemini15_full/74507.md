frappe:/ˈfræpeɪ/| n. |冰沙；快打咖啡|A frappe is a blended iced beverage, often made with coffee, milk, and ice.  It can also refer to a type of iced coffee drink.  The exact recipe varies depending on the region and establishment.|例句：I ordered a caramel frappe to cool down on this hot day.  我点了一杯焦糖冰沙来消暑。|近义词：milkshake, smoothie, iced coffee|反义词：hot coffee
