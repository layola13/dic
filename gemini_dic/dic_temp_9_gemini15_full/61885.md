emptied:/ˈɛmptɪd/| v. |清空；使空虚|To remove the contents of something; to make something empty.|例句：He emptied the trash can. 他清空了垃圾桶。  The rain emptied the reservoir. 雨水使水库空了。|近义词：drained, evacuated, cleared|反义词：filled, replenished|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He empties the trash every day. = 他每天都倒垃圾。
She empties the dishwasher after every meal. = 她每顿饭后都清空洗碗机。

一般过去时:
I emptied the bottle. = 我喝光了瓶子里的东西。
They emptied the swimming pool for cleaning. = 他们把游泳池排空以便清洁。

现在完成时:
We have emptied all the boxes. = 我们已经清空了所有箱子。
She has emptied her bank account. = 她已经花光了银行账户里的钱。

过去完成时:
He had emptied the tank before the journey. = 他在旅程开始前已经清空了油箱。
They had emptied the shelves before the new stock arrived. = 新货到达之前，他们已经清空了货架。

将来时:
I will empty the ashtrays later. = 我过会儿会倒烟灰缸。
She will empty the bucket when she finishes cleaning. = 她清洁完毕后会倒掉桶里的东西。

虚拟语气:
If I had emptied the tank, the car wouldn't have broken down. = 如果我当时清空了油箱，汽车就不会抛锚了。
I wish I had emptied the dishwasher yesterday. = 我真希望我昨天把洗碗机清空了。

被动语态:
The bottle was emptied. = 瓶子被清空了。
The swimming pool is emptied every year for maintenance. = 游泳池每年都会被排空进行维护。

疑问句:
Have you emptied the bin? = 你倒垃圾了吗？
Did he empty the suitcase? = 他把行李箱清空了吗？

否定句:
I didn't empty the dishwasher. = 我没有清空洗碗机。
They haven't emptied the recycling bin yet. = 他们还没清空回收箱。
