hypothalamo-hypophysial: /ˌhaɪpəʊθæləməʊhaɪpəˈfɪziəl/ | adj. | 下丘脑-垂体的 | Relating to both the hypothalamus and the hypophysis (pituitary gland).  This term describes structures, processes, or connections involving both the hypothalamus and the pituitary gland.  For example, the hypothalamo-hypophysial portal system is a crucial link between these two brain regions.


例句：

The hypothalamo-hypophysial axis plays a vital role in regulating hormone secretion. = 下丘脑-垂体轴在调节激素分泌中起着至关重要的作用。

Studies of the hypothalamo-hypophysial tract have revealed important insights into the neural control of endocrine function. = 对下丘脑-垂体束的研究揭示了神经内分泌功能调控的重要见解。


词源:

* **hypothalamus:**  From Greek *hypo* (under) + *thalamus* (inner chamber, bed).  Refers to the part of the brain below the thalamus.

* **hypophysis:** From Greek *hypo* (under) + *physis* (growth).  Refers to the pituitary gland, located below the hypothalamus.


近义词:

There aren't direct synonyms, as this term is specific to the anatomical relationship between the hypothalamus and pituitary.  However, terms like "pituitary-hypothalamic" could be used interchangeably in some contexts.


反义词:

There are no direct antonyms.


词性:

Adjective
