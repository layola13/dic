letting-up: /ˈlɛtɪŋ ʌp/ | v. | 缓和；减弱；放松；停止 |  The phrase "letting up" describes a decrease in intensity or a cessation of something, typically something unpleasant or difficult.  It implies a gradual easing rather than a sudden stop. | 例句：The rain is finally letting up. 雨终于小了。

The pressure on the team is letting up now that the deadline has passed.  截止日期已过，团队的压力现在减轻了。| 近义词：easing, subsiding, diminishing, abating | 反义词：intensifying, worsening, escalating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain is letting up. = 雨正在减弱。
The pressure on him is letting up. = 对他的压力正在减轻。


一般过去时:
The storm let up after several hours. = 暴风雨持续数小时后减弱了。
The pain let up after I took the medicine. = 我服药后疼痛减轻了。


现在完成时:
The rain has let up, so we can go out. = 雨停了，所以我们可以出去了。
The coughing has let up since I started taking the medication. = 自从我开始服药以来，咳嗽已经减轻了。


过去完成时:
The headache had let up before he went to bed. = 他睡觉前头痛已经减轻了。
The noise had let up by the time the police arrived. = 等警察到达时，噪音已经减弱了。


将来时:
The doctor hopes the fever will let up soon. = 医生希望高烧很快就会退。
I think the wind will let up later in the day. = 我想风会在今天晚些时候减弱。


虚拟语气:
If the rain would let up, we could go for a walk. = 如果雨能停下来，我们可以去散步。
I wish this headache would let up. = 我希望这个头痛能快点好。


被动语态: (Letting up is not typically used in the passive voice)


疑问句:
Is the snow letting up yet? = 雪停了吗？
Is the pain letting up at all? = 疼痛有没有减轻？


否定句:
The rain isn't letting up at all. = 雨一点也没停。
The noise hasn't let up all night. = 噪音整夜都没有停止。



PHRASE->
let up on someone/something: 对某人/某事放松
let up a bit: 稍微减弱
let someone up: 允许某人起来
let something up: 放松某事
let up the pressure: 减轻压力
