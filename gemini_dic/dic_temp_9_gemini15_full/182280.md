removes:/rɪˈmuːvz/| vt.|去除，移除，消除|To take away something from somewhere or something.|The cleaner removes stains from carpets. 清洁工去除地毯上的污渍。 He removed his coat. 他脱掉了外套。|近义词：take away, eliminate, delete|反义词：add, install, insert


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He removes his shoes before entering the house. = 他进屋前脱掉鞋子。
She removes the weeds from her garden regularly. = 她定期清除花园里的杂草。


一般过去时:
The surgeon removed the tumor successfully. = 外科医生成功地切除了肿瘤。
I removed the old wallpaper yesterday. = 我昨天撕掉了旧壁纸。


现在完成时:
They have removed all the obstacles. = 他们已经移除了所有障碍。
She has removed her makeup. = 她已经卸妆了。


过去完成时:
He had removed the files before the inspection. = 他在检查之前已经删除了文件。
They had removed the furniture before painting the walls. = 他们在粉刷墙壁之前已经移走了家具。


将来时:
We will remove the trash tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上会把垃圾扔掉。
The company will remove the faulty product from the market. = 公司将会把有缺陷的产品从市场上撤下。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would remove that offensive post. = 如果我是你，我会删除那条冒犯人的帖子。
I wish I had removed the stain before it set. = 我真希望我能在污渍凝固之前把它清除掉。


被动语态:
The stain was removed easily. = 污渍很容易就被去除了。
The old building will be removed next month. = 旧建筑物下个月将被拆除。


疑问句:
Will you remove the dishes from the table? = 你会把盘子从桌子上拿走吗？
Have they removed the broken chair yet? = 他们把坏掉的椅子搬走了吗？


否定句:
He didn't remove the snow from the driveway. = 他没有清除车道上的积雪。
She couldn't remove the stubborn stain. = 她无法去除顽固的污渍。


PHRASE->
