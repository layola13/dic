loadbearing: /ˈloʊdˌbɛrɪŋ/ | adj. | 承重的，承载的 |  Describes a structural element (such as a wall or beam) that is designed and built to support a load, typically weight from above.  | Example: The load-bearing walls in this house are made of brick. 这栋房子的承重墙是用砖砌成的。The building's load-bearing capacity needs to be assessed.  建筑物的承重能力需要评估。| 近义词：supporting, weight-bearing | 反义词：non-loadbearing, decorative


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The central column is load-bearing. = 中心柱是承重的。
That wall is load-bearing and cannot be removed. = 那堵墙是承重的，不能拆除。


一般过去时:
The old bridge's load-bearing structure was compromised. = 老桥的承重结构受损了。

现在完成时:
They have reinforced the load-bearing beams. = 他们已经加固了承重梁。

过去完成时:
The architects had underestimated the load-bearing capacity of the foundation. = 建筑师低估了地基的承重能力。
