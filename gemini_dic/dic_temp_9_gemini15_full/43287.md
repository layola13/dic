craftspersons:/ˈkræftsˌpɜːrsənz/| n. |工匠，技工|A craftsperson is a skilled worker who makes things by hand.  This term is often preferred over "craftsman" as it is more inclusive of all genders.|例句：The craftspersons at the fair displayed their beautiful pottery. 集市上的工匠们展示了他们精美的陶器。The craftspersons carefully restored the antique furniture. 工匠们小心翼翼地修复了古董家具。|近义词：artisan, artisan, craftsman, craftswoman, skilled worker|反义词：amateur, unskilled worker|名词
