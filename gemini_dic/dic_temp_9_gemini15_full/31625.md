chatbot:/ˈtʃætˌbɒt/ /ˈtʃætˌbɑːt/| n. |聊天机器人|A chatbot is a computer program designed to simulate conversation with human users, especially over the internet.  Chatbots are often used for customer service, providing information, or entertainment. |Example:  Many companies now use chatbots on their websites to answer customer queries. 许多公司现在在其网站上使用聊天机器人来解答客户疑问。|近义词：conversational AI, virtual assistant |反义词：human operator
