immunize:/ˈɪmjuːnaɪz/| vt.|使免疫；使免除|To protect someone or something from a disease by giving them a vaccine or by giving them something that will create immunity.|例句：The doctor immunized the child against measles. 医生给孩子接种了麻疹疫苗。|例句：We need to immunize ourselves against negative thinking.我们需要使自己免受消极思想的影响。|近义词：vaccinate, protect, shield|反义词：infect, endanger


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor immunizes children against polio. = 医生给孩子们接种小儿麻痹症疫苗。
She immunizes her plants against pests. = 她使她的植物免受害虫侵害。

一般过去时:
They immunized the entire village against cholera. = 他们给整个村庄的人民接种了霍乱疫苗。
He immunized himself against criticism. = 他使自己免受批评。

现在完成时:
The government has immunized millions against influenza. = 政府已经为数百万人口接种了流感疫苗。
She has immunized herself from the negativity. = 她已经使自己免受消极情绪的影响。

过去完成时:
They had immunized the horses before the race. = 比赛前他们已经给马接种了疫苗。
He had immunized himself against any potential backlash. = 他已经使自己免受任何潜在的反弹。

将来时:
The hospital will immunize all newborns against hepatitis B. = 医院将为所有新生儿接种乙型肝炎疫苗。
They will immunize the crops against disease. = 他们将使农作物免受疾病侵害。

虚拟语气:
If we immunized everyone, we would eradicate the disease. = 如果我们为每个人接种疫苗，我们将根除这种疾病。
I wish they had immunized the dog against rabies. = 我希望他们已经为那条狗接种了狂犬病疫苗。

被动语态:
The children were immunized against measles. = 孩子们接种了麻疹疫苗。
The plants are being immunized against frost. = 植物正在被预防冻害。

疑问句:
Have you been immunized against tetanus? = 你接种过破伤风疫苗了吗？
Are the animals immunized against disease? = 动物们接种了预防疾病的疫苗了吗？

否定句:
They did not immunize the cattle against anthrax. = 他们没有为牛接种炭疽病疫苗。
She has not immunized herself against disappointment. = 她并没有使自己免受失望。


PHRASE->
