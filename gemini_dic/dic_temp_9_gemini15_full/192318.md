scribblement:/ˈskrɪblmənt/| n. |涂鸦；潦草的字迹 | A scribblement is something that has been written or drawn carelessly or hastily, often illegibly.  It suggests a lack of care or attention to detail. | Example: The child's scribblement was barely recognizable as a drawing.  孩子的涂鸦几乎认不出是什么画。|近义词：scrawl, doodle, jotting |反义词：masterpiece, calligraphy
