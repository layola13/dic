presterilizing: /ˌpriːˈstɛrəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 预先消毒；预先灭菌 |  The act of sterilizing something before use or a process.  It implies sterilization before the main process or application. | Example: Presterilizing the surgical instruments is crucial to prevent infection. 预先消毒手术器械对于预防感染至关重要。 | 近义词: pre-sterilization, prior sterilization | 反义词: post-sterilization, contamination
