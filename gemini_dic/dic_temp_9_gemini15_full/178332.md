reconditioning:/ˌriːˈkənˌdɪʃənɪŋ/| v. n.| 再调整；翻新；修复 |The process of restoring something to its original condition or a better condition.  It involves repairing, replacing, and upgrading components to improve functionality and extend lifespan. |例句：The car underwent a complete reconditioning before being sold. 这辆车在出售前进行了全面翻新。

The factory specializes in the reconditioning of used engines. 这家工厂专门从事旧发动机的翻新。

近义词：refurbishing, renovation, rehabilitation, overhaul |反义词：destruction, demolition, ruin |动词
