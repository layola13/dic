compromised:/ˈkɒmprəmaɪzd/ /ˈkɑːmprəmaɪzd/| v. adj. n.| 妥协；受损害；危害；让步；|To weaken or damage something; to make something less effective; to be forced to make concessions.|The security system was compromised. 安全系统受到了破坏。|The negotiations were compromised by a lack of trust. 谈判因缺乏信任而受损。|近义词：weakened, impaired, endangered, jeopardized|反义词：strengthened, improved, secured


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He compromises his principles for money. 他为了钱而牺牲了自己的原则。
They often compromise on important decisions. 他们经常在重要决策上妥协。


一般过去时:
The hacker compromised the company's database. 黑客破坏了公司的数据库。
She compromised her position by leaking confidential information. 她泄露了机密信息，从而损害了自己的地位。


现在完成时:
The virus has compromised the computer's operating system. 病毒已经破坏了计算机的操作系统。
His health has been compromised by years of smoking. 他多年的吸烟损害了他的健康。


过去完成时:
They had compromised their safety by ignoring the warnings. 他们因为忽视警告而危及了自己的安全。
The deal had been compromised by the unexpected economic downturn. 这笔交易因意外的经济衰退而受损。


将来时:
The negotiations will be compromised if they can't agree on a price. 如果他们不能就价格达成一致，谈判将会破裂。
Further delays will compromise the project's timeline. 进一步的延误将影响项目的进度。


虚拟语气:
If he compromised his ideals, he would achieve his goals. 如果他妥协了他的理想，他就能实现他的目标。
I wish he hadn't compromised his integrity. 我真希望他没有损害他的正直。


被动语态:
The network was compromised by a sophisticated malware attack. 网络受到复杂恶意软件攻击的破坏。
Our plans were compromised by the sudden change of weather. 我们的计划因突如其来的天气变化而受阻。


疑问句:
Has the integrity of the data been compromised? 数据的完整性是否受到损害？
Were the negotiations compromised by outside influence? 谈判是否受到外部影响而受损？


否定句:
The security system was not compromised. 安全系统没有受到破坏。
He did not compromise his values despite the pressure. 尽管面临压力，他也没有放弃自己的价值观。


PHRASE->
compromise with: 与…妥协
reach a compromise: 达成妥协
compromise on: 在…方面妥协
at the expense of: 以…为代价
a serious compromise: 严重的妥协
