hygienics:/haɪˈdʒiːnɪks/| n.|卫生学，卫生习惯|The science of maintaining health and preventing disease through sanitation, hygiene, and public health measures.|例句：Good hygienics are essential to prevent the spread of infectious diseases. 良好的卫生习惯对于预防传染病的传播至关重要。

例句：The course covered various aspects of hygienics, including food safety and waste disposal. 这门课程涵盖了卫生学的各个方面，包括食品安全和垃圾处理。

近义词：sanitation, hygiene, public health

反义词：unsanitation, filth, uncleanliness

词性：名词
