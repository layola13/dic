god-daughters: /ˈɡɑːdˌdɔːtərz/ | n. | 教女；干女儿 |  A goddaughter is a girl or woman who is considered a daughter by someone who is her godparent.  This relationship is established through a religious ceremony, usually a christening or baptism.  |


例句：

She is my goddaughter, and I am very proud of her.  = 她是我的教女，我非常为她骄傲。

I have three goddaughters, and we are very close. = 我有三个教女，我们关系非常亲密。


近义词：

adopted daughter (养女), spiritual daughter (精神上的女儿)


反义词：

godson (教子)


词性：名词 (plural)
