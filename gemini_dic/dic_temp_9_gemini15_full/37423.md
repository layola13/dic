commie:/ˈkɑːmi/| n. adj.|共产党员；共产主义者；(贬义)共产主义分子|A commie is a slang term, often derogatory, for a communist or someone perceived as a communist.  The term carries strong negative connotations and is often used to express disapproval or hostility towards communism and its adherents.  It is considered offensive by many.|He called his political opponent a commie during the debate. 他在辩论中称他的政治对手为共产主义分子。|communist, socialist|capitalist, anti-communist|名词，形容词
