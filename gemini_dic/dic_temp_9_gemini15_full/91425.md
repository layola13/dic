hyetography:/haɪɪˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. |雨量图，雨量记录|A graphical representation of rainfall data, often showing the amount of rainfall over time or across a geographical area.  It visually depicts the intensity, duration, and frequency of rainfall events. |The hyetograph showed a significant rainfall event lasting for several hours.  雨量图显示了一场持续数小时的大雨。  The city's hyetograph for the past decade reveals a trend of increasing rainfall.  过去十年该市的雨量图显示降雨量呈增加趋势。|近义词：rainfall chart, pluviograph |反义词：None specifically;  terms like "drought chart" might be considered contextual opposites.
