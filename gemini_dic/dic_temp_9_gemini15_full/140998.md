onerate:/ˈɒnəreɪt/| /ˈɑːnəreɪt/ | vt. |宣告无罪；免除责任；免除…的义务 |To declare someone not guilty of a crime; to free someone from a charge or accusation; to relieve someone of a duty or obligation. |例句：The court exonerated him of all charges. 法院宣告他所有指控都不成立。

The jury exonerated the defendant after hearing the evidence. 陪审团在听取证据后宣告被告无罪。


近义词：acquit, absolve, clear, vindicate |反义词：convict, incriminate, condemn


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The judge exonerates the suspect. =法官宣告嫌疑人无罪。
The company exonerates its employees from liability. = 公司免除其员工的责任。


一般过去时:
The court exonerated the accused. = 法院宣告被告无罪。
The investigation exonerated the police officer. = 调查结果为警官免除了责任。


现在完成时:
The evidence has exonerated the prime suspect. = 证据已为主要嫌疑人洗清了嫌疑。
The new findings have exonerated the scientist from blame. = 新发现使这位科学家免受责备。


过去完成时:
The new evidence had exonerated him before the trial. = 在审判之前，新的证据已经为他洗脱了罪名。
They had exonerated her after a thorough investigation. = 经过彻底调查后，他们为她洗脱了嫌疑。


将来时:
The results of the investigation will exonerate him. = 调查结果将为他洗脱罪名。
The new evidence will exonerate the wrongly accused man.  = 新证据将为被冤枉的人洗脱罪名。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence were stronger, it would exonerate him. = 如果证据更充分，它就能为他洗脱罪名。
If they had more evidence, they could exonerate him completely. = 如果他们有更多证据，就能完全为他洗脱罪名。


被动语态:
He was exonerated by the court. = 他被法院宣告无罪。
She was exonerated after a lengthy trial. = 经过漫长的审判后，她被宣告无罪。


疑问句:
Will the investigation exonerate the suspect? = 调查会为嫌疑人洗脱罪名吗？
Has the court exonerated the defendant? = 法院是否已宣告被告无罪？


否定句:
The court did not exonerate him. = 法院没有宣告他无罪。
The evidence did not exonerate the suspect. = 证据没有为嫌疑人洗脱罪名。



PHRASE->

词源:
源自于拉丁语 "exonerare"，由 "ex-" (out) 和 "onerare" (burden) 组成，字面意思是"解除负担"。
