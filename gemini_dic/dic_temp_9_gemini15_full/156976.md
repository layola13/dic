pillaging:/ˈpɪlədʒɪŋ/| n. |掠夺；抢劫；洗劫|The act of looting or plundering, especially in wartime or during a riot.  It involves taking goods or property by force or violence. |例句：The invading army was accused of pillaging villages and towns.  侵略军被指控洗劫村庄和城镇。

例句：The pirates engaged in extensive pillaging, stealing everything of value. 海盗进行了大规模的抢劫，偷走了所有值钱的东西。


近义词：plundering, looting, sacking, raiding|反义词：donating, giving, contributing|词性：名词
