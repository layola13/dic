domiciliate:/ˈdɒmɪsaɪleɪt/ | /ˈdɑːməˌsaɪleɪt/ | vt. | 使定居；确定…的住所 | To establish someone or something as a resident in a particular place.  | The company was domiciled in Delaware to take advantage of its favorable tax laws.  该公司在特拉华州注册成立，以利用其有利的税收法律。| establish, settle, reside | displace, evict | 动词


USAGE->

They domicile their families in the countryside. = 他们把家人安置在乡下。
The company domiciles its headquarters in New York. = 这家公司将其总部设在纽约。
