dovecotes:/ˈdʌvkəʊts/| n. |鸽舍；鸽房|A dovecote is a structure built to house pigeons or doves.  It's often a small, often decorative building, sometimes integrated into other structures like barns or houses.|例句：The old dovecote in the garden was full of cooing birds. 花园里古老的鸽舍里充满了咕咕叫的鸟儿。

The ancient farmhouse boasted a charming dovecote attached to its side.  古老的农舍自豪地拥有一个附在其侧面的迷人的鸽舍。|近义词：pigeon house, dovecot|反义词：无
