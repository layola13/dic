noncollection: /ˌnɒnkəˈlɛkʃən/ | n. | 非收集；未收集 | The absence of collection or the state of not being collected.  It refers to a situation where something is not gathered, assembled, or compiled. |  例句：The noncollection of taxes led to a budget deficit.  税款未收导致预算赤字。


例句：The museum's noncollection of modern art is a significant gap.  该博物馆未收藏现代艺术是一个很大的缺憾。


近义词：lack, absence, omission


反义词：collection, gathering, accumulation


词性: 名词
