regranted:/rɪˈɡræntɪd/ | v. | 再授予；重新授予 |  To grant again; to give or bestow something again.  This word is formed by combining "re-" (again) and "granted" (past participle of "grant"). It implies a previous grant that is being renewed or re-issued. | The company regranted the license after the initial agreement expired.  公司在初始协议到期后重新授予了许可证。| granted, awarded | revoked, denied | 动词
