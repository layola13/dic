non-Buddhists: /ˌnɒnˈbʊdɪsts/ | n. |非佛教徒 | People who do not follow Buddhism.  This term refers to individuals who do not adhere to the tenets and practices of Buddhism. It encompasses a broad range of individuals with diverse religious beliefs or no religious belief at all. | Example: The temple welcomed both Buddhists and non-Buddhists to the meditation retreat.  寺庙欢迎佛教徒和非佛教徒参加冥想静修。|近义词: non-believers (in Buddhism),  people of other faiths |反义词: Buddhists
