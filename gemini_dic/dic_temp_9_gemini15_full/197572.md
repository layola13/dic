sheltie:/ˈʃɛlti/| n. | シェルティ（一种牧羊犬）| A Sheltie is a small, fluffy dog breed known for its intelligence, loyalty, and herding instincts.  They are a variety of the Shetland Sheepdog. |  My friend owns a beautiful Sheltie.  我的朋友养了一只漂亮的苏格兰牧羊犬。
Shepherds often use Shelties to herd sheep. 牧羊人经常用苏格兰牧羊犬来放羊。|近义词：Shetland Sheepdog, miniature collie|反义词：无
