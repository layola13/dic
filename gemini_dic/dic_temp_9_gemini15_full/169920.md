prospers:/ˈprɒspərz/| /ˈprɑːspərz/ | vi. | 繁荣；兴旺；成功 | To be successful and wealthy; to thrive.  | The business is prospering under his leadership.  这家公司在他的领导下蓬勃发展。| The garden is prospering this year.  今年花园长得很好。 | thrive, flourish, succeed | fail, decline, suffer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company prospers in the current market. = 这家公司在目前的市场上蓬勃发展。
Our garden prospers because of the good weather. = 由于天气好，我们的花园欣欣向荣。


一般过去时:
The town prospered during the gold rush. = 在淘金热期间，这个小镇繁荣起来。
His business prospered after he invested in new technology. = 他投资新技术后，生意兴隆。


现在完成时:
The economy has prospered since the reforms were implemented. = 自从改革实施以来，经济已经繁荣起来。
Our family has prospered through hard work and dedication. = 通过辛勤工作和奉献，我们的家庭过上了富裕的生活。


过去完成时:
The country had prospered for decades before the war. = 在战争爆发前，这个国家已经繁荣了几十年。
The company had prospered under his grandfather's leadership. = 在他祖父的领导下，公司曾经非常兴旺。


将来时:
The city will prosper if the new infrastructure project is completed. = 如果新的基础设施项目建成，这座城市将会繁荣起来。
We believe our business will prosper in the coming years. = 我们相信我们的业务在未来几年将会蓬勃发展。


虚拟语气:
If they had invested wisely, their business would have prospered. = 如果他们投资明智，他们的生意就会兴隆。
I wish my business prospered as much as my neighbor’s. = 我希望我的生意也能像我邻居的生意一样兴旺。


被动语态:
The city was prospered by the development of new industries. = 这个城市因新兴产业的发展而繁荣起来。(被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The region was not prospered by the recent drought. = 最近的干旱并没有使这个地区繁荣起来。(被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用)


疑问句:
Will the project prosper? = 这个项目会成功吗？
Does the company prosper despite the economic downturn? = 尽管经济低迷，这家公司是否仍然兴旺？


否定句:
The farm didn't prosper because of the bad harvest. = 由于收成不好，农场没有兴旺起来。
His plans did not prosper as he had hoped.  = 他的计划并没有像他希望的那样成功。
