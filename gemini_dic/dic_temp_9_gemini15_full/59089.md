dye:/daɪ/| n., vt.|染料；染色|To color something using a dye or stain.|The artist used a vibrant dye to color the fabric.  艺术家用鲜艳的染料给织物染色。|She dyed her hair red. 她把头发染成了红色。|stain, color|bleach, wash|名词, 动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory dyes fabrics every day. = 工厂每天都染色。
He dyes his hair a different color each month. = 他每个月都染不同的发色。

一般过去时:
She dyed her dress blue last week. = 她上周把她的裙子染成了蓝色。
They dyed the wool with natural pigments. = 他们用天然颜料给羊毛染色。

现在完成时:
I have dyed my hair several times. = 我染过几次头发了。
She has dyed the fabric a beautiful shade of purple. = 她把布料染成了美丽的紫色。

过去完成时:
He had dyed his hair black before the interview. = 面试前他已经把头发染成了黑色。
They had dyed the shirts before shipping them. = 他们发货前已经把衬衫染了色。

将来时:
I will dye my hair blonde tomorrow. = 我明天要把我头发染成金色。
She will dye the fabric using a new technique. = 她将使用一种新技术来染色。

虚拟语气:
If I dyed my hair green, people would stare. = 如果我把头发染成绿色，人们会盯着看。
I wish I had dyed my hair a brighter color. = 我真希望我当时把头发染得更亮一些。

被动语态:
The fabric is dyed using a special process. = 布料是用一种特殊的工艺染色的。
The shirts were dyed before they were sold. = 衬衫在出售前就已经染过色了。

疑问句:
Did you dye your own hair? = 你自己染的头发吗？
What kind of dye did they use? = 他们用了什么染料？

否定句:
I didn't dye my hair; it's my natural color. = 我没有染头发；这是我的自然发色。
She didn't dye the fabric because she ran out of dye. = 她没有染色，因为染料用完了。
