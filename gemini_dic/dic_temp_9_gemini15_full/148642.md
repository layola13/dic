overthriftiness:/ˌoʊvərˈθrɪfti.nəs/| n. |过度节俭；吝啬；过度节省|Excessive frugality; stinginess; extreme economy.  It's a negative trait characterized by an unhealthy obsession with saving money to the point of deprivation.  This can manifest in various ways, from refusing necessary expenditures to hoarding possessions. |His overthriftiness prevented him from enjoying life's simple pleasures.  他的过度节俭使他无法享受生活中的简单乐趣。

Her overthriftiness led to a dilapidated house and insufficient clothing for her children.她的过度节俭导致房屋破旧，孩子们衣服不足。|近义词：parsimony, miserliness, stinginess, penuriousness|反义词：extravagance, profligacy, generosity, munificence
