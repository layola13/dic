leaf-through: /liːf θruː/ | v. | 快速翻阅；浏览 | To turn the pages of a book or magazine quickly, looking at various parts but not reading everything in detail. | 例句：I leafed through the magazine, looking for interesting articles. 我快速翻阅了杂志，寻找有趣的文章。  I leafed through the book before deciding to buy it. 我在决定买这本书之前快速翻阅了一下。| 近义词：browse, skim, glance through | 反义词：read thoroughly, study carefully | 动词
