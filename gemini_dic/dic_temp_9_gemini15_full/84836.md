hang it up: /hæŋ ɪt ʌp/ | phrasal verb | 停止；放弃；退休 | To stop doing something; to give up; to retire.  | 例句：He decided to hang it up after 30 years in the business. 他决定在做了30年生意后退休。 |近义词：quit, give up, retire, cease | 反义词：continue, persevere


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hangs it up every evening. = 他每天晚上都把它挂起来。
They hang their coats up in the closet. = 他们把外套挂在壁橱里。

一般过去时:
She hung the painting up on the wall. = 她把画挂在了墙上。
He hung his hat up before leaving. = 他离开前把帽子挂了起来。

现在完成时:
I have hung the laundry up to dry. = 我已经把洗好的衣服晾起来了。
They have hung the decorations up for the party. = 他们已经为派对挂起了装饰品。

过去完成时:
She had hung the dress up before going to bed. = 她睡觉前已经把裙子挂起来了。
He had hung the phone up before I could speak. = 我还没来得及说话他就挂断了电话。

将来时:
I will hang the picture up tomorrow. = 我明天会把照片挂起来。
She will hang the lights up for the holiday. = 她会为节日挂起灯饰。

虚拟语气:
If I had more space, I would hang more pictures up. = 如果我有更多空间，我会挂更多照片。
I wish I had hung the washing up sooner. = 我真希望我早点把衣服晾起来。

被动语态:
The painting was hung up in the gallery. = 这幅画被挂在画廊里。
The clothes were hung up to dry. = 衣服被晾起来烘干。

疑问句:
Where did you hang the key up? = 你把钥匙挂在哪里了？
Have you hung the curtains up yet? = 你已经把窗帘挂起来了吗？

否定句:
He didn't hang the phone up. = 他没有挂断电话。
She hasn't hung the pictures up yet. = 她还没有把照片挂起来。
