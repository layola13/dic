rosette:/rəʊˈzɛt/ | n. |莲座，玫瑰花结 | A rosette is a decorative object resembling a rose, often made of ribbon or other material.  It can also refer to a rose-shaped arrangement of something else, such as leaves or petals. |例句：The cake was decorated with a beautiful red rosette.  这个蛋糕装饰着一个美丽的红色玫瑰花结。

The building featured intricate rosettes carved into the stonework. 这座建筑的石雕上装饰着复杂的莲座。|近义词：floral decoration, ornament, embellishment |反义词：plainness, simplicity
