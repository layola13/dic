scalled:/skæld/ | v. | 剥去…的鳞；批评；斥责 | To remove the scales from (a fish or other scaly creature); to criticize severely; to scold. |例句：He scaled the fish before cooking it. 他在烹饪前去除了鱼鳞。  |例句：The teacher scaled the student for his bad behavior. 老师因学生的不良行为而斥责他。|近义词：descale, criticize, rebuke, scold |反义词：praise, commend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scales the fish every day. = 他每天都刮鱼鳞。
She scales her vegetables before cooking. = 她在烹饪前削她的蔬菜。


一般过去时:
He scaled the salmon before grilling it. = 他在烤三文鱼之前去除了鱼鳞。
The boss scaled him for his mistakes. = 老板因他的错误而斥责他。


现在完成时:
I have scaled all the potatoes. = 我已经把所有土豆都削好了。
She has scaled the project for its unrealistic goals. = 她因其不切实际的目标而批评了该项目。


过去完成时:
He had scaled the fish before I arrived. = 我到达之前他已经去除了鱼鳞。
They had scaled the plan for its flaws. = 他们已经因为计划中的缺陷而批评了该计划。


将来时:
I will scale the carp tomorrow. = 我明天将清理鲤鱼。
She will scale him for his laziness. = 她将因他的懒惰而斥责他。


虚拟语气:
If I scaled the fish, it would cook faster. = 如果我去除鱼鳞，它会煮得更快。
If he had scaled the project earlier, it might have succeeded. = 如果他早些时候批评了该项目，它可能会成功。


被动语态:
The fish was scaled by the chef. = 鱼被厨师去鳞了。
He was scaled by his manager for missing the deadline. = 他因为错过截止日期而被经理斥责了。


疑问句:
Have you scaled the potatoes yet? = 你把土豆削好了吗？
Was he scaled for his behavior? = 他因为他的行为而受到斥责了吗？


否定句:
I didn't scale the fish. = 我没有去鳞。
She didn't scale the project. = 她没有批评该项目。
