dizzard:/ˈdɪzəd/| n.|狂风暴雨，暴风雪|A blizzard is a severe snowstorm with strong winds and low visibility.|例句：The blizzard forced the closure of schools and businesses.  暴风雪迫使学校和企业关闭。|近义词：snowstorm, snow squall, gale|反义词：calm, fair weather|名词
