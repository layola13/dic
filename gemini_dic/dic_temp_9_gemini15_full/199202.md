silken:/ˈsɪlkən/| adj.|丝绸的；柔软的；光滑的|Made of silk or resembling silk in texture; smooth and soft.|例句：Her silken gown shimmered in the candlelight.  她的丝绸长袍在烛光下闪闪发光。

例句：The silken touch of the fabric felt luxurious against her skin.  丝绸般柔软的面料触感让她感觉奢华。

近义词：smooth, soft, silky, satiny
反义词：rough, coarse, harsh
词性:形容词
