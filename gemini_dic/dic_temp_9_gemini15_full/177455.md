rebating:/rɪˈbeɪtɪŋ/| n. v.|回扣；折扣；退还；返还|The act of reducing a price or amount owed, often as an incentive or reward.  It can refer to a discount given to a customer, a refund of a portion of a payment, or a payment made by a manufacturer to a retailer for purchasing a large quantity of goods.|例句：The company offers a rebating program for bulk purchases. 这家公司对批量购买提供返利计划。|例句：We received a rebate on our car insurance. 我们获得了汽车保险的回扣。|近义词：discount, refund, repayment, allowance|反义词：surcharge, markup, increase


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company rebates a percentage of the purchase price. = 公司退还购买价格的一部分。
He rebates his customers for early payment. = 他对提前付款的顾客给予回扣。


一般过去时:
They rebated the customer for the damaged goods. = 他们因货物损坏而向顾客退还款项。
The manufacturer rebated the retailer for the large order. = 制造商因大宗订单而向零售商返还款项。


现在完成时:
They have rebated the full amount to the customer. = 他们已将全款退还给客户。
The government has rebated taxes to stimulate the economy. = 政府已退税以刺激经济。


过去完成时:
The customer had already rebated the excess payment. = 客户已退还多余的付款。
The company had rebated the funds before the deadline. = 公司在截止日期前已退还资金。


将来时:
We will rebate you the difference if the price drops. = 如果价格下降，我们将退还差价。
They will rebate the customers for any defects. = 他们将因任何缺陷而向客户退款。


虚拟语气:
If they rebated the full price, I would buy it. = 如果他们退还全价，我会买它。
I wish they had rebated the shipping cost. = 我希望他们已经退还了运费。


被动语态:
The amount was rebated to the customer's account. = 款项已退回到客户的账户。
A portion of the tax was rebated by the government. = 一部分税款已由政府退还。


疑问句:
Does the store rebate for returns? = 这家商店是否退还退货款？
Will they rebate the shipping costs? = 他们会退还运费吗？


否定句:
The company does not rebate on damaged goods. = 公司不退还损坏货物的款项。
They did not rebate the customer for the delay. = 他们没有因为延误而向客户退还款项。
