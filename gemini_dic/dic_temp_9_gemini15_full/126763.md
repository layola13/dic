nannies:/ˈnæniz/ | n. |保姆，育儿嫂 |  The plural of "nanny," referring to people who care for children.  |例句：Many families employ nannies to help with childcare.  许多家庭雇佣保姆来帮忙照顾孩子。

例句：The nannies were all highly qualified and experienced. 这些保姆都非常合格且经验丰富。


近义词：childcare providers, babysitters (for shorter periods), au pairs


反义词：None directly opposite, but  "parents" could be considered in the context of childcare responsibility.


词性：noun
