peabird:/ˈpiːbɜːrd/| n. |豌豆鸟|A small, brightly colored bird, often associated with eating peas or frequenting pea fields.  The term is not a formally recognized ornithological name and may refer to various species depending on region and context.  It's more of a colloquial or descriptive term. |Example: "The children enjoyed watching the peabirds flitting through the garden." = 孩子们喜欢观看豌豆鸟在花园里飞来飞去。|近义词:  (depending on the specific species)  finch, sparrow, bunting |反义词: N/A
