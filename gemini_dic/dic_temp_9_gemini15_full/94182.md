It emphasizes the urgency or importance of something needing to be done.  The 's indicates a possessive or plural form depending on context. |例句：The imperativeness of the situation demanded immediate action.  情况的紧急性要求立即采取行动。 The doctor stressed the imperativeness of the medication. 医生强调了药物的必要性。|近义词：urgency, necessity, compulsion, mandatory nature|反义词：optionality, permissiveness, non-essentiality|名词
