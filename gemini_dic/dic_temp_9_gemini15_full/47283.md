de-democratizing: /ˌdiːdɪˈmɒkrətaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 去民主化；取消民主；使非民主化 | The process of removing or undermining democratic principles, institutions, or practices.  It often involves suppressing political freedoms, limiting citizen participation, and concentrating power in the hands of a few. | Example: The recent political upheavals are causing concern about the de-democratizing trend in the region.  最近的政治动荡引发了人们对该地区去民主化趋势的担忧。 | 近义词：authoritarianism, autocratization | 反义词：democratization


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government is de-democratizing the country by silencing the opposition. = 政府通过压制反对派来使国家去民主化。
They are de-democratizing the electoral process through voter suppression. = 他们通过压制选民来使选举过程去民主化。


一般过去时:
The military junta de-democratized the nation through a coup d'état. = 军政府通过政变使国家去民主化。
The dictator de-democratized the country by eliminating all political opponents. = 独裁者通过清除所有政治对手来使国家去民主化。


现在完成时:
The ruling party has de-democratized the media through censorship and control. = 执政党通过审查和控制使媒体去民主化。
They have de-democratized the judicial system by undermining the independence of the judges. = 他们通过破坏法官的独立性使司法系统去民主化。


过去完成时:
By the time the revolution began, the ruling power had already de-democratized the society. = 到革命开始时，统治势力已经使社会去民主化了。
The people realized that the government had de-democratized the education system for years. = 人民意识到政府多年来一直在使教育体系去民主化。


将来时:
The increasing authoritarianism suggests the country will further de-democratize in the coming years. = 日益增长的专制主义表明，该国将在未来几年进一步去民主化。
Experts predict that without intervention, the region will de-democratize rapidly. = 专家预测，如果没有干预，该地区将迅速去民主化。


虚拟语气:
If the current trend continues, the country would completely de-democratize. = 如果目前的趋势继续下去，该国将完全去民主化。
I wish the government hadn't de-democratized the press so severely. = 我希望政府不要如此严厉地使新闻界去民主化。


被动语态:
The country was de-democratized through a series of repressive measures. = 该国通过一系列压制性措施被去民主化了。
The electoral system is being de-democratized by manipulating the voting process. = 选举制度正在通过操纵投票过程而被去民主化。


疑问句:
Is the government de-democratizing the country through its policies? = 政府是否正在通过其政策使国家去民主化？
How has the ruling party de-democratized the institutions? = 执政党是如何使这些机构去民主化的？


否定句:
The government did not de-democratize the country overnight; it was a gradual process. = 政府并没有一夜之间使国家去民主化；这是一个渐进的过程。
They have not completely de-democratized the society, but they have made significant inroads. = 他们并没有完全使社会去民主化，但他们已经取得了重大进展。



PHRASE->

erosion of democracy = 民主制度的侵蚀
undermining democracy = 破坏民主
suppression of dissent = 压制异见
centralization of power = 权力集中
