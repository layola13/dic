entomb:/ɪnˈtuːm/| vt.|埋葬；掩埋；隐藏|To place (a dead body) in a tomb; bury.  To enclose or hide completely.|例句：The pharaoh was entombed with all his treasures.  法老和他所有的宝藏一起被埋葬了。The secrets were entombed in the ancient scrolls. 这些秘密被隐藏在古老的卷轴中。|近义词：bury, inter, encase, immure|反义词：unearth, exhume, reveal


USAGE->

They entomb the bodies in the crypt. = 他们把尸体埋葬在墓穴里。
The artist entombs his emotions in his paintings. =这位艺术家将他情绪隐藏在他的画作中。


一般过去时:
The ancient Egyptians entombed their royalty with great ceremony. = 古埃及人以盛大的仪式埋葬他们的皇室成员。
