slimming: /ˈslɪmɪŋ/ | adj. | 使苗条的；减肥的；修身的 | Making someone or something appear slimmer or thinner; relating to the process of losing weight.  |例句：She's on a slimming diet. 她正在节食减肥。  | 近义词：weight-reducing, slenderizing, svelte | 反义词：fattening, enlarging


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new dress has a slimming effect. = 这件新衣服有显瘦的效果。
That diet plan is slimming many people. = 那个减肥计划让很多人变瘦了。


一般过去时:
The surgery successfully slimmed her down. = 手术成功地让她瘦了下来。
She slimmed down significantly after joining the gym. = 她加入健身房后明显瘦了。


现在完成时:
She has slimmed down considerably since last year. = 自从去年以来，她已经瘦了很多。
I have slimmed my waistline by eating healthy food. = 我通过健康饮食瘦了腰围。


过去完成时:
She had slimmed down before her wedding. = 她在婚礼前已经瘦了下来。
He had slimmed down considerably by the time he went on vacation. = 他去度假的时候已经瘦了很多。


将来时:
I will slim down before summer. = 我会在夏天到来之前瘦下来。
She will slim down by following a strict diet plan. = 她会通过严格的节食计划来减肥。


虚拟语气:
If I followed a strict diet, I would slim down quickly. = 如果我严格节食，我会很快瘦下来。
I wish I could slim down without exercising so much. = 我希望我不需要那么多的运动就能瘦下来。


被动语态:
Her figure was slimmed by regular exercise. = 她通过规律的运动使身材变苗条了。

疑问句:
Does this dress have a slimming effect? = 这件衣服有显瘦的效果吗？

否定句:
