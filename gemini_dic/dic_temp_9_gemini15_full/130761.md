/ˌnɒnæmˈfɪbiəsnəs/ | adj. | 非两栖性；非两栖的 |  Lacking the characteristics of amphibians; inability to live both on land and in water.  The nonamphibiousness of the reptile was evident in its scaly skin and preference for dry environments.  |近义词：terrestriality (陆生性), nonaquatic (非水生的) | 反义词： amphibiousness (两栖性)


USAGE->

This word is an adjective, and as such, it doesn't have verb conjugations.  Its usage is primarily attributive or predicative, describing a noun.

Attributive:
The reptile's nonamphibiousness is a key feature. = 爬行动物的非两栖性是一个关键特征。
The study focused on the nonamphibiousness of desert animals. = 这项研究的重点是沙漠动物的非两栖性。


Predicative:
Its skin is nonamphibious. = 它的皮肤是非两栖的。(This usage is less common and may sound slightly unnatural.  A more natural phrasing might be "Its skin is not amphibious.")
The lifestyle of the animal proved to be nonamphibious. = 该动物的生活方式被证明是非两栖的。


PHRASE->

There are no common phrases directly using "nonamphibiousness".  The concept is usually expressed differently.  For example, one might say:
