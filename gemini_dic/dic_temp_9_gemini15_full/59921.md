edentulous:/ɪˈdentʃələs/| adj.|无牙的，缺牙的|Lacking teeth; toothless.|The elderly woman was edentulous and relied on dentures.|近义词：toothless,  lacking teeth|反义词：dentate, toothed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is edentulous. = 他没有牙齿。
She is edentulous due to gum disease. = 她因为牙龈疾病而没有牙齿。

一般过去时:
My grandfather was edentulous in his old age. = 我爷爷老年时没有牙齿了。
The patient was edentulous after the accident. = 这位病人事故后就没有牙齿了。

现在完成时:
He has been edentulous for many years. = 他多年来都没有牙齿了。
She has become edentulous recently. = 她最近才变得没有牙齿。

过去完成时:
He had been edentulous before he got dentures. = 他在装假牙之前就没有牙齿了。
She had been edentulous since her childhood. = 她从小就没有牙齿。

将来时:
He will be edentulous soon if he doesn't take care of his teeth. = 如果他不注意牙齿健康，很快就会没有牙齿。
She will likely be edentulous by the time she is eighty. = 到她八十岁的时候，很可能就没有牙齿了。

虚拟语气:
If he were edentulous, he would need dentures. = 如果他没有牙齿，他就需要假牙。
I wish I weren't edentulous. = 我真希望我没有牙齿。


被动语态:
The patient was found to be edentulous during the examination. = 医生在检查中发现病人没有牙齿。
The edentulous condition was caused by poor oral hygiene. = 无牙状态是由口腔卫生差造成的。


疑问句:
Is he edentulous? = 他没有牙齿吗？
Was the patient edentulous before the treatment? = 病人在治疗前没有牙齿吗？


否定句:
He is not edentulous; he has a full set of teeth. = 他不是无牙的；他有一副完整的牙齿。
She was not edentulous; she had a few remaining teeth. = 她不是无牙的；她还有一些牙齿。


PHRASE->

edentulous mandible = 无牙下颌
