camwood:/ˈkæmˌwʊd/| n. |非洲血竭木，樟科植物|A reddish-brown wood from various African trees, especially of the genus Baphia, used as a dye and in carving.  The heartwood is prized for its rich color and durability.|The artist used camwood to create a deep, reddish-brown hue in his painting.  艺术家用非洲血竭木在其绘画中创造出深沉的红棕色调。|近义词：dye wood, red wood|反义词：无
