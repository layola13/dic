hootenanny:/ˈhuːtənænɪ/| n. |欢闹的聚会；乡村音乐演唱会|A hootenanny is a lively, informal gathering, typically featuring folk music and singing, often with audience participation.  It's characterized by a relaxed and convivial atmosphere. |例句：They threw a hootenanny to celebrate the summer solstice.  他们举办了一场欢闹的聚会来庆祝夏至。

例句：Last night's hootenanny was a blast! 昨晚的乡村音乐演唱会太棒了！


近义词：sing-along, jamboree, get-together, party|反义词：formal event, solemn occasion|词性：名词
