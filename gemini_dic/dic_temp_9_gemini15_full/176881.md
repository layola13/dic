reaggravate:/ˌriːˈæɡrəveɪt/| vt.|使恶化；使加重|To make (a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling) worse.|The rain reaggravated the already flooded streets.  雨水使本来已经泛滥的街道更加糟糕。|His careless words reaggravated the already tense situation. 他粗心大意的话使本来就已经紧张的局势更加恶化。|worsen, exacerbate, intensify|improve, alleviate, mitigate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The stress reaggravates his health problems. = 压力加重了他的健康问题。
He reaggravates the wound by scratching it. = 他抓伤口使伤口恶化。


一般过去时:
The storm reaggravated the damage to the building. = 暴风雨使建筑物的损坏更加严重。
She reaggravated her ankle injury during the race. = 她在比赛中加重了脚踝的伤势。


现在完成时:
The constant arguing has reaggravated their relationship. =  不断的争吵恶化了他们的关系。
He has reaggravated his back problem by lifting heavy objects. = 他搬重物加重了他的背部问题。


过去完成时:
The doctor explained that the patient had reaggravated his condition by ignoring medical advice. = 医生解释说，病人因为忽视了医嘱而加重了他的病情。
They had reaggravated the conflict by making insensitive remarks. = 他们因发表不敏感的言论而加剧了冲突。


将来时:
The expected heatwave will reaggravate the drought. = 预计的热浪将加剧旱情。
I'm afraid that excessive exercise will reaggravate my knee injury. = 我担心过度运动会加重我的膝盖伤势。


虚拟语气:
If he had rested, he wouldn't have reaggravated his injury. = 如果他休息一下，他就不会加重他的伤势了。
I wish he hadn't reaggravated the situation with his comments. = 我真希望他不要因为他的评论而加剧局势。


被动语态:
The situation was reaggravated by the recent events. = 近期发生的事件使局势恶化。
His injury was reaggravated during the game. = 他的伤势在比赛中加重了。


疑问句:
Will this medication reaggravate my allergies? = 这种药会加重我的过敏反应吗？
Did his actions reaggravate the conflict? = 他的行为是否加剧了冲突？


否定句:
The treatment did not reaggravate his condition. = 治疗并没有加重他的病情。
She tried to avoid activities that would reaggravate her pain. = 她尽量避免会加重她疼痛的活动。
