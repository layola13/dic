saturations:/ˌsætʃəˈreɪʃənz/| n.|饱和度；饱和状态|The state of being saturated.  It refers to the degree to which a substance is filled with another substance, often in the context of color, sound, or a solution.|例句：The high saturation of the colors in the painting was striking.  这幅画中色彩的高饱和度令人印象深刻。
例句：The audio system reached its saturation point at maximum volume. 音响系统在最大音量时达到了饱和点。|近义词：fulness, impregnation|反义词：unsaturation, dilution|名词
