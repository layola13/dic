inure:/ɪˈnjʊr/ | vt. |使习惯于；使适应；使不受影响 | to make someone or something less sensitive to something unpleasant |

例句：
He inured himself to the cold by spending hours outdoors each day.  = 他通过每天在户外待数小时来使自己习惯寒冷。

Years of hardship had inured her to pain. = 多年的苦难使她对痛苦习以为常。


近义词：accustom, habituate, harden


反义词：sensitize


词性：动词
