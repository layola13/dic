healed:/hiːld/ | v. | 治愈；康复；痊愈 | To restore to health or soundness; cure.  | The doctor healed his wound. 医生治愈了他的伤口。  The cut on my arm has healed nicely. 我胳膊上的伤口已经痊愈了。 | cured, recovered, mended | infected, diseased, sick | 动词
