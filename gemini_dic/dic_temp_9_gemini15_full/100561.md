interworking:/ˌɪntərˈwɜːrkɪŋ/ | n. |互操作性，互通性 |The ability of different systems or components to work together.  It often refers to the seamless exchange of information and services between disparate systems. |Example: The success of the project depends on the interworking of all the different software modules.  这个项目的成功取决于所有不同软件模块的互操作性。|近义词：interoperability, compatibility |反义词：incompatibility, isolation
