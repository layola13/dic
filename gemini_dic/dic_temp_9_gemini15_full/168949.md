profiled:/proʊˈfaɪld/ | v. | 描绘…的轮廓；突出；塑造形象 | To create a description of someone or something; to give prominence or emphasis to something; to shape or mold someone's character or image. | 例句：The newspaper profiled the successful entrepreneur.  报纸报道了这位成功的企业家的事迹。

The documentary profiled the lives of refugees.  这部纪录片描绘了难民的生活。


近义词：described, highlighted, featured, portrayed, shaped | 反义词：ignored, overlooked, obscured


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He profiles the latest trends in technology. 他分析最新的科技趋势。
The company profiles its products on its website.  这家公司在其网站上展示其产品。

一般过去时:
She profiled the artist for the magazine. 她为杂志撰写了这位艺术家的个人介绍。
The news report profiled the victims of the disaster.  新闻报道介绍了灾难中的受害者。


现在完成时:
They have profiled several candidates for the job. 他们已经为这份工作挑选了几位候选人。
The journalist has profiled many influential figures.  这位记者已经报道过许多有影响力的人物。


过去完成时:
The author had profiled the historical figure before writing the novel. 作者在写小说之前已经对这位历史人物做过介绍。
The team had profiled the criminal before the arrest.  团队在逮捕之前已经对罪犯进行了画像。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If they profiled the candidate properly, they would have hired him. 如果他们对候选人进行了适当的评估，他们就会雇用他。
I wish they had profiled the situation more thoroughly.  我希望他们对情况进行更彻底的分析。


被动语态:
The politician was profiled in the local newspaper.  这位政治家在当地报纸上受到了关注。
The project was profiled by the company's CEO. 该项目由公司首席执行官进行了介绍。


疑问句:
Will the article profile the challenges facing the company? 这篇文章会介绍公司面临的挑战吗？

否定句:
The report did not profile the environmental impact of the project. 报告没有介绍该项目的环保影响。
They didn't profile the witnesses properly.  他们没有对证人进行适当的描述。
