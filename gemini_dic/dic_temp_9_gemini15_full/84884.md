hangout:/ˈhæŋaʊt/ | n., v. | 闲逛；聚会；常去的地方 |  A place where people frequently gather socially; to spend time in a relaxed way, often with friends. |例句：The park is a popular hangout for teenagers.  公园是青少年们常去的聚集地。|例句： We hung out at the mall all afternoon. 我们整个下午都在商场闲逛。|近义词：gather, socialize, meet, congregate | 反义词：isolate, seclude


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hangout at the coffee shop every Saturday. = 他们每个星期六都在咖啡店闲逛。
I hangout with my friends after school. = 我放学后和朋友们一起闲逛。

一般过去时:
We hung out at the beach yesterday. = 我们昨天在海滩上闲逛。
She hung out with her classmates last night. = 她昨晚和同学们一起闲逛。

现在完成时:
I have hung out with them many times. = 我已经和他们一起闲逛过很多次了。
They have hung out at that bar since college. = 他们大学以来就经常去那家酒吧。

过去完成时:
We had hung out before the movie started. = 电影开始之前我们就已经闲逛过了。
They had hung out there for hours before we arrived. = 在我们到达之前，他们已经在那里闲逛了好几个小时。

将来时:
We will hangout at the park tomorrow. = 我们明天会在公园闲逛。
They will hangout with their friends this weekend. = 他们这个周末会和朋友们一起闲逛。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would hangout with you. = 如果我有更多时间，我会和你一起闲逛。
I wish I could hangout with my friends more often. = 我希望我能更经常地和朋友们一起闲逛。

被动语态:
(Hangout is rarely used in passive voice.  A better phrasing would be needed depending on context.)  


疑问句:
Where do you usually hangout? = 你通常在哪里闲逛？
Do they hangout often? = 他们经常一起闲逛吗？

否定句:
I didn't hangout with anyone last night. = 我昨晚没有和任何人一起闲逛。
They don't hangout at that place anymore. = 他们不再去那个地方闲逛了。


PHRASE->
hang out with = 和…一起闲逛
hang out at = 在…闲逛
chill out and hang out = 放松一下，一起闲逛
a hangout spot = 聚集地，常去的地方
to hang out together = 一起闲逛
