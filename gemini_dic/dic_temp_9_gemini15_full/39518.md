conquer:/ˈkɒŋkə(r)/, /ˈkɑːŋkər/| vt. |征服；战胜；克服 |To gain control of (a place or people) by military force. To overcome or succeed in dealing with (a problem or difficulty).|例句：The Romans conquered much of Europe. 罗马人征服了大部分欧洲。|例句：She conquered her fear of heights. 她克服了恐高症。|近义词：vanquish, overcome, subdue, master|反义词：surrender, yield, succumb|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army conquers new territories. = 军队征服新的领土。
He conquers his fear of public speaking. = 他克服了对公开演讲的恐惧。

一般过去时:
Alexander the Great conquered Persia. = 亚历山大大帝征服了波斯。
She conquered her addiction to drugs. = 她战胜了她对毒品的瘾。

现在完成时:
The empire has conquered many nations. = 这个帝国已经征服了许多国家。
He has conquered his shyness. = 他已经克服了他的害羞。

过去完成时:
The invaders had conquered the city before the reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达之前，侵略者已经征服了这座城市。
She had conquered her fear of flying before her trip to Europe. = 在她去欧洲旅行之前，她已经克服了她的飞行恐惧症。

将来时:
They will conquer the mountain tomorrow. = 他们明天将征服这座山。
He will conquer his procrastination and finish the project. = 他将克服拖延症并完成项目。

虚拟语气:
If only I could conquer my laziness! = 要是我能克服我的懒惰就好了！
I wish I could conquer my fear of spiders. = 我希望我能克服我害怕蜘蛛的恐惧。

被动语态:
The city was conquered by the enemy. = 这个城市被敌人征服了。
His fear was conquered by his determination. = 他的决心克服了他的恐惧。

疑问句:
Can we conquer this challenge? = 我们能克服这个挑战吗？
Has she conquered her illness? = 她战胜了她的疾病吗？

否定句:
They did not conquer the land. = 他们没有征服那块土地。
He could not conquer his anger. = 他无法控制他的愤怒。
