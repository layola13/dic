entertainer:/ˌɛntərˈteɪnər/ | n. | 表演者，艺人 | A person whose job is to entertain people, for example by singing, dancing, telling jokes, or acting.  | Example:  He's a talented entertainer who can sing, dance, and play the guitar. 他是一位才华横溢的艺人，能歌善舞，还会弹吉他。|近义词：performer, artist, comedian, musician | 反义词：None specifically, but words implying seriousness or lack of entertainment could be considered antonyms in context.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He entertains the audience with his magic tricks. = 他用魔术表演来娱乐观众。
The comedian entertains the crowd with jokes. = 喜剧演员用笑话逗乐观众。


一般过去时:
She entertained her guests with stories of her travels. = 她用旅行见闻招待客人。
The band entertained at the wedding reception. = 乐队在婚礼招待会上表演。


现在完成时:
They have entertained thousands of people with their music. = 他们用音乐娱乐了成千上万的人。
The circus has entertained children for generations. = 马戏团几代以来一直为孩子们带来欢乐。


过去完成时:
He had entertained the thought of quitting his job. = 他曾考虑过辞职。
The family had entertained many guests before. = 这家人以前招待过很多客人。


将来时:
We will entertain our friends at a barbecue. = 我们将在烧烤会上招待朋友。
The theatre will entertain audiences with a new play. = 剧院将上演新剧来娱乐观众。


虚拟语气:
If I were richer, I would entertain more often. = 如果我有钱一点，我会更频繁地招待客人。
If she had known, she would have entertained them better. = 如果她知道的话，她会更好地招待他们。


被动语态:
The guests were entertained royally. = 客人们受到了盛大的款待。
The audience was entertained by the magician's performance. = 观众被魔术师的表演所吸引。


疑问句:
Does he entertain often? = 他经常招待客人吗？
Who will entertain at the party? = 谁将在派对上表演？


否定句:
He doesn't entertain very often. = 他不经常招待客人。
The show didn't entertain the audience. = 这场演出没有娱乐观众。
