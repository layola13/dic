reservoir:/ˈrezərvwɑːr/| n.|蓄水池；水库；储藏器；[喻] 储藏；积累|A reservoir is a large artificial lake used to store water for later use, typically for drinking, irrigation, or industrial purposes. It can also refer to a place where something is stored or accumulated.  |例句：The city's water supply depends on the reservoir.  这个城市的供水依赖于水库。

例句：The reservoir of talent in the company is impressive. 公司的人才储备令人印象深刻。|近义词：tank, lake, store, accumulation|反义词：consumption, depletion|词性:名词

The reservoir holds millions of gallons of water. = 这个水库储存着数百万加仑的水。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
