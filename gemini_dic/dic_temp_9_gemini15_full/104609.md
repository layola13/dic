/ˈkɑːpə/ | n. | 卡帕（毛利语的一种披风） |  A kapa is a Māori cloak, traditionally woven from flax or other plant fibres. It is often highly decorated and holds significant cultural importance.  |  This kapa is a family heirloom, passed down through generations.  这个卡帕是家族的传家宝，代代相传。 |  cloak, mantle |  (无明显反义词) | 名词
