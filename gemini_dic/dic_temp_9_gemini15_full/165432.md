prelect:/ˈpriːlekt/ | v. | 预讲；宣讲；演讲 | To deliver a lecture or discourse; to read aloud; to preach.  | Example: The professor prelected on the intricacies of quantum physics. 教授就量子物理的复杂性进行了演讲。|近义词：lecture, discourse, preach | 反义词：listen, hear


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prelects on history every Tuesday. = 他每周二都讲授历史。
She prelects to the students about the importance of ethics. = 她向学生们宣讲道德的重要性。


一般过去时:
The priest prelected on the parable of the Good Samaritan. = 牧师宣讲了好撒玛利亚人的比喻。
He prelected eloquently on the subject of Shakespeare. = 他雄辩地阐述了莎士比亚的话题。


现在完成时:
He has prelected at this university for over twenty years. = 他在这所大学讲学二十多年了。
She has prelected extensively on the topic of sustainable development. = 她就可持续发展这个话题做了大量的演讲。


过去完成时:
Before the conference, he had prelected on the latest findings in his research. = 会议之前，他已经就其研究的最新发现作了演讲。
The speaker had prelected for an hour before the audience began to ask questions. = 演讲者演讲了一个小时后，听众才开始提问。


将来时:
He will prelect at the upcoming conference. = 他将在即将到来的会议上发表演讲。
She will prelect on the dangers of climate change. = 她将就气候变化的危险性发表演讲。


虚拟语气:
If he prelected more clearly, more students would understand. = 如果他讲得更清楚一些，更多的学生就能理解。
I wish he had prelected on that topic last semester. = 我希望他上学期就那个话题做过演讲。


被动语态:
The topic was prelected upon by several experts. = 这个话题被几位专家讲解过。
The lecture will be prelected in the main auditorium. = 讲座将在主礼堂举行（此处被动语态略显不自然，更常用其他动词）。


疑问句:
Will he prelect at the seminar? = 他会在研讨会上演讲吗？
Who prelected at the conference yesterday? = 昨天是谁在会议上演讲的？


否定句:
He did not prelect on that topic. = 他没有就那个话题进行演讲。
She will not prelect without proper preparation. = 她没有充分准备就不会演讲。



PHRASE->
prelect upon/on: 就…发表演讲
prelect to: 向…发表演讲
prelect eloquently: 雄辩地演讲
prelect extensively: 广泛地演讲
prelect formally: 正式地演讲
