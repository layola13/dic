jumbo:/ˈdʒʌmboʊ/| adj. n.|巨大的，庞大的；大型飞机|Extremely large;  a large aircraft, especially a Boeing 747.|例句：The jumbo jet carried over 500 passengers. 这架大型喷气式飞机载了500多名乘客。

例句：That's a jumbo-sized candy bar! 那是一块巨型糖果棒！


近义词：gigantic, colossal, enormous, immense|反义词：tiny, miniature, small


词源:  The word "jumbo" originates from the name of a large African elephant, Jumbo, who was a famous attraction at a London zoo in the 19th century.  His fame led to the word being used to describe anything exceptionally large.


用法说明：

Jumbo主要用作形容词和名词。

作为形容词，它修饰名词，表示"巨大的"、"庞大的"。例如：a jumbo jet (大型喷气式飞机), a jumbo shrimp (大虾), a jumbo package (大包装)。

作为名词，它通常指波音747大型飞机。例如：We flew to London on a jumbo. 我们乘坐波音747飞往伦敦。


需要注意的是，Jumbo作为名词使用时，通常特指波音747，而不仅仅是泛指任何大型飞机。
