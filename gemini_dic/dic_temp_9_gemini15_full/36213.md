cogitation:/ˌkɒdʒɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. |沉思；思考；冥想 |The act of thinking deeply and carefully; meditation.  It often implies a prolonged and serious consideration of a subject. |例句：After much cogitation, she decided to accept the job offer.  经过一番深思熟虑之后，她决定接受这份工作。|例句：His cogitation on the problem led to a breakthrough solution. 他对这个问题的深入思考导致了一个突破性的解决方案。|近义词：contemplation, reflection, meditation, deliberation |反义词：impulsiveness, rashness
