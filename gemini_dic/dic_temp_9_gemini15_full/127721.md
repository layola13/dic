negligences:/ˈneɡlɪdʒənsɪz/| n. |疏忽；过失；玩忽职守|The plural form of negligence, referring to multiple instances of carelessness or neglect.  It implies a failure to exercise the care that a reasonable person would exercise in a similar situation.| Examples:  The doctor's negligences led to the patient's death. 医生们的疏忽导致了病人的死亡。 The company's repeated negligences resulted in a lawsuit.  公司的反复疏忽导致了一场诉讼。| carelessness, neglect, oversight, inattention | diligence, care, attention | noun
