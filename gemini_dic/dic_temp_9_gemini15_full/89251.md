homini:/ˈhɒmɪnaɪ/| n. |人属；人类；人（属）|The word "hominini" refers to a taxonomic tribe within the family Hominidae, encompassing humans and their extinct ancestors.  It includes the genera *Homo* (humans) and *Australopithecus*, among others.
