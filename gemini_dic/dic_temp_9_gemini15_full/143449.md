outfought:/ˌaʊtˈfɔːt/| vt.|击败，战胜|To defeat someone or something in a fight or competition by being stronger or more skillful.|例句：Our team outfought the opponents in the final game and won the championship. 我们的队伍在决赛中击败了对手，赢得了冠军。
例句：Despite being smaller, the boxer outfought his opponent through superior technique.  尽管体型较小，但拳击手凭借高超的技术击败了对手。|近义词：defeat, overcome, conquer, vanquish|反义词：be defeated by, be overcome by, be conquered by|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outfights his opponents regularly. = 他经常击败他的对手。
They outfight their rivals in every match. = 他们每场比赛都击败对手。

一般过去时:
The underdog outfought the champion. = 弱者击败了冠军。
She outfought her opponent with skillful jabs. = 她凭借娴熟的刺拳击败了对手。

现在完成时:
The team has outfought all its rivals this season. = 这个赛季球队已经击败了所有对手。
He has outfought every opponent he has faced. = 他击败了他遇到的每一个对手。

过去完成时:
They had outfought their opponents in previous matches. = 他们在之前的比赛中击败了对手。
The army had outfought the rebels in the earlier battles. = 军队在之前的战斗中击败了叛军。

将来时:
We believe our team will outfight their opponents tomorrow. = 我们相信我们的球队明天会击败对手。
I think he will outfight his challenger easily. = 我认为他会轻松击败挑战者。

虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he would have outfought his opponent. = 如果他训练更刻苦，他就能击败对手。
If they had a better strategy, they might have outfought the enemy. = 如果他们有更好的策略，他们也许就能击败敌人。

被动语态:
The champion was outfought by the underdog. = 冠军被弱者击败了。
The enemy was outfought in the battle. = 敌人在这场战斗中被击败了。

疑问句:
Did our team outfight the other team? = 我们的队伍击败了另一支队伍吗？
Will they outfight their competitors in the upcoming contest? = 他们会在即将到来的比赛中击败竞争对手吗？

否定句:
Our team did not outfight their opponents. = 我们的队伍没有击败对手。
He couldn't outfight his stronger opponent. = 他无法击败比他更强的对手。
