particularise: /pəˈtɪkjʊləraɪz/ | vt. | 详细说明；具体化；列举细节 | To describe or specify something in detail; to give particulars of. | 例句：The report particularised the various problems facing the company.  报告详细说明了公司面临的各种问题。


例句：Please particularise the points you disagree with. 请具体说明你不同意的地方。


近义词：detail, specify, itemize, enumerate, delineate


反义词：generalize, summarize, abstract


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He particularises his needs every time he orders. 他每次点单都会详细说明他的需求。
She particularises the ingredients in her recipes. 她会在食谱中详细列出食材。


一般过去时:
The witness particularised the events leading up to the accident. 目击者详细描述了事故发生前的经过。
He particularised the changes he wanted made to the contract. 他详细说明了他想对合同进行的修改。


现在完成时:
She has particularised all the necessary information for the project. 她已经为项目列出了所有必要的信息。
They have particularised their objections to the proposal. 他们已经详细说明了他们对该提案的异议。


过去完成时:
He had particularised his concerns before the meeting began. 会议开始前，他已经详细说明了他的担忧。
She had particularised the steps involved in the process. 她已经详细说明了该过程中的各个步骤。


将来时:
The manager will particularise the new policy during the meeting. 经理将在会议上详细说明新的政策。
They will particularise the reasons for the delay in their report. 他们将在报告中详细说明延误的原因。


虚拟语气:
If he particularised his requirements, we could provide better service. 如果他详细说明他的要求，我们就能提供更好的服务。
I wish he had particularised the problem more clearly. 我希望他更清楚地说明这个问题。


被动语态:
The issues were particularised in the final report. 这些问题在最终报告中得到了详细说明。
The instructions were particularised to ensure everyone understood. 说明书中进行了详细说明，以确保每个人都理解。


疑问句:
Did she particularise the details of the incident? 她是否详细说明了事件的细节？
Will they particularise the changes they’re implementing? 他们会详细说明他们正在实施的更改吗？


否定句:
He did not particularise the reasons for his absence. 他没有详细说明他缺席的原因。
The document did not particularise the financial implications. 文件没有详细说明财务影响。

词源:  From the Latin "particularis" (particular) + "-ise" (to make or become).  Essentially, it means "to make particular" or "to make specific."
