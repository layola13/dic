deepening:/ˈdiːpənɪŋ/ | v. adj. n. |加深；变深；深化 |The deepening of the river made it difficult to cross. 河水的加深使得过河变得困难。|近义词：intensifying, intensifying,  aggravating |反义词：shallowing, lightening


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The crisis is deepening. = 危机正在加深。
His understanding of the subject is deepening. = 他对这个主题的理解正在加深。


一般过去时:
The recession deepened last year. = 经济衰退在去年加剧了。
The color of the paint deepened over time. = 油漆的颜色随着时间的推移加深了。


现在完成时:
The hole has deepened significantly. = 洞已经加深了很多。
My knowledge of history has deepened considerably. = 我对历史的了解已经大大加深了。


过去完成时:
The conflict had deepened before the peace talks began. = 和谈开始之前，冲突已经加剧了。
The mystery had deepened with each passing day. = 随着每一天的过去，谜团越来越深了。


将来时:
The economic problems will deepen further. = 经济问题将进一步加深。
The relationship between them is likely to deepen over time. = 他们之间的关系可能会随着时间的推移而加深。


虚拟语气:
If the recession deepened, the government would have to intervene. = 如果经济衰退加剧，政府就必须干预。
I wish the color of the paint had deepened more. = 我希望油漆的颜色能更深一些。


被动语态:
The mystery was deepened by the discovery of new evidence. = 新证据的发现加深了这个谜团。
The riverbed was deepened by the continuous flow of water. = 河床由于持续的水流而加深了。


疑问句:
Is the crisis deepening? = 危机正在加深吗？
Is your understanding of the problem deepening? = 你对这个问题的理解在加深吗？


否定句:
The relationship did not deepen. = 关系没有加深。
The color didn't deepen as much as I'd hoped. = 颜色没有像我希望的那样加深。
