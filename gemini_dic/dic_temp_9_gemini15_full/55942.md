dizziness:/ˈdɪzinəs/| n. |头晕，眩晕 |A sensation of whirling and loss of balance.  It can be a symptom of various medical conditions.| 例句：The dizziness made her stumble. 头晕让她跌倒了。

The heat and lack of sleep caused dizziness. 炎热和睡眠不足导致头晕。


近义词：lightheadedness, giddiness, vertigo |反义词：steadiness, stability

词源:
The word "dizziness" comes from the Middle English word "diseynes," which ultimately derives from the Old English word "dysig," meaning "foolish, dizzy, senseless."  The modern sense of physical dizziness evolved from the older meaning of mental confusion or disorientation.
