countermyth:/ˈkaʊntərmɪθ/| n. |反神话；谬误；对普遍认知的否定|A countermyth is a belief or story that contradicts a widely held belief or myth.  It often challenges established narratives or explanations, presenting an alternative perspective.  For example, a countermyth to the idea that all immigrants are criminals might be stories highlighting the positive contributions of immigrants to society. |The countermyth of the "lazy worker" is often used to justify exploitative labor practices.  反“懒惰工人”的谬论经常被用来为剥削性的劳动行为辩护。|myth, misconception, fallacy|truth, fact, reality|名词
