flite:/flaɪt/| n. |飞行；航班；逃跑；一阵；一阵（风、雨等）|A flight is a journey in an aircraft.  A flight can also refer to the act of flying or escaping. |例句：My flight is delayed. 我的航班延误了。
例句：The bird took flight.  鸟儿飞了起来。|近义词：journey, voyage, escape, burst|反义词：landing, arrival


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird takes flight. = 鸟儿起飞了。
He flies to London every week. = 他每周都飞往伦敦。


一般过去时:
The plane flew to New York. = 飞机飞往纽约。
She fled the scene. = 她逃离了现场。


现在完成时:
The pilot has flown over 1000 hours. = 飞行员已经飞行了超过1000小时。
They have escaped from prison. = 他们已经越狱了。


过去完成时:
The bird had flown away before I arrived. = 我到达之前，鸟已经飞走了。
She had already fled the country when the police arrived. = 当警察到达时，她已经逃离了那个国家。


将来时:
I will fly to Paris next month. = 我下个月将飞往巴黎。
They will escape in the night. = 他们将在夜间逃跑。


虚拟语气:
If I had wings, I would fly. = 如果我有翅膀，我会飞。
If I had known, I would have escaped. = 如果我知道的话，我就逃跑了。


被动语态:
The plane was flown by a skilled pilot. = 这架飞机由一名熟练的飞行员驾驶。
The prisoner was flown to another prison. = 囚犯被送往另一个监狱。


疑问句:
Does this flight go to Chicago? = 这趟航班去芝加哥吗？
Did they escape? = 他们逃脱了吗？


否定句:
The plane did not fly. = 飞机没有飞。
He did not escape. = 他没有逃脱。


PHRASE->
in flight = 在飞行中
take flight = 起飞；逃走
a flight of stairs = 一段楼梯
a flight of fancy = 一时的幻想
a flight of imagination = 想象力
