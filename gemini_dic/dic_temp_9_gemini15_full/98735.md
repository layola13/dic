insurmountably: /ˌɪnsərˈmaʊntəbli/ | adj. | 不可逾越地，无法克服地 |  In a way that cannot be overcome or surpassed.  It describes a situation or problem that is impossible to deal with or solve. |


例句：

The challenges facing the company were insurmountably difficult. = 面对公司的挑战是难以克服的。

The task proved insurmountably hard, even for the most experienced team. =  这项任务证明即使对于最有经验的团队来说也是难以完成的。


近义词：

invincibly, unconquerably, insuperably


反义词：

surmountably, conquerable, manageable


词性：形容词
