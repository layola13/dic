depletable: /dɪˈpliːtəbl/ | adj. | 可耗尽的，可枯竭的 | Capable of being depleted; exhaustible.  Something that is depletable can be used up or reduced to a low level. |


The Earth's natural resources, like oil and coal, are depletable. = 地球上的自然资源，例如石油和煤炭，是可耗尽的。

We need to find sustainable alternatives to our depletable energy sources. = 我们需要找到可持续的替代能源来替代我们目前的可耗尽能源。


近义词: exhaustible, finite, consumable


反义词: inexhaustible, infinite, renewable


词性: adjective
