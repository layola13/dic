roughhead:/ˈrʌfhed/| n.|粗头，（鱼）多棘鱼|A roughhead is a type of fish characterized by its rough, spiny head.  The term can also be used more generally to describe someone or something with a rough or unkempt head of hair.|例句：The fisherman caught a large roughhead.  渔夫钓到了一条大棘鱼。 The child, with his roughhead and dirty clothes, looked neglected.  那个孩子蓬头垢面，衣衫褴褛，看起来很受忽视。|近义词：spinyhead,  (for person) unkempt, shaggy|反义词：smoothhead, (for person) well-groomed
