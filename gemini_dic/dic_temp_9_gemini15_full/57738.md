dream:/driːm/| n. v.|梦，梦想；做梦|A dream is a series of thoughts, images, and sensations occurring during sleep.  Dreaming can be a way to process emotions, memories, and experiences.  As a verb, it means to have a dream or to imagine something desirable.|例句：I had a strange dream last night.  昨晚我做了一个奇怪的梦。

例句：She dreams of becoming a doctor. 她梦想成为一名医生。|近义词：fantasy, vision, aspiration|反义词：reality, actuality|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I dream every night. = 我每晚都做梦。
He dreams of success. = 他梦想成功。

一般过去时:
I dreamt of flying last night. = 我昨晚梦见飞翔了。
She dreamed of a better life. = 她梦想过更好的生活。

现在完成时:
I have dreamed of this moment for years. = 我多年来一直梦想这一刻。
He has dreamt of traveling the world. = 他一直梦想环游世界。

过去完成时:
I had dreamt of meeting her before I actually did. = 在我真正见到她之前，我就梦见过她。
They had dreamed of owning a house for a long time. = 他们很久以前就梦想拥有一个房子。


将来时:
I will dream sweet dreams tonight. = 今晚我会做个好梦。
She will dream of her future. = 她将梦想她的未来。


虚拟语气:
I wish I could dream of better things. = 我希望我能梦想更好的事情。
If I were you, I would dream bigger. = 如果我是你，我会梦想更大。


被动语态:
(The verb "dream" is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
What did you dream about last night? = 你昨晚梦到什么了？
Do you often dream? = 你经常做梦吗？


否定句:
I didn't dream at all last night. = 我昨晚根本没做梦。
He doesn't dream very often. = 他不经常做梦。
