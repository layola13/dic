headbanging:/ˈhedˌbæŋɪŋ/| n. |甩头；激烈的摇头|The act of repeatedly banging one's head, typically to the rhythm of music, often associated with heavy metal music and other genres.  It can be a form of self-expression, a release of energy, or simply a way to enjoy the music.|例句：The crowd was headbanging wildly during the band's final song.  人群在乐队的最后一首歌中疯狂地甩头。

例句：He injured his neck from excessive headbanging at the concert. 他在音乐会上过度甩头导致颈部受伤。


近义词：head-banging (same meaning, alternative spelling), rocking (in the context of rocking one's head to music)|反义词：None directly; antonyms would describe the opposite of the action, such as stillness or calmness.


词性: Noun
