reexchanges:/ˌriːɪksˈtʃeɪndʒɪz/| n.|再交换；重新交换|The act of exchanging something again, often after an initial exchange.  It implies a second or further exchange of goods, information, or ideas.|例句：The two countries engaged in reexchanges of diplomatic personnel after a period of strained relations.  两个国家在关系紧张一段时间后重新进行了外交人员的交换。|例句：The market saw significant reexchanges of stocks following the announcement.  公告发布后，市场出现了大量的股票重新交易。|近义词：re-exchanges,  further exchanges, renewed exchanges|反义词：single exchange, initial exchange|名词

The company reexchanges faulty products for new ones. = 公司将有故障的产品与新的产品进行重新交换。
They frequently reexchange ideas during brainstorming sessions. = 他们在头脑风暴会议期间经常重新交换想法。
