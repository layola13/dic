self-interpreted:/ˌsɛlfɪnˈtɜːrpɹɪtɪd/|/ˈsɛlfɪnˈtɜːrprɪtɪd/| adj. |自我解释的，自行诠释的 |Describes something that is understood or explained by the individual experiencing or encountering it, without external input or confirmation.  It implies a personal and potentially subjective interpretation. |The painting was self-interpreted by the artist, leading to multiple possible meanings.  这幅画是艺术家自己解释的，导致多种可能的含义。|近义词：subjective, personally interpreted, individually understood |反义词：objectively interpreted, universally understood
