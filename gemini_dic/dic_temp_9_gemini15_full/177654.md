recabled:/ˌriːˈkeɪbld/| v. |重新布线|To replace or reinstall the cables of (something).  This usually implies a complete or substantial replacement of the existing cabling system, not just a minor repair.|例句：The entire building was recabled to improve network speed.  整个建筑物都重新布线以提高网络速度。 The technician recabled the server after the power surge.  技术员在电源浪涌后重新连接了服务器。|近义词：rewired, reconnected,  re-routed|反义词：disconnected, uncabled
