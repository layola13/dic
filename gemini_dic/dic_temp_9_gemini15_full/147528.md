overpassing: /ˌoʊvərˈpæsɪŋ/ | v. |超越，忽略，越过 |The act of going over or beyond something; neglecting or disregarding something.  It often implies a disregard for rules or limitations. |例句：His overpassing of the speed limit resulted in a ticket. 他超速行驶导致被罚单。

例句：The committee's overpassing of the objections led to a disastrous outcome. 委员会无视反对意见导致了灾难性的结果。


近义词：transgressing, neglecting, disregarding, bypassing, ignoring, overlooking

反义词：observing, heeding, respecting, considering, acknowledging

词源:
The word "overpassing" is formed from the prefix "over-" (meaning above, beyond, or excessively) and the verb "passing" (meaning going past or beyond).  It's a relatively straightforward combination reflecting the word's meaning.
