rafts:/ræfts/| n.|木筏；救生艇；大量；(一批) |A raft is a flat structure made of logs or other buoyant material, used as a float or small boat.  It can also refer to a large number or quantity of something. |例句：They built a raft to cross the river.他们造了一只木筏过河。


例句：The river was filled with rafts of logs.河里漂着大量的木头。


近义词：float, debris


反义词：submersible, single unit


词性：名词
