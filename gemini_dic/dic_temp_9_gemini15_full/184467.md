restacking: /ˌriːˈstækɪŋ/ | v. |重新堆放，重新码放 |The act of stacking something again, often after it has been disturbed or reorganized.  This involves rearranging items into a new stack or stacks. |例句：The warehouse workers are restacking the boxes after the delivery. 仓库工人正在卸货后重新码放箱子。

The librarian is restacking the books after the book fair. 图书管理员在图书展后正在重新整理书籍。


近义词：rearranging, reorganizing, restacking, re-shelving |反义词：unstacking, scattering, dispersing |词性:动词
