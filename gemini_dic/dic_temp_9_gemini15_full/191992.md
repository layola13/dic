scoria:/ˈskɔːriə/| n. |火山渣，渣块|Dark, glassy volcanic rock with a vesicular texture.  It is formed when lava cools rapidly and contains numerous gas bubbles. Scoria is often reddish-brown or black in color and is relatively light in weight.|The volcanic eruption left behind a field of dark scoria.|pumice, cinders|solid rock|
