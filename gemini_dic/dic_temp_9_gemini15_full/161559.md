potshotted:/ˈpɑːtʃɒtɪd/ | v. |  随意射击；随意批评；信口开河 | To make a casual or haphazard shot at something, often without aiming carefully; to make a casual or unthinking criticism. | 例句：The comedian potshotted the politician's hairstyle during his act.  喜剧演员在表演中随意批评了这位政治家的发型。

The journalist potshotted the company's new product in his review.  记者在他的评论中随意批评了该公司的新产品。


近义词：criticize, snipe at,  take a shot at,  lambaste (较为强烈)

反义词：praise, commend, applaud
