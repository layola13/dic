rework:/ˌriːˈwɜːrk/| vt. n.|返工；修改；重新制作|To work on (something) again in order to improve or correct it.  This can refer to a process, a product, or a piece of writing.|例句：We had to rework the design after the initial tests revealed flaws. 我们在初步测试发现缺陷后不得不重新设计。

例句：The editor asked her to rework the final chapter. 编辑让她修改最后一章。


近义词：redo, revise, amend, revamp, refine|反义词：finish, complete|动词
