showman:/ˈʃoʊmæn/| n.|表演者，炫耀者|A person who is skilled at putting on a show; someone who is flamboyant or theatrical in their behaviour.|例句：He was a natural showman, captivating the audience with his charisma. 他是一个天生的表演者，以其魅力吸引了观众。 He's a bit of a showman, always trying to impress people. 他有点爱炫耀，总是试图给人们留下深刻印象。|近义词：performer, entertainer, impresario|反义词：wallflower, introvert
