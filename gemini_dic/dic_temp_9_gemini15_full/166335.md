prerelate:/ˌpriːrɪˈleɪt/ | v. |预先关联；预先联系；预先建立关系 |To connect or relate something beforehand; to establish a connection or relationship in advance.  |例句：The researchers prerelated the experimental data to the theoretical model. 研究人员将实验数据预先与理论模型关联起来。

例句：The team prerelated the project timeline to the funding availability. 团队预先将项目时间表与资金可用性联系起来。


近义词：preconnect, preassociate, foreshadow

反义词：disconnect, disassociate,  postrelate (not a standard word, but conveys the opposite meaning)

词性:动词
