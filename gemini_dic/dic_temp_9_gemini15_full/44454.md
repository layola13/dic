cross-the-line:/krɒs ðə laɪn/ | phrasal verb | 逾越界限；越界；行为过分 | To cross the line means to behave in a way that is unacceptable or goes beyond what is considered appropriate. This phrase often implies a transgression of moral, ethical, or social boundaries. | Example:  He crossed the line when he started making personal attacks during the debate. 他在辩论中开始人身攻击，已经逾越了界限。


Example:  Her joke crossed the line and offended many people in the audience. 她的玩笑太过分了，冒犯了台下许多人。


Near synonyms: overstep, transgress, violate, exceed, go too far


Antonyms:  respect boundaries, remain within limits, behave appropriately


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He crosses the line frequently. = 他经常越界。
She crosses the line with her comments. = 她说话总是越界。

一般过去时:
He crossed the line last night. = 他昨晚越界了。
They crossed the line during the negotiation. = 他们在谈判中越界了。

现在完成时:
He has crossed the line many times. = 他已经多次越界了。
She has crossed the line and lost all credibility. = 她已经越界，失去了所有的信誉。

过去完成时:
He had crossed the line before I even spoke. = 我还没开口他就已经越界了。
They had crossed the line, and the deal was off. = 他们已经越界，交易告吹了。

将来时:
He will cross the line if he continues this behavior. = 如果他继续这种行为，他会越界的。
She will cross the line if she isn't careful. = 她如果不小心，就会越界。


虚拟语气:
If he crossed the line, he would face consequences. = 如果他越界，他将面临后果。
I wish he hadn't crossed the line like that. = 我真希望他不要那样越界。

被动语态:
The line was crossed by him. = 界限被他逾越了。（较为不自然，很少用被动语态）
The line was crossed during the meeting. = 会议期间界限被逾越了。

疑问句:
Did he cross the line? = 他越界了吗？
Has she ever crossed the line? = 她曾经越界过吗？

否定句:
He didn't cross the line. = 他没有越界。
She hasn't crossed the line yet. = 她还没有越界。


PHRASE->
cross the line of decency  = 逾越体面的界限
cross the line into something = 发展到…的程度
cross the line between...and... = 越过…和…之间的界限
cross the line of acceptable behavior = 逾越可接受的行为界限
cross the moral line = 逾越道德底线
