eruptiveness: /ɪˈrʌptɪvnəs/ | n. | 爆发性；猛烈；突然发生 | The sudden and violent nature of something, especially a volcanic eruption or a similar outburst of energy or emotion.  It refers to the tendency or likelihood of something to erupt or burst forth suddenly and forcefully. |

例句：

The eruptiveness of the volcano surprised everyone.  = 火山的爆发性让每个人都大吃一惊。

The eruptiveness of his anger was frightening. = 他怒气的爆发令人恐惧。


近义词：explosiveness, outburst, vehemence, suddenness, violence


反义词：calmness, placidity, serenity, gradualness


词性：名词
