/hɛndʒɪz/| n.|围栏；篱笆；圈地；（尤指史前的）环状土木结构|A henge is a prehistoric monument, typically consisting of a circular enclosure of large stones.  The word is also used more generally to refer to any circular enclosure or fence.|The Stonehenge is a famous henge.|近义词：enclosure, fence, ring|反义词：opening
