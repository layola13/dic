cricking:/ˈkrɪkɪŋ/| n.| 嘎吱声；颈部疼痛 | The sound or sensation of something creaking or cracking, often referring to the neck.  It can also refer to the act of making such a sound.| 例句：I heard a cricking sound from the old door. 我听到旧门发出嘎吱声。
The cricking in my neck is unbearable. 我脖子嘎吱嘎吱的疼得受不了。|近义词：creaking, cracking, snapping |反义词：smooth, silent.
