gipsyisms:/ˈdʒɪpsiˌɪzəmz/ | n. | 吉普赛人的习语；吉普赛语；流浪者的语言 |  Words or phrases characteristic of the speech of Romani people (often considered slang or dialect).  The term can be considered offensive due to its historical association with negative stereotypes.  |例句：His speech was peppered with gipsyisms, making it difficult for outsiders to understand. 他的讲话中夹杂着吉普赛习语，使外人难以理解。


例句：The novel is filled with colourful gipsyisms that add to its authenticity. 这部小说充满了色彩鲜艳的吉普赛习语，增加了它的真实性。


近义词：Romani words, dialect, slang, argot


反义词：Standard language, formal language


词性: 名词
