restrengthened:/ˌriːˈstrɛŋθənd/| v. |重新加强；增强；巩固 |To make stronger again; to reinforce.  The strengthened economy has helped to restrengthened the social fabric.  经济的复苏帮助重新巩固了社会结构。|近义词：reinforce, strengthen, consolidate |反义词：weaken, undermine


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They restrengthen their defenses. = 他们加强他们的防御。
The government restrengthens its commitment to education. = 政府加强了其对教育的承诺。


一般过去时:
The alliance restrengthened its position after the victory. = 联盟在胜利后巩固了其地位。
He restrengthened his resolve to succeed.  = 他重新下定决心要成功。


现在完成时:
The company has restrengthened its market share. = 公司已经重新巩固了其市场份额。
We have restrengthened our ties with our allies. = 我们已经加强了与盟友的关系。


过去完成时:
They had restrengthened their defenses before the attack. = 他们在攻击前已经加强了他们的防御。
She had restrengthened her belief in her abilities before the competition. = 她在比赛前已经增强了她对自身能力的信心。


将来时:
They will restrengthen the bridge after the flood. = 洪水过后他们将加固桥梁。
The team will restrengthen its lineup for the playoffs. = 球队将为季后赛加强阵容。


虚拟语气:
If they had restrengthened their defenses, they would have won the battle. = 如果他们加强了防御，他们就会赢得战斗。
I wish they had restrengthened the building's foundation.  = 我希望他们当时加固了建筑的地基。


被动语态:
The economy was restrengthened by government intervention. = 经济因政府干预而得到加强。
The team's morale was restrengthened by the coach's speech. = 球队的士气因教练的讲话而得到增强。


疑问句:

否定句:
