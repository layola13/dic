skews:/skjuːz/ | v. | 歪斜；曲解；使有偏差 | To cause (something) to be not straight or aligned; to give a misleading or false account of; to distort or bias (data or results). |例句：The strong wind skewed the boat.  强风使船偏离航向。 |例句：He skewed the facts to support his argument. 他歪曲事实以支持他的论点。 |近义词：distort, warp, slant, bias |反义词：straighten, align, correct
