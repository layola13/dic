mendacity:/menˈdæsɪti/ | n. |虚假；谎言；欺骗性 |The act of being untruthful; dishonesty; falseness.  It refers to the state or quality of being mendacious, characterized by a tendency to lie or deceive. |例句：His mendacity was exposed when his alibi fell apart.  他的谎言在他不在场的证据被揭穿后暴露无遗。

His entire defense rested on a foundation of mendacity.  他的整个辩护都建立在谎言的基础上。|近义词：deceit, falsehood, deception, prevarication |反义词：truth, honesty, veracity
