self-promotions:/ˈsɛlf prəˈmoʊʃənz/| n.|自我推销；自我宣传|The act of promoting oneself or one's work, often through advertising or other forms of publicity.  It can involve highlighting one's skills, achievements, or expertise to gain recognition or advance one's career.  This can be done through various channels, such as social media, networking events, or creating a personal brand.|例句：His constant self-promotions on social media annoyed many of his followers. 他在社交媒体上不断自我推销，惹恼了许多粉丝。

例句：The company's self-promotions were effective in increasing brand awareness.  公司的自我宣传有效地提高了品牌知名度。|近义词：self-advertisement, publicity, promotion, marketing|反义词：self-deprecation, modesty
