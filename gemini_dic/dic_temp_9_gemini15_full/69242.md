feast-days:/ˈfiːstˌdeɪz/ | n. |节日，庆典日 |Days set aside for religious or cultural celebration, often involving special meals, ceremonies, or observances.|例句：Christmas and Easter are important feast-days in many countries.  圣诞节和复活节是许多国家重要的节日。|例句：Many feast-days are celebrated with traditional foods and festivities. 许多节日都以传统食物和庆祝活动来庆祝。|近义词：holidays, festivals, celebrations |反义词：workdays, ordinary days
