ingredient:/ɪnˈɡriːdiənt/| n. |成分，原料 |A component of a mixture, especially a food mixture.  An essential element. |Example: The main ingredients of this cake are flour, sugar, and eggs.  这个蛋糕的主要成分是面粉、糖和鸡蛋。|近义词：component, constituent, element |反义词：product, result
