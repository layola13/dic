digitizing:/ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |数字化；将…转换成数字形式 |To convert information into a digital format, suitable for use by a computer.  This can involve various processes, such as scanning images, converting audio to MP3, or encoding text. |例句：The museum is digitizing its entire collection of photographs.  博物馆正在将其全部照片收藏数字化。

The company is digitizing its customer records. 这家公司正在将其客户记录数字化。


近义词：digitalizing, converting to digital | 反义词：analogizing
