personal:/ˈpɜːrsənəl/|adj.|个人的，私人的；亲自的|relating to a particular person; private and confidential.|例句：This is my personal opinion. 这是我个人的意见。|例句：He made a personal appearance at the meeting. 他亲自出席了会议。|近义词：private, individual, own|反义词：public, shared, impersonal

USAGE->

This is my personal car. = 这是我的私家车。
His personal beliefs influence his work. = 他的个人信仰影响他的工作。

She shared her personal experiences with us. = 她与我们分享了她的个人经历。
