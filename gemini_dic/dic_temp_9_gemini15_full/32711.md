chlorinates:/ˈklɔːrɪneɪts/| vt.|用氯处理；使氯化|To treat (something) with chlorine.  The process involves adding chlorine to a substance, often to disinfect or purify it.|例句：The city chlorinates its water supply to kill bacteria.  城市用氯处理自来水以杀死细菌。

例句：The swimming pool is chlorinated daily to maintain hygiene. 游泳池每天都进行氯化处理以保持卫生。


近义词：treat with chlorine, disinfect


反义词：dechlorinate


词性：动词
