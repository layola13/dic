rasterize:/ˈræstəraɪz/| vt.|光栅化，栅格化|To convert an image or graphic into a raster image, which is made up of a grid of pixels.|例句：The software rasterizes vector graphics into bitmaps for display on the screen.  软件将矢量图形光栅化为位图以便在屏幕上显示。

例句：We need to rasterize this image before printing it on the t-shirt. 我们需要先将这幅图像光栅化，才能把它印在T恤上。


近义词：pixelate, grid


反义词：vectorize


词性:动词
