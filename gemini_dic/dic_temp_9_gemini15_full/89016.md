例句：The vast, empty landscape filled her with a sense of hollowness.  广阔空旷的景色让她感到空虚。


近义词：emptiness, void, vacancy, insincerity, fakeness, meaninglessness

反义词：fullness, solidity, sincerity, substance, reality
