nondomestically:/ˌnɑːnˌdəˈmɛstɪkli/ | adv. |非国内地；在国外；国际上 |Adverb meaning "not domestically" or "in a foreign country".  It indicates that something is happening or is done outside of a particular country's borders, often in an international context. |例句：The company sources some of its materials nondomestically. 这家公司从国外采购部分材料。

The product is sold nondomestically through a network of international distributors. 该产品通过国际经销商网络在国外销售。|近义词：internationally, globally, overseas, abroad |反义词：domestically
