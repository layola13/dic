resworn:/riːˈswɔːrn/| v.|重新宣誓，再次宣誓|To swear an oath again; to reaffirm a previous oath or commitment.  The verb "resworn" is the past participle of "reswear."|例句：The judge resworn the witness after he admitted to lying during his initial testimony. = 法官在证人承认在最初的证词中撒谎后让他再次宣誓。
The newly elected president was resworn into office. = 新当选的总统再次宣誓就职。|近义词：reaffirmed, renewed, rededicated|反义词：resigned, renounced
