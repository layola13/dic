clicher:/ˈkliʃeɪ/| n.|陈词滥调，老生常谈|A cliché is a phrase or opinion that is overused and betrays a lack of original thought.|例句：His description of the sunset was a cliché.  他的日落描写是陈词滥调。|例句：Using clichés in your writing makes it sound unoriginal. 在写作中使用陈词滥调会使它听起来缺乏创意。|近义词：platitude, truism, stereotype|反义词：originality, novelty
