pranging:/ˈpræŋɪŋ/| v.|乱敲，乱打，猛击 | To strike or hit something repeatedly and forcefully, often in a disorganized or uncontrolled manner.  The term often implies a noisy and potentially damaging action.|例句：The children were pranging on the drums all afternoon. 孩子们整个下午都在猛敲鼓。|例句：The hail pranged against the windows. 冰雹猛烈地击打着窗户。|近义词：banging, hammering, hitting, thrashing |反义词：gently tapping, caressing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pranging the metal with a hammer. = 他正在用锤子猛击金属。
They are pranging on the door. = 他们正在猛敲门。


一般过去时:
The vandals pranged the car windows. = 那些破坏分子砸碎了汽车的窗户。
He pranged the piano keys furiously. = 他愤怒地猛击钢琴键。


现在完成时:
The storm has pranged the roof tiles. = 暴风雨已经击坏了屋顶瓦片。
They have pranged the fence with a baseball bat. = 他们用棒球棍猛击篱笆。


过去完成时:
The thieves had pranged the safe open before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，小偷们已经猛击保险箱将其打开。
The wind had pranged the branches against the house before the storm subsided. = 在暴风雨平息之前，风已经把树枝猛烈地撞击到房子上。


将来时:

The protesters will prang on the gates of the parliament. = 抗议者们将猛烈敲打议会的大门。


虚拟语气:
If he pranged the door hard enough, it might open. = 如果他用力猛击门，它可能会打开。
I wish he hadn't pranged the vase. = 我真希望他没有猛击花瓶。


被动语态:
The window was pranged by a stray baseball. = 窗户被一颗飞来的棒球击碎了。
The door has been pranged repeatedly by angry customers. = 门已经被愤怒的顾客反复猛击。


疑问句:
Was the car pranged in the accident? = 这辆车在事故中被撞了吗？（此处pranged 指的是损坏）
Are they pranging on the walls again? = 他们又在猛击墙壁了吗？


否定句:
He didn't prang the drum very hard. = 他并没有用力敲鼓。
She didn't prang the door; she knocked politely. = 她没有猛击门；她礼貌地敲门。
