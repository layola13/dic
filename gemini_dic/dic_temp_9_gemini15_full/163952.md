predicating:/ˈprɛdɪkeɪtɪŋ/| v. |断定；基于…作论断；以…为前提|  The act of stating something as a fact or belief; asserting; affirming.  It often involves basing a claim on evidence or assumptions. |例句：He was predicating his argument on faulty data. 他基于错误的数据来论证他的观点。

例句：She is predicating her happiness on the success of her business. 她把快乐寄托在事业的成功上。


近义词：asserting, affirming, postulating, assuming


反义词：denying, refuting, contradicting


词性：动词
