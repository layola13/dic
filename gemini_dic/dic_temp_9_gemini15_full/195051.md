semibarbaric:/ˌsɛmɪˈbɑːrˌbærɪk/| adj.|半野蛮的，半开化的|Partially or somewhat barbaric; exhibiting characteristics of both civilization and barbarity.  It suggests a blend of refined and unrefined aspects, often with a hint of savagery lurking beneath a veneer of civility. | The semibarbaric king's court was a strange mix of opulent decor and brutal punishments.  半野蛮的国王的宫廷奇异地混合着奢华的装饰和残酷的惩罚。|近义词：half-civilized, partly savage, uncouth |反义词：civilized, refined, cultured
