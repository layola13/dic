reagree:/ˌriːəˈɡriː/ | v. | 再同意，重新同意 | To agree again; to agree once more after previous disagreement or reconsideration.  | Example: After further discussion, they reagreed to the terms of the contract.  再次讨论后，他们重新同意了合同条款。|近义词：agree again, reaffirm, concur again | 反义词：disagree, dissent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reagree on the project's timeline. = 他们重新就项目的期限达成一致。
We reagree to meet at 3 p.m. = 我们重新约定下午3点见面。


一般过去时:
The committee reagreed to the proposed changes. = 委员会重新同意了提议的变更。
He reagreed to her request after careful consideration. = 他经过仔细考虑后，重新同意了她的请求。


现在完成时:
They have reagreed to the revised budget. = 他们已经重新同意了修订后的预算。
We have reagreed to collaborate on the next project. = 我们已经重新同意在下一个项目上合作。


过去完成时:
They had reagreed to the terms before the deadline. = 他们早在截止日期前就重新同意了条款。
She had reagreed to the offer after reconsidering her options. = 在重新考虑了她的选择后，她重新同意了这个提议。


将来时:
They will reagree to the contract after reviewing the amendments. = 他们将在审查修正案后重新同意合同。
We will reagree on the strategy once we have more data. = 一旦我们有了更多数据，我们将重新就战略达成一致。


虚拟语气:
If they reagreed, the project could proceed. = 如果他们重新同意，项目就能继续进行。
I wish they had reagreed to my proposal. = 我希望他们能重新同意我的提议。


被动语态:
The decision was reagreed upon by the board. = 该决定得到了董事会的重新同意。
The plan was reagreed to after several revisions. = 该计划经过多次修改后得到了重新同意。


疑问句:
Did they reagree to the terms? = 他们是否重新同意了条款？
Will they reagree to the proposal? = 他们会重新同意这个提议吗？


否定句:
They did not reagree to the original plan. = 他们没有重新同意最初的计划。
The parties failed to reagree on a compromise. = 各方未能就妥协重新达成一致。


PHRASE->
reagree on  重新就...达成一致
reagree to 重新同意
reagree with  重新同意某人的意见
reagree the terms 重新商定条款
reagree the contract 重新签订合同
