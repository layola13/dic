quasi-poetical:/ˈkwɑːzaɪˌpoʊɪtɪkl/|/ˌkwɑːziːˈpəʊɪtɪkəl/ | adj. | 类似诗歌的；略带诗意的 |  Having qualities resembling poetry, but not fully meeting the criteria of formal poetry; suggestive of poetry.  It often implies a heightened or imaginative use of language, but without the strict metrical or rhyming patterns of traditional poetry. |The novel's prose had a quasi-poetical quality, filled with evocative imagery and metaphorical language. 这部小说的散文具有类似诗歌的品质，充满了富有表现力的意象和比喻语言。


|近义词：poetic, lyrical, figurative, evocative, expressive |反义词：prosaic, literal, unimaginative, mundane |
