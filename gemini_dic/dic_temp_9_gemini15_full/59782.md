ecoterrorism:/ˌekoʊˈtɛrərɪzəm/ | n. |生态恐怖主义 |The use of violence or the threat of violence to achieve environmental or political goals.  It often involves actions that damage or destroy property or harm people. |Example: The bombing of the logging company's office was considered an act of ecoterrorism.  这次对伐木公司办公室的炸弹袭击被认为是一起生态恐怖主义行为。|近义词：environmental terrorism, radical environmentalism|反义词：environmental protection, conservation
