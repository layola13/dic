pre-lunch: /ˌpriːˈlʌntʃ/ | adj. | 午餐前的 |  Referring to the time period or activities immediately before lunch.  It often implies a sense of anticipation or preparation for the meal. | 例句：We had a pre-lunch meeting to discuss the project. = 我们在午餐前开会讨论项目。

例句：The pre-lunch rush at the cafe was intense. = 咖啡馆午餐前的拥挤非常激烈。


近义词：ante-lunch, before-lunch
反义词：post-lunch, after-lunch
词性：形容词
