employing: /ɪmˈplɔɪɪŋ/ | v. | 使用；雇用；运用 |  The act of using or putting something to work, or the act of hiring someone for a job.  | 例句：The company is employing new technologies to improve efficiency. = 这家公司正在采用新技术来提高效率。


例句：They are employing a new marketing strategy. = 他们正在采用一种新的营销策略。


近义词：using, utilizing, hiring, engaging


反义词：dismissing, firing, neglecting, ignoring


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is employing hundreds of people. = 这家公司雇佣了数百人。
She is employing a new teaching method. = 她正在采用一种新的教学方法。

一般过去时:
They employed a lot of people last year. = 他们去年雇佣了很多员工。
The artist employed vibrant colors in his painting. = 艺术家在他的画作中使用了鲜艳的色彩。

现在完成时:
We have employed several new strategies this year. = 今年我们已经采用了多种新策略。
The factory has employed over a thousand workers since it opened. = 自工厂开业以来，已经雇佣了超过一千名工人。

过去完成时:
They had employed many workers before the factory closed. = 在工厂倒闭之前，他们已经雇佣了许多工人。
The company had employed that strategy unsuccessfully in the past. = 公司过去曾尝试过该策略，但未成功。

将来时:
They will employ more staff next year. = 他们明年将雇佣更多员工。
We will be employing new software to manage the project. = 我们将使用新的软件来管理项目。

虚拟语气:
If they employed more skilled workers, the product quality would improve. = 如果他们雇佣更多熟练工人，产品质量将会提高。
I wish they employed a more effective marketing approach. = 我希望他们采用更有效的营销方法。

被动语态:
New technologies are being employed to improve the process. = 正在采用新技术来改进流程。
Many workers were employed by the construction company. = 许多工人被这家建筑公司雇用。

疑问句:
Are they employing anyone new? = 他们正在雇用新人吗？
What methods are you employing to solve this problem? = 你正在采用什么方法来解决这个问题？

否定句:
They are not employing anyone at the moment. = 他们目前没有雇用任何人。
The company did not employ enough qualified staff. = 这家公司没有雇用足够的合格员工。
