overfill:/ˌoʊvərˈfɪl/ | vt. |过度填充，装满过多 |To fill something to excess; to fill beyond its capacity. |例句：Don't overfill the bathtub, or it will overflow.  不要把浴缸装得太满，否则会溢出来。  |例句：He overfilled the suitcase and had to check a second bag. 他把行李箱塞得太满了，不得不托运第二个行李箱。|近义词：overflow, overload, cram |反义词：underfill, deplete |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overfill the glasses every time. = 他们每次都把杯子装得太满。
He overfills his schedule with appointments. = 他把日程安排得满满的。


一般过去时:
She overfilled the washing machine last night. = 她昨晚把洗衣机装得太满了。
We overfilled the car with luggage for the trip. = 我们把车装满了行李去旅行。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

She had overfilled the forms with unnecessary information. = 她已经把表格填写得过多，包含了不必要的信息。

虚拟语气:
