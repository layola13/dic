indentations:/ˌɪnˌdenˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 缩进，(文本的)段落缩进 | Indentations are used to improve the readability of text.  缩进用于提高文本的可读性。 |近义词：indents, recesses | 反义词：alignment, flush
