pasteurized:/ˈpæstʃəraɪzd/| adj.|巴氏消毒的；经过巴氏灭菌的|Treated with heat to kill harmful bacteria, typically at a temperature of around 72°C for 15 seconds. This process is used to preserve food and beverages like milk and juice.  |例句：This milk is pasteurized and safe to drink. 这牛奶经过巴氏消毒，可以安全饮用。|例句：The juice is pasteurized to extend its shelf life.  这款果汁经过巴氏灭菌以延长保质期。|近义词：sterilized, treated (with heat)|反义词：raw, unpasteurized|形容词
