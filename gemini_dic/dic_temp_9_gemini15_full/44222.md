crockery:/ˈkrɒkəri/ | n. |陶器，瓦器|Pottery, especially earthenware, used for domestic purposes.  It typically refers to a collection of plates, dishes, cups, and other tableware. |例句：We need to buy some new crockery for the kitchen. 我们需要为厨房买一些新的陶器。

例句：She carefully arranged the delicate crockery on the shelf. 她小心翼翼地把精致的陶器摆放在架子上。


近义词：pottery, earthenware, tableware, china


反义词：None (no direct antonym exists; the opposite concept would be something like "metalware" or "plasticware")

词源:
The word "crockery" ultimately derives from the Middle English word "crok," meaning "pot."  This term evolved through various stages, linking to words signifying earthenware vessels, and eventually settling into its modern usage encompassing a range of domestic pottery.
