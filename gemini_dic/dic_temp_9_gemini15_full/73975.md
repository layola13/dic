fortition:/fɔːrˈtɪʃən/| n.|防御工事；强化；巩固|The act of strengthening or reinforcing something, especially a defensive position or structure.  It can also refer to the structure itself.|Example: The fortition of the castle walls made it almost impregnable. 城堡城墙的防御工事使其几乎坚不可摧。|近义词：reinforcement, strengthening, fortification|反义词：weakening, demolition
