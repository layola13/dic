dinged:/dɪŋd/| v. |轻微撞击；损坏；批评|To make a small, usually insignificant dent or mark on something; to criticize mildly. |例句：I dinged my car door on a lamppost.我把车门轻轻撞在路灯柱上了。
The critic dinged the movie for its predictable plot. 批评家批评这部电影的情节可预测。|近义词：dented, scratched, criticized, slammed|反义词：repaired, praised


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dings his car frequently. = 他经常刮坏他的车。
She dings the paint on her walls. = 她把墙上的油漆刮花了。

一般过去时:
I dinged the fender on my bike yesterday. = 我昨天把自行车的挡泥板刮花了。
He dinged the table when he moved it. = 他移动桌子时把桌子刮了一下。

现在完成时:
I have dinged my new phone case. = 我把我的新手机壳刮花了。
She has dinged the car several times this month. = 这个月她已经几次刮花了汽车。


过去完成时:
I had dinged the car before I even noticed. = 我甚至还没注意到就刮花了车。
They had dinged the wall before they painted it. = 他们在粉刷墙壁之前已经刮花了墙壁。


将来时:
I will probably ding the car again. = 我可能会再次刮坏汽车。
He will ding the wall if he's not careful. = 如果他不小心，他会刮坏墙壁。


虚拟语气:
If I had been more careful, I wouldn't have dinged the bumper. = 如果我更小心一点，我就不会刮坏保险杠了。
If I were to ding this antique table, I'd be devastated. = 如果我把这张古董桌子刮花了，我会很沮丧。


被动语态:
The car was dinged in the parking lot. = 车子在停车场被刮花了。
The wall was dinged by the moving boxes. = 墙壁被搬家的箱子刮花了。


疑问句:
Did you ding the car? = 你刮花了车吗？
Has she dinged the furniture yet? = 她把家具刮花了没有？


否定句:
I didn't ding the car. = 我没有刮坏车。
He hasn't dinged the table. = 他没有刮坏桌子。


PHRASE->
dinged up:  破损的，受损的
badly dinged: 严重受损的
slightly dinged: 轻微受损的
get dinged: 受损，受批评
dinged and dented: 凹凸不平的
