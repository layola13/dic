cloaks:/kloʊks/| n. |斗篷，掩盖物 |A cloak is a loose outer garment without sleeves, typically worn for warmth or protection from the weather. It can also refer to something that conceals or disguises something else. |例句：She wore a long, dark cloak to protect herself from the rain. 她穿着一件长长的黑色斗篷来保护自己免受雨淋。

The detective used a clever disguise to cloak his true identity.  侦探用巧妙的伪装来掩盖他的真实身份。|近义词：mantle, cape, cover, disguise |反义词：exposure, revelation


USAGE->

He cloaks his intentions carefully. 他小心翼翼地掩盖他的意图。
The mountain is cloaked in mist.  山被雾笼罩着。
