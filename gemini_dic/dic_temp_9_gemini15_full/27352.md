Cambodians:/kæmˈboʊdiən/| n. |柬埔寨人；柬埔寨语|The word "Cambodians" refers to the people of Cambodia, a country in Southeast Asia.  It can also refer to the Khmer language spoken in Cambodia.|例句：Many Cambodians are skilled farmers. 许多柬埔寨人是熟练的农民。

例句：The Cambodian government is working to improve infrastructure. 柬埔寨政府正在努力改善基础设施。


近义词：Khmer people (more formal)


反义词：无


词性：名词
