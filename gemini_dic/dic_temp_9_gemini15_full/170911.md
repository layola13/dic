pseudoministerial: /ˌsjuːdoʊˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəl/ | adj. | 似部长职务的；假装部长的；装腔作势的 |  Resembling or suggestive of a minister or ministerial office; affectedly or ostentatiously ministerial.  It often implies a pretense or superficial resemblance to the authority or formality associated with a minister. |


例句：

His pseudoministerial pronouncements were met with skepticism.  他的装腔作势的部长式声明遭到了怀疑。

The pseudoministerial style of the document made it difficult to understand.  该文件的装腔作势的部长式风格使其难以理解。


近义词：officious, pompous, pretentious, affected

反义词：unassuming, humble, modest


词源:

pseudo- (from Greek *pseudo-* meaning "false" or "sham") + ministerial (from minister + -ial,  referring to a minister or their office)
