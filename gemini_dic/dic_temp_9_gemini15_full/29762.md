catstitch: /ˈkætˌstɪtʃ/ | n. | 猫爪绣，猫步缝 | A type of embroidery stitch that resembles a cat's paw print.  It is a simple decorative stitch used to create a textured effect, often in a repeating pattern. | Example:  The blanket was adorned with a charming catstitch pattern.  这条毯子装饰着迷人的猫爪绣图案。 | 近义词: chain stitch (链式缝), running stitch (走线缝) | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑与复杂的绣法对比)
