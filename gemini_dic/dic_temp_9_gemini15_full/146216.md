overexpenditures:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspɛndɪtʃərz/| n.|超支，过度支出|Expenditures that exceed the budgeted or planned amount.  It refers to spending more money than was originally allocated or intended.|The company's overexpenditures led to a significant budget deficit. 公司的超支导致了严重的预算赤字。
The project's overexpenditures were attributed to unforeseen complications.  项目的超支归因于不可预见的复杂情况。|excess spending, budget overruns|underspending, savings|名词
