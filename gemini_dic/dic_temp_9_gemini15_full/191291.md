scavengers:/ˈskævɪndʒərz/| n.|食腐动物；拾荒者|Animals that feed on dead animals or decaying matter.  People who search for and collect discarded items. |例句：Vultures are scavengers that play a vital role in the ecosystem.  秃鹰是生态系统中起着至关重要作用的食腐动物。

Hyena are scavengers, often eating leftovers from other predators.  鬣狗是食腐动物，经常吃其他捕食者剩下的食物。


近义词：carrion eaters, foragers, rummagers, wreckers|反义词：hunters, producers, creators|名词
