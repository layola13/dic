feuds:/fjuːdz/| n.|世仇，长期不和|A feud is a prolonged and bitter quarrel or dispute.  It often involves families or groups, and can last for generations. |The Hatfields and McCoys had a famous feud.  |近义词：quarrel, dispute, conflict, rivalry|反义词：reconciliation, peace, harmony


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The families are still feuding over the land. =  这些家族还在为土地争吵不休。
He feuds with his neighbor over the property line. = 他和邻居为地界问题争吵。


一般过去时:
The two clans feuded for centuries. = 这两个家族世仇数百年。
They feuded bitterly over the inheritance. = 他们为遗产而激烈争吵。


现在完成时:
They have feuded since childhood. = 他们从小就一直不和。
The families have feuded for generations over a simple misunderstanding. =  几个世纪以来，这些家族因为一个简单的误解而争吵不休。


过去完成时:
They had feuded for years before they finally reached a settlement. = 在最终达成和解之前，他们已经争吵多年了。
The families had feuded for decades before the peace treaty was signed. =  在和平条约签署之前，这些家族已经争吵了几十年。


将来时:
I fear they will feud over the will again. = 我担心他们会再次为遗嘱争吵。
They will likely feud for years to come if the issue remains unresolved. = 如果问题得不到解决，他们很可能会继续争吵多年。


虚拟语气:
If they hadn't feuded, they might have become good friends. = 如果他们没有争吵，他们可能会成为好朋友。
I wish they wouldn't feud so openly. = 我希望他们不要公开争吵。


被动语态:
The feud was finally settled after many years. = 多年后，这场世仇终于得到了解决。
The matter was feuded over for years, causing deep division in the community. = 这件事被争吵多年，导致社区严重分裂。


疑问句:
Are they still feuding? = 他们还在争吵吗？
Why are those two families feuding? = 为什么那两个家族在争吵？


否定句:
They are not feuding anymore. = 他们不再争吵了。
The families did not feud openly; their conflict was mostly behind closed doors. =  这些家族并没有公开争吵；他们的冲突大多是私下进行的。
