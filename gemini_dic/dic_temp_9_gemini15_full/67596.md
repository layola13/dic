extravagances:/ɪkˈstrævəɡənsɪz/ | n. |奢侈；挥霍；过分 |  Excessive spending, luxury, or display; an instance of this.  It often implies a lack of restraint or excessive indulgence. |例句：His extravagant lifestyle led to financial ruin.他的奢侈生活导致了经济破产。

例句：The party was full of extravagant decorations and delicious food.  派对充满了奢华的装饰和美味的食物。


近义词：excesses, luxuries, indulgences, prodigality

反义词：frugality, thrift, economy, restraint


词性: 名词(复数)
