cognitivism:/ˈkɒɡnɪtɪvɪzəm/|/ˈkɑːɡnɪtɪvɪzəm/| n. |认知主义；认知论|The philosophical study of the mind and its processes, emphasizing the role of knowledge and understanding in shaping experience and behavior.  Cognitivism contrasts with behaviorism, which focuses primarily on observable actions. |Example:  Cognitivism is a dominant approach in contemporary psychology. 认知主义是当代心理学的主要方法。|近义词：mentalism, intellectualism|反义词：behaviorism
