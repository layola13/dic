forlornly:/fɔːˈlɔːrnli/ | adv. | 凄凉地；悲惨地；绝望地 | In a forlorn and hopeless manner; wretchedly.  It describes an action performed in a state of abandonment, misery, or despair. | 例句：He wandered forlornly through the deserted streets. 他凄凉地徘徊在空荡荡的街道上。

She looked forlornly out the window at the pouring rain. 她绝望地望着窗外倾盆大雨。


近义词：desolately, miserably, hopelessly, dejectedly


反义词：joyfully, happily, cheerfully, hopefully


词性: 副词
