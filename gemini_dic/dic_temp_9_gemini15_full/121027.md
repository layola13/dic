miscompute:/ˌmɪskəmˈpjuːt/| vt.|误算，计算错误|To calculate incorrectly.  This verb implies an error in the calculation process, rather than a simple mistake in the input data. |Example:  The engineers miscomputed the load-bearing capacity of the bridge, resulting in its collapse. 工程师们误算了桥梁的承重能力，导致桥梁坍塌。

Example: They miscomputed the project's timeline, leading to significant delays. 他们误算了项目的工期，导致了严重的延误。

近义词：miscalculate, err, make a mistake in calculating

反义词：compute accurately, calculate correctly
