increases:/ɪnˈkriːsɪz/| v.|增加，增长|To become or make greater in size, amount, number, or degree.|例句：The company's profits increased by 10% last year.  去年公司利润增长了10%。|例句：The demand for electric cars is increasing rapidly. 对电动汽车的需求正在迅速增长。|近义词：grow, augment, expand, heighten|反义词：decrease, diminish, reduce, lessen|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price of gas increases every year. = 天然气价格每年都在上涨。
Demand for skilled workers increases steadily. = 对熟练工人的需求稳步增长。

一般过去时:
The population increased dramatically after the war. = 战争结束后人口急剧增加。
My salary increased last month. = 我上个月涨工资了。

现在完成时:
The company's market share has increased significantly. = 公司的市场份额已大幅提高。
Global temperatures have increased over the past century. = 在过去的几十年里，全球气温上升了。

过去完成时:
The number of participants had increased by the time the conference began. = 到会议开始时，参与者的数量已经增加了。
Before the storm, the river had increased in volume. = 暴风雨来临之前，河水水量增加了。


将来时:
Sales are expected to increase next quarter. = 预计下个季度销售额将会增长。
The company plans to increase its investment in research and development. = 公司计划增加研发投资。

虚拟语气:
If the government increased funding, the project would be successful. = 如果政府增加资金，这个项目就能成功。
I wish the company would increase my salary. = 我希望公司能给我涨工资。

被动语态:
The tax rate was increased by the government. = 税率由政府提高了。
Production was increased to meet the growing demand. = 为了满足日益增长的需求，产量增加了。

疑问句:
Did the number of students increase this year? = 今年学生人数增加了没有？
Will your income increase next year? = 你的收入明年会增加吗？

否定句:
The number of birds did not increase last year. = 去年鸟类的数量没有增加。
The company's profits did not increase as expected. = 公司的利润并没有像预期那样增长。


PHRASE->
increase in = 增加
increase by = 增加
significant increase = 重大增长
steady increase = 稳定增长
gradual increase = 逐渐增加
