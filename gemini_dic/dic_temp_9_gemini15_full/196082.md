sephardic:/sɛfˈɑːrdɪk/| adj.|塞法迪的；西班牙犹太人的|Of or relating to Sephardic Jews or their culture and language.  Sephardic Jews are descendants of Jews who lived in the Iberian Peninsula (Spain and Portugal) before the expulsion of 1492. Their culture is rich and diverse, blending elements of Jewish tradition with influences from the Iberian Peninsula and other places where they settled after the expulsion.|The Sephardic community in this city is quite vibrant. 这个城市的塞法迪社区非常活跃。|Jewish, Iberian|Ashkenazi
