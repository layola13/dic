nectariferous:/ˌnek.təˈrɪf.ər.əs/| adj.|分泌花蜜的，蜜腺的|Producing or containing nectar.  The word combines "nectar" (sweet liquid produced by plants) and "-iferous" (bearing, producing).|Example:  Many nectariferous flowers attract pollinators with their sweet nectar. 许多分泌花蜜的花朵以其甜美的花蜜吸引传粉者。


The bee landed on a nectariferous blossom.  蜜蜂停落在一个分泌花蜜的花朵上。|近义词：honey-bearing, nectar-producing|反义词：nectarless (lacking nectar)
