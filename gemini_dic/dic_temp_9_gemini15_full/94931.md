in-name-of: /ɪnˈneɪməv/ | prep. | 以…的名义；代表… |  This prepositional phrase indicates that an action is performed or something is done on behalf of or in the authority of someone or something.  It implies authorization or representation. | 例句：They donated to the charity in the name of their deceased father. = 他们以他们已故父亲的名义向慈善机构捐款。


The company acted in the name of progress. = 公司以进步的名义行事。


近义词：on behalf of, in the stead of, representing | 反义词：in one's own name, personally


词性：介词短语
