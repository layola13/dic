one's: /wʌnz/ | pron. | 某人的；一个人的 |  Possessive pronoun indicating belonging to a person previously mentioned or easily understood from context.  It's a contraction of "one's own". |例句：One should always consider one's limitations.  人应该总是考虑自身的局限性。


例句：He needs to improve one's writing skills. 他需要提高自己的写作技能。


近义词：his, her, its, their, your, my  | 反义词：N/A | 词性：代词

词源:
The word "one's" is a contraction of "one's own," with "own" being an adjective indicating possession.  The word "one" itself has Old English roots, related to words meaning "single" or "one."  The possessive form developed naturally through grammatical evolution.
