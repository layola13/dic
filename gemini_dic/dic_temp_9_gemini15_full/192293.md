screwcap:/ˈskruːkæp/| n. |螺旋盖，旋盖 | A screwcap is a type of bottle cap that is screwed onto the bottle, rather than being pressed or snapped on.  It is commonly used for wine bottles and other beverages. |Example: The screwcap on the wine bottle was difficult to remove.  这个葡萄酒瓶的螺旋盖很难拧开。  The new screwcap design prevented leakage. 新的螺旋盖设计防止了泄漏。|近义词：twist-off cap, twist cap |反义词：cork, crown cap
