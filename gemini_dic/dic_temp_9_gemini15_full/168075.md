/ˈprɪvəlɪdʒər/| vt.|赋予特权；特别优待|To grant someone special rights or advantages.|The company privileges its top performers with extra vacation time.  这家公司给予其表现最佳的员工额外假期。|favor, benefit, advantage|disadvantage, disadvantage, penalize|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He privileges his children with expensive gifts. = 他用昂贵的礼物优待他的孩子。
The school privileges students with high GPAs. = 学校优待GPA高的学生。

一般过去时:
The king privileged his loyal subjects with land grants. = 国王用土地赏赐优待他的忠实臣民。
She privileged him with her confidence. = 她把她的信任给予了他。


现在完成时:
They have privileged their employees with flexible work arrangements. = 他们已经给予员工灵活的工作安排。
The company has privileged its customers with early access to the new product. = 公司已经给予客户优先体验新产品的权利。


过去完成时:
The judge had privileged the defendant with a lenient sentence. = 法官已经给予被告从轻判决。
She had privileged her friend with some confidential information. = 她已经向她的朋友透露了一些机密信息。


将来时:
The government will privilege certain industries with tax breaks. = 政府将给予某些行业税收优惠。
They will privilege the winners with a trip to Hawaii. = 他们将奖励优胜者去夏威夷旅行。


虚拟语气:
If the company privileged its employees more, they would be more loyal. = 如果公司更优待员工，他们会更忠诚。
I wish they privileged me with more information. = 我希望他们能给我更多信息。


被动语态:
Certain groups are privileged with access to better resources. = 某些群体享有获得更好资源的特权。
The information was privileged, and therefore couldn't be disclosed. = 该信息是特权信息，因此不能泄露。


疑问句:
Does the company privilege its employees with bonuses? = 公司是否给予员工奖金？
Are these clients privileged with special discounts? = 这些客户是否享有特别折扣？


否定句:
The system does not privilege one group over another. = 该系统不会优待任何一个群体。
The judge did not privilege the defendant with a lighter sentence. = 法官没有给予被告从轻判决。


PHRASE->
privilege to (do something):  荣幸做某事

privileged communication:  特权沟通
privileged position:  优越的地位
feel privileged: 感到荣幸
