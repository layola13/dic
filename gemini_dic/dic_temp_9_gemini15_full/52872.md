diminishingly:/dɪˈmɪnɪʃɪŋli/| adv.|逐渐减弱地，逐渐减少地|Gradually decreasing or becoming less.  It describes a process of reduction or decline in size, importance, or intensity.|例句：The light diminishedly as the sun set.  夕阳西下，光线逐渐减弱。

The sound of the music diminishedly as we walked away from the concert hall.  我们离开音乐厅时，音乐声逐渐减弱。


近义词：gradually, progressively, increasingly faintly,  decreasingly


反义词：increasingly,  growingly, expandingly


词性:副词
