debate:/dɪˈbeɪt/| n., v.| 辩论，讨论；争论 | A formal discussion on a particular topic in a public meeting or legislative assembly.  To engage in argument or discussion. |例句：The debate on climate change is ongoing. 气候变化的辩论仍在继续。

The students debated the merits of different political systems. 学生们就不同政治制度的优缺点进行了辩论。|近义词：discussion, argument, dispute, controversy |反义词：agreement, accord, consensus


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They debate the issue every week. = 他们每周都辩论这个问题。
We debate the topic in class. = 我们在课堂上讨论这个话题。


一般过去时:
The politicians debated fiercely last night. = 政治家们昨晚进行了激烈的辩论。
They debated the proposal for hours. = 他们就这项提案辩论了数小时。


现在完成时:
We have debated this topic many times. = 我们已经多次讨论过这个话题。
They have debated the bill since last month. = 自上个月以来，他们一直在辩论这项法案。


过去完成时:
They had debated the issue before the vote. = 他们在投票前就已经讨论过这个问题了。
She had debated the implications thoroughly. = 她已经彻底地讨论了其影响。


将来时:
We will debate the motion tomorrow. = 我们明天将对这项动议进行辩论。
They will debate the new policy next week. = 他们下周将讨论新的政策。


虚拟语气:
If we debated more openly, we might find common ground. = 如果我们更公开地辩论，我们可能会找到共同点。
I wish we had debated the issue more thoroughly before deciding. = 我希望我们在决定之前更彻底地讨论过这个问题。


被动语态:
The issue was debated extensively. = 这个问题被广泛讨论。
The proposal is being debated in parliament. = 该提案正在议会中进行辩论。


疑问句:
Will they debate the proposal? = 他们会讨论这个提案吗？
Are they debating the merits of the plan? = 他们正在辩论该计划的优缺点吗？


否定句:
They did not debate the matter further. = 他们没有进一步讨论此事。
We will not debate this point any longer. = 我们不会再讨论这一点。


PHRASE->
engage in debate = 参加辩论

public debate = 公开辩论
under debate = 正在讨论中
fuel the debate = 加剧辩论
