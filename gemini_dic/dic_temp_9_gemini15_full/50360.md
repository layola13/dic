depopularize:/ˌdiːˈpɒpjʊləraɪz/| vt. |使不再流行；使不受欢迎|To cause something to become less popular or fashionable.  The new technology depopularized the old methods. 新技术使旧方法不再流行。|近义词：disfavor, discredit, unpopularize |反义词：popularize
