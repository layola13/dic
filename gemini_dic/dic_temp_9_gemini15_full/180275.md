refreshed:/rɪˈfreʃt/ | adj. |精神焕发的；耳目一新的；更新的 |Feeling renewed in strength, energy, or freshness; invigorated.  The word also describes something that has been made new or updated.|例句：After a long sleep, I felt completely refreshed.  长睡之后，我感觉完全恢复了精神。

例句：The website has been refreshed with new content and features. 网站内容和功能已经更新。


近义词：invigorated, revitalized, rejuvenated | 反义词：exhausted, tired, depleted | 词性：形容词
