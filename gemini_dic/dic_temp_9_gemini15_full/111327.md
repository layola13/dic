live-ins:/ˈlɪvɪnz/| n.|住家佣人；同居者|A person who lives and works at their employer's home, often as a domestic servant or caregiver.  It can also refer to a couple who live together without being married.|例句：She found a live-in nanny to help with the children. 她找到了一位住家保姆来帮忙照顾孩子。|例句：They are a live-in couple, planning their wedding for next year.他们是一对同居的情侣，计划明年结婚。|近义词：domestic, resident, cohabitant|反义词：outsider, commuter
