forthcoming: /ˈfɔːrθkʌmɪŋ/ | adj. |即将到来的；乐于助人的；直率的 |

英文解析:  Forthcoming can describe something that is going to happen soon or something that is readily given or offered.  It can also describe a person who is frank and communicative.

例句:

The forthcoming election is causing much excitement.  即将到来的选举引起了极大的兴奋。

Her forthcoming novel is highly anticipated.  她即将出版的小说备受期待。


He was always forthcoming with information. 他总是乐于提供信息。

She gave a forthcoming account of the events. 她对事件作了坦率的叙述。


近义词: approaching, imminent, impending, frank, open, candid

反义词: reluctant, hesitant, reserved, secretive, distant
