non-vascular: /ˌnɒnˈvæskjələr/ | adj. | 无维管束的；非维管的 | Lacking vessels for conducting fluids, as in certain plants.  This refers to plants that do not possess specialized tissues (xylem and phloem) for transporting water and nutrients.  They rely on other methods for internal transport, often involving simpler processes like diffusion. | Example: Mosses and liverworts are examples of non-vascular plants.  苔藓和地钱是无维管束植物的例子。|近义词：avascular | 反义词：vascular
