semimetals:/ˈsemiˌmɛtəlz/| n. |半金属|Elements that have properties intermediate between those of metals and nonmetals. They are usually brittle, and their electrical conductivity is intermediate between that of metals and nonmetals, often increasing with increasing temperature.  |Example: Silicon and germanium are important semimetals used in the electronics industry.|近义词: metalloids|反义词: metal, nonmetal
