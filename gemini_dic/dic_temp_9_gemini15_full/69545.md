fellow-feeling:/ˈfɛloʊˌfiːlɪŋ/| n. | 同情；同感；互相关心 | A feeling of sympathy and understanding for another person's suffering or distress.  It implies a shared experience or understanding that fosters empathy. |  The fellow-feeling among the refugees was palpable.  难民们之间的同情心是显而易见的。

He showed great fellow-feeling for the victims of the earthquake. 他对地震受害者表现出极大的同情。


近义词：empathy, sympathy, compassion, commiseration | 反义词：apathy, indifference, unconcern | 词性：名词
