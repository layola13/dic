in-midst:/ɪnˈmɪdst/| prep.|在…之中；在…当中 |In the midst of something means during or in the middle of a situation, event, or activity.  It emphasizes being surrounded or enveloped by something.|例句：He found himself in the midst of a raging storm. 他发现自己身处暴风雨之中。|例句：In the midst of all the chaos, she remained calm. 在一片混乱之中，她保持着冷静。|近义词：among, amidst, surrounded by |反义词：outside, apart from


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am in the midst of writing a report. = 我正在写报告。
They are in the midst of a heated discussion. = 他们正在进行激烈的讨论。


一般过去时:
We were in the midst of a meeting when the fire alarm rang. =  火警响起时，我们正在开会。
She was in the midst of preparing dinner when the phone rang. = 她正在准备晚餐时电话响了。


现在完成时:
I have been in the midst of this project for months. = 我已经参与这个项目好几个月了。
They have been in the midst of renovations since spring. = 自春天以来，他们一直在进行装修。


过去完成时:
We had been in the midst of a long journey when the car broke down. = 汽车抛锚时，我们已经长途跋涉了一段时间了。
She had been in the midst of a difficult situation before she sought help. = 在寻求帮助之前，她一直处于困境之中。


将来时:
I will be in the midst of a conference next week. = 我下周将参加一个会议。
They will be in the midst of their exams next month. = 他们下个月将参加考试。


虚拟语气:
If I were in the midst of a crisis, I would try to stay calm. = 如果我处于危机之中，我会尽量保持冷静。
I wish I weren't in the midst of so much work. = 我真希望我没有这么多工作要做。


被动语态:
The city was in the midst of a major reconstruction. =  这座城市正处于大规模重建之中。(This is less common for "in the midst of")
The project was in the midst of being assessed. =  该项目正在接受评估。(This usage is more common)


疑问句:
What were you in the midst of when I called? = 我打电话时你在忙什么？
Are you in the midst of something important right now? = 你现在正忙着重要的事情吗？


否定句:
He wasn't in the midst of anything important. = 他当时并没有忙着什么重要的事情。
They were not in the midst of a conflict. = 他们没有处于冲突之中。



PHRASE->
in the midst of chaos = 在混乱之中
in the midst of a crisis = 在危机之中
in the midst of battle = 在战斗中
in the midst of a conversation = 在谈话中
in the midst of nowhere = 在荒郊野外
