individuative: /ɪnˌdɪvɪdʒuˈeɪtɪv/ | adj. | 个体化的；区分的 |  Relating to or characteristic of an individual; serving to distinguish or identify an individual.  It emphasizes the unique qualities of a single entity, differentiating it from a group or class. |


例句：

The individuative approach to education focuses on the unique needs of each student.  = 个体化的教育方法关注每个学生的独特需求。

His individuative style of painting set him apart from his contemporaries. = 他独特的绘画风格使他与同时代人区别开来。


近义词：individualistic, distinctive, unique, specific

反义词：general, collective, common, generic

词性:形容词
