remelting: /ˌriːˈmɛltɪŋ/ | v. | 再熔化；重熔 | The process of melting something that has already been melted.  It often refers to recycling materials by melting them down again. | Example: The company specializes in the remeltinng of aluminum cans for reuse.  这家公司专门从事铝罐的再熔化以供重复使用。The gold was remeltinng to create new jewelry. 黄金被重熔以制作新的首饰。| 近义词：refining, melting down, recycling | 反义词：solidifying, freezing
