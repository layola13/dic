half-shared: /ˈhæfʃeərd/ | adj. | 半共享的，部分共享的 |  Describes something that is shared or used by two or more parties, but not equally or completely.  It implies a partial or incomplete sharing arrangement. | Example:  They have a half-shared office, meaning they both use the same space but have separate areas.  他们共用一个办公室，这意味着他们都使用同一个空间，但有各自的区域。|近义词:partially shared, jointly used (in part)|反义词:fully shared, entirely private, sole possession|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The apartment has a half-shared kitchen. = 这套公寓有一个半共享的厨房。
They have a half-shared responsibility for the project. = 他们对这个项目负有部分责任。


一般过去时:
We had a half-shared bedroom when we were kids. = 我们小时候共用一个卧室。
The company had a half-shared database with its partner. = 该公司与其合作伙伴共用一个数据库。


现在完成时:
They have had a half-shared workspace for the past year. = 过去一年他们一直共用一个工作空间。
We have half-shared the costs of the vacation. = 我们分担了假期的费用。


过去完成时:
Before the merger, they had already had a half-shared marketing team. = 在合并之前，他们已经共用了一个营销团队。
They had half-shared the profits from the previous project. = 他们分得了上一项目的利润。


将来时:
We will have a half-shared office in the new building. = 我们将在新大楼里共用一个办公室。
They will half-share the expenses for the conference. = 他们将分担会议的费用。


虚拟语气:
If we had a half-shared apartment, we could save money. = 如果我们共用一套公寓，我们可以省钱。
I wish we had a half-shared car instead of two separate ones. = 我希望我们共用一辆车而不是两辆单独的车。


被动语态:
The resources were half-shared among the three teams. = 资源被三个团队共享。
The profits were half-shared between the two partners. = 利润被两个合伙人平分。


疑问句:
Is the printer half-shared between departments? = 打印机是部门之间共享的吗？
Will the costs be half-shared by the two companies? = 这笔费用将由两家公司分担吗？


否定句:
The resources were not half-shared fairly among the team members. = 资源在团队成员之间分配不均。
The responsibility was not half-shared equally. = 责任没有平均分担。
