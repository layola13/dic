sincerest: /sɪnˈsɪərɪst/ | adj. | 最真诚的，最诚挚的 | Most sincere; heartfelt.  It expresses the superlative degree of sincerity.  |例句：He offered his sincerest apologies for his mistake. 他为他犯的错误表示最诚挚的歉意。

例句：She expressed her sincerest gratitude for their help. 她对他们的帮助表达了最诚挚的谢意。


近义词：most heartfelt, most genuine, truest, deepest | 反义词：insincere, feigned, hypocritical | 词性:形容词
