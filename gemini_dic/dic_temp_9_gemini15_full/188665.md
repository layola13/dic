rumoured:/ˈruːməd/| adj.| 盛传的，谣传的 |  Said to be true but not yet proven.  It implies a lack of certainty and often suggests the information comes from unreliable sources. | 例句：He is rumoured to be stepping down. 他据说要辞职了。|  例句：The rumoured merger between the two companies never happened.  两家公司之间传闻中的合并最终没有发生。| 近义词：reported, whispered, alleged | 反义词：confirmed, verified
