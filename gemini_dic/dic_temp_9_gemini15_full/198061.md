shoes:/ʃuz/ | n. |鞋子 |Footwear covering the feet.  Shoes protect the feet from the elements and provide support while walking. They come in various styles, materials, and designs. |例句：I need to buy a new pair of shoes. = 我需要买一双新鞋。

例句：Her shoes were covered in mud. = 她的鞋子沾满了泥巴。


近义词：boots, sandals, slippers |反义词：bare feet


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He wears shoes every day. = 他每天都穿鞋。
She buys new shoes every season. = 她每个季度都买新鞋。


一般过去时:
I bought new shoes last week. = 我上周买了双新鞋。

现在完成时:
I have worn these shoes for years. = 我已经穿这些鞋子好几年了。
She has bought three pairs of shoes this month. = 这个月她已经买了三双鞋。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
