simples:/ˈsɪmpəl/ | adj. |简单的，简易的 | Simple means easy to understand or do; not complicated.  It can also refer to something plain or unadorned. |Example: This is a simple problem.  这个是一个简单的问题。| Example:  She wore a simple dress. 她穿着一件简单的连衣裙。|近义词：easy, uncomplicated, straightforward |反义词：complex, complicated, intricate
