dechristianize:/ˌdiːˈkrɪstʃənaɪz/| vt. |去基督教化；使摆脱基督教的影响|To remove the influence of Christianity from something or someone.  The process involves eliminating Christian practices, beliefs, or institutions.|例句：The government attempted to dechristianize the country through the suppression of religious schools and the promotion of secular education.  政府试图通过取缔宗教学校和推广世俗教育来使国家去基督教化。

例句：The communist regime systematically dechristianized the population by confiscating church property and persecuting religious leaders. 共产主义政权通过没收教堂财产和迫害宗教领袖来系统地使人民去基督教化。


近义词：secularize, desacralize | 反义词：Christianize
