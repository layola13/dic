disintegrate:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪt/| v.|崩解；瓦解；衰败 | to break into small parts or pieces and become weak or destroyed | The old building is slowly disintegrating.  这座旧建筑正在慢慢地崩解。The empire disintegrated into smaller kingdoms.  帝国瓦解成较小的王国。| crumble, decompose, decay | integrate, solidify, unite |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ice cream disintegrates quickly in the sun. = 冰激凌在阳光下很快融化。
The empire is disintegrating slowly. = 帝国正在缓慢地瓦解。


一般过去时:
The ancient statue disintegrated over centuries. = 这座古老的雕像在几个世纪中逐渐崩解。
The team disintegrated after their star player left. = 球队在他们的明星球员离开后土崩瓦解。


现在完成时:
The evidence has disintegrated over time. = 证据随着时间的推移已经消失了。
His hopes have disintegrated since the accident. = 自从事故发生以来，他的希望破灭了。


过去完成时:
The bridge had disintegrated before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，桥梁已经坍塌了。
The relationship had disintegrated long before they announced their separation. = 在他们宣布分手之前，他们的关系早已破裂。


将来时:
The old car will disintegrate completely within a year. = 这辆旧车将在一年内完全报废。
Without maintenance, the building will disintegrate rapidly. = 没有维护，这座建筑将会迅速倒塌。


虚拟语气:
If the building disintegrated, people would be in danger. = 如果建筑物倒塌，人们将处于危险之中。
I wish my old car hadn't disintegrated so quickly. = 我真希望我的旧车不会这么快就报废。


被动语态:
The building was disintegrated by the earthquake. = 建筑物在地震中被摧毁了。
The old documents were disintegrated by the rain. = 这些旧文件被雨水冲毁了。


疑问句:
Will the material disintegrate under pressure? = 材料会在压力下分解吗？
Is the old wall disintegrating? = 这堵旧墙正在崩塌吗？


否定句:
The concrete did not disintegrate easily. = 混凝土不易崩解。
The agreement did not disintegrate despite the many difficulties. = 尽管困难重重，协议并未破裂。
