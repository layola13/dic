calculatable: /ˈkælkjuleɪtəbl/ | adj. | 可计算的，可预测的 |  Able to be calculated or predicted. The probability of rain tomorrow is calculatable based on current weather patterns.  根据当前的天气模式，明天下雨的概率是可以预测的。 | predictable, foreseeable, estimable | incalculable, unpredictable, unforecastable |
