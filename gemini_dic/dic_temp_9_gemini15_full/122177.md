mistutoring:/mɪsˈtjuːtərɪŋ/| n.|错误教学，误导性教学|The act of teaching incorrectly or misleadingly; imparting false or inadequate knowledge or skills.|例句：The mistutoring of the students led to their poor performance on the exam. 错误的教学导致学生们考试成绩很差。|近义词：misinstruction, misguidance, malinstruction|反义词：good teaching, effective instruction|名词
