narrators:/nəˈreɪtərz/| n. |叙述者，讲述者 | People or characters who tell a story.  Narrators can be first-person (telling the story from their own perspective), second-person (directly addressing the reader), or third-person (telling the story from an outside perspective).|Example: The novel has two narrators, one reliable and one unreliable. 这部小说有两个叙述者，一个可靠，一个不可靠。|近义词：storytellers, speakers, recounters |反义词：listeners, audience
