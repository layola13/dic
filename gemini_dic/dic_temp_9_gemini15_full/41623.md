correct:/kəˈrekt/| adj. v.|正确的，恰当的；改正，纠正|

英文解析：
As an adjective, "correct" means free from error; accurate; conforming to fact or truth.  As a verb, it means to make or set right; rectify; amend.

例句：
The answer is correct. = 答案是正确的。

The teacher corrected my mistakes. = 老师纠正了我的错误。


近义词：accurate, right, proper, rectify, amend

反义词：incorrect, wrong, false, erroneous


词性: adjective, verb
