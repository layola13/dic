pampering:/ˈpæmpərɪŋ/| n.| 娇惯；纵容；过分溺爱 | The act of treating someone with excessive care and attention.  |例句：The pampering she received at the spa left her feeling refreshed.  她享受的SPA护理让她感觉神清气爽。 |例句：His pampering of his children led to their spoiled behavior. 他对孩子的过分溺爱导致了他们被宠坏的行为。|近义词：indulgence, coddling, spoiling |反义词：neglect, deprivation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pampers his dog excessively. = 他过分溺爱他的狗。
She pampers herself with long baths. = 她用长时间的洗澡来纵容自己。

一般过去时:
They pampered their guests with delicious food. = 他们用美味的食物款待客人。
I pampered myself with a massage after a long day. = 劳累了一天后，我给自己做了个按摩来放松一下。

现在完成时:
She has pampered herself all week. = 她整个星期都在纵容自己。
He has pampered his son since he was a baby. = 从儿子还是婴儿的时候，他就一直过分溺爱他。

过去完成时:
She had pampered herself before going to the party. = 去参加派对之前，她好好地纵容了自己一番。
They had pampered their clients for years, building a loyal following. = 他们多年来一直悉心款待客户，从而建立了忠实的客户群体。

将来时:
I will pamper myself with a weekend getaway. = 我打算用一个周末短途旅行来好好犒劳自己。
They will pamper their new puppy with lots of toys. = 他们将用许多玩具来宠爱他们的新小狗。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pamper myself more often. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常地纵容自己。
I wish I could pamper myself with a trip to the beach. = 我真希望自己可以去海边旅行好好放松一下。

被动语态:
The guests were pampered throughout their stay. = 客人在整个住宿期间都受到了悉心款待。
He was pampered as a child, resulting in a lack of self-reliance. = 他小时候被娇生惯养，导致缺乏自立能力。

疑问句:
Do you often pamper yourself? = 你经常纵容自己吗？
How do you plan to pamper your loved ones this holiday season?  = 你计划如何在今年的节日里好好款待你的亲人？

否定句:
She doesn't pamper herself enough. = 她对自己不够好。
He didn't pamper his children, believing discipline was more important. = 他没有娇惯他的孩子，因为他认为纪律更重要。
