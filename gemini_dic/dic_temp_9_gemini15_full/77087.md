garrison:/ˈɡærɪsən/| n. v.|卫戍部队；驻军；（尤指军事）守备；配备卫戍部队|

英文解析：A garrison is a group of soldiers stationed in a particular place, especially a fort or town, to defend it.  It can also refer to the act of providing such troops.

例句：

The town was under heavy garrison during the war. = 战争期间，该镇驻扎着重兵。

A small garrison defended the remote outpost. = 一个小型的卫戍部队保卫着偏远的哨所。


近义词：troops, military force,  defense force,  occupying force

反义词：None directly, but concepts like "unprotected", "undefended" are antonymic to the state of being garrisoned.


词性：Noun, Verb
