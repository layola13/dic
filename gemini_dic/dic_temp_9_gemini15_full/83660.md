词源:
From haem(o)- + static.  "haem(o)-" is from Greek  αἷμα (haima) meaning "blood", and "static"  comes from the Greek  στατικός (statikos) meaning "causing to stand still".  Therefore, haemostatic literally means "causing blood to stand still".
