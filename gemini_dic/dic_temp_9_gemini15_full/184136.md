resistible:/rɪˈzɪstəbl/ | adj. | 可抵抗的；难以抗拒的 |  Able to be resisted; tempting and attractive.  | The cake's aroma was almost irresistible. 那蛋糕的香味几乎让人难以抗拒。|  resistable | irresistible |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
That temptation is resistible. = 那种诱惑是可以抵抗的。
This offer is resistible for me. = 这个提议对我来说是可以拒绝的。


一般过去时:
The urge to eat the entire cake was resistible yesterday. = 昨天想吃掉整个蛋糕的冲动是可以抵抗的。
His proposal was resistible to her. = 他的求婚她可以拒绝。


现在完成时:
The pressure has been resistible so far. = 到目前为止，压力是可以承受的。
Many temptations have been resistible for him. = 许多诱惑对他来说都是可以抗拒的。


过去完成时:
The temptation had been resistible before the diet started. = 在开始节食之前，这种诱惑是可以抵抗的。
The offer had been resistible before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，这个提议是可以拒绝的。


将来时:
The urge will be resistible once I've had a good meal. = 一旦我吃饱了，这种冲动就能够抵挡了。
That temptation will be resistible for her in the future. = 将来那诱惑对她来说是可以抗拒的。


虚拟语气:
If the temptation were resistible, I would resist it. = 如果诱惑是可以抗拒的，我会抗拒它。
If the offer had been resistible, I would have refused it. = 如果提议是可以拒绝的，我就会拒绝它。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
