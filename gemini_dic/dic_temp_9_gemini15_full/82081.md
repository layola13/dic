grizzly:/ˈɡrɪzli/| adj. n.|灰熊的；灰色的；凶猛的；令人不快的|Of or relating to a grizzly bear;  having a grayish color;  causing fear or apprehension; unpleasant or gloomy.|例句：The grizzly bear is a dangerous predator. 灰熊是一种危险的捕食者。The grizzly weather made the hike unpleasant.  阴沉的天气使得徒步旅行变得不愉快。|近义词：gray, ferocious, formidable, unpleasant|反义词：pleasant, gentle, mild
