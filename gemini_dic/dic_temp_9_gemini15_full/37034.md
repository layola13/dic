comanager:/ˈkoʊmænɪdʒər/ | n. |联席经理，共同经理 | A comanager is one of two or more people who share the responsibility of managing something, such as a company, department, or project.  They typically work together to make decisions and oversee operations. | Example:  The project was managed successfully by two comanagers who complemented each other's skills.  这个项目由两位互补技能的联席经理成功管理。 |近义词：joint manager, co-administrator |反义词：sole manager, single manager |名词
