sarcophagous:/sɑːrˈkɒfəɡəs/| adj.|食肉的；食尸体的|Flesh-eating; feeding on dead bodies.  The term refers to organisms that consume flesh, whether living or dead.|例句：Some insects are sarcophagous, feeding on decaying animal matter.  一些昆虫是食肉的，以腐烂的动物尸体为食。

例句：The sarcophagous beetles played a vital role in the ecosystem by breaking down carcasses. 食腐甲虫通过分解尸体在生态系统中发挥着至关重要的作用。


近义词：carnivorous, flesh-eating, necrophagous

反义词：herbivorous


词源：From Greek *sarkophagos, from sarx (flesh) + phagein (to eat).  The word combines the Greek words for "flesh" and "to eat."
