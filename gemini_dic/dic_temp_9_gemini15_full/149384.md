pacify:/ˈpæsɪfaɪ/| vt.| 使平静；抚慰；平息；安抚 |to bring peace or quiet to (a place or person) ; to make someone calm or less angry | The mother pacified her crying baby with a bottle. 母亲用奶瓶哄哭了婴儿平静下来。 The police tried to pacify the angry mob. 警察试图平息愤怒的暴民。| appease, soothe, calm, tranquilize | agitate, inflame, provoke, excite | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pacifies his child with a story. = 他用故事哄孩子平静。
She pacifies her anger through meditation. = 她通过冥想来平息怒火。

一般过去时:
The police pacified the riot with tear gas. = 警察用催泪瓦斯平息了暴乱。
He pacified her fears with a gentle hug. = 他温柔地拥抱她，平息了她的恐惧。


现在完成时:
The government has pacified the rebel groups through negotiations. = 政府通过谈判平息了叛乱组织。
She has pacified her anxiety by practicing yoga. = 她通过练习瑜伽来缓解焦虑。


过去完成时:

词源:
From Middle French *pacifier, from Latin pacificare "to make peaceful," from pacis "peace" (from PIE root *pag- "to bind, fasten") + -ficare "to make".
