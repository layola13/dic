keep-out:/kiːpˈaʊt/| n. adj. |禁止入内，勿入|A keep-out area is a place where people are not allowed to go.  It can be a sign, a barrier, or an area designated as off-limits.  The term can also be used as an adjective to describe something that is restricted or forbidden. | 例句：There's a keep-out sign on the door. 门上贴着禁止入内的标志。

The construction site is a keep-out zone.  建筑工地是禁区。


近义词：no entry, prohibited, restricted, off-limits |反义词：open, accessible, welcome


词性：名词，形容词
