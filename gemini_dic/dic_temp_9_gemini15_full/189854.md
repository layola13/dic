sambas:/ˈsæmbəz/| n. |桑巴舞；桑巴舞曲 |A samba is a Brazilian dance and musical genre. It's characterized by its rhythmic beat and lively movements.  |例句：She learned to dance the samba in Rio. 她里约热内卢学会跳桑巴舞。

例句：The band played a lively samba. 乐队演奏了一首欢快的桑巴舞曲。


近义词：rumba, bossa nova (similar Brazilian dance/music styles) |反义词：无


词源：源自巴西葡萄牙语“samba”，其起源尚不明确，但可能与非洲舞蹈和音乐传统有关。
