oceanographer:/ˌoʊʃəˈnɑːɡrəfər/ | n. |海洋学家 |A scientist who studies the ocean.  Oceanographers investigate various aspects of the ocean, including its physical properties, chemical composition, biological life, and geological features. | Dr. Smith is a renowned oceanographer specializing in marine ecosystems. 史密斯博士是一位专门研究海洋生态系统的著名海洋学家。  Many oceanographers are involved in research related to climate change. 许多海洋学家都参与了与气候变化相关的研究。 | marine biologist, ocean scientist | (no direct antonym)
