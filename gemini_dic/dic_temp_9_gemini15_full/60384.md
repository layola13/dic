egyptologist:/ˌiːdʒɪpˈtɒlədʒɪst/| n. |埃及学家|A person who studies ancient Egypt, its culture, history, language, and archaeology.|例句：Many egyptologists believe the pyramids were built by skilled laborers, not slaves. 许多埃及学家认为金字塔是由熟练的劳动者而不是奴隶建造的。  例句：The renowned egyptologist presented her findings at the international conference.  这位著名的埃及学家在国际会议上介绍了她的研究成果。|近义词：archaeologist (if focusing on the archaeological aspects), historian (if focusing on the historical aspects)|反义词：无
