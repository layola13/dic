mothballs:/ˈmɒθbɔːlz/ /ˈmɑːθbɔːlz/ | n. |樟脑丸；防蛀球 |Small, hard balls made of a chemical substance, used to repel moths and other insects that can damage clothes and other fabrics.  They have a strong, distinctive smell. |例句：I put some mothballs in the closet to keep the moths away.我把一些樟脑丸放在衣橱里驱赶飞蛾。

例句：The strong smell of mothballs permeated the old trunk.  老箱子里弥漫着浓烈的樟脑丸气味。


近义词：insect repellent, moth repellent |反义词：None
