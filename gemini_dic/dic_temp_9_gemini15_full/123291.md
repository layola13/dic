monogastric: /ˌmɒnəˈɡæstrɪk/| adj.| 单胃的；单室胃的 | Having only one stomach compartment.  Monogastric animals, unlike ruminants, have a simple, single-chambered stomach.  They digest food using enzymes and acids secreted in their stomach. Examples include humans, pigs, and dogs. | 例句：Humans are monogastric animals, unlike cows which are ruminants.  人类是单胃动物，不像牛是反刍动物。|近义词：simple-stomached |反义词：ruminant, polygastric
