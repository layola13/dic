d-lock:/ˈdiːlɒk/| n. |U型锁，D型锁 |A D-lock is a type of lock shaped like the letter "D", typically used to secure bicycles or motorcycles.  It consists of a hardened steel shackle that passes through a U-shaped bow.  |例句：He secured his bike to the rack with a sturdy d-lock. 他用一个结实的D型锁把自行车锁在了架子上。|例句：The thief managed to break the d-lock with bolt cutters.  小偷用钢筋钳弄断了U型锁。|近义词：padlock, chain lock |反义词：unlock, open
