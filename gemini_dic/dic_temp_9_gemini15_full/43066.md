coworkers: /ˈkoʊwɜːrkərz/ | n. |同事| People who work together at the same place. Coworkers often collaborate on projects, share information, and build relationships within a workplace.  |  例句：I get along well with most of my coworkers. 我和大多数同事相处融洽。

例句：My coworkers helped me celebrate my birthday. 我的同事帮我庆祝了生日。


近义词：colleagues, fellow workers, associates | 反义词：N/A


词性: 名词
