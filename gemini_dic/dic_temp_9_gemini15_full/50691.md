dermatologists:/ˌdɜːrməˈtɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. |皮肤科医生| Specialists in the diagnosis and treatment of skin disorders. They deal with a wide range of conditions, from acne and eczema to skin cancer. |The dermatologists recommended a new cream for my eczema. 皮肤科医生建议我用一种新的药膏来治疗我的湿疹。|dermatologist, skin specialist| general practitioner, internist| n.
