psycho:/ˈsaɪkoʊ/| adj. n.|精神病的；精神病患者；变态者|relating to the mind, especially its abnormal aspects; relating to or characterized by psychosis; a person who is mentally ill, especially one with a psychosis.|例句：He's a psycho; he threatened to kill me. 他是个疯子；他威胁要杀了我。  The psycho drama unfolded slowly.  精神病的戏剧慢慢展开。|近义词：insane, mad, deranged, lunatic, psychopath|反义词：sane, rational, normal
