loads:/loʊdz/| n. v.|大量，负载；装载|a large quantity or number of something; to put a load on something|例句：There are loads of apples in the basket.  篮子里有很多苹果。  He loads the truck with boxes. 他用箱子装载卡车。|近义词：many, much, numerous, fill, load up|反义词:few, little, unload


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He loads the truck every day. = 他每天都装载卡车。
There are loads of people at the concert. = 音乐会上有很多的人。


一般过去时:
She loaded the dishwasher last night. = 她昨晚装了洗碗机。
They loaded the car with luggage. = 他们把行李装进了车里。


现在完成时:
I have loaded all the photos onto my computer. = 我已经把所有照片上传到我的电脑上了。
We have loaded the truck with supplies. = 我们已经用物资装满了卡车。


过去完成时:
They had loaded the ship before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他们已经装好了船。
She had loaded the software before she started working. = 在她开始工作之前，她已经安装好了软件。


将来时:
He will load the furniture tomorrow. = 他明天将搬运家具。
They will load the containers onto the ship next week. = 他们下周将把集装箱装上船。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would load all the data. = 如果我有更多时间，我会加载所有数据。
I wish I had loaded the backup before the crash. = 我真希望在崩溃之前已经加载了备份。


被动语态:
The truck was loaded with goods. = 卡车装满了货物。
The software is being loaded onto the computer. = 软件正在被加载到电脑上。


疑问句:
How many boxes did you load? = 你装了多少箱子？
Are the containers already loaded? = 集装箱已经装好了吗？


否定句:
He didn't load the washing machine. = 他没有装洗衣机。
They haven't loaded the van yet. = 他们还没装货车。


PHRASE->
loads of: 大量的
a load of: 一堆，一大堆
carry a load: 负重
unload a load:卸货
under a heavy load: 在重压下
