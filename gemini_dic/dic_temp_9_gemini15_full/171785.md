It refers to someone who pays close attention to detail and adheres strictly to rules or conventions.|例句：He was punctilious in his dress and manners. 他在穿着和举止方面一丝不苟。|例句：The lawyer was punctilious in her preparation for the trial.  这位律师为审判做了细致的准备。|近义词：meticulous, scrupulous, precise, exact, fastidious|反义词：sloppy, careless, lax, negligent
