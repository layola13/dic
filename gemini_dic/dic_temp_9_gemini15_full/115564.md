maracas: /məˈrækəz/ | n. | 马拉卡斯（乐器） | A pair of dried gourd shells filled with beans or seeds, that are shaken to produce a rattling sound. They are a percussion instrument commonly used in Latin American music. | Example: The band played a lively tune, accompanied by the rhythmic shaking of maracas. 乐队演奏了一首轻快的曲子，伴随着马拉卡斯的节奏感十足的摇动。 | 近义词：rattle, shaker | 反义词：None
