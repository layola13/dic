sidelining:/ˈsaɪdlaɪnɪŋ/| v. n.| 边线；使靠边站；冷落；|The act of moving someone or something to the side, often temporarily or to a less important position.  It can refer to a physical action or a metaphorical one, implying exclusion or marginalization. |例句：The injury sidelined the star player for the rest of the season.  受伤使这位明星球员本赛季剩余时间都无法上场。

The coach decided to sideline the rookie during the crucial game. 教练决定在关键比赛中让这位新秀替补。|近义词：bench, exclude, marginalize, sideline, shelve|反义词：promote, include, center, feature|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He sidelines his personal projects to focus on work. 他把个人项目搁置一边，专注于工作。
They sideline dissenting opinions in meetings. 他们在会议上压制异议。


一般过去时:
The company sidelined its marketing campaign due to budget cuts.  由于预算削减，公司搁置了其营销活动。
She sidelined her ambitions to raise her children. 她为了抚养孩子而搁置了自己的抱负。


现在完成时:
He has sidelined his creative writing for years. 他多年来一直搁置他的创意写作。
The government has sidelined environmental concerns. 政府一直忽视环境问题。


过去完成时:
They had sidelined the project before the new CEO arrived. 新任CEO到来之前，他们已经搁置了这个项目。
She had sidelined her career aspirations to support her family.为了支持她的家庭，她已经搁置了她的职业抱负。


将来时:
They will sideline the less important tasks to focus on the deadlines. 他们将搁置不太重要的任务，专注于截止日期。
The company will sideline the new product launch due to unforeseen circumstances.  由于不可预见的情况，公司将搁置新产品的发布。


虚拟语气:
If they sidelined the issue, the conflict would have been avoided. 如果他们当时搁置这个问题，冲突就可以避免了。
I wish they hadn't sidelined my suggestions. 我希望他们没有忽视我的建议。


被动语态:
The proposal was sidelined due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该提案被搁置了。
The concerns of the workers were sidelined by management. 工人们的担忧被管理层忽视了。


疑问句:
Why was his opinion sidelined? 为什么他的意见被忽视了？
Will the project be sidelined again? 这个项目还会被搁置吗？


否定句:
They didn't sideline the problem; they addressed it directly. 他们没有回避这个问题；他们直接解决了它。
The manager didn't sideline the employee's concerns. 经理没有忽视员工的担忧。
