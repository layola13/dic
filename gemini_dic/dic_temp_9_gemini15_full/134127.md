nonimperatively:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpɛrətɪvli/| adv. |非命令式地，非强制性地 |In a manner that is not commanding or directive; without using commands or orders.  It emphasizes a request, suggestion, or statement of fact rather than a direct order.| 例句：He suggested the changes nonimperatively, making them seem like mere suggestions rather than demands. 他非命令式地建议了这些改变，使它们听起来像是建议而不是要求。
He explained the situation nonimperatively, avoiding any tone of coercion. 他非命令式地解释了情况，避免了任何强迫的语气。|近义词：indirectly, suggestively, tentatively |反义词：imperatively, commandingly, forcefully
