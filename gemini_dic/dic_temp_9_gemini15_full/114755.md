malpighian: /mælˈpɪdʒən/ | adj. n. | 马尔皮基氏的；马尔皮基氏体的 | Relating to Marcello Malpighi, an Italian anatomist, or to structures he discovered.  Malpighian tubules are excretory organs found in insects and other arthropods. | The insect's malpighian tubules filter waste products from the hemolymph.  昆虫的马尔皮基氏小管过滤血淋巴中的废物。|  None provided | None provided | adjective, noun
