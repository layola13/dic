kenorland:/ˈkiːnɔːrlænd/| n. |肯诺兰古陆|Kenorland is a proposed supercontinent that existed during the Neoarchean and Paleoproterozoic eras, approximately 2.7 to 2.1 billion years ago.  It is named after the Kenoran orogeny, a significant mountain-building event associated with its formation and breakup.|The Kenorland supercontinent played a crucial role in the early evolution of Earth's continental crust and played a significant role in the early history of life.|近义词：supercontinent (although it's a broader term)|反义词：N/A|名词
