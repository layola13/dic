nonmetallurgic: /ˌnɒnˌmɛtəlˈɜːrdʒɪk/ | adj. |非冶金学的| Not relating to or connected with metallurgy.  This adjective describes something that is outside the scope of metallurgy, the science and technology of extracting metals from their ores and refining them for use. |例句：The research project focused on nonmetallurgic aspects of material science.  该研究项目侧重于材料科学的非冶金学方面。


例句：The company's nonmetallurgic division developed new ceramic materials. 公司的非冶金部门开发了新型陶瓷材料。


近义词：non-metallurgical


反义词：metallurgic, metallurgical


词性：形容词
