deportees:/ˌdɛpɔːrˈtiːz/| n. |被驱逐出境者，被放逐者 |Those who have been deported from their country.  People forced to leave their country and often their homes.  The term often carries a connotation of injustice or political persecution. |例句：The deportees were left stranded at the border. 被驱逐出境者被遗弃在边境。

The plight of the deportees is a matter of grave concern. 被驱逐出境者的困境令人严重关切。|近义词：expellees, exiles, refugees |反义词：immigrants, citizens


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government deports illegal immigrants.  政府驱逐非法移民。
Many countries deport criminals.  许多国家驱逐罪犯。

一般过去时:
They deported him for violating visa regulations. 他们因其违反签证规定而将其驱逐出境。
The dictator deported thousands of political opponents. 独裁者驱逐了成千上万的政治对手。

现在完成时:
The country has deported over 10,000 people this year. 今年该国已驱逐出境超过一万人。
The authorities have deported him several times. 当局已经将他驱逐出境数次。

过去完成时:
They had already deported him before the news broke.  在消息传出之前，他们就已经将他驱逐出境了。
The government had deported many dissidents before the revolution.  革命之前，政府已经驱逐了许多持不同政见者。

将来时:
They will deport him if he doesn't leave voluntarily. 如果他不自愿离开，他们将会驱逐他。
The court will decide whether to deport the defendant. 法院将决定是否驱逐被告。

虚拟语气:
If they deported him, he would lose his home. 如果他们驱逐他，他将失去家园。
I wish they hadn't deported him so unfairly. 我真希望他们没有如此不公平地驱逐他。

被动语态:
He was deported for his criminal activities. 他因犯罪活动而被驱逐出境。
Many refugees were deported back to their war-torn country.  许多难民被遣送回饱受战争蹂躏的国家。

疑问句:
Were the deportees given any assistance? 被驱逐出境者是否得到了任何帮助？
Will he be deported to his country of origin? 他会被驱逐回他的原籍国吗？

否定句:
The government did not deport him despite the evidence.  尽管有证据，政府也没有驱逐他。
They were not deported because of lack of evidence. 由于缺乏证据，他们没有被驱逐出境。
