pervert:/pərˈvɜːrt/ | /ˈpɜːrvɜːrt/| n., v.| 变态；歪曲；使堕落 | To corrupt, distort, or turn from a proper course.  To change something from its original meaning or purpose.  As a noun, it refers to a person with a sexual perversion. |例句：He tried to pervert the course of justice. 他试图歪曲司法程序。  The dictator perverted the truth to maintain his power. 独裁者歪曲事实以维持其权力。(v.) He is a sexual pervert. 他是个性变态。(n.) |近义词：(v.) corrupt, distort, warp, misrepresent; (n.) deviant, degenerate, sadist, masochist |反义词：(v.) rectify, correct, improve, uphold; (n.) normal, healthy
