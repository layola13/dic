manifesting:/ˈmænɪfɛstɪŋ/ | v. | 显现；表现；实现；(尤指通过积极思考) 吸引 |  The act of bringing something into physical reality, often through positive thinking and visualization. It implies a deliberate process of focusing one's energy and intention to create a desired outcome. | 例句：She's manifesting her dream of owning a house by saving diligently and visualizing herself living there.  她正在通过勤奋储蓄和想象自己住在那里来实现拥有房子的梦想。

例句：He is manifesting his anger through aggressive behavior. 他正通过侵略性行为表现出他的愤怒。


近义词：realizing, materializing, actualizing, creating, attracting | 反义词：concealing, hiding, suppressing, ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am manifesting a positive attitude. = 我正在展现积极的态度。
They are manifesting their support for the cause. = 他们在表达对这项事业的支持。


一般过去时:
She manifested great courage in the face of danger. = 面对危险，她表现出极大的勇气。
He manifested symptoms of the illness last week. = 他上周表现出了这种疾病的症状。


现在完成时:
We have manifested our commitment to this project. = 我们已经展现了我们对这个项目的承诺。
She has manifested her artistic talent through numerous exhibitions. = 她通过多次展览展现了她的艺术天赋。


过去完成时:
They had manifested their dissatisfaction before the meeting. = 会议前他们已经表达了不满。
He had manifested a clear interest in the subject before the interview. = 面试前他已经对这个主题表现出了明显的兴趣。


将来时:
I will manifest my gratitude by sending a thank-you note. = 我将通过寄送感谢信来表达我的感激之情。
They will manifest their intentions in the near future. = 他们将在不久的将来表明他们的意图。


虚拟语气:
If I could manifest my desires, I would be very happy. = 如果我能实现我的愿望，我会非常快乐。
I wish I could manifest the power to change the world. = 我希望我能展现出改变世界的力量。


被动语态:
His anger was manifested in a loud outburst. = 他的愤怒以大声喊叫的方式表现出来。
The results of the experiment were manifested clearly in the data. = 实验的结果在数据中清晰地显示出来。


疑问句:
How did he manifest his intentions? = 他是如何表达他的意图的？
Does she manifest any symptoms of the disease? = 她表现出任何疾病的症状吗？


否定句:
He did not manifest any signs of illness. = 他没有任何生病的迹象。
They did not manifest any interest in the proposal. = 他们对这个提案没有任何兴趣。



PHRASE->
manifest destiny: 天命论
manifest function: 显函数
manifest content: 表层内容(弗洛伊德心理学)
to manifest itself: 表现出来
manifest error: 明显错误
