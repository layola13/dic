dosages:/ˈdoʊsɪdʒɪz/ | n. |剂量，用量 |The amounts of medicine or drug to be taken at one time or regularly.  It refers to the quantity of a substance to be administered at one time or at specified intervals. |例句：The doctor adjusted the dosages of the medication to better manage the patient's symptoms.  医生调整了药物的剂量以更好地控制病人的症状。

The recommended dosages for children are different from those for adults.儿童的推荐剂量与成人的不同。|近义词：doses, quantities |反义词：overdose (反义词并非完全对等，overdose 指过量服用)|名词
