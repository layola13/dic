promoter:/prəˈmoʊtər/|/ˈprəʊməʊtə(r)/| n. v.| 推广者；促进者；发起人；启动子 |  A promoter is someone who actively promotes or supports something, such as a product, event, or person.  It can also refer to a region of DNA that initiates transcription of a particular gene. |例句：He is a promoter of new technologies. 他是新技术的推广者。  The company hired a promoter to increase brand awareness. 公司雇佣了一位推广员来提高品牌知名度。 |近义词：advocate, supporter, sponsor, exponent |反义词：detractor, opponent, critic| 名词，动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company promotes its new product. = 公司正在推广其新产品。
She promotes healthy eating habits. = 她提倡健康的饮食习惯。


一般过去时:
They promoted him to manager. = 他们提拔他为经理。
The campaign promoted awareness of the issue. = 该运动提高了人们对这个问题的认识。


现在完成时:
They have promoted several new employees this year. = 今年他们已经提拔了几名新员工。
The company has promoted its products through social media. = 公司通过社交媒体推广了它的产品。


过去完成时:
They had already promoted the event before I arrived. = 我到达之前，他们已经宣传过这个活动了。
The company had promoted the product extensively before its launch. = 公司在产品发布之前已经广泛地宣传了该产品。


将来时:
We will promote the concert on radio and television. = 我们将在广播和电视上宣传这场音乐会。
They will promote her to a higher position next year. = 他们明年会把她提升到更高的职位。


虚拟语气:
If they promoted the product more aggressively, they would sell more. = 如果他们更积极地推广产品，他们就能卖出更多。
I wish they had promoted me sooner. = 我希望他们早点提拔我。


被动语态:
The product was promoted through various channels. = 该产品通过各种渠道进行推广。
He was promoted to the head of the department. = 他被提升为部门主管。


疑问句:
How will you promote your new business? = 你将如何推广你的新业务？
Who promoted this event? = 谁推广了这个活动？


否定句:
They didn't promote the product effectively. = 他们没有有效地推广该产品。
The company did not promote the employee to a higher position. = 公司没有将员工提升到更高的职位。
