hoverers: /ˈhʌvərərz/ | n. |盘旋者，徘徊者 | Those who hover or linger, often in an uncertain or hesitant way.  They might be birds, insects, or people. | 例句：The hoverers outside the shop window eventually went inside.  商店橱窗外的徘徊者最终走进了商店。

例句：The helicopter hoverers were a constant presence during the rescue operation. 直升机盘旋者在救援行动中始终存在。


近义词：waiters, lingerers, dawdlers, loiterers | 反义词：rushers, speeders


词性: 名词
