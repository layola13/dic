footband: /ˈfʊtˌbænd/ | n. | 脚带，护足带 | A band worn around the foot, often for support or medical purposes.  It can also refer to a decorative band around the foot of something, such as a piece of furniture. | Example:  The athlete wore a footband to support his injured ankle.  运动员戴着护足带以支撑他受伤的脚踝。


近义词：ankle support, foot strap, pediment (in the context of architectural decoration)

反义词：无直接反义词
