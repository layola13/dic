propone:/prəˈpoʊn/ | vt. |提议，建议 | to suggest or put forward a plan, proposal, or idea for consideration. |例句：He proposed a new method for solving the problem. 他提出了一种解决这个问题的新方法。

例句：The committee will propose amendments to the bill.  委员会将提议对该法案进行修正。


近义词：suggest, recommend, offer, put forward | 反义词：oppose, reject, refuse


词源:  From Old French *proposen (past participle *proposé), from Latin proponere "to put forward, offer," from pro- "forward" + ponere "to put."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He proposes a new solution every week. 他每周都提出一个新的解决方案。
They propose a change in company policy. 他们提议更改公司政策。

一般过去时:
She proposed a toast to the happy couple. 她提议为这对幸福的夫妇敬酒。
The scientist proposed a revolutionary theory.  这位科学家提出了一种革命性的理论。

现在完成时:
They have proposed several different plans. 他们已经提出了几个不同的计划。
She has proposed a marriage to him. 她向他求婚了。

过去完成时:
He had proposed a compromise before the argument escalated. 在争论升级之前，他已经提议妥协。
They had proposed the idea several times before it was finally accepted. 在这个想法最终被接受之前，他们已经提出过好几次了。

将来时:
The government will propose new taxes next year. 政府明年将提出新的税收。
She will propose a different approach to the problem. 她将提出解决这个问题的不同方法。

虚拟语气:
If he proposed a better solution, we would accept it. 如果他提出更好的解决方案，我们会接受。
I wish he had proposed a more realistic plan. 我希望他当时提出一个更现实的计划。

被动语态:
A new law was proposed by the senator. 参议员提议了一项新法律。
Several amendments were proposed to the constitution.  对宪法提出了几项修正案。

疑问句:
What did he propose at the meeting? 他在会议上提出了什么？
What solutions are they proposing? 他们正在提出什么解决方案？

否定句:
He didn't propose any concrete solutions. 他没有提出任何具体的解决方案。
They didn't propose a change to the schedule. 他们没有提议更改日程安排。
