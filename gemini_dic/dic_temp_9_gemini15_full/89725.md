hopelessness:/ˈhoʊpləsnəs/| n. |绝望；无望；令人沮丧的事物|The feeling or state of having no hope.  It often refers to a situation or condition that seems impossible to improve or overcome. |例句：The hopelessness of the situation weighed heavily on her.  情况的绝望让她感到沉重不堪。

例句：He felt a sense of hopelessness after failing the exam repeatedly.  屡次考试失败后，他感到绝望。


近义词：despair, despondency, dejection|反义词：hope, optimism, confidence|词性：名词
