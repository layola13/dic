despicably: /ˈdɛspɪkəbli/ | adj. | 可鄙地，卑鄙地 |  In a despicable manner; contemptibly.  It describes actions or behavior that are worthy of contempt or disgust. | 例句：He treated her despicably, ignoring her pleas for help. 他可耻地对待她，无视她求助的恳求。


He behaved despicably towards his former friend. 他对他以前的友人表现得卑鄙无耻。


近义词：contemptibly, basely, vilely, shamefully, meanly | 反义词：admirably, honorably, nobly, commendably
