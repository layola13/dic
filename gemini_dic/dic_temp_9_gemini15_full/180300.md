refrigerations:/ˌrɪˌfrɪdʒəˈreɪʃənz/| n.|制冷；冷藏；冷冻|The process of removing heat from a space or substance to lower its temperature.  It is essential for food preservation and various industrial applications.|例句：Proper refrigerations is crucial for preventing food spoilage.  正确的冷藏对于防止食物腐败至关重要。|例句：The advancements in refrigerations technology have significantly improved food safety. 制冷技术的进步大大提高了食品安全。|近义词：cooling, chilling, freezing|反义词：heating, warming
