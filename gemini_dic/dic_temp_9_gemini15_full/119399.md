microfab:/ˈmaɪkroʊfæb/| n.|微细加工；微细制造|The process of manufacturing micro-sized devices or structures, typically using techniques like photolithography.|例句：The company specializes in microfab processes for creating advanced semiconductors. 这家公司专门从事制造先进半导体的微细加工工艺。|近义词：microfabrication, miniaturization|反义词：macromanufacturing
