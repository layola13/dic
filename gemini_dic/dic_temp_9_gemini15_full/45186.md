cuisines:/kwɪˈziːnz/| n.|烹饪风格；菜系|The styles of cooking and food preparation that are characteristic of a particular country, region, or culture.  It often refers to a collection of dishes and cooking techniques. |例句：French cuisines are known for their rich sauces and buttery flavors. 法国菜以其浓郁的酱汁和黄油味而闻名。|例句：The restaurant offers a wide variety of cuisines from around the world. 这家餐厅提供来自世界各地的各种菜系。|近义词：cooking styles, culinary traditions, gastronomy|反义词：无
