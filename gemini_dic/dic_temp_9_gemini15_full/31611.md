chastiser:/ˈtʃæstʌɪzər/ | n. |惩罚者；惩戒者；批评者 |One who chastises; a punisher.  A chastiser inflicts punishment or reprimand, often for wrongdoing or misbehavior. They may be a person in authority, like a parent or teacher, or someone who simply believes in correcting others' actions. |例句：The strict teacher was a chastiser of laziness and inattention.  这位严格的老师是懒惰和不专心的惩罚者。

The headmaster acted as a chastiser for the unruly students. 校长充当了那些不守规矩的学生的惩罚者。|近义词：punisher, disciplinarian, corrector |反义词：indulger, enabler


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chastises his children for bad behavior. 他因孩子的不良行为而惩罚他们。
The coach chastises the team for their poor performance. 教练因球队糟糕的表现而责备他们。


一般过去时:
She chastised him for his rudeness. 她因他的粗鲁而责备他。
The principal chastised the students for their disruptive behavior. 校长因学生扰乱秩序的行为而惩罚他们。


现在完成时:
He has chastised his son several times this week. 本周他已经多次惩罚了他的儿子。
She has chastised him for his repeated mistakes. 她因他反复犯错而责备他。


过去完成时:

将来时:

The manager will chastise the employees for missing deadlines. 经理将因员工错过最后期限而责备他们。


虚拟语气:
If he were to misbehave, his father would chastise him severely. 如果他行为不端，他的父亲会严厉惩罚他。
I wish I hadn't behaved so badly; my parents would have chastised me less. 我真希望我没有表现得那么糟糕；我的父母就不会那么责备我了。


被动语态:
The student was chastised for cheating on the exam.  学生因考试作弊而受到惩罚。
The team was chastised for their lack of effort.  球队因缺乏努力而受到责备。


疑问句:
Will he be chastised for his actions? 他会因他的行为而受到惩罚吗？
Has she ever been chastised by her boss? 她曾经被老板责备过吗？


否定句:
He did not chastise his child for the minor mistake. 他没有因为孩子的小错误而惩罚他。
She will not chastise him unless he repeats the offense. 除非他再次犯错，否则她不会责备他。
