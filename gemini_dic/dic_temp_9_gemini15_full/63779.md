epicureans:/ˌɛpɪˈkʊriən/| n.|享乐主义者；伊壁鸠鲁学派信徒|followers of Epicurus, emphasizing pleasure as the highest good, but a pleasure understood as tranquility and freedom from fear and pain, not mere sensual gratification.|例句：Many consider the pursuit of hedonistic pleasure as the defining characteristic of epicureans. 许多人认为对享乐主义的追求是享乐主义者的决定性特征。|例句：The epicureans gathered for a feast of fine food and wine. 享乐主义者们聚在一起享用美食和美酒。|近义词：hedonists, sensualists|反义词：ascetics, puritans
