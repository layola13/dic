iglus:/ˈɪɡluːz/| n.|冰屋|A dome-shaped house built of blocks of hard-packed snow.|Example:  The Inuit people traditionally built igloos for shelter during winter.  因纽特人传统上在冬天建造冰屋作为住所。|近义词：snow house,  shelter|反义词：house, building (in the sense of a permanent structure)

USAGE->

They build igloos from snow. = 他们用雪建造冰屋。
The igloo provides warmth and shelter. = 冰屋提供温暖和庇护。
