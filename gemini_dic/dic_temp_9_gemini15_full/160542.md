/pɔːrks/ | n. |猪肉 |Pork refers to the meat from a pig.  It is a common food source worldwide and comes in various cuts, each with its own texture and flavor. |Example: I'm making pork chops for dinner tonight. 今晚我做猪排。|近义词：pig meat, swine |反义词：无

She buys pork from the local butcher. = 她从当地屠夫那里买猪肉。
