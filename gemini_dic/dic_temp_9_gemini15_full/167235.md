preteritness:/ˈpriːtərɪtnəs/ | n. |过去性，过去时态 |The quality or state of being preterite; the tense of verbs expressing past time.  It refers to the grammatical tense used to describe actions or states that occurred in the past.  |Example: The preteritness of the verb "walked" clearly indicates that the action happened in the past.  过去式动词“walked”的过去性清楚地表明该动作发生在过去。|近义词：pastness, past tense |反义词：futurity, future tense |名词
