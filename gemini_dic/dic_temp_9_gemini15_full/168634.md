procollectivisms:/ˌproʊkəˈlɛktɪvɪzəm/| n. |集体主义倾向；集体主义思想|The advocacy or practice of prioritizing collective goals and needs over individual ones.  It often involves a belief in the importance of social harmony and cooperation.  Note that this word is less common than "collectivism" and often implies a strong or potentially excessive emphasis on collective action.|例句：The rise of procollectivisms in certain societies can lead to the suppression of individual rights.  某些社会中集体主义倾向的兴起可能导致个人权利的压制。|近义词：collectivism, communitarianism, social solidarity|反义词：individualism, egoism
