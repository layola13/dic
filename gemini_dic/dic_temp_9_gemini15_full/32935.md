chopfallen: /ˈtʃɒpˌfɔːlən/ | adj. | 沮丧的，失望的 | dejected; despondent; disheartened |

英文解析:  The word "chopfallen" describes a state of being dejected, disheartened, or deeply disappointed.  It implies a loss of spirit or energy, often following some setback or misfortune. The imagery suggests a head that has been "chopped down," hence the feeling of being deflated or utterly discouraged.


例句:

After the team's crushing defeat, the players were chopfallen.  球队惨败后，球员们都垂头丧气。

He was chopfallen when he learned he hadn't gotten the promotion. 他得知自己没有得到晋升时，非常沮丧。


近义词: dejected, despondent, disheartened, discouraged, downcast, crestfallen


反义词: elated, cheerful, joyful, optimistic, buoyant


词性: adjective
