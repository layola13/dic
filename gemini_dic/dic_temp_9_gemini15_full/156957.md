pilgrimed:/ˈpɪlɡrɪmd/| v.|朝圣；旅行|To travel to a sacred place as an act of religious devotion.|例句：They pilgrimed to Mecca to perform the Hajj.  他们前往麦加朝觐。|例句：Many people pilgrimed to the shrine to pay their respects. 许多人前往神殿朝拜。|近义词：travelled, journeyed, went on pilgrimage|反义词：stayed, remained


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They pilgrimage to the shrine every year. = 他们每年都去神殿朝圣。
He pilgrimages to Lourdes for healing. = 他去卢尔德朝圣寻求治愈。

一般过去时:
We pilgrimed to Santiago de Compostela last summer. = 我们去年夏天去圣地亚哥德孔波斯特拉朝圣。
She pilgrimed to the holy mountain to pray. = 她去圣山祈祷朝圣。

现在完成时:
They have pilgrimed to many sacred sites around the world. = 他们已经去过世界各地许多圣地朝圣。
She has never pilgrimed before. = 她以前从未朝圣过。

过去完成时:
Before he died, he had pilgrimed to all the major Buddhist temples in Tibet. = 他死前，已经朝圣了西藏所有主要的佛教寺庙。
They had already pilgrimed to Jerusalem several times. = 他们已经多次去耶路撒冷朝圣。

将来时:
We will pilgrimage to Varanasi next year. = 我们明年将去瓦拉纳西朝圣。
She will pilgrimage to the Himalayas to meditate. = 她将去喜马拉雅山冥想朝圣。

虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would pilgrimage to Rome. = 如果我有时间，我会去罗马朝圣。
I wish I could pilgrimage to Mecca someday. = 我希望有一天能去麦加朝圣。

被动语态:
The ancient route is still pilgrimed by many people. = 这条古老的路线仍然被许多人用于朝圣。
The temple was pilgrimed to for centuries. = 这个寺庙几个世纪以来一直是朝圣的地方。


疑问句:
Have you ever pilgrimed to a holy site? = 你曾经去过圣地朝圣吗？
Where will they pilgrimage next? = 他们接下来要去哪里朝圣？


否定句:
He did not pilgrimage this year. = 他今年没有去朝圣。
They haven't pilgrimed to that temple yet. = 他们还没有去那座寺庙朝圣。
