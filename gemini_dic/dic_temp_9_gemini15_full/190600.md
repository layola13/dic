satsang:/ˈsʌtsæŋ/| n.|圣会；灵修聚会；圣人的陪伴|A satsang is a gathering, typically in a spiritual or religious context, where people come together for the purpose of spiritual learning, discussion, and communion.  It often involves listening to a spiritual teacher or guru, chanting, meditation, and sharing experiences. The focus is on self-realization and connection with a higher power or spiritual truth.|Example:  Many people attend satsang to deepen their spiritual practice and connect with like-minded individuals.  许多人参加圣会以加深他们的灵修实践并与志同道合的人联系。|近义词：spiritual gathering, religious meeting, spiritual discourse|反义词：secular meeting, worldly gathering|名词
