do-away-with: /duː əˈweɪ wɪð/ | vt. | 废除；去掉；消灭；除掉；干掉 | To get rid of something completely; to abolish or eliminate something.  | 近义词：abolish, eliminate, remove, discard, get rid of | 反义词：establish, introduce, retain, keep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We do away with outdated rules. = 我们废除了过时的规章制度。
They do away with the old system. = 他们取消了旧制度。


一般过去时:
The company did away with its inefficient processes. = 公司取消了低效的流程。
The government did away with the tax. = 政府取消了这项税收。


现在完成时:
They have done away with many of the old traditions. = 他们已经废除了许多旧传统。
We have done away with the unnecessary paperwork. = 我们已经取消了不必要的文书工作。


过去完成时:
They had done away with the old equipment before the new arrived. = 新设备到达之前，他们已经处理掉了旧设备。
The factory had done away with its polluting practices years ago. = 工厂几年前就已经停止了污染行为。


将来时:
The city will do away with its aging infrastructure. = 该城市将淘汰其老化的基础设施。
We will do away with this problem soon. = 我们很快就会解决这个问题。


虚拟语气:
If we did away with this law, it would cause chaos. = 如果我们废除这项法律，将会造成混乱。
I wish they would do away with these pointless meetings. = 我希望他们能取消这些毫无意义的会议。


被动语态:
The old building was done away with last year. = 那座旧建筑物去年被拆除了。
The outdated regulations are being done away with gradually. = 过时的规章制度正在逐渐被废除。


疑问句:
Will they do away with the program? = 他们会取消这个项目吗？
What should we do away with to save money? = 我们应该取消什么来节省开支？


否定句:
We will not do away with the project. = 我们不会取消这个项目。
They did not do away with the problem easily. = 他们并没有轻易解决这个问题。


PHRASE->
do away with the old = 废除旧的
do away with the problem = 解决问题
do away with the system = 取消系统
do away with the evidence = 销毁证据
do away with someone = 杀掉某人 (This usage is less common and should be used cautiously, as it carries a strong connotation of murder.)
