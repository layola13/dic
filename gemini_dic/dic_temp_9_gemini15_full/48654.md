defenses:/dɪˈfɛnsɪz/| n. |防御措施；辩护；防御工事 |Means of protection against attack or harm,  also the arguments made in defense of something or someone. |例句：The country strengthened its defenses against possible invasion.  这个国家加强了防御工事以防入侵。

例句：The lawyer presented a strong defense for her client. 律师为她的当事人进行了强有力的辩护。

近义词：protections, safeguards, fortifications, arguments

反义词：attacks, offenses, assaults


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The city has strong defenses. = 这个城市有强大的防御力量。
Our defenses are inadequate. = 我们的防御措施不足。

一般过去时:
Their defenses collapsed under the enemy's attack. = 他们的防御工事在敌人的攻击下崩溃了。
The company's defenses against the lawsuit were weak. = 公司针对诉讼的辩护很薄弱。

现在完成时:
They have improved their defenses significantly. = 他们已经大大改善了他们的防御能力。
The government has mounted a vigorous defense of its policies. = 政府对它的政策进行了强有力的辩护。

过去完成时:
Their defenses had been weakened by previous battles. = 他们的防御工事在之前的战斗中已经被削弱了。
The lawyer had already prepared a strong defense before the trial. = 律师在审判前已经准备好了强有力的辩护。

将来时:
We will strengthen our defenses against cyberattacks. = 我们将加强防御网络攻击的防御能力。
The company will likely present a robust defense in court. = 该公司可能会在法庭上提出强有力的抗辩。

虚拟语气:
If we had better defenses, we would be safer. = 如果我们有更好的防御措施，我们会更安全。
If they had presented a stronger defense, they might have won the case. = 如果他们提出了更强有力的辩护，他们可能就赢了这场官司。

被动语态:
The castle's defenses were breached by the enemy. = 城堡的防御工事被敌人突破了。
His arguments were easily refuted; his defense was weak.  他的论点很容易被驳斥；他的辩护很无力。

疑问句:
What are our defenses against this threat? = 我们针对这一威胁的防御措施是什么？

否定句:

The defendant offered no defense against the charges. = 被告对指控没有提出任何辩护。
