rep:/rɛp/| n. v.|重复，代表，副本|to repeat something; to represent or stand for something; a copy or duplicate.|例句：He repeated the question. 他重复了这个问题。
The initials "USA" rep the United States of America.  缩写“USA”代表美利坚合众国。|近义词：repeat, duplicate, represent, copy|反义词：original, unique


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reps his company well. 他很好地代表了他的公司。
I rep the same thing every day. 我每天重复同样的事情。

一般过去时:
She repped the product at the conference. 她在会议上代表了该产品。
They repped the song many times. 他们多次重复这首歌。

现在完成时:
He has repped this brand for five years. 他已经代表这个品牌五年了。
I have repped the same message over and over. 我一遍又一遍地重复着同样的信息。

过去完成时:
She had repped the company before starting her own business. 在自己创业之前，她曾代表过这家公司。
They had repped the play multiple times before the final performance. 在最终演出之前，他们已经排练过多次了。

将来时:
He will rep the team at the upcoming meeting. 他将在即将举行的会议上代表该团队。
I will rep this idea again next week. 我下周将再次重复这个想法。


虚拟语气:
If he repped the product better, he would sell more. 如果他更好地代表了这个产品，他就会卖出更多。
I wish I had repped the situation more accurately. 我希望我更准确地描述了这种情况。

被动语态:
The product was repped by several people. 该产品由几个人代表。
The message was repped repeatedly throughout the presentation. 在整个演示过程中，该信息被反复重复。

疑问句:
Who will rep our company? 谁将代表我们公司？
Does he frequently rep the same ideas? 他经常重复同样的想法吗？

否定句:
He didn't rep the company well. 他没有很好地代表公司。
They didn't rep the information correctly. 他们没有正确地重复信息。

PHRASE->
rep for (代表): He reps for a large tech company. 他代表一家大型科技公司。
rep somebody (代替某人):  Can you rep me at the meeting? 你能代替我去参加会议吗？
rep oneself (自我介绍): She repped herself confidently. 她自信地自我介绍。
rep the facts (陈述事实):  Let's rep the facts clearly. 让我们清楚地陈述事实。
rep a song (重复演唱歌曲): They rep a song many times. 他们重复演唱歌曲很多次。
