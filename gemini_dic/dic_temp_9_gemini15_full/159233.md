poetically:/poʊˈɛtɪkli/ | adv. |诗意地；富有诗意地 | In a way that is characteristic of poetry; in a manner suggesting the beauty of poetry.  |The poem described the sunset poetically. 这首诗诗意地描写了日落。 | figuratively, lyrically | prosaically, plainly | 副词
