halfpennies:/ˈhæpəniz/| n.|半便士（复数）|The plural of halfpenny, referring to small British coins.  It is now largely obsolete as the halfpenny coin is no longer in circulation.|例句：He found a few halfpennies in his old coin jar. 他在他旧的硬币罐里发现了一些半便士。|近义词：small change, pennies (informally)|反义词：pounds, shillings (obsolete)|名词
