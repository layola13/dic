pick:/pɪk/ | vt., n. | 采摘；挑选；选择；扒窃 | To select or choose; to gather; to steal. | 例句：He picked a red apple from the tree. 他从树上摘了一个红苹果。

He picked the best candidate for the job. 他挑选了最适合这份工作的候选人。

近义词：choose, select, gather, steal | 反义词：leave, reject, drop


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I pick apples every Sunday. = 我每个星期天都摘苹果。
She picks her clothes carefully. = 她仔细挑选衣服。


一般过去时:
He picked a fight with me yesterday. = 他昨天和我打了一架。
They picked up the trash in the park. = 他们捡起了公园里的垃圾。


现在完成时:
I have picked all the ripe tomatoes. = 我已经摘了所有成熟的西红柿。
She has picked a beautiful dress for the party. = 她为派对挑选了一件漂亮的裙子。


过去完成时:
He had picked the flowers before she arrived. = 她到达之前他已经采了花。
They had picked the winning lottery numbers. = 他们已经选好了中奖彩票号码。


将来时:
I will pick you up at the airport. = 我将在机场接你。
She will pick a university next year. = 她明年将选择一所大学。


虚拟语气:
If I picked that dress, I would look great. = 如果我选了那件衣服，我会看起来很棒。
I wish I had picked a different path in life. = 我真希望我人生中选择了不同的道路。


被动语态:
The winner will be picked tomorrow. = 明天的获胜者将会被选出来。
The apples were picked early this morning. = 苹果是今天早上很早就被摘下来的。


疑问句:
What will you pick for your dessert? = 你要选什么甜点？
Which candidate did they pick? = 他们选了哪位候选人？


否定句:
I didn't pick any flowers. = 我没有采任何花。
She didn't pick up the phone. = 她没有接电话。


PHRASE->
pick up: 捡起，接人，学会
pick out: 挑选，辨认出
pick on: 欺负，挑剔
pick at: 挑剔，少量地吃
pick and choose: 挑挑拣拣
