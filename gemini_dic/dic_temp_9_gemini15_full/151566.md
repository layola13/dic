parta:/ˈpɑːrtə/| n.|部分，零件|a component or section of something larger.|The car's engine has many parts.|近义词：section, component, piece|反义词：whole


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This machine has many parts. = 这台机器有很多零件。
The cake is divided into several parts. = 蛋糕被分成几部分。

一般过去时:
He repaired the broken part of the machine. = 他修理了机器损坏的部分。
We separated the mixture into its component parts. = 我们将混合物分离成其各个组成部分。

现在完成时:
They have assembled all the parts of the furniture. = 他们已经组装好所有家具零件。
I have already fixed the faulty part of the system. = 我已经修复了系统故障的部分。

过去完成时:
Before the accident, all the parts of the engine were in perfect condition. = 事故发生前，引擎的所有零件都处于完美状态。
She had already removed the damaged parts before the inspection. = 检查前她已经拆除了损坏的部件。

将来时:
We will order the missing parts tomorrow. = 我们明天将订购缺少的零件。
The technician will replace the worn-out parts next week. = 技师下周将更换磨损的零件。

虚拟语气:
If I had all the necessary parts, I would build the model. = 如果我有所有必要的零件，我就会建造模型。
I wish I had the spare part to fix the machine. = 我希望我有一个备用零件来修理机器。

被动语态:
The parts are being inspected for quality. = 零件正在接受质量检查。
The damaged parts were replaced by the mechanic. = 损坏的零件被技工更换了。

疑问句:
What parts of the project are you responsible for? = 你负责项目的哪些部分？
Which parts are missing from the kit? = 工具包里缺少哪些零件？

否定句:

He didn't find the missing part. = 他没有找到丢失的零件。
