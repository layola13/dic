close-knit: /ˌkloʊsˈnɪt/ | adj. | 紧密团结的，关系密切的 |  Describes a group of people or a family that is very close and supportive of each other.  The members share strong bonds and a sense of unity. |

例句：
The close-knit community rallied together after the hurricane.  紧密的社区成员在飓风过后团结一致。

They are a close-knit family, always there for each other.  他们是一个关系密切的家庭，总是互相支持。


近义词：tight-knit, united, cohesive, bonded


反义词：disunited, fragmented, estranged, distant


词性:形容词
