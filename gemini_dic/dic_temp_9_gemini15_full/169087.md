progressiveness:/prəˈɡrɛsɪvnəs/ | n. |进步性；先进性；革新性 |The quality of being progressive; the tendency to support or favor progress or reform.  It often refers to forward-thinking ideas or actions that aim to improve society. |例句：The progressiveness of his ideas was praised by many.  他的思想的进步性受到了许多人的赞扬。

例句：The company's progressiveness in adopting new technologies is impressive. 这家公司采用新技术的进步性令人印象深刻。

近义词： advancement, innovation, reformism, modernism

反义词： conservatism, traditionalism, reactionism, retrogression


词性: 名词
