pro-Israelis: /ˌproʊ ˈɪzreɪliːz/ | adj. | 亲以色列的 |  Supportive of Israel and its policies.  This term describes individuals, groups, or policies that favor Israel.  It often implies a strong alignment with Israel's political and security interests.  Note that this term can be used neutrally or pejoratively depending on context.


例句：

Many pro-Israelis groups lobbied the government to increase military aid. = 许多亲以色列团体游说政府增加军事援助。

The pro-Israelis stance of the politician was widely criticized. = 这位政治家的亲以色列立场受到了广泛批评。


近义词： pro-Zionist, Israel-supporting


反义词： anti-Israel, pro-Palestinian


词性: 形容词
