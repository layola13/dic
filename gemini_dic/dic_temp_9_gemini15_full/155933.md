photodrome:/ˈfoʊtoʊdroʊm/| n. |摄影场；摄影棚|A photographic studio or location, especially one designed for large-scale or specialized photography.  It often implies a more elaborate and controlled environment than a simple studio.|例句：The photographer rented a large photodrome to shoot the commercial.|近义词：photography studio, shooting location, set|反义词：N/A
