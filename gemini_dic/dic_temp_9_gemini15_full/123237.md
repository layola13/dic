monocular:/mɒˈnɒkjʊlə(r)/| adj. n. |单眼的；单筒的；单目镜|Relating to or using only one eye; having or using only one eyepiece.  A monocular is an optical instrument for viewing distant objects, similar to binoculars but with only one eyepiece.|例句：He suffered from monocular vision after the accident.  事故后他患上了单眼视力障碍。

The telescope was a monocular, not binoculars. 望远镜是单筒的，不是双筒的。|近义词：one-eyed (informal), single-eyed|反义词：binocular, stereoscopic
