kinless:/ˈkɪnləs/| adj.|无亲戚的，无家属的 | Without relatives or family.  The word describes a person who has no living relatives or family members. It highlights a state of isolation and loneliness stemming from a lack of familial connections. | Example: He felt utterly kinless after the death of his last surviving aunt. 他在最后一位在世的姑妈去世后感到完全无依无靠。|近义词：relativeless, familyless, parentless|反义词：well-connected, close-knit
