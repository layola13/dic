predetected:/ˌpriːdɪˈtɛktɪd/| adj.|预先探测到的，预先检测到的 | Previously detected; discovered or identified in advance.  This word is formed by combining "pre-" (meaning before) and "detected" (past participle of "detect").  It implies that something was found or identified before a specific event or time. |例句：The predetected anomaly triggered an automatic alert. 预先探测到的异常触发了自动警报。


The system's predetected failures were addressed before launch. 系统预先检测到的故障在发射前得到了解决。|近义词：anticipated, foreseen, foreknown |反义词：unexpected, unforeseen, unanticipated |
