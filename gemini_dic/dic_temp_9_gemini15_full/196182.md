sepulchred:/ˈsɛpʌlktʃər/| n. |坟墓，墓穴；埋葬处 |A sepulchre is a tomb, especially one of imposing size or structure.  It often evokes a sense of age, mystery, and perhaps even decay.  The word carries a more literary and formal tone than the simple word "grave" or "tomb".|The ancient sepulchre was discovered deep within the mountain.|近义词：tomb, grave, mausoleum |反义词：birthplace, life
