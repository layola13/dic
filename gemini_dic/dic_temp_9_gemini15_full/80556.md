goodwill:/ˈɡʊdˌwɪl/ | n. | 商誉；善意；好意 |  Intangible asset of a business, representing the value of factors such as reputation, customer loyalty, and brand recognition.  It also refers to friendly and helpful feelings. |

例句：

The company's goodwill is a valuable asset. = 该公司的商誉是一项宝贵的资产。

He acted out of goodwill, wanting to help. = 他出于好意，想帮忙。


近义词：favor, benevolence, kindliness, reputation, brand equity

反义词：ill will, malice, antagonism, bad reputation


词性：名词
