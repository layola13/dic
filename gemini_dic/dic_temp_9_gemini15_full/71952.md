flight-decks:/ˈflaɪtˌdɛks/| n. |飞行甲板|The flight deck is the area on an aircraft carrier or other ship where aircraft take off and land.  It's also sometimes used to refer to the cockpit of an aircraft, though this is less common.|例句：The pilots were preparing for takeoff on the flight deck. 飞行员正在飞行甲板上准备起飞。
The captain oversaw the entire operation from the flight deck. 机长从飞行甲板监督整个行动。|近义词：flight-control, flying deck,  cockpit (for aircraft)|反义词：hangar, maintenance bay |名词
