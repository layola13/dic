pollinium:/pəˈlɪniəm/| n. |花粉块, 花粉团 |A pollinium is a mass of pollen grains that is transferred as a single unit during pollination in orchids and some other plants.  It is often attached to a sticky structure called a viscidium. |Example: The bee carried away a pollinium from the orchid flower.  蜜蜂从兰花上带走了一团花粉块。|近义词：pollen mass |反义词：无
