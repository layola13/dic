It describes a state of frantic or uncontrolled movement or activity, often accompanied by incoherent or nonsensical speech. It can also refer to someone talking rapidly and incoherently. |

The word is a compound, combining "jackrabbit" (a type of hare known for its speed) with the "-ing" suffix to create a verb describing rapid, uncontrolled movement, similar to that of a jackrabbit.
