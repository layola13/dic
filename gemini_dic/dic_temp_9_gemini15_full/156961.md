pilgrimize:/ˈpɪlɡrɪmaɪz/| v.|朝圣；去朝圣|To make a pilgrimage; to travel to a sacred place.|例句：Many people pilgrimize to Mecca every year. 每年都有许多人前往麦加朝圣。|例句：They decided to pilgrimize to the holy shrine.他们决定去朝圣地朝圣。|近义词：make a pilgrimage, journey to a holy place|反义词：N/A


USAGE->

They pilgrimize to the shrine annually. = 他们每年都去那个神殿朝圣。
We pilgrimize to the mountains for spiritual renewal. = 我们去山里朝圣以寻求精神上的更新。

She pilgrimized to Santiago de Compostela last year. = 她去年去圣地亚哥-德孔波斯特拉朝圣。
