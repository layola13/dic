diversionist: /daɪˈvɜːʒənɪst/ | n. | 转向者；破坏分子；离题者 | A person who uses diversionary tactics, often to distract attention from a main objective or to cause disruption.  A diversionist might be involved in guerrilla warfare, sabotage, or other acts of disruption. | 例句：The diversionist planted a bomb to draw the police away from the main heist.  = 破坏分子安置了一枚炸弹，以将警察从主要抢劫现场引开。

The protesters employed diversionist tactics, creating a smokescreen to cover their escape. = 抗议者使用了转移注意力的策略，制造烟幕来掩盖他们的逃跑。


近义词：saboteur, subverter, troublemaker | 反义词：collaborator, supporter
