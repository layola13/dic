coats: /koʊts/ | n. |外套，涂层 |A coat is an outer garment worn to protect the body from the weather.  It can be made from various materials and styles.  |例句：He put on his winter coat before going outside. 他出门前穿上了他的冬衣。|例句：The car needs a new coat of paint. 这辆车需要重新喷漆。|近义词：jacket, overcoat, wrap |反义词：underwear, undershirt


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He coats the bread with butter. = 他用黄油涂面包。
She wears a warm coat every day. = 她每天都穿一件保暖外套。

一般过去时:
They coated the walls with paint. = 他们用油漆粉刷墙壁。
He wore a long coat to the wedding. = 他穿着长外套去参加婚礼。

现在完成时:
She has coated the cake with chocolate. = 她已经用巧克力涂抹了蛋糕。
I have worn this coat for five years. = 我已经穿这件外套五年了。

过去完成时:
He had coated the metal before painting it. = 他在油漆金属之前已经涂上了涂层。
She had worn that coat many times before it got damaged. = 那件外套在她损坏之前已经被穿了很多次。

将来时:
They will coat the roof with sealant. = 他们将用密封剂涂抹屋顶。
He will wear a new coat to the party. = 他将穿一件新外套去参加派对。

虚拟语气:
If it were colder, I would wear a thicker coat. = 如果更冷的话，我会穿一件更厚的衣服。
I wish I had a warmer coat for this weather. = 我希望我有一件更保暖的外套来应对这种天气。

被动语态:
The furniture was coated with varnish. = 家具涂上了清漆。
The bridge will be coated with a protective layer. = 桥梁将涂上保护层。

疑问句:
What kind of coat are you wearing? = 你穿的是什么外套？
Does your coat keep you warm enough? = 你的外套够暖和吗？

否定句:
