lamellated:/ˈlæməleɪtɪd/| adj.|层状的，薄板状的|Consisting of or arranged in thin layers or plates.  The term is often used to describe materials with a layered structure, like certain types of rocks or wood.|例句：The rock formation showed a clearly lamellated structure.  这种岩石构造显示出清晰的层状结构。
The microscope revealed the lamellated nature of the bone sample. 显微镜揭示了骨骼样本的层状性质。|近义词：layered, stratified, foliated|反义词：massive, homogeneous
