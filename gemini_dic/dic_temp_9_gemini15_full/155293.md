phantasm:/ˈfæntæzəm/| n. |幻想；幽灵；幻影|A phantasm is a figment of the imagination; a ghost or specter; an apparition.  It often refers to something unreal or illusory, appearing to be real but lacking substance.  The term often carries a sense of unease or the supernatural.|例句：The flickering candlelight cast strange phantasms on the wall.  烛光闪烁，在墙上投下奇异的幻影。

The detective dismissed the witness's account as mere phantasm.  侦探认为证人的陈述只是幻想。


近义词：illusion, apparition, specter, ghost, chimera, vision|反义词：reality, truth, fact|名词
