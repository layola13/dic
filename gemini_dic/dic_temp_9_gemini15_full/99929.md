internets:/ˈɪntərˌnɛts/| n.|互联网（非正式说法）|The plural form of "internet," often used informally to refer to the global network of interconnected computer networks.  It emphasizes the interconnectedness and multitude of networks. | Example:  "I spent hours on the internets last night." = “我昨晚在互联网上花了几个小时。” |近义词：internet, online world, cyberspace |反义词：offline, disconnected


USAGE->
This word doesn't have verb, adjective, or adverb forms.  It's a noun used informally. The grammatical structures applied would be the same as for "internet".
