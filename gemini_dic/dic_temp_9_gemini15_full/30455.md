centres:/ˈsɛntərz/ | n. | 中心；中央；中心点 | The word "centres" is the plural form of "centre," meaning the middle point or the most important part of something.  It can refer to a geographical location, a point of activity, or a focus of interest. | Example:  Many cultural centres are located in London. 许多文化中心位于伦敦。  The earth is the centre of our solar system. 地球是我们太阳系的中心。|近义词：middles, hubs, cores | 反义词：outskirts, peripheries


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The business centres on customer satisfaction. = 这家公司以顾客满意为中心。
Several cultural centres thrive in this city. = 这个城市里好几个文化中心都蓬勃发展。


一般过去时:
The meeting centred on budget cuts. = 会议的中心议题是预算削减。
All activities centred around the main stage. = 所有活动都围绕着主舞台展开。


现在完成时:
The debate has centred on the same issues for weeks. = 这场辩论已经围绕着同样的议题进行了数周。
Our efforts have centred on improving customer service. = 我们的努力一直集中在改善客户服务上。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

The entire operation was centred around a single objective. = 整个行动都围绕着一个目标展开。
