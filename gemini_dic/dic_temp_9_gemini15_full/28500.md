cardamons:/ˈkɑːdəmənz/| n.|豆蔻(复数)|The word "cardamoms" refers to the dried seed pods of several plants in the genus *Elettaria* and *Amomum*, which are used as a spice.  They have a warm, spicy, and slightly sweet flavor.  The pods contain numerous small, dark brown seeds that are the part most commonly used in cooking. |Example:  The recipe called for a generous pinch of cardamoms. 这道菜谱需要大量豆蔻。

The fragrant cardamoms added a unique flavor to the curry.  芳香的豆蔻为咖喱增添了独特的风味。|近义词：spices, aromatics|反义词：无|名词
