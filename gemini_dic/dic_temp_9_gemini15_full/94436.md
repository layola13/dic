implored:/ɪmˈplɔːrd/ | vt. |恳求；哀求；乞求 |to beg someone earnestly or desperately to do something.  |例句：She implored him to reconsider. 她恳求他重新考虑。|例句：He implored his captor for mercy. 他向绑架者乞求怜悯。|近义词：beseech, entreat, plead, supplicate |反义词：command, order


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He implores his boss for a raise. = 他恳求老板加薪。
She implores her friend for help. = 她恳求朋友帮忙。

一般过去时:
He implored his father for forgiveness. = 他恳求父亲原谅他。
She implored the judge to show mercy. = 她恳求法官开恩。

现在完成时:
I have implored him repeatedly to stop. = 我一再恳求他停止。
She has implored her family for support. = 她一直在恳求家人支持。

过去完成时:
He had implored her to leave before the storm hit. = 他曾在暴风雨来临之前恳求她离开。
They had implored the authorities for assistance. = 他们曾恳求当局提供帮助。

将来时:
I will implore him to change his mind. = 我将恳求他改变主意。
She will implore her parents for understanding. = 她将恳求父母理解。

虚拟语气:
I would implore you to reconsider your decision. = 我恳求你重新考虑你的决定。
If I were you, I would implore him for help. = 如果我是你，我会恳求他帮忙。

被动语态:
He was implored to reconsider his position. = 他被恳求重新考虑他的立场。
The judge was implored to show leniency. = 法官被恳求宽大处理。

疑问句:
Did she implore you to come? = 她恳求你来吗？
Has he ever implored anyone for help before? = 他以前是否曾恳求任何人帮忙？

否定句:
He did not implore her to stay. = 他没有恳求她留下。
She did not implore them for assistance. = 她没有恳求他们帮忙。
