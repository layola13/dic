furriness:/ˈfɜːrinəs/| n.|毛茸茸；多毛；毛皮状|The quality or state of being furry; covered with fur.  It can also refer to a feeling or appearance suggestive of fur.|例句：The kitten's furriness was irresistible. 小猫毛茸茸的样子令人无法抗拒。

The plush toy had a delightful furriness.  毛绒玩具有着令人愉悦的毛茸茸质感。|近义词：fuzziness, shagginess, plushness|反义词：smoothness, baldness

词源:源自fur (毛皮) + -iness (名词后缀，表示“性质”、“状态”)，表示“毛茸茸的性质或状态”。
