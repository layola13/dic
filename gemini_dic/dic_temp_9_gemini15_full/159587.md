pollened:/ˈpɒlənd/| adj.|充满花粉的；沾满花粉的|Covered or dusted with pollen.  The air was thick with pollened dust.|The bee's legs were pollened with bright yellow.|近义词：powdered, dusted|反义词：pollen-free
