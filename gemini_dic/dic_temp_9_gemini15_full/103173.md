jest:/dʒɛst/| n., v.|玩笑；戏弄；嘲弄|A jest is something said or done to cause amusement, typically a joke.  To jest means to make jokes; to speak or act playfully or playfully mockingly.|例句：He told a jest about a clumsy waiter. 他讲了一个关于笨手笨脚的服务员的玩笑。
例句：Don't jest about such serious matters. 不要拿这么严肃的事情开玩笑。|近义词：joke, prank, quip, banter|反义词：seriousness, solemnity|名词，动词
