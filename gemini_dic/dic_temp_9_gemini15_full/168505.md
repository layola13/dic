problematize:/ˈprɒbləmətaɪz/| /ˈprɑːbləmətɑɪz/| vt.|使成问题；对…提出质疑；使复杂化|To present or regard something as a problem or matter of contention; to raise questions about the validity or assumptions of something.|例句：The author problematizes the simple narrative of progress. 作者对简单的进步叙事提出了质疑。|例句： The study problematizes the effectiveness of the new policy.  这项研究对新政策的有效性提出了质疑。|近义词：question, challenge, complicate|反义词：simplify, clarify, accept


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He problematizes every aspect of the project. 他对项目的各个方面都提出了质疑。
They problematize the traditional view of gender roles. 他们对传统的性别角色观提出了质疑。

一般过去时:
She problematized the assumptions underlying the research. 她对这项研究的潜在假设提出了质疑。
The philosopher problematized the concept of free will.  这位哲学家对自由意志的概念提出了质疑。

现在完成时:
Researchers have problematized the use of artificial intelligence in education. 研究人员对在教育中使用人工智能提出了质疑。
Critics have problematized the ethical implications of genetic engineering. 批评家们对基因工程的伦理影响提出了质疑。

过去完成时:
They had problematized the methodology before beginning the experiment. 他们在实验开始之前就对方法论提出了质疑。
Historians had problematized the official account of the war. 历史学家们对战争的官方说法提出了质疑。

将来时:
She will problematize the notion of objectivity in science. 她将对科学中的客观性概念提出质疑。
They will problematize the assumptions behind the economic model. 他们将对经济模型背后的假设提出质疑。

虚拟语气:
If he problematized the issue earlier, we could have avoided the crisis. 如果他早点对这个问题提出质疑，我们就能避免这场危机。
I wish they had problematized the design flaws before launching the product. 我希望他们在发布产品之前就对设计缺陷提出质疑。

被动语态:
The issue was problematized by several participants. 这个问题被几位参与者提出了质疑。
The assumptions of the theory were problematized in the subsequent debate. 该理论的假设在随后的辩论中受到了质疑。

疑问句:
Does the article problematize any of the existing theories? 这篇文章是否对任何现有的理论提出了质疑？
How does the author problematize the character's actions? 作者是如何对人物的行为提出质疑的？

否定句:
The speaker did not problematize the findings of the study. 演讲者没有对这项研究的结果提出质疑。
The book does not problematize the historical context. 这本书没有对历史背景提出质疑。
