cigarettes:/ˌsɪɡəˈrɛts/| n. |香烟，纸烟 |A small roll of finely cut tobacco wrapped in paper for smoking.  Cigarettes are a major cause of preventable diseases and death worldwide. |He lit a cigarette and inhaled deeply. 他点燃了一支香烟，深深地吸了一口。  She quit smoking cigarettes last year. 她去年戒了烟。| cigars, tobacco | healthy foods, exercise | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I smoke cigarettes every day. = 我每天都抽烟。
He lights a cigarette before work. = 他上班前会点燃一支烟。

一般过去时:
I smoked cigarettes for many years. = 我抽烟很多年了。
She smoked a cigarette after dinner. = 她晚饭后抽了一支烟。

现在完成时:
I have smoked cigarettes since I was 18. = 从我18岁起我就抽烟了。
She has quit smoking cigarettes for a year. = 她戒烟一年了。

过去完成时:
I had smoked a pack of cigarettes before the meeting. = 会议前我抽了一包烟。
He had never smoked cigarettes before that day. = 在那一天之前他从未抽过烟。

将来时:
I will quit smoking cigarettes next month. = 我下个月要戒烟。
She will probably smoke a cigarette after the presentation. = 她演示之后可能会抽支烟。

虚拟语气:
If I didn't smoke cigarettes, I would be healthier. = 如果我不抽烟，我会更健康。
I wish I had never started smoking cigarettes. = 我真希望我从未开始抽烟。

被动语态:
Cigarettes are sold everywhere. = 香烟到处都有卖。
Millions of cigarettes are consumed daily. = 每天消耗数百万支香烟。

疑问句:
Do you smoke cigarettes? = 你抽烟吗？
How many cigarettes do you smoke a day? = 你一天抽几支烟？

否定句:
I don't smoke cigarettes anymore. = 我不再抽烟了。
He doesn't like smoking cigarettes. = 他不喜欢抽烟。
