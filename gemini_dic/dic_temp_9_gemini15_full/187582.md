romanticizing: /rəˈmæn.tɪ.saɪ.zɪŋ/ | v. |浪漫化；理想化 | To represent or think about something in a way that is more romantic or idealized than it really is.  |例句：He was romanticizing his past relationship, ignoring the many arguments and disagreements they had. 他在浪漫化他过去的恋情，忽略了他们之间多次的争吵和分歧。

例句：She romanticized the idea of living in Paris, imagining charming cafes and endless artistic inspiration. 她浪漫化了住在巴黎的想法，想象着迷人的咖啡馆和无尽的艺术灵感。


近义词：idealizing, idealization, glamorizing, sentimentalizing | 反义词：realistic, downplaying, trivializing
