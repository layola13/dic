scrounged:/ˈskraʊndʒd/| v.| 乞讨；骗取；搜刮；勉强获得 | To obtain (something) by scrounging or begging.  To search for and collect (something usable) from discarded materials. | 例句：He scrounged a cigarette from a passerby. 他向路人讨了一支烟。

He scrounged enough food for a week from the dumpster. 他从垃圾箱里翻出了够吃一周的食物。


近义词：beg, cadge, sponge, forage, scavenge | 反义词：give, donate, provide


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scrounges money from his friends. 他向朋友们要钱。
She scrounges around for old clothes. 她四处搜寻旧衣服。

一般过去时:
They scrounged food from the neighbours. 他们向邻居们讨要食物。
I scrounged a ride home. 我蹭了一程回家。

现在完成时:
He has scrounged enough materials for his project. 他已经为他的项目搜集到了足够的材料。
We have scrounged together a meal. 我们勉强凑合了一顿饭。

过去完成时:
She had scrounged a place to sleep before the storm hit.  暴风雨来临之前，她已经找到一个睡觉的地方了。
They had scrounged some firewood before nightfall. 天黑之前，他们已经收集了一些柴火。

将来时:
I will scrounge some money to buy a new book. 我会想办法弄些钱买一本新书。
They will scrounge around for a solution. 他们会四处寻找解决办法。

虚拟语气:
If I needed money, I would scrounge it from my family. 如果我需要钱，我会向家人要。
I wish I hadn't scrounged that old car – it keeps breaking down. 我真希望我没有弄到那辆旧车——它总是坏。

被动语态:
A meal was scrounged together for the homeless man. 为那个无家可归的人勉强凑合了一顿饭。
The materials for the project were scrounged from various sources.  项目的材料是从各种来源搜集来的。

疑问句:
Where did he scrounge that old bicycle? 他在哪儿弄到那辆旧自行车的？
Did you scrounge enough money for the trip? 你为这次旅行弄到足够的钱了吗？

否定句:
He didn't scrounge any money from me. 他没向我要钱。
They couldn't scrounge up enough support for their cause.  他们无法为自己的事业获得足够的支持。
