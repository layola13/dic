explat:/ɛkˈspleɪt/| v.|解释，阐述，开发，剥削| to explain something clearly or in detail; to use something to the greatest advantage; to treat someone unfairly in order to benefit from their work.|例句：He explicated the theory in simple terms. 他用简单的术语解释了这个理论。  例句：The company is exploiting its workers by paying them very low wages. 这家公司通过支付非常低的工资来剥削其工人。|近义词：explain, clarify, utilize, exploit|反义词：confuse, obscure, neglect, protect
