candidate:/ˈkændɪdeɪt/| n.|候选人，应试者|A person who is considered for or is applying for a job, position, or award.|例句：He is a strong candidate for the job. 他是这份工作的有力候选人。|例句：She's a candidate for the scholarship. 她是奖学金的申请者。|近义词：applicant, contender, nominee|反义词：voter, elector


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a candidate for mayor. = 他是市长候选人。
She is a strong candidate in the race. = 她是比赛中强有力的竞争者。


一般过去时:
He was a candidate in the last election. = 他是上次选举的候选人。
She was a candidate for the position, but didn't get it. = 她曾是这个职位的候选人，但没有得到。


现在完成时:
He has been a candidate for several positions. = 他曾是多个职位的候选人。
She has been a candidate for the award three times. = 她曾三次角逐该奖项。


过去完成时:
He had been a candidate before, but he withdrew. = 他以前曾是候选人，但他退出了。
She had been a candidate for years before she finally won. = 在她最终获胜之前，她已经做了多年的候选人。


将来时:
He will be a candidate in the next election. = 他将在下次选举中成为候选人。
She will be a candidate for the presidency. = 她将竞选总统。


虚拟语气:
If he were a candidate, he would win. = 如果他是候选人，他会赢。
If she had been a candidate, she might have won. = 如果她是候选人，她可能会赢。


被动语态:
He was considered a strong candidate. = 他被认为是一位强有力的候选人。
She was nominated as a candidate for the award. = 她被提名为该奖项的候选人。


疑问句:
Is he a candidate for the job? = 他是这份工作的候选人吗？
Who are the candidates for the election? = 谁是这次选举的候选人？


否定句:
He is not a candidate for the position. = 他不是这个职位的候选人。
She was not a candidate for the scholarship. = 她不是奖学金的申请者。


PHRASE->
potential candidate =  潜在候选人

ideal candidate =  理想候选人
successful candidate = 成功候选人
strong candidate =  强劲候选人
