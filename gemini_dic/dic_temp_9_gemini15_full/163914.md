predetermine:/ˌpriːdɪˈtɜːrmɪn/| vt.|预先决定；预定|To decide or settle beforehand; to fix or determine in advance.|The outcome of the election seemed predetermined.  选举的结果似乎早已注定。|The date of the meeting was predetermined. 会议日期是预先确定的。|determine, decide, settle|chance, accident
