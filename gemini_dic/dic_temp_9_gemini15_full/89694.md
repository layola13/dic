hooting:/ˈhuːtɪŋ/| n. |呼叫声；猫头鹰叫声|The sound of an owl or other bird.  It can also refer to the act of shouting or yelling.|例句：The hooting of the owl filled the night. 猫头鹰的叫声充满了夜晚。

例句：I heard the distant hooting of a train. 我听到远处火车鸣笛的声音。


近义词：shouting, yelling, whooping,  owl call

反义词：silence, quiet


词性：名词
