environmental:/ɪnˌvaɪrənˈmɛntəl/ | adj. | 环境的；有关环境的 | Relating to the environment.  | Example:  Environmental protection is crucial for our future.  环境保护对我们的未来至关重要。  |近义词：ecological, environmentalist | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory produces environmental pollution. = 工厂产生环境污染。
Environmental issues are a major concern. = 环境问题是一个主要关注点。


一般过去时:
The government implemented new environmental regulations. = 政府实施了新的环境法规。
The environmental impact was significant. = 环境影响是巨大的。


现在完成时:
Scientists have studied the environmental effects of climate change. = 科学家们已经研究了气候变化的环境影响。
We have made significant progress in environmental protection. = 我们在环境保护方面取得了显著进展。


过去完成时:
They had already assessed the environmental risks before starting the project. = 他们在启动项目之前就已经评估了环境风险。
The company had ignored previous environmental warnings. = 公司之前忽略了环境警告。


将来时:
The new policy will improve environmental standards. = 新政策将提高环境标准。
They will monitor the environmental conditions carefully. = 他们将仔细监测环境状况。


虚拟语气:
If we acted now, we could prevent further environmental damage. = 如果我们现在采取行动，我们可以防止进一步的环境破坏。
It is vital that we address environmental concerns immediately. = 我们必须立即解决环境问题至关重要。


被动语态:
Environmental laws are enforced by the government. = 环境法由政府执行。
The report was criticized for its lack of environmental data. = 该报告因缺乏环境数据而受到批评。


疑问句:
What are the environmental consequences of this project? = 这个项目的环保后果是什么？
Are there any environmental concerns related to this development? = 这个开发项目是否存在任何环境问题？


否定句:
The company did not comply with environmental regulations. = 公司没有遵守环境法规。
This product is not environmentally friendly. = 此产品不环保。
