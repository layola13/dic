pillar:/ˈpɪlər/ | n. | 柱子；支柱；栋梁 | A strong upright support, usually of stone, wood, or metal, used in buildings or other structures.  It can also refer to a person or thing of great importance that provides support or strength. | Example: The ancient temple was supported by massive stone pillars. 这座古老的寺庙由巨大的石柱支撑。  The teacher is a pillar of the community.这位老师是社区的支柱。|近义词：column, support, mainstay |反义词：weakness, detriment


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The pillars support the roof. = 柱子支撑着屋顶。
He is a pillar of strength to his family. = 他是他家庭的支柱。

一般过去时:
The old pillars collapsed during the earthquake. = 老柱子在地震中倒塌了。
She was a pillar of the community in her younger years. = 她年轻时是社区的支柱。

现在完成时:
The pillars have stood for centuries. = 这些柱子已经矗立了几个世纪。
He has been a pillar of support throughout my difficulties. = 在我遇到困难的时候，他一直是我的支柱。

过去完成时:
The pillars had already weakened before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，柱子就已经变弱了。
She had been a pillar of the organization for many years before retiring. = 她在退休前已经为这个组织做了多年的支柱。

将来时:
The new pillars will be made of steel. = 新柱子将由钢制成。
He will continue to be a pillar of strength for his family in the future. = 他将来会继续成为他家庭的支柱。

虚拟语气:
If those pillars were stronger, the building would not have collapsed. = 如果那些柱子更结实，建筑物就不会倒塌了。
If he were a pillar of the community, he would help more. = 如果他是社区的支柱，他会提供更多帮助。

被动语态:
The pillars were carefully inspected. = 柱子受到了仔细检查。
He was regarded as a pillar of the community. = 他被视为社区的支柱。

疑问句:
Are those pillars strong enough? = 那些柱子够结实吗？
Is he a pillar of the community? = 他是社区的支柱吗？

否定句:
The pillars are not strong enough to support the weight. = 柱子不够结实，无法支撑重量。
He is not a pillar of the community; he is often controversial. = 他不是社区的支柱；他经常引起争议。
