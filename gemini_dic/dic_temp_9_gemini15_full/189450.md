USAGE->
一般现在时:
The boat sails smoothly on the calm sea. = 这艘船在平静的海面上平稳地航行。
He sails every weekend. = 他每个周末都去航海。


一般过去时:
The ship sailed away from the harbor. = 船驶离了港口。
They sailed across the Atlantic Ocean. = 他们横渡了大西洋。


现在完成时:
We have sailed around the world. = 我们已经环游世界了。
She has sailed in many competitions. = 她参加过许多比赛。


过去完成时:
They had sailed for days before they reached land. = 他们在到达陆地之前已经航行了好几天了。
The captain had sailed these waters many times. = 船长多次航行过这些水域。


将来时:
We will sail to the Caribbean next year. = 我们明年将航行到加勒比海。
They will sail the boat tomorrow. = 他们明天将驾驶那艘船。


虚拟语气:
If I had sails, I would sail around the world. = 如果我有帆，我就会环游世界。
I wish I were sailing in the sun now. = 我希望我现在正在阳光下航行。


被动语态:
The boat was sailed by an experienced captain. = 这艘船由一位经验丰富的船长驾驶。
The sails were damaged in the storm. = 船帆在暴风雨中受损了。


疑问句:
Does the boat sail well in strong winds? = 这艘船在强风中航行得好吗？
When will the ship sail? = 船什么时候启航？


否定句:
The ship didn't sail today because of the storm. = 由于暴风雨，船今天没有航行。
They don't sail often. = 他们不常航海。
