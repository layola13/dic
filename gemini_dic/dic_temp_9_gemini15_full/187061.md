riven:/ˈrɪvən/| adj. v.|分裂的；撕裂的；受折磨的|Split or broken; deeply affected by distress or conflict.|The land was riven by drought.  这片土地因干旱而干裂。|The country was riven by civil war.  这个国家被内战撕裂了。|近义词：split, torn, fractured, shattered|反义词：united, whole, intact


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The earthquake rives the mountain. = 地震撕裂了山脉。
The argument rives the family apart. = 争吵使这个家庭分崩离析。

一般过去时:
The storm rived the old ship. = 暴风雨撕裂了那艘旧船。
His grief rived his heart. = 他的悲伤撕裂了他的心。

现在完成时:
The years have riven the ancient monument. = 岁月已将这古老的纪念碑破坏殆尽。
The conflict has riven the community. = 冲突已经分裂了这个社区。

过去完成时:
The land had been riven by centuries of erosion. = 这片土地已被数百年的侵蚀所破坏。
The family had been riven by years of discord. = 这个家庭多年来一直因不和而分裂。

将来时:
The next earthquake will likely rive the fault line further. = 下一次地震可能会进一步撕裂断层线。
The relentless pressure will eventually rive the dam. = 不断的压力最终会冲垮大坝。

虚拟语气:
If the bridge were riven, we would be stranded. = 如果桥梁被撕裂，我们将被困住。
I wish the conflict hadn't riven our nation. = 我真希望冲突没有分裂我们的国家。

被动语态:
The mountain was riven by the earthquake. = 山被地震撕裂了。
The fabric of society was riven by the revolution. = 社会结构被革命撕裂了。

疑问句:
Was the ancient city riven by war? = 古城是被战争摧毁的吗？
Is the community riven by factionalism? = 这个社区是被派系斗争分裂的吗？

否定句:
The earthquake didn't rive the building. = 地震没有破坏那栋建筑物。
The disagreement didn't rive their friendship. = 分歧并没有破坏他们的友谊。


PHRASE->
riven with  充满...的（裂缝，痛苦等）
riven by  被...撕裂的
deeply riven  深深分裂的
riven apart  被撕裂
riven asunder  被完全撕裂
