crabeater: /ˈkræbiːtə(r)/ | n. |食蟹者；(尤指)食蟹海豹|  A crabeater is an animal that eats crabs, most notably the crabeater seal.  The name is somewhat misleading, as their primary diet consists of krill, not crabs.  |例句：The crabeater seal, despite its name, doesn't primarily eat crabs. = 食蟹海豹，顾名思义，主要并不吃螃蟹。

The crabeater seal is a remarkable creature adapted to the harsh Antarctic environment. = 食蟹海豹是一种适应南极洲恶劣环境的非凡生物。

近义词：krill eater (for the seal), crustacean eater |反义词：crab avoider, herbivore |词性：名词
