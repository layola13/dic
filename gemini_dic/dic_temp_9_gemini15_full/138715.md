nullity:/nʌˈlɪti/ | n. |无效；无效性；无价值；不存在；废除；婚姻无效|The legal invalidity or lack of force of something, especially a marriage or contract.  It refers to something that is void or has no legal effect.|例句：The contract was declared a nullity due to a lack of proper signature. =由于缺乏适当的签字，合同被宣布无效。
The court declared the marriage a nullity. = 法院宣布该婚姻无效。|近义词：invalidity, voidness, ineffectiveness|反义词：validity, effectiveness, force
