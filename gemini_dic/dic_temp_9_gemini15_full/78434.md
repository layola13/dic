get-togethers:/ˈɡɛtˌtʊɡɛðərz/| n. |聚会，集会 |A get-together is an informal social gathering of people.  It can be planned or spontaneous, and usually involves socializing and conversation. |例句：We're having a get-together at my house this weekend. = 我们这周末在我家举行一个聚会。

例句：The team had a get-together after winning the championship. = 团队在赢得冠军后举行了一次聚会。

近义词：gathering, meeting, party, reunion |反义词：separation, parting
