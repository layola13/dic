ethical:/ˈɛθɪkəl/ | adj. | 道德的；伦理的 | Relating to moral principles or the branch of knowledge dealing with these.  It concerns questions of right and wrong behavior. | Example: It is ethically questionable to use animals for testing cosmetics.  这在伦理上值得怀疑，用动物测试化妆品。

Example: The company has a strong ethical code. 这家公司有严格的道德规范。


近义词：moral, righteous, virtuous | 反义词：immoral, unethical, wrong


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He acts ethically in his business dealings. 他在商业交易中行为道德。
She considers the ethical implications of her research. 她考虑她研究的伦理意义。


一般过去时:

He behaved ethically even under pressure. 他即使在压力下也表现得道德。


现在完成时:
They have always acted ethically in their business practices. 他们始终在商业实践中行为道德。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
