lesses:/ˈlɛsiz/| n.|租赁物；租约；租金|The plural of "lease".  It refers to multiple lease agreements or the things leased.|例句：The building's lesses include several small businesses and a large corporation. 这座建筑物的租赁物包括几家小企业和一家大公司。|例句：Negotiating the lesses proved more difficult than anticipated. 谈判租赁比预期的要困难。|近义词：rentals, tenancies|反义词：ownership
