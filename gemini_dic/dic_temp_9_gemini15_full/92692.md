icebreaker:/ˈaɪsˌbreɪkər/| n. |破冰船；打破僵局的事物 |A person, thing, or activity that helps to break the ice (to make people who have just met feel more comfortable with each other).  |例句：The icebreaker game helped the new team members get to know each other better. 破冰游戏帮助新团队成员更好地了解彼此。

例句：Telling a joke was a good icebreaker at the party. 在派对上讲个笑话是个不错的破冰方法。


近义词：conversation starter,  opening,  initiator


反义词：barrier, obstacle, impediment


词性: 名词
