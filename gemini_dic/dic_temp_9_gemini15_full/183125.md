reposition:/ˌriːˈpɒzɪʃən/ /ˌriːˈpɔːzɪʃən/ | vt. n. |重新定位；重新摆放；改变…的地位 |To place or establish something again in a different position, often to improve its effectiveness or appeal.  |例句：The company needs to reposition its brand to attract a younger audience. 公司需要重新定位其品牌以吸引更年轻的受众。

The marketing team decided to reposition the product as a premium item. 营销团队决定将该产品重新定位为高端产品。

近义词：relocate, readjust, restyle, revamp |反义词：fix, stabilize, maintain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company repositions its products annually. = 公司每年都会重新定位其产品。
We reposition the furniture every spring. = 我们每年春天都会重新摆放家具。


一般过去时:
They repositioned the statue after the storm. = 暴风雨后，他们重新摆放了雕像。
The CEO repositioned the company's strategy. = 首席执行官重新调整了公司的战略。


现在完成时:
The brand has repositioned itself as more eco-friendly. = 该品牌已将自身重新定位为更环保。
We have repositioned the marketing campaign several times. = 我们已经多次调整了营销活动。


过去完成时:
They had repositioned the company before the merger. = 他们曾在合并之前就重新调整了公司。
The artist had repositioned the canvas before adding the final touches. = 艺术家在添加最后的润色之前已经重新摆放了画布。


将来时:
We will reposition the website next month. = 我们下个月将重新调整网站。

虚拟语气:
