reached:/riːtʃt/ | v. |到达；实现；达成；联系上 | To arrive at a place or point in time or space; to achieve a goal or objective; to make contact with someone. |例句：We reached the summit at noon.  我们中午到达了山顶。|例句：She finally reached her goal of becoming a doctor. 她最终实现了成为医生的目标。|近义词：arrived, attained, achieved, contacted |反义词：missed, failed, left


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reaches the station every morning at 7. 他每天早上7点到达车站。
She reaches out to her friends regularly. 她定期与朋友们联系。


一般过去时:
They reached their destination safely. 他们安全到达目的地。
He reached a compromise with his business partner. 他与他的商业伙伴达成了妥协。


现在完成时:
We have reached a critical point in the negotiations. 我们在谈判中已到达了一个关键点。
She has reached out to several potential investors. 她已经联系了几位潜在投资者。


过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, they had already reached an agreement. 当我到达时，他们已经达成协议了。
Before the deadline, they had reached a consensus on the project. 在截止日期前，他们就项目达成了共识。


将来时:
We will reach the city by nightfall. 我们将在夜幕降临前到达城市。
They will reach a decision by the end of the week. 他们将在本周末做出决定。


虚拟语气:
If he had tried harder, he might have reached his full potential. 如果他更加努力，他也许就能充分发挥自己的潜力。
I wish I had reached him before he left. 我希望在他离开之前我已经联系到他了。


被动语态:
The agreement was reached after many hours of discussion. 经过数小时的讨论后，协议达成。
The decision was reached unanimously. 这一决定是一致通过的。


疑问句:
Have you reached a conclusion yet? 你得出结论了吗？
Did they reach the summit successfully? 他们成功登顶了吗？


否定句:
