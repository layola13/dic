gunny-bag:/ˈɡʌniˌbæɡ/| n. |麻袋；粗布袋 |A gunny bag is a large, strong bag made of burlap or similar coarse fabric, often used for storing or transporting goods.  |例句：The potatoes were stored in a large gunny bag.  土豆储存在一个大麻袋里。
The farmer carried the grain in a gunny bag. 农民用麻袋扛着谷物。|近义词：sack, burlap bag |反义词:None
