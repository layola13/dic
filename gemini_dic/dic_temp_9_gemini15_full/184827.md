resuspensions:/ˌriːsəˈspɛnʃənz/| n.| 再悬浮；再悬挂 |The act of suspending something again after it has been settled or precipitated.  It often refers to the redispersion of a solid in a liquid or the redispersion of a precipitate.  |例句：The resuspension of the sediment in the river caused increased turbidity. 河流中沉淀物的再悬浮导致浊度增加。

例句：The researchers studied the resuspension characteristics of different types of soil particles. 研究人员研究了不同类型土壤颗粒的再悬浮特性。


近义词：redispersion, re-suspension

反义词：settling, precipitation

词性:名词
