particularized: /pərˈtɪkjʊləraɪzd/ | adj. | 特定的；详细说明的 |  Giving specific details; made particular.  It refers to something that has been specified or described in detail, focusing on individual aspects rather than general characteristics. |


例句：

The report particularized the problems faced by small businesses.  报告详细说明了小型企业面临的问题。

He gave a particularized account of the accident. 他对事故做了详细的描述。


近义词：specified, detailed, delineated, itemized

反义词：general, vague, unspecified, abstract

词性: adjective
