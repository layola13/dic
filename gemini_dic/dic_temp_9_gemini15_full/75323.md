frorn:/frɔːrn/| adj.| 前面的；以前的；在前的|  This word does not exist in standard English. It seems to be a misspelling of "from".  If you meant "from", please see the explanation below.


from:/frɒm/| prep.| 来自；从；从……起；由于；因为|Indicates origin, source, or starting point.  It shows the relationship between something and its beginning or cause. |Examples: I received a letter from my friend. (我收到了一封来自朋友的信。)  She walked from the park to the library. (她从公园步行到图书馆。) |近义词：out of, since, off,  |反义词: to, into, towards


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I come from China. = 我来自中国。
The package is from Amazon. = 这个包裹来自亚马逊。

一般过去时:
He came from a small town. = 他来自一个小镇。
The news came from a reliable source. = 这个消息来自可靠的消息来源。

现在完成时:
She has been away from home for a long time. = 她离开家已经很久了。
We have received many emails from our customers. = 我们已经收到了很多来自客户的邮件。

过去完成时:
They had already left before I came from work. = 我下班回来之前他们已经离开了。
I had heard about the accident from several people. = 我已经从几个人那里听说过这起事故。

将来时:
I will be back from my trip next week. = 我下周将从旅行中回来。
She will send a package from the shop. = 她将从商店寄一个包裹。

虚拟语气:
If I came from a wealthy family, I would have a different life. = 如果我来自一个富裕的家庭，我的生活就会不一样。
I wish I came from a better background. = 我希望我来自一个更好的背景。

被动语态:
The information was received from our supplier. = 信息是从我们的供应商那里收到的。
The decision was made from the best intentions. = 这个决定是出于好意的。

疑问句:
Where are you from? = 你是哪里人？
From where did the idea come? = 这个想法从哪里来？

否定句:
I don't come from a big city. = 我不是来自大城市。
The product is not from this factory. = 这个产品不是来自这家工厂。

far from = 远非；决非
from scratch = 从零开始
free from = 免于；没有
