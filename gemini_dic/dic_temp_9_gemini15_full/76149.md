fuseless: /ˈfjuːzəlɪs/ | adj. | 无保险丝的；不带熔断器的 | Without a fuse; lacking a fuse.  The term usually refers to electrical systems or devices. |  例句：The fuseless circuit breaker provided reliable overcurrent protection.  无保险丝断路器提供了可靠的过电流保护。


例句：This fuseless power supply is designed for high reliability.  这个无保险丝电源的设计是为了提高可靠性。


近义词：fuse-less (same meaning, less common)


反义词：fused


词性：形容词
