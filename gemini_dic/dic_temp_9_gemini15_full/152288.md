patter:/ˈpætər/| n., v.|喋喋不休；模式；轻拍声；|A rapid succession of sounds; a repeated or characteristic pattern of sounds or actions; a light, rapid tapping sound.|例句：The children's patter of tiny feet could be heard on the stairs. 孩子们的小脚丫在楼梯上发出轻快的脚步声。|近义词：babble, chatter, rhythm, beat|反义词：silence, stillness
