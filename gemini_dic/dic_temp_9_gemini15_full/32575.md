chirograph:/ˈkaɪrəɡræf/| n.|手写文件；亲笔签名；契约|A chirograph is a document written entirely by hand, especially a legal document or deed signed by the parties involved.  It emphasizes the handwritten aspect, implying authenticity and personal commitment.|Example:  The ancient chirograph detailed the land transfer between the two families.  古老的手写文件详细记录了两大家族之间的土地转让。|近义词：handwriting, manuscript, deed|反义词：print, typescript
