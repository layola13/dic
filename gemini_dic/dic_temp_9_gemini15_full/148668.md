overtime:/ˈoʊvərtaɪm/ | n. adv.|加班；超时；加班费 |  Extra time worked beyond one's normal working hours.  |例句：He worked overtime to finish the project. 他加班完成了项目。|例句：They paid overtime for the extra hours. 他们支付了加班费。|近义词：extra time, extra hours|反义词：regular hours, standard time|名词，副词
