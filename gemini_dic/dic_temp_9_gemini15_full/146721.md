overhonest: /ˌoʊvərˈɑːnɪst/ | adj. | 过分诚实的，过于坦率的 | Excessively honest; to a degree that is considered tactless or inappropriate.  It implies a lack of diplomacy or consideration for the feelings of others. |  He was overhonest in his criticism, hurting her feelings unintentionally. 他在批评中过于坦率，无意中伤了她的感情。  She's overhonest about her flaws; it makes her seem insecure. 她对自己缺点过于坦诚，这让她显得不自信。|近义词：indiscreet, blunt, frank, candid | 反义词：tactful, diplomatic, subtle
