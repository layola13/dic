salter:/ˈsɔːltər/| n. v.| 撒盐者；腌制者；盐渍机 | A person or thing that salts something.  A salter is someone who adds salt to food, or a machine used for salting. |例句：The fisherman is a skilled salter of fish.  这位渔民是位熟练的鱼类腌制者。


The automated salter efficiently processes large quantities of meat. 这台自动盐渍机高效地处理大量的肉类。|近义词：seasoner, preserver|反义词：desalter (反义词较为宽泛，因为salter的动作是加盐，反义词应是去除盐分)|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He salts the meat every day. = 他每天都给肉加盐。
She salts her vegetables before cooking. = 她在烹饪蔬菜之前加盐。


一般过去时:
They salted the fish yesterday. = 他们昨天腌制了鱼。
He salted the soup to improve its taste. = 他加盐调味汤。


现在完成时:
I have salted all the potatoes. = 我已经把所有的土豆都腌好了。
She has salted the beef for several hours. = 她已经腌牛肉好几个小时了。


过去完成时:
They had salted the fish before they grilled it. = 他们烤鱼之前已经腌制好了。
She had salted the vegetables before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经把蔬菜腌好了。


将来时:
We will salt the pork tomorrow. = 我们明天将腌制猪肉。
They will salt the cucumbers before pickling. = 他们将在腌制黄瓜之前先加盐。


虚拟语气:
If I salted the food more, it would taste better. = 如果我多加点盐，味道会更好。
I wish I had salted the pasta before cooking it. = 我真希望我在烹饪意面之前加了盐。


被动语态:
The fish was salted and dried. = 鱼被腌制并晒干了。
The meat is salted with special herbs. = 肉用特殊的香草腌制。


疑问句:
Do you salt your popcorn? = 你在爆米花里加盐吗？
Does she salt the vegetables excessively? = 她是否加过多的盐在蔬菜里？


否定句:
I don't salt my oatmeal. = 我不在我的燕麦粥里加盐。
They didn't salt the chicken properly. = 他们没有正确地给鸡肉加盐。


PHRASE->
salt away (储蓄；保存):  He salts away a portion of his earnings each month. 他每个月都存一部分钱。
salt of the earth (极好的人；好人): She's the salt of the earth, always willing to help. 她是非常好的人，总是乐于助人。
take with a grain of salt (持怀疑态度；半信半疑): I'd take his claims with a grain of salt. 我会对他所说的话持怀疑态度。
worth one's salt (称职的；有价值的):  Any employee worth their salt would have noticed the problem. 任何称职的员工都会注意到这个问题。
add salt to injury (雪上加霜；火上浇油): Losing the game and then breaking my leg added insult to injury. 输了比赛，然后又摔断了腿，真是雪上加霜。


词源:
The word "salter" derives directly from the Old English word "salt," meaning salt, and the suffix "-er," indicating an agent or doer of an action.  Its evolution is straightforward, reflecting the simple process of adding salt.
