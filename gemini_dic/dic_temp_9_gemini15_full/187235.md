robes:/roʊbz/| n.|长袍，礼服|Long, loose outer garments, often worn as part of a religious or ceremonial dress, or as a comfortable garment at home.|例句：The monks wore simple brown robes.  僧侣穿着简单的棕色长袍。

例句：She relaxed in her comfortable silk robes after a long day.  她长途跋涉一天后，穿着舒适的丝绸长袍放松身心。


近义词：gown, cloak, mantle|反义词：None specifically.

词源：源自古法语 robe，最终源自拉丁语 rapere（夺取，抢夺），指其遮盖身体的功能。
