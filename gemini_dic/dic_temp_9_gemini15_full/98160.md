inscience:/ɪnˈsaɪəns/| n. |在科学中，科学方面|In science, observation and experimentation are crucial. 在科学中，观察和实验至关重要。|近义词：scientifically|反义词：unscientifically

一般现在时:
Scientific research contributes to our understanding of the world. 科学研究有助于我们了解世界。
New discoveries are constantly being made in science. 科学领域不断有新的发现。


一般过去时:
Scientists conducted experiments to test their hypotheses. 科学家们进行了实验来检验他们的假设。
The results of the study were published in a scientific journal.  这项研究的结果发表在科学期刊上。


现在完成时:
Scientists have made significant progress in understanding genetics. 科学家们在理解基因方面取得了重大进展。
Many breakthroughs have occurred in the field of medicine. 医学领域发生了许多突破。


过去完成时:
Before the experiment, they had prepared all the necessary equipment. 实验前，他们已经准备好了所有必要的设备。
The scientists had already analyzed the data before they published their findings. 科学家们在发表研究结果之前已经分析了数据。


将来时:
Scientists will continue to explore the mysteries of the universe. 科学家们将继续探索宇宙的奥秘。
New technologies will revolutionize the field of science. 新技术将彻底改变科学领域。


虚拟语气:
If we had more funding, we could conduct more research. 如果我们有更多资金，我们可以进行更多研究。
It is essential that scientists maintain high ethical standards. 科学家们必须保持高尚的道德标准。


被动语态:
The experiment was carefully designed to minimize errors. 实验被精心设计以最大限度地减少错误。
New theories are constantly being developed and tested. 新理论不断被发展和检验。


疑问句:
What are the implications of this scientific discovery? 这一科学发现的意义是什么？
How does this research contribute to our understanding of climate change? 这项研究如何帮助我们了解气候变化？


否定句:
This hypothesis is not supported by the experimental data. 实验数据不支持这一假设。
The results of the study did not show any significant differences. 研究结果没有显示任何显著差异。
