pengolin:/ˈpɛŋɡəlɪn/| n. |穿山甲|A mammal of the order Pholidota, characterized by its scaly body and long snout.  They are nocturnal insectivores found in Africa and Asia.|例句：The pangolin is a shy, nocturnal creature. 穿山甲是一种害羞的夜行动物。|例句：The illegal wildlife trade threatens the survival of the pangolin. 非法野生动物贸易威胁着穿山甲的生存。|近义词：scaly anteater|反义词：无
