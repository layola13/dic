drayed:/dreɪd/| v.|拖拉，缓慢地移动|To pull or carry (a heavy load) with difficulty or slowly.|The farmer drayed the heavy cart across the field. 农民费力地把沉重的马车拉过田地。|近义词：hauled, dragged, lugged |反义词：pushed, propelled.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drays the heavy load every day. 他每天都拖运沉重的货物。
The cart drays slowly uphill.  这辆车缓慢地向上坡爬行。

一般过去时:
They drayed the timber to the sawmill. 他们把木材拖到锯木厂。
The oxen drayed the wagon through the mud.  牛用车在泥泞中缓慢地拉动货车。

现在完成时:
The workers have drayed all the materials to the construction site. 工人们已将所有材料运到建筑工地。
He has drayed that heavy box for hours. 他已经拖了那个沉重的箱子几个小时了。

过去完成时:
They had drayed the stones before the rain started. 下雨前他们已经把石头运走了。
The farmer had drayed the harvest to the barn before sunset.  日落前农民已将收成运到谷仓。

将来时:
They will dray the furniture to the new house tomorrow. 他们明天将把家具运到新房子。
The contractor will dray the building materials next week. 承包商下周将运送建筑材料。

虚拟语气:
If I had a horse, I would dray the hay more easily. 如果我有一匹马，我会更容易地运送干草。
I wish I could dray this heavy crate by myself. 我希望我能自己搬动这个沉重的板条箱。

被动语态:
The heavy equipment was drayed to the site by a large truck. 大型卡车将重型设备运送到了现场。
The goods were drayed to the warehouse safely. 货物安全地运送到了仓库。

疑问句:

否定句:

He couldn't dray the barrel alone. 他无法独自搬动那个桶。
