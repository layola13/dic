severus:/sɪˈvɪərəs/| adj.|严厉的，严峻的，严重的 | Severe means very strict, harsh, or serious. It can describe a person's manner, a situation, or a problem.  |例句：He was a Severus professor, known for his strict discipline. 他是一位以严格的纪律著称的严厉教授。

例句：The storm caused severe damage to the coastal areas. 暴风雨对沿海地区造成了严重的破坏。

近义词：stern, strict, harsh, rigorous, serious, grim

反义词：lenient, mild, gentle, merciful


词源：源自拉丁语 *severus，意思是“严厉的，严峻的”。
