词源:
The word "imprecision" is derived from the prefix "im-" (meaning "not") and the word "precision," which comes from the Latin word "praecisio," meaning "a cutting off," "a cutting short," or "a determining."  Therefore, "imprecision" literally means "not cut off" or "not precisely determined."
