resolutioner: /ˌrezəˈluːʃənər/ | n. |决议支持者；决议通过者 | A person who supports or votes for a resolution.  This term is primarily used in formal contexts, often relating to political or organizational settings.  It emphasizes the active role of the individual in enacting the resolution. | Example: The resolutioner's impassioned speech swayed many undecided members to vote in favor.  决议支持者的热情演讲说服了许多未决定的成员投票赞成。|近义词：supporter, advocate, proponent |反义词：opponent, detractor
