echoed:/ˈɛkoʊd/ | v. | 回响；重复；模仿 | To repeat or imitate a sound or words; to resound.  | The singer's voice echoed through the hall. 歌手的歌声在大厅回荡。 The teacher echoed the student's concerns. 老师回应了学生的担忧。 | repeated, reiterated, mimicked | silent, unheard


USAGE->

The empty canyon echoes with the sound of my voice.  空旷的山谷回荡着我的声音。
His words echo the sentiments of many. 他的话语反映了许多人的心声。
