shatterproof:/ˈʃætəpruːf/| adj.|防碎的，抗碎的|Resistant to shattering or breaking; designed to withstand impacts without breaking.  |例句：The phone case is shatterproof, protecting the phone from damage.  手机壳是防碎的，可以保护手机免受损坏。

例句：These shatterproof glasses are perfect for children. 这些防碎眼镜非常适合儿童。


近义词：unbreakable, impact-resistant,indestructible|反义词：fragile,breakable


词源:
The word "shatterproof" is a compound word formed from "shatter" and "proof".

* **Shatter:**  Comes from Old English *scaterian*, meaning "to scatter" or "to break into pieces".  This ultimately relates to Proto-Germanic roots.

* **Proof:**  From Old French *prove*, meaning "to test" or "to try".  This word shares origins with Latin *probare*.


用法说明:
