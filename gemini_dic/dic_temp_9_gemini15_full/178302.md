reconciled: /ˈrɛkənˌsaɪld/ | vt. | 使和解；调和；使一致；接受；妥协 | To restore friendly relations between; settle a dispute; bring into harmony or agreement. | 例句：They finally reconciled after a long quarrel. 他们经过长时间的争吵后终于和解了。

例句：The couple reconciled after many years of separation.  这对夫妇在分居多年后重归于好。


近义词：reunite, settle, resolve, harmonize, adjust

反义词：estrange, alienate, divide, separate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reconciles with his brother every year. 他每年都和他的兄弟和解。
She reconciles her work and family life. 她把工作和家庭生活协调得很好。


一般过去时:
They reconciled their differences. 他们化解了彼此的矛盾。
The warring factions reconciled after years of conflict.  交战的派系在多年的冲突后达成和解。


现在完成时:
She has reconciled herself to her fate. 她已经接受了自己的命运。
We have reconciled our accounts. 我们已经对账了。


过去完成时:
He had reconciled himself to the idea of failure before he started.  在他开始之前，他已经做好了失败的思想准备。
They had reconciled their differences before the meeting. 会议前他们已经解决了分歧。


将来时:
I hope they will reconcile soon. 我希望他们很快能和解。
The two countries will reconcile their trade disputes. 这两个国家将解决他们的贸易纠纷。


虚拟语气:
If only they could reconcile their differences!  如果他们能化解彼此的矛盾就好了！
I wish I could reconcile my conflicting desires. 我希望我能调和彼此冲突的愿望。


被动语态:
Their differences were eventually reconciled. 他们的分歧最终得到解决。
The opposing viewpoints were reconciled in the final report.  最终报告中调和了对立的观点。


疑问句:
Have you reconciled your bank statement? 你对银行对账单核对过了吗？
Can we reconcile these conflicting accounts? 我们能调和这些相互矛盾的说法吗？


否定句:
They could not reconcile their differences. 他们无法调和他们的分歧。
The two sides refused to be reconciled. 双方拒绝和解。


PHRASE->
reconcile oneself to something:  使自己适应…；接受…
reconcile A with B: 使A与B一致
be reconciled to:  对…感到满意；接受…
reconcile differences:  调解分歧
reconcile accounts: 对账


词源:
From Old French *reconcilier, from Latin reconciliare "to reconcile," from re- "again" + conciliare "to unite, win over," from concilium "council, assembly."  The sense development is from bringing together opposing groups in council to a more general sense of bringing things into agreement.
