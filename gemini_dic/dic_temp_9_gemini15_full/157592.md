pitfalled: /ˈpɪtfɔːld/ | adj. | 充满陷阱的；有危险的 |  Adorned or beset with pitfalls; fraught with danger or difficulty.  | The project was pitfalled from the start, with funding issues and conflicting priorities.  这个项目从一开始就充满了陷阱，资金问题和相互冲突的优先事项。 | perilous, hazardous, treacherous | safe, secure
