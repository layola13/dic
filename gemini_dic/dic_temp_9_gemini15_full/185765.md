reviews:/rɪˈvjuːz/| n.|评论，回顾，复习|Written opinions about a book, film, product etc.  The reviews for the new restaurant were excellent.|例句：The film received mostly positive reviews. 这部电影获得了大部分好评。

例句：I need to write reviews for the new products. 我需要为新产品撰写评论。

近义词：comments, critiques, assessments|反义词：None specifically|词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He writes reviews for a living. = 他靠写评论为生。
She regularly reviews new books for the magazine. = 她定期为杂志评论新书。


一般过去时:
They reviewed the evidence carefully. = 他们仔细审查了证据。
I reviewed my notes before the exam. = 我在考试前复习了笔记。


现在完成时:
She has reviewed hundreds of books in her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她已经评论了数百本书。
He has reviewed the contract and found no errors. = 他已经审查了合同，没有发现错误。


过去完成时:
They had reviewed the proposal before the meeting. = 他们会议前已经审查了提案。
She had reviewed her notes several times before the test. = 考试前她已经多次复习笔记了。


将来时:
They will review the application next week. = 他们下周将审查申请。
I will review the chapter before the quiz. = 我将在测验前复习这一章。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would review the document thoroughly. = 如果我有更多时间，我会彻底审查这份文件。
I wish I had reviewed my notes more carefully. = 我希望我更仔细地复习了我的笔记。


被动语态:
The book was reviewed by several critics. = 这本书被几位评论家评论过。
The proposal is being reviewed by the committee. = 提案正在由委员会审查。


疑问句:
Have you reviewed the document yet? = 你审查过文件了吗？
Will they review the decision? = 他们会复审决定吗？


否定句:
He didn't review the contract properly. = 他没有正确地审查合同。
She hasn't reviewed the evidence yet. = 她还没有审查证据。



PHRASE->
book reviews: 书评
movie reviews: 影评
product reviews: 产品评论
customer reviews: 客户评论
peer reviews: 同行评审
