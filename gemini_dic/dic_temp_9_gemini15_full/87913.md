hieroglyphs:/ˈhaɪəroʊɡlɪfs/ | n. | 象形文字 | Pictures or symbols representing words, ideas, or sounds, used as a system of writing, especially by the ancient Egyptians.  | Example: The Rosetta Stone helped scholars decipher ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs.  罗塞塔石碑帮助学者破译了古埃及象形文字。 |近义词： ideograms, pictographs | 反义词：alphabet (as a contrasting writing system)
