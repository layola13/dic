non-leveraged:/ˌnɒnˈlɛvərɪdʒd/ | adj. |  非杠杆的；无杠杆的 | Not using borrowed money or assets to increase the potential return of an investment.  This term is commonly used in finance and investing.  |  Example:  The company pursued a non-leveraged acquisition strategy, relying solely on its own resources. 这家公司采取了非杠杆收购战略，完全依靠自身资源。  The non-leveraged portfolio performed well despite the market downturn.  尽管市场低迷，非杠杆投资组合的表现仍然良好。 |  近义词: unleveraged, debt-free | 反义词: leveraged, indebted
