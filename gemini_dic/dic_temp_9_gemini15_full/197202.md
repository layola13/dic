shaper:/ˈʃeɪpər/| n. v.|造型者；塑造者；成形器；影响者 |A shaper is a person or thing that shapes something.  This can refer to a physical process of giving something a form, or a more metaphorical shaping of ideas, opinions, or character. |例句：The sculptor was a skilled shaper of clay.  雕塑家是一位熟练的粘土造型者。
The new CEO is a shaper of company culture. 新任首席执行官是公司文化的塑造者。|近义词：molder, former, sculptor, influencer |反义词：destroyer, ruiner


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The potter shapes the clay on the wheel. = 陶工在轮子上塑造粘土。
The wind shapes the sand dunes. = 风塑造沙丘。


一般过去时:
The artist shaped the wood into a bird. = 艺术家把木头雕成一只鸟。
The teacher shaped the students' understanding of the topic. = 老师塑造了学生对这个话题的理解。


现在完成时:
She has shaped her career through hard work and dedication. = 她通过努力工作和奉献塑造了自己的职业生涯。
The years have shaped his character. = 岁月磨砺了他的性格。


过去完成时:
He had shaped his argument before the debate. = 他在辩论前已经构思好了自己的论点。
The storm had shaped the coastline dramatically. = 暴风雨已经极大地改变了海岸线。


将来时:
The company will shape the future of the industry. = 这家公司将塑造该行业的未来。
The new law will shape the way we live. = 新法律将改变我们的生活方式。


虚拟语气:
If I had the right tools, I could shape the metal perfectly. = 如果我有合适的工具，我就能完美地塑造金属。
I wish I could shape my own destiny. = 我希望我能掌控自己的命运。


被动语态:
The clay is shaped by the potter's hands. = 粘土被陶工的手塑造。
The landscape was shaped by glaciers. = 地形是由冰川塑造的。


疑问句:
How does experience shape our personality? = 经验如何塑造我们的个性？
What factors shape public opinion? = 哪些因素塑造了公众舆论？


否定句:
He couldn't shape the dough into a loaf. = 他无法把面团揉成面包。
Their opinions did not shape the final decision. = 他们的意见并没有影响最终决定。


PHRASE->

shape up:  改进；振作起来
in shape:  处于良好状态
out of shape:  身体状况不佳
take shape:  成形；形成
shape shifter:  变形者 (a creature that can change its shape)
