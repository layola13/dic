coan:/koʊæn/| n. |冠(一种头饰)|A coan is a type of headdress, typically a conical cap worn by certain groups of people.  It's often made of stiff material and may be decorated.|Example: The ancient Egyptian pharaoh wore a magnificent coan during ceremonies.  古代埃及法老在仪式上戴着华丽的冠。|近义词：mitre, coronet, tiara|反义词：None specifically.
