rotas:/ˈroʊtəs/ | n. |轮班表，值班表|A rota is a schedule showing the times when people have to work.  It's often used in organizations where staff work in shifts or need to be on call. |例句：The hospital rota shows Dr. Smith is on duty tonight. 医院的轮班表显示史密斯医生今晚值班。|例句：We need to create a rota for covering the weekend shifts. 我们需要制定一个周末轮班表。|近义词：schedule, timetable, roster |反义词：无
