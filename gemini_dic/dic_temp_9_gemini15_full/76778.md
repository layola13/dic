gametophyte:/ˈɡæmɪtoʊfaɪt/ | n. |配子体 |The gametophyte is the multicellular haploid phase in the life cycle of plants and algae that undergo alternation of generations.  It produces gametes (sex cells) by mitosis.  The gametophyte generation alternates with the sporophyte generation, which is diploid and produces spores by meiosis. |Example: In mosses, the leafy green plant is the gametophyte, while the sporophyte is a stalk with a capsule at the top.  苔藓中，叶状绿色植物是配子体，而孢子体是顶部带有蒴的茎。|近义词：gamete-producing generation |反义词：sporophyte
