insuperable:/ɪnˈsuːpərəbl/| adj.|不可克服的，无法战胜的|incapable of being overcome; insurmountable.  The challenges they faced were insuperable.|例句：The task seemed insuperable at first, but with determination, they succeeded.  起初这项任务似乎无法克服，但凭借决心，他们成功了。|近义词： insurmountable, unconquerable, invincible|反义词：surmountable, conquerable
