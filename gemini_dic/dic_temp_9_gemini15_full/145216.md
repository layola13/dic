overbear:/ˌoʊvərˈbɛr/| vt. adj.|专横的；盛气凌人的；压倒；使难以忍受|to be domineering or overbearing; to be too much to bear|例句：His overbearing manner alienated many people. 他盛气凌人的态度使许多人疏远了他。  例句：The heat was overbearing; we had to retreat to the shade. 炎热难耐，我们不得不退到阴凉处。|近义词： domineering, bossy, tyrannical, overwhelming |反义词：submissive, meek, gentle, tolerable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He overbears his colleagues with his opinions. = 他用自己的观点压制同事。
The heat overbears us in the afternoon. = 下午的热浪让我们难以忍受。


一般过去时:
She overbore her opponent in the debate. = 她在辩论中压倒了对手。
The noise overbore me, and I couldn't concentrate. = 噪音让我难以忍受，我无法集中注意力。


现在完成时:
His arrogance has overborne many people. = 他的傲慢已经压倒了许多人。
The evidence has overborne all doubt. = 证据已经消除了所有的疑虑。


过去完成时:
They had overborne all opposition before the project started. = 在项目开始之前，他们已经克服了所有的反对意见。
The weight of the responsibility had overborne him. = 责任的重担压垮了他。


将来时:
His ambition will overbear his common sense. = 他的野心将压倒他的常识。
I fear that the pressure will overbear her. = 我担心压力会压垮她。


虚拟语气:
If he were less overbearing, he would have more friends. = 如果他不那么专横，他就会有更多朋友。
If the heat had not overborne us, we could have finished the work. = 如果热浪没有让我们难以忍受，我们就能完成工作了。


被动语态:
His ideas were overborne by the majority. = 他的想法被大多数人否决了。
The small town was overborne by the flood. = 小镇被洪水淹没了。


疑问句:
Does his overbearing attitude bother you? = 他盛气凌人的态度让你困扰吗？
Will the storm overbear the bridge? = 暴风雨会摧毁这座桥吗？


否定句:
He did not overbear his colleagues with his ideas.  = 他没有用他的想法压制他的同事。
The evidence did not overbear the jury's doubts. = 证据并没有消除陪审团的疑虑。

overbearing power = 压倒一切的力量
