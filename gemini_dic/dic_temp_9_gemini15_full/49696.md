demob:/ˈdiːmɒb/| v.|复员，退伍|To discharge (soldiers, sailors, or airmen) from military service, especially at the end of a war.|例句：He was demobbed in 1945. 他在1945年复员。|例句：Thousands of soldiers were demobbed after the war ended. 战争结束后，数千名士兵复员了。|近义词：discharge, dismiss, release|反义词：recruit, enlist
