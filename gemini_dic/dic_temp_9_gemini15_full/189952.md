sanctimonious:/ˌsæŋktɪˈmoʊniəs/| adj.|假虔诚的；伪善的；道貌岸然的|Characterized by or showing an excessive or affected piety; self-righteous.  It suggests a hypocritical display of religious devotion or moral superiority.|例句：His sanctimonious pronouncements on morality were contradicted by his own behavior. 他关于道德的假道学说法与他自己的行为相矛盾。

例句：She delivered a sanctimonious lecture on the evils of smoking while secretly enjoying a cigarette. 她发表了一篇关于吸烟危害的假道学演讲，却偷偷地抽着烟。|近义词：hypocritical, self-righteous, pious, holier-than-thou|反义词：sincere, genuine, humble, unassuming|词性：形容词
