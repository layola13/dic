come-on:/ˈkʌmɑːn/|/ˈkʌmɒn/| interj. n. v.| 加油；来吧；得了；瞎说；诱惑；劝诱 |An expression of encouragement, urging, or protest.  It can be used in various contexts and with different tones.|例句：Come on, you can do it! 加油，你能行的！
例句：That's a come-on, don't believe it.  那是诱惑，别相信。|近义词：encouragement, persuasion, enticement, temptation,  baloney|反义词：discouragement, dissuasion


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Come on, let's go. = 来吧，咱们走吧。
He always comes on strong to women. = 他总是对女人很主动。


一般过去时:
She came on to him at the party. = 她在派对上对他献殷勤。
The salesman came on too strong, so I left. = 那个推销员太咄咄逼人了，所以我离开了。


现在完成时:
He's come on a lot since I last saw him. = 自从我上次见到他以来，他进步很大。
They've come on in leaps and bounds. = 他们的进步突飞猛进。


过去完成时:
I had come on to the idea before, but I hadn't pursued it. = 我以前想过这个主意，但我没有继续进行。
She had come on to many men before meeting him. = 在遇到他之前，她曾对许多男人献殷勤。


将来时:
I'll come on at your convenience. = 我会在你方便的时候来。
The show will come on at 8 pm. = 节目将在晚上8点开始。


虚拟语气:
If he came on stronger, she might have noticed him. = 如果他更主动一些，她可能会注意到他。
I wish he wouldn't come on so strong. = 我希望他不要这么咄咄逼人。


被动语态:
The new product is being come on to the market aggressively. = 新产品正在积极地推向市场。
He was easily come on to by her charm. = 他很容易被她的魅力吸引。


疑问句:
Come on, what's the problem? = 得了吧，问题是什么？
Is he coming on to her? = 他在对她献殷勤吗？


否定句:
Don't come on so strong. = 别那么咄咄逼人。
He didn't come on to her, he was just friendly. = 他并没有对她献殷勤，他只是很友好。
