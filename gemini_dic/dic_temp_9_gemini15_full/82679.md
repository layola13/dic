guest:/ɡɛst/| n.|客人，宾客 |A person who is invited to visit someone's home or attend a social event.  A person staying temporarily at a hotel or other accommodation.| 例句：We had several guests over for dinner last night.  昨晚我们请了几位客人来吃晚饭。

We are expecting a large number of guests at the wedding. 婚礼上我们预计会有很多宾客。


近义词：visitor, invitee, boarder | 反义词：host, resident


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The guest enjoys the party. = 客人享受着派对。
The guests are staying at a hotel. = 客人们住在一家酒店。

一般过去时:
The guest left early. = 客人早早离开了。
Many guests attended the conference. = 许多客人参加了会议。

现在完成时:
The guest has already arrived. = 客人已经到了。
Several guests have checked into the hotel. = 好几位客人已经入住酒店了。

过去完成时:
The guest had already eaten before we arrived. = 我们到达之前客人已经吃过了。
All the guests had left by midnight. = 到午夜时所有客人都离开了。

将来时:
The guest will arrive tomorrow. = 客人明天到达。
Many guests will attend the concert.  许多客人将参加音乐会。

虚拟语气:
If the guest were here, he would be happy. = 如果客人在这里，他会很高兴。
I wish the guest had stayed longer. = 我希望客人能多待一会儿。

被动语态:
The guest was welcomed warmly. = 客人受到了热烈的欢迎。
The room was prepared for the guest. = 房间已为客人准备好。

疑问句:
Is the guest staying long? = 客人会住很久吗？
How many guests are expected? = 预计有多少客人？

否定句:
The guest didn't come. = 客人没来。
There weren't many guests at the party. = 派对上没有很多客人。
