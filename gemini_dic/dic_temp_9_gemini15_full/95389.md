inches:/ˈɪntʃɪz/| n.|英寸|A unit of length equal to 1/12 of a foot.|例句：The table is 36 inches long. 这张桌子长36英寸。|The snake measured 12 inches. 那条蛇长12英寸。|近义词：centimeter|反义词：无
