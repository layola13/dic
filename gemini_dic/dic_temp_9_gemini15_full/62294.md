endanger:/ɪnˈdeɪndʒər/ | vt. |危及；使处于危险境地 | To expose to danger or risk; to jeopardize.  |例句：Pollution endangers the health of millions.  污染危及数百万人的健康。

例句：The reckless driver endangered the lives of others.  鲁莽的司机危及了他人的生命。


近义词：threaten, imperil, jeopardize, risk | 反义词：protect, safeguard, secure | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory's emissions endanger the local wildlife. = 工厂的排放危及当地野生动物。
Her careless actions endanger her own safety. = 她粗心的行为危及她自身的安全。


一般过去时:
The storm endangered the coastal communities. = 暴风雨危及了沿海社区。
His reckless behavior endangered his career. = 他鲁莽的行为危及了他的事业。


现在完成时:
Human activity has endangered many species. = 人类活动已经危及了许多物种。
Their irresponsible choices have endangered the future of the company. = 他们不负责任的选择已经危及了公司的未来。


过去完成时:
The faulty brakes had endangered the passengers' lives. = 故障的刹车曾危及乘客的生命。
He had endangered his reputation by lying. = 他撒谎危及了自己的名誉。


将来时:
Climate change will endanger coastal cities. = 气候变化将危及沿海城市。
The new policy may endanger the rights of minorities. = 新政策可能会危及少数群体的权利。


虚拟语气:
If he continued to drink, he would endanger his health. = 如果他继续喝酒，他会危及自己的健康。
I wish he hadn't endangered his job by being late. = 我真希望他没有因为迟到而危及他的工作。


被动语态:
Many animals are endangered by habitat loss. = 许多动物由于栖息地丧失而濒临灭绝。
The project was endangered by a lack of funding. = 该项目由于缺乏资金而面临危险。


疑问句:
Does this activity endanger the environment? =这项活动会危及环境吗？
Could this decision endanger national security? =这个决定会危及国家安全吗？


否定句:
The new law does not endanger the rights of citizens. = 新法律不会危及公民的权利。
The police ensured the area was safe and the public was not endangered. = 警方确保该地区安全，公众没有受到威胁。
