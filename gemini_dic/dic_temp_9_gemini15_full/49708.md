demobilizing: /ˌdiːˈməʊbɪlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 解散；复员；使…停止运作 | The act of demobilizing is the process of disbanding or discharging soldiers from military service, or more generally, of ceasing to operate or function.  It often involves the return of soldiers to civilian life and the dismantling of military structures. | 例句：The army is demobilizing after the war.  军队在战争结束后正在复员。The company is demobilizing its workforce due to economic downturn.  由于经济低迷，公司正在裁员。| 近义词：disbanding, discharging, decommissioning | 反义词：mobilizing, activating
