craftworks:/ˈkræftwɜːrks/| n.|工艺品；手工艺品；手工艺作品|Craftworks refers to objects or works created by skilled manual labor, often involving artistry and design.  It can encompass a wide range of items, from pottery and jewelry to furniture and sculptures.  The term emphasizes the skill and craftsmanship involved in the creation process.|例句：The museum showcased a stunning collection of local craftworks.  博物馆展示了一批当地令人惊叹的手工艺品。

例句：Her craftworks are known for their intricate detail and unique designs. 她的手工艺品以其精细的细节和独特的设计而闻名。|近义词：handicrafts, artworks, artisanry|反义词：mass-produced items, factory products|名词
