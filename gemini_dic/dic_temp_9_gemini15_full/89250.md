homing:/ˈhoʊmɪŋ/ | adj., n. | 返家的；寻家的；制导的；寻的 |  Relating to the instinct or ability of an animal to find its way home, or to a device or system that guides something to a target. |例句：The homing pigeon returned to its loft after a long journey.  这只信鸽在长途旅行后回到了它的鸽舍。


例句：The missile uses a sophisticated homing system. 这枚导弹使用的是一个复杂的制导系统。


近义词：returning, guiding, navigating | 反义词：straying, lost, unguided | 词性:形容词，名词
