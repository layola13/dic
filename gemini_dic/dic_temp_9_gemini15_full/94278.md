impersonation:/ɪmˌpɜːrsəˈneɪʃən/| n. |模仿；假扮；扮演；人格化|The act of imitating someone's voice, mannerisms, or character, often to deceive or entertain.  It can also refer to the embodiment of a quality or concept in a person or thing.|例句：His impersonation of the president was hilarious.  他的总统模仿秀非常搞笑。
例句：The actor's impersonation of the famous detective was incredibly convincing.  这位演员对那位著名侦探的模仿令人难以置信地逼真。|近义词：mimicry, simulation, portrayal, enactment|反义词：self, authenticity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He impersonates famous actors. = 他模仿著名的演员。
She impersonates a singer at the party. = 她在派对上扮演一位歌手。


一般过去时:
