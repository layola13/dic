screening: /ˈskriːnɪŋ/ | n. v. | 筛选；放映；检查 |

英文解析:The noun "screening" refers to the process of showing a film or other visual media, or the act of examining or testing something to identify something undesirable. As a verb, "to screen" means to show a film, to examine or test something, or to protect something from something else.


例句:

(Noun - showing a film):
The film screening was sold out. = 电影放映售罄了。

(Noun - examining/testing):
The airport security screening was thorough. = 机场安检非常仔细。


(Verb - showing a film):
They will be screening the documentary next week. = 他们下周将放映这部纪录片。

(Verb - examining/testing):
They screened the blood samples for the virus. = 他们对血液样本进行了病毒检测。


近义词: (Noun)  projection, examination, inspection, test, vetting
(Verb)  show, display, examine, inspect, test, vet


反义词: (Noun)  acceptance (depending on context), omission
(Verb)  acceptance (depending on context)


词性: noun, verb
