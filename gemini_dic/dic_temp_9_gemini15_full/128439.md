newport:/ˈnjuːpɔːrt/|/ˈnjuːpɔːrt/| n. |纽波特（地名）|A city or town, often with a port, in various locations, most famously Newport, Rhode Island in the US and Newport, Wales in the UK.  The name often suggests a new port or a recently established port city. |例句：Newport, Rhode Island is known for its Gilded Age mansions.  罗德岛的纽波特以其镀金时代的豪宅而闻名。

例句：We spent a week exploring the charming town of Newport in Wales. 我们花了一周时间探索了威尔士纽波特迷人的小镇。


近义词：haven, port, harbor
