inversely:/ɪnˈvɜːrsli/| adv.|反向地，相反地|In an inverse manner; oppositely.|The amount of time spent studying is inversely proportional to the amount of time spent playing video games.|近义词：conversely, reciprocally|反义词：directly, proportionately
