loneliness: /ˈloʊnlinəs/ | /ˈləʊnlinəs/ | n. |孤独；寂寞 | A feeling of sadness because one has no friends or company.  It's a state of being alone and often feeling unhappy or isolated as a result. |例句：The loneliness of the long winter nights was almost unbearable. 漫长的冬夜的孤独几乎让人难以忍受。

例句：Her loneliness intensified after her husband passed away.  她丈夫去世后，她的孤独感加剧了。


近义词：solitude, isolation, desolation, forlornness | 反义词：companionship, togetherness
