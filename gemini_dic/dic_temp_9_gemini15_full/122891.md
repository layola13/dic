mollycoddler:/ˈmɑːlikoʊdlər/ | n. |溺爱孩子的人；过分迁就的人 |A person who excessively pampers or indulges a child or another person.  |例句：He's such a mollycoddler; he never lets his children face any consequences for their actions. 他真是个溺爱孩子的家伙，从来不让他的孩子们为自己的行为承担任何后果。|例句：She's become a mollycoddler in her old age, constantly fussing over her grandchildren.  她上了年纪后变得很溺爱孙子辈了，总是围着他们转。|近义词：spoiler, pamperer, coddler |反义词：disciplinarian, strict parent
