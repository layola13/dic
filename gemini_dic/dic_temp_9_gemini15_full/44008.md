criminals:/ˈkrɪmɪnəlz/| n. |罪犯，犯人|People who have committed crimes.  Criminals can range from petty thieves to violent offenders.  The study of criminals and their behavior is a major field of criminology.|The criminals were apprehended by the police.|近义词：offenders, convicts, lawbreakers|反义词：law-abiding citizens, innocents
