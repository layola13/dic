mosaicked:/mɒˈzeɪkt/| adj.| 马赛克的；用马赛克装饰的 | Decorated with or resembling a mosaic.  A mosaicked surface is one that is covered with small pieces of colored material, like glass or stone, to create a design or picture. | 例句：The bathroom floor was beautifully mosaicked with tiny, iridescent tiles.  浴室地板用细小的、彩虹色的瓷砖漂亮地装饰成马赛克图案。

The kitchen backsplash was mosaicked in shades of blue and green. 厨房的挡板是用蓝色和绿色的马赛克装饰的。


近义词：tiled, inlaid, tessellated | 反义词：plain, undecorated | 词性：adjective


词源:  Derived from "mosaic," ultimately from Latin *musivum, from Greek  μῶσα (mosa), meaning "muse" (the arts were associated with the Muses). The connection comes from the elaborate and artistic nature of mosaic work.
