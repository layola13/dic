extemporized:/ɪkˈstɛmpəraɪzd/| v. |即兴创作的；临时准备的 | To speak or perform without preparation; to improvise.  |例句：He extemporized a speech on the topic of climate change. 他即兴发表了关于气候变化的演讲。|近义词：improvised, ad-libbed, impromptu |反义词：rehearsed, prepared, planned

词源:
From Late Latin *extemporarius, from Latin ex tempore "out of time," from ex "out of" + tempore "time."  The sense development is from "without premeditation" to "improvised."
