mingles:/ˈmɪŋɡəlz/| v.|混合；交往；融合|To mix or combine, or to come together and form a group.|例句：The different cultures mingled together at the festival.  不同的文化在节日里融合在一起。|例句：The guests mingled freely at the reception. 客人们在招待会上自由地交往。|近义词：mix, blend, combine, intermingle|反义词：separate, segregate
