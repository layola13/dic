reauthorization:/ˌriːˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |重新授权；再授权 |The act of authorizing something again; granting authority or power once more.  It often refers to the renewal of legal authority, typically for a government program or agency. |Example: The reauthorization of the Clean Air Act extended its provisions for another decade.  重新授权《清洁空气法案》将其条款延长了十年。

The bill's reauthorization is crucial for the continuation of its vital services.  该法案的重新授权对其重要服务的持续开展至关重要。|近义词：renewal, revalidation, extension |反义词：termination, cancellation, revocation |名词
