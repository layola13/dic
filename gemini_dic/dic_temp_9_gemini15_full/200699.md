sleeplessness:/ˈsliːpləsnəs/| n.|失眠，难以入睡|The inability to sleep or stay asleep.  It can be a symptom of various medical or psychological conditions, or it can be caused by stress, anxiety, or changes in routine.|例句：His sleeplessness was caused by the upcoming exam. 他的失眠是由即将到来的考试引起的。
例句：She suffers from chronic sleeplessness and needs professional help. 她患有慢性失眠，需要专业帮助。|近义词：insomnia, inability to sleep|反义词：sound sleep, restful sleep
