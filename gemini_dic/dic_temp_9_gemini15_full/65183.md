ethnology:/ˌeθˈnɒlədʒi/| n. |民族学，人种学|The scientific study of the origins and cultures of different human races and peoples.|例句：Ethnology explores the diverse customs and beliefs of various societies.  民族学探讨了各个社会不同的习俗和信仰。|例句：Her research focuses on the ethnology of indigenous tribes in the Amazon. 她的研究重点是亚马逊土著部落的民族学。|近义词：anthropology, cultural anthropology|反义词：N/A
