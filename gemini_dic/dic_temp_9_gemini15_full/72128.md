flooded:/ˈflʌdɪd/ | adj. v. | 洪水的；淹没的；充满的 | overwhelmed with something; inundated; filled to excess. |例句：The streets were flooded after the heavy rain.  大雨过后，街道被淹了。
The market is flooded with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价进口商品。|近义词：inundated, overwhelmed, deluged |反义词：drained, empty


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The river floods every spring. = 河水每年春天都会泛滥。
He floods the market with cheap goods. 他用廉价商品充斥市场。


一般过去时:
The basement flooded during the storm. = 暴风雨期间，地下室被淹了。
They flooded the streets with protesters. 他们用抗议者挤满了街道。


现在完成时:
The area has been flooded several times this year. = 今年该地区已经多次被洪水淹没。
The news has flooded social media.  这条新闻充斥着社交媒体。


过去完成时:
The house had flooded before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，房子已经被淹了。
The company had flooded the market with a similar product before. 公司之前已经用类似产品充斥市场。


将来时:
The river will flood if it rains heavily. = 如果下大雨，河水就会泛滥。
They will flood the city with advertisements. 他们将用广告充斥城市。


虚拟语气:
If it rained heavily, the streets would be flooded. = 如果下大雨，街道就会被淹。
If they had advertised more, they wouldn't have flooded the market. 如果他们广告打的更多，就不会让市场饱和。


被动语态:
The town was flooded by the rising river. = 城镇被上涨的河水淹没了。
The market was flooded with counterfeit goods. 市场充斥着假冒伪劣商品。


疑问句:
Was the house flooded? = 房子被淹了吗？
Is the market flooded with similar products? 市场被类似产品充斥了吗？


否定句:
The streets weren't flooded. = 街道没有被淹。
The market wasn't flooded with that product. 市场并没有被那种产品充斥。


PHRASE->

flood of: 一大批，大量
flood with: 充满，淹没
flood out: 被洪水冲走
flood insurance: 洪水保险
flash flood: 突发洪水
