PHRASE->
Oxford comma(牛津逗号): A comma placed before the "and" or "or" in a list of three or more items.
Serial comma(串行逗号):  Another name for the Oxford comma.
Comma splice(逗号连接):  Incorrectly joining two independent clauses with only a comma.
Introductory comma(引导逗号): A comma used after an introductory phrase or clause.
Parenthetical comma(插入语逗号): Commas used to set off parenthetical elements.
