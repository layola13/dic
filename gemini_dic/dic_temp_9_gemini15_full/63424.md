entrap:/ɪnˈtræp/| vt.|诱捕；使落入圈套|To catch or trap someone in a place or situation from which they cannot easily escape; to lure someone into a trap.|例句：The police entrapped the suspect into confessing. 警方诱使嫌疑犯认罪。|例句：He was entrapped by a clever scheme. 他被一个巧妙的计划所诱骗。|近义词： ensnare, snare, trap, lure|反义词： liberate, free, release|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They entrap criminals using undercover officers. = 他们使用卧底警官诱捕罪犯。
The company entraps its customers with misleading advertisements. = 公司用误导性广告诱骗顾客。


一般过去时:
The hunter entrapped a fox in a cleverly hidden snare. = 猎人用巧妙隐藏的陷阱诱捕了一只狐狸。
The con artist entrapped the elderly woman out of her savings. = 骗子诱骗了这位老年妇女的积蓄。


现在完成时:
The police have entrapped several members of the drug cartel. = 警方已经诱捕了贩毒集团的几名成员。
The company has entrapped countless customers through deceptive marketing. = 公司通过欺骗性营销诱骗了无数顾客。


过去完成时:
The detectives had entrapped the thief before he could escape the country. = 探员在他逃离该国之前已经诱捕了小偷。
The con man had entrapped his victims through a series of elaborate lies. = 骗子通过一系列精心编造的谎言诱骗了他的受害者。


将来时:
The authorities will entrap the fugitive as soon as he returns to the city. = 当逃犯回到城市后，当局就会将其诱捕。
They will entrap more customers with their new advertising campaign. = 他们将通过新的广告宣传活动诱骗更多顾客。


虚拟语气:
If they had entrapped the leader, the entire organization would have collapsed. = 如果他们诱捕了领导人，整个组织就会崩溃。
I wish I had entrapped the thief before he stole my car. = 我真希望在我车被偷之前就把小偷诱捕了。


被动语态:
The suspect was entrapped by the clever detective. = 嫌疑犯被这位聪明的侦探诱捕了。
Many innocent people have been entrapped by fraudulent schemes. = 许多无辜的人被欺诈计划所诱骗。


疑问句:
Were the suspects entrapped or did they willingly participate? = 嫌疑人是被诱捕的还是自愿参与的？
How many people have been entrapped by this scam? = 有多少人被这个骗局所诱骗？


否定句:
The police did not entrap the suspect; he confessed voluntarily. = 警方没有诱捕嫌疑犯；他自愿认罪。
The company claims it did not entrap its customers with false promises. = 公司声称它没有用虚假承诺诱骗顾客。
