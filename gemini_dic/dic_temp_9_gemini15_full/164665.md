preexplaining:/ˌpriːɪkˈspleɪnɪŋ/ | v. |预先解释，提前说明 |To explain something before it happens or is done, in order to prepare someone for it.  |例句：The teacher preexplained the difficult concepts before the assignment.  老师在布置作业之前预先解释了那些难懂的概念。 |例句：The tour guide preexplained the itinerary, highlighting key points of interest.导游提前说明了行程安排，重点介绍了几个景点。 |近义词：prefacing, foreshadowing, introducing |反义词：postponing, delaying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preexplains the process before starting. = 他在开始之前预先解释了这个过程。
She preexplains the rules to the students. = 她向学生预先解释了规则。


一般过去时:
The professor preexplained the exam format yesterday. = 教授昨天预先解释了考试的格式。
They preexplained the safety procedures before the experiment. = 他们在实验前预先解释了安全程序。


现在完成时:
She has preexplained the project's timeline to the team. = 她已经向团队预先解释了项目的进度安排。
He has preexplained all the potential risks involved. = 他已经预先解释了所有潜在的风险。


过去完成时:
They had preexplained the consequences of their actions. = 他们已经预先解释了他们行为的后果。
She had preexplained the instructions multiple times. = 她已经多次预先解释了说明。


将来时:
The instructor will preexplain the lab procedures tomorrow. = 教师明天将预先解释实验室的程序。
We will preexplain the steps involved in the registration process. = 我们将预先解释注册过程中的步骤。


虚拟语气:
If he had preexplained the instructions clearly, there would have been fewer mistakes. = 如果他清楚地预先解释了说明，错误就会少一些。
I wish she had preexplained the complex calculations. = 我希望她能预先解释一下复杂的计算。


被动语态:
The instructions were preexplained before the class began. = 在上课之前，说明已经预先解释过了。
The safety concerns were preexplained to the participants. = 安全问题已经预先向参与者解释过了。


疑问句:
Was the procedure preexplained to everyone? = 程序是否已经预先向所有人解释过了？
Will the benefits be preexplained to all employees? = 所有的利益是否会预先向所有员工解释？


否定句:
He did not preexplain the potential difficulties. = 他没有预先解释潜在的困难。
They didn't preexplain the complicated steps. = 他们没有预先解释那些复杂的步骤。
