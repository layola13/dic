denoising: /ˈdiːnɔɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 降噪；去噪 | The process of removing unwanted noise from a signal, image, or audio.  It aims to improve the quality and clarity of the data by suppressing or eliminating noise. | Example:  The image underwent denoising to enhance its details. 这张图片经过降噪处理以增强细节。  The audio engineer used advanced denoising techniques to clean up the recording. 音频工程师使用先进的降噪技术来清理录音。 | 近义词: noise reduction, noise suppression, filtering | 反义词: noise addition, noise amplification | 名词
