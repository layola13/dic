quadripennate:/ˌkwɑːdrɪˈpɛneɪt/| adj.|四羽的，具有四根羽枝的|Having four pinnae or leaflets.  This term is primarily used in botany to describe a leaf or leaflet with four primary divisions. |The quadripennate leaf of the fern was a remarkable sight.|近义词：multipinnate (if referring to many divisions)|反义词：unipinnate (if referring to a single division),bipinnate(if referring to two divisions)
