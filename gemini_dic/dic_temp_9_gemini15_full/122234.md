miswrite:/ˈmɪsraɪt/| vt.|写错，误写|To write something incorrectly.  |例句：I miswrote his name on the check. 我在支票上写错了他的名字。|例句：She miswrote the address, so the letter was returned. 她地址写错了，所以信被退回了。|近义词：err, mistake, misspell|反义词：write correctly, spell correctly
