consumes:/kənˈsuːmz/| vt.|消耗，消费，吞噬|to use fuel, energy, or time, especially in large amounts; to eat or drink something|例句：The car consumes a lot of fuel. 这辆车很费油。

例句：He consumes three meals a day. 他一天吃三顿饭。

近义词：devours, uses up, spends, exhausts |反义词：produces, creates, generates|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The engine consumes a lot of fuel. = 这台引擎消耗大量的燃料。
She consumes a large amount of coffee each day. = 她每天喝大量的咖啡。

一般过去时:
The fire consumed the entire forest. = 大火吞噬了整片森林。
He consumed a whole pizza in one sitting. = 他一口气吃掉了一个披萨。

现在完成时:
The drought has consumed the entire water supply. = 干旱已经耗尽了所有的水源。
We have consumed all the snacks. = 我们已经吃光了所有的零食。

过去完成时:
The factory had consumed tons of raw materials before the strike. = 罢工前，工厂已经消耗了数吨原材料。
They had consumed all the food before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经吃完了所有的食物。

将来时:
The project will consume a lot of time and resources. = 这个项目将耗费大量的时间和资源。
They will consume all the available resources before the end of the year. = 他们将在年底前消耗所有可用资源。

虚拟语气:
If the fire had consumed the house, we would have lost everything. = 如果大火烧毁了房子，我们将失去一切。
I wish I hadn't consumed so much chocolate yesterday. = 我真希望昨天我没有吃那么多巧克力。

被动语态:
A lot of energy is consumed by the city each day. = 城市每天消耗大量的能源。
The entire forest was consumed by the fire. = 整片森林被大火吞噬。

疑问句:
How much energy does your computer consume? = 你的电脑消耗多少能量？
What resources are consumed in the production process? = 生产过程中消耗了什么资源？

否定句:
The car doesn't consume much fuel. = 这辆车不太费油。
She doesn't consume sugary drinks anymore. = 她不再喝含糖饮料了。
