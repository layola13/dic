pawnable:/ˈpɔːnəbl/| adj.| 可典当的，可抵押的 | capable of being pawned; suitable for use as collateral.  |例句：My grandmother's antique jewelry is pawnable. 我祖母的古董首饰是可以典当的。

例句：He needed quick cash, so he pawned his pawnable watch. 他急需现金，所以他典当了他的手表。


近义词：pledgeable, mortgageable | 反义词：unpawned, unmortgageable


词源:  Derived from "pawn," meaning to deposit something as security for a loan.  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or suitability.  The word's origins trace back to Old French "pan," meaning "pledge" or "pawn," which ultimately derives from Latin "pignus" with the same meaning.
