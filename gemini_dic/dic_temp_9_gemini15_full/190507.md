satchels:/ˈsætʃəlz/| n. |小提包；书包|A satchel is a small bag, typically made of leather or fabric, carried on the shoulder or across the body.  It often has a strap and is used to carry books, personal items, or other small belongings. |例句：She carried her books in a brown leather satchel. 她用一个棕色的皮革小提包装着书。

例句：The student carried his satchel to school every day. 那个学生每天都背着他的书包去上学。


近义词：bag, pouch, knapsack, rucksack|反义词：None (no direct antonym)|词性：名词

词源:
The word "satchel" ultimately derives from the Old French word "sachet," meaning "small bag" or "pocket."  This, in turn, likely comes from the Latin word "saccus," meaning "sack" or "bag."  The word evolved through Middle English and Old French to arrive at its modern form.
