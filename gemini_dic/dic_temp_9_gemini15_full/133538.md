nonfiler:/ˈnɒnˌfaɪlər/| n. |未提交纳税申报者|A person who is not required to file a tax return, or who chooses not to file one.  This typically applies to individuals whose income falls below a certain threshold. |Example:  Many students are nonfilers because their income is too low to require filing a tax return. 许多学生因为收入太低而无需报税，所以是未提交纳税申报者。|近义词：non-filer (alternative spelling)|反义词：filer
