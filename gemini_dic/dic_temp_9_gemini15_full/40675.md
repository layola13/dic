conversation: /ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃən/ /ˌkɑːnvərˈseɪʃən/ | n. |谈话；会话|A talk between two or more people, especially an informal one.|例句：We had a long conversation about our plans for the future. 我们就未来的计划进行了长时间的谈话。|例句：The conversation drifted to the topic of politics. 谈话转向了政治话题。|近义词：talk, chat, dialogue, discussion|反义词：silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They have a conversation every day. = 他们每天都会聊天。
We often have conversations about work. = 我们经常谈论工作。


一般过去时:
We had a pleasant conversation last night. = 我们昨晚进行了愉快的谈话。
They had a heated conversation about the project. = 他们就项目进行了激烈的讨论。


现在完成时:
I have had several conversations with him about this. = 我已经和他就此谈过几次了。
She has had many conversations with her therapist. = 她已经和她的治疗师谈过很多次了。


过去完成时:
They had already had a conversation before the meeting. = 他们已经在会议前谈过了。
I had never had a conversation like that before. = 我以前从未有过这样的谈话。


将来时:
We will have a conversation tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上会谈谈。
They will have a serious conversation about the problem. = 他们会认真讨论这个问题。


虚拟语气:
I wish I had had a chance to have a conversation with her. = 我真希望有机会和她聊聊。
If I had known, I would have had a different conversation. = 如果我知道的话，我会进行一场不同的谈话。


被动语态:
The conversation was recorded. = 这段谈话被记录下来了。
The topic of the conversation was quickly changed. = 谈话的主题很快就被转移了。


疑问句:
What was the conversation about? = 谈话的内容是什么？
Did you have a good conversation with him? = 你和他谈话愉快吗？


否定句:
We didn't have a chance to have a conversation. = 我们没有机会进行谈话。
There was no conversation between them. = 他们之间没有谈话。


PHRASE->
engage in conversation = 进行谈话
start a conversation = 开始谈话
have a conversation = 进行谈话
overhear a conversation = 偷听到谈话
join a conversation = 加入谈话
