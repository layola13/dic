coloureds:/ˈkʌlədz/ | n. |有色人种，非白人 |A term used, primarily in South Africa, to refer to people of mixed race or non-white descent.  The term is now widely considered offensive due to its historical association with racial segregation and oppression. |  The government's policies towards the coloureds were highly controversial.  政府对有色人种的政策极具争议。|近义词：people of color (preferred), mixed-race people |反义词：whites
