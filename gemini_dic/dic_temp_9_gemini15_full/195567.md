semipreserved:/ˌsemiprɪˈzɜːvd/| adj.| 半保藏的，半保存的 | Partially preserved; not fully preserved but treated to extend shelf life.  Semipreserved foods often undergo processes like pasteurization or mild canning to prevent spoilage but don't have the extended shelf life of fully preserved foods. | Example:  The semipreserved pickles will last for a few weeks in the refrigerator.  这半保藏的泡菜放在冰箱里可以保存几周。|近义词：partially preserved, extended shelf life | 反义词：fully preserved, unpreserved
