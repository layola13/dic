predisposition:/ˌpriːdɪsˌpəˈzɪʃən/| n. |倾向；素质；易感性 |A predisposition is an inclination or tendency towards something, often a negative trait or condition.  It suggests an increased likelihood or susceptibility, but not a certainty.  It can be genetic, environmental, or a combination of both. | 例句：He has a predisposition to heart disease due to family history. 他由于家族遗传史而有患心脏病的倾向。  She has a predisposition for artistic expression. 她有艺术表达的倾向。|近义词：tendency, inclination, susceptibility, proneness |反义词：resistance, immunity
