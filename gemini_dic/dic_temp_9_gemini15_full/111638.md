locally:/ˈloʊkəli/| adv.|局部地，本地地，当地地|In a local area or place; within a particular region or community.  Locally sourced ingredients are used in the restaurant.|例句：这家餐馆使用当地采购的食材。 The problem is being addressed locally. |例句：这个问题正在当地得到解决。|近义词：regionally, domestically, nationally |反义词：globally, internationally, widely


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store sources its produce locally. = 这家商店的农产品来自本地。
They sell locally made crafts. = 他们销售本地制作的手工艺品。


一般过去时:
The festival was celebrated locally last year. = 去年这个节日是在当地庆祝的。
We shopped locally when we visited the town. = 我们参观小镇时在当地购物。


现在完成时:
The company has expanded its business locally. = 这家公司已在当地扩展业务。
They have sourced materials locally for many years. = 他们多年来一直从当地采购材料。


过去完成时:
They had already established a strong local presence before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们已经在当地建立了强大的影响力。
The project had been largely funded locally. = 该项目主要由当地资助。


将来时:
We will source our products locally in the future. = 我们将来将在当地采购产品。
The government will invest more in locally owned businesses. = 政府将对当地企业进行更多投资。


虚拟语气:
If we marketed locally, we might see increased sales. = 如果我们在当地进行营销，可能会看到销售额增长。
It would be better if we sourced our ingredients locally. = 如果我们从当地采购原料会更好。


被动语态:
The products are manufactured locally. = 这些产品是在当地制造的。
The event is being promoted locally. = 这次活动正在当地推广。


疑问句:
Are the ingredients sourced locally? = 配料是当地采购的吗？
Is the product made locally? = 这个产品是当地制造的吗？


否定句:
The company doesn't source its materials locally. = 这家公司不从当地采购材料。
They didn't sell their products locally. = 他们没有在当地销售他们的产品。
