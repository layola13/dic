intervene:/ˌɪntərˈviːn/| v.|干预，介入，调停|To come between disputing people, groups, or things; to interfere.  It implies taking action to influence a situation, often to prevent a negative outcome or to resolve a conflict.|例句：The government intervened to prevent a strike. 政府介入以防止罢工。|例句：She intervened in the argument to try to calm things down. 她介入争论以试图平息事态。|近义词：interfere, mediate, arbitrate|反义词：ignore, neglect, abstain


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The mediator intervenes in disputes to find solutions. 调解员介入纠纷以寻找解决方案。
He often intervenes when his children fight. 他经常在他孩子们打架时进行干预。


一般过去时:
The army intervened to restore order. 军队介入以恢复秩序。
She intervened on behalf of the refugees. 她为难民进行了干预。


现在完成时:
The UN has intervened in several conflicts this year. 联合国今年已介入过几次冲突。
He has intervened several times to help his colleagues. 他已经多次介入帮助他的同事。


过去完成时:
Before the police intervened, the situation had become very violent. 在警方介入之前，情况已经变得非常暴力。
They had already intervened several times before the final decision was made. 在最终决定做出之前，他们已经介入过几次了。


将来时:
The government will intervene if the economy worsens. 如果经济恶化，政府将进行干预。
I will intervene if necessary. 如果必要的话，我会介入。


虚拟语气:
If I had intervened earlier, things might have been different. 如果我早点介入，情况可能会有所不同。
It would have been better if he had not intervened. 如果他没有介入，那就好了。


被动语态:
The dispute was intervened by the union. 纠纷由工会进行了干预。
The situation was intervened in by the authorities. 局势受到了当局的干预。


疑问句:
Will the international community intervene? 国际社会会干预吗？
Did anyone intervene to stop the fight? 有人介入阻止这场争斗吗？


否定句:
The government did not intervene in the crisis. 政府没有介入这场危机。
They chose not to intervene in the family matter. 他们选择不干预家庭事务。


PHRASE->

intervene in: 介入...
intervene on behalf of: 代表...进行干预
intervene to: 为了...而干预
intervene with: 对...进行干预
successful intervention: 成功干预
