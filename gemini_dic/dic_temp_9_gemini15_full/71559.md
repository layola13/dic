flashcard:/ˈflæʃkɑːrd/| n. |抽认卡，索引卡 |A flashcard is a card bearing information on one side, used for memorization.  They are often used for learning vocabulary, facts, or other information. |例句：She used flashcards to learn the French vocabulary. 她用抽认卡学习法语词汇。

例句：The teacher distributed flashcards to the students for a quick review.老师分发了抽认卡给学生们快速复习。


近义词：index card, study card |反义词：无
