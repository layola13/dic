conveyors:/kənˈveɪərz/ | n. |传送带，输送机 | A conveyor is a mechanical device for moving materials, such as packages, from one place to another.  | Example: The factory uses conveyors to move the finished products to the packaging area.  工厂使用传送带将成品运送到包装区。  The airport uses a conveyor belt system to move luggage to the baggage claim. 机场使用传送带系统将行李运送到行李提取处。|近义词：transporter, belt, carrier |反义词：None
