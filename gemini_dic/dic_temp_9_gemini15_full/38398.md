conceptualize:/kənˈsɛptʃuəlaɪz/ | vt. |概念化，构想 | To form a concept or idea of something; to imagine or visualize something.  | Example:  The artist conceptualized a new sculpture using recycled materials.  艺术家用回收材料构想了一个新的雕塑。  |近义词：envision, imagine, visualize, formulate | 反义词：misunderstand, misconceive | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conceptualizes new marketing strategies every week. 他每周都会构想新的营销策略。
She conceptualizes her artwork through dreams and emotions. 她通过梦想和情感来构想她的艺术作品。

一般过去时:
They conceptualized the project during a brainstorming session. 他们在头脑风暴会议期间构想了这个项目。
The architect conceptualized a futuristic building design. 建筑师构想了一种未来主义的建筑设计。

现在完成时:
We have conceptualized several potential solutions to the problem. 我们已经构想出几个可能的解决方案。
She has conceptualized a groundbreaking theory in physics. 她已经构想出一个物理学领域的突破性理论。

过去完成时:
They had conceptualized the plan before the meeting. 他们在会议之前就已经构想好了计划。
The team had conceptualized a new product line by the end of the year.  团队在年底前已经构想出一条新的产品线。

将来时:
They will conceptualize a new campaign for next year's product launch. 他们将为明年的产品发布构想一个新的宣传活动。
She will conceptualize a new approach to teaching math. 她将构想一种新的数学教学方法。

虚拟语气:
If I could conceptualize a better solution, I would. 如果我能构想出一个更好的解决方案，我会的。
I wish I could conceptualize a perfect world. 我希望我能构想出一个完美的世界。

被动语态:
The new strategy was conceptualized by the marketing team.  新的策略是由营销团队构想的。
The building design was conceptualized with sustainability in mind. 建筑设计是在考虑可持续性的前提下构想的。

疑问句:
How did they conceptualize such a complex system? 他们是如何构想出如此复杂的系统？
Have you conceptualized a solution to this problem yet? 你已经构想出这个问题的解决方案了吗？

否定句:
He could not conceptualize the enormity of the task. 他无法构想出这项任务的巨大性。
They did not conceptualize the potential risks involved. 他们没有构想出潜在的风险。


PHRASE->
conceptualize a plan 制定计划
conceptualize a design 设计构想
conceptualize a strategy 制定策略
conceptualize a solution 构想解决方案
conceptualize a theory 构想理论

词源:
From the verb "conceive" (to form in the mind; imagine) + "-alize" (to make or become).  Ultimately derived from Latin *concipere ("to take hold of, seize, grasp").
