razzes:/ˈræzɪz/| v.| 嘲笑；嘘；斥责 | To tease or ridicule someone; to express strong disapproval with loud noises. |例句：The audience razzed the comedian for his poor jokes.  观众因为他的笑话很糟糕而嘲笑他。|例句：The hecklers razzed the speaker throughout his speech.  那些捣乱的人在演讲者整个演讲过程中都在嘘他。|近义词：mock, jeer, heckle, taunt |反义词：praise, applaud
