melanin:/ˈmɛlənɪn/| n. |黑色素|A dark brown to black pigment occurring in the hair, skin, and iris of the eye in people and animals. It is responsible for the protection of the skin from the harmful effects of ultraviolet radiation.|The melanin in her skin protected her from the strong sun.|近义词：pigment|反义词： (缺乏黑色素) albinism
