embroider:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdər/ | vt. | 刺绣；装饰；润色 | To decorate (cloth or other material) with needlework; to add details to (a story, account, etc.) to make it more interesting. | 例句：She embroidered a beautiful flower on the dress. 她在一件衣服上绣了一朵漂亮的花。  She embroidered the story with fanciful details. 她用奇特的细节润色了这个故事。| 近义词：decorate, embellish, adorn | 反义词：simplify, strip
