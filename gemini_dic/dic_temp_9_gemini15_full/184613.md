restrainer:/rɪˈstreɪnər/ | n. | 抑制物；约束物；抑制者 | A person or thing that restrains something.  Something that limits or controls something else. | Example: The seatbelt acted as a restrainer in the car crash.  安全带在车祸中起到了约束作用。

The police used pepper spray as a restrainer to subdue the suspect.警方使用胡椒喷雾作为约束物制服嫌疑人。|近义词：constraint, restraint, check, curb, control |反义词：accelerator, stimulant, impetus
