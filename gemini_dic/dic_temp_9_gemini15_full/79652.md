glossography:/ɡlɒˈsɒɡrəfi/| n. |词汇表；术语汇编；图解词典|A glossography is a written collection of glosses, often including explanations and illustrations. It's a specialized dictionary or lexicon focusing on a particular field or subject, often providing concise definitions and visual aids.  |Example: The archaeologist compiled a detailed glossography of ancient Sumerian cuneiform script, including images of the tablets and their translations. |近义词：lexicon, glossary, terminology, dictionary|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to its opposite—a work lacking definitions or explanations.|名词
