skateboarding:/ˈskeɪtˌbɔːrdɪŋ/| n. |滑板运动|The activity of riding on a skateboard.|例句：He enjoys skateboarding in the park. 他喜欢在公园里玩滑板。|近义词：skating, board riding|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He skateboards every weekend. = 他每个周末都玩滑板。
She skateboards with her friends. = 她和朋友们一起玩滑板。

一般过去时:
I skateboarded in the street when I was young. = 我年轻的时候在街上玩滑板。
They skateboarded down the hill yesterday. = 他们昨天从山上滑板下来。

现在完成时:
He has skateboarded since he was a child. = 他从孩提时代就开始玩滑板了。
She has never skateboarded before. = 她以前从未玩过滑板。

过去完成时:
I had skateboarded for five years before I broke my leg. = 我玩滑板五年后才摔断了腿。
They had never skateboarded on such a steep hill. = 他们从未在如此陡峭的山坡上玩过滑板。

将来时:
We will skateboard at the beach tomorrow. = 我们明天将在海滩上玩滑板。
They will be skateboarding in the competition next week. = 他们下周将参加滑板比赛。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would skateboard more often. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常地玩滑板。
I wish I could skateboard as well as him. = 我希望我能像他一样滑板滑得好。

被动语态:
The skateboard was broken during the competition. = 滑板在比赛中坏了。
The park is being used for skateboarding. = 公园正在被用来玩滑板。

疑问句:

否定句:
