imperiousness:/ɪmˈpɪəriəsnəs/| n. |专横；傲慢；盛气凌人|The feeling of superiority and entitlement that leads one to domineer over others; an overbearing manner. It often manifests as a disregard for the feelings or opinions of others.  |例句：Her imperiousness alienated many of her colleagues.  她的盛气凌人使许多同事疏远了她。


例句：The imperiousness of the manager created a hostile work environment.  经理的专横跋扈造成了一个充满敌意的工作环境。


近义词：haughtiness, arrogance, domineeringness, high-handedness, overbearingness


反义词：humility, meekness, deference, submissiveness


词源:  Derived from the adjective "imperious," which comes from the Latin word "imperiosus," meaning "commanding," "ruling," or "powerful."  This in turn originates from "imperium," meaning "command," "power," or "empire."
