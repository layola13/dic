lay eyes on: /leɪ aɪz ɒn/ | phrasal verb | 看见；看到 | To see something or someone for the first time, or after a long time.  |例句：I finally laid eyes on the painting I'd been longing to see. 我终于看到了我一直渴望看到的画作。

例句：It wasn't until I arrived in Paris that I laid eyes on the Eiffel Tower. 直到我到达巴黎，我才看到埃菲尔铁塔。


近义词：see, catch sight of, glimpse, behold


反义词：miss, overlook


词性: phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I lay eyes on my lost cat every day. = 我每天都能看到我丢失的猫。
They lay eyes on a beautiful sunset. = 他们看到了美丽的日落。

一般过去时:
I laid eyes on her for the first time yesterday. = 我昨天第一次见到她。
We laid eyes on a rare bird during our hike. = 我们在徒步旅行时看到了一种稀有的鸟。

现在完成时:
I have laid eyes on many amazing things in my travels. = 在我的旅行中，我见过许多令人惊奇的事物。
She has laid eyes on the ocean for the first time in her life. = 她一生中第一次看到了海洋。

过去完成时:
I had never laid eyes on such a magnificent building before. = 我以前从未见过如此宏伟的建筑。
They had laid eyes on the suspect several times before the arrest. = 在逮捕之前，他们已经几次见过嫌疑人。

将来时:
I will lay eyes on my friend again soon. = 我很快就会再次见到我的朋友。
We will lay eyes on the new product at the trade show. = 我们将在贸易展览会上看到新产品。

虚拟语气:
If I laid eyes on a ghost, I would scream. = 如果我看到鬼，我会尖叫。
I wish I could lay eyes on him one last time. = 我希望我还能最后一次见到他。

被动语态: (This phrasal verb is rarely used in the passive voice)


疑问句:
Have you ever laid eyes on a real-life unicorn? = 你曾经见过真正的独角兽吗？
When did you first lay eyes on your spouse? = 你是什么时候第一次见到你的配偶的？

否定句:
I haven't laid eyes on him in years. = 我好几年没见过他了。
They never laid eyes on the stolen jewelry. = 他们从未见过被盗的珠宝。


PHRASE->
lay eyes on someone/something = 看见某人/某物
to lay eyes on a beautiful sight = 看到美丽的景色
to lay eyes on a rare animal = 看到稀有动物
to lay eyes on a long-lost friend = 见到失散多年的朋友
to lay eyes on a masterpiece = 看到杰作
