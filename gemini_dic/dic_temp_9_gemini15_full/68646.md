far-reaching: /ˌfɑːrˈriːtʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 深远的；影响广泛的 |  Having a wide range or effect.  It suggests consequences or influence that extend over a large area or a long period. |

例句：

The effects of the new law are far-reaching. = 这项新法律的影响深远。

His research has had a far-reaching impact on the field. = 他的研究对该领域产生了深远的影响。


近义词：extensive, widespread, comprehensive, sweeping, profound

反义词：limited, restricted, narrow, insignificant, superficial

词性：形容词
