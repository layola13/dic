delegated:/ˈdɛlɪɡeɪtɪd/| adj. | 被委托的；被授权的 |  Past participle of "delegate".  It describes something that has been entrusted to someone else.  | Example: The task was delegated to the junior staff.  这项任务被委托给了初级员工。  |近义词：assigned, entrusted, committed | 反义词：reserved, retained, kept | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The work is delegated to the team. = 工作被委托给团队。
The responsibilities are delegated fairly. = 责任被公平地分配。


一般过去时:
The project was delegated to a new manager. = 项目被委托给一位新的经理。
The authority was delegated to the committee. = 权力被委托给了委员会。


现在完成时:
The tasks have been delegated effectively. = 任务已被有效地委托。
The responsibilities have been delegated to different departments. = 责任已被委托给不同的部门。


过去完成时:
The decision had been delegated before the meeting. = 决定在会议之前已被委托。
The power had been delegated to the vice president. = 权力已被委托给了副总裁。


将来时:
The project will be delegated to a consultant. = 项目将被委托给一位顾问。
The work will be delegated to the most experienced member. = 工作将被委托给经验最丰富的成员。


虚拟语气:
If the task were delegated properly, it would be completed on time. = 如果任务被妥善委托，它就能按时完成。
I wish the responsibility had been delegated more efficiently. = 我希望责任能够更有效地被委托。


被动语态:
The tasks were delegated by the project manager.  = 任务是由项目经理委托的。
The responsibilities are being delegated to suitable candidates. = 责任正在被委托给合适的候选人。


疑问句:
Has the task been delegated yet? = 任务已经委托了吗？
To whom was the project delegated? = 项目委托给了谁？


否定句:
The task was not delegated to the right person. = 任务没有委托给合适的人。
The work has not yet been delegated. = 工作尚未委托。


PHRASE->

delegated powers: 授权
delegated legislation: 授权立法
delegated responsibility: 委托责任
delegated tasks: 委托任务
