exobiology:/ˌɛksoʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi/ | n. | 外星生物学 | The branch of biology concerned with the origin, evolution, and distribution of life beyond Earth.  It explores the possibility of extraterrestrial life and investigates the conditions that might support it. | Example:  Exobiology research often focuses on extremophiles, organisms that thrive in extreme environments on Earth, as they may offer clues to the potential for life on other planets.  外星生物学研究通常关注嗜极生物，即在地球极端环境中繁荣发展的生物，因为它们可能为其他星球上的生命潜力提供线索。|近义词：astrobiology |反义词：terrestrial biology
