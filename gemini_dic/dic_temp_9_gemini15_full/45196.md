culet:/kjuːˈlet/| n. |亭，琢磨后的宝石的底尖|The small, flat surface at the bottom of a cut gemstone.  It is the last facet to be cut and is usually quite small.  A well-cut culet helps to enhance the brilliance of the stone. |例句：The diamond's culet was perfectly polished. 这颗钻石的亭子被完美地抛光了。|The culet on this ruby is slightly uneven. 这颗红宝石的亭子略有不平。|近义词：base, bottom (in this context)|反义词：pavilion (the top of a gemstone)
