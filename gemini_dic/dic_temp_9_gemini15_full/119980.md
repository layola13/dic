millers:/ˈmɪlərz/| n.|磨坊主；磨坊工人；（复数）磨坊主家族|People who operate or own a mill, especially a flour mill.  The term can also refer to a family that has been involved in milling for generations.|例句：The millers were known throughout the valley for their excellent flour. 谷地里的人们都知道这些磨坊主生产的优质面粉。|例句：The old millers' house stood on a hill overlooking the river. 老磨坊主的房子坐落在俯瞰着河流的山丘上。|近义词：miller (单数), millworkers, flour makers|反义词：N/A
