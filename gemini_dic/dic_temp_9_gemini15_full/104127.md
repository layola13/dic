The word often describes a person's behavior or a situation's atmosphere.|例句：The dog was jumpy after the fireworks.  那只狗放完烟花后很紧张不安。|例句：The atmosphere in the room was jumpy after the announcement.  宣布之后房间里的气氛很紧张。|近义词：nervous, restless, skittish, edgy, fidgety|反义词：calm, relaxed, tranquil, composed
