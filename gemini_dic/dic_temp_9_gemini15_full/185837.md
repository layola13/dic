revival:/rɪˈvaɪvəl/| n. v.| 复兴；复活；苏醒；复苏；重新流行 |The act or process of reviving something.  It can refer to the renewed interest or activity in something, or the restoration of something to life or consciousness. |例句：The city is experiencing a revival of its arts scene.  这座城市正在经历其艺术领域的复兴。

The play received a revival on Broadway. 这部戏在百老汇复排了。


近义词：renewal, resurgence, rebirth, restoration |反义词：decline, decay, death, extinction |名词,动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The city experiences a cultural revival. = 这座城市正在经历文化复兴。
This band revives classic rock songs. = 这个乐队翻唱经典摇滚歌曲。


一般过去时:
The old tradition revived last year. = 去年旧传统复兴了。
The doctor revived the unconscious patient. = 医生使昏迷的病人苏醒过来。


现在完成时:
The town has revived its economy. = 这个城镇已经复兴了它的经济。
The company has revived its marketing strategy. = 公司已经调整了它的营销策略。


过去完成时:
They had revived the old factory before the new investment. = 在新的投资之前，他们已经恢复了旧工厂的运转。
The government had revived the national spirit after the war. = 战争之后，政府已经重新鼓舞了民族精神。


将来时:
The project will revive the local economy. = 这个项目将复兴当地经济。
They will revive the old play for the anniversary. = 他们将为周年纪念演出复排这部老戏。


虚拟语气:
If they revived the old building, it would attract tourists. = 如果他们修复了旧建筑，它将会吸引游客。
I wish they would revive the old tradition. = 我希望他们能恢复旧传统。


被动语态:
The old building was revived by the city council. = 旧建筑被市议会修复了。
The patient was revived by the paramedics. = 病人被医护人员救活了。


疑问句:
Will the company revive its product line? = 公司会恢复它的产品线吗？
Can this treatment revive the dying plant? = 这种疗法能使快死的植物复苏吗？


否定句:
They did not revive the old project. = 他们没有恢复旧项目。
The doctor could not revive the patient. = 医生没能使病人苏醒过来。
