nonrealists:/ˌnɒnˈriːəlɪsts/ | n. |非现实主义者；反现实主义者 |Those who reject or oppose realism, especially in art, politics, or philosophy.  They believe that reality is subjective or constructed rather than objective and independent of the observer.  |Example:  Many nonrealists in the art world champion abstract expressionism.  许多艺术界的非现实主义者支持抽象表现主义。  |近义词：idealists, antirealists |反义词：realists |名词
