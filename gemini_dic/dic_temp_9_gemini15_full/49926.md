denaturalize:/ˌdiːnætʃərəlaɪz/| vt.|取消国籍；使非自然化；使不自然|To deprive someone of their nationality.  This can be done by a government for various reasons, often involving accusations of disloyalty or fraud in the acquisition of citizenship.|例句：The government denaturalized him for lying on his citizenship application.  政府因他在公民申请表上撒谎而取消了他的国籍。|例句：The harsh chemicals denaturalized the fibers, making the fabric weak and brittle.  强烈的化学物质使纤维失去自然特性，使织物变弱变脆。|近义词：deprive of citizenship, expatriate|反义词：naturalize
