company:/ˈkʌmpəni/| n. |公司，企业，陪伴，同伴 |A company is an organization that sells goods or services.  A company can also refer to the people you are with. |例句：He works for a large technology company. 他在一间大型科技公司工作。|例句：I enjoyed the company of my friends. 我喜欢和朋友在一起。|近义词：firm, corporation, enterprise, association, fellowship |反义词：solitude, isolation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company manufactures cars. = 这家公司生产汽车。
Does the company offer health insurance? = 这家公司提供医疗保险吗？


一般过去时:
The company launched a new product last year. = 这家公司去年推出了一款新产品。
Did the company make a profit last quarter? = 上个季度这家公司盈利了吗？


现在完成时:
The company has expanded its operations. = 这家公司已经扩大了业务。
Has the company ever faced a major crisis? = 这家公司曾经面临过重大危机吗？


过去完成时:
The company had already filed for bankruptcy before the merger. = 在合并之前，该公司已经申请破产。
Had the company considered other options before making this decision? = 在做出这个决定之前，该公司是否考虑过其他方案？


将来时:
The company will open a new office next month. = 这家公司下个月将开设新办公室。
Will the company be able to meet its targets? = 这家公司能够实现其目标吗？


虚拟语气:
If the company were to merge with its competitor, it would become the market leader. = 如果这家公司与竞争对手合并，它将成为市场领导者。
I wish the company had invested more in research and development. = 我希望该公司在研发方面投入更多资金。


被动语态:
The product was manufactured by the company. = 该产品是由该公司生产的。
The company's new strategy is being implemented. = 该公司的新的战略正在实施中。


疑问句:
What company do you work for? = 你在哪家公司工作？
Which company offers the best price? = 哪家公司提供最优惠的价格？


否定句:
The company does not offer discounts. = 这家公司不提供折扣。
The company didn't respond to our inquiry. = 这家公司没有回复我们的询问。
