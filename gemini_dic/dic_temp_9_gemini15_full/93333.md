ill-used:/ɪlˈjuːzd/ | adj. | 被虐待的；被滥用的；被糟蹋的 |  Describes something or someone that has been treated badly, unfairly, or unkindly; often implies neglect or exploitation.  | Example: The ill-used horse was thin and weak.  这匹被虐待的马又瘦又弱。  The ill-used equipment was beyond repair.  被滥用的设备无法修理。|近义词：abused, mistreated, neglected, exploited | 反义词：well-treated, cherished, cared for | 词性：形容词


USAGE->

The old tools are ill-used. = 这些旧工具被滥用了。
This system is ill-used by many people. = 很多人滥用这个系统。


一般过去时:
The dog was ill-used by its previous owner. = 这条狗以前被主人虐待过。
