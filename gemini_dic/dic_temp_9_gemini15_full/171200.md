psychopaths:/ˈsaɪkəpæθs/ | n. |精神病患者，反社会人格者 | A psychopath is a person suffering from a personality disorder characterized by antisocial behavior, lack of empathy, and disregard for the feelings of others.  They may be superficially charming but are manipulative and often commit crimes. | 例句：The serial killer was diagnosed as a psychopath. 这个连环杀手被诊断为精神病患者。 | 例句：His psychopathic tendencies made him a dangerous individual. 他的反社会倾向使他成为一个危险人物。| 近义词：sociopath, antisocial personality,  | 反义词：empathic, compassionate, altruistic | 名词
