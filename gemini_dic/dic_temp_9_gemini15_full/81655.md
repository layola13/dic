green:/ɡriːn/| adj. n.|绿色的；绿色植物|The word "green" derives from the Old English word "grēne," meaning "green, fresh."  It's related to other Germanic words with similar meanings.  As an adjective, it describes the color green. As a noun, it refers to the color itself or green plants, foliage, or grassy areas. |例句：The grass is green.  草是绿色的。

The leaves are turning green. 叶子正在变绿。
