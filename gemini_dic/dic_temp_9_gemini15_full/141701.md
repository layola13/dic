or:/ɔːr/| conj.|或者，要么，还是|A conjunction used to connect alternatives.|  I'll have tea or coffee. 我要喝茶或咖啡。|近义词:either, otherwise|反义词:and


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eats rice or noodles for lunch. 他午餐吃米饭或面条。
She drinks tea or juice. 她喝茶或果汁。


一般过去时:
They went to the park or the beach. 他们去了公园或海滩。
He played football or basketball. 他踢足球或打篮球。


现在完成时:
I have finished my homework or my project. 我已经完成了我的家庭作业或我的项目。
She has read the book or watched the movie. 她已经读了这本书或看了这部电影。


过去完成时:
He had eaten lunch or dinner before he left. 他离开前已经吃过午饭或晚饭。
She had visited her parents or her friends. 她已经拜访了她的父母或她的朋友们。


将来时:
We will go to the cinema or stay at home. 我们将去看电影或待在家里。
They will study English or French. 他们将学习英语或法语。


虚拟语气:
I would prefer tea or coffee. 我更喜欢茶或咖啡。
If it rains, we will stay home or go to the library. 如果下雨，我们将待在家里或去图书馆。


被动语态:
The cake was made with chocolate or vanilla. 蛋糕是用巧克力或香草做的。
The decision will be made by the committee or the manager.  决定将由委员会或经理做出。


疑问句:
Would you like tea or coffee? 你想喝茶还是咖啡？
Do you prefer to go to the beach or the mountains? 你更喜欢去海滩还是去山上？


否定句:
He doesn't like coffee or tea. 他不喜欢咖啡也不喜欢茶。
She didn't go to the party or the concert. 她没有去派对也没有去音乐会。


PHRASE->
or else:否则
or so:大约
one or two:一两个
more or less:或多或少
as or than:如同或胜于
