odour:/ˈoʊdər/ /ˈəʊdə(r)/| n. |气味，臭味 |A smell, especially one that is unpleasant.  |The odour of stale cigarettes filled the room.  这间屋子充满了陈旧香烟的气味。|smell, scent, fragrance |fresh air |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cheese has a strong odour. = 这奶酪有很浓的气味。
This flower emits a pleasant odour. = 这朵花散发着宜人的香味。


一般过去时:
The room retained a lingering odour of paint. = 屋子里还残留着油漆的余味。
The garbage produced a foul odour. = 垃圾散发出一股恶臭。


现在完成时:
The incident has left a bad odour on my reputation. = 这件事给我的名声留下了污点。
We have noticed a strange odour coming from the basement. = 我们注意到地下室传来一股奇怪的气味。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
