hut-like:/ˈhʌtˌlaɪk/| adj.|像小屋的，小屋似的|Resembling a hut in appearance or structure.  It suggests a small, simple, and often rudimentary dwelling.|例句：The small, hut-like structure was barely visible amidst the dense foliage.  这间小屋似的建筑物在茂密的树叶中几乎不可见。

The child built a hut-like fort out of blankets and chairs. 孩子用毯子和椅子搭建了一个像小屋一样的堡垒。|近义词：cabin-like, shack-like, makeshift, rudimentary|反义词：palatial, grand, luxurious
