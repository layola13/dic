retraining:/ˌriːˈtreɪnɪŋ/| n. |再培训，职前培训 |The process of teaching someone new skills so they can do a different job.  It often involves adults changing careers or updating their existing skills.| 例句：The company offered retraining opportunities to its employees affected by the restructuring.  公司为受重组影响的员工提供了再培训机会。

The government is investing heavily in retraining programs for unemployed workers. 政府正在大力投资失业工人的再培训项目。|近义词：reskilling, upskilling, further training |反义词：layoff, dismissal
