overroasted:/ˌoʊvərˈroʊstɪd/| adj. |烤焦的，烤过头了的|  Describes food that has been cooked for too long in an oven or other heat source, resulting in a dry, hard, and potentially burnt texture.  |The chicken was overroasted and quite dry.  烤鸡烤过头了，非常干。|  burnt, scorched, charred | undercooked, rare |形容词
