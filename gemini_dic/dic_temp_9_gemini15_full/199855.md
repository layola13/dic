sitter:/ˈsɪtər/| n.|保姆；照看者；（尤指）临时照看孩子的人|A sitter is a person who sits and looks after someone or something, especially a child.  It often refers to someone who temporarily cares for children while their parents are away.|例句：She found a reliable sitter for her children. 她为她的孩子们找到了一位可靠的保姆。|例句： We need a sitter for tonight's party. 我们今晚的派对需要一位保姆。|近义词：babysitter, caregiver, guardian|反义词：N/A|名词
