quantify:/ˈkwɑːntɪfaɪ/ /ˈkwɒntɪfaɪ/ | vt. |量化；确定…的数量 |To express or measure the quantity of something.|The researchers tried to quantify the impact of the new policy. 研究人员试图量化新政策的影响。  |measure, determine, assess |qualify, describe |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Scientists quantify data using various statistical methods. 科学家们使用各种统计方法对数据进行量化。
We quantify the risk before making any investment decisions. 我们在做任何投资决定之前都会量化风险。


一般过去时:
The study quantified the effects of pollution on air quality. 这项研究量化了污染对空气质量的影响。
They quantified the amount of rainfall in the region. 他们量化了该地区的降雨量。


现在完成时:
Researchers have quantified the relationship between diet and disease. 研究人员已经量化了饮食与疾病之间的关系。
The team has quantified the impact of climate change on sea levels.  研究小组已经量化了气候变化对海平面的影响。


过去完成时:
Before the experiment, they had quantified the initial conditions.  实验之前，他们已经量化了初始条件。
The scientists had quantified the amount of pollutants in the river before the cleanup. 科学家们在清理之前已经量化了河里的污染物数量。


将来时:
We will quantify the results of the experiment next week. 我们下周将对实验结果进行量化。
The company will quantify its carbon footprint by the end of the year. 公司将在年底前量化其碳足迹。


虚拟语气:
If we could quantify happiness, we could measure its impact on well-being. 如果我们能够量化幸福，我们就能衡量它对福祉的影响。
It is important that we quantify the risks involved.  重要的是，我们必须量化所涉及的风险。


被动语态:
The data was quantified using a complex algorithm. 数据是使用复杂的算法进行量化的。
The impact of the drug has been quantified in several clinical trials.  这种药物的影响已经在几项临床试验中得到量化。


疑问句:
How can we quantify the intangible benefits of education? 我们如何量化教育的无形益处？
Can we accurately quantify the long-term effects of this policy? 我们能否准确地量化这项政策的长期影响？


否定句:
We cannot quantify the exact number of participants. 我们无法量化参与者的确切人数。
The study failed to quantify the impact of the intervention. 这项研究未能量化干预措施的影响。
