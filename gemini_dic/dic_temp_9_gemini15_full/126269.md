myelomata:/ˌmaɪəˈloʊmətə/| n. |骨髓瘤（复数）|Multiple myeloma, also known as myelomatosis, is a cancer of plasma cells found in the bone marrow.  It's characterized by the proliferation of abnormal plasma cells that produce excessive amounts of a single type of antibody.  The excess antibodies can cause damage to various organs and tissues. |The patient was diagnosed with myelomata and began chemotherapy immediately.|近义词：myelomatosis|反义词：无
