It's involved in flexing, abducting, and laterally rotating the hip, as well as flexing the knee. |The sartorius muscle is responsible for the characteristic cross-legged sitting position. |近义词: (no direct synonyms, as it refers to a specific muscle) |反义词: (no direct antonyms)
