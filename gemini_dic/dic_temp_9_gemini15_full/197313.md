shatters:/ˈʃætərz/ | vt. | 粉碎；打破；使破灭 | To break something into many small pieces; to destroy something completely or suddenly; to severely damage or undermine something. | 例句：The earthquake shattered the building. 地震摧毁了那座建筑物。

The news shattered his hopes. 这个消息粉碎了他的希望。


近义词：smash, crush, destroy, ruin, break | 反义词：mend, repair, build, construct | 动词


USAGE->
