denomination:/ˌdɛnəˈmeɪʃən/| n. |教派；币值；名称；命名|The act of naming or designating; the name or designation given; a class or group having a common name.  It can also refer to the face value of a coin or banknote. |例句：The church is divided into several denominations.  这个教会分成好几个教派。

例句：The denomination of this bill is ten dollars. 这张钞票的面值是十美元。

近义词：sect, sect, name, designation, value

反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be applied depending on the context (e.g., "unity" as opposed to "denominations" in a religious context).

词性:Noun
