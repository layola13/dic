headhunt:/ˈhɛdˌhʌnt/| vt.|猎头,物色人才|To actively seek out and recruit someone for a job, especially a senior position, often by approaching them directly.|例句：The company headhunted him from a rival firm.  这家公司从竞争对手公司挖走了他。|例句：They're headhunting for a new CEO. 他们正在物色一位新的首席执行官。|近义词：recruit, poach, solicit|反义词：lay off, fire


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They headhunt promising young graduates every year. = 他们每年都物色有前途的年轻毕业生。
The company headhunts executives from competing firms. = 这家公司从竞争公司挖角高管。


一般过去时:
