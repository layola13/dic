cofounded:/koʊˈfaʊndɪd/| v.|共同创立，共同创建|To establish or create something, such as a company or organization, together with others.|例句：They cofounded a successful tech startup. 他们共同创立了一家成功的科技创业公司。|近义词：established, founded, initiated|反义词：destroyed, dissolved


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cofound many successful businesses. = 他们共同创立了许多成功的企业。
We cofound a non-profit organization to help the homeless. = 我们共同创立了一个非营利组织来帮助无家可归的人。

一般过去时:
They cofounded the company in 2010. = 他们在2010年共同创立了这家公司。
