contrastingly:/kənˈtræstɪŋli/ | adv. | 对比地；相反地 | In a way that shows a noticeable difference.  It emphasizes the difference between two things or situations. |例句：Contrastingly, the second experiment yielded entirely different results. 对比之下，第二个实验产生了完全不同的结果。

例句：The first painting was vibrant and colorful; contrastingly, the second was muted and somber. 第一幅画色彩鲜艳；相反，第二幅画则色调暗淡。


近义词：conversely, conversely, in contrast, by contrast, on the other hand


反义词：similarly, likewise, correspondingly


词性: 副词
