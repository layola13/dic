navarch:/ˈneɪvɑːrk/| n. |海军元帅，海军上将|A navarch is a high-ranking naval officer, typically equivalent to an admiral.  The term is less common in modern usage but has historical significance, particularly in ancient Greece.|Example:  The navarch commanded a powerful fleet.|近义词：admiral, fleet admiral|反义词：private (as in a private sailor), seaman
