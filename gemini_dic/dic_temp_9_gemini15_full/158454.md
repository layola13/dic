plectognath:/ˈplektəɡnæθ/| n. |硬骨鱼类(一类鱼)|A plectognath is a fish belonging to the order Plectognathi, characterized by fused or reduced bones in their jaws and a generally robust body structure.  These fish often possess unique defensive mechanisms, such as spines, inflatable bodies, or potent toxins.|Example:  The pufferfish is a well-known example of a plectognath.  河豚是一种众所周知的硬骨鱼类。|近义词:  (none precisely equivalent,  needs to be described by characteristics like pufferfish, boxfish, etc.)|反义词: (none)|名词
