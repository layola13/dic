sequencer:/ˈsiːkwɛnsər/| n. | 测序仪；排序器；顺序发生器 | A sequencer is a device or program that controls the order in which events occur, often in a repeating or patterned way.  This can range from controlling the order of notes in music to controlling the steps in a manufacturing process. | Example:  The music sequencer allowed the composer to easily arrange and modify the song's structure.  测序仪让作曲家能够轻松地编排和修改歌曲的结构。 |近义词：controller, programmer, arranger | 反义词：randomizer, disorganizer
