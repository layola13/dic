europeans:/ˌjʊərəˈpiːənz/| n. |欧洲人|People who live in or are from Europe.  The term encompasses a vast diversity of cultures, languages, and ethnicities.|Example: Europeans have a rich history of art and literature. 欧洲人拥有丰富的艺术和文学历史。  Many Europeans enjoy traveling within their continent. 许多欧洲人喜欢在他们的大陆旅行。|近义词：Europeans,  inhabitants of Europe|反义词：Non-Europeans
