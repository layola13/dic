revitalizes:/ˌriːˈvaɪtəlaɪzɪz/ | vt. |使复兴；使恢复活力；使重新充满活力 | To give new life or energy to something; to restore to a vigorous state.  |例句：The new manager revitalized the company's marketing strategy.  新的经理使公司的营销策略焕发了活力。

例句：The injection of funding revitalized the struggling arts program.  资金的注入使苦苦挣扎的艺术项目恢复了生机。


近义词：rejuvenate, regenerate, refresh, reinvigorate, renew

反义词：deplete, exhaust, drain, weaken, deteriorate

词性:动词
