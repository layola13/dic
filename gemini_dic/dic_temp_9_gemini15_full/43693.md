creditable:/ˈkrɛdɪtəbl/ | adj. |值得称赞的；令人钦佩的；可信的 |Deserving of praise; worthy of belief.  It implies that something is good enough to earn respect or approval. |例句：He gave a creditable performance in the play. 他在剧中表演值得称赞。

例句：Her explanation of the incident was perfectly creditable. 她对事件的解释完全可信。


近义词： commendable, laudable, praiseworthy, respectable, believable, plausible

反义词：discreditable, shameful, dishonorable, unbelievable, implausible

词性：形容词
