colombier:/kəˈlɒmbiə(r)/| n. |鸽舍；鸽子养殖场|A colombier is a dovecote or pigeon house, a structure built to house pigeons or doves.  It can range from a simple structure to an elaborate building. |例句：The old colombier stood proudly in the middle of the field.  古老的鸽舍傲然地立在田野中央。|例句：The colombier was filled with the gentle cooing of hundreds of birds.  鸽舍里回荡着数百只鸟儿温柔的咕咕声。|近义词：dovecote, pigeon house|反义词：无
