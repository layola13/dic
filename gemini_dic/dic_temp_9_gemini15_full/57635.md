draughtsperson:/ˈdrɑːftsˌpɜːrsn/ | n. |绘图员，制图员 | A person who makes technical drawings.  Draughtspeople create detailed plans and diagrams for architects, engineers, and other professionals. They use computer-aided design (CAD) software and other tools to produce accurate and precise drawings. | The draughtsperson meticulously prepared the blueprints for the new building.  绘图员一丝不苟地准备了新建筑的蓝图。|  technical artist, designer |  (no direct antonym)
