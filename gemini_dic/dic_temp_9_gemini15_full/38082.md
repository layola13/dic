compost:/ˈkɒmpɒst/| n. v.|堆肥； компост|A mixture of decaying organic matter, such as leaves and kitchen waste, used to enrich soil.  Composting is also the process of making this mixture.|例句：We added compost to the soil to improve its fertility. 我们往土壤里添加了堆肥来提高它的肥力。
例句：She composts all her vegetable scraps. 她把所有的蔬菜残渣都用来堆肥。|近义词：manure, humus|反义词：N/A
