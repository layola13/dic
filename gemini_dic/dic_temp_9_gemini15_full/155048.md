petitionists:/pəˈtɪʃənɪsts/| n. |请愿者，请愿人 |People who sign or present a formal written request to an authority.  They are involved in the act of petitioning, aiming to influence a decision or change a policy.| The petitionists gathered outside the parliament building to voice their concerns about the new law. 请愿者们聚集在议会大楼外，表达他们对新法律的担忧。|supplicants, petitioners, signatories|opponents, adversaries
