light:/laɪt/| n., adj., v.|光；轻的；点燃|Light is a form of energy that enables us to see. 光是一种使我们能够看见的能量形式.  Light travels at a very high speed. 光以非常快的速度传播。|近义词：brightness, illumination,  easy, slight |反义词：darkness, heavy, dark |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun lights up the sky. = 太阳照亮了天空。
This lamp gives off a soft light. = 这盏灯发出柔和的光。

一般过去时:
He lit a candle. = 他点燃了一根蜡烛。
She switched on the light. = 她打开了灯。

现在完成时:
They have lit the bonfire. = 他们已经点燃了篝火。
The sun has set, and it's getting dark. = 太阳已经下山了，天色渐暗。

过去完成时:
They had lit the candles before the power went out. = 电力中断之前，他们已经点燃了蜡烛。
The sun had already set when we arrived. = 我们到达时太阳已经下山了。

将来时:
We will light the candles for the birthday party. = 我们将为生日派对点燃蜡烛。
The sun will rise tomorrow morning. = 明天早上太阳会升起。

虚拟语气:
If I had a lighter, I would light the candle. = 如果我有一个打火机，我会点燃蜡烛。
I wish the sun would shine more often. = 我希望太阳能经常照耀。

被动语态:
The room was lit by a single lamp. = 房间里只有一盏灯照明。
The stage was beautifully lit for the performance. = 舞台为演出做了精美的灯光布置。

疑问句:
Is there enough light to read? = 有足够的阅读光线吗？
Did you turn off the light before you left? = 你离开前关灯了吗？

否定句:
The room wasn't light enough. = 房间光线不足。
He didn't light the fire. = 他没有点火。


PHRASE->
light up = 点亮；使…高兴
bring to light = 揭露；使…为人所知
shed light on = 阐明；解释
see the light = 醒悟；明白
in the light of = 鉴于；考虑到
