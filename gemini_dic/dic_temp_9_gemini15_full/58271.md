drunkenhead:/ˈdrʌŋkənhed/| n. |酒鬼，醉汉 |A drunkenhead is a person who is habitually drunk or intoxicated.  It is a more informal and often derogatory term than "alcoholic." |Example: He was a complete drunkenhead, stumbling around the streets at midnight.|近义词：drunkard, sot, lush, inebriate |反义词：teetotaler, abstainer
