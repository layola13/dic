excretive:/ɪkˈskriːtɪv/| adj.|排泄的；分泌的|Relating to or characterized by excretion or secretion.  The word refers to the process of eliminating waste products from the body or the substance eliminated.|例句：The excretive system plays a vital role in maintaining bodily health.  排泄系统在维持身体健康方面起着至关重要的作用。

例句：The kidneys perform an essential excretive function by filtering waste from the blood. 肾脏通过过滤血液中的废物来执行重要的排泄功能。


近义词：excretory, secretory|反义词：absorptive, ingestive
