locus:/ˈloʊkəs/| n. |基因座；位置；轨迹；地点|The specific location of a gene or DNA sequence on a chromosome.  It can also refer to a place, position, or area. |例句：The locus of the gene for eye color is on chromosome 15.  基因座眼色的基因位于15号染色体上。


例句：The crime scene was the locus of the investigation. 犯罪现场是调查的中心地点。


近义词：location, position, site, place, point


反义词：N/A


词源:  From Latin *locus ("place").  The biological meaning developed in the early 20th century with the study of genetics.
