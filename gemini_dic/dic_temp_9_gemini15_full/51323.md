detasseled: /dɪˈtæsld/ | vt. | 去雄(玉米) | To remove the tassels from (corn).  The process of detasseling involves removing the male flower clusters (tassels) from corn plants to prevent self-pollination and ensure that the desired hybrid seeds are produced.  | Example:  Farmers detasseled the corn to improve the quality of the crop.  农民去除玉米雄穗以提高作物质量。  The crew spent the summer detasseling thousands of acres of corn.  这个团队花了整个夏天给数千英亩的玉米去雄穗。 | 近义词: emasculate (in the context of plants) | 反义词: tassel (verb: to add tassels)
