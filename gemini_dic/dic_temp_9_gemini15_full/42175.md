coughs:/kɒfs/ /kɔːf/| v.|咳嗽|To expel air from the lungs suddenly with a harsh sound, typically because of an irritation or illness.|例句：He coughs violently every morning. 他每天早上剧烈咳嗽。|例句：The dust made me cough. 灰尘呛得我咳嗽。|近义词：hack, splutter|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He coughs frequently. = 他经常咳嗽。
She coughs a lot when she has a cold. = 她感冒时咳嗽得很厉害。

一般过去时:
He coughed all night. = 他整夜咳嗽。
The baby coughed and then cried. = 婴儿咳嗽了一下，然后哭了。

现在完成时:
I have coughed up blood. = 我咳出了血。
She has been coughing for days. = 她已经咳嗽好几天了。

过去完成时:
He had coughed so much that he was hoarse. = 他咳得太多，声音嘶哑了。
She had coughed up a lot of phlegm before she felt better. = 她咳出很多痰后才感觉好些。

将来时:
I think I will cough all night. = 我想我会咳嗽一整夜。
He will cough less after taking the medicine. = 吃了药后，他会咳嗽得少一些。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't smoked so much, I wouldn't cough so much. = 如果我不抽那么多烟，我就不会咳嗽这么厉害。
I wish I didn't cough so much. = 我希望我不咳嗽得那么多。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The cough was induced by the dust. = 咳嗽是由灰尘引起的。(较为正式)
His lungs were irritated, causing him to cough. = 他的肺部受到刺激，导致他咳嗽。(强调被动)

疑问句:
Are you coughing? = 你在咳嗽吗？
Why does he cough so much? = 他为什么咳嗽得这么厉害？

否定句:
I don't cough often. = 我不经常咳嗽。
He didn't cough during the meeting. = 他在会议期间没有咳嗽。


PHRASE->
a coughing fit = 一阵咳嗽
a dry cough = 干咳
a hacking cough = 阵阵剧烈咳嗽
a persistent cough = 持续性咳嗽
a whooping cough = 百日咳
