fantasises:/ˈfæntəsaɪzɪz/| v.|幻想；梦想；编造|To indulge in fantasy; to create or imagine something not real.|例句：She fantasises about living in a castle. 她幻想住在城堡里。He fantasises about winning the lottery. 他幻想赢得彩票。|近义词：dreams, imagines, daydreams|反义词：realizes, actualizes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fantasises about travel. = 他幻想旅行。
She fantasises about becoming a famous singer. = 她幻想成为一名著名的歌手。

一般过去时:
I fantasized about owning a yacht. = 我曾经幻想拥有游艇。
They fantasized about a life free from worries. = 他们曾幻想过一种没有忧虑的生活。

现在完成时:
She has fantasized about this moment for years. = 她多年来一直幻想这一刻。
He has fantasized about escape many times. = 他多次幻想过逃跑。

过去完成时:
I had fantasized about meeting him before we actually met. = 在我们真正见面之前，我曾幻想过见到他。
They had fantasized about success before their big break. = 在他们取得重大突破之前，他们曾幻想过成功。

将来时:
She will fantasise about her future career. = 她将会幻想她未来的职业。
He will fantasise about his next vacation. = 他将会幻想他的下一个假期。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would fantasize less about travel and more about reality. = 如果我有钱，我就会少幻想旅行，多想想现实。
I wish I could fantasize less about impossible things. = 我希望我能少幻想一些不可能的事情。

被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used passively.)  The story was fantasized by the author. = 这个故事是作者虚构的。(不太常用)
The escape plan was fantasized in detail. = 逃亡计划被详细地幻想出来。(不太常用)

疑问句:
Does he often fantasize about his childhood? = 他经常幻想他的童年吗？
What does she fantasize about most? = 她最常幻想什么？

否定句:
He doesn't fantasize about unrealistic goals. = 他不幻想不切实际的目标。
She didn't fantasize about fame and fortune. = 她没有幻想名利。


PHRASE->
fantasize about = 幻想……
fantasize wildly = 胡思乱想
fantasize incessantly = 不停地幻想
fantasize vividly = 生动地幻想
fantasize creatively = 富有创造性地幻想
