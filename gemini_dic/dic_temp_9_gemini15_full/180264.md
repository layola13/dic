reframed:/riːˈfreɪmd/| v.|重新构建；重新诠释；改变框架 | To present or interpret something in a new or different way.  It involves changing the perspective or context of something. | 例句：He reframed the argument to make it more persuasive. 他重新构建了论点使其更有说服力。

例句：The artist reframed the painting to enhance its impact. 艺术家重新装裱了画作以增强其冲击力。


近义词：reinterpreted, recast, reformulated, restructured

反义词：maintained, preserved, left unchanged

词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reframes his ideas regularly. = 他定期重新构建自己的想法。
She reframes her experiences to find meaning. = 她重新诠释自己的经历以寻找意义。

一般过去时:
They reframed the project after the initial setback. = 他们在初步受挫后重新构建了项目。
He reframed the question to elicit a different response. = 他重新表述了问题以引出不同的回应。

现在完成时:
She has reframed the debate numerous times. = 她已经多次重新构建了这场辩论。
They have reframed their business strategy to adapt to market changes. = 他们已经重新构建了他们的商业战略以适应市场变化。

过去完成时:
We had reframed the contract before signing. = 我们在签字之前已经重新构建了合同。
She had reframed her understanding of the situation after speaking to him. = 在和他谈话后，她已经重新理解了情况。

将来时:
They will reframe the legislation to address public concerns. = 他们将重新构建立法以解决公众担忧。
He will reframe his presentation to make it clearer. = 他将重新构建他的演示文稿使其更清晰。

虚拟语气:
If they reframed the proposal, they might get more support. = 如果他们重新构建提案，他们可能会得到更多支持。
I wish she had reframed her criticism more constructively. = 我希望她能更具建设性地重新构建她的批评。

被动语态:
The issue was reframed by the media. = 这个问题被媒体重新构建了。
The narrative has been reframed to suit the new evidence. = 故事已经被重新构建以适应新的证据。

疑问句:
How can we reframe this problem to find a solution? = 我们如何重新构建这个问题以找到解决方案？
Will they reframe their approach to the project? = 他们会重新构建他们对项目的方法吗？

否定句:
They did not reframe the question. = 他们没有重新构建问题。
The company refused to reframe its policy. = 公司拒绝重新构建其政策。
