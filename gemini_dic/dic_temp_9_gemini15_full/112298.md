lord:/lɔːrd/| n. |领主；上帝；大人；法官| A person who has power and authority, especially a nobleman or a person of high rank.  It can also refer to God. |例句：He was a powerful lord in the medieval times. 他在中世纪是一个强大的领主。

例句：The lord of the manor held great sway over his tenants.庄园领主对其佃户拥有很大的权力。


近义词：nobleman, master, peer, God

反义词：serf, peasant, subordinate
