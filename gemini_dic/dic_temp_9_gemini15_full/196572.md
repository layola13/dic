sessions:/ˈseʃənz/ | n. |会议；课程；一段时间；(电脑)会话|A period of time dedicated to a particular activity; a meeting or series of meetings devoted to a particular subject.  It can also refer to a single instance of a computer session.|例句：The therapy sessions were helpful.  治疗疗程很有帮助。

The judge adjourned the court sessions until tomorrow.  法官将法庭休庭至明天。


近义词：meetings, periods, sittings, terms|反义词：interruption, recess
