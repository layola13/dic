peter-out: /ˈpiːtər aʊt/ | v. |逐渐减弱；逐渐消失；逐渐终止| To gradually diminish or dwindle to nothing; to end gradually. |例句：The excitement gradually petered out.  兴奋逐渐消退了。|例句：The meeting petered out after an hour. 会议在一个小时后逐渐结束。|近义词：fade, die away, dwindle, taper off |反义词：intensify, increase, escalate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The noise peters out gradually. = 噪音逐渐减弱。
His enthusiasm peters out slowly. = 他的热情慢慢消退。


一般过去时:
The protest petered out after a few days. = 抗议活动几天后逐渐平息了。
The party petered out as the night wore on. = 夜深了，聚会也逐渐散了。


现在完成时:
The rain has petered out. = 雨已经停了。
Interest in the project has petered out. = 对这个项目的兴趣已经消退了。


过去完成时:
The discussion had petered out before he arrived. = 他到达之前，讨论已经结束了。
The fire had petered out by the time the firefighters arrived. = 消防员到达时，火已经熄灭了。


将来时:
The storm will peter out by morning. = 到早上风暴就会平息了。
The conversation will likely peter out soon. = 对话可能很快就会结束。


虚拟语气:
If the funding had not petered out, the project would have been completed. = 如果资金没有耗尽，项目本来就能完成了。
If the energy petered out, we would have to stop. = 如果能量耗尽，我们就得停下来。


被动语态:
The project was petered out by lack of funding. = 该项目因缺乏资金而终止。
The argument was petered out by the mediator. = 调解员使争论逐渐平息。


疑问句:
Did the argument peter out? = 争论平息了吗？
Will the music peter out soon? = 音乐很快就会结束吗？


否定句:
The protest didn't peter out; it escalated. = 抗议活动没有平息，反而升级了。
The conversation didn't peter out; it continued for hours. = 对话并没有结束，而是持续了好几个小时。


PHRASE->
peter out to nothing = 完全消失
peter out gradually = 逐渐减弱
peter out completely = 完全消失
slowly peter out = 慢慢消失
peter out and end = 逐渐结束
