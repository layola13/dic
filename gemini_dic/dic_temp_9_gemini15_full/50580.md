derailed:/dɪˈreɪld/ | vt. |使脱轨；使受挫折；破坏；扰乱 | To cause (a train or vehicle) to leave its tracks; to disrupt or obstruct the progress of something. | 例句：The train was derailed by a landslide.  火车被山体滑坡脱轨了。 | 例句：His career was derailed by a scandal.  他的职业生涯因丑闻而受挫。 | 近义词：disrupt, upset, obstruct, thwart | 反义词：facilitate, aid, assist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scandal derails his political career. = 这场丑闻破坏了他的政治生涯。
He derails the meeting with his endless questions. = 他没完没了的问题扰乱了会议。

一般过去时:
The unexpected rain derailed our picnic plans. = 突如其来的雨水打乱了我们的野餐计划。
The project was derailed by funding issues. = 该项目因资金问题而受阻。

现在完成时:
The economic crisis has derailed many businesses. = 经济危机已经使许多企业倒闭。
His health problems have derailed his training schedule. = 他的健康问题打乱了他的训练计划。

过去完成时:
The accident had derailed the train before it reached the station. = 事故发生前火车就已经脱轨了。
They had derailed the negotiations by making unreasonable demands. = 他们通过提出不合理的要求破坏了谈判。

将来时:
The strike will derail the production schedule. = 罢工将打乱生产计划。
Further delays will likely derail the project completely. = 进一步的延误很可能会完全破坏该项目。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't been injured, his career wouldn't have been derailed. = 如果他没有受伤，他的职业生涯就不会受挫了。
If we had planned better, the trip wouldn't have been derailed. = 如果我们计划得更好，旅行就不会被打乱了。

被动语态:
The train was derailed by a sudden storm. = 火车被突如其来的暴风雨脱轨了。
The peace talks were derailed by disagreements. = 和谈因分歧而破裂。

疑问句:
Was the project derailed by a lack of funding? = 项目是因为缺乏资金而受阻的吗？
Will the bad weather derail our flight? = 恶劣天气会影响我们的航班吗？

否定句:
The unexpected event did not derail the team's progress. = 意料之外的事件并没有阻碍团队的进步。
We managed to avoid being derailed by the setbacks. = 我们设法避免了因挫折而受阻。
