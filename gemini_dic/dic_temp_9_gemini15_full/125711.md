municipalism:/mjuːˈnɪsɪpəlɪzəm/| n. |市政主义；地方自治主义|The belief that local communities should have more control over their own affairs and resources.  It often involves advocating for greater autonomy for municipalities and emphasizing local solutions to local problems. This ideology often contrasts with centralized government control.|Example:  The rise of municipalism in the region led to increased investment in local infrastructure.  市政主义在该地区的兴起导致对当地基础设施的投资增加。|近义词：localism, decentralization |反义词：centralization, nationalism |名词
