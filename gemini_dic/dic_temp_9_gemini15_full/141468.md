opining:/əˈpiːnɪŋ/ | v. |发表意见；表达看法；认为 | expressing an opinion; stating a belief or judgment.  | The judge opined that the evidence was insufficient.  法官认为证据不足。 |expressing, stating, suggesting, declaring| disagreeing, opposing, contradicting|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is opining on the current political situation. 他正在评论当前的政治局势。
She opines that the new policy is flawed. 她认为新政策有缺陷。


一般过去时:
The professor opined that the student's essay was well-written. 教授认为学生的论文写得很好。
He opined on the matter with great conviction. 他很有信心地发表了对这件事的看法。


现在完成时:
She has opined on this topic many times before. 她之前在这个话题上发表过多次意见。
They have opined that the project is too ambitious. 他们认为这个项目过于雄心勃勃。


过去完成时:
He had opined that the results were inconclusive before the further tests. 在进一步测试之前，他认为结果并不确定。
She had opined against the proposal before the meeting. 会议之前她就反对过这个提案。


将来时:
The expert will opine on the cause of the accident. 专家将对事故原因发表意见。
He will opine on the matter after reviewing the evidence. 他将在审查证据后发表意见。


虚拟语气:
If he opined differently, the outcome might have been different. 如果他的意见不同，结果可能会有所不同。
I wish he had opined more clearly. 我希望他表达得更清楚一些。


被动语态:
It was opined by many that the defendant was guilty. 许多人认为被告有罪。(注意被动语态在此处较少见)
The matter was opined upon extensively in the press.  媒体对此事进行了广泛的评论。(注意被动语态在此处较少见)


疑问句:
What did he opine about the new regulations? 他对新规有什么看法？
How would you opine on the current economic climate? 你如何看待当前的经济形势？


否定句:
He did not opine on the subject. 他没有就此事发表意见。
She did not opine that the plan was feasible. 她不认为这个计划可行。


PHRASE->
opining on (对...发表意见)
opining that (认为...)
opining with (以...开头发表意见)
opining against (反对...)
opining in favor of (赞成...)
