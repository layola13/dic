myriapods:/ˈmɪriəpɒdz/| n. |多足类动物|Myriapods are invertebrate animals characterized by having numerous legs. They include centipedes, millipedes, and other similar creatures.  They are found in diverse habitats across the globe and play important ecological roles.|例句：The rainforest was teeming with myriapods of all shapes and sizes.  热带雨林里挤满了各种形状和大小的多足类动物。|近义词：many-legged creatures, arthropods|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with animals with few or no legs.
