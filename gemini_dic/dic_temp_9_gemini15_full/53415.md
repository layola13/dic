disannex:/dɪsˈænɛks/| vt.|取消兼并；脱离；使不再附属|To formally remove (a territory or region) from annexation or political control by a larger entity.  The act of disannexing is the opposite of annexing.|例句：The residents fought to have their town disannexed from the city.  居民们努力争取让他们的城镇脱离该市的管辖。

例句：The treaty led to the disannexation of several territories.  该条约导致几个地区脱离。


近义词：detach, separate, sever, release|反义词：annex, attach, incorporate


词性:动词
