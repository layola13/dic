fingerless:/ˈfɪŋɡərles/| adj.|无指的，露指的|Lacking fingers or finger coverings.  It describes gloves, mittens, or other garments that leave the fingers exposed.|例句：She wore fingerless gloves to keep her hands warm while still being able to type. 她戴着露指手套，这样既能保暖又能打字。

例句：The artist sculpted a fingerless hand for the statue.艺术家为雕像雕刻了一只无指的手。

近义词：ungloved (for gloves), open-fingered


反义词：fingered, full-fingered


词性：形容词
