deframed:/dɪˈfreɪmd/ | vt. | 诋毁；诬陷；使免受指控 | To remove the frame from (a picture, etc.); to clear (someone) of blame or accusation.  |例句：The journalist was deframed by a rival newspaper. = 这名记者被竞争对手报纸诬陷了。

The police investigation deframed the suspect. = 警方调查为嫌疑人洗清了罪名。

近义词：libel, slander, calumniate, exculpate

反义词：incriminate, frame, accuse
