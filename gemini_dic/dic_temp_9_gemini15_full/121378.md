mishandled:/ˌmɪsˈhændld/| vt.|处理不当；误操作|To handle something badly or incorrectly, often leading to negative consequences.  |例句：The company mishandled the crisis, leading to a loss of public trust. 公司处理危机不当，导致公众信任丧失。|例句：He mishandled the delicate situation, making things worse. 他处理棘手的情况不得当，使情况更加恶化。|近义词：mismanage, botch, blunder, mismanage|反义词：manage well, handle skillfully, expertly handled|动词

词源:
The word "mishandled" is formed by adding the prefix "mis-" (meaning "badly" or "wrongly") to the verb "handle".  "Handle" itself comes from Old English "handel,"  meaning "grip, grasp."  Therefore, "mishandled" literally means "badly gripped" or "wrongly managed."
