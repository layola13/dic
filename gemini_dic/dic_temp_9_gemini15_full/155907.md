photochronograph:/ˈfoʊtoʊkrɒnəɡræf/ | n. |摄影计时器，照片计时器 |A device that records a series of photographs at short intervals to show movement.  It is essentially a combination of a camera and a chronograph, used to study motion and events over time.  |Example:  The scientists used a photochronograph to study the flight of birds.  科学家们用摄影计时器来研究鸟类的飞行。|近义词：high-speed camera, time-lapse camera |反义词：None readily available.
