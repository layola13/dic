immailed:/ɪˈmeɪld/ | vt. | 邮件发送 | To send an email to someone.  | Example: I immailed him the document. 我给他发了邮件。 | 近义词：emailed, sent an email | 反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I email my friends every day. = 我每天都给我的朋友们发邮件。
She emails her boss regularly. = 她定期给老板发邮件。


一般过去时:
He emailed me last night. = 他昨晚给我发了邮件。
They emailed the report to the client. = 他们把报告发邮件给了客户。


现在完成时:
I have emailed him three times this week. = 我这周已经给他发了三次邮件。
She has emailed her application. = 她已经发了申请邮件。


过去完成时:
I had emailed the proposal before the meeting. = 我在会议之前已经发了提案的邮件。
They had emailed the information to the committee. = 他们已经把信息发邮件给了委员会。


将来时:
I will email you the details tomorrow. = 我明天会把细节发邮件给你。
She will email the results when they are ready. = 结果准备好后她会发邮件。


虚拟语气:
If I had emailed him sooner, he would have known. = 如果我早点给他发邮件，他就知道了。
I wish I had emailed her earlier. = 我真希望我早点给她发邮件。


被动语态:
The document was emailed to everyone. = 文件已发送给所有人。
The invitations were emailed last week. = 邀请函是上周发邮件的。


疑问句:
Have you emailed him yet? = 你给他发邮件了吗？
Did she email the report? = 她发了报告的邮件吗？


否定句:
I didn't email him because I forgot. = 我没有给他发邮件，因为我忘了。
She hasn't emailed me back yet. = 她还没回复我的邮件。


PHRASE->
email someone = 给某人发邮件
email a document = 发送文档
email an attachment = 发送附件
email a photo = 发送照片
reply to an email = 回复邮件
