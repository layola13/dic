cheeks:/tʃiːks/ | n. | 面颊，脸颊 | The fleshy parts of the face below the eyes and on either side of the nose.  Cheeks are often associated with expressions and blushing. |例句：Her cheeks flushed with embarrassment.  她的脸颊羞红了。

例句：He has rosy cheeks. 他有着红润的脸颊。


近义词：jowl (侧脸颊) , side of the face


反义词：无


词源：源自古英语 *cēace,  与古高地德语 *chiuha, 古撒克逊语 *kēke,  古弗里西亚语 *kēke, 中荷兰语 *kake 同源，最终可能源自原始日耳曼语 *kaukō。
