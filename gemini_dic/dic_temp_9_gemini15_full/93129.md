ify:/ˈɪfaɪ/ | v. | 使成为；使具有…性质 | To make or become something; to give something a particular quality or characteristic. |  He tried to *ify* the situation. 他试图使情况变得明朗。 The event *ified* the community.  这次活动使社区团结一心。 | 近义词：make, render, transform | 反义词：destroy, undo

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I beautify my home. = 我美化我的家。
They simplify the process. = 他们简化了流程。

一般过去时:
She intensified her efforts. = 她加大了努力。
He solidified his position. = 他巩固了他的地位。

现在完成时:
We have purified the water. = 我们已经净化了水。
They have magnified the image. = 他们已经放大了图像。

过去完成时:
She had already identified the problem. = 她已经确定了问题。
They had simplified the instructions. = 他们已经简化了说明。

将来时:
He will clarify his statement. = 他将澄清他的声明。
We will intensify the search. = 我们将加强搜寻。

虚拟语气:
If I were to personify courage, I would choose a lion. = 如果我要将勇气拟人化，我会选择一只狮子。
I wish I could mystify them. = 我希望我能迷惑他们。

被动语态:
The plan was simplified. = 计划被简化了。
The process was purified. = 流程被净化了。

疑问句:
Does this beautify the room? = 这是否美化了房间？
Can we simplify the task? = 我们能简化这项任务吗？

否定句:
I didn't falsify the document. = 我没有伪造文件。
They didn't glorify violence. = 他们没有美化暴力。
