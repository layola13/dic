flourishing:/ˈflʌrɪʃɪŋ/| adj.|繁荣的；兴旺的；茂盛的|  Thriving, successful, and growing rapidly.  It describes something that is developing very well and is successful.|例句：The company is flourishing under its new CEO. 这家公司在其新任首席执行官的领导下蓬勃发展。|例句：The flowers are flourishing in the garden. 花园里的花朵盛开着。|近义词：thriving, prosperous, booming, blossoming|反义词：declining, failing, withering, stagnant|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The business is flourishing. = 这家企业正在蓬勃发展。
The plants are flourishing in the sun. = 植物在阳光下茁壮成长。

一般过去时:
The economy flourished in the 1990s. = 经济在20世纪90年代蓬勃发展。
The town flourished after the discovery of gold. = 在发现金矿后，这个小镇繁荣起来。

现在完成时:
The company has flourished since it changed its strategy. = 自从改变策略以来，公司一直蓬勃发展。
Our garden has flourished this summer. = 我们的花园今年夏天长势喜人。


过去完成时:
The empire had flourished for centuries before its decline. = 这个帝国在其衰落之前已经繁荣了几个世纪。
The business had flourished before the economic downturn. = 在经济低迷之前，这家企业曾非常兴旺。


将来时:
The city will flourish with the new infrastructure projects. = 随着新的基础设施项目的实施，这座城市将会繁荣起来。
We believe the business will flourish in the future. = 我们相信这家企业将来会蓬勃发展。


虚拟语气:
If the government implemented better policies, the economy would flourish. = 如果政府实施更好的政策，经济就会繁荣。
I wish my business would flourish as it did last year. = 我希望我的生意能像去年一样兴旺。


被动语态:
The city was flourished by the development of new technologies. =  这座城市因新技术的开发而繁荣起来。(Note:  While "flourish" is usually not used passively, this example demonstrates a potential passive construction emphasizing the effect of an action on the city's flourishing.)
The plants were flourished by the careful attention of the gardener. = 植物因园丁的精心照料而长势喜人。(Similarly, this emphasizes the effect of the gardener's actions.)


疑问句:
Is the business flourishing? = 这家企业是否蓬勃发展？
Are the flowers flourishing this year? = 今年花朵长势好吗？


否定句:
The business is not flourishing. = 这家企业没有蓬勃发展。
The plants are not flourishing without enough water. = 没有足够的水分，植物就不会生长茂盛。
