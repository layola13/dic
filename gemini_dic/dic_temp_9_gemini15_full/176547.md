re-seeks:/ˌriːˈsiːks/| v.|重新寻找，再次寻求|To seek again; to make a renewed attempt to find or obtain something.|例句：After the initial search proved fruitless, the detectives re-seeked the missing evidence.  警方初步搜查无果后，再次寻找失踪的证据。|例句：He re-seeked his lost love, hoping to rekindle their relationship. 他重新寻找他失去的爱人，希望能重燃他们的爱情。|近义词：re-search, pursue again, seek anew|反义词：abandon, give up
