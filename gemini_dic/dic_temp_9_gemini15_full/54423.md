disinfectants:/dɪsɪnˈfɛktənts/| n. |消毒剂|Substances that kill or inhibit the growth of microorganisms, such as bacteria, viruses, and fungi, thereby reducing the risk of infection.  They are used to sterilize surfaces, equipment, and even some bodily wounds. |例句：The nurse used disinfectants to clean the wound before applying a bandage. 护士在包扎伤口前用消毒剂清洁了伤口。

例句：Hospitals use strong disinfectants to prevent the spread of infections. 医院使用强效消毒剂来防止感染的传播。


近义词：antiseptics, germicides, sterilizers |反义词：contaminants
