outscooped:/ˌaʊtˈskuːpt/| v. |抢先报道，先于…报道|To publish or broadcast news before someone else.|The smaller news outlet outscooped the major networks on the breaking story.  这家较小的新闻机构抢在大型新闻网络之前报道了这条突发新闻。|scoop, beat|be beaten, be late|动词

He often outscoops his rivals. = 他经常抢在竞争对手之前报道新闻。
The journalist outscoops the competition with her exclusive interviews. = 这位记者凭借她的独家采访抢在竞争对手之前。


一般过去时:
They outscooped the other newspapers with their breaking news report. = 他们凭借他们的突发新闻报道抢在其他报纸之前。
The local news channel outscooped the national networks on the story. = 当地新闻频道抢在全国性新闻网络之前报道了这条新闻。
