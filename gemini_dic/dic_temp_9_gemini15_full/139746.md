ocker:/ˈɒkə(r)/| n. adj.|粗鲁无礼的人；澳大利亚的乡下人；粗俗的；澳大利亚白人男性|A person, especially a man, from the working class in Australia, often perceived as being loud, aggressive, and uncouth.  The term can be offensive.|例句：He's a bit of an ocker, always telling loud jokes.|近义词：bogan, larrikin, yob (UK)|反义词：refined, sophisticated, cultured|名词，形容词
