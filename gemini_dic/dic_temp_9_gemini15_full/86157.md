heartpea: /ˈhɑːrtpiː/ | n. | 心豆 | A heart-shaped pea, typically referring to a variety of  Lathyrus species,  especially those with edible seeds.  It's often used interchangeably with "sweet pea," though technically sweet pea usually refers to the ornamental variety. | Example:  The garden was bursting with vibrant heartpeas. 花园里盛开着鲜艳的心豆。 |近义词: sweet pea,  Lathyrus (genus) |反义词:  None specifically, but  "bitter pea" could be considered a contrast if referring to inedible varieties.
