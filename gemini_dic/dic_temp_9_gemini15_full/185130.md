retinning: /rɪˈtɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 重新镀锡 | To coat (something, especially metal) again with tin.  This usually refers to a process of applying a new layer of tin to a previously tinned surface, often to repair or restore its protective coating.  The process aims to restore the corrosion resistance and other properties provided by the tin layer. | Example: The plumber retinned the copper pipes to prevent leaks.  水管工重新镀锡了铜管以防止漏水。 | 近义词: re-tinning, tinning (again) | 反义词: detinning, removing tin coating | 词性: Verb
