kingfisher:/ˈkɪŋˌfɪʃər/| n. |翠鸟|A kingfisher is a type of bird that is known for its brightly colored plumage and its ability to dive into water to catch fish.  They are found worldwide, except for Antarctica and South America. They are typically found near water bodies like rivers and lakes.|例句：The kingfisher plunged into the river and emerged with a small fish in its beak.  翠鸟扎入河中，叼着一条小鱼浮出水面。  The vibrant blue of the kingfisher stood out against the green of the reeds.  翠鸟鲜艳的蓝色在绿色的芦苇丛中格外醒目。|近义词：halcyon (in a poetic or literary context)|反义词：None specifically.
