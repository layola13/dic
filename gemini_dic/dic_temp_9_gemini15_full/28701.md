carloads:/ˈkɑːrloʊdz/ | n. | 车装数量；几车货物 |  A carload refers to the amount that can be carried in one car, typically a railroad car or a truck. It is often used to describe a large quantity of something. |例句：We shipped several carloads of oranges to the market. 我们向市场运送了几车橙子。

例句：The factory produced carloads of toys during the holiday season.  节日期间，这家工厂生产了大量的玩具。


近义词：truckloads, shipments, consignments


反义词：少量, small quantities


词性: 名词
