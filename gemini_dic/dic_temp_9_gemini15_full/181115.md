reinfecting:/ˌriːɪnˈfɛktɪŋ/ | v. | 再感染；重复感染 |  The act of infecting someone or something again after a previous infection.  It implies a recurrence of the infection, often after a period of recovery or remission.  The term is frequently used in the context of infectious diseases, but can also apply to other contexts where something is repeatedly introduced or imposed. |

例句：

The virus is capable of reinfecting even those who have recovered from a previous infection. = 这种病毒即使是那些已经从之前的感染中恢复过来的人也可能再次感染。

The recurring problem of reinfecting the computer with malware was frustrating. = 电脑反复感染恶意软件的问题令人沮丧。


近义词：reinoculating, reintroducing (in certain contexts)

反义词：curing, eradicating
