clad:/klæd/ | adj. |穿着…衣服的；覆盖…的 | Covered or clothed; wearing something.  | 例句：The children were clad in warm coats. 孩子们穿着暖和的大衣。 | 例句：The mountains were clad in snow.  山峦覆盖着白雪。 | 近义词：dressed, clothed, covered | 反义词：naked, bare

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is clad in a suit. = 他穿着西装。
She is clad in a beautiful gown. = 她穿着美丽的长袍。

一般过去时:
They were clad in rags. = 他们衣衫褴褛。
The soldiers were clad in camouflage. = 士兵们穿着迷彩服。

现在完成时:
The statue has been clad in gold leaf. = 雕像已被镀金箔。
The building has been clad in modern materials. = 这栋建筑物已被现代材料覆盖。

过去完成时:
The hikers had been clad in waterproof jackets. = 登山者们之前都穿着防水夹克。
She had been clad in her finest clothes for the occasion. = 她为了这次场合穿上了她最好的衣服。

将来时:
The actors will be clad in period costumes. = 演员们将穿着古装。
He will be clad in a tuxedo for the wedding. = 他将穿燕尾服参加婚礼。

虚拟语气:
If he were clad in warmer clothes, he wouldn't be so cold. = 如果他穿得暖和些，他就不会这么冷了。
I wish I were clad in something more comfortable. = 我希望我穿得更舒服些。

被动语态:
The building is clad in stone. = 这栋建筑物用石头覆盖。
The walls were clad in wood paneling. = 墙壁用木板镶面。

疑问句:
Is the baby clad in a diaper? = 婴儿穿着尿布吗？
Were the guests clad in formal attire? = 客人们穿着正式服装吗？

否定句:
He is not clad in appropriate clothing for the weather. = 他穿的衣服不适合这个天气。
The house was not clad in brick, but in wood. = 这房子不是砖砌的，而是木制的。
