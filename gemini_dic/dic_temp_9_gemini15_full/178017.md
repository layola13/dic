recklessness:/ˈrɛkləsnəs/ | n. |鲁莽；轻率；不顾后果 |The quality or state of being reckless; disregard for danger or consequences.  It often involves a lack of caution and a tendency to take unnecessary risks. |例句：His recklessness led to a serious car accident.  他的鲁莽导致了一场严重的车祸。

例句：The recklessness of the driver caused the accident. 司机的鲁莽导致了事故。


近义词：rashness, carelessness, imprudence, foolhardiness, temerity

反义词：caution, prudence, carefulness, circumspection
