cutler:/ˈkʌtlər/| n. |刀具制造商；刀具商；餐具|A cutler is a person who makes or sells knives, forks, spoons, and other cutlery.  Cutlers traditionally crafted their goods by hand, but now many use machinery.|例句：He inherited his father's business as a cutler.|近义词：cutlery maker, knife maker|反义词:|
