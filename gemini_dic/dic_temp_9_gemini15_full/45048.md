cubbing:/ˈkʌbɪŋ/ | n. | 幼兽狩猎；初次狩猎体验；殴打；训斥 | The act of hunting young animals, especially foxes, or the experience of a young person's first hunt.  It can also refer to the act of beating or reprimanding someone. | 例句：The hunt involved cubbing young foxes.  = 这场狩猎包括猎杀幼狐。  The teacher gave the student a good cubbing for his bad behavior. = 老师因为学生的恶劣行为狠狠地训斥了他。 | 近义词：hunting (young animals), beating, reprimanding | 反义词：protecting, praising
