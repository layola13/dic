intertrigo:/ˌɪntərˈtraɪɡoʊ/| n. |皮肤摩擦|Inflammation of skin areas where two skin surfaces touch, such as the armpits, groin, and between the toes.  This is often caused by moisture, friction, and warmth, creating a favorable environment for bacterial or fungal growth. |Example:  The baby developed intertrigo in the skin folds of his neck.  婴儿颈部皮肤褶皱处出现了汗疹。|近义词：rash, chafing, dermatitis|反义词：None specifically, but conditions indicating healthy skin could be considered opposites.
