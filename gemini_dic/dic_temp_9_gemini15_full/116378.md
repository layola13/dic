masting:/ˈmæstɪŋ/| n.|桅杆安装；装设桅杆|The act of fitting masts to a ship or other vessel.  It also refers to the masts themselves, collectively.|例句：The masting of the new yacht took several days. 新游艇的桅杆安装花了几天时间。|例句：The ship's masting was damaged in the storm.  这艘船的桅杆在暴风雨中受损。|近义词：rigging, fitting(masts)|反义词：de-masting(removing masts)
