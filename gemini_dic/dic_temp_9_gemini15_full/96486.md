indivisibility:/ˌɪndɪvɪzəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可分割性，完整性 |The state or quality of being indivisible; inability to be divided.  It refers to something that cannot be divided into smaller parts without losing its essential nature or identity. | Example: The indivisibility of the atom was once a widely held scientific belief. 原子不可分割曾经是一个被广泛接受的科学信念。The indivisibility of the family unit is crucial to their stability. 家庭单元的不可分割性对他们的稳定至关重要。|近义词：unity, wholeness, oneness |反义词：divisibility, separability |名词
