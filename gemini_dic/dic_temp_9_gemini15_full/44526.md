crossroads:/ˈkrɒsˌroʊdz/ /ˈkrɒsroʊdz/| n.|十字路口，关键时刻，转折点|A crossroads is a place where two roads meet and cross each other.  It can also refer to a point in time when a crucial decision must be made, a turning point in one's life or a situation.|例句：We stood at the crossroads, unsure which way to go. 我们站在十字路口，不知道该走哪条路。

例句：He found himself at a crossroads in his career. 他发现自己职业生涯到了一个转折点。|近义词：intersection, juncture, turning point|反义词：straight path, single direction
