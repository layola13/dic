chemists:/ˈkɛmɪsts/ | n. |化学家 | A chemist is a scientist who studies chemistry. Chemists work in a variety of settings, including universities, government laboratories, and private industry. They conduct research, develop new products, and teach others about chemistry. | Example: Many chemists are working on new ways to produce clean energy. 许多化学家正在研究生产清洁能源的新方法。 |近义词:alchemist, chemical engineer|反义词: None
