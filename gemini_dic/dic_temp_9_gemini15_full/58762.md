dunker:/ˈdʌŋkər/| n. |浸面包者；猛力投篮者；(尤指)用力灌篮者|A dunker is someone who dunks food, especially bread, into a liquid, or someone who performs a dunk shot in basketball.  The term often implies a forceful action. |例句：He's a skilled dunker, known for his powerful slams. 他是一位技术娴熟的灌篮高手，以其强大的灌篮而闻名。|例句：She's a dunker of her biscuits in tea. 她喜欢把饼干蘸着茶吃。|近义词：dipper (for food), slammer (for basketball)|反义词：N/A
