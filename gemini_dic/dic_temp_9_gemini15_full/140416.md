ok'ing: /oʊˈkɪŋ/ | v. | 批准；同意；认可 |  The act of giving approval or consent; the process of saying "OK" to something.  It's a gerund form of the verb "OK." | 例句：The manager is ok'ing the new project proposal. = 经理正在批准新的项目提案。  They are still ok'ing the final design. = 他们仍在批准最终设计。 | 近义词：approving, sanctioning, authorizing | 反义词：rejecting, disapproving, vetoing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is ok'ing the budget. = 他正在批准预算。
She is constantly ok'ing new initiatives. = 她不断批准新的倡议。

一般过去时:
They ok'ed the contract yesterday. = 他们昨天批准了合同。
The board ok'ed the merger proposal last week. = 董事会在上周批准了合并提案。

现在完成时:
The committee has ok'ed the changes. = 委员会已经批准了这些更改。
Management has already ok'ed the marketing plan. = 管理层已经批准了营销计划。

过去完成时:
They had ok'ed the payment before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经批准了付款。
The CEO had ok'ed the investment before leaving for vacation. = 首席执行官在休假前已经批准了投资。

将来时:
The team will ok the report by Friday. = 团队将在周五批准报告。
We expect they will ok the request soon. = 我们预计他们很快就会批准请求。

虚拟语气:
If they ok'ed the loan, we could start the project. = 如果他们批准贷款，我们就可以开始项目。
I wish the bank would ok my application quickly. = 我希望银行能尽快批准我的申请。

被动语态:
The application was ok'ed by the committee. = 该申请已获得委员会批准。
The budget is being ok'ed as we speak. = 预算正在被批准。

疑问句:
Has the proposal been ok'ed yet? = 提案已经批准了吗？
Will they ok the changes before the meeting? = 他们在会议前会批准这些更改吗？

否定句:
They didn't ok the request. = 他们没有批准请求。
The plan wasn't ok'ed by the higher-ups. = 该计划未获得高层的批准。
