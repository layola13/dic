nonforeclosing: /ˌnɒnˈfɔːrˌkloʊzɪŋ/ | adj. | 不止赎的，非取消抵押的 | Not involving or resulting in the foreclosure of a mortgage or other lien.  This term typically describes a loan modification or other restructuring agreement that avoids the foreclosure process. | 例句：The bank offered a nonforeclosing loan modification to help the homeowner avoid foreclosure.  银行提供了一项非取消抵押贷款修改方案，以帮助房主避免丧失抵押品赎回权。

The lender implemented a nonforeclosing workout plan to assist the struggling borrower.  贷款人实施了一项非取消抵押的解决方案来帮助陷入困境的借款人。


近义词：avoiding foreclosure, preventing foreclosure, forbearance


反义词：foreclosing
