nonfindings:/ˌnɒnˈfaɪndɪŋz/| n. |未发现的事物；阴性结果|The absence of expected findings; results indicating the lack of a specific condition or phenomenon.  Nonfindings are often as important as positive findings in research and medical diagnosis.|例句：The nonfindings of the study suggested that the hypothesis was incorrect.  这项研究的阴性结果表明假设是错误的。


The absence of any significant changes in the patient's condition after treatment was a key nonfinding. 治疗后患者病情无明显变化，这是一个关键的阴性结果。|近义词：negative results, absence of findings, lack of evidence|反义词：positive findings, significant results|名词
