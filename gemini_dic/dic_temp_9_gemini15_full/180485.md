regeled:/rɪˈɡiːld/| v.|款待，盛宴款待|To entertain lavishly; to feast.|They regeled their guests with a sumptuous feast. 他们用丰盛的盛宴款待他们的客人。|近义词：feast, entertain, regale|反义词：neglect, starve

They regale the audience with jokes. 他们用笑话招待观众。
