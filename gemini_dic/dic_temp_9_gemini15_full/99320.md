intercultural: /ˌɪntərˈkʌltʃərəl/ | adj. |跨文化的，国际文化间的| Relating to or involving different cultures.  |例句：The program promotes intercultural understanding and cooperation.  该项目促进跨文化理解与合作。  |近义词：cross-cultural, multinational, international |反义词：monocultural
