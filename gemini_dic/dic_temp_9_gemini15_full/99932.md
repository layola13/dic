interning:/ɪnˈtɜːrnɪŋ/ | n. | 实习；实习期 | The act of working in a specific field as an intern; a period of internship.  |  She's currently interning at a prestigious law firm. 她目前在一家著名的律师事务所实习。 | apprenticeship, traineeship | employment, job | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is interning at Google this summer. 他今年夏天在谷歌实习。
She interns at a hospital every weekend. 她每个周末都在医院实习。


一般过去时:
I interned at a marketing agency last year. 我去年在一个市场营销机构实习。
They interned with a renowned architect. 他们与一位著名的建筑师一起实习。


现在完成时:
She has interned at several companies. 她已经在几家公司实习过。
He has been interning for six months. 他已经实习了六个月。


过去完成时:
I had interned at a bank before I started my current job. 在我开始现在的工作之前，我在一家银行实习过。
They had already interned with that company before they applied. 在他们申请之前，他们已经在该公司实习过。


将来时:
I will be interning in London next year. 我明年将在伦敦实习。
She will intern at a design studio after graduation. 她毕业后将在设计工作室实习。


虚拟语气:
If I had interned at that company, I would have learned more. 如果我在那家公司实习过，我会学到更多。
I wish I were interning in a more challenging environment. 我希望我在一个更有挑战性的环境中实习。


被动语态:
Many students are interned by large companies every year. 每年许多学生都被大公司聘为实习生。
He was interned at a research facility. 他在一个研究机构实习。


疑问句:
Are you interning anywhere this summer? 你今年夏天在任何地方实习吗？
Where did she intern last year? 她去年在哪里实习？


否定句:
I am not interning this semester. 这个学期我没有实习。
He did not intern at that company. 他没有在那家公司实习。
