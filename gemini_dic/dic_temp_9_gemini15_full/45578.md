curls:/kɜːrlz/| n. v.|卷发；卷曲；蜷缩|英文解析：Curls can refer to a strand of hair that grows or is styled in a curved or spiral shape, or the act of something bending into a curved shape.  As a verb, it means to form into curls or to move in a curving way.|例句：Her long curls cascaded down her back. =她长长的卷发瀑布般垂在背上。  The smoke curled upwards into the air. = 烟向上盘旋到空中。|近义词：rings, ringlets, coils, twists|反义词：straighten, uncurl|
