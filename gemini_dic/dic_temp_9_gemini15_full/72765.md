fogie:/ˈfoʊdʒi/ | n. | 老顽固；守旧者 | An old-fashioned and often stubborn person, resistant to new ideas or ways of doing things.  | He's such a fogie; he refuses to use a computer. 他是个老顽固，拒绝使用电脑。| 近义词：old-timer, reactionary, fuddy-duddy, traditionalist | 反义词：modernist, progressive, innovator
