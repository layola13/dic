dorsal:/ˈdɔːrsəl/ | adj. | 背部的；背面的 | Relating to the back or upper surface of an animal, plant, or organ.  | 例句：The dorsal fin of the shark sliced through the water.  鲨鱼的背鳍划破水面。
The dorsal side of the leaf is darker green. 叶子背面是深绿色。|近义词：posterior, back, upper | 反义词：ventral, anterior, lower | 词性：形容词
