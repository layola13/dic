overdraw:/ˈoʊvərˈdrɔː/|/ˈəʊvəˈdrɔː/| vt. n.|透支；过度绘制|To withdraw more money from a bank account than is actually available, resulting in a negative balance.  It can also refer to exceeding a limit in drawing or painting.|例句：He overdrew his account and incurred a penalty fee. 他透支了账户，因此产生了罚款。

例句：The artist overdrew the lines, making the drawing less precise. 艺术家过度绘制线条，使图画不够精确。


近义词：exceed, overspend, overextend|反义词：underspend, underdraw|动词
