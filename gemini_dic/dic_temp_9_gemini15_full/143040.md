outblot:/ˈaʊtblɒt/| v.|冲淡；使…黯然失色|To surpass or overshadow something in importance or prominence.|The new discovery completely outblotted all previous research in the field.  新的发现完全使该领域以前的所有研究黯然失色。|近义词：eclipse, overshadow, surpass|反义词：enhance, emphasize
