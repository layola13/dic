chill:/tʃɪl/| n., v., adj.| 冷却；寒冷；轻松；放松；令人兴奋的人或事物；|

英文解析:As a noun, "chill" refers to a feeling of coldness or a cold feeling. As a verb, it means to become cold or to make something cold. As an adjective, it means cold, relaxed, or calm.  It can also describe something exciting or impressive.

例句:
The chill wind blew across the plains. = 刺骨的寒风吹过平原。

The soup needs to chill before serving. = 汤需要冷却后再食用。

Let's chill out and watch a movie. = 我们放松一下，看部电影吧。

He's a real chill guy. = 他是个很轻松随和的家伙。


近义词:cool, relax, calm,  (n.) coldness, frost


反义词:heat, warm, excitement (depending on context), tense


词性:noun, verb, adjective
