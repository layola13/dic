preobstructions:/ˌpriːəbˈstrʌkʃənz/| n.| 预先存在的障碍；先前的阻碍 |  Obstacles or impediments that exist before a particular action or process.  This word is not commonly used and is essentially a combination of "pre-" (before) and "obstructions."  It suggests obstacles encountered *before* the main event or problem. | The preobstructions to the project included securing funding and obtaining necessary permits.  = 项目开始前的障碍包括资金筹措和获得必要的许可证。


The team faced numerous preobstructions before they could even begin construction. =  团队在开始施工前就面临许多障碍。| 近义词：prior obstacles, preliminary hindrances, initial impediments | 反义词：facilitations, aids, assistances | 名词
