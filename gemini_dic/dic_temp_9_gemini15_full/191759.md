scink:/sɪŋk/| n.|石龙子|A skink is a lizard belonging to the family Scincidae.  Skinks are characterized by their smooth, often shiny scales that overlap like shingles. They are found worldwide in a variety of habitats.|Example:  The gardener spotted a bright green skink darting across the patio. 园丁看到一条亮绿色的石龙子飞快地穿过天井。|近义词：lizard (more general term)|反义词：无|名词
