go-ape:/ɡoʊ eɪp/| v.|发狂；暴怒；激动；|to behave in a wild and uncontrolled way, often because of anger or excitement|例句：He went ape when he heard the news. 他听到这个消息后就发狂了。
He went ape over the new toy. 他对新玩具兴奋不已。|近义词：go berserk, go crazy, lose it|反义词：remain calm, stay composed|词性:动词


USAGE->

I go ape when I’m stressed. = 我压力大的时候会发疯。

He went ape at the concert. = 他在音乐会上激动得发狂。

They've gone ape several times this week. = 他们本周已经好几次发疯了。
