oestruation:/ˌɛstrʊˈeɪʃən/| n. |动情期，发情期|The cyclical period of sexual receptivity in female mammals, characterized by physiological changes preparing the body for reproduction.  It involves hormonal fluctuations and changes in the reproductive organs.|例句：The bitch's oestruation cycle typically lasts around three weeks. 这条母狗的发情期通常持续大约三周。|例句：Understanding the timing of oestruation is crucial for successful breeding in animals. 了解发情期的时机对于动物的成功繁殖至关重要。|近义词：estrus, heat (in animals)|反义词：anestrus (absence of estrus)
