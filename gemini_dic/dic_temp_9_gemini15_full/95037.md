inaction:/ˈɪnæksʃən/| n. |不作为；无行动；怠慢|The absence of action; failure to act.  It can refer to a deliberate choice not to act, or a failure to act due to laziness, incompetence, or other reasons.|例句：His inaction led to the company's downfall. 他的不作为导致了公司的倒闭。

例句：The police's inaction in the face of the riot was criticized. 警方面对暴乱的不作为受到了批评。

近义词：passivity, inactivity, idleness, lethargy, inaction, negligence

反义词：action, activity, diligence
