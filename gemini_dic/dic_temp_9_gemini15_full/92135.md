hypersensibilities:/ˌhaɪpərˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪtiz/| n.|过敏症；过度敏感性|Hypersensibilities are exaggerated responses of the immune system to typically harmless substances.  These responses can range from mild discomfort to life-threatening anaphylaxis.|Example:  Many people suffer from food hypersensitivities, such as allergies to peanuts or shellfish.  许多人患有食物过敏症，例如对花生或贝类的过敏。|近义词:allergies, sensitivities, overreactions|反义词:hyposensitivities, insensitivities
