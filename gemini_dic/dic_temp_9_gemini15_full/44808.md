crusaded:/kruˈseɪdɪd/| v. |参加十字军东征；为…而斗争；积极从事| past tense and past participle of crusade.  To participate in a crusade; to campaign vigorously for a cause.  |例句：Many people crusaded against injustice. 许多人积极反对不公正。  He crusaded for better working conditions. 他为改善工作条件而奔走呼号。|近义词：campaigned, fought, championed |反义词：ignored, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He crusades for animal rights. = 他为动物权利而斗争。
They crusade against poverty. = 他们反对贫困。


一般过去时:
She crusaded for women's suffrage. = 她为妇女选举权而斗争。
They crusaded against corruption. = 他们反对腐败。


现在完成时:
He has crusaded for environmental protection for many years. = 他多年来一直为环境保护而奔走呼号。
They have crusaded against discrimination. = 他们一直在反对歧视。


过去完成时:
She had crusaded for social justice before she became a politician. = 在她成为政治家之前，她就一直在为社会正义而斗争。
They had crusaded against the war for years before it finally ended. = 在战争最终结束之前，他们已经为反对战争奔走呼号多年了。


将来时:
She will crusade for better education. = 她将为更好的教育而奋斗。
They will crusade against climate change. = 他们将反对气候变化。


虚拟语气:
If he had crusaded harder, he might have succeeded. = 如果他更努力地奋斗，他也许会成功。
I wish she had crusaded against that injustice. = 我真希望她能反对那种不公正。


被动语态:
The cause was crusaded for by many people. = 许多人为这项事业而奋斗。
The issue was crusaded against by several organizations. = 几个组织都反对这个问题。


疑问句:
Did they crusade against that policy? = 他们反对那项政策了吗？
Will he crusade for peace? = 他会为和平而奋斗吗？


否定句:
He did not crusade against the government. = 他没有反对政府。
They did not crusade for that cause. = 他们没有为那个事业而奋斗。


PHRASE->
crusade against = 反对…的运动
crusade for = 为…而斗争
a crusade of = 一场…的运动
launch a crusade = 发起一场运动
religious crusade = 宗教战争
