clear-ups:/ˈklɪərʌps/| n.|清理；澄清；解决|The process of cleaning or tidying something, or of making something clearer or easier to understand.  It can refer to the act of cleaning itself, or the result of the cleaning. |例句：The clear-ups after the party took hours.  派对后的清理工作花了几个小时。


例句：The police investigation aimed at a clear-up of the recent crime wave. 警方调查旨在解决最近的犯罪浪潮。


近义词：cleanings, tidy-ups, solutions, clarifications


反义词：messes, confusions, complications


词性：名词
