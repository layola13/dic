proverb:/ˈprɑːvɜːrb/ /ˈprɒvɜːb/| n. |谚语，格言|A proverb is a short, well-known saying that expresses a common truth or piece of advice.  Proverbs are often metaphorical and use imagery to convey meaning.|Example: "A bird in the hand is worth two in the bush."  (手里的一只鸟胜过树上两只鸟。)|近义词：saying, maxim, adage|反义词：无
