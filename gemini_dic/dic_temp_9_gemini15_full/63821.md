epididymis:/ˌɛpɪˈdɪdɪmɪs/ | n. | 附睾 | The epididymis is a long, coiled tube that sits on top of the testicle. It stores and matures sperm before they are ejaculated.  | Example: The doctor examined the patient's epididymis as part of the fertility evaluation. 医生检查了病人的附睾作为生育能力评估的一部分。 | 近义词:  (无直接近义词，可根据语境用testicle或spermatic duct代替部分含义) | 反义词: (无)
