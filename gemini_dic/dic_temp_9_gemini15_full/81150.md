grand's:/ɡrændz/| n.|奶奶的；祖父的；（指财产、金额等）巨大的|Grand's is a possessive form indicating belonging to a grandmother or grandfather. It's often used informally to refer to a grandmother's possessions, money, or property.  The word "grand" itself means impressive in size, scope, or extent.  |例句：We're going to spend Christmas at Grand's house. 我们将在奶奶家过圣诞节。|近义词：grandmother's, grandfather's, grandma's, grandpa's|反义词：N/A|名词
