cetacean:/sɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |鲸类动物|A cetacean is a marine mammal belonging to the order Cetacea, which includes whales, dolphins, and porpoises.  They are characterized by their streamlined bodies, powerful tails, and adaptations for aquatic life.|Example:  Whales, dolphins, and porpoises are all types of cetaceans.  鲸鱼、海豚和鼠海豚都是鲸类动物。|近义词：whale (in a general sense), dolphin (in a general sense), porpoise (in a general sense)|反义词：None (no direct antonym)|名词
