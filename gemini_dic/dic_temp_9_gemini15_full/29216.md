casketed:/ˈkæskɪtɪd/| adj.| 装入棺材的；被装入棺材的 | Placed in a casket; entombed.  The body was casketed and prepared for burial.  |例句：The casketed remains were transported to the cemetery.  遗体被装入棺材送往墓地。 The casketed remains lay in state for public viewing.  装在棺材里的遗体供公众瞻仰。|近义词：entombed, interred, encoffined |反义词：exhumed, unearthed
