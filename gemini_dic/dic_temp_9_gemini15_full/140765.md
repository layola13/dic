omnivorousness:/ˌɒmˈnɪvərəsnəs/ | /ˌɑːmˈnɪvərəsnəs/ | n. |杂食性；无所不食|The quality or state of being omnivorous; the habit of eating both plants and animals.  It refers to a diet that includes both plant and animal matter.| Example: The omnivorousness of humans allows for a varied and adaptable diet. 人类的杂食性使得他们能够拥有多样化且适应性强的饮食。The omnivorousness of raccoons makes them successful survivors in diverse environments.  浣熊的杂食性使它们能够在不同的环境中成功生存。|近义词：voraciousness, polyphagy|反义词：herbivorism, carnivorism
