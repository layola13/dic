overboldly:/ˌoʊvərˈboʊldli/ | adv. |过于大胆地，鲁莽地 |  Excessively bold; audacious; recklessly.  It implies a degree of boldness that is excessive or inappropriate, often leading to negative consequences. |例句：He overboldly challenged the authority of his superiors, resulting in his dismissal. 他过于大胆地挑战上司的权威，导致被解雇。 He overboldly entered the lion's den. 他鲁莽地闯入了狮子的巢穴。|近义词：rashly, recklessly, presumptuously, audaciously |反义词：cautiously, timidly, hesitantly |无词性
