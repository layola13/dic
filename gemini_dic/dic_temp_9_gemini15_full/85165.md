hardwoods:/ˈhɑːrdˌwʊdz/| n. |硬木；阔叶木|Hardwoods are dense, durable woods from broadleaf trees.  They are often used in furniture making and flooring.  |例句：The table is made of hardwoods, making it very strong and long-lasting. 这张桌子是用硬木做的，所以非常结实耐用。

例句：Oak and maple are examples of valuable hardwoods. 橡木和枫木是珍贵硬木的例子。


近义词：hard timber, broadleaf woods|反义词：softwoods
