raven:/ˈreɪvən/| n.|渡鸦，乌鸦|A large, all-black bird known for its intelligence and its deep, croaking call.  Ravens are often associated with mystery and folklore.|Example: The raven perched on the statue, its black feathers gleaming in the moonlight.  一只渡鸦栖息在雕像上，它黑色的羽毛在月光下闪闪发光。|近义词：crow, corvid|反义词：None.
