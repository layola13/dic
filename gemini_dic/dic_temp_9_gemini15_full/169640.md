proplastics: /ˌproʊˈplæstɪks/ | n. | 前体塑料 |  A material that is processed to become a plastic.  Proplastics are usually in a less-refined state compared to final plastic products and may require further processing, such as melting or molding, to achieve their desired form and properties. They are often used as raw materials in the manufacturing of various plastic items. |  The manufacturer uses various proplastics to create different types of plastic containers. 制造商使用各种前体塑料来制造不同类型的塑料容器。


近义词: pre-plastics, plastic precursors

反义词:  finished plastics, plastic products


词性: 名词
