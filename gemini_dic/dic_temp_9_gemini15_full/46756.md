dandyize:/ˈdændiˌaɪz/| vt.|使花俏；使打扮得漂亮；使变得时髦|To make or become excessively or affectedly elegant or fashionable; to adorn or decorate in a showy manner.|例句：He dandyized his appearance for the party. 他为了参加派对打扮得花枝招展。 He tried to dandyize his simple cottage. 他试图把他的简朴小屋打扮得漂漂亮亮的。|近义词：embellish, adorn, beautify|反义词：simplify, roughen
