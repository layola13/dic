engagements:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənts/ | n. | 约会；订婚；承诺；任务；占用 |  Engagements refer to several things:  a formal agreement to be married, a promise or commitment to do something,  a scheduled appointment or meeting, or the act of being involved or occupied. |


例句：

They announced their engagement at the party. = 他们在派对上宣布了他们的订婚。

She has many professional engagements this month. = 她这个月有很多职业上的约会。


近义词：appointments, commitments, promises, involvements


反义词：disengagements, cancellations, breaches (of promise)


词性：名词
