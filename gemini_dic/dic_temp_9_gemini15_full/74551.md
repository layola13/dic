fraud:/frɔːd/| n. |欺诈；骗局；诈骗犯 |The act of deceiving someone in order to gain something, usually money.  It involves dishonesty and illegal activity. |例句：He was convicted of fraud and sentenced to five years in prison. 他犯了欺诈罪，被判处五年监禁。

例句：The company was accused of insurance fraud.  这家公司被指控犯有保险欺诈罪。


近义词：deception, swindle, deceit, cheating, trickery |反义词：honesty, integrity, truthfulness |词性:名词
