preimpose:/ˌpriːɪmˈpoʊz/| vt.|预先强加；预先规定|To impose beforehand; to establish or ordain in advance.  The rules were preimposed before the game even started.  比赛开始前就预先规定了规则。|近义词：impose, predetermine, preordain|反义词：remove, abolish
