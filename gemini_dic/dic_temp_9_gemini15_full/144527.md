outvanish: /ˌaʊtˈvænɪʃ/ | v. |消失，绝迹| To disappear completely and permanently; to vanish utterly.  The word is a relatively rare and emphatic form, combining "out" (completely) and "vanish" (to disappear). |例句：The ancient civilization outvanished without a trace.  古老的文明彻底消失得无影无踪。  The hope of a peaceful resolution outvanished as the conflict escalated. 和平解决的希望随着冲突的升级而彻底破灭。|近义词：disappear, vanish, fade, evaporate|反义词：appear, emerge, materialize|动词
