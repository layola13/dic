irritancy:/ˈɪrɪtənsi/| n.|刺激性，炎症|The quality or state of being irritant; the power to irritate or cause inflammation.  It refers to the ability of a substance to cause irritation, often involving inflammation or discomfort. |例句：The irritancy of the detergent caused a rash on her hands.  (这种清洁剂的刺激性导致她手上起了皮疹。) The study assessed the ocular irritancy of the new chemical. (这项研究评估了这种新化学物质对眼睛的刺激性。) |近义词：irritation, inflammation, sting, chafing |反义词：soothing, calming
