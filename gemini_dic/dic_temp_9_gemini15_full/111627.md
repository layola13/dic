locality's: /ləʊˈkælətiz/ | n. | 地方的；当地特有的 |  The possessive form of "locality," referring to something belonging to or characteristic of a particular place or region.  It describes the specific features, characteristics, or attributes associated with a given area. | 例句：The locality's unique dialect is fascinating.  = 当地的独特方言令人着迷。

The locality's history is rich with tales of pirates. = 当地的历史充满了海盗故事。


近义词：region's, area's, district's, neighborhood's, place's

反义词：universal, global, worldwide


词性：名词(possessive form)
