morels:/ˈmɔːrəlz/| n. |羊肚菌|A morel is an edible mushroom with a distinctive honeycomb-like cap. They are highly prized for their unique flavor and texture.  Morels are typically found in spring, often in wooded areas. |例句：I found some delicious morels growing near the old oak tree. 我在古老的橡树附近发现了一些美味的羊肚菌。

例句：The chef prepared a gourmet dish featuring fresh morels.  厨师准备了一道以新鲜羊肚菌为特色的美食。
