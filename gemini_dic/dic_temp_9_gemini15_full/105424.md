kidnappings:/ˈkɪdnæpɪŋz/| n.|绑架（事件）|The plural form of "kidnapping," referring to multiple instances of abduction.  It denotes the act of unlawfully taking a person hostage, usually for ransom or other malicious intent. |例句：The recent increase in kidnappings has caused widespread fear in the community. 近期绑架事件增多，导致社区民众普遍感到恐惧。

例句：Police are investigating a series of kidnappings in the city. 警方正在调查该市发生的一系列绑架案。


近义词：abductions, seizures


反义词：releases (of hostages)


词性: 名词
