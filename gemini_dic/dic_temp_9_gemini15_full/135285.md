nonparticipants:/ˌnɒn.pɑːrˈtɪsɪpənts/ | n. |非参与者，未参与者 | People who did not take part in an activity or event.  | Example: The study focused on the attitudes of both participants and nonparticipants. 这项研究关注参与者和非参与者的态度。 |近义词: bystanders, onlookers, outsiders |反义词: participants, members, attendees
