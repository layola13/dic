debilitate:/dɪˈbɪlɪteɪt/ | vt. |使衰弱；使虚弱；削弱 | to make someone or something weaker and less effective |The illness debilitated him for months.  疾病使他虚弱了好几个月。|The long journey debilitated the travelers. 漫长的旅程使旅行者们疲惫不堪。|weaken, enfeeble, impair |strengthen, invigorate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The illness debilitates him. = 这场疾病使他虚弱。
Overwork debilitates many people. = 过度工作使许多人虚弱。


一般过去时:
The accident debilitated him for years. = 那次事故使他虚弱了好几年。
The drought debilitated the crops. = 干旱使庄稼枯萎。


现在完成时:
The illness has debilitated him significantly. = 这场疾病已使他严重虚弱。
Years of stress have debilitated her immune system. = 多年的压力已削弱了她的免疫系统。


过去完成时:
He had been debilitated by the disease before he sought treatment. = 他在就医之前就已经被疾病折磨得虚弱不堪了。
The army had been debilitated by the long campaign. = 这支军队在漫长的战役中已被削弱。


将来时:
The surgery will debilitate him for a few weeks. = 手术会使他虚弱几周。
The heatwave will debilitate the elderly. = 热浪会使老年人虚弱。


虚拟语气:
If he continued to work so hard, he would be debilitated. = 如果他继续如此努力地工作，他会变得虚弱。
I wish the illness hadn't debilitated him so severely. = 我真希望这场疾病没有让他如此虚弱。


被动语态:
He was debilitated by the heat. = 他被热坏了。
The army was debilitated by disease and famine. = 这支军队被疾病和饥荒削弱了。


疑问句:
Will the treatment debilitate him? = 治疗会使他虚弱吗？
Has the long journey debilitated the climbers? = 漫长的旅程是否使登山者们虚弱了？


否定句:
The exercise did not debilitate him; it strengthened him. = 运动并没有使他虚弱；反而使他强壮了。
The medicine did not debilitate her as much as the doctor had feared. = 药物并没有像医生担心的那样使她虚弱。



PHRASE->
be debilitated by = 被…削弱
debilitating illness = 衰弱性疾病
debilitating effects = 虚弱的影响
severely debilitated = 严重虚弱
debilitating fatigue = 令人虚弱的疲劳
