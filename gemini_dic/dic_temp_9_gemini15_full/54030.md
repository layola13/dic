discursus:/dɪˈskɜːrsəs/| n. |论述；谈话；讨论|A discursive essay or speech covers a range of points, often without a clear structure.  A discursive conversation meanders from topic to topic.|例句：His discursive style made the lecture difficult to follow. 他的散漫的风格使得讲座难以理解。  The two friends engaged in a long and discursive conversation. 这两个朋友进行了长时间的漫谈。|近义词：discourse, discussion, conversation, treatise|反义词：conciseness, brevity|名词
