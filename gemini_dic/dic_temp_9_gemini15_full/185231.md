retractor's: /rɪˈtræktər/ | n. | 牵引器；拉钩 | A surgical instrument used to retract or hold back tissue or organs during an operation, providing better access to the surgical site.  | Example: The surgeon used a retractor to keep the wound open during the procedure. 外科医生在手术过程中使用拉钩来保持伤口张开。 | 近义词：retracter, spreader  | 反义词：None (反义词概念与功能相反，而非词义相反) | 名词
