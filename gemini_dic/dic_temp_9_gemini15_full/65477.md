europan:/jʊˈroʊpən/| n. |木卫二；欧罗巴|A moon of Jupiter, also known as Jupiter II. It is the fourth largest of Jupiter's moons and is known for its icy surface and potential for subsurface oceans.  |Example:  Scientists believe that Europan's subsurface ocean could potentially harbor life.  科学家相信木卫二的地下海洋可能潜藏着生命。|近义词：Jupiter II|反义词：无
