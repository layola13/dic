oystercatchers:/ˈɔɪstərkætʃərz/ | n. |蛎鹬（鸟类）|Oystercatchers are a group of wading birds in the family Haematopodidae.  They are characterized by their long, orange or red bills, used for prying open shellfish. |例句：The oystercatchers were feeding on the mudflats.  蛎鹬在泥滩上觅食。|例句： We watched a flock of oystercatchers searching for food along the shoreline. 我们观察到一群蛎鹬沿着海岸线觅食。|近义词：shorebird, wader |反义词：无
