conceiver:/kənˈsiːvər/| n. |构想者；设想者；孕育者 |One who conceives; one who forms a plan or idea.  A conceiver is someone who originates or develops an idea, plan, or invention. They are creative and imaginative individuals who bring forth new concepts. |例句：The conceiver of the project deserves much credit for its success.  该项目的构想者应为其成功而获得大量赞誉。

例句：She is a brilliant conceiver, always coming up with innovative ideas. 她是一位杰出的构想者，总是能想出创新的主意。


近义词：inventor, originator, creator, innovator

反义词：destroyer, terminator, obliterator


词性:名词


USAGE->

The team conceives a new marketing strategy. = 团队构想了一个新的营销策略。
He conceives innovative solutions to complex problems. = 他构想出解决复杂问题的创新方案。

现在完成时:

虚拟语气:
