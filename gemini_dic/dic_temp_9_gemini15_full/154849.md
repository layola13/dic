pervadingly:/pərˈveɪdɪŋli/ | adv. | 普遍地；弥漫地；渗透地 |  Describes something that is widespread and influential, affecting all parts of something. It suggests a gradual and pervasive influence. |例句：The feeling of anxiety pervadingly affected the team's performance. 焦虑的情绪普遍影响了团队的表现。

The aroma of freshly baked bread pervadingly filled the bakery. 新鲜烘焙面包的香味弥漫在整个面包房。 | 近义词：widely, universally, thoroughly, comprehensively | 反义词：locally, sparsely, superficially
