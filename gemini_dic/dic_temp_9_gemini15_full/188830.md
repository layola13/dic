rushlight:/ˈrʌʃlaɪt/| n. | 芦苇灯；微弱的光 | A rushlight is a small lamp, typically made from a rush (a type of plant) dipped in tallow or grease, providing a dim light.  It was a common source of lighting in the past, particularly among poorer people. | Example: The only light in the room came from a flickering rushlight.  房间里唯一的灯光来自一盏忽明忽暗的芦苇灯。|近义词：candle, lamp (in a historical context) |反义词：bright light, strong light |名词
