garret:/ˈɡærɪt/| n.|阁楼，顶楼房间|A garret is a room at the top of a house, especially a small and poorly lit one.  It is often used as a store room or living quarters for poor people.|例句：The artist lived in a cramped garret, filled with canvases and paint.|近义词：attic, loft|反义词：basement, cellar
