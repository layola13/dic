haplessness:/ˈhæpləsnəs/| n. |不幸；厄运；霉运 |The state of being hapless; misfortune; ill luck.  Haplessness often involves a lack of control over one's circumstances, leading to undesirable outcomes.  It suggests a passive acceptance of misfortune rather than active resistance or fault. |例句：His haplessness in love was legendary.  他的爱情不幸是出了名的。
His haplessness led to him losing his job and his apartment. 他的霉运导致他丢了工作和公寓。|近义词：misfortune, ill luck, adversity, bad luck |反义词：good fortune, luck, success, prosperity |词性:名词
