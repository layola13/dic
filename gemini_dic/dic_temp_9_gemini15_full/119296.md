micro-machining: /ˈmaɪkroʊ ˈmæʃɪnɪŋ/ | n. |微细加工，微加工|The process of creating very small features and structures, typically on the scale of micrometers, using specialized techniques.  This often involves removing material from a substrate to create intricate designs.  Applications are widespread in various fields, including microelectronics, biomedical devices, and sensors.|例句：Micro-machining is used to create miniature sensors for medical applications. 微细加工用于制造用于医疗应用的微型传感器。

例句：The intricate gears in the micro-robot were produced through precise micro-machining. 微型机器人中复杂的齿轮是通过精确的微细加工制造的。


近义词：microfabrication, micromilling, micro-manufacturing|反义词：macro-machining, large-scale machining|词性:noun
