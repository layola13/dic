desoldering:/ˌdiːˈsɒldərɪŋ/| v.|脱焊，除焊|The process of removing solder from a joint.  It is the opposite of soldering.|例句：The technician carefully performed the desoldering of the faulty component. 技师仔细地进行了故障组件的脱焊操作。


例句：We need specialized tools for efficient desoldering of surface mount components. 我们需要专业的工具才能有效地去除表面贴装元件的焊锡。

反义词：soldering

词源:
The word "desoldering" is a combination of the prefix "de-" (meaning "to remove" or "to undo") and the word "soldering," which refers to the process of joining metal using solder.  Therefore, desoldering means the removal of solder.
