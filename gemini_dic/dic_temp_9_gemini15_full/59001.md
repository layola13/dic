dux:/dʌks/ | n. |领袖，领导者，优秀学生|A dux is the top-ranking student in a class or school.  The term is primarily used in some parts of the world, particularly in Australia, New Zealand, and South Africa.| Example: She was the dux of her graduating class. 她是她毕业班的优秀毕业生。|近义词：leader, top student, scholar |反义词：underachiever, laggard
