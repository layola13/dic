例句：Her constant caviling made it impossible to work with her. 她不断的吹毛求疵让人无法和她一起工作。


近义词：criticism, nitpicking, quibbling, faultfinding | 反义词：praise, commendation, approval | 词性：名词


词源:  The word "caviling" comes from the Middle English word "cavillen," which itself is derived from the Old French word "caviller," meaning "to quibble" or "to find fault."  The ultimate origin is likely from the Latin "cavilla," meaning "jest" or "trick," suggesting an origin in playful but ultimately petty argumentation.
