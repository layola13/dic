gunny-bags:/ˈɡʌni bæɡz/| n.|麻袋；黄麻袋|Gunny bags are sturdy bags made from jute, a natural fiber.  They are commonly used for storing and transporting various goods.|例句：The potatoes were stored in large gunny bags. 土豆储存在大的麻袋里。
The farmer used gunny bags to carry his harvest. 农民用麻袋装着他的收成。|近义词：hessian bags, burlap sacks|反义词：refined packaging, elegant containers|名词
