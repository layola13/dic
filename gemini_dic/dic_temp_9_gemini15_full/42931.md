covenant:/ˈkʌvənənt/ | n. v. |圣约；契约；协议；约定 | A covenant is a formal agreement, especially one that is made in writing and has legal force. It can refer to a solemn agreement between God and humanity, or a formal contract between individuals or groups.  |例句：The two countries signed a covenant to cooperate on environmental issues.  两个国家签署了一项关于环境问题的合作协定。

The ancient covenant between God and his people is described in the Bible. 圣经中描述了上帝和他的子民之间的古老约定。


近义词：agreement, contract, pact, treaty | 反义词：breach, violation

词性：名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They covenant to support each other. = 他们约定互相支持。
The company covenants to protect customer data. = 公司承诺保护客户数据。


一般过去时:
They covenanted to meet the deadline. = 他们约定在截止日期前完成。
The two nations covenanted peace. = 这两个国家缔结了和平条约。


现在完成时:
We have covenanted to uphold our values. = 我们已经约定要坚持我们的价值观。
The parties have covenanted to resolve their disputes peacefully. = 各方已约定以和平方式解决争议。


过去完成时:
They had covenanted to share the profits equally. = 他们曾经约定平均分配利润。
The tribes had covenanted to defend each other against attack. = 各部落曾约定互相防御攻击。


将来时:
We will covenant to work together towards a common goal. = 我们将约定为共同目标而一起努力。
They will covenant to abide by the terms of the agreement. = 他们将约定遵守协议条款。


虚拟语气:
If they covenanted to help, it would make a huge difference. = 如果他们约定帮忙，这将会有很大的不同。
I wish they had covenanted to be more responsible. = 我希望他们当时已经约定承担更多责任。


被动语态:
The agreement was covenanted by both parties. = 协议得到了双方的约定。
The land was covenanted to the church for charitable purposes. = 这块土地被约定用于教会的慈善事业。


疑问句:
Did they covenant to keep the secret? = 他们是否约定保守秘密？
Has the company covenanted to provide after-sales service? = 公司是否约定提供售后服务？


否定句:
They did not covenant to share the information. = 他们没有约定分享信息。
The parties have not covenanted to extend the contract. = 各方没有约定延长合同。


PHRASE->
break a covenant = 违反约定
enter into a covenant = 订立契约
covenant of grace = 恩典之约
covenant of works = 行为之约
covenant marriage = 盟约婚姻
