dud:/dʌd/| n. |废物，无用之物，哑弹|A dud is something that fails to function as expected, especially a device that fails to explode.  It can also refer to a person who is incompetent or unsuccessful. |例句：The firework was a dud and didn't go off. 这支烟花是哑弹，没有爆炸。 The new software turned out to be a complete dud.  这个新软件结果完全是废物。|近义词：failure, flop, lemon, bomb|反义词：success, masterpiece, hit

This plan is a dud.  = 这个计划失败了。
