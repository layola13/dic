It often refers to a method of writing or representation that focuses on individual instances or cases rather than general principles or rules.  | Example:  The researcher adopted an idiographic approach, focusing on the unique experiences of each participant in the study.  研究人员采用了一种特质描述法，重点关注研究中每个参与者的独特经历。|近义词：descriptive, particularistic |反义词：nomothetic, generalizing
