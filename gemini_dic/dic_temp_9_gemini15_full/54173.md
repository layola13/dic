disentwine:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtwaɪn/| vt.|解开；梳理；区分|To separate or untangle something that is intertwined or tangled.  The act of disentangling something that is twisted or knotted together.|例句：He carefully disentwined the headphone wires. 他小心地解开了耳机线。

例句：She disentwined the truth from the lies. 她从谎言中理清了真相。


近义词：untangle, unravel, unknot, separate, extricate|反义词：entangle, intertwine, knot, complicate
