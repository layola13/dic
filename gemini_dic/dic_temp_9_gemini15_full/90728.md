humiliating: /hjuːˈmɪlieɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人羞辱的，丢脸的 |  causing someone to feel ashamed and foolish by injuring their dignity and self-respect.  | The humiliating defeat left the team demoralized. 令人羞辱的失败使球队士气低落。  | shameful, mortifying, embarrassing, degrading | uplifting, honoring, flattering |
