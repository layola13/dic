presided:/prɪˈzaɪdɪd/ | v. |主持，担任主席 |To be in charge of a meeting, ceremony, or other event.  |例句：The judge presided over the trial.  法官主持了审判。
|例句：The chairman presided at the meeting. 主席主持了会议。|近义词：chair, lead, govern, conduct |反义词：participate (as a member, not leader),  obey (if referring to someone who usually presides)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The judge presides over the court. = 法官主持法庭。
She presides at the board meetings. = 她主持董事会会议。

一般过去时:
He presided over the ceremony. = 他主持了仪式。
The mayor presided at the town hall meeting. = 市长主持了市政厅会议。

现在完成时:
She has presided over many important events. = 她主持过许多重要的活动。
He has presided at the committee for five years. = 他担任该委员会主席五年了。

过去完成时:
The previous chairman had presided over the company for ten years. = 前任主席曾主持公司十年。
Before the new judge arrived, the veteran judge had presided over many cases. = 在新法官到来之前，资深法官已主持过许多案件。

将来时:
The new president will preside over the inauguration ceremony. = 新总统将主持就职典礼。
She will preside at the next conference. = 她将主持下一次会议。

虚拟语气:
If he were here, he would preside over the debate. = 如果他在这儿，他会主持辩论。
I wish the experienced lawyer had presided over the trial. = 我希望经验丰富的律师主持了审判。

被动语态:
The meeting was presided over by the CEO. = 会议由首席执行官主持。
The ceremony will be presided over by a distinguished guest. = 典礼将由一位尊贵的客人主持。

疑问句:
Who presided over the meeting? = 谁主持了会议？
Will the headmaster preside at the graduation ceremony? = 校长将主持毕业典礼吗？

否定句:
He did not preside at the meeting; he was just a participant. = 他没有主持会议；他只是一个参与者。
The judge did not preside over the case due to a conflict of interest. = 由于利益冲突，法官没有主持此案。


PHRASE->
preside over = 主持
preside at = 主持
preside in = 在…主持
preside upon = 在…主持
preside the meeting = 主持会议

词源:
From Latin praesidere "to sit before, preside," from prae- "before" + sedēre "to sit."  The sense development is from "sitting in a place of authority" to "being in charge".
