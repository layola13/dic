opiniative:/əˈpɪniətiv/| adj.|固执己见的；武断的；专横的|Expressing opinions firmly and often unreasonably.  It implies a strong tendency to impose one's views on others, often without considering other perspectives.  |例句：He's very opiniative and refuses to listen to anyone else's point of view. 他非常固执己见，拒绝听取任何人的意见。

例句：Her opiniative nature often led to conflict with her colleagues. 她的固执己见经常导致与同事发生冲突。


近义词：dogmatic, opinionated, assertive, peremptory, dictatorial


反义词：open-minded, receptive, tolerant, flexible


词性:形容词
