conceptualizer:/kənˈsɛpʃuəlaɪzər/| n. |概念化者，概念制定者 | A person who forms or develops concepts; someone who creates or clarifies abstract ideas.  |The architect acted as a conceptualizer, translating the client's vision into a tangible design.  建筑师扮演着概念化者的角色，将客户的愿景转化为具体的方案。| theorist, innovator, originator |  (反义词较难直接对应，可考虑与具体语境下的反义词搭配，例如：practical worker, implementer) | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The conceptualizer designs innovative ideas. = 概念化者设计创新的想法。
He conceptualizes new marketing strategies. = 他构思新的营销策略。


一般过去时:
The artist conceptualized a powerful new painting. = 艺术家构思了一幅有力的新画作。
She conceptualized the project's overall direction. = 她构思了项目的总体方向。


现在完成时:
They have conceptualized several successful products. = 他们已经构思出几种成功的产品。
The team has conceptualized a groundbreaking solution. = 团队已经构思出一个突破性的解决方案。


过去完成时:
He had conceptualized the plan before presenting it. = 他在提出计划之前就已经构思好了。
They had conceptualized the campaign for months. = 他们已经构思这个活动好几个月了。


将来时:
She will conceptualize the next advertising campaign. = 她将构思下一个广告活动。
We will conceptualize a new approach to the problem. = 我们将构思解决这个问题的新方法。


虚拟语气:
If he were a conceptualizer, he would create groundbreaking designs. = 如果他是概念化者，他会创造突破性的设计。
I wish I could conceptualize ideas as effectively as he does. = 我希望我能像他一样有效地构思想法。


被动语态:
The project was conceptualized by a team of experts. = 这个项目是由一个专家团队构思的。
New ideas are being conceptualized daily. = 每天都在构思新的想法。


疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't conceptualize the plan properly. = 他没有恰当地构思计划。
They hadn't conceptualized the potential risks. = 他们没有预想到潜在的风险。
