ingestions:/ɪnˈdʒɛʃənz/ | n. |摄入；吞咽；吸收|The act of taking something, especially food, into the body by swallowing or absorbing it.  It often refers to the process of taking in substances, whether intentionally or accidentally.|例句：The doctor warned about the dangers of accidental ingestions of cleaning fluids. 医生警告了意外摄入清洁液的危险。

例句：The patient's symptoms suggested a possible ingestion of poison. 病人的症状表明可能摄入了毒药。


近义词：absorption, swallowing, consumption |反义词：excretion, expulsion, ejection |词性:名词
