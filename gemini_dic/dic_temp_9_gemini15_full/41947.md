cosmopolitism:/ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtɪzəm/ | n. |世界主义；国际主义；世界性；大都会特性 |The ideology that all human beings belong to a single community, based on a shared morality.  It emphasizes a global perspective, interconnectedness, and the transcendence of national boundaries. |例句：Her cosmopolitan outlook was shaped by years of living abroad.她的世界主义观点是多年在国外生活形成的。

例句：The city's cosmopolitan atmosphere attracted people from all over the world.这座城市的世界性氛围吸引了来自世界各地的人们。


近义词：internationalism, globalism, universalism |反义词：provincialism, nationalism, parochialism
