misos:/ˈmiːsoʊz/| n.|味噌|A thick, brown paste made from fermented soybeans, rice, barley, salt, and a type of koji (a fungus). It is a staple ingredient in Japanese cuisine, adding a savory umami flavor to soups, sauces, and marinades.|例句：This miso soup is incredibly flavorful. 这味噌汤非常美味。|近义词：paste, condiment|反义词:N/A
