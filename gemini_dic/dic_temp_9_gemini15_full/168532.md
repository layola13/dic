procapitalist: /ˌproʊˈkæpɪtəlɪst/ | adj. | 亲资本主义的，支持资本主义的 |  Favoring or supporting capitalism.  This term describes individuals, policies, or ideologies that advocate for or promote the principles and practices of capitalism, often emphasizing free markets, private property, and entrepreneurship. | Example: His procapitalist views were evident in his support for deregulation.  他的亲资本主义观点在他的支持放松管制中显而易见。 | 近义词: capitalist, pro-market, free-market | 反义词: anticapitalist, socialist, communist |
