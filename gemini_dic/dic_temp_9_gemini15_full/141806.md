orbit:/ˈɔːrbɪt/| n. v.|轨道；运行轨道；绕…运行|The path of a celestial body or artificial satellite as it revolves around another of greater mass.  It can also refer to a sphere of influence or activity.|例句：The Earth orbits the Sun. 地球绕太阳运行。
The satellite is in a stable orbit around the planet. 这颗卫星绕行星运行轨道稳定。|近义词：path, course, revolution|反义词：stationary, still|名词，动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The moon orbits the Earth. = 月球绕地球运行。
The planets orbit the sun. = 行星绕太阳运行。

一般过去时:
The satellite orbited the Earth for many years. = 这颗卫星绕地球运行多年。
The spacecraft successfully orbited Mars. = 宇宙飞船成功绕火星运行。

现在完成时:
The spacecraft has orbited the planet several times. = 宇宙飞船已经绕该行星运行了好几次。
The satellite has completed its orbit. = 卫星已经完成了它的轨道运行。

过去完成时:
The comet had orbited the sun many times before its final approach. = 这颗彗星在其最后一次接近太阳之前已经绕太阳运行了许多次。
The probe had already orbited Jupiter before transmitting the data. = 探测器在传输数据之前已经绕木星运行。

将来时:
The new satellite will orbit the Earth at a higher altitude. = 新卫星将在更高的轨道上绕地球运行。
The spacecraft will orbit the moon before landing. = 宇宙飞船将在着陆前绕月球运行。

虚拟语气:
If the satellite were to orbit closer, it would collect more data. = 如果卫星能更近地运行，它就能收集更多数据。
I wish the planet orbited faster. = 我希望这颗行星运行得更快。

被动语态:
The Earth is orbited by the moon. = 地球被月球绕着转。
The planet was orbited by a spacecraft last year. = 这颗行星去年被一艘宇宙飞船环绕。

疑问句:
Does the moon orbit the Earth? = 月球绕地球运行吗？
How long will the satellite orbit the planet? = 卫星将绕行星运行多久？

否定句:
The comet did not orbit the sun. = 这颗彗星没有绕太阳运行。
The satellite does not orbit the Earth anymore. = 卫星不再绕地球运行。
