overhunting:/ˌoʊvərˈhʌntɪŋ/| n. |过度狩猎|The act of hunting to an excessive degree, resulting in the depletion or extinction of a species or population.  Overhunting is a major threat to many wildlife populations globally.  |Example: Overhunting of whales in the 19th century nearly led to their extinction.|近义词：excessive hunting, poaching, depletion|反义词：conservation, protection, preservation|名词
