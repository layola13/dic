hungered:/ˈhʌŋɡərd/| v.|渴望，感到饥饿|To experience a strong desire or need, often for food but also for other things.|例句：The refugees hungered for freedom. 难民渴望自由。
例句：After a long hike, we hungered for a hot meal. 长途跋涉后，我们渴望一顿热饭。|近义词：craved, yearned, longed|反义词：satisfied, satiated


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He hungers for knowledge. 他渴望知识。
They hunger for power. 他们渴望权力。


一般过去时:
I hungered for a taste of home. 我渴望尝到家乡的味道。
She hungered for her mother's love. 她渴望得到母亲的爱。


现在完成时:
He has hungered for success his whole life. 他一生都在渴望成功。
They have hungered for recognition for years. 他们多年来一直渴望得到认可。


过去完成时:
Having hungered for days, they finally found food. 挨饿多日后，他们终于找到了食物。
She had hungered for a child for many years before she adopted one. 在收养孩子之前，她多年来一直渴望拥有一个孩子。


将来时:
I will hunger for adventure when I retire. 我退休后会渴望冒险。
They will hunger for change after this election. 这次选举后，他们会渴望改变。


虚拟语气:
If I were in his place, I would hunger for justice. 如果我处在他的位置，我会渴望正义。
I wish I didn't hunger for things I can't have. 我希望我不渴望我得不到的东西。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
His desires were rarely hungered for by others. 他的愿望很少被别人渴望。 (This sentence is awkward and uncommon.)


疑问句:
Do you hunger for something more in your life? 你生活中渴望得到更多的东西吗？
Does he hunger for approval? 他渴望得到认可吗？


否定句:
I do not hunger for fame. 我不渴望名利。
They did not hunger for material possessions. 他们不渴望物质财富。


PHRASE->
hunger for: 渴望...
hunger strike: 绝食
a hunger pang: 一阵饥饿感
to satisfy one's hunger: 满足饥饿
feel a hunger: 感觉饥饿
