ethenoid:/ˈiːθɪnɔɪd/ | adj. | 乙烯的；含乙烯基的 | Relating to or denoting the presence of an ethene (ethylene) group, C=C, in a molecule.  Ethenoid compounds are unsaturated hydrocarbons containing a carbon-carbon double bond.  | Example:  The ethenoid linkage in the molecule is crucial for its reactivity.  乙烯键在分子中的存在对其反应性至关重要。 |近义词： unsaturated (in this context) | 反义词： saturated (in this context)
