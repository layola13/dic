look after: /ˌlʊkˈæftər/ | v. |照顾，照料，负责 | To care for someone or something.  |例句：She looks after her elderly mother. 她照顾她年迈的母亲。|例句：Could you look after my bag while I go to the restroom?  你能帮我照看一下我的包吗？我去趟洗手间。|近义词：take care of, care for, attend to |反义词：neglect, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I look after my pet dog every day. = 我每天都照顾我的宠物狗。
They look after their children very well. = 他们把孩子们照顾得很好。

一般过去时:
She looked after her sick neighbor last week. = 上周她照顾了她生病的邻居。
He looked after the house while his parents were away. = 他父母不在的时候，他负责看家。


现在完成时:
I have looked after this plant for five years. = 我已经照顾这株植物五年了。
They have looked after their grandparents for many years. = 他们照顾祖父母很多年了。


过去完成时:
She had looked after the children all day before her husband came home. = 她丈夫回家前，她已经照顾孩子们一整天了。
He had looked after the business for ten years before he retired. = 他退休前已经经营这家公司十年了。


将来时:
I will look after your cat while you are on vacation. = 你度假期间我会照顾你的猫。
She will look after the baby tomorrow. = 她明天会照顾婴儿。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would look after more animals. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会照顾更多的动物。
I wish I could look after my sick friend better. = 我希望我能更好地照顾我生病的朋友。


被动语态:
The children are well looked after at the daycare center. = 孩子们在日托中心得到很好的照顾。
The plants were looked after by the gardener. = 植物由园丁照料。


疑问句:

否定句:
I don't look after anyone else's pets. = 我不照顾别人的宠物。
He didn't look after the equipment properly. = 他没有正确地保管设备。
