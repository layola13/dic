mythologies:/mɪˈθɒlədʒiz/ | n. |神话学；神话故事集 | The study and collection of myths from various cultures.  Mythologies often reveal a culture's values and beliefs. |例句：Greek mythologies are well-known for their gods and heroes.  希腊神话以其众神和英雄而闻名。

例句：Comparative mythologies explore common themes across different cultures. 比较神话学探索不同文化中的共同主题。


近义词：myths, legends, folktales | 反义词：reality, facts
