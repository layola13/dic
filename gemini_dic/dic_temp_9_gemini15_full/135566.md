nonpoliticization: /ˌnɒnˌpɒlətɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |非政治化；避免政治化 | The process of removing something from political influence or control; preventing something from becoming politicized.  It emphasizes keeping an issue or organization free from partisan politics or undue political interference. | Example:  The nonpoliticization of the judiciary is crucial for maintaining public trust.  司法部门的非政治化对于维护公众信任至关重要。 | 近义词：depoliticization, apoliticism | 反义词：politicization
