provenience: /prəˈviːnjəns/ | n. | 出处；起源；来源 | The place of origin or discovery of an artifact.  It refers to the history of an object's location, including where it was found and how it got there.  This is especially important in archaeology and art history. | Example: The provenience of the ancient pottery was meticulously documented.  古代陶器的出处被仔细记录了下来。


The provenience of the artifact is uncertain.  该文物的来源不明确。| 近义词：origin, source, derivation | 反义词：destination, terminus
