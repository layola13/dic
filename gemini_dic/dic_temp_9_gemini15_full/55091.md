disrespectful: /ˌdɪsrɪˈspektfəl/ | adj. | 不尊重的，无礼的 | Showing a lack of respect.  |例句：His disrespectful remarks offended many people.  他的不尊重言论冒犯了许多人。|近义词：rude, impolite, discourteous, insolent, contemptuous |反义词：respectful, polite, courteous


USAGE->

Their behavior is disrespectful and unacceptable. = 他们的行为不尊重且不可接受。


一般过去时:
