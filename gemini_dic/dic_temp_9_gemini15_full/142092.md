orientalist:/ˌɔːriənˈtælɪst/| n. adj.|东方学者；东方主义者；具有东方主义特征的 |A person who studies or writes about the cultures and societies of the East, often from a Western perspective.  The term can be used neutrally, but often carries a negative connotation due to its historical association with colonialist attitudes and biases. |例句：Edward Said's book "Orientalism" is a seminal work on the subject.  爱德华·萨义德的著作《东方学》是关于这一主题的开创性著作。

He is a renowned orientalist, specializing in ancient Persian literature. 他是一位著名的东方学者，专门研究古代波斯文学。


近义词：scholar, expert, specialist (in Eastern studies) |反义词：N/A (the antonym depends on the context and whether the positive or negative connotation is emphasized) |词性：n. adj.
