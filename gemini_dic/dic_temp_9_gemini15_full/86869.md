hemin:/ˈhiːmɪn/| n. |血红素|Iron-containing porphyrin complex that's the oxygen-carrying component of hemoglobin.  It's a crucial part of red blood cells and plays a vital role in transporting oxygen throughout the body. Hemin is also important in certain diagnostic tests and has potential applications in medicine. |The doctor ordered a hemin test to determine the cause of the patient's anemia.|近义词：heme|反义词：无
