elated:/ɪˈleɪtɪd/| adj.|兴高采烈的，欣喜若狂的|Feeling or expressing great happiness or triumph.|She was elated at the news of her promotion. 她听到晋升的消息后欣喜若狂。|近义词：joyful, thrilled, delighted, ecstatic|反义词：depressed, despondent, dejected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is elated about his success. = 他对自己的成功感到兴高采烈。
They are elated to hear the good news. = 他们听到好消息后非常兴奋。

一般过去时:
She was elated after winning the race. = 她在赢得比赛后非常兴奋。
We were elated by the beautiful scenery. = 我们被美丽的景色所陶醉。


现在完成时:
I have been elated ever since I received the award. = 自从获得奖项以来，我一直兴高采烈。
They have been elated by the positive feedback. = 他们对积极的反馈感到非常兴奋。


过去完成时:
He had been elated all day until he received the bad news. = 他一整天都兴高采烈，直到他收到坏消息。
She had been elated about the trip until it was canceled. = 她对这次旅行感到很兴奋，直到它被取消。


将来时:
We will be elated when we finally graduate. = 当我们最终毕业时，我们会非常兴奋。
She will be elated to see her family again. = 她再次见到家人会非常高兴。


虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would be elated. = 如果我中了彩票，我会欣喜若狂。
I wish I were elated like that. = 我希望我能像那样兴奋。


被动语态:
He was elated by the unexpected gift. = 他因为意外的礼物而兴高采烈。（被动语态不太常用）
The team was elated by their victory. = 球队因胜利而兴高采烈。（被动语态不太常用）


疑问句:
Are you elated about the upcoming trip? = 你对即将到来的旅行感到兴奋吗？
Is he elated by the news? = 他对这个消息感到兴奋吗？


否定句:
She wasn't elated by the results. = 她对结果并不兴奋。
He is not elated at all. = 他一点也不兴奋。


PHRASE->
be elated by/at/with = 因…而兴高采烈
feel elated = 感到兴奋
become elated = 变得兴奋
remain elated = 保持兴奋
utterly elated = 极其兴奋
