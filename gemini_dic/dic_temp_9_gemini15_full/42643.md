countersigned:/ˈkaʊntərsaɪnd/ | vt. | 副署，共同签署 | To sign (a document) in addition to another signature, typically to indicate approval or authentication.  | Example: The contract was countersigned by the CEO to show her approval.  这份合同由首席执行官副署以示批准。 | 近义词：cosigned, endorsed | 反义词：unsigned
