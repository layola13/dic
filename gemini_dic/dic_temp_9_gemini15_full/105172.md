keverchief:/ˈkɛtʃɪf/| n. |方巾，头巾|A kerchief is a square piece of cloth, typically folded and tied around the head or neck.  It can be made of various materials and is often used for decoration or protection from the sun or cold.|She wore a silk kerchief to protect her hair from the sun. 她戴着丝绸方巾以保护头发不受阳光照射。| headscarf, scarf |  (无明确反义词)|名词
