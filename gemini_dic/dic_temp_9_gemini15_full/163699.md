prededicate:/ˌpriːˈdɛdɪkeɪt/| vt.|预先奉献；预先献身于|To dedicate something beforehand; to commit oneself to something in advance.|例句：He prededicated his life to the pursuit of knowledge. 他预先将自己的一生奉献给了知识的追求。|例句：The artist prededicated her new painting to the memory of her grandmother.  这位艺术家预先将她的新画作献给祖母的纪念。|近义词：dedicate, commit, consecrate |反义词：neglect, abandon|动词
