nonspontaneous: /ˌnɒnˌspɒnˈteɪniəs/ /ˌnɑːnˌspɑːnˈteɪniəs/ | adj. |非自发的；非自愿的；自然的|Not occurring naturally or spontaneously; requiring external energy or force.  It describes processes or events that do not occur on their own but need an external push or input to happen.|


The formation of rust on iron is a spontaneous process, while the decomposition of rust back into iron and oxygen is a nonspontaneous process.  铁生锈是一个自发过程，而锈分解成铁和氧气则是一个非自发过程。


The climb up the mountain was nonspontaneous; we had to exert considerable effort.  爬山不是自发的；我们必须付出相当大的努力。


近义词：forced, unnatural, deliberate, contrived


反义词：spontaneous, natural, automatic
