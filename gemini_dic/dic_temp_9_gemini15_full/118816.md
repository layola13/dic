metamorphoses:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəʊsiːz/| n. |变形；蜕变；变态；(尤指昆虫的)变态过程 |The process of transformation from an immature form to an adult form in two or more distinct stages.  It often refers to a marked change in appearance, character, or circumstances.  In literature, it often describes a significant change in a character's personality or a story's plot.|例句：The caterpillar underwent a complete metamorphosis to become a butterfly.  毛毛虫经历了完全变态，变成了蝴蝶。

The novel charts the hero's metamorphoses from a naive youth to a seasoned warrior. 这部小说描绘了主人公从天真少年到经验丰富的战士的蜕变过程。

|近义词：transformation, transfiguration, mutation, evolution |反义词：stasis, stagnation
