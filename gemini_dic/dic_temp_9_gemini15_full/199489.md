sindhis:/ˈsɪndhiz/| n. |信德人；信德语|Sindhis are an ethnolinguistic group primarily inhabiting the Sindh province of Pakistan and parts of India.  They speak Sindhi, a language of the Indo-Aryan branch of the Indo-European language family.  The culture of the Sindhis is rich and diverse, influenced by various historical events and interactions.|例句：Many Sindhis migrated to India after the partition of 1947.  许多信德人在1947年分治后迁移到印度。|近义词：Sindhi people, Sindhi speakers|反义词：无
