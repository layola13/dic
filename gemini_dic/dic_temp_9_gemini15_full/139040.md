oar:/ɔːr/| n.|桨；橹|A long pole with a blade at one end, used for propelling a boat.|例句：He used the oar to row the boat across the lake. 他用桨划船穿过湖泊。|近义词：paddle|反义词:none

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He uses an oar to row the boat. = 他用桨划船。
The rower grips the oar firmly. = 划船者紧紧抓住桨。

一般过去时:
They rowed the boat with wooden oars. = 他们用木桨划船。
He pulled the oar hard to reach the shore. = 他用力划桨以到达岸边。

现在完成时:
She has used that oar for many years. = 她已经用了那支桨很多年了。
They have rowed many miles with these oars. = 他们用这些桨划了很远的路。

过去完成时:
He had broken his oar before the race. = 他在比赛前弄坏了他的桨。
They had already used the oars before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他们已经用过了那些桨。

将来时:
We will use the oars to cross the river. = 我们将用桨渡过这条河。
He will need a strong oar for that current. = 他需要一把结实的桨来应对那股水流。

虚拟语气:
If I had a spare oar, I would continue rowing. = 如果我有一支备用桨，我会继续划船。
I wish I had a lighter oar. = 我希望我有一支更轻的桨。

被动语态:
The oar was broken in the storm. = 桨在暴风雨中断了。
The boat is propelled by oars. = 这艘船由桨推动。

疑问句:
Is this oar strong enough? = 这支桨够结实吗？
Whose oar is this? = 这是谁的桨？

否定句:
He didn't have an oar. = 他没有桨。
They couldn't find the oars. = 他们找不到桨。
