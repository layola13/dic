privier:/ˈpriviər/| n.|私密顾问，知情人|a person who is privy to information not known to others.  Someone with privileged access to information.|例句：He was a privier to their secret negotiations. 他是他们秘密谈判的知情人。|近义词：insider, confidant, confidante |反义词：outsider, stranger
