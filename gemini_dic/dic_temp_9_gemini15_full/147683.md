overpoweringly:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərɪŋli/| adv. |压倒性地，势不可挡地 | overwhelmingly strong or great; to such an extent as to be overwhelming.  |例句：The beauty of the scenery was overpoweringly beautiful. 这风景的美景令人难以置信地美丽。|例句：The evidence against him was overpoweringly strong. 反对他证据确凿。|近义词：圧倒的に (圧倒的に), overwhelmingly, tremendously, incredibly |反义词：slightly, mildly, barely
