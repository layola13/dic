sergeants:/ˈsɑːrdʒənts/| n.|警长；中士|A sergeant is a non-commissioned officer of relatively high rank in the army, navy, air force, or police force.|例句：The sergeant ordered the soldiers to advance.  警长命令士兵们前进。|例句：Two sergeants were killed in the ambush. 伏击中两名中士阵亡。|近义词：corporal, lieutenant|反义词：private, recruit
