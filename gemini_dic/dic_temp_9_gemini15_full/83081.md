guru-ships:/ˈɡʊruʃɪps/ | n. | 导师地位；大师身份；（尤指精神导师的）指导关系 | The state or position of being a guru;  the relationship of guidance, especially spiritual guidance, provided by a guru.  It often implies a system of mentorship, discipleship, and the transmission of knowledge or wisdom. | Example:  The guru-ships of many spiritual leaders are built upon years of dedication and study. = 许多精神领袖的导师地位是建立在多年的奉献和学习之上的。  His guru-ship extended beyond simple instruction, encompassing a profound personal connection. = 他的导师地位超越了简单的指导，包含着深刻的个人联系。 | 近义词：mentorship, discipleship, guidance, tutelage | 反义词：ignorance, lack of guidance
