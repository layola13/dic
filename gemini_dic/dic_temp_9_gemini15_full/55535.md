distributionist: /ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃənɪst/ | n. | 分配主义者；分配主义的拥护者 | An advocate of distributism, an economic system that promotes the widespread ownership of the means of production.  | Example:  Many distributionists believe that widespread property ownership leads to a more just and equitable society.  许多分配主义者认为广泛的财产所有制能够带来更加公正和公平的社会。  | 近义词：socialist (in some contexts), decentralist | 反义词：centralist, capitalist (in some contexts) | 名词
