hibiscus: /hɪˈbɪskəs/ | n. |木槿属植物；木槿花 | A hibiscus is a genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, Malvaceae.  They are known for their large, showy flowers in various colors.  Many species are cultivated as ornamentals. | 例句：The hibiscus flowers bloomed brightly in the tropical garden.  热带花园里的木槿花盛开。 | 例句：She wore a hibiscus flower in her hair. 她头上戴着一朵木槿花。|近义词：rose mallow, rose of sharon (depending on the specific species)|反义词:  (无直接反义词，可根据语境考虑与非开花植物或不艳丽的花朵对比)|名词
