factorial:/fækˈtɔːriəl/|/n.|阶乘|The factorial of a non-negative integer n, denoted by n!, is the product of all positive integers less than or equal to n.  For example, 5! = 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 = 120.  The factorial of 0 is defined as 1 (0! = 1).|The factorial of 4 is 24.  4! = 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 = 24.|近义词:product(in this context)|反义词:None|名词
