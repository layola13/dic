crosswalks:/ˈkrɒsˌwɔːks/ | n. | 人行横道，斑马线 | Marked areas on a road where pedestrians can safely cross.  They are usually marked with stripes and sometimes have traffic signals. | Example:  Pedestrians should only cross the street at the crosswalks. 行人只应在人行横道上过马路。  |近义词： pedestrian crossing, zebra crossing | 反义词：  N/A | 名词
