denumber:/dɪˈnʌmbər/| vt.|编号，给…编号 |To assign a number to (something) in a series or system.  |例句：The library staff denumbered all the new books. 图书馆的工作人员给所有新书编了号。  |近义词：number, enumerate, index |反义词：de-number (取消编号，无直接反义词)|动词


USAGE->

They denumber the packages every day. = 他们每天给包裹编号。
The system denumbers the incoming calls automatically. = 系统自动为来电编号。


一般过去时:
The researcher denumbered the samples before the experiment. = 研究人员在实验前对样本进行了编号。
The company denumbered all the products last year. = 公司去年给所有产品编了号。


现在完成时:
They have denumbered all the exhibits for the museum. = 他们已经为博物馆的所有展品编了号。

过去完成时:
