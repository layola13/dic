shragger:/ʃræɡər/| n. |耸肩的人，漫不经心的人|A person who habitually shrugs their shoulders, often implying indifference or lack of care.  It is informal and somewhat derogatory.|例句：He's such a shragger; he never seems to care about anything.|近义词：apathistic, indifferent, unconcerned|反义词：concerned, caring, enthusiastic|
