radioactivity:/ˌreɪdiouækˈtɪvəti/| n. |放射性；放射现象|The spontaneous emission of radiation from the nucleus of an unstable atom.  This process results in the transformation of the atom into a different atom or a different isotope of the same atom.|例句：The radioactivity in the soil is a concern for local residents.  土壤中的放射性让当地居民担忧。|例句：Exposure to high levels of radioactivity can be dangerous.  暴露于高水平的放射性物质中是危险的。|近义词：radiation, emissions|反义词：stability
