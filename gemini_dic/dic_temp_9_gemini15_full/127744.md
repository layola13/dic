negotiator: /nɪˈɡoʊʃieɪtər/ | n. |谈判者，协商者 | A person skilled in negotiating agreements between people or groups.  |例句：He's a skilled negotiator, always able to reach a favorable outcome. 他是一位熟练的谈判者，总是能够达成有利的结果。|例句：The experienced negotiator managed to resolve the conflict peacefully.  经验丰富的谈判者成功地和平解决了冲突。|近义词：mediator, arbitrator, intermediary |反义词：obstructor, impediment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The negotiator presents his proposal. = 谈判者提出了他的建议。
She negotiates contracts for the company. = 她为公司谈判合同。


一般过去时:
The negotiator successfully concluded the deal. = 谈判者成功地完成了交易。
He negotiated a lower price for the car. = 他为这辆车谈到了一个较低的价格。


现在完成时:
They have negotiated a peace treaty. = 他们已经谈判达成了一项和平条约。
She has negotiated several major contracts this year. = 今年她已经谈成了几份重要的合同。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, they had negotiated the main points. = 会议之前，他们已经协商好了主要内容。
He had negotiated with the supplier for weeks before reaching an agreement. = 在达成协议之前，他已经与供应商谈判了几周。


将来时:
The team will negotiate the terms of the agreement tomorrow. = 团队明天将协商协议条款。
She will negotiate a new salary with her boss. = 她将与老板协商新的薪水。


虚拟语气:
If he were a better negotiator, he would have gotten a better deal. = 如果他是一位更好的谈判者，他就能达成更好的交易。
I wish I had a better negotiator on my side. = 我希望我这边能有更好的谈判者。


被动语态:
The contract was negotiated by a team of experts. = 合同由专家团队协商达成。
The terms of the agreement are being negotiated at the moment. = 目前正在协商协议条款。


疑问句:
Who is the chief negotiator in this case? = 此案的主要谈判者是谁？
Will you be the negotiator for this project? = 你会担任这个项目的谈判者吗？


否定句:
He did not negotiate well in that situation. = 他在那样的情况下谈判得不好。
The company refused to negotiate further. = 公司拒绝进一步谈判。


PHRASE->

experienced negotiator:经验丰富的谈判者
tough negotiator:强硬的谈判者
principled negotiator:有原则的谈判者
