pursuantly:/pərˈsuːəntli/ | adv. | 依照；根据；遵照 | In accordance with; following; according to.  The contract was executed pursuant to the agreement. | 近义词：accordingly, consequently, therefore | 反义词：contrarily, conversely


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We act pursuant to the law. = 我们依照法律行事。
They proceed pursuant to the instructions. = 他们按照指示进行。


一般过去时:
He acted pursuant to his beliefs. = 他根据自己的信仰行事。
She responded pursuant to the email. = 她按照邮件回复。


现在完成时:
They have acted pursuant to the agreement. = 他们已经按照协议行事了。
We have proceeded pursuant to the plan. = 我们已经按照计划进行了。


过去完成时:
They had acted pursuant to the old rules. = 他们按照旧规则行事。
She had responded pursuant to the previous instructions. = 她按照之前的指示回复了。


将来时:
We will act pursuant to the new guidelines. = 我们将按照新的指导方针行事。
They will proceed pursuant to the revised plan. = 他们将按照修改后的计划进行。


虚拟语气:
If we knew the facts, we would act pursuant to them. = 如果我们知道事实，我们会根据事实行事。
If she had understood, she would have responded pursuant to the request. = 如果她理解了，她就会按照要求回复。


被动语态:
The decision was made pursuant to the board's recommendation. = 这一决定是根据董事会的建议做出的。
The project is being managed pursuant to the established protocols. = 该项目正在按照既定的规程进行管理。


疑问句:
Will they act pursuant to our request? = 他们会按照我们的要求行事吗？
Did she respond pursuant to the instructions? = 她是否按照指示回复了？


否定句:
They did not act pursuant to the contract. = 他们没有按照合同行事。
He did not respond pursuant to the guidelines. = 他没有按照指导方针回复。


PHRASE->

pursuant to the terms = 根据条款
