saqqara:/sækˈkɑːrə/| n. |萨卡拉|Saqqara is an ancient Egyptian burial ground located on the west bank of the Nile, near Memphis.  It's famous for its step pyramid of Djoser and numerous other pyramids, tombs, and temples from the Old Kingdom period.  It provides invaluable insights into ancient Egyptian civilization, religious beliefs, and funerary practices.  |例句：Saqqara is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a major archaeological area in Egypt.  萨卡拉是联合国教科文组织世界遗产，也是埃及重要的考古遗址。|The Step Pyramid of Djoser at Saqqara is considered an architectural marvel. 萨卡拉的阶梯金字塔被认为是建筑奇迹。|近义词：Necropolis (general term for a large cemetery)|反义词:None|名词
