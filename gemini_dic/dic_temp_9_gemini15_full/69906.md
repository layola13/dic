fertilizer:/ˈfɜːrtɪlaɪzər/| n. |肥料，化肥 |A substance added to soil to enhance the growth of plants by providing essential nutrients.|例句：Farmers use fertilizer to increase crop yields.  农民使用化肥来提高作物产量。|例句：Organic fertilizer is better for the environment than chemical fertilizer. 有机肥料比化肥更环保。|近义词：manure, nutrient, plant food |反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer uses fertilizer on his crops. = 农民在他的庄稼上使用化肥。
They apply fertilizer regularly to their garden. = 他们定期向花园施肥。

一般过去时:
We fertilized the lawn last week. = 我们上周给草坪施了肥。
She added fertilizer to the soil before planting. = 她在种植之前向土壤中添加了肥料。

They have already fertilized the fields this season. = 他们本季已经给田地施过肥了。
