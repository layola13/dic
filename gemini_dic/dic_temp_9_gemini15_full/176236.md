re-carries:/ˌriːˈkæriz/| v.|重新承载，再次携带|To carry something again or anew; to transport something again.  The word is formed by the prefix "re-" meaning "again" and the verb "carries" which is the present tense third person singular of the verb "carry".|例句：The airline re-carries the delayed luggage to its final destination. = 航空公司将延误的行李重新运送到最终目的地。

The delivery service re-carries the damaged package after it was repaired. = 快递服务在包裹修复后再次运送。|近义词：retransport, convey again, carry anew |反义词：unload, discard, abandon.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-carries the groceries after dropping some. = 他掉了些杂货后又重新提着它们。
She re-carries her bag to the front of the bus. = 她把包重新拿到公交车前。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:

She has re-carried the message to her boss several times. = 她已经多次向老板转达了这个消息。


过去完成时:

Before the storm hit, they had re-carried all the valuable furniture inside. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他们已将所有贵重的家具重新搬进屋内。

虚拟语气:
