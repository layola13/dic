mandarins:/ˈmændərɪnz/| n. |柑橘；橘子；高级官员；普通话|Mandarins are a type of citrus fruit.  Mandarins are also a term for high-ranking officials, especially in China.  The term can also refer to the Mandarin Chinese language.|例句：I love the sweet taste of mandarins. 我喜欢柑橘的甜味。


The high-ranking officials were called mandarins.  高级官员被称为 mandarins.


近义词：tangerines (for the fruit), officials, elites|反义词：暂无
