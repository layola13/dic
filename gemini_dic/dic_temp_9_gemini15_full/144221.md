outskirt:/ˈaʊtskɜːrt/| n.|郊区，边缘|The outskirts of a town or city are the areas that are furthest from its centre.  |例句：We live on the outskirts of town. 我们住在城郊。|例句：The factory is located on the outskirts of the city. 这家工厂位于城市郊区。|近义词：periphery, border, fringe |反义词：center, downtown


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory operates on the outskirts. = 工厂在郊区运行。
He lives on the outskirts of London. = 他住在伦敦郊区。


一般过去时:
They lived on the outskirts during their childhood. = 他们童年时住在郊区。
The event took place on the outskirts of the town. = 活动发生在城镇郊区。


现在完成时:
We have lived on the outskirts for ten years. = 我们在郊区住了十年了。
She has always preferred the outskirts to the city center. = 她总是更喜欢郊区而不是市中心。


过去完成时:
They had already moved to the outskirts before the city expanded. = 城市扩张之前，他们已经搬到郊区了。
Before the war, they had lived peacefully on the outskirts. = 战争爆发前，他们平静地住在郊区。


将来时:
We will move to the outskirts next year. = 我们明年将搬到郊区。
They will build a new park on the outskirts. = 他们将在郊区建造一个新的公园。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would buy a house on the outskirts. = 如果我有钱，我会在郊区买一套房子。
I wish we lived on the outskirts instead of this busy city. = 我希望我们住在郊区而不是这个繁华的城市。


被动语态:
A new shopping mall is being built on the outskirts. = 一个新的购物中心正在郊区建设中。
The area was previously overlooked, but now the outskirts are being developed rapidly. = 这个地区以前被忽视，但现在郊区正在迅速发展。


疑问句:
Do you live in the city center or on the outskirts? = 你住在市中心还是郊区？
Is there anything interesting to do on the outskirts? = 郊区有什么有趣的事情可做吗？


否定句:
They don't live on the outskirts; they live downtown. = 他们不住在郊区，他们住在市中心。
He didn't grow up on the outskirts, but in the city. = 他不是在郊区长大的，而是在城市里长大的。
