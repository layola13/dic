self-assessment:/ˌsɛlf əˈsɛsmənt/ | n. | 自我评估，自我评价 | The act of judging one's own abilities, performance, or character.  It involves reflecting on one's strengths and weaknesses to identify areas for improvement. | 例句：The course includes a self-assessment to help students identify their learning gaps. 这门课程包括一次自我评估，以帮助学生找出他们的学习差距。

例句：  Her self-assessment revealed a need for better time management skills.  她的自我评估显示她需要改进时间管理技能。


近义词：self-evaluation, introspection, appraisal, review

反义词：external evaluation, peer assessment
