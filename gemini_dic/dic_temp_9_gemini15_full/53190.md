dipstick:/ˈdɪpstɪk/| n. |油尺，液位计|A dipstick is a rod with a calibrated scale used to measure the level of a liquid in a container, such as the oil level in a car engine.|例句：I checked the dipstick to see how much oil was left in the car. 我检查了油尺看看车里还剩多少油。

例句：The mechanic used a dipstick to measure the transmission fluid. 机械师用油尺测量变速箱油位。


近义词：gauge, indicator|反义词：N/A|词性:n.
