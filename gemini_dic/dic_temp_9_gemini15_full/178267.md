recomputation:/ˌriːkɒmpjuˈteɪʃən/ | n. |重新计算 |The process of calculating something again.  It often implies a correction or refinement of a previous calculation. |Example: The recomputation of the tax liability revealed a significant error. 重新计算税款责任揭示了一个重大错误。  The recomputation of the trajectory showed the spaceship was off course.  轨迹的重新计算显示宇宙飞船偏离了航线。|近义词： recalculation, recomputing |反义词： initial calculation, first computation |名词
