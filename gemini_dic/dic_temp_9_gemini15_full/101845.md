is:/ɪz/| v. |是；存在；为；构成 | To express existence, identity, or a state of being. It is the third-person singular present tense form of the verb "to be". |例句：The sky is blue. 天空是蓝色的。 The book is on the table. 书在桌子上。|近义词：exists, constitutes, amounts to |反义词：is not, isn't


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a doctor. = 他是一位医生。
She is singing a song. = 她正在唱歌。

一般过去时:
It was a sunny day. = 那是一个阳光明媚的日子。
They were at the park. = 他们在公园里。

现在完成时:
He has been to Paris. = 他去过巴黎。
She has finished her work. = 她已经完成了她的工作。

过去完成时:
The movie had already started when we arrived. = 我们到达时电影已经开始了。
They had eaten dinner before they went out. = 他们出去之前已经吃过晚饭了。


将来时:
The meeting will be tomorrow. = 会议将在明天举行。
I will be there soon. = 我很快就会到那里。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would go. = 如果我是你，我会去。
It is important that he be on time. = 他准时很重要。


被动语态:
The window was broken. = 窗户被打破了。
The cake is being baked. = 蛋糕正在烘焙中。


疑问句:
Is he coming? = 他要来吗？
Is it raining? = 天在下雨吗？


否定句:
He is not a teacher. = 他不是老师。
It is not finished yet. = 它还没有完成。



PHRASE->
That's it! = 就这样！
It is up to you. = 这取决于你。
What is it? = 怎么了？
It's all right. = 没关系。
It depends. = 看情况。
