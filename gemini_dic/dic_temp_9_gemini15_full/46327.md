dabs:/dæbz/| n. v.|轻拍；少量；污点|A dab is a small amount of something, or a light touch or tap.  The verb means to apply something lightly with a dabbing motion.|例句：She applied a dab of cream to her face. 她往脸上抹了一点乳霜。


She dabbed at her eyes with a tissue. 她用纸巾轻轻擦拭眼睛。|近义词：spot, smear, touch, pat|反义词：large amount, copious amount


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dabs paint onto the canvas. = 他轻轻地把颜料点在画布上。
She dabs her lipstick on quickly. = 她迅速地涂了点口红。

一般过去时:
The artist dabbed the brush in the paint. = 艺术家把画笔蘸了一下颜料。
He dabbed at the spill with a cloth. = 他用布擦拭了溅出的东西。

现在完成时:
She has dabbed perfume behind her ears. = 她已经把香水涂抹在耳后了。
He has dabbed some honey onto his toast. = 他已经在吐司上涂了一点蜂蜜。

过去完成时:
She had dabbed the wound with antiseptic before bandaging it. = 在包扎伤口之前，她已经用消毒剂轻涂了伤口。
They had dabbed the excess water off the surface. = 他们已经把表面的多余水分擦掉了。

将来时:
I will dab some more paint onto the flower. = 我会在花朵上再涂一些颜料。
She will dab the frosting onto the cake. = 她会在蛋糕上涂抹糖霜。

虚拟语气:
If I needed more paint, I would dab some from the tube. = 如果我需要更多颜料，我会从管子里取一点。
I wish I had dabbed more sunscreen on my nose. = 我真希望我在鼻子上涂抹更多防晒霜。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

She didn't dab her tears away. = 她没有擦去眼泪。
