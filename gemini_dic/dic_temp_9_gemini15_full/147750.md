overprogram:/ˌoʊvərˈproʊɡræm/ | vt. |过度编程；编写过多程序| To write more program code than is necessary or efficient; to add unnecessary complexity to a program.|例句：The software was overprogrammed, leading to slow performance and frequent crashes.  软件过度编程，导致性能缓慢且频繁崩溃。|例句：He overprogrammed the game, making it unnecessarily complicated for the players. 他过度编程游戏，使得游戏对玩家来说不必要地复杂。|近义词：overengineer, complicate, overload|反义词：simplify, streamline
