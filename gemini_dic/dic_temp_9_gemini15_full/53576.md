disblame: /dɪsˈbleɪm/ | v. | 免责；否认责任 | To deny responsibility for; disown.  | The company disclaimed all responsibility for the accident.  公司否认对事故负有任何责任。 | deny, repudiate, disavow | accept, own, acknowledge | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disclaims any knowledge of the incident. = 他否认了解此事。
The company disclaims responsibility for lost items. = 公司否认对遗失物品负责。

一般过去时:
She disclaimed any involvement in the conspiracy. = 她否认参与了阴谋。
The government disclaimed responsibility for the economic crisis. = 政府否认对经济危机负责。

现在完成时:
They have disclaimed all liability for damages. = 他们已经否认对损害赔偿承担任何责任。
He has disclaimed any connection with the organization. = 他已经否认与该组织有任何联系。

过去完成时:
She had disclaimed all responsibility before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，她就已经否认一切责任。
The lawyer had disclaimed all knowledge of his client's actions. = 律师在之前已经否认了解他当事人的行为。

将来时:
He will disclaim all responsibility for the project's failure. = 他将否认对项目失败负任何责任。
The company will disclaim any warranty on the product. = 公司将否认该产品的任何保修。

虚拟语气:
If he disclaimed responsibility, he would avoid prosecution. = 如果他否认责任，他就可以避免起诉。
I wish he had disclaimed the rumors sooner. = 我希望他早点否认那些谣言。

被动语态:
Responsibility for the accident was disclaimed by the driver. = 司机否认了对事故的责任。
All liability was disclaimed by the manufacturer. = 制造商否认了一切责任。

疑问句:
Did he disclaim his involvement in the crime? = 他是否否认参与了犯罪？
Does the company disclaim any warranties on their products? = 公司是否否认其产品有任何保修？

否定句:
He did not disclaim his guilt. = 他没有否认他的罪行。
The company did not disclaim responsibility for the faulty product. = 公司没有否认对有缺陷产品的责任。
