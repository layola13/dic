placemat:/ˈpleɪsˌmæt/ | n. | 餐垫 | A small mat placed under a plate or cutlery at a meal.  It protects the table and can add to the table setting's aesthetic appeal. | Example:  I bought some new placemats for the holiday dinner. 我为节日晚餐买了一些新的餐垫。  The placemats were decorated with a festive design. 餐垫上装饰着节日图案。|近义词：table mat, coaster (for drinks)|反义词：None
