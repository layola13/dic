fractured:/ˈfræktʃərd/| adj.|破裂的；骨折的；分裂的；不连贯的|Broken, cracked, or splintered; also used to describe something that is divided or disorganized.  It can refer to bones, objects, or even concepts and ideas.|例句：The vase lay fractured on the floor.  花瓶碎在地上。


例句：His fractured leg required surgery. 他的骨折的腿需要手术。


近义词：broken, cracked, splintered, fragmented, shattered, disrupted


反义词：intact, whole, unbroken, unified


词性：形容词
