discouraged:/dɪsˈkɜːrɪdʒd/ | adj. | 气馁的；沮丧的；灰心的 | feeling less hopeful and enthusiastic |  The constant rain discouraged us from going for a walk.  持续的雨让我们打消了去散步的念头。 | disheartened, dejected, despondent | encouraged, inspired, motivated | 形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is discouraged by the failure. = 他因失败而气馁。
She is always discouraged by her low grades. = 她总是因为低分而沮丧。

一般过去时:
I was discouraged after the interview. = 面试后我感到灰心。
They were discouraged by the lack of support. = 他们因为缺乏支持而感到沮丧。

现在完成时:
I have been discouraged many times, but I never give up. = 我多次感到气馁，但我从未放弃。
She has been discouraged by the recent setbacks. = 她因最近的挫折而感到灰心。

过去完成时:
He had been discouraged before he finally succeeded. = 他在最终成功之前曾多次感到气馁。
We had been discouraged by the previous attempts. = 我们之前的尝试让我们感到沮丧。

将来时:
I will be discouraged if I don't get the job. = 如果我没有得到这份工作，我会感到灰心。
They will be discouraged if their plan fails. = 如果他们的计划失败，他们会感到沮丧。

虚拟语气:
If I were not discouraged, I would try again. = 如果我不气馁，我会再试一次。
I wish I were not so discouraged by the news. = 我真希望这个消息不会让我这么沮丧。

被动语态:
He was discouraged from pursuing his dream.  = 他被劝退了，放弃了他的梦想。
The team was discouraged by the negative feedback. = 团队受到了负面反馈的影响而感到沮丧。

疑问句:
Are you discouraged by the results? = 你对结果感到沮丧吗？
Is she discouraged by the difficulty of the task? = 她对任务的难度感到灰心吗？

否定句:
I am not discouraged by the challenges. = 我不会被挑战吓倒。
He is not discouraged easily. = 他不容易气馁。


PHRASE->

be discouraged from doing sth. = 被劝退做某事
feel discouraged = 感到气馁
become discouraged = 变得灰心
seem discouraged = 看起来很沮丧
utterly discouraged = 完全气馁
