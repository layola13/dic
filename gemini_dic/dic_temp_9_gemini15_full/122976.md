monadiform:/ˈmɒnədaɪfɔːrm/| adj. | 单细胞的；单体形的 |  Resembling or characteristic of a monad; unicellular.  The term refers to organisms or structures that are single-celled or consist of a single unit. | Example:  The amoeba is a monadiform organism.  变形虫是一种单细胞生物。 |近义词: unicellular, single-celled | 反义词: multicellular, complex
