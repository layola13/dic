heartstruck: /ˈhɑːrtstrʌk/ | adj. | 心碎的；痴迷的 | deeply affected by love or infatuation; emotionally overwhelmed by love, often in a painful way.  | 例句：She was heartstruck after her boyfriend left her. = 她男友离开后心碎了。  He was completely heartstruck by her beauty. = 他完全被她的美貌迷住了。 | 近义词： heartbroken, lovesick, smitten, infatuated | 反义词：unmoved, indifferent, apathetic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is heartstruck by her beauty. = 他被她的美貌迷住了。
She is heartstruck over the loss of her pet. = 她为失去宠物而心碎。

一般过去时:
He was heartstruck when she rejected him. = 当她拒绝他时，他心碎了。
She was heartstruck after the death of her grandfather. = 她爷爷去世后心碎了。

现在完成时:
He has been heartstruck ever since she left. = 从她离开后，他就一直心碎。
She has been heartstruck many times in her life. = 她一生中多次心碎。

过去完成时:
He had been heartstruck before he met her. = 在他遇到她之前，他就已经心碎了。
She had been heartstruck by his charm before she knew his true nature. = 在她了解他的真实本性之前，她就已被他的魅力迷住了。

将来时:
He will be heartstruck if she leaves him. = 如果她离开他，他会心碎的。
She will be heartstruck if her team loses the championship. = 如果她的队伍输掉冠军赛，她会心碎的。

虚拟语气:

I wish I wasn't so heartstruck. = 我真希望自己不要这么心碎。

被动语态:  (This adjective is not typically used in the passive voice.)
