dugongs:/ˈduːɡɒŋz/| n. |儒艮|A large, herbivorous marine mammal with a streamlined body, paddle-like flippers, and a horizontally flattened tail.  Dugongs are found in warm coastal waters of the Indo-Pacific region and are closely related to manatees.|例句：Dugongs are gentle giants of the ocean. 儒艮是海洋中的温柔巨人。|例句：The dugong population is threatened by habitat loss and hunting. 儒艮种群受到栖息地丧失和狩猎的威胁。|近义词：sea cow (informal)|反义词:|词性:n.
