skirmishingly:/ˈskɜːrmɪʃɪŋli/ | adv. | 冲突地；轻微冲突地；零星地 |  In a manner suggestive of a skirmish; lightly or sporadically.  The word suggests a small, brief, and perhaps insignificant conflict or engagement. | 例句：The two armies skirmishingly engaged along the border.  两军在边境沿线零星地交战。  They skirmishingly debated the issue, never reaching a consensus. 他们零星地争论这个问题，从未达成共识。 | 近义词：sporadically, intermittently, fitfully, sporadically  | 反义词：continuously, consistently, relentlessly | 副词
