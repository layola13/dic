celeriacs:/səˈlɛəriæks/| n. |根芹菜；根茎芹菜 | A celery root, also known as a celeriac, is a root vegetable with a knobby, bulbous root and stalks similar to celery. It has a slightly nutty and earthy flavor.  |例句：The chef used celeriac in the soup to add a unique earthy flavor.  这位厨师在汤里用了根芹菜来增添独特的泥土味。

例句： I roasted the celeriac with herbs and spices for a delicious side dish. 我用香草和香料烤根芹菜做成美味的配菜。


近义词：celery root, knob celery |反义词：无


词源:  The word "celeriac" is derived from the Latin word *apium*, meaning "celery," combined with the suffix "-iac," which denotes a specific type or variety.  The root "celery" itself comes from the Old French *celeri*, ultimately from the Latin *apium graveolens*,  referring to the strong-smelling celery plant.
