impactors:/ɪmˈpæktərz/| n. |撞击物；影响因素|Objects that strike a surface with force, causing an impact.  It can also refer to things that have a significant effect or influence. |例句：The researchers studied the impactors found in the crater. 研究人员研究了在陨石坑中发现的撞击物。

例句：The economic impactors of the pandemic were widespread.  疫情的经济影响因素是广泛的。


近义词：projectiles, agents, influencers |反义词：None (the antonym depends on the context)|词性:名词
