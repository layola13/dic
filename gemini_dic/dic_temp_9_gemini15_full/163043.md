preclearances: /ˌpriːˈklɪərənsɪz/ | n. | 预先许可；预先批准；预先通关 | The process of obtaining approvals or permissions in advance.  It often refers to obtaining necessary permits or authorizations before commencing an activity, especially in areas like import/export, travel, or construction.  | 例句：The project was delayed due to insufficient preclearances.  项目因预先许可不足而延误。


The company secured all necessary preclearances before launching the product.  该公司在推出产品之前获得了所有必要的预先许可。


近义词：prior approvals, advance authorizations, pre-approvals | 反义词：post-clearance, after-approval


词性: 名词
