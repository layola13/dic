earthworm:/ˈɜːθwɜːrm/| n. |蚯蚓|A segmented worm that lives in the soil and feeds on organic matter. Earthworms are important for soil aeration and fertility.  They are also known as nightcrawlers.|例句：The earthworms helped aerate the soil. 蚯蚓帮助土壤通气。

例句： We found many earthworms after the rain. 雨后我们发现了许多蚯蚓。


近义词：nightcrawler, angleworm

反义词：无


词性：名词
