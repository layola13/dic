recurred:/rɪˈkɜːrd/| v.|再次发生，反复出现 |To happen or occur again.|The pain recurred after a few hours.  几小时后疼痛又复发了。|The problem of poverty has recurred throughout history. 贫困问题贯穿于整个历史。|reappear, repeat, happen again| cease, end, stop|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem recurs frequently. = 这个问题经常反复出现。
This pattern recurs throughout the book. = 这种模式贯穿全书。


一般过去时:
The symptoms recurred after a week. =  一周后症状又复发了。
The error recurred in the next iteration. = 错误在下一轮迭代中再次出现。


现在完成时:
The headache has recurred several times. = 头痛已经复发了数次。
The issue has recurred since the last meeting. = 自上次会议以来，这个问题一直反复出现。


过去完成时:
The pain had recurred before he took the medication. =  在他服药之前，疼痛已经复发了。
The same mistake had recurred multiple times. = 同样的错误已经多次重复出现。


将来时:
The doctor thinks the illness will recur. = 医生认为这种疾病会复发。
I fear the problem will recur next year. = 我担心这个问题明年还会出现。


虚拟语气:
If the problem recurred, we would need to find a new solution. = 如果问题再次出现，我们就需要找到一个新的解决方案。
I wish the pain wouldn't recur. = 我希望疼痛不会再次出现。


被动语态:
The same mistake was recurred in the report. =  报告中再次出现了同样的错误。(被动语态用在此处略显不自然，主动语态更常见)
The error was recurred by the system. = 系统再次出现了错误。(被动语态用在此处略显不自然，主动语态更常见)


疑问句:
Has the pain recurred since your last appointment? = 自上次就诊以来，你的疼痛复发了吗？
Will the problem recur in the future? = 这个问题将来还会再次出现吗？


否定句:
The symptoms did not recur. = 症状没有复发。
The problem hasn't recurred since the fix. = 自从修复后，问题就没有再出现过。


PHRASE->
recurred again =再次发生
recurred frequently = 频繁复发
recurred unexpectedly =出乎意料地复发
recurred with intensity =剧烈复发
recurred in waves =反复发作
