foresighted:/ˈfɔːrsaɪtɪd/| adj.|有远见的；深谋远虑的；预见性强的|Having or showing foresight; able to predict and plan for the future.  The foresighted investor bought land before the city expanded.|近义词：farsighted, prescient, provident|反义词：shortsighted, myopic, improvident


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is foresighted in his business dealings. = 他在商业交易中很有远见。
She is always foresighted about potential problems. = 她总是能预见到潜在的问题。


一般过去时:
The company was foresighted in its diversification strategy. = 这家公司在多元化战略方面很有远见。
They were foresighted enough to invest in renewable energy. = 他们有远见地投资了可再生能源。


现在完成时:
The government has been foresighted in its infrastructure investments. = 政府在基础设施投资方面一直很有远见。
She has been foresighted in saving for her retirement. = 她为退休储蓄一直很有远见。


过去完成时:
They had been foresighted in securing the contract before the competition. = 他们在竞争之前就预先获得了合同，这显示了他们的远见。
He had been foresighted in buying the property at a lower price. = 他有远见地在较低的价格买下了这处房产。


将来时:
The project will be foresighted in its approach to sustainability. = 该项目在可持续发展的方法上将很有远见。
We will be foresighted in our planning for the future. = 我们在未来的规划中将很有远见。


虚拟语气:
If they had been more foresighted, they would have avoided the crisis. = 如果他们更有远见，他们就能避免这场危机。
I wish I had been more foresighted in my investments. = 我希望我在投资方面更有远见。


被动语态:
The decision was foresighted, given the subsequent events. = 考虑到随后的事件，这个决定是很有远见的。
Their planning has been considered foresighted by many experts. = 他们的计划被许多专家认为是很有远见的。


疑问句:
Was the decision foresighted? = 这个决定有远见吗？
Are their actions foresighted? = 他们的行动有远见吗？


否定句:
The plan was not foresighted enough. = 这个计划不够有远见。
His actions were not foresighted. = 他的行动缺乏远见。


PHRASE->
foresighted planning = 深谋远虑的规划
foresighted leadership = 有远见的领导
foresighted investment = 有远见的投资
a foresighted person = 一个有远见的人
foresighted approach = 有远见的做法

词源:
fore- (前缀，表示"在前"、"预先") + sighted (有视力的；有洞察力的)  最终来源于古英语的 *sīht "sight" (视力)。  "sighted"在此处并非指"有视力"的字面意思，而是引申为"有洞察力"、"有预见性"。
