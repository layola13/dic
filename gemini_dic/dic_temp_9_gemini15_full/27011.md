calculative: /ˈkælkjuleɪtɪv/ | adj. | 算计的；精明的；有心机的 |  Deliberately calculating; scheming; shrewd.  This word implies a person is skillful at making plans and achieving goals, often in a way that is self-serving and possibly manipulative. | 例句：He was a calculative businessman, always looking for an advantage. 他是一个精明的商人，总是寻找优势。


例句：Her calculative nature allowed her to climb the corporate ladder quickly. 她的精明让她迅速爬上了公司阶梯。


近义词：scheming, shrewd, cunning, calculating, manipulative, strategic


反义词：naive, ingenuous, guileless, unsuspecting


词性：形容词
