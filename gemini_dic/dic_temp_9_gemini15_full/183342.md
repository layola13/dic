reproceeds:/ˈriːprɑːsiːdz/| n.|收益，收入，所得|The proceeds from the sale of the house will be used to pay off the mortgage.  出售房屋的收益将用于偿还抵押贷款。|近义词：earnings, revenue, income|反义词：losses, deficits, expenditure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The proceeds from the concert are donated to charity. = 音乐会的收益捐给了慈善机构。
Our company's proceeds are higher this year than last year. = 我们公司今年的收益比去年高。


一般过去时:
The proceeds of the auction went to a good cause. = 拍卖所得用于公益事业。
The proceeds from the event exceeded our expectations. = 活动的收益超出了我们的预期。


现在完成时:
The company has reinvested the proceeds from its latest product launch. = 公司已将其最新产品发布的收益进行了再投资。
We have already received the proceeds from the investment. = 我们已经收到了投资的收益。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, they had already spent the proceeds from their book sales. = 到年底，他们已经花掉了卖书的收入。
Before the project concluded, they had allocated the proceeds to different departments. = 项目结束前，他们已将收益分配给不同的部门。


将来时:
The proceeds from the upcoming fundraiser will go towards building a new school. = 未来筹款活动的收益将用于建造一所新学校。
We expect the proceeds from the investment to be substantial. = 我们预计这项投资的收益将非常可观。


虚拟语气:
If the proceeds were higher, we could expand our business. = 如果收益更高，我们就能扩大业务。
I wish the proceeds from the investment were more than they are. = 我希望投资收益比现在多。


被动语态:
The proceeds were distributed among the shareholders. = 收益在股东之间分配。
The proceeds are being used to fund further research. = 收益正在被用于资助进一步的研究。


疑问句:
What are the proceeds from the sale? = 销售收益是多少？
Where will the proceeds be allocated? = 收益将分配到哪里？


否定句:
The proceeds did not meet our projections. = 收益没有达到我们的预期。
The investment did not yield the expected proceeds. = 这项投资没有产生预期的收益。
