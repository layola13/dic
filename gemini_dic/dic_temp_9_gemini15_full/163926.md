predevelops:/ˌpriːdɪˈvɛləps/| v.|预先开发；提前开发|To develop something before it is needed or before the main development process begins.  This often involves preparatory work, planning, or research.|例句：The company predevelops software components to accelerate the main project. 公司预先开发软件组件以加快主要项目进度。|例句：The city predevelops infrastructure in anticipation of future growth.  城市预先开发基础设施以应对未来的增长。|近义词：prepare, plan,  pioneer|反义词：postdevelop, delay
