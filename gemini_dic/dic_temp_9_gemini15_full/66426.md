exegesis:/ˌɛksəˈdʒiːsɪs/ | n. | 解释；阐释；详细的解说 |  A critical explanation or interpretation of a text, especially of scripture.  It involves a detailed and systematic study of a text to understand its meaning, context, and significance. | Example: The professor's exegesis of the poem illuminated its hidden meanings.  教授对这首诗的解释阐明了其隐藏的意义。|近义词：interpretation, explanation, commentary, analysis | 反义词：misinterpretation, misunderstanding
