/kəˈreɪdʒ/ | n. |勇气；胆量 |Courage is the ability to confront fear and uncertainty with bravery and determination. It involves facing challenges head-on, even when the outcome is unknown.  Courage can manifest in different ways, from standing up for what's right to overcoming personal obstacles.  |例句：It took great courage to speak out against the injustice.  说出来反对不公正需要极大的勇气。

例句：She showed remarkable courage in the face of danger. 她面对危险表现出非凡的勇气。


近义词：bravery, boldness, valor, fortitude |反义词：cowardice, fear, timidity |名词
