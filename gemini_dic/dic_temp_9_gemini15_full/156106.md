photoshoots:/ˈfoʊtoʊʃuːts/| n.|照片拍摄，写真拍摄|A photoshoot is a session of taking photographs, typically for professional purposes, such as for a magazine or advertising campaign.|例句：The model prepared for her upcoming photoshoot.  模特为即将到来的写真拍摄做准备。
例句：We had a photoshoot at the beach yesterday. 我们昨天在海滩进行了一次照片拍摄。|近义词：photo session, photography session|反义词：None.
