redeployed:/ˌriːdɪˈplɔɪd/| v.|重新部署，重新分配|To deploy (troops, equipment, or resources) again or in a different place or way.  This implies a previous deployment and a subsequent shift in location, function, or purpose.|例句：The army was redeployed to the northern border.  军队被重新部署到北部边境。

例句：The company redeployed its resources to focus on its core business. 公司重新分配资源，专注于核心业务。

近义词：redistribute, relocate, reposition

反义词：withdraw, remove
