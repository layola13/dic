donation: /doʊˈneɪʃən/ | n. |捐赠；捐款 |The act of giving something, especially money, to a person or an organization that needs it.  A donation can be anything from money to goods or services. |例句：She made a generous donation to the charity. 她向慈善机构慷慨捐款。

例句：The museum received a large donation of art from a private collector. 博物馆收到一位私人收藏家捐赠的大量艺术品。

近义词：contribution, gift, offering, alms

反义词：receipt, withholding

词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The charity receives many donations. =  慈善机构收到许多捐款。
He regularly makes donations to his local church. = 他定期向当地教堂捐款。


一般过去时:
She donated a significant sum to the hospital. = 她向医院捐赠了一笔可观的款项。
They donated blood during the blood drive. =他们在献血活动中献血。


现在完成时:
We have donated over $10,000 this year. = 今年我们已经捐赠了超过一万美元。
She has made several donations to environmental causes. = 她已经向环保事业捐款好几次了。


过去完成时:
They had already donated before the appeal was launched. = 在发起呼吁之前，他们就已经捐款了。
Before the event, he had donated all the necessary equipment. = 活动之前，他已经捐赠了所有必要的设备。


将来时:
I will donate my old clothes to the homeless shelter. = 我将把我的旧衣服捐给收容所。
She will donate to the Red Cross after she gets paid. = 她拿到工资后将向红十字会捐款。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would donate to that cause. = 如果我有更多钱，我会捐给那个事业。
I wish I could donate more to help the victims. = 我希望我能捐更多钱来帮助受害者。


被动语态:
The money was donated to a worthy cause. = 这笔钱被捐给了有价值的事业。
The books are being donated to the school library. = 这些书正在被捐赠给学校图书馆。


疑问句:
Will you donate to the campaign? = 你会捐款给这个活动吗？
How much did you donate? = 你捐了多少钱？


否定句:
He did not donate to the fundraiser. = 他没有向筹款活动捐款。
She refused to donate any more money. = 她拒绝再捐钱了。


PHRASE->
make a donation = 捐款
receive a donation = 收到捐款
charitable donation = 慈善捐款
online donation = 在线捐款
corporate donation = 公司捐款
