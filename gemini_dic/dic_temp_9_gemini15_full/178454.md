reconstitution:/ˌriːkɒnstɪˈtʃuːʃən/| n.|重建；复原；恢复；(食品)复原|The act of restoring something to its former state or condition; the process of rebuilding or reforming something that has been destroyed or damaged.  It can also refer to the process of making a food product edible again by adding water or other liquids.|例句：The reconstitution of the government after the coup was a slow and difficult process. 政变后政府的重建是一个缓慢而艰难的过程。

The rapid reconstitution of the dried soup was achieved by adding hot water.  快速地用热水冲调干汤就完成了复原。|近义词：reconstruction, restoration, revival|反义词：destruction, demolition, ruin|名词
