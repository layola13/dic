crypts:/krɪpts/ | n. |地穴；墓穴；(计算机)加密存储区 | A crypt is an underground chamber or vault, especially one beneath a church, used as a burial place.  In computer science, a crypt refers to a secure storage area for encrypted data. |例句：The ancient crypt contained many beautifully preserved sarcophagi.  古老的地穴里保存着许多保存完好的石棺。

The company uses crypts to store sensitive customer information. 公司使用加密存储区来存储敏感的客户信息。|近义词：vault, tomb, sepulcher |反义词：open space, surface
