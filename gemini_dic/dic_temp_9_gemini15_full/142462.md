oscitantly:/ˈɒsɪtəntli/ | adj. | 打哈欠地；昏昏欲睡地；无精打采地 | Yawning; in a drowsy or lethargic manner.  Showing a lack of energy or enthusiasm. |例句：He listened oscitantly to the long, boring lecture. 他昏昏欲睡地听着冗长乏味的讲座。

The cat sat oscitantly in the sunbeam, barely moving.  那只猫无精打采地躺在阳光下，几乎一动不动。

近义词：drowsily, sleepily, lethargically, languidly, listlessly

反义词：alertly, energetically, vibrantly, keenly

词源：源自拉丁语词根 "oscitans,"  现在分词形式 of "oscitare," meaning "to yawn."  This reflects the connection between yawning and drowsiness.
