culpably:/ˈkʌlpəbli/| adv.|应受谴责地，咎由自取地| deserving blame; blameworthy.  It describes an action or state that is deserving of blame or condemnation.|例句：He acted culpably in neglecting his duties. 他因忽视职责而应受谴责。

例句：She was culpably ignorant of the risks involved. 她对所涉及的风险竟如此无知，真是咎由自取。


近义词：blameworthily, guiltily, faultily, reprehensibly

反义词：innocently, blamelessly, justifiably


词性: 副词
