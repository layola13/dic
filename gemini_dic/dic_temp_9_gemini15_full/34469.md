clawed:/klɔːd/ | v. adj. | 爪抓；用爪抓伤；（动物）有爪的 | To scratch, scrape, or pull with claws.  Also describes something with claws or claw marks. | 例句：The cat clawed at the door. 猫用爪子抓门。 The victim had deep clawed wounds on his back. 受害者后背有深深的抓伤。|近义词：scratched, scratched at, clawed at | 反义词：caressed, stroked


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The cat clawed the furniture. = 猫抓坏了家具。
The bear clawed at the tree trunk. = 熊抓挠着树干。

一般过去时:
The dog clawed its way out of the box. = 狗自己从箱子里爬了出来。
She clawed at the attacker's face. = 她抓挠袭击者的脸。


现在完成时:
The kitten has clawed the sofa several times. = 小猫已经几次抓坏沙发了。
The bird has clawed its way to the top of the tree. = 鸟已经费力地爬到了树顶。


过去完成时:
The thief had clawed open the window before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，小偷已经用爪子撬开了窗户。
She had clawed her way to the top of her profession. = 她已经努力奋斗到了她职业的顶峰。


将来时:
The lion will claw at its prey. = 狮子会用爪子抓捕猎物。
I think the cat will claw the carpet again. = 我想猫还会再抓地毯。


虚拟语气:
If the cat were to claw me, I would scream. = 如果猫抓我，我会尖叫。
I wish the dog hadn't clawed the curtains. = 我真希望狗没有抓破窗帘。


被动语态:
The wood was clawed by a bear. = 木头被熊抓过了。
The door was clawed open. = 门被抓开了。


疑问句:
Did the cat claw your arm? = 猫抓你的胳膊了吗？
Has the dog clawed the rug? = 狗抓地毯了吗？


否定句:
The cat didn't claw the furniture. = 猫没有抓坏家具。
The bird did not claw at its cage. = 鸟没有抓笼子。


PHRASE->

fiercely clawed = 猛烈抓挠的
