honky-tonk:/ˈhɑːŋkiˌtɑːŋk/| n. adj.|喧闹的酒吧；乡土音乐；俗气的；|A honky-tonk is a small, usually rundown bar, often with live music, typically country or western.  The term can also be used to describe the music itself or a style that is considered gaudy or cheap.|例句：He spent his evenings in the honky-tonks of Nashville. 他在纳什维尔的乡村酒吧度过他的夜晚。
The band played some classic honky-tonk tunes. 乐队演奏了一些经典的乡村音乐。|近义词：dive bar, roadhouse, honky-tonk bar|反义词：upscale bar, elegant establishment
