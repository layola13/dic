module's:/ˈmɒdʒuːlz/ /ˈmɑːdʒuːlz/ | n. | 模块的；单元的 |  The possessive form of "module," indicating belonging or association with a module.  It refers to something that belongs to or is a part of a module.  For example, it might describe the functions of a module, the properties of a module, or the contents of a module. | Example:  The module's functions are clearly defined.  (该模块的功能定义明确。)  The project requires the module's complete source code. (该项目需要该模块的完整源代码。) | 近义词: unit's, component's, section's | 反义词:  None (possessive forms don't have direct antonyms) | noun (possessive)
