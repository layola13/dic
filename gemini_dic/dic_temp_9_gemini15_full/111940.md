lolling:/ˈlɒlɪŋ/ | adj., v. | 懒洋洋地；松弛地；闲荡；傻笑 |  Relaxed and drooping; hanging loosely; to lounge or recline lazily; to hang loosely.  |例句：The dog lay lolling in the sun.  那条狗懒洋洋地躺在阳光下。

The old man sat lolling in his armchair.  那位老人懒洋洋地坐在扶手椅上。


近义词：sagging, drooping, lounging, reclining, sprawling | 反义词：tense, upright, alert, stiff


词性：形容词，动词

词源:
The word "loll" ultimately derives from Old Norse *lulla, meaning "to swing" or "to dangle".  This is related to the Old English word *lullan, with a similar meaning.  The sense of lazy relaxation developed later.
