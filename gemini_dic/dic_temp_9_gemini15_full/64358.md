eration:/ˌɛrəˈreɪʃən/| n. | 世代；时代；一代人 | The process of producing offspring or the succession of offspring.  The word refers to a period of time roughly equivalent to the average lifespan of a generation of people. It can also refer to a specific generation of people, such as "the baby boomer generation." | Example:  My family has lived in this town for three generations.  我的家族在这个镇上住了三代人。

The current generation is more tech-savvy than previous ones.  当代人比前几代人更精通技术。| 近义词：generation, epoch, period | 反义词：N/A
