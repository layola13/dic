guffawed:/ɡʌˈfɔːd/| v.|哄笑，大笑|To laugh loudly and boisterously.  It implies a hearty, often unrestrained laughter.|The comedian's joke was so funny that the audience guffawed.|近义词：laugh uproariously, roar with laughter|反义词：whimper, sob
