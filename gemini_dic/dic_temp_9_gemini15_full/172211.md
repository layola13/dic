push-start:/pʊʃˈstɑːrt/| n. |按压启动(系统)| A system for starting an engine by pressing a button, usually without using a key.|例句：My new car has a push-start ignition system.我的新车有按压启动点火系统。|例句：The push-start button is located on the center console.按压启动按钮位于中控台上。|近义词：keyless ignition, button start|反义词：conventional ignition, key start|名词
