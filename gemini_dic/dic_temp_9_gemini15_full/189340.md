sag:/sæɡ/ | v. n. | 下垂；松弛；衰退 | To sink, droop, or hang down heavily; to become less firm or strong. |例句：The old roof sagged under the weight of the snow.  古老的屋顶在雪的重量下下垂了。|The economy is showing signs of sagging. 经济显示出衰退的迹象。|近义词：droop, slump, decline |反义词：rise, elevate, strengthen |动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The branches sag under the weight of the apples. = 苹果的重量压弯了树枝。
The old man's shoulders sag with age. = 老人双肩因衰老而下垂。


一般过去时:
The bridge sagged dangerously before collapsing. = 桥梁在坍塌前危险地向下倾斜。
The tent sagged in the middle after the rain. = 雨后帐篷中间下垂了。


现在完成时:
The roof has sagged considerably since the last storm. = 自从上次暴风雨以来，屋顶已经相当下垂了。
My spirits have sagged after hearing the bad news. = 听到坏消息后，我的精神萎靡不振了。


过去完成时:
The rope had sagged significantly before it finally broke. = 绳子在最终断裂之前已经明显下垂了。
The morale of the troops had sagged after days of fighting. = 经过几天的战斗后，士兵们的士气低落了。


将来时:
The floor will sag if you put too much weight on it. = 如果你在上面放太多的重量，地板就会下垂。
The market is expected to sag in the coming months. = 预计市场在未来几个月将会下滑。


虚拟语气:
If the supports were stronger, the bridge wouldn't sag so much. = 如果支撑更坚固，桥就不会下垂这么多。
I wish my energy wouldn't sag so much in the afternoon. = 我希望我的精力在下午不会下降这么多。


被动语态:
The line was sagged by the weight of the clothes. = 衣物的重量使绳子下垂了。
The branch was sagged by the heavy snow. = 大雪压弯了树枝。


疑问句:
Is the bridge sagging dangerously? = 桥梁是否危险地下垂？
Does the fabric sag after washing? = 洗后织物是否下垂？


否定句:
The new bridge doesn't sag. = 新桥没有下垂。
The building's foundation doesn't sag. = 建筑物的基础没有下垂。
