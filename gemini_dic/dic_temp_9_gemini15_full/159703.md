polybutadiene: /ˌpɒliˈbjuːtədaɪn/ | n. | 聚丁二烯 | A synthetic rubber made by polymerization of butadiene.  It is used in various applications, including tires, adhesives, and sealants.  The properties of polybutadiene can be adjusted by varying the polymerization process. | Example: Polybutadiene is a key component in many high-performance tires. 聚丁二烯是许多高性能轮胎的关键成分。  The adhesive contains polybutadiene for enhanced flexibility.  该粘合剂含有聚丁二烯以增强柔韧性。|近义词: synthetic rubber, elastomer |反义词:  (No direct antonym, but materials with opposite properties could be considered, such as rigid plastics)|
