rebaptized:/ˌriːˈbæptaɪzd/| v.|重新施洗，再次洗礼 |To baptize again; to subject to a second baptism, often implying a change of faith or a reaffirmation of commitment.|例句：He was rebaptized after converting to a different denomination. 他皈依不同的教派后重新受洗。|例句：The church offered rebaptism for those who felt their previous baptism was invalid. 教堂为那些觉得之前的洗礼无效的人提供重新洗礼。|近义词：rechristen, reconsecrate|反义词：unbaptize (not a common word)
