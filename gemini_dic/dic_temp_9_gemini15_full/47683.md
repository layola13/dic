debugged:/dɪˈbʌɡd/| v. |调试，排除故障|To remove errors or bugs from (a computer program or system).  |例句：The programmer spent hours debugging the software. 程序员花了数小时调试软件。

例句：After debugging the code, the program finally ran smoothly. 调试代码后，程序终于顺利运行了。


近义词：troubleshoot, fix, correct |反义词：error, bug, malfunction|动词
