carbonarism:/ˌkɑːrbəˈnærɪzəm/| n. |碳素主义；秘密结社活动；|The political and social movement characterized by secret societies and revolutionary plots, especially in Italy during the 19th century.  Carbonarism was a powerful force in the Risorgimento, aiming to unify Italy and overthrow foreign rule.|例句：The rise of Carbonarism in Italy reflected the widespread dissatisfaction with Austrian rule.  意大利碳素主义的兴起反映了对奥地利统治的普遍不满。|近义词：Conspiracy, secret society, revolutionary movement |反义词：conformity, obedience
