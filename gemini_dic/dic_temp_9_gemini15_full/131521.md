noncomedogenic:/ˌnɒnkɒmɪdəˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 不致粉刺的，不生粉刺的 | Not likely to cause acne.  This term is used to describe skincare products and ingredients that are less likely to clog pores and cause breakouts. | Example:  Many dermatologists recommend using noncomedogenic moisturizers to prevent acne. 许多皮肤科医生建议使用不致粉刺的保湿霜来预防痤疮。

|近义词:acne-free, pore-unclogging |反义词:comedogenic
