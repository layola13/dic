hardfern:/ˈhɑːrdfɜːrn/ | n. |硬蕨 | A type of fern with stiff, leathery fronds, typically found in dry or exposed habitats.  Hard ferns are generally more resistant to drought and harsh conditions than other ferns. |Example:  The hardfern thrived even in the rocky, sun-baked hillside.  硬蕨甚至在岩石嶙峋、阳光曝晒的山坡上也能茁壮成长。 |近义词:  tough fern, rock fern |反义词:  delicate fern, soft fern
