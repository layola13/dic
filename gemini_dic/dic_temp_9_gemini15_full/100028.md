interpervading: /ˌɪntərˈpɜːrveɪdɪŋ/ | adj. | 弥漫的；渗透的；遍布的 |  Existing or spreading throughout; permeating.  This word suggests a more complete and subtle spread than simply "permeating." It implies a deep influence and integration. |

例句：

A sense of unease was interpervading the entire atmosphere.  = 一种不安的情绪弥漫在整个氛围中。

The philosophy of the time was interpervading all aspects of society. = 当时的哲学渗透到社会的各个方面。


近义词：permeating, pervading, saturating, suffusing


反义词：isolated, separated, confined


词性:形容词
