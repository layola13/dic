infections:/ɪnˈfɛkʃənz/ | n. | 感染（疾病）；传染病 | The plural form of "infection," referring to instances of disease caused by pathogenic microorganisms.  Infections can range from mild to life-threatening, depending on the causative agent and the individual's immune response. | Example:  The hospital is taking precautions to prevent the spread of infections.  医院正在采取预防措施以防止感染的蔓延。


The doctor treated her infections promptly.医生迅速治疗了她的感染。|近义词：contaminations, infestations |反义词：sterility, health
