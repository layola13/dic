reflectances:/rɪˈflɛktənsɪz/| n.|反射率（复数）|The measure of how much light or other radiation is reflected by a surface.  It is usually expressed as a percentage or a decimal fraction. |例句：The reflectances of different materials vary widely. 不同材料的反射率差异很大。
The high reflectances of the snow caused the area to appear brighter. 雪的高反射率使该区域看起来更明亮。|近义词：reflectivity, reflection coefficients|反义词：absorptivity
