pyramid: /ˈpɪrəmɪd/ | n. |金字塔；锥体；(等级制度的)层级 | A pyramid is a structure with a square or triangular base and sloping sides that meet in a point at the top.  It can refer to a geometrical shape or a social structure.  The word often evokes images of ancient Egyptian monuments. |例句：The Great Pyramid of Giza is one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.  吉萨大金字塔是古代世界七大奇迹之一。

例句：The company's organizational structure is a pyramid, with the CEO at the top. 这家公司的组织结构是一个金字塔，CEO位于顶端。


近义词：cone, spire, hierarchy | 反义词：flat, horizontal

词源:
The word "pyramid" comes from the Greek word "pyramis," which ultimately derives from the Egyptian word for a pyramid. The Greek word was adopted into Latin and then into other European languages.  The Egyptian origin reflects the structures' prominence in ancient Egyptian culture.
