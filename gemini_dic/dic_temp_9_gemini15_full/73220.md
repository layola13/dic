forcers: /ˈfɔːrsərz/ | n. | 强迫者；强制因素 | People or things that force something to happen.  Those who compel or coerce;  influencing factors that compel a certain outcome. |例句：The economic forcers behind the decision were undeniable.  经济因素促成了这一决定是不可否认的。

The rising sea levels are powerful forcers of coastal erosion.  海平面上升是导致海岸侵蚀的强大因素。


近义词：compellers, coercers, drivers, agents, influences | 反义词：enablers (in some contexts)

词源:  From the verb "force," ultimately from Latin *fortis ("strong"). The suffix "-er" indicates an agent or doer of the action.
