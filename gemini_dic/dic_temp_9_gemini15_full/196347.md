serif:/ˈsɛrɪf/ | n. |衬线(字体)|A serif is a small decorative stroke that finishes off a main stroke of a letter, used in many typefaces.  Serifs are often found in traditional typefaces and can give the text a more formal or classic feel. |Example:  The book was printed in a serif typeface. 这本书是用衬线字体印刷的。|近义词：stroke, flourish|反义词：sans-serif
