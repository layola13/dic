hawkster:/ˈhɔːkstər/| n. | 骗子，江湖郎中，奸商 | A hawkster is someone who is considered to be a swindler, a charlatan, or a dishonest salesperson, often using aggressive or manipulative tactics. They prey on the gullible and exploit their trust for personal gain.  |  He's a real hawkster, selling fake miracle cures to desperate people. 他是个十足的骗子，向绝望的人们兜售虚假的灵丹妙药。| 近义词：con man, swindler, charlatan, scammer | 反义词：honest person, trustworthy person
