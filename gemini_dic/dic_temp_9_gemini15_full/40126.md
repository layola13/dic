contemplation:/ˌkɒntɛmˈpleɪʃən/| n. |沉思，冥想；注视 |The act of thinking deeply and carefully about something.  It often involves prolonged reflection and consideration. |例句：She sat in quiet contemplation, gazing at the ocean. 她静静地沉思着，凝视着大海。

例句：After hours of contemplation, he finally made a decision.经过几个小时的沉思，他终于做出了决定。

近义词：meditation, reflection, consideration, pondering |反义词：impulsiveness, hastiness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He contemplates his future every day. = 他每天都在思考他的未来。
She contemplates the meaning of life. = 她思考着人生的意义。


一般过去时:
He contemplated the problem for hours. = 他思考这个问题几个小时。
She contemplated her options carefully. = 她仔细考虑了她的选择。


现在完成时:
I have contemplated this decision for weeks. = 我已经考虑这个决定几周了。
They have contemplated the consequences of their actions. = 他们已经思考了他们行为的后果。


过去完成时:
He had contemplated quitting his job for months before he finally did it. = 在他最终辞职之前，他已经考虑辞职好几个月了。
She had contemplated the possibility of moving abroad for years. = 她多年来一直在考虑搬到国外居住的可能性。


将来时:
I will contemplate my options before I answer. = 我会在回答之前仔细考虑我的选择。
She will contemplate the offer carefully. = 她会仔细考虑这个提议。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would contemplate the matter further. = 如果我有更多时间，我会进一步思考这个问题。
I wish I had contemplated the risks more carefully. = 我希望我当时更仔细地考虑了风险。


被动语态:
The problem was contemplated by the committee. = 这个问题由委员会仔细考虑了。
The proposal is being contemplated by the board. = 董事会正在考虑该提案。


疑问句:
What are you contemplating? = 你在思考什么？
What were they contemplating at the meeting? = 会议上他们在考虑什么？


否定句:
He did not contemplate the possibility of failure. = 他没有考虑失败的可能性。
She did not contemplate the long-term consequences. = 她没有考虑长远的后果。


PHRASE->
in contemplation of = 考虑到；为了
lost in contemplation = 陷入沉思
deep contemplation = 深思熟虑
a moment of contemplation = 一刻的沉思
subject to contemplation = 值得考虑的
