conception:/kənˈsɛpʃən/| n.|概念，构想，怀孕|The act of conceiving; the formation of a concept or idea in the mind.  It can also refer to the process of becoming pregnant.|例句：His conception of justice is quite different from mine. 他的正义观与我的大相径庭。

例句：The conception of the new building took many months of planning. 新建筑的设计花了几个月的时间规划。

近义词：idea, notion, thought, understanding,  imagining, pregnancy

反义词：misconception, misunderstanding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist's conception of the scene is breathtaking. = 艺术家对场景的构想令人叹为观止。
My conception of happiness involves travel and adventure. = 我对幸福的理解包括旅行和冒险。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:
