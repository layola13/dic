gantry:/ˈɡæn.tri/| n.|龙门架，门式起重机|A gantry is a structure, typically a framework of beams and girders, supporting a hoist or crane that can move along it.  Gantries are used to lift and move heavy objects in various industrial settings. |例句：The workers used a gantry crane to lift the heavy steel beams into place. 工人们用龙门起重机将沉重的钢梁吊装到位。The gantry spanned the entire factory floor.  龙门架跨越了整个工厂车间。|近义词：crane (in some contexts), hoisting structure|反义词：无
