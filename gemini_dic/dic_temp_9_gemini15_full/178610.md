recouped:/rɪˈkuːpt/| vt.|恢复；补偿；挽回|To regain or recover something lost or spent.  It can refer to recovering from illness, regaining a loss (financial or otherwise), or recovering something stolen.|例句：The company recouped its losses within a year. 公司在一年内收回了损失。

例句：After a long illness, she finally recouped her strength.  一场大病之后，她终于恢复了体力。


近义词：recover, regain, recoup, compensate, reimburse


反义词：lose, forfeit


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recoups his losses through diligent work. 他通过勤奋工作弥补了损失。
The company recoups its investment through increased sales. 公司通过增加销售额来收回投资。


一般过去时:
She recouped her energy after a restful night's sleep. 她经过一夜安宁的睡眠后恢复了精力。
The farmer recouped his harvest after the storm.  暴风雨过后，农民收回了他的收成。


现在完成时:
They have recouped all their expenses from the project. 他们已经收回了项目的所有开支。
We have recouped our initial investment. 我们已经收回了最初的投资。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, the company had recouped its losses. 到年底，公司已经收回了损失。
She had recouped her strength before the marathon.  马拉松比赛开始前，她已经恢复了体力。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

If she had rested more, she would have recouped her strength more quickly. 如果她休息更多，她就能更快地恢复体力。


被动语态:

词源:
The word "recouped" comes from the Old French word "recouper," meaning "to cut back" or "to cut again".  This ultimately derives from the Latin "re-" (again) and "capere" (to take).  The semantic shift from "cutting back" to "regaining" likely occurred over time.
