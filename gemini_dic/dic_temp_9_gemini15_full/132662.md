nondisinterested:/ˌnɒndɪsˈɪntərɪstɪd/| adj. |无私的，公正的|Not influenced by personal feelings or selfish interests; impartial.  This word is a less common and somewhat archaic alternative to "unbiased" or "impartial." It emphasizes the absence of self-interest, suggesting a commitment to objectivity even more strongly than "unbiased."|The judge's nondisinterested opinion was crucial to the case's fair resolution.  法官无私的意见对案件的公正解决至关重要。|unbiased, impartial, objective|biased, prejudiced, partial|形容词
