self-reckonings:/ˈsɛlf ˈrɛkənɪŋz/| n.|自我反省；自我审视|The act of examining one's own thoughts, feelings, and actions; self-assessment; introspection.|例句：After the argument, she engaged in some serious self-reckonings about her behavior.  争吵之后，她认真地进行了自我反省。|例句：His self-reckonings led him to a better understanding of his motivations. 他的自我反省使他更好地理解了自己的动机。|近义词：introspection, self-examination, self-analysis|反义词：self-deception, denial|名词
