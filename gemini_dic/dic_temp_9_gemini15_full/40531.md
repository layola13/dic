controllingly:/kənˈtroʊlɪŋli/ | adv. | 控制地，支配地 |  In a controlling manner; showing a desire to control others.  This adverb describes actions or behaviors that exhibit a dominating or manipulative tendency. | Example: He spoke controllingly, as if expecting immediate obedience. 他控制性地说话，好像期望立即服从一样。  She looked at him controllingly, her eyes narrowed. 她控制性地望着他，眼睛眯了起来。|近义词：dominatingly, authoritatively, manipulatively | 反义词：submissively, permissively


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't have tense conjugations like verbs.  It describes the *manner* of an action.

Example Sentence Structures:

He acted controllingly. 他行为专制。
She spoke controllingly to her employees. 她对员工说话专横。
They behaved controllingly throughout the meeting. 他们在整个会议期间表现得控制欲很强。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases directly using "controllingly".  The word is used to modify verbs describing actions.  Phrases would be formed by combining "controllingly" with verbs describing behavior, such as:
