mergers:/ˈmɜːrdʒərz/ | n. | 合并；兼并 | The joining of two or more companies or organizations to form a single entity. This often happens to increase efficiency, expand market share, or gain access to new technologies or resources.  The process can be complex, involving legal, financial, and operational considerations. | Example: The recent mergers in the airline industry have led to higher ticket prices. 最近航空业的合并导致机票价格上涨。The two companies announced their merger last week. 两家公司上周宣布合并。 | 近义词：consolidations, amalgamations | 反义词：divisions, splits | 名词
