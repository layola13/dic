junketeers:/dʒʌŋˈkiːtərz/ | n. | 寻欢作乐者；公款旅游者 | People who take trips paid for by someone else, often for pleasure or personal gain, especially those using public funds.  They often engage in lavish spending and activities during these trips. | The junketeers spent the taxpayers' money on extravagant dinners and expensive hotels.  公款旅游者把纳税人的钱花在了奢侈的晚餐和昂贵的酒店上。  The scandal involved several high-ranking officials who were revealed to be frequent junketeers.  丑闻牵涉到几名被揭露经常公款旅游的高级官员。 | pleasure-seekers, sightseers, trippers |  hard workers, frugal people | 名词
