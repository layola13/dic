gabel:/ˈɡeɪbəl/| n.|山墙；叉；(尤指两股) 叉状物 |A gable is the triangular part of a wall at the end of a pitched roof.  A gable can also refer to a forked object, especially one with two prongs. |例句：The house had a charming gable end.  这房子有一个迷人的山墙端。  The farmer used a gable to lift the hay.  这位农民用一个叉子来抬起干草。|近义词：peak, apex, fork |反义词：base, foundation
