clamp-down:/klæmpˈdaʊn/| n. v.|压制；严厉打击；加强管制|The act of suppressing something, typically by enforcing stricter rules or regulations.  A forceful action to stop something undesirable.|例句：There has been a clampdown on illegal drug trafficking.  = 对非法贩毒活动进行了严厉打击。

例句：The government implemented a clampdown on immigration. = 政府对移民实施了更严格的管制。


近义词：crackdown, suppression, restriction, enforcement

反义词：leniency, relaxation, liberalization


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government clamps down on illegal activities. = 政府严厉打击非法活动。
They clamp down on noise pollution. = 他们压制噪音污染。

一般过去时:
The police clamped down on the protests. = 警察严厉镇压了抗议活动。
The school clamped down on cheating. = 学校严厉打击作弊行为。

现在完成时:
Authorities have clamped down on corruption. = 当局已经严厉打击腐败。
They have clamped down on unauthorized gatherings. = 他们已经禁止未经授权的集会。

过去完成时:
The company had clamped down on expenses before the crisis. = 公司在危机爆发前已经控制了开支。
They had clamped down on the spread of misinformation. = 他们已经控制了虚假信息的传播。

将来时:
The administration will clamp down on tax evasion. = 政府将严厉打击逃税行为。
They will clamp down on the use of illegal drugs. = 他们将严厉打击毒品的使用。

虚拟语气:
If the government clamped down harder, the problem might be solved. = 如果政府采取更强硬的措施，这个问题或许就能解决。
I wish they would clamp down on these irresponsible drivers. = 我希望他们能严厉打击这些不负责任的司机。

被动语态:
Illegal gambling was clamped down on by the police. = 非法赌博受到了警方的严厉打击。
The spread of the virus was clamped down on by strict quarantine measures. = 通过严格的隔离措施控制了病毒的传播。

疑问句:
Will the authorities clamp down on the rising crime rate? = 当局会严厉打击不断上升的犯罪率吗？
Did the school clamp down on bullying effectively? = 学校有效地打击了欺凌行为吗？

否定句:
The government did not clamp down on the protests as expected. = 政府没有像预期的那样严厉镇压抗议活动。
They didn't clamp down hard enough on the problem. = 他们对这个问题的打击力度不够。
