preen:/priːn/| vt. vi.|打扮，梳理羽毛；自鸣得意|To make oneself neat and tidy, especially in a vain or conceited way; to smooth and clean one's feathers. |例句：The peacock preened its feathers.  孔雀梳理着它的羽毛。

The actress preened herself before the mirror.  女演员对着镜子打扮自己。


近义词：groom, primp, strut, preen oneself


反义词：neglect, ignore, slouch


词性:动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preens his hair every morning. = 他每天早上都梳理头发。
She preens herself before going out. = 她出门前都会打扮一番。


一般过去时:
The bird preened its feathers after bathing. = 洗完澡后，鸟儿梳理羽毛。
He preened himself on his success. = 他为自己的成功而沾沾自喜。


现在完成时:
She has preened herself for the party. = 她已经打扮好了准备去参加派对。
He has preened his feathers all day. = 他一整天都在自我陶醉。


过去完成时:
She had preened her hair before the interview. = 面试前她已经梳理好了头发。
He had preened himself for hours before the meeting. = 会议前他已经自我陶醉了好几个小时。


将来时:
She will preen her hair before the wedding. = 她将在婚礼前梳理头发。
He will preen himself on his achievement. = 他将为自己的成就而自鸣得意。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would preen myself more. = 如果我有更多时间，我会打扮得更漂亮。
I wish I could preen myself like her. = 我希望我能像她那样打扮自己。


被动语态:
His hair was preened to perfection. = 他的头发梳理得完美无缺。
Her appearance was preened for the occasion. = 她的外表是为这次场合精心打扮的。


疑问句:
Does he preen his feathers often? = 他经常梳理羽毛吗？
Does she preen herself before every date? = 她每次约会前都会打扮自己吗？


否定句:
He didn't preen his hair this morning. = 他今天早上没有梳理头发。
She didn't preen herself before the interview. = 面试前她没有打扮自己。


PHRASE->
preen oneself = 自鸣得意
preen one's feathers = 梳理羽毛；自鸣得意
to preen and posture = 摆姿势，炫耀
preen with pride = 骄傲地打扮
preening and posing = 梳妆打扮并摆姿势


词源:
源自古法语 *prenier，意为“清洁，梳理”，其词根可能与古法语 *prunier（修剪）有关。最终追溯到拉丁语 *pruīna（霜），可能暗示了梳理羽毛以去除霜冻或污垢的含义。
