catnapped:/kætˈnæpt/| v.|打盹儿；小睡|To take a short nap, usually unexpectedly or unintentionally.  It implies a brief and light sleep.|例句：I catnapped on the couch after lunch. 我午饭后在沙发上打了个盹儿。

例句：The lecture was so boring that I almost catnapped. 讲座太无聊了，我差点睡着了。


近义词：dozed, napped, slumbered|反义词：stayed awake, remained alert|词性：动词
