perineurial:/ˌpɛrɪniˈʊəriəl/| adj.|周围神经膜的；神经束膜的| Relating to or affecting the perineurium, the connective tissue sheath surrounding a nerve fascicle (a bundle of nerve fibers).  | Example: The perineurial sheath plays a crucial role in protecting the nerve fibers. 周围神经膜鞘在保护神经纤维方面起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：perineural (有时可互换使用，但 perineurial 更精确地指 perineurium)|反义词：None specifically, but words describing conditions affecting other nerve structures could be considered antonyms in a contextual sense.
