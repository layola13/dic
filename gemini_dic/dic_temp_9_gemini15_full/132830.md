nonedible: /ˌnɒnˈedɪbl/ | adj. | 不可食用的，不能吃的 | Not suitable or safe for eating.  | Example:  Many wild mushrooms are nonedible and could be poisonous. 许多野生蘑菇不可食用，可能有毒。


The berries growing on that bush are nonedible.  那个灌木丛上长的浆果不可食用。


近义词: inedible, uneatable, unfit to eat | 反义词: edible, palatable


词性: adjective
