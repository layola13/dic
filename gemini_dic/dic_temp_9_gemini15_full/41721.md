corrode:/kəˈroʊd/|/kəˈrəʊd/| vt.|腐蚀，侵蚀|To gradually destroy (a metal, stone, etc.) by chemical action; to be gradually destroyed in this way.|例句：Acid rain corrodes stone statues. 酸雨腐蚀石像。

The sea water corroded the metal pipes. 海水腐蚀了金属管道。


近义词：erode, wear away, eat away|反义词：protect, preserve, strengthen|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The acid corrodes the metal slowly. = 酸缓慢地腐蚀金属。
Rust corrodes iron. = 锈腐蚀铁。


一般过去时:
The statue was corroded by the rain. = 雕像被雨水腐蚀了。
The pipe corroded over time. = 管子随着时间推移而腐蚀了。


现在完成时:
The acid has corroded the metal completely. = 酸已经完全腐蚀了金属。
The rain has corroded the stonework. = 雨水腐蚀了石砌建筑。


过去完成时:
The metal had corroded before we noticed it. = 我们注意到之前，金属就已经腐蚀了。
The building's foundation had corroded due to years of neglect. = 由于多年的忽视，建筑的地基已经腐蚀了。


将来时:
The salt water will corrode the boat hull. = 海水会腐蚀船体。
The pipeline will corrode if not properly protected. = 如果没有得到适当的保护，管道将会腐蚀。


虚拟语气:
If the metal were exposed to the acid, it would corrode quickly. = 如果金属暴露在酸中，它会很快腐蚀。
I wish the corrosion hadn't corroded the engine so badly. = 我真希望腐蚀没有严重腐蚀发动机。


被动语态:
The metal is corroded by the chemicals. = 金属被化学物质腐蚀。
The bridge was corroded by the sea. = 桥梁被海水腐蚀了。


疑问句:
Does the acid corrode the plastic? = 酸会腐蚀塑料吗？
Has the salt water corroded the engine? = 海水腐蚀了发动机吗？


否定句:
The coating prevents the metal from corroding. = 涂层防止金属腐蚀。
The stainless steel doesn't corrode easily. = 不锈钢不容易腐蚀。


PHRASE->
corrosive action  腐蚀作用
electrochemical corrosion 电化学腐蚀
stress corrosion 应力腐蚀
corrosion inhibitor 缓蚀剂
corrosion resistance 抗腐蚀性


词源:
From Latin *corrodere, from com- + rodere "to gnaw."  字面意思是“一起啃噬”。
