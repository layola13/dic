degree:/dɪˈɡriː/| n.|程度，等级，学位|A degree is a unit of measurement of angles, temperature, or other quantities.  It can also refer to an academic rank achieved after completing a course of study at a college or university. |例句：The temperature reached a high degree of 35 degrees Celsius. 温度达到了35摄氏度的高温。|例句：She earned a degree in engineering from MIT. 她获得了麻省理工学院的工程学位。|近义词：level, extent, rank, qualification |反义词：insignificance, triviality
