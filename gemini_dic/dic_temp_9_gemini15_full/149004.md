ovulation:/ˌɒvjuˈleɪʃən/| n. |排卵|The release of a mature egg from the ovary.  This process is crucial for conception, as it makes the egg available to be fertilized by sperm.|例句：Ovulation typically occurs around day 14 of a 28-day menstrual cycle.  排卵通常发生在28天月经周期的第14天左右。|例句：Understanding your ovulation cycle can help you plan a pregnancy or avoid it. 了解你的排卵周期可以帮助你计划怀孕或避免怀孕。|近义词：egg release, egg discharge|反义词：menstruation (in the sense of the opposite phase of the menstrual cycle)
