hedgerow: /ˈhedʒroʊ/ | n. | 树篱；灌木丛 | A row of shrubs or bushes, typically forming a boundary.  | Example:  The birds nested in the dense hedgerow.  鸟儿在茂密的树篱里筑巢。  The old hedgerow separated the fields.  古老的树篱隔开了田野。 | 近义词：fence, boundary, thicket | 反义词：open field


USAGE->

The hedgerow protects the fields from the wind. = 树篱保护田地免受风害。
That hedgerow is full of wildflowers. = 那条树篱里长满了野花。
