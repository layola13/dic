epistle:/ɪˈpɪsəl/| n. |书信；（尤指）长篇正式信件|A formal or official letter, especially a long one.  The apostle Paul wrote many epistles to the early Christian churches.|例句：The professor received an epistle from a former student. 教授收到了一位前学生的来信。
例句：The ancient scroll contained several epistles from emperors.  古老的卷轴包含了几个皇帝的书信。|近义词：letter, message, communication|反义词：telegram (in terms of length and formality)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He writes epistles to his family every week. = 他每周都给家人写信。
She sends epistles to her pen pal in another country. = 她给在另一个国家的笔友寄信。


一般过去时:
He wrote a long epistle to his friend. = 他给朋友写了一封长信。
She received an epistle from her grandfather. = 她收到了她祖父的来信。


现在完成时:
He has written many epistles throughout his life. = 他一生写过许多信。
She has received several epistles from her publisher. = 她收到了她出版商的几封信。


过去完成时:
He had written the epistle before the deadline. = 他在截止日期之前写好了信。
She had received the epistle before the meeting. = 她在会议之前收到了那封信。


将来时:
He will write an epistle to the editor. = 他将给编辑写一封信。
She will send an epistle to her family. = 她将给家人寄一封信。


虚拟语气:
If he had written an epistle, she would have answered. = 如果他写信了，她就会回复。
I wish I had written an epistle sooner. = 我真希望我早点写信。


被动语态:
The epistle was written in elegant prose. = 这封信是用优美的散文写的。
The epistle is being read by the committee. = 这封信正在由委员会阅读。


疑问句:
Did you write an epistle to him? = 你给他写信了吗？
Has she received my epistle yet? = 她收到我的信了吗？


否定句:
He didn't write an epistle to her. = 他没有给她写信。
She hasn't received the epistle yet. = 她还没收到那封信。
