feedings:/ˈfiːdɪŋz/| n. |喂食；进食；饲养|The act of giving food to someone or something.  It can refer to a single instance of feeding or a series of feedings.  The plural form indicates multiple instances. |例句：The baby's feedings are scheduled every three hours. = 婴儿的喂食时间安排在每三个小时一次。

The daily feedings of the livestock are crucial for their health. = 牲畜的日常喂食对它们的健康至关重要。|近义词：nourishment, sustenance, meals|反义词：starvation, fasting

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer feeds the chickens twice a day. = 农民每天喂鸡两次。
Regular feedings are essential for a newborn baby's growth. = 定期喂养对新生儿的成长至关重要。

一般过去时:
She fed the stray cat a bowl of milk. = 她给流浪猫喂了一碗牛奶。
The zookeeper carefully fed the lions their lunch. = 动物园管理员仔细地喂狮子吃午饭。

现在完成时:
They have finished feeding all the animals. = 他们已经喂完了所有的动物。
We have fed the dog, so he's quiet now. = 我们已经喂过狗了，所以他现在很安静。

过去完成时:
The birds had already been fed before the storm hit. =暴风雨来临之前，鸟儿们已经被喂过了。
She had fed the baby before putting him to bed. = 她哄宝宝睡觉前已经喂过他了。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
