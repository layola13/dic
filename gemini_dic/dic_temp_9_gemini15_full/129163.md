no-shows:/ˈnoʊ ʃoʊz/| n. |爽约者；未出席者 |People who fail to attend an appointment or event without giving notice.  |例句：The restaurant had a high number of no-shows on Friday night.  周五晚上这家餐馆有很多爽约者。|例句：Due to the numerous no-shows, the seminar had to be cancelled. 由于大量的爽约，研讨会不得不取消。|近义词：absentees, non-attendees |反义词：attendees, participants |名词
