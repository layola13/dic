portension:/pɔːrˈtɛnʃən/| n. |预兆；征兆；预感|A portent or omen, especially of something bad; a foreboding.|The dark clouds were a portension of the storm to come.  乌云是即将到来的暴风雨的预兆。|The uneasy silence was a portension of trouble. 不安的沉默预示着麻烦。|omen, presentiment, foreboding|auspiciousness, good omen|名词
