half-sunken:/ˈhɑːf ˈsʌŋkən/| adj.|半沉的，半没入的|Partially submerged or sunk.  Describes something that is only partly underwater or buried.|The half-sunken ship was a hazard to navigation. 半沉的船只对航行构成危险。 The half-sunken treasure chest lay just beneath the surface. 半沉的宝箱就在水面下。|submerged, partially submerged|fully emerged, afloat|形容词
