manuscripts:/ˈmænjuˌskrɪpts/| n.|手稿；原稿|Written documents or texts, especially those of historical or literary significance, before printing.  They may be handwritten or typed.|例句：The museum houses a collection of ancient manuscripts.  博物馆收藏着许多古代手稿。|例句：Scholars spent years studying the newly discovered manuscripts. 学者们花了数年时间研究新发现的手稿。|近义词：documents, writings, texts|反义词：print, publication
