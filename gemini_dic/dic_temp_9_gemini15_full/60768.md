electrolytic:/ˌɪˌlektrəˈlɪtɪk/| adj. |电解的；电解作用的 | Relating to or involving electrolysis.  Electrolytic processes use an electric current to drive a chemical reaction.  |例句：Electrolytic cells are used in many industrial processes. 电解池被用于许多工业过程中。

例句：The electrolytic refining of copper produces high-purity metal.  电解精炼铜能生产高纯度的金属。


近义词：electrolytical


反义词：None (no direct antonym, but the opposite would be a non-electrolytic process)


词性：形容词
