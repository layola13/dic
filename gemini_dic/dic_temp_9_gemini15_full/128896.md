例句：She nimbly avoided the oncoming car. 她敏捷地避开了迎面而来的汽车。


近义词：agility, deftly, lightly, quickly, spryly |反义词：clumsily, awkwardly, heavily, slowly

词源:
The word "nimbly" is derived from the Middle English word "nimble," which in turn comes from the Old English word "niman," meaning "to take."  The sense of quickness and dexterity evolved from the idea of being quick to seize or grasp something.
