It operates in a centralized or unified manner. | Example: The software operates nondistributively, meaning all processing occurs on a single server.  这个软件是非分布式运行的，这意味着所有处理都在一台服务器上进行。 | 近义词: centrally, unitarily | 反义词: distributively, decentralizedly
