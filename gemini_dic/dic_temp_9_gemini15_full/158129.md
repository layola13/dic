plated:/ˈpleɪtɪd/| adj.|镀层的；电镀的|Covered with a thin layer of metal, typically for protection or decoration.  |例句：The jewelry was plated with gold. 这件首饰镀金了。|例句：He bought a plated silver tray. 他买了一个镀银的托盘。|近义词：coated, covered, overlaid|反义词：unplated, bare
