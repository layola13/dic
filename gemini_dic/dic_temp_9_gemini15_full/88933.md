holding:/ˈhoʊldɪŋ/ | n. v. | 保持；持有；抓住；举行；拥有；支撑；包含 |


英文解析:
As a noun, "holding" refers to something that is held, possessed, or owned. It can also refer to an act of holding something or a meeting or event. As a verb, "holding" refers to the action of keeping something in one's possession or grasp, or to sustaining something.  It can also refer to the act of organizing or conducting an event.

例句:
The company's holdings include several large office buildings.  这家公司的资产包括几栋大型办公楼。

The police officer was holding the suspect at gunpoint.  警察用枪指着嫌疑犯。


近义词:
possession, grasp, grip, control, ownership, seizure,  (verb) retaining, keeping, maintaining


反义词:
releasing, relinquishing, letting go, abandoning (depending on context)


词性:
Noun, Verb
