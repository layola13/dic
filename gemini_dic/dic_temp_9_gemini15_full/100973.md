invader:/ɪnˈveɪdər/| n. |入侵者，侵略者|A person or thing that invades a place or country.  Someone or something that intrudes or encroaches upon something.|例句：The invaders were quickly defeated by the army. 入侵者很快就被军队击败了。|例句：The weeds are invaders of my carefully tended garden. 杂草侵占了我精心照料的花园。|近义词：aggressor, conqueror, intruder, trespasser |反义词：defender, protector, liberator


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The invader occupies the land. = 入侵者占领了这片土地。
The invaders threaten the local population. = 入侵者威胁着当地居民。

一般过去时:
The invader conquered the city. = 入侵者征服了这座城市。
The invaders destroyed many buildings. = 入侵者摧毁了许多建筑物。

现在完成时:
The invaders have taken control of the region. = 入侵者已经控制了该地区。
The invaders have caused significant damage. = 入侵者造成了重大损失。

过去完成时:
The invaders had already established a base before the counterattack. = 反攻之前，入侵者已经建立了基地。
The army had anticipated the invaders' strategy. = 军队已经预料到了入侵者的战略。

将来时:
The invaders will likely face strong resistance. = 入侵者很可能会面临强烈的抵抗。
The invaders will attempt to seize the capital. = 入侵者将试图夺取首都。

虚拟语气:

疑问句:
