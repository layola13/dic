observantly:/əbˈzɜːrvəntli/| adv.|细心地；观察入微地|In a way that shows careful observation and attention to detail.  It implies a keen awareness of one's surroundings and the ability to notice subtle things that others might miss.|例句：She observantly noted the slight tremor in his hand. 她细心地注意到他手微微颤抖。

例句：He observantly watched the children playing in the park, noticing even their small gestures. 他仔细地观察孩子们在公园里玩耍，甚至注意到他们细微的动作。


近义词：attentively, carefully, keenly, watchfully


反义词：casually, carelessly, inattentively


词性: 副词
