self-knowledge:/ˈsɛlf ˈnɑːlɪdʒ/| n. |自我认知，自我了解 |Understanding of one's own character, feelings, motives, and desires.  It involves a deep awareness of one's strengths, weaknesses, values, beliefs, and how these factors influence thoughts, feelings, and behaviors.  |Example:  Developing self-knowledge is crucial for personal growth and making informed decisions.  发展自我认知对于个人成长和做出明智的决定至关重要。


Example:  Through introspection and self-reflection, she gained valuable self-knowledge. 通过反省和自我反思，她获得了宝贵的自我认知。


近义词：self-awareness, self-understanding, introspection |反义词：self-deception, ignorance, unawareness |词性：名词
