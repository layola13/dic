investigated:/ɪnˈvɛstɪɡeɪtɪd/ | v. |调查，研究|To have made a systematic or formal inquiry into a matter; to have examined or researched something thoroughly. |例句：The police investigated the crime scene carefully. 警方仔细调查了犯罪现场。

例句：The scientist investigated the properties of the new material. 科学家研究了这种新材料的特性。


近义词：examined, explored, researched, probed, scrutinized | 反义词：ignored, neglected

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police investigate crimes. = 警方调查犯罪案件。
Scientists investigate the causes of diseases. = 科学家研究疾病的起因。

一般过去时:
The detective investigated the case thoroughly. = 侦探彻底调查了这个案件。
They investigated the accident to find the cause. = 他们调查了事故以找到原因。

现在完成时:
The committee has investigated the matter and submitted its report. = 委员会已经调查了此事并提交了报告。
Scientists have investigated the effects of climate change for decades. = 科学家们几十年来一直在研究气候变化的影响。

过去完成时:
The police had investigated the suspect before arresting him. = 警方在逮捕他之前已经调查过嫌疑人了。
Before the trial, the lawyers had investigated the witness's testimony. = 在审判之前，律师们已经调查过证人的证词。

将来时:
The authorities will investigate the allegations of corruption. = 当局将调查腐败指控。
We will investigate the possibility of a new approach. = 我们将研究一种新方法的可能性。

虚拟语气:
If the police had investigated more thoroughly, they might have found the culprit. = 如果警方调查更彻底一些，他们可能已经找到了罪犯。
I wish they had investigated the problem more carefully. = 我希望他们更仔细地调查这个问题。

被动语态:
The case was investigated by the FBI. = 此案由联邦调查局调查。
The matter is being investigated by a special committee. = 此事正在由一个特别委员会调查。

疑问句:
Has the accident been investigated yet? = 事故已经调查过了吗？
Will the complaint be investigated thoroughly? = 投诉会得到彻底调查吗？

否定句:
The committee did not investigate the claims properly. = 委员会没有妥善调查这些说法。
They have not yet investigated the full extent of the damage. = 他们尚未调查损害的全部程度。
