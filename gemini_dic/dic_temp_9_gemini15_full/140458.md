old-fashioned:/ˌoʊldˈfæʃənd/| adj.|过时的；老式的；老派作风的|  Something old-fashioned is no longer in style or is considered outdated. It can refer to styles, methods, or ideas that were popular in the past but are now considered old-fashioned.  |例句：Her dress was old-fashioned, but elegant.她的裙子过时了，但却很优雅。

Her cooking methods are quite old-fashioned.她的烹饪方法相当老式。


近义词：outdated, antiquated, obsolete, passé, traditional

反义词：modern, contemporary, up-to-date, newfangled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This car is old-fashioned. = 这辆车很老式。
His ideas are old-fashioned. = 他的想法很过时。

一般过去时:
That style of music was old-fashioned even then. = 那种音乐风格即使在当时也很过时。
The furniture in the house was old-fashioned. = 房子的家具很老式。


现在完成时:
Fashion has changed so much; many styles have become old-fashioned. = 时尚变化如此之大，许多风格都已过时。
His methods have become old-fashioned in today's fast-paced world. = 他的方法在当今快节奏的世界中已经过时了。


过去完成时:
By the time the new models came out, the old ones had already become old-fashioned. = 当新款上市时，旧款已经过时了。
The technology they used had already become old-fashioned. = 他们使用的技术已经过时了。


将来时:
These clothes will soon become old-fashioned. = 这些衣服很快就会过时。
That style of architecture will likely be old-fashioned in a few years. = 那样的建筑风格几年后可能会过时。


虚拟语气:
If I wore that dress, I would look old-fashioned. = 如果我穿那件裙子，我会看起来很老土。
If he adopted those methods, his business would be old-fashioned. = 如果他采用那些方法，他的生意就会落后。


被动语态:
His ideas were considered old-fashioned by many people. = 他的想法被许多人认为是过时的。
That design was deemed old-fashioned and replaced. = 那个设计被认为过时并被取代了。


疑问句:
Is this design old-fashioned? = 这个设计过时了吗？
Do you think his approach is old-fashioned? = 你认为他的方法过时了吗？


否定句:
Her style is not old-fashioned at all. = 她的风格一点也不过时。
His technology is not old-fashioned; it's cutting-edge. = 他的技术并非过时；它是尖端的。
