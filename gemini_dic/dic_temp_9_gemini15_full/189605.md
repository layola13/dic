salimetry:/səˈlɪmɪtri/| n. |盐度测定法|The measurement of the salt content of a substance, typically water.  Salimetry involves techniques to determine the salinity or salt concentration, often expressed as parts per thousand (ppt) or practical salinity units (PSU).| Example:  The researchers used salimetry to analyze the salinity of the ocean water samples.  盐度测定法被研究人员用来分析海水样本的盐度。|近义词：halimetry|反义词：无|名词
