It often involves a system of ropes and knots that allows for precise control of the horse's head and neck.|例句：The cowboy used a hockamore to restrain the wild mustang.  牛仔用勒马绳控制住那匹野马。|近义词：hackamore, bosal|反义词：loose rein(松缰绳)|名词
