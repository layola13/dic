infancy:/ˈɪnfənsi/| n. |婴儿期，初期|The state or period of being an infant; the early stage of development.  It refers to the earliest stage of life, typically from birth to about 18 months, characterized by rapid physical and cognitive growth.  It can also be used metaphorically to refer to the early stages of something else, like an institution or a project.|例句：Her infancy was marked by several illnesses.她的婴儿时期患过几次病。

例句：The company is still in its infancy. 这家公司仍处于起步阶段。|近义词：babyhood, childhood (in a broader sense), beginning, commencement |反义词：adulthood, maturity
