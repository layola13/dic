restauration: /ˌrɛstəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 修复；重建；恢复；复兴；(尤指)餐馆 | The act of restoring something to its former state or condition;  the process of repairing or renewing something old, damaged, or decayed.  It can also refer to the re-establishment of a monarchy or government, or a revival of a particular style or period.  Specifically, it can refer to a restaurant. |

例句：

The restoration of the old church took many years.  | 这座古教堂的修复工程耗时多年。

The restoration of the monarchy was met with mixed reactions. | 君主制的恢复引起了褒贬不一的反应。


近义词：repair, renovation, rehabilitation, revival

反义词：destruction, demolition, ruin
