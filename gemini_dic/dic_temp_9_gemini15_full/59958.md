edicts:/ˈiːdɪkts/| n.|法令；命令；训令|an official order issued by a person in authority|The king issued edicts to control the country. 国王发布法令来控制国家。|近义词：decree, ordinance, proclamation, mandate|反义词：recommendation, suggestion

一般现在时:
The government issues many edicts each year. = 政府每年发布许多法令。
He often makes edicts without consulting others. = 他经常在不咨询他人意见的情况下发布命令。


一般过去时:
The emperor issued an edict to ban the practice. = 皇帝发布法令禁止这种做法。
They received an edict from the governor last week. = 他们上周收到了州长的法令。


现在完成时:
The authorities have issued several edicts concerning public safety. = 当局已经发布了几项关于公共安全的法令。
Several edicts have been issued to improve the living conditions.  = 已经发布了几项法令以改善生活条件。


过去完成时:
The people had already heard the edict before the messenger arrived. = 在信使到达之前，人们已经听说了法令。
The rebellion had started before the king issued his edict. = 在国王发布法令之前，叛乱就已经开始了。


将来时:
The council will issue an edict on the matter next week. = 理事会下周将就此事发布法令。
They predict the new government will issue many edicts. = 他们预测新政府将发布许多法令。


虚拟语气:
If the king had issued a different edict, the outcome would have been different. = 如果国王发布不同的法令，结果就会不同。
I wish the government would issue an edict to protect the environment. = 我希望政府能发布法令来保护环境。


被动语态:
The edict was issued by the supreme court. = 该法令由最高法院发布。
Many edicts are issued every year by the local government. = 地方政府每年发布许多法令。


疑问句:
What edict was issued last month? = 上个月发布了什么法令？
Has the new government issued any edicts yet? = 新政府是否已经发布任何法令？


否定句:
The government did not issue an edict concerning the protest. = 政府没有发布关于抗议的法令。
The judge refused to issue the edict requested by the plaintiff. = 法官拒绝发布原告要求的法令。
