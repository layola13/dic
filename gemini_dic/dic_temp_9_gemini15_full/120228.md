mineworker:/ˈmaɪnwɜːrkər/| n. |矿工|A person who works in a mine, extracting minerals or other resources.  |例句：Many mineworkers lost their jobs after the mine closed.  许多矿工在矿井关闭后失去了工作。|例句：The mineworkers' union is negotiating for better safety standards. 矿工工会在协商争取更好的安全标准。|近义词：miner, collier (especially for coal miners)|反义词：None directly applicable.
