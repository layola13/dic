reversion's:/rɪˈvɜːʒən/ | n. | 复原；回复；逆转；回归 | The act or process of reverting to a previous state or condition;  a return to a former state or condition.  It often refers to a legal or technical return to a prior state. |例句：The reversion of the land to the original owner was legally contested.  土地所有权的回归原业主受到了法律上的争议。

The reversion to older methods proved less efficient than expected.  回到旧方法证明效率低于预期。


近义词：return, relapse, reversion, throwback, reversal | 反义词：progression, advancement, development | 词性：名词
