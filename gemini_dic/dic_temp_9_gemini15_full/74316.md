fractures:/ˈfrækʃərz/| n. |骨折；断裂；裂缝 |The breaking of a hard object or material.  Fractures can range from hairline cracks to complete breaks.  They often occur in bones due to trauma, but can also happen in other materials like rock or glass. |例句：The accident caused multiple fractures in his leg.  这次事故导致他腿部多处骨折。

The earthquake caused fractures in the earth's surface. 地震在地表造成了裂缝。|近义词：breaks, cracks, fissures |反义词：integrity, wholeness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ice fractures under pressure. 冰在压力下断裂。
This material fractures easily.  这种材料容易断裂。


一般过去时:
The vase fractured when it fell. 花瓶掉下来时碎了。
The bone fractured during the accident. 骨头在事故中骨折了。


现在完成时:
The pipe has fractured in several places. 管子好几个地方都裂开了。
The building has fractured under the weight of the snow. 建筑物在雪的重量下出现了裂缝。


过去完成时:
The bridge had fractured before the inspection. 桥梁在检查之前就已经断裂了。
The foundation had fractured, causing the building to collapse. 地基已经断裂，导致建筑物倒塌。


将来时:
The glass will fracture if you drop it. 如果你把它扔掉，玻璃会碎。
The rock will fracture under the immense pressure. 岩石会在巨大的压力下断裂。


虚拟语气:
If the bone had not fractured, he could have played in the game. 如果骨头没有骨折，他就能参加比赛了。
If the glass were to fracture, it would be dangerous. 如果玻璃碎了，那就危险了。


被动语态:
The bone was fractured in the accident. 骨头在事故中骨折了。
The vase was fractured beyond repair. 花瓶摔坏了，无法修复。


疑问句:
Has the bone fractured completely? 骨头完全断了吗？
Did the pipe fracture due to corrosion? 管子是因为腐蚀而断裂的吗？


否定句:
The glass did not fracture. 玻璃没有碎。
The structure did not fracture under the load. 结构在负载下没有断裂。
