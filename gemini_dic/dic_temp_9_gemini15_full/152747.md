peavey:/ˈpiːviː/| n.|长柄叉,杠杆|A peavey is a long-handled, hooked tool used for lifting and maneuvering logs.  It is commonly used in logging and forestry.  The hook is used to catch and grip the log, while the long handle provides leverage.|例句：The logger used a peavey to roll the heavy log onto the truck.  伐木工用长柄叉把沉重的圆木滚到卡车上。|近义词：cant hook, peavy hook|反义词:N/A
