shrewd:/ʃruːd/| adj.|精明的，狡猾的，机智的 |having or showing sharp powers of judgment; astute.  A shrewd businessman always looks for opportunities to make money. 一个精明的商人总是寻找赚钱的机会。|近义词：astute, clever, sharp, cunning |反义词：naive, foolish, gullible


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is shrewd in business dealings. = 他在商业交易方面很精明。
She is a shrewd negotiator. = 她是一位精明的谈判者。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
They have been shrewd investors. = 他们一直是精明的投资者。
She has proved herself to be shrewd in her career choices. = 她已经证明自己在职业选择方面很精明。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
