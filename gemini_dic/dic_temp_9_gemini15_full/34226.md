clankety-clank: /ˈklæŋkɪtiˈklæŋk/ | adj. | 当啷当啷作响的；发出连续的金属撞击声的 |  Onomatopoeic word describing a repetitive clanging sound, typically of metal objects hitting each other.  It evokes a sense of repeated, somewhat rhythmic, metallic noise. | 例句：The old train rattled along the tracks with a clankety-clank sound.  这列旧火车发出当啷当啷的声音在轨道上行驶。

The rusty gate swung open with a clankety-clank.  生锈的大门发出当啷当啷的声音打开了。


近义词：clanging, clattering, banging,  jangling (if smaller metallic objects)


反义词：silent, quiet, hushed


词性：形容词
