co-morbid:/ˌkoʊˈmɔːrbɪd/| adj.| 并发的；共病的 | Existing simultaneously with another medical condition.  It refers to the presence of one or more additional conditions co-occurring with a primary condition.  For example, depression and anxiety are often co-morbid. | 例句：Depression and anxiety are often co-morbid conditions.  抑郁症和焦虑症常常是并发症。

The patient presented with a co-morbid condition of diabetes and heart disease.  这位病人同时患有糖尿病和心脏病。


近义词：concurrent, concomitant, associated, simultaneous

反义词：unrelated, independent


词性：形容词
