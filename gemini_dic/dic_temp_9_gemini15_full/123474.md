monostichous:/mɒnˈɒstɪkəs/ | adj. |单排的；单列的|Having or arranged in a single row or line.  This term is primarily used in botany to describe the arrangement of leaves or flowers on a stem. |例句：The leaves of this plant are monostichous, arranged in a single vertical line.|近义词：uniseriate|反义词：pluriseriate, multiseriate
