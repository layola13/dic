clownfish:/ˈklaʊnˌfɪʃ/| n. |小丑鱼|A clownfish, also known as anemonefish, is a fish that lives in anemones. They are brightly colored and have a symbiotic relationship with the anemones, meaning they both benefit from living together. The anemone protects the clownfish from predators, and the clownfish helps clean the anemone and may bring it food.|例句：The clownfish darted in and out of the anemone's tentacles. 小丑鱼在海葵的触手中穿梭。|例句：Many species of clownfish are popular in home aquariums. 许多种小丑鱼在家庭水族箱中很受欢迎。|近义词：anemonefish|反义词：无
