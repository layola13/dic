pancetta:/pænˈtʃɛtə/| n. |意大利熏腌猪腩肉|Cured, typically smoked, pork belly.  It's often used in Italian cooking and is known for its salty, savory flavor.  |Example: The pancetta added a delicious smoky flavor to the pasta dish.  意大利熏肉为意面增添了美味的烟熏味。|近义词：bacon (less cured and smoked), guanciale (cured cheek)|反义词：无
