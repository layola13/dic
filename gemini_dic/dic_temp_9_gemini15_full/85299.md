harness:/ˈhɑːrnɪs/| n. vt.| 马具；利用；控制；束缚 | A system of straps and buckles used to control or restrain a horse or other animal; to control and make use of natural resources, especially to produce energy; to control or exploit something to one's advantage. |例句：The farmer harnessed his horse to the plough.  农民用马具把马套在犁上。|例句：We need to harness the power of the sun. 我们需要利用太阳能。|近义词：equipment, utilize, exploit, control |反义词：release, liberate, free


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They harness the wind to generate electricity. = 他们利用风力发电。
He harnesses his skills to achieve his goals. = 他利用自己的技能来实现目标。

一般过去时:
The pioneers harnessed the power of rivers. = 拓荒者们利用河流的能量。
She harnessed her creativity to design the product. = 她运用她的创造力来设计产品。

现在完成时:
Scientists have harnessed the power of the atom. = 科学家们已经利用了原子的力量。
We have harnessed renewable energy sources. = 我们已经利用了可再生能源。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
