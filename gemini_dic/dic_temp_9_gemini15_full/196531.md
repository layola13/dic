servoing:/sɜːrˈvoʊɪŋ/ | v. |伺服控制，自动控制 | The process of using a servo mechanism to control a system.  It involves using feedback to maintain a desired state or position. | Example: The robotic arm was servoing its position to precisely pick up the component.  机器人手臂正在进行伺服控制，以精确地拾取组件。|近义词：controlling, regulating, adjusting |反义词：manually operating, uncontrolled
