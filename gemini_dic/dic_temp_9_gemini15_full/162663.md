preassembly:/ˌpriːəˈsɛmbli/| n. |预组装；预先组装|The act of assembling components beforehand, typically in a factory setting, to simplify later assembly or installation.  This reduces on-site assembly time and potential errors. |例句：The preassembly of the engine blocks significantly sped up the car manufacturing process.  预先组装发动机缸体大大加快了汽车制造过程。

例句：The preassembly of the furniture saved us hours of work at home. 家具的预组装为我们节省了数小时的家庭作业时间。

近义词：prefabrication, prior assembly

反义词：final assembly, on-site assembly

词性：名词
