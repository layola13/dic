endurement: /ɪnˈdʒʊərəns/ | n. |忍耐力；持久性；容忍；忍耐|The ability to endure; perseverance; tolerance.  |例句：Her endurance during the marathon was truly remarkable.  她的马拉松耐力确实令人瞩目。

例句：The building's endurance is a testament to its solid construction. 这座建筑物的持久性证明了其坚固的结构。


近义词：patience, perseverance, fortitude, stamina, tolerance

反义词：intolerance, impatience, weakness


词源:  The word "endurement" comes from the Old French word "endurer," meaning "to endure," which itself derives from the Latin word "indurare," meaning "to harden."  The suffix "-ment" is a noun-forming suffix, indicating a state or process.
