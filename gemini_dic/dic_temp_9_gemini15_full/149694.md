pakora:/pəˈkɔːrə/| n.|印度油炸小吃|Deep-fried fritters from the Indian subcontinent, made from vegetables, lentils, or meat batter.  They are a popular street food and snack.|例句：I ordered a plate of vegetable pakora for a snack. 我点了一盘蔬菜油炸小吃当点心。

例句：The pakora were crispy on the outside and soft on the inside.  这些油炸小吃外酥里嫩。


近义词：fritter, bhaji (similar Indian fritters), tempura (Japanese equivalent)
反义词：无
词性:名词
