commiserate:/kəˈmɪzəreɪt/| vt.|表示同情；同情；怜悯|To express or feel sympathy or pity.  Commiseration involves sharing the feelings of another person who is experiencing misfortune or suffering.|例句：I commiserated with her on the loss of her job. 我对她失去工作表示同情。|例句：We commiserated over our failed exam. 我们为考试不及格而互相安慰。|近义词：sympathize, empathize, console, condole|反义词：rejoice, celebrate


USAGE->

He commiserates with his friend on his misfortune. = 他对他朋友的不幸表示同情。
She commiserates with the victims of the disaster. = 她对灾难的受害者表示同情。
