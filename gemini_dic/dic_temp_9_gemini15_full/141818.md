orbiting:/ˈɔːrbɪtɪŋ/|/ˈɔːrbɪtɪŋ/| adj. v.ing|环绕的；绕轨道运行的|Moving in an orbit around a celestial body.  The term describes the movement of one object around another due to gravitational forces.|例句：The moon is orbiting the Earth. 月球绕地球运行。
The satellite is orbiting the planet.  卫星正在绕行星运行。|近义词：revolving, circling, rotating|反义词：stationary, still


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The Earth is orbiting the sun. = 地球正在绕太阳运行。
The satellites are orbiting the planet. = 卫星正在绕行星运行。

一般过去时:
The space station orbited the Earth for many years. = 太空站绕地球运行了许多年。
The comet orbited the sun before disappearing. = 彗星绕太阳运行后消失了。

现在完成时:
The spacecraft has been orbiting Mars for several months. = 这艘宇宙飞船绕火星运行已经有几个月了。
The satellite has successfully orbited the Earth since its launch. = 自发射以来，卫星已成功绕地球运行。

过去完成时:
The probe had orbited Jupiter before transmitting the data. = 探测器在传输数据之前已经绕木星运行。
The mission had been orbiting the moon for a week before landing. = 任务在着陆前一周一直在绕月球运行。

将来时:
The new telescope will orbit Earth at a higher altitude. = 新望远镜将在更高的轨道上绕地球运行。
The astronauts will be orbiting the moon next week. = 宇航员下周将绕月球运行。

虚拟语气:
If the planet were orbiting faster, the climate would be different. = 如果这颗行星运行速度更快，气候就会不同。
I wish the satellite were orbiting closer to the target. = 我希望卫星能更靠近目标运行。

被动语态:
The planet is orbited by several moons. = 这颗行星被几颗卫星环绕。
The sun is orbited by all the planets in our solar system. = 太阳系中所有的行星都绕着太阳运行。

疑问句:
Is the spaceship orbiting the Earth? = 宇宙飞船是否正在绕地球运行？
Are those stars orbiting a central black hole? = 那些恒星是否绕着一个中心黑洞运行？

否定句:
The comet isn't orbiting the sun anymore. = 彗星不再绕太阳运行了。
The debris isn't orbiting the Earth in a stable path. = 碎片并没有在稳定的轨道上绕地球运行。
