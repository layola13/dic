rename: /ˌriːˈneɪm/ | vt. | 重命名，改名 | To give a new name to (someone or something).  |  They renamed the street after the famous poet. 他们以著名诗人的名字重新命名了这条街。|  rechristen, retitle |  retain original name | v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The programmer renames the file every day. = 程序员每天都重命名文件。
They rename the product to attract more customers. = 他们重命名产品以吸引更多顾客。


一般过去时:
She renamed her cat Mittens. = 她把她的猫改名为米腾斯。
The company renamed its flagship product last year. = 公司去年更名了其旗舰产品。


现在完成时:
He has renamed all the files on his computer. = 他已经重命名了他电脑上的所有文件。
We have renamed the project to reflect its new focus. = 我们已经重命名了项目以反映其新的重点。


过去完成时:
They had renamed the building before the ceremony. = 典礼前他们已经改建了大楼的名字。
She had renamed the document several times before she was satisfied. = 在她满意之前，她已经多次重命名了该文档。


将来时:
I will rename the folder tomorrow. = 我明天将重命名文件夹。
They will rename the street once the construction is finished. = 建设完成后，他们将重新命名这条街道。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would rename that file. = 如果我是你，我会重命名那个文件。
It would be better if we renamed the project to something more descriptive. = 如果我们把项目改成更具描述性的名字会更好。


被动语态:
The file was renamed by the administrator. = 文件被管理员重命名了。
The project is being renamed to better reflect its goals. = 项目正在被重命名以更好地反映其目标。


疑问句:

否定句:
