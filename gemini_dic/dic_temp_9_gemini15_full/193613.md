self-awarenesses:/ˌsɛlf əˈwɛːrnəsɪz/| n. |自我意识（复数）|The understanding and recognition of one's own thoughts, feelings, and behaviors.  It involves being mindful of one's internal state and how it influences actions and interactions with others.  Self-awareness is a crucial component of emotional intelligence and personal growth.|例句：Developing strong self-awarenesses is key to effective leadership.  培养强大的自我意识是有效领导的关键。

例句：Her years of therapy helped her cultivate greater self-awarenesses about her emotional triggers.  她多年的治疗帮助她培养了对情绪触发因素更强的自我意识。


近义词：self-knowledge, introspection, mindfulness, self-perception|反义词：self-ignorance, unawareness, obliviousness|名词
