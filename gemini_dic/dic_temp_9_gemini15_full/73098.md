fopling:/ˈfɒplɪŋ/| n.|花花公子，紈绔子弟|A fopling is a young man who is excessively concerned with his appearance and manners, often in an affected or effeminate way.  They are often portrayed as superficial and lacking in substance.|例句：He was a fopling, more interested in his powdered wig than in serious conversation. 他是个花花公子，对他的假发比对严肃的谈话更感兴趣。  The fopling preened himself in the mirror before going to the ball.  这位花花公子在去舞会前对着镜子打扮自己。|近义词：dandy, coxcomb, beau, fop|反义词：rugged individual, plain man
