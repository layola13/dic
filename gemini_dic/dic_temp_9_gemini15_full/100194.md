inter/ˈɪntər/| v.|埋葬；中断；打扰|To place (a dead body) in a grave or tomb; to bury.  Also, to interrupt or discontinue something.|例句：They interred the body in consecrated ground. 他们在神圣的土地上埋葬了遗体。|例句：The project was interred due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目被搁置了。|近义词：bury, entomb, suspend, discontinue|反义词：exhume, resurrect, continue|动词
