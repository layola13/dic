decriminalising: /ˌdiːˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使非罪化，使合法化 | The process of removing the criminal status from an act or behavior.  It involves changing laws to make something that was previously illegal no longer a crime. | 例句：Many countries are decriminalising the possession of small amounts of marijuana. = 许多国家正在使少量持有大麻行为非罪化。

Many advocates are pushing for the decriminalisation of drug use. = 许多倡导者正在推动毒品使用的非罪化。


近义词：legalising,  deregulating


反义词：criminalising


词性:动词
