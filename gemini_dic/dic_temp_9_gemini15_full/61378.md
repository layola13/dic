emblaze:/ɪmˈbleɪz/| vt.|点燃；装饰；使辉煌|to set something on fire; to decorate or adorn something brilliantly; to make something shine brightly.|例句：The bonfire blazed merrily, emblazing the night sky.  篝火熊熊燃烧，照亮了夜空。

The artist emblazed the shield with the family crest. 艺术家在盾牌上绘上了家族纹章，使其辉煌夺目。|近义词：ignite, illuminate, adorn, decorate|反义词：extinguish, darken, dull
