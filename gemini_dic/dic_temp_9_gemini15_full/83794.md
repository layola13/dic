hairlines:/ˈheəlaɪnz/| n.|发际线；细线；裂纹|A hairline is the line where the hair on your head begins.  It can also refer to a very thin line or a crack in a surface.|例句：His hairline is receding. 他的发际线后移了。
The crack in the vase was just a hairline fracture. 花瓶上的裂缝只是一条细小的裂纹。|近义词：part (of hair), fissure, crack|反义词：None specifically.
