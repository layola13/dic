disjunctively:/dɪsˈdʒʌŋktɪvli/| adv.|析取地，非此即彼地 |  Adverb indicating a connection between propositions or statements where one or the other is true, but not necessarily both. It signifies a choice between alternatives.  | Example:  The two conditions are stated disjunctively; either A or B must be true.  两个条件是析取陈述的；要么A是真的，要么B是真的。 | 近义词: alternatively, separately | 反义词: conjunctively, concurrently
