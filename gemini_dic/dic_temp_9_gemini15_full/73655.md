forgave:/fərˈɡeɪv/| vt.|宽恕；原谅；赦免|To stop feeling angry or resentful towards (someone) for an offense, flaw, or mistake.|例句：I forgave him for his mistake. 我原谅了他的错误。|例句：She forgave her sister for breaking her favorite vase. 她原谅了姐姐打破她最喜欢的花瓶。|近义词：pardon, excuse, absolve|反义词：condemn, punish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I forgive you. = 我原谅你。
He forgives her often. = 他经常原谅她。

一般过去时:
She forgave him for lying. = 她原谅了他说谎。
They forgave their parents for their mistakes. = 他们原谅了父母的错误。

现在完成时:
I have forgiven him many times. = 我已经原谅了他很多次。
She has forgiven her friend for the hurtful words. = 她已经原谅了朋友那些伤人的话。

过去完成时:
He had forgiven her before she apologized. = 在她道歉之前，他已经原谅了她。
They had forgiven each other before the trip. = 他们在旅行之前已经互相原谅了。

将来时:
I will forgive you if you apologize sincerely. = 如果你真诚道歉，我会原谅你。
She will forgive him, eventually. = 她最终会原谅他。


虚拟语气:
I would forgive him if he asked for forgiveness. = 如果他请求原谅，我会原谅他。
If I were you, I would forgive her. = 如果我是你，我会原谅她。

被动语态:
His mistakes were forgiven by his family. = 他的错误被家人原谅了。
The offenders were forgiven after expressing remorse. = 犯错者在表达悔恨后被原谅了。

疑问句:
Will you forgive me? = 你会原谅我吗？
Have they forgiven each other? = 他们互相原谅了吗？

否定句:
I cannot forgive you for what you did. = 我无法原谅你所做的事情。
She did not forgive him easily. = 她不容易原谅他。


PHRASE->
forgive and forget =  一笑泯恩仇

I beg your forgiveness = 我请求你的原谅
ask for forgiveness = 请求原谅
forgive my intrusion = 请原谅我的冒昧
