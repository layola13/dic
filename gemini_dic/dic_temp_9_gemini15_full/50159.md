deny oneself:/dɪˈnaɪ wʌnˈsɛlf/ | v. | 克己，克制，忍耐 | To deny oneself means to refuse oneself something enjoyable or desirable, often for a higher purpose or goal.  It implies self-discipline and sacrifice. | He denied himself the pleasure of dessert to lose weight. 他克制自己不吃甜点以减肥。|She denied herself sleep to finish the project. 她为了完成项目而牺牲睡眠。 | indulge oneself, give in to temptation | give oneself over to,  indulge in | v.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He denies himself luxuries. 他克己不享受奢侈品。
She denies herself sweets. 她克制自己不吃糖果。


一般过去时:
He denied himself a vacation last year. 他去年放弃了休假。
She denied herself chocolate for a month. 她一个月没吃巧克力。


现在完成时:
He has denied himself many pleasures to achieve his goals. 为了达到目标，他已克制了许多享乐。
She has denied herself sleep for weeks to finish her thesis. 她为了完成论文已经几周没睡好觉了。


过去完成时:
He had denied himself entertainment for years before he finally took a holiday. 在他最终休假之前，他已经多年没有娱乐了。
She had denied herself food for days before collapsing from exhaustion.  在她因精疲力竭而倒下之前，她已经几天没吃东西了。


将来时:
He will deny himself social events to focus on his studies. 他将放弃社交活动专注于学习。
She will deny herself dessert for the next week. 她下周将不吃甜点。


虚拟语气:
If he denied himself more, he would achieve his goal quicker. 如果他更克己一些，他就能更快地实现目标。
If she had denied herself distractions, she would have finished the project sooner. 如果她克制住分心的事情，她就能更快地完成项目。


被动语态:  (The verb "deny oneself" is rarely used passively.)  A more natural phrasing would be to use a different construction.  For example:

Pleasures were denied to him.  享受被剥夺了他。
Sleep was denied to her.  睡眠被剥夺了她。


疑问句:
Does he deny himself anything? 他克制自己不做任何事吗？
Does she deny herself luxuries? 她克制自己不去享受奢侈品吗？


否定句:
He doesn't deny himself anything. 他什么都不克制自己。
She doesn't deny herself treats occasionally. 她偶尔不会克制自己享用美食。
