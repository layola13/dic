morceau:/mɔːrˈsoʊ/|/mɔːrˈsəʊ/| n. |乐曲；片段；一小部分 |A short musical piece, especially one for piano.  It can also refer to a small piece of something, like a passage of writing or a small portion of food. |例句：He played a beautiful morceau on the piano. 他在钢琴上演奏了一首优美的乐曲。

例句：The chef prepared a delicious morceau of chocolate cake. 厨师准备了一块美味的巧克力蛋糕。

近义词：piece, passage, section, fragment

反义词：whole, entirety
