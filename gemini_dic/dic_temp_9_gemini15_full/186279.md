rhizostomata:/ˌraɪzəˈstoʊmətə/| n. |根口纲 |A group of jellyfish characterized by their fused oral arms forming a complex, root-like structure for feeding.  They lack individual tentacles and have a distinctive umbrella shape. | Example: The *Rhizostomata* are a fascinating group of jellyfish with unique feeding mechanisms. 根口纲是一种具有独特摄食机制的令人着迷的水母类群。|近义词：Medusozoa (a broader term)|反义词：None specifically;  perhaps  Scyphozoa (another class of jellyfish with different characteristics)
