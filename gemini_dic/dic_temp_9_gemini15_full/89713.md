hope-chests: /ˈhoʊpˌtʃɛsts/ | n. |希望箱；嫁妆箱 | A hope chest is a large, often decorated chest used traditionally by young women to store items they would need for their future household after marriage.  These items could include linens, clothes, and other household goods.  The act of accumulating items in the chest symbolized hope for a successful marriage and future. |She carefully placed the embroidered tablecloth in her hope chest.|近义词：bridal chest, dowry chest |反义词：无
