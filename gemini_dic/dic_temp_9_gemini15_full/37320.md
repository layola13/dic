commemoration:/ˌkəˌmɛməˈreɪʃən/| n. |纪念，庆祝 |The act of remembering and showing respect for someone or something, especially someone who is dead or a past event.  It involves a ceremony or activity to honor the memory of the person or event. |例句：The commemoration of the war dead was a solemn occasion.  战争死难者的纪念活动是一个庄严的场合。

The annual commemoration of the founding of the city attracted thousands of visitors.  每年纪念建城都会吸引成千上万的游客。

|近义词：celebration, remembrance, memorialization |反义词：forgetting, neglect |词性:名词
