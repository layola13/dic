convalesced:/kɒnˈvəleɪst/ | v. | 康复；恢复健康 | To recover health and strength after illness or injury. |例句：After a long illness, she finally convalesced.  一场大病之后，她最终康复了。|例句：He convalesced at a seaside resort. 他在一个海滨度假胜地休养。|近义词：recover, recuperate, rehabilitate |反义词：worsen, deteriorate


USAGE->

He convalesces slowly after major surgery. 他大手术后恢复得很慢。
She convalesces at home with the help of her family. 她在家人的帮助下在家休养。
