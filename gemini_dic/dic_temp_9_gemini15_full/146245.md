overextended:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈstɛndɪd/| adj. |过度扩展的；债台高筑的；筋疲力尽的 |  Beyond one's capacity or resources; stretched too far.  Describes a situation where someone or something has taken on more than it can handle, leading to strain or difficulty. |

例句：
The company is overextended financially and risks bankruptcy.  公司财务过度扩张，面临破产风险。

The overextended team needed a break.  过度劳累的团队需要休息。


近义词：overburdened, strained, stretched, depleted, exhausted

反义词：underutilized, relaxed, rested, well-resourced
