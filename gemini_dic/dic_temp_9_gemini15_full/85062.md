hard-drinking: /ˌhɑːrdˈdrɪŋkɪŋ/ | adj. | 嗜酒的，酒量大的 |  Describes someone who drinks a lot of alcohol regularly.  It often implies a habit that may be excessive or problematic. | 例句：He has a reputation for being a hard-drinking man. 他以嗜酒闻名。  He's a hard-drinking, hard-living sort of character. 他是个嗜酒、放荡不羁的人。| 近义词：heavy-drinking, boozy, alcoholic (in extreme cases) | 反义词：teetotaling, abstemious
