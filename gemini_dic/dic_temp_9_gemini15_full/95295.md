incarnate:/ɪnˈkɑːrneɪt/| adj., v.|化身；具体化；赋予肉体|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "incarnate" means existing in bodily form; embodied.  As a verb, it means to give a body to (a spirit or god); to embody or represent in human form.  It often implies a powerful or extreme manifestation of a quality or concept.

例句:
He was the incarnate devil, causing mischief wherever he went. 他是魔鬼的化身，走到哪里都捣乱。

The goddess appeared incarnate in a beautiful woman. 女神以一位美丽的女子形象出现。


近义词:embodied, embodied, personified, exemplified

反义词:spiritual, abstract, disembodied


词性:Adjective, Verb
