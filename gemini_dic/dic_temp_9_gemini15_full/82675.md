guesstimation:/ˌɡɛs.tɪˈmeɪ.ʃən/| n. |粗略估计，大概估算 |A guesstimation is a rough calculation or estimate, often made quickly and without precise data. It's a blend of "guess" and "estimation".|例句：I made a guesstimation of the project cost at around $10,000. 我粗略估计项目成本约为 10,000 美元。

例句：The guesstimation for the number of attendees was about 500.  对与会人数的粗略估计约为 500 人。


近义词：approximation, estimation, rough estimate, ballpark figure |反义词：precise calculation, exact measurement |词性：名词
