It is an extremely radioactive element; its most stable known isotope, <sup>281</sup>Ds, has a half-life of approximately 10 seconds.  It was first synthesized in 1994 at the Gesellschaft für Schwerionenforschung (GSI) Helmholtz Centre for Heavy Ion Research in Darmstadt, Germany, from which it derives its name. | The synthesis of darmstadtium was a major achievement in nuclear physics, pushing the boundaries of our understanding of the periodic table and the stability of superheavy elements. | 近义词:None (as it is a unique element)| 反义词:None (as it is a unique element)| noun
