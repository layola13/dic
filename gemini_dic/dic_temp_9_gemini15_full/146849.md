overindividualization: /ˌoʊvərˌɪndɪvɪdʒuəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |过度个人化；个人主义泛滥 | The excessive focus on individual needs and preferences to the detriment of collective goals or social cohesion.  It often leads to a lack of empathy and understanding towards others.  | Example: The overindividualization of modern society has led to increased social isolation and a decline in community spirit.  过度个人化的现代社会导致了社会隔离的加剧和社区精神的衰退。|近义词：individualism, egoism, self-centeredness |反义词：collectivism, community spirit, social cohesion | 名词
