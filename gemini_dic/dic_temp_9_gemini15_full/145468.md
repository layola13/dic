overclassifications:/ˌoʊvərˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |过度分类；分类过多|The creation of too many categories or classes, often leading to confusion and inefficiency.  This can occur in various fields, such as data analysis, library science, and taxonomy.  For example, excessively detailed categorization of documents in a library can make it difficult for users to find what they need. |例句：The overclassifications in the database made it difficult to retrieve relevant information. 数据库中过多的分类使得检索相关信息变得困难。


The overclassifications of plant species hampered the research progress. 植物物种的过度分类阻碍了研究进展。


近义词：overcategorization, excessive classification, over-categorization|反义词：underclassification, insufficient classification|名词
