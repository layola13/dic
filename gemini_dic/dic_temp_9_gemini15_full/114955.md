manatee:/ˈmænətiː/| n. |海牛|A large, slow-moving, plant-eating aquatic mammal with a rounded body, flippers, and a paddle-shaped tail.  Manatees are found in warm coastal waters and rivers of the Americas and West Africa. They are also known as sea cows.|例句：The manatee gently grazed on the seagrass. 海牛轻轻地吃着海草。

例句：We saw a family of manatees swimming near the shore. 我们看到一群海牛在岸边游泳。


近义词：sea cow

词源:
