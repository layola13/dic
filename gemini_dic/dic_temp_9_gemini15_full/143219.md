/aʊtˈkʌlz/| n.|淘汰，剔除|The outculls were sold at a lower price.  淘汰品以较低的价格出售。|近义词：cull, reject, refuse |反义词：select, choose |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer outculls the weakest animals each year. = 农场主每年淘汰最弱的动物。
They regularly outcull underperforming employees. = 他们定期淘汰表现不佳的员工。


一般过去时:

The breeder outculled the unhealthy puppies from the litter. = 饲养员从窝里淘汰了不健康的幼犬。
