falconet:/ˈfɑːlkənɛt/| n. |小型猎鹰；轻型火炮|A small falcon; a small cannon, typically of the 16th and 17th centuries.|The falconet was a popular weapon for its size and portability.  小型猎鹰是一种流行的武器，因为它体积小且便于携带。| 近义词：small cannon, light gun | 反义词：heavy artillery
