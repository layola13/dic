isn't:/ɪznt/| contraction|不是|It is a contraction of "is not".  It is used as a shortened form of the negative form of the verb "to be" in the third-person singular present tense. |He isn't coming to the party. 他不来参加派对了。|is, is not|is, are|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
That isn't true. = 那不是真的。
She isn't happy. = 她不开心。


一般过去时:
It wasn't easy. = 这不容易。
They weren't there. = 他们不在那里。


现在完成时:
He isn't finished yet. = 他还没完成。
It hasn't rained today. = 今天没下雨。


过去完成时:
She hadn't told him. = 她还没告诉他。
We hadn't seen it before. = 我们以前没见过它。


将来时:
It isn't going to snow. = 将不会下雪。
They aren't going to win. = 他们不会赢。


虚拟语气:
If it weren't for you, I'd be lost. = 如果没有你，我就迷路了。
I wish it weren't so cold. = 我希望不要这么冷。


被动语态:
The problem isn't being addressed. = 这个问题没有得到解决。
The book isn't being read. = 这本书没有被阅读。


疑问句:
Isn't it beautiful? = 它不漂亮吗？
Isn't she talented? = 她很有天赋，不是吗？


否定句:
I don't think that's true, it isn't. = 我认为那不是真的，它不是。
That isn't what I meant. = 那不是我的意思。


PHRASE->
Isn't it? = 是不是？
Isn't that...? = 那不是……吗？
That isn't fair. = 那不公平。
Isn't that amazing? = 那不是很神奇吗？
It isn't easy. = 这不容易。
