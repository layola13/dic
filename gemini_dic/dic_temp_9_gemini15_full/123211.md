monocistronic: /ˌmɒnəʊˈsɪstrɒnɪk/| adj. |单顺反子的|  Describing a messenger RNA (mRNA) molecule that codes for only one protein.  Unlike polycistronic mRNA, which codes for multiple proteins, a monocistronic mRNA contains a single open reading frame (ORF) that is translated into a single polypeptide chain. This is characteristic of eukaryotic cells, though some prokaryotic mRNAs are also monocistronic. |The eukaryotic mRNA is monocistronic, meaning that each mRNA molecule translates only one protein. = 真核生物的mRNA是单顺反子的，这意味着每个mRNA分子只翻译一种蛋白质。|近义词: single-gene |反义词: polycistronic
