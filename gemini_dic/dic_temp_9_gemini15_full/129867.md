non-inflationary: /ˌnɑːnɪnˈfleɪʃənəri/ | adj. | 非通货膨胀的；抑制通货膨胀的 | Not causing or characterized by inflation.  Describes economic policies or situations where the general price level remains stable or increases only slightly. |

例句：

The government implemented non-inflationary fiscal policies to maintain price stability. = 政府实施了非通货膨胀的财政政策以维持价格稳定。

The central bank adopted a non-inflationary monetary policy to control inflation. = 中央银行采取了非通货膨胀的货币政策来控制通货膨胀。


近义词：anti-inflationary, deflationary (in some contexts)

反义词：inflationary
