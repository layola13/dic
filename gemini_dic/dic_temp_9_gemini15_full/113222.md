lushly:/ˈlʌʃli/| adv.|茂盛地；繁茂地；丰富地 |  In a lush or luxurious manner; abundantly.  The adverbial form of "lush." It describes something done in a way that is rich, abundant, or overflowing. |例句：The garden was lushly planted with colorful flowers. 花园里种满了色彩鲜艳的花朵，非常茂盛。  The novel is lushly described, overflowing with vivid imagery. 这部小说描写得非常生动，充满了鲜明的意象。|近义词：abundantly, richly, luxuriantly, profusely|反义词：sparsely, meagerly, scantily
