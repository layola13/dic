defect:/ˈdiːfekt/| n. vt.|缺点；缺陷；瑕疵；不足；逃跑；叛变| a shortcoming, flaw, or imperfection;  to desert or abandon one's post or duty|The car has a defect in the engine. 这辆车发动机有缺陷。  The soldier defected to the enemy.  这个士兵叛逃到敌人那边。|近义词：flaw, imperfection, blemish, fault |反义词：perfection, excellence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine has a defect. = 这台机器有个缺陷。
This product shows several defects. = 这个产品显示出几个缺陷。


一般过去时:
The software had a serious defect. = 该软件存在严重缺陷。
A major defect was discovered in the bridge's design. = 在桥梁的设计中发现了一个重大缺陷。


现在完成时:
They have discovered a defect in the system. = 他们已经发现了系统中的一个缺陷。
We have corrected all known defects in the software. = 我们已经纠正了软件中所有已知的缺陷。


过去完成时:
The engineer had already identified the defect before the test. = 工程师在测试前就已经发现了缺陷。
They had not noticed the defect until after the product launch. = 直到产品发布后，他们才注意到缺陷。


将来时:
The inspection will reveal any defects in the material. = 检查将揭示材料中的任何缺陷。
We will fix the defect as soon as possible. = 我们将尽快修复缺陷。


虚拟语气:
If the product had no defects, it would sell better. = 如果产品没有缺陷，它会卖得更好。
I wish there were no defects in the software. = 我希望软件没有缺陷。


被动语态:
The defect was discovered during testing. = 缺陷是在测试过程中发现的。
The defects are being repaired now. = 缺陷正在被修复。


疑问句:
Are there any defects in this batch of products? = 这批产品有什么缺陷吗？
What kind of defects are you referring to? = 你指的是什么类型的缺陷？


否定句:
The product has no defects. = 该产品没有缺陷。
There were no significant defects found in the study. = 在研究中没有发现重大的缺陷。



PHRASE->
major defect: 主要缺陷
minor defect: 次要缺陷
manufacturing defect: 制造缺陷
design defect: 设计缺陷
cosmetic defect: 表面缺陷
