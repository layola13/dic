flyfish:/ˈflaɪfɪʃ/| n.|飞鱼|A type of fish that can "fly" short distances by gliding above the water using its large pectoral fins.|例句：The flyfish soared above the waves before plunging back into the ocean. 飞鱼在海浪上方翱翔，然后又潜入大海。|近义词：flying fish|反义词:None.
