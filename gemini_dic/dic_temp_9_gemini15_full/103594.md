joined:/dʒɔɪnd/| v.| 加入，连接，结合 | To become a member of a group or organization; to connect or unite. |例句：She joined the tennis club last year. 她去年加入了网球俱乐部。

例句：The two pieces of wood were joined together with glue. 两块木头用胶水粘在一起。

近义词：attached, united, combined, connected | 反义词：separated, disconnected, parted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I join the gym every Monday. = 我每个星期一都去健身房。
They join the choir every Sunday. = 他们每个星期天都参加唱诗班。

一般过去时:
He joined the army last year. = 他去年参军了。
She joined the company in 2020. = 她于2020年加入了公司。

现在完成时:
I have joined several online communities. = 我已经加入了几个在线社区。
She has joined the board of directors. = 她已经加入了董事会。

过去完成时:
Before the meeting started, they had already joined the conference call. = 会议开始前，他们已经加入了电话会议。
He had joined the party before I arrived. = 我到达之前他已经加入了聚会。

将来时:
I will join the marathon next month. = 我下个月将参加马拉松比赛。
She will join us for dinner tonight. = 她今晚将和我们一起吃饭。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would join the club. = 如果我是你，我会加入这个俱乐部。
I wish I had joined the trip. = 我真希望我参加了那次旅行。

被动语态:
The two teams were joined together for the project. = 两个团队为了这个项目而联合起来。
The pieces were joined by a strong adhesive. = 这些碎片被强力胶粘合在一起。

疑问句:
Will you join us for lunch? = 你要和我们一起吃午饭吗？
Did he join the protest? = 他参加了抗议活动吗？

否定句:
I didn't join the party. = 我没有参加聚会。
He didn't join the discussion. = 他没有参加讨论。

词源:
The word "joined" comes from the Old English word "gegynged," which means "joined," "united," or "connected."  It is the past tense and past participle of the verb "join."  The root is related to words signifying union or connection.
