puncture:/ˈpʌŋktʃər/|/ˈpʌŋktʃə(r)/| n. vt.|刺孔；穿孔；（轮胎）扎破；削弱；（计划等的）破坏|To make a small hole in something with a sharp point; to weaken or damage something.|例句：The nail punctured the tire.  钉子扎破了轮胎。

例句：His confidence was punctured by the criticism. 他的信心被批评削弱了。


近义词：pierce, perforate, deflate|反义词：repair, mend, inflate|词性:n. vt.
