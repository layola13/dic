induviae: /ɪnˈduːviːaɪ/ | n. | 脱落物；蜕皮；残余物 |  The shed outer coverings of insects, especially of the pupae or chrysalises.  Specifically refers to the remains or coverings left behind after molting or shedding.  | Example: The entomologist carefully collected the induviae to study the insect's development.  昆虫学家仔细收集蜕皮来研究昆虫的发育。 | 近义词：exuviae, cast-offs, remains, shed skin | 反义词：N/A
