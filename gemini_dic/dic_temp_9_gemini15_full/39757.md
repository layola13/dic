consoler:/kənˈsoʊlər/| n. |安慰者，慰藉者|A consoler is a person who offers comfort and support to someone who is grieving or distressed.  |例句：She was a true consoler to her grieving friend. 她是一位真正安慰她悲伤朋友的人。|近义词：comforter, supporter|反义词：tormentor, abuser

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The consoler offers comfort. = 安慰者提供安慰。
He consoles his friend regularly. = 他定期安慰他的朋友。

一般过去时:
The consoler offered kind words. = 安慰者说了些好话。
She consoled her brother after his loss. = 她在她哥哥失去后安慰了他。

现在完成时:
The consoler has offered much support. = 安慰者提供了很多支持。
She has consoled many people through difficult times. = 她已经在艰难时期安慰了许多人。

过去完成时:
The consoler had already provided comfort before I arrived. = 在我到达之前，安慰者已经提供了安慰。
He had consoled her many times before this incident. = 在这次事件之前，他已经多次安慰过她。

将来时:
The consoler will offer solace. = 安慰者将提供慰藉。
She will console him when he's ready. = 当他准备好时，她会安慰他。

虚拟语气:
If I were a better consoler, I would offer more support. = 如果我是一个更好的安慰者，我会提供更多支持。
I wish I could have been a better consoler for her. = 我希望我能成为她更好的安慰者。

被动语态:
He was consoled by his family. = 他受到了家人的安慰。
The grieving widow was consoled by many friends. = 悲伤的寡妇受到了许多朋友的安慰。

疑问句:
Who consoled her after the accident? = 事故后是谁安慰了她？
Will someone console him? = 会有人安慰他吗？

否定句:
She didn't console him effectively. = 她没有有效地安慰他。
He couldn't console himself over the loss. = 他无法因失去而安慰自己。
