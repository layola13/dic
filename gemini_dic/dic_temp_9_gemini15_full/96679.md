indwelling:/ɪnˈdwɛlɪŋ/| adj.|居住的；内在的；萦绕心头的|Existing or residing within; inherent; constantly occupying one's thoughts.|例句：The indwelling spirit guided her actions. 内在的精神指引着她的行动。
例句：He felt an indwelling sense of unease. 他感到一种挥之不去的不安。|近义词：inherent, intrinsic, resident|反义词：external, extrinsic, transient|形容词
