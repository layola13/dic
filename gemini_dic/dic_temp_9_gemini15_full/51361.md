detergent:/dɪˈtɜːrdʒənt/ | n. |清洁剂，洗涤剂 |A substance used for cleaning, especially a washing powder or liquid.  |例句：This detergent is effective at removing grease stains.  这款清洁剂能有效去除油渍。
例句：I prefer liquid detergent to powder detergent. 我更喜欢液体洗涤剂而不是粉状洗涤剂。|近义词：cleanser, soap, wash |反义词：dirt, grime

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The detergent cleans the clothes effectively. = 这款洗涤剂能有效清洁衣物。
She uses a mild detergent for her delicate fabrics. = 她用温和的洗涤剂清洗她的精致面料。

一般过去时:
I used a new detergent last week. = 我上周用了一种新的洗涤剂。
He spilled detergent all over the floor. = 他把洗涤剂洒了一地。

现在完成时:
We have used this detergent for years. = 我们已经使用这种洗涤剂多年了。
She has just bought a new bottle of detergent. = 她刚买了一瓶新的洗涤剂。

过去完成时:
I had already used all the detergent before he arrived. = 他到达之前我已经用完了所有的洗涤剂。
They had run out of detergent, so they couldn't wash the clothes. = 他们用完了洗涤剂，所以不能洗衣服。

将来时:
I will buy some more detergent tomorrow. = 我明天会再买一些洗涤剂。
They will need a stronger detergent for those stubborn stains. = 对于那些顽固的污渍，他们需要更强的洗涤剂。

虚拟语气:
If I had more detergent, I would wash all the clothes. = 如果我有更多洗涤剂，我会把所有的衣服都洗了。
I wish I had bought a bigger bottle of detergent. = 我真希望我买了一大瓶洗涤剂。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

词源:
The word "detergent" comes from the Latin word "detergere," meaning "to wipe off" or "to clean."  The suffix "-gent" indicates an agent or substance that performs an action.
