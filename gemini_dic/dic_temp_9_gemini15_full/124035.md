morph:/mɔːrf/| /mɔːrf/ | v. n. |变形；变化；使变形；形态 |To change smoothly from one form or state into another, or to cause something to do this.  | The caterpillar morphed into a butterfly. 毛毛虫变成了蝴蝶。|The software morphs seamlessly between different modes. 软件在不同的模式之间无缝转换。|  transform, change, alter | remain the same,  stay  | 动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist morphs the clay into a beautiful sculpture. = 艺术家将粘土塑造成美丽的雕塑。
The chameleon morphs its color to match its surroundings. = 变色龙改变颜色以匹配周围环境。

一般过去时:
The tadpoles morphed into frogs over several weeks. = 蝌蚪在几周内变成了青蛙。
The company morphed its business model to adapt to the market. = 公司改变了其商业模式以适应市场。

现在完成时:
The landscape has morphed significantly since the last time I visited. = 自我上次访问以来，风景已经发生了很大的变化。
The technology has morphed beyond recognition in just a few years. = 这项技术在短短几年内就面目全非了。

过去完成时:
Before the metamorphosis, the insect had morphed into a pupa. = 在变态之前，昆虫已经变成了蛹。
By the time the project was completed, it had morphed into something completely different. = 到项目完成时，它已经变成了完全不同的东西。

将来时:
The city will morph into a modern metropolis in the next decade. = 在接下来的十年里，这座城市将变成一个现代化的大都市。
The artist plans to morph the image into a more abstract form. = 艺术家计划将图像转换成更抽象的形式。

虚拟语气:
If the company had morphed its strategy earlier, it would have been more successful. = 如果公司早点改变战略，就会更成功。
I wish the design had morphed into something more user-friendly. = 我希望设计能变成更用户友好的东西。

被动语态:

否定句:
