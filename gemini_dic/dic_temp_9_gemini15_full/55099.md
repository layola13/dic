disreverence:/dɪsrɪˈvʌrəns/| n.| 不敬，无礼 | Lack of reverence; disrespect.  It often implies a deliberate or contemptuous disregard for something sacred or respected. |例句：His disreverence towards the flag shocked many.  他的不敬国旗的行为震惊了很多人。

例句：The comedian's act, while humorous to some, was seen by others as disreverence towards religious traditions.  这位喜剧演员的表演，虽然对一些人来说很有趣，但其他人却认为是对宗教传统的亵渎。


近义词：disrespect, irreverence, impiety, contempt, scorn |反义词：reverence, respect, piety, veneration
