practitioners:/prækˈtɪʃənərz/ | n. |从业者，实践者 |People who regularly practice a profession, skill, or activity.  Those who engage in a particular profession. |例句：Experienced practitioners in the field of law often have a deep understanding of legal procedures.  经验丰富的法律从业者通常对法律程序有深入的了解。

例句：Many practitioners of yoga emphasize the importance of mindfulness. 许多瑜伽练习者都强调正念的重要性。


近义词：professionals, experts, specialists,executants


反义词：amateurs, novices, beginners, laymen
