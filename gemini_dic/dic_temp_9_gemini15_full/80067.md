goals:/ɡoʊlz/| n.|目标，目的，球门|Goals are the things you want to achieve.  目标是你想要达成的目标。|The team scored three goals in the match. 球队在那场比赛中进了三个球。|objectives, aims, targets|failures, setbacks|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My goals for this year are to learn a new language and travel to Europe. = 我今年的目标是学习一门外语和去欧洲旅行。
What are your long-term goals? = 你的长期目标是什么？


一般过去时:
Her goals last year were to graduate and find a job. = 她去年的目标是毕业和找工作。
What were your goals in college? = 你大学时的目标是什么？


现在完成时:
I have achieved most of my goals this year. = 我今年已经实现了我大部分的目标。
She has not yet reached all her goals. = 她还没有实现所有目标。


过去完成时:
By the end of the project, we had already met all our goals. = 到项目结束时，我们已经实现了所有目标。
They had failed to achieve their primary goal. = 他们未能实现他们的主要目标。


将来时:
I will set new goals for myself next year. = 我明年会为自己设定新的目标。
What goals will you pursue after graduation? = 毕业后你将追求什么目标？


虚拟语气:
If I had set realistic goals, I would have achieved more. = 如果我设定了现实的目标，我会取得更大的成就。
I wish I had set clearer goals at the beginning. = 我希望一开始就设定更明确的目标。


被动语态:
The goals of the project were clearly defined. = 项目的目标被明确定义。
The goals were not met due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，目标未能实现。


疑问句:
What are your goals for the future? = 你未来的目标是什么？
What are the main goals of this meeting? = 此次会议的主要目标是什么？


否定句:
He did not achieve his goals. = 他没有实现他的目标。
We failed to meet our sales goals. = 我们未能达到我们的销售目标。


PHRASE->
set goals = 制定目标
achieve goals = 实现目标
reach goals = 达成目标
long-term goals = 长期目标
short-term goals = 短期目标
