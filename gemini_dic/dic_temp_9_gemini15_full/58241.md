drum-out:/drʌmˈaʊt/| vt.|驱逐，逐出；喧闹地演奏|To force someone to leave a place or group, often in a forceful or noisy way.  It can also refer to playing a drum loudly and rhythmically.|例句：The band drummed out a lively tune. 乐队喧闹地演奏了一支欢快的曲子。The soldiers drummed out the traitor. 士兵们把叛徒赶了出去。|近义词：expel, eject, oust|反义词：welcome, admit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They drum out a new rhythm every week. = 他们每周都会演奏一个新的节奏。
The school drums out disruptive students. = 学校会把扰乱秩序的学生开除。


一般过去时:
The army drummed out the deserter. = 军队把逃兵赶了出去。
The band drummed out a celebratory march. = 乐队喧闹地演奏了一支庆祝进行曲。
