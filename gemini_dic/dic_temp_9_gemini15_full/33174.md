chromism:/ˈkroʊmɪzəm/| n. |变色性，变色现象|The ability of a substance to change color under the influence of external factors such as light, heat, or pressure.  It often refers to the reversible color change. Examples include thermochromism (heat-induced color change), photochromism (light-induced color change), and electrochromism (electrically induced color change).|The chameleon's chromism allows it to blend seamlessly with its surroundings. 变色龙的变色能力使它能够与周围环境完美融合。  The chromism of the gemstone is a fascinating phenomenon. 这颗宝石的变色现象令人着迷。|thermochromism, photochromism, electrochromism|achromatism (lack of color)|名词
