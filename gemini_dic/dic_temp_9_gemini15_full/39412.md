conn:/kɒn/| v. |连接；联合；联系|To join or connect; to link together.|The two towns are connected by a new highway. 这两个城镇由一条新的高速公路连接。|近义词：join, link, unite|反义词：disconnect, separate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He connects the computer to the internet. 他把电脑连接到互联网。
They connect the two ideas in their essay. 他们在论文中将这两个想法联系起来。


一般过去时:
She connected the dots on the page. 她连接了页面上的点。
The engineers connected the new pipeline to the existing system. 工程师们将新的管道连接到现有系统。


现在完成时:
Have you connected the printer to your laptop yet? 你已经把打印机连接到你的笔记本电脑了吗？
They have connected all the systems. 他们已经连接了所有系统。


过去完成时:
The police had connected the suspect to the crime scene.  警方已将嫌疑人与犯罪现场联系起来。
Before the presentation, she had connected all the devices. 在演示之前，她已经连接了所有设备。


将来时:
They will connect the new building to the power grid. 他们将把新建筑连接到电网。
We will connect the two databases next week. 我们下周将连接这两个数据库。


虚拟语气:
If they connected the wires correctly, the system would work. 如果他们正确地连接了电线，系统就能工作。
I wish I had connected my phone to the charger before I left. 我希望在我离开之前我已经把手机连接到充电器上。


被动语态:
The two cities are connected by a high-speed rail line. 这两座城市由一条高速铁路连接。
The system was connected improperly. 系统连接不当。


疑问句:
How do you connect to the Wi-Fi network? 你如何连接到Wi-Fi网络？
Is the device properly connected? 设备连接正确吗？


否定句:
He couldn't connect to the server. 他无法连接到服务器。
The software failed to connect the two files. 软件未能连接这两个文件。


PHRASE->
