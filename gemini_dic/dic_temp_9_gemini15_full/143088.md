outbreak:/ˈaʊtbreɪk/ | n. |爆发，突然发生|A sudden increase in the number of cases of a disease.|例句：There was a sudden outbreak of cholera in the city.  城市里突然爆发了霍乱疫情。|例句：The outbreak of war caused widespread panic.  战争爆发引起了广泛的恐慌。|近义词：eruption, epidemic, flare-up |反义词：cessation, abatement


USAGE->
一般现在时:
An outbreak of flu occurs every winter. = 每到冬天都会爆发流感。
The rapid outbreak of the fire surprised everyone. = 火灾的迅速爆发让每个人都感到惊讶。


一般过去时:
A severe outbreak of food poisoning occurred last week. = 上周发生了一起严重的食品中毒事件。
The outbreak of the riot was unexpected. = 暴乱的爆发是出乎意料的。


现在完成时:
There have been several outbreaks of violence in the city this year. = 今年城市里已经发生了几起暴力事件。
Scientists have studied the outbreak of the disease for years. = 科学家们已经研究这种疾病的爆发多年了。


过去完成时:
Before the emergency services arrived, the fire had already reached its peak during the outbreak. = 在紧急服务人员到达之前，火灾在爆发期间已经达到顶峰。
By the time the police arrived, the outbreak of fighting had already subsided. = 等警察赶到的时候，打斗已经平息了。


将来时:
Health officials predict another outbreak of the virus next year. = 卫生官员预测明年病毒还会再次爆发。
Experts warn that a new outbreak is possible in the near future. = 专家警告说，近期可能再次爆发。


虚拟语气:
If there were an outbreak of Ebola, the consequences would be devastating. = 如果爆发埃博拉疫情，后果将是灾难性的。
I wish there hadn't been such a devastating outbreak of the disease. = 我真希望不会有如此严重的疾病爆发。


被动语态:
The outbreak was quickly contained by the authorities. = 疫情很快就被当局控制住了。
The cause of the outbreak is still being investigated. = 爆发的起因仍在调查中。


疑问句:
What caused the outbreak of the disease? = 导致这次疾病爆发的起因是什么？
When did the outbreak begin? = 爆发始于何时？


否定句:
There was no outbreak of disease in the area. = 该地区没有发生疾病爆发。
The government denied that there was an outbreak of civil unrest. = 政府否认发生了内乱。
