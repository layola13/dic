overdevoted: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪvoʊtɪd/ | adj. | 过度献身的；过分忠诚的 | Excessively devoted; showing excessive loyalty or dedication, often to a fault.  |  He was overdevoted to his work, neglecting his family and health. 他过度投入工作，忽视了家庭和健康。 She's overdevoted to that cause, donating far more than she can afford. 她对那件事业过度投入，捐赠的钱远远超出了她的承受能力。 |近义词： excessively dedicated, overly committed, fanatical | 反义词： apathetic, indifferent, unconcerned |  词性：形容词
