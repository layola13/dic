penned:/pend/| v.|写下，著述|To write something.|The author penned a moving story about his childhood. 作者写了一个关于他童年的感人故事。|wrote, composed, authored|erased, deleted|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pens a new blog post every week. 他每周撰写一篇新的博客文章。
She pens poetry in her spare time.  她利用空闲时间写诗。

一般过去时:
I penned a letter to my grandmother yesterday. 昨天我给祖母写了一封信。
He penned a short story for the competition. 他为比赛写了一个短篇故事。

现在完成时:
She has penned several bestselling novels. 她已经撰写了几本畅销小说。
They have penned a joint statement on the issue. 他们就此事发表了一份联合声明。

过去完成时:
He had penned the first draft before the deadline. 他在截止日期前完成了初稿。
She had penned a response before she received his email. 在收到他的邮件之前，她已经写好了回复。

将来时:
I will pen a thank-you note after the meeting. 会议结束后，我将写一封感谢信。
She will pen a detailed report on the project. 她将撰写一份关于该项目的详细报告。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pen a longer essay. 如果我有更多时间，我会写一篇更长的文章。
I wish I could pen a novel as good as his. 我希望我能写出一部像他那样好的小说。

被动语态:
The letter was penned by a very skilled writer. 这封信是一位非常熟练的作家写的。
The agreement was penned by both parties. 协议由双方签署。

疑问句:
Did you pen that poem yourself? 你自己写的这首诗吗？
Who penned this article? 谁写的这篇文章？

否定句:
I did not pen that letter. 我没有写那封信。
She hasn't penned a single word today. 她今天一个字也没写。
