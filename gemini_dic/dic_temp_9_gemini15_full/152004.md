patch's:/pætʃz/| n.|补丁；修补；小块土地|A patch is a small area of something that is different from the area around it.  It can also refer to a piece of material used to mend something.|例句：He sewed a patch onto his jeans to repair the hole. 他缝了一块补丁到他的牛仔裤上以修补那个洞。

例句：The farmer cultivated a small patch of land. 这位农民耕种了一小块土地。|近义词：mend, repair, piece|反义词：whole, complete|名词
