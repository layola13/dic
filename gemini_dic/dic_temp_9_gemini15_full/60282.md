egged:/ɛɡd/| v. |用蛋砸；怂恿；激励 | to throw eggs at someone or something; to urge or encourage someone to do something, often something foolish or risky. |例句：The protesters egged the politician's car.  抗议者向这位政治家的车扔鸡蛋。
The kids egged each other on to jump off the bridge. 孩子们互相怂恿跳下桥。|近义词：incite, urge, goad |反义词：discourage, dissuade |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eggs his friends on to do crazy things. 他怂恿他的朋友们做疯狂的事情。
They often egg the houses of their rivals. 他们经常往对手的房子扔鸡蛋。


一般过去时:
The crowd egged the speaker on.  人群怂恿演讲者继续说下去。
Someone egged my car last night. 昨晚有人往我的车扔鸡蛋。


现在完成时:
The children have egged the teacher's car several times. 孩子们已经几次往老师的车扔鸡蛋了。
They have egged me on to apply for the job. 他们一直鼓励我申请这份工作。


过去完成时:
He had egged them on to break the law. 他之前怂恿他们违法。
The vandals had egged several cars before the police arrived.  破坏分子在警察到来之前已经往好几辆车扔鸡蛋了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
