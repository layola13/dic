kwanzaa:/kwɑːnˈzɑː/|/ˈkwɑːn.zɑː/| n. | kwanzaa节 | Kwanzaa is a week-long celebration held from December 26 to January 1, honoring African heritage and culture. It involves seven principles (Nguzo Saba), each represented by a symbol and discussed daily.  |  Many families celebrate Kwanzaa by lighting candles, sharing a feast, and reflecting on the principles. | 近义词：holiday, festival | 反义词：无
