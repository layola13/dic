clammily:/ˈklæmɪli/| adv.| 偷偷摸摸地；秘密地；闷闷不乐地 |In a clammy manner; secretly; sullenly.  The word evokes a feeling of secrecy, dampness, and potentially unease.  |例句：He slipped away clammily, avoiding eye contact. 他偷偷摸摸地溜走了，避免眼神接触。

例句：She answered clammily, her voice barely a whisper. 她闷闷不乐地回答，声音几乎是耳语。

近义词：secretly, stealthily, furtively, sullenly

反义词：openly, frankly, cheerfully
