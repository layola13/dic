phyloplankton:/ˌfaɪloʊˈplæŋktən/ | n. |浮游植物 | Phytoplankton are microscopic plants that drift in the water. They are the base of the aquatic food web and are crucial for producing oxygen. | Example:  The abundance of phytoplankton in the ocean directly impacts the marine ecosystem.  海洋中浮游植物的丰度直接影响海洋生态系统。 |近义词：microscopic algae, microalgae |反义词：zooplankton (浮游动物)
