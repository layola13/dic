litheness:/ˈlaɪθnəs/ | n. |轻盈；灵活；柔软 | The quality or state of being lithe; suppleness; flexibility.  It often refers to a graceful and effortless movement. |例句：The dancer's litheness was breathtaking.  舞者的轻盈令人叹为观止。

例句：Her movements were characterized by remarkable litheness and grace. 她的动作以非凡的轻盈和优雅为特征。

近义词：agility, suppleness, flexibility, grace, nimbleness

反义词：stiffness, rigidity, clumsiness, awkwardness


词源: 源自古英语 *līþ,  意思是“柔软的，顺滑的”。  这个词根也与“lithe”（柔软的，灵活的）相关。
