predefect:/ˌpriːˈdɛfɛkt/| adj.| 预先缺陷；先有缺陷 |  Describing a defect that exists before a certain point in time or process, often implying a pre-existing flaw or imperfection.  It suggests a fault that was present before something else happened, such as a manufacturing process or a specific event. | Example: The predefect in the material caused the entire batch to be rejected.  这个材料的预先缺陷导致整批产品被拒收。|近义词：prior defect, initial flaw, inherent defect |反义词：postdefect, subsequent flaw
