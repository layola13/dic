exploitative:/ɪkˈsplɔɪtətɪv/ | adj. |剥削性的，利用的 |  Characterized by or involving exploitation; taking unfair advantage of someone or something for personal gain.  | The company's exploitative labor practices led to widespread protests. 这家公司的剥削性劳动行为引发了广泛的抗议。  |  oppressive, unjust, predatory | fair, equitable, just | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory owner is exploitative of his workers. = 工厂老板剥削他的工人。
This business model is exploitative of its customers. = 这种商业模式剥削了它的顾客。


一般过去时:
The landlord was exploitative of his tenants. = 房东剥削了他的租户。
The company's practices were exploitative in the past. = 这家公司过去的做法是剥削性的。


现在完成时:
The government has been exploitative of its natural resources. = 政府一直在剥削其自然资源。
They have consistently been exploitative of their employees. = 他们一直都在剥削他们的员工。


过去完成时:
The colonists had been exploitative of the indigenous population. = 殖民者一直在剥削当地居民。
The company had already been exploitative before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，该公司就已经存在剥削行为。


将来时:
He will continue to be exploitative of his position. = 他将继续利用他的职位谋取私利。
The new law will hopefully prevent exploitative practices. = 新法律有望防止剥削行为。


虚拟语气:
If the company were less exploitative, its employees would be happier. = 如果公司不那么剥削，员工就会更快乐。
I wish the system wasn't so exploitative of the poor. = 我希望这个制度不要那么剥削穷人。


被动语态:

The resources were exploited by the exploitative government. = 资源被剥削性的政府所剥削。


疑问句:

Are their business practices truly exploitative? = 他们的商业行为真是剥削性的吗？


否定句:
The new policy is not exploitative. = 新政策不是剥削性的。
The company claims its practices are not exploitative. = 公司声称其做法并非剥削性的。


PHRASE->
exploitative labor practices: 剥削性的劳动行为
exploitative pricing: 剥削性的定价

exploitative use of resources: 对资源的剥削性利用
highly exploitative: 高度剥削的


词源:
exploitative源于动词exploit，意思是“利用，开发”，加上形容词后缀-ative，表示“具有……性质的”。  Exploit本身则来自法语exploiter，最终源于拉丁语ex-（出，外）+plectere（编织，交织），引申出“利用，开发”的意思。
