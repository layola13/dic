harsh:/hɑːrʃ/| adj.|严厉的；严酷的；刺耳的；粗糙的 |  Unpleasantly rough or severe.  Characterized by severity, rigor, or lack of leniency. |例句：The teacher gave him a harsh punishment. 老师对他进行了严厉的惩罚。

例句：The desert sun is harsh and unforgiving.  沙漠的阳光严酷无情。


近义词：severe, rigorous, stern, rough, unpleasant, austere

反义词：gentle, mild, lenient, soft, pleasant, kind


词源:  Old English *hearh "rough, rugged," of Germanic origin; related to Old High German harag "rough, hairy," Old Norse harr "hair."  The sense development is likely from "rough to the touch" to "rough in manner or treatment."
