overbaked:/ˌoʊvərˈbeɪkt/| adj. |烤过头了的；过度烘烤的| Cooked for too long, resulting in dryness and sometimes burning.  The cake was overbaked and quite dry.|近义词：burnt, scorched, dried out |反义词：underbaked, moist


USAGE->

The bread is overbaked. = 面包烤过头了。
