reenactment:/ˌriːˈækn̩t/| n. |历史事件的重现；再现；模拟表演 |The act of recreating a past event, typically a historical battle or significant moment, for educational, entertainment, or commemorative purposes.  It often involves participants dressing in period costumes and using historically accurate equipment. |例句：The reenactment of the Civil War battle was incredibly detailed and immersive.  内战战役的再现非常详细和身临其境。

例句：The museum organized a reenactment of the famous trial. 博物馆组织了一次著名的审判的重现。


近义词：recreation, reproduction, reconstruction, simulation |反义词：destruction, demolition

词性：名词
