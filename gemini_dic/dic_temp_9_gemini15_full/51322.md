detains:/dɪˈteɪnz/ | vt. | 拘留；耽搁；阻止 | To keep someone in an official place, such as a police station, because they are suspected of committing a crime; to delay or prevent something from happening. |例句：The police detained him for questioning.  警察拘留他进行讯问。


The customs officers detained the package because it contained illegal substances. 海关官员扣留了包裹，因为它含有违禁品。

近义词：hold, arrest, delay, impede, restrain | 反义词：release, liberate, free


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police detain suspects for questioning. = 警方拘留嫌疑人进行讯问。
He detains information that could be helpful to the investigation. = 他隐瞒了可能对调查有帮助的信息。

一般过去时:
They detained him at the airport. = 他们在机场拘留了他。
The heavy rain detained us at home. = 大雨把我们困在了家里。

现在完成时:
The authorities have detained several protesters. = 当局已拘留了几名抗议者。
The snowstorm has detained the flights. = 暴风雪延误了航班。

过去完成时:
They had detained him for hours before releasing him. = 他们在释放他之前已经拘留了他几个小时。
The traffic jam had detained us, causing us to miss our appointment. = 交通堵塞耽误了我们，导致我们错过了约会。

将来时:
They will detain him until his trial. = 他们会将他拘留到审判为止。
The bad weather will likely detain the travelers. = 恶劣天气可能会耽误旅客行程。

虚拟语气:
If they detained him, they would have found the evidence. = 如果他们拘留了他，他们就会找到证据。
I wish they hadn’t detained the package; it was important. = 我真希望他们没有扣留包裹；它很重要。

被动语态:
He was detained by the police. = 他被警方拘留了。
The flight was detained due to bad weather. = 由于恶劣天气，航班被延误了。

疑问句:
Were they detained at the border? = 他们在边境被拘留了吗？
Will the meeting be detained? = 会议会被耽搁吗？

否定句:
They didn't detain him because they lacked evidence. = 他们没有拘留他，因为他们缺乏证据。
The storm didn’t detain the ship. = 风暴没有耽误那艘船。


PHRASE->
detain someone for questioning = 拘留某人进行讯问
detain a package = 扣留包裹
detain a shipment = 扣留货物
detain a suspect = 拘留嫌疑犯
be detained in custody = 被拘留


词源:
From Old French *detenir, from Latin detinēre, from tenere "to hold".  The prefix "de-" intensifies the meaning.
