scarlet:/ˈskɑːrlɪt/| adj.|猩红色的，鲜红色的|A bright red color.  It is often associated with passion, danger, and sometimes royalty.|例句：Her dress was a vibrant scarlet.她的裙子是鲜艳的猩红色。

例句：The cardinal's robes were a deep scarlet. 红衣主教的袍子是深猩红色。


近义词：crimson, ruby, vermilion, red

反义词：pale, white


词源:  The word "scarlet" comes from the Old French word "escarlate," which ultimately derives from the Arabic word "qirmizi," referring to a type of kermes dye obtained from insects.  This dye produced a deep red color.
