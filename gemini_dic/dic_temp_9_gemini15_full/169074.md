progress:/ˈprɑːɡrɛs/ /ˈprɒɡrɛs/| n. v. |进步；进展；发展|

英文解析：Movement forward or onward; advancement.  The act of moving forward toward a destination or goal.  The process of improving or developing.

例句：
The project is progressing well. = 项目进展顺利。

She has made significant progress in her studies. = 她在学业上取得了显著进步。


近义词：advancement, development, improvement, growth

反义词：regression, deterioration, decline, setback


词性：名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He progresses steadily in his career. = 他在事业上稳步前进。
The company makes steady progress. = 公司稳步发展。

一般过去时:
The scientists progressed with their experiment. = 科学家们继续他们的实验。
The country progressed economically. = 这个国家在经济上取得了进步。

现在完成时:
We have progressed significantly in our understanding of the disease. = 我们对这种疾病的理解有了显著的进步。
They have made great progress on the project. = 他们在这个项目上取得了很大的进展。

过去完成时:
They had progressed far before we joined the team. = 在我们加入团队之前，他们已经取得了很大的进展。
The negotiations had progressed smoothly until the last moment. = 谈判一直进行得很顺利，直到最后一刻。

将来时:
We will progress to the next stage of the plan. = 我们将进入计划的下一阶段。
The technology will progress rapidly in the coming years. = 未来几年，这项技术将迅速发展。

虚拟语气:
If we progressed more quickly, we could finish earlier. = 如果我们进展更快一些，我们就能早点完成。
I wish the project had progressed more smoothly. = 我希望这个项目进展得更顺利一些。

被动语态:
The work is being progressed efficiently. = 工作正在有效地推进。
The project was progressed quickly. = 项目进展迅速。

疑问句:
How is the project progressing? = 项目进展如何？
What progress has been made? = 取得了什么进展？

否定句:
The negotiations didn't progress as expected. = 谈判进展不如预期。
The team hasn't progressed much recently. = 团队最近进展不大。
