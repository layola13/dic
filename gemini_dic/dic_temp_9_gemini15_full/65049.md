/iːtiːˈdiː/|/ɛtɪˈdiː/ |abbr.|电子论文|Electronic Thesis or Dissertation.  It refers to a digital version of a thesis or dissertation submitted as a requirement for an academic degree.  |例句：My ETD is due next week. = 我的电子论文下周到期。

例句：I submitted my ETD to the university library. = 我已将我的电子论文提交给了大学图书馆。


近义词：digital thesis, electronic dissertation

反义词：hardcopy thesis, print dissertation


词性:abbr.
