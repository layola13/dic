counterprove:/ˈkaʊntə(r)pruːv/| vt.|反证，驳倒|To prove something to be false or incorrect by providing evidence to the contrary.  It involves presenting evidence that contradicts a statement or proposition, thereby demonstrating its falsity.|Example: The new evidence counterproves the defendant's alibi. 新证据反驳了被告的不在场证明。|近义词：refute, disprove, rebut|反义词：prove, verify, confirm
