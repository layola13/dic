rubayat:/ˈrubaɪjæt/| n.|鲁拜集，四行诗|A rubaiyat is a collection of quatrains, especially one by Omar Khayyam.  The term often refers specifically to the collection of quatrains by the Persian poet Omar Khayyam.  It is characterized by its AABA rhyming scheme and often deals with themes of life, death, love, and wine.|Example: Edward Fitzgerald's translation of Omar Khayyam's *Rubaiyat* is widely known.|近义词：quatrain collection, verse collection|反义词：N/A|名词
