sew:/soʊ/| v.|缝纫，缝合|To join or unite (pieces of fabric or other material) with needle and thread, or a similar process.|She sewed a button onto her shirt. 她把纽扣缝在了衬衫上。|She carefully sewed the tear in her jeans. 她仔细地缝补了她牛仔裤上的破洞。|mend, stitch, darn|rip, tear


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I sew every Sunday. = 我每个星期天都缝纫。
She sews beautiful dresses. = 她缝制美丽的连衣裙。

一般过去时:
I sewed a new dress last week. = 我上周缝了一件新裙子。
My grandmother sewed all of our clothes. = 我奶奶缝制了我们所有的衣服。

现在完成时:
I have sewn many quilts in my life. = 我一生缝制了许多棉被。
She has sewn that button on many times. = 她已经缝过很多次那个纽扣了。

过去完成时:
I had sewn the dress before the party started. = 派对开始之前我已经缝好了裙子。
She had sewn all the patches on before she washed the jeans. = 在她洗牛仔裤之前，她已经缝好了所有的补丁。

将来时:
I will sew a new skirt tomorrow. = 我明天会缝一条新裙子。
She will sew the curtains for the living room. = 她将缝制客厅的窗帘。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would sew a quilt. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会缝制一床棉被。
I wish I could sew as well as my mother. = 我希望我能像我母亲一样擅长缝纫。

被动语态:
The button was sewn on securely. = 纽扣被牢固地缝上了。
The dress is being sewn by a professional seamstress. = 这件衣服正在由一位专业的裁缝缝制。

疑问句:
Do you sew? = 你会缝纫吗？
Can you sew a button? = 你会缝纽扣吗？

否定句:
I don't sew very often. = 我不经常缝纫。
She didn't sew the hem properly. = 她没有把下摆缝好。



PHRASE->
sew on: 缝上
sew up: 缝合；解决（问题）
sew together: 缝合在一起
hand-sew: 手缝
machine-sew: 机器缝
