dissect:/dɪˈsɛkt/ | vt. | 解剖；仔细分析 | To cut up (a body, plant, or other organism) in order to study its internal parts.  To analyze something thoroughly, often in a critical way. | 例句：The biologist dissected the frog to examine its organs. 生物学家解剖青蛙以检查其器官。

The surgeon carefully dissected the tumor. 外科医生仔细地解剖了肿瘤。


例句：Let's dissect this problem and find a solution. 让我们仔细分析这个问题并找到解决方案。

The critic dissected the author's latest novel, pointing out its flaws. 批评家仔细分析了作者的最新小说，指出了其缺陷。


近义词：analyze, examine, scrutinize,  deconstruct

反义词：synthesize, construct, assemble


词性：动词
