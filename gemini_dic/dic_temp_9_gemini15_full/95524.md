including:/ɪnˈkluːdɪŋ/ | prep. | 包括；包含 |  Including means comprising or containing as part of a whole. It introduces an item or items that are part of a larger group or set. | Example: The price includes tax and delivery.  价格包含税费和送货费用。|  Example:  The tour includes a visit to the museum and a boat trip. 旅行包括参观博物馆和乘船游览。|近义词：containing, encompassing, comprising | 反义词：excluding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The package including all the necessary components. = 包裹包括所有必要的组件。
This list including all the names. = 这个列表包括所有名字。


一般过去时:
The meeting included a presentation on the new product. = 会议包括关于新产品的演示。
The report included several charts and graphs. = 报告包括几个图表。


现在完成时:
The project has included significant contributions from many volunteers. = 该项目已经包含了许多志愿者的重要贡献。
She has included all the relevant details in her report. = 她在报告中包含了所有相关的细节。


过去完成时:
They had included the missing information before submitting the report. = 他们在提交报告之前已经包含了缺失的信息。
The final count had included all the votes cast. = 最终计数包含了所有投出的选票。


将来时:
The next update will include several bug fixes and new features. = 下次更新将包含多个错误修复和新功能。
The exhibition will include works by both established and emerging artists. = 展览将包括知名和新兴艺术家的作品。


虚拟语气:
If the plan included more details, we would have a better understanding. = 如果计划包含更多细节，我们会更好地理解。
I wish the contract included a clause about termination. = 我希望合同中包含关于终止的条款。


被动语态:
All the necessary documents were included in the package. = 所有必要的文档都包含在包裹中。
The new features are being included in the next software release. = 新功能将包含在下一个软件版本中。


疑问句:
Does the price include shipping? = 价格包含运费吗？
Does the menu include vegetarian options? = 菜单包含素食选择吗？


否定句:
The original plan did not include any contingency measures. = 原计划不包含任何应急措施。
The list does not include his name. = 列表中不包含他的名字。
