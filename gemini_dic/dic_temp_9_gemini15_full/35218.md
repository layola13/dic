clue:/kluː/| n.|线索，提示|A piece of information that helps you solve a problem or mystery.|The detective followed the clue to the killer's hideout.  侦探顺着线索找到了杀手的藏身之处。|hint, indication, suggestion|mystery, secret|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The clue helps us solve the puzzle. = 这条线索帮助我们解开了谜题。
This clue points to the suspect. = 这条线索指向嫌疑人。


一般过去时:
The clue led the police to the criminal. = 这条线索引导警方找到了罪犯。
The detective found a vital clue yesterday. = 侦探昨天找到了一条至关重要的线索。


现在完成时:
The police have found several clues. = 警方已经找到几条线索。
They have followed all the clues they found. = 他们已经追查了他们找到的所有线索。


过去完成时:
The investigators had already found the clue before the suspect escaped. = 嫌疑犯逃跑前，调查人员已经找到了线索。
They had followed many clues before solving the case. = 在破案之前，他们已经追查过许多线索。


将来时:
The new evidence will provide a crucial clue. = 新证据将提供一条至关重要的线索。
We will look for more clues tomorrow. = 我们明天将寻找更多线索。


虚拟语气:
If we had another clue, we could solve the mystery. = 如果我们有另一条线索，就能解开这个谜团。
I wish I had a clue about what happened. = 我真希望知道发生了什么。


被动语态:
The clue was discovered by a sharp-eyed witness. = 这条线索是由一位眼尖的目击者发现的。
The clues are being analyzed by the forensic team. = 这些线索正在由法医小组进行分析。


疑问句:
Do you have any clues? = 你有任何线索吗？
What clues did the detective find? = 侦探发现了什么线索？


否定句:

We found no clues at the crime scene. = 我们在犯罪现场没有发现任何线索。
