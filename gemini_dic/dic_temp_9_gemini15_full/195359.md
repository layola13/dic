semimanagerially:/ˌsɛmiˈmænɪdʒəriəli/| adv.| 半管理地，部分管理地 |  This word describes actions or processes that are carried out in a way that is partly managerial but not fully so. It suggests a level of involvement or influence that is less than a full manager's.  |The project was run semimanagerially, with team members taking on some leadership responsibilities.|近义词：partially managerially, quasi-managerially |反义词：fully managerially, completely managerially

词源:

The word is formed from the combination of:

* **semi-**:  A prefix meaning "half," "partly," or "partially."
* **managerial**: An adjective referring to management or managers.
* **-ly**: An adverbial suffix.


Therefore, "semimanagerially" literally means "in a partly managerial way."
