prides:/praɪdz/| n.|骄傲，自豪，自尊；（狮子的）群；精华；自负|Pride is a feeling of deep pleasure or satisfaction derived from one's own achievements, qualities, or possessions, or from those of someone with whom one is closely associated.  It can also refer to a group of lions.  |例句：He takes pride in his work. 他为自己的工作感到自豪。

例句：The lion prides are scattered across the savanna. 狮群散布在稀树草原上。


近义词：vanity, conceit, arrogance, boastfulness|反义词：humility, modesty, shame


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prides himself on his cooking skills. = 他为自己的厨艺而自豪。
She prides herself on her honesty. = 她以自己的诚实为荣。


一般过去时:
He prided himself on his achievements. = 他为自己的成就感到自豪。
They prided themselves on their teamwork. = 他们为他们的团队合作感到自豪。


现在完成时:
She has prided herself on her independence all her life. = 她一生都以自己的独立为荣。
He has prided himself on being a successful businessman. = 他一直以成为一名成功的商人而自豪。


过去完成时:
He had prided himself on his quick wit, but this time he was stumped. = 他一直以自己机智敏捷而自豪，但这次他却束手无策了。
She had prided herself on her ability to remain calm under pressure, but this time she panicked. = 她一直以自己能够在压力下保持冷静而自豪，但这次她却慌了神。


将来时:
He will pride himself on finishing the marathon. = 他将会以完成马拉松为荣。
She will pride herself on her ability to speak multiple languages. = 她将会以自己能说多种语言为荣。


虚拟语气:
If he had finished the project on time, he would have prided himself on his efficiency. = 如果他按时完成了项目，他就会以自己的效率为荣。
I wish I could pride myself on my organizational skills, but I am rather disorganized. = 我希望我能以我的组织能力为荣，但我相当缺乏条理。


被动语态:
His achievements were prided by everyone. = 他的成就受到所有人的赞扬。 (Note:  The passive voice is less common with "pride".)
Her work ethic is prided upon by her colleagues. = 她的职业道德受到同事的赞赏。(Note:  The passive voice is less common with "pride".)


疑问句:
Does he pride himself on his appearance? = 他以自己的外表为傲吗？
What does she pride herself on? = 她以什么为荣？


否定句:
He doesn't pride himself on his looks. = 他并不以自己的外貌为傲。
She did not pride herself on her academic achievements. = 她并不以自己的学术成就为荣。
