conchology:/kɒŋˈkɒlədʒi/| n. |贝壳学；软体动物学|The study of shells and mollusks.  Conchology involves the classification, identification, and collection of shells, often focusing on their morphology, distribution, and evolutionary history. |例句：He has a vast collection of shells from around the world, showcasing his passion for conchology. 他收藏了来自世界各地的各种贝壳，展现了他对贝壳学的热情。|近义词：malacology, shell collecting|反义词：无
