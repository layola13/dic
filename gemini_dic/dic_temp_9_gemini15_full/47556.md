deauthorizes:/ˌdiːˈɔːθəraɪzɪz/ | vt. |取消授权；撤销授权 | To remove someone's authority or power; to revoke authorization. |例句：The company deauthorized his access to the confidential files. 公司取消了他访问机密文件的权限。|例句：The government deauthorized the use of that pesticide. 政府撤销了使用那种杀虫剂的授权。|近义词：revoke, rescind, invalidate, disauthorize |反义词：authorize, empower

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company deauthorizes access to sensitive data without proper security clearance. = 公司未经适当的安全许可，便取消了访问敏感数据的权限。
He regularly deauthorizes accounts that are inactive for more than six months. = 他定期取消超过六个月不活跃的账户的权限。


一般过去时:
The manager deauthorized the project after discovering financial irregularities. = 经理在发现财务违规后取消了该项目的授权。
They deauthorized the software license due to a breach of contract. = 由于违反合同，他们取消了软件许可证。


现在完成时:
The system administrator has deauthorized all unauthorized users. = 系统管理员已取消所有未经授权用户的权限。
The board has deauthorized the proposed merger due to unforeseen circumstances. = 董事会由于不可预见的情况取消了拟议中的合并。


过去完成时:
The court had deauthorized the previous ruling before the new evidence emerged. = 在新的证据出现之前，法院已经取消了之前的裁决。
They had deauthorized the employee's access even before the investigation began. = 甚至在调查开始之前，他们就已经取消了该员工的访问权限。


将来时:
The CEO will deauthorize the outdated policies next quarter. = 首席执行官将在下个季度取消过时的政策。
The IT department will deauthorize the old server once the new one is online. = 新服务器上线后，IT 部门将取消旧服务器的授权。


虚拟语气:
If the security breach hadn't happened, they wouldn't have deauthorized the system. = 如果没有发生安全漏洞，他们就不会取消该系统的授权。
It would be irresponsible if the government didn't deauthorize the dangerous chemical. = 如果政府不取消这种危险化学品的授权，那将是不负责任的。


被动语态:
His access was deauthorized due to a security violation. = 由于违反安全规定，他的访问权限被取消了。
The software license was deauthorized after the payment was overdue. = 付款逾期后，软件许可证被取消了。


疑问句:
Was his access to the database deauthorized? = 他的数据库访问权限被取消了吗？
Have they deauthorized the use of this technology? = 他们是否取消了这项技术的授权？


否定句:
The company did not deauthorize his access to the system. = 公司没有取消他访问该系统的权限。
They haven't deauthorized the project yet. = 他们还没有取消该项目的授权。
