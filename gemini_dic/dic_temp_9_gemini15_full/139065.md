oatcake:/ˈoʊtkeɪk/ | n. |燕麦饼，烤燕麦饼 | A thin, flat cake made from oatmeal.  Oatcakes are typically unleavened and can be savory or sweet, depending on the ingredients. They are a traditional food in Scotland and parts of northern England. |Example:  He enjoyed a simple breakfast of oatcakes with cheese. 他享用了一顿简单的早餐，吃了奶酪燕麦饼。  |近义词：oatmeal biscuit, bannock (in some regions)|反义词：None specifically;  the antonym would depend on the context (e.g., a rich cake if contrasting texture and richness).
