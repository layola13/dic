centrical:/ˈsɛntrɪkl/| adj.|中心的，中央的| Relating to or situated at or near the center.  The word emphasizes the importance or dominance of the central position.|The church is centrical to the village life. 教堂是村庄生活的中心。The arguments presented were centrical to the debate.  提出的论点是辩论的中心。|近义词：central, medial|反义词：peripheral, marginal
