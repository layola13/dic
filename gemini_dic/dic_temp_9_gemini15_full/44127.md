criss:/krɪs/| adj.| 交错的，交叉的 |  crossing or intersecting in a pattern resembling a crisscross; arranged or done in a crisscross pattern.  |例句：The threads were crisscrossed to form a strong fabric. 线交错编织成结实的布料。The streets crisscross the city. 街道纵横交错贯穿城市。|近义词：interlaced, interwoven, intersecting |反义词：parallel, straight
