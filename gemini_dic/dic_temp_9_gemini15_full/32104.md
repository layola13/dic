chestfuls:/ˈtʃɛstfʊlz/| n.|一箱一箱的；满满一箱；许多|The word "chestfuls" is the plural form of "chestful," meaning a quantity that fills a chest. It implies a large amount or number of something.  It's often used figuratively to describe an abundance.|例句：The farmer harvested chestfuls of apples.  这个农民收获了一箱箱的苹果。

The pirates buried chestfuls of gold coins. 海盗们埋藏了满满一箱箱的金币。|近义词：loads, heaps, piles|反义词：a few, a little, handful|词性：名词
