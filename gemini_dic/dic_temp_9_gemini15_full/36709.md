collisionally:/kəˈlɪʒənəli/ | adv. |碰撞地；冲突地 | Adverb describing something happening as a result of collisions.  It indicates a process or event influenced by or occurring through collisions between particles or objects. | Example: The gas is heated collisionally.  气体通过碰撞被加热。 |近义词：impactfully, through collisions | 反义词：smoothly, effortlessly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, so it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It doesn't have tenses or voice.  However, it can be used in various sentence structures.

Simple Sentence:
The molecules reacted collisionally. = 分子通过碰撞发生反应。
The energy was transferred collisionally. = 能量通过碰撞转移。


Complex Sentence:
Because the particles were highly energetic, the reaction proceeded collisionally. = 因为粒子能量很高，反应通过碰撞进行。
It was determined that the process occurred collisionally, not radiatively. =  人们确定该过程是通过碰撞发生的，而不是通过辐射发生的。


Compound Sentence:
The system reached equilibrium collisionally, and the reaction rate slowed down. = 系统通过碰撞达到平衡，反应速率减慢。
The atoms interacted collisionally, and a new compound was formed. = 原子通过碰撞相互作用，形成了一种新的化合物。
