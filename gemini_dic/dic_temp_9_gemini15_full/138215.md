noshers:/ˈnɒʃəz/| n. |随便吃东西的人；爱吃零食的人 |A nosher is someone who frequently eats snacks or small amounts of food between meals.  They enjoy nibbling and often have a preference for convenient or tasty treats.  The term often carries a slightly informal or even playful connotation. |例句：He's a real nosher; he's always got something to munch on. 他是个十足的零食爱好者，总是在吃东西。|近义词：snacker, nibbler, grazer |反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be someone who rarely snacks.
