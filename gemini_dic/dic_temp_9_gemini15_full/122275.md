mitigators: /ˈmɪtɪɡeɪtərz/ | n. | 缓解因素；减轻因素 | Things that lessen the severity of something; factors that reduce the impact or harm of something.  | Example:  Effective emergency planning is one of the key mitigators of disaster impact.  有效的应急计划是减轻灾害影响的关键因素之一。


Example:  The company implemented several mitigators to reduce the risk of data breaches.  该公司实施了几项缓解措施以降低数据泄露的风险。| 近义词：alleviators, reducers, moderators | 反义词：exacerbators, intensifiers | 名词
