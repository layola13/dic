overfondness:/ˌoʊvərˈfɑːndnəs/ | n. |过度喜爱，溺爱 |Excessive fondness; an excessive degree of affection or love.  It often implies a degree of spoiling or enabling. |例句：Her overfondness for her grandson led her to spoil him rotten.  她的过度溺爱导致她把孙子宠坏了。


例句：His overfondness of sweets contributed to his weight problem. 他对糖果的过度喜爱导致了他的体重问题。


近义词：doting, indulgence, spoiling, pampering


反义词：neglect, indifference, harshness


词性：名词
