re-clean:/ˌriːˈkliːn/| vt.|重新清洁，再清洁|To clean something again, often more thoroughly than the first time.|例句：I had to re-clean the kitchen after the party.  派对后我不得不重新打扫厨房。|例句：The surgeon decided to re-clean the wound before stitching it. 外科医生决定在缝合伤口前重新清洁伤口。|近义词：clean again, reclean, rewash|反义词：dirty, soil


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-clean the office every Friday. = 他们每个星期五都重新打扫办公室。
She re-cleans the bathroom after each use. = 她每次使用后都重新清洁浴室。

一般过去时:
He re-cleaned the car after the mud splattered it. = 泥巴溅到车上后，他重新清洗了汽车。
We re-cleaned the carpets after the dog had an accident. = 狗出了意外后，我们重新清洁了地毯。

现在完成时:
I have re-cleaned the windows three times this week. = 我本周已经三次重新擦洗窗户了。
They have re-cleaned the entire house in preparation for the guests. = 他们为了迎接客人已经重新打扫了整个房子。

过去完成时:
She had re-cleaned the kitchen before her mother-in-law arrived. = 她的婆婆来之前，她已经重新打扫了厨房。
We had re-cleaned the pool after the leaves had fallen in. = 树叶落入泳池后，我们已经重新清洁了泳池。

将来时:
They will re-clean the swimming pool tomorrow. = 他们明天将重新清洁游泳池。
She will re-clean the apartment before the landlord's inspection. = 她会在房东检查之前重新打扫公寓。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would re-clean the entire house. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新打扫整个房子。
If she were more diligent, she would re-clean her room more often. = 如果她更勤快一些，她会更频繁地重新打扫她的房间。

被动语态:

否定句:
