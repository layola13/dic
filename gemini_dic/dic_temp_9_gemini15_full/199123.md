silencing: /ˈsaɪlənsɪŋ/ | v. | 使沉默；压制；使无效 | The act of making something silent or ineffective; suppressing or quieting something.  |  例句：The government attempted silencing dissent.  政府试图压制异见。

例句：The protestors were silenced by the police.  抗议者被警察压制了。


近义词：muting, quieting, suppressing, stifling, muffling, silencing


反义词：amplifying, encouraging, promoting, activating


词性：动词
