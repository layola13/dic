firebrick:/ˈfaɪərbrɪk/| n.|耐火砖|A firebrick is a brick that is resistant to high temperatures and is used in fireplaces, furnaces, and other high-heat applications.  It is typically made from refractory clay.|例句：The fireplace was lined with firebricks to withstand the intense heat.|近义词：refractory brick|反义词：None
