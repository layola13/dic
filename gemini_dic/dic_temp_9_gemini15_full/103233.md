jettisonable: /ˈdʒɛtɪsənəbəl/ | adj. | 可抛弃的；可丢弃的 | Capable of being jettisoned; able to be thrown away or discarded, especially in an emergency.  It implies that something is not essential and can be sacrificed if necessary, often to improve the chances of survival or success. |The extra weight was jettisonable in case of emergency.  在紧急情况下，额外的重量是可以抛弃的。 | dispensable, expendable, discardable | essential, indispensable, necessary |
