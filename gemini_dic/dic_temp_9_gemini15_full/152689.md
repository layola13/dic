pearl:/pɜːrl/| n. |珍珠；珍宝；精华|A pearl is a hard, smooth, lustrous spherical gem produced by some mollusks.  Pearls are highly valued for their beauty and rarity.  The word can also refer to something precious or excellent.|例句：She wore a necklace of pearls. 她戴着一串珍珠项链。

例句：The singer's voice was a pearl of beauty.  这位歌手的声音如同美妙的珍珠。


近义词：gem, jewel, treasure |反义词：dross, worthless, trash |词性：名词
