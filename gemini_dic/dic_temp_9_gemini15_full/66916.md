expelling:/ɪkˈspɛlɪŋ/| v.|驱逐，排出，开除|The act of forcing someone or something out;  removing someone from a place or group.  |例句：The school expelled the student for cheating. 学校因作弊而开除了这名学生。|例句：The volcano expelled a massive cloud of ash. 火山喷发出巨大的火山灰云。|近义词：ejecting, ousting, dismissing, removing|反义词：admitting, welcoming, including


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school expels students for serious misconduct. = 学校因严重不当行为而开除学生。
The pump expels water from the flooded basement. = 水泵将水从被淹的地下室排出。


一般过去时:
They expelled him from the club for his rude behavior. = 他们因他的粗鲁行为而将他逐出俱乐部。
The pressure expelled the air from the tire. = 压力将轮胎里的空气排出。


现在完成时:
The government has expelled several diplomats. = 政府已驱逐了几名外交官。
The machine has expelled all the waste products. = 机器已排出所有废物。


过去完成时:
They had expelled him before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他们已经将他开除了。
The volcano had expelled lava for days before it finally quieted. = 火山在最终平静下来之前已经喷发了几天熔岩。


将来时:
They will expel him if he continues to break the rules. = 如果他继续违反规章制度，他们将会开除他。
The system will automatically expel any unauthorized access attempts. = 系统将自动驱逐任何未经授权的访问尝试。


虚拟语气:
If they expelled him, there would be a protest. = 如果他们开除他，就会有抗议。
I wish they had expelled him sooner. = 我希望他们早点把他开除。


被动语态:
He was expelled from the university for plagiarism. = 他因剽窃而被大学开除。
The waste products were expelled by the machine. = 废物被机器排出。


疑问句:
Will they expel him from the team? = 他们会将他逐出球队吗？
Is the pump effectively expelling the water? = 水泵有效地排出水吗？


否定句:
They did not expel him, despite his many mistakes. = 尽管他犯了很多错误，他们也没有开除他。
The system did not expel the error message. = 系统没有排出错误消息。


PHRASE->
expel from = 从...中驱逐
expel by force = 强行驱逐
expel with force = 用力排出
expel gases = 排放气体
expel a student = 开除一个学生
