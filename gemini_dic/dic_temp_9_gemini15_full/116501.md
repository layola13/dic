maternal:/məˈtɜːnəl/| adj.|母性的，母亲的；母系的| Relating to or characteristic of a mother.  It often implies a nurturing and protective quality. |例句：She showed great maternal instinct towards her children. 她对她的孩子们表现出了伟大的母性本能。

例句：The maternal ward is where new mothers stay after giving birth. 产科病房是产后母亲居住的地方。


近义词：motherly, motherlike

反义词：paternal
