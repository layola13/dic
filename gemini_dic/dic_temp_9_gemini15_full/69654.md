fence-mending: /ˈfɛnsˌmɛndɪŋ/ | n. | 修补篱笆；和解 |

英文解析:The act of repairing a fence, often used metaphorically to describe the process of restoring a damaged relationship or repairing a rift.  It implies making amends and restoring harmony.

例句:

After a bitter argument, they engaged in some serious fence-mending.  一场激烈的争吵之后，他们开始认真地修复关系。

The politician's fence-mending efforts after the scandal were largely unsuccessful.  这位政治家在丑闻后的和解努力基本上没有成功。


近义词: reconciliation, rapprochement, making amends, peacemaking

反义词: estrangement, alienation, widening the rift
