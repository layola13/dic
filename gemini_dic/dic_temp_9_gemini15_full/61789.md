emphasise:/ˈɛmfəsaɪz/ | vt. |强调，重视 |To give special importance or prominence to something in speaking or writing.  |The report emphasised the need for improved safety procedures.  这份报告强调了改进安全程序的必要性。|stress, highlight, underline |downplay, understate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He emphasises the importance of hard work. 他强调努力工作的重要性。
She emphasises the details in her painting. 她强调她绘画中的细节。

一般过去时:
The teacher emphasised the key points of the lesson. 老师强调了这节课的重点。
He emphasised his commitment to the project. 他强调了他对这个项目的承诺。

现在完成时:
She has emphasised the urgency of the situation. 她已经强调了情况的紧急性。
They have emphasised the need for cooperation. 他们已经强调了合作的必要性。

过去完成时:
He had emphasised the dangers of smoking before. 他以前就强调过吸烟的危害。
She had emphasised the importance of punctuality many times. 她已经多次强调准时重要性。

将来时:
He will emphasise the benefits of the new program. 他将强调新项目的益处。
She will emphasise the importance of customer satisfaction. 她将强调客户满意度的重要性。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would emphasise the positive aspects. 如果我是你，我会强调积极的方面。
I wish he had emphasised the risks involved. 我希望他当时强调了其中的风险。

被动语态:
The importance of teamwork was emphasised in the presentation.  团队合作的重要性在演示中得到了强调。
The need for change is being emphasised by many experts.  许多专家正在强调变革的必要性。

疑问句:
What points did the speaker emphasise in his speech? 演讲者在他的演讲中强调了哪些要点？
Does the article emphasise the positive or negative aspects? 这篇文章是强调积极方面还是消极方面？

否定句:
He didn't emphasise the difficulties he faced. 他没有强调他面临的困难。
The report did not emphasise the financial implications.  该报告没有强调财务影响。
