/pɑː/ | pron. |  什么，哪个 |  The word "what" is an interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something. It can refer to things, actions, qualities, or ideas. | What is your name?  = 你叫什么名字？

What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？


近义词：which (在选择中), how (在方式上) | 反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？


一般过去时:
What did you see? = 你看到了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you done today? = 你今天做了什么？
What has she achieved? = 她取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you eaten before you came? = 你来之前吃了什么？
What had they decided before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们决定了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like? = 天气会怎么样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being discussed now? = 现在正在讨论什么？


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道该说什么。
He didn't tell me what he wanted. = 他没有告诉我他想要什么。



PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What's going on? = 发生了什么事？
What about...? = ......怎么样？
What if...? = 如果......将会怎样？
No matter what = 无论如何
