lepidoptera:/ˌlɛpɪˈdɒptərə/| n. |鳞翅目昆虫|The order Lepidoptera comprises moths and butterflies, characterized by their scaly wings.  Lepidoptera are a diverse group of insects with a wide range of sizes, colors, and habitats.|例句：The study of Lepidoptera is called lepidopterology.  鳞翅目昆虫的研究叫做鳞翅学。|例句：Many species of Lepidoptera are important pollinators. 许多鳞翅目昆虫是重要的传粉媒介。|近义词：butterflies and moths (collectively)|反义词：无
