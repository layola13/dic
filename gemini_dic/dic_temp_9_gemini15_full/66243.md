exclamatorily:/ˌɛkskləˈmeɪtərɪli/| adv. |感叹地，惊叹地 |In a way that shows strong feeling; emphatically.  It describes the manner in which something is said or done, emphasizing the emotional intensity.|例句：She exclaimed, "You did it!" exclamatorily. 她惊叹地说：“你做到了！”|近义词：emphatically, dramatically, excitedly |反义词：calmly, quietly, indifferently
