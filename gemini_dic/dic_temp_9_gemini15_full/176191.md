re-approach:/ˌriːəˈproʊtʃ/| vt.|重新尝试；再次接近；重新探讨|To approach again; to make a second attempt to deal with or solve something.|例句：After the initial rejection, she decided to re-approach the problem from a different angle.  首次被拒绝后，她决定从不同的角度重新审视这个问题。|例句：He re-approached the bank for a loan after improving his credit score.  在信用评分提高后，他再次向银行申请贷款。|近义词：revisit, reconsider, retry|反义词：abandon, give up|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-approaches the task with renewed determination. = 他以新的决心重新开始这项任务。
They re-approach the negotiation with a fresh perspective. = 他们以新的视角重新开始谈判。


一般过去时:
She re-approached her boss about the promotion. = 她再次向老板提起升职的事。
They re-approached the subject after a cooling-off period. = 经过一段冷静期后，他们再次谈论这个话题。


现在完成时:
He has re-approached the client several times. = 他已经多次联系了客户。
We have re-approached the problem from various perspectives. = 我们已经从各个角度重新审视了这个问题。


过去完成时:
She had re-approached the committee before the final decision. = 在最终决定之前，她已经再次向委员会提出了这个问题。
They had re-approached the architect about the design flaws. = 他们之前已经再次找到建筑师讨论设计缺陷。


将来时:
He will re-approach the issue after gathering more data. = 他将在收集更多数据后再次讨论这个问题。
They will re-approach the investors with a revised proposal. = 他们将带着修改后的提案再次与投资者洽谈。


虚拟语气:
If I were to re-approach the situation, I would handle it differently. = 如果我要重新处理这种情况，我会采取不同的方法。
I wish I had re-approached him before he left. = 我希望在他离开之前再次找到他。


被动语态:
The proposal was re-approached by the board after careful consideration. = 董事会经过仔细考虑后再次审议了该提案。
The matter will be re-approached at the next meeting. = 这件事将在下次会议上再次讨论。


疑问句:
Will they re-approach the negotiations? = 他们会重新开始谈判吗？
Should we re-approach the problem from a different angle? = 我们应该从不同的角度重新审视这个问题吗？


否定句:
He did not re-approach the topic during the meeting. = 他在会议上没有再次谈论这个话题。
They chose not to re-approach the lender after the initial refusal. = 在第一次被拒绝后，他们选择不再联系贷款机构。
