reconciler:/ˈrɛkənˌsaɪlər/| n. |调解者；和解者；对账员 |A reconciler is a person or thing that brings opposing parties or sides together.  It can also refer to software or a process that matches up data from different sources to ensure accuracy. |例句：The mediator acted as a reconciler, helping the two feuding families find common ground. 调解员充当调解人的角色，帮助两个不和的家庭找到共同点。|例句：The bank uses sophisticated software as a reconciler to check for discrepancies in its accounts. 银行使用复杂的软件作为对账员来检查其账户中的差异。|近义词：mediator, arbitrator, adjuster |反义词：divider, antagonist

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The software reconciles the accounts daily. = 该软件每天对账。
He reconciles conflicting reports. = 他调和相互冲突的报告。


一般过去时:
The team reconciled the differences between the two versions. = 团队协调了两个版本之间的差异。
She reconciled her bank statement with her transactions. = 她将银行对账单与她的交易进行核对。


现在完成时:
They have reconciled their differences and are now friends again. = 他们已经和解了，现在又是朋友了。
The accountant has reconciled the accounts for the past quarter. = 会计已经核对了上一季度的账目。


过去完成时:
After much deliberation, they had reconciled their opposing viewpoints. =经过一番深思熟虑后，他们最终调和了彼此对立的观点。
Before the audit, she had reconciled all the discrepancies in the data. = 在审计之前，她已经协调了数据中所有不一致的地方。


将来时:
We will reconcile the budget with the actual expenses. = 我们将把预算与实际支出进行核对。
He hopes to reconcile with his estranged brother soon. = 他希望很快能和疏远的兄弟和解。


虚拟语气:
If they reconciled, the company would be stronger. = 如果他们和解，公司会更强大。
I wish I could reconcile my desire for success with my need for personal time. = 我希望我能调和我对成功的渴望和对个人时间的需求。


被动语态:
The differences were reconciled through careful negotiation. = 通过仔细协商，这些分歧得到了解决。
The accounts are reconciled monthly. = 账户每月对账。


疑问句:
How do you reconcile these conflicting facts? = 你如何调和这些相互矛盾的事实？
Have they reconciled yet? = 他们和解了吗？


否定句:
The two parties could not reconcile their differences. = 双方无法调和分歧。
He has not yet reconciled himself to the loss. = 他还没有接受这个损失。
