/ˈpɛskiːst/| adj. |最讨厌的，最烦人的 | most annoying or troublesome;  The peskiest thing about the project was the constant interruptions.  |近义词：most annoying, most irritating, most bothersome |反义词：most pleasant, most delightful |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flies are the peskiest things in the summer. = 夏天苍蝇是最烦人的东西。
That child is the peskiest I've ever met. = 那孩子是我见过的最烦人的孩子。


一般过去时:
The mosquitoes were the peskiest insects during the camping trip. = 在露营旅行中，蚊子是最烦人的昆虫。
Yesterday's traffic jam was the peskiest part of my day. = 昨天的交通堵塞是我一天中最烦人的部分。


现在完成时:
The paperwork has been the peskiest aspect of this job. = 文书工作一直是这份工作中最烦人的方面。
This persistent cough has been the peskiest thing all week. = 这阵持续的咳嗽这周一直是最烦人的事情。


过去完成时:
The malfunctioning printer had been the peskiest problem all week before it was finally fixed. = 在最终修复之前，这台故障的打印机一直是整个星期中最烦人的问题。
The constant interruptions had been the peskiest part of the meeting. = 会议上不断的被打扰是会议中最烦人的部分。


将来时:
The upcoming deadline will be the peskiest part of the next few weeks. = 即将来临的截止日期将会是未来几周中最烦人的部分。
I suspect the long journey will be the peskiest part of the vacation. = 我怀疑漫长的旅程将会是假期中最烦人的部分。


虚拟语气:
If only those pesky mosquitoes weren't so peskiest, the camping would have been perfect. = 如果那些讨厌的蚊子没有那么烦人，露营本来会很完美。
If that leak hadn't been so peskiest, I could have finished painting the room today. = 如果那个漏水没有那么烦人，我今天就能把房间粉刷完了。


被动语态:
The most peskiest problem is being addressed by the team. = 团队正在处理最烦人的问题。
The constant interruptions were being handled by the assistant. = 助理正在处理不断的打扰。


疑问句:
What is the peskiest thing about your job? = 你工作中最烦人的事情是什么？
What was the peskiest part of the trip? = 旅行中最烦人的部分是什么？


否定句:
The project wasn't that peskiest; it was manageable. = 这个项目并没有那么烦人；它是可控的。
The weather wasn't peskiest at all; it was actually quite pleasant. = 天气一点也不烦人；实际上相当宜人。


PHRASE->

The peskiest problem = 最烦人的问题
The peskiest child = 最烦人的孩子
The peskiest task = 最烦人的任务
The peskiest customer = 最烦人的顾客


词源:
源自pesky (烦人的)，加上了最高级后缀-est。Pesky 的词源尚不明确，但可能与古英语或苏格兰语中的词语有关，表示“微不足道”或“令人讨厌”。
