pixelates:/ˈpɪksəleɪts/| v.|像素化，使成像素块状|To break an image or video into pixels, often resulting in a loss of detail and a blocky appearance.  This usually happens when an image is scaled down or compressed too much.|例句：The image pixelates when you enlarge it too much.  当您过分放大图像时，图像会变得像素化。

The video pixelates when the internet connection is slow.  当互联网连接缓慢时，视频会变得像素化。|近义词：blockify, rasterize|反义词：sharpen, enhance

词源:

The word "pixelates" is derived from "pixel," a contraction of "picture element."  The suffix "-ates" indicates a process or action.  Therefore, "pixelates" describes the process of becoming composed of or resembling pixels, typically due to low resolution or compression.
