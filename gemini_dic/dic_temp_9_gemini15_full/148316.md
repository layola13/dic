oversophisticated: /ˌoʊvərsəˈfɪstɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过分精致的；矫揉造作的；过于复杂的 |

英文解析:  Going beyond what is necessary or natural in an attempt to be sophisticated; excessively refined or elaborate; affectedly sophisticated.  It implies an artificiality or pretentiousness that detracts from genuine elegance or simplicity.


例句:

Her oversophisticated prose felt contrived and lacked genuine emotional depth.  她的过于精致的散文显得做作，缺乏真挚的情感深度。

The oversophisticated design of the building clashed with the surrounding rustic landscape.  建筑物过于精致的设计与周围质朴的乡村景观格格不入。


近义词: affected, pretentious, artificial, elaborate, ornate, mannered, highfalutin


反义词: simple, plain, unpretentious, natural, straightforward


词性: adjective (形容词)
