词源:
The word "caffeinated" is derived from "caffeine," which itself originates from "coffee."  The suffix "-inated" indicates the presence or treatment with caffeine.  "咖啡因"的英文"caffeine"源于"咖啡"（coffee），后缀"-inated"表示含有或经咖啡因处理。
