puniest: /ˈpjuːniɪst/ | adj. | 最小的，最弱的，最不重要的 |  The smallest, weakest, or least important.  It's a superlative form of "puny," emphasizing the extreme degree of smallness or weakness. |  例句：The puniest seedling struggled to survive the harsh winter.  最小的幼苗努力在严酷的冬天生存。

The puniest argument couldn't sway her decision. 最薄弱的论据无法改变她的决定。


近义词：smallest, weakest, least, insignificant, minuscule, trifling, feeble


反义词：largest, strongest, most important, significant, powerful


词性：形容词
