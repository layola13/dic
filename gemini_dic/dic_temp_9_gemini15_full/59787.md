ecotour:/ˈekoʊtʊr/| n. |生态旅游|A form of tourism involving visiting fragile, pristine, and usually protected areas within a framework that minimizes environmental impact and benefits local people.|例句：We took an ecotour to the Amazon rainforest. 我们去了亚马逊雨林进行了一次生态旅游。  We are planning an ecotour to Costa Rica next year. 我们计划明年去哥斯达黎加进行生态旅游。|近义词：nature tourism, responsible tourism, sustainable tourism |反义词：mass tourism, unsustainable tourism |名词
