clipping:/ˈklɪpɪŋ/| n. |剪报；剪下来的东西；修剪|A clipping is a piece of something, usually paper, that has been cut out, often from a larger publication like a newspaper or magazine.  It can also refer to the act of cutting something. |例句：I have a clipping of the article from yesterday's newspaper. 我有一份昨天报纸上的文章剪报。

例句：The gardener did some clipping of the hedges. 园丁修剪了一下树篱。


近义词：cutting, excerpt, snippet|反义词：pasting, attaching


词源：源自古英语 *clipian，意为“拥抱，抓住”。后来演变为“剪断，修剪”的意思。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gardener clips the hedges regularly. = 园丁定期修剪树篱。
She clips coupons from the newspaper. = 她从报纸上剪下优惠券。

一般过去时:
He clipped the article and saved it. = 他剪下了那篇文章并保存了下来。
They clipped the roses to shape the bushes. = 他们修剪玫瑰以塑造灌木的形状。

现在完成时:
I have clipped all the important information. = 我已经剪下了所有重要的信息。
She has clipped her hair short. = 她已经把头发剪短了。

过去完成时:
He had clipped the coupons before he went shopping. = 他在去购物之前已经剪好了优惠券。
She had clipped the roses before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，她已经修剪好了玫瑰花。

将来时:
I will clip the photos for the scrapbook. = 我将剪下照片用于剪贴簿。
He will clip the overgrown branches tomorrow. = 他明天将剪掉那些过度生长的树枝。

虚拟语气:
If I had clipped the coupon, I would have saved some money. = 如果我剪了优惠券，我就省钱了。
I wish I had clipped that article; it was interesting. = 我真希望我剪下了那篇文章；那篇文章很有趣。

被动语态:
The hedges are clipped twice a year. = 树篱一年修剪两次。
The article was clipped from the magazine. = 这篇文章是从杂志上剪下来的。

疑问句:
Did you clip the picture? = 你剪了那张照片吗？
Have you ever clipped your own hair? = 你曾经自己剪过头发吗？

否定句:
I didn't clip the coupon. = 我没有剪优惠券。
She hasn't clipped her nails yet. = 她还没有剪指甲。
