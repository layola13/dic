representing:/ˌreprɪˈzentɪŋ/ | v. |代表；象征；表现；扮演 | To act on behalf of someone or something; to symbolize or embody something; to portray or depict something. |例句：He is representing his company at the conference. 他代表他的公司参加会议。

例句：The painting represents a scene from the artist's childhood. 这幅画描绘了艺术家童年时代的一个场景。


近义词： embodying, symbolizing, portraying, depicting, standing for

反义词：misrepresenting, concealing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist is representing the beauty of nature in his painting. = 艺术家在他的画作中展现了自然之美。
She represents her country in international competitions. = 她代表她的国家参加国际比赛。


一般过去时:
He represented his client in court. = 他在法庭上代表他的客户。
The sculpture represented a mythical creature. = 这座雕塑代表了一个神话中的生物。


现在完成时:
She has represented her district in parliament for many years. = 她多年来一直代表她的选区在议会工作。
They have consistently represented the interests of their members. = 他们一直代表着会员们的利益。


过去完成时:
He had represented the company before the merger. = 在公司合并之前，他曾代表过该公司。
The lawyer had represented many clients in similar cases. = 这名律师曾代理过许多类似案件的客户。


将来时:
She will represent her school at the debating competition. = 她将代表她的学校参加辩论比赛。
He will be representing our company at the trade show. = 他将在贸易展会上代表我们公司。


虚拟语气:
If he were representing a larger company, he would have more influence. = 如果他代表的是一家更大的公司，他就会有更大的影响力。
I wish I were representing my country at the Olympics. = 我希望我能代表我的国家参加奥运会。


被动语态:
The interests of the minority were well represented in the final decision. = 少数人的利益在最终决定中得到了很好的体现。
The company is represented by a powerful lobby group. = 这家公司由一个强大的游说团体代表。


疑问句:
Who is representing the company at the meeting? = 谁在会议上代表公司？
Does this symbol represent freedom or oppression? = 这个符号代表自由还是压迫？


否定句:
The painting does not represent the artist's true feelings. = 这幅画并不代表艺术家的真实感受。
He did not represent his constituents' views accurately. = 他没有准确地代表其选民的观点。


PHRASE->
