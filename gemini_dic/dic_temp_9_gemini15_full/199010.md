signable:/ˈsaɪnəbl/ | adj. | 可签署的，可签名的 | Capable of being signed; suitable or fit to be signed.  | Example: The contract is signable by both parties.  该合同双方均可签署。  |近义词：signatory, subscribable | 反义词：unsignable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The document is signable. = 这份文件是可以签署的。
This form is signable online. = 这张表格可以在线签署。

一般过去时:
The contract was signable last week. = 上周该合同是可以签署的。
The agreement was signable before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，协议是可以签署的。

现在完成时:
The document has been signable since yesterday. = 这份文件从昨天起就可以签署了。
The application has been signable for a week now. = 申请表一周以来都可以签署了。

过去完成时:
The form had been signable before the changes were made. = 在修改之前，表格是可以签署的。
The agreement had been signable until the last minute. = 直到最后一刻，协议都是可以签署的。

将来时:
The contract will be signable tomorrow. = 合同明天可以签署。
The application will be signable after the review. = 审核后，申请表可以签署。

虚拟语气:
If the document were signable, I would sign it. = 如果文件可以签署，我会签署它。
I wish the contract were signable now. = 我希望合同现在可以签署。

被动语态:
The document is considered signable by the court. = 法院认为该文件是可以签署的。
The application is found to be signable. = 发现申请表是可以签署的。


疑问句:
Is the contract signable? = 合同可以签署吗？
Is this form signable by minors? = 未成年人可以签署这份表格吗？

否定句:
The document is not signable yet. = 文件还不能签署。
The contract was not signable without amendments. = 没有修改，合同就不能签署。
