goldilocks-economy:/ˈɡoʊldɪˌlɑːksɪˈkɑːnəmi/ | n. | 金发姑娘经济 | An economy that is neither too hot nor too cold, but just right; characterized by moderate growth, low inflation, and low unemployment.  It's a period of sustained economic expansion without overheating. | The goldilocks economy of the 1990s saw strong growth with relatively low inflation.  上世纪90年代的“金发姑娘经济”呈现强劲增长，通胀率相对较低。| 近义词：sweet spot economy, moderate economic growth | 反义词：stagflation, recession
