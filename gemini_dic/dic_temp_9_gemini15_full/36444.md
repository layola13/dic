colchicum:/ˈkɒlkɪkəm/| n. |秋水仙 |A genus of autumn-blooming flowering plants in the family Colchicaceae, known for their cup-shaped flowers and poisonous properties.  They are often mistaken for crocuses. |例句：Colchicum autumnale, also known as autumn crocus, is a common species of colchicum.  秋水仙，也称为秋番红花，是一种常见的秋水仙种类。|例句：The vibrant purple colchicums added a splash of color to the autumn garden.  鲜艳的紫色秋水仙为秋天的花园增添了一抹色彩。|近义词：autumn crocus, meadow saffron |反义词：无
