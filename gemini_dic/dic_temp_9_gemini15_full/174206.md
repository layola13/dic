quells:/kwɛlz/ | vt. |压制；平息；减轻 | To suppress or extinguish, especially a disturbance or rebellion.  |例句：The police quickly quelled the riot.  警察迅速平息了暴乱。|例句：She tried to quell her rising anger. 她试图压制自己日益增长的怒火。|近义词：suppress, subdue, quell, stifle, extinguish |反义词：incite, provoke, instigate
