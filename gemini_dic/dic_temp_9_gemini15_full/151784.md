pass-off:/ˈpɑːs ɒf/|/ˈpæsoʊf/| vt. n.|冒充；转让；推脱|To represent something as being something else; to transfer ownership or responsibility; to evade responsibility.|例句：He tried to pass off his forgery as a genuine painting. 他试图将他的赝品冒充为真画。  They passed off the blame to their subordinates. 他们把责任推卸给下属。|近义词：misrepresent, palm off, shirk|反义词：own up, admit, accept responsibility|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He passes off his work as his own. = 他把自己做的工作冒充成自己的。
The company passes off defective products to customers. = 公司把有缺陷的产品卖给顾客。

一般过去时:
She passed off the antique as a modern replica. = 她把古董冒充成现代复制品。
They passed off the responsibility to the new team. = 他们把责任推给了新团队。

现在完成时:
He has passed off counterfeit goods for years. = 他多年来一直在销售假货。
The manager has passed off the difficult task to his assistant. = 经理已将这项困难的任务交给他的助手。

过去完成时:
They had passed off the blame before the investigation began. = 在调查开始之前，他们就已经推卸了责任。
She had passed off the fake jewelry as genuine before anyone noticed. = 在任何人注意到之前，她已经把假首饰冒充成真的了。

虚拟语气:
