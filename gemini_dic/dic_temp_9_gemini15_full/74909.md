Fresnel lenses are often used in lighthouses, projectors, and other applications where it is necessary to focus light over a large area. | Example: The lighthouse used a Fresnel lens to project its beam far out to sea.  灯塔使用菲涅耳透镜将光束投射到很远的海上。|近义词：lens (general term) |反义词：无
