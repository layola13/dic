eye-catching: /ˈaɪˌkætʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 引人注目的，醒目的 |  Attractive or noticeable; designed to attract attention.  | 例句：The shop window display was very eye-catching.  商店橱窗陈列非常引人注目。

The new car's design is eye-catching and modern. 这辆新车的造型新颖别致，非常吸引人。| 近义词：striking, striking, showy, arresting, conspicuous | 反义词：unremarkable, inconspicuous, dull, bland

WORD ORIGIN->
The word "eye-catching" is a compound word formed from "eye" and "catching."  "Catching" in this context means "able to attract or capture."  The combination signifies something that readily grabs the attention of the eye.
