declassifies:/dɪˈklæsɪfaɪz/ | vt. |解密；解除保密级别 | To remove the classification of (something secret or confidential), making it available to the public or a wider range of people.  | The government declassified the documents after 50 years.  政府在50年后解密了这些文件。|  release, unclassify | classify, secrete, conceal | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government declassifies sensitive information carefully. = 政府谨慎地解密敏感信息。
She declassifies documents related to the case. = 她解密与案件相关的文件。


一般过去时:
They declassified the files last year. = 他们去年解密了这些文件。
The agency declassified the report after a thorough review. = 该机构在彻底审查后解密了报告。


现在完成时:
