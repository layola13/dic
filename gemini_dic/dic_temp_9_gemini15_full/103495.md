jockeylike:/ˈdʒɒkiˌlaɪk/|/ˈdʒɑːkiˌlaɪk/| adj.|像骑师的；精明强干的；|Resembling a jockey; characterized by shrewdness and skill in maneuvering or manipulating.  The word evokes the image of a jockey's agility, quick thinking, and strategic maneuvering. |例句：He had a jockeylike ability to slip through the crowd unnoticed. 他有一种像骑师一样能够在人群中悄无声息地溜走的能力。

He executed the complex financial deal with a jockeylike precision. 他以骑师般精准的技巧完成了复杂的金融交易。|近义词：shrewd, skillful, cunning, agile, nimble|反义词：clumsy, awkward, inept, unskilled
