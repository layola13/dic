generation's: /ˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. |  一代人的；…的时代 |  The possessive form of "generation," indicating belonging to or characteristic of a particular generation.  It refers to a group of people born and living around the same time. |


例句：

My generation's values are different from my parents'. = 我这一代人的价值观与我父母那一代不同。

This museum showcases a generation's worth of artistic achievements. = 这个博物馆展示了一代人的艺术成就。


近义词：epoch's, era's


反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑用表示个体或非群体性的词语来对比，例如 individual's


词性：名词
