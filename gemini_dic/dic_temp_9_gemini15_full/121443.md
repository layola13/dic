misinterpret:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɜːrprɪt/ | vt. |误解，曲解 | To understand incorrectly; to give the wrong meaning to. | 例句：He misinterpreted my silence as agreement. 他误解了我的沉默，认为我同意了。

例句：The journalist misinterpreted the politician's statement, leading to a public outcry.  记者误解了这位政治家的声明，导致了公众的强烈抗议。


近义词：misunderstand, misconstrue, misjudge

反义词：understand, comprehend
