roleplay:/ˈroʊlˌpleɪ/ /ˈrəʊlˌpleɪ/ | n., v. |角色扮演；扮演角色；模拟表演 |

英文解析：Role-playing is the acting out of a particular role, often in a fictional setting.  It can be used for entertainment, education, therapy, or training. As a verb, it refers to the act of engaging in role-playing.

例句：

They enjoyed a roleplay where they were detectives solving a mystery. = 他们玩得很开心，角色扮演侦探破案。

The teacher used roleplay to help her students understand different perspectives. = 老师用角色扮演来帮助学生理解不同的观点。


近义词：acting, simulation, dramatization, improvisation

反义词：reality, factual representation


词性：名词和动词
