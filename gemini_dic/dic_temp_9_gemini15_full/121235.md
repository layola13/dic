miserable:/ˈmɪzərəbl/| adj.|痛苦的；悲惨的；令人难受的|Feeling or expressing great unhappiness or unhappiness.  The word implies a state of deep unhappiness, often prolonged and affecting one's overall well-being. |例句：He felt miserable after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后感到很痛苦。

例句：The weather was miserable, cold and rainy. 天气很糟糕，又冷又下雨。


近义词：unhappy, wretched, unhappy, dejected, downcast, despondent |反义词：happy, joyful, cheerful, delighted
