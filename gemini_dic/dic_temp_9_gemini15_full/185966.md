rewardingly:/rɪˈwɔːrdɪŋli/| adv.|令人欣慰地；值得的；有益的 | Giving a feeling of satisfaction or reward; worthwhile.  |例句：She found the work rewardingly challenging. 她发现这项工作很有挑战性，也很有成就感。

例句：The long journey was rewardingly beautiful. 漫长的旅程令人欣慰地美丽。


近义词：satisfyingly, profitably, fruitfully, beneficially

反义词：unrewardingly, unprofitably, frustratingly

词性：副词
