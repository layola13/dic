flay:/fleɪ/| vt.|剥皮；严厉批评；鞭打|to remove the skin from (an animal); to criticize severely; to whip|例句：The butcher flayed the rabbit before cooking it.屠夫在烹饪兔子之前剥了它的皮。  The critic flayed the author's new novel.评论家严厉批评了作者的新小说。|近义词：skin, scourge, castigate, lambaste|反义词：praise, commend, protect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flays the hide from the deer. = 他剥鹿皮。
She flays her opponents in the debate. = 她在大辩论中严厉批评她的对手。


一般过去时:
They flayed the animal for its hide. = 他们剥了动物的皮取皮毛。
The teacher flayed him for his bad behavior. = 老师因为他的不良行为而严厉批评了他。


现在完成时:
He has flayed many animals in his life. = 他一生剥过很多动物的皮。
The media has flayed the politician for his lies. = 媒体因他的谎言而严厉批评了这位政治家。


过去完成时:
They had flayed the ox before the ceremony. = 他们在仪式前已经剥了牛皮。
The manager had flayed the employee for his repeated mistakes. = 经理因为员工的反复错误而严厉批评了他。


将来时:
He will flay the sheep tomorrow. = 他明天将剥羊皮。
The newspaper will flay the company for its unethical practices. = 报纸将因其不道德的行为而严厉批评该公司。


虚拟语气:
If he flayed the skin carefully, it would be perfect. = 如果他小心地剥皮，它就会完美无缺。
I wish the critic hadn't flayed my work so harshly. = 我希望评论家不要如此严厉地批评我的作品。


被动语态:
The animal was flayed and its hide was used for clothing. = 动物被剥皮，其皮毛被用来做衣服。
His actions were flayed by the public. = 他的行为受到了公众的严厉批评。


疑问句:
Did they flay the fish before cooking it? = 他们在烹饪之前是否剥了鱼皮？
Will the journalist flay the government's new policy? = 记者会严厉批评政府的新政策吗？


否定句:
He did not flay the cat. = 他没有剥猫皮。
The review did not flay the film; instead, it praised it. = 这篇评论并没有严厉批评这部电影；相反，它对它赞扬有加。
