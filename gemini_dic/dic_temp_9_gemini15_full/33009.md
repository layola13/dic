chorizo:/tʃəˈriːzoʊ/| n. |辣香肠|A spicy, cured sausage, typically made from pork.  It originates from Spain and is characterized by its smoky, garlicky, and paprika-rich flavor.  |例句：The paella was delicious, especially with the addition of chorizo.  西班牙海鲜饭真美味，尤其是加了辣香肠之后。

例句：I bought some chorizo to make a Spanish-style omelet. 我买了些辣香肠来做西班牙式煎蛋卷。


近义词：sausage, salchichon (similar cured sausage)| 反义词：无| 词性：名词
