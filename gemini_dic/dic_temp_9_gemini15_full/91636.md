hyperboles:/haɪˈpɜːrbəliːz/ | n. |  夸张法；夸张的言辞 | Exaggerated statements or figures of speech.  Hyperboles are used to create emphasis or humor.  |  例句：His claims of being the best singer in the world are pure hyperbole. 他自称是世界上最好的歌手，纯粹是夸张。


例句：The author uses hyperbole to convey the overwhelming nature of the character's grief. 作者用夸张的手法来表达人物悲痛欲绝的感受。


近义词：exaggeration, overstatement


反义词：understatement, litotes


词性：n.
