excavational:/ˌɛksˌkæveɪʃənəl/| adj. |挖掘的；发掘的；考古学的|Relating to or involving excavation.  The term refers to activities, processes, or findings connected to digging up and uncovering something, typically buried or hidden. This often relates to archaeological digs but can also apply to other contexts like mining or construction.|例句：The excavational team uncovered ancient artifacts.  挖掘队发现了古代文物。

The excavational work revealed the city's ancient foundations. 发掘工作揭示了这座城市古老的地基。

近义词：archaeological, digging, exploratory

反义词：constructional, building, erecting

词性:形容词
