retrospect:/ˈrɛtrəʊspekt/| n.|回顾，追溯，回想|The act of looking back on or reviewing past events or situations.  It often implies considering the significance or consequences of those events.|例句：In retrospect, I should have studied harder for the exam.  回顾过去，我本应该为考试更努力学习。|例句：With the benefit of retrospect, it's clear that the decision was a mistake.  事后看来，很明显这个决定是错误的。|近义词：review, reflection, consideration|反义词：prospect, anticipation

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retrospectively considers his past actions. = 他回顾性地思考他过去的行动。
She retrospectively analyzes the data. = 她回顾性地分析数据。


一般过去时:
They retrospectively reviewed the project's progress. = 他们回顾性地审查了项目的进展。
He retrospectively examined his life choices. = 他回顾性地检查了他的人生选择。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I had thought more carefully before making that decision in retrospect. = 我希望我在回顾中做出那个决定之前考虑得更周到一些。


被动语态:

否定句:
