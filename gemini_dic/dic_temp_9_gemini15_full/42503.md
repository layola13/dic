counterinfluencing: /ˌkaʊntərɪnˈfluːənsɪŋ/ | v. | 反向影响，对抗性影响 | The act of exerting influence to counteract or oppose another influence.  It involves actively working against a prevailing trend, opinion, or force. | Example: The government's counterinfluencing campaign aimed to combat the spread of misinformation.  政府的反向影响运动旨在打击虚假信息的传播。| 近义词：counteracting, opposing, resisting | 反义词：reinforcing, supporting, promoting


USAGE->

They are counterinfluencing the negative social media campaign. = 他们在对抗负面的社交媒体宣传活动。
The company counterinfluences its competitors' marketing strategies. = 这家公司对抗其竞争对手的营销策略。
