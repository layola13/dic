malaya:/məˈleɪə/| n. |马来亚| Malaya refers to the geographical area comprising Peninsular Malaysia (West Malaysia).  Historically, it encompassed a larger region including parts of present-day Singapore and southern Thailand.  The term is often used in historical contexts. |例句：Malaya was a British colony until independence in 1957. 马来亚是英国殖民地，直到1957年独立。|近义词：Peninsular Malaysia (more precise modern term)|反义词：无|名词
