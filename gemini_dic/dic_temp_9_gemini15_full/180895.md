rehung: /ˌriːˈhʌŋ/ | v. |重新悬挂| To hang something again, especially after it has been taken down.  This word is the past participle of the verb "rehang."|


例句：

The paintings were rehung in the new gallery.  =  这些画在新画廊里重新悬挂了。

After the storm, we rehung all the pictures. =  暴风雨过后，我们重新挂上了所有的画。


近义词：re-hang, hang again


反义词：take down, remove


词性：动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They rehang the artwork every six months. = 他们每六个月重新悬挂一次艺术品。
He rehangs the curtains after cleaning them. = 他清洗窗帘后重新挂上。


一般过去时:
The museum rehung the exhibition last week. = 博物馆上周重新展出了展览。
She rehung the mirror after fixing the frame. = 她修理好相框后重新挂上了镜子。


现在完成时:
They have rehung the tapestry in the dining room. = 他们已经把挂毯重新挂在餐厅了。
We have rehung the lights since the power outage. = 停电后我们已经重新安装了灯。


过去完成时:
They had rehung the photos before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经重新挂好了照片。
She had rehung the curtains before the party started. = 派对开始前，她已经重新挂好了窗帘。


将来时:
They will rehang the paintings tomorrow. = 他们明天将重新悬挂这些画。
We will rehang the artwork after the renovation. = 装修后我们将重新悬挂艺术品。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rehang all the pictures. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新挂上所有的照片。
I wish I had rehung the curtains before the rain started. = 我希望在雨开始下之前我已经重新挂上窗帘了。
