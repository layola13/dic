photogrammetry: /ˌfoʊtoʊɡræˈmɛtri/ | n. | 摄影测量学 | The science and technology of obtaining reliable measurements from photographs.  It involves taking overlapping photographs of an object or area from different viewpoints, then using specialized software to process these images and create 3D models or accurate measurements. | Example: Photogrammetry is used to create detailed 3D models of archaeological sites.  摄影测量学被用于创建考古遗址的详细3D模型。  | 近义词：image analysis, 3D modeling | 反义词：N/A
