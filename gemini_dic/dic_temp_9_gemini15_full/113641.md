machine-gun: /məˈʃiːnɡʌn/ | n. | 机枪 | A fully automatic weapon that fires a rapid stream of bullets.  | 例句：The soldiers opened fire with their machine guns.  士兵们用机枪开火。 | 例句：The sound of the machine gun was deafening. 机枪的声音震耳欲聋。| 近义词：automatic weapon, rapid-fire gun | 反义词： (无直接反义词，可考虑 hand-held weapon as a contrast)


USAGE->

词源:
The word "machine-gun" is a compound word, combining "machine" (referring to its mechanical operation) and "gun" (referring to its function as a firearm).  The term arose with the development of automatic firearms in the late 19th and early 20th centuries.
