harpings:/ˈhɑːrpɪŋz/ | n. | 唠叨；喋喋不休；反复提及 | Repeated mention or discussion of a particular subject, often in a tiresome or annoying way.  It often implies a dwelling on something unpleasant or negative. |例句：His constant harpings on his misfortunes were wearing everyone out.  他不断地唠叨他的不幸，让每个人都筋疲力尽。

His harpings on the unfairness of the system fell on deaf ears. 他对制度不公正的喋喋不休无人理会。


近义词：complaining, nagging, whining, lamenting, dwelling on, reiterating |反义词：silence, ignoring, overlooking


词源:  Derived from the verb "harp," which originally referred to playing the harp.  The figurative meaning evolved to describe the repetitive and sometimes monotonous nature of playing a single note or tune, and then extended to repetitive speech.
