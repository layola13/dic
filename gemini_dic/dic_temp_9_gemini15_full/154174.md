periodicalist: /ˌpɪəriˈɒdɪkəlɪst/ | n. |期刊编辑；期刊撰稿人| A person who writes for or edits periodicals.  This term is relatively uncommon and often replaced by more specific titles such as "editor," "journalist," or "freelance writer" depending on the context.|The periodicalist submitted his article on time.|journalist, editor, writer|  (no direct antonym, but could be considered opposite to someone who avoids writing or publishing)|名词
