fireworks:/ˈfaɪərwɜːrks/| n.|烟火，焰火|Explosive devices used for display, typically producing bright flashes of light and loud bangs.|例句：The fireworks display was spectacular.  烟火表演非常壮观。|例句：We watched the fireworks from the hilltop. 我们从山顶观看烟火。|近义词：pyrotechnics|反义词：None
