defiled:/dɪˈfaɪld/| vt. adj.|玷污；污染；亵渎；败坏|To make something unclean or impure, especially in a way that is considered morally wrong.  It can refer to physical objects, places, or moral principles.|例句：The vandals defiled the ancient monument.  破坏者玷污了古迹。

The sacred temple was defiled by the invaders. 侵略者亵渎了神圣的庙宇。|近义词：pollute, contaminate, profane, corrupt, violate |反义词：purify, cleanse, sanctify
