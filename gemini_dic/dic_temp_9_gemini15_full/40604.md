convector:/kənˈvɛktər/| n. |对流器，热对流器|A device that uses convection to distribute heat.  Convectors are often used for heating rooms. They work by heating air, which then rises and circulates throughout the space.|例句：The convector in the bathroom kept the room warm and cozy. 浴室里的对流器使房间温暖舒适。|例句：We installed a new convector to improve the heating efficiency in our living room. 我们安装了一个新的对流器来提高客厅的采暖效率。|近义词：radiator, heater|反义词：None specifically|名词
