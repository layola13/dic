revolutionising: /ˌrevəˈluːʃənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 彻底改变；革新 |  The process of fundamentally changing something, often in a positive way. It implies a dramatic and transformative shift. | 例句：The internet is revolutionising the way we communicate.  互联网正在彻底改变我们的沟通方式。 | 例句：This new technology is revolutionising the healthcare industry. 这项新技术正在彻底改变医疗保健行业。 | 近义词：transforming, changing, reforming, modernising | 反义词：preserving, maintaining, stagnating | 动词


USAGE->
