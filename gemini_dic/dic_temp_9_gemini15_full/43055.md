cowleeching:/ˈkaʊˌliːtʃɪŋ/| n. |牛蛭疗法|The practice of using leeches (specifically medicinal leeches) on cattle to treat various ailments.  This was a historical practice and is not common today.  It involved applying leeches to specific areas of the cow's body to draw out blood, believed to alleviate inflammation or other conditions.|例句：The farmer used cowleeching as a traditional treatment for his cow's swollen udder. = 这位农民用牛蛭疗法治疗他奶牛肿胀的乳房。  The historical practice of cowleeching is now largely obsolete. = 牛蛭疗法这种历史疗法现在基本上已经过时了。|近义词：hirudotherapy (leech therapy), bloodletting|反义词：None specifically, but modern veterinary treatments would be considered opposites.|名词
