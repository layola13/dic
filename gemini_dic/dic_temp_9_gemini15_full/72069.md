flittern:/ˈflɪtərn/| v.|飞舞；闪烁；轻快地移动 |To move quickly and lightly; to flutter or shimmer.|The butterflies flittered among the flowers. 蝴蝶在花丛中飞舞。The candlelight flittered in the breeze. 烛光在微风中闪烁。|近义词：flutter, flicker, shimmer|反义词：remain still, stay put
