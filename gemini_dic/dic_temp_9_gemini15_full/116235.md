masquerades:/ˌmæskəˈreɪdz/| v. n.|化装舞会；伪装；假扮|To disguise or conceal one's true nature, identity, or feelings; a social gathering of people wearing masks and costumes.|例句：The thief masquerades as a policeman to steal the money.  小偷假扮警察偷钱。

例句：The party was a masquerade ball, and everyone wore elaborate costumes. 晚会是一个化装舞会，每个人都穿着精心制作的服装。


近义词：disguise, pretend, feign, camouflage|反义词：reveal, expose, unveil|动词
