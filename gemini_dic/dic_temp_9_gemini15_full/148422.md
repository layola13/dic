overstep:/ˌoʊvərˈstɛp/ | vt. |逾越；超越；越轨 | To go beyond the limits of (something), especially in a way that is unacceptable or causes problems.  |例句：He overstepped his authority by ordering the troops to fire. 他越权下令部队开火。 |例句：Don't overstep the boundaries of professional conduct. 不要越过职业行为的界限。|近义词：transgress, exceed, infringe, trespass |反义词：obey, conform, adhere
