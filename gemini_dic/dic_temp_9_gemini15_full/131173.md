nonbreeders:/ˈnɒnˈbriːdərz/ | n. |不繁殖者，未繁殖的动物|Animals or plants that do not breed or reproduce.  The term often refers to animals within a population that are capable of breeding but choose not to, or are prevented from doing so due to various factors. |Example:  The study focused on the proportion of nonbreeders within the penguin colony. 这项研究关注的是企鹅群体中不繁殖者的比例。The high percentage of nonbreeders in this year's population suggests environmental stress. 今年种群中高比例的不繁殖者暗示着环境压力。|近义词: non-reproducers, sterile individuals (in some contexts)|反义词: breeders, reproducers
