malingerer:/məˈlɪŋɡərər/ | n. |装病者；装病逃避责任的人 | A person who pretends to be ill in order to avoid work or duty.  |例句：He was accused of being a malingerer and shirking his responsibilities. 他被指控为装病逃避责任者。

例句：The manager suspected that some employees were malingerers, faking illnesses to avoid the demanding project.  经理怀疑一些员工是装病者，假装生病以逃避要求严格的项目。


近义词：faker, shirker, slacker, simulator


反义词：hard worker, diligent worker, conscientious worker


词性：名词
