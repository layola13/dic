pre-visit: /ˌpriːˈvɪzɪt/ | adj. | 事先探访的；访问前的 |  Describes something that happens or is done before a visit. It typically refers to preparations or actions taken in anticipation of a visit. | 例句：The pre-visit consultation helped alleviate the patient's anxieties. 事先的会诊帮助减轻了病人的焦虑。  The pre-visit checklist ensured we had everything we needed.  访问前的清单确保我们拥有所需的一切。| 近义词：antecedent, preliminary, prior | 反义词：post-visit, subsequent
