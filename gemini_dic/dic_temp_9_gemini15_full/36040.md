codle:/ˈkɒdl/ | v. | 溺爱；娇惯；慢炖；煨  | To treat someone with excessive care and attention, often to the point of spoiling them; to cook (food) slowly in simmering liquid. |例句：She coddled her son, never letting him face any difficulties. 她溺爱她的儿子，从不让孩子面对任何困难。  |例句：The chef coddled the vegetables in a rich broth. 厨师用浓郁的肉汤慢炖蔬菜。|近义词：pamper, spoil, simmer, stew |反义词：neglect, ignore, harsh


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He coddles his children. = 他溺爱他的孩子。
She coddles the baby. = 她娇惯着婴儿。


一般过去时:
My grandmother coddled me when I was sick. = 我生病时祖母很照顾我。
They coddled the chicken in a creamy sauce.  = 他们用奶油酱慢炖鸡肉。


现在完成时:
She has coddled him all his life. = 她一生都在溺爱他。
I have coddled this stew for hours. = 我已经炖这个炖菜好几个小时了。


过去完成时:
She had coddled him since he was a child. = 她从小就溺爱他。
They had coddled the vegetables until they were tender. = 他们把蔬菜炖得很软烂。


将来时:
I will coddle the soup until it thickens. = 我会把汤炖到浓稠为止。
She will coddle her grandson excessively. = 她会过度溺爱她的孙子。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't coddle him so much. = 如果我是你，我不会这么溺爱他。
I wish I hadn't coddled him so much when he was younger. = 我真希望我年轻的时候没有那么溺爱他。


被动语态:
The child was coddled by his parents. = 这个孩子被他的父母娇惯着。
The vegetables are being coddled in a slow cooker. = 蔬菜正在慢炖锅里慢炖。


疑问句:
Do you coddle your children? = 你溺爱你的孩子吗？
Are you coddling the meat? = 你在炖肉吗？


否定句:
They don't coddle their children. = 他们不溺爱他们的孩子。
I don't intend to coddle the situation. = 我不打算姑息养奸。

coddle in = 沉浸在...中
coddle down = 安静下来; 慢慢入睡
