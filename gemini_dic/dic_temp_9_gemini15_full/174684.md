quot:/kwəʊt/|pron.|引号，报价|quotation marks; a set of symbols used to indicate a direct quotation.  |例句：He put the title in quotes. 他把标题放在引号里。|例句：The quote was inaccurate.  这个引语不准确。|近义词：quotation marks, inverted commas|反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The article uses many quotes from the interview. = 这篇文章使用了许多来自采访的引语。
He often uses quotes in his speeches. = 他经常在演讲中使用引语。

一般过去时:
She quoted the poem in her essay. = 她在论文中引用了这首诗。
The lawyer quoted the relevant law. = 律师引用了相关的法律。

现在完成时:
I have quoted this passage many times. = 我已经多次引用过这段话。
They have quoted several experts on the subject. = 他们已经引用了几位该领域的专家。

过去完成时:
He had quoted the same source in his previous book. = 他在他的上一本书中引用过相同的来源。
She had quoted Shakespeare extensively in her thesis. = 她在她的论文中广泛引用了莎士比亚。

将来时:
I will quote him directly in my report. = 我将在我的报告中直接引用他。
She will quote statistics to support her argument. = 她将引用统计数据来支持她的论点。

虚拟语气:
If I quoted that statistic, people would doubt my credibility. = 如果我引用那个统计数据，人们会怀疑我的可信度。
I wish I had quoted the correct source. = 我希望我引用了正确的来源。

被动语态:
The passage was quoted extensively in the media. = 这段话被媒体广泛引用。
His words were quoted out of context. = 他的话被断章取义。

疑问句:
What did he quote in his presentation? = 他在他的演示文稿中引用了什么？
Can you quote the exact words? = 你能引用确切的话吗？

否定句:
The article did not quote any sources. = 这篇文章没有引用任何来源。
I don't recall quoting that statement. = 我不记得引用过那句话。


PHRASE->
quote unquote = 原话照录
to quote somebody = 引用某人说的话
to quote something = 引用某事物
a direct quote = 直接引语
out of quote = 结束引用
