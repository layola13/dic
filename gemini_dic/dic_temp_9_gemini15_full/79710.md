glucose:/ˈɡluːkoʊs/ | n. |葡萄糖 |A simple sugar that is an important energy source in living organisms; it is a component of many carbohydrates.  Glucose is metabolized in the body to produce energy. |Example:  The body breaks down glucose to provide energy for cells.  身体分解葡萄糖为细胞提供能量。|近义词：dextrose, grape sugar |反义词：N/A
