patois:/ˈpætˌwɑː/|/ˈpætwɑː/| n. |方言，土语|A dialect or language, especially a regional one with low prestige.  It often refers to a nonstandard form of speech, particularly one considered to be informal or uneducated.  The term can be controversial due to its potential for implying inferiority.|例句：The locals spoke a patois I couldn't understand. 当地人说一种我听不懂的方言。|例句：His patois was a mixture of French and Creole. 他的方言是法语和克里奥尔语的混合体。|近义词：dialect, vernacular, jargon, slang|反义词：standard language, formal language|名词
