judgeships:/ˈdʒʌdʒʃɪps/ | n. |法官职位；裁判职位 |The position or office of a judge.  It refers to the role and authority of a judge, often within a specific court or jurisdiction.  |例句：The judgeships are highly sought after positions.  法官职位是极受欢迎的职位。

例句：The competition for the judgeships was fierce.  竞争法官职位非常激烈。


近义词：judgeship (singular form), judicial office, judicial post


反义词：无直接反义词，但可以考虑与“lack of judicial authority”或“absence of a judge”之类的短语形成对比。


词性: 名词
