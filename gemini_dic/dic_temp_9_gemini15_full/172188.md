purvey:/pərˈveɪ/| vt.|供应，提供；兜售；传播(尤指不好的东西)|to supply or provide something, especially goods or services; to sell (especially illicit goods); to spread or promote (something undesirable) |例句：The company purveys high-quality goods to its customers. 这家公司向顾客提供优质商品。

例句：The tabloid purveyed scandalous rumors about the celebrity. 这家小报散布了关于这位名人的丑闻传闻。


近义词：supply, provide, vend, peddle, disseminate, propagate|反义词： withhold, suppress


词性:动词
