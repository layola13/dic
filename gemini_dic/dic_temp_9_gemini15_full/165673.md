premonopolize: /ˌpriːməˈnɒpəlʌɪz/ | v. | 预先垄断；提前垄断 | To gain control of (a market, industry, etc.) before others can, often through unfair or unethical means.  | Example:  The large corporation attempted to premonopolize the emerging market for renewable energy technologies.  大型公司试图预先垄断新兴的可再生能源技术市场。


近义词: corner the market, dominate, control, preempt


反义词:  to allow fair competition, to democratize


词性: 动词
