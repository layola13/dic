internal: /ɪnˈtɜːrnəl/ | adj. | 内部的；国内的；内在的 | Relating to or situated on the inside of something; inner.  |例句：The company's internal communications system was down. 公司的内部通讯系统瘫痪了。|例句：He suffered internal injuries in the accident. 他在事故中受了内伤。|近义词：inner, interior, inward |反义词：external, outer, exterior
