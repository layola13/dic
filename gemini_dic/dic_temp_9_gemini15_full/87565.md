heteropathy:/ˌhɛtərˈɒpəθi/| n.|异种疗法，异病疗法|A system of medical treatment that uses remedies that produce symptoms different from those of the disease being treated.| 例句：Homeopathy is a type of heteropathy, using substances that would cause the symptoms in a healthy person to treat those symptoms in a sick person.  顺势疗法是一种异种疗法，它使用会引起健康人出现症状的物质来治疗病人身上的这些症状。|近义词：allopathy (in contrast)|反义词：homeopathy
