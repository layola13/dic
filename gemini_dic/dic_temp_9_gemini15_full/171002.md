pseudosatirical: /ˌsjuːdoʊˈsætərɪkəl/ | adj. |似讽刺的，伪讽刺的 | Apparently satirical but lacking genuine satirical intent or effect; superficially resembling satire.  It often uses the techniques of satire—irony, sarcasm, exaggeration—but without the underlying purpose of social or political critique. | 例句：His humor, while employing satirical devices, felt ultimately pseudosatirical, lacking the biting edge of true satire. 他的幽默虽然运用了讽刺手法，但最终感觉是伪讽刺，缺乏真正讽刺的尖锐。


例句：The comedian's routine, though peppered with irony, came across as pseudosatirical rather than genuinely insightful social commentary.  这位喜剧演员的表演虽然充满了讽刺意味，但却更像是伪讽刺而不是真正有见地的社会评论。


近义词：mock-satirical, superficially satirical, ironic (in a weak sense), facetious


反义词：satirical, genuinely critical, incisive


词性：形容词
