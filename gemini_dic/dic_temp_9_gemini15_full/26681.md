cadaverine:/kəˈdævərɪn/| n. |尸胺|A foul-smelling diamine, (1,5-pentanediamine),  produced by the putrefaction of animal tissues and having a particularly strong odor.  It is toxic.|例句：The cadaverine smell emanating from the decaying carcass was overwhelming.  腐烂的尸体散发出的尸胺气味令人难以忍受。|近义词：putrescine|反义词:None specifically.
