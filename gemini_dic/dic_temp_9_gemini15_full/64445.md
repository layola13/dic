/ɛˈrɪdənəs/ | n. |  埃里丹努斯座（星座名） | Eridanus is a large constellation in the southern celestial hemisphere. It is named after the river Eridanus in Greek mythology.  The constellation is long and winding, resembling a river.  | The constellation Eridanus is visible in the winter sky. = 埃里丹努斯星座在冬季的天空中可见。  |  Aquarius, Cetus |  (无明显反义词) | 名词
