nickers:/ˈnɪkərz/| n.|马裤，灯笼裤|Underpants, especially those worn by men, women, or children.  They are typically loose-fitting and comfortable.  The term is more common in British English.|例句：He wore his nickers under his trousers. 他在长裤里面穿着马裤。|例句：The child's nickers were stained with mud.  孩子的马裤沾满了泥巴。|近义词：pants, trousers, breeches (depending on context)|反义词：None directly applicable.  The antonym would depend on what "nickers" are being contrasted with (e.g., shorts, a dress).|名词
