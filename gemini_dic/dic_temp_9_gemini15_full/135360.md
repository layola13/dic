nonperceptively:/ˌnɒnpəˈsɛptɪvli/| adv. |非感知地，无感知地 |Without being perceived; insensibly.  It describes an action or process that occurs without being noticed or detected by the senses. |Example: The disease progressed nonperceptively, with no noticeable symptoms in its early stages.  疾病发展得不易察觉，早期没有明显的症状。|近义词：imperceptibly, insensibly, subtly |反义词：perceptibly, noticeably, obviously|副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  The usage is straightforward; it modifies a verb to indicate the action happens without being perceived.


Example Sentences:

The change happened nonperceptively.  变化发生得很不易察觉。

The poison acted nonperceptively, leaving no immediate symptoms. 毒药作用不易察觉，没有立即出现症状。


PHRASE->

There are no common phrases directly using "nonperceptively".  Its usage is typically within a sentence describing an action.
