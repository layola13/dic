hootingly: /ˈhuːtɪŋli/ | adv. |  喧闹地；吵闹地；嘲弄地 |  In a hooting manner; noisily and derisively.  It suggests a sound like an owl's hoot, often implying mockery or derision. |


例句：

He laughed hootingly at my suggestion. = 他嘲弄地大笑我的建议。

The crowd hooted and jeered hootingly at the unpopular politician. = 人群喧闹地嘲笑那个不受欢迎的政治家。


近义词：derisively, mockingly, scornfully, jeeringly

反义词：respectfully, admiringly, approvingly


词性：副词
