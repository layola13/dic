pleadings:/ˈpliːdɪŋz/| n. |诉状，辩诉状|Written statements filed with a court that explain a party's claims or defenses in a lawsuit.  They set forth the factual and legal basis for the case. |Example: The judge reviewed the pleadings before making a ruling.  法官在作出裁决之前审查了诉状。|近义词：documents, submissions, briefs|反义词：N/A
