gallingness:/ˈɡɔːlɪŋnəs/| n. |令人恼火；令人苦恼；令人不快|The gallingness of his constant interruptions was unbearable.  他不断打断人的行为令人难以忍受。|近义词：annoyance, irritation, exasperation |反义词：pleasure, delight


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations like "is," "was," "will be," etc.  It describes a state of being.  Therefore, tense changes are not applicable.


例句：

The gallingness of the situation was almost too much to bear.  情况的令人恼火之处几乎让人难以忍受。


The gallingness of her constant criticism wore him down. 她不断的批评让他精疲力尽。
