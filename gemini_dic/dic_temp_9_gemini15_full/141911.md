/ˈɔːrdn/| n.|顺序，等级，命令，圣秩|The natural or proper order of things.  The arrangement of things in a particular order.|例句：The books were arranged in alphabetical order.  书按字母顺序排列。|例句：He gave the order to attack. 他下令进攻。|近义词：sequence, series, rank, command|反义词：chaos, disorder, randomness|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The planets orbit in a specific order. = 行星按照特定的顺序运行。
What is the order of operations in mathematics? = 数学中的运算顺序是什么？


一般过去时:
The soldiers followed the orders precisely. = 士兵精确地执行了命令。
She received an order for 1000 units. = 她收到了一张1000个单位的订单。


现在完成时:
They have placed the order for the new equipment. = 他们已经下了新设备的订单。
We have rearranged the furniture in a new order. = 我们已经按照新的顺序重新布置了家具。


过去完成时:
He had already given the order before the problem arose. = 在问题出现之前，他已经下达了命令。
The documents had been filed in chronological order. = 文件已经按时间顺序存档。


将来时:
I will give the order to proceed at dawn. = 我将在黎明时分下达前进的命令。
She will arrange the flowers in a beautiful order. = 她将把花排列成美丽的图案。


虚拟语气:
If I were in charge, I would issue a different order. = 如果我负责，我会下达不同的命令。
I wish I had known the order of events earlier. = 我真希望早点知道事情的顺序。


被动语态:
The troops were given strict orders. = 部队收到了严格的命令。
The items were arranged in ascending order. = 项目按升序排列。


疑问句:
What is the correct order of events? = 事件的正确顺序是什么？
In what order should I complete these tasks? = 我应该按什么顺序完成这些任务？


否定句:
He did not follow the order. = 他没有遵守命令。
The files were not in alphabetical order.  文件不是按字母顺序排列的。



PHRASE->
in order to = 为了
out of order = 坏了的；混乱的
in order = 井然有序地
put in order = 整理；排列
word of order = 命令；指示
