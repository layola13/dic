polyatomic:/ˌpɒliəˈtɒmɪk/ /ˌpɑːliəˈtɑːmɪk/| adj.|多原子的|Consisting of more than one atom; specifically, of more than two atoms.  A polyatomic ion is a charged chemical species composed of two or more atoms covalently bonded or of a metal complex that can be considered as a single unit.  | Example: Water (H₂O) is a polyatomic molecule.  The hydroxide ion (OH⁻) is a polyatomic ion.|近义词：multiatomic|反义词：monatomic, diatomic
