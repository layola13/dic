preconcealing: /ˌpriːkənˈsiːlɪŋ/ | v. |预先隐藏；预先掩盖 |  The act of hiding or concealing something beforehand.  It implies a deliberate act of preventing something from being known or seen in advance. |例句：The government was accused of preconcealing evidence crucial to the investigation. 政府被指控预先隐瞒对调查至关重要的证据。

The company preconcealed its financial difficulties from investors.  这家公司向投资者隐瞒了其财务困难。| 近义词：concealing, masking, hiding, suppressing | 反义词：revealing, disclosing, exposing |  词性: 动词
