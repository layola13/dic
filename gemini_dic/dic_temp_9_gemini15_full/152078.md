paternalists: /pəˈtɜːrnəlɪsts/ | n. |家长式统治者；家长作风的人|People who believe in or practice paternalism.  They often make decisions for others, believing they know what is best, even if it restricts the autonomy of those they govern or care for. |例句：The paternalists on the board ignored the employees' concerns about working conditions.  (董事会中的家长式统治者们无视员工对工作条件的担忧。)  The company's paternalistic policies stifled innovation and creativity among its employees. (公司的家长式政策扼杀了员工的创新和创造力。) |近义词：authoritarians, custodians, guardians |反义词：liberals, individualists
