indamage:/ɪnˈdæmɪdʒ/ | adj. | 受损的；损坏的 | In a state of damage; impaired or harmed.  | The car was in damage after the accident.  这辆车在事故后受损了。 The reputation of the company was in damage after the scandal. 这家公司的声誉在丑闻后受损了。 | damaged, impaired, spoiled | undamaged, intact, perfect | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine is in damage. = 机器受损了。
The building is in damage after the fire. = 大火之后，建筑物受损了。

一般过去时:
The vase was in damage after it fell. = 花瓶掉落后受损了。

现在完成时:
The bridge has been in damage since the earthquake. = 自地震以来，桥梁一直受损。

过去完成时:

The painting had been in damage for years before it was restored. = 这幅画在修复之前已经损坏多年了。
