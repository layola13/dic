monument:/ˈmɑːnjumənt/ /ˈmɒnjumənt/| n. |纪念碑，纪念物|A monument is a structure or object built to commemorate a person, event, or idea.  It serves as a lasting reminder and often holds historical or cultural significance.  Examples include statues, buildings, and even natural formations. | The Lincoln Memorial is a magnificent monument to the 16th U.S. President.  林肯纪念堂是为纪念美国第16任总统而建的一座宏伟纪念碑。

The Washington Monument stands as a testament to American ingenuity. 华盛顿纪念碑是美国独创精神的象征。|memorial, landmark, memorial,  | insignificance (反义词较为抽象，与其作用相反)|名词
