outmarch:/ˌaʊtˈmɑːrtʃ/| v.|超过；强于；胜过|To surpass or exceed in marching or in any activity or quality analogous to marching; to excel.|The seasoned soldiers outmarched the rookies by several miles.|近义词：outshine, outstrip, surpass |反义词：underperform, lag behind


USAGE->
