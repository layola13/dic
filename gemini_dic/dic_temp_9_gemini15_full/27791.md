canning:/ˈkænɪŋ/| n. vt.|罐头食品；罐装；腌制|The process of preserving food in airtight cans or jars.|例句：Home canning is a great way to preserve summer's bounty. 家庭罐装是保存夏季丰收的好方法。|例句：The factory produces canned goods for supermarkets. 这家工厂生产超市销售的罐头食品。|近义词：preserving, bottling, pickling|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She cans peaches every summer. = 她每年夏天都做桃子罐头。
They can tomatoes for the winter. = 他们为冬天腌制西红柿。

一般过去时:
My grandmother canned fruits and vegetables. = 我祖母腌制水果和蔬菜。
He canned his own salmon. = 他自己腌制鲑鱼。

I have canned several jars of jam this year. = 今年我已经腌制了几罐果酱。
She has canned all the tomatoes from her garden. = 她已经把花园里所有的西红柿都腌制了。
