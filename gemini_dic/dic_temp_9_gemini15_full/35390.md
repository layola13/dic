co-operationists:/koʊˌɒpəˈreɪʃənɪsts/| n. |合作主义者，合作论者|Those who believe in and advocate for cooperation, often in the context of international relations or economics. They emphasize collaboration and mutual benefit over competition and conflict.  |The co-operationists argued for a peaceful resolution to the conflict, emphasizing the mutual benefits of trade and diplomacy. 合作主义者主张通过和平手段解决冲突，强调贸易和外交的互惠互利。|collaborators, cooperators|competitors, individualists|名词
