nonpetroleum:/ˌnɒnˈpɛtroʊliəm/| adj.|非石油的，不含石油的|Not derived from or related to petroleum.  This adjective describes substances, processes, or industries that do not utilize or originate from petroleum.| Example:  The company is investing heavily in nonpetroleum energy sources. 这家公司正在大力投资非石油能源。|近义词：petroleum-free, non-oil|反义词：petroleum-based, oil-based
