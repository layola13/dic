gratification:/ˌɡrætɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |满足；喜悦；酬谢 |The feeling of satisfaction or pleasure one gets from doing or achieving something.|He felt a deep sense of gratification at completing the project. 他完成项目后感到非常满足。|近义词：satisfaction, pleasure, delight |反义词：disappointment, dissatisfaction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The job provides a sense of gratification. = 这份工作带来满足感。
My biggest gratification comes from helping others. = 我最大的满足感来自帮助他人。


一般过去时:
She experienced great gratification after winning the competition. = 赢得比赛后，她感到非常满足。
His gratification at his son's success was evident. = 他对儿子成功的喜悦溢于言表。
