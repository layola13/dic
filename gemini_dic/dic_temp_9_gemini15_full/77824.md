generations:/ˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənz/| n. |几代人，世代 |A generation is all of the people born and living at about the same time, regarded collectively.  The term often refers to a period of roughly 20-30 years.  The word can also refer to the average length of time between the birth of parents and the birth of their children. |例句：Three generations of my family lived in this house. 我家的三代人都住在这所房子里。|例句：The younger generation is more tech-savvy than the older generation. 年轻一代比老一代更精通科技。|近义词：successions, ages, eras |反义词：N/A
