hitherward:/ˈhɪðərwərd/| adv.|向此地，到这里|Moving or directed toward this place.  It implies movement towards the speaker's location.|例句：The travelers journeyed hitherward, seeking shelter from the storm. 旅人们向这里赶来，寻求躲避暴风雨的庇护。

例句：The birds flew hitherward, drawn by the abundance of food. 鸟儿飞向这里，被丰富的食物吸引。


近义词：hereward, toward here, this way|反义词：thitherward, away from here, yonder


词性:副词
