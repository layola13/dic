overrepresentativeness:/ˌoʊvərˌreprɛzɛnˈteɪtɪvnɪs/ | n. |过度代表性，样本偏差 |The tendency to overestimate the likelihood of events that are easily recalled, often because they are vivid or recent.  This can lead to inaccurate judgments and decisions.  For example, if someone reads several news stories about shark attacks, they might overestimate the probability of a shark attack happening to them, even though statistically it's extremely rare.  |The media's overrepresentation of crime in certain neighborhoods creates a skewed perception of reality. | underrepresentation |  N/A
