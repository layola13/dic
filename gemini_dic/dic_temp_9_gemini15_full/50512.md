deprived:/dɪˈpraɪvd/| adj. | 贫困的；缺乏的；被剥夺的 | lacking something, especially something essential or necessary;  deprived of something important.  |例句：Many children in the slums are deprived of proper education. 贫民窟的许多孩子都缺乏适当的教育。

The family lived in deprived conditions, lacking basic necessities like food and shelter.  这个家庭生活在贫困的条件下，缺乏食物和住所等基本必需品。


近义词：impoverished, disadvantaged, underprivileged, destitute | 反义词：privileged, affluent, well-off


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is deprived of his freedom. = 他被剥夺了自由。
The children are deprived of parental care. = 孩子们缺乏父母的照顾。

一般过去时:
They were deprived of their rights. = 他们被剥夺了权利。
The prisoners were deprived of all contact with the outside world. = 囚犯们被剥夺了与外界的任何联系。

现在完成时:
She has been deprived of her inheritance. = 她被剥夺了继承权。
The drought has deprived the farmers of their crops. = 干旱使农民们失去了庄稼。

过去完成时:
He had been deprived of sleep for days. = 他几天没睡好觉了。
The refugees had been deprived of food and water for weeks. = 难民们已经几周没有食物和水了。

将来时:
They will be deprived of their jobs if the factory closes. = 如果工厂倒闭，他们将失去工作。
The government may deprive them of their citizenship. = 政府可能会剥夺他们的公民身份。

虚拟语气:
If he were deprived of his sight, he would be devastated. = 如果他失明了，他会非常沮丧。
I wish I hadn't been deprived of the opportunity. = 我真希望我没有失去这个机会。

被动语态:
He was deprived of his property. = 他的财产被没收了。
The rights of the accused were deprived. = 被告的权利被剥夺了。

疑问句:
Was he deprived of his inheritance? = 他被剥夺了继承权吗？
Are these children deprived of education? = 这些孩子缺乏教育吗？

否定句:
He was not deprived of his basic needs. = 他并没有缺乏基本需求。
The project was not deprived of funding. = 这个项目并没有缺乏资金。

词源:
The word "deprived" comes from the Old French word "depriver," meaning "to take away from,"  which itself has Latin roots in "privare," meaning "to deprive," "rob," or "take away."  The prefix "de-" indicates removal or separation.
