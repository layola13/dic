ill:/ɪl/ | adj. | 病的；坏的；邪恶的 |  Ill refers to being unwell or sick, or something bad or negative. It can also describe something morally wrong or wicked. |  He's been ill all week. 他整个星期都病了。  The effects of the storm were ill. 暴风雨的影响很糟糕。 | sick, unwell, bad, evil | healthy, well, good, virtuous | 形容词
