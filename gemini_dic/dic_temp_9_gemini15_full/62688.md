USAGE->
一般现在时:
The actions engender positive changes. = 这些行动带来积极的改变。
Her words engender hope in others. = 她的言语给他人带来希望。

一般过去时:
The war engendered widespread suffering. = 那场战争带来了普遍的苦难。
His speech engendered a sense of unity. = 他的演讲营造了一种团结感。

现在完成时:
The reforms have engendered significant progress. = 改革带来了显著的进步。
Their efforts have engendered a new era of cooperation. = 他们的努力带来了合作的新时代。

过去完成时:
The initial conflict had engendered deep mistrust between the two groups. = 最初的冲突已经在两组之间造成了深深的不信任。
Years of neglect had engendered a sense of hopelessness. = 多年的忽视已经产生了一种绝望感。

将来时:
The new policy will engender economic growth. = 新政策将带来经济增长。
The upcoming election will engender much debate. = 即将到来的选举将会引发大量的辩论。

虚拟语气:
If the project were successful, it would engender many benefits. = 如果项目成功，它将带来许多好处。
I wish the new regulations would engender more fairness. = 我希望新规定能带来更多公平。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
