hornpout:/ˈhɔːrnˌpaʊt/| n. |角须鱼，胡子鲶|A type of freshwater fish with barbels resembling horns.  Hornpouts are bottom-dwelling fish known for their ability to survive in low-oxygen environments. They are often found in muddy or silty areas of lakes and rivers. |例句：The fisherman caught a large hornpout near the riverbank. = 渔夫在河岸边钓到一条大角须鱼。

例句：We observed several hornpouts hiding under rocks in the stream. = 我们观察到几条角须鱼躲在溪流里的石头下面。
