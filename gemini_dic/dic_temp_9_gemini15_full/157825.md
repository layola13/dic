plagiarism:/ˈpleɪdʒərɪzəm/| n. |剽窃；抄袭 |The act of using another person's words or ideas without giving them credit.  It is a serious academic offense and can have significant consequences.  |Example:  The professor accused the student of plagiarism after finding significant similarities between his essay and a published article. = 教授发现学生的论文与一篇已发表的文章有很大的相似之处，指控其剽窃。

The student's paper was found to be a blatant case of plagiarism. = 学生的论文被发现是公然的剽窃行为。|近义词：theft, copying, appropriation |反义词：originality, innovation |名词
