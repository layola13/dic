practical jokes:/ˈpræktɪkl ʒoʊks/| n.|恶作剧|A practical joke is a trick played on someone, often involving harmless deception or surprise, intended to be funny rather than malicious.|例句：He played a practical joke on his friend by replacing the sugar with salt. 他用盐代替糖恶作剧地捉弄了他的朋友。|近义词：prank, trick, jape|反义词：serious act, malicious act
