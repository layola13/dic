provostry:/ˈprɒvəstri/ | n. |教长职务；教长管辖区；教长住所|The office, jurisdiction, or residence of a provost, especially in a university or college.  It often refers to the administrative functions and responsibilities of a provost.|Example: The provostry was responsible for overseeing the university's academic affairs. 教长办公室负责监督大学的学术事务。|近义词：provostship|反义词：N/A
