caisson:/ˈkeɪsən/| n.|沉箱；气密仓；围堰|A caisson is a watertight structure used in various engineering projects, such as bridge building or underwater construction. It provides a dry working environment below the waterline.  |例句：The workers used a caisson to build the bridge's foundations under the river. 工人们用沉箱在河下建造桥梁的地基。

例句：Divers entered the caisson to repair the underwater pipeline. 潜水员进入气密仓修理水下管道。|近义词：cofferdam, coffer, airtight chamber |反义词：open structure

词源:
