elaboratenesses:/ɪˌlæbəˈreɪtəsnəsiz/ | n. | 精细之处；复杂性；详尽之处 | The elaboratenesses of the design were breathtaking.  设计的精细之处令人叹为观止。|近义词：intricacies, complexities, details |反义词：simplicities, plainness


词源：elaborate (v.) + -ness (suffix)  elaborate 源自拉丁语 elaborare，由ex- (out) + laborare (to work) 组成，意为“to work out, develop fully”。  -ness是名词后缀，表示状态或性质。


英文解析：Elaborateness is a noun form indicating the quality of being elaborate, meaning involving many details, complex, or carefully planned and executed.  The plural, elaboratenesses, emphasizes multiple instances or aspects of this quality.  It highlights the numerous intricate details or complexities present in something.


例句：

The elaboratenesses of the cathedral's architecture were truly awe-inspiring. 大教堂建筑的精细之处真是令人敬畏。

The artist's painting showcased the elaboratenesses of her brushstrokes and color blending. 艺术家的绘画展示了她笔触和色彩融合的精细之处。


用法说明：

elaboratenesses 主要用于书面语，用来描述事物中大量的细节或复杂性，强调其精细和复杂程度。  它比简单的"details"或"complexity"更强调精致和细致的程度。

由于其较为正式和书面化的性质，在日常口语中使用较少。
