derogatory:/dɪˈrɒɡətəri/| adj. |贬低的；轻蔑的；毁谤的 | Expressing a low opinion of someone or something; belittling.  |例句：His derogatory remarks about her intelligence were unacceptable. 他对她智力的贬低性言论是不可接受的。
例句：The article contained several derogatory comments about the politician's character. 这篇文章包含了几条关于这位政治家品格的贬损言论。


近义词：disparaging, belittling, insulting, contemptuous, pejorative
反义词：complimentary, flattering, praising, respectful


词源:
From Late Latin *derogatorius, from Latin derogare "to take away, detract from," from de- "from, away" + rogare "to ask, propose."  The sense development is from "taking away from" to "lessening the value or importance of."
