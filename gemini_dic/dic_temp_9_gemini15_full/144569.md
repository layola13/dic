outwanders:/ˌaʊtˈwɒndərz/| v.| 漂泊；漫游；流浪|To wander or travel far from home; to roam.|例句：The nomads outwandered across the vast desert.  游牧民族在广阔的沙漠中四处游荡。|例句：He outwandered for years, searching for his lost love. 他流浪多年，寻找他失去的爱人。|近义词：roam, wander, stray, ramble|反义词：settle, stay, remain
