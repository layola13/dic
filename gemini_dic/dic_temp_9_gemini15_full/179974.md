refiguring:/ˌriːˈfɪɡjərɪŋ/ | v. |重新计算；重新配置；重新考虑 |  To calculate or arrange something again; to rethink or reconsider something.  The process of recalculating, rearranging, or reconsidering. | 例句：We're refiguring the budget to accommodate unexpected expenses. 我们正在重新计算预算以适应意外开支。

例句：The architect is refiguring the building's layout to improve natural light. 建筑师正在重新规划建筑物的布局以改善自然采光。


近义词：recalculating, rearranging, reconsidering, re-evaluating, rethinking


反义词：finalizing, confirming, solidifying


词性：动词
