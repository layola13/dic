ignescent: /ɪɡˈnɛsnt/ | adj. | 发光的；发火的；燃烧的 |  Producing or tending to produce fire or light; glowing.  The word suggests a potential for fire or light, not necessarily an active flame. |


例句：

The ignescent embers glowed in the darkness.  =  暗淡的余烬在黑暗中发光。

The ignescent coals were carefully tended. =  人们小心地照看着发红的煤块。


近义词：glowing, incandescent, luminous, fiery

反义词：extinguished, dark, dull


词源:

From Latin *ignescere (to become fiery), from ignis (fire).  The suffix "-escent" indicates a process of becoming or tending towards a state.
