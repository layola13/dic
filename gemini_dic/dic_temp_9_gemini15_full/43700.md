crediting:/ˈkrɛdɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 归功于；记入贷方；认可 | To give credit to someone or something; to attribute something to someone or something; to enter as a credit in an account. | 例句：He was crediting his success to his hard work. 他把他的成功归功于他的努力工作。

The company credited the payment to his account. 公司把付款记入他的账户。


近义词：attributing,ascribing, acknowledging | 反义词：debiting, blaming


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are crediting the artist with a new style. = 他们将这种新的风格归功于这位艺术家。
The bank credits interest to my account monthly. = 银行每月将利息记入我的账户。

一般过去时:
She credited her success to her supportive family. = 她把成功归功于她支持她的家人。
The accountant credited the wrong account. = 会计记错了账户。

现在完成时:
We have credited your account with the correct amount. = 我们已经将正确的金额记入您的账户。
They have credited the discovery to a team of researchers. = 他们已将这项发现归功于一个研究团队。

过去完成时:
The historian had credited the invention to the wrong person. = 历史学家曾将这项发明归功于错误的人。
By the end of the year, they had credited all the payments. = 到年底，他们已经记入了所有的付款。

将来时:
They will credit the payment to your account within 24 hours. = 他们将在24小时内将付款记入您的帐户。
The professor will credit the students for their hard work. = 教授将称赞学生们的辛勤工作。

虚拟语气:
If they credited me with the idea, I would be very happy. = 如果他们把这个想法归功于我，我会非常高兴。
I wish they credited the team for their efforts. = 我希望他们能肯定团队的努力。

被动语态:
The payment was credited to the wrong account. = 付款记入了错误的账户。
His efforts were credited with saving the company. = 他的努力被认为挽救了公司。

疑问句:
Is the payment being credited to the correct account? = 付款是否记入了正确的账户？
To whom will the discovery be credited? = 这项发现将归功于谁？

否定句:
They did not credit him with any originality. = 他们没有认为他有原创性。
The bank did not credit my account with the interest. = 银行没有将利息记入我的账户。


PHRASE->
credit with = 认为…具有；把…归功于
credit to = 归功于
to one's credit = 值得赞扬的是
give credit where credit is due = 论功行赏
take credit for = 邀功
