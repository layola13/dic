menthene:/ˈmɛnθin/| n. |薄荷烯|A cyclic terpene hydrocarbon found in peppermint oil and other essential oils. It is a colorless liquid with a characteristic minty odor.  |Menthene is a common constituent of many essential oils, contributing to their characteristic aroma.|近义词：limonene, terpinene|反义词：无
