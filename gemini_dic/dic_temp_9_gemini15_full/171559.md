pull-together:/pʊl təˈɡɛðər/| phrasal verb|齐心协力；振作起来；整理；恢复；|To work together effectively as a team; to regain control of oneself and one's emotions; to organize or tidy something.|例句：The team pulled together to finish the project on time. 团队齐心协力按时完成了项目。


例句：She pulled herself together after hearing the bad news. 听到坏消息后，她振作了起来。|近义词：collaborate, cooperate, compose oneself, rally, organize|反义词：fall apart, disintegrate, crumble, disorganize|词性: phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They pull together on most projects. = 他们在大多数项目上通力合作。
The team pulls together to achieve its goals. = 团队齐心协力以实现其目标。


一般过去时:
We pulled together to overcome the crisis. = 我们齐心协力克服了危机。
The company pulled together after the scandal. = 丑闻过后，公司团结一心。


现在完成时:
They have pulled together to build a strong community. = 他们齐心协力建立了一个强大的社区。
We have pulled together through many difficult times. = 我们一起度过了许多艰难的时期。


过去完成时:
They had pulled together before the presentation. = 他们在演示之前就已齐心协力。
She had pulled herself together before entering the room. = 她进房间前已经振作起来了。


将来时:
We will pull together to support him. = 我们将团结一致支持他。
They will pull together to launch the new product. = 他们将齐心协力推出新产品。


虚拟语气:
If we pulled together, we could achieve more. = 如果我们齐心协力，就能取得更大的成就。
If she had pulled herself together earlier, things would have been better. = 如果她早点振作起来，情况就会好得多。


被动语态:
The project was pulled together by a dedicated team. = 这个项目是由一个敬业的团队完成的。(不太常用，pull together 通常是主动语态)
The resources were pulled together for the emergency. = 为紧急情况准备好了资源。(不太常用，pull together 通常是主动语态)


疑问句:
Can we pull together to solve this problem? = 我们能否齐心协力解决这个问题？
Will they be able to pull together in time? = 他们能否及时团结一致？


否定句:
They didn't pull together, so the project failed. = 他们没有齐心协力，所以项目失败了。
She couldn't pull herself together after the accident. = 事故后她无法振作起来。
