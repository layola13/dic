/ˈiːkwəlaɪzərz/| n. |均衡器；平衡器 |A device or system that makes things equal or balances them.  Equalizers are used in audio engineering to adjust the balance of different frequencies in a sound signal,  in photography to adjust the brightness of an image, and in other fields to achieve balance or fairness. | Example: The audio engineer used an equalizer to boost the bass in the recording.  音频工程师使用均衡器来增强录音中的低音。  The photographer adjusted the equalizer to balance the brightness across the image. 摄影师调整均衡器以平衡图像的亮度。|近义词：balancer, leveler |反义词：distorter, unbalancer |名词
