interlinear:/ˌɪntərˈlɪniər/ | adj. | 行间注释的；逐行翻译的 |  An interlinear translation is one that places a translation of a text directly above or below each line of the original language.  It's a method used to aid in understanding a foreign language text. | Example:  The student used an interlinear Bible to understand the original Greek.  学生用对照圣经来理解原文希腊语。|近义词：parallel, bilingual |反义词：None directly opposite, but could be considered opposite to a non-annotated translation.
