overruling: /ˌoʊvərˈruːlɪŋ/ | adj. v. | 否决；推翻；比…更重要 |  to reject a decision that has already been made, especially by a court of law; to be more important than something else. |例句：The appeals court overruled the lower court’s decision. 上诉法院推翻了下级法院的判决。The judge overruled the objection.  法官驳回了异议。|近义词：rejecting, overriding, superseding |反义词：upholding, confirming


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The higher court overrules the lower court's decision. = 上级法院否决了下级法院的判决。
He often overrules his assistant's suggestions. = 他经常否决他助手的建议。


一般过去时:
The judge overruled the defendant's objection. = 法官驳回了被告的异议。
The Supreme Court overruled the previous ruling. = 最高法院推翻了先前的裁决。


现在完成时:
The committee has overruled the initial proposal. = 委员会已经否决了最初的提案。
They have overruled all objections to the plan. = 他们已经否决了对该计划的所有异议。


过去完成时:
The appellate court had overruled the trial court's verdict. = 上诉法院已经推翻了初审法院的判决。
She had overruled her initial reservations about the project. = 她已经放弃了她对该项目的最初疑虑。


将来时:
The president will likely overrule the cabinet's decision. = 总统可能会否决内阁的决定。
The court will overrule the injunction. = 法院将推翻禁令。


虚拟语气:
If the judge overruled the motion, the trial would continue. = 如果法官驳回动议，审判将继续进行。
I wish the boss had overruled my foolish suggestion. = 我真希望老板驳回了我愚蠢的建议。


被动语态:
The lower court's decision was overruled by the appeals court. = 下级法院的判决被上诉法院推翻了。
His suggestion was overruled by the management. = 他的建议被管理层否决了。


疑问句:
Will the court overrule the previous ruling? = 法院会推翻先前的裁决吗？
Did the committee overrule the proposed amendment? = 委员会是否否决了拟议的修正案？


否定句:
The judge did not overrule the objection. = 法官没有驳回异议。
The higher authority did not overrule the decision of the lower court. = 上级机关没有推翻下级法院的裁决。
