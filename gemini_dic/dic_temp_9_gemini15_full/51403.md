determining: /dɪˈtɜːrmɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 决定；确定；影响；限定 |  Determining the cause of the problem is crucial.  |  decide, establish, influence, define |  undecided, uncertain | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is determining the best course of action. = 他正在决定最佳行动方案。
She determines the company's marketing strategy. = 她决定公司的营销策略。


一般过去时:
The judge determined the sentence. = 法官判决了刑期。
They determined to finish the project by Friday. = 他们决定在星期五之前完成项目。


现在完成时:
We have determined the winner of the competition. = 我们已经确定了比赛的获胜者。
She has determined to learn a new language. = 她已决定学习一门外语。


过去完成时:
They had determined the facts before making a decision. = 他们在做出决定之前已经确定了事实。
He had determined to quit his job months ago. = 他几个月前就决定辞职了。


将来时:
The scientists will determine the effectiveness of the new drug. = 科学家们将确定这种新药的有效性。
She will determine what steps to take next. = 她将决定下一步采取哪些措施。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would determine what is best for yourself. = 如果我是你，我会决定什么对你最好。
I wish I had determined to study harder earlier. = 我真希望我早点决定努力学习。


被动语态:
The winner will be determined by a panel of judges. = 获胜者将由评审团决定。
The date of the meeting has been determined. = 会议日期已确定。


疑问句:
How do you determine the value of this item? = 你如何确定这件物品的价值？
What factors determine success in business? = 哪些因素决定了商业上的成功？


否定句:
He couldn't determine the source of the leak. = 他无法确定泄漏的来源。
We haven't yet determined a date for the party. = 我们还没有确定聚会的日期。
