cross-checker:/ˈkrɒsˌtʃɛkər/ | n. |交叉检查员，复核员 | A person who verifies the accuracy of data or work by comparing it to another source or method.  This often involves comparing results from different systems or processes to identify discrepancies or errors. | Example: The cross-checker meticulously compared the two datasets to ensure consistency. 交叉检查员仔细比较了两个数据集以确保一致性。 |近义词：verifier, auditor, inspector |反义词：N/A
