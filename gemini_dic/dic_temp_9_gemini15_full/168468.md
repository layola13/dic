probationer:/prəˈbeɪʃənər/ | n. | 缓刑期的人；实习生；试用期员工 | A person undergoing a probationary period, especially one serving a probationary sentence or undergoing training for a profession.  | Example: He is a probationer teacher at the local high school. 他是当地高中的一名实习教师。 |近义词：trainee, apprentice, novice | 反义词：veteran, expert


USAGE->

The probationer teaches English to beginners. =  实习教师教初学者英语。
She is a probationer lawyer and works hard every day. = 她是一名实习律师，每天努力工作。


一般过去时:
The probationer completed his probationary period successfully. = 实习生成功完成了他的试用期。
He was a probationer at the firm last year. = 他去年是该公司的一名实习生。

The probationer has already passed his driving test. = 实习生已经通过了驾驶考试。
