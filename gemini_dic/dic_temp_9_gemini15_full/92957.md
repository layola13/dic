ideologists:/ˌaɪdiˈɒlədʒɪsts/| n. |意识形态学家，思想家|People who develop, express, or promote the ideas of an ideology.  They are often influential in shaping political thought and action. |例句：The ideologists of the revolution promoted a vision of social equality.  革命的意识形态学家们宣传了社会平等的愿景。


例句：Many prominent ideologists contributed to the shaping of the nation's political landscape. 许多著名的思想家为塑造国家的政治格局做出了贡献。


近义词：theorists, thinkers, doctrinaires, philosophers


反义词：pragmatists (in a contrasting sense)


词性:名词
