half-addressed:/ˌhɑːf əˈdrɛst/ | adj. | 半完成的；未充分解决的；含糊其辞的 | Partially addressed; incompletely dealt with; vague or unclear.  |例句：The problem is half-addressed; we need a more comprehensive solution.  问题只解决了一半；我们需要一个更全面的解决方案。

例句：His explanation was half-addressed, leaving many questions unanswered.他的解释含糊其辞，留下许多问题没有回答。| 近义词：incomplete, unfinished, unresolved, vague, ambiguous | 反义词：complete, resolved, clear, explicit | 词性：形容词
