howitzer:/ˈhaʊɪtsər/| n. |榴弹炮|A howitzer is a type of artillery gun that fires shells at a high angle, allowing it to reach targets over obstacles or behind cover.  It is characterized by its shorter barrel than a cannon, but longer than a mortar.  Howitzers are used to provide indirect fire support to ground troops. |The army deployed several howitzers to provide covering fire.  军队部署了几门榴弹炮来提供掩护火力。
