what:/wɑːt/| pron.|什么；多么；怎么；什么样|The word "what" is an interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something. It can also be used as an exclamation to express surprise or disbelief. |例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！|近义词：which, how|反义词:nothing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？


一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 聚会上发生了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you been doing? = 你一直在做什么？
What has she accomplished? = 她取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you done before you came here? = 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the concert? = 音乐会之前他们吃了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天做什么？
What will the weather be like? = 天气会怎么样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to expect. = 我希望我知道会发生什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built in that area? = 那个区域正在建造什么？


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道该说什么。
I didn't see what happened. = 我没看到发生了什么。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What's going on? = 发生什么事了？
What's the matter? = 怎么了？
What's new? = 有什么新鲜事吗？
What's your point? = 你的重点是什么？
