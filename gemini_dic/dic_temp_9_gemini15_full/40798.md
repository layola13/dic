convoke:/kənˈvoʊk/ | vt. |召集，召开|To call together to a meeting; summon.|The president convoked a meeting of his cabinet to discuss the crisis. 总统召集内阁会议讨论危机。|近义词：summon, convene, call, assemble |反义词：disperse, adjourn


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee convoked a meeting every month. = 委员会每月召开一次会议。
The king convoked his advisors to the castle. = 国王召集他的顾问到城堡。


一般过去时:
The principal convoked a special assembly last Friday. = 校长上周五召开了一次特别集会。
The general convoked his troops before the battle. = 将军在战斗前召集了他的军队。


现在完成时:
The board has convoked several emergency sessions this year. = 董事会今年已经召开了几次紧急会议。
They have convoked a meeting to discuss the new policy. = 他们已经召开会议讨论新政策。


过去完成时:
The president had convoked a press conference before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，总统已经召开了一场新闻发布会。
The organizers had convoked a preliminary meeting to plan the event. = 组织者已经召开了一次预备会议来策划活动。


将来时:
The governor will convoke a special session of the legislature next week. = 州长下周将召开立法机关的特别会议。
The chairman will convoke the meeting as soon as all members arrive. = 主席将在所有成员到达后立即召开会议。


虚拟语气:
If the mayor convoked an emergency meeting, the problem could be solved quickly. = 如果市长召开紧急会议，问题就能很快得到解决。
I wish the committee had convoked a meeting sooner to address this issue. = 我希望委员会早点召开会议来处理这个问题。


被动语态:
A meeting was convoked to discuss the budget. = 召开了一次会议来讨论预算。
The delegates were convoked by the president. = 代表们是由总统召集的。


疑问句:
Will the headmaster convoke a meeting to address the students' concerns? = 校长会召开会议来处理学生们的担忧吗？
Has the council convoked a meeting to decide the matter? = 理事会已经召开会议来决定此事了吗？


否定句:
The teacher did not convoke a class meeting that day. = 老师那天没有召开班级会议。
The union has not yet convoked a strike. = 工会尚未宣布罢工。
