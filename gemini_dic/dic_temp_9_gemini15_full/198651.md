shutterbugs:/ˈʃʌtərˌbʌɡz/| n. |摄影爱好者，痴迷于摄影的人|Enthusiastic amateur photographers; people who are passionate about photography and take many pictures.|例句：He's a real shutterbug; he always has his camera with him. 他是个真正的摄影爱好者；他总是带着相机。

例句：Many shutterbugs flocked to the scene of the accident to capture the moment. 许多摄影爱好者蜂拥到事故现场拍摄那一刻。


近义词：photography enthusiast, camera buff, photo fanatic|反义词：None (It's a descriptive term, not something with a direct opposite)|词性：名词
