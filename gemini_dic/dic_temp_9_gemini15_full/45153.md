cudgelled:/ˈkʌdʒəld/| v. |痛打；苦思；反复考虑 | To beat or strike repeatedly with a cudgel (a short, heavy stick).  It can also figuratively mean to think hard about something, to ponder intensely. |例句：The angry mob cudgelled the thief.暴怒的暴徒痛打小偷。

The writer cudgelled his brain for an idea.  作家绞尽脑汁想出一个主意。

近义词：beat, strike, thrash, pummel, ponder, contemplate, mull over |反义词：nurture, soothe, caress
