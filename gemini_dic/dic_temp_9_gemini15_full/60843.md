electrotin: /ɪˌlɛkˈtroʊtɪn/ | n. | 电镀锡 |  A coating of tin applied to a metal surface by an electrolytic process.  This process protects the base metal from corrosion. | Example: The can is made of steel with an electrotin coating.  这个罐子是用镀锡钢制成的。 | 近义词：tin plating, tin coating | 反义词：None specifically.  The antonym would describe the absence of a tin coating. | 名词
