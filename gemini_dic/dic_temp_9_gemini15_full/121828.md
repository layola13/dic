misreliance: /ˌmɪsrɪˈlaɪəns/ | n. |错误信赖，误信 |The act of relying on something that is incorrect or misleading; a mistaken trust or confidence.  It often implies a negative consequence resulting from this misplaced trust. |例句：His misreliance on the faulty data led to the project's failure. 他的错误依赖于有缺陷的数据导致了项目的失败。

His misreliance on the lawyer's advice cost him dearly. 他错误地依赖律师的建议，为此付出了巨大的代价。|近义词：mistrust, misconception, misjudgment, delusion |反义词：trust, reliance, confidence |名词
