recrudescent: /ˌriːkrʊˈdɛsnt/ | adj. | 复发的；重新恶化的；再次发生的 |  Relating to or characterized by a recurrence of something, especially a disease or undesirable condition after a period of abatement.  It suggests a renewed outbreak or a worsening of a previously controlled situation. |

例句：

The recrudescent infection required a new course of antibiotics. = 复发的感染需要新的抗生素疗程。

His recrudescent anger surprised everyone. = 他再次爆发的怒火让每个人都感到惊讶。


近义词：recurrent, recurring, resurgent, revived, renewed

反义词：abated, subsided, diminished, healed


USAGE->

一般现在时:

The disease is recrudescent in this region. = 这种疾病在这个地区正在复发。
His symptoms are recrudescent. = 他的症状正在复发。


一般过去时:

The violence recrudesced after a period of peace. = 在一段和平时期之后，暴力事件再次发生。
The infection recrudesced last week. = 感染在上周复发。


现在完成时:

The problem has recrudesced several times this year. = 今年这个问题已经复发了好几次。
His prejudice has recrudesced in recent years. = 他近几年的偏见又死灰复燃了。


过去完成时:

The war had recrudesced before the peace talks began. = 和谈开始之前，战争就已经再次爆发了。
The symptoms had recrudesced before he reached the hospital. = 他到达医院之前，症状就已经复发了。


将来时:

The conflict will likely recrudesce if no solution is found. = 如果找不到解决方案，冲突很可能会再次爆发。
We fear the disease will recrudesce in the fall. = 我们担心这种疾病会在秋季复发。


虚拟语气:

If the disease recrudesced, we would need to quarantine the area. = 如果疾病复发，我们就需要隔离该地区。
If his anger recrudesced, the meeting would be a disaster. = 如果他的怒火再次爆发，会议就会是一场灾难。


被动语态:

The conflict was recrudesced by the recent border incident. = 最近的边境事件导致冲突再次爆发。
The infection was recrudesced by the change in weather. = 天气的变化导致感染复发。


疑问句:

Is the problem recrudescing? = 问题正在复发吗？
Will the symptoms recrudesce? = 症状会复发吗？


否定句:

The disease did not recrudesce after treatment. = 治疗后疾病没有复发。
His anger did not recrudesce. = 他的怒火没有再次爆发。


PHRASE->

recrudescent infection: 复发性感染

词源:

From Latin *recrudescere, from re- + crudus "raw, uncooked," hence "sore, harsh".  The sense development is from a raw, sore place reopening to a general sense of recurrence of something unpleasant.
