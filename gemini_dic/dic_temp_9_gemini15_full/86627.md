helium:/ˈhiːliəm/| n. |氦|A chemical element, a colorless, odorless, inert gas, the lightest of the noble gases, used in balloons and in cryogenics.|例句：Helium is lighter than air. 氦气比空气轻。|例句：Liquid helium is used to cool superconducting magnets. 液氦用于冷却超导磁体。|近义词：noble gas|反义词：无
