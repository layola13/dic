neurochemistry:/ˌnʊroʊˈkɛmɪstri/ | n. | 神经化学 | The study of the chemical processes within and relating to the nervous system.  Neurochemistry examines the roles of various neurochemicals such as neurotransmitters and neuromodulators in the brain and nervous system.  It also explores how these chemicals affect behavior, cognition, and disease. | Example:  Research in neurochemistry has greatly advanced our understanding of Alzheimer's disease.  神经化学的研究极大地提高了我们对阿尔茨海默病的理解。  |近义词: neural chemistry |反义词:  (无直接反义词，但可以考虑与心理学或其他神经科学分支对比) | 名词
