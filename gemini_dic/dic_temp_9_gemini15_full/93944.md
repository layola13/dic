immunology:/ˌɪmjuːˈnɒlədʒi/|/n.|免疫学|The branch of biology that studies the immune system, including its physiological functioning in states of both health and disease; and malfunctions of the immune system (immunopathology).  |Immunology is a complex field of study that involves many different aspects of the body's defense mechanisms.|近义词：immunobiology|反义词：无
