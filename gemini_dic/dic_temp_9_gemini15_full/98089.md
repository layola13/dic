inquisitiveness:/ˌɪnˈkwɪzɪtɪvnəs/| n. |好奇心，好问|The quality of being curious and asking questions.  It is a desire to learn or know more about something.|Her inquisitiveness led her to discover a hidden passage in the old house.她的好奇心使她发现老房子里的一条隐蔽通道。|curiosity,  inquisitiveness|indifference, apathy|名词
