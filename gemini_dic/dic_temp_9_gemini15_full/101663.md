irrelation: /ˌɪrɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 无关，不相关 | Lack of relation; unconnectedness.  The statement bears no relation to the facts; it is an irrelation.  | 例句：The irrelation between the two events is striking. 这两个事件之间毫无关联，令人震惊。

例句：The irrelation of his actions to his words was evident to everyone.  他的行为与他的话语毫不相干，这一点每个人都看得出来。


近义词：irrelevance, unrelatedness, unconnectedness

反义词：relevance, connection, relation


词性：名词
