cut-throat:/ˈkʌtθroʊt/| adj.|残酷的；激烈的；不择手段的|Extremely competitive or ruthless; characterized by fierce competition and a disregard for ethical considerations.  It implies a willingness to harm others to achieve success.|例句：The business world can be a cut-throat environment. 商界可能是一个残酷的环境。|例句：The two companies engaged in a cut-throat price war. 这两家公司参与了一场残酷的价格战。|近义词：ruthless, merciless, fierce, competitive, aggressive|反义词：gentle, merciful, lenient, cooperative
