perfect:/ˈpɜːrfɪkt/ /ˈpɝːfɛkt/| adj., v., n.|完美的；使完美；完美的人或事物|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "perfect" means complete, without fault or defect.  It describes something that is ideally excellent or flawless. As a verb, it means to make something perfect or to improve something to a high standard. As a noun, it refers to something that is perfect or a person who is exceptionally skilled.


例句:
This is a perfect day for a picnic. = 今天是野餐的完美日子。

Her performance was simply perfect. = 她的表演简直完美无缺。


近义词:
ideal, flawless, impeccable, faultless, immaculate, excellent, superb


反义词:
imperfect, flawed, defective, faulty, deficient, terrible, awful


词性:
adjective, verb, noun
