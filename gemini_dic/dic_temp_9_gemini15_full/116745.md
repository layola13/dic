maunderingly:/ˈmɔːndərɪŋli/ | adv. | 唠唠叨叨地；漫无目的地；含糊不清地 | rambling and incoherent speech or thought; in a slow, rambling, and pointless way. |  He maunderingly recounted the events of the day, losing his train of thought several times. 他唠唠叨叨地叙述了当天发生的事情，好几次都失去了思路。 | 近义词： ramblingly, aimlessly, vaguely | 反义词： concisely, clearly, purposefully

She maunders about the garden, picking flowers. = 她漫无目的地在花园里闲逛，采摘鲜花。
