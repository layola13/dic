perspective:/pərˈspektɪv/| n.|视角，观点，透视画法|A way of regarding situations, facts, etc., and judging their relative importance.|The painting shows excellent perspective. 这幅画展现了极好的透视效果。  From her perspective, the situation looked very different. 从她的角度来看，情况看起来大相径庭。| viewpoint, standpoint, outlook|misunderstanding, misinterpretation|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What perspective do you hold on this issue? = 你对这个问题有什么看法？
What is the artist's perspective in this painting? = 这幅画中艺术家的视角是什么？


一般过去时:
What perspective did he offer on the problem? = 他对这个问题提供了什么观点？
What was the company's perspective on the merger? = 公司对并购有什么看法？


现在完成时:
What perspectives have you gained from this experience? = 你从这次经历中获得了什么见解？
What has changed your perspective on life? = 什么改变了你对生活的看法？


过去完成时:
What perspective had she held before the event? = 事件发生前她持有什么观点？
What perspectives had they considered before making the decision? = 他们在做决定之前考虑过哪些观点？


将来时:
What perspective will you adopt in your presentation? = 你将在你的演示文稿中采用什么视角？
What perspective will the report offer on the future of the industry? = 这份报告将对行业的未来提供什么展望？


虚拟语气:
If I had a different perspective, I might act differently. = 如果我有不同的看法，我可能会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had a clearer perspective on the matter. = 我希望我对这件事有更清晰的看法。


被动语态:
The issue was viewed from different perspectives. = 这个问题从不同的角度来看待。
The problem was analyzed from a historical perspective. = 这个问题是从历史的角度进行分析的。


疑问句:
What is your perspective on the current economic situation? = 你对当前经济形势有什么看法？
From what perspective are you judging this situation? = 你从什么角度来判断这种情况？


否定句:
He didn't offer a useful perspective on the debate. = 他对这场辩论没有提供有用的见解。
I don't share her perspective on this issue. = 我并不认同她对这个问题的看法。


PHRASE->
in perspective:  从正确的角度；客观地
from my perspective:  从我的角度来看
a different perspective:  不同的视角
put something into perspective:  使…显得不那么重要
gain perspective: 获得新的视角
