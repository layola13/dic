febrifuge:/ˈfɛbrɪfjuːdʒ/| n. |退热药；退烧药 |A febrifuge is a medicine that reduces fever.  Febrifuges work by lowering the body's temperature. |Example: The doctor prescribed a febrifuge to bring down the patient's high fever.  医生开了退烧药来降低病人的高烧。|近义词：antipyretic|反义词：pyrogenic|名词
