savory:/ˈseɪvəri/ | adj. | 美味的；咸的；可口的 | Having a salty or piquant flavor rather than a sweet flavor; appetizing.  |  例句：The savory stew was a perfect complement to the sweet dessert. 这道美味的炖菜与甜点完美搭配。

例句：I prefer savory dishes to sweet ones. 我更喜欢咸味菜肴而不是甜味菜肴。


近义词：appetizing, flavorful, tasty, piquant, pungent | 反义词：sweet, sugary


词源：From Old French *savori "savory, tasty," from Latin sapor "taste, flavor," from sapere "to taste."
