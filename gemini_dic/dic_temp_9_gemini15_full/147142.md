overlooks:/ˌoʊvərˈlʊks/ | vt. | 忽视；俯瞰；眺望 | To fail to notice or consider something; to have a view from above. |例句：He overlooked a crucial detail in the contract. 他忽略了合同中的一个重要细节。|例句：Our hotel room overlooked the ocean. 我们的酒店房间可以俯瞰大海。|近义词：ignore, neglect, disregard,command, survey |反义词：notice, observe, consider, heed |动词
