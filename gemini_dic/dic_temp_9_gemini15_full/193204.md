seduce:/səˈduːs/ | vt. | 诱惑；勾引；煽动 | To entice or persuade (someone) to do something, typically something morally wrong, by using charm or flattery.  | 例句：He was seduced by the promise of easy money. 他被轻松赚钱的承诺所诱惑。

He tried to seduce her into betraying her friends. 他试图诱使她背叛朋友。


近义词：tempt, entice, allure, charm, attract | 反义词：deter, dissuade, repel


词源:  From Old French *seduire, from Latin *seducere, from se- ("apart") + ducere ("to lead").  The original meaning was "to lead astray," implying a moral deviation.
