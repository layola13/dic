defile:/dɪˈfaɪl/| vt. n.| 弄脏；亵渎；破坏；污损；|to spoil or damage the purity or sanctity of something; to make something dirty.  |例句：The vandals defiled the monument.  破坏分子亵渎了纪念碑。

The soldiers defiled the sacred grove. 士兵们亵渎了神圣的树林。


近义词：pollute, contaminate, desecrate, profane, corrupt|反义词：purify, cleanse, sanctify|动词
