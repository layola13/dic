inopportune:/ˌɪnɒpəˈtjuːn/| adj.| 不合时宜的，不适时的 |  Untimely; inconvenient; not appropriate or suitable to the circumstances.  It implies that the timing is awkward or wrong. | 例句：It was inopportune to ask for a raise right before the company announced layoffs.  公司宣布裁员之前要求加薪时机很不合适。

例句：  He chose an inopportune moment to mention his ex-girlfriend. 他选择了一个很不合适的时机来提起他的前女友。


近义词： untimely, ill-timed, inappropriate, inconvenient, awkward, malapropos

反义词： opportune, timely, appropriate, convenient, suitable

词性：形容词
