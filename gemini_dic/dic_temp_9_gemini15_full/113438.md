lynch:/lɪntʃ/| vt. n.|私刑处死；私法处置|To kill (someone), especially by hanging, without a legal trial.|The mob lynched the accused thief.暴民私刑处死了被指控的小偷。|近义词：execute, hang, murder|反义词：acquit, pardon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They lynch criminals in some parts of the world. = 在世界某些地区，他们私刑处死罪犯。
He lynches his opponents with harsh criticism. = 他用尖刻的批评来“私刑处死”他的对手。


一般过去时:
The angry crowd lynched the suspected murderer. = 愤怒的暴民私刑处死了被怀疑的凶手。
They lynched him without a fair trial. = 他们在他没有得到公平审判的情况下私刑处死了他。
