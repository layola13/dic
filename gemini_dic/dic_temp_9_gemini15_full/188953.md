rvs:/ɑːrviːz/| n. |休闲车；房车 |Recreational vehicles are motor vehicles designed for leisure activities and often used for camping and travel. They range from small camper vans to large, luxurious motorhomes.|例句：Many families enjoy taking their RVs on cross-country road trips. 许多家庭喜欢开着房车进行跨国公路旅行。|近义词：camper van, motorhome |反义词：sedan, sports car


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drives his RV across the country every summer. 他每年夏天都开着他的房车横跨全国。
They rent RVs for their family vacations. 他们租房车进行家庭度假。

一般过去时:
We drove our RV to the beach last year. 我们去年开着房车去了海滩。
They rented an RV for their trip to Yellowstone National Park. 他们为去黄石国家公园的旅行租了一辆房车。

现在完成时:
She has owned her RV for five years. 她拥有她的房车五年了。
They have traveled extensively in their RV. 他们用房车旅行了很多地方。
