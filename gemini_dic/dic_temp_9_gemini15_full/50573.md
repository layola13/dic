deracinates:/dɪˈræsəneɪts/| vt.|使脱离；使无根；使失去民族特性|To uproot; to remove from one's native land; to displace.|例句：The war deracinated the population, scattering families and destroying communities.  战争使人民流离失所，使家庭四分五裂，破坏了社区。

例句：The forced migration deracinated the people from their cultural heritage.  强制迁移使人民失去了他们的文化遗产。


近义词：uproot, displace, dislodge, root out, exile

反义词：implant, establish, settle, integrate

词性:动词
