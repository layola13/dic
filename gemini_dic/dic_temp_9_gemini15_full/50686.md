dermatography:/ˌdɜːrməˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. |皮肤描记术，皮肤划痕症 |The process of producing a skin lesion by stroking the skin.  It can refer to a diagnostic procedure or a condition called dermatographism (also known as dermographism).  |Example:  The doctor performed dermatography to assess the patient's skin sensitivity.  医生进行了皮肤描记术以评估患者的皮肤敏感性。  |近义词：dermographism, skin writing |反义词：无
