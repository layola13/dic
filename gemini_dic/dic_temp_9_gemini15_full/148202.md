overshoot:/ˌoʊvərˈʃuːt/| vt. n.|超过，越过；射过头；超出目标|To go past a target or intended destination; to exceed a limit or boundary.|例句：The plane overshot the runway.飞机跑过了跑道。|例句：He overshot his budget.他超支了。|近义词：exceed, surpass, transcend|反义词：undershoot, fall short
