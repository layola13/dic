photographs:/ˈfoʊtəɡræfs/ | n. |照片，摄影作品 |Photographs are a way to capture memories. 照片是捕捉记忆的一种方式。|pictures, images, snaps |  |Noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He photographs birds in the wild. = 他拍摄野生鸟类。
She photographs landscapes for a living. = 她以拍摄风景为生。


一般过去时:
I photographed the sunset last night. = 我昨晚拍下了日落。
They photographed the event for the newspaper. = 他们为报纸拍摄了该事件。


现在完成时:
She has photographed many famous people. = 她已经拍摄过许多名人。
He has photographed the same location multiple times. = 他多次拍摄过同一个地点。


过去完成时:
I had photographed the scene before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，我已经拍下了现场照片。
They had photographed the building from every angle. = 他们已经从各个角度拍摄了这座建筑物。


将来时:
I will photograph the wedding next month. = 我下个月将拍摄婚礼。
She will photograph the concert for her blog. = 她将为她的博客拍摄音乐会。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better camera, I would photograph more often. = 如果我有一台更好的相机，我会更频繁地拍照。
I wish I had photographed that amazing view. = 我真希望我拍下了那令人惊叹的景色。


被动语态:
The event was photographed by several journalists. = 该事件被几名记者拍摄下来。
The scenery was photographed from a helicopter. = 风景是从直升机上拍摄的。


疑问句:
Did you photograph the children at the party? = 你在派对上给孩子们拍照了吗？
Have you ever photographed wildlife? = 你曾经拍摄过野生动物吗？


否定句:
I didn't photograph the accident. = 我没有拍摄事故。
She didn't photograph the meeting. = 她没有拍摄会议。
