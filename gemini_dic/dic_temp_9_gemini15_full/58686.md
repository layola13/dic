dumped:/dʌmpt/ | vt. |抛弃；倾倒；解雇；|to get rid of something unwanted, usually in a careless or abrupt manner; to dismiss someone from a job; to suddenly end a romantic relationship.|例句：He dumped his old car at the junkyard. 他把旧车扔在了废车场。|例句：She dumped him after discovering his lies.  她发现他的谎话后甩了他。|近义词：abandoned, discarded, dismissed, jilted |反义词：kept, retained, hired, cherished |


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He dumps his trash every week. = 他每周都倒垃圾。
She dumps her feelings onto her diary. = 她把自己的感情倾泻在日记里。

一般过去时:
They dumped the old furniture. = 他们扔掉了旧家具。
He dumped his girlfriend last night. = 他昨晚甩了他的女朋友。

现在完成时:
I have dumped all my old clothes. = 我已经把所有旧衣服都扔掉了。
She has dumped three boyfriends this year. = 她今年已经甩了三个男朋友。

过去完成时:
They had dumped the evidence before the police arrived. = 警察来之前，他们已经销毁了证据。
He had dumped her before she found out the truth. = 在她发现真相之前，他已经甩了她了。

将来时:
I will dump this broken chair tomorrow. = 我明天要把这把坏椅子扔掉。
He will dump his project if he doesn't get funding. = 如果得不到资金，他将放弃他的项目。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't dump him so easily. = 如果我是你，我不会那么轻易地甩了他。
I wish I hadn't dumped that old painting. = 我真希望我没有扔掉那幅旧画。

被动语态:
The old building was dumped with trash. = 那栋旧建筑被垃圾堆满了。
He was dumped by his company last month. = 他上个月被公司解雇了。

疑问句:
Where did you dump the rubbish? = 你把垃圾扔到哪里了？
Why did she dump him? = 她为什么甩了他？

否定句:
He didn't dump the box. = 他没有扔掉那个箱子。
She didn't dump her boyfriend, they broke up mutually. = 她没有甩她的男朋友，他们和平分手了。
