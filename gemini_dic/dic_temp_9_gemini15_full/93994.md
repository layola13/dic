impairments:/ɪmˈpɛərmənts/ | n. | 损伤；缺陷；障碍 |  A weakening or damage of a body part or function.  Impairments can range from mild to severe and affect various aspects of a person's life.  They can be physical, cognitive, or sensory. |


Example 1:  His hearing impairments made it difficult to follow the conversation.  他的听力障碍使他难以跟上谈话。

Example 2: The accident resulted in several physical impairments, including limited mobility. 事故导致了几处身体损伤，包括行动不便。


Synonyms:  deficiencies, disabilities, handicaps, defects, flaws

Antonyms:  improvements, enhancements, strengths, advantages
