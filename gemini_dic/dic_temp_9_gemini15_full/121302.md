misfocus:/ˌmɪsˈfoʊkəs/ | v. | 错焦，焦点模糊 | To fail to focus correctly; to be out of focus.  | Example: The photographer misfocused the lens, resulting in a blurry image.  摄影师镜头错焦，导致图像模糊。  The camera misfocused on the subject, making it difficult to see the details. 相机对焦主体错误，使细节难以辨认。|近义词：blur, defocus | 反义词：focus, sharpen | 动词
