fraught:/frɔːt/| adj.|充满…的；焦虑不安的；充满危险的 |full of or accompanied by (something undesirable);  fraught with danger or difficulty.|例句：The situation is fraught with danger.  情况危机四伏。|例句：Their marriage was fraught with difficulties. 他们的婚姻充满困难。|近义词：burdened, laden, filled, charged |反义词：calm, peaceful, serene

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The journey is fraught with peril. = 这段旅程充满危险。
The atmosphere is fraught with tension. = 气氛紧张。


一般过去时:
The negotiations were fraught with difficulties. = 谈判充满了困难。
The meeting was fraught with emotion. = 会议充满了情绪。


现在完成时:
The project has been fraught with delays. = 项目一直被延误所困扰。
Their relationship has been fraught with conflict. = 他们的关系一直冲突不断。


过去完成时:
The mission had been fraught with unforeseen challenges. = 任务充满了不可预见的挑战。
The situation had been fraught with uncertainty. = 局势充满了不确定性。


将来时:
The expedition will be fraught with risks. = 探险将充满风险。
The upcoming election will be fraught with controversy. = 即将来临的选举将充满争议。


虚拟语气:
If the mission were fraught with danger, we would still proceed. = 即使任务充满危险，我们仍然会继续。
If the negotiations were fraught with such difficulties, we would have abandoned them. = 如果谈判充满了如此多的困难，我们就会放弃。


被动语态:

The entire process was fraught with complications. = 整个过程都充满了复杂的问题。


疑问句:
Is the situation fraught with danger? = 情况危险吗？
Is this task fraught with difficulties? = 这个任务充满困难吗？


否定句:
