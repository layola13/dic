nonspontaneousnesses:/ˌnɑːnˌspɒnˈteɪniəsnɪsəz/| n. |非自发性(复数)|The plural form of "nonspontaneity," referring to multiple instances or aspects of processes or events that do not occur naturally or without external intervention.  It describes situations where an action or process requires an external force or energy input to occur. |The nonspontaneousnesses of chemical reactions in the lab often require the input of heat or electricity.  实验室中化学反应的非自发性通常需要热量或电能的输入。  The multiple nonspontaneousnesses observed in the system point to a need for further investigation.  在系统中观察到的多种非自发性表明需要进一步调查。|近义词：lack of spontaneity, forcedness, artificiality |反义词：spontaneities
