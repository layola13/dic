chattered:/ˈtʃætəd/| v.|喋喋不休地说话；(牙齿)打颤 |To talk rapidly and continuously, especially about unimportant things;  To make a clicking or rattling sound, as teeth do from cold or fear.|例句：The children chatted excitedly about their day.孩子们兴奋地聊着他们的一天。|The old woman's teeth chattered in the cold. 老妇人在寒冷中牙齿打颤。|近义词：babbled, jabbered, gossiped|反义词：be silent, be quiet


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They chatter incessantly. = 他们喋喋不休。
The birds chatter in the trees. = 鸟儿在树上叽叽喳喳。

一般过去时:
She chatted with her friends for hours. = 她和朋友们聊了几个小时。
My teeth chattered during the blizzard. = 暴风雪期间我的牙齿打颤。

现在完成时:
We have chattered away the whole afternoon. = 我们整个下午都在闲聊。
The wind has chattered the windows all night. = 风整夜都在使窗户发出嘎嘎声。

过去完成时:
They had chattered about the movie before I joined them. = 我加入他们之前，他们一直在聊那部电影。
The engine had chattered alarmingly before it finally stopped. = 发动机在最终停止之前发出令人担忧的嘎嘎声。

将来时:

I think my teeth will chatter if we stay out any longer. = 我想如果我们再待在外面一会儿，我的牙齿就会打颤。

虚拟语气:

I wish they would stop chattering so loudly. = 我希望他们能停止大声闲聊。
