domesticating: /dəˈmɛstɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. |驯化；使驯服；使适应家庭生活 | 使适应居家生活；使成为家养的；使变得温顺；使变得平淡无奇 |

The farmer is domesticating the wild horses. = 农民正在驯服野马。

They are domesticating the new puppy. = 他们正在驯服新的小狗。


近义词：taming, civilizing, acclimating

反义词：wilding, untaming


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are domesticating the wild wolves. = 他们正在驯化野狼。
We domesticate many kinds of animals. = 我们驯化了许多种动物。

一般过去时:
Farmers domesticated wheat thousands of years ago. = 农民几千年前就驯化了小麦。
She domesticated the stray cat. = 她把流浪猫驯服了。

现在完成时:
Humans have domesticated many plant species. = 人类已经驯化了许多植物物种。
We have domesticated several dogs from the shelter. = 我们已经从收容所领养并驯服了几只狗。

过去完成时:
They had domesticated the lion before the show. = 他们表演前已经驯服了那头狮子。
The ancient people had domesticated dogs for hunting. = 古代人已经驯化了狗用于狩猎。

将来时:
Scientists will domesticate new plant varieties for food security. = 科学家们将驯化新的植物品种以保障粮食安全。
We will domesticate this kitten. = 我们将驯服这只小猫。

虚拟语气:
If they domesticated that bird, it might sing. = 如果他们驯服了那只鸟，它可能会唱歌。
I wish I could domesticate a tiger. = 我真希望我能驯服一只老虎。

被动语态:
Many crops have been domesticated by humans. = 许多作物已经被人类驯化。
The wild horse is being domesticated. = 野马正在被驯化。

疑问句:
Have you ever domesticated a pet? = 你有没有驯养过宠物？
Are they domesticating the animals for the circus? = 他们是否在为马戏团驯化动物？

否定句:
He hasn't domesticated any animals yet. = 他还没有驯服任何动物。
They didn't domesticate the wild birds. = 他们没有驯服那些野鸟。


PHRASE->
domesticate animals = 驯化动物
domesticate plants = 驯化植物
domesticate a wild animal = 驯服野生动物
domesticate a new breed = 培育新的品种
domesticate the environment = 使环境适应人类居住


词源:
domesticating 源自于动词 domesticate，它来自拉丁语 domesticus，意思是“家庭的”，它本身来源于domus，“房子，家”。  因此，domesticating 的根本含义是“使成为家庭的一部分”或“使适应家庭环境”。
