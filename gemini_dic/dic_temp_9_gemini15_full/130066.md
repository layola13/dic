non-positive:/ˌnɒnˈpɒzətɪv/| adj. |非正的，非正数的 |  Describes a number that is either zero or negative.  It excludes positive numbers. | Example: Zero and negative numbers are non-positive. 零和负数是非正数。  The non-positive solutions to the equation are x ≤ 0.  该方程的非正数解为x ≤ 0。 |近义词：negative or zero |反义词：positive
