reboiling:/ˌriːˈbɔɪlɪŋ/ | v. | 再煮沸；重新煮沸 | The act of boiling something again.  It often implies a previous boiling process was insufficient or the substance needs further processing. | Example: The chef decided to reboil the sauce to thicken it.  厨师决定重新煮沸酱汁使其浓稠。  The soup needed reboiling after it had cooled. 汤冷却后需要重新煮沸。 | 近义词：reheating, simmering | 反义词：cooling, freezing
