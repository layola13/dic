necrotic:/nɛˈkrɒtɪk/| adj.|坏死的；组织坏死的| Relating to or characterized by necrosis (the death of cells or tissues through injury or disease).  Necrotic tissue is dead and often appears black or dark. |Example: The surgeon removed the necrotic tissue to prevent further infection. 外科医生切除了坏死组织以防止进一步感染。 The wound showed signs of necrotic tissue around the edges. 伤口边缘显示出坏死组织的迹象。|近义词：gangrenous, mortified, devitalized |反义词：living, healthy, viable
