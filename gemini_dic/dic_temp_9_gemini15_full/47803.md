decarbonise:/ˌdiːˈkɑːrbənaɪz/| vt.|脱碳；减少碳排放|To reduce or remove carbon dioxide from (something), especially the atmosphere.|例句：The government is committed to decarbonising the energy sector by 2050. 政府致力于到2050年使能源部门脱碳。|例句：Many companies are actively seeking ways to decarbonise their operations.许多公司都在积极寻求减少其运营碳排放的方法。|近义词：decarbonize|反义词：carbonize

一般过去时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
