lateen:/ləˈtiːn/| n.|三角帆|A lateen sail is a triangular sail, typically on a single mast, that is attached to a long yard (spar) that is angled at approximately 45 degrees to the mast. This arrangement allows the sail to be efficiently used across a wide range of wind angles.|The lateen sail was a crucial element of Mediterranean shipping for centuries.|近义词：triangular sail|反义词：square sail
