planktonic:/plæŋkˈtɒnɪk/ | adj. | 浮游的；浮游生物的 | Relating to or characteristic of plankton.  Planktonic organisms drift in water currents and are unable to swim against them. | Example: Planktonic algae are the base of many aquatic food webs. 浮游藻类是许多水生食物网的基础。  The planktonic larvae of many marine invertebrates are dispersed widely by ocean currents. 许多海洋无脊椎动物的浮游幼虫被洋流广泛传播。 | 近义词：plankton-dwelling, pelagic (in a broader sense) | 反义词：benthic (bottom-dwelling)
