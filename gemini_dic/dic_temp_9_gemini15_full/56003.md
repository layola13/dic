do:/duː/|v.|做，干，进行|To perform or carry out an action or task.|例句：I do my homework every day. 我每天都做作业。|近义词：make, perform, execute|反义词：undo


dos:/doʊz/|n.|剂量，一剂|A measured amount of something, especially medicine.|例句：Take two dos of this medicine three times a day.  一天服用这种药两次，每次两剂。|近义词：portion, dose, measure|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I do the dishes every day. = 我每天都洗碗。
He does his work diligently. = 他勤奋地工作。


一般过去时:
She did her best. = 她尽力了。
They did the project together. = 他们一起完成了这个项目。


现在完成时:
I have done all my chores. = 我已经完成了所有的家务。
She has done a great job. = 她做得很好。


过去完成时:
He had done his homework before dinner. = 他在晚饭前完成了作业。
They had done everything they could. = 他们已经尽力了。


将来时:
I will do my best tomorrow. = 我明天会尽力而为。
We will do the work together. = 我们将一起完成这项工作。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would do it differently. = 如果我是你，我会做得不一样。
I wish I had done it better. = 我希望我做得更好。


被动语态:
The work was done quickly. = 工作很快就完成了。
The dishes are done. = 碗洗好了。


疑问句:
Do you understand? = 你明白吗？
Does he know the answer? = 他知道答案吗？


否定句:
I don't like doing housework. = 我不喜欢做家务。
He doesn't do his work properly. = 他工作做得不好。


PHRASE->
do one's best = 尽力
do without = 没有…也行
do up = 装饰；系好
do over = 重做
do away with = 废除
