directionality:/ˌdaɪrɛkʃəˈnæləti/ | n. |方向性，定向性 | The property of having a direction; the tendency to proceed in a particular direction.  It refers to the inherent bias or preference for one direction over another. This can apply to various contexts, including physical movement, data flow, or social interactions. |例句：The directionality of the wind affected the flight path. 风向影响了飞行路线。

The study focused on the directionality of information flow in neural networks.  该研究侧重于神经网络中信息流的方向性。


近义词：orientation, tendency, bias | 反义词：randomness, undirectedness, isotropy
