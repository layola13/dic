reinducements:/ˌriːɪnˈduːsmənts/ | n. | 再引诱；再次劝诱；新的诱因 |  The act of inducing or persuading someone again, often after a previous attempt has failed; new incentives or inducements offered.  | Example:  After the initial offer was rejected, the company offered reinducements to secure the deal.  公司在最初的报价被拒绝后，提供了新的诱因以确保交易达成。|近义词：reinforcements, incentives, inducements | 反义词：deterrents, disincentives
