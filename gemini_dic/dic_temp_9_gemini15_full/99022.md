interagent: /ˌɪntərˈeɪdʒənt/ | n. |交互代理，中间代理，互动代理 | An interagent is a software agent or system that mediates or facilitates communication and interaction between other agents or systems.  It often acts as a bridge or intermediary, translating between different protocols or formats, managing transactions, or resolving conflicts. | Example: The interagent successfully translated the data requests from the customer service application to the database server.  中间代理成功地将来自客户服务应用程序的数据请求翻译成了数据库服务器。 | 近义词: intermediary, mediator, go-between | 反义词:  direct link, primary agent | 词性: noun
