inumbrate:/ɪˈnʌmbɹeɪt/| vt.|遮蔽，使朦胧，使暗淡|to cast a shadow over; overshadow; darken|The tall building inumbrated the small houses below.  高楼遮蔽了下面的矮房。|The clouds inumbrated the sun. 云彩遮蔽了太阳。|近义词：shade, obscure, eclipse|反义词：illuminate, brighten

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tall building inumbrates the small houses below. = 高楼遮蔽了下面的矮房。
The clouds often inumbrate the sun in the afternoon. = 下午云彩经常遮蔽太阳。

一般过去时:
The storm inumbrated the entire town. = 暴风雨遮蔽了整个城镇。
The thick fog inumbrated the distant hills. = 浓雾遮蔽了远处的山丘。


现在完成时:
The scandal has inumbrated his career. = 丑闻已经损害了他的职业生涯。(Note:  While not a direct synonym, this example shows how the effect of inumbration persists.)
The prolonged drought has inumbrated the hopes of a good harvest. = 持续的干旱已经使丰收的希望变得渺茫。(Similar note as above)


过去完成时:
The heavy curtains had inumbrated the room before he lit the candles. = 他点蜡烛之前，厚厚的窗帘已经遮蔽了房间。
The clouds had inumbrated the view long before the storm broke. = 在暴风雨来临之前很久，云彩就已经遮蔽了视野。


将来时:
The approaching eclipse will inumbrate the city for a short period. = 即将来临的日食将短暂地遮蔽这座城市。
The new skyscraper will inumbrate the neighboring buildings. = 新建的摩天大楼将会遮蔽附近的建筑物。


虚拟语气:
If the sun were inumbrated, it would be darker. = 如果太阳被遮蔽，就会更暗。
I wish the clouds wouldn't inumbrate the view. = 我希望云彩不要遮蔽视野。


被动语态:
The small houses were inumbrated by the tall building. = 矮房被高楼遮蔽了。
The sun was inumbrated by the passing clouds. = 太阳被飘过的云彩遮蔽了。


疑问句:
Is the view inumbrated by the fog? = 视野被雾遮蔽了吗？
Will the new building inumbrate our garden? = 新建筑会遮蔽我们的花园吗？


否定句:
The sun was not inumbrated; it shone brightly. = 太阳没有被遮蔽；它明亮地照耀着。
The small town was not inumbrated; it lay in full sunlight. = 小镇没有被遮蔽；它沐浴在阳光下。
