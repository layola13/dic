disported:/dɪˈspɔːrtɪd/| v.|嬉戏，玩乐；消遣|to amuse oneself; to divert oneself; to engage in playful activity |例句：The children disported themselves in the park. 孩子们在公园里玩耍。

例句：The king disported himself with his courtiers. 国王和他的朝臣们一起寻欢作乐。


近义词：played, frolicked, gambolled, diverted, amused

反义词：toiled, labored, worked


词性：动词

PHRASE->
disport oneself = 自娱自乐
disport in = 在...中玩乐
disport with = 玩弄...
disport among = 在...之间玩耍
disport upon = 在...上玩乐


词源:
From Old French desporter "to divert, amuse oneself," from des- "away" + porter "to carry," from Latin portare.  The sense shifted from "carrying oneself away" (to a place of amusement) to the amusement itself.
