hublike:/ˈhʌblaɪk/| adj.|中心枢纽似的，像轮轴的|Resembling a hub; having a central point from which things radiate or connect.  The word combines "hub" (a central point of activity, connection, or distribution) with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance.|例句：The city's transportation system is hublike, with radial roads extending from the central station.  这个城市的交通系统像个中心枢纽，放射状的道路从中央车站延伸出去。

例句：The company's organizational structure is hublike, with the CEO at the center and various departments branching out. 公司的组织结构像个中心枢纽，首席执行官位于中心，各部门从中分支出来。


近义词：centralized, radial, networked

反义词：decentralized, dispersed, scattered

词性：形容词
