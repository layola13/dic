curley:/ˈkɜːrli/| adj.|卷曲的，蓬乱的|Having curls or a curly appearance.|His hair was curley and dark.|近义词：wavy, frizzy, kinky|反义词：straight, smooth


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The curls are curly. = 卷发是卷曲的。
His hair is curly. = 他的头发是卷曲的。

一般过去时:
Her hair was curly when she was young. = 她年轻的时候头发是卷曲的。
The wool was curly. = 那羊毛是卷曲的。

现在完成时:
His hair has been curly all his life. = 他一辈子头发都是卷曲的。
The yarn has remained curly after washing. = 洗完后纱线仍然是卷曲的。

过去完成时:
Her hair had been curly before she straightened it. = 在她把头发拉直之前，她的头发是卷曲的。
The rope had been curly before it was stretched. = 绳子在拉直之前是卷曲的。

将来时:
His hair will likely remain curly. = 他的头发可能会保持卷曲。
The wool will be curly after processing. = 加工后羊毛将是卷曲的。

虚拟语气:
If his hair were curly, he would look better. = 如果他的头发是卷曲的，他会更好看。
I wish my hair were curly. = 我希望我的头发是卷曲的。

The yarn is made curly by special treatment. = 纱线通过特殊处理而变得卷曲。
His hair was styled curly for the party. = 他的头发为了参加派对而被烫成了卷发。

疑问句:
Is your hair curly? = 你的头发是卷曲的吗？
Are those curls naturally curly? = 那些卷发是自然卷吗？

否定句:
His hair isn't curly; it's straight. = 他的头发不是卷曲的，是直的。
The wool isn't curly enough for this project. = 这羊毛对于这个项目来说不够卷曲。
