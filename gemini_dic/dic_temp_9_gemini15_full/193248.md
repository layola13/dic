seeded:/ˈsiːdɪd/| adj. v. |播种的；已播种的；(比赛)种子选手；| 英文解析：Past participle of seed.  As an adjective, it describes something that has had seeds planted in it or is designated as a top-ranked competitor. As a verb, it means to plant seeds or to select top-ranked competitors. |例句：The field was seeded with wheat.  这块地播种了小麦。

The seeded players advanced to the next round. 种子选手晋级下一轮。|近义词：sown (adj.), planted (v.), sown (v.)|反义词：harvested (adj.), unseeded (adj.)
