disassembler:/ˌdɪs.əˈsɛm.blər/| n. |反汇编器|A disassembler is a computer program that translates machine code into assembly language.  It performs the reverse operation of an assembler.  Disassemblers are commonly used in reverse engineering, software analysis, and debugging. |Example:  The programmer used a disassembler to understand the functionality of the compiled code.  程序员使用反汇编器来理解编译代码的功能。|近义词：decompiler (partially), debugger|反义词：assembler
