overshadowingly: /ˌoʊvərˈʃædoʊɪŋli/ | adv. | 极其地；压倒性地；遮蔽地 | Extremely; to a degree that overshadows other things.  The word implies a dominance or significance that surpasses other aspects. |例句：The beauty of the sunset was overshadowedingly magnificent.  日落的美景极其壮丽。

The success of the project was overshadowedingly positive, exceeding all expectations.  项目的成功极其显著，超出了所有预期。

近义词：overwhelmingly, incredibly, extraordinarily, remarkably

反义词：insignificantly, negligibly, marginally


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and thus does not conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Its usage is primarily descriptive, modifying the adjective or verb it precedes.  Examples using different sentence structures:


Affirmative Sentence:
The evidence was overshadowedingly compelling.  证据极其令人信服。
His contribution to the team was overshadowedingly important. 他对团队的贡献极其重要。


Negative Sentence:
The argument was not overshadowedingly convincing.  论点并非极其令人信服。
Her performance, while good, was not overshadowedingly impressive. 她的表现虽然不错，但并不极其令人印象深刻。


Interrogative Sentence:
Was the impact of the news overshadowedingly negative? 新闻的影响极其负面吗？
Is this overshadowedingly difficult task achievable?  这项极其困难的任务能够完成吗？


Exclamatory Sentence:
Overshadowingly beautiful! 极美！
Overshadowingly successful! 极度成功！


PHRASE->
There are no common set phrases that directly include "overshadowingly."  Its use is more as a standalone adverb to intensify other words.
