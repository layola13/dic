merchandizing: /ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 商品销售，商品化，商品策划 | The planning and execution of the marketing of goods, including presentation, promotion, and sales.  It involves all aspects of getting a product from the manufacturer to the consumer. |  例句：The company's successful merchandizing campaign boosted sales significantly. 这家公司成功的商品销售活动显著地提高了销量。

例句：Effective merchandizing is crucial for attracting customers and increasing revenue.有效的商品策划对于吸引顾客和增加收入至关重要。


近义词：marketing, sales promotion, product management | 反义词：N/A | 词性：名词
