loss-leaders:/ˈlɒsˌliːdər/| n.|亏本商品，赔本促销商品|Goods or services sold at a price below their cost to stimulate sales of other, more profitable goods or services.|Supermarkets often use loss-leaders to attract customers, such as selling milk at a loss to encourage purchases of other groceries.|近义词：price leader, promotional item|反义词：high-profit item
