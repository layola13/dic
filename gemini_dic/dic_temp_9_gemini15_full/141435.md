ophthalmometer:/ˌɒfθælˈmɒmɪtər/| n. |眼压计，眼球计|An instrument for measuring the curvature of the cornea of the eye.  It is used in optometry and ophthalmology to help determine the refractive error of the eye.|例句：The ophthalmologist used an ophthalmometer to measure the curvature of my cornea.  眼科医生用眼压计测量了我角膜的曲率。|近义词：keratometer|反义词:|无
