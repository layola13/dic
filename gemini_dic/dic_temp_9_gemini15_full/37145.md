come-to:/kʌmˈtuː/| v.|苏醒，恢复知觉；达成协议；开始理解|To regain consciousness; to reach an agreement; to begin to understand.|例句：He came to after the accident. 他事故后苏醒过来了。 She came to understand his motives. 她开始理解他的动机。|近义词：recover, regain consciousness, understand, realize|反义词：lose consciousness, remain unconscious


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He comes to every morning at 7 am. = 他每天早上7点醒来。
They come to an agreement after a long negotiation. = 经过长时间的谈判后，他们达成了协议。


一般过去时:
She came to after a few minutes. = 她几分钟后苏醒了。
The two parties came to a compromise. = 双方达成了妥协。


现在完成时:
I have come to realize the importance of hard work. = 我已经意识到努力工作的重要性。
They have come to a decision about the project. = 他们已经就项目做出了决定。


过去完成时:
He had come to understand his mistake before he apologized. = 他在道歉之前已经意识到了自己的错误。
The team had come to a standstill before the new manager arrived. = 新经理到来之前，团队已经陷入僵局。


将来时:
She will come to understand the truth eventually. = 她最终会明白真相的。
We hope they will come to an amicable settlement. = 我们希望他们能达成友好协议。


虚拟语气:
If I were to come to a different conclusion, I would let you know. = 如果我得出不同的结论，我会让你知道的。
I wish I had come to this realization sooner. = 我希望我早点意识到这一点。


被动语态:
A decision was finally come to after much debate. = 经过多次辩论后，最终做出了决定。(注意：come to 在被动语态中使用较少)
No conclusion was come to at the meeting. = 会议上没有达成任何结论。(注意：come to 在被动语态中使用较少)


疑问句:
Did he come to quickly after the fall? = 他摔倒后很快就苏醒了吗？
Have they come to a decision yet? = 他们做出决定了吗？


否定句:
He didn't come to for hours after the surgery. = 手术后他几个小时都没苏醒。
They didn't come to an agreement on the price. = 他们没有就价格达成一致。


PHRASE->
come to a standstill = 停滞不前
come to terms with = 接受；习惯于
come to grips with = 认真处理；对付
come to light = 被发现；真相大白
come to life = 活跃起来
