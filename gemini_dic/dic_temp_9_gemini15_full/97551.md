ingrave:/ɪnˈɡreɪv/| vt.|雕刻，铭刻|To cut or carve words or designs into a hard surface, such as stone or metal.  This usually implies a permanent inscription.|例句：They ingraved his name on the tombstone. 他们在墓碑上刻了他的名字。

例句：The artist ingraved a delicate design onto the silver locket.艺术家在银质锁匙上雕刻了一个精致的设计。


近义词：carve, etch, emboss, inscribe|反义词：erase, obliterate|动词
