monocrat:/ˈmɒnəkræt/| n. |独裁者，专制统治者 |A monocrat is a ruler who governs with absolute power, without being subject to any constitutional or other limits.  They often rule by decree and suppress dissent. |Example: The monocrat ruled the country with an iron fist, silencing all opposition.  独裁者铁腕统治国家，压制一切反对声音。|近义词：autocrat, despot, tyrant |反义词：democrat
