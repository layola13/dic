sheetful: /ˈʃiːtful/ | n. | 一床的量；满满一床的量 | A quantity sufficient to fill a sheet or bed; a bedful.  The word implies a large or full amount. | Example: The laundry basket held a sheetful of dirty clothes. 洗衣篮里装满了脏衣服。(暗示数量很多) | 近义词: bedful, load, plenty | 反义词: handful (相对较少)


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations like "What".  The grammatical structures below are applicable to sentences *using* "sheetful".
