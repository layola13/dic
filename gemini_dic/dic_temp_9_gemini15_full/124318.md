motherlessness: /ˈmʌðərləsnəs/ | n. | 失去母亲；没有母亲的状态 | The state of being without a mother, either through death, abandonment, or other circumstances.  It often carries significant emotional and psychological implications for the individual. | 例句：The children experienced profound motherlessness after their mother's untimely death.  孩子们在母亲过早去世后经历了深刻的失去母亲的痛苦。

The profound motherlessness she felt shaped her personality. 她感受到的深深的失去母亲的痛苦塑造了她的性格。


近义词：orphanhood (if also fatherless), maternal deprivation | 反义词：motherhood, maternal presence
