methodize:/ˈmɛθədaɪz/ | vt. |使有条理；使系统化；用方法论处理|To arrange or organize something according to a system or method.  It implies bringing order and efficiency to a previously disorganized or chaotic situation. |例句：He methodized his approach to the project, breaking it down into manageable tasks. 他使他的项目方法井然有序，将其分解成易于管理的任务。

例句：She methodized her notes, creating a clear outline for her essay. 她整理了她的笔记，为她的论文创建了一个清晰的提纲。


近义词：systematize, organize, order, rationalize | 反义词：disorganize, randomize, confuse


词性：动词
