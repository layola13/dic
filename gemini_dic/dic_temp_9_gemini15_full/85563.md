hated-on:/ˈheɪtɪd ɑːn/| adj.|被憎恨的，受到攻击的|This term describes someone or something that is the target of widespread hatred or criticism.  It implies a sustained and often unfair or unwarranted attack.|例句：He's been hated-on by the media for his controversial views. 他因为其有争议的观点而受到媒体的攻击。 |例句：The new policy has been hated-on by the public. 新政策受到了公众的强烈反对。|近义词：vilified, attacked, criticized, condemned|反义词：loved, praised, adored, celebrated|形容词
