guanxi: /ˈɡwænʃiː/ | n. |关系；人脉；联系 |  The word "guanxi" (关系) is a Chinese term that refers to the complex system of social networks and relationships that are central to doing business and navigating life in Chinese culture. It encompasses the web of connections, obligations, and reciprocal favors that exist between individuals and groups.  Guanxi is built and maintained through trust, mutual benefit, and long-term commitment.  It's more than just networking; it’s about cultivating deep, meaningful connections based on reciprocity and shared understanding.  Successfully navigating guanxi often requires understanding social hierarchies, showing respect, and demonstrating trustworthiness. | 例句：Cultivating strong guanxi is crucial for success in Chinese business.  建立良好的人脉关系对在中国经商至关重要。
