fed:/fed/| v.| 喂养；供给；使吃饱；（使）充满；|to give food to a person or animal|例句：She fed her baby a bottle. 她用奶瓶喂她的宝宝。|例句：The farmer fed the cattle. 农民喂了牲口。|近义词： nourish, supply, satiate |反义词： starve, deprive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer feeds the chickens every morning. = 农民每天早上喂鸡。
He feeds his dog twice a day. = 他一天喂两次狗。

一般过去时:
I fed the cat last night. = 我昨晚喂了猫。
She fed the birds in the park. = 她在公园里喂鸟。

现在完成时:
I have fed the dog already. = 我已经喂过狗了。
They have fed the animals. = 他们已经喂了动物。

过去完成时:
She had fed the baby before she went to work. = 她上班前已经喂了宝宝。
They had fed the horses before the race. = 比赛前他们已经喂了马。

将来时:
I will feed the fish tomorrow. = 我明天将喂鱼。
He will feed the birds when he gets home. = 他回家后将喂鸟。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would feed the stray cats. = 如果我有更多时间，我会喂那些流浪猫。
I wish I had fed the dog earlier. = 我真希望我早点喂狗。

被动语态:
The animals are fed twice a day. = 动物一天喂两次。
The birds were fed by the children. = 鸟儿们是被孩子们喂的。

疑问句:
Do you feed your pets regularly? = 你定期喂养你的宠物吗？
Does she feed the rabbits every day? = 她每天都喂兔子吗？

否定句:
I didn't feed the dog this morning. = 我今天早上没有喂狗。
They haven't fed the chickens yet. = 他们还没喂鸡。


PHRASE->
fed up with: 对...感到厌倦
well-fed: 养尊处优的，吃得很好的
overfed: 喂得过饱的
fed-up: 厌倦的，不满的
be fed up: 厌倦
