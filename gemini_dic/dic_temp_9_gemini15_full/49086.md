deictic: /daɪkˈtɪk/ | adj. |指示的；指示性的 | Relating to or denoting a word or expression whose meaning is dependent on the context in which it is used (e.g., here, there, now, then).  Such words are called deictics and their meaning changes depending on who is speaking, where they are, and when they are speaking.  | The word "here" is deictic because its meaning depends on the speaker's location. |近义词：demonstrative, pointing |反义词：abstract, general


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This is my house. = 这是我的房子。
That is a beautiful tree. = 那是一棵美丽的树。


一般过去时:
I was there yesterday. = 我昨天在那里。
He pointed to that building over there. = 他指着那边的那栋建筑。


现在完成时:
I have been here since morning. = 我从早上就在这里了。
She has just left that place. = 她刚刚离开那个地方。


过去完成时:
I had already been there before you arrived. = 你到达之前我已经在那里了。
They had seen that movie before. = 他们之前看过那部电影了。


将来时:
I will be there tomorrow. = 我明天会在那里。
We will meet you at that cafe then. = 我们到时候在那家咖啡馆和你见面。


虚拟语气:
If I were there, I would help you. = 如果我在那里，我会帮你。
If it were now, we would go. = 如果是现在，我们就去了。


被动语态:
This matter was discussed here yesterday. = 这件事昨天在这里讨论过了。
That problem is being addressed now. = 那个问题正在得到解决。


疑问句:
Where are you going? = 你要去哪里？
When will you be here? = 你什么时候来这里？


否定句:
That is not my car. = 那不是我的车。
I was not there at that time. = 我当时不在那里。


PHRASE->
here and there = 到处
here and now = 现在这里
from here on = 从现在开始
there and back = 来回
over there = 在那边
