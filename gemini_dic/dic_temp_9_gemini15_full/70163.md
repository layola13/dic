fibbing:/ˈfɪbɪŋ/| v.|说谎，撒谎 | Telling a lie, especially a small or unimportant one.  |例句：He was fibbing about his age. 他在撒谎，关于他的年龄。|例句：Stop fibbing!  别撒谎了！|近义词：lying, prevaricating, fabricating |反义词：telling the truth, being honest |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is fibbing about his whereabouts. = 他正在撒谎，关于他的行踪。
They fib about their accomplishments. = 他们在吹嘘他们的成就。


一般过去时:
She fibbed to her parents about her grades. = 她对她父母撒谎了，关于她的成绩。
He fibbed to avoid punishment. = 他撒谎以避免惩罚。


现在完成时:
Have you been fibbing to me all along? = 你一直在骗我吗？
They have fibbed repeatedly and lost our trust. = 他们一再撒谎，失去了我们的信任。


过去完成时:
I realized he had been fibbing to me the whole time. = 我意识到他一直在骗我。
She had fibbed about her age for years. = 她多年来一直在谎报年龄。


将来时:
He will fib again if given the chance. = 如果有机会，他会再次撒谎。
They will probably fib to protect themselves. = 他们可能会撒谎来保护自己。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't fibbing, he would tell us the truth. = 如果他没有撒谎，他会告诉我们真相。
I wish she hadn't fibbed about the accident. = 我希望她没有对事故撒谎。


被动语态:
The truth was fibbed about by many witnesses. = 真相被许多证人隐瞒了。（被动语态中"fib"用得较少，此句意为真相被歪曲）
The matter was glossed over with fibs. = 这件事被谎言掩盖了。


疑问句:
Is he fibbing, or is he telling the truth? = 他是在撒谎，还是在说实话？
Are they fibbing to get out of trouble? = 他们在撒谎以逃避麻烦吗？


否定句:
He wasn't fibbing; he was simply mistaken. = 他不是在撒谎；他只是弄错了。
She didn't fib; she told the whole truth. = 她没有撒谎；她说了全部的真相。


PHRASE->
fib a little = 稍微撒个小谎
a little white fib = 善意的谎言
fib one's way out of something = 撒谎摆脱困境
fib about something = 就某事撒谎
tell a fib = 撒谎
