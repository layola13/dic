ploughwright:/ˈplaʊraɪt/| n. |犁匠，制犁人 |A ploughwright is a person who makes or repairs ploughs.  Ploughwrights were traditionally skilled craftsmen with a deep understanding of wood, metal, and the mechanics of plowing. Their work was essential to agriculture. |The old ploughwright meticulously repaired the broken ploughshare.  这位老犁匠一丝不苟地修理着破损的犁铧。|craftsman, artisan| (no direct antonym, but perhaps) unskilled laborer
