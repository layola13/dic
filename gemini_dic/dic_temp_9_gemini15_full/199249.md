siltations:/sɪlˈteɪʃənz/ | n. |淤积；泥沙淤积 |The process of depositing silt, especially in a river or harbor.  It refers to the accumulation of silt, a sedimentary material composed of fine sand, clay, and other mineral particles. Siltation can significantly impact waterways, reducing their depth and affecting navigation, aquatic life, and water quality. |例句：The siltation of the river is a major environmental concern. 河流淤积是一个主要的环保问题。

The excessive siltation caused the dam to fail. 过度的淤积导致大坝垮塌。


近义词：sedimentation, silting |反义词：dredging (removing silt)


词性：名词
