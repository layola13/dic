lavenders:/ˈlævəndərz/ | n. |薰衣草（复数） |The word "lavenders" is the plural form of "lavender," referring to multiple plants of the genus Lavandula or their flowers.  It is used to describe a collection of lavender plants or the collective scent of lavender.  |Example: The fields were filled with beautiful lavenders. 薰衣草田里开满了美丽的薰衣草。 The soap has a delicate scent of lavenders. 这块肥皂散发着淡淡的薰衣草香味。|近义词: lavender plants, lavender flowers |反义词:  (no direct antonym, but contrasting scents could be mentioned:  rose, jasmine)|名词
