inmesh:/ɪnˈmɛʃ/ | vt. |使纠缠；使陷入；使卷入 | To involve someone or something in a difficult or complicated situation. | 例句：The scandal inmeshed the mayor in controversy.  丑闻使市长卷入争议。

例句：The investigation inmeshed several high-ranking officials.  调查牵涉到几位高级官员。


近义词：entangle, involve, implicate, ensnare | 反义词： disentangle, extricate, free


词性:动词
