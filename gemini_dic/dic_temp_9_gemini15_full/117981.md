memorandum:/ˌmeməˈrændəm/| n. |备忘录；便笺；记录 |A memorandum is a written record or communication, typically one dealing with business matters.  It's often used for internal communication within an organization. | Example:  The manager sent a memorandum outlining the new company policy.  经理发送了一份备忘录，概述了新的公司政策。|近义词：note, memo, reminder |反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team sends a memorandum every week. = 团队每周发送一份备忘录。
She writes a memorandum to clarify the issue. = 她写了一份备忘录来澄清这个问题。

一般过去时:
They sent a memorandum last Friday. = 他们上周五发送了一份备忘录。
He wrote a memorandum explaining the situation. = 他写了一份备忘录解释了情况。

现在完成时:
We have sent several memoranda this month. = 这个月我们已经发送了几份备忘录。
She has received the memorandum and responded. = 她已经收到了备忘录并作出了回应。

过去完成时:
They had already sent the memorandum before the meeting. = 会议之前他们已经发送了备忘录。
He had drafted the memorandum but hadn't sent it yet. = 他已经起草了备忘录，但还没有发送。

将来时:
We will send a memorandum next week. = 我们下周将发送一份备忘录。
She will prepare a memorandum for the board meeting. = 她将为董事会会议准备一份备忘录。

虚拟语气:
If I had written a memorandum, the confusion would have been avoided. = 如果我写了一份备忘录，混乱就能避免了。
It is important that the memorandum be sent promptly. = 及时发送备忘录很重要。

被动语态:
The memorandum was sent yesterday. = 备忘录昨天发送了。
The new policy is outlined in the memorandum. = 新政策在备忘录中概述。

疑问句:
Has the memorandum been received? = 备忘录收到了吗？
When will the memorandum be sent? = 备忘录什么时候发送？

否定句:
The memorandum was not sent on time. = 备忘录没有按时发送。
She did not receive the memorandum. = 她没有收到备忘录。
