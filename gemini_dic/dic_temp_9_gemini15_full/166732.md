presidential: /ˌprɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl/ | adj. |总统的；总统职务的；与总统相关的 | Relating to or characteristic of a president or the office of president.  |例句：The presidential campaign is heating up.  总统竞选活动正在升温。

例句：He delivered a presidential address to the nation. 他向全国发表了总统讲话。


近义词：presidentially,  executive (in some contexts)


反义词：subordinate,  non-presidential


词性：形容词

The presidential debate is televised nationally. = 总统辩论在全国进行电视转播。
Presidential decisions often affect millions of people. = 总统的决定往往影响数百万人民。

词源:
From President +‎ -ial.  The word "president" itself comes from the Latin *praesidens, present participle of praesidere "to preside," from prae- "before" + sedēre "to sit."  The suffix "-ial" is a common adjective-forming suffix deriving from the Latin "-alis".
