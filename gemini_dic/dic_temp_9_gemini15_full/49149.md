dejectednesses:/dɪˈdʒɛktɪdnɪsɪz/| n.|沮丧的状态，消沉，意志消沉|The state of being dejected; low spirits; depression.|例句：Her dejectednesses were palpable, evident in her slumped posture and downcast eyes.  她的沮丧显而易见，从她无精打采的姿势和低垂的目光中就能看出。

例句：The prolonged rain and the subsequent cancellation of the picnic only added to their dejectednesses.  持续的雨水和随后野餐的取消只会加剧他们的沮丧。


近义词：despondency, dejection, depression, discouragement, low spirits|反义词：elation, exhilaration, cheerfulness, optimism|词性:名词
