cryptogram:/ˈkrɪptoʊɡræm/ | n. |密码；隐语；密码电报 | A cryptogram is a message written in code or cipher; a puzzle that needs to be deciphered.  It can also refer to a coded message itself, regardless of the method used. | Example: The detective spent hours trying to solve the cryptogram left by the thief.  侦探花了几个小时试图解开小偷留下的密码。  The ancient scroll contained a complex cryptogram that baffled scholars for centuries.  古老的卷轴包含一个复杂的密码，困扰了学者几个世纪。 |近义词：cipher, code, secret message |反义词：plaintext, cleartext
