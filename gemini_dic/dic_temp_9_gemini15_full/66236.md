exclaimers:/ɪkˈskleɪmərz/| n.|感叹词|Exclamations are words or phrases used to express strong emotion.  They are often interjections and are punctuated with an exclamation mark (!).|Examples: "Wow!", "Ouch!", "Help!"  These are all exclaimers expressing surprise, pain, and need for assistance, respectively.|近义词：interjections, cries, shouts|反义词：None (as it's a class of words, not a word with an opposite meaning).
