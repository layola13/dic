men:/men/| n.|男人们，人们|The plural form of "man," referring to adult male humans.  It can also be used more generally to refer to people, though this usage is less common and can sometimes be considered sexist.|例句：The men were working hard.  男人们正在努力工作。|例句：The men and women of the village gathered. 村庄里的男人和女人们聚集在一起。|近义词：males, fellows, guys|反义词：women
