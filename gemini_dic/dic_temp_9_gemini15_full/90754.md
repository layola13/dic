hummus:/ˈhʌməs/| n.|鹰嘴豆泥|A creamy dip or spread made from cooked, mashed chickpeas (or other beans), tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and olive oil.  It is a popular food in the Middle East and Mediterranean regions.|例句：I love to eat hummus with pita bread. 我喜欢用皮塔饼吃鹰嘴豆泥。|例句：The restaurant is known for its delicious hummus and falafel. 这家餐馆以其美味的鹰嘴豆泥和法拉费而闻名。|近义词：dip, spread|反义词：无

词源:
The word "hummus" is derived from the Arabic word "ḥummus" (حمص), which means "chickpea".  The word ultimately derives from the ancient Egyptian word for chickpea. The dish itself has ancient roots in the Levant region.
