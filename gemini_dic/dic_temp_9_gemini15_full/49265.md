delibar:  /ˈdɛlɪbər/ | v. | 深思熟虑；仔细考虑 | To think carefully and thoroughly about something before making a decision. |例句：He deliberated for a long time before making his decision. 他在做决定之前仔细考虑了很久。|近义词：consider, ponder, contemplate, reflect |反义词：rush, hurry


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They deliberate the matter carefully. = 他们仔细考虑这件事。
We deliberate on the best course of action. = 我们仔细权衡最佳行动方案。

一般过去时:
The jury deliberated for hours before reaching a verdict. = 陪审团经过数小时的商议才做出裁决。
She deliberated over the pros and cons of each option. = 她仔细权衡了每个选项的利弊。

现在完成时:
They have deliberated on the proposal for weeks. = 他们已经考虑这个提案好几个星期了。
We have deliberated and reached a consensus. = 我们已经仔细考虑并达成共识。

过去完成时:
After having deliberated for days, they finally made a decision. = 经过几天的仔细考虑后，他们最终做出了决定。
She had deliberated extensively before accepting the offer. = 在接受这个提议之前，她已做了充分的考虑。

将来时:
The committee will deliberate on the budget next week. = 委员会下周将审议预算。
We will deliberate the issue further before taking action. = 我们将在采取行动之前进一步审议此事。

虚拟语气:
If we had deliberated more carefully, we might have made a better decision. = 如果我们当时考虑得更仔细一些，我们可能会做出更好的决定。
I wish I had deliberated more before signing the contract. = 我真希望在签合同之前我考虑得更周全一些。

被动语态:
The matter was deliberated extensively by the board. = 这件事受到了董事会的广泛讨论。
The proposal is being deliberated by the committee. = 委员会正在审议该提案。

疑问句:
Have they deliberated the options thoroughly? = 他们是否已经彻底考虑了各种选择？
Will the council deliberate the new regulations? = 理事会将审议新规吗？

否定句:
He did not deliberate sufficiently before acting. = 他在行动之前没有充分考虑。
They didn't deliberate the implications of their decision. = 他们没有仔细考虑他们决定的影响。
