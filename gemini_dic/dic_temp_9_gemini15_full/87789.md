hibernator:/ˈhaɪbərˌneɪtər/| n. |冬眠动物，休眠者 |An animal that hibernates; a person or thing that is inactive for a period.  |Example: Bears are well-known hibernators.  熊是众所周知的冬眠动物。|近义词：dormant creature, inactive being |反义词：active animal, summer-active creature
