fixability: /ˌfɪksəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可修复性，可修理性 | The ability to be fixed or repaired.  It refers to the ease with which something can be mended or restored to its original condition.  This term often applies to physical objects, systems, or situations. |

例句：

The fixability of the software is a major concern for users.  软件的可修复性是用户的主要担忧。

The high fixability of this car model makes it a popular choice. 这款汽车的高可修复性使其成为热门之选。


近义词：repairability, mendability, rectifiability

反义词：irreparability, unfixability


词性：名词
