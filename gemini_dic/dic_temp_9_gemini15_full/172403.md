puzzling: /ˈpʌzəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人费解的；使人困惑的 | causing confusion or difficulty; perplexing. | The instructions were quite puzzling. | 近义词：confusing, perplexing, baffling, mystifying | 反义词：clear, simple, understandable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The puzzle is puzzling me. = 这个谜题让我很困惑。
His behavior is puzzling to everyone. = 他的行为让每个人都很困惑。


一般过去时:
The riddle was puzzling. = 这个谜语令人费解。
The situation was quite puzzling at the time. = 当时的情况相当令人费解。


现在完成时:
The case has been puzzling investigators for months. = 这个案件已经让调查人员困惑了几个月。
This problem has been puzzling me all day. =  这个问题困扰了我一整天。


过去完成时:
I had been puzzling over the problem for hours before I found the solution. = 在找到解决方案之前，我已经苦思冥想这个问题几个小时了。
The detective had been puzzling over the clues for days. = 侦探已经苦思线索好几天了。


将来时:
