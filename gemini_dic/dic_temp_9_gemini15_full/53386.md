disagreeing:/ˌdɪsəˈɡriːɪŋ/| adj.|不一致的；有分歧的|Expressing or characterized by a difference of opinion.|The twins were disagreeing about which toy to play with.  双胞胎在争论玩哪个玩具。|The committee members were in constant disagreement. 委员会成员意见持续不一。|agreeing, concurring|agreeing, concurring

USAGE->

一般现在时:
They are disagreeing about the best course of action.  他们在对最佳行动方案有分歧。
We are constantly disagreeing on minor issues. 我们经常在一些小问题上意见不合。


一般过去时:
They disagreed about the details of the plan. 他们对计划的细节意见不一。
He disagreed with her assessment of the situation. 他不同意她对局势的评估。


现在完成时:
They have disagreed on this matter for years.  他们在这个问题上已经争论多年了。
We have disagreed several times this week.  我们本周已经几次意见不合了。


过去完成时:
They had disagreed before the meeting even began. 会议还没开始他们就已经意见不合了。
She had disagreed with his methods in the past. 她过去曾不同意他的方法。


将来时:
They will disagree on the budget allocation.  他们将在预算分配上产生分歧。
I think we will disagree on this point. 我认为我们在这个问题上会有分歧。


虚拟语气:
If they disagreed, they would have to find a compromise. 如果他们意见不一，他们就必须找到一个折衷方案。
I wish they wouldn't disagree so much. 我希望他们不要总是意见不合。


被动语态:
The proposal was disagreed with by several members.  这项提案遭到几位成员的反对。
His ideas were constantly disagreed upon. 他的想法总是遭到反对。


疑问句:
Are they disagreeing again? 他们又在意见不合了吗？
Were they disagreeing about the project?  他们对这个项目有分歧吗？


否定句:
They didn't disagree on the main points.  他们在主要问题上没有分歧。
We don't disagree on the importance of the issue. 我们对这个问题的重要性没有异议。


PHRASE->
be disagreeing with someone: 与某人意见不合
in disagreement with: 与……不一致
a point of disagreement: 分歧点
a disagreement over: 关于……的争论
reach a disagreement: 达成一致意见(反义用法)
