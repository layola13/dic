It implies going beyond the point of reasonable stimulation or challenge. |例句：The overprovocation of the bear led to a dangerous attack.  熊的过度挑衅导致了危险的袭击。

例句：His constant overprovocation of his colleagues created a hostile work environment. 他不断地过度挑衅同事，造成了一种敌对的工作环境。
