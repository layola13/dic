cinnamon:/ˈsɪnəmən/| n.|肉桂；肉桂粉|A spice made from the inner bark of several trees of the genus Cinnamomum, having a warm, sweet aroma and a pungent, sweet taste. It is used extensively in both sweet and savory dishes, and also in perfumes and medicine.|例句：The aroma of cinnamon filled the kitchen. 肉桂的香味充满了厨房。

例句：She added a dash of cinnamon to her coffee. 她往咖啡里加了一点肉桂粉。|近义词：cassia, spice|反义词：无

词源:
The word "cinnamon" comes from the Old French "canelle," which in turn is derived from the Latin "canna," meaning "reed" or "cane," referring to the shape of the cinnamon bark.  The ultimate origin is likely from the Greek word κιναμωμον (kinamōmon).
