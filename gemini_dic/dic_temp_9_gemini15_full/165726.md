prenebular: /ˌpriːˈnɛbjələr/ | adj. |  星云前的，星云之前的 |  Relating to the period of time before the formation of a star system from a nebula.  It describes the conditions and processes that existed before a nebula collapsed to form stars and planets. | Example:  Prenebular conditions are thought to have played a crucial role in determining the composition of planetary systems. 星云前的条件被认为在决定行星系统的组成方面起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: protostellar (早期恒星的) | 反义词: post-nebular (星云之后的) |  无
