canoeist:/kəˈnuːɪst/| n. |独木舟运动员，划独木舟的人|A person who canoes.  A canoeist needs skill and balance to navigate the rapids.|例句：The canoeist paddled skillfully through the white water.  独木舟运动员熟练地划桨穿过激流。

The experienced canoeist effortlessly navigated the challenging currents.  经验丰富的独木舟运动员轻松地驾驭了充满挑战的水流。|近义词：paddler, kayaker |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who uses a different watercraft.
