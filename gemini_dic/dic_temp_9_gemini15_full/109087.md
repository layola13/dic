legators:/ˈlɛɡeɪtərz/ | n. |遗嘱执行人，立遗嘱者|A legator is a person who leaves a legacy in a will.  They are the testator or testatrix who makes the will and leaves behind bequests.|The legator left a significant sum of money to the local library in their will.|近义词：testator, testatrix |反义词：beneficiary
