certify:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪ/ | vt. |证明；证实；批准；发证书 |To confirm or guarantee something to be true, genuine, or of a specified standard.  |例句：The laboratory certified that the water was safe to drink.  实验室证明水可以安全饮用。

例句：The organization certified the quality of the product.  该组织认证了产品的质量。


近义词：verify, validate, confirm, attest |反义词：deny, refute, disprove |词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He certifies the accuracy of the data. 他证明数据的准确性。
The company certifies its products to meet safety standards.  这家公司认证其产品符合安全标准。


一般过去时:
The doctor certified the cause of death. 医生证明了死因。
They certified the document as authentic.  他们证明文件是真实的。


现在完成时:
The lab has certified the results of the tests. 实验室已经认证了测试结果。
She has certified her expertise in the field. 她已经证明她在该领域的专业知识。


过去完成时:
The committee had certified the plan before the meeting. 委员会在会议前已经批准了该计划。
He had certified the documents as genuine before submitting them. 他在提交文件之前已经证明了文件是真实的。


将来时:
The authority will certify the building's compliance with regulations. 当局将证明该建筑符合法规。
They will certify the election results once the counting is complete.  一旦计票完成，他们将认证选举结果。


虚拟语气:
If the expert certified the findings, we would be able to proceed. 如果专家证明了这些发现，我们就能继续进行。
I wish the committee had certified the proposal earlier. 我希望委员会早点批准这项提案。


被动语态:
The documents were certified by the notary public. 文件由公证人证明。
The product is certified to meet international standards. 产品经认证符合国际标准。


疑问句:
Has the shipment been certified as safe?  这批货物是否已证明安全？
Will the results be certified before the deadline?  结果会在截止日期前得到认证吗？


否定句:
The judge did not certify the witness's testimony.  法官没有证明证人的证词。
The company failed to certify its products, resulting in a recall.  该公司未能对其产品进行认证，导致产品召回。


PHRASE->
certified copy:  经认证的副本
certified mail: 挂号信
certified check: 保证支票
certified public accountant (CPA): 注册会计师
certified organic:  认证有机


词源:
From Old French *certifier (from Latin certificare, from certus "sure" + facere "to make").
