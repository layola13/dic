oversweetnesses: /ˌoʊvərˈswiːtnəsɪz/ | n. | 过甜；甜得过分 | The excessive sweetness of something; instances or amounts of excessive sweetness.  |例句：The oversweetnesses in the dessert almost made me sick. 甜点甜得过分，差点让我吃吐了。


The oversweetnesses of the candy overwhelmed the subtle flavors of the fruit. 糖果甜得过分，盖过了水果的微妙风味。


近义词：excessive sweetness, cloying sweetness, saccharine excesses | 反义词：lack of sweetness, blandness, tartness


词性: 名词


词源:

* **over-**:  prefix indicating excess or to a greater degree. (来自表示超过或程度更大的前缀)
* **sweet-**:  relating to the taste sensation of sweetness. (与甜味感觉相关的)
* **-ness**: suffix forming abstract nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition. (构成抽象名词的后缀，表示状态、品质或状况)
* **-es**: plural suffix for nouns ending in -s, -sh, -ch, -x, or -z. (名词复数后缀，用于以-s，-sh，-ch，-x或-z结尾的名词)


因此，"oversweetnesses"  是 "sweetness" (甜度) 的复数形式，并由前缀 "over-"  强调了甜度的过量。
