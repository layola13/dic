niche:/niːʃ/ | n. |生态位；合适的职位；专门市场；壁龛|A specialized segment of the market for a particular kind of product or service.|The company found a niche in the market for organic food. 这家公司在有机食品市场找到了一个利基市场。|近义词：specialty, recess, alcove |反义词：mainstream, general


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His company occupies a niche market. = 他的公司占据着一个利基市场。
This product fills a specific niche. = 此产品填补了一个特定的市场空白。


一般过去时:
They successfully carved out a niche for themselves. = 他们成功地为自己开辟了一个利基市场。
The artist found her niche in portrait painting. =这位艺术家在肖像画中找到了自己的位置。


现在完成时:
The company has established a strong niche in the industry. = 该公司已在业内建立了强大的市场地位。
The product has filled a previously unfilled niche. = 该产品填补了以前未被填补的市场空白。


过去完成时:
Before the innovation, the company hadn't found its niche. = 在创新之前，该公司还没有找到自己的定位。
They had already established their niche in the market before the competitor arrived. = 在竞争对手到来之前，他们已经在市场上确立了自己的地位。


将来时:
They will create a new niche in the market with their innovative product. = 他们将凭借其创新产品在市场上创造一个新的利基市场。
The company hopes to expand into new niches in the future. =该公司希望将来扩展到新的利基市场。


虚拟语气:
If they had a better marketing strategy, they would have found their niche sooner. = 如果他们有更好的营销策略，他们本可以更快地找到自己的定位。
I wish I had found my niche earlier in my career. = 我希望在我的职业生涯早期就能找到自己的定位。


被动语态:
A niche was created by the demand for specialized products. = 对专业产品的需求创造了一个利基市场。
The market niche is being filled by a growing number of startups. = 越来越多的初创公司正在填补市场空白。


疑问句:
What niche does your company occupy? = 贵公司占据哪个利基市场？
Does this product have a niche in the market? = 此产品在市场上是否有利基市场？


否定句:
The product didn't find its niche in the market. = 该产品没有在市场上找到自己的定位。
They failed to establish a niche for their product. = 他们未能为其产品建立一个利基市场。


PHRASE->
find one's niche = 找到自己的定位
a niche market = 利基市场
fill a niche = 填补市场空白
market niche = 市场细分
occupy a niche = 占据一个生态位/市场地位
