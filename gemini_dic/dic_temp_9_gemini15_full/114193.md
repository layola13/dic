maiden:/ˈmeɪdn/| n. adj.|少女；处女；未婚女子；初次；最初的|A young woman who has not been married;  something that is the first of its kind.|例句：She was a maiden of great beauty. 她是一位非常美丽的少女。|例句：This is the maiden voyage of the ship. 这是这艘船的处女航。|近义词：girl, virgin, unmarried, first, initial|反义词：married, experienced, veteran
