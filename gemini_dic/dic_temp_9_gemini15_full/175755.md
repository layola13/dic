raptors:/ˈræptərz/| n.|猛禽|A raptor is a bird of prey, typically a diurnal bird of the order Falconiformes, such as an eagle, hawk, falcon, or kite, that has sharp talons and a hooked beak.|例句：Eagles and hawks are examples of raptors.  老鹰和鹰是猛禽的例子。|例句：The documentary showcased the hunting techniques of various raptors. 这部纪录片展示了各种猛禽的狩猎技巧。|近义词：birds of prey, predatory birds|反义词：prey
