newsroom:/ˈnjuːzruːm/ | n. |新闻编辑室|A room in a television or radio station, or a newspaper office, where journalists work.  It is the place where news is gathered, written, and prepared for broadcast or publication.|例句：The newsroom was buzzing with activity as the breaking news story unfolded.  新闻编辑室里一片忙碌，因为突发新闻正在发生。

例句：Reporters rushed to the newsroom to file their stories before the deadline.  记者们赶到新闻编辑室，在截止日期前提交他们的稿件。


近义词：press room, news bureau, editorial office | 反义词：None (no direct antonym)
