eyeleteer:/ˈaɪˌlɪtɪər/| n. |打眼工人，装金属环工人|A person who inserts eyelets (small metal rings) into materials such as leather, fabric, or paper.  Eyeleteers use specialized tools to create holes and insert the eyelets, often working in factories or workshops. |例句：The eyeleteer skillfully punched holes and inserted the eyelets into the leather belt. 打眼工人熟练地打孔并将金属环插入皮带。|近义词：eyelet maker, grommet installer|反义词：无
