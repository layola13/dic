postmatch: /ˈpoʊstˌmætʃ/ | adj. | 赛后的 | Relating to or occurring after a match or game.  This adjective describes something that happens after a sporting contest or competition.  |例句：The postmatch interview revealed the winning team's strategy. 赛后采访揭示了获胜球队的策略。

例句：The postmatch analysis showed several key errors in the losing team's play. 赛后分析显示输球球队在比赛中犯了几个关键错误。


近义词：after-match, postgame


反义词：prematch, pregame
