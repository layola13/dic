dunking:/ˈdʌŋkɪŋ/| n. vt.|浸，蘸；灌篮|The act of submerging something in a liquid, or forcefully throwing a basketball through the hoop.  It can also refer to the act of immersing oneself in a situation or activity.|例句：He dunked the donut in his coffee. 他把甜甜圈浸在咖啡里。|例句： LeBron James is famous for his powerful dunking. 勒布朗·詹姆斯以其强大的灌篮而闻名。|近义词：dipping, immersing, plunging (for submerging); slamming (for basketball)|反义词：lifting, raising (for submerging); missing (for basketball)|动词
