discussed:/dɪˈskʌst/| v. |讨论过的；商讨过的 |To talk about something with someone else, often in order to make a decision or to exchange ideas. |例句：We discussed the problem at length. 我们详细讨论了这个问题。|近义词：talked about, debated, considered |反义词：ignored, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They discuss the project every week. = 他们每周讨论这个项目。
We discuss important matters in our meetings. = 我们在会议上讨论重要的事情。


一般过去时:
They discussed the budget yesterday. = 他们昨天讨论了预算。
We discussed the plan for the party last night. = 我们昨晚讨论了聚会的计划。


现在完成时:
They have discussed the proposal several times. = 他们已经讨论过这个提案好几次了。
We have discussed this issue many times before. = 我们之前已经多次讨论过这个问题了。


过去完成时:
They had discussed the terms before signing the contract. = 他们在签合同之前已经讨论过条款了。
We had discussed the implications thoroughly before making a decision. = 我们在做决定之前已经彻底讨论过其影响了。


将来时:
They will discuss the matter further tomorrow. = 他们明天将进一步讨论此事。
We will discuss the new strategy at the next meeting. = 我们将在下一次会议上讨论新的策略。


虚拟语气:
If they had discussed the problem earlier, they might have found a solution. = 如果他们早点讨论这个问题，他们可能已经找到了解决方案。
I wish they had discussed the consequences more carefully. = 我希望他们更仔细地讨论过后果。


被动语态:
The matter was discussed at the last meeting. = 上次会议讨论了此事。
The new policy is being discussed by the committee. = 委员会正在讨论这项新政策。


疑问句:
Did they discuss the deadline? = 他们讨论了截止日期吗？
Have they discussed the potential risks? = 他们讨论过潜在的风险吗？


否定句:
They didn't discuss the details. = 他们没有讨论细节。
We haven't discussed the next steps yet. = 我们还没有讨论下一步的步骤。


PHRASE->
discuss the matter = 讨论此事
discuss a topic = 讨论一个话题
discuss the issues = 讨论这些问题
discuss at length = 详细讨论
discuss thoroughly = 彻底讨论
