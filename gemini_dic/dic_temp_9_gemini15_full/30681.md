certain: /ˈsɜːrtn/ /ˈsɜːrtn/ | adj. | 确定的；无疑的；某种；特定的 |

英文解析：Having no doubt; sure.  Also used to mean a particular but unspecified thing or person.

例句：
I'm certain he'll succeed. = 我确信他会成功。

I have a certain feeling that something is wrong. = 我有一种不好的预感。


近义词：sure, definite, positive, confident, specific

反义词：doubtful, uncertain, unsure, dubious


词性：形容词
