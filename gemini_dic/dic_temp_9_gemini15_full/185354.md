retroanalytical: /ˌrɛtroʊænəˈlɪtɪkəl/ | adj. | 逆向分析的，反向分析的 |  Relating to or using a method of analysis that works backward from a known result to determine the preceding events or causes.  It involves tracing back a process or sequence of events to understand its origin or development. | Example:  Retroanalytical studies are crucial in forensic science to reconstruct the sequence of events leading to a crime.  逆向分析研究对于法医学重建导致犯罪的一系列事件至关重要。 | 近义词：backwards, reverse, inverse | 反义词：prospective, forward | 词性:形容词
