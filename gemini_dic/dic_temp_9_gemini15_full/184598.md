restow:/ˈrɛstoʊ/|v.|恢复，重建|To restore something to its former state or condition.|例句：They are working to restow the damaged building. 他们正在努力修复受损的建筑物。|近义词：restore, rebuild, reinstate|反义词：destroy, demolish, ruin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He restows the antique furniture. = 他修复古董家具。
They restow the old house to its original glory. = 他们把老房子修复到原来的辉煌。

一般过去时:
She restowed the damaged painting. = 她修复了受损的油画。
The team restowed the historical monument. = 团队修复了这座历史古迹。

现在完成时:
They have restowed the damaged bridge. = 他们已经修复了受损的桥梁。
We have restowed the old family photos. = 我们已经修复了旧的家庭照片。

过去完成时:
The artist had restowed the sculpture before the exhibition. = 艺术家在展览前已经修复了雕塑。
They had restowed the ancient temple before the tourists arrived. = 游客到达之前，他们已经修复了古老的寺庙。

将来时:
He will restow the engine next week. = 他下周将修复引擎。
The company will restow the damaged equipment. = 公司将修复损坏的设备。

虚拟语气:
If I had the tools, I would restow the clock. = 如果我有工具，我会修理这台钟。
I wish I could restow the broken vase. = 我真希望我能修复这个破碎的花瓶。

被动语态:
The car was restowed by a skilled mechanic. = 这辆车由一位熟练的技工修复。
The old painting is being restowed in the museum. = 这幅旧画正在博物馆里修复。

疑问句:
Will they restow the damaged statue? = 他们会修复受损的雕像吗？
Can you restow my broken computer? = 你能修理我的坏电脑吗？

否定句:
They did not restow the broken chair. = 他们没有修理坏掉的椅子。
He cannot restow the antique clock. = 他无法修复这台古董钟。
