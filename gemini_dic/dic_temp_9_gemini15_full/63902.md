epiperipheral: /ˌɛpɪpɛrɪˈfɛrəl/ | adj. | 附属的；次要的；外围的 |  Relating to or situated on the periphery or outer surface;  of secondary or minor importance.  The word combines "epi-" (upon, over, outside) and "peripheral" (relating to the periphery).  It suggests something that is not only peripheral, but also situated on the outer surface or layer of something else. | Example:  The epiperipheral symptoms were less concerning than the central issue.  附属症状比核心问题不那么令人担忧。| 近义词: secondary, marginal, ancillary, peripheral | 反义词: central, primary, principal, main
