channeller's:/ˈtʃænələr z/| n. | 通灵者(的) | A channeller is a person who claims to be able to communicate with spirits or other entities from other realms.  The word "channeller's" is the possessive form, indicating belonging to a channeller. | Example: The channeller's message was surprisingly insightful. 通灵者的讯息出乎意料地富有洞见。 |近义词: medium's, psychic's |反义词: N/A
