deafens:/ˈdɛfənz/| vt.|使聋; 使震耳欲聋|to make someone unable to hear, or to make a sound so loud that it is impossible to hear anything else.|The loud music deafened me.  震耳欲聋的音乐让我听不见了。
The explosion deafened everyone within a mile radius. 爆炸使方圆一英里内的人都聋了。|stun, overwhelm|hear, listen|动词

一般现在时:
The loud music deafens me.  震耳欲聋的音乐让我听不见了。
The constant noise deafens the workers.  持续的噪音使工人们听不见了。

一般过去时:
The explosion deafened everyone nearby. 爆炸使附近的人全都听不见了。
The loud rock concert deafened her for a few hours.  嘈杂的摇滚音乐会让她几个小时都听不见。

现在完成时:
The years of working in a noisy factory have deafened him.  多年在嘈杂的工厂工作已经让他失聪了。
The repeated exposure to loud sounds has deafened many musicians.  反复暴露在巨大的噪音下已经使许多音乐家失聪。

过去完成时:
He had been deafened by a previous explosion before the accident.  事故发生前，他已经因为之前的爆炸而失聪了。
She had already been deafened by the constant gunfire before she reached the shelter.  在她到达避难所之前，持续的枪声已经让她失聪了。


将来时:
The upcoming concert will likely deafen those in the front row.  即将到来的音乐会可能会让前排的人听不见。
Prolonged exposure to this level of noise will deafen you eventually. 长时间暴露在这种噪音水平下最终会让你失聪。

虚拟语气:
If I had worn earplugs, I wouldn't have been deafened. 如果我戴了耳塞，我就不会变聋了。
If the band played any louder, they would deafen the entire audience. 如果乐队再大声一点，他们就会震聋整个观众。

被动语态:
He was deafened by a bomb blast. 他被炸弹爆炸震聋了。
Many people were deafened by the fireworks display. 许多人被烟花表演震聋了。

疑问句:
Was he deafened by the explosion? 他是被爆炸震聋的吗？
Could the music have deafened her temporarily? 音乐会暂时让她失聪了吗？

否定句:
The music wasn't loud enough to deafen me. 音乐不够大声，不会让我失聪。
The sound didn't deafen him completely.  声音并没有完全让他失聪。
