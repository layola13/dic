intercampus: /ˌɪntərˈkæmpəs/ | adj. | 校际的 | Relating to or involving more than one campus of a university or college.  | 例句：The intercampus bus service connects the main campus with the satellite campus. 校际巴士服务连接主校区和卫星校区。

例句：The intercampus competition was fierce, with each campus vying for the top spot. 校际比赛非常激烈，每个校区都争夺第一名。


近义词：cross-campus, between-campus


反义词：intracampus (within a single campus)


词性: 形容词
