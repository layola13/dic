espied:/ɪˈspaɪd/| vt.| 瞥见；发现；侦察到 | To catch sight of something or someone unexpectedly or suddenly.  |例句：I espied a figure in the distance. 我在远处瞥见了一个身影。


例句：From the hilltop, we espied the enemy camp. 从山顶上，我们发现了敌人的营地。


近义词：catch sight of, glimpse, discern, detect, observe


反义词：miss, overlook


词性：动词
