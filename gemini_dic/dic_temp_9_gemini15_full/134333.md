/nɑːnɪnˈʃʊərənsɪz/ | n. | 非保险；缺乏保险 | The lack of insurance coverage; the state of not having insurance.  This term often refers to situations where insurance is expected or required but is absent. | Example:  The company faced significant noninsurances in its liability coverage. 公司在其责任保险方面面临着严重的保险不足。  The high rate of noninsurances among young drivers is a concern. 年轻司机中高比例的缺乏保险令人担忧。 | 近义词: lack of insurance, uninsured status, absence of coverage | 反义词: insurance, insured status, coverage |
