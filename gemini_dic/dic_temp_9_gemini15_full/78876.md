ginglymus:/ˈdʒɪŋɡlɪməs/| n. |枢状关节；铰链关节|A ginglymus is a type of synovial joint that allows movement in only one plane, like a hinge.  The elbow and knee are examples of ginglymus joints. They are also known as hinge joints.|The elbow is a classic example of a ginglymus joint.|近义词：hinge joint|反义词：ball-and-socket joint, saddle joint
