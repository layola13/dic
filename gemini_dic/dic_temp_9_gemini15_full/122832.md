molestation:/ˌmɒlɪˈsteɪʃən/| n.|性骚扰；虐待；干扰|The act of annoying or disturbing someone, especially by repeatedly harassing or persecuting them.  It often refers to sexual abuse, but can also include other forms of harassment or mistreatment. |例句：The molestation of children is a serious crime. 儿童性骚扰是严重的犯罪。

例句：He was accused of molestation after repeatedly harassing his coworker. 他因反复骚扰同事而被指控性骚扰。


近义词：abuse, harassment, assault, persecution, violation


反义词：protection, care, kindness


词源:  From Middle French *molestacion, from Late Latin molestātiōnem (nominative molestātio), from molestāre "to disturb, annoy," from Latin molestus "troublesome, annoying."  The sexual connotation developed later.
