gambit:/ˈɡæmbɪt/| n.|策略，开局妙招，冒险行动|A gambit is an opening move in a game, especially chess, that involves some risk or sacrifice to gain an advantage.  It can also refer to a remark or action made to start a conversation or gain an advantage.|例句：Her opening gambit was to compliment his tie. = 她的开场白是赞美他的领带。
例句：He made a risky gambit in the negotiation. = 他在谈判中采取了冒险的策略。|近义词：strategy, tactic, maneuver, ploy|反义词：caution, prudence
