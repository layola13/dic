ibuprofens:/ˌaɪbjuˈproʊfɛnz/ | n. |布洛芬（复数） | Ibuprofens are nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) used to relieve pain and reduce fever. They work by inhibiting the production of prostaglandins, which are substances that contribute to inflammation, pain, and fever. |例句：The doctor prescribed ibuprofens to alleviate the patient's post-surgical pain.  医生开了布洛芬来减轻病人术后的疼痛。

例句：Many over-the-counter pain relievers contain ibuprofens as their active ingredient. 许多非处方止痛药都含有布洛芬作为活性成分。


近义词：NSAIDs (nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs), analgesics


反义词：无直接反义词，但其疗效的相反可以是增加疼痛或发烧的药物。


词性:名词
