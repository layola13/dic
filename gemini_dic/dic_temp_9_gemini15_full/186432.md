ribbonwood: /ˈrɪbənˌwʊd/ | n. | 缎带木,  (尤指)山茱萸属植物 | A type of wood, often referring to a species of Cornus (dogwood), characterized by its layered or ribbon-like grain.  The name is descriptive of the wood's appearance. | Example: The artisan carefully selected a piece of ribbonwood for the intricate carving.  工匠仔细挑选了一块缎带木用于复杂的雕刻。  The furniture was made from a particularly beautiful ribbonwood. 这件家具是用特别漂亮的缎带木制成的。| 近义词: dogwood (in some contexts), layered wood | 反义词:  (no direct antonym,  but one might contrast it with woods having uniform grain)
