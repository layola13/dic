hanukkahs:/ˈhɑːnəkəz/ | n. | 逾越节（复数） | The plural form of Hanukkah, the Jewish festival of lights.  It refers to multiple celebrations of Hanukkah. |  We celebrated two Hanukkahs this year, one with my family and one with my wife's.  今年我们庆祝了两次光明节，一次和我的家人一起，一次和妻子的家人一起。 |  Festivals of lights, Jewish holidays |  Christmas(非近义词，但属于同类节日) |  n.

Hanukkah gelt (光明节钱币):  Chocolate coins given as gifts during Hanukkah.
Hanukkah candles (光明节蜡烛): Candles used in the menorah during Hanukkah.
Hanukkah latkes (光明节土豆饼): Potato pancakes traditionally eaten during Hanukkah.
Hanukkah menorah (光明节烛台): A nine-branched candelabrum used during Hanukkah.
Hanukkah gifts (光明节礼物): Presents exchanged during the festival of Hanukkah.
