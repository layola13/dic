rhumbas:/ˈrʌmbəz/| n. |伦巴舞；伦巴舞曲|A rhumba is a dance of Cuban origin, characterized by its rhythmic steps and hip movements. It's often performed to lively, syncopated music.|例句：They danced a passionate rhumba under the moonlight. 他们在月光下跳了一支充满激情的伦巴舞。|近义词：dance, salsa, mambo|反义词：None
