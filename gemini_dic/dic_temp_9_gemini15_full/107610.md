landtag: /ˈlændˌtæɡ/ | n. |州议会；邦议会 | A legislative assembly in some German states.  It's the parliament of a German state (Bundesland).  The term is derived from the combination of "Land" (meaning "state" or "country") and "Tag" (meaning "meeting" or "assembly"). | Example: The Baden-Württemberg Landtag recently passed a new environmental protection law.  巴登-符腾堡州议会最近通过了一项新的环境保护法。 | 近义词: state legislature, parliament, assembly | 反义词:  executive branch, government (as a whole) | 名词
