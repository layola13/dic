furnishment:/ˈfɜːrnɪʃmənt/| n.| 家具；摆设；惩处；装饰 |The act of furnishing or equipping; the state of being furnished.  It can refer to the provision of furniture, the process of decorating a space, or the act of imposing a punishment. |例句：The furnishing of the new apartment was completed. 新公寓的装修完成了。
The judge decided on a harsh punishment for the crime. 法官决定对这起罪行进行严厉的惩罚。|近义词：equipment, decoration, penalty, chastisement |反义词：removal, emptiness, reward


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The shop furnishes many homes with beautiful furniture. 这家商店为许多家庭提供精美的家具。
He furnishes his apartment with antique pieces. 他用古董装饰他的公寓。


一般过去时:
They furnished the room with modern furniture. 他们用现代家具布置了房间。
The court furnished the defendant with a lawyer. 法庭为被告提供了一名律师。


现在完成时:
She has furnished her new house beautifully. 她已经漂亮地布置了她的新房子。
The committee has furnished the report with detailed statistics. 委员会已在报告中提供了详细的统计数据。


过去完成时:
They had furnished the house before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前，他们已经布置好房子了。
The lawyer had furnished the court with all the necessary documents. 律师已向法庭提交了所有必要的证件。


将来时:
We will furnish the hall for the wedding reception. 我们将布置大厅以举办婚礼招待会。
The company will furnish the employees with new computers. 公司将为员工配备新电脑。


虚拟语气:
If they furnished the room differently, it would look much better. 如果他们以不同的方式布置房间，它看起来会好得多。
I wish they had furnished the evidence earlier. 我希望他们早点提供证据。


被动语态:
The house was furnished by a professional interior designer. 这所房子是由一位专业的室内设计师布置的。
The prisoners were furnished with basic necessities. 囚犯们得到了基本的生活必需品。


疑问句:
How will you furnish the new office? 你将如何布置新办公室？
What materials were used to furnish the room? 用什么材料布置的房间？


否定句:
They did not furnish the necessary information. 他们没有提供必要的信息。
The government did not furnish adequate funds for the project. 政府没有为该项目提供足够的资金。


PHRASE->

furnish a room: 布置房间
furnish details: 提供细节
