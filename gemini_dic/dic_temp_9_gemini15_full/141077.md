onomatopoeically:/ˌɒnəˌmætəpəˈiːkəli/ | adv. |拟声地，象声地 |  In a manner that imitates sounds.  The word itself is formed onomatopoeically.  | 例句：The author described the sound of the waterfall onomatopoeically, using words like "roar" and "cascade". 作者拟声地描述了瀑布的声音，使用了诸如“咆哮”和“飞流直下”之类的词语。

例句：The children onomatopoeically imitated the buzzing of the bees.  孩子们拟声地模仿蜜蜂的嗡嗡声。


近义词：mimetically, imitatively


反义词：abstractly, non-representatively


词源:  From onomatopoeia + -ically.  Onomatopoeia itself comes from Greek ὀνοματοποιία (onomatopoiía), from ὄνομα (ónoma) "name" + ποιέω (poiéō) "to make".  Thus, the word's origin reflects its meaning: the creation of words that imitate sounds.
