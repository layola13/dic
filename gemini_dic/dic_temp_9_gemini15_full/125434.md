multiplane:/ˈmʌltiˌpleɪn/| n. |多平面；多层画面|A multiplane camera is a device used in animation to create a three-dimensional effect by moving layers of artwork at different speeds and distances from the camera.|The Disney film "Snow White" was one of the first to use a multiplane camera to create depth and realism.|近义词：layered animation, depth-of-field effect|反义词：single-plane animation|名词
