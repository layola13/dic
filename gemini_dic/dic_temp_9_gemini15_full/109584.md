lethal:/ˈliːθəl/ | adj. | 致命的，致死的 | capable of causing death; deadly.  |例句：The snake's venom is lethal. 这条蛇的毒液是致命的。|例句：That dose of poison is lethal.  那一剂毒药是致命的。|近义词：deadly, fatal, mortal | 反义词：harmless, innocuous | 词性:形容词
