joinable: /ˈdʒɔɪnəbl/ | adj. | 可加入的，可连接的 | Able to be joined or connected.  Something that is joinable can be added to a group, system, or network.  | 例句：This online forum is joinable; you just need to create an account.  这个在线论坛是可以加入的；你只需要创建一个账户。

The game is joinable at any time, even if a match has already started.  即使比赛已经开始，也可以随时加入游戏。

近义词：connectable, attachable, accessible


反义词：unjoinable, inaccessible, unconnected


词源:  Derived from the verb "join" + "-able," a suffix indicating capability or possibility.  The word's history traces back to the Old English verb "ge-joinan," meaning "to join together."
