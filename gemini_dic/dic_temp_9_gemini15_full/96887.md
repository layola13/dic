unexpectedly: /ˌʌnɪkˈspektɪdli/ | adv. | 出乎意料地，意外地 | In an unexpected manner; contrary to expectation.  |例句：Unexpectedly, it started to rain. =出乎意料的是，开始下雨了。
例句：He unexpectedly won the lottery. = 他意外地中了彩票。|近义词：surprisingly, unexpectedly, suddenly, abruptly |反义词：expectedly, predictably
