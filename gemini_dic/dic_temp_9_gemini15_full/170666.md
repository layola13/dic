pseudo-rationalist:/ˈsuːdoʊˈræʃənəlɪst/| adj. |似是而非的理性主义者；伪理性主义者 |Someone who appears to use reason and logic but whose reasoning is flawed or based on biased assumptions.  They often mimic the style and language of rational thought but lack genuine intellectual rigor.  Their arguments might seem logical on the surface but ultimately fail to withstand scrutiny.  |Example:  The pseudo-rationalist used statistics selectively to support his predetermined conclusion.  伪理性主义者选择性地使用统计数据来支持他预先确定的结论。|近义词：illogical, irrational, sophistical, specious |反义词：rational, logical, reasonable
