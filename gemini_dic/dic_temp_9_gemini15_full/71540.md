flare-ups:/ˈfleəˌʌps/| n.|突然发作，加剧；闪光；突然爆发|A sudden worsening of a problem, especially a disease or conflict.  A brief, intense burst of light or flame.|例句：He experienced several flare-ups of his asthma over the winter.  他整个冬天哮喘反复发作了好几次。

例句：The photographer captured a dramatic flare-up of the bonfire. 摄影师捕捉到了篝火剧烈燃烧的景象。|近义词：outbreaks, exacerbations, recurrences|反义词：remission, abatement, subsidence|名词
