pessimists:/ˈpɛsɪmɪsts/| n. |悲观主义者|People who tend to expect the worst or to see the worst aspects of things. They often focus on potential problems and difficulties rather than opportunities and positive outcomes.  |例句：Pessimists often believe that things will always go wrong. 悲观主义者通常认为事情总是会出错。 |例句：Many pessimists find it difficult to see the silver lining in any situation. 许多悲观主义者发现很难在任何情况下看到一线希望。|近义词：defeatest, cynics|反义词：optimists, idealists|名词
