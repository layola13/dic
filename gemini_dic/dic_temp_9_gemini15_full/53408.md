disambiguation: /ˌdɪsˌæmbɪɡjuˈeɪʃən/ | n. |消除歧义；明确含义|The process of resolving ambiguity by identifying the intended meaning of a word, phrase, or sentence.  It involves clarifying the different possible interpretations of a term and selecting the most appropriate one within the given context. |Example: The disambiguation of the word "bank" as either a financial institution or the side of a river requires considering the surrounding text.  The disambiguation of the ambiguous pronoun "he" was crucial to understanding the sentence. |近义词：clarification, elucidation, specification |反义词：confusion, ambiguity
