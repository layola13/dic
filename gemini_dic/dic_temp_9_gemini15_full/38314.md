concealing: /kənˈsiːlɪŋ/ | v. | 隐藏；遮掩；掩盖 | To hide or keep secret; to prevent from being seen or known. | 例句：He was concealing a weapon under his coat. 他外套底下藏着一件武器。 | 例句：The company tried to conceal its financial problems from investors.  这家公司试图向投资者隐瞒其财务问题。 | 近义词：hiding, masking, disguising, suppressing | 反义词：revealing, disclosing, exposing

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He conceals his true feelings. 他隐藏他的真实感受。
They conceal the evidence. 他们隐瞒证据。

一般过去时:
She concealed the truth from her parents. 她向父母隐瞒了真相。
The magician concealed the rabbit in his hat. 魔术师把兔子藏在帽子中。

现在完成时:
He has concealed his identity for years. 他多年来一直隐瞒身份。
They have concealed the truth from the public. 他们一直向公众隐瞒真相。

过去完成时:
She had concealed the letter before her husband arrived home. 她在她丈夫回家之前已经藏好了信。
They had concealed their intentions until the last moment. 他们直到最后一刻才泄露他们的意图。

将来时:
He will conceal the documents from the authorities. 他将向当局隐瞒这些文件。
They will conceal their plan until the right time. 他们会等到合适的时候再公布他们的计划。

虚拟语气:
If he concealed the truth, he would be in trouble. 如果他隐瞒真相，他就会有麻烦。
I wish she hadn't concealed her feelings from me. 我希望她没有向我隐瞒她的感受。

被动语态:
The truth was concealed from the public. 真相被隐瞒了公众。
The evidence has been concealed by the police. 证据已被警方隐瞒。

疑问句:
Is he concealing something from us? 他是不是在向我们隐瞒什么？
What are they concealing? 他们在隐瞒什么？

否定句:
He didn't conceal his anger. 他没有掩饰他的愤怒。
They didn't conceal their disappointment. 他们没有掩饰他们的失望。
