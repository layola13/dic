/ɪntərˈmɛʒər/ | n. |  中间测量；中间值 |  A measurement taken at an intermediate point or time, often to monitor progress or assess a process.  It is not a standard dictionary word and its usage is highly contextual.  The meaning is derived from the combination of "inter-" (between, among) and "measure".  | Example:  "The intermeasure taken halfway through the experiment showed a significant improvement." = “实验进行到一半时进行的中间测量显示出显著的改进。” |  intermediate measurement, midpoint reading |  final measurement  | noun
