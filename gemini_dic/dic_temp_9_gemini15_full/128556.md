niagar: /naɪˈæɡərə/ | n. |尼亚加拉（瀑布）|Niagara refers to Niagara Falls, a group of three waterfalls at the southern end of Niagara Gorge, spanning the border between the United States and Canada.  The name is derived from the Iroquois word "Ongiara," meaning "the strait."  It can also refer to the surrounding region. |例句：Niagara Falls is a breathtaking natural wonder. 尼亚加拉瀑布是一个令人叹为观止的自然奇观。

The Niagara River flows from Lake Erie to Lake Ontario. 尼亚加拉河从伊利湖流向安大略湖。
