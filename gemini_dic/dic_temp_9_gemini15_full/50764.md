des:/dez/| adj.|  值得的，应得的，应有的 | deserving; meriting; suitable |  The team deserves credit for their hard work.  这个团队值得为他们的努力付出表扬。 |  merited, earned, rightful | undeserved, unworthy | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He deserves a reward. = 他值得奖励。
This project deserves our full attention. = 这个项目值得我们全力以赴。


一般过去时:
She deserved the prize. = 她应该得到这个奖。
They deserved a break after all that hard work. = 经过所有努力后，他们应该休息一下。


现在完成时:
He has deserved his success. = 他应得他的成功。
They have deserved a better life. = 他们应该过上更好的生活。


过去完成时:
She had deserved a better outcome. = 她本应该得到更好的结果。
They had deserved more respect from their boss. = 他们本应该从老板那里得到更多的尊重。


将来时:
You will deserve a promotion. = 你将得到晋升。
She will deserve the praise for her efforts. = 她将因为她的努力而得到赞扬。


虚拟语气:
He deserved to win, if only he had tried harder. = 如果他更努力一些，他本应该赢的。
She deserved to be happier. = 她本应该更快乐。


被动语态:
The punishment was deserved. = 惩罚是应得的。
The praise was deservedly given. = 赞扬是理所当然的。


疑问句:
Does he deserve a second chance? = 他值得第二次机会吗？
Do they deserve our sympathy? = 他们值得我们的同情吗？


否定句:
He doesn't deserve to be treated like that. = 他不应受到这样的待遇。
They don't deserve such harsh criticism. = 他们不值得如此严厉的批评。
