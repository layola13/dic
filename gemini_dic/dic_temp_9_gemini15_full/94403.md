implicated:/ˈɪmplɪkeɪtɪd/| adj. v.|牵涉的；暗示的；包含的|to show that someone or something is involved in something bad or illegal; to suggest or involve something indirectly|The evidence implicated him in the crime.  证据表明他参与了犯罪。|involved, connected, suggested|exculpated, cleared, absolved|形容词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence implicated him in the conspiracy. = 证据表明他参与了阴谋。
This action implicates the company in illegal activities. = 这一行为使公司牵涉到非法活动中。


一般过去时:
The investigation implicated several high-ranking officials. = 调查牵涉到几名高级官员。
The study implicated poor diet as a risk factor for heart disease. = 该研究暗示不良饮食是心脏病的危险因素。


现在完成时:
The scandal has implicated numerous politicians. = 这场丑闻已经牵涉到许多政客。
Recent research has implicated environmental factors in the development of the disease. = 最近的研究已经将环境因素与疾病的发展联系起来。


过去完成时:
The police had implicated him in previous crimes, but lacked sufficient evidence. = 警方之前曾将他牵涉到之前的罪行中，但缺乏足够的证据。
It had been implicated that the company had falsified accounting records.  =  有迹象表明该公司伪造了会计记录。


将来时:
The upcoming report will likely implicate more individuals. = 即将来临的报告可能会牵涉到更多的人。
Further investigations will implicate the true culprits. = 进一步的调查将揭露真正的罪犯。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence implicated him, he would be arrested. = 如果证据牵连到他，他就会被逮捕。
I wish the report hadn't implicated my department in the mistake. = 我希望报告没有将我的部门牵涉到这个错误中。


被动语态:
He was implicated in the fraud. = 他被牵涉到欺诈案中。
The company was implicated in a bribery scandal. = 这家公司牵涉到一起贿赂丑闻中。


疑问句:
Was anyone else implicated in the robbery? = 还有其他人牵涉到抢劫案中吗？
Is the company implicated in the environmental damage? = 这家公司是否牵涉到环境破坏中？


否定句:
The investigation did not implicate him in the crime. = 调查没有将他牵涉到犯罪中。
The report did not implicate any specific individuals. = 该报告没有牵涉到任何特定个人。


PHRASE->
be implicated in = 牵涉到…中
implicated evidence = 牵连证据
highly implicated = 严重牵涉
directly implicated = 直接牵涉
indirectly implicated = 间接牵涉
