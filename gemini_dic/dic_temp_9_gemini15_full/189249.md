sadder:/ˈsædər/| adj. |更悲伤的，更令人悲伤的 |More sorrowful or depressing than before; causing more sadness.  |例句：The news made him sadder than ever before. 这个消息使他比以往任何时候都更悲伤。
例句：The sadder truth is that we may never find the missing cat. 更令人悲伤的事实是我们可能再也找不到那只失踪的猫。|近义词：more sorrowful, more mournful, more depressing |反义词：happier, more cheerful, more joyful |形容词
