chilies:/ˈtʃɪliz/| n. |辣椒(复数)|Chillies are the fruits of plants belonging to the genus Capsicum, and are widely used as a spice and condiment.|例句：I added some chillies to the curry to make it spicier. 我在咖喱里加了一些辣椒让它更辣。  例句：These chillies are incredibly hot; handle with care. 这些辣椒非常辣；小心处理。|近义词：peppers, hot peppers|反义词：sweet peppers
