pre-Columbian: /ˌpriː kəˈlʌmbiən/ | adj. |哥伦布之前的；前哥伦布时期的 | Relating to the period in the history of the Americas before the arrival of Christopher Columbus in 1492.  This term encompasses the cultures, civilizations, and events that occurred in North, Central, and South America before European contact. | Example: Pre-Columbian art often features intricate designs and symbolism.  哥伦布之前的艺术作品通常具有复杂的设计和象征意义。|近义词: pre-contact |反义词: post-Columbian
