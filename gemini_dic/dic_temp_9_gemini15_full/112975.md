lull:/lʌl/| n., v.|平静；使平静；哄；骗；暂时停顿|a period of calm or quiet|The storm lulled for a while before resuming.|近义词：calm, quiet, soothe, pacify|反义词：agitation, turmoil, excitement


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wind lulls at night. = 夜晚风停息了。
A gentle music lulls the baby to sleep. = 轻柔的音乐哄婴儿睡觉。


一般过去时:
The storm lulled briefly before intensifying. = 暴风雨短暂地平息后又加剧了。
She lulled him into a false sense of security. = 她哄骗他产生虚假的安全感。


现在完成时:
The war has lulled, but peace isn't guaranteed. = 战争已经平息，但和平并非有保障。
The constant noise has finally lulled. = 持久的噪音终于停息了。


过去完成时:
The tension had lulled before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，紧张气氛已经缓和了。
The argument had lulled, leaving them both exhausted. = 争论已经平息，使他们两人都筋疲力尽。


将来时:
The storm will lull soon. = 风暴很快就会平息。
I hope the crisis will lull by next week. = 我希望危机在下周能得到缓解。


虚拟语气:
If the storm would only lull, we could leave. = 如果风暴能平息的话，我们就能离开了。
I wish the pain would lull. = 我希望疼痛能减轻。


被动语态:
The audience was lulled into complacency. = 观众被哄得自满起来。
The baby was lulled to sleep by the gentle rocking. = 婴儿被轻轻的摇晃哄睡着了。


疑问句:
Will the fighting ever lull? = 战斗会永远平息吗？
Does the music lull you? = 这音乐能让你平静吗？


否定句:
The storm didn't lull all night. = 暴风雨整夜都没有平息。
His attempts to lull her suspicions failed. = 他试图减轻她的怀疑，但失败了。



PHRASE->
lull into complacency = 使…自满
lull someone into a false sense of security = 使某人产生虚假的安全感
lull a baby to sleep = 哄婴儿睡觉
a lull in the conversation = 对话中的停顿
a lull in the fighting = 战斗中的间歇
