depolarization:/ˌdiːpəʊləraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |去极化；膜去极化 | The process of a cell membrane losing its polarization;  a decrease in the absolute value of the membrane potential. This typically occurs when the membrane potential becomes less negative.  | Example:  The rapid depolarization of the neuron's membrane triggers the release of neurotransmitters.  神经元的膜快速去极化触发神经递质的释放。


The influx of sodium ions during an action potential causes depolarization. 动作电位期间钠离子的内流导致去极化。|近义词：depolarisation (英式拼写) |反义词：repolarization, hyperpolarization
