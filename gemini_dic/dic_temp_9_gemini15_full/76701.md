gamba:/ˈɡæmbə/| n.|甘巴（一种乐器）,腿,脚|A gamba is a type of bowed string instrument, usually played vertically.  The word can also refer to a leg or foot, particularly in certain contexts.|例句：The musician played a beautiful melody on the viola da gamba.  演奏家用维奥尔琴演奏了一首优美的旋律。|近义词：leg, foot (in some contexts), viol|反义词：hand, arm
