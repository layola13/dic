/ˌpriːˌɡærənˈtɔːrz/ | n. | 预担保人；预先保证人 |  A preguarantor is a person or entity who agrees to guarantee a loan or obligation before the primary guarantor.  This often happens in situations where the primary guarantor's creditworthiness is questionable or needs additional support. Preguarantors provide an extra layer of security for the lender. |  The bank required a preguarantor to secure the large loan for the new business.  银行要求一个预担保人来担保这家新企业的巨额贷款。 | co-guarantor, secondary guarantor |  (暂无明确反义词，可以考虑：unguarantor, 但此词并不常用) | 名词
