fifths:/fɪfts/| n.|五分之一；五分之几|The word "fifths" refers to one of five equal parts of a whole.  It is the plural form of "fifth".|例句：She ate two fifths of the pizza. 她吃了披萨的五分之二。

例句：The recipe calls for three fifths of a cup of sugar.  这个食谱需要三分之二杯糖。


近义词：one-fifth (singular), portions, fractions


反义词：wholes, entirety


词性：名词
