fohn:/fɔːn/| n. |焚风，热风 |A warm, dry wind that descends the leeward slopes of mountains, often causing a rapid increase in temperature and a decrease in relative humidity.| 例句：The fohn wind caused the snow to melt rapidly. 焚风导致积雪迅速融化。  The hikers were caught in a strong fohn, leading to dehydration.  徒步旅行者遭遇强烈的焚风，导致脱水。|近义词：chinook, Santa Ana wind |反义词：cold wind, moist wind |名词
