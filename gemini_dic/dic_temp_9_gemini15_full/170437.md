proximity:/prɒkˈsɪməti/|n.|邻近，接近；亲近|The nearness in space, time, or relationship.  It refers to the state of being close or near to something or someone.|例句：The proximity of the two houses caused a dispute over property lines.  两座房子过于靠近，导致了关于地界纠纷。
例句：Due to its proximity to the city center, the apartment is very expensive. 由于靠近市中心，这套公寓非常昂贵。|近义词：nearness, closeness, vicinity|反义词：distance, remoteness
