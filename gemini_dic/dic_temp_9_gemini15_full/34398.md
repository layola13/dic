classlessnesses:/ˈklɑːsləsnəsɪz/| n. |阶级缺失的多种状态；缺乏阶级意识的多种情况|The multiple instances or manifestations of a lack of class structure or class consciousness.  This word is extremely rare and largely theoretical, referring to the plural instances of a state lacking class distinctions.|例句：The sociologist analyzed the classlessnesses evident in the utopian community.  社会学家分析了乌托邦社会中明显的阶级缺失现象。

The various classlessnesses across different societies highlight the complexities of social stratification.  不同社会中各种阶级缺失的情况突显了社会分层的复杂性。


近义词：lacks of class distinctions, absence of class structures,  equalities (in a broad sense)


反义词：class structures, social stratification, hierarchies


词性: Noun (plural)
