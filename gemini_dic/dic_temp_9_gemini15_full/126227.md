mycelium:/maɪˈsiːliəm/ | n. |菌丝体；菌丝群 | The mycelium is the vegetative part of a fungus, consisting of a network of fine white filaments (hyphae).  It is responsible for nutrient uptake and absorption.  | 例句：The mycelium of the mushroom spread underground, forming a vast network.  菌菇的菌丝体在地下蔓延，形成一个巨大的网络。

例句：A healthy mycelium is crucial for the growth and development of mushrooms. 健康的菌丝体对于蘑菇的生长发育至关重要。


近义词：mycelia (复数), hyphae (individual filaments)


反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑与子实体(fruiting body)相对，但并非完全反义。


词性：名词
