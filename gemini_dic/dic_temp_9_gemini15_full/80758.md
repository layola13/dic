gossamery:/ˈɡɒsəməri/| adj.|轻薄的；精巧的；像蛛丝的|Characterized by lightness, delicacy, and seeming fragility; resembling a spiderweb.  The word evokes a sense of ethereal beauty and tenuousness.|例句：The gossamery wings of the butterfly fluttered in the gentle breeze. 蝴蝶轻薄的翅膀在微风中轻轻飘动。

The gossamery veil she wore barely concealed her beauty. 她戴着轻薄的面纱，几乎遮不住她的美丽。


近义词：delicate, flimsy, diaphanous, sheer, airy|反义词：heavy, sturdy, robust, thick, coarse|词性:形容词

词源:
