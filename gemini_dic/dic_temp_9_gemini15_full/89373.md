homoorgan:/ˈhoʊmoʊˌɔːrɡən/ | n. |同源器官|Homologous organ,  a structure or organ that appears in different animals, underlining anatomical commonalities demonstrating descent from a common ancestor.  It may serve different purposes in different animals but show similar underlying structure. |例句：The forelimbs of whales, bats, and humans are examples of homoorgan, despite their different functions.  鲸鱼、蝙蝠和人类的前肢是同源器官的例子，尽管它们的功能不同。|近义词：homologue, analogous structure (注意区分，analogous structure 指功能相似但结构不同的器官)|反义词：analogous structure |名词
