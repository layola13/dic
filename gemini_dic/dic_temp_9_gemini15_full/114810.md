mamaluke:/ˈmæməluːk/| n.|马穆鲁克兵，马穆鲁克王朝|A mamluke was a slave soldier, especially one of the military caste that dominated Egypt and Syria in the medieval period.  The term often refers to the Mamluk Sultanate (1250-1517).|例句：The Mamlukes were renowned for their military prowess and their powerful sultanate.|近义词：slave soldier, mercenary|反义词：master, civilian
