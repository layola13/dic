insincere:/ɪnˈsɪnɪə(r)/| adj. |不真诚的，虚伪的 |Not expressing genuine feelings; not sincere.  It implies a lack of honesty or authenticity in one's words or actions.  |例句：His apology seemed insincere; he didn't look genuinely remorseful.  他的道歉听起来不真诚；他看起来并不真正悔过。

His insincere compliments made her uncomfortable. 他虚情假意的赞美让她感到不舒服。|近义词：dishonest, hypocritical, false, artificial, affected |反义词：sincere, genuine, authentic, heartfelt, truthful
