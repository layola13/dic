communiques:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪz/ | n. | 公报；官方声明 | Official announcements or statements, especially from a government or other authoritative body.  The word emphasizes the formal and often concise nature of the communication. | The government issued a series of communiques outlining its new economic policy.  政府发布了一系列公报，概述了其新的经济政策。


The White House released a communique denying the allegations. 白宫发表了一份公报否认这些指控。


近义词：announcement, statement, bulletin, declaration | 反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting informal or unofficial communication would be antonyms in context (e.g., rumor, whisper).  | 名词
