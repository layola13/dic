retheorize:/ˌriːˈθiːəraɪz/| vt.|重新理论化；重新构想|To formulate a new theory or explanation for something, often revising or replacing a previous one.|例句：Scientists are retheorizing the origins of the universe in light of new data. 科学家们根据新的数据正在重新理论化宇宙的起源。
例句：We need to retheorize our approach to marketing given the recent changes in consumer behavior. 鉴于消费者行为的近期变化，我们需要重新构想我们的营销方法。|近义词：revise, reformulate, reinterpret|反义词：accept, confirm, verify|动词
