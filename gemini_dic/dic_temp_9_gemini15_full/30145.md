cek:/tʃɛk/| n. |支票；核对；检查|a written order directing a bank to pay money|例句：I wrote a check for $100. 我写了一张100美元的支票。|例句：Please check your work carefully. 请仔细检查你的工作。|近义词：verify, inspect, examine|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I check my email every morning. = 我每天早上都检查我的电子邮件。
He checks the oil level in his car regularly. = 他定期检查汽车的油位。

一般过去时:
She checked her bank balance yesterday. = 她昨天查看了她的银行存款余额。
They checked into the hotel late last night. = 他们昨晚很晚才入住酒店。

现在完成时:
I have checked my luggage three times. = 我已经检查了三次我的行李。
She has checked the facts before writing the report. = 在撰写报告之前，她已经核实了事实。

过去完成时:
He had checked the equipment before starting the experiment. = 在开始实验之前，他已经检查了设备。
They had checked the answers before submitting the test. = 他们提交测试之前已经检查了答案。

将来时:
I will check the weather forecast before leaving. = 我离开前会查看天气预报。
She will check the documents tomorrow morning. = 她明天早上会检查文件。

虚拟语气:
If I had checked the map, I wouldn't have gotten lost. = 如果我查看了地图，我就不会迷路了。
I wish I had checked the price before buying it. = 我希望我在买之前检查过价格。

被动语态:
The luggage was checked by the security personnel. = 行李由安保人员检查。
The accounts are checked monthly by the auditor. = 账户每月由审计员检查。

疑问句:
Did you check the spelling? = 你检查拼写了吗？
Have you checked the tickets? = 你检查票了吗？

否定句:
I didn't check my email this morning. = 我今天早上没有检查我的电子邮件。
She didn't check the facts before drawing the conclusion. = 她得出结论之前没有核实事实。
