overhomogenize:/ˌoʊvərˈhoʊmədʒənaɪz/| vt.|过度均质化；过度使均匀|To make something too uniform or homogeneous, losing its diversity or individuality.  This often applies to cultural or social contexts.|例句：The government's policies risk overhomogenizing the country's diverse cultural traditions.  政府的政策有可能使国家多元的文化传统过度均质化。
例句：The mass production of identical products has led to an overhomogenization of consumer choices.  批量生产相同的产品导致消费者选择过度均质化。|近义词：standardize, uniformize, equalize|反义词：diversify, differentiate
