sederunt: /sɛdəˈrʌnt/ | n. | 出席人员名单；会议记录 | A list of those present at a meeting, especially a formal one, often including the date and place.  It can also refer to the act of sitting down, or being seated. | Example: The sederunt of the board meeting showed ten directors in attendance.  出席董事会会议的人员名单显示有十位董事出席。| 近义词：minutes, proceedings, attendance list | 反义词：absence list
