hydrogens:/ˈhaɪdrədʒənz/| n.|氢原子(复数)|The simplest element, consisting of one proton and (usually) one electron.  It's extremely reactive and readily forms compounds with many other elements.|例句：Water molecules are made up of two hydrogens and one oxygen. 水分子由两个氢原子和一个氧原子组成。|例句：The study focused on the behavior of hydrogens in different chemical environments. 该研究重点关注氢原子在不同化学环境中的行为。|近义词：hydrogen atoms|反义词:无
