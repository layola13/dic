palmyra:/ˈpælmaɪrə/| n. |棕榈树城；棕榈树；槟榔树|A city in Syria, historically significant for its ancient ruins and palm trees.  It also refers to the palm tree itself, especially the date palm.|例句：Palmyra was once a thriving oasis city. 帕米拉曾经是一个繁荣的绿洲城市。|  例句：The date palms of Palmyra are renowned for their sweet fruit. 帕米拉的枣椰树以其甜美的果实而闻名。|近义词：date palm, oasis city|反义词：无
