coigne:/kɔɪn/| n. |墙角；支撑物；斜角；突出部 | A projecting stone or piece of wood, especially at the corner of a building; a wedge or support.  |例句：The old house had a coigne of weathered stone at each corner.  这座老房子每个角落都有一块风化的石头突出部。

The sculptor used a coigne to support the heavy statue while he worked. 雕塑家用一个楔子来支撑沉重的雕像，以便工作。|近义词：cornerstone, angle, projection |反义词：recess, indentation
