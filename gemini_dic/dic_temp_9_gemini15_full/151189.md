parchesi:/pɑːrˈtʃeɪsi/| n. |帕切西（一种棋盘游戏）|A board game for two to four players, similar to Ludo and backgammon, played on a cross-shaped board with numbered spaces.  Players race their pieces around the board, trying to be the first to get all their pieces to the home space.|  We played parchesi for hours last night.  我们昨晚玩了几个小时的帕切西。| Ludo, backgammon |  (no clear antonym) |名词
