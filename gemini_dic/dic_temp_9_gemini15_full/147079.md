词源:

The word is formed from:

* **over-**:  prefix indicating excess or to a great extent.
* **liberalization**: the act of making something more liberal; removing restrictions or controls.  This comes from "liberal" (free from restraint) + "-ization" (process of making something).  "Liberal" itself has roots in Latin *liberalis* (pertaining to a free person).
