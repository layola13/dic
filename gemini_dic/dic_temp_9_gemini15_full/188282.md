rsvping: /ˌɑːrsviːˈpiːɪŋ/ | v. | 回复；答复 (RSVPing is a shortened form of "responding to RSVP") |  RSVPing is the act of replying to a request for a response, typically to an invitation.  It often involves confirming attendance or declining an invitation. | Example: I'm rsvping to the wedding invitation tonight.  今晚我要回复婚礼邀请函。 | 近义词：replying, responding, confirming, acknowledging | 反义词：ignoring, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am rsvping to all the invitations. = 我正在回复所有的邀请函。
He rsvps to emails promptly. = 他会迅速回复邮件。


一般过去时:
I rsvped to the party invitation yesterday. = 我昨天回复了派对邀请函。
She rsvped and said she couldn't make it. = 她回复说她不能来。


现在完成时:
I have rsvped to the conference. = 我已经回复了会议邀请。
They have rsvped and confirmed their attendance. = 他们已经回复并确认出席。


过去完成时:
I had already rsvped before they sent the reminder. = 在他们发送提醒之前，我已经回复了。
She had rsvped to the event weeks ago. = 几周前她就回复了这个活动的邀请。


将来时:
I will rsvp to the dinner invitation tomorrow. = 我明天将回复晚宴邀请。
He will rsvp as soon as he knows his schedule. = 他一旦知道自己的日程安排就会回复。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would rsvp immediately. = 如果我有时间，我会立即回复。
I wish I had rsvped sooner. = 我真希望我早点回复。


被动语态:
The invitations were rsvped to promptly. = 邀请函得到了及时的回复。
The RSVPs are being processed. = 回复正在处理中。


疑问句:
Have you rsvped yet? = 你回复了吗？
When will you be rsvping? = 你什么时候回复？


否定句:
I haven't rsvped yet. = 我还没回复。
He didn't rsvp to the email. = 他没有回复邮件。


PHRASE->
rsvp by [date] = 请在[日期]之前回复
rsvp with regrets = 如不能出席请告知
please rsvp = 请回复
rsvp required = 必须回复
rsvp to [email address] = 请回复至[邮箱地址]
