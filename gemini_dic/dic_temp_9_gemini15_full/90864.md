hungrily:/ˈhʌŋɡrili/| adv. | 饥饿地；渴望地 |  The word "hungrily" describes an action performed with great hunger or eagerness. It implies a strong desire for something, often food, but can also extend to other desires.  |例句：He looked hungrily at the delicious cake. 他渴望地望着那块美味的蛋糕。

The dog ate its food hungrily.  这只狗饿狼吞虎地吃着它的食物。


近义词：eagerly, avidly, ravenously, voraciously | 反义词：reluctantly, unenthusiastically


词源:源自形容词hungry加上副词后缀-ly。hungry源自古英语hungrig,由hungor（饥饿）+ -ig（形容词后缀）构成。
