recommending:/ˌrɛkəˈmɛndɪŋ/| v. |推荐；建议|The act of suggesting or proposing something as suitable or advisable.  It is the present participle of the verb "recommend."|例句：I am recommending this restaurant to all my friends. 我向我所有的朋友推荐这家餐厅。

例句：The doctor is recommending a course of physiotherapy. 医生建议进行物理治疗疗程。


近义词：suggesting, proposing, advising, advocating | 反义词：discouraging, dissuading


词性：动词
