leven:/ˈlevən/| n. |十一|The number eleven is between ten and twelve.|例句：Eleven people attended the meeting. 十一人参加了会议。|近义词：eleventh|反义词:无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Eleven is a prime number. = 11是一个质数。
There are eleven players on a football team. = 一个足球队有十一个队员。


一般过去时:
There were eleven apples in the basket. = 篮子里有十一个苹果。
Eleven people came to the party. = 十一人来参加了聚会。


现在完成时:
I have counted eleven birds. = 我已经数了十一个鸟。
She has lived in eleven different cities. = 她已经住在十一个不同的城市里了。


过去完成时:
We had already eaten eleven cookies. = 我们已经吃了十一个饼干。
They had seen eleven movies that week. = 他们那周已经看了十一部电影。


将来时:
There will be eleven guests at the dinner. = 晚宴将会有十一位客人。
I will bake eleven cupcakes for the party. = 我将为派对烤十一个纸杯蛋糕。


虚拟语气:
If there were eleven apples, we would have enough for everyone. = 如果有十一个苹果，我们就足够每人一个了。
I wish I had eleven more dollars. = 我真希望我还有十一个美元。


被动语态:
Eleven questions were asked during the interview. = 面试中问了十一个问题。
Eleven books were donated to the library. = 十一本书捐给了图书馆。


疑问句:
How many are eleven plus five? = 十一加五等于多少？
Are there eleven people in the room? = 房间里有十一个人吗？


否定句:
There weren't eleven cars in the parking lot. = 停车场里没有十一个车。
It isn't eleven o'clock yet. = 现在还没到十一点。
