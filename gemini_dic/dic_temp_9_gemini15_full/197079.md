shamefacedly:/ʃeɪmˈfeɪsɪdli/| adv.|羞愧地，尴尬地|The adverb "shamefacedly" describes an action performed with a feeling of shame or embarrassment.  It suggests a visible display of shame, often through blushing or downcast eyes. |He shamefacedly admitted to breaking the vase. 他羞愧地承认打破了花瓶。|ashamedly, guiltily, sheepishly|boldly, confidently, proudly|副词
