rumples:/ˈrʌmplz/| vt. n.|弄皱；弄乱；褶皱|To make something wrinkled or uneven; a wrinkle or unevenness.|The cat rumples the blanket.  猫把毯子弄皱了。|He sat down heavily, rumples his clothes. 他重重地坐下，弄皱了他的衣服。| wrinkle, crease| smooth, flatten|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rumples his hair. = 他揉乱了他的头发。
She often rumples the sheets. = 她经常把床单弄皱。

一般过去时:
The wind rumpled the leaves. = 风吹皱了树叶。
He rumpled the paper in his hands. = 他把纸揉皱在手里。

现在完成时:
The journey has rumpled my clothes. = 这趟旅程把我的衣服弄皱了。
She has rumpled the perfectly ironed shirt. = 她把熨得平平整整的衬衫弄皱了。

过去完成时:
He had already rumpled the newspaper before I could read it. = 我还没来得及看，他已经把报纸揉皱了。
The children had rumpled all the toys. = 孩子们把所有的玩具都弄乱了。

将来时:
The strong wind will ruckle and rumple the sails. = 强风将使船帆褶皱和起皱。
I think the new sofa will soon rumple. = 我想新的沙发很快就会起皱。

虚拟语气:
If he sat down carefully, he wouldn’t rumple his clothes. = 如果他小心地坐下，他就不会把衣服弄皱。
I wish I hadn't rumpled the important document. = 我真希望我没有把重要的文件弄皱。

被动语态:
The sheets were rumpled by the cat. = 床单被猫弄皱了。
The clothes were badly rumpled after the long journey. = 长途旅行后，衣服皱巴巴的。

疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't rumple his shirt this time. = 他这次没有把衬衫弄皱。
She didn't mean to rumple the tablecloth. = 她不是故意把桌布弄皱的。
