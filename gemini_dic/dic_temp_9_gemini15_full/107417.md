lammergeier:/ˈlæmɜːrdʒaɪər/| n. |胡兀鹫|A large bird of prey of the Old World, having a distinctive reddish-brown plumage and a powerful hooked beak.  It is known for its habit of dropping bones from a height to break them open and access the marrow.|The lammergeier soared effortlessly above the mountain peaks. 胡兀鹫轻松地翱翔在山峰之上。|近义词：bearded vulture|反义词:None
