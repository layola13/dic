elizabethanism:/ˌɪˌlɪzəˈbiːθənɪzəm/| n. |伊丽莎白时代；伊丽莎白风格|The cultural and artistic characteristics of the reign of Queen Elizabeth I of England (1558-1603).  This includes literature, architecture, music, fashion, and politics.  Elizabethanism is characterized by a flourishing of the arts, exploration, and a sense of national pride.|例句：Shakespeare is the quintessential example of Elizabethanism in literature.  莎士比亚是伊丽莎白时代文学的典型代表。

The period of Elizabethanism witnessed significant advancements in naval exploration.  伊丽莎白时代见证了航海探险的重大进步。|近义词：Elizabethan age, Elizabethan era|反义词：无
