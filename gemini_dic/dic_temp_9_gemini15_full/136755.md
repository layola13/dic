nonsentient: /ˌnɒnˈsɛnʃənt/| adj. |无知觉的，无感觉的 | Lacking the capacity for feeling or sensation; inanimate.  |例句：The computer, despite its advanced capabilities, remains a nonsentient machine.  计算机，尽管功能先进，仍然是一台无知觉的机器。

例句：Rocks and mountains are nonsentient objects.  岩石和山脉是无知觉的物体。


近义词：unfeeling, inanimate, insensible, unconscious, unperceptive

反义词：sentient, conscious, feeling, perceptive

词源：The word "nonsentient" is a combination of the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "sentient" (meaning "capable of feeling or perceiving").  "Sentient" itself comes from the Latin word "sentire," meaning "to feel" or "to perceive."
