obsessives:/əbˈsɛsɪvz/| n.|痴迷者，沉迷者|People who are excessively preoccupied with a particular thing, idea, or feeling.|例句：The obsessives in the online forum dedicated hours to debating minor details.  在线论坛里的痴迷者们花了好几个小时争论细枝末节。|近义词：addicts, fanatics, maniacs|反义词：indifferent people, apathetic people
