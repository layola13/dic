searchingly:/ˈsɜːrtʃɪŋli/ | adv. | 探索地；探究地；锐利地；逼问地 | In a way that searches or examines thoroughly and intently; penetratingly.  It implies a deep, insightful examination or questioning. |例句：He looked at her searchingly, trying to understand her true feelings. 他意味深长地看着她，试图了解她的真实感受。

例句：She questioned the witness searchingly, leaving no stone unturned. 她逼问证人，没有放过任何细节。


近义词：penetratively, inquisitively, piercingly, intensely


反义词：superficially, casually, vaguely
