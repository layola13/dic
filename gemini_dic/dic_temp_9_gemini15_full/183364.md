reproducibly:/ˌriːprəˈdjuːsəbli/| adj.| 可重复地，可再现地 |  Able to be reproduced; capable of being repeated with consistent results.  It implies that an experiment, process, or result can be obtained again and again under the same conditions. | The experiment was conducted reproducibly, yielding the same results each time.  实验结果可重复，每次都得到相同的结果。| consistently, reliably | unreproducibly, inconsistently
