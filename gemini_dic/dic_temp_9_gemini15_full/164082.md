prediscriminating: /ˌpriːdɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 预先歧视的；有偏见的 |  Showing bias or prejudice beforehand; tending to discriminate before having full information.  This word is a relatively rare and less common usage. It suggests a predisposition towards discrimination, a pre-existing bias that influences judgment before all facts are considered. |  The prediscriminating attitude of the hiring manager led him to overlook qualified female applicants.  = 这位招聘经理事先就存在偏见的态度导致他忽略了合格的女申请人。  |  biased, prejudiced, discriminatory |  unbiased, impartial, fair |  adj.
