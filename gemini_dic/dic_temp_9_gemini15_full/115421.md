mantis:/ˈmænɪs/| n. |螳螂|A mantis is an insect of the order Mantodea, characterized by its raptorial forelegs, which it uses to capture prey.  Mantises are known for their predatory behavior and often exhibit camouflage.  They are found in diverse habitats worldwide.|例句：The mantis patiently waited, camouflaged among the leaves, for its unsuspecting prey.  螳螂耐心地等待着，伪装在树叶中，等待着毫无戒心的猎物。

例句：I saw a praying mantis in my garden yesterday.  我昨天在我的花园里看到一只螳螂。|近义词：praying mantis (more common name)|反义词: N/A|词性:n.
