recaning:/ˌriːˈkeɪnɪŋ/| n. |重新编织(椅子等);修理(椅子等)|The process of repairing or replacing the woven seat of a chair or other piece of furniture.  It involves removing the old cane webbing and replacing it with new. |例句：The antique chair needed recaning before it could be used again. 这把古董椅子需要重新编织才能再次使用。The recaning of the chairs took several days. 重新编织这些椅子花了几天时间。|近义词：reseating, repairing, restoring |反义词：destroying, breaking, ruining |名词
