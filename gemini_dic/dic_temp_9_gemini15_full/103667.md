jolly:/ˈdʒɒli/ /ˈdʒɑːli/| adj., adv., v.|快乐的；愉快的；非常；完全；使高兴；热闹|

英文解析:
As an adjective, "jolly" means cheerful and merry. As an adverb, it means "very" or "extremely." As a verb, it means to make someone happy or to have a lively and enjoyable time.  The word has a slightly informal and old-fashioned feel to it in many contexts.

例句:
He's a jolly good fellow. = 他是个非常好的家伙。

We had a jolly good time at the party. = 我们在派对上玩得很开心。


近义词:
cheerful, merry, happy, gay, joyous, delightful, amusing, fun, merry, excellent, very, extremely

反义词:
sad, unhappy, miserable, gloomy, morose, depressing
