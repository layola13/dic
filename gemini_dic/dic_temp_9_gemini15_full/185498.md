reusableness: /ˌriːˈjuːzəbələnəs/ | n. | 可重用性，可再利用性 | The ability of something to be used again.  This refers to the characteristic of being able to be used multiple times without significant loss of function or value.  It's often applied to materials, products, and processes. | Example: The reusableness of the plastic containers reduces waste.  可重复使用的塑料容器减少了浪费。 The high reusableness of the software components speeds up development. 软件组件的高可重用性加快了开发速度。 | 近义词: recyclability, repeatability,  re-usability | 反义词: disposability, single-use  | 名词
