focalizing:/ˈfoʊkəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使聚焦；使成为焦点 | The act of focusing attention on a particular aspect or element, often within a narrative or description. It involves selecting a specific perspective or point of view through which the events and details are presented.  | Example: The author skillfully focalizes the narrative through the child's innocent eyes, highlighting their unique perspective on the events.  作者巧妙地通过孩子的纯真视角来聚焦叙事，突显了他们对事件的独特看法。 | 近义词：focusing, centering, highlighting | 反义词：diffusing, dispersing | 动词
