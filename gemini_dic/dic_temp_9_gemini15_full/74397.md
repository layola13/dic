framings:/ˈfreɪmɪŋz/| n.|框架；构架；结构；（对事件或信息的）诠释；表达方式|The act of framing something, or the way in which something is framed.  It can refer to the physical act of putting something in a frame, or the metaphorical act of structuring or interpreting something.|例句：The framings of the windows were made of oak. 窗框是用橡木做的。

例句：The museum's framings of the historical events were controversial. 博物馆对历史事件的诠释是有争议的。|近义词：structures, interpretations, presentations, constructions|反义词：lack of structure, misrepresentation
