practicing:/ˈpræktɪsɪŋ/| v.|练习，实践|The act of doing something repeatedly to improve one's skill or ability.  It implies a continuous and focused effort towards mastery.|例句：She is practicing the piano every day. = 她每天都在练习钢琴。

例句：He's been practicing his English speaking skills. = 他一直在练习英语口语。


近义词：rehearsing, exercising, training, drilling|反义词：neglecting, ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am practicing the guitar. = 我正在练习吉他。
They are practicing for the upcoming game. = 他们在为即将到来的比赛练习。


一般过去时:
I practiced the piano for two hours yesterday. = 我昨天练了两个小时钢琴。
We practiced our presentation all afternoon. = 我们整个下午都在排练我们的演示文稿。


现在完成时:
I have been practicing yoga for three years. = 我练习瑜伽三年了。
She has practiced law for over a decade. = 她从事法律工作十多年了。


过去完成时:
I had practiced the speech many times before the presentation. = 我在演讲之前已经练习了很多次了。
They had practiced the dance routine for weeks before the performance. = 他们在演出前几周就一直在练习舞蹈动作了。


将来时:
I will be practicing my French tomorrow. = 我明天将练习我的法语。
We will practice the song again next week. = 我们下周将再次练习这首歌。


虚拟语气:
If I practiced more, I would be a better musician. = 如果我多练习，我会成为一个更好的音乐家。
I wish I had practiced harder for the exam. = 我希望我为考试更加努力地练习了。


被动语态:
The skills are practiced regularly. = 这些技能定期练习。
The play was practiced intensively for months. = 这出戏排练了几个月。


疑问句:
Are you practicing for the marathon? = 你在为马拉松做准备吗？
What are they practicing? = 他们在练习什么？


否定句:
I am not practicing today. = 我今天不练习。
He didn't practice his lines before the play. = 他在演出前没有练习台词。
