multicurrency: /ˌmʌltiˈkʌrənsi/ | n. | 多币种，多种货币 |  Refers to the ability to handle transactions and accounts in multiple currencies.  It's commonly used in international finance, banking, and e-commerce contexts. | Example: The software offers multicurrency support, allowing users to manage accounts in various currencies.  这个软件提供多币种支持，允许用户管理各种货币的账户。 | 近义词: multi-currency, multiple currencies | 反义词: single currency, monocurrency | 名词
