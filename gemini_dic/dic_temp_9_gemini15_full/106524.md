krill:/krɪl/| n. |磷虾|Small, shrimp-like crustaceans that live in large swarms in the ocean. They are a vital food source for many marine animals, including whales, seals, and penguins.| 例句：Antarctic krill are a crucial part of the Antarctic food web.  南极磷虾是南极食物网的重要组成部分。
Many seabirds depend on krill for their survival. 许多海鸟依靠磷虾生存。|近义词：shrimp, crustacean|反义词：无
