painstaking:/ˈpeɪnzteɪkɪŋ/| adj.|煞费苦心的；一丝不苟的；费力的 |  Meticulous and diligent; requiring or showing great care and effort.  |例句：The restoration of the painting was a painstaking process.  修复这幅画是一个费力的过程。

例句：She gave a painstakingly detailed account of the accident. 她对事故做了详尽细致的描述。


近义词：meticulous, diligent, careful, thorough, assiduous, laborious

反义词：careless, negligent, sloppy, cursory, superficial
