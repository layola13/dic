pretincture:/prɪˈtɪŋktʃər/| n.| 预浸液，预染液 |A pretincture is a preliminary tincture or staining, often used in microscopy or histology to prepare a sample for further processing or staining. It might involve a light staining to highlight certain structures before applying a more intense or specific stain.  |Example: The pretincture allowed for better visualization of the cell nuclei before the main staining procedure.  预浸液使得在主要染色程序之前更好地观察细胞核。|近义词：preliminary stain, initial dye |反义词：final stain, counterstain|名词
