hemautography:/ˌhiːməʊˈtɒɡrəfi/| n. |血细胞图谱；血液自动记录|The study or process of graphically recording the distribution of blood cells.  It is a specialized technique used in hematology to analyze blood samples.  While the term is less common now, it historically referred to methods of automatically recording blood cell counts and characteristics.|Example:  The research team used hemautography to analyze the blood samples from the patients with leukemia.|近义词:hematology, blood cell analysis|反义词: N/A
