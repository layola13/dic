overbalance:/ˌoʊvərˈbæləns/| vt. vi. n.|使失去平衡；失去平衡；不平衡；优势|To cause (something) to lose its balance; to lose one's balance; a lack of balance; a preponderance.|例句：The heavy load caused the cart to overbalance.  重物使手推车失去平衡。 The dancer overbalanced and fell. 舞蹈演员失去平衡摔倒了。The overbalance of power led to conflict. 权力失衡导致了冲突。|近义词：tip, upset, topple, imbalance|反义词：balance, equilibrium
