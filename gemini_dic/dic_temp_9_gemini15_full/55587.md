disuniformity: /ˌdɪsjuːnɪˈfɔːrməti/ | n. | 不均匀性；不一致性 | The lack of uniformity; unevenness or inconsistency.  It refers to a state where things are not uniform or consistent, showing variations or discrepancies. |


例句：

The disuniformity in the paint job was quite noticeable.  油漆工作的参差不齐非常明显。

The disuniformity of the soil texture affected the crop yield. 土壤质地的不均匀影响了作物产量。


近义词：inconsistency, unevenness, irregularity, heterogeneity, variation


反义词：uniformity, consistency, regularity, homogeneity


词性：名词


词源：

* **dis-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of"
* **uniform-**:  from Latin "uniformis," meaning "of one form"
* **-ity**: suffix forming nouns of state or condition
