place:/pleɪs/ | n. | 地方，位置，场所 | A place where people can relax and have fun. 一个人们可以放松和玩乐的地方。|近义词：spot, location, site, position |反义词：absence, nowhere


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This place is beautiful. = 这个地方很漂亮。
Where does he work? = 他在哪里工作？


一般过去时:
We went to a nice place yesterday. = 昨天我们去了一个不错的地方。
Where did you live before? = 你以前住在哪里？


现在完成时:
I have been to many places in China. = 我去过中国很多地方。
Have you ever visited this place before? = 你以前来过这个地方吗？


过去完成时:
I had already been to that place before. = 我以前已经去过那个地方了。
Where had they gone before the accident? = 事故发生前他们去过哪里？


将来时:
We will go to a new place next week. = 我们下周要去一个新的地方。
Where will you be tomorrow? = 你明天会在哪里？


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would travel to many places. = 如果我有更多钱，我会去很多地方旅行。
I wish I were in a better place right now. = 我希望我现在在一个更好的地方。


被动语态:
The meeting place was changed. = 会议地点被改变了。
This place is often visited by tourists. = 这个地方经常被游客参观。


疑问句:
Where is the library? = 图書館在哪里？
What place is this? = 这是什么地方？


否定句:
There is no place like home. = 没有地方比家更好了。
He didn't find a suitable place to live. = 他没有找到合适的地方居住。


PHRASE->
take place:发生
in place:在适当的位置
out of place:不合适
place an order:下订单
in the first place:首先
