oxyhaemacyanin: /ˌɒksiˈhiːməsaɪənɪn/ | n. |血蓝蛋白|  A blue respiratory protein containing copper, found in the hemolymph of many mollusks and arthropods. It transports oxygen throughout the body. |Example: The oxyhaemocyanin in the blood of the octopus allows it to survive in oxygen-poor environments.  章鱼血液中的血蓝蛋白使其能够在缺氧环境中生存。|近义词:hemocyanin|反义词:None|名词
