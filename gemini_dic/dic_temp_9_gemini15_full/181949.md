remarking:/rɪˈmɑːrkɪŋ/| v.|评论；谈论；值得注意的 |Making a comment or observation; noting something worthy of attention.|例句：He remarked on the beauty of the sunset. 他评论了日落的美丽。|例句：The doctor remarked upon the patient's rapid recovery. 医生对病人的迅速康复表示注意。|近义词：commenting, observing, noting|反义词：ignoring, overlooking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is remarking on the current political situation. = 他正在评论当前的政治局势。
She remarks on the beauty of the scenery every time she visits. = 她每次来访都评论风景的美丽。

一般过去时:
They remarked that the food was delicious. = 他们评论说食物很美味。
He remarked upon her intelligence and wit. = 他评论了她的聪明和才智。

现在完成时:
She has remarked on the changes in the neighborhood. = 她已经评论了附近的变化。
He has remarked several times on the importance of punctuality. = 他已经多次评论了准时的重要性。

过去完成时:
They had remarked on the strange atmosphere before the incident. = 事情发生前他们就注意到了奇怪的气氛。
She had remarked several times about his strange behavior. = 她之前就多次评论过他奇怪的行为。

将来时:
He will remark on the results of the experiment tomorrow. = 他明天将评论实验的结果。
She will remark on the beauty of the dress during the party. = 她在派对上会评论这件礼服的美丽。

虚拟语气:
If he had remarked on the problem earlier, it might have been solved. = 如果他早点评论这个问题，它也许就解决了。
I wish he had remarked on the potential dangers. = 我希望他早点指出潜在的危险。

被动语态:
The importance of the event was remarked upon by many. = 许多人对这一事件的重要性进行了评论。
His talent was remarked upon by all the judges. = 所有评委都注意到了他的才能。


疑问句:
What did he remark about the meeting? = 他对会议有什么评论？
Did she remark on the quality of the products? = 她对产品的质量有什么评论吗？

否定句:
He didn't remark on the incident. = 他没有评论这件事。
She didn't remark on his rude behavior. = 她没有评论他的粗鲁行为。


PHRASE->
remark on = 对…发表评论
remark about = 对…发表评论
make a remark = 发表评论
pass remark = 顺便说说
remarkable = 显著的
