keelhaul:/ˈkiːlˌhɔːl/| vt.|  拖曳水底；严惩；残酷对待 | To drag (someone) along the bottom of a ship's keel as a punishment. This was a brutal form of punishment used historically in the navy, involving dragging a person underwater along the ship's keel.  |例句：They keelhauled the sailor for desertion.  他们因为逃兵罪而把水手拖曳在船底。  |近义词：flog, punish severely,  |反义词：reward, praise
