buyouts:/ˈbaɪaʊts/| n. |收购；买断；买断交易|The acquisition of a company's assets or stock by another company or investor, often resulting in the cessation of the acquired company's operations or its integration into the acquiring entity.  It involves purchasing all or a controlling share of a company's equity.|例句：The company announced a management buyout, giving its employees ownership.|近义词：acquisition, takeover, purchase|反义词：sell-off, divestiture
