plummer:/ˈplʌmər/| n. |管道工，水管工 |A plumber is a tradesperson who specializes in installing and maintaining water and drainage systems. They work with pipes, fittings, fixtures, and appliances related to water supply and waste disposal.|例句：The plumber fixed the leaky faucet.  管道工修好了漏水的龙头。|例句：We need to call a plumber to repair the broken pipe. 我们需要叫个管道工来修理破裂的管道。|近义词：pipefitter, sanitary engineer |反义词：无
