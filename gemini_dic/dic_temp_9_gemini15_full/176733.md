read-through: /ˈriːdθruː/ | n. |通读；预读；练习朗读 | A complete reading of something, often for practice or preparation.  It can also refer to a rehearsal run-through. |例句：She did a read-through of the script before the first rehearsal. 她排练前通读了一遍剧本。


例句：The director called for a read-through of the play.  导演要求进行剧本的通读排练。


近义词：rehearsal, review, practice reading


反义词：cursory glance, skimming


词性:名词
