shucker:/ˈʃʌkər/| n. |剥壳者；牡蛎壳剥除工；（尤指）玉米剥皮工|A person who shucks (removes the shells or husks from) things, especially oysters or corn.|例句：He worked as a shucker in a seafood restaurant. 他在一家海鲜餐厅做剥壳工。|例句：The shucker expertly removed the corn kernels from the cob. 剥壳工熟练地将玉米粒从玉米棒上剥下来。|近义词：husker, sheller|反义词：planter (反义词较为宽泛，指种植者，与剥壳者形成对照)|名词
