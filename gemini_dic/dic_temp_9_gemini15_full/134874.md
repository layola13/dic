nonmonogamous:/ˌnɒnməˈnɒɡəməs/| adj. |非一夫一妻制的；非单偶制的 |Not based on marriage to only one person at a time.  It describes relationships where individuals have romantic or sexual relationships with more than one person at the same time, with the knowledge and consent of all involved.  |例句：The couple chose to live a nonmonogamous lifestyle. 这对夫妇选择过非一夫一妻制的生活。

例句：Many people are exploring nonmonogamous relationships as a way to challenge traditional relationship structures. 许多人正在探索非一夫一妻制的关系，以此来挑战传统的婚恋结构。


近义词：polyamorous, open-relationship, multi-partnered


反义词：monogamous


词性：形容词
