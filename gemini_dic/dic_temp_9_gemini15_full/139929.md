odist:/ˈoʊdɪst/ | n. | 颂诗作家，抒情诗人 | A writer of odes.  An odist is a poet who writes odes, which are typically lyrical poems expressing strong feelings or commemorating events. | Example: John Keats was a renowned odist, celebrated for his beautiful and emotionally resonant odes.  约翰·济慈是一位著名的颂诗作家，以其优美而感人的颂诗而闻名。 | 近义词：poet, lyricist | 反义词：prosaist (This is a less direct antonym; it refers to a writer of prose, contrasting with the poetic nature of an odist.)
