gibbous:/ˈɡɪbəs/| adj.|凸出的， gibbous moon 凸月|  Describes a phase of the Moon or a similar celestial body when more than half but not all of it is illuminated, appearing as a bulging or humped shape.|The gibbous moon hung heavy in the night sky.|近义词：convex, bulging, humped|反义词：concave, waning
