mis-reformed:/ˌmɪsˈfɔːrmd/| adj.| 畸形的，被错误改造的 | Incorrectly or badly reformed;  having undergone a flawed or incomplete process of reformation or restructuring. The mis-reformed building code allowed for unsafe construction practices.  被错误修改的建筑规范允许了不安全的施工做法。|近义词：deformed, malformed, flawed, imperfect, distorted |反义词：reformed, improved, perfected, well-structured
