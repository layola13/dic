rabies:/ˈreɪbiːz/| n. |狂犬病|A serious viral disease that affects the central nervous system, typically transmitted through the bite of an infected animal.|例句：The dog had rabies and had to be put down. 这条狗患有狂犬病，不得不被安乐死。|近义词：hydrophobia|反义词:None
