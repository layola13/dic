dangerous:/ˈdeɪndʒərəs/| adj.|危险的，有害的|The word "dangerous" describes something that poses a threat or risk of harm, injury, or damage. It can refer to physical dangers like a dangerous animal or a dangerous road, or to abstract dangers like a dangerous situation or a dangerous idea. |例句：That knife is dangerous.  那把刀很危险。

例句：The storm created a dangerous situation.  暴风雨制造了一个危险的局面。

近义词：hazardous, perilous, risky, unsafe, threatening

反义词：safe, secure, harmless, innocuous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
That job is dangerous. = 那份工作很危险。
This area is dangerous at night. = 这个区域晚上很危险。

一般过去时:
The situation was dangerous yesterday. = 昨天的局势很危险。
The road was dangerous because of the ice. = 由于结冰，这条路很危险。

现在完成时:
The situation has become dangerous. = 局势已经变得危险了。
The bridge has been dangerous for years. = 这座桥多年来一直很危险。


过去完成时:
The situation had become dangerous before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，情况已经变得很危险了。
They had known the area was dangerous. = 他们知道那个地区很危险。

将来时:
The situation will become dangerous if we don't act. = 如果我们不采取行动，情况将会变得危险。
The weather forecast predicts dangerous conditions tomorrow. = 天气预报预测明天会有危险的天气状况。


虚拟语气:
If I knew it was dangerous, I wouldn't have gone. = 如果我知道那很危险，我就不会去了。
I wish I had known it was so dangerous. = 我真希望我早知道这么危险。


被动语态:
The area is considered dangerous. = 该地区被认为很危险。
The bridge was declared dangerous and closed. = 这座桥被宣布为危险并关闭。


疑问句:
Is this path dangerous? = 这条路危险吗？
Are there any dangerous animals in this area? = 这个地区有危险的动物吗？


否定句:
The situation is not dangerous. = 情况并不危险。
The road isn't dangerous during the daytime. = 白天这条路不危险。
