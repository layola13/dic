conveniences:/kənˈviːniənsɪz/| n. |便利设施；方便；便利 |Things that make life easier or more comfortable.  Conveniences are features or services that make tasks easier or more pleasant. |例句：The apartment complex boasts many conveniences, such as a swimming pool and laundry facilities.  这个公寓大楼拥有许多便利设施，例如游泳池和洗衣设施。|例句：Modern conveniences like dishwashers and washing machines save us a lot of time and effort.  像洗碗机和洗衣机这样的现代化便利设施为我们节省了很多时间和精力。|近义词：amenities, facilities, advantages, comforts |反义词：inconveniences, hardships, difficulties |名词
