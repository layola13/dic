co-sovereign:/ˌkoʊˈsɑːvrən/| adj.| 共主权的，联合主权的 |  Relating to or denoting a state or territory ruled jointly by two or more sovereigns.  This term implies a shared or divided sovereignty, where ultimate authority is not solely vested in a single ruler or entity.  | 例句：The two kingdoms existed in a state of co-sovereignty for many years.  = 这两个王国多年来处于联合主权状态。


The treaty established a co-sovereign arrangement between the two nations, with shared control over certain territories. = 这项条约在两国之间建立了联合主权安排，共同控制某些领土。| 近义词：joint sovereign, shared sovereign | 反义词：sole sovereign, absolute sovereign |  adj.
