resets:/ˈriːsɛts/ | vt. n. | 重置；恢复默认设置；使恢复正常 | To return (something) to a previous state or condition, typically one of default settings or zero value.  | 例句：The system automatically resets after a power failure. = 系统在断电后会自动重置。 | 例句：He performed a hard reset on his computer to fix the software glitch. = 他对电脑进行了硬重置以修复软件故障。 | 近义词：restart, reboot, initialize, restore | 反义词：set, establish


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The computer resets automatically after a power outage. = 电脑在停电后会自动重启。
He resets his password every month. = 他每个月都会重置密码。


一般过去时:
I reset the router after it stopped working. = 路由器停止工作后，我重新启动了它。
She reset her alarm clock after accidentally snoozing it. = 她不小心按了贪睡后重新设置了闹钟。


现在完成时:
Have you reset your preferences yet? = 你已经重置你的偏好设置了吗？
The technician has reset the server multiple times. = 技术人员已经多次重置服务器。


过去完成时:
The system had reset itself before the engineer arrived. = 工程师到达之前，系统已经自行重置了。
She had reset the game before starting a new level. = 在开始新关卡之前，她已经重置了游戏。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

疑问句:
