pootled:/ˈpuːtəld/| v.|闲逛，慢慢地走，懒洋洋地走|To move slowly and leisurely, often in a relaxed or aimless manner.|例句：We pootled around the town, enjoying the sights. 我们在镇上闲逛，欣赏着景色。例句：After lunch, we pootled down to the beach. 午饭后，我们慢慢地走到海滩。|近义词：strolled, ambled, sauntered|反义词：rushed, hurried


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They pootle around the park every afternoon. = 他们每天下午都在公园里闲逛。
We pootle along the coast road. = 我们沿着海岸公路慢慢地走。


一般过去时:
She pootled through the countryside. = 她悠闲地穿过乡村。
They pootled home after the picnic. = 野餐后，他们慢慢地回家了。
