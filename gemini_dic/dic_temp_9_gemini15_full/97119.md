infighters:/ˈɪnfaɪtərz/| n.|内战者；近战格斗者|A person who fights from close range, especially in boxing or other contact sports.  They are characterized by their aggressive close-quarters fighting style.|Example:  He's a natural infighter, always looking to get inside and land powerful punches. 他天生就是个近战格斗者，总是试图近身攻击并击出重拳。  Many MMA fighters are skilled infighters. 许多综合格斗运动员都是熟练的近战格斗者。|近义词：brawler, slugger, close-range fighter|反义词：outfighter, boxer (in the sense of a long-range boxer)|名词
