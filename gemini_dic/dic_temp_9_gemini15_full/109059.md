legality:/liˈɡæləti/| n. |合法性；法律效力|The legality of the contract is questionable.  该合同的合法性值得怀疑。|近义词：lawfulness, validity |反义词：illegality


英文解析：The state or quality of being legal; conformity to the law.  The legality of an action or situation is whether it is allowed by law.


例句：

The legality of the new law is being challenged in court. 新法律的合法性正在法庭上受到质疑。

They questioned the legality of the police search. 他们质疑警方搜查的合法性。


词源：

From legal + -ity.  "Legal" comes from the Old French *legal, from Latin legalis, from lex, legis "law".  "-ity" is a suffix forming nouns of quality or state.
