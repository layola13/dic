interglacial:/ˌɪntərˈɡleɪʃəl/| n. |间冰期|A period of warmer temperatures between two glacial periods within an ice age.  Interglacial periods are characterized by the retreat of glaciers and ice sheets, rising sea levels, and changes in vegetation and animal life.  |例句：The current interglacial period, the Holocene epoch, began about 11,700 years ago.  目前的间冰期，全新世时期，始于大约11700年前。

The last interglacial period, the Eemian, lasted from about 130,000 to 115,000 years ago. 上一个间冰期，艾姆间冰期，持续了大约从13万年前到11.5万年前。|近义词：warm period, interstadial |反义词：glacial period, ice age
