caveating:/ˈkævɪˌeɪtɪŋ/| v.| 附带警告；附加限制条件 |  Adding a warning or proviso; qualifying a statement.  |例句：He agreed to the plan, caveating that it would require considerable funding. 他同意了这个计划，但附加条件是需要大量资金。

例句：The government announced the new policy, caveating that it would be reviewed after six months. 政府宣布了这项新政策，但附加条件是六个月后将对其进行审查。


近义词：qualifying, conditioning, limiting, restricting


反义词：unconditionally affirming, unconditionally supporting


词性: 动词
