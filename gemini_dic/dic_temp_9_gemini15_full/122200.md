misuse:/ˌmɪsˈjuːz/| vt.|滥用，误用|To use something incorrectly or for the wrong purpose.  It often implies a harmful or inappropriate use.|例句：He misused his power as manager to benefit his friends. 他滥用经理的职权来帮助他的朋友。|例句：She misused the company car for personal errands. 她把公司汽车用于私人事务。|近义词：abuse, mishandle, misapply|反义词：use properly, utilize effectively|动词


USAGE->

He misuses his authority. = 他滥用职权。
