elevate:/ˈɛləveɪt/| vt. |提升；举起；提高；鼓舞；使兴奋| to raise something to a higher position; to improve or increase something to a higher level; to uplift someone's spirits|例句：They elevated the bridge to allow larger ships to pass. 他们提升了桥梁以允许更大的船只通过。

例句：The news elevated his spirits. 这个消息使他精神振奋。


近义词：raise, lift, hoist, promote, uplift, enhance


反义词：lower, depress, degrade, diminish


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He elevates the weights every day. 他每天举重。
The company elevates its employees based on merit. 公司根据功绩提升员工。

一般过去时:
She elevated the platform to a higher level. 她把平台提升到了更高的水平。
The good news elevated my mood considerably. 好消息极大地改善了我的心情。

现在完成时:
They have elevated the statue to a prominent position. 他们已将雕像提升到显眼的位置。
This experience has elevated my understanding of the subject.  这次经历提升了我对这个主题的理解。

过去完成时:
He had elevated his game before the final match. 在决赛之前他已经提升了自己的水平。
The team had elevated their performance significantly by the end of the season. 到赛季结束时，球队已经显著提升了他们的表现。

将来时:
They will elevate the train tracks to prevent flooding. 他们将抬高火车轨道以防止洪水。
The new policy will elevate the standard of living for many families. 新政策将提高许多家庭的生活水平。

虚拟语气:
If they elevated the bridge, the traffic would flow more smoothly. 如果他们提升桥梁，交通就会更加顺畅。
I wish they would elevate the conversation to a more professional level. 我希望他们能把谈话提升到更专业的层面。

被动语态:
The bridge was elevated to accommodate larger ships. 桥梁被提升以容纳更大的船只。
His spirits were elevated by the unexpected gift. 他因意外的礼物而精神振奋。

疑问句:
Will they elevate the building? 他们会提升建筑物吗？
How did the experience elevate your perspective?  这次经历如何提升了你的视角？

否定句:
They did not elevate the prices. 他们没有提高价格。
The situation did not elevate his mood at all.  这种情况丝毫没有改善他的心情。
