respecify:/ˌriːˈspɛsɪfaɪ/| vt.|重新指定；重新说明；再规定|To specify again or differently; to restate the specifications of.|The engineer had to respecify the dimensions of the bridge after the initial design proved flawed.  工程师在最初的设计被证明有缺陷后不得不重新指定桥梁的尺寸。|redefine, restate, revise|accept, approve|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They respecify the requirements every year. = 他们每年都会重新规定要求。
We respecify the parameters for the experiment. = 我们重新指定实验的参数。


一般过去时:
The client respecified their needs after the initial proposal. = 客户在收到初步提案后重新说明了他们的需求。
The team respecified the project timeline. = 团队重新规定了项目时间表。


现在完成时:
The company has respecified its production goals. = 公司已经重新规定了其生产目标。

过去完成时:

They had respecified the contract terms before signing. = 他们在签署之前已经重新规定了合同条款。


将来时:
We will respecify the budget next month. = 我们下个月将重新指定预算。
