/ˈkaʊntərtʃɑːrdʒɪz/ | n. |反诉，反控告 | Accusations made in response to accusations made by someone else.  It refers to a counterclaim or a retaliatory accusation.  | The defendant filed countercharges against the plaintiff, alleging defamation.  被告对原告提起反诉，指控其诽谤。 | counterclaims, rebuttals |  charges, accusations |
