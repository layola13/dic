corroborate:/kəˈrɒbəreɪt/| vt.|证实；确证|To support or confirm (a statement, theory, or finding) with evidence.|例句：The witness's testimony corroborated the suspect's alibi. 目击证人的证词证实了嫌疑人的不在场证明。|例句：Further research is needed to corroborate these findings. 需要进一步的研究来证实这些发现。|近义词：confirm, verify, substantiate, validate|反义词：contradict, refute, deny


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The evidence corroborates his story. = 证据证实了他的说法。
She corroborates the witness's account. = 她证实了证人的说法。

一般过去时:
The results corroborated the hypothesis. = 结果证实了假设。
The study corroborated previous findings. = 这项研究证实了先前的发现。

现在完成时:
Recent studies have corroborated the effectiveness of the treatment. = 最近的研究已经证实了这种治疗的有效性。
The new evidence has corroborated the police's theory. = 新证据证实了警方的理论。

过去完成时:
Previous research had corroborated the link between smoking and lung cancer. = 先前的研究已经证实了吸烟和肺癌之间的联系。
The historical records had corroborated the legend. = 历史记录证实了这个传说。

将来时:
Further tests will corroborate the diagnosis. = 进一步的测试将证实诊断结果。
We hope the upcoming data will corroborate our findings. = 我们希望即将到来的数据会证实我们的发现。

虚拟语气:
If more evidence were to corroborate this theory, it would be widely accepted. = 如果有更多证据证实这一理论，它将被广泛接受。
I wish the results had corroborated our initial hypothesis. = 我希望结果能证实我们最初的假设。

被动语态:
His alibi was corroborated by several witnesses. = 他的不在场证明得到了几位证人的证实。
The theory is corroborated by recent studies. = 该理论得到了最近研究的证实。

疑问句:
Does this evidence corroborate your claims? = 这个证据能证实你的说法吗？
Can we find further data to corroborate the results? = 我们能找到进一步的数据来证实结果吗？

否定句:
The data did not corroborate the hypothesis. = 数据没有证实假设。
Nothing corroborated the rumor. = 没有什么能证实这个谣言。
