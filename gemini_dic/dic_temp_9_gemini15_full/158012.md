plant:/plænt/ /plɑːnt/| n. vt.| 植物；工厂；种植；安置|A plant is a living organism of the kind exemplified by trees, shrubs, herbs, grasses, ferns, and mosses, typically growing in a permanent site, absorbing water and inorganic substances through its roots, and synthesizing nutrients in its leaves by photosynthesis.|例句：The garden is full of beautiful plants. 花园里长满了美丽的植物。  The new factory is a large plant. 新工厂是一个大型工厂。|近义词：flora, vegetation, factory, facility|反义词：animal, creature
