checkerboarding:/ˈtʃɛkərˌbɔːrdɪŋ/ | n. |棋盘格图案；棋盘式排列 |The arrangement of something in a pattern resembling a checkerboard, characterized by alternating squares of different colors or textures.  |例句：The field was a checkerboarding of green and brown from the crops. 田野里绿棕色相间的庄稼呈现出棋盘格图案。

例句：The designer used checkerboarding in the fabric to create a visually interesting effect. 设计师在织物中使用了棋盘格图案来创造视觉上的趣味效果。


近义词：checkered pattern, tessellation, mosaic


反义词：uniform pattern, solid color


词性：名词
