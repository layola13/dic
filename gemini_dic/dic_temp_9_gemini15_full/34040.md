citrusy:/ˈsɪtrəsi/| adj.|柑橘味的，柑橘香的|Having the flavor or scent of citrus fruits.  The word evokes the characteristic tangy and slightly sweet aroma and taste associated with oranges, lemons, limes, grapefruits, etc.|例句：The cake had a delicious citrusy aroma. 这蛋糕有令人愉快的柑橘香味。

例句：The marmalade had a strong citrusy flavor. 这果酱有浓郁的柑橘味。


近义词：lemony, orangy, limey, zesty


反义词：bland, tasteless


词性:形容词
