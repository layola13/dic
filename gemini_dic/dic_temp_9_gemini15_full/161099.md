postdocs:/ˈpoʊstˌdɑːks/| n. |博士后研究人员|Postdoctoral researchers are scholars who have completed their doctoral degrees and are conducting further research, often under the supervision of a professor or principal investigator. They typically hold temporary positions. |Many postdocs choose to pursue academic careers after their postdoctoral research.|近义词：postdoctoral fellows, postdoctoral scholars|反义词： undergraduate students, graduate students
