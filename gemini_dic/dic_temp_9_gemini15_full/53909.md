discounts:/ˈdɪskaʊnts/| n.|折扣，优惠|A reduction in the usual price of something.|The store offers discounts to students. 这家商店向学生提供折扣。|近义词：reductions, rebates, bargains|反义词：surcharges, markups


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store offers discounts on weekends. = 这家商店周末提供折扣。
They give discounts to loyal customers. = 他们给予忠实顾客折扣。

一般过去时:
They gave a 10% discount on all items last week. = 他们上周所有商品都打了九折。
The shop gave me a discount because I bought so much. = 因为我买了这么多东西，店主给了我折扣。

现在完成时:
They have offered many discounts this month. = 这个月他们已经提供了很多折扣。
The company has given us discounts for years. = 多年来，该公司一直给我们折扣。

过去完成时:
I had already received a discount before I paid. = 我付款前就已经获得了折扣。
They had given out all the discounts before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，他们已经把所有的折扣都发放完了。

将来时:
They will offer discounts during the holiday season. = 他们在节日期间会提供折扣。
The store will give a discount if you use this coupon. = 如果你使用这张优惠券，商店会给你打折。

虚拟语气:
If they offered a bigger discount, I would buy it. = 如果他们提供更大的折扣，我会买的。
I wish they would give me a discount on this item. = 我希望他们能在这个商品上给我打折。

被动语态:
Discounts are offered to senior citizens. = 给老年人提供折扣。
A discount was given to me by the cashier. = 收银员给了我折扣。

疑问句:
Are there any discounts available? = 有什么折扣吗？
Do you offer discounts for bulk purchases? = 大宗购买有折扣吗？

否定句:
They don't offer discounts on sale items. = 他们不给特价商品打折。
The store didn't give me a discount. = 商店没有给我打折。
