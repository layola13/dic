pest:/pest/| n.|害虫，讨厌的人|a small animal or insect that destroys crops or food supplies; an annoying person|例句：The farmer sprayed pesticide to kill the pests in his field.  农民喷洒杀虫剂来杀死田里的害虫。

例句：He's a real pest; he keeps bothering me. 他真是个讨厌鬼，他一直烦我。|近义词：vermin, nuisance, plague|反义词：benefactor, help

词源:
The word "pest" comes from the Old French word "peste," meaning "plague" or "disease."  Over time, its meaning shifted to include any troublesome or destructive creature, and eventually settled on its current meaning of a destructive insect or other small animal.
