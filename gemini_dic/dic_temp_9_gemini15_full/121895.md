missamples:/mɪsˈsæmpəlz/ | n. |错误样本，误采样|  A missample is a sample that is not representative of the population from which it was drawn, often due to bias in the sampling process. This can lead to inaccurate conclusions or inferences.  |例句：The study's conclusions were questionable due to the presence of several missamples in the data set.  这项研究的结论由于数据集中存在几个错误样本而值得怀疑。|近义词：erroneous sample, flawed sample, biased sample |反义词：representative sample, accurate sample |名词
