pyrometrical:/ˌpaɪrəˈmɛtrɪkəl/ | adj. |测温的，高温测量的| Relating to or using pyrometry.  Pyrometry is the science and technology of measuring high temperatures.  |例句：The pyrometrical analysis revealed the furnace temperature exceeded the safety limit.  高温测量分析显示炉温超过安全限值。

例句：The experiment used pyrometrical methods to determine the melting point of the alloy.  该实验使用测温方法来确定合金的熔点。


近义词：thermometric (if referring to temperature measurement generally), calorimetric (if referring to heat measurement)

反义词：None directly opposite, but antonyms could be related to terms implying absence of temperature measurement.


词性：形容词
