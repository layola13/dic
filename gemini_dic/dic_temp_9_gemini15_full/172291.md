put-on-airs:/pʊt ɑːn ɛrz/| v. | 装腔作势，摆架子 | To behave in a way that is intended to impress people and show that you are of a higher social class than you really are.  It often involves artificial or affected behavior. |  She put on airs when talking to the new neighbors, trying to impress them with her knowledge of fine wines. 她跟新邻居说话时装腔作势，试图用她对名酒的了解来给他们留下深刻印象。


He puts on airs about his expensive car. 他对自己的豪车很炫耀。| 近义词：pretend, affect, show off, swagger | 反义词：be humble, be modest, be down-to-earth | 动词
