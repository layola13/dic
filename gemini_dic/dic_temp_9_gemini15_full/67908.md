face-to-face: /feɪs tu feɪs/ | adj. |面对面的|  Directly in the presence of another person or people; in person.  |例句：We had a face-to-face meeting to discuss the project. 我们进行了面对面的会议来讨论这个项目。

例句：It's much better to communicate face-to-face rather than by email.  面对面交流比通过电子邮件好得多。


近义词：in person, personally, direct | 反义词：remote, virtual, online


词性：形容词 (Adjective)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They communicate face-to-face every day. = 他们每天面对面交流。
The students have face-to-face classes. = 学生们有面对面授课。


一般过去时:
We had a face-to-face conversation yesterday. = 我们昨天进行了面对面的谈话。
They conducted a face-to-face interview last week. = 他们上周进行了一次面对面的面试。


现在完成时:
I have had several face-to-face meetings with the client. = 我已经与客户进行了多次面对面的会议。
They have never had a face-to-face meeting before. = 他们以前从未有过面对面的会议。


过去完成时:
Before the phone call, they had already had a face-to-face meeting. = 在打电话之前，他们已经进行了一次面对面的会议。
She realized that she had never had a true face-to-face conversation with him. = 她意识到她从未和他进行过真正的面对面交谈。


将来时:
We will have a face-to-face meeting next week. = 我们下周将举行一次面对面的会议。
They will conduct a face-to-face interview tomorrow. = 他们明天将进行一次面对面的面试。


虚拟语气:
It would be better if we could have a face-to-face meeting. = 如果我们可以进行面对面的会议，那就更好了。
I wish I could have a face-to-face discussion with him. = 我希望我能和他进行一次面对面的讨论。


被动语态:
The meeting was conducted face-to-face. = 会议是面对面进行的。
The information was relayed face-to-face. = 信息是面对面传递的。


疑问句:
Was the interview conducted face-to-face? = 面试是面对面进行的吗？
Will the training be face-to-face or online? = 培训是面对面的还是在线的？


否定句:
The meeting was not conducted face-to-face; it was online. = 会议不是面对面进行的，而是在线进行的。
They did not have a face-to-face meeting; they communicated via email. = 他们没有进行面对面的会议；他们通过电子邮件交流。

face-to-face communication = 面对面交流
