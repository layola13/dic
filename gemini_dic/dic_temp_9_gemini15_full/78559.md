ghostwrote:/ˈɡoʊst roʊt/| vt.|代笔；替...捉刀|To write something, such as a book or article, for someone else, who is then credited with writing it.  This is often done secretly or anonymously.|例句：She ghostwrote several best-selling novels for a famous author. 她为一位著名作家代笔了几部畅销小说。

例句：The politician ghostwrote his autobiography, hiring a writer to craft the story of his life.|这位政客找人代笔写了他的自传，雇佣了一位作家来撰写他的人生故事。


近义词：write for, compose for, author for|反义词：self-authored, independently wrote|动词
