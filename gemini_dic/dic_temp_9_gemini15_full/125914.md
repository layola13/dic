muser:/ˈmjuːzər/| n.|沉思者，冥想者|A person who is lost in thought; a dreamer.  |例句：He sat there, a muser lost in his own world. 他坐在那里，像个沉思者，沉浸在自己的世界里。|例句：She was a muser, often gazing out the window, lost in reverie. 她是个沉思者，经常凝视着窗外，陷入沉思。|近义词：dreamer, daydreamer, thinker, contemplator |反义词：doer, activist


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He muses on the meaning of life. = 他沉思人生的意义。
She muses about her future plans. = 她思考着未来的计划。

一般过去时:
He mused on the beauty of the sunset. = 他沉思着日落的美丽。
She mused about her childhood memories. = 她沉思着童年的回忆。

现在完成时:
He has mused for hours on this problem. = 他已经思考这个问题好几个小时了。
She has mused over the implications of the news. = 她一直在思考这个消息的含义。

过去完成时:
He had mused for days before reaching a decision. = 他思考了好几天才做出决定。
She had mused over the possibilities before making a choice. = 她考虑了各种可能性后才做出选择。

将来时:
He will muse on this matter further. = 他会进一步思考这件事。
She will muse upon the events of the day. = 她会沉思今天发生的事情。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would muse more often. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更经常地沉思。
I wish I could muse peacefully without interruption. = 我希望我能平静地沉思，不受打扰。

被动语态:  (The verb "muse" is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
What are you musing about? = 你在思考什么？
Does he often muse on philosophical questions? = 他经常思考哲学问题吗？

否定句:
He didn't muse on the subject for long. = 他并没有长时间思考这个话题。
She didn't muse over the details. = 她没有仔细思考细节。


PHRASE->
muse over/upon: 仔细考虑
muse on: 沉思
lose oneself in musings: 陷入沉思
deep in musings: 陷入沉思中
a moment of musing: 一刻的沉思
