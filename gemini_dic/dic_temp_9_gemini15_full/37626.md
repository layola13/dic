communistically:/ˌkɒmjuˈnɪstɪkli/ | adv. |共产主义地，按共产主义原则 |In a manner characteristic of communism or communists.  It describes actions, systems, or beliefs that adhere to or reflect the principles of communism.|例句：The farm was run communistically, with everyone sharing the profits equally.  这个农场是共产主义式经营的，大家平均分配利润。 The country attempted to reorganize its economy communistically.  这个国家试图按共产主义原则重组其经济。|近义词：collectively, socialistically |反义词：capitalistically, individualistically
