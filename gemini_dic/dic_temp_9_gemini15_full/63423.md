entrapped:/ɛnˈtræpt/| vt.|诱捕；使陷入困境|To catch or become caught in something.  It implies a feeling of being trapped or unable to escape.|The bird was entrapped in the net.  这只鸟被困在了网里。 The family felt entrapped by their financial difficulties.  这个家庭感觉被经济困难困住了。| ensnare, snare, trap, ensnatch | free, release, liberate | 动词
