overreward:/ˌoʊvərɪˈwɔːrd/| vt.|过度奖励，给予过高的报酬|To reward someone excessively or beyond what is deserved or appropriate.|The company overrewarded its executives, leading to resentment among other employees. 公司过度奖励高管，导致其他员工不满。|reward, compensate, remunerate|underreward, underpay|动词

The company overrewards employees for minor achievements. = 公司对员工的小小成就给予过高的奖励。
