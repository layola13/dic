lithophytic:/ˌlɪθəˈfɪtɪk/| adj.| 岩生的；石生的 | Growing on rocks or stones; living on rocks.  The term refers to plants, fungi, or other organisms that thrive in rocky environments, deriving nutrients and support from the rock surface. | Example: Many lithophytic orchids are found in tropical rainforests, clinging to tree trunks and branches.  许多岩生兰花都生长在热带雨林中，依附在树干和树枝上。  |近义词：saxicolous, rupicolous | 反义词：terrestrial
