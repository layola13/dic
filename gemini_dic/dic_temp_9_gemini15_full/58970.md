dutchify:/ˈdʌtʃɪfaɪ/| vt.|使荷兰化；使具有荷兰特色|To make or become like something from the Netherlands; to introduce Dutch characteristics into something.|例句：They dutchified the restaurant's menu by adding several traditional Dutch dishes.他们通过添加几道传统的荷兰菜来使这家餐馆的菜单荷兰化。|例句：The artist dutchified his painting style by incorporating elements of Dutch Golden Age art.这位艺术家通过融入荷兰黄金时代艺术的元素来使他的绘画风格荷兰化。|近义词：Netherlandicize|反义词：No direct antonym, but one could say "to internationalize" or "to generalize" as contrasting actions.|动词
