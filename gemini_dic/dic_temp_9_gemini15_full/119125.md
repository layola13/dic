metropolite:/məˈtrɒpəlɪt/| n. |大主教，都会主教|A metropolitan is a bishop of a major city, ranking above other bishops in the same province.  The term often implies significant religious and administrative authority. |Example: The metropolite delivered a powerful sermon to the congregation.|近义词：archbishop|反义词：parish priest
