hyperemesis:/ˌhaɪpərˈɛmɪsɪs/ | n. |剧烈呕吐 |Excessive vomiting, often to the point of dehydration and electrolyte imbalance.  It is a symptom that can be associated with various conditions, including pregnancy (hyperemesis gravidarum),  gastroenteritis, and certain medications. |Example:  She experienced severe hyperemesis during her first trimester of pregnancy. 她怀孕的头三个月经历了严重的剧烈呕吐。

The condition required hospitalization due to severe hyperemesis. 由于剧烈呕吐，她需要住院治疗。|近义词：severe vomiting, excessive vomiting |反义词：None (it's a symptom, not a condition with a direct opposite)|名词
