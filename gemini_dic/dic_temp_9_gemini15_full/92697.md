icefall:/ˈaɪsfɔːl/| n. |冰崩，冰瀑 | A mass of ice breaking away from a glacier and falling down a steep slope.  It is a type of glacier movement characterized by the rapid descent of a large body of ice. |例句：The climbers were forced to detour around a massive icefall.  登山者被迫绕过一个巨大的冰崩。
The spectacular icefall attracted many tourists.壮观的冰瀑吸引了许多游客。|近义词：ice avalanche, glacier fall |反义词:  glacial advance (反义词较弱，因为冰崩是冰川运动的一种形式)|名词
