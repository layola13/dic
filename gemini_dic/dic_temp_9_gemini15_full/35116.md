cloudbursts:/ˈklaʊdbɜːsts/| n.|暴雨，骤雨|A cloudburst is a sudden, heavy downpour of rain.  It is typically characterized by its intensity and short duration.|例句：The cloudburst flooded the streets in minutes. 暴雨在几分钟内淹没了街道。

例句：We were caught in a cloudburst on our way home. 我们回家途中遭遇了暴雨。|近义词：deluge, downpour, torrent|反义词：drought, dryness|词性 n.
