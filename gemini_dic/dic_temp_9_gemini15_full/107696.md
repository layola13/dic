lano:/ˈlɑːnoʊ/| n. |拉诺（地名，西班牙语）| Llano is a Spanish word meaning "plain" or "flat land". It is often used as a place name in Spanish-speaking regions, particularly in the southwestern United States and Latin America.  | Example: The Llano Estacado is a vast, high plain in the southwestern United States.  拉诺埃斯塔卡多是美国西南部一个广阔的高原。|近义词：plain, prairie, pampas |反义词：mountain, hill
