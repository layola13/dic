dimmer:/ˈdɪmər/| n. adj. v.| 调光器；使变暗淡的；暗淡的 | A device for controlling the intensity of a light; something that makes something less bright; less bright | The dimmer switch allowed her to adjust the brightness of the lamps. 调光开关使她能够调节灯的亮度。  He used a dimmer to create a more romantic atmosphere. 他用调光器营造更浪漫的气氛。 The room was dimly lit, creating a dimmer atmosphere. 房间灯光昏暗，营造出一种暗淡的气氛。 |近义词：darkener, attenuator,  faint |反义词：brightener, intensifier, brilliant


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The dimmer switch dims the lights smoothly. = 调光开关平滑地调暗灯光。
He dims the lights before watching a movie. = 他在看电影之前调暗灯光。


一般过去时:
She dimmed the lights to create a cozy atmosphere. = 她调暗灯光以营造温馨的氛围。
The sun dimmed behind the clouds. = 太阳被云层遮挡变暗了。


现在完成时:
They have dimmed the stage lights for the performance. = 他们已经为演出调暗了舞台灯光。
The star has dimmed considerably in recent years. = 这颗恒星近年来已经暗淡了许多。


过去完成时:
He had dimmed the lights before his guests arrived. = 在客人到达之前，他已经调暗了灯光。
The sun had dimmed significantly before the storm broke. = 暴风雨来临之前，太阳已经暗淡了很多。


将来时:
She will dim the lights when it gets dark. = 天黑时她会调暗灯光。
The star will dim and eventually disappear. = 这颗恒星将逐渐暗淡并最终消失。


虚拟语气:
If I had a dimmer switch, I would dim the lights. = 如果我有一个调光开关，我会调暗灯光。
I wish the sun would dim a little; it's too bright. = 我希望太阳能暗淡一点；太亮了。


被动语态:
The lights were dimmed for the presentation. = 灯光为演示而被调暗。
The brightness of the screen can be dimmed automatically. = 屏幕的亮度可以自动调暗。


疑问句:
Can the lights be dimmed? = 灯光可以调暗吗？
Does the dimmer switch work properly? = 调光开关工作正常吗？


否定句:
The dimmer switch doesn't work. = 调光开关坏了。
He didn't dim the lights before going to bed. = 他睡觉前没有调暗灯光。
