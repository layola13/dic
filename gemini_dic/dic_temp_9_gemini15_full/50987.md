desireful:/dɪˈzaɪərfəl/| adj.|渴望的；热切的|Having or showing a strong desire; eager.|The desireful child begged for a puppy.|近义词：eager, yearning, longing|反义词：indifferent, apathetic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is desireful of success. = 他渴望成功。
She is desireful to help others. = 她渴望帮助他人。


一般过去时:
He was desireful of a promotion. = 他渴望升职。
She was desireful to learn more. = 她渴望学习更多。


现在完成时:
He has been desireful of a new car for years. = 多年来，他一直渴望拥有一辆新车。
She has been desireful to travel the world. = 她一直渴望环游世界。


过去完成时:
He had been desireful of a quiet life before the incident. = 事发前，他一直渴望平静的生活。
She had been desireful to finish her project before the deadline. = 她一直渴望在截止日期前完成她的项目。


将来时:
He will be desireful of a peaceful retirement. = 他将渴望过上平静的退休生活。
She will be desireful to see her grandchildren. = 她将渴望见到她的孙子孙女。


虚拟语气:
If he were desireful of it, he would have tried harder. = 如果他渴望得到它，他会更加努力。
If she were desireful to go, she would have already booked the tickets. = 如果她渴望去，她早就订票了。


被动语态:
Success is desired by many. = 许多人都渴望成功。(注意：desireful本身不常用被动语态)
A quiet life was desired by him. = 他渴望平静的生活。(注意：desireful本身不常用被动语态，此句使用被动语态表达渴望)


疑问句:
Is he desireful of a new job? = 他渴望一份新工作吗？
Are they desireful to participate? = 他们渴望参与吗？


否定句:
He is not desireful of conflict. = 他不渴望冲突。
She is not desireful to cause trouble. = 她不渴望惹麻烦。


PHRASE->
be desireful of something = 渴望某物
feel desireful = 感到渴望
become desireful = 变得渴望
express a desireful attitude = 表达渴望的态度
a desireful look = 渴望的眼神
