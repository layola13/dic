pound-the-pavement:/paʊnd ðə ˈpeɪvmənt/| v. |四处奔走（找工作）|To actively and persistently seek something, typically a job, by visiting many places and talking to many people.  This idiom emphasizes the physical act of walking from place to place. |She spent months pounding the pavement, handing out resumes and networking. 她花了几个月的时间四处奔走，分发简历和建立人脉。| actively search, job hunt, network | passively wait,  rely on online applications.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They pound the pavement every day looking for work. = 他们每天都在四处奔走找工作。
He pounds the pavement trying to find a new client. = 他四处奔走试图找到一个新客户。


一般过去时:
She pounded the pavement for weeks before landing the job. = 在找到工作之前，她四处奔走了好几个星期。
They pounded the pavement all day, but had no luck. = 他们一整天都在四处奔走，但没有运气。


现在完成时:
I have been pounding the pavement for months without success. = 我已经四处奔走了几个月，但没有成功。
He has pounded the pavement since graduating college. = 他自从大学毕业以来就一直在四处奔走。


过去完成时:
She had pounded the pavement for a year before she finally gave up. = 在她最终放弃之前，她已经四处奔走了整整一年。
They had pounded the pavement tirelessly, but still hadn't found what they were looking for. = 他们不知疲倦地四处奔走，但仍然没有找到他们想要的东西。


将来时:
I will pound the pavement tomorrow looking for a new apartment. = 我明天将四处奔走寻找新公寓。
She will pound the pavement until she finds a suitable position. = 她会四处奔走直到找到合适的位置。


虚拟语气:
If I needed a job, I would pound the pavement without hesitation. = 如果我需要一份工作，我会毫不犹豫地四处奔走。
I wish I hadn't pounded the pavement so much; it was exhausting. = 我真希望我没有四处奔走那么多；太累了。


被动语态:
The streets were pounded by job seekers all day long. = 街道上整天都被求职者走遍了。(This usage is less common and sounds unnatural.)
The city's sidewalks have been pounded by countless job hunters over the years. = 多年来，这座城市的街道被无数的求职者走遍了。(This usage is more natural in the present perfect.)


疑问句:
Are you planning to pound the pavement this week? = 你计划这周四处奔走吗？
Will he continue to pound the pavement even after all this time? = 即使过了这么长时间，他还会继续四处奔走吗？


否定句:
He didn't pound the pavement; he relied on online applications. = 他没有四处奔走；他依靠在线申请。
She won't pound the pavement; she has a good network of contacts. = 她不会四处奔走；她有很好的人脉。
