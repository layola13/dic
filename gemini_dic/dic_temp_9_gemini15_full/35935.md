cod's:/kɒdz/| n.|鳕鱼的；鳕鱼肉|The possessive form of "cod," referring to something belonging to or associated with cod (a type of fish).  It is often used in the context of food, such as "cod's liver oil."|Example: Cod's liver oil is a rich source of vitamin D. 鳕鱼肝油是维生素D的丰富来源。|近义词:None specifically,  but "haddock's" or "halibut's" could be used in similar contexts.|反义词:None|名词
