glist:/ɡlɪst/| v.|闪光；闪耀|To shine with a soft, unsteady light; glitter.  |例句：The dewdrops glistened on the spiderweb. 露珠在蜘蛛网上闪闪发光。|例句：Her eyes glistened with unshed tears.  她的眼睛闪着未落下的泪光。|近义词：glitter, shimmer, sparkle|反义词：dull, matte


USAGE->

The diamonds glisten in the light. = 钻石在灯光下闪闪发光。
The river glistens under the moonlight. = 河水在月光下闪闪发光。
