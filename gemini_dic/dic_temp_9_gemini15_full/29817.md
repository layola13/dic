cauldron:/ˈkɔːldrən/| n.|大锅，坩埚|A large, round pot, typically used for cooking over an open fire, or in a more figurative sense, a place or situation of intense activity or turmoil.|The witch stirred the bubbling cauldron. 女巫搅拌着沸腾的大锅。
The political situation is a cauldron of unrest. 政治局势动荡不安。|pot, kettle, boiler|calm, peace
