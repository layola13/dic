encourage:/ɪnˈkɜːrɪdʒ/| vt. |鼓励，激励 |To give support, confidence, or hope to someone.  To stimulate or promote the development of something.|例句：Her teacher encouraged her to apply to university.  她的老师鼓励她申请大学。

例句：The warm weather encouraged the plants to grow.  温暖的天气促进了植物的生长。


近义词：motivate, inspire, spur, stimulate |反义词：discourage, dissuade, hinder, impede |词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He encourages his students to think critically. = 他鼓励他的学生批判性思考。
She encourages her children to pursue their dreams. = 她鼓励她的孩子们追求梦想。


一般过去时:
My parents encouraged me to study hard. = 我的父母鼓励我努力学习。
The coach encouraged the team before the game. = 教练在比赛前鼓励了队员们。


现在完成时:
They have encouraged many young people to enter the field. = 他们已经鼓励了许多年轻人进入这个领域。
She has encouraged me throughout my career. = 她在我整个职业生涯中一直鼓励我。


过去完成时:
He had encouraged her to quit her job before she finally decided to. = 他在她最终决定之前就鼓励她辞职。
The teacher had encouraged the students to participate actively in class. = 老师曾鼓励学生积极参与课堂活动。


将来时:
I will encourage my son to learn a musical instrument. = 我将鼓励我的儿子学习乐器。
The government will encourage investment in renewable energy. = 政府将鼓励对可再生能源的投资。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would encourage him to try again. = 如果我是你，我会鼓励他再试一次。
I wish I had encouraged her to follow her passion. = 我真希望我鼓励她追随她的热情。


被动语态:
The students were encouraged by their teacher's support. = 学生们受到了老师的支持鼓励。
The project was encouraged by the local government. = 当地政府鼓励了这个项目。


疑问句:
Does she encourage her employees to work overtime? = 她鼓励她的员工加班吗？
Do you encourage your children to read books? = 你鼓励你的孩子读书吗？


否定句:
He didn't encourage her to take risks. = 他没有鼓励她冒险。
They did not encourage laziness in the workplace. = 他们不鼓励在工作场所懒惰。


PHRASE->
encourage someone to do something:鼓励某人做某事
encourage growth:促进增长
encourage participation:鼓励参与
encourage innovation:鼓励创新
encourage development:促进发展


词源:
源自古法语 *encoragier，来自古法语 corage "勇气, 心脏",  最终来自拉丁语 cor "心脏".  前缀 en- 表示“使……”, 因此encourage 的字面意思是“使……有勇气”。
