expensiveness:/ɪkˈspɛnsɪvnəs/ | n. |昂贵；花费巨大 | The expensiveness of the project led to its cancellation.  这个项目的昂贵导致其被取消。|近义词：costliness, high price, dearness |反义词：cheapness, affordability


英文解析：The state or quality of being expensive; high cost.  指昂贵的状态或特性；高成本。


例句：

The expensiveness of the hotel made it inaccessible to many tourists.  酒店的高昂价格使得许多游客无法入住。

The expensiveness of healthcare is a major concern for many families.  医疗保健的高昂费用是许多家庭的主要担忧。


词源:

expense (n.) + -ive (adj. suffix) + -ness (n. suffix)

expense 源自古法语 despense 和拉丁语 dispensa，意思是“支出，费用”。  -ive 是形容词后缀，表示“具有…性质的”。 -ness 是名词后缀，表示“状态，性质”。  因此，expensiveness 的字面意思是“具有高昂费用的状态”。


USAGE->  (由于expensiveness是名词，没有动词变化，以下列举名词的用法)

单数名词:
The expensiveness of the car surprised me. 这辆车的昂贵让我吃惊。
We need to consider the expensiveness of the materials. 我们需要考虑材料的昂贵。

复数名词:  (expensiveness 通常不用复数)


所有格:
The expensiveness of their lifestyle is shocking. 他们生活方式的昂贵令人震惊。
