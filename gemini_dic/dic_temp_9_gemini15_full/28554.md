cardiospasm: /ˈkɑːdiəʊˌspæzəm/ | n. | 心脏痉挛；食管痉挛 | A functional disorder of the esophagus characterized by intermittent spasms of the lower esophageal sphincter, causing difficulty in swallowing.  This is also sometimes referred to as diffuse esophageal spasm. | Example:  The patient presented with symptoms consistent with cardiospasm, including dysphagia and chest pain.  患者出现与心脏痉挛一致的症状，包括吞咽困难和胸痛。| 近义词: esophageal spasm, diffuse esophageal spasm | 反义词:  None specifically, but the absence of cardiospasm could be described as normal esophageal function. | 名词
