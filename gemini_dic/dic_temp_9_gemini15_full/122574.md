moderatism:/ˈmɒdərətɪzəm/ | n. |温和主义，适度主义 |The advocacy of moderation or avoidance of extremes, especially in political or religious matters.  It emphasizes practicality and compromise over ideological purity.  |例句：The government's approach to economic reform was characterized by moderatism, seeking gradual change rather than radical upheaval. 政府对经济改革的处理方式以温和主义为特征，寻求循序渐进的变革而非激进的变革。

例句：Her political views leaned towards moderatism, rejecting both far-left and far-right extremism.  她的政治观点倾向于温和主义，拒绝极左和极右的极端主义。


近义词：temperance, moderation, pragmatism |反义词：extremism, radicalism, fanaticism
