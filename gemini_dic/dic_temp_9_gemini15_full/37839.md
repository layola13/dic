compete:/kəmˈpiːt/ | v. |竞争，比赛 |To strive for superiority or victory over others; to contend.  | Example:  Many companies compete for the same customers. 许多公司争夺相同的客户。  | Example:  The two athletes competed fiercely for the gold medal. 两名运动员为金牌激烈竞争。 |近义词：rival, contend, vie | 反义词：cooperate, collaborate |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They compete in the market for the best price. = 他们在市场上竞争最佳价格。
He competes in marathon races every year. = 他每年都参加马拉松比赛。


一般过去时:
She competed in the national swimming championships. = 她参加了全国游泳锦标赛。
We competed against each other in the chess tournament. = 我们在国际象棋锦标赛中互相竞争。


现在完成时:
They have competed in several international competitions. = 他们已经参加了几项国际比赛。
She has competed at the highest level of professional tennis. = 她曾在职业网球的最高水平上竞技。


过去完成时:
He had competed in many races before he won his first major championship. = 在赢得他的第一个主要冠军之前，他已经参加过许多比赛。
They had competed against each other many times before the final match. = 在决赛之前，他们已经多次互相竞争。


将来时:
I will compete in the upcoming track and field meet. = 我将参加即将到来的田径运动会。
She will compete for a place on the national team. = 她将竞争国家队的一个位置。


虚拟语气:
If I were younger, I would compete in the Olympics. = 如果我年轻一些，我会参加奥运会。
I wish I could compete professionally, but I lack the resources. = 我希望我能专业地参加比赛，但我缺乏资源。


被动语态:
The race was competed in record time. = 这场比赛以创纪录的时间完成。（此处被动语态略显奇怪，通常用其他表达）
The prize was competed for fiercely by the contestants. = 选手们激烈地争夺奖品。（更自然的说法）


疑问句:
Will you compete in the upcoming election? = 你会参加即将到来的选举吗？
Who will compete against her in the finals? = 在决赛中谁将和她竞争？


否定句:
He didn't compete in the last tournament. = 他没有参加上次的比赛。
They chose not to compete in the market this year. = 他们选择今年不参与市场竞争。


PHRASE->
compete against = 与…竞争
compete for = 竞争…
compete with = 与…竞争
compete in = 参加…比赛
compete successfully = 成功竞争
