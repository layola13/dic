necroses:/nɛˈkroʊsiːz/| n.|坏死；组织坏死|The death of body tissue.  This is usually due to a lack of blood supply. Necrosis can affect any part of the body. |例句：The infection caused necrosis of the soft tissue. 感染导致软组织坏死。|例句：Extensive necrosis of the liver was observed during the autopsy. 在尸检中观察到肝脏广泛坏死。|近义词：gangrene, mortification, infarction|反义词：regeneration, healing|名词
