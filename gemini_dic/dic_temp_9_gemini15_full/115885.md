marmalade:/ˈmɑːməleɪd/ | n. |橘子酱，果酱|A preserve made of citrus fruit, typically oranges, marmalade is usually made with the fruit's peel and pulp, resulting in a thick, sweet, and slightly bitter spread. It's often eaten on toast or bread.| For breakfast, I had toast with marmalade. 早饭我吃了涂了橘子酱的面包。|jam, preserve|—|
