see-thru: /siːˈθruː/ | adj. | 透明的；看得透的 |  Able to be seen through; transparent.  It describes something that allows light to pass through, making the objects behind it visible. | Example:  The see-thru curtains allowed the sunlight to flood the room.  = 透明的窗帘让阳光洒满了房间。  The see-through blouse was a bit too revealing for the office. = 这件透明的衬衫对于办公室来说有点太暴露了。| 近义词：transparent, translucent, diaphanous | 反义词：opaque, solid
