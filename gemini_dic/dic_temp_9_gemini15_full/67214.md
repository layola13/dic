expressly:/ɪkˈspresli/ | adv. |明确地；特意地；专门地 |Clearly and unmistakably; in a direct and unambiguous manner; for a specific purpose.  | The instructions were expressly designed to be easy to follow.  指令是特意设计得容易理解的。 He expressly stated that he would not attend. 他明确表示不会参加。|clearly, specifically, definitely |implicitly, vaguely, generally |副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He expressly forbids smoking in his office. 他明确禁止在他的办公室吸烟。
We expressly state our terms and conditions. 我们明确声明我们的条款和条件。


一般过去时:
She expressly told me not to tell anyone. 她明确告诉我不要告诉任何人。
They expressly refused the offer. 他们明确拒绝了这个提议。


现在完成时:
I have expressly warned him about the dangers. 我已经明确警告过他危险。
We have expressly stated our position on this issue. 我们已经明确阐述了我们在这个问题上的立场。


过去完成时:
He had expressly forbidden any unauthorized access. 他已经明确禁止任何未经授权的访问。
They had expressly requested a private room. 他们已经明确要求一个私人房间。


将来时:
She will expressly state her intentions in the meeting. 她将在会议上明确说明她的意图。
They will expressly support the new project. 他们将明确支持这个新项目。


虚拟语气:
If I had known it was expressly forbidden, I wouldn't have done it. 如果我知道这是明确禁止的，我不会那样做的。
I wish he had expressly stated his requirements earlier. 我希望他早点明确说明他的要求。


被动语态:
Smoking is expressly forbidden in this area.  在这个区域吸烟是被明确禁止的。
The rules were expressly stated in the contract. 规则在合同中被明确说明。


疑问句:
Did he expressly forbid it? 他明确禁止了吗？
Was it expressly stated in the agreement? 它在协议中被明确说明了吗？


否定句:
He did not expressly state his opinion. 他没有明确表达他的意见。
It wasn't expressly mentioned in the report. 报告中没有明确提及它。
