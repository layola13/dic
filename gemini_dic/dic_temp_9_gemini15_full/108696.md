leaky:/ˈliːki/| adj.|漏的；渗漏的|Allowing liquid or gas to enter or escape through unintended openings.  The roof is leaky and needs repairing.|近义词：porous, perforated, pervious |反义词：watertight, airtight


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The roof is leaky. = 屋顶漏水。
The pipe is leaky and needs replacing. = 水管漏水需要更换。


一般过去时:
The boat was leaky after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，船漏了。
The faucet was leaky, so we called a plumber. = 水龙头漏水，所以我们叫了水管工。


现在完成时:
The roof has been leaky for months. = 屋顶已经漏水好几个月了。
The dam has sprung a leak and is now leaky. = 水坝出现了裂缝，现在漏水了。


过去完成时:
The bucket had been leaky all day, so it was almost empty. = 水桶一整天都在漏水，所以几乎空了。
The ship had been leaky for days before it finally sank. = 那艘船在最终沉没之前已经漏水好几天了。


将来时:
