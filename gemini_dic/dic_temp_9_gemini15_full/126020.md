muss:/mʌs/| n. v.|凌乱；混乱；弄乱；胡乱做|A state of disorder or untidiness; to make something untidy or disordered.|例句：The room was in a terrible muss.  房间乱七八糟的。|例句：Don't muss your hair. 不要弄乱你的头发。|近义词：mess, disorder, jumble |反义词：order, tidiness, neatness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The children muss their toys every day. = 孩子们每天都把玩具弄乱。
He always musses his hair when he's stressed. = 他压力大的时候总是弄乱头发。

一般过去时:
She mussed her dress while climbing the fence. = 她爬栅栏时弄皱了衣服。
They mussed up the living room during the party. = 他们在派对上把客厅弄得乱七八糟。

现在完成时:
The dog has mussed the carpet again. = 狗又把地毯弄脏了。
I have mussed my hair trying to get the cat out of the tree. = 我试图把猫从树上弄下来，把头发弄乱了。

过去完成时:
He had mussed his papers before the important meeting. = 他在重要会议之前把文件弄乱了。
She had mussed the cake while trying to frost it. = 她试图给蛋糕抹霜时把蛋糕弄乱了。

将来时:
The wind will muss her carefully styled hair. = 风会把她的精心梳理的头发弄乱。
They will muss the garden if they aren't careful. = 如果他们不小心，会把花园弄乱。

虚拟语气:
If he had been more careful, he wouldn't have mussed the painting. = 如果他更小心一点，就不会把画弄坏了。
I wish I hadn't mussed the tablecloth. = 我真希望我没有弄脏桌布。

被动语态:
The papers were mussed by the wind. = 文件被风吹乱了。
The room was mussed after the party. = 派对后房间乱七八糟的。

疑问句:
Did you muss my documents? = 你把我的文件弄乱了吗？
Has anyone mussed the arrangement of the flowers? = 有人把花的摆设弄乱了吗？

否定句:
He didn't muss the room intentionally. = 他不是故意把房间弄乱的。
She didn't muss her hair; it was naturally messy. = 她没有弄乱头发；那是天生就乱糟糟的。
