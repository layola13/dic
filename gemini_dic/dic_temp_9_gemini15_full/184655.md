restringe:/rɪˈstrɪndʒ/| vt. |限制；约束；压缩|To restrict something means to limit its size, extent, or power.  It often implies a forceful or controlling action.| The government has restricted the sale of alcohol. 政府限制了酒类的销售。| The new rules will severely restrict our movements. 新规将严重限制我们的行动。|近义词：limit, confine, curb, restrain|反义词：expand, enlarge, broaden|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government restricts the import of certain goods. = 政府限制某些商品的进口。
He restricts his spending to essentials. = 他把开支限制在必需品上。


一般过去时:
They restricted access to the building. = 他们限制了进入大楼的通道。
The drought restricted the growth of crops. = 干旱限制了农作物的生长。


现在完成时:
The new law has restricted freedom of speech. = 新法律限制了言论自由。
The company has restricted its operations due to the pandemic. = 由于疫情，公司已经限制了其业务。


过去完成时:
They had restricted access to the server before the security breach. = 在安全漏洞发生之前，他们已经限制了对服务器的访问。
The company had restricted employee travel for months. = 公司已经限制员工出差好几个月了。


将来时:
The city will restrict traffic in the downtown area. = 城市将限制市中心地区的交通。
The government will restrict the use of certain chemicals. = 政府将限制使用某些化学物质。


虚拟语气:
If the budget were larger, we wouldn't have to restrict our research. = 如果预算更大，我们就不必限制我们的研究了。
I wish they hadn't restricted our access to information. = 我真希望他们没有限制我们获取信息。


被动语态:
Access to the website was restricted. = 网站访问被限制了。
Our movements are being restricted by the security measures. = 我们的行动受到安全措施的限制。


疑问句:
Does the law restrict the sale of firearms? = 这部法律是否限制枪支的销售？
Will the new regulations restrict our ability to innovate? = 新规章是否会限制我们的创新能力？


否定句:
The company does not restrict employee internet usage. = 公司不限制员工的互联网使用。
The rules did not restrict our ability to participate. = 这些规则并没有限制我们参与的能力。



PHRASE->
restrict access to = 限制对…的访问
restrict movement = 限制行动
restrict the flow = 限制流量
restrict the use of = 限制…的使用
strictly restrict = 严格限制
