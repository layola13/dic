minicircuits:/ˈmɪniˈsɜːrkɪts/ | n. | 微型电路 | Miniature electronic circuits, often integrated circuits (ICs) that are smaller than standard circuits. They are used in a wide variety of electronic devices. | Example: The new smartphone uses advanced minicircuits to improve signal processing.  这款新智能手机采用先进的微型电路来改善信号处理。 |近义词：integrated circuits, microcircuits | 反义词：macroscopic circuits
