redeterminations:/ˌriːdiˌtɜːrmɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. |重新确定，再决定 |The process of reconsidering and establishing something again.  This often involves reviewing previous decisions or calculations and making new ones based on updated information or circumstances.  |例句：The committee is undertaking redeterminations of the project's budget. 委员会正在对项目的预算进行重新确定。


例句：The court ordered redeterminations of the damages awarded to the plaintiffs. 法院命令重新确定判给原告的损害赔偿。


近义词：recalculations, reconsiderations, reassessments, revisions


反义词：finalizations, confirmations


词性:名词
