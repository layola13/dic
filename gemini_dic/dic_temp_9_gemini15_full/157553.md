pitas:/ˈpiːtɑːz/| n. |皮塔饼 |A pita is a type of flatbread, typically made from wheat flour, that is baked in a hot oven. It is characterized by its pocket-like shape, which is created by puffing during baking.  Pitas are often used as a sandwich wrap or to accompany dips and other foods.| Example: I had a pita pocket filled with falafel and hummus for lunch. 我午餐吃了一个装满鹰嘴豆泥和鹰嘴豆泥的皮塔饼。|近义词：flatbread, pocket bread |反义词：N/A
