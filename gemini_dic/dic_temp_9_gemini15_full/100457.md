intertwining:/ˌɪntərˈtwaɪnɪŋ/ | n. adj. | 交织；缠绕 | The act of twisting or being twisted together.  It can also describe things that are closely connected or interwoven. |例句：The intertwining branches formed a natural arch.  交织的树枝形成了一道天然的拱门。

The vines were intertwining around the fence. 藤蔓缠绕在栅栏上。


近义词：entanglement, interweaving, interlacing, braiding | 反义词：separation, disentanglement


词性:名词和形容词
