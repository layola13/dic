onyxes:/ˈɒnɪksiːz/| n.|缟玛瑙(复数)|Onyxes refers to the plural form of onyx, a gemstone known for its banded appearance.  It's a variety of chalcedony, a cryptocrystalline form of silica, and is prized for its beauty and use in jewelry and decorative objects.  |Example: The ancient Egyptian pharaohs were adorned with exquisite onyxes.  古代埃及法老佩戴着精美的缟玛瑙。


The collection boasted a stunning array of onyxes, each with its unique banding. 这收藏拥有令人惊叹的各种缟玛瑙，每块都有其独特的条带。|近义词：agates, chalcedonies|反义词：无
