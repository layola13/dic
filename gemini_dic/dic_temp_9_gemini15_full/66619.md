exilers:/ɪkˈzaɪlərz/ | n. | 流亡者，被放逐者 | People who have been forced to leave their country, typically for political reasons.  Exilers often face hardship and displacement. | Example: The political exilers sought refuge in a neighboring country.  流亡的政治犯在邻国寻求庇护。 | 近义词: refugees, exiles, expatriates | 反义词: citizens, natives
