intercombination:/ˌɪntərˌkɒmbɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |互组合，相互结合|The process or result of combining different things or elements, especially in a way that produces a new or improved effect.  It often refers to the combination of different electronic states or energy levels in an atom or molecule. |Example: The intercombination of colors created a vibrant and harmonious effect.  互色的组合创造了一种充满活力且和谐的效果。 The intercombination of different musical styles is a hallmark of his work. 不同音乐风格的相互结合是他作品的标志。|近义词：combination, synthesis, fusion |反义词：separation, division, segregation |名词
