organza:/ɔːrˈɡændʒə/| n.|欧根纱|A sheer, lightweight, plain-woven fabric, typically made of silk, rayon, or nylon.  It is often used for dresses, scarves, and other garments requiring a delicate and translucent material. |例句：The bride's dress was made of shimmering organza.  新娘的婚纱是用闪亮的欧根纱做的。

例句：The curtains were made from a delicate, white organza. 窗帘是由精细的白色欧根纱制成的。


近义词：chiffon, voile|反义词：暂无

词源:
The word "organza" is believed to derive from the French word "organdi," which itself may have originated from "organzine," a type of silk thread.  The exact etymology is debated, but the connection to silk and its fine texture is clear.
