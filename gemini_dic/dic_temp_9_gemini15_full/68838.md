fasciitis:/ˌfæʃiˈaɪtɪs/| n. | 筋膜炎 | Inflammation of the fascia, the fibrous connective tissue that surrounds muscles and other organs.  Fasciitis can occur in various parts of the body, leading to pain and stiffness. |  He suffers from plantar fasciitis, a common cause of heel pain. 他患有足底筋膜炎，这是导致脚跟疼痛的常见原因。 |  tendinitis, myositis |  (none readily available) | 名词
