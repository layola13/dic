constellations:/ˌkɒnstəˈleɪʃənz/| n. |星座，星群 |A constellation is a group of stars that forms a recognizable pattern in the night sky.  Constellations are often named after mythological figures or objects. |例句：The Big Dipper is a well-known constellation. 北斗七星是一个著名的星座。

例句：Ancient cultures used constellations to navigate and tell stories. 古代文明利用星座进行导航和讲述故事。

近义词：asterisms, star groups

反义词：None (no direct antonym exists)

词性:noun
