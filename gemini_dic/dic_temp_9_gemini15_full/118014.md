memorized:/ˈmeməraɪzd/ | v. |记住的，熟记的 | to have learned something so well that you can remember it perfectly |例句：I have memorized the poem.  我记住了这首诗。|近义词：learned, committed to memory, recalled |反义词：forgotten, overlooked |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I memorize new vocabulary every day. = 我每天都记住新的词汇。
She memorizes lines for the play. = 她为戏剧背台词。

一般过去时:
I memorized the entire speech last night. = 我昨晚背下了整篇演讲稿。
He memorized the capital cities of all the countries. = 他记住了所有国家的首都。


现在完成时:
I have memorized all the important dates for the exam. = 我已经记住了考试的所有重要日期。
She has memorized the entire Shakespearean sonnet. = 她已经记住了整首莎士比亚十四行诗。


过去完成时:
I had memorized the script before the audition. = 我在试镜前已经背好了剧本。
They had memorized the lyrics before the concert. = 他们演唱会前已经记住了歌词。


将来时:
I will memorize the formula for the test tomorrow. = 我明天将记住考试的公式。
She will memorize the piano piece for the recital. = 她将为独奏会记住这首钢琴曲。


虚拟语气:
If I had memorized the poem, I would have recited it perfectly. = 如果我记住了这首诗，我就会完美地背诵出来。
I wish I had memorized the directions. = 我真希望我记住了路线。


被动语态:
The poem was memorized by many students. = 这首诗被很多学生背诵了。
The entire play was memorized by the cast. = 全部戏剧都被演员们背诵了。


疑问句:
Have you memorized the vocabulary list? = 你记住了词汇表吗？
Did he memorize the song lyrics? = 他记住了歌曲歌词吗？


否定句:
I didn't memorize the lecture notes. = 我没有记住讲义笔记。
She couldn't memorize the long poem. = 她记不住这首长诗。



PHRASE->
memorize something by heart = 死记硬背
memorize something word for word = 一字不差地记住
memorize a speech = 背诵演讲
memorize a poem = 背诵诗歌
memorize a phone number = 记住电话号码
