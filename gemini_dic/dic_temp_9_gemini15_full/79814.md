glycolide:/ˈɡlaɪkəlaɪd/| n. |乙交酯|A cyclic ester formed from the condensation of two molecules of glycolic acid.  It is a white crystalline solid used in the synthesis of biodegradable polymers, such as polyglycolide (PGA) and poly(lactic-co-glycolic acid) (PLGA).|Example: Glycolide is a key monomer in the production of biodegradable sutures.|近义词：None (unique chemical compound)|反义词：None (no direct antonym)|名词
