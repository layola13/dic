hero-worshipped: /ˈhɪəroʊˌwɜːrʃɪpt/ | adj. | 被奉若神明的；盲目崇拜的 |  Adjective describing someone or something that is excessively admired or idolized, often to an unreasonable degree.  It implies a level of blind admiration that may disregard flaws or negative aspects. |

例句：He was hero-worshipped by his younger brother, who imitated his every move.  他被他的弟弟奉若神明，弟弟模仿他的一举一动。

例句：The young actress was hero-worshipped by her fans, who sent her countless letters and gifts. 年轻的女演员被她的粉丝们奉若神明，他们给她寄去了无数的信件和礼物。


近义词：idolized,  deified,  reverenced,  adored


反义词：despised, scorned,  disdained,  rejected


词源：源自"hero" (英雄) + "worship" (崇拜).  "Worship" itself comes from Old English *weorþscype, meaning "worth, honor, dignity."  The combination creates a term indicating excessive and often uncritical admiration of a hero figure.
