potholes:/ˈpɒtˌhoʊlz/ /ˈpɑːtˌhoʊlz/| n.|坑洼，路面坑洞|A pothole is a hole in a road surface, caused by wear and tear or lack of maintenance.  Potholes can damage vehicles and cause accidents.|The car hit a pothole and blew a tire. 这辆车撞到一个坑洞，轮胎爆了。|Many potholes mar the streets after the heavy rain.  大雨过后，许多坑洼破坏了街道。|ruts, craters|smooth road surface|名词
