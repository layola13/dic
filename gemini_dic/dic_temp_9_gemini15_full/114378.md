makabayan:/ˌmɑːkəˈbaɪjɑːn/| n. adj.|爱国主义的；民族主义的|Patriotic; nationalist; relating to national identity and pride.  The term often carries connotations of Filipino nationalism and its associated movements.|例句：The makabayan movement advocated for social justice and national sovereignty.  爱国运动倡导社会正义和国家主权。

例句：He expressed his makabayan sentiments through his art. 他通过艺术表达了他的爱国情怀。


近义词：nationalistic, patriotic, pro-national


反义词：unpatriotic, anti-nationalist


词性: 名词，形容词
