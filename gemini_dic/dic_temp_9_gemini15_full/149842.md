paleozoic:/ˌpeɪliəˈzoʊɪk/ | adj. n. |古生代的；古生代 | Relating to or denoting the Paleozoic Era, the earliest period in which abundant animal and plant fossils are found.  | The Paleozoic Era saw the evolution of many invertebrate groups. 古生代见证了许多无脊椎动物群体的进化。 |  Mesozoic, Cenozoic | Precambrian
