fruiterer:/ˈfruːtəriər/| n.|水果商，水果贩|A fruiterer is a person who sells fruit, either from a shop or a stall.|例句：The local fruiterer had the best mangoes in town. 当地水果商有城里最好的芒果。|例句：He's a fruiterer who sources his produce from local farms. 他是一个从当地农场采购农产品的水果商。|近义词：fruit seller, greengrocer|反义词：N/A
