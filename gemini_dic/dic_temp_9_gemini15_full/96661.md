industrializing: /ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 工业化 | The process of developing industries in a region or country.  It involves the transformation of an economy from primarily agrarian to one dominated by manufacturing and related sectors. This often leads to significant social and environmental changes. |  例句：Many developing countries are undergoing rapid industrializing. 许多发展中国家正在经历快速工业化。

例句：The government's policies are designed to accelerate the industrializing process. 政府的政策旨在加快工业化进程。


近义词：modernizing, developing, mechanizing

反义词：deindustrializing, agrarianizing
