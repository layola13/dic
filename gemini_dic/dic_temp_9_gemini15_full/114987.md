mandala:/ˈmændəlɑː/|/ˈmændælə/| n. |曼荼罗，轮状图案|A mandala is a geometric design or pattern that represents the cosmos or deities in various religions, often used in meditation.  It is typically circular in shape and features intricate details.  |例句：The artist spent months creating a breathtaking mandala.  艺术家花了几个月的时间创作了一个令人叹为观止的曼荼罗。

例句：She found the intricate details of the mandala calming and meditative. 她发现曼荼罗复杂的细节令人平静和冥想。


近义词：diagram, chart, design, symbol


反义词：chaos, disorder
