overmoralizing: /ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 道德说教过度；过分强调道德 |  The act of excessively emphasizing morality, often to the point of being preachy, judgmental, or insensitive. It involves imposing one's moral standards on others without considering context or individual circumstances. |

例句：

His overmoralizing lectures on the evils of modern society alienated many students.  他的关于现代社会罪恶的过度说教的演讲疏远了许多学生。

The constant overmoralizing in the film undermined its overall message. 电影中不断的道德说教破坏了它的整体信息。


近义词：preaching, moralizing, sermonizing, sanctimoniousness, being judgmental

反义词：tolerance, open-mindedness, understanding, empathy
