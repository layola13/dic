diverberation:/daɪˌvɜːbəˈreɪʃən/| n. |回响；反响；回荡|The reverberation of the explosion shook the ground. 爆炸的回响震动了地面。|近义词：echo, resonance, repercussion|反义词：silence, stillness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sound reverberates through the valley. = 声音在山谷中回荡。
The news reverberates across the nation. = 这消息在全国引起反响。


一般过去时:
The applause reverberated throughout the hall. = 掌声在整个大厅回荡。
The impact of the announcement reverberated through the company. = 公告的影响在公司里引起了反响。


现在完成时:
The effects of the scandal have reverberated through the political system. = 丑闻的影响已经在政治体系中产生了回响。
The artist's work has reverberated through generations. = 艺术家的作品影响了几代人。


过去完成时:
The initial shock had reverberated through the community before the full story emerged. = 在事情真相大白之前，最初的震惊已经在社区中产生了回响。
The news of the war had reverberated across the globe before the official announcement. = 在官方宣布之前，战争的消息已经在全球引起反响。


将来时:
The decision will reverberate for years to come. = 这个决定将在未来几年产生影响。
The consequences of this action will reverberate throughout the organization. = 此举的后果将在整个组织中产生回响。


虚拟语气:
If the news were to reverberate across the world, what would happen? = 如果这个消息传遍全世界，会发生什么？
If the sound had reverberated more intensely, we would have heard it from farther away. = 如果声音回荡得更强烈，我们就能从更远的地方听到它。


被动语态:
The message was reverberated across all social media platforms. = 这条消息在所有社交媒体平台上传播开来。
The impact of the earthquake was reverberated throughout the region. = 地震的影响遍及整个地区。


疑问句:
How long will the reverberation last? = 回响会持续多久？
Does the sound reverberate in this room? = 这个房间里的声音会回响吗？


否定句:
The sound did not reverberate clearly in the small room. = 声音在这个小房间里回荡得不清晰。
The announcement did not reverberate as strongly as expected. = 公告的反响不如预期强烈。

词源:
From Middle French *réverbérer, from Latin reverberāre "to beat back, rebound," from re- "back" + verberāre "to strike, beat."
