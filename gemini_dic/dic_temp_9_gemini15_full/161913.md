prairie:/ˈprɛəri/| n. |大草原，牧场 |A prairie is a large, flat area of grassland with few or no trees.  It is characterized by its rich, fertile soil and abundance of grasses and wildflowers.  Prairies are found in various parts of the world, including North America, South America, and Eurasia. |例句：The wind swept across the vast prairie. 风横扫着广阔的大草原。|例句：We saw herds of bison grazing on the prairie. 我们看到成群的野牛在大草原上吃草。|近义词：grassland, plain, steppe |反义词：forest, jungle, woodland |名词

现在完成时:
Farmers have cultivated much of the prairie. = 农民们已经耕种了大部分草原。
The prairie has changed significantly over time. = 大草原随着时间的推移发生了显著变化。

过去完成时:
