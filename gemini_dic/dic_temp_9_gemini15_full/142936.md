out-trump:/ˌaʊtˈtrʌmp/| vt.|胜过；超过；压倒|To surpass or exceed in importance, influence, or achievement.|例句：His new proposal out-trumped all previous suggestions. 他的新提议胜过了所有先前的建议。
例句：The company's innovative marketing strategy out-trumped its competitors.  这家公司的创新营销策略胜过了竞争对手。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, overshadow|反义词：undermine, be inferior to|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He out-trumps his opponents in every debate. = 他在每一次辩论中都胜过他的对手。
The new technology out-trumps the old one in efficiency. = 新技术在效率上胜过旧技术。

一般过去时:
Their efforts out-trumped expectations. = 他们的努力超出了预期。
His performance out-trumped all the other actors. = 他的表演胜过了所有其他演员。

现在完成时:
The team has out-trumped all its rivals this season. = 这个赛季，这支球队已经击败了所有对手。
She has out-trumped her previous best score. = 她已经超过了她之前的最好成绩。

过去完成时:
He had out-trumped his rival in the previous round. = 在上一轮中，他已经击败了他的对手。
The company had out-trumped its competitors in the market share. = 公司在市场份额上已经超过了竞争对手。

将来时:
This new strategy will out-trump the competition. = 这一新战略将胜过竞争对手。
I believe his innovative idea will out-trump all others. = 我相信他的创新理念将胜过所有其他理念。

虚拟语气:
If he had worked harder, he might have out-trumped his opponent. = 如果他更努力工作，他可能会胜过他的对手。
If only I had known, I could have out-trumped them. = 如果我知道的话，我就可以胜过他们了。

被动语态:
His proposal was out-trumped by a more innovative one. = 他的提案被一个更具创新性的提案所超越。
The previous record was out-trumped by a new athlete. = 之前的记录被一位新的运动员打破了。

疑问句:
Will his new product out-trump the existing ones in the market? = 他的新产品会在市场上胜过现有产品吗？
Did her performance out-trump the others in the contest? = 她在比赛中的表现胜过其他人了吗？

否定句:
His argument didn't out-trump the evidence presented by the opposition. = 他的论点没有胜过反对派提出的证据。
Their efforts did not out-trump the challenges they faced. = 他们的努力没有克服他们面临的挑战。
