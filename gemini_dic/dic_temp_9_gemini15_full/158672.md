plonk:/plɒŋk/|/plɑŋk/| v., n.|砰的一声放下；沉闷地倒下；(俚语)随便放下；笨拙地放下 |To put or drop something down heavily and without care; to fall heavily and noisily; (slang) to put something down carelessly; to place something down clumsily. |例句：He plonked the suitcase on the floor. 他砰的一声把行李箱扔在地上。He plonked himself down in the chair. 他重重地坐在了椅子上。|近义词：thump, drop, dump, fling|反义词：place carefully, gently put down|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plonks his keys on the table. = 他把钥匙砰的一声放在桌子上。
She plonks the book down and walks away. = 她把书重重地放下就走了。

一般过去时:
He plonked the heavy box on the floor. = 他把沉重的箱子砰的一声放在地板上。
She plonked herself onto the sofa, exhausted. = 她筋疲力尽地重重地倒在沙发上。

现在完成时:
I've plonked all the groceries on the counter. = 我把所有杂货都砰的一声放在柜台上。
He's plonked his dirty clothes on the floor again. = 他又把脏衣服砰的一声扔在地上了。

过去完成时:
She had plonked the dishes in the sink before leaving. = 她离开前已把盘子砰的一声扔进了水槽。
They had plonked their bags down and started unpacking. = 他们把包重重地放下就开始整理行李了。

将来时:
He will plonk the bag down as soon as he gets home. = 他一到家就会把包重重地放下。
She will plonk the baby in the crib and go to bed. = 她会把婴儿放在婴儿床里然后去睡觉。

虚拟语气:
If he were tired, he would plonk himself down. = 如果他累了，他会重重地坐下。
I wish I could just plonk myself down on a beach. = 我真希望自己能躺在沙滩上休息。

被动语态:
The bag was plonked on the table. = 包被砰的一声放在桌子上。
The books were plonked carelessly on the shelf. = 书本被随便地扔在书架上。

疑问句:
Did he plonk the glass on the table? = 他是不是把玻璃杯砰的一声放在桌子上？
Will she plonk the baby down in the cot? = 她会把婴儿放在婴儿床里吗？

否定句:
He didn't plonk the vase on the table; he placed it gently. = 他没有把花瓶砰的一声放在桌子上；而是轻轻地放的。
She didn't plonk the food down; she carefully arranged it. = 她没有把食物重重地放下；而是小心地摆放好。
