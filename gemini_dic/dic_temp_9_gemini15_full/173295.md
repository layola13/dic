quasi-cordial: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈkɔːrdʒəl/ | adj. |似亲切的，貌似友好的 | Seemingly cordial; showing a pretense of cordiality;  giving a superficial impression of warmth and friendliness, but lacking genuine warmth.  It suggests a politeness that is not entirely sincere or heartfelt. |例句：Their quasi-cordial exchange masked a deep-seated rivalry.  他们的貌似友好的交流掩盖了根深蒂固的竞争。

Their relationship was quasi-cordial; they were polite but not truly friendly. 他们的关系看似友好；他们很礼貌，但并非真正友好。|近义词：polite, civil, formal, superficially friendly |反义词：hostile, unfriendly, antagonistic, cold
