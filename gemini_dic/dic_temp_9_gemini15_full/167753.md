primacies:/ˈpraɪməsiz/| n.|首要地位；优先权；卓越；统治地位|The supremacy or preeminence of something.|例句：The primacy of the church in medieval society was unquestioned. 中世纪社会中教会的首要地位是毋庸置疑的。  The primacy of national interests often overrides moral considerations. 国家利益的首要地位往往凌驾于道德考虑之上。|近义词：supremacy, predominance, preeminence|反义词：subordination, inferiority


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The primacy of education is emphasized in many countries. = 许多国家都强调教育的首要地位。
His primacy in the field is undeniable. = 他在该领域的领先地位是不可否认的。


一般过去时:
The primacy of agriculture in the economy declined in the 20th century. = 农业在经济中的首要地位在20世纪下降了。
Her primacy as a painter was challenged by a younger artist. = 她作为画家的首要地位受到了一个年轻艺术家的挑战。


现在完成时:
The company has maintained its primacy in the market for many years. = 这家公司多年来一直保持着在市场上的领先地位。
Their primacy has been challenged by technological advancements. = 他们的首要地位受到了技术进步的挑战。


过去完成时:
Before the internet, the primacy of newspapers as a news source was unquestioned. = 在互联网出现之前，报纸作为新闻来源的首要地位是毋庸置疑的。
By the time he retired, his primacy in the field had been well established. = 等他退休时，他在该领域的领先地位已经确立了。


将来时:
Artificial intelligence may challenge the primacy of human intelligence in the future. = 人工智能将来可能会挑战人类智能的首要地位。
We predict that their primacy in the industry will continue. = 我们预测他们在这个行业的领先地位将持续下去。


虚拟语气:
