self-usurped: /ˌsɛlf juˈzɜːrpt/ | adj. | 自我篡夺的，擅自夺取的 |  Describes something taken or claimed without right or authority, especially by oneself.  It implies a seizure of power or position illegitimately. | 例句：His self-usurped authority was challenged by the council.  他的擅自夺权受到了议会的挑战。

His self-usurped title was quickly exposed as fraudulent.  他擅自使用的头衔很快就被揭露为虚假的。


近义词：self-appointed, unauthorized, arrogated, appropriated, assumed


反义词：legitimate, authorized, rightful, justly acquired


词性: Adjective
