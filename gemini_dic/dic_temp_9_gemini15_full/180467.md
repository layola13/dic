regather:/ˌriːˈɡæðər/| vt.|重新聚集；重新收集|To gather again; to collect again.  The verb "regather" implies a previous gathering that has been dispersed or lost, and the action of bringing things or people back together.|例句：The shepherd regathered his scattered flock. 牧羊人重新聚集了他分散的羊群。
例句：After the storm, the villagers regathered their belongings. 风暴过后，村民们重新收集了他们的财物。|近义词：reassemble, reunite, recollect|反义词：disperse, scatter, dissipate|动词
