misdefine:/ˌmɪsˈdeɪn/| vt.|误解；曲解；对…下错误定义|To give a wrong or inaccurate definition of (something).  It implies a misunderstanding or misrepresentation of the true meaning.|例句：He misdefined the term "democracy" in his speech, leading to confusion among the audience. 他在演讲中曲解了“民主”一词的含义，导致听众感到困惑。|例句：The dictionary misdefined the word, causing many people to use it incorrectly. 这本词典对这个词的定义有误，导致许多人误用它。|近义词：misinterpret, misconstrue, misrepresent|反义词：define accurately, clarify
