reshifts:/ˈriːʃɪfts/| v. |重新转移；重新分配|To shift something again or to a new position or arrangement.  The verb "reshift" implies a previous shift and a subsequent readjustment. |例句：The company reshifted its marketing budget to focus on digital advertising. 公司重新调整了营销预算，重点放在数字广告上。

例句：The tectonic plates reshifted, causing a minor earthquake. 地壳板块再次移动，导致了一次小地震。


近义词：rearrange, reposition, redistribute, reallocate |反义词：maintain, keep, leave unchanged|动词
