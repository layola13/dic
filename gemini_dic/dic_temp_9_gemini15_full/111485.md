loadstar:/ˈloʊdˌstɑːr/| n.| 指路星，向导，目标 | A loadstar is something that guides or directs; a guiding principle or objective.  It is often used figuratively to represent something that inspires or motivates. | Example:  The company's new CEO is considered a loadstar of innovation. 这家公司的新任首席执行官被认为是创新的引路人。|近义词：guide, beacon, leader, inspiration |反义词：misdirection, hindrance, obstacle |名词

词源:
The word "loadstar" comes from the Middle English word "lode sterre," which means "guiding star."  "Lode" comes from Old English "lāde," meaning "way, course, path," and "sterre" is an Old English word for "star."  The word evolved to "loadstar" reflecting the pronunciation shift over time.
