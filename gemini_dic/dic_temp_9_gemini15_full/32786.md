choice:/tʃɔɪs/| n. |选择，抉择；选择权；精选；上品 |The act of selecting or the opportunity or right to select; the person or thing that is selected.  |例句：I had no choice but to accept his offer. 我别无选择，只能接受他的提议。|例句：The shop offers a wide choice of goods. 这家商店提供各种各样的商品。|近义词：selection, option, alternative |反义词：necessity, compulsion


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has many choices in his career. = 他在职业生涯中有许多选择。
What is your choice of dessert? = 你选择什么甜点？


一般过去时:
Her choice surprised everyone. = 她的选择让每个人都感到惊讶。
They made a wise choice. = 他们做出了明智的选择。


现在完成时:
I have made my choice. = 我已经做出了我的选择。
She has had many choices in life. = 她一生中有很多选择。


过去完成时:
By then, I had already made my choice. = 到那时，我已经做出了我的选择。
He regretted the choice he had made. = 他后悔自己做出的选择。


将来时:
You will have many choices in the future. = 你将来会有很多选择。
What choice will you make tomorrow? = 你明天会做出什么选择？


虚拟语气:
If I had had more choices, I would have chosen differently. = 如果我有更多选择，我会做出不同的选择。
I wish I had a wider choice of colors. = 我希望我有更多颜色的选择。


被动语态:
The winner was chosen by a panel of judges. = 获胜者由评审团选出。(选择是被动的)
The best option was chosen for the project. = 为该项目选择了最佳方案。(选择是被动的)


疑问句:
What is your choice? = 你的选择是什么？
Which choice do you prefer? = 你更喜欢哪个选择？


否定句:
I had no choice in the matter. = 在这件事上我别无选择。
There was no other choice available. = 没有其他选择。
