koto:/ˈkoʊtoʊ/ | n. |箏；琴曲|A traditional Japanese stringed instrument with thirteen strings, played by plucking.  It is known for its delicate and resonant sound, often used in classical Japanese music.|Example:  The koto's melodious sound filled the concert hall.  箏的旋律声充满了音乐厅。|近义词：biwa (another Japanese stringed instrument)|反义词：None|名词
