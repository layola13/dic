imagines:/ɪˈmædʒɪnz/ | v. |想象，设想 |To form a mental picture of (something not present to the senses); to conceive of as existing or happening.  |例句：She imagines herself as a famous actress. 她想象自己是一位著名的女演员。
|例句：He imagines what it would be like to live on Mars. 他想象着在火星上生活会是什么样的。|近义词：conceives, envisions, visualizes |反义词：knows, understands


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He imagines a better future. = 他想象着一个更美好的未来。
I imagine you're tired after that long journey. = 我猜想你经过这么长的旅程后一定很累了。


一般过去时:
She imagined a life of luxury. = 她想象着奢华的生活。
They imagined they were explorers in a jungle. = 他们想象自己是丛林探险家。


现在完成时:
I have imagined many scenarios. = 我已经想象过许多情况。
Have you ever imagined winning the lottery? = 你曾经想象过中彩票吗？


过去完成时:
She had imagined everything would go smoothly. = 她曾以为一切都会顺利进行。
He had imagined a different outcome. = 他曾设想过不同的结果。


将来时:
I will imagine a new story. = 我将构思一个新的故事。
They will imagine the possibilities. = 他们将设想各种可能性。


虚拟语气:
I wish I could imagine a solution. = 我希望我能想出一个解决办法。
If I imagined it correctly, it will work. = 如果我的设想正确，它就能奏效。


被动语态:
A better future is imagined by many. = 许多人想象着一个更美好的未来。(被动语态在此用法较少见)
The scenario was imagined in great detail. = 这个场景被详细地设想出来了。


疑问句:
What do you imagine will happen next? = 你想象接下来会发生什么？
Can you imagine life without technology? = 你能想象没有科技的生活吗？


否定句:
I cannot imagine him doing such a thing. = 我无法想象他会做这样的事。
She didn't imagine the consequences. = 她没有预料到后果。


PHRASE->
imagine that = 想象一下
imagine if = 想象一下如果

just imagine = 想象一下
let's imagine = 让我们想象一下
