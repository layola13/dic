reflexively:/rɪˈflɛksɪvli/ | adv. | 反射地；本能地；无意识地 |  Acting or done in a reflexive manner; instinctively or without conscious thought.  | Example: He reflexively flinched at the loud noise. 他本能地对巨大的噪音吓了一跳。 |近义词：instinctively, automatically, spontaneously | 反义词：deliberately, consciously


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She reflexively touches her hair when she's nervous. = 她紧张的时候会下意识地摸头发。
He reflexively reaches for his phone when he hears a notification. = 他听到通知时会下意识地去拿手机。


一般过去时:
I reflexively ducked when the ball came flying towards me. = 球朝我飞来时，我本能地躲开了。
She reflexively shut her eyes when the bright light flashed. = 强光一闪，她本能地闭上了眼睛。


现在完成时:
He has reflexively avoided eye contact with strangers all his life. = 他一生都本能地避免与陌生人进行眼神交流。
She has reflexively responded to criticism with defensiveness. = 她一直下意识地以防御姿态回应批评。


过去完成时:
Having reflexively grabbed the knife, he realized his mistake. = 他下意识地抓起刀子后，才意识到自己的错误。
She had reflexively stepped back before she even processed the danger. =  在她意识到危险之前，她就本能地退后了一步。


将来时:
I will reflexively pull away if you touch me unexpectedly. = 如果你意外地碰我，我会本能地缩回手。
She will reflexively react to any sudden movement. = 她会对任何突然的举动本能地做出反应。


虚拟语气:
If I saw a snake, I would reflexively jump back. = 如果我看到蛇，我会本能地跳回去。
I wish I hadn't reflexively answered without thinking. = 我真希望我没有不假思索地回答。


被动语态:
The action was reflexively performed without conscious thought. = 这个动作是下意识地完成的，没有经过深思熟虑。
His response was reflexively triggered by the loud sound. = 他对响亮的声音做出了本能的反应。


疑问句:
Did he reflexively reach for his weapon? = 他是否本能地去拿武器？
Does she reflexively shield her eyes from bright light? = 她是否会本能地用手遮挡强光？


否定句:
He didn't reflexively respond to the question. = 他并没有本能地回答这个问题。
She didn't reflexively flinch at the sight of blood. = 她看到血并没有本能地退缩。
