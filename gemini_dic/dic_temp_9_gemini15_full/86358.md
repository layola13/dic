hectolitre:/ˈhɛktoʊˌliːtər/| n. |百升|A hectolitre is a metric unit of volume equal to 100 litres.  It is used primarily in measuring large quantities of liquids, such as wine or grain. |The winery produced 50 hectolitres of wine this year.  这家酒厂今年生产了50百升葡萄酒。The farmer harvested 200 hectolitres of barley.  这位农民收获了200百升大麦。|kilolitre, decalitre|millilitre, litre|
