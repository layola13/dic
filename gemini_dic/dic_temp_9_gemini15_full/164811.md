prefilter:/ˈpriːfɪltər/| n.|预过滤器，预滤器|A device or process that filters something before it reaches a main filter.  It is used to remove larger particles or impurities to protect the main filter and extend its lifespan.|例句：The engine's prefilter removes large debris before it reaches the main air filter. 引擎的预过滤器在杂物到达主空气过滤器之前将其去除。The water treatment plant uses a prefilter to remove sediment from the raw water. 水处理厂使用预过滤器从原水中去除沉淀物。|近义词：precleaner, preliminary filter|反义词：main filter, final filter
