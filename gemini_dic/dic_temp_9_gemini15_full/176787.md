readjudicate:/ˌriːˈdʒuːdɪkeɪt/| vt.|复审，重新审判|To re-examine and judge (a case, decision, etc.) again.|例句：The court decided to readjudicate the case due to new evidence. 法院决定因新的证据而重新审理此案。例句：The appeal court will readjudicate the lower court's decision. 上诉法院将重新审理下级法院的判决。|近义词：reconsider, review, re-examine|反义词：uphold, confirm
