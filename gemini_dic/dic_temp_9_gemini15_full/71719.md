flavoury: /ˈfleɪvəri/ | adj. | 美味的，可口的 | Having a strong and pleasant taste.  | The soup was richly flavoured with herbs and spices. 这汤加了香草和香料，味道浓郁可口。|  savoury, tasty, delicious | bland, tasteless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cake is flavoured with vanilla. = 蛋糕是用香草调味的。
This dish is flavoured with ginger. = 这道菜用生姜调味。

一般过去时:
She flavoured the chicken with garlic. = 她用大蒜给鸡肉调味。
They flavoured the bread with herbs. = 他们用香草给面包调味。

现在完成时:
I have flavoured the coffee with cinnamon. = 我已经用肉桂给咖啡调味了。
She has flavoured the yogurt with fruit. = 她已经用水果给酸奶调味了。

过去完成时:
He had flavoured the stew with wine before serving it. = 上菜前他已经用葡萄酒给炖菜调味了。
They had flavoured the ice cream with chocolate before freezing it. = 他们冷冻冰淇淋之前已经用巧克力调味了。

将来时:
I will flavour the cookies with chocolate chips. = 我将会用巧克力碎片给饼干调味。
She will flavour the tea with lemon. = 她将会用柠檬给茶调味。

虚拟语气:
If I flavoured the soup with more salt, it would be tastier. = 如果我用更多的盐给汤调味，它会更美味。
I wish I had flavoured the cake with more almonds. = 我希望我用更多的杏仁给蛋糕调味。

被动语态:
The pudding is flavoured with rum. = 布丁是用朗姆酒调味的。
The cookies are flavoured with different kinds of nuts. = 饼干是用不同种类的坚果调味的。

疑问句:
Is the bread flavoured with anything? = 面包加了什么调味料吗？
How is the sauce flavoured? = 酱汁是怎么调味的？

否定句:
The meat wasn't flavoured with any spices. = 肉没有加任何香料。
The dessert isn't flavoured with artificial sweeteners. = 甜点没有用人造甜味剂调味。
