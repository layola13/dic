reimmerge:/ˌriːɪˈmɜːrdʒ/| v.|再次浮现；重新出现|To emerge again; to reappear after being submerged or hidden.|例句：After the storm subsided, the sun reemerged from behind the clouds.  暴风雨过后，太阳从云层后面重新出现。|例句：The issue of climate change has reemerged as a major political concern. 气候变化问题再次成为主要的政治关注点。|近义词：reappear, resurface, return|反义词：submerge, disappear


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun reemerges every morning. = 太阳每天早晨都会重新出现。
He reemerges from his shell after a long time. = 他很久之后才重新变得活跃起来。


一般过去时:
The lost dog reemerged from the woods. = 走失的狗从树林里重新出现了。
She reemerged as a powerful leader after years of silence. =  多年沉默之后，她重新成为一位有影响力的领导者。


现在完成时:
The economy has reemerged stronger than before. = 经济已经比以前更加强劲地复苏了。
Hope has reemerged in their hearts after the tragedy. = 悲剧发生后，希望重新燃起了他们的希望。


过去完成时:
After the floodwaters had receded, the town slowly reemerged. = 洪水退去后，小镇慢慢地重新出现了。
They realized that the problem had reemerged after they thought it was solved. = 他们意识到，在他们认为问题已经解决之后，问题又再次出现了。


将来时:
The comet will reemerge in the night sky next year. = 这颗彗星明年将在夜空中再次出现。
The company plans to reemerge into the market next quarter. = 公司计划在下个季度重新进入市场。


虚拟语气:
If the economy were to reemerge quickly, we would see growth soon. = 如果经济能够迅速复苏，我们很快就会看到增长。
I wish the lost treasure would reemerge one day. = 我希望失落的宝藏有一天能够重现。


被动语态:
The sunken ship was reemerged by the salvage team. = 沉船被打捞队重新打捞出来了。(此句被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The truth was reemerged during the investigation.  真相在调查过程中被揭露了。(此句被动语态更自然)


疑问句:
Will the missing child reemerge safely? = 失踪的孩子会安全地出现吗？
When will the project reemerge from the planning stage? = 这个项目什么时候才能从规划阶段进入下一个阶段？


否定句:
The lost painting has not reemerged. = 失落的画作尚未出现。
The evidence did not reemerge during the trial. = 在审判过程中没有出现新的证据。
