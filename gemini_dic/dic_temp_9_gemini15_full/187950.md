rotisseried:/rəˈtɪsərid/| adj.|烤肉的；旋转烤制的| Cooked on a rotisserie.  The term describes food that has been cooked using a rotisserie, a rotating spit that cooks food evenly by exposing all sides to heat.|例句：The rotisseried chicken was juicy and flavorful.  这只旋转烤鸡肉多汁美味。 The restaurant is famous for its rotisseried lamb. 这家餐厅以其旋转烤羊肉而闻名。|近义词：roasted, grilled|反义词：boiled, steamed

词源:
From "rotisserie," ultimately from the Old French *rostissoir*,  a compound of *rostir* "to roast" (from Latin *torrere*) and  *-issoir*, a suffix denoting an instrument or place.
