ruby:/ˈruːbi/ /ˈruːbiː/| n. |红宝石；红宝石色；红宝石酒|A precious stone, a red variety of corundum, valued for its color and brilliance.  It's also used to describe a deep red color and a type of red wine.|例句：She wore a ring with a beautiful ruby. 她戴着一枚镶有漂亮红宝石的戒指。

例句：The sunset painted the sky in shades of ruby.  日落把天空染成了红宝石般的颜色。

近义词：garnet, carbuncle|反义词：sapphire (in terms of color) |词性：名词
