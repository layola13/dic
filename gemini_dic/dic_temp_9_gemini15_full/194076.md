self-hope: /ˌsɛlfˈhoʊp/ | n. | 自我期许，自我希望 |  A feeling of optimism and confidence in one's own abilities and future; belief in one's capacity to achieve goals and overcome challenges.  It emphasizes the internal source of hope, originating from within oneself rather than external factors. | 例句： Cultivating self-hope is crucial for navigating difficult times.  =  培养自我期许对于应对艰难时期至关重要。


Her self-hope propelled her forward despite setbacks. = 尽管遭遇挫折，她的自我期许仍然推动她前进。


近义词：self-belief, self-confidence, optimism, self-efficacy | 反义词：despair, hopelessness, pessimism, self-doubt | 词性：名词
