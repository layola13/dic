eremite:/ˈɛrəmʌɪt/ | n. |隐士，隐者 | A person who lives a solitary life, often for religious reasons.  An eremite withdraws from society to live in seclusion, often in a remote place. | Example:  The eremite lived in a cave high in the mountains, far from human contact.  这位隐士住在高高的山上的一个洞穴里，远离人类接触。|近义词：recluse, ascetic, hermit |反义词：socialite, extrovert
