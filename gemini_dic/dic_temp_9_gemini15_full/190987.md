scallions:/ˈskæljənz/ | n. | 葱，细香葱 | Scallions are a type of onion with a mild flavor and long, slender green stalks. They are often used as a garnish or in salads and stir-fries.  | Example: I added some chopped scallions to the soup for extra flavor. 我在汤里加了一些切碎的葱来增加风味。 | 近义词：spring onions, green onions | 反义词：无
