roughneck:/ˈrʌfnek/| n. adj.|粗鲁的人；石油工人；（指人）粗暴的；粗野的|A roughneck is a person, especially a worker in the oil or gas industry, who performs physically demanding tasks.  The term can also describe someone who is rough and uncouth in behavior. |例句：The roughnecks on the oil rig worked long hours in dangerous conditions.  石油钻井平台上的粗鲁工人长时间在危险的条件下工作。

The roughneck was known for his aggressive behavior and lack of manners.  这个粗鲁的人以其侵略性的行为和缺乏礼貌而闻名。|近义词：brute, tough guy, oilfield worker|反义词：gentleman, refined person
