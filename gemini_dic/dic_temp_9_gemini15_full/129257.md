nodation:/noʊˈdeɪʃən/ | n. |点头；点头示意；点头的动作 |The act of nodding; a nod.  Nodation is a nonverbal communication that often signifies agreement or understanding.  | Example: The nodation of her head confirmed her agreement to the plan.  点头的动作证实了她同意这个计划。|近义词：nod, headshake, inclination |反义词：shaking the head
