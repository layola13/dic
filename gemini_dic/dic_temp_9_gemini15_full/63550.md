envenime:/ɪnˈvɛnɪm/| vt.|使充满毒液；使恶化；毒化|To fill with poison; to make bitter or more harmful; to poison.|例句：The snake envenomed its prey. 蛇毒化了它的猎物。The situation was further envenomed by their arguments. 他们的争吵进一步恶化了局势。|近义词：poison, embitter, taint|反义词：heal, soothe, purify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He envenoms the well water. 他毒化了井水。
The politician envenoms the public discourse. 这个政客毒化了公共舆论。

一般过去时:
The scorpion envenomed its victim. 蝎子毒化了它的受害者。
Her words envenomed the already tense atmosphere. 她的言语使本来就紧张的气氛更加恶化。

现在完成时:
The rumors have envenomed the relationship between them. 谣言已经毒化了他们之间的关系。
Years of conflict have envenomed the political landscape. 多年的冲突已经毒化了政治格局。


过去完成时:
He had envenomed the situation with his reckless accusations. 他轻率的指控已经恶化了局势。
The media had envenomed the debate with biased reporting. 媒体以偏颇的报道恶化了这场辩论。


将来时:
His actions will envenom the situation further. 他的行为将进一步恶化局势。
The spread of misinformation will envenom public opinion. 虚假信息的传播将会毒化公众舆论。


虚拟语气:
If he had not interfered, the situation would not have been envenomed. 如果他不干涉，情况就不会恶化。
I wish that the media hadn't envenomed the debate. 我希望媒体没有恶化这场辩论。


被动语态:
The water supply was envenomed by industrial waste. 水源被工业废物污染了。
The atmosphere was envenomed by their constant bickering.  气氛因他们不断的争吵而恶化。


疑问句:
Was the food envenomed? 食物被毒了吗？
Has the dispute been envenomed by outside forces?  这场争端是被外部势力恶化了吗？


否定句:
The snake did not envenom its victim. 蛇没有毒化它的受害者。
He did not intend to envenom the situation with his remarks. 他无意通过他的言论来恶化局势。
