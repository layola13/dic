cashew-nuts: /ˈkæʃuːnʌts/ | n. |腰果| Cashew nuts are kidney-shaped nuts that grow on the cashew apple. They are known for their sweet and slightly salty flavor and are often roasted or used in desserts and savory dishes.  | Example: I love to snack on roasted cashew nuts. 我喜欢吃烤腰果。  |近义词:macadamia nuts, peanuts, almonds |反义词:None.

词源:
The word "cashew" is derived from the Portuguese word "caju," which in turn comes from the Tupi word "acajú."  The Tupi language is indigenous to Brazil, where the cashew tree originated.  "Nuts" is a standard English word referring to the seed of certain fruits.
