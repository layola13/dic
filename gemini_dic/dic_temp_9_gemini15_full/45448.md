curassow:/kəˈræsoʊ/| n. |巨冠鸟|A curassow is a large, mainly tropical bird belonging to the family Cracidae.  They are known for their large size, colorful plumage, and distinctive crests. They are primarily found in Central and South America.|Example: The resplendent curassow is a striking bird with iridescent feathers.|近义词：cracid (a broader term)|反义词：无
