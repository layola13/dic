repraising:/ˌriːˈpreɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 再赞扬，再次称赞 |  Repraising refers to praising or commending something or someone again, often after a period of time or a new development. It implies a continuation or reinforcement of positive assessment. | The manager was repraising the team for their successful project completion.  经理再次称赞团队成功完成项目。| praising, commending, applauding | criticizing, condemning, blaming | 动词
