endeavors:/ɪnˈdevərz/ | n. |努力，尝试；事业 |努力，尽力去做某事；指为了达到某个目标而付出的努力和尝试。它通常指持续的努力，而不是一次性的尝试。 |例句：Her endeavors to learn Chinese were admirable.  她的努力学习中文令人钦佩。

例句：Their endeavors to climb the mountain were thwarted by bad weather. 他们攀登这座山的努力由于恶劣天气而受阻。

近义词：efforts, attempts, struggles, pursuits | 反义词：inaction, idleness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He endeavors to improve his English. = 他努力提高他的英语水平。
She endeavors to finish the project on time. = 她努力按时完成项目。

一般过去时:
He endeavored to help his friend. = 他尽力帮助他的朋友。
She endeavored to explain the situation. = 她努力解释情况。

现在完成时:
He has endeavored to understand the problem. = 他一直努力理解这个问题。
She has endeavored to make a difference in the world. = 她一直努力改变世界。

过去完成时:
He had endeavored to warn them, but they didn't listen. = 他曾试图警告他们，但他们没有听。
She had endeavored to reach the summit, but failed. = 她曾努力到达山顶，但失败了。

将来时:
He will endeavor to complete the task. = 他将努力完成这项任务。
She will endeavor to find a solution. = 她将努力找到解决方案。

虚拟语气:
If he endeavored more diligently, he would succeed. = 如果他更加努力，他就会成功。
I wish she had endeavored to understand my point of view. = 我希望她曾努力理解我的观点。

被动语态:
Great efforts were endeavored to solve the crisis. = 为了解决危机，付出了巨大的努力。(注意：被动语态用endeavor不常见，通常用effort等词语)
The project was endeavored to be completed on time. =  该项目试图按时完成。(同样，被动语态用endeavor不常见，表达略显笨拙)


疑问句:
What endeavors are you undertaking? = 你正在从事什么努力？
What endeavors did they make to resolve the conflict? = 他们为了解决冲突做了哪些努力？

否定句:
He did not endeavor to hide his feelings. = 他并没有努力隐藏他的感情。
They did not endeavor to understand the situation. = 他们没有努力去理解情况。
