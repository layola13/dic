overworn:/ˌoʊvərˈwɔːrn/| adj. |磨损的；过时的；陈腐的 | excessively worn; showing the effects of prolonged use or wear;  outdated or old-fashioned. |例句：His overworn boots showed years of hard work. 他的旧靴子显示出多年的辛勤工作。


例句：The overworn clichés in his speech made it uninteresting. 他演讲中陈腐的套话使它索然无味。


近义词：worn-out, frayed, shabby, dated, passé


反义词：new, fresh, unused, modern, contemporary


词性:形容词


词源:over- (表示过度) + worn (过去分词，来自wear)  表示过度磨损，引申为过时陈旧。
