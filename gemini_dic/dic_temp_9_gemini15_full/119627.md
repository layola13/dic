middle-ageing: /ˌmɪdlˈeɪdʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 中年期的，步入中年的 |  Describing the process or state of becoming middle-aged.  It emphasizes the transition and the changes associated with reaching middle age.  |  The middle-ageing population is increasingly concerned about healthcare costs.  中年人口越来越关注医疗成本。  He's going through a period of middle-ageing, questioning his life choices. 他正经历着中年期，质疑自己的人生选择。 | middle-aged, aging | youthful, young
