ciceronianism:/ˌsɪsəˈroʊniənɪzəm/| n. |西塞罗式风格；雄辩术；华丽的文风|The style of writing or speaking characterized by elegance, elaborate sentence structure, and balanced phrasing, as exemplified by the Roman orator Cicero.  It often involves the use of rhetorical devices and a formal tone.|His speeches were characterized by a marked ciceronianism, impressing the audience with their eloquence and precision.  他的演讲具有明显的西塞罗式风格，其雄辩和精准给听众留下了深刻印象。|Classical style, eloquence, ornate style|Plain style, simple language|名词
