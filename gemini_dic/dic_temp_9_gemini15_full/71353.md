flacked:/flækt/| v.|猛烈抨击，严厉批评|to criticize severely or attack vehemently.|The politician flacked his opponent's policies in a televised debate.  这位政治家在电视辩论中猛烈抨击了对手的政策。|The journalist flacked the company's unethical practices.  这位记者严厉批评了该公司的违规行为。|criticized, attacked, lambasted, denounced|praised, supported, defended|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flacks his rivals relentlessly. = 他无情地猛烈抨击他的对手。
They constantly flack the government's new policies. = 他们不断地猛烈抨击政府的新政策。

The media flacked the celebrity's scandalous behavior. = 媒体猛烈抨击了这位名人的丑闻行为。
