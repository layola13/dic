doomer:/ˈduːmər/| n. adj.|末日论者；悲观主义者；绝望的人|A person who believes that the world is ending or is heading towards inevitable disaster, often expressing this belief with cynicism and pessimism.  They often focus on negative aspects of society and the environment and lack hope for the future. |例句：He's become a real doomer lately, constantly complaining about climate change and societal collapse.  他最近变得非常悲观，不断抱怨气候变化和社会崩溃。

例句：The online community is full of doomers who predict an impending economic crisis.  网络社区充斥着预测即将到来的经济危机的末日论者。


近义词：pessimist, catastrophist, nihilist|反义词：optimist, utopian

虚拟语气:
