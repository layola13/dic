healthier:/ˈhɛlθiər/| adj. |更健康的，比较健康的 |More healthy; possessing or enjoying good health to a greater degree.  It's a comparative adjective, indicating a greater degree of health than something else.|例句：Eating more fruits and vegetables will make you healthier.  多吃水果和蔬菜会让你更健康。

例句：This diet is healthier than the one I was following before.  这个饮食比我之前遵循的饮食更健康。


近义词：healthier, more robust, fitter, stronger |反义词：unhealthier, sicker, weaker |词性：形容词
