pharyngitis:/ˌfærɪnˈdʒaɪtɪs/| n. |咽炎|Inflammation of the pharynx (the back of the throat).  It can be caused by viral or bacterial infections, and symptoms often include sore throat, difficulty swallowing, and sometimes fever. |例句：The doctor diagnosed her with pharyngitis and prescribed antibiotics. 医生诊断她患有咽炎并开了抗生素。|例句：He suffered from acute pharyngitis after a bout of the flu. 他流感之后患上了急性咽炎。|近义词：throat infection, pharyngeal inflammation |反义词：healthy pharynx
