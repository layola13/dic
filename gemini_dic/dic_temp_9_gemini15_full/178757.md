rectors:/ˈrektərz/| n.| 校长；牧师；指导者 |  A rector is a person who holds a particular position of authority or responsibility, typically a head of a school, college, or university, or a pastor of a church.  |例句：The rector of the university gave a speech at the graduation ceremony.  大学的校长在毕业典礼上发表了演讲。|例句：The rector of the parish church was well-respected in the community. 教区的牧师在社区里很受尊敬。|近义词：principal, headmaster, pastor, president, dean |反义词：student, parishioner
