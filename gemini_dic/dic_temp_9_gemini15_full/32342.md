childermas:/ˈtʃɪldərˌmæs/| n. |圣婴节（12月28日）|Childmas, also known as Childermas, is a Christian feast day observed on December 28. It is a day traditionally associated with the slaughter of the innocents described in the Gospel of Matthew.  In some traditions, it marks the end of the Christmas season.  The name is a contraction of "children's mass" or "children's day".|The Childermas celebrations varied greatly across different regions and time periods. Some involved festivities and gifts for children, while others were more somber in remembrance of the biblical event.|近义词：Holy Innocents' Day|反义词：无
