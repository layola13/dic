cleverer:/ˈklɛvərər/| adj.|更聪明的，更机灵的|More intelligent or skillful than others.  It is the comparative form of the adjective "clever".|He is cleverer than his brother. 他比他弟弟更聪明。She is a cleverer strategist than her opponent. 她是比对手更聪明的战略家。|smart, intelligent, bright, astute|stupid, foolish, dull, obtuse|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is cleverer than his sister. = 他比他姐姐更聪明。
She is cleverer at solving puzzles than I am. = 她解谜比我更聪明。

一般过去时:
He was cleverer at maths than he was at English. = 他数学比英语更聪明。
She was cleverer in handling that situation than I expected. = 她处理这种情况比我预期的更聪明。


现在完成时:
He has become cleverer since he started reading more. = 自从他开始阅读更多书籍后，他变得更聪明了。
She has proven herself cleverer than many of her peers. = 她已经证明自己比许多同龄人更聪明。


过去完成时:
He had been cleverer in his youth, but age had dulled his wits. = 他年轻时更聪明，但年龄使他的智慧变得迟钝。
She had already proven herself cleverer than her opponents, guaranteeing her victory. = 她已经证明自己比对手更聪明，保证了她获胜。


将来时:
He will be cleverer than his classmates after he completes this course. = 完成这门课程后，他将比他的同学们更聪明。
She will surely be cleverer at negotiations after attending this training. = 参加这次培训后，她肯定会在谈判方面更聪明。


虚拟语气:
If he were cleverer, he would have avoided that mistake. = 如果他更聪明，他就会避免那个错误。
If she had been cleverer, she would have seen through his deception. = 如果她更聪明，她就会识破他的欺骗。


被动语态:
(The comparative form "cleverer" is rarely used in the passive voice.  The passive voice would focus on the cleverness itself, not the comparison.)


疑问句:
Is he cleverer than his brother? = 他比他弟弟更聪明吗？
Is she cleverer at this game than he is? = 她玩这个游戏比他更聪明吗？


否定句:
He isn't cleverer than his brother. = 他不如他弟弟聪明。
She is not cleverer than I thought she was. = 她没有我想象的那么聪明。
