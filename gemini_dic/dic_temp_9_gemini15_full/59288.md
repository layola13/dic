/ˈsiːɡræs/| n.| 海草 |Seagrass refers to several species of flowering plants that live in marine environments. They are found in shallow coastal waters around the world and form extensive underwater meadows.  Seagrasses play a vital role in the marine ecosystem, providing habitat and food for numerous organisms, and contributing to water quality.| Seagrass beds provide important habitats for fish and invertebrates.  |近义词：seagrass meadow, eelgrass (specific type)| 反义词：None|名词
