colorimetry:/ˌkʌlərˈɪmɪtri/| n. |比色法；测色法|The scientific study and measurement of color.  It involves the objective assessment of color using instruments and standardized methods.|Example: Colorimetry is crucial in various industries, from printing to food production, to ensure consistent color reproduction.|近义词：color measurement, spectrophotometry|反义词：None specifically, but concepts like subjective color perception could be considered contrasting.|名词
