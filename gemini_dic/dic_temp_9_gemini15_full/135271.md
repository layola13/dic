nonparental: /ˌnɑːn.pəˈrentəl/ | adj. | 非父母的；非亲生的 | Not relating to or involving parents.  Describes situations, relationships, or care arrangements that do not involve parents. |

例句：
The child received nonparental care.  孩子接受了非父母的照顾。

The study examined the effects of nonparental childcare on child development.  这项研究调查了非父母托儿对儿童发展的影响。


近义词： nonpaternal, unrelated

反义词： parental
