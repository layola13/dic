latticed:/ˈlætɪst/| adj.|格状的；有格子的；|having a pattern or structure of crossed strips or bars, like a lattice.  It often describes a surface or structure. |例句：The window was latticed, allowing a gentle breeze to filter through.  窗户是格状的，让微风轻轻吹过。

例句：The garden was enclosed by a latticed fence. 花园被格状篱笆围起来。


近义词：trellised, cross-barred, reticulated|反义词：solid, unpatterned


词源:  From Middle English *latis, from Old French *latice, from Latin *latus ("lath, slat").  The word's evolution reflects the use of thin strips of wood (laths) to create lattice structures.
