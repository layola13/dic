misthrowing: /ˌmɪsˈθroʊɪŋ/ | v. | 扔错，投掷错误 | The act of throwing something incorrectly or to the wrong place.  It implies a lack of accuracy or precision in the throwing action. | 例句：The pitcher's misthrowing cost the team the game. = 投手的投球失误让球队输掉了比赛。

The quarterback's misthrowing of the ball resulted in an interception. = 四分卫扔球失误导致对方拦截。


近义词：throwing badly, misdirecting, erring, fumbling


反义词：throwing accurately, hitting the target, succeeding


词性：动词
