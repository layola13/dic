overtrains:/ˌoʊvərˈtreɪnz/ | v. |过度训练|To train excessively; to subject to more training than is beneficial or healthy.  This can lead to injury, burnout, and decreased performance.|例句：He overtrains every day, neglecting rest and recovery. 他每天过度训练，忽略了休息和恢复。 He overtrained his pitching arm and had to sit out the season. 他过度训练了他的投球手臂，不得不缺席整个赛季。|近义词：overwork, exhaust, overdo |反义词：undertrain, rest
