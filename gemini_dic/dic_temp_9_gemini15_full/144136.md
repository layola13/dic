outseeking: /ˌaʊtˈsiːkɪŋ/ | adj. | 寻求外部的；追求外界的；渴望外界的 | Seeking something outside oneself; actively pursuing external experiences or information.  |  例句：Her outseeking nature led her to travel the world and experience diverse cultures.  她的寻求外部事物的天性促使她周游世界，体验不同的文化。

例句：His outseeking personality made him a successful entrepreneur, constantly seeking new opportunities and partnerships. 他寻求外界的个性使他成为一名成功的企业家，不断寻求新的机遇和合作伙伴。


近义词：exploratory, adventurous, inquisitive, outward-looking


反义词：introspective, inward-looking, self-contained


词性: Adjective (形容词)


词源:  The word "outseeking" is a relatively modern coinage, formed by combining the prefix "out-" (meaning "outside" or "beyond") with the present participle "seeking."  The root "seek" has Old English origins, related to the concept of searching or looking for something.
