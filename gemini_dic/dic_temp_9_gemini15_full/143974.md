outrageously:/aʊˈtreɪdʒəsli/ | adj. | 令人震惊地；过分地；极其；非常 | Extremely shocking or offensive; excessively; to a shocking degree.  |例句：He behaved outrageously at the party. 他在派对上行为极其放肆。

例句：The prices at that restaurant are outrageously high. 那家餐馆的价格高得离谱。


近义词：shockingly, scandalously, excessively, incredibly, remarkably, extremely

反义词：mildly, moderately, reasonably, acceptably


词源:  Derived from "outrage" (n.), meaning a feeling of anger and resentment, ultimately from Old French "outrager" (to offend, insult), from "outre" (beyond, excessive) + "ager" (to act). The "-ously" suffix indicates an adverbial form, but it's commonly used adjectivally.
