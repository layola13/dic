reminiscence:/ˌremɪˈnɪs(ə)ns/| n. |回忆，追忆|The act or process of recalling past experiences, events, or people; a memory or collection of memories, especially happy ones.  It often involves a sentimental or nostalgic feeling.|例句：Her reminiscences of childhood were filled with warmth and laughter.  她的童年回忆充满了温暖和欢笑。

例句：He shared his reminiscences of his time in the army. 他分享了他服役期间的回忆。


近义词：memory, recollection, remembrance, retrospection|反义词：forgetfulness, oblivion|词性：名词
