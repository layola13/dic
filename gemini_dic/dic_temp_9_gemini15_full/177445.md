rebated:/rɪˈbeɪtɪd/| adj. v.|已回扣的；给予回扣|Returned money; a discount.  |The rebated price made the purchase more attractive.  已回扣的价格使购买更具吸引力。|discounted, reduced|full price, original price|动词过去式和过去分词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company rebates a portion of the purchase price. = 公司退还一部分购买价格。
They rebate customers for early payment. = 他们对提前付款的客户给予回扣。


一般过去时:
The store rebated me 10% on my purchase. = 商店在我的购买中给了我10%的回扣。
We were rebated the shipping cost. = 我们获得了运费的退款。


现在完成时:
They have rebated the tax on the purchase. = 他们已经退还了购买商品的税款。
The government has rebated several billion dollars in taxes. = 政府已经退还了几十亿美元的税款。


过去完成时:
After I complained, they had rebated the full amount. = 我投诉后，他们已经退还了全部款项。
The company had already rebated the faulty product before we noticed. =在我们注意到之前，公司已经退还了有缺陷的产品。


将来时:
The manufacturer will rebate a percentage of the price. = 制造商将退还一部分价格。
They will rebate the customers for any damages. = 他们将向客户退还任何损失。


虚拟语气:
If they rebated the price, I would buy it. = 如果他们降低价格，我会买它。
I wish they had rebated the shipping fee. = 我希望他们已经退还了运费。


被动语态:
The tax was rebated to the customer. = 税款已退还给客户。
A portion of the cost will be rebated. = 一部分成本将被退还。


疑问句:
Was the shipping cost rebated? = 运费退了吗？
Will the price be rebated? = 价格会降低吗？


否定句:
The company did not rebate the money. = 公司没有退还这笔钱。
They will not rebate the faulty product. = 他们不会退还有缺陷的产品。
