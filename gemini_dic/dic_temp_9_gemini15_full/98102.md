incredible:/ɪnˈkredəbl/| adj. |难以置信的，极好的|extremely good or great;  so extraordinary as to seem impossible to believe.|例句：The view from the mountaintop was incredible.  山顶的景色令人难以置信。|例句：She's an incredible actress. 她是一位了不起的女演员。|近义词：unbelievable, amazing, astonishing, extraordinary|反义词：believable, ordinary, commonplace
