preadvise:/priːədˈvaɪz/ | vt. | 预先通知，预先告知 | To inform or warn someone in advance.  |例句：The company preadvised its clients of the impending price increase. 公司提前通知客户即将上涨的价格。

例句：They preadvised the committee of the potential risks. 他们预先向委员会通报了潜在的风险。


近义词：forewarn, notify in advance, give prior notice to

反义词：postpone informing, keep secret


词性：动词
