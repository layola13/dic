hye:/haɪ/| int.|嘿，你好|A casual greeting, similar to "hi" or "hey".  It's an informal way to address someone.  |Example: Hye, how's it going? 嘿，最近怎么样？|近义词：hi, hey, hello |反义词: N/A |词性: int.


USAGE->
This word is an interjection and doesn't have tense conjugation like verbs.  The usage is always the same.


PHRASE->
There aren't common phrases specifically using "hye" as it's a very informal and short greeting.  It's typically used independently.
