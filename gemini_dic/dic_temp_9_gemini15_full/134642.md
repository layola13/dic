nonmalignance: /ˌnɒnməˈlɪɡnəns/ | n. | 无恶性；良性；无恶意 | The absence of malice or ill will; benignity.  It refers to a lack of harmful intent or action.  This word is relatively rare and often used in specific contexts, particularly in medical or legal settings where it contrasts with malignancy. | Example:  The doctor confirmed the nonmalignance of the tumor.  医生确认肿瘤是良性的。  The court noted the nonmalignance of the defendant's actions. 法院注意到被告的行为没有恶意。| 近义词：benignity, harmlessness, innocuousness | 反义词：malignancy, malevolence, malice |  名词
