/ˈneɪzəlaɪz/ | vt. | 鼻音化；使带鼻音 | To pronounce (a vowel or syllable) with nasal resonance, as in French.  This involves lowering the soft palate, allowing air to pass through the nasal cavity during the articulation of the sound.  | The singer nasalises the vowels excessively. 歌手过度地将元音鼻音化。|  resonate,  vocalize |  (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑用“to pronounce without nasal resonance” 来表达反义) | 动词
