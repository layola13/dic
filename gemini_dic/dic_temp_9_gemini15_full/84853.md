hangars:/ˈhæŋɑːrz/ | n. |飞机库，机棚 |A hangar is a large building where aircraft are stored and maintained.  |例句：The airplane was safely tucked away in the hangar. 这架飞机安全地停放在机库里。|例句：The mechanics worked tirelessly in the hangar, repairing the damaged aircraft. 机械师们在机库里不知疲倦地工作，修理受损的飞机。|近义词：shed, shelter |反义词：None.
