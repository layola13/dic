reconsent:/ˌriːkənˈsɛnt/| v.|重新同意，再次同意 |To give consent again; to consent anew.  It implies a previous consent that may have been withdrawn or needs reaffirmation. |例句：After reviewing the contract, she reconsented to the terms.  在她审查合同后，她再次同意了条款。

例句：The patient reconsented to the surgery after further explanation. 在进一步解释后，病人再次同意了手术。


近义词：reapprove, reaffirm, ratify|反义词：withdraw consent, object, refuse


词性：动词
