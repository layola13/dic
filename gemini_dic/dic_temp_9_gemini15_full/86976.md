hendrix:/ˈhɛndrɪks/| n. |亨德里克斯（姓氏）|Hendrix is a surname of English origin.  It is a habitational name from places called Hendrix, in Yorkshire and Gloucestershire, which are derived from the Old English personal name "Hendra" + "ric," meaning "Hendra's power" or "Hendra's domain."  The name is most famously associated with Jimi Hendrix, the influential guitarist.|Jimi Hendrix was a legendary guitarist. |近义词:无|反义词:无|名词
