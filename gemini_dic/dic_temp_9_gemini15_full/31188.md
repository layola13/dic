changemaker:/ˈtʃeɪndʒˌmeɪkər/| n. |变革者，革新者| A person who creates positive social or environmental change.  They are often driven by a strong sense of social justice and a commitment to improving the lives of others.  Changemakers can be individuals, groups, or organizations working at local, national, or international levels. | Example:  Malala Yousafzai is a powerful changemaker advocating for girls' education.  玛拉拉·优素福扎伊是一位强大的变革者，倡导女孩接受教育。  |近义词：innovator, reformer, reformer, revolutionary |反义词：reactionary, obstructionist | 名词
