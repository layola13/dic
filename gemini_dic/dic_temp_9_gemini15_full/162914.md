precertifications:/ˌpriːˌsɜːtɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |预先认证；预先资格认证 |  The process of obtaining certifications or approvals before a main process or event.  This often involves meeting specific requirements or standards in advance.  |例句：The project requires several precertifications before we can begin construction.  这个项目在我们开始施工前需要几个预先认证。

例句：The company expedited the precertifications to meet the deadline.  公司加快了预先认证流程以满足最后期限。


近义词：prior approvals, advance authorizations, preliminary qualifications


反义词：post-certifications, subsequent approvals


词性: noun
