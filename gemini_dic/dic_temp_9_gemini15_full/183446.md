reptiliform:/ˈrɛptɪlɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 爬行动物形的；似爬行动物的 | Resembling a reptile in form or structure.  The term often refers to the elongated, limbless, or nearly limbless body shape characteristic of many reptiles like snakes and some lizards. | Example: The fossil shows a reptiliform body plan, suggesting a close relationship to early reptiles.  化石显示出爬行动物形的体型，表明它与早期爬行动物关系密切。|近义词：serpentine, snakelike, wormlike |反义词：mammaliform, avian
