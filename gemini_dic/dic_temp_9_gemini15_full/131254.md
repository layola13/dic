noncarrier:/ˌnɒnˈkæriər/| n. |非承运人，非承运商|A noncarrier is a business or individual that arranges transportation of goods but does not own or operate the means of transportation.  They act as intermediaries, facilitating the shipment of goods through contracts with carriers.  They are distinct from carriers, who own and operate the vehicles or vessels used for transportation.|Example: The freight forwarder acted as a noncarrier, contracting with a trucking company to move the goods.|近义词：freight forwarder, shipper (in some contexts)|反义词：carrier
