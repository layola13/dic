moulting:/ˈmoʊl.tɪŋ/| n. |蜕皮；换毛；脱落|The shedding of an outer layer, such as skin, feathers, or fur, in an animal.  This process is often seasonal or cyclical, allowing for the replacement of old or damaged coverings.|例句：The snake is undergoing moulting. 这条蛇正在蜕皮。

例句：The birds are moulting their feathers.  这些鸟正在换毛。


近义词：shedding, sloughing, casting off |反义词：growing, retaining


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The snake moults every few months. = 蛇每隔几个月蜕一次皮。
The bird is moulting its feathers now. = 这只鸟现在正在换毛。


一般过去时:
The caterpillar moulted several times before becoming a butterfly. = 毛毛虫在变成蝴蝶之前蜕了好几次皮。
The dog moulted heavily in the spring. = 春天狗大量脱毛。


现在完成时:
The snake has moulted twice this year. = 这条蛇今年已经蜕皮两次了。
The parrot has moulted its bright green feathers. = 鹦鹉已经换掉了它鲜艳的绿色羽毛。


过去完成时:
The bird had moulted before the migration. = 这只鸟在迁徙前已经换毛了。
The snake had moulted its skin before I saw it. = 我看到它之前，蛇已经蜕皮了。


将来时:
The snake will moult again next month. = 蛇下个月还会再蜕皮。
The dog will moult heavily in the spring. = 狗将在春天大量脱毛。


虚拟语气:
If the bird moulted earlier, it would have a better chance to survive winter. = 如果鸟早点换毛，它就能更好地度过冬天。
If the snake had moulted successfully, it would not be so weak. = 如果蛇成功蜕皮，它就不会这么虚弱了。


被动语态:
The feathers are moulted every year. = 羽毛每年都会脱落。

疑问句:

否定句:
