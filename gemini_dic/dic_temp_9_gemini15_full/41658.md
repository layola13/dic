correlates:/ˈkɔːrəleɪts/ | v. | 关联；相互关联 | To have a mutual relationship or connection, often implying a statistical relationship.  |  Studies show that smoking correlates with lung cancer.  研究表明吸烟与肺癌相关。|  relates, associates, connects |  disconnects, separates | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Smoking correlates with lung cancer. = 吸烟与肺癌相关。
Income correlates with education level. = 收入与教育水平相关。

一般过去时:
The study showed that stress correlated with heart disease. = 研究表明压力与心脏病相关。
Researchers found that exercise correlated with improved mood. = 研究人员发现运动与改善情绪相关。

现在完成时:
Many studies have correlated obesity with various health problems. = 许多研究都将肥胖与各种健康问题联系起来。
Scientists have correlated climate change with rising sea levels. = 科学家们已经将气候变化与海平面上升联系起来。

过去完成时:
Previous research had correlated sleep deprivation with impaired cognitive function. = 先前的研究已经将睡眠不足与认知功能障碍联系起来。
The data had correlated economic growth with reduced poverty. = 数据显示经济增长与贫困减少相关。

将来时:
Future studies will correlate social media use with mental health. = 未来的研究将把社交媒体的使用与心理健康联系起来。
Scientists predict that they will correlate genetic factors with disease risk. = 科学家预测他们将把遗传因素与疾病风险联系起来。

虚拟语气:
If these factors correlated, we would have a stronger conclusion. = 如果这些因素相关，我们的结论会更有力。
If the data correlated more strongly, the results would be more significant. = 如果数据相关性更强，结果将更有意义。

被动语态:
The two variables were found to be correlated. = 发现这两个变量是相关的。
It was shown that air pollution correlated with respiratory illnesses. = 研究表明空气污染与呼吸系统疾病相关。

疑问句:
Does smoking correlate with heart disease? = 吸烟与心脏病相关吗？
Do these two variables correlate significantly? = 这两个变量的相关性是否显著？

否定句:
The study did not find a correlation between the two variables. = 该研究没有发现这两个变量之间的相关性。
There was no correlation between rainfall and crop yields. = 降雨量和作物产量之间没有相关性。


PHRASE->

positively correlated = 正相关
negatively correlated = 负相关
