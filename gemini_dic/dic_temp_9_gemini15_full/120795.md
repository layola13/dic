misarticulations:/ˌmɪsɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪʃənz/| n. |发音错误，言语不清|Errors in the articulation of speech sounds, often due to developmental, neurological, or other factors.  These can range from mild substitutions to severe distortions or omissions of sounds.|The child's misarticulations were noticeable in their difficulty pronouncing certain consonant blends.|近义词：speech errors, articulation disorders, pronunciation problems|反义词：clear articulation, perfect pronunciation
