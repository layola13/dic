grievance:/ˈɡriːvəns/| n. |委屈，不满，冤情 |A grievance is a real or imagined wrong or other cause for complaint or protest.  It refers to a feeling of resentment over something unjust, unfair, or wrong. |例句：The workers voiced their grievances about low wages and poor working conditions. 工人们表达了他们对低工资和恶劣工作条件的不满。

例句：She filed a formal grievance against her employer for unfair dismissal. 她就公司不公平解雇向雇主提交了正式申诉。


近义词：complaint, complaint, protest, objection, gripe

反义词：satisfaction, contentment


词性:名词
