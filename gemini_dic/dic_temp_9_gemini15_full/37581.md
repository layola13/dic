communally:/kəˈmjuːnəli/ | adv. | 公社地；公有地；共同地 | In a communal manner; shared by or done by a group of people.  | 例句：The land was used communally by the villagers. 村民们共同使用这块土地。


例句：They decided to raise the children communally. 他们决定共同抚养孩子。


近义词：jointly, collectively, together, in common

反义词：individually, privately, separately

词性:副词
