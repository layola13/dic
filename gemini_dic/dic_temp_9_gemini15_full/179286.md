redivide:/ˌriːdɪˈvaɪd/| vt.|重新划分，再分配|To divide something again, especially in a different way.|例句：The land was redivided after the war.  战争结束后土地被重新划分。
例句：The company decided to redivide the workload among its employees. 公司决定重新分配员工的工作量。|近义词：reapportion, redistribute, subdivide|反义词：unite, combine|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They redivide the resources every year. = 他们每年都重新分配资源。
We redivide the tasks based on individual strengths. = 我们根据个人的优势重新分配任务。

一般过去时:
The government redivided the electoral districts. = 政府重新划分了选举区。
She redivided her time to focus on her studies. = 她重新分配了时间，专注于学习。

现在完成时:
They have redivided the profits among the shareholders. = 他们已经将利润重新分配给了股东。
We have redivided the responsibilities since the merger. = 自从合并以来，我们已经重新分配了责任。

过去完成时:
They had redivided the inheritance before the will was read. = 遗嘱宣读之前，他们已经重新分配了遗产。
She had redivided her portfolio before the market crash. = 市场崩盘之前，她已经重新调整了投资组合。


将来时:
The council will redivide the budget for next year. = 理事会将重新分配明年的预算。
He will redivide his property before he dies. = 他会在死前重新分配他的财产。

虚拟语气:
If we redivided the workload, it would be fairer. = 如果我们重新分配工作量，就会更公平。
I wish they had redivided the funds more equitably. = 我希望他们能更公平地重新分配资金。

被动语态:
The land was redivided by the local authority. = 土地被地方当局重新划分了。
The tasks were redivided to improve efficiency. = 任务被重新分配以提高效率。

疑问句:

否定句:
