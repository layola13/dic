repossessing: /ˌriːpəˈzɛsɪŋ/ | v. | 收回；收回（财产） | The act of taking back something, especially property, that has been pledged as security for a loan or that has been purchased on credit and payments have not been made.  | Example: The bank is repossessing his car because he hasn't made his payments.  银行正在收回他的汽车，因为他没有付款。  | 近义词：reclaiming, recovering, seizing | 反义词：releasing, surrendering


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bank repossesses cars every month. = 银行每个月都收回汽车。
He is repossessing the house due to unpaid mortgage. = 他因为未付抵押贷款而收回房屋。

一般过去时:
They repossessed the property last week. = 他们上周收回该房产。
The lender repossessed the vehicle after several missed payments. = 贷款人因为多次未付款而收回车辆。

现在完成时:
The company has repossessed over 100 vehicles this year. = 今年该公司已收回100多辆车。
She has repossessed her belongings from her ex-husband. = 她已从前夫那里取回了她的财物。

过去完成时:
The bank had already repossessed the house before he could pay. = 他还没来得及付款，银行就已经收回房子了。
They had repossessed several items before the auction. = 拍卖前他们已经收回了几件物品。

将来时:
The landlord will repossess the apartment if the rent is not paid. = 如果不付房租，房东将收回公寓。
They will repossess the equipment if the contract is breached. = 如果违反合同，他们将收回设备。

虚拟语气:
If he paid his debts, they wouldn't be repossessing his car. = 如果他偿还债务，他们就不会收回他的汽车。
If I had money, I wouldn't be repossessing my belongings. = 如果我有钱，我就不会收回我的财物。

被动语态:
His car was repossessed last month. = 他的车在上个月被收回了。
The property is being repossessed due to non-payment. = 该房产因未付款而正在被收回。

疑问句:
Will the bank repossess my house? = 银行会收回我的房子吗？
Is the company repossessing the equipment? = 公司正在收回设备吗？

否定句:
The bank did not repossess the car. = 银行没有收回汽车。
They are not repossessing the property at this time. = 他们目前没有收回该房产。
