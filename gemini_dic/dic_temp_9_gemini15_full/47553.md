deauthorizations:/ˌdiːˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |取消授权；撤销授权|The act of removing or revoking someone's authority or authorization.  It often involves formally withdrawing permission or power previously granted.  This can apply to various contexts, from individuals losing access to systems to organizations losing licenses or permits.|例句：The company announced deauthorizations for several of its outdated software programs. 公司宣布取消授权几款过时的软件程序。|例句：The government's deauthorizations of several mining projects led to widespread protests. 政府取消了几项采矿项目的授权，导致了大规模抗议。|近义词：revocations, withdrawals, cancellations|反义词：authorizations, empowerments|名词
