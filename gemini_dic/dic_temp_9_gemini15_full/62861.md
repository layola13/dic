enjoyingly:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪɪŋli/| adv. |愉快地，尽情地 |In a way that shows enjoyment or pleasure.  It describes how an action is performed. |She sang enjoyingly.|happily, delightfully, pleasurably|unhappily, miserably, dismally|副词


USAGE->

He works enjoyingly. = 他愉快地工作。
