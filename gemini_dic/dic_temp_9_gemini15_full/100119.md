interpreted:/ɪnˈtɜːrprɪtɪd/| v. adj.|解释的；口译的|To explain the meaning of something; to translate spoken words from one language into another.|例句：The teacher interpreted the poem for the students. 老师为学生们解释了这首诗。|例句：The conference had simultaneous interpretation in several languages.  会议有几种语言的同声传译。|近义词：explained, translated, rendered|反义词：misunderstood, misinterpreted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He interprets the data carefully. = 他仔细地解释数据。
She interprets the law differently. = 她对法律的解释不同。


一般过去时:
The translator interpreted the speech flawlessly. = 翻译员完美地翻译了演讲。
He interpreted the silence as agreement. = 他把沉默理解为同意。


现在完成时:
They have interpreted the results of the study. = 他们已经解释了这项研究的结果。
She has interpreted this role many times. = 她已经多次诠释这个角色。


过去完成时:
He had interpreted the message incorrectly. = 他已经错误地解读了信息。
They had interpreted the map before setting out. = 他们出发前已经解读了地图。


将来时:
She will interpret the poem at the recital. = 她将在朗诵会上解释这首诗。
He will interpret the results for the board. = 他将向董事会解释结果。


虚拟语气:
If he interpreted the contract differently, the outcome would have been different. = 如果他对合同的解释不同，结果就会不同。
I wish I had interpreted his intentions correctly. = 我希望我正确地理解了他的意图。


被动语态:
The poem was interpreted in many different ways. = 这首诗以许多不同的方式被解释。
The data will be interpreted by the research team. = 数据将由研究团队进行解释。


疑问句:
How did you interpret that statement? = 你如何解释那句话？
How will the results be interpreted? = 结果将如何解释？


否定句:
He didn't interpret the signal correctly. = 他没有正确地解释信号。
She didn't interpret the clues correctly. = 她没有正确解读线索。
