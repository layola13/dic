gastrocolic:/ˌɡæstrəˈkɒlɪk/ | adj. |胃结肠的；胃结肠反射的 | Relating to both the stomach and the colon.  The term often refers to the gastrocolic reflex, a physiological reflex that involves increased motility in the colon in response to the presence of food in the stomach. | Example:  The gastrocolic reflex is responsible for the urge to defecate after eating. 胃结肠反射是进食后产生便意的原因。| 近义词:  colo-gastric (less common) | 反义词: None specifically.
