circlets:/ˈsɜːklɪts/| n.|小环，小圈，环状物|A circlet is a small circle or ring, often made of precious metal and worn as jewelry.  It can also refer to a ring-shaped object or arrangement.|例句：She wore a circlet of flowers in her hair. 她头上戴着花环。

例句：The artist created a circlet of light in his painting.  艺术家在他的画中创造了一个光环。
