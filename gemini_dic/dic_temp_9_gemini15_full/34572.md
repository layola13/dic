clearer:/ˈklɪərər/| adj.|更清晰的，更明白的，更清楚的 | More clear; easier to understand or see.  |例句：The explanation became clearer after the teacher drew a diagram.  老师画了个图表后，解释就更清楚了。
例句：The picture is clearer with the new lens. 用新的镜头，照片更清晰了。|近义词： clearer, brighter, more distinct, more lucid |反义词：blurred, unclear, vague, ambiguous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The instructions are clearer now. = 说明现在更清晰了。
The water is clearer in this part of the lake. = 湖的这个部分水更清澈。

一般过去时:
The audio became clearer after I adjusted the settings. = 我调整了设置后，音频变得更清晰了。
Her vision was clearer after the surgery. = 手术后她的视力更清晰了。

现在完成时:
Things have become clearer since I spoke to him. = 自从我和他谈话后，事情变得更清楚了。
The situation has cleared up and is clearer now. = 局势已经明朗，现在更清晰了。

过去完成时:
The evidence had become clearer by the time the trial began. = 到审判开始时，证据已经更加清晰了。
After further investigation, the motive had become clearer. = 经过进一步调查，动机已经更加清晰了。

将来时:
The image will be clearer with a higher resolution. = 用更高的分辨率，图像会更清晰。
Hopefully, the situation will become clearer tomorrow. = 希望明天情况会更明朗。


虚拟语气:
If the instructions were clearer, I wouldn't have made so many mistakes. = 如果说明更清晰，我不会犯这么多错误。
It would be clearer if you provided more details. = 如果你提供更多细节，会更清晰。

被动语态:
The problem was made clearer by the professor's explanation. = 教授的解释使问题更清晰了。
The details are being made clearer through further research. = 通过进一步的研究，细节正在变得更清晰。

疑问句:
Is the recording clearer now? = 录音现在清晰了吗？
Is this explanation clearer to you? = 这个解释对你来说更清晰了吗？

否定句:
The message wasn't clearer, it was more confusing. = 信息并没有更清晰，反而更令人困惑。
The picture isn't clearer even after zooming in. = 即使放大后，图片也没有更清晰。
