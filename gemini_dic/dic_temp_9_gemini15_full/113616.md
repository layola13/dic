macheted:/məˈʃɛtɪd/| v.|用大砍刀砍；用大砍刀砍杀|Past participle of "machete".  Describes something that has been cut or attacked with a machete.  It implies a forceful and often violent action.|例句：The path was macheted through the dense jungle.  这条路是用大砍刀砍出来的，穿过茂密的丛林。

The field had been macheted to clear the weeds. 这块地已经被用大砍刀清理杂草了。|近义词：cut, hacked, slashed|反义词：built, constructed
