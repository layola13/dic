precipitous: /prɪˈsɪpɪtəs/ | adj. | 陡峭的；急剧的；仓促的 |  characterized by or proceeding with extreme speed or abruptness; dangerously high or steep.  | 例句：The hikers faced a precipitous climb up the mountain. 登山者面临着陡峭的山路攀登。

The company made a precipitous decision to lay off half its workforce.  这家公司仓促决定裁员一半。


近义词：sheer, abrupt, steep, sudden, hasty, rash | 反义词：gradual, gentle, slow, deliberate

词源:
