choo-choos:/ˈtʃuːtʃuːz/| n.|火车（尤指儿童用语）|A childish term for a train, often used playfully or affectionately.|例句：The little boy loved to play with his toy choo-choos.  小男孩喜欢玩他的玩具火车。|例句：  She excitedly pointed at the passing choo-choos. 她兴奋地指着经过的火车。|近义词：train, locomotive|反义词：无
