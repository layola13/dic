horsemen:/ˈhɔːrsmən/| n.|骑手；骑士；马夫|A horseman is a person who rides a horse, especially one skilled in riding.  The word often evokes images of knights or skilled riders.|例句：The horseman rode swiftly across the plains.  这位骑手迅速地穿过平原。|例句：He was a skilled horseman, known for his daring feats. 他是一位技艺高超的骑手，以其大胆的技艺而闻名。|近义词：rider, cavalier, jockey|反义词：pedestrian
