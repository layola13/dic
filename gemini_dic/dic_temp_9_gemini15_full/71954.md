flighted:/ˈflaɪtɪd/| adj.|有翼的；有航班的；分阶段进行的 | Having wings; relating to or involving flights; done in stages or phases. |例句：A flighted arrow soared through the air.  一支带翼的箭飞上了天空。

例句：The project is flighted over several months.  这个项目分几个阶段进行。


近义词：winged (有翼的), phased (分阶段的), staged (分阶段的)
反义词：wingless (无翼的), unphased (不分阶段的), continuous (持续不断的)
词性：形容词
