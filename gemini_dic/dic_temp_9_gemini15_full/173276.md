quasi-contented:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ kənˈtɛntɪd/| adj.| 几乎满足的，似满足的 |  Feeling almost but not completely content; a state of partial satisfaction. It implies a lingering sense of incompleteness or dissatisfaction despite a general feeling of contentment. | Example:  He felt quasi-contented with his life, but a nagging feeling of unfulfillment persisted. 他对自己的生活感到几乎满足，但一种挥之不去的失落感依然存在。|近义词：partly satisfied, somewhat content, mildly pleased | 反义词：completely dissatisfied, utterly discontent, profoundly unhappy
