paltrinesses:/ˈpɔːltrɪnɪsɪz/| n.| 微不足道的事物；琐碎；少量 |The plural of paltriness, referring to instances of being paltry (small, insignificant, worthless).  It emphasizes the multiplicity of such insignificant things. |例句：The paltrinesses of his excuses only served to further irritate her. 他那些微不足道的借口只会让她更生气。

例句：His arguments were reduced to a series of paltrinesses, lacking any real substance. 他的论点沦为一系列微不足道的东西，缺乏任何实际内容。


近义词：trivialities, insignificances, trivialities, minutiae, pettinesses

反义词：importances, significances, magnitudes


词性：名词 (plural)


词源：源自中古英语 *paltreie，意为“破布；垃圾”。最终源自古法语 *paltroi，“破布”。
