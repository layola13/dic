hippuric:/hɪˈpjʊərɪk/| n.|马尿酸的；马尿酸|Hippuric acid is an organic compound that is found in the urine of mammals, particularly herbivores. It is formed in the liver from benzoic acid and glycine.|The presence of hippuric acid in urine can be an indicator of exposure to certain chemicals.|近义词：benzoic acid derivative|反义词：无
