picrite:/ˈpɪkraɪt/| n. |辉绿岩|A dark-colored, mafic igneous rock, typically rich in olivine and pyroxene, and relatively poor in quartz.  It is often found in intrusive settings as dikes and sills. |Example: The geologist identified the sample as a picrite porphyry due to its distinctive mineral composition and texture.  地质学家根据其独特的矿物成分和结构，将样品鉴定为辉绿岩斑岩。|近义词：basalt, gabbro|反义词：rhyolite, granite|名词
