prescription: /prɪˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. |处方，药方；规定，指示 | A prescription is a written order from a doctor for a medicine.  It details the medication, dosage, and instructions for use.  It can also refer to a rule or guideline. |例句：The doctor wrote me a prescription for antibiotics. 医生给我开了抗生素的处方。|例句：The company followed the prescription for success outlined in the business plan. 公司遵循了商业计划中概述的成功方案。|近义词：formula, recipe, order, instruction |反义词：prohibition, ban


USAGE->

The doctor writes a prescription for the patient. = 医生为病人开处方。
He usually follows the doctor's prescription. = 他通常遵循医生的处方。
