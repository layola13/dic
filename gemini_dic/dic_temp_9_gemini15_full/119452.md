micromanagement:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌmænɪdʒmənt/ | n. | 微观管理，事无巨细的管理 | The act of managing people or processes in excessive detail or with excessive control.  This often stifles creativity and autonomy. | Example:  The CEO's micromanagement created a stressful work environment.  首席执行官的微观管理造成了一个压力巨大的工作环境。


Example: His micromanagement of the project stifled creativity and slowed down progress. 他对项目的微观管理扼杀了创造力并减缓了进度。


近义词: close supervision, overmanagement, overcontrol, excessive control


反义词: delegation, empowerment, autonomy, laissez-faire management


词性: 名词
