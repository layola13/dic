crosswinds:/ˈkrɒswɪndz/ | n. |侧风，横风 | Winds blowing from the side, across the direction of travel.  Crosswinds can make flying, sailing, or driving more difficult. | Example: The pilot had to adjust the plane's course to compensate for the strong crosswinds.  飞行员不得不调整飞机的航向以补偿强烈的侧风。

The strong crosswinds made it difficult to steer the boat. 强烈的侧风使驾驶船只变得困难。|近义词：side winds, lateral winds |反义词：headwinds, tailwinds
