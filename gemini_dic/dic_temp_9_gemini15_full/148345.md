overspecializes:/ˌoʊvərˈspeʃəlaɪzɪz/ | v. |过度专门化；过于专门化 | To specialize to an excessive degree, often to the detriment of a broader understanding or skill set.  This verb implies that the specialization is harmful or unproductive due to its narrow focus. |例句：The company overspecializes in a niche market, making it vulnerable to changes in consumer demand. 公司在一个利基市场过度专门化，使其容易受到消费者需求变化的影响。

The education system overspecializes students, limiting their career options later in life. 教育体系使学生过度专业化，限制了他们以后的职业选择。


近义词：hyper-specializes, over-focuses |反义词：generalizes, diversifies
