decussate:/dɪˈkʌseɪt/| v.|交叉；十字交叉|To intersect or cross in an X shape.  The term often refers to anatomical structures or lines.|例句：The optic nerves decussate at the optic chiasm.  视神经在视交叉处交叉。

例句：The branches of the plant decussated, creating a dense network. 植物的枝条交叉，形成一个密集的网络。


近义词：intersect, cross, interlace|反义词：parallel, run alongside


词性:动词
