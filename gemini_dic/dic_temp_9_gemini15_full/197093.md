shames:/ʃeɪmz/| n. v.|羞愧；使蒙羞|The feeling of being ashamed and disgraced; to cause someone to feel ashamed or disgraced.|例句：His actions brought shame on his family.  他的行为给他的家人带来了耻辱。|例句：Don't shame yourself by cheating. 不要因为作弊而羞愧自己。|近义词：dishonor, disgrace, humiliation|反义词：pride, honor, glory|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shames his family with his behavior. = 他用自己的行为使他的家庭蒙羞。
Shame often accompanies guilt. = 羞愧常常伴随着内疚。


一般过去时:
His lies shamed his parents. = 他的谎话使他的父母蒙羞。
The scandal shamed the entire organization. = 这场丑闻使整个组织蒙羞。


现在完成时:
His actions have shamed him deeply. = 他的行为使他深感羞愧。
She has shamed her country with her actions. = 她用自己的行为使她的国家蒙羞。


过去完成时:
He had shamed himself before he even began to speak. = 他还没开始说话就已经羞愧难当了。
They had shamed their ancestors with their poor choices. = 他们用自己糟糕的选择玷污了祖先的名誉。


将来时:
His betrayal will shame him forever. = 他的背叛将永远使他蒙羞。
This decision will shame the company. = 这个决定将使公司蒙羞。


虚拟语气:
If he knew the consequences, he wouldn't shame himself like this. = 如果他知道后果，他不会这样让自己蒙羞。
I wish I hadn't shamed my friends yesterday. = 我真希望昨天我没有让我的朋友们蒙羞。


被动语态:
He was shamed by his own incompetence. = 他因为自己的无能而蒙羞。
The company was shamed by the revelation of the scandal. = 公司因丑闻的曝光而蒙羞。


疑问句:
Does his behavior shame him? = 他的行为让他感到羞愧吗？
Will this decision shame the company? = 这个决定会让公司蒙羞吗？


否定句:
His actions didn't shame him at all. = 他的行为并没有让他感到羞愧。
She wouldn't be shamed by her past mistakes. = 她不会因为过去的错误而感到羞愧。


PHRASE->
put to shame: 使相形见绌
feel shame: 感到羞愧
bring shame on: 给…带来耻辱
a source of shame: 羞耻的根源
a shameful act: 可耻的行为

词源:
The word "shame" comes from Old English *scamu, meaning "shame, disgrace, dishonor," and ultimately from Proto-Germanic *skamuz.  The verb "to shame" developed later.
