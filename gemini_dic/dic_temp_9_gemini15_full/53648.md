discharged:/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒd/| v. adj. n.| 卸下；释放；解雇；排出；出院 | to relieve from a duty, obligation, or responsibility; to release from confinement or service; to dismiss from employment; to expel or emit; to leave a hospital after treatment |例句：The soldiers were discharged from the army after their service. 士兵服役结束后被退伍。The doctor discharged the patient from the hospital. 医生让病人出院了。|近义词：release, dismiss, fire, expel, emit |反义词：employ, retain, confine, admit
