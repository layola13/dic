mountaineering: /ˌmaʊnˈtɪnɪərɪŋ/ | n. |登山运动；登山术 |The activity of climbing mountains.  It involves physical endurance, technical skill, and planning. Mountaineering can range from easy hikes to extremely challenging expeditions on high-altitude peaks. |例句：He has a passion for mountaineering and has climbed many of the world's highest mountains. 他热爱登山运动，已经攀登过世界上许多最高的山峰。

例句：Mountaineering requires specialized equipment, including ropes, ice axes, and crampons. 登山运动需要专门的设备，包括绳索、冰镐和冰爪。


近义词：mountain climbing, alpinism | 反义词：None (Antonyms are not directly applicable) | 词性: noun
