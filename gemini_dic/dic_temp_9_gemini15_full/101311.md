inweave:/ɪnˈwiːv/ | vt. | 编织；交织；融入 | To interweave; to blend or combine closely.  |例句：She inwove strands of gold into the fabric. 她将金线编织到织物中。

例句：The artist inwove various symbols into the painting.  艺术家将各种象征融入绘画中。


近义词：interweave, intertwine, interlace, blend, combine | 反义词：separate, disentangle


词性：动词
