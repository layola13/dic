perbromide:/ˈpɜːrbroʊmaɪd/| n.|过溴化物|A perbromide is a chemical compound containing the tribromide ion (Br₃⁻) or a similar polybromide anion.  These compounds are often formed by the reaction of bromine with a bromide salt. They are typically dark-colored and relatively unstable.|Example:  The reaction of bromine with potassium bromide can produce potassium tribromide, a perbromide.  反应溴和溴化钾可以生成三溴化钾，一种过溴化物。|近义词:polybromide|反义词:None specifically, but compounds without polybromide anions could be considered antonyms in a chemical context.
