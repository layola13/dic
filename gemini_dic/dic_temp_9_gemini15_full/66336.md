exculpable:/ɪkˈskʌlpəbl/| adj.| 可原谅的；无罪的 | excusable; justifiable | The judge found the defendant's actions exculpable under the circumstances.  法官认为被告在那种情况下行为是可以原谅的。|近义词：justifiable, pardonable, forgivable |反义词：blameworthy, culpable, guilty
