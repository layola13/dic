slack:/slæk/| n., v., adj.|松弛的；懈怠；忽略；松懈；闲聊|

英文解析:
As a noun, "slack" refers to a period of inactivity or a lack of tension.  As a verb, it means to neglect one's duties or to become less taut. As an adjective, it describes something loose, relaxed, or not taut.

例句:
There's been a lot of slack in the office since the holidays.  假期过后办公室里松懈了很多。

The rope went slack and the bucket fell to the ground. 绳子松了，桶掉到了地上。


近义词:
(n.) negligence, remissness, looseness, relaxation
(v.) neglect, ignore, shirk, relax
(adj.) loose, relaxed, careless, negligent


反义词:
(n.) tension, tightness, diligence, activity
(v.) work hard, attend to, tighten
(adj.) taut, tight, diligent, attentive


词性:
Noun, Verb, Adjective
