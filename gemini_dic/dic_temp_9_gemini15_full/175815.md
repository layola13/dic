raisin:/ˈreɪzən/| n.|葡萄干|A raisin is a dried grape.  Raisins are often used in baking, cereals, and other foods.  They are a good source of fiber and natural sugars.|例句：I added some raisins to my oatmeal for extra sweetness. 我在我的燕麦粥里加了一些葡萄干来增加甜味。

The cake contained plenty of raisins and nuts. 这个蛋糕里有很多葡萄干和坚果。|近义词：dried grape|反义词：fresh grape
