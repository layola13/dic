conceptible:/kənˈsɛptəbəl/ | adj. | 可想象的；可理解的；可构思的 | capable of being conceived or imagined; imaginable.  | The plan, while ambitious, is perfectly conceivable.  这个计划虽然雄心勃勃，但完全可以设想。  | imaginable, thinkable, conceivable, imaginable | inconceivable, unimaginable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The idea is conceivable. = 这个想法是可以想象的。
This outcome is conceivable. = 这种结果是可想象的。

一般过去时:
The solution was conceivable. = 这个方案是可以想象的。
The plan was conceivable at the time. = 当时这个计划是可以想象的。

现在完成时:
Many scenarios have been conceivable. = 许多情况都是可以想象的。
A peaceful resolution has been conceivable all along. = 一直以来和平解决都是可以想象的。

过去完成时:
It had been conceivable that he would resign. = 他辞职是可以想象的。

将来时:
The project will be conceivable in the future. = 未来这个项目是可以想象的。
New technologies will make many things conceivable. = 新技术将使许多事情成为可能。

虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:
