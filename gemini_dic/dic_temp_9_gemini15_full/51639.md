deviability:/ˌdiːviəˈbɪləti/| n. |可偏离性，可变性，偏差度|The degree to which something can deviate from a standard or norm.  It often refers to the extent to which a system, process, or behavior can depart from its expected or intended path.  The concept is relevant in various fields, including statistics, engineering, and social sciences.|例句：The deviability of the experimental results raised concerns about the reliability of the methodology.  实验结果的可偏离性引发了对方法可靠性的担忧。|近义词：variability, deviation, deflection, divergence|反义词：constancy, invariability, inflexibility|名词
