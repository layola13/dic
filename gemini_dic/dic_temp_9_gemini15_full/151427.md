parodies:/ˈpærədiz/ | n. |模仿作品，滑稽模仿 |A parody is a humorous imitation of a serious work, often using exaggeration or distortion to create a comedic effect. It can target a specific work or a general style or genre.  Parodies often comment on or critique the original work, but can also simply be for entertainment. |例句：The movie is a hilarious parody of superhero films. 这部电影是对超级英雄电影的滑稽模仿。 |例句：His stand-up routine included several parodies of famous politicians. 他的脱口秀包括几个著名政治家的模仿表演。|近义词：satire, caricature, lampoon, spoof |反义词：original, authentic
