fertility:/fɜːˈtɪləti/ | /fɚˈtɪləti/ | n. | 生育能力；肥沃；多产 | The ability to produce offspring; the state of being fertile.  It can refer to the ability of land to produce abundant crops, or the capacity of a person or animal to reproduce. | 例句：The fertility of the soil is crucial for a good harvest.  土壤的肥沃程度对获得好收成至关重要。

The couple underwent fertility treatments to help them conceive. 这对夫妇接受了生育治疗以帮助他们受孕。

近义词：fecundity, fruitfulness, productiveness | 反义词：sterility, barrenness, infertility
