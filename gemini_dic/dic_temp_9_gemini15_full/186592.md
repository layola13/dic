ridiculously:/rɪˈdɪkjʊləsli/ | adv. | 荒谬地；可笑地；极其 |  The adverb "ridiculously" means in a way that is extremely silly or unreasonable. It emphasizes the degree to which something is absurd or laughable. |例句：He was ridiculously dressed for the occasion. 他穿着打扮极其不适合这个场合。

例句：The price of that car is ridiculously high. 那辆车的价格高得离谱。


近义词：absurdly, laughably, incredibly, unbelievably, excessively

反义词：reasonably, sensibly, logically


词源:

The word "ridiculously" derives from the adjective "ridiculous," which ultimately comes from the Latin word "ridiculus," meaning "laughable" or "worthy of laughter."  The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective into an adverb, indicating manner or degree.
