clefts:/klɛfts/| n.|裂缝，裂口|A cleft is a split, crack, or fissure in something.  It often refers to a natural opening or division, such as a rock formation or a part of the body.|例句：The earthquake caused deep clefts in the earth. 地震在地面上造成了深深的裂缝。  The baby was born with a cleft palate.  婴儿出生时患有腭裂。|近义词：crack, fissure, split, gap |反义词：union, whole, intact
