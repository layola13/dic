energises: /ˈɛnərdʒaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 使充满活力；使精力充沛；激励 | To give energy to; invigorate; stimulate.  | 例句：The music energises the crowd. = 音乐使人群充满活力。  The good news energised her. = 好消息使她精神振奋。 | 近义词：invigorate, stimulate, revitalize, animate | 反义词：debilitate, exhaust, drain, enervate | 动词
