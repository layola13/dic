pithecanthropus: /ˌpɪθɪˈkænθrəpəs/ | n. | 人猿 |  Extinct genus of hominids from the Pleistocene epoch, known from fossil remains found in Java and China.  It was once considered a direct ancestor of modern humans, but this view is now largely outdated.  The genus is now considered to be part of the Homo genus. | Example:  The discovery of Pithecanthropus erectus fossils revolutionized our understanding of human evolution. = 爪哇猿人的化石发现彻底改变了我们对人类进化的理解。  | 近义词:  Homo erectus (in modern classification) | 反义词:  None (as it refers to a specific extinct genus) |
