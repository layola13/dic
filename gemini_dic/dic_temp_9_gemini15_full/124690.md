movies:/ˈmuːviz/| n.|电影；影片|Motion pictures; films.  Movies are a popular form of entertainment.|例句：I love watching action movies. 我喜欢看动作电影。

例句：We went to the cinema to see the new movies. 我们去电影院看新电影了。


近义词：films, motion pictures, pictures

反义词：None (no direct antonym)


词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I watch movies every weekend. = 我每个周末都看电影。
He enjoys watching romantic movies. = 他喜欢看浪漫电影。


一般过去时:
We watched a horror movie last night. = 我们昨晚看了一部恐怖电影。
She saw three movies at the festival. = 她在这个电影节上看了三部电影。


现在完成时:
I have seen that movie twice. = 我看过那部电影两次了。
They have watched many movies this month. = 这个月他们看了很多电影。


过去完成时:
I had already seen the movie before you told me about it. = 在你告诉我之前我已经看过那部电影了。
They had watched all the nominated movies before the awards ceremony. = 在颁奖典礼之前他们已经看过所有提名的电影了。


将来时:
I will watch that movie tomorrow. = 我明天会看那部电影。
We will be watching movies all weekend. = 我们整个周末都会看电影。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would watch more movies. = 如果我有更多时间，我会看更多电影。
I wish I could watch that movie again. = 我希望我能再看一遍那部电影。


被动语态:
The movie was directed by a famous director. = 这部电影是由一位著名的导演执导的。
Many movies are shown at this cinema every day. = 这家电影院每天放映很多电影。


疑问句:
What movies are you watching? = 你在看什么电影？
Have you seen any good movies lately? = 你最近看过什么好电影吗？


否定句:
I don't like watching scary movies. = 我不喜欢看恐怖电影。
He hasn't seen that movie yet. = 他还没看过那部电影。
