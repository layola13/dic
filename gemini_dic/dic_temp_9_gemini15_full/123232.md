monocropping:/ˈmɒnəʊkrɒpɪŋ/|/ˈmɑːnəʊkrɑːpɪŋ/| n. |单一种植；单一作物种植|The agricultural practice of growing a single crop in a field, repeatedly, year after year.|例句：Monocropping leads to soil depletion and increased susceptibility to pests. 单一种植会导致土壤贫瘠，并更容易受到害虫侵害。|例句：The farmer abandoned monocropping in favor of crop rotation.  这位农民放弃了单一种植，转而采用轮作。|近义词：single cropping, sole cropping|反义词：crop rotation, polyculture
