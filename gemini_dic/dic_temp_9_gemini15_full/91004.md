husbanded:/ˈhʌzbəndɪd/| v. |节俭地使用；精打细算地管理；培养；悉心照料 |to manage or use something carefully and economically; to cultivate or care for something diligently. |例句：She carefully husbanded her resources. 她精打细算地使用她的资源。|例句：The farmer husbanded his crops throughout the growing season.  这位农民在整个生长季节都悉心照料他的庄稼。|近义词：conserved, managed, cultivated, nurtured |反义词：wasted, squandered, neglected
