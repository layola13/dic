词源:
The word "nitrophenol" is a combination of "nitro-" (from nitrogen dioxide, indicating the presence of a nitro group -NO2) and "phenol" (a hydroxyl-substituted benzene ring).  Therefore, it literally means a phenol with a nitro group attached.
