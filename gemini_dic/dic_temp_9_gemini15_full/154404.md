/pɜːmiənz/| n.|二叠纪|The Permian Period is a geologic period and system that spans 47 million years from the end of the Carboniferous Period 298.9 million years ago to the beginning of the Triassic Period 251.9 million years ago.|The Permian Period saw the rise of reptiles and the extinction of many marine species. 二叠纪见证了爬行动物的兴起和许多海洋物种的灭绝。|Carboniferous, Triassic|Jurassic, Cretaceous|名词
