dozier:/ˈdoʊzər/| n. |瞌睡虫，懒惰的人|A dozier is a person who is habitually drowsy or sleepy; someone who is lazy or sluggish.  The word often carries a playful or affectionate connotation.|He's such a dozier; he's always falling asleep during meetings. 他真是个瞌睡虫，总是在会议上睡着。|近义词：sleepyhead, sluggard, laggard|反义词：energetic, active, diligent
