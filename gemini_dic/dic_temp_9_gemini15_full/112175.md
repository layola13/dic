looking-back:/ˈlʊkɪŋbæk/| v. |回顾，追忆|Looking back, I realize I made the right decision. 回顾过去，我意识到我做出了正确的决定。|近义词：reviewing, reminiscing, reflecting|反义词：looking forward, anticipating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am looking back on my childhood. = 我正在回顾我的童年。
We are looking back at the events of last year. = 我们正在回顾去年的事件。


一般过去时:
She looked back at her life with regret. = 她带着遗憾回顾自己的一生。
They looked back on their journey with fondness. = 他们怀着喜爱之情回顾他们的旅程。


现在完成时:
I have been looking back at old photographs. = 我一直在翻看旧照片。
He has looked back on his career with satisfaction. = 他满意地回顾了自己的职业生涯。


过去完成时:
Having looked back at the data, she changed her mind. = 回顾了数据之后，她改变了主意。
Before making the decision, they had looked back at previous projects. = 在做决定之前，他们回顾了之前的项目。


将来时:
I will look back on this day with happiness. = 我将快乐地回忆这一天。
We will look back and see how far we've come. = 我们将回顾过去，看看我们走了多远。


虚拟语气:
If I looked back, I would see many mistakes. = 如果我回顾过去，我会看到许多错误。
I wish I had looked back before making that decision. = 我真希望在做那个决定之前能回顾一下。


被动语态:  (被动语态在"looking back"这个词组中使用较少，通常用主动语态)
The past year's events are being looked back on with mixed emotions. = 过去一年的事件正被人们以复杂的心情回顾着。
The project's success will be looked back on with pride. = 这个项目的成功将会被人们自豪地回忆。


疑问句:
Are you looking back on your time in college? = 你在回顾你大学时光吗？
What are we looking back at today? = 我们今天在回顾什么？


否定句:
I'm not looking back at the past with anger. = 我并没有带着愤怒回顾过去。
They didn't look back at their mistakes. = 他们没有回顾他们的错误。


PHRASE->
look back on sth. = 回顾某事
look back to sth. = 回顾到某事
look back at sth. = 回顾某事
look back with sth. = 以某种方式回顾
looking back to the past = 回顾过去
