prance:/præns/| v.| 腾跃，欢快地小跑 |To move with high steps, especially in a lively or proud way.  It often implies a light and playful movement. |例句：The horse pranced proudly at the head of the parade.  这匹马在游行队伍的最前面得意地腾跃着。

例句：The children pranced around the room, full of energy. 孩子们在房间里充满活力地蹦蹦跳跳。


近义词：caper, skip, frolic, prance about


反义词：trudge, lumber, plod


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prances around the stage. = 他在舞台上蹦跳。
The foal prances in the pasture. = 小马驹在牧场上蹦跳。


一般过去时:
She pranced happily down the street. = 她高兴地在街上蹦蹦跳跳。
The dancer pranced gracefully across the floor. = 舞蹈演员优雅地在舞池上跳跃。


现在完成时:
The horse has pranced through the field. = 马儿已经欢快地跑过田野了。
The children have pranced all afternoon. = 孩子们整个下午都在蹦蹦跳跳。


过去完成时:
He had pranced across the stage before the lights dimmed. = 灯光变暗之前，他曾在舞台上跳跃。
The ballerina had pranced effortlessly before the curtain fell. = 幕布落下之前，芭蕾舞演员轻松自如地跳跃。


将来时:
The pony will prance when it sees its owner. = 小马看到它的主人就会蹦跳。
She will prance confidently during her performance. = 她在表演中将会自信地跳跃。


虚拟语气:
If I could prance, I would join the parade. = 如果我能跳跃，我会参加游行的。
I wish I could prance as gracefully as she does. = 我希望我能像她一样优雅地跳跃。


被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
The horse was pranced around by the trainer.  (这匹马被驯马师牵着蹦跳)(比较生硬)
The toy pony can be pranced by a child. (玩具小马可以被孩子玩着蹦跳)


疑问句:
Does the horse prance easily? = 这匹马容易腾跃吗？
Will the children prance at the party? = 孩子们会在派对上蹦跳吗？


否定句:
The tired horse did not prance. = 疲惫的马没有腾跃。
He didn't prance, he just walked. = 他没有蹦跳，只是走着。



PHRASE->
prance about: 蹦蹦跳跳
prance around: 绕着...蹦跳
prance with joy: 欣喜若狂地跳跃
prance along: 欢快地走
prance on: 继续蹦跳


词源:
The word "prance" ultimately derives from the Old French word "brancer," meaning "to brandish" or "to wave."  Over time, the meaning shifted to the lively, energetic movement associated with a horse's proud display.
