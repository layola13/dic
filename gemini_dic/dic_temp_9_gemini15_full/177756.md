recasting:/ˌriːˈkæstɪŋ/| n. |重铸；改写；重新塑造 |The act of reshaping or reforming something, especially by changing its structure or presentation.  It often involves changing the wording, structure, or format of something to improve clarity, style, or effectiveness.  This can apply to text, ideas, plans, or even metal. |例句：The director decided on a complete recasting of the play's final scene.  导演决定彻底改写该剧的结尾场景。

The editor suggested a recasting of the article to make it more engaging for readers. 编辑建议改写这篇文章，使其更吸引读者。


近义词：reformulation, restructuring, revision, remake, refashioning |反义词：original version,  unchanged form |名词
