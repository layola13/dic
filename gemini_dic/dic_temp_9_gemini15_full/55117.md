disrupting:/dɪsˈrʌptɪŋ/ | v. |扰乱；中断；破坏 |To interrupt (a system, process, or event) so as to prevent its normal operation or progress.  |例句：The protest disrupted traffic in the city center.  抗议活动扰乱了市中心的交通。

例句：The unexpected snowstorm disrupted air travel for days.  突如其来的暴风雪扰乱了多日的航空旅行。


近义词：interrupting, interfering, hindering, upsetting, deranging | 反义词：organizing, facilitating, smoothing, improving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The constant noise is disrupting my concentration. = 持续的噪音正在扰乱我的注意力。
They are disrupting the meeting with their loud talking. = 他们大声说话扰乱了会议。


一般过去时:
The power outage disrupted the factory's production. = 停电打断了工厂的生产。
The strike disrupted the supply chain. = 罢工扰乱了供应链。


现在完成时:
The pandemic has disrupted global supply chains. = 大流行已经扰乱了全球供应链。
Technological advancements have disrupted many traditional industries. = 技术进步已经扰乱了许多传统产业。


过去完成时:
The heavy rain had disrupted the train schedule before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，大雨已经扰乱了火车时刻表。
The previous management had disrupted the company's finances. = 之前的管理层扰乱了公司的财务状况。


将来时:
The planned construction will disrupt traffic for several months. = 计划中的建设将扰乱几个月的交通。
The upcoming storm is expected to disrupt power supplies. = 即将来临的风暴预计会扰乱电力供应。


虚拟语气:
If they disrupted the system again, they would face serious consequences. = 如果他们再次扰乱系统，他们将面临严重后果。
I wish they hadn't disrupted the presentation. = 我真希望他们没有扰乱演示。


被动语态:
The meeting was disrupted by a sudden power cut. = 会议由于突然停电而被打断。
The project is being disrupted by unforeseen delays. = 项目因不可预见延误而受到干扰。


疑问句:
Is the construction work disrupting the neighborhood? = 建设工程是否扰乱了邻里生活？
What factors are disrupting the market? = 哪些因素正在扰乱市场？


否定句:
The new system did not disrupt the workflow. = 新系统没有扰乱工作流程。
The company's efforts to prevent disruption were successful. = 公司防止中断的努力取得了成功。


PHRASE->
disrupt the peace = 扰乱治安
disrupt the market = 扰乱市场
disrupt the ecosystem = 破坏生态系统
disrupt the order = 打乱秩序
disrupt the balance = 打破平衡

词源:
disrupting 源自拉丁语 “disruptus”,  由 "dis-" (分开，否定) 和 "ruptus" (打破，裂开) 构成。  因此，其字面意思就是“打破，分开”。
