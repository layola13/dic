perigonium:/ˌpɛrɪˈɡoʊniəm/ | n. |花被,花萼和花冠的合称 | The perigonium is the collective term for the calyx and corolla of a flower, particularly in flowers where the calyx and corolla are not easily distinguishable.  It encloses and protects the reproductive organs. | Example: The perigonium of the lily is brightly colored and attracts pollinators.  百合的花被颜色鲜艳，吸引传粉者。 |近义词: perianth (more common term) | 反义词: None  | 词性:名词
