milkman:/ˈmɪlkˌmæn/ | n. |送牛奶的人| A person whose job is to deliver milk to homes.  |Example: The milkman comes every morning at 7 a.m. 送牛奶的人每天早上7点来。 The milkman used to leave the milk on the doorstep. 送牛奶的人过去常常把牛奶放在门口。|近义词：milk deliverer, dairyman |反义词：N/A
