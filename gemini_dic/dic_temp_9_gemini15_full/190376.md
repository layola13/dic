sargo:/ˈsɑːrɡoʊ/ | n. | 笛鯛；沙戈鱼 | A sargo is any of several species of marine fish belonging to the family Sparidae, characterized by their robust bodies, strong jaws, and often striking coloration. They are popular game fish and are also consumed as food. | Example:  The angler caught a large sargo near the reef.  钓者在礁石附近钓到一条大沙戈鱼。 | 近义词：porgy, sea bream | 反义词：  (无直接反义词，可考虑与淡水鱼对比)
