gerboa:/dʒərˈboʊə/| n. |跳鼠|A small desert rodent with long hind legs for jumping and a long tail.  Gerboas are nocturnal and live in burrows. They are found in Africa, Asia, and parts of Europe.|例句：The gerboa hopped across the sand with surprising speed.  这只跳鼠以惊人的速度跳过沙地。
The researchers observed the gerboa's unique burrowing techniques. 研究人员观察了跳鼠独特的挖洞技巧。|近义词：jerboa(同义词，有时被认为是拼写错误)|反义词:None.
