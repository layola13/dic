outwoven: /ˌaʊtˈwoʊvən/ | adj. | 编织出来的；织好的 | Woven or knitted so as to be visible or prominent on the surface.  It implies a thoroughness or complexity in the weaving process, resulting in a prominent or intricate design. | The intricately outwoven tapestry depicted scenes from mythology.  这幅错综复杂的挂毯描绘了神话故事的场景。 |  woven, knitted | unwoven (反义词，但并非完全反义，因为outwoven强调编织结果的可见性) | 形容词
