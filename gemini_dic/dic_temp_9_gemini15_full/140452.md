old:/oʊld/|adj.|老的，旧的，古老的|The word "old" describes something that has existed for a long time. It can refer to age, condition, or historical significance.  |例句：That's an old car. = 那是一辆旧车。
That building is very old. = 那座建筑物非常古老。|近义词：ancient, aged, elderly, antique|反义词：new, young, modern


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The house is old. = 这房子很旧。
This painting is old. = 这幅画很旧。


一般过去时:
The car was old. = 这辆车很旧。
The book was old. = 这本书很旧。


现在完成时:
The building has been old for centuries. = 这座建筑已经存在了几个世纪。
My grandfather has been old for many years. = 我的祖父已经老了很多年了。


过去完成时:
The car had been old even before I bought it. = 这辆车在我买它之前就已经很旧了。
The vase had been old for generations. = 这个花瓶已经流传了好几代了。


将来时:
The house will be old in a hundred years. = 这栋房子一百年后就会很旧了。
The technology will be old soon. = 这项技术很快就会过时了。


虚拟语气:
If the car weren't so old, I would drive it. = 如果这辆车不是那么旧，我会开它。
I wish this computer weren't so old. = 我希望这台电脑不要这么旧。


被动语态:
The old house is being renovated. = 这栋旧房子正在被翻新。
The old traditions are being forgotten. = 古老的传统正在被遗忘。


疑问句:
Is the car old? = 这辆车旧吗？
Is this painting old? = 这幅画旧吗？


否定句:
The car is not old. = 这辆车不旧。
The house is not very old. = 这房子不算很旧。
