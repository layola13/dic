provisoes:/prəˈvaɪzoʊz/ | n. | 附款；条件；限制 |  Plural of proviso.  Conditions or stipulations that must be met before an agreement or contract becomes effective.  They represent exceptions or qualifications to a main agreement.  | Example: The contract was signed with several important provisoes regarding payment and delivery. 这份合同签署时附带了关于付款和交付的几个重要条件。  |近义词：conditions, stipulations, qualifications, reservations | 反义词：unconditional terms, absolute terms | 名词
