remobilization: /ˌriːˌmoʊbɪləˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 重新动员；恢复活力 | The act of mobilizing again; the process of restoring something to activity or effectiveness.  It often refers to the redeployment of resources, personnel, or assets. | 例句：The remobilization of troops after the ceasefire was a complex undertaking.  战后停火后部队的重新动员是一项复杂的工作。

The remobilization of the economy after the pandemic required significant government intervention. 大流行后经济的复苏需要政府的大力干预。


近义词：reactivation, revitalization, reorganization | 反义词：demobilization, inactivation
