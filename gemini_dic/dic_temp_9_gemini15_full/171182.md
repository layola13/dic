psychologize:/saɪˈkɒlədʒaɪz/| vt.|心理分析；从心理学角度解释|To interpret or explain (something, especially someone's behavior) in terms of psychology.  To attribute psychological motivations to.|例句：He tried to psychologize her reluctance to commit. 他试图从心理学角度解释她不愿承诺的原因。

例句：Don't psychologize me!  别对我进行心理分析！


近义词：analyze, interpret, explain

反义词：ignore, overlook


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He psychologizes his patients' behavior. = 他对病人的行为进行心理分析。
She psychologizes every situation. = 她对每种情况都进行心理分析。


一般过去时:
The therapist psychologized the patient's dreams. = 治疗师对病人的梦进行了心理分析。
They psychologized his anger as a result of childhood trauma. = 他们将他的愤怒解释为童年创伤的结果。


现在完成时:
She has psychologized the situation extensively. = 她已经对情况进行了广泛的心理分析。
He has psychologized his own behavior for years. = 他多年来一直在分析自己的行为。


过去完成时:
The professor had psychologized the character's actions before the lecture. = 教授在讲座前就已经对人物的行为进行了心理分析。
They had psychologized the data before reaching a conclusion. = 他们在得出结论之前已经对数据进行了心理分析。
