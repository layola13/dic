resupplied:/ˌriːsəˈplaɪd/| v. | 再供应；补充供应 | To supply again; to replenish.  The verb "resupply" implies a previous supply that has been depleted or consumed, and the action refers to restoring that supply. |例句：The troops were resupplied with food and ammunition.  部队得到了食物和弹药的补充供应。

The outpost was resupplied by air after the road was blocked.  道路被封锁后，前哨站通过空运得到了物资补充。|近义词：replenish, restock, refill |反义词：deplete, exhaust


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They resupply the shelves every morning. = 他们每天早上都补充货架上的商品。
The company resupplies its stores regularly. = 这家公司定期为其门店补充商品。


一般过去时:
The soldiers were resupplied with water after the long march. = 长途跋涉后，士兵们得到了水的补充。
We resupplied the camping gear before heading into the wilderness. = 我们在进入荒野之前补充了露营装备。


现在完成时:

They have resupplied the hospital with much-needed medicines. = 他们已经为医院补充了急需的药品。


过去完成时:

The team had resupplied their fuel tanks before the long flight. = 团队在长途飞行之前已经补充了燃料箱。

否定句:
