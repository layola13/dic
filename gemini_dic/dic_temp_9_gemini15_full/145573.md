overcompliant:/ˌoʊvər kəmˈplaɪənt/| adj.|过度顺从的，过分服从的|Excessively compliant; showing excessive willingness to agree to requests or demands.  It suggests a level of compliance that may be unhealthy or detrimental to the individual.|例句：Her overcompliant nature led her to take on too much work, causing burnout.  她过度顺从的天性让她承担了太多的工作，导致了倦怠。

例句：He was overcompliant to his boss's every whim, neglecting his own needs. 他对老板的每一个想法都过度顺从，忽略了自己的需求。


近义词：subservient, docile, obsequious, overly agreeable

反义词：defiant, rebellious, insubordinate, assertive

词性:形容词
