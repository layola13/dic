played:/pleɪd/| v.| 扮演；玩；播放；参加比赛 | played is the past tense and past participle of the verb "play". It indicates an action that was completed in the past.  |例句：They played football in the park.他们在公园里踢足球。  They played a beautiful song. 他们演奏了一首优美的歌曲。|近义词：acted, performed, competed |反义词：stopped, ceased


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plays the piano every day. = 他每天弹钢琴。
She plays volleyball with her friends. = 她和朋友们一起打排球。

一般过去时:
I played basketball yesterday. = 我昨天打篮球了。
We played cards last night. = 我们昨晚打牌了。

现在完成时:
She has played the violin since she was five. = 她从五岁起就开始拉小提琴了。
They have played many matches this season. = 这个赛季他们已经参加了很多比赛了。

过去完成时:
He had played the game many times before he won. = 在他获胜之前，他已经玩过很多次这个游戏了。
We had played for hours before we realized how late it was. = 我们玩了几个小时才意识到已经很晚了。

将来时:
They will play in the final next week. = 他们下周将在决赛中比赛。
We will play a new game tonight. = 我们今晚将玩一个新的游戏。

虚拟语气:
If I had played better, we would have won. = 如果我打得更好，我们就赢了。
I wish I had played more often when I was younger. = 我希望我年轻的时候能多玩一些。

被动语态:
The song was played on the radio this morning. = 这首歌今天早上在广播里播放了。
The game was played in heavy rain. =  比赛是在大雨中进行的。

疑问句:
Did you play any sports in school? = 你在学校参加过任何体育运动吗？
Where did they play the concert? = 他们在哪里举办音乐会的？

否定句:
He didn't play well in the match. = 他在比赛中表现不好。
She didn't play any musical instruments. = 她不会演奏任何乐器。
