grammaticaster:/ˌɡræmˈætɪkæstər/| n. |语法学家，语法吹毛求疵者 |A person who is excessively concerned with grammar and its correct usage, often to the point of pedantry.  They may be overly critical of grammatical errors and insist on strict adherence to grammatical rules. |例句：He's such a grammaticaster; he corrects everyone's grammar, even in casual conversation. 他是个十足的语法吹毛求疵者；他纠正所有人的语法错误，即使是在非正式的谈话中。|例句：Don't be such a grammaticaster;  it's just a minor grammatical error. 不要那么吹毛求疵；这只是一个小的语法错误。|近义词：purist, pedant, grammarian (but with a negative connotation)|反义词：lax, careless (regarding grammar)|名词
