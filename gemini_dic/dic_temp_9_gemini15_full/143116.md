outbulking:/ˌaʊtˈbʌlkɪŋ/| v.|胜过；超过（体积或数量）|To exceed in bulk or size; surpass in volume or quantity.|例句：The new building outbulks all the others in the neighborhood.  新的建筑物超过附近所有其他建筑物的规模。|例句：His contributions outbulked those of all the other researchers combined. 他的贡献超过了所有其他研究人员贡献的总和。|近义词：surpass, exceed, overshadow|反义词：underwhelm, be dwarfed by


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new skyscraper outbulks all the surrounding buildings. = 新建的摩天大楼超过了周围所有建筑物。
His generosity outbulks that of any other donor. = 他的慷慨超过了任何其他捐赠者。


一般过去时:
The ancient oak tree outbulked all the other trees in the forest. = 古老的橡树超过了森林里所有其他的树木。
Her performance outbulked everyone else's in the competition. = 她在比赛中的表现超过了其他所有人。


现在完成时:
The project has outbulked our initial estimations in terms of cost. = 项目的成本已经超过了我们最初的估计。
His influence has outbulked that of any other leader in the company. = 他的影响力已经超过了公司里任何其他领导者。


过去完成时:
The storm had outbulked all previous weather events in the region. = 暴风雨的规模已经超过了该地区以往的所有天气事件。
The damage had outbulked our worst fears.  = 造成的损失已经超过了我们最坏的担忧。


将来时:
The proposed dam will outbulk any existing structures in the area. = 拟建的大坝将超过该地区任何现有的建筑物。
Their efforts will outbulk ours in the long run. = 从长远来看，他们的努力将超过我们的努力。


虚拟语气:
If the project were completed, it would outbulk all previous endeavors. = 如果项目完成，它将超过所有之前的努力。
I wish my contribution had outbulked the others. = 我希望我的贡献能够超过其他人。


被动语态:
The small house was outbulked by the imposing castle. = 小房子被雄伟的城堡比了下去。
The previous record was outbulked by the new athlete. = 先前的记录被新的运动员打破了。


疑问句:
Does the new stadium outbulk the old one? = 新体育场是否比旧体育场大？
Will the proposed development outbulk existing structures? = 拟议中的开发项目是否会超过现有建筑物？


否定句:
The smaller boat did not outbulk the larger one. = 小船没有比大船大。
His efforts did not outbulk those of his colleagues. = 他的努力并没有超过他的同事们。
