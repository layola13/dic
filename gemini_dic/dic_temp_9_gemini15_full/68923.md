fatalism: /ˈfeɪtəlɪzəm/ | n. |宿命论；听天由命|The belief that all events are predetermined and inevitable; the acceptance of all things as inevitable.  It often involves a sense of helplessness and resignation in the face of life's challenges. |例句：His fatalism prevented him from even trying to improve his situation. 他认命，甚至没有尝试改善自己的处境。

例句：She accepted her fate with a quiet fatalism. 她平静地接受了自己的命运。


近义词：determinism, predestination, resignation, acceptance

反义词：free will, optimism, agency, control
