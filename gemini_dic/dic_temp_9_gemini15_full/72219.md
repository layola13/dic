fluorescent:/flʊˈrɛsnt/ | adj. | 荧光的，发出荧光的 |  Emitting or reflecting light as a result of absorbing radiation of a shorter wavelength;  glowing in the dark.  | Example: The fluorescent lights in the office hummed quietly.  办公室里的荧光灯发出轻微的嗡嗡声。


The fluorescent paint glowed under the black light.  荧光涂料在紫外线下发出光芒。|  luminous, phosphorescent | opaque, dull
