cyanosis:/saɪəˈnoʊsɪs/| n. |发绀|A bluish discoloration of the skin and mucous membranes due to insufficient oxygen in the blood.|The baby's cyanosis was a sign of a serious heart defect.|近义词：blue discoloration, hypoxia|反义词：rubor (redness)


USAGE->

Cyanosis indicates a lack of oxygen. = 发绀表明缺氧。
The patient's cyanosis is concerning. = 病人的发绀令人担忧。

The baby experienced cyanosis after birth. = 婴儿出生后出现了发绀。
His cyanosis resolved after oxygen therapy. = 他在氧疗后发绀消退了。

The doctor has noted the patient's cyanosis. = 医生已经注意到病人的发绀。
