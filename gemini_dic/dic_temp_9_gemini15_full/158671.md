plongée:/plɔ̃ʒeɪ/| n.|跳水；潜水；俯冲|A plunge; a dive; a sudden descent.  It can refer to the act of diving into water, or a sudden drop in something else, such as a market.  The word is often used in the context of scuba diving or other underwater activities.|例句：He took a deep plongée into the ocean. 他深深地潜入大海。  The stock market experienced a sudden plongée. 股市经历了突然的暴跌。|近义词：dive, plunge, immersion|反义词：ascent, rise
