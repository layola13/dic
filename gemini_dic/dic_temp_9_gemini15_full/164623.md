preexist:/ˌpriːɪɡˈzɪst/| v.|预先存在，先于…存在 |To exist before something else; to precede in time.  |例句：The condition appeared to preexist the injury.  这种病症似乎在受伤前就已存在。|例句：The stars preexisted the Earth. 星星早于地球就已存在。|近义词：antedate, precede, forerun|反义词：postdate, succeed, follow|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The universe preexists all life. = 宇宙先于所有生命存在。
These beliefs preexist the creation of the nation. = 这些信仰早于国家的建立。

一般过去时:
The ancient civilization preexisted the Roman Empire. = 古代文明早于罗马帝国存在。
The fossil preexisted the discovery. = 那个化石在发现之前就存在了。

现在完成时:
The traditions have preexisted for centuries. = 这些传统已经存在了几个世纪。
Scientific evidence has preexisted the current theory. = 科学证据早于现行理论就已存在。

过去完成时:
The rock formations had preexisted the volcanic eruption. = 岩石构造在火山爆发前就已存在。
The ancient artifacts had preexisted the settlement. = 古代文物早于定居点就已存在。

将来时:
Will the company preexist the merger? = 公司会在并购后继续存在吗？
The contract will preexist any legal challenges. = 合同将先于任何法律挑战而存在。

虚拟语气:
If the conditions preexisted, the experiment would have been invalid. = 如果条件预先存在，实验结果将无效。
I wish the evidence had preexisted the trial. = 我希望证据在审判前就已存在。

被动语态:
The theory was preexisted by many earlier studies. = 该理论是由许多早期的研究预先存在的。
The findings were preexisted by decades of research. = 这些发现是几十年研究的结果。

疑问句:
Did the symptoms preexist the illness? = 症状是在疾病之前就存在吗？
Does the infrastructure preexist the development project? = 基础设施是在发展项目之前就存在吗？

否定句:
The symptoms did not preexist the illness. = 症状不是在疾病之前就存在的。
The technology did not preexist the need for it. = 这项技术并非在需要之前就存在的。
