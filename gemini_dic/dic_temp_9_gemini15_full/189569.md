salespeople:/ˈseɪlzˌpiːpl/| n.|销售人员，业务员|People who sell goods or services.  They are employed by companies to promote and sell products to customers. |例句：Our salespeople are highly trained and knowledgeable about our products. 我们的销售人员都接受过严格的培训，并且对我们的产品非常了解。

例句：The company's success depends heavily on the effectiveness of its salespeople.  公司的成功很大程度上取决于其销售人员的效率。


近义词：sales representatives, sellers, agents, representatives

反义词：customers, buyers

词源:
salespeople is a compound word.  "Sales" comes from the Old French word "sales," meaning "sale," ultimately derived from the Latin word "sal," meaning "salt."  "People" is from the Old English word "people."  The combination signifies individuals involved in sales activities.
