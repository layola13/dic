depleter: /dɪˈpliːtər/ | n. |消耗者，耗尽者 | A person or thing that depletes something.  Someone or something that uses up or reduces the supply of something. | The overuse of groundwater is a major concern for many communities, making the well a depleter of precious resources.  过度使用地下水是许多社区的主要担忧，这使得水井成为宝贵资源的消耗者。|近义词：consumer, user, reducer, diminisher |反义词： replenisher, restorer


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The factory depletes the local water supply. = 工厂正在消耗当地的水资源。
He depletes his savings every month. = 他每个月都在消耗他的积蓄。


一般过去时:
The drought depleted the river. = 干旱使河流枯竭。
The war depleted the country's resources. = 战争消耗了该国的资源。


现在完成时:
Years of overfishing have depleted fish stocks. = 多年的过度捕捞已经使鱼类资源枯竭。
The long journey has depleted our supplies. = 漫长的旅程已经消耗了我们的补给。


过去完成时:
They had depleted their funds before the project was finished. = 在项目完成之前，他们已经用光了资金。
The army had depleted its ammunition by the end of the battle. = 在战斗结束时，军队已经用光了弹药。


将来时:
Continued pollution will deplete the ozone layer. = 持续的污染将消耗臭氧层。
This rate of spending will deplete our savings by next year. = 以这种消费速度，到明年我们的积蓄就会耗尽。


虚拟语气:
If we continue at this rate, we will deplete our resources. = 如果我们继续以这种速度，我们将耗尽我们的资源。
If they had not depleted their savings, they could have bought a house. = 如果他们没有用光积蓄，他们就能买房子了。


被动语态:
The forest was depleted by logging. = 森林被砍伐殆尽。
The water supply was depleted by the drought. = 水资源被干旱消耗殆尽。


疑问句:
What has depleted the ozone layer? = 什么东西消耗了臭氧层？
What is depleting the natural resources? = 什么东西正在消耗自然资源？


否定句:
The company did not deplete its reserves. = 公司没有消耗其储备。
The farmer did not deplete the soil of nutrients. = 农民没有耗尽土壤中的养分。
