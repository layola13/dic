eskimo:/ˈɛskɪmoʊ/| n. |爱斯基摩人；因纽特人 |A person belonging to any of the indigenous peoples inhabiting the Arctic regions of North America, Greenland, and Siberia.  The term is now considered outdated and potentially offensive by many, with "Inuk" (singular) and "Inuit" (plural) being preferred. | Example: The term "Eskimo" is considered outdated and offensive by many. = “爱斯基摩人”这个词现在被许多人认为是过时且冒犯的。|近义词：Inuk (singular), Inuit (plural), Inupiaq, Yupik |反义词：无
