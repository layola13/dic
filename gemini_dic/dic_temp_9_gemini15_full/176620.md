reacclimatize:/ˌriːəˈklaɪmətaɪz/ | v. |重新适应气候；重新适应环境 | To become accustomed to a new climate or environment again after a period of adjustment to a different one.  |例句：After spending a week at sea level, I needed a few days to reacclimatize to the mountain altitude.  在海平面待了一周后，我需要几天时间才能重新适应高山海拔。

例句：The hikers spent several days reacclimatizing to the thinner air at higher altitude.  徒步旅行者花了好几天时间重新适应高海拔地区稀薄的空气。


近义词：adjust, adapt, acclimatize | 反义词：maladapt
