parroted:/ˈpærəˌtɪd/ | vt. |鹦鹉学舌般重复；机械地模仿|To repeat something without understanding it, or to imitate someone's words or behaviour mindlessly.|He parroted the teacher's words without really understanding their meaning. 他鹦鹉学舌般重复老师的话，并没有真正理解其含义。|She parroted the latest fashion trends. 她盲目模仿最新的时尚潮流。|近义词：mimicked, imitated, echoed |反义词：originated, innovated
