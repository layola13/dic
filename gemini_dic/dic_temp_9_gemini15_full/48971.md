degeneration:/ˌdɪdʒɛnəˈreɪʃən/| n. | 退化；衰退；堕落 | The gradual decline or deterioration of something, especially in physical, mental, or moral qualities.  It can refer to a decline in the quality, strength, or effectiveness of something over time; a process of deterioration or decay; or a decline in moral or social behavior. | Example: The degeneration of the city's infrastructure is a serious concern.  这个城市的公共设施日益衰败令人担忧。 The degeneration of his moral character was evident in his actions.  他道德品质的败坏在他行为中显而易见。| 近义词：decay, decline, deterioration, degradation | 反义词：improvement, advancement, regeneration, progress |  名词
