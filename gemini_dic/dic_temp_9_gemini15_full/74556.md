frauds:/frɔːdz/ | n. |欺诈；骗局；骗子 |A fraud is a person who is deceitful and dishonest, typically by making false statements or claims to gain an advantage.  Frauds can also refer to the acts of deception themselves. |例句：He was convicted of several frauds. 他被判犯有几项欺诈罪。

The company was exposed for its elaborate frauds. 这家公司因其精心策划的欺诈行为而曝光。


近义词：deceptions, swindles, cheats, hoaxes, scams |反义词：honesty, truthfulness, integrity
