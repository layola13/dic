grazed:/ɡreɪzd/| v.|轻微擦伤；放牧；轻抚；浏览 |To lightly scrape or abrade the skin; to feed (animals) on pasture; to touch or caress lightly; to browse or glance over something quickly.|例句：The child grazed his knee in the fall. 孩子摔倒时擦破了膝盖。  The sheep grazed peacefully in the meadow. 羊群在草地上安详地吃草。|近义词：scratch, graze, browse, skim|反义词：gouge, deeply wound

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grazes his sheep every morning. = 他每天早上放牧他的羊。
She grazes the meadow with her cows. = 她用她的奶牛放牧这片草地。

一般过去时:
I grazed my knee on the curb. = 我在路沿上擦破了膝盖。
The cattle grazed in the field yesterday. = 昨天牛群在田野里吃草。

现在完成时:
The cattle have grazed the pasture bare. = 牛群把牧场吃光了。
He has grazed his knee and needs a bandage. = 他擦破了膝盖，需要个绷带。

过去完成时:
The sheep had grazed most of the field before the rain started. = 下雨之前，羊群已经吃掉了大部分的草地。
They had grazed the land for centuries. = 他们已经放牧这片土地几个世纪了。


将来时:
The cows will graze in the new pasture tomorrow. = 奶牛明天将在新的牧场上吃草。
We will graze the sheep in the north field next week. = 我们下周将在北边的田野里放牧羊群。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the ground was so rocky, I wouldn't have grazed my knee. = 如果我知道地面这么崎岖，我就不会擦破膝盖了。
If the sheep had grazed more slowly, they wouldn't have eaten all the grass. = 如果羊群吃草慢一点，它们就不会把所有的草都吃光了。


被动语态:
The field was grazed bare by the sheep. = 这片田地被羊群吃光了。
His knee was grazed in the accident. = 他的膝盖在事故中擦伤了。


疑问句:
Did the sheep graze in the field today? = 羊今天在田里吃草了吗？
Have you ever grazed your knee badly? = 你曾经严重擦破过膝盖吗？


否定句:
The cows didn't graze in the field today. = 牛今天没在田里吃草。
I didn't graze my knee, I just bumped it. = 我没有擦破膝盖，只是碰了一下。


PHRASE->

graze out = 放牧；使…过度放牧
graze over = 轻掠；浏览
graze the land = 放牧土地
to graze cattle = 放牧牛群
