patriarchates:/ˈpeɪtriɑːrkeɪts/| n. |父权制；族长统治；家长统治 |A system of society or government controlled by men.  It refers to a social system where men hold primary power and predominate in roles of political leadership, moral authority, social privilege and control of property.  This system often involves the subordination of women and other marginalized groups.|例句：Many societies throughout history have been characterized by strong patriarchates. 历史上许多社会都以强大的父权制为特征。|例句：The feminist movement actively challenges patriarchal structures and norms. 女权运动积极挑战父权结构和规范。|近义词：male dominance, patriarchy, male supremacy |反义词：matriarchy, egalitarianism
