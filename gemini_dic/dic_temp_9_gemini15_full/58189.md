drowsiness:/ˈdraʊzinəs/ | n. |困倦；嗜睡；昏昏欲睡 | A feeling of being sleepy and wanting to sleep.  It is often a symptom of tiredness, illness, or the side effect of medication. |例句：The drowsiness from the medication made it difficult to drive.  药物引起的困倦使得开车很困难。

例句：After a long day's work, drowsiness overcame her. 长时间的工作后，困倦感袭来。


近义词：sleepiness, lethargy, somnolence | 反义词：wakefulness, alertness


词源:  Derived from the word "drowse," meaning to sleep lightly or doze.  The suffix "-ness" indicates a state or condition.  Ultimately, it traces back to Old English *drūsian, meaning "to be drowsy."
