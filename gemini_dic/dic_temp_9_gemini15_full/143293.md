outdream: /ˌaʊtˈdriːm/ | vt. |超越梦想；梦想得胜于；在梦想中胜过|To surpass in dreaming; to exceed in imaginative achievement.  |例句：He outdreamed his rivals, creating a story that captured the hearts of millions. 他在梦想中超越了他的对手，创作了一个打动数百万人心灵的故事。
|例句：The innovative design outdreamed all previous attempts, setting a new standard for the industry. 创新设计超越了以往所有尝试，为行业树立了新的标准。|近义词：outshine, surpass, exceed, outstrip|反义词：underachieve, fail, fall short|动词
