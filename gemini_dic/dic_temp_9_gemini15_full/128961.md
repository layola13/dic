ninth:/nɪnθ/| adj.|第九的|The ninth month of the year is September.  一年中的第九个月是九月。|近义词：eighth, tenth|反义词：first


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This is the ninth time I've called. = 这是我第九次打电话了。
He is the ninth person in line. = 他是队伍中的第九个人。


一般过去时:
She finished ninth in the race. = 她在这场比赛中获得了第九名。
The ninth inning was very exciting. = 第九局非常精彩。


现在完成时:
They have finished ninth in three consecutive years. = 他们连续三年获得第九名。
We have been to this restaurant nine times. = 我们已经来过这家餐厅九次了。


过去完成时:
By the ninth hour, the meeting had already ended. = 到第九个小时，会议已经结束了。
He had already tried eight times before his ninth attempt. = 在第九次尝试之前，他已经尝试过八次了。


将来时:
The concert will start at nine o'clock, that's the ninth hour. = 音乐会将于九点开始，那是第九个小时。
We will be leaving on the ninth of next month. = 我们将于下个月九号离开。


虚拟语气:
If this were the ninth inning, I would be nervous. = 如果这是第九局，我会很紧张。
I wish it were the ninth of the month already. = 我希望现在已经是这个月的第九号了。


被动语态:
The ninth chapter was written last. = 第九章最后写成。
The prize was awarded to the ninth-place finisher. = 奖项颁发给了获得第九名的选手。


疑问句:
Is this the ninth floor? = 这是九楼吗？
Which one is the ninth planet from the sun? = 哪一颗是离太阳第九远的行星？


否定句:
It is not the ninth, but the tenth. = 这不是第九，而是第十。
This isn't the ninth time we've met. = 这不是我们第九次见面。
