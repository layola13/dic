localizable:/ˈloʊkəlaɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 可本地化的 | Able to be adapted or translated for use in a specific locale or region.  This typically involves adapting text, images, and other elements to suit the cultural and linguistic preferences of a target audience.  The process often includes translation, but also considers factors like date/time formats, currency, measurement units, and even color schemes. |


例句：

The software is fully localizable, allowing for easy adaptation to different languages and regions.  该软件完全可本地化，便于适应不同的语言和地区。

Our marketing materials need to be localizable to reach a wider international audience. 我们的营销材料需要本地化才能覆盖更广阔的国际受众。


近义词：adaptable, translatable, customizable


反义词：unadaptable, untranslatable, fixed


词性: adjective
