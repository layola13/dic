gourami: /ˈɡaʊrɑːmi/ | n. | 攀鲈鱼 | A gourami is any of numerous species of freshwater fish belonging to the family Osphronemidae, known for their labyrinth organs that allow them to breathe atmospheric air.  They are popular aquarium fish, prized for their vibrant colors and interesting behavior. |  例句：The aquarium showcased a variety of gourami species, each with unique markings.  这家水族馆展示了各种攀鲈鱼，每种都有独特的标记。
