involves:/ɪnˈvɒlvz/ | vt. |包含；牵涉；使参与 | To include or contain (something) as a part or component; to have as a necessary or integral part. To bring into a situation or activity; to engage or participate in. |例句：The project involves a lot of hard work.  这个项目需要很多努力。

The accident involved three cars. 这起事故涉及三辆汽车。


近义词：include, encompass, entail, engage, participate | 反义词：exclude, omit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The job involves long hours. = 这份工作需要长时间工作。
This recipe involves several steps. = 这个食谱包含好几个步骤。


一般过去时:
The investigation involved many people. = 调查牵涉到很多人。
The argument involved a misunderstanding. = 那场争论源于误解。


现在完成时:
The scandal has involved several high-profile figures. = 这场丑闻已经牵涉到几位知名人士。
My work has involved extensive travel. = 我的工作需要大量的出差。


过去完成时:
The plan had involved a secret meeting. = 该计划曾包括一次秘密会议。
It had involved more effort than we anticipated. = 这比我们预期的需要更多努力。


将来时:
The next stage will involve testing the prototype. = 下一步将涉及测试原型。
The project will involve collaboration with other teams. = 该项目将需要与其他团队合作。


虚拟语气:
If the plan involved more risk, we wouldn't do it. = 如果这个计划风险更大，我们不会去做。
I wish the job didn't involve so much paperwork. = 我希望这份工作不需要这么多文书工作。


被动语态:
Many people were involved in the accident. = 许多人卷入了这场事故。
He was involved in a car crash last week. = 他上周卷入了一起车祸。


疑问句:
What does the job involve? = 这份工作包括什么？
Does the plan involve any risks? = 这个计划包含任何风险吗？


否定句:
The plan does not involve any extra cost. = 该计划不涉及任何额外费用。
The project didn't involve him at all. = 这个项目根本没有牵涉到他。


PHRASE->
be involved in: 参与...
involve oneself in: 卷入...
get involved in: 卷入...
involved with: 与...有关系
deeply involved: 深陷其中
