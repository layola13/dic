malarias:/məˈleəriəz/| n.|疟疾（复数）|A plural form of malaria, referring to multiple instances or types of the disease.  Malaria is a disease caused by Plasmodium parasites transmitted to humans through the bites of infected female Anopheles mosquitoes.|例句：The doctor confirmed the presence of several malarias in the village. 医生证实村子里存在几种疟疾。|近义词：malarial fevers|反义词：health, wellness
