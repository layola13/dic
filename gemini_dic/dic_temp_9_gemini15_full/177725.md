recapped:/ˌriːˈkæpt/| v.|总结，概括；重新戴上帽子|To summarize or briefly restate the main points of something; to put a cap back on something.|例句：The teacher recapped the main points of the lesson. 老师总结了课程要点。

例句：He recapped the bottle after pouring the drink. 他倒完饮料后重新盖上了瓶盖。

近义词：summarized, reviewed, reiterated|反义词：detailed, elaborated

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recaps the meeting every week. 他每周都总结会议内容。
She recaps her notes before the exam. 她在考试前复习笔记。


一般过去时:
They recapped the project's progress yesterday. 他们昨天总结了项目的进展。
The speaker recapped the key findings of the study. 演讲者总结了研究的主要发现。


现在完成时:
I have recapped all the important information. 我已经总结了所有重要的信息。
She has recapped the story for the children. 她已经为孩子们总结了故事。


过去完成时:
He had recapped the events leading up to the accident. 他已经总结了导致事故发生的相关事件。
They had recapped the previous meeting's decisions before starting the new one. 他们在开始新会议之前已经总结了上次会议的决定。


将来时:
We will recap the highlights of the conference tomorrow. 我们明天将总结会议的亮点。
She will recap the chapter before the quiz. 她考试前会总结这一章节。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would recap the entire book. 如果我有时间，我会总结整本书。
I wish I could recap the entire lecture. 我希望我能总结整个讲座。


被动语态:
The main points were recapped at the end of the presentation. 演示文末总结了要点。
The story will be recapped in the next episode. 故事将在下一集总结。


疑问句:
Will you recap the instructions for me? 你能帮我总结一下说明吗？
Did he recap the events accurately? 他是否准确地总结了事件？


否定句:
He didn't recap the details of the incident. 他没有总结事件的细节。
They didn't recap the meeting properly. 他们没有正确地总结会议。
