poofs:/pʊfs/| interj. v.|砰的一声；突然消失；|The sound of something suddenly disappearing or deflating; to disappear suddenly and without warning.|例句：The balloon poofed! 气球砰的一声消失了！ The magician poofed away. 魔术师突然消失了。|近义词：vanish, disappear, whoosh|反义词：appear, materialize
