clampdown:/ˈklæmpdaʊn/| n. v.|镇压；压制；严格控制|The act of suppressing something, typically by force or authority; a period of strict control or enforcement.|例句：There has been a clampdown on illegal immigration.  = 对非法移民进行了镇压。|例句：The government implemented a clampdown on drug trafficking. = 政府对贩毒进行了严厉打击。|近义词：crackdown, suppression, restriction|反义词：leniency, relaxation, freedom


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government clamps down on dissent. = 政府压制异见。
They clamp down on illegal activities. = 他们严厉打击非法活动。

一般过去时:
The police clamped down on the protesters. = 警察镇压了抗议者。
The authorities clamped down on the spread of misinformation. = 当局压制了虚假信息的传播。

现在完成时:
They have clamped down on the use of social media. = 他们已经加强了对社交媒体使用的控制。
The company has clamped down on employee expenses. = 公司已经严格控制了员工的开支。

过去完成时:
The regime had clamped down on all forms of opposition before the election. = 政权在选举前已经压制了所有形式的反对。
The school had clamped down on cheating after several incidents. = 在发生几起作弊事件后，学校已经加强了对作弊行为的打击。

将来时:
The government will clamp down on tax evasion. = 政府将打击逃税行为。
They will clamp down on the illegal sale of weapons. = 他们将严厉打击非法武器销售。

虚拟语气:
If they clamped down harder, the problem might be solved. = 如果他们采取更强硬的措施，问题或许能够解决。
I wish the government would clamp down on corruption. = 我希望政府能够打击腐败。

被动语态:
Dissent was clamped down on by the authorities. = 异见受到了当局的压制。
Illegal activities are being clamped down on by the police. = 非法活动正在受到警方的打击。

疑问句:
Will the government clamp down on the rising crime rate? = 政府会打击不断上升的犯罪率吗？
Is the company clamping down on employee absenteeism? = 公司正在打击员工旷工吗？

否定句:
The government did not clamp down on the protests. = 政府没有镇压抗议活动。
They did not clamp down on the spread of rumors. = 他们没有压制谣言的传播。
