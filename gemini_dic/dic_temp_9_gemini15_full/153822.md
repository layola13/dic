perceiving:/pərˈsiːvɪŋ/| v.|感知，察觉，理解|To become aware of something through the senses or the mind; to understand or interpret.|例句：I perceived a change in his attitude. 我察觉到他态度的变化了。  She perceived the danger instantly. 她立刻察觉到了危险。|近义词： noticing, detecting, understanding, comprehending|反义词：misunderstanding, ignoring, overlooking|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I perceive a shift in the market. = 我察觉到市场上出现了转变。
They perceive the situation differently. = 他们对形势的看法不同。

一般过去时:
He perceived a subtle difference in the two paintings. = 他觉察到这两幅画之间细微的差别。
She perceived the irony in his statement. = 她察觉到他话语中的讽刺意味。

现在完成时:
We have perceived a growing trend in online shopping. = 我们已经注意到网上购物的趋势越来越明显。
They have perceived the need for change. = 他们已经意识到了变革的必要性。

过去完成时:
I had perceived the problem before he mentioned it. = 在他提起之前，我已经意识到了这个问题。
She had perceived his reluctance to commit. = 她之前就已经察觉到他不愿意承诺。

将来时:
We will perceive the results of the experiment next week. = 我们下周将了解实验的结果。
He will perceive the truth eventually. = 他最终会明白真相。


虚拟语气:
If I perceived the danger earlier, I would have acted differently. = 如果我早点察觉到危险，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I had perceived the consequences before making the decision. = 我真希望在做决定之前我已经预见到后果了。


被动语态:
The change was perceived by many as a positive development. = 许多人认为这一变化是积极的进展。
The problem is perceived to be quite serious. = 这个问题被认为相当严重。


疑问句:
Do you perceive any problems with the plan? = 你认为这个计划有什么问题吗？
How did they perceive the situation? = 他们是如何看待这种情况的？


否定句:
I didn't perceive any hostility in his tone. = 我没有察觉到他语气中的敌意。
They didn't perceive the risk involved. = 他们没有意识到其中的风险。


PHRASE->
perceive as = 把…看作
perceive to be = 认为是
perceive the importance of = 意识到…的重要性
perceive a pattern = 察觉到某种模式
perceive a threat = 感知到威胁
