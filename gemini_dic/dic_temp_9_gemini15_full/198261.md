shortwave:/ˈʃɔːrtweɪv/| adj. n.|短波（的）|Relating to or denoting radio waves with wavelengths between 10 and 100 metres.  Shortwave radio is used for long-distance communication. |例句：Shortwave radio broadcasts can reach far distances. 短波广播可以传播很远的距离。

例句：The shortwave band is often used for international communication. 短波频段通常用于国际通信。
