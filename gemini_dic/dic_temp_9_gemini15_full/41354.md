corium:/ˈkɔːriəm/| n.|真皮，革层|The corium is the thick layer of living tissue below the epidermis that forms the true skin, containing blood capillaries, nerve endings, and connective tissue.|例句：The surgeon carefully examined the corium for any signs of infection. 外科医生仔细检查了真皮是否有感染迹象。|近义词：dermis, cutis|反义词：epidermis
