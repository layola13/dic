improvises:/ˈɪmprəvaɪz/| v.|即兴创作，临时应变|To create or perform something without preparation; to devise or arrange spontaneously.|例句：The jazz musician improvises a solo.  这位爵士乐手即兴演奏了一段独奏。

例句：She had to improvise a solution when the original plan fell through. 她不得不临时想出一个解决方案，因为原来的计划失败了。

近义词：extemporize, ad-lib, improvise, invent


反义词：plan, rehearse, prepare


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He improvises a song every night. 他每晚都即兴创作一首歌曲。
She often improvises during her speeches. 她经常在演讲中即兴发挥。


一般过去时:

They improvised a meal using whatever ingredients they had. 他们用现有的食材临时做了一顿饭。

过去完成时:
