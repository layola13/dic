formosa:/fɔːrˈmoʊzə/| n.|福尔摩沙；台湾|The word "Formosa" is a Portuguese word meaning "beautiful island," historically used to refer to Taiwan.  It reflects the early European explorers' impression of the island's scenic beauty.|The island of Formosa is renowned for its stunning natural landscapes.|近义词：Taiwan (modern term)|反义词：None.
