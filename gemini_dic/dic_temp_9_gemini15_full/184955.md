retaxed:/ˌriːˈtækst/| adj.|重新征税的，重新课税的|Relating to or involving the re-imposition or adjustment of a tax.|The retaxed property is now worth more than before.|近义词：reassessed, re-evaluated|反义词：untaxed, tax-exempt

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government retaxes imported goods. = 政府对进口商品重新征税。
They retax properties annually. = 他们每年对房产重新征税。
