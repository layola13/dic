co-defendant:/ˌkoʊdɪˈfɛndənt/| n. |共同被告|A co-defendant is a person who is charged with the same crime as another person and is tried in the same court case.| 例句：The co-defendant testified against the main suspect, leading to his conviction. 共同被告指证了主要嫌疑人，导致他被定罪。|The co-defendant's testimony helped the prosecution's case.  共同被告的证词帮助了检方的案件。|近义词：joint defendant, fellow defendant |反义词：plaintiff, complainant
