opposites:/ˈɑːpəzɪts/ | n. | 对立面，反义词 | Things that are opposite to each other; antonyms.  |  Heat and cold are opposites.  热和冷是相反的。The two brothers are opposites in every way. 这两个兄弟在各方面都截然相反。| antonyms, contraries | similars, synonyms | 名词
