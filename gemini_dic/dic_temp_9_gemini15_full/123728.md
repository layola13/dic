moonscapes:/ˈmuːnˌskeɪps/| n. |月球景观；月面景色|A moonscape is a landscape that resembles the surface of the moon, typically characterized by barrenness, craters, and a lack of vegetation.  It often refers to a desolate or barren terrestrial landscape.|The bombing left the area looking like a moonscape.|近义词：lunar landscape, desolate landscape|反义词：lush landscape, fertile landscape
