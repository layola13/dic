fourchette:/fɔːrˈʃɛt/|/ˈfɔːrʃɛt/| n. |餐叉；（尤指）小叉；（某些乐器的）叉状部件|A fourchette is a small fork, especially one used for eating desserts or appetizers.  It can also refer to a fork-shaped part of a musical instrument. |例句：The dessert was served with a delicate silver fourchette.  甜点用一把精致的银叉上桌。
