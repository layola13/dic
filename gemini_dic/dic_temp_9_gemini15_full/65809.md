evidently:/ˈɛvɪdəntli/ | adv. |明显地；显然地 | Clearly or obviously.  It implies that something is easily perceived or understood based on available evidence. |例句：Evidently, he was not telling the truth.  = 明显地，他没说实话。
例句：Evidently, she was upset by the news. = 显然，她被这个消息弄得很沮丧。|近义词：clearly, obviously, apparently, plainly, seemingly |反义词：obscurely, unclearly, doubtfully
