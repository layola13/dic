derivement:/dɪˈraɪvmənt/ | n. | 派生；导出；获得 | The derivement of the formula is complex.  公式的推导过程很复杂。 | 近义词：derivation, deduction | 反义词：origin, source


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scientist derives new theories from his experiments. = 科学家从他的实验中推导出新的理论。
This company derives its profits from selling software. = 这家公司从销售软件中获得利润。


一般过去时:
They derived great satisfaction from their work. = 他们从工作中获得了极大的满足感。
He derived the answer from the equation. = 他从方程式中推导出答案。


现在完成时:
We have derived many benefits from this project. = 我们从这个项目中获得了许多好处。
She has derived a lot of knowledge from her studies. = 她从学习中获得了大量的知识。


过去完成时:
They had derived the conclusion before the presentation. = 他们在演示之前已经得出了结论。
He had derived the equation from the data. = 他已经从数据中推导出了方程式。


将来时:
We will derive the results by next week. = 我们下周将得出结果。
They will derive a new method for solving the problem. = 他们将推导出解决这个问题的新方法。


虚拟语气:
If we had more data, we could derive a more accurate conclusion. = 如果我们有更多数据，我们就能得出更准确的结论。
I wish I could derive more meaning from this book. = 我希望我能从这本书中获得更多意义。


被动语态:
The formula was derived by a famous mathematician. = 这个公式是由一位著名的数学家推导出来的。
The conclusion was derived from the experimental results. = 结论是从实验结果中得出的。


疑问句:
How did you derive that conclusion? = 你是怎么得出那个结论的？
Where did he derive the information from? = 他是从哪里获得信息的？


否定句:
We could not derive any meaningful results from the experiment. = 我们无法从实验中获得任何有意义的结果。
They did not derive any benefit from the investment. = 他们从投资中没有获得任何好处。
