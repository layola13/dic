saurian:/ˈsɔːriən/| adj. n.|蜥蜴的；爬行动物的；古代爬行动物|relating to or resembling a lizard or other reptile, especially a large extinct one.  The word often evokes images of dinosaurs and other prehistoric reptiles.|例句：The museum boasts a stunning collection of saurian fossils. 这个博物馆拥有令人惊叹的蜥蜴化石收藏。

例句：Many scientists believe that birds are descended from saurian ancestors. 许多科学家认为鸟类起源于蜥蜴祖先。


近义词：reptilian, reptilic


反义词：mammalian, avian


词性：adjective, noun
