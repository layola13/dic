extrajudicial:/ˌɛkstrədʒuˈdɪʃəl/ | adj. | 法院以外的；不受司法约束的 |  Not conducted according to or permitted by the established legal processes; done outside the jurisdiction of a court.  | 例句：The extrajudicial killing of the suspect sparked widespread outrage.  嫌疑人的法外处决引发了广泛的愤怒。

例句：The government was accused of engaging in extrajudicial detention. 政府被指控从事法外拘留。


近义词：unlawful, illegal, unauthorized, summary


反义词：judicial, legal, legitimate


词性：形容词
