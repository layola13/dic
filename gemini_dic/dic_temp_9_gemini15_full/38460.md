concessively:/kənˈsɛsɪvli/| adv.| 勉强地；让步地；承认地 | In a way that shows willingness to concede or admit something.  It implies a reluctant or hesitant agreement. |例句：He conceded, albeit concessively, that the evidence was against him. 他勉强承认证据对他不利。


例句：She conceded,  concessively, to his request for a raise. 她勉强同意了他的加薪请求。


近义词：reluctantly, grudgingly, unwillingly


反义词：willingly, readily, enthusiastically


词性:副词
