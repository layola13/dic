retaxes: /ˌriːˈtæksɪz/ | n. | 再征税；二次征税 |  The act of taxing something again, often after an initial tax has been levied. This typically happens when the initial tax is deemed insufficient or when new circumstances warrant additional taxation.  | Example: The government announced retaxes on luxury goods to fund infrastructure projects.  政府宣布对奢侈品再次征税以资助基础设施项目。|近义词: double taxation, surtax | 反义词: tax reduction, tax exemption | 名词
