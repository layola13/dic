shafts:/ʃɑːfts/ | n. |轴，杆，柄；光线；矿井竖井；(力量、影响等的)冲击 | A shaft is a long, relatively slender, rod-like structure, often cylindrical or slightly tapered, used for transmitting power or supporting a load.  It can also refer to a beam of light or a vertical passage in a mine. |例句：The engine's drive shaft transmits power to the wheels.  发动机的传动轴将动力传递给车轮。
The sun's shafts pierced the clouds. 太阳的光柱穿透云层。|近义词：rod, axle, spindle, beam |反义词：surface (in the context of a mine shaft)
