enliven:/ɪnˈlaɪvən/| vt.|使活跃；使生动；使振奋|To make something more lively or interesting.|The music enlivened the party. 音乐使聚会活跃起来。|近义词：animate, invigorate, stimulate|反义词：depress, dull, dampen|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The comedian's jokes enliven the atmosphere. = 喜剧演员的笑话活跃了气氛。
Bright colors enliven the room. = 明亮的色彩使房间显得生动。

一般过去时:
The news enlivened her spirits. = 这个消息使她精神振奋。
The unexpected guest enlivened the dinner party. = 不速之客使晚宴活跃起来。

现在完成时:
The new teacher has enlivened the classroom. = 新老师使课堂活跃起来。
The addition of fresh herbs has enlivened the dish. = 添加新鲜香草使这道菜更加美味可口。

过去完成时:
The good news had enlivened their spirits before the meeting. = 在会议之前，好消息已经使他们精神振奋。
He had enlivened the presentation with some humorous anecdotes. = 他用一些幽默的轶事使演示文稿生动有趣。

将来时:
The upcoming concert will enliven the town. = 即将来临的音乐会将使小镇活跃起来。
I hope the new decorations will enliven the living room. = 我希望新的装饰能使客厅更加生动。

虚拟语气:

否定句:
