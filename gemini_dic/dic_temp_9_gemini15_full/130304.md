non-synthetic:/ˌnɑːn sɪnˈθɛtɪk/| adj. |非合成的；天然的|Not made by chemical synthesis; natural.|The company prides itself on using only non-synthetic ingredients in its products. 这家公司自豪地宣称其产品只使用非合成成分。|近义词：natural, organic|反义词：synthetic, artificial
