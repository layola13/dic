parasites:/ˈpærəsaɪts/| n.|寄生虫，寄生生物|Organisms that live on or in a host and get their food from or at the expense of their host.  They can be plants, animals, or microorganisms.|例句：Many parasites cause diseases in their hosts.  许多寄生虫会导致宿主患病。|例句：Tapeworms are a type of intestinal parasite. 绦虫是一种肠道寄生虫。|近义词：pest, vermin|反义词：host
