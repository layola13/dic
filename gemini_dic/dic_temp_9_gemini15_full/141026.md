onion: /ˈʌnjən/ | n. | 洋葱 | A bulbous plant with a pungent smell and taste, used as a vegetable.  | Example:  I added a chopped onion to the soup. 我在汤里加了切碎的洋葱。  |近义词：bulb |反义词：无

词源:
The word "onion" comes from the Old French "oignon," ultimately derived from the Latin "unio," meaning "a single pearl," referring to the layered structure of the bulb.
