irradicate:/ɪˈrædɪkeɪt/ | vt. | 根除；消灭；杜绝 | To remove or destroy completely; to get rid of something harmful | The government launched a campaign to eradicate poverty. 政府发起了一场根除贫困的运动。 | 近义词：eliminate, exterminate, abolish | 反义词：foster, cultivate, promote


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We eradicate pests from our crops annually. = 我们每年都清除农作物上的害虫。
The company eradicates all traces of its competitors. = 这家公司根除了所有竞争对手的痕迹。


一般过去时:
They eradicated smallpox worldwide. = 他们根除了全世界的天花。
The farmer eradicated weeds from his field. = 农民清除了他田里的杂草。


现在完成时:
Scientists have eradicated many diseases. = 科学家们已经根除了许多疾病。
We have eradicated the problem of water pollution in this area. = 我们已经根除了该地区的水污染问题。


过去完成时:
The government had eradicated corruption before the election. = 政府在大选前已经根除了腐败。
They had eradicated the disease from the village before the outbreak spread. = 在疫情蔓延之前，他们已经将疾病从村庄根除了。


将来时:
We will eradicate hunger in our community. = 我们将在我们的社区中消除饥饿。
The new technology will eradicate the need for manual labor. = 新技术将消除对体力劳动的需求。


虚拟语气:
If we acted decisively, we could eradicate this problem. = 如果我们果断行动，就能根除这个问题。
It's time we eradicated poverty from our society. = 是时候我们从社会上根除贫困了。


被动语态:
Malaria was eradicated from this region years ago. = 多年前这个地区的天花就被根除了。
The disease is being eradicated through vaccination. = 通过疫苗接种正在根除这种疾病。


疑问句:
Can we truly eradicate all forms of discrimination? = 我们真的能够根除所有形式的歧视吗？
Has the problem of homelessness been eradicated in your city? = 你们城市是否已经根除了无家可归的问题？


否定句:
We cannot eradicate poverty overnight. = 我们不能一夜之间根除贫困。
They failed to eradicate the invasive species. = 他们未能根除入侵物种。


PHRASE->
eradicate poverty = 根除贫困
eradicate disease = 根除疾病
eradicate illiteracy = 根除文盲
eradicate corruption = 根除腐败
eradicate racism = 根除种族主义

词源:
源自拉丁语 "e-" (out) + "radix" (root), 字面意思是从根部拔除。
