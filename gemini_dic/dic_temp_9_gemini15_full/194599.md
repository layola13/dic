self-surveyed:/ˌsɛlfˈsɜːveɪd/| adj.| 自我评估的，自行调查的 |  Describes something that has been assessed or investigated by the person or entity involved, without external input or assistance.  | Example: The self-surveyed data indicated a high level of job satisfaction among employees.  自我评估的数据表明员工的满意度很高。 | 近义词：self-assessed, self-reported, independently evaluated | 反义词：externally evaluated, professionally assessed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company self-surveys employee satisfaction annually. = 公司每年都会进行员工满意度自我评估。
He self-surveys his progress on the project weekly. = 他每周都会对项目进展进行自我评估。


一般过去时:
The students self-surveyed their learning outcomes after the course. = 课程结束后，学生们对自己的学习成果进行了自我评估。
She self-surveyed her strengths and weaknesses before applying for the job. = 在申请工作之前，她对自己进行了优势和劣势的自我评估。


现在完成时:
They have self-surveyed their needs and identified areas for improvement. = 他们已经对自己的需求进行了自我评估，并确定了改进领域。
We have self-surveyed the market and found a niche for our product. = 我们已经对市场进行了自我评估，并为我们的产品找到了一个利基市场。


过去完成时:
He had self-surveyed his options before making a final decision. = 在做出最终决定之前，他已经对自己的选择进行了自我评估。
The team had self-surveyed their performance before the project review. = 项目评审之前，团队已经对自己的表现进行了自我评估。


将来时:
The researcher will self-survey his participants' experiences after the experiment. = 实验结束后，研究人员将对参与者的经历进行自我评估。
She will self-survey the effectiveness of her training program next month. = 下个月她将对培训项目的有效性进行自我评估。


虚拟语气:
If I had self-surveyed my capabilities earlier, I would have chosen a different path. = 如果我早点对自己能力进行自我评估，我就会选择不同的道路。
It would be helpful if everyone self-surveyed their contribution to the team. = 如果每个人都能对自己对团队的贡献进行自我评估，那就太好了。


被动语态:
The data was self-surveyed by the participants themselves. = 数据是由参与者自己进行自我评估的。
The company's performance will be self-surveyed by its employees. = 公司的表现将由其员工进行自我评估。


疑问句:
Have you self-surveyed your readiness for the exam? = 你已经对考试的准备情况进行了自我评估了吗？
Will the customers self-survey their satisfaction with the product? = 客户会对产品的满意度进行自我评估吗？


否定句:
The students did not self-survey their progress adequately. = 学生们没有充分地对自己的进步进行自我评估。
The company decided not to self-survey employee morale this year. = 公司决定今年不进行员工士气的自我评估。
