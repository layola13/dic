isaiah: /aɪˈzeɪə/| n. |以赛亚；以赛亚书|The name Isaiah comes from the Hebrew word Yeshayahu, meaning "Yahweh is salvation" or "Yahweh saves".  It refers to both the biblical prophet and the book of the Bible he is attributed to have written.  The book of Isaiah is a major prophetic book in the Hebrew Bible and the Old Testament, containing prophecies, poems, and oracles. |例句：The book of Isaiah is a significant work of religious literature.  以赛亚书是重要的宗教文学作品。

The prophet Isaiah is known for his powerful pronouncements of God's judgment and promise of redemption. 以赛亚先知以其对上帝审判和救赎承诺的有力宣告而闻名。|近义词：prophet(先知), oracle(神谕) |反义词：N/A
