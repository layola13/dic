moulding:/ˈmoʊldɪŋ/ | n. |造型，模塑；装饰线；铸件 |The decorative shaping of a substance, typically a soft material, into a particular form.  It can also refer to the resulting shaped object or a decorative strip. |例句：The ornate moulding around the fireplace was a beautiful detail.  壁炉周围华丽的装饰线是一个美丽的细节。

The wooden moulding added a classic touch to the room.  木质装饰线为房间增添了经典气息。

|近义词：casting, shaping, forming, decoration |反义词：removal, destruction |名词
