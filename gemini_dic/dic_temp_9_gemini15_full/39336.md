conjecturing:/kənˈdʒɛktʃəriŋ/| v.|推测；猜测；臆断|The act of forming an opinion or conclusion based on incomplete information; guesswork or speculation.|例句：She spent hours conjecturing about the meaning of the cryptic message. 她花了数小时推测那条神秘信息的意思。|例句：The scientist was conjecturing about the origins of the universe.  这位科学家正在推测宇宙的起源。|近义词：speculating, hypothesizing, surmising|反义词：knowing, proving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is conjecturing about the future of artificial intelligence. 他正在推测人工智能的未来。
They are constantly conjecturing about the motives of their rivals. 他们不断地猜测对手的动机。


一般过去时:
She conjectured that the painting was a forgery. 她推测这幅画是伪造的。
The detective conjectured about the killer's identity. 侦探推测了杀手的身份。


现在完成时:
They have conjectured that the earthquake caused the tsunami. 他们推测地震引发了海啸。
Scientists have conjectured about the possibility of life on other planets. 科学家们一直在推测其他星球上存在生命的可能性。


过去完成时:
He had conjectured that the company would go bankrupt. 他曾推测该公司会破产。
The historian had conjectured about the reasons for the war. 这位历史学家曾推测战争的原因。


将来时:
She will conjecture about the results of the election. 她将推测选举的结果。
They will be conjecturing about the meaning of the dream. 他们将会推测梦境的含义。


虚拟语气:
If I were to conjecture, I would say the project will fail. 如果我要推测的话，我会说这个项目会失败。
I wish I hadn't conjectured so carelessly about his intentions. 我真希望我没有如此草率地推测他的意图。


被动语态:
The cause of the accident is being conjectured upon by the police. 警察正在推测事故的原因。
The meaning of the inscription was conjectured to be religious in nature.  人们推测铭文的意思具有宗教性质。


疑问句:
What are you conjecturing about? 你在推测什么？
What did the experts conjecture about the situation? 专家们对这种情况作出了什么推测？


否定句:
I don't conjecture about things I don't understand. 我不会推测我不理解的事情。
He didn't conjecture that the evidence was fabricated. 他没有推测证据是伪造的。
