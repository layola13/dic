impenitence: /ɪmˈpɛnɪtəns/ | n. |  不悔改；执迷不悟；顽固不化 |  A feeling or condition of not repenting for one's sins or offenses;  a refusal to show remorse or regret.  It implies a hardened attitude and a lack of willingness to change one's behavior. |  His impenitence in the face of overwhelming evidence was shocking.  他的行为令人震惊，面对压倒性的证据，他依然不知悔改。

His impenitence led to a longer prison sentence.  他的执迷不悟导致了更长的监禁时间。|  remorse, contrition, repentance |  repentance, remorse, contrition
