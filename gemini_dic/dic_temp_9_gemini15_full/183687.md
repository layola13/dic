reroofed:/ˌriːˈruːft/| v. |重新盖屋顶|To put a new roof on (a building).  This is the past participle of the verb "reroof." It describes a building that has had its roof replaced.|例句：The house was reroofed last year.  去年这房子重新盖了屋顶。

The old barn was reroofed using reclaimed materials.  旧谷仓用回收材料重新盖了屋顶。


近义词：re-roofed, re-covered (the roof)
反义词：None (antonyms describe the opposite action, not the state of having a new roof)


词性:动词(过去分词)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reroof houses for a living. = 他们以翻新屋顶为生。
We reroof our houses every ten years. = 我们每十年翻新一次屋顶。


一般过去时:
He reroofed the shed last weekend. = 他上周末重新盖了棚屋的屋顶。
The company reroofed the school building. = 那家公司重新盖了校舍的屋顶。


现在完成时:
They have reroofed the church. = 他们已经重新盖好了教堂的屋顶。
We have reroofed our house twice. = 我们已经两次重新盖了房子的屋顶。


过去完成时:
They had reroofed the building before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他们已经重新盖好了建筑物的屋顶。
The contractor had reroofed the garage by the time we arrived. = 当我们到达时，承包商已经重新盖好了车库的屋顶。


将来时:

If they reroofed the house, it would look much better. = 如果他们重新盖了屋顶，房子看起来会好得多。


被动语态:

词源:  The word "reroofed" is formed by adding the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "anew") to the verb "roofed" (the past participle of "roof").  Therefore, it literally means "roofed again."
