rind:/raɪnd/| n.|果皮，兽皮|The tough outer covering of some fruits and vegetables, or the outer layer of some cheeses.  It often refers to a thick, leathery skin.|例句：The orange rind is bitter.  橘子皮很苦。

例句：He carefully removed the rind from the cheese before serving it. 他在食用前小心地去掉了奶酪的外皮。


近义词：peel, skin, crust, husk|反义词：flesh, pulp

词性:名词
