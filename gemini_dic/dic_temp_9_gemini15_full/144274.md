outsource:/ˈaʊtˌsɔːrs/|/ˈaʊtˌsɔːrs/| vt.|外包，外判|To obtain (goods or a service) from an outside or foreign supplier, especially in place of an internal source.|例句：Many companies outsource their customer service to call centers in other countries.  许多公司将其客户服务外包给其他国家的呼叫中心。

例句：We decided to outsource the software development to a specialized firm. 我们决定将软件开发外包给一家专业公司。


近义词：contract out, subcontract, delegate


反义词：insource, internalize


词性:动词
