couples:/ˈkʌpəlz/| n.|夫妇；情侣；对；几对|A couple is two people who are married or in a romantic relationship.  It can also refer to two things considered as a pair. |例句：Several couples were dancing in the ballroom.  几对夫妇在舞厅跳舞。

例句：The artist created a beautiful couple of paintings.艺术家创作了两幅美丽的画作。


近义词：pairs, duos, twosomes|反义词：singles, individuals|词性:名词
