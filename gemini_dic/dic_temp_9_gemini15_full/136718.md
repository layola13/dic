nonsense:/ˈnɑːnsɛns/ /ˈnɒnsɛns/| n.|胡说八道，废话，无意义|Foolish or senseless talk, action, or idea.  Something that is meaningless or illogical.|例句：What you're saying is sheer nonsense. 你说的全是胡说八道。

例句：Don't talk nonsense! 不要说胡话！


近义词：rubbish, drivel, balderdash, twaddle, trash|反义词：sense, reason, logic|词性:名词


词源:
The word "nonsense" comes from the Middle English "nonesens," which itself originated from the Old English "non" (not) and "sens" (sense).  It literally means "without sense" or "lacking sense."  The word's meaning has remained consistent throughout its history, referring to something that is illogical, foolish, or meaningless.
