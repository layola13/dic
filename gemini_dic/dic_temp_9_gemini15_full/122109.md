mistletoe:/ˈmɪs(ə)ltoʊ/| n. |槲寄生|A plant with white berries that grows on other trees, especially used as a Christmas decoration.  It is traditionally associated with kissing under it. |例句：We hung mistletoe in the doorway for the Christmas party. 我们在门口挂上了槲寄生，为圣诞派对做准备。

例句：The mistletoe berries are poisonous if eaten.  槲寄生的浆果如果食用是有毒的。


近义词：None (unique plant) | 反义词：None (unique plant)| 词性:noun
