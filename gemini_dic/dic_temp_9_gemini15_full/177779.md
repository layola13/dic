receded:/rɪˈsiːdɪd/ | v. | 褪去；消退；撤回；收回；后退 | To move back or away from a previous position; to become less intense or noticeable. |

例句：

The floodwaters finally receded.  = 洪水最终退去了。

His hairline has receded significantly over the years. = 这些年来，他的发际线明显后移了。


近义词：withdraw, retreat, subside, ebb, fade


反义词：advance, proceed, progress, surge, increase


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The tide recedes twice a day. = 潮汐一天退两次。
His influence is slowly receding. = 他的影响力正在慢慢减弱。

一般过去时:
The pain receded after he took the medicine. = 他服药后疼痛减轻了。
The army receded from the border. = 军队从边境撤军了。

现在完成时:
The floodwaters have receded, leaving behind a trail of mud. = 洪水退去，留下了一条泥泞的小路。
His anger has receded somewhat since the incident. = 自那件事发生后，他的怒气消退了一些。

过去完成时:
The symptoms had receded before the doctor arrived. = 在医生到达之前，症状已经消退了。
The crowd had receded after the concert ended. = 音乐会结束后，人群散去了。

将来时:
The swelling will recede in a few days. = 肿胀会在几天内消退。
I believe the tensions will recede after the meeting. = 我相信会议结束后紧张局势将会缓和。

虚拟语气:
If the storm receded, we could go to the beach. = 如果暴风雨消退了，我们就可以去海滩了。
I wish my fear would recede. = 我希望我的恐惧能够消失。

被动语态:
The waters were receded by the engineers. = 水位被工程师们降低了。(This is a less common usage)
The problem was not receded. = 问题没有解决。(This is a less common usage, "resolved" would be preferred)


疑问句:
Has the inflammation receded yet? = 炎症消退了吗？
Will the symptoms recede quickly? = 症状会很快消退吗？

否定句:
The floodwaters haven't receded yet. = 洪水还没有退去。
The pain didn't recede despite taking the medicine. = 尽管服药了，疼痛也没有减轻。
