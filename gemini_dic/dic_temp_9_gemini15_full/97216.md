inflatedness: /ɪnˈfleɪtɪdnəs/ | n. | 膨胀；通货膨胀；夸张；自负 | The state of being inflated; excessive pride or self-importance; a condition of being swollen or puffed up;  an increase in the general price level of goods and services in an economy. |


例句：

The inflatedness of his ego was remarkable. = 他极度的自负令人瞩目。

The government is concerned about the rising inflatedness. = 政府担心通货膨胀加剧。


近义词：swelling, puffiness, inflation, exaggeration, pomposity, conceit

反义词：deflation, shrinkage, humility, modesty


词性：名词
