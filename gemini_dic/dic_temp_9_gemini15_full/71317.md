fizzing:/ˈfɪzɪŋ/| adj. |嘶嘶作响的；兴奋的|Making a hissing sound like that of a carbonated drink; excitedly bubbly.  |例句：The champagne was fizzing merrily in the glass. 香槟酒在杯中欢快地嘶嘶作响。  The kids were all fizzing with excitement before the trip.孩子们在旅行前都兴奋地激动不已。|近义词：effervescent, bubbling, sparkling|反义词：flat, still


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The soda is fizzing. = 苏打水正在冒泡。
The champagne fizzes in the glass. = 香槟酒在杯中冒泡。

一般过去时:
The drink fizzed delightfully. = 饮料愉快地冒泡。
The soda fizzed when I opened it. = 我打开苏打水时，它冒泡了。

现在完成时:
The drink has been fizzing for hours. = 这饮料已经冒泡几个小时了。
The bottle has fizzed over. = 瓶子里的液体溢出来了。

过去完成时:
The soda had already fizzed before I could taste it. = 我还没来得及尝，苏打水就已经不冒泡了。
The champagne had fizzed out by the time we got there. = 等我们到的时候，香槟酒已经不冒泡了。

将来时:
The lemonade will fizz nicely once it's chilled. = 柠檬水冷却后会很好地冒泡。
The soda will fizz when you open it. = 打开苏打水后，它会冒泡。

虚拟语气:
If the drink were fizzing more, it would be more refreshing. = 如果饮料冒泡更多，它会更清爽。
I wish the soda was still fizzing. = 我真希望苏打水还在冒泡。

被动语态:
The soda was fizzed open. = 苏打水被打开并冒泡了。(不太常用，因为fizzing通常是主动发生的)
The drink is being fizzed with excitement.  = 这饮料因兴奋而变得充满活力。(比喻用法)


疑问句:
Is the drink still fizzing? = 饮料还在冒泡吗？
Why is the soda fizzing so much? = 为什么苏打水冒泡这么多？


否定句:
The drink isn't fizzing anymore. = 饮料不再冒泡了。
The champagne didn't fizz at all. = 香槟酒根本没有冒泡。
