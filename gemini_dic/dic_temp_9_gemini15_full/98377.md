insociability: /ˌɪnˌsoʊʃəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不合群；孤僻；社交障碍 |  The inability or unwillingness to be sociable; shyness or reclusiveness.  It often stems from social anxiety, autism spectrum disorder, or other underlying conditions. |  His insociability made it difficult for him to make friends. 他的不合群使他很难交到朋友。  Her extreme insociability was a symptom of her anxiety disorder.  她的极度不合群是她焦虑症的症状。 |  unsociability, shyness, reclusiveness | sociability, gregariousness, extroversion | 名词
