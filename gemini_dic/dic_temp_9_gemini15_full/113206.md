lurky:/ˈlɜːrki/ | adj. | 可疑的；鬼鬼祟祟的；偷偷摸摸的 | Suggestive of something suspicious or secretive; shady.  It implies hidden or clandestine activity, often with negative connotations. | 例句：There was a lurky figure lurking in the shadows.  =  阴影里有一个鬼鬼祟祟的身影。

There's something lurky about his sudden wealth. = 他突然暴富有点可疑。


近义词：suspicious, shady, secretive, clandestine, furtive, shifty, underhand


反义词：open, aboveboard, transparent, honest


词性：形容词
