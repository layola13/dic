miming:/ˈmaɪmɪŋ/ | v. | 模仿，哑剧表演 |  The act of expressing oneself or acting out a scene without speaking, often using gestures and facial expressions.  | 例句：The children were miming animals in the park. 孩子们在公园里模仿动物。

例句：He gave a captivating performance, miming a scene from a famous play. 他表演了一个引人入胜的节目，模仿了一个著名戏剧中的场景。


近义词：acting out, pantomiming, gesturing | 反义词：speaking, verbalizing | 词性：动名词

词源:  The word "miming" comes from the noun "mime," which ultimately derives from the Greek word "mimos" (μῖμος), meaning "imitator" or "actor."  The Greek word entered Latin as "mimus," and then into various modern European languages, eventually evolving into the English "mime" and "miming".
