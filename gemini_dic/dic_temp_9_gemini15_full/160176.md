pond:/pɑːnd/| n. |池塘；水塘|A pond is a small body of still water, smaller than a lake.  It is often artificial, but can also occur naturally.|例句：The children were playing in the pond.孩子们在池塘里玩耍。
例句：There are many beautiful lilies growing in the pond. 池塘里长着许多美丽的睡莲。|近义词：pool, lake (smaller than)|反义词：ocean, sea
