neurodivergence:/ˌnʊroʊdaɪˈvɜːrdʒəns/| n. |神经多样性|The condition of having neurological differences, such as autism, ADHD, dyslexia, or Tourette's syndrome, that deviate from what is considered typical or neurotypical.  It emphasizes the natural variation in human brains and cognition, rather than viewing these differences as deficits.|Example:  Understanding neurodivergence is crucial for creating inclusive environments.  理解神经多样性对于创造包容性环境至关重要。|近义词：neurodiversity (often used interchangeably)|反义词：neurotypicality
