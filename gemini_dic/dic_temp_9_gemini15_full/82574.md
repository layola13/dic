guardage:/ˈɡɑːrdɪdʒ/| n.|保护；监护；保管；看守|The act of guarding or protecting something or someone; the state of being guarded or protected.  It can also refer to the period of time during which someone is under guard or protection. |例句：The guardage of the royal jewels was entrusted to the most experienced guards. 皇家珠宝的守护工作托付给了经验最丰富的警卫。

例句：Her guardage during her travels was paramount to her safety.  在她旅行期间，保护她的安全至关重要。


近义词：protection, guardianship, custody, safekeeping, surveillance

反义词：neglect, abandonment, exposure


词源：源自古法语 *guardia(ge), 源于古法语 guarde "guard",  最终源于日耳曼语根 *ward- "to watch, guard".
