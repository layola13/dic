oxhide:/ˈɒkshaɪd/| n. |牛皮|Oxhide is the tough, thick hide of an ox, used to make leather.  It is known for its durability and strength.|The craftsman used oxhide to create a sturdy shield.|近义词：cowhide, bullhide|反义词：无
