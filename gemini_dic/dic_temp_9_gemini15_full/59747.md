ecologist:/ɪˈkɒlədʒɪst/|/ˈiːkɒlədʒɪst/| n. |生态学家|A scientist who studies ecology, the relationships between organisms and their environment.  Ecologists investigate how living things interact with each other and their surroundings, including factors like climate, soil, and other organisms.|  Dr. Smith is a renowned ecologist specializing in rainforest ecosystems. 史密斯博士是一位著名的生态学家，专门研究雨林生态系统。|  environmental scientist, biologist, conservationist |  (无明确反义词)|名词
