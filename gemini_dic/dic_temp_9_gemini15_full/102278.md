its:/ɪts/| pron.|它的；牠的；它们的；(所有格代词)|Its  refers to something previously mentioned and shows possession. It is the possessive form of the pronoun "it," and it never takes an apostrophe.|例句：The dog wagged its tail.  那条狗摇着它的尾巴。

The cat is playing with its toy. 猫正在玩它的玩具。


近义词：his (for male animals), her (for female animals) |反义词:无|词性:代词
