divisor: /dɪˈvaɪzər/ | n. |除数，约数| A number that divides another number without leaving a remainder.  It's a factor of that number. | Example: In the equation 12 ÷ 3 = 4, 3 is the divisor.  12 is the dividend, and 4 is the quotient. |近义词：factor, divider |反义词：dividend (in the context of division)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The divisor in this problem is 5. = 这道题的除数是5。
What is the divisor of 24? = 24的除数是什么？


一般过去时:
The divisor was 7. = 除数是7。
What was the divisor they used? = 他们用了什么除数？


现在完成时:
We have found all the divisors of 100. = 我们已经找到了100的所有约数。
Have you identified the greatest common divisor? = 你已经确定了最大公约数了吗？


过去完成时:
They had already determined the divisor before the calculation. = 他们在计算之前就已经确定了除数。
What had been the divisor in the previous step? = 上一步的除数是什么？


将来时:
The divisor will be 11. = 除数将是11。
What will the divisor be in the next equation? = 下一个方程式的除数将会是什么？


虚拟语气:
If the divisor were 2, the answer would be different. = 如果除数是2，答案就会不同。
I wish I had known the divisor earlier. = 我真希望我早点知道除数。


被动语态:
The number was divided by the divisor. = 这个数字被除数除。
The divisor was correctly identified. = 除数被正确地识别出来了。


疑问句:
What is the divisor? = 除数是什么？
Which number is the divisor in this case? = 在这种情况下，哪个数字是除数？


否定句:
That number is not a divisor of 15. = 那个数字不是15的约数。
The divisor was not correctly applied. = 除数没有正确地应用。


PHRASE->
greatest common divisor (GCD) = 最大公约数
least common multiple (LCM)  (related concept, not a direct phrase with divisor) = 最小公倍数
divisor of zero = 零因子
proper divisor = 真约数
divisor function = 除数函数
