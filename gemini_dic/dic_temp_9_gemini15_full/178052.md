reclasses:/ˌriːˈklæsɪz/| vt.|重新分类，重新归类|To classify or categorize something again, often using a different system or criteria.|The librarian reclassified the books according to a new Dewey Decimal System. 图书管理员根据新的杜威十进制系统对书籍进行了重新分类。|近义词：recategorize, reorganize, re-sort|反义词：classify (initially)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reclassifies its assets annually. = 公司每年都会重新分类其资产。
He reclassifies the data according to new parameters. = 他根据新的参数对数据进行重新分类。


一般过去时:
They reclassified the documents after the audit. = 审计后，他们对文件进行了重新分类。
The government reclassified the information as top secret. = 政府将该信息重新归类为最高机密。


现在完成时:
She has reclassified all the files on her computer. = 她已经重新分类了她电脑上的所有文件。
The team has reclassified the project's risks. = 团队已经重新评估了项目的风险。


过去完成时:
They had reclassified the items before the inventory. = 他们在清点存货之前已经对物品进行了重新分类。
The researcher had reclassified the species based on new findings. = 研究人员根据新的发现对物种进行了重新分类。


将来时:
The bank will reclassify the loan as non-performing. = 银行将把贷款重新分类为不良贷款。
We will reclassify the expenses after the budget review. = 预算审查后，我们将重新分类支出。


虚拟语气:
If they reclassified the data, the results would be different. = 如果他们重新分类数据，结果就会不同。
I wish they had reclassified the priorities earlier. = 我希望他们早点重新确定优先级。


被动语态:
The data was reclassified by the analyst. = 数据由分析师重新分类。
The documents are being reclassified for security reasons. = 出于安全原因，文件正在被重新分类。


疑问句:
Will the products be reclassified next quarter? = 产品下个季度会被重新分类吗？
Have the items been reclassified according to the new standards? = 这些物品是否已根据新标准重新分类？


否定句:
They did not reclassify the accounts this month. = 他们这个月没有重新分类账户。
The company has not yet reclassified its liabilities. = 公司尚未重新分类其负债。
