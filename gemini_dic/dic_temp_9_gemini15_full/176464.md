re-objected:/ˌriːəbˈdʒɛktɪd/ | v. | 再反对，再次提出异议 | To object again; to raise an objection a second time. This word implies a previous objection has already been made.  | Example: After the judge overruled his first objection, the lawyer re-objected on the grounds of hearsay.  律师第一次的异议被法官驳回后，又以传闻证据为由再次提出异议。 |近义词:  protested again, opposed again, remonstrated again | 反义词:  agreed, concurred, accepted | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-objects to the proposal every time it's mentioned. = 他每次提到这个提案就再次反对。
They re-object to the new rules whenever they are enforced. = 他们每次执行新规就再次反对。


一般过去时:
The defendant re-objected to the evidence presented by the prosecution. = 被告再次反对检方提出的证据。
She re-objected to the judge's ruling, but it was again overruled. = 她再次反对法官的裁决，但再次被驳回。


现在完成时:
The defense attorney has re-objected several times throughout the trial. = 辩护律师在整个审判过程中已经多次再次提出异议。
They have re-objected to the construction project, citing environmental concerns. = 他们再次反对该建筑项目，理由是环境问题。


过去完成时:
He had re-objected before the judge even had a chance to speak. = 在法官有机会说话之前，他已经再次提出异议了。
The committee had re-objected to the budget several times before finally approving it. = 委员会在最终批准预算之前已经多次再次提出异议。


将来时:
They will re-object to the motion if it is brought up again. = 如果再次提出这项动议，他们将会再次提出异议。
The union will re-object to the proposed wage cuts if they are not revised. = 如果不修改拟议的工资削减方案，工会将再次提出异议。


虚拟语气:
If he re-objected, the judge would likely overrule him again. = 如果他再次提出异议，法官很可能会再次驳回。
I wish they hadn't re-objected to the plan, as it would have saved a lot of time. = 我希望他们没有再次反对这个计划，因为这将节省很多时间。


被动语态:
The proposal was re-objected to by several members of the board. = 该提案遭到董事会几名成员的再次反对。
His arguments were re-objected to, even after being clarified. = 他的论点即使在澄清之后也再次遭到反对。


疑问句:
Will they re-object to the decision? = 他们会再次反对这个决定吗？
Did he re-object to the amendment? = 他再次反对这项修正案了吗？


否定句:
They did not re-object to the new policy after further discussion. = 经过进一步讨论后，他们没有再次反对这项新政策。
He didn't re-object to her suggestion after she explained it more thoroughly.  = 在她更详细地解释之后，他没有再次反对她的建议。


PHRASE->
re-object to a ruling = 再次反对裁决
re-object to a decision = 再次反对决定
re-object to a proposal = 再次反对提案
re-object on grounds of = 以...为由再次反对
re-object vehemently = 强烈地再次反对
