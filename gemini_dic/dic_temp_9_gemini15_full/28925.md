carriers:/ˈkæriərz/| n.|运输工具；携带者；航空公司；传播者|A carrier is something or someone that transports or carries something else.  This can range from a physical object like a ship or truck, to a biological entity like a mosquito carrying a disease, to an organization like an airline. |例句：The shipping carriers are responsible for delivering the goods on time.  运输公司负责准时送货。

例句：Several carriers offer flights to that destination.几家航空公司提供飞往该目的地的航班。


近义词：transporter, conveyor, transmitter, vector|反义词：receiver, recipient
