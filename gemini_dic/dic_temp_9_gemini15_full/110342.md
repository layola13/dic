lighthouses:/ˈlaɪthˌhaʊzɪz/ | n. |灯塔；灯塔群 | A lighthouse is a tower with a light that guides ships at sea.  Lighthouses are typically located on coastlines or islands. | 例句：The lighthouse stood tall against the stormy sea.  灯塔在暴风雨的海面上高耸屹立。

The old lighthouse has been restored to its former glory.  这座古老的灯塔已被修复到昔日的辉煌。|近义词：beacon, landmark |反义词：无
