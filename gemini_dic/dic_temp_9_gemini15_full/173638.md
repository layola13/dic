quasi-intimated: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ ɪnˈtɪmeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 暗示；似是而非地暗示 |

英文解析：The word "quasi-intimated" combines "quasi," meaning "seemingly," "apparently," or "to a certain extent," with "intimated," meaning "to imply or suggest indirectly."  Therefore, "quasi-intimated" describes something that was hinted at or suggested in a subtle or indirect way, but not explicitly stated.  It suggests a lack of directness or certainty in the communication.

例句：

He quasi-intimated his displeasure with a slight frown. = 他略微皱眉，似是而非地暗示了他的不满。

Her silence quasi-intimated her agreement to the proposal. = 她的沉默似乎暗示了她同意这项提议。


近义词：implied, suggested, hinted, insinuated, alluded to

反义词：stated explicitly, declared openly, directly communicated

词性：形容词
