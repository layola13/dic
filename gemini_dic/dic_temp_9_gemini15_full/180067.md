reflexer: /rɪˈflɛksər/ | n. | 反射器，反射装置 | A device that reflects light, sound, or other waves.  It is often used in optical systems or other applications where redirection of energy is required. | Example: The photographer used a reflector to soften the harsh sunlight on the subject's face.  摄影师用反光板来柔化照射在拍摄对象脸上的强烈阳光。 |近义词：reflector, mirror | 反义词：absorber
