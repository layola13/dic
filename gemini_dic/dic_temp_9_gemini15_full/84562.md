hammerings:/ˈhæmərɪŋz/ | n. |猛击；痛击；责骂；|The continuous hammerings of the waves against the cliffs were deafening.  海浪不断地猛击悬崖，震耳欲聋。|The team suffered a series of hammerings in the early part of the season.  球队在赛季初遭遇了一系列惨败。|近义词：beatings, poundings, thrashings |反义词：gentle treatment, caresses


USAGE->

The waves are hammering the shore. = 海浪正在拍打海岸。

过去完成时:
