fanaticize:/fəˈnætɪsaɪz/| vt.|使狂热；使极端化 |To make someone or something become excessively enthusiastic or zealous; to make fanatical.  |例句：The politician's speeches fanaticized his followers, leading them to engage in violent acts.  这位政治家的演讲使他的追随者狂热起来，导致他们参与暴力行为。

The constant media coverage fanaticized the public's interest in the trial.  持续的媒体报道使公众对审判的兴趣达到了狂热的程度。|近义词：radicalize,extremize|反义词：moderate,calm
