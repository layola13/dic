descendants:/dɪˈsɛndənts/ | n. | 后代，子孙 | People who are related to someone who lived a long time ago.  They are the offspring of an ancestor. |  The descendants of the original settlers still live in the area.  这片地区的原住民后代至今仍居住在此。| offspring, posterity, progeny | ancestors, predecessors | 名词

The descendants of the Vikings are scattered across the globe. = 维京人的后代遍布全球。
His descendants maintain the family farm. = 他的后代仍然经营着家族农场。

一般过去时:
The king's descendants ruled for many centuries. = 国王的子孙统治了几个世纪。
Her descendants sold the ancestral home. = 她的后代卖掉了祖传的房子。

现在完成时:
Scientists have traced the descendants of this ancient species. = 科学家们已经追踪到了这个古老物种的后代。
Many descendants have embraced new technologies.  = 许多后代已经接受了新技术。

过去完成时:

将来时:

Her descendants will inherit a vast fortune. = 她的后代将继承巨额财富。

虚拟语气:
If I were a descendant of royalty, I would have a different life. = 如果我是皇室后裔，我的生活将会不同。
I wish I were a descendant of famous artists.  = 我希望我是著名艺术家的后代。

被动语态:
