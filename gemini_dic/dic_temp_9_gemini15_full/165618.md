premiers:/ˈpremiərz/| n. | 首次公演；首相；总理 |  A premiers refers to the first public performance of a play, film, etc., or to a prime minister or premier.  | Example: The film premiered at the Cannes Film Festival. 这部电影在戛纳电影节首映。The new prime minister will address the nation tonight. 新任总理今晚将向全国发表讲话。|近义词: debuts, premieres (for performance), prime ministers, heads of government |反义词:  None directly applicable, depending on context (e.g.,  "re-release" for film premiere).


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The play premiers tonight. = 这部戏今晚首映。
The prime minister premiers a new policy. = 首相推出了一项新政策。


一般过去时:
The movie premiered last week. = 电影上周首映。

现在完成时:
The director has premiered three successful films. = 这位导演已成功首映了三部电影。
Several countries have had female premiers. = 几个国家都有女总理。
