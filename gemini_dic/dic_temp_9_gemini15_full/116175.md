masculinizations:/ˌmæskjʊlɪnaɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |男性化，男性特征|The process of becoming or making something more masculine; the act or instance of masculinizing.  It can refer to biological, social, or psychological aspects. |例句：The masculinizations of certain bird species are often linked to increased aggression.  某些鸟类的雄性化特征通常与攻击性增强有关。

例句：  Studies on the masculinizations of language have revealed interesting cultural variations. 关于语言男性化的研究揭示了有趣的文化差异。


近义词：virilisation, maleness, manliness |反义词：feminization
