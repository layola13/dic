corrugator:/ˈkɒrəɡeɪtər/| n. |皱眉肌，起皱器|A corrugator is a muscle of the face, specifically one of a pair of muscles located above the eyebrows that are responsible for drawing the eyebrows together and causing wrinkles on the forehead.  It can also refer to a machine that corrugates (makes wavy or grooved) material.| Example:  The corrugator muscles are responsible for the characteristic frown lines on the forehead.  皱眉肌会造成额头上的典型皱眉纹。|近义词：brow furrower|反义词：None directly, but muscles that smooth the forehead could be considered antonyms functionally.
