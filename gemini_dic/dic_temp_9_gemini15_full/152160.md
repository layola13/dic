/pætənəsiz/ | n. | 轻拍；轻抚；抚摸；（尤指）爱抚；轻拍声 | The act of patting; a light stroke or tap.  It often implies a gentle, comforting gesture. |例句：The mother gave her child a pat on the head.  母亲轻轻拍了拍孩子的头。

例句：The soft patnesses of the cat's fur were soothing. 猫毛柔软的轻抚声很抚慰人心。

近义词：caresses, strokes, taps | 反义词：None directly opposite, but harsh blows could be considered an antonym. | 名词
