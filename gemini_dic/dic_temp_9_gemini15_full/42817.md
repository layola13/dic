courages:/ˈkʌrɪdʒɪz/| n.|勇气；鼓励|The word "courages" is the plural form of "courage," meaning bravery or the ability to face fear and difficulty. It can also refer to acts of encouragement.|例句：Her courageous actions inspired others.  她的勇敢行为鼓舞了其他人。|近义词：bravery, boldness, valor|反义词：cowardice, fear, timidity|名词
