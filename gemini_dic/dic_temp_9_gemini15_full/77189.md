gaspereau:/ˈɡæspəroʊ/| n. |北美小河鱼|A small, silvery fish of the smelt family, found in the rivers and coastal waters of eastern North America.  Gaspereau are anadromous, meaning they spend part of their lives in freshwater rivers and part in saltwater oceans. They are highly valued as a food source and are important to the ecosystem.|例句：The gaspereau run is an important event for the local community.  当地社区非常重视河鲱鱼的洄游。|例句：The river was teeming with gaspereau during their spawning season. 产卵季节，河里挤满了河鲱鱼。|近义词：smelt, alewife (depending on the specific species)|反义词:  N/A
