go-to-bed-with: /ˌɡoʊ tuː ˈbɛd wɪð/ | phrasal verb | 与…睡觉，与…发生性关系 | This phrase describes the act of sharing a bed with someone, often implying sexual intimacy.  The meaning is heavily context-dependent; it can be used euphemistically or explicitly. | 例句：He went to bed with her after the party. 他在派对后和她睡了。


例句：


She refused to go to bed with him. 她拒绝和他上床。


They went to bed with each other after a long courtship. 经过长时间的恋爱之后，他们在一起了。


近义词: sleep with, have sex with, be intimate with


反义词: abstain from, avoid


词性: phrasal verb


USAGE->

This phrasal verb doesn't lend itself to typical grammatical tense conjugation in the same way as a regular verb.  Its usage is primarily in the simple past and present tenses, reflecting the nature of the action it describes.

一般现在时:
He often goes to bed with his wife. 他经常和妻子一起睡觉。
She goes to bed with a book every night. 她每天晚上睡觉前都会看书。

一般过去时:
They went to bed with each other last night. 他们昨晚在一起了。
He went to bed with her after the dance. 舞会后，他和她睡了。

现在完成时:
I have never gone to bed with anyone else. 我从未和其他人睡过。
She has gone to bed with him several times. 她和他睡过好几次了。


过去完成时:
This tense is less common with this phrasal verb.  It would only be used in very specific contexts.  For example:

They had gone to bed with each other before the argument. 他们在争吵之前就已经睡在一起了。

将来时:
I don't plan on going to bed with him. 我不打算和他上床。
They intend to go to bed with each other tonight. 他们打算今晚在一起。



虚拟语气:
If I went to bed with him, I would regret it. 如果我和他上床，我会后悔的。
I wish I hadn't gone to bed with him. 我真希望我没有和他上床。



被动语态:  The passive voice is grammatically awkward and rarely used with this phrase.

疑问句:
Did he go to bed with her? 他和她睡了吗？
Have you ever gone to bed with someone you didn't know? 你有没有和你不认识的人睡过？


否定句:
I didn't go to bed with him. 我没有和他睡。
She has never gone to bed with anyone before. 她以前从未和任何人睡过。



PHRASE->

The phrase "go to bed with" itself is a complete phrase.  There aren't common, meaningful phrasal expansions.  Its meaning is largely fixed.
