respond:/rɪˈspɑːnd/| /rɪˈspɒnd/| v.|回应，反应，答复|To answer or reply to someone or something.|He responded to my question with a smile. 他笑着回答了我的问题。|She responded quickly to the emergency. 她迅速回应了紧急情况。|answer, reply, react|ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He responds to emails promptly. 他及时回复邮件。
They respond well to positive reinforcement. 他们对积极的强化反应良好。

一般过去时:
She responded to the advertisement. 她回应了广告。
He responded angrily to the criticism. 他愤怒地回应了批评。

现在完成时:
I have responded to all your emails. 我已经回复了你所有的邮件。
She has not responded to my calls yet. 她还没有回复我的电话。

过去完成时:
He had already responded before she called. 他在她打电话之前就已经回复了。
They had responded to the crisis effectively. 他们有效地应对的危机。

将来时:
I will respond to your letter tomorrow. 我明天会回复你的信。
She will respond to the survey by Friday. 她将在周五之前回复调查问卷。

虚拟语气:
If he responded honestly, it would be better. 如果他诚实地回应，会更好。
I wish she had responded more positively. 我希望她能更积极地回应。

被动语态:
His comments were responded to by many people. 他的评论受到了许多人的回应。
The email was responded to within an hour. 这封邮件在一小时内得到了回复。

疑问句:
How did he respond to your proposal? 他如何回应你的提案？
Did she respond to your invitation? 她回应你的邀请了吗？

否定句:
He didn't respond to my message. 他没有回复我的信息。
She didn't respond at all. 她根本没有回应。


PHRASE->
respond to: 回应，对...作出反应
respond in kind: 以牙还牙
respond positively: 积极回应
respond negatively: 消极回应
respond promptly: 及时回应
