fastest: /ˈfæstɪst/ | adj. | 最快的 | Superlative form of "fast," describing something that moves or happens at the greatest speed.  It indicates the highest degree of speed compared to others. |例句：The cheetah is the fastest land animal. 猎豹是陆地上跑得最快的动物。

例句：He finished the race in the fastest time. 他以最快的速度完成了比赛。


近义词：quickest, speediest | 反义词：slowest, laziest


词源:  The word "fastest" is derived from the Old English word "fæst," meaning "firm, fixed, strong."  Over time, its meaning shifted to denote speed and swiftness. The "-est" suffix indicates the superlative degree of comparison.
