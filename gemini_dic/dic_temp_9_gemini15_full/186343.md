rhumbaing: /rʊmˈbæŋ/ | v. | 跳伦巴舞 |  Dancing the rhumba.  A lively dance of Hispanic origin, characterized by a rhythmic sway of the hips and a distinctive beat. |  例句：They were rhumbaing on the dance floor.  他们在舞池里跳伦巴舞。

例句：The band played a rhumba, and everyone started rhumbaing. 乐队演奏了一首伦巴舞曲，于是每个人都开始跳伦巴舞。


近义词：dancing, swaying, gyrating

反义词：无
