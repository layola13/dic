single:/ˈsɪŋɡəl/| adj., n.| 单一的；单个的；单身的；|  Describing something that is one only, not part of a pair or group;  unmarried.| 例句：He lives a single life. 他过着单身生活。  She bought a single ticket. 她买了一张单程票。|近义词：sole, lone, unmarried, unattached|反义词：married, double, multiple, paired
