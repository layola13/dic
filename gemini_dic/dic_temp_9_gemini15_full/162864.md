precedence:/ˈprɛsɪdəns/| n. |优先权；优先地位；先例|The importance of something, or the fact that it should be dealt with before other things.  It also refers to a previous case or example that is used to support later decisions.|例句：The meeting will begin after the chairman's precedence. 会议将在主席发言后开始。
例句：The court gave precedence to the earlier ruling. 法院优先考虑了早先的裁决。|近义词：priority, preference, priority, precedence, seniority|反义词：subordination, posteriority
