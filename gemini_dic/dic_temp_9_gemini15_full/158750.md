plowing:/ˈplaʊɪŋ/| v.|耕作，犁地|The act of turning over soil with a plow to prepare it for planting.  It can also refer to the process of moving through something with difficulty. |例句：The farmer is plowing the field. 农民正在耕地。

例句：The ship plowed through the heavy seas. 船艰难地驶过汹涌的大海。|近义词：tilling, cultivating, ploughing (UK spelling)|反义词：harvesting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plows the field every spring. 他每年春天都耕地。
They plow the land before planting. 他们在播种前耕地。

一般过去时:
The farmer plowed the field yesterday. 农民昨天耕了地。
We plowed through the snow to reach the cabin. 我们趟着雪走到小屋。

现在完成时:
They have plowed all the fields. 他们已经耕完所有的地了。
She has plowed through three books this week. 她这周已经读完了三本书。

过去完成时:
They had plowed the field before the rain started.  下雨前他们已经耕好了地。
We had plowed through most of the report before lunch.  午饭前我们已经完成了大部分报告。

将来时:
They will plow the field next week. 他们下周将耕地。
He will plow through the paperwork this afternoon. 他今天下午将处理这些文件。

虚拟语气:
If it weren't raining, we would plow the field. 如果不下雨，我们将耕地。
I wish I had plowed the field earlier. 我真希望我早点耕地。

被动语态:
The field was plowed last week.  这块地在上周被耕了。
The snow was plowed from the driveway.  车道上的雪被清理掉了。

疑问句:
Have they plowed the field yet? 他们已经耕地了吗？
Will you plow the field tomorrow? 你明天耕地吗？

否定句:
They didn't plow the field today. 他们今天没有耕地。
He hasn't plowed the field yet. 他还没耕地。
