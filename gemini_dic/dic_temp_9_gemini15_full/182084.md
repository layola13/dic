remilitarize:/ˌriːˈmɪlɪtəraɪz/| vt.|重新军事化；使重新武装|To restore military forces or equipment to (a place or area).  The act of equipping a place or region with military forces again after a period of demilitarization.|例句：The government decided to remilitarize the border region after the recent attacks.  政府决定在最近的袭击之后重新军事化边境地区。|近义词：rearm, fortify, reinforce|反义词：demilitarize
