reacidifications:/ˌriːæsɪdɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n.| 再酸化作用；再次酸化 | The process of becoming acidic again after a period of being less acidic.  This term is often used in environmental science, particularly concerning bodies of water or soil. | Example: The reacidifications of lakes in Scandinavia after a period of recovery is a concerning trend.  斯堪的纳维亚湖泊在恢复一段时间后再次酸化是一个令人担忧的趋势。|近义词：re-acidification, renewed acidification |反义词：neutralization, alkalinization
