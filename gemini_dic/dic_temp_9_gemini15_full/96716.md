ineffervescence:/ˌɪnɛfərˈvɛsəns/| n. |不活泼；平静；沉闷|Lack of effervescence; stillness; quietness.  It refers to the absence of lively or exciting qualities.  The word suggests a state of calm or dullness, the opposite of bubbling excitement.|The party lacked ineffervescence; it was surprisingly subdued.|近义词：stillness, quietude, calmness, lethargy|反义词：effervescence, excitement, liveliness, vivacity
