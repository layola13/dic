reexcavating:/ˌriːˈɛkskəveɪtɪŋ/| v.|重新挖掘；再次挖掘 |  The act of digging up something again, typically something that has been previously excavated.  It implies a renewed effort to uncover or examine something that was already unearthed. |例句：Archaeologists are reexcavating the site to look for more artifacts. 考古学家正在重新挖掘该遗址以寻找更多文物。


The team is reexcavating the ancient city to find more evidence of its history.  团队正在重新挖掘这座古城，以寻找更多关于其历史的证据。|近义词：re-explore, reinvestigate |反义词：bury, conceal
