potholing:/ˈpɒtˌhəʊlɪŋ/| n. |探洞，洞穴探险|The exploration of caves, especially those requiring specialized equipment and techniques.|例句：Potholing is a dangerous but rewarding hobby. 探洞是一项危险但有益的爱好。

例句：He spent his weekend engaging in potholing in the Yorkshire Dales. 他周末在约克郡谷地进行探洞活动。


近义词：cave exploration, spelunking|反义词：None|词性：名词


词源:  The word "potholing" is a combination of "pot" and "hole".  Historically, "pot holes" referred to small, roughly cylindrical cavities in the ground, often found in limestone regions.  The term evolved to describe the exploration of these and similar underground formations, eventually encompassing a wider range of cave exploration activities.
