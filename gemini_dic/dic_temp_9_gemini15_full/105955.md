kludgers:/ˈklʌdʒərz/| n.|拙劣的技工；笨拙的人；蹩脚的程序员|A kludger is someone who produces clumsy or inelegant work, often through a lack of skill or understanding.  They might be a mechanic who makes repairs in a haphazard way, or a programmer who writes inefficient and poorly structured code.  The term often carries a negative connotation, implying a lack of professionalism or competence.|例句：The mechanic was a kludger; his repairs were always temporary and unreliable.  那个技工很笨拙；他的修理总是临时的，不可靠的。

The software was written by a kludger and is full of bugs.  这个软件是由一个蹩脚的程序员编写的，充满了漏洞。|近义词：bungler, botcher, hack, amateur|反义词：expert, professional, skilled artisan
