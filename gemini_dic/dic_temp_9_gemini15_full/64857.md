espousal:/ɪˈspaʊzəl/ | n. |拥护；支持；提倡；婚姻 |The act of espousing; advocacy; support.  It can also refer to the act of marrying someone. |例句：His espousal of socialist ideals alienated many of his former friends. = 他对社会主义理想的拥护使他疏远了许多以前的朋友。

例句：Their espousal was a joyous occasion for family and friends. = 他们的婚礼对家人和朋友来说是一个快乐的时刻。

近义词：support, advocacy, championship, marriage

反义词：opposition, rejection, repudiation


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He espouses conservative values. = 他拥护保守的价值观。
The organization espouses environmental protection. = 该组织提倡环境保护。

一般过去时:
She espoused the cause of women's rights. = 她拥护妇女权利的事业。
They espoused each other in a small church. = 他们在小教堂里结了婚。

现在完成时:
He has espoused this belief for many years. = 他多年来一直信奉这种信念。
They have espoused a new approach to education. = 他们采用了新的教育方法。

过去完成时:
She had espoused his views before she met him. = 在她遇到他之前，她就认同了他的观点。
They had espoused a different philosophy in the past. = 他们过去信奉的是不同的哲学。

将来时:
He will espouse the cause of the poor. = 他将拥护穷人的事业。
They will espouse a more sustainable lifestyle. = 他们将过上更可持续的生活方式。

虚拟语气:
If he espoused those views, he would be ostracized. = 如果他支持那些观点，他会遭到排斥。
I wish he had espoused a more moderate approach. = 我希望他采取更温和的方式。

被动语态:
These ideas were espoused by many intellectuals. = 这些观点被许多知识分子所拥护。
Their marriage was espoused in a grand ceremony. = 他们的婚礼以盛大的仪式举行。

疑问句:
Does he espouse any particular political party? = 他是否支持任何特定的政党？
Will they espouse a more environmentally friendly policy? = 他们会采取更环保的政策吗？

否定句:
He does not espouse violence as a solution. = 他不主张暴力作为解决方法。
They did not espouse those outdated beliefs. = 他们没有支持那些过时的信仰。


PHRASE->

espousal of a cause = 拥护一项事业
espousal of marriage = 结婚
espousal of new ideas = 接受新思想
espousal of faith = 信仰的宣誓
espousal of the policy = 支持这项政策


词源:

From Old French *espouser, from Vulgar Latin *exsponsare, from Latin sponsus, past participle of spondere "to promise," from Proto-Indo-European *spe- "to offer, promise."  The meaning shifted from "to promise in marriage" to the broader sense of "to support or advocate."
