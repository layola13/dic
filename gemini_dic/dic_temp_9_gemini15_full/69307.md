febrile:/ˈfiːbraɪl/| adj.|发烧的；兴奋的；狂热的|Characterized by or exhibiting fever; relating to or caused by fever.  Also, showing heightened excitement or nervous energy.|例句：The child had a febrile seizure.  孩子发生了发热性抽搐。

例句：His febrile speech betrayed his anxiety. 他兴奋的演讲暴露了他的焦虑。


近义词： feverish, excited, feverish, agitated|反义词：calm, apathetic, unemotional


词源:  From Latin *febrilis, from febris "fever".
