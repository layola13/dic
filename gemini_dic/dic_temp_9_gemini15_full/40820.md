convoyance:/kənˈvɔɪəns/| n. |运送，运输；交通工具；便利；文件；证明 |The act of conveying; transportation; a means of conveyance; a document conveying information or legal rights.  |例句：The conveyance of goods across the border was heavily regulated. 边境货物的运输受到严格管制。


例句：The old carriage was a charming conveyance. 这辆旧马车是一种迷人的交通工具。


近义词：transport, carriage, transfer, vehicle, document


反义词：immobility, stagnation


词性：名词


USAGE->

The company conveys goods daily. = 公司每天运送货物。
