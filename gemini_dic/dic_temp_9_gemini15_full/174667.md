quoit:/kwɔɪt/| n.|飞盘；铁环；|A quoit is a heavy ring, typically of metal, that is thrown at a peg or other target in a game.  The plural is quoits. |例句：He hurled the quoit with surprising force. 他以惊人的力量投掷铁环。
例句：The game of quoits is popular in many parts of the world.  掷铁环游戏在世界许多地方都很流行。|近义词：ring, discus|反义词：无|名词
