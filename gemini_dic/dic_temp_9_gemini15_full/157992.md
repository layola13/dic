planktons:/ˈplæŋktənz/| n. |浮游生物|Planktons are microscopic organisms that drift in water. 浮游生物是漂浮在水中的微观有机体。|近义词：microscopic organisms, plankton (plankton is the singular form)|反义词：nekton

These plankton photosynthesize to produce energy. = 这些浮游生物通过光合作用产生能量。
