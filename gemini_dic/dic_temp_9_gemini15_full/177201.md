rearrested:/ˌriːəˈrɛstɪd/| v. |再次逮捕；重新逮捕|To arrest someone again, typically after they have been released or acquitted on a previous charge.  The verb is formed by adding the prefix "re-" to the verb "arrest."|例句：He was rearrested just days after being released on bail. 他保释释放几天后就被再次逮捕了。

例句：The suspect was rearrested for questioning in connection with a separate crime. 嫌疑人因与另一宗罪行有关而被重新逮捕以接受讯问。


近义词：reapprehend, re-arrest


反义词：release, acquit


词性：动词
