outgoings:/ˈaʊtɡoʊɪŋz/| n.|支出；开支；开销|Expenditures or payments made regularly.  They encompass various costs associated with maintaining a household, running a business, or fulfilling personal obligations.|例句：Our monthly outgoings include rent, utilities, and groceries. = 我们每月的支出包括房租、水电费和杂货。|例句：The company's outgoings have increased significantly this year. = 公司今年的支出大幅增加。|近义词：expenses, expenditure, costs|反义词：income, revenue, earnings
