词源:
From pharmaceut- +‎ -ic.  Pharmaceut- comes from Late Latin *pharmaceutica, from Ancient Greek φαρμακευτική (pharmakeutikḗ), from φάρμακον (phármakon, "drug, medicine, poison").  The suffix "-ic" forms adjectives.
