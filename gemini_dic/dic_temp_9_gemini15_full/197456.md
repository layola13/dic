sheepless:/ˈʃiːpləs/| adj.|缺乏羊的；没有羊的；睡不着觉的|Without sheep; unable to sleep.|例句：The farmer was sheepless after the wolves attacked his flock.  农夫在狼袭击了他的羊群后，失去了羊。|例句：I was sheepless last night, tossing and turning all night. 我昨晚睡不着，整晚辗转反侧。|近义词：sleepless, insomniac (referring to inability to sleep)|反义词：sheep-filled (referring to the presence of sheep), restful (referring to sleep)
