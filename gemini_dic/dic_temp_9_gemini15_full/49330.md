delimitation:/ˌdiːlɪmɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |界限的确定；划界；限定 |The act of setting the limits or boundaries of something.  It involves defining the scope, extent, or range of a particular area, concept, or process. |Example: The delimitation of the project's scope was crucial for its success. 项目范围的界定对其成功至关重要。|近义词：definition, demarcation, limitation, specification |反义词：indefinition, indefiniteness, expansion

WORD ORIGIN->
The word "delimitation" comes from the Latin word "delimitare," which is a combination of "de-" (meaning "off," "away," or "from") and "limitare" (meaning "to limit" or "to bound").  Therefore, "delimitation" literally means "the act of setting limits or boundaries."
