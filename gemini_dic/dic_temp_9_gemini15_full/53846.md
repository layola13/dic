discontentation:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛnteɪʃən/ | n. | 不满，不满足 | A state of dissatisfaction; unhappiness with one's circumstances.  | The widespread discontentation led to widespread protests.  普遍的不满导致了大规模的抗议。  | dissatisfaction, unhappiness, unrest | contentment, satisfaction, happiness | 名词
