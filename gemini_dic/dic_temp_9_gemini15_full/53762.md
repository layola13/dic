discomfort:/dɪsˈkʌmfərt/| n.| 不适，不舒服；不安 | a feeling of slight pain, unhappiness, or worry | The patient complained of discomfort in his chest.  病人抱怨胸部不适。|  She felt a sense of discomfort at the party.  她在派对上感到不安。 | unease, distress, inconvenience | comfort, ease, pleasure

This situation brings me great discomfort. = 这种情况让我非常不舒服。
