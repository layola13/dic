drawloom: /ˈdrɔːluːm/ | n. |提花织机| A drawloom is a type of loom that allows for the weaving of complex patterns by using a system of heddles and harnesses to lift individual warp threads. This allows for intricate designs to be created, unlike simpler looms that produce only plain weaves.  |The intricate tapestry was woven on a drawloom, showcasing the weaver's skill. 这幅复杂的挂毯是用提花织机织成的，展现了织工的技艺。|jacquard loom, tapestry loom|simple loom|
