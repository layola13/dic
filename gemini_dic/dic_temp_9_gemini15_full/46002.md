cybercafe:/ˈsaɪbərˌkæfeɪ/| n. |网吧|A cybercafe is a place that provides computers with Internet access to the public, often for a fee.  It typically offers additional services such as food and drinks. |Example: I spent the afternoon at the cybercafe, catching up on emails and checking social media.  我在网吧待了一下午，处理邮件和查看社交媒体。|近义词：internet cafe, digital cafe |反义词:library (in the sense of free access)|名词
