sinews:/ˈsɪnjuːz/ | n. |肌腱；筋；力量；实力 |The sinews are the tough cords of tissue that connect muscles to bones.  Sinews also refer to strength and power, both physical and mental. |例句：The athlete's sinews were visibly bulging as he strained.  这位运动员用力时，肌腱明显隆起。

The company's success depends on its strong sinews in the market. 公司的成功取决于其强大的市场实力。|近义词：tendons, muscles, strength, power |反义词：weakness, frailty, feebleness |
