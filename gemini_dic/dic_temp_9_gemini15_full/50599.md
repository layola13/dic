derby:/ˈdɑːrbi/ /ˈdɜːrbi/| n.|德比，德比赛，（某种）帽，|A derby is a sporting contest, especially one between two teams from the same city or region. It can also refer to a type of stiff felt hat.  The word's origins are complex and relate to the town of Derby, England.|例句：The annual football derby between Liverpool and Everton is always a fiercely contested match.  每年利物浦和埃弗顿之间的足球德比战总是竞争激烈。The man wore a stylish derby hat. 那个人戴着一顶时尚的德比帽。|近义词：competition, contest, match, hat|反义词：无
