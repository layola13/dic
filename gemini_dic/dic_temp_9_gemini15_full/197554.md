shellings:/ˈʃɛlɪŋz/| n. |去壳；炮击；剥壳；|The act of removing shells from something, such as nuts or shellfish, or the act of shelling (bombarding) with artillery.  |例句：The shellings of the nuts took a long time.  剥壳坚果花了很长时间。

例句：The continuous shellings of the city caused widespread damage. 城市持续不断的炮击造成了广泛的破坏。|近义词：peeling, shelling (bombardment), bombardment |反义词：shelling (covering with shells)
