rethank:/riːˈθæŋk/| v. |重新感谢，再次感谢 |To express gratitude again; to thank someone a second time or more.  |例句：I want to rethank you for your generous gift. 我想再次感谢你的慷慨馈赠。|例句：I rethanked her for her help after reflecting on how much it meant.  我仔细思考了她帮助我的意义后，再次向她表示感谢。|近义词：thank again, express renewed gratitude |反义词：criticize, blame


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I rethank my friends for their support. = 我再次感谢朋友们的支持。
We rethank the volunteers for their hard work. = 我们再次感谢志愿者们的辛勤工作。

一般过去时:
She rethanked him for the flowers. = 她再次感谢他送的花。
They rethanked the audience for their warm applause. = 他们再次感谢观众的热烈掌声。

现在完成时:
I have rethanked everyone who helped me. = 我已经再次感谢了所有帮助我的人。
She has rethanked the committee for their efforts. = 她已经再次感谢了委员会的努力。

过去完成时:
He had rethanked her before leaving. = 他在离开之前已经再次感谢了她。
They had rethanked the sponsors before the event ended. = 活动结束后，他们已经再次感谢了赞助商们。

将来时:
I will rethank them at the next meeting. = 我将在下一次会议上再次感谢他们。
We will rethank our donors in our next newsletter. = 我们将在下一期简报中再次感谢我们的捐赠者。

虚拟语气:
If I had the chance, I would rethank her for everything. = 如果我有机会，我会再次感谢她的一切。
I wish I could rethank him in person. = 我希望能够亲自再次感谢他。
