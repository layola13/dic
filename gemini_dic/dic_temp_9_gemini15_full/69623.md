feminised: /ˈfɛmɪnaɪzd/ | adj. | 女性化的；使女性化的 |  To make or become more feminine in appearance or character.  |例句：The feminised portrayal of the character in the film drew criticism.  电影中对人物女性化的刻画引起了批评。

例句：The feminised language used in the marketing campaign aimed to attract a female audience.  营销活动中使用的女性化语言旨在吸引女性观众。


近义词：feminized, effeminate (for men), womanly


反义词：masculine, manly, virile


词源:  Derived from "feminine" + "-ised" (British English spelling) or "-ized" (American English spelling).  "Feminine" ultimately comes from Latin *femina "woman".  The suffix "-ised/-ized" indicates the process of making something or becoming something.
