shipmate:/ˈʃɪpmeɪt/| n. |船员，同船人 |A fellow crew member on a ship.  |例句：He became close friends with his shipmates during the long voyage. 他在漫长的航海过程中与他的船员们成为了亲密的朋友。|近义词：crewmate, fellow sailor |反义词：enemy, opponent|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My shipmate helps me with my work. = 我的船员帮我工作。
He often chats with his shipmates. = 他经常和他的船员们聊天。

一般过去时:
My shipmate helped me yesterday. = 我的船员昨天帮我。
They talked with their shipmates last night. = 他们昨晚和他们的船员们聊天。

现在完成时:
I have known my shipmate for many years. = 我认识我的船员很多年了。
He has sailed with his shipmates on many voyages. = 他和他的船员们一起航行了许多次。

过去完成时:
I had already met my shipmate before the voyage began. = 在航行开始之前我已经见过我的船员了。
They had worked together with their shipmates on previous voyages. = 他们之前在航行中与他们的船员们一起工作过。

将来时:
I will see my shipmate next week. = 我下周将见到我的船员。
They will sail with their shipmates next month. = 他们下个月将与他们的船员们一起航行。

虚拟语气:
If I were a shipmate, I would understand his difficulties better. = 如果我是船员，我会更好地理解他的困难。
I wish I had more time to spend with my shipmates. = 我希望我有更多时间与我的船员们相处。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
