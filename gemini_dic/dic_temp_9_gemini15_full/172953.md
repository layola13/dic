qualm:/kwɑːm/| n.|疑虑；不安；内疚感|A feeling of doubt or uncertainty, or a feeling of uneasiness or hesitation about doing something. It can also refer to a sudden feeling of nausea.|例句：I have some qualms about the safety of the plan. 我对计划的安全有些疑虑。|例句：She had a qualm about lying to her friend. 她对向朋友撒谎感到不安。|近义词：doubt, scruple, misgiving, hesitation|反义词：certainty, confidence, assurance|名词
