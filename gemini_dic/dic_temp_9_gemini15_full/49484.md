delusory:/dɪˈluːzəri/| adj.|欺骗的；虚幻的；妄想的 | Based on or characterized by delusion; deceptive; illusory.  It refers to something that is false or misleading, often due to a delusion or a mistaken belief. |例句：His grandiose plans proved to be delusory. 他的宏伟计划证明是虚幻的。


His delusory hope of winning the lottery led him to ignore his financial responsibilities. 他赢得彩票的妄想希望导致他忽视了他的财务责任。|近义词：illusory, deceptive, fallacious, imaginary |反义词：real, true, factual, genuine
