operas:/ˈɒpərəz/ | n. |歌剧 |Plural of opera; a dramatic work in one or more acts, set to music for singers and instrumentalists.  Operas typically have arias, recitatives, choruses, and an orchestra. | Example:  Many famous operas are based on Shakespearean plays. 许多著名的歌剧都是基于莎士比亚的戏剧。

The Metropolitan Opera presented a stunning new production of Carmen.  大都会歌剧院上演了一部令人惊叹的卡门新制作。


近义词: musical drama, dramatic work, musical  | 反义词: (no direct antonym)

词源:
The word "opera" comes from the Italian word "opera," which means "work."  It was originally used to describe a musical work, and eventually came to be specifically associated with the dramatic musical form we know today.
