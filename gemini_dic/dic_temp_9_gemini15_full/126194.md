muzzling:/ˈmʌzəlɪŋ/ | v. | 束嘴，戴口笼；压制，阻止 | The act of putting a muzzle on an animal or suppressing speech or expression.  |例句：The dog was muzzled to prevent it from biting. = 这条狗被戴上嘴套以防止它咬人。


例句：The government is accused of muzzling the press. = 政府被指控压制新闻界。

近义词：gagging, silencing, restraining, suppressing

反义词：unleashing, freeing, liberating

词性：动词
