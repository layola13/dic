churr:/tʃɜːr/| int.| 澳洲俚语：兄弟，朋友，问候语 |  A slang term of address, mainly used in Australia and New Zealand, expressing camaraderie or greeting. It's similar to "mate" or "hey". | Churr, mate! How's it going? 兄弟，你好吗？|近义词：mate, hey, bro |反义词：无
