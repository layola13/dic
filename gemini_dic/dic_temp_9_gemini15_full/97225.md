inflationary:/ɪnˈfleɪʃənəri/ | adj. | 通货膨胀的；膨胀的 | Relating to or characterized by inflation, especially a general increase in prices and fall in the purchasing value of money.  | Example: The country is experiencing inflationary pressures due to rising energy costs. 这个国家由于能源成本上升而面临通货膨胀压力。  The government implemented policies to combat inflationary trends. 政府实施了抑制通货膨胀趋势的政策。|近义词：inflationist, expansive |反义词：deflationary

词源:

From *inflation* +‎ *-ary*.  "Inflation" itself comes from the Latin *inflatio*, meaning "a swelling," from *inflare*, "to inflate," from *in-* "in" + *flare*, "to blow."  The "-ary" suffix creates an adjective.
