overfreighting:/ˌoʊvərˈfreɪtɪŋ/| n. |超载，装载过多 |The act of loading a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle with more cargo than it is designed to carry, or than is safe.  This can lead to instability, damage, and even accidents.|例句：The captain was warned against overfreighting the vessel, as it could lead to capsizing. 船长被警告不要超载船只，因为这可能导致倾覆。  The airline was fined for overfreighting their planes, resulting in delays and structural damage. 航空公司因超载飞机而被罚款，导致延误和结构损坏。|近义词： overloading, overpacking |反义词： underloading, underpacking |名词
