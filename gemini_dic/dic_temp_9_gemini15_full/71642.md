flatted:/ˈflætɪd/ | adj. | 调低的，降调的；（尤指乐器）调低的 |  Describes a musical note or chord that is lowered in pitch, typically by a half step.  It often refers to instruments that are tuned to a lower pitch than standard. | Example: The piano was flatted a half-step to match the singer's range.  钢琴降半音以配合歌手的音域。 | 近义词：lowered, depressed (in pitch) | 反义词：sharped, raised (in pitch)
