overhonoring: /ˌoʊvərˈɑːnərɪŋ/ | v. | 过度尊敬；过分推崇 |  The act of excessively honoring or respecting someone or something, often to the point of being inappropriate or excessive.  It implies going beyond what is considered appropriate or deserved. |


例句：

His overhonoring of the dictator led to a blind acceptance of his policies. = 他对独裁者的过度尊敬导致了他盲目接受其政策。

The company's overhonoring of its founder stifled innovation and new ideas. = 公司对其创始人的过度推崇扼杀了创新和新思想。


近义词：excessive praise, hyperbole, adulation,  flattery, idolization

反义词：underestimation, disregard, disrespect, slight


词性:动词
