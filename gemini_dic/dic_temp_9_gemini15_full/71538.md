flare:/fleər/| n. vi. vt.|闪光；突然爆发；加剧；(裙子的)喇叭形下摆|a sudden brief burst of bright light or flame; a sudden increase in intensity;  a wide, flaring part at the bottom of a garment.|例句：The fire flared up suddenly. 火突然燃起来了。The skirt has a wide flare. 这条裙子有宽大的喇叭形下摆。|近义词：blaze, burst, intensify, broaden|反义词：dim, fade, lessen, narrow|名词，不及物动词，及物动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sun flares brightly today.  = 今天太阳光线强烈。
The conflict flares up periodically. = 冲突周期性地加剧。


一般过去时:
The candle flared briefly before going out. = 蜡烛短暂地闪了一下然后熄灭了。
His anger flared at the news. = 他听到这个消息后勃然大怒。


现在完成时:
The fire has flared up several times. = 火已经燃起好几次了。
Tensions have flared between the two countries. = 两国之间的紧张关系已经加剧。


过去完成时:
The argument had flared before I arrived. = 我到达之前争吵就已经爆发了。
The disease had flared up again after a period of remission. = 疾病在缓解期后再次复发。


将来时:
The volcano might flare up again soon. = 火山可能很快会再次爆发。
The situation will likely flare in the coming weeks. = 这种情况很可能会在接下来的几周内恶化。


虚拟语气:
If the sun flared, we would be in danger. = 如果太阳爆发，我们将处于危险之中。
I wish the argument hadn't flared so violently. = 我真希望争论没有那么激烈。


被动语态:
The flare was seen from miles away. = 远处都能看到闪光。
The issue was allowed to flare without intervention. = 这个问题在没有干预的情况下被允许加剧。


疑问句:
Did the fire flare up last night? = 昨晚火势是否加剧了？
Will the conflict flare up again? = 冲突还会再次爆发吗？


否定句:
The candle didn't flare at all. = 蜡烛根本没有闪光。
The situation didn't flare up as badly as expected. = 情况并没有像预期的那样严重恶化。


PHRASE->
