hazmat:/ˈhæzmæt/| n. adj.|危险材料；有害物料|Hazardous materials; materials that are dangerous or pose a risk to health, safety, or the environment.  The term is a shortened form of "hazardous materials."|例句：The hazmat team responded to the chemical spill.  危险材料处理小组对化学品泄漏做出了反应。
The hazmat suit protected the worker from the toxic fumes. 防化服保护工人免受有毒烟雾的侵害。|近义词：dangerous goods, hazardous substances|反义词：safe materials, harmless substances|名词，形容词
