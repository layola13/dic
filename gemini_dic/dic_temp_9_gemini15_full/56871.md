dos:/doʊz/| n. |剂量，(复数) 剂量；(计算机) 磁盘操作系统 | a measured portion of medicine or another substance to be taken at one time;  In computing, a family of disk operating systems. | 例句：The doctor prescribed two doses of the medicine a day.  医生开了每天两剂药。 | 近义词：portion, amount, quantity | 反义词：N/A


dos:/doʊz/| v. |给…服药；使…习惯于 | to administer medicine or other substances to; to accustom or habituate. | 例句：The nurse dosed the patient with painkillers. 护士给病人服用了止痛药。| 近义词：administer, give, apply | 反义词：N/A
