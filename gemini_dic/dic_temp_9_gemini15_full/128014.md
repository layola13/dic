nephrotomy:/nɛˈfrɒtəmi/ | n. |肾切开术|Surgical incision into the kidney.  A nephrotomy is a procedure performed to access the kidney for various reasons, such as removing a kidney stone, repairing damage, or draining an abscess. |The surgeon performed a nephrotomy to remove the large kidney stone. 外科医生进行了肾切开术以去除大的肾结石。|renal surgery, incision into the kidney| kidney repair, nephrectomy (反义词，并非完全反义，而是另一种手术)|名词
