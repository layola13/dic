outreason:/ˌaʊtˈriːzən/| vt.|以理服人，胜过…的推理|To defeat or surpass someone in an argument or debate by using superior reasoning.  It implies a victory achieved through logic and evidence.|例句：He completely outreasoned his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中完全以理服人，击败了他的对手。|例句：Despite her emotional appeal, she was outreasoned by the facts presented by the defense. 尽管她感情上很有感染力，但她被辩方提出的事实驳倒了。|近义词：outargue, outwit, overpower (in argument)|反义词：be out-reasoned, be defeated in argument|动词
