proeducation: /ˌproʊˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃən/ | adj. | 支持教育的；促进教育的 |  Supporting or promoting education.  This term is not a standard dictionary entry and is a relatively recent coinage, combining "pro" (in favor of) and "education".  It describes a stance or action that advocates for or actively supports educational initiatives. | Example:  The proeducation policies of the new government have led to increased funding for schools.  新政府的支持教育政策导致学校经费增加。 | 近义词: pro-educational, education-supporting, educationalist | 反义词: anti-education, education-opposing
