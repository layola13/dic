deliberating:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪtɪŋ/| v.|仔细考虑；认真商讨|The act of considering carefully; discussing something thoroughly before making a decision.  It implies a thoughtful and unhurried process.|例句：The committee is deliberating on the proposed budget. 委员会正在仔细考虑拟议的预算。

例句：The jury was deliberating for hours before reaching a verdict. 陪审团在做出裁决之前商讨了数小时。


近义词：considering, pondering, contemplating, debating, discussing


反义词：rushing, deciding hastily, improvising


词性：动词(现在分词形式)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are deliberating the terms of the contract. = 他们正在仔细考虑合同条款。
We are deliberating whether to accept the offer. = 我们正在考虑是否接受这个提议。


一般过去时:
The council deliberated for several hours before voting. = 理事会在投票前讨论了好几个小时。
They deliberated long and hard before making a decision. = 他们经过长时间的认真考虑才做出决定。


现在完成时:
They have been deliberating the matter for days. = 他们已经考虑这件事好几天了。
We have deliberated carefully and reached a conclusion. = 我们已经仔细考虑并得出了结论。


过去完成时:
They had deliberated extensively before presenting their proposal. = 他们在提出建议之前已经进行了广泛的讨论。
She had deliberated for weeks before accepting the job offer. = 在接受这份工作之前，她已经考虑了几个星期。


将来时:
The committee will be deliberating the issue next week. = 委员会下周将讨论这个问题。
They will deliberate the matter further before making a final decision. = 他们会在做出最终决定之前进一步讨论此事。


虚拟语气:
If they were deliberating honestly, they would reach a better solution. = 如果他们诚实地考虑，他们会找到更好的解决方案。
I wish they were deliberating more thoughtfully. = 我希望他们能更认真地考虑。


被动语态:
The matter was being deliberated by the board of directors. = 董事会正在考虑此事。
The proposal is being deliberated by the committee. = 委员会正在审议该提案。


疑问句:
Are they still deliberating? = 他们还在商讨吗？
What are they deliberating about? = 他们在讨论什么？


否定句:
They are not deliberating seriously enough. = 他们考虑得不够认真。
The court did not deliberate before passing judgment. = 法院没有经过考虑就做出了判决。


PHRASE->

deliberate decision = 慎重决定
deliberate falsehood = 蓄意谎言
