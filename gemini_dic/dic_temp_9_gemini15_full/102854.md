jaspilite:/ˈdʒæspəlaɪt/| n. |赤铁矿石，红石英岩|A jaspilte is a sedimentary rock composed primarily of chert and hematite.  It's characterized by its distinctive red, brown, or yellow banding, caused by variations in the concentration of hematite. Jaspilite is often found in Precambrian banded iron formations (BIFs), providing valuable clues about Earth's early atmosphere and geological processes.|The geologist identified the red bands in the rock formation as jaspilte.|近义词：banded iron formation (BIF), hematite rock |反义词：无
