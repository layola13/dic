rainwaters:/ˈreɪnwɔːtərz/| n.|雨水；雨水收集物|Water that has fallen as rain.|The rainwater collected in the barrels was used to water the garden. 雨水收集在桶里用来浇灌花园。|  collected rainwater, precipitation|  dryness|名词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
Rainwater often collects in puddles after a storm. = 暴雨过后，雨水常常积在水坑里。
The rainwater flows into the river. = 雨水流入河中。

一般过去时:
The rainwater damaged the crops last year. = 去年雨水损坏了庄稼。
We collected rainwater in buckets during the heavy downpour. = 大雨倾盆时，我们用桶收集雨水。

现在完成时:
The farmers have used rainwater to irrigate their fields this season. = 农民们本季一直在用雨水灌溉田地。
We have collected enough rainwater for the next few weeks. = 我们已经收集了足够几周使用的雨水。

过去完成时:
The ground was already saturated because it had rained heavily the previous night. = 因为前一天晚上下了大雨，地面已经饱和了。
The well had run dry because the rainwater hadn't replenished it. = 井干涸了，因为雨水没有补充。


将来时:
We will use rainwater harvesting to conserve water. = 我们将利用雨水收集来节约用水。
They will need to find alternative water sources if the rainwater supply is insufficient. = 如果雨水供应不足，他们将需要寻找替代水源。

虚拟语气:
If we had collected rainwater, we would not have run out of water. = 如果我们收集了雨水，就不会缺水了。
I wish we had a system to collect rainwater. = 我希望我们有一个收集雨水的系统。

被动语态:
The garden was watered with rainwater. = 花园是用雨水浇灌的。
Rainwater is often used for irrigation in dry areas. = 雨水经常用于干旱地区的灌溉。

疑问句:
Can rainwater be used for drinking? = 雨水可以饮用吗？
Is the rainwater clean enough for irrigation? = 雨水足够干净用来灌溉吗？

否定句:
The rainwater was not clean enough to drink. = 雨水不够干净，不能饮用。
They did not collect rainwater because they had enough from other sources. = 他们没有收集雨水，因为他们有其他足够的水源。
