fasttrack: /ˈfæsttræːk/ | n., v. | 快速通道；快速提升；快速发展 |  To accelerate the progress of something or someone.  |  例句：The company fast-tracked her to a senior management position. 公司把她快速提升到高级管理职位。  The government is fast-tracking the development of renewable energy. 政府正在快速发展可再生能源。| 近义词：accelerate, expedite, rush | 反义词：delay, hinder, slow down


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fast-tracks his career. = 他快速提升自己的职业生涯。
They fast-track the project. = 他们快速推进这个项目。


一般过去时:
She fast-tracked the application. = 她加快了申请的处理速度。
The company fast-tracked the new product launch. = 公司快速推出了新产品。


现在完成时:
They have fast-tracked several employees this year. = 今年他们已经快速提升了几名员工。
We have fast-tracked the process significantly. = 我们已经大大加快了流程。


过去完成时:
They had fast-tracked the legislation before the election. = 他们在选举前已加快了立法进程。
She had fast-tracked her education to graduate early. = 她为了提前毕业而加快了学习进度。


将来时:
They will fast-track the bill through Congress. = 他们将加快该法案在国会的审批速度。
The company will fast-track the hiring process. = 公司将加快招聘流程。


虚拟语气:
If they fast-tracked the project, it would be finished sooner. = 如果他们加快项目进度，它就能更快完成。
I wish they had fast-tracked my application. = 我希望他们能加快我的申请进程。


被动语态:
The project was fast-tracked by the management team. = 该项目由管理团队快速推进。
He was fast-tracked into a leadership role. = 他被快速提升到领导职位。


疑问句:
Will they fast-track the development? = 他们会加快发展吗？
Was the application fast-tracked? = 申请被加快处理了吗？


否定句:
They did not fast-track the project. = 他们没有加快项目进度。
The company will not fast-track the hiring process. = 公司不会加快招聘流程。
