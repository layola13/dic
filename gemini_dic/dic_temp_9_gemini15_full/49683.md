demita:/dəˈmaɪtə/| n.|减量，减少；小份；(尤指酒的)半品脱|A demita is a small portion or quantity, especially a half-pint of a liquid, such as wine or beer.  The word is also used to refer to a reduction in something.|例句：The chef prepared a demita of the soup for the tasting.  厨师准备了一小份汤用于品尝。|近义词：reduction, decrease, diminution, half-pint|反义词：increase, augmentation
