/mɪstər/ | n. | 先生 | Mr. is an abbreviation for "mister," a title of courtesy used before a man's surname.  It is a formal way of addressing or referring to a man. | Example: Mr. Smith is a kind man. 史密斯先生是一位善良的人。 |近义词:Sir,  |反义词:  |词性:n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Mr. Jones teaches English. = 琼斯先生教英语。
Mr. Brown works at the bank. = 布朗先生在银行工作。

一般过去时:
Mr. Lee visited us last week. = 李先生上周来看望我们。
Mr. Davis helped me with my project. = 戴维斯先生帮我完成了我的项目。

现在完成时:
Mr. Garcia has finished his work. = 加西亚先生已经完成了他的工作。
Mr. Chen has lived here for ten years. = 陈先生在这里住了十年了。

过去完成时:
Mr. Wilson had already left when I arrived. = 我到达的时候，威尔逊先生已经离开了。
Mr. Kim had completed the report before the meeting. = 会议之前，金先生已经完成了报告。

将来时:
Mr. Rodriguez will attend the conference. = 罗德里格斯先生将参加会议。
Mr. Tanaka will be traveling to Japan next month. = 田中先生下个月将去日本旅行。

虚拟语气:
If Mr. Adams were here, he would help us. = 如果亚当斯先生在这里，他会帮助我们。
I wish Mr. Miller were more patient. = 我希望米勒先生更有耐心。

被动语态:
Mr. Johnson was promoted last year. = 约翰逊先生去年被提升了。
The project was completed by Mr. Lee. = 这个项目是由李先生完成的。

疑问句:
Is Mr. Brown at home? = 布朗先生在家吗？
Do you know Mr. Zhang? = 你认识张先生吗？

否定句:
Mr. White is not here today. = 怀特先生今天不在。
Mr. Park did not attend the meeting. = 朴先生没有参加会议。
