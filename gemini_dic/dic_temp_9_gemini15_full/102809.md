jargonesque:/ˌdʒɑːrɡəˈnesk/| adj.|  像行话的；充满行话的；晦涩难懂的 |  Resembling jargon; full of jargon; obscure and difficult to understand. The language is characterized by the use of specialized or technical terminology that is difficult for those outside the field to comprehend.  | The professor's jargonesque lecture was incomprehensible to most of the students.  教授的充满行话的演讲让大多数学生听不懂。 | technical, obscure, convoluted | clear, simple, understandable | 形容词
