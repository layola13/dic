fixed-up:/fɪkst ʌp/| v. |修理好；改善；打扮；安排好 |To repair, improve, or decorate something; to arrange or settle something.  |例句：He fixed up the old car. 他修好了那辆旧车。
|例句：They fixed up a meeting between the two parties. 他们安排了两方会面。|近义词：repair, improve, renovate, arrange, settle|反义词：break, damage, destroy, disarrange, disrupt|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They fix up the house every year. = 他们每年都修理房子。
He fixes up old bikes and sells them. = 他修理旧自行车然后卖掉。

一般过去时:
She fixed up the broken chair. = 她修好了坏掉的椅子。
We fixed up a deal with the supplier. = 我们和供应商达成了协议。

现在完成时:
They have fixed up the leaky roof. = 他们已经修好了漏水的屋顶。
She has fixed up a date with Tom. = 她已经和汤姆约好了时间。

过去完成时:
He had fixed up the engine before the race. = 他在比赛前已经修好了引擎。
They had fixed up a meeting room before the conference. = 他们会议前已经准备好会议室。

将来时:
I will fix up the computer tomorrow. = 我明天会修理电脑。
We will fix up a time to meet next week. = 我们下周会安排时间见面。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would fix up the garden. = 如果我有更多时间，我会修理花园。
I wish I had fixed up the problem earlier. = 我希望我早点解决这个问题。

被动语态:
The old house is being fixed up. = 老房子正在被修理。
The meeting was fixed up quickly. = 会议很快就被安排好了。

疑问句:
Have you fixed up the broken window? = 你修好了坏掉的窗户了吗？
Will they fix up the problem soon? = 他们会很快解决这个问题吗？

否定句:
I didn't fix up the bike yet. = 我还没修好自行车。
They haven't fixed up a meeting time. = 他们还没安排好会议时间。


PHRASE->
fix up a date: 约会
fix up a deal: 达成协议
fix up a problem: 解决问题
fix up a meeting: 安排会议
fix up someone:  帮助某人 (通常指在困境中)
