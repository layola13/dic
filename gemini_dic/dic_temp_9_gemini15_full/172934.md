qualified:/ˈkwɒlɪfaɪd/ /ˈkwɑːlɪfaɪd/| adj.|合格的，胜任的，有资格的|Having the necessary skills, knowledge, or qualifications to do something.|He is a qualified doctor. 他是一位合格的医生。|近义词：competent, eligible, capable|反义词：ineligible, unqualified, incompetent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She is qualified for the job. = 她胜任这份工作。
He is a qualified teacher. = 他是一位合格的老师。


一般过去时:
The candidate was qualified for the position. = 候选人符合该职位的资格。
They were qualified engineers. = 他们是合格的工程师。


现在完成时:
She has qualified as a lawyer. = 她已取得律师资格。
He has qualified for the Olympics. = 他已获得奥运会参赛资格。


过去完成时:
He had qualified for the next round before the final match. = 在决赛之前，他已经获得了下一轮的资格。
She had qualified as a doctor many years ago. = 多年前她就取得了医生的资格。


将来时:
He will qualify for the next level soon. = 他很快就会晋级到下一级。
She will be qualified to teach after she finishes her training. = 她完成培训后将有资格执教。


虚拟语气:
If he were qualified, he would apply for the job. = 如果他有资格，他会申请这份工作。
If she had qualified earlier, she would have had a better chance. = 如果她早点获得资格，她就会有更好的机会。


被动语态:
He was qualified by the board. = 他获得了董事会的资格认证。
The candidates are qualified by the committee. = 候选人由委员会进行资格审查。


疑问句:
Is she qualified to drive a bus? = 她有资格驾驶公共汽车吗？
Are they qualified for this project? = 他们有资格参与这个项目吗？


否定句:
He is not qualified for the position. = 他不符合该职位的资格。
She was not qualified to give medical advice. = 她没有资格提供医疗建议。


PHRASE->
highly qualified = 高度胜任的

suitably qualified = 合适合格的
professionally qualified = 专业合格的
