precontrols:/ˌpriːkənˈtrəʊlz/| n.|预先控制，预控|Controls that are implemented before a process begins.  These controls aim to prevent problems from occurring in the first place.  They are often used in risk management and quality control.|例句：The factory implemented strict precontrols on raw materials to ensure high-quality products.  工厂对原材料实施了严格的预先控制以确保产品的高质量。

The company's precontrols minimized potential safety hazards. 这家公司的预先控制措施最大限度地减少了潜在的安全隐患。|近义词：preventive measures, safeguards, precautions|反义词：post-controls, reactive measures|名词
