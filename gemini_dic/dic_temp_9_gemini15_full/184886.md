retails:/ˈriːteɪlz/| v.|零售，转述|To sell goods to the public in relatively small quantities.|例句：That shop retails a wide variety of goods. 那家商店零售各种各样的商品。|例句：He retailed the gossip he'd heard. 他转述了他听到的小道消息。|近义词：sells, distributes, peddles|反义词：wholesales


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store retails clothing and accessories. = 这家商店零售服装和配饰。
He retails the latest news to his friends. = 他向朋友们转述最新的消息。

一般过去时:
The company retailed its products through online channels. = 公司通过在线渠道零售其产品。
She retailed the story to everyone she met. = 她向遇到的每一个人转述了这个故事。

现在完成时:
They have retailed organic food for over five years. = 他们已经零售有机食品五年多了。
He has retailed many interesting anecdotes throughout his career. = 他在他的职业生涯中转述了许多有趣轶事。

过去完成时:
Before the pandemic, they had retailed their products in physical stores. = 大流行之前，他们已经在实体店零售他们的产品了。
She had retailed the secret to a few close friends before it was made public. = 在秘密公开之前，她已经向几个密友转述了这个秘密。

将来时:
We will retail our new product line next month. = 我们下个月将零售我们的新产品线。
He will retail the information to the relevant authorities. = 他将把信息转述给相关部门。

虚拟语气:
If they retailed their products at a lower price, they would sell more. = 如果他们以更低的价格零售他们的产品，他们就会卖出更多。
I wish I hadn't retailed that sensitive information. = 我真希望我没有转述那个敏感信息。

被动语态:
The products are retailed nationwide. = 这些产品在全国范围内零售。
The news was retailed quickly throughout the community. = 这个消息很快就在社区里传开了。

疑问句:
Does this store retail organic produce? = 这家商店零售有机农产品吗？
Did he retail the information to anyone else? = 他有没有把信息转述给其他人？

否定句:
The company does not retail its products directly to consumers. = 这家公司不直接向消费者零售其产品。
She didn't retail the rumor, she kept it to herself. = 她没有转述那个谣言，她把它烂在了肚子里。
