centripetalism:/ˌsɛntriˈpiːtəlɪzəm/| n.|向心主义，趋中主义|The tendency of a political system to concentrate power in a central authority or location.|例句：The rise of centripetalism in the nation led to the suppression of regional autonomy.  向心主义在国家的兴起导致了对地区自治的压制。|近义词：centralization, unification|反义词：decentralization, fragmentation|
