procreating:/proʊˈkreɪtɪŋ/ | v. |生育，繁殖|The act of producing offspring.  It involves the biological process of creating new individuals of a species.  This can refer to humans, animals, or plants. |例句：Many couples are choosing to delay procreating until they are financially stable. 许多夫妇选择等到经济稳定后再生育。

例句：The rapid procreating of rabbits led to an overpopulation problem. 兔子迅速繁殖导致了人口过剩问题。


近义词：reproducing, breeding, multiplying, generating


反义词：sterilizing, preventing reproduction


词性:动词
