jerrymander:/ˈdʒɛriˌmændər/ | n. v. | 杰利蝾螈式划分选区；人为操纵选区划分 | To manipulate the boundaries of (an electoral constituency) so as to favor one party or class.  | The governor's party gerrymandered the state's congressional districts to maintain their power. 州长所在的政党为了维持其权力，对州内的国会选区进行了杰利蝾螈式划分。 |  redistricting, manipulation | fair apportionment | 名词，动词
