monopolizer:/məˈnɒpəlaɪzər/ | n. |垄断者，独占者 | A person or entity that controls or dominates a particular market or industry to the exclusion of others.  |例句：The company was accused of being a ruthless monopolizer, driving smaller businesses into bankruptcy. 这家公司被指控为冷酷无情的垄断者，将小型企业逼入破产。

例句：He became a monopolizer of the local fruit market through aggressive pricing and strategic acquisitions. 他通过积极的定价和战略收购，成为当地水果市场的垄断者。


近义词：monopolist, dominator, controller

反义词：competitor, sharer


词性: 名词
