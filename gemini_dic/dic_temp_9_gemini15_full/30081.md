/siːˈdiːz/ | n. | 光盘；压缩光盘 | Compact discs are a popular way to store and play music.  光盘是存储和播放音乐的流行方式。| 近义词：optical disc | 反义词：digital download


USAGE->

一般现在时:
CDs are used to store music. = CD 用于存储音乐。
He listens to CDs in his car. = 他在车里听CD。

一般过去时:
I bought some CDs at the store yesterday. = 我昨天在商店买了一些CD。
She played her favorite CDs last night. = 她昨晚播放了她最喜欢的CD。

现在完成时:
I have collected many CDs over the years. = 多年来我收集了很多CD。
She has listened to that CD countless times. = 她已经听过那张CD无数次了。

过去完成时:
I had already listened to the CD before he asked me about it. = 在他问我之前我已经听过这张CD了。
They had burned all the CDs before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前他们已经烧掉了所有的CD。

将来时:
I will buy a new CD tomorrow. = 我明天会买一张新的CD。
She will play the CD at the party. = 她将在派对上播放这张CD。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would buy that limited edition CD. = 如果我有钱，我会买那张限量版CD。
I wish I had bought that CD when it was on sale. = 我真希望我在打折的时候买了那张CD。

被动语态:
The CDs were damaged in the accident. = CD在事故中受损了。
The music was copied from a CD. = 音乐是从CD复制的。

疑问句:
Do you have any CDs? = 你有CD吗？
Where did you get those CDs? = 你在哪儿买的那些CD？

否定句:
I don't have any CDs. = 我没有CD。
He didn't play the CD. = 他没有播放CD。
