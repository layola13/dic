patchworker:/ˈpætʃˌwɜːrkər/| n. |拼凑者；修补匠；（尤指）拼布者 |A patchworker is someone who mends things by patching them, especially someone who makes patchwork quilts.  This term often carries a connotation of creating something new and beautiful from disparate parts. | Example:  She's a skilled patchworker, creating stunning quilts from scraps of fabric. 她是一位技艺精湛的拼布者，用布头制作出令人惊艳的被子。|近义词：mender, repairer, quilter |反义词：None specifically, but words implying creating something entirely new could be considered antonyms in a certain context. |名词
