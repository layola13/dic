follow-up: /ˈfɑːloʊ ʌp/ /ˈfɒləʊ ʌp/ | n., v. |后续行动，跟踪；后续 |To continue or repeat something that has already started, often to check on its progress or to ensure that it is completed successfully.  |例句：We need a follow-up meeting to discuss the next steps. 我们需要举行一次后续会议来讨论接下来的步骤。

例句：I'll send a follow-up email to confirm the appointment. 我会发送一封后续邮件来确认预约。


近义词：continuation, sequel, follow-on, subsequent action | 反义词：preceding action, initial action


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company follows up on all customer complaints. = 公司会跟进所有客户的投诉。
She follows up on every lead she gets. = 她会跟踪她得到的每一个线索。

一般过去时:
He followed up on the interview with a thank-you note. = 他在面试后发了一封感谢信作为后续。
They followed up on the investigation and found new evidence. = 他们继续调查并发现了新的证据。

现在完成时:
We have followed up on several promising leads. = 我们已经跟踪了几条有希望的线索。
She has followed up with the client several times. = 她已经几次与客户进行了后续联系。

过去完成时:
They had followed up with the supplier before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已与供应商进行了后续联系。
She had followed up on the project before leaving for vacation. = 她休假前已对项目进行了后续处理。

将来时:
I will follow up with you next week. = 我下周会和你联系。
They will follow up on the application process. = 他们将跟踪申请流程。

虚拟语气:
If I had followed up sooner, I might have gotten the job. = 如果我早点跟进，我可能就得到这份工作了。
I wish I had followed up on that opportunity. = 我真希望我当时跟进了那个机会。

被动语态:
The issue will be followed up by the manager. = 这个问题将由经理跟进。
All complaints are followed up within 24 hours. = 所有投诉将在24小时内得到跟进。

疑问句:
Will you follow up on this matter? = 你会跟进这件事吗？
Have they followed up on the results of the survey? = 他们有没有跟踪调查结果？

否定句:
They did not follow up on the request. = 他们没有跟进请求。
I will not follow up with him again. = 我不会再和他联系了。
