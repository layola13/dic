invect:/ɪnˈvɛkt/| vt.|痛骂；猛烈抨击|To utter vehement censure or invective; to attack or denounce vehemently.|例句：The senator invected against the proposed tax increase. 参议员猛烈抨击拟议中的税收增加。

例句：He invected against the government's handling of the crisis. 他痛骂政府处理危机的措施。
