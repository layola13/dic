sellar:/ˈsɛlə(r)/| n.|马鞍;鞍座;（尤指）自行车鞍座|A saddle, especially for a bicycle.  A sellar is a seat, typically for riding an animal, but the term is most commonly used today to refer to bicycle saddles. |例句：He adjusted the sellar on his bicycle before the race. 他在比赛前调整了他的自行车鞍座。

例句：The old sellar was worn and cracked from years of use.  旧马鞍因多年的使用而磨损开裂。


近义词：saddle, seat


反义词：无


词性：名词
