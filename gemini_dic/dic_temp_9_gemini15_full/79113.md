gladding:/ˈɡlædɪŋ/| v.|使高兴；使快乐|To make someone happy or joyful.  It often implies a more active and deliberate act of bringing joy than simply "making happy".|例句：The good news gladdened her heart. 好消息使她心花怒放。|例句：The children's laughter gladdened the old woman's day. 孩子们的笑声让这位老妇人一天都很快乐。|近义词：delighting, cheering, elating, pleasing|反义词：saddening, depressing, disheartening|动词
