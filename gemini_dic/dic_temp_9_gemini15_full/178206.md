recommendation:/ˌrekəmenˈdeɪʃən/ | n. | 推荐；建议 |  A recommendation is a suggestion or advice given to someone, often about a course of action, a product, or a person. It can be formal or informal.  |  The doctor's recommendation was to rest for a week. 医生建议休息一周。  |  suggestion, proposal, advice |  criticism, objection | noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gives many recommendations to his students. 他给他的学生很多建议。
The committee makes several recommendations for improvement. 委员会提出了一些改进建议。


一般过去时:
She received a strong recommendation from her previous employer. 她从之前的雇主那里得到了一封强烈的推荐信。
The board gave its recommendation after careful consideration. 董事会在仔细考虑后给出了建议。


现在完成时:
The company has received numerous recommendations for new products.  公司收到了许多关于新产品的建议。
He has made several recommendations to improve the efficiency of the department. 他已经提出了一些提高部门效率的建议。


过去完成时:
They had already given their recommendation before the meeting began. 会议开始前他们已经给出了建议。
The committee had made its recommendation based on extensive research. 委员会根据广泛的研究做出了建议。


将来时:
The report will include specific recommendations for future action. 报告将包含关于未来行动的具体建议。
The manager will make a recommendation to the board at the next meeting. 经理将在下次会议上向董事会提出建议。


虚拟语气:
I would recommend that you seek professional advice. 我建议你寻求专业建议。
If I were you, I would follow his recommendation. 如果我是你，我会听从他的建议。


被动语态:
Her application was highly recommended by her professor. 她的申请得到了教授的高度推荐。
The changes were recommended by the committee. 这些改变是委员会建议的。


疑问句:
Do you have any recommendations for restaurants in this area? 你对这个地区的餐馆有什么推荐吗？
What is your recommendation for solving this problem? 你对解决这个问题有什么建议？


否定句:
I don't have any recommendations for you at this time. 我现在没有给你任何建议。
The committee did not recommend any changes to the policy. 委员会没有建议对政策进行任何更改。
