relapsed:/rɪˈlæpst/| v.|复发；故态复萌|To fall back into a previous condition, typically a bad or undesirable one, after a period of improvement.|例句：He relapsed into his old bad habits after a period of sobriety. 他戒酒一段时间后又故态复萌了。|例句：After months of recovery, she relapsed into depression. 康复数月后，她又陷入了抑郁。|近义词：recur, revert, backslide|反义词：recover, improve


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He relapses into depression every winter. 他每年冬天都会复发抑郁症。
She relapses into old habits whenever she's stressed.  她每当压力大的时候就会故态复萌。


一般过去时:
He relapsed into drug addiction last year. 他去年吸毒复吸了。
She relapsed after a promising start to her recovery. 她在一开始恢复的迹象良好之后复发了。


现在完成时:
He has relapsed several times since his initial recovery. 自从他第一次康复以来，他已经复发过好几次了。
She hasn't relapsed since her last treatment. 自从她上次治疗以来就没有复发了。


过去完成时:
He had relapsed before he even started therapy. 他甚至在开始治疗之前就已经复发了。
She had relapsed several times before finally seeking help.  在她最终寻求帮助之前，她已经复发过好几次了。


将来时:
He fears he will relapse if he stops taking his medication. 他担心如果停止服用药物，他会复发。
She is worried that she might relapse under pressure. 她担心自己会在压力下复发。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't relapsed, he would be doing much better now. 如果他没有复发，他现在会好得多。
I wish she hadn't relapsed; it's so disheartening. 我真希望她没有复发；这太令人沮丧了。


被动语态:
His recovery was reported to have been successful, but he later relapsed. 据报道他的康复很成功，但他后来复发了。(被动语态在此处应用较少，通常用主动语态)
It is feared that many patients relapse after treatment.  人们担心许多患者治疗后会复发。(被动语态在此处应用较少，通常用主动语态)


疑问句:
Has he relapsed again? 他又复发了？
Did she relapse after leaving the rehab center? 她离开康复中心后复发了？


否定句:
He hasn't relapsed since his last treatment. 他自从上次治疗后就没有复发了。
She didn't relapse, despite the challenges she faced.  尽管她面临挑战，但她并没有复发。



PHRASE->
relapse into addiction  复吸成瘾

relapse into old habits  故态复萌
relapse prevention  复发预防
risk of relapse  复发风险
