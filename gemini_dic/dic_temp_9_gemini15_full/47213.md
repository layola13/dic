daysman:/ˈdeɪzmən/| n. |仲裁人，调解人 |A daysman is a person who settles disputes or acts as an arbitrator, especially in a legal or religious context.  The term often implies a neutral third party who helps resolve conflict peacefully.  It carries a sense of fairness and impartiality. |Example: The two warring factions finally agreed to let a daysman decide their fate.  两个交战派系最终同意让一位仲裁人决定他们的命运。|近义词：arbitrator, mediator, referee, judge |反义词：provocateur, agitator
