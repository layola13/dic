laundered:/ˈlɔːndərd/| adj.|洗过的；漂白的；（尤指非法所得）洗钱的 | washed (especially money obtained illegally) to make it seem legitimate.  |例句：The laundered money was used to buy a luxury yacht. 洗钱后获得的钱被用来购买豪华游艇。

The laundered shirts were crisp and clean. 洗过的衬衫又脆又干净。|近义词：cleaned, purified, bleached (for clothing); cleaned, legitimized (for money)|反义词：dirty, soiled, tainted (for clothing); illicit, illegal (for money)|
