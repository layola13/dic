monoclonal:/ˌmɒnəʊˈkləʊnəl/| adj.| 单克隆的；单株的 |  Relating to or denoting antibodies produced by a single clone of cells and consisting of identical antibody molecules.  Monoclonal antibodies are used in medical diagnostics and therapeutics. | 例句：Monoclonal antibodies are a powerful tool in cancer treatment. 单克隆抗体是癌症治疗中的一种强有力工具。
例句：The research focused on producing monoclonal antibodies specific to the target protein. 研究重点是生产针对目标蛋白的单克隆抗体。


词源:
mono- (single) + clonal (relating to clones)


近义词:
monospecific (in the context of antibodies)


反义词:
polyclonal (referring to antibodies produced by multiple clones of cells)


USAGE->
There is no grammatical tense conjugation for adjectives.  The usage of "monoclonal" is always descriptive.
