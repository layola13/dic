skiing:/ˈskiːɪŋ/| n.|滑雪|The sport or activity of gliding over snow on skis.|例句：He enjoys skiing in the Alps. 他喜欢在阿尔卑斯山滑雪。|近义词：snowboarding, sledding|反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I ski every weekend. = 我每个周末都滑雪。
She skis with great skill. = 她滑雪技术很高超。


一般过去时:
We skied in the mountains last winter. = 我们去年冬天在山上滑雪。
He skied down the slope very fast. = 他以很快的速度滑下山坡。


现在完成时:
I have skied several times this season. = 这个季节我已经滑雪几次了。
They have skied all over the world. = 他们已经在世界各地滑雪了。


过去完成时:
I had skied before, so I wasn't nervous. = 我以前滑过雪，所以我并不紧张。
She had skied that slope many times before. = 她以前滑过那条雪道很多次了。


将来时:
We will ski in Colorado next year. = 我们明年将在科罗拉多州滑雪。
They will be skiing in Switzerland next month. = 下个月他们将在瑞士滑雪。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would go skiing. = 如果我有更多时间，我会去滑雪。
I wish I were skiing in the mountains right now. = 我真希望我现在正在山上滑雪。


被动语态:
The ski resort was closed due to heavy snow. = 滑雪胜地因大雪关闭。
The slopes are often groomed before the season starts. = 滑雪道通常在赛季开始前进行整理。


疑问句:
Do you like skiing? = 你喜欢滑雪吗？
Have you ever been skiing? = 你曾经滑过雪吗？


否定句:
I don't ski very often. = 我不经常滑雪。
She didn't go skiing because of the bad weather. = 因为天气不好，她没有去滑雪。
