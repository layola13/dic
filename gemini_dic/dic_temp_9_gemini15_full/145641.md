overcontentedness: /ˌoʊvər kənˈtɛntɪdnəs/ | n. | 过度满足；自满；自鸣得意 |  A state of excessive satisfaction with oneself or one's achievements, often to the point of complacency and lack of motivation. It implies a feeling of being too comfortable and resistant to change or improvement. |  His overcontentedness prevented him from seeking new challenges.  他的过度满足阻止了他寻求新的挑战。  The company's overcontentedness led to its eventual downfall. 公司的过度自满导致了它最终的衰落。 | complacency, self-satisfaction, smugness | dissatisfaction, ambition, drive | 名词
