nonprospect:/ˈnɑːnˌprɒspekt/| n. |非潜在客户，没有希望的客户|A nonprospect is someone who is not considered a potential customer or client.  They are unlikely to ever become a customer, due to factors such as lack of need, lack of budget, or lack of interest. |Example:  The salesperson quickly identified the visitor as a nonprospect and moved on to other potential clients.  销售人员很快认出访客并非潜在客户，然后转向其他潜在客户。|近义词：unqualified lead, unsuitable candidate |反义词：prospect, potential customer
