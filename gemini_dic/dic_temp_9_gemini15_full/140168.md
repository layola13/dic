offension:/əˈfɛnʃən/| n.|冒犯；得罪；引起反感的事物|The act of causing offense or the state of being offended; something that causes offense.  It often implies a deliberate act, though not necessarily.  The degree of offense can vary greatly.|例句：His remarks caused great offense to many people. = 他的话冒犯了很多人。

His constant complaining was a source of offense to everyone. = 他不断的抱怨让每个人都很反感。|近义词：insult, affront, slight, transgression|反义词：compliment, praise, approval
