payn:/peɪn/| n.|痛苦，疼痛；努力，辛劳；惩罚 | Pain is an unpleasant sensation.疼痛是一种令人不快的感觉。The pain in his leg was unbearable. 他腿上的疼痛难以忍受。|近义词：ache, suffering, agony |反义词：pleasure, comfort, ease |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wound causes me pain. = 伤口使我疼痛。
She feels a sharp pain in her chest. = 她感到胸部一阵剧痛。


一般过去时:
I felt a lot of pain after the surgery. = 手术后我感到剧烈的疼痛。
He endured much pain during his illness. = 他在生病期间忍受了很多痛苦。


现在完成时:
She has experienced considerable pain in her life. = 她一生经历过相当多的痛苦。
I have suffered from back pain for years. = 我多年来一直受背痛的折磨。


过去完成时:
He had already felt the pain before the doctor arrived. = 在医生到达之前他已经感到疼痛了。
She had endured years of pain before seeking treatment. = 在寻求治疗之前，她忍受了多年的痛苦。


将来时:
I'm afraid I will feel pain after the injection. = 我担心注射后我会感到疼痛。
The surgery will cause some pain, but it will be temporary. = 手术会引起一些疼痛，但它是暂时的。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the procedure would cause so much pain, I wouldn't have done it. = 如果我知道这个手术会引起这么大的痛苦，我不会做的。
I wish I hadn't felt so much pain yesterday. = 我真希望昨天没有那么痛苦。


被动语态:
The pain was relieved by the medication. = 疼痛被药物缓解了。
The patient's pain was carefully monitored. = 病人的疼痛被仔细监测。


疑问句:
What kind of pain are you experiencing? = 你正在经历什么样的疼痛？
Is there anything I can do to ease your pain? = 我能做些什么来减轻你的痛苦吗？


否定句:
He felt no pain after the operation. = 手术后他没有感到任何疼痛。
She didn't want to show her pain to others. = 她不想向别人展示她的痛苦。


PHRASE->
