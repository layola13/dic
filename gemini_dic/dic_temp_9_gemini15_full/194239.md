self-medications:/ˌsɛlfˌmɛdəˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |自我治疗；自行用药|The act of treating oneself for an illness or condition without consulting a medical professional.  This often involves using over-the-counter medications or other remedies.  Self-medication can be risky and may delay proper medical treatment.|例句：Self-medications with over-the-counter painkillers can lead to dependence.  自行服用非处方止痛药会导致依赖。

例句：He resorted to self-medications to cope with his anxiety. 他依靠自我治疗来应对焦虑。

近义词：self-treatment, home remedies

反义词：professional medical treatment, doctor's care

词性:名词
