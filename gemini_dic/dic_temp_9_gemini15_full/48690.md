defiable:/dɪˈfaɪəbəl/ | adj. | 可定义的；可反抗的；可蔑视的 | Capable of being defined or explained; capable of being resisted or defied; capable of being scorned or treated with contempt.  | The terms of the contract were clearly definable. 合同条款明确可定义。 The government's policies were highly definable. 政府的政策是高度可定义的。|近义词：definable, describable, explicable | 反义词：undefinable, inexplicable
