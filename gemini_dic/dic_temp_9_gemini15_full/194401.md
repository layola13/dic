self-punisher: /ˈsɛlf ˈpʌnɪʃər/ | n. |自我惩罚者，自我虐待者 | A person who inflicts punishment on themselves, often as a form of self-discipline, self-hate, or coping mechanism.  This can manifest in various ways, including physical self-harm, restrictive diets, or compulsive behaviors. | Example: He was a self-punisher, constantly berating himself for perceived failures.  他是一个自我惩罚者，不断地责备自己犯下的错误。  |近义词：self-flagellator, masochist |反义词：self-lover, self-care advocate |
