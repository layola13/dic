okaying:/oʊˈkeɪɪŋ/ | v. | 批准；认可；同意 | The manager is okaying the new budget.  经理正在批准新的预算。 |近义词：approving, sanctioning, authorizing | 反义词：rejecting, disapproving, vetoing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is okaying the project proposal. = 他正在批准项目提案。
They are okaying the new design. = 他们正在认可新的设计。

一般过去时:
She okayed the request for leave. = 她批准了休假申请。
The committee okayed the changes to the policy. = 委员会批准了对政策的更改。

现在完成时:
They have okayed the final version of the report. = 他们已经批准了报告的最终版本。
He has okayed the payment. = 他已经批准了付款。


过去完成时:
She had okayed the contract before leaving for the meeting. = 她在去开会前已批准了合同。
They had okayed the budget before the fiscal year ended. = 他们在财政年度结束前已批准了预算。

将来时:
The board will okay the merger next week. = 董事会下周将批准合并。
He will okay the document once he reviews it. = 他审查文件后会批准它。


虚拟语气:
If the boss okayed the plan, we would proceed. = 如果老板批准了计划，我们就将继续进行。
I wish the bank would okay my loan application. = 我希望银行能批准我的贷款申请。

被动语态:
The proposal was okayed by the board. = 该提案得到了董事会的批准。
The application is being okayed by the department. = 该申请正在由部门审批。

疑问句:
Has the client okayed the design yet? = 客户是否已经批准了设计？
Will they okay the budget increase? = 他们会批准预算增加吗？

否定句:
He didn't okay the purchase order. = 他没有批准采购订单。
The committee did not okay the proposed changes. = 委员会没有批准拟议的更改。
