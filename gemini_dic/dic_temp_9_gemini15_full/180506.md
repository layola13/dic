regenerator:/ˈriːdʒɛnəreɪtər/| n. |再生器；修复器；更新器 |A regenerator is a device or system that restores or revitalizes something, often by recovering and reusing energy or materials.  It can refer to a variety of devices depending on the context, including those used in heat exchangers, gas turbines, and biological systems. | Example: The power plant uses a regenerator to recapture waste heat and improve efficiency.  再生发电厂使用再生器来回收余热并提高效率。|近义词：renovator, restorer, reviver |反义词：destructor, destroyer

词源:
The word "regenerator" comes from the Latin prefix "re-" (again) and "generare" (to produce, beget, generate).  It literally means "to produce again" or "to create anew," reflecting its function of restoring or renewing something.
