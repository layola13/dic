shonen:/ˈʃoʊnən/| n.|少年；少年漫画|A shonen is a young boy, typically in his pre-teens or early teens.  The term is also used to describe a genre of Japanese manga and anime targeted towards young boys.|例句：He's a shonen brimming with energy. 他是一个充满活力的少年。|例句：My brother loves reading shonen manga. 我弟弟喜欢看少年漫画。|近义词：boy, lad, youth|反义词：adult, man
