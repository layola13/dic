self-surrenders: /ˌsɛlf səˈrɛndərz/ | v. | 自首；自动投降 | To give oneself up to the authorities; to surrender oneself voluntarily.  |例句：The suspect self-surrendered to the police after a week on the run.  嫌疑犯潜逃一周后向警方自首。

The terrorist group self-surrendered to the United Nations peacekeeping forces.  恐怖组织向联合国维和部队自首。| 近义词：surrender, give oneself up, turn oneself in | 反义词：resist, fight, flee

被动语态:  (This verb is rarely used in the passive voice because it implies a voluntary action.)  这个动词很少用被动语态，因为它暗示了一个自愿的行为。
