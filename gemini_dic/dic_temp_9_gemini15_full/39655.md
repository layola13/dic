conservative:/kənˈsɜːrvətɪv/|/ˈkɑːnsɜːrvətɪv/| adj., n. |保守的；保守派|

英文解析:
(adj.)  Characterized by or believing in the principle of conserving or preserving existing structures and institutions; tending to oppose change.  Relating to or denoting a political philosophy or party that favors tradition and gradual development.  (n.) A person who is conservative in their political or social views.


例句:
He holds conservative views on social issues.  = 他在社会问题上持有保守的观点。

She is a member of the Conservative Party. = 她是保守党的一员。


近义词:
traditional, traditionalist, orthodox, conventional, prudent, cautious


反义词:
liberal, progressive, radical, revolutionary, avant-garde


词性:
adjective, noun
