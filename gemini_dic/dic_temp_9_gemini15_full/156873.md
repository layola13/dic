pigtails:/ˈpɪɡteɪlz/| n.|辫子，羊角辫|Two pigtails are a hairstyle where the hair is divided into two sections and braided or tied into a ponytail at the back of the head.  They are often associated with young girls.|例句：The little girl wore her pigtails tied with bright pink ribbons. 小女孩扎着亮粉色蝴蝶结的羊角辫。|例句：She had long, blonde pigtails that bounced as she ran. 她留着长长的金发辫子，跑起来一蹦一跳的。|近义词：braids, plaits|反义词：loose hair, unbound hair
