offsprings:/ˈɔːfsprɪŋ/ | n. | 子孙，后代 | The descendants of a person, animal, or plant.  |例句：The old woman was surrounded by her numerous offspring.  这位老妇人被她众多的子孙围绕着。

例句：The apple tree bore a plentiful offspring this year. 这棵苹果树今年结了很多果实。


近义词：progeny, descendants, children | 反义词：ancestors, predecessors | 词性：名词
