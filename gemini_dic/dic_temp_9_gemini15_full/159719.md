polychroism:/ˈpɒliˌkroʊɪzəm/| n. |多色性，多色现象|The property of appearing different colours when viewed in different directions, especially of certain crystals.  This is due to the different absorption of light along different crystallographic axes.|例句：The mineral exhibited striking polychroism, shifting from deep blue to emerald green depending on the viewing angle.  这种矿物表现出显著的多色性，根据视角的不同，颜色从深蓝色变为翠绿色。  |近义词：pleochroism|反义词：monochroism|
