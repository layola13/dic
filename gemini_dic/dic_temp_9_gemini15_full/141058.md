on/off:/ɒn/ /ɔːf/| adj. adv.|开/关；接通/断开；上演/下演 |On means in operation or switched on; off means not in operation or switched off.  It can also refer to a performance being on or off. |例句：The lights are on. 灯亮着。 The radio is off. 收音机关掉了。|近义词：on: active, engaged; off: inactive, disengaged |反义词：on: off; off: on


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The light is on. = 灯亮着。
The TV is off. = 电视关着。

一般过去时:
The power was on all night. = 整个晚上电力都供应着。
The show was off due to bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，演出取消了。


现在完成时:
The lights have been on for hours. = 灯已经亮了好几个小时了。
The machine has been off since yesterday. = 机器从昨天开始就关着了。


过去完成时:
The engine had been off for a while before we restarted it. = 在我们重新启动之前，引擎已经关闭了一段时间。
The play had already been off when we arrived. = 我们到达时，戏剧已经结束。


将来时:
The concert will be on next week. = 音乐会下周举行。
The system will be off for maintenance. = 系统将关闭进行维护。


虚拟语气:
If the switch were on, the machine would work. = 如果开关打开，机器就会工作。
I wish the TV were off; the noise is disturbing. = 我希望电视关掉；噪音很烦人。


被动语态:
The lights were turned on by the staff. = 灯是由工作人员打开的。
The system was switched off automatically. = 系统自动关闭了。


疑问句:
Is the oven on? = 烤箱开着吗？
Is the computer off? = 电脑关着吗？


否定句:
The engine isn't on. = 引擎没有启动。
The phone is not off; it's ringing. = 电话没有关机；它正在响铃。
