plaintiff:/ˈpleɪntɪf/| n. |原告，起诉人 |The plaintiff in the lawsuit claimed damages for breach of contract. |近义词：complainant, claimant |反义词：defendant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plaintiff files a lawsuit. = 原告提起诉讼。
The plaintiff presents evidence. = 原告提交证据。


一般过去时:
The plaintiff filed a lawsuit last week. = 原告上周提起诉讼。
The plaintiff testified in court yesterday. = 原告昨天在法庭上作证。


现在完成时:
The plaintiff has presented all her evidence. = 原告已经提交了她所有的证据。
The plaintiff has been waiting for a decision for months. = 原告已经等待判决数月了。


过去完成时:
The plaintiff had already submitted the documents before the hearing. = 在听证会之前，原告已经提交了文件。
The plaintiff had prepared her case thoroughly before trial. = 原告在审判前已充分准备了她的案情。


将来时:
The plaintiff will present her closing arguments tomorrow. = 原告将于明天陈述她的结案陈词。
The plaintiff will appeal the decision if she loses. = 如果原告败诉，她将上诉。


虚拟语气:
If the plaintiff were to win, she would receive a large settlement. = 如果原告胜诉，她将获得一大笔赔偿金。
I wish the plaintiff had more evidence to support her claim. = 我希望原告有更多证据来支持她的主张。


被动语态:
The case was filed by the plaintiff. = 此案由原告提起。
The plaintiff's claims are being reviewed by the court. = 法院正在审查原告的主张。


疑问句:
Who is the plaintiff in this case? = 此案的原告是谁？
What are the plaintiff's demands? = 原告的要求是什么？


否定句:

The plaintiff's claim was not accepted by the court. = 法院没有接受原告的主张。
