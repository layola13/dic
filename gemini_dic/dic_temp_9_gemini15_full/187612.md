romped:/rɒmpt/| /rɑːmpt/ | v. |嬉戏；奔跑；轻松获胜 |To play boisterously; to run or move in a lively manner; to win easily.  |例句：The children romped in the park.孩子们在公园里嬉戏。 The home team romped to victory.主队轻松获胜。|近义词：frolic, gambol, romp around,canter,  gallop |反义词：trudge, creep, crawl


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They romp in the garden every afternoon. = 他们每天下午都在花园里嬉戏。
The dog romps happily with its owner. = 这只狗和它的主人快乐地嬉戏。

一般过去时:
The children romped on the beach all day. = 孩子们一整天都在海滩上嬉戏。
She romped through the exam. = 她轻松通过了考试。

现在完成时:
We have romped through all the challenges. = 我们已经克服了所有的挑战。
The team has romped to victory in every game. = 这支队伍在每一场比赛中都轻松获胜。

过去完成时:
They had romped through the first half of the game. = 他们轻松地完成了比赛的上半场。
She had romped through her studies before the exam. = 她在考试前轻松完成了学业。


将来时:

They will romp to victory if they play their best. = 如果他们尽力发挥，他们将轻松获胜。


虚拟语气:

I wish I could romp around like I did when I was a child. = 我希望我还能像小时候那样无忧无虑地玩耍。
