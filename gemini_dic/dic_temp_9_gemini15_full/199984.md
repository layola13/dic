skateboards:/ˈskeɪtˌbɔːrdz/ | n. |滑板 | A skateboard is a board with wheels, used for riding on.  | 例句：He learned to ride a skateboard at the age of seven. 他七岁时学会了滑板。 | 例句：The park is popular with skateboarders.  这个公园很受滑板爱好者的欢迎。| 近义词：longboard, scooter (部分情况下) | 反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He skateboards every weekend. = 他每个周末都玩滑板。
They skateboard in the park. = 他们在公园里玩滑板。

一般过去时:
I skateboarded yesterday. = 我昨天玩了滑板。
She skateboarded down the hill. = 她从山上滑板下来了。

现在完成时:
He has skateboarded since he was a child. = 他从小就玩滑板了。
They have skateboarded all afternoon. = 他们整个下午都在玩滑板。

过去完成时:
I had skateboarded before I learned to snowboard. = 我在学会单板滑雪之前就玩过滑板了。
She had skateboarded many times before she fell. = 她摔倒之前玩过很多次滑板了。

将来时:
I will skateboard tomorrow. = 我明天将玩滑板。
We will skateboard at the beach. = 我们将在海滩上玩滑板。

虚拟语气:
If I had a skateboard, I would skateboard every day. = 如果我有一块滑板，我每天都会玩。
I wish I could skateboard like Tony Hawk. = 我希望我能像托尼·霍克那样玩滑板。

被动语态:
Skateboards are often used for transportation. = 滑板经常被用作交通工具。
The skateboards were damaged in the accident. = 滑板在事故中受损了。

疑问句:
Do you skateboard? = 你玩滑板吗？
Where do they skateboard? = 他们在哪里玩滑板？

否定句:
I don't skateboard. = 我不玩滑板。
He didn't skateboard today. = 他今天没玩滑板。


PHRASE->
electric skateboard = 电动滑板

skateboard trucks = 滑板卡车（底盘）
skateboard wheels = 滑板轮子
