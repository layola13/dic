contort:/kənˈtɔːrt/ | vt. | 扭曲；使变形；曲解 | To twist or bend out of shape; to distort. | 例句：The dancer contorted her body into a strange pose. 舞者将身体扭曲成一个奇怪的姿势。  | 例句：He contorted the facts to fit his argument. 他歪曲事实以符合他的论点。 | 近义词：twist, distort, warp, wrench | 反义词：straighten, unbend, rectify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He contorts his face in disgust. = 他厌恶地扭曲着脸。
The artist contorts the figures in his paintings. = 艺术家在他的画作中扭曲了人物形象。

一般过去时:
She contorted her body to fit through the narrow gap. = 她扭曲身体以穿过狭窄的缝隙。
The accident contorted the metal frame of the car. = 事故扭曲了汽车的金属框架。

现在完成时:
The heat has contorted the plastic pipes. = 热量已经扭曲了塑料管道。
Years of stress have contorted his personality. = 多年的压力扭曲了他的性格。

过去完成时:
He had contorted the evidence to support his claim. = 他已经歪曲证据来支持他的说法。
The wind had contorted the trees into grotesque shapes. = 风已经把树木扭曲成怪诞的形状。

将来时:
The intense heat will contort the asphalt. = 强烈的热量会使沥青变形。
The pressure will contort the metal until it breaks. = 压力会使金属扭曲直到断裂。

虚拟语气:
If I tried to contort myself like that, I would hurt myself. = 如果我试图那样扭曲自己，我会受伤的。
I wish I hadn't contorted the truth in my report. = 我真希望我没有在我的报告中歪曲事实。

被动语态:
His face was contorted by pain. = 他的脸因疼痛而扭曲。
The metal was contorted beyond repair. = 金属被扭曲得无法修复。

疑问句:
Was his face contorted with anger? = 他的脸是愤怒地扭曲了吗？
Is the image contorted by the lens? = 图像是被镜头扭曲了吗？

否定句:
He didn't contort the facts in his presentation. = 他在他的陈述中没有歪曲事实。
She didn't contort her body during the performance. = 她在表演中没有扭曲她的身体。


PHRASE->
contort one's face = 扭曲脸部

mentally contorted = 精神错乱的


词源:
From the Latin *contortus, past participle of contorquēre, "to twist together," from con- "together" + torquēre "to twist."
