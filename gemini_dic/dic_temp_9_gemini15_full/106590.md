kublai:/ˈkuːblaɪ/| n.|忽必烈|Kublai Khan, the fifth Khagan of the Mongol Empire and the founder of the Yuan dynasty in China.  He is known for his significant military achievements, his patronage of the arts and sciences, and his attempts at cultural exchange between East and West.|Example: Kublai Khan's reign marked a significant period in Chinese history. 忽必烈的统治是中国历史上一个重要的时期。|近义词:Genghis Khan (in the context of Mongol emperors)|反义词:None|名词
