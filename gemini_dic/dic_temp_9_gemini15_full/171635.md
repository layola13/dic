pulpless:/ˈpʌlpləs/| adj.| 无果肉的；无髓的 | Lacking pulp;  without pulp.  The term is often used to describe fruits or teeth. | 例句：The orange was pulpless and dry.  这橙子没有果肉而且干巴巴的。

The dentist said I needed a root canal because the tooth was pulpless. 牙医说我需要根管治疗，因为我的牙齿没有牙髓了。|近义词：pulp-free, pithless (for fruits)|反义词：pulpy, full of pulp|
