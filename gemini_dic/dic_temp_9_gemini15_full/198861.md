sidesaddle:/ˈsaɪdˌsædəl/| n.|侧鞍；骑侧鞍的姿势|A way of riding a horse with both legs on one side of the animal, typically used by women in the past.|例句：She rode sidesaddle through the park. 她骑着侧鞍穿过公园。

例句：Many women preferred to ride sidesaddle in the Victorian era. 维多利亚时代许多妇女喜欢骑侧鞍。|近义词：astride (but implies a different posture)|反义词：astride (in the sense of a conventional riding posture)|名词
