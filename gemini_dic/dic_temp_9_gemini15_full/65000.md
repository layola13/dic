estranger:/ɪˈstreɪndʒər/| v. n. |使疏远；使陌生；局外人；陌生人 |To make someone feel isolated or alienated; to make something unfamiliar or foreign.  |例句：The years of silence had estranged the two brothers.  多年不联系使两兄弟疏远了。  例句：He felt like an estranger in his own home. 他感觉像在自己家里一样陌生。|近义词：alienate, isolate, separate |反义词：unite, reconcile, befriend |动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He estranges his family with his actions. = 他用自己的行为使家人疏远。
They estrange themselves from the community. = 他们把自己和社区隔离开来。


一般过去时:
The argument estranged the two friends. = 那次争吵使这两个朋友疏远了。
His betrayal estranged him from his colleagues. = 他的背叛使他和同事们疏远了。


现在完成时:
Years of fighting have estranged the two countries. = 多年的战争使这两个国家疏远了。
His bad behavior has estranged him from his friends. = 他糟糕的行为使他与朋友们疏远了。


过去完成时:
They had already estranged themselves before the final break-up. = 在最终决裂之前，他们就已经疏远了。
The long separation had estranged the family. = 长时间的分离使这个家庭疏远了。


将来时:
Continued conflicts will estrange the neighbors. = 持续的冲突将会使邻居们疏远。
His arrogance will estrange him from potential allies. = 他的傲慢会使他与潜在的盟友疏远。


虚拟语气:
If he continued to act this way, he would estrange everyone around him. = 如果他继续这样行事，他会和周围所有人疏远。
I wish I hadn't said those things; it estranged her from me. = 我真希望我没有说过那些话；那使她疏远了我。


被动语态:
He felt estranged by their coldness. = 他感到被他们的冷漠疏远了。
The community was estranged by the government's actions. = 社区被政府的行为疏远了。


疑问句:
What estranged them? = 是什么使他们疏远了？
Has anything estranged you from your family? = 有什么事使你和家人疏远了吗？


否定句:
The experience didn't estrange them; it brought them closer. = 这段经历并没有使他们疏远；反而使他们更加亲近。
He tried to avoid behavior that would estrange his friends. = 他试图避免那些会使朋友疏远他的行为。


PHRASE->
estrange oneself from = 与…疏远

feel estranged = 感到疏远
become estranged = 变得疏远
