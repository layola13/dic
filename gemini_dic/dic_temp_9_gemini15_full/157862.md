plaid:/plæd/| n. adj.| 格子花呢；苏格兰格子呢 | A plaid is a type of textile with a pattern consisting of crisscrossed horizontal and vertical bands of color.  It is often associated with Scotland. As an adjective, it describes something having this pattern. | Example:  She wore a beautiful plaid skirt. 她穿了一条美丽的格子裙。  The plaid blanket was warm and cozy.  那条格子毯子又暖和又舒适。|近义词：tartan, checked, checkered|反义词：plain, solid
