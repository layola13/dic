词源:
The word "nonbinding" is a compound word.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "binding" is an adjective meaning "obligatory" or "legally enforceable".  Therefore, "nonbinding" means "not legally enforceable".  The root of "bind" traces back to Proto-Germanic *bindan, meaning "to bind."
