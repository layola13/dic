minimalism: /ˈmɪnɪməlɪzəm/ | n. | 极简主义；极简风格 | The philosophy or practice of using or including only the things that are essential.  It emphasizes simplicity, functionality, and reduction of clutter. |例句：Her apartment is a testament to minimalism; it's sparsely furnished but beautifully organized.  她的公寓是极简主义的典范；家具很少，但井然有序。

例句：The minimalist design of the website makes it easy to navigate.  网站的极简设计使其易于浏览。


近义词：simplicity, spareness, austerity | 反义词：maximalism, extravagance, opulence
