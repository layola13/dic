paracentesis:/ˌpærəsɛnˈtiːsɪs/| n. |穿刺术；穿刺法|The surgical puncture of a cavity for the aspiration of fluid.  This procedure is used to remove fluid from various body cavities, such as the abdomen (abdominocentesis), chest (thoracentesis), or pericardium (pericardiocentesis).|例句：The doctor performed a paracentesis to relieve the pressure caused by the fluid buildup in the patient's abdomen.  医生进行了穿刺术以缓解病人腹腔积液引起的压力。

例句：A paracentesis is often used to diagnose and treat ascites. 穿刺术常用于诊断和治疗腹水。


近义词：centesis, puncture, tap|反义词：None|词性：名词


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, tense considerations are not applicable in the same way as with verbs.  The usage will depend on the context of the sentence.
