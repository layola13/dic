eet:/iːt/|n.|吃，饭，食物|The act of eating or the food eaten.|例句：The cat enjoyed its eet.  猫享受它的食物。|近义词：meal, food, repast|反义词：fasting, starvation|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I eat breakfast every morning. = 我每天早上吃早餐。
They eat lunch at noon. = 他们中午吃午饭。

一般过去时:
She ate a big dinner last night. = 她昨晚吃了一顿丰盛的晚餐。
He ate all the cookies. = 他把所有的饼干都吃掉了。

现在完成时:
I have eaten all the cake. = 我已经把蛋糕都吃掉了。
They have eaten dinner already. = 他们已经吃过晚饭了。

过去完成时:
She had eaten before I arrived. =在我到达之前，她已经吃过了。
We had eaten all the snacks before the movie started. = 电影开始前，我们已经吃掉了所有的零食。

将来时:
I will eat at the restaurant tonight. = 我今晚将在餐馆吃饭。
They will eat pizza for dinner. = 他们晚饭将吃披萨。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would eat more vegetables. = 如果我是你，我会多吃蔬菜。
I wish I had eaten less dessert. = 我希望我没有吃那么多甜点。

被动语态:
The food was eaten quickly. = 食物很快就被吃掉了。
The cake is being eaten by the children. = 蛋糕正在被孩子们吃。

疑问句:
What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？
Have you eaten yet? = 你吃了吗？

否定句:
I did not eat anything today. = 我今天什么都没吃。
She doesn't eat meat. = 她不吃肉。


PHRASE->
eat up = 吃光
eat out = 在外面吃饭
eat in = 在家吃饭
have a bite to eat = 简单吃点东西
eat one's words = 收回说过的话
