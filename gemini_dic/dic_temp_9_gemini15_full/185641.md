reverbs:/ˈrɛvɜːrbz/| n.|混响，回声|The reverberations of the explosion were felt miles away. 爆炸的回声在几英里外都能感觉到。|近义词：echo, resonance|反义词：silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music reverberates throughout the hall. 音乐在整个大厅回响。
The sound of the bell reverberates across the valley.  钟声在山谷中回荡。


一般过去时:
The applause reverberated in the large concert hall.  掌声在大型音乐厅回荡。
The impact of the news reverberated throughout the community.  这个消息的影响在整个社区引起反响。


现在完成时:
The effects of the earthquake have reverberated across the region.  地震的影响已经在该地区产生了回响。
The singer's voice has reverberated in my memory for years. 歌手的歌声多年来一直回荡在我的记忆中。


过去完成时:
The echoes of the past had reverberated in his mind.  过去的回声在他脑海中回荡。
The scandal's effects had reverberated through the government before the investigation began.  丑闻的影响在调查开始之前就已在政府中产生震动。


将来时:
The announcement will reverberate across the nation.  这一声明将在全国引起反响。
The impact of the decision will reverberate for years to come.  这一决定的影响将在未来几年持续回荡。


虚拟语气:
If the news were true, it would reverberate throughout the world. 如果消息属实，它将在全世界引起反响。
I wish the sound of the waterfall could reverberate eternally.  我希望瀑布的声音能够永远回荡。


被动语态:
The message was reverberated by the media.  这条消息被媒体广泛传播。
The news was reverberated across the internet.  这条新闻在互联网上广泛传播。


疑问句:
Does the sound reverberate in this room?  这个房间的声音会回响吗？
How long will the effects of this decision reverberate?  这个决定的影响会持续多久？


否定句:
The sound did not reverberate in the small room.  声音在这个小房间里没有回响。
The impact of the event did not reverberate as expected.  事件的影响并未像预期的那样产生反响。
