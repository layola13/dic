reteam:/riːˈtiːm/| v.|重组队伍；重新组队|To form a new team or group, often from existing members.|The company decided to reteam their marketing and sales departments to improve synergy. 公司决定重组其市场营销和销售部门以提高协同效应。|reform, reorganize|disband, dissolve|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They reteam the project members every quarter. = 他们每季度都会重新组建项目团队。
We reteam our sales force annually to boost performance. = 我们每年都会重组销售团队以提高业绩。


一般过去时:
The coach reteamed the players after a series of losses. = 教练在球队连续失利后重新调整了球员阵容。
They reteamed the project after discovering a critical flaw in the initial design. = 他们在发现初始设计中的严重缺陷后重新组建了项目团队。


现在完成时:
The company has reteamed several departments to improve efficiency. = 公司已经重组了几个部门以提高效率。
They have reteamed the marketing campaign several times. = 他们已经多次调整了营销活动团队。


过去完成时:
After the merger, they had reteamed all the departments before the new fiscal year began. = 合并后，他们在新财政年度开始前已经重组了所有部门。
The project manager had reteamed the development team twice before the project was finally completed. = 项目经理在项目最终完成之前已经两次重组了开发团队。


将来时:
They will reteam the research group next month. = 他们下个月将重新组建研究小组。
We will reteam the project team after the initial assessment. = 我们将在初步评估后重新组建项目团队。


虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we would reteam the entire project. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们会重新组建整个项目团队。
It would be wise to reteam the sales team if their performance continues to decline. = 如果他们的业绩继续下滑，明智的做法是重组销售团队。


被动语态:
The project team was reteamed after the initial phase. = 项目团队在初始阶段后进行了重组。
The sales team is being reteamed to address the recent decline in sales. = 销售团队正在重组以解决近期销售额下降的问题。


疑问句:
Will they reteam the marketing department next year? = 他们明年会重组市场营销部门吗？
Should we reteam the project after the initial results? = 我们应该在获得初步结果后重新组建项目团队吗？


否定句:
They did not reteam the project despite the setbacks. = 尽管遇到挫折，他们并没有重组项目团队。
We will not reteam the research group unless absolutely necessary. = 除非绝对必要，否则我们不会重组研究小组。
