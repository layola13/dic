sandiest: /ˈsændiɪst/ | adj. | 最沙质的，含沙最多的 | Most full of sand; having the most sand.  The word is the superlative form of "sandy". | 例句：The sandiest beach I've ever seen was in the Bahamas. 我见过的最沙质的海滩在巴哈马群岛。

The sandiest part of the desert was near the riverbed. 沙漠中最沙质的部分靠近河床。


近义词：most sandy,  grittiest (if referring to texture) | 反义词：clayey, rocky, smooth


词性：形容词
