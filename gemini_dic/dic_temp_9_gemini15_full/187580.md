romanticized:/rəʊˈmæn.tɪ.saɪzd/| adj.|浪漫化的；理想化的|To represent or treat something in a romantic or idealized way, often ignoring or downplaying negative aspects.  It often implies a subjective and potentially inaccurate portrayal.|例句：The movie romanticized the lives of the pioneers, ignoring the hardships they faced.  这部电影浪漫化了拓荒者的生活，忽略了他们所面临的困难。|近义词：idealized, idealized, sentimentalized|反义词：realistic, factual
