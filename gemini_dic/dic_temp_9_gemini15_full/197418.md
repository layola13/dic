sheds:/ʃɛdz/ | n. v. | 棚屋；脱落；流出；散发 |  A simple structure, typically made of wood or metal, used for storage or shelter; to cast off or lose (something); to cause (something) to flow or fall | 例句：The gardener stores his tools in the shed. 园丁把工具存放在棚屋里。 The snake sheds its skin. 蛇蜕皮。|近义词：hut, cabin, cast off, discard,  |反义词：retain, keep, accumulate |
