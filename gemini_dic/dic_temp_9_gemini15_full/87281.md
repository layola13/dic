hermeneutic:/ˌhɜːrməˈnjuːtɪk/ | n. adj. |解释学；解释的；诠释的 |The branch of knowledge dealing with interpretation, especially of the Bible or literary texts.  It involves the methods and principles used to understand and explain texts. |例句：Hermeneutical analysis is crucial for understanding complex literary works. 解释学分析对于理解复杂的文学作品至关重要。

例句：The professor's lecture focused on the hermeneutic approach to biblical studies. 教授的讲座重点关注圣经研究的解释学方法。


近义词：exegesis, interpretation, explication | 反义词：misinterpretation, misunderstanding | 词性：名词，形容词
