mathematics:/ˌmæθəˈmætɪks/ | n. |数学 |The science of numbers, quantities, and shapes, and the relationships between them.  It involves the use of logic and reasoning to solve problems and develop theories.  Mathematics is a fundamental tool used in many fields, including science, engineering, and finance. |Example:  He excels in mathematics and plans to study engineering. 他擅长数学，并计划学习工程学。|  Example: The professor explained the principles of advanced mathematics. 教授讲解了高等数学的原理。|近义词：arithmetic, calculations, computation |反义词：None.
