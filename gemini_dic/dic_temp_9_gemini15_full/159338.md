poisonings:/ˈpɔɪzənɪŋz/| n.|中毒（复数）|The act of poisoning someone or something, or the state of being poisoned;  instances of poisoning.|例句：Multiple poisonings were reported in the town after the chemical spill.  镇上化学品泄漏后，有多起中毒事件报告。

例句：The investigation into the recent poisonings is ongoing.  对最近中毒事件的调查正在进行中。


近义词：intoxications, envenomations|反义词：cures, treatments
