ladening:/ˈleɪdnɪŋ/| n.|装载；装货|The act of loading something, especially a ship or vehicle.  It refers to the process of filling something with a load. |例句：The ladening of the ship took all day.  船的装载工作花了一整天。

例句：The careful ladening of the fragile cargo was essential to prevent damage during transit.  小心装载易碎货物对于防止运输途中损坏至关重要。


近义词：loading, shipment, freight


反义词：unloading, discharge


词性：名词
