pervasion:/pərˈveɪʒən/| n. |弥漫；渗透；遍布 |The pervasive influence of technology in modern life.  现代生活中技术的影响无处不在。|近义词：penetration, diffusion, spread |反义词：exclusion, isolation, confinement


英文解析:The act of pervading or the state of being pervaded; the spread of something throughout a place or thing.  It suggests a widespread and often subtle influence.


例句：

The pervasiveness of social media is undeniable. 社交媒体的普及是不可否认的。

The pervading sense of anxiety in the room was palpable.  房间里弥漫着一种明显的焦虑感。


词源:

From the Latin *pervadere*, meaning "to go through, permeate".  The prefix "per-" means "through," and the verb "vadere" means "to go."

His influence pervades the entire organization. 他的影响遍及整个组织。


一般过去时:

Her sadness pervaded every aspect of her life.  她的悲伤渗透到她生活的方方面面。


现在完成时:

Technology has pervaded almost every aspect of our daily lives. 科技已经渗透到我们日常生活的几乎每一个方面。

Doubt has pervaded his mind since the incident.  自从那件事以来，怀疑一直萦绕在他的心头。


过去完成时:

By the time the police arrived, a sense of panic had pervaded the crowd.  警察到达时，恐慌的情绪已经蔓延到人群中。

Fear had pervaded the village long before the attack.  早在袭击发生之前，恐惧就已经弥漫在村庄里。


将来时:

Optimism will pervade the atmosphere once the crisis is over.  危机过后，乐观情绪将会弥漫在空气中。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
