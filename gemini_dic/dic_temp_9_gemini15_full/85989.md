headstand:/ˈhɛdˌstænd/ | n. |头倒立 | A yoga pose where one balances on their head and hands, supporting their body weight.  It's a challenging pose requiring strength and balance. | Example:  She effortlessly performed a headstand during yoga class. 她轻松地在瑜伽课上做了头倒立。 | 近义词：inversion, head balance | 反义词：handstand

词源:
The word "headstand" is a compound word formed from "head" and "stand".  "Head" refers to the head, the part of the body used to support oneself in the pose, while "stand" indicates the upright position maintained during the pose.  Its etymology is straightforward and reflects the action directly.
