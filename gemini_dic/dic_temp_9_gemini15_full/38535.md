conclave:/ˈkɒŋkleɪv/ /ˈkɑːŋkleɪv/| n.|秘密会议；教宗选举会议|A private or secret meeting, especially one of a group of cardinals to elect a new pope.  The term often implies secrecy and importance.|例句：The cardinals entered the conclave to elect the new Pope.  枢机主教们进入秘密会议选举新教皇。

例句：A conclave of scientists met to discuss the new research findings. 一群科学家秘密会面讨论新的研究结果。


近义词：meeting, assembly, caucus, gathering

反义词：public meeting, open forum
