manifested:/ˈmænɪfestɪd/ | v. | 显示；表明；表现；成为现实 | To show something clearly, through actions or appearances; to become apparent or visible; to make (a feeling, quality, etc.) evident. |例句：His anger manifested itself in shouting. 他的愤怒以叫喊的方式表现出来。  The symptoms of the disease manifested themselves within days.  疾病的症状几天内就显现出来了。|近义词：showed, revealed, displayed, demonstrated, appeared |反义词：concealed, hidden, suppressed, obscured


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His talent manifests itself in his artwork. = 他的天赋在他的艺术作品中展现出来。
The problem manifests as a rash. = 这个问题表现为皮疹。

一般过去时:
The disease manifested quickly. = 这种疾病迅速显现。
Her sadness manifested in tears. = 她的悲伤以泪水的方式表现出来。

现在完成时:
The symptoms have manifested since the surgery. = 手术后症状已经显现。
His genius has manifested throughout his career. = 他的天才在他的整个职业生涯中都得到了体现。

过去完成时:
The effects had manifested before the diagnosis. = 影响在诊断之前就已经显现了。
The problem had manifested itself long before the meeting. = 这个问题早在会议之前就已经显现了。


将来时:
The effects will manifest within a week. = 效果将在一个星期内显现。
The results will manifest themselves in the next report. = 结果将在下一份报告中体现出来。


虚拟语气:
If the disease manifested earlier, treatment would have been more effective. = 如果这种疾病早些显现，治疗效果会更好。
I wish the symptoms had manifested sooner. = 我希望症状能早点显现。

被动语态:
The anger was manifested in a violent outburst. = 愤怒以暴力的爆发方式表现出来。
The problem was manifested by a series of errors. = 这个问题由一系列错误表现出来。

疑问句:
How did the problem manifest itself? = 这个问题是如何表现出来的？
Does the disease manifest differently in children? = 这种疾病在儿童身上表现是否不同？

否定句:
The symptoms did not manifest immediately. = 症状并没有立即显现。
The error did not manifest until later. = 错误直到后来才显现。
