maharajas:/məˈhɑːrədʒɑːz/| n. |大君们，王公们 |The plural of maharaja, referring to multiple rulers or princes in India.  The word evokes images of lavish lifestyles, opulent palaces, and immense power. |例句：The maharajas once ruled vast territories in India.  大君们曾经统治着印度的大片领土。|例句：Historians are studying the lives of the maharajas and their contributions to Indian culture. 历史学家们正在研究大君们的生活及其对印度文化的贡献。|近义词：princes, rulers, kings, potentates |反义词：subjects, commoners
