/priːˈɛmpərər/| n. |前任皇帝|A preemperor is a person who was formerly an emperor.  The term is relatively uncommon and primarily used in historical contexts.  It emphasizes the past status of the individual. |Example: The preemperor's memoirs detailed his reign and eventual abdication.  前任皇帝的回忆录详细描述了他的统治和最终退位。|近义词：former emperor, ex-emperor|反义词：current emperor, reigning emperor|名词
