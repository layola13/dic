congratulate:/kənˈɡrædʒuleɪt/| vt. |祝贺，恭喜|To express pleasure to someone on their success or good fortune.|例句：I congratulate you on your success. 我祝贺你成功。|例句：We congratulated her on her engagement. 我们祝贺她订婚。|近义词：felicitate, commend |反义词：condemn, criticize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I congratulate you on your achievement. = 我祝贺你取得的成就。
They congratulate the winners every year. = 他们每年都祝贺获胜者。

一般过去时:
She congratulated him on his graduation. = 她祝贺他毕业。
We congratulated them on their new house. = 我们祝贺他们拥有新房子。

现在完成时:
I have congratulated him on his promotion. = 我已经祝贺他升职了。
They have congratulated the team on their victory. = 他们已经祝贺球队取得胜利了。

过去完成时:
They had congratulated her before she gave her speech. = 在她发表演讲之前，他们已经祝贺她了。
I had congratulated him on his birthday before he left. = 在他离开之前，我已经祝贺他生日快乐了。

将来时:
I will congratulate him when he wins the race. = 当他赢得比赛时，我会祝贺他。
We will congratulate her on her wedding day. = 我们将在她的婚礼当天祝贺她。

虚拟语气:
I would congratulate him if he won the prize. = 如果他赢得奖品，我会祝贺他。
If I had known, I would have congratulated her earlier. = 如果我知道的话，我会早点祝贺她。

被动语态:
He was congratulated by his colleagues on his retirement. = 他退休时受到了同事们的祝贺。
The team was congratulated on their excellent performance. = 球队因其优异的表现而受到祝贺。

疑问句:
Did you congratulate her on her birthday? = 你祝贺她生日快乐了吗？
Will you congratulate them on their marriage? = 你会祝贺他们的婚姻吗？

否定句:
I didn't congratulate him on his new job. = 我没有祝贺他找到新工作。
They didn't congratulate the winner. = 他们没有祝贺获胜者。


PHRASE->
congratulate someone on something = 祝贺某人某事
congratulate oneself on = 为…而自鸣得意
offer one's congratulations = 表示祝贺
warmest congratulations = 最热烈的祝贺
hearty congratulations = 衷心的祝贺
