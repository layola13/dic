overpreach:/ˌoʊvərˈpriːtʃ/| v.|夸夸其谈；言过其实；过分强调|To speak or preach excessively or beyond what is appropriate or necessary; to overemphasize a point or message.  |例句：He overpreached his case to the judge, resulting in a less favorable outcome. 他在法官面前夸夸其谈，结果导致结果不那么理想。


例句：The politician overpreached his commitment to environmental protection, leading some to doubt his sincerity.  这位政治家过分强调了他对环境保护的承诺，导致一些人怀疑他的真诚。


近义词：exaggerate, overstate, oversell, belabor


反义词：understate, downplay


词性：动词
