hemicycle:/ˈhɛmɪˌsaɪkəl/| n. |半圆形剧场；半圆形座位|A semicircular structure, especially a seating arrangement in a legislative assembly or theatre.  It is often used to describe the seating arrangement in a legislative chamber where seats are arranged in a semicircle.|Example: The council chamber was a hemicycle, allowing all members to see each other clearly.|近义词：semicircle, amphitheatre|反义词：full circle, complete circle|名词
