non-indictment:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdaɪtmənt/| n. |不予起诉；未被起诉|The decision not to indict someone.  A non-indictment means that a grand jury has decided not to bring formal charges against an individual.|例句：The grand jury returned a non-indictment, much to the relief of the suspect.  大陪审团作出了不予起诉的决定，这让嫌疑人松了一口气。

The prosecutor decided to pursue a non-indictment, citing insufficient evidence.  检察官决定不予起诉，理由是证据不足。


近义词：lack of indictment, dismissal of charges|反义词：indictment
