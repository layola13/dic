outlearning:/ˌaʊtˈlɜːrnɪŋ/| v.|超越学习，胜过学习|The act of surpassing someone or something else in learning or knowledge.|例句：She outlearned all her classmates in the advanced physics course. 她在这门高级物理课程中胜过了所有同学。|近义词：surpassing, excelling, exceeding|反义词：underlearning, lagging behind|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outlearns his peers in mathematics. = 他在数学方面胜过他的同龄人。
They outlearn their competitors through continuous improvement. = 通过持续改进，他们胜过竞争对手。


一般过去时:
She outlearned him in the debate. = 她在那场辩论中胜过他。
The team outlearned their opponents by studying their strategies. = 该团队通过研究对手的策略而胜过他们。


现在完成时:
He has outlearned many experts in his field. = 他在他的领域里已经胜过了许多专家。
The company has outlearned its rivals in adapting to market changes. = 这家公司在适应市场变化方面已经胜过竞争对手。


过去完成时:
They had outlearned their previous knowledge by the time of the exam. = 到考试的时候，他们已经超越了以前的知识。
She had outlearned her tutor and now taught him new techniques. = 她已经超过了她的导师，现在正在教他新的技巧。


将来时:
She will outlearn him with consistent effort. = 她将通过持续努力胜过他。
They will outlearn their limitations with the help of new technology. = 借助新技术，他们将克服自己的局限性。


虚拟语气:
If she had studied harder, she would have outlearned her rival. = 如果她学习更努力，她就会胜过她的竞争对手。
I wish I had outlearned my weaknesses earlier. = 我希望我早点克服自己的弱点。


被动语态:
He was outlearned by his younger sister in chess. = 他在国际象棋比赛中被妹妹击败。
Their strategies were outlearned by the innovative approaches of the new company. = 他们的策略被新公司的创新方法所超越。


疑问句:
Did he outlearn his opponent in the competition? = 他在比赛中胜过对手了吗？
Can they outlearn the challenges presented by the project? = 他们能够克服项目带来的挑战吗？


否定句:
He did not outlearn his colleague in the research. = 他在研究中没有胜过他的同事。
They failed to outlearn the difficulties of the task. = 他们没能克服任务的困难。
