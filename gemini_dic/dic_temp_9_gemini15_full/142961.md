outarm:/ˈaʊtɑːrm/| n.|外臂；伸出的臂；(机械臂等)伸出的臂|An outarm is an arm that extends outwards, often referring to a mechanical arm or a part of a structure that projects outward.  It can also refer to a person's arm when it is outstretched.|例句：The robot's outarm reached across the table to grab the object.  机器人的伸出臂伸过桌子去抓取物体。|例句：He held his outarm straight to steady himself. 他伸直手臂以保持平衡。|近义词：extension, projection, reach|反义词：inarm (not a common word)|名词
