sauciness:/ˈsɔːsɪnəs/| n. | 厚脸皮；放肆；调皮；活泼 |  A cheeky or impudent quality; boldness.  It can also refer to a lively and spirited manner. |例句：Her sauciness charmed everyone in the room. 她的厚脸皮迷住了房间里的每个人。

例句：The sauciness of his remarks was quite unexpected. 他话语中的放肆出乎意料。


近义词：cheekiness, impudence, impertinence, boldness, spirit, liveliness

反义词：timidity, shyness, meekness, submissiveness


词源：源自法语“sauce”，意为调味酱，引申为“风趣，机智”，再引申为“厚脸皮”。  The word evolved from the sense of being playfully bold or spirited, like a spicy sauce that adds zest.
