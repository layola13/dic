reptiliferous:/ˌrepˈtɪlɪfərəs/| adj. |含爬行动物的；产爬行动物的|Containing or yielding reptiles.  The reptiliferous strata of the Mesozoic Era are rich in fossils.|例句：The discovery of reptiliferous rocks in this area has excited paleontologists. 这个地区发现含爬行动物岩石的消息让古生物学家们兴奋不已。|近义词：reptilian (in the sense of relating to reptiles), saurian|反义词：mammaliferous (containing mammals)
