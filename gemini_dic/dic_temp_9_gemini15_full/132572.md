nondidactically:/ˌnɒnˌdɪˈdæktɪkli/| adj. |非教学地；非教诲地；非训诫地 |Not in a way that is intended to teach or instruct.  It implies a lack of pedagogical approach, often suggesting an informal or incidental manner of presentation. |The professor, nondidactically, shared a personal anecdote to illustrate the point.  教授非教学性地分享了一个个人轶事来阐明这一点。|近义词：informally, casually, incidentally |反义词：didactically, pedagogically, instructively
