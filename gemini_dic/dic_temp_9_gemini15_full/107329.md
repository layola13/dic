lamberts:/ˈlæmbərts/| n.|朗伯（亮度单位）|A lambert (L) is a unit of luminance, a measure of the luminous intensity per unit area of a light source.  It is defined as 1/π candela per square centimeter (cd/cm²).  It's an older unit, and the candela (cd) is now the preferred SI unit.|The display's luminance was measured at 300 lamberts.|近义词:  candela per square meter (cd/m²) (SI unit),nit|反义词:None.
