quasi-dejectedly:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ dɪˈdʒɛktɪdli/ | adv. | 几乎沮丧地，有气无力地 | Almost dejectedly; in a manner suggesting mild dejection or discouragement.  It implies a state of being slightly downcast or disheartened, but not completely despondent. | Example: He walked quasi-dejectedly towards the empty house, a shadow of his former self. 他几乎沮丧地走向空荡荡的房子，昔日的影子。|近义词: dispiritedly, despondently, listlessly, dejectedly | 反义词: cheerfully, enthusiastically, energetically | 副词
