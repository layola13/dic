caatinga:/ka.aˈtʃiŋ.ɡɐ/| n. |卡廷加植被|A type of tropical dry broadleaf forest or shrubland vegetation that occurs in northeastern Brazil.  It is characterized by drought-resistant plants, thorny shrubs, and cacti. The caatinga biome is unique and highly diverse, despite its arid conditions.  It's home to many endemic species, meaning they are found nowhere else on Earth.|The caatinga is home to a variety of unique animal species.|近义词：xerophilous vegetation, dry forest, thorn scrub|反义词：rainforest, tropical rainforest
