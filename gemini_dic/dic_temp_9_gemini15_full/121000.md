misclassifying: /ˌmɪsklǽsɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. |错误分类，误分类 |The act of incorrectly categorizing or assigning something to a wrong class or group. This often happens due to errors in judgment, insufficient data, or flawed algorithms.|例句：The software was misclassifying many images, leading to inaccurate results.  软件错误分类了许多图像，导致结果不准确。

The algorithm's misclassifying of benign tumors as malignant caused unnecessary anxiety for patients. 算法将良性肿瘤误分类为恶性肿瘤，给患者带来了不必要的焦虑。|近义词：miscategorizing, mislabeling, misidentifying |反义词：classifying correctly, accurately categorizing
