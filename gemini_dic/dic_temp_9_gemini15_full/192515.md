scrutineer:/ˌskruːtɪˈnɪər/| n. |细查者；仔细审查者；监票员|A person who examines or investigates something carefully.  They often check for accuracy, validity, or compliance with rules.  In elections, a scrutineer checks the voting process for fairness and accuracy.|The election scrutineer carefully examined each ballot.  选举监票员仔细检查每一张选票。|Examiner, inspector, investigator, auditor|None (Antonyms would depend on the context, e.g., someone who overlooks details)|名词
