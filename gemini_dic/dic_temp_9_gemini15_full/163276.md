precondemn:/ˌpriːkənˈdɛm/| vt.|预先谴责；提前定罪|To condemn in advance, especially without sufficient evidence or before a fair trial.  It implies a judgment made prematurely and often unjustly.|例句：The media has been accused of precondemning the suspect before the trial even began.媒体被指控在审判开始之前就预先谴责了嫌疑人。

例句：It is unfair to precondemn a new policy before it has even been implemented. 在一项新政策甚至尚未实施之前就预先谴责它是 不公平的。


近义词：prejudge, prejudicate, condemn beforehand, denounce beforehand

反义词：exonerate, acquit, vindicate


词性:动词
