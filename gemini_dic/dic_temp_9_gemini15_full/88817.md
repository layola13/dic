hogback:/ˈhɒɡˌbæk/| n.| 猪背脊，(地质)猪背岭 | A long, narrow ridge with a steep slope on one side and a gentle slope on the other, resembling a pig's back.  | Example: The hogback formation was a prominent feature of the landscape.  那座猪背岭是这片景观中显著的特征。|近义词：ridge, cuesta|反义词：valley, plain
