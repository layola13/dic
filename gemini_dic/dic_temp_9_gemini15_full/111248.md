litigant:/ˈlɪtɪɡənt/| n. |诉讼当事人，诉讼参与者 |A litigant is a person involved in a lawsuit.  A litigant can be either a plaintiff or a defendant. |Example: The litigants agreed to settle the case out of court.  诉讼双方同意庭外和解。  The litigant presented compelling evidence to the judge.  诉讼当事人向法官提交了令人信服的证据。|近义词：party, plaintiff, defendant |反义词：judge, jury, observer
