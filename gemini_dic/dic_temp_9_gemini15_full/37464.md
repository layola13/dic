commissionary:/kəˈmɪʃənəri/| n.|专员；委员；专卖店；军需处|A commissioner is an official appointed to a particular function, often with the power to investigate and decide on matters.|例句：The commissionary investigated the allegations of corruption. 专员调查了腐败指控。|例句：The army's commissionary supplied food and equipment to the troops. 陆军的军需处为部队提供食物和装备。|近义词：agent, delegate, representative|反义词：N/A|名词
