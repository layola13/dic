gophers:/ˈɡoʊfərz/ | n. |土拨鼠；囊鼠 |A gopher is a small rodent that lives underground, known for its digging abilities and cheek pouches.  They are often considered pests due to their burrowing habits that can damage gardens and lawns. |例句：The farmer set traps to catch the gophers damaging his crops.  这位农民设下陷阱来捕捉破坏他庄稼的土拨鼠。

例句：We saw a gopher pop its head out of its burrow. 我们看到一只土拨鼠从洞穴里探出头来。


近义词：pocket gopher, ground squirrel |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with above-ground animals. |词性:名词
