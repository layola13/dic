gnatworm: /ˈnætˌwɜːrm/ | n. | 蚊蝇幼虫；(尤指) 胃蝇幼虫 | A gnatworm is the larva of a gnat, especially a type of fly whose larvae can infect the stomach of animals, including humans.  It is often used to refer to the larvae of botflies, which are parasitic flies.  | Example:  The cattle were suffering from a severe infestation of gnatworms.  牛群遭受了严重的蚊蝇幼虫感染。  The doctor confirmed that the child had a gnatworm infection in their stomach. 医生确认孩子胃部感染了蚊蝇幼虫。 | 近义词：fly larva, botfly larva | 反义词：N/A
