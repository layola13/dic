get through:/ɡet θruː/ | vt. | 通过；完成；接通电话；克服；度过 | To succeed in communicating with someone by telephone. To finish something successfully. To manage to cope with a difficult situation or period of time. To pass through something. |

例句：

I finally got through to customer service after being on hold for an hour. = 我在等待了一个小时后终于接通了客服电话。

He got through all his work before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前完成了所有工作。


近义词：complete, finish, overcome, succeed, survive, pass

反义词：fail, give up, succumb

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I get through a lot of work every day. = 我每天完成很多工作。
She gets through three cups of coffee a day. = 她一天喝三杯咖啡。

一般过去时:
I got through the exam with flying colors. = 我顺利通过了考试。
They got through a difficult time together. = 他们一起度过了艰难的时期。

现在完成时:
We have got through all the preparations. = 我们已经完成了所有的准备工作。
She has got through a lot of challenges in her life. = 她一生克服了很多挑战。

过去完成时:
He had got through his presentation before the questions began. = 他在提问开始之前完成了他的演示。
By the time we arrived, they had got through most of the food. = 我们到达的时候，他们已经吃掉了大部分食物。

将来时:
I will get through this difficult period.  = 我会度过这段艰难的时期。
They will get through the project by next week. = 他们下周会完成这个项目。

虚拟语气:

I wish I could get through to him; he doesn't seem to understand. = 我希望我能让他明白；他似乎不明白。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
