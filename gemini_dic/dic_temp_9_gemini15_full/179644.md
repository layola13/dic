reenergizing:/ˌriːˈɛnərˌdʒaɪzɪŋ/| adj.| 重新充满活力；使精力充沛的 |  Giving new energy or vitality to something or someone; revitalizing.  |例句：The weekend provided a much-needed reenergizing break.  周末提供了急需的恢复活力的休息。

例句：The speaker's passionate speech was reenergizing for the audience. 演讲者的热情演讲使听众重新充满活力。


近义词：revitalizing, invigorating, refreshing, stimulating

反义词：exhausting, draining, depleting, weakening

词性：形容词
