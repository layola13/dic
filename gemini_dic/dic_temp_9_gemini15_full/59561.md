ebbed:/ɛbd/| v.|退去；衰退；减弱|To move away from the shore; to decline or decrease gradually.|The tide ebbed slowly, revealing more of the beach. 潮水慢慢退去，露出了更多的沙滩。|The enthusiasm for the project has ebbed.  对这个项目的热情已经减退了。|flowed, increased, waxed|waned, decreased
USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tide ebbs twice a day. = 潮汐每天退两次。
The river ebbs slowly after the rain. = 雨后河水慢慢退去。

一般过去时:
The water ebbed away from the shore. = 水从岸边退去了。
The pain gradually ebbed after taking the medicine. = 服药后疼痛逐渐减轻。

现在完成时:
The floodwaters have finally ebbed. = 洪水终于退去了。
His influence has ebbed since his retirement. = 他退休后影响力减弱了。

过去完成时:
The tide had ebbed before we reached the beach. = 我们到达海滩前，潮水已经退了。
The crowd had ebbed away before the concert finished. = 音乐会结束前，人群已经散去了。

将来时:
The tide will ebb later this afternoon. = 今天下午晚些时候潮水将会退去。
His anger will ebb as time goes by. = 随着时间的推移，他的怒气将会消退。

虚拟语气:
If the tide had ebbed sooner, we could have reached the island. = 如果潮水早点退去，我们就能到达岛屿了。
If his enthusiasm had not ebbed, the project would have succeeded. = 如果他的热情没有减退，项目本会成功。

被动语态:
(Ebbed is rarely used passively)  ebbed很少用被动语态


疑问句:
Has the tide ebbed yet? = 潮水退了吗？
Is the river ebbing now? = 河水现在正在退去吗？

否定句:
The tide did not ebb as quickly as expected. = 潮水没有像预期的那样快退去。
The pain did not ebb even after taking the medicine. = 服药后疼痛也没有减轻。


PHRASE->
ebb and flow = 盛衰；潮涨潮落
ebb away = 逐渐消失；逐渐减弱
ebb tide = 退潮
ebb current = 退流
on the ebb = 正在衰退；正在减少
