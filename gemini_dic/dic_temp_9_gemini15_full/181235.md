reinspection:/ˌriːɪnˈspɛkʃən/| n. |复查，复验|The act of inspecting something again.  It often implies a previous inspection was inadequate or that new information requires a second look. |例句：The building failed its initial inspection and requires a reinspection. 这栋建筑物第一次检查不合格，需要复查。

例句：After the accident, a reinspection of all safety procedures was mandated. 事故发生后，强制要求对所有安全程序进行复查。


近义词：re-examination, review, second inspection |反义词：initial inspection, first inspection |词性：名词
