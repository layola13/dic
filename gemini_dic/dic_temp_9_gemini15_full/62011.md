enamored:/ɪˈnæmərd/ | adj. | 倾心的；迷恋的 |  Feeling or showing strong admiration or love for someone or something.  | Example: She was completely enamored with the charming stranger. 她完全被那个迷人的陌生人迷住了。  |近义词：infatuated, captivated, smitten | 反义词：indifferent, apathetic | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is enamored with her beauty. = 他迷恋她的美貌。
They are enamored of the new technology. = 他们迷恋这项新技术。


一般过去时:
She was enamored of his wit and charm. = 她被他的才智和魅力迷住了。
I was completely enamored with the scenery. = 我完全被风景迷住了。


现在完成时:
He has been enamored with that actress for years. = 多年来他一直迷恋那位女演员。
We have been enamored of the idea since we first heard about it. = 从我们第一次听说这个主意以来，我们就一直对它很着迷。


过去完成时:
She had been enamored with him since their first meeting. = 从他们第一次见面起，她就一直迷恋着他。
They had been enamored with the idea before they decided to invest. = 在他们决定投资之前，他们就对这个主意很着迷了。


将来时:
He will be enamored with his new car. = 他将迷恋他的新车。
I think she will be enamored of the proposal. = 我认为她会喜欢这个提议。


虚拟语气:
If I were enamored with someone, I would tell them. = 如果我爱上某人，我会告诉他们。
I wish I were not so enamored with this project. = 我希望我不那么迷恋这个项目。


被动语态:
(Note:  "Enamored" is rarely used passively.)


疑问句:
Is he enamored with his work? = 他迷恋他的工作吗？
Are you enamored with this painting? = 你迷恋这幅画吗？


否定句:
He is not enamored with the idea. = 他并不喜欢这个主意。
She was not enamored of his behavior. = 她不喜欢他的行为。


PHRASE->
be enamored of/with = 迷恋；倾心于
completely enamored = 完全被迷住
utterly enamored = 彻底迷恋
deeply enamored = 深深迷恋
head over heels enamored =  深深迷恋 (比喻:倾心)
