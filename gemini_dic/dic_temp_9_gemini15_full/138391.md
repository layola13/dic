nototherium:/ˌnoʊtəˈθɪəriəm/| n. | 南美大地懒 | Nototherium is an extinct genus of ground sloth from the Pleistocene epoch of Australia.  Fossils have been found across a wide area, suggesting a broad distribution across the continent. They were large, heavily built animals, likely herbivores. |The discovery of Nototherium fossils provided valuable insights into the megafauna of Pleistocene Australia.|近义词:Megatherium (giant ground sloth)|反义词:N/A
