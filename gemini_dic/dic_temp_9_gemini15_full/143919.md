outprize: /aʊtˈpraɪz/ | vt. |胜过，超过，优于|To surpass or exceed in value, excellence, or importance.| The quality of her work outprized that of her colleagues.  她的作品质量超过了她的同事。|近义词：surpass, excel, outdo, transcend |反义词：underperform, be inferior to |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His talent outprizes all others in the competition. = 他在比赛中的才能胜过其他人。
This strategy outprizes the previous one in terms of efficiency. = 就效率而言，这个策略优于之前的策略。


一般过去时:
Her performance outprized all expectations. = 她的表现超出了所有人的预期。
The ancient artifact outprized all other finds in the excavation. = 那件古代文物超过了发掘中所有其他发现的价值。


现在完成时:
Their dedication has outprized any initial skepticism. = 他们的奉献精神已经战胜了最初的怀疑。
The new technology has outprized all previous attempts to solve the problem. = 新技术已经超越了之前所有解决这个问题的尝试。


过去完成时:
The team's effort had outprized all previous records. = 球队的努力已经超过了之前的记录。
His contribution had outprized everyone else's on the project. = 他对这个项目的贡献超过了其他所有人。


将来时:
This invention will outprize all its competitors in the market. = 这项发明将在市场上胜过所有竞争对手。
Their combined efforts will outprize the challenges ahead. = 他们共同的努力将战胜未来的挑战。


虚拟语气:
If only her creativity outprized her self-doubt. = 如果她的创造力能战胜她的自我怀疑就好了。
I wish his ambition outprized his fear of failure. = 我希望他的雄心壮志能战胜他对失败的恐惧。


被动语态:
His achievements were outprized by none. = 他的成就无人能及。
The company's profits were outprized by the competitors this year. = 今年公司的利润被竞争对手超越了。


疑问句:
Does her intelligence outprize her physical strength? = 她的智力是否超过她的体力？
Will this new product outprize the existing ones in the market? = 这款新产品会在市场上胜过现有产品吗？


否定句:
His effort did not outprize the difficulties he faced. = 他的努力并没有克服他面临的困难。
The benefits of the plan did not outprize its risks. = 该计划的好处并没有超过其风险。
