lopping:/ˈlɒpɪŋ/|/ˈlɑːpɪŋ/| vt. n.|砍掉树枝；修剪；|The act of cutting off branches from a tree or shrub, often to shape or maintain it.  It can also refer to the act of cutting something off roughly or abruptly.|例句：The gardener was lopping branches from the overgrown apple tree. 园丁正在砍掉那棵苹果树上过长的树枝。

例句：The heavy snow caused several large branches to lop off the oak tree. 大雪导致几根大树枝从橡树上断裂。


近义词：pruning, trimming, cutting, chopping|反义词：growing, cultivating|动词
