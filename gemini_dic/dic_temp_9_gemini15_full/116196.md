mask:/mæsk/| n./v.|面具；掩饰；蒙面|A mask is something worn to cover part of the face, often for disguise or protection.  It can also refer to something that conceals or disguises something else. |例句：She wore a beautiful Venetian mask to the ball. 她戴着一个漂亮威尼斯面具去参加舞会。

He tried to mask his disappointment. 他试图掩饰自己的失望。|近义词：disguise, cover, conceal|反义词：reveal, uncover
