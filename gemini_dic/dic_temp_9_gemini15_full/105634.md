king-hit:/ˈkɪŋhɪt/| v.| 猛地一拳击倒；突然袭击；出其不意地攻击|To strike someone unexpectedly and forcefully, usually with a fist.  The term often implies a powerful blow that knocks the victim unconscious or to the ground. It's predominantly used in Australian English.|例句：He king-hit the other man during the argument, leaving him unconscious. 他在争吵中猛地一拳击倒了另一个人，导致对方昏迷不醒。
He was king-hit from behind and robbed. 他从背后遭到袭击并被抢劫。|近义词：knock out, sucker punch, ambush, surprise attack|反义词：gentle tap, caress
