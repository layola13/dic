excavate:/ˈɛkskeɪveɪt/| vt.|挖掘；发掘；开凿|To remove earth carefully to find buried objects or remains.|例句：Archaeologists excavated the ancient city for years. 考古学家挖掘这座古城多年了。|例句：They are excavating a new tunnel under the river. 他们正在河下开凿一条新隧道。|近义词：dig, unearth, unearth, disinter|反义词：bury, conceal, fill in|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They excavate the site every day. = 他们每天都挖掘这个遗址。
We excavate for fossils. = 我们挖掘化石。

一般过去时:
The team excavated the tomb last year. = 团队去年挖掘了这座墓穴。
Archaeologists excavated the ruins. = 考古学家挖掘了这些废墟。

现在完成时:
They have excavated several ancient artifacts. = 他们已经挖掘出几件古代文物。
We have excavated a large area. = 我们已经挖掘了一个很大的区域。

过去完成时:
They had excavated the site before the rain came. = 雨来之前他们已经挖掘了这个遗址。
The workers had excavated the foundation before the building began. = 在建筑开始之前，工人们已经挖掘好了地基。

将来时:
They will excavate the area next month. = 他们下个月将挖掘该区域。
We will excavate the trench tomorrow. = 我们明天将挖掘沟槽。

虚拟语气:
If they excavated deeper, they might find more artifacts. = 如果他们挖得更深，可能会发现更多文物。
I wish we had excavated that area sooner. = 我真希望我们早点挖掘那个区域。

被动语态:
The site was excavated by a team of experts. = 这个遗址是由一个专家团队挖掘的。
The tunnel is being excavated. = 隧道正在被挖掘。

疑问句:
Have they excavated the entire area? = 他们已经挖掘了整个区域了吗？
When will the excavation begin? = 挖掘工作什么时候开始？

否定句:
They did not excavate the site properly. = 他们没有正确地挖掘该遗址。
We haven't excavated anything interesting yet. = 我们还没有挖掘到任何有趣的东西。


PHRASE->
