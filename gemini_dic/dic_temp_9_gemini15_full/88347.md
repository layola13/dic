hippolith: /ˈhɪpəˌlɪθ/ | n. | 髋骨化石 | A fossilized bone or other mineralized tissue from the hip region of an animal.  Hippoliths are often found in paleontological contexts and can provide valuable information about extinct species. | Example:  The paleontologists discovered several hippoliths belonging to a previously unknown dinosaur species.  古生物学家发现了属于先前未知恐龙物种的几块髋骨化石。 | 近义词:pelvic fossil, hip bone fossil | 反义词: N/A
