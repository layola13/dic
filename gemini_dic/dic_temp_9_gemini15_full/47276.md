de-coding:/ˌdiːˈkoʊdɪŋ/ | n. |解码；译码；破译 | The process of converting coded information into a usable or understandable form.  This includes translating secret messages, interpreting signals, or transforming data from one format to another. | Example: The team worked tirelessly on the decoding of the enemy's encrypted messages.  团队夜以继日地破译敌人的加密信息。|近义词：decryption, deciphering | 反义词：encoding, encryption | 名词
