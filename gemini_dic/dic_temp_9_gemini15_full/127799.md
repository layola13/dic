neighed:/neɪd/| v.|马嘶叫|To make the characteristic loud, high-pitched sound of a horse.|The horse neighed loudly when it saw its owner.  马看到它的主人后大声嘶叫。|The stallion neighed in challenge to the other horses.  种马嘶叫着向其他马匹挑战。| whinnied, screamed |  (无明显反义词, 可用silent或quiet作对比)| 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The horse neighed every morning. = 马每天早上都嘶叫。
He often neighed when he was excited. = 他兴奋的时候经常嘶叫。

一般过去时:
The horse neighed loudly last night. = 昨晚马大声嘶叫。
She neighed when she heard the familiar sound. = 当她听到熟悉的声音时，她嘶叫了一声。

现在完成时:
The horse has neighed several times today. = 马今天已经嘶叫了好几次了。
The colt has never neighed before. = 这匹小马驹以前从未嘶叫过。

过去完成时:
The horse had neighed before I even entered the stable. = 我还没进马厩，马就嘶叫了。
They had neighed continuously for hours before they were finally calmed. = 在最终平静下来之前，它们已经连续嘶叫了好几个小时。

将来时:
The horse will neigh when it hears the bell. = 马听到铃声就会嘶叫。
I think the foal will neigh soon. = 我想小马驹很快就会嘶叫了。

虚拟语气:
If the horse neighed again, I would be startled. = 如果马再嘶叫，我会吓一跳。
I wish the horse wouldn't neigh so loudly at night. = 我希望马晚上不要嘶叫那么大声。


被动语态:
A loud neigh was heard across the field. = 田野那边传来一声响亮的马嘶声。(被动语态较少用在此处)
The neigh was barely audible. = 马嘶声几乎听不见。(被动语态较少用在此处)

疑问句:
Did the horse neigh? = 马嘶叫了吗？
Why did the horse neigh so loudly? = 为什么马嘶叫得那么大声？

否定句:
The horse didn't neigh. = 马没有嘶叫。
The pony didn't neigh at all. = 小马驹根本没有嘶叫。


PHRASE->

The horse neighed its displeasure. = 马嘶叫以表达它的不满。
The horse neighed a greeting. = 马嘶叫着打招呼。
The horse neighed a warning. = 马嘶叫着发出警告。
He neighed with delight. = 他高兴地嘶叫。
