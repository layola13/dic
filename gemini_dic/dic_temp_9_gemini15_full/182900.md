repertoire:/ˈrɛpərtwɑːr/|/ˈrɛpərˌtwɑːr/| n. |曲目，保留剧目，全部技能|A repertoire is the collection of pieces a performer or group is ready to perform; it can also refer to a person's entire range of skills or accomplishments.  |The pianist's repertoire included works by Bach, Mozart, and Beethoven.  这位钢琴家的保留曲目包括巴赫、莫扎特和贝多芬的作品。

The actress expanded her repertoire with a challenging new role.  这位女演员通过一个充满挑战的新角色扩展了她的表演技能范围。|  range, collection, stock,  skill set |  deficiency, lack |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His repertoire includes classical and jazz pieces. = 他的保留曲目包括古典乐和爵士乐作品。
The company's repertoire is quite diverse. = 这家公司的产品线非常多样化。

虚拟语气:
