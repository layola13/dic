repeddled:/ˌriːˈpɛdl/| v.|再次兜售，再次推销|To sell (something) again, especially after it has been previously sold or rejected.|The used car dealership repeddled the vehicle after minor repairs.  二手车经销商在进行小修后再次销售了这辆车。|近义词：resell, re-market, peddle again|反义词：discard, abandon

They repeddle their wares at the flea market. = 他们在跳蚤市场再次兜售他们的商品。
