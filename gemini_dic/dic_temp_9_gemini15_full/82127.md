grok:/ɡrɒk/| /ɡrɑːk/| vt.|理解透彻，完全领会|to understand profoundly and intuitively; to empathize with|例句：I finally grokked the concept after hours of study. =经过数小时的学习，我终于理解了这个概念。
例句：He really grokked the feelings of the characters in the novel. = 他真的体会到了小说中人物的情感。|近义词：understand, grasp, comprehend, empathize with|反义词：misunderstand, misconceive

I grok the problem now. = 我现在理解这个问题了。
They grok the meaning of the poem. = 他们理解这首诗的意思。

She grokked the instructions perfectly. = 她完全理解了指示。

现在完成时:

They had grokked the situation before taking action. = 他们采取行动之前已经理解了情况。
