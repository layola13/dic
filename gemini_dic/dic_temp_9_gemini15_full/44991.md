crystallizes: /ˈkrɪstəlaɪz/ | v. | 使结晶；使明确；(想法、计划等)成形 | To form crystals; to become clear or definite; to take a definite form (especially of ideas or plans). |近义词：solidify, clarify, define |反义词：dissolve, confuse, obscure


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The solution crystallizes slowly. = 溶液缓慢结晶。
His ideas crystallize into a concrete plan. = 他的想法逐渐形成一个具体的计划。


一般过去时:
The sugar crystallized overnight. = 糖在夜间结晶了。
Her vision for the company crystallized during the brainstorming session. = 她在头脑风暴会议期间公司愿景变得清晰。


现在完成时:
The project has finally crystallized after months of planning. = 经过几个月的计划，项目终于成形了。
My thoughts on the matter have crystallized over time. = 我对这件事的看法随着时间的推移变得清晰了。


过去完成时:
By the time the meeting ended, their strategy had crystallized. = 到会议结束时，他们的策略已经明确了。
The artist had crystallized his ideas through years of sketching. = 艺术家通过多年的素描，使他的想法清晰起来。


将来时:
The chemical will crystallize when the temperature drops. = 当温度下降时，化学物质将结晶。
I believe my understanding of this will crystallize after further study. = 我相信经过进一步的研究，我对这一点的理解会更加清晰。


虚拟语气:
If the conditions were right, the substance would crystallize. = 如果条件合适，物质就会结晶。
If he had more time, his ideas might crystallize into a book. = 如果他有更多的时间，他的想法可能会形成一本书。


被动语态:
The sugar was crystallized by slowly cooling the syrup. = 糖是通过慢慢冷却糖浆而结晶的。
The plan was crystallized through many revisions and debates. = 该计划通过多次修改和辩论最终成形。


疑问句:
Does the solution crystallize easily? = 溶液容易结晶吗？
Have his thoughts on the subject crystallized yet? = 他对这个主题的看法已经明确了吗？


否定句:
The solution didn't crystallize because the temperature wasn't low enough. = 溶液没有结晶，因为温度不够低。
His ideas haven't crystallized yet; he needs more time to think. = 他的想法还没有成形；他需要更多时间思考。


PHRASE->
crystallize out =析出晶体
crystallize into = 形成…；发展成…
crystallize around =围绕…形成
crystallize opinion = 明确意见
crystallize the issue = 突出问题
