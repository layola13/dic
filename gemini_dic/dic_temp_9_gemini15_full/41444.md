cornfloor:/ˈkɔːrnflɔːr/| n. |谷仓；谷物仓库|A cornfloor is a floor or area where corn (maize) or other grains are stored, often part of a barn or other agricultural building. It's a place for drying, threshing, or storing harvested crops.|例句：The farmers gathered their corn harvest on the cornfloor to let it dry before storage. 农民们把收割的玉米收集在谷仓的地面上晾干，然后再储存。|近义词：granary, storehouse, barn|反义词：None specifically.
