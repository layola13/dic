downlooked:/ˌdaʊnˈlʊkt/| v.|轻视，看不起|To look down on someone or something; to regard with contempt or disdain.|例句：He felt downlooked upon by his wealthier colleagues. 他感觉被自己更富有的同事看不起。|例句：She downlooked the suggestions from the junior staff. 她轻视了来自初级员工的建议。|近义词：despise, scorn, disdain, belittle|反义词：admire, respect, esteem
