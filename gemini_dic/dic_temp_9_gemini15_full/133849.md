nongraduation: /ˌnɒnˌɡrædʒuˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 未毕业；未获得学位 | The state of not graduating or not receiving a degree.  It often implies a failure to complete the requirements for a degree, not simply choosing not to graduate. |  例句：His nongraduation was a disappointment to his family.  他的未毕业让家人很失望。

例句：The high rate of nongraduation among first-generation college students is a serious concern.  大学新生中高比例的未毕业率是一个严重的问题。


近义词：failure to graduate, non-completion of studies, dropping out (informal)


反义词：graduation, degree completion


词性：名词
