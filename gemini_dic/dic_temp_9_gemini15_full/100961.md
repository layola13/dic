inuring:/ɪˈnjʊərɪŋ/ | v. | 使习惯于；使适应；使硬化 | to make someone or something less sensitive to something unpleasant or painful by gradually exposing them to it |  The soldiers gradually became inured to the harsh conditions. | accustom, habituate, harden | sensitize, soften |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is inuring himself to the cold weather. = 他正在使自己适应寒冷的天气。
They are inuring their children to the rigors of discipline. = 他们在使他们的孩子习惯于严格的纪律。


一般过去时:
She inured herself to the pain of losing her job. = 她使自己习惯于失去工作的痛苦。
We inured ourselves to the long working hours. = 我们使自己习惯于长时间的工作。


现在完成时:
He has inured himself to the pressures of his demanding job. = 他已经习惯了他那份要求很高的工作带来的压力。
They have inured themselves to the constant noise of the city. = 他们已经习惯了城市里不断的噪音。


过去完成时:
She had inured herself to the criticism before the presentation. = 在演示之前，她已经习惯了批评。
They had inured themselves to the difficulties of the journey before setting out. = 他们在出发之前已经习惯了旅途的艰难。


将来时:
He will inure himself to the challenges of the new role. = 他将使自己适应新角色的挑战。
They will inure themselves to the heat of the desert. = 他们将使自己适应沙漠的炎热。


虚拟语气:
If I had inured myself to the stress earlier, I would have handled it better. = 如果我早点适应压力，我会处理得更好。
I wish I had inured myself to public speaking earlier. = 我希望我早点适应公开演讲。


被动语态:
He was inured to the harsh realities of life. = 他习惯了生活的残酷现实。(被动语态在此处不太常见，因为inure通常是主动语态)
The soldiers were slowly inured to the sounds of war. = 士兵们慢慢地习惯了战争的声音。(被动语态在此处不太常见，因为inure通常是主动语态)


疑问句:
How long will it take to inure yourself to the new environment? = 需要多长时间才能适应新的环境？
Are you inuring yourself to the cold gradually? = 你正在逐渐适应寒冷吗？


否定句:
He did not inure himself to the discomfort. = 他没有使自己习惯于这种不适。
She failed to inure herself to the long hours of study. = 她没能使自己习惯于长时间的学习。


PHRASE->
inure oneself to something = 使自己习惯于某事
inure to hardship = 使习惯于苦难
inure to pain = 使习惯于痛苦
inured to the elements = 习惯于各种天气
inured by experience = 经验使之变得无动于衷
