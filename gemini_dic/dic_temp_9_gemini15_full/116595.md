matriculating: /məˈtrɪkjuleɪtɪŋ/ | v. |入学，注册(大学)|  The act of enrolling in a college or university; beginning formal studies.  It implies a formal process of admission. |例句：Many students are matriculating at the university this fall. 今年秋天许多学生正在这所大学入学。

例句：He is matriculating in the engineering program. 他正在工程专业入学。


近义词：enrolling, registering, entering, signing up

反义词：graduating, leaving, withdrawing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is matriculating at Harvard. = 他正在哈佛大学入学。
She matriculates at Oxford every year. = 她每年都在牛津大学入学。


一般过去时:
I matriculated at the University of California, Berkeley. = 我在加州大学伯克利分校入学。
They matriculated last September. = 他们去年九月入学。


现在完成时:
She has matriculated at several universities. = 她已经在几所大学入学。
He has just matriculated into the medical school. = 他刚刚进入医学院入学。


过去完成时:
Before he started his research, he had matriculated at a prestigious university. = 在他开始研究之前，他已在一所著名大学入学。
By the time she graduated, she had matriculated at three different colleges. = 到她毕业时，她已经在三所不同的大学入学。


将来时:
I will matriculate at Stanford next year. = 我明年将在斯坦福大学入学。
They will matriculate at the university in the autumn. = 他们将在秋季进入这所大学入学。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would matriculate at a private university. = 如果我有更多钱，我就会在一所私立大学入学。
I wish I had matriculated at a different school. = 我真希望我当时在不同的学校入学。


被动语态:
He was matriculated into the prestigious program. = 他被录取进入这个著名的项目。
Many students were matriculated at the university. = 许多学生被这所大学录取。


疑问句:
Are you matriculating this semester? = 你这个学期入学吗？
When did he matriculate? = 他是什么时候入学的？


否定句:
He did not matriculate last year. = 他去年没有入学。
She will not matriculate at that university. = 她不会在那所大学入学。


PHRASE->
matriculate at = 在...入学
matriculate into = 进入...入学
matriculation ceremony = 入学典礼
time of matriculation = 入学时间
matriculation requirements = 入学要求

词源:
From Latin *matriculāri, from matricula "register," diminutive of matrix "mother," "womb," from mater "mother."  The original sense was of being entered into a register, hence becoming a member of an institution.
