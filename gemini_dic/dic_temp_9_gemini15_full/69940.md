fesse:/ˈfɛs/| n.|横纹，带状纹章图案|A fesse is a horizontal band across a shield in heraldry, occupying one-third of its height. It is one of the ordinary charges in heraldry, and can be used alone or in combination with other charges.  The fesse can be of any tincture (color), and can be charged with other figures.|The family crest featured a gold fesse on a blue field.  家族纹章以蓝色底色上有一条金色横纹为特色。|band, bar, stripe| (No direct antonym, as it's a specific heraldic term)|名词
