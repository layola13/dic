serum:/ˈsɪərəm/| n.|血清；精华液|A serum is a clear, yellowish liquid that separates from blood when it clots.  It contains antibodies and other proteins.  It can also refer to a liquid preparation for medicinal or cosmetic use.|例句：The doctor ordered a blood serum test to check for antibodies.医生下令进行血清测试以检查抗体。


例句：This face serum promises to reduce wrinkles.  这款面部精华液承诺减少皱纹。|近义词：antiserum,  blood serum|反义词:None|词性:名词

词源:
