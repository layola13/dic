oxfords:/ˈɒksfərdz/ /ˈɑːksfɔːrdz/| n. |牛津鞋；牛津布|Oxford shoes are a type of formal shoe characterized by closed lacing.  Oxford cloth is a tightly woven, fine cotton fabric.|例句：He wore polished oxfords to the wedding. 他穿着擦亮的牛津鞋去参加婚礼。

例句：The shirt is made of high-quality Oxford cloth. 这件衬衫是由优质牛津布制成的。|近义词：balmorals (for shoes), broadcloth (for fabric)|反义词：sneakers, sandals (for shoes), loosely woven fabric (for fabric)|名词
