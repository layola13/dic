evangile:/eˈvæn(d)ʒəl/| n.|福音；圣经|The word "evangile" is an archaic or literary term for "gospel."  It refers to the good news of salvation through Jesus Christ, as recounted in the four canonical gospels of the New Testament.  The term is derived from the Old French and ultimately from the Late Latin "evangelium," itself a transliteration of the Greek "εὐαγγέλιον" (euangelion).|例句：The priest read from the evangile.  牧师朗读福音书。 The evangile brought hope to the downtrodden. 福音给受压迫的人带来了希望。|近义词：gospel, good news|反义词：bad news, ill tidings|名词
