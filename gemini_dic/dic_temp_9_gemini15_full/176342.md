re-evaluated:/ˌriːɪˈvæljueɪtɪd/ | v. |重新评估，重新评价 | To evaluate something again, often with more information or a different perspective.  |The committee re-evaluated the proposal after receiving new data. 委员会在收到新的数据后重新评估了该提案。| reassess, reconsider, reappraise | accept, approve | Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-evaluate the project annually. = 他们每年都会重新评估这个项目。
We re-evaluate our strategies regularly. = 我们定期重新评估我们的策略。


一般过去时:
The teacher re-evaluated John's test after he found a mistake. = 老师在发现错误后重新评估了约翰的考试成绩。
The company re-evaluated its marketing plan after poor sales. = 公司在销售不佳后重新评估了其营销计划。


现在完成时:
They have re-evaluated the risks associated with the project. = 他们已经重新评估了与该项目相关的风险。
She has re-evaluated her career goals since graduating. = 自从毕业以来，她已经重新评估了自己的职业目标。


过去完成时:
They had re-evaluated the budget before making a final decision. = 他们在做出最终决定之前已经重新评估了预算。
The team had re-evaluated the data before presenting its findings. = 该团队在提交调查结果之前已经重新评估了数据。


将来时:
We will re-evaluate the situation next week. = 我们下周将重新评估这种情况。
The board will re-evaluate the CEO's performance next quarter. = 董事会将在下个季度重新评估首席执行官的表现。


虚拟语气:
If we had more information, we would re-evaluate our decision. = 如果我们有更多信息，我们会重新评估我们的决定。
It is essential that we re-evaluate the current strategy. = 我们必须重新评估当前的策略。


被动语态:
The proposal was re-evaluated by the committee. = 该提案已由委员会重新评估。
The budget will be re-evaluated next month. = 预算将于下个月重新评估。


疑问句:
Will the company re-evaluate its pricing strategy? = 公司会重新评估其定价策略吗？
Have they re-evaluated the safety procedures? = 他们是否重新评估了安全程序？


否定句:
They did not re-evaluate the evidence. = 他们没有重新评估证据。
The manager refused to re-evaluate the employee's performance. = 经理拒绝重新评估员工的表现。

re-evaluate the options = 重新评估选项
