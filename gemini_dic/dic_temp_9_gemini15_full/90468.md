hubbles:/ˈhʌbəlz/| n.|哈勃（姓氏）；哈勃望远镜|The word "Hubble" primarily refers to the famous space telescope, the Hubble Space Telescope, named after astronomer Edwin Hubble.  It can also refer to the surname Hubble.|例句：The Hubble telescope has provided stunning images of distant galaxies. 哈勃望远镜提供了令人惊叹的遥远星系的图像。

例句：His name is Hubble. 他的名字是哈勃。|近义词：telescope (when referring to the telescope)|反义词：无|词性：名词
