outgaining: /ˌaʊtˈɡeɪnɪŋ/ | v. |超过，胜过 | surpassing or exceeding in gain or achievement. |例句：The company is outgaining its competitors in market share.  这家公司正在市场份额上超过其竞争对手。

例句：Our team outgained the opponents by a significant margin in yards gained.  我们在获得码数方面以显著优势超过了对手。


近义词：surpassing, exceeding, outperforming, surpassing, outstripping

反义词：underperforming, lagging behind, losing to
