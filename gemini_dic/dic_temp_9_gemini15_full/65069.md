eternities:/ɪˈtɜːrnətiz/ | n. |永恒，漫长时期 |  A period of time that is infinitely long; eternity.  The word often implies a vast and immeasurable length of time, not necessarily literal eternity. |例句：He felt he had waited for eternities. 他感觉他已经等了无数个世纪。

例句：They seemed to spend eternities arguing over the details.  他们似乎花了无穷无尽的时间争论细节。


近义词：aeons, ages, epochs, millennia

反义词：brief moment, instant, fleeting time

词性：名词
