gaganaw:/ˈɡɑːɡənɑːw/| n. |加加纳(印度教神话中的天界)|Gaganaw is a Sanskrit word referring to the sky or heavens in Hindu mythology. It often appears in the context of descriptions of celestial realms and divine beings.  The word evokes a sense of vastness, beauty, and transcendence.|例句：In Hindu mythology, the gods reside in gaganaw.  在印度神话中，众神居住在天界。|近义词：heaven, sky, firmament|反义词：earth, ground|名词
