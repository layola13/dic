notarizes:/ˈnoʊtəraɪzɪz/ | v. | 使...公证；认证 | To give legal validity to (a document, signature, etc.) by notarization.  |  The lawyer notarized the contract. 律师对合同进行了公证。 The notary notarized my signature on the deed. 公证员对我在契约上的签名进行了认证。 |  certify, authenticate, validate |  invalidate, disavow | 动词
