resourcefulness:/rɪˈsɔːrsfəlns/| n. |足智多谋；机智；应变能力 |The ability to find quick and clever ways to overcome difficulties.  It involves using available resources effectively and creatively to achieve goals. |例句：Her resourcefulness allowed her to survive the harsh conditions in the wilderness.  她的足智多谋使她能够在野外恶劣的环境中生存下来。

例句：His resourcefulness in solving the problem impressed everyone. 他解决问题的机智给每个人留下了深刻的印象。


近义词：ingenuity, inventiveness, creativity, adaptability, cleverness |反义词：incompetence, helplessness, ineptitude
