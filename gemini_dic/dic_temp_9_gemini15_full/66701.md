exonerating: /ɪɡˈzɒnəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. (present participle) | 证明无罪；免除责任；使免受指控 |  The act of clearing someone of blame or guilt.  It implies removing the burden of responsibility or accusation. |

例句：

The new evidence exonerating the suspect led to his release from prison. = 新证据证明嫌疑人无罪，导致他被释放出狱。

The report exonerated the company from any responsibility for the accident. = 该报告免除了该公司对事故的任何责任。


近义词：acquit, absolve, clear, vindicate

反义词：condemn, convict, incriminate, inculpate
