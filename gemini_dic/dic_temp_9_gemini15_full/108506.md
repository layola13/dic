laymen:/ˈleɪmən/| n. |外行，非专业人士 |A layman is a person who is not an expert in a particular subject.  Laymen often lack the specialized knowledge and training of professionals. |Example:  The doctor explained the procedure to the laymen in simple terms.  医生用简单的术语向外行解释了这个过程。|近义词：amateur, novice, non-expert |反义词：expert, professional, specialist
