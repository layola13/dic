preconsecrates:/ˌpriːˈkɒnsɪkreɪts/ | vt. |预先奉献；预先祝圣|To consecrate beforehand or in advance; to dedicate something before its official use or purpose.|The priest preconsecrated the altar before the main ceremony.  = 牧师在主要仪式之前预先祝圣了祭坛。

The church was preconsecrated in a private ceremony before the public opening. = 这座教堂在公开开放之前在一个私人仪式上进行了预先祝圣。


近义词：preordain, predetermine, dedicate, hallow


反义词：desecrate, profane, violate


词性：动词
