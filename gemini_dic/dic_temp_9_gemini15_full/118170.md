mentee:/ˌmɛnˈtiː/| n. |指导者，受指导者，（尤指）师徒关系中的学生|A person who is guided and advised by a mentor.|例句：She is a dedicated mentee, always eager to learn from her mentor. 她是一位尽职尽责的受指导者，总是渴望向她的导师学习。

例句：He found a great mentor who helped him become a successful mentee in his chosen field. 他找到了一位很棒的导师，帮助他成为所在领域里成功的受指导者。


近义词：pupil, apprentice, protégé, trainee|反义词：mentor


词源:源自动词"ment" (指导，培养)，加上后缀"-ee" (表示接受动作的人)。  The word is a relatively modern coinage, emerging in the late 20th century as a counterpart to "mentor".


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a mentee of a well-known entrepreneur. = 他是一位知名企业家的受指导者。
The company provides mentorship programs for its mentees. = 公司为其受指导者提供指导计划。


一般过去时:
She was a mentee in a leadership development program. = 她曾是领导力发展项目中的受指导者。
He became a mentee under the wing of a successful author. = 他成为了一位成功作家的门下弟子。


现在完成时:
He has been a mentee for three years. = 他已经当了三年的受指导者。
She has benefited greatly from being a mentee. = 她从成为受指导者中受益匪浅。


过去完成时:
He had been a mentee before he started his own business. = 在他创业之前，他曾是一位受指导者。
She had already been a mentee for several years when she launched her own project. = 当她启动自己的项目时，她已经做了几年的受指导者了。


将来时:
She will be a mentee in the upcoming program. = 她将成为即将开始的项目中的受指导者。
He will continue to be a mentee even after completing the program. = 即使完成项目后，他仍将继续担任受指导者。


虚拟语气:
If he were a mentee, he would have more opportunities. = 如果他是受指导者，他将会有更多机会。
I wish I had been a mentee earlier in my career. = 我希望在我的职业生涯早期就成为一名受指导者。


被动语态:

The mentees are carefully selected for the program. = 受指导者被仔细挑选参加该项目。


疑问句:

Who is your mentee? = 你的受指导者是谁？


否定句:
He is not a mentee. = 他不是受指导者。
She was not a mentee in that program. = 她不是那个项目中的受指导者。
