overrewards:/ˌoʊvərɪˈwɔːrdz/| v.|过度奖励，给予过多的奖励 | To reward excessively or beyond what is deserved or appropriate.  |例句：The company overrewards its executives, leading to resentment among other employees. 公司对高管的奖励过度，导致其他员工不满。|例句：His exceptional performance was overrewarded with a bonus far exceeding expectations. 他的杰出表现获得了远超预期的奖金，属于过度奖励。|近义词：overcompensate, overpay, lavish |反义词：underpay, underreward |动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company overrewards its top performers. = 公司过度奖励其顶尖员工。
They often overreward employees for minor achievements. = 他们经常因为微不足道的成就而过度奖励员工。


一般过去时:
The CEO overrewarded himself last year. = 去年CEO给自己发了过高的奖金。
The project team was overrewarded for completing the task ahead of schedule. = 项目团队因为提前完成任务而获得了过高的奖励。


现在完成时:
The company has overrewarded its executives for years. = 公司多年来一直过度奖励其高管。
They have overrewarded him for his contribution to the project. = 他们因为他在项目中的贡献而过度奖励了他。


过去完成时:

The management had overrewarded the sales team before realizing the sales figures were inflated. = 管理层在意识到销售数据被夸大之前，就已经过度奖励了销售团队。


将来时:
The company will likely overreward its employees again this year. = 公司今年很可能会再次过度奖励员工。
They will overreward the team if they meet their ambitious targets. = 如果他们达到雄心勃勃的目标，他们将过度奖励团队。


虚拟语气:
If the company hadn't overrewarded its employees, they wouldn't have become complacent. = 如果公司没有过度奖励员工，他们就不会变得自满。
I wish they hadn't overrewarded him so much; it created unfairness. = 我真希望他们没有给他那么多的奖励；这造成了不公平。


被动语态:
The employees were overrewarded for their work on the project. = 员工因为在项目上的工作而获得了过高的奖励。
The executives felt they were overrewarded compared to the rest of the staff. = 高管们觉得他们与其他员工相比，获得了过高的奖励。


疑问句:

Do you think the bonus system overrewards certain individuals? = 你认为奖金制度是否过度奖励某些个人？


否定句:

They did not intend to overreward the team, but the bonus turned out to be quite substantial. = 他们无意过度奖励团队，但奖金最终相当可观。
