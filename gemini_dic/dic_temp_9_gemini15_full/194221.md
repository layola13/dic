self-made:/ˌsɛlfˈmeɪd/ | adj. |白手起家的，自力更生的|  Describes someone who has achieved success or wealth through their own efforts, without help from others.  It emphasizes the individual's hard work and determination. |例句：He is a self-made millionaire, having started his business from scratch. 他是一位白手起家的百万富翁，他的事业是从零开始的。|  例句：She is a self-made woman who overcame many obstacles to achieve her goals. 她是一位自力更生的女性，克服了许多障碍才实现了自己的目标。|近义词：self-reliant, independent, bootstrapped |反义词：privileged, inherited, entitled
