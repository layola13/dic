non-unitarian:/ˌnɒnjuːnɪˈteəriən/| adj.|非一元论的；非唯一神的|Not adhering to Unitarianism;  rejecting the doctrine of the oneness of God.  It often implies a belief in a plurality of gods or in a concept of God that differs significantly from the Unitarian conception.|例句：His theological views were considered non-unitarian by the church elders.  他的神学观点被教会长老们认为是非一元论的。|近义词：polytheistic, pluralistic|反义词：unitarian
