glorying:/ˈɡlɔːriɪŋ/| v.|得意洋洋；沾沾自喜；为…而自豪|Taking great pride or pleasure in something.  It implies a feeling of self-satisfaction, sometimes to an excessive degree.|例句：He was glorying in his success. 他为自己的成功而沾沾自喜。

例句：She was glorying in the beauty of the sunset. 她陶醉于日落的美丽。


近义词：rejoicing, exulting, triumphing, boasting

反义词：despairing, lamenting, mourning

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
If I had won the lottery, I would have gloried in my good fortune. 如果我中了彩票，我会为我的好运气而沾沾自喜。

被动语态:
His achievements were gloried in by his family and friends. 他的成就受到了家人和朋友的赞扬。
The beauty of the artwork was gloried in by the critics.  评论家们赞扬了这件艺术品的美丽。


疑问句:

否定句:
He did not glory in his wealth; he used it to help others. 他并没有为自己的财富而沾沾自喜；他用它来帮助他人。
She didn't glory in her success; she remained humble. 她并没有为自己的成功而沾沾自喜；她依然保持谦逊。
