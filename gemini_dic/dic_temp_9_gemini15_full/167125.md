presuspicious:/ˌpriːsəˈspɪʃəs/| adj. |令人怀疑的，可疑的 |Arousing suspicion or doubt; likely to cause suspicion.  This word combines "pre-" (before) and "suspicious," suggesting something that gives rise to suspicion before full evidence is available.|例句：The presuspicious activity near the bank prompted a police investigation.  银行附近可疑的活动促使警方展开调查。


例句：His presuspicious behavior made everyone nervous. 他可疑的行为让每个人都紧张不安。|近义词：dubious, questionable, suspect, shady|反义词：trustworthy, reliable, aboveboard
