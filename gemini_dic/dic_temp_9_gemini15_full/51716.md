division:/dɪˈvɪʒən/ | n. |分割，划分；部门，科；除法；裂痕|The act of dividing or separating something into parts; a part or section of something; the mathematical process of dividing one number by another; a crack or split.|例句：The division of the land into smaller plots increased the yield.  土地分割成较小的地块提高了产量。|例句：The marketing division is responsible for advertising campaigns. 市场部负责广告宣传活动。|近义词：section, part, department, separation, fraction|反义词：union, combination, whole, integration|名词


USAGE->

The company plans a new division next year. = 公司计划明年设立一个新的部门。
