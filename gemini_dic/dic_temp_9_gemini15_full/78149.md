geophysicists:/ˌdʒiːoʊfɪˈzɪsɪsts/| n. |地球物理学家|Scientists who study the Earth's physical properties, including its composition, structure, and processes.|例句：Geophysicists use seismic waves to image the Earth's interior. 地球物理学家使用地震波来成像地球内部。|例句：Many geophysicists are involved in the exploration for oil and gas. 许多地球物理学家参与石油和天然气的勘探。|近义词：geologists, seismologists|反义词:无
