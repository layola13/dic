diplomatics: /dɪpləˈmætɪks/ | n. |外交学；外交；外交文件研究 | The study of diplomatic practice and the documents associated with it.  It involves the analysis of treaties, correspondence, and other official records to understand historical events and diplomatic relations.  | 例句：The course on diplomatics helped students understand the nuances of international negotiations.  这门外交学课程帮助学生理解国际谈判的细微之处。

例句：  She is an expert in diplomatics, specializing in 18th-century European treaties. 她是外交学专家，专门研究18世纪的欧洲条约。


近义词：diplomacy, international relations, foreign affairs | 反义词：N/A


词源:  From  "diplomacy" + "-ics" (suffix forming nouns denoting a branch of study or a system of principles).  The root ultimately traces back to Greek *diplōma ("a letter folded double; a document," from *diploun ("doubled")).
