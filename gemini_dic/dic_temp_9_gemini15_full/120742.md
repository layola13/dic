misanalyzing:/ˌmɪsænəˈlaɪzɪŋ/| v. |误解；分析错误 |  The act of interpreting or understanding something incorrectly.  It involves a flawed analysis leading to a wrong conclusion. | 例句：He was misanalyzing the data, leading to incorrect conclusions. 他误解了数据，导致了错误的结论。

例句：Misanalyzing the market trends led to the company's downfall.  误解市场趋势导致了公司的倒闭。


近义词：misinterpreting, misunderstanding, misreading, misconstruing | 反义词：analyzing correctly, understanding accurately
