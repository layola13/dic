earn:/ɜːrn/| /ɝn/ | vt. |赚得；获得；赢得 |to receive money or something else as payment for work or an investment.  |She earns a good salary as a doctor. 她作为一名医生赚取了不错的薪水。|He earned a reputation for honesty. 他赢得了诚实的声誉。| gain, acquire, win, obtain | lose, forfeit |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I earn a living as a teacher. = 我靠教书为生。
She earns a high salary in her job. = 她在工作中赚取高薪。

一般过去时:
He earned a lot of money last year. = 他去年赚了很多钱。
They earned their keep by working hard. = 他们通过努力工作维持生计。

现在完成时:
I have earned enough money for a new car. = 我已经攒够了买新车的钱。
She has earned a promotion through hard work. = 她通过努力工作赢得了晋升。

过去完成时:
He had earned his degree before he got the job. = 他在得到这份工作之前已经获得了学位。
They had earned a great deal of respect in the community. = 他们在社区赢得了极大的尊重。

将来时:
I will earn more money next year. = 我明年会赚更多钱。
She will have earned a fortune by the time she retires. = 她退休时将会赚得一笔财富。

虚拟语气:
If I earned more money, I would travel more. = 如果我赚更多钱，我会去旅游更多。
I wish I earned a higher salary. = 我希望我能赚更高的薪水。

被动语态:
A lot of money was earned by the company last year. = 公司去年赚了很多钱。
His respect was earned through years of hard work. = 他的尊重是通过多年的努力工作而获得的。

疑问句:
How much do you earn a month? = 你一个月赚多少钱？
What did he earn from his investment? = 他从投资中赚了多少钱？

否定句:
I don't earn enough money to support my family. = 我赚的钱不够养家糊口。
She didn't earn a bonus this year. = 她今年没有拿到奖金。


PHRASE->
earn a living = 谋生
earn one's keep = 养活自己
earn respect = 赢得尊重
earn a reputation = 赢得名声
earn one's stripes =  获得晋升（通过努力工作）
