non-dependencies: /ˌnɒn dɪˈpɛndənsiz/ | n. |非依赖性，非依赖项 |  Non-dependencies refer to elements, components, or factors that are not reliant or dependent on other elements within a system or process.  This term is often used in software engineering, project management, and other fields where systems are analyzed for their inter-relationships.  It highlights the independent nature of certain parts. | Example: The modular design minimized non-dependencies, allowing for independent updates of individual components.  = 模块化设计最大限度地减少了非依赖项，允许独立更新各个组件。 | 近义词:  independence, autonomy, self-sufficiency | 反义词: dependencies, interdependencies, reliance
