ovipositing:/ˌoʊvɪˈpɒzɪtɪŋ/ | v. |产卵；卵生|The act of laying eggs, especially by insects.  It encompasses the entire process from selecting a suitable site to depositing the eggs.  |例句：The butterfly was ovipositing on the underside of a leaf.  这只蝴蝶正在叶子的背面产卵。|例句：Many species of wasps oviposit in or on their prey. 许多种黄蜂在其猎物身上或体内产卵。|近义词：laying eggs, egg-laying|反义词：viviparous (live birth)
