nondependents:/ˌnɑːnˈdɪpɛndənts/| n. |非被抚养人，非供养人 |People who are not financially dependent on someone else, such as a spouse or parent.  This term is often used in contexts like census data, insurance, or financial aid applications. |Example:  The number of nondependents in the household increased after the children moved out.  这个家庭的非供养人数量在孩子们搬出去后增加了。
|近义词：independent individuals, self-supporting individuals |反义词：dependents
