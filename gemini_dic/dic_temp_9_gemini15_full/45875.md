cute:/kjuːt/| adj.|可爱的，讨人喜欢的|  Cute describes something or someone that is attractive in a charming or delightful way, often small and appealing.  It often implies innocence and sweetness. | Example:  That puppy is so cute!  那只小狗太可爱了！ The baby's smile is so cute. 婴儿的笑容太可爱了。|近义词：adorable, charming, delightful, sweet, endearing |反义词：ugly, repulsive, unpleasant, hideous
