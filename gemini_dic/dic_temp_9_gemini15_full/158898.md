plundering:/ˈplʌndərɪŋ/| v.|掠夺；抢劫；洗劫|The act of stealing valuable things from a place, especially during a war or other violent event.  It implies a forceful and often widespread taking of possessions.|例句：The pirates were plundering the coastal villages. 海盗正在掠夺沿海村庄。

例句：The invading army spent weeks plundering the city.  侵略军花了数周时间洗劫这座城市。


近义词：looting, pillaging, ransacking, despoiling

反义词：donating, contributing, giving


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are plundering the museum. = 他们正在洗劫博物馆。
The soldiers are plundering the town. = 士兵们正在掠夺城镇。

一般过去时:
The Vikings plundered the monastery. = 维京人洗劫了修道院。
The bandits plundered the caravan. = 强盗抢劫了商队。

现在完成时:
They have plundered the entire village. = 他们已经洗劫了整个村庄。
The rebels have plundered many towns. = 叛乱分子已经掠夺了许多城镇。

过去完成时:
The city had been plundered before the army arrived. = 在军队到达之前，这座城市已经被洗劫一空了。
The villagers had already been plundered by the time help arrived. = 等到救援到来时，村民们已经被抢劫过了。

将来时:
They will plunder the next town tomorrow. = 他们明天将掠夺下一个城镇。
The pirates will plunder the ship tonight. = 海盗今晚将洗劫那艘船。

虚拟语气:
If they plundered the bank, they would be arrested. = 如果他们抢劫银行，他们会被逮捕。
I wish they hadn't plundered the artifacts. = 我真希望他们没有掠夺那些文物。

被动语态:
The city was plundered by the enemy. = 这座城市被敌人洗劫了。
The village has been plundered repeatedly. = 这个村庄被多次掠夺。

疑问句:
Were the treasures plundered? = 这些宝藏被掠夺了吗？
Who plundered the temple? = 谁洗劫了寺庙？

否定句:
They did not plunder the shops. = 他们没有抢劫商店。
The army didn't plunder the peaceful villages. = 军队没有掠夺和平的村庄。
