redeliberated:/ˌriːdɪˈlɪbəreɪtɪd/| v.|重新考虑，再审议 |To consider something again, especially after a previous decision has been made.  |例句：The jury redeliberated the case after hearing new evidence.  陪审团在听到新的证据后重新审理了此案。
例句：After much debate, the committee redeliberated its initial proposal.  经过多次辩论，委员会重新审议了其最初的提案。|近义词：reconsider, review, rethink |反义词：decide, conclude
