nonimpressionability: /ˌnɒnɪmˈprɛʃənəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不易受影响，冷漠，无动于衷|The inability to be influenced or affected; lack of susceptibility.  This word describes a state of being resistant to external influences, ideas, or emotions. It suggests a lack of responsiveness or receptivity. |The nonimpressionability of the judge was evident in his unwavering adherence to the law, regardless of public opinion.  法官对法律的坚定遵守，而不受公众舆论的影响，充分体现了他的不易受影响。

|近义词: insusceptibility, impassivity, imperviousness, unresponsiveness |反义词: susceptibility, impressionability, sensitivity, responsiveness |
