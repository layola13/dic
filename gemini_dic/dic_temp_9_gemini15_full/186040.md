rewording:/ˌriːˈwɜːrdɪŋ/| n.|改写；重新措辞|The act of expressing something again using different words; phrasing something differently.|例句：The rewording of the contract made it much clearer.  合同的改写使它清晰多了。|近义词：rephrasing, paraphrasing, reformulation|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be leaving the wording unchanged.
