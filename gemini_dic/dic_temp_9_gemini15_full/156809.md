pigeonry:/ˈpɪdʒənri/| n. |鸽舍；养鸽场|A structure or enclosure for keeping pigeons.  It can range from a simple coop to a large, elaborate building.  |例句：The old pigeonry was falling into disrepair.  古老的鸽舍正在逐渐破败。|例句：He spent his weekends tending his prize-winning pigeons in his spacious pigeonry. 他周末都花在宽敞的鸽舍里照料他获奖的鸽子。|近义词：dovecote, pigeon house|反义词：无

词源:
The word "pigeonry" is derived from the Old French word "pigeonnier," which itself comes from the Latin word "pipio," meaning "a young pigeon." The suffix "-ry" indicates a place or collection of something.
