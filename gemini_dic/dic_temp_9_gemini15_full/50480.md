depression: /dɪˈprɛʃən/ | n. | 沮丧；抑郁症；萧条；低气压 |

英文解析：A state of low mood and aversion to activity that can affect a person's thoughts, behavior, feelings, and sense of well-being.  It can range from mild sadness to severe mental illness.  The term can also refer to a period of economic decline or a lowering of something, such as atmospheric pressure.

例句：
He's suffering from depression and needs professional help.  他患有抑郁症，需要专业人士的帮助。

The Great Depression of the 1930s devastated the global economy.  20世纪30年代的大萧条摧毁了全球经济。


近义词：dejection, despondency, melancholy, downturn, slump, recession

反义词：elation, euphoria, boom, prosperity, high


词性: 名词
