lignin: /ˈlɪɡnɪn/ | n. |木质素|A complex polymer that is a major component of the cell walls of plants, especially wood, providing rigidity and strength.  It is a crucial component of the secondary cell walls of plants and is responsible for much of their structural support.|  The lignin in the wood made it very difficult to carve.  这木头里的木质素使得它很难雕刻。| cellulose, hemicellulose |  (no direct antonym, but could be described as the opposite of easily degradable plant matter) | 名词
