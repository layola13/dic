chair:/tʃeər/ | n. |椅子 |A seat with a back for one person.|例句：Please take a chair. 请坐。
例句：The chair is made of wood. 这把椅子是木头做的。|近义词：seat, stool |反义词：standing position

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sits in the chair every day. 他每天都坐在椅子上。
The chair stands in the corner. 椅子放在角落里。

一般过去时:
She sat on the chair yesterday. 她昨天坐在椅子上。
They moved the chair to another room. 他们把椅子搬到了另一个房间。

现在完成时:
I have sat in this chair for hours. 我已经在这把椅子上坐了好几个小时了。
We have repaired the broken chair. 我们已经修好了坏掉的椅子。

过去完成时:
He had already sat in the chair before I arrived. 我到达之前他已经坐在椅子上了。
They had moved the chair before the meeting started. 会议开始前他们已经把椅子移开了。

将来时:
I will sit in that chair tomorrow. 我明天将坐在那把椅子上。
She will buy a new chair next week. 她下周将买一把新椅子。

虚拟语气:
If I had a chair, I would sit down. 如果我有一把椅子，我就坐下。
I wish I had a comfortable chair to sit in. 我希望我有一把舒服的椅子可以坐。

被动语态:
The chair was broken by the child. 椅子被孩子弄坏了。
The chairs are arranged in a circle. 椅子被排成一个圆圈。

疑问句:
Is there a chair available? 有空椅子吗？
Where is the chair? 椅子在哪里？

否定句:
There is no chair in the room. 房间里没有椅子。
He didn't sit on the chair. 他没有坐在椅子上。
