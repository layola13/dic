notionally:/ˈnoʊʃənəli/ | adv. |名义上地；理论上地；概念上地 | In theory or principle, but not necessarily in reality.  It suggests something is true in name or concept, but may not be true in practice. |例句：Notionally, the project is complete, but there are still a few minor issues to resolve.  名义上，项目已经完成了，但仍有一些小问题需要解决。

例句：The company is notionally profitable, but its actual cash flow is negative.该公司名义上盈利，但实际现金流为负。


近义词：theoretically, nominally, conceptually, ostensibly | 反义词：actually, really, practically, in reality
