grieving:/ˈɡriːvɪŋ/| v. |悲伤，哀悼|The act of feeling or expressing sorrow or grief, especially for the death of a loved one. It involves a complex emotional response including sadness, anger, guilt, and acceptance.  |例句：She is grieving the loss of her husband. 她正为失去丈夫而悲伤。

例句：The family is grieving the death of their beloved pet.  这个家庭正为他们心爱的宠物的死而悲伤。


近义词：mourning, lamenting, sorrowing, mourning, bewailing

反义词：celebrating, rejoicing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grieves for his lost friend. 他为失去的朋友而悲伤。
They grieve the loss of their home. 他们为失去家园而悲伤。


一般过去时:
She grieved deeply after her mother's death.  她母亲去世后悲痛欲绝。
We grieved for many months following the accident. 事故发生后，我们悲痛了几个月。


现在完成时:
He has been grieving since his father passed away.  自从他父亲去世后，他一直很悲痛。
They have grieved for years over the loss of their child.  他们为失去孩子而悲痛多年了。


过去完成时:
She had grieved for years before she finally began to heal.  在她最终开始痊愈之前，她已经悲痛了多年。
They had grieved intensely before they could accept the reality. 在他们能够接受现实之前，他们曾非常悲痛。


将来时:
He will grieve the loss of his job for a long time. 他会为失去工作而悲伤很长时间。
I know she will grieve for many years to come. 我知道她还会悲伤很多年。


虚拟语气:
If I lost my dog, I would grieve terribly. 如果我失去了我的狗，我会非常悲伤。
I wish I hadn't made him grieve so much. 我希望我没有让他如此悲伤。


被动语态:
The loss was deeply grieved by the entire community.  整个社区都为这损失深感悲痛。
His death was grieved by all who knew him. 所有认识他的人都为他去世而悲伤。


疑问句:
Is she still grieving the loss of her father? 她还在为失去父亲而悲伤吗？
Are they grieving over the loss of their business? 他们还在为失去生意而悲伤吗？


否定句:
He didn't grieve openly, but his sadness was evident. 他没有公开悲伤，但他的悲伤是显而易见的。
They did not grieve as much as expected. 他们没有像预期的那样悲伤。
