lovers:/ˈlʌvərz/| n.| 爱人，情侣 |People who are in love and have a romantic relationship.|例句：The lovers strolled hand-in-hand along the beach.  情侣们手牵着手沿着海滩散步。|近义词：sweethearts, partners, couples|反义词：enemies, adversaries


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lovers meet secretly every Tuesday. = 这对恋人每周二秘密会面。
My friends are lovers of classical music. = 我的朋友们是古典音乐爱好者。

一般过去时:
The lovers argued last night. = 这对恋人昨晚吵架了。
They were lovers in college. = 他们大学时是恋人。

现在完成时:
They have been lovers for five years. = 他们已经恋爱五年了。
We have seen many lovers walking in the park. = 我们看到许多情侣在公园里散步。

过去完成时:
They had been lovers for a year before they got married. = 他们结婚前已经恋爱一年了。
I had never seen such devoted lovers before. = 我以前从未见过如此恩爱的恋人。

将来时:
The lovers will be married next month. = 这对恋人下个月将结婚。
They will always be lovers in my heart. = 在我的心里，他们将永远是恋人。

虚拟语气:
If they were lovers, they would be very happy. = 如果他们是恋人，他们会非常幸福。
I wish I had known they were lovers sooner. = 我真希望我早点知道他们是恋人。

被动语态:
The lovers were often seen together. = 这对恋人经常被看到在一起。
Their love story was written in the book. = 他们的爱情故事写在那本书里。

疑问句:
Are they lovers? = 他们是恋人吗？
Who are your lovers? = 你的爱人们是谁？（此句用法较少见，通常指对某事物热爱的人）

否定句:
They are not lovers; they are just friends. = 他们不是恋人，只是朋友。
He wasn't her lover; he was just a friend. = 他不是她的爱人，只是朋友。
