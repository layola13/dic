This term refers to the relationships, interactions, and comparisons between distinct populations, often focusing on genetic exchange, competition, or other ecological dynamics.  It is used in various fields, including biology, ecology, genetics, and sociology. | Example: The research focused on interpopulation gene flow in migratory birds.  这项研究侧重于候鸟种群间的基因流动。  |近义词：intergroup, cross-population |反义词：intrapopulation (种群内) |名词
