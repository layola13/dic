dispersedly:/dɪˈspɜːrsɪdli/ | adv. | 分散地，散乱地 | In a scattered or spread-out manner.  | The seeds were dispersedly sown across the field. 种子分散地撒在田野里。 |  scatteredly, diffusely, widely | concentratedly, centrally


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't conjugate like verbs or decline like nouns.  It doesn't have tenses, voices, or moods.  Its usage is primarily descriptive, modifying verbs or entire clauses.


Example Sentences:

The protesters were dispersedly positioned throughout the city square. 抗议者分散地分布在城市广场。

The wildflowers bloomed dispersedly across the meadow. 野花分散地开放在草地上。
