fernery:/ˈfɜːrnəri/| n. |蕨类植物园；蕨类植物丛|A fernery is a place where ferns are grown, either indoors or outdoors.  It can refer to a dedicated area in a garden, a greenhouse, or even a room in a house specifically designed for cultivating ferns.|例句：The botanical garden boasts a stunning fernery with hundreds of different fern species.  这家植物园拥有一个令人惊叹的蕨类植物园，里面有数百种不同的蕨类植物。

The shaded fernery provided a cool and tranquil retreat.  阴凉的蕨类植物园提供了一个凉爽宁静的休憩场所。|近义词：fern garden, fern house|反义词：无
