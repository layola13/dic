intercommissions:/ˌɪntər.kəˈmɪʃənz/| n. |相互佣金；相互委托；(尤指公司间的)相互业务往来|Payments or fees exchanged between companies for referring clients or business to each other.  It often involves a percentage of the profits or fees generated from the referred business.|例句：The two companies have a strong history of intercommissions, benefiting both significantly. 这两家公司有着强大的相互佣金往来历史，双方都从中受益匪浅。


The two marketing firms agreed on a system of intercommissions for cross-promotion.|两家营销公司就交叉推广的相互佣金制度达成了协议。

近义词：mutual commissions, reciprocal referrals, cross-promotion agreements|反义词：No direct antonym, but the opposite concept could be  independent operations or competitive arrangements.
