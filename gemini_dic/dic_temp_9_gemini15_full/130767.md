nonanalogously:/ˌnɒnænˈæləɡəsli/ | adv. |非类似地；不同地 |  Not in a similar or analogous way; differently.  It emphasizes a lack of similarity or correspondence between two things. | Example:  The two situations are nonanalogously related; one involves a legal dispute, the other a personal disagreement.  两种情况的关系并不类似；一种涉及法律纠纷，另一种涉及个人分歧。|近义词：differently, dissimilarly, unconformably | 反义词：analogously, similarly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".  It modifies verbs, describing how an action is performed.  The grammatical structures will vary depending on the sentence structure.


Example 1 (Simple sentence): He approached the problem nonanalogously to the previous one. 他处理这个问题的方式与之前不同。

Example 2 (Complex sentence): Although they faced similar challenges, they handled them nonanalogously, leading to different outcomes. 尽管他们面临着类似的挑战，但他们的处理方式却大相径庭，导致了不同的结果。

There are no common phrases specifically using "nonanalogously".  Its usage is within sentences, modifying verbs to show a difference in approach or comparison.
