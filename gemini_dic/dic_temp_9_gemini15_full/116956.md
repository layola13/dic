mazourka:/məˈzʊrkə/| n. |玛祖卡舞曲|A Mazurka is a Polish dance in 3/4 time, typically lively and with a characteristic rhythmic accent on the second or third beat.  It is often characterized by its strong, accented rhythm and its use of a distinctive syncopated rhythm. The music is usually lively and spirited. |Example: Chopin's Mazurkas are some of his most popular works.  肖邦的玛祖卡舞曲是他最受欢迎的作品之一。|近义词：polonaise (波兰舞曲), waltz (华尔兹)|反义词：无
