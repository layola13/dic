catlinite:/ˈkætlaɪnaɪt/| n. |红石, 卡特林石 |A fine-grained, reddish-brown pipestone quarried near Pipestone, Minnesota, and used by Native Americans for making pipes and other ceremonial objects.  Catlinite is a type of argillite, a sedimentary rock formed from clay and silt. |The Native American artisans carved intricate designs into the catlinite.  美洲原住民工匠在卡特林石上雕刻出复杂的图案。|近义词：pipestone, argillite |反义词：无
