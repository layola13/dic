shar:/ʃɑːr/ | v. | 分享，分担，削尖 | To divide and distribute among several people; to participate in something with others; to make something pointed.  |例句：Let's shar the cost. = 让我们分摊费用。|例句：We shared a pizza. = 我们一起吃了一个披萨。|近义词：divide, distribute, partake, split | 反义词：hoard, keep to oneself, conceal |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They share a flat. = 他们合住一套公寓。
She shares her toys with her friends. = 她和朋友们分享她的玩具。

一般过去时:
We shared a taxi to the airport. = 我们合乘一辆出租车去机场。
He shared his experiences with us. = 他与我们分享了他的经验。


现在完成时:
I have shared my ideas with the team. = 我已经和团队分享了我的想法。
She has shared her life story with many people. = 她已经和许多人分享了她的人生故事。


过去完成时:
They had shared the secret before the meeting. = 他们早在会议前就分享了这个秘密。
She had shared her concerns with her parents before making the decision. = 在做决定之前，她已经和父母分享了她的担忧。


将来时:
We will share the profits equally. = 我们将平均分配利润。
They will share their knowledge with the students. = 他们将把他们的知识与学生分享。


虚拟语气:
If I had shared my concerns earlier, things might have been different. = 如果我早点表达我的担忧，情况可能就不一样了。
I wish I had shared my feelings with him. = 我真希望我当时和他分享了我的感受。


被动语态:
The profits were shared equally among the partners. = 利润在合伙人之间平均分配。
The information will be shared with all employees. = 信息将与所有员工共享。


疑问句:
Will you share your food with me? = 你愿意和我分享你的食物吗？
Do they share the same beliefs? = 他们有相同的信仰吗？


否定句:
He didn't share his feelings. = 他没有分享他的感受。
She doesn't share my opinion on this matter. = 她在这个问题上不同意我的意见。
