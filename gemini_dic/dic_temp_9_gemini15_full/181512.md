rekeyboard:/ˌriːˈkiːbɔːrd/ | n. |重新键盘输入；重新键入 |A rekeyboard is the act of typing something again, often to correct errors or to improve the text. It can also refer to the process of re-entering data using a keyboard.  |例句：After discovering several typos, she performed a rekeyboard of the entire document.  发现几个错别字后，她重新键入了整篇文档。

The software prompted a rekeyboard of the sensitive data to ensure accuracy. 该软件提示重新输入敏感数据以确保准确性。|近义词：re-type, re-enter |反义词：N/A
