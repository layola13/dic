mistreats:/ˌmɪsˈtriːts/| v.|虐待；粗暴对待|To treat (a person or animal) cruelly or badly.|例句：He mistreats his animals. 他虐待他的动物。

例句：The company mistreats its employees. 这家公司虐待其员工。


近义词：abuse, ill-treat, maltreat, mishandle

反义词：cherish, care for, nurture


USAGE->

She mistreats her children. = 她虐待她的孩子。
