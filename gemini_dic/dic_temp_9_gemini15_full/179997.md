refinances:/ˌriːˈfaɪnænsɪz/| v.| 再融资，重新贷款 | To obtain a new loan to replace an existing one, typically at a lower interest rate.  |例句：He refinanced his mortgage to get a lower interest rate. 他重新贷款以获得更低的利率。
例句：The company refinanced its debt to improve its financial position.  这家公司进行了债务再融资以改善财务状况。|近义词：renegotiate, restructure |反义词：foreclose


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He refinances his mortgage every few years. = 他每隔几年就重新贷款抵押。
They refinance their business loans regularly. = 他们定期为其商业贷款进行再融资。

一般过去时:
She refinanced her car loan last month. = 她上个月为她的汽车贷款进行了再融资。
We refinanced our home loan to consolidate debt. = 我们重新贷款抵押房屋以合并债务。

现在完成时:
He has refinanced his mortgage twice this year. = 他今年已经两次重新贷款抵押了。
They have refinanced their loans to take advantage of lower rates. = 他们已经进行了贷款再融资以利用较低的利率。

过去完成时:
Before buying the new house, they had refinanced their existing mortgage. = 在购买新房之前，他们已经对现有的抵押贷款进行了再融资。
She had refinanced her student loans before graduating. = 她毕业前已经对学生贷款进行了再融资。

将来时:
We will refinance our mortgage next year. = 我们明年将重新贷款抵押。
They plan to refinance their business loan in the spring. = 他们计划在春季为其商业贷款进行再融资。

虚拟语气:
If I had refinanced sooner, I would have saved more money. = 如果我早点再融资，我会省更多钱。
I wish I had refinanced my mortgage last year. = 我希望我去年就对我的抵押贷款进行了再融资。

被动语态:
The mortgage was refinanced at a lower interest rate. = 该抵押贷款以较低的利率进行了再融资。
Their loans are being refinanced by a new lender. = 他们的贷款正在由新的贷款人进行再融资。

疑问句:
Have you ever refinanced a loan? = 你曾经进行过贷款再融资吗？
Will they refinance their debt this quarter? = 他们本季度会对债务进行再融资吗？

否定句:
They did not refinance their mortgage. = 他们没有重新贷款抵押。
She hasn't yet refinanced her student loans. = 她还没有对学生贷款进行再融资。


PHRASE->

词源:

The word "refinance" is a combination of the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "anew") and the verb "finance," ultimately derived from the Old French word "financier" (meaning "to pay, settle").  Therefore, "refinance" literally means "to finance again."
