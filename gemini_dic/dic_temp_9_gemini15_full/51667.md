deviceless:/dɪˈvaɪsləs/ | adj. | 无设备的，无需设备的 | Without devices or equipment.  This adjective describes something that operates or exists without the need for physical devices or apparatus.  | Example:  The deviceless control system relies entirely on software.  这个无设备控制系统完全依赖于软件。 The deviceless authentication method uses biometric data.  这种无设备身份验证方法使用生物识别数据。 | 近义词: device-free, equipment-free | 反义词: device-rich, equipped
