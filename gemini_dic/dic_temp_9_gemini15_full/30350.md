centauric: /sɛnˈtɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 半人马座的；与半人马座有关的 | Relating to the constellation Centaurus.  The term often refers to the Alpha Centauri star system, the closest star system to our sun. | Example:  The discovery of a potentially habitable planet in a centauric system sparked excitement among scientists. 半人马座星系中可能存在宜居行星的发现激起了科学家们的兴奋。|近义词: Centaurian | 反义词:  None specifically; antonyms would depend on the context.
