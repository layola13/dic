came along:/keɪm əˈlɒŋ/|/v.|出现；来到；进展；|to appear or arrive unexpectedly or at a particular time; to make progress|例句：A new opportunity came along.  一个新的机会出现了。
  
A great new singer came along and changed the industry.一位伟大的新歌手出现了并改变了这个行业。


近义词：arrived, appeared, emerged, progressed|反义词：disappeared, vanished
