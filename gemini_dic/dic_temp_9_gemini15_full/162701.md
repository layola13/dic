preaxial: /ˌpriːˈæksiəl/ | adj. | 近轴的，前轴的 | Relating to the side of a limb or body part that is closest to the body's midline.  Preaxial structures are those that develop on the thumb side of the hand or the big toe side of the foot.  | Example:  The preaxial border of the hand is the thumb side. 近轴的手掌边缘是大拇指一侧。| 近义词：radial (for hand), hallux (for foot) | 反义词：postaxial
