de-shells:/ˌdiːˈʃɛlz/ | vt. |去壳；去除外壳 | To remove the shell or outer covering from something.  |例句：She de-shelled the peas before adding them to the soup. 她把豌豆去壳后才加到汤里。 |例句：The factory de-shells nuts before packaging them. 工厂在包装坚果之前先去壳。| 近义词：shell, husk, peel, shuck | 反义词：shell (as a noun, referring to the shell itself)
