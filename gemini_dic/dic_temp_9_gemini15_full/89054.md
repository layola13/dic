holohedral:/ˌhɒləʊˈhiːdrəl/ | adj. | 全面的；全形的；完全的 |  Relating to or denoting a crystal system in which all the faces required by symmetry are present.  It describes a crystal class possessing the maximum symmetry possible for a given crystal system.  | Example:  The mineral pyrite often exhibits holohedral cubic symmetry.  这矿物黄铁矿通常呈现全形的立方对称性。 | 近义词：complete, perfect, full | 反义词：hemihedral, merohedral
