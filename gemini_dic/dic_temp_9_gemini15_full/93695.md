imitation:/ˌɪmɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 模仿；仿制品；赝品 | The act of imitating someone or something; a copy that is not genuine.  |例句：His painting is a skillful imitation of Monet's style.  他的画是莫奈风格的巧妙模仿。

例句：That diamond ring is a clever imitation; it's hard to tell it's not real.  那枚钻戒是件精巧的仿制品；很难分辨出它不是真的。

近义词：copy, reproduction, facsimile, counterfeit, simulation

反义词：original, genuine, authentic
