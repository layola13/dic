pushpins: /ˈpʊʃpɪnz/ | n. |图钉；按钉 | Small pins with a head that is pushed into a surface to hold something in place.  They are typically used to attach papers, maps, or other items to a corkboard, bulletin board, or wall. |例句：I used pushpins to hang the poster on the wall. 我用图钉把海报挂在墙上。

I secured the map to the corkboard with pushpins. 我用图钉把地图固定在软木板上。


近义词：thumbtacks, tacks | 反义词：None (no direct antonym)
