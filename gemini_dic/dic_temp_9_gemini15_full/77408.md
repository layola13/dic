gave off:/ɡeɪv ɒf/| /ɡeɪv ɔːf/ | v. |散发；发出；表现出 |To emit or release something, typically a smell, light, or heat.  It can also refer to the impression or feeling someone conveys. |例句：The flowers gave off a sweet fragrance.  花散发着甜蜜的香味。

The campfire gave off a lot of heat.  篝火散发着大量的热量。


近义词：emitted, radiated, released, exuded, projected |反义词：absorbed, retained


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The candle gives off a warm glow. = 蜡烛发出温暖的光芒。
The engine gives off a lot of noise. = 发动机发出很大的噪音。


一般过去时:
The factory gave off noxious fumes. = 工厂排放出有害烟雾。
The perfume gave off a strong scent. = 香水散发出浓烈的香味。


现在完成时:
The oven has given off a burning smell all afternoon. = 烤箱整个下午都散发着烧焦的气味。
The situation has given off a sense of unease. = 情况给人一种不安的感觉。


过去完成时:
The old house had given off a musty odor for years. = 这栋老房子多年来一直散发着霉味。
Before the explosion, the device had given off strange signals. = 爆炸前，装置发出了奇怪的信号。


将来时:
The new technology will give off less pollution. = 新技术将产生更少的污染。
The party will give off a festive atmosphere. = 派对将营造出节日的气氛。


虚拟语气:
If the machine gave off less heat, it would be more efficient. = 如果机器散发的热量更少，效率就会更高。
I wish the flowers gave off a more pleasant scent. = 我希望这些花散发更令人愉悦的香味。


被动语态:
A strange odor was given off by the unknown substance. = 一种奇怪的气味是由未知物质散发出来的。
No harmful radiation is given off by this device. = 此设备不会发出任何有害辐射。


疑问句:
What kind of smell is given off by that plant? = 那棵植物散发着什么气味？
Does the device give off any harmful emissions? = 这台设备会排放任何有害物质吗？


否定句:
The heater didn't give off much heat. = 暖气片散发的热量不多。
The lamp didn't give off enough light to read by. = 灯光不足以看书。
