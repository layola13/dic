panther:/ˈpænθər/| n. |黑豹；美洲豹|A large, dark-colored cat, typically with black spots or rosettes.  Panthers are known for their stealth and hunting prowess.|例句：The panther silently stalked its prey through the jungle.  黑豹悄无声息地在丛林中跟踪猎物。|例句：We saw a black panther in the zoo. 我们在动物园里看到一只黑豹。|近义词：leopard, jaguar, cougar|反义词:None
