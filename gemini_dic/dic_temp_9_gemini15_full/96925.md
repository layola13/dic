inexpressibles:/ˌɪnɪkˈsprɛsəblz/| n.|不可言喻的事物，难以表达的感情|Things that cannot be expressed in words; feelings or experiences too intense or profound to be adequately described.  |例句：The beauty of the sunset was inexpressible.  日落的美丽是无法用言语形容的。|例句：Her grief at the loss of her child was inexpressible. 她失去孩子的悲痛是无法形容的。|近义词：indescribable, unutterable, unspeakable|反义词：expressible, describable
