grimy:/ˈɡraɪmi/| adj.|肮脏的，污秽的|Covered with grime; dirty.  The word evokes a sense of ingrained dirt, often sticky or greasy.|例句：The grimy windows needed a good cleaning.  = 肮脏的窗户需要好好擦洗一下。

例句：His hands were grimy from working in the garden. = 他在花园里干活，双手脏兮兮的。


近义词：dirty, filthy, soiled, stained, mucky, grubby


反义词：clean, spotless, pristine


词性：形容词
