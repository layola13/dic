rearise:/ˌriːˈraɪz/| v.|再出现，复现，重新出现 |To appear or become known again after a period of time.  |例句：The issue of climate change has rearised with renewed urgency. 气候变化问题再次以新的紧迫性出现。|例句：After a long silence, the rumor rearised in the media.  长时间沉默之后，谣言在媒体上再次出现。|近义词： reappear, resurface, recur |反义词：disappear, vanish
