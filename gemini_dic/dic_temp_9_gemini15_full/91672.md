hypercivilization:/ˌhaɪpərˌsɪvəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |高度文明；超级文明|A hypothetical society characterized by extremely advanced technology and social organization, often exceeding the capabilities and understanding of current human civilization. It often implies a loss of certain human qualities or a dystopian outcome.|例句：Some science fiction explores the potential dangers of a hypercivilization that has lost touch with its humanity.  一些科幻小说探讨了与人类失去联系的高度文明的潜在危险。|近义词：advanced civilization, highly developed society, utopian society (if positive), dystopian society (if negative)|反义词：primitive society, underdeveloped society
