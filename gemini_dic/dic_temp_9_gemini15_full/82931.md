gumption:/ˈɡʌmpʃən/| n. |胆量；魄力；闯劲；机智；干劲 |The spirit and initiative to do something, especially something difficult or unpleasant.|例句：It takes a lot of gumption to start your own business.  开创自己的事业需要很大的胆量。|例句：She showed great gumption in tackling the problem head-on. 她勇敢地直接面对问题，表现出极大的魄力。|近义词：initiative, drive, grit, nerve, spirit|反义词：timidity, cowardice, apathy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has the gumption to try. = 他有胆量去尝试。
She shows great gumption in her work. = 她工作中展现出极大的干劲。

一般过去时:
He showed gumption in facing the crisis. = 他在面对危机时表现出胆量。
