epigrammist:/ˈɛpɪɡræmɪst/| n. |警句作家；隽语作家 |A person who writes epigrams.  An epigram is a short, witty, and often satirical poem. Epigrammists are skilled in using concise language to express clever and insightful thoughts, often with a twist or unexpected turn at the end. | Oscar Wilde was a famous epigrammist, known for his sharp wit and memorable sayings. 奥斯卡·王尔德是一位著名的警句作家，以其敏锐的才智和令人难忘的格言而闻名。| wit, satirist | verbose, dull |
