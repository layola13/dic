premodifier: /ˈpriːmɒdɪfaɪər/ | adj. |前置修饰语| A word or phrase that comes before a noun or noun phrase to modify its meaning.  It describes, limits, or qualifies the noun. | Example:  In the phrase "the big red ball," "big" and "red" are premodifiers. | 近义词: attributive adjective, modifying word | 反义词: postmodifier


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The large red house stands on the hill. = 大的红色的房子矗立在山上。
The diligent student completed the assignment early. = 勤奋的学生提前完成了作业。


一般过去时:
The old wooden chair broke. = 那把旧木椅坏了。
The exciting movie thrilled the audience. = 那部令人兴奋的电影让观众激动不已。


现在完成时:
The newly painted walls look beautiful. = 新粉刷的墙壁看起来很漂亮。
The recently published book has become a bestseller. = 最近出版的这本书已经成为畅销书。


过去完成时:
The previously unknown artist's work was discovered. = 以前不为人知的艺术家的作品被发现了。
The long-awaited results were finally announced. =  期待已久的成果终于公布了。


将来时:
The upcoming election will be crucial. =  即将到来的选举将至关重要。
The planned trip will take place next month. = 计划中的旅行将在下个月进行。


虚拟语气:
If I had a faster car, I would win the race. = 如果我有一辆更快的车，我就能赢得比赛。(faster is a premodifier to car)
I wish I had a more comfortable chair. = 我希望我有一把更舒服的椅子。(more comfortable is a premodifier to chair)


被动语态:
The stolen painting was recovered. = 被盗的画被找回了。(stolen is a premodifier to painting)
The damaged goods were returned to the store. = 受损的货物被退回了商店。(damaged is a premodifier to goods)


疑问句:
What kind of music do you like? = 你喜欢什么类型的音乐？(kind of is a premodifier to music)
Which specific details are important? = 哪些具体的细节很重要？ (specific is a premodifier to details)


否定句:
I don't have any extra money. = 我没有额外的钱。(extra is a premodifier to money)
There were no significant changes. = 没有显著的变化。(significant is a premodifier to changes)
