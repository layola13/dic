reimbibe:/ˌriːɪmˈbaɪb/| vt.|再饮，再吸收，重温|To drink again; to absorb again; to revive or re-experience (a feeling, memory, etc.).|例句：After a long day's work, he decided to reimbibe his favorite whiskey.  下班后，他决定再喝一杯他最喜欢的威士忌。

例句：She tried to reimbibe the joy of her childhood summers. 她试图重温童年夏日的快乐。


近义词：drink again, re-absorb, relive, recall


反义词：expel, forget, abandon


词性:动词
