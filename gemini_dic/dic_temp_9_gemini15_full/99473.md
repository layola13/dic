interference:/ˌɪntərˈfɪərəns/ | n. | 干涉；干扰；阻碍 | The act of interfering or the state of being interfered with; something that interferes.  It can refer to the obstruction of a process or the disruption of a signal or wave. |例句：Their interference in the negotiations was unhelpful. 他们对谈判的干涉毫无帮助。

例句：Radio interference made it difficult to listen to the broadcast.  无线电干扰使收听广播变得困难。


近义词：intervention, obstruction, disruption, impediment | 反义词：cooperation, assistance, support | 词性：名词
