schiller:/ˈʃɪlər/| n. |席勒；席勒式风格|The name Schiller refers primarily to Johann Christoph Friedrich von Schiller, a prominent German poet, playwright, and historian.  His works significantly influenced German literature and romanticism.  The term can also refer to a style reminiscent of his works, characterized by dramatic intensity, idealism, and often a focus on historical or philosophical themes.| 例句：We studied Schiller's "Wilhelm Tell" in literature class.  我们在文学课上学习了席勒的《威廉·退尔》。|近义词：Goethe (in terms of influence on German literature), romantic (referring to the style)|反义词：None directly applicable|名词
