sheartail: /ˈʃɛərteɪl/ | n. |剪尾鸟；剪尾蜂鸟 | A bird, typically a hummingbird, with a deeply forked tail.  The term is often used specifically for the birds in the genus *Hirundinea*. |例句：The iridescent sheartail hovered near the flowers. 这只闪闪发光的剪尾蜂鸟在花朵附近盘旋。

例句：Several species of sheartail are found in the Andes mountains. 安第斯山脉发现几种剪尾鸟。


近义词：forked-tailed bird (泛指)


反义词：无


词性：名词
