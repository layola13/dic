dewberry:/ˈduːbɛri/ | n. |野黑莓；露莓|A dewberry is a type of blackberry, usually smaller and with a more tart flavor than cultivated blackberries. They grow wild and are often found trailing along the ground.  |例句：The children enjoyed picking dewberries in the field.孩子们喜欢在田里采摘露莓。|例句：We made a delicious pie using the freshly picked dewberries.我们用刚采摘的新鲜露莓做了一个美味的馅饼。|近义词：blackberry, bramble|反义词：N/A
