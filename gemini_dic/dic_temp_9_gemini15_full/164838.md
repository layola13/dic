It describes the developmental phase leading up to flowering, often characterized by specific physiological and morphological changes. | Example: The preflowering stage of the rice plant is crucial for determining yield.  水稻的孕穗期对于确定产量至关重要。  | 近义词: antefloral, pre-anthesis | 反义词: postflowering, flowering
