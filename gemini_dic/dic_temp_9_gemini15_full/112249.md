lootings:/ˈluːtɪŋz/| n.|抢劫；洗劫；掠夺|The act of stealing goods, especially during a riot or war.  It refers to multiple instances of looting.|例句：The city was in chaos after the earthquake, with widespread lootings reported. 地震过后，城市一片混乱，据报道发生了大范围的抢劫。

例句：The police are investigating the lootings that occurred during the protest.  警方正在调查抗议活动期间发生的抢劫事件。


近义词：plundering, pillaging, ransacking, robbing

反义词：donation, giving, contribution


词性：名词
