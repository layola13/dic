persecution:/ˌpɜːrsɪˈkjuːʃən/| n. |迫害，压迫 |The systematic oppression of a group of people.  It often involves violence, discrimination, and denial of basic human rights.  |例句：The persecution of religious minorities continues in some parts of the world.  世界某些地区对宗教少数群体的迫害仍在继续。|例句：Throughout history, many groups have suffered persecution for their beliefs. 纵观历史，许多群体都因其信仰而遭受迫害。|近义词：oppression, harassment, victimization, persecution |反义词：protection, liberation, tolerance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Religious minorities often face persecution in many countries. = 在许多国家，宗教少数群体经常面临迫害。
The government persecutes those who speak out against them. = 政府迫害那些公开反对他们的人。

一般过去时:
The early Christians suffered intense persecution under the Roman Empire. = 早期基督教徒在罗马帝国时期遭受了残酷的迫害。
They were persecuted for their political beliefs. = 他们因政治信仰而遭到迫害。

现在完成时:
Many groups have experienced persecution throughout history. = 许多群体在历史上都经历过迫害。
The victims have suffered years of persecution. = 受害者遭受了多年的迫害。

过去完成时:
Before the revolution, the people had endured decades of persecution. = 在革命之前，人民忍受了几十年的迫害。
They had already been persecuted for expressing their views. = 他们因表达自己的观点而早已受到迫害。

将来时:
If the current policies continue, more people will face persecution. = 如果目前的政策继续下去，更多的人将面临迫害。
We fear that they will be persecuted for their activism. = 我们担心他们会因为他们的行动主义而受到迫害。

虚拟语气:
If there were no persecution, many people would live more freely. = 如果没有迫害，许多人将会生活得更自由。
I wish religious persecution would end worldwide. = 我希望全世界的宗教迫害能够结束。

被动语态:
Many innocent people were persecuted during the war. = 许多无辜的人在战争期间遭到迫害。
The activists are being persecuted for their peaceful protests. = 这些活动家因为和平抗议而受到迫害。

疑问句:
Why are these people being persecuted? = 为什么这些人受到迫害？
What forms of persecution are they experiencing? = 他们正在经历哪些形式的迫害？

否定句:
The government does not tolerate persecution of any kind. = 政府不容忍任何形式的迫害。
They were not persecuted for their beliefs, but for their actions. = 他们不是因为信仰而受到迫害，而是因为他们的行为。
