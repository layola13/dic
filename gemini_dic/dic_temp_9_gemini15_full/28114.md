capitalizes: /ˈkæpɪtəlaɪz/ | vt. | 将…大写；利用；获利 | To write or print (something) in capital letters; to take advantage of (something) to gain benefit; to convert into capital. | 例句：Please capitalize the first letter of each sentence. 请将每个句子的首字母大写。| 例句：She capitalized on her experience to get the job. 她利用自己的经验获得了这份工作。| 近义词：uppercase, utilize, exploit | 反义词：lowercase, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He capitalizes every proper noun. = 他把每个专有名词都大写。
The company capitalizes on the latest technology. = 这家公司利用最新的技术。

一般过去时:
She capitalized the title of her essay. = 她把论文的标题大写了。
They capitalized on the opportunity to expand their business. = 他们抓住机会拓展了业务。

现在完成时:
He has capitalized all the important words. = 他已经把所有重要的单词都大写了。
The company has capitalized on its success in the market. = 公司已经利用其在市场上的成功。

过去完成时:
They had capitalized the company name before the launch. = 他们在发布之前已经把公司名称大写了。
She had capitalized on her connections to get the interview. = 她利用自己的人脉关系获得了面试机会。

将来时:
He will capitalize the headings in the report. = 他将把报告中的标题大写。
The company will capitalize on the growing demand for its products. = 公司将利用其产品日益增长的需求。

虚拟语气:
If I had capitalized on that opportunity, I would be more successful now. = 如果我当时抓住那个机会，现在我会更成功。
I wish I had capitalized on my education earlier. = 我真希望我早点利用我的教育背景。

被动语态:
The words were capitalized in the final draft. = 在最终稿中，这些词被大写了。
The opportunity was capitalized on by a rival company. = 机会被一家竞争对手公司利用了。

疑问句:
Is the word "Capitalize" capitalized correctly? = “Capitalize”这个词大写正确吗？
Does the company plan to capitalize on this new trend? = 公司计划利用这一新趋势吗？

否定句:
He did not capitalize the names in his document. = 他没有在他的文件中大写姓名。
The company did not capitalize on the marketing opportunity. = 公司没有抓住营销机会。
