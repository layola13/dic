lacing:/ˈleɪsɪŋ/| n. v.|系带；花边；编织；|The act of fastening something with laces; a decorative braid or cord.  |例句：The shoelaces were untied. 鞋带开了。

The intricate lacing on her dress was beautiful. 她衣服上复杂的蕾丝很漂亮。|近义词：fastening, tying, braiding|反义词：untying, unfastening
