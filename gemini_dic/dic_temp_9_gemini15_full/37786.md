comped:/kəmpd/| v. |免费提供；报销；补偿|To provide something, typically a service or good, free of charge.  This often occurs in the context of businesses offering complimentary goods or services to customers.|例句：The hotel comped us a room upgrade.  酒店免费为我们升级了房间。

例句：The restaurant comped our dessert after a long wait. 餐厅在我们久等之后免费赠送了甜点。


近义词：gave free, offered free, complimentary, subsidized

反义词：charged for, billed for


词性:动词
