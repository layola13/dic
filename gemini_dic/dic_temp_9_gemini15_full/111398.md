USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore its usage is not categorized by verb tenses like a verb.  Instead, it modifies verbs and describes how an action is performed.

Example 1:  He spoke livingly about his travels. 他兴致勃勃地谈论他的旅行。
Example 2: The colors in the painting were used livingly and effectively.  这幅画的色彩运用得非常鲜明有效。


PHRASE->
There are no common set phrases using "livingly".  Its usage is quite straightforward as a single adverb.
