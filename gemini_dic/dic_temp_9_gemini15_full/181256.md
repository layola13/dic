reinstatements:/ˌriːɪnˈsteɪtmənts/| n. |恢复职位；恢复原状；复职|The act of reinstating someone or something; restoring to a former position, rank, privilege, or status.|例句：The company announced the reinstatements of several employees who had been unfairly dismissed. 公司宣布恢复几名被不公平解雇员工的职位。

例句：After a thorough investigation, the committee voted for the reinstatement of his driving license.  在彻底调查后，委员会投票决定恢复他的驾驶执照。


近义词：restoration, resumption, reinstallation, reinstatement


反义词：dismissal, removal, termination


词性:名词
