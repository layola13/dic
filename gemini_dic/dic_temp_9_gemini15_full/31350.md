characterization:/ˌkærəktəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |人物刻画；特性描述；特征描述 |The process of creating and developing a character.  This includes their physical appearance, personality traits, motivations, and relationships with other characters.  It can also refer to the description of the characteristics of something. |例句：The novel's strength lies in its superb characterization. 这部小说的力量在于它极好的人物刻画。

例句：The geologist's report included a detailed characterization of the rock samples. 地质学家的报告包括对岩石样品的详细特性描述。

近义词：portrayal, depiction, delineation, description

反义词：misrepresentation
