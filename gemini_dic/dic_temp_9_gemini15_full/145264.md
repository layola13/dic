overboils:/ˌoʊvərˈbɔɪlz/ | v. |煮过头；过度沸腾|To boil for too long, causing the food to become too soft or mushy.  |例句：I overboiled the pasta, and now it's stuck together. 我把意大利面煮过头了，现在都粘在一起了。  |例句：The milk overboiled and spilled all over the stove. 牛奶煮沸溢出，洒满了炉子。|近义词：boil excessively, overcook |反义词：undercook
