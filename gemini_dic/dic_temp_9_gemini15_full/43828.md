creolizing:/ˈkriːəlaɪzɪŋ/| v.|克里奥尔化；混合；融合|The process by which a language or culture is transformed through the interaction of different linguistic or cultural groups.  It often involves the simplification of grammar, the adoption of vocabulary from other languages, and the emergence of new cultural practices.|例句：The creolizing of Caribbean music involved a blending of African, European, and indigenous musical traditions. 加勒比海音乐的克里奥尔化包含了非洲、欧洲和土著音乐传统的融合。|例句：The island's unique culture is a result of centuries of creolizing. 岛屿独特的文化是几个世纪克里奥尔化 的结果。|近义词：hybridization, mixing, blending, fusion|反义词：purification, homogenization
