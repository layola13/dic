shoeless: /ˈʃuːləs/ | adj. | 赤脚的，没穿鞋的 | Without shoes.  The adjective describes a person or thing lacking shoes. |

例句：

The children were running around shoeless in the park. = 孩子们在公园里赤脚跑来跑去。

He walked shoeless through the dewy grass. = 他赤脚走在露湿的草地上。


近义词：barefoot, unshod

反义词：shod, wearing shoes


词性：形容词
