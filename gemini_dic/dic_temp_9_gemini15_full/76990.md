gardeners:/ˈɡɑːrdənərz/| n. |园丁，种植者 |People who cultivate gardens.  Gardeners are responsible for planting, tending, and maintaining plants in gardens. They may specialize in different types of gardens, such as vegetable gardens, flower gardens, or landscaping. |例句：The gardeners planted hundreds of tulips in the spring. 春天，园丁们种了数百株郁金香。

例句：Experienced gardeners know how to protect their plants from pests.  经验丰富的园丁知道如何保护他们的植物免受害虫侵害。
