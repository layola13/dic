dictat:/ˈdɪkteɪt/| vt.|口授，命令，支配|to speak or read aloud (words to be written down by another person)|The teacher dictated the essay topic to the students.老师向学生口授了作文题目。|近义词：pronounce, dictate, prescribe|反义词：obey, follow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dictates his letters to his secretary. 他向他的秘书口述信件。
The boss dictates the work schedule. 老板决定工作安排。


一般过去时:
She dictated the terms of the contract. 她口述了合同条款。
The general dictated the battle plan. 将军下达了作战计划。


现在完成时:
The author has dictated the entire manuscript. 作者已口述完成整个手稿。
The government has dictated its policies for years. 政府多年来一直强加其政策。


过去完成时:
He had dictated the letter before the meeting. 他在会议前已口述完信件。
The king had dictated his will before his death. 国王在死前已口述了他的遗嘱。


将来时:
She will dictate the speech tomorrow. 她明天将口述演讲稿。
The judge will dictate the sentencing. 法官将宣判。


虚拟语气:
If he dictated the rules, we would follow them. 如果他制定规则，我们会遵守。
I wish the government didn't dictate our lives. 我希望政府不要支配我们的生活。


被动语态:
The speech was dictated by the president. 这篇演讲是由总统口述的。
The terms of the agreement were dictated by the larger company. 协议条款是由大公司决定的。


疑问句:
Will the teacher dictate the answers?老师会口述答案吗？
Does he dictate his emails? 他会口述电子邮件吗？


否定句:
He did not dictate the terms of the agreement. 他没有决定协议条款。
The teacher didn't dictate the exam questions. 老师没有口述考试题目。
