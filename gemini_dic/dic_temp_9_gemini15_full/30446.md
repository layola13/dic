centrebit:/ˈsɛntərˌbɪt/| n. |中心钻头，中心钻|A centrebit is a woodworking tool used to bore large holes, typically in wood.  It has a central point that guides the bit and cutting edges that remove the material.  The size of the hole is determined by the diameter of the bit.|Example: He used a centrebit to create a large hole for the doorknob.|近义词：forstner bit, auger bit|反义词：None
