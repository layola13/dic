mini-lp:/ˈmɪniˌɛlp/| n. |迷你长播放唱片|A mini-LP (also known as a mini-album or EP) is a musical recording that is shorter than a standard LP (long-playing record) but longer than a single. It typically contains 4-7 tracks and is often used as a way for artists to release new material between full-length albums.  |Example: The band released a mini-lp to preview songs from their upcoming album. 乐队发行了一张迷你专辑来预告他们即将发行的专辑中的歌曲。|近义词：mini-album, EP (extended play)|反义词：full-length album, LP|名词
