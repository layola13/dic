cratch:/krætʃ/ | n. vt. | 抓挠；刮擦；划痕 | To scratch; a scratch. |例句：The cat scratched the furniture. 猫抓坏了家具。 The surface had several scratches. 表面有几道划痕。 |近义词：scrape, scratch, scrape, claw |反义词：smooth, polish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scratches his head when he's thinking. = 他思考问题时会挠头。
She scratches the lottery ticket. = 她刮开彩票。

一般过去时:
The dog scratched at the door. = 狗抓挠着门。
I scratched my arm on a branch. = 我的胳膊被树枝刮伤了。


现在完成时:
I've scratched my car.  = 我把车刮花了。
The cat has scratched the sofa again. = 猫又抓了沙发。


过去完成时:
He had scratched the car before he noticed. = 他在注意到之前就已经刮花了车。
She had scratched her leg on a rock. = 她的腿被石头划伤了。


将来时:
I will scratch the paint off the wall. = 我将把墙上的油漆刮掉。
She will scratch her back against the tree. = 她会把背靠在树上抓痒。


虚拟语气:
If I scratched the lottery ticket, I might win. = 如果我刮开彩票，我可能会中奖。
I wish I hadn't scratched my car. = 我真希望我没有刮坏我的车。


被动语态:
The car was scratched during the accident. = 车在事故中被刮花了。
The wall was scratched by the cat. = 墙壁被猫抓伤了。


疑问句:
Did the cat scratch the furniture? = 猫抓坏家具了吗？
Has anyone scratched this painting? = 有人划坏这幅画了吗？


否定句:
I didn't scratch the table. = 我没有刮坏桌子。
The cat didn't scratch the sofa this time. = 猫这次没有抓沙发。


PHRASE->
scratch the surface: 触及表面；略知一二
scratch a living: 勉强维持生计
from scratch: 从头开始
scratch and sniff: 刮一刮闻一闻 (指卡片或贴纸)
scratch team: 临时的队伍
