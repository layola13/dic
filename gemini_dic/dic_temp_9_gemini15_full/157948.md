planetes:/pləˈnɛtiːz/| n. |行星(复数)|The word "planetes" is the plural form of the Latin word "planeta," meaning "wanderer."  It refers to celestial bodies orbiting a star, especially those that are large enough to have become rounded by their own gravity.  The term is often used in astronomical contexts, sometimes interchangeably with "planets," though "planets" usually refers to bodies within our solar system. |Example: The planetes in our solar system are fascinating objects of study.  我们的太阳系中的行星是令人着迷的研究对象。|近义词:planets|反义词:star(in certain contexts)|名词
