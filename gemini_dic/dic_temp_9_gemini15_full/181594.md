relationality: /ˌreɪˈleɪʃənælɪti/ | n. | 关系性；关联性；相互关系 | The state or quality of being relational; the interconnectedness of things.  It emphasizes the importance of relationships and interactions in understanding phenomena. | 例句：The study highlighted the relationality between the environment and human health. 这项研究强调了环境与人类健康之间的关系性。


The relationality of the characters in the novel made it compelling. 小说中人物之间的关系性使其引人入胜。| 近义词：interconnectedness, interdependence, relatedness | 反义词：isolation, independence, separateness | 词性：名词
