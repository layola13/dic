loosener:/ˈluːsənər/| n. |松动器，松弛剂|A tool or substance that loosens something.  For example, a loosener can be a tool used to loosen nuts and bolts, or a chemical agent used to loosen soil.|例句：The plumber used a pipe loosener to fix the leak. 水管工用管子松动器修理漏水。The gardener added a soil loosener to improve drainage. 园丁添加了土壤松动剂以改善排水。|近义词：relaxant, softener, loosening agent|反义词：tightener, solidifier
