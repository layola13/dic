preacquaint:/ˌpriːəˈkweɪnt/| v.|预先认识，先期了解|To have some prior knowledge or familiarity with something or someone before a formal or deeper acquaintance.  The word is relatively rare and often found in more formal or specialized contexts.|例句：I preacquainted myself with the company's history before the interview. 我在面试前预先了解了公司的历史。

例句：The professor preacquainted the students with the key concepts before the lecture. 教授在讲座前先让学生们了解了关键概念。


近义词：familiarize, introduce, prepare, prime|反义词：unfamiliarize, ignore, neglect|词性:动词
