micronuclear:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈnuːkliər/| adj.| 微核的；小核的 | Relating to or denoting a small nucleus, especially the small, usually inactive nucleus of a ciliate protozoan.  | Example: The micronuclear genome is significantly smaller than the macronuclear genome. 微核基因组比大核基因组小得多。 |近义词：nuclear (in a smaller context) |反义词：macronuclear
