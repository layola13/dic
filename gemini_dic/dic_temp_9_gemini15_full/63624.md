enzymes:/ˈɛnzaɪmz/ | n. |酶 | Enzymes are biological catalysts that speed up chemical reactions in living organisms. They are essential for all life processes.  | Example: Digestive enzymes break down food molecules. 消化酶分解食物分子。  Many enzymes are involved in DNA replication. 许多酶参与DNA复制。|近义词：catalyst, ferment |反义词：无
