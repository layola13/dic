overwhelm:/ˌoʊvərˈwelm/ | vt. |压倒；淹没；制服；使不知所措 | To overcome (someone) with a strong feeling or effect. To defeat completely.  To cover completely, usually with water. |例句：The amount of work overwhelmed her.  工作量压倒了她。

The floodwaters overwhelmed the village.  洪水淹没了村庄。

近义词：submerge, inundate, crush, defeat, swamp |反义词：support, encourage, strengthen


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The news overwhelms him. = 这个消息让他不知所措。
Too much work overwhelms me daily. =  每天过多的工作都让我不堪重负。

一般过去时:
The army overwhelmed the enemy. =  军队击败了敌人。
The sudden noise overwhelmed her. = 突如其来的噪音让她吓了一跳。

现在完成时:
The sheer volume of emails has overwhelmed me. = 邮件数量之多让我应接不暇。
The beauty of the scenery has overwhelmed her. =  风景之美让她惊叹不已。

过去完成时:
She had been overwhelmed by grief after her mother's death. =  母亲去世后，她一直沉浸在悲伤之中。
The team had been overwhelmed by the opposing side's strength. =  球队被对方强大的实力击败了。

将来时:
The project will overwhelm us if we don't get more help. = 如果我们得不到更多帮助，这个项目将会压垮我们。
I fear the coming holiday season will overwhelm me with stress. = 我担心即将到来的假日季会让我压力过大。

虚拟语气:
If I had been better prepared, I wouldn't have been overwhelmed. = 如果我准备充分些，就不会不知所措了。
I wish I hadn't been so overwhelmed by the task. = 我希望我不会被这项任务压垮。

被动语态:
She was overwhelmed by the kindness of strangers. = 她被陌生人的善意所感动。
The city was overwhelmed by the flood. = 城市被洪水淹没了。

疑问句:
Was she overwhelmed by the news? = 她被这个消息压垮了吗？
Are you overwhelmed by your workload? = 你被你的工作量压垮了吗？

否定句:
The challenge didn't overwhelm him. = 这个挑战并没有难倒他。
She wasn't overwhelmed by the pressure. = 她并没有被压力压垮。



PHRASE->
be overwhelmed with = 被…压垮；被…淹没
overwhelmed by = 被…压倒；被…征服
feel overwhelmed = 感到不知所措
become overwhelmed = 变得不知所措
overwhelmed by emotion = 情绪激动
