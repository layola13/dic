shear:/ʃɪər/| /ʃɪr/ | vt. n.|剪，修剪；偏航；切变；剪力|To cut something such as hair, wool, or fabric with shears.  It also refers to a force that causes one part of a body to slide over another.|例句：The barber sheared my hair. 理发师剪了我的头发。

The wind shear caused the plane to lose control.  风切变导致飞机失控。|近义词：clip, trim, cut, lop|反义词：grow, lengthen
