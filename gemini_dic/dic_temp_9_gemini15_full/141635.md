optimally:/ˈɒptɪməli/| /ˈɑːptɪməli/ | adv. | 最优地；最佳地 | In the best or most favorable way; ideally.  | 例句：The system is designed to operate optimally under a wide range of conditions.  系统设计为可在各种条件下最佳运行。


例句：The project was completed optimally, within budget and ahead of schedule. 项目按预算完成，并且提前完成，达到了最佳效果。


近义词：ideally, perfectly, best, most effectively


反义词：poorly, badly, ineffectively, suboptimally


词性:副词
