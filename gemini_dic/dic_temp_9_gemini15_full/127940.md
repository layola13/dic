neopaganism:/ˌniːoʊˈpeɪɡənɪzəm/| n. |新异教；新异教徒 |A modern religious movement that draws inspiration from pre-Christian polytheistic traditions, but is not a revival of those traditions.  Neopaganism is diverse, with various traditions and beliefs. |Many neopagans celebrate the solstices and equinoxes. 许多新异教徒庆祝冬至和春分。|Modern Paganism, Wicca|Christianity,  Judaism, Islam|
