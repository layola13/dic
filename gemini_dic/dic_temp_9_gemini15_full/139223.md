objurgation:/ˌɒbʒɜːˈɡeɪʃən/| n. |训斥；责骂；斥责 | A harsh rebuke or criticism.  |例句：The teacher's objurgation silenced the noisy classroom. 老师的训斥使喧闹的教室安静下来了。

例句：His objurgation of the employee was unwarranted and unprofessional. 他对员工的斥责是不必要的，而且不专业。


近义词：rebuke, reprimand, reproach, scolding, chastisement

反义词：praise, commendation, compliment


词性: 名词


词源:  From Middle French *objurgation, from Latin objurgatio, from objurgare "to scold, rebuke," from ob- "against" + jurgare "to swear, quarrel."
