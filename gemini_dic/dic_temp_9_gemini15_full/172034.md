Purim:/ˈpuːrɪm/| n. |普珥节|The Jewish festival of Purim celebrates the deliverance of the Jewish people from Haman's plot to exterminate them, as recounted in the Book of Esther.  It is a joyous and festive occasion marked by costumes, feasting, and the reading of the Megillah (scroll of Esther).|Example:  We celebrated Purim with costumes, hamantaschen, and a lively Megillah reading. 我们穿着盛装，吃着三角饼干，兴高采烈地朗读《以斯帖记》来庆祝普珥节。|近义词：Festival, holiday|反义词：None|名词
