self-creative: /ˌsɛlfˈkriːeɪtɪv/ | adj. | 自我创造的，具有创造力的 |  Self-creative refers to something or someone that is capable of creating or producing something new or original independently, without external influence or guidance. It emphasizes the inner drive and initiative for creation.  | 例句：Her self-creative spirit led her to design and build her own house.  她的自我创造精神促使她设计并建造了自己的房子。


The self-creative artist produced a series of stunning paintings.这位具有自我创造力的艺术家创作了一系列令人惊叹的绘画作品。| 近义词：inventive, innovative, original, imaginative | 反义词：unoriginal, unimaginative, derivative
