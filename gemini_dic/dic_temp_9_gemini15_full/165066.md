preimagining:/ˌpriːɪˈmædʒɪnɪŋ/| v.|预先想象，预想|The act of imagining something before it happens; forming a mental image of a future event or situation.  It often implies a degree of anticipation or planning.|例句：She spent hours preimagining her wedding day, visualizing every detail. 她花了数小时预想她的婚礼，想象每一个细节。

例句：The architect preimagined the building's impact on the surrounding landscape. 建筑师预想了这座建筑物对周围景观的影响。


近义词：forecasting, envisioning, anticipating, visualizing, conceiving


反义词：retrospecting, reminiscing (these refer to the past, not the future)


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preimagines the outcome of the negotiations. 他预想谈判的结果。
They preimagine their future together. 他们预想他们的未来。

一般过去时:
She preimagined a successful career for herself. 她预想自己拥有一个成功的职业生涯。
He preimagined the difficulties he might face. 他预想了他可能面临的困难。

现在完成时:
I have preimagined many scenarios for this project. 我已经预想过这个项目的许多方案。
We have preimagined the potential problems and solutions. 我们已经预想了潜在的问题和解决方案。

过去完成时:
She had preimagined her speech many times before delivering it. 在发表演讲之前，她已经预想了很多次。
They had preimagined the consequences of their actions.  他们在采取行动之前已经预想到了后果。


将来时:
I will preimagine the trip before I go. 我会在出发前预想一下旅程。
We will preimagine how to respond to various situations. 我们将预想如何应对各种情况。

虚拟语气:
If I had preimagined the challenges, I would have been better prepared. 如果我预想到了挑战，我会准备得更好。
I wish I had preimagined the difficulties I would face. 我真希望我预想到了我将面临的困难。

被动语态:
The potential risks were preimagined by the team. 团队预想了潜在的风险。(This is less common, as preimagining is usually an active process)
The impact of the decision was preimagined by the manager.  经理预想了这个决定的影响。(This is less common, as preimagining is usually an active process)

疑问句:
Did you preimagine the possibility of failure? 你预想过失败的可能性吗？
Have you preimagined all the possible outcomes? 你预想过所有可能的结果吗？

否定句:
They did not preimagine the extent of the damage. 他们没有预想到损害的程度。
I did not preimagine the level of difficulty. 我没有预想到困难的程度。


PHRASE->
preimagine the future 预想未来
preimagine the consequences 预想后果
preimagine the possibilities 预想可能性
preimagine a scenario 预想一种情景
preimagine success 预想成功
