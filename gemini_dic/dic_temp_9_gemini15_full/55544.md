distributor:/dɪˈstrɪbjuːtər/ | n. |分销商，经销商 |A distributor is a person or company that supplies goods to stores and businesses.  They act as an intermediary between the manufacturer and the retailer. |Example:  Our company is the main distributor of this product in the region.  我们的公司是该产品在该地区的主要经销商。|近义词：supplier, wholesaler, dealer |反义词：consumer, retailer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The distributor supplies goods to stores nationwide. = 该分销商向全国各地的商店供应商品。
This company distributes products globally. = 这家公司在全球分销产品。


一般过去时:
The distributor delivered the goods last week. = 分销商上周送来了货物。
They distributed the leaflets in the city center. = 他们把传单分发在市中心。


现在完成时:
The distributor has expanded its network significantly. = 该分销商已大幅扩展其网络。
The company has distributed over a million units this year. = 今年该公司已分销了超过一百万件产品。


过去完成时:
The distributor had already shipped the order before the payment arrived. = 付款到达之前，分销商已经发货了。
They had distributed all the brochures by the end of the day. = 他们当天结束前已分发完所有宣传册。


将来时:
The distributor will launch a new product next month. = 分销商将于下个月推出新产品。
The company will distribute the goods to all its retailers. = 公司将把货物分销给所有零售商。


虚拟语气:
If the distributor were to offer better prices, we would buy more. = 如果分销商提供更好的价格，我们会购买更多。
I wish the distributor had a wider range of products. = 我希望分销商的产品种类更广泛。


被动语态:
The goods were distributed efficiently by the distributor. = 货物由分销商有效地分销。
The new product is being distributed across the country. = 新产品正在全国范围内分销。


疑问句:
Who is the main distributor of this brand? = 这个品牌的 主要分销商是谁？
Where does the distributor source its products? = 分销商从哪里采购其产品？


否定句:
The distributor did not fulfill the order on time. = 分销商没有按时完成订单。
The company does not distribute products directly to consumers. = 公司不直接向消费者分销产品。
