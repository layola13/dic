outleaping: /ˌaʊtˈliːpɪŋ/ | v. |超越，超过；跳出 |  The act of leaping beyond or exceeding something else.  It suggests surpassing in a dynamic, energetic way, often involving a jump or sudden movement forward. |

例句：

The athlete's outleaping performance astonished the crowd.  = 运动员超越众人的表现震惊了观众。

His ambition outleaped his abilities. = 他的雄心壮志超过了他的能力。


近义词：surpassing, exceeding, outstripping, transcending

反义词：underperforming, lagging behind, falling short


词性：动词 (verb)
