paranoias:/ˌpærəˈnɔɪəz/| n.|偏执狂；妄想症|The plural form of "paranoia," referring to multiple instances of delusional or excessive suspiciousness.  It can also refer to a general atmosphere or feeling of suspicion and distrust.|例句：His paranoias led him to believe everyone was plotting against him. 他的偏执狂使他相信每个人都在密谋对付他。|例句：The paranoias surrounding the election fueled further division within the country.围绕选举的各种猜疑加剧了国内的分裂。|近义词：delusions, suspicions, anxieties, fears |反义词：trust, confidence, security
