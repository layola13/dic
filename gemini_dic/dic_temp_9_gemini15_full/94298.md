imperturbability: /ˌɪmpɜːrtɜːrpəˈbɪləti/ | n. | imperturbability |  不动声色；镇静；沉着 |  The quality of being imperturbable; calmness; composure; coolness under pressure.  It suggests an ability to remain calm and unperturbed even in the face of stress, danger, or provocation.  | Example:  The captain displayed remarkable imperturbability during the storm.  船长在暴风雨中表现出非凡的镇静。

His imperturbability in the face of danger was astonishing. 他面对危险时的镇静令人吃惊。|  近义词: composure, calmness, equanimity, serenity, coolness | 反义词: agitation, nervousness, anxiety, panic, fluster
