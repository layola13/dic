词源:

The word "hyperconservatism" is a modern coinage, formed by combining:

* **hyper-**:  A prefix meaning "excessive," "over," or "beyond."
* **conservatism**:  The belief in preserving traditional values and institutions.

Therefore, "hyperconservatism" literally means "excessive conservatism."
