/ˈɛmˌeɪsˌeɪ/|abbr.|多重采样抗锯齿|Multisample Anti-Aliasing,一种图像处理技术，通过对像素进行多次采样来减少锯齿效应，提高图像质量。|MSAA is a technique used in computer graphics to reduce aliasing artifacts, particularly jagged edges.  It works by sampling the image multiple times per pixel.| 例句：The game looks much smoother with MSAA enabled.  启用MSAA后，游戏看起来流畅多了。|近义词：FXAA, TAA|反义词：无
