polyspory:/ˈpɒliˌspɔːri/ | n. |多孢子现象，多孢子生殖 |The production of more than one spore by a single sporangium or ascus.  This phenomenon is common in certain fungi and algae. | Example:  The observation of polyspory in this fungal species suggests a novel reproductive strategy.  这个真菌物种中多孢子现象的观察表明了一种新的繁殖策略。 |近义词：multispory |反义词：monospony
