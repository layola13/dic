preemptioner: /priˈɛmpʃənər/ | n. |优先权获得者；先占者；预先占有者 | One who preempts; one who acquires something before others.  A person or entity that exercises preemptive rights or actions. |  The preemptioner bought the land before anyone else had a chance.  优先权获得者在其他人有机会之前就购买了这块土地。 |近义词：prior claimant, first-comer, anticipator |反义词：latecomer, follower
