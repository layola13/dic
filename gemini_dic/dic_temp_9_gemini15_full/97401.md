infrequency:/ɪnˈfriːkwənsi/ | n. | 罕见；不频繁 | The infrequency of rainfall led to a severe drought.  降雨的稀少导致了严重的干旱。 |近义词：rarity, scarcity, uncommonness |反义词：frequency, commonness


英文解析: Infrequency refers to the state or quality of being infrequent;  the fact or condition of something happening or appearing only rarely.  It emphasizes the unusualness or lack of regularity of an event or occurrence.


例句:

The infrequency of bus service in rural areas is a major problem.  农村地区公交服务的稀少是一个主要问题。

His infrequency of visits to his parents caused concern. 他很少去看望父母，这引起了担忧。


词源:

The word "infrequency" is derived from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") and the noun "frequency," which ultimately comes from the Latin word "frequens" meaning "frequent" or "crowded."  Therefore, "infrequency" literally means "not frequent."
