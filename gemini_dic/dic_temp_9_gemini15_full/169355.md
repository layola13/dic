promulgated:/ˈprɒmʌlɡeɪtɪd/ | v. | 公布；颁布；宣传 | To make (a law, decree, etc.) known officially or publicly; to spread or promote (an idea or belief). | The government promulgated a new law regarding environmental protection. 政府颁布了一项关于环境保护的新法律。 | 近义词：announce, proclaim, disseminate, broadcast | 反义词：suppress, conceal, withhold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government promulgates new laws regularly. = 政府定期颁布新法律。
They promulgate their ideas through various media channels. = 他们通过各种媒体渠道宣传他们的理念。


一般过去时:
The king promulgated a decree to end the war. = 国王颁布了一道法令来结束战争。
The school promulgated a new dress code last year. = 学校去年颁布了一套新的着装规范。


现在完成时:
The company has promulgated its new marketing strategy. = 公司已经公布了它的新营销策略。
They have promulgated their findings to the scientific community. = 他们已经向科学界公布了他们的研究结果。


过去完成时:
The court had promulgated a ruling before the trial began. = 在审判开始之前，法院已经发布了一项裁决。
Before the conference, the organizers had promulgated the agenda. = 在会议之前，组织者已经公布了议程。


将来时:
The president will promulgate the new economic plan next week. = 总统下周将公布新的经济计划。
The university will promulgate its admission requirements soon. = 大学很快就会公布其入学要求。


虚拟语气:
If the government promulgated stricter regulations, pollution would decrease. = 如果政府颁布更严格的法规，污染就会减少。
I wish the company had promulgated the changes earlier. = 我希望公司早点公布这些变化。


被动语态:
The new law was promulgated last month. = 新法在上个月颁布。
The decision was promulgated to all employees. = 这一决定已公布给所有员工。


疑问句:
When will the new policy be promulgated? = 新政策什么时候公布？
Has the court promulgated a judgment yet? = 法院是否已经发布判决？


否定句:
The company did not promulgate the results of the investigation. = 公司没有公布调查结果。
The government has not yet promulgated the proposed amendment. = 政府尚未颁布拟议的修正案。


PHRASE->
promulgate a law = 颁布法律
promulgate a doctrine = 传播教义
promulgate an idea = 传播思想
promulgate a policy = 公布政策
widely promulgated = 广泛传播


词源:
From Latin *promulgare, from pro- ("forth") + vulgare ("to publish").  The word combines the idea of "publishing" or "making public" with an emphasis on "forward" or "openly".
