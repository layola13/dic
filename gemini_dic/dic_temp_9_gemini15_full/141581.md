oppressingly:/əˈprɛsɪŋli/ | adv. |令人压抑地，令人窒息地 |  Describes something that causes a feeling of being weighed down, suppressed, or burdened. It implies a sense of heaviness and constraint, often in a psychological or emotional sense, but can also refer to physical conditions. |例句：The oppressive heat made it difficult to work.  令人窒息的酷暑让人难以工作。

The atmosphere in the room felt oppressingly silent.  房间里的气氛令人压抑地寂静。


近义词：stiflingly, suffocatingly, crushingly, heavily, unbearably


反义词：lightly, refreshingly, liberatingly, exhilaratingly


词性:副词
