PHRASE->
epicranial aponeurosis (帽状腱膜):  The fibrous sheet connecting the frontalis and occipitalis muscles.
epicranial muscles (颅顶肌): The muscles of the scalp.
epicranial hemorrhage (颅顶出血): Bleeding within the epicranium.
epicranial swelling (颅顶肿胀): Swelling of the scalp.
injury to the epicranium (颅顶损伤): Damage to the scalp.
