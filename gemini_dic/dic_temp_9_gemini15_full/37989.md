complication:/ˌkɒmpɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |并发症；复杂化；复杂的事物 |A complication is something that makes a situation more difficult or complex. It can refer to an unforeseen problem that arises during a process, or a complex detail that adds to the overall difficulty.  |例句：The surgery was a success, but there were some complications afterward. 手术很成功，但之后有一些并发症。|例句：The project faced numerous complications due to unforeseen circumstances.  由于不可预见的情况，该项目面临许多复杂的问题。|近义词：problem, difficulty, complexity, intricacy |反义词：simplicity, ease
