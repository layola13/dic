catwalk:/ˈkætˌwɔːk/|/ˈkætˌwɔːk/| n. | (1) 天桥；(2) (时装表演的)T型台；(3)  狭窄的通道 |(1) A narrow, raised walkway, typically found in factories or other industrial settings. (2) A raised runway used by models in fashion shows. (3) A narrow passageway or pathway. |例句：The workers walked across the catwalk to inspect the machinery. 工人们走过天桥去检查机器。|例句：The supermodel gracefully strutted down the catwalk in a stunning gown. 超级名模优雅地身着华丽礼服走过T台。|近义词：runway, walkway, passage |反义词：broadway, wide path
