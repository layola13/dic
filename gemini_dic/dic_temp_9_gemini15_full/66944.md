exper:/ɛkspɜːr/| n.|专家；经验者|An expert is a person who has a high level of knowledge or skill in a particular area.  Experiences are things that happen to a person, shaping their knowledge and understanding.|例句：He's an exper in ancient history. 他是古代史专家。|近义词：specialist, expert, professional|反义词：novice, amateur, beginner
