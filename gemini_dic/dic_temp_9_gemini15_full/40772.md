convinces:/kənˈvɪnsɪz/ | v. | 说服；使确信 | To persuade someone or something to believe that something is true. |

例句：
His arguments convinced the jury of his innocence. = 他的论点使陪审团相信他是无罪的。

The evidence convinced me that he was lying. = 证据使我相信他在撒谎。


近义词：persuade, satisfy, assure

反义词：dissuade, disbelieve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He convinces everyone with his charm. = 他用他的魅力说服了每个人。
She convinces her clients to buy her products. = 她说服她的客户购买她的产品。

一般过去时:
The doctor convinced him to quit smoking. = 医生说服他戒烟。
Her speech convinced the audience of her point. = 她的演讲使听众相信了她的观点。

现在完成时:
He has convinced many people to join his cause. = 他已经说服许多人加入他的事业。
She has convinced me that I should travel more. = 她已经说服我应该多旅行。

过去完成时:
I had convinced myself that I was right. = 我已经说服自己我是对的。
They had convinced the board to approve the project. = 他们已经说服董事会批准该项目。

将来时:
I will convince him to change his mind. = 我将会说服他改变主意。
She will convince them of her capabilities. = 她将会使他们相信她的能力。

虚拟语气:
If I could convince him, I would. = 如果我能说服他，我会的。
I wish I could convince her to stay. = 我希望我能说服她留下。

被动语态:
The jury was convinced by the evidence. = 陪审团被证据说服了。
He was convinced of his mistake. = 他被说服了他犯了错误。

疑问句:
Can you convince him to help us? = 你能说服他帮助我们吗？
Will she be convinced by my arguments? = 她会被我的论点说服吗？

否定句:
I couldn't convince him to go. = 我无法说服他去。
She wasn't convinced by his explanation. = 她没有被他的解释说服。


PHRASE->
convince someone of something = 使某人相信某事
be convinced that = 确信…
convince someone to do something = 说服某人做某事
convince oneself that = 自我安慰
convince someone of their mistake = 使某人认识到自己的错误
