gnathostoma:/ˌnæθəˈstoʊmə/| n. |颚口线虫属；颚口线虫 |A genus of nematodes, parasitic worms that infect humans and animals, primarily through the consumption of undercooked fish or frogs.  They are characterized by their spiny mouthparts. |例句：Infection with *Gnathostoma spinigerum* can cause a migratory subcutaneous larva migrans.  颚口线虫感染会导致迁移性皮下幼虫移行症。|近义词：nematode, parasite |反义词：N/A
