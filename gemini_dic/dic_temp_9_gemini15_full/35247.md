clung:/klʌŋ/| v.|粘住；坚持；依附|To hold on tightly to something; to adhere closely to a surface; to remain attached or connected.|The child clung to his mother's leg. 孩子紧紧抓住他母亲的腿。|The climber clung to the rock face. 登山者紧紧抓住岩壁。|adhere, stick, hold on|let go, detach, release|动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
He clings to his beliefs. 他坚持他的信仰。
The ivy clings to the wall. 常春藤攀附在墙上。

一般过去时:
She clung to the hope of rescue. 她抱着获救的希望。
The scent of rain clung to the air. 雨水的味道弥漫在空气中。

现在完成时:
I have clung to this idea for years. 我多年来一直坚持这个想法。
The paint has clung stubbornly to the surface. 油漆顽固地粘在表面上。

过去完成时:
He had clung to the wreckage for hours. 他在残骸上坚持了好几个小时。
The smell had clung to his clothes all day. 这种气味整天都粘在他的衣服上。

将来时:
She will cling to her principles. 她会坚持她的原则。
The scent will cling to the fabric. 气味会粘在织物上。

虚拟语气:
If he clung to his ideals, he would have been happier. 如果他坚持自己的理想，他会更快乐。
I wish I hadn't clung to that old habit. 我真希望我没有坚持那个旧习惯。

被动语态:
The child was clung to by his mother. 孩子被母亲紧紧抱着。(不太常用，主动语态更常见)
The poster was clung to the wall by the glue. 海报被胶水粘在墙上。(不太常用，主动语态更常见)

疑问句:
Does the fabric cling to the body? 这种布料贴身吗？
Why did he cling to that false hope? 他为什么坚持那虚假的希望？

否定句:
He didn't cling to his anger. 他没有坚持他的愤怒。
The wallpaper didn't cling to the wall properly. 墙纸没有好好地贴在墙上。


PHRASE->
cling to: 坚持，依附
cling on: 坚持下去
cling together: 紧紧抱在一起
cling to life: 顽强地活着
cling to hope: 抱有希望

词源->
源自古英语 *clingan，意为“粘住，依附”。
