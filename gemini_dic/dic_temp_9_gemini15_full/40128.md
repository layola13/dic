contemplatist:/ˈkɒntɛmpleɪtɪst/|/ˈkɑːntɛmpleɪtɪst/| n. |沉思者，冥想者 |A contemplatist is a person who spends a lot of time in contemplation or meditation, reflecting deeply on philosophical or spiritual matters. They are often characterized by introspection and a thoughtful, reflective nature.  |Example: The renowned artist was also a contemplatist, often spending hours lost in thought before beginning a new painting.  著名的艺术家也是一位沉思者，在开始一幅新画之前，他经常会花数小时沉思。|近义词：meditator, thinker, philosopher, mystic |反义词：action-oriented person, impulsive person
