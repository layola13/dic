heated:/ˈhiːtɪd/ | adj. | 激烈的；愤怒的；热烈的 |  characterized by intense emotion, especially anger; made hot.  |例句：The debate became heated, with both sides shouting their opinions.  这场辩论变得激烈，双方都大声喊叫自己的意见。

例句：The heated argument lasted for hours.  这场激烈的争论持续了几个小时。


近义词：passionate, fervent, intense, angry, fervent, animated | 反义词：calm, cool, apathetic, indifferent, placid


词源:  From the past participle of *heat*.  The sense of "angry, passionate" developed later, likely from the idea of something being "heated up" like a heated argument.
