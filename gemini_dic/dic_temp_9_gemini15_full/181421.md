reiterative:/rɪˈɪtərətɪv/ | adj. |重复的，反复的 | characterized by repetition; repetitive.  The process was reiterative, involving many repeated steps.  |例句：The lecture was reiterative, covering the same points multiple times. 这场讲座内容重复，多次涵盖相同的要点。

例句：His arguments were reiterative and lacked originality. 他的论点重复且缺乏原创性。


近义词：repetitive, repetitive, redundant, iterative | 反义词：original, novel, unique | 词性：形容词


词源:  From Latin *reiterātus, past participle of reiterāre "to repeat," from re- "again" + iterāre "to repeat," from iterum "again."  The word's evolution reflects its core meaning of repeated action or process.
