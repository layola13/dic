caesareans:/sɪˈzəriən/| n.|剖腹产|A surgical operation for delivering a baby by cutting through the mother's abdomen and uterus.|例句：She had a caesarean section because of complications during labor. 她因为分娩过程中的并发症进行了剖腹产。|例句：The doctor recommended a caesarean after the baby's heart rate dropped.  医生在婴儿心率下降后建议进行剖腹产。|近义词：cesarean section, C-section|反义词：vaginal delivery
