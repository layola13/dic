repetitive:/rɪˈpɛtətɪv/| adj.|重复的，单调的|characterized by or given to repetition;  lacking variety and originality.|The repetitive nature of the work made him bored.  这工作的重复性让他感到厌倦。| monotonous, redundant, reiterative | varied, diverse, original


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine performs repetitive tasks efficiently. = 这台机器高效地执行重复性任务。
His speech is repetitive and lacks substance. = 他的演讲重复且缺乏实质内容。


一般过去时:
The factory worker performed repetitive actions all day. = 工厂工人整天都在做重复性的工作。
She found the repetitive tasks tedious and tiring. = 她发现重复性的工作乏味且令人疲惫。


现在完成时:
He has completed many repetitive assignments this month. = 他这个月已经完成了许多重复性的任务。
She has felt the effects of repetitive strain injury in her hands. = 她感到她的手受到了重复性劳损的影响。


过去完成时:
They had already completed several repetitive steps before the error was detected. = 在错误被发现之前，他们已经完成了几个重复的步骤。
He had experienced repetitive stress injuries in the past. = 他过去曾经历过重复性劳损。


将来时:
She will face repetitive tasks in her new job. = 她将在新工作中面临重复性的任务。
They will automate repetitive processes to improve efficiency. = 他们将使重复性流程自动化以提高效率。


虚拟语气:
If the task were less repetitive, I would enjoy it more. = 如果任务不那么重复，我会更喜欢它。
I wish my work wasn't so repetitive. = 我希望我的工作不要那么重复。


被动语态:
Repetitive tasks are often automated. = 重复性的任务通常会被自动化。
Repetitive movements can cause injuries. = 重复性的动作会造成损伤。


疑问句:
Is this work repetitive? = 这工作重复吗？
Are there any repetitive elements in the design? = 设计中是否有任何重复的元素？


否定句:
The project is not repetitive at all. = 这个项目一点也不重复。
The presentation was not repetitive and held my attention. = 演示文稿不重复，并吸引了我的注意力。
