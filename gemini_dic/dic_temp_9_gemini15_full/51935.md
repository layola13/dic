diabetic:/ˌdaɪəˈbɛtɪk/ | adj. | 糖尿病的；患糖尿病的 | Relating to or suffering from diabetes.  | The diabetic patient needed insulin injections.  这位糖尿病患者需要注射胰岛素。 |  diabetic, insulin-dependent | healthy, non-diabetic
