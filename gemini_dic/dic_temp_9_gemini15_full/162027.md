praying:/ˈpreɪɪŋ/ | v. |祈祷；恳求；祈望 |The act of making a request or expressing a wish to a deity or higher power.  It can also imply earnest hoping or wishing for something.|例句：She was praying for her sick child's recovery.  她正在为她生病的孩子祈祷康复。|例句：He was praying for rain to save his crops. 他正在祈祷下雨来拯救他的庄稼。|近义词：beseeching, imploring, supplicating |反义词：cursing, blaspheming|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are praying for peace. = 他们在祈祷和平。
I pray every morning. = 我每天早晨祈祷。


一般过去时:
She prayed before the exam. = 考试前她祈祷了。
We prayed for guidance. = 我们祈祷得到指引。


现在完成时:
He has prayed for forgiveness. = 他一直在祈祷得到原谅。
They have prayed for many years. = 他们已经祈祷了很多年了。


过去完成时:
She had prayed for a miracle. = 她曾祈祷过奇迹。
They had prayed all night. = 他们整夜都在祈祷。


将来时:
I will pray for your success. = 我会祈祷你成功。
We will pray for a better future. = 我们会祈祷一个更好的未来。


虚拟语气:
I would pray for help if I were in trouble. = 如果我遇到麻烦，我会祈祷得到帮助。
If I were you, I would pray for understanding. = 如果我是你，我会祈祷获得理解。


被动语态:
Prayers were said for the victims. = 为受害者进行了祈祷。
A prayer was offered for the deceased. = 为死者献上了祈祷。


疑问句:
Are you praying? = 你在祈祷吗？
What are you praying for? = 你在为什祈祷？


否定句:
She didn't pray that day. = 她那天没有祈祷。
They don't pray regularly. = 他们不定期祈祷。


PHRASE->
pray for: 为…祈祷
pray to: 向…祈祷
pray silently: 默默祈祷
pray earnestly: 恳切祈祷
pray incessantly: 不停地祈祷
