geogony:/dʒiˈɒɡəni/| n. |地质生成学，地球起源论|The study of the origin and formation of the Earth.  It encompasses the processes that shaped the planet from its earliest stages to its present state, including the formation of its core, mantle, crust, and atmosphere.  Geogony often overlaps with other scientific disciplines, such as geology, geophysics, and astronomy.|例句：Theories of geogony attempt to explain the formation of Earth's continents and oceans. 地球起源论试图解释地球大陆和海洋的形成。

例句：Research in geogony is crucial for understanding the evolution of our planet and the conditions that led to the emergence of life. 地质生成学的研究对于理解我们星球的演化以及生命出现的条件至关重要。


近义词：cosmogony (宇宙起源论), planetary formation (行星形成)
反义词：无


词性：名词
