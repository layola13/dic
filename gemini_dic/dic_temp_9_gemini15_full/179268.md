redistribute:/ˌriːˈdɪstrɪbjuːt/ | vt. |重新分配，再分配 | To distribute again or anew.  It implies a previous distribution that is being altered or corrected. |例句：The government plans to redistribute wealth from the rich to the poor.  政府计划将财富从富人手中重新分配给穷人。

例句：The teacher redistributed the worksheets after realizing some students didn't receive them.老师在意识到有些学生没有收到试卷后重新分发了试卷。


近义词：reallocate, reallot, redistribute, apportion anew

反义词：concentrate, centralize
