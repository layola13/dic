moonfish:/ˈmuːnˌfɪʃ/ | n. | 月鱼 | A type of deep-sea fish, typically silver or silvery-blue, with large, round eyes and a laterally compressed body.  They are known for their unusual appearance and adaptations to the deep sea environment.  |  例句：The moonfish, with its oddly shaped body, is a fascinating creature of the deep ocean. 月鱼奇特的体型，是深海中引人入胜的生物。
