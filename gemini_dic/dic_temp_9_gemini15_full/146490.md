overgambling: /ˌoʊvərˈɡæmblɪŋ/ | n. |过度赌博|Excessive gambling; gambling to an extent that is harmful or destructive. It often leads to financial ruin, relationship problems, and addiction.|

例句：His overgambling led to significant debt and family problems.  他的过度赌博导致了严重的债务和家庭问题。

例句：The counselor helped him understand the destructive nature of his overgambling. 顾问帮助他理解过度赌博的破坏性本质。


近义词：excessive gambling, compulsive gambling, gambling addiction

反义词：responsible gambling, moderate gambling
