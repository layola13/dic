houseparty: /ˈhaʊspɑːrti/ | n. |家庭聚会，屋内派对 | A social gathering held at someone's home, typically involving a relatively informal group of people.  | Example:  We're having a house party to celebrate my birthday. 我们要举办一个家庭聚会来庆祝我的生日。 |近义词：home party, gathering, get-together |反义词：formal event, public party
