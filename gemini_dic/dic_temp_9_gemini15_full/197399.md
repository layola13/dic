sheaves:/ʃiːvz/| n.|捆，束；(植物的)穗；(数据)集合|A sheaf is a collection of things, usually stalks of grain or other plants, bound together.  In mathematics, it refers to a collection of data. |例句：The farmer gathered the sheaves of wheat.  农民收集了小麦的麦捆。  例句：The mathematician worked with sheaves of data. 数学家处理数据集合。|近义词：bundle, bunch, collection|反义词：scatter, disperse|名词
