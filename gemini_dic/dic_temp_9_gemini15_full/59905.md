edemas:/ɪˈdiːməz/| n.|水肿，浮肿|Swelling of soft tissues due to fluid accumulation.  It is a symptom, not a disease in itself, and can be caused by various factors, including heart failure, kidney disease, and allergic reactions.|例句：The patient presented with significant edema in his lower extremities.  这位病人下肢出现严重水肿。

例句：  The doctor attributed the facial edema to an allergic reaction. 医生将面部水肿归因于过敏反应。


近义词：swelling, puffiness, turgor


反义词：dehydration


词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Edema affects many people with heart failure.  心力衰竭的许多人都患有水肿。
The swelling indicates potential edema.  肿胀表明可能有水肿。


一般过去时:
His ankles showed severe edema after the long flight. 长途飞行后，他的脚踝出现了严重水肿。
The doctor diagnosed her with pulmonary edema.  医生诊断她患有肺水肿。


现在完成时:

The patient has developed significant edema in his legs.  病人腿部出现了严重水肿。


过去完成时:
He had suffered from edema for several weeks before seeking medical attention. 在就医之前，他已经患有几周的水肿了。
The condition had progressed to generalized edema.  病情已发展为全身性水肿。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

否定句:
