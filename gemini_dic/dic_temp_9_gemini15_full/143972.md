outraged:/aʊtˈreɪdʒd/ | adj. | 义愤填膺的；愤怒的 | Extremely angry and annoyed, especially because of something unfair or morally wrong.  | 例句：He was outraged by the injustice of the verdict. 他对判决的不公正感到义愤填膺。

例句：The public was outraged at the politician's lies. 公众对这位政治家的谎言感到愤怒。


近义词：indignant, furious, incensed, enraged, angered | 反义词：calm, composed, serene, pleased


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is outraged by the news. 他对这个消息感到愤怒。
The public is outraged by the government's decision. 公众对政府的决定感到愤怒。


一般过去时:
She was outraged at his behavior. 她对他行为感到愤怒。
They were outraged by the unfair treatment. 他们对不公平的待遇感到愤怒。


现在完成时:
I have been outraged by his constant lies. 我一直对他不断的谎言感到愤怒。
We have been outraged by the lack of transparency. 我们一直对缺乏透明度感到愤怒。


过去完成时:
He had been outraged by the news before he even heard the details.  在他甚至听到细节之前，他已经对这个消息感到愤怒了。
They had been outraged by the company's actions for years. 他们多年来一直对公司的行为感到愤怒。


将来时:
She will be outraged when she hears about it. 当她听到这件事时，她会感到愤怒。
The people will be outraged if the truth is revealed. 如果真相大白，人们会感到愤怒。


虚拟语气:
I would be outraged if that happened to me. 如果那件事发生在我身上，我会很愤怒。
If I were you, I would be outraged by their behavior. 如果我是你，我会对他们的行为感到愤怒。


被动语态:
The community was outraged by the crime. 社区对这起犯罪感到愤怒。(被动)
The public has been outraged by the lack of action. 公众因缺乏行动而感到愤怒。(被动)


疑问句:
Are you outraged by the situation? 你对这种情况感到愤怒吗？
Is she outraged by his actions? 她对他行为感到愤怒吗？


否定句:
He wasn't outraged by the criticism. 他对批评并不愤怒。
They weren't outraged by the delay. 他们对延误并不愤怒。


PHRASE->
be outraged at/by: 对…感到愤怒
feel outraged: 感到愤怒
express outrage: 表达愤怒
outraged reaction: 愤怒的反应
a wave of outrage: 一阵愤怒
