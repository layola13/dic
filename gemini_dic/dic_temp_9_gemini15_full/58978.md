duteous:/ˈduːtiəs/| adj.|尽职的；孝顺的；恭敬的|Characterized by a diligent performance of duty; obedient; respectful.  It implies a strong sense of obligation and a willingness to fulfill one's responsibilities.|例句：He was a duteous son, always eager to please his parents. 他是个孝顺的儿子，总是渴望讨好父母。

例句：The duteous nurse cared for her patients with unwavering dedication. 尽职的护士以坚定不移的奉献精神照顾她的病人。


近义词：obedient, respectful, diligent, conscientious, compliant|反义词：disobedient, disrespectful, negligent, remiss, insubordinate|词性：形容词
