siroccos:/sɪˈrɒkoʊz/| n.|西罗科风，热风|A sirocco is a hot, dry wind that blows from North Africa across the Mediterranean Sea to Southern Europe.|例句：The sirocco brought with it a wave of intense heat and dust. 西罗科风带来了一波强烈的热浪和尘土。 The farmers worried about the effect of the sirocco on their crops.  农民们担心西罗科风会影响他们的庄稼。|近义词：hot wind, desert wind|反义词：cold wind, refreshing breeze|名词
