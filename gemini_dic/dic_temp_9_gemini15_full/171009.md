pseudoscopic:/ˌsuːdəʊˈskɒpɪk/| adj.| 似立体镜的，伪立体镜的 |  Relating to or denoting a reversed stereoscopic effect, in which concave surfaces appear convex and vice versa.  It describes an image that appears three-dimensional but with the depth reversed.  | Example: The pseudoscopic image showed the valley as a mountain peak.  伪立体图像显示山谷像山峰一样。


The pseudoscopic image was unsettling to viewers.  伪立体图像让观众感到不安。|近义词：reversed stereoscopic, inverted depth|反义词：stereoscopic
