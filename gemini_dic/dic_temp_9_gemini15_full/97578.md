ingurgitation: /ˌɪŋɡɜːrˈdʒɪteɪʃən/ | n. | 狼吞虎咽；狂饮；吞咽 | The act of swallowing something quickly and greedily.  It often implies excessive consumption. | 例句：The ingurgitation of the entire pizza in under five minutes impressed, and slightly disgusted, everyone.  = 五分钟内狼吞虎咽地吃完整个披萨，让每个人都印象深刻，也略感反胃。

The ingurgitation of alcohol led to his regrettable behavior later that evening. = 狂饮导致他当晚的行为令人遗憾。| 近义词：gulping, gobbling, devouring, consuming | 反义词：sipping, nibbling, savoring

词源:
