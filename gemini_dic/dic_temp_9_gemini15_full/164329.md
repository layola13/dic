preelecting:/priːɪˈlɛktɪŋ/ | v. | 预选；提前选举 |  The act of selecting or choosing someone or something in advance of a formal election or decision.  It often implies a process of narrowing down choices before a final selection. | 例句：The committee is preelecting candidates for the upcoming mayoral race.  委员会正在为即将到来的市长竞选预选候选人。


The party is preelecting delegates to the national convention.  该党正在预选参加全国代表大会的代表。


近义词：preselecting, nominating, shortlisting  | 反义词：postponing, delaying

词源:
The word "preelecting" is formed by adding the prefix "pre-" (meaning "before" or "in advance of") to the verb "electing" (the act of choosing or selecting).  Therefore, it literally means "choosing or selecting beforehand".
