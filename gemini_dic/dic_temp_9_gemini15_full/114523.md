malaises:/ˈmælɛɪz/| n.|身心不适；普遍的不安；疾病征兆 |A feeling of discomfort or illness whose exact cause is difficult to identify.  It often refers to a general feeling of unease or dissatisfaction, not necessarily a specific physical ailment. |例句：She suffered from a general malaise and lack of energy. 她感到全身不适，缺乏精力。

例句：The economic malaise is affecting many businesses. 经济低迷正在影响许多企业。


近义词：discomfort, uneasiness, unease, illness, sickness, ailment, disquiet

反义词：well-being, health, comfort, ease


词性：名词
