eleuthero: /ɪˈluːθərəʊ/ | n. | 西伯利亚人参 |  A genus of shrubs in the family Araliaceae, native to Siberia and northeastern Asia, known for its purported adaptogenic properties.  Eleutherococcus senticosus, commonly known as Siberian ginseng, is the most well-known species.  Its roots and rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and are believed to enhance physical endurance, improve immune function, and reduce stress.
