held off:/held ɒf/| v.|推迟，阻止，抵挡|To delay or postpone something; to prevent something from happening or someone from doing something.|例句：The meeting was held off until next week.  会议推迟到下周举行。|例句：The soldiers held off the enemy attack. 士兵们抵挡住了敌人的进攻。|近义词：postpone, delay, prevent, resist|反义词：accelerate, expedite, hasten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They hold off the announcement until next month. = 他们把公告推迟到下个月。
He holds off making a decision. = 他推迟做决定。

一般过去时:
She held off replying to his email. = 她推迟回复他的邮件。
We held off the attack successfully. = 我们成功地抵挡住了攻击。

现在完成时:
They have held off making any changes. = 他们一直没有做任何改变。
The doctors have held off performing surgery until she is stronger. = 医生们一直推迟手术，直到她身体更强壮。


过去完成时:
She had held off telling him the bad news until after the party. = 她把坏消息推迟到聚会之后才告诉他。
The team had held off their opponents for the majority of the game. = 这支球队在比赛的大部分时间里都抵挡住了对手。


将来时:
We will hold off on the project until we have more funding. = 我们将推迟项目，直到我们获得更多资金。
They will hold off signing the contract until they review it thoroughly. = 他们将在仔细审查合同后才签署。


虚拟语气:
If we had held off making a decision, we would have avoided this problem. = 如果我们推迟做决定，我们就不会遇到这个问题。
I wish I had held off speaking so rashly. = 我希望我没有那么冲动地说话。


被动语态:
The launch was held off due to bad weather. = 由于天气不好，发射被推迟了。
The attack was held off by the brave soldiers. = 勇敢的士兵们抵挡住了攻击。


疑问句:
Will they hold off the announcement? = 他们会推迟公告吗？
Did they hold off the attack? = 他们抵挡住了攻击吗？


否定句:
They didn't hold off the project. = 他们没有推迟项目。
She didn't hold off telling him the truth. = 她没有推迟告诉他真相。


PHRASE->
hold off on = 推迟
hold off against = 抵挡
hold off from = 避免
hold off until = 推迟到...为止
hold off the rain = 挡雨 (非正式)
