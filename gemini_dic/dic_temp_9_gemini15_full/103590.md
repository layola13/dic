join-up: /ˈdʒɔɪn ʌp/ | v. |联合；加入；集合；合并 | To come together or combine; to become a member of a group or organization. | 例句：The two companies decided to join up and create a larger enterprise.  两家公司决定合并，创建一个更大的企业。
The soldiers joined up to fight for their country.  士兵们参军保卫他们的国家。


近义词：combine, unite, merge, consolidate, integrate | 反义词：separate, divide, disband, split


USAGE->

They join up every Saturday for a hiking trip. = 他们每周六都一起远足。
The two rivers join up near the town. = 两条河在小镇附近汇合。
