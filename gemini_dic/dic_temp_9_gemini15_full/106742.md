kyushu:/ˈkjuːʃuː/| n.|九州岛|Kyushu is the third-largest of the four main islands of Japan, located southwest of Honshu. It's known for its volcanic landscapes, hot springs (onsen), and unique culture.|例句：We spent a week exploring the beautiful landscapes of Kyushu. 我们花了一周时间探索九州美丽的风景。|近义词：island|反义词:mainland
