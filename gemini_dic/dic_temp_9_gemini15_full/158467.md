pledger: /ˈplɛdʒər/ | n. | 保证人；抵押人 |  A person who makes a pledge or gives a guarantee; someone who provides collateral.  | Example: The bank requires a pledger for such a large loan.  这家银行要求这么大笔贷款要有担保人。| 近义词：guarantor, surety, security | 反义词：borrower (in the context of a loan) |  名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is the pledger of the loan. = 他是这笔贷款的担保人。
She acts as a pledger for her friend's business. = 她担任朋友生意的担保人。


一般过去时:
He was the pledger in that deal. = 他在那笔交易中是担保人。
They were pledgers for the project. = 他们是该项目的担保人。


现在完成时:
He has been a reliable pledger for many years. = 多年来，他一直是一位可靠的担保人。
She has acted as a pledger on several occasions. = 她曾多次担任担保人。


过去完成时:
He had been a pledger before the company went bankrupt. = 公司破产前，他曾是担保人。
They had already been pledgers for similar projects. = 他们之前已经为类似项目做过担保人。


将来时:
He will be the pledger for the new venture. = 他将成为新项目的担保人。
She will act as a pledger for her son's loan. = 她将为儿子的贷款担保。


虚拟语气:
If he were a pledger, the loan would be approved. = 如果他是担保人，贷款就会获得批准。
If she had been a pledger, the consequences might have been different. = 如果她是担保人，后果可能会有所不同。


被动语态:
The loan was guaranteed by a reliable pledger. = 这笔贷款由一位可靠的担保人担保。
The project was secured with a pledger from a reputable firm. = 该项目由一家信誉良好的公司的担保人提供担保。


疑问句:
Who is the pledger for this contract? = 这份合同的担保人是谁？
Is she a suitable pledger for this large investment? = 她适合担任这笔巨额投资的担保人吗？


否定句:
He is not the pledger; it's his brother. = 他不是担保人，是他哥哥。
She was not a pledger for the risky venture. = 她不是那项风险投资的担保人。
