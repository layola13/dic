chattel:/ˈtʃætəl/| n. |动产，财产|Movable property;  personal possessions as opposed to real estate.  It refers to anything that can be moved and is owned,  excluding land and buildings.|例句：The antique furniture was part of the deceased's chattel.  这套古董家具是死者遗产的一部分。|例句：He listed all his chattels in the will. 他在遗嘱中列出了他所有的动产。|近义词：goods, personal property, effects|反义词：real estate, realty, immovable property|名词
