diagnostics:/ˌdaɪæɡˈnɒstɪks/ /ˌdaɪæɡˈnɒstɪks/| n.|诊断学；诊断程序；诊断信息|The branch of medicine dealing with the identification of diseases.  It also refers to the processes and tools used to identify problems in a system or device. |例句：The doctor ordered some diagnostics to determine the cause of the patient's illness. 医生开了些诊断检查来确定病人患病的原因。

例句：The IT team ran diagnostics on the server to find the source of the error. IT团队对服务器进行了诊断以查找错误的根源。


近义词：diagnosis, assessment, examination, testing|反义词：treatment, cure|名词
