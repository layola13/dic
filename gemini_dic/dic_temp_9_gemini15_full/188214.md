rower:/ˈroʊər/ /ˈrəʊə(r)/| n. |划船者，赛艇运动员|A rower is someone who rows a boat, especially in competitive rowing.  Rowers need strength, endurance, and coordination.|例句：He is a skilled rower and has won many competitions. 他是一位技艺高超的划船运动员，赢得了许多比赛。

例句：The rower pulled hard on the oars, propelling the boat forward.  划船者用力划桨，推动船前进。

近义词：oarsman, sculler

反义词：None directly applicable.  (Could consider terms like "passenger" in a boat context, but this is not a true antonym).

词性:Noun
