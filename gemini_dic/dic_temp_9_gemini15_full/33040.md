choosing: /ˈtʃuːzɪŋ/ | v. | 选择；挑选 |  The act of selecting or making a choice.  It is the present participle of the verb "to choose". | 例句：She is choosing a dress for the party. = 她正在为派对选一件衣服。

He's choosing between two job offers. = 他正在选择两个工作机会中的一个。


近义词：selecting, picking, opting, deciding | 反义词：rejecting, refusing


词性：动词（现在分词）


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are choosing a new car. = 他们正在选择一辆新车。
We are choosing sides in this conflict. = 我们在这个冲突中正在选择立场。


一般过去时:
I was choosing a gift for my friend. = 我当时正在为我的朋友挑选礼物。
She was choosing between two apartments. = 她当时正在选择两套公寓中的一个。


现在完成时:
He has been choosing gifts all morning. = 他整个上午都在挑选礼物。
They have been choosing carefully for weeks. = 他们已经仔细挑选了几个星期了。


过去完成时:
We had been choosing for hours before we finally decided. = 我们在最终决定之前已经挑选了几个小时。
She had been choosing a dress for the wedding since last month. = 自从上个月以来，她一直在为婚礼挑选礼服。


将来时:
They will be choosing a new CEO next week. = 他们下周将要选择一位新的首席执行官。
I will be choosing a major next semester. = 下个学期我将要选择专业。


虚拟语气:
If I were choosing, I'd pick the blue one. = 如果让我选择，我会选蓝色的那个。
I wish I were choosing my own path in life. = 我希望自己能选择自己的人生道路。


被动语态:
The winner will be chosen by lottery. = 获胜者将通过抽签的方式选出。
A new design is being chosen for the building. = 正在为该建筑物选择一个新的设计。


疑问句:
Are you choosing the red or the blue one? = 你选红色还是蓝色那个？
What are they choosing to do tonight? = 他们今晚选择做什么？


否定句:
I am not choosing sides in this argument. = 我不会在这个争论中选择立场。
They were not choosing wisely. = 他们当时选择得并不明智。


PHRASE->
choosing words carefully = 小心谨慎地选择词语
choosing a career path = 选择职业道路
choosing your battles = 选择你的战斗（明智地选择冲突）
choosing sides = 选择立场
choosing a life partner = 选择人生伴侣
