fragmentist: /ˈfræɡmənɪst/ | n. |碎片主义者，片段作家| A person who creates or appreciates art, literature, or other forms of expression that are characterized by fragmentation, discontinuity, or incompleteness.  They often focus on juxtaposing disparate elements to create a whole, or embrace the inherent incompleteness of experience.  |  The artist is considered a fragmentist, deliberately leaving his works unfinished to convey a sense of incompleteness.  碎片主义艺术家故意留下未完成的作品，以表达一种不完整感。 |  patchworker, collagist |  completist, unifier | 名词
