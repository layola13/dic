overserves:/ˌoʊvərˈsɜːrvz/ | vt. |供应过多；过度服务 |To serve more than is needed or wanted.  |例句：The waiter overserved the customers, leading to wasted food and an inflated bill.  服务员供应过多，导致食物浪费和账单膨胀。|例句：The bartender overserved the already intoxicated patron, contributing to a dangerous situation. 酒保过度为已经醉酒的顾客提供酒水，导致危险情况的发生。|近义词：overcater, oversupply |反义词：underserve

词源:
The word "overserves" is formed by combining the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") with the verb "serves" (meaning "to provide or supply").  Therefore, it literally means "to serve too much".
