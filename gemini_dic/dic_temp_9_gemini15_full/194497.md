self-ruins:/ˌsɛlfˈruːɪnz/| n. |自我毁灭；自取灭亡 |The act of destroying oneself or one's own prospects through one's own actions.  It often implies a gradual process of decline caused by internal flaws or poor choices.  |例句：His career was marked by self-ruin brought on by excessive drinking and gambling.  他的职业生涯因酗酒和赌博而自我毁灭。

His empire, built on shaky foundations, was destined for self-ruin.  他的帝国建立在动荡的基础上，注定要自我毁灭。


近义词：self-destruction, downfall, ruin, destruction, demise |反义词：self-preservation, success, triumph |名词
