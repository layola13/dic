sequoia:/sɪˈkwɔɪə/| n. |红杉；巨杉|A sequoia is a type of redwood tree, known for its immense size and longevity.  Sequoias are found in California and Oregon.|例句：The giant sequoia trees are a breathtaking sight. 巨大的红杉树是令人叹为观止的景象。|近义词：redwood|反义词：无
