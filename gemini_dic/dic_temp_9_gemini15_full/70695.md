finalizing: /ˈfaɪnəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使最终确定；完成…的最后阶段 | To complete (something) or bring it to a final state; to put the finishing touches on something. |例句：We are finalizing the details of the contract. 我们正在敲定合同的细节。

例句：They are finalizing the arrangements for the wedding.  他们正在完成婚礼的安排。


近义词：completing, concluding, perfecting, finishing | 反义词：starting, beginning, initiating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are finalizing the report. = 我们正在完成报告。
They finalize the designs every Friday. = 他们每周五完成设计。

一般过去时:
We finalized the deal yesterday. = 我们昨天完成了这笔交易。
She finalized her presentation last night. = 她昨晚完成了她的演示文稿。

现在完成时:
I have finalized my travel plans. = 我已经完成了我的旅行计划。

过去完成时:

将来时:
