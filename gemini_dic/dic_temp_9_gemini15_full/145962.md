overdistorted:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪstɔːrtɪd/ | adj. |过度失真的，扭曲严重的| Extremely distorted; beyond the point of reasonable interpretation or representation.  The image was so overdistorted it was unrecognizable. |近义词：grossly distorted, severely warped, excessively mangled |反义词： undistorted, accurate, clear


例句：

The radio signal was overdistorted due to atmospheric interference.  无线电信号由于大气干扰而严重失真。

The artist's overdistorted portrayal of reality made the painting unsettling. 艺术家对现实的过度扭曲描绘使这幅画令人不安。
