princess:/ˈprɪnsɛs/|/ˈprɪnsɛs/| n. |公主；王妃|A female member of a royal family, especially the daughter of a king or queen.  A princess is often associated with elegance, grace, and a life of privilege.|例句：Cinderella was a kind princess. 灰姑娘是一位善良的公主。|例句：The princess married a handsome prince. 公主嫁给了一位英俊的王子。|近义词：royal, lady, noblewoman|反义词：commoner
