ora:/ˈɔːrə/ | n. | 预言；神谕；口头表达；说话能力；口才 | A divine utterance or revelation; a prophecy.  |例句：The oracle predicted a great victory.  神谕预言了一场伟大的胜利。
|例句：The politician's ora captivated the audience.  这位政治家的口才迷住了听众。|近义词：prophecy, prediction, pronouncement |反义词：silence, stillness
