prefloration:/ˌpriːflɔːˈreɪʃən/| n. |花芽排列；花蕾的排列方式|The arrangement of floral parts in a flower bud before it opens.  Prefloration describes how the sepals, petals, and other floral organs are positioned relative to one another within the unopened bud. This arrangement can be important for identifying different plant species. |Example: The prefloration of the tulip is imbricate, meaning the petals overlap each other.郁金香的花蕾排列方式是覆瓦状的，意思是花瓣互相重叠。|近义词：floral arrangement, bud arrangement|反义词：anthesis (flowering)|名词
