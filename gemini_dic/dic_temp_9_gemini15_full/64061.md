epitomize:/ɪˈpɪtəmaɪz/ | vt. | 成为…的典型；是…的缩影；完美地体现 | To be a perfect example of something; to embody or represent the essential characteristics of something. |例句：Her work epitomizes the spirit of the times. 她的作品完美地体现了时代的精髓。  |例句：The building epitomizes the architectural style of the 1920s. 这座建筑是20世纪20年代建筑风格的缩影。|近义词：embody, represent, exemplify, typify |反义词：misrepresent, contradict, belie | 动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He epitomizes success. = 他是成功的典范。
This painting epitomizes her artistic style. = 这幅画完美地体现了她的艺术风格。

一般过去时:
The film epitomized the era's anxieties. = 这部电影是那个时代焦虑的缩影。
Her performance epitomized grace and elegance. = 她的表演体现了优雅和高贵。

现在完成时:
His career has epitomized dedication and hard work. = 他的职业生涯体现了奉献和努力。
This project has epitomized innovation in the field. = 这个项目完美地体现了该领域的创新。


过去完成时:
The novel had epitomized the social issues of its time. = 这部小说完美地体现了当时的社会问题。
His previous work had epitomized a different approach. = 他以前的作品体现了一种不同的方法。


将来时:
The new building will epitomize modern architecture. = 新建筑将成为现代建筑的典范。
Her upcoming novel will epitomize her maturity as a writer. = 她即将出版的小说将体现她作为作家的成熟。


虚拟语气:
If her work were to epitomize the genre, it would be a significant achievement. = 如果她的作品能成为该类型的典范，那将是一项重大成就。
If only his life had epitomized his ideals. = 但愿他的生活能体现他的理想。


被动语态:
The city is epitomized by its stunning skyline. =这座城市以其令人惊叹的天际线为其典型代表。
The movement was epitomized by its strong leader. =这场运动以其强有力的领导者为典型代表。


疑问句:
Does this design epitomize minimalist style? = 这个设计体现了极简主义风格吗？
What event epitomizes the spirit of the revolution? = 哪个事件是革命精神的缩影？


否定句:
His actions did not epitomize honesty. = 他的行为并不体现诚实。
The film failed to epitomize the complexities of the situation. = 这部电影未能体现局势的复杂性。
