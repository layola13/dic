contorting:/kənˈtɔːrtɪŋ/ | v. | 扭曲；歪曲；曲解 | To twist or bend out of shape; to distort the meaning of something. | 例句：The acrobat contorted his body into a strange shape.  杂技演员把身体扭曲成奇怪的形状。
The politician contorted the facts to suit his argument.  政治家歪曲事实以符合他的论点。 |近义词：twist, distort, warp, wrench | 反义词：straighten, unbend, clarify
