kopje:/ˈkɒpjɪ/| n. |小丘，小石山 |A kopje is a small, isolated hill or rocky outcrop, common in southern Africa.  It's often formed by erosion-resistant rock. |例句：The landscape was dotted with kopjes, offering stunning views.  这片风景点缀着许多小丘，景色壮观。

例句：We saw a herd of zebras grazing on the slopes of a distant kopje. 我们看到一群斑马在远处一个小丘的山坡上吃草。


近义词：hill, mound, knoll, outcrop |反义词：plain, valley
