pendulums: /ˈpɛndʒʊləmz/ | n. | 摆，钟摆 |  A pendulum is a weight suspended from a pivot so that it can swing freely.  It's a device that demonstrates the principle of simple harmonic motion. Pendulums are used in clocks and other timing devices. | The grandfather clock's pendulums swung rhythmically.  这个落地钟的钟摆有节奏地摆动着。 The scientist used a pendulum to demonstrate the effect of gravity. 科学家用钟摆演示了重力的作用。 | oscilloscopes, swings |  (no direct antonyms, but concepts like "static" or "immobile" are opposite to its motion) | 名词
