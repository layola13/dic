confidentiality: /ˌkɒnfɪdɛnʃiˈælɪti/ | /ˌkɑːnfɪdɛnʃiˈælɪti/ | n. | 机密性，保密性 | The quality or state of being confidential; the act of keeping something secret.  It implies a trust relationship where information is shared only with those authorized to receive it. |


例句：

The doctor emphasized the importance of patient confidentiality.  医生强调了病人保密的重要性。

Maintaining confidentiality is crucial in this line of work.  在这个行业中，保持机密性至关重要。


近义词：secrecy, privacy, discretion


反义词：disclosure, openness, transparency


词性：名词
