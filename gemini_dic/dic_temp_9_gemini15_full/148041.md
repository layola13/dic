overrule:/ˌoʊvərˈruːl/|/ˈoʊvəruːl/| vt.|推翻；否决；凌驾于…之上|To reject or override a previous decision, ruling, or opinion.  It implies authority to set aside a prior decision.|The judge overruled the objection.  法官驳回了异议。|The higher court overruled the lower court's decision. 上级法院推翻了下级法院的判决。|uphold, supersede|sustain, affirm|动词


USAGE->

The judge overrules objections frequently. = 法官经常驳回异议。
