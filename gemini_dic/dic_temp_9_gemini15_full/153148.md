pelecypoda:/pɛlɪˈsɪpɒdə/| n. |双壳类动物| Pelecypoda is a taxonomic class that traditionally includes bivalve mollusks, characterized by their laterally compressed bodies enclosed within two shells hinged together.  This class is now largely considered obsolete, with its members distributed among different subclasses within the class Bivalvia. | Example:  Many pelecypods are filter feeders, drawing in water and extracting nutrients. 许多双壳类动物是滤食性动物，吸入水并提取营养物质。 |近义词:bivalve, lamellibranch |反义词:N/A
