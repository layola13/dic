commit:/kəˈmɪt/ | vt. n.| 承诺；犯罪；委托；提交；（使）承担义务 | to pledge or bind oneself to a course of action; to perpetrate (a crime); to entrust or consign (something to someone's care); to send (something) to a place for safekeeping or processing; to engage or involve oneself or someone else in a particular course of action. |例句：He committed himself to finishing the project by Friday. 他承诺在星期五之前完成这个项目。  He committed a serious crime. 他犯了一桩重罪。 |近义词：pledge, entrust, consign, dedicate, perpetrate | 反义词：neglect, abandon, forsake | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I commit to my goals daily. = 我每天都致力于我的目标。
She commits to her work wholeheartedly. = 她全心全意地投入工作。

一般过去时:
He committed a mistake. = 他犯了一个错误。
They committed to the plan. = 他们承诺遵守计划。

现在完成时:
I have committed myself to this project. = 我已经全身心地投入到这个项目中。
She has committed many acts of kindness. = 她做了很多善事。

过去完成时:
He had already committed to the deal before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，他已经答应了这笔交易。
They had committed themselves to the cause for years. = 他们多年来一直致力于这项事业。

将来时:
I will commit to learning a new language next year. = 我明年将致力于学习一门外语。
They will commit to the new strategy. = 他们将致力于新的策略。

虚拟语气:
If I committed to it, I would succeed. = 如果我全力以赴，我会成功的。
I wish I had committed to studying harder. = 我真希望我以前学习更努力些。

被动语态:
The funds were committed to the research project. = 资金已拨给该研究项目。
The case was committed to a higher court. = 此案已提交上级法院。

疑问句:
Are you committed to this relationship? = 你对这段感情认真吗？
Has he committed any crimes? = 他犯过什么罪吗？

否定句:
I will not commit to that. = 我不会答应那件事。
He didn't commit himself to a definite answer. = 他没有给出明确的答复。
