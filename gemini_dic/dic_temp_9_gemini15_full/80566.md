goody-goody:/ˈɡʊdi ˈɡʊdi/| adj.|假装虔诚的；假正经的；过于循规蹈矩的|A goody-goody person is someone who pretends to be very good and moral, often in an annoying way. They are excessively pious or virtuous, often to a point of being hypocritical.  |例句：He's such a goody-goody; he always follows the rules even when it's not necessary. 他太假正经了，即使没有必要，他总是遵守规则。
  
例句：Stop being such a goody-goody and join the fun! 别那么假正经了，来一起玩吧！


近义词：sanctimonious, self-righteous, pious, moralistic, holier-than-thou


反义词：rebellious, wicked, naughty, mischievous, impious


词性：形容词
