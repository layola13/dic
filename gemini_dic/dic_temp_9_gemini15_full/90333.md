housings:/ˈhaʊzɪŋz/ | n. | 住房；外壳；(机器的)外罩 |  The plural of "housing," referring to buildings or shelters for people, or protective coverings for equipment or components.  | Example: The government is investing heavily in social housings to address the housing shortage. 政府正在大力投资社会保障性住房以解决住房短缺问题。  The electronic housings were damaged in the fire. 电子设备的外壳在火灾中受损。 |近义词：lodgings, shelters, casings, enclosures | 反义词： homelessness (反义词是相对的，指无家可归的状态) | 名词
