confining:/kənˈfaɪnɪŋ/| adj.| 限制性的；狭窄的；幽闭的 | Restricting or limiting freedom of movement or expression.  |例句：The confining atmosphere of the small room made her feel claustrophobic.  狭小的房间里压抑的气氛让她感到幽闭恐惧。

例句：He felt confining pressure from his demanding job. 他感到自己要求很高的工作带来了很大的压力。


近义词：restricting, limiting, cramped, constricting, stifling

反义词：open, free, spacious, liberating

词性：形容词


USAGE->
