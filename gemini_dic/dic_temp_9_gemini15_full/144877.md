over-represent:/ˌoʊvərˈrɛprɪˌzɛnt/| vt.|过分代表，夸大代表 |To represent (something) in a way that is greater than its actual proportion or importance.  This often implies a bias or distortion of reality.|例句：Minorities are often over-represented in crime statistics. 少数族裔在犯罪统计中常常被夸大其词。|例句：The survey over-represented the opinions of young people.  这项调查夸大了年轻人的意见。|近义词：exaggerate, inflate, misrepresent|反义词：underrepresent, understate|动词
