isopogonous:/ˌaɪsoʊˈpɒɡənəs/| adj.|等柱的，同柱的|Having stamens of equal length.  The term is primarily used in botany to describe flowers where the stamens are all approximately the same length.  This contrasts with flowers where stamens have varying lengths.|Example: The isopogonous flowers of this species are easily distinguishable from those of its relatives.|近义词：homostaminous|反义词：heterostaminous
