circus:/ˈsɜːrkəs/|/ˈsɝːkəs/| n. |马戏团；杂技；喧嚣；混乱 |A circus is a form of entertainment that features various acts such as acrobats, clowns, trained animals, and other performers.  It's often characterized by a large tent or arena. |The children were thrilled by the circus performers. 孩子们对马戏团的表演者感到兴奋。The political debate turned into a circus. 政治辩论变成了闹剧。|carnival, spectacle, show |order, calm |名词
