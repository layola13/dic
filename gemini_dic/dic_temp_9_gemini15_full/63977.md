epistaxis:/ˌɛpɪˈstæksɪs/| n. |鼻出血，鼻腔出血|Bleeding from the nose.  It is a common condition that can be caused by a variety of factors, including trauma, infection, and blood clotting disorders.|例句：He suffered a severe epistaxis after the accident.  事故后他发生了严重的鼻出血。|例句：The doctor treated his epistaxis with nasal packing. 医生用鼻腔填塞法治疗了他的鼻出血。|近义词：nosebleed|反义词：无
