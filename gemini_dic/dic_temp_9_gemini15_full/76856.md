gangion:/ˈɡæŋɡiən/| n.|吊环；(钟摆等的)悬挂物|A gangion is a ring or shackle used to suspend something, such as a bell or a weight.| The gangion on the ship's bell was tarnished with age.  船钟上的吊环因年代久远而失去光泽。The heavy weight was attached to the crane by a sturdy gangion.  重物通过一个坚固的吊环连接到起重机上。|近义词：ring, shackle, link|反义词：无
