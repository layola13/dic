enripen:/ɪnˈraɪpən/| vt.|使成熟；使完善|To become ripe or mature; to bring to a state of ripeness or maturity.  The process of becoming ripe or mature; to develop fully.|例句：The sun enripened the grapes.  阳光使葡萄成熟。

例句：Years of experience have enriched and ripened his judgment. 多年的经验使他的判断力更加成熟完善。


近义词：mature, mellow, perfect

反义词：underripe, immature

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun enripenes the fruit. = 阳光使水果成熟。
The wine enripenes in the cellar. = 这酒在地窖里陈酿成熟。


一般过去时:
The warm weather ripened the tomatoes. = 炎热的天气使西红柿成熟了。
The artist spent years ripening his skills. =这位艺术家花了数年时间磨练他的技艺。


现在完成时:
The fruit has ripened on the vine. = 水果已经在藤上成熟了。
His ideas have ripened over time. = 他的想法随着时间的推移而日趋成熟。


过去完成时:
The fruit had ripened before the harvest. = 水果在收割前就已经成熟了。
The plan had ripened in his mind for months. = 这个计划在他脑子里酝酿了几个月。


将来时:
The mangoes will ripen soon. = 芒果很快就会成熟。
His understanding will ripen with experience. = 他的理解会随着经验的积累而加深。


虚拟语气:
If the weather were warmer, the fruit would ripen faster. = 如果天气更暖和，水果就会成熟得更快。
I wish my plans had ripened sooner. = 我希望我的计划早点成熟。


被动语态:
The fruit is ripened by the sun. = 水果被阳光晒熟了。
The plan was ripened by careful consideration. = 这个计划经过仔细考虑才最终定案。


疑问句:
Has the fruit ripened yet? = 水果成熟了吗？
Will the plan ripen in time for the meeting? = 这个计划会在会议前成熟吗？


否定句:
The fruit didn't ripen properly. = 水果没有完全成熟。
His ideas haven't ripened enough yet. = 他的想法还不够成熟。
