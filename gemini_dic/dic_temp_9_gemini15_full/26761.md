caecostomy:/ˌsiːkəˈstɒmi/ | n. |盲肠造口术|The surgical creation of an artificial opening into the cecum (the first part of the large intestine).  This procedure is performed to relieve bowel obstruction, drain an abscess, or provide a means for fecal diversion.| Example: The patient underwent a caecostomy to relieve a severe bowel obstruction.  病人接受了盲肠造口术以缓解严重的肠梗阻。|近义词：cecostomy|反义词：N/A
