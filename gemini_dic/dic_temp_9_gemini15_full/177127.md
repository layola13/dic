reapproached:/ˌriːəˈproʊtʃt/| v.|重新接近；再次提出；重新探讨|To approach again; to bring up (a subject, etc.) again; to reconsider.|例句：After the initial rejection, he reapproached the committee with a revised proposal.  重新考虑后，他带着修改后的提案再次找到委员会。

例句：The lawyer reapproached the witness with a different line of questioning. 律师用不同的提问方式再次盘问了证人。


近义词：revisit, reconsider, approach again, readdress

反义词：avoid, ignore, neglect


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reapproaches the problem from a different angle. 他从不同的角度重新审视这个问题。
They reapproach the topic every meeting. 他们每次会议都会重新讨论这个话题。

一般过去时:
She reapproached her boss about a raise. 她再次向老板申请加薪。
He reapproached the task with renewed energy. 他以新的活力重新开始这项工作。

现在完成时:
They have reapproached the subject several times. 他们已经多次重新讨论了这个话题。
I have reapproached the lender for a loan extension. 我已经再次向贷款机构申请延长贷款期限。

过去完成时:
She had reapproached her ex-boyfriend before he moved away.  在他搬走之前，她已经再次和他联系过。
They had reapproached the negotiation several times before reaching an agreement.  在达成协议之前，他们已经多次重新谈判。

将来时:
He will reapproach the board with a new plan. 他将带着一个新的计划再次向董事会提出建议。
They will reapproach the issue after further research. 他们将在进一步研究后重新考虑这个问题。

虚拟语气:
If he reapproached the situation calmly, he might have found a solution. 如果他冷静地重新面对这种情况，他或许就能找到解决办法。
I wish she had reapproached me before making a decision. 我希望她在做决定之前能够再次和我联系。

被动语态:
The issue was reapproached by the committee. 这个问题被委员会重新审议。
The proposal was reapproached with a more positive attitude.  这个提案以更积极的态度再次被提出。

疑问句:
Will they reapproach the problem? 他们会重新考虑这个问题吗？

否定句:
He did not reapproach the seller after the first offer was rejected.  第一次报价被拒绝后，他没有再次联系卖家。
She will not reapproach her former colleague.  她不会再次联系她的前任同事。
