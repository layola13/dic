oneness:/ˈwʌn.nəs/ | n. |合一；统一；唯一性 | The state of being one; unity; a single entity.  It emphasizes the interconnectedness and interdependence of all things.  |例句：The experience of oneness with nature is profound.  体验与自然的合一是一种深刻的感受。

例句：The artist aimed to capture the oneness of humanity in his painting. 艺术家试图在他的画作中捕捉人类的统一性。


近义词：unity, wholeness, totality, singularity, integration

反义词：division, separation, fragmentation, duality, multiplicity

词性:名词
