rejecting:/rɪˈdʒektɪŋ/ | v. |拒绝；排斥；抵制 | The act of refusing to accept, consider, or allow something.  It implies a forceful dismissal or a strong negative response. |例句：He's rejecting all offers of help. 他拒绝了一切帮助。|例句：The body is rejecting the transplanted organ.  身体正在排斥移植的器官。|近义词：refusing, dismissing, spurning, repudiating |反义词：accepting, embracing, welcoming


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are rejecting the new policy. = 他们正在拒绝这项新政策。
She is rejecting his advances. = 她正在拒绝他的追求。

一般过去时:
He rejected my proposal. = 他拒绝了我的提议。
The company rejected her application. = 公司拒绝了她的申请。

现在完成时:
They have rejected several job offers. = 他们已经拒绝了好几个工作机会。
She has rejected all attempts at reconciliation. = 她拒绝了所有和解的尝试。

过去完成时:
I had rejected his offer before he changed his mind. = 他改变主意之前，我已经拒绝了他的提议。
She had rejected several suitors before she met him. = 在遇到他之前，她已经拒绝了好几个追求者。

将来时:
They will reject the bill. = 他们将否决该法案。
He will likely reject any compromise. = 他很可能会拒绝任何妥协。

虚拟语气:
If he rejected the offer, he would regret it. = 如果他拒绝了这个提议，他会后悔的。
I wish they hadn't rejected my idea. = 我真希望他们没有拒绝我的想法。

被动语态:
His application was rejected. = 他的申请被拒绝了。
The plan was rejected by the committee. = 该计划被委员会否决了。

疑问句:
Are they rejecting the changes? = 他们正在拒绝这些改变吗？
Will the court reject the case? = 法院会驳回此案吗？

否定句:
They are not rejecting the idea. = 他们并没有拒绝这个主意。
He did not reject her offer. = 他没有拒绝她的提议。
