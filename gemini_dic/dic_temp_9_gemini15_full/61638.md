eminence: /ˈɛmɪnəns/ | n. |卓越，显赫；高地；突出；(尊称)阁下 | high status and reputation; a position of prominence; a raised area of land |例句：His eminence as a scholar is undeniable.  他的学者声誉无可否认。

例句：The eminence of the hill offered a stunning view.  小山丘的高耸之处提供了令人惊叹的景色。


近义词：prominence, distinction, prestige, elevation, height

反义词：obscurity, insignificance, humility, depression, lowliness

词源：源自拉丁语eminentia，意为“突出，高地”，由eminere派生而来，意为“突出，高耸”。
