preobstruct: /ˌpriːəbˈstrʌkt/ | v. | 预先阻碍；事先阻挠 | To obstruct or hinder beforehand; to impede in advance.  | The new regulations preobstructed the company's expansion plans. 新规定预先阻碍了公司的扩张计划。|近义词：preclude, prevent, hinder, impede |反义词：facilitate, assist, promote |动词
