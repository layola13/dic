pompless:/ˈpɒmpləs/| adj.|不张扬的；不炫耀的；不起眼的；不浮夸的|Not ostentatious or showy; unpretentious.  The word implies a quiet dignity and lack of need for outward display.|例句：He led a pompless life, preferring quiet evenings at home to lavish parties. 他过着不张扬的生活，宁愿在家安静地度过夜晚也不参加奢华的派对。

例句：Her pompless demeanor belied her considerable achievements.  她不张扬的态度掩盖了她相当大的成就。


近义词：unassuming, modest, humble, simple, understated|反义词：ostentatious, showy, flamboyant, pretentious

词性:形容词
