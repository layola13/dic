pretransporting:/ˌpriːtrænˈspɔːrtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先运输；运输前的准备 | The act of transporting something before it is needed or used, often involving preparation and planning to ensure smooth delivery.  This includes activities such as packaging, labeling, and scheduling. | Example: Pretransporting the equipment ensured a smooth start to the experiment. 预先运输设备确保实验顺利开始。  |近义词:  preparing for transport, advance shipping, pre-shipment  | 反义词: post-transporting,  after-delivery | 动词
