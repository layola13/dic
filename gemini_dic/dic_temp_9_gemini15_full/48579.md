defeated:/dɪˈfiːtɪd/ | adj. |被打败的；失败的；沮丧的 | Defeated means having been overcome in a contest, battle, or struggle. It can also describe a feeling of discouragement or despair. |例句：The army was defeated after a long and bloody battle.  军队在经过一场漫长而血腥的战斗后被打败了。

The athlete felt defeated after losing the final race. 运动员在输掉决赛后感到沮丧。|近义词：beaten, conquered, overcome, discouraged, despondent |反义词：victorious, successful, triumphant, encouraged, hopeful |
