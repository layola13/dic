detractingly:/dɪˈtræktɪŋli/| adv.| 贬低地；减损地；不利地 |  In a way that diminishes or detracts from something. It implies a negative impact or lessening of value, quality, or importance. |例句：He spoke detractingly about his competitor's new product. 他贬低地谈论了他竞争对手的新产品。

例句：The constant interruptions detractingly affected the flow of the presentation.  不断的打断不利地影响了演示的流程。


近义词：disparagingly, negatively, unfavorably, downplaying | 反义词：favorably, positively, commendably


词性：副词
