psalmographist:/sælˈmɒɡrəfɪst/| n. |诗篇作者，圣诗作者|A psalmographist is a person who writes psalms or religious songs, particularly those of a lyrical or devotional nature.  They often focus on themes of praise, worship, lament, or supplication to a deity.  The term is less common in modern usage, with "poet," "hymn writer," or "songwriter" often preferred.|The ancient psalmographist penned verses of profound spiritual insight.|近义词：poet, hymn writer, songwriter, lyricist |反义词：N/A
