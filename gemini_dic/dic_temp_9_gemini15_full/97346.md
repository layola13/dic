infotainment:/ˈɪnfoʊteɪnmənt/ | n. |信息娱乐 |Information and entertainment combined, especially as provided by electronic media.  It blends factual information with entertainment value to engage the audience.  For example, a news program with humorous segments or an interactive game show with educational elements could be considered infotainment. |Example:  The news channel's new morning show is a blend of hard news and infotainment.  这个新闻频道的早间新闻节目融合了硬新闻和信息娱乐。|近义词：edutainment, entertainment news|反义词：pure news, serious documentary|名词
