over-impose:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈpoʊz/ | vt. |叠加；强加；附加|To place or lay something over something else; to impose something excessively or forcefully.|例句：The designer over-imposed a floral pattern onto the plain fabric. 设计师在素色布料上叠加了花卉图案。
The government over-imposed new regulations on businesses. 政府对企业强加了新的规章制度。|近义词：superimpose, overlay, enforce|反义词：remove, eliminate
