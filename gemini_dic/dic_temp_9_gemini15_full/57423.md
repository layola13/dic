drabble:/ˈdræbəl/| n.|百字小故事，短篇小说|A drabble is a very short story, exactly 100 words long.  It is a form of creative writing that challenges writers to be concise and impactful within a strict word limit. |Example:  I wrote a drabble about a lonely astronaut on Mars. 我写了一个关于在火星上孤独宇航员的百字小故事。|近义词：short story, vignette, flash fiction|反义词：novel, epic
