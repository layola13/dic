reexporting:/ˌriːˈɛkspɔːrtɪŋ/ | v. | 再出口，转口 | The act of importing goods and then exporting them to another country, often with minimal processing or value addition.  It involves the re-selling of goods that have already been imported. | Example:  The company specializes in reexporting electronics from Asia to Europe.  这家公司专门从事从亚洲到欧洲的电子产品再出口业务。|近义词：re-export, transshipment | 反义词：importing, domestic sales


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reexport textiles to South America. = 他们将纺织品再出口到南美洲。
The company reexports agricultural products. = 这家公司再出口农产品。


一般过去时:
We reexported the goods last week. = 我们上周再出口了这些商品。
China reexported a large quantity of steel. = 中国再出口了大量的钢铁。


现在完成时:
They have reexported several containers of goods this month. = 他们这个月已经再出口了几集装箱的货物。
The country has reexported significant amounts of manufactured goods. = 该国已经再出口了大量的制成品。


过去完成时:
They had reexported the goods before the new tariffs were imposed. = 在新的关税实施之前，他们已经再出口了这些商品。
The company had reexported all the products by the deadline. = 公司在截止日期前已将所有产品再出口。


将来时:
We will reexport the goods next month. = 我们下个月将再出口这些货物。
The company will reexport the products to a new market. = 公司将把产品再出口到一个新的市场。


虚拟语气:
If they had better infrastructure, they could reexport more efficiently. = 如果他们有更好的基础设施，他们就能更有效地再出口。
If we reexported these goods, we would make a significant profit. = 如果我们再出口这些商品，我们将获得可观的利润。


被动语态:
The goods are being reexported to several countries. = 这些货物正在被再出口到几个国家。
The surplus products were reexported to other markets. = 多余的产品被再出口到其他市场。


疑问句:
Are they reexporting the goods through a third country? = 他们是否通过第三国再出口货物？
Why are we reexporting these products? = 我们为什么要再出口这些产品？


否定句:
We did not reexport the damaged goods. = 我们没有再出口损坏的货物。
They will not be reexporting any products this quarter. = 他们本季度不会再出口任何产品。
