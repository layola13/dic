colonizers:/ˈkɑːlənaɪzərz/ | n. |殖民者，殖民主义者 | People who establish control over another territory, often exploiting its resources and people.  Colonizers often impose their culture and systems of governance on the colonized population. |Example: The colonizers exploited the land and its people for their own economic gain. 殖民者为了自身的经济利益剥削了这片土地及其人民。
