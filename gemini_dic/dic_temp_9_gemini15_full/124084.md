morro:/ˈmɔːroʊ/| n.|小山，小丘；高地 | A morro is a steep, rocky hill or small mountain, often with a flat top.  The term is particularly associated with Brazil and other parts of South America. | Example: The city is built on a series of morros overlooking the ocean.  这座城市建在俯瞰大海的一系列小山丘上。|近义词：hill, mound, knoll|反义词：plain, valley
