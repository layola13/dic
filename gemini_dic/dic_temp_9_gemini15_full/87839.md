hiddenly:/ˈhɪdnli/| adv.|秘密地，暗中地，偷偷地 |Secretly; in a way that is not easily noticed or known.  |例句：She hiddenly slipped a note into his pocket. 她偷偷地把一张纸条塞进了他的口袋。

例句：The enemy troops moved hiddenly through the forest. 敌军偷偷地穿过了森林。


近义词：secretly, stealthily, clandestinely, furtively

反义词：openly, publicly, overtly


词源：源自形容词hidden（隐藏的），加上副词后缀-ly构成。hidden本身来自古英语 *hydde，意思是“隐藏”。
