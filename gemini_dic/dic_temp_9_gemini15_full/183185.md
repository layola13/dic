reprehensible:/ˌrɛprɪˈhɛnsəbəl/ | adj. |应受谴责的；可耻的 | Deserving of condemnation or criticism; blameworthy.  |例句：His behavior was reprehensible and he was fired. 他的行为应受谴责，他被解雇了。|例句：The massacre was a reprehensible act of violence. 那次大屠杀是一起应受谴责的暴力行为。|近义词：blameworthy, culpable, shameful, scandalous |反义词：praiseworthy, commendable, laudable


USAGE->

His actions are reprehensible. = 他的行为应受谴责。
That behavior is reprehensible. = 那种行为是应受谴责的。
