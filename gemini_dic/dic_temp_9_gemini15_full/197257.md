sharked:/ʃɑːkt/| v.| 欺诈；敲诈；欺骗；骗取 | To cheat or swindle someone, often by using deceptive tactics.  It implies a more aggressive and premeditated act of deception than simply "cheating". |例句：He sharked his way to the top of the company. 他通过欺骗手段爬到了公司的高层。

例句：They sharked the elderly woman out of her life savings. 他们骗取了这位老妇人的毕生积蓄。


近义词： swindle, defraud, cheat, con, fleece


反义词：help, assist, benefit


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sharks investors out of their money. = 他骗取投资者的钱财。
She sharks her customers with misleading advertisements. = 她用误导性广告欺骗她的顾客。


一般过去时:
The con artist sharked the tourists out of their passports. = 骗子骗走了游客的护照。
He sharked his business partner out of a significant share of the profits. = 他骗取了他的商业伙伴很大一部分利润。


现在完成时:
They have sharked millions of dollars from unsuspecting victims. = 他们已经从毫无戒心的受害者那里骗取了数百万美元。
She has sharked her way to a position of power through deceit. = 她通过欺骗手段获得了权力地位。


过去完成时:
Before the scandal broke, he had sharked his clients for years. = 在丑闻曝光之前，他已经欺骗了他的客户多年。
They had sharked the company out of its valuable intellectual property.  = 他们已经骗取了公司的宝贵知识产权。


将来时:
He will shark more unsuspecting people if he's not caught. = 如果他不被抓住，他会欺骗更多毫无戒心的其他人。
She will shark her creditors out of their payments. = 她将骗取债权人的付款。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't sharked his investors, his company wouldn't have collapsed. = 如果他没有欺骗他的投资者，他的公司就不会倒闭。
I wish I hadn't been sharked out of my inheritance. = 我真希望我没有被骗走我的遗产。


被动语态:
Many people were sharked out of their savings by the fraudulent scheme. = 许多人在诈骗计划中被骗走了积蓄。
The investors were sharked by a sophisticated Ponzi scheme. = 投资者被一个复杂的庞氏骗局所欺骗。


疑问句:
Was he sharked out of his money? = 他的钱是被骗走的吗？
Were the customers sharked by the company's false advertising? = 顾客是被公司的虚假广告欺骗了吗？


否定句:
He didn't shark anyone; he earned his wealth honestly. = 他没有骗任何人；他的财富是诚实赚来的。
The company didn't shark its customers; it offered fair prices. = 公司没有欺骗它的顾客；它提供了公平的价格。

shark a living = 不择手段地谋生
