pretraining:/ˈpriːtreɪnɪŋ/| n. |预训练|The process of training a machine learning model on a large dataset before fine-tuning it for a specific task.  This initial training provides the model with a foundation of knowledge and generalizable features.|例句：Pretraining the model on a massive text corpus significantly improved its performance on downstream tasks.  预训练模型在一个海量文本语料库上显著提高了其在下游任务中的性能。|近义词：pre-training, initial training|反义词：finetuning
