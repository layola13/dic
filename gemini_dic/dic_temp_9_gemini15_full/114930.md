manageresses:/ˌmænɪˈdʒɛsɪz/| n.|女经理们|The plural form of manageress, referring to multiple female managers.  It is now largely considered archaic and sexist, replaced by the gender-neutral "managers".|例句：The manageresses of the department decided on a new marketing strategy. = 该部门的女经理们决定了一个新的营销策略。
The meeting was attended by several highly competent manageresses. =  几位非常能干的女经理参加了会议。|近义词：managers (gender-neutral), female executives, female supervisors|反义词：None directly, but the implied opposite is male managers or subordinate staff.
