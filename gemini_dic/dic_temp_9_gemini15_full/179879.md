reexpressing:/ˌriːɪkˈspresɪŋ/| v.|重新表达；再次表达|The act of expressing something again, often in a different way or with greater clarity or emphasis.  It involves conveying the same fundamental meaning but with altered wording, structure, or style.|例句：Reexpressing his ideas in simpler terms made the presentation more accessible.  重新用更简单的术语表达他的想法使演示更容易理解。

例句：The professor reexpressed her disappointment with the students' low scores. 教授再次表达了她对学生低分的失望。|近义词：rephrasing, restating, reformulating, paraphrasing|反义词：obscuring, concealing, misrepresenting
