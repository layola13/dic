overnighted:/ˌoʊvərˈnaɪtɪd/| adj. v. |隔夜的；通宵的；一夜之间寄送的 |Sent overnight, the package arrived this morning. |adj: overnight success,  v: overnight a package| 近义词: expedited, express  | 反义词: delayed, slow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We overnight packages to clients regularly. = 我们定期将包裹快递给客户。
They overnight their reports to the manager every week. = 他们每周都将报告通宵寄给经理。


一般过去时:
I overnighted the documents to the lawyer. = 我将文件快递给了律师。
She overnighted the application before the deadline. = 她在截止日期前快递了申请表。


现在完成时:
They have overnighted all the important documents. = 他们已经快递了所有重要的文件。
We have overnighted the samples for testing. = 我们已经快递了用于测试的样品。


过去完成时:
He had overnighted the parcel before leaving for the airport. = 他在去机场之前已经快递了包裹。
She had overnighted the files so they arrived in time for the meeting. = 她已经快递了文件，以便它们能够及时到达会议。


将来时:
I will overnight the contract to you. = 我会把合同快递给你。
They will overnight the goods to the warehouse. = 他们将把货物快递到仓库。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the deadline was so soon, I would have overnighted the report. = 如果我知道截止日期这么近，我就应该把报告快递过去。
It would have been better if we had overnighted the order. = 如果我们当时快递了订单就好了。


被动语态:
The package was overnighted to ensure timely delivery. = 包裹被快递以确保及时送达。
The documents were overnighted to the head office. = 文件被快递到总部。


疑问句:
Did you overnight the letter? = 你把信快递了吗？
Will they overnight the samples? = 他们会快递样品吗？


否定句:
I didn't overnight the package; I sent it by regular mail. = 我没有快递包裹；我是通过普通邮件发送的。
They didn't overnight the documents; they faxed them instead. = 他们没有快递文件；他们改用传真发送了。
