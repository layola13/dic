hurriedness:/ˈhɜːridnɪs/| n. |匆忙；仓促；急躁 | The state or quality of being hurried; haste; urgency.  It often implies a lack of calmness and deliberation. |例句：His hurriedness prevented him from completing the task properly. 他因为匆忙而没能正确完成任务。


例句：The hurriedness of her departure suggested she was in trouble. 她匆忙离开的样子暗示她遇到了麻烦。


近义词：haste, rush, urgency, speed, precipitancy


反义词：leisureliness, calmness, deliberation, slowness, patience
