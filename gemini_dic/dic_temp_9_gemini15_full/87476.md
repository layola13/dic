hetaera:/hɛˈtɛrə/| n. |高级妓女；交际花|A hetaera was a high-class courtesan in ancient Greece, often highly educated and cultured, unlike ordinary prostitutes.  They were companions, sometimes even intellectual equals, to wealthy and influential men. |Example: Aspasia, the renowned hetaera, was known for her wit and intelligence, and her influence on Pericles.  阿斯帕西娅，这位著名的交际花，以其才智和影响伯里克利的智谋而闻名。|近义词：courtesan, mistress, kept woman |反义词：virgin, nun
