man-of-his-word: /ˌmæn əv ɪz wɜːrd/ | n. |言而有信的人，说到做到的人 | A person who always keeps their promises and does what they say they will do.  | He's a man of his word; if he says he'll do it, he will. 他是个言而有信的人；如果他说他会做，他就会做。 |近义词：truthful person, reliable person, trustworthy person, person of integrity |反义词：liar, promise breaker, unreliable person


USAGE->

She is always a man of her word. = 她总是言而有信。
