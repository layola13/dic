relaid:/ˌriːˈleɪd/| v.|重新铺设；重新安置；转述|To lay something again; to place something in a new position; to repeat or transmit information.|例句：The workers relaid the railway tracks after the storm.暴风雨过后，工人们重新铺设了铁轨。

例句：She relaid the story to her friend, adding some details.她向朋友转述了这个故事，并添加了一些细节。

近义词：reinstall, relay, repeat, retell|反义词：remove, dismantle
