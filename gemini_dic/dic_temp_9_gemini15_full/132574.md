nondieters:/ˌnɑːnˈdaɪətərz/ | n. |节食者以外的人；不节食的人 | People who do not follow a diet.  The study focused on the eating habits of nondieters. 这项研究侧重于不节食者的饮食习惯。|近义词：non-dieters, those who don't diet |反义词：dieters

Nondieters often eat intuitively. = 不节食的人通常凭直觉吃饭。
They enjoy a variety of foods without restriction. = 他们享受各种各样的食物，没有限制。
