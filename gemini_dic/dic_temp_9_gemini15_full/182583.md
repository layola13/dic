reoccur:/ˌriːəˈkɜːr/| /ˌriːoʊˈkɜːr/| v.|再次发生，反复出现|To happen or occur again.  The word implies a previous occurrence. |例句：The symptoms of the illness reoccurred after a few weeks.  疾病的症状几周后又复发了。|例句：The same problem keeps reoccurring in our team meetings.  同样的问题一直在我们的团队会议上反复出现。|近义词：recur, repeat, happen again|反义词：cease, end, disappear


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The problem reoccurs frequently. = 这个问题经常反复出现。
His headaches reoccur every Monday. = 他每个星期一都会头痛。

一般过去时:
The pain reoccurred after the surgery. = 手术后疼痛又复发了。
The error reoccurred in the latest version. = 该错误在最新版本中再次出现。

现在完成时:
The issue has reoccurred several times this year. = 今年这个问题已经反复出现好几次了。
The symptoms have reoccurred since the last treatment. = 自上次治疗以来，症状又复发了。

过去完成时:
The problem had reoccurred before they found a solution. = 在他们找到解决方案之前，问题已经反复出现过了。
She realized the mistake had reoccurred after reviewing the data. = 她复查数据后才意识到错误又犯了。

将来时:
The doctor fears the infection will reoccur. = 医生担心感染会再次发生。
I'm worried the fault might reoccur. = 我担心错误可能会再次发生。

虚拟语气:
If the symptoms reoccurred, I would seek immediate medical attention. = 如果症状再次出现，我会立即就医。
I wish the problem wouldn't reoccur. = 我希望这个问题不会再次发生。

被动语态:
The error was reported to have reoccurred in the system. = 系统中据报道该错误再次出现。
It is feared that the disease might reoccur. = 人们担心这种疾病可能会再次发生。


疑问句:
Will the problem reoccur? = 这个问题还会再次发生吗？
Does the pain ever reoccur? = 疼痛还会再次出现吗？

否定句:
The problem didn't reoccur after the fix. = 问题在修复后没有再次发生。
The glitch hasn't reoccurred since the software update. = 自软件更新以来，故障没有再次发生。


PHRASE->
reoccur frequently = 频繁复发
reoccur unexpectedly = 出乎意料地再次发生
reoccur periodically = 定期复发
reoccur with regularity = 有规律地反复出现
reoccur at intervals = 间隔地再次发生
