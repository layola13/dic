defoliation:/ˌdiːˌfoʊliˈeɪʃən/| n. |落叶，脱叶，除叶|The removal of leaves from trees or plants, either naturally or artificially.  Defoliation can be caused by disease, insects, herbicides, or natural processes. |例句：The herbicide caused significant defoliation in the affected area. 除草剂导致受影响区域出现严重的落叶现象。

例句：The autumn defoliation transformed the landscape into a breathtaking display of color. 秋天的落叶使风景变成了令人叹为观止的色彩展示。


近义词：leaf fall, leaf drop, deleafing|反义词：leaf growth, afforestation
