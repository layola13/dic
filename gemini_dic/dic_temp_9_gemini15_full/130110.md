non-purchase: /ˌnɒnˈpɜːrtʃəs/ | n. | 未购买；未购商品 | The act or instance of not buying something; something that was not bought.  It refers to the absence of a purchase or a transaction where a purchase was expected or considered. | Example: The high price led to a high rate of non-purchase. 高价导致了高比例的未购买。 The non-purchase of the software delayed the project. 未购买该软件延误了项目。 | 近义词: abstention from purchase, refusal to buy, failure to buy | 反义词: purchase, buying, acquisition
