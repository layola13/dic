felicify:/fəˈlɪsɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 使幸福；使快乐 | To make happy; to bring happiness to.  The word is relatively rare and often used in a playful or literary context. | 例句：He tried to felicify his grieving friend with a thoughtful gift. 他试图通过一份体贴的礼物来使他悲伤的朋友快乐。

例句：The comedian's jokes felicified the audience.  喜剧演员的笑话使观众们快乐。


近义词：gladden, elate, delight, exhilarate, cheer | 反义词：sadden, depress, dishearten, dismay


词源:  From "felic" (meaning happy, from Latin *felix, *felic-),  + "-ify" (a suffix meaning "to make").  The word is a relatively modern coinage, not found in older dictionaries.
