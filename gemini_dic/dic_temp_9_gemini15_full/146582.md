overgraze:/ˈoʊvərɡreɪz/ | vt. |过度放牧 | To graze (land) to the extent that the vegetation is damaged and the land becomes less productive.  Overgrazing can lead to soil erosion, desertification, and a loss of biodiversity. | 例句：The sheep overgrazed the pasture, leaving it barren and unproductive.  绵羊过度放牧了牧场，使其变得贫瘠且不产出。

The cattle overgrazed the hillside, resulting in significant soil erosion. 牛群过度放牧山坡，导致严重的土壤侵蚀。


近义词：overpasture, deplete, exhaust


反义词：undergraze, preserve, conserve


词性:动词
