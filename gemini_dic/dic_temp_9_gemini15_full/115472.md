manufacturing: /ˌmænjuˈfæktʃərɪŋ/ | n. | 制造业；制造；生产 | The process of converting raw materials into finished goods through the use of machinery, tools, and human labor.  It encompasses all activities involved in creating products, from design and development to production and distribution. | 例句：The company's manufacturing process is highly automated. 这家公司的制造流程高度自动化。

例句：  China is a global leader in manufacturing. 中国是全球制造业的领导者。


近义词：production, fabrication,  manufacture (verb) | 反义词：consumption, destruction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory manufactures cars. = 工厂生产汽车。
They manufacture clothing. = 他们生产服装。


一般过去时:
The company manufactured its first product last year. = 该公司去年生产了它的第一个产品。
Ford manufactured the Model T. = 福特制造了T型车。


现在完成时:
We have manufactured over 100,000 units this year. = 今年我们已经生产了超过10万件产品。
They have manufactured many different types of products. = 他们已经生产了许多不同类型的产品。


过去完成时:
They had manufactured the parts before the assembly began. = 他们在组装开始之前已经制造好了零件。
The factory had manufactured millions of toys by the end of the year. = 到年底，工厂已经生产了数百万个玩具。


将来时:
We will manufacture a new model next year. = 我们明年将生产一个新车型。
The plant will manufacture more efficient solar panels. = 该工厂将生产更高效的太阳能电池板。


虚拟语气:
If they had better equipment, they would manufacture more products. = 如果他们有更好的设备，他们就能生产更多产品。
I wish we could manufacture our products locally. = 我希望我们能够在当地生产我们的产品。


被动语态:
The cars are manufactured in Japan. = 这些汽车是在日本制造的。
The toys are being manufactured in China. = 这些玩具正在中国制造。


疑问句:
Where are these products manufactured? = 这些产品在哪里制造？
What is being manufactured in that factory? = 那家工厂在生产什么？


否定句:
They do not manufacture electronics. = 他们不生产电子产品。
The factory was not manufacturing anything at the time. = 工厂当时什么也没有生产。
