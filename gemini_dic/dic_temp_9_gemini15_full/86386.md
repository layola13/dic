hedgehog:/ˈhɛdʒhɒɡ/ /ˈhɛdʒhɔːɡ/| n. |刺猬 |A small mammal covered in spines.  Hedgehogs are nocturnal and omnivorous, feeding on insects, worms, and other small creatures. They are known for their ability to curl into a ball when threatened. |例句：The hedgehog curled into a tight ball when the dog approached.  这只刺猬在狗靠近时蜷成一团。

例句：We saw a hedgehog crossing the road last night.  昨晚我们看到一只刺猬过马路。


近义词：porcupine (if referring to the spines) |反义词：None  |词性：名词
