liberalization:/ˌlɪbərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 自由化；放宽管制 | The process of making something more liberal; the removal of restrictions or controls.  It often refers to economic policies, but can also apply to social or political systems.  | Example: The liberalization of the telecommunications industry led to increased competition and lower prices.  自由化电信行业导致竞争加剧和价格下降。  |近义词： deregulation, reform, opening up | 反义词： restriction, control, regulation | 名词
