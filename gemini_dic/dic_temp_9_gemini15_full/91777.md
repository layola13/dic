hyperenthusiastically:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪkli/| adv. |极其热情地，过分热情地 |Extremely enthusiastically; excessively so.  It suggests an enthusiasm that might be considered over-the-top or even excessive. |例句：He hyperenthusiastically embraced the new project, working day and night to complete it. 他极其热情地投入新项目，夜以继日地工作以完成它。


例句：She hyperenthusiastically described her trip to Paris, making everyone around her feel somewhat overwhelmed. 她极其热情地描述了她去巴黎的旅行，让周围的人都感到有点不知所措。


近义词：passionately, fervently, zealously, excitedly


反义词：apathetically, indifferently, unenthusiastically, apathetically


词性:副词
