monetarist: /ˈmɒnɪtərɪst/| /ˈmɑːnɪtərɪst/ | n. |货币主义者|A monetarist is an economist who believes that the money supply is the primary determinant of economic activity and inflation.  Monetarists advocate for controlling inflation by managing the money supply. |The monetarist school of thought emphasizes the importance of controlling the money supply to stabilize the economy. |近义词：monetary theorist, quantity theorist |反义词：Keynesian


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The monetarist believes that inflation is primarily caused by an excessive money supply. = 货币主义者认为通货膨胀主要是由过多的货币供应引起的。
Monetarists often advocate for independent central banks. = 货币主义者经常主张独立的中央银行。


一般过去时:
Milton Friedman was a prominent monetarist economist. = 米尔顿·弗里德曼是一位著名的货币主义经济学家。
Many economists rejected monetarist ideas during the 1970s. = 许多经济学家在20世纪70年代拒绝了货币主义的思想。


现在完成时:
Monetarists have long argued for a stable monetary policy. = 货币主义者长期以来一直主张实施稳定的货币政策。
Recent economic data have supported some monetarist predictions. = 最近的经济数据支持了一些货币主义者的预测。


过去完成时:
Before the 1970s, Keynesian economics had dominated over monetarist thought. = 在20世纪70年代之前，凯恩斯主义经济学占据主导地位，超过了货币主义思想。
Economists had already begun to question the effectiveness of purely Keynesian policies before the monetarist resurgence. = 在货币主义复兴之前，经济学家就已经开始质疑纯粹凯恩斯主义政策的有效性。


将来时:
Central banks will likely continue to consider monetarist principles in their policy decisions. = 中央银行可能会继续在其政策决策中考虑货币主义原则。
Monetarists predict that increased money supply will lead to higher inflation. = 货币主义者预测，货币供应增加会导致通货膨胀上升。


虚拟语气:
If the government had followed monetarist advice, inflation might have been lower. = 如果政府听取了货币主义者的建议，通货膨胀可能更低。
It is desirable that central banks would pay more attention to the monetarist perspective. = 中央银行更关注货币主义者的观点是可取的。


被动语态:
Monetarist theories were initially met with skepticism. = 货币主义理论最初受到怀疑。
The money supply is often manipulated by central banks, according to monetarist principles. = 根据货币主义原则，货币供应经常由中央银行操纵。


疑问句:
Are all economists monetarists? = 所有经济学家都是货币主义者吗？
What are the main tenets of monetarist thought? = 货币主义思想的主要信条是什么？


否定句:
Not all economists agree with monetarist views on inflation. = 并非所有经济学家都同意货币主义者关于通货膨胀的观点。
The government did not heed the monetarist warnings about excessive spending. = 政府没有理会货币主义者关于过度支出的警告。
