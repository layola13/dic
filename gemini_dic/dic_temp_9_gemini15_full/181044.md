reinclines:/ˌriːɪnˈklaɪnz/ | v. |重新倾斜；再次倾向；重新考虑 | To incline again; to return to a previous inclination or opinion.  |例句：After considering the new evidence, the judge reinclined towards the defendant's version of events.  法官在考虑了新的证据后，再次倾向于被告的陈述。

例句：He initially opposed the plan, but after further discussion, he reinclined toward supporting it. 他最初反对这个计划，但在进一步讨论后，他又倾向于支持它。


近义词：reconsider, re-evaluate, revert (to a previous position)

反义词：reject, abandon, oppose


词性:动词
