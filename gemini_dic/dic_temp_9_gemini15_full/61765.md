empathize:/ˈɛmpəθaɪz/ | vt. | 同情；感同身受 | To understand and share the feelings of another.  | 例句：I empathize with her grief after losing her pet. 我理解她失去宠物后的悲伤。 | 例句：It's important to empathize with people from different backgrounds.  理解来自不同背景的人很重要。| 近义词：sympathize, understand, relate to | 反义词：be indifferent to, be unsympathetic to | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I empathize with his situation. = 我同情他的处境。
She empathizes with the characters in the book. = 她对书中的人物感同身受。

一般过去时:
I empathized with her when she told me about her loss. = 当她告诉我她的损失时，我感同身受。
He empathized with the homeless man's plight. = 他同情那个无家可归的人的困境。

现在完成时:
I have empathized with many people throughout my life. = 我一生中同情过很多人。
She has empathized with the struggles of single mothers. = 她对单亲母亲的困境感同身受。

过去完成时:
I had empathized with him before, so I understood his reaction. = 我以前就同情过他，所以我理解他的反应。
She had empathized with similar situations in the past. = 她过去曾对类似的情况感同身受。

将来时:
I will empathize with you if you ever find yourself in a difficult situation. = 如果你遇到困难，我会同情你。
She will empathize with the victims of the disaster. = 她将会同情灾难的受害者。

虚拟语气:
If I were in her shoes, I would empathize with her more. = 如果我是她，我会更能理解她。
I wish I could empathize with him better. = 我希望我能更好地理解他。

被动语态:
His pain was empathized with by everyone present. = 在场的人都同情他的痛苦。(不太常用，主动语态更常见)
Her struggles were empathized with by her friends and family. = 她的朋友和家人同情她的挣扎。(不太常用，主动语态更常见)

疑问句:
Do you empathize with the characters in the story? = 你同情故事中的人物吗？
Can you empathize with someone who has never experienced what you have? = 你能同情从未经历过你所经历过的事情的人吗？

否定句:
I cannot empathize with his actions. = 我无法同情他的行为。
She didn't empathize with the villain's motivations. = 她不同情反派的动机。


PHRASE->
empathize with = 同情…
fully empathize = 完全理解
deeply empathize = 深切同情
try to empathize = 试图设身处地为他人着想
fail to empathize = 无法感同身受
