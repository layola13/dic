duchy:/ˈdʌtʃi/| n. |公国；公爵领地|A duchy is a territory ruled by a duke or duchess.  It is a type of feudal state, historically often associated with significant land ownership and political power.  The term can also refer to the rank or dignity of a duke or duchess.|例句：The Duchy of Lancaster is a significant part of the British monarchy.  兰开斯特公国是英国君主制的重要组成部分。

例句：The small duchy was known for its rich history and unique culture. 这个小公国以其丰富的历史和独特的文化而闻名。


近义词：principality, county (in some contexts)


反义词：empire (in terms of scale), republic (in terms of government)


词性:名词
