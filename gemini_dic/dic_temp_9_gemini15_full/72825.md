foliate:/ˈfoʊliˌeɪt/| adj., v.|叶状的；长叶子的；使成叶状|Having leaves; consisting of or resembling leaves.  To develop leaves; to become leafy.|The tree began to foliate in the spring. 春天树木开始长叶。|近义词：leafy, frondose|反义词：leafless, barren

The plant foliates quickly in warm weather. =  这种植物在温暖的天气里生长迅速。
