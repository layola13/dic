retype:/riːˈtaɪp/ | vt. |重新打字，重新输入 |To type something again.  This often implies correcting errors or improving the original typing.| 例句：I had to retype the entire document because of a formatting error. = 因为格式错误，我不得不重新输入整个文档。

例句：She asked me to retype the letter, correcting the spelling mistakes. = 她让我重新打这封信，改正拼写错误。


近义词：re-enter, rewrite, re-key

词性:动词
