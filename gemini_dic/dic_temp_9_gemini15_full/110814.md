linnaeans:/lɪˈneɪən/| adj.|林奈的；林奈式分类法的|Relating to or characteristic of the system of biological classification devised by Carl Linnaeus.  The Linnaean system uses binomial nomenclature (two-part names, genus and species) to classify organisms.  |Example:  The Linnaean classification of plants is still widely used today.  林奈植物分类系统至今仍被广泛使用。 The student presented a Linnaean analysis of the collected insects. 学生展示了对收集昆虫的林奈式分析。|近义词：taxonomic (in the context of classification)|反义词：无|
