counterquestion:/ˈkaʊntərkwestʃən/| n. |反问，反诘|A question asked in reply to another question, often to challenge or refute the original question.  It's a question used to answer a question, often implying disagreement or questioning the premise of the original question.|例句：When asked why he was late, he responded with a counterquestion: "And what time did you arrive?" 当被问到为什么迟到时，他反问道：“那你几点到的？”|近义词：rebuttal, rejoinder, retort|反义词：answer, response
