hunts:/hʌnts/| v.|狩猎；搜寻；追捕|To search for and kill (wild animals) for food or sport; to search actively for something.|例句：The hunters spent the whole day hunting deer. 猎人花了整天的时间狩猎鹿。

例句：The police are hunting for the escaped criminal. 警方正在追捕逃犯。|近义词：chase, pursue, seek, track|反义词：be hunted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hunts deer every autumn. 他每年秋天都狩猎鹿。
She hunts for bargains in the market. 她在市场上寻找便宜货。

一般过去时:
They hunted rabbits in the woods yesterday. 他们昨天在树林里打兔子。
He hunted for his keys for an hour. 他找钥匙找了一个小时。

现在完成时:
I have hunted in this forest many times. 我在这个森林里打猎很多次了。
She has hunted for a new job since she graduated. 她毕业后一直在找新工作。

过去完成时:
They had hunted all day before they found a deer. 他们在找到一只鹿之前已经打了一整天猎了。
He had hunted for the lost document for weeks before he finally found it. 他找了几个星期丢失的文件才最终找到。

将来时:
We will hunt foxes next weekend. 我们下周末将去猎狐。
She will hunt for a better apartment next month. 她下个月将寻找更好的公寓。

虚拟语气:
If I hunted more often, I would be fitter. 如果我更经常打猎，我会更健康。
I wish I had hunted that opportunity. 我真希望我抓住了那个机会。

被动语态:
The deer were hunted by the hunters. 鹿被猎人猎杀了。
The escaped criminal is being hunted by the police. 逃犯正在被警方追捕。

疑问句:
Do you hunt? 你打猎吗？
What are you hunting for? 你在找什么？

否定句:
He doesn't hunt anymore. 他不再打猎了。
She didn't hunt for a job last year. 她去年没有找工作。
