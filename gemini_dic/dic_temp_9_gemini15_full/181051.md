reinclusion:/ˌriːɪnˈkluːʒən/| n. |重新纳入；重新接纳；重新包含|The act or process of including someone or something again; the state of being included again.  It often implies a previous exclusion or separation.|例句：The reinclusion of the expelled students was met with mixed reactions.  重新接纳被开除的学生引起了褒贬不一的反应。

例句：After a period of isolation, her reinclusion into the team boosted morale.  在她一段时间的隔离后，重新回到团队提升了士气。


近义词：reintegration, readmission, restoration, resumption|反义词：exclusion, expulsion, omission|词性：名词
