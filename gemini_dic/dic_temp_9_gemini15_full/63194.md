entanglingly:/ɪnˈtæŋɡlɪŋli/| adv.|纠缠地，复杂地|In a way that entangles or involves complexities.|The wires were entanglingly intertwined, making it difficult to untangle them.|近义词：complexly, intricately, confusingly|反义词：simply, clearly, straightforwardly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and as such doesn't have tense conjugations like verbs.  It describes the manner in which something happens.


例句：

The situation developed entanglingly, with multiple parties having conflicting interests.  = 情况发展得错综复杂，多方利益冲突。

He explained the rules in an entanglingly convoluted manner. = 他以一种令人费解的复杂方式解释了规则。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "entanglingly."  Its usage is primarily straightforward as a descriptive adverb.
