seismologically:/ˌsaɪzˈmɒlədʒɪkli/ | adv. | 地震学地；从地震学的角度 | Relating to or in accordance with the principles of seismology.  The study of earthquakes and seismic waves.  | 例句：The earthquake was analyzed seismologically to determine its magnitude and origin.  地震被地震学地分析以确定其震级和起源。

The area was seismologically active, experiencing numerous tremors. 该地区地震活动频繁，多次发生震颤。


近义词：seismically (though less encompassing) | 反义词：N/A  | 词性: 副词
