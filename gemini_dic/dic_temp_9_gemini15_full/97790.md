injunctions:/ɪnˈdʒʌŋkʃənz/ | n. | 法令；命令；禁止令 | A court order requiring a person to do or not do a particular action.  Injunctions are often used to prevent harm or to maintain the status quo pending further legal action. | Example: The court issued an injunction prohibiting the company from polluting the river. 法院发布禁令，禁止该公司污染河流。  The judge granted a temporary injunction to stop the demolition of the building.  法官批准了一项临时禁令，以阻止建筑物的拆除。 |近义词：order, command, mandate, prohibition | 反义词：permission, authorization

The court issues injunctions to protect property rights. = 法院发布禁令以保护财产权。
