nonending: /ˈnɒnˈɛndɪŋ/ | adj. | 无尽的；持续不断的 | unending means having or seeming to have no end. It describes something that continues without stopping or interruption.  | Example: The unending cycle of birth, death, and rebirth fascinated her.  无尽的生死轮回让她着迷。  The unending stream of tourists overwhelmed the small town.  络绎不绝的游客淹没了这个小镇。 | 近义词：infinite, ceaseless, continuous, perpetual, interminable | 反义词：ending, finite, limited, brief
