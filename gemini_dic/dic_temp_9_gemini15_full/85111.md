hardener:/ˈhɑːrdənər/| n. |硬化剂，固化剂 |A substance that increases the hardness or firmness of a material.  Hardeners are commonly used in various industries to improve the properties of materials like resins, plastics, and adhesives.|例句：The epoxy resin requires a hardener to cure properly.  环氧树脂需要硬化剂才能正确固化。

The addition of a hardener significantly improved the strength of the concrete. 添加硬化剂显著提高了混凝土的强度。|近义词： curing agent, setting agent |反义词：softener
