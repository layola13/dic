non-redeemable: /ˌnɒn rɪˈdiːməbl/ | adj. | 不可赎回的，无法兑换的 |  Not able to be exchanged for something else, especially for cash.  This term often refers to securities or other financial instruments that cannot be converted into cash or other assets. | 例句：The non-redeemable bonds offered a higher interest rate than redeemable ones.  这笔不可赎回债券的利率比可赎回债券高。

The gift certificate was non-redeemable after the expiration date.  礼券过期后就不能兑换了。


近义词：irredeemable, unredeemable, non-refundable


反义词：redeemable, refundable


词性：形容词
