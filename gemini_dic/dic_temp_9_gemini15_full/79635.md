glossic:/ˈɡlɒsɪk/ | adj. |光泽的；有光泽的 | relating to or resembling gloss; having a smooth, shiny surface.  | The glossic finish on the car made it look brand new. 这辆车的光泽饰面使它看起来焕然一新。|近义词：shiny, lustrous, polished |反义词：dull, matte, rough
