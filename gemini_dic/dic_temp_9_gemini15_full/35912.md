cocreated:/ˌkoʊkrɪˈeɪtɪd/ | v. | 共同创造，协同创作 |  Past participle of "cocreate," meaning to create something jointly or collaboratively.  It emphasizes the shared effort and contribution of multiple individuals or entities in the creative process. |例句：The mural was co-created by students from different schools. 这幅壁画是由不同学校的学生共同创作的。

The new product was co-created by the design team and the marketing department. 新产品是由设计团队和市场部共同创作的。|近义词：collaborated, jointly created, developed together |反义词：created independently, solely produced |动词
