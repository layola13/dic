glass-blowing:/ˈɡlæsbˌloʊɪŋ/| n. |玻璃吹制|The art or process of shaping molten glass by blowing air into it through a blowpipe.|例句：He is a master of glass-blowing, creating intricate and beautiful pieces. 他是一位玻璃吹制的宗师，创作出精美复杂的艺术品。
例句：The workshop offered a class in glass-blowing, teaching participants basic techniques. 工作室开设了玻璃吹制课程，教授学员基本技巧。|近义词：glassforming|反义词:无
