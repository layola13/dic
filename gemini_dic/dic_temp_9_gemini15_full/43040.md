cowherd:/ˈkaʊhɜːrd/ | n. |牧牛人 |A cowherd is a person who herds cows.  They are responsible for guiding and protecting a group of cows, often in a pasture or field.  |例句：The cowherd skillfully guided the herd across the river. 牧牛人熟练地引导牛群过河。  The old cowherd shared stories of his life with the children.  老牧牛人与孩子们分享了他的人生故事。|近义词：herdsman, cattleman |反义词：N/A
