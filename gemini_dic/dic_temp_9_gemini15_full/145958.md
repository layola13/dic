词源：

* **over-**:  prefix indicating excess or to a great degree. (表示过度或极度)
* **distended**: past participle of the verb "distend," meaning to swell or expand abnormally. (动词“distend”的过去分词，意思是异常肿胀或扩张)
* **distend**: from Latin *distendere* "to stretch out, extend," from *dis-* "apart" + *tendere* "to stretch." (源于拉丁语 *distendere* "拉伸，延伸"，来自 *dis-* "分开" + *tendere* "拉伸")
