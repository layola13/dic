remove:/rɪˈmuːv/|/ˈriːmuːv/| vt.|去除，移动，免职|To take away or get rid of something or someone; to move something from one place to another; to dismiss someone from a job or position.|例句：Please remove your shoes before entering.  请进门前脱鞋。|例句：The surgeon removed the tumor successfully. 外科医生成功地切除了肿瘤。|近义词：eliminate, delete, take away, displace|反义词：add, install, appoint, retain|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They remove the trash every day. = 他们每天都清理垃圾。
He removes his hat when he enters a building. = 他进屋时会脱帽。


一般过去时:
She removed the stain from the carpet. = 她去除了地毯上的污渍。
The teacher removed the student from the classroom. = 老师把学生从教室里带了出去。


现在完成时:
They have removed all the obstacles. = 他们已经移除了所有障碍。
She has removed her makeup. = 她已经卸妆了。


过去完成时:
He had removed the files before the inspection. = 他在检查前已经移除了文件。
They had removed the furniture before painting the walls. = 他们粉刷墙壁之前已经移走了家具。


将来时:
We will remove the snow from the driveway tomorrow. = 我们明天将铲除车道上的积雪。
They will remove the old equipment next week. = 他们下周将移除旧设备。


虚拟语气:
If I removed this obstacle, the project would be completed. = 如果我移除了这个障碍，项目就能完成了。
I wish I had removed that photo before posting it. = 我真希望在发帖之前就把那张照片删掉了。


被动语态:
The furniture was removed by the movers. = 家具是由搬家公司搬走的。
The problem will be removed soon. = 这个问题很快就会解决。


疑问句:
Can you remove this splinter? = 你能帮我取出这根刺吗？
Will they remove the damaged tree? = 他们会移除那棵受损的树吗？


否定句:
He couldn't remove the stubborn stain. = 他无法去除那顽固的污渍。
She didn't remove her shoes. = 她没有脱鞋。



PHRASE->
remove oneself = 离开
remove doubt = 消除疑虑
remove a threat = 消除威胁
remove from office = 免职
remove the appendix = 切除阑尾
