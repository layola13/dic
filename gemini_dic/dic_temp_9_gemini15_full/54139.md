disenfranchising: /ˌdɪsɪnˈfrænˌtʃaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |剥夺…的权利；使失去公民权；使丧失特权 | The act of depriving someone of a right or privilege, especially the right to vote.  |例句：The new law is disenfranchising many low-income voters. 这项新法律剥夺了许多低收入选民的投票权。

例句： Systemic racism has historically disenfranchised Black communities.  系统性种族主义历来剥夺了黑人社区的权利。


近义词：disabling, depriving, dispossessing

反义词：empowering, enfranchising

词性：动词
