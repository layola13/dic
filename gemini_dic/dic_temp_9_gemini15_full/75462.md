frump:/frʌmp/| n. adj.|邋遢的女人；乏味的人；邋遢的；乏味的|A frump is a woman who is considered unattractive because of her dowdy clothes and untidy appearance.  It can also be used to describe something dull or boring. |例句：She used to be a frump, but now she's stylish and confident. 她过去是个邋遢的女人，但现在她时尚而自信。  The presentation was a complete frump; it was boring and lacked any energy.  这次演示很乏味；它很无聊，缺乏活力。|近义词：dowdy, drab, frumpy, slob, bore|反义词：stylish, chic, glamorous, exciting
