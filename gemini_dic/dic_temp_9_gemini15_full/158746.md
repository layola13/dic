plowfoot:/ˈplaʊfʊt/| n. |犁铧；犁底；笨拙的人|A plowfoot is the bottom part of a plow, the metal part that cuts into the soil. It can also be used figuratively to describe someone clumsy or awkward.  |例句：The plowfoot was worn down after years of use.  犁铧经过多年的使用已经磨损了。

The plowfoot hit a rock, causing the plow to break. 犁底撞到一块石头，导致犁坏了。


近义词：plowshare (犁铧), share (犁铧的一部分) |反义词：N/A |词性：名词
