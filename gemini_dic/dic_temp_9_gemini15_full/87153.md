herbivora:/ˌhɜːrˈbɪvərə/| n. |食草动物|Animals that feed on plants. Herbivores play a crucial role in the ecosystem by controlling plant populations and transferring energy from plants to other trophic levels.  |例句：Cows are herbivores.  奶牛是食草动物。 |例句：Deer, rabbits, and sheep are all examples of herbivores. 鹿、兔子和绵羊都是食草动物的例子。|近义词：plant-eater, grazer|反义词：carnivore, omnivore|名词
