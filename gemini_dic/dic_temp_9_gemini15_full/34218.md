clangour:/ˈklæŋɡər/ | n. |喧嚣，嘈杂声 | A loud, resonant, and confused noise; a clamor.  |例句：The clangour of the city traffic was deafening. 城市交通的喧嚣声震耳欲聋。|例句：The clangour of the blacksmith's hammer echoed through the valley. 铁匠的锤声在山谷回荡。|近义词：clamor, din, uproar, racket |反义词：silence, quiet, hush |名词
