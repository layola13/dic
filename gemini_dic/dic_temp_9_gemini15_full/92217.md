hypertext:/ˈhaɪpərtɛkst/| n. |超文本|Hypertext is a system of computer-linked text that allows users to navigate through a network of information by clicking on highlighted words or phrases, which then lead to related information.|Example: The World Wide Web is a prime example of hypertext, enabling users to jump between different web pages.|近义词：interlinked text, networked text|反义词：linear text
