scab-like:/ˈskæblaɪk/| adj.|像痂的，痂状的|Resembling a scab in appearance or texture.  The term describes something that is dry, crusted, and often brownish in color, similar to the appearance of a wound scab.|例句：The burn left a scab-like lesion on his arm.  烧伤在他手臂上留下了一个像痂一样的病灶。The dried mud formed a scab-like crust on the pot. 干涸的泥土在锅上形成了像痂一样的硬壳。|近义词：crusty, scabby, encrusted|反义词：smooth, soft, supple
