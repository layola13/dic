mining:/ˈmaɪnɪŋ/| n. v.| 采矿；挖掘；开采 | The extraction of valuable minerals or other geological materials from the earth, usually from an ore body, lode, vein, seam, reef or placer deposit.  It involves the breaking of rocks, and often the processing of extracted materials. | 例句：Coal mining is a dangerous job.  煤矿开采是一项危险的工作。

例句：The company is investing heavily in gold mining. 这家公司正在大力投资黄金开采。


近义词：excavation, extraction, quarrying


反义词：refilling, restoration


词性：名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mine coal in this region. = 他们在该地区采煤。
The company mines gold in South Africa. = 这家公司在南非开采黄金。


一般过去时:
They mined coal for centuries. = 他们采煤已有几个世纪了。
Miners mined gold in the Klondike. = 淘金者在克朗代克地区采金。


现在完成时:
They have mined all the coal in this area. = 他们已经采完了这个地区的煤。
The company has mined millions of tons of ore. = 这家公司已经开采了数百万吨矿石。


过去完成时:

The miners had mined for years before striking gold. = 矿工们在发现黄金前已经采矿多年了。


将来时:
They will mine more coal next year. = 他们明年将开采更多煤炭。

虚拟语气:
