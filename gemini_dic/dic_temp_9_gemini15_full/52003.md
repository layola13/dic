diagonal:/daɪˈæɡənəl/| adj., n.|对角线的，斜线的；对角线|A diagonal line, or a line running diagonally across a shape or surface, connects two opposite corners.  It can also refer to something arranged or running diagonally.|例句：The diagonal across the square is longer than its sides.  正方形的对角线比它的边长。

The building has a striking diagonal design. 这栋建筑物有一个引人注目的斜线设计。|近义词：slanting, oblique|反义词：straight, perpendicular
