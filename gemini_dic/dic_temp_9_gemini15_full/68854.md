fashionista:/ˌfæʃənˈɪstə/ | n. |时尚达人，时尚人士 | A person who is very interested in fashion and who follows the latest fashion trends.  They are often considered to have a strong sense of style and are influential in the fashion world. | Example: She's a real fashionista; her outfits are always impeccably stylish. 她是一位真正的时尚达人；她的服装总是无可挑剔地时尚。 |近义词：fashion icon, trendsetter, style guru |反义词：sloven, dowdy
