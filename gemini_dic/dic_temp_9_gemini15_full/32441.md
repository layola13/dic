chimers:/ˈkaɪmər/| n.|嵌合体；幻想；怪诞的事物|A chimera is an organism that contains cells from two or more distinct lineages.  It can also refer to a thing that is hoped for but is illusory or improbable. |例句：The scientist created a chimeric mouse by combining cells from two different strains.  科学家通过结合两种不同品系的细胞创造了一个嵌合小鼠。

The new project, while ambitious, felt like a chimera to some of the investors.  虽然雄心勃勃，但这个新项目对一些投资者来说感觉像是一个幻想。|近义词：hybrid, fantasy, illusion|反义词：reality, truth
