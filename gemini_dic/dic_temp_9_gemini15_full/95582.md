incoming: /ˈɪnkʌmɪŋ/ | adj. | 即将到来的，进入的 | Arriving or about to arrive; approaching.  |例句：The incoming tide brought with it a lot of seaweed. 即将到来的潮水带来了许多海藻。
The incoming president is expected to announce new policies. 即将上任的总统预计将宣布新的政策。|近义词：approaching, arriving, imminent |反义词：outgoing, departing
