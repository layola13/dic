horsepower:/ˈhɔːrspaʊər/| n. |马力|A unit of power equal to 746 watts, originally defined as the power a horse could produce.|The car has a horsepower of 200. 这辆车有200马力。|近义词：power, wattage|反义词:None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This engine produces 150 horsepower. = 这台引擎产生150马力。
The car's horsepower is impressive. = 这辆车的马力令人印象深刻。


一般过去时:
The old tractor had less horsepower than the new one. = 旧拖拉机的马力比新的少。
That motorcycle boasted a high horsepower rating. = 那辆摩托车拥有很高的马力额定值。
