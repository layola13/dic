semiobjective:/ˌsemiːəbˈdʒektɪv/| adj.|半客观的，介于客观和主观之间|Semi-objective refers to something that is partly objective and partly subjective. It contains elements of both factual observation and personal interpretation or bias.  It acknowledges the influence of the observer's perspective while attempting to maintain a degree of objectivity.|The critic's review was semiobjective, acknowledging the film's technical merits while expressing personal reservations about its plot.|近义词：partially objective, relatively objective|反义词：completely subjective, wholly objective
