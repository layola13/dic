covens:/ˈkʌvənz/| n.|女巫集会；一群；一伙|A coven is a group of witches, especially one that meets for rituals.  The term is often used in fiction, but it also has historical roots in actual witchcraft practices.  The word can also refer to any group of people, often with negative connotations, suggesting a secretive or malicious gathering.|例句：The villagers whispered about the coven meeting in the woods. 村民们私下议论着在树林里开会的巫婆们。

例句：A coven of gossips spread rumors throughout the town. 一群爱说闲话的人在镇上散布谣言。|近义词：group, gathering, assembly, clique|反义词：individual, solitary
