coactively:/koʊˈæktɪvli/ | adv. | 合作地；协同地 | Acting together; in cooperation.  This describes actions or processes that occur jointly or in collaboration, often implying a synergistic effect. | Example: The team members worked coactively to achieve their ambitious goal.  团队成员合作努力以实现其宏伟目标。|近义词：cooperatively, collaboratively, jointly, synergistically | 反义词：independently, separately, individually
