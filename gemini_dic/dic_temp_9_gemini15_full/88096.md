hightailed:/haɪˈteɪld/| v.|飞奔；逃窜；迅速离开 | to leave quickly; to run away quickly.  |例句：When the police arrived, the thief hightailed it out of the building.  警察一来，小偷就从大楼里逃跑了。

例句：  Seeing the dog, the cat hightailed it up a tree.  看到狗，猫飞快地窜上树。


近义词：scram, bolt, flee, dash, scoot


反义词：saunter, stroll, amble


词性：动词
