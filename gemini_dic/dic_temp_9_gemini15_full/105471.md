killed-off:/kɪld ˈɔːf/| v. |消灭；淘汰；扼杀；使…终止|To kill or destroy something completely; to eliminate or put an end to something.|The disease killed off a large portion of the population.  这种疾病消灭了大部分人口。|The new technology has killed off many traditional industries. 新技术淘汰了许多传统产业。|近义词：eliminate, eradicate, exterminate, wipe out|反义词：create, foster, nurture, develop|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They kill off pests every year. = 他们每年都消灭害虫。
The company kills off inefficient products. = 公司淘汰效率低下的产品。

一般过去时:
The frost killed off the entire crop. = 霜冻毁掉了全部庄稼。
The government killed off the rebel group. = 政府消灭了叛乱组织。

现在完成时:
The drought has killed off many trees. = 干旱已经使许多树木枯死。
Pollution has killed off many species of fish. = 污染已经消灭了许多鱼类。

过去完成时:
The volcano had killed off most of the local wildlife before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，火山已经消灭了当地的大部分野生动物。
The pesticide had killed off the beneficial insects before we realized it. = 在我们意识到之前，杀虫剂已经杀死了有益昆虫。

将来时:
The rising sea levels will kill off coastal ecosystems. = 海平面上升将会消灭沿海生态系统。
They will kill off the invasive species. = 他们将会消灭入侵物种。

虚拟语气:
If we don't act now, climate change will kill off many species. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，气候变化将会消灭许多物种。
I wish the government hadn't killed off that important research program. = 我真希望政府没有取消那个重要的研究项目。

被动语态:
Many animals were killed off by habitat loss. = 许多动物因栖息地丧失而被消灭。
The old traditions were killed off by modernization. = 旧传统被现代化所淘汰。

疑问句:
Will this new policy kill off small businesses? = 这项新政策会消灭小型企业吗？
What measures can we take to prevent this disease from killing off the livestock? = 我们能采取什么措施来防止这种疾病消灭牲畜？

否定句:
The new medicine didn't kill off the infection. = 新药没有消灭感染。
We didn't want to kill off the entire project, just the problematic parts. = 我们不想取消整个项目，只是有问题的部分。
