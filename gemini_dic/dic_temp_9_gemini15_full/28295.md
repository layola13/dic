capucine:/kæpjuːˈsiːn/|/ˈkæpjʊsaɪn/| n. |金莲花；（某些植物的）橙黄色；卡普钦修女服饰|A capucine is a plant of the genus Tropaeolum, typically having bright orange or yellow flowers.  The name also refers to a shade of orange-yellow, similar to the color of the flowers, and historically to the brown robe worn by Capuchin friars.  |The garden was ablaze with capuchines. 花园里金莲花盛开。|The dress was a beautiful capucine color. 这件衣服是美丽的橙黄色。|近义词：nasturtium (for the plant), orange-yellow|反义词：无
