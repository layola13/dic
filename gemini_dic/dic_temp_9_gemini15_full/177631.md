reburied:/ˌriːˈbɛriːd/| v. |重新埋葬|To bury (something, especially a body) again.  This often implies that the original burial was disturbed or incomplete.|例句：The remains were reburied with full military honors.  遗体以全军礼重新下葬。

例句：After the flood, the villagers reburied their ancestors' remains in a higher location.  洪水过后，村民们将祖先的遗体重新埋葬在了更高的地方。


近义词：inter again, re-inter.
反义词：exhume, unearth.
词性：verb
