kilters:/ˈkɪltərz/| n.|良好状态，最佳状态|In good order; in proper working condition.  The machine is in kilter.|例句：The engine is finally back in kilter after the repairs. 引擎经过修理后终于恢复了正常运转。

The car is out of kilter. 这辆车出了故障。|近义词：working order, good repair, tip-top shape|反义词：disrepair, broken, malfunctioning
