degenerate:/dɪˈdʒɛnəreɪt/| v. adj. n.| 退化；堕落；衰退；简并 |To decline or deteriorate in quality, strength, or character; to become less complex or organized;  to lose the characteristics of a particular type or class.  |例句：The quality of life in the city has degenerated over the years.  城市的居住质量这些年来一直在下降。

The moral standards of the society have degenerated.  社会的道德标准已经堕落了。


近义词：deteriorate, decline, decay, corrupt, degrade|反义词：improve, enhance, elevate, progress, ascend|动词


degenerate:/dɪˈdʒɛnəreɪt/| adj.| 退化的；堕落的；衰退的；简并的 | Having lost the characteristics of a particular type or class; showing signs of decline or deterioration. |例句：A degenerate form of the disease.  一种退化的疾病形式。

He led a degenerate life. 他过着放荡的生活。


近义词：corrupt, depraved, degraded, decadent|反义词：advanced, progressive, superior, healthy, virtuous|形容词


degenerate:/dɪˈdʒɛnəreɪt/| n.| 堕落的人；颓废的人 | A person who has lost the characteristics of a particular type or class; someone who has low moral standards. |例句：He was considered a degenerate by his family. 他被家人认为是一个堕落的人。

The novel features a cast of degenerates. 这部小说以一群堕落的人为角色。


近义词：debauchee, libertine, reprobate, scoundrel|反义词：saint, paragon, model citizen|名词


词源:  From Latin *degenerāre "to deviate from one's kind," from de- "away from" + genus "kind, race, birth."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The environment degenerates slowly but surely. = 环境缓慢但肯定地退化了。
His health degenerates gradually. = 他的健康状况逐渐恶化。

一般过去时:
The empire degenerated after the death of the emperor. = 皇帝死后，帝国衰落了。
The building degenerated into a ruin. = 这栋建筑物变成了废墟。

现在完成时:
The situation has degenerated considerably. = 局势已经大大恶化了。
His behavior has degenerated over the years. = 他这些年的行为越来越糟糕了。

过去完成时:
The political situation had degenerated before the war started. = 在战争爆发之前，政治局势已经恶化了。
The moral standards had degenerated significantly by that time. = 到那时，道德标准已经严重败坏。

将来时:
The situation will degenerate further if no action is taken. = 如果不采取行动，情况将进一步恶化。
His health will degenerate rapidly without treatment. = 不治疗的话，他的健康会迅速恶化。

虚拟语气:
If he continued to drink, he would degenerate further. = 如果他继续酗酒，他将会更加堕落。
If we didn't take action, the situation would have degenerated into chaos. = 如果我们不采取行动，情况就会陷入混乱。


被动语态:
The building was allowed to degenerate. = 这栋建筑被允许荒废。
The system has been allowed to degenerate. = 这个系统被允许衰退。


疑问句:

Is this a degenerate case? = 这是一个简并的情况吗？


否定句:
The situation did not degenerate as quickly as expected. = 情况没有像预期的那样迅速恶化。
The quality of the product did not degenerate over time. = 产品质量没有随着时间推移而下降。
