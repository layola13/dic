nonfermented:/ˌnɒnfərˈmɛntɪd/ | adj. |未发酵的|Not subjected to fermentation; not fermented.  This adjective describes substances or foods that have not undergone the process of fermentation. |Example: The nonfermented juice retains its fresh, fruity flavor.  未发酵的果汁保留了其新鲜的水果风味。  The baker used nonfermented dough for this flatbread.  面包师用未发酵的面团制作这种薄饼。|近义词：unfermented, raw|反义词：fermented
