masochistically:/ˌmæsəˈkɪstɪkli/| adv.| 受虐狂般地；自虐地 |  Deriving from masochism, this adverb describes actions or behaviors characterized by the enjoyment of pain or suffering, either physical or emotional.  It implies a deliberate seeking out of unpleasant experiences for a sense of gratification. | 例句：He masochistically embraced the criticism, finding a strange satisfaction in it. 他受虐狂般地接受了批评，从中找到了一种奇怪的满足感。  She masochistically continued working despite the obvious burnout. 她自虐般地继续工作，尽管明显已经筋疲力尽了。| 近义词：self-destructively, self-punishingly | 反义词：sadistically
