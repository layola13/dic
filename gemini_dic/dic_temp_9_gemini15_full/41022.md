copatriot:/ˈkoʊpeɪtriət/ | n. | 同胞，同国人 | A fellow citizen or national of the same country.  |  例句：He fought alongside his compatriots in the war. 他在战争中与他的同胞并肩作战。  | 近义词：compatriot, fellow citizen, countryman | 反义词：foreigner, alien |  名词
