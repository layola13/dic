munch-out:/mʌntʃ aʊt/| v.|狼吞虎咽地吃；大吃一顿 |To eat a large amount of food, often quickly and noisily.|例句：We munched out on pizza last night.  我们昨晚狼吞虎咽地吃了比萨。|例句：Let's munch out at that new burger joint.咱们去那家新开的汉堡店大吃一顿吧。|近义词：gorge, devour, gobble, pig out |反义词：nibble, peck
