chanty:/ˈtʃænti/| n.| 船歌；海员号子 | A chant is a song, typically one with a repetitive melody, sung by sailors while working on a ship.  Chanty is a slightly older spelling.  | Example: The sailors sang a hearty chanty as they hauled up the anchor.  水手们在起锚时唱着响亮的船歌。 | 近义词：sea shanty, work song | 反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sailors chant a song. = 水手们唱着一首歌。
He chants a mantra every morning. = 他每天早上都念诵咒语。


一般过去时:
They chanted a protest song yesterday. = 他们昨天唱了一首抗议歌。
The monks chanted prayers throughout the night. = 僧侣们整夜都在诵经。


现在完成时:
The choir has chanted this piece many times. = 合唱团已经唱过这首曲子很多次了。
She has chanted affirmations to boost her confidence. = 她一直在念诵肯定句来增强自信。


过去完成时:
They had chanted for hours before the leader arrived. = 领导到达之前，他们已经唱了几个小时了。
The crowd had chanted slogans all afternoon. = 下午整个下午人群都在高呼口号。


将来时:
We will chant the school song at the ceremony. = 我们将在仪式上唱校歌。
The fans will chant their team's name during the game. = 球迷们将在比赛期间高呼他们球队的名字。


虚拟语气:
If they chanted louder, they might be heard. = 如果他们唱得更响亮，可能会被听到。
I wish I had chanted with them. = 我真希望我当时和他们一起唱。


被动语态:
The song was chanted by the whole crew. = 这首歌被全体船员演唱。
The mantra is chanted daily by many people. = 这句咒语每天都被很多人念诵。


疑问句:

否定句:
They didn't chant the whole song. = 他们没有把整首歌都唱完。
She doesn't chant often. = 她不经常唱歌。
