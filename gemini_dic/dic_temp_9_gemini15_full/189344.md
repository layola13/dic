sagaciously:/səˈɡeɪʃəsli/| adv.|  精明地；睿智地；明智地 |Showing or characterized by keen mental discernment and good judgment; shrewdly.  |例句：She sagaciously avoided the risky investment. 她明智地避免了那项风险投资。

例句：The old farmer sagaciously predicted the coming storm based on the changing weather patterns.  老农根据变化的天气模式，精明地预测到了即将到来的暴风雨。


近义词：wisely, shrewdly, cleverly, intelligently, judiciously

反义词：foolishly, unwisely, recklessly, stupidly


词源：源自拉丁语 *sagāx, “perceptive, quick-witted,”  from *sagāre, “to perceive keenly,” related to *sagīre, “to perceive by scent.”  其词根与嗅觉感知有关，引申出敏锐的洞察力。
