iridescence:/ˌɪrɪˈdɛsəns/ | n. |彩虹色；变彩；闪光 | The quality of exhibiting rainbow-like colours, typically as the result of diffraction or interference of light.  This effect is often seen in soap bubbles, oil slicks, and certain gemstones. |例句：The iridescent sheen of the butterfly's wings captivated the children. 蝴蝶翅膀上彩虹般的闪光迷住了孩子们。

例句：The oil slick on the water displayed a beautiful iridescence. 水上的油膜呈现出美丽的彩虹色。


近义词：shimmer, gleam, glitter, opalescence | 反义词：dullness, matte

词源:

iridescence 来自于 iris (彩虹) + -escent (带有...性质的，逐渐成为...)。因此，iridescence字面意思是“像彩虹一样的”。  Iris 本身源于希腊语 ἶρις (iris)，指彩虹女神。
