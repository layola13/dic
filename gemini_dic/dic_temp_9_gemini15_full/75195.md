frizzy:/ˈfrɪzi/| adj.|卷曲的，蓬松的|Having a lot of small, tight curls; untidy and messy in appearance.  It describes hair that is curly and often difficult to manage.|例句：Her frizzy hair was a challenge to style.  她的卷发很难打理。


例句：The humidity made his frizzy hair even more unruly. 潮湿的天气使得他的卷发更加难以打理。


近义词：curly, kinky, woolly, bushy


反义词：straight, smooth, sleek


词性:形容词
