dumpster:/ˈdʌmpstər/ | n. |垃圾箱，垃圾桶 |A large metal container, usually open-topped, used for the disposal of waste.  |例句：The dumpster was overflowing with trash.  垃圾箱里垃圾堆积如山。

例句：He threw the old furniture into the dumpster. 他把旧家具扔进了垃圾箱。

近义词：trash can, garbage can, refuse bin

反义词：无
