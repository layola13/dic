governorates:/ˈɡʌvərnəts/ | n. |省；省份；总督辖区 |  A governorate is a territorial division, often of a country,  governed by a governor.  It's a term often used in the Middle East and some other regions.  The specific powers and responsibilities of a governorate can vary depending on the country's political system. | Example: Egypt is divided into several governorates.  埃及被划分为几个省份。  The governorate of Cairo is the most populous. 开罗省人口最多。|近义词：province, prefecture, region |反义词：None (Antonyms are context-dependent and don't directly apply) |名词
