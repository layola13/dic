misadvised:/ˌmɪsədˈvaɪzd/| adj.|被误导的；不智的|Giving bad or wrong advice; wrongly advised.|例句：He was misadvised by his lawyer and lost the case. 他被律师误导了，输掉了官司。|近义词：ill-advised, misguided, badly advised|反义词：well-advised, wisely advised


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is misadvised about the risks involved. = 他对所涉及的风险被误导了。
The company is misadvised in its marketing strategy. = 公司的营销策略被误导了。


一般过去时:
She was misadvised by her friends to quit her job. = 她被朋友误导，辞掉了工作。
The investors were misadvised on the potential returns. = 投资者们对潜在回报被误导了。


现在完成时:

We have been misadvised regarding the legal implications. = 关于法律影响，我们已经被误导了。


过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
