domestic:/dəˈmɛstɪk/ | adj., n. |国内的；家庭的；驯养的；家用的 | Relating to one's own country; not foreign or international.  Also relating to the home, family, or household; tame or domesticated. |例句：The airline offers both domestic and international flights. 这家航空公司提供国内和国际航班。

例句：She enjoys doing domestic chores like cooking and cleaning. 她喜欢做家务，例如烹饪和清洁。


近义词：inland, national, home, internal, household | 反义词：foreign, international, wild, external


词性: adjective, noun
