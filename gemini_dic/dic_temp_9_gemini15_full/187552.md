romanizer:/ˈroʊmənaɪzər/ | n. | 罗马化工具；罗马化者 | A person or thing that converts text from one writing system to the Roman alphabet.  A romanizer can be a software program, a person skilled in transliteration, or a system of rules for converting writing systems. | Example:  The researcher used a sophisticated romanizer to transcribe the ancient manuscript.  研究人员使用复杂的罗马化工具来转录古代手稿。|近义词：transliterator, converter |反义词：N/A
