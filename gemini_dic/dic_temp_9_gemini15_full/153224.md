pelted:/ˈpɛltɪd/| vt.|用东西猛击；向…投掷；(雨、雪等)猛烈打击|to strike repeatedly with things thrown; to hit with a shower of something; to strike repeatedly with blows or missiles|例句：The children pelted the snowman with snowballs.孩子们用雪球猛砸雪人。

例句：The protesters pelted the police with stones. 抗议者向警察投掷石头。

近义词：bombarded, showered, battered,  attacked

反义词：protected, shielded, defended

词性:动词

词源:
The word "pelted" comes from the Middle English word "pelten," meaning "to throw," which in turn is derived from the Old English word "pyltan," with the same meaning.  The root likely connects to words implying a sudden or forceful impact.
