half-offended:/ˌhæf əˈfɛndɪd/ | adj. | 半生气，有点生气 | Slightly offended; feeling a little bit insulted or annoyed, but not extremely so.  This word implies a milder form of offense than "offended". | Example: He seemed half-offended by my joke, but I think he was mostly just surprised. 他好像对我的玩笑有点生气，但我认为他主要只是很惊讶。


近义词: slightly offended, mildly annoyed, a little put out, somewhat displeased

反义词: delighted, pleased, unoffended, thrilled
