fineness:/ˈfaɪnəs/| n. |精致，细度，纯度 |The fineness of the silk is remarkable.  丝绸的精致令人惊叹。|近义词：refinement, delicacy, purity |反义词：coarseness, crudeness, impurity


英文解析：Fineness refers to the quality of being fine or delicate, particularly in texture, structure, or detail. It can also denote the degree of purity or the fineness of a substance, such as gold or silver.


例句：

The fineness of the craftsmanship is evident in every detail.  工艺的精细程度在每一个细节中都显而易见。

The fineness of the gold is measured in karats. 黄金的纯度是用克拉来衡量的。


词源:

源自古英语 *fīn,  意为“精细的，纯净的”。  这与“fine” (精细的，美好的) 词根相同。
