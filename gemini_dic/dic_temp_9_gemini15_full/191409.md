scheming:/ˈskiːmɪŋ/| adj.|阴谋的；诡计多端的；策划的| characterized by scheming or plotting; crafty; cunning.  It implies the use of underhanded or devious methods to achieve a goal. |例句：He had a scheming look in his eyes. 他眼中带着阴险的神情。

例句：Her scheming nature led her to betray her friends. 她阴险的本性导致她背叛了朋友。


近义词：plotting, conniving, Machiavellian,  intriguing, devious, crafty, cunning

反义词：honest, straightforward, candid, ingenuous, aboveboard


词源:  From "scheme," ultimately from Old French *escheme, from Late Latin *schema, from Greek *skhēma ("form, figure"). The meaning shifted from "plan" to "underhanded plan."

过去完成时:
