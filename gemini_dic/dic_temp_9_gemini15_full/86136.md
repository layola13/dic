hearth:/hɑːrθ/| n.|炉膛；壁炉；家；家庭；中心|The central part of a fireplace, usually made of brick or stone, where a fire is built.  It often symbolizes the home and family.|例句：The family gathered around the hearth on Christmas Eve. 圣诞夜，家人围坐在壁炉旁。
例句：The warmth of the hearth filled the room. 壁炉的温暖充满了整个房间。|近义词：fireplace, home, center|反义词：outside, exterior

词源:
The word "hearth" ultimately derives from the Old English word "heorþ," meaning "fireplace" or "hearth."  This word, in turn, has connections to Proto-Germanic and Proto-Indo-European roots related to the concepts of "burning" and "warmth."
