declamatorily:/ˌdekləˈmeɪtərili/| adv. |朗诵地；夸夸其谈地；慷慨激昂地 |In a declamatory manner; with rhetorical flourish and emphasis.  It suggests a style of speaking that is dramatic, forceful, and often showy. |例句：He spoke declamatorily, emphasizing each word for dramatic effect. 他慷慨激昂地讲话，着重强调每个词以增强戏剧效果。

例句：She declaimed her opinions declamatorily, leaving no room for dissent. 她夸夸其谈地表达了自己的意见，不容置疑。


近义词：oratorically, rhetorically, bombastically, dramatically


反义词：quietly, softly, modestly, humbly


词性:副词
