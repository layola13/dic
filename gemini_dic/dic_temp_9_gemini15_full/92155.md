hypersentimental:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛntɪmɛntəl/| adj. | 过度感伤的，多愁善感的 | Excessively sentimental; overly emotional and sensitive.  Characterized by extreme or exaggerated sentimentality. | 例句：Her hypersentimental response to the sad movie surprised everyone.  她的过度感伤的反应让每个人都感到惊讶。

例句：His hypersentimental nature often led him to make impulsive decisions based on emotion rather than logic. 他过度感伤的性格经常导致他做出基于情感而非逻辑的冲动决定。


近义词：overly sentimental, maudlin, mushy, sentimentalized, weepy

反义词：apathy, stoicism, unemotional, callous, indifferent


词性：形容词
