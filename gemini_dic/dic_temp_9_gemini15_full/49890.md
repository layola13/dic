demurest: /dɪˈmjʊərɪst/ | adj. | 最娴静的，最端庄的 | Most modest and reserved in manner.  It is the superlative form of "demure". |例句：She was the demurest girl at the ball, barely speaking a word. 她是在舞会上最娴静的女孩，几乎一言不发。

例句：Of all the candidates, she presented the demurest image. 在所有候选人中，她展现出最端庄的形象。


近义词：modest, reserved, shy, quiet, retiring |反义词：bold, forward, assertive, outgoing


词性：形容词
