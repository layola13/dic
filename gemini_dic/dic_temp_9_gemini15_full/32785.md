choice:/tʃɔɪs/|/n.|选择，抉择|The act of choosing; the right or opportunity to choose.|例句：I had no choice but to leave. 我别无选择，只能离开。|例句：The choice is yours. 选择权在你。|近义词：selection, option, alternative|反义词：necessity, compulsion


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has many choices in his career. = 他在职业生涯中有很多选择。
The store offers a wide choice of products. = 这家商店提供各种各样的产品。


一般过去时:
She made a wise choice in her investment. = 她在投资方面做出了明智的选择。
They had several choices for dinner. = 他们有几种晚餐选择。


现在完成时:
I have made my choice. = 我已经做出了选择。
We have had many choices throughout our lives. = 我们一生中有很多选择。


过去完成时:
She had already made her choice before I spoke. = 我还没说话，她就做出了选择。
They had considered several choices before deciding. = 他们在决定之前考虑了几个选择。


将来时:
I will have many choices to make in the future. = 我将来会有很多选择要做。
She will make her choice tomorrow. = 她明天会做出选择。


虚拟语气:
If I had had more choices, I would have chosen differently. = 如果我有更多选择，我会做出不同的选择。
I wish I had a wider choice of colors. = 我希望我有更多颜色的选择。


被动语态:
A choice was made by the committee. = 委员会做出了选择。
Many choices are offered to the customers. = 向顾客提供了许多选择。


疑问句:
What is your choice? = 你的选择是什么？
Do you have a choice in this matter? = 在这件事上你有选择权吗？


否定句:
I have no choice in the matter. = 我在这件事上没有选择权。
There was no other choice available. = 没有其他选择。
