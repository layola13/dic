compliances:/kəmˈplaɪənsɪz/ | n. | 遵守；服从；顺从；符合 | The act of conforming to a rule, standard, or law; the state of being compliant.  It often refers to meeting regulations or requirements. | Example:  The company’s recent actions demonstrate a commitment to environmental compliances.  这家公司的近期行为表明其致力于遵守环保规定。

The factory failed to meet safety compliances. 这家工厂未能达到安全合规要求。


近义词：conformity, adherence, obedience, observance | 反义词：disobedience, defiance, noncompliance


词源:  From the verb "comply," which comes from the Old French word "complaisir," meaning "to please" or "to fulfill."  The suffix "-ance" indicates a state or condition.
