deplored:/dɪˈplɔːrd/| v.|谴责，悲叹，对…感到遗憾|To express strong disapproval of; to regret deeply.|The government deplored the violence. 政府谴责了暴力行为。  She deplored the lack of funding for education. 她对教育经费不足感到遗憾。|condemn, denounce, regret, lament|approve, praise, celebrate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They deplore the rising crime rate. = 他们谴责不断上升的犯罪率。
We deplore the destruction of the rainforest. = 我们对雨林的破坏感到痛心。


一般过去时:
He deplored the decision to go to war. = 他对发动战争的决定表示谴责。
She deplored the loss of her grandmother. = 她对祖母的去世深感悲痛。


现在完成时:
They have deplored the government's handling of the crisis. = 他们一直谴责政府处理危机的方案。
We have deplored the lack of action on climate change for years. = 多年来，我们一直对气候变化问题缺乏行动表示遗憾。


过去完成时:
She had deplored the injustice of the situation for months. = 她几个月来一直对这种情况的不公正感到遗憾。
They had deplored the company's unethical practices before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他们就对公司的违反道德的行为表示谴责。


将来时:
He will deplore the consequences of his actions. = 他将对自己的行为造成的后果表示遗憾。
They will deplore the loss of such a talented artist. = 他们将会为失去如此有才华的艺术家而感到悲痛。


虚拟语气:
I deplored that the project was cancelled. = 我对该项目被取消感到遗憾。(用过去式表示与现在事实相反的虚拟语气)
If I had known, I would have deplored the consequences. = 如果我知道的话，我会对后果表示遗憾。(用过去完成时表示与过去事实相反的虚拟语气)


被动语态:
The government's actions were deplored by many. = 许多人谴责政府的行为。
The decision was deplored by environmental groups. = 环境保护组织谴责这一决定。


疑问句:
Do you deplore the actions of the company? = 你谴责这家公司的行为吗？
Did they deplore the outcome of the election? = 他们对选举的结果感到遗憾吗？


否定句:
He did not deplore the outcome of the meeting. = 他并不对会议的结果感到遗憾。
They do not deplore the use of force in this instance. = 在这种情况下，他们并不谴责使用武力。


PHRASE->
deplore the lack of… = 对…的缺乏表示遗憾
deplore the fact that… = 对…这一事实表示遗憾
deeply deplore = 深深谴责
publicly deplore = 公开谴责
strongly deplore = 强烈谴责

词源:
源自古法语 *deplorer，由 de-  (向下) + plorer (哭泣)构成。
