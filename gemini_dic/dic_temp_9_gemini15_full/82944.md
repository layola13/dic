gun-for: /ɡʌn fɔːr/ | phrasal verb | 积极争取；极力追求；热切期盼 | To actively seek or strive for something; to eagerly anticipate something.  |例句：She's gunning for the promotion. 她正在积极争取晋升。|近义词：strive for, aim for, pursue, yearn for |反义词：avoid, shun, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are gunning for the top spot in the league. = 他们在积极争取联赛榜首的位置。
He guns for every opportunity to learn new skills. = 他积极争取每一个学习新技能的机会。


一般过去时:
She gunned for the lead role in the play. = 她积极争取了该剧的主角。
They gunned for a better future for their children. = 他们为孩子争取更好的未来。


现在完成时:
He has been gunning for that position for years. = 他多年来一直积极争取那个职位。
They have gunned for a more equitable society. = 他们一直在争取一个更加公平的社会。


过去完成时:
She had gunned for the scholarship before realizing she didn't meet the requirements. = 她曾经积极争取过奖学金，后来才意识到自己不符合条件。
They had gunned for a peaceful resolution before resorting to violence. = 在诉诸暴力之前，他们曾努力争取和平解决。


将来时:
She will gun for the championship title next year. = 明年她将争取冠军头衔。
They will gun for a higher market share. = 他们将争取更高的市场份额。


虚拟语气:
If I were her, I would gun for the presidency. = 如果我是她，我会争取总统职位。
I wish I had gunned for that job opportunity earlier. = 我真希望我早点争取那个工作机会。


被动语态:
The promotion was gunned for by many ambitious employees. = 许多有抱负的员工都在积极争取晋升。
The contract was gunned for by several competing companies. = 几家竞争公司都在积极争取这份合同。


疑问句:
Is she gunning for the CEO position? = 她是在争取首席执行官职位吗？
Are they gunning for a merger with our company? = 他们是在争取与我们公司合并吗？


否定句:
He didn't gun for the job, preferring to stay in his current role. = 他没有争取那份工作，更愿意留在目前的职位上。
They didn't gun for the contract, deciding it was too risky. = 他们没有争取那份合同，认为风险太大。
