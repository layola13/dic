mitten:/ˈmɪtən/| n.|手套（指无分指的手套）|A mitten is a type of glove that covers the thumb separately but keeps the fingers together in a single compartment.  Mittens are often warmer than gloves because they reduce heat loss from the fingers.|He wore thick woolen mittens to keep his hands warm in the snow. 他戴着厚厚的羊毛手套来保暖。|glove, glove| ---
