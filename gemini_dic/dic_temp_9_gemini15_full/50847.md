deseeded:/dɪˈsiːdəd/| adj.|去籽的；已去除种子的|Having had the seeds removed.  The word describes something from which seeds have been taken out.  |例句：The deseeded grapes were perfect for making juice. 去籽的葡萄非常适合做果汁。

例句：She bought a bag of deseeded plums for her pie. 她买了一袋去籽李子准备做馅饼。


近义词：seedless, pitted (for fruits with pits)


反义词：seeded


词性：形容词
