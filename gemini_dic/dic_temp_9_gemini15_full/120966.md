mischarged:/ˌmɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒd/ | v. | 错收费，错误收费 | To charge incorrectly or unfairly; to make a mistake in charging.  |例句：I was mischarged for my hotel room; the bill was much higher than expected. 我酒店房间被错收费了；账单比预期的要高得多。|例句：The customer service representative apologized for mischarging my credit card. 客服代表为错误收费向我道歉。|近义词：overcharge, wrongly charge, bill incorrectly |反义词：correctly charge, fairly charge |动词
