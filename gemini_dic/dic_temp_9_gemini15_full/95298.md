incarnating:/ɪnˈkɑːrneɪtɪŋ/| v.|化身；赋予肉体；体现|The process of embodying or taking on a physical form, especially of a deity or spirit; to give a bodily form to; to exemplify or represent perfectly.|例句：The goddess incarnating as a swan.  女神化身为天鹅。

例句：His actions incarnated the spirit of generosity.  他的行为体现了慷慨的精神。|近义词：embodying, embodying, manifesting, personifying|反义词：dematerializing, disembodying
