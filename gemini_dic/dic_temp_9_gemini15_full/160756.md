poseuse:/pəʊˈzɜːz/ | n. |矫揉造作的女人，装腔作势的人 |A woman who affects a particular pose or manner, especially one considered artificial or affected.  She is known for her theatrical poses and dramatic pronouncements.  |例句：She was a poseuse, always striking dramatic poses and speaking in affected tones. 她是个装腔作势的女人，总是摆出戏剧化的姿势，用做作的语气说话。  |近义词：coquette, affected person, phony,  pretender |反义词：genuine, authentic, sincere, natural
