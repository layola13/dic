calamity:/kəˈlæməti/| n. |灾难，灾祸 |A calamity is an event causing great and often sudden damage or distress; a disaster.  It often refers to a widespread event affecting many people. |例句：The earthquake was a terrible calamity that devastated the city.  这场地震是一场可怕的灾难，摧毁了这座城市。  The flood was a major calamity for the farmers.  这场洪水给农民带来了巨大的灾难。|近义词：disaster, catastrophe, cataclysm |反义词：blessing, fortune, good luck |名词
