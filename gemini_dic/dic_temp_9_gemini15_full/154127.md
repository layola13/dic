peril:/ˈperəl/| n.|危险，风险|danger, risk, or threat; something that is likely to cause harm or injury.|例句：He ignored the peril of climbing the mountain alone. 他忽视了独自攀登这座山的危险。|例句：The ship was in grave peril. 这艘船处于严重的危险之中。|近义词：danger, hazard, risk, threat|反义词：safety, security


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm presents a great peril to sailors. = 暴风雨给水手们带来了巨大的危险。
This project is fraught with peril. = 这个项目充满了危险。


一般过去时:
The explorers faced many perils on their journey. = 探险家们在旅途中面临许多危险。
The company narrowly escaped peril. = 公司险些遭遇危险。


现在完成时:
We have avoided many perils so far. = 到目前为止，我们已经避免了许多危险。
The situation has presented unforeseen perils. = 局势出现了意想不到的危险。


过去完成时:
They had underestimated the perils of the expedition. = 他们低估了探险的危险。
I had never been in such peril before. = 我以前从未处于如此危险的境地。


将来时:
The rising sea levels will pose a great peril to coastal communities. = 海平面上升将对沿海社区构成巨大危险。
This decision could lead to peril in the long run. = 从长远来看，这个决定可能会导致危险。


虚拟语气:
If we had been more careful, we could have avoided that peril. = 如果我们更小心一些，就能避免那种危险。
I wish we hadn't put ourselves in such peril. = 我真希望我们没有让自己陷入如此危险的境地。


被动语态:
The climbers were endangered by the peril of falling rocks. = 攀登者面临落石的危险。
The city was put in peril by the approaching hurricane. = 即将到来的飓风使这座城市陷入了危险之中。


疑问句:
What perils do we face in this undertaking? = 我们在这项工作中面临哪些危险？
Is there any peril in taking this shortcut? = 走这条捷径有危险吗？


否定句:
There is no peril in following the instructions carefully. = 仔细按照说明操作没有危险。
The journey was not without peril. = 这段旅程并非没有危险。
