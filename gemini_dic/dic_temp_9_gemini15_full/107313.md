lambastes:/læmˈbeɪsts/| vt.|痛斥；严厉批评|To criticize severely or angrily.|The critic lambasted the new film for its poor acting and weak plot. 批评家严厉批评这部新电影的糟糕演技和薄弱情节。|近义词：criticize, rebuke, denounce, castigate|反义词：praise, commend, applaud|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lambastes his students regularly. = 他经常痛斥他的学生。
The newspaper lambastes the government's policies. = 这家报纸痛斥政府的政策。

一般过去时:
The teacher lambasted the student for cheating. = 老师因作弊而痛斥学生。
She lambasted him for his carelessness. = 她因他的粗心大意而痛斥他。

现在完成时:

He has lambasted the opposition party repeatedly. = 他一再痛斥反对党。

过去完成时:
The manager had lambasted the team before the meeting. = 经理在会议前痛斥了团队。
She had lambasted her brother for his irresponsible behavior. = 她曾因她弟弟不负责任的行为而痛斥他。

疑问句:
