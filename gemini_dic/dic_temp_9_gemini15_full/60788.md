electrometry:/ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒmətri/ | n. | 电测法；静电计量法 | The measurement of electrical quantities, especially potential difference, using an electrometer.  Electrometry is crucial in various fields, from medical diagnostics to environmental monitoring. | Example:  The researchers used electrometry to precisely measure the voltage across the membrane. 研究人员用电测法精确测量了膜上的电压。 |近义词：electrostatic measurement, potential measurement |反义词：N/A
