mulberry:/ˈmʌlbəri/| n. |桑树；桑葚|The word "mulberry" refers to both the tree and its fruit.  Mulberry trees are known for their sweet, dark-colored berries.  These berries are often used in jams, pies, and wines.|例句：The birds were feasting on the ripe mulberries in the garden. 鸟儿们正在花园里享用成熟的桑葚。

例句：We picked a basket full of mulberries for a pie. 我们摘了一篮桑葚准备做馅饼。

近义词：None (as it refers to a specific type of tree and fruit)
反义词：None (no direct antonym)

词性: Noun
