interactive: /ˌɪntərˈæktɪv/ | adj. | 交互式的，互动式的 |  Characterized by or involving interaction; allowing or encouraging a two-way flow of information between a user and a computer or other machine.  | 例句：This is an interactive whiteboard, allowing for collaborative work. 这是一块交互式白板，允许协同工作。

例句：The museum features many interactive exhibits designed to engage visitors. 这个博物馆有很多互动式展品，旨在吸引参观者。


近义词：reciprocal, responsive, mutually responsive | 反义词：passive, unidirectional, one-way


词源:  The word "interactive" is derived from the verb "interact," which itself is a combination of the prefix "inter-" (meaning "between" or "among") and the verb "act" (meaning "to do" or "to perform").  Therefore, "interactive" literally means "acting between" or involving reciprocal action.
