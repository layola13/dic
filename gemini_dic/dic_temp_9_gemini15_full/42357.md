counterargues:/ˌkaʊntərˈɑːrɡjuːz/ | vt. | 反驳；提出反驳意见 | To present a counterargument; to offer reasons or evidence against an argument or claim.  | He counterargued that the proposed solution was impractical. 他反驳说，拟议的解决方案不切实际。  The lawyer counterargued the prosecution's evidence. 律师反驳了检方的证据。| argue against, refute, rebut | agree, concede | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He counterargues that the policy is ineffective. = 他反驳说这项政策无效。
She counterargues every point I make. = 她反驳我提出的每一个论点。


一般过去时:
They counterargued the defendant's claims. = 他们反驳了被告的主张。
The witness counterargued the accusations. = 证人反驳了指控。


现在完成时:
He has counterargued this point many times. = 他已经多次反驳过这一点。
She has counterargued effectively against her opponent. = 她有效地反驳了对手。


过去完成时:
They had counterargued before the judge ruled. = 在法官做出裁决之前，他们就已经进行了反驳。
The lawyer had counterargued all the evidence presented. = 律师已经反驳了所有提交的证据。


将来时:
He will counterargue the opposition's proposal. = 他将反驳反对派的提议。
She will counterargue with facts and figures. = 她将用事实和数据进行反驳。


虚拟语气:
If he were here, he would counterargue that position. = 如果他在的话，他会反驳那个立场。
I wish I had counterargued more effectively. = 我真希望我当时能更有效地反驳。


被动语态:
The proposal was counterargued by several experts. = 这项提议遭到几位专家的反驳。
His arguments were counterargued convincingly. = 他的论点得到了令人信服的反驳。


疑问句:

What points did he use to counterargue? = 他用什么论点来反驳？


否定句:
He didn't counterargue the evidence. = 他没有反驳证据。
