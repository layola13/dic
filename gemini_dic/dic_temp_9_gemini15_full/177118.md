reappraising:/ˌriːəˈpreɪzɪŋ/| v.|重新评估，重新评价 |To reconsider and reassess something.| The company is reappraising its assets after the merger. 并购后，公司正在重新评估其资产。| reassess, re-evaluate | undervalue, underassess | 动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reappraising the value of the property. = 他们正在重新评估该房产的价值。
We reappraise our strategies quarterly. = 我们每季度重新评估我们的策略。


一般过去时:
The committee reappraised the situation after receiving new evidence. = 委员会在收到新的证据后重新评估了情况。
She reappraised her life choices after her divorce. = 离婚后，她重新评估了她的人生选择。


现在完成时:
They have reappraised the risks involved in the project. = 他们已经重新评估了该项目中涉及的风险。
The government has reappraised its economic policies. = 政府已经重新评估了其经济政策。


过去完成时:
After the accident, he had reappraised his priorities in life. = 事故之后，他重新评估了他生活中的优先事项。
The investors had reappraised their investment strategy before making a new decision. = 投资者在做出新的决定之前重新评估了他们的投资策略。


将来时:
They will reappraise the building's structural integrity next month. = 他们下个月将重新评估建筑物的结构完整性。
The board will reappraise the company's performance at the end of the year. = 董事会将在年底重新评估公司的业绩。


虚拟语气:
If they were to reappraise the contract, they might find some loopholes. = 如果他们要重新评估合同，他们可能会发现一些漏洞。
I wish I had reappraised my options before signing the agreement. = 我希望在签署协议之前我已经重新评估了我的选择。


被动语态:
The situation is being reappraised by the experts. = 情况正在由专家重新评估。
The artwork was reappraised by a renowned auction house. = 这件艺术品被一家著名的拍卖行重新估价。


疑问句:
Are they reappraising the budget for the project? = 他们是否正在重新评估该项目的预算？
Will the company be reappraising its marketing strategy? = 公司是否会重新评估其营销策略？


否定句:
They are not reappraising the situation at this time. = 他们目前没有重新评估情况。
The company did not reappraise its assets last year. = 公司去年没有重新评估其资产。
