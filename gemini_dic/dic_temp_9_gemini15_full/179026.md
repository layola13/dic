redelivered:/ˌriːdɪˈlɪvərd/| v. |重新递送；再次交付；重新发表|To deliver something again.  This implies a previous delivery that was perhaps unsuccessful, incomplete, or required a second attempt.|例句：The package was redelivered after the first attempt failed.  包裹第一次投递失败后被重新投递了。

The courier redelivered the important documents to the correct address.  快递员将重要文件重新送到了正确的地址。|近义词：retransmit, resend, resubmit|反义词：withhold, retain
