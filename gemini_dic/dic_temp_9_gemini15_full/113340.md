luxuriated:/ˈlʌkʃəriˌeɪtɪd/| v.|沉溺于；享受；过奢侈的生活 | to take great pleasure in something; to indulge oneself in luxury. |例句：She luxuriated in the warmth of the sun. 她沉溺于阳光的温暖。|例句：They luxuriated in their newfound wealth. 他们沉溺于他们新获得的财富。|近义词：indulged, reveled, basked |反义词：abstained, denied, suffered


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He luxuriates in his success. = 他沉溺于他的成功。
She luxuriates in the quiet of the countryside. = 她享受着乡村的宁静。

一般过去时:
They luxuriated in the comfort of their hotel room. = 他们享受着酒店房间的舒适。
We luxuriated in the delicious meal. = 我们尽情享受美味佳肴。

现在完成时:
She has luxuriated in the freedom of retirement. = 她一直享受着退休的自由。
He has luxuriated in his wealth for years. = 他多年来一直过着奢侈的生活。

过去完成时:
They had luxuriated in the spa all afternoon. = 他们整个下午都在水疗中心享受。
She had luxuriated in the hot bath before going to bed. = 她睡前在热水浴中享受了一番。

将来时:
We will luxuriate in the beautiful scenery of the mountains. = 我们将在美丽的山景中尽情享受。
They will luxuriate in the luxury of their new yacht. = 他们将在他们新的游艇的奢华中享受。

虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would luxuriate in a long soak in the tub. = 如果我有时间，我会在浴缸里好好泡个澡。
I wish I could luxuriate in a tropical beach. = 我真希望能在热带海滩享受一番。

被动语态:
The guests were luxuriated with top-notch service.  = 客人们享受了顶级服务。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，很少使用)

否定句:
