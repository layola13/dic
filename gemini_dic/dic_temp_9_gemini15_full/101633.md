irreducible:/ˌɪrɪˈdjuːsəbl/| adj.|不可约的，不可简化的 | incapable of being reduced or simplified further.  It implies a fundamental or essential quality that cannot be broken down into smaller or simpler components.  |例句：The problem is irreducible to a simple equation.  这个问题无法简化为一个简单的方程式。

The prime factorization of a number is irreducible.  一个数的质因数分解是不可约的。


近义词：indivisible, fundamental, basic, elemental |反义词：reducible, simplifiable, decomposable |词性:adjective

词源:
The word "irreducible" comes from the prefix "ir-" (meaning "not") + "reducible" (meaning "able to be reduced").  "Reducible" in turn comes from the verb "reduce," ultimately derived from the Latin word "reducere," meaning "to lead back."  Therefore, "irreducible" literally means "not able to be led back" or "not able to be simplified".
