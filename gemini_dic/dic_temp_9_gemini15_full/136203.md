nonreliably:/ˌnɒn rɪˈlaɪəbli/| adv.|不可靠地，不稳定地 |  Describes something that cannot be relied upon consistently or dependably; it is prone to failure or inaccuracy. |例句：The old machine functioned nonreliably, often breaking down unexpectedly.  这台旧机器运行不可靠，经常意外故障。The witness testified nonreliably, contradicting himself several times.  证人证词不可靠，几次自相矛盾。|近义词：unreliably, inconsistently, erratically, unpredictably |反义词：reliably, dependably, consistently, steadily
