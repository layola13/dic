semiquartile:/ˌsɛmiˈkwɔːrtaɪl/| n.|四分位数中点|A semiquartile is the midpoint between a quartile and the next quartile in a data set.  It is calculated by averaging the values of the two quartiles.  Semiquartiles are less commonly used than quartiles themselves, but they can provide additional information about the distribution of data.  They help to further subdivide the data into more refined segments, offering a more granular view of data spread and central tendency. |The semiquartile range is a measure of statistical dispersion.|近义词：midquartile|反义词：无
