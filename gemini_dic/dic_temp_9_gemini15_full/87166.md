herbs:/hɜːrbz/| n. |草药；香草|Plants used for flavoring food, for medicine, or for fragrance.  Herbs are often used in cooking to add flavor and aroma to dishes. They can also have medicinal properties and be used in traditional medicine. |例句：She added fresh herbs to the pasta sauce. 她往意大利面酱里加了新鲜香草。

She grows a variety of herbs in her garden.  她在花园里种植各种香草。


近义词：spices, plants, greenery|反义词：None specifically.  The antonym would depend on the context (e.g.,  "spices" if contrasting the use of herbs vs. spices).|名词
