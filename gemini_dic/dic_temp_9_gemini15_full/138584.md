nuancing:/ˈnuːɑːnsɪŋ/| n. |细微差别；细致的表达；微妙的差别|The subtle nuances of meaning in the poem were lost in translation.  这首诗歌中微妙的含义在翻译中丢失了。| refinement, subtlety, shading | crudeness, bluntness, simplicity |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist is nuancing the colors in his painting. = 艺术家正在细致地调整他画作中的颜色。
She nuances her arguments to appeal to a wider audience. = 她细致地调整论点以吸引更广泛的受众。


一般过去时:
He nuanced his speech to avoid offending anyone. = 他委婉地表达了自己的观点，以避免冒犯任何人。
The writer nuanced the character's personality throughout the novel. = 作者在小说中细致地刻画了人物的性格。


现在完成时:
She has nuanced her approach to teaching over the years. = 这些年来，她已经完善了她的教学方法。
The company has nuanced its marketing strategy to target a younger demographic. = 公司已经调整了其营销策略，以针对更年轻的人群。


过去完成时:
They had nuanced their position before the negotiations began. = 在谈判开始之前，他们已经调整了他们的立场。
He had nuanced his response to the criticism before delivering it. = 在回应批评之前，他已经仔细考虑过自己的措辞。


将来时:
She will nuance her presentation to make it more engaging. = 她将完善她的演示文稿，使其更引人入胜。
The politician will nuance his message to appeal to undecided voters. = 这位政治家将调整他的信息，以吸引未决定的选民。


虚拟语气:
If she nuanced her writing style, it would be more impactful. = 如果她改进写作风格，效果会更好。
I wish he had nuanced his remarks; they were too harsh. = 我希望他当时能委婉一些；他的话太尖刻了。


被动语态:
The argument was nuanced by the inclusion of new evidence. = 通过加入新的证据，论点得到了更精细的阐述。
His ideas were nuanced and difficult to understand at first. = 他的想法很微妙，一开始很难理解。


疑问句:
How did she nuance her response to the difficult question? = 她如何巧妙地回答了那个难题？
Does the artist nuance the light and shadow in his work? = 艺术家是否在他的作品中细致地处理光影？


否定句:
He didn't nuance his criticism; he was simply blunt. = 他没有委婉地表达他的批评；他只是直截了当。
The presentation lacked nuance and clarity. = 演示文稿缺乏细致和清晰度。
