pulleyless:/ˈpʊliːlɪs/ | adj. | 无滑轮的；不使用滑轮的 |  Lacking pulleys; not using pulleys.  This term describes systems or mechanisms that operate without the use of pulleys for lifting, conveying, or transferring objects. | Example: The pulleyless elevator uses a hydraulic system instead of traditional cables and pulleys.  这个无滑轮的电梯使用液压系统而不是传统的缆绳和滑轮。  | 近义词:pulley-free | 反义词:pulley-based
