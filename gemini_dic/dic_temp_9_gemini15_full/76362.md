gadgeteers:/ˈɡædʒɪtiərz/| n. |小发明家；热衷于小工具的人 |A gadgeteer is someone who is enthusiastic about gadgets and often invents or modifies them. They are typically fascinated by technology and enjoy experimenting with new devices.  |例句：He's a gadget enthusiast, always tinkering with new devices. 他是个热衷于小工具的人，总是摆弄新设备。

例句：Many gadgeteers contributed to the development of early computers. 许多小发明家为早期计算机的发展做出了贡献。

近义词： inventor, tinkerer, innovator

反义词： Luddite (someone opposed to new technology)
