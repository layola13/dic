reenslavements:/ˌriːɪnˈsleɪvmənts/| n.|重新奴役；再次奴役 |The act of enslaving someone again after they have been freed or the state of being re-enslaved.  This term highlights the cyclical nature of enslavement and the repeated subjugation of individuals or groups.  It carries a strong connotation of injustice and oppression. |例句：The historical accounts detail the reenslavements of freed people after the Civil War.  历史记载详细描述了内战后被解放的人再次被奴役的情况。

The ongoing struggle against systemic racism and inequalities reveals the insidious nature of reenslavements in various forms.  持续不断的反对系统性种族主义和不平等的斗争揭示了各种形式的再次奴役的阴险本质。|近义词：resubjugation, re-imprisonment (in a figurative sense)|反义词：emancipation, liberation|名词
