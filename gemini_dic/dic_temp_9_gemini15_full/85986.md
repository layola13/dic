headspaces:/ˈhɛdˌspeɪsɪz/ | n. |思维空间；精神空间；内心世界 |The term "headspace" refers to the mental or emotional space within a person's mind, encompassing their thoughts, feelings, and mental state.  It can also refer to the amount of mental capacity available for processing information or dealing with challenges. |例句：She needed some headspace to process the news. 她需要一些时间来消化这个消息。

例句：Finding headspace amidst the chaos was proving difficult. 在混乱中找到思维空间证明是困难的。


近义词：mindset, mental state, inner world, mental capacity |反义词：Overwhelm, stress, chaos |词性:名词
