sledlike:/ˈslɛdlaɪk/| adj.|雪橇似的，像雪橇的|Resembling a sled; having the characteristics of a sled.  The word combines "sled" with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance.|例句：The long, low, sledlike vehicle sped across the ice.  这辆又长又矮，像雪橇一样的车辆在冰面上飞驰。 The children built a sledlike contraption out of cardboard boxes.孩子们用纸箱搭建了一个像雪橇一样的装置。|近义词：sleighlike, toboggan-like|反义词:  upright, vertical.
