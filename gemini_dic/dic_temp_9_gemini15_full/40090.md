containment:/kənˈteɪnmənt/| n. |遏制；控制；抑制；容纳；包围 |The act of keeping something harmful under control or within limits; the state of being contained.  It can also refer to the act of holding something within a space or boundary. |例句：The containment of the virus is the top priority.  病毒的遏制是重中之重。

The successful containment of the fire prevented further damage.  成功的火灾控制避免了进一步的损失。


近义词：control, restriction, confinement, limitation, enclosure |反义词：spread, escalation, release, expansion |词性: 名词
