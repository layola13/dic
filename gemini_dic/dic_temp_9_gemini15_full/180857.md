reheaters:/ˌriːˈhiːtərz/ | n. | 再加热器，回热器 | A device or system used to reheat something, especially steam or gases in a power plant or industrial process.  Reheaters are crucial components in various power generation systems, boosting efficiency by raising the temperature and pressure of the working fluid.  | Example: The power plant uses advanced reheaters to improve its overall thermal efficiency.  火力发电厂使用先进的再热器来提高其整体热效率。 |近义词：heater, superheater | 反义词：cooler
