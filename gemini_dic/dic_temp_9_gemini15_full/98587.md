institutor:/ˈɪnstɪˌtjuːtər/| n. |创立者；制定者；发起人 |One who establishes or founds something, especially an institution or system.  A person who initiates or sets up something. |例句：The institutor of the new program was praised for his vision.  这位新项目的创立者因其远见卓识而受到赞扬。|例句：He is considered the institutor of modern physics. 他被认为是现代物理学的奠基人。|近义词：founder, originator, establisher, initiator |反义词：destroyer, terminator, abolisher
