dosed:/doʊzd/| v.| 给…服药；使…充满；| to administer a dose of medicine to someone or something; to fill or saturate something |例句：The doctor dosed the patient with antibiotics. 医生给病人服用抗生素。|例句：The air was dosed with a strange perfume. 空气中弥漫着一种奇怪的香水味。|近义词：medicate, administer, saturate |反义词：withdraw, remove
