dentalization:/ˌdɛntəlɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |齿音化，齿化|The process of a sound becoming dental;  the articulation of a sound with the tongue against the teeth.  It's a phonetic process where a sound's place of articulation shifts towards the teeth. |Example: The /t/ in "eight" can sometimes undergo dentalization, becoming slightly more like a dental [t̪].  八这个单词中的/t/有时会发生齿音化，变得更像齿音[t̪]。|近义词：dentalization, dentization|反义词：palatalization
