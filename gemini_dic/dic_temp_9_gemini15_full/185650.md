reverencing: /ˈrɛvərənsɪŋ/ | v. | 尊敬；敬畏；崇拜 | Showing deep respect; honoring.  The act of showing reverence. | 例句：The students were reverencing their teacher for his wisdom and guidance. 学生们尊敬他们的老师，因为他拥有智慧和指导能力。

例句：She reverenced her ancestors by visiting their graves every year. 她每年都去祭拜祖先以示尊敬。


近义词：honoring, respecting, venerating, worshipping | 反义词：disrespecting, ignoring, insulting


USAGE->

He reverences his elders. = 他尊敬他的长辈。
They reverence the traditions of their culture. = 他们敬重他们文化的传统。
