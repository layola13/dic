It implies a lack of order, planning, or system in the way something is done.  | Example: His approach to the problem was nonmethodical and ultimately unproductive.  他的解决问题的方法很不系统，最终没有结果。  |近义词：unsystematic, haphazard, disorganized, chaotic, random | 反义词：methodical, systematic, organized, orderly
