pagodas:/pəˈɡoʊdəz/| n. |宝塔；佛塔 | A pagoda is a tiered tower, typically of wood or stone, with multiple eaves.  They are often associated with East Asian cultures, particularly China, Japan, Korea, and other parts of Southeast Asia.  Pagodas are used for various purposes, including religious worship, storing relics, and serving as landmarks. |例句：The ancient pagodas of Bagan stand as silent witnesses to a glorious past.  蒲甘的古老宝塔静静地见证着辉煌的过去。

The ornate pagodas along the river were breathtaking. 河边装饰华丽的宝塔令人叹为观止。|近义词：temple, shrine, stupa|反义词：None
