caldrons:/ˈkɔːldrənz/| n.|大锅；坩锅|A cauldron is a large, round pot, typically used for cooking over an open fire.  It is often associated with witchcraft and magic in literature and folklore.|例句：The witches stirred the potion in their bubbling cauldron. 女巫们在她们沸腾的大锅里搅拌着药剂。|例句：The blacksmith heated the metal in his cauldron before shaping it. 铁匠在塑形之前将金属加热在他的大锅里。|近义词：pot, kettle, boiler|反义词：small pot, cup
