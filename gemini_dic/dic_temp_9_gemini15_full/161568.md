potted:/ˈpɒtɪd/ /ˈpɑːtɪd/ | adj. | 盆栽的；罐装的；腌渍的 |  Having been planted in a pot; preserved in a jar or other container.  | 例句：He has a collection of potted plants on his balcony. 他阳台上有一盆盆栽植物。

例句：The shop sells a variety of potted herbs. 这家商店出售各种各样的盆栽香草。


近义词：planted (if referring to plants), canned (if referring to food), preserved


反义词：wild (if referring to plants), fresh (if referring to food)


词源:  From "pot" + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  The word reflects the action of placing something into a pot, whether a plant or food.
