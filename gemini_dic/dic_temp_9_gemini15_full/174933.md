radiale:/reɪdiˈɑːliː/|/ˈreɪdieɪl/| adj.|辐射的；径向的；轮辐状的|Relating to or resembling rays or radii; diverging from a common center.  The term is used in various scientific and technical fields to describe structures or patterns that spread outwards from a central point.|例句：The spokes of a bicycle wheel are arranged in a radiale pattern. 自行车车轮的辐条呈辐射状排列。|例句：The radiale symmetry of a starfish is striking. 海星的辐射对称性非常引人注目。|近义词：radial, radiating, ray-like|反义词：linear, centralized
