reign:/reɪn/ | n., v. |统治；朝代；支配；盛行|

英文解析：As a noun, reign refers to the period during which a monarch rules.  As a verb, it means to rule as a monarch or to be prevalent or dominant.

例句 (Noun):

The reign of Queen Victoria was a long and prosperous one.  维多利亚女王的统治时期漫长而繁荣。

His reign as CEO marked a significant turning point for the company. 他担任首席执行官的时期标志着公司的一个重要转折点。


例句 (Verb):

The king reigned for over fifty years. 国王统治了五十多年。

Peace reigned throughout the land.  全国一片祥和。


近义词 (Noun): rule, dominion, sovereignty, supremacy

近义词 (Verb): rule, govern, dominate, prevail


反义词 (Noun): anarchy, chaos, disorder

反义词 (Verb): to be overthrown, to be defeated


词性:Noun, Verb
