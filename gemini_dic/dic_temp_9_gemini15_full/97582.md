inhabit:/ɪnˈhæbɪt/ | vt. |居住，栖息|To live in or occupy (a place or environment).  |例句：Millions of people inhabit this vast city. 数百万的人居住在这个巨大的城市。
例句：Many different species of birds inhabit the rainforest. 许多不同种类的鸟类栖息在雨林中。|近义词：occupy, populate, dwell in |反义词：vacate, abandon, desert |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds inhabit the forest. = 鸟儿栖息在森林里。
They inhabit a small village. = 他们居住在一个小村庄里。


一般过去时:
The family inhabited the old house for many years. = 这家人居住在那栋老房子里很多年了。
Dinosaurs inhabited the Earth millions of years ago. = 恐龙在数百万年前居住在地球上。


现在完成时:
We have inhabited this apartment since last year. = 我们自从去年就住在这个公寓里了。
They have inhabited the island for generations. = 他们世世代代居住在这个岛屿上。


过去完成时:
Before the flood, the village had inhabited the riverbank for centuries. = 在洪水到来之前，这个村庄世世代代居住在河岸边。
The settlers had inhabited the land long before the arrival of the colonists. = 在殖民者到来之前，定居者们就早已居住在这片土地上了。


将来时:
We will inhabit a new house next year. = 我们明年将住进新房子。
They will inhabit the newly built apartments soon. = 他们很快就会住进新建成的公寓里。


虚拟语气:
If they inhabited a larger house, they would have more space. = 如果他们住在一所更大的房子里，他们就会有更多空间。
I wish we inhabited a place closer to the city. = 我希望我们住在一个离城市更近的地方。


被动语态:
The island was inhabited by indigenous people for centuries. = 这个岛屿几个世纪以来一直被土著居民居住。
This area is being inhabited by an increasing number of people. = 这个地区正在被越来越多的人居住。


疑问句:
Does anyone inhabit that remote island? = 有人居住在那个偏远的岛屿上吗？
Who inhabited this house before us? = 在我们之前谁住过这所房子？


否定句:
No one inhabits that abandoned building. = 没有人居住在那座废弃的建筑物里。
The island was not inhabited until the 18th century. = 直到18世纪，这个岛屿才有人居住。
