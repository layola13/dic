fictionalising: /ˈfɪkʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |虚构化；创作虚构故事|The act of making something fictional;  representing something as fiction.  It involves creating a narrative, characters, and events that are not based on reality, often drawing inspiration from real-life experiences or ideas. |例句：He was fictionalising his childhood experiences in his new novel. 他在他的新小说中虚构了他的童年经历。

He's fictionalising the events of the war for dramatic effect. 他为了戏剧效果而虚构了战争的事件。


近义词：romanticizing, fabulating, mythologizing |反义词：documenting, chronicling, reporting
