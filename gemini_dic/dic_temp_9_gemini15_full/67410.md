externalizes:/ˈɛkˌstɜːrnəlaɪzɪz/ | vt. |外部化；客观化；显现|To make something external or outward; to express or reveal one's inner feelings or thoughts outwardly.|例句：He externalizes his anger by shouting. 他通过大喊来发泄怒气。|例句：The company externalizes its IT services.  这家公司将其IT服务外包。|近义词：express, manifest, reveal, project |反义词：internalize, repress, suppress |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He externalizes his stress through exercise. = 他通过运动来释放压力。
She externalizes her emotions in her art. = 她通过艺术来表达自己的情感。


一般过去时:
He externalized his frustrations by quitting his job. = 他通过辞职来发泄自己的挫败感。
The artist externalized her inner turmoil in her latest painting. = 艺术家在她的最新画作中表达了她内心的骚动。


现在完成时:
She has externalized many of her childhood traumas through therapy. = 她通过治疗将许多童年创伤外部化了。
He has externalized his business operations to a third-party company. = 他已将其业务运营外包给第三方公司。


过去完成时:
They had externalized their anxieties before the presentation. = 他们在演示之前已经发泄了他们的焦虑。
The company had externalized its manufacturing process to a cheaper overseas supplier. = 公司已将其制造流程外包给一个更便宜的海外供应商。


将来时:
He will externalize his ideas in a new book. = 他将在一本新书中表达他的想法。
The company will externalize its customer service department. = 公司将把客户服务部门外包出去。


虚拟语气:
If he externalized his feelings more often, he wouldn't be so stressed. = 如果他能更经常地表达自己的感受，他就不会那么有压力了。
I wish I could externalize my creativity more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地表达我的创造力。


被动语态:
His anger was externalized through aggressive behavior. = 他的愤怒通过攻击性行为表现出来。
The company's IT infrastructure is being externalized to a cloud provider. = 公司的IT基础设施正在外包给云提供商。


疑问句:

否定句:
He doesn't externalize his emotions easily. = 他不容易表达自己的情绪。
The company does not externalize its research and development activities. = 公司不将其研发活动外包。
