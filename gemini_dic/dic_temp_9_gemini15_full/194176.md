self-invented:/ˌsɛlfɪnˈvɛntɪd/| adj.| 自创的；自行发明的 |  Something self-invented is created or devised by oneself, rather than being borrowed or adapted from elsewhere. It implies originality and independent creation. |  He used a self-invented method to solve the problem. 他用一种自创的方法解决了这个问题。|近义词：original, homemade, custom-made, self-devised | 反义词：borrowed, copied, adapted
