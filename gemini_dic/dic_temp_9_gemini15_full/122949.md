momma:/ˈmɑːmə/| n. |妈妈，妈咪 |An informal term for "mother," often used affectionately.  It's a common term of endearment, particularly used by children or in informal settings. |例句：The momma bear protected her cubs.  母熊保护着她的幼崽。|例句：Call your momma and tell her you're safe.  打电话给你妈妈告诉她你安全了。|近义词：mom, mother, mama|反义词：None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My momma cooks delicious meals. = 我的妈妈做饭很好吃。
Momma sings me lullabies every night. = 妈妈每天晚上都给我唱摇篮曲。

一般过去时:
Momma baked a cake for my birthday. = 妈妈为我的生日烤了一个蛋糕。
My momma helped me with my homework. = 我妈妈帮我做了作业。


现在完成时:
Momma has made so many sacrifices for me. = 妈妈为我做了很多牺牲。
My momma has always been there for me. = 我妈妈一直都在我身边。


过去完成时:
My momma had already left when I woke up. = 我醒来时，妈妈已经离开了。
I realized that momma had been right all along. = 我意识到妈妈一直是对的。


将来时:
