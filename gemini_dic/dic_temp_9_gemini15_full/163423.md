preconsultation: /ˌpriːkɒnsəlˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 预先咨询；术前咨询 | A consultation that takes place before a main consultation or procedure, often used to gather information, assess needs, and plan for the main consultation.  | Example: The preconsultation with the surgeon allowed me to ask questions and alleviate my anxiety before the operation.  | 近义词： preliminary consultation, prior consultation, initial assessment | 反义词：postconsultation
