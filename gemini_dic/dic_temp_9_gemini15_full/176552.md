re-sentence:/ˌriːˈsɛntəns/| vt.|重新措辞，改写句子|To rephrase or restructure a sentence; to express the same idea in a different way.|例句：I need to re-sentence this paragraph to make it clearer. 我需要改写这段话使其更清晰。|近义词：rephrase, reformulate, rewrite|反义词：leave unchanged


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-sentences his essays regularly. = 他定期改写他的论文。
She re-sentences every sentence for clarity. = 她为了清晰起见改写每个句子。


一般过去时:
The editor re-sentenced the awkward passage. = 编辑改写了那段别扭的文字。
He re-sentenced the entire chapter. = 他改写了整章内容。


现在完成时:
She has re-sentenced the introduction three times. = 她已经三次改写了引言。
They have re-sentenced the entire document to improve its flow. = 他们已经改写了整篇文档以改善其流畅性。


过去完成时:
He had re-sentenced the letter before sending it. = 他在寄出信之前已经改写了它。
She had re-sentenced the poem several times before she was satisfied. = 在她满意之前，她已经几次改写了这首诗。


将来时:

被动语态:
