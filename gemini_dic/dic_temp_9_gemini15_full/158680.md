plop:/plɒp/|/plop/| vt. n.|扑通一声落下；沉重地放下|To fall or drop heavily or suddenly, often with a soft thud.  The sound made by something falling heavily or suddenly. |例句：The book plopped onto the floor. 书扑通一声掉在地上。  The frog plopped into the pond. 青蛙扑通一声跳进池塘。|近义词：thump, thud, splash|反义词：float, drift
