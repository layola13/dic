influencing:/ˈɪnflʊənsɪŋ/| v.|影响；施加影响|The act of exerting influence or power over someone or something.  It suggests a process of affecting someone's opinions, behaviors, or actions.|例句：His speeches were incredibly influencing, swaying public opinion towards the war.|近义词：affecting, impacting, swaying, persuading|反义词：neglecting, ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The marketing campaign is influencing consumer behavior. = 营销活动正在影响消费者的行为。
His words are influencing her decision. = 他的话正在影响她的决定。

一般过去时:
The teacher's encouragement influenced his choice of career. = 老师的鼓励影响了他职业的选择。
The movie influenced my understanding of history. = 这部电影影响了我对历史的理解。

现在完成时:
The artist's work has influenced countless others. =这位艺术家的作品影响了无数其他人。
Recent events have influenced public opinion. = 近期事件影响了公众舆论。

过去完成时:
His childhood experiences had influenced his adult life. = 他童年的经历影响了他的成年生活。
The previous government's policies had influenced the current economic situation. = 上届政府的政策影响了当前的经济形势。

将来时:
This new technology will influence the way we work. = 这项新技术将影响我们的工作方式。
Future trends will influence the design of the product. = 未来趋势将影响产品的造型设计。

虚拟语气:
If he had listened to my advice, it might have influenced his decision. =如果他听了我的建议，那可能会影响他的决定。
I wish my opinion had influenced the outcome. =我希望我的意见能影响结果。


被动语态:
The decision was influenced by political pressures. = 这个决定受到了政治压力的影响。
Her opinion was influenced by her family. = 她的观点受到了家庭的影响。

疑问句:
What factors are influencing your decision? = 什么因素正在影响你的决定？
Is the weather influencing your plans? = 天气是否正在影响你的计划？

否定句:
The weather didn't influence our plans. = 天气没有影响我们的计划。
His arguments didn't influence the judge's decision. = 他的论点没有影响法官的判决。


PHRASE->
influencing factors = 影响因素
influencing people = 影响人们
influencing events = 影响事件
influencing behavior = 影响行为
strongly influencing = 强烈影响
