half-enforced:/ˌhɑːfɪnˈfɔːrst/| adj.| 半强制执行的，部分实施的 | Partially or incompletely enforced; implemented only to a limited extent.  |The half-enforced curfew led to widespread confusion and frustration. 半强制执行的宵禁导致了广泛的混乱和沮丧。 | partially enforced, loosely implemented | fully enforced, completely implemented | 形容词

This rule is half-enforced by the company. = 公司对这条规定执行不彻底。
