clarifier:/ˈklærɪfaɪər/| n. |澄清剂；净化器；阐明者 |A clarifier is a device or substance used to clarify something, typically a liquid, by removing impurities or sediment.  It can also refer to someone who clarifies something, making it easier to understand. |例句：The water treatment plant uses a clarifier to remove suspended solids from the water.  污水处理厂使用澄清器去除水中的悬浮固体。

The professor's explanation acted as a clarifier, resolving the students' confusion. 教授的解释起到了澄清作用，消除了学生的困惑。|近义词：purifier, filter, elucidator |反义词：obfuscator, confuser
