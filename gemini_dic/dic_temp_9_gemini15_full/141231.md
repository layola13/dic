ootype:/ˈoʊoʊtaɪp/| n. |卵巢型; 卵黄型|A structure or type of organ found in some female animals, specifically related to egg production or the formation of the egg shell.  It's often associated with the oviduct and plays a role in the maturation and deposition of eggs.|The ootype is a specialized structure within the female reproductive tract.|近义词：egg-laying organ, reproductive organ|反义词：无
