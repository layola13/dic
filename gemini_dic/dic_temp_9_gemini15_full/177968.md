reciprocals:/rɪˈsɪprəkəlz/ | n. |相互的，互惠的；倒数 |The relationship between two or more people or things that mutually affect each other.  Also refers to a mathematical concept where one number is the multiplicative inverse of another (a number multiplied by its reciprocal equals 1).| 例句：The two countries enjoyed reciprocal trade agreements.  = 这两个国家享有互惠贸易协定。

The reciprocal of 2 is 1/2. = 2 的倒数是 1/2。 |近义词：mutual, reciprocal, complementary |反义词：unilateral, one-sided |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The two countries enjoy reciprocal trade agreements. = 这两个国家享有互惠贸易协定。
They show reciprocal respect for each other's cultures. = 他们互相尊重彼此的文化。

一般过去时:
The companies had reciprocal business dealings. = 这些公司有过相互的业务往来。
They exchanged reciprocal gifts during the holidays. = 他们在假期互赠礼物。

现在完成时:
We have had many reciprocal exchanges of ideas. = 我们已经进行了许多相互的思想交流。
The two organizations have developed a reciprocal relationship. = 这两个组织已经建立了互惠关系。

过去完成时:
They had already established a reciprocal agreement before the negotiations began. = 谈判开始之前，他们已经建立了互惠协议。
The countries had enjoyed reciprocal diplomatic relations for many years. = 多年来，两国一直保持着互惠外交关系。

将来时:
The companies will establish reciprocal partnerships next year. = 公司明年将建立互惠伙伴关系。
We will explore reciprocal opportunities for collaboration. = 我们将探索互惠合作的机会。

虚拟语气:
If we had reciprocal trust, we could achieve more. = 如果我们相互信任，我们可以取得更多成就。
I wish we had reciprocal understanding. = 我希望我们有相互理解。

被动语态:
Reciprocal agreements were signed by both parties. = 双方签署了互惠协议。
Reciprocal benefits are expected from this collaboration. = 预计这次合作将带来互惠利益。

疑问句:
Are there any reciprocal benefits to this arrangement? = 这项安排有什么互惠的好处吗？
Do you believe in reciprocal relationships? = 你相信互惠关系吗？

否定句:
The relationship was not reciprocal; one party benefited more than the other. = 这段关系并非互惠的；一方受益多于另一方。
Their actions were not reciprocal to our efforts. = 他们的行为并没有回应我们的努力。

词源:
From Latin *reciprocus, from recipere "to receive, take back," from re- "back" + capere "to take."  The mathematical sense developed later, reflecting the idea of "mutual" exchange or inversion.
