nonnarcotics:/ˌnɒnˈnɑːrkɒtɪks/ | n. |非麻醉药 |Nonnarcotics are medications that do not have a narcotic effect, meaning they do not produce analgesia (pain relief) through opioid receptor binding.  They are often used to treat pain, inflammation, or other conditions, but without the risk of addiction or other side effects associated with narcotics. |例句：Many over-the-counter pain relievers are nonnarcotics. 许多非处方止痛药是非麻醉药。|近义词：analgesics (for pain relief), non-opioids|反义词：narcotics, opioids|名词
