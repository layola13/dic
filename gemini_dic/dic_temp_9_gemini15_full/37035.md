comanagers:/ˌkoʊˈmænɪdʒərz/ | n. |联席经理，共同经理 |Co-managers are two or more individuals who share the responsibilities of managing a team, department, or organization.  They work collaboratively to make decisions and achieve common goals. |例句：The project was overseen by two comanagers who worked closely together.  这个项目由两位密切合作的联席经理负责监督。


例句：The marketing team thrived under the leadership of its two comanagers.  在两位联席经理的领导下，营销团队蓬勃发展。


近义词：joint managers, co-directors, team leaders


反义词：sole manager, single manager


词性：名词
