petrary:/ˈpɛtrɛri/| n.|岩石收藏；岩石陈列室|A collection of rocks, often displayed in a museum or private collection.  It can also refer to a place where rocks are studied or displayed.|The museum's petrary boasts a stunning collection of fossilized specimens.|近义词：rock collection, lithotheca|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a collection of something else, e.g., a numismatic collection.|名词
