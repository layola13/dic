chop-down:/tʃɑːp daʊn/ | vt. |砍倒，砍伐|To cut down (a tree or other plant) with an axe or similar tool.  This phrase emphasizes the action of felling something, usually a tree, completely to the ground.|例句：The lumberjacks chopped down the giant redwood.  伐木工砍倒了巨大的红杉树。
They decided to chop down the old apple tree because it was diseased. 他们决定砍掉那棵老苹果树，因为它生病了。|近义词：fell, cut down |反义词：plant, cultivate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They chop down trees every day. = 他们每天都砍树。
The farmer chops down the weeds regularly. = 农民定期砍除杂草。

一般过去时:
The storm chopped down many trees. = 暴风雨刮倒了许多树木。
He chopped down the old oak tree last week. = 他上周砍倒了那棵古老的橡树。

现在完成时:
They have chopped down all the trees in the forest. = 他们已经砍倒了森林里所有的树木。
The workers have chopped down many trees this year. = 工人们今年已经砍倒了许多树木。

过去完成时:
They had chopped down most of the trees before the rain started. = 下雨之前他们已经砍倒了大部分树木。
The construction crew had chopped down the trees before they started building. = 建筑队在开始施工之前已经砍倒了树木。

将来时:
We will chop down the dead branches tomorrow. = 我们明天将砍掉枯枝。
The city will chop down the trees to widen the road. = 城市将砍伐树木以拓宽道路。

虚拟语气:
If I had an axe, I would chop down that tree. = 如果我有把斧头，我会砍掉那棵树。
I wish they hadn't chopped down that beautiful oak. = 我真希望他们没有砍掉那棵美丽的橡树。

被动语态:
Many trees were chopped down during the hurricane. = 飓风期间许多树木被砍倒。
The old tree was chopped down to make way for a new building. = 老树被砍倒为新建筑让路。

疑问句:
Will they chop down the trees near the river? = 他们会砍伐河边的树木吗？
Have they already chopped down the diseased tree? = 他们已经砍掉那棵病树了吗？

否定句:
We didn't chop down any trees today. = 我们今天没有砍任何树。
They will not chop down the trees without permission. = 他们未经许可不会砍树。
