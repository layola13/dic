nonesuch:/ˈnʌnsʌtʃ/| n. |无与伦比的人或物；绝无仅有之物 |Something or someone without equal; something unparalleled.  The word implies that there is nothing comparable to the subject being described. |例句：This cake is a nonesuch; I've never tasted anything so delicious. 这蛋糕是无与伦比的；我从未尝过如此美味的东西。


例句：His performance was a nonesuch, setting a new standard for excellence. 他的表演是无与伦比的，为卓越树立了新的标准。


近义词： paragon, peerless, unparalleled, matchless, unsurpassed


反义词： commonplace, ordinary, mediocre, inferior


词性:noun
