share-out:/ʃeəraʊt/| vt. n.|分配，分发；分享|To divide something among several people.  The act of dividing something among several people.|例句：The teacher shared out the sweets among the children.老师把糖果分给了孩子们。

例句：They shared out the profits equally among the partners.他们平均分配了合伙人的利润。


近义词：distribute, allocate, divide, apportion|反义词：hoard, keep, retain|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They share out the work fairly. = 他们公平地分配工作。
We share out the responsibilities equally. = 我们平均分配责任。


一般过去时:
The manager shared out the bonuses last week. = 经理上周发了奖金。
