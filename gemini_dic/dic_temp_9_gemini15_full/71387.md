flagon:/ˈflæɡən/| n.|酒瓶；大酒壶|A flagon is a type of container, typically made of glass or metal, used for holding and serving alcoholic beverages, especially wine or ale.  It is usually larger than a bottle and often has a handle.|He poured ale from a flagon into two tankards.|近义词：bottle, jug, flask|反义词：None
