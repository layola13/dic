non-disclosure: /ˌnɒn.dɪˈskləʊʒər/ | n. | 不公开；保密 | The act of not revealing something; secrecy.  It often refers to agreements that legally bind parties to keep certain information confidential. | Example:  The non-disclosure agreement prevented the employee from sharing the company's trade secrets.  员工的保密协议阻止了他泄露公司的商业秘密。| 近义词：secrecy, confidentiality, concealment | 反义词：disclosure, revelation |  名词
