publish:/ˈpʌblɪʃ/| vt. |出版，发表，公布|To make (a book, article, etc.) available to the public by printing or electronic means.  It also means to make something known publicly.|例句：The author finally published his novel after years of hard work. 作者经过多年的努力终于出版了他的小说。

例句：The university published the research findings in a prestigious journal.  大学在一个著名的期刊上发表了研究结果。


近义词：issue, release, announce, disseminate |反义词：suppress, withhold, conceal |词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company publishes several books each year. = 这家公司每年出版几本书。
She publishes her blog posts weekly. = 她每周发表她的博客文章。


一般过去时:
They published the news immediately. = 他们立即发布了这个消息。
He published his first poem at the age of 16. = 他16岁时发表了他的第一首诗。


现在完成时:
The author has published three novels so far. =这位作者迄今为止已经出版了三部小说。
They have published the results of the study online. = 他们已将研究结果在线发布。


过去完成时:
She had published several articles before she got her doctorate. = 在获得博士学位之前，她已经发表了几篇文章。
The newspaper had published the story before the police could intervene. = 在警方介入之前，报纸已经刊登了这个故事。


将来时:
We will publish the report next month. = 我们下个月将发布报告。
The band will publish their new album in the spring. = 乐队将在春天发行他们的新专辑。


被动语态:
The book was published in 2023. = 这本书于2023年出版。
The news was published widely across all major media outlets.  = 这则新闻在所有主要媒体上广泛传播。


疑问句:
When will the article be published? = 这篇文章什么时候发表？
Has the journal published your research yet? = 该期刊是否已经发表了你的研究？


否定句:
They did not publish the results of the experiment. = 他们没有公布实验结果。
The newspaper refused to publish the controversial article. = 报纸拒绝发表这篇有争议的文章。


PHRASE->
publish a book = 出版一本书
publish an article = 发表一篇文章
publish findings = 公布调查结果
publish online = 在线发布
self-publish = 自我出版
