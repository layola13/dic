pedagogues:/ˈpɛdəɡɔːɡz/| n. |教育家；教师；训导者 |A pedagogue is a teacher, especially one who is strict or dogmatic in their methods.  The term often carries a slightly negative connotation, suggesting an overly formal or didactic approach to teaching. | Example:  The old pedagogue insisted on rote learning.  这位老教师坚持死记硬背。  The renowned pedagogue revolutionized the way children learned mathematics.  这位著名的教育家彻底改变了孩子们学习数学的方式。|近义词：teacher, educator, instructor, tutor |反义词：student, pupil
