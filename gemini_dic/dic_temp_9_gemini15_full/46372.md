daddies:/ˈdædiz/| n.|爸爸们，父亲们|The plural form of "daddy," referring to multiple fathers or father figures.  It can also be used informally and affectionately to refer to a group of men.|例句：The daddies were watching their children play in the park.  爸爸们正在看着他们的孩子在公园里玩耍。|例句：Those daddies are really cool.|那些爸爸们真的很酷。|近义词：fathers, papas, pops|反义词：无
