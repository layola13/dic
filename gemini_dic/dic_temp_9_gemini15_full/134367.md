nonintermittent: /ˌnɒnɪnˈtɜːmɪtənt/ | adj. |非间歇的，连续的|  Not intermittent; continuous or uninterrupted.  The word is formed by combining "non-" (meaning "not") with "intermittent" (meaning "occurring at irregular intervals").|


例句：The machine ran nonintermittent for twenty-four hours.  这台机器连续运转了二十四小时。

The nonintermittent rain caused flooding in the low-lying areas.  持续不断的降雨导致低洼地区发生洪水。


近义词：continuous, uninterrupted, constant, unceasing, steady


反义词：intermittent, sporadic, occasional, irregular


词性：形容词
