floggers:/ˈflɒɡərz/ | n. |鞭打者；（尤指）虐待狂；（网络用语）过度使用鞭打主题的博主或内容创作者|  A person who flogs (whips or beats) someone or something; especially, a person who derives sexual gratification from inflicting pain through whipping or beating. In internet slang, it refers to people who excessively use whipping themes in their blogs or content creation.| 例句：The floggers were arrested for animal cruelty.  鞭打者因虐待动物而被捕。

The online floggers' content is considered by many to be disturbing and inappropriate. 网络上那些过度使用鞭打主题的博主的内容被许多人认为是令人不安和不合适的。|近义词：whippers, abusers, sadists|反义词：caretakers, protectors|名词
