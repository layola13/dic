noncommittal: /ˌnɒnkəˈmɪtəl/ | adj. | 不承担责任的；不表态的；含糊其辞的 |  Not expressing or revealing commitment to a particular opinion or course of action.  |

例句：He gave a noncommittal answer to the reporter's question. 他对记者的提问含糊其辞。

例句：Her noncommittal expression gave no indication of her feelings. 她不置可否的表情没有流露出她的感情。


近义词：uncommitted, vague, ambiguous, equivocal, uncertain, hesitant

反义词：committal, definite, explicit, clear, unequivocal


词性：形容词
