inexplosive:/ˌɪnɪkˈspləʊsɪv/| adj. |非爆炸性的，不爆炸的| Not explosive; not tending to explode.  The controlled burn was designed to be inexplosive and safe for the surrounding environment.|近义词：nonexplosive, safe, harmless |反义词：explosive, volatile, dangerous

例句：

The new chemical compound was found to be inexplosive under normal conditions. = 新的化合物在正常条件下被发现是非爆炸性的。

The engineers designed an inexplosive demolition technique to minimize damage to the surrounding buildings. = 工程师们设计了一种非爆炸性拆除技术，以最大限度地减少对周围建筑物的破坏。
