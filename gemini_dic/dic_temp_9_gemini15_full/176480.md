re-pinning:/ˌriːˈpɪnɪŋ/| v.|重新别上，重新固定 |The act of pinning something again, often after it has come undone or needs to be repositioned.  It implies a previous pinning action. |例句：She spent the morning re-pinning her collection of vintage postcards. = 她花了整个上午重新别上她收藏的复古明信片。

例句：The tailor had to re-pin the garment several times to achieve the perfect fit. =裁缝不得不几次重新别上衣服才能达到完美的合身效果。


近义词：refastening, reattaching, securing


反义词：unpinning, detaching, removing


词性:动词
