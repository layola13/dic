paraldehyde:/ˈpærælˌdaɪd/ | n. |巴拉醛|A colorless, volatile liquid with a characteristic pungent odor, used as a hypnotic and sedative. It is a cyclic trimer of acetaldehyde. |Paraldehyde is an effective sedative, but its use has declined due to side effects. 巴拉醛是一种有效的镇静剂，但由于其副作用，其使用已减少。|近义词：hypnotic, sedative |反义词：stimulant
