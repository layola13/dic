comparing:/kəmˈpɛərɪŋ/ | v. |比较，对照 |The act of examining two or more things to identify similarities and differences.  It often involves judging the relative value or importance of the things being compared. |例句：She spent the morning comparing prices at different stores. 她上午花时间在不同的商店比较价格。

例句：Comparing the two paintings reveals subtle differences in brushstrokes.  比较这两幅画揭示了笔触上的细微差别。


近义词：contrasting, differentiating, juxtaposing | 反义词：None (Antonyms depend on the context. For example, if comparing implies finding similarities, the antonym might be ignoring differences.) | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are comparing apples and oranges. = 他们在比较苹果和橙子。
She is comparing her two job offers. = 她正在比较她的两个工作机会。


一般过去时:
He compared the two poems. = 他比较了两首诗。
We compared notes after the exam. = 考试后我们互相交流了一下。


现在完成时:
I have compared several different models. = 我已经比较了几种不同的型号。
She has compared her salary with her colleagues'. = 她已经把她自己的工资和同事们的工资做了比较。


过去完成时:
They had compared their results before presenting their findings. = 他们在提交研究结果之前已经比较了他们的结果。
He had compared various solutions before choosing the best one. = 他在选择最佳方案之前已经比较了各种方案。


将来时:
We will compare the data later. = 我们稍后会比较数据。
She will compare the cost-effectiveness of different methods. = 她将比较不同方法的成本效益。


虚拟语气:
If I were to compare them, I would say this one is better. = 如果我要比较它们，我会说这个更好。
I wish I had compared more options before making my decision. = 我希望在做决定之前我能够比较更多的选择。


被动语态:
The two products are being compared in the advertisement. = 这两种产品正在广告中被比较。
The results were compared by an independent team. = 结果由一个独立的团队进行了比较。


疑问句:
Are you comparing me to your ex? = 你是在拿我和你的前任比较吗？
How do you compare these two approaches? = 你如何比较这两种方法？


否定句:
He didn't compare his work to others'. = 他没有把他的作品与别人的作品进行比较。
They did not compare the data accurately. = 他们没有准确地比较数据。
