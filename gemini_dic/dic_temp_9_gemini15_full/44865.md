crux:/krʌks/| n. |关键，核心，症结|The crux of the matter is that we need more money.  事情的关键是我们需要更多的钱。|近义词：core, essence, heart, gist |反义词：superficiality, periphery


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The crux of the problem is its complexity. = 问题的关键在于它的复杂性。
What is the crux of his argument? = 他论点的核心是什么？


一般过去时:
The crux of the debate was the funding issue. = 辩论的关键是资金问题。
What was the crux of their disagreement? = 他们分歧的关键是什么？


现在完成时:
The crux of the matter has always been a lack of communication. = 问题的关键一直是缺乏沟通。
What has been the crux of your difficulties? = 你的困难的关键是什么？


过去完成时:
The crux of the situation had been unclear until now. = 直到现在，情况的关键才变得清晰。
What had been the crux of the conflict before the intervention? = 干预之前冲突的关键是什么？


将来时:
The crux of the negotiations will be the price. = 谈判的关键将是价格。
What will be the crux of the upcoming election? = 下届选举的关键是什么？


虚拟语气:
If only we knew the crux of the problem, we could solve it easily. = 如果我们知道问题的关键，就能很容易地解决它。
I wish I had known the crux of the matter earlier. = 我希望我早点知道事情的关键。


被动语态:
The crux of the issue was poorly explained in the report. = 报告中对问题的关键解释得很糟糕。
The crux of the decision is being carefully considered. = 正在仔细考虑决定的关键。


疑问句:

否定句:
