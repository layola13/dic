eligibility:/ˌɛlɪdʒəˈbɪləti/| n. |资格；合格；符合条件|The state or quality of being eligible; fitness or worthiness to be chosen or selected.|The eligibility criteria for the scholarship are quite strict.  奖学金的资格标准非常严格。|He questioned the eligibility of the candidate. 他质疑该候选人的资格。|qualification, suitability|ineligibility, disqualification


USAGE->
