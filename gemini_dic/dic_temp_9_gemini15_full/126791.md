It's a very short unit of time used in measuring the speed of electronic processes and other extremely fast events. | Example: The computer chip processed the data in a few nanoseconds.  计算机芯片在几纳秒内处理了数据。  The laser pulse lasted only a few nanoseconds. 激光脉冲只持续了几纳秒。|近义词：microsecond (though much longer)|反义词：eons, millennia (though much longer)|名词
