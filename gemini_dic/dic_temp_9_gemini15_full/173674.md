quasi-liberal:/ˈkwɑːzi ˈlɪbərəl/ | adj. |准自由主义的，似自由主义的 |  Describes something that resembles or partially embodies liberal principles but does not fully adhere to them; often implies a compromise or inconsistency in the application of liberal ideals. It suggests a position that is close to, but not identical with, classical liberalism.  | Example:  The government's policies, while labeled "quasi-liberal," still maintained significant restrictions on individual freedoms.  政府的政策，虽然被贴上“准自由主义”的标签，但仍然对个人自由施加了相当大的限制。|近义词：semi-liberal, nominally liberal |反义词：authoritarian, conservative
