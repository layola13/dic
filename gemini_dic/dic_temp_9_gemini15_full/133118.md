nonescalating: /ˌnɒnˈɛskəleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不升级的，未升级的 | Not increasing in intensity or severity.  This word describes a situation or process that is not getting worse or more intense. It implies a state of stability or even improvement, in contrast to escalation. | Example: The situation remained nonescalating, despite the initial tensions.  情况尽管一开始很紧张，但仍然没有升级。  The negotiations maintained a nonescalating dialogue. 谈判保持着不升级的对话。| 近义词：stable, unchanging, steady, controlled | 反义词：escalating, intensifying, worsening, deteriorating
