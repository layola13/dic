continued:/kənˈtɪnjuːd/ | adj. | 继续的；延续的 | Continuing; prolonged; uninterrupted.  |例句：The meeting continued for another hour. 会议又持续了一个小时。 |例句：The rain continued throughout the night. 雨整夜不停地下着。|近义词：prolonged, extended, ongoing | 反义词：ended, finished, ceased |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The work continues daily. = 工作每天都在继续。
The show continues to be popular. = 这部剧依然很受欢迎。

一般过去时:
The rain continued all afternoon. = 雨持续了整个下午。
The game continued until nightfall. = 游戏持续到夜幕降临。

现在完成时:
The project has continued for several months. = 这个项目已经持续了几个月。
The drought has continued for years. = 干旱已经持续多年。

过去完成时:
The strike had continued for weeks before it was settled. = 罢工持续了几周才解决。
The problem had continued since the beginning. = 这个问题从一开始就一直存在。

将来时:
The concert will continue until midnight. = 音乐会将持续到午夜。
The negotiations will continue next week. = 谈判将在下周继续进行。

虚拟语气:
If the rain continued, we would stay inside. = 如果雨继续下，我们就待在屋里。
I wish the good weather continued forever. = 我希望好天气永远持续下去。

被动语态:
The meeting was continued after a short break. = 短暂休息后会议继续进行。
The project is continued by a new team. = 项目由一个新的团队继续进行。

疑问句:
Does the meeting continue today? = 会议今天继续吗？
Will the work continue tomorrow? = 工作明天会继续吗？

否定句:
The movie didn't continue after the intermission. = 电影中场休息后没有继续。
The rain did not continue for long. = 雨没有持续很久。


PHRASE->
continued success = 持续的成功
continued efforts = 持续的努力
continued growth = 持续的增长
continued improvement = 持续的改进
continued support = 持续的支持
