psychogenesis:/ˌsaɪkoʊˈdʒɛnəsɪs/ | n. |心理成因；精神发生；心理起源 | The origination of a physical or mental disorder in the mind.  It refers to the development of a condition or illness caused by psychological factors, rather than purely biological or physical ones.  Psychogenesis emphasizes the role of the mind in shaping health and illness. | Example:  The doctor suspected a psychogenic cause for the patient's paralysis, as there was no neurological evidence of damage.  医生怀疑患者瘫痪的原因是心理因素，因为没有神经学证据表明受损。


|近义词: psychosomatic origin, mental causation |反义词:somatogenesis, biological causation |词性: noun
