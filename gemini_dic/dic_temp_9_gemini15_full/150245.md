panderisms:/ˈpændərɪzəm/| n.|迎合；逢迎；讨好；谄媚|The act of pandering; excessive or blatant attempts to please or win favor, often at the expense of integrity or principle.  It often refers to the use of simplistic, clichéd, or sensationalized material designed to appeal to lowbrow tastes.|例句：The politician's speech was full of panderisms aimed at appealing to the lowest common denominator.  这位政治家的演讲充满了迎合大众低级趣味的谄媚之词。|近义词：appeasement,  fawning,  toadyism,  kowtowing|反义词：integrity,  principle,  honesty
