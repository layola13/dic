renounced:/rɪˈnaʊnst/| vt.|放弃，抛弃，宣布放弃|To give something up, especially a claim, right, or privilege; to formally reject or disavow a belief, cause, or allegiance.  |例句：He renounced his claim to the throne. 他放弃了他对王位的继承权。

例句：She renounced her faith in the church. 她放弃了她对教会的信仰。

近义词：abandon, relinquish, forgo, repudiate, abjure

反义词：accept, embrace, retain, uphold

一般现在时:
He renounces his citizenship every year. = 他每年都放弃他的国籍。
They renounce violence as a means of conflict resolution. = 他们放弃暴力作为冲突解决的手段。

一般过去时:
She renounced her inheritance after a bitter family dispute. = 在激烈的家庭纠纷后，她放弃了她的继承权。
He publicly renounced his support for the controversial policy. = 他公开放弃了他对这一有争议政策的支持。

现在完成时:
He has renounced his past life of crime. = 他已经放弃了他过去犯罪的生活。
The organization has renounced all ties to extremist groups. = 该组织已经放弃了与极端组织的所有联系。

过去完成时:
She had renounced her beliefs before she met him. = 在遇到他之前，她已经放弃了她的信仰。
They had renounced their allegiance to the king before the revolution. = 他们在革命之前就已经放弃了对国王的效忠。

将来时:
He will renounce his title when he retires. = 他退休时将放弃他的头衔。
She will renounce her membership if the organization doesn't change its policies. = 如果该组织不改变其政策，她将放弃其会员资格。

虚拟语气:
If he renounced his position, he would avoid the scandal. = 如果他放弃他的职位，他就能避免丑闻。
I wish he had renounced his harmful habits earlier. = 我希望他早点放弃他那些有害的习惯。

被动语态:
His citizenship was renounced due to his criminal activities. = 由于他的犯罪活动，他的国籍被剥夺了。
His claim to the property was renounced by the court. = 法院放弃了他对该财产的所有权。

疑问句:

Has she renounced her claim to the inheritance? = 她放弃了她对遗产的继承权了吗？

否定句:
He did not renounce his position despite the mounting pressure. = 尽管压力越来越大，但他并没有放弃他的职位。
She has not renounced her commitment to the cause. = 她并没有放弃她对事业的承诺。
