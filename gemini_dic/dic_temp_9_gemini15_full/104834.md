keelage:/ˈkiːlɪdʒ/| n. |龙骨费；船底费；搁浅费|The charge made for the use of a keel in a harbor or river.  It is a fee paid for the privilege of using a keel to support a vessel in shallow waters or on a riverbed.|例句：The keelage fee was quite high in that port.  那个港口的龙骨费相当高。|例句：They had to pay significant keelage to keep their ship afloat in the shallows. 他们在浅滩上不得不支付大量的龙骨费来保持他们的船只漂浮。|近义词：dockage, wharfage|反义词：N/A
