galosh:/ɡəˈlɒʃ/ /ɡəˈlɑːʃ/| n.| 胶鞋；套鞋 | Overshoes, typically made of rubber, worn over shoes to protect them from water and mud.  |例句：I wore my galoshes to keep my feet dry in the rain. 我穿着胶鞋以防止雨天淋湿脚。  |例句：She put on her galoshes before going out in the snow.  下雪前她穿上了胶鞋。|近义词：overshoes, rubbers, boots (in some contexts)|反义词：None specifically, but could be considered opposite to sandals or other open footwear. |名词
