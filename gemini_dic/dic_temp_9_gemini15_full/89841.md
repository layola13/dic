hornlessness:/ˈhɔːrn.ləsnəs/| n. |无角；无角性状|The absence of horns in an animal.  It's a genetic trait found in some breeds of cattle and goats. |例句：The hornlessness of the polled Hereford cattle is a desirable trait.  无角的赫里福德牛的无角性状是一个理想的性状。

例句：Hornlessness is a recessive gene in many cattle breeds.  在许多牛品种中，无角性状是隐性基因。
