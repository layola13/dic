catcalls:/ˈkætˌkɔːlz/| n.| 嘘声；口哨声；调戏(尤指对女性的性骚扰)|Loud, rude, and often sexual comments shouted at someone, especially a woman in public.  Catcalls are a form of street harassment. |例句：She was subjected to a barrage of catcalls as she walked down the street. = 她走在街上时遭到一阵阵的嘘声和调戏。
例句：The campaign aims to end catcalls and other forms of gender-based street harassment. = 该运动旨在结束嘘声和其他形式的基于性别的街头骚扰。|近义词：harassment, heckling, jeers, whistles, shouts|反义词：praise, compliments
