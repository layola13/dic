preparedness:/prɪˈpɛərnəs/| n. |准备状态；防备；警惕性|The state of being prepared for something, especially something unpleasant or dangerous.  It involves having the necessary resources, plans, and skills to deal with a situation effectively.|例句：The government's preparedness for the hurricane was inadequate. 政府对飓风的防范准备不足。|例句：Good preparedness is essential for success in any endeavor.  充分的准备对于任何事业的成功都至关重要。|近义词：readiness, readiness, preparation|反义词：unpreparedness, negligence
