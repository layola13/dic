decivilize:/ˌdiːˈsɪvəlaɪz/| vt.|使野蛮化；使粗鲁化；使堕落|To deprive of the characteristics of civilization; to make or become uncivilized or barbarous.  The war decivilized the country, plunging it into chaos and brutality.|近义词：barbarianize, brutalize, coarsen|反义词：civilize, refine, cultivate|动词

They decivilize the society with their actions. = 他们的行为使社会野蛮化。
He decivilizes himself by neglecting his manners. = 他忽略礼仪，使自己变得粗鲁。


一般过去时:
