decompiles:/ˌdiːˈkɒmpaɪlz/ | /ˌdiːˈkɑːmpaɪlz/ | vt. |反编译|To translate machine code or bytecode back into a higher-level programming language, such as Java or C++.  This process is the reverse of compilation. |例句：The programmer decompiled the software to understand its functionality. 程序员反编译了软件以了解其功能。|例句：He used a decompiler to reverse-engineer the proprietary software. 他使用反编译器来逆向工程专有软件。|近义词：disassemble, reverse-engineer |反义词：compile
