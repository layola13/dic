crustation:/krʌsˈteɪʃən/| n. |甲壳类动物|A crustacean is an aquatic invertebrate animal of the large group Crustacea, which includes crabs, lobsters, shrimps, and barnacles. They are characterized by their hard exoskeleton, segmented bodies, and jointed appendages.|例句：Crabs, lobsters, and shrimps are all examples of crustaceans.  螃蟹、龙虾和虾都是甲壳类动物的例子。|例句：The study focused on the reproductive behavior of a specific species of crustacean. 这项研究侧重于特定甲壳类动物的繁殖行为。|近义词： shellfish, arthropod |反义词：vertebrate
