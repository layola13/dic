manipulates:/məˈnɪpjʊleɪts/ | vt. |操纵；控制；操作；篡改 |To control or influence (a person or situation) cleverly or unscrupulously.  To handle or operate (a mechanism, process, etc.). |例句：He skillfully manipulates public opinion. 他巧妙地操纵舆论。

The dictator manipulates the country's economy for personal gain. 独裁者操纵国家经济以谋取私利。


近义词：control, influence, manage, exploit, engineer | 反义词：aid, assist, help


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He manipulates the data to support his theory. 他操纵数据以支持他的理论。
She manipulates the puppets with great skill. 她熟练地操纵木偶。


一般过去时:
The magician manipulated the cards expertly. 魔术师熟练地变戏法。
They manipulated the evidence to frame the innocent man. 他们篡改证据陷害无辜者。


现在完成时:
He has manipulated the market for years. 他多年来一直在操纵市场。
She has manipulated her way to the top of the company. 她通过手段爬到了公司的高层。


过去完成时:
They had manipulated the accounts before the audit. 他们在审计之前就已篡改了账目。
He had manipulated the situation to his advantage. 他已经操纵局势以利于自己。


将来时:
She will manipulate the robot arm to perform the task. 她将操纵机械臂执行任务。
They will manipulate the stock prices to make a profit. 他们将操纵股价以获利。


虚拟语气:
If he manipulated the system, he would be caught. 如果他操纵系统，他会被抓住。
I wish I hadn't manipulated the situation. 我真希望我没有操纵局面。


被动语态:
The data was manipulated to fit the results. 数据被操纵以符合结果。
The machine is manipulated by a remote control. 机器由遥控器操纵。


疑问句:
Who manipulated the evidence? 谁篡改了证据？
How did he manipulate the situation to his advantage? 他是如何操纵局势以利于自己的？


否定句:
He did not manipulate the results. 他没有操纵结果。
She did not manipulate the stock market. 她没有操纵股市。


PHRASE->
manipulate data 数据操纵
manipulate the market 操纵市场
manipulate the system 操纵系统
manipulate public opinion 操纵舆论
skillfully manipulate 熟练地操纵

词源:
From Late Latin *manipulare "to handle," from manus "hand" + -pulare (suffix of frequentative verbs).  The meaning evolved from the literal handling of objects to the figurative control of people or situations.
