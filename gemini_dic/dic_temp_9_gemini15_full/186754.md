rigs:/rɪɡz/| n. vt.|钻井平台；装备；操纵；|英文解析：A rig is a structure used for drilling for oil or gas, or a complex system of equipment or tools.  As a verb, it means to manipulate or control something in a dishonest or unfair way.|例句：The oil rig was a massive structure out in the ocean.  这口海上油井是一个巨大的建筑物。They rigged the election to ensure their candidate won.他们操纵了选举以确保他们的候选人获胜。|近义词：platform, apparatus, manipulate, fix|反义词：dismantle, disarm, honestly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rigs the fishing boat every morning. 他每天早上都装备渔船。
They rig the sails before setting sail. 他们起航前先调整帆。


一般过去时:
The company rigged the accounting to hide its losses. 公司操纵账目以掩盖其损失。
She rigged up a makeshift shelter from the rain. 她用简易材料搭建了一个避雨棚。


现在完成时:
They have rigged the voting machines multiple times. 他们多次操纵了投票机。
He has rigged the game by cheating. 他通过作弊操纵了比赛。


过去完成时:
The police discovered that the suspects had rigged the evidence. 警方发现嫌疑人伪造了证据。
They had rigged the system before the investigation began.  他们在调查开始之前就操纵了系统。


将来时:
They will rig the lights for the concert. 他们将为音乐会安装灯光。
We will rig the tent in the backyard. 我们将在后院搭帐篷。


虚拟语气:
If they rigged the election, it would be a scandal. 如果他们操纵了选举，那将是一场丑闻。
I wish they hadn't rigged the competition. 我真希望他们没有操纵比赛。


被动语态:
The game was rigged from the beginning.  比赛从一开始就被操纵了。
The boat is being rigged for the upcoming race.  船正在为即将到来的比赛做准备。


疑问句:
Was the competition rigged? 比赛被操纵了吗？
Are they rigging the election? 他们在操纵选举吗？


否定句:
They did not rig the results. 他们没有操纵结果。
She didn't rig the test, she simply studied hard. 她没有作弊考试，她只是努力学习。
