cartons:/ˈkɑːrtənz/ | n. |纸箱；盒；卡通片 | A carton is a container, usually made of cardboard, used for packaging goods.  It can also refer to a container for liquids, like milk or juice. In some contexts, it can refer to a collection of cartoons or comic strips. |例句：He packed the books in several sturdy cartons. 他把书装进几个结实的纸箱里。

例句：The milk came in a carton. 牛奶装在纸盒里。


近义词：box, container, package | 反义词：None  | 词性：名词
