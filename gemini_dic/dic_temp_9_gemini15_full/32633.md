chisel:/ˈtʃɪzəl/| n. vt.|凿子；雕刻|A chisel is a tool with a sharp, flat edge used for shaping wood, stone, or metal by cutting.|例句：He used a chisel to carve the statue. 他用凿子雕刻雕像。|近义词：gouge, graver|反义词：hammer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chisels the wood. = 他在雕刻木头。
The sculptor chisels the marble. = 雕塑家正在雕刻大理石。

She chiseled the ice. = 她凿了冰。
