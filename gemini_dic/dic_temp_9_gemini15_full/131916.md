noncontinency:/ˌnɑːnkənˈtɪn(d)ʒənsi/| n. |非连续性；不连续性 |The lack of continuity or connection; discontinuity.  Noncontinency refers to a situation where there is a break or gap in something that is usually continuous, such as a process, a sequence of events, or a spatial arrangement. It implies a lack of coherence or connection between parts. | Example: The noncontinency of the geological strata suggests a period of significant upheaval. 地质层的非连续性表明存在一段重大变革时期。|近义词：discontinuity, interruption, break, gap |反义词：continuity, coherence, consistency |名词
