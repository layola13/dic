formulates:/ˈfɔːrmjuleɪts/ | vt. | 制定；构想；表达 | To create or develop (a plan, system, etc.) in a detailed and organized way.  | 例句：The committee formulated a new policy to address the issue.  委员会制定了一项新政策来解决这个问题。

例句：She carefully formulated her response to the difficult question. 她仔细地构思了她对这个难题的回答。


近义词：develop, devise, create, frame, design | 反义词：dismantle, destroy, abolish | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scientist formulates a new hypothesis. = 科学家制定了一个新的假设。
She formulates her thoughts carefully before speaking. = 她说话前会仔细地组织自己的思绪。


一般过去时:
The government formulated a new economic plan. = 政府制定了一个新的经济计划。
He formulated his ideas into a coherent argument. = 他将自己的想法构成了一个连贯的论点。


现在完成时:
They have formulated a comprehensive strategy. = 他们已经制定了一个全面的战略。
The company has formulated a new marketing campaign. = 公司已经制定了一个新的营销方案。


过去完成时:
She had formulated her response before he even finished asking the question. = 在他甚至问完问题之前，她就已构思好了她的回答。
The team had formulated a plan, but it was ultimately rejected. = 团队已经制定了一个计划，但最终被否决了。


将来时:
The researchers will formulate a new experimental design. = 研究人员将制定一个新的实验设计。
We will formulate our response after reviewing all the evidence. = 我们将在审查所有证据后制定我们的回应。


虚拟语气:
If I were to formulate a solution, I would start by... = 如果我要制定一个解决方案，我会从……开始。
I wish I had formulated my argument more clearly. = 我希望我当时能更清晰地表达我的论点。


被动语态:
A new policy was formulated by the committee. = 委员会制定了一项新的政策。
The plan was carefully formulated over several months. = 该计划经过数月的精心制定。


疑问句:
How did they formulate their strategy? = 他们是如何制定他们的战略的？
Has the committee formulated a response yet? = 委员会是否已经制定了回应？


否定句:
The team failed to formulate a coherent plan. = 团队未能制定一个连贯的计划。
He didn't formulate his thoughts properly before writing the report. = 他在写报告之前没有好好组织自己的思路。


PHRASE->
formulate a plan 制定计划
formulate a theory 提出理论
formulate a policy 制定政策
formulate a response 制定回应
formulate a question 提出问题


词源:
来自拉丁语 *formulare，意思是“制定，表达”。  最终源于拉丁语 *forma，意思是“形式，形状”。
