oilbird: /ˈɔɪlbɜːrd/ | n. |油鸱|A nocturnal bird of the genus Steatornis, found in tropical South America, that feeds on fruits and whose young are exceptionally fat.  It is also known as guácharo. | 例句：The oilbird's unique echolocation system allows it to navigate in complete darkness. 油鸱独特的回声定位系统使它能够在完全黑暗的环境中导航。| 例句：The young oilbirds are so fat that their rendered fat was once a valuable source of oil.  油鸱的幼鸟非常肥胖，它们的脂肪曾经是重要的油脂来源。|近义词：guácharo |反义词:  N/A
