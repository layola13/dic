missionarize:/ˈmɪʃənəraɪz/| vt.|传教；使从事传教活动|To engage in missionary work; to convert or attempt to convert (someone) to a particular religion or belief system.|例句：The missionaries missionarized throughout the region, trying to convert the locals to Christianity. 传教士们在整个地区传教，试图将当地人改信基督教。|近义词：evangelize, proselytize|反义词：secularize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They missionarize in remote villages. = 他们在偏远村庄传教。
He missionarizes to the local population. = 他向当地居民传教。


一般过去时:
The priest missionarized in Africa for many years. = 那位牧师在非洲传教多年。
They missionarized among the indigenous people. = 他们在土著居民中传教。


现在完成时:
She has missionarized in several countries. = 她已经在几个国家传教。
They have missionarized effectively in this community. = 他们在这一社区有效地传教。


过去完成时:
He had missionarized extensively before he retired. = 他退休前已广泛传教。
The missionaries had missionarized there for decades. = 传教士们在那里传教了几十年。


将来时:
We will missionarize in the Amazon rainforest next year. = 我们明年将在亚马逊雨林传教。
She will missionarize in South America. = 她将在南美洲传教。


虚拟语气:
If I were to missionarize, I would choose a rural area. = 如果我要传教，我会选择一个农村地区。
I wish I could missionarize more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地传教。


被动语态:
The region was missionarized by Catholic priests. = 该地区曾由天主教神父传教。
The tribe was missionarized centuries ago. = 这个部落几个世纪前就已接受了传教。


疑问句:

否定句:
