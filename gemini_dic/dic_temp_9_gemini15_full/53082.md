dipetalous:/daɪˈpɛtələs/| adj. |二瓣的，有两枚花瓣的 |Having two petals.  This term is primarily used in botany to describe flowers with only two petals.  The prefix "di-" means two, and "-petalous" refers to petals. | Example:  The dipetalous flower was a delicate shade of pink.  这朵二瓣花是淡淡的粉色。|近义词：bipetalous (though less common)|反义词：polypetalous (many petals), apetalous (lacking petals)
