hereby:/ˌhɪərˈbaɪ/ | adv. |据此，特此，因此 |This adverb indicates that something is done or happens by means of this specific statement or document. It emphasizes the act of stating or declaring something officially. |例句：I hereby declare the meeting closed.  我特此宣布会议结束。

例句：The court hereby orders the defendant to pay damages. 法院据此判决被告赔偿损失。

近义词：thereby, thus, therefore, consequently

反义词：N/A

词性：副词
