deteriorates:/dɪˈtɪəriəreɪts/ | v. |恶化；变坏；退化 | To become progressively worse.  |例句：The weather is deteriorating rapidly. 天气正在迅速恶化。

The situation in the country has deteriorated sharply. 该国局势急剧恶化。

近义词：worsen, decline, degenerate, decay | 反义词：improve, enhance, ameliorate

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The situation deteriorates slowly. =局势缓慢恶化。
His health deteriorates gradually. =他的健康逐渐恶化。


一般过去时:
The building deteriorated over the years. = 这座建筑物年久失修。
Their relationship deteriorated after the argument. = 他们争吵后关系恶化了。


现在完成时:
The condition of the patient has deteriorated significantly. = 病人的病情已显著恶化。
The quality of the product has deteriorated since the change in management. = 自从管理层变动以来，产品的质量下降了。


过去完成时:
The bridge had deteriorated before the accident. = 这座桥在事故发生前就已经破损了。
The economy had deteriorated considerably before the government intervened. = 在政府干预之前，经济已经大幅恶化。


将来时:
The situation will deteriorate further without intervention. = 如果不干预，情况将进一步恶化。
His health is expected to deteriorate in the coming weeks. = 预计他的健康状况将在未来几周内恶化。


虚拟语气:
If we don't act now, the situation will deteriorate. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，情况将会恶化。
I wish the situation hadn't deteriorated so quickly. = 我真希望情况没有恶化得这么快。


被动语态:
The building is being deteriorated by the weather.  = 这座建筑物正在被风雨侵蚀。
The environment has been deteriorated by pollution. = 环境已被污染破坏。


疑问句:
Is the situation deteriorating? = 情况正在恶化吗？
Has the quality of the product deteriorated? = 产品的质量下降了吗？


否定句:
The situation hasn't deteriorated yet. = 局势尚未恶化。
The building did not deteriorate significantly. = 这座建筑物没有显著恶化。


PHRASE->
rapidly deteriorate:迅速恶化
steadily deteriorate:稳步恶化
seriously deteriorate:严重恶化
significantly deteriorate:显著恶化
gradually deteriorate:逐渐恶化
