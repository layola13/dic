physiologic:/ˌfɪziəˈlɒdʒɪk/| adj.|生理的，生理学的|Relating to the normal functions of living organisms.  It refers to the processes and functions of living things, typically at the level of organs and organ systems.|例句：The patient showed a physiologic response to the medication.  病人对药物产生了生理反应。
The study examined the physiologic effects of stress on the heart. 这项研究检查了压力对心脏的生理影响。|近义词：biological, physiological, natural|反义词：pathological, unnatural
