redesigns:/ˌriːdɪˈzaɪnz/| n. |重新设计；重新设计作品 |The process of designing something again, often to improve it or to adapt it to a new purpose.  It refers to the plural form of "redesign," encompassing multiple instances of redesigning. |例句：The company's redesigns of its products resulted in increased sales. 公司对其产品的重新设计导致销售额增加。

The website's redesigns have been met with mixed reviews.  网站的重新设计褒贬不一。|近义词：renovations, makeovers, overhauls |反义词：original designs, initial designs |名词
