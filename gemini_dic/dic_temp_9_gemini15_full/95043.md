inactivation:/ˌɪnˌæktɪˈveɪʃən/ | n. |失活；钝化；无效化 | The process of making something inactive or ineffective.  It can refer to a variety of processes, including the rendering of a biological agent, such as a virus or enzyme, harmless;  the deactivation of a chemical compound; or the disabling of a device or system. | Example: Heat inactivation of the virus prevented its spread.  热失活病毒阻止了其传播。 The inactivation of the enzyme resulted in a halt to the reaction.  酶的失活导致反应停止。 |近义词：deactivation, disabling, suppression |反义词：activation, stimulation
