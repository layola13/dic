honoraria:/ˌɒnəˈreəriə/ /ˌɑːnəˈreriə/ | n. | 酬金；谢礼；报酬 | A sum of money paid to someone for services rendered, especially in a professional capacity, but not as a salary.  It is often given for lectures, consultations, or other professional services.  | The professor received an honorarium for his lecture. 教授因其演讲获得了酬金。  He was given an honorarium for his work on the project. 他因参与该项目的工作而获得了报酬。 | fees, remuneration, payment |  penalty, punishment | 名词
