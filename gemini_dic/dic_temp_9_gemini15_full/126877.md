naps:/næps/| n.|小睡；午睡|a short sleep, especially during the day|例句：I usually take a nap after lunch. 我通常午饭后小睡一会儿。|例句：The baby took several naps throughout the day. 婴儿在白天睡了好几次午觉。|近义词：doze, slumber, siesta|反义词：wakefulness, insomnia


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He naps every afternoon. = 他每天下午都小睡一会儿。
She often naps on the couch. = 她经常在沙发上小睡。

一般过去时:
I napped for an hour yesterday. = 我昨天睡了一个小时。
They napped in the park. = 他们在公园里小睡了一会儿。

现在完成时:
I have napped twice today. = 我今天已经睡了两次午觉了。
She hasn't napped yet. = 她还没睡午觉。

过去完成时:
I had napped before the meeting. = 我在开会前睡过一会儿。
She had napped before the children arrived. = 孩子们来之前她已经睡过一会儿了。

将来时:
I will nap after work. = 我下班后要小睡一会儿。
He will nap on the plane. = 他将在飞机上小睡一会儿。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would nap. = 如果我有时间，我会小睡一会儿。
I wish I could nap now. = 我真希望现在能小睡一会儿。

被动语态:  (Naps is rarely used in the passive voice.  The action of napping is inherently active.)


疑问句:
Do you often nap? = 你经常小睡吗？
Does he nap during the day? = 他白天小睡吗？

否定句:
I don't usually nap. = 我通常不小睡。
She didn't nap yesterday. = 她昨天没有小睡。
