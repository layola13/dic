facade:/fəˈsɑːd/|/fəˈsæd/| n. |正面；外观；虚假的外表 |The building's facade was beautifully decorated, but the interior was dilapidated.  这栋建筑的正面装饰精美，但内部却破败不堪。The company presented a facade of success, while secretly struggling with debt.  这家公司表面上很成功，但实际上却背负着巨额债务。|front, exterior, veneer |reality, truth, substance |名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The facade of the building is impressive. = 这栋建筑的正面令人印象深刻。
This company maintains a positive facade to the public. = 这家公司对外保持着积极的形象。


一般过去时:
The old facade of the house was replaced. = 房子的旧正面被更换了。
They carefully maintained a facade of composure during the crisis. = 他们在危机期间小心翼翼地保持着镇静的姿态。


现在完成时:
The building's facade has been renovated. = 这栋建筑的正面已经翻新了。
They have successfully maintained a facade of unity despite their disagreements. = 尽管存在分歧，他们仍然成功地维持了团结的表面。


过去完成时:
The facade had already crumbled before the earthquake. = 地震发生前，正面就已经坍塌了。
They had presented a facade of indifference, but I knew they were worried. = 他们表现出一副漠不关心的样子，但我明白他们在担心。


将来时:
The new facade will be completed next month. = 新的正面将于下个月完工。
They will continue to present a facade of strength to their investors. = 他们将继续向投资者展现实力的形象。


虚拟语气:
If the facade were stronger, the building wouldn't have collapsed. = 如果正面更坚固，建筑物就不会倒塌。
I wish the facade of their relationship wasn't so deceptive. = 我希望他们关系的表面不那么具有欺骗性。


被动语态:
The facade was damaged by the storm. = 正面被风暴破坏了。
A facade of secrecy is often maintained by governments. = 政府通常会保持秘密的外表。


疑问句:
What is the material of the facade? = 正面的材料是什么？
Is this just a facade, or is there real change? = 这仅仅是表面现象，还是真的发生了变化？


否定句:
The facade is not as strong as it appears. = 正面不像看起来那么坚固。
Their friendly facade didn't hide their underlying tension. = 他们友好的外表掩盖不了他们潜在的紧张关系。
