nonsurgically:/ˌnɒnˈsɜːdʒɪkli/ | adj. |非手术的，非外科手术的 |  Not involving surgery; done without surgery.  | Example:  The doctor recommended nonsurgical treatment for the herniated disc. 医生建议对椎间盘突出进行非手术治疗。 The condition can often be managed nonsurgically.  这种情况通常可以通过非手术方法来治疗。|近义词：conservative, medical, noninvasive |反义词：surgical, operative
