eozoic:/iːˈoʊzɪk/ | adj. |早期的，始生代的 | Relating to the Eozoic era, an early period of geological time.  The term is now largely obsolete, often replaced by "Archean" or "Precambrian".  The Eozoic era is characterized by the formation of the Earth's crust and the earliest evidence of life. | Example: The eozoic rocks contain some of the oldest known fossils. 早期的岩石包含一些已知最古老的化石。 | 近义词：Archean, Precambrian, primordial | 反义词：Recent, modern
