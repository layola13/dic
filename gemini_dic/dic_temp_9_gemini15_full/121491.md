misleader:/mɪsˈliːdər/ | n. | 误导者，欺骗者 | A person or thing that misleads.  Someone who intentionally provides false or inaccurate information to deceive others. | Example: The misleading advertisement led many consumers to buy a faulty product.  这个误导性广告导致许多消费者购买了有缺陷的产品。 |近义词：deceiver, fraud, cheat | 反义词：guide, truth-teller
