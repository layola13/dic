panel:/ˈpænl/| n.|嵌板；面板；小组；全体成员|A panel is a flat, rectangular piece of wood, metal, or other material, often used as part of a larger structure or surface.  It can also refer to a group of people selected for a specific purpose, such as a discussion or investigation.|例句：The artist painted a beautiful landscape on the wooden panel.  艺术家在一块木板上画了一幅美丽的风景画。

The expert panel discussed the environmental issues.专家小组讨论了环境问题。|近义词：board, section, group, committee|反义词：whole, entirety
