reregisters:/ˌriːˈredʒɪstərz/| v.|重新注册；再次登记|To register again; to register something a second time.  The verb implies a prior registration that is being repeated or updated.|例句：He reregistered for the course after failing the first time. 他第一次考试不及格后重新注册了这门课程。|例句：The company reregistered its trademark to protect it from infringement. 公司重新注册了其商标以防止侵权。|近义词：re-enroll, re-register, reinstate|反义词：unregister, deregister|动词
