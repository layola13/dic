convicts:/ˈkɒnvɪkts/| n. |罪犯，囚犯|People who have been found guilty of a crime and sentenced to prison.  Convicts often face significant challenges reintegrating into society after their release.|例句：The convicts were transported to a remote island.  这些罪犯被押送到一个偏远的岛屿。|例句：Many convicts struggle to find employment after serving their sentences. 许多罪犯服刑后难以找到工作。|近义词：prisoners, inmates, offenders|反义词：law-abiders, innocents

一般现在时:
The court convicts many criminals each year. = 法院每年判决许多罪犯。
He convicts her of lying. = 他认定她在说谎。


一般过去时:
The jury convicted him of murder. = 陪审团判定他犯有谋杀罪。

现在完成时:
They have convicted several suspects in the case. = 他们已经在此案中判决了几名嫌疑人。
The court has convicted him twice before. = 法院之前已经判决过他两次。


过去完成时:
By the time the trial ended, they had convicted all the defendants. = 到审判结束时，他们已经判决了所有被告。

将来时:
