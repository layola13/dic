flicked:/flɪkt/| vt.|轻弹；轻拂；快速移动|To move or cause to move with a quick, light action.  |例句：She flicked the switch and the lights came on. 她轻弹开关，灯亮了。|例句：He flicked a speck of dust from his shoulder. 他轻拂掉肩上的灰尘。|近义词：snapped, flipped, jerked|反义词：pushed, shoved


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He flicks the light switch. = 他轻弹电灯开关。
She flicks her hair. = 她轻甩头发。

一般过去时:
He flicked the switch. = 他轻弹了开关。
She flicked the dust off the table. = 她把桌上的灰尘拂掉了。

现在完成时:
He has flicked the ashes from his cigarette. = 他已经弹掉了香烟灰。
She has flicked through the magazine. = 她已经快速翻阅了杂志。

过去完成时:
He had flicked the light switch before the power went out. =  停电前他已经关了灯。
She had flicked through the report before the meeting. = 会议前她已经快速浏览了报告。

将来时:
He will flick the switch when he enters the room. = 他进房间时会轻弹开关。
She will flick the dust off the furniture. = 她会把家具上的灰尘拂掉。


虚拟语气:
If he flicked the switch, the lights would come on. = 如果他轻弹开关，灯就会亮。
I wish I had flicked the mosquito away before it bit me. = 我真希望我在蚊子咬我之前把它拍死。

被动语态:
The switch was flicked. = 开关被弹动了。
The dust was flicked off the table. = 桌上的灰尘被拂掉了。

疑问句:
Did he flick the switch? = 他弹动开关了吗？
Has she flicked through the book yet? = 她翻阅过这本书了吗？

否定句:
He didn't flick the switch. = 他没有弹动开关。
She didn't flick the hair out of her eyes. = 她没有把头发从眼睛前拂开。


PHRASE->
flick through: 快速翻阅
flick away: 拂去
flick on/off: 快速打开/关闭
flick a cigarette: 弹烟灰
flick a glance: 快速地瞥一眼
