promontories:/ˈprɒmənˌtɔːriz/| n. |海角；岬；突出物 |A promontory is a high point of land that juts out into the sea or a large body of water.  It is often steep and rocky. |例句：The ship sailed past the rugged promontories of the coast. 这艘船驶过了崎岖海岸的海角。

The lighthouse stands on a dramatic promontory overlooking the ocean. 灯塔矗立在一个俯瞰大海的壮丽海角上。


近义词：headland, cape, point, bluff |反义词：inlet, bay
