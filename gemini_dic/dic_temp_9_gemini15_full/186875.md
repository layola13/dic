rink:/rɪŋk/| n.|溜冰场；（尤指）冰球场|A rink is a specially prepared surface, usually of ice, used for skating, especially ice hockey.  |例句：The children had a great time at the ice rink. 孩子们在溜冰场玩得很开心。

例句：We watched the hockey game at the rink. 我们在溜冰场观看了冰球比赛。


近义词：skating rink, ice-skating rink|反义词：无


词源:  The word "rink" ultimately derives from the Old Norse word "hringr," meaning "ring" or "circle." This reflects the circular shape of early skating areas.  Over time, the word evolved through Middle English and became associated specifically with skating surfaces.
