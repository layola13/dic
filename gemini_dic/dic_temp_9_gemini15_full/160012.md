polysulphuret:/ˌpɒliˈsʌlfjuərɛt/| n. |多硫化物|A polysulphide is a chemical compound containing a chain of sulfur atoms.  These chains can vary in length, leading to different polysulphides. They are often found as anions (negatively charged ions) in various compounds.  They are known for their characteristic yellow color and often have a strong smell. Polysulphides are used in various industrial applications, including vulcanization of rubber and the production of certain chemicals.|Example: Sodium polysulphide is used in the production of certain dyes. 钠多硫化物用于某些染料的生产。|近义词：polysulfide|反义词：N/A
