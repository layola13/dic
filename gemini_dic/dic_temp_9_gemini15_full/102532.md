jadeite:/ˈdʒeɪdaɪt/| n. |硬玉|A hard, translucent to opaque, green to white mineral, a variety of pyroxene, used as a gemstone.  It is highly prized for its beauty and durability, and is often carved into jewelry and ornaments.|例句：The necklace was made of exquisite jadeite, its green hue shimmering in the light. 这条项链是用精美的硬玉制成的，其绿色在灯光下闪闪发光。|例句：The museum boasts a stunning collection of ancient jadeite carvings.  博物馆拥有令人惊叹的古代硬玉雕刻收藏。|近义词：nephrite (another type of jade)|反义词:|
