engraving:/ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋ/| n.|雕刻；雕版印刷|The art or process of cutting designs and pictures into a hard surface, especially for printing.  It can also refer to the design or picture itself. |例句：The museum boasts a fine collection of 18th-century engravings.  这个博物馆拥有大量18世纪的精美版画收藏。|例句：He carefully made an engraving of the family crest. 他仔细地刻制了家族纹章的版画。|近义词：etching, carving, inscription|反义词：smoothing, polishing|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist engraves beautiful designs onto the metal. = 艺术家在金属上雕刻精美的图案。
He engraves names onto jewelry. = 他在首饰上刻名字。


一般过去时:
She engraved a message on the locket. = 她在锁匙上刻了一条信息。
They engraved the date on the monument. = 他们在纪念碑上刻上了日期。


现在完成时:
He has engraved hundreds of pieces of jewelry. = 他已经雕刻了数百件首饰。
She has engraved her initials into the tree. = 她已将她的姓名首字母刻在树上。


过去完成时:
They had engraved the plaque before the ceremony. = 他们在仪式之前已经刻好了牌匾。
He had engraved the ring before he proposed.  = 他在求婚前已经刻好了戒指。


将来时:
I will engrave your name on this trophy. = 我会把你的名字刻在这个奖杯上。
She will engrave a special message on the watch. = 她会在手表上刻上特殊的信息。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would engrave a picture of my dog. = 如果我有更多时间，我会刻一张我狗的照片。
I wish I had engraved a date on the gift. = 我真希望我在礼物上刻上日期。


被动语态:
The trophy was engraved with the winner's name. = 奖杯上刻上了获胜者的名字。
The design was beautifully engraved onto the wood. = 图案被精美地雕刻在木头上。


疑问句:
Is this picture an engraving? = 这幅图是版画吗？
Will you engrave a custom design for me? = 你会为我雕刻一个定制的设计吗？


否定句:
I didn’t engrave the message correctly. = 我没有正确地刻上信息。
He didn’t want to engrave the logo. = 他不想刻这个标志。
