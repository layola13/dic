maskable:/ˈmæskəbəl/| adj.| 可屏蔽的；可遮蔽的 |  Able to be masked or ignored; capable of being suppressed or prevented from operating.  It often refers to an interrupt or signal that can be temporarily disabled. | Example: The interrupt is maskable, allowing the system to prioritize tasks.  这个中断是可以屏蔽的，允许系统优先处理任务。 The error messages are maskable, but it's better to address the underlying issue. 错误信息是可以屏蔽的，但是最好解决根本问题。|近义词：suppressible, ignorable|反义词：unmaskable, mandatory
