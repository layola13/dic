gauntry:/ˈɡɔːntri/| n. |龙门架；装卸架 |A gantry is a type of overhead crane or structure that is used to support heavy loads, typically in industrial settings.  It consists of a framework that spans an area, with a hoist or other lifting mechanism for moving the loads. |例句：The workers used a gantry crane to lift the large container onto the ship. 工人们用龙门起重机将大型集装箱吊到船上。
