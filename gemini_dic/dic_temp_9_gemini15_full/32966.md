They are characterized by the presence of a notochord, a dorsal hollow nerve cord, pharyngeal slits, and a post-anal tail at some point in their development.  | Example:  Mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, and fish are all members of the phylum Chordata. 脊索动物门包括哺乳动物、鸟类、爬行动物、两栖动物和鱼类。|近义词:vertebrates(部分)|反义词:invertebrates(部分)|名词
