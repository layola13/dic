reindulged:/ˌriːɪnˈdʌldʒd/| v.|再次沉溺，再次放纵|To indulge again; to allow oneself to enjoy something again after a period of abstinence or restraint.|例句：After a week of healthy eating, she reindulged in a delicious chocolate cake.  = 在一周的健康饮食后，她又吃了一个美味的巧克力蛋糕。

例句：He reindulged his passion for collecting stamps after a long break. = 他在长时间的休息后，再次沉迷于集邮。


近义词：overindulge, pamper, spoil


反义词：restrain, deny, abstain


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reindulges his sweet tooth every weekend. = 他每个周末都再次放纵他的嗜甜。
They reindulge in their favorite hobby after a long day of work. = 他们一天辛苦工作后，再次沉迷于他们最喜欢的爱好。

一般过去时:
She reindulged in a long bath after a stressful day. = 她在一个压力很大的日子之后再次享受了长时间的泡澡。
We reindulged in our favorite pizza after months of dieting. = 我们在数月的节食后再次享用了我们最喜欢的披萨。

现在完成时:
I have reindulged in my love for classic movies lately. = 我最近再次沉迷于经典电影。
She has reindulged her passion for painting after years of neglect. = 她多年忽视自己的绘画热情后，又再次沉迷其中。

过去完成时:
He had reindulged in unhealthy habits before he decided to change his lifestyle. = 他在决定改变生活方式之前，曾经再次沉迷于不健康的习惯。
They had reindulged in their old arguments before they finally resolved their issues. = 他们在最终解决问题之前，曾经再次陷入老生常谈的争吵。

将来时:
I will reindulge in a good book after finishing my work. = 我完成工作后会再次沉迷于一本好书。
She will reindulge in her favorite dessert after she loses weight. = 她减肥成功后会再次享用她最喜欢的甜点。

虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would reindulge in my hobby of playing the guitar. = 如果我有时间，我会再次沉迷于我的吉他爱好。
I wish I could reindulge in a long vacation. = 我真希望我能再次享受一个长假。

被动语态:  (reindulge 不常用被动语态)
His desires were reindulged by his family. = 他的愿望得到了家人的再次满足。(比较生硬)
His craving for sweets was reindulged by a box of chocolates. = 他对糖果的渴望被一盒巧克力满足了。(比较生硬)


疑问句:
Will you reindulge in that unhealthy habit again? = 你还会再次沉迷于那个不健康的习惯吗？
Did she reindulge in her old vices after all these years? = 这么多年后，她还会沉迷于她以前的恶习吗？

否定句:
He did not reindulge his appetite for junk food. = 他没有再次放纵自己吃垃圾食品的欲望。
She refused to reindulge in her past mistakes. = 她拒绝再次沉迷于过去的错误。


PHRASE->
reindulge in (something):  再次沉溺于……
reindulge oneself:  再次放纵自己
reindulge one's senses:  再次放纵感官
reindulge one's passions:  再次放纵自己的激情
reindulge in luxury:  再次享受奢华
