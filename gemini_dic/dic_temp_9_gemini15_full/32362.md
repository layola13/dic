childproofed:/ˈtʃaɪldpruːft/| adj.|防儿童的，儿童安全防护的|Made safe to prevent injury or access by children.  This term describes something designed or modified to prevent children from accessing or harming themselves with a product or environment.|例句：The cabinet was childproofed with special locks.  这个橱柜安装了特殊的锁，以防止儿童接触。

The house has been completely childproofed. 这所房子已经完全进行了儿童安全防护。|近义词：child-resistant, safe for children, secure|反义词：accessible to children, dangerous to children
