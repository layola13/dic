ingredience:/ɪnˈɡriːdiəns/| n. |成分，配料|The word "ingredience" is an obsolete or rarely used synonym for "ingredient". It refers to a component or element that is part of a mixture or compound, especially in cooking or a recipe.  While grammatically correct, its usage is highly discouraged in modern English due to its obsolescence and the readily available and preferred synonym "ingredient".|The cake's ingredience included flour, sugar, and eggs. = 蛋糕的成分包括面粉、糖和鸡蛋。|ingredient|component, element|
