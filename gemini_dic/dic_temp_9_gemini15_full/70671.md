finagled:/fɪˈnæɡld/| vt.|耍手段获得；骗取；巧妙地处理|To obtain or achieve something by trickery or manipulation.  It implies a degree of cunning or slyness.|例句：He finagled his way into the VIP section. 他耍手段混进了贵宾区。

例句：She finagled a discount on the price. 她巧妙地争取到了折扣。


近义词：manipulated, wheedled, coaxed,  conned,  swindled

反义词：honestly obtained, legitimately acquired


词性：动词
