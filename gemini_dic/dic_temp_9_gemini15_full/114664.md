His maliceful actions caused irreparable damage to their relationship.  他怀有恶意的行为给他们的关系造成了无法弥补的损害。


近义词：malevolent, spiteful, malicious, vindictive, ill-willed

反义词：benevolent, kind, good-natured, well-meaning
