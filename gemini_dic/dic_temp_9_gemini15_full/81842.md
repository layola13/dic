greys:/ɡreɪz/| n.|灰色，灰白，(Grey的复数)|The word "greys" refers to multiple instances of the color grey or to various shades of grey.|例句：The artist used many shades of greys in the painting.  这位艺术家在画中使用了许多种灰色。|近义词：grays, shades of gray|反义词：bright colors, vibrant colors


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sky is grey today. = 今天天空是灰色的。
Her hair is greying. = 她的头发正在变灰。


一般过去时:
The walls were grey before we painted them. = 我们粉刷墙壁之前，墙壁是灰色的。
His beard was already greying at a young age. = 他年轻的时候胡子就已经开始变灰了。


现在完成时:
The weather has been grey all week. = 这周天气一直都是阴天。
His hair has greyed considerably since I last saw him. = 自从我上次见到他以来，他的头发已经灰白了许多。


过去完成时:
The sky had been grey all day before the storm broke. = 在暴风雨来临之前，天空一整天都是灰色的。
She had already greyed significantly by the time she turned fifty. = 到她五十岁的时候，她的头发已经明显地变灰了。


将来时:
The sky will be grey tomorrow, according to the forecast. = 根据预报，明天天空将是灰色的。
His hair will likely grey further as he gets older. = 随着年龄的增长，他的头发可能会进一步变灰。


虚拟语气:
If the walls were grey, I would paint them. = 如果墙壁是灰色的，我会把它们粉刷一下。
I wish my hair weren't greying so fast. = 我希望我的头发不要变灰得这么快。


被动语态:
The painting was dominated by greys. = 这幅画以灰色为主。
His hair was greying prematurely. = 他的头发过早地变灰了。


疑问句:
Are those greys or blues? = 那些是灰色还是蓝色？
Is his hair greying prematurely? = 他的头发过早地变灰了吗？


否定句:
The dress wasn't grey, it was silver. = 这件衣服不是灰色的，是银色的。
Her hair hasn't greyed much yet. = 她的头发还没怎么变灰。
