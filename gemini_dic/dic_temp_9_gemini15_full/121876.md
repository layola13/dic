misruled:/ˌmɪsˈruːld/| v. |统治不善；治理混乱|To govern or rule badly or unjustly.  The verb implies a lack of fairness, competence, or effectiveness in leadership. It suggests oppression, tyranny, or mismanagement. |例句：The country was misruled for decades under a corrupt dictator.  这个国家在腐败的独裁者统治下，几十年来一直治理混乱。

The people suffered under the misruled regime.  人民在统治不善的政权下受苦。


近义词：misgovern, maladminister, tyrannize, oppress

反义词：govern well, rule justly, administer effectively


词源：mis- (wrongly, badly) + ruled (past participle of rule, meaning to govern)
