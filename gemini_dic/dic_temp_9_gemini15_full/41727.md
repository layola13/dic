corrodes:/kəˈroʊdz/ | v. |腐蚀，侵蚀；逐渐破坏 |To gradually destroy something by chemical action; to wear away gradually.|The acid corroded the metal pipe.  酸腐蚀了金属管道。|The constant criticism corroded his confidence.  持续的批评逐渐损害了他的信心。|erodes, wears away, deteriorates |strengthens, improves, builds up |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The acid corrodes the metal. = 酸腐蚀金属。
Rust corrodes iron. = 锈腐蚀铁。

一般过去时:
The sea corroded the rocks. = 海水侵蚀了岩石。
The harsh weather corroded the paintwork. = 恶劣的天气腐蚀了油漆。

现在完成时:
The statue has been corroded by the rain. = 这座雕像已经被雨水腐蚀了。
Years of neglect have corroded the building's structure. = 多年来的忽视已经腐蚀了建筑物的结构。

过去完成时:
The pipe had corroded before we noticed the leak. = 我们注意到泄漏之前，管道就已经腐蚀了。
The bridge had corroded significantly due to saltwater exposure. = 由于暴露在盐水中，桥梁已经严重腐蚀。

将来时:
The salt water will corrode the metal components. = 盐水会腐蚀金属部件。
Prolonged exposure to the elements will corrode the paint. = 长时间暴露在自然环境中会腐蚀油漆。

虚拟语气:
If the metal were exposed to acid, it would corrode quickly. = 如果金属暴露在酸中，它会很快腐蚀。
If we had acted sooner, the damage might not have corroded so extensively. = 如果我们早点采取行动，损害可能不会腐蚀得这么严重。

被动语态:
The metal is corroded by the acid. = 金属被酸腐蚀。
The building's foundation is being corroded by groundwater. = 建筑物的基础正在被地下水腐蚀。

疑问句:
Is the metal corroded? = 金属被腐蚀了吗？
Has the pipeline corroded significantly? = 管道严重腐蚀了吗？

否定句:
The acid does not corrode the plastic. = 酸不会腐蚀塑料。
The coating prevents the metal from corroding. = 涂层防止金属腐蚀。
