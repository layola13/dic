pay-as-you-go:/ˈpeɪ æz ju ˈɡoʊ/| adj.|按次付费的，即付即用的|A system where you pay for a service only when you use it, rather than paying in advance or through a subscription.|例句：Many mobile phone plans are pay-as-you-go.  许多手机计划都是按次付费的。|例句：This parking lot operates on a pay-as-you-go basis. 这个停车场采用即付即用的方式。|近义词：prepaid, on-demand|反义词：subscription-based, pre-paid
