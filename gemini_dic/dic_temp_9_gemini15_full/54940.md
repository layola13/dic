dispossession:/ˌdɪspəˈzeʃən/ | n. | 剥夺财产；驱逐；没收 | The act of depriving someone of possession of something, especially property or land; the state of being deprived of possession.  | Example: The dispossession of Native Americans from their ancestral lands was a tragic event.  土著美国人被剥夺他们祖传土地的行为是一场悲剧。  The dispossession of the farmers left them destitute. 农民被剥夺财产后变得一贫如洗。| 近义词：deprivation, eviction, confiscation | 反义词：acquisition, possession, restitution |
