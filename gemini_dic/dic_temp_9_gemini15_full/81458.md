gratitude:/ˈɡrætɪtuːd/| n. |感激之情，感谢 |The feeling of being grateful for something.  It involves appreciation for what one has received or experienced.  |例句：I expressed my gratitude for their help. 我表达了对他们帮助的感激之情。
|例句：She felt a deep sense of gratitude towards her mentor. 她对她的导师怀有深深的感激之情。|近义词：thankfulness, appreciation, thank you|反义词：ingratitude


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I feel gratitude for my family. = 我对我的家人心怀感激。
They show gratitude to their teachers. = 他们对老师表示感激。

一般过去时:
She expressed gratitude for the gift. = 她表达了对这份礼物的感激。
He felt gratitude after receiving the award. = 他在获得奖项后感到感激。

现在完成时:
I have shown my gratitude to them. = 我已经向他们表达了我的感激之情。
We have felt immense gratitude for their support. = 我们对他们的支持深感感激。

过去完成时:
He had already expressed his gratitude before leaving. = 他在离开之前已经表达了他的感激之情。
She had felt a sense of gratitude long before that. = 在那很久之前她就心怀感激了。

将来时:
I will express my gratitude in a letter. = 我将通过信函表达我的感激之情。
They will show their gratitude by volunteering. = 他们将通过志愿服务来表达他们的感激之情。

虚拟语气:
I would feel more gratitude if they acknowledged my efforts. = 如果他们认可我的努力，我会感到更加感激。
If I had known, I would have expressed my gratitude earlier. = 如果我知道的话，我会早点表达我的感激之情。

被动语态:
Her kindness was met with gratitude. = 她的善良得到了感激。
Their help is received with gratitude. = 他们的帮助受到了感激。

疑问句:
Do you feel gratitude for your blessings? = 你对你的福分心怀感激吗？
Does he show gratitude to others? = 他对别人表示感激吗？

否定句:
He felt no gratitude for their help. = 他对他们的帮助没有一丝感激。
