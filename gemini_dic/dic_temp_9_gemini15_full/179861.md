reexploring:/ˌriːɪkˈsplɔːrɪŋ/ | v. |重新探索；再次探索 |  The act of exploring something again, often with a new perspective or approach.  It implies a previous exploration has already taken place. | 例句：The team is reexploring the cave system, hoping to find new passages.  团队正在重新探索这个洞穴系统，希望能找到新的通道。

The researchers are reexploring the historical data, using advanced analytical techniques. 研究人员正在使用先进的分析技术重新探索历史数据。


近义词：reexamining, revisiting, reinvestigating, re-investigating

反义词：ignoring, neglecting, overlooking


词性：动词
