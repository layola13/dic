misorder:/mɪsˈɔːrdər/| vt. | 弄乱；错误排序 | To arrange incorrectly or in a disordered manner.  It implies a deviation from the correct or expected order. |例句：He misordered the files, making it difficult to find the information. 他把文件弄乱了，很难找到信息。


例句：The librarian misordered the books, placing fiction among the biographies. 图书管理员把书放错了地方，把小说放在了传记中间。


近义词：disarrange, jumble, muddle, scramble


反义词：order, arrange, organize


词性：动词
