piedstall: /ˈpiːdstɔːl/ | n. |脚凳；矮凳| A low stool, especially one used to support the feet.  The word is now largely archaic or dialectal. It derives from "pied" (meaning "variegated" or "many-colored," related to "piebald") and "stall" (a seat or stand).  This suggests the piedstall was often decorated in a multi-colored way. | Example:  She rested her feet on the richly piedstall. 她把脚放在色彩丰富的脚凳上。 |近义词：footstool, stool |反义词：无  |词性：名词
