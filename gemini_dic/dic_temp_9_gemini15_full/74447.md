frangipani:/ˌfrænˈdʒɪpəni/| n. |鸡蛋花，缅栀子|A frangipani is a tropical tree with fragrant, usually white or yellow flowers. The name is also used for the flower itself.  Frangipani flowers are known for their intense, sweet fragrance, often described as intoxicating. They are popular in tropical regions and are frequently used in perfumes, lotions, and other beauty products.|Example: The air was filled with the intoxicating scent of frangipani.|近义词：plumeria|反义词：无
