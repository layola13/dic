limiters:/ˈlɪmɪtərz/| n.|限制器，限制因素|Something that limits or restricts something else.  A limiter can be a physical device, a rule, a constraint, or any factor that prevents something from exceeding a certain boundary or level.|例句：Speed limiters prevent cars from going too fast.  速度限制器防止汽车行驶过快。
The budget is a major limiter on our project. 预算对我们的项目是一个主要的限制因素。|近义词：constraints, restrictions, restraints, boundaries|反义词：enhancers, expanders, accelerators
