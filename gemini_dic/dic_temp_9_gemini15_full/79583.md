gloopy:/ˈɡluːpi/| adj. | 粘稠的；糊状的；软塌塌的 | Thick and sticky or slimy; lacking firmness or resilience.  |例句：The jam was gloopy and difficult to spread. 果酱很粘稠，很难涂抹。

例句：The cake was gloopy in the middle. 蛋糕中间软塌塌的。


近义词：sticky, slimy, viscous, gooey, thick, syrupy | 反义词：thin, watery, firm, solid


词性：形容词
