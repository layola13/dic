knesset:/kˈnɛsɛt/| n.|以色列议会|The Knesset is the unicameral parliament of Israel.  It is responsible for passing laws, approving the government's budget, and overseeing the executive branch.|例句：The Knesset debated the new proposed law for several hours. 以色列议会就新的提案辩论了好几个小时。  The members of the Knesset voted on the budget. 以色列议会议员对预算进行了投票。|近义词：parliament, legislature, congress|反义词：None.
