militantly:/ˈmɪlɪtəntli/| adv. | 激进地；好战地 | In an aggressive and uncompromising manner.  |例句：The group protested militantly against the new law.  该组织激进地抗议新法律。

例句：He argued his point militantly, refusing to compromise. 他激进地辩论他的观点，拒绝妥协。


近义词：aggressively, belligerently, combatively, fiercely

反义词：peacefully, passively, mildly, conciliatorily


词源:  Derived from the word "militant," which comes from the Latin word "militans," the present participle of "militare," meaning "to serve as a soldier."  The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective into an adverb.
