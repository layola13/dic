/tʃaɪz/| n.|柴，茶 |Chai is a spiced tea beverage that originated in India.  Chai is typically made with black tea, milk, and a blend of spices such as cardamom, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves.|例句：I love the warm, comforting taste of chai on a cold day.  今天喝一杯暖和舒适的柴真是太好了。|近义词：masala tea, spiced tea |反义词：coffee, water
