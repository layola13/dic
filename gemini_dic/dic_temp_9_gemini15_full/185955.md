reward:/rɪˈwɔːrd/| n., vt.|奖赏；报酬；酬劳 |Something given in return for service, merit, or achievement.|例句：He received a reward for his bravery. 他因勇敢而获得奖励。|例句：She was rewarded with a promotion. 她获得了晋升的奖励。|近义词：prize, recompense, compensation, payment|反义词：punishment, penalty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rewards his employees generously. = 他慷慨地奖励他的员工。
The company rewards hard work and dedication. = 公司奖励努力工作和奉献精神。


一般过去时:
They rewarded him for his honesty. = 他们因为他诚实而奖励了他。
The teacher rewarded the students with extra recess. = 老师奖励学生额外的课间休息时间。


现在完成时:
She has been rewarded for her years of service. = 她多年来的服务得到了回报。
He has not yet been rewarded for his efforts. = 他的努力尚未得到回报。


过去完成时:
They had already rewarded the winner before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经奖励了获胜者。
She had never been rewarded for her hard work before. = 她以前从未因为努力工作而得到过奖励。


将来时:
The company will reward its top performers. = 公司将奖励其表现最佳的员工。
We will reward you for your cooperation. = 我们将奖励你的合作。


虚拟语气:
If I had worked harder, I would have been rewarded. = 如果我工作更努力，我就会得到奖励。
I wish I had been rewarded for my efforts. = 我希望我的努力得到回报。


被动语态:
The winner was rewarded with a trophy. = 获胜者获得了一个奖杯作为奖励。
The employees are rewarded for their achievements. = 员工因其成就而获得奖励。


疑问句:
Will you be rewarded for your work? = 你的工作会得到奖励吗？
How will the winners be rewarded? = 获胜者将如何获得奖励？


否定句:
He was not rewarded for his efforts. = 他的努力没有得到回报。
They did not reward the employee for his extra work. = 他们没有因为员工的额外工作而奖励他。


PHRASE->
rewarding experience = 有益的经历
reward system = 奖励制度
rich reward = 巨额奖励
moral reward = 道德上的奖励
deserved reward = 应得的奖励
