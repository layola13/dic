contextualise:/kənˈtekstʃuəlaɪz/ | vt. |语境化；使具有语境；解释语境| To place or study something within its context. To explain or interpret something in terms of its context.  |例句：It is important to contextualise the findings of the study within the broader social and economic trends.  将这项研究的结果置于更广泛的社会和经济趋势中加以解释非常重要。

例句：We need to contextualise this event within the history of the region. 我们需要将这一事件置于该地区的历史背景下进行解读。


近义词：situate, place in context, interpret, explain


反义词：decontextualize, isolate


词性：动词
