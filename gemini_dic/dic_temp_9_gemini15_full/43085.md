cowriting:/ˈkoʊraɪtɪŋ/ | n. |合作写作|The act of writing collaboratively, often involving multiple authors working together on a single piece of writing.  It's a process where individuals contribute their unique skills and perspectives to create a unified work.| Example: The novel was a product of extensive cowriting, with each author contributing chapters from different perspectives. 这部小说是广泛合作写作的产物，每个作者都从不同的角度贡献章节。|近义词：collaborative writing, joint authorship |反义词：sole authorship, individual writing |名词
