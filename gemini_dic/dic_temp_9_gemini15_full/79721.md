glueing:/ˈɡluːɪŋ/ | v. | 胶合；粘贴 | The act of sticking things together using glue.  It refers to the process of applying glue and joining surfaces. | Example:  He was glueing the broken vase back together. 他正在把破花瓶粘起来。  The carpenter was glueing the pieces of wood. 木匠正在粘木头碎片。|近义词：pasting, sticking, adhering | 反义词：separating, detaching


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am glueing the picture to the wall. = 我正在把图画粘到墙上。
She glues the stamps into her album. = 她把邮票粘到她的集邮册里。


一般过去时:
He glued the two pieces of fabric together. = 他把两块布粘在一起了。
They glued the poster on the board. = 他们把海报贴在黑板上了。


现在完成时:
I have glued all the pieces of the model together. = 我已经把模型的所有部件都粘在一起了。
She has glued the broken toy back together. = 她已经把坏掉的玩具粘好了。


过去完成时:
He had glued the parts together before painting them. = 他在涂漆之前已经把零件粘在一起了。
They had glued the wallpaper before I arrived. = 我到之前他们已经把壁纸贴好了。


将来时:
I will glue the broken plate tomorrow. = 我明天会把破盘子粘起来。
She will be glueing the tiles later this afternoon. = 她今天下午晚些时候会粘瓷砖。


虚拟语气:
If I had some glue, I would glue this broken chair. = 如果我有胶水，我会把这张坏椅子粘起来。
I wish I had glued the paper correctly. = 我真希望我把纸粘对了。


被动语态:
The broken vase was glued back together. = 破花瓶被粘起来了。
The poster is being glued to the wall. = 海报正在被贴到墙上。


疑问句:
Are you glueing the pieces together? = 你正在把这些碎片粘在一起吗？
Is she glueing the picture to the cardboard? = 她正在把图画粘到硬纸板上吗？


否定句:
I am not glueing anything right now. = 我现在什么也没粘。
She didn't glue the button back on. = 她没有把纽扣缝回去。


PHRASE->
glueing together = 粘合在一起
glueing down = 粘牢
glueing on = 粘上
glueing up = 堵塞；封堵
hot glueing = 热熔胶粘合
