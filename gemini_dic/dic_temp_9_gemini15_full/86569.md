heliconia:/ˌhɛlɪˈkoʊniə/| n. |鹤望兰属植物，蝎尾蕉|Heliconia is a genus of flowering plants in the family Heliconiaceae.  They are native to the tropical Americas and the Pacific Ocean islands and are known for their brightly colored bracts that resemble lobster claws.  |Example: The vibrant heliconias added a tropical touch to the garden.  这艳丽的蝎尾蕉为花园增添了热带风情。|近义词：false bird of paradise (though this refers to a specific species)|反义词：无
