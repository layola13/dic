palea:/ˈpælɪə/| n.|颖片，护颖|The palea is one of the two bracts enclosing a floret in grasses.  It is typically smaller and thinner than the lemma.|例句：The palea and lemma together protect the flower's reproductive parts. 颖片和外稃共同保护花的生殖部分。|The lemma is the outer bract, while the palea is the inner bract.|近义词：glume (in a broader sense)|反义词：lemma (in relation to a grass floret)
