reconcentrating: /ˌriːˈkɒnsənˌtreɪtɪŋ/ | v. |重新集中；重新集中精力 | The act of concentrating again; focusing one's attention or efforts anew.  It implies a previous state of being dispersed or unfocused, followed by a renewed effort to bring things together. |例句：After a long break, she was reconcentrating her efforts on finishing her dissertation.  =  长时间休息后，她重新集中精力完成她的论文。


例句：The company is reconcentrating its resources on its core products. = 公司正在将其资源重新集中在其核心产品上。


近义词：refocusing, refocusing one's attention, regrouping, recomposing


反义词：dispersing, diffusing, scattering


词性：动词 (verb)
