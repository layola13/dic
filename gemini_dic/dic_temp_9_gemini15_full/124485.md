moulders:/ˈmoʊldərz/| v. n.| 发霉；使腐烂；塑造；(动词的第三人称单数现在式)|To decay or rot; to shape or mold something.|例句：The old bread is starting to moulder. 这块旧面包开始发霉了。 The ancient ruins are slowly mouldering away. 古代遗址正在慢慢地腐朽。|近义词：decay, rot, decompose, shape, form|反义词：preserve, conserve


USAGE->

The old wood moulders slowly. = 旧木头慢慢地腐烂。
He moulders the clay into a beautiful vase. = 他把粘土塑造成一个美丽的瓶子。
