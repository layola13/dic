kHz:/kɪloʊhɜːrts/|abbr.|千赫兹|kilohertz (kHz) is an abbreviation for kilohertz, a unit of frequency equal to one thousand hertz (1000 Hz).  It's commonly used to measure the frequency of sound waves, radio waves, and other oscillating phenomena.|例句：The radio station broadcasts at 98.5 kHz.  这个电台以98.5千赫兹广播。The audio signal has a frequency of 20 kHz.  音频信号的频率为20千赫兹。|近义词：kilocycles per second (kcps) (less common)|反义词：无
