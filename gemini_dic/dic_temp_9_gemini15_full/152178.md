patriarchal: /ˌpeɪtriˈɑːrkəl/ | adj. | 父权制的；家长统治的 | Relating to or characteristic of a patriarch or patriarchs; relating to a system of society or government controlled by men.  |  例句：Many societies are still deeply patriarchal, with men holding most positions of power. 许多社会仍然是高度父权制的，男性占据着大部分权力职位。

例句：The patriarchal structure of the company made it difficult for women to advance. 这家公司的父权结构使得女性难以晋升。


近义词：paternalistic, male-dominated, authoritarian | 反义词：matriarchal, egalitarian, feminist
