sellotapes:/ˈsɛləteɪps/| n. |胶带；透明胶带|A brand name for adhesive tape, often used generically to refer to any type of cellophane tape.  It's a thin, transparent adhesive tape used for sealing envelopes, packaging items, and various other purposes.|例句：I need some sellotape to fix this package. 我需要一些胶带修补这个包裹。

例句：She used sellotape to mend the ripped page. 她用胶带粘合了撕破的纸页。


近义词：adhesive tape, cellophane tape, sticky tape|反义词：None (as it describes a type of object)|名词
