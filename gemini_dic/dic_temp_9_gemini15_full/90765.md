humoring: /ˈhjuːmərɪŋ/ | v. | 迁就；纵容；逗乐 |

英文解析:To humor someone means to indulge or comply with their wishes or whims, often to avoid conflict or to appease them. It can also mean to entertain or amuse someone.

例句:
He was humoring his child by letting her have another cookie. = 他迁就他的孩子，让她又吃了一块饼干。

She humored her elderly mother's strange requests. = 她迁就她年迈母亲的奇怪要求。


近义词:indulging, appeasing, accommodating, entertaining, amusing

反义词:opposing, resisting, defying
