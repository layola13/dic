citraconic: /ˌsɪtrəˈkɒnɪk/ | adj. | 柠檬酸的，反丁烯二酸的 | Citraconic refers to a type of unsaturated dicarboxylic acid, structurally related to itaconic acid and mesaconic acid.  It's characterized by a specific arrangement of its carbon-carbon double bond and carboxyl groups. | Example:  The research focused on the polymerization of citraconic anhydride. 这项研究的重点是反丁烯二酸酐的聚合。 | 近义词:  mesaconic, itaconic (related isomers) | 反义词:  saturated (in reference to the double bond) | 词性:形容词
