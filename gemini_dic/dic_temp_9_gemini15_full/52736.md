dikes:/daɪks/| n.|堤坝，防洪堤|A dike is an artificial wall or embankment built to prevent the flooding of low-lying land by the sea or a river.|The Netherlands is famous for its extensive system of dikes.|近义词：embankment, levee, dam|反义词：breach (in a dike)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dikes protect the city from flooding. = 堤坝保护城市免受洪水侵袭。
Farmers maintain the dikes regularly. = 农民定期维护堤坝。


一般过去时:
The dikes held during the storm. = 堤坝在风暴中屹立不倒。
They repaired the damaged dikes quickly. = 他们迅速修复了受损的堤坝。


现在完成时:
They have strengthened the dikes recently. = 他们最近加固了堤坝。

过去完成时:
