pregalvanizing:/ˌpriːˈɡælvənaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 预镀锌的；镀锌处理过的 |  Pre-galvanizing refers to the process of applying a zinc coating to a metal (usually steel) before further fabrication or processing. This protects the metal from corrosion.  | Example: The pregalvanized steel sheets were used to construct the building's frame. 预镀锌钢板被用来建造建筑物的框架。 | 近义词：precoated, zinc-coated | 反义词：uncoated, ungalvanized
