persia:/ˈpɜːʒə/|/ˈpɜːʃə/| n. |波斯；伊朗|Ancient kingdom and region in southwestern Asia, now mainly Iran.  The name derives from the Old Persian word *Parsa*, meaning "Persian". The term is often used historically, but "Iran" is the modern official name. |Example: Persia was once a vast and powerful empire. 波斯曾经是一个巨大而强大的帝国。  The rugs are a beautiful example of Persian craftsmanship.  这些地毯是波斯工艺的精美典范。|近义词：Iran, Elam|反义词：None
