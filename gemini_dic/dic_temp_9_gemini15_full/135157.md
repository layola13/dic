nonopacity: /ˌnɒnəʊˈpæsɪti/ | adj. | 非不透明性；透明度 | Lack of opacity; the quality or state of being transparent or translucent.  It refers to the ability of something to allow light to pass through it, either completely (transparent) or partially (translucent).  The opposite of opacity. |The nonopacity of the glass allowed the sunlight to stream through. 玻璃的透明度使阳光能够穿透。  The nonopacity of the stained-glass window allowed some light to filter into the room. 彩绘玻璃窗的半透明性让一些光线过滤到房间里。|近义词：transparency, translucence |反义词：opacity
