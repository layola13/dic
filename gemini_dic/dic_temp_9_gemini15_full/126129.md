mutilate:/ˈmjuːtɪleɪt/ | vt. |使残缺不全；使严重受损；伤害；毁坏 | To inflict serious damage on something, especially by cutting or tearing off a part.  |例句：The vandals mutilated the statue.  破坏者毁坏了雕像。

例句：The accident severely mutilated his hand.  事故严重地损坏了他的手。

近义词：damage, injure, maim, cripple, deform | 反义词：repair, restore, heal
