geosystems:/ˈdʒioʊˌsɪstəmz/ | n. | 地球系统；地学系统 |  A geosystem is a complex, interacting system of Earth's physical, chemical, biological, and human components. It encompasses the interactions between the atmosphere, hydrosphere, lithosphere, biosphere, and anthroposphere.  The study of geosystems involves understanding the interconnectedness of these components and how they influence each other. | Example:  The Amazon rainforest is a vital geosystem, playing a crucial role in regulating global climate.  亚马逊雨林是一个重要的地球系统，在调节全球气候方面发挥着至关重要的作用。 |近义词：Earth system, planetary system |反义词：N/A | 名词
