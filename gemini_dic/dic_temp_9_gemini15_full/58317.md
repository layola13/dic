dryer:/ˈdraɪər/| n. |烘干机，干燥器 |A dryer is a machine that uses heat or air to dry clothes or other items.  |例句：I put the wet clothes in the dryer.我把湿衣服放进了烘干机。|例句：The industrial dryer is used to dry grains.工业干燥机用于干燥谷物。|近义词：dehydrator, drier|反义词：humidifier
