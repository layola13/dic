slavers:/ˈsleɪvərz/| n.|奴隶贩子，奴隶船|People who engaged in the slave trade, buying, selling, and transporting enslaved people.  They often operated ships specifically designed for transporting slaves across vast distances under inhumane conditions.|Example: The slavers packed the slaves tightly into the ship's hold, resulting in many deaths.|近义词：slave traders, traffickers|反义词：abolitionists
