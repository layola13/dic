serialized:/ˈsɪəriəlaɪzd/| adj.|连续剧的；连载的；系列化的| Consisting of or arranged in a series; published or broadcast in installments.|例句：The novel was serialized in a magazine. 这部小说在杂志上连载。 The TV show is a serialized drama. 这部电视剧是连续剧。|近义词：sequential, episodic, installment|反义词：single, complete


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The magazine serializes a new novel each year. = 这本杂志每年连载一部新小说。
This website serializes the latest news. = 这个网站连载最新的新闻。


一般过去时:
The newspaper serialized the story over several weeks. = 这家报纸在几周内连载了这个故事。
They serialized the book into a TV series. = 他们把这本书改编成了电视剧。


现在完成时:
The author has serialized several novels in this magazine. = 这位作者在这本杂志上连载过几部小说。
The company has serialized its products into different lines. = 这家公司将其产品系列化成不同的产品线。


过去完成时:
Before the TV series aired, they had serialized the story in a magazine. = 在电视剧播出之前，他们已经在杂志上连载了这个故事。
The author had serialized the first three chapters before the publishing company contacted him. = 作者在出版社联系他之前已经连载了前三章。


将来时:
The company will serialize the data for easier processing. = 公司将对数据进行序列化以便于处理。
They will serialize the comic book into a movie. = 他们将把这部漫画书改编成电影。


虚拟语气:
If they serialized the book, it might be more popular. = 如果他们把这本书连载，它可能会更受欢迎。
I wish they had serialized the story in a more exciting way. = 我希望他们能以更激动人心的方式连载这个故事。


被动语态:
The story was serialized in the local newspaper. = 这个故事在当地报纸上连载。
The data is being serialized for transmission. = 数据正在序列化以进行传输。


疑问句:
Is the novel being serialized? = 这部小说正在连载吗？
Was the story serialized in a magazine or newspaper? = 这个故事是在杂志还是报纸上连载的？


否定句:
The novel wasn't serialized; it was published as a single volume. = 这部小说不是连载的；它是作为单行本出版的。
The company doesn't serialize its data anymore. = 公司不再序列化其数据了。
