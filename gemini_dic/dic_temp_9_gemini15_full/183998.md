reshorten: /ˌriːˈʃɔːrtn/ | vt. | 再次缩短；重新缩短 | To shorten something again or to a further extent.  |例句：We had to reshorten the rope after it stretched. 我们不得不再次缩短绳子，因为它伸长了。


例句：The tailor had to reshorten the dress after the first alteration.  裁缝在第一次修改后不得不再次修改裙子的长度。


近义词：shorten, curtail, abbreviate | 反义词：lengthen, extend, prolong | 词性：动词
