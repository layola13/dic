cupper:/ˈkʌpər/| n. |铜匠，制杯者|A cupper is a person who makes or repairs cups, or who works with copper.  The term is somewhat archaic and less common in modern usage.  It can also refer to someone who practices cupping therapy.|例句：The old cupper skillfully repaired the dented copper pot.  老铜匠熟练地修理了凹陷的铜锅。|近义词：coppersmith, metalsmith|反义词：无
