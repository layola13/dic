pictograms:/ˈpɪktoʊɡræmz/| n. |象形文字，图画文字 |Pictures or symbols used to represent words or ideas.  Pictograms are often used in signage and communication where language barriers might exist. They are a type of visual communication. |例句：The ancient Egyptians used pictograms extensively in their writing system. 古代埃及人广泛使用象形文字书写。


例句：The restroom signs used pictograms to indicate men's and women's facilities.  洗手间的标志使用象形文字来指示男女厕所。


近义词：ideograms, symbols, icons


反义词：alphabet (in the sense of a phonetic writing system)


词性:名词
