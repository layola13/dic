receiver: /rɪˈsiːvər/ | n. | 接收者；收受者；电话听筒 | A person or thing that receives something.  A device for receiving signals, such as a telephone receiver. |例句：He was the receiver of the stolen goods. 他是赃物的接收者。

The radio receiver picked up a faint signal. 收音机接收到了微弱的信号。|近义词：recipient, acceptor, handset |反义词：sender, transmitter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He receives many emails every day. = 他每天收到很多邮件。
The company receives a lot of applications. = 公司收到很多申请。

一般过去时:
She received a letter from her friend. = 她收到她朋友的一封信。
They received a warm welcome. = 他们受到了热烈的欢迎。

现在完成时:
I have received your message. = 我已经收到你的消息了。
We have received many complaints recently. = 最近我们收到了很多投诉。

过去完成时:
They had received the news before I called. = 在我打电话之前，他们已经收到了消息。
She had received several awards by the time she was 30. = 到她30岁的时候，她已经获得了好几个奖项。

将来时:
He will receive his salary next week. = 他下周将收到他的薪水。
We will receive the results soon. = 我们很快就会收到结果。

虚拟语气:
If I received a million dollars, I would travel the world. = 如果我收到一百万美元，我会周游世界。
I wish I had received his email earlier. = 我希望我早点收到他的邮件。

被动语态:
The package was received yesterday. = 包裹昨天收到了。
The information is received by the computer. = 信息由电脑接收。

疑问句:
Did you receive my email? = 你收到我的邮件了吗？
Has she received the invitation? = 她收到邀请了吗？

否定句:
He did not receive any calls. = 他没有接到任何电话。
They haven't received a reply yet. = 他们还没有收到回复。
