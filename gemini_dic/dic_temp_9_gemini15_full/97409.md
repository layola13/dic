infringement:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒmənt/ | n. |侵犯；违反 | The act of violating a law, rule, or agreement; an instance of such violation.  It often involves encroaching on someone's rights or property. |例句：The unauthorized use of copyrighted material is an infringement of intellectual property rights. 未经授权使用受版权保护的材料是对知识产权的侵犯。|例句：His actions constituted a clear infringement of the company's policies. 他的行为构成了对公司规章制度的明显违反。|近义词：violation, breach, transgression, encroachment |反义词：compliance, observance, adherence |名词
