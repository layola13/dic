They represent a compromise or attempt to appeal to a broad audience by avoiding controversial stances. | Example:  His political speeches were full of middle-of-the-roadisms, failing to offer any clear policy positions.  他的政治演讲充满了中庸之道的言论，未能提出任何明确的政策立场。|近义词：moderation, centrism, compromise, neutrality |反义词：extremism, radicalism, bias
