radiosignature: /ˈreɪdioʊsɪɡnətʃər/ | n. |无线电特征信号，无线电特征|The unique pattern of radio waves emitted by an object, used for identification and analysis.  This can refer to natural sources like planets or stars, or artificial sources like spacecraft or communication devices.  |例句：Scientists are studying the radiosignature of pulsars to understand their behavior. 科学家正在研究脉冲星的无线电特征信号以了解它们的活动方式。

例句：The spacecraft's unique radiosignature allowed ground control to track its position accurately.  航天器的独特无线电特征信号使地面控制能够准确地跟踪其位置。


近义词：radio emission, radio signal, spectral signature |反义词：无|词性：名词
