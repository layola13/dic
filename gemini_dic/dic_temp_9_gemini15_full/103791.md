jousted:/ˈdʒuːstɪd/| v.| 骑马比武；争论；对抗 | To engage in a joust; to contend or argue forcefully. |例句：The knights jousted fiercely in the tournament.  骑士们在锦标赛中激烈地比武。


例句：They jousted over the best way to solve the problem.  他们就解决问题的最佳方法进行了激烈的争论。


近义词：fought, battled, contended, argued, clashed


反义词：agreed, cooperated, compromised
