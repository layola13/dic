front-of-house:/frʌnt əv haʊs/| n. |前厅；前台；前场|The area of a business, especially a restaurant or theater, that is accessible to the public.  It encompasses customer-facing aspects of the operation.|Example: The front-of-house staff greeted guests warmly. 前台员工热情地接待了客人。|近义词：public area, reception area|反义词：back-of-house
