considerable:/kənˈsɪdərəbl/| adj.|相当大的；值得考虑的；重要的|  Meaning:  Significantly large in size, extent, amount, or importance. It suggests something is substantial enough to warrant attention or action. | Example:  He showed considerable skill in handling the difficult situation. 他在处理棘手局面时展现出相当的技巧。  The project requires considerable funding. 这个项目需要大量的资金。|Synonyms: substantial, significant, important, noteworthy, appreciable|Antonyms: insignificant, negligible, minor, trifling|词性:形容词
