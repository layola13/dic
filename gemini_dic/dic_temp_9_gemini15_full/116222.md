masonry:/ˈmeɪsənri/ | n. | 石工；砌石结构；石砌建筑 | The art or work of a mason; construction of stone or brick.  It involves the skillful arrangement and bonding of stones or bricks to create walls, structures, or other features. |例句：The old church boasts impressive masonry. 这座古老的教堂拥有令人印象深刻的石砌结构。

例句：The ancient walls are a fine example of Roman masonry.  这些古老的城墙是罗马石工的绝佳范例。


近义词：stonework, brickwork | 反义词：woodwork, framework
