enshrinement:/ɪnˈʃraɪnmənt/ | n. | 奉为经典；神圣化；铭记；珍藏 | The act of enshrining; the state of being enshrined.  It implies a special reverence or respect for something, often placing it in a position of honor or importance.  | 例句：The museum's enshrinement of the artist's work cemented his legacy. 博物馆对这位艺术家作品的奉为经典巩固了他的传奇地位。


The enshrinement of human rights in the constitution is a landmark achievement.宪法中对人权的奉为经典是一项具有里程碑意义的成就。| 近义词：consecration, sanctification, preservation, commemoration | 反义词：desecration, disregard, neglect, forgetting |
