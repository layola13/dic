plastron:/ˈplæstrən/| n.|胸甲，腹甲|A plastron is the flat, usually ventral (belly) part of the shell of a turtle or tortoise, or a similar structure in other animals.  It's also used to describe a decorative piece of clothing or armor worn on the chest.|The tortoise's plastron was smooth and unblemished.|近义词：breastplate, ventral shield|反义词：carapace
