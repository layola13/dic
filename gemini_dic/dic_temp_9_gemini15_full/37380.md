commentate:/ˈkɒmənteɪt/ /ˈkɑːmənˌteɪt/| vt.|评论；解说；为…做解说|To provide spoken commentary on an event, typically a sporting event or broadcast.|例句：He commentates on football matches for a national radio station. 他为国家电台解说足球比赛。|例句：The news anchor commentated on the latest political developments. 新闻主播对最新的政治发展进行了评论。|近义词：narrate, describe, explain|反义词：None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He commentates on the game every week. 他每周都为比赛做解说。
She commentates on current events for a major news network. 她为一家主要新闻网络评论时事。

一般过去时:
He commentated on the Olympic Games last year. 他去年为奥运会做了解说。
She commentated on the election results for the local news. 她为当地新闻评论了选举结果。

现在完成时:
He has commentated on hundreds of sporting events. 他已经为数百场体育赛事做过解说。
She has commentated on this topic many times before. 她之前已经多次评论过这个话题。

过去完成时:
He had commentated on the match before the final whistle blew. 在终场哨响之前，他已经为比赛做了解说。
She had commentated on the situation before the press conference began. 在新闻发布会开始之前，她已经对这种情况进行了评论。

将来时:
He will commentate on the World Cup final next month. 他下个月将为世界杯决赛做解说。
She will commentate on the upcoming debate live on TV. 她将在电视上对即将到来的辩论进行现场评论。

虚拟语气:
If he were to commentate on the race, it would be very exciting. 如果他来解说这场比赛，将会非常精彩。
I wish she had commentated on the film more critically. 我希望她能更批判性地评论这部电影。

被动语态:
The game was commentated on by a well-known sports journalist. 这场比赛由一位著名的体育记者解说。
The event was commentated on live from the scene. 活动现场进行了现场解说。

疑问句:
Who will commentate on the match tonight? 今晚谁将为比赛做解说？
Will she commentate on the election results? 她会评论选举结果吗？

否定句:
He didn't commentate on the controversial issue. 他没有评论这个有争议的问题。
She will not commentate on the incident any further. 她不会再对这一事件进行评论。
