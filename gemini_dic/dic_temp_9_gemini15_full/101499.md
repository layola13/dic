irises:/ˈaɪrɪsɪz/| n.|虹膜；鸢尾花；(复数)|The colored part of the eye, surrounding the pupil.  Also, a genus of flowering plants with showy flowers.|例句：The irises in her eyes were a striking shade of green.  她的眼睛虹膜是令人惊艳的绿色。

例句：The garden was ablaze with colorful irises. 花园里盛开着五彩缤纷的鸢尾花。


近义词：pupil (referring to the eye's colored part),  flag (referring to the flower)
反义词：无


词源: From Old French *iris, from Latin īris, from Greek ἴρις (iris), "rainbow," referring to the variety of colors found in the flower, and later applied to the eye's colored part.
