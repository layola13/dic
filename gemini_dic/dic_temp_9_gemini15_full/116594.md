matriculates:/məˈtrɪkjuleɪts/ | v. |正式入学；注册入学 | To be officially enrolled as a student at a college or university.  |  He matriculated at Oxford University in 2020. 他于2020年在牛津大学注册入学。 | enroll, register, enter |  expel, withdraw | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He matriculates at the university this fall. = 他今年秋天在大学注册入学。
She matriculates at Harvard next year. = 她明年在哈佛大学注册入学。


一般过去时:
I matriculated at Cambridge in 1998. = 我在1998年剑桥大学注册入学。
He matriculated from a prestigious high school. = 他毕业于一所著名的中学。


现在完成时:
She has matriculated at the medical school. = 她已在医学院注册入学。
They have matriculated and are now taking classes. = 他们已经注册入学，现在正在上课。


过去完成时:
Before he started his graduate studies, he had matriculated at a different university. = 在他开始研究生学习之前，他曾在另一所大学注册入学。
She had matriculated at the college and then transferred to another institution. = 她已经在学院注册入学，然后转学到另一所学校。


将来时:
I will matriculate at the University of London next September. = 我明年九月将在伦敦大学注册入学。
He will matriculate at the engineering school next year. = 他明年将在工程学院注册入学。


虚拟语气:
If I had matriculated earlier, I would have more credits now. = 如果我早点注册入学，我现在就会有更多的学分。
I wish I had matriculated at a different university. = 我希望我在不同的大学注册入学。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
