pointedly:/ˈpɔɪntɪdli/ | adv. | 直截了当地；尖锐地；故意地 | In a pointed manner; with emphasis or intention.  It suggests a deliberate and often critical or challenging way of speaking or acting. | 例句：He pointedly ignored her question. 他故意不理睬她的问题。

She pointedly refused to answer. 她尖锐地拒绝回答。| 近义词：sharply, directly, deliberately, pointedly, emphatically | 反义词：indirectly, vaguely, subtly, casually
