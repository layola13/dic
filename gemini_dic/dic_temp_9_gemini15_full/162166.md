pre-Israelites:/ˌpriːˈɪzreɪəlaɪts/| n. |以色列人之前的民族；以色列人的祖先|The term "pre-Israelites" refers to the groups of people who inhabited the land of Canaan before the arrival and settlement of the Israelites.  These groups are mentioned in the Bible and archaeological findings suggest a complex and diverse population.  The exact ethnic and cultural relationships between these groups and the later Israelites are still debated by scholars.|The archaeological evidence suggests that the pre-Israelites were a diverse collection of Canaanite tribes.|近义词:Canaanites, ancient inhabitants of Canaan|反义词:Israelites
