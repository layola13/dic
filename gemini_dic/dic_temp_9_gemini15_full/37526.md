commonalities:/ˌkɒmənˈælɪtiz/ | /ˌkɑːmənˈælɪtiz/ | n. |共同点，共通之处 |Features or qualities that are shared by two or more things.  Commonalities can refer to similarities in characteristics, attributes, or experiences. |The commonalities between the two paintings are striking. 这两幅画的共同点令人印象深刻。 |similarities, resemblances, parallels, affinities |differences, dissimilarities, contrasts
