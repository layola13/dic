goosander:/ˈɡuːsændər/| n. | 鹊鸭 | A large diving duck with a long, slender bill, a crested head, and a distinctive plumage varying by sex and season.  Goosanders are known for their fish-eating diet and their ability to dive deep to catch prey. | Example: We saw a goosander diving for fish in the lake. 我们看到一只鹊鸭在湖里潜水捕鱼。| 近义词：merganser (though this is a broader term encompassing several species)| 反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with ducks that don't dive deep for fish.
