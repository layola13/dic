major-domo:/ˌmeɪdʒərˈdoʊmoʊ/| n. |管家；总管 |A major-domo is the chief steward or manager of a household, especially a large one.  They are responsible for overseeing the household staff and managing the day-to-day operations.  |例句：The major-domo announced the arrival of the guests.  管家宣布客人到了。

The family relied on their major-domo to handle all their affairs.  这个家庭依靠他们的管家来处理所有事务。|近义词：steward, butler, household manager |反义词：None specifically, but words implying lack of organization or management could be considered antonyms.
