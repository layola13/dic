iconoclastic:/ˌaɪkənəˈklæstɪk/| adj.|破坏偶像的；反传统的；挑战权威的 |Attacking or rejecting cherished beliefs and institutions.  The term is derived from the historical practice of iconoclasm, the destruction of religious images.  |例句：His iconoclastic views on religion often caused controversy. 他对宗教的颠覆性观点常常引起争议。


例句：The artist's iconoclastic style challenged traditional notions of beauty.这位艺术家的反传统风格挑战了传统的审美观念。


近义词：rebellious, subversive, radical, unconventional, heretical


反义词：traditional, conventional, orthodox, conservative


词性：形容词
