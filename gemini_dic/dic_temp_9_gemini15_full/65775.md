everyone: /ˈɛvriwʌn/ | pron. | 每个人，人人 | everyone refers to all the people in a particular group or place. It is used as a pronoun and does not take a plural verb. | Everyone is welcome to attend the party.  每个人都欢迎参加派对。 | everybody, all, each person | nobody, no one


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Everyone knows the answer. = 每个人都知道答案。
Everyone likes this song. = 每个人都喜欢这首歌。

一般过去时:
Everyone enjoyed the concert. = 每个人都享受了这场音乐会。
Everyone went home early. = 每个人都早早回家了。

现在完成时:
Everyone has received an invitation. = 每个人都收到了邀请。
Everyone has finished their work. = 每个人都完成了他们的工作。

过去完成时:
Everyone had left before the rain started. = 在下雨开始之前每个人都离开了。
Everyone had finished eating by the time I arrived. = 我到达的时候每个人都已经吃完了。

将来时:
Everyone will be there tomorrow. = 明天每个人都会在那里。
Everyone will receive a prize. = 每个人都会收到奖品。

虚拟语气:
If everyone cooperated, the project would be successful. = 如果每个人都合作，项目就会成功。
I wish everyone understood the importance of this. = 我希望每个人都能理解这件事的重要性。

被动语态:
Everyone was surprised by the news. = 每个人都被这个消息震惊了。
Everyone is expected to participate. = 每个人都被期望参与。

疑问句:
Is everyone here? = 每个人都在吗？
Does everyone understand? = 每个人都明白吗？

否定句:
Not everyone agrees with the decision. = 不是每个人都同意这个决定。
Everyone didn't like the movie. = 不是每个人都喜欢这部电影。(注意：此句并非完全正确的否定表达，更准确的表达是Not everyone liked the movie.)
