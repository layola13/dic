denounce:/dɪˈnaʊns/ | vt. | 公开谴责；告发；声讨 | To publicly condemn or criticize (someone or something).  |例句：The government denounced the terrorist attack. 政府谴责了恐怖袭击。

例句：He denounced his former colleagues for their unethical behavior. 他谴责了他的前同事的不道德行为。


近义词：condemn, criticize, rebuke, censure | 反义词：praise, commend, applaud


词源：From Old French denuncer, from Latin denunciāre "to announce publicly, proclaim, report," from de- "down, away" + nuntiāre "to announce, report," from nuntius "messenger."

一般现在时:
The activists denounce the new policy.  活动家们谴责这项新政策。
They denounce corruption in government. 他们谴责政府腐败。

一般过去时:
The senator denounced the proposed legislation. 参议员谴责了拟议中的立法。
She denounced her husband's infidelity to the press. 她向媒体揭露了她丈夫的不忠。

现在完成时:
The organization has denounced the violence against civilians. 该组织已经谴责了针对平民的暴力行为。
He has repeatedly denounced the regime's human rights abuses. 他一再谴责该政权侵犯人权的行为。

过去完成时:
The protesters had denounced the government's inaction before the riot.  暴乱发生前，抗议者们已经谴责了政府的不作为。
The witness had denounced the criminal to the police.  证人之前已经向警方告发了罪犯。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If he denounced the crime, he might face retaliation. 如果他举报了这起犯罪，他可能会面临报复。
I wish the media would denounce the spread of misinformation. 我希望媒体能够谴责虚假信息的传播。

被动语态:
The proposed law was denounced by human rights groups. 这项拟议的法律受到了人权组织的谴责。
His actions were denounced as unethical. 他的行为被谴责为不道德的。

疑问句:
Will they denounce the company's actions?  他们会谴责这家公司的行为吗？
Did anyone denounce the fraud? 有人举报这起欺诈行为吗？

否定句:
He did not denounce the unfair treatment. 他没有谴责这种不公平的待遇。
They refused to denounce their beliefs. 他们拒绝放弃自己的信仰。
