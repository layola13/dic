例句：The writer's style reflects years of intense cerebration.  这位作家的文风反映了他多年的深入思考。


近义词：thinking, cogitation, reflection, contemplation |反义词：inaction, stupor, unconsciousness |词性:名词


词源:  From Latin *cerebratio, from cerebrum "brain".
