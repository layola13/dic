idiocy:/ˈɪdiəsi/| n. |愚蠢；蠢事；白痴行为 |Extreme foolishness or lack of intelligence.  It often implies a lack of judgment or common sense, and can refer to a single action or a general characteristic. |His decision to invest all his money in that scheme was sheer idiocy. 他的决定把所有钱投资到那个计划中简直是愚蠢至极。 The whole project was marked by idiocy from start to finish.  整个项目从头到尾都充满了愚蠢的行为。|foolishness, stupidity, silliness, absurdity |intelligence, wisdom, brilliance, sagacity
