sagaciousness:/səˈɡeɪʃəsnəs/ | n. |睿智；精明；明智 |The quality of having or showing great wisdom and understanding.  It implies a deep understanding of people, situations, and the world around them, often combined with shrewd judgment and foresight. |例句：Her sagaciousness in handling the negotiations impressed everyone.  她的谈判技巧睿智老练，给每个人留下了深刻印象。

The company's success is a testament to the sagaciousness of its leadership.  公司的成功证明了其领导层的睿智。|近义词：wisdom, shrewdness, intelligence, discernment, perspicacity |反义词：foolishness, stupidity, ignorance, naivete
