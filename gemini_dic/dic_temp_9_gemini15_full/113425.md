lymphocytotoxin:/ˌlɪmfəʊˈsaɪtɒksɪn/| n. |淋巴细胞毒素|A substance that is toxic to lymphocytes.  Lymphocytotoxins are produced by certain cells of the immune system and can play a role in both the immune response and autoimmune diseases.  They can kill lymphocytes directly or indirectly by damaging their DNA or other cellular components.|Example:  The research team investigated the effects of the newly discovered lymphocytotoxin on T-cell populations.  研究团队调查了新发现的淋巴细胞毒素对T细胞群的影响。|近义词：lymphotoxin|反义词：lymphocyte growth factor|词性：名词
