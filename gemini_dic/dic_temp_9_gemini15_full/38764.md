condor:/ˈkɒndɔːr/| n. |秃鹰，神鹰|A large, powerful bird of prey found in the Andes Mountains of South America and California.|例句：The Andean condor is a majestic bird with a wingspan of up to 10 feet.  安第斯秃鹰是一种雄伟的鸟，翼展可达10英尺。|例句：California condors were once on the brink of extinction, but conservation efforts have helped their numbers increase. 加利福尼亚秃鹰曾经濒临灭绝，但保护工作帮助它们的数量增加了。|近义词：vulture, eagle |反义词：None
