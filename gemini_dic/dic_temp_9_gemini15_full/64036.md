epithem:/ˈɛpɪθɛm/| n.|主题句；格言；典型；代表|A concise sentence or phrase expressing an idea or maxim; an embodiment or representative example.|The inscription on the tombstone served as an epithem of his life's work. 墓碑上的铭文是他毕生工作的主题句。|近义词：maxim, motto, proverb, embodiment|反义词：None specifically.


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  Therefore, tense-related explanations (past, present, future, etc.) are irrelevant.  The usage is explained below demonstrating different sentence structures.


Simple Sentence:
The book's epithem perfectly captured its essence. 这本书的主题句完美地捕捉到了它的本质。

Compound Sentence:
The artist's work was an epithem of modernism, and it was widely praised.这位艺术家的作品是现代主义的典型代表，并受到了广泛赞扬。

Complex Sentence:
Because the poem's epithem was so powerful, it resonated deeply with readers. 由于这首诗的主题句如此有力，它引起了读者的强烈共鸣。

Passive Voice:
His life was considered an epithem of selfless service. 他的生活被认为是无私奉献的典范。
