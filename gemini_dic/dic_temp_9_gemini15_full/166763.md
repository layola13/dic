presliced:/ˈpriːslaɪst/| adj.|预切片的，预先切好的|Already cut into slices before being sold or used.  This adjective describes food items that have been sliced beforehand, typically for convenience.|例句：The supermarket sells presliced cheese for easy snacking. 超市出售预切片奶酪，方便零食食用。

The deli counter offers a selection of presliced meats. 熟食柜台提供各种预切肉类。


近义词：precut, sliced, ready-to-eat|反义词：whole, uncut|词性：形容词
