hints:/hɪnts/ | n. |暗示；提示；线索 | A hint is a suggestion or piece of information that indirectly indicates something.  It can be a subtle clue or a more direct piece of advice. |例句：He gave me a hint about the answer. 他给了我关于答案的提示。

例句：The detective followed the hints left at the crime scene.  侦探顺着犯罪现场留下的线索追查。


近义词：clue, suggestion, indication, implication, tip


反义词：clarification, explanation


词性:名词
