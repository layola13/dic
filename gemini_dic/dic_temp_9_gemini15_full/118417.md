merry-go-round: /ˈmɛriɡoʊraʊnd/ | n. |旋转木马，循环往复的事物 | A type of amusement ride consisting of a rotating circular platform with seats for riders.  The term can also be used figuratively to describe a situation that repeats itself endlessly. |例句：The children loved riding on the merry-go-round at the fair.孩子们喜欢在游乐场乘坐旋转木马。 The negotiations seemed like a never-ending merry-go-round. 谈判似乎像一个永无止境的循环。|近义词：carousel, roundabout, cycle |反义词：static situation, standstill
