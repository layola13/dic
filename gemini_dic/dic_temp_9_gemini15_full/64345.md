erased:/ɪˈreɪzd/ | vt. | 擦掉；抹去；删除 | To remove something written or drawn, or to remove information from a computer or other electronic device. |例句：He erased the mistake with an eraser. 他用橡皮擦掉了错误。

例句：The teacher erased the words from the board. 老师从黑板上擦掉了字。


近义词：rub out, delete, remove, wipe out | 反义词：write, draw, add, insert | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He erases the whiteboard every day. = 他每天都擦白板。
She erases her mistakes carefully. = 她仔细地擦掉她的错误。


一般过去时:
I erased the file by mistake. = 我不小心删除了文件。
He erased the drawing from the paper. = 他从纸上擦掉了图画。


现在完成时:
She has erased all the data from the hard drive. = 她已经从硬盘中删除了所有数据。
They have erased the evidence. = 他们已经销毁了证据。


过去完成时:
He had erased the message before I could read it. = 我还没来得及看他就删掉了信息。
She had erased the drawing before anyone noticed it. = 在任何人注意到之前，她已经擦掉了图画。


将来时:
I will erase this file later. = 我稍后会删除此文件。
He will erase the chalk marks on the wall. = 他会擦掉墙上的粉笔痕迹。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the importance, I wouldn't have erased the file. = 如果我知道重要性，我就不会删除文件了。
I wish I hadn't erased that photo. = 我真希望我没有删除那张照片。


被动语态:
The message was erased from the server. = 信息已从服务器中删除。
The drawing was erased accidentally. = 图画是不小心擦掉的。


疑问句:

否定句:
