potentates:/ˈpoʊtənˌteɪts/|/ˈpəʊtənˌteɪts/| n. |当权者；统治者；有权势的人 |Powerful rulers, especially monarchs.  The word often implies absolute power and authority. |例句：The potentates of Europe met to discuss the war. 欧洲的统治者们会面讨论战争。

例句：The ancient potentates ruled with an iron fist.  古代的统治者们铁腕统治。


近义词：monarchs, rulers, sovereigns, magnates, authorities

反义词：subjects, commoners, the powerless


词性：名词
