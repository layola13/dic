composed:/kəmˈpoʊzd/| adj. v.| 沉着的；镇定的；组成的；构成的 | Composed describes someone who is calm and in control of their emotions, or something that is made up of various parts. |例句：She remained composed despite the chaos. 她即使在混乱中也保持镇静。  The painting is composed of vibrant colors. 这幅画由鲜艳的色彩构成。|近义词：calm, serene, tranquil, collected |反义词：agitated, flustered, disturbed, frantic
