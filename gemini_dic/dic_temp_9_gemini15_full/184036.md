residential: /ˌrezɪˈdɛnʃəl/ | adj. | 住宅的；居住的 | Relating to or consisting of residences; used or designed for living in.  |例句：That area is primarily residential, with quiet streets and family homes.  那个地区主要是住宅区，街道安静，住着很多家庭。

例句：The new development includes both residential and commercial properties. 新开发项目包括住宅和商业地产。


近义词：domestic, dwelling, inhabited | 反义词：commercial, industrial


词源:  From Middle French *résidentiel, from Latin *residēns, present participle of residēre "to sit back, remain, dwell".

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The building is residential. = 这栋建筑是住宅。
This area is mainly residential. = 这个区域主要是住宅区。


一般过去时:
The area was primarily residential before the factory was built. = 在工厂建成之前，这个区域主要是住宅区。
That house was residential, but it's now a shop. = 那栋房子以前是住宅，现在是商店了。


现在完成时:
The neighborhood has become increasingly residential. = 这个街区变得越来越像住宅区了。
They have recently converted that building into residential use. = 他们最近将那栋建筑改建成了住宅。


过去完成时:
The land had been residential for generations before the developers bought it. = 在开发商买下这块地之前，它已经世代是住宅区了。
The area had been largely residential until the recent influx of businesses. = 直到最近涌入大量企业之前，该地区一直主要是住宅区。


将来时:
The area will become more residential in the future. = 未来该地区将变得更加住宅化。
That land will be developed for residential purposes. = 那块地将用于住宅开发。


虚拟语气:
If it were residential, we would buy it. = 如果它是住宅区，我们会买下它。
I wish this area were more residential. = 我希望这个区域更像住宅区。


被动语态:
The property is being developed for residential use. = 该房产正在开发用于住宅用途。
The building was designed to be residential. = 这栋建筑的设计目的是住宅。


疑问句:
Is this area residential? = 这个区域是住宅区吗？
Is that building residential or commercial? = 那栋建筑是住宅还是商业建筑？


否定句:
The building is not residential; it's an office. = 这栋建筑不是住宅；它是办公室。
That area is not entirely residential; there are some shops. = 那个区域并非完全是住宅区；有一些商店。
