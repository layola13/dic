discards:/dɪsˈkɑːrdz/ | vt. n. |丢弃；抛弃；废除 | To throw something away because it is useless or unwanted.  |例句：He discarded the old letter. 他扔掉了那封旧信。 | 近义词：throw away, get rid of, abandon, reject | 反义词：keep, retain, preserve | 动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He discards old newspapers every week. = 他每周都丢弃旧报纸。
She discards broken toys. = 她丢弃坏掉的玩具。


一般过去时:
They discarded the faulty equipment. = 他们丢弃了有故障的设备。
I discarded my old phone. = 我丢弃了我的旧手机。


现在完成时:
He has discarded all his old clothes. = 他已经丢弃了他所有的旧衣服。
She has discarded her old habits. = 她已经改掉了她的旧习惯。


过去完成时:
They had discarded the evidence before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们已经销毁了证据。
She had discarded her hopes of winning. = 她已经放弃了获胜的希望。


将来时:
He will discard the broken chair tomorrow. = 他明天会丢弃那把坏掉的椅子。
They will discard the outdated system next year. = 他们明年将会废除过时的系统。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would discard that old coat. = 如果我是你，我会丢掉那件旧外套。
I wish I had discarded that bad investment sooner. = 我真希望我早点放弃那笔糟糕的投资。


被动语态:
The old furniture was discarded. = 旧家具被丢弃了。
The plan was discarded because of its flaws. = 由于计划有缺陷，该计划被放弃了。


疑问句:
What did they discard? = 他们丢弃了什么？
Will they discard the proposal? = 他们会放弃这个提议吗？


否定句:
He didn't discard the letter. = 他没有丢弃那封信。
She wouldn't discard her dreams. = 她不会放弃她的梦想。
