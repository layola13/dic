mira: /ˈmɪrə/ | n. | 奇迹，奇迹般的事物 | A miracle is an event that is not explicable by natural or scientific laws and is therefore attributed to supernatural causes.  It often involves a remarkable and unexpected occurrence, often defying the odds. | 例句：The doctor called her recovery a miracle.  医生称她的康复是个奇迹。


例句：It was a miracle that no one was hurt in the accident.  事故中没有人受伤真是个奇迹。


近义词：wonder, marvel, prodigy | 反义词：disaster, catastrophe


词源:  From Latin *miraculum, from mirari "to wonder at."  The word reflects the sense of awe and astonishment associated with events considered to be divinely influenced or beyond normal explanation.
