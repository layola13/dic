pleats:/pliːts/| n.|褶,褶皱|A pleat is a double or multiple fold in a garment or other fabric, often made decorative.  It is created by folding the fabric and then pressing or stitching it in place.|例句：The skirt had several pleats at the waist. 这条裙子腰部有几道褶皱。

例句：The designer used intricate pleats to create a unique texture. 设计师用复杂的褶皱创造出独特的质感。


近义词：fold, crease, wrinkle|反义词：smooth, flat|词性:名词
