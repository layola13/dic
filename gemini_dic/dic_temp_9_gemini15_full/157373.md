pipetted:/ˈpɪpɛtɪd/ | v. | 用移液管吸取；用移液器移取 |  Past tense and past participle of "pipet" or "pipette".  It describes the action of transferring a liquid using a pipette. | Example: The scientist carefully pipetted the solution into the test tube.  科学家小心地用移液管将溶液移入试管。  |近义词：transferred, dispensed | 反义词: poured (in a less precise manner)
