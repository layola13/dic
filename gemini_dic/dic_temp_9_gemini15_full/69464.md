feinted:/ˈfeɪntɪd/| v.|假装；虚弱；昏厥|To make a sudden, deceptive movement, typically to evade an opponent or attack; to pretend; to lose consciousness briefly.|例句：The boxer feinted to the left before throwing a right hook. 拳击手向左虚晃一招，然后打出一记右勾拳。

The actress feinted a faint to gain sympathy. 女演员假装晕倒以博取同情。|近义词：pretended, simulated, swooned|反义词：honestly, truthfully, genuinely

He feints left and then punches right. = 他虚晃左拳然后打出右拳。
She often feints illness to avoid work. = 她经常假装生病来逃避工作。
