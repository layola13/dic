purline:/ˈpɜːrlaɪn/| n.|横撑木, 檩条 | A purline is a horizontal timber or steel beam used in roof construction to support the rafters or trusses.  It runs parallel to the ridge and helps distribute the weight of the roof. | Example: The purlines were spaced evenly across the roof's width.  横撑木均匀地分布在屋顶的宽度上。|近义词：rafter, beam, joist |反义词：none | 名词
