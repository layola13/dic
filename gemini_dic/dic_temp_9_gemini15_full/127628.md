needlepointing: /ˈniːdlˌpɔɪntɪŋ/ | n. | 刺绣；针绣 | The art or process of creating pictures or designs by stitching with a needle and thread onto a fabric, often using a specific pattern or design.  | She enjoys needlepointing and has created many beautiful pieces. 她喜欢刺绣，创作了很多美丽的作品。 | embroidery, crewelwork |  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with painting or other forms of art) | 名词
