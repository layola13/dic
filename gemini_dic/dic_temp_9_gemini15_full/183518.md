repunishments:/ˌriːˈpʌnɪʃmənts/ | n. | 再惩罚，重复惩罚 | Repeated punishments;  the act of punishing someone again for the same offense.  This word is not commonly used and might be considered informal or even non-standard. It is essentially a combination of "re-" (again) and "punishments." | Example:  The repunishments for minor infractions were excessive.  再犯小错的重复惩罚是过分的。 |近义词: repeated penalties, further punishments, additional punishments |反义词:rewards, forgiveness, pardon | 名词
