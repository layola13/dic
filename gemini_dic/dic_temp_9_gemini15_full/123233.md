monocropping:/ˈmɒnəʊkrɒpɪŋ/| n. |单一种植；单一作物种植|The practice of growing a single crop in a field year after year.  This can lead to soil depletion and increased vulnerability to pests and diseases.|例句：Monocropping of wheat has depleted the soil nutrients in this area.  单一种植小麦已经耗尽了该地区的土壤养分。|例句：The farmer's reliance on monocropping made his farm susceptible to disease outbreaks. 这位农民依赖单一种植，使得他的农场容易爆发疾病。|近义词：single cropping, sole cropping|反义词：crop rotation, polyculture|名词
