cavity:/ˈkævəti/| n.|空腔，洞穴，龋洞|A cavity is a hollow space within a solid object or in the body.  It can refer to a hole in a tooth (a cavity), a space within the body (such as the chest cavity or abdominal cavity), or a hollow space in a material.|例句：The dentist found a cavity in my tooth.  牙医在我的牙齿里发现了一个龋洞。

例句：The chest cavity protects vital organs. 胸腔保护着重要的器官。|近义词：hole, hollow, space, void|反义词：solid, fullness
