heartseed:/ˈhɑːrtsid/| n. |心核；心形种子|The heartseed refers to the central part of a seed, often the embryo or the nutritive tissue surrounding it. It can also refer to a small, heart-shaped seed.|例句：The heartseed of the watermelon is small and black.  西瓜的心核又小又黑。|例句：The gardener carefully planted the heartseed hoping for a bountiful harvest. 园丁小心地播种心形种子，希望能获得丰收。|近义词：kernel, core, embryo|反义词：shell, husk
