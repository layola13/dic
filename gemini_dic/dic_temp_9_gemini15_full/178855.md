red-handed: /ˌredˈhændɪd/ | adj. | 现行犯的；赃物在手的 |  Caught in the act of committing a crime; with blood or other incriminating substance on one's hands.  | 例句：The thief was caught red-handed trying to steal the jewels.  小偷试图偷珠宝时被当场抓住。

The police arrested the suspect red-handed. 警察当场逮捕了嫌疑犯。


近义词：caught in the act, in flagrante delicto | 反义词：innocent, acquitted
