disimprove:/ˌdɪsɪmˈpruːv/| vt.|使恶化；使变坏|To make something worse; to deteriorate.|The constant rain disimproved the condition of the roads.  持续的雨水使路况恶化了。|The new policy disimproved the company's morale. 新政策使公司士气低落。|improve, worsen|improve|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disimproves the situation with his actions. = 他用自己的行为使情况恶化。
The policy disimproves the quality of life for many people. = 这项政策降低了许多人的生活质量。


一般过去时:
The storm disimproved the already weak bridge. = 暴风雨使原本就脆弱的桥梁更加糟糕。
His careless mistake disimproved the project's timeline. = 他粗心的错误使项目的进度延误了。


现在完成时:
Years of neglect have disimproved the building's structure. = 多年的忽视已经使建筑物的结构恶化了。
His constant negativity has disimproved his relationships with others. = 他持续的消极态度已经破坏了他与他人的关系。


过去完成时:
The drought had disimproved the farmland before the rain came. = 在下雨之前，干旱已经使农田状况恶化了。
The lack of funding had disimproved the school's facilities. = 资金不足已经使学校的设施恶化了。


将来时:
The predicted heatwave will disimprove air quality in the city. = 预计的热浪将恶化城市空气质量。
Without maintenance, the machine will disimprove over time. = 没有维护，机器会随着时间的推移而性能下降。


虚拟语气:
If he continued to ignore the problem, it would disimprove the situation further. = 如果他继续忽视这个问题，情况会进一步恶化。
I wish he hadn't taken that action; it disimproved everything. = 我真希望他没有采取那个行动；那使一切变得更糟。


被动语态:
The situation was disimproved by his rash decision. = 他的鲁莽决定使情况恶化了。
The environment has been disimproved by pollution. = 环境已经被污染所破坏。


疑问句:
Does this new plan disimprove our chances of success? = 这个新计划会降低我们成功的几率吗？
How did his actions disimprove the company's reputation? = 他的行为是如何损害公司声誉的？


否定句:
His efforts did not disimprove the situation; they improved it. = 他的努力并没有使情况恶化；反而使情况改善了。
The new software did not disimprove the user experience; it enhanced it. = 新软件并没有降低用户体验；反而增强了它。
