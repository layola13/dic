non-collegiate:/ˌnɑːnˈkɑːliːdʒɪət/ | adj. | 非大学的；非学院的 | Not relating to or connected with a college or university.  This adjective describes something or someone that is outside the college or university system. | Example:  He pursued a non-collegiate education through independent study. 他通过自学获得了非大学教育。  The non-collegiate program offers alternative learning pathways. 非大学项目提供替代学习途径。 | 近义词: extramural, independent, non-academic (in some contexts) | 反义词: collegiate, academic
