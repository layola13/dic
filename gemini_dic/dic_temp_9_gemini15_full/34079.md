civilist:/ˈsɪvɪlɪst/| n. |平民；非战斗人员；注重礼仪的人|A civilian is a person who is not a member of the armed forces.  A civilist is someone who prioritizes civility and politeness in their interactions.|例句：The bomb injured several civilians. 这枚炸弹炸伤了几名平民。

The civilist always uses polite language, even when frustrated.  这位注重礼仪的人即使沮丧时也总是使用礼貌的语言。|近义词：non-combatant, civilian, polite person |反义词：soldier, military personnel, rude person|词性：名词
