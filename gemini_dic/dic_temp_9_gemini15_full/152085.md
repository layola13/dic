pates:/peɪts/| n.| 帕特（法式肉酱），肝酱 |  A paste of seasoned ground meat, often served as an appetizer or spread.  It can be made from various meats, such as chicken, duck, or pork liver, and is typically seasoned with herbs and spices. |  Example: The restaurant is famous for its delicious chicken pates. 这家餐馆以其美味的鸡肉酱闻名。 |近义词：spread, pâté (French)|反义词：无
