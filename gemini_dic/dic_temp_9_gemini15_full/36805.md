colonelcy:/ˈkɜːnəlsɪ/| n. |上校军衔；上校职位|The rank or position of a colonel.  It refers to the office or status held by a colonel,  not necessarily the person themselves.  |Example: He aspired to the colonelcy of the regiment. 他渴望获得该团的上校军衔。|近义词：colonel's rank, colonel's position|反义词：private's rank (a very low rank)
