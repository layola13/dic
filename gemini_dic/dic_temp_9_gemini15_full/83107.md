gustard:/ˈɡʌstɑːrd/ | n. |蛋奶冻，卡仕达酱 |Custard is a culinary preparation based on a cooked mixture of milk or cream and eggs, often flavored with sugar and vanilla.  It may be served as a dessert on its own, or as a component of other desserts, such as trifles or tarts. |Example:  The creamy custard was the perfect ending to the meal.  这道奶油蛋奶冻是这顿饭完美的结束。 The chef prepared a rich custard for the pie.  厨师为馅饼准备了丰富的蛋奶冻。|近义词：pudding, cream |反义词：N/A
