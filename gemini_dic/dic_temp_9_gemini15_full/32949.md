choppy:/ˈtʃɑːpi/|adj.|波浪起伏的；不流畅的；杂乱无章的|Characterized by short, irregular movements or bursts; jerky; uneven.  It can describe the movement of water, the style of writing or speech, or other things that lack smoothness.|例句：The sea was choppy, making the boat ride quite rough.  = 海面波浪起伏，使乘船相当颠簸。

The editor criticized the author's choppy writing style. = 编辑批评了作者不流畅的写作风格。


近义词：jerky, uneven, rough, irregular, bumpy, abrupt |反义词：smooth, flowing, even, continuous
