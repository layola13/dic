clamatorial:/ˌklæməˈtɔːriəl/| adj.|喧闹的；大声喊叫的；叫喊的| Relating to or characterized by loud cries or shouts.  It often describes the vocalizations of animals, but can also be used figuratively to describe loud and boisterous human behavior.|例句：The clamatorial calls of the monkeys echoed through the jungle.  猴子的叫声在丛林中回荡。

例句：The clamatorial protest outside the government building was deafening.  政府大楼外的抗议声震耳欲聋。


近义词：vociferous, clamorous, boisterous, vociferant

反义词：quiet, silent, hushed, subdued


词性:形容词
