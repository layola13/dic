preteach:/priːˈtʃiːtʃ/| vt.|预先教授，提前讲解|To teach something beforehand; to give preliminary instruction in.|例句：The teacher pretaught the vocabulary before reading the story.老师在读故事之前预先教授了词汇。|例句：The instructor pretaught the safety procedures before the students began the experiment.  指导老师在学生开始实验之前预先讲解了安全规程。|近义词：instruct beforehand, introduce beforehand,  prepare|反义词：post-teach
