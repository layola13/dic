legislatress:/ˈlɛdʒɪsleɪtrəs/| n.|女立法者，女议员|A woman who is a member of a legislature.  It refers to a female legislator, a woman who makes or enacts laws. |例句：The legislatress championed the bill for equal pay. 这名女议员大力支持同工同酬的法案。|例句：Several legislatresses voted against the proposed amendment.好几位女议员投票反对拟议的修正案。|近义词：lawmaker, legislator, representative, assemblywoman|反义词：constituent (in the sense of being represented, not the opposite of a legislator)|词性:名词
