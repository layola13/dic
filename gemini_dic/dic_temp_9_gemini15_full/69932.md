fervour:/ˈfɜːrvər/|/ˈfɝːvɚ/| n. |热情；热忱；激情 |Great energy or enthusiasm in pursuit of a cause or objective.  It implies a strong feeling often bordering on passion. |例句：He spoke with great fervour about his beliefs. 他满怀热情地谈论他的信仰。

例句：The campaign was conducted with considerable fervour. 这场运动开展得非常热烈。


近义词：passion, zeal, enthusiasm, ardour, intensity |反义词：apathy, indifference, coldness, lack of enthusiasm


词性:名词
