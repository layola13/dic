outacted:/ˌaʊtˈæktɪd/| v.|  超过…的演技；胜过…的表演 |To surpass in acting skill or performance.  |例句：The young actress outacted the veteran in the final scene.  年轻女演员在最后一幕中胜过老戏骨的表演。
例句：He completely outacted his co-stars, making them seem amateurish by comparison. 他完全胜过他的搭档，使他们相比之下显得业余。|近义词：outperform, eclipse, surpass |反义词：underact, be outdone by


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outacts everyone in the play. = 他在剧中演技胜过所有人。
She outacts her rivals consistently. = 她总是演技胜过她的对手。


一般过去时:
He outacted his co-star in the last scene. = 他在最后一幕的演技胜过他的搭档。
The child outacted the adults in the production. = 在这场演出中，孩子的演技胜过成年人。


现在完成时:
She has consistently outacted her peers throughout her career. = 在她整个职业生涯中，她一直演技胜过她的同龄人。
He has outacted many established actors.  = 他已经胜过许多知名演员的演技。


过去完成时:
By the end of the play, he had outacted everyone on stage. = 到剧终时，他的演技已经胜过舞台上的所有人。
She had outacted her nervousness by the time the curtain rose. = 当幕布升起时，她已经克服了紧张的情绪，并展现出精湛的演技。


将来时:
She will outact all her competitors in the upcoming competition. = 在即将到来的比赛中，她将胜过所有竞争对手的演技。
He believes he will outact the leading man in the new film. = 他相信在新的电影中，他的演技将胜过男主角。


虚拟语气:
