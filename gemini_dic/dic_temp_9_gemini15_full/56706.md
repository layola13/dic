doomsayer:/ˈduːmseɪər/| n. |预言灾祸者，悲观主义者|A person who predicts disaster or misfortune.  Someone who habitually predicts doom or disaster.|例句：The doomsayer predicted a global economic collapse. 预言灾祸者预言全球经济崩溃。

例句：Some doomsayers warned of the impending environmental crisis. 一些预言灾祸者警告即将到来的环境危机。


近义词：prophet of doom, Cassandra, pessimist, alarmist

反义词：optimist, visionary


词性:名词
