sanitizes:/ˈsænɪtaɪzɪz/| v.|消毒；净化；使卫生|To make something clean and free from germs.  |例句：The nurse sanitizes the instruments before surgery. 护士在手术前对器械进行消毒。|例句：The company sanitizes its production facilities regularly to maintain hygiene standards. 公司定期对生产设施进行消毒以保持卫生标准。|近义词：sterilize, disinfect, cleanse |反义词：contaminate, pollute


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She sanitizes her hands before eating. = 她吃饭前会洗手消毒。
The restaurant sanitizes its surfaces daily. = 这家餐馆每天都会消毒其表面。


一般过去时:
