doublers:/ˈdaʊblərz/| n.|加倍者；双倍数；复制品|A doubler is a person or thing that doubles something, or makes it twice as much.  It can also refer to a duplicate or copy.|例句：The company hired doublers to increase production.  这家公司雇佣了加倍人员来提高产量。|例句：The baker used a doubler to create two identical cakes. 面包师用了一个复制器制作了两个相同的蛋糕。|近义词：multiplier, duplicate, copy|反义词：halver, single, original
