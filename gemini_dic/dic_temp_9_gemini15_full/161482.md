potboilers:/ˈpɒtˌbɔɪlər/| n. |粗制滥造的通俗小说，为钱而写的作品|A potboiler is a work of art, especially a novel or play, that is produced quickly and without much effort, primarily for financial gain rather than artistic merit.  It is often considered to be of low quality and lacking in originality. |Example: He churned out potboilers to pay his bills. 他写些粗制滥造的小说来付账单。|近义词：hackwork, trash, pulp fiction|反义词：masterpiece, classic, work of art|名词
