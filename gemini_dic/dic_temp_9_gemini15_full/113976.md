magic:/ˈmædʒɪk/ | n. adj. | 魔法，魔术；不可思议的，神奇的 | The ability to influence events by supernatural means, or by seemingly supernatural means.  It can also describe something extraordinary or seemingly impossible. | 例句：The magician performed a breathtaking magic trick. 魔术师表演了一个令人叹为观止的魔术。

The beauty of the sunset was pure magic. 日落的美景简直是神奇的。|近义词：wizardry, sorcery, enchantment, wonder |反义词：reality, ordinariness, mundane
