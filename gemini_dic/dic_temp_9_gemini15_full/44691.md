/krʌdɪŋ/| n. |粗制滥造；劣质产品堆积；混乱不堪|The term "crudding" refers to the act of creating something quickly and carelessly, often resulting in a low-quality or messy outcome.  It can also refer to the accumulation of unwanted or useless things, creating a cluttered and disorganized environment. |例句：The programmer's crudding approach to the project resulted in a buggy and inefficient application. 程序员粗制滥造的项目方法导致了一个满是bug且效率低下的应用程序。


例句：The garage was a scene of utter crudding, filled with broken tools and discarded parts. 车库一片混乱不堪，到处都是坏掉的工具和废弃的零件。|近义词：sloppiness, shoddiness, messiness, clutter|反义词：meticulousness, precision, orderliness|名词
