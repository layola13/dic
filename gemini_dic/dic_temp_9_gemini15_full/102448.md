jackfruits:/ˈdʒækfruːts/| n.| 菠萝蜜 | A large tropical fruit with a spiky rind and sweet, yellow flesh. It is known for its size and distinctive aroma.  | Example: The jackfruit was so large it needed two people to carry it. 这颗菠萝蜜很大，需要两个人才能搬动。  |近义词：breadfruit (面包果，外观相似但味道不同)|反义词：无 |名词
