newsgroup:/ˈnuːzɡruːp/| n. |新闻组|An online discussion forum where users can post messages and engage in discussions on a specific topic.  Newsgroup are typically organized hierarchically by topic.|Example:  I found a lot of useful information on the alt.comp.programming newsgroup. 我在alt.comp.programming新闻组上找到了很多有用的信息。|近义词：online forum, discussion board, message board|反义词：None
