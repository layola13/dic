/kwɜːrkər/ | n. |古怪的人，怪癖的人 |A quirky person is someone who is slightly strange or eccentric in a way that is often appealing.  They are unconventional and have peculiar habits or interests.  |He's a quirky character, always saying unexpected things. 他是个古怪的人，总是说一些意想不到的话。  She has a quirky sense of humor. 她有古怪的幽默感。|eccentric, oddball, odd, peculiar |normal, conventional, typical |名词
