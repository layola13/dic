slayed:/sleɪd/| v.| 杀死；彻底击败；惊艳；大获成功 | To kill (someone).  To defeat someone or something decisively. To impress greatly; to stun.  To be extremely successful or popular. |例句：The knight slayed the dragon. = 那位骑士杀死了那条龙。
The team slayed their opponents. = 这支队伍彻底击败了他们的对手。
Her performance slayed the audience. = 她的表演惊艳了观众。
Her new album totally slayed. = 她的新专辑大获成功。|近义词：killed, defeated, impressed, wowed, succeeded |反义词：failed, lost, disappointed
