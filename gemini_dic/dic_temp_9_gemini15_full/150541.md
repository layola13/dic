panzers:/ˈpɑːntsərz/|/ˈpæn.zɚz/| n. |装甲车；坦克兵|German plural of Panzer, meaning "armored vehicle" or "tank".  The word is often used to refer to German tanks, especially those of World War II.|例句：The German panzers advanced rapidly across the plains. 德国装甲部队迅速横越平原。 The panzer division suffered heavy losses. 装甲师遭受了重大损失。|近义词：tanks, armored vehicles, armored cars|反义词：infantry (步兵)
