ethological:/ˌiːθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/| adj. |行为学的；动物行为学的| Relating to the scientific and objective study of animal behavior.  Ethology focuses on observable behaviors in natural settings and emphasizes the evolutionary and ecological contexts of behavior.  | Example:  Ethological studies have revealed fascinating insights into the social structures of chimpanzees. 行为学研究揭示了关于黑猩猩社会结构的迷人见解。 |近义词：behavioral, zoological (in some contexts)|反义词：none specifically, but antonyms might depend on the specific behavioral aspect being discussed (e.g., if discussing aggressive behavior, the opposite might be docile behavior).|
