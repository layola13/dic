faustian:/ˈfɔːstɪən/| adj.|浮士德式的；与浮士德有关的；不惜一切代价追求知识或权力|Relating to or characteristic of Faust, the legendary figure who sold his soul to the devil in exchange for knowledge and power; characterized by a willingness to sacrifice everything for knowledge or power.  It implies a reckless pursuit of ambition, often with disastrous consequences.|例句：His faustian ambition led to his downfall. 他的浮士德式的野心导致了他的垮台。

例句：The novel explores the faustian bargain between the scientist and the corporation. 这部小说探讨了科学家与公司之间达成的浮士德式的交易。


近义词：reckless, ambitious, devilish,  unscrupulous

反义词：virtuous, moral, ethical, scrupulous

词性：形容词
