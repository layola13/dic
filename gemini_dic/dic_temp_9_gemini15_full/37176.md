come-your-way:/kʌm jɔːr weɪ/| phrasal verb|将会到来；即将发生|This phrase implies that something good, often unexpected, is about to happen to someone.  It suggests a fortunate event or opportunity is approaching.  |例句：Good things will come your way if you keep working hard. 如果你继续努力工作，好事就会降临在你身上。
  
例句：A great opportunity will come your way soon.  很快就会有一个绝佳的机会摆在你面前。


近义词：arrive, happen, occur, befall (in a positive sense)


反义词：misfortune, adversity, setback


词性: phrasal verb
