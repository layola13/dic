compose:/kəmˈpoʊz/| /kəmˈpəʊz/ | vt.  |创作；作曲；构成；使平静 |to create or produce (something, typically a work of art, music, or literature); to arrange or form (something) from separate parts; to settle or calm (someone)|例句：She composed a beautiful poem. 她创作了一首美丽的诗歌。|例句：The team is composed of experts from various fields. 这个团队由来自各个领域的专家组成。|近义词：write, create, form, make, settle, calm |反义词：destroy, disrupt, agitate
