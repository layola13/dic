globulin:/ˈɡlɒbjʊlɪn/ | n. | 球蛋白 | A simple protein that is soluble in dilute salt solutions but insoluble in water. Globulins are found in blood plasma and other body fluids and play an important role in various biological processes, including immune response and transport of substances.  | Example:  Immunoglobulins are a type of globulin that play a crucial role in the immune system. 免疫球蛋白是一种在免疫系统中起关键作用的球蛋白。


The serum contains a variety of globulins. 血清含有各种球蛋白。
