sanitarian:/ˌsænɪˈteəriən/| n. |公共卫生人员，卫生管理员|A sanitarian is a person who is trained and employed to protect public health, typically by inspecting and regulating places that handle food or waste.|The sanitarian inspected the restaurant for cleanliness and safety violations.|近义词：health inspector, public health officer|反义词：polluter, unsanitary person
