parhelium:/pɑːˈhiːliəm/| n. |幻日|A parhelion is a bright spot of light that sometimes appears on either side of the sun, typically 22 degrees from the sun. It is an atmospheric optical phenomenon caused by the refraction of sunlight by ice crystals in the atmosphere.  Parhelia are often seen in association with halos. |Example:  The stunning sunrise was even more spectacular with the parhelia flanking the sun.  壮丽的日出因为两侧的幻日而更加壮观。|近义词：sun dog (less formal)|反义词：无|名词
