quasi-offensively: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ ɒˈfɛnsɪvli/ | adj. | 近乎冒犯地，略带冒犯地 |  Describes an action or statement that is almost offensive, but not quite. It suggests a subtle or unintentional slight rather than a deliberate insult.  | Example: He made a quasi-offensively casual remark about her weight. 他对她的体重做了一个近乎冒犯的随意评论。


近义词: mildly offensive, slightly insulting, subtly offensive, inconsiderate


反义词: complimentary, flattering, respectful, polite


词性: adjective (形容词)
