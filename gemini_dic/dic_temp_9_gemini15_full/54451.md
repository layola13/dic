disinherited:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhɛrɪtɪd/| adj.|被剥夺继承权的；被逐出继承权的| deprived of an inheritance or the right of inheritance.  The disinherited son felt bitter and resentful. 被剥夺继承权的儿子感到痛苦和怨恨。|近义词：dispossessed, deprived|反义词：inherited

He is disinherited by his father. = 他被父亲剥夺了继承权。
The child is disinherited because of his bad behavior. = 因为行为不端，孩子被剥夺了继承权。
