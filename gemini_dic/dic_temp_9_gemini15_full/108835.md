leaviness:/ˈliːvɪnəs/ | n. |沉重感，压抑感；繁重；沉闷 |A feeling of heaviness; oppression;  weightiness; dullness.  It can refer to a physical sensation or a metaphorical feeling of sadness or depression. |例句：The leaviness in the air before the storm was palpable.  暴风雨前的空气中弥漫着一种明显的沉重感。

The leaviness of her grief was almost unbearable.  她悲伤的沉重感几乎难以忍受。


近义词：heaviness, oppressiveness, weight, depression, gloom |反义词：lightness, buoyancy, cheerfulness, gaiety


词源：源自leave (离开)的形容词形式leavy (多叶的，茂盛的) ,  加上后缀-ness形成名词，引申出沉重感。  The original sense of "leavy" implied a sense of weight or density, hence the shift in meaning to a feeling of heaviness.
