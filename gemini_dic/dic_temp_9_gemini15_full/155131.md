petticoats:/ˈpetɪˌkoʊts/| n.|衬裙，短衬裙|A petticoat is an underskirt, traditionally worn under a dress or skirt to add fullness or warmth.  It can be made from various materials and styles.|例句：She wore a fluffy petticoat under her wedding dress. 她婚纱下穿着一件蓬松的衬裙。|例句：In the Victorian era, petticoats were an essential part of women's clothing. 维多利亚时代，衬裙是女性服装的重要组成部分。|近义词：underskirt, underslip|反义词：N/A
