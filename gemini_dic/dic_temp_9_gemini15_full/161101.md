postdrug:/ˈpoʊstdrʌɡ/| adj.| 药物后的；药物使用后的 | Relating to the period after the administration of a drug;  the effects or consequences following drug use or treatment. |例句：Postdrug effects can include drowsiness and nausea. 药物后的作用可能包括嗜睡和恶心。 |例句：The study investigated postdrug cognitive function in patients.  该研究调查了患者药物使用后的认知功能。|近义词：post-treatment, post-medication |反义词：predrug
