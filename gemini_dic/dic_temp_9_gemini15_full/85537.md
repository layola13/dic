hatchel: /ˈhætʃəl/ | n. | 梳麻机；粗梳机 | A hatchel is a tool used in the processing of flax or hemp fibers to separate and straighten the fibers.  It typically consists of a frame with many sharp pins or teeth. |  The flax was carefully hatcheled to remove impurities and align the fibers.  = 亚麻经过仔细的梳理以去除杂质并使纤维排列整齐。 | comb, heckle |  (无明显反义词) | n.
