misfed:/ˈmɪsˌfed/| adj.|喂养不好的；营养不良的|Not properly or sufficiently fed; suffering from malnutrition.  The word implies a lack of proper nourishment, often due to neglect or insufficient food.|例句：The misfed puppy was weak and lethargic.  这只喂养不好的小狗又虚弱又懒散。|例句：The children, misfed and neglected, were taken into care. 这些营养不良且被忽视的孩子被送进了收容所。|近义词：underfed, malnourished, starved|反义词：well-fed, nourished
