conchie:/ˈkɒntʃi/ | n. | 逃兵；拒服兵役者 | A person who illegally avoids military service, especially during wartime.  Often refers to someone who deserts their post or avoids conscription altogether. | Example:  He was branded a conchie after fleeing to Canada to avoid the draft. 他逃往加拿大躲避征兵，被贴上了逃兵的标签。  | 近义词：deserter, draft dodger, objector | 反义词：conscientious objector (though this term has a different connotation)
