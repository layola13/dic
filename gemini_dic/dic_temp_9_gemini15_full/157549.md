pita-breads: /ˈpiːtə bredz/ | n. | 皮塔饼（复数） | Flat, unleavened bread, typically made from wheat flour, that is baked in a hot oven until puffy.  It is often split open and filled with various ingredients. | Example:  We had pita breads with hummus and falafel for lunch.  我们午餐吃了涂了鹰嘴豆泥和鹰嘴豆饼的皮塔饼。 |近义词：pocket bread, flatbread | 反义词：None specifically.
