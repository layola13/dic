clobberer:/ˈklɒbərər/ | n. |痛打者；猛击者；破坏者 |A person who clobbers; someone who inflicts heavy blows.  The term often implies a forceful and possibly brutal attack.  |例句：The boxer was a fearsome clobberer, known for his devastating punches.  拳击手是一个可怕的痛打者，以其毁灭性的拳击而闻名。
The angry mob was a clobberer of property, smashing windows and overturning cars.  愤怒的暴徒是破坏财产的暴徒，砸碎窗户，推翻汽车。|近义词：attacker, assailant, hitter, bruiser, bully |反义词：protector, defender, helper
