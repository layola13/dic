reawakens:/ˌriːəˈweɪkənz/| v.|重新唤醒；重新激起；使复苏|To awaken again; to rouse from inactivity or dormancy; to revive or rekindle (a feeling, interest, etc.).|The sun reawakens the sleeping flowers.  太阳重新唤醒了沉睡的花朵。

The old story reawakened her painful memories.  这个旧故事重新唤起了她痛苦的回忆。


近义词：revive, reanimate, resuscitate, renew

反义词：extinguish, suppress, quell
