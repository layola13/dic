sickbed:/ˈsɪkbɛd/| n. |病床；卧床 |A sickbed is a bed occupied by someone who is ill.  The term often evokes a sense of illness, suffering, and vulnerability. |Example: He lay in his sickbed, weak and feverish. 他躺在病床上，虚弱而发烧。  She spent weeks in her sickbed recovering from the surgery. 她卧床几周以从手术中恢复过来。|近义词：bed of sickness, sickroom |反义词：无
