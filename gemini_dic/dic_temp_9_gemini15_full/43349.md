crams:/kræmz/| vt. n.|塞满；挤满；死记硬背；|to force a large amount of something into a small space; to study intensively for a short time before an exam |例句：He crammed all his clothes into the suitcase. 他把所有的衣服塞进手提箱里。
The students crammed for the exam. 学生们为考试突击学习。|近义词：stuff, pack,  cram for |反义词:empty, unload

USAGE->

He crams his belongings into the small backpack. 他把他的东西塞进小背包里。
She crams for her history exam every night. 她每天晚上都为历史考试突击学习。
