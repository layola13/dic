cruels:/ˈkruːəlz/ | adj. | 残酷的，无情的 | Cruel refers to behavior that is deliberately causing pain or suffering to others, showing a lack of compassion or pity. It emphasizes the intentional infliction of harm. | 例句：The cruel dictator oppressed his people.  残酷的独裁者压迫他的子民。


The cruel winter killed many of the crops. 残酷的冬天毁了很多庄稼。


近义词：brutal, heartless, ruthless, savage, merciless | 反义词：kind, compassionate, merciful, gentle, humane | 词性:形容词
