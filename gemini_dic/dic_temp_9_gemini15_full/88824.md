hogger:/ˈhɒɡər/ | n. v. | 贪婪的人；霸占者；占用过多的人；大量占用（某物）的人 |  A person who takes or uses more than their fair share of something; someone who hogs something.  |例句：He's a real hogger when it comes to the food.  他吃东西的时候很贪婪。  He was a hogger of the spotlight. 他很爱出风头。|近义词：greedy person, grabber, monopolist, monopolizing|反义词：altruist, giver, sharer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hogs the blanket every night. = 他每晚都霸占着毯子。
She hogs the conversation. = 她总是霸占着谈话。

一般过去时:
He hogged all the food at the party. = 他在派对上把所有的食物都吃光了。
They hogged the best seats in the theatre. = 他们霸占了剧院里最好的座位。

现在完成时:
He has hogged the computer all afternoon. = 他整个下午都在霸占着电脑。
She has hogged the limelight for years. = 她多年来一直占据着人们的视线。

过去完成时:
He had hogged the microphone before anyone else could speak. = 在其他人能说话之前，他已经霸占了麦克风。
They had hogged all the resources before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，他们已经霸占了所有资源。

将来时:
He will hog the remote control all evening. = 他整个晚上都会霸占着遥控器。
They will hog the road during the rush hour. = 他们在高峰期会占用道路。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't hogged all the cookies, there would have been enough for everyone. = 如果他没有霸占所有的饼干，每个人都会有足够的饼干。
I wish he wouldn't hog the conversation all the time. = 我希望他不要总是霸占着谈话。

被动语态:
The resources were hogged by the larger companies. = 资源被大公司垄断了。
The best seats were hogged by those who arrived early. = 最好的座位被先到的人占了。

疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't hog the spotlight; he shared it with others. = 他没有抢风头；他和别人一起分享了。
They didn't hog the resources; they used them fairly. = 他们没有霸占资源；他们公平地使用了它们。
