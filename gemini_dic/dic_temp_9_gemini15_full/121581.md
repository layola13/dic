miso:/ˈmiːsoʊ/| n.|味噌|A fermented soybean paste used as a condiment in Japanese cuisine. It is made from soybeans, rice or barley, salt, and a type of mold called koji.  The paste is typically salty and umami-rich, with varying degrees of sweetness and color depending on the ingredients and fermentation process.|例句：I love adding miso paste to my soup for a savory flavor. 我喜欢在汤里加味噌来增加鲜味。

例句：This restaurant is famous for its miso-glazed black cod. 这家餐馆以它用味噌烧制的黑鳕鱼而闻名。


近义词：fermented paste, soybean paste|反义词：None.
