nonpragmatical: /ˌnɒnˌpræɡˈmætɪkəl/ | adj. |非语用学的；非务实的；不切实际的 | Not concerned with the practical aspects of language use; focusing solely on the structure and meaning of language independent of context.  |例句：His analysis of the text was entirely nonpragmatical, ignoring the speaker's intentions and the social context.  他的文本分析完全是非语用学的，忽略了说话者的意图和社会背景。


例句：The purely nonpragmatical approach to linguistics often overlooks the nuances of communication. 纯粹的非语用学语言学方法往往忽略了交流的细微之处。


近义词：theoretical, abstract, unpractical, idealistic


反义词：pragmatic, practical, realistic, applied


词性：形容词
