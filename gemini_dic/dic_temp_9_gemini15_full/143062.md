outbound:/ˈaʊtˌbaʊnd/| adj.|外向的；出境的；(航班等)离境的|Going or directed outwards or away from a centre or place.  It often refers to journeys, flights, or shipments leaving a particular location.|例句：The outbound flight was delayed due to bad weather. 出境航班因恶劣天气延误了。

例句：This company's outbound marketing strategy is very effective. 这家公司的外向营销策略非常有效。


近义词：outgoing, outward, departing, outgoing


反义词：inbound, incoming, returning


词性：形容词
