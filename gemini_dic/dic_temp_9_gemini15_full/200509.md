slanted:/ˈslæntɪd/ | adj. |倾斜的；有偏见的；歪斜的 |Tilted or sloping;  having a bias or prejudice.  |例句：The slanted roof leaked during the storm.  暴风雨期间倾斜的屋顶漏水了。 The newspaper article presented a slanted view of the events. 报纸文章对事件的描述有失偏颇。|近义词：oblique, inclined, biased, prejudiced |反义词：vertical, upright, unbiased, impartial


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The building is slanted. = 这座建筑物是倾斜的。
The picture is slanted to the left. = 图片向左倾斜。

一般过去时:
The tree slanted after the windstorm. = 风暴过后，树木倾斜了。
He slanted his cap to one side. = 他把帽子斜戴在一侧。

现在完成时:
The tower has slanted noticeably over the years. = 多年来，塔楼明显倾斜了。
She has slanted her reporting to favor her friend. = 她歪曲了报道以偏袒她的朋友。

过去完成时:
The road had slanted sharply before the accident. = 事故发生前，道路已经急剧倾斜了。
He had slanted the information to mislead the public.  他歪曲了信息以误导公众。


将来时:
The pole will slant further in the strong wind. = 在强风中，杆子会进一步倾斜。
They will slant their campaign towards younger voters. = 他们将把竞选活动倾斜于年轻选民。

虚拟语气:
If the tower slanted any further, it would collapse. = 如果塔再倾斜一点，它就会倒塌。
I wish the media hadn't slanted the story so much. = 我希望媒体没有如此歪曲这个故事。

被动语态:
The photograph was slanted to create a dramatic effect. = 照片被倾斜以营造戏剧性的效果。
The news was slanted to favor the government. = 新闻被歪曲以偏袒政府。

疑问句:
Is the fence slanted? = 栅栏倾斜了吗？
Is the reporting slanted against the opposition? = 报道是否对反对派有偏见？

否定句:
The lines on the paper are not slanted. = 纸上的线条没有倾斜。
The judge's decision was not slanted in favor of either side. = 法官的决定没有偏袒任何一方。


PHRASE->
slant across = 横跨

slant over = 倾斜
slant down = 向下倾斜
