duarchy:/ˈduːɑːrki/| n. |二元统治；双重统治 |A system of government in which two people or groups share power.  It often implies a balance of power, but can also suggest conflict or instability. |Example: The company operated under a duarchy, with the CEO and the chairman sharing equal authority.  这家公司在首席执行官和董事长共享平等权力的情况下实行二元统治。|近义词：diarchy, binate rule |反义词：monarchy, autocracy
