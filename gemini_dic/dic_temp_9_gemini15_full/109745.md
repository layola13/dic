leverwood:/ˈliːvərˌwʊd/| n. |杠杆木，撬棍木|A type of wood, often very hard and strong, traditionally used for making levers and other tools requiring strength and durability.  It may refer to specific species or be a general term.  The exact species varies regionally.|Example:  The carpenter chose a piece of leverwood for the handle of his chisel.  = 木匠选择了一块杠杆木来做他的凿子的柄。|近义词：hardwood, strong wood|反义词：softwood
