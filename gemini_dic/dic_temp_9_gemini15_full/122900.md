molosses:/məˈlæsɪz/ | n. |糖蜜，糖浆 | Molasses is a thick, dark brown syrup produced during the refining of sugarcane or sugar beets. It's a byproduct of the sugar-making process and is often used in baking and cooking. |Example:  The baker used molasses to add a rich flavor to the gingerbread.  烘焙师用糖蜜为姜饼增添浓郁的香味。|近义词：treacle, syrup |反义词：None specifically, but terms suggesting refined sugar might be considered opposite.
