overassumed:/ˌoʊvərəˈsuːmd/| v.|想当然；过分假设；预先假设|To assume something to an excessive degree; to make an assumption that is unwarranted or incorrect.  It implies a degree of presumption or unjustified confidence.|例句：He overassumed the client's needs, leading to a failed project. 他想当然地认为客户的需求，导致项目失败。

例句：She overassumed her colleague's agreement, causing a major misunderstanding. 她想当然地认为同事同意，导致了很大的误解。


近义词：presume, suppose, conjecture, take for granted


反义词：verify, confirm, doubt, question


词性:动词
