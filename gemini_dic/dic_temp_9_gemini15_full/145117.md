overarching:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːrtʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 首要的；包罗万象的；支配一切的 |  Describes something that is very important and influences many other things.  It suggests a comprehensive or dominant nature, encompassing a wide range of elements or aspects. |


例句：
The overarching goal of the project is to improve public health.  = 这个项目的首要目标是改善公共卫生。

The overarching theme of the conference was climate change. =  会议的主题是气候变化。


近义词：principal, paramount, dominant, comprehensive, encompassing

反义词：minor, insignificant, subordinate, trivial


词性：形容词
