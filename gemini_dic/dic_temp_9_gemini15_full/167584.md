prework:/ˈpriːwɜːrk/ | n. |前期工作，预备工作 | Preparatory work done before a main task or event.  It often involves planning, preparation, and other activities necessary for the smooth execution of the main task. | Example:  The prework for the conference included sending out invitations and arranging the venue.  前期会议的准备工作包括发出邀请函和安排场地。|近义词：preparation, groundwork, groundwork, preliminary work |反义词：afterwork, follow-up
