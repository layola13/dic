disintegrating: /dɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 瓦解；崩解；崩溃 |  The process of separating into constituent parts or losing cohesion.  This word describes something falling apart, breaking down, or decaying. It often implies a gradual process. | 例句：The empire was disintegrating from within. = 这个帝国正在从内部瓦解。

The old building is disintegrating rapidly. = 这栋旧建筑正在迅速崩解。


近义词：decaying, crumbling, decomposing, collapsing, breaking down | 反义词：integrating, consolidating, uniting, forming
