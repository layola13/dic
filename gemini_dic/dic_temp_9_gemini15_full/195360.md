semimanneristic: /ˌsɛmiˌmænəˈrɪstɪk/ | adj. | 半矫饰主义的，部分矫饰的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a style of art or literature that exhibits some, but not all, of the characteristics of Mannerism.  It suggests a partial or incomplete adoption of Mannerist principles, often blending them with other stylistic elements. | The painting shows a semimanneristic style, incorporating elements of both Mannerism and naturalism.  这幅画展现了一种半矫饰主义的风格，融合了矫饰主义和自然主义的元素。 | 近义词：partially Mannerist,  somewhat Mannerist | 反义词：purely naturalistic, completely unmannered
