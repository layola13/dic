protogalactic:/ˌproʊtoʊɡəˈlæktɪk/ | adj. | 原星系的；星系形成前的 |  Relating to or characteristic of the early stages of galaxy formation, before a galaxy has fully formed.  Protogalactic clouds are thought to be the precursors to galaxies. | Example:  Astronomers are studying protogalactic clouds to understand the processes involved in galaxy formation. 天文学家正在研究原星系云团，以了解星系形成过程中涉及的进程。 | 近义词：pre-galactic, early galactic | 反义词：fully formed galactic
