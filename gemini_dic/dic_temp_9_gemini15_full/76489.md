galahs:/ɡəˈlɑːz/| n. |澳洲鹦鹉|A galah is a medium-sized, predominantly grey and pink parrot native to Australia.  They are known for their distinctive pink cheeks and crest. They are common throughout much of Australia, inhabiting various habitats including woodlands, grasslands, and agricultural areas. Galahs are gregarious birds, often seen in large flocks.
