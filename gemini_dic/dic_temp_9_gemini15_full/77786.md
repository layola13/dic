generalissimo: /ˌdʒɛnəˈrælɪsɪmoʊ/ | n. | 最高统帅，大元帅 | The supreme commander of an armed force, especially in a one-man dictatorship.  A generalissimo holds ultimate authority over all military and often civilian affairs. | Example: Francisco Franco was the generalissimo of Spain during the Spanish Civil War.  弗朗西斯科·佛朗哥在西班牙内战期间是西班牙的最高统帅。 |近义词：supreme commander, commander-in-chief | 反义词：private, subordinate | 词性：名词
