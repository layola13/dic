rejoicer:/rɪˈdʒɔɪsər/| n. |庆祝者；欣喜者|A person who rejoices; one who feels or expresses great joy.  |例句：The news of her engagement made her a true rejoicer.  她的订婚消息让她成为一个真正庆祝的人。|近义词：celebrant, reveler, enthusiast |反义词：mourner, complainer |名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The child rejoices at the sight of his new toy. = 孩子看到他的新玩具很高兴。
She rejoices in her success. = 她为自己的成功而高兴。


一般过去时:
He rejoiced at the good news. = 他听到好消息很高兴。
They rejoiced when their team won. = 他们的球队获胜时，他们欣喜若狂。


现在完成时:
We have rejoiced over this victory for days. = 我们为这场胜利庆祝了好几天。
She has rejoiced at every milestone she has achieved. = 她为她取得的每一个里程碑而欢欣鼓舞。


过去完成时:
They had rejoiced at the birth of their child before the unexpected tragedy struck. = 在意外悲剧发生之前，他们为孩子的出生而欢欣鼓舞。
He had rejoiced in his freedom before realizing the heavy responsibilities. = 他在意识到沉重的责任之前，曾为自己的自由而欢欣鼓舞。


将来时:
We will rejoice when the project is finally completed. = 项目最终完成后，我们将欢欣鼓舞。
They will rejoice at the prospect of a long vacation. = 他们将为漫长的假期前景而欢欣鼓舞。


虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would rejoice greatly. = 如果我中了彩票，我会非常高兴。
I wish I could rejoice with them, but I am too busy. = 我希望我能和他们一起庆祝，但我太忙了。


被动语态:
(The word "rejoice" is rarely used in the passive voice.  A more natural phrasing would be needed.)  很少用rejoice的被动语态。


疑问句:
Does she rejoice at the news? = 她对这个消息感到高兴吗？
Will they rejoice at the outcome? = 他们会为结果而高兴吗？


否定句:
He did not rejoice at the outcome of the election. = 他对选举的结果并不高兴。
She doesn't rejoice in other people's misfortune. = 她不会为别人的不幸而高兴。


PHRASE->
rejoice in: 为...而高兴
rejoice over: 为...而庆祝
rejoice with: 与...一起庆祝
rejoice at: 因...而高兴
a cause for rejoicing:  值得庆祝的原因
