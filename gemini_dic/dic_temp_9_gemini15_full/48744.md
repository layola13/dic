USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scientist defines the terms carefully. = 科学家仔细地定义了这些术语。
This law defines the rights of citizens. = 这部法律规定了公民的权利。


一般过去时:
The teacher defined the key concept for the students. = 老师为学生定义了关键概念。
He defined his goals clearly before starting the project. = 他在开始项目之前清楚地定义了他的目标。


现在完成时:
She has defined her career path successfully. = 她已经成功地规划了自己的职业道路。
The team has defined the project scope and timeline. = 团队已经确定了项目的范围和时间表。


过去完成时:
They had defined the parameters before the experiment began. = 他们在实验开始之前就定义了参数。
The committee had defined its objectives by the end of the meeting. = 委员会在会议结束时已经确定了其目标。


将来时:
We will define the next steps in our meeting tomorrow. = 我们将在明天的会议上确定接下来的步骤。
The company will define its new strategy next quarter. = 公司将在下个季度确定其新的战略。


虚拟语气:
If we defined our goals more clearly, we would be more successful. = 如果我们更清晰地定义我们的目标，我们会更成功。
I wish the instructions defined the process more precisely. = 我希望说明能更精确地定义这个过程。


被动语态:
The term "sustainability" is defined differently across various fields. = “可持续性”一词在各个领域中的定义有所不同。
The rules of the game were clearly defined before the match. = 比赛规则在比赛开始前已明确规定。


疑问句:
How do you define success? = 你如何定义成功？
Does the contract define the responsibilities of each party? = 合同是否定义了各方的责任？


否定句:
The law doesn't clearly define the boundaries of free speech. = 这部法律没有明确界定言论自由的界限。
He failed to define his position on the issue. = 他未能就这个问题表明立场。
