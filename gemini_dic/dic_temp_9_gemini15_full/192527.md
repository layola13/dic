scrutinizes:/ˈskruːtənaɪz/| vt.|仔细检查；细察；审查|To examine or inspect closely and thoroughly. |例句：The detective scrutinized the evidence carefully.  侦探仔细检查了证据。
例句：She scrutinized the contract before signing it.  她在签合同之前仔细审查了合同。|近义词：examine, inspect, investigate, analyze|反义词：overlook, ignore, neglect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scrutinizes every detail of the report. 他仔细检查报告的每一个细节。
She scrutinizes her work before submitting it. 她在提交作业之前仔细检查自己的工作。


一般过去时:
The judge scrutinized the witness's testimony.  法官仔细审查了证人的证词。
They scrutinized the map for clues. 他们仔细检查地图寻找线索。


现在完成时:
The police have scrutinized all the evidence. 警方已经仔细检查了所有证据。
She has scrutinized the data for months. 她已经审查了几个月的数据。


过去完成时:
The committee had scrutinized the proposal before voting.  委员会在投票前已经仔细审查了提案。
He had scrutinized the document several times. 他已经仔细检查过几次文件了。


将来时:
The auditors will scrutinize the company's accounts.  审计员将仔细审查公司的账目。
She will scrutinize the manuscript before publication.  她将在出版前仔细审查手稿。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would scrutinize the document more carefully. 如果我有更多时间，我会更仔细地检查文件。
I wish I had scrutinized the contract more thoroughly.  我希望我当时更彻底地审查了合同。


被动语态:

疑问句:
