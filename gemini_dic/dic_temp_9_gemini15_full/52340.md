dictaphone:/ˈdɪktəfoʊn/| n. |录音机|A dictaphone is a machine for recording speech, typically used for dictation.  It is an older term, and many modern devices perform the same function.|例句：The secretary used a dictaphone to record the boss's dictation.  秘书用录音机记录老板的口述。

The lawyer transcribed the dictaphone recording of the witness's testimony. 律师抄录了证人证词的录音记录。|近义词：voice recorder, recording device|反义词：None.
