meeknesses: /ˈmiːknɪsəz/ | n. | 温顺；谦逊；懦弱 | The plural form of "meekness," referring to the quality of being meek; mildness of temper or spirit; humility; submissiveness; a lack of assertiveness.  It can also imply weakness or a lack of courage. | 例句：His meeknesses prevented him from standing up for himself. 他的懦弱使他无法为自己辩护。  Her meeknesses were often misinterpreted as weakness. 她的谦逊常常被误解为软弱。 | 近义词：humility, gentleness, submissiveness, mildness | 反义词：assertiveness, aggression, boldness, arrogance | 名词
