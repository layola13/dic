half-understood: /ˌhɑːfˌʌndərˈstʊd/ | adj. | 半懂的；似懂非懂的 | Partially understood; grasped only incompletely.  The meaning or implication is not fully clear. | 例句：The lecture was dense and left me with a half-understood feeling.  这堂课内容晦涩难懂，让我感觉似懂非懂。


The instructions were half-understood, leading to several mistakes.  说明书理解得不透彻，导致好几个错误。

近义词：partially understood, vaguely grasped, imperfectly comprehended, dimly perceived

反义词：fully understood, completely grasped, perfectly comprehended


词性: adjective
