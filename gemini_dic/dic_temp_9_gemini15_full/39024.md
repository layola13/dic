conflagration:/ˌkɒnfləˈɡreɪʃən/| n. |大火，大火灾 | A large destructive fire.  | The conflagration destroyed most of the city.  这场大火摧毁了这座城市的大部分地区。| fire, inferno, blaze |  quenching, extinguishing | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A conflagration threatens the entire forest. = 一场大火威胁着整个森林。
The conflagration spreads rapidly. = 大火迅速蔓延。


一般过去时:
A terrible conflagration occurred last night. = 昨晚发生了一场可怕的大火。
The conflagration consumed the building in minutes. = 大火几分钟内就吞噬了整栋建筑物。


现在完成时:
The firefighters have contained the conflagration. = 消防员已经控制住了大火。
The investigation has revealed the cause of the conflagration. = 调查已经揭示了这场大火的起因。


过去完成时:
The firefighters had fought the conflagration for hours before it was contained. = 消防员在控制住大火之前已经与大火搏斗了几个小时。
The building had already been largely consumed by the conflagration before the fire department arrived. = 在消防部门到达之前，建筑物已经被大火吞噬了大部分。


将来时:
The strong winds may cause the conflagration to spread further. = 强风可能会导致大火进一步蔓延。
Experts predict that the conflagration will be difficult to extinguish completely. = 专家预测这场大火很难完全扑灭。


虚拟语气:
If there had been more firefighters, the conflagration might have been contained sooner. = 如果有更多的消防员，这场大火可能会更快得到控制。
I wish the conflagration hadn't destroyed so many homes. = 我真希望这场大火没有摧毁这么多房屋。


被动语态:
The conflagration was quickly extinguished by the firefighters. = 大火很快被消防员扑灭了。
The cause of the conflagration is being investigated. = 大火的起因正在调查中。


疑问句:
What caused the conflagration? = 导致这场大火的原因是什么？
How widespread was the conflagration? = 这场大火有多么严重？


否定句:
The firefighters did not allow the conflagration to spread further. = 消防员没有让大火进一步蔓延。
There was no evidence of arson in the conflagration. = 这场大火中没有纵火的证据。


PHRASE->
a great conflagration = 一场大火
a widespread conflagration = 一场大范围的大火
a devastating conflagration = 一场毁灭性的大火
a raging conflagration = 一场猛烈的火灾
the conflagration spread = 大火蔓延


词源:
From Medieval Latin *conflagratio, from Latin conflagrāre "to burn together," from com- "together" + flagrare "to burn."
