rebuilding:/ˌriːˈbɪldɪŋ/| v.|重建；改造；复兴|The act of building something again after it has been damaged or destroyed;  the process of restoring something to a former state.|例句：The city is undergoing a massive rebuilding project after the earthquake.  地震后，这座城市正在进行大规模的重建项目。
例句：Rebuilding trust after a betrayal takes time and effort. 背叛之后重建信任需要时间和努力。|近义词：reconstruction, renovation, restoration|反义词：destruction, demolition


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are rebuilding the old bridge. = 他们在重建那座旧桥。
We rebuild our house every year. = 我们每年都重建我们的房子。

一般过去时:
They rebuilt the old church. = 他们重建了那座古老的教堂。
The company rebuilt its image after the scandal. = 那家公司在丑闻之后重建了它的形象。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:
They will rebuild the school next year. = 他们明年将重建学校。
The city will rebuild the damaged areas after the flood. = 洪水过后，城市将重建受损地区。

虚拟语气:
