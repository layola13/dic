embargo:/ɛmˈbɑːrɡoʊ/| n. |禁运；贸易禁运 |An official ban on trade or other commercial activity with a particular country.  |The government imposed an embargo on all imports of oil. 政府对所有石油进口实施了禁运。|sanction, blockade |free trade, open market |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government imposes an embargo on certain goods. = 政府对某些商品实行禁运。
An embargo restricts trade between countries. = 禁运限制了国家之间的贸易。


一般过去时:
The country lifted the embargo last year. = 该国去年取消了禁运。
They imposed an embargo on weapons exports. = 他们对武器出口实施了禁运。


现在完成时:
The embargo has significantly impacted the economy. = 禁运已经严重影响了经济。
Several countries have imposed an embargo on that nation. = 几个国家已经对那个国家实施了禁运。


过去完成时:
The trade had already suffered before the embargo was imposed. = 在实施禁运之前，贸易就已经受到了损害。
The company had anticipated the embargo and prepared accordingly. = 该公司已经预料到禁运并做好了相应的准备。


将来时:
The government will likely extend the embargo. = 政府可能会延长禁运。
They will impose a new embargo if the situation worsens. = 如果情况恶化，他们将实施新的禁运。


虚拟语气:
If the embargo were lifted, the economy would improve. = 如果禁运解除，经济将会好转。
I wish the embargo hadn't been imposed. = 我希望禁运没有被实施。


被动语态:
The embargo was imposed to pressure the regime. = 实施禁运是为了向政权施压。
The goods were subject to an embargo. = 这些商品受到了禁运。


疑问句:
Is there an embargo on these products? = 这些产品有禁运吗？
When will the embargo be lifted? = 禁运什么时候解除？


否定句:
There is no embargo on food exports. = 食品出口没有禁运。
The government did not impose an embargo on that country. = 政府没有对那个国家实施禁运。


PHRASE->
trade embargo = 贸易禁运
oil embargo = 石油禁运
arms embargo = 军火禁运
embargo on exports = 出口禁运
impose an embargo = 实施禁运
