deconcentration: /ˌdiːkɒnsənˈtreɪʃən/ | n. | 分散；去集中；注意力不集中 | The process of reducing the concentration of something, especially power or industry.  It often refers to the dispersal of economic activity away from a central point to more geographically distributed locations. | 例句：The government implemented policies to promote deconcentration of industry in the region.  政府实施了政策以促进该地区产业的分散。

例句：The deconcentration of power led to a more equitable distribution of resources.权力分散导致资源分配更加公平。


近义词：decentralization, dispersal, diffusion | 反义词：concentration, centralization
