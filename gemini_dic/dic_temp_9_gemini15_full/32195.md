chic:/ʃik/| adj.|别致的；时髦的；雅致的|Stylish and fashionable; elegant and sophisticated.|例句：She always looks so chic. 她总是看起来那么时尚。|例句：The boutique offered a chic collection of dresses. 这家精品店提供了一系列别致的连衣裙。|近义词：stylish, fashionable, elegant, sophisticated, smart|反义词：dowdy, unfashionable, frumpy, drab


USAGE->

一般现在时:
She is chic in her simple dress. = 她穿着简单的连衣裙也很时尚。
That restaurant is chic and expensive. = 那家餐厅很时尚也很贵。


一般过去时:
The actress looked chic at the premiere. = 女演员在首映式上看起来很时尚。
Her outfit was very chic last night. = 她昨晚的服装非常时尚。


现在完成时:
She has always been chic, even in her youth. = 她年轻的时候就很时尚，一直到现在。
They have become much more chic since they moved to Paris. = 他们自从搬到巴黎后就变得更时尚了。


过去完成时:
She had already been chic before she became famous. = 在她成名之前就已经很时尚了。
The building had been chic in its time. = 这座建筑在其建造的年代曾非常时尚。


将来时:
She will look chic in that dress. = 她穿上那件裙子会很时尚。
The new collection will be very chic. = 新系列将会非常时尚。


虚拟语气:
If she wore that dress, she would look chic. = 如果她穿上那件裙子，她会看起来很时尚。
I wish I could afford to dress as chic as she does. = 我真希望我能像她一样穿得起这么时尚的衣服。


被动语态:
The dress was considered chic by fashion critics. = 这件裙子被时尚评论家认为是时尚的。
The new designs are being hailed as incredibly chic. = 新的设计被誉为极其时尚。


疑问句:
Is she chic in that outfit? = 她穿着那套衣服时尚吗？
Isn't that dress chic? = 那件裙子不时尚吗？


否定句:
Her style is not chic. = 她的风格并不时尚。
The restaurant wasn't particularly chic. = 那家餐厅并不是特别时尚。
