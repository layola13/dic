pronucleus:/ˈproʊˌnjuːkliəs/| n. |前核|The haploid nucleus of a sperm or egg before fusion with the opposite gamete's nucleus to form the zygote nucleus.  It is the nucleus of the gamete before fertilization.  |Example: The male and female pronuclei fuse to form the zygote nucleus.  雄性和雌性前核融合形成合子核。

|Example: During fertilization, the sperm pronucleus and the egg pronucleus merge. 在受精过程中，精子前核和卵子前核融合。|近义词：gamete nucleus (部分情况下)|反义词：zygote nucleus|名词
