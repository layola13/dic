opposability:/ˌɒpəʊzəˈbɪləti/ | /ˌɑːpəʊzəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 对指性，拇指对掌功能 | The ability of the thumb to be placed opposite the fingers, enabling grasping and manipulation of objects.  This is a crucial characteristic of primate hands. | Example: The high degree of opposability in human thumbs allows for fine motor skills. 人类拇指高度的对指性使得精细的运动技能成为可能。  The opposability of the thumb is essential for many everyday tasks. 拇指的对指性对于许多日常任务至关重要。|近义词：dexterity, manipulability | 反义词：inability to oppose, non-opposability |
