rementioning:/ˌriːˈmɛnʃənɪŋ/ | v. |再次提及；重新提起 | The act of mentioning something again.  It implies a previous mention, often to emphasize a point or to correct a misunderstanding. |例句：The professor kept rementioning the importance of deadlines. 教授不断地再次强调截止日期的重要性。

例句：His rementioning of the incident made everyone uncomfortable. 他再次提起那件事使每个人都感到不舒服。


近义词：recalling, reiterating, repeating, bringing up again | 反义词：ignoring, overlooking, forgetting | 词性：动名词
