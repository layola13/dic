deathblow:/ˈdɛθbloʊ/| n. |致命一击；毁灭性打击；决定性打击 |A deathblow is a decisive blow that kills someone or something, or that destroys or ruins it completely. It refers to an action or event that causes irreparable harm or leads to the end of something.  |例句：The new regulations dealt a deathblow to the small businesses. 新规对小型企业造成了毁灭性打击。


The scandal proved to be the deathblow to his political career.  丑闻成为他政治生涯的致命一击。|近义词：coup de grace, fatal blow, knockout blow, finishing blow |反义词：救星，起死回生
