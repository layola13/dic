knolling:/ˈnɒlɪŋ/| n. |整齐摆放(物品)|The act of arranging objects in a grid-like pattern, typically with the objects aligned to their longest side.  This method is often used for organizing tools, supplies, and other items in a visually appealing and efficient manner.  It's a popular organizational technique, often seen in design studios and workshops.|Example:  He meticulously knolling his tools before starting the project. 他在开始项目之前一丝不苟地摆放他的工具。|近义词：organizing, arranging, tidying|反义词：cluttering, disorganizing|名词
