on-loan: /ˌɒnˈləʊn/ | adj. | 借用的；租借的 |  Describes something that is temporarily borrowed or rented.  It typically refers to a person (often a sports player) or an item temporarily transferred to another organization or individual. |


例句：

The footballer is on loan from Manchester United to Everton.  足球运动员是从曼联租借到埃弗顿的。

The library has several books on loan to other institutions. 图书馆有几本书借给了其他机构。


近义词：borrowed, rented, leased


反义词：owned, permanent


词性：形容词
