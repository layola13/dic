darkened:/ˈdɑːrkənd/ | adj. v. | 变暗的；使变暗 |  Made dark or darker;  obscured by darkness.  The verb form indicates the process of becoming or making something dark. | 例句：The room darkened as the sun set.  房间在日落时变暗了。


The sky darkened ominously before the storm.暴风雨来临前，天空阴沉地暗了下来。


近义词：dimmed, obscured, shadowed, darkened

反义词：brightened, illuminated, lightened


词性: Adjective, Verb


USAGE->

The room darkens slowly at sunset. 日落时房间慢慢变暗。
He darkens the mood with his depressing stories. 他用他令人沮丧的故事使气氛变得沉闷。
