hierogram:/ˈhaɪərəɡræm/| n. |象形文字；圣像图|A hierogram is a picture or symbol representing a word, syllable, or sound, especially one used in ancient Egyptian writing.  It's often a stylized representation of an object or concept.  The term sometimes also refers to a sacred or symbolic image, often used in religious contexts.|Example: The ancient Egyptians used a complex system of hierograms to record their history and beliefs.  埃及人使用复杂的象形文字系统来记录他们的历史和信仰。|近义词：pictogram, ideogram, glyph |反义词：alphabet (in the sense of a phonetic writing system)
