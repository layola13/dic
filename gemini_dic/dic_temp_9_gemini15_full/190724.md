sav:/sæv/ | v. | 保存；拯救；节约 | To keep something safe or to rescue someone or something from danger; to avoid wasting something. | 例句：I saved the document before closing the program. 我在关闭程序之前保存了文档。  I saved the drowning child. 我救了那个溺水的孩子。| 近义词：preserve, rescue, conserve | 反义词：waste, squander, destroy | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He saves money every month. = 他每个月都存钱。
She saves all her important emails. = 她保存了她所有重要的邮件。


一般过去时:
They saved the cat from the tree. = 他们把猫从树上救了下来。
He saved a lot of time by taking the shortcut. = 他抄近路节省了很多时间。


现在完成时:
I have saved enough money for a new car. = 我已经攒够了买新车的钱。
She has saved all her old photographs. = 她保存了她所有的旧照片。


过去完成时:
We had saved the best piece of cake for dessert. = 我们把最好的一块蛋糕留着当甜点。
They had saved enough money for their trip. = 他们已经为他们的旅行存够了钱。


将来时:
I will save you a seat at the dinner. = 我会给你留个座位。
He will save the documents to the cloud. = 他会将文档保存到云端。


虚拟语气:
If I had saved more money, I could have bought a house. = 如果我存的钱更多，我就能买房子了。
I wish I had saved that email. = 我真希望我保存了那封邮件。


被动语态:
The document was saved automatically. = 文档自动保存了。
The child was saved by a brave firefighter. = 一个勇敢的消防员救了那个孩子。


疑问句:
Did you save the file? = 你保存文件了吗？
Will you save me a piece of pie? = 你会给我留一块派吗？


否定句:
He didn't save the document. = 他没有保存文档。
She didn't save enough money for the trip. = 她没有存够旅行的钱。


PHRASE->
