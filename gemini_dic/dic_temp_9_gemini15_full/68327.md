fall flat:/fɔːl flæt/ | phrasal verb | 失败；不受欢迎；没有效果 |  To fail to have the intended effect or to be unsuccessful; to be received without enthusiasm or interest. |例句：His jokes fell flat.  他的笑话很冷。

His attempt to impress her fell flat. 他试图给她留下深刻印象，但失败了。


近义词：flop, fail, bomb, backfire, misfire | 反义词：succeed, triumph, hit, work, be a success | 词性: phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His jokes always fall flat. = 他的笑话总是很冷。
Her new product ideas usually fall flat. = 她新的产品创意通常不受欢迎。


一般过去时:
The comedian's routine fell flat last night. = 昨晚喜剧演员的表演很失败。
The surprise party fell flat because nobody knew about it. = 惊喜派对失败了，因为没有人知道。


现在完成时:
My attempts to start a conversation have fallen flat. = 我尝试开始对话的尝试都失败了。
All my plans for the weekend have fallen flat. = 我所有的周末计划都泡汤了。


过去完成时:
I realised my speech had fallen flat when I saw the audience's blank expressions. = 当我看到观众茫然的表情时，我意识到我的演讲失败了。
They were disappointed that their proposal had fallen flat. = 他们的提议失败了，他们感到很失望。


将来时:
I fear my presentation will fall flat. = 我担心我的演示会失败。
If we don't market it properly, the launch will fall flat. = 如果我们没有做好市场推广，产品发布会就会失败。


虚拟语气:
If I had been more prepared, my presentation wouldn't have fallen flat. = 如果我准备充分一点，我的演示就不会失败了。
I wish my jokes hadn't fallen flat at the party. = 我希望我的笑话在派对上不会那么冷场。


被动语态:
The proposal was deemed to have fallen flat. = 该提案被认为是失败的。
His performance was criticised for falling flat. = 他的表演因失败而受到批评。


疑问句:

否定句:
His speech didn't fall flat; it was a huge success. = 他的演讲没有失败；它非常成功。
The marketing campaign didn't fall flat; it exceeded all expectations. = 市场营销活动没有失败；它超出了所有预期。
