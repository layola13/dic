denting:/ˈdɛntɪŋ/| v.|凹陷；使凹陷|The action of making something become indented or dented.  It refers to the process of creating a dent or dents in a surface.|例句：The car sustained denting to its bumper in the accident.  车在事故中保险杠受损凹陷。

例句：The hail storm caused significant denting to the car's roof.  冰雹风暴导致汽车车顶严重凹陷。


近义词：indenting, damaging, marring|反义词：repairing, smoothing, straightening


USAGE->

The hail is denting the car. = 冰雹正在砸坏汽车。
He is denting the metal with a hammer. = 他正在用锤子敲打金属使其凹陷。
