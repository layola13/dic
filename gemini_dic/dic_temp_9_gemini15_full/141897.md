orderable: /ˈɔːrdərəbl/ | adj. | 可订购的，可预订的 |  Able to be ordered; available for ordering.  This adjective describes something that can be requested and obtained, typically through a formal process like placing an order.  | 例句：The new model of the phone is orderable online. = 这款新手机可以在网上订购。  The limited-edition cake is orderable only until the end of the week. = 限量版蛋糕只能在本周末前预订。| 近义词：available, obtainable, procurable | 反义词：unavailable, unobtainable, out of stock
