overlubricating: /ˌoʊvərˈluːbrɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 过度润滑 | The act of applying more lubricant than is necessary or beneficial.  This can lead to various problems, including increased friction,  reduced efficiency, and even equipment damage. | Example: Overlubricating the engine bearings caused them to overheat and fail.  过度润滑发动机轴承导致它们过热并损坏。 | 近义词: overgreasing | 反义词: underlubricating
