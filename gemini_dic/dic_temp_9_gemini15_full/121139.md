misdelivery:/ˌmɪsˈdɪlɪvəri/| n. |误送，错投 |The act of delivering something to the wrong person or place.  This often refers to mail, packages, or other goods.  It can also refer to the incorrect delivery of information or a message.|例句：The misdelivery of the package caused a delay in the project.  包裹的误投导致项目延期。

The misdelivery of the important documents resulted in a significant setback. 重要文件的误送导致了严重的挫折。|近义词：wrong delivery, incorrect delivery, misdirection|反义词：correct delivery, accurate delivery|名词
