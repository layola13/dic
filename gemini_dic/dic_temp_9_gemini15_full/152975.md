pediments:/ˈpedɪmənts/| n.|三角楣，山墙饰|A pediment is a triangular gable, typically found above a portico or doorway, often decorated with sculpture or other ornamentation.  It's a classical architectural feature. |The temple's pediment was adorned with intricate carvings depicting scenes from mythology.|gable, tympanum|  (反义词缺乏直接对应，可考虑使用lack of pediment)|名词
