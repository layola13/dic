misheard:/ˌmɪsˈhɜːrd/|/ˈmɪsˌhɜːrd/| adj. v. |误听的；听错的 |Incorrectly heard or understood.  |例句：I misheard the announcement and missed the train. 我误听公告，错过了火车。

例句：The instructions were misheard, leading to a costly mistake. 指令被误听，导致了代价高昂的错误。


近义词：misunderstood, misinterpreted |反义词：heard correctly, understood clearly|词性：verb (past participle) , adjective
