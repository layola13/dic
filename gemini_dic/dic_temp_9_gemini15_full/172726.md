quad:/kwɑːd/| n. |四；四倍；四元组；四边形|A quad is a group of four things, or something with four parts or sides. It can refer to a group of four students living together, four-wheeled vehicle, or a four-sided polygon.|例句：We have a quad of students sharing a dorm room. 我们有四个学生合住一个宿舍房间。|近义词：quadruple, quartet, quadrangle|反义词：single, binary, ternary
