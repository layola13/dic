precirculation:/ˌpriːˌsɜːrkjuˈleɪʃən/ | n. |预发行，预先发行 |The process of distributing something, such as a publication or information, before its official release.  This allows for feedback and testing before the wider release. |Example: The precirculation of the draft report allowed for valuable input from stakeholders. 预发行报告草案使利益相关者能够提供宝贵的意见。|近义词：advance distribution, preliminary release |反义词：post-publication, after-release |名词
