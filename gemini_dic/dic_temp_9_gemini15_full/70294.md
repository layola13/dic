fidgeted:/ˈfɪdʒɪtɪd/| v.|烦躁不安地动来动去，坐立不安|To move restlessly, usually due to nervousness, impatience, or boredom.  |例句：He fidgeted nervously in his seat while waiting for the interview. 他在等待面试时，紧张不安地在他座位上动来动去。

例句：She fidgeted with the pen, unable to sit still. 她不停地拨弄着钢笔，坐立不安。


近义词：restless, fidgety, squirmed, wriggled, jittery|反义词：calm, still, relaxed, composed|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fidgets constantly during lectures. = 他上课时总是坐立不安。
She fidgets with her hair when she's nervous. = 她紧张的时候会不停地拨弄头发。

一般过去时:
He fidgeted throughout the entire meeting. = 他整个会议都坐立不安。
She fidgeted with her jewelry before the performance. = 演出前她不停地摆弄她的首饰。

现在完成时:
He has fidgeted all morning. = 他整个上午都坐立不安。
She has fidgeted so much that she's worn out. = 她坐立不安弄得筋疲力尽了。

过去完成时:
He had fidgeted for hours before his name was called. = 在他的名字被叫到之前，他已经坐立不安了几个小时。
She had fidgeted all day waiting for the results. = 她整天都焦急地等待结果。

将来时:
He will fidget if he has to wait much longer. = 如果他还要等更久，他会坐立不安的。
She will fidget during the long flight. = 在漫长的飞行过程中，她会坐立不安的。

虚拟语气:
If he were more confident, he wouldn't fidget so much. = 如果他更有自信，他就不会这么坐立不安了。
I wish I didn't fidget so much during presentations. = 我希望我在演示过程中不要这么坐立不安。

被动语态: (很少用被动语态)
His nervousness was evident as he was seen fidgeting. = 他紧张不安的神情显而易见，因为他被看到在不停地动来动去。


疑问句:
Does he fidget a lot? = 他是不是经常坐立不安？
Is she fidgeting because she's bored? = 她是不是因为无聊才坐立不安的？


否定句:
He didn't fidget during the presentation. = 他在演示过程中没有坐立不安。
She didn't fidget once during the entire movie. = 整个电影过程中她一次都没有坐立不安。


PHRASE->
fidget with something:  不停摆弄某物
fidget nervously: 紧张地坐立不安
fidget about:  四处走动，坐立不安
fidget in one's seat: 在座位上坐立不安
stop fidgeting: 不要再坐立不安了

词源:
The word "fidgeted" comes from the verb "fidget," which  likely originated from a Middle English word related to "fitch," meaning "to move quickly."  The sense of restless movement developed over time.
