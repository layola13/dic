decorating:/ˈdɛkəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 装饰；装修 | The act of making something look more attractive by adding things to it.  It can refer to the process of adding decorations to a space, object, or person. | 例句：She is decorating her new apartment. = 她正在装饰她的新公寓。

She spent the weekend decorating the Christmas tree. = 她周末花了时间装饰圣诞树。


近义词：adorn, embellish, ornament | 反义词：strip, undress, despoil | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They decorate their house for every holiday. = 他们每个节日都装饰他们的房子。
We are decorating the cake with frosting. = 我们正在用糖霜装饰蛋糕。


一般过去时:
He decorated the Christmas tree with lights and ornaments. = 他用彩灯和装饰品装饰了圣诞树。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
