knee:/niː/| n. |膝盖，膝|The joint between the thigh and the leg.  It is used for bending and supporting the body.  |例句：He knelt down on one knee to propose to her. 他单膝跪地向她求婚。
例句：The pain in his knee was severe.  他的膝盖疼痛剧烈。|近义词：joint, articulation|反义词:无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My knee hurts. = 我的膝盖疼。
She bends her knees. = 她弯曲她的膝盖。


一般过去时:
I injured my knee yesterday. = 我昨天伤了膝盖。
He fell and scraped his knee. = 他摔倒了，擦伤了膝盖。


现在完成时:
I have had knee pain for weeks. = 我几周来膝盖一直疼。
She has never broken a knee. = 她从未摔断过膝盖。


过去完成时:
I had already hurt my knee before the game started. = 在比赛开始之前，我的膝盖就已经受伤了。
He had fallen and scraped his knee before I arrived. = 我到达之前，他已经摔倒擦伤了膝盖。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
