nostrum's:/ˈnɒstrəmz/ | n. |秘方；（尤指无效的）疗法；江湖郎中药 |a medicine, remedy, or system of treatment not scientifically proven and often of questionable value.  It often implies a quack remedy. |例句：His nostrum for all ills was a daily dose of sunshine and fresh air.  他的包治百病的秘方是每天晒太阳和呼吸新鲜空气。

His nostrum's effectiveness is highly questionable. 他的秘方疗效非常值得怀疑。


近义词：remedy, cure, panacea, quackery

反义词：proven treatment, effective medicine


词性：名词 (复数形式)
