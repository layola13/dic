commandership:/kəˈmændʃɪp/| n. |指挥权；指挥职位；统率；领导能力|The authority and responsibility of a commander; the position or rank of a commander; the act, art, or manner of commanding.  It can also refer to the qualities of a good leader.|例句：He demonstrated exceptional commandership during the crisis. 他在危机期间展现了卓越的指挥能力。|例句：The commandership of the battalion was given to Captain Jones.  营的指挥权交给了琼斯上尉。|近义词：leadership, command, authority, control|反义词：subordination, obedience, following
