discobolus:/dɪsˈkɒbələs/| n. |掷铁饼者；掷铁饼雕像|A statue or representation of a discus thrower, especially the famous classical Greek bronze statue by Myron.  The word itself refers to both the athlete and the artwork depicting them.  |例句：The Discobolus is a masterpiece of classical Greek sculpture. 掷铁饼者是希腊古典雕塑的杰作。|例句：The museum's prized possession is a Roman copy of the famous Discobolus.  博物馆的珍藏是一尊著名的掷铁饼者雕像的罗马复制品。|近义词：athlete, thrower, statue |反义词：N/A
