dieselization:/ˌdiːzəlʌɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |柴油化；改用柴油机|The process of converting something to use diesel fuel or diesel engines.  This often refers to the transition from gasoline or other fuels to diesel in vehicles, power generation, or other applications.|例句：The dieselization of the railway system improved efficiency and reduced operating costs.  铁路系统的柴油化提高了效率并降低了运营成本。

The increasing dieselization of the trucking industry is contributing to air pollution in many cities. 卡车行业的柴油化加剧正在导致许多城市的空气污染。|近义词：diesel conversion,  fuel conversion|反义词：gasolization (less common)|名词
