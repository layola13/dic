protodynastic: /ˌproʊtoʊdaɪˈnæstɪk/ | adj. | 原王朝的；前王朝的 | Relating to the period in ancient Egypt before the first dynasty, characterized by the development of a centralized state and the beginnings of royal power.  | Example:  Protodynastic Egypt saw the emergence of sophisticated pottery techniques. 原王朝时期的埃及出现了复杂的陶器制作技术。| 近义词: predynastic, early dynastic | 反义词: late dynastic
