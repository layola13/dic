repossessable: /ˌriːpəˈsɛsəbl/ | adj. | 可收回的，可收回的财产 | Capable of being repossessed; able to be taken back by a creditor or other party.  | The repossessable car was towed away after the owner defaulted on payments. 这辆可收回的汽车在车主拖欠付款后被拖走了。 |  recoverable, retrievable | unrecoverable, irretrievable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bank repossesses many cars every year. = 银行每年收回很多汽车。
This item is repossessable if payments are missed. = 如果错过付款，这件物品是可以收回的。


一般过去时:
The lender repossessed the house due to non-payment. = 由于未付款，贷款人收回了房屋。
They repossessed the equipment after the contract breach. = 违反合同后，他们收回了设备。


现在完成时:
The company has repossessed several vehicles this month. = 本月公司已收回了几辆车。
The creditor has repossessed the property due to unpaid debt. = 债权人因债务未付而收回了财产。


过去完成时:
The bank had already repossessed the car before he contacted them. = 他联系他们之前，银行已经收回了汽车。
The landlord had repossessed the apartment before the tenant could move out. = 在租户搬出之前，房东已经收回了公寓。


将来时:
The creditor will repossess the goods if the payments are not made. = 如果不付款，债权人将收回货物。
The company will repossess the equipment next week. = 公司下周将收回设备。


虚拟语气:
If the payments were made on time, the car would not be repossessable. = 如果按时付款，汽车就不会被收回。
If I had the money, I wouldn't let them repossess my house. = 如果我有钱，我就不会让他们收回我的房子。


被动语态:
The property was repossessed by the bank last week. = 该物业上周被银行收回。
The car is being repossessed because of unpaid loans. = 汽车正在被收回，因为贷款未还。


疑问句:
Is the item repossessable under these circumstances? = 在这种情况下，该物品是否可以收回？
Are these items repossessable after the contract expires? = 合同到期后，这些物品是否可以收回？


否定句:
The car is not repossessable at this time. = 目前这辆车不可收回。
The debt is not repossessable without a court order. = 没有法院命令，债务是不可收回的。
