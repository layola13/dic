preface:/ˈprɛfɪs/| n. |前言，序言 |A preface is an introductory statement in a book, typically explaining the author's purpose or the background to the work.  It's placed before the main text. |The preface to the novel explained the author's inspiration. 这部小说的前言解释了作者的灵感。| introduction, foreword, prologue |  | n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The author writes a preface for his new book. = 作者为他的新书写前言。
This preface explains the book's main arguments. = 这篇前言解释了本书的主要论点。


一般过去时:
The editor added a preface to the collection of essays. = 编辑在论文集中添加了一个前言。
She wrote a moving preface to her memoir. = 她为她的回忆录写了一个感人的前言。

过去完成时:

The author had already written the preface when he started the main text. = 作者在开始写正文之前就已经写好了前言。


将来时:
