culminates:/ˈkʌlmɪneɪts/ | v. |达到顶点；达到高潮；告终|To reach a high point of development; to end or climax.|The years of hard work culminated in her promotion to CEO.  多年努力最终使她晋升为首席执行官。|The festival culminates in a spectacular fireworks display.  节日以壮观的烟火表演结束。|peaks, climaxes, apex, zenith |begins, starts, commences|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The project culminates in a final presentation. = 这个项目以最终的演示文稿告终。
His efforts culminate in a successful business. = 他的努力最终成就了一家成功的企业。


一般过去时:
The party culminated in a lively dance. = 晚会以热闹的舞蹈告终。
Their long journey culminated in reaching the summit. = 他们的漫长旅程最终到达了山顶。


现在完成时:
The investigation has culminated in several arrests. = 调查已经导致了几次逮捕。
Her career has culminated in international recognition. = 她的事业最终获得了国际认可。


过去完成时:
The negotiations had culminated in a signed agreement. = 谈判最终达成了一份签署的协议。
After years of struggle, he had finally culminated in his goal. = 经过多年的奋斗，他最终实现了目标。


将来时:
The concert will culminate in a breathtaking performance by the lead singer. = 音乐会将以主唱令人叹为观止的表演结束。
Our fundraising efforts will culminate in a grand gala. = 我们的筹款活动将以盛大的晚会结束。


虚拟语气:
If the plan culminated successfully, we would celebrate. = 如果计划成功结束，我们将庆祝。
If only his efforts had culminated in a better outcome. = 但愿他的努力能有更好的结果。


被动语态:
The project is expected to culminate in a public exhibition. = 预计该项目将以公开展览结束。
The meeting was culminated in a unanimous decision. = 会议以一致的决定结束。


疑问句:
Does the story culminate in a happy ending? = 故事结局幸福吗？
When does the festival culminate? = 节日什么时候结束？


否定句:
The play didn't culminate in a satisfying resolution. = 这部戏剧没有以令人满意的结局结束。
His efforts did not culminate in the desired results. = 他的努力没有达到预期的效果。


PHRASE->

culminate with = 以...结束
culminate at = 在...达到顶点
culminate upon = 以...为顶点
culminate to =  发展到...
