colonialize:/kəˈloʊniˌaɪz/|/ˈkɒlənaɪz/| vt.|殖民化；开拓殖民地|To establish control over (a place or people) and exploit its resources.  This often involves settling in and establishing political control, often accompanied by cultural and economic dominance.|例句：The European powers colonialized much of Africa and Asia during the 19th century.  欧洲列强在19世纪殖民了非洲和亚洲的大部分地区。

例句：The company is accused of trying to colonialize the local culture through its marketing strategies. 这家公司被指控试图通过其营销策略来殖民当地文化。


近义词：conquer, occupy, settle, subjugate| 反义词：decolonize, liberate
