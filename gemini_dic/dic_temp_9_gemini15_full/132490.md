nondesecration: /ˌnɒnˌdesɪˈkreɪʃən/ | n. | 非亵渎；未被亵渎 | The act or state of not desecrating something; the preservation of something from desecration.  It implies a respect for something sacred or significant.  The word is relatively rare and often found in formal or specialized contexts. | Example: The museum ensured the nondesecration of the ancient artifacts.  博物馆确保了古代文物的完整无损。（避免亵渎）| 近义词: preservation, protection, safeguarding | 反义词: desecration, violation, profanation | 名词
