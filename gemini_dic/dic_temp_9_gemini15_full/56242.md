dog-handling: /ˈdɒɡˌhændlɪŋ/ | n. |训犬；犬只处理 | The skill and practice of training and managing dogs.  It encompasses various aspects, from basic obedience to advanced competitive activities. |例句：Her dog-handling skills are exceptional; she won first place in the agility competition.  她的训犬技巧非常出色；她在敏捷性比赛中获得了第一名。

例句：The police officer received specialized training in dog-handling techniques.  这位警官接受了专业的训犬技术培训。


近义词：dog training, canine handling, animal handling |反义词：dog neglect, animal abuse |词性:名词
