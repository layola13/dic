pilfer:/ˈpɪlfər/| vt.|偷窃（少量东西）|To steal (something, especially something of little value).  It often implies stealing something in a sneaky or petty way.|例句：He was caught pilfering stationery from the office. 他被抓到偷办公室的文具。|例句：The thief pilfered a few coins from the cash register. 小偷从收银机里偷了几枚硬币。|近义词：steal, rob, thieve, purloin|反义词：donate, give


USAGE->

He pilfers small items from the store. = 他从商店偷窃小件物品。
She pilfers money from the till. = 她从收银机里偷钱。
