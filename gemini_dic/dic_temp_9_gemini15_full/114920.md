manageably:/ˈmænɪdʒəbli/ | adj. | 可管理地；易于处理地 |  Able to be managed or controlled easily; easily handled.  | The workload was manageably small. 工作量很小，易于处理。|The project's complexity was surprisingly manageable. 项目的复杂程度出乎意料地容易处理。 | manageable,  controllable | unmanageable, overwhelming, uncontrollable
