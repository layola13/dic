other-fish-fry: /ˈʌðər fɪʃ fraɪ/ | n. | 小人物；无足轻重的人；无关紧要的事 |  Refers to insignificant people or things; those who are not important or influential.  It's a figurative expression, drawing a parallel to small fish being less significant than larger ones in a fish fry (a large gathering or group). | Example: "Don't worry about the other fish fry; focus on the main issues."  别担心那些无关紧要的小事；关注主要问题。 | 近义词：small fry, insignificant people, minor details | 反义词：big shots, VIPs, important matters |  名词
