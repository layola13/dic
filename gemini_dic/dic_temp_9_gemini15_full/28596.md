caregivings:/ˈkɛərˌɡɪvɪŋz/ | n. | 担忧；顾虑；忧虑 | Feelings of anxiety or worry about something.  Caregivings often relate to responsibilities or potential negative outcomes. | Example:  Her caregivings about her son's future were considerable. 她对她儿子的未来感到相当担忧。  His caregivings prevented him from taking the risk. 他的担忧阻止了他冒险。 | 近义词：concerns, worries, anxieties, apprehensions | 反义词：confidence, assurance, peace of mind |  名词
