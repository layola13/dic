cowslips:/ˈkaʊzlɪps/| n.|报春花|A cowslip is a small, yellow wildflower that blooms in spring.  It's known for its delicate, drooping flowers.  The name is thought to derive from the shape of the flower resembling a cow's lip.|例句：The meadow was carpeted with vibrant yellow cowslips.  牧场上铺满了鲜艳的黄色报春花。

The cowslips grew abundantly near the stream. 报春花在小溪边大量生长。|近义词：primrose, marsh marigold|反义词:None.
