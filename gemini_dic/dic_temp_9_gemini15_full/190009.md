sandblasted:/ˈsændˌblæstɪd/| adj.|喷砂处理过的;被喷砂侵蚀的| Treated with a stream of sand propelled by air or other gas to clean or etch a surface.  The process is used to clean, roughen, or shape a variety of materials. |例句：The glass was sandblasted to create a frosted effect.  玻璃经过喷砂处理，创造出磨砂效果。

The stone sculpture was sandblasted to remove years of grime. 石雕被喷砂处理以去除多年的污垢。

近义词：abraded, etched, textured|反义词：smooth, polished
