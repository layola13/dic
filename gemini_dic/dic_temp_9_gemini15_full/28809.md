carotene:/ˈkærətiːn/| n.|胡萝卜素|A pigment that is responsible for the orange and yellow colors in many plants and fruits.  Carotenes are also important precursors to vitamin A.  They are found in carrots, sweet potatoes, and other orange and yellow vegetables. |例句：Carrots are a good source of beta-carotene, which the body converts to vitamin A. 胡萝卜是β-胡萝卜素的良好来源，人体会将其转化为维生素A。|例句：The vibrant orange color of the pumpkin is due to the presence of various carotenoids. 南瓜鲜艳的橙色是由于存在各种类胡萝卜素。|近义词：carotenoid|反义词：无
