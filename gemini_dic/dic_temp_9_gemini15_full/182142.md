remittitur:/ˌrɛmɪˈtʊtər/ | n. | 汇款；减刑；发回；撤销 |  A remittitur is a court order that reduces the amount of a judgment or sentence. It can also refer to the act of sending money.  | The court issued a remittitur, reducing the defendant's sentence by five years. | reduction, abatement | increase, aggravation | 名词
