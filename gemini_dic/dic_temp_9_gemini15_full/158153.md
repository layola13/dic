plathelminth:/ˌplætˈɛlmɪnθ/| n. |扁形动物|A flatworm; any invertebrate of the phylum Platyhelminthes, characterized by a flat, ribbon-like body and usually lacking a coelom (body cavity).  They are typically hermaphroditic and often parasitic.| Example:  Many species of plathelminths are parasitic, living in the intestines of other animals. 许多种类的扁形动物是寄生的，生活在其他动物的肠道里。|近义词: flatworm |反义词:  (no direct antonym, as it refers to a phylum)|名词
