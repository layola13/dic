imagoes:/ɪˈmæɡoʊz/| n. | 成虫，羽化后的昆虫 | The plural of imago, referring to the sexually mature adult stage of an insect after metamorphosis.  Imagoes are often the dispersal stage of the insect's life cycle. | Example:  The butterflies emerged from their pupae as imagoes, ready to reproduce.  蝴蝶从蛹中羽化成成虫，准备繁殖。| 近义词：adult insect, mature insect | 反义词：larva, pupa, nymph
