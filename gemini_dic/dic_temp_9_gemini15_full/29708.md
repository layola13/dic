catholicity: /ˌkæθəˈlɪsəti/ | n. | 天主教性；普遍性；宽容；博大；包容性 | The quality of being catholic; broad-mindedness; universality.  It refers to the inclusiveness and comprehensiveness of the Catholic Church, but can also be used more generally to describe a broad-minded or universal outlook. | The catholicity of his tastes extended to all forms of art.  他的兴趣广泛，涵盖了各种艺术形式。  | universality, comprehensiveness, inclusiveness, breadth | narrow-mindedness, intolerance, bigotry, exclusivity | 名词
