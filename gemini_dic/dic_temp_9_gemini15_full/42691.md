countertenor: /ˈkaʊntəˌtɛnər/ | n. | 男高音; 反男高音 | A male singer with a voice that has the range of a tenor but is produced using a falsetto technique.  Countertenors sing in a high register, typically above the tenor range, using a technique that allows them to sing high notes without the strain of a normal tenor voice. | Example:  The countertenor's soaring high notes filled the cathedral.  这位反男高音的嘹亮高音充满了整个大教堂。

|近义词: alto (in some contexts), falsettist | 反义词: bass, baritone | 词性: noun
