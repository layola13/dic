margaritiferous:/ˌmɑːrɡærɪˈtɪfərəs/| adj. |含珍珠的；产珍珠的|Producing or containing pearls.  The term refers to organisms, environments, or substances that contain or produce pearls.  |例句：The margaritiferous oyster produced a beautiful, lustrous pearl. 这颗产珍珠的牡蛎产出了一颗美丽光泽的珍珠。

The margaritiferous waters of the region are known for their high-quality pearls.  这个地区的产珍珠水域以其高质量的珍珠而闻名。|近义词：pearly, nacreous |反义词：pearl-less
