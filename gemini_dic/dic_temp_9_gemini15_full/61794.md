emphasized:/ˈɛmfəsaɪzd/ | v. |强调，重视 | To give special importance or prominence to (something) in speaking or writing.  | The report emphasized the need for more research. 这份报告强调了需要进行更多研究。 | stressed, highlighted, underlined | downplayed, ignored | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He emphasizes the importance of punctuality. 他强调准时的重要性。
She emphasizes her point with strong examples. 她用强有力的例子来强调她的观点。


一般过去时:
The teacher emphasized the key concepts. 老师强调了关键概念。
He emphasized the urgency of the situation. 他强调了情况的紧急性。


现在完成时:
She has emphasized the need for change many times. 她多次强调了变革的必要性。
They have emphasized the importance of teamwork throughout the project. 在整个项目中，他们一直强调团队合作的重要性。


过去完成时:
He had emphasized the risks involved before the decision was made. 在做出决定之前，他已经强调了所涉及的风险。
She had emphasized the deadline several times, but they still missed it. 她已经多次强调了截止日期，但他们还是错过了。


将来时:
He will emphasize the benefits of the new policy. 他将强调新政策的好处。
She will emphasize the importance of safety procedures during the training. 在培训期间，她将强调安全程序的重要性。


虚拟语气:
If he emphasized his points more clearly, people would understand better. 如果他更清晰地强调他的观点，人们就会更好地理解。
I wish she had emphasized the consequences earlier. 我希望她早点强调后果。


被动语态:
The importance of education was emphasized in the speech. 演讲中强调了教育的重要性。
The need for immediate action was emphasized by the committee. 委员会强调了立即采取行动的必要性。


疑问句:
What points did the speaker emphasize? 演讲者强调了哪些要点？
Does the article emphasize any specific findings? 这篇文章是否强调任何具体的发现？


否定句:
The report did not emphasize the negative aspects. 这份报告没有强调消极方面。
He didn't emphasize the importance of details. 他没有强调细节的重要性。
