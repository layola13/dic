rematching:/ˌriːˈmætʃɪŋ/| v.|重新匹配，再匹配|The act of matching again; finding a new match or pairing.  It often implies a previous matching that was unsuccessful or needs to be repeated.|例句：The dating app allowed for rematching with users you previously rejected.  约会软件允许你重新匹配之前拒绝过的用户。

例句：After the initial pairings proved unsatisfactory, the organizers implemented a rematching process.  在最初的配对证明不令人满意之后，组织者实施了一个重新匹配的过程。


近义词：recoupling, re-pairing, remaking


反义词：unmatching, separating


词性:动词
