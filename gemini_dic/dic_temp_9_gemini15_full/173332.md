quasi-delightedly: /ˈkwɑːzi dɪˈlaɪtɪdli/ | adv. | 近乎愉快地，装作高兴地 |  Expressing a feeling that is only partly or superficially delighted. It suggests a pretense or lack of genuine enthusiasm. | Example:  She smiled quasi-delightedly at the news, but I could see a flicker of doubt in her eyes. 她对这个消息报以近乎愉快的笑容，但我能看出她眼中闪过一丝怀疑。 | 近义词:  feignedly delighted, half-heartedly pleased, with a forced smile | 反义词:  dismayed, unhappy, genuinely displeased
