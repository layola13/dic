The word "dumbs" is not a standard English word.  There is no entry for it in major dictionaries.  It's likely a misspelling or a non-standard usage.  The closest correct word is "dumb," which itself has evolved in meaning over time.  Therefore, I will analyze "dumb" instead.


dumb: /dʌm/ | adj. | 哑的；愚蠢的；令人震惊的 |  Silent; unable to speak; lacking intelligence;  extremely surprising or shocking. |  例句：The baby was born dumb.  婴儿生下来就是哑巴。 |  The question was dumb. 这个问题很愚蠢。 |近义词：mute, speechless, stupid, foolish, idiotic, astonishing, astounding|反义词： articulate, intelligent, clever, bright, unsurprising


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is dumb. = 他很笨。
The dog is dumb. = 这只狗很安静。


一般过去时:
He was dumb with surprise. = 他惊讶得说不出话来。
She was dumbstruck. = 她惊呆了。


现在完成时:
He has been dumb since the accident. = 自从事故发生后，他就哑巴了。
They have been dumb about the whole situation. = 他们对整个情况都保持沉默。


过去完成时:
He had been dumb for years before the operation. = 手术前他已经哑了好几年了。
She had been dumbstruck when she saw the damage. = 当她看到损坏时，她惊呆了。


将来时:
He will be dumb if he doesn't study. = 如果他不学习，他会很笨。
She will be dumb with shock. = 她会惊呆的。


虚拟语气:
If he were dumb, he couldn't communicate. = 如果他是哑巴，他就无法交流。
If I were dumb, I wouldn't know what to say. = 如果我是哑巴，我就不知道该说什么。


被动语态:
He was made dumb by the accident. = 他因事故而变哑了。
(Passive voice is less common with "dumb" as an adjective)


疑问句:
Is he dumb? = 他哑巴吗？
Was she dumbstruck? = 她惊呆了吗？


否定句:
He is not dumb; he's just shy. = 他不笨，他只是害羞。
She wasn't dumbstruck; she was just very calm. = 她没有惊呆，她只是非常平静。
