quasi-adopts:/ˈkwɑːzi əˈdɒpts/| v.|准收养，近似收养 |  To take on the characteristics or responsibilities of adoption, but without the full legal or formal status.  This often implies a partial or informal adoption, perhaps through fostering, guardianship, or a close, parent-like relationship. | Example:  The elderly couple quasi-adopted their niece after her parents passed away, providing a home and support but not formally adopting her. =  这对老年夫妇在她父母去世后准收养了他们的侄女，为她提供住所和支持，但并没有正式收养她。|近义词：foster, take in, informally adopt|反义词：formally adopt, abandon
