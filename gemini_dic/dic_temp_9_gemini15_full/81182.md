grandfatherless: /ˈɡrænfɑːðərlɛs/ | adj. | 无祖父的；缺乏祖父关爱的 | Lacking a grandfather;  characterized by the absence of a grandfather's influence or presence.  The term can refer to a literal lack of a grandfather figure due to death, absence, or never having one, or it can refer to a metaphorical lack of the guidance, support, or influence typically associated with a grandfather figure.

例句：

She grew up in a grandfatherless home, lacking the male role model she craved. = 她在一个没有祖父的家中长大，缺乏她渴望的男性榜样。

The play explores the themes of fatherlessness and grandfatherless childhoods in a poignant way. = 这部戏以令人心酸的方式探讨了缺乏父亲和祖父的童年主题。


近义词：fatherless (if referring to the lack of male role model), without a grandfather, orphaned (if the grandfather's absence is due to death)

反义词：having a grandfather, with a grandfather figure

词性: adjective (形容词)
