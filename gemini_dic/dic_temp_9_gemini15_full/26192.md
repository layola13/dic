butment:/ˈbʌtmənt/| n.|桥台；桥墩；支撑物|A structure of masonry, concrete, or other material that supports the end of a bridge, wall, or other structure.  It provides stability and distributes the weight of the structure to the ground.|例句：The bridge butments were built of solid granite.  桥台是用坚固的花岗岩建造的。
The old wall had collapsed, leaving only the butments standing.  老墙倒塌了，只剩下桥台还屹立着。|近义词：abutment, pier, support|反义词：unsupported
