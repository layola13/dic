plumed:/pluːmd/| adj.| 羽毛状的；装饰有羽毛的； |Adorned with or resembling feathers.  It suggests elegance and often a sense of grandeur or showiness. |例句：The plumed helmet of the knight was a symbol of his rank.  骑士的羽毛头盔是他地位的象征。

例句：The dancer's costume was richly plumed, creating a stunning visual effect. 舞者的服装装饰着华丽的羽毛，创造出令人惊艳的视觉效果。


近义词：feathered, ornamented, adorned

反义词：bare, unadorned


词源：源自古英语 *plum(m)ed，  plum 的过去分词形式，意为“覆盖羽毛”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird is plumed in vibrant colours. = 这只鸟羽毛颜色鲜艳。
His hat is plumed with ostrich feathers. = 他的帽子装饰着鸵鸟羽毛。

一般过去时:
They plumed the arrow with eagle feathers. = 他们用鹰毛装饰箭。
The artist carefully plumed the hat for the painting. = 艺术家仔细地为绘画中的帽子装饰羽毛。

现在完成时:
The ceremonial headdress has been beautifully plumed. = 这顶仪式用的头饰已被精美地装饰上羽毛。
She has plumed her hat with a delicate bird feather. = 她用精致的鸟羽装饰了她的帽子。

过去完成时:
The archer had plumed his arrows before the competition. = 弓箭手在比赛前已经装饰好了他的箭。
The designer had already plumed the dress before the model's arrival. = 设计师在模特到达之前已经装饰好了裙子。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If the hat were plumed more elaborately, it would be more impressive. = 如果帽子装饰得更华丽一些，就会更令人印象深刻。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
