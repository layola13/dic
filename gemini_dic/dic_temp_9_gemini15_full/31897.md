cheesecloths:/ˈtʃiːzkloθz/| n.|粗棉布，细棉布|A cheesecloth is a loosely woven cotton fabric, often used for straining liquids or wrapping foods.|例句：She used a cheesecloth to strain the yogurt. 她用一块粗棉布过滤酸奶。|例句：The cheese was wrapped in cheesecloth to allow it to breathe. 奶酪用细棉布包裹以利于透气。|近义词：fine muslin, butter muslin|反义词:heavy fabric
