skite:/skaɪt/| v.| 吹牛，炫耀 | To boast or brag, often excessively or about unimportant things.  | He skited about his new car all evening. 他整个晚上都在炫耀他的新车。 | boast, brag, show off | humble, modest | 动词


USAGE->

He skites about his achievements. = 他吹嘘他的成就。
She skites constantly about her travels. = 她不断炫耀她的旅行经历。
