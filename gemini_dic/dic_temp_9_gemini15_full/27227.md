calm:/kɑːm/|adj., v.|平静的；使平静|Peaceful, tranquil, quiet, and free from disturbance.  It can describe a person, place, or situation.|例句：The sea was calm after the storm.  暴风雨过后，大海平静了。|例句：She took a deep breath to calm her nerves. 她深吸一口气来平复她的紧张情绪。|近义词：peaceful, tranquil, serene, composed|反义词：agitated, turbulent, stormy, frantic|形容词，动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The lake remains calm.  湖水依然平静。
He calms himself before the speech. 他在演讲前使自己平静下来。


一般过去时:
The music calmed her. 音乐使她平静下来。
The storm calmed down quickly. 暴风雨很快就平息了。


现在完成时:
The situation has calmed down considerably. 局势已经平静了很多。

过去完成时:
After the argument, they had calmed down. 争吵过后，他们平静下来了。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If only I could calm my anxiety. 但愿我能平静我的焦虑。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

词源:

The word "calm" originates from the Old French word "calm," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "calmus," meaning "still, quiet."  The evolution reflects the consistent association of the word with a state of stillness and peace.
