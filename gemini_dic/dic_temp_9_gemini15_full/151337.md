parity's:/ˈpærətiz/| n.|平等；同等；相同；平价|The state or condition of being equal, especially in status, amount, or value.  It often refers to the equivalence of two things.|例句：Gender parity in the workplace is still a long way off. 工作场所的性别平等还有很长的路要走。|例句：The two countries are working towards parity in trade. 这两个国家正在努力实现贸易平等。|近义词：equality, equivalence, similarity|反义词：inequality, disparity
