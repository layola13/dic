nonpantheistically: /ˌnɒnˌpænθiˈɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 非泛神论地 | Not in a pantheistic manner; without identifying God with the universe.  |例句：He interpreted the text nonpantheistically, emphasizing the distinctness of God from creation. 他以非泛神论的方式解读这段经文，强调上帝与创造物之间的区别。


近义词：transcendentally, theistically


反义词：pantheistically


词性：副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and as such, it doesn't have verb conjugations like a verb.  Its usage is primarily descriptive, modifying a verb or an entire clause.  Examples demonstrating its usage in different sentence structures:

Simple Sentence:
He argued nonpantheistically. 他以非泛神论的方式辩论。


Complex Sentence:
Although many interpretations lean towards pantheism,  he chose to explain the mythology nonpantheistically. 尽管许多解释倾向于泛神论，但他选择以非泛神论的方式解释神话。


Compound Sentence:
The theologian presented a nonpantheistic view; therefore, the audience understood God's transcendence.  神学家提出了非泛神论的观点；因此，听众理解了上帝的超越性。


PHRASE->
There aren't common phrases specifically using "nonpantheistically" due to its specialized meaning.  However, it can be used in phrases like:

A nonpantheistic interpretation of...  对……的非泛神论解释
To view something nonpantheistically  以非泛神论的视角看待某事物
A nonpantheistically-informed perspective  一个基于非泛神论的视角
Thinking nonpantheistically  以非泛神论的方式思考
Explaining something nonpantheistically  以非泛神论的方式解释某事物
