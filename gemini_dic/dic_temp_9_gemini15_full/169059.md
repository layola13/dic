programmability:/ˌproʊɡræməˈbɪləti/|/n.|可编程性，程序化能力|The ability to be programmed or controlled by a program.  It refers to the extent to which a system or device can be adapted to perform different tasks by modifying its program or instructions.|例句：The high programmability of this software allows for extensive customization.  这个软件的可编程性很高，允许进行广泛的定制。

例句：The robot's programmability enables it to perform a wide variety of tasks.  这个机器人的可编程性使其能够执行各种各样的任务。


近义词：configurability, adaptability, flexibility


反义词：inflexibility, rigidity


词性：名词
