foxed:/fɒkst/|ˈfɑːkst/| adj.|迷惑的；醉醺醺的；狡猾的|Confused, bewildered, or intoxicated; also, cunning or sly.|例句：I'm completely foxed by this puzzle.  = 我被这个谜题完全搞糊涂了。  |例句：He was foxed after drinking too much. = 他喝多了之后醉醺醺的。|近义词：confused, bewildered, drunk, intoxicated, cunning, sly|反义词：clear, sober, straightforward


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is foxed by the riddle. = 他被这个谜语难住了。
The clever fox foxed the hunter. = 那只狡猾的狐狸骗过了猎人。


一般过去时:
The complex instructions foxed me. = 复杂的指令把我弄糊涂了。
The magician foxed the audience with his trick. = 魔术师用他的魔术戏法迷惑了观众。


现在完成时:
The sudden change of plans has foxed everyone. = 计划的突然改变让每个人都感到困惑。
The detective has been foxed by the criminal's cunning. = 侦探被罪犯的狡猾难住了。


过去完成时:
I had been foxed by his charming demeanor before realizing his true intentions. = 在意识到他的真实意图之前，我一直被他迷人的举止迷惑了。
They had been foxed by the misleading clues. = 他们被误导的线索迷惑了。


将来时:
The new regulations will fox many businesses. = 新规将使许多企业感到困惑。
This complex problem will fox even the most experienced engineers. = 这个问题即使对于最有经验的工程师来说也很棘手。


虚拟语气:

I wish I weren't so foxed by his lies. = 我真希望我不那么容易被他谎言迷惑。

否定句:
