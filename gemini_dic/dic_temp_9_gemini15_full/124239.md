mossie: /ˈmɒzi/ | n. | 蚊子 | A small flying insect with a long proboscis that sucks the blood of animals, often transmitting diseases.  | Example:  A mossie landed on my arm and bit me.  一只蚊子落在我的胳膊上咬了我。 | 近义词: mosquito, gnat (smaller than a mosquito) | 反义词:  None directly, but could be considered the opposite of something that benefits humans.
