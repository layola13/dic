illumine:/ɪˈluːmɪn/| vt.|照亮；启迪；阐明|to light up; to enlighten; to make clear|例句：The moon illumined the night sky. 月亮照亮了夜空。 The philosopher's words illumined my understanding. 哲学家的言辞启迪了我的理解。|近义词：light up, illuminate, enlighten|反义词：darken, obscure|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun illuminates the earth. = 太阳照亮地球。
The teacher illuminates complex concepts for students. = 老师为学生阐明复杂的概念。

一般过去时:
The candles illumined the dark room. = 蜡烛照亮了黑暗的房间。
The speech illumined the audience's understanding of the issue. = 演讲使听众对这个问题有了更清晰的理解。

现在完成时:
The artist's work has illumined the lives of many. = 艺术家的作品照亮了许多人的生活。
Her research has illumined the mysteries of the ancient civilization. = 她的研究阐明了古代文明的奥秘。

过去完成时:
The discovery had illumined the path to a new theory. = 这一发现为一个新理论指明了道路。
The previous experiments had illumined the limitations of the method. = 先前的实验已经阐明了该方法的局限性。

将来时:
The new technology will illumine the future of medicine. = 新技术将照亮医学的未来。
The upcoming report will illumine the causes of the accident. = 即将来临的报告将阐明事故的原因。

虚拟语气:
If the moon were full, it would illumine the landscape beautifully. = 如果月亮是满月，它将美丽地照亮风景。
If he had studied harder, the subject would have illumined itself to him. = 如果他学习更努力，这个科目就会变得清晰明了。

被动语态:
The room was illumined by the setting sun. = 房间被落日照亮了。
The mystery was illumined by the detective's clever deduction. = 侦探的巧妙推理阐明了这个谜团。

疑问句:
What illumined the path ahead? = 什么照亮了前方的道路？
What evidence illumines the suspect's guilt? = 什么证据证明了嫌疑人的罪行？

否定句:
The darkness was not illumined by any light source. = 黑暗没有任何光源照亮。
The explanation did not illumine the problem at all. = 这个解释根本没有阐明问题。
