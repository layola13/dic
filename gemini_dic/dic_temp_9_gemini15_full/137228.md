nonsurrealistically: /ˌnɒnsəˌriːəlɪˈstɪkli/ | adv. |非超现实地，非超现实主义地 |  Not in a surreal manner; in a way that is realistic or ordinary.  This word is formed by adding the negative prefix "non-" to "surrealistically," which itself is the adverbial form of "surrealistic."  | 例句：He described the event nonsurrealistically, focusing on the factual details rather than the bizarre aspects. 他以非超现实的方式描述了这一事件，侧重于事实细节而不是奇异的方面。


The painting was depicted nonsurrealistically, lacking the dreamlike quality of surrealist art. 这幅画的描绘是非超现实的，缺乏超现实主义艺术的梦幻般品质。 | 近义词：realistically, naturally, conventionally | 反义词：surrealistically, fantastically, bizarrely
