foully:/ˈfaʊli/| adv.| 卑鄙地；恶劣地；肮脏地 | In a foul or wicked manner; disgustingly; disgracefully. | 例句：He foully murdered his wife. 他卑鄙地谋杀了他的妻子。

例句：They played the game foully and were penalized. 他们比赛作弊，受到了处罚。


近义词：vilely, wickedly, basely, abominably, shamefully

反义词：honorably, fairly, justly, decently


词源:  From *foul* + *-ly*.  "Foul" itself has a long history, deriving from Old English *fūl*, meaning "dirty, muddy, unpleasant."
