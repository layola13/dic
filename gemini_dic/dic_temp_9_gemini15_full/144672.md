outwrote:/aʊtˈroʊt/| v.|胜过(写作)|To surpass or exceed in writing; to write more or better than.|例句：He outwrote his rival in the essay contest. 他在论文比赛中胜过了他的对手。|近义词：outshine, outdo, surpass|反义词：underwrite


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outwrites his colleagues in terms of productivity. = 他在写作效率方面胜过他的同事。
She outwrites most of her classmates in creative writing. = 她在创意写作方面胜过大多数同学。


一般过去时:
The young author outwrote the established writer in terms of originality. = 年轻的作家在原创性方面胜过了资深作家。
In the debate, she outwrote her opponent, presenting a more compelling argument. = 在辩论中，她以更令人信服的论点胜过了她的对手。


现在完成时:
She has outwritten all her previous works with her latest novel. = 她最新的这部小说已经超越了她以前的所有作品。
He has outwritten many established authors in the genre. = 他在该类型的小说中已经超越了许多著名的作家。


过去完成时:
By the time the competition ended, he had outwritten everyone else. = 当比赛结束时，他已经超过了其他所有人。
Before the deadline, she had outwritten her initial draft significantly. = 在截止日期之前，她已经大幅度地改进了她的初稿。


将来时:
I believe she will outwrite her competitor in the upcoming poetry slam. = 我相信她将在即将到来的诗歌朗诵比赛中胜过她的竞争对手。
He is confident that he will outwrite his rivals in the novel writing contest. = 他有信心在小说创作大赛中胜过他的对手。


虚拟语气:
If he had practiced more, he might have outwritten his opponent. = 如果他练习更多，他可能就胜过他的对手了。
If she had focused on her argument, she would have outwritten him easily. = 如果她专注于她的论点，她就会轻松地胜过他。


被动语态:
(Outwrote is rarely used passively.)  The established writer was outdone by the newcomer. = 老牌作家被新人超越了。(使用outdo的被动语态作代替)
His previous work was surpassed by his latest novel. = 他的前一部作品被他的最新小说超越了。(使用surpass的被动语态作代替)


疑问句:
Did he outwrite his opponent in the debate? = 他在辩论中胜过他的对手了吗？
Will she outwrite the other contestants in the writing competition? = 她会在写作比赛中胜过其他参赛者吗？


否定句:
He didn't outwrite her, but he still put up a good fight. = 他没有胜过她，但他仍然进行了顽强的斗争。
She didn't outwrite him in terms of sheer volume, but her work was more impactful. = 就数量而言，她没有胜过他，但她的作品更有影响力。



PHRASE->
outwrite someone in a competition = 在比赛中胜过某人写作
outwrite someone on a topic = 在某个话题上写得比某人好
outwrite someone's best work = 超过某人最好的作品
outwrite a rival = 超过竞争对手
outwrite the competition = 超过所有竞争者
