flour:/ˈflaʊər/| n. |面粉|A powdery substance made by grinding grain, especially wheat, used for making bread, cakes, and pastries.  Flour is a staple ingredient in many cuisines worldwide.  It's available in various types, each with different properties affecting the final product.| Example: The baker used all-purpose flour to make the bread.  烘焙师用通用面粉制作面包。|近义词：powder, meal|反义词：None
