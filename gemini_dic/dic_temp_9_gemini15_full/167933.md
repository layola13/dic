printer:/ˈprɪntər/| n. |打印机|A machine that prints text or images onto paper or other materials.|例句：I need to buy a new printer for my home office. 我需要为我的家庭办公室买一台新打印机。|近义词：printing machine, imaging device|反义词:None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The printer prints documents quickly. = 这台打印机打印文件很快。
He prints photos at home. = 他在家打印照片。


一般过去时:
The printer printed the report last night. = 打印机昨晚打印了报告。
She printed her essay before submitting it. = 她打印了论文后再提交。


现在完成时:
The printer has printed hundreds of pages. = 这台打印机已经打印了数百页。
I have printed all the necessary documents. = 我已经打印了所有必要的文档。


过去完成时:
The printer had already printed the documents before the meeting started. = 会议开始前，打印机已经打印好了文件。
She had printed the invitation before sending it out. = 她寄出邀请函之前已经打印好了。


将来时:
The printer will print the documents tomorrow. = 打印机明天将打印文件。
He will print the photos after he finishes editing them. = 他编辑完照片后会打印出来。


虚拟语气:
If the printer worked, I would print the report now. = 如果打印机能用，我现在就打印报告。
I wish the printer wasn't broken; I would have printed the documents already. = 我真希望打印机没坏，我已经把文件打印出来了。


被动语态:
The documents were printed by the printer. = 文件是由打印机打印的。
The photos are being printed right now. = 照片正在打印中。


疑问句:
Is the printer working? = 打印机能用吗？
Where is the printer located? = 打印机在哪里？


否定句:
The printer doesn't work. = 打印机坏了。
He didn't print the document. = 他没有打印文件。
