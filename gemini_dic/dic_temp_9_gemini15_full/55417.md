distillates:/ˈdɪstɪleɪts/| n.|蒸馏物；馏出物|The process of distilling a liquid, typically to purify it or separate it from other components.  The resulting purified liquid is called a distillate. Distillates are often used in various industries, including the production of alcoholic beverages, perfumes, and pharmaceuticals.|例句：The distillates from the fractional distillation column were analyzed for purity.  馏分塔的馏出物被分析纯度。|例句：The fragrant distillates were used to create a luxurious perfume.  芳香的馏出物被用来创造奢华的香水。|近义词：condensate, extract|反义词：residue, remainder|名词
