mayan:/ˈmaɪæn/| adj. n.|玛雅的；玛雅人|Relating to the Maya civilization of ancient Central America, its people, or its culture.  The word can refer to the language, the people, or the culture itself.|例句：The Mayan civilization created impressive pyramids and sophisticated calendar systems.  玛雅文明创造了令人印象深刻的金字塔和复杂的历法系统。|近义词：Mayan-related |反义词：None.
