preslicing: /ˈpriːˌslaɪsɪŋ/ | adj. | 预切片的；预先切片的 |  Describing something that has been sliced before it is used or sold, typically in a prepared or packaged form.  | Example: The supermarket offers a variety of presliced cheeses. 超市提供各种预切片奶酪。 | 近义词: pre-cut, pre-sliced, pre-chopped | 反义词: whole, unsliced
