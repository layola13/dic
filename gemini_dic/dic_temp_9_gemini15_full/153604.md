pentarchy:/ˈpentɑːki/| n. |五国统治；五权分立|A pentarchy is a form of government consisting of five rulers or powers.  The term is most often associated with the five patriarchates of the early Christian Church.  |Example: The early Church's pentarchy was a complex system of power sharing amongst the five major patriarchates.  早期教会的五国统治是五个主要宗主教区之间复杂的权力分享体系。|近义词：quinquarchy, five-part rule|反义词：monarchy, dictatorship|
