productionize:/prəˈdʌkʃənaɪz/ | vt. | 使产品化；使商业化；将…投入生产 | To prepare (something, such as a software application or a process) for production or commercial use. This involves adapting it for a production environment, ensuring scalability, reliability, and security.  | Example:  The company is working to productionize its new AI model.  公司正在努力将其新的AI模型投入生产。

The team is focused on productionizing the prototype. 团队专注于将原型产品化。|近义词：commercialize, industrialize, implement | 反义词：de-productionize (not a common word, but implies the opposite process)
