slams:/slæmz/| vt. n.|猛击；砰地关上；猛烈抨击|to shut (a door, window, etc.) with a loud bang; to hit or strike with force; to criticize severely.|例句：He slammed the door in anger. 他生气地砰地关上了门。 He slammed his fist on the table. 他用力地把拳头砸在桌子上。|近义词：bang, crash, bash, criticize, condemn|反义词：gently close, praise


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slams the door every time he leaves. = 他每次离开都砰地关上门。
She slams her books on the desk. = 她把书重重地摔在桌子上。


一般过去时:
He slammed the door shut. = 他砰地关上门。
The critics slammed his new movie. = 批评家们猛烈抨击了他的新电影。


现在完成时:
He has slammed the door several times today. = 他今天已经砰地关了几次门了。
The media has slammed the government's response. = 媒体猛烈抨击了政府的回应。


过去完成时:
He had slammed the door before I could say anything. = 我还没来得及说什么，他就砰地关上门了。
The newspaper had slammed the politician's behavior before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，报纸就抨击了这位政治家的行为。


将来时:
He will slam the door if you annoy him. = 如果你惹恼了他，他会砰地关上门。
The opposition party will slam the new policy. = 反对党将会猛烈抨击这项新政策。


虚拟语气:
If he slammed the door, I would be very angry. = 如果他砰地关上门，我会非常生气。
I wish he hadn't slammed the door like that. = 我真希望他不要那样砰地关上门。


被动语态:
The door was slammed shut. = 门被砰地关上了。
His arguments were slammed by the opposition. = 他的论点遭到反对派的猛烈抨击。


疑问句:
Did he slam the door? = 他砰地关上门了吗？
Will they slam the new proposal? = 他们会猛烈抨击新的提案吗？


否定句:
He didn't slam the door. = 他没有砰地关上门。
The review didn't slam the performance. = 这篇评论并没有抨击这次演出。
