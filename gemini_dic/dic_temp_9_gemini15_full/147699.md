overpreciseness:/ˌoʊvərprɪˈsaɪsnəs/| n. |过分精确；过于精确；吹毛求疵|Excessive attention to detail;  the quality of being too precise. It often implies an unnecessary or counterproductive level of detail.  This can lead to inefficiency and a lack of flexibility.|The overpreciseness of his instructions made it difficult for the team to adapt to changing circumstances.  他的指示过于精确，使得团队难以适应不断变化的情况。|近义词：perfectionism, meticulousness, pedantry|反义词：vagueness, imprecision, sloppiness
