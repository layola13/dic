shown:/ʃoʊn/ | /ʃəʊn/ | v.过去分词 | 显示的；展示的；被展示的 | Shown is the past participle of the verb "show".  It describes something that has been displayed, exhibited, or demonstrated. It can also mean something that has been proven or made clear. | 例句：The results shown in the graph are quite surprising. 图表中显示的结果相当令人惊讶。

The evidence shown in court proved his innocence. 法庭上展示的证据证明了他的清白。


近义词：displayed, exhibited, demonstrated, revealed, presented | 反义词：hidden, concealed, obscured | 词性：形容词（过去分词作形容词）


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This is shown in the experiment. = 这在实验中显示出来了。
The data shown supports our hypothesis. = 显示的数据支持我们的假设。

一般过去时:
The results were shown to the audience. = 结果被展示给了观众。
The painting was shown at the gallery. = 这幅画在画廊展出。

现在完成时:
The results have been shown to be accurate. = 结果已被证明是准确的。
The effects have been shown in many studies. = 这些影响已经在许多研究中被证明。

过去完成时:
The evidence had been shown before the trial began. = 在审判开始之前，证据已经被展示过了。
It had been shown that the theory was flawed. =  已经证明了该理论存在缺陷。

将来时:
The findings will be shown at the conference. = 这些发现将在会议上展示。
The movie will be shown next week. = 电影下周上映。

虚拟语气:
If the data were shown to be incorrect, we would revise our approach. = 如果数据被证明是错误的，我们将修改我们的方法。
If the evidence had been shown earlier, the outcome might have been different. = 如果证据早点被展示出来，结果可能会有所不同。

被动语态:
The problem is shown to be complex. = 问题被证明是复杂的。
The movie is shown every night. = 这部电影每晚都上映。

疑问句:
Is the data shown accurately? = 显示的数据准确吗？
Was the film shown at the festival? = 这部电影在电影节上展出过吗？

否定句:
The error was not shown in the initial analysis. = 初始分析中没有显示出错误。
The results were not shown conclusively. = 结果没有得到确凿的证明。
