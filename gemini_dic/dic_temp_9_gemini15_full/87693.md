hexametrical:/ˌhɛksəˈmɛtrɪkəl/| adj.|六音步的；六步格的 | Relating to or consisting of hexameter verse.  Hexametrical verse is a type of meter in poetry, characterized by lines containing six metrical feet. | Example:  The poem is written in hexametrical verse, giving it a grand and flowing rhythm.  这首诗是用六步格写的，赋予它宏伟流畅的节奏。|近义词：dactylic (if referring to the type of foot), six-foot |反义词：monometrical, dimetrical
