musicalization:/ˌmjuːzɪkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |音乐化；使具有音乐性|The process of adapting or creating something with musical elements, or the result of such a process.  This can refer to adapting a story, play, or other work into a musical, adding music to a film or video game, or simply enhancing the musicality of a piece of writing.| Example: The musicalization of the novel was a huge success. 这部小说的音乐化改编非常成功。The musicalization of the scene added depth and emotion.  这个场景的音乐化处理增加了深度和情感。|近义词：adaptation (if referring to adapting a work), scoring (if referring to adding music to a visual medium)|反义词：de-musicalization (removing musical elements)
