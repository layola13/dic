It emphasizes the repeated nature of this personality trait.|例句：His irasciblenesses were legendary, leading to many strained relationships.  他的易怒是出了名的，导致许多关系紧张。|近义词：irritabilities, fits of anger, choleric tendencies |反义词：calmness, placidity, equanimity
