risky:/ˈrɪski/| adj.|危险的，冒险的|Involving or exposing to the chance of injury or loss.  Something that is risky has a high probability of resulting in an undesirable outcome.|例句：Investing in the stock market can be risky.  投资股市可能很冒险。

例句：Climbing Mount Everest is a risky undertaking. 攀登珠穆朗玛峰是一项冒险的事业。


近义词：dangerous, hazardous, perilous, precarious|反义词：safe, secure, harmless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The investment is risky. = 这项投资很冒险。
That activity seems risky to me. = 那项活动在我看来很冒险。


一般过去时:
The decision was risky. = 那个决定很冒险。
