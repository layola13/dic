shuttle:/ˈʃʌtəl/| n., v.|航天飞机；穿梭；往返运送；迅速移动|A vehicle or device that transports people or things between two places repeatedly; to move quickly back and forth.  |例句：The space shuttle returned safely to Earth.  航天飞机安全返回地球。
The children shuttled between their grandparents' houses. 孩子们在祖父母家之间来回奔波。|近义词：ferry, transit,convey, dart|反义词：stay, remain


USAGE->

The shuttle runs every hour. = 航天飞机每小时运行一次。
He shuttles between his office and home. = 他往返于办公室和家之间。


一般过去时:
