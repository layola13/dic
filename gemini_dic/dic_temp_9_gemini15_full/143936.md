outpushes: /ˌaʊtˈpʊʃɪz/ | v. | 向外推；驱逐；超越 |  To push outwards; to force or drive out; to surpass or exceed. |

例句：

The expanding gas outpushes the piston. =膨胀的气体推动活塞。

The stronger team outpushed their opponents in the final game. = 更强的球队在决赛中超越了对手。


近义词：expels, pushes out, surpasses, exceeds

反义词：pulls in, attracts, yields to


词性: 动词
