mercat:/ˈmɜːrkət/| n. |集市，市场 |A mercat is a marketplace, especially one that is open-air and often held regularly.  The word is of Catalan origin and is still used in some regions.|例句：The town square was transformed into a bustling mercat every Saturday.  这个镇广场每个星期六都会变成一个热闹的集市。|近义词：market, marketplace, bazaar |反义词：none
