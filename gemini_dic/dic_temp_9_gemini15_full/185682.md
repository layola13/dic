reversals:/rɪˈvɜːrsəlz/ | n. | 逆转；反转；颠倒 | The act of reversing something or the state of being reversed.  It can refer to a change to the opposite state or direction, or a change in fortune or circumstances.  |例句：The company experienced several reversals of fortune before finally succeeding. 公司在最终成功之前经历了几次命运的逆转。

例句：The sudden reversals in the weather caused widespread disruption. 天气突变导致了广泛的混乱。


近义词：inversions, overturnings, turnarounds, reversals of fortune


反义词：continuations, progressions, advancements


词性：名词
