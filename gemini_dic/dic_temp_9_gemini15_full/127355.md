NCO:/ˌɛnˈsiːoʊ/|/ˌɛnˈsiːəʊ/| n. |士官，军士|Non-commissioned officer; a military officer who holds a rank below commissioned officer.  NCOs typically lead and train enlisted personnel and are responsible for many aspects of unit administration and operations.| Example: The sergeant, a highly respected NCO, led his platoon with courage and skill.  这位军士长，一位备受尊敬的士官，勇敢而熟练地带领他的排。|近义词：sergeant, corporal, staff sergeant|反义词：commissioned officer
