overpolitic: /ˌoʊvərˈpɑːlətɪk/ | adj. | 过于圆滑的；过于世故的；矫揉造作的 | Excessively polite or diplomatic; excessively mindful of political considerations.  Showing excessive concern with political correctness or social etiquette, often to the point of artificiality or insincerity. |


例句：

His overpolitic response avoided the real issues at hand.  他的回答过于圆滑，避开了真正的问题。

Her overpolitic demeanor felt contrived and unnatural. 她的过于世故的举止显得做作而不自然。


近义词：

insincere, artificial, affected, diplomatic (in a negative sense), mannered, calculating


反义词：

genuine, sincere, straightforward, frank, candid, blunt


词性：形容词
