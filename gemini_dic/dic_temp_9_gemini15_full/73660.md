forged:/fɔːdʒd/| v. |伪造；锻造；锤炼；形成 |To shape metal by heating and hammering; to make or create something, often dishonestly; to create something strong or enduring.|例句：The blacksmith forged a strong sword.  铁匠锻造了一把坚固的剑。
He forged a signature on the check. 他伪造了支票上的签名。|近义词：fabricate, counterfeit, shape, mold |反义词：destroy, dismantle
