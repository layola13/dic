overinflate:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfleɪt/ | vt. |过度充气；夸大；(经济)过度膨胀 | To inflate beyond the normal or recommended level.  This can refer to physical objects like balloons or tires, or to abstract concepts like prices or expectations. |

例句：

The company overinflated its earnings to attract investors. = 公司夸大其收益以吸引投资者。

Don't overinflate the tires; it's dangerous. = 不要过度充气轮胎；这很危险。


近义词：exaggerate, inflate excessively, overestimate

反义词：underinflate, deflate
