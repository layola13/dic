murdering:/ˈmɜːdə(r)ɪŋ/| v.| 谋杀；残杀；杀害 |The act of killing a person unlawfully and intentionally.  It implies a deliberate and malicious act. |例句：He was accused of murdering his wife. 他被指控谋杀了他的妻子。

例句：The serial killer was found guilty of murdering five young women. 连环杀手被判犯有谋杀五名年轻女性的罪行。


近义词：slaughtering, assassinating, killing


反义词：saving, rescuing, protecting
