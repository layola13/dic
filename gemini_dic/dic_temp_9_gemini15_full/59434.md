earthquakes:/ˈɜːθkweɪks/| n. |地震|A sudden and violent shaking of the ground, typically causing great destruction, as a result of movements within the earth's crust or volcanic action.|例句：The earthquakes caused widespread damage to buildings and infrastructure.  地震对建筑物和基础设施造成了广泛的破坏。
例句：Many people lost their homes and lives in the recent earthquakes.  最近的地震导致许多人失去家园和生命。|近义词：tremors, quakes, seismic activity|反义词：calm, stillness
