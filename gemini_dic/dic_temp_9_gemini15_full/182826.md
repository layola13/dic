repeater:/rɪˈpiːtə(r)/| n. |中继器；复读机；重复者 |A repeater is a device that receives a signal and retransmits it at a higher level or to a greater distance.  It is used to extend the range of a signal, such as in telecommunications or networking.  A repeater can also refer to someone who repeats something, like a student repeating a lesson. |例句：The network uses repeaters to boost the Wi-Fi signal throughout the building.  这个网络使用中继器来增强整个建筑物的Wi-Fi信号。

The student acted as a repeater for the teacher's instructions.这个学生充当了老师指示的复述者。|近义词：relay, amplifier, reiterator |反义词：originator, initiator
