handshaker:/ˈhændˌʃeɪkər/| n.| 握手的人；(尤指)频繁握手的人 | A person who shakes hands, especially one who does so frequently.  | Example: He's a notorious handshaker, always greeting everyone with a firm handshake. 他是个出了名的爱握手的人，总是热情地和每个人握手。 |近义词：greeter, welcomer |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who avoids physical contact.
