middlings:/ˈmɪdlɪŋz/| n.| 中等产品；粗面粉；次等品 |The coarser, less valuable parts of grain remaining after the finer flour has been removed.  Middlings are often used as animal feed or in the production of less refined flour. |例句：The middlings were separated from the fine flour.  = 粗面粉与细面粉分离了。


例句：The farmer used the middlings to feed his pigs. = 农民用粗面粉喂他的猪。


近义词：offal, refuse, by-products


反义词：fine flour, premium product


词性:名词
