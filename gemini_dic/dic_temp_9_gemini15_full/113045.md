luminousnesses:/ˌluːmɪˈnʌsɪsɪz/| n. |发光度；明亮度|The plural form of "luminosity," referring to the amount or intensity of light emitted by a source.  It describes the brightness or radiance of something.  The study compared the luminousnesses of different stars.|近义词：brightnesses, intensities, radiances|反义词：darknesses, dullnesses
