pseudo-legal:/ˈsuːdoʊˈliːɡəl/ | adj. |似是而非的法律的；伪装成法律的 |  Something that resembles legal language or processes but lacks true legal standing or validity. It often uses legal terminology to create a false impression of legitimacy.  | Example: The contract contained several pseudo-legal clauses designed to confuse the average person.  这个合同包含一些旨在迷惑普通人的似是而非的法律条款。|近义词：quasi-legal, sham legal, specious legal | 反义词：legitimate, valid, genuine |
