prealluded:/ˌpriːəˈluːdəd/| v. |预先提及，暗示 |To mention or hint at something beforehand.  The word is formed by combining "pre-" (before) and "alluded" (referred indirectly).| 例句：He prealluded to the difficulties we might face in the project. 他预先提到了我们在项目中可能面临的困难。

例句：The speaker prealluded to the upcoming changes in company policy. 演讲者预先暗示了公司政策即将发生的变化。


近义词：foreshadow, hint at, suggest, imply


反义词：explicitly state, clearly explain


词性:动词
