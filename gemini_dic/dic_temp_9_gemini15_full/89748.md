hopping-mad: /ˈhɒpɪŋ mæd/ | adj. | 非常生气，怒不可遏 | Extremely angry; furious.  |  He was hopping mad when he found out his car had been scratched. 他发现他的车被刮花了，气得跳脚。 |近义词：furious, enraged, livid, incandescent with rage | 反义词：calm, serene, placid


USAGE->

She is hopping mad about the injustice. = 她对这种不公正非常愤怒。
