romanced:/rəˈmænst/| vt. n. adj.|使浪漫化；浪漫史；浪漫的|To make something seem more romantic than it actually is; a romantic story or relationship; characterized by or suggestive of romance.|例句：He romanced the story of their meeting, making it sound far more dramatic than it was. 他美化了他们相遇的故事，使其听起来比实际情况更戏剧化。  The novel is a sweeping romance set in the 1920s. 这部小说是一部以20年代为背景的波澜壮阔的浪漫史。|近义词：idealize, romanticize, fictionalize|反义词：realistic, matter-of-fact, mundane|动词，名词，形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He romances the past. = 他美化过去。
She romances her life story. = 她美化自己的人生故事。


一般过去时:
They romanced the details of their adventure. = 他们美化了他们冒险的细节。
He romanced the truth to impress her. = 他为了给她留下好印象而美化了真相。


现在完成时:
She has romanced her experiences in Africa. = 她已经美化了她非洲的经历。
He has romanced the history of his family. = 他已经美化了他家族的历史。


过去完成时:
They had romanced the events leading up to the war. = 他们已经美化了导致战争发生的事件。
She had romanced her childhood memories. = 她已经美化了她童年的记忆。


将来时:
He will romance the story for the next generation. = 他将为下一代美化这个故事。
They will romance their travels to create a captivating tale. = 他们将美化他们的旅行经历，创造一个引人入胜的故事。


虚拟语气:
If he romanced the story more, it would be more appealing. = 如果他更美化这个故事，它会更吸引人。
I wish she hadn't romanced the truth so much. = 我真希望她不要那么美化真相。


被动语态:
The events were romanced to create a more exciting narrative. = 事件被美化以创造更令人兴奋的叙述。
The history of the town has been romanced over the years. = 多年来，这个城镇的历史一直被美化。


疑问句:
Did he romance the facts? = 他美化了事实吗？
Does she romance her past relationships? = 她美化她过去的恋情吗？


否定句:
He didn't romance the details; he told it as it was. = 他没有美化细节；他如实地说了。
She doesn't romance her life; she's very down-to-earth. = 她不美化她的生活；她非常务实。
