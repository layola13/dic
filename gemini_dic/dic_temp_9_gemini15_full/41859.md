cos's:/kɒzɪz/| pron.| 他的；她的；它的 |  This is a contraction of "of his," "of hers," or "of its."  It shows possession.  | Example: That's cos's book. =那是他的书。|近义词: his, hers, its |反义词:  None


USAGE->

That is cos's car. =那是他的车。
This is cos's responsibility. =这是他的责任。

将来时:

PHRASE->
There is no common phrase specifically using "cos's" as a standalone unit.  The possessive form is incorporated into phrases naturally.  Examples would be: "cos's house," "cos's job,"  "cos's opinion," etc.
