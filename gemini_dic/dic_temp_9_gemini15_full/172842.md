quadruped:/ˈkwɑːdruped/| n.|四足动物|A quadruped is an animal that has four feet.  The term generally refers to mammals, reptiles, and amphibians that walk on four legs, although some invertebrates may also be considered quadrupeds based on their locomotion. |例句：Horses, dogs, and cats are all quadrupeds. 马、狗和猫都是四足动物。

例句：Elephants are large quadrupeds known for their intelligence.  大象是四足动物，以其智力而闻名。


近义词：four-footed animal, tetrapod (more scientific)|反义词：biped (two-legged animal) |词性:noun
