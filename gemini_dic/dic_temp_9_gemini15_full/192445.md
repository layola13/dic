scrubbier:/ˈskrʌbiər/| adj.| 更加粗糙的；更加肮脏的；更不整洁的 | More rough, dirty, or untidy.  It describes something that is less clean or well-maintained than something else.  | 例句：The scrubbier parts of the city were filled with litter.  = 城市中比较脏乱的部分堆满了垃圾。 The scrubbier side streets were poorly lit. = 比较脏乱的小巷灯光昏暗。|近义词：shabbier, dirtier, messier, rougher |反义词：cleaner, tidier, neater
