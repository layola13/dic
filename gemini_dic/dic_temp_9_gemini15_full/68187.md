faintness:/ˈfeɪntnəs/| n. |虚弱；晕厥；微弱 |A feeling of weakness or dizziness, often preceding fainting.  It can also refer to something barely perceptible; lacking strength or intensity. |例句：The heat caused a sudden faintness. 炎热导致一阵晕眩。

例句：The faintness of the moonlight made it difficult to see. 月光的微弱使得难以视物。

近义词：weakness, dizziness, lightheadedness, swoon

反义词：strength, vigor, robustness
