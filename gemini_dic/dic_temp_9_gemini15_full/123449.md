monopteral: /mɒnˈɒptərəl/ | adj. | 单柱式庙宇的；只有一排柱子的 |  Relating to or denoting a building, especially a temple, with a circular colonnade of columns supporting a dome or conical roof.  A monopteral structure is characterized by its single circular row of columns surrounding a central space, often open to the sky.  |例句：The ancient temple was a beautiful example of monopteral architecture.  这座古老的寺庙是单柱式建筑的完美典范。


The design of the garden included a charming monopteral gazebo. 花园的设计包括一个迷人的单柱凉亭。


近义词：circular, rotunda (referring to the structure as a whole) | 反义词：polystylar (having many columns), peristyle (having a colonnade surrounding a courtyard)


词源:  From Greek *monos ("single") + pteron ("wing, column").  The term originally referred to temples with a single row of columns, reflecting the wings of a bird.
