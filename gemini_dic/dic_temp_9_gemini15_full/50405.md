depositor:/dɪˈpɒzɪtə(r)/|/dɪˈpɑːzɪtər/| n. |存款人，寄存者 |A depositor is a person or entity that places money in a bank account or other financial institution.  |Example: The bank contacted the depositor to verify the large withdrawal.  银行联系了存款人以核实大额取款。

The depositor requested a statement of their account balance. 存款人要求提供其账户余额的报表。|近义词：payer, contributor, investor |反义词：borrower, debtor
