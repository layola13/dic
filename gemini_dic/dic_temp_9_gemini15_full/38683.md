condensate:/ˈkɒndɛnseɪt/ /ˈkɑːndənˌseɪt/| n.|冷凝物；浓缩物|The process by which a gas changes to a liquid.  The liquid formed by this process.  Also refers to something that has been made more concentrated. |例句：Water vapor in the air condensed into dew on the grass. 空气中的水蒸气凝结成草上的露水。
The condensate from the distillation process was collected. 蒸馏过程中的冷凝物被收集起来。|近义词：condensation, extract |反义词：vapor, gas
