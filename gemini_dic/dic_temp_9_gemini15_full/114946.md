manana:/mɑːˈnɑːnɑː/|/ˈmænænɑː/| n. |明天；改天|  A Spanish word meaning "tomorrow."  It is often used informally to indicate a postponement or delay.  |  We'll do it mañana. 我们明天做吧。|  tomorrow, later | today, now |名词
