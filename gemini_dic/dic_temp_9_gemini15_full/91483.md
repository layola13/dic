hymenium:/haɪˈmiːniəm/| n. | 子实层 | The hymenium is the fertile layer of a fungus, typically located on the underside of a mushroom cap or on the surface of other fungal structures. It contains the spore-producing structures (basidia or asci).  |Example: The hymenium of this mushroom is covered in tiny gills. 这个蘑菇的子实层布满了细小的菌褶。|近义词：fertile layer, spore-bearing surface |反义词：sterile layer
