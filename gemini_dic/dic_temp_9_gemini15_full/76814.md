gamosepalous:/ˌɡæmoʊˈsɛpələs/| adj. | 合生的；合萼的 | Having sepals united to form a tube or cup.  This term is used in botany to describe flowers where the sepals (the outermost whorl of floral parts) are fused together, rather than being separate and distinct. | Example: The gamosepalous calyx of the flower was a striking feature.  这朵花的合生萼片是其显著特征。|近义词：synsepalous |反义词：chorisepalous
