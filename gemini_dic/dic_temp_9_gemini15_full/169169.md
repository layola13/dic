proletariat: /ˌproʊləˈtɛəriət/ | n. |无产阶级，劳工阶级 | The proletariat, as defined by Marx, is the working class who lack ownership of the means of production and must sell their labor to survive.  |例句：The proletariat rose up in revolution against the bourgeoisie.  无产阶级起来反抗资产阶级。  |近义词：working class, laborers, commoners |反义词：bourgeoisie, aristocracy, elite
