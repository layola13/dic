fortable: /ˈkʌmftəbl/ | adj. | 舒适的，舒服的 |  Feeling physical ease and relaxation; free from discomfort.  |例句：The chair is comfortable to sit in. 这把椅子坐起来很舒服。|例句：I feel comfortable in my own home. 我在自己家里感觉很舒服。|近义词：cozy, restful, pleasant |反义词：uncomfortable, uneasy,  disagreeable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chair feels comfortable. = 这把椅子感觉很舒服。
The room is comfortable. = 这个房间很舒适。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
