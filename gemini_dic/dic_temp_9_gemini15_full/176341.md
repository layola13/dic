re-evaluate:/ˌriːɪˈvæljuˌeɪt/| vt.|重新评估；重新评价|To evaluate something again, often with more information or a different perspective.  It implies a reconsideration of a previous judgment or assessment.|例句：We need to re-evaluate the project's budget in light of the new data.  我们需要根据新的数据重新评估项目的预算。|例句：The company decided to re-evaluate its marketing strategy after a poor sales quarter. 公司在销售业绩不佳的一个季度后决定重新评估其营销策略。|近义词： reassess, reconsider, reappraise |反义词： accept, confirm


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-evaluate their strategies every year. = 他们每年都会重新评估他们的策略。
We re-evaluate the risks associated with this project. = 我们重新评估与该项目相关的风险。


一般过去时:
The committee re-evaluated the proposals. = 委员会重新评估了这些提案。
She re-evaluated her career path after the layoff. = 在裁员后，她重新评估了自己的职业道路。


现在完成时:
They have re-evaluated the situation several times. = 他们已经多次重新评估了这种情况。
The company has re-evaluated its pricing model. = 公司已经重新评估了其定价模式。


过去完成时:
They had re-evaluated the evidence before reaching a verdict. = 他们在做出判决之前已经重新评估了证据。
The manager had re-evaluated the employee's performance prior to the meeting. = 经理在会议之前已经重新评估了员工的表现。


将来时:
We will re-evaluate the contract next week. = 我们下周将重新评估合同。
They will re-evaluate the plan after the initial trial. = 他们在初步试验之后将重新评估计划。


虚拟语气:
If they had more information, they would re-evaluate their decision. = 如果他们有更多信息，他们就会重新评估他们的决定。
I wish I had re-evaluated my options before accepting the job. = 我希望在接受这份工作之前，我已经重新评估了自己的选择。


被动语态:
The project's budget will be re-evaluated next month. = 项目预算将在下个月重新评估。
The marketing strategy was re-evaluated after the campaign. = 活动结束后，营销策略被重新评估。


疑问句:
Will you re-evaluate the proposal based on the new information? = 你会根据新的信息重新评估提案吗？
Have they re-evaluated the risks involved? = 他们是否已经重新评估了所涉及的风险？


否定句:
They did not re-evaluate their assumptions. = 他们没有重新评估他们的假设。
The company will not re-evaluate the policy at this time. = 公司目前不会重新评估这项政策。
