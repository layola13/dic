orchards:/ˈɔːrtʃərdz/| n. |果园|An area of land where fruit trees are grown.|例句：The orchards were filled with the sweet scent of ripening apples.  果园里弥漫着成熟苹果的甜香。|近义词：grove, plantation|反义词:None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The orchard produces a large amount of apples every year. = 这个果园每年生产大量的苹果。
We visit the local orchards in autumn. = 我们秋天去当地的果园。


一般过去时:
They picked apples in the orchard last week. = 他们上周在果园里摘苹果。
The old orchard was abandoned many years ago. =  那个老果园很多年前就被废弃了。


现在完成时:
We have visited that orchard many times. = 我们去过那个果园很多次了。
They have already harvested all the cherries from the orchard. = 他们已经从果园里收割了所有的樱桃。


过去完成时:
They had already picked all the apples before the storm arrived. =  暴风雨来临之前，他们已经摘完了所有的苹果。
We had never seen such a beautiful orchard before. = 我们以前从未见过如此美丽的果园。


将来时:
We will visit the orchard next weekend. = 我们下周末将去果园。
They will plant new trees in the orchard next spring. = 他们明年春天将在果园里种新树。


虚拟语气:
If we had more land, we would plant a large orchard. = 如果我们有更多的土地，我们会种一个大果园。
I wish I could own an orchard one day. = 我希望有一天能拥有一个果园。


被动语态:
The orchard is managed by a local farmer. = 果园由当地一位农民管理。
The apples were harvested by hand. = 苹果是手工采摘的。


疑问句:
Is this orchard open to the public? = 这个果园对公众开放吗？
Where is the nearest orchard? = 最近的果园在哪里？


否定句:
The orchard doesn't produce enough fruit this year. = 果园今年的水果产量不足。
They didn't pick any apples from the orchard yesterday. = 他们昨天没有从果园里摘苹果。
