okapi:/oʊˈkɑːpi/| n. |欧卡皮|A rare, shy, and elusive mammal native to the northeast Democratic Republic of the Congo, known for its distinctive zebra-like stripes on its hindquarters and long neck.|Example: The okapi is a fascinating creature that resembles both a giraffe and a zebra.  欧卡皮是一种迷人的动物，它既像长颈鹿又像斑马。|近义词:giraffe (in some respects)|反义词:None|名词
