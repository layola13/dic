north-western:/ˌnɔːrθˈwɛstən/| adj.|西北的，西北方的|Describing something situated in or relating to the northwest.  It can refer to a direction, a region, or a wind.|例句：The wind was blowing from the north-western direction.  西北风正在吹拂。|例句：They live in the north-western part of the country. 他们住在该国西北部。|近义词：northwesterly|反义词：south-eastern, south-westerly, north-easterly
