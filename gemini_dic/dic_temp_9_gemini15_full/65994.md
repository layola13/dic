exam:/ɪɡˈzæm/| n. |考试，测验| a formal test of knowledge or ability |例句：The exam was very difficult.  考试非常难。|例句：I have an exam tomorrow. 我明天有个考试。|近义词：test, examination, quiz |反义词:None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The exam starts at 9 am. = 考试九点开始。
He takes the exam every year. = 他每年都参加考试。

一般过去时:
I passed the exam. = 我通过了考试。
She failed the exam last week. = 她上周考试不及格。

现在完成时:
I have finished the exam. = 我已经完成了考试。
She has taken many exams this semester. = 这个学期她参加了很多考试。

过去完成时:
I had already finished the exam when he arrived. = 他到达时我已经完成了考试。
They had studied hard before the exam. = 考试前他们努力学习了。

将来时:
I will take the exam next week. = 我下周将参加考试。
The exam will be held in the auditorium. = 考试将在礼堂举行。

虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I would have passed the exam. = 如果我学习更努力，我就通过考试了。
I wish I had studied more for the exam. = 我真希望我为考试多复习一些。

被动语态:
The exam is graded by the professor. = 考试由教授批改。
The exam was postponed due to the storm. = 考试因暴风雨而推迟。

疑问句:
When is the exam? = 考试是什么时候？
What subjects are on the exam? = 考试考哪些科目？

否定句:
I didn't study for the exam. = 我没有为考试学习。
The exam wasn't easy. = 考试并不容易。
