hydraulical:/haɪˈdrɔːlɪkəl/| adj.|水力的；液压的|Relating to or operated by the pressure of water or other liquid.|The dam uses a hydraulic system to generate electricity. 这座水坝利用液压系统发电。|近义词：hydrolic|反义词：manual, mechanical
