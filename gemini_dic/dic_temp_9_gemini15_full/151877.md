passives:/ˈpæsɪvz/| n.|被动语态|Passive voice is a grammatical construction where the subject of the sentence receives the action of the verb.  It is often used when the actor is unknown, unimportant, or obvious from the context.  It is formed using a form of the verb "to be" + past participle.|The book was written by Jane Austen. (这本书是简·奥斯汀写的。)|active voice, passive construction|active voice


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car is washed every week. = 这辆车每周都洗一次。
The problem is discussed frequently. =这个问题经常被讨论。

一般过去时:
The letter was sent yesterday. = 信昨天寄出去了。
The food was cooked by my mother. = 这食物是我妈妈做的。

现在完成时:
The work has been completed. = 工作已经完成了。
The report has been submitted. = 报告已经提交了。

过去完成时:
The decision had been made before the meeting. = 会议之前决定已经做出了。
The house had been painted before they moved in. = 他们搬进去之前房子已经粉刷过了。

将来时:
The project will be finished next month. = 项目下个月将完成。
The meeting will be held tomorrow. = 会议将在明天举行。

虚拟语气:
It would be better if the problem were solved quickly. = 如果这个问题能迅速解决就好了。
If the rules were followed, there would be fewer accidents. = 如果遵守规则，事故就会减少。

被动语态:
The damage was caused by the storm. = 损害是由暴风雨造成的。
The movie is being shown at the cinema. = 电影正在电影院上映。

疑问句:
Was the letter received? = 信收到了吗？
Is the meeting being recorded? = 会议正在录音吗？

否定句:
The task was not completed on time. = 任务没有按时完成。
The message was not understood. = 信息没有被理解。


PHRASE->
be passive about = 对…采取被动态度
remain passive = 保持被动
become passive = 变得被动
passive resistance = 被动抵抗
passive voice = 被动语态
