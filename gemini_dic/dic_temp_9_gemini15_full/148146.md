oversensitized: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度敏感的，反应过度的 |  Excessively sensitive; reacting too strongly to things.  |  He's become oversensitized to criticism after years of harsh feedback. 他多年来受到严厉的批评，变得对批评过度敏感了。 |近义词：hypersensitive, overly sensitive, touchy |反义词：insensitive, thick-skinned, unfeeling
