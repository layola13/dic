词源:

The word originates from the verb "degrade," which comes from the Old French "degrader" (to lower in rank), ultimately derived from the Latin "de-" (down, from) + "gradus" (step, degree).  The suffix "-ness" transforms the adjective "degrading" into a noun, signifying the quality or state of being degrading.
