larvae:/ˈlɑːrviː/ | n. | 幼虫；幼体 | The immature, usually wormlike, form of an insect or other invertebrate animal, before it becomes an adult.  Larvae typically differ significantly in appearance and habits from the adult form.  They are a stage in the life cycle of many animals undergoing metamorphosis. | Example: The butterfly larvae fed voraciously on the leaves. 蝴蝶幼虫贪婪地吃着树叶。

The mosquito larvae wriggled in the stagnant water. 蚊子幼虫在死水中蠕动。|近义词：grub, maggot, nymph (depending on the specific insect)|反义词：imago (adult insect)
