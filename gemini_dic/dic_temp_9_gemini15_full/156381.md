physiotherapist: /ˌfɪzioʊˈθerəpɪst/ | n. | 物理治疗师 | A healthcare professional who helps patients recover from injuries, illnesses, or disabilities through physical methods.  Physiotherapists use exercise, manual therapy, and other techniques to improve movement, reduce pain, and restore function. | Example: My physiotherapist helped me regain my mobility after my knee surgery.  我的物理治疗师帮助我在膝盖手术后恢复了行动能力。|近义词：physical therapist, rehabilitation therapist | 反义词：无
