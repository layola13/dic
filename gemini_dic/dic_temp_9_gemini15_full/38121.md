comprehends:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛndz/| /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛndz/| vt.|理解，包含，包括|To understand something completely; to include something within something else.|例句：I don't comprehend his behaviour. 我不明白他的行为。|例句：The course comprehends a wide range of topics. 这门课程涵盖了广泛的主题。|近义词：understand, grasp, encompass, include|反义词：misunderstand, misconstrue, exclude


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He comprehends the instructions perfectly. = 他完全理解了这些指示。
She comprehends the complexities of the situation. = 她理解了局势的复杂性。

一般过去时:
I comprehended the meaning of the poem after reading it several times. = 读了几遍后，我理解了这首诗的意思。
They comprehended the gravity of the situation only after the accident. = 只有事故发生后，他们才理解了情况的严重性。

现在完成时:
I have comprehended the main points of the lecture. = 我已经理解了讲座的主要内容。
She has comprehended the significance of the discovery. = 她已经理解了这一发现的重要性。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
