onesie:/ˈwʌnzi/| n. |连体衣，连身衣 |A one-piece garment worn as underwear or outerwear, typically by babies and young children. It often has a flap for easy diaper changing.|例句：My baby looks adorable in his new onesie. 我的宝宝穿着他的新连体衣看起来很可爱。

例句：She wore a fleece onesie to stay warm. 她穿着一件羊毛绒连体衣保暖。


近义词：jumpsuit, romper, playsuit |反义词：separate garments (e.g., shirt and pants)


词源:The word "onesie" is a blend of "one" and "piece," referring to its single-piece design.  It emerged in the latter half of the 20th century as a common term for baby clothing.  The term's popularity has increased significantly as it's become a common clothing item for adults as well.
