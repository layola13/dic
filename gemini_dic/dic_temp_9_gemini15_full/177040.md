reannexations:/ˌriːæˌnekˈseɪʃənz/ | n. |重新兼并；重新并吞 |The act of reannexing something, especially territory.  It implies a previous annexation followed by a period of separation, and then a subsequent re-acquisition.  |例句：The reannexations following the war led to significant border disputes.  战争后的重新兼并导致了重大的边界争端。

The historical reannexations of Alsace-Lorraine caused much tension between France and Germany.  阿尔萨斯-洛林的历史性重新兼并导致法国和德国之间紧张局势加剧。|近义词：reoccupation, reconquest, reassumption |反义词：cession, relinquishment
