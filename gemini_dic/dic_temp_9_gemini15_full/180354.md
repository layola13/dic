refunding:/rɪˈfʌndɪŋ/| v. |退款；偿还|The act of returning money that has been paid for goods or services that were not satisfactory or were not delivered.  It involves giving back the original payment to the customer. |例句：The company is processing my refunding request. 公司正在处理我的退款请求。  The store offered a full refunding for the defective product. 商店为有缺陷的产品提供了全额退款。|近义词：reimbursement, repayment|反义词：charging, billing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company is refunding the customer's money. = 公司正在退款给顾客。
They are refunding the purchase price. = 他们正在退还购买价格。

一般过去时:
The store refunded my money yesterday. = 商店昨天把我的钱退给了我。
They refunded the full amount. = 他们退还了全款。

现在完成时:
I have already been refunded. = 我已经被退款了。
They have refunded several customers this week. = 本周他们已经为几位顾客进行了退款。

过去完成时:
They had already refunded the payment before I contacted them. = 在我联系他们之前，他们已经退款了。
The customer had been refunded the previous day. = 顾客前一天已经收到了退款。

将来时:
The company will refund your money within a week. = 公司将在一个星期内退还你的钱。
We will be refunding all eligible customers. = 我们将为所有符合条件的顾客退款。

虚拟语气:
If they refunded my money, I would be happy. = 如果他们退还我的钱，我会很高兴。
I wish they would refund the extra charges. = 我希望他们能退还额外的费用。

被动语态:
The money was refunded to the customer. = 钱退还给了顾客。
The refund was processed quickly. = 退款很快就被处理了。

疑问句:
Will they be refunding the shipping costs? = 他们会退还运费吗？
Have you been refunded yet? = 你已经退款了吗？

否定句:
The company did not refund my money. = 公司没有退还我的钱。
They refused to refund the purchase. = 他们拒绝退款。
