murderer:/ˈmɜːrdərər/ | n. | 凶手，杀人犯 | A murderer is a person who has committed murder.  Murderers are often prosecuted and punished by law. | 例句：The police are searching for the murderer.  警察正在搜寻凶手。

The murderer confessed to the crime.  凶手承认了罪行。


近义词：killer, manslayer, assassin | 反义词：victim


词源:  The word "murderer" comes from the Old French word "murderer," which ultimately derives from the Old Norman French "murdrer," meaning "to commit murder."  The term "murder" itself has roots in Old French "murdrer" and ultimately, Old Norse "morðr," meaning "murder," "killing," or "death."
