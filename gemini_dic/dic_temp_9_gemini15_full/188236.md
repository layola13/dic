royalist: /ˈrɔɪəlɪst/ | n. |保皇派，忠君者 | A person who supports a monarchy or a monarch.  They believe in the system of government by a king or queen and often actively advocate for the rights and power of the monarch. |  Many royalists gathered to celebrate the Queen's birthday.  许多保皇派聚集在一起庆祝女王的生日。The country is deeply divided between royalists and republicans. 这个国家在保皇派和共和派之间严重分裂。| republican, monarchist | republican, revolutionary | 名词
