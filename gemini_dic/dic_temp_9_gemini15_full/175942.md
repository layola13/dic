rationalizer:/ˈræʃənəlaɪzər/| n. |合理化者；为…找借口的人|A person who rationalizes; someone who finds seemingly reasonable explanations for their actions or beliefs, often to avoid confronting uncomfortable truths or feelings.  |He's a skilled rationalizer, always finding excuses for his mistakes. 他是一个熟练的找借口的人，总是为他的错误找借口。|apologist, justifier, explainer |critic, condemner


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rationalizer justifies his actions. = 合理化者为他的行为辩护。
She rationalizes her bad habits. = 她为自己的坏习惯找借口。

一般过去时:
He rationalized his decision after the fact. = 他过后才为他的决定找借口。
They rationalized their poor performance. = 他们为他们糟糕的表现找借口。

现在完成时:
She has rationalized her fears away. = 她已经把恐惧合理化了。
He has rationalized his behavior to his boss. = 他已经向老板解释了他的行为。

过去完成时:
He had rationalized his actions before admitting the truth. = 他在承认真相之前已经为自己的行为找了借口。
They had rationalized their mistakes throughout the project. = 他们在整个项目中一直在为他们的错误找借口。

将来时:
She will rationalize her choices later. = 她以后会为自己的选择找借口。
He will rationalize his spending habits. = 他将为他的消费习惯找借口。

虚拟语气:
If he were honest, he wouldn't rationalize his behavior. = 如果他诚实的话，他就不会为自己的行为找借口。
I wish he wouldn't rationalize his mistakes. = 我希望他不为自己的错误找借口。

被动语态:
His actions were rationalized by him. = 他的行为被他合理化了。
The mistakes were rationalized by the team leader. = 错误被团队领导合理化了。

疑问句:

否定句:
