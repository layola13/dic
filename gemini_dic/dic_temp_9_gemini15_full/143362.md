outfamed:/ˌaʊtˈfeɪmd/| v. |胜过…的名声；比…更有名|To surpass in fame; to exceed in reputation.|His achievements outfamed his predecessors. 他的成就胜过了他的前辈。|surpassed, excelled, overshadowed|unknown, obscure|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outfames all other artists in the city. 他胜过城里所有其他艺术家。
This novel outfames all her previous works. 这部小说比她以前的所有作品都更有名。


一般过去时:
Her performance outfamed all expectations. 她的演出超出了所有人的预期。
The invention outfamed the inventor himself. 这项发明比发明者本人更有名。


现在完成时:
His work has outfamed him. 他的作品比他本人更有名。
This painting has outfamed all others in the gallery. 这幅画在画廊里比其他所有画都更有名。


过去完成时:
By that time, his reputation had outfamed him. 到那时，他的名声已经超过了他本人。
The discovery had outfamed its initial impact.  这一发现的影响已经超过了最初的影响。


将来时:
This new technology will outfame all existing systems. 这项新技术将胜过所有现有的系统。
His upcoming book is predicted to outfame his previous bestsellers. 他的新书预计将比他之前的畅销书更受欢迎。


虚拟语气:
If only his talent had been recognized earlier, he would have outfamed all his contemporaries. 如果他的天赋早些被认可，他就会比他同时代的所有人都更有名。
I wish my writing could outfame my brother's. 我希望我的写作能够比我哥哥的更有名。


被动语态:
He was outfamed by his younger brother. 他被他的弟弟比下去了。
The old tradition was outfamed by modern trends.  古老的传统被现代潮流所取代。


疑问句:
Does his work truly outfame his contemporaries? 他的作品真的比他同时代的人更有名吗？
Will this new movie outfame its predecessors? 这部新电影会比之前的电影更有名吗？


否定句:
His early work did not outfame his later masterpieces. 他的早期作品不如他后来的杰作出名。
The play did not outfame the actors' performances. 这部戏剧不如演员的表演出名。
