shitake:/ʃɪˈtɑːki/| n. |香菇|A type of edible mushroom, known for its distinctive umami flavor and meaty texture.  It is widely cultivated and used in Asian cuisine.|例句：The stir-fry was delicious, thanks to the addition of shitake mushrooms. 这道炒菜很好吃，多亏加了香菇。|例句：I love the earthy flavor of shitake mushrooms in my ramen. 我喜欢拉面里香菇的泥土味。|近义词：mushroom, fungus|反义词:None.
