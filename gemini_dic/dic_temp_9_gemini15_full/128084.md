nervously:/ˈnɜːvəsli/| adj.|紧张地，不安地| feeling or showing nervousness.  The adverb form of "nervous." It describes actions or states characterized by anxiety, apprehension, or unease.|He nervously adjusted his tie before the interview. 他在面试前紧张地整理了一下领带。She nervously tapped her fingers on the table. 她紧张地用手指敲打着桌子。|anxiously, uneasily, worriedly|calmly, confidently, assuredly|形容词
