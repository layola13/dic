ginsengs:/ˈdʒɪnˌsɛŋz/| n. |人参（复数）|The plural form of ginseng, referring to multiple ginseng plants or roots. Ginseng is a genus of slow-growing perennial plants with fleshy roots, valued for their medicinal properties.|例句：The farmer cultivates several varieties of ginsengs on his land.  这位农民在他的土地上种植了几个品种的人参。|例句：These ginsengs are known for their high levels of ginsenosides. 这些参以其高含量的皂苷而闻名。|近义词： ginseng root, panax|反义词：无
