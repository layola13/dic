consider:/kənˈsɪdər/ | vt.  | 考虑；认为；体谅 | To think carefully about something, typically before making a decision.  | I need to consider all the options before I decide.  我需要考虑所有选择后再做决定。 | contemplate, ponder, contemplate, weigh | ignore, disregard | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I consider this a great opportunity. = 我认为这是一个绝佳的机会。
They consider the proposal carefully. = 他们仔细考虑了这个提案。


一般过去时:
She considered her options before accepting the job. = 她接受工作前考虑了她的选择。
We considered all the factors before making a decision. = 我们在做决定之前考虑了所有因素。


现在完成时:
He has considered many factors before reaching a conclusion. = 他在得出结论之前已经考虑了许多因素。
We have considered your request and will respond soon. = 我们已经考虑了你的请求，很快就会回复。


过去完成时:
They had considered several plans before settling on this one. = 他们在决定这个计划之前已经考虑过几个计划了。
I had considered quitting my job, but I decided against it. = 我曾考虑过辞职，但我最终放弃了。


将来时:
I will consider your offer carefully. = 我会仔细考虑你的提议。
She will consider the consequences before acting. = 她会在采取行动之前考虑后果。


虚拟语气:
I would consider moving if I had the money. = 如果我有钱的话，我会考虑搬家。
If I were you, I would consider his advice. = 如果我是你，我会考虑他的建议。


被动语态:
The proposal was considered by the committee. = 这个提案被委员会考虑了。
Your application will be considered shortly. = 你的申请很快就会被考虑。


疑问句:
Did you consider the risks involved? = 你考虑过其中涉及的风险吗？
Have you considered all the possibilities? = 你考虑过所有可能性了吗？


否定句:
I didn't consider the implications of my actions. = 我没有考虑我行为的含义。
They didn't consider his feelings. = 他们没有考虑他的感受。


PHRASE->
take into consideration = 考虑到
give consideration to = 认真考虑
under consideration = 正在考虑中
out of consideration for = 出于对…的考虑
for your consideration = 供你考虑


词源:
consider 源于古法语 *considerer，来自拉丁语 *considerare，由 con-（一起）和 sidus, sideris（星星）构成。最初的意思是“观察星星”，后来引申为“仔细观察”、“思考”。
