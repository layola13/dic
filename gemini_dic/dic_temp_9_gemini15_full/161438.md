postzygapophysis:/ˌpōstˌzaɪɡæˈpɒfɪsɪs/| n. |后关节突|A bony process projecting backward from the posterior side of a vertebra, articulating with the prezygapophysis of the vertebra below.  It forms part of the zygapophyseal joint which provides stability to the vertebral column.|The postzygapophysis of the third lumbar vertebra was fractured.|近义词：posterior articular process|反义词：prezygapophysis
