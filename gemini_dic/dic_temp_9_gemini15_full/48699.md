defibrillated:/dɪˈfɪbrɪleɪtɪd/| adj. v.过去式及过去分词|除颤的；已除颤的|  The past tense and past participle of defibrillate.  It describes a state after a defibrillator has been used.|The patient was defibrillated after suffering a cardiac arrest.  病人心脏骤停后接受了除颤。|近义词：shocked (in the context of electric shock), revived|反义词：None specifically, but  words implying continued cardiac arrest could be considered antonyms.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor defibrillates the patient. = 医生给病人除颤。
They defibrillate the heart to restore its rhythm. = 他们对心脏进行除颤以恢复其节奏。


一般过去时:
The paramedics defibrillated the victim. = 急救人员对受害者进行了除颤。
He was defibrillated twice before his heart started beating again. = 他的心脏在再次跳动之前接受了两次除颤。


现在完成时:
The patient has been defibrillated, and his condition is improving. = 病人已经接受了除颤，他的病情正在好转。
Medical staff have defibrillated several patients today. = 医疗人员今天已经为几名病人进行了除颤。


过去完成时:
The doctors had defibrillated him several times before he finally responded. = 医生在他最终恢复反应之前已经对他进行了多次除颤。
Before the ambulance arrived, they had already defibrillated the patient twice. = 在救护车到达之前，他们已经对病人进行了两次除颤。


将来时:
The doctor will defibrillate the patient if necessary. = 必要时，医生将对病人进行除颤。
They will defibrillate him again if his heart stops. = 如果他的心脏停止跳动，他们将再次对他进行除颤。


虚拟语气:
If the defibrillator had been used sooner, the patient might have survived. = 如果除颤器早一点使用，病人可能就活下来了。
If they had defibrillated him immediately, the outcome could have been different. = 如果他们立即对他进行除颤，结果可能会有所不同。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
