nonascetics:/ˌnɒnəˈsɛtɪks/| n. |非禁欲主义者|People who do not practice asceticism; those who indulge in worldly pleasures and comforts.  They reject the ascetic lifestyle of self-denial and austerity. |Example:  In a world increasingly focused on material possessions, nonascetics are becoming the majority.  在一个越来越关注物质财富的世界里，非禁欲主义者正成为多数。|近义词: hedonists, pleasure-seekers, sensualists |反义词: ascetics, hermits, saints
