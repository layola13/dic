domiciles:/ˈdɒmɪsaɪlz/| n.|住所，住宅|A domicile is a person's permanent residence; their home.|例句：The family finally found their dream domicile in the countryside.  这个家庭最终在乡下找到了他们的梦想之家。|例句：His official domicile is listed as London. 他的官方住所登记在伦敦。|近义词：residence, dwelling, abode, home|反义词：None directly opposite, but perhaps "nomadic lifestyle" could be considered a contrasting concept.
