retract:/rɪˈtrækt/| vt.  |收回；撤回；缩回；(使)缩进|to pull or draw back; to withdraw or take back (a statement, promise, etc.)|例句：The company retracted its earlier statement. 公司撤回了之前的声明。|例句：The cat retracted its claws. 猫收回了爪子。|近义词：withdraw, revoke, rescind|反义词：extend, protrude, assert

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retracts his hand quickly. = 他迅速地缩回了手。
The company retracts its offer. = 公司撤回了它的报价。

一般过去时:
She retracted her accusation. = 她撤回了她的指控。
The scientist retracted his theory. = 科学家撤回了他的理论。


现在完成时:
They have retracted the article. = 他们已经撤回了那篇文章。
The government has retracted its policy. = 政府已经撤回了它的政策。


过去完成时:
He had retracted his statement before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经撤回了他的声明。
The newspaper had retracted the false story. = 报纸已经撤回了那个虚假报道。


将来时:
They will retract the faulty product. = 他们将会收回有瑕疵的产品。
The editor will retract the offensive article. = 编辑将会撤回那篇冒犯性的文章。


虚拟语气:
If he knew the truth, he would retract his claim. = 如果他知道真相，他会收回他的说法。
I wish I had retracted my words earlier. = 我真希望我早点收回我的话。


被动语态:
The statement was retracted by the spokesperson. = 该声明由发言人收回。
The article was retracted due to inaccuracies. = 文章因不准确而被撤回。


疑问句:
Will they retract their support? = 他们会收回他们的支持吗？
Has the company retracted the defective product? = 公司是否已经收回有缺陷的产品？


否定句:
He did not retract his statement despite the evidence. = 尽管有证据，他也没有收回他的声明。
The newspaper refused to retract the article. = 报纸拒绝撤回那篇文章。


PHRASE->
retract a statement = 收回声明
retract one's words = 收回前言
retract an offer = 撤回提议
retract one's claim = 收回主张
retract a promise = 撤回承诺
