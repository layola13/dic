herdess:/ˈhɜːrdɛs/| n. |牧羊女，牧羊人（女子）|A herdess is a woman who tends to a herd of animals, typically sheep or goats.  She is responsible for their care, including feeding, sheltering, and protecting them.|The herdess led her flock to the green pasture.  牧羊女带领她的羊群到绿色的牧场上。|Shepherdess, shepherd (female)|None|名词
