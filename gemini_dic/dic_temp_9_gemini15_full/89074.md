holostomatous: /ˌhɒləˈstoʊmə təs/ | adj. | 全口的；具全口的 | Having a complete mouth; referring to organisms with a complete, unbroken oral aperture.  The term is primarily used in zoology, particularly in the classification of certain invertebrates.  It contrasts with the condition of having an incomplete or partially divided mouth. |

例句：

The holostomatous condition of this gastropod is a key characteristic for its identification. =  这种腹足动物的全口特征是其鉴定的关键特征。

Some species exhibit a holostomatous larval stage before developing a more specialized mouth structure. =  某些物种在发育出更 specialized 的口部结构之前，会呈现全口的幼虫阶段。


近义词： complete-mouthed (informal)

反义词： schizostomatous (having a split or divided mouth)


词性: 形容词
