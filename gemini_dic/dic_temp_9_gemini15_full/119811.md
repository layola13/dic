mike's:/maɪks/| n. |迈克的；迈克所有物|Mike's is a possessive form indicating ownership or belonging to someone named Mike.  It functions as a noun phrase and can be used as a subject, object, or possessive in a sentence.|例句：This is Mike's car. 这是迈克的车。|例句：Mike's house is very big. 迈克的房子很大。|近义词：Mike's belonging, the property of Mike|反义词：无
