coca: /ˈkoʊkə/ | n. |古柯；古柯叶|

英文解析:Coca is a shrub native to the Andes region of South America.  Its leaves contain cocaine, a powerful stimulant.  The plant has a long history of use by indigenous cultures for both medicinal and ritual purposes.  However, the isolation and use of cocaine from coca leaves has led to significant social and health problems.
