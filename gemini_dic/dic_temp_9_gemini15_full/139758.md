octa:/ˈɒktə/ /ˈɑːktə/| prefix | 八；八倍的 |  A combining form meaning “eight,” used in the formation of compound words.  |  An octagon has eight sides. 八角形有八条边。 | octo-, octa- |  una-, mono- | prefix


USAGE->
This is a prefix, not a standalone word, so it does not have verb conjugations or other grammatical features like "what".  It is used to form compound words.  Examples are provided below.


PHRASE->
octagon: 八角形
octave: 八度音阶
October: 十月
octopod: 八足动物
octosyllable: 八音节词
