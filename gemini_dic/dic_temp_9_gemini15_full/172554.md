pyrogenic:/paɪrəˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj.|热原性的；发热性的 |Originating from or produced by heat.  Relating to or caused by pyrogens. |例句：The pyrogenic reaction caused a high fever. 热原反应导致高烧。

例句：The pyrogenic substances in the injection caused a severe inflammatory response. 注射物中的热原物质引起严重的炎症反应。


近义词：fever-inducing, febrile


反义词：afebrile


词性：形容词
