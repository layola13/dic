/ˌriːˈteɪstɪŋ/| v.|再次品尝，重新品味|The act of tasting something again, often to confirm a previous impression or to experience it anew.  It implies a more deliberate and considered tasting than a simple second bite.|例句：After letting the wine breathe, I enjoyed retasting it.  再次醒酒后，我享受了再次品尝它的乐趣。

例句：He retastes the soup, adjusting the seasoning. 他再次品尝汤，调整调味。


近义词：retaste, re-sample|反义词：first taste, initial tasting|动词
