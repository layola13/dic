huts:/hʌts/| n.|小屋，棚屋|A hut is a small, simple building, often made of wood, mud, or other readily available materials.  It is typically used as a dwelling, especially a temporary one.|例句：The villagers lived in simple huts. 村民们住在简陋的小屋里。|例句：The hikers found shelter in a small hut on the mountain. 登山者在山上一间小屋里找到了避难所。|近义词：cabin, shack, shed|反义词：mansion, palace|名词
