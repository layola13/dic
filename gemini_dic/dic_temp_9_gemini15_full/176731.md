read-out:/ˈriːdˌaʊt/| n. v.|读出，朗读；读数，读出值；输出|A read-out is the act of reading something aloud or the information that is read aloud. It can also refer to a visual display of data.|例句：The computer gave a read-out of the experiment's results. 电脑输出了实验结果。The teacher conducted a read-out of the students' essays. 老师朗读了学生们的文章。|近义词：reading, output, display|反义词：input, recording


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine reads out the temperature every minute. = 机器每分钟读出温度。
He reads out the names of the winners. = 他大声读出获奖者的名字。

一般过去时:
The speaker read out a prepared statement. = 发言人宣读了一份准备好的声明。
She read out the poem beautifully. = 她优美地朗读了这首诗。

现在完成时:
The scientist has read out the data from the experiment. = 科学家已经读出了实验数据。
They have read out all the names on the list. = 他们已经读出了名单上的所有名字。

过去完成时:
He had read out the instructions before starting the task. = 他在开始任务之前已经读出了说明。
She had read out the letter to her family before she died. = 在她去世之前，她已经把信读给了她的家人。

虚拟语气:
