nugget:/ˈnʌɡɪt/| n. |金块；块；少量；精华|A nugget is a small, irregular lump of a precious metal, especially gold, found naturally.  It can also refer to a small, often valuable piece of something, or a small amount of something good. |例句：He found a gold nugget worth thousands of dollars. 他发现了一块价值数千美元的金块。
He shared a nugget of wisdom with me. 他与我分享了一条智慧箴言。|近义词：lump, piece, bit, morsel|反义词：abundance, mass, vast quantity|名词
