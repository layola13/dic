meliaceous:/ˌmɪliˈeɪʃəs/| adj. |楝树科的；楝树属的|Belonging to or characteristic of the Meliaceae, a family of tropical trees and shrubs including mahogany and neem.  The term often describes the characteristics of plants within this family, such as their leaves, fruits, or flowers.|The meliaceous tree yielded valuable timber.  楝树产出珍贵的木材。|近义词：mahogany-like (in reference to wood)|反义词：None specifically, but words describing plants from other families could be considered antonyms in a broad sense.
