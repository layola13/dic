sibilantly:/ˈsɪbɪləntli/| adv.|嘶嘶地，发出嘶嘶声地 |  Describes a sound or action made with a hissing sound, like the sound of a snake or escaping air.  |例句：The snake hissed sibilantly.  这条蛇嘶嘶地叫着。


The wind whistled sibilantly through the trees. 风在树林中嘶嘶地吹着。|近义词：hissing, whispering |反义词：loudly, booming
