phallocentric:/ˌfæləʊˈsɛntrɪk/| adj. |以男性为中心的；阳具中心的 | Centered on or dominated by men or the male perspective; relating to a worldview that prioritizes the phallus (penis) as a symbol of power and dominance.  |The phallocentric nature of the society is evident in its laws and customs. 这个社会的以男性为中心的本质在其法律和习俗中显而易见。|近义词：male-dominated, androcentric, misogynistic |反义词：feminocentric, gynocentric
