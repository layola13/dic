sledged:/ˈslɛdʒd/ | v. |用雪橇运送；猛烈抨击 | to transport something or someone on a sledge; to attack someone verbally in a harsh or aggressive way. | 例句：They sledged the supplies across the snowy mountain. 他们用雪橇把物资运过雪山。 They sledged him mercilessly for his mistakes. 他们无情地猛烈抨击他的错误。 | 近义词：hauled, transported, criticized, attacked | 反义词：praised, commended
