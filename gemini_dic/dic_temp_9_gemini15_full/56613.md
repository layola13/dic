done-for:/dʌnˈfɔːr/| adj.| 完蛋的；不可救药的；失败的 |  Describes something or someone that is ruined, hopeless, or completely unsuccessful. It implies a state of irreversible decline or failure. |例句：After the scandal, his career was done for. 丑闻过后，他的事业彻底完蛋了。
例句：The company is done for; it's drowning in debt. 公司完了，它深陷债务之中。|近义词：ruined, finished, doomed, hopeless, irreversible|反义词：successful, thriving, flourishing, recoverable
