metamorphize:/mɛtəˈmɔːrfəˌzaɪz/ | v. | 变形；使变形；使蜕变 | To change or cause to change in form or nature, especially gradually and completely.  |  The caterpillar metamorphized into a beautiful butterfly. 毛毛虫蜕变成美丽的蝴蝶。  The company has undergone a complete metamorphize in recent years. 近年来，这家公司发生了翻天覆地的变化。 | transform, transmute, change | remain the same, stay constant | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The caterpillar metamorphizes into a butterfly. 毛毛虫变成蝴蝶。
The city is constantly metamorphosing. 城市在不断变化。

一般过去时:
The frog metamorphosed from a tadpole. 青蛙由蝌蚪变来。
The artist metamorphosed the clay into a sculpture. 艺术家将粘土变成了雕塑。

现在完成时:
The larva has metamorphosed into a pupa. 幼虫已蜕变成蛹。
The town has completely metamorphosed since I last visited. 自从我上次来访以来，这个小镇已经完全变了样。

过去完成时:
The rock had metamorphosed over millions of years. 这块岩石经过数百万年的变质。
The caterpillar had metamorphosed before I could observe it. 我还没来得及观察，毛毛虫就已经蜕变了。

将来时:
The pupa will metamorphose into a moth. 蛹将变成飞蛾。
The project will completely metamorphose the company's operations. 这个项目将彻底改变公司的运营方式。

虚拟语气:
If the conditions were right, the insect would metamorphose quickly. 如果条件合适，昆虫会快速蜕变。
I wish the city would metamorphose into a more sustainable place. 我希望这座城市能变成一个更可持续发展的地方。

被动语态:
The rock was metamorphosed by intense heat and pressure. 岩石在高温高压下发生变质。
The building is being metamorphosed into a modern art gallery. 这座建筑正在改造成现代美术馆。

疑问句:

否定句:

The company hasn't metamorphosed enough to compete effectively. 这家公司还没有发生足够的改变以有效地竞争。
