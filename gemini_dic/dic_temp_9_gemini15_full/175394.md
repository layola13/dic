ramadan:/ˌræməˈdɑːn/|/ˈræmədæn/| n. |斋月 |Ramadan is the ninth month of the Islamic lunar calendar, observed by Muslims worldwide as a month of fasting, prayer, reflection, and community.  It is a time for spiritual renewal and increased devotion to Allah. |Example: During Ramadan, Muslims abstain from food and drink from dawn until sunset.  斋月期间，穆斯林从日出到日落禁食饮水。|近义词: none |反义词: none
