inamorata:/ˌɪnæmɔˈrɑːtə/| n. |情妇；恋人 |A woman who is passionately loved; a sweetheart or mistress.  The term often implies a romantic relationship, but not necessarily a legally married one.  It carries a connotation of passionate love and devotion. |Example: He dedicated his latest poem to his inamorata. 他把他最新的诗献给了他的情人。|近义词：lover, sweetheart, mistress|反义词：enemy, rival
