otolith:/ˈoʊtəlaɪt/| n. |耳石；平衡石|A small calcareous concretion in the inner ear of many vertebrates, especially fishes, that functions in balance and orientation.  Otoliths are used in age and growth studies of fish. |例句：The otoliths of the fish provided valuable data on its age and growth.  这条鱼的耳石为其年龄和生长提供了宝贵的数据。

例句：Scientists examined the otoliths to determine the fish's migration patterns. 科学家检查了耳石以确定鱼类的迁徙模式。


近义词：statolith (less common)|反义词：无|词性:名词
