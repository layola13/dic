misgendering:/mɪsˈdʒɛndəriŋ/| v.|错误地称呼性别|The act of referring to someone using a gender that does not align with their gender identity.  This can be unintentional or deliberate, and is often considered disrespectful and hurtful.|Example:  He was upset by the constant misgendering he experienced at work. 他对在工作中不断被错误称呼性别感到沮丧。|近义词：gendering incorrectly, wrongly identifying gender, mislabeling gender |反义词：gendering correctly, correctly identifying gender
