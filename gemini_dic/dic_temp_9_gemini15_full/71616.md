flatcar:/ˈflætˌkɑːr/|/ˈflætˌkɑːr/| n. |平板车，平车 |A flatcar is a railroad car without sides or a top, used for carrying heavy or bulky goods.  |例句：The heavy machinery was transported on a flatcar.  重型机械是用平板车运输的。|例句：The flatcar was loaded with steel beams. 这辆平板车装载着钢梁。|近义词：flat wagon, platform car |反义词：boxcar, covered wagon
