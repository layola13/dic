magnetify:/ˈmæɡnɪfaɪ/| vt.|放大，增强|To make something larger or more powerful.  It can refer to physical size or to the intensity of something.|例句：The microscope magnifies the image of the cell. 显微镜放大了细胞的图像。
例句：The incident magnified existing tensions between the two countries.  该事件加剧了两国之间的紧张关系。|近义词：amplify, enlarge, intensify, strengthen|反义词：diminish, reduce, weaken|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lens magnifies the object. = 透镜放大物体。
He magnifies the problem unnecessarily. = 他不必要地夸大了问题。


一般过去时:
The telescope magnified the distant stars. = 望远镜放大了遥远的星星。
She magnified the importance of her role in the project. = 她夸大了她在项目中的作用的重要性。


现在完成时:
The media has magnified the controversy. = 媒体夸大了这场争议。
They have magnified the risks associated with the investment. = 他们夸大了与这项投资相关的风险。


过去完成时:
The rumors had magnified the situation before the truth emerged. = 在真相浮出水面之前，谣言夸大了事态。
He had magnified the difficulties, making the task seem impossible. = 他夸大了困难，使任务看起来不可能完成。


将来时:
The new software will magnify the capabilities of our computers. = 新软件将增强我们电脑的功能。
This decision will magnify the existing inequalities. = 这个决定将加剧现有的不平等。


虚拟语气:
If we used a stronger lens, we would magnify the image more. = 如果我们使用更强的镜头，我们将能更多地放大图像。
If he hadn't magnified the problem, the solution would have been easier. = 如果他没有夸大这个问题，解决方案就会更容易。


被动语态:
The image was magnified by the lens. = 图像是由镜头放大的。
The problem was magnified by the media. = 这个问题被媒体夸大了。


疑问句:
Does this lens magnify the image sufficiently? = 这个镜头能充分放大图像吗？
How much did the incident magnify the existing tensions? = 该事件在多大程度上加剧了现有的紧张局势？


否定句:
The camera does not magnify the object enough. = 相机没有足够地放大物体。
He did not magnify the difficulties, but rather underestimated them. = 他没有夸大困难，而是低估了它们。
