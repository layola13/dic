reconstructions:/ˌriːkənˈstrʌkʃənz/| n. |重建，复原；(对过去的)重新诠释|The process of rebuilding or restoring something that has been damaged or destroyed, or of creating a new understanding or interpretation of something from the past.  It often involves piecing together fragments of information or evidence to create a coherent whole.|例句：The city is undergoing major reconstructions after the earthquake. 地震后，这座城市正在进行大规模的重建。

The historian's reconstructions of the battle were based on limited evidence. 这位历史学家对这场战役的重建是基于有限的证据。

近义词：rebuildings, restorations, reinterpretations, recreations |反义词：demolitions, destructions
