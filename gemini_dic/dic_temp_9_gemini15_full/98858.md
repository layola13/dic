intemperate:/ɪnˈtɛmpərɪt/ | adj. | 不节制的；过度的；放纵的 |  Lacking moderation; excessive.  Characterized by lack of self-control; immoderate. |  例句：His intemperate drinking led to health problems.  他的酗酒导致了健康问题。

例句：Her intemperate remarks offended many people. 她不加节制的发言冒犯了许多人。


近义词：immoderate, excessive, unrestrained, uncontrolled | 反义词：moderate, temperate, restrained, controlled | 词性：adj.


USAGE->

She is intemperate with her criticism. = 她批评过于尖刻。
