cloners:/ˈkloʊnərz/ | n. |克隆者，复制者 | People or things that create clones.  Those who engage in the act of cloning, whether of genes, organisms, or data. | Example: The scientists were skilled cloners, able to replicate genetic material with high precision.  科学家们是熟练的克隆者，能够高精度地复制遗传物质。


Example: The  police investigated the illegal activities of the data cloners. 警方调查了数据克隆者的非法活动。 |近义词：replicators, duplicators |反义词：originators (in the sense of creating something original) |名词
