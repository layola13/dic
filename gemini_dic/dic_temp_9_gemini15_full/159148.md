podcasted:/ˈpɑːdkæstɪd/ | v. |播客化；制作成播客 | To create and distribute an audio or video program as a podcast.  |Example:  He podcasted his weekly radio show. 他把他的每周广播节目制作成了播客。


They podcasted their interviews with local artists. 他们把对当地艺术家的采访制作成了播客。


近义词：recorded, broadcast, streamed |反义词：None  |词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He podcasts his cooking show every week. 他每周都会制作他的烹饪节目播客。
She podcasts interviews with interesting people. 她制作采访有趣人物的播客。

一般过去时:
I podcasted my first episode last month. 我上个月制作了我的第一集播客。
They podcasted a live concert from the venue. 他们从演出场地现场直播制作了播客。

现在完成时:
He has podcasted over 100 episodes of his show. 他已经制作了他节目的超过100集播客。
She has podcasted regularly since 2020. 自2020年以来，她定期制作播客。

过去完成时:
Before launching his podcast, he had podcasted several test episodes. 在推出他的播客之前，他已经制作了几集测试播客。
They had podcasted a few episodes before they gained a significant following. 在他们获得大量追随者之前，他们已经制作了几集播客。

将来时:
I will podcast my interview with the author next week. 我下周将制作我对该作家的采访播客。
She will podcast a special episode for the holiday season. 她将在节日期间制作一集特别节目播客。

虚拟语气:
If I had better equipment, I would podcast more frequently. 如果我有更好的设备，我会更频繁地制作播客。
I wish I had started podcasting sooner. 我真希望我早点开始制作播客。

被动语态:
The interview was podcasted and released online.  这次采访被制作成播客并在网上发布。
Many podcasts are podcasted using inexpensive equipment. 许多播客都是使用廉价设备制作的。

疑问句:
Have you ever podcasted before? 你以前制作过播客吗？
Does he podcast regularly? 他定期制作播客吗？

否定句:
I haven't podcasted anything new lately. 我最近没有制作任何新的播客。
She doesn't podcast as often as she used to. 她不像以前那样经常制作播客了。


PHRASE->
podcast an interview: 制作采访播客
podcast a show: 制作节目播客
podcast live: 现场直播制作播客
podcast regularly: 定期制作播客
podcast using (software/equipment): 使用（软件/设备）制作播客
