rudish:/ˈruːdɪʃ/ | adj. | 粗鲁的；无礼的；粗野的 | Rude or ill-mannered; lacking in refinement or grace.  The word suggests a slightly less severe form of rudeness than "rude" itself, implying a tendency towards rudeness rather than consistently rude behavior. |例句：His rudish behavior at the party embarrassed his mother. 他在派对上的粗鲁行为让他母亲很尴尬。

例句：She apologized for her rudish comment. 她为她粗鲁的言论道歉。


近义词：unrefined, ill-mannered, impolite, discourteous, boorish, churlish


反义词：polite, courteous, refined, well-mannered, gracious
