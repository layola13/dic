entertainment:/ˌɛntəˈteɪnmənt/ | n. |娱乐；消遣 | The act of entertaining; amusement, diversion, or recreation.  It can refer to activities that provide enjoyment, pleasure, or excitement.  This can include various forms such as movies, music, theatre, sports, and social events. | Example: The circus provided hours of entertainment for the children. 马戏团为孩子们提供了数小时的娱乐。

Example:  We need some entertainment for the party. 我们需要一些派对娱乐活动。|近义词：amusement, recreation, diversion |反义词：boredom, tedium
