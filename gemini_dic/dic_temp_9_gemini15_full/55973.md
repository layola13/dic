dm:/diːˈɛm/ | n. |直接信息, 私信 | A direct message, typically sent through a social media platform or online messaging service.  | Example: I sent him a dm about the meeting. 我给他发了条私信关于会议的事。|近义词：private message, personal message |反义词：public post, open message |名词


USAGE->

I send a dm every day. = 我每天都发私信。
He receives many dms. = 他收到很多私信。

She sent a dm yesterday. = 她昨天发了一条私信。
