mongrelize:/ˈmɒŋɡrəlaɪz/ | vt. |使杂交；使混杂；使劣质化 | To cause (a breed of animal, a language, etc.) to degenerate by mixing with inferior elements; to spoil by mixing with something inferior. |例句：The introduction of foreign words mongrelized the local dialect. 引入外来词使当地方言混杂。

The indiscriminate breeding of dogs mongrelized the purebred lines.  不加选择的狗繁殖使纯种血统混杂。


近义词：bastardize, hybridize, debase, corrupt

反义词：purify, refine, improve
