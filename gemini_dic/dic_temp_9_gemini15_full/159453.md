polestar:/ˈpoʊlstɑːr/| n. |北极星；指导原则；目标 | A polestar is a star that appears nearly stationary in the sky and serves as a guide for navigation.  It can also refer to a guiding principle or a goal. | Example: The North Star is a polestar for sailors. 北极星是水手们的导航星。  | Example:  Her unwavering commitment to justice served as a polestar in her life. 她对正义的坚定承诺是她生命中的指导原则。 |近义词：guiding star, lodestar, beacon, objective |反义词：misguidance, distraction
