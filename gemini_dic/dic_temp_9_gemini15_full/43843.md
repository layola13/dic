crept:/krɛpt/| v.|潜行；悄悄地移动|The past tense of "creep".  It describes moving slowly and quietly, often in a stealthy or sneaky manner.  |例句：The cat crept silently through the grass. 猫悄悄地穿过草地。

例句：A thief crept into the house during the night.  一个小偷在夜里潜入了房子。


近义词：slinked, crawled, inched, stole


反义词：stormed, rushed, charged


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He creeps along the wall. 他沿着墙慢慢地走。
The plant creeps along the ground. 植物匍匐在地上生长。


一般过去时:
He crept into the room. 他悄悄地溜进房间。
The burglar crept away silently. 窃贼悄悄地溜走了。


现在完成时:
The ivy has crept up the entire wall. 常春藤爬满了整面墙。
The vine has crept over the fence. 藤蔓爬过了栅栏。


过去完成时:
Before I knew it, he had crept into my bedroom.  在我还没意识到之前，他已经悄悄溜进了我的卧室。
After the storm, the water had crept into the basement. 暴风雨过后，水已经渗入了地下室。


将来时:
He will creep into the shadows. 他将潜入阴影中。
The shadow will creep across the floor. 影子将在地板上慢慢移动。


虚拟语气:
If I crept closer, I might see better. 如果我悄悄靠近一点，我可能会看得更清楚。
I wish he hadn't crept into my office. 我真希望他没偷偷溜进我的办公室。


被动语态: (被动语态很少用于crept)  此动词通常不用于被动语态。


疑问句:
Did he creep into the house? 他偷偷溜进房子了吗？
How did the thief creep away? 窃贼是怎么悄悄溜走的？


否定句:
He didn't creep into the house. 他没有偷偷溜进房子。
The mouse didn't creep across the floor. 老鼠没有在地板上慢慢爬行。


PHRASE->
creep up on someone  逐渐影响某人
creep into something 逐渐进入某种状态
creep out  令人毛骨悚然
creep along  缓慢移动
creep around  四处偷偷摸摸地走动
