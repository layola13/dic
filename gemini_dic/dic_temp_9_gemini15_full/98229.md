inseminating: /ɪnˈsɛmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 人工授精；播种；使充满 | The act of introducing semen into a female's reproductive tract for the purpose of fertilization.  It can also refer to the act of planting seeds in soil. | 例句：The farmer was inseminating his fields with wheat.  = 农民正在用小麦播种他的田地。

例句：The veterinarian inseminated the cow artificially. = 兽医对母牛进行了人工授精。


近义词：impregnating, fertilizing, planting


反义词：sterilizing


词性：动词

词源:
The word "inseminating" comes from the Latin word "inseminare," meaning "to sow seed."  The prefix "in-" means "in" or "into," and "seminare" is derived from "semen," meaning "seed."  Therefore, the word literally means "to sow seed into."
