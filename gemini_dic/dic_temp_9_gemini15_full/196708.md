sewabilities: /ˌsoʊəˈbɪlətiːz/ | n. | 可缝性；缝纫性能 | The ability of a fabric or material to be sewn.  It encompasses factors like its texture, strength, and ease of needle penetration.  | 例句：The high sewabilities of this new fabric make it ideal for garment production.  = 这款新型面料极佳的可缝性使其成为服装生产的理想选择。

The sewabilities of silk vary depending on its weight and weave. = 丝绸的可缝性因其重量和织法而异。


近义词：workability, sewability (singular) | 反义词：unsewability


词性：名词
