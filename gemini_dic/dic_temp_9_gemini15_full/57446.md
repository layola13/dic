dracontic: /dræˈkɒntɪk/ | adj. |严厉的；苛刻的；残酷的 | Extremely severe or cruel; harsh. | The dracontic laws punished even minor offenses severely.  这些严厉的法律甚至对轻微的罪行也处以重罚。|近义词：severe, harsh, rigorous, stringent |反义词：lenient, merciful, mild
