ppc:/piːpiːsiː/|abbr.|每点击付费，按点击付费|Pay Per Click,  a model of internet marketing in which advertisers pay a fee each time one of their ads is clicked.|例句：Many businesses use PPC advertising to drive traffic to their websites. 许多企业使用PPC广告来吸引网站流量。|近义词：CPC (Cost Per Click)|反义词：CPM (Cost Per Mille)
