re-projecting:/ˌriːprəˈdʒɛktɪŋ/| v.|重新投射；再投影；重新规划|The act of projecting something again, often with modifications or onto a different surface.  It can also refer to revising or reconsidering a plan or idea.|例句：The architect is re-projecting the building design to incorporate new energy-efficient features. 建筑师正在重新设计建筑方案，以融入新的节能功能。

例句：The team is re-projecting the sales figures after the recent market downturn.  团队在最近市场低迷后正在重新预测销售额。


近义词：re-evaluating, revising, re-planning, re-imagining


反义词：finalizing, completing


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are re-projecting the film onto a larger screen. = 他们正在把电影重新投影到更大的屏幕上。
We re-project the budget every quarter. = 我们每个季度都会重新规划预算。


一般过去时:
The company re-projected its earnings for the year. = 公司重新预测了今年的收益。
The artist re-projected the image onto the canvas. = 艺术家将图像重新投影到画布上。


现在完成时:
They have re-projected the timeline for the project. = 他们已经重新规划了项目的进度表。
We have re-projected the data to reflect the latest findings. = 我们已经重新预测了数据以反映最新的发现。


过去完成时:

She had re-projected her presentation after receiving feedback. = 在收到反馈后，她已经重新制作了她的演示文稿。


将来时:
