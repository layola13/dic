nonsymptomatic:/ˌnɒnˌsɪmptəˈmætɪk/| adj.|无症状的，不显症状的|Without symptoms or signs of a disease or condition.  The individual may be infected but shows no outward signs of illness.|例句：Many people with COVID-19 are nonsymptomatic and unknowingly spread the virus. 许多感染新冠病毒的人都是无症状的，并且不知不觉地传播了病毒。

例句：She tested positive for the virus, but remained nonsymptomatic throughout her infection. 她病毒检测呈阳性，但在整个感染期间都保持无症状。


近义词：asymptomatic, symptom-free, clinically silent

反义词：symptomatic

词性:形容词
