crock:/krɒk/|n.|破罐子；无用之物；谎话；骗局|A crock is a broken pot or a container made of baked clay. It can also refer to something worthless, ineffective, or a lie.  |例句：That old car is a complete crock.  那辆旧车完全是个破烂玩意儿。

例句：He told me a crock of lies. 他对我说了很多谎话。|近义词：potsherd, rubbish, nonsense, lie, hoax|反义词：treasure, truth, gem
