chessman:/ˈtʃɛsmən/| n. |棋子，棋子般的人物|A chessman is a single piece used in the game of chess.  It can refer to any of the different pieces (pawn, rook, knight, bishop, queen, king). The term can also be used figuratively to describe a person who is a pawn or easily manipulated in a larger game or power struggle.|例句：The black chessman moved diagonally across the board. 黑棋子斜着穿过棋盘。  He was just a chessman in their political game. 他只是他们政治游戏中的一枚棋子。|近义词：piece (in the context of chess), pawn (figuratively)|反义词：mastermind, player (in the context of a game)
