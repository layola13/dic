forwardable: /ˈfɔːrwərdəbl/ | adj. | 可转发的，可转寄的 | Capable of being forwarded; suitable for forwarding.  This adjective describes something that can be easily sent on to someone else, typically an email, message, or document. | 例句：This email is forwardable to all staff members. 这封邮件可以转发给所有员工。

例句：The document is forwardable to the relevant department. 这份文件可以转发给相关部门。


近义词：transmittable, transferable, redirectable


反义词：unforwardable


词性: adjective
