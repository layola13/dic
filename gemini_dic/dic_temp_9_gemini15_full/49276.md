deliberation:/dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən/| n.| 深思熟虑；仔细考虑；商讨；讨论 |The careful consideration of a subject; a discussion or debate.  It often involves a group of people discussing and weighing different options before making a decision. |例句：After much deliberation, the committee decided to approve the proposal.  经过长时间的慎重考虑，委员会决定批准该提案。
例句：The jury's deliberation lasted for several hours. 陪审团的商议持续了几个小时。|近义词：consideration, discussion, debate, reflection, contemplation |反义词：impulsiveness, rashness, haste


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The jury is deliberating on the case. = 陪审团正在审议此案。
She deliberates carefully before making important decisions. = 她做重要决定前会仔细斟酌。

一般过去时:
The council deliberated for hours before reaching a decision. = 理事会经过数小时的商讨才做出决定。
They deliberated the matter thoroughly. = 他们彻底地讨论了这件事。

现在完成时:
They have deliberated the issue extensively. = 他们已经广泛地讨论了这个问题。
We have deliberated for days, but we haven't reached a conclusion yet. = 我们已经讨论了好几天，但还没有得出结论。

过去完成时:
They had deliberated for many hours before they finally agreed. = 他们经过数小时的商讨才最终达成一致。
After they had deliberated the options, they chose the best one. = 在他们仔细权衡各种方案之后，选择了最佳方案。

将来时:
The board will deliberate on the merger next week. = 董事会下周将讨论合并事宜。
They will deliberate the proposal at their next meeting. = 他们将在下次会议上讨论这项提案。

虚拟语气:
If they had deliberated more carefully, they might have made a better decision. = 如果他们当时考虑得更周全一些，可能会做出更好的决定。
I wish they had deliberated before acting so impulsively. = 我真希望他们在行动之前能够仔细考虑一下。

被动语态:
The matter was deliberated at length. = 这件事被详细地讨论了。
The proposal is being deliberated by the committee. = 该提案正在由委员会审议。

疑问句:
Have they deliberated the terms of the contract? = 他们是否已经仔细考虑了合同条款？
Are they still deliberating? = 他们还在讨论吗？

否定句:
They did not deliberate sufficiently before making their decision. = 他们在做决定之前考虑得不够充分。
The committee didn't deliberate on the matter at all. = 委员会根本没有讨论这件事。
