noncommercially:/ˌnɒnkəˈmɜːʃəli/ | adv. |非商业性地，非营利性地 | Not for profit or commercial gain;  without intention of making a profit.  |例句：The software is distributed noncommercially to researchers.  该软件非商业性地分发给研究人员。

例句：The museum operates noncommercially, relying on donations and grants.  这个博物馆非商业化运营，依靠捐款和拨款。


近义词：uncommercially, gratuitously, freely


反义词：commercially, profitably
