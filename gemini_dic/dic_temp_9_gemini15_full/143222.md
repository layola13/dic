outcurses:/ˌaʊtˈkɜːrsɪz/ | n. |咒骂；诅咒；辱骂 |The act of cursing or uttering curses openly or publicly;  a public or vehement denunciation or condemnation.  It implies a more forceful and public expression of anger or disapproval than simply cursing.|例句：His outcurses echoed through the marketplace. 他的咒骂声回荡在市场上。 The politician's speech was filled with outcurses against his opponents.  这位政客的演讲充满了对对手的辱骂。|近义词：denunciation, condemnation, imprecation, execration|反义词：praise, blessing, commendation|名词
