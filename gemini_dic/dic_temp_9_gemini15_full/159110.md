pocketed:/ˈpɒkɪtɪd/ /ˈpɑːkɪtɪd/| vt.| 塞进口袋；赚取；暗中获取 | To put something into a pocket; to secretly acquire or obtain something; to earn or gain something. |例句：He pocketed the coin. 他把硬币塞进口袋。

He pocketed a large profit from the deal. 他从这笔交易中赚取了巨额利润。|近义词：put in, earned, acquired, secured|反义词：lost, forfeited, relinquished


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pockets his change after buying coffee. = 他买完咖啡后把零钱塞进口袋。
She pockets the money she earned. = 她把赚到的钱装进口袋。


一般过去时:
The thief pocketed the wallet. = 小偷把钱包塞进口袋了。
He pocketed the winnings after the game. = 游戏结束后，他把奖金收了起来。


现在完成时:
They have pocketed significant gains this quarter. = 他们本季度获得了可观的收益。
She has pocketed all the evidence. = 她已经把所有证据藏了起来。


过去完成时:
He had pocketed the letter before anyone noticed. = 在任何人注意到之前，他已经把信藏起来了。
The criminal had pocketed the stolen goods. = 罪犯已经把赃物藏起来了。


将来时:
She will pocket the money as soon as she gets it. = 她一拿到钱就会把它装进口袋。
He will pocket the award for his outstanding work. = 他将获得因其杰出工作而获得的奖项。


虚拟语气:
If he had pocketed the money, he would have been caught. = 如果他把钱藏起来，他就会被抓住。
I wish I had pocketed the extra cash. = 我真希望我把额外的现金收起来。


被动语态:
The money was pocketed by the thief. = 钱被小偷装进口袋了。
The profits were quickly pocketed by the company's executives. = 利润很快就被公司高管们赚走了。


疑问句:
Did he pocket the money? = 他把钱装进口袋了吗？
Has she pocketed all the evidence? = 她把所有证据都藏起来了吗？


否定句:
He didn't pocket the money; he gave it to charity. = 他没有把钱装进口袋；他把它捐给了慈善机构。
She didn't pocket the extra money. = 她没有把多余的钱装进口袋。
