/ræpˈtɔːrz/| n. |猛禽类，掠食性鸟类 | Raptors are birds of prey, characterized by sharp talons and hooked beaks, adapted for hunting and killing other animals.  猛禽是食肉鸟类，其特征是锋利的爪子和钩状的喙，适应于捕猎和杀死其他动物。|  Eagles, hawks, falcons, and owls are all examples of raptors.  鹰、鹰、猎鹰和猫头鹰都是猛禽的例子。| 近义词：birds of prey, predators | 反义词：prey
