hollerer:/ˈhɑːlərər/| n. |大声喊叫的人，叫喊者 |A hollerer is someone who shouts or yells loudly.  They might do this to express excitement, anger, or to get someone's attention.  |Example: The hollerer at the game was easily heard above the crowd.|近义词：shouter,yeller,screamer|反义词：whisperee, murmurer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hollerers at the top of his lungs. = 他扯着嗓子大声喊叫。
The children holler frequently when they play. = 孩子们玩耍时经常大声喊叫。


一般过去时:
She hollered for help when she fell. = 她摔倒时大声呼救。
The crowd hollered their approval. = 人群大声欢呼表示赞同。


现在完成时:
He has hollered so much that he's lost his voice. = 他喊叫太多，嗓子都喊哑了。
They have hollered at the referee all game. = 他们整场比赛都在冲着裁判大声喊叫。


过去完成时:
She had hollered until she was hoarse. = 她一直喊叫直到声音嘶哑。
The children had hollered before their parents even realized what was happening. = 孩子们在父母意识到发生了什么事之前就已经大声喊叫了。


将来时:
He will holler when he sees the surprise. = 他看到惊喜时会大声喊叫。
They will holler with excitement if they win. = 如果他们赢了，他们会兴奋地大声欢呼。


虚拟语气:
If he hollered louder, someone might hear him. = 如果他喊得更大声一点，可能有人会听到他。
I wish he wouldn't holler so much. = 我希望他别喊那么大声。


被动语态:
The message was hollered across the field. = 消息在田野里被大声喊叫着传开了。
His name was hollered by the excited fans. = 他名字被兴奋的球迷们大声喊叫着。


疑问句:

否定句:
