natty:/ˈnæti/| adj.|整洁的；漂亮的；时髦的 |Neat and smart in appearance; stylish.  |例句：He looked natty in his new suit. 他穿着新西装显得非常精神。|例句：The natty dresser always turned heads.  这位衣着讲究的人总是引人注目。|近义词：stylish, dapper, smart, chic |反义词：sloppy, unkempt, dowdy
