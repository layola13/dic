英文解析:The plural form of "conciseness," referring to multiple instances or aspects of being concise (expressing much in few words).  It emphasizes the repeated or varied nature of brevity and clarity in a text or speech.


例句：

The author achieved several concisenesses throughout the essay, resulting in a highly effective piece. 作者在整篇文章中都达到了简洁的多个方面，从而创作出一篇非常有效的文章。


The different concisenesses in the legal document ensured clarity and avoided any ambiguity. 法律文件中不同的简洁之处确保了清晰性，并避免了任何歧义。


近义词:brevity, succinctness, terseness
