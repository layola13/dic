plugging:/ˈplʌɡɪŋ/| v. |堵塞；插入；猛推；宣传；辛勤工作 |To fill a hole or gap with something; to insert something firmly into a place; to promote something vigorously; to work hard and continuously. |例句：He was plugging away at his homework. 他在努力做作业。|近义词：inserting, promoting, pushing, jamming |反义词：unblocking, removing, withdrawing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is plugging the hole in the wall. = 他正在堵墙上的洞。
They are plugging the new product on social media. = 他们在社交媒体上大力宣传新产品。


一般过去时:
She plugged the gap in the schedule. = 她填补了日程安排中的空缺。
The company plugged the leak in its finances. = 公司堵住了财务上的漏洞。


现在完成时:
They have plugged all the leaks in the roof. = 他们已经堵住了屋顶的所有漏洞。
We have been plugging away at this project for weeks. = 我们几个星期以来一直在努力进行这个项目。


过去完成时:
He had plugged the sink before leaving for work. = 他在去上班前已经堵住了水槽。
They had plugged the information into the computer before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经将信息输入电脑。


将来时:
She will plug the hole in the dam. = 她将堵住水坝上的洞。
We will be plugging away at this until we finish. = 我们会一直努力直到完成。


虚拟语气:
If I had a spare tire, I would plug the hole. = 如果我有一个备用轮胎，我会堵住洞。
I wish I could plug this leak more quickly. = 我希望我能更快地堵住这个漏洞。


被动语态:
The hole was plugged with cement. = 洞是用水泥堵住的。
The new product is being plugged on television. = 新产品正在电视上大力宣传。


疑问句:
Are you plugging the drain? = 你在堵塞排水管吗？
Is the company plugging the new product effectively? = 公司是否有效地宣传了新产品？


否定句:
He didn't plug the leak in time. = 他没有及时堵住漏洞。
They didn't plug the information into the database. = 他们没有将信息输入数据库。


PHRASE->
plug away at: 努力工作
plug in: 插上；连接
plug something into: 把…插入
plug a hole: 堵洞
plug a leak: 堵漏
