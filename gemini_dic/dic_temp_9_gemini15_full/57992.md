drivel:/ˈdraɪvəl/| v. n.|无聊的废话；胡说八道；口水；|to talk nonsense; to produce nonsense; saliva |例句：He was driveling on about his latest business venture. 他一直在胡扯他的最新商业冒险。

例句：Don't drivel; tell me what really happened. 不要胡说八道；告诉我到底发生了什么。


近义词：babble, chatter, gibberish, twaddle|反义词：sense, reason, logic|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drivels incessantly. = 他没完没了地胡说八道。
She drivels about politics. = 她对政治一通胡说。


一般过去时:
He driveled throughout the meeting. = 他在整个会议上都在胡说八道。
She driveled nonsense to the police. = 她对警察胡说八道。


现在完成时:
He has driveled on this subject for hours. = 他已经在这个话题上胡扯了几个小时。
She has driveled so much, nobody believes her. = 她胡说八道太多，没人相信她。


过去完成时:
He had driveled the same story many times before. = 他以前已经多次说过同样的故事。
She had driveled until she was exhausted. = 她一直胡说八道直到精疲力尽。


将来时:
He will drivel again at the next meeting. = 他将在下次会议上再次胡说八道。
She will drivel until she is interrupted. = 她会一直胡说八道直到被打断。


虚拟语气:
If he driveled any less, we might actually learn something. = 如果他少胡说八道一些，我们或许能学到一些东西。
I wish he wouldn't drivel so much. = 我希望他不要那么多胡说八道。


被动语态:  (Drivel is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Does he often drivel like that? = 他经常那样胡说八道吗？
Is she driveling again? = 她又在胡说八道了吗？


否定句:
He didn't drivel at all during the presentation. = 他在演示过程中根本没有胡说八道。
She doesn't usually drivel like this. = 她通常不会这样胡说八道。



PHRASE->
drivel on = 继续胡说八道

drivel out =  (less common, could imply expelling nonsense)
