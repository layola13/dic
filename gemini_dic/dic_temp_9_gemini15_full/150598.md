paperiness:/ˈpeɪpəri.nəs/| n. |纸质感，纸一般的特性|The quality or state of being like paper; thinness, fragility, or a tendency to crinkle or tear easily.  It can also refer to the texture or appearance of something that resembles paper.|The paperiness of the ancient scroll made it difficult to handle. 这张古老卷轴的纸质感使得它难以处理。The paperiness of the skin indicated dehydration. 皮肤的纸质感表明脱水。|thinness, flimsiness, fragility|sturdiness, robustness|名词
