phytolithology:/ˌfaɪtoʊlɪˈθɑːlədʒi/| n. |植物硅酸体学|The study of phytoliths.  Phytoliths are microscopic silica bodies produced by plants.  The study involves their identification, analysis, and use in reconstructing past environments and plant communities.|例句：Phytolithology is a valuable tool for paleoecological research. 植物硅酸体学是古生态学研究中一种有价值的工具。|近义词：paleobotany (部分重叠)|反义词：无
