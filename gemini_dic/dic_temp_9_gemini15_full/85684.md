have no use for:/ˌhæv ˈnoʊ juːs fɔːr/| phrasal verb|对...没用；不屑；讨厌|This phrase expresses a lack of need or desire for something or someone. It often implies a negative feeling, such as dislike or disdain.  |例句：I have no use for people who lie. 我对说谎的人没好感。

例句：She has no use for his arrogant attitude. 她讨厌他那傲慢的态度。


近义词：dislike, despise, scorn, have no patience for, can't stand


反义词：appreciate, value, cherish, need


词性: phrasal verb
