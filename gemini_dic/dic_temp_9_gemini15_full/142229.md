orogastric:/ˌɔːrəʊˈɡæstrɪk/ | adj. | 胃口的，口腔和胃的 | Relating to both the mouth and the stomach.  This term is used primarily in medical contexts to describe routes of administration or procedures involving both the oral cavity and the stomach. |Example: Orogastric intubation is a procedure that involves inserting a tube through the mouth and into the stomach.  口腔胃管插管是一种通过口腔插入胃管的手术。|近义词：buccogastric |反义词： None specifically, but terms referring to separate oral or gastric routes could be considered opposites.
