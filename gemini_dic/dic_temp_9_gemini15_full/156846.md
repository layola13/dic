pigmentation:/ˌpɪɡmənˈteɪʃən/| n. |色素沉着，着色|The process of coloring or being colored, especially the distribution of pigment in the skin, hair, or eyes.  It refers to the amount and type of melanin in the skin, which determines skin color.|例句：Excessive sun exposure can lead to increased pigmentation.  过度日晒会导致色素沉着增加。|例句：Her skin showed uneven pigmentation after the burn. 烧伤后她的皮肤出现了色素沉着不均。|近义词：coloring, coloration, pigmentation, staining |反义词：depigmentation, lack of color
