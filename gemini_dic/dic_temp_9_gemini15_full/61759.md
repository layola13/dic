empathise:/ˈɛmpəθaɪz/ | v. | 同情，共鸣 | To understand and share the feelings of another.  | Example: I empathise with her loss; it must be incredibly difficult. 我同情她的损失；这肯定非常困难。|近义词：sympathise, understand, relate to | 反义词：be indifferent to, be unsympathetic to


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I empathise with your situation. = 我同情你的处境。
He empathizes with the characters in the book. = 他对书中的人物感同身受。

一般过去时:
She empathised with the homeless man's plight. = 她同情那个无家可归的人的困境。
They empathized with the victims of the natural disaster. = 他们同情自然灾害的受害者。

现在完成时:
I have empathised with many people throughout my career. = 在我的职业生涯中，我同情过很多人。
She has empathized with the struggles of single mothers. = 她对单亲母亲的挣扎感同身受。

过去完成时:
I had empathised with her before, but this time it was different. = 我以前同情过她，但这次不一样了。
He had empathized with their pain, having experienced something similar himself. = 他同情他们的痛苦，因为他自己也经历过类似的事情。

将来时:
I will empathise with him when I hear his story. = 当我听到他的故事时，我会同情他。
She will empathize with the characters' emotional journey. = 她会对人物的情感旅程感同身受。

虚拟语气:
If I were in her shoes, I would empathise with her more deeply. = 如果我处在她那样境地，我会更加同情她。
I wish I could empathise with his feelings better. = 我希望我能更好地理解他的感受。

被动语态:
His pain was empathised with by many. = 很多人同情他的痛苦。(很少用被动语态)
Her situation was empathised with by her friends. = 她的朋友们同情她的处境。(很少用被动语态)

疑问句:
Do you empathise with the characters in the movie? = 你对电影里的人物有共鸣吗？
Can you empathise with someone you disagree with? = 你能同情与你意见相左的人吗？

否定句:
I cannot empathise with his actions. = 我无法理解他的行为。
She didn't empathise with the villain's motives. = 她对反派的动机没有共鸣。


PHRASE->
empathise with = 同情…
empathise deeply = 深切同情
empathise fully = 完全理解
empathise strongly = 强烈同情
empathise readily = 轻易同情
