cheers:/tʃɪərz/| int. n.|干杯；谢谢；加油|An exclamation used to express good wishes, especially when drinking a toast; also used as an informal expression of thanks or encouragement.|例句：Cheers! Let's drink to our success! 干杯！让我们为我们的成功干杯！
例句：Cheers, mate, for your help. 谢谢，伙计，谢谢你的帮助。|近义词：thanks, thank you, bravo|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cheer for their team. = 他们为他们的球队欢呼。
We cheer at the good news. = 我们为好消息欢呼。


一般过去时:
The crowd cheered loudly. = 人群大声欢呼。
She cheered me up when I was sad. = 我难过的时候她让我振作起来。


现在完成时:
They have cheered for their team all season. = 整个赛季他们都为他们的球队加油。
I have never cheered so loudly before. = 我以前从未如此大声地欢呼过。


过去完成时:
They had cheered themselves hoarse before the game ended. = 在比赛结束之前，他们已经喊哑了嗓子。
She had cheered up considerably by the time I saw her. = 我看到她的时候，她已经好多了。


将来时:
The crowd will cheer when the band comes on stage. = 乐队上台时，人群会欢呼。
We will cheer you on at the competition. = 我们会在比赛中为你加油。


虚拟语气:
If they cheered louder, the team might win. = 如果他们欢呼得更响亮，球队可能会赢。
I wish they cheered for the underdog. = 我希望他们能为弱者加油。


被动语态:
The team was cheered on by their fans. = 球队受到了球迷的欢呼。
The winner was cheered by the whole stadium. = 获胜者受到了整个体育场的欢呼。


疑问句:
Will they cheer for the home team? = 他们会为主队加油吗？
Did you cheer at the concert? = 你在音乐会上欢呼了吗？


否定句:
They didn't cheer for the losing team. = 他们没有为输的球队加油。
She didn't cheer me up at all. = 她根本没有让我振作起来。
