graduates:/ˈɡrædʒuets/| n.|毕业生|People who have successfully completed a course of study, especially at a university or college.|例句：Many graduates are finding it difficult to secure jobs in the current economic climate. 许多毕业生发现很难在当前的经济环境下找到工作。
例句：The university is holding a ceremony for its graduates this weekend.  大学本周末将为毕业生举行典礼。|近义词：alumni, former students|反义词：undergraduates


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Graduates often seek employment after graduation. = 毕业生通常在毕业后寻求就业。
Many graduates find jobs in their field of study. = 许多毕业生在他们的学习领域找到工作。


一般过去时:
Many graduates left the university after the ceremony. = 许多毕业生在典礼结束后离开了大学。
