disassociation:/ˌdɪsˌəʊsiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 分离；脱节；解离；游离 | The process of separating or becoming separated; a feeling of detachment or lack of connection.  It can refer to a psychological state, a social phenomenon, or a physical process. | Example: The trauma caused a disassociation from her memories.  创伤导致她与记忆分离。

Example: The disassociation between the government's promises and its actions led to public distrust. 政府的承诺与其行动之间的脱节导致了公众的不信任。


近义词：separation, detachment, dissociation, alienation, estrangement | 反义词：association, connection, integration, unity
