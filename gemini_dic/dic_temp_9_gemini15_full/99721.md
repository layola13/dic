interludes:/ˈɪntərluːdz/| n. |插曲；间奏；幕间剧|A short period of time between two longer periods or events, or a short piece of music played between the parts of a longer piece.|例句：The concert included several interludes of classical guitar music. 音乐会包括几段古典吉他音乐的间奏。  The comedian’s interludes between acts kept the audience entertained.  演员在幕间表演的喜剧小品逗乐了观众。|近义词：intermission, break, pause |反义词：main event, continuity
