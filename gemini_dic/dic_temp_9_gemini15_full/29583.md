catechin:/ˈkætɪtʃɪn/| n. |儿茶素|A type of polyphenol antioxidant found in tea, cocoa, and other plants. Catechins are known for their potential health benefits, including antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. They are also being studied for their potential role in preventing certain diseases.| Example: Green tea is rich in catechins, which contribute to its health benefits.  绿茶富含儿茶素，这使其具有健康益处。|近义词：flavonoid, polyphenol|反义词：N/A
