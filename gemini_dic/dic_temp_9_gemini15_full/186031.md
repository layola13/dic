rewire:/ˌriˈwaɪər/| vt.|重新布线；改变…的线路；使…改弦易辙|To change the wiring of something; to change someone's thinking or behaviour significantly.|例句：We need to rewire the entire house.我们需要重新布线整个房子。

例句：The experience completely rewired his thinking.这段经历彻底改变了他的想法。|近义词：reconfigure, revamp, overhaul|反义词：maintain, preserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rewire old houses for modern appliances.  = 他们为老房子安装现代电器重新布线。
The company rewiers its marketing strategy annually. = 这家公司每年都会调整其营销策略。


一般过去时:
The electrician rewired the kitchen yesterday. = 电工昨天重新布线了厨房。
She rewired her thinking after the accident. = 事故后她改变了自己的想法。


现在完成时:
They have rewired the entire building. = 他们已经重新布线了整栋楼。
He has rewired his approach to problem-solving. = 他已经改变了解决问题的方法。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
