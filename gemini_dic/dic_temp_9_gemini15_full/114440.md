makeover:/ˈmeɪkˌoʊvər/ | n. |彻底改造；改头换面；大变样 |A makeover is a significant change or improvement in the appearance or condition of something, often involving a complete transformation.  It typically refers to a physical change, such as a new hairstyle or clothing style, but can also apply to a more abstract change, such as a business's rebranding. |例句：She gave her kitchen a complete makeover. = 她把她的厨房彻底改造了一番。

She got a makeover before the wedding. = 婚礼前她做了个大变样。


近义词：transformation, revamp, renovation, refit | 反义词： deterioration, decline
