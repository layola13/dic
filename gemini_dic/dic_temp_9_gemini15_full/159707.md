polycarpellary:/ˌpɒliˈkɑːrpəlɛri/| adj.|多心皮的|Having many carpels; referring to a gynoecium composed of multiple carpels.  A carpel is a unit of the female reproductive organ of a flower.  Polycarpellary flowers are common in many flowering plants.|Example: The rose flower exhibits a polycarpellary gynoecium, meaning its pistil is formed from several fused carpels.|近义词：multicarpellate|反义词：monocarpellary
