fucked-up: /fʌkt ˈʌp/ | adj. | 糟糕的；混乱的；严重受损的；精神错乱的 |

英文解析:  This is a highly informal and vulgar term used to describe something that is severely damaged, disordered, or dysfunctional.  It can refer to a physical object, a situation, a person's mental state, or a system.  The intensity of the meaning depends heavily on context.

例句:

His life is completely fucked up after losing his job and his wife.  = 他失去工作和妻子后，生活彻底一团糟。

The economy is fucked up right now. = 目前经济状况很糟糕。


近义词:  messed up, screwed up, damaged, broken, dysfunctional, chaotic, deranged, crazy

反义词:  perfect, flawless, intact, orderly, functional, sane
