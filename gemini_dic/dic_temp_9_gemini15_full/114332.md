maistry: /ˈmɑːstrɪ/ | n. |精通；掌握；技艺；熟练 | mastery refers to a high level of skill or expertise in a particular field or activity, often achieved through years of dedicated practice and learning.  It implies a deep understanding and complete control of the subject matter. |  He demonstrated true mastery of the piano. 他展现了精湛的钢琴技艺。  Her mastery of several languages was impressive. 她掌握多种语言令人印象深刻。| 近义词：expertise, proficiency, skill, command | 反义词：incompetence, inexperience, ignorance |


USAGE->

His mastery of the subject is evident. = 他对这个主题的掌握显而易见。
Her mastery of the violin is truly remarkable. = 她小提琴的造诣确实非凡。
