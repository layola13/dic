seedsmen:/ˈsiːdzmən/| n. |种子商，种子供应商 |A seedsman is a person who sells or supplies seeds, typically for agricultural or horticultural purposes. They may offer a wide variety of seeds, including vegetable seeds, flower seeds, grass seeds, and tree seeds.  Seedsmen often provide advice on seed selection, planting, and cultivation.| The local seedsman had a wide selection of heirloom tomato seeds. 当地种子商有各种各样的传家宝番茄种子。| seed merchant, seed dealer |  (no direct antonym) |名词
