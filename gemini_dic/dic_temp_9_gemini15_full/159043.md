pneumatometer:/ˌnuːməˈtɒmɪtə(r)/ | n. |气压计 |A pneumatometer is an instrument used to measure air pressure or the pressure of other gases.  It is often used in medical settings to measure lung function or in industrial applications to measure gas pressure in pipelines or vessels.  |Example: The doctor used a pneumatometer to assess the patient's lung capacity.  医生用气压计评估病人的肺活量。


|近义词:manometer, pressure gauge |反义词:None directly applicable.  |词性:noun
