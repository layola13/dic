demoralization: /ˌdiːˌmɒrəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |士气低落；泄气；道德沦丧 | The loss of morale; a decline in spirit, enthusiasm, or confidence.  It can also refer to a decline in moral standards or principles. | 例句：The team's demoralization was evident in their lackluster performance. 球队的士气低落从他们无精打采的表现中显而易见。  The widespread corruption led to a general demoralization of the population.  普遍的腐败导致了民众普遍的道德沦丧。 | 近义词：discouragement, despair, dejection, depression | 反义词：encouragement, inspiration, upliftment
