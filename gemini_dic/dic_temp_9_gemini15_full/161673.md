It can also refer to the outskirts or environs of a place. |Example: The artist found inspiration in the quiet pourlieus of the city, far from the bustling downtown.  艺术家在远离繁华市中心的城市边缘地带找到了灵感。|近义词：outskirts, suburbs, periphery, fringe |反义词：center, heart, core |名词
