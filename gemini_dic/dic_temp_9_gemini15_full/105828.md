kit-up:/kɪt ʌp/| v.|装备，配备，打扮|To equip oneself or someone else with necessary items or clothing, especially for a particular activity or purpose.  |例句：The soldiers kitted up before the dawn patrol. 士兵们在黎明巡逻前装备好自己。|例句： We need to kit the new recruits up with the latest equipment.我们需要为新兵配备最新的设备。|近义词：equip, outfit, gear up|反义词：disarm, strip


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They kit up every morning before training. = 他们每天早上训练前都会装备好自己。
The team kits up with the latest technology. = 这个团队配备了最新的技术。

一般过去时:
He kitted up quickly and rushed out the door. = 他迅速装备好，冲出门外。
She kitted herself up in her hiking gear. = 她穿上她的徒步装备。

现在完成时:
They have kitted up for the expedition. = 他们已经为探险做好了装备。
The company has kitted out its employees with new laptops. = 公司已经为员工配备了新的笔记本电脑。

过去完成时:
He had kitted up before the emergency call. = 在紧急呼叫之前，他已经装备好了。
They had kitted out the van with camping supplies. = 他们已经用露营用品装备好了货车。

将来时:
We will kit up early tomorrow for the climb. = 我们明天早些时候将为攀登做好准备。
The army will kit out the new recruits by next week. = 军队将在下周为新兵配备装备。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would kit up more thoroughly. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会更彻底地装备自己。
I wish I had kitted up properly for the cold weather. = 我真希望我为寒冷的天气穿戴得更合适。

被动语态:
The soldiers were kitted up by their sergeant. = 士兵们由他们的军士为他们装备。
The team was kitted out with state-of-the-art equipment. = 该团队配备了最先进的设备。

疑问句:
Have you kitted up for the race yet? = 你准备好参加比赛了吗？
Are the climbers fully kitted out? = 登山者装备齐全了吗？

否定句:
He didn't kit up properly for the hike. = 他没有为徒步旅行做好充分的准备。
They hadn't kitted out the laboratory properly. = 他们没有为实验室做好充分的准备。
