faultily:/ˈfɔːltɪli/ | adv. | 错误地，有缺陷地 |  In a faulty or imperfect manner; defectively.  | The machine was faultily designed and kept breaking down. 这台机器设计有缺陷，一直坏掉。 The experiment was conducted faultily, leading to inaccurate results. 实验进行得有误，导致结果不准确。 | imperfectly, defectively, erroneously | correctly, perfectly | 副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and as such doesn't have tenses like verbs.  It describes the manner in which an action is performed.  It can be used in various sentence structures.


Example Sentences with "Faultily":

Simple Sentence: He argued faultily. 他论证错误。
Complex Sentence: Because the instructions were faultily written, the project failed. 因为说明书写得有误，项目失败了。

PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases using "faultily".  Its usage is straightforward and descriptive.  Phrases would be constructed around it, rather than existing pre-made phrases.  For example:  "faultily constructed argument," "faultily executed plan," "faultily designed system."


词源:
The word "faultily" is derived from the adjective "faulty," which in turn comes from the Middle English word "faulty," meaning "defective" or "imperfect."  The suffix "-ly" is added to form the adverb, indicating the manner in which something is done.
