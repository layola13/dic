gasps:/ɡæsp/| interj. v.|倒吸气；喘息；惊讶地吸气|A short, sharp intake of breath, especially one expressing surprise, shock, or pain.|例句：She gasped at the sight of the accident. 她看到事故现场倒吸了一口气。
例句：He gasped for air after the strenuous exercise. 剧烈运动后，他大口喘气。|近义词：breathe heavily, pant, gasp for breath|反义词:breathe calmly, exhale smoothly|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gasps for air. 他喘不过气来。
She gasps in surprise. 她惊讶地倒吸了一口气。


一般过去时:
He gasped when he saw the snake. 他看到蛇时倒吸了一口冷气。
She gasped in horror at the scene. 她看到这一幕惊恐地倒吸了一口气。


现在完成时:
He has gasped for breath all night. 他整夜都在喘不过气来。
She has gasped every time she saw him since the incident. 自那件事以来，每次看到他她都惊呼。


过去完成时:
He had gasped in shock before he fainted. 他震惊地倒吸了一口气，然后晕倒了。
She had gasped when she first heard the news. 她第一次听到这个消息时惊呼了一声。


将来时:
He will gasp when he sees the results. 他看到结果时会倒吸一口冷气。
She will gasp with delight when she opens her present. 当她打开礼物时会高兴地惊呼。


虚拟语气:
If I saw that, I would gasp. 如果我看到那个，我会倒吸一口冷气。
I wish I hadn't gasped so loudly. 我希望我没有那么大声地惊呼。


被动语态:
(Gasps is rarely used in the passive voice)


疑问句:
Did he gasp when he saw the spider? 他看到蜘蛛时倒吸了一口气吗？
Does she usually gasp at unexpected events? 她通常对意外事件感到震惊吗？


否定句:
He didn't gasp at all. 他根本没有倒吸一口气。
She didn't gasp when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息时并没有惊呼。


PHRASE->
gasp for breath: 喘不过气来
gasp in amazement: 惊讶地倒吸一口气
gasp with laughter: 笑得喘不过气来
let out a gasp: 发出一声惊呼
a gasp of surprise: 一声惊讶的倒吸气
