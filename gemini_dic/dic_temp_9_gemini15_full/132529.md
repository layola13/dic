nondevelopmental:/ˌnɒnˌdɛvəlˈɒpməntəl/ | adj. |非发育性的，非发展性的 | Not relating to or involving development or growth.  This adjective describes something that doesn't contribute to or result from development or growth, often in a biological or psychological context.  | Example:  The researcher found that the drug had nondevelopmental side effects.  研究人员发现这种药物有非发育性的副作用。

The injury was nondevelopmental; it wasn't caused by a developmental problem or process.  这伤并非发育性损伤；它不是由发育问题或过程引起的。


近义词: non-developmental, nonprogressive

反义词: developmental, progressive


词性: adjective
