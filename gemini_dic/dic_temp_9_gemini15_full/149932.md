palliating: /ˈpælieɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 减轻（痛苦）；缓解；掩盖 | To alleviate or lessen the severity of (something, especially pain or distress); to make (something bad) seem less serious.  | 例句：The medication helped palliate the patient's pain.  = 这药帮助减轻了病人的疼痛。

The doctor prescribed painkillers to palliate her suffering. = 医生开了止痛药来减轻她的痛苦。


近义词：alleviate, mitigate, soothe, ease, relieve | 反义词：aggravate, exacerbate, worsen | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The medication palliates the symptoms. = 药物减轻了症状。
They palliate the effects of the disease. = 他们减轻了疾病的影响。


一般过去时:
The nurse palliated the patient's discomfort. = 护士减轻了病人的不适。
He palliated his guilt by making a donation. = 他通过捐款来减轻他的内疚。


现在完成时:
The treatment has palliated her condition significantly. = 治疗已经显著减轻了她的病情。
They have palliated the situation with temporary measures. = 他们用临时措施缓解了局势。


过去完成时:
She had palliated her anxiety with deep breaths before the presentation. = 演讲前，她通过深呼吸来减轻焦虑。
They had palliated the problem with a quick fix, but it returned later. = 他们用权宜之计解决了问题，但后来问题又出现了。


将来时:
The new drug will palliate the side effects of chemotherapy. = 新药将减轻化疗的副作用。
We hope the surgery will palliate his pain. = 我们希望手术能减轻他的痛苦。


虚拟语气:
If only we could find a way to palliate the suffering of the refugees. = 如果我们能找到一种方法来减轻难民的痛苦就好了。
I wish I could palliate the damage that I caused. = 我希望我能减轻我造成的损害。


被动语态:
The pain was palliated by the medication. = 疼痛被药物减轻了。
The situation is being palliated by the government's efforts. = 政府的努力正在缓解局势。


疑问句:
Does this treatment palliate the symptoms effectively? = 这种治疗能有效地减轻症状吗？
Can anything palliate the grief of losing a loved one? = 有什么能减轻失去亲人的悲伤吗？


否定句:
The treatment did not palliate her condition at all. = 治疗根本没有减轻她的病情。
Unfortunately, nothing could palliate his pain. = 不幸的是，什么也无法减轻他的痛苦。


PHRASE->
palliate the effects of = 减轻……的影响
palliate the symptoms of = 减轻……的症状
palliate the pain of = 减轻……的痛苦
palliate a situation = 缓解某种情况
palliative care = 姑息治疗


词源:  From Latin *palliāre "to cloak, cover," from pallium "cloak."  The sense evolved from "covering" to "covering or masking something unpleasant."
