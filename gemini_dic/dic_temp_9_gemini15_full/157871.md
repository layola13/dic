plain-clothes: /ˌpleɪnˈkloʊðz/ | adj. | 便衣的；便服的 |  Wearing ordinary clothes, not a uniform.  The adjective describes clothing that is not distinctive or uniform. It often refers to police officers or detectives who are not in uniform. |


例句：

The plain-clothes police officer was observing the suspect from across the street.  = 便衣警察正在街对面观察嫌疑人。

Several plain-clothes detectives were mingling with the crowd at the concert. = 几名便衣侦探混迹在音乐会的观众中。


近义词：uniformed, civilian


反义词： uniformed


词性: adjective
