prodders:/ˈprɒdərz/| n.| 戳者；激励者；催促者 | A prodder is a person or thing that prods or urges someone to do something.  It can refer to a physical object used for poking or prodding, or a person who persistently encourages or motivates others, sometimes in an annoying way. |例句：The farmer used a prodder to move the cattle. 农民用戳棒赶牛。  He's a constant prodder, always pushing me to work harder. 他是个不停催促的人，总是逼着我更努力工作。|近义词：goad, spur, motivator, instigator |反义词：discourager, deterrent, hinderer
