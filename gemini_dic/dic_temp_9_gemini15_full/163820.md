predeployment:/ˌpriːdɪˈplɔɪmənt/| n. |部署前，预先部署|The activities and preparations undertaken before the deployment of troops, equipment, or systems.  It encompasses planning, training, and logistical arrangements to ensure a smooth and effective deployment.|例句：The predeployment training for the soldiers included extensive simulations and field exercises. = 部署前的士兵训练包括大量的模拟和实地演习。

例句：Thorough predeployment planning is crucial for the success of any military operation. = 充分的预先部署规划对于任何军事行动的成功至关重要。


近义词：preparation, planning, readiness

反义词：postdeployment


词性:名词
