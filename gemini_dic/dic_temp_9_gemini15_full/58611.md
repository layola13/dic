dulls:/dʌlz/| v. adj.|使迟钝；使厌倦；使暗淡；枯燥乏味的；迟钝的 |to make or become dull or less active; lacking brightness, interest, or sharpness|The long, monotonous work dulled his senses. 长期单调的工作使他感觉迟钝。|近义词：bore, tire, blunt,  |反义词:sharpen, excite, brighten, enliven|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The repetitive tasks dull my mind. = 重复性的工作使我的头脑迟钝。
This grey weather dulls the colors of the landscape. =  阴沉的天气使风景的颜色暗淡。


一般过去时:
The long journey dulled their enthusiasm. = 漫长的旅程使他们热情减退。
The constant noise dulled my hearing. =  持续的噪音使我的听力下降。


现在完成时:
Years of hard work have dulled his youthful energy. =  多年的辛勤工作已经消磨了他的青春活力。
The sun has dulled the paint on the house. = 太阳使房子的油漆褪色。


过去完成时:
The argument had dulled the festive atmosphere. =  争吵已经冲淡了节日的氛围。
He had dulled his senses with excessive drinking. = 他因过度饮酒而麻木了感官。


将来时:
The long winter will dull the vibrant colors of the garden. = 漫长的冬天将会使花园鲜艳的颜色暗淡。
Too much screen time will dull your mind. = 过多的屏幕时间会使你的头脑迟钝。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't been so tired, the movie wouldn't have dulled me so much. = 如果我不那么累，这部电影就不会让我那么昏昏欲睡。
I wish the rain wouldn't dull the colors of the flowers. =  我希望雨不会使花朵的颜色暗淡。


被动语态:
His senses were dulled by the medication. = 他的感官因药物而变得迟钝。
The brightness of the colors was dulled by the rain. =  颜色的鲜艳度被雨水冲淡了。


疑问句:
Does the constant noise dull your hearing? =  持续的噪音是否会使你的听力下降？
Does this monotonous work dull your mind? = 这项单调的工作是否会使你感到厌倦？


否定句:
The exciting news didn't dull their spirits. =  令人兴奋的消息并没有使他们的精神低落。
The bright sunshine didn't dull the colors of the painting. =  灿烂的阳光并没有使画的颜色暗淡。

词源:
The word "dull" comes from Old English *dull, meaning "stupid, slow, sluggish".  It's related to other Germanic words with similar meanings.  The sense of "not sharp" developed later.
