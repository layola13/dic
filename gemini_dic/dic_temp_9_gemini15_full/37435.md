commingling: /kəˈmɪŋɡlɪŋ/ | v. | 混合；混杂；掺杂 | The act of mixing or blending together, especially of different things that are not normally combined.  | Example: The commingling of different cultures enriched the city's vibrant atmosphere.  混合不同的文化丰富了这座城市充满活力的氛围。  |近义词：mixing, blending, mingling, intermingling | 反义词：separating, segregating | 动词
