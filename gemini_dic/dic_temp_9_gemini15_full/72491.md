fluorescein:/ˌflɔːrəˈsiːn/| n. |荧光素|A synthetic organic compound that is highly fluorescent, emitting a bright green-yellow light when exposed to ultraviolet or blue light. It is used as a tracer in various applications, including medical diagnostics and environmental studies.|例句：Fluorescein angiography is a medical procedure that uses fluorescein dye to visualize blood vessels in the eye. 荧光素血管造影术是一种医学程序，它使用荧光素染料来观察眼部的血管。Fluorescein is often used to trace the flow of groundwater. 荧光素常用于追踪地下水的流动。|近义词：fluorophore, dye|反义词:None.
