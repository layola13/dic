chefs:/ʃɛfs/ | n. |厨师们|The word "chefs" is the plural form of "chef," referring to a group of professional cooks.  It implies a higher level of culinary skill and artistry than simply a cook. Chefs often work in restaurants, hotels, or other food service establishments.| Example: The chefs prepared a magnificent five-course meal.  厨师们准备了一顿丰盛的五道菜。  The top chefs in the world competed in the culinary Olympics. 世界顶级厨师参加了烹饪奥运会。| cooks, cooks, culinary artists |  (no direct antonym, but perhaps "amateur cooks" or "unskilled kitchen staff") | 名词
