chipboard:/ˈtʃɪpbɔːrd/| n.|刨花板，碎料板|A manufactured wood panel made from wood chips or particles bonded together with a resin binder. It is commonly used in furniture and construction.|例句：The shelves were made of cheap chipboard and quickly began to sag.  书架是用廉价刨花板做的，很快就开始下垂了。|例句：The new kitchen cabinets are made from high-density chipboard for durability. 新的厨房橱柜由高密度刨花板制成，经久耐用。|近义词：particleboard, MDF (medium-density fiberboard)|反义词：solid wood, hardwood|名词
