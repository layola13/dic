neoplatonician:/ˌniːoʊplæˈtɒnɪʃən/ | n. |新柏拉图主义者|A follower or adherent of Neoplatonism.  A Neoplatonician believes in a transcendent One, the source of all being, and a hierarchical descent of emanations leading to the material world. They emphasize the importance of mystical experience and the pursuit of spiritual enlightenment.|Example:  Plotinus was a prominent Neoplatonician philosopher.  普罗提诺是一位杰出的新柏拉图主义哲学家。|近义词: Neoplatonist |反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it describes a follower of a specific philosophy)|
