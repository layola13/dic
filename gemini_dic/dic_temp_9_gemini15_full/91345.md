hydroplaning:/ˈhaɪdrəˌpleɪnɪŋ/| n. |水滑，水漂 |The phenomenon where a vehicle's tires lose contact with the road surface due to a layer of water between the tires and the road, causing loss of control.  |例句：The car started hydroplaning on the wet road, and the driver lost control. 这辆车在湿滑的路上开始水漂，司机失去了控制。  |例句：Heavy rain caused several accidents due to hydroplaning. 大雨导致几起因水滑造成的事故。 |近义词：aquaplaning |反义词:gripping, traction

一般现在时:
Hydroplaning occurs when there is too much water on the road. = 当路面上积水过多时，就会发生水滑现象。
He hydroplanes his boat across the lake. = 他驾驶着他的船在湖面上滑行。


一般过去时:

She hydroplaned her sailboat across the bay. = 她驾驶着她的帆船在海湾上滑行。
