semipoisonous: /ˌsɛmiˈpɔɪzənəs/ | adj. | 半有毒的，微毒的 | Slightly poisonous; capable of causing mild poisoning.  | Example:  Some mushrooms are semipoisonous and should not be eaten.  有些蘑菇略带毒性，不宜食用。

The plant is semipoisonous, causing only mild discomfort if ingested. 这植物略带毒性，即使摄入也只会引起轻微不适。


近义词: mildly toxic, slightly venomous, somewhat poisonous

反义词: non-toxic, harmless, innocuous


词源:

* **semi-**:  From Latin *semi*, meaning "half," "partly," or "incomplete."

* **poisonous**: From Old French *poisonous*, ultimately derived from Latin *potio* ("a drink," "potion"), referring to a poisonous drink.  The suffix *-ous* denotes "full of" or "having the quality of".
