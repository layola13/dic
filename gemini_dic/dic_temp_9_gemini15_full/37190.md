comedown:/ˈkʌmdaʊn/| n.|令人沮丧的经历；下降；衰落|A comedown is an unpleasant experience, especially one that is a significant change from a previous better situation. It can also refer to a physical descent or a decline in status or quality.|例句：After his successful career, his retirement felt like a comedown.  在他成功的职业生涯之后，他的退休感觉像是一次衰落。

例句：The sudden drop in temperature was quite a comedown.  温度的骤降真是令人沮丧。


近义词：letdown, disappointment, setback, decline, downfall|反义词：upswing, improvement, rise, elevation|名词
