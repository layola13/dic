skyride:/ˈskaɪraɪd/| n.|空中缆车之旅；空中索道；空中飞车|A ride on a cable car or similar conveyance that travels high above the ground.|例句：The skyride offered breathtaking views of the valley.  空中缆车之旅提供了令人叹为观止的山谷景色。

例句：We took a thrilling skyride across the mountains. 我们乘坐惊险刺激的空中缆车越过群山。


近义词：cable car ride, aerial tramway ride, chairlift ride|反义词：ground-level ride, walking tour|词性：名词
