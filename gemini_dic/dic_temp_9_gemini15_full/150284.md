panelation:/ˌpænəˈleɪʃən/| n. |嵌板；镶板；分格；面板布置|The arrangement of panels in a structure or design, especially in architecture and engineering.  It refers to the way panels are joined together and arranged to form a larger surface or structure.  The term can also apply to the process of creating or installing these panels. |Example: The building's unique panelation gave it a striking modern aesthetic.  建筑物独特的嵌板设计赋予它令人惊艳的现代美感。The car's innovative panelation improved its aerodynamic performance. 这辆车的创新面板设计提高了它的空气动力学性能。|近义词：partitioning, sectioning, segmentation |反义词：solidity, seamlessness
