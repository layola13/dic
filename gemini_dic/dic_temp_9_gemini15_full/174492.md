quinquagesima:/kwɪŋkwəˈdʒɛzɪmə/| n. | 五旬节前第七个星期日（基督教节日）|The word "quinquagesima" refers to the Sunday before Lent, specifically the seventh Sunday before Easter in the Christian liturgical calendar.  It marks the beginning of the period of preparation for Lent.  The name derives from its position being approximately fifty days before Easter. | Example:  The church held a special service on Quinquagesima Sunday.  五旬节前第七个星期日教堂举行了一场特别的礼拜。|近义词：Septuagesima (less common)|反义词：Easter (opposite in the liturgical calendar)|名词
