reendowed:/ˌriːɪnˈdaʊd/| v.|重新赋予；重新捐赠 |To endow again; to provide with new endowments.| The university was reendowed with a generous donation from an alumnus. 这所大学收到了校友的慷慨捐赠，获得了新的捐赠。 |近义词：reinstate, replenish, refresh |反义词：deprive, divest


USAGE->

The foundation reendows the scholarship program annually. = 该基金会每年都会重新资助奖学金项目。
