feminize:/ˈfemɪnaɪz/| vt.|使女性化；使具有女性特征|To make or become more feminine in appearance or behavior.  It often implies changing something traditionally associated with men to be more associated with women.|例句：He tried to feminize his appearance by growing his hair long. 他试图留长发来使自己的外表更女性化。例句：The toy company feminized the design of the action figure to appeal to a wider audience. 玩具公司将动作玩偶的设计女性化，以吸引更广泛的受众。|近义词：effeminate, womanize|反义词：masculinize, virility|动词
