noctuid:/ˈnɒktjuːɪd/| n. |夜蛾科昆虫|A noctuid is a moth belonging to the family Noctuidae, a large and diverse group known for their nocturnal habits and often cryptic coloration.  They are important components of many ecosystems, serving as pollinators and a food source for other animals. Many species are also agricultural pests.|Example:  Many noctuid species are attracted to artificial lights at night.  许多夜蛾科昆虫在夜间会被人工灯光吸引。|近义词：owlet moth (a less precise term)|反义词：无
