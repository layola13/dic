recharging:/ˌriˈtʃɑːrdʒɪŋ/| v. n. |充电；补充能量；恢复活力|The act of restoring energy to something, typically a battery or a person.  It can refer to the physical process of charging a battery or the metaphorical process of regaining energy and vitality.|例句：My phone needs recharging. = 我的手机需要充电。

例句：After a long day, I need some recharging time. = 漫长的一天后，我需要一些时间来恢复活力。


近义词：replenishing, refueling, revitalizing, restoring

反义词：depleting, draining, exhausting


词性:动词和名词
