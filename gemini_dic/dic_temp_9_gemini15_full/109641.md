lettuce: /ˈlɛtɪs/ | n. | 莴苣，生菜 | A leafy green vegetable commonly used in salads.  Lettuce has a mild, slightly bitter taste and comes in various types, including butter lettuce, romaine lettuce, and iceberg lettuce. |  例句：I added some lettuce to my sandwich. 我在我的三明治里加了一些生菜。

近义词：salad green, greens | 反义词：无

词源:
The word "lettuce" comes from the Old French word "laitue," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "lactuca."  "Lactuca" is related to the Latin word "lac," meaning "milk," referring to the milky sap found in some lettuce varieties.
