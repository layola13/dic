essayism:/ˈɛseɪɪzəm/| n. | 散文写作；散文风格；随笔集 | The style or practice of writing essays.  It often involves personal reflection, subjective opinions, and informal language.  Essayism can be found in various forms, from personal essays to critical essays. | Example:  His work is characterized by a distinctive essayism, blending personal anecdote with insightful social commentary.  他的作品以独特的散文风格为特色，将个人轶事与深刻的社会评论融为一体。| 近义词：essay writing, personal reflection, informal writing | 反义词：formal writing, objectivity, scientific writing
