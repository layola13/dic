chargrilled:/tʃɑːrɡrɪld/| adj.|炭烤的，烧烤的|Cooked over hot coals until the surface is charred.  It implies a strong smoky flavour and often a slightly burnt exterior.|例句：The chargrilled chicken was delicious. 炭烤鸡肉真好吃。 The chargrilled vegetables had a lovely smoky aroma. 炭烤蔬菜散发着宜人的烟熏香气。|近义词：grilled, barbecued, broiled|反义词：boiled, steamed
