mover:/ˈmuːvər/ | n., v. |搬家公司；搬运工；移动者；促成者 | a person or company that moves furniture and belongings from one place to another; something that causes movement or change |例句：We hired a mover to help us relocate to our new house. 我们雇了一家搬家公司来帮我们搬到新家。  The earth's rotation is a mover of the tides. 地球的自转是导致潮汐变化的因素。|近义词：remover, transporter, instigator |反义词：stayer,  immobile object


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The mover transports furniture carefully. = 搬家公司小心地搬运家具。
He moves the box to the corner. = 他把箱子移到角落里。


一般过去时:
The movers carefully packed all our belongings. = 搬家工人仔细地打包了我们所有的物品。
She moved the table to make more space. = 她移动了桌子腾出更多空间。


现在完成时:
They have moved three times this year. = 他们今年已经搬了三次家了。
The company has moved its headquarters to a new city. = 公司已将其总部迁至新的城市。


过去完成时:
They had moved all the furniture before the party started. = 派对开始前他们已经搬完了所有家具。
The project had moved forward significantly before the funding issues arose. = 在资金问题出现之前，项目已经取得了显著进展。


将来时:
We will move to a new apartment next month. = 我们下个月将搬到新公寓。
The company will move its operations to a more efficient location. = 公司将把业务转移到更高效的地方。


虚拟语气:
If I were a mover, I would be very strong. = 如果我是搬家工人，我会非常强壮。
I wish I had moved sooner. = 我真希望我早点搬家。


被动语态:
The boxes were moved quickly and efficiently. = 箱子被迅速有效地搬运了。
The proposal was moved to the next stage of approval. = 该提案被移交到下一个审批阶段。


疑问句:
Who moved my files? = 谁动了我的文件？
Does this mover offer insurance? = 这家搬家公司提供保险吗？


否定句:
He didn't move the heavy boxes. = 他没有搬那些沉重的箱子。
The project hasn't moved forward as quickly as expected. = 项目进展不如预期快。
