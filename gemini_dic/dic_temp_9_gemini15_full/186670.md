right-clicks:/ˈraɪt klɪks/| v. |右键点击|To click a computer mouse button with the right hand.  This action typically brings up a context menu offering various options related to the selected item.| 例句：Right-click on the icon to open the settings menu.  右键点击图标打开设置菜单。

例句：I right-clicked the document and selected "Print". 我右键点击了文档并选择了“打印”。


近义词：context-click


反义词：left-clicks


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He right-clicks on the image frequently. 他经常右键点击图像。
She right-clicks to access the shortcut menu. 她右键点击以访问快捷菜单。


一般过去时:
I right-clicked the file and deleted it. 我右键点击了文件并删除了它。
They right-clicked the link and opened it in a new tab. 他们右键点击了链接并在新标签页中打开了它。


现在完成时:
He has right-clicked many times today. 他今天已经右键点击了很多次了。
She has right-clicked that icon several times without success. 她已经多次右键点击该图标，但没有成功。


过去完成时:
I had right-clicked the button before realizing it was disabled. 我在意识到按钮被禁用之前已经右键点击了它。
They had right-clicked the folder multiple times before finding the correct option. 他们在找到正确的选项之前已经多次右键点击了文件夹。


将来时:
I will right-click the icon to see the options. 我将右键点击图标查看选项。
She will right-click the file and save it as a PDF. 她将右键点击文件并将其另存为PDF。


虚拟语气:
If I right-clicked, I would see a context menu. 如果我右键点击，我会看到一个上下文菜单。
I wish I had right-clicked before deleting that file. 我希望我在删除那个文件之前已经右键点击了它。


被动语态:
The icon should be right-clicked to access the menu. 应该右键点击图标以访问菜单。
The file was right-clicked and then copied. 文件被右键点击然后复制。


疑问句:
Did you right-click before selecting "Delete"? 你在选择“删除”之前右键点击了吗？
Should I right-click this button? 我应该右键点击这个按钮吗？


否定句:
I didn't right-click; I just double-clicked. 我没有右键点击；我只是双击了。
She didn't right-click the link, so she couldn't open it in a new tab. 她没有右键点击链接，所以她无法在新标签页中打开它。
