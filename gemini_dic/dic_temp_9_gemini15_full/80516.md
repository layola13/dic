good-tempered: /ˈɡʊdˈtɛmpərd/ | adj. |脾气好的，性情温和的 | Having a pleasant and patient disposition; not easily angered.  |例句：He's a good-tempered dog, always happy to see people. 他是一只脾气很好的狗，总是很高兴见到人。 |例句：She remained good-tempered despite the long wait. 尽管等待时间很长，她仍然保持着好脾气。|近义词：even-tempered, amiable, mild-mannered, affable |反义词：bad-tempered, irritable, grumpy, choleric
