punish:/ˈpʌnɪʃ/| vt.|惩罚，处罚|To inflict a penalty on (someone) for a crime, offence, or fault.|例句：The judge punished the criminal with a prison sentence.  法官判处罪犯监禁。|例句：He was punished for cheating on the exam. 他因为考试作弊受到了惩罚。|近义词：penalize, chastise, discipline|反义词：reward, pardon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school punishes students who cheat. = 学校惩罚作弊的学生。
They punish wrongdoing severely. = 他们严厉惩罚不法行为。

一般过去时:
The teacher punished him for talking in class. = 老师因为他上课讲话而惩罚了他。
My parents punished me for breaking the vase. = 因为打破了花瓶，我的父母惩罚了我。

现在完成时:
The court has punished him for his crimes. = 法院已经因为他犯下的罪行而惩罚了他。
She has been punished repeatedly for her bad behavior. = 她因为不良行为一再受到惩罚。

过去完成时:
He had already been punished for similar offenses. = 他因为类似的罪行已经被惩罚过了。
They had punished him before, but he continued to misbehave. = 他们以前惩罚过他，但他继续行为不端。

将来时:
The judge will punish the defendant harshly. = 法官将严厉惩罚被告。
The company will punish employees who violate the rules. = 公司将惩罚违反规定的员工。

虚拟语气:
If he had studied harder, he wouldn't have been punished. = 如果他学习更努力，他就不会受到惩罚了。
I wish I hadn't been punished for that mistake. = 我真希望我没有因为那个错误而受到惩罚。

被动语态:
The criminal was punished for his actions. = 罪犯因其行为而受到惩罚。
The students are punished for disrupting the class. = 学生们因为扰乱课堂而受到惩罚。

疑问句:
Will he be punished for his involvement? = 他会因为牵涉其中而受到惩罚吗？
How will they punish the offenders? = 他们将如何惩罚违规者？

否定句:
The court did not punish the defendant. = 法院没有惩罚被告。
They will not punish him without evidence. = 没有证据，他们不会惩罚他。


PHRASE->
punish severely = 严厉惩罚
punish harshly = 严惩
punish unjustly = 不公正地惩罚
punish somebody for something = 因某事惩罚某人
capital punishment = 死刑
