comminatory:/ˈkɒmɪnətəri/| adj.|警告的；威胁的；威吓的 |Serving to warn or threaten; expressing a threat or warning.  The comminatory tone of the letter left no doubt about the sender's intentions. |例句：His comminatory stare silenced the unruly children. 他威胁性的凝视使那些不守规矩的孩子安静下来。
例句：The judge delivered a comminatory speech to the convicted criminal. 法官对被判有罪的罪犯发表了一篇警告性讲话。|近义词：threatening, menacing, admonitory, cautionary |反义词：approving, encouraging, comforting, soothing |词性：形容词

词源:
