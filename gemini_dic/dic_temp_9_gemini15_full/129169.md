noachian:/noʊˈeɪʃən/| adj.|诺亚纪的，早期的|Relating to the Noachian period on Mars, a period of early Martian history characterized by significant geological activity and the possible presence of liquid water.  The term is used in planetary science, specifically in the context of Martian geology.|Example:  The discovery of these layered sediments suggests a Noachian age for this Martian region.  这个分层沉积物的发现表明这个火星区域属于诺亚纪。 |近义词：early Martian, ancient Martian|反义词：Amazonian, Hesperian
