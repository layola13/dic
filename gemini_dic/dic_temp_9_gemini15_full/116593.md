matriculated:/məˈtrɪkjuleɪtɪd/ | v. |正式入学；注册入学 | To be officially enrolled or admitted as a student at a college or university.  |例句：He matriculated at Oxford University in 1998. 他于1998年在牛津大学入学。|例句：After matriculating, she immediately joined the debate team. 入学后，她立即加入了辩论队。|近义词：enrolled, registered, admitted, entered |反义词：withdrew, dropped out


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He matriculates at Harvard this fall. = 他今年秋天在哈佛大学入学。
She matriculates at a prestigious university. = 她在一所名牌大学入学。

一般过去时:
I matriculated at the University of California, Berkeley, in 2010. = 我于2010年在加州大学伯克利分校入学。
They matriculated at different universities. = 他们就读于不同的大学。

现在完成时:
She has matriculated at law school. = 她已进入法学院学习。
He has matriculated and is now a sophomore. = 他已经入学，现在是大二学生。

过去完成时:
Before he matriculated, he had completed his preparatory studies. = 在他入学之前，他已经完成了预科学习。
By the time she matriculated, she had already learned to play the piano. = 等她入学时，她已经学会弹钢琴了。

将来时:
I will matriculate at Stanford next year. = 我明年将在斯坦福大学入学。
She will matriculate at the university after graduating from high school. = 她高中毕业后将在大学入学。

虚拟语气:
If I had matriculated earlier, I would have graduated by now. = 如果我早点入学，我现在就毕业了。
I wish I had matriculated at a different university. = 我真希望我在另一所大学入学。

被动语态:
He was matriculated into the prestigious university. = 他被这所名牌大学录取了。
Many students were matriculated at the college last year. = 去年许多学生被这所大学录取。

疑问句:
When did he matriculate? = 他是什么时候入学的？
Did she matriculate at that university? = 她在那所大学入学了吗？

否定句:
He did not matriculate at that university. = 他没有在那所大学入学。
She did not matriculate until the following year. = 直到第二年她才入学。
