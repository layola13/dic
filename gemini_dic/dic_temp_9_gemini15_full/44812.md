crusading:/kruˈseɪdɪŋ/| v. adj. n.|十字军东征；积极从事；宣传运动；|Engaging in or resembling a crusade; characterized by zealous advocacy of a cause.  A crusade is a religious or moral campaign, often involving fervent advocacy and sometimes aggressive action.|例句：His crusading spirit led him to fight for social justice.他的改革精神促使他为社会正义而斗争。 The crusading journalist exposed the corruption.这位积极的记者揭露了腐败行为。  |近义词：campaigning, advocating, fighting for |反义词：passivity, indifference, apathy


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is crusading against poverty. = 他正在积极从事反贫困运动。
They are crusading for environmental protection. = 他们正在为环境保护而奔走。


一般过去时:
She crusaded for women's rights. = 她为妇女权利而奋斗。
They crusaded against injustice. = 他们反对不公正。


现在完成时:
He has been crusading for years against child labor. = 他多年来一直在积极从事反对童工的运动。
She has crusaded tirelessly for animal welfare. = 她为动物福利不知疲倦地奔走。


过去完成时:
They had crusaded for decades before seeing any real progress. = 在看到任何真正的进展之前，他们已经奋斗了几十年。
He had crusaded against the war before it even began. = 在战争开始之前，他就已经反对这场战争了。


将来时:
She will crusade for better education in the coming years. = 未来几年，她将为改善教育而奋斗。
They will continue crusading for peace. = 他们将继续为和平而奋斗。


虚拟语气:
If he were crusading for a good cause, I would support him. = 如果他为正义事业而奋斗，我会支持他。
I wish she were crusading against corruption more actively. = 我希望她更积极地打击腐败。


被动语态:
The issue was crusaded for by many activists. = 许多活动家为这个问题而奋斗。
The cause is being crusaded for by a growing number of people. = 越来越多的人正在为这个事业而奋斗。


疑问句:
Is he crusading for a specific cause? = 他正在为某个特定的事业而奋斗吗？
Are they crusading against government corruption? = 他们正在反对政府腐败吗？


否定句:
He is not crusading for any political party. = 他没有为任何政党而奋斗。
They are not crusading against this particular issue. = 他们没有针对这个问题而奋斗。


PHRASE->
crusading journalist = 积极的记者
crusading spirit = 改革精神
crusading zeal = 积极的热情
a crusading campaign = 一场积极的运动
a crusading lawyer = 一位积极的律师
