go-to:/ˈɡoʊ tuː/| adj.|首选的；最常用的；最佳的|Something that is a go-to is your first choice or what you rely on most in a particular situation.  It's a reliable and effective solution or option.|例句：This recipe is my go-to for a quick weeknight dinner.  这个食谱是我快速准备工作日晚餐的首选。|例句：For quick fixes, she always has a go-to solution.  对于快速解决问题，她总是有一个最佳方案。|近义词：preferred, favorite, default, best, primary|反义词：least favorite, worst, secondary


USAGE->

This restaurant is my go-to place for Italian food. = 这家餐厅是我吃意大利菜的首选。
My go-to strategy for exams is to start early. = 我考试的首选策略是尽早开始。
