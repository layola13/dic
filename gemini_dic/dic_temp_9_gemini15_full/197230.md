sharedness:/ʃeədnəs/| n. |共享性，共有性 |The state of being shared; the quality of being common to or participated in by two or more individuals or entities.  It often refers to resources, experiences, or ideas.  |例句：The sharedness of their experiences strengthened their bond.  他们的共同经历加强了他们的联系。
The project’s success depended on the sharedness of resources among the team members. 项目的成功取决于团队成员之间资源的共享。|近义词：commonality, community, mutuality |反义词：exclusivity, privacy, individuality |
