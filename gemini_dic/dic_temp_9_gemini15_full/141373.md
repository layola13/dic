operator:/ˈɒpəreɪtə(r)/, /ˈɑːpəreɪtər/| n.  | 操作员；经营者；运算符 | A person who operates a machine or system; a person who runs a business; a symbol or character that performs a specific operation in a computer program or mathematical expression. | 例句：The machine operator skillfully controlled the complex machinery.  这位机器操作员熟练地控制着复杂的机器。  | 例句：The restaurant operator expanded his business to three locations.  这位餐馆经营者将其生意扩展到了三个地点。 | 近义词：worker, manager, function, symbol | 反义词：user (in some contexts), inactive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The operator controls the machinery. = 操作员控制机器。
She operates the business successfully. = 她成功地经营着这家企业。

一般过去时:
The operator repaired the machine yesterday. = 操作员昨天修理了机器。
He operated the crane for many years. = 他操作起重机很多年了。

现在完成时:
The operator has completed the task. = 操作员已完成了任务。
She has operated this business for ten years. = 她经营这家企业十年了。

过去完成时:
The operator had already finished before the manager arrived. = 在经理到达之前，操作员已经完成了。
He had operated the machine for hours before it broke down. = 机器出故障之前他已经操作了几个小时。

将来时:
The operator will start the machine soon. = 操作员很快就会启动机器。
She will operate the new equipment next week. = 她下周将操作新设备。

虚拟语气:
If the operator were more careful, the accident wouldn't have happened. = 如果操作员更小心一些，事故就不会发生了。
I wish the operator had been more experienced. = 我希望操作员更有经验。

被动语态:
The machine is operated by a skilled operator. = 这台机器由一名熟练的操作员操作。
The business was operated by her father for many years. = 这家企业由她父亲经营了很多年。

疑问句:
Who is the operator of this machine? = 这台机器的操作员是谁？
Who operates this business? = 谁经营这家企业？


否定句:
The operator did not follow the instructions. = 操作员没有按照指示操作。
She doesn't operate that type of equipment. = 她不操作那种类型的设备。

词源:
The word "operator" comes from the Latin word "operari," meaning "to work."  It entered English in the 16th century and its meaning has evolved to encompass various roles involving working or managing something, from machines to businesses.
