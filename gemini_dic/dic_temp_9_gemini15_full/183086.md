repopulated:/ˌriːˈpɒpjuleɪtɪd/ | v. |重新居住；重新填充；重新繁殖 | To populate again; to re-inhabit.  The village was repopulated after the war.  | The town was repopulated after the flood.  |近义词：replenish, restock | 反义词：depopulate, evacuate
