epidemiology:/ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiˈɒlədʒi/ | n. | 流行病学 | The study of the distribution and determinants of health-related states or events in specified populations, and the application of this study to the control of health problems.  | Example:  The epidemiology of influenza is complex, varying by season and geographic location. 流行性感冒的流行病学很复杂，会因季节和地理位置而异。 |近义词:  public health, disease surveillance | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，但可考虑与个体医学相对)

词源:

The word "epidemiology" comes from the Greek words:

* **epi-:** upon, on, among
* **demos:** people, population
* **-logia:** study of

Therefore, literally translated, "epidemiology" means "the study of what is upon the people," referring to the study of disease patterns within populations.
