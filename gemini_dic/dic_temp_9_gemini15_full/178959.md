redecorating:/ˌriːˈdɛkəreɪtɪŋ/| v.|重新装饰，重新装修|The act of decorating a space again, often involving changes to the existing décor.|例句：We are redecorating the living room this weekend.  我们这个周末正在重新装修客厅。|例句：They're redecorating the entire house to make it more modern.他们正在重新装修整个房子，使其更现代化。|近义词：renovating, refurbishing|反义词：deteriorating, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are redecorating their bedroom. = 他们正在重新装修他们的卧室。
We redecorate our home every few years. = 我们每隔几年重新装修一次家。

一般过去时:
She redecorated the kitchen last month. = 她上个月重新装修了厨房。
They redecorated the office before the new employees arrived. = 新员工来之前，他们重新装修了办公室。

现在完成时:
He has redecorated his apartment since moving in. = 他搬进来后就重新装修了他的公寓。
We have redecorated the bathroom twice this year. = 今年我们已经两次重新装修了浴室。

过去完成时:
They had redecorated the house before they put it on the market. = 他们在把房子上市之前已经重新装修过了。
She had redecorated the living room several times before she found the perfect style. = 在找到完美的风格之前，她已经多次重新装修了客厅。


将来时:
We will redecorate the dining room next spring. = 我们明年春天将重新装修餐厅。
They are going to redecorate the entire house next year. = 他们打算明年重新装修整栋房子。


虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would redecorate the entire house. = 如果我们有更多钱，我们会重新装修整个房子。
I wish we could redecorate the bathroom, but we don't have the time. = 我希望我们可以重新装修浴室，但是我们没有时间。


被动语态:
The living room is being redecorated at the moment. = 客厅现在正在重新装修。
The office was redecorated last year. = 办公室去年重新装修了。


疑问句:
Are you redecorating your house? = 你正在重新装修你的房子吗？
When are they going to redecorate the bedroom? = 他们什么时候要重新装修卧室？


否定句:
We are not redecorating the kitchen this year. = 今年我们不重新装修厨房。
They did not redecorate the hallway. = 他们没有重新装修走廊。
