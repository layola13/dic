predissolution: /ˌpriːdɪs əˈluːʃən/ | n. | 解散前夕；预先解散；提前解散 | The state or process of being about to dissolve or be dissolved;  the period immediately preceding dissolution.  It often refers to the period before a company, organization, or partnership is officially dissolved. | Example: The company's predissolution activities included selling off assets and paying outstanding debts. 公司在解散前夕的活动包括出售资产和偿还未清债务。| 近义词：pre-liquidation, winding-up (process),  disintegration (process)| 反义词：formation, establishment, inception
