cockroach: /ˈkɑːkroʊtʃ/ /ˈkɒkrəʊtʃ/ | n. |蟑螂| A cockroach is a common household pest, known for its ability to survive in unsanitary conditions.  They are typically brown or black and have long antennae. | 例句：The cockroach scurried across the kitchen floor. 这只蟑螂在厨房的地板上飞快地爬行。

例句：We found a cockroach in the pantry. 我们在储藏室里发现了一只蟑螂。


近义词：pest, insect, bug

反义词：无


词源:  The word "cockroach" has a complex etymology. It ultimately derives from the Spanish word *cucaracha*, which itself has uncertain origins, possibly from a combination of roots meaning "to run" or "to be quick". The word's journey into English reflects the historical spread of cockroaches and the languages of those who encountered them.
