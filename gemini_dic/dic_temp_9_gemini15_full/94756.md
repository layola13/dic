improvisatrice:/ˌɪmprɒvɪzəˈtriːs/ | n. |即兴表演的女演员；即兴创作的女性 | A woman who improvises, especially one who performs impromptu musical compositions or dramatic pieces.  An improvisatrice is a female performer who creates and performs spontaneously, often in music, theatre, or comedy.  | Example: The concert featured a brilliant improvisatrice who captivated the audience with her spontaneous melodies.  这场音乐会有一位才华横溢的即兴表演女演员，她用自发的旋律迷住了观众。 |近义词: improviser, performer, artist |反义词:  planner, pre-meditator
