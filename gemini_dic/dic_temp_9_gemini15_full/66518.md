exhales:/ɪkˈseɪlz/ | vt. |呼气；发出（气体）；流露|To breathe out; to emit (a gas); to reveal or express gradually.|例句：He exhales slowly, releasing the tension in his shoulders. 他慢慢地呼气，放松了肩膀的紧张。|例句：The volcano exhales a plume of smoke.  火山喷出一股烟柱。|近义词：breathe out, emit, vent|反义词：inhales


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exhales deeply before he speaks. = 他在说话之前深深地呼了一口气。
The plant exhales oxygen during photosynthesis. = 植物在光合作用过程中释放氧气。

一般过去时:
She exhaled sharply, surprised by the news. = 她听到这个消息后猛地吸了一口气。
The factory exhaled a cloud of black smoke. = 工厂排出滚滚黑烟。

现在完成时:
The volcano has exhaled ash for days. = 火山已经喷发了几天火山灰了。
He has exhaled a sigh of relief. = 他长舒了一口气，如释重负。

过去完成时:
She had exhaled all the air from her lungs before she spoke. = 她说话前已经把肺里的空气都呼出去了。
The engine had exhaled a plume of steam before it sputtered and died. = 发动机在发出嘶嘶声并熄火之前喷出一股蒸汽。

将来时:
He will exhale the smoke from his cigarette. = 他将把香烟的烟雾呼出。
The machine will exhale compressed air to power the tool. = 机器将排出压缩空气来驱动工具。

虚拟语气:
If he exhaled more slowly, he might feel calmer. = 如果他呼气慢一点，可能会感觉平静一些。
I wish I could exhale all my stress. = 我希望我能释放所有的压力。

被动语态:
The scent was exhaled from the flower. = 花香散发出来。
(Note:  The passive voice is less common with this verb)

疑问句:
Does he exhale smoke frequently? = 他经常吸烟吗？
Does the machine exhale any harmful gases? = 这台机器排放任何有害气体吗？

否定句:
He didn't exhale until he was sure the coast was clear. = 直到他确定安全了才呼气。
The engine didn't exhale any smoke after the repair. = 修理后发动机没有排放任何烟雾。


PHRASE->

exhale relief = 如释重负
exhale frustration = 吐出怨气
