mangles:/ˈmæŋɡlz/| v.| 毁坏；严重损坏；篡改；(尤指肢体)严重受伤|To severely damage or mutilate something, especially by tearing or crushing; to alter (something written or spoken) so as to make it inaccurate or unintelligible. |例句：The car was mangled in the accident.  这辆车在事故中严重损坏。

The storm mangled the trees.  暴风雨摧毁了树木。


近义词：destroy, ruin, mutilate, deface, distort |反义词：repair, restore, improve
