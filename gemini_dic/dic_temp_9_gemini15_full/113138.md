lunkhead:/ˈlʌŋkˌhed/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A lunkhead is a stupid or clumsy person.  This word is informal and often used humorously or with a degree of affection.|例句：He's such a lunkhead; he tripped over the cat again. 他真是个笨蛋，他又绊倒了猫。|例句：Don't be such a lunkhead, just follow the instructions.别那么傻，跟着说明做就行了。|近义词：idiot, fool, oaf, dunce|反义词：genius, intellect, mastermind|名词
