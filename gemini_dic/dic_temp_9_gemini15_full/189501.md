salability:/ˌseɪləˈbɪləti/| n. |适销性，销路，销售能力 |The ability of a product or service to be sold easily.  It often depends on factors such as price, quality, marketing, and consumer demand. |例句：The high salability of the new phone model surprised everyone.  这款新手机型号的高销路让所有人都感到惊讶。

例句：The product's poor design significantly impacted its salability. 产品设计糟糕严重影响了它的销路。


近义词：marketability, vendibility, sales appeal

反义词：unsalability


词性:名词

词源:
The word "salability" is derived from the verb "sell"  + "-able" (suffix indicating capability) + "-ity" (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  It essentially means "the quality of being able to be sold."
