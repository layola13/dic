pyne:/paɪn/| n.|松树；松木|Pine is a type of evergreen tree. 松树是一种常绿树。|例句：The pine trees swayed gently in the breeze. 松树在微风中轻轻摇曳。|近义词：fir, cedar|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pine tree stands tall. = 这棵松树高耸入云。
We plant pine trees every year. = 我们每年都种松树。


一般过去时:
The old pine tree fell during the storm. = 那棵老松树在暴风雨中倒了。
They cut down many pine trees for lumber. = 他们砍伐了许多松树做木材。


现在完成时:
We have planted many pine trees in this area. = 我们在这个地区种了许多松树。
The wind has damaged several pine trees. = 风刮坏了幾棵松樹。


过去完成时:
The pine trees had already lost their needles before the winter. = 在冬天来临之前，松树已经落针了。
They had harvested the pine nuts before the squirrels got to them. = 在松鼠吃到松果之前，他们已经收割了松果。


将来时:
We will plant more pine trees next spring. = 我们明年春天将种植更多松树。
The pine trees will grow taller over time. = 松树会随着时间的推移而长得更高。


虚拟语气:
If it were a pine tree, it would have needles. = 如果那是棵松树，它就会有松针。
I wish I had a pine tree in my backyard. = 我希望我的后院有一棵松树。


被动语态:
The pine trees were planted by the volunteers. = 松树是由志愿者种植的。
The pine wood is being used to build the house. = 松木正在被用来建造房子。


疑问句:
Is that a pine tree? = 那是松树吗？
What kind of pine trees are these? = 这些是什么种类的松树？


否定句:
That isn't a pine tree; it's a fir tree. = 那不是松树；那是冷杉树。
We didn't plant any pine trees this year. = 我们今年没有种松树。
