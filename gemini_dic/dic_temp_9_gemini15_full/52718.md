digynous:/ˈdaɪdʒɪnəs/| adj. |具有两个雌蕊的；双雌蕊的 |Having two pistils.  The term refers to a flower structure with two carpels, which are the female reproductive organs in flowering plants.  These carpels may be fused or separate. | Example:  The digynous flower had two distinct pistils.  这朵双雌蕊的花有两个明显的雌蕊。|近义词: bicarpellate |反义词: monogynous
