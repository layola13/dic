carryall: /ˈkæriɔːl/ | n. | 大包，旅行袋 | A large bag or container used for carrying many things.  It often implies a lack of organization or a capacity for holding miscellaneous items. | Example: She threw everything she needed for the trip into her carryall. 她把旅行所需的一切都扔进了她的大包里。 | 近义词：tote bag, duffel bag, knapsack | 反义词：small bag, purse
