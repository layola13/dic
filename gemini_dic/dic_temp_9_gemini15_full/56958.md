douar:/duˈɑːr/| n.|村庄，小村落，部落|A douar is a small village or hamlet, often used in the context of North African cultures, particularly in Morocco.  It typically refers to a small, relatively isolated settlement, often associated with a specific tribe or clan.|Example:  The douar was nestled in a valley, surrounded by olive groves.  这个村庄坐落在一个山谷里，周围环绕着橄榄树林。|近义词：village, hamlet, settlement|反义词：city, metropolis|名词
