reinstations:/ˌriːɪnˌsteɪʃənz/| n.|恢复职位；重新启用；恢复原状 |The act of reinstating someone or something; the state of being reinstated.  It implies a return to a previous state or position after a period of interruption or removal. |例句：The company announced the reinstatement of its former CEO. 公司宣布恢复其前任首席执行官的职务。

例句：After a thorough investigation, the professor's reinstatement to the university was approved.  经过彻底调查后，教授被批准恢复大学职务。


近义词：restoration, resumption, reinstallation, reinstatement


反义词：removal, dismissal, termination, abolishment
