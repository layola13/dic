role-plays:/ˈroʊlˌpleɪz/| n.|角色扮演；角色扮演游戏|Activities in which people act out different roles, often for practice or training.  It can refer to a single instance or a series of instances.|例句：The teacher used role-plays to help students understand different perspectives.  老师用角色扮演来帮助学生理解不同的观点。

例句：The actors spent weeks rehearsing their role-plays before the opening night.  演员们在开幕式前花了几个星期排练他们的角色扮演。


近义词：dramatizations, simulations, enactments |反义词：None directly applicable.  |词性：名词
