quamash:/ˈkwɑːmæʃ/| n. |北美野百合的球茎；食用植物|A type of edible plant bulb, specifically the Camassia genus, native to North America.  The bulbs were a staple food for many indigenous peoples. |The indigenous people relied heavily on quamash as a food source.  =当地居民严重依赖北美野百合作为食物来源。|  The sweetness of the cooked quamash is reminiscent of chestnuts. =煮熟的北美野百合球茎尝起来像栗子。|近义词：camas bulb, camas lily (referring to the plant)|反义词：无
