gawking:/ˈɡɔːkɪŋ/| v.|呆瞪着；傻看着|To stare openly and stupidly.|The tourists were gawking at the Mona Lisa.|近义词：staring, leering, ogling|反义词：ignoring, overlooking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gawks at the television all evening. = 他整晚都呆呆地盯着电视看。
They gawk at the accident scene. = 他们呆呆地盯着事故现场看。

We gawked at the beautiful sunset. = 我们呆呆地望着美丽的日落。
