lilted:/ˈlɪltɪd/| adj. |轻快活泼的，有节奏的，轻柔起伏的 | Having a light, cheerful, or melodious quality; characterized by a rising and falling intonation.  |例句：Her voice lilted with excitement as she described her trip. 她兴奋地描述着她的旅行，声音轻快活泼。

例句：The lilted melody of the song was instantly catchy. 这首歌轻快活泼的旋律立刻就让人上口了。


近义词：melodious, buoyant, rhythmic, tripping


反义词：monotonous, flat, dull, heavy


词源:  From the verb "lilt," which ultimately derives from Old Norse *ljótr ("sound, tone") and related to Old English *hlūdan ("to make a noise"). The sense of a light, cheerful quality developed over time.
