flavaniline:/ˌflævəˈnɪliːn/| n. |黄酮类碱|A heterocyclic organic compound, a derivative of quinoline, that is used in the synthesis of dyes and pharmaceuticals. It's characterized by a specific arrangement of atoms in its molecular structure, containing both a benzene ring and a pyridine ring fused together.|Flavaniline is used as an intermediate in the production of certain dyes.|近义词: (缺乏直接近义词，因为其化学性质独特)|反义词: (缺乏直接反义词)|名词
