repaved:/ˌriːˈpeɪvd/| v. |重新铺设，修复路面 |To pave (a road, etc.) again.  It implies that the surface had been previously paved and is now being repaved, often due to wear and tear or damage.| The city repaved Main Street last year.  去年市里重新铺设了主街的路面。|The old highway has been repaved.|这条旧高速公路已经重新铺设了。|近义词：resurface, repair, reconstruct|反义词：destroy, ruin


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They repave the roads every few years. = 他们每隔几年重新铺路。
The workers repave the driveway carefully. = 工人们仔细地重新铺设了车道。

一般过去时:
They repaved the street after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，他们重新铺设了街道。
