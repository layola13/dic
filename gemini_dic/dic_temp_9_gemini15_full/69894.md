fertilising: /ˈfɜːrtəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |施肥；使肥沃；受精 | The act of making something fertile, typically land or soil, by adding nutrients.  It can also refer to the fertilization of an egg by sperm. |

例句：
Farmers are fertilising their fields to increase crop yields. = 农民们正在给田地施肥以提高作物产量。

The process of fertilising an egg involves the fusion of sperm and egg. = 卵子受精的过程涉及精子和卵子的融合。


近义词：enriching, manuring, inseminating

反义词：depleting, sterilizing
