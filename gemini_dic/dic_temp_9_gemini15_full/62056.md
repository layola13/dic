encase:/ɪnˈkeɪs/| vt.|包住；包围；装入；封闭|To surround or enclose completely.|The museum encased the ancient artifact in a protective case.  博物馆用保护壳将古代文物包裹起来。|The building was encased in scaffolding. 这栋建筑被脚手架包围着。|envelop, enclose, sheathe|open, expose|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He encases the fragile vase in bubble wrap. 他用气泡膜包裹着易碎的花瓶。
They encase the wires in protective tubing. 他们把电线装进保护管里。
