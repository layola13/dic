sickos:/ˈsɪkoʊz/| n. |变态，精神病患者，怪人 |A term used informally to refer to people considered mentally unstable or exhibiting disturbing behavior.  It's often used derisively or with disgust. The term carries a strong negative connotation and should be used cautiously. |例句：He called those who vandalized the park sickos. 他称那些破坏公园的人是变态。

例句：Some people consider those who enjoy extreme horror movies sickos. 有些人认为喜欢极端恐怖电影的人是变态。


近义词：perverts, weirdos, lunatics, psychos, maniacs |反义词:sane, normal, healthy
