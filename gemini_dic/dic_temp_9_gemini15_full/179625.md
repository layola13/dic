reencouragement:/ˌriːɪnˈkʌrɪdʒmənt/| n. |重新鼓励；再次鼓励|The act of encouraging someone again, after a period of discouragement or setback.  It implies a renewed effort to inspire confidence or hope.|例句：After the initial failure, the team received reencouragement from their coach.  = 首次失败后，球队得到了教练的再次鼓励。


例句：Her reencouragement helped him overcome his fear and try again. = 她的再次鼓励帮助他克服了恐惧并再次尝试。


近义词：reinforcement, fresh encouragement, renewed support|反义词：discouragement, dispiritedness|词性：名词
