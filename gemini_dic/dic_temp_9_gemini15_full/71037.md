firring:/ˈfɜːrɪŋ/ | n. | 嵌条，衬条，填隙物 | Thin strips of wood used to level a surface or fill gaps before applying a finish.  Firring strips are commonly used in construction to create a level surface for flooring, walls, or ceilings. | Example: The carpenter used firring strips to level the uneven wall before installing the drywall.  木匠在安装石膏板之前用嵌条来找平不平整的墙壁。| 近义词：shim, batten | 反义词：None specifically.
