go-one-better:/ɡoʊ wʌn ˈbɛtər/|/ˈɡəʊ wʌn ˈbɛtə(r)/| phrasal v.|更胜一筹；做得更好；超过|To surpass or exceed someone else's achievement or offering.|例句：He offered me £50, but I went one better and offered him £60. 他给我出价50英镑，但我更胜一筹，给他出价60英镑。|例句：After she baked a delicious cake, her sister went one better by creating an amazing chocolate sculpture. 在她烤了一个美味的蛋糕后，她姐姐更胜一筹，创作了一个令人惊叹的巧克力雕塑。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, better|反义词：underperform, fail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They go one better every time. = 他们每次都更胜一筹。
We try to go one better than our competitors. = 我们努力做得比竞争对手更好。

一般过去时:
He went one better and won the race. = 他更胜一筹，赢得了比赛。
She went one better by adding a beautiful ribbon. = 她更胜一筹，加了一条漂亮的丝带。

现在完成时:
They have gone one better this year. = 他们今年更胜一筹。
We have gone one better with our new product. = 我们用我们的新产品更胜一筹。

过去完成时:
He had gone one better and secured the contract. = 他已经更胜一筹并获得了合同。
She had gone one better in the second round. = 在第二轮比赛中，她更胜一筹。

将来时:
They will go one better next time. = 他们下次会更胜一筹。
We will go one better with our next presentation. = 我们将在下一次演示中更胜一筹。
