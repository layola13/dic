/ˈneɪstiɪst/| adj.|最讨厌的；最恶劣的；最肮脏的|Most unpleasant, repulsive, or offensive.  It is the superlative form of "nasty".|例句：That's the nastiest thing I've ever heard.  那是听过最讨厌的事情。

例句：The smell from the garbage was absolutely nasty. 垃圾散发出的气味极其难闻。


近义词：disgusting, repulsive, vile, unpleasant, offensive, hateful, abhorrent|反义词：pleasant, delightful, agreeable, lovely
