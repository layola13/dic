sicker:/ˈsɪkər/| adj.|更病的；更恶心的；更严重的|More ill; more nauseated; more severe.  The comparative form of "sick." It indicates a greater degree of illness or sickness.|例句：He felt sicker than he had ever felt before. 他感觉比以往任何时候都更难受。

例句：The situation is becoming sicker by the day. 情况日益恶化。

近义词：more unwell, more diseased, more ailing

反义词：healthier

词性：形容词
