lachrymation:/ˌlækrɪˈmeɪʃən/| n. |流泪；哭泣|The secretion of tears.  It can be a normal physiological response or a symptom of various conditions.|例句：Excessive lachrymation can be a sign of eye irritation.  过度的流泪可能是眼睛刺激的迹象。

例句：The child's lachrymation was a clear indication of his sadness.  孩子的哭泣清楚地表明了他的悲伤。


近义词：weeping, crying, tearfulness

反义词：dryness (of eyes)


词性: 名词


词源:  From Late Latin *lachrymatio, from Latin lacrima "tear".  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Indo-European root *dʰeh₂- "to suck, to milk," referring to the secretion of tears.
