defends:/dɪˈfɛndz/ | vt. |保卫；辩护；为…辩解 |To protect someone or something from attack or harm; to speak or argue in favor of someone or something. |例句：The soldier bravely defends his country.  士兵勇敢地保卫他的国家。

The lawyer defends his client against the charges. 律师为他的当事人辩护，以对抗指控。|近义词：protect, safeguard, uphold, champion |反义词：attack, assail, betray


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He defends his ideas passionately. = 他热情地捍卫他的思想。
The army defends the border. = 军队保卫边境。


一般过去时:
She defended herself against the accusations. = 她为自己辩驳那些指控。
The castle defended the city from invaders. = 城堡保卫城市免受侵略者侵害。


现在完成时:
He has defended his title for three years. = 他已经卫冕三年了。
The organization has defended the rights of the marginalized. = 该组织一直捍卫弱势群体的权利。


过去完成时:
They had defended their position for hours before surrendering. = 在投降之前，他们已经坚守阵地数小时了。
The lawyer had defended many similar cases before. = 这位律师之前曾处理过许多类似的案件。


将来时:
The government will defend its policies in parliament. = 政府将在议会中捍卫其政策。
We will defend our home against any intruders. = 我们将保卫我们的家园免受任何入侵者的侵害。


虚拟语气:
If he defended himself better, he might have won the case. = 如果他更好地为自己辩护，他或许就能赢得官司。
I wish I had defended my position more strongly. = 我真希望我当时能更强有力地捍卫自己的立场。


被动语态:
The city was defended by a strong army. = 这个城市由一支强大的军队保卫。
His reputation was fiercely defended by his colleagues. = 他的声誉受到同事们的强烈捍卫。


疑问句:
How will you defend your actions? = 你将如何为你的行为辩护？
Does the company defend its products against customer complaints? = 公司是否会为其产品应对客户投诉？


否定句:
He did not defend himself adequately. = 他没有充分为自己辩护。
The government failed to defend the country's interests. = 政府未能捍卫国家的利益。


PHRASE->
defend against = 防御…
defend oneself = 自卫
defend a thesis = 论文答辩
defend one's honor = 保卫自己的名誉
defend the faith = 保卫信仰
