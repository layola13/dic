oceanian:/ˌoʊʃiˈeɪniən/| adj. n.|大洋洲的；大洋洲人；大洋洲语言|Of or relating to Oceania or its inhabitants.  Oceania is a region of the world comprising many islands in the central and southwestern Pacific Ocean.|例句：The Oceanic people have a rich cultural heritage. 大洋洲人民拥有丰富的文化遗产。  Many Australian universities offer courses on Oceanian studies. 许多澳大利亚大学开设大洋洲研究课程。|近义词：Pacific Islander (for people), insular|反义词：continental
