renumerate:/rɪˈnjuːməreɪt/| vt.|重新计算；列举；酬谢 |To enumerate something means to list it, item by item.  To remunerate someone means to pay them for their work or services. |例句：The accountant will renumerate the expenses. 会计将重新计算费用。
The report renumerates the various causes of the accident. 报告列举了事故的各种原因。
The company will renumerate him generously for his hard work. 公司将慷慨地报酬他辛勤的工作。 |近义词：list, recount, enumerate, compensate, reward |反义词：underpay, neglect
