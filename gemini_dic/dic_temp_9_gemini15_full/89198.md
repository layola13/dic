homeshoring: /ˈhoʊmˌʃɔːrɪŋ/ | n. |家庭办公；在家办公 |The practice of working from home for a company or organization, often involving outsourcing or offshoring work to individuals in their homes.|例句：Many companies are embracing homeshoring to reduce office costs and offer employees more flexibility. 许多公司正在采用家庭办公来降低办公成本并为员工提供更大的灵活性。|近义词：telecommuting, remote work, working from home |反义词：office work, on-site work
