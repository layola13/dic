engrosses:/ɪnˈɡroʊsɪz/ | v. | 使全神贯注；使埋头苦干；大量购买 | To occupy or absorb someone's attention completely; to occupy oneself fully.  |The intricate puzzle engrossed him for hours.  复杂的谜题让他全神贯注几个小时。|She was so engrossed in her book that she didn't hear the phone ring. 她全神贯注于读书，没有听到电话铃声。 |absorb, preoccupy, captivate |distract, bore |动词


USAGE->

The book engrosses her. = 这本书让她全神贯注。
The project engrosses the team. = 这个项目让团队全神贯注。
