overland:/ˈoʊvərˌlænd/ | adj., adv. |陆路的；经陆路的；|By land; across land.  It describes travel or transportation that occurs on land, as opposed to by sea or air. |例句：The goods were transported overland. 货物经陆路运输。

The explorers journeyed overland across the vast desert. 探险家们穿越广袤的沙漠，进行了陆路旅程。


近义词：terrestrial, by land | 反义词：oversea, by sea, by air | 词性：形容词，副词

词源:
The word "overland" is a compound word formed from "over" and "land."  "Over" indicates movement across or above something, and "land" refers to the earth's surface.  Therefore, "overland" literally means "across the land."  Its usage dates back to at least the 16th century.
