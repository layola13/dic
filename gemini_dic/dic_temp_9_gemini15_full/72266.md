floss-silk: /flɒs sɪlk/ | n. | 粗丝线，粗丝；丝棉 |  A type of silk that is loosely spun and has a somewhat coarse texture.  It is often used in embroidery or other decorative applications. | 例句：The embroidery was done with a lustrous floss-silk.  这幅刺绣是用闪亮的粗丝线绣制的。| 近义词：silk thread, floss | 反义词：fine silk, smooth silk | 名词
