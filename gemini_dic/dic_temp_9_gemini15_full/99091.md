interbreeds:/ˌɪntərˈbriːdz/| v.| 杂交，混种 |To breed (animals or plants of different breeds or species) together.|The farmer interbreeds his cattle to improve the herd's milk production.  这个农民让他的牛杂交以提高牛群的产奶量。|近义词：crossbreed, hybridize |反义词：inbreed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They interbreed their dogs to produce healthier puppies. = 他们让他们的狗杂交以产生更健康的幼犬。
Farmers often interbreed different varieties of wheat. = 农民经常将不同品种的小麦杂交。


一般过去时:
The scientists interbred two different species of flowers. = 科学家将两种不同的花杂交。
He interbred his horses to improve their racing ability. = 他让他的马杂交以提高它们的赛马能力。


现在完成时:
Breeders have interbred many different types of roses. = 育种家已经杂交了许多不同类型的玫瑰。
They have interbred their cats for generations. = 他们世世代代地让他们的猫杂交。


过去完成时:
Before the experiment, they had interbred the mice for several generations. = 在实验之前，他们已经让老鼠杂交了几代。
The researchers had interbred the plants to study their genetic traits. = 研究人员已经杂交了这些植物以研究它们的遗传特性。


将来时:
They will interbreed the sheep to create a new breed. = 他们将让绵羊杂交以创造一个新的品种。
The zoo will interbreed the endangered species to increase their population. = 动物园将让濒危物种杂交以增加它们的数量。


虚拟语气:
If they interbred these two species, they might create a new hybrid. = 如果他们将这两种物种杂交，他们可能会创造一个新的杂交品种。
I wish they had interbred those plants earlier. = 我希望他们早些杂交那些植物。


被动语态:
The plants were interbred to produce disease-resistant strains. = 植物被杂交以产生抗病菌株。
The animals are being interbred in the laboratory. = 动物正在实验室里被杂交。


疑问句:
Do they interbreed these animals regularly? = 他们定期让这些动物杂交吗？
Why are these species being interbred? = 为什么这些物种正在被杂交？


否定句:
They do not interbreed these two species because they are incompatible. = 他们不将这两种物种杂交，因为它们不相容。
The farmer didn't interbreed his cattle this year. = 农民今年没有让他的牛杂交。
