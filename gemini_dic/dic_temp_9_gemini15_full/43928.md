crewing:/ˈkruːɪŋ/| n.|人员配备；船员；工作组|The act of providing a crew for a vessel or aircraft, or the crew itself;  also, the process of working as part of a crew.|例句：The crewing of the ship was completed before departure.  船只在出发前完成了人员配备。
例句：The film crew's crewing was efficient and professional. 电影摄制组的人员配备高效且专业。|近义词：staffing, manning|反义词：disbanding, dismissing|名词
