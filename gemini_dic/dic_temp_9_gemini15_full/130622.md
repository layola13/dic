nonadjudicatively: /ˌnɒnˌædʒuːdɪˈkeɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 非裁判性地，非司法地 | Without a formal judicial process or decision.  This adverb describes actions or processes that occur outside the framework of a court or other formal adjudicative body. It emphasizes the informal or non-legal nature of the procedure. |

例句：

The dispute was settled nonadjudicatively through mediation.  这场纠纷通过调解非司法地解决了。

The parties chose a nonadjudicative approach to resolve their differences. 当事人选择了一种非司法的方法来解决他们的分歧。


近义词：informally, extrajudicially, amicably, unofficially

反义词：judicially, formally, legally


词性：副词
