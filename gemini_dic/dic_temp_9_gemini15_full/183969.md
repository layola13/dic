reshaping:/ˌriːˈʃeɪpɪŋ/| v. |重塑；改造；改变…的形状 |The process of giving something a new form or structure.  It implies a significant alteration, often to improve or modernize. |例句：The company is reshaping its marketing strategy to attract a younger audience.  公司正在重塑其营销策略以吸引更年轻的受众。

The government's new policies are reshaping the educational system. 政府的新政策正在重塑教育体系。


近义词：remodeling, reforming, transforming, restructuring, reinventing |反义词：preserving, maintaining, upholding |词性：动词
