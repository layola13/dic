daisy-chainnable: /ˈdeɪzi tʃeɪnəbəl/ | adj. | 可菊花链连接的 | Capable of being connected in a daisy chain configuration. This term typically refers to devices or components that can be linked together sequentially, like links in a daisy chain, to form a larger system or network.  |  Example:  These sensors are daisy-chainnable, allowing for easy expansion of the monitoring system. 这些传感器可以菊花链连接，从而可以轻松扩展监控系统。 | 近义词: serially connectable, linkable | 反义词: non-connectable
