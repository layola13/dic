mnemonic's: /nɪˈmɒnɪks/ | n. |助记符；记忆术|A mnemonic is a device, such as a pattern of letters, ideas, or associations, which assists in remembering something.  Mnemonics are techniques a person can use to help them improve their ability to remember something.  They are often used to remember information such as names, dates, lists, or speeches.|例句：She used a mnemonic device to remember the names of all the planets.|近义词：memory aid, reminder|反义词：None
