contaminated:/kənˈtæmɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 受污染的；被感染的 |  Made impure or unclean by contact or mixture.  Something contaminated has had harmful substances added to it, making it unsafe or unsuitable for use. | 例句：The river water is contaminated with industrial waste.  这条河水被工业废料污染了。 | 例句：The food was contaminated with bacteria, causing several people to become ill. 食物被细菌污染，导致多人患病。| 近义词：polluted, infected, tainted, soiled | 反义词：pure, clean, unpolluted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water is contaminated. = 水被污染了。
The soil is contaminated with chemicals. = 土壤被化学物质污染了。

一般过去时:
The river was contaminated after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，河水被污染了。
The food was contaminated by a careless worker. = 食物被一个粗心的工人污染了。

现在完成时:
The land has been contaminated for decades. = 这片土地已经被污染了几十年了。
The well has been contaminated by the nearby factory. = 井水已经被附近的工厂污染了。

过去完成时:
The samples had been contaminated before the test. = 样本在测试之前就已经被污染了。
The water had been contaminated long before anyone noticed. = 水在很久以前就被污染了，直到有人才注意到。

将来时:
The air will be contaminated if the factory continues to operate. = 如果工厂继续运营，空气将会被污染。
The site will be contaminated by the spill. = 该地点将因泄漏而被污染。

虚拟语气:
If the water were contaminated, we would have to find a new source. = 如果水被污染了，我们就必须寻找新的水源。
I wish the soil hadn't been contaminated years ago. = 我真希望几年前土壤没有被污染。

被动语态:
The area was contaminated by the nuclear accident. = 该地区因核事故而受到污染。
The food is being contaminated by the insects. = 食物正在被昆虫污染。

疑问句:
Is the water contaminated? = 水被污染了吗？
Has the food been contaminated? = 食物被污染了吗？

否定句:

The product wasn't contaminated during production. = 产品在生产过程中没有被污染。
