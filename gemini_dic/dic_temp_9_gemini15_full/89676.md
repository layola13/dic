hoopoes:/ˈhuːpuːz/| n. | hoopoe（鸟名，戴胜）|A hoopoe is a bird with a distinctive long, slender bill, a crested head, and often colorful plumage.  They are known for their distinctive flight pattern and calls.|例句：We saw a hoopoe in the park today. 今天我们在公园里看到一只戴胜。|例句：The hoopoe's striking crest is one of its most recognizable features. 戴胜最显著的特征之一就是它引人注目的羽冠。|近义词：upupa (scientific name)|反义词：无|名词
