postrevolutionary: /ˌpoʊstrɛvəˈluːʃənəri/ | adj. | 后革命的，革命后的 |  Relating to or occurring after a revolution.  This adjective describes things that happen, exist, or are created after a significant societal or political upheaval. |

例句：
The postrevolutionary government faced many challenges. = 后革命政府面临许多挑战。

The country underwent significant economic reforms in the postrevolutionary period. =  该国在革命后时期经历了重大的经济改革。


近义词：post-revolution, after-revolution


反义词：prerevolutionary, pre-revolution


词性：形容词
