out-of-character: /ˌaʊt əv ˈkærəktər/ | adj. | 角色之外的；不像其本性的；违反常态的 |  Describes behavior or actions that are inconsistent with a person's usual personality or a character's established personality in a story.  It suggests a departure from the norm, often unexpectedly. | 例句：His outburst was completely out of character. 他的爆发完全不像他的本性。


例句：Her sudden anger was completely out of character for her. 她突然发怒，这完全不像她平时的样子。


近义词：uncharacteristic, atypical, unusual, aberrant


反义词：characteristic, typical, usual, consistent


词性：形容词
