reinvitations:/ˌriːɪnvɪˈteɪʃənz/| n. |再次邀请；再邀请函|The reinvitations were sent out after the initial event was postponed.  再次邀请函在最初的活动推迟后发出。|近义词：second invitations, fresh invitations|反义词：cancellations


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We send out reinvitations when there are cancellations. = 我们在有取消的情况下发出再次邀请。
They issue reinvitations to those who missed the first event. = 他们向错过第一次活动的人发出再次邀请。


一般过去时:
The company sent out reinvitations to all the previous attendees. = 公司向所有之前的与会者发出了再次邀请。
They reinvited all the guests who couldn't make it to the original party. = 他们再次邀请所有不能参加原先派对的宾客。


现在完成时:
They have sent out reinvitations to compensate for the scheduling conflict. = 他们已经发出再次邀请以弥补时间冲突。
We have received several reinvitations this week. = 我们这周收到了几封再次邀请函。


过去完成时:
Before the event, they had already sent out reinvitations to those who declined. = 在活动之前，他们已经向那些拒绝的人发出了再次邀请。
By the time the new date was set, they had already prepared the reinvitations. = 在确定新日期时，他们已经准备好了再次邀请函。


将来时:
We will send out reinvitations next week if necessary. = 如果有必要，我们下周将发出再次邀请。
They will reinvite the important clients for the next presentation. = 他们将再次邀请重要的客户参加下一次演示。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would send out reinvitations. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会发出再次邀请。
I wish they had sent out reinvitations earlier. = 我希望他们早点发出再次邀请。


被动语态:

The guests were reinvited to the rescheduled meeting. = 客人们被再次邀请参加重新安排的会议。


疑问句:

否定句:
