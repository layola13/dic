phrased:/freɪzd/ | adj. | 用短语表达的；措辞讲究的 | Expressed in a phrase or phrases; characterized by careful or effective wording.  |例句：His phrased answer was carefully considered. 他的回答措辞谨慎。 |例句：The poem's phrased lines created a rhythmic effect. 这首诗的语句营造了一种节奏感。|近义词：worded, expressed, formulated |反义词：unarticulated, rambling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The instructions are phrased clearly. = 说明写得很清楚。
The question is phrased ambiguously. = 这个问题表达含糊不清。


一般过去时:
He phrased his response diplomatically. = 他外交辞令地回应。
She phrased her complaint politely. = 她礼貌地提出了她的抱怨。


现在完成时:
They have phrased the proposal effectively. = 他们已经有效地表述了这个提案。
The artist has phrased the music beautifully. = 艺术家已经把音乐表达得很优美。


过去完成时:
He had phrased his argument convincingly before the debate. = 他在辩论之前已经令人信服地阐述了他的论点。
She had phrased her letter carefully before sending it. = 她仔细斟酌过信件措辞后再寄出。


将来时:
I will phrase my answer carefully. = 我会仔细组织我的答案。
They will phrase their concerns professionally. = 他们会专业地表达他们的担忧。


虚拟语气:
If I phrased it differently, they might understand. = 如果我换个说法，他们可能会理解。
I wish I had phrased my request more clearly. = 我希望我当时能把我的请求表达得更清楚些。


被动语态:
The question was phrased incorrectly. = 这个问题问错了。
The poem's lines were beautifully phrased. = 这首诗的诗句表达得很优美。


疑问句:
How is the question phrased? = 这个问题是怎么表达的？
Is the contract phrased legally? = 合同的措辞是否符合法律规定？


否定句:
The instructions were not phrased clearly enough. = 说明不够清楚。
He didn't phrase his thoughts effectively. = 他没有有效地表达他的想法。


PHRASE->
well-phrased = 表达清晰的
poorly phrased = 表达糟糕的
carefully phrased = 措辞谨慎的
ambiguously phrased = 模棱两可的
clearly phrased = 清晰表达的
