coppice:/ˈkɒpɪs/| n. |小树林；灌木丛；萌芽林|A coppice is a small wood in which the trees are cut down periodically to encourage new growth.  It's often characterized by dense undergrowth and young trees. |The ancient coppice was alive with the sounds of birdsong.  古老的小树林里充满了鸟鸣声。 The woodland path wound through a dense coppice.  林间小路蜿蜒穿过茂密的小树林。|woodland, thicket, scrub|open field, forest (in the sense of a large, mature forest)|名词
