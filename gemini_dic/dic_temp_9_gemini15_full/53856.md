discontentments:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛntmənts/ | n. | 不满；不满足 | Plural of discontentment; a state of dissatisfaction or unhappiness.  It often refers to a feeling of unhappiness caused by something that is not satisfactory. |例句：The growing discontentments among the workers led to a strike. 工人们日益增长的不满导致了罢工。

例句：Her discontentments with her job eventually led her to quit. 她对工作的诸多不满最终导致她辞职。


近义词：dissatisfaction, unhappiness, disgruntlement, resentment, grievance

反义词：satisfaction, contentment, happiness, fulfillment


词性: 名词
