healers:/ˈhiːlərz/ | n. |治疗者，治愈者 |People or things that heal or cure.  They may be individuals, methods, or substances.  The term often refers to those who practice alternative or complementary medicine. |Example:  Many healers use a combination of herbal remedies and spiritual practices.  许多治疗者结合草药疗法和精神疗法。

The ancient healers used natural remedies. 古代治疗者使用天然药物。|近义词：curers, therapists, practitioners |反义词：destroyers, injurers
