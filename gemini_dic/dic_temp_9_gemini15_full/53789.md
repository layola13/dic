discomposes:/ˌdɪskəmˈpoʊz/ | vt. |使分解；使腐烂；使不安；使沮丧 | to cause something to decay or break down into simpler parts; to disturb the composure or tranquility of someone |例句：The humid weather discomposes the food quickly.潮湿的天气使食物迅速腐烂。The news discomposes him greatly. 这个消息使他非常不安。|近义词：decompose, decay, upset, disturb |反义词：compose, stabilize, calm, soothe
