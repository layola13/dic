lofts:/lɒfts/|ˈlɔːfts/| n.|阁楼；顶楼公寓；高层建筑；（思想等的）高尚；（鸟等的）栖息地|A loft is a room or space under a roof, often used for storage.  It can also refer to a stylish apartment in a converted warehouse or industrial building. |例句：The artist has a spacious loft in the city.  这位艺术家在城里有一间宽敞的阁楼。

例句：They converted the old factory into luxury lofts. 他们把旧工厂改造成豪华顶层公寓。


近义词：attic, garret, upper floor, penthouse

反义词：basement, cellar


词源：源自古英语 *loft,意为“空中空间，屋顶”。
