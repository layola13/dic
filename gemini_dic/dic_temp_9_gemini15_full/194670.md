self-usurps:/ˌsɛlf juˈzɜːrps/| v.| 篡夺；擅自占据；霸占 |To seize or arrogate power or authority to oneself, often in an illegal or unjust manner.  It implies a taking of power that rightfully belongs to another or to a higher authority.|例句：The ambitious general self-usurped the throne after the king's death.  这位野心勃勃的将军在国王死后篡夺了王位。


The company's CEO self-usurped the board's decision-making power.  公司首席执行官擅自 usurped 了董事会的决策权。|近义词：usurp, seize, arrogate, appropriate, commandeer |反义词：cede, relinquish, surrender, abdicate
