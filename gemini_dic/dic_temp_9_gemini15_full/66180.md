exchanged:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒd/| v. |交换；兑换；交流 |To give something to someone and receive something else from them in return; to give or receive something in return for something else.|例句：They exchanged gifts. 他们交换了礼物。
They exchanged addresses. 他们交换了地址。

近义词：swapped, traded, bartered
反义词：kept, retained


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They exchange ideas regularly. = 他们定期交流思想。
We exchange greetings every morning. = 我们每天早上互相问候。

一般过去时:
I exchanged my old car for a new one. = 我用旧车换了一辆新车。
They exchanged phone numbers after the meeting. = 会议结束后他们交换了电话号码。

现在完成时:
We have exchanged emails several times. = 我们已经通过邮件交流了几次。
She has exchanged her dollars for euros. = 她已经把美元兑换成欧元了。

过去完成时:
He had exchanged his clothes before going to the party. = 他去参加聚会之前已经换了衣服。
They had exchanged pleasantries before getting down to business. = 他们在开始谈正事之前已经寒暄过了。

将来时:
They will exchange vows at the wedding ceremony. = 他们将在婚礼仪式上交换誓言。
We will exchange information with our partners. = 我们将与合作伙伴交换信息。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would exchange my old phone for a new one. = 如果我有更多钱，我会把我的旧手机换成新的。
I wish I could exchange this shirt for a different size. = 我希望我能把这件衬衫换成不同尺寸的。

被动语态:
The gifts were exchanged during the party. = 礼物在聚会上被交换了。
Currency can be exchanged at most banks. = 货币可以在大多数银行兑换。

疑问句:
Did they exchange gifts? = 他们交换礼物了吗？
Will you exchange your dollars for pounds? = 你会把美元兑换成英镑吗？

否定句:
They didn't exchange words. = 他们没有说话。
I haven't exchanged my currency yet. = 我还没有兑换货币。
