screensaver:/ˈskriːnˌseɪvər/ | n. |屏幕保护程序 |A program that displays moving images or patterns on a computer screen to prevent damage from burn-in when the screen is left unused for a period of time.  It also often serves as a form of decoration or entertainment. |Example: I set a beautiful nature screensaver on my laptop. 我在我的笔记本电脑上设置了一个美丽的自然风景屏幕保护程序。 My screensaver is a slideshow of my vacation photos. 我的屏幕保护程序是度假照片的幻灯片播放。|近义词：screen blank, display saver |反义词：N/A
