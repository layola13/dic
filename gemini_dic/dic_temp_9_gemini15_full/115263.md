manipulatory:/məˈnɪpjʊlətəri/ | adj. |操纵的；控制的；摆布的 |  Characterized by or involving manipulation; tending to manipulate.  It suggests a skillful but often unfair or unethical control over people or situations for one's own advantage. | 例句：His manipulatory tactics alienated many of his colleagues.  他的操纵策略疏远了他的许多同事。

His manipulatory behavior was evident in his constant attempts to control others. 他的操纵行为在他不断试图控制他人中显而易见。


近义词：controlling, controlling, scheming, devious,  machiavellian | 反义词：honest, straightforward, genuine, open


词源：源于动词manipulate，manipulate来源于拉丁语manus（手）和-pulare（处理，操作）。因此，manipulate字面意思是“用手操作”，引申为“操纵，控制”。  -tory是形容词后缀，表示“有…性质的”，“与…有关的”。
