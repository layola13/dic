reprievers:/rɪˈpriːvərz/| n.|赦免者；缓刑者；暂缓执行死刑者|A repriever is a person or thing that saves someone or something from death or a difficult situation.  It can also refer to someone who grants a reprieve.|The judge was the prisoner's repriever.  法官是囚犯的赦免者。|The rain was a repriever for the parched land. 雨水拯救了干旱的土地。| rescuer, savior | executioner, destroyer
