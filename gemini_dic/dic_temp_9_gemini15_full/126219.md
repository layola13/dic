myalgic:/maɪˈælʒɪk/ | adj. | 肌痛的；肌痛性疾病的 | Relating to or denoting myalgia.  The term is often used in the context of myalgic encephalomyelitis (ME), also known as chronic fatigue syndrome (CFS).  | Example: She experienced myalgic symptoms, including widespread muscle pain and fatigue.  她经历了肌痛症状，包括广泛的肌肉疼痛和疲劳。 | 近义词: painful, aching, sore | 反义词: painless, comfortable
