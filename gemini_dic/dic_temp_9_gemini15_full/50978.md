desirableness:/ˌdɛzɪˈræbəlnəs/| n.|合意性；理想性；令人向往的特性|The state or quality of being desirable; the extent to which something is wanted or liked.  It often refers to the attractiveness or appeal of something.|例句：The desirableness of the property increased after the renovations were completed.  房产装修完成后，其吸引力增强了。
例句：The desirableness of a quiet life is often underestimated. 安静生活的理想性常常被低估。|近义词：attractiveness, appeal, desirability, charm|反义词：unattractiveness, repulsiveness, undesirability


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tenses like present, past, future, etc., are not applicable.  The word itself expresses a state of being.


PHRASE->
high desirableness = 高度令人向往
degree of desirableness = 理想程度
relative desirableness = 相对的理想性
lack of desirableness = 缺乏吸引力
perceived desirableness = 感知到的理想性
