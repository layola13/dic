overclogs:/ˈoʊvərklɒɡz/| v.|堵塞过度，过度拥挤|to obstruct or block excessively; to be excessively crowded.|例句：The heavy rain overclogged the drains. 大雨堵塞了排水沟。|例句：The streets were overclogged with holiday shoppers. 节日购物者使街道过度拥挤。|近义词：congest, block, obstruct, jam|反义词：clear, unclog
