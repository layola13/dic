regrasps: /ˌriːˈɡræspz/ | v. |重新抓住；再次掌握| To grasp something again.  It implies a prior grasping and then a subsequent re-grasping, often suggesting a loss or near-loss of the object or concept before the re-grasping. |例句：After dropping the vase, she regrasped it quickly to prevent it from shattering. = 掉落花瓶后，她迅速地重新抓住它以防止它破碎。

He regrasped the concept after reviewing the lecture notes. = 在复习讲义后，他又一次掌握了这个概念。


近义词：retake, recapture, regain, recover | 反义词：release, let go, drop
