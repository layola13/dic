favorable:/ˈfeɪvərəbl/| adj.|有利的，顺利的，赞成的|Favorable means giving you an advantage or more likely to succeed.  It can also mean expressing approval or support.|例句：The weather forecast is favorable for our picnic.  天气预报对我们的野餐有利。|例句：The response to our proposal was favorable.  我们提案的回应是积极的。|近义词：advantageous, auspicious, positive, supportive|反义词：unfavorable, adverse, negative, hostile|词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The conditions are favorable for growth. =  生长条件有利。
The reviews are favorable to the new movie. =  对这部新电影的评论很好。


一般过去时:
The court's decision was favorable to the defendant. = 法院的判决对被告有利。
The results of the experiment were favorable. = 实验结果令人满意。


现在完成时:
The changes have been favorable to the company's bottom line. =  这些变化对公司的利润底线有利。
The response to the product launch has been overwhelmingly favorable. = 产品发布的反响极其正面。


过去完成时:
The circumstances had been favorable up until that point. =  直到那时为止，情况一直都很顺利。
The evidence had been favorable to the prosecution before the new witness testified. =  在新的证人作证之前，证据对检方有利。


将来时:
The outlook for the market is favorable. = 市场前景看好。
The prospects for a peaceful resolution are favorable. = 和平解决的前景良好。


虚拟语气:
If the conditions were favorable, we would proceed. = 如果条件有利，我们将继续进行。
It would be favorable if we could reach a compromise. = 如果我们能达成妥协，那就太好了。


被动语态:
The project was viewed favorably by the investors. =  投资者对该项目评价很高。
The proposal was favorably received by the committee. = 委员会对提案表示赞同。


疑问句:
Is the public opinion favorable to the new policy? = 公众舆论是否支持这项新政策？
Are the conditions favorable for planting? = 种植条件是否适宜？


否定句:
The feedback was not favorable. = 反馈并不积极。
The economic conditions are not favorable for investment. = 经济形势不利于投资。
