foliage:/ˈfoʊliɪdʒ/| n. |树叶；叶子茂盛|The dense foliage blocked out the sun.  茂密的树叶遮挡了阳光。|The autumn foliage was spectacular. 秋天的树叶景色壮观。|近义词：leaves, greenery |反义词：barrenness


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The foliage provides shade in the summer. 夏天的树叶提供了阴凉。
The leaves of the tree are a vibrant green. 树叶是鲜艳的绿色。


一般过去时:
The foliage turned brown in the fall. 秋天树叶变成了棕色。
Last year, the foliage was particularly beautiful. 去年，树叶特别漂亮。


现在完成时:
The garden has developed beautiful foliage this year. 花园今年长出了美丽的树叶。
The trees have lost their foliage for the winter.  树木已经落叶过冬了。


过去完成时:
The leaves had already fallen before the first frost.  第一次霜冻来临之前，树叶已经落光了。
By the time we arrived, the foliage had changed color. 我们到达的时候，树叶已经变色了。


将来时:
The foliage will turn red and gold in the autumn. 秋天树叶将变成红色和金色。
Next spring, the trees will have new foliage.  明年春天，树木将长出新的树叶。


虚拟语气:
If only the foliage were greener!  要是树叶更绿就好了！
I wish the foliage were more vibrant. 我希望树叶更鲜艳。


被动语态:
The foliage is often damaged by strong winds. 树叶经常被强风破坏。
The garden's foliage was carefully trimmed. 花园的树叶被仔细修剪过。


疑问句:
Is the foliage dense enough to provide shade? 树叶茂密到足以遮荫吗？
What kind of foliage is best for this climate?  什么类型的树叶最适合这种气候？


否定句:
The foliage wasn't as lush as it had been last year.  树叶不像去年那么茂盛。
There was no foliage on the bare branches. 光秃秃的树枝上没有树叶。
