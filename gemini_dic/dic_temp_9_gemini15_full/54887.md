displume:/dɪˈpluːm/| vt.|使…失去羽毛；使…丧失自尊；贬低；使…沮丧|To deprive (someone or something) of feathers; to strip of plumes; to deprive of pride, spirit, or self-confidence. |例句：The harsh criticism displumed him.  严厉的批评使他沮丧。 The bird was displumed by the cat.  那只鸟被猫弄掉了羽毛。|近义词：deflate, humiliate, degrade|反义词：inflate, exalt, elevate|动词
