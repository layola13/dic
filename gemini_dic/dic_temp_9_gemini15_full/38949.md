confider:/kənˈfaɪdər/ | n. | 知己，心腹朋友 | A person to whom one confides private matters.  Someone you trust and share your secrets with. | Example:  She is my closest confider, someone I can always rely on for support and understanding.  她是我最亲密的知己，一个我总是可以依靠寻求支持和理解的人。 |近义词： confidant, trusted friend, intimate | 反义词：stranger, enemy


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is a good confider for his friends. = 他是朋友们的好知己。
She confides in her sister regularly. = 她经常向姐姐倾诉。


一般过去时:
My grandfather was a great confider to his family. = 我爷爷是家人伟大的倾诉对象。
He confided in his best friend about his problems. = 他向他最好的朋友倾诉了他的问题。


现在完成时:
I have confided in her about my deepest fears. = 我已经向她倾诉了我最深的恐惧。
She has always been a loyal confider to me. = 她一直是我的忠实知己。


过去完成时:
He had confided in her before he made the decision. = 他在做决定之前已经向她倾诉了。
She had confided in many people about her past. = 她已经向许多人倾诉过她的过去。


将来时:
I will confide in him about my future plans. = 我将向他倾诉我的未来计划。
She will confide in her therapist about her anxieties. = 她将向她的治疗师倾诉她的焦虑。


虚拟语气:
If I had a confider, I would feel much better. = 如果我有一个知己，我会感觉好得多。
I wish I had a confider to share my worries with. = 我希望我有一个知己可以分享我的担忧。


被动语态:
The secret was confided to a trusted friend. = 这个秘密被倾诉给了一个值得信赖的朋友。
His problems were confided in his family. = 他的问题被倾诉给了他的家人。


疑问句:
Who is your closest confider? = 你最亲密的知己是谁？
Do you have a confider you can always turn to? = 你有一个可以随时求助的知己吗？


否定句:
He is not a good confider; he often gossips. = 他不是一个好的倾诉对象；他经常八卦。
She did not confide in anyone about her feelings. = 她没有向任何人倾诉她的感受。


PHRASE->
a trusted confider = 一个值得信赖的知己
a close confider = 一个亲密的知己
to be a confider to someone = 成为某人的知己
to confide in a confider = 向知己倾诉
