checklisting: /ˈtʃɛklɪstɪŋ/ | n. |核对清单(过程) |The act of creating or using a checklist; the process of systematically verifying items against a list.  It involves systematically going through a list to ensure that everything is completed or accounted for. |例句：The pre-flight checklisting is crucial for safe operation.  飞行前的清单核对对安全运行至关重要。

例句：The project manager ensured thorough checklisting before the launch. 项目经理在启动前确保了彻底的清单核对。

近义词：verification, inspection, inventorying, auditing

反义词：neglect, oversight

词性:名词
