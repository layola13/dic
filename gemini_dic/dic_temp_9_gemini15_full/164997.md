prehearings:/ˈpriːˌhɪərɪŋz/ | n. |预审；预先听证会 | Preliminary hearings held before a full trial to determine if there is enough evidence to proceed.  预审在正式审判前举行，以确定是否有足够的证据继续进行。| 近义词： preliminary hearings, preliminary investigations | 反义词：final hearings, trial
