calendrer:/ˈkæləndər/| n.|日历，日程表|A calendar is a system of organizing days for social, religious, commercial or administrative purposes.  It's a chart or series of pages showing the days, weeks, and months of a particular year. |例句：I marked the important dates on my calendar. 我在我的日历上标注了重要的日期。|例句：  She uses a digital calendar to manage her appointments. 她使用电子日历来管理她的约会。|近义词：schedule, diary, planner|反义词：无
