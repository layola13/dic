evapone:/ɪˈvæpəneɪt/| v.|蒸发|To change from a liquid or solid state into a gas.  Evaporation is a common process in nature, and it's essential for the water cycle. |例句：The water evaporated quickly in the hot sun.  水在烈日下很快蒸发了。|例句：The puddle evaporated overnight. 水洼一夜之间蒸发了。|近义词：vaporize, dry up|反义词：condense, precipitate|动词
