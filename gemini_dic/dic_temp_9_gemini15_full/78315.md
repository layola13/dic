gerontocrat:/ˈdʒerənˈtɒkræt/|/ˈdʒɛrənˈtɑːkræt/| n. |老年统治者；老年政治家|A gerontocrat is a ruler or member of a ruling class who is elderly.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting that the elderly are in power due to age and not necessarily competence or merit. | Example: The country was ruled by a gerontocracy, a group of gerontocrats clinging to power despite their advanced age and declining abilities.  这个国家由一群老年统治者统治，他们尽管年纪大了，能力下降了，却仍然死抓着权力不放。|近义词：elder statesman, old fogy (负面)|反义词：youth leader, young reformer
