emeritus: /ɪˈmɛrɪtəs/ | adj. | 名誉的；退休荣誉的 |  An emeritus professor or other official is one who has retired from a position but retains an honorary title and often some privileges.  The word implies continued respect and recognition for past service and contributions. |


例句：

He is an emeritus professor of history at the university. 他是该大学的历史系名誉教授。

She continues to be active in the field, even in her emeritus status. 即使退休了，她仍然活跃在这个领域。


近义词：honorary, retired (in the context of retaining title and respect)


反义词：active (in the context of employment), non-honorary


词性:形容词
