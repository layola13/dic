docile:/ˈdɒsaɪl/ /ˈdɑːsəl/| adj.|温顺的；驯服的；易于管教的|easily taught, led, or managed; submissive.|The docile sheep followed the shepherd. 温顺的羊跟着牧羊人。|近义词：obedient, submissive, tractable, compliant|反义词：rebellious, unruly, intractable, defiant

一般现在时:
The dog is docile and friendly. 这只狗温顺而友好。
The students are generally docile and eager to learn. 学生们通常都很温顺，并且渴望学习。

虚拟语气:
If the horse were more docile, riding it would be easier. 如果这匹马更温顺一些，骑它就会更容易。

被动语态:

疑问句:
