nonsocialnesses:/ˌnɒnˈsəʊʃəlˌnɛsɪz/| n. |非社交行为；不善社交的各种表现|The plural of "nonsocialness," referring to instances or aspects of being unsociable or antisocial.  It encompasses a range of behaviors and attitudes indicative of a lack of social interaction or engagement.  This word is relatively rare and often used in academic or specialized contexts.|例句：His nonsocialnesses were evident in his reluctance to attend parties and his avoidance of social gatherings. 他不善社交的表现很明显，他不愿意参加聚会，并且避免社交聚会。


例句：The study explored the various nonsocialnesses exhibited by individuals with autism spectrum disorder.  这项研究探讨了自闭症谱系障碍患者表现出的各种不善社交的行为。


近义词：antisocial behaviors, unsociable tendencies, asocial traits, social withdrawal, social isolation


反义词：sociability, social skills, gregariousness, extroversion
