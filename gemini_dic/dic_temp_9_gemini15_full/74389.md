framer:/ˈfreɪmər/| n. v. |构架者；框架制造者；制定者；陷害者|One who makes or constructs frames; one who frames or formulates something; someone who sets up or plans something, especially something illegal.|The carpenter is a skilled framer, building sturdy frames for houses.  这个木匠是个熟练的框架制造者，为房屋建造坚固的框架。|The framer of the legislation faced intense criticism.  这项立法的制定者面临着激烈的批评。|近义词：constructor, designer, architect, plotter|反义词：deconstructor, destroyer|名词，动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He frames pictures professionally. = 他专业地为图片装裱。
The government frames its policies carefully. = 政府谨慎地制定其政策。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:
