nonincestuous: /ˌnɒnɪnˈsɛstʃuəs/| adj. |非乱伦的，非近亲结婚的 | Not involving sexual relations between close relatives.  The term emphasizes the absence of incestuous relationships. | 例句：The marriage was declared nonincestuous after a thorough investigation.  彻底调查后，这桩婚姻被宣布为非乱伦的婚姻。

The family has always maintained a strictly nonincestuous lineage.  这个家族一直保持着严格的非乱伦的血统。


近义词：unincestuous, legitimate (in the context of marriage) | 反义词：incestuous


词性：形容词

词源:

The word is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the adjective "incestuous" (relating to incest).  "Incestuous" ultimately derives from the Latin word "incestus," meaning "unholy," "polluted," or "profane," related to the idea of ritual impurity associated with incest.
