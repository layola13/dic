clear-cut: /ˌklɪrˈkʌt/ | adj. | 清晰的；明确的；易懂的 | Easily understood; obvious and unambiguous.  |  The evidence against him was clear-cut.  | unambiguous, distinct, definite | vague, ambiguous, obscure


例句：

The instructions were clear-cut, so everyone understood what to do.  = 指示很明确，所以每个人都明白该做什么。

The difference between right and wrong was clear-cut in this case. = 在这种情况下，是非曲直非常明确。


近义词：unambiguous, distinct, definite, explicit, obvious

反义词：vague, ambiguous, obscure, uncertain, indistinct
