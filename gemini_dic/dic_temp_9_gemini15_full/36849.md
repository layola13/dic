colonnades:/ˌkɒləˈneɪdz/| n.|柱廊，柱行|A colonnade is a series of columns, usually supporting a roof or entablature.  They are often found in classical architecture and create a visually striking and imposing effect. |例句：The ancient Roman forum was lined with impressive colonnades. 古罗马广场两侧排列着令人印象深刻的柱廊。

The museum's entrance was framed by elegant colonnades. 博物馆的入口由优雅的柱廊环绕。|近义词：arcade, portico, peristyle|反义词：无
