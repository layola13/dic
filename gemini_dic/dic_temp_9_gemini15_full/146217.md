overexpends:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspɛndz/ | v. |超支；过度花费 | To spend more money than one has or than is intended.  |例句：The company overspent on its marketing campaign and went into debt. 公司在营销活动上超支，导致负债。|例句：He overspent his budget on Christmas presents. 他在圣诞礼物上超支了。|近义词：exceed, surpass, squander |反义词：underspend, save, economize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overspend on luxury goods every month. = 他们每个月都在奢侈品上超支。
We overspend on entertainment regularly. = 我们经常在娱乐方面超支。


一般过去时:
The government overspent on infrastructure projects last year. = 政府去年在基础设施项目上超支了。
She overspent on her holiday and had to borrow money. = 她在假期超支了，不得不借钱。


现在完成时:
They have overspent their savings this year. = 他们今年已经透支了储蓄。
We have overspent on our credit card again. = 我们又用信用卡超支了。


过去完成时:
They had overspent their budget before the project even began. = 项目开始前他们就已经超支了预算。
She had overspent her allowance by the end of the week. = 到周末她已经超支了零花钱。


将来时:
I think we will overspend if we don't stick to the plan. = 我认为如果我们不坚持计划，我们会超支的。
They fear they will overspend on the wedding. = 他们担心婚礼会超支。


虚拟语气:
If they had planned better, they wouldn't have overspent. = 如果他们计划得更好，就不会超支了。
I wish I hadn't overspent on that dress. = 我真希望我没有在那件裙子上超支。


被动语态:
The budget was overspent due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，预算超支了。
The funds were overspent on unnecessary expenses. = 资金在不必要的开支上超支了。


疑问句:
Did they overspend on their vacation? = 他们在度假时超支了吗？
Have you ever overspent on a purchase? = 你曾经在购买商品时超支过吗？


否定句:
We didn't overspend on our trip. = 我们旅行时没有超支。
They haven't overspent this month. = 他们这个月没有超支。


PHRASE->
overspend on = 在...上超支
overspend the budget = 超支预算
significantly overspend = 大幅超支
constantly overspend = 经常超支
dangerously overspend = 危险地超支
