leafletter: /ˈliːfˌlɛtər/ | n. |小册子，传单|A leaflet is a small printed sheet of paper, often folded, that gives information or advertisement.  It is often used for advertising or propaganda. | Example:  I picked up a leafletter advertising the local farmers' market. 我捡到了一张宣传当地农贸市场的传单。


The company distributed leaflets to promote their new product. 公司分发了传单来宣传他们的新产品。| pamphlet, flyer, brochure | poster | n.
