degrade:/dɪˈɡreɪd/ | vt. vi. n.| 降低；使恶化；降级 | To lower in rank, status, or quality.  |例句：The quality of the water has degraded due to pollution.  水质因污染而恶化了。|例句：He was degraded from his position after the scandal.  丑闻之后，他被降职了。|近义词：deteriorate, worsen, lower, diminish |反义词：improve, enhance, upgrade, elevate |动词、名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The environment degrades slowly but steadily. = 环境缓慢但稳定地恶化。
He constantly degrades his employees in public. = 他不断地在公共场合贬低他的员工。

一般过去时:
The building degraded over time. = 这座建筑物随着时间的推移而逐渐破败。
The government degraded his status after the findings. = 政府在调查结果出来后降低了他的地位。

现在完成时:
The value of the currency has degraded significantly. = 货币价值已大幅贬值。
His reputation has degraded due to his actions. = 他的名声因他的行为而受损。

过去完成时:
The bridge had degraded significantly before the collapse. = 桥梁在倒塌之前已经严重损坏。
His health had degraded considerably before he sought medical help. = 他在寻求医疗帮助之前，健康状况已经严重恶化。


将来时:
The ecosystem will degrade if we continue to pollute. = 如果我们继续污染，生态系统将会恶化。
His mental state will further degrade without treatment. = 如果不进行治疗，他的精神状态将会进一步恶化。

虚拟语气:
If we didn't pollute, the environment wouldn't degrade. = 如果我们不污染，环境就不会恶化。
I wish my skills hadn't degraded so much during my break. = 我希望我的技能在我休息期间没有退化那么多。


被动语态:
The old house was degraded by years of neglect. = 老房子由于多年的忽视而破败不堪。
His position was degraded due to misconduct. = 他的职位因行为不端而被降低。


疑问句:
Has the quality of the air degraded in recent years? = 近年来空气质量是否恶化了？
Will his social status degrade after this incident? = 这件事之后他的社会地位会下降吗？


否定句:
The quality of the product did not degrade over time. = 产品质量并没有随着时间的推移而下降。
They refused to degrade the importance of education.  他们拒绝贬低教育的重要性。
