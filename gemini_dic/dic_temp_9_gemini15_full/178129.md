recognition: /ˌrekəɡˈnɪʃən/ | n. | 识别，认出，承认 | The act of recognizing or being recognized; acknowledgment or appreciation.  The process of identifying something or someone. |例句：His years of hard work finally earned him recognition. 他多年的努力终于得到了认可。

例句：The software uses facial recognition to unlock your phone.  这个软件使用面部识别来解锁你的手机。


近义词：identification, acknowledgment, appreciation, detection, discernment | 反义词：misidentification, ignorance, disregard


USAGE->

She recognizes her friend from afar. = 她从远处认出了她的朋友。
The company recognizes the importance of innovation. = 公司认识到创新的重要性。
