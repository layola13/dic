enema:/ˈɛnɪmə/ | n. | 灌肠；灌肠剂 | The introduction of fluid into the rectum and colon via the anus, usually for cleansing or medical purposes.  | Example: The doctor ordered an enema to prepare the patient for a colonoscopy.  医生为病人进行结肠镜检查开了灌肠剂。|近义词：rectal irrigation, colonic lavage |反义词：N/A

词源:
The word "enema" comes from the New Latin word *enema*, which is itself derived from the Ancient Greek word κλύσμα (klýsma), meaning "a washing, a clyster".  The Greek word is related to the verb κλύζω (klýzō), meaning "to wash, to rinse".
