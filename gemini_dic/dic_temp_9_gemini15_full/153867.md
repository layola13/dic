perchlorates:/ˈpɜːrklɔːreɪts/| n. |高氯酸盐|Salts of perchloric acid (HClO₄), containing the perchlorate anion (ClO₄⁻).  They are strong oxidizing agents and are used in various applications, but some are also considered environmental pollutants.|Example:  Perchlorates are used in rocket propellants and fireworks. 高氯酸盐用于火箭推进剂和烟花。|近义词：oxidizers(氧化剂)|反义词：reducing agents(还原剂)
