definitiveness:/ˌdɛfɪˈnɪtɪvnəs/| n. |确定性，最终性，权威性 |The state or quality of being definite; conclusiveness.  It implies a feeling of certainty and finality.  |例句：The report lacked definitiveness, leaving many questions unanswered.这份报告缺乏确定性，留下许多悬而未决的问题。

The jury's verdict brought a sense of definitiveness to the long trial. 陪审团的裁决为漫长的审判带来了确定性。|近义词：certainty, conclusiveness, finality, authority |反义词：uncertainty, ambiguity, doubt, tentativeness |词性 n.
