dexter:/ˈdɛkstər/| adj.|灵巧的，熟练的；右边的| skillful and clever, especially with one's hands; relating to the right side of the body|例句：He is dexterous with his hands. 他手很灵巧。  He showed great dexterity in handling the situation. 他在处理这种情况时表现出极大的技巧。|近义词：skillful, adept, adroit, deft|反义词：clumsy, awkward, inept, gauche|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is dexterous in his work. = 他工作很熟练。
She is dexterous with a needle. = 她用针很灵巧。


一般过去时:
He was dexterous in his youth. = 他年轻时很灵巧。

He has been dexterous all his life. = 他一辈子都很灵巧。
She has become more dexterous since taking the course. = 自从上了这门课后，她变得更灵巧了。


过去完成时:
