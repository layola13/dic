loamier:/ˈloʊmiər/ | adj. | 更肥沃的，更像壤土的 | More like loam; richer and more fertile.  Loamier soil is better for growing plants.  | The loamier soil in the valley produced abundant crops.  = 山谷里更肥沃的土壤产出了丰收的庄稼。 The garden flourished after being amended with loamier compost. = 花园在用更肥沃的堆肥改良后蓬勃发展。| richer, more fertile | sandy, rocky, infertile | 形容词
