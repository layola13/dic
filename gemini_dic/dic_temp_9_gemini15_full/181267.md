reinstitution:/ˌriːɪnstɪˈtuːʃən/| n. |重建，恢复；重新设立|The re-establishment or restoration of something that has been abolished or discontinued.  It implies a formal or official act of bringing something back into existence or operation.|例句：The reinstitution of the death penalty is a highly controversial issue.  死刑的恢复是一个极具争议的问题。

例句：The reinstitution of the old tax laws led to widespread protests.  旧税法的恢复导致了广泛的抗议。


近义词：restoration, reinstatement, re-establishment|反义词：abolition, cancellation, removal|词性：名词
