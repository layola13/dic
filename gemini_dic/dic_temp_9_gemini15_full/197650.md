sherlock:/ˈʃɜːrlɒk/ /ˈʃɜːrlɑːk/| n. |夏洛克（男子名）；福尔摩斯（人物名）| A surname of English origin, now often associated with the fictional detective Sherlock Holmes created by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle.  The name itself is of uncertain origin, potentially related to Old English words associated with "bright" or "famous." In modern usage, it's frequently used as a given name and is strongly linked to the image of a brilliant, perceptive, and somewhat eccentric detective.| He is named Sherlock after the famous detective. 他以著名的侦探夏洛克命名。


The name Sherlock Holmes is synonymous with deduction and investigation.  夏洛克·福尔摩斯这个名字是演绎和调查的代名词。
