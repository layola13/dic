chikungunya: /ˌtʃɪkənˈɡuːnjə/ | n. |基孔肯雅病毒；基孔肯雅热 | A viral disease transmitted to humans by infected mosquitoes.  Symptoms include fever, severe joint pain, muscle pain, headache, nausea, and rash. | The outbreak of chikungunya virus has caused widespread illness. 基孔肯雅病毒的爆发导致了广泛的疾病。  |  None specifically provided. | None specifically provided. | noun
