hither:/ˈhɪðə(r)/| adv.|到此，到这里；至此|This word indicates movement towards the speaker's location. It is now largely archaic or poetic, replaced in modern English by "here."|例句：Come hither, child.  过来，孩子。|例句：Hitherto, we have not faced such challenges. 到目前为止，我们还没有面临过这样的挑战。|近义词：here, hitherward|反义词：yon, thence, yonder


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He comes hither every day. = 他每天都来这里。
They go hither and thither. = 他们到处乱跑。


一般过去时:
She went hither in a hurry. = 她匆忙地赶到这里。
The bird flew hither from the trees. = 鸟儿从树上飞到这里来。


现在完成时:
He has come hither many times. = 他来过这里很多次。
They have been hither before. = 他们以前来过这里。


过去完成时:
I had already come hither before the meeting. = 我在会议开始之前就已经到这里了。
She had never been hither until that day. = 直到那天她才第一次来这里。


将来时:
He will come hither tomorrow. = 他明天会来这里。
They will go hither and yon. = 他们将到处走动。


虚拟语气:
If he were to come hither, I would be happy. = 如果他来这里，我会很高兴。
I wish he would come hither soon. = 我希望他很快来这里。


被动语态:
The package was sent hither yesterday. = 包裹昨天被送到了这里。
News was brought hither quickly. = 消息很快被传到这里。


疑问句:
Did he come hither? = 他来这里了吗？
Will they go hither? = 他们会来这里吗？


否定句:
He did not come hither yesterday. = 他昨天没来这里。
