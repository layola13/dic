haliography:/ˌheɪliˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. |日光照相术，日光摄影术 |The process of producing photographs using sunlight.  It is an obsolete photographic process. |Example:  Haliaiography was a popular method of photography in the late 19th century. 日光照相术在19世纪后期是一种流行的摄影方法。|近义词：heliography (the more common term) |反义词：None.
