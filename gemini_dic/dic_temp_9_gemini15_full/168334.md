pro-scripture:/ˈproʊˌskrɪpʃər/| adj.|拥护圣经的；圣经支持的|Supporting or in accordance with the Bible or scripture.  The pro-scripture movement emphasized a literal interpretation of the Bible.|例句：His pro-scripture views influenced his political stances. 他拥护圣经的观点影响了他的政治立场。|近义词：biblical, scriptural, orthodox|反义词：anti-scriptural, heretical
