retinoscopy:/ˌrɛtɪˈnɒskəpi/| n. |视网膜检影法|The objective examination of the eye to determine refractive error using a retinoscope.  This involves shining a light into the patient's eye and observing the movement of the light reflex to assess the need for corrective lenses.|The optometrist performed retinoscopy to determine the patient's refractive error. 眼科医生进行视网膜检影法来确定病人的屈光不正。| ophthalmoscopy, refraction| hyperopia (farsightedness), myopia (nearsightedness)|名词
