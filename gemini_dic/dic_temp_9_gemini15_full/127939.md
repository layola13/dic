neons:/ˈniːɑːnz/ | n. |霓虹灯，霓虹灯广告|Brightly colored tubes containing gas that glow when electricity passes through them.  They are often used in advertising signs.|例句：The neon signs of Times Square are iconic.  时代广场的霓虹灯标志性十足。

例句：The city at night was ablaze with neon lights. 夜晚的城市被霓虹灯照亮。

近义词：sign, light, illumination

反义词：darkness, obscurity
