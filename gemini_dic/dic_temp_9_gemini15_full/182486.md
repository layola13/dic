It often refers to the replenishment of depleted resources, such as soil or ecosystems. | Example: The renourishment of the beach involved pumping sand back onto the shore.  海滩的重新滋养包括将沙子抽回岸边。The renourishment project aimed to restore the degraded wetlands.  该复壮项目旨在恢复退化的湿地。|近义词：restoration, revitalization, replenishment |反义词：depletion, degradation, deterioration |名词
