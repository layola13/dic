cosmologer:/kɒzˈmɒlədʒər/| n. |宇宙学家|A cosmologist is a scientist who studies cosmology, the origin and development of the universe. Cosmologists use observations of the universe, such as the cosmic microwave background radiation and the distribution of galaxies, to develop and test theories about the universe's evolution.  |Example: The cosmologist presented her findings on dark energy at the conference.  宇宙学家在会议上展示了她关于暗能量的研究结果。|近义词：astrophysicist, astronomer |反义词：无
