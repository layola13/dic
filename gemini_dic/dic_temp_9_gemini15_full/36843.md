colonizer:/ˈkɑːlənaɪzər/|/ˈkɒlənaɪzər/| n. |殖民者，殖民主义者 | A person who establishes control over another territory, often with the intent of exploiting its resources and people.  Colonizers often impose their own culture and systems of governance on the colonized population. | Example: The European colonizers exploited the natural resources of the Americas.  欧洲殖民者剥削了美洲的自然资源。The actions of the colonizers led to widespread suffering and injustice. 殖民者的行为导致了广泛的苦难和不公正。|近义词：settler, conqueror, imperialist |反义词：colonized, indigenous people
