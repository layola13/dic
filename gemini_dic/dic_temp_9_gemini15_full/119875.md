USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army militates against the rebels. =  军队与叛军作战。
His aggressive nature militates against his success. = 他侵略性的天性阻碍了他的成功。

一般过去时:
The general militated against the enemy's advance. = 将军抵抗敌人的推进。
Her strong beliefs militated against her accepting the job. = 她坚定的信念使她无法接受这份工作。

现在完成时:
The government has militated against corruption for years. = 政府多年来一直在打击腐败。
His actions have militated against his own interests. = 他的行为损害了他自身的利益。

过去完成时:
They had militated against the ruling party for decades. = 他们几十年来一直反对执政党。
She had militated against injustice throughout her career. = 她职业生涯中一直反对不公正。

将来时:
The new law will militate against further environmental damage. = 新法律将有助于防止进一步的环境破坏。
He will militate against any attempt to change the rules. = 他将反对任何试图改变规则的企图。

虚拟语气:
If he militated against the proposal, it would be rejected. = 如果他反对这项提案，它就会被否决。
I wish he would militate against the injustice he sees. = 我希望他能反对他不公正的现象。

被动语态:
The plan was militated against by the opposition party. = 该计划遭到反对党的反对。
His reputation was militated against by his past mistakes. = 他过去的错误影响了他的名声。

疑问句:
Does his attitude militate against his chances of promotion? = 他的态度是否会影响他的晋升机会？
Does the evidence militate against the defendant's claim? = 证据是否对被告的主张不利？

否定句:
His calm demeanor did not militate against his effectiveness. = 他冷静的态度并没有影响他的效率。
The new policy did not militate against economic growth. = 新政策并没有阻碍经济增长。
