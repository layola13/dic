heartiest:/ˈhɑːrtiəst/| adj. | 最诚挚的；最热烈的；丰盛的 | Most sincere; warmest; most abundant.  The word implies a strong and overflowing feeling or quantity. |例句：She gave me the heartiest welcome. 她给了我最热烈的欢迎。

例句：The family enjoyed a heartiest Thanksgiving dinner.  这个家庭享用了一顿丰盛的感恩节晚餐。


近义词：warmest, sincerest, most abundant, fullest, most generous

反义词：coldest, insincere, meager, scanty


词源：源自古英语 *heorte "心"，后来演变成现代英语heart，加上-iest表示最高级形容词后缀。  The suffix "-iest" indicates the superlative form, signifying the greatest degree of the quality.
