herniated:/ˈhɜːnieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 疝气的；患疝的 |  Describes a condition where an organ or tissue protrudes through the wall that normally contains it, often due to a weakness or tear in that wall.  This is commonly associated with a hernia. | Example: He was suffering from a herniated disc in his lower back. 他患有下背部椎间盘突出。  |近义词：protruded, ruptured, displaced | 反义词：intact, undamaged


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The disc is herniated. = 椎间盘突出。
The patient presents with a herniated abdomen. = 病人出现腹部疝气。


一般过去时:
His disc herniated last week. = 他的椎间盘上周突出。
The hernia was herniated during a heavy lift. = 疝气是在举重物时突出的。


现在完成时:
He has been herniated for months. = 他已经疝气好几个月了。(This usage is slightly less common and often replaced with a phrasing like "He has had a hernia for months.")
My uncle has herniated a disc lifting furniture.=我叔叔搬家具时椎间盘突出了.


过去完成时:
She had already herniated a disc before the accident. = 事故发生前，她已经椎间盘突出了。
The patient had herniated several times previously. = 该患者以前曾多次疝气。


将来时:
The doctor thinks he will herniate if he continues to lift heavy objects. = 医生认为如果他继续举重物，他会疝气的。
If he doesn't change his lifestyle, he will likely herniate again. = 如果他不改变生活方式，很可能会再次疝气。


虚拟语气:
If he had not lifted that heavy box, he might not have herniated. = 如果他没有搬那个重箱子，他可能就不会疝气了。
I wish I hadn't herniated my back. = 我真希望我的背没有疝气。


被动语态:
The disc was herniated during a fall. = 椎间盘在跌倒时突出了。
The hernia was surgically repaired after it herniated. = 疝气突出后进行了手术修复。


疑问句:
Is his disc herniated? = 他的椎间盘突出吗？
Has the patient ever herniated before? = 病人以前有没有过疝气？


否定句:
His disc is not herniated. = 他的椎间盘没有突出。
The surgery was successful, and the hernia was not herniated again. = 手术成功，疝气没有再次突出。
