preconcentrations: /ˌpriːkɒnsənˈtreɪʃənz/ | n. |预浓缩；预富集|The process of increasing the concentration of a substance before further analysis or processing.  This often involves separating the analyte from a large volume of sample matrix. |例句：The sample underwent preconcentrations before being analyzed by gas chromatography. 样品在气相色谱分析前进行了预浓缩。

例句：Advanced preconcentrations techniques are essential for detecting trace amounts of pollutants in environmental samples.  先进的预浓缩技术对于检测环境样品中痕量污染物至关重要。
