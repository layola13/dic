shaker:/ˈʃeɪkər/| n. v.|震动器；搅拌器；摇床；（尤指）调酒师|A shaker is a container used for mixing drinks, especially cocktails. It can also refer to a person or thing that shakes something, or a type of religious group. |例句：He used a cocktail shaker to mix the martini. 他用鸡尾酒调酒器调制马提尼酒。


The shaker was filled with colorful beads.  摇床里装满了五颜六色的珠子。


近义词：mixer, agitator, tumbler|反义词：stabilizer


词源:  From the verb "shake," ultimately from Proto-Germanic *skūkan.
