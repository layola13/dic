lacquering:/ˈlækərɪŋ/| n. |漆器；上漆；施漆工艺|The process of applying lacquer to a surface to protect or decorate it.  Lacquering is a traditional craft that requires skill and patience.|例句：The beautiful lacquered box was a family heirloom. 这精美的漆器盒子是传家宝。

The artist spent weeks perfecting the lacquering on the furniture.  艺术家花了数周时间完善家具上的漆器。|近义词：varnishing, enameling, polishing |反义词：stripping (removing lacquer)
