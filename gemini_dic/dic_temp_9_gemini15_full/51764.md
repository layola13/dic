devotement:/dɪˈvoʊtmənt/| n. |奉献，献身；忠诚 |The act of devoting time, effort, or oneself to a cause, person, or activity.  It implies a deep commitment and dedication.  |例句：Her devotement to her family is admirable.  她的对家庭的奉献令人钦佩。

例句：His lifelong devotement to the arts is evident in his extensive collection. 他毕生献身于艺术，从他大量的收藏中可见一斑。


近义词：dedication, commitment, loyalty, consecration

反义词：neglect, apathy, indifference, disloyalty

词性:名词
