screen-tests:/ˈskriːnˌtɛsts/| n.|屏幕测试，试镜|Screen tests are used in the film industry to assess an actor's suitability for a role.  They involve filming the actor performing scenes from the script or improvising.  The footage is then reviewed by the director and casting team.|例句：The actress underwent several screen tests before landing the lead role.  这位女演员在获得主角之前进行了多次试镜。

The casting director reviewed the screen tests of all the applicants. 选角导演审查了所有申请者的试镜录像。|近义词：auditions, screen trials|反义词：final selection, casting decision
