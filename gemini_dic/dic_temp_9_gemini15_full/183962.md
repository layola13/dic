reshaken:/riːˈʃeɪkən/| adj.| 再受震动的；再次动摇的 |  Having been shaken again; disturbed or unsettled once more.  This word implies a previous shaking and a subsequent reiteration of that unsettling experience.  It suggests a lingering effect and a renewed sense of instability or disquiet. |例句：After the earthquake aftershocks, the city felt reshaken.  地震余震过后，城市再次感到震动。

The building, reshaken by the second explosion, finally collapsed.  第二次爆炸后，大楼再次摇晃，最终倒塌。|近义词：unsettled, disturbed, shaken up, rattled |反义词：calm, composed, settled, undisturbed
