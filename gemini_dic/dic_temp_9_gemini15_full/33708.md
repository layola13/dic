cipolin:/ˈtʃɪpəlɪn/| n. |一种大理石|A type of marble characterized by its veined appearance, typically with alternating bands of light and dark colors, often green and white.  It is a metamorphic rock formed from limestone or dolomite. Its name likely derives from its resemblance to the scales of a fish (cipollino in Italian means "small onion").|The statue was carved from a beautiful block of cipolin marble.|近义词：marble, limestone|反义词：N/A
