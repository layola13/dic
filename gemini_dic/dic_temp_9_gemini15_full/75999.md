fur:/fɜːr/| n. |毛皮，毛；软毛；兽皮|The soft, hairy covering of an animal's skin.  It can also refer to the animal skin itself, used for clothing or other purposes. |例句：The cat's fur was soft and smooth.  这只猫的毛又软又顺。|例句：She wore a coat made of fox fur. 她穿着一件狐皮大衣。|近义词：pelage, fleece, hide|反义词：skin (bare skin)
