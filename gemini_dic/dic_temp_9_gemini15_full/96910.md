inexplainable: /ˌɪnɪkˈspleɪnəbl/ | adj. |无法解释的，不可思议的 | incapable of being explained or accounted for.  Something inexplicable is beyond understanding or explanation. It defies logical explanation. |

例句：

The disappearance of the painting remains inexplicable.  这幅画的消失仍然无法解释。

The strange noises coming from the attic were inexplicable.  从阁楼传来的奇怪声音令人费解。


近义词：unaccountable, incomprehensible, mysterious, baffling, enigmatic

反义词：explainable, understandable, comprehensible


词性:形容词
