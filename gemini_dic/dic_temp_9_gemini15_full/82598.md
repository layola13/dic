guarding:/ˈɡɑːrdɪŋ/| v.|守护，保护，警戒|The act of protecting or keeping watch over something or someone.  It implies a watchful and protective presence, often to prevent harm or loss.|例句：The soldiers were guarding the border. 士兵们正在守卫边境。

例句：The dog was guarding the house.  这只狗守护着房子。


近义词：protecting, defending, watching, shielding|反义词：abandoning, neglecting, endangering|动词
