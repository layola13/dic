contaminate:/kənˈtæmɪneɪt/| vt.|污染；弄脏|To make something impure or unclean by polluting or poisoning it.|例句：The river was contaminated by industrial waste. 河水被工业废料污染了。|例句：The food was contaminated with bacteria. 食物被细菌污染了。|近义词：pollute, infect, taint, corrupt|反义词：purify, cleanse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory contaminates the river with chemical waste. = 工厂用化学废料污染河流。
He contaminates his work with personal feelings. = 他把个人感情带入工作中，影响了工作。


一般过去时:
The oil spill contaminated the coastline. =漏油事故污染了海岸线。
The accident contaminated the water supply. =事故污染了供水。


现在完成时:
The soil has been contaminated by years of pesticide use. =多年使用杀虫剂已经污染了土壤。
The area has been contaminated by radiation. =该地区已被辐射污染。


过去完成时:
They discovered that the food had been contaminated before it reached the market. =他们发现食物在到达市场之前就已经被污染了。
The investigators determined that the well water had been contaminated for months. =调查人员确定井水已经被污染了好几个月。


将来时:
The new factory might contaminate the air. = 新工厂可能会污染空气。
If we don't act now, the river will be contaminated. =如果我们现在不采取行动，河流将被污染。


虚拟语气:
If the water were contaminated, we would have to find a new source. =如果水被污染了，我们就得寻找新的水源。
It is vital that the samples not be contaminated. =样本绝对不能被污染。


被动语态:
The water supply was contaminated by a chemical leak. =供水系统因化学物质泄漏而受污染。
The land has been contaminated by the factory. =这块土地被工厂污染了。


疑问句:
Has the air been contaminated by the fire? = 火灾是否污染了空气？
Is the food contaminated? = 食物被污染了吗？


否定句:
The water is not contaminated. =水没有被污染。
We ensured that the experiment was not contaminated. =我们确保实验没有受到污染。


PHRASE->
