noncommitment:/ˌnɒnkəˈmɪtmənt/ | n. | 不承诺；不承担义务；不确定性 | The state or an instance of not being committed to a particular course of action or belief.  It suggests a lack of firm decision or pledge. |  例句：Her noncommitment to the project worried the team.  她的不承诺让团队很担心。

例句：The company's noncommitment to environmental protection drew criticism. 公司对环境保护的不承诺招致批评。


近义词：hesitation, indecision, ambiguity, uncertainty, vagueness

反义词：commitment, pledge, dedication, resolve, certainty


词性：名词
