cocoyams:/ˈkoʊkoʊjæmz/ | n. |芋头（复数）|A starchy tuberous plant, *Colocasia esculenta*, of the arum family, widely cultivated in tropical regions for its edible corms.  Also refers to the corms themselves, which are used as a staple food in many parts of the world.| Example: Cocoyams are a vital part of the diet in many Caribbean islands.  芋头是许多加勒比海岛屿饮食的重要组成部分。  |近义词：taro, dasheen|反义词：None|名词
