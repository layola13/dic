disappear:/ˌdɪsəˈpɪr/| v.|消失，不见|To cease to be visible, disappear from sight, or vanish from existence.|The magician made the rabbit disappear. 魔术师让兔子消失了。 The symptoms disappeared after a few days.  几天后症状消失了。| appear, vanish, fade|appear, materialize|verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun disappears below the horizon every evening. 太阳每天晚上都消失在地平线以下。
He disappears into the crowd easily. 他很容易消失在人群中。

一般过去时:
The plane disappeared from the radar screen. 飞机从雷达屏幕上消失了。
My keys disappeared mysteriously. 我的钥匙神秘地消失了。

现在完成时:
The missing child has disappeared without a trace. 失踪的孩子无影无踪地消失了。
Many species have disappeared due to habitat loss. 许多物种由于栖息地丧失而消失了。

过去完成时:
The thief had disappeared before the police arrived.  警察到达之前，小偷已经消失了。
She had disappeared into the night by the time I got home. 我回家的时候，她已经消失在夜幕中了。

将来时:
The snow will disappear as the weather warms up. 天气暖和起来后，雪就会融化。
The problem will disappear once we find a solution. 一旦我们找到解决办法，问题就会消失。

虚拟语气:
If the problem disappeared overnight, we'd be very happy. 如果问题一夜之间消失了，我们会非常高兴。
I wish my worries would disappear. 我希望我的担忧能够消失。

被动语态:
The evidence was made to disappear. 证据被故意销毁了。
The clues have been mysteriously disappeared. 线索神秘地消失了。

疑问句:
Did the pain disappear after you took the medicine?  你服药后疼痛消失了没有？
Where did the money disappear to? 钱去哪儿了？

否定句:
The pain didn't disappear even after taking the medicine. 服药后疼痛也没有消失。
The problem won't disappear on its own.  问题不会自行消失。
