inknot:/ˈɪŋkɑːt/| n. |墨水结；墨迹|A knot or stain of ink.  A small, hardened mass of dried ink.|例句：He accidentally left an inknot on his important document. 他不小心在他的重要文件上留下了一个墨迹。|近义词：ink stain, blot|反义词：cleanliness

复数形式:

所有格形式:
The inknot's size surprised him. 墨迹的大小让他吃惊。


介词短语:
The letter was covered in inknots. 信上全是墨迹。
