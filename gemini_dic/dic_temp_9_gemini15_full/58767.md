dunks:/dʌŋks/| vt. n.|扣篮；浸；猛力投掷|To submerge something quickly and forcefully in a liquid;  To throw something forcefully downward; In basketball, to throw the ball through the hoop from above the rim.|例句：He dunked the cookies in the milk. 他把饼干浸在牛奶里。  He dunked the ball with amazing power. 他以惊人的力量扣篮。|近义词：submerge, plunge, slam|反义词：lift, raise


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He dunks the basketball every game. = 他每场比赛都扣篮。
She dunks her biscuits in tea. = 她把饼干浸在茶里。

一般过去时:
He dunked the ball during the final seconds. = 他在最后几秒钟扣篮。
She dunked the cookies into the chocolate. = 她把饼干浸到巧克力里。

现在完成时:
He has dunked the ball many times this season. = 这个赛季他扣篮很多次了。
She has dunked her donut in coffee already. = 她已经把甜甜圈浸在咖啡里了。

过去完成时:
He had dunked the ball before the buzzer sounded. = 在蜂鸣器响起之前，他已经扣篮了。
She had dunked the bread in the egg batter before frying it. = 在油炸之前，她已经把面包浸在蛋糊里了。

将来时:
He will dunk the ball again next game. = 下场比赛他还会再次扣篮。
She will dunk the chips in salsa. = 她会把薯片浸在莎莎酱里。

虚拟语气:
If he were taller, he could dunk the ball easily. = 如果他更高，他就能轻松扣篮。
If she had more time, she would dunk the cake. = 如果她有更多时间，她会把蛋糕浸一下。

被动语态:
The cookies were dunked in the milk by the child. = 饼干被孩子浸在牛奶里。
The ball was dunked with such force that the net ripped. = 球被如此用力地扣篮，以至于球网撕裂了。

否定句:
