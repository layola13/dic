/kaɪˈækiŋz/| n. |皮划艇运动；皮艇运动|The activity of propelling a kayak through water.  Kayaking involves using a paddle to move a kayak across water. It's a popular recreational activity and also a competitive sport.|例句：He enjoys kayaking on the lake every weekend. 他每个周末都喜欢在湖上划皮划艇。

例句：Kayaking is a great way to explore the coastline. 皮划艇是探索海岸线的好方法。


近义词：canoeing, paddling|反义词：无
