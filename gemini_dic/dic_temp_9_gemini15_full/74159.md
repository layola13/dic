fount:/faʊnt/| n. |喷泉；源泉；字体；丰富来源 |A fount is a spring or fountain of water.  It can also refer to a source or origin of something, or a type of typeface. |例句：The fount of wisdom flowed from the ancient texts.  智慧的源泉来自古代典籍。|近义词：source, spring, origin, wellspring |反义词：end, terminus, conclusion
