footholds:/ˈfʊtˌhoʊldz/| n.|立足点；立锥之地；依附点|Places where one can put one's feet for support, especially when climbing or on uneven ground.  It can also refer to a position of advantage or security.|例句：The climber searched for secure footholds on the sheer cliff face. 登山者在陡峭的悬崖上寻找安全的立足点。

The new government needs to establish strong footholds in the economy. 新政府需要在经济领域建立牢固的立足点。|近义词：supports, grips, handholds|反义词： insecurity, instability
