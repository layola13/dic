handshakes:/ˈhændʃeɪks/| n. |握手；(一次)握手|The act of shaking hands as a greeting or to show agreement.  Handshakes are a common form of nonverbal communication. |例句：He offered his hand in a handshake. 他伸出手来握手。

例句：The two leaders exchanged handshakes before the meeting. 两位领导人在会议前进行了握手。


近义词：greeting, clasp, grip |反义词：none |词性：名词


USAGE->

They exchange handshakes regularly. = 他们定期握手。


一般过去时:
