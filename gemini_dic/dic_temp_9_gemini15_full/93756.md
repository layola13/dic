immeasurable: /ɪˈmɛʒərəbl/ | adj. | 不可估量的；无限的；巨大的 | Too great to be measured; vast beyond calculation.  |  The benefits of this program are immeasurable. 这个项目的益处是无法估量的。| boundless, infinite, vast, immense | measurable, limited, finite
