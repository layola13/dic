It often involves anticipating potential problems and addressing them proactively to prevent escalation.  | Example: The contract included a clause to address predisputes regarding intellectual property rights.  该合同包含一项条款，用于解决有关知识产权的预先争议。 |近义词：preliminary disagreements, anticipatory conflicts | 反义词：post-dispute resolutions, aftermath conflicts | 名词
