fluoborate:/ˈfluːoʊbɔːreɪt/| n.|氟硼酸盐|A salt of fluoboric acid (HBF4). Fluoborates are commonly used in electroplating, as catalysts, and in other industrial applications.  They are typically stable and soluble in water.|例句：The researchers used a fluoborate electrolyte in their electroplating experiment. 研究人员在电镀实验中使用了氟硼酸盐电解液。|近义词：fluoroborate (同义词)|反义词：无
