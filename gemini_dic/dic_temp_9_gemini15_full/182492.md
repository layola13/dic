renovating: /ˈrɛnəveɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 翻新；装修；革新 | The act of making something old or worn-out look new again.  This can apply to buildings, objects, or even systems.  It implies improvement and restoration. |例句：They are renovating their old house. = 他们正在翻新他们的老房子。

例句：The company is renovating its outdated software. = 公司正在更新其过时的软件。


近义词：refurbishing, restoring, modernizing, updating | 反义词：decaying, deteriorating, demolishing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are renovating the kitchen. = 我们正在装修厨房。
They renovate houses for a living. = 他们以装修房屋为生。

一般过去时:
They renovated the bathroom last year. = 他们去年装修了浴室。
She renovated her old car. = 她翻新了她的旧车。

现在完成时:
We have renovated three houses this year. = 今年我们已经装修了三套房子。
They have completely renovated the building. = 他们已经彻底翻修了这座建筑。

过去完成时:
They had renovated the hotel before the grand opening. = 他们在盛大开幕之前已经装修了酒店。
She had renovated her portfolio before submitting it. = 她在提交作品集之前已经对其进行了更新。

将来时:
We will renovate the living room next month. = 我们下个月将装修客厅。
They will be renovating the office during the summer. = 他们将在夏天装修办公室。

虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would renovate the entire house. = 如果我们有更多钱，我们将翻新整栋房子。
I wish I could renovate my old bicycle. = 我真希望我能翻新我的旧自行车。

被动语态:
The house is being renovated at the moment. = 这栋房子目前正在装修。
The software was renovated last month. = 该软件上个月进行了更新。

疑问句:
Are they renovating the building? = 他们正在装修那栋建筑物吗？
Is the car being renovated? = 这辆车正在翻新吗？

否定句:
We are not renovating the garage this year. = 今年我们不装修车库。
They did not renovate the shop. = 他们没有装修商店。
