disinvested:/ˌdɪsɪnˈvɛstɪd/ | v. |撤资；放弃投资；剥夺…的投资 | To withdraw investment from something.  This can refer to a company divesting itself of assets, an individual selling off investments, or a government withdrawing support from a project or industry. |例句：The company disinvested from the failing project.  公司撤出了那个亏损项目。|例句：He disinvested his portfolio of high-risk stocks. 他抛售了他投资组合中高风险的股票。|近义词：divest, withdraw, sell off |反义词：invest, fund


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company disinvests in failing businesses. = 公司对亏损企业撤资。
She disinvests regularly to diversify her portfolio. = 她定期撤资以使投资组合多元化。

一般过去时:
They disinvested in the oil industry last year. = 他们去年撤出了石油行业投资。
He disinvested his savings from the real estate market. = 他从房地产市场撤回了储蓄。

现在完成时:
The government has disinvested heavily in public transportation. = 政府已大幅削减公共交通投资。
We have disinvested from that stock because of its poor performance. = 我们因为这只股票表现不佳而撤资了。


过去完成时:
Before the economic downturn, they had disinvested from several projects. = 在经济衰退之前，他们已经撤出了几个项目投资。
She had disinvested her funds before the market crash. = 市场崩盘前，她已经撤回了资金。


将来时:
They will disinvest in fossil fuels over the next decade. = 未来十年，他们将撤出化石燃料的投资。
We will disinvest if the risks become too high. = 如果风险过高，我们将撤资。


虚拟语气:
If they had disinvested earlier, they would have avoided heavy losses. = 如果他们早点撤资，就能避免巨额损失。
I wish I had disinvested from that company years ago. = 我真希望几年前就从那家公司撤资了。


被动语态:
Millions of dollars were disinvested from the research program. = 数百万美元的资金从该研究项目中撤出。
The project was disinvested in due to budget constraints. = 由于预算限制，该项目被撤资。


疑问句:
Has the company disinvested from any of its subsidiaries? = 公司是否已从任何子公司撤资？
Will they disinvest in renewable energy? = 他们会撤出可再生能源的投资吗？


否定句:
The investor did not disinvest despite the market volatility. = 尽管市场波动，投资者并没有撤资。
They haven't disinvested from the technology sector. = 他们还没有撤出科技行业的投资。


PHRASE->
disinvest in = 撤出…的投资
disinvest from = 从…撤资
strategic disinvestment = 战略性撤资
partial disinvestment = 部分撤资
forced disinvestment = 被迫撤资
