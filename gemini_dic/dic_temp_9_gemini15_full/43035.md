cowgirls:/ˈkaʊɡɜːrlz/| n. |女牛仔|A cowgirl is a woman who works on a ranch or farm, often riding horses and involved in cattle herding.  The term often evokes a sense of independence, skill, and a rugged lifestyle. |The cowgirls expertly herded the cattle across the plains.  那些女牛仔熟练地将牛群赶过平原。| female cowboys, ranch hands (women)|  (近义词和反义词不适用，因为词义本身不包含对立的概念)|名词
