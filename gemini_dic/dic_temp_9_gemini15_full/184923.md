retaped:/riːˈteɪpt/ | v. |重新录音；重新粘贴 | To record something again; to tape something again.  |例句：The singer retaped the song after discovering a mistake. 歌手发现错误后重新录制了这首歌。|例句：The artist carefully retaped the torn painting.艺术家小心地重新粘贴了撕裂的画作。|近义词：rerecorded, reattached |反义词：removed, detached


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retapes his podcasts every week. 他每周都重新录制他的播客。
She retapes the edges of her artwork. 她重新粘贴她艺术作品的边缘。

一般过去时:
They retaped the interview after the power outage. 停电后，他们重新录了采访。
He retaped the loose button on his shirt. 他重新缝上了衬衫上松动的纽扣。

现在完成时:
I have retaped the audio several times to get it perfect. 我已经多次重新录音以使其完美。
She has retaped the poster to the wall. 她已经把海报重新贴到了墙上。

过去完成时:
We had retaped the scene before the director arrived. 在导演到达之前，我们已经重新拍摄了那个场景。
The technician had retaped all the wires before turning the machine on. 技术员在启动机器之前已经重新粘贴了所有电线。
