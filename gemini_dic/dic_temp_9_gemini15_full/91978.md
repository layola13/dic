hypernationalistic: /ˌhaɪpərˌnæʃənəlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 极端民族主义的 | Extremely nationalistic; characterized by excessive patriotism and a belief in the superiority of one's own nation.  It often involves aggressive or hostile attitudes towards other nations. |


例句：

The government's hypernationalistic rhetoric inflamed tensions with neighboring countries.  政府极端民族主义的言论激化了与邻国的紧张关系。

His hypernationalistic views led him to reject any form of international cooperation.  他极端民族主义的观点使他拒绝任何形式的国际合作。


近义词：ultranationalistic, chauvinistic, jingoistic, excessively patriotic


反义词：internationalist, cosmopolitan, globalist


词性: 形容词
