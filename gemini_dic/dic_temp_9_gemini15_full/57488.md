drag-on:/dræɡˈɔːn/| v.| 拖延，延长，唠叨 | To prolong something, especially in an annoying way; to speak tediously and at length. |例句：The meeting dragged on for hours.  会议拖延了好几个小时。

例句：He dragged on and on about his trip. 他喋喋不休地谈论他的旅行。


近义词：prolong, extend, delay, procrastinate


反义词：expedite, hasten, accelerate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project drags on. 项目进展缓慢。
He drags on about his problems. 他没完没了地抱怨他的问题。

一般过去时:
The negotiations dragged on for weeks.  谈判持续了好几个星期。
She dragged on about her childhood. 她没完没了地谈论她的童年。

现在完成时:
The repairs have dragged on for months. 修理工作已经拖延了几个月。
He has dragged on about that story for years. 多年来他一直在喋喋不休地讲述那个故事。

过去完成时:
The case had dragged on for years before it finally went to trial.  案件在最终开庭审理之前已经拖延了好几年。
She had dragged on about her ex-boyfriend for hours before we finally left.在我们最终离开之前，她已经没完没了地谈论她前任男友好几个小时了。

将来时:
The construction will drag on into next year.  施工将持续到明年。
I'm afraid this conversation will drag on for a long time. 我担心这次谈话会持续很长时间。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't dragged on so much, we would have finished earlier. 如果他没有拖延那么多，我们就能早点完成了。
I wish he wouldn't drag on so much when he tells a story. 我希望他讲故事的时候不要说得那么冗长。

被动语态:
The decision was dragged on until the last minute.  决定被拖延到最后一刻。
The meeting was dragged on by unnecessary debates. 会议因为不必要的辩论而被拖长了。

疑问句:
Why does this process always drag on? 为什么这个过程总是拖延？
Will the meeting drag on late into the night? 会议会拖到深夜吗？

否定句:
The work didn't drag on as long as we expected. 工作没有像我们预期的那样拖延。
He didn't drag on about his accomplishments, thankfully.  谢天谢地，他没有喋喋不休地谈论他的成就。
