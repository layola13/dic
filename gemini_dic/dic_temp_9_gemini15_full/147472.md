overopinionatedly:/ˌoʊvərəˈpɪnjənˌeɪtɪdli/ | adv. | 过于武断地；固执己见地 |  The adverb "overopinionatedly" describes an action performed in an excessively opinionated manner, suggesting stubbornness, inflexibility, and a disregard for others' viewpoints. It implies a domineering and assertive expression of opinions, often without sufficient justification or consideration for alternative perspectives. | He overopinionatedly dismissed all counterarguments, refusing to consider any other possibility. 他过于武断地驳斥了所有反驳意见，拒绝考虑任何其他可能性。 |  dogmatically, assertively, stubbornly |  open-mindedly, receptively, considerately | adv.
