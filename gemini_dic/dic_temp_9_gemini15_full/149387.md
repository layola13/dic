pacing:/ˈpeɪsɪŋ/| n. v.|步速；节奏；踱步|The act of walking at a steady pace or the speed at which someone or something moves.  It can also refer to the rhythm or tempo of something, such as a speech or a piece of music. |例句：He set a brisk pacing for the marathon. 他为马拉松设定了快速的步速。

The speaker's pacing was too slow and monotonous. 演讲者的节奏太慢，太单调了。|近义词：stride, gait, tempo, rhythm|反义词：hurrying, rushing, speeding
