nonstainer: /nɒnˈsteɪnə(r)/ | n. |非染色剂；不染色物 | A substance that does not stain or color something.  A nonstainer is typically used in contrast to a stain or dye, referring to materials or substances that do not leave a mark or alter the color of the material they are applied to. | Example:  "The new paint is a nonstainer, so spills won't leave permanent marks." = "这种新型油漆是非染色剂，所以溢出物不会留下永久性痕迹。" | 近义词：unstainer, non-coloring agent | 反义词：stain, dye, colorant |
