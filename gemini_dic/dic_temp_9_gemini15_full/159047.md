pneumo: /ˈnjuːmoʊ/ | adj. |肺的；气体的| Relating to the lungs or air.  |The term "pneumothorax" refers to air in the pleural space surrounding the lungs.|近义词：pulmonary, respiratory |反义词：N/A


英文解析:
The combining form "pneumo-" is derived from the Greek word "pneumon" (πνευμών), meaning "lung". It's used as a prefix in medical terminology to indicate a relationship with the lungs or air.

例句:
Pneumococcal pneumonia is a serious lung infection.  =肺炎球菌肺炎是一种严重的肺部感染。

Pneumoconiosis is a lung disease caused by inhaling dust. =尘肺病是由吸入灰尘引起的肺部疾病。
