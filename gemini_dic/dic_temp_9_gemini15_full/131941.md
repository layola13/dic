noncontradictories:/ˌnɑːnkɑːntrəˈdɪktəriːz/ | n. | 非矛盾的命题；不互相矛盾的事物 |  Things that do not contradict each other; propositions that can be true at the same time without logical inconsistency.  These statements are noncontradictories because they don't clash; one doesn't negate the other. | The statements "The sky is blue" and "Grass is green" are noncontradictories.  The concepts of "hot" and "cold" are noncontradictories in that they are distinct but not mutually exclusive. | synonyms: consistencies, compatibles | antonyms: contradictions, inconsistencies | noun
