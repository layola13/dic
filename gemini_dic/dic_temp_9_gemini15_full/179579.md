reemerge:/ˌriːɪˈmɜːrdʒ/ | v. |重新出现；复出；再次涌现 | To appear or become known again after a period of time. |

例句：

The sun reemerged from behind the clouds.  太阳从云层后面重新出现。

After a long illness, she reemerged into public life.  经过长期疾病后，她重新出现在公众视野中。


近义词： reappear, resurface, return, revive

反义词： disappear, vanish, fade


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The old traditions reemerge in modern society.  古老的传统在现代社会中重新出现。
His talent reemerges every time he plays guitar. 他每次弹吉他时，他的天赋都会重新显现。

一般过去时:
The lost dog reemerged after three days.  失踪的狗三天后重新出现。
The issue reemerged during the debate.  这个问题在辩论中再次出现。

现在完成时:
The band has reemerged with a new album.  乐队带着一张新专辑重新复出了。
The symptoms have reemerged after a period of remission.  症状在缓解期后再次出现。

过去完成时:
Before the storm hit, the sun had reemerged briefly.  暴风雨来临之前，太阳短暂地重新出现过。
After the scandal, his reputation had reemerged, somewhat tarnished.  丑闻之后，他的名誉虽然有些受损，但又重新出现了。

将来时:
The comet will reemerge in the night sky next year.  这颗彗星明年将再次出现在夜空中。
I believe the problem will reemerge if not dealt with properly. 我认为如果处理不当，这个问题将会再次出现。

虚拟语气:
If the economy improved, the business might reemerge stronger. 如果经济好转，这家企业可能会更强大地重新出现。
I wish the old feeling of friendship would reemerge. 我希望旧日友谊的感觉能够重现。

被动语态:
The lost painting was reemerged in an auction.  失窃的画作在一场拍卖会上再次出现。
The truth was reemerged after years of investigation.  经过多年的调查，真相终于大白。


疑问句:
Will the problem reemerge after the solution?  解决方法之后，问题还会再次出现吗？
When will the star reemerge in the sky?  这颗星什么时候会再次出现在天空中？

否定句:
The lost treasure never reemerged.  失落的宝藏再也没有出现过。
His old habits did not reemerge after his rehabilitation. 康复之后，他的旧习惯没有再出现。
