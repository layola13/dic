disappears:/dɪs.əˈpɪrz/ | v. |消失，不见 |To cease to be visible; vanish from sight.  It can also refer to something ceasing to exist or becoming undetectable.| The magician made the rabbit disappear.  魔术师让兔子消失了。|The sun disappeared behind the clouds. 太阳消失在云层后面。 |appears, materializes |appears, manifests| verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun disappears below the horizon every evening.  太阳每天晚上都消失在地平线以下。
Magic tricks often involve objects that disappear and reappear. 魔术戏法通常涉及消失和重现的物体。

一般过去时:
The train disappeared into the tunnel. 火车消失在隧道里。
My keys disappeared mysteriously; I couldn't find them anywhere. 我的钥匙神秘地消失了；我到处都找不到它们。

现在完成时:
The symptoms have disappeared since I started the new medication.自从我开始服用新药以来，症状已经消失了。
My fear of public speaking has disappeared after years of practice.经过多年的练习，我害怕公开演讲的恐惧消失了。

过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, the snow had disappeared. 当我到达时，雪已经消失了。
The evidence had disappeared before the police could investigate. 在警方调查之前，证据就消失了。

将来时:
The pain will disappear once the medication takes effect. 一旦药物生效，疼痛就会消失。
The problem will disappear if we address the root cause. 如果我们解决根本原因，问题就会消失。

虚拟语气:
If the pain didn't disappear, I would see a doctor. 如果疼痛没有消失，我会去看医生。
I wish my worries would disappear.我希望我的担忧能消失。

被动语态:
The evidence was made to disappear by someone. 证据被某人弄消失了。
The problem will be disappeared if we find a solution. 如果我们找到解决方案，问题将会消失。

疑问句:
Has the pain disappeared yet? 疼痛消失了没有？
Did the evidence disappear naturally? 证据是自然消失的吗？


否定句:
The problem hasn't disappeared yet. 问题还没有消失。
The symptoms didn't disappear after taking the medicine. 服药后症状没有消失。


PHRASE->
disappear without a trace: 彻底消失
disappear into thin air: 凭空消失
make something disappear: 使某物消失
vanish into thin air: 突然消失
fade away and disappear: 逐渐消失
