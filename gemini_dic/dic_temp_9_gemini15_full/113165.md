lupus:/ˈluːpəs/| n.|狼疮|A chronic autoimmune disease in which the body's immune system attacks healthy tissues.|例句：She was diagnosed with lupus several years ago. 她几年前被诊断出患有狼疮。|近义词：autoimmune disease|反义词:None.


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations.  Therefore, tenses like past, present, future, etc., are not applicable.  The word itself describes a condition.


PHRASE->
systemic lupus erythematosus: 系统性红斑狼疮
discoid lupus: 盘状狼疮
drug-induced lupus: 药物诱发性狼疮
lupus nephritis: 狼疮性肾炎
cutaneous lupus: 皮肤狼疮
