methodologically:/ˌmɛθədəˈlɒdʒɪkli/| adv. |方法上地；方法论地 |In a manner relating to methodology; systematically and according to a specific method.  It describes how something is done in terms of its method or approach. |The study was methodologically sound, employing rigorous data collection and analysis techniques.  这项研究方法严谨，采用了严格的数据收集和分析技术。| systematically, systematically, rigorously | haphazardly, unsystematically
