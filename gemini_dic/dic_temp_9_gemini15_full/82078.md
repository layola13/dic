grizzler:/ˈɡrɪzlər/| n.|脾气暴躁的人，抱怨者，（尤指）爱发牢骚的老年人|A grizzler is a person, typically an elderly one, who is habitually grumpy, complaining, or irritable.  They are often characterized by their tendency to grumble and find fault.|例句：He's become a real grizzler in his old age. 他上了年纪后变得非常爱发牢骚。|例句：The old grizzler sat on the park bench, muttering to himself.  那个爱发牢骚的老头坐在公园长椅上，自言自语。|近义词：grumbler, complainer, grouch, curmudgeon|反义词：optimist, cheerful person, good-natured person|名词
