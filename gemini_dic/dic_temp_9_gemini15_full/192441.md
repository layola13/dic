scrubbed:/skrʌbd/| v.|擦洗；冲洗；去除；取消|To clean something by rubbing it hard, especially with a brush and water.  Also to remove something from a surface by rubbing. Or to cancel or postpone something.|例句：She scrubbed the floor until it shone. 她用力擦洗地板直到闪闪发光。

例句：The doctor scrubbed in for the operation. 医生为手术做了术前准备（彻底洗手消毒）。


近义词：clean, wash, scour, wipe, cancel, postpone|反义词：dirty, soiled, leave alone, keep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scrubs the dishes every night. = 他每天晚上都洗碗。
She scrubs the bathtub regularly. = 她定期擦洗浴缸。


一般过去时:
I scrubbed the kitchen floor yesterday. = 我昨天擦洗了厨房地板。
They scrubbed the paint off the walls. = 他们把墙上的油漆擦掉了。


现在完成时:
I have scrubbed the stains out of the carpet. = 我已经把地毯上的污渍擦掉了。
She has scrubbed the deck clean. = 她已经把甲板擦干净了。


过去完成时:
He had scrubbed the car before the rain started. = 下雨前他已经把车洗干净了。
They had scrubbed the potatoes before boiling them. = 他们在煮土豆之前已经把土豆擦洗干净了。


将来时:
I will scrub the bathroom tomorrow. = 我明天将擦洗浴室。
She will scrub the paint off the window. = 她将把窗户上的油漆擦掉。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would scrub the whole house. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把整个房子都擦洗干净。
I wish I had scrubbed the dishes earlier. = 我真希望我早点洗碗。


被动语态:
The floor was scrubbed clean. = 地板被擦洗干净了。
The walls were scrubbed to remove the graffiti. = 墙壁被擦洗以去除涂鸦。


疑问句:
Did you scrub the pots and pans? = 你洗锅碗瓢盆了吗？
Have you scrubbed the floor yet? = 你已经擦洗地板了吗？


否定句:
I didn't scrub the car. = 我没有洗车。
She hasn't scrubbed the bathtub yet. = 她还没擦洗浴缸。
