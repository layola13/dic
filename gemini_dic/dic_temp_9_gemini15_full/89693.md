hooters:/ˈhuːtərz/| n.|胡特斯餐厅；猫头鹰叫声；嘘声 |A chain of restaurants known for their waitresses' attire and atmosphere; the sound made by an owl; a sound of disapproval made by putting one's lips together and blowing air through them. |例句：We went to Hooters for lunch. = 我们去胡特斯餐厅吃午饭了。  He heard the hooters of the owls in the forest. = 他听到森林里猫头鹰的叫声。  The audience gave a few hooters of disapproval. = 观众发出了几声表示反对的嘘声。|近义词：restaurant (for the restaurant), owl sounds (for the owl sounds), boos (for the disapproval sound) |反义词：silence (for the sounds), approval (for the disapproval)|名词
