redisburse:/ˌriːdɪsˈbɜːrs/| vt.|重新分配，再分配|To distribute again or anew; to pay out again.|The company will redisburse the funds to its shareholders. 公司将把资金重新分配给股东。|近义词：redistribute, reallocate, reapportion|反义词：collect, gather

The government redisburses the tax revenue annually. = 政府每年重新分配税收收入。
The company redisburses profits to its investors quarterly. = 公司每季度向投资者重新分配利润。
