falsettist:/fɔːlˈsɛtɪst/| n. |男高音; 假声男歌手 | A falsettist is a male singer who sings in falsetto.  They use their head voice to produce high-pitched notes, rather than their chest voice. | Example: The opera featured a remarkable falsettist whose high notes were breathtaking.  这位歌剧演出中有一位非凡的假声男歌手，他的高音令人叹为观止。 |近义词：countertenor, high tenor |反义词：bass, baritone
