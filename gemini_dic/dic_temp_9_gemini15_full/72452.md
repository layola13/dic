flumed:/fluːmd/| v.|用水槽输送；用沟渠引流|To convey (something, especially water or timber) in a flume.|The miners flumed the gold down the mountain.  矿工们用水槽把黄金从山上运下来。|They flumed the logs to the mill. 他们用水槽把原木运到磨坊。|transport, channel|block, dam

The water flumes down the mountainside. = 水顺着山坡流下来。

The workers flumed the ore to the processing plant. = 工人们用水槽把矿石运送到加工厂。
They flumed the timber downstream. = 他们用水槽把木材运到下游。
