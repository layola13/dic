playgame:/pleɪɡeɪm/| n. v.|玩游戏|The act of playing a game; a game itself.  It can be used as a noun referring to the activity or as a verb describing the action of playing.|例句：Playing video games is his favorite pastime. 玩电子游戏是他的最爱。|例句：Let's play a game of chess. 我们来下盘棋吧。|近义词：game, pastime, amusement|反义词：work, labor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plays games every day. = 他每天都玩游戏。
They play board games on weekends. = 他们周末玩棋盘游戏。

一般过去时:
She played a video game last night. = 她昨晚玩了一个电子游戏。
We played cards at the party. = 我们在派对上玩牌。

现在完成时:
I have played this game many times. = 我玩过这个游戏很多次了。
They have played all the levels. = 他们已经玩完了所有关卡。

过去完成时:
He had played the game before, so he knew the rules. = 他以前玩过这个游戏，所以他知道规则。
She had played for hours before she finally won. = 她玩了好几个小时才最终赢了。

将来时:
We will play a new game tomorrow. = 我们明天将玩一个新的游戏。
They will play online games this evening. = 他们今晚将玩网络游戏。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would play more games. = 如果我有更多时间，我会玩更多游戏。
I wish I could play that game. = 我真希望我能玩那个游戏。

被动语态:
The game is played by millions of people. = 这个游戏被数百万人玩。
The new game is being played by everyone. = 正在人人都玩这款新游戏。

疑问句:
Do you play games often? = 你经常玩游戏吗？
What kind of games do they play? = 他们玩什么类型的游戏？

否定句:
He doesn't play violent games. = 他不玩暴力游戏。
She didn't play the game well. = 她玩得不好。
