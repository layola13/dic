pronged:/prɒŋd/ /prɑːŋd/| adj.| 多叉的；多方面的；多管齐下的 | Having more than one point, branch, or division.  It often describes a plan or approach that involves multiple parts or aspects working together. | 例句：The company launched a three-pronged attack on the market. 公司对市场发起了三管齐下的进攻。

例句：His argument was a multi-pronged attack on the government's policies. 他的论点是对政府政策的多方面攻击。


近义词：multifaceted, multi-faceted, many-sided, multifaceted, complex


反义词：single-pronged, simple, straightforward


词性:形容词
