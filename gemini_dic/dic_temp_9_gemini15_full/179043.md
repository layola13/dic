redemonstrating:/ˌriːˈdɛmənstreɪtɪŋ/| v.|再次演示；重新展示 |The act of demonstrating something again, often to reinforce understanding or address questions. It implies a previous demonstration has already taken place. |例句：The teacher was redemonstrating the experiment to the students who had missed the first showing. 教师正在向错过第一次演示的学生再次演示实验。

例句：The technician was redemonstrating the software's functionality after a user reported a bug. 技术人员在用户报告错误后重新演示了软件的功能。


近义词：re-enacting, showing again, re-explaining


反义词：concealing, hiding, obscuring


词性：动词
