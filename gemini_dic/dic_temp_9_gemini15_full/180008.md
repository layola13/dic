refinement:/rɪˈfaɪnmənt/| n. |精致；优雅；改进；提纯|The act of improving something by making small changes; the process of removing impurities from a substance; elegance and sophistication of manner or taste.|例句：The refinement of the design made it more user-friendly.  设计的改进使其更易于使用。|例句：The refinery processes crude oil into gasoline.  炼油厂将原油加工成汽油。|近义词：improvement, enhancement, sophistication, polish|反义词：roughness, crudeness, coarseness|名词


USAGE->

The chef focuses on the refinement of his dishes. = 这位厨师专注于改进他的菜肴。
