nitroglycerin:/ˌnaɪtroʊˈɡlɪsərɪn/| n. |硝化甘油|A heavy, oily, explosive liquid made from nitric acid, sulfuric acid, and glycerol (glycerine). It is used in the manufacture of explosives and as a medicine to treat angina (chest pain).|例句：Nitroglycerin is a powerful explosive used in mining. 硝化甘油是一种用于采矿的强力炸药。|例句：Doctors sometimes prescribe nitroglycerin to patients experiencing angina.医生有时会为患有心绞痛的病人开硝化甘油。|近义词：glyceryl trinitrate|反义词：无
