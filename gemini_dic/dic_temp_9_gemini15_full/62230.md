encroachers:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃərz/| n. |侵占者，蚕食者 |People or things that encroach upon something else, gradually taking over or invading it.  They often trespass on land or rights. |例句：The encroachers on the national park were fined heavily. 国家公园里的侵占者被处以重罚。
例句：The expanding city's encroachers threatened the local wildlife.  不断扩张的城市侵占者威胁到当地野生动物。 |近义词：intruders, trespassers, invaders |反义词：protectors, preservers
