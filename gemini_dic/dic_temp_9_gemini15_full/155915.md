photocopiable:/ˈfoʊtoʊkɑːpiəbl/ | adj. | 可影印的，可复印的 |  Able to be photocopied.  This adjective describes something that can be easily and successfully reproduced using a photocopier.  | Example: This worksheet is photocopiable for classroom use. 这张工作表可供课堂使用影印。 | 近义词：reproducible, duplicable | 反义词：non-reproducible
