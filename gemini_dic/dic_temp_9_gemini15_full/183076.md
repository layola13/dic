repondering:/rɪˈpɒndərɪŋ/ | v. | 回答；回应；考虑 |  The act of responding or considering something thoughtfully.  It implies a more deliberate and thoughtful response than a simple answer.  | Example: He spent the evening pondering the implications of his decision. 他花了整个晚上思考他决定的影响。 | 近义词：considering, reflecting, contemplating, deliberating | 反义词：ignoring, disregarding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pondering the problem. = 他正在思考这个问题。
She is pondering her options. = 她正在考虑她的选择。


一般过去时:
He pondered the question for a long time. = 他长时间地思考这个问题。
She pondered the implications of her actions. = 她仔细考虑了她行为的影响。


现在完成时:
I have been pondering this issue for days. = 我几天来一直在思考这个问题。
They have pondered the matter carefully. = 他们仔细考虑了这件事。


过去完成时:
He had pondered the matter before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前已经考虑过这件事了。
She had pondered the possibilities for weeks. = 她已经考虑这些可能性好几个星期了。


将来时:
I will ponder this proposal before responding. = 我将在回应这个提案之前仔细考虑。
They will ponder their next move. = 他们将仔细考虑他们的下一步行动。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would ponder the matter further. = 如果我有更多时间，我会进一步考虑这件事。
I wish I had pondered the consequences more carefully. = 我希望我当时更仔细地考虑了后果。


被动语态:
The problem was being pondered by the committee. = 这个问题正在由委员会考虑。
The proposal is being pondered by the management. = 这个提案正在由管理层考虑。


疑问句:
Are you pondering the meaning of life? = 你在思考人生的意义吗？
What are they pondering over there? = 他们在那边思考什么？


否定句:
He did not ponder the consequences of his actions. = 他没有考虑他行为的后果。
She didn't ponder the offer for long. = 她没有长时间考虑这个提议。


PHRASE->
ponder over = 仔细考虑
ponder a question = 思考一个问题
ponder the implications = 考虑影响
ponder the possibilities = 考虑可能性
long and hard ponder = 深思熟虑
