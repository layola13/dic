minesweepers:/ˈmaɪnswiːpərz/| n. |扫雷舰（复数）|Ships designed to locate and detonate or disarm naval mines.  They play a crucial role in clearing waterways for safe navigation. |The minesweepers cleared the harbor of mines before the ships could enter.|近义词：minehunters|反义词：无
