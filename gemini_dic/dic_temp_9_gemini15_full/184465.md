restack:/ˌriːˈstæk/| vt.|重新堆放；重新码放|To stack (something) again; to rearrange items in a stack.|例句：We need to restack the shelves after the inventory. 我们需要盘点后重新整理货架。

例句：The librarian had to restack the books after the children were finished. 图书管理员不得不让孩子们看完书后重新码放书籍。


近义词：reorganize, rearrange, restow|反义词：scatter, disperse|动词
