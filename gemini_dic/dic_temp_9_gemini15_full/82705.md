guia:/ˈɡiːə/ | n. |指南，向导，导游 | A guia is a person who guides or leads others, especially tourists.  It can also refer to a guidebook or manual.  |例句：The tour guia led us through the ancient ruins.  导游带领我们参观了古老的废墟。

例句：I used a hiking guia to find my way through the mountains. 我用徒步旅行指南在山里找到方向。

近义词：guide, leader, manual, handbook

反义词：follower
