peristerite:/pəˈrɪstəraɪt/| n. |透闪石；鸽石|A variety of plagioclase feldspar exhibiting a characteristic iridescent play of colors, often described as a shimmering or pearly effect. This effect is due to the microscopic lamellar structure of the mineral. Peristerite is relatively rare and is valued as a gemstone.|The peristerite in her ring shimmered beautifully in the sunlight.|近义词：labradorite (similar iridescent effect)|反义词：无
