nookiest:/ˈnuːkiːst/| adj.|最舒适的，最隐蔽的，最僻静的|Most comfortable, secluded, or private.  The word implies a cozy and delightful seclusion.|例句：The nookiest little cafe was tucked away down a cobbled alley. 最舒适的小咖啡馆藏身于一条鹅卵石小巷深处。  The nookiest corner of the library was my favorite spot to read. 图书馆里最隐蔽的角落是我最喜欢的阅读场所。|近义词：coziest, snuggest, most secluded |反义词：most exposed, least private
