inert gases:/ˈɪnɜːrt ɡæsɪz/| n.|惰性气体|Inert gases are chemical elements that are very unreactive. They exist as monatomic gases under normal conditions.|Example: Helium, neon, and argon are examples of inert gases. 氦气、氖气和氩气是惰性气体的例子。|近义词：noble gases|反义词：reactive gases
