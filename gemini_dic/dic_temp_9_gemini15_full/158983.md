plutocratizing: /ˌpluːtoʊkrəˈtaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |使成为寡头统治；使受富豪统治 | The process of concentrating power and wealth in the hands of a small elite, thus creating or reinforcing a plutocracy.  |例句：The country is slowly plutocratizing, with the gap between rich and poor widening dramatically. 这个国家正在慢慢走向寡头统治，贫富差距急剧扩大。

The increasing influence of wealthy donors is plutocratizing the political system. 富裕捐赠者的影响力日益增强，正在使政治体系走向寡头统治。| 近义词：oligarchizing, enriching (the elite), concentrating wealth | 反义词：democratizing, egalitarianizing
