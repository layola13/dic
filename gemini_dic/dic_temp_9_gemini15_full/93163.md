igniters:/ɪɡˈnaɪtərz/ | n. | 点火器；引发者 | Devices that ignite something, such as a fuel or explosive.  They can be mechanical, electrical, or chemical. | Example: The car's igniters failed, preventing the engine from starting.  汽车的点火器失灵了，导致发动机无法启动。The social unrest acted as an igniter for widespread protests. 社会动荡成为引发大规模抗议的导火索。 | 近义词：kindlers, starters, triggers | 反义词：extinguishers, suppressors | 名词
