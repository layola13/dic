canvassed:/ˈkænvəst/| v.| 仔细调查；游说；征求意见；在画布上作画 | To discuss or seek opinions from (a group of people). To solicit votes or support. To cover with canvas. |例句：The team canvassed the neighborhood for support. (团队挨家挨户地争取支持。)  The artist canvassed the landscape onto the large canvas. (艺术家把风景画在了大画布上。) |近义词：surveyed, polled, solicited, canvassed |反义词：ignored, neglected


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They canvas the neighborhood for donations every year. = 他们每年都在附近街区征集捐款。
We canvas public opinion on the new policy. = 我们正在调查公众对新政策的意见。

一般过去时:
The candidates canvassed extensively before the election. = 候选人在选举前进行了广泛的游说。
The artist canvassed the scene before starting to paint. = 艺术家在开始作画前仔细观察了场景。


现在完成时:
They have canvassed the entire city for potential customers. = 他们已经走遍了整个城市寻找潜在客户。
The committee has canvassed the members' opinions on the proposal. = 委员会已经征求了成员们对该提案的意见。


过去完成时:
They had canvassed the area thoroughly before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经彻底勘察了该地区。
The politician had canvassed every household in the district before the election. = 在选举前，这位政治家已经走访了该地区的每一个家庭。


将来时:
We will canvas the local businesses for sponsorships. = 我们将向当地企业寻求赞助。
The campaign team will canvas the voters next week. = 活动团队下周将进行选民游说。


虚拟语气:
If we had canvassed more voters, we might have won the election. = 如果我们争取更多的选民，我们可能会赢得选举。
I wish I had canvassed the market before launching the product. = 我希望在推出产品之前我已经对市场进行了调查。


被动语态:
The neighborhood was canvassed for information about the crime. = 街坊邻居们被询问有关犯罪的信息。
Public opinion was canvassed before the decision was made. = 在做出决定之前，征求了公众意见。


疑问句:
Have they canvassed the entire community? = 他们是否已经走访了整个社区？
Did you canvas your friends for support? = 你有没有向你的朋友寻求支持？


否定句:
They did not canvas the area properly. = 他们没有对该地区进行适当的调查。
We didn't canvas enough people for the survey. = 我们没有为调查征求足够多的人的意见。
