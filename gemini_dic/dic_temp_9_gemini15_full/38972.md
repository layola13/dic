confinedness: /kənˈfaɪndnɪs/ | n. | 侷促；限制；禁锢 | The feeling or state of being confined; restriction of movement, opportunity, or expression.  It often implies a feeling of being trapped or limited. |例句：The confinedness of the small apartment began to weigh on her.  狭小的公寓让她感到压抑。

例句：The confinedness of his life led to feelings of isolation and frustration. 他受限的生活导致了他感到孤独和沮丧。


近义词：restriction, limitation, constraint, imprisonment, confinement, claustrophobia (if referring to a feeling)


反义词：freedom, openness, spaciousness, liberty


词性：名词
