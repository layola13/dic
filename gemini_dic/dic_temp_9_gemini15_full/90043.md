hospitalise: /ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪz/ /ˈhɑːspɪtəlaɪz/ | vt. | 将…送入医院；使住院 | To place (someone) in a hospital for treatment.  |  The doctor decided to hospitalise the patient after the accident.  医生决定在事故后将病人送入医院。 | admit, confine | discharge, release | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hospital routinely hospitalises patients with severe injuries. = 医院通常会收治重伤病人。
They hospitalise children with infectious diseases immediately. = 他们立即将患有传染病的儿童送入医院。


一般过去时:
The surgeon hospitalised her after the operation. = 手术后，外科医生把她送进了医院。
He was hospitalised for a week due to pneumonia. = 他因肺炎住院一周。


现在完成时:
They have hospitalised over 100 patients this month. = 这个月他们已经收治了100多名病人。
She has been hospitalised three times this year. = 她今年已经住院三次了。


过去完成时:
He had been hospitalised twice before this incident. = 在这次事件之前，他已经住院过两次了。
The doctors had hospitalised her quickly after the car crash. = 车祸发生后，医生迅速将她送进了医院。


将来时:
They will hospitalise him if his condition worsens. = 如果他的病情恶化，他们会将他送入医院。
The paramedics will hospitalise the victim immediately. = 救援人员会立即将受害者送往医院。


虚拟语气:
If his condition deteriorated, they would hospitalise him immediately. = 如果他的病情恶化，他们会立即将他送入医院。
I wish they had hospitalised him sooner. = 我希望他们早点把他送进医院。


被动语态:
Many patients are hospitalised each day. = 每天都有许多病人住院。
He was hospitalised against his will. = 他被强迫住院。


疑问句:
Will they hospitalise the child? = 他们会把孩子送进医院吗？
Was he hospitalised after the accident? = 事故后他住院了吗？


否定句:
They did not hospitalise him because his injuries were minor. = 因为他的伤势较轻，他们没有把他送进医院。
The doctor decided not to hospitalise her. = 医生决定不让她住院。
