disassembles:/ˌdɪs əˈsɛmbəl/ | vt. |拆卸；分解；解体 |To take something apart into its separate components.  |例句：The mechanic disassembles the engine to repair it. 机械师拆卸发动机以修理它。|例句：The instructions explain how to disassemble the furniture. 说明书解释了如何拆卸家具。|近义词：take apart, dismantle, decompose |反义词：assemble, construct, build


USAGE->

He disassembles the computer every year for cleaning. 他每年拆卸电脑进行清洁。
She disassembles the toy to understand how it works. 她拆卸玩具来了解它的工作原理。
