sarment:/ˈsɑːrmənt/| n.|藤蔓；枝条；（植物的）嫩枝|A sarment is a young shoot or twig of a vine or other climbing plant. It often refers to a flexible, slender stem that trails or climbs.  |Example: The gardener carefully pruned the sarments of the grapevine to encourage fruit production. 园丁仔细修剪葡萄藤的嫩枝以促进果实生长。|近义词：tendril, vine, shoot, twig|反义词：trunk, branch (mature)
