interradiates:/ˌɪntərˈeɪdieɪts/| v. |互相辐射；相互影响|To radiate or spread mutually; to affect one another reciprocally.|The colors in the stained glass window interradiate, creating a vibrant and dazzling effect.  彩绘玻璃窗中的颜色相互辉映，创造出鲜艳夺目的效果。|The ideas of the two philosophers interradiate throughout their works. 两位哲学家的思想在他们的著作中相互影响。|  irradiate, influence, affect |  isolate, separate | 动词

These ideas interradiate to form a new theory. = 这些思想相互影响形成了一个新的理论。
