outmuscled:/ˌaʊtˈmʌsəld/| vt.|胜过；以力量强于|To defeat or surpass someone by having greater strength or power.|例句：The stronger team outmuscled their opponents in the final match. 更强的队伍在决赛中以力量优势战胜了对手。

例句：He completely outmuscled his competitor in the business deal. 他在商业交易中完全以实力压倒了竞争对手。


近义词：overpower, overwhelm, outmatch, surpass|反义词：be outmatched, be overpowered
