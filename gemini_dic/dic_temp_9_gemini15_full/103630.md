jojoba oil:/ˈdʒoʊˌdʒoʊbə ɔɪl/| n. |霍霍巴油|Jojoba oil is a liquid wax ester produced from the seeds of the jojoba plant (Simmondsia chinensis). It is used in cosmetics, skincare products, and as a lubricant.|Jojoba oil is a popular ingredient in many skincare products. = 霍霍巴油是许多护肤产品中的一种流行成分。|The oil is known for its moisturizing properties. =这种油以其保湿性能而闻名。|近义词：moisturizer, emollient|反义词：N/A
