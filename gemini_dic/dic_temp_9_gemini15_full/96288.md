indifferentist:/ɪnˈdɪfərənɪst/| n. |漠不关心的人；无动于衷者|A person who is indifferent; one who shows apathy or lack of concern.  |Example: He remained an indifferent observer, neither helping nor hindering. 他保持着冷漠的旁观者姿态，既不帮忙也不阻挠。|近义词：apathistic, unconcerned, detached|反义词：enthusiast, passionate, caring|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The indifferent person ignores the suffering of others. = 漠不关心的人无视他人的痛苦。
He is an indifferentist towards political issues. = 他对政治问题漠不关心。


一般过去时:
She was an indifferentist during the crisis. = 危机期间她漠不关心。

现在完成时:
He has remained an indifferentist throughout his life. = 他一生都漠不关心。
She has shown indifference to many important matters. = 她对许多重要的事情都表现出冷漠。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
