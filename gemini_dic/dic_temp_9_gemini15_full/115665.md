marg:/mɑːrdʒ/| n.|页边空白；利润；盈利|The term "marg" is a shortened form of "margin," referring to the blank space around the text on a page or the profit made on a sale.|例句：The book had wide margins. 这本书有宽阔的页边空白。|例句：The company's profit margin increased this year.  今年公司的利润率增加了。|近义词：border, edge, profit, gain|反义词：loss, deficit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The book has wide margins. = 这本书有宽阔的页边空白。
The company maintains a healthy profit margin. = 公司保持着健康的利润率。


一般过去时:
The printer added extra margins to the document. = 打印机在文档中添加了额外的页边空白。
The business reported a lower margin last quarter. = 该企业上季度报告利润率下降。


现在完成时:
They have increased their profit margins significantly. = 他们已经显著提高了利润率。
We have reduced the margins on the printed page. = 我们已经减少了印刷页面的边距。


过去完成时:
They had already adjusted the margins before the printing. = 他们在印刷之前已经调整了页边距。
The company had lost significant margin in the previous year. = 公司在上一年的利润率大幅下降。


将来时:
We will adjust the margins of the poster. = 我们将调整海报的页边距。
The company expects to improve its profit margin next year. = 公司预计明年将提高利润率。


虚拟语气:
If the margins were wider, the text would be easier to read. = 如果页边距更宽，文本就更容易阅读。
If the company had a higher profit margin, it would invest more. = 如果公司有更高的利润率，它就会进行更多投资。


被动语态:
The margins were adjusted by the designer. = 页边距由设计师调整。
The profit margin was significantly impacted by the economic downturn. = 利润率受到经济衰退的严重影响。


疑问句:
What are the margins on this product? = 这个产品的利润率是多少？
What are the page margins set to? = 页边距设置为多少？


否定句:
The document does not have sufficient margins. = 该文档没有足够的页边距。
The company did not achieve its projected profit margin. = 公司没有达到预期的利润率。


PHRASE->
profit margin: 利润率

gross margin: 毛利率
operating margin: 营业利润率
margin call: 保证金追加通知
