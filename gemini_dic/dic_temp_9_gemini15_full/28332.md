caramelise: /ˈkærəməlaɪz/ | vt. |焦糖化；使…变成焦糖色 | To heat sugar until it melts and turns brown, often used with other ingredients like onions or fruit.  |例句：I caramelised the onions until they were golden brown.我把洋葱焦糖化直到它们变成金棕色。|例句：The chef caramelised the apples before adding them to the pie.  厨师在把苹果加入馅饼之前先将其焦糖化。|近义词：brown, candy |反义词：unbrown
