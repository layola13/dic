USAGE->
This word is an adverb,  and doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".  It describes *how* an action is performed.  There are no tenses to conjugate.

例句1: He smiled lopsidedly. 他歪着嘴笑了笑。
例句2: The table was lopsidedly arranged. 桌子摆放得歪歪扭扭的。
