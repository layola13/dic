harmed:/hɑːmd/| v. adj.| 受害的；受损的 | To cause physical injury or damage to someone or something.  |例句：The accident harmed several people. 事故中多人受伤。 The storm harmed the crops. 暴风雨损坏了庄稼。|近义词：injured, damaged, hurt, impaired |反义词：helped, benefited, aided


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pollution harms the environment. = 污染危害环境。
He harms his health by smoking. = 他抽烟危害健康。


一般过去时:
The fire harmed the building severely. = 大火严重损坏了建筑物。
The careless driver harmed the pedestrian. = 粗心的司机撞伤了行人。


现在完成时:
The storm has harmed the coastal areas. = 暴风雨已经损害了沿海地区。
Years of neglect have harmed the old house. = 多年的忽视损坏了这座老房子。


过去完成时:
They had harmed the ecosystem before the regulations were implemented. = 他们在法规实施之前就已经破坏了生态系统。
The company had harmed its reputation before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，公司就已经损害了自己的声誉。


将来时:
The new policy will harm small businesses. = 新政策将损害小型企业。
The drought will harm the crops. = 干旱将损害庄稼。


虚拟语气:
If we continue polluting, we will harm the planet irreparably. = 如果我们继续污染，我们将无法弥补地破坏地球。
I wish I hadn't harmed the bird. = 我真希望我没有伤害那只鸟。


被动语态:
Many people were harmed in the earthquake. = 地震中许多人受伤。
The environment has been harmed by industrial pollution. = 环境受到了工业污染的破坏。


疑问句:
Was anyone harmed in the accident? = 事故中有人受伤吗？
Has the project harmed the local community? = 这个项目是否损害了当地社区？


否定句:
The incident didn't harm anyone. = 这件事没有伤害任何人。
The medicine didn't harm the patient. = 这药没有伤害病人。


PHRASE->
be harmed by = 被…伤害
harm oneself = 自残
cause harm to = 对…造成伤害
do harm to = 对…造成伤害
harmful to = 对…有害
