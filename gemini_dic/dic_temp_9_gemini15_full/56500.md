domesticate:/dəˈmɛstɪkeɪt/ | vt. |驯化；使驯服；使适应家庭生活 |To bring (an animal or plant) under human control and care; to adapt (something) for use or consumption by humans. |例句：Humans domesticated dogs thousands of years ago. 人类几千年前就驯化了狗。|例句：The chef domesticated the wild mushrooms before using them in the dish. 厨师在将野生蘑菇用于菜肴之前对其进行了驯化处理。|近义词：tame, cultivate, train |反义词：wild, untamed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Farmers domesticate animals for various purposes. = 农民出于各种目的驯养动物。
They domesticate plants to improve their yields. = 他们驯化植物以提高产量。


一般过去时:
Ancient civilizations domesticated many plant species. = 古代文明驯化了许多植物物种。
The farmer domesticated a wild horse. = 农民驯服了一匹野马。


现在完成时:
Humans have domesticated a wide range of animals. = 人类已经驯化了各种各样的动物。
Scientists have domesticated several new plant varieties. = 科学家们已经驯化了几种新的植物品种。


过去完成时:
By the time agriculture developed, humans had already domesticated some animals. = 在农业发展之前，人类就已经驯化了一些动物。
They had domesticated the cats before they moved into the house. = 他们搬进房子之前就已经把猫驯服了。


将来时:
Researchers will continue to domesticate new plant species. = 研究人员将继续驯化新的植物物种。
We will domesticate the dog with patience and consistency. = 我们将以耐心和毅力来驯服这只狗。


虚拟语气:
If we domesticated this plant, it could revolutionize agriculture. = 如果我们驯化这种植物，它可能会彻底改变农业。
I wish I could domesticate this wild cat. = 我真希望我能驯服这只野猫。


被动语态:
Many crops have been domesticated over centuries. = 许多农作物经过几个世纪的驯化。
The wild bird was domesticated by a kind family. = 那只野鸟被一个善良的家庭驯服了。


疑问句:

否定句:
Some animals cannot be domesticated. = 有些动物无法驯化。
They did not domesticate the wild boar. = 他们没有驯服野猪。
