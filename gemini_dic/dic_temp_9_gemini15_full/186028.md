rewinding:/ˌriːˈwaɪndɪŋ/| v. |倒带；倒退；回顾|To reverse the process of recording or playing something, especially a tape or film.  It can also refer to reversing a process or going back to a previous state.|例句：I rewound the tape to listen to the song again. 我倒带重新听那首歌。

例句：He's been rewinding his life, trying to figure out where he went wrong. 他一直在回顾自己的人生，试图找出自己错在哪里。


近义词：reversing, backtracking, replaying|反义词：fast-forwarding, advancing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rewinds the cassette tape. = 他倒带磁带。
She rewinds the film to review a scene. = 她倒带电影来回顾一个场景。


一般过去时:
I rewound the video to watch the best part again. = 我倒带录像带以便再次观看最佳部分。
They rewound the clock to correct their mistake. = 他们试图挽回局面以纠正错误。(比喻用法)


现在完成时:
She has rewound the entire conversation in her head. = 她已经在脑中反复回想了整段对话。
He has rewound the film several times. = 他已经倒带了好几次电影。


过去完成时:
After the argument, he had rewound the events in his mind, trying to understand what went wrong. = 争吵过后，他反复回想事情的经过，试图弄明白哪里出了问题。
Before making the decision, she had rewound the entire process to identify potential pitfalls. = 在做决定之前，她已经仔细回顾了整个过程以找出潜在的陷阱。


将来时:
I will rewind the recording before I listen again. = 我将在再次收听之前倒带录音。
We will rewind the project and start over. = 我们将重新启动项目并从头开始。


虚拟语气:
If I had a rewind button for life, I would use it. = 如果我生活中有一个倒带按钮，我会使用它。
I wish I could rewind time and change my past decisions. = 我希望能够倒回时间并改变我过去的决定。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
