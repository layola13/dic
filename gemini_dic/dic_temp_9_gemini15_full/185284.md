retread:/ˌriːˈtred/| v. n. adj.| 翻新轮胎；翻新；重蹈覆辙 | To put a new tread on (a tire) or to reuse something previously used.  |例句：The mechanic retreaded the old tires. = 机械师翻新了旧轮胎。  They retreaded the same old arguments. = 他们重蹈覆辙，又开始了同样的争论。|近义词：retire, refurbish, recycle |反义词：new, unused
