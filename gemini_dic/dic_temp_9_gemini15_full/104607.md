kaolinize:/ˈkeɪəlaɪn/| vt.|高岭土化；使变成高岭土状|To convert (a substance) into kaolin or a kaolin-like substance.  The process often involves the alteration of feldspar minerals into kaolinite. |例句：The prolonged weathering of feldspar-rich rocks can kaolinize them, creating deposits of valuable kaolin clay. 长期风化富含长石的岩石可以使它们高岭土化，从而形成有价值的高岭土矿床。The intense heat and pressure in the metamorphic process partially kaolinized the original granite. 变质过程中强烈的热量和压力部分地使原来的花岗岩变成了高岭土状。|近义词：decompose, alter, weather|反义词：N/A|动词
