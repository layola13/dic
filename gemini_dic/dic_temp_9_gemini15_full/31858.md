cheerfulness:/ˈtʃɪərfʊlnəs/| n. |快乐；愉悦；高兴|The state or quality of being cheerful; gladness; merriment.  It refers to a disposition or feeling of happiness and optimism.|Her cheerfulness was infectious, brightening the room.|近义词：gaiety, mirth, joviality, glee, happiness |反义词：sadness, gloom, depression, dejection
