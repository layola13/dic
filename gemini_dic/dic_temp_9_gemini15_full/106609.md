Kuiper:/ˈkaɪpər/| n.|柯伊伯带|The Kuiper Belt is a region of the Solar System beyond the planets, containing many icy bodies.  柯伊伯带是太阳系外围行星轨道之外的一个区域，包含许多冰体。|近义词：trans-Neptunian region |反义词：inner Solar System
