preachily:/ˈpriːtʃɪli/| adj.|说教地，教训地，冗长地说教地|In a preachy manner;  characterized by excessive moralizing or lecturing.  It often implies a condescending or irritating tone.|例句：He gave a preachy speech about the importance of hard work. 他发表了一个说教式的演讲，谈论努力工作的重要性。

例句：Her constant preachy pronouncements on healthy eating drove everyone crazy. 她不断地对健康饮食说教，让每个人都抓狂。


近义词：didactic, moralistic, sanctimonious, condescending, lecturing


反义词：unassuming, humble, modest, tolerant


词性：形容词
