remarket:/ˌriːˈmɑːrkɪt/| vt.|再次推销；转售；重新上市|To sell (something) again, especially after it has been used or returned.|The company will remarket the product with a new advertising campaign.  这家公司将通过新的广告宣传活动再次推销该产品。|The used car was remarked to a new customer. 这辆二手车被转售给了一位新顾客。|sell again, resell, re-sell|discard, keep|动词


USAGE->

They remarket their products every year. = 他们每年都会重新上市他们的产品。
The company remarkets used equipment. = 这家公司转售二手设备。
