ferriferous:/ˈfɛrɪfərəs/| adj. |含铁的；铁质的|Containing iron, or relating to iron.  The term often refers to rocks and minerals containing iron.  |例句：The ferriferous soil was rich in nutrients. 这片含铁的土壤富含营养物质。
The geologist identified the ferriferous rock formation. 地质学家鉴别出了含铁的岩层。|近义词：iron-bearing, ferruginous|反义词：iron-free

WORD ORIGIN->
The word "ferriferous" is derived from the Latin word "ferrum" meaning "iron" and the suffix "-ferous,"  meaning "bearing" or "producing."  Therefore, "ferriferous" literally means "iron-bearing."
