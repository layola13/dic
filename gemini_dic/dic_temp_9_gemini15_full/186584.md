ridiculer:/rɪˈdɪkjʊlə(r)/| n. |嘲笑者，讥讽者 | A ridiculer is a person who ridicules or mocks others.  They often make fun of someone's appearance, beliefs, or actions.  |例句：He became a target for the ridiculers of the town. 他成了镇上嘲笑者的目标。|近义词：mocker, scoffer, jeerer |反义词：supporter, admirer
