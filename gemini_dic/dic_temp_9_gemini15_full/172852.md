quadrupled:/kwɑːˈdruːpəld/| v.|使四倍；增至四倍|To multiply or be multiplied by four.  It indicates an increase to four times the original amount or size.|The company's profits quadrupled last year.  去年公司利润增长了四倍。|The population of the city has quadrupled in the last fifty years.  该城市人口在过去五十年里增长了四倍。|increased, multiplied|decreased, halved
