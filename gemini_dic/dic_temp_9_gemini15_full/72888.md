例句：The doctor scheduled follow-ups to monitor the patient's recovery.医生安排了后续检查以监测病人的恢复情况。


近义词：further investigations, follow-on actions, subsequent steps, monitoring


反义词：initial actions, primary steps, preceding actions


词性: 名词
