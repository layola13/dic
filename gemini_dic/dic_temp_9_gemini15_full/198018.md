shocklike:/ˈʃɑːklaɪk/ | adj. | 类似休克的，令人震惊的 | Resembling a shock; sudden and intense.  It can refer to a physical sensation similar to an electric shock or a sudden, intense emotional experience.  | Example:  The news delivered a shocklike feeling of disbelief.  这个消息带来了令人难以置信的震惊感。The sudden, shocklike pain made him jump.  突如其来的、类似电击的疼痛使他跳了起来。| 近义词：stunning, jarring, startling, electrifying | 反义词：gentle, gradual, soothing
