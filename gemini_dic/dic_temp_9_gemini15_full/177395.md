reawake:/ˌriːəˈweɪk/| vt.|重新唤醒；重新使活跃|To wake someone or something up again; to bring something back to life or activity.|例句：The thunder reawakened the sleeping child. 雷声重新唤醒了熟睡的孩子。|例句：The news reawakened her interest in politics. 这条新闻重新燃起了她对政治的兴趣。|近义词：revive, rouse, awaken|反义词：lull, soothe, quiet
