She is mentioned in the Book of Joshua in the Bible and is considered a figure of faith by some.  The name is also used metaphorically to represent someone or something wicked or immoral.|The story of Rahab is a testament to God's mercy and grace. |近义词: harlot, prostitute, sinner |反义词: saint, righteous person
