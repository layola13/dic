risen: /ˈrɪzn/ | v. | 已经升起；上涨；升高 | The past participle of "rise". It indicates a completed action of rising.  It can refer to physical elevation,  a rise in levels (like temperature or water), or an improvement in status or condition. | 例句：The sun has risen.  太阳已经升起。

The price of oil has risen sharply. 油价大幅上涨。


近义词：ascended, elevated, climbed, increased, improved | 反义词：fallen, descended, dropped, decreased, worsened | 词性：动词(过去分词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This tense doesn't use "risen".  The present tense of "rise" is "rises" or "rise".
The sun rises in the east. 太阳从东方升起。
Prices rise during inflation.通货膨胀期间物价上涨。


一般过去时:
This tense doesn't use "risen". The simple past tense is "rose".
The sun rose at 6am. 太阳在早上6点升起。
The water level rose after the rain.雨后水位上涨。


现在完成时:
The sun has risen. 太阳已经升起。
The temperature has risen significantly.气温显著上升。


过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, the sun had risen. 我们到达时，太阳已经升起。
The river had risen to dangerous levels before the dam broke. 大坝决堤前，河水已上涨到危险的水平。


将来时:
The sun will rise tomorrow. 太阳明天会升起。
The water level is expected to rise. 预计水位将会上涨。


虚拟语气:
If the sun had risen earlier, we would have left sooner. 如果太阳早点升起，我们就会早点离开。
I wish the water level hadn't risen so quickly. 我希望水位没有上升得这么快。


被动语态:
"Risen" is already a passive participle, it doesn't have a passive form.


疑问句:
Has the sun risen yet? 太阳升起来了吗？
Has the temperature risen above freezing? 气温升到冰点以上了吗？


否定句:
The sun hasn't risen yet. 太阳还没升起。
The price of gas hasn't risen this month. 本月汽油价格没有上涨。
