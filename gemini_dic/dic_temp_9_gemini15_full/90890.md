hunt-down:/hʌntˈdaʊn/| v.|追捕；追寻；搜寻|To search for and find someone or something, especially in order to catch or kill them.  This phrase emphasizes determination and persistence in the search.|例句：The police hunted down the escaped prisoner.  警方追捕了逃犯。|例句：She spent months hunting down rare books for her collection. 她花了几个月时间搜寻珍本书籍以充实她的收藏。|近义词：track down, pursue, chase, apprehend|反义词：release, free, let go


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police hunt down criminals every day. = 警察每天都在追捕罪犯。
They hunt down information for their reports. = 他们为他们的报告搜集信息。

一般过去时:
The detectives hunted down the killer. = 侦探们追捕到了凶手。
We hunted down the missing documents. = 我们找到了丢失的文件。

现在完成时:
The authorities have hunted down most of the suspects. = 当局已经追捕到了大部分嫌疑人。
She has hunted down all the evidence she needs. = 她已经找到了所有她需要的证据。

过去完成时:
They had hunted down the thieves before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们已经抓住了小偷。
The journalist had hunted down the story for years. = 这名记者多年来一直在追寻这个故事。

将来时:
The team will hunt down the source of the leak. = 该团队将追查泄密的来源。
We will hunt down the best deals for our holiday. = 我们将寻找最优惠的假期交易。

虚拟语气:
If they had more resources, they could hunt down the perpetrators more easily. = 如果他们有更多资源，他们就能更容易地追捕罪犯。
I wish I could hunt down the answer to this riddle. = 我希望我能找到这个谜语的答案。

被动语态:
The fugitive was hunted down after a week on the run. = 逃犯在逃亡一周后被捕。
The truth will eventually be hunted down. = 真相最终会被揭露。

疑问句:
Will they ever hunt down the missing child? = 他们会找到失踪的孩子吗？
How long will it take to hunt down this information? = 找到这个信息需要多长时间？

否定句:
They didn't hunt down the culprit. = 他们没有抓住罪犯。
The search party failed to hunt down the lost hikers. = 搜索队未能找到迷路的徒步旅行者。
