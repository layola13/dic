maidenhairs:/ˈmeɪdənheərz/| n.| maidenhair fern(s);少女的头发；铁线蕨|The word "maidenhairs" refers to a type of fern, specifically the genus *Adiantum*, characterized by their delicate, dark, wiry stems and light, fan-shaped fronds.
