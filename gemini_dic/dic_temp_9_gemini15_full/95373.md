incestuous: /ɪnˈsɛstʃuəs/ | adj. |乱伦的，近亲结婚的 | Relating to or characteristic of incest.  The adjective describes something related to or having the characteristics of incest, a sexual relationship between people who are too closely related to marry legally.  It can refer to actions, relationships, or even a general atmosphere or feeling. | 例句：The novel explores the incestuous relationship between the brother and sister. 这部小说探讨了兄妹之间的乱伦关系。


The accusations of incestuous behavior shocked the family.  乱伦行为的指控震惊了整个家庭。| 近义词：incestual, consanguineous (related by blood) | 反义词： unrelated, legitimate (in the context of marriage)
