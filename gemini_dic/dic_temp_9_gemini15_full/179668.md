reenjoying:/ˌriːɪnˈdʒɔɪɪŋ/| v.|再次享受，重新享受|To experience the pleasure of something again.  It implies a previous enjoyment that is being repeated or revisited. |例句：After a long break, I'm reenjoying my hobby of painting.  再次休息后，我重新享受起了我的绘画爱好。

例句：She is reenjoying the peace and quiet of the countryside after the city's hustle and bustle.  在城市喧嚣之后，她再次享受着乡村的宁静。


近义词：rediscovering, reliving, savoring, relishing

反义词：disliking, loathing, despising


词性：动词

I am reenjoying my vacation. = 我正在再次享受我的假期。
They are reenjoying their childhood memories. = 他们正在重新回味童年的回忆。

一般过去时:

过去完成时:

We had reenjoyed the scenery before the weather changed. = 天气变化之前，我们已经再次欣赏过这风景了。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:  (被动语态在此处不常见且略显不自然)  The benefits of the program were reenjoyed by the participants. = 项目的益处被参与者再次享受到了。

词源:  The word "reenjoying" is formed by adding the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "anew") to the present participle "enjoying" of the verb "enjoy."  The root "enjoy" comes from Old French *enjoir, from en- + joir "to enjoy, rejoice," from Latin gaudere "to rejoice."
