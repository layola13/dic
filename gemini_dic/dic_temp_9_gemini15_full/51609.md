devata:/dɛˈvɑːtə/| n. |神，女神；神格|A devata is a deity or divine being in Hinduism and other related religions.  They are often associated with specific natural forces, aspects of the cosmos, or human qualities.  The term encompasses a wide range of divine beings, from major gods and goddesses to lesser spirits and personifications.|Example:  The ancient scriptures describe many powerful devatas residing in the heavens.  古代经文描述了许多居住在天上的强大神灵。|近义词：deity, god, goddess, divinity|反义词：mortal, human, profane|名词
