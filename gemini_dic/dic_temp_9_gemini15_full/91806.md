hyperfemininity: /ˌhaɪpərˌfemɪˈnɪnɪti/ | n. | 超女性化；极度女性化 | The exaggerated or stereotypical manifestation of femininity.  It often involves conforming to societal expectations of women in an extreme way, potentially to the detriment of individual expression or autonomy.  This can manifest in dress, behavior, and attitudes. |  Her hyperfemininity was evident in her elaborate wardrobe and demure demeanor.  她的超女性化体现在她精致的衣着和温顺的态度上。 |  extreme femininity, exaggerated femininity |  masculinity, androgyny | 名词
