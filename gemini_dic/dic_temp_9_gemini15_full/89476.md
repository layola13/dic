honeyed:/ˈhʌnid/| adj.|甜言蜜语的；甜的；谄媚的 |Having the sweetness of honey; excessively sweet or flattering.  The word suggests an artificial or insincere sweetness, often used to deceive or persuade. |例句：Her honeyed words couldn't hide her true intentions.  她的甜言蜜语掩盖不了她的真实意图。
Her honeyed smile was unconvincing. 她的甜美的笑容令人难以置信。


近义词：sugary, sweet, candied, cloying, saccharine, flattering, sycophantic

反义词：bitter, harsh, blunt, sincere, unsweetened


词性：形容词
