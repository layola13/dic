/draɪz/ | n. | 干燥；干旱 | Dryness refers to the absence of moisture or liquid. It can describe a state, condition, or process.  | The desert is characterized by its dry climate.  沙漠以其干燥的气候为特征。| humid, arid | wet, moist | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weather is dry today. = 今天天气干燥。
This bread is dry. = 这面包干了。


一般过去时:
The well dried up last summer. = 井去年夏天干涸了。
The paint dried quickly. = 油漆很快就干了。


现在完成时:
The river has dried up. = 河水干涸了。
The clothes have dried in the sun. = 衣服在阳光下晒干了。


过去完成时:
The land had dried out before the planting season. = 在种植季节开始之前，土地已经干涸了。
The paint had dried before we could clean the brushes. = 油漆干了之后我们才能清洗刷子。


将来时:
The laundry will dry quickly in this hot weather. = 在这种炎热的天气下，衣服会很快干。
The river will likely dry up if it doesn't rain soon. = 如果不久不下雨，河水很可能会干涸。


虚拟语气:
If it were dry, we could go for a walk. = 如果天气干燥，我们就可以去散步。
I wish the paint had dried faster. = 我希望油漆干得更快一些。


被动语态:
The clothes were dried in the dryer. = 衣服是用烘干机烘干的。
The land is being dried by the sun. = 土地正在被太阳晒干。


疑问句:
Is the weather dry today? = 今天天气干燥吗？
Are the clothes dry yet? = 衣服干了吗？


否定句:
The cake isn't dry. = 蛋糕不干。
The well didn't dry up. = 井没有干涸。
