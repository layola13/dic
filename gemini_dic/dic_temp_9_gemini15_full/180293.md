refrigerant:/rɪˈfrɪdʒərənt/ | n. |制冷剂 |A refrigerant is a substance used in a refrigeration cycle that undergoes a phase transition from a gas to a liquid and back again, absorbing heat during the gas-to-liquid transition and releasing heat during the liquid-to-gas transition.  This process is used to cool things down. |Example:  The refrigerator uses a special refrigerant to keep food cold.  制冷机使用特殊的制冷剂来保持食物的冰冷。  The mechanic added more refrigerant to the air conditioning unit. 技工向空调单元添加了更多制冷剂。|近义词：coolant, chilling agent |反义词:heating agent
