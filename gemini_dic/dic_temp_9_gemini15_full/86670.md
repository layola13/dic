hellfire:/ˈhɛlˌfaɪr/| n. |地狱之火；极度痛苦；猛烈的炮火|The intense heat and suffering of hell, often used metaphorically to describe intense suffering or destruction.|例句：The speaker described the war as a hellfire of destruction.  演讲者将战争描述为一场毁灭性的地狱之火。|例句：The criticism was like hellfire, burning away any self-confidence. 批评就像地狱之火，烧掉了所有的自信。|近义词：inferno, damnation, fiery torment|反义词：heaven, paradise, bliss
