patten:/ˈpætən/| n.|木底鞋；鞋跟；图案；样式|A patten is a type of overshoe, typically made of wood, worn to protect shoes from mud or water.  It can also refer to a type of heel or a repeated design or pattern.|例句：She wore pattens to keep her shoes dry in the muddy field. 她穿着木底鞋，以保持鞋子在泥泞的田地里不被弄湿。  The fabric had a repeating floral patten. 这块布料有重复的花卉图案。|近义词：overshoe, clog, galosh, design, model|反义词：irregularity, disorder
