commercialized:/ˌkəˈmɜːrʃəlaɪzd/| adj.|商业化的；商品化的|transformed into something designed for commercial sale or profit.  The process of making something geared towards profit.|The artist felt his work had been commercialized and lost its original meaning.  艺术家觉得他的作品被商业化了，失去了原本的意义。|近义词：marketed,  monetized,  profitized|反义词：uncommercialized, artisanal


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He commercializes his art by selling prints online. 他通过在线销售印刷品来商业化他的艺术。
The company commercializes its new technology by licensing it to other firms. 这家公司通过将其技术许可给其他公司来将其商业化。


一般过去时:
They commercialized the song and made a lot of money. 他们将这首歌商业化并赚了很多钱。
The festival was once a local event but it was commercialized over time.  这个节日曾经是一个地方性活动，但随着时间的推移，它被商业化了。


现在完成时:
Many traditional crafts have been commercialized in recent years. 近年来，许多传统工艺都已商业化。
The internet has commercialized many aspects of our lives.  互联网已经将我们生活的许多方面商业化了。


过去完成时:
By the time the book was published, the story had been commercialized for film. 到这本书出版的时候，这个故事已经被商业化拍成电影了。
Before the invention of the internet, many businesses had not yet commercialized their operations online. 在互联网发明之前，许多企业还没有将其业务在线商业化。


将来时:
They will commercialize their new product next year. 他们将在明年将其新产品商业化。
The government plans to commercialize the space program. 政府计划将太空计划商业化。


虚拟语气:
If they had commercialized the product earlier, they would have made more money. 如果他们早点将产品商业化，他们就会赚到更多钱。
I wish I hadn't commercialized my art; I miss the creative freedom. 我真希望我没有将我的艺术商业化；我怀念创作的自由。


被动语态:
The idea was commercialized by a large corporation. 这个想法被一家大公司商业化了。
The technology has been commercialized for use in various industries. 这项技术已被商业化，用于各个行业。


疑问句:
Has the technology been fully commercialized yet? 这项技术已经完全商业化了吗？
Will this new invention be commercialized soon?  这项新发明很快就会商业化吗？


否定句:
The artist refused to commercialize his work.  这位艺术家拒绝将他的作品商业化。
The company decided not to commercialize the software.  该公司决定不将该软件商业化。
