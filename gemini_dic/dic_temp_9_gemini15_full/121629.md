misparaphrasing: /ˌmɪsˈpærəfreɪzɪŋ/ | n. | 误释；曲解；歪曲解释 | The act of paraphrasing something incorrectly or misleadingly, often intentionally. It involves changing the meaning of the original text or statement.  | 例句：His misparaphrasing of the contract led to a significant misunderstanding.  他的合同误释导致了严重的误解。  | 近义词：misinterpretation, misrepresentation, distortion | 反义词：accurate paraphrasing, faithful representation |  词性: 名词
