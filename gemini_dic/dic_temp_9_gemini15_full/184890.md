retainable: /rɪˈteɪnəbl/ | adj. | 可保留的，可保持的 | Capable of being retained; able to be kept or preserved.  | The information is retainable for up to 24 hours.  这些信息最多可保留24小时。|  maintainable, sustainable |  unretainable, dispensable | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The contract is retainable. = 合同是可保留的。
This knowledge is retainable. = 这些知识是可以保留的。

一般过去时:
The data was retainable for only a short time. = 数据只能短期保留。
The color was retainable even after washing. = 即使洗涤后颜色也能保留。

现在完成时:
The company has retained all the important documents. = 公司已保留所有重要文件。(被动语态的主动表达)
The evidence has proved retainable. = 证据已被证明是可以保留的。(被动语态的主动表达)


过去完成时:
They had retained the right to appeal. = 他们保留了上诉权。（被动语态的主动表达）
The information had been retainable until the system crashed. = 信息在系统崩溃之前是可以保留的。

将来时:
The information will be retainable for at least a year. = 信息至少可以保留一年。
The company will retain all the necessary documents. = 公司将保留所有必要的文档。(被动语态的主动表达)

虚拟语气:
If the information were retainable, we would have a better record. = 如果信息能够保留，我们将有更好的记录。
I wish this feeling were retainable forever. = 我希望这种感觉能永远保留下去。

被动语态:
The documents are retained by the company. = 文件由公司保留。
The heat is retainable in this container. = 热量可以储存在这个容器中。

疑问句:
Is this information retainable? = 这些信息可以保留吗？
Are these memories retainable? = 这些记忆可以保留吗？

否定句:
The color is not retainable after repeated washing. = 反复清洗后颜色无法保留。
The evidence is not retainable. = 证据无法保留。


PHRASE->
highly retainable:  高度可保留的
easily retainable: 易于保留的
long-term retainable: 长期可保留的
difficult to retainable:  难以保留的
cost-effective retainable:  经济高效的可保留的


词源:
retain (动词) + -able (形容词后缀)  retain 源自古法语retenir，最终源于拉丁语retinēre，意为“to hold back, keep”。  -able 表示“能够…的”。
