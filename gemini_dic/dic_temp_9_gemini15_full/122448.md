mobile:/ˈmoʊbaɪl/ /ˈməʊbaɪl/| adj., n. |移动的；手机|

英文解析:As an adjective, "mobile" means able to move or be moved freely and easily. As a noun, it commonly refers to a mobile phone or cellphone.


例句:
The mobile sculpture swayed gently in the breeze.  移动的雕塑在微风中轻轻摇曳。

He answered the call on his mobile phone. 他用手机接听了电话。


近义词: (adj.) movable, portable, transferable, nomadic; (n.) cellphone, cell phone, smartphone


反义词: (adj.) stationary, immobile, fixed, static


词性: adjective, noun
