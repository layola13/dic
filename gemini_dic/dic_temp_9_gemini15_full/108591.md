lead-down-the-garden-path:/ˌliːd daʊn ðə ˈɡɑːrdən pɑːθ/|/ phrasal verb | 欺骗；误导；引诱上当 | To deceive or mislead someone, often by giving them false hopes or promises.  | Example: He led me down the garden path with his promises of riches. 他用他关于财富的承诺欺骗了我。|近义词：deceive, mislead, trick, hoodwink |反义词：guide honestly, inform truthfully |词性：phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They lead many investors down the garden path with their false claims. = 他们用虚假的声明欺骗了许多投资者。
He leads her down the garden path with sweet words. = 他用甜言蜜语欺骗她。


一般过去时:
The salesman led her down the garden path with his high-pressure sales tactics. = 销售员用高压推销手段欺骗了她。
They led the public down the garden path about the true costs of the project. = 他们误导了公众关于项目真实成本的情况。


现在完成时:
The company has led its customers down the garden path with its misleading advertising. = 公司用误导性广告欺骗了它的顾客。
He has led me down the garden path many times before. = 他以前多次欺骗过我。


过去完成时:
I realized that I had been led down the garden path by his charming demeanor. = 我意识到我被他迷人的举止欺骗了。
The government had led the nation down the garden path with its economic policies. = 政府用其经济政策误导了国家。


将来时:
They will lead many more people down the garden path with their fraudulent scheme. = 他们将用他们的欺诈计划欺骗更多的人。
She will probably lead him down the garden path again. = 她可能会再次欺骗他。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't led me down the garden path, I wouldn't have lost so much money. = 如果他没有欺骗我，我就不会损失那么多钱。
I wish I hadn't been led down the garden path by his promises. = 我希望我没有被他许下的诺言所欺骗。


被动语态:
Many people were led down the garden path by the deceptive marketing campaign. = 许多人被具有欺骗性的营销活动所欺骗。
Investors were led down the garden path by the company's false assurances. = 投资者被公司的虚假保证所欺骗。


疑问句:
Were they led down the garden path by the politician's promises? = 他们是被政客的承诺所欺骗了吗？
Has she been led down the garden path by his charm? = 她是否被他迷人的魅力所欺骗？


否定句:
He didn't lead me down the garden path; he told me the truth. = 他没有欺骗我；他告诉我真相。
They weren't led down the garden path; they knew exactly what they were doing. = 他们没有被欺骗；他们确切地知道自己在做什么。


PHRASE->
be led down the garden path = 被欺骗

walk someone down the garden path = 慢慢地、循序渐进地欺骗某人
take someone down the garden path = 欺骗某人
have been led down the garden path = 曾经被欺骗
