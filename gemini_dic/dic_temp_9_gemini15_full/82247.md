groundbreaking:/ˈɡraʊndˌbreɪkɪŋ/ | adj. |开创性的，突破性的 |  Innovative and important; creating new possibilities.  It describes something that is new and significant, often leading to major advancements or changes in a field. |例句：Her research on cancer is groundbreaking.  她的癌症研究是开创性的。

例句：The new drug represents a groundbreaking development in the fight against Alzheimer's disease.  这种新药代表着在对抗阿尔茨海默病的斗争中取得了突破性进展。


近义词：innovative, revolutionary, pioneering, epoch-making, transformative | 反义词：conventional, traditional, outdated, stale
