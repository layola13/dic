impierceable: /ˌɪmpiˈɜːrsəbl/ | adj. | 不可穿透的，坚不可摧的 |  Incapable of being pierced or penetrated; impenetrable.  |  The impierceable armor protected the knight from the enemy's attacks.  这坚不可摧的盔甲保护骑士免受敌人的攻击。 The impierceable wall of silence surrounding the incident frustrated investigators.  围绕事件的沉默之墙令调查人员感到沮丧。 |  impenetrable, invincible, unbreakable | pierceable, vulnerable, penetrable | 形容词

词源:
The word "impierceable" is formed from the prefix "im-" (meaning "not") and the word "pierceable" (meaning "capable of being pierced").  "Pierce" itself comes from the Old French word "percier," ultimately from the Latin "perforare" (to bore through).
