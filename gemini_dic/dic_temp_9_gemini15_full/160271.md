ponytailed:/ˈpɔːniˌteɪld/|/ˈpoʊniˌteɪld/| adj.|扎马尾辫的；留着马尾辫的|Having one's hair tied back in a ponytail.  This adjective describes a person's hairstyle.|例句：The ponytailed girl was running across the field.  那个扎着马尾辫的女孩跑过田野。

例句：I saw a ponytailed woman jogging in the park this morning. 今天早上我在公园里看到一个留着马尾辫的女人慢跑。


近义词：pigtailed (if braids are involved),  hair-tied (more general)


反义词：loose-haired (hair not tied up)


词性:形容词
