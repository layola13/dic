oversupplies:/ˌoʊvər səˈplaɪz/| n.|供应过剩，供过于求|An excess of goods or services available in a market.|例句：The current oversupplies of oil have driven down prices.  目前的石油供应过剩导致价格下跌。

例句：The market is suffering from oversupplies of agricultural products. 市场正遭受农产品供应过剩的困扰。


近义词：surplus, glut, excess, abundance|反义词：shortage, scarcity, deficiency|词性:名词
