darers:/ˈdɛərərz/| n.| 敢于尝试者，挑战者 |Those who dare to take risks or face challenges. People who are brave enough to attempt difficult or dangerous things.|例句：The mountain climbers were true darers, facing treacherous conditions.  那些登山者是真正的挑战者，面对着危险的环境。

例句：The new business venture attracted many darers seeking fortune. 这项新的商业冒险吸引了许多寻求财富的挑战者。


近义词：adventurers, risk-takers, daredevils, challengers

反义词：cowards, faint-hearted, timid


词源:  Derived from the verb "dare," meaning to have the courage to do something difficult or dangerous. The suffix "-er" indicates a person who performs the action.  The word's origins trace back to Old English *darian, meaning "to dare, to challenge."
