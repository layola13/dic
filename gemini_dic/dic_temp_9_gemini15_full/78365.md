gesticulate:/dʒɛˈstɪkjuleɪt/ | v. | 做手势，打手势 | To make gestures, especially when speaking, to emphasize one's words or express emotion.  | He gesticulated wildly as he told the story. 他讲故事时激动地比划着。  |  signal, mime, motion |  (无明确反义词，可考虑：remain still, be motionless) | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gesticulates wildly when he's excited. = 他兴奋的时候会激动地打手势。
She often gesticulates to emphasize her points. = 她经常用手势来强调她的观点。


一般过去时:
The speaker gesticulated forcefully during his presentation. = 演讲者在演讲期间有力地做着各种手势。
He gesticulated angrily at the driver who cut him off. = 他愤怒地朝插队司机比划。


现在完成时:
He has gesticulated so much that his arm is tired. = 他做了这么多手势，胳膊都累了。
She has gesticulated throughout the entire meeting. = 她在整个会议期间都在打手势。


过去完成时:
He had gesticulated for several minutes before anyone understood him. = 他比划了好几分钟，别人才明白他的意思。
She had gesticulated impatiently before the train finally arrived. = 火车终于到达之前，她不耐烦地比划了好一阵子。


将来时:
He will gesticulate enthusiastically during the debate. = 他将在辩论中热情地打手势。
She will gesticulate to help explain the complicated concept. = 她会用手势来帮助解释这个复杂的概念。


虚拟语气:
If he were more articulate, he wouldn't have to gesticulate so much. = 如果他表达能力更强，他就不用做那么多手势了。
I wish he wouldn't gesticulate so much; it's distracting. = 我希望他不要做那么多手势，这很分散注意力。


被动语态:
His frustration was gesticulated clearly to everyone in the room. = 他的沮丧情绪通过手势清楚地表达给了房间里的每个人。
The message was gesticulated effectively by the mime artist. = 哑剧演员有效地用手势表达了信息。(较为不自然，很少用被动语态)


疑问句:
Does he often gesticulate when he speaks? = 他说话时经常做手势吗？
Why did she gesticulate so dramatically? = 她为什么做那么夸张的手势？


否定句:
He didn't gesticulate at all during the meeting. = 他在会议期间根本没有做任何手势。
She didn't gesticulate; she simply spoke calmly and clearly. = 她没有做手势；她只是平静而清晰地说话。


PHRASE->
wildly gesticulate = 疯狂地打手势
forcefully gesticulate = 有力地打手势
animatedly gesticulate = 生动地打手势
meaningfully gesticulate = 有意义地打手势
frantically gesticulate = 疯狂地打手势


词源:
From Late Latin *gesticulāri, from Latin gestus ("gesture, action, attitude") + -āri (frequentative suffix).
