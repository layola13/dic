chapelry:/ˈtʃæpəlri/| n. |教区（尤指不设教堂的教区）, 牧师区 |A chapelry is a district served by a chapel, especially one that is not a parish church.  It often refers to a smaller religious community within a larger parish. |The village was a chapelry attached to the larger parish of St. Mary's.  这个村庄是一个附属于圣玛丽大教区的教区。|近义词：parish (有时), district |反义词：diocese (更大的行政区域)
