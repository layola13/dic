engarrison:/ɪnˈɡærɪsən/ | vt. |驻军于；配备守军 |To place troops in (a place) for protection or defense; to provide (a place) with a garrison. |例句：The city was engarrisoned by a large army.  这座城市驻扎着一支庞大的军队。|例句：The fort was engarrisoned with experienced soldiers.  要塞配备了经验丰富的士兵。|近义词： garrison, fortify, man |反义词：evacuate, abandon
