multidrug: /ˈmʌltidrʌɡ/ | adj. | 多药的，多种药物的 |  Relating to or involving the use of multiple drugs.  | 例句：Multidrug-resistant tuberculosis is a serious global health problem.  耐多药结核病是一个严重的全球性健康问题。|近义词：polypharmaceutical |反义词：monodrug
