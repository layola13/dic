kabob:/kəˈbɑːb/| n.|烤肉串；肉串|A small piece of meat (or sometimes vegetables) cooked on a skewer.  The term often refers to Middle Eastern or Mediterranean cuisine, but it's used more broadly now. |例句：We had delicious lamb kabobs for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了美味的羊肉串。

例句：The chef prepared a variety of vegetable kabobs.  厨师准备了各种蔬菜串。


近义词：skewer, shish kebab


反义词：无


词性:名词

词源:
The word "kabob" comes from the Persian word "کباب" (kabaab), which means "roasted meat."  It entered English through various European languages, reflecting the widespread popularity of this style of cooking across the Middle East and beyond.
