messieurs:/ˈmɛsɪəz/| n. |先生们；各位先生|The French word "messieurs" is the plural form of "monsieur," meaning "mister" or "sir" in English. It is a formal and polite way to address a group of men.  It is rarely used in modern English, except in very formal settings or to maintain a sense of old-world formality. |例句：Messieurs, we are ready to begin the meeting. 先生们，我们准备开始会议了。|近义词：gentlemen, sirs|反义词：ladies (as a contrasting term)
