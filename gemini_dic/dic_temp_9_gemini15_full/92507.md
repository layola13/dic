hypothesized:/haɪˈpɒθəsaɪzd/ | /haɪˈpɑːθəsaɪzd/ | v. | 假设；提出假设 | To form a hypothesis; to assume something as a basis for reasoning.  | The scientist hypothesized that the new drug would cure the disease.  科学家假设这种新药能治愈这种疾病。 | theorized, postulated, conjectured | proved, confirmed, verified | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hypothesizes that the Earth is flat. = 他假设地球是平的。
She hypothesizes a connection between stress and illness. = 她假设压力与疾病之间存在联系。


一般过去时:
They hypothesized a link between diet and cancer. = 他们假设饮食与癌症之间存在联系。
The researchers hypothesized that climate change would affect migration patterns. = 研究人员假设气候变化会影响迁徙模式。


现在完成时:
Scientists have hypothesized that dark matter exists. = 科学家们已经假设暗物质存在。
Researchers have hypothesized about the origins of the universe for centuries. = 研究人员几个世纪以来一直在假设宇宙的起源。


过去完成时:
They had hypothesized that the results would be different. = 他们曾假设结果会有所不同。
Before the experiment, she had hypothesized a correlation between the two variables. = 在实验之前，她已经假设这两个变量之间存在相关性。


将来时:
They will hypothesize about the effects of the new policy. = 他们将假设新政策的影响。
She will hypothesize that the economic downturn is temporary. = 她将假设经济衰退是暂时的。


虚拟语气:
If I hypothesized correctly, the experiment would succeed. = 如果我的假设正确，实验就会成功。
I wish I had hypothesized differently. = 我希望我当初的假设有所不同。


被动语态:
It was hypothesized that the findings were statistically significant. = 人们假设这些发现具有统计学意义。
A connection between sleep and memory was hypothesized by the researchers. = 研究人员假设睡眠和记忆之间存在联系。


疑问句:
What did the researchers hypothesize about the cause of the problem? = 研究人员对这个问题的原因作出了什么假设？
What have you hypothesized in your latest study? = 你在最新的研究中提出了什么假设？


否定句:
They did not hypothesize any connection between the two events. = 他们没有假设这两个事件之间有任何联系。
The study did not hypothesize a causal relationship. = 这项研究没有假设因果关系。


PHRASE->
It is hypothesized that... = 人们假设……

The study hypothesized that... = 这项研究假设……
The results support the hypothesis that... = 结果支持……的假设
Based on the hypothesis that... = 基于……的假设
