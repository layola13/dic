overdistort:/ˌoʊvərˈdɪstɔːrt/ | v. |过度扭曲；歪曲；夸张 |To distort or exaggerate excessively.  It implies a significant departure from the truth or original form, often with the intention of misleading or misrepresenting. |例句：The media overdistorted the politician's statement, making him seem far more radical than he actually was.媒体过度歪曲了这位政治家的声明，使他看起来比实际情况激进得多。

The artist overdistorted the features of the portrait, creating a grotesque effect.艺术家过度扭曲了肖像的特点，创造了一种怪诞的效果。


近义词：exaggerate, misrepresent, warp, contort | 反义词：accurately represent, truthfully portray
