dadaist:/ˈdædəɪst/| adj. n. |达达主义的；达达主义者 |Relating to or characteristic of Dadaism, an artistic and literary movement of the early 20th century that rejected reason and logic and celebrated irrationality and absurdity.  |例句：The Dadaist art movement challenged traditional notions of art and beauty.|近义词：avant-garde, surrealist |反义词：classicist, traditionalist
