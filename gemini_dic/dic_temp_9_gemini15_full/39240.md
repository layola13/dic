congratulational:/kənˌɡrædʒuˈleɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 祝贺的，表示祝贺的 | Expressing congratulations.  The word is rarely used on its own; it typically appears in phrases like "congratulational message" or "congratulational remarks". | Example:  He sent a congratulational message to his friend. 他给朋友发了一条祝贺短信。  |近义词：congratulatory | 反义词：None (no direct antonym, but words expressing the opposite sentiment such as "consolatory" might be considered) |  形容词
