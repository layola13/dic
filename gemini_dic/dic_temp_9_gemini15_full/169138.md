projectress:/prɒdʒɛkˈtrɛs/| n. |女项目经理，女负责人| A woman who manages or directs a project.  The word is a relatively rare and somewhat archaic formation, combining "project" with the feminine suffix "-ress". It's not widely used in modern English. |Example:  She was appointed projectress for the new building development. 她被任命为新建筑开发项目的负责人。|近义词：project manager, director, supervisor, leader |反义词：subordinate, follower |名词
