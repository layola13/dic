domesticator:/dəˈmɛstɪkeɪtər/ | n. |驯养者；驯化者 | A person or thing that domesticates animals or plants.  Someone who tames wild animals or cultivates wild plants for human use. | Example: The early domesticators of dogs likely selected for traits like docility and trainability. 早期驯养狗的人可能选择了温顺和易训练等特征。  |近义词: tamer, breeder, cultivator |反义词:  None specifically, but  "wild animal keeper" could be considered a contrasting role.
