crutching:/ˈkrʌtʃɪŋ/| v.|拄拐杖；依赖；支撑|The act of using crutches to walk or support oneself;  to rely heavily on something or someone for support.|例句：He was crutching around after his leg injury. 他腿受伤后拄着拐杖走动。|例句：She's crutching on her parents for financial support. 她依靠父母提供经济支持。|近义词：limping, leaning on, relying on|反义词：walking unaided, self-reliant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He crutches around the house after his surgery. = 他手术后在家拄着拐杖走动。
They crutch on their old habits. = 他们依赖着他们的老习惯。

一般过去时:
She crutched to the bathroom. = 她拄着拐杖去浴室。
He crutched heavily on his brother's advice. = 他严重依赖他哥哥的建议。

现在完成时:
He has been crutching since his accident. = 自从他出事以来，他一直拄着拐杖。
She has crutched on her family for emotional support. = 她一直依靠家人给予情感支持。

过去完成时:
He had been crutching for months before the surgery. = 在手术前，他已经拄拐杖好几个月了。
They had crutched on their outdated technology for years. = 他们已经依赖过时的技术多年了。

将来时:
He will crutch to the store tomorrow. = 他明天将拄着拐杖去商店。
She will crutch on her friends for help with the project. = 她将在项目中依靠朋友的帮助。


虚拟语气:
