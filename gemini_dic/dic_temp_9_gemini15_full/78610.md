gibbon:/ˈɡɪbən/| n. |长臂猿|A gibbon is a small ape with long arms and no tail, found in the forests of Southeast Asia.  Gibbons are known for their acrobatic movements through the trees. |例句：The gibbon swung effortlessly from branch to branch. 长臂猿轻而易举地从树枝间荡过。  The researchers observed the behavior of a group of gibbons in the wild. 研究人员观察了一群野生长臂猿的行为。|近义词：ape (in the context of a small, tree-dwelling ape)|反义词：None specifically.
