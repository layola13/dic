de-americanizing:/ˌdiːæmɛrɪkənˈaɪzɪŋ/| v.|去美国化；去除美国特色 |The process of removing American influences or characteristics from something.  This can refer to culture, policy, or other aspects. |例句：The country is actively pursuing a policy of de-americanizing its economy.  这个国家正在积极推行去美国化经济政策。


例句：The film's director consciously de-americanized the story to make it more universally appealing. 电影导演有意识地去掉了故事中的美国特色，使其更具普遍吸引力。


近义词：denationalizing, deculturalizing


反义词：americanizing


词性:动词
