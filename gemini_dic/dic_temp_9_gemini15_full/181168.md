reingulf:/ˌriːɪnˈɡʌlf/| vt.|重新吞没；再次吞噬|To swallow or engulf again; to overwhelm or submerge once more.|例句：The rising tide began to reingulf the sandcastle.  涨潮开始再次吞没沙堡。
The memories of the past reingulfed him. 过去的记忆再次将他淹没。|近义词：engulf, overwhelm, submerge, inundate|反义词：rescue, save, liberate
