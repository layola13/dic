curialist:/ˈkjʊəriəlɪst/ | n. | 枢密院成员；枢密顾问 | A member of a curia, especially a member of the papal curia.  A curialist is someone who is part of the administrative system of the Roman Catholic Church, specifically the Curia Romana. They typically hold high-ranking positions and advise the Pope on various matters.  | Example:  The curialist presented his recommendations to the Pope.  枢密院成员向教宗呈递了他的建议。 | 近义词: papal official, Vatican official | 反义词: layman, outsider
