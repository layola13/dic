homer:/ˈhoʊmər/| n. |荷马；本垒打；(尤指)猛击本垒打| A homer is a home run in baseball. It is also a reference to Homer, the ancient Greek poet.  |例句：He hit a grand slam homer. 他击出了一个满贯本垒打。|近义词：home run, grand slam (for baseball)|反义词：strikeout (for baseball)
