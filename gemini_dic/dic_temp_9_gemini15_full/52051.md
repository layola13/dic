diallers:/ˈdaɪəlz/ | n. |拨号器，自动拨号器 |A dialler is a device or software that automatically dials telephone numbers.  It can be a physical device attached to a phone or a software program on a computer. |例句：Many telemarketing companies use sophisticated diallers to contact potential customers. 许多电话营销公司使用复杂的拨号器来联系潜在客户。


例句：The built-in dialler on my phone makes it easy to call contacts from my address book.  我手机上内置的拨号器使我很容易从通讯录中拨打联系人电话。


近义词：autodialer, caller ID, modem (in some contexts) |反义词：manual dialing device (a less specific antonym) |词性:名词
