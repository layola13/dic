fodderer:/ˈfɒdə(r)ər/| n. |饲养员；提供饲料者|One who provides fodder, especially for livestock.  A fodderer supplies food for animals. |例句：The farmer acted as a fodderer for his herd of cattle.  这个农民为他的牛群提供饲料。|例句：The fodderer ensured the animals had enough to eat throughout the winter.  饲养员确保动物们整个冬天都有足够的食物。|近义词：feeder, provider|反义词：N/A
