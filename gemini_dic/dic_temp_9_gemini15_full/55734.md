dive-bombing:/ˈdaɪvˌbɒmbɪŋ/ | n. |俯冲轰炸；突然袭击 |The act of an aircraft descending steeply to attack a target.  It can also refer to a sudden, unexpected attack or criticism. |例句：The enemy planes carried out dive-bombing raids on the city.  敌机对城市进行了俯冲轰炸。

例句：His sudden, sharp criticism felt like a dive-bombing attack on my self-esteem. 他突然尖锐的批评感觉像是在猛烈攻击我的自尊心。

近义词：strafing, attack, assault, blitz

反义词：support, defense, protection
