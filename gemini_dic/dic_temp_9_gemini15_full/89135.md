Homebuyers search for properties online. = 购房者在网上搜索房产。
Homebuyers often compare prices from different agents. = 购房者经常比较不同中介的价格。

一般过去时:
The homebuyers viewed several houses last weekend. = 购房者上周末看了几套房子。
The homebuyers made an offer on a charming bungalow. = 购房者对一栋迷人的平房提出了报价。

现在完成时:
The homebuyers have saved enough for a down payment. = 购房者已经攒够了首付。
The homebuyers have been pre-approved for a mortgage. = 购房者已经获得了抵押贷款的预先批准。

过去完成时:
The homebuyers had already found their dream house before they even started looking. = 购房者甚至在开始寻找之前就已经找到了梦想中的房子。(This example is less common with this specific noun)
Before the homebuyers made an offer, they had their finances checked. = 在购房者出价之前，他们已经检查了自己的财务状况。

将来时:
The homebuyers will close on their new home next month. = 购房者下个月将完成新房的交易。
The homebuyers will be moving in next month. = 购房者下个月将搬进去。

虚拟语气:
If the homebuyers had more money, they would buy a bigger house. = 如果购房者有更多的钱，他们会买一栋更大的房子。
I wish the homebuyers had found a better deal. = 我希望购房者能找到一个更好的交易。

被动语态:
Many houses are being viewed by homebuyers this week. = 本周许多房屋正在被购房者观看。
The mortgage application was approved by the homebuyers' bank. = 购房者的银行批准了抵押贷款申请。

疑问句:
Are these homebuyers looking for a condo or a house? = 这些购房者是正在寻找公寓还是房子？
What type of home are the homebuyers interested in? = 购房者对哪种类型的房子感兴趣？

否定句:
The homebuyers did not find a suitable house in the city. = 购房者在城里没有找到合适的房子。
These homebuyers aren't interested in properties with a pool. = 这些购房者对带游泳池的房产不感兴趣。
