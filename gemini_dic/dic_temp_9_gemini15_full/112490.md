loup:/luːp/| n.|狼|A large canine mammal, typically with gray or brown fur, that lives in packs and hunts prey.|Example: The loup howled at the moon.  那只狼对着月亮嚎叫。|近义词：wolf, canine|反义词：lamb, sheep


USAGE->
This word is a noun and does not conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.  It can be used in different sentence structures, however.

Example 1 (Simple sentence): The loup stalked its prey.  那只狼跟踪它的猎物。

Example 2 (Complex sentence):  Because the loup was hungry, it hunted aggressively. 因为那只狼饿了，所以它凶猛地狩猎。

Example 3 (Sentence with prepositional phrase): The loup in the forest howled. 森林里的狼嚎叫了。
