changeover's: /ˈtʃeɪndʒˌoʊvər/ | n. | 变换；转换；切换；交接 | The process of changing from one thing to another, or the instance of this happening.  It often refers to a complete shift or replacement.  The possessive 's' indicates ownership or relationship.  For example, "the factory's changeover" refers to the changeover belonging to or associated with the factory. | The factory's changeover to the new production line was completed smoothly. = 工厂顺利完成了向新的生产线的转换。The changeover's impact on the workforce was minimal. = 这次转换对员工的影响很小。 | shift, transition, switch, conversion | continuity, stagnation | noun
