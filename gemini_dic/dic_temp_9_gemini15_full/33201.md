chromospheric:/ˌkroʊməˈsfɪrɪk/| adj.|色球的；色球层的|Relating to the chromosphere, the relatively thin layer of the sun's atmosphere above the photosphere and below the corona.  It is characterized by a reddish hue and is visible during a total solar eclipse.|例句：The chromospheric eruption released a significant amount of energy. 色球爆发释放了大量的能量。|近义词：solar (in the context of the sun's atmosphere)|反义词：photospheric, coronal|
