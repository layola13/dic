siemenses:/ˈziːmənsiz/| n.|西门子(公司)|Siemens is a global technology powerhouse, active in various fields.  西门子是一家活跃于各个领域的全球科技巨头。|近义词：None(as a proper noun)|反义词：None(as a proper noun)|名词


USAGE->

This word, being a proper noun (the name of a company), does not conjugate like verbs or adjectives.  Therefore, standard grammatical tenses (present, past, future, etc.) and voice (active/passive) do not apply.  The word itself remains unchanged.

例句：

I work for Siemens. = 我在西门子工作。

My friend bought a Siemens washing machine. = 我的朋友买了一台西门子洗衣机。


PHRASE->

Siemens AG = 西门子公司 (full name)
Siemens building = 西门子大楼
Siemens technology = 西门子技术
Siemens product = 西门子产品
Siemens engineer = 西门子工程师

词源:

The word "Siemens" originates from the surname of Werner von Siemens, the founder of the Siemens company.  His name, and thus the company's name,  has German origins.
