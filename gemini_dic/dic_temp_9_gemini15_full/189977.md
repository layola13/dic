sanctuary:/ˈsæŋktʃuəri/ | n. |圣所；庇护所；保护区 | A sacred place; a place of refuge or protection; a protected area for wildlife. |例句：The temple was a sanctuary for the villagers.  这座寺庙是村民们的圣所。

The bird sanctuary provides a safe haven for endangered species.  这个鸟类保护区为濒危物种提供了一个安全的避难所。

|近义词：refuge, refuge, shelter, haven, reserve |反义词：danger, threat, peril |词性：名词
