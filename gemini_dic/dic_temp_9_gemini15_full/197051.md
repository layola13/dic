sham:/ʃæm/| n., v.| 假象；骗局；假装；冒充 | A sham is something that is not what it appears to be; it is false or fraudulent.  It can also refer to the act of pretending or deceiving. |例句：The election was a complete sham. = 这场选举完全是一场骗局。

The painting was a sham; it was a clever forgery. = 这幅画是假的；这是一件精巧的伪造品。


近义词：fraud, hoax, deception, pretense, imitation | 反义词：authenticity, genuineness, reality, truth
