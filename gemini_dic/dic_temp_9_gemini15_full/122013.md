misstate:/mɪsˈsteɪt/| vt.|错误陈述，曲解|To represent falsely; to state incorrectly.  The politician misstated the facts to win the election. |近义词：misrepresent, distort, falsify |反义词：accurately state, correctly represent, clarify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misstates the figures in his report. = 他在他的报告中错误地陈述了数字。
She misstates her qualifications on her resume. = 她在简历上错误地陈述了自己的资格。


一般过去时:
The witness misstated the time of the accident. = 目击者错误地陈述了事故发生的时间。
He misstated his intentions to the investors. = 他向投资者错误地陈述了他的意图。


现在完成时:
The company has misstated its earnings for the past two quarters. = 该公司过去两个季度的收益都存在错误陈述。
She has repeatedly misstated the facts to the court. = 她多次向法庭错误陈述事实。


过去完成时:
The lawyer realized he had misstated the client's address in the previous hearing. = 律师意识到他在之前的听证会上错误地陈述了客户的地址。
They discovered that they had misstated the amount of the contract. = 他们发现他们错误地陈述了合同金额。


将来时:
He will misstate the facts again if given the chance. = 如果有机会，他会再次错误地陈述事实。
The government is likely to misstate the economic outlook. = 政府可能会错误地陈述经济前景。


虚拟语气:
If he had not misstated the facts, the outcome would have been different. = 如果他没有错误地陈述事实，结果就会不同。
I wish he hadn't misstated his position on the issue. = 我希望他没有在该问题上错误地陈述自己的立场。


被动语态:
The figures were misstated in the financial report. = 财务报告中数字被错误地陈述了。
The information was deliberately misstated to mislead the public. = 信息被故意错误地陈述以误导公众。


疑问句:
Did he misstate the facts on purpose? = 他是故意错误陈述事实的吗？
Could the witness have misstated what she saw? = 目击者可能错误地陈述了她所看到的情况吗？


否定句:
He did not misstate his intentions; he simply explained them poorly. = 他并没有错误地陈述他的意图；他只是解释得不好。
The company claims it did not misstate its profits. = 该公司声称它没有错误地陈述其利润。

seriously misstate = 严重误报
grossly misstate = 极度歪曲
