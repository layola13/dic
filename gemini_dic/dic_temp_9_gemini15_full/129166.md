no-win: /ˈnoʊ wɪn/ | adj. | 输多赢少；必输的；没有赢面 | A situation where there is no possibility of success or a positive outcome, regardless of the action taken.  | 例句：This negotiation is a no-win situation; whatever we do, we'll lose something.  这场谈判是一个必输的局面；无论我们做什么，都会有所损失。  |  例句：It's a no-win game trying to please everyone.  试图取悦所有人是一场必输的游戏。| 近义词：hopeless, futile, losing, unwinnable | 反义词：win-win, advantageous, favorable | 形容词
