polyendocrine:/ˌpɒliˈɛndəkrɪn/| /ˌpɑːliˈɛndrəkrɪn/ | adj. | 多内分泌的 | Affecting or relating to multiple endocrine glands.  The term describes conditions or diseases involving dysfunction of more than one endocrine gland.  | Example: Polyendocrine autoimmune syndrome type 1 is a rare genetic disorder characterized by polyendocrine failure. 多内分泌自身免疫综合征1型是一种罕见的遗传性疾病，其特征是多内分泌功能衰竭。|近义词：multiglandular | 反义词：monoglandular
