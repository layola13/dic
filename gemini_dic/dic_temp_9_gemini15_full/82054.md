gristmill:/ˈɡrɪstmɪl/| n. |磨坊，谷物磨坊|A gristmill is a mill that grinds grain into flour or meal.  It typically uses a water wheel or other power source to turn a large stone wheel that crushes the grain. |例句：The old gristmill by the river was a landmark in the town. 这条河边的古老磨坊是镇上的一个地标。
The farmer took his wheat to the gristmill to be ground into flour. 农民把他的小麦拿到磨坊去磨成面粉。|近义词：mill, flour mill|反义词：None
