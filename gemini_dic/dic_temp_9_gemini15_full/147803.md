overproves:/ˌoʊvərˈpruːvz/| v.|过度证明；过分证明|To prove something to an excessive degree; to provide more evidence than is necessary.  The evidence overproves the defendant's guilt.  This case overproves the need for stricter regulations.|近义词：over-demonstrates, excessively proves|反义词：underprove, fail to prove|
