/dʒoʊkɪŋ/| v.|开玩笑，说笑话|The act of making jokes or being playful and humorous.  It often implies a lighthearted and unserious intention. |例句：He was just joking; don't take it seriously. 他只是开玩笑，别当真。|例句：Stop joking around and help me with this. 别闹了，帮我干这个。|近义词：teasing, jesting, kidding|反义词：serious, earnest


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is joking around with his friends. = 他正在和朋友们开玩笑。
She jokes a lot to lighten the mood. = 她经常开玩笑来活跃气氛。

一般过去时:
He joked about his embarrassing moment. = 他拿他尴尬的时刻开玩笑。
They joked with each other all evening. = 他们整个晚上都在互相开玩笑。

现在完成时:
She has been joking all day. = 她整天都在开玩笑。
He has joked about that topic many times. = 他多次就那个话题开玩笑。

过去完成时:
I had joked about it before, but he didn't get it. = 我之前就开过这个玩笑，但他没听懂。
They had joked about the situation earlier that day. = 他们那天早些时候就对这种情况开过玩笑。

将来时:
I will joke about it later. = 我以后会开这个玩笑。
They will be joking about this for years to come. = 他们未来几年都会拿这件事开玩笑。

虚拟语气:
If I knew he was so sensitive, I wouldn't have joked about his haircut. = 如果我知道他这么敏感，我就不会拿他的发型开玩笑了。
I wish I hadn't joked about his weight. = 我真希望我没有拿他的体重开玩笑。

被动语态:
His jokes were often joked about behind his back. = 他的笑话经常在他背后被拿来开玩笑。
The situation was joked about by many people. = 许多人都拿这种情况开玩笑。

疑问句:
Are you joking? = 你在开玩笑吗？
Were they joking when they said that? = 他们说那话的时候是在开玩笑吗？

否定句:
He wasn't joking; he was serious. = 他不是开玩笑，他是认真的。
I don't joke about such things. = 我不会拿这种事情开玩笑。


PHRASE->
joke around = 开玩笑
play a joke on = 开某人的玩笑
crack a joke = 开个玩笑
tell a joke = 讲个笑话
be joking = 开玩笑 (强调状态)
