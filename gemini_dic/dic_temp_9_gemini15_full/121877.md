They abuse their power and disregard the well-being of their people. |Example: The misruler's reign was marked by widespread famine and unrest.  这个暴君的统治时期充满了广泛的饥荒和动乱。|近义词：tyrant, despot, oppressor |反义词：benevolent ruler, just leader |名词
