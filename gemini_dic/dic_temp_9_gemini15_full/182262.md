remounted:/ˌriːˈmaʊntɪd/ | v. |重新安装，重新装上，再次骑上 |To mount again; to put something back in place; to get back on a horse or other animal. |例句：The mechanic remounted the engine after the repair. 机械师修理后重新安装了发动机。
The rider remounted his horse after the fall. 骑手摔倒后又重新骑上了他的马。|近义词：reinstall, remount, reinstate |反义词：dismount, uninstall
