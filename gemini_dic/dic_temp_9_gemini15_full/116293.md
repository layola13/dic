massively:/ˈmæsɪvli/| adv.| 大规模地；巨大地；非常 |extremely or to a very great extent.  |例句：The project was massively over budget.  这个项目严重超支了。|例句：The earthquake massively damaged the city. 地震严重破坏了这座城市。|近义词：hugely, enormously, vastly, immensely |反义词：slightly, minimally, insignificantly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company massively expands its market share. 公司大幅扩张市场份额。
They massively invest in new technologies. 他们大力投资新技术。

一般过去时:
The storm massively affected the coastal areas. 暴风雨严重影响了沿海地区。
The government massively increased spending on education. 政府大幅增加了教育支出。

现在完成时:
The population has massively increased in recent years. 近年来人口急剧增长。
The company has massively improved its efficiency. 公司已经极大地提高了效率。

过去完成时:
They had massively underestimated the cost of the project. 他们严重低估了项目的成本。
The damage had been massively increased by the subsequent floods. 后续的洪水使破坏程度大大加剧。

将来时:
The company will massively increase its production next year. 公司明年将大幅增加产量。
The impact of climate change will massively affect the environment. 气候变化的影响将严重影响环境。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:
The city was massively damaged by the hurricane.  这座城市被飓风严重破坏了。

疑问句:

否定句:
