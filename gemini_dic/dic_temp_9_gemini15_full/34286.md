clarity:/ˈklærəti/| n. |清晰度；明晰；透明度 |The clarity of the explanation helped me understand the concept. 解释的清晰度帮助我理解了这个概念。|近义词：clearness, lucidity, transparency |反义词：vagueness, ambiguity, obscurity


clarity: /ˈklærəti/ | n. |清晰，明了，透明 |The clarity of the water allowed us to see the fish swimming below. 水的清澈让我们看到下面游动的鱼。|近义词：precision, distinctness, perspicuity |反义词：confusion, ambiguity, murkiness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The clarity of the image is excellent. = 图像的清晰度非常好。
This explanation lacks clarity. = 这个解释缺乏清晰度。

一般过去时:
The clarity of the speaker's voice improved during the presentation. = 演讲者的声音在演讲过程中变得更清晰了。
The article lacked clarity in its argument. = 这篇文章的论点不够清晰。


现在完成时:
The improvements have brought greater clarity to the project. = 这些改进使项目更加清晰。
We have achieved significant clarity on the issue. = 我们在这个问题上取得了显著的清晰度。


过去完成时:
The team had already achieved clarity on the design before the meeting. = 会议之前，团队已经对设计有了清晰的认识。
By then, the situation had gained much clarity. = 到那时，情况已经变得清晰多了。


将来时:
The new software will provide greater clarity in data analysis. = 新软件将使数据分析更加清晰。
Further research will bring more clarity to the subject. = 进一步的研究将使这个主题更加清晰。


虚拟语气:
If the instructions were clearer, there would be less confusion. = 如果说明更清晰，就不会有那么多的困惑。
I wish the report had more clarity. = 我希望报告更清晰些。


被动语态:
The issue was clarified by the new evidence. = 新证据澄清了这个问题。
The plan's details need to be clarified. = 计划的细节需要澄清。


疑问句:
Is there any clarity on this matter? = 这件事有明确的说法吗？
What is lacking in clarity? = 哪里不够清晰？


否定句:
The instructions were not clear; there was a lack of clarity. = 说明不清楚；缺乏清晰度。
The presentation lacked clarity. = 演示文稿不够清晰。



PHRASE->

词源:
Clarity comes from the Latin word "claritas," meaning "brightness, clearness."  The word evolved through Old French "clarté" before entering English.
