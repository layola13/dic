recommenced:/ˌriːkəˈmɛnst/| v.|重新开始，继续|To begin something again after an interruption.|The rain stopped, and the game recommenced. 雨停了，比赛重新开始。|They recommenced their conversation after a brief pause. 他们短暂暂停后继续他们的谈话。|restarted, resumed, continued|stopped, ceased, ended|动词

He recommences his studies every semester. = 他每学期都重新开始学习。
She recommences her work after a long break. = 她休息很久后重新开始工作。
