inter-actively: /ˌɪntərˈæktɪvli/ | adv. | 交互地，互动地 | In a way that involves interaction between a person and a computer or other machine.  | 例句：The software allows users to inter-actively design their own websites.  该软件允许用户交互式地设计自己的网站。  The children learned math concepts inter-actively through games. 孩子们通过游戏以互动的方式学习数学概念。| 近义词：interactively, reciprocally | 反义词：passively, unidirectionally

词源:
The word "inter-actively" is derived from the prefix "inter-" meaning "between" or "among," and the word "actively," which describes an action being performed in an energetic and engaged manner.  The combination creates a term specifying action involving reciprocal exchange or communication.
