peckings:/ˈpɛkɪŋz/ | n. |啄食；争夺；等级制度 |The act of pecking, often referring to birds pecking at food or to a hierarchical system where individuals compete for dominance.  It can also refer to repeated, often aggressive, actions. |例句：The chickens were engaged in a furious pecking over the scattered grain.  母鸡们为了散落的谷物而激烈地啄食。

The constant pecking of the woodpecker damaged the tree.  啄木鸟不停地啄食损坏了树木。


近义词：prodding, pecking order, hierarchy, competition |反义词：cooperation, harmony, submission |名词
