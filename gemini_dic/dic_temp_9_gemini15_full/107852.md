larding:/ˈlɑːrdɪŋ/| v.| 脂肪；肥肉；插播；(用肥肉) 填塞；|The act of inserting strips of fat into meat during cooking.  It's a culinary technique used to add flavor and moisture.|例句：The chef lards the roast with bacon to keep it juicy.  厨师用熏肉填塞烤肉以保持其多汁。

例句：She lards her speeches with anecdotes to make them more engaging. 她用轶事来填充她的演讲，使它们更引人入胜。|近义词：interspersing, interjecting, stuffing|反义词：removing, deleting
