desensitises:/ˌdiːˈsɛnsɪtaɪzɪz/| v.|使脱敏；使麻木；使不敏感|To make someone less sensitive to something, especially something unpleasant or shocking, by repeated exposure to it.  It often refers to reducing emotional or physical sensitivity.|例句：Constant exposure to violence on television desensitises viewers to its horrors.  长时间观看电视暴力会使观众对其恐怖之处麻木。

例句：The repeated use of strong language in the media desensitises people to its impact. 媒体中反复使用强烈的语言会让人们对其影响麻木。


近义词：numb, deaden, dull, inure


反义词：sensitize, heighten, sharpen


词性：动词
