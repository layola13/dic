incinerating:/ɪnˈsɪnəreɪtɪŋ/| v. |焚烧；烧成灰烬|The act of burning something to ashes.  This typically involves high temperatures to completely destroy the material.|The factory was incinerating waste materials. 工厂正在焚烧废料。 They are incinerating the old tires. 他们正在焚烧旧轮胎。|burning, cremating|preserving, saving|动词
