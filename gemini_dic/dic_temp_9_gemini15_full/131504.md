noncolonial: /ˌnɑːnˈkəloʊniəl/ | adj. | 非殖民地的；反殖民的 | Not relating to or characteristic of colonialism; opposed to colonialism.  |  例句：The museum's exhibition explored noncolonial perspectives on African art.  博物馆的展览探讨了非洲艺术的非殖民地视角。


例句：The country's noncolonial history is rich and complex. 这个国家的非殖民历史丰富而复杂。


近义词：anticolonial, postcolonial


反义词：colonial


词性：形容词
