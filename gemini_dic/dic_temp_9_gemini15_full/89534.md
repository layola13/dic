honorability:/ˌɒnəˈræbɪləti/| /ˌɑːnərəˈbɪləti/| n. |荣誉；高尚；正直|The quality or state of being honorable; integrity; uprightness.  It refers to the possession of high moral standards and a strong sense of ethics.  This includes qualities like honesty, fairness, and trustworthiness.  |例句：His honorability was never questioned throughout his long career.  他的高尚品格在他漫长的职业生涯中从未受到质疑。
