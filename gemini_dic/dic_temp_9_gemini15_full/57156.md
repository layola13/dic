dowdies:/ˈdaʊdiz/| n.|邋遢的女人；不修边幅的女人|A dowdy is a woman who is considered unattractive because of her untidy or old-fashioned clothes and appearance.  The term often carries a judgmental and somewhat derogatory connotation.|例句：She felt dowdy in her old clothes. 她穿着旧衣服感觉很邋遢。
She tried to avoid looking dowdy. 她试图避免看起来邋遢。|近义词：frump, slattern, drab|反义词：stylish, chic, elegant|名词
