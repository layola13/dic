reimagines:/ˌriːɪˈmædʒɪnz/| vt.|重新想象；赋予新的形象；重新构想|To conceive or represent anew; to give a new image or form to something.|The company reimagines its product line every year to stay ahead of the competition.  这家公司每年都会重新设计其产品线以保持竞争优势。|近义词：reimagine, reinvent, reconceive, revamp|反义词：maintain, preserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reimagines the story from a different perspective. 他从不同的角度重新构想这个故事。
She reimagines classic fairy tales with a modern twist. 她将经典童话故事赋予现代风格。


一般过去时:
The artist reimagined the landscape in a surreal style.  艺术家以超现实的风格重新诠释了这幅风景画。
They reimagined the old building as a modern art gallery. 他们将旧建筑重新设计成现代美术馆。


现在完成时:
The director has reimagined the classic novel for a contemporary audience.  导演已将这部经典小说重新演绎，以适应当代观众的口味。
She has reimagined her life after the accident. 事故后，她重新规划了自己的生活。


过去完成时:
He had reimagined the scene countless times before finally writing it down. 在最终写下之前，他已经无数次地重新构想过这个场景。
They had reimagined the project multiple times before settling on the final design. 在最终确定设计方案之前，他们已经多次重新构想这个项目。


将来时:
The team will reimagine the website to improve user experience.  团队将重新设计网站以改善用户体验。
She will reimagine her career path after taking a sabbatical. 休假后，她将重新规划自己的职业道路。


虚拟语气:
If I could, I would reimagine my past mistakes. 如果可以的话，我会重新审视我过去犯的错误。
I wish I could reimagine my childhood home. 我真希望能够重新想象一下我的童年之家。


被动语态:
The story was reimagined by a new generation of writers. 这个故事被新一代作家重新演绎。
The old building is being reimagined as a luxury hotel. 旧建筑正在被改造成一家豪华酒店。


疑问句:
How would you reimagine this painting? 你会如何重新诠释这幅画？
Will they reimagine the entire marketing strategy? 他们会重新构思整个营销策略吗？


否定句:
He did not reimagine the ending of the novel. 他没有重新构想小说的结局。
She refused to reimagine her life in a different city. 她拒绝在不同的城市重新规划自己的生活。
