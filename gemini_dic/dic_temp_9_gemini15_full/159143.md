podalgia:/poʊˈdældʒə/| n. |足痛；足部疼痛|Foot pain; pain in the foot.  The term usually refers to a persistent or recurring pain, rather than a temporary ache.|例句：He suffered from chronic podalgia, making walking difficult. 他患有慢性足痛，行走困难。

例句：The runner's podalgia was exacerbated by his worn-out shoes.  这位跑步者的足痛因他穿的旧鞋而加重。


近义词：foot pain, foot ache, metatarsalgia (if the pain is in the metatarsals)

反义词：无 (antonyms are not applicable, as this is a description of a condition)

词性:名词
