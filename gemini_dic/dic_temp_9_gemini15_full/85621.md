hauler:/ˈhɔːlər/| n. |运输者；搬运工；重型卡车|A hauler is a person or vehicle that transports goods or materials.  It can refer to a person who hauls things, or a large truck designed for heavy hauling.|例句：The farmer used a hauler to transport his harvest to the market.  这位农民用一辆运输车把他的收成运到市场。


The construction company hired a hauler to remove the debris.  这家建筑公司雇了一辆运输车来清除垃圾。|近义词：transporter, carrier, truck, lorry|反义词：receiver (in the sense of receiving goods, not the opposite of hauling)|词性:名词


USAGE->

The hauler transports goods across the country. = 这辆运输车在全国各地运输货物。
He hauls heavy equipment for a living. = 他以搬运重型设备为生。
