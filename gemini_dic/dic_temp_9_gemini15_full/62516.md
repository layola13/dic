endpoints:/ˈɛndˌpɔɪnts/ | n. |端点，终点，接口 |The endpoints of a line segment are the two points that define the segment.  端点是定义线段的两点。| 近义词：terminals, extremities, interfaces | 反义词：midpoints, centers
