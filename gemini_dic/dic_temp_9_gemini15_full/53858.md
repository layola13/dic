discontinuable: /ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuəbl/ | adj. | 可中断的；可停止的 | Capable of being discontinued or stopped.  This word implies that something can be ceased or ended, either temporarily or permanently.  It often describes processes, services, or actions. |


例句：

The subscription service is discontinuable at any time.  =  该订阅服务随时可以取消。

The project, though important, is discontinuable if funding is not secured. =  尽管该项目很重要，但如果资金没有保障，也可以停止。


近义词：stoppable, terminable, interruptible

反义词：continuous, uninterrupted, permanent


词性：形容词
