lotta:/ˈlɑːtə/| pron.|许多，大量|A shortened, informal version of "a lot of". It's used colloquially to indicate a large quantity or number of something.  |例句：I have a lotta homework to do tonight.  今晚我有很多家庭作业要做。|例句：She's got a lotta friends. 她有很多朋友。|近义词：many, much, a lot of, heaps of, tons of|反义词：few, little, a small amount of


USAGE->
