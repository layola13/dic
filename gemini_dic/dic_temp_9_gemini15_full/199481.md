sincerity:/sɪnˈsɛrəti/| n. |真诚；诚恳；真挚|The quality of being free from deceit or hypocrisy; honesty and genuineness.  It often refers to a person's feelings or intentions being genuine and honest. |例句：Her sincerity was evident in her heartfelt apology.  她的真诚在她发自肺腑的道歉中显而易见。

例句：His sincerity impressed everyone present. 他的真诚给在场的所有人都留下了深刻的印象。


近义词：honesty, truthfulness, genuineness, candor, integrity

反义词：dishonesty, deceit, hypocrisy, insincerity, falseness


词性:名词
