lecturer:/ˈlɛktʃərər/ | n. | 讲师；讲座人 | A person who gives lectures, especially at a college or university.  |  He is a lecturer in history at Oxford University. 他是牛津大学的一名历史学讲师。 | professor, instructor, teacher | student, listener | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The lecturer explains the concept clearly. = 讲师清晰地解释了这个概念。
He lectures on ancient history. = 他讲授古代史。


一般过去时:
The lecturer delivered a fascinating lecture yesterday. = 讲师昨天做了一个精彩的讲座。
She lectured at the university for many years. = 她在那所大学做了很多年的讲师。


现在完成时:
The lecturer has already finished his presentation. = 讲师已经完成了他的演讲。
