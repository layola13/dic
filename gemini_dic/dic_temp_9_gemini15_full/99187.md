intercitizenship: /ˌɪntərˈsɪtɪzənʃɪp/ | n. |跨国公民身份；国际公民身份| The concept of intercitizenship refers to the rights and responsibilities individuals hold across national borders, acknowledging their interconnectedness and shared humanity. It challenges the traditional notion of citizenship limited to a single nation-state.  Intercitizenship emphasizes transnational identities, affiliations, and participation in global governance. | Example:  The increasing interconnectedness of the world has led to a growing interest in the concept of intercitizenship.  跨界日益紧密的全球化促使人们对跨国公民身份的概念越来越感兴趣。|近义词：transnational citizenship, global citizenship |反义词：nationalism, isolationism |名词
