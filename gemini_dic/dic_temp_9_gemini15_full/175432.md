ramekins:/ˈræmɪkɪnz/| n. |小烤盘，小焗烤盘|Small, oven-safe dishes, typically made of ceramic or stoneware, used for baking individual portions of food, such as soufflés, custards, or gratins.  They are often shallow and round or oval in shape.|Example:  She baked individual chocolate lava cakes in ramekins. 她用小烤盘烤了单个熔岩巧克力蛋糕。|近义词：oven-safe dish, small baking dish|反义词：large baking pan
