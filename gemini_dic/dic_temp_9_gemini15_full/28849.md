carpers:/ˈkɑːrpərz/ | n. |吹毛求疵者，抱怨者 | People who find fault or complain constantly. They are critical and often focus on minor details.  |例句：The carpers in the audience constantly interrupted the speaker with petty complaints.  听众中的吹毛求疵者不断地用琐碎的抱怨打断演讲者。

例句：Despite the overwhelming positive feedback, a few carpers still found flaws in the project. 尽管好评如潮，一些吹毛求疵者仍然在项目中发现了缺陷。|近义词：critics, complainers, fault-finders, grumblers |反义词：praisers, flatterers, supporters

They carp about everything. = 他们对每件事都抱怨。
