multinationals:/ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənəlz/| n. |跨国公司；多国公司 |Companies that operate in more than one country, often with a headquarters in one country and subsidiary companies or branches in others. They typically engage in international trade and investment. |例句：Many multinationals are headquartered in developed countries but operate factories in developing nations. 许多跨国公司的总部设在发达国家，但在发展中国家设有工厂。|例句：The rise of multinationals has led to increased global trade and interconnectedness. 跨国公司的兴起导致了全球贸易和互联互通的增加。|近义词：transnationals, international corporations, global companies |反义词：domestic companies, local businesses |名词
