favel:/ˈfævəl/| n.|贫民窟，贫民区|A favela is a Brazilian shantytown or slum, typically characterized by densely packed housing, inadequate sanitation, and poverty.  The term is often used to describe similar informal settlements in other parts of the world.|例句：The favela was vibrant with life despite its challenging circumstances.  这个贫民窟尽管环境恶劣，却充满生机。|近义词：slum, shantytown, ghetto|反义词：suburb, affluent area|名词
