rustlings:/ˈrʌs(ə)lɪŋz/| n. |沙沙声，瑟瑟声 |The soft sounds of leaves and branches moving gently in the breeze.  |例句：The rustlings in the bushes made me jump.  灌木丛中的沙沙声让我吓了一跳。|例句：I heard the rustlings of silk as she moved. 我听到她移动时丝绸的沙沙声。|近义词：whispers, murmurs, susurrus |反义词：loud noises, booming sounds |名词
