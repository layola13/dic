pick out:/pɪk aʊt/ | vt. |挑选；辨认出；看出；摘取 | To select or choose something from a group; to identify or distinguish something from its surroundings; to notice or discern something. |例句：I picked out a new dress for the party. 我为聚会挑选了一件新衣服。

例句：She picked out the errors in his report. 她指出了他报告中的错误。


近义词：select, choose, identify, discern, spot | 反义词：miss, overlook, ignore | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He picks out the best apples from the basket. 他从篮子里挑选最好的苹果。
They pick out the important details from the document. 他们从文件中挑选出重要的细节。

一般过去时:
She picked out a beautiful painting at the art gallery. 她在美术馆挑选了一幅美丽的画。
We picked out the flaws in the design. 我们挑出了设计中的缺陷。

现在完成时:
I have picked out a gift for my friend’s birthday. 我已经为朋友的生日挑选了一份礼物。
They have picked out the winning team. 他们已经选出了获胜的队伍。

过去完成时:
She had picked out her outfit before the event began. 活动开始前，她已经选好了衣服。
We had picked out the location for the picnic before the weather changed. 天气变化之前，我们已经选好了野餐地点。

将来时:
I will pick out a new car next month. 我下个月将挑选一辆新车。
They will pick out the finalists for the competition. 他们将选出比赛的决赛选手。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pick out a more thoughtful gift. 如果我有更多时间，我会挑选一份更周到的礼物。
If she were here, she would pick out the best candidate. 如果她在，她会选出最好的候选人。


被动语态:
A winning design was picked out from hundreds of entries.  一个获奖的设计从数百个参赛作品中被选中。
The errors were picked out by the editor.  错误被编辑找出来了。


疑问句:
Have you picked out a dress for the wedding yet? 你已经为婚礼选好衣服了吗？
Which car did they pick out for the race?  他们为比赛选了什么车？


否定句:
I couldn’t pick out the right size shoes. 我找不到合适的尺码的鞋子。
He didn’t pick out any mistakes in my essay. 他没有挑出我论文中的任何错误。
