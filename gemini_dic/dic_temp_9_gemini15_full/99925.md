internee:/ˌɪntərˈniː/| n. |实习生，被拘留者|A person undergoing a period of supervised practical training in a profession or occupation; someone confined or detained, especially during a war.|例句：She's an internee at a law firm. 她是一家律师事务所的实习生。|例句：During the war, many civilians were internees. 战争期间，许多平民都被拘留了。|近义词：trainee, intern, prisoner|反义词：employer, supervisor
