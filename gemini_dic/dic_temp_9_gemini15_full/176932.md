realize:/ˈriːəlaɪz/ | vt. |意识到，实现，了解 |To understand a situation, fact, or feeling fully; to achieve or fulfill (something planned or desired).|例句：I didn't realize how late it was. 我没意识到已经这么晚了。

例句：He finally realized his ambition to become a doctor. 他最终实现了成为一名医生的抱负。


近义词：understand, comprehend, achieve, accomplish | 反义词：misunderstand, ignore, neglect | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I realize the importance of this project. = 我意识到这个项目的重要性。
They realize their mistake. = 他们意识到他们的错误。


一般过去时:
She realized she had lost her keys. = 她意识到她丢了钥匙。
He realized his dream of traveling the world. = 他实现了环游世界的梦想。


现在完成时:
I have realized how much I love her. = 我已经意识到我有多爱她。
They have realized their potential. = 他们已经发挥了自己的潜能。


过去完成时:
I had realized my error before submitting the report. = 我在提交报告之前就意识到了我的错误。
She had realized the danger before it was too late. = 在为时已晚之前，她意识到了危险。


将来时:
I will realize my goals one day. = 我总有一天会实现我的目标。
They will realize the benefits of this change soon. = 他们很快就会意识到这种变化的好处。


虚拟语气:
If I realized the consequences, I would not have done it. = 如果我意识到了后果，我不会那样做的。
I wish I had realized the truth earlier. = 我真希望我早点意识到真相。


被动语态:
The problem was realized by the team. = 这个问题被团队意识到了。
His potential has never been fully realized. = 他的潜力从未得到充分发挥。


疑问句:
Do you realize the risk involved? = 你意识到其中涉及的风险吗？
Does he realize how much he means to me? = 他意识到他对我的重要性吗？


否定句:
I didn't realize the depth of his sadness. = 我没有意识到他悲伤的程度。
They didn't realize the impact of their actions. = 他们没有意识到他们行为的影响。



PHRASE->
realize a dream  实现梦想
realize a profit  实现盈利
realize one's potential  发挥潜能
realize the importance of  意识到……的重要性
realize the truth  意识到真相
