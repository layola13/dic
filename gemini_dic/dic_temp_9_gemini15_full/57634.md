draughtsmen:/ˈdrɑːftsmən/ | n. |绘图员，制图员 | A draughtsman is a person who makes technical drawings, especially of buildings or machines.  They are skilled in using drafting tools and software to create precise and detailed plans and diagrams.  | Example: The experienced draughtsman meticulously created the blueprints for the new bridge.  经验丰富的绘图员一丝不苟地绘制了新桥的蓝图。  |近义词： draftsman, technical drawer, designer |反义词： N/A
