outpouring:/ˈaʊtpɔːrɪŋ/ | n. |倾泻；大量涌现；大量表达 | A large amount of something that comes suddenly and forcefully.  It often refers to emotions, support, or something physical. |例句：There was an outpouring of grief after the tragic accident.  悲剧事故发生后，人们悲痛欲绝。

例句：The outpouring of donations helped the victims rebuild their lives.  大量的捐款帮助受害者重建生活。


近义词：effusion, torrent, flood, deluge


反义词：lack, scarcity, dearth


词性：名词
