The crowd became phrensied with excitement as the band took the stage. 乐队上台时，人群兴奋地狂热起来。


近义词：frantic, frantic, wild, frenzied, manic, deranged, rabid, berserk, demented | 反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil, serene


词源:  From Middle English *frensy, from Old French freneisie, from Latin phrenesis, from Greek phrēn- (phren-), "mind, diaphragm" + -ēsis, "-itis" (inflammation). The original meaning referred to a mental illness, but the modern meaning focuses on the outward manifestation of that illness.
