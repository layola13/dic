puddlier:/ˈpʌdliər/| n. | 涉水者；(尤指)在泥泞中行走的人 | A puddlier is someone who walks or works in mud or puddles, especially in a careless or clumsy manner.  They might be involved in activities that necessitate wading through mud, like farming or construction. | Example: The farmer, a seasoned puddlier,  sloshed through the muddy field without a second thought. =  这位农民，一位经验丰富的涉水者，毫不犹豫地趟过了泥泞的田地。|近义词：mudlark, wader |反义词：None directly applicable.
