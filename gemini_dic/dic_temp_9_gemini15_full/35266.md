clustered:/ˈklʌstərd/ | adj. | 成群的；聚集的 | Grouped or gathered closely together.  | The sheep were clustered together in the field.  羊群聚集在田野里。 The houses were clustered around the village green. 房屋聚集在村中心的绿地上。| grouped, aggregated, concentrated | dispersed, scattered | 形容词


USAGE->

过去完成时:
