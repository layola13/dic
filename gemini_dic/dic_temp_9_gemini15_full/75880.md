fund:/fʌnd/| n. v.|资金；基金；资源；资助|The word "fund" comes from the Old French word "fond," meaning "bottom" or "foundation," which further developed from the Latin word "fundus," meaning "bottom" or "base."  This reflects the idea of a fund as the base or foundation of something. As a noun, it refers to a sum of money saved or made available for a specific purpose. As a verb, it means to provide money for something. |例句：The company has a large fund for research and development. 这家公司有大量的研发资金。

The university received a generous fund to build a new library.  大学收到了慷慨的捐款来建造一座新的图书馆。|近义词：money, capital, resources, grant, endowment|反义词：debt, deficit, poverty|名词,动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They fund many research projects. = 他们资助许多研究项目。
The company funds its operations through various means. = 这家公司通过各种途径为其运营提供资金。


一般过去时:
The government funded the construction of the new highway. = 政府资助了新高速公路的建设。
She funded her education with student loans. = 她用助学贷款资助了自己的教育。


现在完成时:
We have funded several charitable organizations this year. = 今年我们已经资助了几个慈善组织。
They have funded the project completely. = 他们已经完全资助了这个项目。


过去完成时:
They had funded the project before the deadline. = 他们已在截止日期前为该项目提供了资金。
She had funded her trip before she left. = 她动身前已为自己的旅行筹集了资金。


将来时:
The company will fund the expansion project next year. = 公司明年将为扩张项目提供资金。
We will fund the research once we secure the grant. = 我们一旦获得拨款，就会资助这项研究。


虚拟语气:
If we had more funds, we could expand our business. = 如果我们有更多资金，我们就能扩大业务。
I wish we could fund that project. = 我希望我们能够资助那个项目。


被动语态:
The project was funded by a private foundation. = 该项目由一个私人基金会资助。
The research is being funded by the government. = 这项研究正在由政府资助。


疑问句:
How will the project be funded? = 这个项目将如何获得资金？
Who funded the research? = 谁资助了这项研究？


否定句:
The company did not fund the project. = 公司没有资助该项目。
We cannot fund the project without more investors. = 没有更多投资者，我们就无法资助该项目。
