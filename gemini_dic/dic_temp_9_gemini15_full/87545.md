heterologous:/ˌhɛtərˈɒləɡəs/| adj.| 异源的；异种的 |  Of different origin; derived from a different source or organism.  The term often refers to  genes, proteins, or other biological molecules derived from different species or strains. |

例句：

The researchers used heterologous expression to produce the human protein in bacteria.  研究人员利用异源表达在大肠杆菌中生产人类蛋白质。

Heterologous gene expression is a common technique in biotechnology. 异源基因表达是生物技术中一种常见的技术。


近义词：foreign, dissimilar, unlike, alien


反义词：homologous, endogenous


词性：形容词
