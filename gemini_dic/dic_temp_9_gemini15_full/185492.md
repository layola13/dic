reupholstering:/ˌriːˈuːpɒlstəriŋ/| v.|重新装饰（家具）|The act of replacing the upholstery on furniture.  It involves removing the old fabric, padding, and other materials, and then installing new ones. |Example: We're reupholstering the old armchair to give it a new lease of life. 我们正在重新装饰旧扶手椅，让它焕发新生。|近义词:refurbishing, renovating, recovering|反义词:None specifically, but the opposite would be leaving the upholstery as it is.|动词
