hover:/ˈhʌvər/| v.|盘旋，犹豫，徘徊|To remain in one place in the air.|The helicopter hovered above the building. 直升机盘旋在建筑物上方。The bird hovered over its nest. 鸟儿盘旋在巢穴上方。|近义词：float, linger, hang|反义词：descend, plunge|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hummingbird hovers near the flower.  蜂鸟在花附近盘旋。
He hovers over his work, unsure of what to do next. 他犹豫地在他工作上徘徊，不确定下一步该做什么。

一般过去时:
The helicopter hovered over the accident scene. 直升机盘旋在事故现场上空。
She hovered near the door, waiting for him to leave. 她徘徊在门边，等他离开。


现在完成时:
The bee has been hovering around the honey pot for minutes. 蜜蜂已经在蜂蜜罐周围盘旋了好几分钟了。
He has been hovering over that decision for days. 他已经纠结那个决定好几天了。
