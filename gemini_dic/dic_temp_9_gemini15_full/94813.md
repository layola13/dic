impulsivity: /ˌɪmpʌlˈsɪvɪti/ | n. |冲动性，易冲动| The tendency to act on a sudden urge or impulse without considering the consequences.  It often involves a lack of planning or forethought. |例句：His impulsivity often led him into trouble.  他的冲动经常给他带来麻烦。

例句：Her impulsivity caused her to quit her job without another lined up.她的冲动让她在没有找到另一份工作的情况下辞职了。


近义词：rashness, recklessness, impulsiveness, spontaneity | 反义词：deliberation, carefulness, premeditation

词性：名词
