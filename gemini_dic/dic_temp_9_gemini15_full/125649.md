mummifications:/ˌmʌmɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 木乃伊化（过程）；制作木乃伊 | The process of embalming and drying a body to prevent decay, especially as practiced by the ancient Egyptians.  This process often involved removing internal organs, treating the body with natron, and wrapping it in linen bandages. |  The mummifications of ancient Egyptian pharaohs were elaborate rituals. 古埃及法老的木乃伊制作是复杂的仪式。  The discovery of the mummifications helped scientists understand ancient burial practices. 木乃伊化的发现帮助科学家了解古代的丧葬习俗。 | embalming, preservation | decomposition, decay |  名词
