sinology:/ˌsɪnˈɒlədʒi/ | n. |汉学；中国学；中国研究 | The academic study of China, its languages, history, culture, and society.  It encompasses a wide range of disciplines, including history, literature, philosophy, art, and politics. | Example:  Her expertise lies in sinology, particularly the Tang Dynasty poetry.  她擅长汉学，尤其是唐代诗歌。 | 近义词: Chinese studies, China studies | 反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑与其他区域研究学科对比，如Japanology)
