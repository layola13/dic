sleeves:/sliːvz/| n.|袖子，袖套|The part of a garment that covers the arm.  Sleeves can vary greatly in length, style, and design, depending on the garment and fashion trends. |例句：The sleeves of her dress were too long.  她的裙子袖子太长了。

例句：He rolled up his sleeves to wash the dishes. 他卷起袖子洗碗。


近义词：cuffs (袖口),  (in some contexts) arms (手臂)

反义词：None directly, but antonyms might relate to the absence of sleeves, such as sleeveless.

词性:名词


词源:

Old English *slēf, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch sloef and German Schlauf ‘loop’.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His shirt sleeves are long. = 他的衬衫袖子很长。
The sleeves of this jacket are too tight. = 这件夹克的袖子太紧了。


一般过去时:
Her sleeves were dirty after gardening. = 她园艺后袖子脏了。
The sleeves of his coat were torn. = 他外套的袖子撕破了。


现在完成时:
I have shortened the sleeves of my coat. = 我已经把外套的袖子改短了。
She has mended the sleeves of her sweater. = 她已经缝补了她毛衣的袖子。


过去完成时:
He had already rolled up his sleeves before he started working. = 他在开始工作之前就已经卷起了袖子。
The sleeves of the dress had been stained before she wore it. = 这件裙子的袖子在她穿之前就已经弄脏了。


将来时:
I will lengthen the sleeves of my dress. = 我将把裙子的袖子加长。
She will have the sleeves of her jacket altered. = 她将把夹克的袖子改一下。


虚拟语气:
If the sleeves were longer, it would be perfect. = 如果袖子更长一些，就完美了。
I wish the sleeves of this shirt were shorter. = 我希望这件衬衫的袖子短一些。


被动语态:
The sleeves were carefully sewn. = 袖子被仔细缝制。
The sleeves are being embroidered. = 袖子正在被绣花。


疑问句:
Are the sleeves detachable? = 袖子可以拆卸吗？
How long are the sleeves? = 袖子有多长？


否定句:
The sleeves are not long enough. = 袖子不够长。
The sleeves weren't dirty. = 袖子不脏。


PHRASE->

up one's sleeves = 偷偷地进行；暗中策划
