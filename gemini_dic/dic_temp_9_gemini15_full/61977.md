enabler:/ɪˈneɪblər/ | n. |促成因素；使能者；怂恿者 | A person or thing that makes it possible for something to happen or someone to do something.  It can also refer to someone who enables another person's harmful behavior by supporting or protecting them. |例句：His supportive family was an enabler of his success.  他的支持家庭是他成功的促成因素。

例句：She became an enabler for her addicted brother, continually bailing him out of trouble. 她成了她吸毒兄弟的怂恿者，不断地把他从困境中解救出来。


近义词：facilitator, supporter, accomplice (in the negative sense) | 反义词：obstacle, impediment, hinderer
