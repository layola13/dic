inhered:/ɪnˈhɪrd/| v.|固有的，内在的|To exist as a permanent, essential, or characteristic attribute.|The flaws inherent in the system.  这个系统的固有缺陷。|近义词：intrinsic, innate, inborn|反义词：acquired, extrinsic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The property inherent in the design. = 设计中固有的特性。
This problem inheres in the nature of the subject. = 这个问题存在于主题的本质之中。


一般过去时:
The right inherent in the constitution inhered in the people. = 宪法中固有的权利属于人民。
His arrogance inhered in his nature. = 他的傲慢是他的天性使然。


现在完成时:
The problems inherent in the project have become more apparent. = 项目中固有的问题变得更加明显。
It has always inhered in my belief that honesty is the best policy. = 我一直相信诚实为上策。


过去完成时:
Before the discovery, scientists had believed the property inhered in the substance. = 在发现之前，科学家们认为这种特性存在于该物质中。
It had always inhered in their family tradition to be hospitable. = 热情好客一直是他们家族的传统。


将来时:
The defects inherent in the design will likely remain.  = 设计中的缺陷可能会持续存在。
The company’s culture will likely still inhere in its values. = 公司文化很可能会仍然体现在其价值观中。


虚拟语气:
If the problem inhered in the software, we would have to rewrite it. = 如果问题存在于软件中，我们就必须重写它。
I wish this inherent bias did not inhere in their judgment. = 我希望他们的判断中不存在这种固有偏见。


被动语态:
The flaw was found to inhere in the manufacturing process. = 缺陷被发现存在于制造过程中。
The problem is believed to inhere in the underlying assumptions. = 这个问题据信存在于根本假设中。


疑问句:
Does this problem inhere in the nature of the task? = 这个问题是否在于任务的本质？
Where does the difficulty inhere? = 困难在于哪里？


否定句:
The difficulty does not inhere in the technology itself. = 困难并不在于技术本身。
The problem didn't inhere in the design; it was a manufacturing error. = 问题不在于设计；而是制造错误。
