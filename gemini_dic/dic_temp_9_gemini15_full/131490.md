noncollapsable: /ˌnɒnkəˈlæpsəbl/ | adj. |不可折叠的，不可坍塌的 | Not capable of collapsing or being folded.  This adjective describes something that cannot be compressed or flattened.  It's often used to describe structures or materials designed to maintain their shape and integrity under pressure.  | Example: The noncollapsable  container ensured the safe transport of the fragile specimens.  不可折叠的容器确保了易碎标本的安全运输。 The bridge's noncollapsable design withstood the earthquake. 这座桥梁的不可坍塌设计经受住了地震的考验。 |近义词：uncollapsible, rigid, inflexible | 反义词：collapsible, foldable, flexible |
