pipelayer:/ˈpaɪplaɪər/| n. |管道铺设者；管道工|A person who lays or installs pipes.  Pipelayers work on various projects, from residential plumbing to large-scale oil and gas pipelines.|例句：The pipelayer carefully connected the new pipe to the existing system. 管道铺设者小心地将新管道连接到现有系统。 The experienced pipelayer completed the task efficiently and safely.  经验丰富的管道铺设者高效安全地完成了任务。|近义词：pipe fitter, plumber (in some contexts)|反义词：N/A|名词
