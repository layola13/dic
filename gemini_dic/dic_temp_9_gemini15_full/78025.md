gentrifier: /ˈdʒɛntrɪfaɪər/ | n. |  使中产阶级化者；进行中产阶级化改造的人 | A person who buys property in a "rundown" neighborhood, improving it and raising property values, often displacing long-term residents.  | Example: The influx of gentrifiers into the area has driven up rents and forced many longtime residents to relocate.  使中产阶级化者涌入该地区导致租金上涨，迫使许多长期居民搬迁。| 近义词：developer, renovator, urban revitalizer | 反义词：slum dweller (in the context of displacement)
