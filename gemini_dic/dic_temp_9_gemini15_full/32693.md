chloralamide:/ˌklɔːrˈæləmaɪd/| n. |氯醛酰胺|A hypnotic drug formerly used as a sedative and hypnotic.  It's a derivative of chloral hydrate.  However, due to its potential side effects and the availability of safer alternatives, it's largely been replaced.|Example: Chloralamide was once a common sedative, but its use has declined significantly.  氯醛酰胺曾是一种常见的镇静剂，但其使用已大幅减少。|近义词：chloral hydrate (though not a direct synonym, it shares similar properties)|反义词：stimulant|名词
