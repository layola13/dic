pipeclay:/ˈpaɪpkleɪ/| n. |陶土；烟斗粘土|A fine-grained, white clay used to make tobacco pipes and other ceramic objects. It is typically smooth and easily molded.  |例句：The artisan carefully shaped the pipeclay into a delicate pipe bowl.  这位工匠小心地将陶土塑造成一个精致的烟斗碗。

The sculptor used pipeclay to create lifelike figurines.  雕塑家用陶土创作了栩栩如生的雕像。|近义词：potter's clay, china clay |反义词：None specifically, but coarse clay could be considered an antonym.|名词
