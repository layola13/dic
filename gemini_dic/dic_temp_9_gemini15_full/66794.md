expand:/ɪkˈspænd/ | vt. vi. n. |扩张；扩展；详述；阐述 | to increase in size, number, or amount; to make something larger or more detailed |例句：The company plans to expand its operations into new markets.  公司计划将其业务扩展到新的市场。

The balloon expanded rapidly as it was filled with air. 气球充气后迅速膨胀。


近义词：enlarge, extend, amplify, elaborate | 反义词：contract, shrink, condense, reduce | 词性：动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The metal expands when heated. 金属受热膨胀。
The company expands its business globally. 公司在全球扩张业务。

一般过去时:
The universe expanded after the Big Bang. 宇宙在大爆炸后膨胀。
He expanded on his initial statement. 他详细阐述了他的最初声明。

现在完成时:
The city has expanded significantly in recent years. 近年来，这座城市已大幅扩张。
She has expanded her knowledge of history. 她已经扩展了她对历史的了解。

过去完成时:
The empire had expanded its territory before the war. 战争爆发前，帝国已经扩张了其领土。
He had expanded his ideas in the previous meeting. 他在之前的会议上已经详细阐述了他的想法。

将来时:
The business will expand into new markets next year. 这家公司明年将拓展新的市场。
They will expand the scope of the research. 他们将扩大研究范围。

虚拟语气:
If the economy expanded, more jobs would be created. 如果经济扩张，就会创造更多的就业机会。
I wish I had expanded on my point earlier. 我希望我早点详细说明我的观点。

被动语态:
The bridge was expanded to accommodate more traffic. 桥梁进行了扩建以容纳更多交通流量。
The argument was expanded upon in the next chapter. 下一章对论点进行了详细阐述。

疑问句:
Does the universe still expand? 宇宙还在膨胀吗？
Will the company expand its product line? 公司会扩大其产品线吗？

否定句:
The metal did not expand as expected. 金属没有按预期膨胀。
The team did not expand on their findings. 团队没有详细阐述他们的发现。


PHRASE->
expand on: 详细阐述
expand into: 扩展到
expand upon: 进一步阐述
rapid expansion: 快速扩张
economic expansion: 经济扩张
