foyer:/ˈfɔɪeɪ/| n.| 门厅；休息室；剧院门厅 | An entrance hall or lobby, especially in a public building such as a theater or hotel.  |The foyer of the theater was crowded with people waiting for the show to begin. 剧院的门厅挤满了等待演出开始的人。The hotel foyer offered comfortable seating and a welcoming atmosphere.  酒店的门厅提供舒适的座位和温馨的氛围。| lobby, hall, vestibule |  (None strongly antonymous)| 名词
