The word "drumbeatings" is derived from the noun "drumbeat" (the sound of a drum) plus the suffix "-ings," which forms nouns indicating a process, activity, or result.  Therefore, "drumbeatings" refers to the action or sounds of repeated drum beats.  The word's etymology traces back to the Old English word "druma," referring to a drum.
