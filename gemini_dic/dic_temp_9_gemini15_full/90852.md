hunger:/ˈhʌŋɡər/| n.|饥饿；渴望；贪婪|a strong feeling of needing food; a strong desire or need for something|例句：The children were suffering from hunger.孩子们正遭受饥饿的折磨。|例句：She had a hunger for knowledge. 她渴望知识。|近义词：starvation, appetite, craving|反义词：satiety, fullness

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I feel hunger. = 我感到饥饿。
He often experiences hunger pangs. = 他经常感到一阵阵饥饿。

一般过去时:
I felt a deep hunger after the long hike. = 长途跋涉后我感到非常饥饿。
They suffered from hunger during the war. = 战争期间他们饱受饥饿之苦。

现在完成时:
I haven't felt this much hunger in years. = 多年以来我都没有感到如此饥饿。
She has been fighting hunger her whole life. = 她一生都在与饥饿作斗争。

过去完成时:
They had been hungry for days before they found food. = 他们在找到食物之前已经饿了好几天了。
I had not felt such intense hunger before. = 我以前从未感到如此强烈的饥饿感。

将来时:
I will be hungry by dinner time. = 到吃晚饭的时候我会饿了。
They will likely suffer from hunger if the aid doesn't arrive soon. = 如果援助很快不到达，他们很可能会挨饿。

虚拟语气:
If I had eaten breakfast, I wouldn't be so hungry now. = 如果我吃了早餐，现在就不会这么饿了。
I wish I hadn't skipped lunch; I'm starving now. = 我真希望我没有错过午餐；我现在饿死了。

被动语态:
Hunger is often caused by poverty. = 饥饿往往是由贫困造成的。
The problem of hunger is being addressed by many organizations. = 许多组织正在解决饥饿问题。

疑问句:
Are you hungry? = 你饿了吗？
How long have you been feeling this hunger? = 你饿了多久了？

否定句:
I am not hungry right now. = 我现在不饿。
They were not hungry after the large meal. = 他们吃完丰盛的一餐后并不饿。
