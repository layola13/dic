repreparing:/ˌriːprɪˈpeərɪŋ/| v.|重新准备，再次准备|The act of preparing something again;  making something ready a second time or after some interruption.|例句：He was repreparing the presentation after receiving new data. 他在收到新的数据后正在重新准备演示文稿。
例句：She spent the weekend repreparing her application for the scholarship. 她周末花时间重新准备奖学金申请。|近义词：preparing again,  re-doing, reviewing|反义词：finishing, completing
