reinserting: /ˌriːɪnˈsɜːrtɪŋ/ | v. |重新插入；重新装入 | The act of putting something back into its original place or position, especially after it has been removed.  |例句：The surgeon is reinserting the patient's implant. 外科医生正在为病人重新植入植体。

例句：They are reinserting the data into the database after a system failure. 系统故障后，他们正在将数据重新插入数据库。


近义词：reintroducing, replacing, restoring, implanting

反义词：removing, extracting, deleting


词性：动词 (present participle of reinsert)
