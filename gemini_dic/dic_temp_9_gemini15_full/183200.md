represcribe:/ˌriːˈpraɪskraɪb/| vt.|重新开处方；再次开处方|To prescribe (a medicine or treatment) again.  This often implies a continuation of a previous prescription, perhaps with a modification or adjustment.|例句：The doctor represcribed the antibiotics after the infection persisted.  医生在感染持续后再次开了抗生素处方。

例句：The patient needed the doctor to represcribe her medication after running out. 病人用完药后需要医生再次开处方。


近义词：reissue, renew, refill|反义词：discontinue, withdraw
