riverlike:/ˈrɪvərlaɪk/| adj. | 河流似的，蜿蜒的 | Resembling a river; flowing or winding like a river.  |例句：The long, riverlike road wound its way through the valley. 这条长长的，像河流一样的道路蜿蜒穿过山谷。

例句：The riverlike stream meandered through the meadow. 这条河流般的溪流蜿蜒穿过草地。


近义词：streamlike, meandering, winding, serpentine

反义词：straight, direct, linear


词性：形容词
