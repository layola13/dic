megafauna: /ˈmɛɡəˌfɔːnə/ | n. | 巨型动物群 |  大型动物群，指体重超过44公斤的动物，通常指在某个特定时期或地点占主导地位的动物。 This term is often used in paleontology and ecology to refer to large animals that are characteristic of a particular time or place. | 例句：The Pleistocene megafauna included mammoths, mastodons, and giant ground sloths. 更新世巨型动物群包括猛犸象、乳齿象和巨型地懒。

The extinction of the Australian megafauna remains a mystery. 澳大利亚巨型动物群的灭绝仍然是一个谜。


近义词：large animals, giant animals | 反义词：microfauna
