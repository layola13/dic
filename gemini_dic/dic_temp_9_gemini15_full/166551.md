prescheduled:/ˌpriːˈskɛdʒuːld/ | adj. | 预先安排的，预定的 |  Already planned or arranged in advance.  | The prescheduled meeting was cancelled due to unforeseen circumstances. 预先安排的会议由于不可预见的情况而被取消。  | scheduled, planned, arranged | unscheduled, impromptu, unplanned | 形容词
