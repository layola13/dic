ketonic:/kiˈtoʊnɪk/| adj.|酮的；酮症的| Relating to or characterized by ketones.  Ketones are chemical compounds produced by the body when it breaks down fat for energy.  The term is often used in the context of metabolic processes and medical conditions.|例句：The patient exhibited ketonic breath, a characteristic sign of diabetic ketoacidosis.  病人表现出酮症呼气，这是糖尿病酮症酸中毒的特征性迹象。|例句：A ketonic diet is low in carbohydrates and high in fats, forcing the body to burn fat for energy.|酮类饮食碳水化合物含量低，脂肪含量高，迫使身体燃烧脂肪来获取能量。|近义词：ketogenic|反义词：non-ketonic
