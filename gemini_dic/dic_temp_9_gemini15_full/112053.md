longed:/lɒŋd/| /lɔŋd/| v.|渴望，思念|To want something or someone very much; to yearn for.|例句：She longed for the days when she was young and carefree. 她渴望回到年轻无忧无虑的时光。
例句：He longed to see his family again. 他渴望再次见到家人。|近义词：yearned, craved, desired|反义词：disliked, abhorred|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I long for a peaceful life. = 我渴望平静的生活。
He longs to travel the world. = 他渴望环游世界。

一般过去时:
She longed to be a dancer when she was young. = 她年轻时渴望成为一名舞蹈家。
They longed for their homeland after years abroad. = 在国外多年后，他们渴望回到自己的祖国。

现在完成时:
I have longed for this moment for years. = 我渴望这一刻已经很多年了。
She has longed to see her grandchildren. = 她一直渴望见到她的孙子孙女。

过去完成时:
He had longed to meet his hero before he died. = 他在他死之前一直渴望见到他的英雄。
They had longed for a better life before they immigrated. = 他们移民之前一直渴望过更好的生活。


将来时:
I will long for this place when I am gone. = 我离开后会思念这个地方。
She will long for her friends after she moves away. = 她搬走后会思念她的朋友们。

虚拟语气:
If I had the chance, I would long for nothing. = 如果我有机会，我将一无所求。
I wish I had not longed for what I couldn't have. = 我真希望我没有渴望我无法得到的东西。

被动语态:
(Longed is rarely used in the passive voice.  A more natural phrasing would be needed.)  The opportunity was longed for by many. = 许多人渴望得到这个机会。(This is awkward, better phrasing is recommended.)
His return was longed for by his family. = 他的家人渴望他的回归。

疑问句:
What do you long for most in life? = 你生活中最渴望的是什么？
Did she long for anything in particular? = 她特别渴望什么吗？

否定句:
I do not long for fame or fortune. = 我不渴望名利。
He did not long for the company of others. = 他并不渴望与他人为伴。


PHRASE->
long for sth = 渴望某事物
long to do sth = 渴望做某事
long to see sb = 渴望见到某人
long after sth = 久久思念某事物
longingly look at sb/sth = 渴望地注视着某人/某物
