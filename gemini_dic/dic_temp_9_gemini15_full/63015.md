enquires:/ɪnˈkwaɪərz/| v.|询问；打听；查询|To ask for information.|The customer enquired about the price of the product.  顾客询问了产品的价格。|The librarian enquired whether I had a library card. 图书管理员询问我是否有借书证。|ask, inquire, investigate|ignore, disregard|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enquires about the weather every morning. 他每天早上都询问天气。
She enquires about the job opportunities. 她询问工作机会。


一般过去时:
I enquired about the lost luggage. 我询问了丢失的行李。
They enquired about the train schedule. 他们询问了火车时刻表。


现在完成时:
He has enquired about the flight details. 他已经询问了航班详情。
She has enquired about the availability of the tickets. 她已经询问了门票的供应情况。


过去完成时:
I had enquired about the hotel before booking. 我在预订前已经询问过酒店情况。
They had enquired about the price before making a decision. 他们在做决定之前已经询问过价格。


将来时:
I will enquire about the visa requirements. 我将询问签证要求。
She will enquire about the opening hours. 她将询问营业时间。


虚拟语气:
If I enquired, I might get an answer. 如果我询问，我可能会得到答案。
I wish I had enquired sooner. 我真希望我早点问。


被动语态:
The information was enquired about by many people. 许多人询问了这个信息。
The price has been enquired about frequently. 价格经常被询问。


疑问句:
Have you enquired about the job? 你询问过这份工作了吗？
Did you enquire about the delivery time? 你询问过送货时间了吗？


否定句:
I did not enquire about the details. 我没有询问细节。
They didn't enquire about the cost. 他们没有询问费用。


PHRASE->
enquire about = 询问关于...
enquire into = 调查...
enquire after = 询问（某人的健康状况）
make enquiries = 进行询问
enquire of = 向...询问
