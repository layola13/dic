give-up-the-ghost:/ɡɪv ʌp ðə ɡoʊst/| v. |断气；去世；放弃；停止运转|To die; to cease functioning; to abandon or relinquish something.|The old car finally gave up the ghost.  这辆旧车最终报废了。|The patient gave up the ghost after a long illness. 病人经过长期疾病后去世了。|expire, die, decease|live, survive, persist|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gives up the ghost every night when he tries to finish his assignment. 他每晚试图完成作业时都放弃了。
They give up the ghost easily when they face difficulties. 他们遇到困难很容易就放弃。


一般过去时:
The old machine gave up the ghost last week.  那台旧机器上周坏了。
My grandfather gave up the ghost peacefully in his sleep. 我爷爷在睡梦中安详地去世了。


现在完成时:
The project has given up the ghost due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目已经失败了。
He has given up the ghost on his dream of becoming a doctor. 他已经放弃了成为医生的梦想。


过去完成时:
The engine had given up the ghost before we reached the destination. 我们到达目的地之前引擎就已经坏了。
They had given up the ghost on the negotiations long before the deadline. 他们在截止日期很久以前就已经放弃了谈判。


将来时:
The old computer will give up the ghost soon. 这台旧电脑很快就会坏掉。
I fear that the business will give up the ghost without more investment. 我担心如果没有更多投资，这家公司将会倒闭。


虚拟语气:
If the engine hadn't given up the ghost, we would have arrived on time. 如果引擎没有坏掉，我们就能准时到达。
If I hadn't given up the ghost, I might have finished the marathon. 如果我没有放弃，我或许就能完成马拉松比赛。


被动语态:

The plan was given up as a lost cause.  这个计划被认为是失败的。


疑问句:

否定句:
