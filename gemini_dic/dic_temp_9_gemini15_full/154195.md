periople:/ˈpɛriəʊpl/| n. |角质层，(指甲的)半月痕|A thin, horny layer that covers the developing nail.  It is often referred to as the eponychium or cuticle, although technically it is distinct from the cuticle. The periople helps protect the nail matrix and the developing nail from environmental factors. |例句：The periople is a thin, transparent layer that protects the nail matrix.  角质层是一层薄而透明的层，保护着指甲基质。


The periople is essential for maintaining the health of the nail.  角质层对于维持指甲的健康至关重要。


近义词：eponychium, cuticle (although technically distinct) |反义词：None. |词性：名词
