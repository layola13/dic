preterrestrial: /ˌpriːtɛˈrɛstriəl/ | adj. | 超越地球的；地球以外的 |  Relating to or occurring beyond the Earth; extraterrestrial.  The term often implies a connection to or origin from a location beyond Earth, but not necessarily another planet. It emphasizes the "beyond Earth" aspect rather than specifically alien life. | Example: Preterrestrial phenomena are often the subject of science fiction.  超越地球的现象往往是科幻小说的主题。  |近义词：extraterrestrial, extramundane | 反义词：terrestrial
