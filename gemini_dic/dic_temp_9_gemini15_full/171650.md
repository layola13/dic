pulsation:/pʌlˈseɪʃən/ | n. |搏动；脉动；跳动 |The rhythmic expansion and contraction, especially of an organ or blood vessel.  It refers to a regular beat or throbbing.  |例句：The pulsation of the artery was strong and regular.  动脉的搏动强劲而规律。

例句：I could feel the pulsation in my temples after the intense workout. 剧烈运动后，我能感觉到太阳穴的跳动。


近义词：throb, beat, palpitation, vibration | 反义词：stillness, quiescence
