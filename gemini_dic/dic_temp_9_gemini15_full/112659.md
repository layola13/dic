lowed:/laud/| v.|允许，降低，减弱|to permit or allow something; to make something lower or weaker in intensity or volume|例句：They allowed him to leave early. 他们允许他早退。 The volume slowly lowered. 音量慢慢降低了。|近义词：permitted, reduced, lessened |反义词：prohibited, raised, increased

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They allow pets in the hotel. = 他们允许宠物入住酒店。
He allows his children to stay up late. = 他允许他的孩子们晚睡。

一般过去时:
She allowed me to borrow her car. = 她允许我借她的车。
The teacher allowed extra time for the test. = 老师为考试增加了额外的时间。

现在完成时:
They have allowed the project to proceed. = 他们已经允许项目继续进行。
He has allowed himself to become complacent. = 他让自己变得自满。

过去完成时:
She had allowed him to use her computer. = 她曾允许他使用她的电脑。
They had allowed the situation to deteriorate. = 他们曾允许情况恶化。

将来时:
They will allow visitors after 3pm. = 他们将在下午3点后允许游客进入。
He will allow more time for the project. = 他将为该项目留出更多时间。

虚拟语气:
If only they allowed smoking here! = 要是他们允许在这里吸烟就好了！
I wish they allowed dogs on the beach. = 我希望他们允许狗在海滩上玩耍。

被动语态:
Visitors are allowed only in designated areas. = 游客只允许在指定区域内活动。
The project was allowed to continue despite the setbacks. = 尽管遇到挫折，项目仍被允许继续进行。

疑问句:
Are we allowed to bring our own food? = 我们允许自带食物吗？
Is smoking allowed in this building? = 这栋楼允许吸烟吗？

否定句:
They do not allow outside food. = 他们不允许外带食物。
She didn't allow any interruptions. = 她不允许任何打断。


PHRASE->
allow for:考虑到, 顾及
allow of:容许
allow oneself:放纵
allow time:留出时间
to allow someone to do something:允许某人做某事
