endoctrine:/ɛnˈdɒktraɪn/ | vt. |灌输；教唆；向…灌输教义| To indoctrinate someone is to teach them a set of beliefs, often in a forceful or forceful way, so that they accept them without questioning. This often involves suppressing critical thinking and opposing viewpoints.| 例句：The cult leader attempted to indoctrinate his followers with his bizarre beliefs.  邪教领袖试图向他的追随者灌输他那怪诞的信仰。| 例句：The government tried to indoctrinate children with patriotic propaganda. 政府试图用爱国主义宣传来灌输孩子们。| 近义词：inculcate, inculcate, brainwash | 反义词：educate (in a critical and open-minded way), enlighten
