personate:/ˈpɜːrsənˌeɪt/| vt.|扮演；模仿；假扮|To assume the character or appearance of; to act the part of.|例句：He personated a police officer to gain entry to the building. 他假扮警察进入大楼。|例句：The actor personated Hamlet convincingly.  这位演员令人信服地扮演了哈姆雷特。|近义词：imitate, impersonate, represent|反义词：be oneself, be authentic


USAGE->
