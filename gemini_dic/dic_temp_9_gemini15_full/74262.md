foxtail:/ˈfɒkˌteɪl/ /ˈfɑːkˌteɪl/| n. |狐尾;狐尾草; (某些植物的)穗状花序 |A foxtail is a type of grass with a bushy, tail-like seed head resembling a fox's tail.  It can refer to the tail of a fox itself, or to various plants with similar seed heads.  |例句：The dog chased its tail, which resembled a miniature foxtail.  这只狗追着它像微型狐尾一样的尾巴。

The foxtail millet is a crucial crop in arid regions.  狐尾粟是干旱地区的重要作物。


近义词：brush (referring to the shape), plume (referring to the shape), tail (referring to the animal's tail)
反义词：None directly applicable.  The antonym would depend on the context (e.g., if referring to a plant, a plant with a different type of seed head).

词源:
