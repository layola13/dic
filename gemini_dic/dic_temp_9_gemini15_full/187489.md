rollerblading:/ˈroʊlərˌbleɪdɪŋ/ | n. |滑旱冰，玩轮滑 | The act of moving on rollerblades.  It is a popular recreational activity and a form of exercise. |例句：She enjoys rollerblading along the beach. 她喜欢沿着海滩滑旱冰。

例句：Rollerblading is a great way to stay fit. 滑旱冰是保持健康的绝佳方式。


近义词：rollerskating, skating


反义词：walking, cycling (These are alternative forms of locomotion, not direct opposites)


词性：名词(gerund)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rollerblades every morning. = 他每天早上都滑旱冰。
They rollerblade in the park on weekends. = 他们周末在公园滑旱冰。

一般过去时:
I rollerbladed to work yesterday. = 我昨天滑旱冰去上班。
We rollerbladed around the lake last Sunday. = 我们上周日绕着湖滑旱冰。

现在完成时:
She has rollerbladed for five years. = 她滑旱冰五年了。
They have never rollerbladed before. = 他们以前从未滑过旱冰。

过去完成时:
He had rollerbladed before he broke his leg. = 他摔断腿之前滑过旱冰。
We had already rollerbladed for an hour when it started to rain. = 下雨的时候我们已经滑旱冰一个小时了。

将来时:
I will rollerblade tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上会滑旱冰。
They will rollerblade at the beach this summer. = 他们今年夏天会在海滩滑旱冰。

虚拟语气:
If I had rollerblades, I would rollerblade every day. = 如果我有旱冰鞋，我每天都会滑旱冰。
I wish I could rollerblade as well as she does. = 我希望我能像她一样滑旱冰。

被动语态:  (Rollerblading itself cannot be easily put into the passive voice.  You would need to change the sentence structure.)
The park is often used for rollerblading. = 公园经常被用来滑旱冰。
Rollerblades are designed for speed and maneuverability. = 旱冰鞋的设计是为了速度和机动性。


疑问句:
Do you like rollerblading? = 你喜欢滑旱冰吗？
Where do they rollerblade? = 他们在哪里滑旱冰？

否定句:
I don't rollerblade very often. = 我不经常滑旱冰。
He didn't rollerblade because of the rain. = 因为下雨，他没滑旱冰。


PHRASE->
go rollerblading = 去滑旱冰
rollerblading rink = 滑旱冰场
rollerblading shoes = 旱冰鞋
rollerblading accident = 滑旱冰意外
inline rollerblading = 直排轮滑
