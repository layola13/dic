outbargained: /ˌaʊtˈbɑːrɡeɪnd/ | v. | 胜过；在讨价还价中战胜 | To get a better price or deal than someone else in a negotiation.  | 例句：I outbargained the seller and got the antique chair for half the asking price. 我在讨价还价中胜过卖家，以半价买到了那把古董椅。

例句：The shrewd businessman outbargained his competitors and secured the lucrative contract.  精明的商人胜过竞争对手，获得了有利可图的合同。


近义词：outmaneuver, outsmart, surpass, outdo,  undersell


反义词：be outbargained, be undersold
