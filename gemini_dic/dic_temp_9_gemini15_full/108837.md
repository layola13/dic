leavings:/ˈliːvɪŋz/| n.|残余物；遗物；剩余物|The remnants or remains of something, especially after a process of use, consumption, or decay.  It can also refer to something left behind after someone's departure.|例句：The leavings of the feast were quickly devoured by the stray cats.  宴会剩下的食物很快被流浪猫吃掉了。

例句：After the storm, there were many leavings scattered around the yard. 暴风雨过后，院子里散落着许多残留物。


近义词：remnants, remains, residue, scraps, leftovers|反义词：whole, complete, intact|词性：名词
