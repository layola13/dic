coffeehouse:/ˈkɔːfihaʊs/ | n. |咖啡馆 |A coffeehouse is an establishment that primarily serves coffee, tea, and other beverages, often along with pastries, desserts, and light meals.  It typically provides a relaxed atmosphere for socializing, studying, or working. |例句：We met at a cozy coffeehouse near the university. 我们在大学附近一家舒适的咖啡馆见面了。|近义词：cafe, coffee shop, bistro |反义词:None
