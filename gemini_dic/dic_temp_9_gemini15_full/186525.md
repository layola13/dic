ricocheting: /ˈrɪkəʃeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 反弹，弹回 |  The act of bouncing off a surface at an angle.  This word describes something that rebounds or deflects off a surface. It often implies a series of bounces. | 例句：The bullet ricocheted off the wall and into the ceiling. 子弹击中墙壁后反弹到天花板上。


The sound of the ball ricocheted through the empty stadium.  球的声音在空荡荡的体育馆里回荡。 | 近义词：rebound, bounce, deflect | 反义词：penetrate, pierce
