circled:/ˈsɜːrkl(d)/| adj. v.|圈起来的；环绕的；|环绕；围绕；圈出；|例句：The word was circled in red.  这个词用红色圈起来了。|近义词：encircled, ringed, surrounded |反义词：uncircled, open


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He circles the globe annually. 他每年环游世界。
The birds circle overhead. 鸟儿在头顶盘旋。

一般过去时:
She circled the paragraph with a pen. 她用笔圈起了这段话。
The plane circled the airport before landing. 飞机在降落前绕机场盘旋。

现在完成时:
They have circled the mountain twice. 他们已经绕山转了两圈。
The hawk has circled for hours.  这只鹰盘旋了好几个小时。

过去完成时:
He had circled the building before entering. 他在进入大楼前绕着大楼转了一圈。
The police had circled the area for hours. 警员们在该地区盘旋了好几个小时。


将来时:
We will circle back to this topic later. 我们稍后会回到这个话题。
The drone will circle the building. 无人机将绕着建筑物盘旋。

虚拟语气:
If I had circled the important points, I would have understood better. 如果我把重点圈起来，我就会理解得更好。
I wish I had circled the correct answer. 我真希望我把正确答案圈出来了。

被动语态:
The important words were circled by the teacher.  重要的单词被老师圈起来了。
The area was circled by police cars. 该地区被警车包围了。

疑问句:
Have you circled all the correct answers? 你把所有正确的答案都圈起来了吗？
Did he circle the wrong number? 他是不是圈错了数字？

否定句:
I didn't circle the correct answer. 我没有圈出正确的答案。
She didn't circle the entire paragraph. 她没有圈出整段话。
