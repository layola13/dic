octosyllable:/ˈɒktəsɪləbəl/| n. |八音节词|A word of eight syllables.  An octosyllable is a word that contains eight syllables. This is a relatively uncommon word length, as words tend to be shorter for ease of pronunciation and understanding.| Example:  The word "incomprehensibilities" is sometimes cited as an octosyllable, although syllable counts can vary depending on pronunciation.|近义词: eight-syllable word |反义词: monosyllable, disyllable
