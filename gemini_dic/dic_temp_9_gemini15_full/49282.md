deliberator:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪtər/| n. |深思熟虑者；审议者；协商者 |A person who thinks carefully before making a decision; someone involved in a formal discussion or consideration of something. |例句：The deliberator spoke thoughtfully before casting his vote. 深思熟虑者在投票前深思熟虑地发言了。  |例句：The jury deliberators were sequestered for several days. 陪审团成员被隔离数日进行审议。|近义词：considerer, ponderer, consultant, arbitrator |反义词：impulser, rash person


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The deliberator considers all aspects before deciding. = 深思熟虑者在决定之前会考虑所有方面。
She deliberates carefully before speaking. = 她说话前会仔细斟酌。


一般过去时:
