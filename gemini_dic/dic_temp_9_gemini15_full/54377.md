dishwasher:/ˈdɪʃˌwɒʃər/ /ˈdɪʃˌwɑːʃər/| n. |洗碗机|A machine that washes dishes automatically.  It uses hot water and detergent to clean dishes, usually placed inside a basket or rack.|例句：We bought a new dishwasher last week. = 我们上周买了一台新的洗碗机。

例句：Our dishwasher is broken, so we have to wash dishes by hand. = 我们的洗碗机坏了，所以我们必须手工洗碗。


近义词：automatic dish washer|反义词：hand washing (of dishes)

词源:
The word "dishwasher" is a compound word.  "Dish" refers to plates, cups, and other tableware.  "Washer" is a person or machine that washes something.  The combination describes a machine specifically designed for washing dishes.  The word's origin is straightforward, reflecting its function.
