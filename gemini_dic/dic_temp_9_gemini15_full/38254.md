comrade:/ˈkɒmreɪd/ /ˈkɑːmreɪd/ | n. |同志，战友 |A comrade is a person who shares one's activities, purpose, or beliefs, especially a fellow member of a political organization.  The term often implies a sense of shared struggle and commitment to a common cause.  Historically, it's strongly associated with socialist and communist movements. |  He fought alongside his comrades in the revolution. 他和他的同志们一起参加了革命。

The miners showed great solidarity with their comrades on strike. 矿工们对罢工的同志们表现出极大的团结。|  fellow, companion, colleague | enemy, opponent | noun
