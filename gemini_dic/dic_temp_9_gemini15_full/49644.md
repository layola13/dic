demilitarize:/ˌdiːˈmɪlɪtəraɪz/| vt.|非军事化；解除武装|To remove military forces or weapons from (a place).  It also implies removing the military character or function from something.|例句：The treaty called for the demilitarization of the border region.  该条约要求对边境地区进行非军事化。

例句：The country decided to demilitarize its islands to reduce tensions with its neighbors.  这个国家决定将其岛屿非军事化，以减少与邻国的紧张关系。


近义词：disarm, neutralize|反义词：militarize
