deindustrialization:/ˌdiːɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |去工业化；工业衰退 | The process of decline in industrial activity in a region or economy. This can be caused by various factors, including automation, globalization, and the shift towards service-based economies.  | Example: The deindustrialization of the Rust Belt led to significant job losses and economic hardship.  去工业化“锈带”导致了大量的失业和经济困难。 | 近义词：industrial decline, economic restructuring | 反义词：industrialization
