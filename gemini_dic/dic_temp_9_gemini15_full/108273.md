launderings:/ˈlɔːndərɪŋz/| n. |洗钱行为；清洗（尤指非法所得）|The act of laundering, especially money obtained illegally.  It involves disguising the origins of illegally obtained money by passing it through legitimate businesses or accounts. |例句：The authorities are investigating allegations of large-scale launderings. 当局正在调查大规模洗钱的指控。

例句：He was charged with multiple counts of launderings related to drug trafficking. 他被指控犯有多项与贩毒相关的洗钱罪。


近义词：money laundering, cleaning (of money), concealment, disguising


反义词：disclosure, revelation


词性：名词
