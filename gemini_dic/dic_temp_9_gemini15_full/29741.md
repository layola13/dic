catnip:/ˈkætˌnɪp/| n. |猫薄荷|A plant of the mint family, *Nepeta cataria*, whose leaves and stems contain an essential oil that has a stimulating effect on cats, causing them to roll, purr, and rub against it.  |例句：My cat went crazy when she smelled the catnip.  我的猫闻到猫薄荷就发疯了。


例句：The catnip toy was a huge success with my feline friend.  这个猫薄荷玩具深受我的猫朋友喜爱。
