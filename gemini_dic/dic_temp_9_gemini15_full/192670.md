scuttlebutt:/ˈskʌtəlˌbʌt/| n.|小道消息，谣言|Informal conversation or gossip, especially in a group of people.|The scuttlebutt is that the company is going bankrupt.  公司要破产的消息满天飞。|Rumor, gossip, hearsay |Truth, fact|名词
