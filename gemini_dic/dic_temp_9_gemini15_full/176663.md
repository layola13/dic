reachs:/riːtʃɪz/| v.|到达；伸手够到；达到；联系到|The word "reaches" is the third-person singular present tense form of the verb "reach." It indicates that someone or something is attaining a destination, extending to a point, achieving a goal, or making contact.  |例句：The river reaches the sea. 这条河通向大海。|例句：She reaches for the book on the shelf. 她伸手去够书架上的书。|近义词：arrive, attain, achieve, contact|反义词：miss, fail, depart


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reaches his destination every day at 5 pm. 他每天下午5点到达目的地。
The branch reaches out over the water. 树枝伸向水面。

一般过去时:
They reached the summit of the mountain yesterday. 他们昨天到达了山顶。
I reached out to him for help, but he didn't answer. 我向他寻求帮助，但他没有回应。

现在完成时:
She has reached a high level of proficiency in English. 她在英语方面已达到很高的熟练程度。
He has reached out to several companies for job opportunities. 他已经联系了几家公司寻求工作机会。

过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, they had already reached an agreement. 当我们到达时，他们已经达成协议了。
Before the deadline, she had reached her sales target. 在截止日期之前，她已经达到了销售目标。

将来时:
We will reach the airport in an hour. 我们一小时后将到达机场。
The project will reach completion by the end of the year. 该项目将在年底前完成。

虚拟语气:
If only I had reached him sooner! 要是我早点联系上他就好了！
I wish I had reached my goal last year. 我真希望去年能实现我的目标。

被动语态:
The agreement was reached after long negotiations. 经过长时间的谈判，协议达成了。
The decision was reached by a majority vote.  该决定以多数票通过。

疑问句:
Has she reached the conclusion yet? 她得出结论了吗？
Did they reach a consensus?  他们达成共识了吗？

否定句:
He didn't reach the finish line. 他没有到达终点线。
They haven't reached a decision yet. 他们还没有做出决定。
