bustier:/ˈbʌstiər/| n.|紧身胸衣；束胸衣|A close-fitting bodice, often boned, that provides support and shaping to the breasts and waist.  It's typically worn as an undergarment or as outerwear for fashion. |例句：She wore a black bustier under a sheer blouse. 她穿着一件黑色紧身胸衣在透明衬衫里面。

例句：The bustier was beautifully embellished with lace and beads. 这件紧身胸衣饰以精美的蕾丝和珠子。


近义词：corset, bodice, bra|反义词：loose top, flowing dress|词性:名词


Etymology:  The word "bustier" comes from the French word "buste," meaning "bust" or "torso."  It evolved to describe a garment that tightly fits the bust and upper body.
