gangplanks:/ˈɡæŋˌplæŋks/| n. |跳板；舷梯 |A gangplank is a movable bridge used to provide access between a ship and the shore or another vessel.  It is typically a narrow, temporary walkway made of planks of wood or metal. |例句：The sailors lowered the gangplank to allow the passengers ashore. 水手放下跳板让乘客上岸。

例句：We boarded the ship using a sturdy gangplank. 我们用结实的跳板登上了船。


近义词：gangway, walkway, bridge (in this context) |反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept would be a lack of access.


词性：名词
