portly:/ˈpɔːrtli/| adj.|肥胖的，丰满的；庄重的，体面的|Slightly fat; stout; dignified and imposing in manner or appearance.  The word often suggests a certain degree of dignity and respectability along with the physical bulk.|例句：He was a portly gentleman with a booming laugh. 他是一位身材丰满、笑声洪亮的绅士。
例句：The portly mayor addressed the crowd with confidence.  身材丰满的市长自信地向人群致辞。|近义词：stout, corpulent, plump, stout, substantial|反义词：thin, slender, slim, gaunt
