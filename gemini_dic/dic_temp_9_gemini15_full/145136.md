/ˌoʊvərɑːrˈtɪkjuleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 过分清晰地发音；过分强调发音 |  Speaking with excessive clarity and precision, often to the point of sounding unnatural or affected.  This can involve emphasizing individual sounds or syllables more than is typical in natural speech. |


He was overarticulating, trying to make himself understood over the loud music. = 他在过分清晰地发音，试图让自己在嘈杂的音乐声中被理解。


She overarticulated each word, making her speech sound stilted and unnatural. = 她每个单词都过分清晰地发音，使她的讲话听起来生硬而不自然。


近义词:  hyperarticulate, pronounce carefully, enunciate clearly


反义词: mumble, slur,  speak indistinctly


词性: 动词
