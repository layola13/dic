melters:/ˈmɛltərz/| n.|熔化者；融化物|Those who or that which melts something.  A melter is someone or something that causes melting, or the process of melting itself.|例句：The intense heat turned the snow into melters.  强烈的热量使雪变成了融化的水。(Here "melters" refers to the melted snow.)  The steel melters worked tirelessly in the foundry. 钢铁熔化工在铸造厂不知疲倦地工作着。(Here "melters" refers to the people.)|近义词：fusers, liquefiers|反义词：freezers, solidifiers|名词
