gratify:/ˈɡræṭɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 使满足；使高兴；使喜悦 | To give (someone) pleasure or satisfaction.  | 例句：The good news gratified her immensely.  好消息让她非常高兴。

例句：His success gratified his parents. 他的成功使他的父母感到欣慰。


近义词：please, satisfy, delight, fulfill | 反义词：disappoint, dissatisfy, frustrate | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gift gratifies her. = 这份礼物让她很高兴。
Good grades gratify her parents. = 好成绩让她的父母很高兴。

一般过去时:
The news gratified him. = 这个消息让他很高兴。
The concert gratified the audience. = 音乐会让观众很满意。

现在完成时:
The results have gratified the team. = 结果让团队很满意。
Her hard work has gratified her. = 她的努力让她感到满足。

过去完成时:
They had been gratified by the positive feedback. = 积极的反馈让他们感到很满意。
I had been gratified to see her progress. = 看到她的进步让我感到很欣慰。

将来时:
This promotion will gratify him greatly. = 这次升职会让他非常高兴。
The new policy will gratify many people. = 新政策会让很多人满意。

虚拟语气:
It would gratify me to help you. = 帮助你我会很高兴。
I wish the outcome would gratify everyone involved. = 我希望结果能令所有参与者满意。

被动语态:
He was gratified by her understanding. = 他很高兴她能理解。
The company was gratified by the increase in sales. = 公司对销售额的增长感到满意。

疑问句:
Will this decision gratify the shareholders? = 这个决定会让股东们满意吗？
Does this outcome gratify you? = 这个结果让你满意吗？

否定句:
The results did not gratify the researchers. = 结果并没有让研究人员满意。
The explanation didn't gratify her at all. = 这个解释一点也没让她满意。
