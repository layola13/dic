constitutional:/ˌkɒnstɪˈtjuːʃənəl/ /ˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəl/| adj., n. |宪法的；符合宪法的；本质的；保健的；体制的| relating to a constitution; formed or authorized by or in accordance with a constitution; of or relating to the physical constitution of a person or thing; a walk or other form of exercise taken for one's health|The constitutional right to free speech is fundamental.  宪法赋予的言论自由权是根本性的。

The judge ruled the law unconstitutional. 法官裁定该法律违宪。

近义词：fundamental, inherent, basic, legal

反义词：illegal, unconstitutional

一般现在时:
The law is constitutional. = 这法律是合宪的。
His behavior is generally constitutional. = 他的行为通常是符合规范的。

一般过去时:
The court declared the law unconstitutional. = 法院宣布该法律违宪。
The amendment made the law constitutional. = 该修正案使法律符合宪法。


现在完成时:
The court has ruled several laws unconstitutional this year. = 今年法院已裁定几项法律违宪。
They have ensured the process remains constitutional. = 他们确保这个过程仍然符合宪法。


过去完成时:
The judge had already declared the law unconstitutional before the appeal. = 在上诉之前，法官已经宣布该法律违宪。
They had long suspected the action was unconstitutional. = 他们早就怀疑这项行动违宪。


将来时:
The Supreme Court will decide whether the new law is constitutional. = 最高法院将决定新法律是否合宪。
We believe the new policy will prove constitutional. = 我们相信这项新政策将被证明是合宪的。


虚拟语气:
If the law were constitutional, it would be enforced. = 如果法律合宪，它就会被执行。
I wish the government would act in a more constitutional manner. = 我希望政府能以更符合宪法的方式行事。


被动语态:
The bill was declared unconstitutional by the Supreme Court. = 该法案被最高法院宣布违宪。
The matter was debated to ensure a constitutional outcome. =  为了确保结果符合宪法，此事经过了辩论。


疑问句:
Is this action constitutional? = 这个行动合宪吗？
Are these regulations constitutional? = 这些规章合宪吗？


否定句:
The law is not constitutional. = 这法律不合宪。
His actions were not constitutional. = 他的行为不合宪。

词源:

The word "constitutional" derives from the Latin word "constitutio," meaning "establishment," "composition," or "arrangement."  This root reflects the core meaning of the word relating to the fundamental structure or organization of something, particularly a government or state.  The suffix "-al" indicates an adjective form.
