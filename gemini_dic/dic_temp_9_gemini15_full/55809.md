divest:/daɪˈvɛst/ | vt. |剥夺；脱去；放弃；使…摆脱|To deprive someone of something, especially power, rights, or possessions; to rid oneself of something.|The company is divesting itself of its unprofitable assets.  公司正在剥离其无利可图的资产。|The government divested him of his title. 政府剥夺了他的头衔。|invest, retain | retain, keep |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He divests himself of his responsibilities. = 他卸下了自己的责任。
The company divests its assets regularly. = 这家公司定期剥离资产。


一般过去时:
She divested herself of her expensive jewelry. = 她脱下了她昂贵的珠宝。
The country divested itself from the colonial empire. = 这个国家摆脱了殖民帝国。


现在完成时:
They have divested their holdings in the failing company. = 他们已经剥离了他们在这家快要倒闭的公司中的股份。
The organization has divested itself of its controversial policies. = 该组织已经放弃了其有争议的政策。


过去完成时:
He had divested himself of all his worldly possessions before joining the monastery. = 在加入修道院之前，他已经放弃了他所有的世俗财产。
The government had divested the company of its license before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，政府已经吊销了该公司的执照。


将来时:
She will divest herself of her power to her successor. = 她将把权力交给她的继任者。
The corporation will divest its non-core businesses next year. = 该公司将在明年剥离其非核心业务。


虚拟语气:
If the company divested its failing division, it might have survived. = 如果公司剥离了其亏损部门，它或许就能生存下来。
I wish I had divested myself of that bad investment sooner. = 我真希望我早点摆脱那笔糟糕的投资。


被动语态:
He was divested of his authority by the board. = 他被董事会剥夺了权力。
The company's assets were divested to pay off debts. = 该公司的资产被剥离以偿还债务。


疑问句:
Will the government divest itself of its stake in the oil company? = 政府会剥离其在石油公司的股份吗？
Has the company divested any assets recently? = 公司最近剥离了任何资产吗？


否定句:
He did not divest himself of his responsibilities easily. = 他并没有轻易卸下他的责任。
The company refused to divest itself of its controversial holdings. = 该公司拒绝剥离其有争议的股份。


PHRASE->
divest oneself of = 摆脱；放弃
divest of power = 剥夺权力
divest of rights = 剥夺权利
divest from = 脱离；放弃投资
divest in = 在…投资(反义词用法，需注意语境)
