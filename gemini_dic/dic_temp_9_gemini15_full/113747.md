macromeric:/ˌmækrəˈmɛrɪk/| adj. |大分子(的)；巨分子的|Relating to or consisting of macromolecules.  Macromeric structures are typically large and complex, often composed of many repeating units. |例句：The macromeric structure of the protein was analyzed using X-ray crystallography. 蛋白质的巨分子结构是使用X射线晶体学分析的。|近义词：polymeric, high-molecular-weight |反义词：monomeric
