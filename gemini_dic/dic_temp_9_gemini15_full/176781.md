readings:/ˈriːdɪŋz/ | n. |阅读；读物；读数；课程；测量结果|Readings refer to the act of reading, the material being read, or the results obtained from measuring something.|例句：Her readings on the subject were extensive.  她的关于这个主题的阅读非常广泛。|例句：The meter's readings showed a significant increase in energy consumption. 仪表的读数显示能源消耗量大幅增加。|近义词：studies, recitations, measurements|反义词：writings (in the sense of producing rather than consuming text)
