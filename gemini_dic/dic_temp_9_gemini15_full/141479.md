opinionatist: /əˌpɪnjənˈɪst/ | n. | 固执己见者，武断的人 | A person who is stubbornly attached to their own opinions and unwilling to consider others'.  They often express their views forcefully and dogmatically, regardless of evidence or opposing arguments. |  He's such an opinionatist; he never listens to anyone else's perspective. 他是个固执己见的人；他从来不听取别人的意见。  She's become a real opinionatist since joining that online forum.自从加入那个在线论坛后，她变得非常固执己见。 | dogmatist, bigot,  opinionated person | open-minded person, tolerant person, flexible person | 名词
