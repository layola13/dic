here-and-now:/ˌhɪər ænd naʊ/| adj. |此时此刻，当下|  The term "here-and-now" refers to the present moment, the immediate surroundings, and the current situation. It emphasizes immediacy and the lack of focus on the past or future.  |例句：The therapist focused on the here-and-now, addressing the client's immediate concerns.  治疗师专注于当下，处理客户的当务之急。

The here-and-now approach is crucial in crisis management.  在危机管理中，当下的方法至关重要。|近义词：immediate, present, current, instant |反义词：past, future, distant, remote |形容词
