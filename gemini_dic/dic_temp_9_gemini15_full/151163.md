parboiling:/ˈpɑːrˌbɔɪlɪŋ/ | v. | 预煮；半煮 | To partially boil (food), typically as a preliminary step in cooking.  Parboiling helps to soften food, reduce cooking time, or remove undesirable substances. |  例句：The rice was parboiled before being added to the stir-fry.  大米在添加到炒菜之前先进行了预煮。

例句：She parboiled the potatoes to make them easier to mash. 她把土豆预煮一下，以便更容易捣碎。


近义词：blanching, scalding | 反义词：overboiling, fully cooking

词源:
