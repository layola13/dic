carry-the-ball:/ˈkæri ðə bɔːl/| phrasal verb| 承担责任；负责；带头；主导|To be the person who takes the lead or responsibility for a project or task, especially in a team or organization.  It originates from American football, where the "ball carrier" is the player with the ball and the primary focus of the play.|例句：He's carrying the ball on this project, so we should support him. 他在这个项目上承担主要责任，所以我们应该支持他。
He decided to carry the ball and take charge of the fundraising event. 他决定带头负责筹款活动。|近义词：take the lead, take charge, take responsibility, be in charge|反义词：delegate, abandon, neglect
