perturbator:/pərˈtɜːrbeɪtər/| n.|扰乱者；干扰物|A perturbator is something or someone that causes disturbance or disruption.|The constant noise from the construction site was a real perturbator to my work.|近义词：disruptor, disturber, agitator|反义词：stabilizer, pacifier


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine is a perturbator to the experiment. = 这台机器干扰了实验。
He is a perturbator of the peace. = 他是和平的扰乱者。


一般过去时:
The unexpected rain was a perturbator to our picnic plans. = 突如其来的雨扰乱了我们的野餐计划。
The scandal acted as a perturbator for the company's reputation. = 丑闻扰乱了公司的声誉。


现在完成时:
His actions have been a perturbator to the entire community. = 他的行为已经扰乱了整个社区。
The new policy has been a perturbator to the market stability. = 新政策扰乱了市场稳定。


过去完成时:
The faulty equipment had been a perturbator to the production line for weeks. = 故障的设备已经扰乱了生产线几周了。
The prior conflict had been a perturbator to the peace talks. = 先前的冲突扰乱了和平谈判。


将来时:
The upcoming election will be a perturbator to the political landscape. = 即将到来的选举将扰乱政治格局。
The new regulations will be a perturbator to the industry. = 新规将扰乱该行业。


虚拟语气:
If that noise were a perturbator, I would complain. = 如果那个噪音是干扰源，我会投诉。
I wish that the unexpected visitor weren't such a perturbator. = 我希望那个不速之客不要这么扰人。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
