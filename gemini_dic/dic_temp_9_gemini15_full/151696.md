partnering:/ˈpɑːrtnərɪŋ/| v.|合作，伙伴关系|The act of working together with someone or something; a collaborative relationship.|例句：We are partnering with a local charity to raise money. 我们正在与当地一家慈善机构合作筹款。|近义词：collaborating, cooperating, teaming up|反义词：competing, opposing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are partnering with a new supplier. = 我们正在与一个新的供应商合作。
They partner with many different organizations. = 他们与许多不同的组织合作。


一般过去时:
We partnered with them on the last project. = 我们在上一个项目中与他们合作。
The company partnered with a technology firm. = 这家公司与一家科技公司合作。


现在完成时:
We have partnered with this company for five years. = 我们与这家公司合作了五年。
They have partnered on several successful ventures. = 他们已经合作开展了几项成功的企业。


过去完成时:
We had partnered with them before, so we knew what to expect. = 我们以前就和他们合作过，所以知道会发生什么。
The company had partnered with several other firms before launching this product. = 在推出这款产品之前，该公司已经与其他几家公司合作过。


将来时:
We will be partnering with a new marketing agency next year. = 我们明年将与一家新的营销机构合作。
They will partner with us on the upcoming project. = 他们将在即将到来的项目中与我们合作。


虚拟语气:
If we partnered with them, we could reach a wider audience. = 如果我们与他们合作，我们可以接触到更广泛的受众。
I wish we had partnered with a more experienced team. = 我希望我们与一个更有经验的团队合作。


被动语态:
The project is being partnered by several different organizations. = 这个项目由几个不同的组织合作进行。
The new initiative was partnered by a major corporation. = 这项新举措得到了一个大型公司的支持（合作）。


疑问句:
Are you partnering with anyone on this project? = 你在这个项目中与任何人合作吗？
Who are they partnering with? = 他们与谁合作？


否定句:
We are not partnering with that company anymore. = 我们不再与那家公司合作了。
They did not partner with us on the previous project. = 他们没有在我们之前的项目中与我们合作。


PHRASE->
partnering agreement = 合作协议
strategic partnering = 战略合作
global partnering = 全球合作
cross-functional partnering = 跨职能合作
value-added partnering = 增值合作
