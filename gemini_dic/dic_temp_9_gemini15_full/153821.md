perceives:/pərˈsiːvz/| vt.|感知，察觉，理解|To become aware of something through the senses or the mind; to interpret or understand something in a particular way.|例句：He perceives the world differently after his accident. 他事故后对世界的感知不同了。|例句：She perceives a threat in his words. 她从他的话语中察觉到威胁。|近义词：sees, understands, recognizes, detects|反义词：misunderstands, ignores, overlooks|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He perceives the danger. = 他察觉到危险。
She perceives the subtle nuances in the music. = 她感知到音乐中微妙的细微之处。

一般过去时:
I perceived a change in his attitude. = 我察觉到他态度的变化。
They perceived the situation as hopeless. = 他们认为形势毫无希望。

现在完成时:
We have perceived a significant improvement. = 我们已经察觉到显著的改进。
She has perceived the truth behind the lies. = 她已经看穿了谎言背后的真相。

过去完成时:
He had perceived the pattern before anyone else. = 他比任何人都早察觉到这种模式。
They had perceived the risk, but ignored it. = 他们已经察觉到风险，但是忽略了。

将来时:
I will perceive the difference once I try both. = 一旦我尝试过两者，我就会察觉到区别。
They will perceive it as an act of aggression. = 他们会把它理解为侵略行为。

虚拟语气:
If I perceived the problem earlier, I would have acted sooner. = 如果我早些察觉到问题，我就会早点采取行动。
I wish I had perceived the danger. = 我真希望我当时察觉到了危险。

被动语态:
The situation was perceived as critical. = 局势被认为是危急的。
The painting is perceived differently by different people. = 这幅画被不同的人以不同的方式理解。

疑问句:
How do you perceive this situation? = 你如何看待这种情况？
What did they perceive as a threat? = 他们认为什么是威胁？

否定句:
He didn't perceive the irony. = 他没有察觉到讽刺意味。
She didn't perceive the seriousness of the situation. = 她没有意识到情况的严重性。


PHRASE->
