re-arguments:/ˌriˈɑːrɡjumənts/ | n. | 再辩论，再次论证 | Repeated arguments or discussions on a previously debated topic.  It often implies a continuation or renewal of a previous legal or academic debate. | Example: The court scheduled re-arguments to clarify certain points of law. 法院安排了再次辩论以澄清某些法律要点。|近义词：reconsideration, review, further arguments |反义词：conclusion, settlement
