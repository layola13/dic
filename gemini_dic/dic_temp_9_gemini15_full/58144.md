drosses:/ˈdrɒsɪz/| n. |废渣；劣质品；糟粕|The plural form of "dross," referring to worthless or impure substances, especially waste matter.  It can also refer to something of low quality or value.|例句：The drosses from the smelting process were carefully collected for recycling. 冶炼过程中的废渣被小心地收集起来回收利用。 The editor removed all the drosses from the manuscript, leaving only the essential parts. 编辑从稿件中删除了所有糟粕，只留下重要部分。|近义词：refuse, waste, rubbish, detritus, scum|反义词：treasure, gem, essence, purity
