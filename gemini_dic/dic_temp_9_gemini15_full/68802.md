fascial:/ˈfæʃəl/| adj. |筋膜的；筋膜组织的|Relating to fascia, a sheet or band of fibrous tissue that covers, supports, or separates muscles and organs.  Fascial tissues play a crucial role in body structure and function.|The physiotherapist focused on releasing tension in the patient's fascial layers.  理疗师专注于放松病人的筋膜层。|fibrous, connective|  (No direct antonym, but "unrestricted" might be used in a contrasting context)|形容词
