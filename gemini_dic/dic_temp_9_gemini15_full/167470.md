preventible: /prɪˈvɛntəbəl/ | adj. | 可预防的，可避免的 |  capable of being prevented; avoidable.  | Many accidents are preventable with proper safety measures. 许多事故只要采取适当的安全措施就能避免。  | avoidable, avertable | unavoidable, inevitable |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This disease is preventable. = 这种疾病是可以预防的。
Preventable measures are important. = 预防措施很重要。


一般过去时:
The accident was preventable. = 这起事故是可以避免的。
That mistake was easily preventable. = 那错误很容易避免。


现在完成时:
Many deaths have been preventable. = 许多死亡本来是可以避免的。
This problem has proven to be preventable. = 这问题已被证明是可以预防的。


过去完成时:
The damage could have been preventable. = 损害本来是可以避免的。
The tragedy was preventable, but it wasn't prevented. = 这场悲剧是可以预防的，但没有预防。


将来时:
