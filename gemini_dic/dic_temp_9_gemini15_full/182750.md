reparked:/ˌriːˈpɑːrkt/ | v. |重新停放 |To park (a vehicle) again, typically in a different location.  |例句：After realizing he'd parked too close to the hydrant, he reparked his car further down the street.  重新意识到他停得太靠近消防栓了，他把车停在了更远的地方。|例句：The delivery driver reparked the truck to avoid blocking the driveway. 送货司机重新停放卡车以避免挡住车道。|近义词：reposition, relocate |反义词：unpark
