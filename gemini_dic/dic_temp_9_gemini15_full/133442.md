It implies a relaxed attitude towards order and perfection. | Example:  The nonfastidiousness of his approach to cooking resulted in a rather haphazard but ultimately delicious meal.  他烹饪方法的不讲究导致了一顿相当随意但最终美味的饭菜。|近义词：unfussiness, carelessness, sloppiness |反义词：fastidiousness, meticulousness, precision
