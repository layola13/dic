scatterer:/ˈskætərər/| n. |散射体，散射器|An object or substance that scatters something, such as light, sound, or particles.|例句：The raindrops acted as scatterers of the sunlight, creating a rainbow.  雨滴充当了阳光的散射体，创造了一道彩虹。|例句：The rough surface of the wall served as a sound scatterer, preventing echoes. 墙壁粗糙的表面充当了声音散射器，防止了回声。|近义词：diffuser, disperser|反义词：concentrator, focuser|名词
