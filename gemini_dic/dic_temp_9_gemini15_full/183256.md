reprieved:/rɪˈpriːvd/| v.|缓刑；赦免；使免于；|to cancel or postpone the punishment of (someone, especially someone condemned to death); to save (someone or something) from a difficult or unpleasant situation.|例句：The prisoner was reprieved at the last minute.  犯人在最后一刻被赦免了。
例句：The company was reprieved from bankruptcy by a last-minute investment.  公司因最后一刻的投资而免于破产。|近义词：pardon, save, spare|反义词：execute, condemn, punish
