digitising: /ˈdɪdʒɪtaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |数字化；将…转换成数字形式 | The process of converting information into a digital format, typically from an analog format. This involves representing data using a system of discrete values, such as binary code (0s and 1s).  | Example:  The library is digitising its entire collection of books. 图书馆正在将其全部藏书数字化。

The museum is digitising its historical photographs. 博物馆正在将其历史照片数字化。


近义词：digitalizing, converting to digital, encoding


反义词：analogizing (converting to analog)

词源:
