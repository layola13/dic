preimprovement:/ˌpriːɪmˈpruːvmənt/| n. | 预先改进；事先改进 |  A preimprovement refers to improvements or enhancements made before a main process, project, or event. It's about proactively addressing potential issues or enhancing existing aspects to optimize the final outcome.  | Example:  The preimprovement of the software reduced bugs in the final release.  预先改进软件减少了最终版本的错误。 | 近义词：advancement, preparation, enhancement | 反义词：post-improvement, deterioration
