pelicans:/ˈpɛlɪkənz/ | n. | 鹈鹕 | A large waterbird with a long beak and a pouch under its beak for catching fish.  Pelicans are known for their cooperative fishing techniques. |  We saw a flock of pelicans diving for fish in the bay. 我们在海湾看到一群鹈鹕在潜水捕鱼。  A brown pelican soared overhead.一只棕鹈鹕在头顶翱翔。|  |  |Noun
