petunia: /pɪˈtjuːniə/ | n. |矮牵牛，碧冬茄 | A petunia is a flowering plant with trumpet-shaped flowers, often cultivated as an ornamental plant. They are known for their vibrant colors and ease of growth.  | Example:  The garden was filled with colorful petunias. 花园里盛开着五彩缤纷的矮牵牛。


The petunias were a riot of color.  矮牵牛花开得一片绚丽。|  近义词：flowering plant, annual | 反义词： (None directly opposite, but could be contrasted with plants lacking showy flowers)
