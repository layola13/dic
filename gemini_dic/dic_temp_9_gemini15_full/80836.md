gouging:/ˈɡɔːdʒɪŋ/ | n. v. |敲竹杠；漫天要价；挖出；凿出 |The act of cutting or digging a hole or channel in something;  The act of charging excessively high prices. |例句：The landlord was accused of gouging tenants with exorbitant rent increases.  房东被指控以过高的租金涨幅敲竹杠。  The surgeon carefully gouged out the infected tissue. 外科医生小心地挖出了感染的组织。|近义词：overcharging, extortion, fleecing, excavating |反义词：undercharging, fair pricing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gouges his customers. 他敲诈他的顾客。
The dentist is gouging the tooth. 牙医正在凿牙。

一般过去时:
They gouged a hole in the wall. 他们凿了一个洞在墙上。
The company gouged its customers last year.  这家公司去年敲诈了它的顾客。


现在完成时:
The company has gouged its customers for years. 这家公司多年来一直在敲诈它的顾客。
He has gouged out his own eye. 他已经挖出了自己的眼睛。


过去完成时:
They had gouged a deep hole before the rain started.  在下雨之前他们已经挖了一个很深的洞。
The company had gouged its customers before the scandal broke. 在丑闻爆发之前，该公司已经敲诈了它的顾客。


将来时:
They will gouge another hole in the road.  他们会在路上再挖一个洞。
The company will gouge its customers again next year.  这家公司明年还会敲诈它的顾客。


虚拟语气:
If they gouged the price, they would make more money. 如果他们抬高价格，他们就能赚更多钱。
I wish they hadn’t gouged me so much. 我真希望他们没有敲诈我那么多。


被动语态:
The customers were gouged by the company. 顾客被公司敲诈了。
The hole was gouged out by the machine.  这个洞是由机器挖出来的。


疑问句:
Are they gouging the prices? 他们是不是在抬高价格？
Was the hole gouged by hand or machine?  这个洞是手工挖的还是机器挖的？


否定句:
They didn't gouge the customers.  他们没有敲诈顾客。
The surgeon didn't gouge the wrong tissue. 外科医生没有挖错组织。
