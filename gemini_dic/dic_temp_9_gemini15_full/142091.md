orientalism:/ˌɔːriənˈtælɪzəm/| n. |东方学；东方主义；对东方的刻板印象|The study of the languages, literature, and cultures of the East.  It can also refer to a Western style of depicting the East, often with stereotypical and exoticized portrayals.|例句：His novel is criticized for its simplistic and stereotypical Orientalism. 他的小说因其简单化和刻板的东方主义而受到批评。

例句：The exhibition explores the history of Orientalism in Western art.  这次展览探讨了西方艺术中东方主义的历史。


近义词：Eastern studies, orientalism (adj), exoticization


反义词：Realism, accurate representation
