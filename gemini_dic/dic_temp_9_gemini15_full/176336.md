re-equipment:/ˌriːɪˈkwɪpmənt/| n. |重新装备；再装备 |The process of replacing or updating existing equipment with new or improved equipment.  It involves acquiring new tools, machinery, or technology to enhance productivity, efficiency, or capabilities. |例句：The factory underwent a major re-equipment program to modernize its production lines.  工厂进行了大规模的重新装备计划，以实现其生产线的现代化。|例句：The army's re-equipment with advanced weaponry significantly improved its combat readiness.  军队用先进武器重新装备大大提高了其战斗准备能力。|近义词：refitting, modernization, upgrade |反义词：disposal, decommissioning |名词
