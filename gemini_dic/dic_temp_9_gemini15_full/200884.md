slinking:/slɪŋkɪŋ/| v.|潜行；偷偷摸摸地走；鬼鬼祟祟地移动|Moving in a smooth, quiet, and stealthy way, often suggesting something secretive or shameful.  |例句：The cat slinked through the tall grass.  那只猫偷偷地穿过高高的草丛。
The thief slinked away into the shadows.  小偷偷偷摸摸地溜进了阴影里。|近义词：creeping, sneaking, gliding, sliding|反义词：striding, marching, swaggering

USAGE->

一般现在时:
He slinks around the house. 他在房子周围偷偷摸摸地走动。
The shadow slinks behind the building. 影子悄悄地潜伏在建筑物后面。


一般过去时:
She slinked out of the room. 她偷偷地溜出了房间。
The dog slinked away after being scolded. 狗被训斥后偷偷地溜走了。


现在完成时:
He has slinked away from his responsibilities. 他一直逃避责任。
The problem has slinked into the background. 问题已经慢慢淡化到背景中了。


过去完成时:
The thief had slinked away before the police arrived.  警察到达之前，小偷已经偷偷溜走了。
She had slinked into the shadows before anyone noticed.  还没人注意到她之前，她就已偷偷潜入阴影之中。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If he were to slink away, no one would notice. 如果他偷偷溜走，没有人会注意到。
I wish he wouldn't slink around like that. 我希望他不要那样鬼鬼祟祟地四处走动。


被动语态:
(The verb "slink" is rarely used in the passive voice because it implies an active, deliberate action.)


疑问句:
Is he slinking away from his problems? 他是在逃避他的问题吗？
Why is the cat slinking through the bushes? 猫为什么在灌木丛中偷偷摸摸地走动？


否定句:
He didn't slink away; he walked out boldly. 他没有偷偷溜走；他大胆地走了出去。
She wouldn't slink away; she confronted the issue directly. 她不会偷偷溜走；她直接面对了这个问题。
