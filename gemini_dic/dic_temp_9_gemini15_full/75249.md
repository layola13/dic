from rags to riches:/frɒm ˈræɡz tə ˈrɪtʃɪz/| adj. phrase |白手起家；从贫穷到富有 |This idiom describes someone who has risen from poverty to great wealth.  It emphasizes the dramatic change in someone's socioeconomic status. |Example:  He went from rags to riches after inventing a successful app. 他发明了一个成功的应用程序后，白手起家，变得富有。|近义词：self-made, from humble beginnings |反义词：from riches to rags
