polyglycol:/ˈpɑːliˌɡlaɪkɒl/ | n. | 聚乙二醇 | A type of polymer composed of repeating units of ethylene glycol.  Polyglycols are used in a wide variety of applications, including as solvents, lubricants, and in the production of pharmaceuticals and cosmetics. | Example: This lotion contains polyglycol as a moisturizer.  这款润肤露含有聚乙二醇作为保湿剂。|近义词：polyethylene glycol (PEG), polyalkylene glycol | 反义词：无
