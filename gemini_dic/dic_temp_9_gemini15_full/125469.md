multiplier:/ˈmʌltɪplaɪər/| n. |乘数，倍增器，倍数|A multiplier is a number or quantity that multiplies another.  It can refer to a factor that amplifies or increases something, often in a system or process.|The company's new marketing campaign acted as a multiplier, boosting sales significantly.|近义词：factor, magnifier, amplifier|反义词：divisor, reducer
