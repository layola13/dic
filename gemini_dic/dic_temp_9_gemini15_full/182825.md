repeatedly:/rɪˈpiːtɪdli/ | adv. |反复地，多次地 |  Repeatedly means on many occasions; again and again. It emphasizes the recurrence of an action or event. |例句：He repeatedly ignored my warnings. 他反复无视我的警告。 |例句：The experiment was repeatedly conducted to ensure accuracy.  实验被反复进行以确保准确性。 |近义词：again and again, repeatedly, over and over, time and time again |反义词：once, singly, rarely


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repeatedly checks his phone. = 他反复检查他的手机。
They repeatedly ask the same question. = 他们反复问同样的问题。

一般过去时:
She repeatedly apologized for her mistake. = 她反复为她的错误道歉。
We repeatedly tried different solutions. = 我们反复尝试不同的解决方案。

现在完成时:
They have repeatedly emphasized the importance of safety. = 他们反复强调安全的重要性。
He has repeatedly failed the exam. = 他反复考试不及格。

过去完成时:
She had repeatedly warned him about the danger. = 她曾反复警告他危险。
They had repeatedly requested a meeting. = 他们曾反复请求开会。

将来时:
He will repeatedly ask for help. = 他将反复寻求帮助。
They will repeatedly test the new product. = 他们将反复测试新产品。


虚拟语气:
If he repeatedly asked, she would eventually agree. = 如果他反复请求，她最终会同意。
I wish I hadn't repeatedly interrupted him. = 我真希望我没有反复打断他。

被动语态:
The message was repeatedly broadcast on the radio. = 这条消息被反复广播。
The experiment has been repeatedly conducted. = 实验已经被反复进行了。

疑问句:
Has he repeatedly called you? = 他反复给你打电话了吗？
Have they repeatedly ignored your requests? = 他们反复无视你的请求了吗？

否定句:
He did not repeatedly deny the allegations. = 他并没有反复否认这些指控。
She has not repeatedly contacted him. = 她并没有反复联系他。
