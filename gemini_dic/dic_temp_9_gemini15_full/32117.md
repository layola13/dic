cheetah:/ˈtʃiːtə/|/ˈtʃiːtɑː/| n. |猎豹|A cheetah is a large, slender, spotted feline that is the fastest land animal.  It is known for its speed and agility in hunting prey.|例句：The cheetah sprinted across the savanna in pursuit of its prey.  猎豹在草原上飞奔追逐猎物。|例句：We saw a cheetah lounging in the shade of an acacia tree. 我们看到一只猎豹懒洋洋地躺在金合欢树的树荫下。|近义词：leopard, jaguar|反义词: slow animal

词源:
The word "cheetah" comes from Hindi चीता (cītā), ultimately deriving from Sanskrit चित्रक (citrakā), meaning "spotted one".  The word entered English through Persian.
