governmentalizes:/ˌɡʌvərˈmɛntəlaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 使…政府化；使…受政府管辖 | To bring under the control or influence of a government; to make something subject to government regulation or administration.  | Example: The privatization of the railway system was a significant step towards de-governmentalizing the industry.  铁路系统的私有化是使该行业摆脱政府管辖的重要一步。  The increasing governmentalization of healthcare is a concern for many.  医疗保健日益政府化令许多人担忧。 | 近义词：nationalize, regulate, control | 反义词：privatize, deregulate, decentralize | 动词

词源:
The word "governmentalizes" is derived from "government" + "-alize," a suffix that means "to make or become."  Thus, it literally means "to make something like a government" or "to bring under governmental control."  The "-ize" suffix is of Greek origin.
