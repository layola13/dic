monogrammatic:/ˌmɒnəˈɡræmætɪk/| adj. | 单字母的；只有一个字母的 | Relating to or consisting of a monogram; characterized by the use of a monogram.  A monogrammatic design uses only one or a few interwoven initials. | 例句：The stationery featured a monogrammatic design, simply using the client's initials intertwined.  这家文具店特色是单字母的设计，只是将客户的姓名首字母交织在一起。


The artist's signature was highly monogrammatic, almost illegible. 艺术家的签名非常单一，几乎难以辨认。| 近义词：monogrammed, initialed | 反义词：polygraphic, multi-lettered
