happier:/ˈhæpiər/ | adj. | 更快乐的，更幸福的 |  Happier describes a greater degree of happiness or contentment than before.  It is the comparative form of the adjective "happy." | Example: I am happier now that I have a new job. = 我现在找到新工作后更快乐了。  She feels happier since she started exercising regularly. = 自从她开始定期锻炼以来，她感觉更快乐了。 | 近义词：more joyful, merrier, more cheerful | 反义词：sadder, unhappier, more miserable | 形容词
