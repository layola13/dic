positivity:/ˌpɒzɪˈtɪvɪti/ /ˌpɑːzəˈtɪvɪti/| n. |积极性；乐观；肯定 |The quality or state of being positive; optimism; a tendency to be cheerful and hopeful.  It involves maintaining a positive attitude and outlook, even in the face of difficulties. |例句：Her positivity is infectious; it brightens everyone's day. 她的积极性具有感染力；它照亮了每个人的一天。

例句：The team's positivity helped them overcome the challenges they faced.  团队的积极性帮助他们克服了面临的挑战。


近义词：optimism, cheerfulness, hopefulness, confidence

反义词：negativity, pessimism, despair, cynicism

词性：名词
