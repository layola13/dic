outsweetening: /ˌaʊtˈswiːtənɪŋ/ | v. | 超过…甜；甜度超过 |  The act of surpassing something else in sweetness.  It implies a competition or comparison of sweetness.  This word is less common and often used in a figurative sense. |

例句：

The baker's new recipe was outsweetening all the others in the competition. = 面包师的新配方在比赛中甜度超过了所有其他配方。

Her compliments were outsweetening any form of genuine praise. = 她的赞美胜过任何形式的真诚赞扬。


近义词：surpassing, exceeding, outdoing (in sweetness)

反义词：under-sweetening, less sweet than


词性：动词
