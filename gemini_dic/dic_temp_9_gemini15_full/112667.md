lowering:/ˈloʊərɪŋ/ | v. adj. n. |降低；使沮丧；阴沉的 | To move something down to a lower position or level; to make something less in amount, degree, or intensity;  a lowering of something; a gloomy or depressing atmosphere |例句：He was lowering the flag. 他正在降旗。 The lowering of taxes boosted the economy. 减税促进了经济发展。 The lowering sky threatened rain.阴沉的天空预示着下雨。|近义词：decreasing, depressing, reducing, lowering, dulling|反义词：raising, elevating, lifting, brightening, cheering|动词，形容词，名词


USAGE->
