dullify:/ˈdʌləfaɪ/| vt.|使迟钝；使减弱；使变淡|To make something less effective, intense, or interesting.|The rain dullified the vibrant colours of the painting. 雨水冲淡了绘画中鲜艳的色彩。|近义词：weaken, diminish, deaden|反义词：intensify, sharpen, enhance|动词


USAGE->

The constant noise dullifies my concentration. = 持续的噪音使我无法集中注意力。
This medicine dullifies the pain. = 这药减轻了疼痛。
