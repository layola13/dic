pesterers:/ˈpɛstərərz/| n. |烦扰者，纠缠者 |Those who repeatedly annoy or bother someone.  People who persistently pester are often unwelcome.  |例句：The constant pesterers outside the shop were driving customers away.  = 商店外那些不停纠缠的人把顾客都赶走了。

例句：Ignoring the pesterers is sometimes the best way to deal with them. = 忽视那些纠缠者有时是处理他们的最好办法。


近义词：annoyers, bothers, harassers, tormentors, nuisances

反义词：helpers, comforters, soothers


词性：名词
