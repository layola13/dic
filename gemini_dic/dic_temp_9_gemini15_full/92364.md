hypochondriac:/ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndrɪæk/| n. |臆想症患者；疑病症患者|A person who is abnormally anxious about their health and who interprets minor bodily symptoms as signs of serious illness.  They often believe they have, or are about to develop, a serious medical condition despite reassurances from medical professionals.|例句：He's a hypochondriac; every little ache or pain sends him running to the doctor.|近义词：worrier,  patient with illness anxiety disorder|反义词：healthy, robust
