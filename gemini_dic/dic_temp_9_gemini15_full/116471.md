materialism:/məˈtɪəriəlɪzəm/ | n. |唯物主义；物质至上主义 |The philosophical theory that matter is the only reality.  Materialism emphasizes the importance of physical things over spiritual or intellectual concerns. | Example:  His materialism was evident in his obsession with accumulating wealth. 他对积累财富的痴迷表明了他是一个唯物主义者。|近义词：physicalism, pragmatism |反义词：idealism, spiritualism
