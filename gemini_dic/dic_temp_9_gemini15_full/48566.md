default:/dɪˈfɔːlt/| adj. n. v.|默认的；违约；默认为；|adj.  Being the option that is selected automatically unless an alternative is specified.|n. The failure to fulfill an obligation, especially a financial one. |v. To fail to fulfill an obligation.|例句：The default setting is English.  默认设置是英语。|近义词：standard, preselected, omission|反义词：explicit, customized, fulfillment

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system defaults to the last used setting. = 系统默认为上次使用的设置。
What does the printer default to when it is turned on? = 打印机启动时默认设置为什麽？


一般过去时:
The company defaulted on its loan payments. = 公司未能偿还贷款。
He defaulted on his taxes last year. = 他去年没有按时缴税。


现在完成时:
They have defaulted on their mortgage payments for three months. = 他们已经拖欠抵押贷款三个月了。
Has the software defaulted to the correct settings? = 软件是否已默认为正确的设置？


过去完成时:
The bank had already defaulted on its promise to provide funding. = 银行已经违背了提供资金的承诺。
Before the crash, many investors had defaulted on their margin calls. = 在崩盘之前，许多投资者没有支付保证金追加保证金的要求。


将来时:
If they don't pay soon, they will default on their credit card payments. = 如果他们不尽快付款，他们将无力偿还信用卡债务。
The company is expected to default on its bonds next year.  = 预计该公司明年将无力偿还债券。


虚拟语气:
If I had known the consequences, I wouldn't have defaulted on the loan. = 如果我知道后果，我就不会拖欠贷款了。
If he had not defaulted on his payment, he wouldn't have been sued. = 如果他没有拖欠付款，他就不会被起诉。


被动语态:
The account was defaulted on due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，账户出现了违约。
The loan agreement was defaulted upon by the borrower. = 借款人违反了贷款协议。


疑问句:
Does this software default to metric units? = 这个软件默认使用公制单位吗？
Will the program default to the existing file? = 程序会默认使用现有文件吗？


否定句:
The system did not default to the previous settings. = 系统没有默认为之前的设置。
The customer did not default on their payments. = 客户没有拖欠付款。
