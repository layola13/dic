fables:/ˈfeɪbəlz/| n. |寓言；虚构故事 |A fable is a short story, typically with animals as characters, conveying a moral.  Fables often use anthropomorphism, giving human qualities to animals or inanimate objects.  |Example: Aesop's fables are well-known for their moral lessons.  伊索寓言以其道德教训而闻名。

|Example:  The fable of the tortoise and the hare teaches us the importance of perseverance. 龟兔赛跑的寓言故事告诉我们坚持的重要性。|近义词：allegory, parable, moral story |反义词：truth, reality |名词
