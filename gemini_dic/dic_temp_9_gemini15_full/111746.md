locusts:/ˈloʊkəsts/| n.|蝗虫|A locust is a type of grasshopper that travels in large swarms, often causing significant damage to crops.  Locusts are known for their ability to reproduce rapidly and consume vast amounts of vegetation.|Example: The locusts descended upon the fields, devouring everything in their path.  蝗虫飞落田间，吞噬着沿途的一切。  The farmer watched in despair as the locust swarm destroyed his entire harvest. 农民绝望地看着蝗虫群摧毁了他的全部收成。|近义词：grasshopper, insect, pest|反义词：N/A
