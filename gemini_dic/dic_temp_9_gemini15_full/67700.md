exultancy:/ɪɡˈzʌltənsi/| n. |狂喜；得意；欢欣鼓舞|A feeling of triumphant joy; elation.  It describes a feeling of great happiness and triumph, often resulting from a victory or achievement.|例句：Her exultancy was evident in her radiant smile. 她的喜悦之情从她灿烂的笑容中显而易见。

例句：The team's exultancy after winning the championship was infectious.  球队赢得冠军后的喜悦感染了所有人。


近义词：elation, jubilation, triumph, rejoicing, glee

反义词：despair, dejection, misery, sadness, despondency

词性:名词
