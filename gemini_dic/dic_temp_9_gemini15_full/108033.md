lasts:/læsts/ | v. |持续，维持|To continue to exist or happen for a particular period of time. |The meeting lasted for three hours.  会议持续了三个小时。|The pain lasted all night. 疼痛持续了一整夜。|continues, endures |ends, finishes|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This meeting usually lasts two hours. = 这个会议通常持续两个小时。
The game lasts for 90 minutes. = 这场比赛持续90分钟。

一般过去时:
The concert lasted three hours. = 音乐会持续了三个小时。
The film lasted longer than I expected. = 电影比我预期的要长。

现在完成时:
The drought has lasted for months. = 干旱已经持续了好几个月了。
The project has lasted longer than planned. = 项目持续的时间比计划的要长。

过去完成时:
The storm had lasted all night before it finally stopped. = 暴风雨持续了一整夜才最终停止。
The war had lasted for decades before peace was finally achieved. = 战争持续了几十年才最终实现和平。

将来时:
The festival will last for a week. = 节日将持续一周。
I think the rain will last until tomorrow. = 我认为雨会持续到明天。

虚拟语气:
If the meeting lasted any longer, I would have missed my train. = 如果会议持续更长时间，我就赶不上火车了。
I wish this cold weather would last forever. = 我希望这寒冷的天气能永远持续下去。

被动语态:
The meeting was expected to last two hours but it lasted three. = 会议预计持续两小时，但实际持续了三小时。
The repairs are expected to last about a week. = 维修预计大约需要一周时间。

疑问句:
How long does the battery last? = 电池能用多久？
How long will the effects of the medicine last? = 药物的效果能持续多久？

否定句:
The battery didn't last very long. = 电池没用多久。
The party didn't last as long as we hoped. = 聚会没有持续我们希望那么久。


PHRASE->
last but not least = 最后但同样重要的是
last chance = 最后机会
