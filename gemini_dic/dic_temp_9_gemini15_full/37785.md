compear:/kəmˈpɛər/| v.|比较，对照|To compare means to examine two or more things to discover their similarities and differences.  It involves identifying points of likeness and contrast.|例句：Let's compear the two proposals before making a decision.让我们在做决定之前比较这两个提案。|例句：The teacher compared the students' essays to assess their writing skills.老师比较了学生的文章以评估他们的写作能力。|近义词：contrast, liken, match|反义词：differentiate (in the sense of highlighting differences), separate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He compares apples and oranges. = 他比较苹果和橘子。
She compares prices before buying anything. = 她在买任何东西之前都会比较价格。

一般过去时:
I compared my work to hers. = 我把我的作品和她的作品作比较。
They compared notes after the exam. = 考试后他们互相交换意见。


现在完成时:
She has compared several different models. = 她已经比较了几种不同的型号。
We have compared our results with previous studies. = 我们已经将我们的结果与之前的研究进行了比较。


过去完成时:
He had compared the two options before making a choice. = 他在做出选择之前已经比较了这两个选项。
They had compared their notes before the presentation. = 他们在演示之前已经比较了他们的笔记。


将来时:
I will compare the features of both products tomorrow. = 我明天将比较这两个产品的特性。
We will compare the data once we receive it. = 我们收到数据后会进行比较。


虚拟语气:
If I were to compare them, I'd say this one is better. = 如果我要比较它们，我会说这个更好。
I wish I had compared the prices earlier. = 我希望我早点比较一下价格。


被动语态:
The two paintings were compared by the art critic. = 这两幅画被艺术评论家进行了比较。
The data is being compared using a statistical software. = 数据正在使用统计软件进行比较。


疑问句:
How do you compare these two approaches? = 你如何比较这两种方法？
Can you compare the benefits of both options? = 你能比较这两个选项的好处吗？


否定句:
He didn't compare the different options. = 他没有比较不同的选择。
She doesn't compare her work to anyone else's. = 她不把自己的作品与其他人的作品进行比较。


PHRASE->
compare and contrast = 比较和对比
compare notes = 交换意见
compare to = 比作
compare with = 与...比较
in comparison with = 与...相比
