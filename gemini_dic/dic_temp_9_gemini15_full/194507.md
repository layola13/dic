self-safety:/ˌsɛlfˈseɪfti/| n.|自我安全，自身安全 |The protection of oneself from harm or danger.  It encompasses physical, emotional, and psychological well-being. |例句：Self-safety is paramount when traveling alone at night.  独自一人夜间旅行时，自身安全至关重要。|例句：The company emphasized self-safety training for all employees. 公司强调所有员工的自我安全培训。|近义词：personal safety, self-preservation |反义词：self-neglect, recklessness|名词
