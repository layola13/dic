illegally:/ɪˈliːɡəli/| adj. |非法地，违法地 | acting in a way that is against the law.  |例句：He illegally downloaded the software. 他非法下载了软件。|例句：They were illegally occupying the land. 他们非法占用土地。|近义词：unlawfully, illicitly, improperly |反义词：legally, lawfully


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They illegally park their car every day. = 他们每天都非法停车。
He illegally copies music files. = 他非法复制音乐文件。


一般过去时:
She illegally entered the building. = 她非法进入了大楼。
They illegally obtained the documents. = 他们非法获得了这些文件。


现在完成时:
He has illegally downloaded many movies. = 他已经非法下载了很多电影。
She has illegally accessed confidential information. = 她已经非法访问了机密信息。


过去完成时:
They had illegally disposed of the waste before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经非法处理了废物。
He had illegally obtained a passport before his trip. = 他在旅行前已经非法获得了护照。


将来时:
They will illegally dump the toxic waste. = 他们将会非法倾倒有毒废物。
He will illegally sell the drugs. = 他将会非法贩卖毒品。


虚拟语气:
If he illegally obtained the information, he would be punished. = 如果他非法获取了信息，他将会受到惩罚。
If they illegally used the software, they might face legal action. = 如果他们非法使用了该软件，他们可能会面临法律诉讼。


被动语态:
The drugs were illegally imported into the country. = 这些毒品被非法进口到这个国家。
The land was illegally occupied by the developers. = 这块土地被开发商非法占用。


疑问句:
Did they illegally obtain the funds? = 他们是否非法获得了资金？
Were the goods illegally manufactured? = 这些商品是否是非法制造的？


否定句:
They did not illegally download the music. = 他们没有非法下载音乐。
He did not illegally access the data. = 他没有非法访问数据。
