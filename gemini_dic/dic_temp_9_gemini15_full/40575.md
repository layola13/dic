contusioned: /kənˈtuʒənd/ | adj. | 受挫伤的；瘀伤的 | bruised; suffering from a contusion.  | The athlete's contusioned leg prevented him from competing.  这位运动员腿部受伤，无法参加比赛。 | bruised, battered, injured | healthy, uninjured


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The athlete's leg is contusioned. = 运动员的腿受伤了。
His skin is contusioned after the fall. = 他摔倒后皮肤淤青了。

一般过去时:
His arm was contusioned in the accident. = 他的手臂在事故中受伤了。
Her knee was contusioned during the game. = 她的膝盖在比赛中受伤了。

现在完成时:
His face has been contusioned since the fight. = 自从打架以来，他的脸就一直淤青。
She has been contusioned several times this year. = 她今年已经受了几次伤。

过去完成时:
He had been contusioned before the match even started. = 比赛开始前他就已经受伤了。
She had been contusioned multiple times in the past. = 她过去受过多次伤。

将来时:
His nose will likely be contusioned after the punch. = 他挨了一拳后，鼻子很可能会受伤。
The boxer's face will be contusioned after the fight. = 拳击手比赛后脸会受伤。

虚拟语气:
If he had been more careful, he wouldn't have been contusioned. = 如果他更小心一点，就不会受伤了。
I wish I hadn't been contusioned during the hike. = 我真希望在远足中没有受伤。

被动语态:
His shoulder was contusioned during the tackle. = 他的肩膀在铲球时受伤了。
His body was contusioned in the car crash. = 他的身体在车祸中受伤了。

疑问句:
Is his eye contusioned? = 他的眼睛受伤了吗？
Was his ankle contusioned badly? = 他的脚踝伤得很严重吗？

否定句:
His leg wasn't contusioned; it was only scraped. = 他的腿没有受伤，只是擦伤了。
Her arm wasn't contusioned, despite the fall. = 尽管摔倒了，她的手臂却没有受伤。


PHRASE->
severely contusioned:  严重挫伤
badly contusioned:  严重挫伤
slightly contusioned: 轻微挫伤
multiple contusions: 多处挫伤
contusioned areas: 挫伤部位

词源:
From Middle French *contusion, from Latin contusio, from contundere "to bruise, batter," from con- "together" + tundere "to beat, strike."
