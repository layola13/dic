dissemblance:/dɪˈsɛmbləns/| n. |不相似；伪装；掩饰 |The lack of resemblance or similarity; a disguise or concealment.  It often implies a deliberate attempt to hide the truth or one's true feelings. |例句：His dissemblance of grief was unconvincing.  他的悲伤伪装令人难以置信。

例句：There was a striking dissemblance between the two paintings, despite their similar subject matter. 尽管两幅画的主题相似，但它们之间却有着显著的不同。


近义词：dissimulation, deception, disguise, concealment, mask

反义词：similarity, resemblance, truthfulness, honesty

词性：名词
