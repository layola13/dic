pavior:/ˈpeɪvjər/| n.|铺路者，铺路工|A person who lays paving stones or other materials to create a paved surface.  |例句：The pavior skillfully laid the bricks to create a beautiful patio. 铺路工熟练地铺设砖块，创造了一个美丽的庭院。|例句：The team of paviors worked tirelessly to complete the road repairs. 铺路工团队辛勤工作以完成道路维修。|近义词：paver, road worker|反义词：无
