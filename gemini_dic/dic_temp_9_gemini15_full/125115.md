multi-access: /ˌmʌltiˈækses/ | n. |多址访问，多路访问 |  The ability of multiple users or devices to access a shared resource, such as a network or communication channel, simultaneously or in a time-shared manner.  It involves techniques and protocols to manage and coordinate access to prevent conflicts and ensure efficient resource utilization. | Example:  Multi-access protocols are crucial for efficient network operation. 多址访问协议对于高效的网络运行至关重要。  The new system utilizes a multi-access database. 新系统使用多址访问数据库。| 近义词: shared access, multiple access | 反义词: single access, exclusive access | 名词
