iconographies:/ˌaɪkənˈɒɡrəfiz/ | n. |图像学；图像研究；图像符号学 |The study of images and their symbolic meaning within a particular culture or historical period. It involves analyzing the visual elements, their arrangement, and their cultural context to understand the message conveyed.  |Example:  The iconographies of ancient Egyptian art reveal much about their religious beliefs and social structures.  埃及古艺术的图像学揭示了他们许多宗教信仰和社会结构。


Example: The book explores the rich iconographies of Renaissance painting, focusing on the symbolism of specific objects and figures. 这本书探讨了文艺复兴绘画丰富的图像学，重点是特定物体和人物的象征意义。
