meta-ethical:/ˌmɛtəˈɛθɪkəl/| adj. |元伦理学的；超伦理学的| Relating to the branch of ethics that explores the fundamental nature of moral judgments.  It examines the meaning of moral terms, the justification of moral principles, and the metaphysics of morality.  It does not deal with specific moral issues, but with the underlying framework of morality itself. | Example:  Meta-ethical discussions often focus on whether moral truths are objective or subjective. 元伦理学讨论通常集中在道德真理是客观还是主观的问题上。|近义词：moral philosophical,  normative|反义词：applied ethics, descriptive ethics
