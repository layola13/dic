rethatch:/ˌriːˈtætʃ/| vt.|重新盖草屋顶；重新铺盖|To cover (a roof) again with thatch.  This involves replacing old, worn-out thatch with new material.|例句：The old cottage needs rethatching. 这间老小屋需要重新盖草屋顶。

例句：The farmer decided to rethatch the barn roof after the storm.  暴风雨过后，农民决定重新盖谷仓的屋顶。


近义词：re-roof, thatch anew|反义词：unroof, remove thatch|词性:动词
