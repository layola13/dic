employee:/ɪmˈplɔɪiː/| n. |雇员，职员 | A person employed for wages or salary, especially at non-managerial or non-professional level.  | Example:  The company has over 500 employees. 这家公司有超过500名雇员。 |近义词：worker, staff member, associate |反义词：employer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The employee works hard every day. = 这位员工每天努力工作。
Our employees complete their tasks efficiently. = 我们的员工高效地完成他们的任务。


一般过去时:
The employee finished the project on time. = 这位员工按时完成了项目。
Many employees left the company last year. = 去年许多员工离开了公司。


现在完成时:
The employee has completed several important projects. = 这位员工已经完成了几个重要的项目。
The company has hired many new employees this year. = 公司今年已经招聘了许多新员工。


过去完成时:
The employee had already finished his work before the meeting. = 会议之前，这位员工已经完成了他的工作。
The company had employed him for five years before he resigned. = 他辞职前，公司已经雇佣了他五年。


将来时:
The employee will start his new job next week. = 这位员工下周将开始他的新工作。
The company will hire more employees next quarter. = 公司下个季度将招聘更多员工。


虚拟语气:
If the employee were more careful, he wouldn't make so many mistakes. = 如果这位员工更细心一些，他就不会犯这么多错误。
I wish the employee had informed me earlier. = 我希望这位员工早点通知我。


被动语态:
The employee was promoted last month. = 这位员工上个月被提升了。
Many employees are being trained for new skills. = 许多员工正在接受新技能培训。


疑问句:
Is the employee satisfied with his job? = 这位员工对他的工作满意吗？
How many employees does the company have? = 公司有多少员工？


否定句:
The employee didn't come to work today. = 这位员工今天没有来上班。
The company didn't hire any new employees this month. = 公司这个月没有招聘任何新员工。


PHRASE->
key employee: 核心员工

temporary employee: 临时员工
former employee: 前雇员


词源:
The word "employee" comes from the verb "employ," which ultimately derives from the Old French word "emploier," meaning "to use," "to employ," or "to occupy."  The "-ee" suffix indicates the recipient of the action, signifying the person who is employed.
