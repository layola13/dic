dislocation:/ˌdɪsˌloʊˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |脱位；错位；移位；混乱 |The displacement of a bone from its joint.  A dislocation can cause pain, swelling, and limited movement.  It often requires medical intervention to reset the bone. |The shoulder dislocation required surgery to repair.  肩膀脱位需要手术修复。
The earthquake caused a significant dislocation of the land. 地震导致了严重的土地移位。|subluxation, displacement |alignment, fixation |名词
