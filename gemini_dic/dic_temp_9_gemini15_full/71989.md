flinder:/ˈflɪndər/| n.|碎片；小片；（尤指）木片|A small, thin piece of something, especially wood, that has broken off.  |例句：The wind scattered flinders of wood across the yard. 风把木片吹散在院子里。  |例句：The shattered vase lay in flinders on the floor.  破碎的花瓶碎片散落在地上。|近义词：fragment, splinter, shard |反义词：whole, intact
