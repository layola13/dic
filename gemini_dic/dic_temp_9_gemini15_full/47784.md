decanting:/dɪˈkæntɪŋ/| v.|倾倒，倒酒|The careful pouring of a liquid from one container to another, especially without disturbing the sediment.|例句：We decanted the wine to remove the sediment. 我们把酒倒出来去除沉淀物。例句：The chemist carefully decanted the solution into a clean beaker. 化学家小心地将溶液倒入一个干净的烧杯中。|近义词：pouring, transferring|反义词：None specifically.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They decant the wine before serving. = 他们上菜前会先倒酒。
We decant the old oil into a new container. = 我们把旧油倒进新的容器里。


一般过去时:
He decanted the red wine into a decanter. = 他把红葡萄酒倒进一个酒瓶里。
She decanted the cloudy liquid, leaving the sediment behind. = 她把浑浊的液体倒掉，留下沉淀物。


现在完成时:
I have decanted the wine several times this week. = 本周我已经倒了几次酒了。
They have decanted all the chemicals for the experiment. = 他们已经为实验倒出了所有的化学药品。


过去完成时:
The sommelier had decanted the bottle before the guests arrived. = 服务员在客人到达之前已经把酒倒好了。
She had decanted the mixture carefully to avoid spillage. = 她小心地倾倒混合物以避免溢出。


将来时:
We will decant the champagne before the celebration. = 我们将在庆祝活动前倒香槟酒。
The bartender will decant the whiskey to aerate it. = 酒保将倒出威士忌酒来使其氧化。


虚拟语气:
If I had a decanter, I would decant the wine. = 如果我有一个酒瓶，我会把酒倒出来。
I wish I had decanted the wine earlier; it would taste better now. = 我真希望早点把酒倒出来；现在喝起来会更好。


被动语态:
The wine was decanted before serving. = 酒在上菜前已倒好。
The solution is being decanted into a separate container. = 溶液正在被倒入一个单独的容器中。


疑问句:
Have you decanted the wine yet? = 你把酒倒好了吗？
Should we decant this bottle of wine? = 我们应该把这瓶酒倒出来吗？


否定句:
They didn't decant the wine, so it was cloudy. = 他们没有把酒倒出来，所以酒很浑浊。
I haven't decanted the oil yet. = 我还没把油倒出来。


PHRASE->
decant wine = 倒酒
decant a solution = 倒溶液
carefully decant = 小心地倾倒
decant into a container = 倒入容器中
decant the sediment = 除去沉淀物
