old-age: /ˈoʊld eɪdʒ/ | n. |老年；高龄| The period of life after middle age, typically considered to begin at 65.  It is characterized by physical and mental decline. |例句：She enjoys her old age, surrounded by her grandchildren. 她很享受老年生活，孩子们都围在她身边。|例句：Many people worry about the financial burden of old age. 许多人担心老年生活的经济负担。|近义词：senescence, senility |反义词：youth, adolescence
