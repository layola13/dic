cutups:/ˈkʌtʌps/| n.|拼贴画；古怪的人；捣蛋鬼|Pieces of text or images cut up and rearranged to create a new work of art or literature; also refers to someone unconventional or mischievous.|例句：He made some interesting cutups from old magazines. 他用旧杂志做了一些有趣的拼贴画。|例句：The band's music is a chaotic cutup of different genres.  乐队的音乐是不同类型音乐的混乱拼贴。|近义词：collage, montage, prankster, troublemaker|反义词：conformist, well-behaved
