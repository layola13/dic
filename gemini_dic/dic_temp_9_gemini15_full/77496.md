gazette:/ɡəˈzɛt/ | n. |官方公报；政府公报；期刊；杂志 |An official public notice or announcement, especially one published regularly.  A newspaper or journal. |The government gazette announced the new tax laws. 政府公报宣布了新的税法。 The local gazette published an article about the town's history. 当地报纸发表了一篇关于小镇历史的文章。 |newspaper, journal, periodical |(近义词无明确的反义词，可根据语境选择如：rumor, unofficial announcement) |名词

词源:

The word "gazette" comes from the Italian word "gazzetta," a small coin.  In the 16th century, Venice had a handwritten news sheet that cost a gazzetta, and the name stuck to the publication itself, eventually spreading to other languages.
