preformats:/ˈpriːfɔːrmæts/| n.|预格式化，预先格式化|Preformats refers to data or files that have been formatted in advance, usually to a specific standard or for a particular application.  This often involves setting specific parameters like font, spacing, margins, etc., before the data is used or processed.|Example: The preformats for the report were created using a specialized software.  报告的预格式化是使用专门的软件创建的。|近义词：preformatted, formatted, prepared|反义词：unformatted, raw
