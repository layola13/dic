hippies:/ˈhɪpiz/| n.|嬉皮士|A person, especially in the 1960s and 1970s, who rejected conventional values and lifestyles, often living in a commune and advocating peace and love.|例句：The hippies of the 1960s advocated peace and love.  六十年代的嬉皮士提倡和平与爱。|近义词：counterculture, peacenik|反义词：establishment, conformist
