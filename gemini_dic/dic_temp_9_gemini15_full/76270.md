fuzzy: /ˈfʌzi/ | adj. | 模糊的，不清楚的；绒毛的，柔软的 |

英文解析:  Not clear, distinct, or precise; vague. Also, covered with or resembling fuzz; soft and fluffy.

例句:

The photograph was fuzzy and difficult to see clearly.  照片模糊不清，难以看清楚。

The peach felt fuzzy to the touch.  桃子摸起来毛茸茸的。


近义词: unclear, vague, indistinct, blurry, hazy, downy, fluffy

反义词: clear, sharp, distinct, precise, crisp


词性: adjective
