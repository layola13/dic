nervio:/ˈnɜːrvi.oʊ/|/ˈnɛrvi.oʊ/| n. |神经；胆量；紧张；|A nerve is a whitish fiber or bundle of fibers that transmits impulses of sensation, motion, etc., between the brain or spinal cord and other parts of the body.  It can also refer to courage or boldness, or a feeling of nervousness or anxiety.|例句：The doctor checked the patient's nerves after the accident.医生在事故后检查了病人的神经。|例句：She showed great nerve in facing the difficult situation. 她面对困境表现出极大的勇气。|近义词：nerve, fiber, courage, bravery|反义词：calmness, composure, cowardice
