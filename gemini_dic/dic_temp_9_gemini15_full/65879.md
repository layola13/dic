evolutionism:/ˌɛvəˈluːʃənɪzəm/ | n. |进化论|The theory that species originate from common ancestors and have changed over time through natural selection and other mechanisms.  It encompasses various perspectives on the processes and mechanisms driving evolutionary change. |The modern synthesis of evolutionism combines Darwinian natural selection with genetics. 现代进化论综合了达尔文的自然选择理论和遗传学。|Darwinism,  natural selection|creationism,  intelligent design|名词
