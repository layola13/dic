overstriving: /ˌoʊvərˈstraɪvɪŋ/ | v. | 过度努力；力求完美 |  The act of trying too hard; excessive striving for perfection or success.  It often implies a negative connotation, suggesting that the effort is counterproductive or harmful. | 例句：His overstriving for perfection led to burnout.  他的过度追求完美导致了倦怠。

His overstriving to please everyone left him feeling exhausted. 他过度努力取悦所有人，结果感到筋疲力尽。


近义词：overexertion, overreaching, straining, striving excessively,  perfectionism

反义词：slacking, neglecting, underachieving, complacency
