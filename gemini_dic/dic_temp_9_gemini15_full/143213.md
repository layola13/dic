outcrowd:/ˌaʊtˈkraʊd/| v.|超过…的人数，人数多于，胜过|To exceed in number; to outnumber.  The protesters outcrowded the police.  抗议者的人数超过了警察。|近义词：outnumber, surpass, exceed|反义词：be outnumbered by


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fans outcrowd the players at the stadium. = 球迷人数超过了体育场里的球员。
The birds outcrowd the insects in the garden. = 花园里鸟类的数量超过了昆虫。


一般过去时:
The visitors outcrowded the locals during the festival. = 节日期间游客人数超过了当地居民。
The applicants outcrowded the available positions. = 申请者人数超过了可提供的职位数量。


现在完成时:
The supporters have outcrowded the opponents at every game this season. = 这个赛季的每一场比赛，支持者的人数都超过了对手。
The online comments have outcrowded the positive reviews. = 网上的负面评论数量超过了正面评价。


过去完成时:
By the time the concert started, the fans had outcrowded the venue. = 音乐会开始时，歌迷已经挤满了场地。
The applications had outcrowded the capacity of the system. = 申请数量已经超过了系统的容量。


将来时:
They expect the audience will outcrowd the previous record. = 他们预计观众人数将会超过之前的记录。
We predict the demand will outcrowd the supply. = 我们预测需求量将会超过供应量。


虚拟语气:
If they had advertised more, the attendees would have outcrowded the hall. = 如果他们多做些广告，参加者就会挤满大厅。
I wish the participants had outcrowded the previous year's event. = 我希望参加者人数超过去年的活动。


被动语态:
The small town was outcrowded by tourists during the summer. = 夏季期间，这个小镇被游客挤满了。
The meeting room was outcrowded by the attendees. = 会议室被与会者挤满了。


疑问句:

Will the concert tickets outcrowd the available seats? = 音乐会门票的数量是否会超过可用的座位数量？


否定句:
The home team did not outcrowd the visiting team in the stadium. = 主队在体育场的人数没有超过客队。
The number of registered voters did not outcrowd the polling station's capacity. = 登记选民的数量没有超过投票站的容量。
