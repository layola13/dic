skillet:/ˈskɪlɪt/| n. |煎锅，平底锅|A shallow, usually round, pan with a long handle, used for frying or cooking food.  |例句：She fried the eggs in a cast-iron skillet. 她用铸铁煎锅煎鸡蛋。|例句：I seasoned the skillet before cooking the steak. 我在烹饪牛排之前调味了煎锅。|近义词：frying pan, pan|反义词:None
