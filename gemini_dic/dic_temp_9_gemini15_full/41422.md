cornerer: /ˈkɔːrnərər/ | n. | 垄断者；囤积居奇者；逼迫者 | One who corners a market or commodity; someone who forces someone into a difficult situation. | Example: The cornerer drove up the price of wheat, causing hardship for many.  囤积居奇者抬高了小麦价格，导致许多人生活艰难。|近义词：monopolizer, speculator, exploiter | 反义词：altruist, benefactor


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He corners the market in rare stamps. 他垄断了稀有邮票市场。
The company corners the market on software. 这家公司垄断了软件市场。


一般过去时:
They cornered the market on oil last year. 他们去年控制了石油市场。
The speculator cornered the silver market. 投机者控制了白银市场。


现在完成时:
She has cornered the market in organic vegetables. 她已经垄断了有机蔬菜市场。
He has cleverly cornered the market in this niche product. 他巧妙地垄断了这个利基产品的市场。


过去完成时:
The traders had cornered the market before the price rose sharply.  交易员在价格暴涨之前就控制了市场。
The investors had cornered the gold market before the announcement.  投资者在公告发布之前就控制了黄金市场。


将来时:
They will corner the market once they secure the patent. 他们一旦获得专利，就会垄断市场。
He believes he will corner the market with his new invention. 他相信他的新发明将垄断市场。


虚拟语气:
If he cornered the market, he would become very wealthy. 如果他控制了市场，他就会变得非常富有。
If they had cornered the market earlier, they would have made more profit. 如果他们早点控制市场，他们就会赚更多钱。


被动语态:
The market was cornered by a group of investors. 市场被一群投资者控制了。
The commodity market was cornered by a single company.  商品市场被一家公司垄断了。


疑问句:
Did he corner the market? 他控制了市场吗？
Has she cornered the market on this product? 她垄断了这款产品的市场吗？


否定句:
He did not corner the market. 他没有控制市场。
They haven't cornered the market yet. 他们还没有垄断市场。


PHRASE->

corner the market: 垄断市场
drive up the price: 抬高价格
force into a corner: 把...逼入绝境
put someone in a corner: 使某人处于困境
corner the market in : 垄断...市场
