ravenlike:/ˈreɪvənlaɪk/| adj.|像乌鸦的；乌鸦似的；阴沉的；不吉利的|Resembling a raven; having the characteristics of a raven, such as darkness, gloominess, or ominousness.  The word often implies a sense of mystery, darkness, or ill omen associated with ravens.|例句：The ravenlike darkness descended upon the lonely moor.  乌鸦似的黑暗笼罩着荒凉的沼泽。 The ravenlike stare of the stranger made me uneasy.  陌生人乌鸦般凝视让我感到不安。|近义词：crowlike, dark, gloomy, ominous, sinister|反义词：bright, cheerful, sunny, auspicious
