cleanliness:/ˈklɛnlɪnəs/| n. |清洁；干净；卫生 |The state or quality of being clean.  It refers to the habit of keeping things clean and tidy.  |例句：The cleanliness of the hospital is of utmost importance. 医院的清洁至关重要。

例句：Maintaining cleanliness in the kitchen prevents the spread of bacteria. 保持厨房清洁可以防止细菌传播。

近义词：purity, hygiene, tidiness, sanitation |反义词：dirtiness, filth, squalor, uncleanliness |词性:名词
