reminisces:/ˌremɪˈnɪsɪz/| v.|回忆，追忆|To think or talk about past experiences.|例句：She reminisced about her childhood summers spent at the beach. 她回忆起童年在海滩度过的夏天。|近义词：recall, remember, recollect|反义词：forget


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reminisces about his travels frequently. = 他经常回忆他的旅行。
She reminisces about her college days with fondness. = 她怀念地回忆起大学时光。


一般过去时:
We reminisced about old times last night. = 昨晚我们回忆了往事。
He reminisced about his grandfather's stories. = 他回忆起他祖父的故事。


现在完成时:
She has reminisced about that trip many times. = 她已经多次回忆起那次旅行。
They have reminisced about their youth for hours. = 他们已经回忆了几个小时的青春。


过去完成时:
Before he died, he had reminisced about his life extensively. = 在他去世之前，他广泛地回忆了他的生活。
After the reunion, they had reminisced for hours about old friends. = 聚会之后，他们花了几个小时回忆旧友。


将来时:
I will reminisce about this day when I'm older. = 我老了以后会回忆起今天。
We will reminisce about this amazing journey for years to come. = 未来几年，我们会回忆起这段令人惊叹的旅程。


虚拟语气:
If I could reminisce about one moment, it would be my graduation. = 如果我能回忆一个时刻，那就是我的毕业典礼。
I wish I could reminisce more freely about my childhood. = 我希望我能更自由地回忆我的童年。


被动语态:
His childhood adventures were often reminisced about by his family. = 他童年的冒险经常被家人回忆起。
The old photos prompted many memories to be reminisced about. = 老照片促使人们回忆起许多往事。


疑问句:
Do you often reminisce about your past? = 你经常回忆过去吗？
What do you reminisce about most? = 你最怀念什么？


否定句:
He didn't reminisce about the difficult times. = 他没有回忆那些艰难的时光。
She rarely reminisces about her former job. = 她很少回忆起她以前的工作。


PHRASE->
reminisce about = 回忆……
reminisce with = 与……一起回忆
fondly reminisce = 深情地回忆
happily reminisce = 开心地回忆
bitterly reminisce = 苦涩地回忆
