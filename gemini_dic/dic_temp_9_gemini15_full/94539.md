impossibly:/ɪmˈpɒsəbli/ | adv. | 不可能地，非常地 |  It describes something that is extremely difficult or impossible to do or achieve. It can also be used for emphasis, similar to "incredibly" or "extremely". | 例句：The task was impossibly difficult.  这项任务难到令人难以置信。
The baby was impossibly cute.  这个婴儿可爱极了。|近义词：incredibly, unbelievably, extraordinarily, extremely | 反义词：possibly, easily
