retaking:/ˌriːˈteɪkɪŋ/| v.|重考；重做；重新获取|The act of taking something again, typically an exam or a task.|例句：She is retaking her driving test. 她正在重考驾照。|例句：The photographer asked them to retake the picture. 摄影师要求他们重拍照片。|近义词：redoing, repeating, retrying |反义词：completing, finishing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is retaking the math exam. 他正在重考数学考试。
They are retaking the survey. 他们正在重新进行调查。

一般过去时:
She retook the history test. 她重考了历史考试。
He retook the photograph. 他重拍了照片。

现在完成时:
I have retaken the course twice. 我已经重修这门课两次了。
She has retaken the exam and passed this time. 她已经重考并通过了考试。

过去完成时:
He had retaken the exam before the deadline. 他在截止日期前重考了考试。
They had retaken the photos before realizing the mistake. 他们意识到错误之前已经重拍了照片。

将来时:
I will retake the test next week. 我下周将重考。
They will retake the scene tomorrow. 他们明天将重拍这个场景。

虚拟语气:
If I had studied harder, I would have retaken the exam with confidence. 如果我学习更努力，我会充满信心地重考。
I wish I had retaken that class. 我真希望我重修了那门课。

被动语态:
The exam was retaken by many students. 许多学生都重考了考试。
The photos were retaken due to technical issues.  由于技术问题，照片被重拍了。

疑问句:
Are you retaking any courses this semester?  这个学期你重修了什么课程吗？
Will he be retaking the driving test soon? 他很快会重考驾照吗？

否定句:
I am not retaking the exam. 我不重考了。
He did not retake the photograph. 他没有重拍照片。
