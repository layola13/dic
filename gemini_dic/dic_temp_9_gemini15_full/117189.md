measure out:/ˈmɛʒər aʊt/| vt.|量出，量准；分配|To determine the quantity of something, typically by using a measuring instrument, and then to distribute or portion it out.|例句：Please measure out two cups of flour for the cake. 请量出两杯面粉做蛋糕。  

例句：The pharmacist carefully measured out the prescribed medication. 药剂师仔细地量出了规定的药量。


近义词：portion out, dispense, allocate, distribute|反义词：N/A|词性：动词
