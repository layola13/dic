roused: /raʊzd/ | vt. |唤醒；激起；引起；鼓动 | To wake someone from sleep; to excite or stimulate someone or something. |例句：The loud noise roused me from my sleep.  吵闹声把我从睡梦中吵醒了。

The speech roused the crowd to action.  演讲激起了群众的行动。

近义词：awakened, stirred, stimulated, excited | 反义词：soothed, calmed, quieted | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The alarm clock rouses me every morning. = 闹钟每天早上都把我叫醒。
He rouses his children for school. = 他叫醒他的孩子们去上学。


一般过去时:
The sudden noise roused her from her slumber. = 突然的噪音把她从睡梦中惊醒。
The news roused the community to protest. = 这条新闻激起了社区的抗议。


现在完成时:
The scandal has roused public outrage. = 这场丑闻激起了公众的愤怒。
The coach has roused the team's spirits before the big game. = 教练在大型比赛前鼓舞了球队的士气。


过去完成时:
The detective had roused the suspect from his drunken stupor.  侦探已将醉醺醺的嫌疑犯叫醒。
She had roused herself from her depression and started a new life. 她从沮丧中振作起来，开始了新的生活。


将来时:
The news will rouse many people to action. = 这个消息将会激起许多人的行动。
The coming storm will rouse the sea.  即将来临的风暴将激起大海。


虚拟语气:
If the fire alarm had roused everyone in time, the damage would have been less. = 如果火灾警报及时叫醒每个人，损失就会少一些。
I wish the speech had roused more people to help. 我希望演讲能激起更多人来帮忙。


被动语态:
He was roused from his sleep by the telephone. = 他被电话铃声吵醒了。
The audience was roused by his passionate speech.  听众被他充满激情的演讲所打动。


疑问句:
What roused you from your sleep? = 什么把你从睡梦中吵醒？

否定句:
The gentle rain did not rouse the sleeping cat.  温柔的雨并没有吵醒熟睡的猫。
Nothing could rouse him from his apathy.  没有什么能使他从冷漠中振作起来。
