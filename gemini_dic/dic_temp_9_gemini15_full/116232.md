masqueraded:/ˌmæskəˈreɪdɪd/ | v. |伪装；假扮；掩饰 | To disguise or conceal one's true identity, nature, or feelings; to pretend to be someone or something else.  |例句：He masqueraded as a doctor to gain access to the hospital. 他伪装成医生以进入医院。


例句：She masqueraded her true intentions behind a smile. 她用微笑掩盖了她的真实意图。


近义词：disguise, pretend, feign, impersonate


反义词：reveal, expose, unveil


词性：动词
