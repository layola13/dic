reflexes:/ˈriːflɛksɪz/| n.|反射; 反射动作|The automatic responses of the body to stimuli, often involving the nervous system.  Reflexes are involuntary actions.  They can be simple, like the knee-jerk reflex, or complex, like the withdrawal reflex from a hot object.|例句：The doctor tested his reflexes with a hammer.医生用锤子测试了他的反射。

例句：Her reflexes were quick and efficient, enabling her to dodge the flying object.她的反应很快很有效率，让她躲过了飞来的物体。|近义词：instincts, reactions|反义词：conscious actions, voluntary movements|名词
