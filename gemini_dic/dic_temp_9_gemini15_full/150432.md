pansy:/ˈpænzɪ/| n.|紫罗兰，（尤指）三色堇；懦弱的人|A pansy is a type of small flowering plant with petals in various colors, often purple, yellow, and white.  It can also be used as a derogatory term for a man considered to be effeminate.  |例句：The garden was full of colorful pansies. 花园里盛开着五颜六色的三色堇。

He was called a pansy for crying. 他因为哭泣而被称为懦夫。|近义词：violet, viola, weakling, milksop|反义词：brave, courageous, strong
