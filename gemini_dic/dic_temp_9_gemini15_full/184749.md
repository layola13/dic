resumed:/rɪˈzuːmd/ | v. | 再开始；继续；重新开始；概括陈述 | To begin again after a pause or interruption; to continue after an interruption; to summarize or briefly restate. | 例句：He resumed his work after a short break. 他短暂休息后继续工作。

例句：The meeting resumed after a coffee break. 会议在咖啡休息后重新开始。


近义词：recommence, restart, continue, reiterate | 反义词：cease, stop, discontinue


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resumes his work at 8 am every day. 他每天早上8点继续工作。
She resumes her studies after the summer break. 暑假过后她继续学习。


一般过去时:
The trial resumed after a lengthy delay. 审判在长时间延误后继续进行。
He resumed his seat after answering the question. 他回答问题后又坐下了。


现在完成时:
They have resumed their negotiations. 他们已经恢复谈判了。
She has resumed her running routine after her injury. 她受伤后恢复了跑步的日常习惯。


过去完成时:
The game had resumed after the rain stopped. 雨停后比赛又重新开始了。
The meeting had resumed before he arrived. 他到达之前会议已经重新开始了。


将来时:
We will resume our discussion tomorrow. 我们明天将继续讨论。
The conference will resume after lunch. 午饭后会议将继续。


虚拟语气:
If he resumed his training, he might win the race. 如果他恢复训练，他可能会赢得比赛。
I wish he resumed his studies. 我希望他继续学习。


被动语态:
The meeting was resumed after a short break. 会议在短暂休息后重新开始。
The game will be resumed tomorrow.  比赛将于明天继续。


疑问句:
Will the game resume soon? 比赛很快就会恢复吗？
When will they resume their travels? 他们什么时候会继续旅行？


否定句:
He did not resume his position after the accident. 事故后他没有恢复他的职位。
The project has not yet resumed. 项目尚未重启。


PHRASE->
resume work: 恢复工作
resume negotiations: 恢复谈判
resume operations: 恢复运营
resume contact: 恢复联系
resume one's studies: 继续学业


词源:
From Old French *resumer, from Latin resumere, from re- "again" + sumere "to take".  The meaning evolved from "to take again" to its current senses of restarting or summarizing.
