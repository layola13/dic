/kɑːrs/| n.|诅咒；厄运|A curse is a word or phrase used to express anger or annoyance, or to bring bad luck to someone.|例句：He muttered a curse under his breath. 他低声咒骂了一句。|近义词：malediction, imprecation|反义词：blessing


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations.  Therefore, tense variations (present, past, future, etc.) are not applicable.


PHRASE->
There are limited common phrases directly using "curse".  More common are phrases involving the act of cursing or its effects.  Examples include:

a curse upon someone: 对某人施加诅咒
under a curse: 受到诅咒
lift a curse: 解除诅咒
lay a curse: 施加诅咒
a family curse: 家族诅咒
