mum:/mʌm/| n. |妈妈，母亲|A mother, especially when addressing her.  |例句：Mum made me a delicious cake.  妈妈给我做了一个美味的蛋糕。|例句：I called my mum to tell her the good news. 我打电话给我妈妈告诉她这个好消息。|近义词：mother, mom, mama|反义词：无
