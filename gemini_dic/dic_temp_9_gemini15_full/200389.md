skylights:/ˈskaɪlaɪts/| n.|天窗；天窗玻璃|A skylight is a window set into a roof or ceiling to allow natural light into a building.  Skylights are often used in rooms with limited natural light, such as basements or attics.|例句：The skylights in the attic let in plenty of natural light.  阁楼的天窗让充足的自然光线照射进来。

The architect designed the house with several skylights to maximize natural light. 建筑师设计了这座房子，并安装了几个天窗以最大限度地利用自然光。|近义词：roof window, rooflight|反义词：无|名词
