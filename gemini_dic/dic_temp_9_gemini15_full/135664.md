/ˌnɒnˌprefrənʃəlɪzəm/ | n. |非优惠待遇；非歧视待遇 |The absence of preferential treatment or discrimination in trade or other areas.  It refers to a system where all trading partners are treated equally, without special advantages or disadvantages granted to any particular country or group. |Example:  The WTO promotes nonpreferentialisms in international trade to foster fair competition.  世界贸易组织促进国际贸易中的非优惠待遇，以促进公平竞争。|近义词：equal treatment, nondiscrimination |反义词：preferential treatment, discrimination |名词
