ploughed:/plaʊd/| v.|耕地；费力地前进；|to turn over the earth of (land) with a plough|The farmer ploughed his fields in the spring. 农民在春天耕种了他的田地。|近义词：tilled, furrowed|反义词：unploughed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ploughs the fields every year. = 他每年都耕种田地。
They plough the land before planting. = 他们在播种前耕地。

一般过去时:
The farmer ploughed the land last week. = 农民上周耕了地。
She ploughed through the book in one sitting. = 她一口气读完了那本书。

现在完成时:
We have ploughed all the fields. = 我们已经耕完了所有的田地。
He has ploughed through a lot of paperwork today. = 他今天处理了很多文件。

过去完成时:
They had ploughed the field before the rain came. = 雨下之前他们已经耕好了地。
She had ploughed through the first chapter by evening. = 到晚上她已经读完了第一章。

将来时:
They will plough the fields next week. = 他们下周将耕种田地。
He will plough through the mountain of work soon. = 他很快就会完成那堆工作。

虚拟语气:
If he ploughed the field, the crops would grow better. = 如果他耕种了田地，庄稼就会长得更好。
I wish I had ploughed through that report earlier. = 我希望我早点看完那份报告。

被动语态:
The fields were ploughed in the spring. = 田地是在春天耕种的。
The book was ploughed through by many readers. = 这本书被许多读者读完了。

疑问句:
Did they plough the field yesterday? = 他们昨天耕地了吗？
Have you ploughed through that difficult chapter yet? = 你已经读完那篇困难的章节了吗？

否定句:
They didn't plough the field because of the rain. = 因为下雨，他们没有耕地。
He hasn't ploughed through the entire document. = 他还没有看完整个文件。
