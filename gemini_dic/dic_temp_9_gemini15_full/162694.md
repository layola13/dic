preauthorizing: /ˌpriːˈɔːθəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 预先授权，预先批准 |  The act of granting authorization or approval in advance. This often involves setting up systems or processes to automatically approve certain transactions or actions that meet predefined criteria.  | Example: The system is designed for preauthorizing payments, reducing manual processing.  系统设计用于预先授权付款，减少人工处理。  |近义词：preapproving, authorizing in advance | 反义词：denying, rejecting | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bank preauthorizes transactions exceeding $1000. = 银行预先授权超过1000美元的交易。
We preauthorize all employee travel expenses. = 我们预先授权所有员工的差旅费用。


一般过去时:
The company preauthorized the purchase of new software. = 公司预先批准了购买新软件。
They preauthorized the release of funds for the project. = 他们预先批准了为该项目拨款。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

They had preauthorized the payment before the goods were shipped. = 货物运送前，他们已经预先授权付款。
