counter-threat: /ˈkaʊntərθrɛt/ | n. | 反制威胁 | A threat or action made in response to a previous threat; a retaliatory threat.  | Example: The country's military buildup was seen as a counter-threat to its neighbor's aggressive actions. 这个国家的军事集结被视为对其邻国侵略行为的反制威胁。 | 近义词：retaliation, reprisal, countermeasure | 反义词：submission, appeasement


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government issues a counter-threat to the terrorist organization. = 政府向恐怖组织发出反制威胁。
Our company develops a counter-threat strategy to protect our data. = 我们公司制定了反制威胁策略来保护我们的数据。


一般过去时:
The alliance launched a counter-threat after the attack. = 联盟在袭击后发起了反制威胁。
He responded with a counter-threat to the bully's intimidation. = 他以反制威胁回应了恶霸的恐吓。


现在完成时:
The nation has issued several counter-threats in response to the ongoing conflict. = 为了回应持续的冲突，这个国家已经发出了几项反制威胁。
They have developed a sophisticated counter-threat system to defend against cyberattacks. = 他们已经开发了一个复杂的反制威胁系统来防御网络攻击。


过去完成时:
Before the negotiations, they had prepared a strong counter-threat. = 在谈判之前，他们已经准备好了强有力的反制威胁。
The company had already implemented a counter-threat protocol when the breach occurred. = 当漏洞发生时，该公司已经实施了反制威胁协议。


将来时:
The military will deploy a counter-threat if necessary. = 必要时，军队将部署反制威胁。
We will formulate a comprehensive counter-threat plan to mitigate potential risks. = 我们将制定一个全面的反制威胁计划以减轻潜在风险。


虚拟语气:
If they issued a threat, we would have a counter-threat ready. = 如果他们发出威胁，我们将准备好反制威胁。
It is vital that we have a credible counter-threat in place. = 至关重要的是，我们必须制定一个可信的反制威胁。


被动语态:
A counter-threat was issued in response to the hostile actions. = 为了回应敌对行动，发出了反制威胁。
The counter-threat strategy is being developed by a team of experts. = 反制威胁策略正在由专家团队开发。


疑问句:
What is our counter-threat to their nuclear capabilities? = 我们对抗他们的核能力的反制威胁是什么？
Does the company have a robust counter-threat policy? = 公司是否有健全的反制威胁政策？


否定句:
The government did not offer a counter-threat to the aggressor's demands. = 政府没有对侵略者的要求提出反制威胁。
There was no effective counter-threat to the ongoing cyberattacks. = 对持续的网络攻击没有有效的反制威胁。
