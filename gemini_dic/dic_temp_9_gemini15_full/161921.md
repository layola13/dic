praisable:/ˈpreɪzəbl/| adj.|值得赞扬的；令人称赞的|deserving praise; commendable.  |例句：Her dedication to her work is praiseworthy.  她的敬业精神值得赞扬。

例句：His selfless act was truly praiseworthy. 他的无私行为确实令人称赞。

近义词：commendable, laudable, admirable, meritorious

反义词：blameworthy, reprehensible, deplorable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His work is praiseworthy. = 他的工作值得称赞。
Her efforts are praiseworthy. = 她的努力值得赞扬。


一般过去时:
His actions were praiseworthy. = 他的行为值得称赞。
Her kindness was praiseworthy. = 她的善良值得赞扬。


现在完成时:
His contributions have been praiseworthy. = 他的贡献一直值得称赞。
Her achievements have been praiseworthy. = 她的成就一直值得赞扬。


过去完成时:
His previous work had been praiseworthy. = 他以前的工作值得称赞。
Her earlier efforts had been praiseworthy. = 她早期的努力值得赞扬。


将来时:
His future endeavors will be praiseworthy. = 他未来的努力将值得称赞。
Her upcoming project will be praiseworthy. = 她即将开展的项目将值得赞扬。


虚拟语气:
If his work were praiseworthy, he would receive an award. = 如果他的工作值得称赞，他将获得奖励。
If her efforts were praiseworthy, she would be promoted. = 如果她的努力值得赞扬，她将得到晋升。


被动语态:
His actions were praised. = 他的行为受到了称赞。
Her dedication was praised by her colleagues. = 她的奉献精神受到了同事们的赞扬。


疑问句:
Is his behavior praiseworthy? = 他的行为值得称赞吗？
Are her efforts praiseworthy? = 她的努力值得称赞吗？


否定句:
His behavior was not praiseworthy. = 他的行为不值得称赞。
Her actions were not praiseworthy. = 她的行为不值得称赞。


PHRASE->
highly praiseworthy:  非常值得赞扬的
most praiseworthy: 最值得赞扬的
especially praiseworthy: 特别值得赞扬的
well-deserved praiseworthy: 应得的赞扬
utterly praiseworthy: 完全值得赞扬的

词源:
praisable 源自 praise (v.) + -able (adj. suffix).  praise 本身源自古法语 *preisier,  最终源自拉丁语 plaudere "to applaud".  -able 表示“能够…的，适合…的”。
