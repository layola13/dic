crinkles:/ˈkrɪŋkəlz/| n. |褶皱；皱纹；沙沙声|Small wrinkles or creases; a slight, uneven surface.  It can also refer to the sound of something rustling or crackling. |例句：The old photograph had many crinkles around the edges.  老照片边缘有很多褶皱。

The silk fabric crinkled as she moved.  丝绸在她移动时发出沙沙声。|近义词：wrinkles, creases, folds, ripples|反义词：smoothness, flatness
