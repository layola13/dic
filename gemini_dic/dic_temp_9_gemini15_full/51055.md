desolation:/ˌdɛsəˈleɪʃən/ | n. |荒凉；凄凉；孤独；绝望 | A state of being deserted or desolate; emptiness, loneliness, and unhappiness.  The feeling of being completely alone and unhappy.  |例句：The desolation of the landscape was breathtaking.  这片景色的荒凉令人叹为观止。

The desolation of her heart was evident in her eyes. 她内心的凄凉在她眼中显而易见。


近义词：devastation, ruin, emptiness, loneliness, despair |反义词：abundance, prosperity, happiness, joy, comfort |词性:名词
