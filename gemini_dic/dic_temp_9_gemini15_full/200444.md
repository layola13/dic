slagged:/slæɡd/| v.| 严厉批评；猛烈抨击；诋毁 | To criticize severely; to attack vehemently; to denigrate.  The performance was slagged by the critics.  演出受到了评论家的严厉批评。|近义词：criticized, condemned, denounced, lambasted | 反义词：praised, commended, lauded
