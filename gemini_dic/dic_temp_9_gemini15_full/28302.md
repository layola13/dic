car:/kɑːr/| n. |汽车，轿车 |A car is a four-wheeled motor vehicle.  |例句：I drive a red car. 我开一辆红色的车。|例句：He bought a new car last week. 他上周买了一辆新车。|近义词：automobile, vehicle, motorcar |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drives a car to work every day. = 他每天开车上班。
She often takes the car to the supermarket. = 她经常开车去超市。


一般过去时:
I drove my car to the beach yesterday. = 我昨天开车去了海滩。
They took the car to the airport. = 他们开车去了机场。


现在完成时:
I have driven this car for five years. = 我已经开这辆车五年了。
She has had her car repaired. = 她的车已经修好了。


过去完成时:
I had driven for hours before I finally arrived. = 我开了几个小时的车才最终到达。
They had taken the car to the mechanic before it broke down again. = 他们在车再次坏掉之前已经把它送到修理工那里了。


将来时:
I will drive to London tomorrow. = 我明天将开车去伦敦。
He will be taking the car to the garage next week. = 他下周将把车送去修理厂。


虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I would drive to the mountains. = 如果我有一辆车，我会开车去山里。
I wish I had a car so I could go on a road trip. = 我希望我有一辆车，这样我就可以去公路旅行了。


被动语态:
The car was stolen last night. = 车昨晚被偷了。
The car is being repaired at the moment. = 车正在修理中。


疑问句:
Do you have a car? = 你有车吗？
Whose car is this? = 这是谁的车？


否定句:
I don't have a car. = 我没有车。
He didn't drive his car today. = 他今天没有开车。
