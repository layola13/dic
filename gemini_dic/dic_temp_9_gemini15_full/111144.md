litharge:/ˈlɪθɑːrdʒ/ | n. |铅丹，氧化铅 | A heavy, reddish-yellow powder of lead(II) oxide, used in the past in pottery glazes and as a pigment.  It is also used in battery production and other industrial processes. |例句：The ancient potters used litharge in their glazes to achieve a specific sheen. 古代陶工使用铅丹在他们的釉料中以达到特定的光泽。

例句：The battery manufacturer uses litharge as a key component in its lead-acid batteries. 电池制造商使用铅丹作为其铅酸电池的关键成分。|近义词：lead oxide, red lead |反义词： (No direct antonym, as it's a specific chemical compound)  |词性:n.
