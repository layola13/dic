microfabrication:/ˌmaɪkroʊfæbriˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 微细加工，微细制造 | The process of fabricating miniature structures, typically on the scale of micrometers. This involves techniques like photolithography, etching, and deposition to create intricate patterns and devices on substrates such as silicon wafers.  | Example: Microfabrication techniques are crucial for the production of microchips and other microelectromechanical systems (MEMS). 微细加工技术对于生产微芯片和其他微机电系统（MEMS）至关重要。 | 近义词: miniaturization, microminiaturization | 反义词: macrofabrication, large-scale manufacturing | 名词
