miniaturises:/ˌmɪniətʃəˈraɪzɪz/| v.|小型化；使缩小|To make something smaller, especially in size or scale.|The company miniaturises electronic components for use in mobile phones.  这家公司将电子元件小型化，用于移动电话。|  reduce, shrink, downsize | enlarge, magnify | 动词
