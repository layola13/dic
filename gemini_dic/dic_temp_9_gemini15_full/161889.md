praeterist:/ˈpreɪtərɪst/| adj. n.|预言实现论者；预言实现论的|Relating to or characterized by praeterism, the theological interpretation of biblical prophecy that sees its fulfillment primarily in events of the past, especially the first century CE.|The praeterist interpretation of the Book of Revelation sees its events as fulfilled in the Roman Empire's destruction in the first century. 预言实现论的解释《启示录》认为其事件在公元一世纪罗马帝国的毁灭中得到了应验。|近义词：futurist (反义词), historicist (相关)|无
