reflower:/ˌriːˈflaʊər/ | v. | 再开花；重新开花 | To flower again after a period of dormancy or after being cut back.  | The roses reflowered beautifully after the harsh winter.  玫瑰在严冬过后又漂亮地开了花。| rebloom, blossom again | wither, fade | v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The roses reflower every spring. = 玫瑰每年春天都重新开花。
This plant reflowers prolifically throughout the summer. = 这种植物整个夏天都大量地重新开花。

一般过去时:
The plant reflowered after a period of drought. = 那株植物在干旱期后重新开花。
The bulbs reflowered more abundantly this year. = 球茎今年开花更多了。

现在完成时:
The azaleas have reflowered since the last rain. = 自从上次下雨以来，杜鹃花又开了。
The orchids have reflowered beautifully after repotting. = 兰花换盆后开得非常漂亮。

过去完成时:
The tree had reflowered before the frost arrived. = 霜冻来临之前，树已经重新开花了。
The flowers had reflowered twice that season. = 那季节里花已经开了两次。


将来时:
The tulips will reflower next spring. =郁金香明年春天会再次开花。
We expect the hydrangeas to reflower in a few weeks. = 我们预计绣球花几周后会再次开花。


虚拟语气:
If the plant received more sunlight, it might reflower sooner. = 如果植物能接受更多阳光，它可能会更快地再次开花。
I wish the lilies would reflower again this year. = 我希望百合花今年能再次开花。

被动语态:
The flowers were reflowered with the help of special fertilizer. = 在特殊肥料的帮助下，花朵重新开放了。(此用法略显不自然，很少使用被动语态)
The garden was planned to be reflowered in the fall. = 花园计划在秋天重新种植花卉来盛开。(此用法更侧重于重新种植而非植物本身再次开花)

疑问句:
Will the daffodils reflower this year? = 水仙花今年还会开花吗？
Do these plants typically reflower after they've been pruned? = 这些植物修剪后通常会再次开花吗？

否定句:
The plant did not reflower this season. = 这种植物这个季节没有重新开花。
The roses didn't reflower after the late frost. = 晚霜过后，玫瑰没有重新开花。
