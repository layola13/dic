neoplasm:/ˈniːoʊplæzəm/| n. |肿瘤，赘生物|An abnormal mass of tissue resulting from excessive cell proliferation that is not controlled by the normal regulatory mechanisms.  Neoplasms can be benign (non-cancerous) or malignant (cancerous).|例句：The doctor discovered a neoplasm during the routine examination. 医生在例行检查中发现了一个肿瘤。

The biopsy revealed a benign neoplasm, thankfully.  谢天谢地，活检显示这是一个良性肿瘤。|近义词：tumor, growth, mass|反义词：normal tissue, healthy tissue|
