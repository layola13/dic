hammering:/ˈhæmərɪŋ/ | v. | 锤击；猛击；连续敲打 | The act of hitting something repeatedly with a hammer or similar object;  a forceful or continuous striking.  It can also refer to a repetitive and forceful action or sound. | 例句：The blacksmith was hammering the hot iron.  铁匠正在锤打烧红的铁。

The constant hammering of the construction work disturbed my sleep.  持续不断的建筑施工声扰乱了我的睡眠。

| 近义词：pounding, banging, striking, hitting | 反义词：gentle tapping, caressing |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is hammering nails into the wall. = 他正在把钉子钉进墙里。
The machine is hammering out the metal sheets. = 机器正在敲打金属板。


一般过去时:
She hammered the metal until it was flat. = 她反复锤打金属直到它变平。
The workers hammered the stakes into the ground. = 工人们把桩子打入地下。


现在完成时:
They have hammered out a compromise. = 他们已经达成妥协。
The rain has been hammering against the window all day. = 雨水整天都在猛烈地敲打着窗户。


过去完成时:
He had hammered the nail in securely before leaving. = 他离开前已经把钉子牢牢地钉进去了。
The storm had hammered the coast for hours. = 暴风雨已经猛烈地袭击了海岸几个小时。


将来时:
They will hammer out a new agreement next week. = 他们下周将敲定一项新协议。

虚拟语气:
If he hammered harder, he would finish sooner. = 如果他锤得更用力，他就能更快完成。
I wish I hadn't hammered on the door so loudly. = 我真希望我没有敲门敲得那么大声。


被动语态:
The metal was hammered into shape. = 金属被锤成型。
The door was hammered on repeatedly. = 门被反复敲打。


疑问句:
Are they hammering the wood now? = 他们现在正在锤木头吗？

否定句:
He didn't hammer the nail straight. = 他没有把钉子钉直。
She wasn't hammering on the table. = 她没有在敲桌子。


PHRASE->

词源:
The word "hammering" originates from the Old English word "hammor," meaning "hammer."  The "-ing" suffix indicates the present participle or gerund form, denoting the action of hammering.
