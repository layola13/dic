This word is a relatively uncommon term and its usage is primarily found in technical or academic contexts, particularly when discussing risks or environmental issues.  It emphasizes the lack of mitigation efforts rather than the presence of negative effects. | Example: The report highlighted the nonmitigations in disaster preparedness, leading to significant losses.  报告强调了防灾准备中缺乏缓解措施，导致了巨大的损失。 | 近义词：lack of mitigation, absence of countermeasures, failure to mitigate | 反义词：mitigation, countermeasures, preventative measures | n.
