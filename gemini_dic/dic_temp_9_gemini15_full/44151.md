critically:/ˈkrɪtɪkəli/ | adv. | 批评地；关键地；危急地 | In a critical manner; expressing adverse or disapproving comments or judgments.  |例句：He critically analyzed the poem's structure. 他批判性地分析了这首诗的结构。|例句：The patient's condition is critically unstable. 病人的病情危急不稳定。|近义词：severely, sharply,  analytically | 反义词：uncritically, favorably, superficially

否定句:

词源:
From the adjective "critical," ultimately deriving from the Greek word "kritikos" (κριτικός), meaning "able to judge," "discriminating," or related to "krisis" (κρίσις), meaning "judgment," "decision."  The suffix "-ally" forms an adverb.
