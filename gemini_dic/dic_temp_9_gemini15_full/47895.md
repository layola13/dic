decennial:/dɛˈsɛniəl/| adj. n.|十年的；十年期|Relating to or covering a period of ten years; occurring every ten years.  A decennial celebration marks a ten-year anniversary.|例句：The company celebrated its decennial anniversary with a large party. 这家公司举办盛大的派对庆祝成立十周年。

例句：The decennial census showed a significant population increase. 十年一度的人口普查显示人口显著增长。


近义词：decadal, ten-year


反义词：None specifically, but words implying shorter periods (annual, biannual) could be considered contextual opposites.


词性：adjective, noun
