practitioner:/prækˈtɪʃənər/| n. |从业者，实践者|A practitioner is someone who regularly practices or uses a particular skill, art, or profession.  They are skilled and experienced in their field.|例句：She is a practitioner of traditional Chinese medicine. 她是一位中医师。|例句：He's a highly skilled practitioner of yoga. 他是一位技艺高超的瑜伽练习者。|近义词：professional, expert, specialist|反义词：amateur, novice, beginner
