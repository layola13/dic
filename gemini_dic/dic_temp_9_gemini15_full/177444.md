rebate:/ˈriːbeɪt/| n. v.|回扣；折扣；减免；退还|A partial refund of the original price of something.|例句：The store offered a rebate on all its appliances. 这家商店对其所有电器都提供折扣。
例句：We received a rebate for the faulty product. 我们收到了有缺陷产品的退款。

词源:  From Middle French *rebattre "to beat back," from re- "back" + battre "to beat."  The sense development is from "to beat back, reduce" to "reduction in price."


近义词：discount, refund, reduction, allowance

反义词：surcharge, markup, increase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store offers a rebate on energy-efficient appliances. = 这家商店提供节能家电的折扣。
He always negotiates for a rebate on his car insurance. = 他总是协商降低他的汽车保险费用。


一般过去时:
They gave me a rebate on my last purchase. = 他们退还了我上次购买商品的部分款项。
The company offered a rebate to loyal customers. = 公司向忠实客户提供回扣。


现在完成时:
I have received a rebate on my taxes this year. = 我今年已经收到了税款减免。
She has claimed a rebate on her energy bills. = 她已经申请了能源费用的退款。


过去完成时:
They had already received their rebate before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经收到了他们的回扣。
We had applied for a rebate but hadn't heard back yet. = 我们已经申请了退款，但还没有收到回复。


将来时:
We will receive a rebate on our next bill. = 我们将在下一张账单中获得折扣。
They will offer a rebate for early registration. = 他们将为提前注册提供折扣。


虚拟语气:
If I bought that car, I would get a rebate. = 如果我买了那辆车，我会得到折扣。
I wish I had known about the rebate earlier. = 我真希望我早点知道这个回扣。


被动语态:
The rebate was processed quickly. = 退款很快就被处理了。
The rebate is being applied to your account. = 退款正在记入您的账户。


疑问句:
Is there a rebate available for this product? = 这款产品有折扣吗？
Will we get a rebate if we return the damaged item? = 如果我们退回损坏的物品，我们会得到退款吗？


否定句:
The store does not offer rebates on sale items. = 这家商店不为特价商品提供折扣。
They did not receive a rebate for their faulty appliance. = 他们没有因为他们的故障电器获得退款。
