enfranchise:/ɪnˈfrænˌtʃaɪz/ | vt. |赋予…权利；给予…公民权；解放；特许经营 | To grant someone the right to vote or to participate in political life.  To grant a franchise to. |例句：The 19th Amendment enfranchised women in the United States.  美国第十九修正案赋予妇女投票权。

The new law enfranchised millions of previously disenfranchised citizens. 这项新法律使数百万先前被剥夺公民权的公民获得了公民权。


近义词：empower, liberate, entitle | 反义词：disenfranchise, suppress

词源:
