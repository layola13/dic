misspells:/ˌmɪsˈspɛlz/| v.|拼写错误|To write or print a word incorrectly.|The student misspells many words in his essay.  学生在他的论文中拼写了很多单词错误。|  The child misspelt 'believe' as 'beleive'. 孩子把“believe”错写成“beleive”。|近义词：miswrite, misspell|反义词：spell correctly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He misspells words frequently. = 他经常拼写错误。
She misspells names easily. = 她很容易拼错名字。

一般过去时:
I misspelled your name in the email. = 我在邮件中拼错了你的名字。
He misspelled the word "definitely". = 他把“definitely”这个词拼错了。

现在完成时:
I have misspelled several words in this document. = 我在这份文件中拼错了好几个单词。
She has misspelled my name on the form. = 她在表格上拼错了我的名字。

过去完成时:
I had misspelled the address, so the letter was returned. = 我把地址拼错了，所以信被退回了。
She realized she had misspelled the word after submitting the test. = 她提交考试后才意识到自己拼错了那个词。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
