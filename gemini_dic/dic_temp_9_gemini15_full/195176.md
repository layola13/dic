semidiameter:/ˌsɛmiˈdaɪæmɪtər/| n.|半径|Half the diameter of a circle or sphere.  A semidiameter is a line segment from the center of a circle or sphere to a point on its circumference or surface. It is half the length of the diameter.|The semidiameter of the Earth is approximately 3959 miles. 地球的半径大约是3959英里。|radius|diameter|
