phalangite:/fəˈlænɡaɪt/| n. |方阵士兵；（古希腊）方阵步兵|A phalangite was a soldier who fought in a phalanx, a closely packed formation of heavily armed infantry.  Phalangites were known for their disciplined fighting style and their use of long spears. |Example: The phalangites stood firm against the enemy's cavalry charge. 方阵士兵们抵挡住了敌人的骑兵冲锋。 The Spartan phalangites were renowned for their courage and military prowess. 斯巴达方阵士兵以其勇气和军事实力而闻名。|近义词：hoplit, spearman|反义词：N/A
