delineation:/ˌdiːliˈneɪʃən/| n. |描绘；轮廓；界限 |The act of outlining or describing something precisely. It refers to a clear and detailed representation of something, often with specific boundaries or features.  |The map showed a clear delineation of the different geographical zones.  地图清晰地描绘了不同的地理区域。
The delineation of responsibilities between the two departments is crucial. 两个部门之间责任的划分至关重要。| demarcation, description, outline, definition | confusion, ambiguity | 名词
