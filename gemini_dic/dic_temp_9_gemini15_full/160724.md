portrayed:/pɔːˈtreɪd/ | vt. | 描绘；扮演；刻画 | To represent or describe someone or something in a particular way, especially in a work of art or literature.  | 例句：The painting portrayed a serene landscape.  这幅画描绘了一幅宁静的风景。

The actor portrayed the character convincingly.  这位演员令人信服地扮演了这个角色。

| 近义词：depict, represent, illustrate, depict, characterize | 反义词：misrepresent, distort | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He portrays the villain perfectly. = 他完美地扮演了反派。
The novel portrays life in a small town. = 这部小说描绘了小镇生活。

一般过去时:
The artist portrayed her as a young woman. = 画家把她描绘成一个年轻女子。
The movie portrayed the war realistically. = 这部电影逼真地展现了战争。

现在完成时:
The media has portrayed the situation unfairly. = 媒体对这种情况的报道不公平。
She has portrayed many different roles throughout her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她扮演过许多不同的角色。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
