perturber:/pərˈtɜːrbər/| n. |扰乱者，干扰物 | A perturber is something or someone that causes disturbance or disruption.  It can refer to a physical object, a person, or an event that disrupts a system or process. |例句：The unexpected snowstorm was a major perturber to the city's transportation system.  突如其来的暴风雪严重扰乱了城市的交通系统。

The noisy construction next door was a perturber to my peaceful morning.  隔壁吵闹的施工扰乱了我平静的早晨。|近义词：disruptor, disturber, interferer |反义词：stabilizer, pacifier, calmer |名词
