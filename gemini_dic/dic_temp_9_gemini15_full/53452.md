disapproval:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːvəl/| n. |不赞成，反对，不认可 |The feeling of not approving of something or someone; condemnation.  It often involves a negative judgment or a sense of dissatisfaction. |例句：His parents showed their disapproval of his lifestyle.  他的父母表达了对他不赞成他的生活方式。

例句：The plan met with considerable disapproval from the board.  这个计划遭到了董事会的大量反对。


近义词：disfavor, objection, opposition, condemnation, censure, criticism |反义词：approval, acceptance, endorsement, sanction


词性: 名词
