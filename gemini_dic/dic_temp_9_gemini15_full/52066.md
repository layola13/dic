dialogs:/ˈdaɪəˌlɒɡz/ /ˈdaɪəˌlɔːɡz/| n. |对话，对白 |Conversations between two or more people; a written or spoken exchange of conversation.  | Example: The play's dialogs were witty and engaging. 这出戏的对话机智引人入胜。The movie's dialogs were realistic and believable. 这部电影的对话真实可信。|近义词：conversations, exchanges, colloquies |反义词：monologue, soliloquy
