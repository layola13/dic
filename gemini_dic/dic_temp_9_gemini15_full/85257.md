/ˈhɑːrməni/| n.|和谐，协调，和睦|A state of agreement or balance;  a pleasing combination of sounds.|The orchestra played in perfect harmony.  乐团演奏得非常和谐。 The two countries signed a treaty to promote regional harmony.  两国签署条约以促进区域和谐。| concord, accord, unity | discord, conflict, chaos | 名词
