mola:/ˈmoʊlə/ | n. | 磨石；臼齿；圆形图案 | A mola is a type of textile art created by the Kuna people of Panama.  It is also a type of grinding stone, and a molar tooth. |例句：The Kuna women are famous for their vibrantly colored molas. 库纳妇女以她们色彩鲜艳的莫拉而闻名。


The mola's intricate designs tell stories. 莫拉复杂的设计讲述着故事。

近义词：grinding stone (if referring to the stone), textile art (if referring to the artwork) | 反义词：None specifically applicable across all meanings. | 词性：名词


Etymology: The word "mola" comes from the Kuna language of Panama.  It's a direct borrowing, reflecting the cultural significance of the textile art within the Kuna community.  The use of "mola" for a grinding stone and molar tooth likely comes from a shared root indicating a rounded or grinding element.  The connection between these meanings is semantic extension.
