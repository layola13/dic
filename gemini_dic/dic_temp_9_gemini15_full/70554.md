filing:/ˈfaɪlɪŋ/| n. v.|归档；文件；呈递；锉削|The act of arranging and storing documents in an organized system; the process of submitting a document or legal claim; the act of using a file to shape or smooth a surface.|例句：The filing of the tax returns is due April 15th.  报税截止日期是4月15日。|例句：He needs to do some filing before the meeting. 他需要在会议前整理一些文件。|近义词：archiving, submitting, registering|反义词：retrieving, discarding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He files his documents every week. = 他每周整理他的文件。
She files a lawsuit against the company. = 她对该公司提起诉讼。

一般过去时:
I filed my taxes last week. = 我上周报了税。
They filed the report on time. = 他们按时提交了报告。

现在完成时:
She has filed all the necessary paperwork. = 她已经提交了所有必要的文书工作。
He has filed three lawsuits this year. = 他今年已经提交了三起诉讼。

过去完成时:
They had filed the complaint before the hearing. = 他们在听证会之前已经提交了投诉。
She had filed her application weeks before the deadline. = 她在截止日期前几周就提交了申请。

将来时:
I will file the report tomorrow. = 我明天将提交报告。
We will file a motion to dismiss the case. = 我们将提出驳回此案的动议。

虚拟语气:
If I had filed the documents on time, I wouldn't be in trouble now. = 如果我按时提交文件，我现在就不会有麻烦了。
I wish I had filed my taxes earlier. = 我希望我早点报税。

被动语态:
The documents were filed correctly. = 文件已正确归档。
The lawsuit is being filed next week. = 诉讼将于下周提起。

疑问句:
Have you filed your taxes yet? = 你报税了吗？
When will the appeal be filed? = 上诉什么时候提交？

否定句:
I didn't file my application. = 我没有提交我的申请。
He hasn't filed the report yet. = 他还没有提交报告。
