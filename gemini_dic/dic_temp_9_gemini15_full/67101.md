explored:/ɪkˈsplɔːrd/| v.|探索，探究，考察|To travel in or through (a place) in order to learn about it.|例句：The explorers explored the Amazon rainforest. 探险家们探索了亚马逊雨林。
例句：She explored the possibility of starting her own business. 她探究了创业的可能性。|近义词：investigated, examined, researched|反义词：ignored, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They explore new ideas every day. = 他们每天都在探索新的想法。
We explore the city on foot. = 我们步行探索这座城市。

一般过去时:
He explored the cave last year. = 他去年探索了那个洞穴。
She explored many different career options. = 她探索了许多不同的职业选择。

现在完成时:
We have explored several options. = 我们已经探索了几个选择。
They have explored the implications of this policy. = 他们已经探索了这项政策的影响。

过去完成时:
They had explored the possibility of cooperation before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经探讨了合作的可能性。
She had explored all avenues before giving up. = 在放弃之前，她已经尝试了所有途径。

将来时:
We will explore the region next year. = 我们明年将探索这个地区。
They will explore the potential benefits of this technology. = 他们将探索这项技术的潜在益处。

虚拟语气:
If I had explored other options, I might have made a different decision. = 如果我当时探索了其他选择，我可能会做出不同的决定。
I wish I had explored my artistic talents earlier. = 我希望我早些时候就探索我的艺术天赋。

被动语态:
The Amazon rainforest is being explored by scientists. = 亚马逊雨林正在被科学家们探索。
The new possibilities are being explored by the team. = 团队正在探索新的可能性。

疑问句:
Have you explored all the possibilities? = 你已经探索了所有可能性了吗？
Did they explore the issue thoroughly? = 他们是否彻底地探索了这个问题？

否定句:
They did not explore the options carefully enough. = 他们没有仔细地探索这些选择。
We have not explored this area in detail yet. = 我们还没有详细探索这个领域。
