misers:/ˈmaɪzərz/| n.|守财奴，吝啬鬼|A miser is a person who hoards wealth and is unwilling to spend it, even on necessities.  They are characterized by extreme frugality and often exhibit anxiety about losing their money. |例句：He was a miser who lived in a dilapidated house despite his considerable fortune. 他是个守财奴，尽管家财万贯，却住在破旧的房子里。|例句：The miser counted his coins every night before bed.  这个守财奴每晚睡前都数他的硬币。|近义词：hoarder, skinflint, penny-pincher, tightwad|反义词：spendthrift, philanthropist, generous person|名词
