overdecorate:/ˌoʊvərˈdɛkəreɪt/ | vt. |过度装饰，装饰过度 | To decorate something excessively or to a point where it is no longer aesthetically pleasing.  It implies that the decoration is overwhelming or detracts from the object's inherent beauty or functionality. | The living room was overdecorated with too many knick-knacks and clashing colors.  客厅被装饰得过于华丽，摆满了太多的小摆设和颜色冲突的物品。


The cake was overdecorated; the frosting hid the delicate sponge underneath. 蛋糕装饰过度；糖霜遮住了下面精致的海绵蛋糕。|近义词：embellish excessively, adorn too much, overload with decoration |反义词：underdecorate, simplify, minimalist
