menilite:/ˈmɛnɪlaɪt/| n. |蛋白石的一种；乳白色蛋白石|A variety of opal, typically milky white or light-colored, often with a waxy or resinous luster.  It is usually found in nodules or concretions.|Example: The geologist identified the sample as menilite, a type of opal found in sedimentary rocks.  地质学家将样本鉴定为蛋白石的一种——燧石，一种在沉积岩中发现的蛋白石。|近义词：opal (general term)|反义词：无|名词
