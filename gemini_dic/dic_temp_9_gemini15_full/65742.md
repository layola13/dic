evergreens:/ˈɛvərˌɡriːnz/| n.|常绿植物；常青树|Plants that retain their leaves throughout the year.  They are a vital part of many ecosystems, providing habitat and food for wildlife.  |例句：Pine trees and holly are examples of evergreens. 松树和冬青是常绿植物的例子。

例句：Many birds build their nests in evergreen trees during the winter. 许多鸟类在冬天会在常绿树上筑巢。

近义词：conifers, perennials

反义词：deciduous trees
