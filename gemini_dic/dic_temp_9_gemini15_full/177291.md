reassisted:/ˌriːəˈsɪstɪd/| v. |再次援助，重新提供帮助 |To assist someone or something again.  This word is less common and often implies a previous instance of assistance followed by a renewed effort.  |例句：After the initial rescue, the coast guard reassisted the stranded sailors with food and water.  海岸警卫队在最初的救援之后，再次向遇难的水手提供食物和水。
