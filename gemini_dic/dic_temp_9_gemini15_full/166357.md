preremoves:/ˌpriːrɪˈmuːvz/| v.|预先清除，预先去除 | To remove something beforehand or in advance.  The process involves eliminating something before another action or event takes place.|例句：The surgeon preremoved the tumor before the main operation. 外科医生在主要手术前预先切除了肿瘤。

例句：The cleaning crew preremoved all the trash before the party started. 清洁人员在派对开始前预先清除了所有垃圾。


近义词：pre-clean, pre-treat, remove beforehand, eliminate in advance


反义词：postpone removal, leave in place


词性:动词
