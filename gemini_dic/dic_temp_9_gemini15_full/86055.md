heaped:/hiːpt/ | adj. | 堆积的；装满的；大量的 |  Filled or covered with a large quantity of something.  It suggests a large amount piled up or overflowing. |例句：The plate was heaped with delicious food.  盘子里堆满了美味的食物。

The garden was heaped with fallen leaves. 花园里堆满了落叶。


近义词：piled, loaded, laden, overflowing, abundant | 反义词：empty, sparse, meager


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plate is heaped with strawberries. 盘子里堆满了草莓。
The farmer heaps the hay into the barn. 农民把干草堆进谷仓。


一般过去时:
She heaped praise on her students. 她对她的学生大加赞赏。
They heaped the gifts onto the table. 他们把礼物堆在桌子上。


现在完成时:
They have heaped up a mountain of debt. 他们已经积累了巨额债务。
She has heaped scorn upon her critics. 她对她的批评者冷嘲热讽。


过去完成时:
He had heaped all the papers onto the desk before he left. 他离开前已将所有文件堆在桌子上。
They had heaped insults upon each other for hours. 他们互相辱骂了几个小时。


将来时:
We will heap the sandbags against the wall. 我们将把沙袋堆在墙边。
They will heap more pressure on the government. 他们将对政府施加更大的压力。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would heap gifts upon my family. 如果我有钱，我会给我的家人送很多礼物。
I wish they would not heap so much work on me. 我希望他们不要给我安排那么多工作。


被动语态:
The table was heaped with presents. 桌子上堆满了礼物。
The field was heaped with snow. 田野里堆满了雪。


疑问句:
Is the bowl heaped with cereal? 碗里堆满了麦片吗？
Were the shelves heaped with books? 书架上堆满了书吗？


否定句:
The basket was not heaped with fruit. 篮子里没有堆满水果。
The box wasn't heaped with presents. 箱子里没有堆满礼物。

词源:
