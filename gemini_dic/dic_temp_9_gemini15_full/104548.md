kamik:/ˈkæmɪk/| n. |卡米克靴（一种防水靴）|A kamik is a type of waterproof boot, traditionally made from sealskin or other animal hides, worn by Inuit and other Arctic peoples.  They are typically ankle-high or higher and provide excellent insulation and protection from the cold and wet.|Example: The Inuit hunter wore his kamik to keep his feet warm and dry in the snow.  = 因纽特猎人穿着他的卡米克靴，让他的脚在雪地里保持温暖干燥。|近义词：boots, mukluks|反义词：sandals, flip-flops
