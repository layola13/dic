qualificator: /ˈkwɑːlɪfaɪkəːr/ | n. |限定词，修饰语 | A word or phrase that limits or modifies the meaning of another word or phrase.  It specifies or restricts the application of a term or concept.  For example, in "the big red car," "big" and "red" are qualifiers. |The adjective "big" is a qualifier that modifies the noun "car".|近义词：modifier, specifier, restrictor |反义词：generalizer, broadener
