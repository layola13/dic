mass-produced:/ˈmæs prəˈduːst/ | adj. | 大量生产的；批量生产的 |  Made in large quantities, typically by machine.  This adjective describes goods that are manufactured on a large scale using automated processes, often resulting in lower costs and greater availability. | Example:  Mass-produced clothing is often cheaper than bespoke items.  批量生产的服装通常比定制服装便宜。  The factory mass-produced toys for the holiday season.  这家工厂为节日大量生产玩具。| 近义词：factory-made, manufactured, industrialized | 反义词：handmade, bespoke, custom-made


USAGE->

These factories mass-produce cars. = 这些工厂大量生产汽车。
They mass-produce clothing for the global market. = 他们为全球市场大量生产服装。
