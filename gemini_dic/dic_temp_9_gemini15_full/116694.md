maturers:/məˈtʃʊrərz/ | n. | 成熟者，成年人 | Individuals who have reached a state of maturity, especially in a specific context like financial markets or biological development.  The term often refers to a group or class of individuals, not just a single person. |  Example: The market is driven by the decisions of mature investors.  这个市场是由成熟的投资者决定的。| 近义词：adults, grown-ups, developed individuals | 反义词：immatures, minors, adolescents | 名词
