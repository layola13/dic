crow:/krəʊ/|n./乌鸦；人群；一大群；（声音）嘎嘎叫|A large, black bird with a harsh cry.  A large number of people. The sound made by a crow.|例句：The crow perched on the branch.  乌鸦停在树枝上。
The crows gathered in the field.  乌鸦聚集在田野里。|近义词：raven, blackbird|反义词：None specifically.
