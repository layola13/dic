pastiest: /ˈpeɪstiɪst/ | adj. | 最粘稠的；最糊状的 | Most pasty; having the consistency of paste.  The word implies an undesirable texture, often referring to food that is excessively thick, sticky, and unappetizing. | Example: The overcooked potatoes were the pastiest I've ever tasted.  这煮得过火的土豆是我吃过最粘稠的。


近义词: thickest, stickiest, gloppiest, gooiest


反义词:  thin, runny, liquid, crisp


词性: adjective
