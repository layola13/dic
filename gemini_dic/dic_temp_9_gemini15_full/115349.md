manometer:/məˈnɒmɪtər/ | n. |压力计，测压计 |An instrument used to measure pressure, typically of gases or liquids.  A manometer usually consists of a U-shaped tube containing a liquid (often mercury), with one arm connected to the system whose pressure is being measured and the other arm open to the atmosphere. The difference in liquid levels in the two arms indicates the pressure difference. |The manometer showed a pressure of 100 kPa. 压力计显示压力为100千帕。

The technician used a manometer to check the tire pressure. 技师用压力计检查轮胎气压。


|近义词：pressure gauge, pressure meter |反义词：None (no direct antonym)|
