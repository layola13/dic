/ɑːrˈdiː/|/ˈrɒd/ |abbr.|路,道路|Abbreviation of "road".  It is used in addresses and on maps to indicate a street or road.|Example: My house is at 123 Main Rd. 我的房子在梅恩路123号。|近义词：street, avenue, way|反义词：N/A|abbr.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The road is long. = 这条路很长。
The road leads to the city. = 这条路通往城市。


一般过去时:
The road was icy yesterday. = 昨天的路很滑。
We took the wrong road. = 我们走错了路。


现在完成时:
They have traveled many roads. = 他们走过很多路。
I have never been down that road before. = 我以前从未走过那条路。


过去完成时:
We had already driven down that road before the accident. = 事故发生之前我们已经开过那条路了。
They had taken the wrong road before realizing their mistake. = 他们意识到错误之前已经走错了路。


将来时:
We will take the scenic road tomorrow. = 我们明天将走风景优美的道路。
The new road will be completed next year. = 新路将于明年完工。


虚拟语气:
If I had taken a different road, I wouldn't have been late. = 如果我走另一条路，我就不会迟到了。
I wish I had taken the shorter road. = 我希望我走了那条更短的路。


被动语态:
The road is being repaired. = 道路正在维修中。
The road was damaged by the storm. = 道路被暴风雨损坏了。


疑问句:
Which road should we take? = 我们应该走哪条路？
Is this the right road? = 这是正确的路吗？


否定句:
That's not the right road. = 那不是正确的路。
We didn't take the highway; we took the country road. = 我们没有走高速公路，我们走了乡村道路。


PHRASE->

off the road = 偏离道路

across the road = 马路对面
