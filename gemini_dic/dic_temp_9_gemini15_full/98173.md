inscription:/ɪnˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. |铭文，题词 | An inscription is something written, carved, or painted on a surface.  It's often a short message or name.  | Example: The ancient tomb bore an inscription detailing the king's life and accomplishments.  这古墓上刻有铭文，详细记载了国王的生平和功绩。 |近义词：engraving, lettering, inscription |反义词：blank, unwritten
