finning:/ˈfɪnɪŋ/| n. v.|鱼翅切割；鳍的切除|The act of removing a shark's fins and discarding the body.  This practice is often condemned as cruel and wasteful.|例句：Shark finning is a cruel and unsustainable practice. 鲨鱼鳍的切割是一种残忍且不可持续的做法。

The illegal finning of sharks is a serious problem.  非法捕捞鲨鱼鳍是一个严重的问题。|近义词：cutting, slicing, trimming|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be leaving the fins intact.|词性：noun, verb
