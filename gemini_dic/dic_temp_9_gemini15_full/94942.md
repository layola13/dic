in-point:/ˈɪn.pɔɪnt/ | n. |要旨；要点；关键点 |The in-point of the argument is the need for more funding.  论点的关键在于需要更多资金。| 近义词：main point, crux, gist | 反义词：irrelevance, digression |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The in-point of this discussion is clear. = 这场讨论的要点很清楚。
What is the in-point of your proposal? = 你提议的要点是什么？


一般过去时:
The in-point of his speech was the importance of education. = 他演讲的要点是教育的重要性。
What was the in-point of the meeting? = 会议的要点是什么？


现在完成时:
The in-point of our work has been to improve efficiency. = 我们工作的重点一直是提高效率。
What has been the in-point of their research so far? = 到目前为止，他们的研究重点是什么？


过去完成时:
The in-point of the debate had been the proposed budget cuts. = 辩论的要点一直是提议的预算削减。
What had been the in-point of their negotiations before the agreement? = 协议达成之前，他们的谈判要点是什么？


将来时:
The in-point of the presentation will be the new product launch. = 演示的重点将是新产品的发布。
What will be the in-point of the next meeting? = 下次会议的重点是什么？


虚拟语气:
If the in-point were clearer, we would understand better. = 如果要点更清楚，我们会更容易理解。
I wish the in-point of the instructions were more obvious. = 我希望说明的要点更明显一些。


被动语态:
The in-point was clearly explained in the introduction. = 要点在引言中得到了清晰的解释。
The in-point is being addressed in the current chapter. = 要点在当前章节中正在讨论。


疑问句:
What is the in-point of all this? = 所有这一切的要点是什么？
What was the in-point of his complaint? = 他抱怨的要点是什么？


否定句:
The in-point was not clearly stated in the report. = 报告中没有明确说明要点。
He didn't understand the in-point of the argument. = 他不明白论点的要点。


PHRASE->
the main in-point = 主要要点
the central in-point = 中心要点
the crucial in-point = 至关重要的要点
the key in-point = 关键要点
the salient in-point = 显著要点
