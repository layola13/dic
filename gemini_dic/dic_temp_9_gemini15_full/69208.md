fear-monger: /ˈfɪrˌmʌŋɡər/ | n. |煽动恐慌者，危言耸听者 |Someone who deliberately spreads fear and alarm, often to achieve a political or other goal.  They exaggerate threats or dangers to manipulate people's emotions. |例句：The politician was accused of being a fear-monger, spreading false rumors about the economy to gain votes.  这位政治家被指控为煽动恐慌者，散布关于经济的虚假谣言以赢得选票。|近义词：alarmist, scaremonger, panic-monger |反义词：pacifist, appeaser


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He fear-mongers about the upcoming election. = 他在散布关于即将到来的选举的恐慌言论。
She fear-mongers on social media to gain followers. = 她在社交媒体上散布恐慌言论以获得粉丝。


一般过去时:
The media fear-mongered about the disease outbreak. = 媒体关于疾病暴发散布了恐慌言论。
He fear-mongered during his campaign speech. = 他在竞选演讲中散布恐慌言论。


现在完成时:

She has fear-mongered for years, always exploiting people's anxieties. = 她多年来一直散布恐慌言论，总是利用人们的焦虑。


过去完成时:

They had fear-mongered extensively, creating a climate of fear. = 他们已经广泛地散布恐慌言论，制造了一种恐惧的气氛。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
