invigorate:/ɪnˈvɪɡəreɪt/ | vt. |使精力充沛；使振奋；使活跃 | To give strength, energy, or life to something or someone. To make something stronger or more active. |例句：The cold air invigorated him.  = 冷空气使他精神焕发。

The news invigorated the team. = 这个消息使团队士气大振。


近义词：energize, revitalize, stimulate, refresh | 反义词：debilitate, enervate, exhaust, weaken | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The morning exercise invigorates me. = 早晨的锻炼使我精力充沛。
Fresh air invigorates the mind. = 新鲜空气使头脑清醒。

一般过去时:
The vacation invigorated her. = 假期使她恢复了活力。
The speech invigorated the audience. = 演讲使听众精神振奋。

现在完成时:
The new strategy has invigorated the company. = 新战略使公司焕发了生机。
This experience has invigorated my belief in myself. = 这段经历增强了我对自己的信心。

过去完成时:
She had been invigorated by the good news before the meeting. = 会议前她已经因好消息而精神焕发。
They had invigorated their efforts after the setback. = 遭遇挫折后，他们更加努力。

将来时:
The new marketing campaign will invigorate sales. = 新的营销活动将促进销售增长。
A good night's sleep will invigorate you. = 一夜好眠会让你精力充沛。

虚拟语气:
If I had a good night's sleep, I would feel invigorated. = 如果我睡个好觉，我会感觉精力充沛。
I wish I could find something to invigorate my creative spirit. = 我希望我能找到一些东西来激发我的创作灵感。

被动语态:
The team was invigorated by the victory. = 团队因胜利而士气大振。
He felt invigorated by the fresh mountain air. = 他感到清新的山间空气使他精神焕发。

疑问句:
What can invigorate a tired mind? = 什么能使疲惫的头脑恢复活力？
Does regular exercise invigorate you? = 定期锻炼能让你精力充沛吗？

否定句:
The long journey did not invigorate him. = 漫长的旅程并没有使他精力充沛。
The boring meeting failed to invigorate the participants. = 枯燥的会议未能使与会者精神振奋。

词源:
The word "invigorate" comes from the Latin word "in-" (meaning "in" or "into") and "vigor" (meaning "strength" or "energy").  Therefore, "invigorate" literally means "to put strength into".
