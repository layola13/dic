cupola'd:/ˈkjuːpələd/ | adj. | 有圆屋顶的；带穹顶的 | Having a cupola; adorned with a cupola.  The word is a shortened form of "cupolaed,"  itself derived from "cupola." |例句：The cupola'd church stood majestically on the hill.  山丘上庄严地矗立着一座带圆屋顶的教堂。

The small cupola'd building housed a valuable collection of maps.  那座带小圆屋顶的小建筑收藏着珍贵的藏图。| 近义词：domed, vaulted | 反义词：flat-roofed
