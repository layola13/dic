dashboard:/ˈdæʃˌbɔːrd/ | n. | 仪表盘；控制面板 | A dashboard is a control panel, usually found in a vehicle, providing key information to the operator at a glance. It typically displays speed, fuel level, engine temperature, and other vital data.  In modern usage, it also refers to similar interfaces in software applications, providing an overview of key metrics and information. | Example:  The dashboard showed the car was low on fuel. 仪表盘显示汽车油量不足。 The marketing dashboard provides a real-time overview of sales figures. 市场营销控制面板提供销售数据的实时概述。 |近义词: control panel, instrument panel, console | 反义词: None  | 名词
