impala:/ɪmˈpɑːlə/| n.| 羚羊，(尤指)黑斑羚 |A medium-sized antelope of central and southern Africa, noted for its graceful build and reddish-brown coat.  The word is borrowed from the Setswana language. |例句：The impala gracefully leaped over the bush.  那只黑斑羚优雅地跳过了灌木丛。

例句：We saw a herd of impala grazing peacefully in the savanna. 我们看到一群黑斑羚在草原上平静地吃草。


近义词：antelope, gazelle


反义词：None (no direct antonym)


词性: noun
