ergotism:/ˈɜːrɡəˌtɪzəm/| n. |麦角中毒；麦角病 |A disease caused by eating grains infected with the fungus Claviceps purpurea, which produces toxic alkaloids.  Symptoms can include gangrene, hallucinations, convulsions, and mental disturbances.  Historically, ergotism was a significant problem in areas relying heavily on rye for food. |The Salem witch trials are believed by some historians to have been influenced by ergotism among the accusers.|近义词：ergot poisoning |反义词:None specifically, but 'healthy state' could be considered an antonym.|名词
