oncoming:/ˈɑːnkʌmɪŋ/| adj.|即将到来的，迎面而来的|Approaching; coming toward you.  The word implies movement towards the speaker or observer.|例句：The oncoming traffic was heavy. 迎面而来的车辆很多。

例句：Be careful of the oncoming car. 注意迎面驶来的汽车。


近义词：approaching, approaching, imminent, impending|反义词：outgoing, receding, departing
