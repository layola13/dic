mop:/mɒp/| /mɑːp/ | n., v. | 拖把；用拖把拖；擦拭 | A mop is a cleaning implement consisting of a handle with absorbent material attached to the end, used for cleaning floors.  To mop is to clean (a floor or surface) with a mop. |

例句：

She mopped the kitchen floor. = 她拖了厨房的地板。

I need a new mop; this one is worn out. = 我需要一把新的拖把；这把旧了。
