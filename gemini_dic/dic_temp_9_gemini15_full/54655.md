disobeisance:/ˌdɪsəˈbiːdiəns/| n. |不服从；违抗；抗命|The act of disobeying; refusal to obey.  It often implies a deliberate and conscious disregard for authority or rules.|例句：His disobeisance of the law led to his arrest.  他的违法行为导致了他被捕。
例句：The children's disobeisance resulted in a loss of privileges. 孩子们的违抗导致他们失去了特权。|近义词：rebellion, defiance, insubordination, disobedience|反义词：obedience, compliance, submission|
