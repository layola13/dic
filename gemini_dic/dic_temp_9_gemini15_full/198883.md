sidewalk:/ˈsaɪdwɔːk/ | n. |人行道| A paved path for pedestrians, usually at the side of a road.  | Example:  I walked on the sidewalk to avoid the traffic. 我走在人行道上以避开交通。| 近义词：pavement (英国英语), walkway, footway | 反义词：roadway, carriageway


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The children play on the sidewalk after school. = 放学后孩子们在人行道上玩耍。
People walk their dogs on the sidewalk every morning. = 人们每天早上都在人行道上遛狗。

一般过去时:
We walked along the sidewalk to get to the park. = 我们沿着人行道走到公园。
She slipped on the icy sidewalk yesterday. = 她昨天在结冰的人行道上滑倒了。

现在完成时:
They have repaved the sidewalk in front of their house. = 他们已经重新铺设了他们家门前的人行道。
I have never seen such a wide sidewalk before. = 我以前从未见过这么宽的人行道。

过去完成时:
The accident had happened on the sidewalk before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，事故已经发生在人行道上了。
She had already crossed the sidewalk when the car sped past. = 汽车飞驰而过时，她已经走过了人行道。


将来时:
We will walk on the sidewalk to avoid the construction. = 我们将走在人行道上以避开施工。
They will repair the damaged sidewalk next week. = 他们下周将修理损坏的人行道。

虚拟语气:
If the sidewalk were wider, more people could walk comfortably. = 如果人行道更宽，更多的人可以舒适地行走。
I wish the sidewalk wasn't so crowded. = 我希望人行道不要这么拥挤。

被动语态:
The sidewalk is being repaired at the moment. = 人行道正在维修中。
The sidewalk was damaged by the recent storm. = 人行道在最近的风暴中受损。

疑问句:
Is this sidewalk safe for children to play on? = 人行道适合孩子们玩耍吗？
Where is the sidewalk leading to? = 这条人行道通向哪里？

否定句:
There is no sidewalk on this street. = 这条街上没有人行道。
He didn't walk on the sidewalk; he walked in the street. = 他没有走在人行道上；他走在街上。
