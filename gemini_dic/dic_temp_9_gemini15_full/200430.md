slackingly: /ˈslækɪŋli/ | adv. | 松懈地；懈怠地 | In a careless or negligent manner; without sufficient effort or energy.  |例句：He completed the task slackingly, resulting in many errors. 他松懈地完成了任务，导致许多错误。


例句：She approached her studies slackingly, leading to poor grades. 她学习态度懈怠，导致成绩很差。


近义词：negligently, carelessly, lazily, perfunctorily, remissly


反义词：diligently, carefully, attentively, meticulously, assiduously


词源:  Derived from the adverbial form of "slack," meaning relaxed or loose. The "-ingly" suffix indicates a manner or way of doing something.  The root word "slack" originates from Old English *slacc, meaning "loose, slow."
