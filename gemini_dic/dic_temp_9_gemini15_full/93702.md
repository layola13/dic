imitator: /ˈɪmɪteɪtər/ | n. | 模仿者，仿造者 | A person or thing that imitates.  Someone who copies the behavior or style of another person. | Example: He's a skilled imitator of famous actors. 他是一位模仿著名演员的高手。  |近义词：copycat, mimic, impersonator |反义词：original, innovator


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird imitates the sounds of other birds. = 这只鸟模仿其他鸟类的叫声。
She imitates her teacher's style of teaching. = 她模仿她老师的教学风格。


一般过去时:
He imitated his father's mannerisms. = 他模仿了他父亲的举止习惯。
The student imitated the example in the textbook. = 学生模仿了课本中的例子。


现在完成时:
She has imitated many famous paintings. = 她已经模仿了许多名画。
The artist has imitated various artistic styles throughout his career. =这位艺术家在他职业生涯中模仿了各种艺术风格。


过去完成时:
He had imitated that famous singer for years before he found his own style. = 在找到自己的风格之前，他模仿那位著名歌手多年了。
The child had imitated her older sister's behavior since she was little. = 这个孩子从小就模仿她姐姐的行为。


将来时:
He will imitate the style of the famous painter in his next project. = 他将在下一个项目中模仿这位著名画家的风格。
The company will imitate the successful strategies of its competitors. = 这家公司将模仿其竞争对手的成功策略。


虚拟语气:
If he imitated her more closely, he might succeed. = 如果他更仔细地模仿她，他可能会成功。
I wish I could imitate her talent for painting. = 我真希望我能模仿她绘画的天赋。


被动语态:
His style was imitated by many young artists. = 他的风格被许多年轻艺术家模仿。
The design has been imitated by several other companies. = 该设计已被几家其他公司模仿。


疑问句:
Does he often imitate others? = 他经常模仿别人吗？
Who is she imitating in that performance? = 在那场演出中她在模仿谁？


否定句:
He doesn't imitate anyone; he's original. = 他不模仿任何人；他是原创的。
She didn't imitate the speaker's tone. = 她没有模仿演讲者的语气。


PHRASE->
a skilled imitator = 一个熟练的模仿者
a close imitator = 一个非常相似的模仿者
a poor imitator = 一个拙劣的模仿者
an unconscious imitator = 一个无意识的模仿者
a clever imitator = 一个聪明的模仿者
