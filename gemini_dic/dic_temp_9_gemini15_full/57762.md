dreamlessness: /ˈdriːmləsnəs/ | n. | 无梦;缺乏梦想;缺乏活力 | The state of not having dreams, either literally or figuratively. It can refer to the absence of dreams during sleep, or the lack of ambition, aspiration, or imagination in one's life.  | Example:  The medication caused dreamlessness.  这种药物导致了无梦睡眠。 The dreamlessness of his existence was palpable. 他生活中缺乏梦想是显而易见的。 | 近义词：lack of dreams, aspirationlessness, listlessness, apathy | 反义词：vivid dreaming, ambition, aspiration, imagination |
