extracorporeal:/ˌɛkstrəˈkɔːrpəriəl/ | adj. | 体外的；体外循环的 | Outside the body; performed outside the body.  | Example: Extracorporeal membrane oxygenation (ECMO) is a life support technique. 体外膜肺氧合 (ECMO) 是一种生命支持技术。

Example: The surgery involved an extracorporeal procedure to repair the damaged artery.  手术包括一个体外手术来修复受损的动脉。


近义词：external, outside

反义词：intracorporeal, internal


词性：形容词
