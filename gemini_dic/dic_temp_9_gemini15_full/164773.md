preferredly:/prɪˈfɜːrəbli/| adv. |最好地，更可取地，理想地 | Preferably, we should finish the project by Friday. |近义词：ideally, optimally, desirably | 反义词：undesirably, unfavorably


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We preferably choose the red one. = 我们最好选择红色的那个。
They preferably work from home. = 他们最好在家工作。


一般过去时:
We preferably chose the red one. = 我们最好选择了红色的那个。
They preferably worked from home. = 他们最好在家工作。


现在完成时:
We have preferably chosen the red one. = 我们最好已经选择了红色的那个。
They have preferably worked from home. = 他们最好一直在家里工作。


过去完成时:
We had preferably chosen the red one. = 我们最好已经选择了红色的那个。(强调选择发生在过去某个时间点之前)
They had preferably worked from home. = 他们最好一直在家里工作。(强调在家工作发生在过去某个时间点之前)


将来时:
We will preferably choose the red one. = 我们最好会选择红色的那个。
They will preferably work from home. = 他们最好会在家里工作。


虚拟语气:
It would be preferably to finish the report today. = 最好今天完成报告。
I wish it were preferably to have a longer vacation. = 我希望最好能有更长的假期。


被动语态:
The red one is preferably chosen. = 红色的是最好选择。
Working from home is preferably done. = 最好在家工作。


疑问句:
Would it be preferably to meet tomorrow? = 明天见面比较好么？
Is it preferably to call or email? = 打电话还是发邮件比较好？


否定句:
We don't preferably use that method. = 我们最好不要用那种方法。
They didn't preferably go there. = 他们最好没去那里。


PHRASE->
preferably if = 如果最好
preferably than = 比...更好
preferably to = 最好...
preferably with = 最好与...一起
preferably without = 最好没有...
