collation:/kəˈleɪʃən/| n. |校对；整理；对照；排序|The act of collating or the state of being collated;  the arrangement of things in order, especially in a systematic way;  a comparison of different things to establish similarities or differences.|例句：The collation of the data took several weeks. 数据整理花了几个星期。
例句：The collation of the manuscripts revealed several discrepancies. 手稿的校对揭示了一些差异。|近义词：arrangement, organization, ordering, sorting|反义词：disorder, disarray, chaos


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He collates the documents every week. 他每周整理文件。
The librarian collates the books regularly. 图书管理员定期整理书籍。

一般过去时:
She collated the research findings yesterday. 她昨天整理了研究结果。
They collated the election results last night. 他们昨晚整理了选举结果。

现在完成时:
We have collated all the necessary information. 我们已经整理了所有必要的信息。
The team has collated the data for the report. 团队已经整理了报告的数据。

过去完成时:
He had collated the evidence before presenting his case. 他在陈述案情之前已经整理了证据。
They had collated the responses before analyzing the survey. 他们在分析调查之前已经整理了回复。

将来时:
She will collate the documents tomorrow. 她明天将整理文件。
They will collate the data next week. 他们下周将整理数据。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would collate the entire collection. 如果我有更多时间，我会整理整个收藏。
It is important that the information be collated carefully.  重要的是要仔细整理信息。

被动语态:
The documents were collated by the assistant. 文件由助理整理。
The data is being collated for the upcoming presentation. 数据正在被整理，用于即将到来的演示。

疑问句:
Have you collated the results yet? 你已经整理结果了吗？
Will the data be collated before the meeting? 数据会在会议前整理吗？

否定句:
He did not collate the information properly. 他没有正确地整理信息。
They haven't yet collated all the responses. 他们还没有整理所有回复。


PHRASE->

词源:
From Old French *colacion, from Latin collatio, from com- ("together") + latus, past participle of ferre ("to carry").  It originally referred to the act of bringing things together, later developing its current meaning.
