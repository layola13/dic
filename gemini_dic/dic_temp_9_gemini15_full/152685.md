pear:/pɛər/| n. |梨；梨树|A pear is a sweet, juicy fruit that grows on a pear tree.  It is typically oval-shaped and has a smooth skin. Pears can be eaten raw, cooked, or used in desserts.|例句：I bought a bag of ripe pears at the market. 我在市场上买了一袋熟透的梨。|例句：The pear tree in our garden is laden with fruit. 我们花园里的梨树上结满了果实。|近义词：fruit, apple |反义词:无
