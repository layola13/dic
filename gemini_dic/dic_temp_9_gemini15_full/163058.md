preclothing: /ˌpriːˈkloʊðɪŋ/ | n. | 预制服装，预先准备的服装 |  Preclothing refers to clothing that is pre-made or pre-prepared, often in a standardized or mass-produced way,  as opposed to custom-made or bespoke clothing. It emphasizes the preparation and readiness of the garment. This term is not commonly used and is more likely to appear in specialized or technical contexts related to manufacturing or supply chain management.  | 例句：The factory specialized in preclothing for the military.  这家工厂专门为军队生产预制服装。  |近义词：ready-to-wear, off-the-rack, mass-produced clothing |反义词： bespoke clothing, custom-made clothing, tailor-made clothing |名词
