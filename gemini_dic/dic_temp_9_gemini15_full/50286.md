depictors:/dɪˈpɪktərz/| n.|描绘者，描写者|Those who depict or represent something visually or narratively.  People or things that visually represent or describe something.|例句：The museum showcased the works of various depictors from different eras.  博物馆展出了不同时代各种描绘者的作品。

例句：The novel's depictors skillfully brought the historical setting to life. 这部小说的描写者们巧妙地将历史背景栩栩如生地展现出来。


近义词：portrayers, illustrators, artists, describers
反义词：None directly opposite, but could consider "obfuscators" (those who obscure or conceal) as a contrast.
词性:名词
