gobble:/ˈɡɑːbl/ /ˈɡɒbl/| vt. & vi.|狼吞虎咽地吃；发出咕咕声|To eat something quickly and noisily; to make a noise like a turkey.|例句：He gobbled his dinner in five minutes. 他五分钟内就狼吞虎咽地吃完了饭。|例句：The turkey gobbled loudly.  那只火鸡大声地咕咕叫。|近义词：devour, gulp, guzzle|反义词：nibble, savor|动词


USAGE->
