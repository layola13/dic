postcrisis:/ˈpoʊstˈkraɪsɪs/| adj.|危机后的，危机过后|Postcrisis refers to the period of time, events, or conditions that follow a crisis.  It implies the aftermath and the ongoing effects of the crisis. |Example: Postcrisis economic recovery proved to be slower than initially anticipated. 危机后的经济复苏证明比最初预期的要慢。  The postcrisis regulatory reforms aimed to prevent future financial meltdowns. 危机后的监管改革旨在防止未来的金融危机。|近义词：post-crisis, after-crisis,  following the crisis|反义词：precrisis, during the crisis|形容词
