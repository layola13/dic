perseveres:/ˌpɜːrsɪˈvɪərz/| v.|坚持不懈；锲而不舍|To continue trying to do or achieve something despite difficulties.|例句：She persevered with her studies despite many setbacks. 她尽管遇到许多挫折，仍然坚持学习。|例句：Despite the challenges, he persevered and finally achieved his goal. 尽管面临挑战，他仍然坚持不懈，最终实现了目标。|近义词：persist, endure, continue|反义词：give up, quit, surrender|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He perseveres in his efforts to learn a new language. = 他坚持努力学习一门外语。
She perseveres even when faced with overwhelming odds. = 即使面对巨大的困难，她也坚持不懈。

一般过去时:
He persevered through the difficult times. = 他坚持度过了艰难时期。
She persevered despite feeling discouraged. = 尽管感到灰心，她仍然坚持不懈。

现在完成时:
He has persevered for many years and finally succeeded. = 他坚持多年，最终成功了。
She has persevered in her artistic pursuits. = 她一直坚持自己的艺术追求。

过去完成时:
He had persevered for months before finally finding a solution. = 他坚持了几个月才最终找到解决方案。
She had persevered through many hardships before achieving her dream. = 在实现梦想之前，她经历了许多磨难并坚持了下来。

将来时:
He will persevere in his quest for knowledge. = 他将坚持不懈地追求知识。
She will persevere no matter what obstacles she faces. = 无论遇到什么障碍，她都会坚持下去。

虚拟语气:
If he had persevered, he would have achieved his goal. = 如果他坚持下去，他就会实现目标。
I wish I had persevered with my studies. = 我真希望我坚持学习。

被动语态:  (Persevere is rarely used in the passive voice because it is an intransitive verb)  This section is omitted.

疑问句:
Will he persevere in the face of adversity? = 面对逆境，他会坚持吗？
Does she persevere in her efforts? = 她是否坚持自己的努力？

否定句:
He did not persevere in his attempts to climb the mountain. = 他没有坚持尝试攀登那座山。
She did not persevere with her project, and it was left unfinished. = 她没有坚持完成她的项目，项目最终没有完成。
