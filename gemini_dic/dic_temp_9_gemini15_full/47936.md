decharm:/diːˈtʃɑːrm/| vt.|解除魅力；使不再有魅力；使…失去吸引力|To remove the charm or attractiveness from something or someone.  To disenchant.|例句：The magician's act decharmed the audience after the initial excitement wore off. 魔术师的表演在最初的兴奋过后失去了吸引力。

例句：The harsh reality of war decharmed the romantic notion of heroism.  战争的残酷现实使英雄主义的浪漫观念失去了魅力。


近义词：disenchant, disenthrall, debunk, defuse


反义词：enchant, charm, fascinate

词性：动词
