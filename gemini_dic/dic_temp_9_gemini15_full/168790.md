/ˈprɒdjuːs/ | /prəˈduːs/ | vt. | 生产；制造；创作；导致；出示；上演 | To bring forth or create something; to make something happen; to show or present something. | The factory produces cars.  工厂生产汽车。  | They produced a wonderful play. 他们创作了一部精彩的戏剧。 | create, manufacture, generate | destroy, consume, halt | v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory produces cars. = 工厂生产汽车。
She produces beautiful paintings. = 她创作美丽的绘画作品。


一般过去时:
The company produced a new product last year. = 公司去年生产了一种新产品。
He produced his ID card to the police officer. = 他向警官出示了他的身份证。


现在完成时:
They have produced several successful films. = 他们已经制作了几部成功的电影。
The drought has produced a serious water shortage. = 干旱导致了严重的缺水。


过去完成时:
The company had produced thousands of units before the order was cancelled. = 订单取消前，公司已经生产了数千个单位。
She had produced evidence supporting her claim. = 她已经提供了证据来支持她的说法。


将来时:
We will produce a report on the findings next week. = 我们下周将就调查结果提交一份报告。
The new technology will produce significant improvements. = 新技术将带来显著的改进。


虚拟语气:
If they produced more goods, they would make more profit. = 如果他们生产更多商品，他们就会赚取更多利润。
I wish they had produced a better plan. = 我希望他们制定一个更好的计划。


被动语态:
The cars are produced in Germany. = 这些汽车是在德国生产的。
The play was produced on Broadway. = 这部戏在百老汇上演。


疑问句:
What does this factory produce? = 这个工厂生产什么？
Who produced this film? = 谁制作了这部电影？


否定句:
The farmer didn't produce enough crops this year. = 今年农民没有生产足够的农作物。
The company failed to produce the required documents. = 公司未能提供所需的文档。
