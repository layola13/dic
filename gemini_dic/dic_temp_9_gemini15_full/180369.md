refurnishes:/ˌriːˈfɜːrnɪʃɪz/| vt.|重新装修；重新布置|To furnish again; to provide with new furniture or fittings.|The landlord refurnishes the apartment every five years.  房东每五年重新装修一次公寓。|They refurnished the house with antique furniture.  他们用古董家具重新布置了房子。|furnish, renovate, redecorate|strip, dismantle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refurnishes his office every year. 他每年都重新装修他的办公室。
She refurnishes her apartment with modern furniture. 她用现代家具重新布置她的公寓。


一般过去时:
They refurnished the hotel last month.  他们上个月重新装修了酒店。
We refurnished our living room after the flood.  洪水过后我们重新布置了客厅。


现在完成时:
She has refurnished her bedroom twice this year. 她今年已经两次重新布置了她的卧室。
They have refurnished the entire house since they moved in.自从他们搬进来后，他们已经重新装修了整栋房子。


过去完成时:
They had refurnished the kitchen before the party.  他们在派对之前已经重新装修了厨房。
She had refurnished the apartment before her parents visited.在她父母来访之前，她已经重新装修了公寓。


将来时:
We will refurnish the bathroom next spring.  我们明年春天将重新装修浴室。
He will refurnish his study with new bookshelves. 他将用新的书架重新布置他的书房。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would refurnish my house. 如果我有钱，我会重新装修我的房子。
If she were to refurnish the living room, she would choose a modern style.如果她要重新装修客厅，她会选择现代风格。


被动语态:

疑问句:
