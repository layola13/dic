readmittance:/ˌriːˈædmɪtəns/| n.|准许重新进入；复原；再接纳|The act of admitting someone or something again;  re-entry; restoration to a former position or state.|例句：After a period of suspension, he applied for readmittance to the club.  一段时间停赛后，他申请重返俱乐部。

例句：Her readmittance to the hospital was necessary after her condition worsened.  她的病情恶化后，必须再次入院。


近义词：re-entry, reinstatement, readmission|反义词：exclusion, expulsion, rejection|名词
