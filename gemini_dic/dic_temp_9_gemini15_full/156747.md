pierced:/pɪrst/ | vt. adj. | 穿孔的；刺穿的；强烈的；尖锐的 | To make a hole through something with a sharp object;  having a hole made by piercing. |


英文解析:The verb "pierced" means to make a hole through something using a pointed object. As an adjective, it describes something that has a hole made in it by piercing, or a strong, sharp feeling or sensation.


例句:

The needle pierced the fabric easily. = 针轻松地刺穿了布料。

The sharp pain pierced my heart. = 剧烈的疼痛刺痛了我的心。


近义词:punctured, perforated, penetrated


反义词:sealed, closed, unbroken


词性: Verb (transitive), Adjective
