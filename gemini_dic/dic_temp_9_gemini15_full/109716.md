leveled:/ˈlevəld/| v. adj.| 使平坦；使夷为平地；达到；分级；使…平等；(级别，水平)相同的 |  to make something flat or level; to reach a particular level or standard;  to divide something into levels or grades; to make things equal. |

例句：
The builders leveled the ground before they started construction.  建筑工人动工前先把地整平了。

The playing field was leveled to ensure a fair game.  为了确保比赛公平，比赛场地被弄平了。


近义词：flattened, evened, aligned, standardized

反义词：unevened, roughened, raised, tiered


词性：动词，形容词
