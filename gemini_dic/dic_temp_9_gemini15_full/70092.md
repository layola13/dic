fevered:/ˈfiːvərd/| adj.|发烧的；狂热的；兴奋的|Characterized by or showing the symptoms of a fever; showing intense or excessive emotion or excitement.|例句：He was feverish and restless. 他发烧且烦躁不安。

例句：The feverish atmosphere of the election campaign was palpable.  选举活动的狂热气氛是显而易见的。


近义词：hot, feverish, excited, passionate|反义词：calm, cool, apathetic, indifferent|词性：adj.


词源:源自fever（发烧）+ -ed (形容词后缀), 表示具有发烧的特征。


USAGE->

The patient is feverish. = 病人发烧。
He feels feverish today. = 他今天感觉发烧。


一般过去时:
She was feverish last night. = 她昨晚发烧了。
The crowd was feverish with excitement. = 人群兴奋地沸腾了。


现在完成时:

虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:
