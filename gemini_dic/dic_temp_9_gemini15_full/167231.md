preterite:/ˈprɛtəraɪt/| adj. n.|过去时态的；过去式|The preterite is a grammatical tense used to describe actions or states that occurred in the past.  It's often used to contrast with the present or future tense.  In some languages, the preterite is used to express completed actions in the past, while other tenses are used for ongoing or habitual actions in the past.  English uses the simple past tense, which is often referred to as the preterite.|The dog barked loudly. (The barking is a completed action in the past.)|past simple, simple past|present, future|adj. n.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He walks to school every day. = 他每天步行去上学。
She sings beautifully. = 她唱歌很美。


一般过去时:
He walked to school yesterday. = 他昨天步行去上学。
She sang beautifully at the concert. = 她在音乐会上唱得很好听。


现在完成时:
He has walked to school many times. = 他去学校步行过很多次了。
She has sung in many concerts. = 她参加过很多音乐会的演出。


过去完成时:
He had walked to school before it started to rain. = 下雨之前他已经步行去学校了。
She had sung that song many times before the competition. = 比赛之前她已经唱过很多次那首歌了。


将来时:
He will walk to school tomorrow. = 他明天将步行去学校。
She will sing at the next concert. = 她将在下一场音乐会上唱歌。


虚拟语气:
If he had walked faster, he would have arrived on time. = 如果他走得快一点，他就能按时到达了。
If she had sung better, she would have won the competition. = 如果她唱得更好，她就能赢得比赛了。


被动语态:
The letter was written yesterday. = 这封信是昨天写的。
The song was sung by a famous singer. = 这首歌是由一位著名的歌手演唱的。


疑问句:
Did he walk to school? = 他步行去学校了吗？
Did she sing at the concert? = 她参加音乐会的演出了吗？


否定句:
He did not walk to school today. = 他今天没有步行去学校。
She did not sing at the concert last night. = 她昨晚没有在音乐会上唱歌。
