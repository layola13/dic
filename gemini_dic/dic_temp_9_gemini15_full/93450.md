illumining: /ɪˈluːmɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 启迪的；阐明的；照亮的 | Giving light; enlightening; clarifying.  | The illumining essay shed light on a complex issue. 这篇启迪性的文章阐明了一个复杂的问题。 | enlightening, clarifying, illuminating, insightful | obscure, confusing, perplexing |  形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun is illumining the garden. = 太阳照亮着花园。
His explanation is illumining the problem. = 他的解释阐明了这个问题。


一般过去时:
The moon illumined the night sky. = 月亮照亮了夜空。
The lecture illumined the topic for the students. = 讲座使学生们明白了这个主题。


现在完成时:
The research has illumined the causes of the disease. = 这项研究已经阐明了这种疾病的病因。
Her words have illumined my understanding of the situation. = 她的言语使我对局势有了更清晰的理解。


过去完成时:
The discovery had illumined the mystery surrounding the ancient civilization. = 这一发现阐明了围绕古代文明的神秘面纱。
His previous work had illumined the path for future researchers. = 他以前的工作为未来的研究者指明了道路。


将来时:
The new technology will illumine many aspects of our lives. = 新技术将照亮我们生活的许多方面。
The upcoming report will illumine the financial situation of the company. = 即将来临的报告将阐明公司的财务状况。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence illumined the truth, we would know what happened. = 如果证据能阐明真相，我们就会知道发生了什么。
I wish the instructions illumined the process better. = 我希望说明书能更好地阐明这个过程。


被动语态:
The room was illumined by the soft candlelight. = 房间被柔和的烛光照亮。
The mystery was illumined by the detective's astute observations. = 这个谜团被侦探敏锐的观察所阐明。


疑问句:

Will the new findings illumine the path toward a cure? = 新的发现能否照亮通向治愈的道路？


否定句:
The explanation did not illumine the situation at all. = 这个解释根本没有阐明情况。
The darkness was not illumined by any light source. = 黑暗没有任何光源照亮。
