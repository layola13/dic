indestructible: /ˌɪnɪˈstrʌktəbl/ | adj. | 不可摧毁的，坚不可摧的 |  Something that is indestructible cannot be destroyed or broken. It is extremely strong and resistant to damage.  | Example:  The diamond is almost indestructible.  这颗钻石几乎坚不可摧。

The fortress was built to be indestructible. 这座堡垒是建成坚不可摧的。|  Synonyms:  unbreakable, invincible, impervious, irreplaceable | Antonyms:  breakable, fragile, vulnerable, destructible |
