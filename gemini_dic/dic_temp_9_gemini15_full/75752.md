fulmars:/ˈfʊlmɑːrz/| n.|海燕属鸟类|A fulmar is a seabird of the family Procellariidae, characterized by its gliding flight and tube-like nostrils.  They are typically found in the North Atlantic and North Pacific Oceans. They are known for their ability to survive on the open ocean for extended periods.|Example: We saw many fulmars soaring effortlessly over the waves.  我们看到许多海燕毫不费力地在波浪上空翱翔。|近义词：petrel, shearwater|反义词：无
