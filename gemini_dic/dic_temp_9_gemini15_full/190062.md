sandstorm:/ˈsændˌstɔːrm/| n. |沙尘暴|A sandstorm is a strong wind that carries a large amount of sand and dust, reducing visibility significantly.  Sandstorms are common in deserts and arid regions. |例句：The sandstorm reduced visibility to almost zero. 沙尘暴导致能见度几乎为零。

例句：We were caught in a fierce sandstorm during our desert trek.  我们在沙漠旅行途中遭遇了猛烈的沙尘暴。


近义词：dust storm, sand blizzard


反义词：calm, clear weather


词性：名词
