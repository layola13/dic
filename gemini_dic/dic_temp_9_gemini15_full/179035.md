redemocratizations:/ˌriːdɪˌmɒkrətaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 再民主化；重新民主化 | The process of restoring democratic systems or principles after a period of authoritarian rule.  It involves the reinstatement of civil liberties, free and fair elections, and independent institutions.  Multiple instances of this process are implied by the plural form. | Example: The redemocratizations in Eastern Europe after the fall of the Soviet Union were complex and varied.  东欧在苏联解体后的再民主化进程复杂且多样化。  The recent redemocratizations in several African countries are fragile and require ongoing support.  几个非洲国家最近的再民主化进程非常脆弱，需要持续的支持。 | 近义词：restorations of democracy, democratic transitions | 反义词：authoritarian consolidations, dictatorial regimes | 名词
