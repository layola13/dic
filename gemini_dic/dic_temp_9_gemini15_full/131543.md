They are usually short films or announcements focusing on important issues. |Example:  Many television stations air noncommercials during the evening news to raise awareness about local charities.  许多电视台在晚间新闻期间播放公益广告，以提高人们对当地慈善机构的认识。|近义词：public service announcements (PSAs),  infomercials (if promoting a cause, not a product)|反义词：commercials, advertisements|名词
