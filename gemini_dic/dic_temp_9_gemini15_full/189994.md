sandalwoods:/ˈsændəlˌwʊdz/| n. |檀香木（复数）|The fragrant wood of various trees of the genus Santalum, used in perfumes, incense, and carvings.  Sandalwoods are highly valued for their aroma and are often used in religious ceremonies.| 例句：The artisan carefully carved the sandalwood into a beautiful statue.  工匠细致地将檀香木雕刻成一尊美丽的雕像。

The temple was filled with the sweet scent of burning sandalwood. 寺庙里弥漫着燃烧的檀香木的香味。

|近义词：sandalwood (单数), fragrant woods|反义词：None specifically, but words implying lack of fragrance could be considered antonyms.  |名词|
