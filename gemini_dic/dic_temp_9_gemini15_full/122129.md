mistranslates:/ˌmɪsˈtrænzleɪts/ | vt. | 误译，曲解 | To translate incorrectly or inaccurately; to misinterpret.  |例句：The software mistranslates many idioms.  该软件误译了许多习语。

例句：He mistranslated the poem, losing much of its original meaning. 他误译了这首诗，失去了许多原意。


近义词：misinterpret, misrender, paraphrase incorrectly


反义词：translate accurately, interpret correctly


词性:动词
