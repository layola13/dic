gonorrhoea:/ˌɡɒnəˈriːə/| n. |淋病|A sexually transmitted infection (STI) caused by the bacterium Neisseria gonorrhoeae.  It can infect the genitals, rectum, and throat.  Symptoms vary, but can include pain during urination, discharge from the penis or vagina, and abdominal pain. If left untreated, it can lead to serious health problems. |Example:  The doctor confirmed that he had contracted gonorrhoea. 医生确诊他患上了淋病。

She was diagnosed with gonorrhoea after experiencing unusual vaginal discharge.  她经历了异常的阴道分泌物后被诊断出患有淋病。|近义词：gonococcal infection |反义词：无
