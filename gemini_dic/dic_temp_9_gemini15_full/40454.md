contravened:/ˌkɒntrəˈviːnd/| /ˌkɑːntrəˈviːnd/ | v. |违反，违背，抵触|To contravene something means to go against or violate it, usually a law, rule, or agreement.  It implies a deliberate or intentional act of breaking the rules.|The company contravened safety regulations, resulting in a fine. 公司违反了安全条例，导致被罚款。

His actions contravened the principles of fairness. 他的行为违反了公平原则。|violate, infringe, breach, transgress|obey, comply, conform|动词
