plasmon:/ˈplæzmɒn/| n. |等离子体,等离子体激元|A plasmon is a quantum of plasma oscillation, a collective oscillation of the free electron gas density in a solid.  It's a quasiparticle resulting from the quantization of plasma oscillations. Plasmons are excited by light interacting with the free electrons in a material, leading to various phenomena like surface plasmon resonance (SPR) and localized surface plasmon resonance (LSPR).|Example: The interaction of light with the plasmon in the gold nanoparticles resulted in a significant color change.|近义词：surface plasmon, collective excitation|反义词：N/A
