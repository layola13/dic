preapply:/priːəˈplaɪ/ | vt. | 预先申请，提前申请 | To apply beforehand or in advance.  | 例句：I decided to preapply for the scholarship to avoid the last-minute rush. 我决定提前申请奖学金以避免最后一刻的匆忙。

例句：Many students preapply for university housing to secure their preferred accommodations. 许多学生提前申请大学宿舍以确保获得他们偏爱的住宿。


近义词：apply in advance, apply early | 反义词：postpone application, delay application | 词性：动词
