checkin:/ˈtʃɛkɪn/| n. v.|登记入住；报到；检票|The act of registering at a hotel, airport, etc., or the process of registering someone or something.  It can also refer to a brief inspection or examination.|例句：I need to check in at the airport two hours before my flight. 我需要在航班起飞前两小时在机场办理登机手续。
例句：The doctor performed a quick checkin on the patient. 医生对病人进行了简单的检查。|近义词：registration, arrival, sign-in, check-up|反义词：checkout, departure


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I check in at the hotel every year. = 我每年都在这家酒店登记入住。
We check in our luggage before boarding the plane. = 我们登机前托运行李。

一般过去时:
They checked in at the reception desk. = 他们在大堂办理了入住手续。
He checked in his bag at the airport. = 他在机场托运了他的行李。

现在完成时:
I have checked in online for my flight. = 我已经为我的航班进行了网上值机。
She has checked in her suitcase. = 她已经托运了她的手提箱。

过去完成时:
They had checked in their luggage before the flight was delayed. = 在航班延误之前，他们已经托运了行李。
We had checked into the hotel before the rain started. = 下雨开始之前，我们已经入住酒店了。

将来时:
I will check in at the hotel tomorrow. = 我明天将在酒店办理入住手续。
We will check in our bags at the designated counter. = 我们将在指定柜台托运行李。

虚拟语气:
If I had checked in online, I wouldn't have had to wait in line. = 如果我进行了网上值机，我就不用排队了。
I wish I had checked in earlier; now the flight is full. = 我真希望我早点办理登机手续；现在航班已经客满了。

被动语态:
Passengers are checked in by the staff. = 旅客由工作人员办理登机手续。
The luggage was checked in efficiently. = 行李被高效地办理了托运。

疑问句:
Have you checked in yet? = 你登记入住了吗？
When do we need to check in for the flight? = 我们什么时候需要办理航班登机手续？

否定句:
I didn't check in online. = 我没有进行网上值机。
They haven't checked in their luggage yet. = 他们还没有托运行李。
