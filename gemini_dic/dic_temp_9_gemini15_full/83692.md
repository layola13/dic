haggish:/ˈhæɡɪʃ/| adj.| 巫婆似的；丑陋的；令人厌恶的 |  Having the characteristics of a hag; ugly, unpleasant, and repulsive.  The word evokes images of an old, withered woman, often associated with witchcraft or malevolence. | Example:  The haggish woman glared at the children.  这名巫婆似的女人怒视着孩子们。

The haggish smell coming from the dumpster was unbearable.  垃圾桶散发出的令人作呕的气味令人难以忍受。 | 近义词：ugly, repulsive, unpleasant, witchlike, forbidding | 反义词：beautiful, attractive, pleasant, charming
