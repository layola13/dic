nondocumentaries:/ˌnɑːnˌdɑːkjuˈmɛntəriz/| n.|非纪录片|Films or television programs that are not documentaries; fictional or narrative films.  They are characterized by storytelling, fictional characters, and narratives that are not based on real events. |Example:  Many popular films are nondocumentaries, focusing on fictional stories and characters. 许多热门电影都是非纪录片，专注于虚构的故事和人物。  The genre of nondocumentaries includes action, comedy, drama, and science fiction. 非纪录片类型包括动作片、喜剧片、剧情片和科幻片。|近义词：fiction films, narrative films, feature films|反义词：documentaries|名词
