misperceived:/ˌmɪspərˈsiːvd/| adj.|误解的，被误认为的|Something that is misperceived is understood or interpreted incorrectly.  It implies a failure to grasp the true meaning or nature of something.|The artist's work was initially misperceived as simplistic, but later critics recognized its profound depth.|近义词：misunderstood, misinterpreted, wrongly perceived|反义词：understood, correctly perceived
