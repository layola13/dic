manslaughtered:/ˈmænˌslɔːtərd/ | v. | 犯过失杀人罪 |  To kill someone without intending to do so, but in circumstances showing a lack of reasonable care.  This is a legal term referring to the unlawful killing of a human being without malice aforethought.  | 例句：He was manslaughtered in a car accident. 他死于一起车祸中的过失杀人。 The driver was charged with manslaughter after the accident.  司机在事故后被控犯有误杀罪。| 近义词：unintentionally killed, negligently killed | 反义词：murdered, assassinated
