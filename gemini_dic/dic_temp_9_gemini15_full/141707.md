oracle:/ˈɔːrəkl/| n. |神谕，预言；预言家；权威人士；(电脑) 甲骨文数据库| a person or thing that can foresee the future; a prophecy or prediction;  a person or organization that is a reliable source of information; Oracle Database (a relational database management system)|例句：The oracle predicted a great victory.  神谕预言了一场伟大的胜利。|例句：She consulted an oracle about her future. 她向一位预言家咨询了她的未来。|近义词：prophet, seer, augur, authority, expert |反义词：ignoramus, novice
