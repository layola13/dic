non-appearance: /ˌnɒnəˈpɪərəns/ | n. | 不出现，缺席 | The absence of someone or something expected to be present.  It can refer to the failure to appear in court, at a meeting, or in any other situation where presence is anticipated. |例句：His non-appearance at the trial raised suspicions.  他的缺席审判引起了怀疑。

His non-appearance at the meeting delayed the project. 他的缺席会议延误了项目。


近义词：absence, default, non-attendance, failure to appear | 反义词：presence, attendance, appearance

词性：名词
