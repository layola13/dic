gorse:/ɡɔːrs/| n.|荆棘；金雀花|A prickly evergreen shrub of the pea family, with yellow flowers.  It is common in Europe and western Asia.|例句：The hills were covered in a dense gorse bush.  山丘上覆盖着茂密的荆棘丛。|例句：The bright yellow gorse flowers added a splash of color to the landscape. 金黄色的金雀花为这片风景增添了一抹色彩。|近义词：whin, furze|反义词：无
