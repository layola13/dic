overwalk:/ˈoʊvərˌwɔːk/ | v. |过度行走；走得太久|To walk too far or for too long a time, often resulting in tiredness or injury. |例句：I overwalked yesterday and now my feet are killing me. 昨天我走了太久，现在我的脚疼死了。|近义词：overexert, overtire |反义词：rest, relax |动词

词源:
The word "overwalk" is a combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") and the verb "walk."  It's a relatively straightforward derivation reflecting the meaning of excessive walking.
