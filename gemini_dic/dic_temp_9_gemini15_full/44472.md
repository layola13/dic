crossbite:/ˈkrɒsbaɪt/| n.| 反颌，交叉咬合|A crossbite is a type of malocclusion where the upper teeth are positioned inside the lower teeth, or vice versa, when the jaws are closed.  This can affect one or more teeth.  It can be caused by various factors, including genetics, thumb-sucking, and oral habits.|例句：The orthodontist diagnosed her with an anterior crossbite.  正畸医生诊断她患有前牙反颌。|例句：He needed braces to correct his crossbite. 他需要戴牙套来矫正他的反颌。|近义词：malocclusion, dental misalignment|反义词：overbite, underbite
