deception:/dɪˈsɛpʃən/| n. |欺骗；骗局；诡计 |The act of deceiving someone; the state of being deceived.  It involves misleading or tricking someone into believing something false. |例句：His deception was uncovered when the police found the stolen goods.  他的欺骗行为在警方找到赃物时被揭露了。

例句：The magician's deception was so clever that no one saw how he did it. 魔术师的骗术如此巧妙，没有人看出他是怎么做到的。


近义词：fraud, trickery, deceit, illusion, sham |反义词：truth, honesty, frankness, veracity |词性:名词
