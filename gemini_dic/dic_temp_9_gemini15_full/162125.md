pre-Easter: /ˌpriːˈiːstər/ | adj. |复活节前的 |  Referring to the period of time immediately before Easter.  It describes events, activities, or preparations that take place in the days or weeks leading up to Easter.  | Example:  The pre-Easter sales were very popular. 复活节前的促销活动非常受欢迎。  The pre-Easter church services were well attended. 复活节前的教堂礼拜仪式参加人数众多。| 近义词: before Easter, ante-Easter (less common) | 反义词: post-Easter | 词性: adjective
