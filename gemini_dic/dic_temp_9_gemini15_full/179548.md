reeler:/ˈriːlər/| n. |卷筒机；蹒跚的人；旋转的人；卷绕机|A reeler is a machine used for winding or reeling something, such as thread or cable.  It can also refer to a person who reels or staggers, often from drunkenness or dizziness. |例句：The fisherman used a reeler to wind in his fishing line.  渔夫用卷线器收线。 The drunkard was a reeler, stumbling down the street.  那个醉汉摇摇晃晃地走在街上。|近义词：winder, spooler, staggerer|反义词：steady person, stable person
