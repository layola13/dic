peered:/pɪərd/| v.|凝视，仔细看|To look closely or intently, especially with difficulty.  It implies a sustained and focused gaze, often with effort to see clearly.|例句：She peered through the fog, trying to see the road ahead. 她透过雾凝视着前方，试图看清道路。|例句：He peered at the small print on the contract. 他仔细查看合同上的小字。|近义词：gaze, stare, glance, scrutinize|反义词：ignore, overlook|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He peers out the window. = 他凝视着窗外。
She peers at the map. = 她仔细看着地图。


一般过去时:
He peered into the darkness. = 他凝视着黑暗。
She peered at the painting. = 她仔细端详那幅画。


现在完成时:
He has peered into the abyss. = 他已经凝视着深渊。
She has peered through many telescopes. = 她已经透过许多望远镜观察。


过去完成时:
He had peered through the keyhole before entering. = 他在进入之前已经透过猫眼窥视了。
She had peered into the box many times. = 她已经多次仔细查看了盒子。


将来时:
He will peer through the telescope tonight. = 他今晚将透过望远镜观察。
She will peer into the mystery. = 她将深入研究这个谜团。


虚拟语气:
If I had better eyesight, I could peer further. = 如果我的视力更好，我就能看得更远。
I wish I could peer into the future. = 我希望我能预知未来。


被动语态:

疑问句:
Were they peering at us? = 他们在窥视我们吗？

否定句:
