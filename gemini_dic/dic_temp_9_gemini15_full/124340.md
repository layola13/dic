motif:/moʊˈtiːf/| n.|主题；主旨；动机；图案；乐句|A motif is a recurring subject, theme, idea, etc., especially in a literary, artistic, or musical work.  It can also refer to a distinctive feature or dominant idea in an artistic or literary composition.  In music, it's a short melodic, rhythmic, or harmonic pattern.|例句：The recurring motif of betrayal runs throughout the novel.  这部小说的贯穿始终的主题是背叛。

The artist used a floral motif in the design of the wallpaper.艺术家在墙纸设计中使用了花卉图案。|近义词：theme, subject, idea, pattern, design|反义词：background, antithesis
