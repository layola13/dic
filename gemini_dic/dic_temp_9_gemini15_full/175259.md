rain:/reɪn/| n. v.|雨，下雨|Water falling in drops from the atmosphere to the earth.|The rain is falling heavily. 雨下得很大。|It rained all day yesterday. 昨天一整天都在下雨。|sunshine, shower|drought, sunshine|n. v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It rains a lot in Seattle. = 西雅图经常下雨。
It often rains in spring. = 春天经常下雨。


一般过去时:
It rained heavily yesterday. = 昨天雨下得很大。
It rained cats and dogs last night. = 昨晚下了一场倾盆大雨。


现在完成时:
It has rained all day. = 一整天都在下雨。
It hasn't rained for weeks. = 好几个星期没下雨了。


过去完成时:
It had rained for days before the flood. = 洪水来临前已经下了好几天雨了。
They had not realized it had rained so much overnight. = 他们没有意识到一夜之间下了这么多雨。


将来时:
It will rain tomorrow. = 明天会下雨。
It is likely to rain later this evening. = 今晚晚些时候可能会下雨。


虚拟语气:
If it rained tomorrow, we would stay home. = 如果明天下雨，我们就待在家里。
I wish it were raining now. = 我希望现在正在下雨。


被动语态:
The crops were damaged by the rain. = 庄稼被雨水毁坏了。
The game was postponed because of the rain. = 比赛因为下雨而被推迟了。


疑问句:
Is it raining outside? = 外面在下雨吗？
Does it rain often in your country? = 你国家经常下雨吗？


否定句:
It doesn't rain much in the desert. = 沙漠地区雨水很少。
It didn't rain at all last week. = 上周根本没下雨。
