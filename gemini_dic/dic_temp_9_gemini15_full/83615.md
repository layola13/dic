haematoxylon:/ˌhiːmætˈɒksɪlən/ | n. | 苏木 |  Haematoxylon is a genus of flowering plants in the family Fabaceae, containing the single species Haematoxylon campechianum, commonly known as logwood or bloodwood.  It is a small to medium-sized tree native to southern Mexico and Central America,  cultivated for its heartwood, which yields a valuable dye known as haematoxylin. | The logwood tree (Haematoxylon campechianum) is a source of haematoxylin, a natural dye used in histology. |近义词: logwood, bloodwood | 反义词: N/A
