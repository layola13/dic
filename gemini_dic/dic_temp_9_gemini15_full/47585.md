debases:/dɪˈbeɪsɪz/| vt.|贬低；降低；使贬值|To reduce the value or quality of something.|The constant negativity debases the team's morale.  持续的消极情绪降低了团队的士气。|The inflation debased the currency. 通货膨胀使货币贬值。|elevate, enhance, improve|uphold, exalt, enhance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He debases the value of his work by overselling it. = 他通过过度推销降低了他作品的价值。
The government's policies debase the national currency. = 政府的政策使本国货币贬值。

一般过去时:
The scandal debased the reputation of the politician. = 这场丑闻损害了这位政治家的名誉。
They debased the gold coins by adding cheaper metals. = 他们通过添加廉价金属来降低金币的成色。

现在完成时:
Years of mismanagement have debased the company's image. = 多年的管理不善已经损害了公司的形象。
The constant lies have debased the trust between them. = 持续的谎言已经损害了他们之间的信任。

过去完成时:
They had debased the currency before the economic crisis hit. = 经济危机爆发前，他们就已经使货币贬值了。
The dictator had debased the moral standards of the society. = 独裁者已经降低了社会的道德标准。

将来时:
The new policy will debase the quality of education. = 新政策将降低教育质量。
Continued inflation will debase the value of savings. = 持续的通货膨胀将贬低储蓄的价值。


虚拟语气:
If they continued this practice, they would debase the entire system. = 如果他们继续这种做法，他们将会败坏整个制度。
I wish he hadn't debased himself with such behavior. = 我真希望他没有做出如此自贬身价的行为。

被动语态:
The currency was debased by excessive printing. = 货币因过度印刷而贬值。
His reputation was debased by the false accusations. = 他的名誉因虚假指控而受损。

疑问句:
Does this behavior debase the standards of our profession? = 这种行为是否降低了我们行业的标准？
How did the government debase the currency? = 政府是如何使货币贬值的？

否定句:
The company did not debase its products by cutting corners. = 公司没有通过偷工减料来降低其产品质量。
He refused to debase himself by accepting bribes. = 他拒绝通过接受贿赂来贬低自己。
