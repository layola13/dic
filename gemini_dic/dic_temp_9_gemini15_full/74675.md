freebooting:/ˈfriˌbuːtɪŋ/| n. |海盗行为；掠夺；擅自使用|The act of plundering or pillaging, especially by pirates or privateers; unauthorized appropriation or use of something.  It often implies a lack of authorization or legitimacy. |例句：The captain was accused of freebooting and piracy.  船长被指控犯有海盗行为和掠夺罪。

例句：The software company is facing a lawsuit for freebooting their competitor's code. 这家软件公司正面临对其擅自使用竞争对手代码的诉讼。


近义词：piracy, plundering, looting, raiding, theft, appropriation

反义词：legitimate use, authorized access, lawful acquisition

词性：名词
