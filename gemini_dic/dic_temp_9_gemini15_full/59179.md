dyslexia:/dɪsˈlɛksɪə/| n. |诵读困难；阅读障碍|A specific learning disability that manifests primarily as a difficulty with accurate and/or fluent word recognition and by problems with spelling and decoding.  It is characterized by difficulties with phonological processing (the manipulation of sounds in language).  Dyslexia is not a result of low intelligence or lack of effort. |He struggles with dyslexia, making reading a challenging task. 他有阅读障碍，阅读对他来说是一项挑战。 She was diagnosed with dyslexia in elementary school. 她在小学时被诊断出患有阅读障碍。|learning disability, reading disability, alexia|typical reading ability, normal reading ability
