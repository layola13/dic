fibula:/ˈfɪbjʊlə/| n. |腓骨|The fibula is the smaller of the two bones in the lower leg, located laterally to the tibia. It plays a role in stabilizing the ankle joint and providing attachment points for muscles.  |Example:  The doctor examined the patient's fibula after the skiing accident.  医生在滑雪事故后检查了病人的腓骨。|近义词：calf bone (informal)|反义词：tibia
