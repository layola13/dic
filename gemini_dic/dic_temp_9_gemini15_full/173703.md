quasi-medieval: /ˈkwɑːziˌmiːdiˈiːvəl/ | adj. |准中世纪的，似中世纪的 |  Resembling or suggestive of the Middle Ages; having characteristics reminiscent of the medieval period, but not entirely or strictly so.  It often implies a combination of medieval elements with aspects of other periods or styles. | The film's setting, with its dark castles and archaic rituals, created a quasi-medieval atmosphere. 这部电影的场景，以其黑暗的城堡和古老的仪式，营造了一种准中世纪的氛围。| 近义词：medieval-like, pseudo-medieval | 反义词：modern, contemporary
