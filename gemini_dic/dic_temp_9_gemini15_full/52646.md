digitalization: /ˌdɪdʒɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 数字化；数位化 | The process of converting information into a digital format.  This involves transforming analog data, such as text, images, and sound, into a series of binary digits (bits) that can be processed by computers. Digitalization is driving significant changes across various industries, leading to increased efficiency, automation, and data-driven decision-making. | Example: The digitalization of healthcare records has improved patient care and reduced administrative burdens.  数字化医疗记录改善了病人护理并减少了行政负担。  | 近义词：digitization, computerization | 反义词：analogization
