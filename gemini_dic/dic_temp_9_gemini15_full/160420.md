poppycock:/ˈpɒpɪkɒk/| n.|胡说八道，废话|Nonsense; something that is untrue or foolish.|Example:  Don't give me that poppycock!  别跟我说这些胡话！|近义词：nonsense, balderdash, rubbish, twaddle|反义词：truth, fact, sense


USAGE->
There is no verb form for "poppycock".  It is solely used as a noun.  Therefore, grammatical tenses do not apply.
