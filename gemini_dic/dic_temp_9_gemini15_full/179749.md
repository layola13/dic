reescalated: /ˌriːˈskəleɪtɪd/ | v. | 再升级；再次加剧 |  To escalate again; to increase the intensity or severity of something a second time.  | The conflict reescalated after a period of relative calm.  冲突在一段相对平静的时期后再次升级。| intensify, exacerbate, worsen | de-escalate, ease, mitigate | 动词


USAGE->

They reescalate the conflict regularly. = 他们定期再次加剧冲突。
