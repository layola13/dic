dukes:/dʒuːks/ | n. |公爵们，公爵的称号|The word "dukes" is the plural form of "duke," referring to multiple male members of the highest rank of nobility.  It can also refer to the titles themselves. |Example: The dukes assembled in the great hall. 公爵们聚集在大厅里。|近义词：noblemen, lords (less formal)|反义词：commoners, peasants
