misproduce:/ˌmɪsprəˈduːs/| vt.|错误生产，生产错误；误产|To produce something incorrectly or badly; to produce a faulty or defective product.|例句：The factory misproduced a batch of faulty widgets.  工厂误产了一批有缺陷的小部件。|例句：The farmer misproduced his crops due to the drought.  由于干旱，这位农民的庄稼歉收。|近义词：malproduce,  manufacture incorrectly|反义词：produce correctly, manufacture perfectly|动词
