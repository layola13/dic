chemist's:/ˈkɛmɪsts/| n.|化学家的；药剂师的|The chemist's shop is on the corner. |近义词：pharmacist's, druggist's|反义词：无


英文解析:
This word is a possessive form of the noun "chemist," indicating ownership or association.  A "chemist" can refer to someone who works in chemistry or a pharmacist who prepares and dispenses medications.  Therefore, "chemist's" can refer to a chemist's shop, laboratory, or belongings.


例句:
I bought some medicine at the chemist's. = 我在药店买了些药。

The chemist's report confirmed the presence of toxins. = 化学家的报告证实了毒素的存在。
