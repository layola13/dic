distend:/dɪˈstɛnd/ | vt. |使膨胀；使扩张；使鼓起 | To swell or cause to swell by pressure from inside.  |例句：The balloon distended as it filled with air.  气球充气后膨胀了。

例句：His stomach distended after eating too much. 他吃太多后肚子胀了起来。


近义词：swell, inflate, expand, bloat | 反义词：contract, shrink, deflate | 词性：动词
