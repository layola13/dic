luther:/ˈluːθər/| n. |路德；马丁·路德|Luther refers to Martin Luther (1483-1546), the seminal figure of the Protestant Reformation.  The name is also used as a surname and sometimes as a given name.|Example: Martin Luther's ideas revolutionized Christianity. 马丁·路德的思想彻底改变了基督教。|近义词:Reformer(改革家) |反义词:None.
