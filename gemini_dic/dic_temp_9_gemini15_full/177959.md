recipient:/rɪˈsɪpiənt/| n. |接受者，收件人，受益人 |A recipient is a person or thing that receives something.  The term often refers to someone who receives a gift, letter, or payment.  It can also refer to someone who benefits from something. |Example: The recipient of the award was visibly moved.  获奖者明显很激动。


The package was delivered to the wrong recipient.包裹送到了错误的收件人手中。|近义词：receiver, addressee, beneficiary |反义词：sender, giver, donor
