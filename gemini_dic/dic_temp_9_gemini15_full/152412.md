pavlovian:/pævˈloʊviən/| adj.|巴甫洛夫的；条件反射的|Relating to or denoting a type of learning in which a response, originally elicited by a specific stimulus, comes to be elicited by a previously neutral stimulus after repeated pairings with the original stimulus.  This is also known as classical conditioning.|The dog exhibited a Pavlovian response, salivating at the sound of the bell after being repeatedly paired with food.|近义词：conditioned, reflexive|反义词：unconditioned, voluntary
