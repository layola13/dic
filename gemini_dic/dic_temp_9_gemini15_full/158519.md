plenty:/ˈplɛnti/| pron. n. adj.|充足；大量；许多|a large amount; more than enough|例句：We have plenty of time. 我们有很多时间。

例句：There's plenty to eat.  有很多吃的。


近义词：abundance, profusion, copiousness, a lot, much

反义词：scarcity, lack, deficiency, shortage


词性：pronoun, noun, adjective


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We have plenty of food. = 我们有很多食物。
There is plenty to see and do in London. = 伦敦有很多值得一看和做的事情。


一般过去时:
They had plenty of money. = 他们有很多钱。
We had plenty of time to spare. = 我们有很多空闲时间。


现在完成时:
We have had plenty of opportunities. = 我们已经有很多机会了。
She has seen plenty of the world. = 她已经看过很多地方了。


过去完成时:
They had already had plenty to drink. = 他们已经喝了很多酒了。
We had had plenty of warning. = 我们已经得到了足够的警告。


将来时:
We will have plenty of time to relax. = 我们将有很多时间放松。
There will be plenty of work to do. = 将会有很多工作要做。


虚拟语气:
If we had plenty of money, we would travel the world. = 如果我们有很多钱，我们会环游世界。
I wish I had plenty of time to finish this project. = 我希望我有充足的时间完成这个项目。


被动语态:
Plenty of time was given to the students. = 给学生们留出了充足的时间。
Plenty of consideration has been given to the problem. = 这个问题已经得到了充分的考虑。


疑问句:
Is there plenty of room for everyone? = 大家都有足够的空间吗？
Do we have plenty of supplies? = 我们有足够的物资吗？


否定句:
We don't have plenty of resources. = 我们没有足够的资源。
There wasn't plenty of evidence. = 没有足够的证据。



PHRASE->
plenty of: 许多的，大量的
plenty more: 更多
more than plenty: 绰绰有余
get plenty of: 获得足够的
have plenty to do:有很多事情要做


词源:
The word "plenty" comes from the Old French word "plente," meaning "fullness," "abundance," or "plenty."  This ultimately traces back to the Latin word "plenus," meaning "full."
