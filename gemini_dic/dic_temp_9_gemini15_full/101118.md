invertible:/ɪnˈvɜːrtəbl/ | adj. | 可逆的，可转换的 | Capable of being inverted or reversed; having an inverse.  Something that is invertible can be undone or reversed.  |  The matrix is invertible.  这个矩阵是可逆的。 The encryption is invertible only with the correct key.  只有使用正确的密钥才能解密。 | reversible, reversible,  reciprocal | irreversible, unidirectional
