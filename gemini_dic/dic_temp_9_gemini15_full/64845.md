espier:/ɪˈspaɪə(r)/| v.|窥探，暗中观察|To catch sight of or see unexpectedly or stealthily; to spy or watch secretly.|例句：He espied a figure lurking in the shadows. 他瞥见一个人潜伏在阴影中。|例句：The detective espied the thief leaving the scene.  侦探看到小偷离开了现场。|近义词：spy, glimpse, observe, detect|反义词：ignore, overlook
