hailed-from:/heɪldˈfrɒm/| phrasal verb| 来自，起源于 | This phrase indicates the place of origin or birthplace of a person or thing.  It implies a strong connection or association with that place. |例句：He hailed from a small town in the south. 他来自南方的一个小镇。


例句：The traditional dish hailed from the mountainous region. 这道传统菜肴起源于山区。


近义词：originated from, came from, stemmed from


反义词： 无直接反义词，但可以考虑表达相反含义的词组，例如：moved to, relocated to


词性: phrasal verb (动词短语)
