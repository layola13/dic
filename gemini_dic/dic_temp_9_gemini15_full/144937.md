overabsorb:/ˌoʊvəræbˈzɔːrb/| vt.|过度吸收；吸收过多|To absorb more of something than is necessary or desirable.  This implies an excessive or undesirable level of absorption.|例句：The soil overabsorbed the fertilizer, leading to runoff and pollution.  土壤过度吸收了肥料，导致径流和污染。
The sponge overabsorbed the water, becoming soggy and useless. 海绵吸收了过多的水，变得湿漉漉的，没用了。|近义词：soak up excessively, saturate excessively|反义词：underabsorb|动词
