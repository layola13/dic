interfollicular: /ˌɪntərfoʊˈlɪkjələr/ | adj. | 毛囊间的；毛囊间的 | Located between hair follicles.  The term describes the anatomical location or position between hair follicles in the skin. It is primarily used in dermatology and histology contexts to describe structures, processes, or substances situated in this interspace. | Example:  Interfollicular epidermis showed increased thickness. 毛囊间表皮显示增厚。  |近义词: interfollicular space, perifollicular (though perifollicular usually implies surrounding, not just between)|反义词: intrafollicular (within the hair follicle)|形容词
