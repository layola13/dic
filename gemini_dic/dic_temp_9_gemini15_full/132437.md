nondepletion: /ˌnɒn.dɪˈpliː.ʃən/ | adj. |非消耗性的，不枯竭的 | Not causing depletion or exhaustion; sustainable.  The nondepletion of resources is a key goal of sustainable development. |近义词：sustainable, inexhaustible, renewable | 反义词：depleting, exhaustible


英文解析：The word "nondepletion" is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the noun "depletion" (meaning the reduction of something).  Therefore, it describes something that does not cause or involve depletion.  It implies a process or resource that is sustainable and does not diminish over time.


例句：

The company boasts a nondepletion policy for its natural resources. = 这家公司吹嘘其自然资源的非消耗政策。

Sustainable agriculture practices emphasize nondepletion of soil nutrients. = 可持续农业实践强调不消耗土壤养分。
