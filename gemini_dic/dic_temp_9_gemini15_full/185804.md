revisionisms:/rɪˈvɪʒənɪzəm/| n. |修正主义；倒退；对既定观点或历史事件的重新诠释|The act of revising or reinterpreting established views, often with the intention of altering or distorting their original meaning.  This often involves reinterpreting historical events, political ideologies, or social norms to fit a particular agenda.  It can be a legitimate academic pursuit or a deliberate attempt to manipulate public understanding. | Example: The historian's work was criticized for its revisionism of the events leading up to the war.  这位历史学家的作品因其对导致战争的事件的修正主义解读而受到批评。|近义词：reinterpretation, reinterpreting, rewriting, distortion |反义词：originalism, traditionalism |名词
