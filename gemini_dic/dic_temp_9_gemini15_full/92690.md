icebox:/ˈaɪsbɒks/| n. |冰箱；冰盒|A container, typically a refrigerator, used for keeping food and drinks cold.|例句：We need a bigger icebox to store all the food for the picnic. 我们需要一个更大的冰箱来储存野餐的所有食物。
例句：The icebox is full of delicious leftovers from last night's dinner. 冰箱里装满了昨晚晚餐剩下的美味佳肴。|近义词：refrigerator, cooler |反义词：oven, heater|名词
