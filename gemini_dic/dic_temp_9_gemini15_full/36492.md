coleopterous:/ˌkoʊliˈɒptərəs/| adj. |鞘翅目的；鞘翅昆虫的 | Belonging to the order Coleoptera, which includes beetles.  The term refers to insects with hardened forewings that protect the delicate hindwings used for flight. | Example:  Many coleopterous insects are known for their vibrant colors and diverse forms.  许多鞘翅目昆虫以其鲜艳的颜色和多样化的形态而闻名。|近义词：beetles,  |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with insects from other orders.
