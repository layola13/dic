coneless:/ˈkoʊnɪləs/ | adj. | 无锥的；无果穗的 | Lacking a cone or cones.  The term is primarily used in botany and horticulture, referring to plants that do not produce cones.  | Example: The coneless variety of fir tree is popular for its unique appearance.  无锥的冷杉品种因其独特的外观而广受欢迎。 | 近义词: coneless (no close synonyms, but could use descriptive phrases like "cone-free," "without cones") | 反义词: coniferous
