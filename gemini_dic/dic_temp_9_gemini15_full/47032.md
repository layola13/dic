data:/ˈdeɪtə/| n. |数据，资料 |Data refers to collections of facts, figures, or other information that can be processed or analyzed. It can be in various forms, such as numbers, text, images, or audio.|The research project requires a large amount of data. 这项研究项目需要大量的数据。|information, facts, figures|fiction, opinion.
