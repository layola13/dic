recooling:/ˌriːˈkuːlɪŋ/| n.|再冷却；重新冷却|The process of cooling something down again after it has already been cooled.  This term is often used in industrial processes or scientific contexts where a substance needs to be cooled multiple times.|例句：The recooling process of the reactor core is crucial for safety.  反应堆核心的再次冷却过程对安全至关重要。
The recooling of the beverage extended its shelf life.  饮料的再次冷却延长了其保质期。|近义词：re-chilling, secondary cooling|反义词：reheating
