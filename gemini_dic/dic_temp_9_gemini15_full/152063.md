patents:/ˈpætənts/| n.|专利，专利权|Exclusive rights granted for an invention, providing the patent holder with the sole right to make, use, and sell the invention for a set period.|例句：She holds several patents on innovative medical devices. 她拥有几项创新医疗设备的专利。|例句：The company is seeking patents for its new technology.  该公司正在为其新技术申请专利。|近义词：intellectual property rights, copyrights|反义词：public domain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He holds multiple patents. = 他拥有多项专利。
The company files many patents each year. = 这家公司每年申请许多专利。


一般过去时:
They obtained a patent for their new software. = 他们获得了他们新软件的专利。
The inventor applied for a patent in 2020. = 发明者在2020年申请了专利。


现在完成时:
We have secured several patents this year. = 我们今年已经获得了多项专利。
She has already filed a patent application. = 她已经提交了专利申请。


过去完成时:
They had already received the patent before the competitor filed their application. = 在竞争对手提交申请之前，他们已经获得了专利。
The company had filed several patents prior to the merger. = 在合并之前，该公司已经申请了几项专利。


将来时:
They will apply for a patent next month. = 他们下个月将申请专利。
The inventor will likely receive the patent soon. = 发明者很可能会很快获得专利。


虚拟语气:
If I had a patent, I would license it. = 如果我有一项专利，我会授权它。
I wish I had patented my invention earlier. = 我真希望我早点申请我的发明专利。


被动语态:
The patent was granted last week. = 专利在上周被授予。
Many patents are filed annually. = 每年都会申请许多专利。


疑问句:

否定句:
