preindebtedness: /ˌpriːɪnˈdɛtɪdnəs/ | n. | 预先负债；预先欠债 |  A state of being indebted before an event or transaction.  It refers to an obligation or liability incurred before a specific point in time. This could be due to past loans, accumulated expenses, or other financial commitments. | Example: The company's preindebtedness significantly hampered its ability to secure a new loan.  这家公司的预先负债严重阻碍了它获得新贷款的能力。 | 近义词: prior debt, antecedent liability | 反义词: prepayment, surplus
