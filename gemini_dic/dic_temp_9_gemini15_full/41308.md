cordwainer:/ˈkɔːrdweɪnər/| n. |制鞋匠，皮革匠|A cordwainer is a craftsman who makes and repairs shoes, traditionally specializing in fine leatherwork.  The term derives from the type of leather used, Cordovan leather, which originated in Córdoba, Spain.|Example:  The skilled cordwainer crafted exquisite boots from the finest calfskin.  这位技艺精湛的制鞋匠用最好的小牛皮制作了精致的靴子。|近义词：shoemaker, cobbler|反义词：无
