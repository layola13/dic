mustered-out:/ˈmʌstərd aʊt/ | adj. | 退役的，退伍的 |  Describes a soldier or other military personnel who have been officially discharged from service.  It emphasizes the completion of the service obligation. | Example:  He was mustered out of the army after serving for five years. 他服役五年后退伍了。 | 近义词：discharged, dismissed, released | 反义词：enlisted, recruited


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army regularly musters out soldiers after their contracts expire. = 军队定期在士兵合同到期后将其遣散。
He is mustered out because of his poor health. = 他因为身体不好而被遣散。


一般过去时:
Thousands of soldiers were mustered out after the war ended. = 战争结束后，数千名士兵退伍。
They mustered him out early due to an injury. = 由于受伤，他们提前将他遣散。


现在完成时:
He has been mustered out and is now looking for a civilian job. = 他已经退伍，现在正在寻找一份平民工作。
Many veterans have been mustered out since the end of the conflict. = 自冲突结束以来，许多退伍军人已经退伍。


过去完成时:
By the time the parade started, most of the soldiers had been mustered out. = 到游行开始时，大多数士兵都已经退伍了。
He had been mustered out months before his application was reviewed. = 在他的申请被审核之前几个月，他已经退伍了。


将来时:
They will be mustered out next month. = 他们下个月将退伍。
The government will muster out the troops once peace is established. = 一旦和平建立，政府将遣散军队。


虚拟语气:
If he had been mustered out sooner, he could have started his new job earlier. = 如果他早点退伍，他就能早点开始他的新工作。
I wish I had been mustered out before the deployment. = 我希望我在部署之前就已经退伍了。


被动语态:
The soldiers were mustered out with full honors. = 士兵们以最高的荣誉退伍。

疑问句:

否定句:
