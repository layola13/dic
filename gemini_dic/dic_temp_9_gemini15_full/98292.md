inside:/ɪnˈsaɪd/ | adv. prep. n. adj. |里面；内部；内侧；内部的 |  Inside the box, there's a surprise.  盒子里面有个惊喜。The bird flew inside.  鸟飞进去了。 |近义词：within, interior |反义词：outside, exterior


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat sits inside. 猫坐在里面。
They work inside the office. 他们在办公室里工作。


一般过去时:
He went inside the house. 他走进房子里。
We stayed inside during the storm. 暴风雨期间我们待在室内。


现在完成时:
She has been inside all day. 她整天都在里面。
They have lived inside the city for years. 他们在这个城市住了好多年。


过去完成时:
The thief had already hidden inside the closet. 小偷已经躲在壁橱里了。
We had been inside for hours before the rain stopped. 雨停之前我们已经在里面待了几个小时了。


将来时:
I will be inside later this evening. 今晚晚些时候我会在里面。
They will go inside when it starts to rain.  下雨的时候他们会进去。


虚拟语气:
If I were inside, I would be safe. 如果我在里面，我会很安全。
I wish I were inside right now. 我希望我现在在里面。


被动语态:
The room was cleaned inside and out.  房间里里外外都被打扫干净了。
The package was damaged inside. 包裹里面损坏了。


疑问句:
Is the dog inside? 狗在里面吗？
What's inside the bag? 包里有什么？


否定句:
The dog isn't inside. 狗不在里面。
There's nothing inside the box. 盒子里什么也没有。


PHRASE->
on the inside: 在里面
from the inside: 从内部

deep inside: 深处


词源:
The word "inside" comes from the Old English "in" meaning "in" and "sid" meaning "side".  It literally means "within the side" or "on the inner side".  The word has evolved over time, retaining its core meaning of location within something.
