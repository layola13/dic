celticize:/ˈsɛltɪsaɪz/| vt.|使凯尔特化；使具有凯尔特特征|To make something or someone more Celtic in character or influence.  This typically involves adopting aspects of Celtic culture, language, or traditions.|The government attempted to celticize the curriculum by incorporating more Irish history and literature. 政府试图通过加入更多爱尔兰历史和文学来使课程更具有凯尔特特色。|近义词：Gaelcise (if specifically referring to Gaelic culture)|反义词：de-celticize
