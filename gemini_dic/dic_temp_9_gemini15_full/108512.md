layover:/ˈleɪoʊvər/| n.|中途停留，中转|A layover is a planned, usually short stop during a journey, especially on a flight,  involving a change of planes.  It's the time spent waiting between connecting flights.|例句：My flight to London has a layover in New York.|近义词：stop, stopover, connection|反义词：direct flight


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flight to London has a layover in Chicago. = 飞往伦敦的航班在芝加哥中转。
There is a short layover between flights. = 航班之间有短暂的中转时间。


一般过去时:
We had a long layover in Dubai. = 我们在迪拜有很长的中转时间。
My layover last time was only an hour. = 我上次的中转时间只有一小时。


现在完成时:
I have had many layovers in my travels. = 我在旅行中经历过许多次中转。
She has never had a layover longer than six hours. = 她从未有过超过六小时的中转时间。


过去完成时:
