coproducing:/ˌkoʊprəˈduːsɪŋ/| v. |联合制作；共同制作|The act of two or more entities working together to create or produce something.  It emphasizes shared responsibility and collaboration in the production process.|例句：Several companies are coproducing the new film. = 几家公司正在联合制作这部新电影。


例句：The university and the local theater are coproducing a new play. = 大学和当地剧院正在联合制作一出新戏。


近义词：collaborating, jointly producing, co-creating


反义词：producing independently, solely producing


词性:动词
