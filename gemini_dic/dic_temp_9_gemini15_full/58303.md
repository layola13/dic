dry-cleans:/ˈdraɪkliːnz/ | v. |干洗|To clean (clothing or other fabrics) using a solvent other than water.|例句：I dry-clean my suits every month. 我每个月都干洗我的西装。

例句：She dry-cleaned her wedding dress after the ceremony. 婚礼仪式后，她把婚纱送去干洗了。|近义词：clean, launder|反义词：hand-wash


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I dry-clean my clothes regularly. = 我定期干洗衣服。
They dry-clean their shirts at the same shop. = 他们在同一家店干洗衬衫。


一般过去时:
She dry-cleaned her favorite dress. = 她干洗了她最喜欢的连衣裙。
We dry-cleaned all the curtains last week. = 我们上周干洗了所有的窗帘。


现在完成时:
I have dry-cleaned this coat twice this year. = 我今年已经干洗过这件外套两次了。
They have dry-cleaned their wedding suits for the upcoming ceremony. = 他们已经为即将到来的仪式干洗了他们的结婚西装。


过去完成时:
She had dry-cleaned the dress before the party. = 派对前她已经把裙子干洗了。
He had dry-cleaned his suit several times before the important meeting. = 在重要的会议之前，他已经干洗过几次他的西装了。


将来时:
I will dry-clean my dress for the interview. = 我将为面试干洗我的衣服。
She will dry-clean her skirt before wearing it to the wedding. = 她会在穿上它去参加婚礼之前把裙子干洗。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would dry-clean my clothes more often. = 如果我有更多钱，我会更频繁地干洗衣服。
I wish I had dry-cleaned that dress before wearing it to the party. = 我真希望在穿那件衣服去参加派对之前把它干洗了。


被动语态:

疑问句:
