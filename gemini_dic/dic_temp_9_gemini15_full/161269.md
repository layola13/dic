postmodernities:/ˌpoʊstməˈdɜːrnətiz/ | n. | 后现代性；后现代主义思潮 | Postmodernities refers to the plural forms of postmodernity, encompassing the diverse and often contradictory aspects of the postmodern condition. It signifies the multiple expressions and interpretations of postmodern thought and culture across different fields and contexts.  It acknowledges the multiplicity and fragmentation inherent in postmodern experience. | Example: The exhibition showcased a wide range of artistic expressions that reflected the complex postmodernities of our times.  展览展示了反映我们时代复杂后现代性的各种艺术表达形式。 | 近义词：postmodern phenomena, postmodern conditions | 反义词：modernity, modernism
