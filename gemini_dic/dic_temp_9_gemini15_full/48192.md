decompressed:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛst/| adj.| 解压缩的；已解压缩的 |  Reduced in size or volume, typically from a compressed or archived file format.  The opposite of compressed. |例句：The decompressed file is now ready to use. 解压缩后的文件现在可以使用了。 The image was decompressed before being displayed. 图片在显示之前已解压缩。 |近义词：uncompressed, expanded |反义词：compressed, compacted
