carburettor: /ˈkɑːrbjʊreɪtər/ | n. |化油器| A device in an internal combustion engine that mixes air and fuel before it enters the engine's cylinders.  It uses a venturi effect to draw fuel into the airstream. | The carburettor needs cleaning; the engine is running poorly. 化油器需要清洗；发动机运转不良。 | injector, fuel injection system |  (no direct antonym, but "fuel injection system" represents a contrasting technology) | 名词

词源:
