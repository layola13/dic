rough-cut:/ˈrʌf kʌt/| adj. n.|粗剪的；粗略的版本|An initial, unpolished version of something, typically a film or video, before editing and refinement.  A rough cut is characterized by its lack of polish and may include unfinished scenes, missing footage, or temporary sound effects.|例句：The director reviewed the rough cut of the film before sending it to the editor.  导演在将电影送去剪辑之前先审阅了粗剪版本。|The editor assembled a rough cut of the documentary. 编辑组装了纪录片的粗剪版本。|近义词：preliminary, unpolished, unfinished, draft|反义词：final cut, polished, refined
