manorialism:/məˈnɔːriəlɪzəm/| n. |庄园制度，领主制度|The economic and social system of the medieval period in Europe, based on the manor and the obligations of peasants to the lord.|The manorial system was characterized by a hierarchical structure with the lord at the top and peasants at the bottom.|近义词：feudalism|反义词：capitalism
