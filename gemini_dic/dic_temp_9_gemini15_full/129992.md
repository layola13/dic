non-necessity:/ˌnɒn.nəˈsɛsɪti/| n. |非必需品，非必要的事物 |A non-necessity is something that is not essential or required; it is something that can be done without.  It refers to goods or services that are not essential for survival or basic needs. | Example:  Luxury cars are considered non-necessities. 豪华轿车被认为是非必需品。

The purchase of a new television is a non-necessity for most families. 对大多数家庭来说，购买一台新电视机是非必需的。|近义词：luxury, dispensable, optional |反义词：necessity, essential, requirement |词性 n.
