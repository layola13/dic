nonrequisites:/ˌnɒnˈrɛkwɪzɪts/| n. |非必修课程；非必要条件 |Things that are not required; courses that are not required for a degree or program.  |Example:  Many students choose to take nonrequisites to broaden their academic horizons. 许多学生选择修读非必修课程来拓宽他们的学术视野。|近义词：electives, optional courses|反义词：requisites, requirements|名词
