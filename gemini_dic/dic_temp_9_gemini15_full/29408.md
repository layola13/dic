cataclysmist: /ˈkætəˌklɪzəmɪst/ | n. | 灾难论者，大灾变论者 | A person who believes in or emphasizes cataclysms (sudden and violent events causing great destruction or change) as significant forces shaping history or the earth.  They often focus on the destructive aspects of such events and their impact on civilizations or ecosystems. |  The cataclysmist argued that the extinction of the dinosaurs was caused by a massive asteroid impact. 灾难论者认为恐龙的灭绝是由一次巨大的小行星撞击造成的。  Some cataclysmists believe that future climate change will trigger a societal collapse. 一些灾难论者认为未来的气候变化将引发社会崩溃。 | 近义词： catastrophist, doomsayer | 反义词： optimist, progressive
