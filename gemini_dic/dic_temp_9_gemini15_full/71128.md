fishing:/ˈfɪʃɪŋ/| n. v.| 钓鱼；捕鱼 | The act of catching fish.  It can refer to the activity itself, the industry, or the sport. | 例句：He enjoys fishing in the lake. 他喜欢在湖里钓鱼。  例句：The fishing industry is vital to the local economy. 渔业对当地经济至关重要。|近义词：angling, fishing for (seeking something indirectly),  angling for (seeking something indirectly) |反义词：None specifically, but "not fishing" could be considered an antonym. |名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fishes every weekend. = 他每个周末都钓鱼。
They fish in the river. = 他们在河里钓鱼。

一般过去时:
I fished with my father yesterday. = 我昨天和爸爸一起钓鱼。
She fished for hours but caught nothing. = 她钓了几个小时，但什么也没钓到。

现在完成时:
We have fished in this lake many times. = 我们在这个湖里钓过很多次鱼了。
He has been fishing since sunrise. = 他从日出开始就一直在钓鱼。

过去完成时:
They had fished all day before they found a good spot. = 他们在找到一个好地方之前钓了一整天鱼。
She had never fished before that trip. = 在那次旅行之前她从未钓过鱼。

将来时:
I will fish tomorrow if the weather is good. = 如果天气好，我明天就去钓鱼。
They will be fishing at the lake next week. = 下周他们将在湖边钓鱼。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would fish more often. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更经常去钓鱼。
I wish I were fishing right now. = 我真希望我现在正在钓鱼。

被动语态:
The fish are fished commercially in this area. = 这个地区商业性捕捞鱼类。
The lake has been overfished. = 这个湖已经被过度捕捞了。

疑问句:
Do you like fishing? = 你喜欢钓鱼吗？
Where do they fish? = 他们在哪里钓鱼？

否定句:
He doesn't fish very often. = 他不经常钓鱼。
I didn't fish yesterday because it rained. = 昨天因为下雨我没有钓鱼。
