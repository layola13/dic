desilting:/dɪˈsɪltɪŋ/| n.|清淤；去淤；排沙|The removal of silt from a reservoir, canal, or other waterway.  This process is crucial for maintaining the efficiency and functionality of water systems. |例句：Regular desilting is essential to prevent flooding. 定期清淤对于防止洪水至关重要。

例句：The desilting of the irrigation canal improved water flow significantly.  灌溉渠的清淤显著改善了水流。


近义词：silt removal, dredging, cleaning


反义词：silting (淤积)


词性：名词
