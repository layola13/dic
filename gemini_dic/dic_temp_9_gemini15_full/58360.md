dual-carriageway: /ˈdjuːəl ˈkærɪdʒweɪ/ | n. | 双向车道；双行道 | A road with a central reservation separating two carriageways, one for traffic in each direction.  |  例句：The dual-carriageway allows for faster and safer travel.  双向车道使得出行更快更安全。 |The new dual-carriageway significantly reduced travel time. 新建的双向车道大大缩短了行程时间。| 近义词：divided highway, motorway (UK), freeway (US) | 反义词：single-carriageway, one-way street |


词源:

* **dual:**  From Latin *dualis*, meaning "two" or "double".
* **carriageway:**  From "carriage" (a vehicle) and "way" (a road or path).  Originally referred to the road surface used by carriages.


用法说明:

dual-carriageway 主要用于英国英语，在美式英语中，通常使用 divided highway, freeway 或 motorway 的对应词语。它指的是中间有隔离带分隔双向行驶的车道。


例句：

* The accident occurred on a busy dual-carriageway. 事故发生在一处繁忙的双向车道上。
* Driving on a dual-carriageway is generally safer than on a single-carriageway. 在双向车道上驾驶通常比在单向车道上更安全。


近义词:

* divided highway (US)
* freeway (US)
* motorway (UK)
* expressway


反义词:

* single-carriageway
* one-way street


PHRASE->

由于dual-carriageway本身是一个名词，不像动词或形容词那样能容易地构成短语，因此，这里提供一些与dual-carriageway相关的常用短语：

* on the dual-carriageway: 在双向车道上
* drive on the dual-carriageway: 在双向车道上行驶
* dual-carriageway section: 双向车道路段
* widening the dual-carriageway: 拓宽双向车道
* the dual-carriageway system: 双向车道系统


由于dual-carriageway是名词，因此没有动词、形容词、副词等不同词性变化和相应的例句。
