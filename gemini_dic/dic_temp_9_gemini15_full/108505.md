layman:/ˈleɪmən/ | n. |外行，门外汉 | A person without professional knowledge or skill in a particular area.  |Example:  The explanation was simplified for the layman.  这个解释被简化了，以便外行也能理解。|近义词：amateur, novice, non-professional |反义词：expert, professional, specialist


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a layman in this field. = 他在这个领域是个外行。
She considers herself a layman when it comes to computers. = 谈到电脑，她认为自己是外行。


一般过去时:
He was a layman before he started studying medicine. = 在他开始学习医学之前，他是个外行。
She acted like a layman during the technical discussion. = 在技术讨论中，她表现得像个外行。


现在完成时:
He has remained a layman throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中都一直是个外行。
She has become a layman in this area after changing her career. = 改变职业后，她在这个领域变成了外行。


过去完成时:
He had been a layman until he took that advanced course. = 直到他上了那门高级课程之前，他一直是个外行。
She had remained a layman despite her years of experience in a related field. = 尽管她在相关领域有多年的经验，但她仍然是个外行。


将来时:
He will remain a layman unless he decides to pursue further education. = 除非他决定继续深造，否则他将仍然是个外行。
She will likely become a layman in this field after retirement. = 退休后，她很可能会在这个领域变成外行。


虚拟语气:
If he were a layman, he wouldn't understand these technical terms. = 如果他是个外行，他就不会理解这些专业术语。
I wish I were a layman so I didn't have to deal with this complicated issue. = 我希望我是一个外行，这样我就不用处理这个复杂的问题了。


被动语态:
The instructions were simplified for the layman. = 说明书被简化了，方便外行理解。
The concept was explained in layman's terms. = 这个概念是用通俗易懂的语言解释的。


疑问句:
Is he a layman in this subject? = 他在这个科目上是外行吗？

否定句:
He is not a layman; he's a seasoned expert. = 他不是外行；他是一位经验丰富的专家。
She is not a layman in this field; she has a PhD. = 她在这个领域不是外行；她拥有博士学位。
