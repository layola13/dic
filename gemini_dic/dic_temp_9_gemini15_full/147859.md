overrates:/ˌoʊvərˈreɪts/ | vt. |过高评价；高估|To estimate or rate (something) too highly.  It implies a judgment that is excessively positive or favorable.|例句：He overrates his own abilities. 他过高估计了自己的能力。

例句：The critics overrate that film; it's really quite mediocre. 评论家们对那部电影评价过高；它实际上相当平庸。


近义词：overestimate, exaggerate, inflate

反义词：underrate, underestimate

The professor overrates the students' potential. = 教授高估了学生的潜力。
