responding:/rɪˈspɑːndɪŋ/| v. |回应；响应；反应|The act of answering or reacting to something.  It implies a reaction to a stimulus or request. |例句：He is responding well to treatment. 他对治疗反应良好。|例句：She responded to my email promptly. 她很快回复了我的邮件。|近义词：answering, reacting, replying|反义词：ignoring, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is responding to the questions. = 他正在回答问题。
They respond positively to feedback. = 他们对反馈反应积极。

一般过去时:
She responded quickly to the emergency. = 她迅速回应了紧急情况。
He responded to the letter with a phone call. = 他用电话回复了这封信。

现在完成时:
The patient has responded well to the medication. = 病人对药物反应良好。
Have you responded to his email yet? = 你已经回复他的邮件了吗？

过去完成时:
They had already responded before the message arrived. = 信息到达之前他们已经回复了。
She had responded negatively to the initial offer. = 她对最初的提议反应消极。

将来时:
He will respond to your inquiry tomorrow. = 他明天将回复你的询问。
They will be responding to the crisis swiftly. = 他们将迅速应对这场危机。

虚拟语气:
If he responded honestly, things would be easier. = 如果他诚实地回应，事情会更容易。
I wish he had responded to my concerns. = 我希望他回应我的担忧。

被动语态:
The request was responded to immediately. = 请求得到了立即的回应。
The survey is being responded to by many people. = 许多人正在回复调查问卷。

疑问句:
How did he respond to your proposal? = 他对你的提议有什么回应？
Has everyone responded to the invitation? = 每个人都回复邀请了吗？

否定句:
He didn't respond to my call. = 他没有回应我的电话。
She hasn't responded to my emails yet. = 她还没回复我的邮件。


PHRASE->
respond to = 回答；回应
respond in kind = 以牙还牙；同等回应
respond favorably = 积极回应
respond negatively = 消极回应
respond appropriately = 恰当回应
