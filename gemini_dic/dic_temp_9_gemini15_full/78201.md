geotif:/ˈdʒioʊtɪf/| n. |地理信息标记文件|A GeoTIFF is a public domain raster format that can store geospatial information alongside raster data.  This means it can embed coordinate system information, allowing geographic location of the image data to be preserved and referenced.  It is based on the TIFF standard, extending it with georeferencing tags.  GeoTIFF files are widely used in GIS (Geographic Information Systems) and remote sensing applications. |Example: The satellite image was saved as a GeoTIFF file to preserve its geographic coordinates.|近义词:TIFF (with georeferencing)|反义词:None.
