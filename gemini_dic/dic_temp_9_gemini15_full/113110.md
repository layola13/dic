lune:/luːn/| n. |新月；月牙；弓形物|A crescent-shaped figure; a slender crescent.  The word is derived from the Latin word "luna," meaning "moon." It often refers to a crescent moon, but can also describe any crescent-shaped object. |例句：The sliver of moon, a delicate lune, hung in the night sky.  夜空中悬挂着一弯纤细的新月。The architect designed a building with a lune-shaped window. 建筑师设计了一座带有月牙形窗户的建筑物。|近义词：crescent, half-moon |反义词：full moon
