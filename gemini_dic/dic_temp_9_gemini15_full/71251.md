five:/faɪv/| num. adj. n.|五，五个|The number after four and before six.  It represents the quantity of five units. |例句：There are five apples on the table. 桌上有五个苹果。|例句：She has five children. 她有五个孩子。|近义词：quintet (n.)|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There are five birds in the tree. = 树上有五只鸟。
Five plus five equals ten. = 五加五等于十。


一般过去时:
There were five cars in the parking lot. = 停车场里有五辆车。
He gave me five dollars. = 他给了我五美元。


现在完成时:
I have seen that movie five times. = 我看过那部电影五次了。
She has had five cups of coffee today. = 她今天已经喝了五杯咖啡。


过去完成时:
I had already read five books before the summer ended. = 夏天结束前我已经读了五本书。
They had eaten five pieces of cake before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经吃了五块蛋糕。


将来时:
I will buy five new shirts next week. = 我下周将买五件新衬衫。
We will have five guests for dinner tomorrow. = 明天我们将有五位客人来吃晚饭。


虚拟语气:
If I had five million dollars, I would travel the world. = 如果我有五百万美元，我就会环游世界。
I wish I had five more days of vacation. = 我希望我有五天更多的假期。


被动语态:
Five students were chosen for the team. = 五名学生被选中参加球队。
Five houses were damaged in the storm. = 五座房屋在暴风雨中受损。


疑问句:
How many apples are there? There are five. = 有多少个苹果？有五个。
Do you have five dollars? = 你有五美元吗？


否定句:
There aren't five people in the room. = 房间里没有五个人。
I don't have five siblings. = 我没有五个兄弟姐妹。
