gesturing: /ˈdʒɛstʃərɪŋ/ | v. | 做手势，示意 |  The act of making gestures; using gestures to communicate.  | 例句：He was gesturing wildly to attract attention. 他在疯狂地打手势以引起注意。

例句：She gestured towards the door, indicating it was time to leave. 她用手势示意该走了。


近义词：signaling, motioning, indicating


反义词：remaining still, being silent


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gestures frequently during his speeches. = 他在演讲中经常做手势。
She gestures politely when she talks. = 她说话时会礼貌地做手势。


一般过去时:
He gestured angrily at the driver. = 他生气地朝司机做了个手势。
She gestured towards the exit. = 她朝出口做了个手势。


现在完成时:
He has gestured to me several times. = 他已经对我做过几次手势。
She has gestured impatiently throughout the meeting. = 她在整个会议期间都有些不耐烦地做着手势。


过去完成时:
He had gestured for help before collapsing. = 他在倒下之前曾示意求救。
She had gestured dismissively before turning away. = 她转身之前不屑地挥了挥手。


将来时:
He will gesture towards the stage when it's your turn. = 轮到你的时候他会朝舞台示意。
She will gesture for silence. = 她会示意大家安静。


虚拟语气:
If he had gestured sooner, we might have avoided the accident. = 如果他早点示意，我们也许就能避免事故。
I wish she had gestured more clearly. = 我希望她能更清晰地示意。


被动语态:
His intentions were gestured at, but never explicitly stated. = 他的意图是通过手势表达的，但从未明确说明。
The instructions were gestured poorly. = 指示的手势很糟糕。


疑问句:
Was he gesturing towards the building? = 他是在朝那栋建筑做手势吗？
Are they gesturing to each other across the room? = 他们在房间对面互相做手势吗？


否定句:
He didn't gesture at all during the presentation. = 他在演示过程中根本没有做任何手势。
She didn't gesture towards the map. = 她没有指着地图做手势。
