non-prosecution: /ˌnɒnˌprɒsɪˈkjuːʃən/ | n. | 不起诉；免于起诉 | The decision not to prosecute someone for a crime.  This can be due to insufficient evidence, a plea bargain, or other reasons. | The prosecutor decided on non-prosecution due to lack of evidence. = 检察官由于缺乏证据而决定不起诉。  The defendant accepted a plea bargain resulting in non-prosecution. = 被告接受了认罪协议，结果免于起诉。|  prosecution, indictment | conviction |
