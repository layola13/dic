position-papers: /pəˈzɪʃən ˌpeɪpərz/ | n. |立场文件，政策文件 | A position paper is a formal statement that explains the position of an organization or individual on a particular issue. It is often used to influence public opinion or to inform decision-makers.  | Example: The government released a position paper outlining its strategy for tackling climate change.  政府发布了一份立场文件，概述了其应对气候变化的战略。| 近义词：policy statement, white paper, manifesto | 反义词：N/A
