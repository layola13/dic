hunk's:/hʌŋks/| n.|大块；大块头；英俊男子|A hunk is a large, thick piece of something, especially food. It can also refer to a large, strong, and attractive man.|例句：He carved a hunk of cheese. 他切了一大块奶酪。

例句：She was completely smitten with the muscular hunk. 她完全被那个肌肉发达的英俊男子迷住了。|近义词：chunk, lump, Adonis, stud|反义词：crumb, bit, insignificant person
