rectitude:/ˈrektɪtjuːd/| n. |正直；公正；正确性 |The quality of being honest and morally correct; righteousness.  Rectitude involves adhering to high moral standards and acting with integrity.  It suggests a steadfast commitment to doing what is right, even when it is difficult. |例句：Her unwavering rectitude in the face of adversity earned her the respect of her colleagues.  她面对逆境时坚定不移的正直赢得了同事们的尊重。

例句：The judge's rectitude was beyond question.  法官的正直毋庸置疑。


近义词：integrity, honesty, uprightness, probity, virtue |反义词：dishonesty, corruption, wickedness, depravity
