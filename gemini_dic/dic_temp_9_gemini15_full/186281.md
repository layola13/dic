rhizotaxis:/ˌraɪzoʊˈtæksɪs/| n. |根系排列；根的发生顺序|The arrangement of roots on a plant, specifically the spatial pattern of their branching and growth.  It's a field of study in botany that explores the mathematical and biological principles governing root system architecture.|Example:  Studies on rhizotaxis reveal complex patterns in root branching that optimize resource acquisition.  根系排列的研究揭示了根系分支中复杂的模式，这些模式优化了资源获取。|近义词：root arrangement, root pattern|反义词：无|
