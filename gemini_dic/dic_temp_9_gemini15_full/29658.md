catfishes:/ˈkætˌfɪʃɪz/| n. |鲶鱼(复数)|Catfishes are a group of fish belonging to the order Siluriformes, characterized by their lack of scales and the presence of barbels around their mouths.|例句：Many species of catfishes are popular food sources.  许多种类的鲶鱼都是受欢迎的食物来源。  |例句： We saw several different types of catfishes in the aquarium. 我们在水族馆里看到了几种不同类型的鲶鱼。|近义词：whiskers(非正式)|反义词:None.
