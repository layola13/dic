preallotted:/ˌpriːəˈlɒtɪd/|/ˈpriːælɑːtɪd/| adj.|预先分配的；预先拨给的|Preallotted means something has been assigned or allocated in advance. It implies a plan or schedule where resources or tasks are distributed before they are needed.|例句：The tickets for the concert were preallotted to the members of the fan club. 音乐会的门票已预先分配给粉丝俱乐部的成员。

The company preallotted a budget for each department's projects. 公司预先为各部门的项目分配了预算。|近义词：preassigned, preallocated, predetermined|反义词：unallocated, unassigned
