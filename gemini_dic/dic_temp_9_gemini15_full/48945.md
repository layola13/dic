defuses:/dɪˈfjuːzɪz/ | vt. |化解；平息；使失效 | To remove or reduce the danger or tension of a difficult or explosive situation.  |例句：The police defused the bomb before it could explode.  警方在炸弹爆炸前将其化解。|例句：His calm demeanor helped defuse the tense situation. 他冷静的态度帮助平息了紧张的局势。|近义词：neutralize, alleviate, calm, pacify |反义词：aggravate, inflame, exacerbate |动词


USAGE->
