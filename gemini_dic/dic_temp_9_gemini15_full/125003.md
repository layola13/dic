mugs:/mʌɡz/| n.|马克杯，粗鲁的人|A mug is a relatively large cup, typically cylindrical in shape, with a handle, used for drinking beverages.  The word can also be used informally as a slang term for a gullible or stupid person.|例句：I enjoy my morning coffee in my favorite mug. = 我喜欢用我喜欢的马克杯喝我的早晨咖啡。

He's such a mug for falling for that scam. = 他真傻，居然会上当受骗。|近义词：cup, tankard, mugger(粗鲁的人)|反义词：glass(杯子，非马克杯)
