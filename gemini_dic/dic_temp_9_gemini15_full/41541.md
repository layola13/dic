coronets:/ˈkɒrənɛts/ | n. |小皇冠；头饰 | A small crown, often worn by a nobleman or noblewoman.  It is typically smaller and less ornate than a full crown. | Example: The princess wore a delicate coronet of pearls and diamonds. 公主戴着一顶精致的珍珠和钻石小皇冠。|近义词： diadem, circlet |反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting lack of royalty or grandeur could be considered antonyms in context.
