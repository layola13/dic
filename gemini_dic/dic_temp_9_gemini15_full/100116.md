interpretative: /ɪnˈtɜːrprɪtətɪv/ | adj. | 解释性的；诠释性的 | Relating to or involving interpretation, especially of artistic works.  It suggests a subjective understanding or explanation rather than a purely objective one. |例句：The professor gave an interpretative dance performance, conveying deep emotional meaning. 教授进行了一场诠释性舞蹈表演，传达了深刻的情感意义。

例句：His interpretative analysis of the poem revealed hidden layers of meaning. 他对这首诗的诠释性分析揭示了隐藏的深层含义。


近义词：explanatory, expository, analytical, subjective

反义词：objective, literal
