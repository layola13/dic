kitchenette:/ˌkɪtʃəˈnet/| n. |小厨房；简易厨房 |A kitchenette is a small kitchen, often found in apartments or hotel rooms, with limited cooking facilities.  It typically includes a sink, a small refrigerator, and perhaps a hot plate or microwave oven, but lacks a full-sized oven and stove. |例句：The apartment was small but had a functional kitchenette. 这套公寓很小，但有一个功能齐全的小厨房。

例句：The hotel room offered a kitchenette, allowing guests to prepare simple meals.  酒店房间提供了一个小厨房，让客人可以准备简单的饭菜。


近义词：mini-kitchen, compact kitchen, small kitchen |反义词：full kitchen, large kitchen
