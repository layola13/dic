self-restriction: /ˌsɛlf rɪˈstrɪkʃən/ | n. |自我约束，自我限制 |The act of limiting or controlling oneself, one's actions, or one's desires.  It often implies a conscious effort to avoid excess or undesirable behavior. |例句：Through self-restriction, she successfully lost weight. 通过自我约束，她成功地减肥了。|例句：His self-restriction on spending helped him save money for a down payment. 他对支出的自我限制帮助他攒够了首付的钱。|近义词：self-control, self-discipline, abstinence, restraint |反义词：indulgence, excess, abandon
