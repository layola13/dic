regulations:/ˌreɡjuˈleɪʃənz/| n.|规章制度，规程|Rules or directives made and maintained by an authority.  Regulations are designed to control or govern conduct. They can be formal laws or informal guidelines.|例句：The company has strict regulations about workplace safety. 公司对工作场所安全有严格的规定。|例句：New regulations on food safety were introduced last year. 去年实施了新的食品安全条例。|近义词：rules, laws, guidelines, ordinances, bylaws|反义词：chaos, disorder, freedom
