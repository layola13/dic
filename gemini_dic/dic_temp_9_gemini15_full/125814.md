murmurers:/ˈmɜːrmərərz/| n. |低声抱怨者；窃窃私语者 |Those who murmur or speak in low, indistinct tones, often expressing discontent or complaint.  They may be voicing their grievances quietly or secretly. |例句：The murmurers in the back of the hall were finally silenced by the speaker.  大厅后面窃窃私语的人最终被演讲者制止了。


例句：The murmurers against the new policy were eventually ignored. 反对新政策的那些低声抱怨者最终被忽视了。


近义词：whisperers, complainers, grumblers


反义词：praisers, supporters, approvers


词性: 名词
