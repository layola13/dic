ripple:/ˈrɪpəl/| n. vi. |涟漪，波纹；影响，冲击波| a small wave on the surface of water, or a slight movement or effect that spreads through a group of people or things |例句：A gentle breeze caused ripples on the lake.  一阵轻风在湖面上泛起涟漪。
The news of the merger sent ripples through the financial markets. 并购的消息在金融市场引起震动。|近义词：wave, undulation, tremor |反义词：calm, stillness

USAGE->

一般现在时:
The pebble ripples the water. = 小石子在水中激起涟漪。
Concerns about the economy ripple through the markets. = 对经济的担忧在市场上引起波澜。


一般过去时:
The stone rippled the calm surface of the pond. = 石头扰乱了池塘平静的水面。
The scandal rippled through the company, damaging its reputation. = 丑闻在公司内部蔓延，损害了其声誉。


现在完成时:
The news of the earthquake has rippled across the globe. = 地震的消息已传遍全球。
The effects of the policy change have rippled throughout the industry. = 政策变化的影响已波及整个行业。


过去完成时:
The initial shock had rippled through the community before help arrived. = 在救援到来之前，最初的震惊已经波及整个社区。
Word of the impending layoffs had rippled throughout the department before the official announcement. = 在正式宣布裁员之前，即将裁员的消息已经在部门内传开了。


将来时:
The announcement will ripple through the industry, causing uncertainty. = 这一声明将波及整个行业，造成不确定性。
The impact of the new technology will ripple across various sectors of the economy. = 新技术的冲击将波及经济的各个部门。


虚拟语气:
If the stone had been thrown harder, it would have rippled the water more significantly. = 如果石头扔得更用力，它就会在水面上激起更大的涟漪。
If the news had not rippled so quickly, the market might not have reacted so strongly. = 如果消息没有传播得那么快，市场可能不会反应如此强烈。


被动语态:
The pond was rippled by the wind. = 池塘被风吹起了涟漪。
The company's image was rippled by the negative publicity. = 该公司的形象因负面宣传而受损。


疑问句:
Does the news ripple through the community? = 这则新闻在社区中引起反响了吗？
Will the effect of this decision ripple throughout the organization? = 这个决定的影响会波及整个组织吗？


否定句:
The news did not ripple through the small town. = 这条消息并没有在小镇上传开。
The calm surface of the water did not ripple. = 平静的水面没有泛起涟漪。
