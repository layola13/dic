paternal:/pəˈtɜːnəl/| adj.|父亲的；父系的；像父亲一样的|Relating to or characteristic of a father.  It can refer to a father's role, authority, or influence;  or the relationship between a father and his child. It can also describe something that resembles a father in its qualities, such as being protective or authoritative.|例句：He showed paternal concern for his employees. 他对他的员工表现出父亲般的关怀。

例句：The paternal instinct is strong in many animals.  许多动物都有很强的父性本能。


近义词：fatherly, fatherlike, patriarchal

反义词：maternal


词性：形容词
