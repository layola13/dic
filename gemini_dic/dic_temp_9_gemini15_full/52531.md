difficulties:/ˈdɪfɪkəltiz/| n.|困难，难题 | Difficulties are challenges that hinder progress or cause problems.  They can be obstacles, setbacks, or impediments.  Difficulties can be of various types, ranging from minor inconveniences to major crises. |例句：We encountered many difficulties during our journey. 我们在旅途中遇到了许多困难。

例句：The project faced numerous difficulties due to lack of funding.  由于资金不足，该项目面临许多困难。


近义词：problems, challenges, obstacles, hardships, troubles, setbacks

反义词：ease, simplicity


词性: 名词
