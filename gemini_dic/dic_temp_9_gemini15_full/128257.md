neuromere:/ˈnʊərəˌmɪər/| n. |神经节段，神经节元|A segment of the developing nervous system in a vertebrate embryo, typically giving rise to a spinal nerve or cranial nerve.|The developing brain and spinal cord are initially segmented into neuromeres.  Developing embryos exhibit distinct neuromeres.|近义词：somite (in the context of segmentation)|反义词：无
