desmogging:/dɪˈsmɑːɡɪŋ/ | n. | 排放检测；尾气检测 | The process of inspecting a vehicle's emission control system to ensure it meets environmental regulations.  It often involves testing the exhaust gases for pollutants. | 例句：The car failed its desmogging test and needs repairs. 这辆车尾气检测不合格，需要维修。 | 例句：Regular desmogging is crucial for maintaining air quality. 定期进行尾气检测对于维护空气质量至关重要。| 近义词：emission testing, exhaust testing | 反义词：无
