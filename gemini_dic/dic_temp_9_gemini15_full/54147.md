disengaging: /ˌdɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪŋ/ | v. | 解脱；脱离；使脱离；使分开 |  The act of freeing oneself or something from an entanglement or connection.  It implies a process of separating or becoming unattached. |


例句：

He was disengaging himself from the complicated project. 他正在摆脱那个复杂的项目。

The soldiers were disengaging from the enemy forces. 士兵们正在脱离敌军。


近义词：detaching, separating, disconnecting, releasing, uncoupling


反义词：engaging, connecting, attaching, coupling


词性：动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He disengages the gears smoothly. 他顺利地挂空挡。
She disengages from the conversation politely. 她礼貌地结束了谈话。


一般过去时:
He disengaged the clutch before stopping the car. 他在停车前松开了离合器。
She disengaged herself from the difficult situation. 她从困境中解脱出来。


现在完成时:
He has disengaged himself from the family business. 他已经脱离了家族企业。
She has disengaged from the online game. 她已经退出了网络游戏。


过去完成时:
He had disengaged the safety before firing the weapon. 他在开火前已经解除了保险。
She had disengaged herself from the toxic relationship. 她已经摆脱了那段有毒的关系。


将来时:
He will disengage the machine once the process is complete.  流程完成后，他会关闭机器。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

词源:

dis- (否定前缀) + engage (使从事；使参与)  The prefix "dis-" indicates the opposite or reversal of the action.  Therefore, "disengaging" means the opposite of engaging—to become free or detached.
