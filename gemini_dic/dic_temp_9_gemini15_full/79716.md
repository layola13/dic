glue: /ɡluː/ | n. v. | 胶水；粘合剂；胶合；粘贴 |  A sticky substance that is used to join things together.  |例句：I need some glue to fix this broken vase.  我需要一些胶水来修补这个破花瓶。


例句：He glued the two pieces of wood together. 他把两块木头粘在一起。


近义词：adhesive, cement, paste | 反义词：separation, detachment


词源:  The word "glue" comes from the Old English word "glu", which ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic *kleuō, meaning "to stick" or "to adhere".  This root is related to other words in Germanic languages with similar meanings.
