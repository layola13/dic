dupper:/ˈdʌpər/| adj.|  时髦的；讲究的；漂亮的 |  Stylish; dapper; neat in appearance.  A dapper gentleman is typically well-dressed and has a sophisticated appearance.  The word often implies a degree of elegance and attention to detail in one's attire and grooming. | 例句：He looked very dapper in his new suit. 他穿着新西装显得非常精神。

The young man was quite dapper, with his perfectly knotted tie and polished shoes.  这位年轻人非常讲究，领带系得整整齐齐，鞋子擦得锃亮。|近义词：stylish, elegant, smart, chic |反义词：sloppy, unkempt, dowdy
