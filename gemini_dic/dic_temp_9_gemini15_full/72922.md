fonder:/ˈfɒndər/ | adj. | 更喜欢的，更疼爱的 | More loving; showing or feeling greater affection.  |She was fonder of her younger son.|她更疼爱她的幼子。|loving, dear, cherished | indifferent, apathetic |形容词
