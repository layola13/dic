for good measure:/fɔːr ɡʊd ˈmɛʒə(r)/| adv. phrase| 额外地；为了保险起见；多此一举地 |  To add something extra as a precaution or for emphasis, often more than is strictly necessary.  | 例句：I added a little more salt for good measure.  我额外加了一点盐，以防万一。

例句：She gave him an extra cookie for good measure. 她给了他一个额外的饼干，作为额外奖励。


近义词：just in case, to be on the safe side, as an added precaution, additionally, besides

反义词：minimally, sparingly, insufficiently


词性：副词词组
