encephalitogenicity: /ɛnˌsefælɪtədʒɛˈnɪsəti/ | n. |脑炎诱发性；致脑炎性 | The ability of a substance or agent to cause encephalitis (inflammation of the brain).  It refers to the potential of a particular substance, such as a virus or toxin, to trigger an inflammatory response in the brain, leading to encephalitis. |Example: The study investigated the encephalitogenicity of the newly discovered virus. 这项研究调查了新发现病毒的脑炎诱发性。

|近义词: neurotoxicity (神经毒性),  inflammatory potential (炎症潜能)|反义词: neuroprotection (神经保护)|词性: 名词
