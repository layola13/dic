non-importation:/ˌnɒnɪmpɔːrˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 不进口；拒绝进口 | The act of refusing to import goods, usually as a form of protest or economic pressure.  It's often used in a political or economic context to boycott goods from a particular country or region. | Example: The colonists engaged in non-importation of British goods to protest taxation without representation.  殖民者拒绝进口英国商品以抗议无代表权的税收。 | 近义词：boycott, embargo | 反义词：importation
