prayable:/ˈpreɪəbəl/| adj.| 可祈祷的；适宜祈祷的 |  Worthy of being prayed for or to; suitable for prayer.  This word is rarely used in modern English and often found in older texts. | 例句：The suffering of the innocent are prayable.  无辜者的苦难值得祈祷。


The situation is prayable; let us offer our prayers.  这种情况值得祈祷；让我们祈祷吧。| 近义词：deserving of prayer, worthy of supplication | 反义词：unworthy of prayer, abhorrent
