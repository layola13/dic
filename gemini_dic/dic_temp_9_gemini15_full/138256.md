not-for-profit:/ˌnɑːt fɔːrˈprɒfɪt/|/ˌnɑːt fɔːrˈprɒfɪt/| adj. |非营利的；公益的 |Describes an organization that does not aim to make a profit, but rather focuses on a social cause or mission.  Its revenue is typically used to further its mission rather than distributed to owners or shareholders. |Many not-for-profit organizations rely on donations and grants.|近义词：charitable, philanthropic |反义词：for-profit, commercial
