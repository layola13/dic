country:/ˈkʌntri/|/ˈkʌntrɪ/| n. |国家；乡村；故乡 |A country is a nation with its own government and territory.  A country can also refer to a rural area. |例句：China is a large and populous country. 中国是一个幅员辽阔、人口众多的国家。

The countryside is beautiful in spring. 春天的乡村景色很美。|近义词：nation, state, land |反义词：city, town

This country produces a lot of rice. = 这个国家生产很多稻米。
Many people visit this country every year. = 每年很多人访问这个国家。


一般过去时:
