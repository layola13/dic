revolution:/ˌrevəˈluːʃən/ | n. v. |革命，旋转；彻底变革 |A fundamental change in power or organizational structures that takes place in a relatively short period of time.  It can be political, social, technological, or scientific.|例句：The American Revolution was a pivotal moment in history. 美国革命是历史上一个关键时刻。 The Earth's revolution around the sun takes one year. 地球绕太阳旋转一周需要一年时间。|近义词：upheaval, overturn, transformation, rotation |反义词：stagnation, status quo, conservation


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The Earth revolves around the sun. = 地球绕着太阳旋转。
She revolutionizes the industry with her innovative ideas. = 她用她的创新理念彻底改变了这个行业。


一般过去时:
The French Revolution changed France forever. = 法国大革命永远地改变了法国。
He revolutionized the way we communicate. = 他彻底改变了我们的沟通方式。


现在完成时:
Technology has revolutionized communication. = 科技已经彻底改变了通讯方式。
The company has undergone several revolutions in its management structure. = 这家公司在其管理结构上经历了几次变革。


过去完成时:
Before the Industrial Revolution, life was very different. = 在工业革命之前，生活大不相同。
The team had already revolutionized their approach to the project. = 团队已经彻底改变了他们处理项目的方式。


将来时:
This new technology will revolutionize the healthcare industry. = 这项新技术将彻底改变医疗保健行业。
The upcoming changes will revolutionize the way we work.  = 即将来临的变化将彻底改变我们的工作方式。


虚拟语气:
If we had a revolution, things would be different. = 如果我们进行了一场革命，情况就会不同。
I wish they would revolutionize the education system. = 我希望他们能彻底改革教育制度。


被动语态:
The country was revolutionized by the young generation. = 这个国家被年轻一代彻底改变了。
The process has been revolutionized by the new software. = 这个过程已经被新的软件彻底改变了。


疑问句:
Will this discovery revolutionize medicine? = 这一发现会彻底改变医学吗？
Did the invention revolutionize the way we travel? = 这个发明是否彻底改变了我们的出行方式？


否定句:
The reforms did not revolutionize the education system. = 这些改革并没有彻底改变教育体系。
His ideas did not revolutionize the field. = 他的想法并没有彻底改变这个领域。



PHRASE->

a revolution in: 一场…方面的革命
cultural revolution: 文化大革命

scientific revolution: 科学革命
