goober:/ˈɡuːbər/| n. | 花生；傻瓜；笨蛋 | A goober is a peanut, especially a peanut in its shell.  It can also be a slang term for a foolish or stupid person. |例句：He planted goobers in his garden. 他在他的花园里种了花生。He's such a goober! 他真是个傻瓜！|近义词：peanut (for the plant/nut),  idiot, fool, moron |反义词：genius, intelligent, smart
