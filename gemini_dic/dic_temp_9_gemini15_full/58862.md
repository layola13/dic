durable: /ˈdʊrəbl/ | adj. | 耐久的；持久的；耐用的 |  lasting for a long time without breaking or becoming weaker; able to withstand wear, pressure, or damage. |


例句：

This watch is very durable; I've had it for ten years and it still works perfectly. 这块手表非常耐用；我已经用了十年了，它仍然完好无损地工作着。

The material is durable enough to withstand heavy use.  这种材料足够耐用，可以承受重度使用。


近义词：lasting, enduring, strong, resistant, tough, robust

反义词：fragile, brittle, weak, flimsy, delicate


词性：形容词
