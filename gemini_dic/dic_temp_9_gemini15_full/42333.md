counter-title:/ˈkaʊntɚˌtaɪtl/| n.| 副标题，次标题 | A counter-title is a secondary title, often used to provide additional information or context to the main title. It's usually smaller and less prominent than the main title. | Example: The main title of the book is "The Adventures of Tom Sawyer," and the counter-title is "A Novel."  主标题是《汤姆·索亚历险记》，副标题是“一部小说”。|近义词：subtitle, secondary title |反义词：main title, principal title

Interrogative Sentence:
