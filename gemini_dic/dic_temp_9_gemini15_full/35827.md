cockler:/ˈkɒklə(r)/| n.| 采集贻贝的人；贻贝壳；(尤指) 牡蛎壳|A cockler is a person who collects cockles, small edible shellfish.  The term can also refer to the shell of a cockle or, more broadly, to an oyster shell.|例句：The cockler spent hours on the beach collecting cockles. 采集贻贝的人在海滩上花了数小时采集贻贝。|例句：He found a beautiful cockle shell on the shore. 他在岸边发现了一个美丽的贻贝壳。|近义词： shellfish gatherer,  cockle picker|反义词： N/A|名词
