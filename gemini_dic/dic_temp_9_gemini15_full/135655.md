nonpreferability:/ˌnɒnˌprefərəˈbɪləti/ | n. |非优选性，非优越性 |The state or quality of not being preferable; lack of preference.  It often refers to a situation where one option is less desirable than another, but not necessarily undesirable.  |Example: The nonpreferability of the proposed solution led to its rejection.  该方案的非优选性导致其被否决。|近义词：undesirability, unattractiveness, inferiority |反义词：preferability, superiority |名词
