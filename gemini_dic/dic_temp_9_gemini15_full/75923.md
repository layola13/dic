fungus:/ˈfʌŋɡəs/| n.|真菌|A fungus is any member of the group of eukaryotic organisms that includes microorganisms such as yeasts and molds, as well as the more familiar mushrooms.  Fungi are classified as a kingdom separate from the plants, animals, and bacteria. They are heterotrophic, meaning they obtain their nutrients from other organisms.|Example: Mushrooms are a type of fungus.|近义词：mold, yeast, mildew|反义词：N/A|名词
