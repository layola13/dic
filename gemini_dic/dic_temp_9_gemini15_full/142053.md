英文解析：
1.  As a noun, "organs" refers to a part of an animal or plant that performs a specific function.  This is the most common usage.  Examples include the heart, lungs, liver, kidneys, etc. in animals, and roots, leaves, stems, etc. in plants.
2.  "Organs" can also refer to an organized body or group of people, often with a specific purpose or function, such as a government organ or an organ of the United Nations.
3.  Historically, and still in some contexts, "organs" refers to a musical instrument, usually a large keyboard instrument with pipes.
