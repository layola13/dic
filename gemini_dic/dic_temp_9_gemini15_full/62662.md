engager:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒər/| vt. n.| 吸引；占用；使参与；雇用；约定；攻击 |To capture the attention or interest of someone; to occupy or involve someone; to hire someone; to make a commitment; to attack. |例句：The movie engaged the audience from beginning to end.  这部电影从头到尾都吸引了观众。  |近义词：attract, involve, employ, commit, attack |反义词：bore, disengage, dismiss, release

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He engages in many extracurricular activities. = 他参与许多课外活动。
The company engages new employees every year. = 公司每年都雇用新员工。


一般过去时:
She engaged a lawyer to handle her case. = 她聘请了一位律师来处理她的案子。
They engaged in a heated debate. = 他们进行了一场激烈的辩论。


现在完成时:
He has engaged himself in the project for months. = 他已经投入这个项目好几个月了。
The army has engaged the enemy. = 军队已经与敌人交战。


过去完成时:
They had engaged the hall for the party before the tickets sold out. = 门票售罄前，他们已经预订了大厅用于派对。
She had engaged herself to him before she knew his true nature. = 在她了解他的真实性格之前，她就已与他订婚了。


将来时:
We will engage a professional cleaner for the event. = 我们将为活动聘请一名专业的清洁工。
The company will engage in more international trade next year. = 公司明年将进行更多国际贸易。


虚拟语气:

I wish I could engage more effectively with my colleagues. = 我希望我能更有效地与同事沟通。

The soldiers were engaged in heavy fighting. = 士兵们参与了激烈的战斗。


疑问句:

否定句:
