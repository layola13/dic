dueting:/ˈdjuːɪtɪŋ/| n.|二重唱，对唱|The act of singing a duet; a performance of a duet.  It refers to the collaborative singing of two voices, often with a harmonized melody.|例句：The soprano and tenor gave a breathtaking dueting of the famous aria.  女高音和男高音演唱了那首著名咏叹调，令人叹为观止。

例句：Their dueting was a highlight of the concert. 他们的二重唱是音乐会的亮点。


近义词：singing together, vocal collaboration,  harmonizing


反义词：solo,  monologue


词性:名词
