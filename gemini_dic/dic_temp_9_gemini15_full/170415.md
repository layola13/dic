prowling:/ˈpraʊlɪŋ/| v.|潜行；徘徊；悄悄走动|To move about stealthily or in a predatory manner, especially in search of prey or something to steal.|例句：A lion was prowling in the jungle.  一只狮子在丛林里潜行。|例句：The thief was prowling the streets looking for an unlocked car.  小偷在街上徘徊寻找没锁的车。|近义词：stalking, creeping, slinking|反义词：standing still, remaining stationary


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat is prowling around the house. = 猫正在房子周围潜行。
He prowls the streets at night. = 他晚上在街上徘徊。

一般过去时:
The tiger prowled through the tall grass. = 老虎在高高的草丛中潜行。
She prowled the shops looking for a bargain. = 她逛商店寻找便宜货。

现在完成时:
The police have been prowling the area for days. = 警方几天来一直在该地区巡逻。
He has prowled the streets for years. = 他在街上游荡多年了。

过去完成时:
The burglar had prowled the neighborhood before breaking in. = 窃贼在闯入之前已经在这个街区潜伏过。
The cat had prowled the garden all night. = 猫整夜都在花园里潜行。

将来时:
The lion will prowl the savanna tonight. = 今晚狮子将在热带草原上潜行。
They will prowl the streets looking for trouble. = 他们将在街上游荡寻找麻烦。

虚拟语气:
If he were prowling around, I would be worried. = 如果他在附近潜行，我会担心。
I wish the bear weren't prowling so close to the campsite. = 我希望熊不要在营地附近徘徊。

被动语态:
The streets were prowled by suspicious figures. = 可疑人物在街上徘徊。
The area is being prowled by the police. = 该地区正在被警方巡逻。

疑问句:
Is the dog prowling again? = 狗又在潜行了？
Were they prowling the building before the robbery? = 抢劫案发生前他们在建筑物里潜行吗？

否定句:
The cat wasn't prowling tonight. = 猫今晚没有潜行。
They didn't prowl the streets last night. = 他们昨晚没有在街上游荡。


PHRASE->
prowling around = 在...附近徘徊
prowling for = 寻找（猎物等）
prowling the streets = 在街上游荡
prowling the night = 夜间潜行
on the prowl = 正在寻找（猎物或机会）
