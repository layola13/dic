intertropical:/ˌɪntəˈtrɒpɪkəl/| adj.|热带的，位于热带的| Situated or occurring in the tropics, between the Tropic of Cancer and the Tropic of Capricorn.  The term refers to the geographical region lying between these two lines of latitude.| 例句：The intertropical convergence zone is a region of low atmospheric pressure near the equator.|近义词：tropical, equatorial|反义词：polar, arctic, antarctic
