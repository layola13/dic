saltbox:/ˈsɔːltbɒks/ /ˈsɔːltˌbɑːks/ | n. |盐盒，盐箱；（美）一种长而低矮的房屋|A saltbox is a type of house, typically long and low with a steeply pitched roof that extends down almost to the ground at the back.  The name derives from its resemblance to the shape of a salt box.  It is a common style of house in New England. |例句：The old saltbox house stood proudly on the hill overlooking the ocean. 这座古老的盐盒式房屋自豪地矗立在俯瞰大海的山丘上。

例句：Many historic saltbox houses are still preserved in the countryside. 许多历史悠久的盐盒式房屋仍然保留在乡村地区。
