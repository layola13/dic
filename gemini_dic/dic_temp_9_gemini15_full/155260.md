phalanxed:/fəˈlæŋkst/| adj.|成密集队形的；排列成方阵的|arranged or moving in a closely packed formation, like a phalanx (a military formation of ancient Greece).  The word evokes images of tightly-packed soldiers, or any similarly dense arrangement.|例句：The soldiers phalanxed themselves against the enemy.  士兵们排列成密集队形以对抗敌人。

The protestors phalanxed in front of the government building. 抗议者们在政府大楼前排列成密集队形。
