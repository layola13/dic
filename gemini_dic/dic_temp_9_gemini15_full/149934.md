palliations:/ˌpælɪˈeɪʃənz/ | n. |缓解；减轻；安慰 | The act of palliating; the alleviation of symptoms without curing the underlying disease.  Palliations focus on improving the quality of life for patients with serious illnesses.  |例句：The doctor prescribed medication for the palliation of his pain.  医生开了药来缓解他的疼痛。

例句：Palliative care provides palliations for patients facing life-threatening illnesses. 姑息治疗为面临危及生命的疾病的患者提供缓解。


近义词：relief, mitigation, alleviation, comfort | 反义词：aggravation, exacerbation | 词性：名词
