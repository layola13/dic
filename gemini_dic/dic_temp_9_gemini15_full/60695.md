electrify:/ɪˈlɛktrɪfaɪ/| vt.|使通电；使电气化；使兴奋；使激动|to supply with electricity; to make something exciting or thrilling |例句：The town was electrified last year. 去年该镇通了电。

The news electrified the whole nation. 这消息使全国上下为之振奋。

近义词：energize, excite, thrill |反义词：de-energize, dull
