carpellum:/kɑːrˈpɛləm/| n. |心皮|The carpellum is the female reproductive organ of a flower, typically consisting of a stigma, style, and ovary.  It is a modified leaf that encloses the ovules.  |Example: The carpel, or carpellum, contains the ovules which develop into seeds after fertilization.  心皮包含胚珠，受精后胚珠发育成果实。|近义词：pistil (although pistil can refer to multiple carpels fused together)|反义词：stamen (male reproductive organ)
