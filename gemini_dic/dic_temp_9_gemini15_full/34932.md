cloakroom: /ˈkloʊkruːm/ | n. |衣帽间；盥洗室|A room in a public building where coats, bags, and other belongings can be left; also a restroom. |例句：We left our coats in the cloakroom. 我们把外套放在衣帽间了。

例句：The theatre's cloakroom was crowded before the show.  剧院的衣帽间在演出前挤满了人。

近义词：coatroom, restroom (when referring to a toilet), lavatory

反义词：无
