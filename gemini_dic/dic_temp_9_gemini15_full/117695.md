melainotype:/ˌmɛləˈɪnoʊtaɪp/| n. |黑素细胞型；黑素细胞类型|A melainoype refers to a classification or type of melanocyte, a cell that produces melanin, the pigment responsible for skin and hair color.  Different melainoypes may exhibit variations in their melanin production, contributing to diverse skin and hair pigmentation patterns.  The term is primarily used in scientific contexts, particularly in dermatology and genetics.| Example:  The study investigated the correlation between specific melainoypes and susceptibility to skin cancer.  这项研究调查了特定黑素细胞型与皮肤癌易感性之间的相关性。|近义词：melanocyte type,  pigment cell type |反义词：无
