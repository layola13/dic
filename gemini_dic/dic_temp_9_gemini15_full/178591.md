recouch:/ˌriːˈkaʊtʃ/| vt.|重新布置；重新陈述；重新安排|To arrange or position (something) again; to restate or rephrase something.|例句：The furniture was recouched to accommodate more guests.  家具重新摆放以容纳更多客人。|例句：He recouched his argument to make it clearer. 他重新陈述了他的论点以使其更清晰。|近义词：rearrange, reposition, reformulate, rephrase|反义词：disarrange, misrepresent
