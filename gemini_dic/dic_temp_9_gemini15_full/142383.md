orthosilicate:/ˌɔːrθoʊˈsɪlɪkeɪt/| n. |原硅酸盐|A silicate mineral in which the silicon atom is bonded to four oxygen atoms, forming a tetrahedral SiO4 unit.  These units are typically independent and not linked together to form chains or sheets. Orthoslicates are a common group of minerals found in various geological settings.|Example: Olivine is a common example of an orthosilicate mineral.橄榄石是原硅酸盐矿物的一个常见例子。|近义词:nesosilicate|反义词:phyllosilicate, inosilicate
