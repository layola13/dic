dugway:/ˈduːɡweɪ/| n.|栈道；人行道；(尤指)山间小路|A dugway is a steep, winding road or path, especially one cut into a hillside.  It often refers to a road built along a steep slope, often with switchbacks. |例句：The dugway was treacherous in the snow.  这条栈道在雪天很危险。|例句：The hikers took the dugway down the mountain. 登山者沿着山间小路下山。|近义词：path, trail, road, track|反义词：highway (in terms of scale and construction)|词性：名词
