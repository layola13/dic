manihot:/ˈmænɪhɒt/| n. |木薯|Manihot is a genus of flowering plants in the spurge family, Euphorbiaceae.  The most important species is Manihot esculenta, commonly known as cassava or yuca, a starchy tuberous root that is a major food crop in many tropical regions. |The cassava plant, Manihot esculenta, is a vital source of carbohydrates for millions.|近义词：cassava, yuca|反义词：无|名词
