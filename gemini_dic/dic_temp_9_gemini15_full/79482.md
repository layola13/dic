globalising: /ˈɡloʊbəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 全球化；使全球化 | The process of making something worldwide in scope.  It often refers to the increasing interconnectedness and interdependence of countries through trade, technology, and cultural exchange. | 例句：The globalising of the economy has led to increased competition. = 经济全球化导致了竞争加剧。

The company is globalising its operations to reach new markets. = 这家公司正在将其业务全球化以开拓新市场。


近义词：internationalizing, worldwide, universalizing | 反义词：localizing, regionalizing

词源:
The word "globalising" is derived from the word "global," which in turn comes from the Latin word "globus," meaning "sphere" or "globe."  The "-ising" suffix indicates the process of becoming or making something global.
