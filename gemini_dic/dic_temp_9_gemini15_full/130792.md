nonannexable: /ˌnɒnænˈɛksəbl/ | adj. | 不可吞并的，不能兼并的 |  Not capable of being annexed or incorporated into another territory or state.  This term typically refers to territories or regions considered politically or geographically unsuitable for annexation due to various factors, including existing treaties, strong local resistance, or strategic considerations. |


例句：

The island nation maintained its sovereignty, proving itself nonannexable due to its strong military and international support. = 这个岛国保持了自己的主权，由于其强大的军事力量和国际支持，证明自己是不可吞并的。


The region's unique cultural heritage made it effectively nonannexable, despite the conquering nation's ambitions. =  该地区独特的文化遗产使其实际上不可吞并，尽管征服国家野心勃勃。


近义词：unincorporable, inalienable (in the context of territory)


反义词：annexable, incorporable


词性：形容词
