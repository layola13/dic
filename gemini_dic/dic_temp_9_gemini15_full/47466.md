dealfish:/ˈdiːlfɪʃ/| n. |海鳝|A dealfish is a deep-sea fish belonging to the family Trachipteridae.  They are characterized by their long, ribbon-like bodies and are often found in the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans.|例句：The dealfish, with its elongated body, is a fascinating creature of the deep sea.  海鳝，以其细长的身体，是深海中迷人的生物。|近义词：ribbonfish, oarfish|反义词：无
