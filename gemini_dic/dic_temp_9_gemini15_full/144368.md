outsteps:/ˈaʊtˌstɛps/| v.|超过，胜过；走出；超越|to go beyond the limits of (something); to surpass or exceed; to step outside|例句：His achievements outstep those of his contemporaries.他的成就超过了他的同时代人。

例句：She outstepped her authority by making that decision without consulting her boss. 她未经老板同意就做出了那个决定，超越了自己的职权范围。


近义词：surpass, exceed, transcend, outdo|反义词：underperform, lag behind|词性:动词
