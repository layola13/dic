It often implies a humorous or slightly off-kilter quality. |例句：The picture hung at a cockeyed angle.  照片歪歪斜斜地挂着。  His cockeyed optimism was infectious. 他古怪的乐观精神具有感染力。 |近义词：askew, awry, lopsided, craziness, eccentricity |反义词：straightness, accuracy, normalcy, sanity
