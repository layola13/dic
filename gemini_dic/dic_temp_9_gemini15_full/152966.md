pedigree:/ˈpedɪɡriː/| n. |血统；家谱；出身；祖先；(动物的)血统证明 |A pedigree is a record of the ancestors of a person or animal, especially one showing that the person or animal is of pure breed.  It can also refer to a person's or animal's lineage or background.  |例句：The horse has a fine pedigree, tracing back to famous racehorses. 这匹马血统高贵，可以追溯到著名的赛马。

例句：His family boasts a long and distinguished pedigree. 他的家族拥有悠久而显赫的家世。


近义词：lineage, ancestry, family tree, descent |反义词：unknown origins, humble beginnings
