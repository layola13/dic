disproved:/ˌdɪsˈpruːvd/| v. |证明是错误的；驳斥|To show that something is wrong or false.|The experiment disproved his theory.  实验证明了他的理论是错误的。|The new evidence disproved the defendant's alibi. 新证据驳斥了被告的不在场证明。|proved, confirmed |unproved, confirmed|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They disprove the myth regularly. = 他们定期驳斥这个神话。
Scientists disprove many hypotheses every year. = 科学家每年都会推翻许多假设。


一般过去时:
The experiment disproved the hypothesis. = 实验驳斥了这个假设。
He disproved the claim with compelling evidence. = 他用令人信服的证据驳斥了这一说法。


现在完成时:
Researchers have disproved many long-held beliefs. = 研究人员已经驳斥了许多长期存在的信念。
Scientists have disproved the theory through extensive research. = 科学家们通过广泛的研究驳斥了这一理论。


过去完成时:
They had disproved the theory before the conference. = 他们在会议之前就已经驳斥了该理论。
The evidence had disproved his initial assumptions. = 证据已经证明了他的最初假设是错误的。


将来时:
They will disprove the rival company's claims soon. = 他们很快就会驳斥竞争对手的说法。
Further research will disprove this outdated notion. = 进一步的研究将驳斥这种过时的观念。


虚拟语气:
If we had more data, we could disprove their theory. = 如果我们有更多的数据，我们就能驳斥他们的理论。
I wish I could disprove the rumors circulating. = 我希望我能驳斥正在流传的谣言。


被动语态:
The theory was disproved by new evidence. = 新证据驳斥了该理论。
His claims were disproved by the facts. = 事实驳斥了他的说法。


疑问句:
Has this hypothesis been disproved yet? = 这个假设已经被驳斥了吗？
Can this theory be disproved with current technology? = 用现有的技术能驳斥这个理论吗？


否定句:
They have not yet disproved the existence of extraterrestrial life. = 他们尚未驳斥外星生命存在的可能性。
The evidence did not disprove the defendant's claim. = 证据并没有驳斥被告的说法。
