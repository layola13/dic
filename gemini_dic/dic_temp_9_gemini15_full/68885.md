fastens:/ˈfæsnz/| vt.| 系紧；固定；使牢固 | To secure or attach something firmly.  The verb "fasten" implies a process of making something secure, often by tying, locking, or clipping. | 例句：She fastened her seatbelt. 她系上了安全带。

She fastened the button on her coat. 她扣上了外套上的扣子。


近义词：secure, attach, tie, close, bind | 反义词：loosen, unfasten, detach


词源:  From Old English *fæstan, meaning "to seize, hold fast," from Proto-Germanic *fastjan.  Related to words like "fast" (meaning firm or fixed).
