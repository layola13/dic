giber:/ˈdʒɪbər/| n. v.|胡言乱语；说话含糊不清 |  Gibber refers to unintelligible or nonsensical talk; rapid, incoherent speech. It can also be used as a verb meaning to speak incoherently. |例句：He started to gibber about aliens and government conspiracies. 他开始胡言乱语地谈论外星人政府阴谋。

The children were gibbering with excitement. 孩子们兴奋地胡言乱语。


近义词：babble, mutter, mumble, rant, rave |反义词：speak clearly, articulate


词源：源自古英语 *gibberen，意思是“说话含糊不清”。
