pre-Israelite: /ˌpriːˈɪzreɪˌlaɪt/ | adj. |以色列人之前的；前以色列的 | Relating to the time or people before the Israelites.  This term typically refers to the period and cultures preceding the emergence of the Israelite nation and their known history.  It encompasses various Canaanite groups and other inhabitants of the Levant. | The archaeological evidence from pre-Israelite settlements in the region reveals a complex tapestry of cultures. |近义词:Canaanite, pre-biblical |反义词:Israelite, post-Israelite
