groats:/ɡroʊts/ | n. |粗粒谷物；碾碎的谷物 | Hulled and coarsely crushed grain, such as oats, barley, or wheat.  Groats are often used as a breakfast cereal or in various dishes. | Example: Oatmeal is made from oat groats. 燕麦片是由燕麦粗粒制成的。  She added barley groats to her soup for extra texture. 她往汤里加了大麦粗粒以增加口感。|近义词：grain, cereal |反义词：flour (finely ground grain)
