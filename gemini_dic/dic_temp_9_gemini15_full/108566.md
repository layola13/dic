/ˌɛlsiːˈdiːz/ | n. |液晶显示器 |Liquid Crystal Displays;  electronic visual display that uses liquid crystals to form images.  LCDs are commonly used in televisions, computer monitors, and other electronic devices. |Example: My new laptop has a high-resolution LCD screen. 我的新笔记本电脑有一个高分辨率的液晶显示屏。|近义词：display, screen, monitor |反义词：CRT (cathode ray tube)
