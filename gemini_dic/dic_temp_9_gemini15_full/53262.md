direst:/ˈdaɪrɪst/| adj.| 最严重的；最糟糕的；极度的 | Most serious or extreme.  It implies a level of severity beyond simply "bad" or "serious." It suggests a critical or life-threatening situation. |例句：He faced the direst consequences of his actions. 他面临着他行为最严重的后果。

例句：The patient is in the direst need of medical attention. 病人最需要医疗护理。


近义词：severest, gravest, most urgent, most critical, desperate

反义词：mildest, least serious, insignificant


词性:形容词
