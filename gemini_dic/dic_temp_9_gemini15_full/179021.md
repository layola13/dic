redeliberating:/ˌriːdɪˈlɪbəreɪtɪŋ/| v. |重新审议；再考虑 |To consider something again, especially after a previous decision has been made.  This implies a more thorough or careful reconsideration than a simple rethinking. |例句：The jury is redeliberating the case after a key witness's testimony.  陪审团在一位关键证人的证词之后正在重新审议此案。
The committee is redeliberating the budget proposal due to unforeseen circumstances. 由于不可预见的情况，委员会正在重新审议预算提案。


近义词：reconsidering, reviewing, reconsidering, reexamining | 反义词：deciding, concluding, finalizing | 词性：动词
