noncorporations: /ˌnɒnkɔːpəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. |非公司组织；非法人团体 | Organizations or entities that are not incorporated as corporations.  They lack the legal structure and limited liability protections afforded to corporations.  Examples include sole proprietorships, partnerships, and limited liability partnerships (LLPs) in some contexts. | Example:  Many small businesses operate as noncorporations to avoid the complexities of corporate registration.  许多小型企业以非公司形式运营，以避免公司注册的复杂性。 | 近义词: unincorporated businesses, partnerships, sole proprietorships | 反义词: corporations, incorporated businesses
