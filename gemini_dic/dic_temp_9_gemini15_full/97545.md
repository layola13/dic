ingratiating:/ɪnˈɡreɪʃieɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |讨好的，谄媚的 |  Trying to gain favor or approval through flattery or excessive charm; often implying insincerity.  | 例句：His ingratiating manner made him popular, but also suspicious.  他的讨好方式使他很受欢迎，但也让人怀疑。


例句：She gave an ingratiating smile to the boss hoping for a promotion. 她对老板献媚地一笑，希望能升职。


近义词：flattering, fawning, obsequious, sycophantic | 反义词：disagreeable, off-putting, blunt, honest
