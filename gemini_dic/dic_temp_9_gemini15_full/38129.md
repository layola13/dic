comprehension:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛnʃən/ /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛnʃən/| n. |理解力；领悟；理解|The ability to understand something.  It involves grasping the meaning, significance, and implications of information. This can refer to understanding spoken or written language, complex ideas, or situations.|例句：His comprehension of the subject was remarkable.他的理解能力很强。

例句：The teacher assessed the students' comprehension through a quiz.老师通过测验评估学生的理解能力。


近义词：understanding, grasp, apprehension, perception|反义词：misunderstanding, confusion, ignorance|词性:名词
