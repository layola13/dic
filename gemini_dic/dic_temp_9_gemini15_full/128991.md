nippingly: /ˈnɪpɪŋli/ | adv. | 尖刻地；刺骨地；寒冷地 |  In a sharp, biting, or severely critical manner; extremely cold. |


例句：

She criticized his work nippingly. = 她尖刻地批评了他的作品。

The wind blew nippingly cold. = 风吹得刺骨地冷。


近义词：sharply, severely, cuttingly, frigidly

反义词：gently, mildly, warmly, kindly


词性: 副词
