communications: /ˌkəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 通信；通讯；沟通 | The exchange of information, ideas, or feelings.  It encompasses various methods and channels for conveying meaning. |例句：The company's communications strategy needs improvement.  这家公司的沟通策略需要改进。

例句：Effective communications are crucial for a successful team.  有效的沟通对于一个成功的团队至关重要。


近义词：correspondence, interaction, transmission, exchange | 反义词：silence, isolation, secrecy | 词性：名词
