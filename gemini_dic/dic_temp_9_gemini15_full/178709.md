recruiter:/rɪˈkruːtər/| n. |招聘人员，招聘官|A person who recruits people to join an organization or become a member of something.|The recruiter contacted me about a job opening.  招聘人员联系我关于一个职位空缺。|The company hired a new recruiter to fill its open positions. 公司聘请了一位新的招聘人员来填补空缺职位。|headhunter, employment agent|n/a


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The recruiter screens applicants daily. = 招聘人员每天筛选申请人。
He recruits talented individuals for his company. = 他为公司招聘有才能的人。


一般过去时:
The recruiter interviewed five candidates yesterday. = 招聘人员昨天面试了五位候选人。
She recruited many new employees last year. = 她去年招聘了许多新员工。


现在完成时:
The company has recruited over 100 employees this year. = 公司今年已经招聘了100多名员工。
They have recruited several top performers for the team. = 他们已经为团队招聘了几位顶尖人才。


过去完成时:
The company had already recruited the team before the project started. = 项目开始前，公司已经招聘好了团队。
He had recruited several candidates before finding the right one. = 他在找到合适的人选之前已经招聘了好几位候选人。


将来时:
The recruiter will interview more candidates next week. = 招聘人员下周将面试更多候选人。
We will recruit more staff as the business expands. = 随着业务扩张，我们将招聘更多员工。


虚拟语气:
If I were a recruiter, I would prioritize cultural fit. = 如果我是招聘人员，我会优先考虑文化契合度。
I wish the recruiter had called me sooner. = 我希望招聘人员早点打电话给我。


被动语态:
Many candidates were recruited by the company last year. = 去年公司招聘了许多候选人。
The position is being actively recruited for. = 正在积极招聘该职位。


疑问句:
Is the recruiter still accepting applications? = 招聘人员还在接受申请吗？
What kind of candidates is the recruiter looking for? = 招聘人员在寻找什么样的候选人？


否定句:
The recruiter didn't contact me about the position. = 招聘人员没有联系我关于这个职位。
She wasn't able to recruit anyone for the role. = 她没能为这个职位招聘到任何人。
