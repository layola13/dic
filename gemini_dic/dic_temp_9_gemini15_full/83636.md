词源:
haemo- (haem-)  from Greek  αἷμα (haima) meaning "blood"
globin- from Greek  γλοῦς (glous) meaning "glue" referring to the protein globin.
-meter from Greek  -μετρον (-metron) meaning "measure"

Therefore, haemoglobinometer literally means "blood-glue-measure" or "blood protein measuring instrument".
