incongenial:/ˌɪnkənˈdʒiːniəl/| adj. | 不合适的；不相容的；令人不快的 | Unpleasant; not suitable or agreeable.  Something incongruous is not in harmony or keeping with its surroundings or context.  It suggests a lack of compatibility or suitability. |


例句：

He found the atmosphere at the meeting incongenial.  他觉得会议的气氛令人不快。

Her personality was completely incongenial to his.  她的个性与他完全不相容。


近义词：unpleasant, disagreeable, incompatible, unsuitable, discordant

反义词：agreeable, pleasant, compatible, suitable, harmonious


词性：形容词
