foliages:/ˈfoʊliɪdʒ/| n.| 叶子；树叶；枝叶 |The mass of leaves of a plant or of plants.  It often refers to the leaves of trees and shrubs, considered collectively. |例句：The autumn foliage was spectacular. 秋天的树叶景色壮观。
例句：The dense foliage prevented us from seeing the house.茂密的树叶挡住了我们看房子的视线。|近义词：leaves, greenery, vegetation |反义词：barrenness
