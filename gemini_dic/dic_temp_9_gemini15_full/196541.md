sesban:/ˈsɛzbæn/| n.|野扁豆属植物；珊瑚豆；|A genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae,  commonly known as sesbanias.  They are often grown as ornamentals or for their edible pods and leaves.  Several species are important nitrogen-fixing plants used in agriculture.|例句：Sesbania grandiflora is a fast-growing tree species used in reforestation projects.  珊瑚豆是一种生长迅速的树种，用于植树造林项目。|近义词：  (depending on the specific species)  other legume plants,  e.g.,  certain types of beans.|反义词： N/A|名词
