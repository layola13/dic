nonwinner: /ˈnɑːnwɪnər/ | n. | 未获胜者，失败者 | A person or thing that has not won a competition or contest.  | Example:  He was a nonwinner in the election. 他在选举中落败了。  |近义词: loser, also-ran | 反义词: winner, champion
