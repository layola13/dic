milieus:/ˈmiːljuːz/| n. |环境；氛围；社会环境 |The social and cultural environment surrounding someone or something.  It refers to the setting, circumstances, or conditions in which something exists or occurs.  |例句：The artist's work reflects the milieus of his childhood.  艺术家的作品反映了他童年的环境。

例句：The political milieu of the 1960s was very different from today's. 20世纪60年代的政治环境与今天大相径庭。|近义词：environment, setting, context, atmosphere, surroundings |反义词：void, vacuum


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and does not conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.
