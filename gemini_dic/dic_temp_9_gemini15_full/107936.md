laryngograph:/ˈlærɪŋɡrɑːf/| n. |喉部描记器，喉检仪|An instrument used to record the movements of the vocal cords during speech or singing.  It measures the airflow and the vibrations of the vocal folds. |The laryngograph helped researchers understand the subtle variations in vocal cord movement during different vowel sounds.  喉部描记器帮助研究人员了解不同元音发音时声带运动的细微变化。|electroglottograph|  |n.
