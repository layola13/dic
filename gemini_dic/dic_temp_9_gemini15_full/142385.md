orthospermous: /ˌɔːrθoʊˈspɜːrməs/ | adj. | 直胚的 | Having a straight embryo;  referring to seeds in which the embryo is not curved or folded.  This term is primarily used in botany to describe a characteristic of seeds.  The embryo lies straight along the axis of the seed. | Example:  The seeds of this plant are orthospermous, unlike those of its curved-embryo relative.  直胚的种子与弯胚的亲属不同。 |近义词: straight-embryoed (less common) | 反义词: campylospermous (curved embryo)
