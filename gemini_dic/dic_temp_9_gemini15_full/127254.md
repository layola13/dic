nav:/næv/| n.|导航，航行，导航系统|Navigation;  a system for finding the way to a place, especially one used in a vehicle or ship.  It can also refer to the act of navigating.|例句：The ship's nav system guided us safely through the storm. 这艘船的导航系统引导我们安全地度过了风暴。|例句：Learning to use the nav app on my phone improved my sense of direction. 学习使用手机上的导航应用提高了我的方向感。|近义词：navigation, guidance, piloting|反义词：None directly opposite, but could be considered opposite to being lost or disoriented.
