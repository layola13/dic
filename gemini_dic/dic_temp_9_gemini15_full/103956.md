judicious:/dʒuˈdɪʃəs/| adj.|明智的；审慎的；有判断力的|Showing good judgment; wise and careful.  It implies a careful consideration of all factors before making a decision.|例句：The judge made a judicious decision in the case.  法官对该案做出了明智的裁决。

例句：Her judicious use of resources ensured the project's success. 她明智地利用资源，确保了项目的成功。


近义词：wise, prudent, sensible, discreet, circumspect|反义词：foolish, imprudent, rash, reckless, indiscreet

词源:
Middle English: from Old French *judicius, from Latin iudicius, from iudicare ‘judge’.  The word ultimately derives from the Latin verb *iudicare, meaning "to judge" or "to deem."
