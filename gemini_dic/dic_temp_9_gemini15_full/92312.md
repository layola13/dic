hypnotherapists:/ˌhɪpnoʊˈθerəpɪsts/ | n. |催眠治疗师 | A hypnotherapist is a person who is trained and qualified to use hypnotherapy to help people address various psychological and emotional issues.  They use hypnosis to induce a state of deep relaxation and heightened suggestibility to help clients overcome problems such as anxiety, phobias, and smoking addiction. |  Many people find hypnotherapists helpful in dealing with stress. |  therapist, hypnotist |  (no direct antonym, perhaps "psychoanalyst" if considering alternative therapies) | 名词
