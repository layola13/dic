intermingling:/ˌɪntərˈmɪŋɡlɪŋ/ | v. n. | 交融；混合；混杂 | The act of mixing or mingling together.  It can refer to the process of things becoming intertwined or the state of being intertwined. |例句：The sounds of laughter and music intermingled in the air.  欢笑声和音乐声在空中交融在一起。

The different cultures intermingled peacefully in the city.  不同的文化在这个城市里和平地融合在一起。


近义词：blend, mix, mingle, fuse, combine | 反义词：separate, segregate, isolate | 动词
