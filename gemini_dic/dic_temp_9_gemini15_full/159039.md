pneumatograph:/njuːˈmætəɡræf/| n. |呼吸描记器|A pneumatograph is an instrument used to record the movements of the chest during respiration.  It measures and graphically represents the changes in the volume of the chest cavity during breathing.  This data can be used to diagnose respiratory problems. |The doctor used a pneumatograph to assess the patient's breathing patterns. 医生用呼吸描记器评估病人的呼吸模式。|  respirograph, spirometer |  |n.
