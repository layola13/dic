contex:/ˈkɒntekst/ /ˈkɑːntekst/| n. |语境，上下文，背景|The meaning of a word can change depending on the context.  一个词的意思会根据语境而改变。|近义词：setting, background, situation |反义词：isolation, detachment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The context of the email is unclear. = 这封邮件的语境不清楚。
The context for this decision is complex. = 做出这个决定的背景很复杂。


一般过去时:
The context of the meeting was highly political. = 会议的背景高度政治化。
The context in which he made that statement was crucial. = 他发表那番话的语境至关重要。


现在完成时:
The historical context has significantly shaped our understanding. = 历史背景极大地影响了我们的理解。
The changing social context has impacted many industries. = 变化的社会背景已经影响了许多行业。


过去完成时:
The context before the war was very different. = 战争前的背景大相径庭。
The context that had been established by previous events was critical. = 由先前事件建立的背景至关重要。


将来时:
The future context will determine the success of the project. = 未来的背景将决定项目的成败。
The context in which the laws will be applied is yet to be seen. = 这些法律将被应用于何种背景还有待观察。


虚拟语气:
If the context were different, the outcome might have been better. = 如果语境不同，结果可能会更好。
I wish the context of the conversation had been clearer. = 我希望谈话的语境更清晰一些。


被动语态:
The context was misinterpreted by the audience. = 语境被观众误解了。
The context is often overlooked in these discussions. = 在这些讨论中，语境经常被忽视。


疑问句:
What is the context of this situation? = 这种情况的背景是什么？
Can you provide more context for your argument? = 你能为你的论点提供更多背景吗？


否定句:
The context was not fully explained. = 语境没有得到充分解释。
There was no context given for their actions. = 他们的行为没有给出任何背景。


PHRASE->

out of context = 脱离上下文
