intervisibility:/ˌɪntərˈvɪzəˈbɪləti/| n. |相互可见性，可互相看见|The ability of two or more points or places to be seen from each other.  This is often used in the context of urban planning, military strategy, and surveillance.  |Example: The architects ensured good intervisibility between the buildings to maximize natural light and create a sense of community. 建筑师们确保建筑物之间良好的相互可见性，以最大限度地利用自然光，并营造社区感。|近义词：mutual visibility, visual access |反义词：obscuration, blockage |名词
