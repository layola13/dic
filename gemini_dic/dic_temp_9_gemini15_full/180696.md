regretful: /rɪˈɡretfəl/ | adj. | 后悔的；遗憾的 | Feeling or expressing regret.  |例句：I'm regretful about missing your birthday party. 我很遗憾错过了你的生日派对。

例句：She cast a regretful glance back at the house. 她回头看了一眼房子，眼神中充满了遗憾。


近义词：sorry, apologetic, repentant, rueful

反义词：unrepentant, pleased, glad, joyful

词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is regretful about his actions. 他对自己的行为感到后悔。
She is regretful that she didn't study harder. 她后悔没有更加努力学习。


一般过去时:
He was regretful about his lost opportunity. 他为失去的机会感到后悔。
She was regretful that she missed the deadline. 她后悔错过了截止日期。


现在完成时:
I have been regretful about my decision for weeks. 我已经后悔我的决定几周了。
They have been regretful about their hasty words. 他们一直为他们说过的话感到后悔。


过去完成时:
I had been regretful about not going to college. 我一直后悔没上大学。
He had been regretful about his unkind remarks. 他一直为自己的不友善言辞感到后悔。


将来时:
I will be regretful if I don't apply for the job. 如果我不申请这份工作，我会后悔的。
She will be regretful if she doesn't seize this chance. 如果她不抓住这次机会，她会后悔的。


虚拟语气:
I would be regretful if I hurt your feelings. 如果我伤害了你的感情，我会感到很抱歉。
She would be regretful if she missed the concert. 如果她错过了音乐会，她会很后悔。


被动语态:
His actions were viewed as regretful by many. 他的行为被许多人认为是令人遗憾的。
The decision was later described as regretful by the committee.  委员会后来将该决定描述为令人遗憾的。


疑问句:
Are you regretful about your choice? 你对你的选择感到后悔吗？
Is he regretful about the way he treated her? 他后悔自己对待她的方式吗？


否定句:
I am not regretful about my decision. 我不后悔我的决定。
She is not regretful about anything she did. 她对她所做的任何事情都不后悔。
