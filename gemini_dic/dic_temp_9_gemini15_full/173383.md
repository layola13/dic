quasi-dreadfully:/ˈkwɑːzaɪˈdrɛdfəli/ | adj. | 几乎可怕地，非常可怕地 |  This word combines "quasi," meaning "seemingly" or "to a certain extent," with "dreadfully," meaning "extremely badly" or "terribly." It describes something that is almost, but not quite, completely dreadful.  It implies a degree of exaggeration or perhaps a subjective perception of dread. | The situation was quasi-dreadfully boring, a tedious meeting that stretched on forever.|近义词：terribly, awfully, horribly | 反义词：delightfully, wonderfully, pleasantly
