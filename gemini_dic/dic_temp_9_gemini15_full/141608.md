opt-out:/ˈɑːpt aʊt/| v.|选择退出，放弃|To choose not to participate in something or to withdraw from something.|

Example 1: I opted out of the company's health insurance plan. 我选择不参加公司的健康保险计划。

Example 2:  Customers can opt out of receiving marketing emails.  顾客可以选择不接收营销邮件。


近义词: withdraw from, decline, refuse, forgo

反义词: opt in, participate, join


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He opts out of the competition every year. 他每年都选择退出比赛。
They opt out of the pension scheme. 他们选择退出养老金计划。

一般过去时:
She opted out of the project last month. 她上个月退出了这个项目。
We opted out of the group insurance. 我们退出了团体保险。

现在完成时:
I have opted out of several mailing lists. 我已经退出了几个邮件列表。
They have opted out of the program since its inception. 自该计划启动以来，他们就退出了。

过去完成时:
He had opted out of the race before the final lap. 在最后一圈之前，他已经退出了比赛。
She had opted out of the negotiations prior to the meeting.  她在会议之前就退出了谈判。

将来时:
I will opt out of the subscription next year. 我明年将取消订阅。
They will opt out of the agreement if the terms are not met. 如果没有达成协议条款，他们将选择退出协议。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would opt out of that deal. 如果我是你，我会拒绝那笔交易。
I wish I had opted out of the investment sooner. 我真希望我早点退出投资。

被动语态:
The option to opt out was clearly explained.  选择退出的选项已清楚解释。
He was opted out of the team due to injury. 由于受伤，他被排除在球队之外。(被动语态略有不自然，通常用主动)

疑问句:
Can I opt out of this program? 我可以退出这个项目吗？
Will they opt out if given the chance? 如果有机会，他们会退出吗？

否定句:
I didn't opt out of the survey. 我没有退出调查。
He couldn't opt out of the mandatory training. 他无法退出强制培训。


PHRASE->
opt out of: 选择退出...
opt out of a program: 选择退出某个项目
opt out of a contract: 选择退出合同
opt out of the system: 选择退出系统
opt out of participation: 选择不参与
