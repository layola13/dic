nyctibune:/nɪkˈtɪbjuːn/| n. |夜行者，夜行性动物| A nyctibune is a nocturnal bird of prey belonging to the family Nyctibiidae, often called potoos.  They are characterized by their cryptic coloration and ability to remain motionless for long periods, resembling a broken branch. | Example: The potoo, a type of nyctibune, blends seamlessly into its surroundings during the day.  夜行性的夜鹰，一种夜行鸟类，白天能与周围环境完美融合。|近义词：potoo, nightjar|反义词：diurnal bird
