convergent:/kənˈvɜːr(d)ʒ(ə)nt/| adj. | 趋同的；收敛的；会聚的 |  Coming together from different directions to one point.  Describes lines, paths, or ideas that move towards a single point or conclusion. |

例句：

The convergent lines of the perspective drawing created a sense of depth. 透视图中汇聚的线条营造了深度感。

The convergent opinions of the experts led to a unified recommendation. 专家们趋同的意见导致了一致的建议。


近义词：meeting, converging, merging, coinciding

反义词：divergent, disparate, diverging


USAGE->

一般现在时:

The lines are convergent.  这些线是汇聚的。
His thoughts are convergent on one point. 他的思想汇聚于一点。


一般过去时:

The paths converged at the crossroads.  这些路径在十字路口汇合了。
Their views converged after a long discussion.  经过长时间的讨论后，他们的观点趋于一致。


现在完成时:

All the evidence has converged to support the theory. 所有证据都已汇聚起来支持这一理论。
The different ideas have converged into a single plan.  不同的想法已经融合成一个计划。


过去完成时:

The researchers had already converged on a solution. 研究人员已经达成一致的解决方案。
Before the meeting, their opinions had converged significantly. 会议之前，他们的意见已经有了很大的趋同。


将来时:

The two rivers will converge downstream.  两条河流将在下游汇合。
We expect their opinions to converge soon. 我们预计他们的意见很快就会达成一致。


虚拟语气:

If the lines were convergent, the triangle would be formed. 如果这些线是汇聚的，就会形成三角形。
If their ideas had converged earlier, the project would have been completed faster. 如果他们的想法早点达成一致，项目就会更快完成。


被动语态:

The data was converged into a single database. 数据被整合到一个数据库中。
The different opinions were gradually converged through discussion. 通过讨论，不同的意见逐渐趋于一致。


疑问句:

Are the lines convergent or divergent? 这些线是汇聚的还是发散的？
Do you think their opinions will converge? 你认为他们的意见会达成一致吗？


否定句:

The lines are not convergent; they are parallel.  这些线不是汇聚的；它们是平行的。
Their arguments did not converge; they remained at odds. 他们的论点没有达成一致；他们仍然意见相左。

词源:

convergent 源自拉丁语 *convergere，由com- (together) 和 vergere (to bend, incline) 构成，字面意思是“一起弯曲”。
