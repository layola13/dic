self-sufficient:/ˌsɛlf səˈfɪʃnt/| adj.|自给自足的，独立的|Able to provide all of one's own needs, without external assistance.|例句：The island community is largely self-sufficient in food production.  这个岛屿社区在粮食生产方面很大程度上自给自足。|例句：She prides herself on being self-sufficient and independent. 她为自己的自给自足和独立而自豪。|近义词：independent, autonomous, self-reliant|反义词：dependent, reliant, needy|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is self-sufficient and doesn't need help. = 他自给自足，不需要帮助。
The farm is self-sufficient in terms of water supply. = 这个农场在供水方面自给自足。


一般过去时:
They were self-sufficient before the war. = 战争前他们自给自足。
The community was largely self-sufficient in the past. = 过去这个社区在很大程度上是自给自足的。


现在完成时:
She has become self-sufficient since moving to the countryside. = 自从搬到乡下后，她已经变得自给自足了。
The project has proven to be self-sufficient in funding. = 该项目已被证明在资金方面是自给自足的。


过去完成时:
They had been self-sufficient for many years before the drought. = 在干旱之前，他们已经自给自足很多年了。
The village had been largely self-sufficient until the road was built. = 直到修路之前，这个村庄一直很大程度上是自给自足的。


将来时:
We will be self-sufficient in energy within five years. = 五年内我们将实现能源自给自足。
The company will strive to become self-sufficient in its operations. = 公司将努力使其运营自给自足。


虚拟语气:
If I were self-sufficient, I wouldn't need a job. = 如果我自给自足，我就不用工作了。
I wish I were more self-sufficient. = 我希望我更自给自足一些。


被动语态:
The community needs to be made self-sufficient. = 这个社区需要变得自给自足。
The system is designed to be self-sufficient in operation. = 该系统的设计目的是在运行中自给自足。


疑问句:
Is he truly self-sufficient? = 他真的自给自足吗？
Are they aiming to be self-sufficient in the future? = 他们目标是在未来实现自给自足吗？


否定句:
The project is not yet self-sufficient. = 该项目尚未自给自足。
He is not completely self-sufficient; he relies on his family for support. = 他并不完全自给自足；他依靠家人来支持。


PHRASE->
a self-sufficient lifestyle = 自给自足的生活方式
become self-sufficient = 变得自给自足
a self-sufficient economy = 自给自足的经济
self-sufficient in food = 在食物方面自给自足
highly self-sufficient = 高度自给自足
