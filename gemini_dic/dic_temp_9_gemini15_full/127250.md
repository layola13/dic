nautiloid:/ˈnɔːtɪlɔɪd/|/ˈnɑːtɪlɔɪd/| n. |鹦鹉螺类；鹦鹉螺化石|An extinct or extant mollusk of the class Cephalopoda, having a chambered, spiral shell.  Nautiloids are characterized by their distinctive coiled shells. They are often found as fossils. |The fossil record shows a rich diversity of nautiloids.  鹦鹉螺化石记录显示了鹦鹉螺的多样性。| ammonite, cephalopod|  |
