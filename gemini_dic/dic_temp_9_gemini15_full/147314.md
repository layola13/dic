overmonopolizing: /ˌoʊvər məˈnɑːpəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |  过度垄断；过度独占 | The act of excessively controlling or dominating a particular market, resource, or area of influence, often to the detriment of others.  |  The company was accused of overmonopolizing the software market, stifling competition and innovation. 这家公司被指控过度垄断软件市场，扼杀了竞争和创新。 | 近义词：monopolizing, dominating, controlling, exploiting | 反义词：sharing, distributing, cooperating, competing | 动词
