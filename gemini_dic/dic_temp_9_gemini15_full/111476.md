loader:/ˈloʊdər/| n. |装载机；加载器；搬运工|A person or thing that loads something.  This can refer to a machine used for loading, a program that loads other programs, or a person who loads things.|例句：The construction worker used a loader to move the heavy stones. 建筑工人用装载机搬运沉重的石头。
例句：The software loader installed the new operating system. 软件加载器安装了新的操作系统。|近义词：carrier,  charger,  installer |反义词：unloader
