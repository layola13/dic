polytheism:/ˈpɒliθiːɪzəm/ /ˌpɑːliˈθiːɪzəm/ | n. | 多神论 | The belief in or worship of more than one god.  Polytheism is a common feature in many ancient religions.  | 例句：Ancient Greece was characterized by its polytheism. 古希腊以其多神论为特征。

Many cultures throughout history have practiced polytheism, each with its own unique pantheon of gods and goddesses.  历史上许多文化都实行过多神论，每个文化都有自己独特的神灵体系。| 近义词：polytheocracy, henotheism (the worship of one god while not denying the existence of others) | 反义词：monotheism, atheism
