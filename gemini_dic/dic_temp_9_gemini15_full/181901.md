remain:/rɪˈmeɪn/| v. |保持；依然；留下；剩余|To continue to exist, be present, or happen.  To stay in the same place or condition.|例句：The question remains unanswered.  问题仍然没有答案。

例句：Despite the storm, the house remained standing. 尽管暴风雨来袭，房子依然屹立不倒。


近义词：stay, persist, endure, continue| 反义词：depart, leave, vanish, disappear| 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He remains optimistic despite the setbacks. 他尽管遇到挫折，仍然保持乐观。
The problem remains unsolved.  问题依然没有解决。

一般过去时:
The soldiers remained at their posts. 士兵们坚守在自己的岗位上。
She remained silent throughout the meeting. 她在整个会议期间保持沉默。

现在完成时:
The mystery remains unsolved. 这个谜团至今未解。
He has remained loyal to his principles. 他一直忠于自己的原则。

过去完成时:
After the storm, only a few houses remained. 暴风雨过后，只剩下几座房子了。
They had remained friends for many years. 他们做了多年的朋友。

将来时:
I hope the situation will remain stable. 我希望情况会保持稳定。
He will remain in charge until his successor is appointed. 他将继续负责，直到他的继任者被任命。

虚拟语气:
If he remained silent, it would be best. 如果他保持沉默，那就最好了。
I wish he remained in the country. 我希望他留在国内。

被动语态:
The issue remains to be discussed. 这个问题还有待讨论。
The decision remains to be seen.  结果有待观察。


疑问句:
Does the problem remain? 问题依然存在吗？
Will they remain in the city? 他们会留在城市吗？

否定句:
He did not remain long. 他没有待很久。
The situation did not remain unchanged. 情况并没有保持不变。
