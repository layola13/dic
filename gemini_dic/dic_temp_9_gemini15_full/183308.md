reproached:/rɪˈproʊtʃt/| vt.|责备；谴责；批评|To express disapproval or criticism of someone.  It implies a feeling of disappointment or hurt.|例句：He reproached her for her carelessness. 他责备她的粗心大意。

例句：The teacher reproached the student for cheating on the exam. 老师因考试作弊而责备学生。


近义词：rebuke, reprimand, scold, upbraid, blame

反义词：praise, commend, applaud


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reproaches her for her mistakes. = 他因她的错误而责备她。
She reproaches herself for her inaction. = 她为自己的不作为而自责。


一般过去时:
He reproached her harshly for lying. = 他严厉地责备她撒谎。
They reproached him for his poor judgment. = 他们责备他判断力差。


现在完成时:
She has reproached him many times for his behavior. = 她多次因他的行为而责备他。
I have reproached myself for not helping him. = 我为没有帮助他而自责。


过去完成时:
He had reproached her before she even spoke. = 她还没开口，他就已经责备她了。
They had reproached him several times for the same mistake. = 他们已经多次因为同样的错误责备他了。


将来时:
I will reproach him for his rudeness. = 我将因他的粗鲁而责备他。
She will reproach herself for her hasty decision. = 她将为她草率的决定而自责。


虚拟语气:
I would reproach him if he were here. = 如果他在的话，我会责备他。
If I had known, I would have reproached him sooner. = 如果我知道的话，我会早点责备他。


被动语态:
He was reproached for his dishonesty. = 他因不诚实而受到责备。
She was reproached by her parents for her bad grades. = 她因为坏成绩而受到父母的责备。


疑问句:
Will you reproach him for his actions? = 你会因为他的行为而责备他吗？
Why did they reproach her? = 他们为什么责备她？


否定句:
He did not reproach her for her mistake. = 他没有因为她的错误而责备她。
I will not reproach you for your decision. = 我不会因为你的决定而责备你。


PHRASE->
reproach oneself = 自责
reproach somebody for something = 因某事责备某人
cast reproach on = 使蒙受责备
a reproach to = 对…的耻辱
beyond reproach = 无可指责
