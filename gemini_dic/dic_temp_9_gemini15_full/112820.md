lucidity: /luːˈsɪdəti/ | n. |清晰度；明晰；头脑清醒| The quality of being clear, easily understood, or rational.  It can refer to clarity of thought, expression, or style. |例句：The lucidity of her explanation made the complex concept easily understandable.  她的解释清晰明了，使这个复杂的概念易于理解。

例句：His lucidity of thought was remarkable, even in his advanced age.  即使在他年事已高的时候，他的思维也异常清晰。


近义词：clarity, perspicuity, intelligibility, coherence, transparency

反义词：confusion, obscurity, muddiness, vagueness, ambiguity


词源:  From Latin *luciditas, from lucidus "clear, bright," from lux, lucis "light."  The word ultimately derives from the Proto-Indo-European root *leuk-, meaning "to shine" or "light."
