fructify:/ˈfrʌktɪfaɪ/| vt. |使结果实；使多产；使成功；使富有成效|To bear or produce fruit; to make fruitful or productive; to become fruitful or productive.|例句：The hard work finally fructified in a successful harvest.  辛勤的工作最终带来了丰收。|例句：His efforts to improve the company finally fructified. 他努力改进公司的措施最终取得了成效。|近义词：bear fruit, yield, prosper, succeed |反义词：fail, wither, stagnate|动词
