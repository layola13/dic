gymnospermous:/ˌdʒɪmnɒˈspɜːməs/| adj.|裸子植物的|Belonging to or characteristic of gymnosperms;  having seeds that are not enclosed in an ovary.  The term refers to plants that produce seeds that are not enclosed within an ovary or fruit.  These seeds are typically borne on the surface of scales or leaves, often arranged in cones.|Example: Conifers, such as pines and spruces, are gymnospermous plants.  裸子植物，例如松树和云杉，是裸子植物。|近义词:coniferous (often used interchangeably, though not entirely accurate)|反义词:angiospermous
