coloring:/ˈkʌlərɪŋ/| n.|着色；颜色；染色；色彩|The act of applying color, the colors used, or the quality of being colorful.|例句：The coloring of the sunset was breathtaking.  日落的颜色令人叹为观止。|例句：She added some coloring to her cake batter. 她往蛋糕糊里加了一些色素。|近义词：hue, tint, shade, pigmentation|反义词：monochromatic, colorless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist is coloring the picture. = 艺术家正在给图画着色。
She colors her hair every month. = 她每月都染发。


一般过去时:
He colored the map with crayons. = 他用蜡笔给地图着色。
They colored the eggs for Easter. = 他们为复活节给鸡蛋染色。


现在完成时:
I have colored all the pages. = 我已经把所有页面都涂色了。
She has been coloring her hair blonde for years. = 她多年来一直把头发染成金黄色。


过去完成时:
She had colored the drawing before the teacher arrived. = 老师来之前，她已经把图画涂色了。
They had colored the eggs before the party started. = 聚会开始前，他们已经把鸡蛋染好色了。


将来时:
I will color the picture tomorrow. = 我明天将会给图片着色。
She will be coloring her hair red next week. = 她下周将会把头发染成红色。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would color the entire book. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把整本书都涂色。

否定句:
