monetizing: /ˈmʌnɪˌtaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 货币化；获利；创收 | To make money from something; to convert something into a source of income. | 例句：The company is monetizing its popular app by introducing in-app purchases. 公司通过引入应用内购买来使其流行的应用程序获利。


例句：They are exploring new ways to monetize their social media platform. 他们正在探索货币化其社交媒体平台的新方法。


近义词：commercializing, profiting from, capitalizing on | 反义词：de-monetizing, giving away for free


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They monetize their blog through advertisements. = 他们通过广告使他们的博客获利。
We monetize our skills by offering consulting services. = 我们通过提供咨询服务来使我们的技能获利。


一般过去时:
The artist monetized his artwork by selling prints online. = 这位艺术家通过在线销售版画使其艺术作品获利。
The musician monetized his music through streaming platforms. = 这位音乐家通过流媒体平台使其音乐获利。


现在完成时:

She has monetized her passion for baking by selling cakes. = 她通过销售蛋糕已经使其对烘焙的热情获利。


过去完成时:

将来时:
