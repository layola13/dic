confessed:/kənˈfɛst/ | vt. |承认，坦白|To admit to be true or to be the case, typically with reluctance.|He confessed to the crime. 他承认了罪行。|近义词：admit, acknowledge, own up |反义词：deny, disclaim


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He confesses his mistakes every day. 他每天都承认自己的错误。
She confesses her love to him. 她向他表白了爱意。


一般过去时:
The thief confessed to the police. 小偷向警察坦白了。
He confessed his guilt to the judge. 他向法官承认了自己的罪行。


现在完成时:
He has confessed his sins to the priest. 他已经向牧师忏悔了自己的罪过。
She has confessed everything to her parents. 她已经把一切都告诉了她的父母。


过去完成时:
He had confessed his crime before the trial. 他在审判前已经承认了自己的罪行。
She had confessed her feelings before he left. 他离开之前，她已经表白了自己的感情。


将来时:
He will confess his wrongdoing soon. 他很快就会承认自己的错误。
She will confess her secret to her best friend. 她会向她最好的朋友坦白她的秘密。


虚拟语气:
If he confessed his feelings, she might forgive him. 如果他表白他的感情，她可能会原谅他。
I wish he had confessed the truth earlier. 我希望他早点说出真相。


被动语态:
His guilt was confessed by him. 他的罪行被他承认了。(较为不自然，通常不用被动语态)
The truth was confessed to the court. 真相在法庭上被承认了。


疑问句:
Did he confess his involvement? 他承认了他的参与吗？
Will she confess her secret? 她会坦白她的秘密吗？


否定句:
He didn't confess his crime. 他没有承认自己的罪行。
She wouldn't confess her mistake. 她不肯承认自己的错误。


PHRASE->
confess to something: 承认某事
confess one's sins: 忏悔罪过
confess one's love: 表白爱意
openly confess: 公开承认
freely confess: 坦然承认
