puppets:/ˈpʌpɪts/| n.|木偶；傀儡；提线木偶|A puppet is a doll, typically one made of wood or cloth, that is manipulated by a person, often by strings or rods.  Puppets are used in puppet shows and other forms of entertainment.|例句：The children were mesmerized by the puppet show.孩子们被木偶戏迷住了。|例句：He felt like a puppet in the hands of his boss. 他觉得自己像老板手中的傀儡。|近义词：marionette, doll|反义词:真人, real person
