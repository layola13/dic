diving:/ˈdaɪvɪŋ/ | n. |潜水；跳水|The act of submerging oneself underwater, often for sport, recreation, or exploration.  It can also refer to the act of jumping or plunging into something.|例句：He enjoys scuba diving in the coral reefs. 他喜欢在珊瑚礁潜水。

例句：The synchronized diving team performed flawlessly.  同步跳水队表现完美。


近义词：submerging, plunging, immersion | 反义词：emerging, surfacing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dives into the pool every morning. 他每天早上都跳进游泳池。
She dives deep to find pearls. 她潜入深海寻找珍珠。


一般过去时:
They dived into the ocean to explore a shipwreck. 他们潜入大海探索沉船。
He dived off the cliff into the water. 他从悬崖跳入水中。


现在完成时:
I have dived in many different oceans. 我在许多不同的海洋潜过水。
She has dived to a depth of 100 meters. 她潜到了100米深。


过去完成时:
He had dived several times before he found the treasure. 他潜了几次才找到宝藏。
They had dived into the lake before the storm hit. 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经跳进了湖里。


将来时:
We will dive in the Great Barrier Reef next summer. 我们明年夏天将在澳大利亚大堡礁潜水。
They will dive to inspect the damaged pipeline. 他们将下潜检查受损的管道。


虚拟语气:
If I could dive, I would explore the Mariana Trench. 如果我能潜水，我会探索马里亚纳海沟。
I wish I could dive with dolphins. 我希望我能和海豚一起潜水。


被动语态:
The underwater camera was dived deep into the ocean. 水下摄像机被潜入深海。
The sunken ship was dived upon by many explorers. 那艘沉船被许多探险家探测过。


疑问句:
Have you ever dived before? 你以前潜水过吗？
Where will they dive tomorrow? 他们明天将在哪里潜水？


否定句:
He did not dive into the water because of the cold. 因为天气寒冷，他没有跳入水中。
She has never dived with sharks. 她从未和鲨鱼一起潜水过。
