decampments:/dɪˈkæmpmənts/| n. |撤营；离开；逃离|The sudden decampments of the army left the town vulnerable.  军队突然撤离，使该镇变得脆弱。|The unexpected decampments of the protesters surprised the police.  抗议者出人意料的撤离让警方感到惊讶。|近义词：departures, withdrawals, evacuations|反义词：arrivals, encampments


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army decamps every year. = 军队每年都撤营。
He decamps to the mountains every summer. = 他每年夏天都去山上避暑。


一般过去时:
The rebels decamped under cover of darkness. = 反叛者在夜色的掩护下撤离了。
They decamped from the battlefield after the defeat. = 他们战败后离开了战场。


现在完成时:
The nomads have decamped to warmer regions. = 游牧民族已经迁徙到温暖的地区。
They have decamped several times this year. = 今年他们已经撤离好几次了。


过去完成时:
The soldiers had decamped before the enemy arrived. = 敌人到达之前，士兵们已经撤离了。
They had decamped hastily, leaving much of their equipment behind. = 他们仓促撤离，留下许多装备。


将来时:
The circus will decamp tomorrow morning. = 马戏团明天早上将要撤离。
We will decamp as soon as the storm passes. = 风暴过后我们就会撤离。


虚拟语气:
If they had decamped earlier, they would have avoided the ambush. = 如果他们早点撤离，就能避免埋伏。
I wish we had decamped before the fighting started. = 我真希望我们在战斗开始前就撤离了。


被动语态:
The camp was decamped by the soldiers. = 营地被士兵们撤离了。(This is less common, as "decamp" is typically used actively.)
The area was decamped by the protesters after the rally. = 集会结束后，抗议者离开了该地区。


疑问句:
Will the troops decamp before nightfall? = 部队会在天黑前撤离吗？
When did the refugees decamp from the war zone? = 难民什么时候撤离战区的？


否定句:
The army did not decamp despite the heavy rain. = 尽管下着大雨，军队也没有撤离。
They refused to decamp until they had completed their mission. = 他们拒绝撤离，直到完成任务。


PHRASE->
decamp in haste = 仓促撤离
decamp under cover of darkness = 趁着夜色撤离
decamp from a place = 从某地撤离
decamp in disorder = 混乱地撤离
secret decampment = 秘密撤离
