cloudlet:/ˈklaʊdlət/| n. |云小片；微型云数据中心|A small-scale cloud computing environment, typically located at the edge of a network, offering computing resources closer to the user.  It's a subset of cloud computing designed to provide localized services and reduce latency. |Example:  The edge computing device deployed a cloudlet to process the real-time data stream.  边缘计算设备部署了一个云小片来处理实时数据流。|近义词：mini-cloud, micro-cloud|反义词：mainframe, large-scale datacenter|名词
