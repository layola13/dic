dressings:/ˈdrɛsɪŋz/ | n. | 调味汁，敷料 | Sauces, usually liquid or semi-liquid, used to add flavor and moisture to food.  They can be used on salads, meats, vegetables, etc. | Example:  The salad dressing was delicious.  这道沙拉酱汁真美味。

The Caesar dressing on the chicken was exquisite.  鸡肉上的凯撒酱汁非常精致。


近义词：sauces, condiments, toppings | 反义词：None (antonyms are context-dependent, e.g., blandness if referring to taste) | 词性：名词
