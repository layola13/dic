cavorted:/kəˈvɔːtɪd/| v.|嬉戏；欢跃；雀跃|To move or dance around in a lively and playful way.|The children cavorted joyfully in the park.孩子们在公园里快乐地嬉戏。|近义词：frolicked, gambolled, romped, leaped, skipped|反义词：slouched, slumped


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The puppies cavort in the garden. = 小狗们在花园里嬉戏。
They cavort with unrestrained joy. = 他们无拘无束地欢跃。

一般过去时:
The horses cavorted in the field. = 马儿们在田野里嬉戏。
We cavorted on the beach until sunset. = 我们在海滩上嬉戏直到日落。

现在完成时:
The children have cavorted all afternoon. = 孩子们整个下午都在嬉戏。
They have cavorted through the streets. = 他们在大街上欢快地跳跃。

过去完成时:
They had cavorted for hours before they finally tired. = 他们在最终疲惫之前已经嬉戏了好几个小时。
The dancers had cavorted elegantly before the performance ended. = 舞蹈演员们在表演结束前优雅地跳跃。

将来时:
The dolphins will cavort in the ocean. = 海豚将在海洋里嬉戏。
We will cavort at the festival. = 我们将在节日上尽情玩乐。

虚拟语气:
If they were younger, they would cavort more freely. = 如果他们年轻一些，他们会更自由地嬉戏。
I wish I could cavort like I did when I was a child. = 我真希望我能像小时候一样尽情玩乐。

被动语态:
(Cavort is rarely used in the passive voice.  A suitable alternative would be needed to describe the scene.)  The scene was filled with frolicking animals. = 场景中充满了嬉戏的动物。
The stage was filled with dancers who seemed to cavort effortlessly. = 舞台上充满了似乎毫不费力地跳跃的舞者。


疑问句:
Did the children cavort in the playground? = 孩子们在操场上嬉戏了吗？
Will the dancers cavort during the performance? = 舞蹈演员们会在表演中嬉戏吗？

否定句:
The tired dogs did not cavort. = 疲惫的狗们没有嬉戏。
The students did not cavort in the classroom. = 学生们没有在教室里嬉戏。
