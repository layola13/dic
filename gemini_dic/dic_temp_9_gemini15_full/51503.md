detrained:/diːˈtreɪnd/| v.|使下火车；使卸下火车|To remove (passengers or goods) from a train.|The passengers detrained at the station.  乘客在车站下车了。The cargo was detrained and transported by truck. 货物已卸下火车并用卡车运输。|近义词：disembark, unload|反义词：embark, load
