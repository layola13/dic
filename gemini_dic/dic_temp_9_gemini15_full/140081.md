of that ilk:/əv ðæt ɪlk/| adj. phrase|那一类人；那种类型|Of that ilk refers to people or things of a similar kind or type.  It implies a shared characteristic or quality, often negative or undesirable.  The phrase is often used humorously or sarcastically.|例句：He’s one of that ilk who believes the earth is flat. 他是那种相信地球是平的人。


例句：All the politicians of that ilk are corrupt.  所有那一类政客都是腐败的。


近义词：of the same kind, of a similar nature, of that sort, that type


反义词：of a different kind, unlike, dissimilar
