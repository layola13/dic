draperies:/ˈdreɪpəriːz/ | n. | 窗帘；帷幔；悬垂织物 |  Draperies are fabrics hung for decoration or to cover windows.  They can be made of various materials and styles. | The heavy draperies blocked out most of the sunlight. 厚重的窗帘遮住了大部分阳光。 The living room was elegantly decorated with velvet draperies. 客厅用天鹅绒窗帘装饰得很优雅。| curtains, hangings | bare walls, exposed windows | 名词
