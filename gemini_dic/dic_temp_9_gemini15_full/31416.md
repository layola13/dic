charier:/ˈtʃɑːriər/| n.| 租车人；包租人；(尤指)马车夫 | A person who hires or rents something, especially a vehicle, particularly a horse-drawn carriage.  | Example: The charier of the carriage arrived late.  租马车的人来晚了。|近义词：hirer, renter, lessee | 反义词：lessor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He charters a car every weekend. = 他每个周末都租一辆车。
She hires a cleaning lady twice a month. = 她每月雇佣清洁工两次。

一般过去时:
They chartered a yacht for their vacation. = 他们为度假租了一艘游艇。
He rented a car for the trip. = 他为这次旅行租了一辆车。

现在完成时:
We have chartered a plane for the conference. = 我们已经为会议租了一架飞机。
They have rented a house for the summer. = 他们已经租了一栋房子过夏天。

过去完成时:
She had chartered a bus before the group arrived. = 在团队到达之前，她已经租了一辆巴士。
They had rented a small apartment before moving to the city. = 在搬到城市之前，他们租了一套小公寓。


将来时:
I will charter a helicopter to see the Grand Canyon. = 我将租一架直升机去看大峡谷。
We will rent a car at the airport. = 我们将在机场租一辆车。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would charter a private jet. = 如果我有更多钱，我会租一架私人飞机。
If they had planned better, they would have rented a larger house. = 如果他们计划得更好，他们就会租一间更大的房子。

被动语态:
A bus was chartered for the school trip. = 一辆公共汽车被租用来进行学校旅行。
A car was rented for the wedding. = 一辆汽车被租用于婚礼。

疑问句:
Did you charter a boat for the fishing trip? = 你为钓鱼旅行租了一条船吗？
Have they rented a car yet? = 他们已经租车了吗？

否定句:
They didn't charter a plane for the journey. = 他们没有为旅程租用飞机。
We haven't rented a car this month. = 这个月我们还没有租车。
