intercarrier: /ˌɪntərˈkæriər/ | n. | 跨运营商的；跨运营商服务 |  Relating to or involving the interaction or cooperation between different telecommunications carriers.  It often refers to services that allow seamless transitions between different network providers. | Example:  Intercarrier roaming allows users to make calls and use data while traveling outside their home network's coverage area.  跨运营商漫游允许用户在离开其家庭网络覆盖区域时拨打电话和使用数据。|近义词: cross-carrier, multi-carrier |反义词: intracarrier (within a single carrier)
