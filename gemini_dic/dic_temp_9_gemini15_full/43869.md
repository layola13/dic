cress:/krɛs/ | n. | 芜菁甘蓝，水田芥 | A pungent, edible plant with small, round leaves, often used in salads.  It includes several species, such as garden cress and watercress. | Example: I added some cress to my sandwich for extra flavor. 我在我的三明治里加了一些水田芥来增加风味。|近义词：watercress, mustard cress | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑与不辛辣的蔬菜对比)| 名词
