fishier:/ˈfɪʃər/| adj.|爱钓鱼的；像鱼的；鱼腥味的 |Having the characteristics of fish; smelling or tasting of fish.  Also, someone who is fond of fishing.|例句：The fishier smell emanating from the riverbank was unbearable. 河岸散发出的鱼腥味令人难以忍受。|例句：He's a fishier, always going fishing on weekends. 他是个钓鱼爱好者，周末总是去钓鱼。|近义词：fishy, piscatorial |反义词：fresh (in the context of smell or taste)
