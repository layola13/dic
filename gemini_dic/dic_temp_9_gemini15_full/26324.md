buys:/baɪz/| v.|购买，买|To purchase something.|He buys groceries every week. 他每周都买杂货。|She buys a new dress for the party. 她买了一件新裙子参加派对。|sells, purchases|sells, refuses


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He buys a newspaper every morning. 他每天早上都买一份报纸。
She buys her coffee from that shop. 她在那家店买咖啡。

一般过去时:
I bought a new car last month. 我上个月买了一辆新车。
They bought tickets to the concert. 他们买了演唱会的票。

现在完成时:
She has bought a new house. 她买了一栋新房子。
He has bought all the ingredients for the cake. 他已经买齐了蛋糕的所有材料。

过去完成时:
I had bought the tickets before the show started.  演出开始前我已经买好了票。
They had bought their plane tickets months in advance.  他们几个月前就买好了飞机票。


将来时:
I will buy a new phone next week. 我下周会买一部新手机。
We will buy presents for our friends. 我们会为朋友们买礼物。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would buy a bigger house. 如果我有更多钱，我会买一栋更大的房子。
I wish I could buy that painting. 我真希望我能买下那幅画。

被动语态:
The tickets were bought online. 票是在网上买的。
The house was bought for a high price. 这栋房子以高价售出。

疑问句:
Do you buy organic vegetables? 你买有机蔬菜吗？
Where do they buy their clothes? 他们在哪里买衣服？

否定句:
I don't buy clothes online. 我不在网上买衣服。
They didn't buy anything at the market. 他们在市场上什么也没买。


PHRASE->
buy into:  相信，接受 (e.g., He bought into the company's new strategy. 他接受了公司的新的战略。)
buy off: 收买 (e.g., They tried to buy off the witness. 他们试图收买证人。)
buy out: 收购 (e.g., The larger company bought out its competitor. 大公司收购了它的竞争对手。)
buy up:  大量购买 (e.g., Investors bought up all the available shares. 投资者买光了所有可用的股票。)
buy time: 拖延时间 (e.g.,  We need to buy time to figure out a solution. 我们需要争取时间来想出一个解决方案。)
