organise:/ˈɔːɡənaɪz/ /ˈɔːrɡənaɪz/ | vt. |组织；安排；筹备 | To put things or people into a particular order, system, or structure; to arrange or plan (an event, activity, etc.). |  例句：We need to organise the office before the new employees arrive. 我们需要在新员工到来之前整理办公室。

例句：She organised a surprise party for her husband's birthday. 她为丈夫的生日举办了一个惊喜派对。


近义词：arrange, plan, coordinate, systematize | 反义词：disorganise, disorder, chaos | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She organises the team meetings every week. = 她每周都组织团队会议。
He organises his time efficiently. = 他有效地安排时间。


一般过去时:
They organised a fundraising event last year. = 他们去年组织了一次筹款活动。
I organised my documents before the presentation. = 我在演示文稿之前整理了我的文件。


现在完成时:
We have organised everything for the conference. = 我们已经为会议准备好了所有事情。
She has organised her thoughts clearly. = 她已经清楚地整理了自己的思路。


过去完成时:
They had organised the logistics before the trip started. = 他们在旅行开始之前已经安排好了后勤工作。
He had organised his notes before the exam. = 他在考试前已经整理好笔记了。


将来时:
We will organise a trip to the beach next month. = 我们下个月将组织一次海滩旅行。
They will organise a meeting to discuss the proposal. = 他们将组织一次会议来讨论这个提案。


虚拟语气:
If I organised my time better, I would be less stressed. = 如果我更好地安排时间，我就会压力小一些。
I wish I had organised my finances more carefully. = 我希望我以前更仔细地规划我的财务。


被动语态:
The event was organised by a local charity. = 这场活动是由当地一家慈善机构组织的。
The meeting will be organised next week. = 会议将在下周组织。


疑问句:
Have you organised the transport yet? = 你已经安排好交通工具了吗？
Will they organise the catering for the party? = 他们会为派对安排餐饮吗？


否定句:
I haven't organised the travel arrangements yet. = 我还没有安排好旅行安排。
They didn't organise the conference properly. = 他们没有妥善地组织会议。


PHRASE->
organise a meeting = 组织会议
organise a trip = 组织旅行
organise your thoughts = 整理思路
organise a campaign = 组织一场活动
organise your schedule = 安排你的日程


词源:
The word "organise" comes from the French word "organiser," which itself comes from the Greek word "organon," meaning "tool" or "instrument." The sense of arranging and structuring things developed from the idea of using tools to create order.
