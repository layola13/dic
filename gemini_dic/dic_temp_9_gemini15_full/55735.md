dive-bombs:/ˈdaɪvˌbɒmz/| v. |俯冲轰炸；猛扑|To attack a target by diving from a high altitude and releasing bombs. This term is often used in military contexts to describe aerial attacks.  It can also be used figuratively to describe a sudden and forceful attack or descent.|例句：The fighter jets dive-bombed the enemy positions.  战斗机俯冲轰炸敌军阵地。

The eagle dive-bombed the unsuspecting rabbit.  鹰猛扑向毫无防备的兔子。|近义词：bomb, attack, swoop|反义词：ascend, soar|动词
