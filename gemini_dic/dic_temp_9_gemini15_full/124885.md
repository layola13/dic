mudding:/ˈmʌdɪŋ/ | v. | 泥泞；弄脏；(尤指车辆)陷进泥里 | To make muddy or dirty; to become stuck in mud, especially of a vehicle.  |例句：The rain mudded the roads. 雨把路弄脏了。
The car got mudded on the track. 车子在赛道上陷进泥里了。|近义词：soiling, miring, bogging down |反义词：cleaning, drying
