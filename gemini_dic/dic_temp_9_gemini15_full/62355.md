endgames:/ˈɛndɡeɪmz/ | n. | 结局；收官阶段；最终阶段 | The final stages of a long process, especially a conflict or competition.  It often implies a decisive or critical phase where the outcome is determined. |

例句：

The negotiations reached their endgames, with both sides preparing for a final showdown.  谈判进入了最终阶段，双方都准备进行最后的摊牌。

The endgame of the chess match was tense and exciting.  国际象棋比赛的收官阶段紧张而刺激。


近义词：final stages, concluding phases, culmination, denouement

反义词：beginning, commencement, outset
