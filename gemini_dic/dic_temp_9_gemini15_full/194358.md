self-praises: /ˌsɛlf ˈpreɪzɪz/ | n. | 自我吹嘘；自夸 |  The act of praising oneself; boasting.  It often implies excessive or immoderate praise. | 例句：His self-praises at the meeting were quite embarrassing. 他在会议上的自我吹嘘相当令人尴尬。

例句：Her constant self-praises made everyone uncomfortable. 她不断的自夸让每个人都感到不舒服。


近义词：boasting, bragging, self-aggrandizement, vainglory

反义词：self-deprecation, humility, modesty


词性：名词
