motorbiker:/ˈməʊtəbaɪkə(r)/| n. |摩托车手|A person who rides a motorcycle.  Motorbikers often participate in rallies and group rides.|例句：The motorbiker sped past us on the highway.  那个摩托车手在高速公路上快速超越了我们。

例句：Many motorbikers gathered for the annual motorcycle show.  许多摩托车手聚集参加年度摩托车展。


近义词：motorcyclist, biker


反义词：pedestrian, cyclist (depending on context)


词性：名词
