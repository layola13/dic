overspice:/ˈoʊvərˈspaɪs/| vt.|加过多的香料；调味过重|To add an excessive amount of spice to (food or drink).  It implies that the added spice overwhelms the other flavors and may make the dish unpleasant to eat.|例句：He overspiced the curry, making it too hot to eat. 他咖喱放了过多的香料，辣得没法吃。  The chef overspiced the stew, ruining its delicate flavor. 厨师在炖菜里放了过多的香料，破坏了它原本细腻的味道。|近义词：overseason, overflavor|反义词：underseason
