resubscribe: /ˌriːsəbˈskraɪb/ | v. |重新订阅；再次订阅 | To subscribe again; to renew a subscription. |  例句：He resubscribed to the magazine after a year's break.  他中断一年后再次订阅了该杂志。


例句：After a trial period, many users choose to resubscribe to the streaming service.  试用期结束后，许多用户选择重新订阅流媒体服务。


近义词：renew, subscribe again | 反义词：unsubscribe, cancel


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resubscribes to the newspaper every year. = 他每年都重新订阅报纸。
She resubscribes to the streaming service every month. = 她每月都重新订阅流媒体服务。

一般过去时:
He resubscribed to the magazine last week. = 他上周重新订阅了杂志。
They resubscribed to the online course after the break. = 休息后他们重新订阅了在线课程。

现在完成时:
I have resubscribed to the gym membership. = 我已经重新订阅了健身房会员资格。
She has resubscribed to the newsletter after a long time. = 过了很久，她重新订阅了时事通讯。

过去完成时:
He had resubscribed to the service before the discount ended. = 在折扣结束前，他已经重新订阅了服务。
They had resubscribed to the software before the update was released. = 在更新发布之前，他们已经重新订阅了软件。

将来时:
I will resubscribe to the magazine next year. = 我明年将重新订阅该杂志。
They will resubscribe to the service once they have more free time. = 他们一旦有更多空闲时间就会重新订阅服务。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would resubscribe to the premium service. = 如果我有更多钱，我会重新订阅高级服务。
I wish I could resubscribe to that amazing podcast. = 我真希望我能重新订阅那个精彩的播客。

被动语态:
The service was resubscribed to by many users. = 许多用户重新订阅了该服务。 (This is less common)
The subscription was resubscribed to automatically. = 订阅自动续订了。(This is more common in this context)

疑问句:
Will you resubscribe to the service? = 你会重新订阅该服务吗？
Has she resubscribed to the magazine yet? = 她已经重新订阅杂志了吗？

否定句:
He did not resubscribe to the newspaper this year. = 他今年没有重新订阅报纸。
She will not resubscribe to the streaming service. = 她不会重新订阅流媒体服务。
