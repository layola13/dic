preadvertising:/ˌpriːˈædvərtaɪzɪŋ/| n. |预先广告；前期广告|The act or process of advertising before a product or service is launched, often to generate anticipation and build brand awareness.|例句：The company engaged in extensive preadvertising to build excitement for the new product launch.  这家公司进行了大量的预先广告宣传，以增强人们对新产品发布的期待。|例句： Preadvertising campaigns often use teaser ads to pique consumer interest. 预先广告活动通常使用预告广告来激起消费者的兴趣。|近义词：prelaunch marketing, advance publicity|反义词：post-advertising
