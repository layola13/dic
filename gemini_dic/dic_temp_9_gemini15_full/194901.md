semi-globe:/ˈsɛmiˌɡloʊb/ | n. |半球；半球形物体 | A semi-globe is half of a globe, representing one hemisphere of the Earth or a celestial body.  It is often used in geographical or astronomical contexts to depict a portion of the Earth's surface or the sky.  | Example: The teacher used a semi-globe to show the students the location of different continents.  老师用半球仪向学生展示不同大陆的位置。  | 近义词：hemisphere, half-globe | 反义词：globe, sphere
