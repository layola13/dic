huffily:/ˈhʌfɪli/| adv.| 愠怒地；傲慢地；不耐烦地 | In a huffily manner, showing irritation or annoyance.  |例句：She huffily refused to answer his question. 她生气地拒绝回答他的问题。|例句：He huffily slammed the door shut. 他生气地砰地关上了门。|近义词：sulkily, petulantly, testily, crossly |反义词：calmly, pleasantly, cheerfully
