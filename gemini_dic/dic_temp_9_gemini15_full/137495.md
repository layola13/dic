nontoxicity: /ˌnɒnˌtɒksˈɪsəti/ | /ˌnɑːnˌtɑːksˈɪsɪti/ | n. |无毒性；非毒性 | The absence of toxic effects; the state or quality of being nontoxic.  It refers to the lack of harmful effects on living organisms. | Example: The nontoxicity of the new cleaning product is a major selling point. 新型清洁产品的无毒性是其主要卖点。 The study confirmed the nontoxicity of the compound to aquatic life. 研究证实了该化合物对水生生物的无毒性。 | 近义词：harmlessness, innocuousness, safety | 反义词：toxicity, harmfulness, danger
