guacharo:/ɡwɑːˈtʃɑːroʊ/|/ˈɡwætʃərəʊ/| n. |油鸱|A nocturnal, oil-producing bird of the family Steatornithidae, found in caves in northern South America. It is also known as the cave oilbird or guácharo.  The bird's fat is rendered into oil, once used by locals. |例句：The guacharo is a fascinating creature, known for its unique nocturnal habits and the oil it produces. 油鸱是一种迷人的生物，以其独特的夜行习性及其产生的油而闻名。

The guacharos' calls echo through the caves at night. 油鸱的叫声在晚上回荡在山洞里。


近义词：cave oilbird, Steatornis caripensis |反义词：无
