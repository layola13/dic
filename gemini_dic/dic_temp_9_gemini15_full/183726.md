resanctioning: /ˌriːˈsæŋkʃənɪŋ/ | v. | 再制裁；重新实施制裁 | The act of imposing sanctions again after a period of suspension or removal.  This often involves reinstating previously levied penalties or implementing new ones. | 例句：The UN Security Council is considering resanctioning the country due to its renewed nuclear program.  联合国安理会正在考虑由于该国重启核计划而对其重新实施制裁。


The company faces resanctioning after failing to comply with the initial agreement. 公司因未能遵守最初协议而面临重新制裁。


近义词：reimposing sanctions, renewing sanctions, reinstating sanctions | 反义词：lifting sanctions, removing sanctions
