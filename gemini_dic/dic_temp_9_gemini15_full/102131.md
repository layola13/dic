isozooid:/ˌaɪsoʊˈzoʊɪd/| n. |同形异体|A zooid that is morphologically similar to other zooids in a colony.  An isozooid is a member of a colony of zooids that are all alike in form and function.| 例句：In this siphonophore colony, all the zooids are isozooids, performing similar tasks.  在这个管水母群体中，所有个体都是同形异体，执行相似的任务。|近义词：homologous zooid|反义词：heterozooid
