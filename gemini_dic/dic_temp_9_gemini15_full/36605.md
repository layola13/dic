collect's:/kəˈlɛkts/| v. |收集，采集；收取；积累 | To gather or bring together; to accumulate; to receive payments. |He collects stamps. 他收集邮票。|The company collects the garbage. 公司收垃圾。|gather, accumulate, amass | disperse, scatter, dissipate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He collects stamps. = 他收集邮票。
She collects antique dolls. = 她收集古董娃娃。

一般过去时:
They collected donations for the charity. = 他们为慈善机构募集捐款。
He collected his mail from the mailbox. = 他从邮箱里取了他的邮件。

现在完成时:
I have collected many seashells on the beach. = 我在海滩上收集了许多贝壳。
She has collected enough evidence to prove her point. = 她已经收集了足够的证据来证明她的观点。

过去完成时:
They had collected all the necessary documents before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经收集了所有必要的文档。
He had collected a large amount of data for his research. = 他为他的研究收集了大量的数据。

将来时:
We will collect the children from school. = 我们将从学校接孩子。
They will collect the tickets at the entrance. = 他们将在入口处收取门票。

虚拟语气:
If I collected all the coupons, I would get a free gift. = 如果我收集所有优惠券，我将获得一份免费礼物。
I wish I had collected more information before making the decision. = 我希望在做决定之前我已经收集了更多信息。

被动语态:
The garbage is collected every Tuesday. = 垃圾每星期二收集一次。
The donations were collected by volunteers. = 捐款是由志愿者收集的。

疑问句:
Does he collect coins? = 他收集硬币吗？
What kind of books do you collect? = 你收集什么类型的书？

否定句:
