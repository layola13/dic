porkiest:/ˈpɔːrkiːst/ | adj. | 最肥的；最像猪的；最贪吃的 | Most like a pig; excessively fat; gluttonous.  The word implies excessive fatness, often in a humorous or slightly derogatory way. |例句：He was the porkiest man I've ever seen. = 他是我见过的最胖的人。 The pig was so porkiest that it could barely walk. 这只猪太肥了，几乎走不动了。|近义词：fattest, chubbiest, greediest |反义词：thinnest, leanest, abstemious
