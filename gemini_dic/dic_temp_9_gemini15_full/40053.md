consumptively:/kənˈsʌmptɪvli/| adv.| 虚弱地；憔悴地；消耗性地 | In a consumptive manner; characterized by consumption or wasting away.  It often describes someone who is weak and wasting away, typically due to a disease like tuberculosis. | 例句：He coughed consumptively, his frail body wracked with illness. 他虚弱地咳嗽着，瘦弱的身体被疾病折磨着。


He lived consumptively, spending far more than he earned. 他过着挥霍的生活，花销远超过收入。| 近义词：weakly, sickly, wastefully | 反义词：healthily, robustly, thriftily
