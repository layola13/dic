orientate:/ˈɔːriɛnteɪt/| vt.| 使适应；使定向；确定…的方向 |To adjust or become adjusted to a new situation or environment; to determine the position of something or someone in relation to the points of a compass. |例句：The course is designed to orientate students to the challenges of university life. 这门课程旨在让学生适应大学生活的挑战。
例句：The pilot used the stars to orientate himself.  飞行员用星星来确定自己的方位。|近义词：adapt, adjust, align|反义词：disorient, confuse|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He orientates himself quickly to new environments. = 他很快就能适应新的环境。
The teacher orientates the students to the new curriculum. = 老师引导学生适应新的课程。


一般过去时:
She orientated herself to the unfamiliar city. = 她适应了这座陌生的城市。
The map orientated him to the location of the museum. = 地图帮助他找到了博物馆的位置。


现在完成时:
They have orientated themselves to the company's culture. = 他们已经适应了公司的文化。
The guide has orientated the tourists to the historical sites. = 导游已经带领游客参观了历史遗迹。


过去完成时:
He had orientated himself before starting the project. = 他在开始项目之前已经做好准备。
She had orientated the team to the new strategy before the meeting. = 她在会议之前已经向团队介绍了新战略。


将来时:
We will orientate the new employees tomorrow. = 我们明天将对新员工进行入职培训。
The program will orientate participants to the latest research findings. = 该项目将使参与者了解最新的研究成果。


虚拟语气:
If I had orientated myself better, I would have avoided getting lost. = 如果我事先做好准备，就不会迷路了。
I wish I had orientated myself more thoroughly before making the decision. = 我希望在做决定之前能更全面地了解情况。


被动语态:
The new recruits were orientated to the company's policies. = 新员工接受了公司政策的培训。
The students were orientated to the research methods. = 学生们接受了研究方法的培训。


疑问句:
How are the new staff orientated to the company? = 公司是如何对新员工进行培训的？
Are the participants properly orientated to the task? = 参与者是否对任务有充分的了解？


否定句:
He didn't orientate himself properly, so he got lost. = 他没有做好方向准备，所以迷路了。
The company didn't orientate the new employees effectively. = 公司没有有效地对新员工进行培训。


PHRASE->
orientate oneself = 确定自己的方位
orientate to = 使适应；使定向于
orientate around = 以…为中心
orientate towards = 朝向；致力于
get oriented = 适应新环境
