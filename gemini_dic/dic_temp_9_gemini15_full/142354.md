orthometric:/ˌɔːrθəˈmɛtrɪk/| adj.|正高的；正射的|Relating to or denoting height above a reference surface such as the geoid, corrected for the curvature of the earth.|The orthometric height of a point is its height above the geoid.  正高是指某点高于大地水准面的高度。|近义词：geodetic,  ellipsoidal|反义词：None.
