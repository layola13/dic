iron-like: /ˈaɪənlaɪk/ | adj. |像铁一样的，坚硬的，坚强的，不屈不挠的 | Resembling iron in hardness, strength, or inflexibility; unyielding; resolute. |例句：His iron-like will helped him overcome many obstacles. 他的钢铁般的意志帮助他克服了许多障碍。

例句：The iron-like grip of the vise held the metal firmly in place.  老虎钳像铁一样牢固地夹住了金属。


近义词：strong, firm, inflexible, unyielding, resolute, steadfast, indomitable

反义词：weak, yielding, flexible, pliable, irresolute, wavering


词性：形容词


词源：iron (铁) + like (像…一样)  The word is a compound adjective formed by combining the noun "iron," referring to the metal, with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance.  This construction signifies something possessing qualities similar to iron, primarily its hardness and strength.
