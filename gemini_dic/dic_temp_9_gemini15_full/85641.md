haunter:/ˈhɔːntər/|/ˈhɑːntər/| n. |萦绕不去的人或事物；鬼魂；常客| A person or thing that haunts a place or a person's mind; a ghost; a frequent visitor.  |例句：The memory of that day is a constant haunter. 那一天的记忆挥之不去。|例句：The old house is said to be haunted by a haunter.据说那栋老房子闹鬼。|近义词：ghost, spirit, specter, frequenter|反义词：none


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ghost haunts the old house. = 鬼魂出没于那栋老房子。
He haunts the library every afternoon. = 他每天下午都去图书馆。


一般过去时:
The ghost haunted the old house for years. = 鬼魂多年来一直出没于那栋老房子。
She haunted the streets looking for him. = 她徘徊在街上寻找他。


现在完成时:
That image has haunted me ever since. = 从那以后，那幅画面一直萦绕在我的心头。
He has haunted this cafe for months. = 他已经在这个咖啡馆常来了好几个月了。


过去完成时:
He had haunted the same bar for years before he met her. = 在他遇见她之前，他已经常去同一家酒吧好多年了。
The fear of failure had haunted her throughout her career. = 失败的恐惧一直困扰着她整个职业生涯。


将来时:
The memories will haunt him for the rest of his life. = 这些记忆将会伴随他一生。
I think this place will be haunted by this incident for years to come. = 我认为这个地方未来几年都将受到这件事的影响。


虚拟语气:
If only I could forget the things that haunt me. = 但愿我能忘记那些萦绕在我心头的事物。
I wish those memories didn't haunt me so much. = 我希望那些回忆不会如此困扰我。


被动语态:
The house is haunted by a malevolent spirit. = 这房子被一个邪恶的灵魂缠绕着。
He felt haunted by his past mistakes. = 他感到被过去的错误所困扰。


疑问句:
What haunts you most about this experience? = 关于这次经历，什么最让你难以忘怀？
Does anything haunt your dreams? = 有什么东西会萦绕在你的梦里吗？


否定句:
Nothing haunts me more than regret. = 没有什么比后悔更让我难以忘怀。
The past doesn't haunt him anymore. = 过去不再困扰他了。


PHRASE->
haunt the memory = 萦绕在记忆中
haunt the mind = 萦绕在心头
haunt a place = 出没于某个地方
haunt someone = 纠缠某人
haunt one's thoughts = 占据某人的思绪
