recriminates:/rɪˈkrɪmɪneɪts/| v.|相互指责，反责|To accuse in return; to make counter-accusations.  It implies a mutual exchange of blame, often in an angry or bitter way.|例句：After the fight, they both recriminated each other for starting it.  争吵后，他们互相指责对方是始作俑者。

例句：The two parties spent hours recriminating, neither willing to admit fault. 双方互相指责了几个小时，谁也不肯承认错误。


近义词：counter-accuse, accuse reciprocally, blame, reproach

反义词：exonerate, absolve, forgive


USAGE->

He recriminates whenever things go wrong. = 每当事情出错时，他都会互相指责。
