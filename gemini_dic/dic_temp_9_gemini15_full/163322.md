preconfines:/ˌpriːkənˈfaɪnz/ | v. | 预先限制；预先限定 | To restrict or confine beforehand; to limit or define in advance.  |The rules preconfined their choices to only three options. 规则预先将他们的选择限制在三个选项中。  | restrict, limit, confine, delimit | unrestricted, open-ended, unlimited | 动词
