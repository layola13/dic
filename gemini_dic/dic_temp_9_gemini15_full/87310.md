hern:/hɜːrn/| n.| 疝气，腹股沟疝 | A hernia is a condition in which part of an organ pushes through a weak area of muscle.  |Example: He developed a hernia while lifting heavy weights. 他在举重时患上了疝气。|近义词：rupture |反义词:  无
