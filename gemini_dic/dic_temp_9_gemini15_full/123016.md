monastical: /məˈnæstɪkəl/ | adj. | 僧侣的；隐修的；禁欲的 | Relating to monasteries or monks; characteristic of monastic life; austere and self-disciplined.  |例句：He led a monastical life, devoting himself to prayer and study. 他过着僧侣般的生活，致力于祈祷和学习。
He adopted a monastical lifestyle, abstaining from worldly pleasures. 他过着禁欲的生活，远离世俗享乐。| 近义词：ascetic, monastic, secluded | 反义词：worldly, secular, hedonistic
