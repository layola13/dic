japanning:/dʒəˈpænɪŋ/| n. vt.|日式漆器工艺；用漆涂饰|The process of coating an object with a varnish, typically black or dark-colored, and then baking it to create a hard, glossy finish.  This technique was originally associated with Japanese lacquerware.|例句：The antique box was beautifully japanning. 这只古董盒漆器工艺精美。

The craftsman carefully japanning the tray. 工匠仔细地给托盘上漆。|近义词：lacquering, varnishing, enameling|反义词：unvarnishing, stripping
