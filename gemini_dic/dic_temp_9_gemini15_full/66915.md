expeller:/ɪkˈspɛlər/| n. |榨油机；排出器；驱逐者 | A machine that extracts oil from seeds or other materials by pressing.  It can also refer to something that expels or forces something out. |例句：The expeller pressed the oil from the soybeans.  榨油机从大豆中榨取油脂。

例句：The pump acted as an expeller, removing the excess water.  泵作为排出器，排出多余的水分。|近义词：extractor, press, ejector |反义词：injector, inserter
