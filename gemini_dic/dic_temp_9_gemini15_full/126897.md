narcissists:/ˈnɑːrsɪsɪsts/| n. |自恋者，自我陶醉者 |A narcissist is a person who has an excessive interest in or admiration of themselves.  They often have a grandiose sense of self-importance and a lack of empathy for others. |Example: He's a classic narcissist; he only talks about himself and his achievements. 他是个典型的自恋狂；他只会谈论自己和自己的成就。  She is a narcissist who demands constant attention and praise. 她是一个自恋狂，需要不断的关注和赞美。|近义词：egotist, self-obsessed, conceited |反义词：humble, modest, selfless |名词
