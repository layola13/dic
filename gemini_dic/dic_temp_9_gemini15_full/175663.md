rantingly:/ˈræntɪŋli/| adv.| 狂热地；滔滔不绝地；怒气冲冲地 |  Characterized by ranting; in a ranting manner.  It suggests a speech or behavior that is passionate, uncontrolled, and often angry or illogical. | 例句：He rantingly denounced the government's policies. 他滔滔不绝地谴责政府的政策。

He spoke rantingly about the injustices he had suffered. 他怒气冲冲地讲述了他遭受的不公正待遇。|近义词：vehemently, furiously, passionately, wildly |反义词：calmly, quietly, serenely, peacefully
