英文解析：The plural form of "complementariness," referring to the instances or aspects of something being complementary.  It highlights the multiple ways in which different elements or components can work together effectively because of their differences.


例句：

The research highlights the numerous complementarinesses between different scientific disciplines.  研究强调了不同科学学科之间众多的互补性。

The success of the project depended on the various complementarinesses of the team members’ expertise.  项目的成功取决于团队成员专业知识的各种互补性。
