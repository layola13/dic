hemsley:/ˈhɛmzli/| n. |亨斯利（姓氏）|Hemsley is a surname of English origin.  It is a locational name from places called Hemsley in Yorkshire, England. These places are named after an Old English personal name, *Hæm* or *Hæma*, plus *lēah*, meaning "woodland clearing". Therefore, Hemsley ultimately means "Hæm's or Hæma's woodland clearing".|The Hemsleys are a prominent family in the area.  |近义词: (无直接近义词，因为是姓氏)|反义词: (无)|名词
