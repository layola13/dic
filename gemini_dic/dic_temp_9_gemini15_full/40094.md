contaminant:/kənˈtæmɪnənt/ | n. | 污染物，杂质 | A substance that makes something impure or poisonous.  | Example:  The water sample contained several harmful contaminants.  这个水样包含几种有害的污染物。

The presence of heavy metals was a significant contaminant in the soil.  土壤中重金属的存在是重要的污染物。|近义词：impurity, pollutant, adulterant |反义词：purifier, cleanser
