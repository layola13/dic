instructional:/ɪnˈstrʌkʃənəl/ | adj. |教学的；指导性的；教育性的 | Relating to or connected with instruction or teaching.  |例句：The instructional materials were well-designed and easy to understand.  教学材料设计精良，易于理解。|例句：The film has a strong instructional component, teaching viewers about the dangers of drug use. 这部电影具有很强的教育意义，向观众传授吸毒的危害。|近义词：educational, didactic, teaching, educative |反义词：non-educational, recreational
