daikon:/ˈdaɪkɒn/|/ˈdaɪkɑːn/| n. |白萝卜，大萝卜|A large white radish, typically long and cylindrical, cultivated in East Asia.  It is often used in Japanese, Korean, and Chinese cuisine.|例句：I used daikon radish in my kimchi. 我在我的泡菜里用了白萝卜。|例句：The daikon was grated and added to the salad. 白萝卜被磨碎后加进了沙拉。|近义词：Japanese radish, oriental radish|反义词：无
