marries:/ˈmæriz/ | vt. |结婚；娶；嫁| To join in marriage; to unite in wedlock.  It can also refer to the act of performing a marriage ceremony.|例句：They married last year. 他们去年结婚了。|例句：The priest married the couple. 牧师为这对夫妇主持婚礼。|近义词：weds, unites, joins in matrimony |反义词：divorces, separates


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He marries her next month. = 他下个月和她结婚。
She marries into a wealthy family. = 她嫁入一个富裕的家庭。


一般过去时:
They married in a small church. = 他们在小教堂里结婚了。
My parents married young. = 我父母结婚很早。


现在完成时:
They have married for ten years. = 他们结婚十年了。
She has married twice. = 她结过两次婚。


过去完成时:
They had married before the war broke out. = 战争爆发前他们就结婚了。
He had married her secretly. = 他曾秘密地娶了她。


将来时:
We will marry in spring. = 我们将在春天结婚。
They will marry soon.  = 他们很快就要结婚了。


虚拟语气:
If I married her, I would be happy. = 如果我娶了她，我会很幸福。
I wish I had married sooner. = 我真希望我早点结婚。


被动语态:
The couple were married by a judge. = 这对夫妇是由法官主持婚礼结婚的。
The ceremony was to be married at sunset. = 婚礼定在日落时举行。


疑问句:
When did they marry? = 他们什么时候结婚的？
Will they marry? = 他们会结婚吗？


否定句:
They did not marry until they were thirty. = 直到三十岁他们才结婚。
She will not marry him. = 她不会嫁给他。


PHRASE->
marry into:  嫁入；娶入
get married: 结婚
married to: 与...结婚
be married: 已婚
happily married: 幸福的婚姻
