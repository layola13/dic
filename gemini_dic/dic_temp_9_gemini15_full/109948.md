liberalizations:/ˌlɪbərəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |自由化；开放化|The process of making something more liberal; the removal of restrictions or limitations.  It often refers to economic, political, or social reforms that increase individual freedoms and reduce government control.|例句：The economic liberalizations in the 1980s led to significant growth.  80年代的经济自由化导致了显著的增长。|例句：Political liberalizations are often a precursor to democratic reforms. 政治自由化往往是民主改革的前兆。|近义词：deregulation, reform, opening up|反义词：restrictions, repression, control|名词
