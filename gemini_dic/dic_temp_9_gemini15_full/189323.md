safes:/seɪfs/ | n. |保险箱；安全的地方|A safe is a strong metal box or container for keeping valuables secure.  It's often used to protect things like money, jewelry, and important documents from theft or damage.|例句：We keep our important documents in a fireproof safe. 我们把重要的文件放在防火保险箱里。

例句：The mountains provided a safe haven from the storm. 山区为暴风雨提供了一个安全的避难所。|近义词：vault, strongbox, security deposit box |反义词：danger, risk, hazard |词性:名词
