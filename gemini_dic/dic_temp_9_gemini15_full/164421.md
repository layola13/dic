preendorses:/ˌpriːɪnˈdɔːrsɪz/| v.|预先认可，预先批准 | To endorse beforehand; to give prior approval to.  This word is a relatively rare blend of "pre-" and "endorses." It implies an endorsement that takes place before an action or event, rather than afterward. | 例句：The committee preendorsed the candidate before the official nomination process. 委员会在正式提名程序之前就预先认可了该候选人。

The company preendorsed the project before securing funding. 公司在获得资金之前就预先批准了该项目。|近义词：preapprove, authorize in advance, sanction beforehand |反义词：reject beforehand, disapprove in advance |动词
