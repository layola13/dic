reblends:/ˈriːblɛndz/| n.| 再混合物；重新混合的东西 | A reblend is a mixture that has been blended again, often to improve its quality or to create a new product.  It's commonly used in the context of food, beverages, and other materials where ingredients are combined and then remixed. For example, coffee beans might be re-blended after initial roasting to achieve a specific flavor profile. |例句：The coffee roaster created a new reblend of Arabica and Robusta beans.  这个咖啡烘焙师创造了一种新的阿拉比卡和罗布斯塔咖啡豆的混合物。The winemaker decided to reblend the wine after fermentation to balance the acidity.  酿酒师决定在发酵后重新混合葡萄酒以平衡酸度。|近义词：remix, recombine, mixture|反义词：separation, unmixing|名词
