est:/ɛst/ | adj. | 最高级的；最…的 |  It is the superlative form of adjectives and adverbs, indicating the highest degree of a quality.  It's often used with "the" before a noun or pronoun. | 例句：She is the tallest girl in the class. 她班上个子最高。 | 例句：This is the most beautiful flower I have ever seen. 这是我见过的最美的花。 | 近义词：most, supreme, ultimate | 反义词：least


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This is the best option. = 这是最好的选择。
He is the strongest person I know. = 他是我认识的最强壮的人。

一般过去时:
That was the best movie I ever saw. = 那是我看过的最好的电影。
She was the happiest person in the room. = 她是房间里最快乐的人。

现在完成时:
This has been the best year of my life. = 这是我生命中最美好的一年。
He has run the fastest marathon of his career. = 他跑了他职业生涯中最快的马拉松。

过去完成时:
That had been the most difficult challenge I'd ever faced. = 那是我曾经面临过的最艰难的挑战。
She had sung the most beautiful song at the concert. = 她在音乐会上唱了最美丽的歌。

将来时:
This will be the best party ever. = 这将是有史以来最好的派对。
He will be the tallest player on the team. = 他将是队里最高的球员。

虚拟语气:
If it were the best solution, we would have chosen it. = 如果这是最好的解决方案，我们会选择它。
I wish I had the best car in the world. = 我希望我有世界上最好的车。

被动语态:
The best solution was chosen. = 最佳方案已被选中。
The most beautiful painting was displayed in the gallery. = 最美的画作陈列在画廊里。

疑问句:
Who is the tallest person in your family? = 你家谁最高？
Which is the best restaurant in town? = 城里哪家餐馆最好？

否定句:
This is not the best way to do it. = 这不是最好的方法。
She is not the most intelligent person I know. = 她不是我认识的最聪明的人。
