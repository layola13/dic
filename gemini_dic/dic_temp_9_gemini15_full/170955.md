pseudopatient:/ˈsuːdəʊˈpeɪʃənt/| n. |伪病人；假病人 |A pseudopatient is a person who pretends to be a patient, typically for research purposes, to evaluate the effectiveness of a mental health facility or similar setting.|例句：In Rosenhan's famous experiment, pseudopatients feigned auditory hallucinations to gain admission to psychiatric hospitals.  在罗森汉著名的实验中，伪病人伪装患有幻听以进入精神病院。|近义词：simulated patient, fake patient |反义词：genuine patient, real patient
