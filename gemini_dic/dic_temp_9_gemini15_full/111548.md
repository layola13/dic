lobbied:/ˈlɒbid/ /ˈlɑːbid/| vt.|游说，进行游说活动|To attempt to influence (a politician or public official) on an issue.  It often involves direct communication and advocacy to persuade them to support a particular viewpoint or policy.|例句：The environmental group lobbied Congress to pass stricter pollution controls. =  环保团体游说国会通过更严格的污染控制措施。

The pharmaceutical company lobbied the government for tax breaks. = 制药公司游说政府减税。


近义词：pressure, influence, advocate for, petition


反义词：oppose, obstruct, hinder


词性:动词
