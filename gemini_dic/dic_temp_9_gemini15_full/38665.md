concussive:/kənˈkʌsɪv/| adj.| 震荡的；冲击的；造成脑震荡的 | Relating to or causing concussion; violently forceful.  |例句：The concussive force of the explosion shattered the windows. 爆炸的冲击力震碎了窗户。
The boxer suffered a concussive blow to the head. 拳击手头部受到重击导致脑震荡。|近义词：impactful, jarring, shocking |反义词：gentle, soft, mild
