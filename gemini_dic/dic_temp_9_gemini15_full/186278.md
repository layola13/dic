rhizopodous:/ˌraɪˈzɒpədəs/| adj.|根足类的；具有根足的|Having or relating to rhizopods, a class of amoeboid protozoa characterized by pseudopodia.  The term describes organisms that move and feed using pseudopodia, temporary projections of the cytoplasm.|Example:  Amoebas are rhizopodous organisms.  变形虫是根足类生物。|近义词：amoeboid|反义词：N/A
