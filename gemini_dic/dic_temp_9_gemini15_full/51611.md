devel:/ˈdɛvəl/ | v. |发展；开发|To develop means to grow or improve over time, or to create something new.|The company is developing a new software program.  这家公司正在开发一个新的软件程序。|The child is developing well. 这个孩子发育良好。|grow, evolve, expand |decline, regress, deteriorate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They develop new products every year. = 他们每年都开发新产品。
The plant develops slowly in this climate. = 在这种气候下，植物生长缓慢。

一般过去时:
The company developed a new marketing strategy. = 公司制定了一个新的营销策略。
She developed a serious illness. = 她得了重病。

现在完成时:
We have developed a strong relationship with our customers. = 我们已经与客户建立了牢固的关系。
They have developed a new technology. = 他们已经开发出一种新技术。

过去完成时:
They had developed the prototype before the presentation. = 他们在演示之前已经开发出了原型。
The city had developed rapidly in the past decade. = 这个城市在过去十年中发展迅速。

将来时:
They will develop a new app next year. = 他们明年将开发一个新的应用程序。
The country will develop its infrastructure. = 这个国家将发展其基础设施。

虚拟语气:
If we had developed this technology earlier, we would have been more competitive. = 如果我们早些开发这项技术，我们的竞争力就会更强。
I wish I had developed better habits earlier in life. = 我希望我早些养成更好的习惯。

被动语态:
The software is being developed by a team of experts. = 这个软件正在由一个专家团队开发。
New strategies are being developed to address the problem. = 正在制定新的策略来解决这个问题。

疑问句:
How did they develop this product? = 他们是如何开发这个产品的？
What factors influenced the development of the city? = 哪些因素影响了这座城市的发展？

否定句:
The project did not develop as planned. = 项目进展不顺利。
The film didn't develop properly. = 胶卷没有冲洗好。


PHRASE->

develop a disease = 患病
develop a skill = 培养技能
develop a theory = 发展理论
