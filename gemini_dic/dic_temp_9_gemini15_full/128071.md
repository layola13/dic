nervier:/ˈnɜːrviər/| adj. |更紧张的；更胆怯的；更大胆的（取决于语境）|More nervous or timid;  Alternatively, showing more nerve or boldness.  The word implies a comparative degree, suggesting someone or something is *more* nervous/timid/bold than something else.|


例句：

He became nervier as the deadline approached. =  随着最后期限的临近，他变得更加紧张。

The nervier climber reached the summit first. =  那个胆子更大的攀登者第一个到达山顶。


近义词：more nervous, more timid, bolder (depending on context), more audacious


反义词：calmer, more relaxed, less timid, less daring


词性：形容词


词源：源自形容词nervous，加后缀-ier构成比较级。nervous源于拉丁语nervus（神经）。
