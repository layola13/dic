neotectonics:/ˌniːoʊtɛkˈtɑːnɪks/| n. |新构造运动学|The study of recent tectonic activity, typically encompassing the last few million years, focusing on its impact on the Earth's surface.  It uses a variety of techniques, including geodetic measurements, paleoseismology, and geomorphology, to understand ongoing tectonic processes and their hazards.|The neotectonics of the region show evidence of significant faulting and uplift. 这个地区的新构造运动显示出明显的断层和抬升迹象。|近义词：active tectonics, recent tectonics|反义词：paleotectonics
