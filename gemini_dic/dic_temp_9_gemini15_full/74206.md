foursided:/ˈfɔːrsaɪdɪd/| adj. |四边的，四面的|Having four sides.  This adjective describes something with four sides, often implying a flat shape but not necessarily a regular shape like a square. |例句：The table has a foursided top. 这张桌子顶部是四方形的。

例句：The foursided building stood proudly on the hill.  这座四面体建筑矗立在山上。


近义词：quadrilateral, tetragonal


反义词：three-sided, five-sided


词性:形容词
