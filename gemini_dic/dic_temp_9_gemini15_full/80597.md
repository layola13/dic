googol:/ˈɡuːɡɒl/| n.|古戈尔（10的100次方）|A googol is the number 1 followed by one hundred zeros.  It's a very large number, often used to illustrate concepts of scale and infinity. |Example:  A googol is far larger than the number of atoms in the observable universe.  古戈尔远大于可观测宇宙中原子数量。|近义词：None (due to its unique magnitude)|反义词：None (due to its unique magnitude)|名词
