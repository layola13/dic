nightman:/ˈnaɪtmæn/| n. |夜间工人，清道夫，夜巡人员|A nightman is a person who works at night, often performing tasks such as cleaning streets or emptying garbage cans. The term is somewhat archaic and can also refer to a night watchman or security guard. |例句：In the old days, the nightman would come around to collect the trash.  = 过去，清道夫会来收垃圾。The nightman patrolled the factory grounds all night. = 夜巡人员整夜巡逻工厂场地。|近义词：street cleaner, sanitation worker, night watchman, security guard|反义词：day worker
