pollutant:/pəˈluːtənt/ | n. |污染物，污染剂 |A substance that contaminates something, especially the environment.  |Example:  Air pollutants are a major cause of respiratory illnesses. 空气污染物是呼吸系统疾病的主要原因。  The factory was accused of releasing harmful pollutants into the river. 工厂被指控向河流排放有害污染物。|近义词：contaminant, impurity, toxin |反义词：purifier, cleanser
