diverge:/daɪˈvɜːrdʒ/ | /dɪˈvɜːrdʒ/ | vi. | 分歧；偏离；背离 | To move or extend in different directions from a common point; to differ in opinion, character, or course.  |例句：The two roads diverge at the crossroads.  两条路在十字路口分岔。
The scientists' opinions diverged on the cause of the disease. 科学家们对疾病的起因意见分歧。|近义词：differ, deviate, branch off |反义词：converge, coincide


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The roads diverge at the intersection. = 道路在交叉路口分岔。
Their opinions diverge on this issue. = 他们在这个问题上的意见相左。

一般过去时:
The paths diverged into the forest. = 小路在森林里分岔了。
Their views diverged sharply after the debate. = 辩论之后，他们的观点截然不同。

现在完成时:
Our paths have diverged considerably since college. = 自从大学毕业后，我们的道路已经大相径庭。
Their ideas have diverged significantly over the years. = 多年来，他们的想法已经大相径庭。

过去完成时:
The river had diverged into several smaller streams. = 河流已分流成几条较小的溪流。
Their political stances had diverged long before the election. = 早在选举之前，他们的政治立场就已经分歧了。

将来时:
The two companies' strategies will diverge in the coming years. = 这两家公司的战略将在未来几年分道扬鑣。
Their interests will likely diverge as they grow older. = 随着年龄的增长，他们的兴趣可能会发生分歧。

虚拟语气:
If their opinions had not diverged, the project might have succeeded. = 如果他们的意见没有分歧，项目可能已经成功了。
I wish our paths had not diverged so dramatically. = 我真希望我们的道路没有如此大的差异。

被动语态:
The road was diverged by the construction. = 道路因施工而被分岔。 (This is less common as diverge is usually intransitive)
The project's goals were diverged from the original plan.  项目的目標偏離了原計劃。(This uses a passive form but is less natural)


疑问句:
Do your views diverge from the majority opinion? = 你的观点与多数人的意见相左吗？
Where do the paths diverge? = 小路在哪里分岔？


否定句:
Their opinions did not diverge significantly. = 他们的意见没有显著分歧。
The two groups' goals do not diverge. = 这两个群体的目标没有分歧。


PHRASE->
diverge from = 偏离于
diverge widely = 大相径庭
diverge sharply = 截然不同
diverge significantly = 显著不同
point of divergence = 分歧点
