oked:/oʊkt/| adj.|好的，同意的，批准的|  It means something has been approved or agreed upon.  It's an informal term often used in short communications, such as text messages or emails.| Example: "Oked the plan." = "批准了计划。" |近义词：approved, agreed, alright, okay|反义词：rejected, disapproved, denied|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He oked the proposal. = 他批准了提案。
They oked the new design. = 他们批准了新的设计。

一般过去时:
She oked the contract yesterday. = 她昨天批准了合同。
He oked my request last week. = 他上周批准了我的请求。

现在完成时:
They have oked the budget. = 他们已经批准了预算。
She has oked the changes. = 她已经批准了这些更改。

过去完成时:
He had oked the document before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经批准了文件。
They had oked the plan before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经批准了计划。

将来时:
They will ok the application next week. = 他们下周将批准申请。
She will ok the purchase order tomorrow. = 她明天将批准采购订单。

虚拟语气:
If he oked the project, we could start immediately. = 如果他批准了项目，我们可以立即开始。
I wish she had oked my leave request. = 我希望她批准了我的休假申请。

被动语态:
The proposal was oked by the manager. = 提案得到了经理的批准。
The changes were oked by the committee. = 这些更改得到了委员会的批准。

疑问句:
Has the board oked the merger? = 董事会批准了并购吗？
Did he ok the expense report? = 他批准了费用报告吗？

否定句:
She didn't ok the request. = 她没有批准请求。
They haven't oked the budget yet. = 他们还没有批准预算。
