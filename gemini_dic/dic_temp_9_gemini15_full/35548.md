coalescent:/ˌkoʊəˈlɛsnt/| adj.| 并合的；融合的；聚合的 | Coming together to form one mass or whole; merging.  |The coalescent droplets formed a single large drop.  合并的小水滴形成了一个大水滴。| convergent, merging, uniting | separate, diverging, disparate |
