shoeshines:/ˈʃuːʃaɪnz/ | n. |擦鞋 (服务)；擦鞋匠|The act of shining shoes; the business of shining shoes; also, a person who shines shoes.  |例句：He makes a living from shoeshines. 他靠擦鞋为生。
例句：The shoeshine stand was busy during the lunch hour.  午餐时间擦鞋摊位很忙。|近义词：shoe polishing, bootblacking |反义词：N/A
