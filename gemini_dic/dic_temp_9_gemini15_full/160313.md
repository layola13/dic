pools:/puːlz/| n.|水池；游泳池；（资金等的）积累|A pool is a small area of still water, often artificial.  It can also refer to a collection of something, such as money or resources.|例句：The children were playing in the swimming pool.孩子们在游泳池里玩耍。

例句：The company has a large pool of talented employees.这家公司拥有一大批优秀员工。|近义词：pond, lake, collection|反义词：dryness, depletion|名词
