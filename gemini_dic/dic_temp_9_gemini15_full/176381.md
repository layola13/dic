re-freshed:/rɪˈfreʃt/| adj. |使精神焕发的；更新的；恢复活力的 |Refreshed means feeling more energetic and awake, often after a period of rest or relaxation.  It can also describe something that has been made new or updated. |例句：I felt refreshed after a long sleep.  我睡了个长觉后感觉精神焕发了。  The website has been refreshed with a new design.  网站已经更新了新的设计。|近义词：invigorated, revitalized, renewed |反义词：exhausted, tired, drained


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cool breeze refreshes me. = 凉爽的微风使我感到清爽。
This drink refreshes the body. = 这饮料使身体恢复活力。


一般过去时:
The rain refreshed the garden. = 雨水使花园恢复了生机。
The vacation refreshed my spirit. = 假期使我的精神焕发。


现在完成时:
The new paint has refreshed the room. = 新油漆使房间焕然一新。
The update has refreshed the software's interface. = 更新使软件界面焕然一新。


过去完成时:
I had refreshed myself before the meeting. = 我在会议前已经让自己恢复了精神。
They had refreshed the data before presenting. = 他们在演示前已经更新了数据。


将来时:
A long walk will refresh you. = 长途步行会让你精神焕发。
The new features will refresh the application. = 新功能将会使应用程序焕然一新。


虚拟语气:
If I had a long rest, I would feel refreshed. = 如果我好好休息一下，我会感到精神焕发。
I wish I had refreshed the page before submitting. = 我希望我在提交之前更新了页面。


被动语态:
The room was refreshed with new furniture. = 房间用新的家具装饰一新。
The software is being refreshed with new security patches. = 软件正在更新新的安全补丁。


疑问句:
Does this drink refresh you? = 这饮料让你感觉清爽吗？

否定句:
