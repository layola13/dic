industrial:/ɪnˈdʌstriəl/| adj.|工业的；产业的|Relating to or concerned with industry.  It often describes things related to manufacturing, production, or the economy.|例句：The industrial revolution transformed society. 工业革命改变了社会。|例句：The city has a large industrial area. 这个城市有一个很大的工业区。|近义词：manufacturing, factory, commercial|反义词：agricultural, rural


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory produces industrial goods. = 工厂生产工业品。
This company is involved in many industrial projects. = 这家公司参与了许多工业项目。

一般过去时:
The industrial revolution began in the 18th century. = 工业革命始于18世纪。
They closed the industrial plant last year. = 他们去年关闭了这家工业工厂。

现在完成时:
Many countries have industrialized rapidly. = 许多国家已经快速工业化。
The city has become a major industrial center. = 这个城市已成为主要的工业中心。

过去完成时:
Before the industrial revolution, most people worked in agriculture. = 在工业革命之前，大多数人从事农业。
They had already invested heavily in industrial equipment. = 他们已经大量投资于工业设备。

将来时:
This sector will continue to grow industrially. = 这个部门将在工业上继续增长。
We will need more skilled workers in the industrial sector. = 我们将在工业领域需要更多熟练工人。

虚拟语气:
If the industrial revolution hadn't happened, the world would be very different. = 如果没有发生工业革命，世界将会大不一样。
I wish the industrial pollution was less severe. = 我希望工业污染不那么严重。

被动语态:
Industrial waste is often dumped illegally. = 工业废物经常被非法倾倒。
The new factory is being built in the industrial park. = 新工厂正在工业园区建设中。

疑问句:
Is this area zoned for industrial use? = 这个区域是否划为工业用途？
What are the major industrial products of this region? = 这个地区的主要工业产品是什么？

否定句:
That area is not suitable for industrial development. = 那个地区不适合工业发展。
The company is not involved in industrial manufacturing. = 这家公司不从事工业制造。
