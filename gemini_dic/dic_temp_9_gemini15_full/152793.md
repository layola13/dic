pectic: /ˈpɛktɪk/ | adj. | 果胶的；与果胶有关的 | Relating to or derived from pectin.  Pectin is a soluble fiber found in the cell walls of plants, particularly fruits.  It's responsible for the firmness and texture of many fruits and vegetables. |

例句：

The jam has a high pectic content, giving it a good set. = 这果酱果胶含量高，所以凝固得很好。

The pectic substances in apples contribute to their texture. = 苹果中的果胶物质有助于其质地。


近义词：pectinaceous (less common)

反义词：None directly opposite, but words describing the opposite effect of pectin (e.g., watery, soft) could be considered contextual opposites.

词性：形容词
