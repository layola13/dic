riced:/raɪst/| v.|  碾成米，切碎成米粒状 | To cut or grind something into small, grain-like pieces, resembling rice.  |例句：He riced the potatoes for the stew. 他把土豆切成米粒状做炖菜。  He riced the carrots for the baby's food. 他把胡萝卜切碎成米粒状给婴儿吃。|近义词：mince, chop finely, grind |反义词：None specifically opposite, but could be considered the opposite of leaving something whole or in large chunks.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He rices the vegetables every morning. 他每天早上都会把蔬菜切碎。
She rices the potatoes before adding them to the soup.  她在把土豆加入汤中之前先将其切碎。
