jealous-of:/ˈdʒɛləs ʌv/| adj.|嫉妒的，羡慕的|Feeling or showing envy of someone or their achievements or possessions.|He was jealous of his brother's success. 他嫉妒他兄弟的成功。|envious, covetous|indifferent, unconcerned|形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is jealous of his friend's new car. 他嫉妒他朋友的新车。
She is always jealous of her sister's beauty. 她总是嫉妒她妹妹的美丽。


一般过去时:
I was jealous of her promotion. 我嫉妒她的升职。
They were jealous of our vacation. 他们嫉妒我们的假期。


现在完成时:
He has been jealous of me since I got the job. 自从我得到这份工作以来，他就一直嫉妒我。
She has always been jealous of her classmates' achievements. 她一直嫉妒她同学们的成就。


过去完成时:
I had been jealous of him for years before he finally moved away. 在他最终搬走之前，我已经嫉妒他好多年了。
She had been jealous of his talent long before he became famous. 在他成名之前很久，她就嫉妒他的才华了。


将来时:
I will be jealous if he gets the award. 如果他获得奖项，我会嫉妒的。
She will probably be jealous when she sees your new house. 当她看到你的新房子时，她可能会嫉妒。


虚拟语气:
If I were him, I wouldn't be jealous of others' success. 如果我是他，我不会嫉妒别人的成功。
I wish I weren't so jealous of her. 我真希望我不那么嫉妒她。


被动语态:
His success was often envied, and he was often the target of jealousy. 他的成功经常被人羡慕，他经常成为嫉妒的目标。
Her beauty was often the reason she was the subject of jealousy from others. 她的美貌经常是她成为别人嫉妒对象的原因。


疑问句:
Are you jealous of my new job? 你嫉妒我的新工作吗？
Is she jealous of your relationship? 她嫉妒你们的感情吗？


否定句:
I am not jealous of his wealth. 我不嫉妒他的财富。
She is not jealous of my achievements. 她不嫉妒我的成就。
