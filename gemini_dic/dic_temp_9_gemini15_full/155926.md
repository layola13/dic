photodetection:/ˌfoʊtoʊdɪˈtɛkʃən/ | n. |光电检测；光电探测 |The process of detecting light and converting it into an electrical signal. This process is fundamental to many technologies, including cameras, optical sensors, and medical imaging systems.  Photodetection involves various methods depending on the type of light and desired output.  | Example: The photodetection system in the camera is highly sensitive, allowing for excellent low-light performance. 光电检测系统非常灵敏，可在低光照条件下提供优异的性能。|近义词：light detection, optical sensing |反义词：None  |名词
