podosperm:/ˈpɒdəʊspɜːm/| n. |胚乳 |A podosperm is a type of seed that has a specialized structure called a podospore, which is a type of spore that develops into a new plant.  Podosperms are found in some species of plants, such as ferns and conifers.  The term is less commonly used now, often replaced with more specific terminology depending on the plant group. | Example: The study focused on the development of the podosperm in the newly discovered fern species.  这个研究集中在新发现的蕨类植物的胚乳发育上。|近义词：seed (in a broader sense), embryo |反义词：None directly applicable.
