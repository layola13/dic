grudges:/ˈɡrʌdʒɪz/| n.|怨恨，积怨|A grudge is a feeling of resentment or ill will toward someone.  It is a persistent feeling of anger or annoyance about something that someone has done.|He held a grudge against his former friend for years. 他多年来一直怨恨他的前朋友。|She harbors no grudges. 她没有怨恨。|resentment, animosity|forgiveness, reconciliation|名词
