shroudy:/ˈʃraʊdi/| adj.|裹尸布的；神秘的，难以理解的；阴沉的 | shrouded in mystery; suggesting death or the grave | The old house stood shrouded in mist.  古老的房子笼罩在雾中。|近义词：mysterious, enigmatic, obscure |反义词：clear, transparent, obvious


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The old house is shrouded in mystery.  = 这栋老房子笼罩着神秘。
This matter remains shrouded in secrecy. = 这件事仍然秘而不宣。


一般过去时:
The village was shrouded in fog. = 村庄被浓雾笼罩。
His face was shrouded in shadow. = 他的脸隐藏在阴影中。


现在完成时:
The truth has been shrouded in secrecy for years. = 真相多年来一直被掩盖。
The details of the accident have been shrouded in mystery. = 事故的细节一直是个谜。


过去完成时:
The evidence had been shrouded in doubt before the trial. = 在审判之前，证据一直被怀疑。
The killer’s motive had been shrouded in mystery for many years. = 凶手的动机多年来一直是个谜。


将来时:
The mountain will be shrouded in snow soon. = 这座山很快就会被积雪覆盖。
The future of the project will likely be shrouded in uncertainty. = 项目的未来可能会充满不确定性。


虚拟语气:
If the truth were not shrouded in mystery, we would know what happened. = 如果真相没有被掩盖，我们会知道发生了什么。
I wish the whole situation wasn't shrouded in such secrecy. = 我希望整个情况不要如此保密。


被动语态:
The crime scene was shrouded in darkness. = 犯罪现场被黑暗笼罩。
The details of the negotiation were shrouded in confidentiality. = 谈判的细节被保密。


疑问句:
Is the future of the company shrouded in uncertainty? = 公司的未来是否充满不确定性？
Is the truth always shrouded in mystery? = 真相总是被神秘笼罩吗？


否定句:
The facts were not shrouded in mystery; they were plain for all to see. = 事实并没有被神秘所掩盖；它们对所有人来说都很清楚。
The situation is not shrouded in secrecy; it's common knowledge. = 情况并非保密；这是众所周知的。
