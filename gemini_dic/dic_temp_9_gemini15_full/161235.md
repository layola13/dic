postman:/ˈpoʊstmæn/ /ˈpəʊstmæn/ | n. |邮递员|A person whose job is to deliver mail.  Postmen typically work for postal services and are responsible for sorting and delivering letters, packages, and other mail items to addresses within a designated area. | The postman delivered the package this morning.  今天早上邮递员送来了包裹。|mail carrier, letter carrier| (无明确反义词)|名词


USAGE->

He delivers the letters to each house. = 他把信送到每家每户。
