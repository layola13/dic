parer:/pær/| vt.|削皮，修整|To remove the skin or outer layer of something, especially fruit or vegetables.  It can also mean to trim or refine something.|例句：I need to pare the apples before making the pie. = 我需要在做馅饼之前削苹果皮。|例句：He pared down his expenses to save money. = 他削减开支以省钱。|近义词：peel, trim, whittle|反义词：add, augment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pares the potatoes every day. = 他每天削土豆皮。
She pares her fingernails carefully. = 她仔细地修剪指甲。

一般过去时:
He pared the apples before baking the pie. = 他在烤馅饼之前削了苹果皮。
She pared down the manuscript to meet the deadline. = 她精简手稿以赶上截止日期。

现在完成时:
I have pared all the carrots. = 我已经削好了所有胡萝卜。
She has pared down her belongings for the move. = 为了搬家，她已经精简了她的物品。

过去完成时:
He had pared the vegetables before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经削好了蔬菜。
She had pared down her speech to fit the time limit. = 她已经精简了她的演讲以符合时间限制。

将来时:
I will pare the potatoes for dinner tonight. = 今晚我将为晚餐削土豆皮。
She will pare down the report before submitting it. = 她将在提交报告之前精简报告。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would pare the manuscript more carefully. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更仔细地修剪手稿。
I wish I had pared down my expenses sooner. = 我希望我早点削减开支。

被动语态:
The apples were pared by the chef. = 苹果是由厨师削皮的。
The manuscript is being pared down by the editor. = 手稿正在被编辑精简。

疑问句:
Do you pare your own vegetables? = 你自己削蔬菜吗？
Has she pared down the budget yet? = 她已经精简预算了吗？

否定句:
He didn't pare the carrots before cooking them. = 他在烹饪胡萝卜之前没有削皮。
She hasn't pared down her collection of books. = 她还没有精简她的藏书。
