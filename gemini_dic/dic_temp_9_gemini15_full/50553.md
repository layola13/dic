deputies:/ˈdɛpjʊtiz/| n.|代理人；代表；副手|  A deputy is a person appointed to act as a substitute for another, especially a person who is elected to act in place of someone else.|The deputies are responsible for managing the daily affairs of the parliament.|近义词：representatives, substitutes, assistants|反义词：principals, superiors
