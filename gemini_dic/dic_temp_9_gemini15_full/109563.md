let-go: /ˈlɛt ɡoʊ/ | v. | 放手；放弃；释怀 | To release one's hold on something; to relinquish control or possession of something; to cease to worry about or be concerned with something. |


例句：

He let go of the rope. = 他松开了绳子。

She decided to let go of her anger. = 她决定放下她的怒气。


近义词：release, relinquish, abandon, forsake, surrender, give up


反义词：hold on, retain, keep, cling to


词性：动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:

He lets go of the ball. = 他松开了球。
They let go of their worries. = 他们放下他们的担忧。


一般过去时:

She let go of the steering wheel. = 她松开了方向盘。
He let go of his dreams. = 他放弃了他的梦想。


现在完成时:

I have let go of many things in my life. = 我一生中已经放下了很多东西。
She has let go of her resentment towards him. = 她已经释怀了她对他积压的怨恨。


过去完成时:

He had let go of the branch before he fell. = 他在掉下来之前已经松开了树枝。
They had let go of their hopes by that time. = 到那时他们已经放弃了希望。


将来时:

I will let go of this anxiety soon. = 我很快就会放下这种焦虑。
She will let go of the past and move on. = 她将放下过去并继续前进。


虚拟语气:

If I let go of my fear, I would be much happier. = 如果我放下我的恐惧，我会快乐得多。
I wish I could let go of this burden. = 我希望我能放下这个负担。


被动语态:  (Let go is rarely used passively)


疑问句:

Will you let go of the prejudice? = 你会放下偏见吗？
Did he let go of the opportunity? = 他放弃了那个机会吗？


否定句:

I cannot let go of my past. = 我无法放下我的过去。
He wouldn't let go of his anger. = 他不肯放下他的怒气。


PHRASE->

let go of: 放下；松开
let it go: 随它去吧；别放在心上
let yourself go: 放纵；不加约束
let someone go: 解雇某人
let something slide: 放任不管


注意事项:  The phrasal verb "let go" is commonly used in various contexts, ranging from physical actions to emotional release.  The meaning is often inferred from context.
