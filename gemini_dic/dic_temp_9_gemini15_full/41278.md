cordiality: /kɔːrdiˈæləti/ | n. | 热情；友善；诚恳 |  The quality of being cordial; friendliness; warmth of feeling.  It suggests a genuine and heartfelt warmth rather than mere politeness. |


例句：

Their cordiality made me feel welcome. = 他们的热情让我感到宾至如归。

The meeting was marked by cordiality and cooperation. = 会议以热情和合作为标志。


近义词：friendliness, warmth, geniality, affability, amity


反义词：hostility, coldness, animosity, antagonism


词性：名词
