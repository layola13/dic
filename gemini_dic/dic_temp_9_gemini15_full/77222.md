gastritic:/ɡæsˈtrɪtɪk/| adj.|胃的；胃炎的|Relating to the stomach; pertaining to gastritis (inflammation of the stomach lining).  Gastritic problems are often caused by infection or irritation.|例句：He experienced severe gastritic pain after eating spicy food. 他吃辛辣食物后经历了严重的胃痛。

例句：The doctor diagnosed her with a gastritic condition requiring medication. 医生诊断她患有需要药物治疗的胃炎。


近义词：stomach, gastric


反义词：None specifically antonymous, but words suggesting healthy stomach function could be considered indirect opposites.


词性:形容词
