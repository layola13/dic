probability:/ˌprɒbəˈbɪləti/|n.|可能性；概率|The probability of winning the lottery is very low.  赢得彩票的可能性非常低。|likelihood, chance, possibility|impossibility, certainty|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The probability of rain is high today. = 今天下雨的可能性很高。
What is the probability of success? = 成功几率是多少？


一般过去时:
The probability of success was higher than we expected. = 成功概率比我们预期的要高。
What was the probability of a car accident yesterday? = 昨天发生车祸的可能性有多大？


现在完成时:
The probability of a cure has increased significantly. = 治愈的可能性已经大大提高。
What probability have we calculated so far? = 到目前为止我们计算出了什么概率？


过去完成时:
The probability of success had been underestimated. = 成功概率被低估了。
What had been the probability of rain before the forecast changed? = 预报改变之前下雨的概率是多少？


将来时:
The probability of success will depend on many factors. = 成功概率将取决于许多因素。
What will the probability of a hurricane be next week? = 下周飓风的概率是多少？


虚拟语气:
If the probability were higher, we would proceed. = 如果概率更高，我们将继续进行。
I wish the probability of success were greater. = 我希望成功的概率更大。


被动语态:
The probability is calculated using a complex formula. = 概率是使用复杂的公式计算的。
The probability of an earthquake was underestimated by the experts. = 地震的可能性被专家低估了。


疑问句:
What is the probability of that happening? = 发生这种情况的概率是多少？
What probability do you assign to this event? = 你对这个事件赋予什么概率？


否定句:
The probability of failure is not negligible. = 失败的可能性不可忽略。
There is no probability that he will win. = 他不可能赢。
