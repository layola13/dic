cataloging:/ˈkætəlɔːɡɪŋ/ | v. |编目，分类|The act of creating or maintaining a catalog, typically of books or other library materials.  It involves describing items, assigning subject headings, and creating access points to facilitate retrieval. |例句：The librarian is diligently cataloging the new acquisitions. 图书管理员正在认真地为新书编目。|例句：Cataloging the museum's vast collection will take years.  对博物馆的巨量藏品进行编目将需要数年时间。|近义词：classifying, indexing, listing |反义词：disorganizing, scattering


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They catalog the books every week. = 他们每周都为书籍编目。
The museum catalogs its artifacts regularly. = 博物馆定期为其文物编目。

一般过去时:
