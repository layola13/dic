self-dissatisfied:/ˌsɛlf ˌdɪsˈsætɪsfaɪd/ | adj. | 自我厌恶的，不满意的 |  Feeling dissatisfaction with oneself; lacking self-esteem or self-acceptance.  This adjective describes someone who is critical and unhappy with their own abilities, achievements, or character. | 例句：He felt self-dissatisfied after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后感到自我厌恶。  She's become increasingly self-dissatisfied with her appearance. 她对自己的外表越来越不满意。 | 近义词：discontented, unhappy with oneself, self-critical, dissatisfied, disheartened | 反义词：self-satisfied, content, pleased with oneself, self-accepting | 词性：形容词
