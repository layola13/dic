词源:
From epicure + -ly.  Epicure derives from Epicurus, a Greek philosopher who advocated for pleasure as the highest good, but in a refined and thoughtful way, not mere hedonism.  Therefore, "epicurely" implies a sophisticated and discerning enjoyment of pleasure, particularly of food and drink.
