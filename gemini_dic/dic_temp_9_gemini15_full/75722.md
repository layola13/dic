full-sized:/ˌfʊlˈsaɪzd/| adj.| 全尺寸的，标准尺寸的 |  Describes something that is of the usual or standard size, not smaller than usual.  It implies a complete or whole size, as opposed to a reduced or miniature version.|例句：He bought a full-sized car, not a compact one. 他买了一辆全尺寸的汽车，而不是小型车。|例句：The model is a full-sized replica of the original. 这个模型是原件的全尺寸复制品。|近义词：standard-sized, regular-sized, normal-sized|反义词：miniature, small, compact
