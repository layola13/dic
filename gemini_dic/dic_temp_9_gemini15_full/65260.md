eucairite:/juːˈkeɪraɪt/| n. |硒铜银矿|A rare silver copper selenide mineral,  usually found in massive or granular form, often with a metallic luster.  Its chemical formula is CuAgSe. It is named after the Greek words "eu" (good) and "kairos" (opportune), referring to its association with other copper and silver minerals.|The eucairite sample displayed a distinct metallic sheen.|近义词：selenide mineral|反义词：无
