overgrieve:/ˌoʊvərˈɡriːv/| v.|过度悲伤，悲痛过度|To grieve excessively or for too long.  It implies a grief that is unhealthy or interferes with normal functioning.|例句：She overgrieves the loss of her pet, hindering her ability to move on. 她过度悲伤宠物的离去，阻碍了她继续前进的能力。

例句：He overgrieved his father's death for years, neglecting his health and relationships. 他多年来过度悲伤父亲的去世，忽视了他的健康和人际关系。|近义词：excessively mourn,  lament excessively|反义词：accept, reconcile
