relimiting: /ˌriːˈlɪmɪtɪŋ/ | v. |重新限制，再限定 |  The act of setting limits again or differently; redefining boundaries.  It implies a previous limitation that is being modified or replaced. | 例句：The company is relimiting its product line to focus on core competencies. 公司正在重新限制其产品线，以专注于核心竞争力。

例句：The government is relimiting the amount of water each household can use due to the drought. 由于干旱，政府正在重新限制每个家庭可以使用的水量。


近义词：restraining, restricting, confining, delimiting | 反义词：expanding, enlarging, broadening | 词性：动词
