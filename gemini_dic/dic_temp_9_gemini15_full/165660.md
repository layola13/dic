premodifies:/ˌpriːˈmɒdɪfaɪz/ | v. | 前置修饰 | To modify (something) by placing it before another element.  This typically refers to the grammatical function of placing an adjective, adverb, or noun phrase before the noun it modifies.  | Example:  The *adjective* "red" premodifies the *noun* "car" in the phrase "red car". | 近义词: modifies, qualifies, describes | 反义词: postmodifies
