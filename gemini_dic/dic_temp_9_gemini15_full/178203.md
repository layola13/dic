recommencing:/ˌriːkəˈmɛnsɪŋ/| v. |重新开始，再开始 |Beginning again; resuming.  |例句：The meeting recommenced after a short break. 会议短暂休息后重新开始。|例句：Work on the project is recommencing next week.  该项目的工程将于下周重新开始。|近义词：resuming, restarting, beginning again |反义词：stopping, ceasing, ending


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are recommencing the negotiations. = 他们正在重新开始谈判。
He recommences his studies after a long break. = 他在长时间休息后重新开始学习。

一般过去时:
The game recommenced after a rain delay. = 雨停后比赛重新开始了。
She recommenced her piano lessons after a year's absence. = 她中断一年后重新开始上钢琴课。

现在完成时:
They have recommenced their search for the missing child. = 他们已经重新开始寻找失踪的孩子。
She has recommenced her exercise routine after recovering from her injury. = 她从伤病中恢复后重新开始锻炼。

过去完成时:
He had recommenced his writing before the deadline approached. = 截止日期临近之前，他已经重新开始写作了。
They had recommenced the construction project after resolving the dispute. = 在解决争端之后，他们重新开始了建筑项目。

将来时:
We will recommence the journey tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上将重新开始旅程。
The company will recommence operations next month. = 公司下个月将重新开始运营。

虚拟语气:
If he had a chance, he would recommence his career in music. = 如果有机会，他会重新开始他的音乐事业。
I wish I could recommence my travels around the world. = 我希望我可以重新开始环游世界。

被动语态:
The meeting was recommenced after a brief interruption. = 会议在短暂中断后重新开始。
The construction project was recommenced once funding was secured. = 一旦资金到位，建筑项目就重新开始了。

疑问句:
When will they recommence the work? = 他们什么时候重新开始工作？
Will the negotiations recommence soon? = 谈判会很快重新开始吗？

否定句:
They did not recommence the project due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，他们没有重新开始项目。
He has not recommenced his writing since his accident. = 自从他出事以来，他就没有重新开始写作。
