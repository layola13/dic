phonographist:/foʊˈnɑːɡrəfɪst/ | n. | 声学家，录音师 | A phonographist is a person skilled in or specializing in phonography, the process of recording sound.  They might be involved in making recordings, transcribing recordings, or studying the science of sound recording. |  He was a skilled phonographist, known for his precise recordings of rare dialects. 他是一位技艺高超的录音师，以其对稀有方言的精确录音而闻名。 The phonographist meticulously transcribed the interview. 录音师一丝不苟地转录了采访内容。 |  sound engineer, recording engineer, transcriber |  (no direct antonym)
