sepiolite:/ˈsiːpiəlaɪt/| n. |海泡石|A hydrous magnesium silicate mineral, usually white or light-colored, that is soft, light, and porous, often found in masses and used for pipes, filters, and other purposes.  It is also known as meerschaum.|例句：Sepiolite is a lightweight mineral often used to carve pipes. 海泡石是一种轻质矿物，常被用来雕刻烟斗。|例句：The porous nature of sepiolite makes it ideal for use as a filter. 海泡石的多孔性使其成为理想的过滤材料。|近义词：meerschaum|反义词：无
