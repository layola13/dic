rehire:/ˌriːˈhaɪər/| vt.|重新雇用，再次聘用|To hire someone again after they have left a job.  It implies a previous employment relationship.|例句：The company decided to rehire its former CEO after a period of poor performance. 公司决定在业绩低迷一段时间后重新聘用其前任首席执行官。
例句：After a year's break, she was rehired by the same firm.  一年休假后，她被同一家公司重新雇用。|近义词：re-employ, reinstate|反义词：fire, dismiss, lay off|动词
