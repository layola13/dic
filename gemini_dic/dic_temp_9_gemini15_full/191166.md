scaredy-cat: /ˈskɛrdiˌkæt/ | n. |胆小鬼，懦夫 | An informal term for someone who is easily frightened. It's a compound word combining "scaredy" (a variant of "scared") and "cat,"  with the "cat" likely chosen for its association with timidity.  |Example: He's such a scaredy-cat; he won't even go near a spider. 他真是个胆小鬼，连蜘蛛都不敢靠近。|近义词：coward, chicken, wimp |反义词：brave, courageous, bold
