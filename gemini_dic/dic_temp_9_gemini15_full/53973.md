discrepancies:/dɪˈskrepənsiz/| n.|差异；不符；矛盾|Differences or inconsistencies between facts, figures, or statements.|例句：There are some discrepancies between the two accounts of the incident.  两个关于事件的描述之间存在一些差异。|例句：The police investigated the discrepancies in the witness testimonies. 警方调查了证词中的出入。|近义词：inconsistencies, differences, disparities, variations|反义词：agreement, consistency, conformity
