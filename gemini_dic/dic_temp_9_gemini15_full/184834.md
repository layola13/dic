resweetening: /ˌriːˈswiːtənɪŋ/ | n. | 再加糖；再次使变甜；重新调味 | The act of making something sweet again, or adding sweetness to something that has lost its sweetness.  This can refer to literal sweetness (like adding sugar to a dish) or figurative sweetness (like rekindling a relationship). |例句：The baker did a resweetening of the cake after tasting it and finding it too bland. 面包师在尝过蛋糕后发现太淡而进行了再加糖处理。


The resweetening of the negotiations improved the chances of a successful agreement.  谈判的再次缓和提高了达成协议的几率。|近义词：sweetening, resugaring, re-sweetening |反义词：unsweetening, desweetening

词源:
