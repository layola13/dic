hollering:/ˈhɑːlərɪŋ/ | v. | 喊叫；大声叫嚷 |  To shout or yell loudly and usually excitedly or angrily.  | 例句：The children were hollering with delight at the playground. 孩子们在操场上高兴地大声喊叫。

例句：He was hollering at the top of his lungs. 他正在扯着嗓子喊叫。


近义词：yelling, shouting, screaming, bawling | 反义词：whispering, murmuring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hollers at the TV during every game. 他在每场比赛中都对着电视大声喊叫。
They holler instructions to the workers. 他们大声向工人喊叫指示。


一般过去时:
She hollered for help when she fell. 她摔倒时大声呼救。
The crowd hollered their approval. 人群大声表示赞同。


现在完成时:
He has hollered at me several times today. 他今天已经对我大声喊叫好几次了。
The team has hollered its victory. 球队已经大声欢呼胜利。


过去完成时:
They had hollered warnings before the storm hit. 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经大声发出警告。
She had hollered until she was hoarse. 她一直大声喊叫直到声音嘶哑。


将来时:
I will holler if I see anything suspicious. 如果我看到任何可疑的东西，我会大声喊叫。
They will holler their support for the candidate. 他们将大声表达对候选人的支持。


虚拟语气:
If I saw a bear, I would holler for help. 如果我看到熊，我会大声呼救。
I wish I hadn't hollered so loudly. 我真希望我没有大声喊叫。


被动语态:
His name was hollered across the field. 他的名字在田野里被大声喊叫。
Warnings were hollered from the rooftop. 警告从屋顶上传来。


疑问句:
Were they hollering because they were scared? 他们是因为害怕而大声喊叫吗？
Is she hollering for attention? 她是在大声喊叫以引起注意吗？


否定句:
He didn't holler when he saw the snake. 他看到蛇时没有大声喊叫。
They didn't holler at the referee. 他们没有对着裁判大声喊叫。
