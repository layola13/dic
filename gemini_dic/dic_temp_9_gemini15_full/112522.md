lovabilities:/ˌlʌvəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |可爱之处，讨人喜欢之处 | The qualities that make someone or something lovable;  charming qualities.  |例句：The puppy's lovabilities were undeniable; its big eyes and playful nature charmed everyone. 这只小狗的可爱之处是不可否认的；它的大眼睛和顽皮的天性迷住了所有人。


例句：Her lovabilities extended beyond her beauty; she possessed a kind heart and a generous spirit. 她的可爱之处不仅仅在于她的美貌；她拥有一颗善良的心和慷慨的精神。


近义词：charms, endearing qualities, appealing traits, attractiveness


反义词：repulsiveness, unattractiveness, unpleasantness


词性：名词
