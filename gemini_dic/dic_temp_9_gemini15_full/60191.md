efficiency:/ɪˈfɪʃənsi/| n. |效率；功效；效能 |The ability to avoid wasting materials, energy, efforts, money, and time in doing something or in producing a desired result.  It often implies a high level of productivity and effectiveness. |例句：The efficiency of the new system is remarkable. 新系统的效率非常显著。

例句：Improving energy efficiency is crucial for reducing our carbon footprint. 提高能源效率对于减少我们的碳足迹至关重要。


近义词：effectiveness, productivity, productiveness, output, performance

反义词：inefficiency, ineffectiveness, wastefulness
