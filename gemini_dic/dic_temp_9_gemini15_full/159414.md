polarizable: /pəˈlɛərəzəbəl/ | adj. | 可极化的，可偏振的 |  Capable of being polarized; easily influenced or swayed.  This refers to the ability of a material to acquire a dipole moment in response to an applied electric field.  In a broader sense, it can also describe something easily influenced or swayed. |


例句：

Certain dielectrics are highly polarizable.  某些电介质是高度可极化的。

The crowd was easily polarizable by the charismatic speaker.  人群很容易被这位有魅力的演讲者所影响。


近义词：susceptible, impressionable, influencible

反义词：inert, resistant, impervious
