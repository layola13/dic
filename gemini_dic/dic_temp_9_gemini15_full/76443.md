gaining:/ˈɡeɪnɪŋ/| v.|获得，增加，增长|The act of acquiring or achieving something desirable.  It implies a gradual increase or accumulation.|例句：He is gaining weight. 他正在增重。
例句：The company is gaining market share.  这家公司正在获得市场份额。|近义词：acquiring, achieving, obtaining, increasing|反义词：losing, decreasing, diminishing|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She is gaining confidence. = 她正在获得自信。
He is gaining experience in his new job. = 他在他新的工作中积累经验。

一般过去时:
They gained a lot of money from the investment. = 他们从投资中赚了很多钱。
The team gained a victory in the final match. = 球队在决赛中取得了胜利。

现在完成时:
We have gained valuable insights from the research. = 我们从研究中获得了宝贵的见解。
She has gained a reputation as a skilled artist. = 她赢得了作为一名技巧娴熟的艺术家的声誉。

过去完成时:
By the time he retired, he had gained considerable wealth. = 等他退休时，他已经积累了相当的财富。
They had gained control of the situation before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，他们已经控制住了局面。

将来时:
We will gain a competitive advantage with this new technology. = 通过这项新技术，我们将获得竞争优势。
The company expects to gain significant profits next year. = 公司预计明年将获得可观的利润。

虚拟语气:
If she studied harder, she would gain better grades. = 如果她学习更努力，她就能取得更好的成绩。
I wish I had gained more experience before starting my own business. = 我希望在我开始自己的事业之前积累更多经验。

被动语态:
The knowledge was gained through years of study. = 这些知识是通过多年的学习获得的。
Significant progress has been gained in the fight against poverty. = 在反贫困斗争中取得了重大进展。

疑问句:
What are you gaining from this relationship? = 你从这段关系中得到了什么？
How much weight have you gained recently? = 你最近增重多少了？

否定句:
He didn't gain anything from the argument. = 他从争论中一无所获。
The company failed to gain the support of the investors. = 公司未能获得投资者的支持。


PHRASE->
gain access to = 获得进入...的权利
gain ground = 逐渐取得进展
gain momentum = 发展势头
gain insight into = 获得对…的了解
gain an advantage = 获得优势
