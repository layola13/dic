faintly:/ˈfeɪntli/ | adv. | 微弱地；模糊地；几乎察觉不到地 | faintly suggests a weak or indistinct quality. It can describe sounds, smells, feelings, or appearances.  |例句：The music played faintly in the distance.  远处传来微弱的音乐声。

The scent of roses faintly perfumed the air. 玫瑰花的香味轻微地弥漫在空气中。


近义词：slightly, dimly, vaguely, weakly | 反义词：strongly, clearly, distinctly | 词性：副词
