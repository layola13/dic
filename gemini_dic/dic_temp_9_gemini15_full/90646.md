humanizing: /ˈhjuːmənʌɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使人性化；赋予人性 | Making something more human or humane;  giving something human qualities or characteristics.  | 例句：The company is trying to humanize its brand by showing the people behind the products. 公司正试图通过展示产品背后的员工来使品牌更人性化。

例句：The new policies aim to humanize the prison system.  新的政策旨在使监狱制度人性化。


近义词：personalizing, personalising,  anthropomorphizing, humanizing,  making humane


反义词：dehumanizing, dehumanising, brutalizing, mechanizing


词性：动词
