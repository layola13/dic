parure:/pəˈrʊər/| n.|首饰套件，成套首饰|A parure is a set of matching jewelry, typically including a necklace, earrings, and a bracelet or brooch.  It is often made of precious metals and gemstones and is designed to be worn together as a coordinated ensemble. |The queen wore a magnificent parure of diamonds and pearls. 女王戴着一套华丽的钻石和珍珠首饰。| jewelry set, ensemble |  (无明显反义词)| 名词
