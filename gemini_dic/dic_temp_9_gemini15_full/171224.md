psychotherapist:/ˌsaɪkoʊˈθɛrəpɪst/| n. |心理治疗师|A mental health professional who treats mental illness and emotional distress through talk therapy and other techniques.|例句：My psychotherapist helped me understand the root of my anxiety. 我的心理治疗师帮助我理解了焦虑的根源。
例句：She sought a psychotherapist to help her cope with her grief. 她寻求一位心理治疗师来帮助她应对悲伤。|近义词：therapist, counselor, psychologist|反义词：N/A
