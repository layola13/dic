mazy:/ˈmeɪzi/| adj. | 迷宫似的；复杂的；混乱的 |  Characterized by complexity or intricacy; labyrinthine.  It refers to something that is difficult to navigate or understand because of its complexity. | 例句：The garden path was mazy and confusing. 花园小路弯弯曲曲，令人困惑。

The instructions were so mazy that I couldn't follow them.  说明太复杂，我无法理解。

| 近义词：complicated, intricate, labyrinthine, perplexing | 反义词：simple, straightforward, clear, uncomplicated |  形容词
