portraitists:/ˈpɔːtrəɪtɪsts/| n. |肖像画家|A portraitist is an artist who specializes in painting or drawing portraits.  They create likenesses of individuals, often emphasizing their personality and character.  |Example:  Many famous portraitists throughout history have captured the essence of their subjects. 许多历史上著名的肖像画家都捕捉到了他们作品人物的精髓。|近义词: portrait painter, likeness artist |反义词: (no direct antonym, but perhaps) abstract artist
