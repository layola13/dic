/ˈbʌsti/| adj.| 繁茂的；生气勃勃的；充满活力的 | Bustling with activity and energy; full of life and vigor. | The bustling city was full of energy.  这个繁华的城市充满了活力。| The market was a busy, bustling place. 市场是一个繁忙喧闹的地方。| quiet, calm | sluggish, inactive


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The city is always bustling with activity. = 这个城市总是充满活力。
The market is bustling with shoppers. = 市场挤满了购物者。


一般过去时:
The streets bustled with people last night. = 昨晚街道上人声鼎沸。
The town bustled with excitement before the festival. = 节日前夕，小镇充满了兴奋。


现在完成时:
The town has bustled with activity since the new shops opened. = 自从新店开张以来，小镇就一直很热闹。
The area has bustled with life for centuries. = 这个地区几个世纪以来一直生机勃勃。


过去完成时:
The city had bustled with life before the war. = 战争爆发前，这座城市生机勃勃。
The village had bustled with preparations for the wedding. = 村庄为婚礼做好了准备，一片忙碌景象。


将来时:
The square will bustle with people during the festival. = 节日期间广场上将人山人海。
The streets will bustle with activity tomorrow. = 街道明天将会非常热闹。


虚拟语气:
If the town bustled more, it would attract more tourists. = 如果小镇更热闹，它就能吸引更多游客。
I wish this quiet village bustled with life. = 我希望这个安静的村庄充满生机。


被动语态:
The area is not usually bustled with tourists. = 这个地区通常游客不多。
The city was bustled with energy during the Olympics. = 奥运期间，这座城市充满了活力。（虽然"bustled"本身不常用被动语态，但此句表达了被动意义）


疑问句:
Does the market usually bustle with shoppers on Saturdays? = 周六市场通常人很多吗？
Does the street bustle with traffic during rush hour? = 高峰期街道上交通繁忙吗？


否定句:
The town wasn't bustling at all last night. = 昨晚小镇一点也不热闹。
The park didn't bustle with people today. = 今天公园里人不多。
