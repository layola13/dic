presteels: /ˈpriːstiːlz/ | n. | 预制钢材 |Prestressed steel is steel that has been subjected to a controlled tensile stress before being used in a structure. This prestressing increases the strength and durability of the steel and allows for the creation of longer spans and more slender structures.  |Example: The bridge was constructed using high-strength presteels to withstand heavy loads.  这座桥是用高强度预应力钢材建造的，以承受重载。|近义词：prestressed steel, prestressed reinforcement|反义词：无|名词
