prattled:/ˈprætld/| v.|喋喋不休地谈论；胡说八道 | to talk continuously and rapidly, especially about unimportant things; to chatter |The children prattled on about their day at school. 孩子们喋喋不休地谈论他们在学校的一天。|近义词：babbled, chattered, gabbled |反义词：remained silent, kept quiet


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prattles incessantly about his achievements. 他喋喋不休地谈论他的成就。
She prattles away happily to her dolls. 她高兴地对她洋娃娃喋喋不休。


一般过去时:
The old woman prattled on about her childhood memories. 老妇人喋喋不休地谈论起她童年的回忆。
He prattled nonsense for hours. 他胡说八道了几个小时。


现在完成时:
She has prattled away all afternoon. 她整个下午都在喋喋不休。
They have prattled enough about the matter. 他们已经对这件事喋喋不休够了。


过去完成时:
He had prattled for so long that everyone was exhausted. 他喋喋不休地说得太久了，以至于每个人都筋疲力尽了。
She had prattled away all morning before realizing she was talking to a wall. 她唠叨了整个上午才意识到自己在对着一堵墙说话。


将来时:
The children will prattle excitedly about their trip to the zoo. 孩子们将会兴奋地喋喋不休地谈论他们去动物园的旅行。
He will prattle on regardless of whether anyone is listening. 不管有没有人在听，他都会喋喋不休地说下去。


虚拟语气:
If he prattled less, people would listen more. 如果他少说一些，人们就会多听一些。
I wish he wouldn't prattle so much. 我希望他不那么喋喋不休。


被动语态:
The story was prattled all over town. 这个故事在城里被到处传播。 (This example is less common, as "prattle" is usually used actively)


疑问句:
Does he always prattle like that? 他总是那样喋喋不休吗？
Why does she prattle so much? 她为什么说那么多废话？


否定句:
He didn't prattle about his problems. 他没有喋喋不休地谈论他的问题。
She didn't prattle to anyone about the incident. 她没有对任何人喋喋不休地谈论那件事。


PHRASE->
prattle on: 喋喋不休地谈论
prattle away: 喋喋不休地说
prattle nonsense: 胡说八道
prattle about: 喋喋不休地谈论…
prattle to: 对…喋喋不休
