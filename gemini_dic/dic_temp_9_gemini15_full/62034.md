enbibe:/ɪmˈbaɪb/| vt.|吸收；吸取；灌输|to absorb or soak up (liquid) or (ideas, information etc) |例句：The dry earth imbibed the rain. 干燥的土地吸收了雨水。|例句：He imbibed the wisdom of his elders. 他吸取了长者的智慧。|近义词：absorb, soak up, assimilate, imbue|反义词：expel, reject, exclude|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plant imbibes water through its roots. = 植物通过根部吸收水分。
She imbibes knowledge from various sources. = 她从各种来源吸取知识。

一般过去时:
The sponge imbibed the spilled milk. = 海绵吸收了洒出的牛奶。
He imbibed the principles of non-violence from Gandhi. = 他从甘地那里吸取了非暴力的原则。

现在完成时:
The soil has imbibed a great deal of moisture. = 土壤已经吸收了大量的水分。
She has imbibed a new philosophy of life. = 她已经吸收了一种新的生活哲学。

过去完成时:
The old wooden chair had imbibed the scent of generations. = 这把旧木椅已经吸收了几代人的气味。
They had imbibed the culture of the region during their travels. = 他们在旅行期间已经吸收了当地的文化。

将来时:
The thirsty land will imbibe the coming rain. = 干渴的土地将吸收即将到来的雨水。
He will imbibe the teachings of the master. = 他将吸收大师的教诲。

虚拟语气:
If the fabric imbibed the dye properly, the color would be more vibrant. = 如果织物能充分吸收染料，颜色就会更鲜艳。
I wish my mind could imbibe information as easily as a sponge. = 我希望我的头脑能像海绵一样容易吸收信息。

被动语态:
The information was imbibed quickly by the students. = 学生们迅速吸收了这些信息。
The liquid was imbibed by the porous material. = 多孔材料吸收了液体。

疑问句:
Does the paper imbibe ink easily? = 这张纸容易吸收墨水吗？
Does he readily imbibe new ideas? = 他容易接受新思想吗？

否定句:
The material did not imbibe the dye effectively. = 材料没有有效地吸收染料。
He refused to imbibe the propaganda. = 他拒绝接受宣传。
