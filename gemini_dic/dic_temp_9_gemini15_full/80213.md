godown:/ˈɡəʊdaʊn/ | n. |货仓；仓库；厂房 |A godown is a warehouse, especially in Asia.  It's often used to store goods, especially agricultural products. |The rice was stored in a large godown. 这批稻米储存在一个大型货仓里。  |warehouse, storehouse, depot |open field, shop (in the sense of a retail store) |名词
