preconsigned:/ˌpriːkənˈsaɪnd/| v.|预先托运，预先寄送|To send or deliver something beforehand; to dispatch in advance.|The goods were preconsigned to the warehouse before the shipment arrived. 货物在货船到达之前就预先运送到仓库了。|近义词：forward, dispatch, ship ahead of time|反义词：delay, postpone
