cliques:/ˈkliːk/| n.|小集团，派系|A small, exclusive group of people; a group of people who exclude others.|例句：There are several cliques in our school.  我们学校有好几个派系。|例句：She tried to avoid the school cliques. 她试图避免学校里的那些小团体。|近义词：group, gang, faction, set|反义词：everyone, the whole group
