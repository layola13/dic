/ˈɪndɪks/ | n. | 指标；索引；指数 | An index is a system for finding information in a book or database.  It usually lists keywords or topics with their page numbers or locations.  | Example: The book has a detailed index at the back. 这本书后面有一个详细的索引。  |近义词：indicator, guide, register | 反义词：None specifically | 名词
