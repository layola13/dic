Pliantnesses is the plural form of "pliantness," which refers to the quality or state of being pliant.  Pliant means easily bent or influenced; flexible; yielding. Therefore, pliantnesses describes multiple instances or aspects of this flexibility, adaptability, or yielding nature.  It can refer to the physical flexibility of a material, or the mental flexibility and adaptability of a person.
