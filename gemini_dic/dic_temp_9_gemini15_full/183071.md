repolling:/ˌriːˈpoʊlɪŋ/ | n. |重新投票，再次投票 |The act of voting again; a second or subsequent vote on the same issue.  It often happens after an initial vote is deemed invalid or inconclusive. |例句：The repolling in the contested constituency was delayed due to logistical issues.  = 有争议选区的重新投票因后勤问题而被推迟。

例句：The judge ordered a repolling after discovering irregularities in the initial election. =  法官在发现初选舞弊后下令重新投票。


近义词：revoting, re-election, second vote |反义词：final vote, single vote |词性：名词
