exclaiming: /ɪkˈskleɪmɪŋ/ | v. | 惊呼；大声喊叫 |  The act of uttering a sharp, sudden cry; expressing strong emotion.  | 例句：She was exclaiming in delight at the unexpected gift. = 她因收到意外的礼物而高兴地惊呼。

例句：The crowd was exclaiming with excitement as the winning team scored. = 当获胜的球队得分时，人群兴奋地欢呼。


近义词：shouting, crying out, yelling, vociferating | 反义词：whispering, murmuring


词性：动词 (verb)
