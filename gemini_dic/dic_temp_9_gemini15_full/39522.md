conquered:/ˈkɒŋkəd/ /ˈkɑːŋkərd/| vt.|征服；战胜；克服|To gain or achieve (something) by overcoming difficulties or opposition. To defeat (a people or army) and take control of their territory.|例句：The Romans conquered Gaul. 罗马人征服了高卢。|例句：She conquered her fear of public speaking. 她克服了对公开演讲的恐惧。|近义词：subdued, overcame, vanquished|反义词：surrendered, yielded, succumbed

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army conquers new lands. = 军队征服了新的土地。
He conquers his fears every day. = 他每天都在战胜恐惧。


一般过去时:
Alexander the Great conquered Persia. = 亚历山大大帝征服了波斯。
She conquered her stage fright. = 她克服了怯场。


现在完成时:
They have conquered many challenges. = 他们已经克服了许多挑战。
The team has conquered its rivals. = 该队战胜了对手。


过去完成时:
The empire had conquered many territories before its decline. = 在衰落之前，帝国已经征服了许多领土。
She had conquered her shyness by the time she gave the speech. = 她演讲时已经克服了害羞。


将来时:
We will conquer this mountain. = 我们将征服这座山峰。
They will conquer the disease. = 他们将战胜这种疾病。


虚拟语气:
If only I could conquer my procrastination! = 要是我能克服拖延症就好了！
If they had conquered their fears, they would have succeeded. = 如果他们克服了恐惧，他们就会成功。


被动语态:
Many countries were conquered by the Roman Empire. = 许多国家都被罗马帝国征服了。
The problem has not yet been conquered. = 这个问题尚未解决。


疑问句:
Has he conquered his addiction? = 他战胜了他的瘾了吗？
Can we conquer this obstacle? = 我们能克服这个障碍吗？


否定句:
They did not conquer the city. = 他们没有征服这座城市。
The disease could not be conquered easily. = 这种疾病不容易被征服。
