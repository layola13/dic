This word is not a standard dictionary entry and appears to be a newly coined or rarely used word formed by combining "pre" and "referring".  It functions as a participle adjective.|例句：The prereferring document outlined the key issues. 预先提及的文件概述了关键问题。 The prereferring candidates were all highly qualified. 预先选择的候选人都是高素质的。|近义词：preliminary, prior, antecedent |反义词：subsequent, following
