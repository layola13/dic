dispensatory: /dɪˈspɛnsəri/ | n. | 药典；药剂书 | A book containing a description of medicinal substances, their preparation, and their use.  It's a comprehensive guide for pharmacists and healthcare professionals, detailing information about drugs and their applications. | The pharmacist consulted the dispensatory to verify the dosage of the medication. 药剂师查阅药典以核实药物的剂量。| formulary, pharmacopoeia |  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "contraindication list" as a contrasting concept) | 名词
