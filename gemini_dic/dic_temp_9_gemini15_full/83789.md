hairless:/ˈheərləs/| adj. |无毛的，秃的|Without hair.  The term applies to animals and humans lacking hair, either naturally or due to medical conditions or treatments.|例句：The Sphynx cat is a hairless breed.  = 斯芬克斯猫是一个无毛品种。

The dog became hairless after the treatment. = 那只狗治疗后变得无毛了。|近义词：bald (for humans), glabrous |反义词：hairy, hirsute


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is hairless. = 他是无毛的。
The cat appears hairless. = 这只猫看起来无毛。

一般过去时:
The dog was hairless after the illness. = 这只狗生病后就无毛了。
She became hairless due to chemotherapy. = 她由于化疗而变得无毛。

现在完成时:
The cat has been hairless since birth. = 这只猫从出生起就无毛。
He has remained hairless despite treatment. = 尽管接受了治疗，他仍然无毛。

过去完成时:
The dog had been hairless for months. = 那只狗已经无毛好几个月了。
They had never seen a hairless cat before. = 他们以前从未见过无毛猫。

将来时:
The treatment will make him hairless. = 这种治疗会使他变得无毛。
She will likely be hairless for several months. = 她很可能在接下来的几个月里都会无毛。

虚拟语气:
If he were hairless, it would be easier to manage his skin condition. = 如果他是无毛的，那么管理他的皮肤状况就会更容易。
I wish my cat weren't hairless; I miss petting his fur. = 我希望我的猫不是无毛的；我想念抚摸它的毛。


被动语态:
The area was made hairless by the laser treatment. = 激光治疗使该区域变得无毛。
His skin was kept hairless using special creams. = 使用特殊的乳膏使他的皮肤保持无毛。

疑问句:
Is the dog hairless? = 这只狗是无毛的吗？
Are all Sphynx cats hairless? = 所有斯芬克斯猫都是无毛的吗？

否定句:
The kitten is not hairless; it has very fine hair. = 小猫不是无毛的；它有非常细的毛发。
The treatment did not make him completely hairless. = 这种治疗并没有使他完全无毛。
