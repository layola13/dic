isotopes:/ˈaɪsətoʊps/ | n. |同位素 | Atoms of the same element that have the same number of protons but a different number of neutrons.  They have the same atomic number but different mass numbers. | Example: Carbon-12 and carbon-14 are isotopes of carbon.  碳12和碳14是碳的同位素。|近义词: nuclides |反义词:  None  |名词
