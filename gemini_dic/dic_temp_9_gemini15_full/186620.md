riffing:/ˈrɪfɪŋ/| v.|即兴演奏，随意发挥，重复某事物|To play a musical riff repeatedly or to improvise on a musical theme.  It can also mean to talk or write about something repeatedly or at length, often in a casual or informal way. |例句：The guitarist was riffing on a blues scale. 吉他手正在即兴演奏布鲁斯音阶。

The comedian spent the whole set riffing on current events.  这位喜剧演员整场表演都在随意谈论时事。|近义词：improvising, jamming, elaborating|反义词：rehearsing, planning, scripting

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is riffing on a catchy tune. = 他正在即兴演奏一首朗朗上口的曲子。
They are riffing on the same joke all night. = 他们整晚都在重复同一个笑话。
