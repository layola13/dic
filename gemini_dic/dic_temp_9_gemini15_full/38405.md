concern:/kənˈsɜːrn/ |/kənˈsɝːn/| n., v.| 关心；担忧；涉及；关系到 |

英文解析:As a noun, "concern" refers to a feeling of worry or anxiety. As a verb, it means to worry, to be relevant to, or to involve.

例句:
(Noun)  His biggest concern is his son's future. = 他最大的担忧是他儿子的未来。
(Noun) The environmental concerns are increasing. = 环境问题日益增多。

(Verb) The issue concerns everyone in the community. = 这个问题关系到社区的每个人。
(Verb)  Don't let this problem concern you too much. = 不要让这个问题太让你担心。


近义词: (Noun) worry, anxiety, apprehension, trouble; (Verb) bother, trouble, affect, involve

反义词: (Noun) indifference, unconcern; (Verb) disregard, ignore


词性:Noun, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My health concerns me. = 我的健康让我担心。
This matter concerns the whole team. = 这件事关系到整个团队。


一般过去时:
The news concerned him deeply. = 这个消息让他深感担忧。
The meeting concerned the budget. = 会议讨论的是预算。


现在完成时:
This issue has concerned me for weeks. = 这件事已经让我担忧了好几周了。
Nothing has concerned him more than his family. = 没有什么比他的家人更让他担忧了。


过去完成时:
I had concerned myself with the problem for days before finding a solution. = 在找到解决方法之前，我已经为这个问题担忧了好几天了。
They had concerned themselves with the project's progress. = 他们之前一直关注着项目的进展。


将来时:
This will concern the board of directors. = 这将关系到董事会。
I know this will concern you. = 我知道这会让你担心。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would concern myself more with your health. = 如果我是你，我会更关心你的健康。
I wish this wouldn't concern me so much. = 我希望这件事不会让我这么担心。


被动语态:
He was concerned about the situation. = 他担心这种情况。

疑问句:

What concerns you most about this project? = 关于这个项目，你最担心什么？


否定句:

He wasn't concerned about the outcome. = 他并不担心结果。
