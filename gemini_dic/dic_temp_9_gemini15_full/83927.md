half-castes:/ˈhɑːfˌkæsts/| n.|混血儿，半血统的人|A derogatory and outdated term referring to people of mixed racial heritage, typically a person with one white and one non-white parent.|The term "half-caste" is considered offensive and should be avoided.  It reflects a colonialist worldview and perpetuates harmful stereotypes.|近义词：mixed-race person, biracial person|反义词:purebred
