semiconscious:/ˌsɛmiˈkɒnʃəs/| adj.|半清醒的，半意识的|Partially conscious; in a state between full wakefulness and sleep.  It implies a reduced level of awareness and responsiveness. |例句：After the accident, he was semiconscious and unable to answer questions. 事故后，他处于半清醒状态，无法回答问题。
例句：She drifted in and out of semiconsciousness throughout the night.  她整夜都在半睡半醒之间徘徊。

近义词：unconscious, drowsy, lethargic, half-awake
反义词：conscious, fully awake, alert
