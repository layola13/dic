oarsman:/ˈɔːrzmən/| n. |划手，桨手 |A person who rows a boat, especially one who is skilled in rowing.  |例句：He was a skilled oarsman, winning many regattas. 他是一位技艺精湛的划手，赢得了许多划船比赛。

例句：The oarsman pulled hard on the oars, driving the boat forward. 桨手用力划桨，推动船前进。


近义词：rower, sculler


反义词：passenger (in the context of a boat)


词性: 名词
