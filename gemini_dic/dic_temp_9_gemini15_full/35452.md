co-work:/ˈkoʊwɜːrk/| v. |合作工作；协同工作|To work together on a project or task.  It implies a shared effort and often a collaborative environment.|例句：We co-worked on the presentation for the client. 我们一起合作完成了客户的演示文稿。
例句：The team co-worked effectively to meet the deadline. 团队有效地合作完成了最后期限。|近义词：collaborate, cooperate, team up|反义词：work independently, work alone|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They co-work on many projects. = 他们一起合作很多项目。
We co-work remotely using online tools. = 我们使用线上工具远程合作。

一般过去时:
We co-worked on that report last week. = 我们上周一起合作完成了那份报告。
They co-worked diligently to finish the task. = 他们努力合作完成了任务。

现在完成时:
We have co-worked on several successful campaigns. = 我们已经合作完成了好几个成功的活动。
They have co-worked with this client for years. = 他们与这个客户合作多年了。

过去完成时:
We had co-worked on many projects before this one. = 在这个项目之前，我们已经一起合作过很多项目了。
They had co-worked together for five years before the company merged. = 公司合并之前，他们已经一起合作了五年。

将来时:
We will co-work on the new marketing strategy next month. = 我们下个月将合作制定新的营销策略。
They will co-work remotely from different locations. = 他们将从不同的地点远程合作。

虚拟语气:
If we co-worked more effectively, we would finish earlier. = 如果我们合作更有效率，就能早点完成。
I wish we had co-worked on the design from the beginning. = 我希望我们从一开始就合作设计。

被动语态:
The project was co-worked on by several teams. = 这个项目是由几个团队合作完成的。
The new software is being co-worked on by developers worldwide. = 这款新软件正在由全球各地的开发人员合作开发。

疑问句:
Do you co-work with anyone on this project? = 你在这个项目中与其他人合作吗？
Will they co-work on the next phase of the project? = 他们会在项目的下一阶段合作吗？

否定句:
We did not co-work on the initial proposal. = 我们没有参与初始提案的合作。
They don't co-work well together. = 他们不太擅长一起合作。
