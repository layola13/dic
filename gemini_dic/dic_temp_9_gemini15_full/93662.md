imbue:/ɪmˈbjuː/ | vt. | 灌输；使充满；染上 | To fill or inspire someone or something with a particular feeling, quality, or idea. | 例句：His poems are imbued with a sense of loss. 他的诗歌充满了失落感。 | 例句：The artist imbued the painting with vibrant colors. 艺术家给这幅画注入了鲜艳的色彩。 | 近义词：infuse, inspire, permeate | 反义词：deprive, drain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher imbues her students with a love of learning. = 老师给她的学生灌输了对学习的热爱。
This fabric imbues a soft, silky feeling. = 这块布料给人一种柔软丝滑的感觉。

一般过去时:
The experience imbued him with a newfound confidence. = 那次经历使他获得了新的自信。
The sunset imbued the sky with fiery hues. = 日落给天空染上了火红的色彩。

现在完成时:
The music has imbued the atmosphere with a sense of calm. = 音乐已经给气氛增添了一种平静的感觉。
This book has imbued me with a new perspective. = 这本书让我有了新的视角。

过去完成时:
He had imbued his speech with passion before he began. = 他在开始演讲前已经给演讲注入了激情。
The ancient stories had imbued the land with a sense of mystery. = 古代的故事已经给这片土地蒙上了一层神秘的面纱。

将来时:
The new policy will imbue the company with greater efficiency. = 新政策将使公司效率更高。
This experience will imbue her with strength and resilience. = 这段经历将使她充满力量和韧性。

虚拟语气:
If the painting were imbued with more emotion, it would be more powerful. = 如果这幅画能表达更多的情感，它就会更有力量。
I wish my writing were imbued with more clarity and grace. = 我希望我的写作能更清晰流畅。

被动语态:
The soldiers were imbued with a strong sense of patriotism. = 士兵们被灌输了强烈的爱国主义精神。
The project was imbued with a spirit of innovation. = 这个项目充满了创新精神。

疑问句:
Is the culture imbued with religious beliefs? = 这种文化是否充满了宗教信仰？
Are the colors imbued with symbolism? = 这些颜色是否具有象征意义？

否定句:
The performance was not imbued with any passion. = 这场表演没有任何激情。
The design was not imbued with any sense of elegance. = 这个设计没有任何优雅感。
