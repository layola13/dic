reenrolled:/ˌriːɪnˈroʊld/| v. |重新注册，重新入学|To enroll again; to register again for a course or program.|He reenrolled in the university after a year's break. 他在休学一年后重新注册了大学。|enroll, register, sign up|drop out, withdraw|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reenroll in the same class every semester. 他们每学期都重新选修同一门课。
She reenrolls in dance classes every year. 她每年都重新报名参加舞蹈课。


一般过去时:
He reenrolled in the program last fall. 他去年秋天重新注册了该项目。
I reenrolled in my college courses after a long break. 我在长时间休息后重新选修了大学课程。


现在完成时:
She has reenrolled in the online course. 她已经重新注册了这门在线课程。
They have reenrolled for the upcoming semester. 他们已经为即将到来的学期重新注册了。


过去完成时:
He had reenrolled before the deadline. 他在截止日期前重新注册了。
She had reenrolled in the class by the time the semester started. 学期开始时，她已经重新选修了这门课。


将来时:
I will reenroll in the yoga class next month. 我下个月将重新注册瑜伽课程。
He will reenroll in the university next year. 他明年将重新入学。
