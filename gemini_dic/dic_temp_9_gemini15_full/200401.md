skyrocketing: /ˈskaɪˌrɒkɪtɪŋ/ | /ˈskaɪˌrɑːkɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 迅速上涨的；猛增的 |  Rapidly increasing; soaring.  |例句：House prices are skyrocketing in the city center. 市中心的房价正在飙涨。|例句：The company's profits are skyrocketing thanks to the new product.  由于新产品，公司的利润正在猛增。|近义词：soaring, surging, booming, escalating | 反义词：plummeting, declining, falling, decreasing | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cost of living is skyrocketing. = 生活成本正在飙升。
Inflation is skyrocketing in many countries. = 许多国家的通货膨胀正在飙升。


一般过去时:
Oil prices skyrocketed after the war broke out. = 战争爆发后，油价暴涨。
The number of tourists skyrocketed during the summer months. = 夏季旅游人数激增。


现在完成时:
House prices have skyrocketed in recent years. = 近年来房价已大幅上涨。
The company's share price has skyrocketed since the announcement. = 自公告发布以来，该公司股价已暴涨。


过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, the demand had skyrocketed. = 我们到达时，需求已经激增。
Before the event, ticket sales had skyrocketed. = 活动开始前，门票销售已经暴涨。


将来时:
Experts predict that energy prices will skyrocket next year. = 专家预测明年能源价格将飙涨。
If the demand continues, prices will skyrocket. = 如果需求持续，价格将会飙升。


虚拟语气:
If the interest rates skyrocketed, the economy would suffer. = 如果利率飙升，经济将会受损。
I wish the cost of healthcare didn't skyrocket. = 我希望医疗费用不要暴涨。


被动语态:
The value of the currency was skyrocketed by the government's policy. = 政府的政策导致货币价值暴涨。(This is less common as "skyrocket" is usually intransitive)
The prices were driven sky high by speculation. = 投机行为导致价格飞涨。(This is a better way to express passive increase)


疑问句:
Is the price of gas skyrocketing again? = 天然气价格又在飙升了吗？
Are housing costs skyrocketing in your area? = 你所在地区的住房成本正在飙升吗？


否定句:
The unemployment rate is not skyrocketing. = 失业率并没有飙升。
Despite the crisis, sales are not skyrocketing. = 尽管发生了危机，销售额并没有飙升。

skyrocketing debt = 飙升的债务
