leather:/ˈlɛðər/| n. |皮革，皮子|The material made from the tanned hide or skin of an animal, especially a cow, used for making shoes, clothing, bags, etc.|例句：These shoes are made of genuine leather. 这双鞋是用真皮做的。

例句：The leather jacket was worn and faded. 这件皮夹克已经磨损褪色了。


近义词：hide, skin, suede|反义词：无


词性：名词
