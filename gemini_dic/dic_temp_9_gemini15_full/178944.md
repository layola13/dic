redecision:/ˌriːdɪˈsɪʒən/| n. |重新决定，再决定|The act of deciding something again; a second decision.|After careful consideration, the committee reached a redecision on the project's budget.  委员会经过仔细考虑，对项目的预算做出了重新决定。|revision, reconsideration|affirmation, confirmation|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is making a redecision about its marketing strategy. = 公司正在重新决定其营销策略。
He redecides his travel plans every week. = 他每周都重新决定他的旅行计划。


一般过去时:
The judge redecided the case after reviewing new evidence. = 在审查了新的证据后，法官重新裁决了此案。
They redecided to postpone the meeting until next week. = 他们重新决定将会议推迟到下周。


现在完成时:
She has redecided to pursue a different career path. = 她已重新决定追求不同的职业道路。
They have redecided on the location for the new store. = 他们已经重新决定了新商店的地址。


过去完成时:
He had redecided to sell his house before the market crashed. = 在市场崩盘之前，他已经重新决定出售他的房子。
We had redecided against going to the concert due to the bad weather. = 由于天气不好，我们之前已经重新决定不去参加音乐会。


将来时:
They will redecide on the matter after further discussion. = 他们将在进一步讨论后重新决定此事。
She will redecide whether to accept the job offer. = 她将重新决定是否接受这份工作机会。


虚拟语气:
If they had redecided earlier, they could have avoided the problem. = 如果他们早些重新决定，他们本可以避免这个问题。
I wish I had redecided to go to college. = 我真希望我重新决定去上大学。


被动语态:
The date of the event was redecided by the organizers. = 活动的日期由组织者重新决定。
The project's scope was redecided after a thorough review. = 项目的范围在彻底审查后被重新决定。


疑问句:
Has the committee redecided on its policy? = 委员会是否已经重新决定了其政策？
Will they redecide the terms of the contract? = 他们会重新决定合同条款吗？


否定句:
They did not redecide their original plan. = 他们没有重新决定他们最初的计划。
The company has not redecided to invest in that project. = 公司还没有重新决定投资那个项目。
