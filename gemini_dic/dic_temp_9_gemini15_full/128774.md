night:/naɪt/ | n. |夜晚，黑夜 |The period of darkness between sunset and sunrise.  Night is often associated with rest, sleep, and mystery. |例句：The night was dark and stormy.  夜晚漆黑而暴风雨交加。|例句：We sat and talked late into the night. 我们一直聊到深夜。|近义词：evening, darkness |反义词：day, daytime
