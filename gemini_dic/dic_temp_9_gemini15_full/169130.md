projection:/prəˈdʒɛkʃən/ | n. | 投影；预测；突出；展现 | The act of projecting or the state of being projected;  a projected image; a prediction or forecast.  It can also refer to the act of attributing one's own feelings to another person. | 例句：The movie was shown using a high-quality projection.  这部电影是用高质量的投影机放映的。| 例句：The economist's projection for next year's growth is optimistic.  这位经济学家对明年经济增长的预测比较乐观。| 近义词：prediction, forecast, estimate, presentation | 反义词：retraction, withdrawal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The projector projects a clear image. = 投影机投射出清晰的图像。
He projects confidence in his presentations. = 他在演讲中展现出自信。


一般过去时:
They projected the film onto a large screen. = 他们把电影投射到一个大屏幕上。
The company projected a loss for the quarter. = 公司预测本季度亏损。


现在完成时:
She has projected her anxieties onto her child. = 她把自己的焦虑投射到了孩子身上。
Scientists have projected a rise in sea levels. = 科学家预测海平面会上升。


过去完成时:
They had projected the budget before the meeting. = 会议前他们已经制定了预算。
The team had projected a win, but they lost. = 团队预测会赢，但他们输了。


将来时:
We will project the data onto the chart. = 我们将把数据投射到图表上。
They will project their sales to increase next year. = 他们预计明年的销售额会增长。


虚拟语气:
If they projected a more realistic budget, they wouldn't be in trouble now. = 如果他们预算了更实际的预算，现在就不会陷入困境了。
I wish I had projected the consequences of my actions. = 我真希望我当时预料到了我的行为的后果。


被动语态:
The image was projected onto the wall. = 图像被投射到墙上。
The sales figures were projected to be lower than expected. = 销售额预计低于预期。


疑问句:
How do you project your voice? = 你如何放声说话？
What are your projections for the coming year? = 你对来年有什么预测？


否定句:
The device didn't project the image clearly. = 设备没有清晰地投射图像。
He doesn't project a very positive image to the public. = 他给公众的印象不太积极。
