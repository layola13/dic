misdelivers:/ˌmɪsdɪˈlɪvərz/| vt.|错误递送，送错|To deliver something to the wrong place or person.  This verb implies a failure to deliver something to its intended recipient.|The courier misdelivered the package to the wrong address. = 快递员把包裹错送到另一个地址了。


The company misdelivered my order; it was sent to a completely different city. = 公司把我的订单送错了，它被送到一个完全不同的城市了。


近义词：misdirect, mishandle, misplace


反义词：deliver correctly, deliver accurately


词性：动词

USAGE->
