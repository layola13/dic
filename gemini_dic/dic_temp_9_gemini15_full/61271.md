emasculating: /ɪˈmæskjʊleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 削弱男性气质的；使无力的；使衰弱的 |  Weakening or diminishing the strength or vigor of; making less effective.  It often refers to the weakening of a man's masculinity or power, but can also be used more broadly to describe the weakening of something's strength or effectiveness. |

例句：

His constant criticism was emasculating to her confidence.  = 他不断的批评削弱了她的自信。

The new regulations are emasculating the power of the local government. = 新的法规削弱了地方政府的权力。


近义词：weakening, debilitating, undermining, castrating (in the sense of removing power or strength)

反义词：empowering, strengthening, invigorating

词性：形容词
