primaried:/ˈpraɪmɛrid/| v.|参加初选；在初选中获得提名|To participate in a primary election, typically to select a candidate for a later general election.  The verb implies the act of voting in a primary or running as a candidate in one.|例句：Many voters primaried for the Democratic candidate. 许多选民参加了民主党候选人的初选。

例句：She primaried against a well-known incumbent. 她参加初选挑战一位知名的现任官员。


近义词：nominate, run in a primary, compete in a primary|反义词：None specifically, but actions such as abstaining from the primary or supporting the general election candidate directly could be considered opposites. |动词
