demonographer:/dɪˈmɒnəɡræfər/ | n. |人口统计学家| A person who studies and analyzes population statistics.  Demonographers collect, interpret, and present data related to population size, distribution, density, growth, and other characteristics. They often use their findings to inform policy decisions and predict future population trends. | The demonographer presented her findings on population aging to the committee.  人口统计学家向委员会展示了她关于人口老龄化的调查结果。| statistician, demographer | (no direct antonym, but opposite function could be described as someone who ignores or opposes population data analysis) | 名词
