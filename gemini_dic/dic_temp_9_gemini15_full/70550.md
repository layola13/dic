filigree:/ˈfɪlɪɡriː/| n. adj.|细工，金银细工；精致的|Ornate work of fine wire, often gold or silver, arranged in delicate patterns.  The term can also be used to describe something delicate and intricate in design.|The silver filigree on the necklace was exquisite.  这条项链上的银丝细工精美绝伦。The filigree detail on the cake was amazing. 蛋糕上精致的细工令人惊叹。|lacework, arabesque|coarse, crude|名词，形容词
