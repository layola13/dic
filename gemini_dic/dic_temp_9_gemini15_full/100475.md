intervals: /ˈɪntərvəlz/ | n. | 间隔；间歇；时期 |  Intervals refer to periods of time between events or points. They can also denote spaces or distances between things.  |例句：The doctor advised taking the medicine at regular intervals. 医生建议定期服用药物。

The music had long intervals of silence. 这音乐有很长的沉默间隔。

近义词：gaps, spaces, periods, phases | 反义词：continuity, constant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The train arrives at intervals of 10 minutes. = 火车每隔10分钟到达一次。
Regular intervals are important for maintaining health. = 定期间隔对保持健康很重要。


一般过去时:
She took breaks at intervals throughout the day. = 她一整天都在间歇性地休息。
They met at irregular intervals. = 他们不定期见面。


现在完成时:
We have worked on this project at intervals for the past year. = 我们过去一年来断断续续地从事这个项目。
She has taken several breaks at intervals during the exam. = 她在考试期间断断续续地休息了好几次。


过去完成时:
They had already met at several intervals before the final decision. = 在最终决定之前，他们已经几次见面了。
Before the announcement, there had been long intervals of silence. = 在宣布之前，曾有过很长时间的沉默。


将来时:
We will meet at regular intervals to discuss the project. = 我们将定期会面讨论项目。
He will take short intervals to rest during the marathon. = 他将在马拉松比赛中短暂休息。


虚拟语气:
If we had shorter intervals between meetings, we would be more productive. = 如果我们缩短会议间的间隔，我们会更有生产力。
I wish there were shorter intervals between the bus arrivals. = 我希望公交车到站的间隔时间能短一些。


被动语态:
The data was collected at predetermined intervals. = 数据是在预定的间隔时间收集的。
The work was done at irregular intervals. = 工作是不定期完成的。


疑问句:
What are the intervals between the scheduled flights? = 预定航班之间的间隔时间是多少？
At what intervals should I take this medication? = 我应该每隔多久服用这种药物？


否定句:
There were no intervals between the songs. = 歌曲之间没有间隔。
The work was not done at regular intervals. = 工作不是定期完成的。
