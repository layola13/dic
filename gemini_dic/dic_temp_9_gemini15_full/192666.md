scutiger:/ˈskjuːtɪdʒər/| n. |蜈蚣(一种)|A scutiger is a type of centipede, specifically those belonging to the genus *Scutigera*.  They are characterized by their long, slender bodies, numerous legs, and rapid movements.  Scutigera coleoptrata, the house centipede, is a common species found in human dwellings.|Example: I saw a scutiger running across the floor last night. 昨晚我看到一只蜈蚣在地板上跑.|近义词:centipede, house centipede|反义词:None specifically, but words describing slow-moving creatures could be considered antonyms in a metaphorical sense.|名词
