siltation:/sɪlˈteɪʃən/ | n. |淤积；泥沙淤积 |The process of silting or being silted up.  The accumulation of silt. |例句：The river's silting is causing problems for navigation. 河流淤积给航运带来了问题。

例句：Excessive siltation has reduced the depth of the harbor.  过度的淤积减少了港口的深度。


近义词：sedimentation, silting


反义词：scouring, dredging (processes that remove silt)


词性：名词
