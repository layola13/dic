chins:/tʃɪnz/| n.|下巴；颏；(下巴的) 下颌|The plural of "chin". It refers to the protruding part of the face below the mouth.  It can also refer to the similar part of an animal's face.|例句：He has a strong chin. 他有一个结实的下巴。

例句：The baby grabbed my chin. 婴儿抓住了我的下巴。

近义词：jaw (下巴，颌骨),  lower jaw (下颌骨)

反义词：无
