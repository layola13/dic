rejuggled:/ˌriˈdʒʌɡld/| v.|重新调整；重新安排；重新洗牌|To rearrange or reorganize something, often in a skillful or clever way.  It implies a previous arrangement that is being altered.|例句：He rejuggled his schedule to accommodate the meeting. 他重新调整了自己的日程安排以适应会议。|例句：The company rejuggled its marketing strategy after poor sales. 公司在销售不佳后重新调整了其营销策略。|近义词：rearrange, reorganize, reshuffle|反义词：disorganize, leave unchanged
