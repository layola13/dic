plots:/plɒts/ /plɑːts/| n. |情节；阴谋；小块土地|A plot is a plan made in secret to do something wrong or illegal, or a series of events that form the story of a book, film, or play.  It can also refer to a small piece of land.|例句：The police uncovered a plot to overthrow the government.  警方揭露了一个推翻政府的阴谋。|例句：The plot of the novel was quite complicated. 这部小说的情节相当复杂。|近义词：scheme, conspiracy, plan, storyline, subplot|反义词：truth, honesty, reality|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gardener plots the vegetables carefully. = 园丁仔细地规划蔬菜种植。
He plots his revenge against his enemies. = 他密谋报复他的敌人。
