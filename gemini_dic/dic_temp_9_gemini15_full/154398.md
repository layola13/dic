permeation:/ˌpɜːmiˈeɪʃən/| n. |渗透，弥漫|The process by which a substance spreads through another substance.| 例句：The permeation of water into the soil is essential for plant growth. 水分渗透到土壤中对于植物生长至关重要。

例句：The permeation of new ideas into the company culture was slow but steady. 新思想渗透到公司文化中是缓慢但稳定的。


近义词：penetration, diffusion, infiltration, spread | 反义词：exclusion, isolation, blockage | 词性: 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Permeation occurs naturally in many systems. = 渗透在许多系统中自然发生。
Water permeates the soil slowly. = 水缓慢地渗透到土壤中。


一般过去时:
The gas permeated the fabric. = 气体渗透了织物。
The aroma permeated the entire room. = 香气弥漫了整个房间。


现在完成时:
The dye has permeated the fabric completely. = 染料已经完全渗透到织物中。
The influence of social media has permeated our lives. = 社交媒体的影响已经渗透到我们的生活中。


过去完成时:
The water had permeated the foundation before we noticed the leak. = 我们注意到泄漏之前，水已经渗透到地基中了。
The rumor had permeated the entire town by the time the truth came out. = 在真相大白之前，谣言已经传遍了整个城镇。


将来时:
The new technology will permeate all aspects of our lives. = 新技术将渗透到我们生活的方方面面。
The effects of climate change will permeate many ecosystems. = 气候变化的影响将渗透到许多生态系统中。


虚拟语气:
If the liquid permeated the barrier, the experiment would fail. = 如果液体渗透了屏障，实验就会失败。
I wish the fragrance would permeate the whole house. = 我希望香味能弥漫整个房子。


被动语态:
The material is permeated by the solvent. = 材料被溶剂渗透。
The culture was permeated by ancient traditions. = 文化被古老的传统所渗透。


疑问句:
Does the water permeate the concrete easily? = 水容易渗透混凝土吗？
How quickly does the smell permeate the air? = 气味多快能弥漫到空气中？


否定句:
The water did not permeate the clay soil. = 水没有渗透到粘土中。
The message failed to permeate the intended audience. = 信息未能传达到目标受众。
