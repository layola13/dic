pretabulate:/ˌpriːˈtæbjuleɪt/| vt.|预先制表；预先列表|To prepare a table or list in advance.  This often involves organizing data before it is needed for analysis or presentation. |例句：The researchers pretabulated the survey results to facilitate the analysis. 研究人员预先制表了调查结果以方便分析。

例句：The marketing team pretabulated the sales figures for the upcoming presentation. 营销团队预先列出了即将到来的演示文稿的销售数据。


近义词：prearrange, prepare, organize


反义词：posttabulate (this word is rarely used)


词性：动词
