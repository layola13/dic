politicking:/ˈpɑːlətɪkɪŋ/| v.|政治活动；搞政治；玩弄权术 |The act of engaging in political activities, often in a manipulative or self-serving way.  It often implies behind-the-scenes maneuvering and deal-making.|例句：The senator was accused of politicking instead of focusing on legislation.  参议员被指责从事政治活动而不是专注于立法。|例句：  The mayor's office is rife with politicking and backroom deals. 市长办公室充斥着政治活动和幕后交易。|近义词：political maneuvering,  intrigue, scheming |反义词：straightforwardness, honesty
