simonize:/ˈsaɪmənaɪz/| vt.|抛光；使光亮|To polish (a surface, especially a car) to a high gloss.  The term comes from Simoniz, a brand name of car wax.|例句：He simonized his car until it gleamed. 他把他的车抛光到闪闪发光。 He spent the afternoon simonizing his classic car. 他花了一下午时间给他的老爷车抛光。|近义词：polish, buff, wax|反义词：roughen, dull
