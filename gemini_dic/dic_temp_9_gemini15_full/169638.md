proplasm:/ˈproʊˌplæzəm/| n. |原生质；细胞质|The fundamental substance of which all living matter is composed. It is a complex mixture of water, proteins, lipids, carbohydrates, and nucleic acids, and is enclosed by a cell membrane.  Protoplasm carries out all the essential functions of life, including metabolism, reproduction, and response to stimuli.|例句：The protoplasm of the cell is essential for its survival. 细胞的原生质对其生存至关重要。|近义词：cytoplasm, cytosol|反义词：N/A
