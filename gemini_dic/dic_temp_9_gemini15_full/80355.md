golds:/ɡoʊldz/| n.|金子（复数）, 金制品（复数）|The plural form of "gold," referring to multiple quantities of gold or gold objects.  It can refer to gold nuggets, gold coins, gold jewelry, etc.|例句：He owns several golds, including gold bars and coins. 他拥有几块金子，包括金条和金币。

例句：The museum displayed many ancient golds. 博物馆展示了许多古代金器。

近义词：golds (as a plural for gold), treasures, riches

反义词：None (as a plural noun, it doesn't have a direct antonym)

词性: 名词 (复数)
