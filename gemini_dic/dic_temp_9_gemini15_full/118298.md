mercurially:/mərˈkjʊəriəli/ | adv. | 反复无常地；像水银一样；迅速地 | Characterized by rapid and unpredictable changes in mood or behavior;  like mercury; swiftly. |例句：His moods changed mercurially throughout the day. 他一天的心情变化无常。  The stock market behaved mercurially in the wake of the news.  消息传出后，股市波动剧烈。|近义词：erratically, capriciously, temperamentally, rapidly | 反义词：steadily, consistently, predictably | 词性：副词
