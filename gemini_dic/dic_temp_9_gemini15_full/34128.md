cladocera:/kləˈdɒsərə/| n. |枝角类动物，水蚤|Cladocera are small crustaceans found in various aquatic environments. They are important components of freshwater ecosystems and serve as a food source for many aquatic animals.|例句：The pond was teeming with cladocera, indicating a healthy ecosystem.  池塘里挤满了枝角类动物，表明这是一个健康的生态系统。|近义词：water flea (水蚤)|反义词：无
