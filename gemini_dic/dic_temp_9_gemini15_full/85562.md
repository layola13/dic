hated:/ˈheɪtɪd/| v. |憎恨，厌恶|To feel intense dislike or disgust for (someone or something).|I hated my job. 我讨厌我的工作。|近义词：detested, loathed, abhorred |反义词：loved, adored


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I hate Mondays. = 我讨厌星期一。
She hates spicy food. = 她讨厌辛辣的食物。

一般过去时:
He hated the movie. = 他讨厌那部电影。
We hated living in that apartment. = 我们讨厌住在那个公寓里。

现在完成时:
I have hated this weather all week. = 我整个星期都讨厌这种天气。
She has hated him ever since he lied to her. = 从他对她撒谎以来，她就一直讨厌他。

过去完成时:
He had hated her for years before they finally broke up. = 在他们最终分手之前，他已经讨厌她多年了。
I had hated that song before I heard the new version. = 在我听到新版本之前，我就讨厌那首歌了。

将来时:
I will hate this job even more when my boss gets back from vacation. = 当我的老板从假期回来时，我会更讨厌这份工作。
She will hate having to work overtime every night. = 她会讨厌每天晚上都要加班。


虚拟语气:
I wish I didn't hate public speaking. = 我真希望我不讨厌公开演讲。
If I hadn't hated math so much in high school, I might have gone to college for engineering. = 如果我高中时没有那么讨厌数学，我可能会上大学读工程。


被动语态:
His actions were hated by everyone. = 他的行为受到所有人的憎恨。
The new policy is hated by the employees. = 新政策受到员工的厌恶。


疑问句:
Do you hate your job? = 你讨厌你的工作吗？
Does she hate cats? = 她讨厌猫吗？


否定句:
I don't hate him, but I don't like him either. = 我不恨他，但我也不喜欢他。
She didn't hate the music, but she found it boring. = 她不讨厌那音乐，但她觉得它很无聊。


PHRASE->
hated rival = 仇敌
deeply hated = 深恶痛绝
to be hated = 令人厌恶
generally hated = 普遍厌恶
hated enemy = 死敌
