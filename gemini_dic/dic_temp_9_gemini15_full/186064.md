rewrites:/ˈriːraɪts/ | n. |改写；改写本 |  The act of writing something again, often to improve it or to make it clearer or more effective.  It refers to a revised version of a piece of writing. | Example:  The professor asked for rewrites of the essays. 教授要求修改论文。  The rewrites improved the clarity of the article significantly.  改写后文章的清晰度显著提高。 |近义词： revisions, amendments, redrafts |反义词： originals


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rewrites his emails before sending them. 他在发送邮件之前会重新修改。
She rewrites her stories multiple times. 她多次改写她的故事。

一般过去时:
I rewrote my essay after receiving feedback. 我在收到反馈后重写了我的论文。
