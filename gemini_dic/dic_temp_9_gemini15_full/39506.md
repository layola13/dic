connoisseur:/ˌkɒnəˈsɜːr/| /ˌkɑːnəˈsɜːr/ | n. |鉴赏家；行家 |A connoisseur has expert knowledge and keen appreciation in a particular field, especially in the fine arts, food, or wine.  They possess refined taste and judgment. |He is a connoisseur of fine wines. 他是葡萄酒鉴赏家。| expert, specialist, aficionado | layman, novice, amateur | 名词

He is a connoisseur of art. = 他是一位艺术鉴赏家。
She is a connoisseur of French cuisine. = 她是一位法国菜的鉴赏家。


一般过去时:

词源:
The word "connoisseur" comes from the Old French word "connoisseur," which means "knowing" or "to know."  It ultimately derives from the Latin verb "cognoscere," meaning "to get to know," "to learn," or "to recognize."
