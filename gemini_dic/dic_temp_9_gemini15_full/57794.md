dredger:/ˈdrɛdʒər/| n.|挖泥船；疏浚船；挖掘机|A dredger is a type of boat or machine used to dredge, which means to remove mud, sand, or other material from the bottom of a body of water to deepen or widen it, or to excavate a channel.|例句：The dredger worked tirelessly to clear the harbor of silt. 这艘挖泥船不知疲倦地清理港口的淤泥。  The port authority used a large dredger to deepen the shipping channel. 港务局用大型挖泥船加深航道。|近义词：excavator, digger|反义词：filler (in the context of filling a channel)|名词
