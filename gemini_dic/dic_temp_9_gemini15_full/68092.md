fader:/ˈfeɪdər/| n.|推子，衰减器|A fader is a sliding control on an audio mixer or similar device, used to gradually increase or decrease the volume or level of a signal.  It's often a long, thin slider. |例句：The sound engineer adjusted the fader to lower the bass.  声音工程师调整了推子以降低低音。|例句：She smoothly faded the music out using the fader on the mixing console. 她用调音台上的推子平滑地淡出了音乐。|近义词：attenuator, potentiometer, slider|反义词：amplifier, booster
