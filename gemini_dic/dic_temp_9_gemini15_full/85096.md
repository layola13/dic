hardboards:/ˈhɑːrdˌbɔːrdz/| n. |硬纤维板；硬板|A hardboard is a type of panel product made by refining wood fibers and pressing them into sheets under high pressure and temperature.  It's a strong, dense material often used as a building material or for crafting.|The walls are covered in hardboards.|近义词：fiberboard, pressboard|反义词：softwood, plywood
