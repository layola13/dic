carnallite:/ˈkɑːrnəlˌaɪt/| n.|光卤石|A naturally occurring potassium magnesium chloride hydrate mineral,  KCl·MgCl₂·6H₂O,  a major source of potassium. It is typically found in evaporite deposits, often associated with other evaporite minerals like halite and gypsum.  Carnallite is usually reddish or brownish in color due to impurities. It is relatively soft and easily soluble in water.| Example: The potash mine yielded a significant amount of carnallite, a crucial ingredient in fertilizer production.  光卤石矿出产了大量的钾盐，这是肥料生产中的一种关键成分。|近义词：potash (as a source of potassium)|反义词：无
