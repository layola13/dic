locationally:/loʊˈkeɪʃənəli/ | adv. | 位置上地；地点上地 |  Relating to or concerning location; in terms of location.  | The project was assessed locationally, considering its proximity to transportation and resources.  项目在位置上进行了评估，考虑了其与交通和资源的接近程度。 | spatially, geographically | non-locationally | 副词
