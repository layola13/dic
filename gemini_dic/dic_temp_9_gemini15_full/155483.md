phenotypically:/ˌfiːnoʊˈtɪpɪkli/ | adj. | 表型上地；从表型角度来看 |  Describing something in terms of its observable characteristics or phenotype, as opposed to its genotype.  It refers to the outwardly visible characteristics of an organism, resulting from the interaction of its genotype and the environment. | The plants were phenotypically identical, despite having different genetic backgrounds.  这些植物在表型上是相同的，尽管它们的基因背景不同。| genetically, genotypically | genotypically | adj.
