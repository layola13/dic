overcommunicate:/ˌoʊvər kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/ | vt. | 过度沟通；信息冗余 | To communicate excessively or to a degree that is unnecessary or counterproductive.  It often implies that the communication is not only excessive but also potentially harmful or annoying. | 例句：He tends to overcommunicate, sending multiple emails about the same issue. 他倾向于过度沟通，会就同一个问题发送多封邮件。


例句：The manager overcommunicated the new policy, leading to confusion among the employees. 经理对新政策进行了过度沟通，导致员工感到困惑。


近义词：redundantly communicate, excessively communicate, over-explain


反义词：undercommunicate,  be concise


词性:动词
