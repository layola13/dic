pacifism: /ˈpæsɪfɪzəm/ | n. |和平主义，非暴力主义 | The belief that any violence is wrong and that all disputes should be settled peacefully.  This ideology advocates for the resolution of conflict through negotiation, diplomacy, and other non-violent means. |  例句：His pacifism prevented him from joining the army.  他的和平主义阻止了他参军。

例句：Many religious groups actively promote pacifism as a core tenet of their faith. 许多宗教团体积极地将和平主义作为其信仰的核心原则来推广。


近义词：nonviolence, peace activism, anti-war movement | 反义词：militarism, warmongering, bellicosity | 词性: 名词
