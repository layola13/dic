inhearse:/ɪnˈhɜːrs/| vt.|排练，预演|To rehearse or practice something, especially a play or speech, beforehand.|例句：The actors inhearsed the play for weeks before the opening night.  演员们在开幕式前几周排练了这部戏。|例句：The lawyer inhearsed his closing argument before the trial.  律师在审判前排练了他的结案陈词。|近义词：rehearse, practice, prepare|反义词：improvise, wing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They inhearse the play every evening. = 他们每天晚上排练戏剧。
The band inhearses new songs before concerts. = 乐队在演出前排练新歌。

一般过去时:
The choir inhearsed for hours yesterday. = 合唱团昨天排练了好几个小时。
