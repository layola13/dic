limeades:/ˌlaɪməˈneɪdz/| n. |柠檬水|A refreshing drink made from lime juice, water, and often sugar.  It's a popular summer beverage. |例句：She made a pitcher of limeades for the party. 她为派对做了一大壶柠檬水。

例句：The cafe offers a variety of flavored limeades. 这家咖啡馆提供各种口味的柠檬水。


近义词：limeade, lemonade (lemonade通常指柠檬水，limeade则更偏向青柠水，但两者常可互换)


反义词：无


词性：名词
