dottable:/ˈdɒtəbl/| adj.| 可点缀的，可加点的 | Able to be dotted or marked with dots; easily marked with dots.  The word suggests a surface or object suitable for receiving dots, perhaps for decoration or marking. |例句：The fabric is dottable with a fine-tipped pen. 这块布料可以用细尖笔点缀。

The surface of the map was dottable, allowing for easy marking of locations. 地图表面是可以点缀的，方便标记位置。|近义词：markable,  punctuatable|反义词：unmarkable,  impregnable (in the sense of resisting marking)
