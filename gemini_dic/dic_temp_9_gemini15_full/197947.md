shittiest: /ˈʃɪtiɪst/ | adj. | 最差的，最糟糕的 |  The worst or most unpleasant of its kind.  It's a superlative form of "shitty," which itself is a vulgar term for "bad" or "poor quality." | 例句：This is the shittiest movie I've ever seen. = 这部电影是我看过的最烂的电影。  That was the shittiest day of my life. = 那是我生命中最糟糕的一天。| 近义词：worst, poorest, most awful, terrible, dreadful | 反义词：best, finest, greatest
