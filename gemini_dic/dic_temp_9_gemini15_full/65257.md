/ˈɛtɪmɑːnz/ | n. | 词源；词根 | Etymons are the original words or word parts from which a word is derived.  They are the historical roots of a word, often from older languages. Studying etymons helps to understand a word's meaning and evolution over time. | The etymons of "telephone" include "tele-" (distant) and "-phone" (sound). | 近义词：root words, origins, sources | 反义词：无
