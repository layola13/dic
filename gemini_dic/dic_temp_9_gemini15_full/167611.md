prey:/preɪ/| n., v.|猎物；捕食|

英文解析:
As a noun, "prey" refers to an animal hunted and killed by another for food.  It can also refer to a person or thing that is easily victimized or exploited.  As a verb, "prey" means to hunt and kill for food, or to take advantage of someone or something.


例句:
The lion stalked its prey through the tall grass.  狮子在高高的草丛中潜行，跟踪它的猎物。

The scammer preyed on the elderly woman's kindness.  骗子利用了这位老妇人的善良。


近义词:
victim, quarry, game, plunder, exploit


反义词:
predator (when used as a noun), hunter (when used as a verb)


词性:
Noun, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hawk preys on small birds.  鹰捕食小鸟。
Lions prey on zebras. 狮子捕食斑马。

一般过去时:
The tiger preyed on a deer last night.  老虎昨晚捕食了一只鹿。
The hunter preyed on the unsuspecting rabbit. 猎人捕食了毫无戒心的兔子。

现在完成时:
The cat has preyed on several mice this week.  猫这周已经捕食了好几只老鼠。
The company has preyed on the public's ignorance. 这家公司一直在利用公众的无知。

过去完成时:
The eagle had preyed on many fish before it was captured.  鹰被捕获之前已经捕食了许多鱼。
The con artist had preyed on numerous victims before he was caught.  这个骗子在被抓住之前已经欺骗了许多受害者。

将来时:
The wolf will prey on the sheep tonight.  狼今晚将捕食绵羊。
The corporation will prey on the less informed consumers. 公司将会利用那些信息较少的消费者。

虚拟语气:
If the lion were to prey on that gazelle, it would be a quick kill. 如果狮子要捕食那只瞪羚，那将会是一击毙命。
If I were to prey on their weaknesses, I could easily win.  如果我利用他们的弱点，我就能很容易地获胜。

被动语态:
The sheep were preyed upon by the wolves. 绵羊被狼捕食了。
The vulnerable population was preyed upon by unscrupulous lenders.  弱势群体被不择手段的贷款人所利用。

疑问句:
What kind of animals does this bird prey on?  这种鸟捕食什么动物？
Does the fox prey on rabbits?  狐狸捕食兔子吗？

否定句:
The bird did not prey on the insects.  这只鸟没有捕食昆虫。
The company does not prey on its customers.  这家公司不会利用它的顾客。
