distinction:/dɪˈstɪŋkʃən/| n.|区别，卓越，荣誉|The act of distinguishing or the state of being distinguished; a difference or contrast between similar things or people; a feature that makes something different from others; exceptional merit or excellence; a mark of honor or distinction.|例句：The distinction between right and wrong is not always clear.  是非对错的区别并不总是那么清晰。

例句：Her work has earned her many distinctions in the field of medicine. 她的工作为她在医学领域赢得了许多荣誉。


近义词：difference, contrast, variation, excellence, achievement, honor|反义词：similarity, resemblance, mediocrity


词源：源自于古法语“distinction”，来自拉丁语“distinguere”，意为“区分，辨别”，由“dis-”（分开）和“stinguere”（刺）构成，字面意思是“刺开，区分”。
