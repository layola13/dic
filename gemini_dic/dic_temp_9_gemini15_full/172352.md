putrification:/ˌpjutrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |腐烂；腐化；败坏 |The process of decay or rotting in a body or other organic matter, caused by the action of bacteria and other microorganisms.  This process involves the breakdown of complex organic molecules into simpler ones, often resulting in unpleasant odors. |例句：The putrification of the corpse was evident from the foul smell.尸体腐烂的迹象从恶臭中显而易见。

例句：The rapid putrification of the fish made it inedible. 鱼迅速腐烂，变得无法食用。


近义词：decay, decomposition, rotting, spoilage |反义词：preservation, conservation
