cups:/kʌps/| n.|杯子，杯状物|A small, open container, typically cylindrical in shape, used for drinking from.  It can also refer to the amount a cup holds.|例句：She drank a cup of tea. 她喝了一杯茶。  I need two cups for the party. 我需要两个杯子来招待客人。|近义词：mug, glass, goblet|反义词:  (无明确反义词)|名词

I drink two cups of coffee every morning. = 我每天早上喝两杯咖啡。
She uses ceramic cups for her tea. = 她用陶瓷杯子喝茶。

一般过去时:
