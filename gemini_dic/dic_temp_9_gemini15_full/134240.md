noninfecting: /ˌnɒnɪnˈfɛktɪŋ/ | adj. | 非感染性的，不感染的 | Not causing infection.  This adjective describes something that does not cause or spread infection or disease.  |


例句：

The new surgical technique uses noninfecting materials to minimize the risk of infection. = 新的外科手术技术使用非感染性材料以最大限度地降低感染风险。

The doctor confirmed that the wound was noninfecting and healing well. = 医生确认伤口没有感染并且恢复良好。


近义词：aseptic, sterile, harmless

反义词：infectious, contagious, pathogenic


词性：形容词
