inclinations:/ˌɪŋklɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. | 倾向；爱好；意向 | A natural tendency or urge to act or feel in a particular way; a predisposition.  It can refer to a preference, a leaning, or a propensity.  The plural form indicates multiple tendencies or preferences. | 例句：His inclinations were towards a life of leisure.  他的倾向是过着悠闲的生活。

His artistic inclinations led him to pursue a career in painting.他的艺术爱好促使他从事绘画事业。


近义词：tendencies, proclivities, predispositions, leanings, propensities | 反义词：aversions, disinclinations


词性:名词
