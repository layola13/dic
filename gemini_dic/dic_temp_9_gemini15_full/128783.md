nightbird:/ˈnaɪtˌbɜːrd/ | n. |夜鸟，夜行鸟 | A bird that is active at night.  Nightbirds often have excellent night vision and hearing. |例句：Owls are a type of nightbird. 猫头鹰是一种夜鸟。

The nighthawk is a fascinating nightbird.  夜鹰是一种令人着迷的夜鸟。|近义词：nocturnal bird |反义词：day bird
