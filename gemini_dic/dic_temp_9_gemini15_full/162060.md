pre-booking:/ˌpriːˈbʊkɪŋ/| n. |预订，提前预订|The act of booking something in advance.  It refers to reserving or securing something, like a flight, hotel room, or table at a restaurant, before the actual date of use or need.|例句：I recommend a pre-booking for the popular restaurant. 我建议提前预订那家很受欢迎的餐厅。

例句：We made a pre-booking for the concert tickets last month. 我们上个月就预订了演唱会门票。


近义词：reservation, advance booking, prior booking|反义词：last-minute booking, spontaneous booking|词性:名词
