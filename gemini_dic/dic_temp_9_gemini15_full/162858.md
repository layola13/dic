precautions:/prɪˈkɔːʃənz/|/n.|预防措施；警惕；小心|/Measures taken in advance to prevent something dangerous, unpleasant, or inconvenient from happening.  Actions taken to avoid potential harm or risks.|例句：We took all necessary precautions to ensure the safety of the children. 我们采取了所有必要的预防措施以确保儿童的安全。|例句：The hikers took precautions against the cold weather. 徒步旅行者采取了防寒措施。|近义词：safeguards, preventative measures, safeguards, precautions|反义词：negligence, carelessness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He takes precautions against fire. = 他采取防火措施。
She always takes precautions when crossing the street. = 她过马路时总是很小心。


一般过去时:
They took precautions before starting the experiment. = 他们在开始实验之前采取了预防措施。

过去完成时:
