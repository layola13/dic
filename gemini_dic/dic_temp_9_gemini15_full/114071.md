magnetograph:/mæɡˈnɛtəɡræf/ | n. | 磁力图仪，磁力记录器 | An instrument used to record variations in the Earth's magnetic field or other magnetic fields.  It measures and records the strength and direction of magnetic fields. | Example:  Scientists used a magnetograph to monitor solar flares.  科学家们用磁力图仪监测太阳耀斑。 The magnetograph produced a detailed record of the magnetic storm. 磁力图仪产生了磁暴的详细记录。 | 近义词:magnetometer (侧重测量)| 反义词:|  词性:名词
