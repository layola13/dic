preachings:/ˈpriːtʃɪŋz/| n. |布道；说教；教诲|The act of preaching, or the things preached; sermons.  It often implies a repetitive or excessive emphasis on moral or religious beliefs.|例句：His preachings on the importance of honesty were well-received. 他的关于诚实重要性的布道很受欢迎。

例句：The constant preachings about saving money became tiresome.  关于省钱的不断说教变得令人厌烦。

近义词：sermons, teachings, exhortations, lectures

反义词：silence, inaction
