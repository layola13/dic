disreputation:/ˌdɪsˌrepjuˈteɪʃən/| n. |坏名声，声名狼藉 |Loss of reputation; ill repute.  Disreputation is the state of having a bad reputation, often due to wrongdoing or scandal. It's the opposite of having a good name or reputation. |His actions brought him into disreputation.  他的行为使他声名狼藉。|The company suffered severe disreputation after the scandal. 这家公司在丑闻后声誉严重受损。|近义词：bad name, ill repute, infamy, disgrace |反义词：good name, reputation, esteem
