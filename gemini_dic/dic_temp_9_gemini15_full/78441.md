get-wise-to:/ɡɛtˈwaɪztuː/ | phrasal verb | 识破；了解；明白 | To understand or realize something, especially something that was previously hidden or unclear.  This phrase often implies a gradual process of understanding, sometimes through experience or observation. |  例句：It took me a while to get wise to his manipulative tactics.  我花了一段时间才识破了他的操纵手段。


例句：Eventually, she got wise to his lies. 她最终识破了他的谎言。


近义词：figure out, understand, realize, catch on to, see through


反义词：misunderstand, be oblivious to, be fooled by


词性: phrasal verb
