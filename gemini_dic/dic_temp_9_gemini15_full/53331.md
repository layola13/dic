disacidify: /ˌdɪsæˈsɪdɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 除去酸性；中和酸性 | To remove acid from something or neutralize its acidity.  | 例句：The soil was disacidified before planting.  种植前土壤进行了酸化处理。 The process disacidifies the waste product, making it safer for disposal. 这个过程使废物脱酸，使其更安全地处理。 | 近义词：neutralize, deacidify | 反义词：acidify
