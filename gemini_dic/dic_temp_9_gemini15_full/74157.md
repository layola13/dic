foundry:/ˈfaʊndri/| n. |铸造厂；铸造车间|A foundry is a factory where molten metal is cast into shapes.  Foundries use various processes to create metal objects, from simple castings to complex components.|例句：The bronze statue was cast in a foundry in Italy. 这尊青铜雕像是意大利的一家铸造厂铸造的。|例句：That company operates a large foundry producing car parts. 那家公司经营着一座大型铸造厂，生产汽车零件。|近义词：casting plant, metalworks |反义词:无
