complementaries:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmɛntəriːz/| n. |互补物；补充物|Things that complete or enhance each other.  They are often related but distinct, working together to form a whole.  For example, in DNA, adenine and thymine are complementary base pairs. | Example: The two businesses formed a successful partnership, their complementary services creating a powerful synergy.  两个企业建立了成功的伙伴关系，他们互补的服务创造了强大的协同效应。|近义词：complements, supplements|反义词：opposites, antagonists|名词
