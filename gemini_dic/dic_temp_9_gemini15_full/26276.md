buttonmold: /ˈbʌtənmoʊld/ | n. |纽扣模具|A mold used to shape buttons.  Button molds are typically used in the mass production of buttons, allowing for the creation of many identical buttons efficiently. They can be made from various materials, such as metal or plastic, and are designed to create buttons of specific shapes and sizes.| Example: The factory used a metal buttonmold to produce thousands of identical plastic buttons.  The new buttonmold created buttons with a unique, textured surface.|近义词: button die, button former |反义词:  N/A
