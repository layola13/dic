proteinaceous:/ˌproʊtiˈneɪʃəs/| adj.|含蛋白质的，蛋白质似的|Consisting of or relating to protein.  The term describes substances or materials composed of or containing proteins.|例句：The proteinaceous matrix supports the cells.  含蛋白质的基质支持细胞。

The sample showed a high proteinaceous content. 样本显示出高含量的蛋白质。


近义词：protein-rich, albuminous|反义词：non-proteinaceous


词源:  From *protein* + *-aceous* (suffix indicating "full of" or "resembling").  The word "protein" itself originates from the Greek word *prōteios*, meaning "primary" or "of first importance," reflecting its fundamental role in living organisms.
