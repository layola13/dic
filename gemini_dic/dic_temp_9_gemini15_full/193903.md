self-doubt:/ˌsɛlfˈdaʊt/| n. |自我怀疑，缺乏自信|Lack of confidence in one's abilities and judgment.  It's a feeling of uncertainty or insecurity about oneself, often leading to hesitation or inaction. |例句：Her self-doubt prevented her from applying for the promotion.  她的自我怀疑阻止了她申请晋升。|例句：Overcoming self-doubt is crucial for personal growth. 克服自我怀疑对个人成长至关重要。|近义词：insecurity, uncertainty, lack of confidence, apprehension |反义词：self-confidence, self-assurance, certainty
