incisions:/ɪnˈsɪʒənz/| n.|切口，切痕|Cuts or wounds made with a sharp instrument, especially during surgery.  The plural form of "incision".|例句：The surgeon made several incisions to access the damaged organ.  外科医生做了几个切口来接近受损的器官。

The incisions healed well after the operation. 手术后的切口恢复得很好。|近义词：cuts, wounds, slits, gashes|反义词：closures, stitches, healings|名词
