monotheistic:/ˌmɑːnəʊθiˈɪstɪk/| adj. |一神论的；一神教的 |Adjective describing a belief in a single god.  It refers to religions that worship only one god, as opposed to polytheistic religions which worship multiple gods. |Example:  Christianity, Islam, and Judaism are monotheistic religions. 基督教、伊斯兰教和犹太教都是一神教。|近义词：unitarian, unicentral |反义词：polytheistic
