insulators:/ˈɪnsjʊleɪtərz/ | n. |绝缘体，隔离体 |A material or substance that does not readily conduct electricity, heat, or sound.  Insulators are used to prevent the flow of electricity, heat transfer, or sound transmission. |例句：Rubber is a good electrical insulator.  橡胶是良好的电绝缘体。

例句：The house was well-insulated to keep the heat in during winter. 这房子隔热性能良好，冬天可以保持温暖。


近义词：dielectric, nonconductor |反义词：conductor
