geneses:/ˈdʒɛnɪsiːz/| n. |起源；创始；发生；（尤指）创世纪|The plural of Genesis, referring to origins or beginnings, especially in the sense of creation or the beginning of something.  It can also refer to the book of Genesis in the Bible.|例句：The geneses of many great civilizations are shrouded in mystery.  许多伟大文明的起源都笼罩在神秘之中。

例句：Understanding the geneses of conflict can help prevent future wars. 了解冲突的起源有助于预防未来的战争。


近义词：origins, beginnings, commencements, sources

反义词：ends, conclusions, terminations
