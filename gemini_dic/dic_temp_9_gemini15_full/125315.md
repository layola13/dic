multihead: /ˈmʌltihed/ | adj. |多头的；多头的（指神经网络等）|Having or involving multiple heads; specifically, in the context of neural networks, referring to an architecture with multiple attention mechanisms operating in parallel.|The multihead attention mechanism allows the model to attend to different parts of the input sequence simultaneously.  多头注意力机制允许模型同时关注输入序列的不同部分。|近义词：polycephalic (referring to multiple heads in a literal sense)|反义词：single-headed
