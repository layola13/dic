secede:/sɪˈsiːd/ | v. |脱离，退出，(尤指州或国家)脱离联邦 |To withdraw formally from membership of a federation or body, especially a political state. |例句：The southern states seceded from the Union.南方各州脱离了联邦。  |例句：Several members seceded from the club after a disagreement.  几次争执后，有几位成员退出了俱乐部。|近义词：withdraw, separate, depart |反义词：join, unite, adhere
