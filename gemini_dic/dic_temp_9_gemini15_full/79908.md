gnawings:/ˈnɔːɪŋz/| n.|啃咬；焦虑；不安|A feeling of anxiety or worry that persists and is difficult to ignore.  It often refers to a persistent, nagging feeling of unease or guilt.|例句：The gnawings of conscience kept him awake at night. 良心上的谴责使他整夜睡不着觉。|例句：He felt the gnawings of hunger in his stomach. 他感到肚子一阵阵的饥饿。|近义词：anxiety, worry, unease, disquiet, qualms|反义词：peace, tranquility, calm, composure|名词
