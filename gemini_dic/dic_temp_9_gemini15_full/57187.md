The term emphasizes both their financial destitution and their social marginalization. |例句：Many down-and-outers sleep on the streets in the city center.  许多无家可归者在市中心街头睡觉。

The number of down-and-outers has increased significantly in recent years. 近年来无家可归者的人数显著增加。
