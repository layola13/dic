juiciest:/ˈdʒuːsɪəst/| adj. | 最多汁的，最鲜美的 |  Most full of juice; most flavorful and appealing.  This word is the superlative form of "juicy". It describes something that is extremely flavorful and moist, often referring to food but can also be used metaphorically. |例句：The juiciest steak I've ever tasted was cooked perfectly medium-rare. 我吃过的最鲜美的牛排是完美的五分熟。

The juiciest gossip was spread throughout the office.  办公室里流传着最劲爆的小道消息。|近义词：most succulent, most flavorful, ripest |反义词：dry, tasteless, arid
