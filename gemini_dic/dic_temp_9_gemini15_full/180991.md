reimport:/ˌriːˈɪmpɔːrt/ | vt. |重新进口，再进口 | To import something again or after a period of time.  This typically refers to goods or products that were previously imported but then ceased to be imported for some reason and are now being imported once more. |Example: The company decided to reimport the popular toy after high consumer demand.  这家公司决定在消费者强烈需求后重新进口这款流行玩具。

The company reimported the discontinued product line. 这家公司重新引进了停产的产品线。


近义词：reintroduce (in the context of trade), reinstate (in the context of import policies)
反义词：export, ban


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reimport textiles from India. 他们从印度重新进口纺织品。
The government reimports certain types of steel.  政府重新进口某些类型的钢材。


一般过去时:
We reimported the machine parts last year. 我们去年重新进口了机器零件。
The country reimported coffee after lifting the embargo.  该国取消禁运后重新进口咖啡。


现在完成时:
They have reimported the product several times. 他们已经多次重新进口该产品。
The company has reimported the raw materials since the supply chain disruption. 自供应链中断以来，该公司已重新进口原材料。


过去完成时:
They had reimported the goods before the new tariffs were imposed. 在新的关税实施之前，他们已经重新进口了货物。
The retailer had reimported the popular item before the holiday season. 零售商在假日季节前重新进口了该畅销商品。


将来时:
We will reimport the components next quarter. 我们将在下个季度重新进口这些组件。
The market analysts predict that they will reimport more wheat next year. 市场分析师预测他们明年将进口更多小麦。


虚拟语气:
If the demand increased, they would reimport the product. 如果需求增加，他们就会重新进口该产品。
If we had known the demand, we would have reimported the item sooner. 如果我们知道需求，我们会早点重新进口该商品。


被动语态:
The goods were reimported last month. 货物上个月重新进口。
The product is being reimported due to popular demand.  由于大众需求，该产品正在重新进口。


疑问句:
Will the company reimport the product next year? 公司明年会重新进口该产品吗？
Have they ever reimported this type of machinery before? 他们以前是否重新进口过这种类型的机械？


否定句:
We did not reimport the items this year. 我们今年没有重新进口这些商品。
The company will not reimport the defective product. 公司不会重新进口有缺陷的产品。
