flatuosity:/flætʃuˈɒsəti/ | n. |夸夸其谈；浮夸；空洞；放屁 | Excessive flatulence or windiness; also, inflated or pompous speech or writing.  The word often implies a lack of substance or sincerity. |例句：His speech was full of flatuosity, lacking any real substance.  他的演讲充满了夸夸其谈，缺乏任何实际内容。


例句：The professor's lecture was characterized by flatuosity rather than insightful analysis. 教授的讲座以夸夸其谈而非有见地的分析为特征。


近义词：pomposity, bombast, grandiloquence, verbosity, windy speech, empty rhetoric


反义词：brevity, conciseness, sincerity, substance, truthfulness


词性：名词
