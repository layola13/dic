chickadee:/ˈtʃɪkeɪdiː/| n. |山雀|A small, active North American songbird, known for its distinctive call.  Chickadees are known for their acrobatic foraging behavior and ability to cache food for later retrieval.| 例句：A chickadee flitted through the branches, searching for seeds.  一只山雀在树枝间飞舞，寻找种子。

例句：I saw a flock of chickadees at the bird feeder this morning.今天早上我在喂鸟器上看到一群山雀。|近义词：titmouse|反义词:|
