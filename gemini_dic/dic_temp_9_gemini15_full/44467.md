crossbarred:/ˈkrɒsbɑːrd/| adj.| 横杆的；有横杆的 | Having horizontal bars or stripes.  The term often refers to something with crossbeams or bars arranged across a structure or surface. | 例句：The crossbarred gate prevented the cattle from escaping.  横杆门阻止了牛逃跑。

The stable door was crossbarred for extra security. 马厩的门加上了横杆以加强安全性。|近义词：barred, cross-braced|反义词：unbarred, open
