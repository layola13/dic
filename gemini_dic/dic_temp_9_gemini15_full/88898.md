hola: /ˈoʊlə/ | interj. | 你好 | A Spanish greeting meaning "hello" or "hi".  It is an informal greeting, suitable for use with friends and family. | Hola, ¿cómo estás? = Hello, how are you? |  Hi, Hello | Goodbye, Farewell | interjection


USAGE->

This word is an interjection and doesn't conjugate like verbs or nouns.  Therefore, typical tense conjugations are not applicable.  The usage is primarily as a greeting.


PHRASE->

Hola, amigo! = Hello, friend!
Hola, ¿qué tal? = Hello, what's up?
Hola, ¿cómo te llamas? = Hello, what's your name?
¡Hola a todos! = Hello everyone!
¿Hola? = Hello? (used to answer a phone call)
