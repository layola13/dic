fragrances:/ˈfreɪɡrənsɪz/ | n. |香味；香气；香水 |Pleasant smells, especially those of flowers or spices.  Fragrances are often used in perfumes and other cosmetic products. |例句：The garden was filled with the sweet fragrances of roses and jasmine. 花园里充满了玫瑰和茉莉的甜香。

例句：Many different fragrances are available in this perfume shop.这家香水店出售许多不同的香水。


近义词： scents, aromas, perfumes, odors (if pleasant) | 反义词：stenches, smells (if unpleasant) | 词性：名词
