sanskritists:/ˈsæŋskrɪtɪsts/ | n. |梵文学者；梵文学家 | A sanskritist is a scholar specializing in Sanskrit, the ancient classical language of India.  They study the language, literature, and culture associated with Sanskrit.  | Example: Many renowned sanskritists have dedicated their lives to translating ancient Sanskrit texts.  许多著名的梵文学家都致力于翻译古代梵文文本。  |近义词:  Sanskrit scholar, Indologist |反义词:  (无明确反义词)
