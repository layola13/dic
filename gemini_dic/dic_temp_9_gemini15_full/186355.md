rhymes:/raɪmz/| n.|押韵；押韵的词|Words that rhyme have the same ending sound.  Rhymes are often used in poetry and songs.|例句：The words "cat" and "hat" are perfect rhymes. 猫和帽是完美的押韵词。|例句：The poem is full of clever rhymes. 这首诗充满了巧妙的押韵。|近义词：assonance, consonance|反义词：dissonance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The words rhyme perfectly. = 这些词完美押韵。
These poems use many rhymes. = 这些诗歌使用了许多押韵。

一般过去时:
