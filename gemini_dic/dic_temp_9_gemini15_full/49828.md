demonstrations:/ˌdɛmənˈstreɪʃənz/ | n. | 示威；演示；证明 |  Public displays of opinion or feeling, typically involving a march or protest.  Also, the act of showing or explaining something, or the process of showing something to be true. |


例句：

The students held a demonstration against the new tuition fees. = 学生们举行示威抗议新的学费。

The teacher gave a demonstration of how to use the new software. = 老师演示了如何使用新软件。


近义词：protests, displays, exhibitions, proofs, shows, illustrations

反义词：concealment, suppression, refutation


词性：名词
