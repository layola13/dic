/dʒiːz/| int.|哎呀；天哪；我的天|An exclamation expressing surprise, annoyance, or dismay.|例句：Gees, that was close! 哎呀，真是太险了！|近义词：Gosh, wow, my goodness, oh my|反义词：无


USAGE->
This word is an interjection, and as such, it doesn't conjugate like verbs or nouns.  Therefore, grammatical tenses don't apply.


PHRASE->
Gees, I'm tired! = 哎呀，我太累了！
Gees, that's a lot of work! = 天哪，这工作太多了！
Gees, what a mess! = 我的天，真是一团糟！
Gees, look at that! = 哎呀，你看那个！
Gees, you scared me! = 哎呀，你吓到我了！
