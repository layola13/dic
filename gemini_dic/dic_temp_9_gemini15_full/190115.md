/ˌsæŋɡwɪˈnɪtiz/| n. |乐观；血气；自信；充满希望|  A feeling of optimism and confidence; cheerful hopefulness.  The word often refers to a confident and optimistic outlook, particularly in the face of adversity. It can also, less commonly, refer to the blood. |例句：Despite the setbacks, she maintained her sanguinities and continued to work towards her goal. 尽管遭遇挫折，她仍然保持乐观，继续为自己的目标努力。

例句：His sanguinities about the future were infectious, inspiring those around him. 他对未来的乐观感染了周围的人。


近义词：optimism, hopefulness, confidence, cheerfulness

反义词：pessimism, despair, hopelessness, despondency


词源:  The word "sanguinity" ultimately derives from the Latin word "sanguis," meaning "blood."  In ancient humoral theory, blood was associated with a cheerful and optimistic temperament.  The connection between blood and sanguinity highlights the historical belief that a person's physical constitution influenced their personality and emotional state.
