left-wings:/ˈlɛft wɪŋz/| n.|左翼；左派|The term "left-wing" refers to the left side of the political spectrum, generally associated with socialist, communist, or progressive ideologies.  These ideologies typically advocate for social equality, economic redistribution, and government intervention to address social and economic issues. |例句：Many left-wing politicians support policies aimed at reducing income inequality. 许多左翼政治家支持旨在减少收入不平等的政策。

例句：The left-wing party gained significant support in the recent election. 左翼政党在最近的选举中获得了广泛的支持。|近义词：leftists, radicals, progressives|反义词：right-wing, conservatives
