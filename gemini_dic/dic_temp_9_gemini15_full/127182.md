naturally:/ˈnætʃrəli/| adv.|自然地，当然|In a way that is expected or usual; without artificiality or effort.|例句：He naturally gravitated towards the other musicians. 他自然而然地被其他音乐家吸引。|例句：Naturally, she was upset when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息后自然很不安。|近义词：automatically, spontaneously, inherently|反义词：unnaturally, artificially, forcedly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She naturally speaks French. = 她自然说法语。
Things naturally fall to the ground. = 物体自然会落到地上。

一般过去时:
He naturally assumed I was lying. = 他自然而然地认为我在撒谎。
She naturally reacted with anger. = 她自然而然地生气了。

现在完成时:
It has naturally evolved over time. = 它自然地随着时间推移而进化了。
The situation has naturally improved. = 局势自然有所改善。

过去完成时:
I naturally had assumed he would be there. = 我自然而然地以为他会来。
She naturally had felt apprehensive. = 她自然感到不安。

将来时:
He will naturally want to know the truth. = 他自然会想知道真相。
Things will naturally sort themselves out. = 事情自然会解决。

虚拟语气:
If you were naturally gifted, you would succeed. = 如果你天赋异禀，你就会成功。
I wish I were naturally more outgoing. = 我希望我天生外向些。

被动语态:
The problem was naturally solved. = 问题自然得到了解决。
The process is naturally regulated. = 这个过程是自然调节的。

疑问句:
Would you naturally do that? = 你会自然而然地那样做吗？
Does she naturally gravitate towards leaders? = 她是否自然而然地被领导者吸引？

否定句:
It didn't naturally occur to me. = 我没有自然而然地想到。
The solution wasn't naturally apparent. = 解决方案并非显而易见。
