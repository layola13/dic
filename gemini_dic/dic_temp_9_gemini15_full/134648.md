nonmalleable: /ˌnɒnˈmælɪəbl/ | adj. |不易改变的；不可塑的；不屈挠的 | Not easily influenced or changed in shape; inflexible.  The term signifies resistance to external forces attempting to modify form, characteristics, or behavior. |

例句：

The company's nonmalleable stance on the issue resulted in a prolonged strike.  公司的立场强硬，导致了旷日持久的罢工。

His nonmalleable personality made it difficult to work with him. 他不易改变的性格使得和他共事很困难。


近义词：inflexible, rigid, unyielding, stubborn, intractable

反义词：malleable, flexible, pliable, adaptable, compliant


词性：形容词
