menstruate:/ˈmɛnstruˌeɪt/| v.|月经来潮；行经|To experience menstruation; the regular discharge of blood from the uterus of a woman who is not pregnant.|例句：She started to menstruate at the age of thirteen. 她十三岁开始来月经。|例句：Many women experience discomfort during menstruation. 许多女性在月经期间会感到不适。|近义词：have one's period, bleed (in this context)|反义词：N/A|动词
