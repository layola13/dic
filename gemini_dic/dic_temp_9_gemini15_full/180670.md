regrease:/ˌriːˈɡriːs/| vt.|重新涂油脂；再加润滑油|To grease again or anew.  The process of applying grease to something again, typically machinery or equipment to ensure smooth operation and prevent wear and tear.|例句：The mechanic had to regrease the car's wheel bearings.  机械师不得不重新给汽车的轮轴承涂抹润滑油。|例句：We regreased the hinges to quiet the squeaking door. 我们重新给铰链涂抹润滑油来消除门的吱吱声。|近义词：re-lubricate, oil, grease|反义词：dry, de-grease
