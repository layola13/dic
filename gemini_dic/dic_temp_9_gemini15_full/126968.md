narrowest:/ˈnæroʊɪst/| adj. |最窄的，最狭窄的 |The adjective "narrowest" is the superlative form of "narrow," meaning having a small distance from one side to the other. It describes something that is the least wide or the most slender in comparison to others.  |例句：The narrowest street in the old town was barely wide enough for a car to pass. 老城里最窄的街道勉强够一辆车通过。

The narrowest gap between the two mountains was only a few meters wide.  两座山之间最窄的缝隙只有几米宽。|近义词：thinnest, slimmest, tightest |反义词：widest, broadest, amplest
