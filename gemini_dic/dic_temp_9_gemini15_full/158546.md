plesiosaur:/ˈpliːsiəsɔːr/| n. | 蛇颈龙 | A plesiosaur is a large extinct marine reptile that lived in the Mesozoic Era.  They were characterized by their long necks, small heads, flippers, and streamlined bodies.  | Example: The fossil discovered was identified as a plesiosaur, a creature from the age of dinosaurs.  这条被发现的化石被鉴定为蛇颈龙，一种来自恐龙时代的生物。 |近义词： marine reptile (though not all marine reptiles are plesiosaurs) |反义词：N/A
