USAGE->
This word is an adjective, therefore it doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".  It describes a noun.

Example Sentences:

Attributive Adjective (modifying a noun):

They have a nonmarital relationship.  他们有婚外关系。

Predicative Adjective (following a linking verb):

Their living arrangement is nonmarital. 他们的生活方式是非婚的。
