limitation:/ˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 限制；局限性；缺点 | A limitation is a restriction or constraint that prevents something from being done or achieved.  It can refer to a shortcoming or defect in something. |  The main limitation of this study is its small sample size. 这个研究的主要局限性在于样本量太小。

There are limitations to what we can achieve with current technology.  用当前的技术，我们所能取得的成就也是有限的。 | restriction, constraint, drawback, handicap | advantage, strength, potential |  名词
