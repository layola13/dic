glaring:/ˈɡlerɪŋ/| adj.|耀眼的；显眼的；令人吃惊的|extremely obvious or conspicuous; harshly bright|The glaring sun made it difficult to see.  耀眼的阳光使得难以看清。

The glaring errors in the report were unacceptable. 报告中明显的错误是不可接受的。|obvious, blatant, conspicuous, striking|subtle, inconspicuous, understated|形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sun is glaring today.  今天阳光耀眼。
His mistake is glaring to everyone. 他的错误对每个人都很明显。


一般过去时:
The light glared harshly last night. 昨晚灯光刺眼。
His anger glared at me. 他的怒火瞪着我。


现在完成时:
The problem has been glaring for weeks.  这个问题已经很明显好几个星期了。
The error has glared in my face since the beginning.  这个错误从一开始就非常明显。


过去完成时:
The flaw had been glaring in the design before the launch. 在产品发布之前，设计缺陷就已经很明显了。
The omission had glared throughout the entire project.  在整个项目中，遗漏之处一直很明显。


将来时:
The consequences will be glaring if we fail to act. 如果我们不采取行动，后果将非常严重。
The results will glare at us if we ignore the warnings. 如果我们忽视警告，结果将非常明显。


虚拟语气:
If the error were more glaring, they would have noticed it. 如果错误更明显，他们就会注意到。
If the truth were glaring, they couldn't deny it. 如果真相显而易见，他们就不能否认。


被动语态:
The mistakes were glaring in his work.  他的作品中错误很明显。
The inconsistencies were glaring throughout the document.  整个文件中前后矛盾之处非常明显。


疑问句:
Is the contrast glaring enough? 对比度够明显吗？
Is the problem glaring? 问题很明显吗？


否定句:
The flaw wasn't glaring at the time.  当时缺陷并不明显。
The solution wasn't glaring.  解决方案并不明显。


PHRASE->
a glaring omission  明显的遗漏

a glaring example  明显的例子
