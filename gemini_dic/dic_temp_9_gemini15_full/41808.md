corticifer:/kɔːrˈtɪsɪfər/ | n. |皮层形成者；树皮生产者|A corticifer is an organism, typically a plant, that produces or forms bark or cork.  It refers to the agent or process responsible for the development of the outer protective layer of a plant stem or root. The term emphasizes the production of cortex (bark) rather than simply its presence. |Example:  The cork oak is a well-known corticifer, prized for its thick, easily harvested bark.  =栓皮栎是著名的皮层形成者，因其厚实的、易于收获的树皮而倍受珍视。|近义词：bark producer, cork producer |反义词：无
