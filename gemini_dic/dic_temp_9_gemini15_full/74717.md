freemen:/ˈfriːmən/| n.|自由民，自由人|A freeman is a person who is free from slavery or serfdom, or who enjoys certain rights and privileges.|例句：In medieval times, freemen enjoyed greater rights than serfs. 中世纪时期，自由民比农奴享有更多的权利。|例句：The newly freed slaves became freemen and could own property. 新获得自由的奴隶成为自由民，可以拥有财产。|近义词：free person, liberated person|反义词：slave, serf
