dement:/dɪˈment/| v.|使发疯；使精神错乱；使衰弱|To cause someone to lose their mind; to drive someone mad; to weaken or impair someone's mental faculties.  |The stress of the situation demented him. 压力使他精神错乱了。|The disease slowly demented her, robbing her of her memory and cognitive abilities.  这种疾病慢慢地使她精神错乱，夺走了她的记忆力和认知能力。|近义词：unhinge, madden, craze, derange |反义词：soothe, calm, restore
