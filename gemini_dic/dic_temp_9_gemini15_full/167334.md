pretransportation: /ˌpriːˌtrænspɔːrˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 预运输，运输前的准备 | The activities and processes that occur before the actual transportation of goods or people.  This includes planning, scheduling, loading, and other preparatory steps.  | Example: Pretransportation checks are crucial to ensure the safety and efficiency of the shipment. 预运输检查对于确保货运的安全和效率至关重要。  | 近义词: preparation for transportation, pre-shipment activities | 反义词: post-transportation, delivery
