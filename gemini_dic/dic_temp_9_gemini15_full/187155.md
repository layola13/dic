roading:/ˈroʊdɪŋ/| n.|道路建设；道路工程；道路状况|The process of constructing, maintaining, or improving roads.  It can also refer to the condition of roads.|例句：The roading project is expected to be completed by next year. 道路建设项目预计将于明年完工。|例句：The poor roading conditions made the journey difficult. 路况不佳使旅程变得困难。|近义词：road construction, roadworks, paving|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "no roads" or "lack of infrastructure".
