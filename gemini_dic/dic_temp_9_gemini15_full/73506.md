foreseers:/fɔːˈsiːərz/| n. |预见者，预言家|Those who can foresee future events or trends.  People with the ability to predict the future, often based on insight, intuition, or analysis. |例句：The ancient foreseers warned of impending doom.  古代的预言家预言了即将到来的厄运。

The company hired market foreseers to anticipate consumer trends. 公司聘请市场预见者来预测消费者趋势。|近义词：prophets, seers, visionaries, predictors |反义词：ignoramus, shortsighted
