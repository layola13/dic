etude:/eˈtjuːd/|/ˈɛtjuːd/| n. |练习曲；练习作品|A short musical composition, typically for a single instrument, designed to help a performer develop a particular skill or technique.  It often features challenging passages or specific musical ideas. |例句：Chopin wrote many beautiful etudes for the piano.  肖邦为钢琴创作了许多美丽的练习曲。

例句：This etude focuses on rapid scales and arpeggios. 这首练习曲侧重于快速音阶和琶音。


近义词：study, exercise, practice piece |反义词：concert piece, performance piece
