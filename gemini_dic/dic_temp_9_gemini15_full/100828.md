introductions:/ˌɪntrəˈdʌkʃənz/ | n. |介绍；引言；绪论 |The plural form of "introduction," referring to multiple instances of introducing something or someone.  It can refer to introductory remarks, chapters, or the act of introducing people. | Examples:  The book begins with a series of introductions to the main characters. 这本书以一系列对主要人物的介绍开始。 The conference featured several introductions of new technologies.  这次会议介绍了几项新技术。 |近义词：presentations, overviews, prefaces |反义词：conclusions, endings
