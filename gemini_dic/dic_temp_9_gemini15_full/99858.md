intermontane:/ˌɪntərˈmaʊnteɪn/| adj.|山间的，山区间的|Situated or occurring within a mountain range.  The term refers to regions, valleys, or plateaus located between mountain ranges.|例句：The intermontane basin is fertile and well-suited for agriculture. 山间盆地肥沃，非常适合农业发展。

例句：Many intermontane valleys are characterized by their unique ecosystems. 许多山间谷地以其独特的生态系统为特征。


近义词：intra-mountainous, intramontane|反义词：coastal, extramontane


词性：形容词
