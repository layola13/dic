preventabilities:/ˌprɪˌvɛntəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可预防性；可防止性 | The inherent capacity or possibility of being prevented or avoided.  It refers to things that could have been prevented, highlighting the potential for intervention. | Example: The report highlighted the preventabilities of many of the accidents.  这份报告强调了许多事故是可以预防的。The high number of preventable deaths from heart disease is a serious concern.  心血管疾病导致的大量可预防死亡是一个严重的问题。|近义词：avoidabilities, preventions |反义词：inevitabilities, certainties |名词
