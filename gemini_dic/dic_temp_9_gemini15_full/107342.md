lamby:/ˈlæmbi/| n.|小羊羔，可爱的昵称|A diminutive of lamb, often used as a term of endearment.  It can refer to a young sheep or be used as a cute nickname.|例句：The farmer showed us his fluffy lamby.  农夫给我们看他的毛茸茸的小羊羔。|例句：My grandma calls me lamby. 我奶奶叫我小羊羔（昵称）。|近义词：lambkin, darling, sweetie|反义词：N/A
