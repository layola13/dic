flea-marketer:/ˈfliːˌmɑːrkətər/| n. |跳蚤市场商贩，旧货商 |A flea-marketer is someone who sells goods at a flea market, often secondhand or antique items.  They are typically independent vendors who rent space at the market to sell their wares.|例句：The flea-marketer had a wide selection of vintage clothing.  这位跳蚤市场商贩有很多复古服装。|例句：Many flea-marketers set up shop early in the morning to secure the best spots.  许多跳蚤市场商贩一大早就来摆摊，以抢占最好的位置。|近义词：secondhand dealer, antique dealer, vendor |反义词：retailer (in a large store)
