hornwork:/ˈhɔːrnwɜːrk/ | n. |角形工事，棱堡 |A defensive structure in the shape of a horn, typically forming part of a larger fortification.  It consists of two bastions joined by a curtain wall, projecting outwards from a main defensive line.  | Example: The hornwork was designed to protect the vulnerable flank of the main fortress. 角形工事的设计是为了保护主堡垒薄弱的侧翼。|近义词：bastion, redoubt, outwork |反义词：None specifically, but  "open ground" could be considered an antonym in terms of defensive function.
