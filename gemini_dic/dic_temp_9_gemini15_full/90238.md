house-to-house: /ˈhaʊs təˈhaʊs/ | adj. |挨家的；逐户的|  Going from house to house; visiting every house in an area.  |例句：They conducted a house-to-house search for the missing child. 他们挨家挨户地搜寻失踪的孩子。  They did a house-to-house survey to gather opinions on the new policy. 他们挨家挨户地进行调查以收集对新政策的意见。|近义词：door-to-door, from door to door|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "general" or "broad" approaches.|形容词
