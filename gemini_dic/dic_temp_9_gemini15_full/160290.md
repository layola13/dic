pooh:/puː/ | interj. |呸；胡说；不屑；轻蔑| An expression of contempt, disgust, or disbelief.  It can also be used to dismiss something as unimportant or silly. | Examples: "Pooh! That's ridiculous!" "Pooh on your plans!" |近义词：nonsense, rubbish, pshaw, bosh| 反义词：N/A |词性:感叹词


USAGE->
This word is primarily an interjection and doesn't have typical grammatical tenses like verbs.  It expresses an emotion or attitude rather than describing an action.  Therefore, the standard tense conjugations are not applicable.


PHRASE->
Pooh-pooh:  To express contempt or disbelief.
Pooh-poohing: The act of expressing contempt or disbelief.
Not a pooh: Not in the slightest, not at all. (This is a less common usage)
That's a pooh!: That's completely untrue!
