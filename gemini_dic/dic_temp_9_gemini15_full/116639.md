mattedly:/ˈmætɪdli/| adv.| 呆滞地；无生气地；沉闷地 | In a dull, lifeless, or spiritless manner.  It describes something done or existing in a way that lacks energy, brightness, or enthusiasm. | 例句：He spoke mattedly, his voice lacking any conviction. 他说话有气无力，声音缺乏任何说服力。


例句：The flowers looked mattedly after a week without water.  一周没有浇水后，花朵看起来枯萎无力。


近义词：dully, listlessly, lifelessly, apathetically, spiritlessly


反义词：vibrantly, energetically, enthusiastically, animatedly


词性:副词
