papoose:/pæˈpuːs/| n. |婴儿；背带；(尤指印第安人的)婴儿背带|A papoose is a baby, especially a Native American baby, often carried in a sling or carrier on the back.  The term can also refer to the sling or carrier itself. |例句：The mother carried her papoose on her back.  母亲背着她的婴儿。
The artist depicted a Native American mother carrying her papoose in a cradleboard. 艺术家描绘了一位印第安母亲在摇篮板上背着她的婴儿。|近义词：baby, infant, child |反义词：adult
