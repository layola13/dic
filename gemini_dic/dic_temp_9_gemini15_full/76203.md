fustigate:/ˈfʌstɪɡeɪt/| vt.|痛打；严厉批评；鞭打|To beat severely; to criticize harshly.|例句：The critic fustigated the author's new novel for its lack of originality. 批评家严厉批评了作者的新小说缺乏原创性。
例句：The angry mob fustigated the thief with sticks and stones. 愤怒的暴民用棍棒和石头痛打小偷。|近义词：beat, thrash, lambaste, castigate|反义词：praise, commend, applaud|动词
