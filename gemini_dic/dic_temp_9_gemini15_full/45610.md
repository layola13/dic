curries:/ˈkʌriz/| n. |咖喱；咖喱菜|Curries are dishes flavored with a blend of spices, typically including turmeric, cumin, coriander, and chili peppers.  They vary widely in taste and ingredients depending on regional and cultural influences. |She made a delicious chicken curry for dinner. 她做了美味的鸡肉咖喱当晚餐。|近义词：stews, dishes|反义词：无
