interstreaking:/ˌɪntərˈstriːkɪŋ/| adj.| 交错的，互相穿插的 |  Describes something that is marked by streaks or lines that intersect or run in different directions.  It implies a complex or interwoven pattern. |例句：The interstreaking of colors in the marble created a beautiful, swirling effect.  大理石中颜色的交错创造了一种美丽而旋转的效果。

The geological formation showed an interesting interstreaking of sedimentary and igneous rock. 地质构造显示出沉积岩和火成岩有趣的相互穿插。


近义词：interwoven, intermingled, interspersed, variegated


反义词：uniform, homogeneous, unvaried


词性:形容词
