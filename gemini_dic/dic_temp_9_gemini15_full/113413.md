lymphadenoma:/ˌlɪmfædəˈnoʊmə/| n. |淋巴腺瘤；淋巴结瘤 |A lymphadenoma is a benign or malignant tumor of a lymph node.  It's a general term and further classification is needed to specify the type of tumor (e.g., lymphoma).| Example: The biopsy revealed a lymphadenoma requiring further investigation.  活检显示为淋巴腺瘤，需要进一步检查。The doctor suspected a lymphadenoma based on the patient's swollen lymph nodes. 医生根据患者淋巴结肿大怀疑是淋巴腺瘤。|近义词：lymph node tumor, lymphoid tumor |反义词：None (it's a descriptive term, not inherently positive or negative)|名词
