demonologer:/ˌdiːməˈnɒlədʒər/ | n. |驱魔师，恶魔学家 |A person who studies or practices demonology.  A demonologist investigates the nature and activities of demons, often in the context of exorcism or spiritual warfare.  |例句：The demonologist performed a ritual to banish the evil spirits from the house.  驱魔师进行了一场仪式，以将邪灵逐出房子。  The renowned demonologist published a new book on the history of demonic possession. 著名的恶魔学家出版了一本关于恶魔附身历史的新书。|近义词：exorcist, demon scholar |反义词：angelologist
