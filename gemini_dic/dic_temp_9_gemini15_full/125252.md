multidiscipline: /ˌmʌltiˈdɪsəplɪn/ | adj. | 多学科的；跨学科的 |  Relating to or involving several different academic disciplines or fields of study.  This term signifies the integration of knowledge and methods from multiple disciplines to address a complex problem or issue.  It emphasizes collaboration and synergy across different fields. |


例句：

The project requires a multidiscipline approach, bringing together engineers, biologists, and economists.  这个项目需要多学科的方法，将工程师、生物学家和经济学家聚集在一起。

A multidiscipline team is crucial for tackling climate change.  一个多学科团队对于应对气候变化至关重要。


近义词：interdisciplinary, cross-disciplinary, transdisciplinary


反义词：single-discipline, uniscientific


词性: adjective
