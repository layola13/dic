globalism:/ˈɡloʊbəlɪzəm/ | n. |全球化 |The policy of extending the influence of a nation throughout the world.  The process of interaction and integration among people, companies, and governments worldwide. |例句：The rise of globalism has led to increased trade and cultural exchange. 全球化的兴起导致了贸易和文化交流的增加。 |例句：Critics of globalism argue that it leads to the exploitation of workers in developing countries. 全球化的批评者认为它会导致发展中国家工人的剥削。|近义词：internationalism, globalization |反义词：nationalism, isolationism, protectionism
