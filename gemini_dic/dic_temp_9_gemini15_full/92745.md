ichthyoid:/ˈɪkθiɔɪd/| adj.|鱼形的，似鱼的|Resembling a fish; fish-like in form or structure.  The term often describes organisms that have evolved to resemble fish, even if they aren't closely related.|例句：The ichthyoid body shape of the extinct reptile helped it swim efficiently.  这种已灭绝的爬行动物鱼形的体型帮助它高效游泳。

例句：The larval stage of this amphibian exhibits distinct ichthyoid features.  这种两栖动物的幼虫阶段呈现出明显的鱼形特征。


近义词：piscine, fishlike|反义词：mammalian, avian
