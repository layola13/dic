retroflexion: /ˌretrəˈflɛkʃən/ | n. | 反屈；反折；(语言)卷舌音 | The bending or turning back of an organ or part.  In linguistics, it refers to a sound produced with the tongue curled back. | Example: The retroflexion of the uterus is a common occurrence during pregnancy. 子宫的反屈在怀孕期间很常见。The pronunciation of the "r" in some Indian languages involves retroflexion.  一些印度语言中“r”的发音涉及卷舌。|近义词：inversion, bending,  curvature (for the physical sense);  (linguistics) apico-postal articulation |反义词：protrusion, extension (for the physical sense); (linguistics)  alveolar articulation
