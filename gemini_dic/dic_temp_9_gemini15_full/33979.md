cistern:/ˈsɪstərn/| n. |蓄水池；水箱|A cistern is a tank or container, usually for storing water.  It can be built into a structure or be a separate unit.|例句：The old house had a cistern in the basement to collect rainwater. 这栋老房子地下室有一个蓄水池用来收集雨水。|例句：The firefighters used the cistern truck to extinguish the fire. 消防员用蓄水车灭火。|近义词：tank, reservoir, container|反义词：N/A
