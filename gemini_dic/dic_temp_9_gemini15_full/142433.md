oscar:/ˈɒskə(r)/| n. |奥斯卡奖；奥斯卡奖得主 |The Academy Award of Merit, informally known as an Oscar, is an award for artistic and technical merit in the American film industry.  It's presented annually by the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences (AMPAS).  |例句：He won an Oscar for Best Actor. 他获得了奥斯卡最佳男演员奖。  |例句：She's an Oscar winner.  她是一位奥斯卡奖得主。|近义词：Academy Award |反义词：无
