performatives:/pərˈfɔːrmətɪvz/ | n. |施为性言语行为；言行一致的行为|  Words or phrases that perform an action when uttered;  utterances that simultaneously describe and perform an act.  They are not merely descriptive statements but rather accomplish something through their very utterance.  Examples include declarations like "I promise," "I apologize," "I name this ship...", and "I pronounce you husband and wife." These words don't just describe a promise, apology, or naming; they constitute the act itself. |  I promise to help you. = 我答应帮助你。  I hereby declare this meeting adjourned. = 我宣布本次会议结束。 | declarations, pronouncements |  descriptive statements | n.
