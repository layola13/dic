intertwines:/ˌɪntərˈtwaɪnz/ | v. | 缠绕；交织；紧密联系 | To twist or twine together; to be closely connected or related. |例句：The trees' branches intertwined overhead, forming a leafy canopy.  树枝在头顶缠绕在一起，形成一个绿叶遮盖。

The fates of the two companies are intertwined. 两家公司的命运紧密相连。


近义词：entwine, interweave, connect, link, blend | 反义词：separate, disconnect, disentangle
