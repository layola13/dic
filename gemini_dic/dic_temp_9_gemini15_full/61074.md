ellipsoid:/ɪˈlɪpsɔɪd/| n.|椭球体|A three-dimensional geometric shape that is a generalization of an ellipse;  it is a quadric surface that is a stretched or compressed sphere.  All plane sections through the center are ellipses, except for one special case where it is a circle.|The Earth is an oblate ellipsoid, slightly flattened at the poles and bulging at the equator. 地球是一个扁椭球体，两极略扁，赤道略鼓。|近义词： spheroid, ovoid|反义词：sphere (in the context of perfect roundness)
