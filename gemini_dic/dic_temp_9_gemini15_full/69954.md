festered:/ˈfɛstərd/| v.|腐烂；恶化；积脓 | To decay and generate pus; to become worse or more intense over time.  |例句：The wound festered and became infected.  伤口溃烂并感染了。  The resentment festered until it exploded into a major argument.  怨恨积聚直到爆发成一场大争吵。|近义词：rot, worsen, deteriorate, suppurate |反义词：heal, improve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wound festers. = 伤口溃烂。
Anger festers in his heart. = 怒火在他心中积聚。

一般过去时:
The cut festered for days. = 伤口溃烂了好几天。
His anger festered after the argument. = 争吵后他的怒火一直未消。

现在完成时:
The problem has festered for too long. = 这个问题已经恶化太久了。
The wound has festered, requiring immediate medical attention. = 伤口已经化脓，需要立即就医。


过去完成时:
The wound had festered before he sought medical help. = 他寻求医疗帮助之前，伤口就已经溃烂了。
The resentment had festered for years before it surfaced. = 怨恨积压多年后才浮出水面。


将来时:
The situation will fester unless addressed immediately. =  如果不立即处理，情况将会恶化。
If left untreated, the infection will fester and spread. = 如果不治疗，感染会恶化并蔓延。


虚拟语气:
If I had cleaned the wound properly, it wouldn't have festered. = 如果我当时好好清洗伤口，它就不会溃烂了。
If he had addressed the issue sooner, the resentment wouldn't have festered. = 如果他早点处理这个问题，怨恨就不会积聚了。


被动语态:
The wound was allowed to fester. = 伤口被任其溃烂。
The problem was allowed to fester, leading to a major crisis. = 问题被任其恶化，导致了重大的危机。


疑问句:
Is the wound festering? = 伤口溃烂了吗？
Is the anger festering inside you? = 你心里积压着怒火吗？


否定句:
The wound didn't fester. = 伤口没有溃烂。
The problem didn't fester because it was addressed promptly. = 问题没有恶化，因为及时得到了处理。


PHRASE->
allow to fester = 任其恶化
let something fester = 放任某事恶化
festering resentment = 积怨
festering wound = 溃烂的伤口
festering anger = 积压的怒火
