gulled:/ɡʌld/| vt.|欺骗，愚弄|To deceive or trick someone.  |例句：He was gulled into buying a worthless painting. 他被骗买了幅毫无价值的画。  |例句：The con artist gulled many people out of their savings.  那个骗子骗走了许多人的积蓄。|近义词：deceived, tricked, cheated, hoodwinked |反义词：enlightened, informed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gulls people with his false promises. = 他用虚假的承诺欺骗人们。
She gulls her friends into doing her work. = 她骗她的朋友帮她做工作。

一般过去时:
The salesman gulled me into buying a car I didn't need. = 销售员骗我买了一辆我不需要的车。
They gulled the old woman out of her life savings. = 他们骗取了老太太一生的积蓄。

现在完成时:
He has gulled countless people throughout his career. = 他职业生涯中已经骗了无数人。
She has been gulled by online scams several times. = 她已经被网络诈骗骗过几次了。

过去完成时:
I realised I had been gulled only after I had signed the contract. = 签完合同后我才意识到自己被骗了。
They discovered that they had been gulled by a fraudulent scheme. = 他们发现自己被一个欺诈计划骗了。

将来时:
I fear he will gull many more people in the future. = 我担心他将来还会骗更多的人。
They will likely gull unsuspecting victims with their elaborate lies. = 他们可能会用精心编造的谎言欺骗毫无戒心的受害者。

虚拟语气:
