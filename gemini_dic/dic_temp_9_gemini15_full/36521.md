colitic:/kəˈlaɪtɪk/| adj.|结肠炎的；患结肠炎的|Relating to or affected by colitis.  Colitis is an inflammation of the colon (large intestine).|例句：He was diagnosed with colitic symptoms after experiencing severe abdominal pain.|近义词：colonic|反义词：None specifically;  antonyms would describe the absence of colitis, such as "healthy", "inflammation-free".|
