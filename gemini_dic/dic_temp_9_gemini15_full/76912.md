gant:/ɡænt/| n. |瘦削的；憔悴的 |extremely thin and bony; haggard.  |He looked gaunt and pale after his illness. 他生病后看起来消瘦苍白。|近义词：emaciated, lean, thin, haggard |反义词：plump, stout, corpulent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The prisoner looks gaunt. = 囚犯看起来很瘦削。
She appears gaunt after a long journey. = 长途旅行后她显得非常憔悴。

一般过去时:
He was gaunt and weak from hunger. = 他因饥饿而消瘦无力。
The refugees were gaunt and exhausted. = 难民们又瘦又累。

现在完成时:
The long fast has made him gaunt. = 长时间的禁食使他变得消瘦。
She has become gaunt since her illness. = 她自从生病后就变得消瘦了。

过去完成时:
He had been gaunt for months before he sought medical help. = 在他寻求医疗帮助之前，他已经消瘦了几个月。
She had become gaunt from worry and lack of sleep. = 她因担心和缺乏睡眠而变得憔悴。

将来时:
He will likely be gaunt by the end of the hike. = 到徒步旅行结束时，他可能会变得很瘦。
She will look gaunt if she doesn't eat properly. = 如果她不吃得当，她会看起来很憔悴。

虚拟语气:
If he had eaten properly, he wouldn't be so gaunt. = 如果他吃得好，他就不会这么瘦了。
If she hadn't worked so hard, she wouldn't look so gaunt. = 如果她没有那么努力工作，她就不会看起来这么憔悴了。

被动语态:
His appearance was made gaunt by the disease. = 他的外貌因疾病而变得憔悴。
Her frame was rendered gaunt by starvation. = 她因饥饿而变得骨瘦如柴。

疑问句:
Is he gaunt because of his illness? = 他是因为生病才这么瘦的吗？
Does she look gaunt to you? = 她看起来很憔悴吗？

否定句:
He is not gaunt; he's just thin. = 他不瘦，只是有点瘦。
She doesn't look gaunt; she's just tired. = 她看起来并不憔悴，只是累了。
