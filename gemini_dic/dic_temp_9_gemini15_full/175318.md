raisers:/ˈreɪzərz/| n. |筹款人；饲养者；提升者|People or organizations that collect money for a cause; those who breed or raise animals; things that lift or elevate something.|例句：The charity relies heavily on its dedicated raisers.  慈善机构非常依赖其敬业的筹款人。

The farmers are experienced cattle raisers. 这些农民是经验丰富的养牛人。


近义词：fundraisers, breeders, elevators|反义词：receivers(in the context of money), destroyers(in the context of raising something)|词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The charity raisers collect donations every year. = 慈善机构的筹款人员每年都会募捐。
Experienced horse raisers carefully select their breeding stock. = 经验丰富的养马人会仔细挑选他们的种马。

一般过去时:
The raisers successfully raised $1 million for the hospital. = 筹款人成功为医院筹集了100万美元。
The farmers raised prize-winning pigs last year. = 农民去年养出了获奖的猪。

现在完成时:
They have raised significant funds for cancer research. = 他们已经为癌症研究筹集了大量资金。

过去完成时:

The breeders had raised the horses for years before selling them. = 饲养员在出售这些马之前已经养了多年。

They will raise chickens on their new farm. = 他们将在他们的新农场里养鸡。

虚拟语气:
