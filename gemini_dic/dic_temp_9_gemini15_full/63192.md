entangles:/ɪnˈtæŋɡlz/ | vt. |使纠缠；使复杂化 |To twist together in a confusing mass; to involve in difficulties. |例句：The fishing net entangled the swimmer.  渔网缠住了游泳者。

The political situation is further entangled by the economic crisis.  经济危机使政治局势更加复杂。|近义词：intertwine, involve, complicate |反义词： disentangle, simplify |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The vines entangle the trees. = 藤蔓缠绕着树木。
He entangles himself in complex arguments. = 他把自己卷入了复杂的争论中。

一般过去时:
The rope entangled my feet. = 绳子缠住了我的脚。
She entangled herself in a web of lies. = 她把自己卷入了一个谎言的圈套中。

现在完成时:
The fisherman has entangled his net in some seaweed. = 渔夫的渔网缠上了海藻。
The scandal has entangled several prominent politicians. = 这场丑闻已经牵涉到几位著名的政治家。

过去完成时:
He had entangled himself in a difficult situation before he realized it. = 他在意识到之前就已经把自己卷入了一个困境。
The detective had entangled the suspect in his own web of lies. = 侦探已经让嫌疑犯陷入了他自己编织的谎言之网。

将来时:
The strong wind will entangle the kite in the treetops. = 大风会把风筝缠在树梢上。
His careless actions will entangle him in legal troubles. = 他粗心大意的行为将会使他陷入法律纠纷。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't been entangled in that project, I would have finished my book. = 如果我没有卷入那个项目，我就已经完成了我的书。
If he had not entangled himself with those people, he would be in a better position now. = 如果他没有和那些人纠缠不清，他现在的情况会更好。

被动语态:
The swimmer was entangled in the fishing net. = 游泳者被渔网缠住了。
The project was entangled in bureaucratic red tape. = 这个项目被官僚主义的繁文缛节所拖累。

疑问句:
Is the wire entangled with the others? = 电线和其他电线缠绕在一起了吗？
Are you sure you're not entangled in something illegal? = 你确定你没有卷入什么非法活动吗？

否定句:
The climber was not entangled by the ropes. = 登山者没有被绳索缠住。
He tried not to entangle himself in office politics. = 他尽量避免卷入办公室政治。
