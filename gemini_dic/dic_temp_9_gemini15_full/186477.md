词源:

* **Richmond:** From the Norman French "Richmund,"  meaning "rich meadow."  It's a place name ultimately deriving from the Old English elements "ric" (powerful, rich) and "mund" (protection, guardian).

* **upon:** A preposition indicating location or position.  Its origin is Old English.

* **Thames:**  The name of the river is of uncertain origin, with several proposed etymologies from various ancient languages, none definitively proven.  It's likely pre-Indo-European in origin.
