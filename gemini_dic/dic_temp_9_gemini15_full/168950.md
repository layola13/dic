profiler:/ˈproʊfaɪlər/| n. |画像绘制者；分析器；性能分析器 |A profiler is a person who creates profiles, often of criminals, but also of other individuals.  It can also refer to a tool that analyzes the performance of software or hardware. |例句：The police profiler created a detailed profile of the suspect.  警察画像绘制者制作了嫌疑人的详细画像。|例句：The software profiler identified a bottleneck in the database. 软件性能分析器识别出数据库中的瓶颈。 |近义词：analyzer, debugger, tracer |反义词：N/A
