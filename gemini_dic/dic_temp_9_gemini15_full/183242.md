repressively:/rɪˈprɛsɪvli/| adv. |压制地；压抑地；残酷地|In a way that suppresses or restricts something, often in a cruel or unfair manner.  It implies a forceful and often unjust control over thoughts, feelings, or actions.|The government ruled repressively, silencing dissent and suppressing freedom of speech.  政府实行高压统治，压制异见，扼杀言论自由。|oppressively, restrictively, tyrannically |liberally, permissively, tolerantly |副词
