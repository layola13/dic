campestral:/kæmˈpɛstrəl/ | adj. |田园的；乡村的；野生的| Relating to or characteristic of the fields or countryside; rustic.  The word evokes a sense of simplicity, natural beauty, and rural life.  It often implies a connection with agriculture or pastoral life. | 例句：The painting depicted a campestral scene of farmers working in the fields. 这幅画描绘了农民在田间劳作的田园风光。

The wildflowers added a touch of campestral charm to the garden. 野花为花园增添了一丝乡村的魅力。


近义词：rural, rustic, pastoral, bucolic | 反义词：urban, metropolitan, city


词源：From Latin *campestris, from campus "plain, field".  The suffix "-tral" is of uncertain origin, possibly from Latin -alis, indicating an adjective.
