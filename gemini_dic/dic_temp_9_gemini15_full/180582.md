registered:/ˈrɛdʒɪstərd/| adj. |已登记的；注册的；已注册的；挂号的 |Officially recorded or entered in a register or other official list.  |例句：He is a registered voter. 他是注册选民。|例句：This is a registered trademark. 这是一个注册商标。|近义词：recorded, enrolled, listed, entered |反义词： unregistered, unrecorded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is registered in Delaware. = 这家公司在特拉华州注册。
He registers his car every year. = 他每年都登记他的汽车。


一般过去时:
She registered for the course last week. = 她上周注册了这门课程。
They registered their complaint with the manager. = 他们向经理登记了他们的投诉。


现在完成时:
I have registered for the marathon. = 我已经报名参加马拉松比赛了。
Have you registered your dog yet? = 你已经给你的狗登记了吗？


过去完成时:
He had registered his dissatisfaction before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经表达了他的不满。
She had already registered her vote before the polls closed. = 在投票站关闭之前，她已经投票了。


将来时:
I will register for the class tomorrow. = 我明天将注册这门课。
They will register their protest publicly. = 他们将在公开场合登记他们的抗议。


虚拟语气:
If I registered early, I would get a discount. = 如果我早点注册，我会得到折扣。
I wish I had registered for the conference. = 我真希望我报名参加了这个会议。


被动语态:
The parcel was registered mail. = 这包裹是挂号邮件。
The trademark has been registered for many years. = 这个商标已经注册多年了。


疑问句:
Is your company registered? = 你的公司注册了吗？
Are you registered to vote? = 你登记投票了吗？


否定句:
The product was not registered. = 该产品未注册。
He didn't register his complaint. = 他没有登记他的投诉。
