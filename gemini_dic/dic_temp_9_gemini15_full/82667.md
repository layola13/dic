guesses:/ˈɡɛsɪz/| n. |猜测；估计|Plural of guess.  Refers to multiple attempts at guessing something.  It can also refer to the answers given in multiple guessing attempts.|The judge listened to the guesses of the contestants.|近义词：conjectures, speculations, estimations|反义词：certainties, facts

一般现在时:
His guesses are usually quite accurate. = 他的猜测通常相当准确。
What are your guesses for the lottery numbers? = 你对彩票号码的猜测是什么？

一般过去时:
Her guesses were all wrong. = 她的猜测都错了。
The detective considered all the witness's guesses. = 侦探考虑了所有证人的猜测。

现在完成时:

They have submitted their guesses for the quiz. = 他们已经提交了他们的测验答案。


过去完成时:

疑问句:
What are your guesses on the outcome of the election? = 你对选举结果有什么猜测？

否定句:
