overintensifying: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈtɛnsɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 过度强化；过度强调 | The act of excessively increasing the intensity or emphasis of something.  It implies an exaggeration or distortion beyond what is necessary or appropriate. |  例句：His overintensifying of the minor flaws in the report overshadowed its overall strengths.  他的报告中对一些小瑕疵的过度强调掩盖了报告的整体优点。


例句：The political campaign was criticized for its overintensifying of negative aspects of the opponent's record.  这场政治运动因其过度强调对手劣迹而受到批评。


近义词：exaggerating, overemphasizing, hyperbolizing, amplifying excessively


反义词：downplaying, understating, minimizing, de-emphasizing


词性: Verb (present participle)
