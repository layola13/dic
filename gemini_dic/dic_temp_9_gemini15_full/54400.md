disimprison:/ˌdɪsɪmˈprɪzən/ | vt. |释放；使自由|To release from imprisonment; to set free.  The word combines the prefix "dis-" (meaning "away from" or "opposite of") with "imprison."  It emphasizes the act of removing someone from confinement.|例句：The prisoner was disimprisoned after serving his sentence.  犯人服刑期满后被释放了。 The revolutionary movement aimed to disimprison the minds of the people.  革命运动旨在解放人们的思想。|近义词：release, liberate, free, emancipate |反义词：imprison, incarcerate, confine, restrain |动词
