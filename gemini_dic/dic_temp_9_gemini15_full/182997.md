replenishes:/rɪˈplɛnɪʃɪz/| v. |补充；再装满；重新供应|To fill something up again; to restore something to its former level or condition. |例句：The rain replenished the reservoir.  雨水补充了水库的水位。
例句：We need to replenish our stock of coffee. 我们需要补充我们的咖啡库存。|近义词：restock, refill, replenish, restore|反义词：deplete, exhaust, empty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store replenishes its stock daily. = 这家商店每天补充库存。
She replenishes her water bottle after each run. = 她每次跑步后都会补充水瓶里的水。

一般过去时:
He replenished the sugar bowl after breakfast. = 早餐后，他补充了糖罐里的糖。
The farmer replenished the field with fertilizer. = 农民用肥料补充了田地的养分。

现在完成时:
They have replenished the emergency supplies. = 他们已经补充了紧急物资。
The company has replenished its funds after a successful quarter. = 公司在成功的一个季度后补充了资金。

过去完成时:
She had replenished the bird feeder before leaving for vacation. = 她在度假前已经补充了喂鸟器的食物。
The team had replenished their energy with a quick break. = 团队在短暂休息后补充了能量。

将来时:
We will replenish the flower pots with fresh soil next week. = 我们下周将用新鲜的土壤补充花盆。
The government will replenish the national reserves. = 政府将补充国家储备。

虚拟语气:
If we replenished our supplies now, we wouldn't run out later. = 如果我们现在补充物资，以后就不会用完了。
I wish I had replenished my gas tank before the road trip. = 我真希望在公路旅行前我已经加满了油箱。

被动语态:
