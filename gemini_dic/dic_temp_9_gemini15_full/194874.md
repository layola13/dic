semi-cylindric: /ˌsɛmiˈsɪlɪndrɪk/ | adj. | 半圆柱形的 |  Having the shape of half a cylinder.  It describes an object that is curved like half a cylinder, with a flat base and a semicircular cross-section. |


例句：

The semi-cylindric tank held a significant amount of water.  = 半圆柱形水箱装满了大量的水。

The architect designed a semi-cylindric roof for the building. = 建筑师为建筑物设计了一个半圆柱形的屋顶。


近义词：half-cylindrical, semi-circular (in cross-section)


反义词：cylindrical, rectangular, square


词性：形容词


词源:

* **semi-**:  From Latin "semi-", meaning "half".

* **cylindric**: From Greek "kylinros" (κύλινδρος), meaning "roller" or "cylinder", ultimately related to "kyliein" (κυλίειν), meaning "to roll".  The suffix "-ric" is an adjectival suffix indicating "relating to" or "characterized by".
