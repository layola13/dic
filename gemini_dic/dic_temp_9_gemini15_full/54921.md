disposer's: /dɪˈspoʊzərz/ | n. | 处理者；处置者；处理设备的所有者 | The person or entity that disposes of something.  It often refers to the owner or operator of a waste disposal system. |

例句：

The disposer's responsibility is to ensure waste is handled safely and according to regulations. = 处理者的责任是确保废物得到安全处理并符合规定。

The city's waste disposer's contract is up for renewal next year. = 该市的废物处理商的合同将于明年到期续签。


近义词：handler, operator, manager, owner

反义词：recipient (in the context of receiving waste, not disposing of it)

词性:名词
