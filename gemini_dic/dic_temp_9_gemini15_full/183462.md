republish:/ˌriːˈpʌblɪʃ/| vt.|再版；重新出版|To publish something again, often with revisions or in a new format.|例句：The book was republished ten years after its initial release. 这本书在其初版十年后重新出版了。|例句：They republished the article with corrections. 他们更正后重新发表了这篇文章。|近义词：reissue, reprint|反义词：suppress, withdraw


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They republish the newspaper daily. = 他们每天都重新出版报纸。
The company republishes classic novels. = 这家公司重新出版经典小说。

一般过去时:
The author republished his first novel last year. = 作者去年重新出版了他的第一部小说。
The magazine republished the interview with the star. = 这本杂志重新刊登了对这位明星的采访。

现在完成时:
They have republished the book several times. = 他们已经重新出版了这本书好几次了。
The publisher has republished the encyclopedia with updated information. = 出版商已经用更新的信息重新出版了这部百科全书。

过去完成时:
They had republished the book before the author passed away. = 在作者去世之前，他们已经重新出版了这本书。
The company had republished the article after receiving many requests. = 在收到许多请求后，该公司已经重新发表了这篇文章。

将来时:
They will republish the book next year. = 他们明年将重新出版这本书。
The publisher will republish the bestseller with a new cover. = 出版商将用新的封面重新出版这部畅销书。

虚拟语气:
If they republished the book, it would sell well. = 如果他们重新出版这本书，它会卖得很好。
I wish they had republished the article sooner. = 我希望他们早点重新发表这篇文章。

被动语态:
The book was republished last month. = 这本书上个月重新出版了。
The article is being republished in several journals. = 这篇文章正在几家期刊上重新发表。

疑问句:
Will the book be republished? = 这本书会重新出版吗？
Has the article been republished yet? = 这篇文章已经重新发表了吗？

否定句:
They did not republish the book this year. = 他们今年没有重新出版这本书。
The company will not republish the magazine. = 公司不会重新出版这本杂志。


PHRASE->
republish a book = 再版一本书
republish an article = 重新发表一篇文章
republish a journal = 重新出版一本期刊
republish a newspaper = 重新出版一份报纸
republish online = 在线重新发布
