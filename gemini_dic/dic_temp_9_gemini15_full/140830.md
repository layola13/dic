on-sale: /ˌɒnˈseɪl/ /ˌɑːnˈseɪl/ | adj. |正在销售的，廉价出售的 |  Something that is on sale is being offered for sale, often at a reduced price.  It indicates a promotional offer or a clearance sale. | 例句：Many winter coats are on sale now.  现在很多冬装正在打折。

The shoes were on sale for half price. 这双鞋正在半价出售。


近义词：discounted, reduced, marked down | 反义词：full price, regular price


词性：形容词
