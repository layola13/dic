reread:/ˌriːˈriːd/| vt.|再读，重读|To read something again.|例句：I reread the email to make sure I understood it correctly. 我重新阅读了邮件以确保我理解正确。|例句：She reread the chapter to better grasp the concepts. 她重读了那一章以便更好地理解概念。|近义词：revise, review|反义词：skim, glance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I reread the book every year. = 我每年都重读这本书。
They reread the instructions carefully. = 他们仔细地重新阅读了说明书。

一般过去时:
I reread the letter last night. = 我昨晚重读了这封信。
She reread the article before submitting her report. = 她提交报告前又读了一遍文章。

现在完成时:
I have reread that novel several times. = 我已经重读过那本小说好几次了。
He has reread the contract to ensure everything is correct. = 他已经重读了合同以确保一切正确。

过去完成时:
I had reread the manuscript before sending it to the publisher. = 我在把它寄给出版商之前已经重读过手稿了。
She had reread the book before discussing it in class. = 她在课堂上讨论这本书之前已经重读过它了。

将来时:
I will reread the book next week. = 我下周会重读这本书。
They will reread the document before signing it. = 他们在签署文件之前会重读一遍。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would reread your essay. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重读你的文章。
I wish I had reread the instructions more carefully. = 我希望我当时更仔细地重读了说明书。

被动语态:
The letter was reread by the secretary. = 这封信被秘书重读了。
The document will be reread by the legal team. = 文件将由法律团队重读。

疑问句:
Have you reread the chapter yet? = 你重读那一章了吗？
Will they reread the report before the meeting? = 会议前他们会重读报告吗？

否定句:
I did not reread the email. = 我没有重读那封邮件。
She will not reread the book again. = 她不会再重读那本书了。


PHRASE->
reread a book = 重读一本书
reread a document = 重读一份文件
reread an article = 重读一篇文章
reread a chapter = 重读一章
reread something carefully = 仔细地重读某事
