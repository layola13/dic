passableness:/ˈpæsəbəlnəs/| n. |及格；勉强过得去；可通过性|The state or quality of being passable; the degree to which something is acceptable or satisfactory.  It often implies a minimum standard being met, but not necessarily exceeding expectations. |例句：The passableness of the bridge is questionable after the recent storm.  最近的风暴过后，这座桥梁的通车情况令人怀疑。

The passableness of her essay was only just sufficient to earn a passing grade.她的论文勉强及格。|近义词：acceptability, adequacy, sufficiency|反义词：unacceptability, inadequacy, failure
