rucksack:/ˈrʌksæk/| n. |背包，旅行包 |A rucksack is a bag carried on one's back, typically used for carrying supplies during hiking or travel.  |例句：I packed my clothes and essentials into my rucksack before the hike. 我在远足前把衣服和必需品都装进了我的背包里。|近义词：backpack, knapsack|反义词：handbag, suitcase
