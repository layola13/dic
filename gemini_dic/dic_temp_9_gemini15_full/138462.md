novelette:/ˌnɒvəˈlɛt/| n. |中篇小说|A novelette is a piece of fiction longer than a short story but shorter than a novel, typically ranging from 7,500 to 40,000 words.  It often features a more developed plot and characters than a short story but less extensive than a novel.|She wrote a captivating novelette about a young woman's journey to self-discovery. 她写了一篇关于一个年轻女子自我发现之旅的引人入胜的中篇小说。|short story, novella|novel|
