motioning:/ˈmoʊʃənɪŋ/ | v. |示意，做手势 |The act of making gestures or movements to communicate or direct.  It implies a non-verbal communication using body language. |例句：She motioned for me to come closer. 她示意我走近一些。

例句：He motioned towards the door, indicating it was time to leave. 他朝门示意，表示该走了。


近义词：gesturing, signaling, indicating, beckoning

反义词：remaining still, staying silent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He motions to the waiter. 他示意服务员。
She motions for silence. 她示意安静。


一般过去时:
He motioned towards the exit. 他朝出口示意。
She motioned me to follow. 她示意我跟着。


现在完成时:
He has motioned to me several times. 他已经示意我几次了。
She has motioned for help. 她已经示意求助。


过去完成时:
He had motioned for assistance before collapsing. 他在倒下之前示意过需要帮助。
She had motioned to her friends to leave.  她已经示意她的朋友们离开。


将来时:
He will motion for a taxi. 他将示意叫一辆出租车。
She will motion to the audience to applaud. 她将示意观众鼓掌。


虚拟语气:
If he motioned, I would understand. 如果他示意，我会明白。
I wish he had motioned sooner. 我希望他早点示意。


被动语态:
The audience was motioned to take their seats. 观众被示意就座。
The children were motioned into the classroom. 孩子们被示意进入教室。


疑问句:
Was he motioning to you? 他是在对你示意吗？
What was she motioning about?  她在示意什么？


否定句:
He didn't motion to anyone. 他没有示意任何人。
She didn't motion for help. 她没有示意求助。
