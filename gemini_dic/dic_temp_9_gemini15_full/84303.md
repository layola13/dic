half-way:/ˌhɑːfˈweɪ/| adj. adv.|中途的，半路上的；一半|Half-way describes something that is at the middle point of a journey or process.  It can be used as an adjective or an adverb.|例句：We met halfway. 我们在中途相遇。

They were halfway through their project. 他们完成了项目的一半。|近义词：midway, partially|反义词：fully, completely


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is halfway through his book. = 他读到书的一半了。
The project is halfway completed. = 项目完成了一半。


一般过去时:
They stopped halfway up the mountain. = 他们在半山腰停了下来。
She was halfway home when it started to rain. = 她回家一半路程的时候开始下雨了。


现在完成时:
We have only gotten halfway there. = 我们只走了一半的路。
They have halfway finished the job. = 他们完成了工作的一半。


过去完成时:
They had already gone halfway before we realized it. = 我们意识到的时候，他们已经走了一半的路了。
She had halfway written the essay before she ran out of time. = 她快写完论文的一半时，时间不够了。


将来时:
We will be halfway to London by nightfall. = 到天黑的时候，我们将到达伦敦的一半路程。
They will have finished halfway by the deadline. = 他们将在截止日期前完成一半。


虚拟语气:
If we started now, we would be halfway by tomorrow. = 如果我们现在开始，明天就能完成一半。
I wish I had been halfway through my studies by now. = 我希望我现在已经完成一半的学业了。


被动语态:
The work was halfway done when the machine broke. = 机器坏的时候，工作完成了一半。
The race was halfway completed when the accident happened. = 事故发生时，比赛完成了一半。


疑问句:

否定句:
