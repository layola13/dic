grasshoppers:/ˈɡræsˌhɑːpər/| n. |蚱蜢，蝗虫|A grasshopper is a jumping insect with long hind legs.  They are usually green or brown and are herbivores. |例句：The children were fascinated by the grasshopper hopping in the field.孩子们对田里跳跃的蚱蜢很着迷。

例句：A swarm of grasshoppers devastated the farmer's crops. 一群蝗虫毁坏了农民的庄稼。

近义词：locust (蝗虫),  hopper (跳跃昆虫)

反义词：None directly applicable.  (Could consider ant, but not a direct opposite)

词性：名词
