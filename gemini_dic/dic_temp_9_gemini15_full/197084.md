shamefully:/ˈʃeɪmfʊli/| adv.| 可耻地；丢脸地 |  In a shameful manner; disgracefully.  It describes an action or situation that causes shame or disgrace. |例句：He shamefully neglected his duties. 他可耻地怠慢了他的职责。

He shamefully lied to the police. 他可耻地对警察撒谎了。


近义词：disgracefully, dishonorably, ignominiously, scandalously

反义词：honorably, respectably, creditably

词性：副词


词源:

The word "shamefully" derives from the Old English word "scamian," meaning "to be ashamed."  Over time, this evolved into "shame," and the addition of the suffix "-fully" transforms the noun into an adverb, indicating a manner or way of acting.
