formative:/ˈfɔːrmətɪv/ | adj. |形成的；塑造的；构成性的| Having a significant influence on the development or formation of something, especially a person's character or beliefs.  It describes something that shapes or influences the development of something else.|


例句：
His formative years were spent in rural Ireland. | 他在爱尔兰农村度过了塑造他人生观的岁月。

The formative period of a child's life is crucial. | 儿童的形成时期至关重要。


近义词：shaping, developmental, molding, influential

反义词：inconsequential, insignificant, unimportant


词性: 形容词
