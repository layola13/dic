neutron:/ˈnjuːtrɒn/| n. |中子|A neutron is a subatomic particle, symbol n or n⁰, with no net electric charge and a mass slightly larger than that of a proton. It is one of the main constituents of the atomic nucleus.  Neutrons are fundamental to nuclear reactions, including nuclear fission and fusion.| Example:  The atomic nucleus contains protons and neutrons.  原子核包含质子和中子。

The stability of an atom's nucleus depends on the balance between protons and neutrons.  原子的核稳定性取决于质子和中子的平衡。|近义词:nucleon|反义词:proton(in terms of charge)|词性:n.
