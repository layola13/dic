doofuses:/ˈduːfʊsɪz/| n.|傻瓜们，笨蛋们|A group of doofuses are causing trouble.一群傻瓜在惹麻烦。|近义词：idiots, morons, imbeciles|反义词：geniuses, intellectuals|名词


英文解析：Doofus is a slang term for a foolish or stupid person.  The plural form is "doofuses".  It carries a somewhat informal and humorous tone, often used playfully rather than as a serious insult.


例句：

Those doofuses forgot to lock the door again. 那些傻瓜又忘了锁门。

The whole project fell apart because of a few doofuses.  整个计划因为几个笨蛋而失败了。
