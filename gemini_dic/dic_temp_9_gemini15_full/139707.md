oceanology:/ˌoʊʃəˈnɑːlədʒi/ | n. | 海洋学 | The scientific study of the ocean, including its physical and chemical properties, its inhabitants, and its interaction with the atmosphere and land.  | Example:  Oceanology is a vast field encompassing many sub-disciplines. 海洋学是一个包含许多子学科的广阔领域。 |近义词：oceanography | 反义词：无
