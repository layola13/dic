glacier:/ˈɡleɪʃər/| n. |冰川|A glacier is a large mass of ice that moves slowly over land. It is formed from the accumulation and compaction of snow over many years. Glaciers are found in high mountain regions and polar areas.  |Example: The majestic glacier carved the valley into its present shape.  这壮丽的冰川将山谷雕刻成了现在的样子。 The retreating glacier left behind a vast expanse of bare rock.  退缩的冰川留下了一片广阔的裸岩。|近义词：ice sheet, ice cap|反义词：None directly contrasting.  |名词
