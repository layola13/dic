pomwater:/ˈpɒmˌwɔːtər/| n. |番石榴汁；番石榴水|A drink made from the pulp of pomegranates.  It can be sweet or tart depending on the variety of pomegranate used and the added ingredients.|例句：The restaurant served a refreshing pomwater with a hint of lime. 这家餐厅供应清爽的石榴汁，略带酸橙味。  |例句：She made a delicious pomwater by blending fresh pomegranates with a little sugar.|她用新鲜石榴加少量糖混合制成了美味的石榴汁。|近义词：pomegranate juice|反义词：无
