detoxification:/ˌdiːtɒksɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 解毒；排毒 | The process of removing toxic substances or qualities.  It can refer to the body's natural processes or medical interventions. | Example: The detoxification process after a drug overdose can be lengthy and complicated. 解毒过程在药物过量后可能漫长而复杂。  The spa offers a range of detoxification treatments. 水疗中心提供一系列排毒疗法。|近义词：purification, cleansing |反义词：intoxication, poisoning |名词

词源:
The word "detoxification" is derived from the prefix "de-" (meaning "removal of" or "opposite of"), "toxic" (relating to poison or toxins), and the suffix "-fication" (meaning "the process of making").  Therefore, it literally means "the process of removing toxins".
