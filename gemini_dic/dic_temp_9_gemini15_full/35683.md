coble:/ˈkoʊbl/| n. |小渔船，平底船 |A small, flat-bottomed boat, typically used for fishing in shallow water.  Coble boats are often made of wood and are traditionally used on the North East coast of England.|例句：The fishermen repaired their coble after the storm.  渔民们在暴风雨后修理了他们的平底小船。|例句：The coble was ideal for navigating the shallow, rocky coastline. 这艘小船非常适合在浅而多岩石的海岸线上航行。|近义词：boat, dinghy, shallop |反义词：large ship, ocean liner |名词
