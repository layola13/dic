dyable:/ˈdaɪəbl/| adj. | 可染色的 | Capable of being dyed; able to absorb and retain dye.  | Example:  This fabric is dyable in a wide range of colors. 这块布料可以染成各种颜色。  | 近义词：colorable, stainable | 反义词：undyable
