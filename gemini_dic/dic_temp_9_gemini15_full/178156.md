recoilingly: /rɪˈkɔɪlɪŋli/ | adv. | 畏缩地；退缩地；反弹地 |  Describes an action or reaction characterized by recoiling, showing fear, disgust, or aversion. It implies a sudden movement backward or away from something unpleasant or frightening. |


例句：

He stared at the gruesome scene recoilingly. 他畏缩地盯着那可怕的景象。

She recoiled recoilingly from the snake. 她畏缩地避开了那条蛇。


近义词：shrinkingly, shrinkingly, flinchingly, recoiling


反义词：boldly, bravely, confidently


词性：副词
