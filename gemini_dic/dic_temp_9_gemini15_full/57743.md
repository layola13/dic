dreamer:/ˈdriːmər/ | n. |梦想家，空想家 |A person who dreams a lot or spends much time thinking about possibilities. Someone who is impractical and unrealistic. |例句：He's a dreamer, always chasing impossible goals. 他是个梦想家，总是追逐不可能的目标。|例句：She's such a dreamer; she's always off in her own world. 她是个十足的空想家；她总是沉浸在自己的世界里。|近义词：visionary, idealist, fantasist |反义词：realist, pragmatist |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a dreamer. = 他是个梦想家。
She dreams of a better future. = 她梦想一个更美好的未来。


一般过去时:
He was a dreamer in his youth. = 他年轻时是个梦想家。
She dreamed of becoming a doctor. = 她梦想成为一名医生。


现在完成时:
He has always been a dreamer. = 他一直是个梦想家。
She has dreamed of this moment for years. = 她多年来一直梦想着这一刻。


过去完成时:
He had been a dreamer all his life. = 他一生都是个梦想家。
She had dreamed of traveling the world before she settled down. = 在她定居之前，她梦想着环游世界。


将来时:
He will always be a dreamer. = 他将永远是个梦想家。
She will dream of success even when facing challenges. = 即使面临挑战，她也会梦想成功。


虚拟语气:
If I were a dreamer, I would travel the world. = 如果我是一个梦想家，我会环游世界。
I wish I were a dreamer with more courage. = 我希望我是一个更有勇气的梦想家。


被动语态:
(This word doesn't usually appear in passive voice)


疑问句:
Is he a dreamer? = 他是个梦想家吗？
Does she dream often? = 她经常做梦吗？


否定句:
He is not a dreamer; he's a pragmatist. = 他不是梦想家；他是个实用主义者。
She doesn't dream about unrealistic things. = 她不做关于不切实际事情的梦。
