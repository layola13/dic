deadline:/ˈdedlaɪn/| n. |最后期限，截止日期 |A deadline is a point in time by which something must be done.  It's the latest time something is due. |The deadline for the project is Friday.  这个项目的截止日期是星期五。
The deadline for submitting applications has passed. 提交申请的截止日期已过。|due date, closing date | extension, postponement | 名词
