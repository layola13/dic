senhorita:/ˌsɛnjoˈriːtə/| n. |小姐，女士|A polite term of address for an unmarried woman in Portuguese and Spanish.  It is equivalent to "Miss" in English.| Example:  "Boa noite, senhorita." = "Good evening, Miss."|近义词：miss, young lady|反义词： señora (married woman)
