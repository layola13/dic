outrushing: /ˈaʊtrʌʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 向外冲的；涌出的 |  Describes something rushing outwards or forcefully exiting.  It emphasizes the speed and force of the outward movement. |

例句：

The outrushing water from the broken dam caused widespread flooding.  = 破裂的大坝涌出的水造成了大范围的洪水。

There was a sound of outrushing air as the door burst open. = 门猛地打开时，传来一阵向外冲出的气流声。


近义词：effluent, rushing, gushing, surging

反义词：inrushing, incoming


词性：形容词
