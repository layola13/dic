renege:/rɪˈniːɡ/| v.|食言；违约；放弃；|To go back on a promise, undertaking, or agreement.|He reneged on his promise to help us. 他违背了他帮助我们的承诺。|近义词：break one's word, go back on, welsh on|反义词：keep one's word, fulfill, honor


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reneges on his debts regularly. = 他经常赖账。
She often reneges on her promises. = 她经常食言。

一般过去时:
The company reneged on its contract. = 公司违反了合同。
He reneged on the deal at the last minute. = 他在最后一刻毁约了。

现在完成时:
They have reneged on their commitment to the project. = 他们已经违背了对项目的承诺。
She has reneged on her promise to me several times. = 她已经几次违背了她对我的承诺。

过去完成时:
