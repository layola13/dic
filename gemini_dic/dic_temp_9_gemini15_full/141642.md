optimising:/ˈɒptɪmaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 优化，使最优 | To make the best or most effective use of (a resource).  | The company is optimising its supply chain to reduce costs.  这家公司正在优化其供应链以降低成本。 | optimize, refine, improve | worsen, deteriorate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are optimising their website for search engines. = 他们正在优化他们的网站以适应搜索引擎。
We optimise our processes regularly. = 我们定期优化我们的流程。


一般过去时:
The engineer optimised the engine's performance. = 工程师优化了引擎的性能。
She optimised her schedule to fit in more work. = 她优化了她的日程安排以便完成更多工作。


现在完成时:
They have optimised their marketing strategy. = 他们已经优化了他们的营销策略。
We have optimised the software for better speed. = 我们已经优化了软件以提高速度。


过去完成时:
They had optimised the design before the launch. = 他们已经在发布之前优化了设计。
She had optimised her code for maximum efficiency. = 她已经优化了她的代码以达到最大效率。


将来时:
We will optimise the system next week. = 我们下周将优化系统。
They will optimise the production process to increase output. = 他们将优化生产流程以提高产量。


虚拟语气:
If we optimised the algorithm, the results would be better. = 如果我们优化算法，结果会更好。
It would be helpful if they optimised the user interface. = 如果他们优化用户界面，将会有所帮助。


被动语态:
The software is being optimised for mobile devices. = 该软件正在被优化以适应移动设备。
The system was optimised by a team of experts. = 该系统由专家团队进行了优化。


疑问句:
Are you optimising your code for speed or memory? = 你是在优化代码以提高速度还是内存？
Have they optimised the database yet? = 他们已经优化数据库了吗？


否定句:
They did not optimise the database properly. = 他们没有正确地优化数据库。
We haven't optimised the workflow yet. = 我们还没有优化工作流程。


PHRASE->
optimise for: 为...优化
optimise performance: 优化性能
optimise resources: 优化资源
optimise the process: 优化流程
optimise the code: 优化代码
