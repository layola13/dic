reciprocal:/rɪˈsɪprəkəl/ | adj., n. |相互的；互惠的；倒数；|英文解析:existing, done, or given in return; involving mutual actions or feelings.  As a noun, it refers to a mathematical inverse. |例句:The two countries have a reciprocal trade agreement.  = 这两个国家之间有互惠贸易协定。

Their relationship was based on reciprocal respect. = 他们的关系建立在相互尊重的基础上。


The reciprocal of 5 is 1/5. = 5 的倒数是 1/5。


The reciprocal of a fraction is obtained by inverting it. = 分数的倒数通过将其倒置获得。


近义词: mutual, interchangeable, inverse |反义词: unilateral, one-sided


词性: adjective, noun
