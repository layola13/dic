embarks:/ɪmˈbɑːrks/| v.| 开始；着手；乘船（或飞机）旅行 |to begin a journey, especially a long or important one, or to start a new project or activity.  |例句：He embarked on a new career in teaching. 他开始从事新的教学工作。|例句：The ship embarked from Southampton. 这艘船从南安普顿起航。|近义词：begin, commence, start, set out |反义词：finish, conclude, end


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He embarks on a new adventure every year. = 他每年都开始一段新的冒险。
She embarks on a new project each month. = 她每个月都开始一个新的项目。


一般过去时:
They embarked on a long journey to Tibet. = 他们开始了一次去西藏的长途旅行。
He embarked on his writing career at a young age. = 他年轻时就开始了他的写作生涯。


现在完成时:
She has embarked on a challenging career in law. = 她已经开始了一项在法律领域具有挑战性的职业。
He has embarked on several business ventures. = 他已经开始了几个商业冒险。


过去完成时:
Before he died, he had embarked on a final painting. = 在他去世之前，他开始创作最后一幅画。
They had embarked on their journey before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经开始了旅程。


将来时:
We will embark on a cruise next summer. = 我们明年夏天将开始一次邮轮旅行。
They will embark on a research project in the fall. = 他们将在秋季开始一个研究项目。


虚拟语气:
If I had the courage, I would embark on that challenging journey. = 如果我有勇气，我会开始那段充满挑战的旅程。
I wish I could embark on a world tour. = 我希望我能开始环球旅行。


被动语态:
The project was embarked upon with great enthusiasm. = 这个项目一开始就充满了热情。
The voyage was embarked upon despite the stormy weather. = 尽管天气暴风雨，航行还是开始了。


疑问句:
When will they embark on their trip? = 他们什么时候开始他们的旅行？
Has he embarked on the new project yet? = 他已经开始新的项目了吗？


否定句:
He did not embark on his planned trip. = 他没有开始他计划中的旅行。
They haven't embarked on the new business venture. = 他们还没有开始新的商业冒险。
