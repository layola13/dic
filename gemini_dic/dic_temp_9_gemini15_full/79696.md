glowstick: /ˈɡloʊstɪk/ | n. | 荧光棒 | A small, sealed plastic tube containing two chemicals that produce light when mixed; used for illumination or decoration.  | Example: The children waved their glowsticks excitedly during the concert. 孩子们在音乐会上兴奋地挥舞着荧光棒。 |近义词：lightstick, luminescent stick | 反义词：None.
