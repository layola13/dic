precogitating: /ˌpriːˈkɒdʒɪteɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预想；预谋；事先考虑 |  Engaging in the act of thinking beforehand; planning or considering something in advance.  It implies a careful and deliberate process of foresight. |


例句：

He spent hours precogitating his presentation, ensuring every detail was perfect. = 他花了几个小时预先构思他的演讲，确保每一个细节都完美无缺。

The general was precogitating a daring attack on the enemy's stronghold. = 将军正在预谋对敌军据点发动一次大胆的攻击。


近义词：premeditating, planning, contemplating, considering, foreseeing


反义词：improvising, winging it, acting spontaneously


词性: 动词
