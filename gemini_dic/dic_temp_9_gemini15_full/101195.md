inviolableness: /ˌɪnˌvaɪələˈbɪlnəs/ | n. |不可侵犯性，神圣不可侵犯| The state or quality of being inviolable;  the inability to be violated, broken, or infringed upon.  This refers to something that is sacred, protected, or immune from attack or interference. |例句：The inviolableness of the human rights is a fundamental principle of international law.  人类权利的神圣不可侵犯是国际法的一项基本原则。

The inviolableness of the constitution is guaranteed by the supreme court.  宪法的不可侵犯性由最高法院保障。|近义词：inviolability, sanctity, inviolability, immunity, security |反义词：vulnerability, violation, infringement
