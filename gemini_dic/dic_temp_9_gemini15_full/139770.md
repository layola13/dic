octahedron:/ɒkˈteɪdrən/| n. |八面体|A solid geometrical figure with eight faces.  It is a polyhedron with eight faces, twelve edges, and six vertices.  Regular octahedrons have eight equilateral triangles as faces. | Example:  The crystal formed a perfect octahedron.  这个晶体形成了一个完美的八面体。  The game piece was shaped like an octahedron. 游戏棋子是八面体形状的。|近义词：polyhedron|反义词：无
