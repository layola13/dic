presbyterian:/ˌprɛzbɪˈtɪəriən/| adj. n. |长老会的；长老会教徒|Of or relating to Presbyterians or Presbyterianism.  A member of a Presbyterian church.|例句：He is a devout Presbyterian. 他是一位虔诚的长老会教徒。

The Presbyterian Church is known for its governance structure. 长老会教堂以其治理结构而闻名。


近义词：Calvinist (in some contexts)


反义词：None directly;  terms like "Catholic" or "Baptist" represent different denominations, not direct opposites.


词性: adjective, noun
