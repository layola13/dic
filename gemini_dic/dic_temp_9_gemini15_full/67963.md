facilitation:/fəˌsɪlɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 促进；协助；便利 | The act of making something easier or more likely to happen.  It often involves removing obstacles, providing resources, or guiding a process. |  The teacher's facilitation of the group discussion led to a productive exchange of ideas.  老师对小组讨论的促进导致了富有成效的思想交流。| assistance, support, mediation | obstruction, hindrance, impediment |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The facilitator facilitates the meeting effectively. = 促进者有效地促进了会议。
Good communication facilitates understanding. = 良好的沟通促进了理解。

一般过去时:
The workshop facilitated a better understanding of the issue. = 研讨会促进了对该问题的更好理解。
She facilitated the negotiation between the two parties. = 她促进了双方之间的谈判。

现在完成时:
The program has facilitated significant improvements in efficiency. = 该计划促进了效率的显著提高。
They have facilitated the development of new technologies. = 他们促进了新技术的开发。

过去完成时:
The prior preparation had facilitated the smooth running of the event. = 事先的准备工作促进了活动的顺利进行。
He had facilitated numerous successful projects before this one. = 在这个项目之前，他已经促进了许多成功的项目。

将来时:
This new software will facilitate data analysis. = 这款新软件将促进数据分析。
The new policy will facilitate international trade. = 新政策将促进国际贸易。

虚拟语气:
If only we had better resources, we could facilitate progress more quickly. = 如果我们有更好的资源，我们可以更快地促进进展。
I wish the government would facilitate the process of obtaining visas. = 我希望政府能简化签证申请流程。

被动语态:
The project was facilitated by a team of experts. = 该项目由一个专家团队促成。
The agreement was facilitated by a neutral third party. = 协议由中立的第三方促成。

疑问句:
How can we facilitate better communication within the team? = 我们如何才能促进团队内部更好的沟通？
What strategies can be used to facilitate learning? = 可以使用哪些策略来促进学习？

否定句:
Lack of funding did not facilitate the research. = 资金不足没有促进研究。
The bureaucratic process did not facilitate the timely completion of the project. = 官僚程序并没有促进项目的及时完成。
