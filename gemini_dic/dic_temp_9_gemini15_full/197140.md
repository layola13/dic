shanghaiers:/ʃæŋˈhaɪərz/| n. |上海人；在上海居住的人|People who live in or are from Shanghai, China.  The term can refer to both native residents and long-term inhabitants.|例句：Many shanghaiers are proud of their city's rich history and culture.  许多上海人都为他们城市丰富的历史和文化感到自豪。

例句：The shanghaiers I met were very friendly and helpful. 我遇到的上海人都非常友好和乐于助人。


近义词：Shanghainese (adj. & n.)|反义词：无
词性:名词
