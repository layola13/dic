ornithologist:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 鸟类学家 | A scientist who studies birds. Ornithologists study the biology, behavior, and ecology of birds.  |  Dr. Jones, a renowned ornithologist, published a groundbreaking study on bird migration.  琼斯博士，一位著名的鸟类学家，发表了一项关于鸟类迁徙的开创性研究。 | zoologist, avian biologist |  (无明显反义词) | 名词
