overgarrison:/ˌoʊvərˈɡærɪsən/| adj.|驻军过多的；过度防守的|Having more troops stationed than necessary; excessively defended.|The overgarrisoned fortress was vulnerable to internal dissent more than external attack.  过度防守的堡垒更容易受到内部叛乱而不是外部攻击的威胁。|近义词：overmanned, overdefended|反义词：undergarrisoned
