pontic:/ˈpɒntɪk/| n. |桥接的；假牙的；（尤指）桥接的牙齿|A pontic is an artificial tooth that replaces a missing tooth and is part of a dental bridge.  It is usually made of porcelain or metal and is cemented to the abutment teeth.  |例句：The dentist fitted her with a three-unit bridge, including two abutment teeth and one pontic.|近义词：artificial tooth, bridge tooth|反义词：natural tooth
