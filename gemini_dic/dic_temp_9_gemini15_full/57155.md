dowdier:/ˈdaʊdiər/| adj.|更阴沉的，更阴暗的，更破旧的 |More gloomy, somber, or dilapidated.  It suggests a worsening of a previously unpleasant state.|例句：The weather became dowdier as the afternoon progressed.  下午天气变得越来越阴沉。

The old house looked even dowdier after the storm.  暴风雨过后，那栋老房子看起来更破旧了。|近义词：gloomier, drearier, shabbier, more dismal|反义词：brighter, cheerier, sunnier, more cheerful
