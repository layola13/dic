non-swimmer: /ˌnɒnˈswɪmər/ | n. |不会游泳的人| A person who cannot swim.  | Example:  He's a non-swimmer, so he'll stay on the beach. 他不会游泳，所以他会待在沙滩上。|近义词: non-athlete (in the context of swimming), unskilled swimmer |反义词: swimmer


USAGE->

The non-swimmer avoids deep water. = 不会游泳的人避开深水。
He is a non-swimmer and prefers to stay on the shore. = 他不会游泳，更喜欢待在岸边。


一般过去时:
The child was a non-swimmer until he took lessons. = 这个孩子在学习游泳之前不会游泳。
