sarges:/ˈsɑːrdʒɪz/| n. |军士，警长|A sarge is an informal term for a sergeant, typically used in the military or police force.  It's a shortened and somewhat disrespectful version of "sergeant".| Example: "The sarge yelled at us for being late."  军士冲我们迟到而大喊。|近义词：sergeant, corporal (depending on rank) |反义词：private (a lower rank)
