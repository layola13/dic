row:/roʊ/| n.|行，排，列|A horizontal line of objects or people.  A single line in a table, document, or other structured display.|例句：The birds were flying in a row. 鸟儿排成一行飞翔。|例句：Please write your name in the first row. 请在第一行写下你的名字。|近义词：line, series, rank|反义词：column


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The flowers are planted in neat rows. = 花整齐地排列成行种植。
There are ten rows of seats in the auditorium. = 礼堂里有十排座位。

一般过去时:
The soldiers marched in perfect rows. = 士兵们排成整齐的队伍行进。
She planted the seedlings in rows across the garden. = 她把幼苗一排排地种在花园里。

现在完成时:
We have arranged the chairs in rows for the presentation. = 我们已经把椅子按行排列好，准备演示。
They have planted the corn in even rows across the field. = 他们已将玉米均匀地排成行种植在田里。


过去完成时:
They had already arranged the tables in rows before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经把桌子排成行了。
The farmer had sown the seeds in rows the previous week. = 农民在上周已经把种子按行播种了。

将来时:
We will plant the trees in rows along the fence. = 我们将在栅栏旁按行种植树木。
They will arrange the chairs in rows for the concert. = 他们将为音乐会把椅子排成行。


虚拟语气:
If the chairs were in rows, it would be easier to see the stage. = 如果椅子排成行，观看舞台会更容易。
I wish the books were arranged in neat rows on the shelves. = 我希望书能整齐地排放在书架上。

被动语态:
The data is displayed in rows and columns. = 数据以行和列的形式显示。
The vegetables were planted in rows by the farmer. = 蔬菜是由农民按行种植的。

疑问句:
How many rows of desks are there in the classroom? = 教室里有多少排课桌？
Are the books arranged in rows on the shelves? = 书是按行排列在书架上的吗？

否定句:
The cars weren't parked in neat rows. = 汽车没有停放成整齐的行列。
The soldiers didn't march in straight rows. = 士兵们没有排成直线行进。
