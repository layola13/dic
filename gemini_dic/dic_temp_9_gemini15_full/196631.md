settle:/ˈsɛtl/ | v., n. | 解决；定居；安顿；沉淀 | to bring to a conclusion or end; to establish oneself in a place; to become calm or quiet | 近义词：resolve, establish, calm,  | 反义词：agitate, unsettle, disturb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He settles his accounts every month. = 他每月结算账目。
They settle their disputes peacefully. = 他们和平地解决纠纷。


一般过去时:
She settled into her new job quickly. = 她很快适应了新的工作。
The dust settled after the storm. = 风暴过后尘埃落定。


现在完成时:
We have settled the argument. = 我们已经解决了争论。
They have settled in their new home. = 他们已经在新家安顿下来了。


过去完成时:
They had settled the matter before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经解决了这件事。
The family had settled in the countryside for generations. = 这个家庭世世代代定居在乡下。


将来时:
We will settle the bill tomorrow. = 我们明天将结账。
I hope the weather will settle soon. = 我希望天气很快好转。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would settle this problem immediately. = 如果我有更多时间，我会立即解决这个问题。
I wish they would settle down and get married. = 我希望他们能安定下来结婚。


被动语态:
The dispute was settled by arbitration. = 纠纷通过仲裁解决。
The matter has been settled amicably. = 这件事已经友好地解决了。


疑问句:
Has the insurance claim been settled yet? = 保险索赔已经处理了吗？
When will the new immigrants settle down? = 新移民什么时候会定居下来？


否定句:
The problem hasn't been settled yet. = 问题还没有解决。
The argument didn't settle easily. = 争论不容易解决。


PHRASE->
settle down: 安定下来
settle for: 勉强接受
settle in: 安顿下来
settle a score: 报复
settle an account: 结账
