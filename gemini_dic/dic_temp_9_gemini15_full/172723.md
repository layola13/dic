quackle:/ˈkwækəl/| v.|嘎嘎叫，大声地说话|To make a series of short, loud, harsh cries, like a duck; to speak in a loud, harsh, or pompous manner.|例句：The ducks quackled loudly in the pond.  鸭子在池塘里大声嘎嘎叫。

The politician quackled his promises to the cheering crowd.  政治家向欢呼的人群大声宣扬他的承诺。|近义词：quack, croak, chatter|反义词：whisper, murmur
