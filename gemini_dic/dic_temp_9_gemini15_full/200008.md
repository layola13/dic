skeet:/skiːt/| n. |飞碟射击；飞碟；（俚语）炫耀；卖弄|A type of clay pigeon shooting where the targets are thrown into the air.|例句：He's a champion skeet shooter. 他是飞碟射击冠军。  He likes to show off his skeet shooting skills. 他喜欢炫耀他的飞碟射击技巧。|近义词：clay pigeon shooting, trapshooting |反义词：无
