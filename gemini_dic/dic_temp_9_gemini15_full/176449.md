re-litigation:/ˌriːlɪtɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n. | 重审；再次诉讼 | The act of bringing a legal case back to court after it has already been decided.  It often involves arguing the same points again, or presenting new evidence.  Re-litigation can be costly and time-consuming. | Example:  The constant re-litigation of the divorce settlement was draining their resources.  持续不断的离婚协议重审耗尽了他们的资源。  The plaintiff attempted re-litigation based on newly discovered evidence.  原告试图基于新发现的证据再次提起诉讼。 | 近义词：retrial, rehearing, renewed litigation | 反义词：final judgment, settlement  | 名词
