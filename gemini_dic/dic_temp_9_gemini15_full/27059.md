calendulin:/ˌkælənˈdʒuːlɪn/ | n. | 花粉媒介蛋白 | A protein found in pollen that promotes the adhesion of pollen grains to the stigma of a flower, facilitating pollination.  It is also involved in other cellular processes. | Example:  The study investigated the role of calendulin in pollen-stigma recognition.  这项研究调查了花粉媒介蛋白在花粉-柱头识别中的作用。 | 近义词： (No perfect synonyms, as it's a specific protein)  | 反义词：(None) | 名词
