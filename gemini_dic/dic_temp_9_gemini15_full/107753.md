lapels:/ˈlæpəlz/| n.|翻领，驳领|The flaps of cloth that fold back on either side of a coat or jacket.  They are often used for decorative purposes and can also have buttonholes for fastening.|例句：He adjusted his suit lapels before the meeting. 他在会议前整理了他的西装翻领。|例句：The lapels of her jacket were trimmed with silk.  她的夹克翻领饰有丝绸。|近义词：collars (有时，取决于上下文)|反义词：无|名词
