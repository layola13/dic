pinioning: /pɪˈnʌɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 捆绑；束缚；限制 | The act of binding or restraining, especially the wings of a bird.  It can also refer to the act of restricting or limiting something. |例句：The eagle was pinioned, unable to fly.  这只鹰被绑住了翅膀，无法飞行。

The police pinioned the suspect's arms behind his back. 警察把嫌疑人的胳膊反绑在身后。

近义词：restraining, confining, binding, shackling | 反义词：freeing, liberating, releasing | 动词
