chirrupper:/ˈtʃɪrʌpər/| n. |快乐的人，兴高采烈的人，（尤指鸟）欢快地鸣叫者 |A chirrupper is a person who is cheerful and lively, often used to describe someone who is excessively or annoyingly cheerful.  It can also refer to a bird that chirps happily. |例句：The children were all chirrupers after receiving their presents.孩子们收到礼物后都兴高采烈。The little bird was a chirrupper, singing its song all morning. 小鸟叽叽喳喳地叫个不停，一上午都在唱歌。|近义词：laugher, merrymaker, chatterbox (for the person aspect); songbird (for the bird aspect) |反义词：gloom, pessimist, grump
