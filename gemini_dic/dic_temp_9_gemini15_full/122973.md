monadic:/ˈmɒnædɪk/ /məˈnædək/| adj.| 单元的；单子的；单项的 | Relating to or denoting a monad; characterized by unity or singleness.  A monadic function is a function that takes only one argument. In philosophy, monadic refers to a single, indivisible unit. | Example:  The program uses a monadic approach to handle asynchronous operations.  这个程序使用单项方法来处理异步操作。 |近义词：unitary, singular, single |反义词：polyadic, multiple, plural
