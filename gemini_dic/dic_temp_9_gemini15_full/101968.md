isobathic:/ˌaɪsoʊˈbæθɪk/| adj.|等深线的；等深度的|Of or relating to lines on a map connecting points of equal depth below a water surface.  Isobaths are used in bathymetry to represent the underwater topography.|例句：The isobathic map showed a steep drop-off near the coastline. 等深线地图显示海岸线附近急剧下降。 The isobathic chart helped the researchers understand the underwater landscape. 等深线图帮助研究人员了解水下地形。|近义词：isobathal|反义词：None specifically, but words implying uneven depth could be considered antonyms.
