reciprocation:/ˌrɛsɪˈproʊkeɪʃən/ | n. |相互作用；互惠；报答 | The act of giving something in return; mutual exchange.  It implies a sense of balance and fairness in the exchange. |例句：The company's reciprocation of our offer was immediate. 公司立即回应了我们的报价。

例句：His kindness was met with reciprocation. 他的好意得到了回报。


近义词：exchange, interchange, mutuality, requital, return | 反义词：unilateralism, one-sidedness


词源:  From Middle French *reciprocation, from Latin reciprocātiōn-, reciprocātiō "a returning, exchanging," from reciprocāre "to return, reciprocate," from reciprocus "returning, reciprocal," from re- "back" + pro "forth" + -cus adjectival suffix.
