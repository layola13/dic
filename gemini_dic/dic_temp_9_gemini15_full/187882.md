rost:/roʊst/|n.|烤肉，烘烤|Roasted meat is a popular dish. 烤肉是一道很受欢迎的菜。|近义词：roast meat, grilled meat|反义词：boiled meat

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He roasts chicken every Sunday. = 他每个星期天都烤鸡。
She roasts vegetables for dinner. = 她晚餐烤蔬菜。

一般过去时:
I roasted a turkey for Thanksgiving. = 我感恩节烤了一只火鸡。
They roasted marshmallows around the campfire. = 他们在营火旁烤棉花糖。

现在完成时:
We have roasted many chickens this year. = 今年我们烤了很多鸡。
She has roasted potatoes several times this week. = 她本周已经烤了几次土豆。

过去完成时:
They had roasted the pig before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经烤好了猪。
He had roasted coffee beans before grinding them. = 他在研磨咖啡豆之前已经烤过了它们。

将来时:
I will roast a lamb for the party. = 我将为派对烤一只羊羔。
We will roast some nuts for the snack. = 我们将烤一些坚果作零食。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would roast a whole pig. = 如果我有更多时间，我会烤一头整猪。
I wish I could roast better than my mother. = 我希望我能比我妈妈烤得更好。

被动语态:
The chicken was roasted until golden brown. = 鸡肉被烤至金黄色。
The vegetables are being roasted in the oven. = 蔬菜正在烤箱里烤。

疑问句:
Is the meat roasted enough? = 肉烤熟了吗？
What kind of meat are you roasting today? = 你今天烤什么肉？

否定句:
I don't roast meat very often. = 我不经常烤肉。
He didn't roast the potatoes long enough. = 他没有把土豆烤够时间。


PHRASE->
Roast beef = 烤牛肉
Roast chicken = 烤鸡
Roast turkey = 烤火鸡
Roast potatoes = 烤土豆
Roast vegetables = 烤蔬菜
