loose-fitting: /ˌluːsˈfɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 宽松的 |  Describes clothing that is not tight-fitting and hangs loosely on the body. It implies a comfortable and less restrictive fit. |例句：The loose-fitting dress was perfect for a hot summer day. 这件宽松的连衣裙非常适合炎热的夏天。

She prefers loose-fitting clothes for comfort. 她喜欢穿宽松的衣服以求舒适。


近义词：baggy, roomy, oversized, comfortable | 反义词：tight-fitting, snug, form-fitting
