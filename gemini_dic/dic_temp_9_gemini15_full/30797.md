cetologist:/ˌsiːˈtɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |鲸类学家|A scientist who studies whales, dolphins, and porpoises.  A cetologist studies the biology, behavior, and ecology of cetaceans. |The cetologist presented her findings on the migration patterns of humpback whales.  鲸类学家展示了她关于座头鲸迁徙模式的研究结果。|marine biologist, oceanographer |(no direct antonym)
