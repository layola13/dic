pieds-à-terre: /ˌpiːz əˈtɛər/ | n. | 住所，临时住所 | A small apartment or house in a city, used by someone who lives somewhere else.  It is often used as a temporary residence or a place to stay when visiting a city. | 例句：She bought a pieds-à-terre in Paris so she could visit whenever she liked.  她买了一套巴黎的住所，这样她就可以随时去拜访。 |近义词：apartment, flat, second home | 反义词：main residence, permanent home
