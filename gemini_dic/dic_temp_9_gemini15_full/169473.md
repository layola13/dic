propaganda:/ˌprɒpəˈɡændə/ /ˌprɑːpəˈɡændə/ | n. |宣传；鼓动；宣传运动 | Information, especially of a biased or misleading nature, used to promote a political cause or point of view.  | The government's propaganda machine worked tirelessly to sway public opinion. 政府的宣传机器不知疲倦地努力影响公众舆论。|  advertising, promotion, publicity | truth, facts, reality | 名词
