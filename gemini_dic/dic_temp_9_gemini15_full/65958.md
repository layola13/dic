USAGE->

一般现在时:
They evacuate the building during fire drills. 他们在消防演习期间疏散大楼。
The government evacuates citizens from war zones. 政府将公民从战区撤离。

一般过去时:
We evacuated our homes when the volcano erupted. 火山爆发时，我们撤离了家园。
The residents evacuated the town before the flood.  居民们在洪水到来之前撤离了小镇。

现在完成时:
The authorities have evacuated thousands of people. 当局已经疏散了数千人。
They have evacuated the hospital due to a gas leak. 由于煤气泄漏，他们已经疏散了医院。


过去完成时:
The villagers had evacuated their homes before the tsunami struck.  村民们在海啸来袭之前已经撤离了家园。
The army had evacuated the city before the enemy advanced.  军队在敌人推进之前已经撤离了城市。

将来时:
We will evacuate the area if the storm worsens. 如果风暴恶化，我们将撤离该地区。
The police will evacuate the building if a bomb threat is confirmed. 如果炸弹威胁得到证实，警方将疏散大楼。

虚拟语气:
If the alarm sounded, we would evacuate immediately. 如果警报响起，我们将立即撤离。
I wish we had evacuated sooner. 我真希望我们早点撤离。

被动语态:
The city was evacuated quickly and efficiently.  这座城市被迅速而有效地疏散了。
The building is being evacuated right now.  大楼现在正在被疏散。

疑问句:
Will they evacuate the school?  他们会疏散学校吗？
Have they evacuated the residents yet? 他们已经疏散居民了吗？

否定句:
