USAGE->

This word is an adverb, and thus does not have tenses like verbs.  It describes the *manner* in which an action is performed.  The grammatical structures below illustrate how adverbs are used in sentences, not the word 'minatorially' specifically.

She works diligently. = 她勤奋地工作。

PHRASE->

There are no common phrases specifically using "minatorially".  The word itself is quite formal and rarely used in idiomatic expressions.  Instead, phrases would be built around the concept of domineering behavior.  For example:

with a minatorially air = 以专横的态度
in a minatorially fashion = 以专横的方式
minatorially dismissed = 专横地驳回
minatorially controlled = 专横地控制
minatorially enforced = 专横地执行
