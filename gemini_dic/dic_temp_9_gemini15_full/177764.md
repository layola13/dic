recaution:/rɪˈkɔːʃən/| n. |预防措施；谨慎；小心|  A precaution is an action taken to prevent something dangerous or unpleasant from happening.  It emphasizes careful planning and consideration to avoid potential risks. |Example:  They took the precaution of locking all the doors and windows before leaving.  他们离开前采取了锁上所有门窗的预防措施。|近义词：precaution, care, heed, foresight|反义词：negligence, carelessness, recklessness|名词
