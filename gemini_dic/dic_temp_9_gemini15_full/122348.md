mixing:/ˈmɪksɪŋ/ | v. |混合；调配；混杂 |The act of combining or blending things together.  It can refer to physical substances, sounds, colors, or even ideas. |例句：The chef is mixing the ingredients for the cake.  厨师正在混合蛋糕的配料。

例句：The sound of the city is a mixing of car horns, sirens, and chatter. 城市的声音是汽车喇叭声、警笛声和闲聊声的混合。

近义词：blending, combining, mingling, amalgamating | 反义词：separating, segregating


USAGE->

He is mixing the paint. = 他正在调配颜料。
She mixes the drinks expertly. = 她熟练地调制饮料。

一般过去时:
