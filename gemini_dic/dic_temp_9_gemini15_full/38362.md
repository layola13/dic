concentrate: /ˈkɒnsənˌtreɪt/ /ˈkɑːnsənˌtreɪt/ | vt. vi. n.| 集中；浓缩；浓度 | to direct one's attention or efforts towards a particular activity, subject, or problem.  |例句：Please concentrate on your work. 请集中精力工作。


例句：The students concentrated on their studies before the exam. 学生们在考试前专注于学习。


近义词：focus, center, centralize | 反义词：distract, diffuse, scatter | 词性：动词


concentrate (as a verb):

一般现在时:
He concentrates on his studies every day. 他每天都专注于学习。
She concentrates hard during exams. 她在考试期间非常专注。

一般过去时:
I concentrated on the lecture yesterday. 我昨天专注于听讲座。
They concentrated their efforts on finding a solution. 他们集中精力寻找解决方案。

现在完成时:
I have concentrated on improving my English skills. 我一直在努力提高我的英语技能。
She has concentrated her research on a specific area. 她把研究集中在一个特定领域。

过去完成时:
He had concentrated on his career before starting a family. 他在组建家庭之前专注于事业。
They had concentrated all their resources on the project. 他们已将所有资源都集中到这个项目上。

将来时:
We will concentrate on finishing the project next week. 我们下周将集中精力完成项目。
She will concentrate her efforts on finding a new job. 她将集中精力寻找一份新工作。

虚拟语气:
If I concentrated harder, I would get better grades. 如果我更专注，我会取得更好的成绩。
I wish I had concentrated more during the lecture. 我希望我在讲座期间更专注一些。

被动语态:
All efforts should be concentrated on solving the problem. 所有努力都应该集中在解决问题上。
The company's resources are being concentrated on product development. 公司的资源正集中在产品开发上。


concentrate (as a noun):

例句：Orange juice concentrate is convenient to use. 浓缩橙汁使用方便。
例句：The concentrate of the solution was very high.  该溶液的浓度很高。


词源:

From Old French *concentrer, from Latin concentrāre "to bring together in one place", from con- "together" + centrum "center".
