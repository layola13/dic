homestead:/ˈhoʊmstɛd/ | n. | 家园；宅地；自耕农的农庄 | A homestead is a house and the land around it, especially one that has been occupied for a long time and is often associated with farming or self-sufficiency.  It can also refer to the act of acquiring land under the Homestead Act. |例句：They built a cozy homestead in the countryside.  他们乡村建造了一个舒适的家园。

例句：The homestead was passed down through generations of the same family. 这个家园世代传承于同一个家族。


近义词：farm, ranch, estate, property | 反义词：apartment, city | 词性: noun
