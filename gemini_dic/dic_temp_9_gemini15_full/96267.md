indictee:/ɪnˈdɪktiː/| n. |被起诉者，被告|A person who has been formally accused of a crime.  An indictee is someone against whom a grand jury has returned an indictment.  They are not yet convicted, only formally charged.|例句：The indictee pleaded not guilty to the charges.  被起诉者对指控表示不认罪。

The indictee's lawyer argued for a reduced sentence. 被告的律师辩称应该减刑。|近义词：defendant, accused|反义词：acquittal, complainant
