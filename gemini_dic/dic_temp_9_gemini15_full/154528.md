perpetuate:/pərˈpetʃuˌeɪt/| vt. |使永久化；使持续；流传|To make something continue for a long time.  It often implies a negative connotation, suggesting the continuation of something undesirable.|The government's policies perpetuate inequality. 政府的政策使不平等持续下去。|近义词：continue, prolong, maintain|反义词：terminate, end, abolish|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He perpetuates the myth. = 他使这个神话流传下去。
We perpetuate harmful stereotypes. = 我们使有害的刻板印象持续存在。


一般过去时:
The war perpetuated hatred between the two countries. = 那场战争使两国之间的仇恨持续下去。
His actions perpetuated the cycle of violence. = 他的行为使暴力循环持续下去。


现在完成时:
This system has perpetuated poverty for generations. = 这个制度几代以来一直使贫困持续下去。
Their silence has perpetuated the misunderstanding. = 他们的沉默使误解持续下去。


过去完成时:
The outdated laws had perpetuated discrimination for decades. = 过时的法律几十年来一直使歧视持续下去。
They had perpetuated a false narrative for years. = 他们多年来一直传播虚假叙事。


将来时:
This policy will perpetuate the problem. = 这一政策将使问题持续下去。
Ignoring the issue will perpetuate the crisis. = 忽视这个问题将使危机持续下去。


虚拟语气:
If we don't act now, we will perpetuate this injustice. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，我们将使这种不公正持续下去。
I wish they wouldn't perpetuate such rumors. = 我希望他们不要再散布这样的谣言。


被动语态:
The legend was perpetuated through storytelling. = 这个传说通过讲故事而流传下来。
The myth is perpetuated by misinformation. = 这个神话由于错误信息而流传。


疑问句:
Does this system perpetuate inequality? = 这个制度是否使不平等持续下去？
Will this action perpetuate the conflict? = 这一行动会使冲突持续下去吗？


否定句:
We must not perpetuate these harmful ideas. = 我们绝不能使这些有害的想法持续下去。
They refused to perpetuate the lie. = 他们拒绝继续撒谎。


PHRASE->
perpetuate a myth = 延续一个神话
perpetuate injustice = 使不公正持续
perpetuate inequality = 使不平等持续
perpetuate violence = 使暴力持续
perpetuate the status quo = 维持现状
