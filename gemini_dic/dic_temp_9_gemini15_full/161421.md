posturing:/ˈpɒstʃərɪŋ/| n. v.|摆姿态；装腔作势；故作姿态 |The act of behaving in a way that is intended to impress or deceive others.  It often involves adopting a particular attitude or appearance to project a certain image. |例句：His posturing as a tough negotiator was unconvincing. 他装作强硬的谈判者，令人难以信服。

例句：The politician's posturing before the election was transparent.  这位政治家在大选前的姿态很虚伪。


近义词：affectation, pretense, showing off, grandstanding

反义词：authenticity, genuineness, sincerity

词性:noun & verb

She constantly postures to impress her colleagues. = 她不断地摆出姿态来给同事留下印象。
