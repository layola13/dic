forming:/ˈfɔːrmɪŋ/ | v. | 形成；构成；塑造；组成 | The process of giving shape or structure to something.  It can refer to the act of creating something, or the state of being created. | 例句：The clay is forming into a beautiful vase. 粘土正在形成一个美丽的瓶子。

The committee is forming a plan to address the issue. 委员会正在制定一个计划来解决这个问题。| 近义词：shaping, creating, making, developing | 反义词：destroying, dismantling, breaking down | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The artist is forming a new sculpture. = 艺术家正在创作一件新的雕塑。
They are forming a new company. = 他们正在组建一家新公司。

一般过去时:
She formed a strong bond with her colleagues. = 她与同事建立了牢固的联系。
The mountains were formed millions of years ago. = 这些山脉形成于数百万年前。

现在完成时:
The government has formed a new task force. = 政府已经组建了一个新的特别工作组。
He has formed a habit of reading before bed. = 他养成了睡前阅读的习惯。

过去完成时:
They had formed a plan before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经制定了一个计划。
The ice had formed overnight. = 冰是在一夜之间形成的。

将来时:
The team will form a strategy for the upcoming game. = 球队将为即将到来的比赛制定策略。
They will form a new government after the election. = 他们将在选举后组建新政府。

虚拟语气:
If they formed a coalition, they would have a better chance of winning. = 如果他们组成联盟，他们将有更大的获胜机会。
I wish I had formed better habits earlier in life. = 我希望我早些时候养成更好的习惯。

被动语态:
The team was formed in 2005. = 该团队成立于2005年。
The opinion was formed based on limited information. = 这一观点是基于有限的信息形成的。

疑问句:
How are clouds formed? = 云是如何形成的？

否定句:
The ice didn't form because the temperature was too high. = 由于温度太高，冰没有形成。
They haven't yet formed a conclusion. = 他们还没有得出结论。
