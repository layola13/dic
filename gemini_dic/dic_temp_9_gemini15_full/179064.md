redenying: /ˌriːˈdeɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 再次否认；重新否认 |  To deny again or once more; to reaffirm a denial.  |例句：He kept redenying his involvement in the crime, despite overwhelming evidence. 他不断否认自己参与了犯罪，尽管有压倒性的证据。

例句：Despite the witness testimony, the defendant continued redenying any wrongdoing.  尽管有证人证词，被告仍然继续否认任何不当行为。


近义词：repudiating, disclaiming, contradicting

反义词：admitting, confessing, acknowledging

词性:动词
