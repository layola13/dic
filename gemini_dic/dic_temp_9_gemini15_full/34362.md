classically:/ˈklæsɪkli/| adv.|经典地，传统地，典型地|In a way that is considered to be typical of the best or most important examples of a particular style, type, or genre.|The building was classically designed. 这座建筑是古典风格设计的。|近义词：typically, traditionally, conventionally|反义词： unconventionally, atypically

词源:
