inexpert:/ˌɪnˈɛkspɜːrt/| adj. |不熟练的；外行的；业余的 |Lacking skill or knowledge in a particular field or activity.  It implies a lack of experience or training, often contrasting with expertise. |例句：His inexpert handling of the boat nearly caused an accident. 他笨拙的驾驶差点导致事故。

例句：The inexpert repair job was evident in the shoddy workmanship.  不熟练的修理工作在粗制滥造的工艺中显而易见。


近义词：unskilled, amateur, unprofessional, clumsy, inept

反义词：expert, skilled, professional, proficient, adept
