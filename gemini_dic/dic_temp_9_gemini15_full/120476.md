minstrel:/ˈmɪnstrəl/| n.|吟游诗人，乐师|A minstrel was a medieval performer who sang, played music, and told stories, often traveling from place to place.  The term is now often associated with controversial forms of entertainment involving blackface. |例句：The minstrel sang ballads and played the lute.  吟游诗人演唱民谣并演奏鲁特琴。|例句：The historical minstrel tradition is now viewed with great sensitivity due to its racist past. 由于其种族主义的过去，历史上的吟游诗人传统现在被人们非常敏感地看待。|近义词：musician, singer, troubadour, bard|反义词：N/A
