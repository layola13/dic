regardlessly:/rɪˈɡɑːdlɪsli/ | adv. | 不顾；不管；不理会 |  Without regard; heedlessly; despite.  It implies a disregard for consequences or the feelings of others. | 例句：He drove regardlessly through the red light. 他不顾红灯闯了过去。  He spoke regardlessly of his former friends. 他不顾及以前朋友的感受地谈论他们。 | 近义词：heedlessly, recklessly, carelessly, indifferently | 反义词：carefully, cautiously, considerately
