kwanzaa:/kwɑːnˈzɑː/| n. |  kwanzaa节 | Kwanzaa is a week-long celebration held from December 26 to January 1, honoring African-American culture and heritage.  It involves seven principles (Nguzo Saba) which are celebrated each day. |  We celebrated Kwanzaa with family and friends last year. 去年我们和家人朋友一起庆祝了 kwanzaa 节。  |  Festival, Holiday |  Christmas (as a contrasting holiday) | 名词
