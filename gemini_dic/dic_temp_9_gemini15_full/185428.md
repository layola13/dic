retting:/ˈrɛtɪŋ/| n.|腐熟；浸渍；沤麻|The process of separating the fibers of a plant, typically flax or hemp, by allowing it to rot or decompose under controlled conditions.  This process softens the plant material making it easier to separate the fibers from the woody core.|例句：The flax was left to retting in the field for several weeks. 亚麻被留在田里浸渍了好几个星期。
例句：Retting is a crucial step in linen production. 腐熟是亚麻生产中的一个关键步骤。|近义词：rotting, decomposition, maceration|反义词：preservation, drying
