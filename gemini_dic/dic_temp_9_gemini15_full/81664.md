green-lit: /ˈɡriːnˌlɪt/ | adj. v. | 绿灯批准；获准 |  Giving approval or authorization for a project, film, etc. to proceed.  |例句：The project was green-lit after a lengthy review process.  项目在经过漫长的审查过程后获得了批准。

The film was green-lit for production. 这部电影获准制作。|近义词：approved, authorized, sanctioned |反义词：rejected, disapproved, vetoed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The studio green-lights many projects each year.  = 电影公司每年批准许多项目。
They green-light new ideas regularly. = 他们定期批准新的想法。


一般过去时:
The committee green-lit the proposal last week. = 委员会上周批准了该提案。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
