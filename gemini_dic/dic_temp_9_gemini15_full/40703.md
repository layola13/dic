converted:/kənˈvɜːrtɪd/| v. |转换的；改变信仰的| To change something into a different form, or to change your beliefs or way of life.|The old factory has been converted into a modern art gallery.  旧工厂已经被改造成一个现代艺术画廊。  |The data was converted into a graph. 数据被转换成图表。|transformed, changed, altered |unchanged, unaltered, original|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He converts dollars to euros. 他把美元换成欧元。
She converts her ideas into actions. 她把她的想法付诸行动。


一般过去时:
They converted the barn into a house. 他们把谷仓改造成房子。
He converted to Christianity. 他皈依了基督教。


现在完成时:
She has converted all her savings into stocks. 她已将所有积蓄转换成股票。
He has converted to a vegan lifestyle. 他已经改吃素食了。


过去完成时:
They had converted the building before the new tenant moved in. 新租户搬进来之前，他们已经改造了那栋建筑。
She had converted to Buddhism many years ago. 多年前她就皈依佛教了。


将来时:
We will convert the attic into a bedroom. 我们将把阁楼改造成卧室。
He will convert his old car to run on electricity. 他将改装他的旧车使其能够使用电力驱动。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would convert my garage into a workshop. 如果我有钱，我会把车库改造成工作室。
I wish I could convert my negative thoughts into positive ones. 我希望我能把消极的想法转换成积极的想法。


被动语态:
The old schoolhouse was converted into a library. 旧校舍被改造成图书馆。
The data is being converted into a usable format. 数据正在被转换成可用的格式。


疑问句:
Have you converted your old photos to digital format? 你是否已将旧照片转换成数位格式？
Will they convert the warehouse into apartments? 他们会把仓库改造成公寓吗？


否定句:
He didn't convert the measurements to metric. 他没有将测量单位转换成公制单位。
They haven't converted to the new system yet. 他们还没有转换到新的系统。


PHRASE->

convert to: 转变为...
convert into: 转换成...
convert from: 从...转换
