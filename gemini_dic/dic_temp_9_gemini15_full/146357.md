overfix:/ˈoʊvərˌfɪks/ | vt. |过度固定；过度校正|To fix or adjust something excessively or to an inappropriate degree, often resulting in a negative outcome.  It implies going beyond the necessary or optimal point of adjustment. |例句：He overfixed the brakes, making them too tight and causing premature wear. 他把刹车调得太紧了，导致刹车过早磨损。

He overfixed the image in the photo editor, losing detail and making it look unnatural. 他在照片编辑器中过度校正了图像，导致细节丢失，看起来不自然。


近义词：overadjust, overcorrect, overcompensate |反义词：underfix, underadjust
