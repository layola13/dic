relocation:/ˌriːləʊˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |迁移；搬迁；重新安置 |The act of moving to a new place and establishing oneself there; the process of moving something to a new place.  Relocation can refer to people, businesses, or even objects. |例句：The company is offering relocation assistance to employees transferring to the new office. 公司正在为调到新办公室的员工提供搬迁协助。

例句：The family's relocation to the countryside was a welcome change.  这个家庭搬到乡下是一次受欢迎的变化。


近义词：removal, transfer, resettlement, transposition |反义词：settlement (in the sense of staying put)


词源:  From "re-" (again) + "locate" (to find the place of).  The word "locate" itself originates from the Latin word "locāre" (to place).
