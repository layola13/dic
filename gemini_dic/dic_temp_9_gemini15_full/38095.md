compote:/kəmˈpoʊt/ | n. |蜜饯，果脯，果酱 |A compote is a sweet, cooked fruit dessert.  It can be served hot or cold, and often includes spices or other flavorings. |Example: The restaurant offered a delicious pear compote with the cheese course. 餐厅在奶酪菜中提供美味的梨蜜饯。|近义词：preserve, conserve, fruit salad |反义词：None specifically.
