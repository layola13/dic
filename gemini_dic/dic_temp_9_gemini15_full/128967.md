nio:/ˈnaɪoʊ/| n.|非同步I/O|Non-blocking I/O, a programming model that allows a program to continue executing while waiting for an I/O operation to complete.  It improves efficiency by not blocking the program's execution.|Example:  NIO frameworks are often used for high-performance network applications.|近义词：asynchronous I/O|反义词：synchronous I/O|名词
