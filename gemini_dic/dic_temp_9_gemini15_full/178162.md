recollect:/ˌrɛkəˈlɛkt/ | vt. | 回忆，想起；重新集合 | To bring (something) back to mind; remember. To gather or bring together again. |例句：I can't recollect his name. 我记不起他的名字了。

例句：The troops were ordered to recollect at dawn. 部队奉命在黎明时重新集合。

近义词：remember, recall, remember, summon (up), retrieve | 反义词：forget, overlook, misremember | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I recollect the details of the meeting.  = 我记得会议的细节。
She recollects her childhood fondly. = 她 fondly 回忆她的童年。


一般过去时:
He recollected the address after a moment's thought. = 他想了一会儿后记起了地址。
They recollected their scattered belongings. = 他们重新收集了他们散落的物品。


现在完成时:
I have recollected everything I needed for the trip. = 我已经收集好旅行所需的一切东西了。
She has recollected herself after the shock. = 她从震惊中恢复过来了。


过去完成时:
He had recollected the password before logging in. = 他在登录之前已经记起了密码。
By the time they reached the destination, they had recollected all the luggage. = 等他们到达目的地时，他们已经把所有的行李都收集齐了。


将来时:
I will recollect the information for you tomorrow. = 我明天会把信息整理好给你。
The committee will recollect to discuss the proposal next week. = 委员会下周将重新集会讨论该提案。


虚拟语气:
If I could recollect the answer, I would tell you. = 如果我能想起答案，我会告诉你的。
I wish I could recollect where I left my keys. = 我真希望我能想起我把钥匙放在哪里了。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
