ginormous:/dʒaɪˈnɔːrməs/| adj. |巨大的，庞大的 | Extremely large; enormous.  The word is a blend of "giant" and "enormous". It is used informally to emphasize the size of something. |例句：The ginormous cake was enough to feed the entire party. 这块巨大的蛋糕足够喂饱整个派对。

例句：He owns a ginormous house in the countryside. 他在乡下拥有一座巨大的房子。


近义词：huge, enormous, immense, colossal, gigantic |反义词：tiny, minuscule, little, small


词性:形容词
