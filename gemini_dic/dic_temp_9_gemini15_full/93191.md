ignorami:/ˌɪɡnəˈrɑːmi/| n.|无知的人；不懂装懂的人|People who pretend to know more than they actually do, especially in a pretentious or arrogant manner.  They often display a superficial understanding of complex subjects.|例句：He's an ignoramus when it comes to art, yet he criticizes every painting he sees. 他在艺术方面是个门外汉，但他却批评他看到的每一幅画。|近义词：dunce, fool, simpleton|反义词：expert, savant, genius|名词
