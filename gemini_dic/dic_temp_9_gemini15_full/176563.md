resilvering: /ˌriːˈsɪlvərɪŋ/ | v. | 再镀银；重新镀银 | The process of applying a new layer of silver to a surface, typically metal, that has previously been silver-plated.  This is often done to restore the shine and reflectivity of the surface, or to repair damage to the existing silver plating. | Example: The antique mirror needed resilvering to restore its clarity.  = 这面古董镜子需要重新镀银以恢复其清晰度。 The tarnished silverware was sent for resilvering. = 生锈的银器被送去重新镀银。|近义词：replating, refinishing | 反义词：de-silvering
