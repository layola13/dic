词源:

The word "overembroidering" is a combination of "over-" (meaning excessively or too much) and "embroidering" (the act of decorating fabric with needlework).  "Embroider" itself comes from the Old French "broder," ultimately from the Germanic root meaning "to braid" or "to work with threads".
