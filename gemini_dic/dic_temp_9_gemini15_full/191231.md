scarus:/ˈskærəs/| n. |鹦嘴鱼|A scarus is a type of fish belonging to the family Scaridae, commonly known as parrotfish.  They are characterized by their beak-like mouths, which they use to graze on algae and coral.  Many species exhibit vibrant colors and patterns.|Example:  The diver observed a vibrant green scarus grazing on the coral reef.  潜水员观察到一条鲜绿色的鹦嘴鱼在珊瑚礁上吃藻类。|近义词：parrotfish (more general term)|反义词：N/A
