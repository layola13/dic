cockneyishly:/ˈkɒkniʃli/| adv.|像伦敦东区人一样地；以伦敦东区口音；粗俗地|In a manner characteristic of Cockney people; with a Cockney accent; vulgarly.|例句：He spoke cockneyishly, dropping his h's and using rhyming slang. 他说着伦敦东区口音，丢掉了h音，还用押韵俚语。

例句：She cockneyishly imitated the Queen's accent, much to everyone's amusement. 她以伦敦东区人的方式模仿女王的口音，逗乐了大家。
