privatise:/ˈpraɪvətaɪz/| vt.|私有化|To transfer (a business, industry, or service) from public to private ownership and control.|例句：The government plans to privatize the railway system. 政府计划将铁路系统私有化。|例句：Many state-owned industries have been privatized in recent years. 近年来，许多国有企业都被私有化了。|近义词：commercialize, denationalize|反义词：nationalize, socialize|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government privatizes state-owned enterprises. = 政府将国有企业私有化。
They privatize the water supply company. = 他们将供水公司私有化。


一般过去时:
The company was privatized last year. = 这家公司去年被私有化了。
The government privatized the airline industry in the 1980s. = 政府在20世纪80年代将航空业私有化了。


现在完成时:
Many countries have privatized their telecommunication industries. = 许多国家已经将其电信行业私有化。
The city has privatized the garbage collection service. = 该市已将垃圾收集服务私有化。


过去完成时:
Before the reforms, the energy sector had never been privatized. = 在改革之前，能源部门从未被私有化。
They had already privatized the postal service before the election. = 他们在选举前就已经将邮政服务私有化了。


将来时:
The government will privatize the energy sector next year. = 政府明年将对能源部门进行私有化。
They will likely privatize the healthcare system in the future. = 未来他们可能会将医疗系统私有化。


虚拟语气:
If they privatized the education system, it would lead to higher costs. = 如果他们将教育系统私有化，将会导致更高的成本。
It would be better if the government didn't privatize the water supply. = 如果政府不将供水私有化就好了。


被动语态:
The railway system is being privatized. = 铁路系统正在被私有化。
The company was privatized by a foreign investor. = 这家公司被一位外国投资者私有化了。


疑问句:
Will the government privatize the postal service? = 政府会将邮政服务私有化吗？
Has the airport been privatized yet? = 机场已经被私有化了吗？


否定句:
The government will not privatize the healthcare system. = 政府不会将医疗系统私有化。
They did not privatize the railway system. = 他们没有将铁路系统私有化。



PHRASE->
privatize the economy = 私有化经济

rapid privatization = 快速私有化
partial privatization = 部分私有化
