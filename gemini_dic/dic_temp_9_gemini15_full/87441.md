hesitates:/ˈhezɪteɪts/| v. |犹豫，迟疑|To pause before saying or doing something, often because of doubt or uncertainty.  It implies a temporary delay or reluctance.|He hesitated before answering the question. 他在回答问题之前犹豫了一下。|She hesitated to jump into the cold water. 她犹豫着要不要跳进冰冷的水里。| waver, falter, pause | decide, act decisively |动词

She hesitates to express her opinions openly. 她犹豫着要不要公开表达自己的观点。
