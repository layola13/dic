pinholes:/ˈpɪnhoʊlz/| n. |针孔；小孔 |Extremely small holes, typically those resembling the size made by a pin.  They can be found in various materials and often indicate damage or flaws. |例句：The film was ruined by pinholes.  胶片被针孔弄坏了。 |例句：I found several pinholes in the fabric. 我在织物上发现了好几个针孔。|近义词：small holes, punctures, perforations |反义词: large openings, gaps
