refeeding:/ˌriːˈfiːdɪŋ/| n. |再喂养；重新喂食|The act of feeding someone or something again after a period of not eating.  It often refers to the process of gradually reintroducing food to someone who has been fasting, malnourished, or recovering from an eating disorder.|例句：The doctor carefully monitored the patient's refeeding process after their prolonged illness.  医生仔细监测了病人长期疾病后的重新喂养过程。

例句：Refeeding syndrome is a serious complication that can occur during the refeeding process. 再喂养综合征是在再喂养过程中可能发生的严重并发症。


近义词：refed, re-nourishment|反义词：starvation, fasting
