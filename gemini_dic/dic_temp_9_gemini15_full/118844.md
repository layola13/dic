metaphosphate:/ˌmɛtəˈfɒsfeɪt/| n. |偏磷酸盐|A salt or ester of metaphosphoric acid.  Metaphosphates are used in various applications, including water treatment, food processing, and as flame retardants. They are characterized by a cyclic structure of phosphate groups.|例句：Sodium metaphosphate is a common ingredient in some food products as an emulsifier. 偏磷酸钠是某些食品中作为乳化剂的常见成分。|  The use of metaphosphates in detergents has declined due to environmental concerns. 由于环境问题，磷酸盐在洗涤剂中的使用已经减少。|近义词：polyphosphate|反义词:orthophosphate
