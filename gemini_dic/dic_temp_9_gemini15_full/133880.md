nongregarious:/ˌnɒnɡrɪˈɡeəriəs/| adj.|不群居的；不合群的；孤僻的 | Not sociable; tending to avoid the company of others.  A nongregarious person prefers solitude or the company of a very few close friends.  |The owl, a largely nocturnal and nongregarious bird, is often misunderstood.  这只猫头鹰，一种主要在夜间活动且不合群的鸟，经常被误解。| solitary, unsociable, aloof, reclusive | gregarious, sociable, outgoing | 形容词
