mud-slinging: /ˈmʌdˌslɪŋɪŋ/ | n. | 诽谤；污蔑；造谣中伤 | The act of making false and defamatory statements about someone, typically during a political campaign or public dispute.  | 例句：The election campaign descended into a bitter mud-slinging match.  这场竞选活动演变成一场恶毒的诽谤大战。


例句：  The candidates resorted to mud-slinging rather than discussing the issues.  候选人采取了诽谤而不是讨论问题的方式。


近义词：character assassination, vilification, defamation, calumny, smear campaign


反义词：praise, commendation, endorsement


词性: 名词
