pentremites:/ˌpɛntriˈmaɪtiz/| n. |海百合类化石|A genus of extinct, stalked crinoids (sea lilies) characterized by a distinctive, five-sided, pentagonal shape.  They are common fossils found in Paleozoic rocks.  Their skeletal structure is composed of calcite plates. |The pentremites fossils found in this area are exceptionally well-preserved.|近义词:crinoid (general term)|反义词:None
