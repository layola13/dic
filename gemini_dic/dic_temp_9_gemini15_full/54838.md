displace:/dɪsˈpleɪs/| vt. |移置；取代；使流离失所 |To move something or someone from its or their usual place or position.  |例句：The new road displaced several families from their homes. 新路使几户人家流离失所。|例句：The old technology has been displaced by newer innovations. 旧技术已被更新的技术取代。|近义词：remove, replace, relocate, supplant |反义词：retain, keep, restore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory displaces workers with robots. = 工厂用机器人取代工人。
He displaces his anger onto others. = 他把怒气发泄到别人身上。


一般过去时:
The earthquake displaced thousands of people. = 地震使数千人流离失所。
The company displaced its headquarters to a new city. = 公司将其总部迁至一座新城市。


现在完成时:
The new technology has displaced many traditional jobs. = 新技术已经取代了许多传统工作。
Recent storms have displaced many families from their homes. = 最近的风暴使许多家庭流离失所。


过去完成时:
The flood had displaced the entire village before help arrived. = 在救援到来之前，洪水已经使整个村庄的人们流离失所。
They had displaced the furniture to make room for the party. = 为了腾出空间举办派对，他们已经移动了家具。


将来时:
The construction project will displace many businesses. = 建设项目将使许多企业被迫搬迁。
Automation will displace many manual labor jobs in the future. = 未来自动化将取代许多体力劳动工作。


虚拟语气:
If the dam broke, it would displace thousands. = 如果大坝决堤，它将使数千人流离失所。
I wish the new policy wouldn't displace so many workers. = 我希望新政策不会使这么多工人失业。


被动语态:
Many families were displaced by the hurricane. = 许多家庭被飓风摧毁家园。
The old building was displaced by a modern skyscraper. = 旧建筑被一座现代摩天大楼取代。


疑问句:
Will the new development displace any residents? = 新开发项目会使任何居民流离失所吗？
How many people were displaced by the war? = 战争使多少人流离失所？


否定句:
The company did not displace any employees during the restructuring. = 公司在重组期间没有裁员。
The government tried to prevent the dam from displacing the local community. = 政府试图阻止大坝使当地社区流离失所。
