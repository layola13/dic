mobloggers:/ˈmoʊˌblɒɡərz/| n. |手机博客博主|People who regularly create and post entries to a mobile blog (a blog accessed and updated primarily via a mobile device).  They often use mobile devices to capture photos and videos to accompany their text posts.| 例句：Many mobloggers use their smartphones to document their daily lives and share their experiences with their followers. 许多手机博客博主用智能手机记录他们的日常生活并与粉丝分享他们的经历。|近义词：mobile bloggers, microbloggers (if the blog posts are short)|反义词：traditional bloggers (if contrasting with the use of mobile devices)
