over-hardens:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrdənz/| v. |过度硬化；硬化过度|To become excessively hard or rigid; to harden beyond a desirable or necessary degree.  This is typically used in reference to materials, but can also be used metaphorically.|例句：The clay over-hardened in the sun, making it difficult to work with.  太阳下粘土过度硬化，使其难以操作。

The concrete over-hardened before the forms could be removed.  模板还没拆除混凝土就过度硬化了。


近义词：over-set, overcure, become brittle


反义词：soften, become pliable, remain flexible


词性:动词
