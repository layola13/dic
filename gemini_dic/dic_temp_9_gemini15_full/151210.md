pardonable: /ˈpɑːdnəbl/ | adj. | 可原谅的，情有可原的 |  Forgivable; excusable.  Something pardonable is something that can be forgiven because there is a good reason for it. |

例句：

Her outburst was pardonable considering the circumstances.  考虑到当时的处境，她的爆发是可以原谅的。

His lateness was pardonable; there was a terrible traffic jam. 他的迟到是可以理解的；路上堵车很严重。


近义词：forgivable, excusable, justifiable, understandable

反义词：unforgivable, inexcusable


词源:

The word "pardonable" comes from the verb "pardon," which itself has Old French origins ("pardoner").  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or possibility.  Therefore, "pardonable" literally means "capable of being pardoned."
