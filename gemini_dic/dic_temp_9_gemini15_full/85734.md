hawked:/hɔːkt/ | vt. |大声叫卖；兜售；（猛烈地）咳嗽； |to sell (goods) by shouting in a public place; to cough forcefully.|例句：The street vendor hawked his wares loudly. 街头小贩大声叫卖他的商品。 |例句：He hawked and spat, clearing his throat. 他咳嗽并吐痰，清了清嗓子。|近义词：cry, peddle, vend |反义词：buy, purchase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hawks his goods in the market every day. = 他每天都在市场上叫卖他的商品。
She hawks her services as a freelance writer. = 她兜售她作为自由撰稿人的服务。

一般过去时:
The old woman hawked her apples on the street corner. = 老妇人在街角叫卖苹果。
He hawked and spat throughout the entire meeting. = 他在整个会议期间都在咳嗽和吐痰。

现在完成时:
They have hawked their products at several trade shows this year. = 他们今年已经在几个贸易展销会上兜售他们的产品了。
She has been hawking up phlegm for the past hour. = 她过去一小时一直在咳痰。

过去完成时:
He had hawked his wares for hours before anyone bought anything. = 他叫卖了几个小时的商品才有人买东西。
She had been hawking and spitting all day, so she felt exhausted. = 她整天都在咳嗽和吐痰，所以感到筋疲力尽。

将来时:
They will hawk their new invention at the upcoming tech fair. = 他们将在即将到来的科技展上兜售他们的新发明。
He will probably hawk and spit again during the presentation. = 他很可能会在演示过程中再次咳嗽和吐痰。

虚拟语气:
If he had hawked his goods more aggressively, he would have made more money. = 如果他更积极地叫卖他的商品，他就会赚到更多钱。
I wish he wouldn't hawk and spit so loudly in public. = 我希望他不要在公共场合大声咳嗽和吐痰。

被动语态:
The products were hawked throughout the city. = 这些产品在整个城市都被叫卖。
His services were hawked online and offline. = 他的服务在线下和线上都被兜售。

疑问句:
Did they hawk their goods successfully? = 他们成功地叫卖了他们的商品吗？
Was the medicine hawked to the unsuspecting public? = 这种药是否被兜售给了毫无戒心的公众？

否定句:
He didn't hawk his wares loudly enough to attract customers. = 他叫卖商品的声音不够大，无法吸引顾客。
They did not hawk their services aggressively enough to gain clients. = 他们兜售服务的力度不够大，无法获得客户。
