carbonization:/ˌkɑːrbənaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |碳化；煤化|The process of converting organic matter into carbon or a carbon-containing residue through the action of heat, pressure, or chemical reaction.  This process is crucial in the formation of coal and is also used in various industrial processes.|例句：The carbonization of wood produces charcoal. 木材的碳化产生木炭。

The high temperatures in the kiln caused the rapid carbonization of the organic material.  窑内的高温导致有机物快速碳化。|近义词：charring, coking|反义词：decarbonization
