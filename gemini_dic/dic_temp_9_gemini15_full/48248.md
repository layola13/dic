decorate:/ˈdɛkəreɪt/ | vt. |装饰，装修 | To make something more attractive by adding things to it.  |例句：She decorated her room with flowers. 她用鲜花装饰了她的房间。|近义词：adorn, ornament, embellish |反义词：strip, undress|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She decorates her apartment every Christmas. = 她每年圣诞节都装饰她的公寓。
They decorate the cake with frosting. = 他们用糖霜装饰蛋糕。

一般过去时:
We decorated the Christmas tree last night. = 我们昨晚装饰了圣诞树。
He decorated the house for his daughter's wedding. = 他为了女儿的婚礼装饰了房子。

现在完成时:
They have decorated the hall for the party. = 他们已经为派对装饰了大厅。
She has decorated her home beautifully. = 她已经漂亮地装饰了她的家。

过去完成时:
They had decorated the church before the ceremony began. = 典礼开始前他们已经装饰了教堂。
We had decorated the cake hours before the guests arrived. = 客人到达前几个小时我们就装饰好了蛋糕。

将来时:
I will decorate my bedroom this weekend. = 我这个周末将装饰我的卧室。
They will decorate the entire building for the festival. = 他们将为节日装饰整栋建筑物。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would decorate the whole house. = 如果我有更多时间，我会装饰整个房子。
I wish I could decorate the cake as beautifully as she does. = 我希望我能像她一样漂亮地装饰蛋糕。

被动语态:
The room was decorated with balloons. = 房间用气球装饰着。
The cake was decorated professionally. = 蛋糕是由专业人士装饰的。

疑问句:
How will you decorate the wedding hall? = 你将如何装饰婚礼大厅？
Will you decorate the tree with lights? = 你会用灯装饰树吗？

否定句:
I didn't decorate the Christmas tree this year. = 我今年没有装饰圣诞树。
They haven't decorated the living room yet. = 他们还没有装饰起居室。



PHRASE->
decorate a room = 装饰房间
decorate a cake = 装饰蛋糕
decorate a Christmas tree = 装饰圣诞树
decorate with flowers = 用鲜花装饰
decorate for a party = 为派对装饰

词源:
From Old French *decorer, from Latin decor, "beauty, ornament."  The word's evolution shows a clear lineage from the concept of "beauty" and "ornament" to the act of applying that beauty and ornament.
