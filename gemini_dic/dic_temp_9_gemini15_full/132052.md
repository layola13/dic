noncosmic: /ˌnɒnˈkɒzmɪk/ | adj. |非宇宙的，非宇宙性的 | Not related to or characteristic of the cosmos or universe; earthly or mundane.  It emphasizes the lack of connection to the vastness and scale of the universe. |例句：His concerns were entirely noncosmic, focused on everyday problems.  他的担忧完全是世俗的，集中在日常问题上。

例句：The artist's work, while beautiful, seemed strangely noncosmic, lacking the usual grandeur of celestial themes. 艺术家的作品虽然很美，但奇怪地缺乏宇宙的宏伟主题。


近义词：terrestrial, earthly, mundane, worldly, sublunary


反义词：cosmic, universal, celestial, astronomical


词性：形容词
