overplanted:/ˌoʊvərˈplæntɪd/| adj.|种植过密的；过度种植的|Having been planted too densely.  The field was overplanted with corn, leading to smaller ears and reduced yield.|近义词：overcrowded, congested |反义词：underplanted, sparsely planted


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The farmer overplants his fields every year. = 这位农民每年都过度种植他的田地。
This garden is overplanted with roses. = 这个花园种满了玫瑰花，过于密集。


一般过去时:
They overplanted the tomatoes last season. = 他们上个季度种的西红柿太密了。
The orchard was overplanted with apple trees ten years ago. = 十年前，果园里的苹果树种得太密了。


现在完成时:
The gardener has overplanted the flowerbeds again this year. = 园丁今年又把花坛种得太满了。
We have overplanted the seedlings, and they are competing for resources. = 我们把幼苗种得太密了，它们正在争夺资源。


过去完成时:
They had overplanted the sunflowers, so they were small and weak. = 他们把向日葵种得太密了，所以它们又小又弱。
The farmer realized he had overplanted his wheat crop after the first rain. = 农民在第一场雨后意识到他种的麦子太多了。


将来时:
I will not overplant my vegetable garden next spring. = 明年春天我不会再把我的菜园种得太满了。
They will likely overplant the potatoes if they don't plan carefully. = 如果他们不仔细规划，他们可能会把土豆种得太密。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't overplanted the lettuce, I would have had a bigger harvest. = 如果我没有把莴苣种得太密，我会收获更多。
I wish I hadn't overplanted the herbs; now they're all leggy. = 我真希望我没有把香草种得太密；现在它们都长得细长了。


被动语态:
The cornfield was overplanted by the inexperienced farmer. = 玉米地被那位经验不足的农民种得太密了。
The flowerbeds were overplanted, resulting in stunted growth. = 花坛种得太密了，导致植物生长矮小。


疑问句:
Was the field overplanted with soybeans? = 豆田种得太密了吗？
Is the vegetable garden overplanted this year? = 今年菜园种得太密了吗？


否定句:
The farmer did not overplant his crops this year. = 农民今年没有过度种植他的庄稼。
They didn't overplant the strawberries, so they produced a bountiful crop. = 他们没有把草莓种得太密，所以产量很高。
