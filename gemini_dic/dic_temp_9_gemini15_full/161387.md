poststimulation: /ˌpoʊststɪmjuˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 刺激后；刺激之后 | The period or process following stimulation; the effects or consequences of stimulation.  It often refers to the period after a stimulus, such as a drug or a physical event, has been applied, and describes the resulting physiological or behavioral changes. | Example:  The researchers measured poststimulation neuronal activity. 研究人员测量了刺激后的神经元活动。


The long-term effects of poststimulation were studied. 刺激后的长期影响被研究了。


近义词: after-effect, post-treatment, sequela (medical context) | 反义词: prestimulation, before stimulation | 词性: noun
