laurate:/ˈlɔːreɪt/| n. |月桂酸酯；月桂酸盐|A laurate is a salt or ester of lauric acid.  Lauric acid is a saturated fatty acid found in coconut oil and palm kernel oil.  Laurates are used in various applications, including cosmetics, detergents, and pharmaceuticals.|Example: Sodium laurate is a common surfactant used in soaps and detergents.  月桂酸钠是肥皂和洗涤剂中常用的表面活性剂。|近义词:  (depending on context) fatty acid salt, surfactant|反义词:  N/A
