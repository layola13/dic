characterizes:/ˈkærəktəraɪzɪz/ | vt. |描绘…的特性；是…的特征；使具有…的特征 | to describe the qualities of someone or something | The artist's work is characterized by its bold colors and expressive brushstrokes.  这位艺术家的作品以其大胆的色彩和富有表现力的笔触为特征。|  distinguishes, defines, marks |  uncharacterizes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The novel characterizes the protagonist as a complex and flawed individual. 这部小说将主人公描绘成一个复杂而有缺陷的个体。
His arrogance characterizes his leadership style. 他的傲慢是其领导风格的特征。


一般过去时:
The historian characterized the era as one of great social and political upheaval. 这位历史学家将那个时代描述为一个巨大的社会和政治动荡时期。
The report characterized the company's actions as unethical. 报告将公司的行为描述为不道德的。


现在完成时:
Researchers have characterized this new virus as highly contagious. 研究人员已经将这种新病毒描述为具有高度传染性。
Her kindness has characterized her throughout her life.  她的善良贯穿了她的一生。


过去完成时:
Scientists had characterized the substance as harmless before the recent findings. 在最近的发现之前，科学家们曾将这种物质描述为无害的。
Historians had characterized the king as a wise and just ruler before new evidence emerged. 在新的证据出现之前，历史学家们曾将这位国王描述为一位英明公正的统治者。


将来时:
Future studies will characterize the long-term effects of this medication. 未来的研究将描述这种药物的长期影响。
Experts predict that the coming decade will be characterized by rapid technological advancements. 专家预测，未来十年将以快速的科技进步为特征。


虚拟语气:
If the data characterized the sample accurately, our conclusions would be valid. 如果数据准确地描述了样本，我们的结论将是有效的。
If the painting characterized the subject's personality, it would be a masterpiece. 如果这幅画准确地刻画了主题人物的性格，那它将是一部杰作。


被动语态:
The period is characterized by its political instability.  这个时期以其政治动荡为特征。（被动语态）
The painting was characterized by its vibrant colors and bold brushstrokes. 这幅画以其鲜艳的色彩和大胆的笔触为特征。（被动语态）


疑问句:
How would you characterize the current economic climate? 你如何评价当前的经济形势？
What characteristics characterize the ideal leader? 理想领导者具有哪些特征？


否定句:
The study did not characterize the participants accurately. 这项研究并没有准确地描述参与者。
The author's style cannot be characterized as traditional. 作者的风格不能被定义为传统风格。


PHRASE->
