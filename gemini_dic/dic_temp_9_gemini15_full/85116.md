USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hardest task is always the first one. = 最难的任务总是第一个。
This puzzle is the hardest one I've ever seen. = 这个谜题是我见过的最难的。


一般过去时:
Yesterday was the hardest day of my life. = 昨天是我生命中最艰难的一天。
That was the hardest decision I ever had to make. = 那是我曾经不得不做的最艰难的决定。


现在完成时:
This has been the hardest year of my life so far. = 到目前为止，这是我生命中最艰难的一年。
That's the hardest problem I've ever solved. = 那是迄今为止我解决过的最难的问题。


过去完成时:
That had been the hardest challenge I'd ever faced. = 那是我曾经面临过的最艰巨的挑战。
The hardest thing I'd ever done was to forgive him. = 我所做过最艰难的事情就是原谅他。


将来时:
The hardest part will be saying goodbye. = 最难的部分将是告别。
Tomorrow will be the hardest day of the competition. = 明天将是比赛中最艰难的一天。


虚拟语气:
If it were the hardest thing to do, I would still try. = 即使是最难做的事情，我也会尝试。
I wish it wasn't the hardest thing I've ever had to do. = 我希望这不是我曾经做过最难的事情。


被动语态:
The hardest questions were answered correctly. = 最难的问题得到了正确的解答。
The hardest tasks are always assigned to the most capable employee. = 最难的任务总是分配给最能干的员工。


疑问句:

否定句:

It wasn't the hardest thing he had ever done. 这不是他曾经做过最难的事情。
