laminarian:/ˌlæmɪˈnɛəriən/| n. |大型褐藻；海带属植物|A laminarian is a type of large brown seaweed belonging to the genus Laminaria.  These seaweeds are commonly known as kelp and are found in cold, nutrient-rich waters. They are characterized by their long, leathery fronds and are important components of kelp forests. | Example:  Many species of laminarian are harvested for food and other commercial uses.  许多海带属植物被收获用于食物和其他商业用途。|近义词：kelp (泛指), seaweed|反义词：N/A
