minimis:/ˈmɪnɪmɪs/| adj.|极小的，微不足道的|Of little importance; insignificant.|The damage was de minimis.  损害微不足道。|近义词：negligible, trifling, insignificant|反义词：significant, substantial


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The de minimis rule applies in this case. = 在这种情况下，微不足道规则适用。
His contribution to the project is de minimis. = 他对项目的贡献微不足道。


一般过去时:
The court ruled that the damages were de minimis. = 法院裁定损害微不足道。
The impact of the change was considered de minimis. = 变化的影响被认为是微不足道的。


现在完成时:
The errors have been de minimis. = 错误微不足道。
The company has claimed that the losses are de minimis. = 公司声称损失微不足道。


过去完成时:
The earlier discrepancies had been de minimis. = 早期差异微不足道。
It had been determined that the effects were de minimis. = 人们已经确定其影响微不足道。


将来时:
The changes will be de minimis. = 变化将微不足道。
The cost increase will likely be de minimis. = 成本增加可能微不足道。


虚拟语气:
If the harm were de minimis, the claim would fail. = 如果损害微不足道，索赔就会失败。
I wish the inconvenience were de minimis. = 我希望不便之处微不足道。


被动语态:
The error was considered de minimis by the court. = 法院认为该错误微不足道。
The damage was deemed de minimis by the insurance company. = 保险公司认为损害微不足道。


疑问句:
Are the differences de minimis? = 差异微不足道吗？
Is the environmental impact de minimis? = 环境影响微不足道吗？


否定句:
The losses were not de minimis. = 损失并非微不足道。
The effect of the medication was not de minimis. = 药物的作用并非微不足道。


PHRASE->
de minimis exception = 微不足道例外
de minimis non curat lex = 法律不问琐事 (a legal maxim)
de minimis rule = 微不足道规则
de minimis value = 微不足道价值
de minimis infringement = 微不足道侵权


词源:
The term comes from the Latin phrase "de minimis non curat lex," meaning "the law does not concern itself with trifles."  "De minimis" itself means "of minimal things" in Latin.
