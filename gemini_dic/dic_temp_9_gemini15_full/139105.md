obdurateness:/ɒbˈdʒuːrətəs/| n. |固执；顽固；执拗 |The obdurateness of his refusal to compromise was astonishing.  他的拒绝妥协的顽固令人震惊。|stubbornness, inflexibility, persistence|yielding, compliance, flexibility|名词


例句：

His obdurateness in the face of overwhelming evidence was remarkable.  面对压倒性的证据，他仍然固执己见，这很了不起。(正面评价，指坚持原则)


Her obdurateness prevented her from seeing the flaws in her plan.  她的固执使她看不到计划中的缺陷。(负面评价，指不听劝告)


近义词：

stubbornness, inflexibility, intransigence, recalcitrance, pertinacity


反义词：

yielding, compliance, flexibility, pliability, amenability


词源：

源自古法语 *obdurer  "to harden, to make obstinate,"  来自拉丁语 *obdurare "to harden,"  来自 *ob- "against" + *durare "to last, endure."  因此，obdurateness 的核心含义是“变得坚硬，难以改变”，引申为“固执，顽固”。
