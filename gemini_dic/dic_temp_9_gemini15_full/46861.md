daredevil:/ˈdɛərˌdɛvəl/| n. adj.| 胆大妄为的人；鲁莽的；大胆的 | A daredevil is a person who enjoys doing dangerous things, showing a reckless disregard for their own safety. 词源:
The word "daredevil" is a compound word.  It combines "dare," meaning to have the courage to do something dangerous or difficult, and "devil," originally referring to evil but in this context, implying recklessness and disregard for consequences.  The combination emphasizes the extreme risk-taking nature of a daredevil.
