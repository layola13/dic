playtest:/ˈpleɪtɛst/ | n. v. |游戏测试，试玩| A playtest is a session where a game, typically a video game, is tested by players to identify bugs, balance issues, and other problems before its official release.  It can also refer to the act of conducting such a test. |例句：The developers conducted a playtest to gather feedback on the new level. 开发人员进行了一次游戏测试以收集关于新关卡的反馈。

The team needs more playtesters for the upcoming alpha.  团队需要更多游戏测试人员来进行即将到来的 alpha 测试。|近义词：game test, trial run, beta test |反义词：release, launch
