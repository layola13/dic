captivity:/kæpˈtɪvɪti/ | n. |囚禁；被囚禁的状态 |The state of being imprisoned or confined.  It often implies a loss of freedom and autonomy.  The duration can vary from a short period to a lifetime.|例句：The bird escaped from captivity after many years.  这只鸟在多年囚禁后逃脱了。|例句：His captivity ended when the war concluded. 他的囚禁在战争结束后结束了。|近义词：imprisonment, confinement, incarceration |反义词：freedom, liberty
