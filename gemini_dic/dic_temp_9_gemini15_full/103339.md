The term is often associated with extremist groups and violence.  The term carries significant negative connotations.|例句：The government is working to counter the activities of jihadists in the region.  政府正在努力打击该地区圣战分子的活动。

The rise of jihadist groups in the Middle East has led to instability and conflict. 中东圣战组织的兴起导致了不稳定和冲突。|近义词：Islamist extremist, militant, terrorist, insurgent|反义词：peacemaker, pacifist
