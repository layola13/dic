privatisation: /ˌpraɪvətaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 私有化 | The process of transferring ownership of a business, industry, or service from the public sector (government) to the private sector (individuals or companies).  It involves the sale of state-owned assets or the contracting out of public services to private firms. |

例句：

The privatisation of the railway system led to higher fares.  铁路私有化导致票价上涨。

The government announced a plan for the privatisation of its state-owned banks. 政府宣布了一项对其国有银行进行私有化的计划。


近义词：privatization (美式拼写), denationalisation

反义词：nationalisation, socialisation
