incubus:/ˈɪŋkjuːbəs/| n. |梦魇；恶梦；梦魔| A male demon or spirit believed to lie on sleeping people, especially women, causing nightmares.  The term has also come to be used more generally to describe a persistent burden or worry. |例句：She suffered from nightmares and believed she was visited by an incubus. 她饱受噩梦困扰，相信自己被梦魔缠身。

例句：The looming deadline felt like an incubus, weighing heavily on his mind.  迫在眉睫的截止日期像个梦魇一样，压得他喘不过气来。|近义词：nightmare, demon, burden, oppression|反义词：comfort, relief, solace
