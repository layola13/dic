pistol:/ˈpɪstəl/| n. |手枪|A pistol is a handgun, a small firearm that is held and fired with one hand.  It is typically designed for close-range combat or self-defense.|例句：He pulled out a pistol and pointed it at the robber. 他掏出手枪指着抢劫犯。|例句：The police officer carried a small, silver pistol. 警官携带一把小型银色手枪。|近义词：handgun, revolver|反义词：rifle, shotgun
