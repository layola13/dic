colorizations: /ˌkʌlərəˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |着色；着色处理；彩色化 |The process of adding color to something, especially a black and white photograph or film.  It also refers to the specific instances of this process or the resulting colored images. |例句：The colorizations of the old film improved its visual appeal.  老电影的着色处理提升了它的视觉吸引力。

The colorizations were painstakingly done by hand.  这些着色工作是费力地手工完成的。|近义词：coloring, pigmentation, enhancement |反义词：desaturation, decolorization
