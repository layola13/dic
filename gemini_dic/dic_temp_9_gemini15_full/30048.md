cc'ing: /ˈsiːsiːɪŋ/ | v. | 抄送；同时发送 |  To send a copy of an email or other message to someone in addition to the primary recipient.  It is a shortened form of "carbon copying."  | 例句：I'm cc'ing my manager on this email so she's aware of the situation.  我将此邮件抄送我的经理，以便她了解情况。


例句：Please cc your supervisor on this email. 请将此邮件抄送你的主管。

近义词：copying, sending a copy to, informing


反义词：excluding, leaving out


词性：动词
