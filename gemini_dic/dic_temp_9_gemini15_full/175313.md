raiseable: /ˈreɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 可提高的；可抬起的；可增加的 | Capable of being raised or increased.  Something that can be lifted, elevated, or increased in amount, value, or level. |

例句：

The table's height is raiseable. = 桌子的高度是可以调节的。

The salary is raiseable depending on performance. = 工资根据业绩可以提高。


近义词：elevateable, liftable, increasable

反义词：unraiseable, fixed, unchangeable


词源:  Derived from the verb "raise" + "-able" (suffix indicating capability).  "Raise" itself has Old English roots, related to concepts of lifting and elevating.
