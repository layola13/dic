fuming:/ˈfjuːmɪŋ/| adj.|冒烟的；怒气冲冲的|extremely angry; showing anger|He was fuming after the car accident. 他在车祸后怒气冲冲。|近义词：angry, enraged, irate, furious|反义词：calm, peaceful, serene

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is fuming about the injustice. 他对这种不公正感到非常愤怒。
She is always fuming when she's stuck in traffic. 她堵车时总是怒气冲冲的。

一般过去时:
He was fuming when he discovered the lie. 他发现谎言后怒气冲冲。
They were fuming because of the delay. 由于延误，他们非常生气。

现在完成时:
She has been fuming all day about the missed opportunity.  她因为错失良机而整天都在生气。
He has been fuming ever since he lost his job. 自从丢了工作以来，他就一直怒气冲冲的。

过去完成时:
He had been fuming for hours before he finally exploded. 他气了好几个小时才终于爆发。
They had been fuming all morning before the meeting. 会议前他们一上午都气冲冲的。

将来时:
He will be fuming when he hears the news. 当他听到这个消息时，他会非常生气。
She will be fuming if she finds out the truth. 如果她发现真相，她会非常生气。

虚拟语气:
If he knew the truth, he would be fuming. 如果他知道真相，他会非常生气。
I wish I hadn't told him; he'd be fuming. 我真希望我没有告诉他；他会非常生气的。

被动语态: (Not applicable; fuming is a stative adjective, not a transitive verb)


疑问句:
Is he still fuming about the incident? 他还在为那件事生气吗？
Are they fuming because of the results? 他们因为结果而生气吗？


否定句:
He wasn't fuming; he was actually quite calm. 他并没有生气；实际上他相当平静。
They weren't fuming, just a little disappointed. 他们并没有生气，只是有点失望。


PHRASE->
fuming mad: 非常生气
fuming with rage: 怒火中烧
to be fuming: 怒气冲冲
fuming and sputtering: 又生气又结巴
let off steam (after fuming): 发泄怒气 (在生气之后)
