olivine:/ˈɒlɪviːn/| n. |橄榄石|A common silicate mineral, typically olive-green in color, consisting of magnesium and iron silicates. It is a major component of the Earth's mantle and is also found in some igneous and metamorphic rocks.|例句：Olivine is a common mineral found in basalt. 橄榄石是玄武岩中常见的矿物。|近义词：peridot (when gem-quality)|反义词：N/A
