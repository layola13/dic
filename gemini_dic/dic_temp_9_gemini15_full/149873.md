palimpsest:/ˈpælɪmpsɛst/| n. |羊皮纸残卷；叠写本；（比喻）被改写或覆盖的文本|A palimpsest is a manuscript or piece of writing material from which the original writing has been scraped or washed off so that the surface can be reused for new writing.  The term is often used figuratively to refer to something that has been altered many times, with traces of previous versions still visible.|The old city is a palimpsest of architectural styles, reflecting its long and complex history.  这座古老的城市是各种建筑风格的叠写本，反映了它漫长而复杂的历史。|composite, overlay|blank slate, pristine document|名词
