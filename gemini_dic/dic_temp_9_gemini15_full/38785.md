conducted:/kənˈdʌktɪd/ | v. | 进行；引导；指挥；传导 |  To organize and carry out.  | The experiment was conducted carefully.  实验进行得很仔细。 | carried out, performed, executed | stopped, halted | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They conduct regular safety checks. = 他们定期进行安全检查。
The orchestra is conducted by a renowned maestro. = 这支管弦乐队由一位著名的指挥家指挥。

一般过去时:
She conducted the interview skillfully. = 她熟练地进行了面试。
The police conducted a thorough investigation. = 警方进行了彻底的调查。

现在完成时:
They have conducted numerous studies on the topic. = 他们已经对这个话题进行了多次研究。
We have conducted ourselves with integrity throughout the process. = 在整个过程中，我们一直保持着正直的行为。

过去完成时:
They had conducted preliminary research before writing the report. = 他们在写报告之前已经进行了初步研究。
The scientist had conducted several experiments to verify his hypothesis. = 科学家已经进行了几次实验来验证他的假设。

将来时:
They will conduct a survey next month. = 他们下个月将进行一项调查。
The company will conduct a training session for new employees. = 公司将为新员工进行培训。

虚拟语气:
If they had conducted the experiment properly, they would have gotten different results. = 如果他们正确地进行了实验，他们就会得到不同的结果。
It's important that the research be conducted ethically. = 重要的是研究必须以道德的方式进行。

被动语态:
The meeting was conducted in a professional manner. = 会议以专业的方式进行。
The survey was conducted by a reputable polling firm. = 调查是由一家信誉良好的民调公司进行的。

疑问句:
How was the project conducted? = 项目是如何进行的？
Who conducted the orchestra last night? = 昨晚谁指挥的管弦乐队？

否定句:
The experiment was not conducted according to protocol. = 实验没有按照规程进行。
They did not conduct any further research after the initial findings. = 在初步调查结果出来之后，他们没有进行任何进一步的研究。
