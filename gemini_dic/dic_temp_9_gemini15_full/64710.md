escharine:/ˈɛskærɪn/| n. | 痂，焦痂(尤指烧伤或溃疡后形成的) | A dark, dry crust or scab formed on the surface of a burn or sore.  Escharine is a medical term referring to the dead tissue that forms a protective covering over a wound. This process is crucial in the healing of burns and certain other injuries. | The escharine on his burn was slow to heal.  他的烧伤处的焦痂愈合得很慢。|  scab, crust | healthy tissue | 名词
