methought:/miˈθɔːt/| v.|我以为，我想|It is the archaic past tense of "to think".  It is now primarily used in literary contexts to create a sense of old-fashioned or formal style.|例句：Methought I saw a ghost. 我以为我看见了鬼。|近义词：I thought, it seemed to me|反义词：I knew, I was certain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I think it's a good idea. = 我认为这是一个好主意。
I think you are right. = 我认为你是对的。

一般过去时:
I thought it was a good idea. = 我认为这是一个好主意。(过去)
I thought you were right. = 我认为你是对的。(过去)


现在完成时:
I have thought about it a lot. = 我已经考虑了很多了。
I have thought about your proposal. = 我已经考虑过你的建议了。


过去完成时:
I had thought it was impossible. = 我曾认为这是不可能的。
I had thought you would come. = 我原以为你会来。


将来时:
I will think about it. = 我会考虑一下的。
I will think about your offer. = 我会考虑你的提议。


虚拟语气:
If I thought it would work, I would do it. = 如果我认为它会奏效，我会去做。
I wish I thought differently. = 我希望我的想法不一样。


被动语态:
(This verb is not typically used in the passive voice)


疑问句:
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What did you think of the movie? = 你觉得这部电影怎么样？


否定句:
I don't think so. = 我不这么认为。
I didn't think it would happen. = 我没想到会发生。


PHRASE->
think over = 仔细考虑
think about = 考虑
think of = 想起；认为
think through = 深思熟虑
think twice = 三思而后行
