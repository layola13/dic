fenestrule:/ˈfɛnɛstruːl/| n.|小窗；窗格|A small window or window-like opening, especially one in a door or wall.  The term often refers to small, decorative windows.|例句：The fenestrule in the church door let in a sliver of light. 教堂门上的小窗透进一丝光线。The intricately designed fenestrules added to the building's charm. 精致设计的小窗增添了建筑的魅力。|近义词：small window, aperture, vent|反义词：large opening, doorway|名词
