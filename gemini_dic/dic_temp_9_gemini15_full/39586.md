consecratedness: /ˌkɒnsɪˈkreɪtɪdnəs/ | /ˌkɑːnsɪˈkreɪtɪdnəs/ | n. |神圣；奉献；圣化 | The state or quality of being consecrated; sacredness; dedication.  It implies a deep sense of devotion and commitment to something considered holy or sacred.  The term often refers to objects, places, or individuals set apart for religious purposes. |


例句：

The consecratedness of the cathedral was palpable; one could feel the weight of centuries of prayer and devotion within its walls. = 大教堂的神圣气氛是显而易见的；人们可以感受到其墙壁内几个世纪祈祷和奉献的重量。

The monk's life, marked by years of prayer and service, radiated a profound consecratedness. =  这位僧侣的生活，以多年的祈祷和服务为标志，散发出深刻的神圣感。


近义词：holiness, sanctity, sacredness, devotion, dedication


反义词：profane, secular, unholy, irreverence


词性: 名词
