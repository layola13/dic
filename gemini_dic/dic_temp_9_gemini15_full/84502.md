halyard:/ˈhæl.jɚd/| n. |（船帆等的）索具，升降索 | A halyard is a rope used to hoist or lower a sail, flag, or other object.  |The sailor adjusted the halyard to raise the mainsail.  水手调整升降索以升起主帆。
The flag halyard snapped in the strong wind. 旗杆上的升降索在大风中断了。| hoisting rope, lift rope |  lowering rope
