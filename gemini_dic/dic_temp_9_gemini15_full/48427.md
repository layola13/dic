deducive:/dɪˈdʌksɪv/| adj.|演绎的；推论的|relating to or using deduction.  Deductive reasoning starts with a general statement, or hypothesis, and examines the possibilities to reach a specific, logical conclusion.|The detective used deductive reasoning to solve the case.  这位侦探用演绎推理破案了。|近义词：inferential, logical|反义词：inductive
