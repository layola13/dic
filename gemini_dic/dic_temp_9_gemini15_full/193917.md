self-elaborated:/ˌsɛlf ɪˈlæbəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 自我阐述的；自行详述的 |  Describes something that has been explained or developed in detail by the person who created or experienced it.  It emphasizes the personal and often subjective nature of the explanation. | Example:  The self-elaborated account of the incident differed significantly from the police report.  （这起事件的自我详述与警方的报告大相径庭。） | 近义词: self-explained, detailed, thorough, expanded | 反义词: concise, brief, summarized
