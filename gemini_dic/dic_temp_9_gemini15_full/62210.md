encouragers:/ɪnˈkɜːrɪdʒərz/ | n. |鼓励者，支持者 |People who offer encouragement and support.  They motivate and inspire others to achieve their goals. |例句：The team's encouragers were instrumental in their victory.  团队的鼓励者对他们的胜利起到了至关重要的作用。

例句：  Positive encouragers can make all the difference in a person's life.积极的鼓励者可以改变一个人的生活。


近义词：supporters, motivators, inspirers, cheerleaders

反义词：detractors, discouragers, critics


词源:  Derived from the verb "encourage," which comes from Old French "encoragier" (to give courage), from "en-" (in, on) + "corage" (courage).  The "-er" suffix forms the noun denoting a person who performs the action.
