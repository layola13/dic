predilect:/ˈpriːdɪlekt/| adj.|偏爱的，特别喜欢的|Having a strong preference or liking for something.  It implies a strong and often unreasoned preference.|例句：She had a predilection for wearing bright colors. 她偏爱穿鲜艳的衣服。
例句：His predilection for spicy food is well-known. 他喜欢吃辣的食物是出了名的。|近义词：preference, fondness, liking, penchant|反义词：aversion, dislike, distaste


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has a predilection for classical music. = 他偏爱古典音乐。
She shows a predilection for sweet desserts. = 她特别喜欢甜点。

一般过去时:
He had a predilection for old movies. = 他过去特别喜欢老电影。
She showed a predilection for historical novels. = 她过去偏爱历史小说。

现在完成时:
I have developed a predilection for hiking in the mountains. = 我已经培养了对山地徒步旅行的偏爱。
They have maintained a predilection for traditional methods. = 他们一直偏爱传统方法。

过去完成时:
She had already developed a predilection for art before she went to college. = 她上大学之前就已经对艺术产生了偏爱。
He had always had a predilection for solitude. = 他一直以来都偏爱独处。


将来时:
I think I will develop a predilection for gardening in the future. = 我想将来我会培养对园艺的偏爱。
She believes she will maintain her predilection for healthy eating. = 她相信她会保持她对健康饮食的偏爱。


虚拟语气:
If I had a predilection for something, I would pursue it passionately. = 如果我偏爱某事，我会充满热情地追求它。
I wish I had a predilection for learning new languages. = 我希望我偏爱学习新的语言。


被动语态:
His predilection for fast cars was noted by everyone. = 大家都注意到了他对跑车的偏爱。
Her predilection for quiet evenings was often overlooked. = 她的对宁静夜晚的偏爱经常被忽视。


疑问句:
What is your predilection in terms of music? = 你在音乐方面有什么偏好？
Does he have a predilection for any particular type of food? = 他对哪种特定类型的食物有偏爱吗？


否定句:
She had no predilection for competitive sports. = 她不喜欢竞技体育。
He showed no predilection for modern art. = 他对现代艺术没有偏爱。



PHRASE->
a predilection for... = 对…的偏爱
have a predilection for...= 有…的偏爱
show a predilection for...= 表现出对…的偏爱
develop a predilection for...= 培养对…的偏爱
maintain a predilection for...= 保持对…的偏爱


词源:
From the Latin *praedilectus, past participle of praediligere, "to prefer greatly," from prae- "before" + diligere "to choose, love."  The word reflects a choice made before others, highlighting the strength of the preference.
