garnishee:/ˌɡɑːrnɪˈʃiː/| v. n.| 扣押(工资或财产)；扣押令 |To legally seize a person's wages or property to pay a debt.  |The creditor garnisheed the debtor's wages.  债权人扣押了债务人的工资。| seize, attach | release, unfreeze | 动词
