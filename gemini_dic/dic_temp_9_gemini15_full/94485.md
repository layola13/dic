importable:/ɪmˈpɔːrtəbl/| adj.| 可导入的，可引入的 | Able to be imported; capable of being brought in from another place or system.  |Example:  The library's new system makes all the old data importable. 图书馆的新系统使得所有旧数据都可以导入。
The plugin is importable into any compatible software. 这个插件可以导入到任何兼容的软件中。|近义词：importable, transferable,  immigrate(v.)|反义词：unimportable,  non-transferable, unexportable.
