mispractises:/ˌmɪsˈpræktɪsɪz/| n.|渎职；不当行为；违规操作|Improper or illegal practices, especially in a professional context.  It often implies a repeated or habitual nature of the wrongdoing.|例句：The doctor was accused of serious mispractises resulting in patient harm.  医生被指控犯有严重渎职，导致病人受伤。

例句：The company's mispractises led to a significant financial penalty.  公司的违规行为导致了巨额的经济处罚。


近义词：malpractice, misconduct, wrongdoing, dereliction of duty, unprofessional conduct


反义词：professionalism, ethical conduct, proper practice


词性:名词
