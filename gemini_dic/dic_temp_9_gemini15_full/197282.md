英文解析:The plural form of "sharpness," referring to the quality of being sharp, pointed, or precise.  It can describe physical objects (like a knife's edge), sounds (a sharp noise), images (a sharp photograph), or even abstract concepts (the sharpness of wit).

例句：

The sharpnesses of the granite sculptures were remarkable. 花岗岩雕塑的锐利程度令人瞩目。

The differences in sharpnesses between the two images were subtle but noticeable. 两张图像之间清晰度的差异细微但明显。


词源:  Derived from the adjective "sharp," ultimately from Old English *scearp, meaning "sharp, keen."  The suffix "-ness" indicates a quality or state.
