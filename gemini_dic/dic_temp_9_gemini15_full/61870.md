empowered: /ɪmˈpaʊərd/ | adj. | 授权的；赋予权力的；得到授权的 |  Feeling confident and capable; having been given the authority or power to do something.  | Example:  She felt empowered by her new position. 她对自己的新职位感到充满力量。  The empowered women's group advocated for change.  这个得到授权的妇女团体倡导变革。 |近义词:authorized, enabled, strengthened | 反义词:powerless, helpless, disempowered


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The program empowers women to start their own businesses. = 这项计划授权妇女创办自己的企业。
He feels empowered by his new skills. = 他对自己的新技能感到充满力量。


一般过去时:
The conference empowered participants to take action. = 会议授权参与者采取行动。
The manager empowered her team to make decisions. = 经理授权她的团队做出决定。


现在完成时:
The movement has empowered millions of people. = 这场运动已经赋予了数百万人的权力。
She has been empowered to lead the project. = 她已被授权领导该项目。


过去完成时:
They had been empowered to act independently. = 他们之前已被授权独立行动。
The committee had empowered the subcommittee to make the final decision. = 委员会之前已授权小组委员会做出最终决定。


将来时:
The new law will empower consumers to protect their rights. = 新法律将授权消费者保护自己的权利。
This training will empower you to handle difficult situations. = 这项培训将使你能够处理棘手的情况。


虚拟语气:
If I were empowered to make changes, I would improve the system. = 如果我有权做出改变，我会改进这个系统。
I wish I were empowered to take more risks. = 我希望我有权承担更多风险。


被动语态:
The employees were empowered to participate in the decision-making process. = 员工被授权参与决策过程。
The community was empowered by the new resources. = 社区因新的资源而得到授权。


疑问句:
Are you empowered to make that decision? = 你有权做出那个决定吗？
Is the team empowered to solve problems independently? = 团队有权独立解决问题吗？


否定句:
The legislation did not empower the agency to take necessary action. = 这部立法并没有授权该机构采取必要的行动。
He didn't feel empowered to speak up at the meeting. = 他在会议上不敢发言。
