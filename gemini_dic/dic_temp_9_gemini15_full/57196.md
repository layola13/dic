down-payments:/ˈdaʊnˌpeɪmənts/| n. |首付，预付款 |A down payment is an initial payment made when buying an expensive item, such as a house or car, with the remaining amount paid later in installments.  It reduces the amount of money that needs to be borrowed. |例句：They saved diligently for several years to make a substantial down payment on their new home.他们辛勤攒了几年钱，以便支付新房的大笔首付。

They put down a 20% down payment on the car.他们付了汽车价格20%的首付。


近义词：initial payment, deposit, advance |反义词：final payment, balance


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The buyer makes a down payment. = 买家支付首付。
Down payments are common practice for large purchases. = 对大型购买来说，首付是普遍的做法。


一般过去时:
She made a large down payment on the house. = 她付了一大笔房款的首付。
We paid a small down payment and financed the rest. = 我们支付了一笔小额首付，其余部分融资。


现在完成时:
They have already made their down payment. = 他们已经支付了首付。
Have you saved enough for a down payment? = 你已经攒够首付了吗？


过去完成时:
Before signing the contract, they had already made the down payment. = 在签合同之前，他们已经支付了首付。
They had not made the down payment on time. = 他们没有按时支付首付。


将来时:
We will make a down payment next month. = 我们下个月将支付首付。
They will need to save more money for the down payment. = 他们需要为首付攒更多的钱。


虚拟语气:
If I had a larger down payment, I could buy a bigger house. = 如果我的首付更多，我就能买更大的房子。
I wish I had made a larger down payment. = 我希望我支付了更大的首付。


被动语态:
A down payment is required for this purchase. = 此次购买需要首付。
The down payment was made on time. = 首付按时支付。


疑问句:
What is the required down payment amount? = 所需首付金额是多少？
How much down payment can you afford? = 你能负担多少首付？


否定句:
They did not make a down payment. = 他们没有支付首付。
The buyer didn't have enough for the down payment. = 买家没有足够的钱支付首付。
