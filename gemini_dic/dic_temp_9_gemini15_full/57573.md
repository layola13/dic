dramatize:/ˈdræməˌtaɪz/| vt.|戏剧化；使戏剧性；夸张地表现|To represent or portray (something) in a dramatic manner; to make something seem more exciting or important than it really is.|例句：He dramatized the incident to make himself seem more heroic. 他夸大其词地描述了这件事以显得自己更英勇。  例句：The play dramatizes the struggles of a young woman in Victorian England. 这部戏剧戏剧化地展现了维多利亚时代一位年轻女性的奋斗。|近义词：exaggerate, sensationalize|反义词：downplay, understate|动词
