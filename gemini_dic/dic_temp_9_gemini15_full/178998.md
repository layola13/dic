redefined:/ˌriːˈdifaɪnd/ | vt. |重新定义，改写定义 | To define something again or in a new way.  It often implies a change from a previous understanding or definition.  |例句：The company has redefined its brand image to attract a younger audience. 公司已重新定义其品牌形象以吸引更年轻的受众。

例句：Scientists have redefined the boundaries of the known universe with their latest discoveries. 科学家们通过最新的发现重新定义了已知宇宙的边界。


近义词：re-interpret, reinterpret, recast, reformulate, re-evaluate, revise


反义词：define (in the original sense), establish (initial definition)


词性：动词
