microphthalmy:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈθælmi/| n. |小眼球症，小眼症 |A condition characterized by abnormally small eyes.  This can range from mildly small eyes to eyes that are severely underdeveloped and may lack normal structures.  The severity varies greatly. |Example: The infant was diagnosed with microphthalmy at birth.  婴儿出生时被诊断患有小眼球症。

The child's microphthalmy significantly impaired her vision.  孩子的眼球小畸形严重影响了她的视力。|近义词：nanophthalmos (though nanophthalmos implies smaller eyes and a higher degree of scleral thickening)|反义词：macrophthalmia (abnormally large eyes)
