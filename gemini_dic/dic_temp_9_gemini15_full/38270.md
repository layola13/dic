conversing:/kənˈvɜːrsɪŋ/ | v. | 交谈，谈话 | The act of engaging in conversation; talking with someone. | 例句：They were conversing animatedly about the latest movie.  他们正在兴致勃勃地谈论最新的电影。  |近义词：chatting, talking, discussing | 反义词：silent, quiet

We converse regularly about our work. = 我们经常谈论我们的工作。
