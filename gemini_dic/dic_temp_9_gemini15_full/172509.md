pyrethrum:/paɪˈriːθrəm/| n. |除虫菊；除虫菊花|A genus of flowering plants in the daisy family, several species of which are cultivated for their insecticidal properties.  Pyrethrum is used to make insecticides that are relatively non-toxic to mammals but effective against insects. |The farmer used pyrethrum to control the pests in his crops.  农民用除虫菊来控制他农作物上的害虫。|insecticide, pesticide|herbicide, fungicide|名词
