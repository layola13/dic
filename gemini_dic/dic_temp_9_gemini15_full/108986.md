left-handedness: /ˈlɛftˈhændɪdnəs/ | n. | 左撇子；惯用左手 | The tendency or condition of being left-handed.  It refers to a preference for using the left hand for tasks such as writing and eating.  This preference is often determined by genetics, but environmental factors might also play a role.


例句：

His left-handedness made writing in traditional notebooks difficult.  他的惯用左手使得使用传统笔记本写字很困难。

Many studies have explored the causes of left-handedness.  许多研究都探索了左撇子的成因。


近义词：sinistrality

反义词：right-handedness


词性：名词
