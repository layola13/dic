coldness:/ˈkoʊldnəs/|/ˈkəʊldnəs/| n. |冷漠；寒冷；冷淡|The state or quality of being cold; lack of warmth or affection.  It can refer to a physical temperature or an emotional state. |例句：The coldness of the winter night made it difficult to sleep. 冬夜的寒冷使人难以入睡。

例句：Her coldness towards him hurt his feelings deeply. 她对他冷漠的态度深深地伤害了他的感情。

|近义词：chilliness, coldness, frigidity, iciness (physical); indifference, aloofness, unfriendliness (emotional)|反义词：warmth, affection, friendliness, cordiality|名词
