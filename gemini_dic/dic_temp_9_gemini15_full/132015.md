noncorrelation:/ˌnɒnkɒrəˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 无相关性，不相关 | The lack of a relationship or correlation between two or more things.  It indicates that changes in one variable do not predict changes in the other.  For example, there might be noncorrelation between ice cream sales and the number of car accidents. | Example: The study showed a noncorrelation between hours of sleep and academic performance.  这项研究表明睡眠时间与学习成绩之间没有相关性。


Example: There is a noncorrelation between shoe size and intelligence. 鞋码和智力之间没有相关性。


近义词: lack of correlation, independence, unrelatedness | 反义词: correlation, dependence, relationship | 词性: noun
