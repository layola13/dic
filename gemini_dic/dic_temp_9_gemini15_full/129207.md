noblest:/ˈnoʊblɪst/| adj. |最崇高的；最优秀的；最贵族的 |Most excellent or distinguished; of the highest moral or intellectual value.  It is the superlative form of "noble".|例句：His noblest act was donating his fortune to charity.  他最崇高的行为是将他的财产捐给了慈善机构。

例句：The noblest aspirations of humanity are peace and understanding. 人类最崇高的愿望是和平与理解。


近义词：finest, best, most excellent, superior, loftiest


反义词： basest, meanest, worst, inferior

词性：形容词
