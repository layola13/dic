modest:/ˈmɑːdɪst/ /ˈmɒdɪst/| adj.|谦虚的；适度的；不大的|Of or having a humble opinion of oneself; not arrogant or boastful.|He was modest about his achievements. 他对自己的成就很谦虚。|近义词：humble, unassuming, unpretentious|反义词：arrogant, boastful, vain


USAGE->
