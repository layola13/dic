envelopes:/ˈɛnvəˌloʊps/ | n. |信封；封套|A flat paper container used to enclose a letter or document for mailing.  Envelopes are typically rectangular and have a flap that seals the contents.|I put the letter in an envelope and mailed it.我把信装进信封寄了出去。|  She addressed the envelope carefully. 她仔细地写上了信封地址。|package, wrapper|open container|
