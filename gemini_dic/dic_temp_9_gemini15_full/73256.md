fore-elders:/ˈfɔːrˌɛldərz/| n.|前辈；祖先；长辈 |  People who lived before the current generation, especially those who are respected for their age, wisdom, or position.  | Example:  The fore-elders of the tribe held a council to discuss important matters.  部落的前辈们举行了一次会议来讨论重要的事务。|近义词：ancestors, predecessors, elders|反义词：posterity, descendants|名词
