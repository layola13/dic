depreciate:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪt/| v.|贬值；贬低；轻视|To decrease in value over time; to belittle or undervalue something. |例句：The value of the car depreciates rapidly in the first few years.  汽车在最初几年贬值很快。|例句：Don't depreciate his efforts; he worked very hard. 不要贬低他的努力；他工作很努力。|近义词：devalue, lessen, undervalue |反义词：appreciate, increase, value


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dollar depreciates against the euro. = 美元兑欧元贬值。
He constantly depreciates his own achievements. = 他总是贬低自己的成就。

一般过去时:
The property depreciated significantly during the recession. = 房地产在经济衰退期间大幅贬值。
She depreciated his kindness by ignoring him. = 她忽略了他，从而贬低了他的好意。


现在完成时:
The car has depreciated considerably since we bought it. = 自从我们买车以来，车价已经大幅贬值了。
He has depreciated his colleagues' work on several occasions. = 他曾多次贬低同事的工作。


过去完成时:
The stock had depreciated sharply before the market closed. = 在市场收盘前，股票已经大幅下跌了。
By the end of the project, she had depreciated the team's hard work. = 到项目结束时，她已经贬低了团队的辛勤工作。


将来时:
The equipment is expected to depreciate over time. = 设备预计会随着时间的推移而贬值。
He will likely depreciate her efforts if she doesn't meet his expectations. = 如果她达不到他的期望，他很可能会贬低她的努力。


虚拟语气:
If the currency depreciated further, the economy would suffer. = 如果货币进一步贬值，经济将遭受损失。
I wish he wouldn't depreciate my ideas so often. = 我希望他不那么频繁地贬低我的想法。


被动语态:
The antique was depreciated by the damage. = 古董因损坏而贬值。
His contributions were depreciated by the management. = 他的贡献被管理层贬低了。


疑问句:
Does the value of this asset depreciate over time? = 这一资产的价值会随着时间推移而贬值吗？
How much did the house depreciate last year? = 去年这栋房子贬值了多少？


否定句:
The value of the painting did not depreciate. = 这幅画的价值没有贬值。
He did not depreciate her hard work. = 他没有贬低她的辛勤工作。


PHRASE->
depreciate in value = 贬值
rapid depreciation = 快速贬值
accelerated depreciation = 加速折旧
depreciate an asset = 资产贬值
depreciate the currency = 货币贬值
