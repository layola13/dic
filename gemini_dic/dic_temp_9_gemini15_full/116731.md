maul:/mɔːl/| n., vt.|猛击；粗暴对待；严重破坏；(动物的)践踏| to handle roughly; to beat or injure severely; to mangle or damage severely |例句：The bear mauled the camper.  熊袭击了露营者。|例句：The vandals mauled the park. 暴徒们毁坏了公园。|近义词：batter, bruise, crush, thrash, destroy |反义词：repair, restore, heal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mauls his food. = 他狼吞虎咽地吃东西。
The dog mauls its toy. = 这只狗撕咬它的玩具。


一般过去时:
The thief mauled the victim. = 小偷袭击了受害者。
The storm mauled the coastline. = 暴风雨摧毁了海岸线。


现在完成时:
The dog has mauled several shoes. = 这只狗已经撕坏了好多鞋子。
The hurricane has mauled the city. = 飓风已经重创了这座城市。


过去完成时:
The bear had mauled the hiker before help arrived. = 在救援到来之前，熊已经袭击了徒步旅行者。
The vandals had mauled the artwork before the police arrived. = 在警察到来之前，破坏分子已经损坏了艺术品。


将来时:
The angry mob will maul the politician. = 愤怒的暴民将袭击这位政治家。
The next storm will maul the crops. = 下一场风暴将会摧毁庄稼。


虚拟语气:
If he had been stronger, he wouldn't have been mauled by the bear. = 如果他更强壮些，就不会被熊袭击了。
If the building had been better constructed, it wouldn't have been mauled by the earthquake. = 如果建筑物建造得更好，就不会在地震中被破坏了。


被动语态:
The car was mauled in the accident. = 汽车在事故中严重受损。
The painting was mauled by the critics. = 这幅画被评论家们狠批。


疑问句:
Was the victim mauled badly? = 受害者受了重伤吗？
Has the building been mauled by the fire? = 建筑物被大火严重损毁了吗？


否定句:
The bear did not maul the campers. = 熊没有袭击露营者。
The storm did not maul the town. = 暴风雨没有摧毁小镇。


PHRASE->
