nuncio:/ˈnʌnsiəʊ/| n. |教廷大使，使节|A nuncio is a papal representative, typically a high-ranking prelate, sent by the Pope to a foreign country to represent the Holy See.  They are often involved in diplomatic relations and represent the Vatican's interests.  |Example: The papal nuncio to the United States played a key role in mediating the dispute.|近义词：envoy, legate, ambassador|反义词：N/A
