cumulus:/ˈkjuːmjʊləs/|/ˈkjumjʊləs/| n. |积云|A cumulus cloud is a puffy, white cloud that is usually associated with fair weather.  They are characterized by their flat bases and rounded tops, often resembling cotton balls. |例句：The sky was filled with fluffy cumulus clouds. 天空布满了蓬松的积云。|例句：The cumulus clouds were building up, suggesting the possibility of a thunderstorm later in the day.  积云正在堆积，预示着当天晚些时候可能会有雷暴。|近义词：cloud, stratocumulus (depending on context)|反义词：cirrus, stratus (depending on context)|名词
