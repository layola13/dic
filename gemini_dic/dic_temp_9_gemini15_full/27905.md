canteen:/kænˈtiːn/ | n. |食堂，餐厅；水壶，饭盒 | A canteen is a place where people can eat, especially a place that provides food and drink for workers, students, or soldiers.  It can also refer to a container for carrying liquid, typically water. |例句：The factory canteen serves lunch to the workers. 工厂食堂为工人提供午餐。

The soldiers carried canteens filled with water. 士兵们带着装满水的水壶。|近义词：cafeteria, mess hall,  flask |反义词：None specifically, but opposite concepts could include "private dining room" or "home kitchen".
