promodern:/ˈproʊˌmɑːdɜːrn/| adj.|前卫现代的，新现代的|  Describes styles or ideas that are a forward-looking and innovative take on modernism, often incorporating elements of the past or other styles in a fresh, unexpected way. It suggests a conscious departure from traditional modernism while retaining its core values of innovation and progress.  |Example: The building's architecture is described as promodern, blending sleek lines with traditional materials.  这座建筑的建筑风格被描述为前卫现代的，它将流畅的线条与传统材料融合在一起。|近义词：avant-garde, contemporary, neo-modern|反义词：traditional, classic, outdated
