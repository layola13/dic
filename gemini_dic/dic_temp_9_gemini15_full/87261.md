heritance:/ɪˈhɛrɪtəns/ | n. | 遗产；继承；遗传 |  Inheritance refers to something that is received from someone who has died, especially property, money, or titles.  It can also refer to the process of inheriting something or the things inherited genetically. | Example:  He received a large inheritance from his grandmother. 他从祖母那里继承了一大笔遗产。 |  Example: The inheritance of certain genes can lead to specific diseases. 某些基因的遗传会导致特定疾病。 | legacy, succession | disinheritance  | 名词
