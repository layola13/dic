overconsolidated:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsɑːlɪdeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度固结的；过度整合的 |  Describes a soil or rock mass that has undergone more compaction than is typical, often leading to reduced porosity and permeability.  This can be due to natural geological processes or human activities like compaction. |The overconsolidated clay layer proved difficult to excavate.  过度固结的粘土层被证明很难挖掘。|  consolidated, compacted | unconsolidated, loose | 形容词
