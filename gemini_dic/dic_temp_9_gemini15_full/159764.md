polyesters:/ˌpɒliˈɛstəz/ /ˌpɑːliˈɛstərz/| n.|聚酯纤维，聚酯；聚酯类物质 |A synthetic fiber made from a polymer of an ester of a dicarboxylic acid and a dialcohol.  Polyesters are known for their strength, durability, wrinkle resistance, and water resistance. They are widely used in clothing, upholstery, and other applications. |例句：Polyester is a popular fabric for making shirts. 聚酯是制作衬衫的流行面料。

例句：Many outdoor clothing items are made from polyester because of its water-resistant properties. 许多户外服装因为其防水性能而采用聚酯纤维制成。


近义词：synthetic fibers, plastics |反义词：natural fibers (e.g., cotton, wool)
