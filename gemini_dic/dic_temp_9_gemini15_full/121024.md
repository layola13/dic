miscomprehensions:/ˌmɪskɒmprɪˈhɛnʃənz/| n. |误解，误读 |A miscomprehension is a failure to understand something correctly.  It often arises from a lack of clarity in communication or a difference in interpretation.  |例句：The miscomprehensions between the two countries led to a diplomatic crisis.  两国之间的误解导致了外交危机。

例句：His miscomprehensions of the instructions resulted in a flawed project. 他对指示的误解导致了项目缺陷。|近义词：misunderstandings, misinterpretations, errors |反义词：understandings, clarity, comprehension |名词
