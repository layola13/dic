passengers:/ˈpæsɪndʒərz/| n.|乘客，旅客|People who are traveling on a public or private vehicle, such as a bus, train, plane, or ship, but are not operating the vehicle.|例句：The passengers boarded the plane. 乘客登上了飞机。
The bus was full of passengers. 公共汽车上挤满了乘客。|近义词：travelers, riders, commuters|反义词：crew, staff
