cran:/kræn/| n.|起重机；鹤；脾气暴躁的人|A crane is a tall, strong machine used for lifting and moving heavy objects.  The word can also refer to a type of bird with long legs and neck, or informally, to a person who is irritable or bad-tempered.|例句：The construction workers used a large crane to lift the steel beams. 建筑工人用大型起重机吊起钢梁。

例句：The crane stood tall and proud against the skyline.  鹤高傲地耸立在城市天际线上。


近义词：derrick, hoist, grump, curmudgeon|反义词：gentle, amiable, pleasant|名词
