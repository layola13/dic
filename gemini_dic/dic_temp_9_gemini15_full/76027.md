furies:/ˈfjʊəriːz/| n.|复仇女神，狂怒|The Furies are avenging goddesses in Greek mythology, representing divine retribution for wrongdoing. They are often depicted as terrifying figures with snakes in their hair and a relentless pursuit of justice.  |例句：The Furies pursued Orestes relentlessly until he was purified. 复仇女神们不懈地追捕俄瑞斯忒斯，直到他得到净化。

例句：His fury at being betrayed was terrifying to witness. 他被背叛后的愤怒令人恐惧。


近义词：wraths, avengers, harpies|反义词：calmness, serenity
