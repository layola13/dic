reefing:/ˈriːfɪŋ/| n. v.|礁石；暗礁；收帆；缩帆|The act of reducing the area of a sail by tying it up.  This is done to reduce the amount of wind the sail catches, typically in strong winds or when maneuvering in close quarters.|The yacht crew began reefing the mainsail as the storm approached.  船员们在暴风雨来临之际开始收紧主帆。|furling, shortening|unreefing|名词，动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reefing the sails. = 他们在收帆。
The boat is reefing on the rocks. = 船在礁石上搁浅。

一般过去时:
We reefed the sails before the storm hit. = 我们在暴风雨来临之前收了帆。
The ship reefed on a hidden rock. = 船触礁了。


现在完成时:
The sailors have reefed the mainsail several times today. = 水手们今天已经几次收紧了主帆。
The damage has been caused by the reefing of the boat. = 船只触礁造成了损坏。


过去完成时:
They had reefed the sails before the squall struck. = 暴风雨来袭之前他们已经收紧了船帆。
The captain had already reefed the sails before warning of the storm came. = 船长在收到暴风雨预警前就已经收帆了。


将来时:
We will reef the sails if the wind picks up. = 如果风力增大，我们将收紧船帆。
The captain will order to reef the sails if the storm gets worse. = 如果风暴加剧，船长将下令收帆。


虚拟语气:
If we had reefed the sails earlier, we wouldn't have capsized. = 如果我们早点收帆，就不会倾覆了。
If the captain had reefed the sails sooner, the damage would have been less. = 如果船长早点收帆，损失就会小一些。


被动语态:
The sails were reefed before the race. = 帆在比赛前被收紧了。
The boat was badly damaged by reefing on a rock. = 船只因触礁而严重受损。


疑问句:
Have you reefed the sails yet? = 你收帆了吗？
Is the boat reefing on that underwater rock? = 船只在那个水下暗礁上搁浅了吗？


否定句:
They didn't reef the sails in time. = 他们没有及时收帆。
The boat did not reef on the shore. = 船没有在岸边搁浅。


PHRASE->
reef points (收帆绳): Used to secure the sail in its reduced position.
reef the mainsail (收紧主帆):  To reduce the size of the mainsail.
reef the jib (收紧前帆): To reduce the size of the jib sail.
take in a reef (收紧帆): A general term meaning to reduce the sail area.
close-reefed (全收帆): The sail is reduced to its smallest size.
