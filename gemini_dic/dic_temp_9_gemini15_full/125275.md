multifariousness:/ˌmʌltiˈfeəriəsnəs/| n. |多种多样；多样性；复杂性 |The multifariousness of human experience is reflected in literature. 人类经验的多样性体现在文学作品中。|近义词：diversity, variety, complexity |反义词：uniformity, simplicity, homogeneity

例句：

The multifariousness of his interests made it difficult to categorize him. 他兴趣的多样性使得很难将他归类。

The museum showcased the multifariousness of cultures around the world. 博物馆展示了世界各地文化的多种多样。


词源:

* **multi-**:  from Latin "multus," meaning "many."  (表示“多”的意思)
* **farious**: from Latin "varius," meaning "varied" or "diverse." (表示“多样”或“不同”的意思)
* **-ness**:  a suffix forming nouns of state, condition, or quality. (构成名词后缀，表示状态、状况或性质)

因此，multifariousness字面意思是“许多不同的种类”。


用法说明:

Multifariousness is used to describe a situation or collection characterized by great variety and complexity.  It emphasizes the numerous and diverse aspects of something.  It's often used to describe a collection of things, ideas, or experiences that are too varied to easily categorize or summarize.  It carries a slightly more formal tone than simpler words like "variety" or "diversity."

(Multifariousness 用于描述以多样性和复杂性为特征的情况或集合。它强调事物的众多和多样化的方面。它经常用于描述难以轻松分类或总结的各种事物、想法或经验的集合。它比“variety”或“diversity”等更简单的词语更正式。)
