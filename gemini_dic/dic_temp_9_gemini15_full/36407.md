cojoined:/kəˈdʒɔɪnd/| adj.|连体的，连接的|Joined together; united.  The term often refers to twins born physically joined. |例句：The cojoined twins shared a liver.  连体双胞胎共用一个肝脏。|例句：The two buildings were cojoined by a covered walkway. 两座建筑物由一条有顶棚的走道连接在一起。|近义词：united, joined, attached, linked|反义词：separated, detached, distinct
