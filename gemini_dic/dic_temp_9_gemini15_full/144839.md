over-manage:/ˌoʊvərˈmænɪdʒ/| v.|过度管理，管理过度|To manage something too much or too strictly, often to the point of hindering effectiveness or causing problems.|例句：The project was over-managed, leading to delays and increased costs.  这个项目管理过度，导致延误和成本增加。

例句：She over-manages her employees, stifling their creativity and initiative. 她过度管理她的员工，扼杀了他们的创造力和主动性。|近义词：micromanage, control excessively, supervise excessively|反义词：undermanage, neglect, laissez-faire|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They over-manage the team. = 他们过度管理团队。
He over-manages his time. = 他过度安排自己的时间。

一般过去时:
The company over-managed the launch. = 公司过度管理了产品发布。
She over-managed her children's activities. = 她过度安排了孩子们的活动。

现在完成时:
We have over-managed this project for months. = 我们几个月来一直过度管理这个项目。

过去完成时:
