lozenge:/ˈlɒzɪndʒ/| /ˈloʊzɛndʒ/| n.|菱形；锭剂；药片|A lozenge is a small, typically medicated, sweet that dissolves slowly in the mouth.  It can also refer to a diamond or rhombus shape.|例句：She sucked on a cough lozenge to soothe her throat. 她含着一颗止咳锭来缓解喉咙痛。


例句：The window was shaped like a lozenge.  窗户是菱形。


近义词：pastille, tablet, troche|  (for the medicinal sense)
