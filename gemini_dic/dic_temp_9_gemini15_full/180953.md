reimagine:/ˌriɪˈmædʒɪn/| vt.|重新想象，重新构想|To imagine something again or in a new way; to conceive of something anew.|例句：The company is trying to reimagine its brand to appeal to a younger audience. 公司正试图重新构想其品牌以吸引更年轻的受众。

例句：Let's reimagine the story from a different perspective. 让我们从不同的角度重新构想这个故事。


近义词：re-envision, reconceive, rethink, reinvent|反义词：ignore, overlook|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reimagine the classic tale for a modern audience.  他们为现代观众重新演绎这个经典故事。
We reimagine our business strategies every year. 我们每年都会重新构想我们的商业策略。


一般过去时:
She reimagined the old house as a modern art gallery. 她将旧房子重新构想成一个现代美术馆。
The designers reimagined the logo to make it more contemporary. 设计师们重新设计了标志，使其更现代化。


现在完成时:
They have reimagined the entire marketing campaign. 他们已经重新构想了整个营销活动。
The artist has reimagined several famous paintings in a new style.  这位艺术家已经以一种新的风格重新诠释了几幅名画。


过去完成时:
He had reimagined the plot before submitting his screenplay. 他在提交剧本之前已经重新构思了剧情。
The architect had reimagined the building's layout to improve efficiency. 建筑师已经重新设计了建筑物的布局以提高效率。


将来时:
They will reimagine the city's public transportation system. 他们将重新构想城市公共交通系统。
We will reimagine the product line to better suit customer needs. 我们将重新设计产品线以更好地满足客户需求。


虚拟语气:
If they reimagined the product, it might sell better. 如果他们重新设计产品，它可能会卖得更好。
I wish they had reimagined the ending of the movie. 我希望他们能重新构思电影的结局。


被动语态:
The old building was reimagined as a luxury hotel. 这座旧建筑被重新设计成一家豪华酒店。
The story is being reimagined for a younger audience. 这个故事正在被重新改编以迎合年轻观众。


疑问句:

Will the company reimagine its outdated processes? 公司会重新设计它过时的流程吗？


否定句:
They did not reimagine the website's design. 他们没有重新设计网站的设计。
The author did not reimagine the character's personality. 作者没有重新塑造人物的性格。
