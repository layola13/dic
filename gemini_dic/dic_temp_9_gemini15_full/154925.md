pessimistic:/ˌpɛsɪˈmɪstɪk/ | adj. |悲观的；厌世的|  Expecting the worst possible outcome.  Characterized by or expressing pessimism. |例句：He's pessimistic about the chances of success. 他对成功的几率持悲观态度。


例句：Her pessimistic outlook on life made her friends worry. 她悲观的人生观让朋友们很担心。


近义词：gloomy, negative, cynical, downbeat


反义词：optimistic, hopeful, positive, sanguine


词性:形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pessimistic about the future. 他对未来感到悲观。
She is always pessimistic when it comes to her health.  说到健康，她总是悲观。


一般过去时:
He was pessimistic after losing his job. 他失业后很悲观。
They were pessimistic that the project would succeed. 他们对项目成功持悲观态度。


现在完成时:
She has been pessimistic since her accident. 自从事故发生后，她就一直很悲观。
We have been pessimistic about the economic outlook for months.  几个月来，我们一直对经济前景持悲观态度。


过去完成时:
He had been pessimistic for years before he finally sought help. 在他最终寻求帮助之前，他已经悲观了好几年。
They had been pessimistic about the outcome, so they weren't surprised. 他们之前就对结果持悲观态度，所以并不感到意外。


将来时:
He will be pessimistic unless things improve. 除非情况好转，否则他会一直悲观。
They will likely be pessimistic if the negotiations fail. 如果谈判失败，他们很可能会悲观。


虚拟语气:
If I were not pessimistic, I'd try harder. 如果我不悲观，我会更加努力。
I wish I weren't so pessimistic about the future. 我希望自己不要对未来那么悲观。


被动语态: (Pessimistic is rarely used passively)  N/A


疑问句:
Is he pessimistic about the outcome? 他对结果是否悲观？
Are they always so pessimistic? 他们总是这么悲观吗？


否定句:
He is not pessimistic; he's actually quite hopeful. 他并不悲观；实际上他相当乐观。
She is not pessimistic about the future, she is confident. 她对未来并不悲观，她很有信心。


PHRASE->
a pessimistic view 一个悲观的看法
a pessimistic forecast 一个悲观的预测
pessimistic expectations 悲观的预期
a pessimistic attitude 悲观的态度
feel pessimistic 感到悲观


词源:
The word "pessimistic" is derived from the Latin word "pessimus," meaning "worst."  It entered English through the French word "pessimiste." The suffix "-istic" indicates an adjective relating to a specific belief or characteristic.
