detonation:/ˌdiːtəˈneɪʃən/| n. |爆轰；爆炸；引爆|The rapid oxidation reaction that produces a supersonic shock wave.  Detonation is characterized by a very high rate of pressure increase and is typically associated with explosives. |例句：The detonation of the bomb caused widespread destruction. 炸弹爆炸造成了大范围的破坏。

例句：The accidental detonation of the fireworks injured several spectators.  意外引爆的烟花导致几名观众受伤。


近义词：explosion, blast, burst


反义词：implosion


词性：名词
