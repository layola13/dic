scaldest: /ˈskɔːldɪst/ | v. | 烫伤；责骂 | To burn the skin with hot liquid or steam; to scold severely. |例句：The hot coffee scalded his tongue. 热咖啡烫伤了他的舌头。 He scalded her for her carelessness. 他因她的粗心大意而严厉斥责她。 |近义词：burn, scald, scold, reprimand |反义词：heal, soothe, praise
