coddle:/ˈkɒdl/| /ˈkɑːdl/| vt.| 溺爱；娇惯；细心照料；慢煮（食物）|To treat someone too kindly and protectively, often to the point of hindering their development; to cook (food) gently in a liquid.|例句：She coddled her son, never letting him face any hardship. 她溺爱她的儿子，从不让孩子面对任何困难。

例句：The chef gently coddled the eggs in warm milk. 厨师用温牛奶轻轻地慢炖鸡蛋。


近义词：pamper, spoil, indulge, cherish| 反义词：neglect, ignore, harsh
