nonsynthesized:/ˌnɒnˈsɪnθɪsaɪzd/ | adj. |未合成的；非合成的 | Not synthesized; not produced artificially.  This adjective describes something that hasn't been created artificially through chemical or biological synthesis. It implies a natural origin or process. | Example: The nonsynthesized drug was extracted from a rare plant.  这种未合成的药物是从一种稀有植物中提取的。|近义词：natural, unsynthesized, native |反义词：synthetic, artificial, manufactured
