country:/ˈkʌntri/| n. |国家；乡村；故乡 |A country is a nation with its own government and territory.  A country can also refer to a rural area. |例句：France is a beautiful country. 法国是一个美丽的国家。  |例句：We spent the weekend in the country. 我们在乡下度过了周末。|近义词：nation, state, land, region |反义词：city, town

词源:

The word "country" comes from the Old French word "cuntrei," which means "region" or "district."  This, in turn, comes from the Latin word "contrata," meaning "opposite," referring to a region lying opposite to another.  The meaning evolved to refer to a nation or territory.
