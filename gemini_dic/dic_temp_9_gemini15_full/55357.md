distanced:/ˈdɪstənst/| adj. v.|疏远的；使疏远；保持距离| distant made into an adjective |The two families became distanced after a bitter argument.  两家人在一次激烈的争吵后疏远了。|近义词：remote, estranged, separated|反义词：close, intimate, near


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He distances himself from the controversial issue. 他与这个有争议的问题保持距离。
They distance themselves from their troubled past. 他们与他们痛苦的过去保持距离。


一般过去时:
The argument distanced them from each other. 那次争吵使他们彼此疏远。
She distanced herself from her former friends. 她与以前的的朋友们疏远了。


现在完成时:
Years of silence have distanced the siblings. 多年的沉默使兄妹俩疏远了。
The scandal has distanced him from his supporters. 这场丑闻使他与支持者们疏远了。


过去完成时:
They had distanced themselves from the project before the deadline. 他们在截止日期之前就已与该项目保持距离。
He had distanced himself from the company's unethical practices. 他早已与公司的违反道德的行为保持距离。


将来时:
She will distance herself from the negativity. 她将与消极情绪保持距离。
They will distance themselves from the conflict. 他们将远离冲突。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would distance myself from that crowd. 如果我是你，我会远离那群人。
I wish I had distanced myself from that toxic relationship sooner. 我真希望我早点远离那段有毒的关系。


被动语态:
He felt distanced by the group's cold behavior. 他感到被团队冷漠的态度疏远了。
She was distanced from the decision-making process. 她被排除在决策过程之外。


疑问句:
Are you distanced from your family? 你和你的家人疏远了吗？
How distanced are they from the truth? 他们距离真相有多远？


否定句:
They were not distanced by the disagreement. 他们并没有因为分歧而疏远。
I don't want to be distanced from my friends. 我不想与我的朋友们疏远。
