It implies a strong, burning effect. | Example: The surgeon applied a cauterant to the wound to stop the bleeding.  外科医生在伤口上涂抹了腐蚀剂以止血。 | 近义词: caustic, corrosive, burning, escharotic | 反义词: soothing, healing |  词性: adjective
