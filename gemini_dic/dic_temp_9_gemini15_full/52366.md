dictatus: /dɪkˈteɪtəs/ | n. | 命令；指令；口述；笔录 |  A dictatus is a statement or command given with authority;  it can also refer to something dictated, such as a text dictated to a secretary.  | 例句：The dictator issued a dictatus to his generals. 独裁者向他的将军们发布了命令。 The final dictatus of the meeting was to implement the new policy. 会议的最终决定是实施新政策。 | 近义词：command, order, mandate, decree, dictation | 反义词：request, suggestion, proposal | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The boss dictates the terms of the contract. = 老板口述合同条款。
He dictates his letters to his assistant. = 他口述信件给他的助手。

一般过去时:
The teacher dictated the spelling words to the class. = 老师向全班同学口述拼写单词。
She dictated a letter to her secretary yesterday. = 昨天她向秘书口述了一封信。

现在完成时:
The author has dictated the entire manuscript. = 作者已口述了整篇手稿。
They have dictated their terms to the opposing party. = 他们已向对方提出了他们的条件。

过去完成时:
The lawyer had dictated the will before the client's death. = 在客户去世前，律师已口述了遗嘱。
She had dictated her memoirs before she became ill. = 她在她生病之前已经口述了她的回忆录。

将来时:
The president will dictate the terms of the peace agreement. = 总统将口述和平协议的条款。
He will dictate his speech to his aide tomorrow. = 他明天将向他的助手口述他的演讲。

虚拟语气:

I wish he had dictated the speech more clearly. = 我希望他更清晰地口述演讲。

被动语态:
The speech was dictated to the stenographer. = 这篇演讲被口述给速记员。
The terms of the agreement were dictated by the stronger party. = 协议条款由实力较强的一方决定。

疑问句:
Was the entire document dictated? = 整份文件是口述的吗？

否定句:
He did not dictate the letter himself. = 他没有自己口述信件。
The judge did not dictate the sentencing. = 法官没有宣判。
