deliriously:/dɪˈlɪriəsli/ | adj. | 精神错乱地；狂喜地；极度兴奋地 | Extremely and wildly excited or elated; behaving in a way showing a loss of contact with reality. | 例句：He laughed deliriously. 他狂喜地大笑。


She danced deliriously, completely lost in the music. 她狂舞着，完全沉浸在音乐中。


近义词：ecstatically, wildly, frantically, ravingly | 反义词：calmly, rationally, soberly


词性：形容词
