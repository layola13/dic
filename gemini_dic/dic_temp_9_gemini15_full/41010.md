coparcenary:/kəˈpɑːsnəri/ | n. | 均分继承 | The joint inheritance of property by two or more coparceners, especially daughters inheriting equally with sons.  This term is particularly relevant in the context of English law and refers to a form of joint tenancy where all inheritors have equal rights and shares.  | Example: Under the new law, daughters have coparcenary rights to their ancestral property.  在新的法律下，女儿拥有她们祖传财产的均分继承权。|近义词：joint tenancy, joint inheritance |反义词：sole inheritance, individual ownership | 名词
