kanto: /ˈkɑːntoʊ/ | n. |关东；关东地区 | Kanto refers to the eastern region of Honshu, Japan's largest island.  It includes Tokyo and several surrounding prefectures.  The area is known for its dense population, significant economic activity, and vibrant culture. | Example: Tokyo is located in the Kanto region.  东京位于关东地区。|近义词: eastern Honshu (东本州)|反义词: Kansai (关西)
