missioner:/mɪˈʃənəri/| n. |传教士，特派员 |A person sent on a mission, especially a religious one.  A missioner is someone sent to a foreign country to spread their religious beliefs or do charitable work. |例句：The missioner dedicated his life to spreading Christianity in Africa.  这位传教士将毕生精力奉献给了在非洲传播基督教。
The young missioner quickly learned the local language and customs. 年轻的传教士很快学会了当地的语言和习俗。|近义词：missionary, envoy, delegate |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who actively opposes missionary work.
