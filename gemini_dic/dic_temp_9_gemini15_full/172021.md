purification: /ˌpjʊrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |净化；提纯；精炼 | The process of removing undesirable substances or impurities from something to make it pure.  This process can apply to various contexts, from purifying water to purifying a chemical compound. |例句：The purification of water is essential for human health.  水的净化对人类健康至关重要。

例句：The purification process removed most of the impurities from the sample.  提纯过程去除了样品中的大部分杂质。

近义词：refinement, cleansing, purification, decontamination |反义词：contamination, pollution, adulteration |词性：名词
