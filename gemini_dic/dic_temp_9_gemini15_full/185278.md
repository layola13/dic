retransportation:/ˌriːˌtrænspɔːrˈteɪʃən/| n. |再运输，重新运输 |The act or process of transporting something again; the movement of something from one place to another after it has already been transported once.  It often implies a return journey or a further stage in a journey. |例句：The retransportation of the damaged goods caused significant delays and extra costs.  重新运输损坏的货物导致了严重的延误和额外成本。

The retransportation of the refugees was a complex logistical operation.难民的重新安置是一项复杂的物流行动。


近义词：redelivery, reshipment, second transport|反义词：initial transport, first transport|词性：名词
