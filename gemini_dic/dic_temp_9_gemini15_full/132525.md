nondetrimentally:/ˌnɒnˌdetrɪˈmɛntəli/ | adv. | 无害地，不损害地 | In a way that does not cause harm or damage.  The proposed changes should be implemented nondetrimentally to the existing infrastructure.  | 近义词：harmlessly, innocuously, safely | 反义词：harmfully, detrimentally


例句：

The new policy was implemented nondetrimentally to the environment. = 新政策的实施对环境没有造成损害。

The company restructured its operations nondetrimentally to its employees. = 公司重组运营并未损害员工的利益。


词源：

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of"
* **detriment**: noun meaning "harm; damage; injury"  (from Latin *dētrimentum*, meaning "loss, damage")
* **-ally**: adverbial suffix


用法说明：

`Nondetrimentally` is an adverb that modifies a verb, describing how an action is performed. It emphasizes that the action causes no harm or damage.  It's a relatively formal word, often used in legal or technical contexts.  It is the adverbial form of the adjective "nondetrimental".
