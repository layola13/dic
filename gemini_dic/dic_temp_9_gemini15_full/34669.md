clerical error:/ˈklɛrɪkəl ˈɛrər/| n.|文书错误，笔误|A clerical error is a mistake made in the course of office work, especially one relating to written documents.  It usually refers to a minor mistake, often a typing error, rather than a significant error of judgment.|例句：The bank apologized for the clerical error that resulted in the incorrect balance.  银行就导致余额错误的文书错误道歉了。


The delay was caused by a simple clerical error in the processing of the application. 延误是由申请处理过程中的简单笔误造成的。|近义词：mistake, blunder, slip, oversight|反义词：accuracy, precision
