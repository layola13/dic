re-handled:/ˌriːˈhændld/| v.|重新处理，再次处理|To handle something again, often after a previous attempt or after some modification or correction.|The document was re-handled to correct the errors.|近义词：reprocessed, reworked, revised|反义词：left untouched, neglected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-handle the packages every day. = 他们每天重新处理包裹。
We re-handle customer complaints regularly. = 我们定期重新处理客户投诉。

一般过去时:
The mechanic re-handled the engine after the initial repair failed. = 机械师在第一次修理失败后重新修理了发动机。
The team re-handled the project proposal after receiving feedback. = 团队在收到反馈后重新处理了项目提案。


现在完成时:
They have re-handled the data several times to ensure accuracy. = 为了确保准确性，他们已经多次重新处理了数据。
The company has re-handled the marketing campaign to improve its effectiveness. = 公司为了提高效率已经重新策划了营销活动。


过去完成时:
She had re-handled the files before the meeting. = 她在会议前已经重新处理了文件。
The manager had re-handled the budget after discovering an error. = 经理在发现错误后重新处理了预算。


将来时:
We will re-handle the shipment if there are any problems. = 如果有任何问题，我们将重新处理货物。
They will re-handle the application next week. = 他们下周将重新处理申请。


虚拟语气:
If the problem persisted, we would re-handle the issue immediately. = 如果问题持续存在，我们将立即重新处理这个问题。
I wish I had re-handled the situation differently. = 我希望我当时能以不同的方式来处理这种情况。


被动语态:
The request was re-handled by the supervisor. = 该请求由主管重新处理。
The documents are being re-handled to ensure compliance. = 正在重新处理文件以确保合规。
