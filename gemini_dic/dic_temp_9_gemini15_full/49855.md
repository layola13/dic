demote:/dɪˈmoʊt/| vt.|降职，降低…的级别|To reduce someone to a lower rank or position.  |例句：He was demoted from captain to lieutenant. 他被从上尉降职为中尉。|例句：The company demoted her after she missed several deadlines.  公司因为她错过了几个最后期限而将她降职。|近义词：relegate, downgrade, reduce|反义词：promote, elevate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They demote employees for poor performance. = 他们因为表现不佳而降职员工。
The company demotes underperforming staff regularly. = 公司定期降职表现不佳的员工。

一般过去时:
He was demoted last year due to misconduct. = 他去年因为行为不端而被降职。
She was demoted after a series of errors. = 她在一系列错误之后被降职。

现在完成时:
They have demoted several managers this quarter. = 本季度他们已经降职了几位经理。
The company has demoted him twice this year. = 今年公司已经将他降职了两次。

过去完成时:
He had been demoted before he even realized his mistakes. = 在他意识到自己的错误之前，他已经降职了。
They had already demoted her by the time the investigation ended. = 等调查结束时，他们已经把她降职了。

将来时:
They will demote him if he continues to underperform. = 如果他继续表现不佳，他们将会将他降职。
The company will demote anyone found guilty of fraud. = 公司将降职任何被发现犯有欺诈罪的人。

虚拟语气:
If he had worked harder, he wouldn't have been demoted. = 如果他工作更努力，他就不会被降职。
I wish they hadn't demoted her; she was a valuable employee. = 我希望他们没有降职她；她是一位宝贵的员工。

被动语态:
He was demoted for his negligence. = 他因为疏忽而被降职。
She was demoted without any warning. = 她没有任何警告就被降职了。

疑问句:
Was he demoted because of his mistakes? = 他是因为他的错误而被降职的吗？
Will she be demoted if she doesn't improve her performance? = 如果她不改善她的表现，她会被降职吗？

否定句:
They didn't demote him despite his poor performance. = 尽管他表现不佳，他们也没有降职他。
She wasn't demoted, she resigned. = 她没有被降职，她辞职了。


PHRASE->
be demoted to = 降职到…职位
demote someone from… to… = 将某人从…降职到…
a demotion in rank = 降职
suffer a demotion = 遭受降职
face demotion = 面临降职
