jackboot:/ˈdʒækˌbuːt/ | n. |高筒靴；法西斯主义的象征 | A heavy, high boot, typically reaching to the knee, traditionally worn by soldiers and horsemen.  The term is often used metaphorically to refer to the oppressive rule of a totalitarian regime, symbolized by the imagery of heavy, inflexible boots. |例句：The soldiers wore jackboots during the parade.  士兵们在阅兵式上穿着高筒靴。

The Nazis' jackboots symbolized their brutal regime. 纳粹的高筒靴象征着他们残酷的政权。|近义词：high boot, riding boot |反义词：sandal, slipper
