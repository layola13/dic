namo:/ˈnɑːmoʊ/|/ˈnæmoʊ/| n.|南无|A transliteration of the Sanskrit word "namaḥ", meaning "obeisance," "bow," "salutation," or "prostration." It is used as a respectful prefix in many religious contexts, particularly in Buddhism, Hinduism, and Jainism.  |例句：Namo Amitabha Buddha. (南无阿弥陀佛)|近义词：obeisance, salutation, homage|反义词：disrespect, defiance
