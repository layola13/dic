casual:/ˈkæʒuəl/| adj.|随便的，非正式的；偶然的|Informal; relaxed; not formal; happening by chance.|例句：She wore a casual dress to the party. 她穿着一件便装参加聚会。

例句：It was a casual meeting, not a formal one. 那是一次非正式的会面，不是正式的。|近义词：informal, relaxed, effortless, offhand|反义词：formal, official, ceremonious, stiff
