poke holes in: /poʊk hoʊlz ɪn/ | phrasal verb | 挑毛病；批评；揭露…的弱点 | To criticize something or someone by pointing out their flaws or weaknesses. | 例句：The critics poked holes in the author's new theory. 批评家们挑出了作者新理论中的漏洞。

例句：

The opposition party tried to poke holes in the government's proposed budget. 反对党试图揭露政府拟议预算中的漏洞。


近义词：criticize, fault, attack, find fault with,  question

反义词：praise, commend, endorse, support


USAGE->
This phrasal verb is primarily used in the present tense and past tense.  It doesn't typically appear in other tenses in a straightforward manner, although it could be used within a broader sentence structure involving those tenses.

一般现在时:
They poke holes in every proposal. 他们对每一个提案都挑毛病。
He constantly pokes holes in my ideas. 他总是挑剔我的想法。


一般过去时:
The journalist poked holes in the politician's alibi.  记者揭露了这位政治家的不在场证明的漏洞。
She poked holes in his argument during the debate.  她在辩论中驳斥了他的论点。


现在完成时:
They have poked holes in the company's safety record. 他们已经揭露了这家公司的安全记录中的问题。
She has poked holes in his claims for years.  多年来她一直都在质疑他的说法。



过去完成时:
Before the presentation, they had poked holes in the data. 演示之前，他们已经发现了数据中的错误。
The team had poked holes in the strategy before implementing it.  团队在实施策略之前已经发现了其中的缺陷。



将来时:
I think he will poke holes in your plan. 我想他会挑你计划的毛病。
The reviewers will poke holes in the manuscript before publication.  审稿人在出版前会挑出稿件中的问题。



虚拟语气:
If I presented that report, they would poke holes in it. 如果我提交这份报告，他们会挑剔它。
If they were to present that data, critics would poke holes in it. 如果他们要提交那份数据，评论家们会找出其中的漏洞。


被动语态:
The theory was poked holes in by several experts.  这个理论被几位专家指出有漏洞。
His arguments were poked holes in during the meeting. 他的论点在会议上被驳斥了。


疑问句:
Will they poke holes in the new policy?  他们会挑剔新政策的毛病吗？
Did the professor poke holes in the student's thesis? 教授是否指出了学生论文中的缺陷？


否定句:
The manager didn't poke holes in the employee's explanation. 经理没有挑剔员工的解释。
They didn't poke holes in the report, despite its flaws. 尽管报告有缺陷，他们并没有挑剔它。
