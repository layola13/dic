ha'penny:/ˈheɪpni/| n. |半便士|A ha'penny is a former British coin worth half a penny.  It's now obsolete but remains in use informally.  The term often evokes a sense of smallness or insignificance.|The old woman only had a ha'penny in her purse.|近义词：halfpenny|反义词:pound, shilling (obsolete)
