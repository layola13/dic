repertories:/ˈrepərtɔːriz/ | n. |曲目，剧目，全部作品，技能范围 |A repertory is a collection of works, especially plays, that a company or performer is prepared to perform.  It can also refer to the entire range of skills or knowledge someone possesses. |例句：The theatre company has a diverse repertory, ranging from Shakespeare to contemporary plays.  这家剧团拥有多元化的剧目，从莎士比亚到当代戏剧。

The actress's repertory includes roles in both classical and modern dramas.  这位女演员的表演曲目包括古典和现代戏剧的角色。|近义词： repertoire, collection, range, scope |反义词：limitation, restriction
