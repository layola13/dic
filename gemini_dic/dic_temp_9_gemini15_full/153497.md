pension:/ˈpɛnʃən/| n.|养老金；退休金；抚恤金|a regular payment made during a person's retirement from an employer or the government.  It is usually based on years of service or contributions.|例句：He receives a generous pension from his former employer. 他从之前的雇主那里领取丰厚的养老金。|例句：She's looking forward to her pension after retiring. 她期待退休后的养老金。|近义词：retirement benefit, annuity|反义词：None


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He receives a pension every month. = 他每月领取养老金。
The company offers a good pension plan. = 公司提供良好的养老金计划。


一般过去时:
My father received a pension until his death. = 我父亲一直领养老金到去世。
