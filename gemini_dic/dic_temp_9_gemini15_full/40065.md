contactless:/ˈkɒntæktləs/ /ˈkɑːntæktləs/ | adj. | 非接触式的，免接触的 |  Without physical contact.  This adjective describes something that can be done or used without directly touching something or someone.  | Example:  Many stores now offer contactless payment options. 许多商店现在提供非接触式支付选项。

The system uses contactless technology. 该系统使用非接触式技术。


近义词：touch-free, non-contact | 反义词：contact, hands-on | 词性：形容词
