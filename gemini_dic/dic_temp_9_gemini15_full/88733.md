hoboes:/ˈhoʊboʊz/| n. |流浪汉，游民 |A hobo is a homeless and usually penniless wanderer, often traveling by freight train.  The term often carries a connotation of freedom and self-reliance, though it also reflects a life of hardship and poverty. |Example: During the Great Depression, many people became hobos, traveling the country in search of work.|近义词：tramp, vagrant, bum |反义词：settler, homeowner
