laserdisc: /ˈleɪzərˌdɪsk/ | n. |激光唱片| A laserdisc is an optical disc storage format used for storing and playing videos and audio.  It was a popular home video format before the advent of DVD and Blu-ray.  The format used a laser to read the data encoded on the disc. | Example: My uncle had a huge collection of laserdiscs, including many rare films.  我的叔叔收藏了很多激光唱片，其中包括许多稀有电影。  He played his favorite laserdisc on a dedicated player. 他在他的专用播放机上播放了他最喜欢的激光唱片。|近义词：videodisc, optical disc |反义词：None  |词性：名词
