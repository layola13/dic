call-outs:/ˈkɔːlˌaʊts/ | n. |插图说明；旁白；呼叫；点名批评 |A call-out is a brief explanation or description accompanying an image, diagram, or other visual element. It can also refer to a vocalization or announcement, or a public criticism.  |例句：The infographic included helpful call-outs to explain complex data.  插图包含了有助于解释复杂数据的说明文字。

The presentation featured several call-outs highlighting key findings.  演示文稿中有一些强调主要发现的旁白。


近义词：annotation, caption, explanation, commentary, rebuke |反义词：omission, silence, ignoring

PHRASE->
call out to = 向...大声喊叫
call out for = 急需；呼吁
call out on = 当众批评；揭露
call someone out = 公开挑战某人
call out a fire = 报警
