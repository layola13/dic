disproportionality: /ˌdɪspɹəˌpɔːʃəˈnæləti/ | n. |不成比例，不相称|The state or quality of being disproportionate; lack of proportion or balance.  It refers to a situation where something is out of balance or not in the correct ratio. This can apply to various aspects, such as size, distribution, or representation.|例句：The disproportionality in sentencing between different racial groups is a serious concern. 种族群体之间量刑的不成比例是一个严重的问题。


The disproportionality of resources allocated to different departments led to inefficiency.分配给不同部门的资源不成比例导致效率低下。|近义词： imbalance, disproportion, inequity, disparity |反义词：proportionality, balance, equity
