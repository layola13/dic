recalls:/rɪˈkɔːlz/ | vt. n. | 回忆；召回；使想起 | To bring (something) back to one's mind; to remember.  Also, to order the return of (something, especially a product) because of a fault. |例句：I recall meeting him once. = 我记得见过他一次。

The company recalled all the faulty cars. = 公司召回了所有有缺陷的汽车。|近义词：remember, recollect, retrieve |反义词：forget


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recalls his childhood often. = 他经常回忆他的童年。
She recalls the details of the accident vividly. = 她清晰地回忆起事故的细节。

一般过去时:
I recalled seeing that movie years ago. = 我记得几年前看过那部电影。
The government recalled the ambassador. = 政府召回了大使。

现在完成时:
Have you recalled the password? = 你想起了密码了吗？
The company has recalled thousands of products due to safety concerns. =  由于安全问题，该公司已经召回了数千种产品。

过去完成时:
He recalled that he had seen her before. = 他回忆起他以前见过她。
The manufacturer had recalled the product before any serious injuries occurred. = 在任何严重伤害发生之前，制造商已经召回了产品。

将来时:
I will recall this day forever. = 我将永远记住这一天。
The company will recall the product next week. = 公司下周将召回该产品。

虚拟语气:
If I could recall his name, I would tell you. = 如果我能想起他的名字，我会告诉你的。
I wish I could recall what happened. = 我希望我能想起发生了什么事。

被动语态:
His early life is vividly recalled in his autobiography. = 他的早年生活在他的自传中被生动地回忆起来。
The defective toys were recalled by the manufacturer. =  有缺陷的玩具被制造商召回了。

疑问句:
Do you recall what happened yesterday? = 你记得昨天发生了什么吗？
Can you recall the name of the restaurant? = 你能想起那家餐馆的名字吗？

否定句:
I can't recall his name. = 我想不起他的名字。
The company did not recall the product. = 公司没有召回该产品。


PHRASE->

bring something to recall = 使想起某事
at the recall of = 根据…的回忆
