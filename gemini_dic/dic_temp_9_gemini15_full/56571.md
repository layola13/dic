domino:/ˈdɒmɪnoʊ/ /ˈdɑːmɪnoʊ/| n.|多米诺骨牌；多米诺效应；|A small, rectangular block of wood or other material, typically with a line dividing its face into two square sections that are marked with a different number of spots or pips. The game of dominoes involves matching up the spots on the blocks.|例句：He knocked over the first domino, and the rest fell like dominoes. 他推倒了第一个多米诺骨牌，其余的骨牌像多米诺骨牌一样倒下。|例句：The collapse of one bank triggered a domino effect in the financial market. 一家银行的倒闭引发了金融市场的连锁反应。|近义词：tile, piece|反义词：无
