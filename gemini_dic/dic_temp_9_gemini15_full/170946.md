pseudoofficials: /ˌsuːdoʊəˈfɪʃəlz/ | n. |假官员，冒牌官员 |  Individuals falsely claiming or acting as officials without legitimate authority.  They may mimic the appearance and actions of genuine officials to deceive others and gain an advantage. This term often implies a lack of genuine power or legitimacy.  | Example: The investigation uncovered a network of pseudoofficials who were extorting money from businesses.  调查发现了一个由假官员组成的网络，他们正在敲诈勒索企业。


近义词: imposters, charlatans, pretenders, fraudsters

反义词: genuine officials, legitimate authorities
