knighthoods:/ˈnaɪthʊd(z)/| n. |爵位；骑士身份复数|The plural form of knighthood, referring to multiple instances of the honor or status of being a knight.|Many knighthoods were bestowed upon brave soldiers after the war.  许多爵位在战争结束后被授予勇敢的士兵。|近义词：chivalry, knighthood (singular)|反义词：commoner, peasantry
