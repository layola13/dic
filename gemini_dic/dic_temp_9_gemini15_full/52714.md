digs:/dɪɡz/| n. v.|挖掘；住所；讽刺；|英文解析：As a noun, "digs" refers to a person's living quarters, often informal or temporary. As a verb, it means to excavate or to make a hole in the ground. It can also be used informally to mean to find something out or to criticize someone. |例句：The archaeologists carefully digs at the ancient site. 考古学家仔细地挖掘着古代遗址。

His digs are small but cozy. 他的住所小而舒适。


近义词： (n.) lodgings, accommodation, residence; (v.) excavate, burrow, unearth,  criticize, ridicule |反义词： (v.) fill, bury, praise, compliment |词性：n. v.

They dug a tunnel under the wall. 他们在地下挖了一条地道。


现在完成时:

将来时:
