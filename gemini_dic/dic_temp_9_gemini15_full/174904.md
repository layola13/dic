racquetball: /ˈrækwɪtbɔːl/ | n. |壁球|A fast-paced indoor racket sport played by two or four players who hit a hollow rubber ball against a wall.  The objective is to score points by hitting the ball so that the opponent cannot return it. |例句：He enjoys playing racquetball after work to relieve stress. 他喜欢下班后打壁球来缓解压力。


例句：My brother is a highly competitive racquetball player. 我弟弟是一位极具竞争力的壁球运动员。


近义词：squash (but racquetball uses a larger ball and different court dimensions)


反义词：None (no direct antonym)


词性: Noun
