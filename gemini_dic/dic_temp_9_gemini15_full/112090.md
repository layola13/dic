longiroster:/lɒŋˈrɒstə(r)/| n. |长名单，冗长的名单|A longiroster is an extensive list, often implying a large number of items or names.  It suggests a list that is unusually long and perhaps unwieldy.  The term often carries a connotation of being excessively long.|例句：The committee's longiroster of potential candidates took weeks to review.  委员会冗长的候选人名单花了数周时间才审查完毕。


The school's longiroster of extracurricular activities overwhelmed the new students. 学校课外活动的冗长名单让新生们不知所措。|近义词：extensive list, long list, roster, register|反义词：short list, concise list|名词
