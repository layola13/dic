overburdensome:/ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrdənsəm/| adj.|过重的；难以承受的；负担过重的| excessively burdensome; overwhelming.  The regulations are overburdensome and stifle innovation.  |近义词：oppressive, crushing, overwhelming, excessive|反义词：light, manageable, easy


例句：

The overburdensome workload led to burnout among the employees.  过重的工 作量导致员工筋疲力尽。

The overburdensome regulations are hindering economic growth.  繁重的规章制度阻碍了经济增长。


词源：

over- (prefix indicating excess) + burdensome (adj. meaning difficult to bear)  “over-”表示超过，过度的；“burdensome”意为难以承受的，沉重的。  “burdensome”本身源于名词“burden”（负担），加后缀“-some”构成形容词。


用法说明：


overburdensome 主要用作形容词，修饰名词，表示某事物过于繁重，难以承受。它强调的是数量或程度上的过量，导致无法应对。


它可以修饰多种名词，例如：

* 工作量 (workload):  an overburdensome workload
* 责任 (responsibility): overburdensome responsibilities
* 规则 (regulations): overburdensome regulations
* 任务 (tasks): overburdensome tasks
* 税收 (taxes): overburdensome taxes


在句子中，overburdensome 通常位于名词之前作定语，例如：The overburdensome regulations were eventually repealed. (繁重的规章制度最终被废除了。)  或者作表语，例如：The task proved overburdensome. (这项任务证明是难以承受的。)
