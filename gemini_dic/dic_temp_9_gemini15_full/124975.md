muggers:/ˈmʌɡərz/| n.|抢劫犯，行凶者|A mugger is a person who attacks and robs someone, typically in a public place.|例句：The police are investigating a series of muggings in the park. 警方正在调查公园里发生的一系列抢劫案。|近义词：robber, thief, attacker|反义词：victim


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Muggers often target vulnerable individuals. = 抢劫犯经常以弱势群体为目标。
He mugs people on the street. = 他在街上抢劫人们。


一般过去时:
Two muggers attacked him last night. = 昨晚两个抢劫犯袭击了他。
The mugger ran away after stealing her purse. = 抢劫犯偷了她的钱包后逃跑了。


现在完成时:
Several muggings have been reported in this area recently. = 最近在这个地区已经报了几起抢劫案。
The police have caught the muggers responsible for the recent attacks. = 警方已经抓到了最近袭击事件的罪魁祸首——那些抢劫犯。


过去完成时:
Before the police arrived, the muggers had already escaped. = 在警察到达之前，抢劫犯已经逃跑了。
She had never been mugged before that incident. = 在那件事发生之前，她从未被抢劫过。


将来时:
The police will increase patrols to prevent future muggings. = 警方将增加巡逻以防止未来的抢劫事件。
He said he will not mug anyone again. = 他说他不会再抢劫任何人。


虚拟语气:
