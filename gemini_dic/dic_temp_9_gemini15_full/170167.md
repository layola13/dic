protophytology:/ˌproʊtoʊfaɪˈtɒlədʒi/ | n. | 原植学 | The study of the earliest forms of plant life, often focusing on fossils and the evolutionary relationships between ancient and modern plants.  | Example: Protophytology helps us understand the origins of plant diversity on Earth. 原植学帮助我们了解地球上植物多样性的起源。 | 近义词:paleobotany (古植物学), paleophytology (古植物学) | 反义词:None
