cracklingly:/ˈkræklɪŋli/| adv.|劈啪作响地；充满活力的；兴奋地 |  Describes something that makes a crackling sound, or something done with great energy and excitement.  |例句：The fire crackled merrily, casting a warm glow on the room. 火噼啪作响，愉快地照亮了房间。

The audience reacted cracklingly to the comedian's jokes.  观众对喜剧演员的玩笑报以热烈的反应。|近义词：crisply, excitedly, vibrantly |反义词：dully, quietly, sluggishly
