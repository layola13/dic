cerberus:/ˈsɜːrbərəs/ | n. |地狱三头犬；看守者|The three-headed dog Cerberus guarded the gates of the underworld in Greek mythology.  Cerberus is often depicted as a fearsome beast with snakes for hair.  The word can also be used metaphorically to represent a difficult or insurmountable obstacle.|例句：In Greek mythology, Cerberus, the monstrous three-headed dog, guarded the gates of Hades. = 在希腊神话中，地狱三头犬刻耳柏洛斯守护着哈迪斯的大门。
