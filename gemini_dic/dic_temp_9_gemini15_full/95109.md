inaniloquent: /ɪˈnænɪləkwənt/ | adj. | 无意义的，空洞的，废话连篇的 |  Characterized by empty or meaningless talk; nonsensical.  It suggests a lack of substance or intelligence in speech.  The word combines "in-" (not) with a form related to "loquacious" (talkative), implying talk that is not meaningful or worthwhile. | The professor's inaniloquent lecture bored the students to tears.  教授空洞无物的演讲让学生们听得昏昏欲睡。|近义词：meaningless, nonsensical, senseless, vapid, jejune |反义词：meaningful, significant, eloquent, articulate
