haemocytometer:/ˌhiːməˈsaɪtɒmɪtər/| n. |血细胞计数器|A haemocytometer (also spelled hemocytometer) is a counting chamber used to count cells (usually blood cells). It's a specialized microscope slide with a grid etched onto its surface.  By counting the cells within a known volume of the grid, one can determine the cell concentration in the original sample. |例句：The technician used a haemocytometer to determine the white blood cell count.  技师使用血细胞计数器来测定白细胞计数。
The researcher carefully loaded the haemocytometer with the cell suspension before counting. 研究人员在计数前小心地将细胞悬液加载到血细胞计数器中。|近义词：cell counter, counting chamber|反义词：无
