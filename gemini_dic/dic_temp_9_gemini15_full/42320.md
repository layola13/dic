counter-revolution:/ˌkaʊntərˌrevəˈluːʃən/| n. |反革命，逆革命|An attempt to overthrow a government or political system that has been established by a revolution, typically by restoring an earlier political system.|The French counter-revolution aimed to restore the monarchy. 法国反革命旨在恢复君主制。|近义词：reaction, backlash, revolt (against the revolution)|反义词：revolution
