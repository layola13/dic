remixing: /ˌriːˈmɪksɪŋ/ | v. | 混音；重新混合；改编 | The act of taking existing material (audio, video, text, etc.) and combining or manipulating it to create a new work.  It often involves rearranging, adding, or subtracting elements to produce a different result. | 例句：The DJ is remixing the song for a club mix.  DJ正在为夜店混音这首歌。

The band is remixing their old hits for a new album. 乐队正在为新专辑重新混音他们的旧歌。


近义词：reworking, re-editing, mashup,  rearranging


反义词：creating from scratch (原创)
