mottos:/ˈmɒtoʊz/| n.|座右铭；格言；箴言|A motto is a short sentence or phrase that expresses a guiding principle or belief.  It often reflects the values or aims of an individual, organization, or institution.  |Example:  The company's motto is "Quality first." 这家公司的座右铭是“质量第一”。|近义词：slogan, maxim, proverb, creed|反义词：none


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is their motto? = 他们的座右铭是什么？
What motto does the school have? = 这所学校的座右铭是什么？


一般过去时:
What was the motto of the Roman Empire? = 罗马帝国的座右铭是什么？
What motto did he live by? = 他的人生格言是什么？


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
