punkest:/ˈpʌŋkɪst/| adj.|最朋克的，最反叛的|Most punk;  extremely rebellious and unconventional.  It's the superlative form of "punk".|例句：Her style is the punkest I've ever seen.她的风格是我见过的最朋克的。

例句：That band's music is the punkest in the city.  那个乐队的音乐是城里最朋克的。


近义词：most rebellious, most unconventional, most anti-establishment


反义词：most conventional, most traditional, most conformist

词性：形容词
