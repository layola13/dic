nonnourishment: /ˌnɒnˈnʌrɪʃmənt/ | n. |营养不良；缺乏营养|The lack of essential nutrients; the state of being without adequate nourishment.  This term often refers to a deficiency in essential nutrients, leading to poor health and development.  It's less commonly used than related terms like "malnutrition" or "undernourishment."|例句：The prolonged drought resulted in widespread nonnourishment among the livestock. 长期干旱导致牲畜普遍营养不良。  The child's nonnourishment was evident in his thin frame and lack of energy.  孩子的营养不良在他瘦弱的身材和缺乏活力中显而易见。|近义词：malnutrition, undernourishment, starvation (in severe cases)|反义词：nourishment, nutrition, well-fed|名词
