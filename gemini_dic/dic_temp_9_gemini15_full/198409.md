showstopper:/ˈʃoʊˌstɑːpər/| n.|令人惊艳的表演；障碍；阻碍物|Something or someone that is so impressive or spectacular that it stops a show (literally or figuratively).  It can also refer to something that prevents progress or halts a process.|例句：Her performance was a real showstopper. 她的表演真是令人惊艳。

例句：The unexpected snowstorm proved to be a showstopper for our travel plans.  突如其来的暴风雪严重阻碍了我们的旅行计划。


近义词：highlight, sensation, blockbuster, obstacle, impediment

反义词：flop, failure, disappointment

词性：名词
