recallment:/rɪˈkɔːlmənt/ | n. |召回；收回；撤回 |The recallment of the faulty products was a costly but necessary step.  召回有缺陷的产品是一项代价高昂但必要的步骤。|近义词：withdrawal, retraction, revocation |反义词：release, introduction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company conducts regular recallments of its products. = 公司定期对其产品进行召回。
This process involves a recallment of all affected items. = 此过程涉及召回所有受影响的物品。


一般过去时:
The government ordered a recallment of the contaminated food. = 政府下令召回受污染的食物。
There was a large-scale recallment of toys last year. = 去年有一次大规模的玩具召回事件。


现在完成时:
The company has completed the recallment of the defective cars. = 公司已经完成了对缺陷汽车的召回。
Several recallments have been issued due to safety concerns. = 由于安全问题，已经发布了几项召回通知。


过去完成时:

They had already begun the recallment process before the news broke. = 在新闻爆出之前，他们就已经开始了召回流程。


将来时:

We will initiate a recallment if any safety issues arise. = 如果出现任何安全问题，我们将启动召回。

If there were a recallment, consumers would be notified immediately. = 如果有召回，消费者会立即收到通知。
