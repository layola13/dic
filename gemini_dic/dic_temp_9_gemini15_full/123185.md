monobasic: /ˌmɒnəʊˈbeɪsɪk/ | adj. | 一元的，单碱的 |  Relating to or denoting an acid that contains only one replaceable hydrogen atom per molecule, or a base that contains only one replaceable hydroxide group per molecule.  A monobasic acid neutralizes one equivalent of a base. A monobasic base neutralizes one equivalent of an acid. |

例句：

Hydrochloric acid (HCl) is a monobasic acid.  | 盐酸（HCl）是一种一元酸。

Sodium hydroxide (NaOH) is a monobasic base. | 氢氧化钠（NaOH）是一种一元碱。


近义词：univalent (in the context of acids and bases)

反义词：polybasic, dibasic, tribasic


词性: adjective
