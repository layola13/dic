corrent:/kəˈrɛkt/| adj.|正确的，恰当的|Correct means free from error; conforming to fact or truth. It can also mean to rectify or adjust something to make it accurate.  |例句：The answer is correct. 答案是正确的。
The mechanic corrected the engine problem. 技师纠正了发动机问题。|近义词：accurate, right, true, proper|反义词：incorrect, wrong, false, inaccurate|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The answer is correct. = 答案是正确的。
This information is correct. = 这个信息是正确的。


一般过去时:
He corrected his mistake. = 他纠正了他的错误。
She corrected the spelling error. = 她纠正了拼写错误。


现在完成时:
He has corrected all the errors in the document. = 他已经纠正了文档中的所有错误。
She has corrected her initial assessment. = 她已经修正了她最初的评估。


过去完成时:
He had already corrected the report before the meeting. = 他在会议之前已经修改好了报告。
They had corrected the problem before I arrived. = 在我到达之前，他们已经解决了这个问题。


将来时:
I will correct the mistake tomorrow. = 我明天会纠正这个错误。
She will correct the data before submitting the report. = 她会在提交报告之前更正数据。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would correct the mistake immediately. = 如果我是你，我会立即纠正这个错误。
It is important that he correct the error. = 他纠正错误很重要。


被动语态:
The mistake was corrected by the editor. = 这个错误被编辑纠正了。
The data will be corrected soon. = 数据很快就会被更正。


疑问句:

Are all the answers correct? = 所有答案都正确吗？


否定句:
The answer is not correct. = 答案不正确。
The information is incorrect. = 信息不正确。
