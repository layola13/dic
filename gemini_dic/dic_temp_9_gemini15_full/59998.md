editorialization:/ˌɛdɪˌtɔːriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 编辑立场; 加入编辑观点;  |The act of expressing opinions or bias in a supposedly objective report or presentation.  It involves injecting personal viewpoints or interpretations into news reporting, commentary, or other forms of writing that aim for neutrality. |Example: The news report was criticized for its blatant editorialization, favoring one political candidate over another.  新闻报道因其公然的编辑立场而受到批评，偏袒了一位政治候选人。|近义词：bias, slant, opinionating |反义词：objectivity, impartiality, neutrality |名词
