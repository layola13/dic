perishingly:/ˈpɛrɪʃɪŋli/| adv. |极度地，非常地，快要死地| Extremely; to a very great extent; almost to the point of death.  It emphasizes the intensity or severity of something, often related to cold, hunger, or other forms of hardship.|例句：The wind was perishingly cold.  = 风冷得要命。

The climbers were perishingly hungry after their long trek. = 登山者在长途跋涉后饿得要死。|近义词：extremely, intensely, terribly, awfully|反义词：mildly, slightly, moderately
