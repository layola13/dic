contemn:/kənˈtɛm/| vt.|轻视；蔑视；鄙视|To despise or scorn (someone or something).  To show contempt for.|例句：He contemned the  hypocrisy of politicians. 他鄙视政客的虚伪行为。  She contemned his cowardly behavior. 她蔑视他懦弱的行为。|近义词：despise, scorn, disdain, spurn|反义词：admire, respect, esteem, venerate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He contemns dishonesty. = 他鄙视不诚实。
They contemn violence. = 他们蔑视暴力。


一般过去时:
She contemned his arrogance. = 她轻视他的傲慢。
The judge contemned the defendant's lies. = 法官鄙视被告的谎言。


现在完成时:
I have contemned such behavior my whole life. = 我一生都鄙视这种行为。
We have contemned his actions from the start. = 我们从一开始就蔑视他的行为。


过去完成时:
They had contemned him for years before he finally changed. = 在他最终改变之前，他们已经蔑视他多年了。
She had contemned his proposals since their first meeting. = 自从他们第一次见面以来，她就一直轻视他的提议。


将来时:
They will contemn his attempts at manipulation. = 他们将鄙视他操纵的企图。
She will contemn his cowardice in the face of danger. = 她将会蔑视他在危险面前的懦弱。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would contemn such cruelty. = 如果我是你，我会鄙视这种残酷。
I wish I had contemned his lies sooner. = 我希望我早点就鄙视他的谎言。


被动语态:
His actions were contemned by the entire community. = 他的行为受到整个社区的谴责。
His ideas were contemned as impractical. = 他的想法被认为不切实际而受到轻视。


疑问句:
Do they contemn his efforts? = 他们是否轻视他的努力？
Does she contemn his opinions? = 她是否鄙视他的意见？


否定句:
I do not contemn everyone who disagrees with me. = 我并不鄙视所有与我意见相左的人。
They did not contemn her honest efforts. = 他们并没有轻视她诚实的努力。


PHRASE->
to be contemned by = 被…所鄙视
to contemn someone's actions = 鄙视某人的行为
to contemn with silent disdain = 用沉默的蔑视来鄙视
to hold someone in contemn = 对某人怀有轻蔑
to cast contemn upon = 向…投以鄙视的目光


词源:
源自拉丁语 *contemnere，由com-（加强语气）+ temnere（轻视）构成。
