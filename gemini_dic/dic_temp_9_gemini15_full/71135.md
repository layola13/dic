fishmonger: /ˈfɪʃˌmʌŋɡər/ | n. |鱼贩；卖鱼的人 | A person who sells fish.  |The fishmonger had a wide selection of fresh seafood.  这个鱼贩有很多新鲜的海鲜。| fish seller, fish dealer | butcher (反义词不完全对等，但指代不同商品的贩卖者)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fishmonger sells fresh fish every day. = 鱼贩每天都卖新鲜的鱼。
He always cleans the fish before selling them. = 他总是在卖鱼之前把鱼清理干净。

一般过去时:
The fishmonger sold out of salmon yesterday. = 鱼贩昨天的三文鱼卖完了。
She bought her fish from the local fishmonger. = 她从当地鱼贩那里买了鱼。

现在完成时:
The fishmonger has supplied this restaurant for years. = 这个鱼贩多年来一直为这家餐馆供应鱼。
He has never seen such a large tuna. = 他从未见过这么大的金枪鱼。


过去完成时:
The fishmonger had already closed his stall when I arrived. = 我到达的时候，鱼贩已经关摊了。
They had sold all the cod before lunchtime. = 他们午餐前就把所有的鳕鱼卖完了。

将来时:
The fishmonger will open his shop at 7 am tomorrow. = 鱼贩明天早上7点开门营业。
She will order more fish for the weekend. = 她会为周末多订一些鱼。

虚拟语气:
If I were a fishmonger, I would sell only sustainable fish. = 如果我是个鱼贩，我只会卖可持续捕捞的鱼。
I wish the fishmonger had more sustainable choices. = 我希望鱼贩有更多可持续的选择。


被动语态:
The fish is often cleaned by the fishmonger before being sold. = 鱼通常在出售前由鱼贩清洗。
Fresh fish is always displayed beautifully by the fishmonger. = 新鲜的鱼总是被鱼贩摆放得漂漂亮亮的。

疑问句:
Where does the fishmonger get his fish? = 鱼贩从哪里进货？
What kind of fish does the fishmonger sell? = 鱼贩卖什么鱼？

否定句:
The fishmonger didn't have any swordfish today. = 鱼贩今天没有卖剑鱼。
He doesn't sell frozen fish. = 他不卖冷冻鱼。
