interquarters:/ˌɪntərˈkwɔːrtərz/ | n. |季度的中间时期，季度之间 |The period between quarters, typically referring to the time between the end of one fiscal quarter and the beginning of the next.  This term is less common and often used in specific financial or accounting contexts.  It refers to a transitional period. |Example:  The company experienced a slight dip in sales during the interquarters.  公司在季度之间经历了销售额的小幅下降。|近义词：transitional period, interim period |反义词：quarter, fiscal quarter
