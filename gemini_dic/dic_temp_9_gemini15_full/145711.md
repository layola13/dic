overcovetous: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌvɪtəs/ | adj. | 贪婪的，极度贪心的 | excessively covetous; greedy beyond measure.  The word implies a degree of greed that goes beyond simple desire or ambition.  | 例句：His overcovetous nature led him to ruin. 他的极度贪婪导致了他走向毁灭。

His overcovetous pursuit of wealth alienated his friends and family. 他对财富的极度贪婪疏远了他的朋友和家人。


近义词：avaricious, rapacious, greedy, grasping, insatiable


反义词：generous, altruistic, unselfish, magnanimous


词源:  The word is a combination of "over-" (meaning excessively or to a great degree) and "covetous" (meaning eagerly desirous of having something). "Covetous" itself derives from the Old French word "coveiter," meaning "to desire greatly," which in turn comes from the Latin "cupere," meaning "to desire."
