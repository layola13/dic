semi-grand:/ˈsɛmiˌɡrænd/| adj.|半宏大的，中等规模的|Partially grand; moderately large or impressive.  It suggests something that is somewhat grand but not fully or completely so. It often implies a compromise between grandness and something more modest.|例句：The semi-grand piano was perfect for the small recital hall. 这架中等规模的钢琴非常适合小型音乐厅。

The hotel offered semi-grand suites, a step up from standard rooms but not quite reaching the luxury of the penthouse suites. 这家酒店提供中等规模的套房，比标准间好一些，但没有达到顶层套房的奢华程度。|近义词：moderately grand, fairly grand, substantial|反义词：small, modest, insignificant
