renationalizes: /ˌriːnæʃənəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 再国有化；重新收归国有 | To return something to national ownership or control.  The government renationalized the railway system after years of privatization.  | 近义词: nationalize, reclaim, reappropriate | 反义词: privatize, deregulate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government renationalizes failing industries. = 政府对那些即将倒闭的行业进行再国有化。
They renationalize the airline every year. = 他们每年都会对航空公司进行再国有化。


一般过去时:
The country renationalized its oil industry in the 1970s. = 这个国家在20世纪70年代对石油工业进行了再国有化。
They renationalized the bank after the financial crisis.  = 他们在金融危机后对银行进行了再国有化。


现在完成时:
The government has renationalized several key industries. = 政府已经对几个关键行业进行了再国有化。
They have renationalized the power grid recently. = 他们最近对电网进行了再国有化。


过去完成时:
Before the election, the party had renationalized the water company. = 大选前，该党已对水务公司进行了再国有化。
It had renationalized several key assets before the economic downturn. = 在经济衰退之前，它已经对几项关键资产进行了再国有化。


将来时:
The new government will renationalize the postal service. = 新政府将对邮政服务进行再国有化。
They will renationalize the railways next year. = 他们明年将对铁路进行再国有化。


虚拟语气:
If the economy worsened, they would renationalize the banks. = 如果经济恶化，他们将对银行进行再国有化。
It would be better if the government renationalized the energy sector. = 如果政府对能源部门进行再国有化，将会更好。


被动语态:
The railway system was renationalized last year. = 铁路系统去年被重新收归国有。
Several industries are being renationalized by the government. = 政府正在对几个行业进行再国有化。


疑问句:
Will the government renationalize the airline? = 政府会对航空公司进行再国有化吗？
Has the company been renationalized? = 这家公司已经被收归国有了吗？


否定句:
The government did not renationalize the telecommunications industry. = 政府没有对电信行业进行再国有化。
They will not renationalize the steel mills. = 他们不会对钢铁厂进行再国有化。


PHRASE->
renationalize the railways = 对铁路进行再国有化
renationalize the banks = 对银行进行再国有化
renationalize state-owned enterprises = 对国有企业进行再国有化
renationalize key industries = 对关键行业进行再国有化
renationalize the energy sector = 对能源部门进行再国有化


词源:
re- (again) + nationalize (to bring under the control of a nation)
nationalize comes from nation (a large body of people united by common descent, history, culture, or language, inhabiting a particular country or territory) + -ize (to make or become)
