recommencement:/ˌriːkəˈmɛnsmənt/| n. |重新开始，恢复|The act of beginning something again after it has been interrupted or stopped.  It often implies a formal or official restarting, such as the commencement of a new session or term.|例句：The recommencement of the negotiations was welcomed by both sides. = 双方都欢迎谈判的重新开始。

例句：After the storm, the recommencement of work on the bridge was delayed. = 风暴过后，桥梁工程的重新开工被推迟了。


近义词：resumption, restart, renewal,reinstatement|反义词：cessation, termination, ending, conclusion|词性:名词
