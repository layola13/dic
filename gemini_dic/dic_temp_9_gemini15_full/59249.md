e-numbers:/ˈiːˌnʌmbərz/| n. |食品添加剂编号 |E-numbers are codes used to identify food additives in the European Union and other countries.  They are assigned by the European Commission and indicate that the additive has been approved for use in food.  Each E-number corresponds to a specific substance, and the number provides a standardized way to list ingredients on food labels.  |Example: Many processed foods contain E-numbers, such as E102 (tartrazine) and E330 (citric acid). 许多加工食品含有食品添加剂编号，例如E102（柠檬黄）和E330（柠檬酸）。|近义词：food additives, food coloring, preservatives |反义词：natural ingredients, unprocessed food |名词
