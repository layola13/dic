narcissus:/nɑːrˈsɪsəs/| n. |水仙花；自恋者 |A narcissus is a flowering plant with a distinctive trumpet-shaped flower.  The term also refers to a person who has excessive admiration of themselves. |例句：The garden was filled with beautiful daffodils and narcissus. 花园里盛开着美丽的水仙和黄水仙。  He's such a narcissist; he only talks about himself. 他真是个自恋狂，只会谈论自己。|近义词：daffodil (for the flower), egotist, self-admirer |反义词：humble, modest, unassuming
