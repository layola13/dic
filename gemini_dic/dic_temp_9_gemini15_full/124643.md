mouthiest:/ˈmaʊθiɪst/ | adj. | 最爱说话的；最喋喋不休的 | Most given to talking; most talkative.  It is the superlative form of "mouthy". |例句：He was the mouthiest person in the room, constantly interrupting others. 他是房间里最爱说话的人，不停地打断别人。
例句：The mouthiest critic was silenced by the overwhelming evidence.  最爱喋喋不休的评论家被压倒性的证据堵住了嘴。 |近义词：most loquacious, most voluble, most garrulous |反义词：most taciturn, most reticent, most silent

PHRASE->
(The word "mouthiest" itself doesn't readily form common phrases. Its usage is primarily as a descriptive adjective.)  The following phrases use "mouthy" which is the positive form:

be mouthy = 夸夸其谈
get mouthy = 开始夸夸其谈
a mouthy person = 一个爱说话的人
mouthy teenager = 一个爱顶嘴的青少年
mouthy response =  尖刻的回答


词源:
The word "mouthiest" is derived from "mouthy," which in turn comes from "mouth"  (the organ used for speaking).  The "-iest" suffix is a superlative ending, indicating the highest degree.  Therefore, "mouthiest" signifies the "most mouthy."
