headed:/ˈhedɪd/ | adj. | 有头的；朝着…方向的；为首的 | Having a head;  moving in a specified direction; leading or managing. |例句：The headed nail is easier to drive in.  带头的钉子更容易敲进去。|例句：The car headed north.  汽车向北行驶。|近义词：leading, foremost, directed |反义词：headless, tailless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is headed by a seasoned expert. = 这个项目由一位经验丰富的专家领导。
The arrow is headed towards the target. = 箭头正指向目标。


一般过去时:
The parade headed down Main Street. = 游行队伍沿着主要街道前进。
The ship headed for the open sea. = 船驶向公海。


现在完成时:
The team has headed off in different directions. = 球队成员已经朝不同的方向出发了。
We have headed towards a solution. = 我们朝着解决方案的方向努力了。


过去完成时:
They had headed home before the storm broke. = 暴风雨来临之前他们已经回家了。
The hikers had headed back to camp before nightfall. = 徒步旅行者在夜幕降临前已经回到营地了。


将来时:
We will head towards the mountains tomorrow. = 我们明天将前往山区。
The company will head into new markets next year. = 公司明年将进军新的市场。


虚拟语气:
If the project were headed by someone more experienced, it would be more successful. = 如果这个项目由更有经验的人领导，它会更成功。
If I were headed in the right direction, I wouldn't be so lost. = 如果我走对了方向，就不会这么迷路了。


被动语态:
The meeting was headed by the CEO. = 会议由首席执行官主持。
The expedition was headed towards the summit. = 探险队正朝着山顶前进。


疑问句:
Is the letter headed correctly? = 信封的地址写对了吗？
Which way is the train headed? = 火车往哪个方向开？


否定句:
The arrow was not headed in the right direction. = 箭头没有指向正确的方向。
The company is not headed for success. = 公司不会成功。


PHRASE->
headed for: 朝…方向前进
well-headed: 头脑清醒的
strong-headed: 固执的
single-headed: 单头的
two-headed: 双头的
