pulsator:/ˈpʌlseɪtər/| n. |脉动器，搏动器|A pulsator is a device that produces a pulsating or rhythmic action, often used in pumps or other machinery to create a regular flow or pressure. It can also refer to something that pulsates or beats rhythmically. |Example: The heart is a natural pulsator, rhythmically pumping blood throughout the body. 心脏是天然的搏动器，有节奏地将血液泵送到全身。  The milking machine uses a pulsator to create a rhythmic suction and release. 挤奶机使用脉动器产生有节奏的吸力和释放。|近义词：oscillator, vibrator|反义词：static device|名词
