remuneratively: /rɪˈmjuːnəreɪtɪvli/ | adv. | 有报酬地；有偿地 |  In a way that provides payment or reward.  This adverb describes actions or processes that are done with compensation. | 例句：He worked remuneratively on the project, earning a substantial fee for his efforts. 他有偿地参与了这个项目，为他的努力获得了可观的报酬。


The company remuneratively compensated its employees for their overtime work. 公司有偿地补偿了员工的加班费。


近义词：profitably, rewardingly, compensatorily | 反义词：gratuitously, uncompensatedly, for free


词源:  The word derives from the verb "remunerate," which comes from the Latin word "remunerare," meaning "to pay back, reward, recompense."  The suffix "-atively" transforms the verb into an adverb, indicating the manner in which something is done.
