oscillate:/ˈɒsɪleɪt/ /ˈɑːsɪleɪt/| v.| 振荡；摆动；犹豫 | to move or swing back and forth regularly | The pendulum oscillated back and forth.  钟摆来回摆动。The needle on the gauge oscillated wildly. 仪表上的指针剧烈摆动。| fluctuate, vibrate, sway | stabilize, remain constant | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The needle oscillates constantly. 指针不断摆动。
He oscillates between hope and despair. 他在希望与绝望之间徘徊。

一般过去时:
The fan oscillated gently. 风扇轻轻地摆动。
She oscillated between two job offers. 她在这两份工作邀请之间犹豫不决。

现在完成时:
The price of oil has oscillated wildly this year. 油价今年波动剧烈。
My opinion on the matter has oscillated over time. 我对这件事的看法随着时间的推移而改变。

过去完成时:
The machine had oscillated for hours before it finally broke down. 机器在最终坏掉之前已经摆动了好几个小时。
Before making the decision, she had oscillated between several options. 在做决定之前，她曾在几个选择之间犹豫不决。

将来时:
The stock price will likely oscillate in the coming weeks. 股价未来几周可能会波动。
He will probably oscillate between acceptance and anger. 他可能会在接受和愤怒之间摇摆不定。

虚拟语气:
If the pendulum oscillated more slowly, we could measure its speed more accurately. 如果钟摆摆动得更慢，我们可以更精确地测量它的速度。
If the temperature oscillated less, the experiment would be more successful. 如果温度波动较小，实验将会更成功。

被动语态:
The data was oscillated to test the system's response. 对数据进行振荡以测试系统的响应。
The image was oscillated to simulate movement. 图像被摆动以模拟运动。

疑问句:
Does the needle oscillate normally? 指针正常摆动吗？
Does the market usually oscillate so dramatically? 市场通常会如此剧烈波动吗？

否定句:
The machine doesn't oscillate smoothly. 机器摆动不平稳。
The temperature didn't oscillate significantly during the experiment. 实验期间温度没有明显波动。
