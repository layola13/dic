reinvoking:/ˌriːɪnˈvoʊkɪŋ/| v.|重新援引；再次祈求；重新启用|The act of invoking again; calling upon again; reactivating something.|例句：The government is reinvoking emergency powers. 政府正在重新启用紧急权力。
例句：She reinvoked the memory of her childhood. 她再次回忆起童年记忆。|近义词：reactivate, revive, recall|反义词：abolish, cancel, repeal|动词
