/ˈhɛlənaɪz/| vt.|使希腊化；使具有希腊特色|To imbue with Greek characteristics or culture; to make something Greek in nature.  The Roman Empire hellenized many conquered territories. 罗马帝国使许多被征服的领土希腊化了。|近义词：graecize|反义词：barbarise


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He hellenizes his garden design with classical statues. = 他用古典雕像使他的花园设计具有希腊特色。
She hellenizes her writing style by incorporating ancient Greek phrases. = 她通过融入古希腊短语使她的写作风格具有希腊特色。

Roman emperors hellenized many aspects of their governance. = 罗马皇帝使他们统治的许多方面希腊化了。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
