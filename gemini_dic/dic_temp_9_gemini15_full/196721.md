sewerage:/ˈsuːərɪdʒ/| n. |污水系统；污水；下水道|The system for collecting and removing wastewater and sewage.  It encompasses pipes, pumps, and treatment plants.|例句：The city's sewerage system is outdated and needs upgrading. = 这个城市的污水系统已经过时，需要升级。

例句：A blockage in the sewerage caused flooding in the basement. = 下水道堵塞导致地下室发生洪水。

近义词：drainage, wastewater system, sewage system |反义词：clean water, purified water
