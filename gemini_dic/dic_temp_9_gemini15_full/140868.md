on-the-point-of:/ˌɒn ðə pɔɪnt əv/|adj.|即将…的；正要…的|about to; on the verge of; at the point of|例句：He was on the point of leaving when the phone rang. 他正要离开时电话响了。|例句：The country was on the point of collapse.  国家正处于崩溃的边缘。|近义词：about to, on the verge of, imminently|反义词：far from, distant from|词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is on the point of making a decision. = 他正要做出决定。
She is on the point of finishing her work. = 她正要完成她的工作。


一般过去时:
He was on the point of leaving when I called. = 我打电话时他正要离开。
They were on the point of arguing when their mother intervened. = 他们正要争吵时，他们的母亲介入了。


现在完成时:
She has been on the point of quitting her job for months. = 她几个月来一直想辞职。
He has been on the point of calling you all day. = 他一整天都想给你打电话。


过去完成时:
I had been on the point of giving up when I finally found the solution. = 我几乎要放弃了，这时我终于找到了解决办法。
They had been on the point of signing the contract when they discovered a clause they didn't like. = 他们正要签合同的时候，发现了一个他们不喜欢的条款。


将来时:
He will be on the point of graduating next month. = 他下个月就要毕业了。
She will be on the point of delivering her speech in a few minutes. = 几分钟后她就要发表演讲了。


虚拟语气:
If he had been on the point of death, he wouldn't have laughed so heartily. = 如果他当时快要死了，他就不会笑得那么开心了。
I wish I hadn't been on the point of leaving when you arrived. = 我真希望你到的时候我还没要离开。


被动语态:
The project was on the point of being cancelled. = 项目即将被取消。
The building was on the point of being demolished. = 这座建筑物即将被拆除。


疑问句:
Was he on the point of resigning? = 他正要辞职吗？
Is she on the point of making a breakthrough? = 她正要取得突破吗？


否定句:
He was not on the point of giving up. = 他并没有要放弃。
The negotiations were not on the point of collapsing. = 谈判并没有即将破裂。


PHRASE->
on the point of collapse = 即将崩溃
on the point of departure = 即将出发
on the point of no return = 无法回头
on the point of explosion = 即将爆发
on the point of success = 即将成功
