repeopling: /ˌriːˈpɑːplɪŋ/ | n. |  重新居住，重新填充人口 | The act of causing an area to be populated again after a decline in population.  This often involves resettlement programs or natural population growth. | 例句：The government launched a massive repeopling initiative for the abandoned villages.  政府启动了一项针对废弃村庄的大规模重新居住计划。

The repeopling of the city center is a slow process. 市中心的重新人口填充是一个缓慢的过程。


近义词：resettlement, repopulation, recolonization | 反义词：depopulation, evacuation


词源:  re- (again) + people (people) + -ing (present participle suffix)  It's a relatively modern word formed by combining the prefix "re-" indicating repetition or renewal with the noun "people" and the present participle suffix "-ing" to create a gerund (noun form of a verb).
