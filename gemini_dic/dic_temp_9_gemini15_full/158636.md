plighted:/ˈplaɪtɪd/| adj. | 许诺的；订婚的；保证的 | pledged or promised, especially in marriage;  bound by a pledge or promise | The plighted couple exchanged rings. 这对订婚的情侣交换了戒指。  |近义词：betrothed, engaged, promised | 反义词：unpledged, free
