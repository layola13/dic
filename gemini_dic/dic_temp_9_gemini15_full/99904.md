internationalisms: /ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlɪzəm/ | n. | 国际主义；国际习语 | Words or phrases that are used in many different languages.  They often originate in one language and then spread to others, sometimes with slight variations in meaning or spelling.  Internationalisms can be words, phrases, or even grammatical structures. | Example:  The word "restaurant" is an internationalism, originating in French and now used worldwide.  Many scientific and technical terms are also internationalisms. | 近义词：globalism, cosmopolitanism | 反义词：nationalism, parochialism |  名词
