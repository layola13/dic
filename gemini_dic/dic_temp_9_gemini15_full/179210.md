rediscussions:/ˌriːdɪˈskʌʃənz/| n. |再次讨论；重新讨论|Further discussions or debates on a previously discussed topic.  It often implies a more in-depth or renewed examination of the subject.|例句：The rediscussions on the budget resulted in several significant changes.  再次讨论预算导致了一些重大变化。

例句：After initial objections, rediscussions led to a compromise.  在最初的反对之后，重新讨论导致了妥协。

近义词：reconsiderations, re-examinations, reviews

反义词：final decisions, conclusions
