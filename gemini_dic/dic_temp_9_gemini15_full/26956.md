calcareous:/ˈkælkeriəs/| adj.|含钙的，石灰质的|Containing calcium carbonate; chalky.  The calcareous soil was ideal for growing certain types of plants.|近义词：chalky, limy|反义词：siliceous


例句：

The calcareous deposits formed a thick layer on the cave walls.  含钙沉积物在洞穴壁上形成了一层厚厚的沉积层。

The farmer added lime to the calcareous soil to improve drainage.  农民向石灰质土壤中添加石灰以改善排水。


词源:

From Latin *calcar "lime, limestone," from calx "lime, limestone," ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱelh₂- "to burn."  The suffix "-ous" indicates "full of" or "characterized by."


用法说明：

calcareous 主要用作形容词，修饰名词，表示含有碳酸钙或具有石灰质特性。 它常用于地质学、生物学和农业等领域。
