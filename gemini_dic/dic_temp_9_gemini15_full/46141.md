cymograph:/ˈsaɪməɡræf/| n. |肌动描记器；肌动描记图 |A cymograph is an instrument used to record the movements or contractions of muscles.  It produces a visual representation of these movements, typically on a rotating drum or chart.  | Example: The scientist used a cymograph to study the rhythmic contractions of the heart.  科学家用肌动描记器来研究心脏的有节奏的收缩。|近义词：myograph |反义词：无
