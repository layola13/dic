scorches:/ˈskɔːrtʃɪz/| v. | 烧焦；炙烤；痛斥 | To burn the surface of something with a sudden, brief application of heat; to criticize severely. | 例句：The sun scorched the grass.  烈日把草烧焦了。The critic scorched the author's new novel in his review. 批评家在他的评论中痛斥了作者的新小说。| 近义词：burn, sear, singe, criticize harshly | 反义词：soothe, calm, praise
