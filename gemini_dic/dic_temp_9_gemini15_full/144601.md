outweaving: /ˌaʊtˈwiːvɪŋ/ | v. | 超越；胜过（在技艺或能力上） |  To surpass or excel in skill or ability.  | The skilled weaver outwaved all her competitors.  熟练的织工技艺胜过所有竞争者。 | surpass, excel, outdo | be surpassed by, be inferior to | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outweaves his colleagues in design. = 他在设计方面胜过他的同事。
The new software outweaves the old one in efficiency. = 新软件在效率上胜过旧软件。

一般过去时:
She outwaved her rival in the knitting competition. = 她在编织比赛中胜过了她的对手。
The team outwaved their opponents in strategy. = 这支队伍在策略上胜过了他们的对手。

现在完成时:
He has outwaved all expectations in his career. = 他在职业生涯中已经超越了所有人的期望。
The company has outwaved its competitors in market share. = 这家公司在市场份额上已经超越了竞争对手。

过去完成时:

She had outwaved all previous records in the marathon. = 她在马拉松比赛中已经打破了所有之前的记录。

将来时:
