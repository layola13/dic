non-clearance: /ˌnɒnˈklɪərəns/ | adj. | 非清关的；未经清关的 | Not having undergone customs clearance.  This term refers to goods or shipments that have not yet completed the necessary customs procedures for legal entry into a country.  | 例句：The non-clearance of the goods caused a significant delay in the project.  未经清关的货物导致项目严重延误。


The non-clearance of the shipment resulted in hefty fines.  货物未经清关导致巨额罚款。


近义词：un-cleared, uncleared, uninspected


反义词：cleared, customs-cleared


词性：形容词
