rooting:/ˈruːtɪŋ/| n. v.|生根；支持；鼓励；挖掘；捣乱|The act of planting or establishing something firmly, or the act of supporting or encouraging someone or something.  It can also refer to the act of searching or digging.|例句：The gardener was busy rooting cuttings. 园丁正忙着扦插。 The fans were rooting for their team.  球迷们都在为他们的球队加油。|近义词：supporting, encouraging, cheering, excavating, digging|反义词：discouraging, opposing, hindering


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The plant is rooting well in its new pot. = 这盆植物在新花盆里长得很好。
She is rooting for her favorite team in the championship game. = 她在冠军赛中为她最喜欢的球队加油。


一般过去时:
The farmer rooted the potato plants carefully. = 农民仔细地种植了土豆。
He rooted through the drawer looking for his keys. = 他翻遍抽屉找钥匙。


现在完成时:
The tree has rooted itself firmly in the ground. = 这棵树已经牢牢地扎根于地下。
They have been rooting for the underdog all season. = 整个赛季他们都在为弱者加油。


过去完成时:
The seeds had rooted before the first frost. = 种子在第一次霜冻来临之前就已经生根了。
She had rooted around in the attic for hours before she found it. = 她花了几个小时在阁楼上翻找才找到它。


将来时:
The cuttings will root easily in moist soil. = 扦插枝条很容易在潮湿的土壤中生根。
I will be rooting for you in your upcoming competition. = 我会在你即将到来的比赛中为你加油。


虚拟语气:
If only I had rooted the plants earlier, they would be taller now. = 早知道我早点种下这些植物就好了，现在它们会长高一些。
If the team played better, the fans would be rooting for them more enthusiastically. = 如果球队表现更好，球迷们就会更热情地为他们加油。


被动语态:
The plant was rooted in the garden bed. = 植物被种植在菜园里。
The seeds were rooted by the gardener.  = 种子由园丁播种。


疑问句:
Is the cutting rooting properly? = 扦插枝条生根了吗？
Are you rooting for the home team tonight? = 你今晚支持主队吗？


否定句:
The plant is not rooting well. = 植物长得不好。
I'm not rooting for either team. = 我不支持任何一方。


PHRASE->

root out: 根除
take root: 生根；扎根
root for: 支持；为...加油
root around: 翻找；搜寻
put down roots: 安家落户
