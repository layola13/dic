pendanted:/pɛnˈdæntɪd/ | adj. |  悬挂的；装饰有垂饰的 |  Adorned with pendants; hanging down like a pendant.  The word describes something decorated with, or resembling, pendants (hanging ornaments).  | 例句：The chandelier was pendanted with glittering crystals.  = 枝形吊灯上垂挂着闪闪发光的晶体。 The earrings were pendanted with tiny pearls. = 耳环上垂挂着小小的珍珠。| 近义词：adorned, decorated, ornamented, hanging | 反义词：plain, undecorated
