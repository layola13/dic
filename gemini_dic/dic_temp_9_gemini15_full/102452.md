jackhammers:/ˈdʒækˌhæmər/| n.|破路机，碎石机|A jackhammer is a pneumatic tool used for breaking up hard surfaces such as concrete or rock.  It uses compressed air to deliver powerful blows.|The construction workers used jackhammers to demolish the old building.  建筑工人用破路机拆除了旧楼。|近义词：pneumatic drill, demolition hammer|反义词:None.
