cross-fertilising: /krɒsˈfɜːtɪlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 交叉授粉；互相借鉴；思想交流 | The act of fertilizing a plant with pollen from a different variety or species, or the transfer of ideas or influences between different groups or fields.  | Example:  The company benefited from cross-fertilising ideas with its international partners. 公司从与国际合作伙伴的思想交流中获益匪浅。 | 近义词：interbreeding, pollination, cross-pollination | 反义词：inbreeding, self-fertilization


USAGE->

They cross-fertilise their ideas regularly. = 他们定期进行思想交流。
The farmers cross-fertilise their crops to improve yield. = 农民们对作物进行杂交以提高产量。
