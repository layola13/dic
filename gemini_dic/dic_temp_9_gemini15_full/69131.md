favorite:/ˈfeɪvərɪt/| adj. n.| 最喜欢的；宠儿 |  A person or thing that is preferred above all others.  |例句：My favorite color is blue. = 我最喜欢的颜色是蓝色。|例句：He is the teacher's favorite. = 他是老师的宠儿。|近义词：preferred, beloved, darling |反义词：least favorite, disliked


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My favorite food is pizza. = 我最喜欢的食物是披萨。
Her favorite hobby is reading. = 她最喜欢的爱好是阅读。

一般过去时:
My favorite book was "To Kill a Mockingbird". = 我最喜欢的书是《杀死一只知更鸟》。
His favorite game was soccer when he was younger. = 他年轻时最喜欢的游戏是足球。

现在完成时:
My favorite band has been touring all year. = 我最喜欢的乐队一直在巡演。
She's always said that her favorite animal has been a dog. = 她总是说她最喜欢的动物一直是狗。

过去完成时:
Before the trip, my favorite activity had been hiking. = 旅行前，我最喜欢的活动是远足。
He had already stated that his favorite song was "Bohemian Rhapsody". = 他已经说过他最喜欢的歌是《波西米亚狂想曲》。


将来时:
I think my favorite sport will always be basketball. = 我认为我最喜欢的运动将永远是篮球。
Her favorite movie will likely be the new Marvel film. = 她最喜欢的电影可能是新的漫威电影。

虚拟语气:
If I had to choose, my favorite dessert would be chocolate cake. = 如果我必须选择，我最喜欢的甜点将是巧克力蛋糕。
I wish my favorite restaurant was closer to my home. = 我希望我最喜欢的餐馆离我家更近。

被动语态:
His painting was chosen as the favorite by the judges. = 他的画作被评委选为最喜欢的。
This product has been consistently ranked as a customer favorite. = 此产品一直被评为顾客最喜欢的产品。

疑问句:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What's your favorite type of music? = 你最喜欢的音乐类型是什么？

否定句:
Broccoli is not my favorite vegetable. = 花椰菜不是我最喜欢的蔬菜。
That song isn't her favorite anymore. = 那首歌不再是她最喜欢的了。
