preidentifying: /ˌpriːaɪˈdɛntɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 预先识别；预先确定 | Identifying something before it is fully known or evident.  The act of pre-identifying involves making a preliminary assessment or judgment about something based on limited information.  | 例句：The system is designed for preidentifying potential risks before they escalate.  系统旨在预先识别潜在风险，防止其升级。


The police conducted preidentifying work on the suspect before the arrest.  警方在逮捕之前对嫌疑人进行了预先识别工作。| 近义词：predetermining, preselecting, anticipating | 反义词：post-identifying, identifying after the fact | 动词
