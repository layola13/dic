overwrites:/ˈoʊvərˌraɪts/ | vt. |覆盖，改写，改写（数据）|To write over or replace (something previously written or recorded).  It implies the previous information is lost or replaced entirely.|The new software overwrites the old version. 新软件覆盖了旧版本。
The program overwrites the existing file. 程序改写了现有的文件。|rewrite, replace|preserve, keep|动词
