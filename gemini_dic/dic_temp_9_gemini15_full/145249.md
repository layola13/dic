overbleaches: /ˌoʊvərˈbliːtʃɪz/ | v. |漂白过度；过度使用漂白剂| To bleach something excessively, resulting in damage or undesirable discoloration.  This word is formed by adding the prefix "over-" to the verb "bleaches," indicating an excess or going beyond the appropriate level. |例句：The sun overbleached her hair, making it straw-like and brittle.  太阳过度漂白了她的头发，使其变得像稻草一样干枯易碎。

The constant use of harsh chemicals overbleached the fabric, weakening its fibers.  持续使用强效化学品过度漂白了织物，削弱了其纤维。|近义词：bleach excessively, overwhiten, discolor excessively|反义词：underbleach (not a common word), restore color
