retrogradation:/ˌrɛtrəʊɡreɪˈdeɪʃən/| n. |逆行；退化；(淀粉)老化|The process of reverting to a previous or less developed state.  This can refer to a physical or metaphorical backward movement or decline. In the context of starch, it refers to the recrystallization of amylose and amylopectin molecules, leading to a firmer texture. |例句：The retrogradation of starch causes bread to become stale.  淀粉的逆行导致面包变硬。

例句：The economic retrogradation of the country was a serious concern.  该国的经济倒退令人担忧。


近义词：reversion, degeneration, deterioration,  (for starch) staling


反义词：progression, advancement, improvement


词性：名词
