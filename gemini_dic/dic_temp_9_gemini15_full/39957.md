constructor's: /kənˈstrʌktərz/ | adj. | 建筑商的；构造者的；建造者的 | Relating to or characteristic of a constructor.  It's a possessive form of the noun "constructor". |例句：The constructor's blueprints were detailed and precise. 建筑商的蓝图详细而精确。 The constructor's fees were surprisingly high. 建筑商的费用出奇的高。|近义词：builder's, developer's, architect's |反义词：N/A


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, not a verb or noun that conjugates.  Therefore, it doesn't have tenses or voices like the example word "what".  Its usage is solely to describe something as belonging to or being related to a constructor.
