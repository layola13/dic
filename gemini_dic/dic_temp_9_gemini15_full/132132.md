nonculpable:/ˈnɒnkʌlpəbl/ | adj. |无过错的，无罪的 | Not deserving blame or punishment.  |例句：The court found the defendant nonculpable in the accident. 法院认定被告在事故中无过错。

例句：The investigation concluded that the employee was nonculpable in the data breach.  调查结论是员工在数据泄露事件中没有过错。


近义词：blameless, guiltless, innocent, faultless


反义词：culpable, guilty, blameworthy, at fault
