griever:/ˈɡriːvər/| n.|悲伤者，哀悼者|A person who grieves, especially one who is mourning the death of a loved one.  Grievers often experience a wide range of emotions, including sadness, anger, guilt, and acceptance, and the grieving process varies greatly from person to person.|例句：The griever lit a candle in memory of her deceased husband.  悲伤者点燃蜡烛以纪念她已故的丈夫。|例句：Support groups for grievers provide a safe space to share their experiences and emotions.  为悲伤者提供的支持小组提供了一个安全的空间来分享他们的经历和感受。|近义词：mourner, bereaved person|反义词：celebrant
