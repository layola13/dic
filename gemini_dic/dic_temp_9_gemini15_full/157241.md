pinnated:/ˈpɪneɪtɪd/| adj.|羽状的；羽裂的|Having parts arranged like a feather;  having leaflets or barbs arranged on either side of a common axis, resembling a feather.  This term is commonly used in botany and zoology to describe the arrangement of leaves, feathers, or other appendages.|例句：The pinnated leaves of the fern unfurled in the spring. 春天，蕨类的羽状叶舒展开来。

The bird's pinnated tail feathers were strikingly beautiful.  这只鸟的羽状尾羽非常漂亮。|近义词：feathery, plume-like|反义词：unbranched, simple
