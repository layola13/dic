defrayal:/dɪˈfreɪəl/| n. |支付，赔偿|The act of defraying or paying; payment or reimbursement of expenses.|The company offered to defray the costs of the trip.|近义词：payment, reimbursement, settlement|反义词：debt, owing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company defrays all travel expenses. = 公司支付所有差旅费用。
He defrays his own living expenses. = 他自己支付生活费用。


一般过去时:
They defrayed the cost of the conference. = 他们支付了会议的费用。
She defrayed her travel expenses with a grant. = 她用拨款支付了她的差旅费用。


现在完成时:
We have defrayed all the necessary costs. = 我们已经支付了所有必要的费用。
The government has defrayed the expenses of the relief effort. = 政府已经支付了救济工作的费用。


过去完成时:
They had defrayed the expenses before the project was completed. = 项目完成之前，他们已经支付了费用。
She had defrayed her college tuition through part-time jobs. = 她通过兼职工作支付了大学学费。


将来时:
The organization will defray the costs of the research. = 该组织将支付研究的费用。
They will defray the expenses of the delegates. = 他们将支付代表的费用。


虚拟语气:
If the company defrayed all the costs, more people would participate. = 如果公司支付所有费用，更多人会参与。
I wish the insurance company would defray the medical bills. = 我希望保险公司能支付医疗费用。


被动语态:
The expenses were defrayed by the organization. = 费用由该组织支付。
The costs will be defrayed by the government. = 费用将由政府支付。


疑问句:
Will the company defray the travel expenses? = 公司会支付差旅费用吗？
How will the costs be defrayed? = 费用将如何支付？


否定句:
The company did not defray the additional costs. = 公司没有支付额外的费用。
They will not defray the expenses without proper documentation. = 没有适当的文件，他们不会支付费用。


PHRASE->
defrayal of expenses = 费用的支付
defrayal of costs = 成本的支付
defrayal of debt = 债务的偿还
partial defrayal = 部分支付
full defrayal = 全额支付
