rechange:/ˌriːˈtʃɑːrdʒ/| vt.| 重新充电；再充电；补充能量 |To charge (a battery, etc.) again after it has been used.|例句：I need to rechange my phone battery. 我需要给我的手机电池重新充电。  I need to rechange my electric car. 我需要给我的电动汽车重新充电。|近义词：recharge, refill, replenish|反义词：discharge, deplete
