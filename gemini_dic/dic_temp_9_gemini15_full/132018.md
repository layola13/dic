noncorrelatively:/ˌnɒnkɒrɛlətɪvli/| adv. |非相关地，不相关地 |Not correlatively; without correlation or interdependence.  This adverb describes a relationship or action that lacks connection or mutual influence.|The two events occurred noncorrelatively; there was no causal link between them.  两个事件发生时毫无关联；两者之间没有因果关系。|近义词：unrelatedly, independently, separately |反义词：correlatively, dependently, interdependently
