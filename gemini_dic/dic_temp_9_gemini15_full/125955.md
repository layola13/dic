musicianer:/ˌmjuːˈzɪʃənər/| n.|音乐家（非正式）|An informal term for a musician, often implying someone who plays music for a living or professionally, but potentially with a slightly less skilled or prestigious connotation than "musician."  It suggests a more active, perhaps itinerant, performer.|例句：He's a musicianer, playing gigs in pubs all over the country. 他是个音乐家，在全国各地的酒吧演出。He’s a talented musicianer, though he’s not yet famous. 他是个有天赋的音乐家，尽管他还没出名。|近义词：musician, player, performer, troubadour|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with "amateur" or "non-musician".|名词
