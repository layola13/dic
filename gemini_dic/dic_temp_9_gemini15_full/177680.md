recall:/rɪˈkɔːl/|/rɪˈkɔːl/| vt. n.|回忆，回想；召回；收回|To bring (something) back to mind; to remember. To order the return of (something).|例句：I recall meeting him years ago. 我记得几年前见过他。|例句：The company recalled all the defective products. 公司召回了所有有缺陷的产品。|近义词：remember, recollect, retrieve, summon|反义词：forget, overlook|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I recall the details of the accident. = 我回忆起事故的细节。
We recall the good old days. = 我们回忆过去的美好时光。


一般过去时:
She recalled her childhood memories. = 她回忆起童年记忆。
He recalled the information he had learned. = 他回忆起他学到的信息。


现在完成时:
I have recalled the events leading up to the incident. = 我已经回忆起导致该事件发生的事件。
Have you recalled everything you need for the trip? = 你是否已经回忆起旅行所需的一切？


过去完成时:
By then, I had recalled everything I knew. = 到那时，我已经回忆起我所知道的一切。
She had recalled the meeting before it started. = 会议开始之前，她已经回忆起会议了。


将来时:
I will recall this day for many years to come. = 未来许多年，我都会记得这一天。
They will recall the details of the contract later. = 他们稍后会回忆起合同的细节。


虚拟语气:
If I could recall his name, I would tell you. = 如果我能想起他的名字，我会告诉你的。
I wish I could recall what he said. = 我真希望我能想起他说的话。


被动语态:
The faulty toys were recalled by the manufacturer. = 有缺陷的玩具被制造商召回了。
The meeting was recalled due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，会议被取消了。


疑问句:
Do you recall the address? = 你记得地址吗？
Can you recall what happened next? = 你能回忆起接下来发生的事情吗？


否定句:
I cannot recall his name. = 我记不起他的名字。
She did not recall seeing him there. = 她不记得在那里见过他。



PHRASE->
recall to mind = 回忆起
recall something vividly = 清晰地回忆起某事
a faulty recall = 有缺陷的召回
a product recall = 产品召回
to be recalled = 被召回
