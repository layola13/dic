milleporite:/ˈmɪləpɔːraɪt/| n. |千孔珊瑚；枝状珊瑚|A milleporite is a type of hydrocoral, characterized by its branching or encrusting growth form and its tiny pores.  These pores house the polyps, the individual animals that make up the colony. Milleporites are often found in shallow, tropical waters and can form large colonies. |Example: The diver carefully examined the vibrant milleporite colony clinging to the reef.  潜水员仔细检查了依附在珊瑚礁上的鲜艳的千孔珊瑚群落。  The researcher collected samples of the milleporite for further study. 研究人员收集了千孔珊瑚样本以进行进一步研究。|近义词：hydrocoral, coral|反义词：无
