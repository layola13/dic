dichroism:/ˈdaɪkroʊɪzəm/| n.|二色性，双色性|The property of a substance to exhibit different colors when viewed in polarized light from different directions.  This is due to the absorption of light at different wavelengths depending on the polarization and direction of the light. |Example: Tourmaline exhibits strong dichroism, appearing green in one direction and pink in another.  绿宝石表现出很强的二色性，在一个方向上看起来是绿色的，而在另一个方向上看起来是粉红色的。|近义词：pleochroism|反义词：isochroism|名词
