ginkgo:/ˈɡɪŋkoʊ/| n. |银杏树，白果树 |Ginkgo is a genus of deciduous trees, commonly known as ginkgo or ginkgo biloba.  It's the only living species of its order, Ginkgoales.  The tree is known for its fan-shaped leaves and unique medicinal properties. |The ginkgo tree in my garden is turning yellow. 我花园里的银杏树正在变黄。

The ancient ginkgo tree is a symbol of resilience.  古老的银杏树是韧性的象征。|maidenhair tree |—| n.
