post-fault:/ˌpoʊstˈfɔːlt/| adj.|故障后，事故后|relating to the period or events after a fault or failure has occurred.  It often describes the analysis, recovery, or response to a fault.|例句：Post-fault analysis revealed a critical software bug. 故障后分析揭示了一个严重的软件错误。|例句：The post-fault procedures were followed meticulously. 故障后程序被一丝不苟地执行。|近义词：post-failure, after-fault |反义词：pre-fault, before-fault
