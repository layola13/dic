handler:/ˈhændlər/| n. v.|处理程序；操控者；管理员|A person or thing that handles something.  A handler can refer to a person who manages or controls something or someone, often in a specific context. It can also refer to a piece of software that manages events or requests. |例句：The event handler processed the click event efficiently.  事件处理程序高效地处理了点击事件。
The dog handler expertly guided the canine through the obstacle course.  训犬师熟练地引导犬只通过障碍物赛道。|近义词：manager, operator, controller, processor, subroutine|反义词：inert, inactive, passive
