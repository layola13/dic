keystroked:/ˈkiːstroʊkt/ | adj. |按键输入的；通过按键产生的|  Describes data or input generated by pressing keys on a keyboard.  It often implies a rapid or repetitive action.| 例句：The keystroked password was incorrect. 按键输入的密码不正确。 The system logged all keystroked commands. 系统记录了所有按键输入的命令。|近义词：typed, entered|反义词：handwritten, spoken
