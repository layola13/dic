garlics:/ˈɡɑːrlɪks/ | n. | 大蒜 (复数) | Garlic is a pungent-smelling vegetable that is used as a flavoring in cooking.  Garlics refers to multiple bulbs of garlic. | Example:  I bought three heads of garlics at the farmer's market. 我在农贸市场买了三头大蒜。  The recipe calls for two cloves of garlics. 这个食谱需要两瓣大蒜。 |近义词：garlic bulbs, garlic cloves (when referring to individual cloves)| 反义词：无 |  名词
