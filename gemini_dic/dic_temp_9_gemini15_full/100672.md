intoxicate:/ɪnˈtɒksɪkeɪt/ /ɪnˈtɑːksɪkeɪt/ | vt. |使陶醉；使喝醉；使兴奋；毒害 |To make someone drunk by giving them alcohol.  To affect someone in such a way that they lose control of their behaviour and emotions.  To excite or thrill someone. |例句：The music intoxicated the crowd. 音乐使人群陶醉。

The wine intoxicated him. 酒把他灌醉了。


近义词：inebriate, inebriate, exhilarate, stimulate, excite |反义词：sober, calm, depress |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wine intoxicates him. = 酒使他陶醉。
The music intoxicates the audience. = 音乐使听众陶醉。

一般过去时:
The strong drink intoxicated him. =烈酒使他醉了。
The party intoxicated her with excitement. =派对让她兴奋不已。


现在完成时:

The success of the project has intoxicated the team. = 项目的成功使团队兴奋不已。


过去完成时:
