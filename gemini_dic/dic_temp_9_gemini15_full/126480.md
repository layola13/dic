mythologue: /mɪˈθɒlədʒ/ | n. |神话学家；神话研究者 | A mythologue is a scholar who studies mythology.  They are experts in the creation, development, and interpretation of myths and legends from various cultures.  | Example:  The renowned mythologue, Dr. Jones, presented a compelling lecture on the symbolism in Greek myths.  著名的神话学家琼斯博士就希腊神话中的象征意义作了一场引人入胜的演讲。|近义词:mythologist, folklorist |反义词: None (no direct antonym)| 名词
