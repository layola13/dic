polemic:/ˈpɒləmɪk/| /pəˈlɛmɪk/ | n. |论战；争论；辩论 |A strong verbal or written attack on someone or something.  A controversial argument. |例句：The article sparked a fierce polemic about the ethics of genetic engineering. 这篇文章引发了一场关于基因工程伦理的激烈论战。


例句：His polemic against the government's policies was widely circulated. 他反对政府政策的论战被广泛传播。


近义词：controversy, debate, dispute, argument, contention


反义词：agreement, harmony, accord, consensus


词性：名词
