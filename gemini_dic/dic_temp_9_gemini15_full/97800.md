injuries:/ˈɪndʒəriːz/| n.|伤害；损伤|Injuries are harm done to a person's body.  They can range from minor cuts and bruises to severe fractures and internal bleeding. The severity of an injury depends on various factors, including the force of the impact, the type of injury, and the individual's overall health.|例句：The accident resulted in several serious injuries. 这起事故导致了几处严重的伤亡。|例句：He sustained minor injuries in the fall. 他在跌倒中受了轻伤。|近义词：wounds, harms, damages|反义词：healing, recovery
