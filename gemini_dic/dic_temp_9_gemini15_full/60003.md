editorializers:/ˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəlaɪzərz/| n. |喜欢发表社论的人；爱发表意见的人 |Those who frequently express their opinions, often in a forceful or opinionated way, especially in written or spoken media.  They often present their views as authoritative pronouncements. |The editorializers on the cable news channels rarely offered balanced perspectives.  电视频道上的评论员们很少提供均衡的观点。  The constant editorializing in the blog post made it difficult to follow the facts. 博客文章中不断的评论使事实难以理解。|commentators, pundits, opinion-makers |(无明确反义词，但可以考虑)listeners, observers|名词
