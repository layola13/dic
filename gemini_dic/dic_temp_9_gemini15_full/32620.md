chirrup:/ˈtʃɪrəp/| v. n.|吱吱叫，啁啾；愉快地说话|To make a short, high-pitched sound, like a bird.|The birds chirruped merrily in the morning sun.  鸟儿在清晨的阳光下快乐地啁啾着。|The children chirruped excitedly about their trip. 孩子们兴奋地叽叽喳喳地谈论着他们的旅行。|twitter, peep, cheep|shout, roar|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The crickets chirp all night long. = 蟋蟀整夜都在鸣叫。
The birds chirp merrily in the trees. = 鸟儿在树上快乐地鸣叫。

一般过去时:
The sparrow chirped a cheerful song. = 麻雀唱了一首快乐的歌。
The baby chirped happily in her sleep. = 婴儿睡梦中快乐地发出啁啾声。

现在完成时:
The birds have chirped since dawn. = 鸟儿从黎明开始就一直在鸣叫。
The crickets have chirped all night. = 蟋蟀整夜都在鸣叫。


过去完成时:
The birds had chirped before the sun rose. = 太阳升起前鸟儿就已经鸣叫了。
The crickets had chirped for hours before I fell asleep. = 我睡着之前蟋蟀已经鸣叫了好几个小时。

将来时:
The birds will chirp at dawn tomorrow. = 明天黎明鸟儿会鸣叫。
I think the birds will chirp loudly today. = 我想今天鸟儿会大声鸣叫。

虚拟语气:
If the birds chirped louder, we would hear them. = 如果鸟儿叫得更响亮，我们就能听到它们。
I wish the birds chirped all day long. = 我希望鸟儿整天都在鸣叫。

被动语态:
A cheerful sound was chirped by the birds. = 一种快乐的声音是由鸟儿发出的。(很少用被动语态)
(This is grammatically correct but rarely used in practice.)

疑问句:
Do the birds chirp in the morning? = 鸟儿早上鸣叫吗？
Did the birds chirp yesterday? = 鸟儿昨天鸣叫了吗？

否定句:
The birds didn't chirp today. = 鸟儿今天没有鸣叫。
The crickets weren't chirping last night. = 蟋蟀昨晚没有鸣叫。

chirrup away =  持续地啁啾
chirrup with delight =  高兴地啁啾
