counterpart:/ˈkaʊntərpɑːrt/| n. |对应的人或物；副本；配对物 | A person or thing holding a similar position in a different place or organization.  |例句：The American ambassador met with his Chinese counterpart. 美国大使会见了中国大使。|例句：This document is a counterpart to the original. 这份文件是原件的副本。|近义词：equivalent, parallel, match |反义词：opposite, dissimilar


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is the counterpart of his brother. = 他是他哥哥的对应人。
The two paintings are counterparts. = 这两幅画是对应的。


一般过去时:
Her counterpart in the other company was very helpful. = 她在那家公司的对应人非常乐于助人。
The document was a perfect counterpart of the original. = 这份文件是原件的完美副本。


现在完成时:
We have found a counterpart for this product. = 我们已经找到了这款产品的对应产品。
They have been counterparts for many years. = 他们多年来一直是对应的人。


过去完成时:
By then, the counterpart had already finished his work. = 到那时，对应人已经完成了他的工作。
The committee had already reviewed the counterpart document. = 委员会已经审查了对应的文件。


将来时:
Your counterpart will contact you soon. = 你的对应人很快就会联系你。
The new model will be the exact counterpart of the previous one. = 新型号将与旧型号完全一致。


虚拟语气:
If I had a counterpart, I could share the workload. = 如果我有对应的人，我可以分担工作量。
I wish I had a counterpart to assist me. = 我希望有对应的人来帮助我。


被动语态:
His role was mirrored by his counterpart in the rival firm. = 他的角色与竞争对手公司中的对应角色相呼应。
The document is being checked by its counterpart. = 文件正在由其对应文件进行核对。


疑问句:
Do you have a counterpart in this project? = 你在这个项目中有对应的人吗？
What is the counterpart of this word in another language? = 这个词在另一种语言中的对应词是什么？


否定句:
He didn't have a counterpart in the other department. = 他在其他部门没有对应的人。
This product doesn't have a direct counterpart in the market. = 此产品在市场上没有直接对应的产品。
