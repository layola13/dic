/hwɑːt/|pron.|什么；多么；什么样；多少|  What is a pronoun that can function as an interrogative, exclamatory, or relative pronoun.  It is used to ask for information, express strong emotion, or introduce a subordinate clause.  |例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！


近义词：which, how much, how many|反义词：N/A|词性：pronoun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？


一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What time did the movie start? = 电影几点开始的？


现在完成时:
What have you been doing lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she accomplished? = 她取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you eaten before you came here? = 你来这里之前吃过什么？
What had they decided before the meeting? = 会议前他们决定了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like? = 天气将会怎么样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built there? = 那里正在建造什么？


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点？
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
He didn't say what he meant. = 他没有说出他的意思。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What's going on? = 发生什么事了？
What if...? = 如果...怎么样？
What for? = 为什么？
What about...? = ...怎么样？
