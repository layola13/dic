princehood:/ˈprɪnshʊd/| n. |王子身份；王位继承权 |The state or condition of being a prince; the rank or dignity of a prince.  It often implies the rights and privileges associated with princely status, and sometimes the expectation of inheriting a throne.  |Example:  His princehood was challenged by rival claimants to the throne.  他的王子身份受到了觊觎王位的竞争者的挑战。|近义词：princedom, royalty |反义词：commonalty, peasantry
