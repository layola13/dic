flamencos: /fləˈmɛŋkoʊz/ | n. |弗拉明戈舞；弗拉明戈舞者（复数） | Flamencos are a passionate and expressive dance form originating from Andalusia, Spain.  It is characterized by its rhythmic footwork, hand claps, and emotional intensity.  The music is typically accompanied by guitars and singing. | 例句：The flamenco dancers captivated the audience with their fiery performance. 弗拉明戈舞者们以他们热情的表演迷住了观众。

The flamenco dancers wore vibrant costumes. 弗拉明戈舞者们穿着鲜艳的服装。| 近义词：flamenco (sing.) | 反义词：无
