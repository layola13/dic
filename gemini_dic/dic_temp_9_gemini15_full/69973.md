festooned:/fɛˈstuːnd/| adj.|装饰华丽的，饰以花彩的|Adorned with ribbons, garlands, or other decorations.  The word implies a festive or celebratory atmosphere.|例句：The streets were festooned with flags for the parade.  街道上装饰着旗帜，为游行做准备。

The house was festooned with Christmas lights. 房子装饰着圣诞灯。


近义词：decorated, adorned, embellished, ornamented|反义词：bare, undecorated, plain|词性：形容词
