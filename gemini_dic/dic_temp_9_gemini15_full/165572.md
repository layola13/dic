premedicate:/ˌpriːˈmɛdɪkeɪt/ | vt. | 预先用药；预防性用药 | To administer medication to someone before a procedure or event to prevent or reduce the likelihood of a negative reaction or complication.  | 例句：The surgeon decided to premedicate the patient to reduce anxiety before the operation.  外科医生决定在手术前给病人预先用药以减轻焦虑。 | 例句：The dentist premedicated the child to minimize discomfort during the procedure. 牙医给孩子预先用药以最大限度地减少治疗过程中的不适。 | 近义词：prophylactically medicate, pre-treat | 反义词：N/A
