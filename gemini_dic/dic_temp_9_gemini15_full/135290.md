nonpartners:/ˈnɑːnˌpɑːrtnərz/ | n. |非合伙人，非伙伴 |Those who are not partners in a business or venture.  People or entities not involved in a partnership. |例句：The nonpartners were informed of the decision after it was made. = 决定做出后，非合伙人被告知了。

The board of directors includes both partners and nonpartners. = 董事会包括合伙人和非合伙人。


近义词：outsiders, non-members, associates (in a negative context) | 反义词：partners, associates (in a positive context), members | 词性: 名词
