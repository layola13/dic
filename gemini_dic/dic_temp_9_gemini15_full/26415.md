byes:/baɪz/| n.|再见，再会|A shortened form of "goodbyes," used as a farewell.|例句：We said our byes and went our separate ways. 我们道别后各奔东西了。|近义词：goodbyes, farewells, adieu|反义词：greetings, hellos|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We say our byes before leaving. = 我们离开前道别。
They always say their byes politely. = 他们总是礼貌地道别。


一般过去时:
We said our byes at the airport. = 我们在机场道别了。
They said their byes and left. = 他们道别后离开了。


现在完成时:
I have said my byes to everyone. = 我已经向每个人道别了。
She has said her byes and gone home. = 她已经道别回家了。


过去完成时:
We had said our byes before the plane took off. = 在飞机起飞前我们已经道别了。
They had said their byes and were already on the train. = 他们已经道别并且已经在火车上了。


将来时:
We will say our byes tomorrow. = 我们明天将道别。
They will say their byes before the ceremony begins. = 典礼开始前他们将道别。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would say our proper byes. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会好好道别。
I wish we had said our byes properly. = 我希望我们当时好好道别了。


被动语态:
Byes were said with tears in their eyes. = 道别时他们眼中含泪。
The byes were quickly said because of the time constraint. = 因为时间紧迫，道别很匆忙。


疑问句:
Have you said your byes? = 你道别了吗？
Did they say their byes before leaving? = 他们离开前道别了吗？


否定句:
We didn't say our byes properly. = 我们没有好好道别。
They didn't say their byes before rushing off. = 他们匆匆忙忙地离开了，没有道别。
