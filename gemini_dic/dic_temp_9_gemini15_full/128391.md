new lease on life:/nuː ˈliːs ɒn laɪf/ | n. |新生，新的开始，重获新生 |A new lease on life refers to a renewed sense of purpose, energy, and optimism, often after a difficult period or a significant change. It implies a feeling of rejuvenation and a chance to start over.  | Example: After retiring, he found a new lease on life by volunteering at the local hospital.  退休后，他在当地医院做志愿者，找到了新的生活目标。 |近义词：second chance, fresh start, rebirth |反义词：despair, hopelessness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has a new lease on life. = 他重获新生。
She enjoys a new lease on life. = 她享受着新的开始。


一般过去时:
He found a new lease on life after his recovery. = 他康复后重获新生。
She received a new lease on life thanks to the surgery. = 她由于手术而重获新生。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
