confuting:/kənˈfjuːtɪŋ/| adj.|驳斥的；反驳的；令人信服地证明错误的|Refuting or proving to be false.  The confuting evidence finally led to the suspect's confession.|近义词：refuting, disproving, contradicting|反义词：confirming, supporting, corroborating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His arguments are confuting the opponent's claims. = 他的论点驳斥了对手的主张。
The evidence confutes the defendant's alibi. = 证据驳斥了被告的不在场证明。


一般过去时:
The scientist confuted the prevailing theory with new data. = 科学家用新的数据驳斥了流行的理论。
Her testimony confuted the witness's account. = 她的证词驳斥了证人的说法。


现在完成时:
The new research has confuted many previous assumptions. = 新的研究已经驳斥了许多以前的假设。
Recent discoveries have confuted the old theory. = 最近的发现已经驳斥了旧理论。


过去完成时:
They had confuted the opposition's arguments before the debate. = 他们在辩论之前就已经驳斥了反对派的论点。
The lawyer had confuted the prosecution's case by the time the trial ended. = 到审判结束时，律师已经驳斥了检方的指控。


将来时:
The upcoming report will confute the current predictions. = 即将发布的报告将驳斥目前的预测。
New evidence will soon confute the widely held belief. = 新的证据很快就会驳斥这种普遍的看法。


虚拟语气:
If I had more evidence, I could confute his claims. = 如果我有更多证据，我就能驳斥他的说法。
If the data were different, it would confute the hypothesis. = 如果数据不同，它就会驳斥这个假设。


被动语态:
His argument was confuted by the opposing team. = 他的论点被对方团队驳斥了。
The theory was confuted by later experiments. = 该理论被后来的实验所驳斥。


疑问句:
Can this evidence confute the accused's statement? = 这证据能驳斥被告的陈述吗？
Does this data confute the accepted theory? = 这个数据能驳斥公认的理论吗？


否定句:
The study did not confute the existing theory. = 这项研究并没有驳斥现有的理论。
His arguments failed to confute the opposing side. = 他的论点未能驳斥对方。


PHRASE->
confuting evidence: 驳斥性证据
confuting argument: 驳斥性论点
confuting the opposition: 驳斥反对派
confuting a theory: 驳斥一种理论
confuting a claim: 驳斥一项说法


词源:
The word "confuting" comes from the Latin word "confutare," meaning "to disprove," "to refute," or "to convict."  It entered English through Old French.
