causalities:/ˌkɔːzˈælətiz/| n. |伤亡人数；因果关系|The number of deaths and injuries resulting from an accident, war, or other event.  It can also refer to the relationship between cause and effect.|例句：The causalities from the earthquake were tragically high. 地震造成的伤亡人数非常惨重。|例句：Understanding the causalities between pollution and climate change is crucial. 理解污染和气候变化之间的因果关系至关重要。|近义词：casualties, fatalities, losses, consequences|反义词：survival, safety
