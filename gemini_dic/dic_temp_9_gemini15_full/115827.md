marketably:/ˈmɑːkɪtəbli/ | adj. | 可销售的；适销的 | Capable of being sold or marketed successfully; commercially viable.  | 例句：The product needs to be more marketably priced to compete. = 这个产品需要更具有市场竞争力的价格才能竞争。

The new features make the software more marketable. = 新功能使该软件更具市场竞争力。


近义词：salable, sellable, commercially viable,  competitive


反义词：unsalable, unmarketable, inviable


词性:形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The product is marketably priced. = 产品价格适销对路。
This design is marketably appealing. = 这个设计很有市场吸引力。

一般过去时:

The company has made its products more marketable. = 公司已使其产品更具市场竞争力。
The brand has become more marketable through effective marketing. = 通过有效的营销，该品牌已变得更具市场竞争力。

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
