jammed:/dʒæmd/| adj. v.|堵塞的；卡住的；塞满的；充满的|  blocked, stuck, crowded, filled; to force something into a small space|例句：The traffic was jammed. 交通堵塞了。

The door was jammed shut. 门被卡住了。|近义词：blocked, stuck, congested, crowded |反义词：unblocked, open, clear


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine jams frequently. = 这台机器经常卡住。
The road is jammed with cars. = 道路被汽车堵塞了。

一般过去时:
The printer jammed yesterday. = 打印机昨天卡住了。
The door jammed, and we couldn't open it. = 门卡住了，我们打不开。

现在完成时:
The road has been jammed all morning. = 马路堵塞了一上午了。
The engine has jammed several times this week. = 这周发动机已经卡了好几次了。


过去完成时:
The car had jammed before we reached the summit. = 我们到达山顶之前汽车就已经卡住了。
The pipeline had jammed, causing a major oil spill. = 管道堵塞了，导致了严重的漏油事故。

将来时:
The traffic will be jammed during rush hour. = 高峰期交通将会堵塞。
I think the lift will jam again.  = 我想电梯还会再卡住。

虚拟语气:
If the printer jammed again, I'd be furious. = 如果打印机再次卡住，我会很生气。
I wish the traffic hadn't jammed. = 我真希望交通没堵塞。

被动语态:
The road was jammed by an accident. = 道路因事故而堵塞。
The pipe was jammed with debris. = 管道被碎屑堵塞了。

疑问句:
Is the toilet jammed? = 马桶堵了吗？
Was the road jammed? = 道路堵塞了吗？

否定句:
The door wasn't jammed. = 门没有卡住。
The printer didn't jam. = 打印机没有卡住。
