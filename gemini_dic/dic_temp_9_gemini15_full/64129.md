epure:/eˈpjʊər/| n.|净图，精图；纯化物|A drawing showing only essential lines and features, often used in engineering or architecture to illustrate a design or concept.  It's a purified or refined version of something, often a drawing or a substance.|例句：The architect presented an epure of the proposed building design to the client. 建筑师向客户展示了拟建建筑设计的净图。|近义词：diagram, schematic, plan, drawing|反义词：complex drawing, detailed drawing
