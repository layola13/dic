It suggests a degree of rudeness or disrespect that goes beyond mere boldness. | 例句：His oversaucy remarks offended many guests.  他的言辞过于放肆，冒犯了许多客人。  The child's oversaucy behavior was unacceptable.  孩子的行为过于放肆，令人无法接受。| 近义词：impertinent, cheeky, presumptuous, audacious, brash | 反义词：respectful, polite, humble, modest, deferential
