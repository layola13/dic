e-books:/ˈiːbʊks/| n. |电子书|Electronic books; books in digital form that can be read on computers, e-readers, or other electronic devices.  They often offer features like adjustable font sizes, highlighting, and note-taking.|例句：I prefer reading e-books because they are convenient and save space. 我更喜欢阅读电子书，因为它们方便而且节省空间。|例句：Many libraries now offer a large selection of e-books for free. 许多图书馆现在都免费提供大量的电子书。|近义词：digital books, online books|反义词：paperbacks, hardcovers
