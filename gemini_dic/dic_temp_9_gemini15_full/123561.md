monsoonal:/mɒnˈsuːnəl/| adj.|季风的；受季风影响的| Relating to or characteristic of monsoons.  The word describes weather patterns, climates, or events influenced by monsoons. |例句：The region experiences monsoonal rains during the summer months.  这个地区在夏季经历季风降雨。

例句：The monsoonal climate of India supports diverse agriculture. 印度的季风气候支持多样化的农业。


近义词：monsoonic, rainy (in the context of monsoon season)
反义词：dry (in the context of monsoon season)
词性：形容词
