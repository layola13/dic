singularist: /ˈsɪŋɡjələrɪst/ | adj. | 独一无二的；强调个体差异的；认为个体差异高于群体相似性的 |  Focusing on or emphasizing the unique qualities of individuals, often to the detriment of group identity or commonality.  It suggests a belief that individual differences are more significant than shared characteristics. |  例句：His singularist approach to teaching allowed each student to flourish at their own pace.  他的教学方法独树一帜，让每个学生都能以自己的节奏茁壮成长。


例句：The artist's singularist style set him apart from his contemporaries.  这位艺术家的独特风格使他与同时代人区别开来。


近义词：individualistic, unique, idiosyncratic | 反义词：collectivist, conformist, uniform


词性: adjective (形容词)
