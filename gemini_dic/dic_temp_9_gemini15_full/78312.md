gerontes:/dʒɛˈrɒntiːz/| n.|老年人，老人；长者|The word "gerontes" refers to a group of elderly men, often holding positions of authority or wisdom within a community, particularly in ancient Greece.  The term carries connotations of experience, respect, and sometimes even conservatism.|例句：The gerontes of the village made the final decisions regarding land allocation.  村里的长老们对土地分配做出了最终决定。|近义词：elders, seniors, patriarchs|反义词：youths, children
