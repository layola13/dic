gate-way's:/ˈɡeɪtˌweɪz/ | n. | 网关；门户；通道 |  A gateway is a point of access or entry, often a system or device that acts as an interface between two different networks or systems.  It can also refer to a physical entrance or a metaphorical point of access to something.  | Example: The company's new website serves as a gateway to its vast database of information.  这家公司的新网站是其庞大数据库信息的门户。 |近义词：entrance, access point, portal | 反义词：exit, blockage
