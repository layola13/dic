hatchets:/ˈhætʃɪts/| n.|小斧头，短柄斧|A hatchet is a small axe with a short handle, typically used for chopping wood or other similar tasks.  It's smaller and lighter than a full-size axe, making it more manageable for smaller jobs or for use in confined spaces.|例句：He used a hatchet to chop the firewood. 他用小斧头砍柴火。

例句：The carpenter used a hatchet to shape the wooden handle. 木匠用小斧头来雕琢木制手柄。


近义词：small axe, hand axe|反义词：None (antonyms are not readily applicable)|词性:名词
