hinglish:/ˈhɪŋɡlɪʃ/| n. |印式英语，混合英语|A blend of Hindi and English, commonly used in India. It often involves using Hindi words or phrases within an English sentence structure, or using English words with Hindi pronunciation or grammar.  This language reflects the linguistic realities of India, where English and Hindi coexist and influence each other. | Example:  "I'm going to the *dukaan* to buy some *namkeen*." (I'm going to the shop to buy some snacks.) |近义词:IndEnglish,  Hindi-English code-switching|反义词:Standard English
