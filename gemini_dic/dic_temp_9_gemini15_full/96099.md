indefinablenesses: /ˌɪnˌdɪˈfaɪnəbəlˌnɛsɪz/ | n. |不可定义性（复数）|The state or quality of being indefinable; multiple instances of something that cannot be precisely defined or explained.  The plural form emphasizes the existence of several such instances or aspects. |例句：The indefinablenesses of human emotion often lead to misunderstandings. 人类情感的不可定义性常常导致误解。


The indefinablenesses of artistic expression make it so captivating. 艺术表达的不可定义性使其如此引人入胜。|近义词：ineffabilities, inexpressibilities, mysteries |反义词：clarity, definability, explicability |名词
