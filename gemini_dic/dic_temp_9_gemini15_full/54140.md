disengage:/ˌdɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/| vt. |脱离，解脱，分开|To release from engagement or connection; to disconnect or separate.  |例句：The soldiers disengaged from the enemy. 士兵们与敌人脱离了接触。

例句：He disengaged the gears before stopping the car. 他在停车前挂空挡。


近义词：disconnect, detach, separate, release, uncouple|反义词：engage, connect, attach, couple|动词
