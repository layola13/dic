piteously:/ˈpɪtiəsli/ | adj. | 可怜地；悲惨地；令人同情地 |  expressing or evoking pity; pathetically.  It describes something done or said in a way that evokes pity or compassion. | 例句：The lost puppy cried piteously.  = 迷路的小狗可怜地哭着。

The beggar looked piteously at the passersby. = 乞丐可怜巴巴地看着过路人。


近义词：pathetically, sadly, sorrowfully, miserably, wretchedly

反义词：joyfully, happily, cheerfully, triumphantly

词性：形容词
