quasi-rejected:/ˈkwɑːziːrɪˈdʒɛktɪd/| adj.| 准被拒绝的，几乎被拒绝的 |  Almost rejected;  partially or seemingly rejected. It implies a rejection that wasn't complete or official, perhaps due to further review or reconsideration. | Example: His grant application was quasi-rejected; they requested further information before making a final decision.  他的拨款申请几乎被拒绝了；他们要求提供更多信息后再做最终决定。| 近义词：nearly rejected, almost rejected, provisionally rejected | 反义词：accepted, approved
