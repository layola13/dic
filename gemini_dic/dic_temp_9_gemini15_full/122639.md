modified:/ˈmɒdɪfaɪd/ /ˈmɑːdəfaɪd/| adj.|修改过的；已变更的；改良的|altered, changed, or adjusted in some way.  It implies a change made to an existing thing, often to improve it or make it more suitable.|例句：The modified car is faster than the original.  修改后的汽车比原车更快。

例句：The modified plan addresses the concerns raised by the community.  修改后的计划解决了社区提出的担忧。


近义词：altered, amended, revised, changed, adjusted

反义词：original, unchanged, unmodified

词性：形容词
