fretter:/ˈfrɛtər/ | v. |烦扰；使苦恼；磨损；|To worry or irritate someone. To wear away by rubbing. |例句：The constant noise fretted him.  持续的噪音让他烦躁。|例句：The rough surface fretted the fabric.粗糙的表面磨损了织物。|近义词：annoy, irritate, chafe, wear away |反义词：soothe, calm, relax

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He frets about his exams. = 他为考试而苦恼。
The constant noise frets me. = 持续的噪音让我烦躁。


一般过去时:
She fretted over her lost keys. = 她为丢失的钥匙而苦恼。
The river fretted the rocks. = 河水冲蚀了岩石。


现在完成时:
He has fretted himself sick. = 他把自己愁病了。
The wind has fretted the stonework. = 风已经侵蚀了石砌结构。


过去完成时:
She had fretted all day about the meeting. = 她整天都在为会议而担忧。
The sea had fretted away the cliff edge. = 海水已经侵蚀掉了悬崖边缘。


将来时:
He will fret about the journey. = 他会为旅途而担忧。
The rain will fret the paintwork. = 雨水会侵蚀油漆。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't have so many problems, I wouldn't fret so much. = 如果我没有这么多问题，我就不会这么焦虑。
I wish I didn't fret so easily. = 我希望我不那么容易焦虑。


被动语态:
He was fretted by the news. = 他被这个消息弄得心烦意乱。
The fabric was fretted by the rough surface. = 织物被粗糙的表面磨损了。


疑问句:
Does the thought of the exam fret you? = 考试的想法让你焦虑吗？
Does the rough texture fret the skin? = 粗糙的纹理会磨损皮肤吗？


否定句:
He didn't fret about the small things. = 他不会为小事而烦恼。
The smooth surface didn't fret the wood. = 光滑的表面不会磨损木材。


PHRASE->
fret over: 为...担忧
fret about: 为...烦恼
fret oneself: 使自己烦恼
fret away: 慢慢磨损
fret out: 烦躁不安
