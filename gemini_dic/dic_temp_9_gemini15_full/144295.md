outspinning: /ˌaʊtˈspɪnɪŋ/ | n. | 超越；胜过；纺纱（尤指精细纺纱） | The act of surpassing or exceeding something, especially in a competitive context; also refers to the process of spinning yarn, particularly fine yarn. |例句：The company's outspinning of its competitors in innovation was remarkable.  这家公司在创新方面超越竞争对手的表现令人瞩目。

The new technology allows for the outspinning of incredibly fine threads. 这项新技术可以纺出极细的纱线。|近义词：surpassing, outdoing, excelling, spinning (in the context of yarn) |反义词：underperforming, lagging behind, losing to
