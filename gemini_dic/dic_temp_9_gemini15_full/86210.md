heathenizes:/ˈhiːðənʌɪzɪz/| vt.|使异教化；使野蛮化 |To convert or cause to adopt the practices or beliefs of heathens; to make barbaric or uncivilized.|例句：The conquering army heathenized the conquered people, forcing them to abandon their traditions and adopt pagan beliefs.  征服的军队使被征服的人民异教化，强迫他们放弃自己的传统并采纳异教信仰。

例句：The isolated tribe's culture was slowly heathenized by contact with the outside world.  与外界接触后，这个孤立部落的文化逐渐被异教化。


近义词：paganize, barbarize, brutalize |反义词：civilize, refine, cultivate |词性：动词
