punchable: /ˈpʌntʃəbl/ | adj. |  易于被击打的；令人想揍的 |  Describing someone or something that provokes a strong desire to punch them or it.  It suggests extreme annoyance or anger. |

例句：

He was so annoying; he was completely punchable. 他太烦人了，真想揍他。

That rude comment was utterly punchable.  那句粗鲁的评论真是让人想打人。


近义词：irritating, infuriating, maddening, annoying, provoking

反义词：pleasant, agreeable, charming, delightful


词性：形容词
