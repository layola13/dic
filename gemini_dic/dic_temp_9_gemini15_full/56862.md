dorsum:/ˈdɔːrsəm/ | n. | 背部；背面；脊；山脊 | The back or upper surface of an animal, especially the back of a human body or the back of a hand.  It also refers to the upper surface of an organ or other anatomical structure. | Example: The cat arched its dorsum.  这只猫弓起了背。  The dorsum of the hand is the back of the hand.  手的背面是手背。|近义词：back, spine, ridge | 反义词：ventral, underside
