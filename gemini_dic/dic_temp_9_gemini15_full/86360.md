hectometer:/ˈhɛktoʊˌmiːtər/| n. |百米，百公尺|A hectometer is a unit of length equal to 100 meters.|The farmer measured the field in hectometers.|近义词：hundred meters|反义词：centimeter, millimeter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The track is 400 hectometers long. = 这条跑道长400百米。
This field measures 10 hectometers across. = 这个田地宽10百米。


一般过去时:
The race covered 2 hectometers. = 比赛覆盖了2百米。
They walked 5 hectometers to the village. = 他们步行5百米到村庄。

We have run more than 3 hectometers today. = 我们今天已经跑了超过3百米了。
