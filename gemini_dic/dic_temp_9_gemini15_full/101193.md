inviolability: /ˌɪnˌvaɪələˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可侵犯性；神圣不可侵犯 | The state or quality of being inviolable; the inability to be violated or infringed upon.  It often refers to rights, laws, or principles that are considered sacred and must be protected. | The inviolability of the home is a fundamental right. 家庭的不可侵犯性是一项基本权利。The inviolability of personal data is crucial in the digital age. 在数字时代，个人数据的不可侵犯性至关重要。| sanctity, immunity, inviolability of contract | violation, infringement, breach |  名词
