missionaries:/ˈmɪʃənɛriz/| n. |传教士 |People who travel to a foreign country with the aim of converting people to their religion.  Missionaries often engage in charitable work and community development alongside their religious activities. |The missionaries worked tirelessly to spread Christianity in the remote villages. 传教士们不遗余力地在偏远村庄传播基督教。  Many missionaries dedicated their lives to serving the poor and needy. 许多传教士将一生奉献给服务穷人和弱势群体。|evangelists, proselytizers |anti-missionaries (not a direct antonym, but represents opposition)|名词
