/ɛfˈtiːɛks/ | n. | FTX |  FTX, a cryptocurrency exchange, was a prominent digital asset trading platform.  It offered a range of services including spot trading, derivatives trading, and leveraged tokens. However, the company filed for bankruptcy in November 2022 amid allegations of mismanagement and fraud. | Example: The collapse of FTX sent shockwaves through the cryptocurrency market.
