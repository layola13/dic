justiciary:/dʒuˈstɪʃəri/| n. |司法机关；法官；审判员 | A justiciary is a system of courts or judges, or a judge in a particular court.  The term often refers to a specific court or system,  rather than a single judge. | Example: Scotland's ancient justiciary system has evolved over centuries. 苏格兰古老的司法系统已经发展了几个世纪。 The justiciary handed down a harsh sentence. 法官做出了严厉的判决。|近义词：judiciary, court, tribunal |反义词：无
