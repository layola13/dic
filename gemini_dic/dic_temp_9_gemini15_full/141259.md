opalescence:/oʊˈpeɪləsəns/ | n. |乳光，乳白光；蛋白石光泽 | A milky or pearly iridescence, like that of an opal.  It is caused by the diffraction of light by colloidal particles within a substance. | The opalescence of the moonstone was captivating. 月长石的乳白色光泽令人着迷。 The sunset cast an opalescence over the clouds. 日落给云彩蒙上了一层乳白色光泽。| iridescence, sheen | dullness, opacity | 名词
