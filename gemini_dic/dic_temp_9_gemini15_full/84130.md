half-meant:/ˈhæfment/| adj.|半开玩笑的；并非真心实意的|Not fully serious or sincere; partly intended.  The statement or action shows a lack of full commitment or seriousness.|His half-meant apology did little to soothe her anger. 他敷衍了事的道歉并没有平息她的怒火。|近义词：insincere, lukewarm, perfunctory|反义词：sincere, heartfelt, earnest
