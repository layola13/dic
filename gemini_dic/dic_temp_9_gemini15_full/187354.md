rocky:/ˈrɑːki/ /ˈrɒki/| adj.|多岩石的；崎岖的；动荡的；坚强的|

英文解析:  Relating to or resembling rock;  characterized by rocks or stones; uneven or rugged in surface; unsteady or insecure;  firm or resolute.


例句:

The path was rocky and difficult to walk on. = 小路崎岖不平，难以行走。

He had a rocky relationship with his father. = 他和父亲的关系很紧张。


近义词:  bumpy, rugged, uneven, unstable, tough, strong

反义词: smooth, even, stable, weak, gentle


词性: adjective
