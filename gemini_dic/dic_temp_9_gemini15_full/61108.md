His elocutions were marked by clarity and precision.他的演讲以清晰和精准为特点。


近义词：oratory, rhetoric, articulation, delivery |反义词：mumbling, stammering, inarticulateness


词源：  From Latin *ēloquī "to speak out," from *ex- "out" + *loquī "to speak."  The suffix "-tion" forms nouns of action or process.
