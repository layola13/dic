compromisers: /ˈkɒmprəmaɪzəz/ | n. | 妥协者，让步者 | Those who make compromises; individuals willing to adjust their positions to reach an agreement.  They often prioritize consensus over maintaining rigid stances. | Example: The peace talks were successful because both sides included skilled compromisers.  和谈成功是因为双方都包含了熟练的妥协者。


近义词: conciliators, mediators, negotiators

反义词:  intransigents, obstructionists, hardliners

词性: 名词
