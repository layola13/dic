maoism:/ˈmaʊɪzəm/| n. |毛泽东思想，毛泽东主义|The political theory and practice of Mao Zedong, emphasizing class struggle, peasant revolution, and the importance of the Communist Party.  It combines Marxist-Leninist ideology with Chinese characteristics.  Maoism is characterized by its emphasis on the role of the peasantry in revolution, continuous revolution, and the importance of self-reliance.|例句：Maoism significantly impacted China's political and social landscape for decades.|近义词：Marxism-Leninism, communism|反义词：capitalism, democracy
