rushier:/ˈrʌʃər/| adj. |更匆忙的，更仓促的 |More hurried or rushed.  It implies a greater degree of urgency or haste than simply "rushed."|例句：The rush-hour traffic was even rushier than usual today. 今天的早高峰交通比平时更拥挤。|例句：His explanation was rushed and rushier than I expected, leaving many questions unanswered.他的解释比我预期的仓促和匆忙，留下许多问题没有回答。|近义词：more hurried, more rushed, faster, quicker |反义词：leisurely, unhurried, calm, deliberate
