clouts:/klaʊts/| n. |影响力；势力；一击；（非正式）耳光|The mayor has considerable clout with the city council. 市长对市议会有相当的影响力。|近义词：influence, power, authority |反义词：powerlessness, weakness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He wields significant clout in the industry. = 他在业界拥有相当的影响力。
Their company holds considerable clout in the market. = 他们的公司在市场上拥有相当的影响力。


一般过去时:
The senator used his clout to get the bill passed. = 参议员利用他的影响力让法案通过。
Her family's clout helped her get the job. = 她家里的势力帮助她得到了这份工作。


现在完成时:
He has gained considerable clout since he became CEO. = 自从他成为CEO以来，他已经获得了相当的影响力。
The company has lost some clout in recent years. = 近年来，该公司失去了一些影响力。


过去完成时:
They had used their clout to secure the contract before the competition even knew about it. = 在竞争对手知道之前，他们就利用自己的影响力拿到了合同。
She had already established her clout in the community before starting her own business. = 在自己创业之前，她已经在社区建立了影响力。


将来时:
He will need all the clout he can muster to win this election. = 他需要尽其所能的影响力来赢得这次选举。
The new policy will likely increase the company's clout in the market. = 新政策可能会增强该公司在市场上的影响力。


虚拟语气:
If he had more clout, he could easily get the project approved. = 如果他更有影响力，他就能轻松获得项目的批准。
I wish I had the clout to change things. = 我希望我有能力改变现状。


被动语态:
His clout was used to unfairly influence the decision. = 他的影响力被用来不正当地影响决策。
The company's clout is being challenged by a new competitor. = 该公司的影响力正受到一家新竞争对手的挑战。


疑问句:
Does he have enough clout to make a difference? = 他有足够的影响力来改变局面吗？
What clout does the organization hold in the international community? = 该组织在国际社会中有何影响力？


否定句:
He doesn't have the clout to influence the outcome. = 他没有能力影响结果。
They lacked the clout to push through their proposed changes. = 他们缺乏推行他们提出的改变的影响力。
