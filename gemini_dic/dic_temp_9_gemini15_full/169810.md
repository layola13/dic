proscolex:/ˈprɒskoʊlɛks/ | n. |前体节，前体|The immature, undifferentiated anterior region of a tapeworm strobilus, preceding the mature proglottids.  It is the region where new proglottids are formed. |例句：The scolex and proscolex of the tapeworm were examined microscopically.  (绦虫的头部和前体节在显微镜下被检查。)

The proscolex is the region of a tapeworm's strobila where new proglottids are formed by asexual reproduction.  It's the undifferentiated, pre-proglottid region at the anterior end of the strobila. |近义词：preproglottid (不完全等同，proscolex特指绦虫)|反义词：strobila (整体结构与部分相对)
