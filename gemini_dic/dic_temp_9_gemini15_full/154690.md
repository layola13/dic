personify:/pərˈsɑːnɪfaɪ/|/ˈpɜːrsənɪfaɪ/| vt.|拟人化；赋予…人性|To give human qualities or attributes to something that is not human, such as an object, animal, or idea.  This can involve representing abstract concepts as human characters or giving inanimate objects human-like characteristics.|The poet personified the wind as a mischievous child.|近义词：anthropomorphize, embody, represent|反义词：dehumanize, depersonalize|动词
