rewrapping:/ˌriːˈræpɪŋ/| v.|重新包装，重新包裹|The act of wrapping something again;  packaging something anew.  It often implies a previous wrapping has been removed or damaged.|例句：The store offered rewrapping services for gifts.  这家商店提供礼品重新包装服务。

例句：After the rain damaged the package, we needed to rewrap the goods. 雨水损坏包裹后，我们需要重新包装货物。


近义词：repackaging, repacking, reboxing


反义词：unwrapping, unwrapping


词性：动词
