nonelectronic: /ˌnɒnˌɛlɛkˈtrɒnɪk/ | adj. |非电子的| Not using or relating to electricity.  This adjective describes things that do not operate using electricity or electronic components.  It emphasizes the absence of electronic functionality.


例句：

The museum boasts a collection of nonelectronic musical instruments. = 博物馆收藏了一批非电子的乐器。

We prefer nonelectronic toys for our children to minimize screen time. = 为了减少孩子们的屏幕时间，我们更喜欢非电子的玩具。


近义词：mechanical, manual, non-electronic


反义词：electronic, electrical


词性:形容词
