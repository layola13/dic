expressways: /ˈɛkspresˌweɪz/ | n. | 高速公路 |  A highway designed for high-speed traffic, typically with multiple lanes and controlled access.  Expressways are characterized by their limited access points, preventing intersections at grade and promoting smoother, faster travel. |  例句：We took the expressway to get to the airport quickly. 我们走高速公路去机场以便快速到达。

例句：Driving on the expressway can be dangerous during rush hour.  在高峰期高速公路上开车很危险。


近义词：freeway, motorway, highway, autoroute


反义词：local road, back road


词性: 名词
