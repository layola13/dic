revolutionism: /ˌrevəˈluːʃənɪzəm/ | n. |革命主义，革命思想|The advocacy or pursuit of revolution as a means of social or political change.  It often involves radical changes in the existing social and political structures. |例句：The rise of revolutionism in the late 18th century led to significant societal shifts.  18世纪末革命主义的兴起导致了重大的社会变革。

例句：His writings were filled with revolutionary ideas and a strong belief in revolutionism.  他的著作充满了革命思想和对革命主义的坚定信念。


近义词：radicalism, insurrectionism, rebellion

反义词：conservatism, traditionalism, status quo
