inquiry:/ɪnˈkwaɪəri/| n. |询问；调查；探究|The act of asking for information; a question; an official investigation.|例句：I made an inquiry about the price of the tickets. 我询问了票价。|例句：The police are conducting an inquiry into the accident. 警方正在对事故进行调查。|近义词：question, investigation, interrogation|反义词：answer, response


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He makes an inquiry about the lost package. 他询问了关于丢失包裹的事情。
She makes frequent inquiries about her son's well-being. 她经常询问她儿子的安危。


一般过去时:
They made an inquiry at the information desk. 他们在问讯处进行了询问。
The detective made several inquiries before arresting the suspect. 警探在逮捕嫌疑人之前进行了多次询问。


现在完成时:
He has made an inquiry about the job opening. 他已经就职位空缺进行了询问。
She has made numerous inquiries but hasn't found a solution yet. 她进行了多次询问，但仍未找到解决办法。


过去完成时:
They had made inquiries before deciding to invest. 他们在决定投资之前已经进行了询问。
She had made several inquiries, but no one knew anything about it. 她进行了多次询问，但没有人知道这件事。


将来时:
He will make an inquiry about the delivery status. 他将询问送货状态。
She will make an inquiry at the customer service center. 她将在客户服务中心进行询问。


虚拟语气:
If I had made an inquiry earlier, I wouldn't be in this situation. 如果我早点询问，我就不会陷入这种情况。
I wish I had made an inquiry before signing the contract. 我真希望在签合同之前我进行了询问。


被动语态:
An inquiry was made about the missing documents. 就丢失的文件进行了询问。
Several inquiries are being made into the company's financial practices. 正在对公司的财务行为进行多项调查。


疑问句:
Is there any inquiry about this matter? 关于此事有疑问吗？
Have you had any inquiries regarding the product? 你对该产品有任何疑问吗？


否定句:
He didn't make any inquiry before making the purchase. 他在购买之前没有进行任何询问。
She didn't make an inquiry about the terms and conditions. 她没有询问条款和条件。


PHRASE->
make an inquiry  进行询问
conduct an inquiry  进行调查
file an inquiry  提交询问
launch an inquiry  启动调查
