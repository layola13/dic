diagramming:/ˈdaɪəˌɡræmɪŋ/ | v. | 绘制图表；图示；用图表表示 | The act of creating diagrams to visually represent information, relationships, or processes.  It involves using various symbols and lines to illustrate a concept or system. | Example:  Diagramming the sentence helped me understand its grammatical structure.  绘制这个句子的图表帮助我理解了它的语法结构。|近义词：charting, schematizing, graphing | 反义词：describing (in words only)


USAGE->

They diagram the circuit. = 他们绘制电路图。
We diagram the data flow. = 我们绘制数据流图。

一般过去时:
He diagrammed the process yesterday. = 他昨天绘制了这个过程的图表。
She diagrammed the sentence structure. = 她绘制了句子的结构图。
