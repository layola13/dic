craver:/ˈkreɪvər/| n. |渴望者，热切追求者|A person who has a strong desire or craving for something, often something considered harmful or unhealthy.  This word emphasizes the intense, almost desperate nature of the desire.|He's a craver of power, always seeking more influence.|近义词：addict, enthusiast, fiend |反义词：indifferent, apathetic


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The smoker is a craver of nicotine. = 这个吸烟者渴望尼古丁。
She is a craver of adventure and excitement. = 她渴望冒险和刺激。

一般过去时:
He was a craver of attention as a child. = 他小时候渴望得到关注。
They were cravers of forbidden knowledge. = 他们渴望被禁止的知识。


现在完成时:
She has been a craver of sweets her whole life. = 她一生都渴望甜食。
He has become a craver of solitude in his old age. = 他上了年纪后变得渴望独处。


过去完成时:
Before the accident, he had been a craver of speed and adrenaline. = 事故发生前，他渴望速度和肾上腺素。
She had been a craver of validation from others for many years. = 多年来，她一直渴望得到别人的认可。


将来时:
He will likely remain a craver of recognition throughout his career. = 他很可能在整个职业生涯中都会渴望得到认可。
She will always be a craver of new experiences. = 她将永远渴望新的体验。


虚拟语气:
If I were a craver of luxury, I would buy that car. = 如果我渴望奢侈品，我会买那辆车。
I wish I weren't such a craver of approval. = 我希望我不那么渴望得到别人的认可。


被动语态:
(This word is rarely used in the passive voice.  A more natural phrasing would be used.)  *The position was craved by many ambitious individuals.*  = 许多有野心的人渴望得到这个职位。
*His approval was craved by everyone in the office.* = 办公室里每个人都渴望得到他的认可。


疑问句:
Is he a craver of praise and admiration? = 他渴望赞扬和钦佩吗？
Are you a craver of strong coffee? = 你渴望浓咖啡吗？


否定句:
She is not a craver of social interaction. = 她不渴望社交互动。
He is not a craver of material possessions. = 他不渴望物质财富。
