excludes:/ɪkˈskluːdz/| vt.|排除；不包括；排斥|To leave out; to prevent from being included.|The final list excludes several names.|最终名单中排除了一些名字。|The study excludes participants with a history of heart disease.|这项研究排除了有心脏病史的参与者。|includes, comprises|includes, contains|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The contract excludes liability for indirect damages. = 合同中不包括对间接损害的赔偿责任。
This tour excludes transportation. = 此行程不包含交通费用。


一般过去时:
The teacher excluded him from the competition. = 老师把他排除在比赛之外。
They excluded that possibility from their analysis. = 他们在分析中排除了那种可能性。


现在完成时:
The company has excluded all non-essential personnel from the meeting. = 公司已将所有非必要人员排除在会议之外。
She has excluded herself from social activities recently. = 她最近把自己排除在社交活动之外。


过去完成时:
He had excluded that possibility before making his decision. = 他在做决定之前就排除了那种可能性。
They had excluded the evidence from the trial. = 他们已经在审判中排除了证据。


将来时:
The new rules will exclude certain types of vehicles from the city center. = 新规将禁止某些类型的车辆进入市中心。
We will exclude him from the project if he continues to be disruptive. = 如果他继续捣乱，我们将把他排除在项目之外。


虚拟语气:
If they excluded that data, the results would be different. = 如果他们排除那些数据，结果就会不同。
I wish they had excluded the controversial topic from the discussion. = 我希望他们能把有争议的话题从讨论中排除掉。


被动语态:
He was excluded from the team because of his poor performance. = 因为他表现不佳，他被排除在队伍之外。
Many products were excluded from the sale. = 许多产品被排除在促销之外。


疑问句:
Does the warranty exclude accidental damage? = 保修是否不包括意外损坏？
Does this price exclude tax? = 这个价格是否不含税？


否定句:
The guarantee does not exclude defects in materials. = 保证不排除材料缺陷。
The agreement explicitly did not exclude liability for negligence. = 该协议明确未排除对过失的责任。



PHRASE->
exclude from = 从…中排除
mutually exclusive = 互相排斥的
to the exclusion of = 排斥…
exclude someone from something = 将某人从某事中排除
barring (excluding) = 除…之外
