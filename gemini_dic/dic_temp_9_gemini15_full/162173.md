pre-Linnean:/ˌpriːˈlɪniən/| adj.|林奈以前的；前林奈时代的 | Relating to the period of biological classification before the system developed by Carl Linnaeus (1707-1778).  This period was characterized by less systematic and less standardized approaches to naming and classifying organisms. | Example:  Pre-Linnean taxonomic systems were often based on arbitrary characteristics.  林奈以前的分类系统通常基于武断的特征。|近义词：pre-systematic |反义词：post-Linnean
