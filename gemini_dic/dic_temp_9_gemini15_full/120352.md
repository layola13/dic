minimised:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪzd/ | v. | 最小化；使减到最小 | To reduce something to the smallest possible amount or degree.  | 例句：The company minimised its losses by cutting costs. 公司通过削减成本将损失降到了最低。 | 例句：We need to minimise the risk of accidents. 我们需要将事故风险降到最低。 | 近义词：reduce, lessen, decrease, diminish | 反义词：maximize, amplify, increase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They minimise their expenses. = 他们尽量减少开支。
He minimises the impact of the problem. = 他尽量减少问题的影响。

一般过去时:
The government minimised the damage caused by the storm. = 政府将风暴造成的损失降到了最低。
She minimised her role in the project. = 她淡化了自己在项目中的作用。

现在完成时:
They have minimised their carbon footprint. = 他们已经将碳足迹降到了最低。
We have minimised the risks involved. = 我们已经将相关的风险降到了最低。

过去完成时:
He had minimised his involvement in the affair. = 他已经尽量减少自己在该事件中的参与。
They had minimised the potential losses before the crisis. = 危机爆发前，他们已经将潜在损失降到了最低。

将来时:
We will minimise the disruption to services. = 我们将把对服务的干扰降到最低。
The company will minimise its environmental impact. = 公司将尽量减少其对环境的影响。

虚拟语气:
If we minimised our spending, we could save more money. = 如果我们减少开支，就能省更多钱。
I wish I had minimised my mistakes. = 我真希望我犯的错误少一些。

被动语态:
The risk was minimised by taking precautions. = 通过采取预防措施，风险被降到了最低。
The impact of the crisis was minimised by the government's response. = 政府的应对措施将危机的冲击降到了最低。

疑问句:
How can we minimise the cost? = 我们怎样才能把成本降到最低？
Have they minimised the potential risks? = 他们是否已经将潜在风险降到最低？

否定句:
They did not minimise the importance of the issue. = 他们并没有轻视这个问题的重要性。
The company failed to minimise its losses. = 公司未能将损失降到最低。
