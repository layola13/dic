quasi-injured:/ˈkwɑːziˈɪndʒərd/| adj.|似受伤的，貌似受伤的 | seemingly injured;  suggesting injury but not necessarily fully injured.  It implies a state that resembles injury but lacks the full severity or confirmation of a true injury.| 例句：He was quasi-injured after the fall, complaining of pain but with no visible wounds. 他摔倒后似受了伤，抱怨疼痛但没有明显的伤口。

例句：The athlete's quasi-injured knee prevented him from participating in the competition. 运动员貌似受伤的膝盖使他无法参加比赛。| 近义词：slightly injured, apparently injured, superficially hurt | 反义词：uninjured, healthy, fully recovered
