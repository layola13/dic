jezabels:/ˈdʒɛzəbɛlz/| n.|耶洗别（女子名，象征淫荡和邪恶的女人）|A Jezebel is a woman who is considered to be wicked, morally unrestrained, and promiscuous. The name comes from the biblical Jezebel, the Phoenician princess who was the wife of King Ahab of Israel and known for her idolatry and cruelty.|例句：She was accused of being a Jezebel, a woman of loose morals and scandalous behavior. 她被指控是耶洗别，一个道德败坏、行为丑闻的女人。

例句：He called her a Jezebel for her manipulative and cruel actions. 他称她为耶洗别，因为她行为具有操纵性和残酷性。
