deniability:/ˌdɛniəˈbɪləti/ | n. |否认；不可否认性 | The state or quality of being deniable; the ability to deny something.  It often refers to the ability to plausibly deny involvement in something, especially something illegal or unethical.  |例句：The company's actions were designed to ensure plausible deniability. 公司的行为旨在确保其可以合理地否认责任。


The lack of direct evidence provided the accused with a degree of deniability. 缺乏直接证据使被告有了一定程度的否认空间。| 近义词：repudiation, disavowal, disclaiming | 反义词：admissibility, accountability, responsibility | 名词
