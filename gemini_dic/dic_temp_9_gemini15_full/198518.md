shriveled:/ˈʃrɪvəld/ | adj. | 枯萎的；干瘪的 |  Describes something that has shrunk and wrinkled, often due to lack of water or aging.  | The shriveled apple was inedible.  这颗干瘪的苹果不能吃了。 | withered, wrinkled, dried-up | plump, full, juicy | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun shrivels the leaves. = 太阳晒干了树叶。
The fruit shrivels in the heat. = 水果在热浪中干瘪了。

一般过去时:
The intense heat shriveled the plants. = 强烈的热量使植物枯萎了。
The grapes shriveled on the vine. = 葡萄在藤上干枯了。

现在完成时:
The drought has shriveled the crops. = 干旱使庄稼枯萎了。
The prolonged heat has shriveled the vegetables. = 长时间的炎热使蔬菜干枯了。

过去完成时:
The leaves had shriveled before the rain came. = 雨来之前叶子已经枯萎了。
The fruit had shriveled considerably by the time we harvested it. = 我们收获的时候，水果已经相当干瘪了。

将来时:
The intense sun will shriveled the flowers. = 强烈的阳光会使花朵枯萎。
The lack of water will shriveled the plants. = 缺水会使植物枯萎。


虚拟语气:
If it hadn't rained, the plants would have shriveled. = 如果不下雨，植物就会枯萎。
If the weather continues like this, the crops will surely shriveled. = 如果天气继续这样下去，庄稼肯定会枯萎。


被动语态:
The leaves were shriveled by the frost. = 叶子被霜冻枯萎了。
The fruit was shriveled by the sun. = 水果被太阳晒干瘪了。


疑问句:
Were the leaves shriveled by the frost? = 叶子是被霜冻枯萎的吗？
Have the grapes shriveled yet? = 葡萄干瘪了吗？


否定句:
The plants haven't shriveled yet. = 植物还没枯萎。
The fruit didn't shriveled despite the heat. = 尽管天气炎热，水果也没有干瘪。

词源:
From Middle English *shrivelen, from Old English *scrincōn ("to shrink").  Ultimately related to the Proto-Germanic root *skrinkōną.
