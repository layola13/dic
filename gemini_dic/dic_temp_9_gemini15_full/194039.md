self-fulfilment:/ˌsɛlf fʊlˈfɪlmənt/| n. |自我实现，自我满足 |The act of fulfilling one's potential and achieving personal goals.  It often involves a sense of purpose and meaning in life. |例句：He found self-fulfilment in his work as a teacher. 他在作为一名教师的工作中找到了自我实现。

例句：Through years of hard work and dedication, she achieved a sense of self-fulfilment. 通过多年的努力和奉献，她实现了一种自我满足感。


近义词：self-actualization, self-realization, personal growth |反义词：dissatisfaction, frustration, unfulfillment


词源:  The word is a compound of "self," meaning oneself, and "fulfilment,"  meaning the act of fulfilling or satisfying something.  "Fulfilment" itself comes from the Old English "fulfyllan," meaning "to fill completely".
