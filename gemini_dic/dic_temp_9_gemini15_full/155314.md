phantom:/ˈfæntəm/| n. adj.|幽灵；幻影；虚幻的；微弱的|A phantom is something that appears to exist but does not, often associated with ghosts or illusions.  It can also refer to something intangible or barely perceptible. |例句：The phantom of the opera haunted the theatre.歌剧魅影萦绕着剧院。The pain was a mere phantom, disappearing as quickly as it arrived.疼痛只是虚幻的，来去匆匆。|近义词：ghost, apparition, illusion, specter |反义词：real, tangible, substantial
