casework:/ˈkeɪsˌwɜːrk/| n. |个案工作；个案处理|The practical work of dealing with individual cases, especially by social workers or other professionals.  This involves investigating and resolving specific problems or situations faced by individuals or families. |例句：The social worker spent considerable time on casework with the troubled family. 社工花了很多时间处理这个有问题的家庭的个案工作。|例句：The lawyer's casework load increased significantly after the merger. 律师事务所并购后，律师的个案工作量显著增加。|近义词：case management, client services, individual assistance|反义词：policy work,  general administration
