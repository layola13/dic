recategorizes:/ˌriːˈkætəɡəraɪzɪz/ | vt. |重新分类，重新归类 |To classify or categorize something again, often in a different way.  |例句：The librarian recategorized the books after the new system was implemented. 图书管理员在新系统实施后对书籍进行了重新分类。
例句：The company recategorized its products to better target specific market segments. 公司重新分类其产品以更好地瞄准特定市场细分。|近义词：reorganize, reclassify, regroup |反义词：categorize (in the original way), classify (in the original way) |动词
