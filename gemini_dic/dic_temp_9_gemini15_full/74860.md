frequents:/frɪˈkwɛnts/| v.|常去，经常光顾|to visit (a place) often or regularly.  |例句：He frequents that coffee shop near his office. 他经常光顾他办公室附近的那家咖啡店。
例句：She frequents the library, borrowing books every week. 她经常去图书馆，每周借书。|近义词：visits, attends, patronizes|反义词：avoids, shuns|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He frequents the gym three times a week. = 他每周去健身房三次。
They frequent that new restaurant downtown. = 他们经常光顾市中心那家新开的餐馆。


一般过去时:
She frequented that bookstore before it closed. = 那家书店关门前她经常去那里。
He frequented the same bar every Friday night. = 他每个星期五晚上都去同一家酒吧。


现在完成时:
He has frequented this restaurant for years. = 他光顾这家餐馆已经很多年了。
She has frequented many different countries. = 她去过很多不同的国家。


过去完成时:
Before moving, she had frequented that cafe for five years. = 在搬家之前，她已经在那家咖啡馆光顾了五年。
They had frequented that club before it became popular. = 那家俱乐部流行起来之前，他们就经常去那里了。


将来时:
I will frequent this shop more often. = 我以后会更经常光顾这家商店。
They will frequent the beach during the summer. = 他们夏天会经常去海滩。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would frequent the museum more often. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常去博物馆。
I wish I could frequent that expensive restaurant. = 我真希望我能经常去那家昂贵的餐馆。


被动语态:
That cafe is frequented by many students. = 那家咖啡馆有很多学生经常光顾。
The library is frequented by scholars and researchers. = 图书馆经常被学者和研究人员光顾。


疑问句:
Do you frequent this place often? = 你经常来这里吗？
Does he frequent the gym regularly? = 他经常去健身房吗？


否定句:
I don't frequent that bar anymore. = 我不再去那家酒吧了。
She doesn't frequent nightclubs. = 她不去夜总会。


PHRASE->
frequently frequents = 经常光顾
regularly frequents = 定期光顾
often frequents = 经常光顾
always frequents = 总是光顾
occasionally frequents = 偶尔光顾
