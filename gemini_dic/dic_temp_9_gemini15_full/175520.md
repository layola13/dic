ran away with: /ˌræn əˈweɪ wɪð/ | phrasal verb | 携...而逃；私奔；赢得；轻易获得；使着迷 |

英文解析：This phrasal verb has several meanings depending on the context.  It can describe running away with something or someone, winning something easily, or being captivated by something.

例句：

The thief ran away with the jewels.  小偷携珠宝而逃。

She ran away with her boyfriend. 她和男朋友私奔了。

He ran away with the first prize. 他轻而易举地赢得了头奖。

The movie ran away with my emotions.  这部电影深深打动了我。

His imagination ran away with him. 他的想象力不受控制地奔腾。


近义词：eloped (私奔), absconded (潜逃), escaped (逃脱), captivated (迷住), won easily (轻易获胜)


反义词： remained with (留下), stayed with (留下), lost (输掉), ignored (忽视)


词性: phrasal verb (短语动词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He runs away with the money every month. 他每个月都卷款潜逃。
The story runs away with the reader's imagination. 故事激发了读者的想象力。

一般过去时:
They ran away with all the valuables. 他们卷走了所有贵重物品。
The team ran away with the victory.  那个队轻松地赢得了胜利。

现在完成时:
She has run away with a circus performer. 她已经和一个马戏团演员私奔了。
The rumor has run away with the facts. 谣言已经盖过了事实真相。

过去完成时:
The suspect had run away with the evidence. 嫌疑人已经携证据潜逃了。
Before I knew it, the day had run away with me.  不知不觉，一天就过去了。

将来时:
They will run away with the trophy. 他们将赢得奖杯。
I fear he will run away with a bad idea. 我担心他会采纳一个糟糕的主意。

虚拟语气:
If he had a chance, he would run away with her. 如果有机会，他会和她私奔。
If the plan had worked, we would have run away with millions. 如果计划成功了，我们将赚到数百万。

被动语态:  (This phrasal verb is rarely used in the passive voice)

疑问句:
Did he run away with the money? 他卷款潜逃了吗？
Has she run away with anyone? 她和谁私奔了吗？

否定句:
He didn't run away with the prize. 他没有赢得奖品。
She didn't run away with her feelings. 她没有被自己的感情冲昏头脑。
