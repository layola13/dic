familiarises: /fəˈmɪliəraɪzɪz/ | vt. | 使熟悉；使了解 | To make someone or something familiar; to make someone or something known.  | 例句：The teacher familiarises her students with the new curriculum. 老师让学生们熟悉新的课程。 | 近义词：acquaint, introduce, initiate | 反义词：alienate, estrange | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He familiarises himself with the new software every day. = 他每天都熟悉新的软件。
The company familiarises its employees with safety procedures. = 公司让员工熟悉安全程序。

一般过去时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
