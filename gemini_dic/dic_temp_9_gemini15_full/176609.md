reaccessions:/ˌriːækˈseʃənz/| n. |重新入藏；再次登记入册|The museum's reaccessions program focuses on updating its catalog and database.  博物馆的重新入藏计划侧重于更新其目录和数据库。|近义词：re-cataloging, re-registration |反义词：declassification, disposal
