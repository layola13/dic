non-metaphorical: /ˌnɒnˌmɛtəˈfɒrɪkəl/ | adj. | 非比喻的，字面的 |  Literal; not using metaphors or figurative language.  The word describes something that is stated directly and plainly, without any implied meaning or symbolic representation.  It emphasizes the absence of metaphorical expression.


例句：

The scientist gave a non-metaphorical account of the experiment, avoiding any colorful language or analogies.  科学家对实验做了非比喻的描述，避免了任何花哨的语言或比喻。


He preferred non-metaphorical language in his writing, aiming for clarity and precision. 他更喜欢在他的写作中使用非比喻性的语言，力求清晰和精确。


近义词：literal, figurative, plain, direct, unambiguous


反义词：metaphorical, figurative, symbolic, allegorical


词性：形容词
