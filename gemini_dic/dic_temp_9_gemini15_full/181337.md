reintroduced:/ˌriːɪntrəˈdjuːst/| v.|重新引入，重新介绍 | To introduce again; to bring back into use or practice.  |例句：The government reintroduced the tax after a period of suspension. 政府在暂停一段时间后重新开征了这项税。

例句：The zoo reintroduced the pandas to their natural habitat. 动物园将熊猫重新引入它们的自然栖息地。


近义词：reinstate, restore, revive |反义词：remove, abolish, eliminate |词性:动词
