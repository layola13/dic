merchant:/ˈmɜːrtʃənt/ | n. | 商人，零售商 | A person or company involved in wholesale trade, especially one dealing with foreign countries or in a specified type of goods.  |  He is a successful merchant who trades in silk. 他是一位成功的丝绸商人。 | trader, dealer, retailer | consumer, producer | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The merchant sells high-quality goods. = 这名商人出售高品质的商品。
That merchant offers competitive prices. = 那位商人提供有竞争力的价格。


一般过去时:
The merchant delivered the goods yesterday. = 商人昨天送来了货物。
The merchant closed his shop early. = 商人早早关了店。


现在完成时:
The merchant has expanded his business significantly. = 这名商人已经大幅扩展了他的业务。
The merchant has been in business for twenty years. = 这名商人经商已有二十年了。

将来时:
