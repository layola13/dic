patchy:/ˈpætʃi/| adj.|零星的；不均匀的；斑驳的|Existing or happening in small, isolated areas or places; not continuous or uniform.|The coverage of the mobile network was patchy in this area. 这个地区的移动网络覆盖不均匀。|The paint job was patchy, with some areas much thicker than others. 油漆工作不均匀，有些地方比其他地方厚得多。|consistent, uniform|even, continuous


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The signal is patchy. = 信号不稳定。
The weather is patchy today. = 今天天气时好时坏。


一般过去时:
The roads were patchy after the storm. = 暴风雨过后道路状况不佳。
His knowledge of history was patchy. = 他对历史的了解很零散。


现在完成时:

将来时:

疑问句:
