potshots:/ˈpɑːtʃɒts/ | n. |随意射击；随意批评；|  A potshot is a shot fired without aiming carefully, or a casual or uncritical remark. It often implies a lack of seriousness or precision.  |例句：He took potshots at the government's policies during the interview. 他在采访中随意批评了政府的政策。

例句：The comedian’s act consisted mainly of potshots at celebrities.  这位喜剧演员的表演主要是一些对名人的随意调侃。


近义词：casual remarks, criticisms, gibes, sneers, barbs

反义词：precise criticism, considered analysis, thoughtful commentary


词性:名词
