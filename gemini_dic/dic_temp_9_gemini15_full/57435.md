drachmai: /ˈdrækmaɪ/ | n. | 德拉克马（古希腊货币单位） | Drachmai is the plural form of drachma, the ancient Greek unit of currency.  It was a silver coin, and its value varied throughout history and across different Greek city-states. The drachma continued to be the currency of Greece until the adoption of the euro in 2001. | The museum displayed ancient Greek artifacts, including several well-preserved drachmai.  博物馆展示了古代希腊文物，其中包括几枚保存完好的德拉克马。


The ancient Greeks used drachmai for everyday transactions. 古代希腊人用德拉克马进行日常交易。
