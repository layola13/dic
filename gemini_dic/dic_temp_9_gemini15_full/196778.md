sexologists: /ˌsɛksˈɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. | 性学家 |  Experts who study human sexuality, including its biological, psychological, and social aspects.  They research sexual behavior, attitudes, and identities, often providing therapy or education related to sexual health. | Example: Many sexologists advocate for comprehensive sex education in schools. 许多性学家提倡在学校进行全面的性教育。| 近义词: sexual scientists, sexuality researchers | 反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑与保守派性观念持有者相对)
