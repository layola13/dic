feracious:/fəˈreɪʃəs/| adj.|多产的；肥沃的；富饶的|Fruitful; producing or capable of producing abundantly; prolific.  The term often refers to both biological productivity (like a fertile land or a prolific animal) and intellectual productivity (like a writer with a prolific output).|例句：The feracious river valley supported a large population.  这条肥沃的河谷养活了大量人口。

例句：She is a feracious writer, publishing several books a year. 她是一位多产的作家，每年出版几本书。


近义词：prolific, fertile, productive, abundant

反义词：barren, infertile, unproductive, sterile


词性：形容词


词源：源自拉丁语 *ferax, *feracis,  意思是“肥沃的，多产的”，  与动词 *ferre ("to bear, carry")  有关，暗示着"生育"或"生产"的能力。
