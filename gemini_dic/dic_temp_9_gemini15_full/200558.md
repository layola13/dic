slathering:/ˈslæðərɪŋ/| n. |涂抹；大量涂抹|The act of applying something thickly or liberally, often in a messy way.  It can refer to the substance being applied or the act of applying it. |例句：The slathering of butter on the toast was excessive.黄油涂在吐司上太多了。

例句：The cake was covered in a thick slathering of chocolate icing. 蛋糕上覆盖着一层厚厚的巧克力糖霜。


近义词：smearing, spreading, daubing, plastering|反义词：dabbing (small amount), sparing (small amount)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slathers butter on his toast every morning. = 他每天早上都在吐司上涂抹黄油。
She slathers sunscreen all over her body before going to the beach. = 她去海滩之前会在全身涂抹防晒霜。


一般过去时:
He slathered paint onto the canvas with gusto. = 他兴致勃勃地在画布上涂抹颜料。
She slathered mayonnaise on her sandwich. = 她把蛋黄酱涂在三明治上。


现在完成时:
She has slathered the entire cake with frosting. = 她已经把整个蛋糕都涂上了糖霜。
They have slathered sunblock on their children. = 他们已经给孩子们涂上了防晒霜。


过去完成时:
He had slathered the walls with wallpaper paste before hanging the wallpaper. = 他在贴壁纸之前已经把墙上涂满了壁纸胶。
She had slathered her face with moisturizer before going to bed. = 她睡觉前已经在脸上涂抹了保湿霜。


将来时:
I will slather the chicken in barbecue sauce before grilling it. = 我会在烤鸡肉之前用烧烤酱涂抹它。
She will slather honey on her pancakes. = 她会在煎饼上涂蜂蜜。


虚拟语气:
If I were to slather this much frosting on the cake, it would be too sweet. = 如果我在蛋糕上涂抹这么多的糖霜，它就会太甜了。
I wish I hadn't slathered so much ketchup on my hot dog. = 我真希望我没有在热狗上涂抹那么多番茄酱。


被动语态:
The bread was slathered with butter. = 面包上涂满了黄油。
The walls were slathered with paint. = 墙壁上涂满了油漆。


疑问句:
Are you slathering that cream on too thickly? = 你涂抹奶油太厚了吗？
Will you be slathering the cake with chocolate ganache? = 你会用巧克力甘纳许涂抹蛋糕吗？


否定句:
He didn't slather the paint on evenly. = 他没有均匀地涂抹油漆。
She didn't slather the lotion on her skin. = 她没有在皮肤上涂抹乳液。


PHRASE->
slather on = 涂抹上
slather with = 用...涂抹
a thick slathering of = 厚厚一层...
slathering of paint = 一层油漆
slathering of butter = 一层黄油

词源:
The word "slather" comes from the Middle English word "slatren," meaning to smear or bedaub.  This ultimately has roots in the Old Norse word "slatra," meaning to smear or daub.
