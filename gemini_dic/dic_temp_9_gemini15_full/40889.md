cookout:/ˈkʊkˌaʊt/ | n. |户外野餐；烧烤 |An outdoor gathering where food is cooked and eaten, typically involving grilling.  |例句：We're having a cookout at the park on Saturday. 我们星期六在公园举行烧烤派对。|例句：The family enjoyed a relaxing cookout by the lake.  家人在湖边享受了一次轻松的烧烤。|近义词：barbecue, picnic, grill |反义词：indoor party


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We have a cookout every summer. = 我们每年夏天都会举行一次烧烤。
They cook hot dogs at the cookout. = 他们在烧烤会上烤热狗。

一般过去时:
