circumferences:/sərˈkʌmfərənsɪz/| n. |周长，圆周；范围，外围 |The distance around a circle or any curved line.  It can also refer to the boundary or scope of something. |例句：The circumference of the Earth is approximately 40,000 kilometers. 地球的周长约为40000公里。

The debate covered a wide circumference of issues.  这场辩论涵盖了广泛的问题。|近义词：perimeter, periphery, scope |反义词：diameter, center
