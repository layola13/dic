inflicting:/ɪnˈflɪktɪŋ/| v. |施加；造成（痛苦、伤害等）|To cause something unpleasant or painful to be suffered.|The storm inflicted severe damage on the coastal towns.暴风雨给沿海城镇造成了严重的破坏。|近义词：causing, inflicting, dealing, administering|反义词：relieving, alleviating, mitigating|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is inflicting pain on himself. 他正在折磨自己。
They are inflicting unnecessary suffering on animals. 他们在对动物施加不必要的痛苦。

一般过去时:
The war inflicted great hardship on the people.  战争给人民带来了巨大的苦难。
She inflicted a serious wound on her opponent. 她给她的对手造成了严重的伤口。


现在完成时:
The government has inflicted significant economic hardship on the population. 政府已经给人民带来了严重的经济困难。
The drought has inflicted severe damage on the crops. 干旱对庄稼造成了严重的损害。


过去完成时:
They had inflicted heavy losses on the enemy before reinforcements arrived. 在增援到达之前，他们已经给敌人造成了重大损失。
She had inflicted a deep cut on her hand before realizing the danger.  在她意识到危险之前，她已经在手上割了一道很深的口子。


将来时:
The new policy will inflict further damage on the economy. 新政策将对经济造成进一步的损害。
He will inflict revenge on those who wronged him. 他将向那些冤枉他的人报复。


虚拟语气:
If he inflicted more damage, he would be arrested. 如果他造成更多破坏，他将被逮捕。
I wish he hadn't inflicted such pain on his family. 我希望他没有给他的家人带来如此痛苦。


被动语态:
Much suffering was inflicted on the refugees.  难民遭受了巨大的苦难。
Significant damage was inflicted on the building by the fire. 建筑物被大火严重损毁。


疑问句:
What kind of pain is he inflicting on himself? 他正在对自己施加什么样的痛苦？
What damage did the hurricane inflict on the city?  飓风给这座城市造成了什么破坏？


否定句:
He didn't inflict any harm on anyone. 他没有伤害任何人。
The accident didn't inflict any lasting damage on his car. 事故没有对他的车造成任何持久性损坏。
