ovist:/ˈoʊvɪst/ | n. | 卵生动物；卵生论者 | A person who believes that the female contributes the most important part to reproduction, believing that the ovum (egg) contains the entire future organism.  The term is now largely obsolete in biology, replaced by more precise terminology. | Example:  The ovist theory was once a prominent view in the field of embryology.  卵生论曾经是胚胎学领域中一种重要的观点。 | 近义词: ovulist (less common) | 反义词: spermist (historical, opposing view)
