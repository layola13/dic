nonreducible: /ˌnɒn.rɪˈdjuːsɪbl/ | adj. |不可约的，不可简化的 |  Incapable of being reduced or simplified further.  Something that is nonreducible cannot be broken down into smaller, simpler components or expressed in a more concise way.  This term is often used in mathematics, logic, and other fields dealing with complex systems or structures.


例句：

The problem proved to be nonreducible to a simpler form.  这个问题被证明无法简化为更简单的形式。

This mathematical equation is nonreducible, meaning it cannot be simplified further.  这个数学方程式是不可约的，这意味着它无法进一步简化。



近义词：irreducible, unreducible, indivisible, fundamental

反义词：reducible, simplifiable, decomposable


词性:  形容词
