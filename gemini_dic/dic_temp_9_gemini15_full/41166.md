copybooks:/ˈkɒpiˌbʊks/ | n. |习字簿；练习本 |A copybook is a type of notebook, typically used by children learning to write, containing examples of handwriting and spaces for practice.  |Example: My teacher checked my copybooks every week to ensure my handwriting was improving.  我的老师每周都会检查我的习字簿，以确保我的书法有所提高。|近义词：exercise book, writing book |反义词：None
