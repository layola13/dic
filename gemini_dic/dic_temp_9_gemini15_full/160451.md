populate:/ˈpɒpjuleɪt/ /ˈpɑːpjuleɪt/| vt.|使居住；填充；构成…的人口|to fill a place with people or animals; to be the people or animals that live in a particular place|例句：The island was populated by a small community of fishermen.  这个岛屿住着一个小渔民社区。

例句：They plan to populate the new housing development with young families.  他们计划让年轻家庭入住新的住宅区。


近义词：inhabit, colonize, settle|反义词：depopulate, evacuate
