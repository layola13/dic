coulter:/ˈkaʊltər/| n.|犁刀，切割器|A coulter is a knife-like blade attached to a plow or other agricultural implement, used to cut the soil before the plowshare.  It helps to make a clean furrow and reduce friction.|例句：The farmer adjusted the coulter on his plow to ensure a straight furrow.  这个农民调整了他犁上的犁刀以确保犁沟笔直。|近义词：cutter, blade|反义词：N/A
