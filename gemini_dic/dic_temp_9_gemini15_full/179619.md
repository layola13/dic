reenclosing: /ˌriːɪnˈkloʊzɪŋ/ | v. | 再封闭；重新装入信封 | The act of enclosing something again, typically after it has been opened or removed from its original enclosure.  This often refers to sending something by mail after it has been opened for inspection or amendment. | Example:  After reviewing the contract, we are reenclosing it for your signature.  = 在审查合同后，我们重新装入信封寄回给您签字。 | 近义词: resealing, re-enveloping | 反义词: opening, unsealing | 动词
