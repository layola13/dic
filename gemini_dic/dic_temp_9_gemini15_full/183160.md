repotting:/ˌriːˈpɑːtɪŋ/ | v. | 翻盆；重新栽种 | The act of removing a plant from its container and planting it in a new, larger container with fresh soil.  This is often done to give the plant more room to grow or to improve the soil condition. |例句：I'm repotting my orchids this weekend.  = 我这个周末要给我的兰花翻盆。


例句：It's time to repot the tomato plants; they've outgrown their pots. = 西红柿该翻盆了，它们已经长得超过花盆了。


近义词：replanting, transplanting


反义词：None (the opposite would be leaving a plant in the same pot)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I repot my plants every spring. = 我每年春天都给我的植物翻盆。
He repots his bonsai trees regularly. = 他定期给他的盆景翻盆。

一般过去时:
I repotted my fern last week. = 我上周给我的蕨类植物翻盆了。
She repotted all her houseplants yesterday. = 她昨天给所有的室内植物都翻盆了。

现在完成时:
I have repotted most of my succulents. = 我已经给大部分的多肉植物翻盆了。
They have repotted the large oak tree. = 他们已经给那棵大橡树换了盆。

过去完成时:
I had repotted all the flowers before the storm arrived.  =暴风雨来临之前，我已经把所有的花都翻盆了。
She had repotted the cactus before she left for vacation. = 她度假之前已经给仙人掌换了盆。


将来时:
I will repot my roses next month. = 我下个月将要给我的玫瑰花翻盆。
They will repot the hydrangeas in the fall. = 他们秋天会给绣球花翻盆。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would repot all my plants. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会把所有的植物都翻盆。
I wish I had repotted the orchids sooner. = 我真希望早点给兰花翻盆。

被动语态:
The plants were repotted by the gardener. = 植物是由园丁翻盆的。
The herbs are being repotted this afternoon. = 香草植物今天下午正在被翻盆。

疑问句:
Have you repotted your African violets yet? = 你已经给你的非洲紫罗兰翻盆了吗？
When will the plants be repotted? = 植物什么时候会被翻盆？

否定句:
I didn't repot my plants last year. = 我去年没有给我的植物翻盆。
They haven't repotted the bonsai tree yet. = 他们还没有给盆景翻盆。


PHRASE->

词源:
re- (prefix indicating again or anew) + potting (from pot + -ing, the gerund form of the verb "to pot," meaning to plant in a pot)
