platooning:/pləˈtuːnɪŋ/| n. |排队行驶；编队行驶|The act of vehicles, especially trucks, driving in a close formation, often aided by technology to improve fuel efficiency and safety.  This is commonly referred to as a platoon. |例句：Autonomous platooning is being tested on highways to reduce fuel consumption. 自动化排队行驶正在高速公路上进行测试，以减少燃油消耗。

例句：The truck platooning system uses radar and communication systems to maintain a safe distance between vehicles.卡车排队系统使用雷达和通信系统来保持车辆之间的安全距离。


近义词：convoying, caravanning |反义词：scattering, dispersing |词性：名词
