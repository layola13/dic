enshrouds: /ɪnˈʃraʊdz/ | vt. |笼罩；遮蔽；掩盖 | To cover or surround completely, especially in a mysterious or secretive way.  |例句：Mist enshrouds the mountaintops. 薄雾笼罩着山顶。


The ancient ruins were enshrouded in mystery. 古代遗址笼罩在神秘之中。|近义词：cover, conceal, shroud, envelop, veil |反义词：reveal, uncover, expose |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fog enshrouds the valley every morning.  = 雾每天早晨都笼罩着山谷。
He enshrouds his true feelings behind a mask of indifference. = 他用冷漠的面具掩盖了他真实的情感。


一般过去时:

She enshrouded herself in a large, warm blanket. = 她用一条又大又暖和的毯子裹住了自己。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

The truth had been enshrouded by lies and deception. = 真相被谎言和欺骗所掩盖。
