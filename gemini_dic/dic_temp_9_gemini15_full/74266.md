foxtrotted:/ˈfɑːkstrɑːtɪd/ | v. |狐步舞; 快速移动 | To move in a foxtrot; to move quickly and smoothly.  |例句：The dancers foxtrotted gracefully across the floor. 舞者优雅地在舞池中跳着狐步舞。 |例句：He foxtrotted through the crowd to reach the stage. 他快速穿过人群到达舞台。|近义词：danced, hurried, sped |反义词：stumbled, shuffled, crawled
