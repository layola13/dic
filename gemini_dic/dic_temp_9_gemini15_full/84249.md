half-smilingly:/ˈhæf ˈsmaɪlɪŋli/| adv.|半笑着地，带着一丝微笑地|The adverb "half-smilingly" describes an action performed with a slight or partial smile.  It suggests a hesitant or ambiguous smile, not a full, genuine smile. |例句：She looked at him half-smilingly, unsure of how to proceed. 她半笑着看着他，不知道该如何继续。

She responded half-smilingly to his joke. 她对他开玩笑半笑着回应。|近义词：with a slight smile, faintly smiling,  amusedly |反义词：frowningly, grimly, sadly
