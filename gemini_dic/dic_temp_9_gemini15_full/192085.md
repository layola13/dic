scoured:/skaʊərd/ | v. | 擦洗；彻底搜查；搜寻 | To rub or scrub hard; to search thoroughly.  |例句：She scoured the pan until it shone. 她用力擦洗平底锅直到它闪闪发光。

例句：The police scoured the area for clues. 警方彻底搜查了该区域寻找线索。|近义词：scrubbed, cleaned, searched, investigated |反义词：left untouched, overlooked


USAGE->
