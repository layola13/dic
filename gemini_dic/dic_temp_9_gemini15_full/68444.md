/fæm/ | n. | 家庭；家族 | A family is a group of people related by blood or marriage.  A family can also refer to a group of people living together. |例句：My family is very important to me. 我的家人对我来说非常重要。|例句：She comes from a large, close-knit family. 她来自一个大家庭，家庭成员关系密切。|近义词：household, clan, kin |反义词: none


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My family lives in London. = 我的家人住在伦敦。
His family owns a large farm. = 他家拥有一个大型农场。

一般过去时:
My family went on vacation last summer. = 我的家人去年夏天去度假了。
Her family lived in the countryside. = 她家以前住在农村。

现在完成时:
My family has lived here for ten years. = 我的家人在这里住了十年了。
His family has traveled extensively. = 他家旅行了很多地方。

过去完成时:
My family had already eaten dinner when he arrived. = 他到的时候，我的家人已经吃过晚饭了。
Her family had lived in that house for generations. = 她家世世代代都住在那所房子里。

将来时:
My family will visit you next month. = 我的家人下个月会去看你。
His family will move to a new house soon. = 他家很快就会搬到新房子里。

虚拟语气:
If my family were here, I would be happier. = 如果我的家人在这里，我会更快乐。
I wish my family lived closer. = 我希望我的家人住得近一点。

被动语态:
The family was surprised by the news. = 这个家庭被这个消息惊呆了。
Family photos were displayed at the wedding. = 婚礼上展示了家庭照片。

疑问句:
Do you have a big family? = 你家是一个大家庭吗？
What does your family do for a living? = 你家靠什么谋生？

否定句:
My family doesn't have a car. = 我家没有车。
Her family didn't attend the ceremony. = 她家没有参加典礼。
