reverified:/ˌriːˈvɛrɪfaɪd/| adj. | 再次验证的；重新验证过的 | Having been verified again.  It implies a previous verification process and a subsequent confirmation of its accuracy or validity. | 例句：The information was reverified by three independent sources.  信息已由三个独立的消息来源再次核实。 The reverified data showed a significant error in the initial findings. 重新验证的数据显示初始结果存在重大错误。|近义词：confirmed, checked, double-checked, corroborated |反义词：unverified, disproven, refuted |形容词
