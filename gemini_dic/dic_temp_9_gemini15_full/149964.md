palmaceous: /pælˈmeɪʃəs/ | adj. |棕榈状的；似棕榈的| Relating to or characteristic of palms.  The term often describes plants with palm-like leaves, even if they are not true palms.  |例句：The palmaceous fronds of the cycad created a tropical ambiance in the garden.  棕榈状的苏铁叶片营造了花园里的热带氛围。

The plant had large, palmaceous leaves, resembling those of a palm tree but belonging to a different family. 这株植物有着巨大的棕榈状叶子，类似于棕榈树的叶子，但属于不同的科。


近义词：palm-like, frondose


反义词：无  (反义词需根据语境而定，例如，如果用来描述叶子形状，可以考虑与针叶状的相对)


词源：源自拉丁语"palma" (棕榈树) + "-aceous" (形容词后缀，表示“类似于…的”)
