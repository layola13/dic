nonmajorities:/ˌnɒnmeɪˈdʒɒrɪtiz/ | n. | 非多数派；少数群体 | Members of a society or group who do not belong to the majority.  This term often refers to groups that are marginalized or underrepresented.  | Example: The nonmajorities in this country face significant challenges in accessing healthcare.  这个国家的少数民族在获得医疗保健方面面临重大挑战。 |近义词：minorities, underrepresented groups | 反义词：majorities
