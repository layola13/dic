overshoes:/ˈoʊvərʃuz/| n. |套鞋，雨鞋|Protective coverings worn over shoes to keep them clean or dry. Overshoes are typically made of rubber or plastic and are designed to be easily slipped on and off.|例句：I wore my overshoes to keep my shoes dry in the rain. 我穿着套鞋，以免雨天鞋子弄湿。|例句：She put on her overshoes before entering the muddy construction site.  她在进入泥泞的建筑工地之前穿上了套鞋。|近义词：galoshes, waterproof footwear|反义词：None.
