haggled:/ˈhæɡld/| v.|讨价还价，争论，拉扯|To argue about the price of something, especially in a market or shop; to argue persistently and aggressively.|例句：We haggled over the price of the carpet for half an hour. 我们就地毯的价格讨价还价了半个小时。|例句：They haggled for days before reaching an agreement. 他们在达成协议之前争论了好几天。|近义词：bargain, negotiate, dispute|反义词：accept, concede


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They haggle over the price of fish at the market. = 他们在市场上讨价还价鱼的价格。
He always haggles with the vendors. = 他总是和摊贩讨价还价。

一般过去时:
We haggled for hours before finally agreeing on a price. = 我们讨价还价了好几个小时才最终商定了一个价格。
She haggled with the shopkeeper about the quality of the goods. = 她与店主就商品的质量讨价还价。

现在完成时:
I have haggled my way to a better deal on several occasions. = 我曾多次通过讨价还价获得了更好的交易。
They have haggled with the contractor over the terms of the contract. = 他们一直在与承包商就合同条款进行讨价还价。

过去完成时:
They had haggled for days, but still couldn't reach an agreement. = 他们已经讨价还价了好几天，但仍然无法达成协议。
We had haggled over the details before signing the contract. = 我们在签署合同之前已经就细节进行了讨价还价。

将来时:
I will haggle with the seller until I get the best price. = 我会和卖家讨价还价，直到得到最好的价格。
We will haggle over the terms of the deal next week. = 我们下周将就交易条款进行讨价还价。

虚拟语气:
If I had haggled harder, I could have gotten a lower price. = 如果我当时更努力地讨价还价，我就能得到更低的价格。
I wish I had haggled more aggressively at the market. = 我真希望我在市场上更强势地讨价还价。

被动语态:
The price was haggled down considerably. = 价格被大幅度地砍价了。
The terms of the contract were being haggled over by both parties. = 合同条款正被双方讨价还价。

疑问句:
Do you usually haggle when you shop? = 你购物时通常会讨价还价吗？
Will they haggle over the delivery date? = 他们会就交货日期讨价还价吗？

否定句:
I didn't haggle over the price; I just bought it. = 我没有讨价还价价格；我只是买了它。
They didn't haggle at all; they accepted the first price offered. = 他们根本没有讨价还价；他们接受了第一个提出的价格。
