exclaims:/ɪkˈskleɪmz/| vt.|惊呼；大声说|To cry out or speak suddenly and vehemently.|She exclaimed in surprise when she saw the gift.|近义词：shout, cry out, interject|反义词：whisper, murmur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exclaims with joy. = 他高兴地惊呼。
She exclaims her agreement. = 她大声表示同意。

一般过去时:
He exclaimed in anger. = 他生气地喊叫。
She exclaimed that she was tired. = 她大声说她累了。

现在完成时:
He has exclaimed his love for her many times. = 他已经多次表达了他对她的爱。
She has exclaimed her innocence repeatedly. = 她一再大声宣称自己的清白。

过去完成时:
He had exclaimed his surprise before he saw the truth. = 在他看到真相之前，他已经惊呼表示惊讶。
She had exclaimed her disappointment before the results were announced. = 在结果公布之前，她就已大声表达了她的失望。

将来时:
He will exclaim with delight when he hears the news. = 当他听到这个消息时，他会高兴地惊呼。
She will exclaim her gratitude to everyone. = 她会向每个人表达她的感激之情。

虚拟语气:
If he saw it, he would exclaim in astonishment. = 如果他看到了，他会惊讶地惊呼。
If she knew, she would exclaim her relief. = 如果她知道，她会如释重负地惊呼。

被动语态:
His surprise was exclaimed by everyone. = 他的惊讶被每个人都喊了出来。(此用法较少见)
Her happiness was exclaimed throughout the town. = 她的快乐传遍了整个城镇。(此用法侧重于消息的传播)


疑问句:
(Exclaims本身并非疑问句的结构，无法直接构成疑问句)


否定句:
He did not exclaim. = 他没有惊呼。
She didn't exclaim anything. = 她什么也没喊。
