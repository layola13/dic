exhilarant: /ɪɡˈzɪlərent/ | adj. |令人兴奋的；令人振奋的 |  Feeling or causing a feeling of great excitement and happiness.  | 例句：The exhilarating news filled everyone with joy.  令人振奋的消息让每个人都充满了喜悦。

The exhilarating ride left us breathless.  令人兴奋的骑行让我们喘不过气来。


近义词：thrilling, stimulating, exciting, invigorating | 反义词：depressing, dull, boring, monotonous

词源:  From Middle English *exhilarant, from Latin *exhilarāns, present participle of exhilarāre "to make cheerful, enliven," from ex- "out, from" + hilaris "cheerful, merry."  The word ultimately derives from the Latin word "hilāris," meaning "cheerful" or "merry."
