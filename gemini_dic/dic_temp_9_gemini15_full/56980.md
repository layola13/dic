double-clicks: /ˈdʌbəl klɪks/ | v. | 双击 | To click a mouse button or other input device twice in quick succession. This action typically selects an item, opens a file, or performs another function depending on the software and context. | 例句：Double-click the icon to open the program. 双击图标以打开程序。


例句：She double-clicked the file to view it. 她双击文件以查看它。


近义词：click twice,  repeatedly click


反义词：single-click


词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He double-clicks the icon every time. 他每次都双击图标。
She double-clicks to select items. 她双击以选择项目。


一般过去时:
I double-clicked the document to edit it. 我双击了文档以编辑它。
They double-clicked the link to visit the website. 他们双击链接以访问该网站。


现在完成时:
He has double-clicked the mouse many times today. 他今天已经多次双击鼠标了。
She has double-clicked on every image in the folder. 她已经双击了文件夹中的每一张图片。


过去完成时:
I had double-clicked the application before the system crashed. 系统崩溃之前，我已经双击了该应用程序。
They had double-clicked the file before realizing it was corrupted. 他们在意识到文件已损坏之前已经双击了它。


将来时:
I will double-click the button to submit the form. 我将双击按钮以提交表单。
She will double-click the link to download the file. 她将双击链接以下载文件。


虚拟语气:
If I double-clicked the icon, it would open the file. 如果我双击图标，它就会打开文件。
I wish I had double-clicked the save button. 我希望我双击了保存按钮。


被动语态:
The icon was double-clicked by the user. 该图标被用户双击了。
The file should be double-clicked to open. 该文件应该双击打开。


疑问句:
Did you double-click the mouse button? 你双击了鼠标按钮吗？
Should I double-click this button? 我应该双击这个按钮吗？


否定句:
He didn't double-click the link. 他没有双击链接。
She does not double-click the icons, she uses the keyboard shortcuts. 她不双击图标，她使用键盘快捷键。
