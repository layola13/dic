shrieker:/ˈʃriːkər/| n. |尖叫者，发出尖锐声音的人或物|A shrieker is a person or thing that shrieks or makes a loud, high-pitched cry.  It often implies a sense of distress, fear, or anger.|例句：The shrieker in the horror movie was truly terrifying.  恐怖电影里的尖叫声真可怕。  The child, a tiny shrieker, wouldn't stop crying.  那个小孩，一个小小的尖叫者，哭个不停。|近义词：screamer, howler, yeller|反义词：whispurer, mutterer
