foreadvise:/fɔːrədˈvaɪz/ | v. |预先通知，预先告知 | To inform or warn in advance.  |例句：We foreadvised them of the potential dangers. 我们预先告知他们潜在的危险。|例句：The company foreadvised its clients of the upcoming price increase. 公司预先通知客户即将涨价。|近义词：pre-notify, warn, inform in advance |反义词：misinform, keep secret
