intersphere:/ˈɪntəsfɪər/| n. |中间层，中间球体|A hypothetical region or layer between two distinct spheres or layers, often used in science fiction contexts to describe a space between dimensions or realities.  It lacks a widely accepted scientific definition and is largely used in fictional settings.  |Example: The spaceship traversed the intersphere, a shimmering veil between our reality and another.  宇宙飞船穿过了中间层，这是我们现实与另一个现实之间的闪烁面纱。|近义词：interstice, intermediary zone|反义词：interior, exterior|名词
