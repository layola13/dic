olla: /ˈɒlə/ | n. |大锅，瓦罐 | A large, usually earthenware, pot used for cooking.  |例句：She simmered the stew in a large olla for hours. 她用一个大瓦罐炖了几个小时的炖菜。|近义词：pot, cauldron, kettle |反义词：small container, bowl

PHRASE->
olla podrida (Spanish):  a type of Spanish stew. (西班牙炖菜)
earthenware olla:  a clay olla. (陶土瓦罐)
Spanish olla:  a traditional Spanish cooking pot. （西班牙传统瓦罐）
olla de barro: (Spanish) clay pot 瓦罐(西班牙语)
broken olla: 破瓦罐


词源:
The word "olla" comes from the Latin word "olla," meaning "pot" or "pan."  It has spread through various Romance languages, retaining its basic meaning.
