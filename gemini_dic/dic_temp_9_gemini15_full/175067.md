radiomicrometer:/ˌreɪdiəʊmaɪˈkrɒmɪtər/ | n. |辐射测微计|An instrument used to measure the intensity of radiation, especially radio waves.  It combines the functions of a radiometer and a micrometer, allowing for precise measurements of radiation at specific wavelengths or frequencies.|Example: The scientist used a radiomicrometer to carefully measure the intensity of the radio waves emitted by the distant galaxy.|近义词：radiometer (less precise)|反义词：无
