resenting: /rɪˈzɛntɪŋ/ | v. | 怨恨；憎恶；不满 |  To feel bitterness or indignation at (a circumstance, action, or person).  |例句：He resented her interference in his affairs. 他怨恨她干涉他的事情。


例句：She was resenting her brother's success. 她嫉妒她哥哥的成功。


近义词：disliking, begrudging, chafing under,  objecting to


反义词：appreciating, enjoying, welcoming


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am resenting his constant criticism. = 我对他不断的批评感到不满。
They are resenting the new rules. = 他们对新规感到不满。

一般过去时:
I resented his behavior. = 我讨厌他的行为。
She resented being ignored. = 她讨厌被忽视。

现在完成时:
I have been resenting his actions for years. = 多年来我一直对他那些行为感到不满。
She has resented his promotion ever since. = 从那以后她就一直对他升职感到不满。

过去完成时:
I had resented his interference before he even apologized. = 在他道歉之前，我就已经对他干涉我的事感到不满。
They had resented the long hours before the company offered overtime pay. = 在公司提供加班费之前，他们就一直对长时间工作不满。

将来时:
I will resent any attempt to control me. = 我将对任何试图控制我的企图感到不满。
They will resent having their privacy invaded. = 他们将对侵犯他们的隐私感到不满。

虚拟语气:
I would resent it if you told my secrets. = 如果你泄露我的秘密，我会生气的。
She would have resented the delay even more if she had known the reason. = 如果她知道原因，她会更加怨恨这延误。

被动语态:
His interference was resented by everyone. = 他的干涉受到了所有人的不满。
The new policy is being resented by many employees. = 许多员工对这项新政策感到不满。

疑问句:
Are you resenting your workload? = 你对你的工作量感到不满吗？
Does he resent my success? = 他嫉妒我的成功吗？

否定句:
I don't resent your help. = 我不讨厌你的帮助。
She doesn't resent his advice. = 她并不讨厌他的建议。


PHRASE->
resent doing something:  怨恨做某事
resent someone's actions: 怨恨某人的行为
resent being treated unfairly: 怨恨被不公平对待
resent the fact that: 怨恨...这个事实
resent someone's interference: 怨恨某人的干涉
