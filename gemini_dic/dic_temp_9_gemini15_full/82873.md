gulliver:/ˈɡʌlɪvər/| n. |格列佛；巨人|The name of the protagonist in Jonathan Swift's satirical novel "Gulliver's Travels".  It is often used to refer to someone very tall or someone who is naive and easily fooled.|例句：He's a real Gulliver, towering over everyone else. 他真是个巨人，比其他人高得多。|近义词：giant, colossus |反义词：dwarf, midget
