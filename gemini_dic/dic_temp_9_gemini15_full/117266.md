mechanises: /ˈmɛkənaɪzɪz/ | v. | 机械化；使机械化 | To make (something) mechanical or automatic; to introduce machines into (a process, industry, etc.).  | The factory mechanised its production line to increase efficiency.  工厂将其生产线机械化以提高效率。  | automate, motorize | dehumanize (in certain contexts) | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mechanise the process to save labor. = 他们使这个流程机械化以节省劳动力。
The company mechanises its operations gradually. = 公司逐渐将其业务机械化。


一般过去时:
The farmer mechanised his farm last year. = 农民去年将他的农场机械化了。
They mechanised the assembly line in the 1980s. = 他们在20世纪80年代将装配线机械化了。


现在完成时:
They have mechanised many aspects of their business. = 他们已经将业务的许多方面机械化了。
The factory has mechanised its production process completely. = 该工厂已完全机械化其生产流程。


过去完成时:
They had mechanised the factory before the strike. = 罢工前他们已经将工厂机械化了。
The company had mechanised its operations long before the competition. = 公司早在竞争对手之前就已将其业务机械化了。


将来时:
They will mechanise the entire operation next year. = 他们明年将使整个运作机械化。
The government will mechanise more aspects of agriculture in the future. = 政府将来会使农业的更多方面机械化。


虚拟语气:
If they mechanised the process, they would save a lot of money. = 如果他们将流程机械化，他们将节省大量资金。
I wish they had mechanised the system earlier. = 我真希望他们早点将系统机械化。


被动语态:
The process was mechanised to improve efficiency. = 流程被机械化以提高效率。
The farm was mechanised with new equipment. = 该农场使用新设备进行了机械化改造。


疑问句:
Have they mechanised the factory yet? = 他们已经将工厂机械化了吗？
Will the company mechanise its production line soon? = 公司很快就会将其生产线机械化吗？


否定句:
They did not mechanise the process. = 他们没有使该流程机械化。
The company has not yet mechanised all of its operations. = 公司尚未将其所有业务都机械化。
