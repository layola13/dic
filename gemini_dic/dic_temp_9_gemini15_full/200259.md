skits:/skɪts/| n. |短剧；滑稽剧；玩笑话 |A skit is a short, humorous play, often performed informally.  Skits are typically lighthearted and comedic, focusing on a single situation or idea. They can be improvised or written, and often involve audience participation.|例句：The students put on a skit for the school talent show. 学生们为学校才艺表演准备了一出短剧。

The children acted out a funny skit about a talking dog. 孩子们表演了一个关于会说话的狗的滑稽短剧。|近义词：sketch, short play, act |反义词：long play, drama
