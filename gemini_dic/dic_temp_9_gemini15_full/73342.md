forefended:/fɔːrˈfɛndɪd/| adj.|被禁止的，被阻止的|Forbidden; prevented.  The word implies a strong prohibition, often by authority.|例句：The ancient law forefended the eating of certain foods during holy periods. = 古代法律禁止在神圣时期食用某些食物。

例句：Access to the restricted area was strictly forefended. = 进入限制区域是被严格禁止的。


近义词：prohibited, forbidden, disallowed, interdicted|反义词：permitted, allowed, authorized|词性：形容词
