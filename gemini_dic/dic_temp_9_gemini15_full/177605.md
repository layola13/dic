rebuild:/ˌriːˈbɪld/ | vt. |重建，改建 |To build something again after it has been destroyed or damaged.  It implies a complete or substantial reconstruction, rather than simply repairing minor damage. |例句：They are rebuilding the bridge after the flood. 洪水过后，他们正在重建桥梁。

例句：The city is slowly rebuilding after the earthquake. 地震后，城市正在缓慢重建。


近义词：reconstruct, restore, renovate | 反义词：destroy, demolish, raze | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rebuild houses after natural disasters. = 他们在自然灾害后重建房屋。
We rebuild our website every year. = 我们每年都重建我们的网站。


一般过去时:
They rebuilt the school after the fire. = 火灾后他们重建了学校。
The company rebuilt its image after the scandal. = 丑闻之后，该公司重建了其形象。


现在完成时:
They have rebuilt the damaged section of the highway. = 他们已经重建了损坏的高速公路路段。
We have rebuilt our trust in each other. = 我们已经重建了彼此之间的信任。


过去完成时:
They had rebuilt the church before the anniversary. = 他们在周年纪念日之前已经重建了教堂。
The government had rebuilt the infrastructure before the next storm season. = 政府在下个风暴季节来临之前已经重建了基础设施。


将来时:
They will rebuild the town after the hurricane. = 飓风过后，他们将重建小镇。
The team will rebuild the engine next week. = 团队下周将重建发动机。


虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would rebuild the old house. = 如果我们有更多钱，我们会重建旧房子。
It is important that they rebuild their lives after the tragedy. = 重要的是他们在悲剧之后重建他们的生活。


被动语态:
The bridge is being rebuilt at the moment. = 桥梁正在重建中。
The old factory will be rebuilt next year. = 旧工厂明年将被重建。


疑问句:
Are they rebuilding the stadium? = 他们正在重建体育馆吗？
Will the city be rebuilt quickly? = 城市会很快重建吗？


否定句:
They did not rebuild the damaged building. = 他们没有重建受损的建筑物。
We cannot rebuild everything overnight. = 我们不可能一夜之间重建一切。


PHRASE->
rebuild confidence = 重建信心
rebuild trust = 重建信任
rebuild relationships = 重建关系
rebuild a team = 重建一个团队
rebuild a life = 重建生活
