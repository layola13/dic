reflexivity:/ˌriːflɛkˈsɪvɪti/ | n. | 自反性；反身性 | The quality or state of being reflexive; the property of a relation or operation that relates an element to itself.  It refers to the ability of something to act upon itself or refer to itself. This concept applies in various fields, including linguistics, mathematics, and social sciences. | Example: The reflexivity of the pronoun "myself" is evident in the sentence "I hurt myself."  The sentence demonstrates the action affecting the subject. | 近义词：self-reference, self-reflection | 反义词：irreflexivity


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The water reflects its surroundings. = 水反映了它的周围环境。
His actions reflect his true character. = 他的行为反映了他的真实性格。


一般过去时:
The mirror reflected my image. = 镜子反射了我的影像。
Her words reflected her deep sadness. = 她的言语反映了她深深的悲伤。


现在完成时:
The research has reflected a significant change in public opinion. = 这项研究反映了公众舆论的重大变化。
His success has reflected years of hard work. = 他的成功反映了他多年的努力。


过去完成时:
The data reflected what had already been suspected. = 数据反映了之前已经怀疑的事情。
The results reflected what had been predicted in earlier studies. = 结果反映了早期研究中预测的内容。


将来时:
The survey will reflect the current economic climate. = 调查将反映当前的经济形势。
The upcoming election results will reflect the people's will. = 即将到来的选举结果将反映民意。


虚拟语气:
If the results reflected the reality, we would have acted differently. = 如果结果反映了现实，我们会采取不同的行动。
I wish my work reflected my true potential. = 我希望我的工作能够反映出我的真正潜力。


被动语态:
The problem was reflected in the low test scores. = 问题反映在低下的考试成绩中。
The company's financial difficulties were reflected in its stock price. = 公司的财务困难反映在其股价上。


疑问句:
Does the data accurately reflect the situation? = 数据是否准确地反映了情况？
How does this painting reflect the artist's emotions? = 这幅画如何反映艺术家的情感？


否定句:
The statistics do not reflect the true level of poverty. = 统计数据并没有反映出真实的贫困程度。
His behavior did not reflect his usual politeness. = 他的行为没有反映出他通常的礼貌。
