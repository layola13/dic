oligarchic:/ˌɒlɪˈɡɑːkɪk/| adj.|寡头政治的；寡头统治的| Relating to or characteristic of an oligarchy; ruled or controlled by a small group of people.  | Example: The country's oligarchic system stifled dissent and limited political participation. 这个国家的寡头政治体系压制了异见并限制了政治参与。  |近义词：plutocratic, aristocratic |反义词：democratic, egalitarian
