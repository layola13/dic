foreign:/ˈfɒrɪn/ /ˈfɔːrən/| adj.|外国的；外来的；异国的|Of, from, or characteristic of a country or language other than one's own.|The restaurant serves foreign cuisine.|这家餐馆供应外国菜肴。|He has a strong foreign accent.|他有一个浓重的外国口音。|近义词：alien, exotic, overseas|反义词：domestic, native


USAGE->
一般现在时:
That company imports foreign goods. = 那家公司进口外国商品。
She speaks several foreign languages. = 她会说几种外语。


一般过去时:
He lived in a foreign country for many years. = 他在一个外国住了很多年。
They studied foreign literature in college. = 他们在大学学习外国文学。


现在完成时:
She has traveled to many foreign lands. = 她去过许多外国。
We have learned a lot about foreign cultures. = 我们已经了解了很多关于外国文化的知识。


过去完成时:
I had never visited a foreign country before. = 我以前从未去过外国。
They had completed their studies of foreign languages. = 他们已经完成了外语学习。


将来时:
He will visit several foreign countries next year. = 他明年将访问几个国家。
She will study foreign policy in graduate school. = 她将在研究生院学习外交政策。


虚拟语气:
If I spoke fluent foreign languages, I would travel more. = 如果我会说流利的外国语，我会旅行更多。
I wish I had studied more foreign languages in school. = 我希望我在学校学过更多外语。


被动语态:

疑问句:
