retentions:/rɪˈtɛnʃənz/ | n. | 保留；保持；记忆力 | The act of retaining something; the state of being retained.  It can refer to keeping something, holding onto something, or the ability to remember something.  | 例句：The company's high employee retention rate is a testament to its positive work environment.  公司的员工高留存率证明了其积极的工作环境。

The high water retention of the soil is crucial for successful agriculture.土壤的高保水性对农业成功至关重要。


近义词：preservation, conservation, memory, recall | 反义词：loss, release, forgetfulness | 名词
