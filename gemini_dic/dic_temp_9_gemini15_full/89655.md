hooky:/ˈhʊki/| n. v.|逃学，逃课；偷懒|To avoid a duty or responsibility, especially school or work.  It can refer to the act of playing hooky or to the absence itself.|例句：The kids played hooky and went to the beach. 孩子们逃学去海滩了。|例句：His hooky from work cost him a promotion. 他逃班让他失去了晋升机会。|近义词：truancy, absence, skipping|反义词：attendance, presence


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He plays hooky every Friday. = 他每个星期五都逃学。
She often plays hooky from work. = 她经常逃班。

一般过去时:
They played hooky yesterday. = 他们昨天逃学了。
He played hooky from his boring meeting. = 他逃了他那无聊的会议。


现在完成时:
I have never played hooky in my life. = 我一生中从未逃过学。
She has played hooky several times this month. = 这个月她已经逃学好几次了。


过去完成时:
He had played hooky so many times that he was expelled. = 他逃学太多次，最终被开除了。
They had played hooky before the teacher noticed. = 老师注意到之前，他们就已经逃学了。


将来时:

She will likely play hooky again next week. = 她下周很可能会再次逃学。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
I didn't play hooky. = 我没有逃学。
She doesn't play hooky often. = 她不经常逃学。
