et:/et/| conj.|和，以及|连接词，用于连接两个词、短语或句子，表示并列关系。|He likes apples and et oranges.|近义词：and, also|反义词：but, however


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He eats apples et oranges. = 他吃苹果和橘子。
She speaks English et French. = 她讲英语和法语。

一般过去时:
They went to the park et the zoo. = 他们去了公园和动物园。
He bought a car et a house. = 他买了一辆车和一栋房子。

现在完成时:
I have read this book et that one. = 我读了这本书和那本书。
She has visited London et Paris. = 她参观了伦敦和巴黎。

过去完成时:
He had finished his work et gone home. = 他完成了他的工作然后回家了。
They had eaten dinner et watched a movie. = 他们吃了晚饭然后看了一部电影。

将来时:
We will go to the beach et the mountains. = 我们将去海滩和山区。
She will study math et science. = 她将学习数学和科学。

虚拟语气:
If I had the time et the money, I would travel. = 如果我有时间和金钱，我会去旅行。
I wish I had more patience et understanding. = 我希望我有更多的耐心和理解。

被动语态:
The cake et the cookies were eaten. = 蛋糕和饼干都被吃掉了。
The report et the presentation were completed. = 报告和演示文稿都完成了。

疑问句:
Do you prefer tea et coffee? = 你更喜欢茶还是咖啡？
Did he choose the red one et the blue one? = 他选了红色那个还是蓝色那个？

否定句:
He doesn't like broccoli et cauliflower. = 他不喜欢西兰花和花椰菜。
She didn't buy the shoes et the bag. = 她没有买鞋子和包。


PHRASE->
et al. = and others (等等)
et cetera = and so on (等等)
give et take = 大致上
now et then = 偶尔
pace et al. = 按步就班
