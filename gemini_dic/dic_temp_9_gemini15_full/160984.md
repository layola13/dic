postamputation:/ˌpoʊstæmpjuˈteɪʃən/| n. |截肢后状态；截肢术后 |The period following an amputation;  the state of being post-amputation.|例句：Postamputation rehabilitation is crucial for regaining function and independence. 截肢术后康复对于恢复功能和独立性至关重要。

例句：The patient experienced phantom limb pain in the postamputation phase.  患者在截肢后阶段经历了幻肢痛。


近义词：postoperative (regarding surgery in general),  post-surgical


反义词：preamputation (before amputation)


词性：名词
