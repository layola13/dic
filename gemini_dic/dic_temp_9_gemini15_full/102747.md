jansenist:/ˈdʒænsənɪst/| n. adj.|杨森主义者；杨森主义的|A follower or advocate of Jansenism, a theological movement within Catholicism emphasizing human depravity and the absolute sovereignty of God's grace.  Jansenists were often accused of heresy due to their strict interpretations of Augustine's teachings.|The Jansenists were known for their austere lifestyle and emphasis on inner piety.|近义词：Catholic (in a specific historical context)|反义词：Jesuit (in a specific historical context)|名词，形容词
