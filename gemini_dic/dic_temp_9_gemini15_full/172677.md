pyxidium:/pɪkˈsɪdiəm/| n. |盖裂蒴果|A pyxidium is a type of dry fruit that opens by a transverse or circumscissile dehiscence, meaning it splits horizontally across the middle to release its seeds.  It's a capsule that opens by a lid.  This is in contrast to other types of capsules that may split along sutures or valves.|Example: The plantain produces a pyxidium fruit containing numerous small seeds.  车前草产生一个盖裂蒴果，其中含有许多细小的种子。|近义词：capsule (more general term)|反义词：berry, drupe (fleshy fruits)|名词
