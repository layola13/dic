divergent:/daɪˈvɜːdʒənt/| adj. |发散的；不同的；分歧的|Differing from each other or from a standard; deviating.|The two roads diverged at the crossroads. 这两条路在十字路口分岔了。|convergent, similar|convergent, uniform|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The opinions of the committee members diverge on this issue.  委员会成员在这个问题上的意见存在分歧。
His views diverge significantly from the party line. 他的观点与党的路线大相径庭。


一般过去时:
Their paths diverged after college. 他们大学毕业后各奔东西。
The river diverged into two smaller streams. 河流分成了两条较小的溪流。


现在完成时:
Our interests have diverged over the years.  多年来，我们的兴趣已经变得不同了。
Their opinions have diverged considerably since the last meeting. 自上次会议以来，他们的意见已大相径庭。


过去完成时:
By the time they reached the crossroads, their paths had already diverged. 当他们到达十字路口时，他们的道路早已分开了。
It had diverged from the original plan. 它已经偏离了最初的计划。


将来时:
Their careers will likely diverge in the future.  他们的职业未来可能会走向不同的方向。
The roads will diverge sharply ahead.  前面的道路将急剧分开。


虚拟语气:
If their paths had not diverged, they might have remained friends. 如果他们的道路没有分开，他们可能仍然是朋友。
I wish my career path hadn't diverged so drastically. 我希望我的职业道路不会如此大的偏离。


被动语态:
The two groups were found to have diverged genetically. 发现这两个群体在基因上存在差异。
The project's goals have been significantly diverged from the original intent. 项目的目标已经与最初的意图大相径庭。


疑问句:
Do your views diverge from the majority opinion? 你的观点与大多数人的意见相左吗？

否定句:
Their opinions did not diverge on this matter.  他们在这一问题上意见没有分歧。
The two theories do not diverge greatly. 这两个理论并没有很大的差异。
