intuned:/ɪnˈtjuːnd/ | adj. | 协调的；和谐的；合拍的 | In tune with; in agreement or harmony.  |  The band was perfectly intuned, creating a beautiful symphony. 乐队演奏得非常协调，创造出一首优美的交响乐。The two companies are intuned on their marketing strategies. 两家公司在市场营销策略上非常协调。|近义词：harmonious, synchronized, coordinated | 反义词：discordant, disharmonious
