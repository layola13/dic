creams:/kriːmz/| n.|乳霜；奶油；涂抹|Creams are used to moisturize the skin. 乳霜用于滋润皮肤。|近义词：lotion, ointment|反义词：dryness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chef creams the butter and sugar together. = 厨师将黄油和糖一起打发。
She creams her face every night. = 她每晚都涂抹面霜。


一般过去时:
He creamed the potatoes until they were smooth. = 他把土豆搅打至顺滑。
I creamed my face after showering. = 洗完澡后我涂了面霜。


现在完成时:

They have creamed the butter for the cake. = 他们已经为蛋糕打发黄油了。


过去完成时:
