mycothrix:/maɪˈkɒθrɪks/ | n. |分枝杆菌属|A genus of bacteria, characterized by branching filaments, belonging to the Actinomycetales order.  Mycothrix are often found in soil and are sometimes associated with disease. The genus includes species like *Mycobacterium tuberculosis*, the causative agent of tuberculosis.|Example:  Some species of *Mycothrix* are pathogenic to humans.|近义词:Actinomyces (部分物种)|反义词:None|名词
