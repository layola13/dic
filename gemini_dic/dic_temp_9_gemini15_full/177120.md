reapprehended:/ˌriːæp.rɪˈhɛndɪd/| v.|重新逮捕，再次理解|To apprehend again; to arrest someone a second time.  It can also mean to understand something again or more fully after initial misunderstanding.|例句：The escaped prisoner was reapprehended near the border.  逃犯在边境附近再次被捕。

The suspect's confusing alibi was reapprehended by the detective, leading to his confession. 侦探重新理解了嫌疑人令人困惑的不在场证明，这导致了他认罪。|近义词：rearrest, recapture, re-arrest, understand again|反义词：release, liberate
