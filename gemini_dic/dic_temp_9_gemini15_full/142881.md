out-of-square:/ˌaʊt əvˈskwer/| adj.|不方正的；不正派的；不协调的|Not aligned or arranged at right angles; not level or straight; unconventional or unorthodox.  It can refer to physical objects or abstract concepts.|例句：The picture frame is slightly out-of-square. = 画框有点歪。
The artist's unconventional style was considered out-of-square by some critics. =  这位艺术家的非传统风格被一些评论家认为是不正派的。|近义词：crooked, askew, uneven, unconventional, unorthodox|反义词：square, straight, aligned, conventional, orthodox


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, tense variations don't apply.  Its usage revolves around describing a state of being.


PHRASE->
out of square with = 与...不一致
out of square on =  在...方面不方正
slightly out of square = 稍微有点歪
completely out of square = 完全不方正
put something out of square = 使某物不方正
