floodplain:/ˈflʌdpleɪn/| n. |洪泛平原，河漫滩|A floodplain is a relatively flat area of land next to a river or stream that is subject to flooding.  It is formed by the deposition of sediment during floods.  The fertile soil of floodplains makes them ideal for agriculture. |例句：The fertile floodplain of the Nile River supported ancient Egyptian civilization. 尼罗河肥沃的洪泛平原支撑着古埃及文明。

The annual flooding of the Mississippi River replenishes the nutrients in its floodplain.密西西比河的年度洪水补充了其洪泛平原的养分。|近义词：alluvial plain, flood plain, bottomland |反义词：highland, upland
