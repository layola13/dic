pargasite:/ˈpɑːrɡəsaɪt/| n. | 透辉石 |A complex inosilicate mineral of the amphibole group, typically dark green to black in color, containing calcium, magnesium, iron, and aluminum. It's often found in metamorphic rocks.  |The metamorphic rock sample contained significant amounts of pargasite.  那块变质岩样本含有大量的透辉石。|hornblende, actinolite|  (no direct antonym, but minerals with contrasting chemical compositions could be considered opposites)|名词
