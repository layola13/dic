philologian:/ˌfɪləˈləʊdʒən/| n. |语言学家|A philologian is a scholar of philology, the study of language in its historical and cultural development.  Philologians analyze the structure, history, and evolution of languages, often focusing on ancient languages and texts.| Example: The philologian spent years deciphering the ancient manuscript.|近义词：linguist, philologist|反义词：None specifically.
