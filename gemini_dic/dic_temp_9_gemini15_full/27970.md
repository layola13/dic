/kænts/ | v. | 不能，不会 |  To be unable to; to lack the ability or power to.  It often implies a lack of skill, opportunity, or permission. | I can't swim. = 我不会游泳。|  cannot, is unable to | can, is able to | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I can't believe it. = 我不敢相信。
She can't speak French. = 她不会说法语。


一般过去时:
I couldn't go to the party. = 我不能去参加聚会。
He couldn't finish the work on time. = 他不能按时完成工作。


现在完成时:
I haven't been able to contact him. = 我一直联系不上他。
She hasn't been able to find her keys. = 她一直找不到她的钥匙。


过去完成时:
I couldn't have done it without your help. = 没有你的帮助我做不到。
They couldn't have predicted the outcome. = 他们无法预测结果。


将来时:
I won't be able to attend the meeting. = 我将无法参加会议。
She won't be able to come tomorrow. = 她明天来不了。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I could finish the project. = 如果我有更多时间，我就能完成这个项目。
I wish I could go to the concert. = 我希望我能去参加音乐会。


被动语态:
It can't be done. = 这做不到。
The problem can't be solved easily. = 这个问题不容易解决。


疑问句:
Can't you hear me? = 你听不见我说话吗？
Can't she drive a car? = 她不会开车吗？


否定句:
I can't help you. = 我帮不了你。
He can't understand the instructions. = 他不明白这些指示。


PHRASE->
can't help but = 不禁
can't stand = 受不了
can't wait = 等不及
can't seem to = 好像不能
can't believe = 不敢相信
