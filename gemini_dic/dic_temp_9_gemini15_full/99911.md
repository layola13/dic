internationalize: /ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使国际化；使具有国际性 | To make something international in character or scope; to give something an international character or application. |例句：The company is internationalizing its operations by opening offices in several countries. 公司正在通过在几个国家开设办事处来使其业务国际化。

例句：The university is committed to internationalizing its curriculum to provide students with a global perspective.  大学致力于使其课程国际化，为学生提供全球视角。


近义词：globalize, globalise


反义词：localize, isolate


词性：动词
