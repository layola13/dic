daybook:/ˈdeɪbʊk/| n. |日记簿；流水账；分类账|A daybook is a book used for recording daily transactions, especially in business. It can be a general ledger or a more specialized record of particular types of transactions.  |Example: The shopkeeper meticulously kept a daybook to track all sales and expenses.|近义词：journal, ledger, register |反义词：None
