plagiarize:/ˈpleɪdʒəraɪz/| vt.|剽窃；抄袭|To steal and pass off (the ideas or words of another) as one's own; use (another's production) without crediting the source.|例句：He plagiarized his essay from a website. 他从网站上剽窃了他的论文。|例句：The student was expelled for plagiarizing his term paper.  这位学生因剽窃学期论文而被开除。|近义词：copy, steal, appropriate, crib|反义词：create, originate, invent|动词


USAGE->

He plagiarizes frequently. = 他经常剽窃。
She plagiarizes from well-known authors. = 她剽窃知名作家的作品。
