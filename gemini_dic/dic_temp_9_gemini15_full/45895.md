cuties:/ˈkjuːtiz/| n.|可爱的人（尤指儿童）|Cute young people, especially children.  The word implies a sense of endearment and often denotes small size and appealing features.|例句：Look at those cuties playing in the park!  看看那些在公园里玩耍的小可爱们！|例句：The kittens are such cuties!  这些小猫真是太可爱了！|近义词：sweethearts, darlings, lovelies|反义词：monsters, brutes


USAGE->

Those puppies are such cuties. = 那些小狗真可爱。
The babies are such cuties today. = 今天这些婴儿真可爱。

一般过去时:
