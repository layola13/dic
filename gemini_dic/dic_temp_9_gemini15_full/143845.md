outplacement:/ˈaʊtplesmənt/| n.|员工安置，离职安置服务|The process of assisting employees who are being laid off or terminated from their jobs in finding new employment.  It typically involves career counseling, job search assistance, and training.|例句：The company offered outplacement services to the employees who were made redundant. 这家公司为被裁员的员工提供了离职安置服务。|例句：  The outplacement firm helped her update her resume and practice interviewing skills.  这家离职安置公司帮助她更新了简历并练习了面试技巧。|近义词：job placement, career transition services|反义词：N/A
