self-starter:/ˈsɛlf ˈstɑːrtər/| n. |主动性强的人，积极主动者 | A self-starter is someone who is proactive and doesn't need to be told what to do. They are able to identify tasks that need to be done and take the initiative to complete them without external motivation.  |He's a self-starter and always looks for new challenges. 他是一个积极主动的人，总是寻求新的挑战。  She impressed her boss by being such a self-starter. 她以其积极主动的态度给老板留下了深刻的印象。| go-getter, initiator, self-motivator | passive person, procrastinator
