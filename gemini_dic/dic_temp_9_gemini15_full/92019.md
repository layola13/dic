hyperoxygenations: /ˌhaɪpərɑːkˈsɪdʒəneɪʃənz/ | n. |高氧合作用（复数）| The process of increasing the oxygen content of the blood or tissues beyond normal levels.  It can be a medical procedure or a physiological response. | Example:  Hyperoxygenations are sometimes used to treat carbon monoxide poisoning. 高氧合作用有时用于治疗一氧化碳中毒。  The diver experienced hyperoxygenations during the deep sea dive. 这名潜水员在深海潜水时经历了高氧合作用。|近义词：overoxygenation,  oxygenation (in a context of excess)|反义词：hypoxia, anoxia
