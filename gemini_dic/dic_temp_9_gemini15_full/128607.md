niching:/ˈnɪtʃɪŋ/| n. |细分市场定位；专业化；寻找特定市场|The act of focusing on a specific, underserved market segment.  This involves identifying a niche market and tailoring products or services to meet its unique needs and preferences.  This strategy aims to reduce competition and increase profitability.|Example: The company achieved success through niche marketing, targeting a small but highly profitable segment of the luxury goods market. |近义词：specialization, segmentation, focusing|反义词：generalization, diversification
