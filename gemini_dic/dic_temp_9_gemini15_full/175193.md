ragouts:/ræˈɡuːz/| n. |炖菜；杂烩|A ragout is a type of stew, typically made with meat and vegetables, often in a rich sauce.  It's characterized by its slow cooking process, which allows the flavors to meld together.  Different cultures have their own variations of ragout. |The chef prepared a delicious beef ragout with mushrooms and red wine.  厨师准备了一道美味的蘑菇红酒牛肉炖菜。|stew, casserole, hotpot|—| 名词
