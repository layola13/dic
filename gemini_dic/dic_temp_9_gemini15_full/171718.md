pummeling: /ˈpʌməlɪŋ/ | v. |痛打；猛击；连续猛击 |  The act of repeatedly hitting someone or something very hard.  |例句：The boxer pummeled his opponent relentlessly. 拳击手毫不留情地猛击对手。

例句：The hail pummeled the roof of the car. 冰雹猛击汽车车顶。


近义词：battering, thrashing, pounding, beating


反义词：caressing, stroking, soothing


词性：动词
