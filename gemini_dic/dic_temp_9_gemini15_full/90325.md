housewives: /ˈhaʊsˌwaɪvz/ | n. |家庭主妇|

英文解析:The plural form of "housewife," referring to women who manage a household and family, typically without outside employment.  It emphasizes their role in domestic life.

例句:
Many housewives find fulfillment in raising their families and managing their homes.  许多家庭主妇在养育家庭和管理家务中找到了满足感。

The survey targeted housewives in suburban areas.  这项调查的目标是郊区地区的家庭主妇。


近义词: homemakers, homebodies (less formal)

反义词: career women, working women


词源:  The word is a compound of "house" and "wife."  "House" refers to the home, and "wife" refers to a married woman.  The suffix "-wife" indicates a woman associated with a specific role or place.  The word evolved naturally from the traditional societal roles of women.
