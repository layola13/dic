fusser:/ˈfʌsər/| n. |爱挑剔的人；烦扰的人|A person who is excessively concerned with minor details or imperfections; someone who worries unnecessarily.|例句：He's such a fusser; he spent hours arranging the flowers. 他真是个爱挑剔的人；他花了几个小时摆弄鲜花。|近义词：nitpicker, fault-finder, worrier|反义词：easygoing person, nonchalant person|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fusses over every little detail. = 他对每一个小细节都过于担心。
She fusses about the house all day. = 她整天在家里忙忙碌碌。


一般过去时:
He fussed over his appearance before the interview. = 面试前，他非常在意自己的外表。
She fussed about the children's clothes. = 她为孩子们挑选衣服而忙乱。


现在完成时:
She has fussed over this project for weeks. = 她为这个项目忙乱了好几个星期了。
He has fussed too much about nothing. = 他为一些无关紧要的事情操心太多。


过去完成时:
She had fussed over the dinner for hours before the guests arrived. = 客人到来之前，她已经为晚餐忙活了几个小时。
He had fussed about the arrangements all day, and still wasn't satisfied. = 他整天都在为安排的事情而烦扰，仍然不满意。


将来时:
She will fuss over her grandson when he visits. = 她孙子来访时，会非常细心地照顾他。
He will fuss over the details of the contract. = 他会仔细琢磨合同的细节。


虚拟语气:
If she knew how to relax, she wouldn't fuss so much. = 如果她知道如何放松，她就不会这么焦虑了。
I wish he wouldn't fuss over every little thing. = 我希望他不要为每一件小事都操心。


被动语态:  (Fusser itself is rarely used passively)
The details were fussed over excessively. = 细节被过度地挑剔了。(This is an example of using "fuss over" passively)
The arrangements were fussed with unnecessarily. = 安排被不必要地弄乱了。


疑问句:
Does he always fuss so much? = 他总是这么焦虑吗？
Why is she fussing about? = 她为什么这么焦虑？


否定句:
He didn't fuss about the delay. = 他对延误并不在意。
She doesn't fuss over her appearance. = 她不在意自己的外表。


PHRASE->
fuss over: 过度关心；对…大惊小怪
fuss about: 为…而忙乱；对…过于操心
fuss with: 拨弄；摆弄
make a fuss: 大惊小怪；小题大做
fuss and bother: 烦扰；大惊小怪
