pipelaying:/ˈpaɪplaɪɪŋ/| n.|管道铺设，铺设管道|The act or process of laying pipes, usually for transporting liquids or gases.  It involves various stages, from planning and design to excavation, pipe joining, and testing.|例句：The pipelaying project was completed ahead of schedule. 管道铺设项目提前完成。

例句：Experienced pipelaying crews are in high demand for offshore oil and gas projects.  经验丰富的管道铺设人员在海上石油和天然气项目中非常抢手。
