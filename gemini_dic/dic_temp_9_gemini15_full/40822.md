convoyer:/kɒnˈvɔɪər/ | n. | 传送带；护送者 | A conveyor is a mechanical device used to transport materials from one place to another.  |例句：The factory uses a conveyor belt to move the boxes. 工厂使用传送带搬运箱子。|近义词：transporter, carrier, escort |反义词：receiver (in the context of receiving materials)|词性:名词
