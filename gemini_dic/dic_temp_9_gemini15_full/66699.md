exonerated:/ɪɡˈzɒnəreɪtɪd/ | v. | 宣告无罪；免除责任 | To clear (someone) from blame or accusation.  | The jury exonerated him of all charges. 陪审团宣告他所有指控不成立。| acquit, absolve, vindicate | convict, condemn, incriminate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The court exonerates innocent people. = 法院宣告无辜者无罪。
He exonerates himself from any responsibility. = 他免除了自己的一切责任。


一般过去时:
The investigation exonerated the suspect. = 调查结果证明嫌疑人无罪。
The judge exonerated her from the charges. = 法官免除了对她的指控。


现在完成时:
The new evidence has exonerated the defendant. = 新证据已证明被告无罪。
She has been exonerated after years of wrongful accusations. = 在多年的错误指控之后，她终于被证明无罪。


过去完成时:
The evidence had exonerated him before the trial even began. = 在审判开始之前，证据就已经证明他无罪了。
They had exonerated the company after a thorough investigation. = 在彻底调查后，他们为公司洗脱了嫌疑。


将来时:
The court will exonerate him, I believe. = 我相信法院会宣告他无罪。
The results of the investigation will exonerate her. = 调查结果将会证明她无罪。


虚拟语气:
If new evidence were found, he would be exonerated. = 如果发现新的证据，他将被宣告无罪。
I wish the truth would come out and exonerate me. = 我希望真相大白，为我洗清冤屈。


被动语态:
He was exonerated after a long and arduous trial. = 在漫长而艰苦的审判后，他被宣告无罪。
The suspect was exonerated due to lack of evidence. = 由于缺乏证据，嫌疑人被宣告无罪。


疑问句:
Will the evidence exonerate the accused? = 证据会为被告洗清罪名吗？
Has the committee exonerated the mayor? = 委员会是否为市长洗脱了嫌疑？


否定句:
The court did not exonerate him; he was found guilty. = 法院没有宣告他无罪；他被判有罪。
The investigation failed to exonerate her from suspicion. = 调查未能为她洗清嫌疑。


PHRASE->
be exonerated from = 免除...的责任
fully exonerated = 完全免罪
exonerated of charges = 指控被撤销
exonerated by the court = 法院宣告无罪
exonerated from blame = 免除责备
