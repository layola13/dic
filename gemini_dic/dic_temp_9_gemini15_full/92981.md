idiograph:/ˈɪdiəɡræf/| n. |表意文字；表意符号|A character or symbol that represents an idea or concept, rather than a sound.  Unlike phonetic alphabets, idiographs don't directly represent the sounds of a language.  They rely on visual representation to convey meaning.|Example: The symbol for "peace" (a dove) is an idiograph.  The Chinese character for "tree" (木) is an idiograph.|近义词：logogram, ideogram|反义词：phoneme, alphabet|名词
