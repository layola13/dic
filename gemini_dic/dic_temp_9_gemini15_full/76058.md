furnacing:/ˈfɜːnəsɪŋ/| n. |冶炼；熔炼；加热；锻造|The process of heating in a furnace, typically for the purpose of melting or refining metals or other materials.  It can also refer to the equipment or the act of using a furnace.|例句：The furnacing of iron ore is a crucial step in steel production.  铁矿石的冶炼是钢铁生产的关键步骤。

The ancient method of furnacing copper involved high temperatures and skilled craftsmanship.  古代炼铜的方法需要高温和熟练的工艺。


近义词：smelting, melting, heating, forging|反义词：cooling, quenching
