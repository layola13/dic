gymnodont:/ˈdʒɪmnədɒnt/ | n. |裸齿鱼类|A gymnodont is a type of fish characterized by the absence of teeth or having rudimentary teeth. They are typically found in marine environments and belong to various families within the order Tetraodontiformes.  |Example:  The pufferfish is a well-known example of a gymnodont.  |近义词: (lacks precise synonyms, needs to be described by characteristics, e.g., pufferfish, porcupinefish) |反义词: (no direct antonym)|名词
