ineluctably:/ɪˈnɛləkəbli/ | adj. | 不可避免地；必然地 | Unable to be resisted or avoided; inevitable.  | The ship, ineluctably drawn by the current, drifted toward the rocks.  船只不可避免地被水流吸引，漂向岩石。 | inevitably, unavoidably, inescapably | optionally, avoidably | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river ineluctably flows towards the sea. = 这条河不可避免地流向大海。
This situation ineluctably leads to failure. = 这种情况必然导致失败。


一般过去时:
The empire ineluctably declined. = 这个帝国不可避免地衰落了。
The evidence ineluctably pointed to his guilt. = 证据不可避免地指向了他的罪行。


现在完成时:
The truth has ineluctably emerged. = 真相已经不可避免地浮出水面。
The consequences have ineluctably unfolded. = 后果已不可避免地显现。


过去完成时:
It had ineluctably become clear that he was wrong. = 他错了这一点已不可避免地变得清晰。
The situation had ineluctably deteriorated. = 局势已不可避免地恶化。


将来时:
The project will ineluctably fail without more funding. = 没有更多资金，项目将不可避免地失败。
The effects of climate change will ineluctably affect us all.  = 气候变化的影响将不可避免地影响我们所有人。


虚拟语气:
If we continued this path, we would ineluctably face disaster. = 如果我们继续走这条路，我们将会不可避免地面临灾难。
If he had acted differently, the outcome might not have been so ineluctably negative. = 如果他采取不同的行动，结果可能不会如此不可避免地负面。


被动语态:
The outcome was ineluctably determined by fate. = 结果由命运不可避免地决定。
The decision was ineluctably influenced by external pressures. = 这个决定不可避免地受到了外部压力的影响。


疑问句:
Is this outcome ineluctably tied to our actions? = 这个结果是否不可避免地与我们的行动相关？
Is it ineluctably true that we are doomed? = 我们注定要灭亡，这是不可避免的真的吗？


否定句:
The situation was not ineluctably doomed; there was still hope. = 情况并非不可避免地注定要失败；仍然有希望。
The future is not ineluctably predetermined; we have free will. = 未来并非不可避免地预先注定；我们拥有自由意志。


PHRASE->

词源:
From Latin *ineluctabilis, from in- ("not") + luctabilus ("wrestable," from luctare ("to wrestle")).
