sigh:/saɪ/| n./v.|叹息，叹气|a deep audible breath expressing sadness, relief, or tiredness|例句：She let out a deep sigh of relief. 她长长地舒了一口气。  例句：He sighed heavily, looking at the pile of work. 他重重地叹了口气，看着那一堆工作。|近义词：groan, gasp, sob|反义词：laugh, cheer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sighs every time he thinks about it. = 他每次想到这件事就叹气。
She sighs frequently when she's stressed. = 她压力大的时候经常叹气。

一般过去时:
He sighed deeply after the exam. = 考试结束后他深深地叹了口气。
She sighed with disappointment when she heard the news. = 听到这个消息后，她失望地叹了口气。

现在完成时:
I have sighed countless times today. = 我今天已经叹了无数次气了。
She has sighed with relief since the project finished. = 自从项目完成以来，她一直如释重负地叹了口气。

过去完成时:
He had sighed with frustration before he finally gave up. = 在他最终放弃之前，他曾沮丧地叹了口气。
She had sighed many times before she finally made a decision. = 在她最终做出决定之前，她已经叹了好几次气了。

将来时:
I will sigh with relief when I finish this report. = 当我完成这份报告时，我会如释重负地叹口气。
He will sigh with exhaustion after the marathon. = 马拉松结束后，他会疲惫地叹口气。

虚拟语气:
If I had known the truth, I wouldn't have sighed with worry. = 如果我知道真相，我不会担心地叹气。
I wish I hadn't sighed in front of her. = 我真希望我没有在她面前叹气。

被动语态:
Sighs were heard throughout the room. = 整个房间都能听到叹息声。
A sigh of relief was breathed by everyone. = 每个人都舒了一口气。

疑问句:
Did you hear him sigh? = 你听到他叹气了吗？
Why did she sigh so sadly? = 她为什么这么悲伤地叹气？

否定句:
He didn't sigh once during the meeting. = 会议期间他一次也没有叹气。
She didn't sigh, she just smiled. = 她没有叹气，只是笑了笑。
