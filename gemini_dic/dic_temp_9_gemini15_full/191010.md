scamble:/ˈskæmbəl/ | vt. vi. n.| 攀登；争夺；（尤指快速而混乱地）移动；炒（蛋）；|to move quickly and clumsily; to scramble eggs; to climb quickly and awkwardly.|例句：The children scrambled over the rocks. 孩子们爬过岩石。|近义词：climb, scramble, clamber|反义词：descend, slide


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scrambles eggs every morning. = 他每天早上都炒鸡蛋。
The monkeys scramble up the trees. = 猴子爬上树。

一般过去时:
She scrambled to her feet after the fall. = 她摔倒后挣扎着站起来。
They scrambled for the best seats. = 他们争抢最好的座位。

现在完成时:
I have scrambled up this mountain before. = 我以前爬过这座山。
They have scrambled the eggs perfectly. = 他们把鸡蛋炒得很好。

过去完成时:
He had scrambled to the top of the hill before the storm hit. =暴风雨来临之前，他已经爬到了山顶。
The climbers had scrambled over many difficult rocks. = 登山者们已经翻越了许多险峻的岩石。


将来时:
We will scramble to meet the deadline. = 我们将努力赶上最后期限。
The soldiers will scramble for cover when the attack begins. = 攻击开始时，士兵们将争先恐后地寻找掩护。


虚拟语气:
If I scrambled harder, I might have won the race. = 如果我更努力地攀爬，我可能会赢得比赛。
If he had scrambled faster, he would have caught the train. = 如果他爬得更快，他就能赶上火车了。


被动语态:
The eggs were scrambled with onions and peppers. = 鸡蛋和洋葱、青椒一起炒。
The message was scrambled and difficult to understand. = 信息被扰乱了，难以理解。


疑问句:
Did you scramble the eggs? = 你炒鸡蛋了吗？
How did they scramble to the top so quickly? = 他们怎么这么快就爬到顶上了？


否定句:
I didn't scramble the eggs, I fried them. = 我没有炒鸡蛋，我是煎的。
He didn't scramble for the ball, he just watched. = 他没有争抢球，只是看着。
