interposing:/ˌɪntərˈpoʊzɪŋ/| v.|插入；介入；打断|To place or come between; to interrupt.|例句：He interposing a comment during the meeting. 他在会议中插话。|例句：She interposing herself between the arguing couple. 她介入争吵的夫妇之间。|近义词：interjecting, intervening, interrupting|反义词：ignoring, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He interposes his opinion frequently. = 他经常插话发表意见。
She interposes herself in every conversation. = 她插手每一场谈话。

一般过去时:
He interposed a question during the lecture. = 他在讲座期间插了一句问话。
She interposed her body to protect the child. = 她用身体保护孩子。

现在完成时:
He has interposed himself in the family dispute. = 他已经介入家庭纠纷。
She has interposed objections at every stage. = 她一直在各个阶段提出异议。

过去完成时:
He had interposed a comment before the speaker could finish. = 在说话人结束之前，他已经插话评论了。
She had interposed her hand to stop the blow. = 她已经出手阻止了那一击。

将来时:

She will interpose herself if the situation requires it. = 如果情况需要，她会介入。
