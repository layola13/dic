mermen:/ˈmɜːrmɛn/ | n. |男性美人鱼；海妖|A mythical creature, typically depicted as a man from the waist up and a fish from the waist down.  Mermen are often associated with the sea and possess magical powers or abilities. They are often portrayed in folklore and literature.|Example:  The legend speaks of mermen who lure sailors to their doom with enchanting songs.  传说中有一种美人鱼会用迷人的歌声引诱水手走向死亡。|近义词：mermaid (female counterpart), sea creature, triton|反义词：N/A
