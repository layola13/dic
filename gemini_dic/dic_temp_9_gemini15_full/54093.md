disembitter:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbɪtər/| vt.|使不再痛苦；使不再怨恨|To free from bitterness or resentment; to alleviate bitterness.  The therapy helped to disembitter him after his divorce.|近义词：soothe, alleviate, assuage|反义词：embitter, aggravate, exacerbate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The therapy disembitters him gradually. = 治疗逐渐使他不再痛苦。
This experience disembitters many people. = 这段经历使许多人不再怨恨。


一般过去时:
The vacation disembittered her after the stressful work. = 假期使她在紧张的工作后不再痛苦。
Talking to a therapist disembittered him from his past trauma. = 和治疗师交谈使他从过去的创伤中解脱出来。


现在完成时:
Has time disembittered her over the loss of her job? = 时间是否让她对失去工作不再痛苦？
The years have disembittered him, allowing him to see things more clearly. = 岁月让他不再怨恨，让他看得更清楚了。


过去完成时:

He had disembittered himself through years of meditation. = 他通过多年的冥想让自己不再痛苦。


将来时:

Hopefully, time will disembitter her from the pain of the breakup. = 希望时间能让她从分手的痛苦中解脱出来。


虚拟语气:

I wish this experience could disembitter her completely. = 我希望这段经历能让她完全摆脱痛苦。


被动语态:
