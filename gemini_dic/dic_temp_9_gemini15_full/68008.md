factoid:/ˈfæktɔɪd/| n. |小道消息；琐碎事实；似是而非的报道|A factoid is a piece of information that is presented as a fact, but is actually false or misleading.  It often spreads rapidly due to repetition and lack of verification.  Factoids can be intentionally created as propaganda or unintentionally spread due to misinformation.|Example: The claim that "the earth is flat" is a persistent factoid, despite overwhelming scientific evidence to the contrary.  这个说法“地球是平的”是一个持续存在的似是而非的报道，尽管有压倒性的科学证据证明并非如此。|近义词：rumor, gossip, anecdote, trivia|反义词：fact, truth, verified information|名词
