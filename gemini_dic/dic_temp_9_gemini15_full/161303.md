postparoxysmal: /ˌpoʊstpæˈrɒksɪzməl/ | adj. | 阵发后期的；发作后的 | Relating to the period following a paroxysm or seizure.  This term is primarily used in the medical field to describe the state or symptoms experienced after a sudden attack or episode, such as an epileptic seizure or a coughing fit.  | 例句：Postparoxysmal fatigue is a common symptom after a seizure.  阵发后疲劳是癫痫发作后的常见症状。

The patient experienced postparoxysmal confusion following the migraine.  患者在偏头痛发作后出现阵发后意识混乱。


近义词：post-ictal (specifically referring to the period after a seizure)


反义词：paroxysmal (during the attack/seizure itself)


词源:  The word is a combination of "post-" (meaning "after") and "paroxysmal" (referring to a sudden attack or spasm).  "Paroxysmal" itself comes from the Greek word "παροξυσμός" (paroxysmos), meaning "exacerbation," "increase," or "violent outburst."
