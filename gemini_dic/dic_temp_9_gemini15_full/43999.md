criminalization: /ˌkrɪmɪnəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 犯罪化；定为犯罪 | The process of making something a crime.  It involves declaring an act or behavior illegal and subject to legal penalties. |  The criminalization of drug use has led to a rise in incarceration rates.  吸毒的犯罪化导致监禁率上升。The increasing criminalization of minor offenses burdens the justice system. 轻微罪行的日益犯罪化给司法系统带来了负担。|  penalization, illegality | legalization | 名词
