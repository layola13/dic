haggle:/ˈhæɡəl/| v.|讨价还价，争论|To argue about the price of something, especially in a market or shop.  |例句：I haggled with the shopkeeper for a better price. 我和店主讨价还价以获得更好的价格。

例句：We haggled over the price of the car for hours before reaching an agreement. 我们就汽车价格讨价还价了几个小时才达成协议。|近义词：bargain, negotiate, barter|反义词：accept, concede


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He haggles over every purchase. = 他每次购物都讨价还价。
They haggle with vendors at the market. = 他们在市场上与小贩讨价还价。

一般过去时:
She haggled for a lower price on the rug. = 她为地毯讨价还价以获得更低的价格。
We haggled with the taxi driver about the fare. = 我们与出租车司机就车费讨价还价。

现在完成时:
I have haggled with many street vendors in my travels. = 在我的旅行中，我已经和许多街头小贩讨价还价了。
They have haggled successfully for a better deal. = 他们成功地讨价还价，获得了更好的交易。

过去完成时:
He had haggled with the seller for days before he finally agreed on a price. = 他与卖家讨价还价了好几天才最终就价格达成一致。
They had haggled over the terms of the contract for weeks. = 他们就合同条款讨价还价了好几个星期。

将来时:
I will haggle with the dealer to get a better price on the used car. = 我将与经销商讨价还价，以便获得二手车更好的价格。
We will haggle over the details of the agreement tomorrow. = 我们明天将就协议的细节进行讨价还价。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would haggle more aggressively. = 如果我是你，我会更强硬地讨价还价。
I wish I had haggled harder for a lower price. = 我真希望我当时更努力地讨价还价以获得更低的价格。

被动语态:
The price was haggled down considerably. = 价格被大幅度地压低了。
The terms of the contract are being haggled over. = 合同条款正在被讨价还价。

疑问句:
Do you usually haggle when you buy things? = 你买东西时通常会讨价还价吗？
Will they haggle over the price of the house? = 他们会就房价讨价还价吗？

否定句:
He didn't haggle at all; he just paid the asking price. = 他根本没有讨价还价；他只是付了要价。
They didn't haggle, accepting the offer immediately. = 他们没有讨价还价，立即接受了报价。
