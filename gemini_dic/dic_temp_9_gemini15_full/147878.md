overreaction:/ˌoʊvərɪˈæʃən/| n. |过度反应，反应过度|An excessively strong or emotional reaction to something.|Her outburst was a clear overreaction to a minor inconvenience.  她的爆发是对一件小麻烦的明显过度反应。|The team's celebration was an overreaction to a small victory. 球队的庆祝活动是对小胜利的过度反应。|近义词：exaggerated response, overblown reaction|反义词：underreaction, calm response

The public's overreaction to the news is concerning. 公众对新闻的过度反应令人担忧。
