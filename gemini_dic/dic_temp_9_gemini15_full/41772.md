corruptions:/kəˈrʌpʃənz/| n. |腐败；堕落；败坏；歪曲 |The plural form of "corruption," referring to instances or acts of dishonest or fraudulent behavior, typically involving those in power.  It can also refer to the deterioration or impairment of something's purity or integrity. |例句：The government is plagued by widespread corruptions.  政府充斥着普遍的腐败行为。

The recent scandals revealed a series of corruptions within the company. 最近的丑闻揭露了公司内部一系列的腐败行为。|近义词：decay, degeneration, dishonesty, depravity |反义词：integrity, honesty, purity
