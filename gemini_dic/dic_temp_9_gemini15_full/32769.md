chock-full:/tʃɒkˈfʊl/| adj.|塞满的，挤满的，充满的|Completely full; packed to capacity.|The suitcase was chock-full of clothes.  这个手提箱塞满了衣服。|近义词：full, brimming, crammed, packed|反义词：empty, vacant


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The box is chock-full of toys. = 箱子里塞满了玩具。
The restaurant is chock-full of people. = 餐厅里挤满了人。

一般过去时:
The room was chock-full of furniture. = 房间里塞满了家具。
The bus was chock-full of passengers. = 公共汽车里挤满了乘客。

现在完成时:

The schedule has been chock-full of meetings this week. = 本周日程安排得满满的。

过去完成时:
The theatre had been chock-full before the show began. = 演出开始前剧院里已经座无虚席。
