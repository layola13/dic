glad-hander:/ˈɡlædˌhændər/| n. |逢迎拍马者，八面玲珑的人|A glad-hander is someone who is excessively friendly and sociable, often in a superficial way, to gain favor or advantage. They are skilled at networking and making connections, but their friendliness may not be genuine.  |例句：The politician was a notorious glad-hander, shaking hands and making promises to everyone he met, regardless of their actual importance.  这位政客是出了名的逢迎拍马者，他遇到每个人都会握手并许下承诺，不管对方有多重要。

The CEO, a seasoned glad-hander, effortlessly charmed the investors.  这位经验丰富的逢迎拍马的首席执行官轻松地迷住了投资者们。|近义词：networker, schmoozer, smooth talker, brown-noser |反义词：introvert, recluse, antisocial
