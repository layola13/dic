entails:/ɪnˈteɪlz/| vt.|包含；需要；意味；牵涉|to involve (something) as a necessary or inevitable part or consequence.  To imply or suggest (something) as a logical consequence.|例句：The job entails a lot of hard work. 这份工作需要付出很多努力。

The success of the project entails the cooperation of all team members.  项目的成功需要所有团队成员的合作。


近义词：involves, implies, necessitates, requires|反义词：excludes, omits


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This job entails long hours. = 这份工作需要长时间工作。
The plan entails significant risks. = 这个计划包含巨大的风险。


一般过去时:
The treaty entailed significant concessions. = 这项条约需要做出重大让步。
The decision entailed unforeseen consequences. = 这个决定带来了意想不到的后果。


现在完成时:
The project has entailed more expense than anticipated. = 这个项目已经花费了比预期更多的费用。
His actions have entailed serious repercussions. = 他的行为已经带来了严重的后果。


过去完成时:
The investigation had entailed months of painstaking work. = 调查已经进行了数月的艰苦工作。
The journey had entailed numerous unexpected delays. = 这段旅程经历了无数意想不到的延误。


将来时:
The new policy will entail changes to our procedures. = 新政策将需要改变我们的程序。
The expansion will entail significant investment. = 扩张将需要大量的投资。


虚拟语气:
If the plan entailed fewer risks, we would proceed. = 如果这个计划风险较小，我们会继续进行。
I wish the project didn't entail so much paperwork. = 我希望这个项目不需要这么多文书工作。


被动语态:
Many responsibilities are entailed in this position. = 这个职位包含许多责任。
Significant costs were entailed by the renovation. = 装修带来了巨大的成本。


疑问句:
What does this decision entail? = 这个决定包含什么？
What changes will the new regulations entail? = 新规将带来哪些变化？


否定句:
The contract doesn't entail any hidden fees. = 合同中没有任何隐藏费用。
The agreement did not entail any obligation on our part. = 该协议并未对我们有任何义务。


PHRASE->
entail upon:  强加于...
entail on:  强加于...
entail in: 包含在...中
entail with:  伴随...
entail something on someone: 把…强加于某人
