词源:
From "phosphate" + "-ate" (a verb suffix indicating to treat with or convert to).  The word ultimately derives from "phosphorus," from Greek  "phos" (light) + "phoros" (bearing).  The suffix "-ate"  indicates a chemical process of combining with a substance.
