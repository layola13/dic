collates:/kəˈleɪts/| vt.|校对，整理|To arrange (papers, cards, etc.) in order.  It often implies checking for accuracy or completeness during the process.|例句：She collated the research papers before submitting them. 她提交研究论文之前先整理了一下。|例句：The secretary collated the documents for the meeting. 秘书整理了会议文件。|近义词：arrange, organize, assemble, sort|反义词：disorganize, jumble, scatter|动词


USAGE->

He collates the data every week. = 他每周整理数据。
She collates the files before archiving them. = 她存档前会整理文件。

I collated the documents yesterday. = 我昨天整理了文件。
