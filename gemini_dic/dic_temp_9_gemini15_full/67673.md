extrusion:/ɪkˈstruːʒən/| n.|挤压;挤出;挤出物|The process of shaping a material by forcing it through a die.  This process is commonly used in manufacturing various products, from plastic pipes to aluminum profiles.|例句：The extrusion of the plastic resulted in a perfectly shaped pipe.  塑料挤压成型了一个完美的管道。

例句：Aluminum extrusion is a common method for creating lightweight and strong structural components. 铝挤压是制造轻便耐用的结构部件的常用方法。


近义词：protrusion, projection, forcing


反义词：None directly opposite, but words suggesting the opposite process such as molding (in some contexts) could be considered.


词性:名词
