handicapped:/ˈhændikæpt/ | adj. |残疾的；有障碍的；受阻碍的 |  Describes a person with a physical, mental, or sensory impairment that limits their ability to perform certain tasks.  It can also refer to something that hinders progress or success.  The term is considered outdated and potentially offensive by some, with "disabled" or "person with a disability" preferred. |  He is handicapped by a speech impediment. 他因口吃而受阻。 The project was handicapped by a lack of funding.  该项目因缺乏资金而受阻。 | disabled, impaired, disadvantaged | able-bodied, enabled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The handicapped child receives special education. = 残疾儿童接受特殊教育。
Many handicapped people are successful in their careers. = 许多残疾人在事业上取得成功。

一般过去时:
The accident handicapped him for life. = 那次事故使他终身残疾。
The storm handicapped travel for days. = 暴风雨使交通中断了好几天。

现在完成时:
His illness has handicapped him significantly. = 他的疾病严重地影响了他。
The company has been handicapped by poor management. = 公司因管理不善而受到阻碍。

过去完成时:
She had been handicapped by poverty since childhood. = 她从小就受贫困的困扰。
The team had been handicapped by injuries throughout the season. = 这个赛季球队一直受到伤病困扰。

将来时:
The new regulations will handicap small businesses. = 新规将对小型企业造成不利影响。
The lack of resources will handicap our efforts. = 资源匮乏将阻碍我们的努力。

虚拟语气:
If he weren't handicapped, he could play sports. = 如果他不是残疾人，他就能参加体育运动。
I wish I weren't handicapped by my shyness. = 我真希望我不那么害羞。

被动语态:
He was handicapped by a childhood illness. = 他从小就患病，这对他造成了影响。
The negotiations were handicapped by a lack of trust. = 谈判因缺乏信任而受阻。

疑问句:
Is he handicapped in any way? = 他有什么残疾吗？
Were the negotiations handicapped by anything? = 谈判受到了什么阻碍吗？

否定句:
He is not handicapped in any significant way. = 他并没有受到什么严重的残疾影响。
The project was not handicapped by a lack of resources. = 该项目并没有因为缺乏资源而受阻。
