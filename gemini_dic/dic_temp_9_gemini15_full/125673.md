muncher:/ˈmʌntʃər/| n. |大吃特吃的人；猛吃的人；（尤指）吃东西发出响声的人|A person who munches food noisily.  Someone who eats a lot, especially in a noisy way.|例句：He's a real muncher; he finished the whole pizza in minutes. 他是个真正的大胃王；几分钟内就吃完了整个披萨。|例句：The noisy muncher across from me was distracting me from my work.  对面发出咀嚼声的人分散了我的注意力。|近义词：eater, gourmand, glutton|反义词：starver, dieter|名词
