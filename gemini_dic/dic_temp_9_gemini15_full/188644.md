rummagers:/ˈrʌmɪdʒərz/| n.|翻找者，搜寻者|People who rummage; those who search thoroughly and often haphazardly.|例句：The rummagers spent hours sifting through the attic's dusty contents. 翻找者花了数小时仔细翻找阁楼里布满灰尘的东西。

例句：Experienced rummagers know where to look for hidden treasures in antique shops.  经验丰富的翻找者知道在哪里可以找到古董店里隐藏的宝藏。


近义词：searchers, investigators, explorers


反义词：organizers, tidiers


词性:名词
