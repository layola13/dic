off-shore:/ˌɔːfˈʃɔːr/| adj. adv. n.|近海的；离岸的；海外的；（公司等）在海外注册的 | Situated or operating away from the shore.  Relating to business activities that are conducted in a country other than the one in which the company is based. |例句：The oil rig is located in off-shore waters. 这座石油钻井平台位于近海海域。

The company moved its operations off-shore to reduce taxes. 这家公司将业务转移到海外以减少税收。


近义词：overseas, foreign, coastal (as a noun), international
反义词：onshore, inland, domestic
