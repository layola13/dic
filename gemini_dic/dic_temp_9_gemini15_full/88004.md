high-schools: /ˈhaɪˌskuːlz/ | n. | 高中 |  High schools are secondary schools that typically cover grades 9 through 12. They provide a comprehensive education that prepares students for college or vocational training. | Example:  Many high schools offer a wide range of extracurricular activities. 许多高中提供各种各样的课外活动。  |近义词: secondary schools, high schools, prep schools | 反义词: elementary schools, primary schools | 名词
