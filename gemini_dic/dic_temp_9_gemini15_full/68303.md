falconry:/ˈfɔːlkənri/| n.|猎鹰术；驯鹰；猎鹰运动|The art or sport of hunting with trained falcons or hawks.|例句：Falconry is a challenging but rewarding pursuit. 猎鹰术是一项充满挑战但又令人满足的追求。|例句：He learned falconry from his grandfather. 他从他祖父那里学会了猎鹰术。|近义词：hawking|反义词：无
