hegemony:/hɪˈdʒɛməni/| n. |霸权；支配；领导地位 |The dominance of one group over others.  It often refers to political, economic, or cultural influence.  Hegemony is not simply power, but the ability to shape the beliefs and values of others so that their dominance is accepted as legitimate. |例句：The country's hegemony in the region is unquestioned. 这个国家在该地区的霸权地位是毋庸置疑的。|例句：The cultural hegemony of Hollywood is felt worldwide. 好莱坞的文化霸权在全世界都有影响。|近义词：dominance, supremacy, ascendancy, predominance |反义词：subordination, subservience
