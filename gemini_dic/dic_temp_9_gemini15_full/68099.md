fads:/fædz/| n.|时尚，流行，一时风尚|A fad is a temporary fashion, interest, or craze that is very popular for a short time and then disappears.  It often lacks lasting value or significance.|Example:  The fidget spinner was a fad that swept the nation a few years ago.  几年前，指尖陀螺风靡全国。


Example: The latest dance craze is just a passing fad; it won't last.  最新的舞蹈热潮只是一时的流行，不会持续很久。|近义词：craze, trend, vogue, fashion|反义词：classic, enduring, timeless

USAGE->
一般现在时:
This fad is gaining popularity. = 这种时尚正在流行起来。
That fad is losing its appeal. = 那股时尚正在失去吸引力。


一般过去时:
That fad was popular in the 1980s. = 那股时尚在20世纪80年代很流行。
The fad quickly faded away. = 那股时尚迅速消退了。


现在完成时:
This fad has already peaked. = 这种时尚已经达到顶峰了。
Several fads have come and gone this year. = 今年已经出现过好几股时尚潮流，然后又消失了。


过去完成时:
By the time I noticed, the fad had already passed. = 当我注意到时，那股时尚已经过去了。
The fad had been popular for a short period before it disappeared. = 那股时尚流行了一段时间后就消失了。


将来时:
This new trend will likely become a short-lived fad. = 这种新趋势很可能成为昙花一现的时尚。
Experts predict that this fad will soon pass. = 专家预测这种时尚很快就会过去。


虚拟语气:
If this were a lasting trend, not a fad, I would invest. = 如果这是一个持久的潮流，而不是昙花一现的时尚，我会投资。
I wish this fad would disappear quickly. = 我希望这种时尚能尽快消失。


被动语态:
The fad was quickly embraced by the public. = 这种时尚很快就被公众接受了。
The fad is being discussed widely on social media. = 这种时尚正在社交媒体上被广泛讨论。


疑问句:
Is this a passing fad or a lasting trend? = 这是昙花一现的时尚还是持久的潮流？
What's the latest fad in fashion? = 最新时尚潮流是什么？


否定句:
This isn't a new fad; it's a classic style. = 这不是新的时尚；这是一个经典风格。
That wasn't just a fad; it had a lasting impact. = 那不仅仅是一时的流行；它产生了持久的影响。
