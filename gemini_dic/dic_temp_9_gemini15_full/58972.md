dutchman:/ˈdʌtʃmən/| n. |荷兰人|A native or inhabitant of the Netherlands.  The term can also refer to a person of Dutch descent.|Example:  He's a Dutchman, born and raised in Amsterdam. 他是个荷兰人，在阿姆斯特丹长大。

He is a Dutchman who speaks fluent English. 他是一位说一口流利英语的荷兰人。|近义词：Netherlander|反义词：无
