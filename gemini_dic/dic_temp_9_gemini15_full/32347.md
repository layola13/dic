childishly: /ˈtʃaɪldɪʃli/ | adj. | 孩子气地；幼稚地 |  Behaving or done in a way that is typical of a child, especially in a silly or immature way.  | 例句：He childishly refused to share his toys. 他孩子气地拒绝分享他的玩具。

例句：She reacted childishly to the criticism. 她对批评的反应很幼稚。


近义词：immaturely, childishly, in a childish manner,  infantilely

反义词：maturely, adultly, rationally, sensibly


词性: 形容词副词


词源:  Derived from the word "childish," which itself comes from "child" + "-ish," a suffix indicating resemblance or quality.  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective "childish" into an adverb, modifying verbs and describing how an action is performed.
