predefending:/ˌpriːdɪˈfɛndɪŋ/| v. |预先防御；提前防御|  The act of defending something before an attack or threat occurs.  It involves taking proactive measures to prevent or mitigate potential harm.  This term is less common than other words expressing similar concepts.|例句：The city council is predefending against potential flooding by reinforcing the riverbanks.  = 市议会正在通过加固河岸来预先防御潜在的洪水。

The company is predefending against a possible lawsuit by gathering all relevant documents. = 公司正在通过收集所有相关文件来预先防御可能的诉讼。|近义词：proactively defending, preventing, safeguarding, preempting |反义词：neglecting, ignoring, jeopardizing
