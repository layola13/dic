pressagent:/ˈprɛsˌeɪdʒənt/| n. |新闻代理人，宣传员 |A press agent is someone whose job is to create and maintain a positive public image for a client, often a celebrity or a company, through media relations and publicity.  They work to get favorable news coverage and promote their client's activities. | Example: The movie star hired a press agent to handle the negative publicity surrounding the scandal.  电影明星雇了一位新闻代理人来处理围绕丑闻的负面宣传。|近义词： publicist, publicist, spokesperson |反义词：critic, detractor
