extended:/ɪkˈstɛndɪd/ | adj. | 延伸的；扩展的；延长的；广阔的 |  Extended refers to something that has been made longer or larger in size, scope, or duration. It can describe physical things, periods of time, or abstract concepts. |例句：The extended family gathered for a reunion. 整个大家庭聚在一起团聚。|例句：The company extended its deadline for submissions. 公司延长了提交截止日期。|近义词：prolonged, expanded, lengthened, amplified |反义词：shortened, curtailed, reduced, limited |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is extended for another month. = 项目延长了一个月。
He extends his hand in greeting. = 他伸出手打招呼。

一般过去时:
They extended their stay in Paris. = 他们在巴黎延长了逗留时间。
She extended the table to accommodate more guests. = 她把桌子拉长以容纳更多客人。

现在完成时:
The road has been extended to the next village. = 这条路已经延伸到下一个村庄了。

过去完成时:

将来时:

The company will extend its product line. = 公司将扩大其产品线。

虚拟语气:
If they extended the deadline, I could finish the project. = 如果他们延长截止日期，我就能完成项目。
I wish they had extended the offer to me. = 我真希望他们把这个机会给了我。

被动语态:
The contract was extended for another year. = 合同延长了一年。

疑问句:

否定句:
