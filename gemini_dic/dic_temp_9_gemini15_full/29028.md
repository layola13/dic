cartooned:/kɑːˈtuːnd/ | adj. | 卡通化的；漫画化的 |  Depicted or represented in the style of a cartoon.  This adjective describes something that has been drawn or presented in a simplified, exaggerated, or humorous way, like a cartoon. | 例句：The cartooned version of the historical event was amusing but lacked accuracy.  卡通化的历史事件版本很有趣，但却缺乏准确性。

The characters in the film were cartooned to the point of being unrealistic. 电影中的人物被卡通化到不切实际的地步。


近义词：caricatured, stylized, animated,  illustrated


反义词：realistic, photorealistic, accurate


词性：形容词
