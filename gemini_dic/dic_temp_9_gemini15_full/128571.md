nibful:/ˈnɪbfʊl/| n. |一小撮；一口；少量|A small amount, especially of food or drink, that can be held in or taken with the nib of a pen or similar implement.  It often implies a small, almost insignificant quantity.|例句：He took a nibful of the delicious cake. 他吃了一小口美味的蛋糕。


例句：She offered me a nibful of her tea. 她给了我一小口茶。


近义词：mouthful, sip, drop, bit


反义词：abundance, plenty, copious amount


词性：名词
