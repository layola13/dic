expansionists:/ɪkˌspænʃənɪsts/| n. |扩张主义者，扩张论者 |Advocates of territorial or ideological expansion.  People who believe in or support expansionism,  especially the policy of extending a nation's power and influence through diplomacy or military force.|例句：The expansionists' aggressive foreign policy led to widespread condemnation. 扩张主义者的侵略性外交政策导致了广泛的谴责。

例句：Many historians criticize the expansionist policies of the 19th century. 许多历史学家批评19世纪的扩张主义政策。


近义词：imperialists, expansionism proponents

反义词：isolationists, contractionists

词性：noun
