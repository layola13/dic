homefront:/ˈhoʊmfrʌnt/| n. |国内战线；后方；家园|The activities and attitudes of the civilian population of a country at war, especially in relation to their support for the military.  It refers to the non-military aspects of a nation's involvement in war, including civilian morale, production of war materials, and economic adjustments.| Example: The homefront played a crucial role in winning World War II.  国内战线在赢得二战中发挥了至关重要的作用。The government implemented rationing on the homefront to conserve resources.  政府在后方实施配给制以节约资源。|近义词：rear, domestic front |反义词：battlefront, front line
