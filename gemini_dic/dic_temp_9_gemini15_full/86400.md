hedonics:/hɪˈdɒnɪks/ | /hɪˈdɑːnɪks/ | n. |享乐主义；快乐学 |The branch of psychology concerned with pleasure and happiness. Hedonics explores the nature of pleasure and how it relates to well-being.  It investigates factors that contribute to happiness, such as positive emotions, life satisfaction, and the absence of negative emotions. |Example:  The study focused on the hedonics of eating chocolate. 这项研究关注的是吃巧克力的享乐。


The pursuit of pleasure and avoidance of pain are central to hedonic theories. 追求快乐和避免痛苦是享乐主义理论的核心。|近义词：pleasure-seeking, sensualism |反义词：asceticism, self-denial |名词
