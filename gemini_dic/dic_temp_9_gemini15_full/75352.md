froth:/frɒθ/ /frɔːθ/| n. v.|泡沫；泡沫状物；激怒；起泡沫|

英文解析:
Noun: A mass of small bubbles in a liquid; a frothy substance.  Also, a state of excitement or agitation.
Verb: To form a froth; to foam.  Also, to become agitated or excited.

例句:

Noun:
The beer had a thick head of froth. = 这啤酒上面有一层厚厚的泡沫。

The sea was white with froth. = 海面满是白沫。


Verb:
The agitated sea frothed against the rocks. = 汹涌的海浪拍打着岩石，激起泡沫。

He frothed at the mouth in rage. = 他愤怒地咬牙切齿。


近义词:
Noun: foam, lather, spume, bubbles
Verb: foam, bubble, effervesce


反义词:
Noun: stillness, calm
Verb:  (No direct antonym, but contextually,  "to calm," "to soothe" might be considered opposites depending on the usage.)


词性:
Noun, Verb
