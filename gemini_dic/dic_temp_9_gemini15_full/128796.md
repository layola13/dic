nighters:/ˈnaɪtərz/| n.|熬夜；通宵达旦|nights spent working or studying, especially late into the night.|例句：I pulled a few all-nighters to finish my project. 我熬了好几个通宵才完成我的项目。|近义词：late nights, sleepless nights|反义词：early nights, good sleep.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pulls all-nighters frequently. = 他经常熬夜。
They often study nighters before exams. = 他们考试前经常通宵学习。

一般过去时:
She pulled an all-nighter last night. = 她昨晚熬夜了。
We pulled several nighters to complete the report. = 我们熬了好几个夜才完成报告。

现在完成时:
I have pulled many nighters this semester. = 这个学期我已经熬了好几次夜了。
They have pulled all-nighters to prepare for the competition. = 为了准备比赛，他们已经熬了好几个夜了。

过去完成时:
He had pulled several nighters before the deadline. = 截止日期之前，他已经熬了好几个夜了。
She had pulled an all-nighter, so she was very tired. = 她熬了一个通宵，所以非常疲惫。
