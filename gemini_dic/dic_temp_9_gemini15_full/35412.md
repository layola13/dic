co-opts:/koʊˈɑːpt/| vt.| 篡夺；利用；吸收；改编 | To take or assume control of (something, especially a process or organization) for one's own purposes, often in a subversive way.  To adopt (an idea, method, etc.) from another source. To appoint someone to a committee or position without their consent. |例句：The government co-opted the opposition's ideas. 政府窃取了反对派的观点。

The company co-opted elements from its competitor's successful marketing campaign. 这家公司借鉴了其竞争对手成功的营销活动中的元素。|近义词：appropriate, usurp, adopt, incorporate |反义词：reject, relinquish, abandon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They co-opt new ideas into their strategies. = 他们将新的理念融入到他们的战略中。
The committee co-opts new members every year. = 委员会每年都吸收新的成员。


一般过去时:
The dictator co-opted the army's support. = 独裁者获得了军队的支持。
The company co-opted the technology from a smaller startup. = 这家公司从一家较小的初创公司那里获得了这项技术。


现在完成时:
They have co-opted many aspects of popular culture into their advertising. = 他们已经将流行文化的许多方面融入到他们的广告中。
The organization has co-opted several influential figures. = 该组织已经吸收了几位有影响力的人物。


过去完成时:
Before the merger, they had already co-opted several key employees from the other company. = 在合并之前，他们已经从另一家公司吸收了几名关键员工。
The rebels had co-opted the local militia's resources. = 叛军已经夺取了当地民兵的资源。


将来时:
They will co-opt the best features of the competing product. = 他们将吸收竞争产品的最佳特性。
The government will likely co-opt the opposition's proposals. = 政府可能会采纳反对派的提议。


虚拟语气:
If they co-opted the technology, they would have a significant advantage. = 如果他们获得了这项技术，他们将拥有显著的优势。
I wish they hadn't co-opted our ideas without permission. = 我希望他们没有未经许可就窃取我们的想法。


被动语态:
The ideas were co-opted by the mainstream media. = 这些观点被主流媒体所采用。
The local government was co-opted by the powerful corporation. = 地方政府被强大的公司所控制。


疑问句:
Were the new policies co-opted from a foreign government? = 这些新政策是从外国政府那里借鉴的吗？
Has the company co-opted any of its competitors' technologies? = 这家公司是否采用了其任何竞争对手的技术？


否定句:
They did not co-opt any of the opposition's policies. = 他们没有采用反对派的任何政策。
The company refused to co-opt the outdated technology. = 该公司拒绝采用过时的技术。
