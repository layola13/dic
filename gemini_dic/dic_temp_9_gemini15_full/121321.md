misframe:/mɪsˈfreɪm/| vt.|曲解；歪曲；误解|To represent (something) in a false or misleading way.|例句：The media misframed the politician's comments, leading to public outrage.媒体歪曲了这位政治家的言论，导致公众愤怒。|近义词：distort, misrepresent, misconstrue|反义词：represent accurately, clarify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The media misframes many political issues. = 媒体歪曲了许多政治问题。
He often misframes his opponents' arguments. = 他经常曲解对手的论点。

一般过去时:
The newspaper misframed the story about the accident. = 报纸歪曲了关于事故的报道。
She misframed her intentions in the email. = 她在邮件中曲解了自己的意图。

现在完成时:
They have misframed the debate for years. = 他们多年来一直在歪曲这场辩论。

过去完成时:
He realized that he had misframed the situation from the beginning. = 他意识到他从一开始就误解了情况。

将来时:
