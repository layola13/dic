jocundly:/ˈdʒɒkəndli/|/ˈdʒɑːkʌndli/| adv.|愉快地；高兴地；欢乐地|In a merry, cheerful, and lighthearted manner.|例句：The children laughed jocundly as they played in the park.孩子们在公园里玩耍时愉快地笑着。

例句：She sang jocundly, her voice filled with joy. 她快乐地唱歌，歌声充满了喜悦。


近义词： merrily, cheerfully, joyfully, gaily, blithely

反义词：sadly, gloomily, sorrowfully, dejectedly
