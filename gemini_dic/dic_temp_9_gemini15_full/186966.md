riser:/ˈraɪzər/| n.|踏板，升起物，升高器 | A riser is a vertical part of a staircase between two steps. It can also refer to something that rises or lifts something else.  | 例句：The risers on the stairs were quite steep.  楼梯的踏板很陡。 The hydraulic riser lifted the platform smoothly.液压升降机平稳地抬起了平台。|近义词：step, elevator, lift |反义词：descender, lowerer
