correcter: /kəˈrektər/ | n. | 校正者；改正者；修正器 | A person or thing that corrects something.  It can refer to a person who proofreads and corrects texts or a machine that automatically corrects errors. |  例句：The editor is a meticulous corrector of grammar and style.  编辑是一位细致的语法和风格校正者。


例句：The spell checker is a useful corrector for writers. 拼写检查器对作家来说是一个有用的校正工具。


近义词：proofreader, editor, reviser, emendator


反义词：erroneous, incorrect


词性: noun
