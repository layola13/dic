cloud:/klaʊd/| n.|云；云状物；阴影；一群；大量|A visible mass of condensed water vapor floating in the atmosphere, typically high above the ground.  It can also refer to something resembling a cloud in appearance or nature, such as a cloud of smoke or a cloud of insects.  Figuratively, it can represent a feeling of uncertainty or gloom.|例句：Dark clouds gathered overhead.  乌云密布在头顶。|例句：A cloud of dust rose from the road. 一阵尘土从路上扬起。|近义词：mist, fog, haze, mass|反义词：clearness, sunshine
