celtic-crosses:/ˈsɛltɪk ˈkrɒsɪz/ | n. | 凯尔特十字架（复数） | Celtic crosses are a type of cross with a ring or circle surrounding the intersection of the arms.  They are a common symbol in Celtic art and are often associated with Christianity and Irish/Scottish culture.  | Example: The ancient graveyard was filled with intricately carved Celtic crosses.  古老的墓地里充满了雕刻精美的凯尔特十字架。  The museum showcased a collection of beautiful Celtic crosses from various regions. 博物馆展出了来自不同地区的精美凯尔特十字架收藏。 | 近义词：Celtic cross (单数),  high crosses | 反义词：无
