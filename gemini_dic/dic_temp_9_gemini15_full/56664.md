donzel:/dɒnˈzɛl/ | n. | 少女，贵妇，侍女 | A young unmarried woman, especially of noble birth; a maiden.  The word often implies innocence and purity, sometimes with a connotation of vulnerability. | Example: The donzel knelt before the king, offering him a goblet of wine.  这位少女跪在国王面前，献上一杯酒。|近义词：maiden, damsel, young woman |反义词：crone, old woman
