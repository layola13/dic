deeding:/ˈdiːdɪŋ/| n.| 授予；给予；行为；行动；(尤指)地产转让|The act of giving or granting something, especially the transfer of property ownership through a deed.  It often refers to the legal process of transferring ownership of land or property.|例句：The deeding of the land to the charity was finalized last week.  上周土地赠予慈善机构的交易最终完成。 The deeding of the property to his son was a contentious issue. 将房产过户给他儿子的事是一个有争议的问题。|近义词：conveyance, transfer, grant, bestowal|反义词：receiving, retention


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer is deeding the land to his daughter. = 农民正在把土地过户给他女儿。
She is deeding her inheritance to the orphanage. = 她正在把她的遗产捐赠给孤儿院。


一般过去时:
He deeded the property to his wife. = 他把房产过户给了他的妻子。
They deeded the house to their children. = 他们把房子过户给了他们的孩子们。


现在完成时:
He has deeded all his assets to his family. = 他已经把他的所有资产都过户给了他的家人。
She has deeded the building to the university. = 她已经把那栋楼捐赠给了大学。


过去完成时:
Before his death, he had deeded his estate to a trust. = 他去世前，已将他的遗产赠予信托基金。
They had deeded the farm to their nephew before moving to the city. = 他们在搬到城市之前，已经把农场过户给了他们的侄子。


将来时:
She will deed the house to her grandson upon her death. = 她去世后将把房子过户给她的孙子。
He will deed the property to the museum next month. = 他下个月将把房产捐赠给博物馆。


虚拟语气:
If he were still alive, he would deed the land to his son. = 如果他还活着，他会把土地过户给他儿子的。
I wish I had deeded the property sooner. = 我真希望我早点把房产过户了。


被动语态:
The property was deeded to the new owner. = 该房产已过户给新业主。
The land was deeded to the conservation group. = 这块土地已过户给环保组织。


疑问句:
Was the land deeded correctly? = 土地过户是否正确？
Will the property be deeded this week? = 这处房产本周会过户吗？


否定句:
He did not deed the house to his brother. = 他没有把房子过户给他兄弟。
She will not deed her estate to a stranger. = 她不会把她的遗产过户给一个陌生人。
