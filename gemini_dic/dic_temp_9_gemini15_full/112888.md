luddites:/ˈlʌdaɪts/| n. |卢德分子，反对技术进步者|People who oppose technological progress, especially the introduction of new machinery.|The Luddites smashed the new weaving machines in the early 19th century.  十九世纪初，卢德分子捣毁了新的织布机。| opponents of technological progress, technology haters | supporters of technological progress| 名词

一般现在时:
The Luddites oppose technological advancements. = 卢德分子反对技术进步。
Many people are still Luddites at heart. = 许多人内心深处仍然是卢德分子。


一般过去时:
The Luddites destroyed many machines during the Industrial Revolution. = 在工业革命期间，卢德分子摧毁了许多机器。
Early Luddites feared the loss of jobs due to automation. = 早期的卢德分子担心自动化导致失业。


现在完成时:
The movement has inspired many discussions about technological progress. =  这场运动激起了许多关于技术进步的讨论。
Historians have studied the Luddite movement extensively. = 历史学家们广泛研究了卢德运动。


过去完成时:
Before the Luddite movement, many workers had already suffered from job displacement. = 在卢德运动之前，许多工人已经遭受了失业的痛苦。
The Luddites had already lost their jobs because of the new machines. = 由于新机器的出现，卢德分子已经失业了。


将来时:
Some fear that future Luddites will oppose artificial intelligence. = 有些人担心未来的卢德分子会反对人工智能。
