It implies a return to a previous allegiance or a shift back to an earlier position after a prior defection.|Example: His redefection to the opposition party surprised everyone.  他的再次叛逃到反对党令所有人震惊。|近义词：backsliding, relapse, recantation|反义词：loyalty, fidelity, steadfastness|名词
