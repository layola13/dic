quasi-considerate:/ˈkwɑːzaɪkənˈsɪdərət/|/ˈkwɑːzi kənˈsɪdərɪt/| adj. |似体谅的；貌似体贴的 | Seemingly considerate, but actually lacking genuine empathy or concern.  It suggests a superficial display of consideration rather than true thoughtfulness. |He offered a quasi-considerate apology, but his tone betrayed a lack of remorse. 他道了个貌似体贴的歉，但语气却暴露了他缺乏悔意。|近义词：pretendly considerate, superficially thoughtful |反义词：inconsiderate, thoughtless, callous
