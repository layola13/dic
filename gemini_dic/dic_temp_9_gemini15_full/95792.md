inconvenienced:/ˌɪnkənˈviːniənst/ | adj. |  造成不便的；感到不便的 |  To cause someone to experience trouble or difficulty; to be troubled or made uncomfortable. |例句：I apologize for any inconvenience caused by the delay.  很抱歉延误给您带来不便。|例句：The noisy construction work inconvenienced the residents. 嘈杂的施工给居民带来了不便。|近义词：bothered, troubled, disrupted |反义词：convenient, comfortable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The noisy neighbors inconvenience me every day. = 吵闹的邻居每天都让我感到不便。
This new policy inconveniences many small businesses. = 这项新政策给许多小型企业带来了不便。


一般过去时:
The flight delay inconvenienced many passengers. = 航班延误给许多乘客带来了不便。
The sudden power outage inconvenienced everyone in the building. = 突然的停电给大楼里的每个人都带来了不便。


现在完成时:
The constant changes have inconvenienced the staff. = 持续不断的变化给员工带来了不便。
The frequent road closures have inconvenienced commuters for months. = 频繁的道路封闭已经给通勤者带来了数月的困扰。


过去完成时:
The storm had inconvenienced travelers for days before the roads reopened. = 在道路重新开放之前，暴风雨已经给旅行者带来了好几天的不便。
They had been inconvenienced by the lack of public transport for weeks. = 他们已经因为缺乏公共交通而感到不便了好几个星期了。


将来时:
The new construction project will inconvenience residents for at least a year. = 新的建筑项目将至少给居民带来一年的不便。
The planned roadworks will inconvenience drivers next week. = 计划中的道路工程将在下周给司机带来不便。


虚拟语气:
If the train were on time, it wouldn't have inconvenienced us so much. = 如果火车准时，就不会给我们带来这么大的不便了。
I wish the bad weather hadn't inconvenienced our trip. = 我真希望糟糕的天气没有影响我们的旅行。


被动语态:
Many people were inconvenienced by the traffic jam. = 许多人因为交通堵塞而感到不便。
The customers were inconvenienced by the store's late opening. = 顾客因为商店开门晚而感到不便。


疑问句:
Were you inconvenienced by the change of plans? = 计划的改变给你带来不便了吗？
How were you inconvenienced by the delay? = 延误给你带来了哪些不便？


否定句:
The new system wasn't designed to inconvenience anyone. = 新系统的设计并非为了给任何人带来不便。
The hotel staff tried their best not to inconvenience the guests. = 酒店工作人员尽力避免给客人带来不便。
