dystocia:/dɪsˈtoʊʃə/| n. |难产，分娩困难 |Difficult or prolonged childbirth;  obstructed labor. It can be caused by various factors related to the mother, the fetus, or the birth canal. | Example: The woman experienced dystocia due to the baby's large size.  这位妇女因婴儿体型过大而经历了难产。|近义词：difficult labor, obstructed labor, prolonged labor |反义词：easy delivery, normal delivery
