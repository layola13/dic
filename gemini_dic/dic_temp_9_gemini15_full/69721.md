feodal:/ˈfiːdəl/| adj.|封建的，封建制度的|Relating to or denoting the feudal system, a medieval system of land tenure where land was granted by a lord in return for service or labour.|例句：The feudal system was characterized by a hierarchical social structure.  封建制度的特点是等级森严的社会结构。
例句：Many European countries had feudal systems during the Middle Ages. 中世纪许多欧洲国家都实行封建制度。|近义词：feudalistic|反义词：modern, democratic
