/ˌɪntərˈpærɪʃ/| adj. |教区间的；跨教区的| Relating to or involving more than one parish.  The word is a combination of "inter-" (meaning "between" or "among") and "parish" (a church district).|The interparish council met to discuss the upcoming fundraising event.  教区间委员会会面讨论即将到来的筹款活动。|近义词：inter-ecclesiastical (if referring to churches)|反义词：intraparish (within a single parish)
