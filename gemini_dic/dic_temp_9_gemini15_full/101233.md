invites:/ɪnˈvaɪts/ | vt. |邀请，恳请|To ask (someone) to come to a social event.|例句：She invites her friends to her birthday party. 她邀请她的朋友参加她的生日派对。|近义词：ask, summon, request |反义词：refuse, reject |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He invites his friends to dinner every week. 他每周都邀请朋友们吃饭。
They invite guests to their home for the holidays. 他们邀请客人来家里过节。

一般过去时:
She invited me to her wedding last year. 去年她邀请我参加她的婚礼。
They invited all their classmates to the graduation ceremony. 他们邀请了所有同学参加毕业典礼。

现在完成时:
He has invited many people to his conference. 他已经邀请很多人参加他的会议了。
She has invited us to her house for a barbecue several times. 她已经几次邀请我们去她家参加烧烤了。

过去完成时:
I had invited them to the party before realizing they were already busy. 我在意识到他们已经很忙之前就邀请他们参加派对了。
She had invited her family to stay before she remembered the limited space in her apartment. 她在她想起公寓空间有限之前就邀请家人来住了。

将来时:
We will invite them to the celebration next month. 我们下个月会邀请他们参加庆祝活动。
They will invite their parents to the school play. 他们将邀请他们的父母来看学校的演出。


虚拟语气:
I wish I had invited her to my party. 我真希望我邀请她参加我的派对。
If I were richer, I would invite all my friends on a trip. 如果我有钱一点，我会邀请所有朋友一起旅行。

被动语态:
He was invited to the prestigious conference. 他被邀请参加了这个著名的会议。
Many people were invited to the wedding celebration. 许多人被邀请参加婚礼庆典。

疑问句:
Did you invite John to the party? 你邀请约翰参加派对了吗？
Will they invite us to their housewarming party? 他们会邀请我们参加他们的乔迁派对吗？

否定句:
I didn't invite him to the meeting. 我没有邀请他参加会议。
They won't invite her to the dinner party. 他们不会邀请她参加晚宴。


PHRASE->
invite someone to do something  邀请某人做某事
invite trouble  自找麻烦
invite criticism  招致批评
an open invitation  公开邀请
a formal invitation 正式邀请
