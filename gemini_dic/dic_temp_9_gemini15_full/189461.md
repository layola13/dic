sainthood:/ˈseɪntˌhʊd/| n. |圣徒身份；圣徒地位 |The state of being a saint; the condition of being canonized.  It refers to the recognition of someone as a saint by a religious authority, often involving a formal process of beatification and canonization. |例句：Many aspire to sainthood, but few achieve it.  许多人都渴望成为圣徒，但很少有人能做到。

例句：The process leading to her sainthood was long and complex.通往她成为圣徒的道路漫长而复杂。


近义词：canonization, sanctification, holiness


反义词：wickedness, sinfulness, damnation


词性:名词
