forgiving: /fərˈɡɪvɪŋ/ | adj. | 宽容的，易于原谅的 | readily willing to forgive and be lenient.  |例句：She's a very forgiving person; she always gives people a second chance.  她是一个非常宽容的人；她总是给人们第二次机会。

例句：He was surprisingly forgiving of his brother's betrayal. 他对他兄弟的背叛出奇地宽容。


近义词：lenient, merciful, tolerant, understanding, compassionate

反义词： unforgiving, unforgiving, vengeful, resentful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is forgiving of others' mistakes. 他宽容别人的错误。
She is always forgiving to those who apologize sincerely. 她总是原谅那些真诚道歉的人。


一般过去时:
She was forgiving even when it was difficult. 即使很难，她也依然宽容。
He was more forgiving than I expected. 他比我预料的更宽容。


现在完成时:
He has been forgiving throughout his life. 他一生都很宽容。
She has always been forgiving, even to those who hurt her deeply. 她一直都很宽容，即使是对那些深深伤害她的人。


过去完成时:
They had been forgiving of each other's flaws for many years. 他们多年来一直互相包容对方的缺点。
I had already forgiven him before he apologized.  在他道歉之前我已经原谅他了。


将来时:
I will be more forgiving in the future. 我将来会更加宽容。
She will likely be forgiving if you apologize sincerely. 如果你真诚道歉，她很可能会原谅你。


虚拟语气:
If I were more forgiving, I would have a happier life. 如果我更宽容一些，我会过得更快乐。
I wish I were more forgiving towards my family. 我希望我能对我的家人更宽容一些。


被动语态:
