seizures:/ˈsiːʒərz/| n.|癫痫发作；突然发作；夺取；没收|Sudden, uncontrolled bursts of electrical activity in the brain.  Seizures can cause a variety of symptoms, from brief periods of staring to violent convulsions.|例句：He suffered from seizures as a child. 他小时候患有癫痫。|例句：The police made a seizure of illegal drugs.警方查获了非法毒品。|近义词：convulsions, fits, attacks, confiscations|反义词：control, calmness
