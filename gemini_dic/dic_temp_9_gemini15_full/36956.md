colourists:/ˈkʌlərists/ | n. |彩色学家；着色家 | A colourist is an artist or designer who is skilled in the use of color, or a person who applies color to something.  They understand the theory and practice of color mixing and application, and how color affects the overall aesthetic impact of a work of art or design. | The colourists of the Renaissance were masters of creating vibrant and realistic scenes.  文艺复兴时期的彩色学家们是创造充满活力和逼真场景的大师。

The film's colourist achieved a stunning visual effect. 电影的色彩师取得了令人惊叹的视觉效果。| 近义词：colorist, artist (in the context of color), designer | 反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who works in monochrome or avoids color.
