scuba-diving:/ˈskuːbəˌdaɪvɪŋ/| n. |水肺潜水| Scuba diving is the activity of exploring underwater environments using self-contained underwater breathing apparatus (scuba).  It allows divers to explore depths beyond what is possible with snorkeling. |Example:  He went scuba diving in the Great Barrier Reef and saw amazing coral. 他在大堡礁潜水，看到了令人惊叹的珊瑚。

Example:  My sister is planning a scuba-diving trip to the Maldives next month. 我姐姐计划下个月去马尔代夫潜水旅行。


近义词：underwater diving, deep-sea diving|反义词：None.

词源:  The word "scuba" is an acronym for "Self-Contained Underwater Breathing Apparatus".  "Diving" is from the Old English word "dūfan," meaning "to dive."  Therefore, scuba-diving is a compound word combining the acronym for the equipment with the action of diving.
