gatekeeping:/ˈɡeɪtkiːpɪŋ/ | n. | 门控；把关；控制信息传播 | The act of controlling access to something, such as information, opportunities, or resources.  This often involves selectively allowing or denying entry based on certain criteria. | 例句：The media's gatekeeping role in shaping public opinion is a subject of much debate.  媒体在塑造公众舆论方面的把关作用是一个备受争议的话题。

The university's strict gatekeeping policies prevented many qualified applicants from being admitted. 大学严格的把关政策阻止了许多合格的申请者被录取。


近义词：control, censorship, filtering, regulation, selection | 反义词：open access, free flow, unrestricted access | 词性:名词
