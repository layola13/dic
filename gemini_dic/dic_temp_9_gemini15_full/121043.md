misconducting:/ˌmɪskənˈdʌktɪŋ/ | v. |行为不端；处理不当 |  The act of behaving improperly or managing something badly; often implies a breach of rules or ethical standards.  | Example: The doctor was accused of misconducting himself during the surgery. 医生被指控在手术过程中行为不端。  The company was fined for misconducting its financial affairs.  该公司因财务管理不善而被罚款。 |近义词：misbehaving, mishandling, maladministering | 反义词：conducting properly, managing well, behaving ethically | 动词
