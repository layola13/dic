reidentifying: /ˌriːaɪˈdɛntɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 再识别；重新识别 | The act of identifying something or someone again, often after a period of time or after some change has occurred.  It implies a previous identification. | 例句：The police are reidentifying the suspect from blurry security footage.  警方正在从模糊的监控录像中重新识别嫌疑人。

例句：The researchers are reidentifying the individuals in the dataset to ensure accuracy. 研究人员正在重新识别数据集中的人员以确保准确性。


近义词：re-identifying, re-recognizing, identifying again, confirming identity


反义词：losing track of, misidentifying, anonymizing


词性：动词 (present participle of reidentify)
