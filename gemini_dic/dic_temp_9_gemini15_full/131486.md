noncollaboration: /ˌnɒnkəʊlæbəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 不合作；缺乏合作 | The lack of cooperation or willingness to work together.  It often implies a deliberate refusal to cooperate, rather than simply a failure to do so. | Example: The project failed due to the noncollaboration between the two teams.  这个项目由于两个团队之间的不合作而失败了。 | 近义词：uncooperativeness, obstruction, resistance, antagonism | 反义词：collaboration, cooperation, teamwork
