scientisms:/ˈsaɪəntɪsɪzəm/ | n. | 科学主义；科学说法 | The excessive belief in the power of science to solve all problems, often to the exclusion of other perspectives.  It can also refer to the use of scientific-sounding language to impress or obfuscate. | Example:  The article's scientisms obscured its central argument.  文章中充斥着科学术语，掩盖了其核心论点。 | 近义词：reductionism, positivism, technocracy | 反义词：humanism, idealism, mysticism |  名词
