free:/friː/| adj., n., v.| 自由的；免费的；空闲的 |  Free means not controlled or restricted by anyone or anything. As a noun, it refers to freedom or exemption. As a verb, it means to release or liberate. | 例句：The birds are free to fly. (鸟儿自由飞翔。)  The museum offers free admission on Tuesdays. (博物馆周二免费入场。) |近义词：liberated, independent, gratis, exempt |反义词： captive, bound, enslaved, occupied, restricted, charged


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is free to go. = 他可以走了。
The birds are free in the sky. = 鸟儿在天上自由飞翔。


一般过去时:
She was free from her worries. = 她摆脱了烦恼。
The prisoner was freed yesterday. = 囚犯昨天被释放了。


现在完成时:
I have been free since last year. = 我从去年开始就自由了。
They have freed many animals from the zoo. = 他们已经从动物园里解救了许多动物。


过去完成时:
He had been free for many years before he was arrested again. = 在他再次被捕之前，他已经自由了很多年。
The slaves had been freed before the war ended. = 在战争结束之前，奴隶们已经被解放了。


将来时:
I will be free next week. = 我下周有空。
They will free the hostages soon. = 他们很快就会释放人质。


虚拟语气:
If I were free, I would travel the world. = 如果我有空，我会周游世界。
I wish I were free from this burden. = 我希望我能摆脱这个负担。


被动语态:
He was freed from prison. = 他被释放出狱。
The country was freed from colonial rule. = 这个国家摆脱了殖民统治。


疑问句:
Are you free this evening? = 你今晚有空吗？
Is the software free to use? = 这个软件免费使用吗？


否定句:
I am not free to talk now. = 我现在没空说话。
The prisoners were not freed. = 囚犯们没有被释放。


PHRASE->
free of charge = 免费的
free from = 免于；摆脱
set free = 释放
free for all = 自由竞争
free and easy = 自由自在
