connective:/kəˈnɛktɪv/ | adj., n. |连接词；连接的；关联的 |A connective is a word or phrase that connects words, phrases, clauses, or sentences.  It shows the relationship between the connected elements.  Connectives can be conjunctions, prepositions, adverbs, or phrases. |Examples:  The sentence "The sun is shining, and the birds are singing" uses "and" as a connective to show addition.  The phrase "in spite of the rain" uses "in spite of" as a connective to show contrast. |近义词：linking word, joining word, conjunction |反义词：disjunctive (in some contexts)
