outmoving:/ˌaʊtˈmuːvɪŋ/| v.|超越，胜过，超过|The act of surpassing or exceeding someone or something in movement, speed, or progress.|例句：The cheetah outmoved the gazelle, catching it easily. 猎豹超越了瞪羚，轻松地抓住了它。|例句：His innovative strategy outmoved his competitors in the market. 他创新的策略在市场上超越了他的竞争对手。|近义词：surpassing, outpacing, outstripping|反义词：lagging behind, falling short


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outmoves his opponents in every match. = 他在每一场比赛中都超越了他的对手。
The company outmoves its competitors in innovation. = 这家公司在创新方面超越了其竞争对手。


一般过去时:
She outmoved her rival in the race. = 她在比赛中超过了她的对手。
The army outmoved the enemy forces. = 军队超越了敌军。


现在完成时:
They have outmoved the previous record holders. = 他们已经超越了之前的纪录保持者。
The project has outmoved its initial timeline. = 项目已经超过了最初的时间表。


过去完成时:
The team had outmoved the other teams by the end of the first half. = 上半场结束时，该队已经超越了其他队。
The runner had outmoved the others before the final lap. = 在最后一圈之前，跑步者已经超过了其他人。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If we worked harder, we could outmove our competitors. = 如果我们更努力工作，我们就能超越竞争对手。
I wish I could outmove my limitations. = 我希望我能超越我的局限性。


被动语态:
The old record was outmoved by the new athlete. = 旧纪录被新的运动员超越了。
The project's deadline was outmoved by unforeseen circumstances. = 项目的截止日期被不可预见的情况推迟了。


疑问句:

否定句:
