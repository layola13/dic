harass:/həˈræs/| vt.|骚扰，烦扰，使烦恼|To annoy or bother persistently.  |例句：He was constantly harassed by the paparazzi. 他不断遭到狗仔队的骚扰。|例句：She harassed her neighbor with late-night noise. 她用深夜的噪音骚扰她的邻居。|近义词：pester, badger, annoy, bother |反义词：soothe, comfort, appease


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They harass him daily. = 他们每天都骚扰他。
She harasses her colleagues with constant complaints. = 她不断抱怨，骚扰她的同事。

一般过去时:
The bully harassed the smaller children. = 那个恶霸骚扰那些年纪较小的孩子。
He harassed me with phone calls. = 他用电话骚扰我。

现在完成时:
They have harassed him for months. = 他们已经骚扰他好几个月了。
She has harassed the landlord repeatedly about the repairs. = 她一再骚扰房东要求修理。

过去完成时:
He had harassed her for years before she finally reported it. = 在她最终举报之前，他已经骚扰她多年了。
They had harassed the witnesses before the trial. = 他们在审判前骚扰了证人。

将来时:
They will continue to harass him until he gives in. = 他们会继续骚扰他，直到他屈服为止。
She will harass him again if he doesn't comply. = 如果他不遵守，她会再次骚扰他。

虚拟语气:
If he harassed her again, she would call the police. = 如果他再次骚扰她，她会报警。
I wish they hadn't harassed me so much. = 我真希望他们没有那么骚扰我。

被动语态:
He was harassed by the police. = 他遭到警方的骚扰。
The victim was harassed online. = 受害者在网上遭到骚扰。

疑问句:
Are they harassing you? = 他们正在骚扰你吗？
Has she been harassed before? = 她以前被骚扰过吗？


否定句:
They didn't harass her. = 他们没有骚扰她。
He wouldn't harass anyone. = 他不会骚扰任何人。


PHRASE->
harass someone relentlessly = 不断地骚扰某人
harass someone sexually = 性骚扰某人
harass someone verbally = 语言骚扰某人
harass someone with phone calls = 用电话骚扰某人
harass someone for money = 勒索某人


词源:
From Old French *harasser, from harasse "pack-saddle, burden, pack," from haras "stud farm," of uncertain origin.  The sense development is from "to burden" to "to weary" to "to annoy persistently."
