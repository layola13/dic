etymology:/ˌɛtɪˈmɒlədʒi/ | /ˌɛtɪˈmɑːlədʒi/| n. | 词源学，语源学 | The study of the origin of words and the way in which their meanings have changed throughout history.  It involves tracing words back to their earliest known forms and analyzing how their sounds and meanings have evolved.  | Example:  The etymology of "nice" is surprising; it originally meant "foolish" or "ignorant".  |近义词：word origin, derivation | 反义词：无
