fence-mendings:/ˈfɛnsˌmɛndɪŋz/| n.|篱笆修补；调解纠纷；和解|The act of repairing fences, both literally and metaphorically.  It refers to the process of restoring relationships or resolving conflicts. |例句：After a long argument, they engaged in some serious fence-mendings.  一场长时间的争论后，他们进行了认真的和解。

例句：The farmer spent the afternoon doing fence-mendings. 农民花了整个下午修补篱笆。


近义词：reconciliation, reconciliation, peacemaking, rapprochement, settlement|反义词：estrangement, alienation, discord
