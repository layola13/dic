readership:/ˈriːdəʃɪp/| n. |读者群，读者人数 |The readership of this magazine is primarily young adults. 这本杂志的读者群主要是年轻人。|近义词：audience, subscribers |反义词：无


英文解析:The number or type of people who read a particular publication.  The collective body of readers of a publication.

例句:

The newspaper boasts a large readership. 这家报纸拥有大量的读者。

The magazine's readership has increased significantly this year. 这本杂志的读者人数今年显著增加。


词性:名词
