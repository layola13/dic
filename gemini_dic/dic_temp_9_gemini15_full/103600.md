join hands:/dʒɔɪn hændz/| phrasal verb| 携手；合作；联合|To join hands means to work together, to cooperate, or to unite in a common cause or effort. It often implies a sense of partnership and mutual support.|例句：Let's join hands to build a better future. 让我们携手共建美好未来。

例句：The two companies joined hands to develop a new product. 两家公司联手开发了一种新产品。


近义词：cooperate, collaborate, unite, combine|反义词：separate, divide, oppose


PHRASE->
join hands in marriage: 结婚
join hands to fight:  联手抗击
join hands against:  联合对抗
join hands with: 与...携手
join hands and hearts:  心手相连


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They join hands to overcome difficulties. = 他们携手克服困难。
We join hands in this project. = 我们在这个项目中携手合作。

一般过去时:
They joined hands to finish the task. = 他们携手完成了任务。
We joined hands to build the house. = 我们携手建造了这所房子。

现在完成时:
We have joined hands to achieve this goal. = 我们已经携手实现了这个目标。
They have joined hands in many projects. = 他们已经合作过很多项目了。

过去完成时:
They had joined hands before the competition began. = 比赛开始前他们就已经联手了。
We had joined hands on similar projects before. = 我们之前已经在类似的项目中合作过。

将来时:
We will join hands to solve this problem. = 我们将携手解决这个问题。
They will join hands to create a better world. = 他们将携手创造一个更美好的世界。

虚拟语气:
If we joined hands, we could achieve more. = 如果我们携手合作，我们可以取得更大的成就。
I wish we had joined hands earlier. = 我希望我们早点携手合作。

被动语态:
Their hands were joined in marriage. = 他们的手在婚礼上被握在一起。（被动语态在此处略显不自然，很少使用）
The project was completed after the two teams joined hands. = 在两个团队合作之后，项目完成了。（此例强调合作的结果）


疑问句:
Will they join hands on this project? = 他们会在这个项目上合作吗？
Have they joined hands with our competitors? = 他们和我们的竞争对手合作了吗？

否定句:
They did not join hands in the past. = 他们过去没有合作过。
We will not join hands with such an unreliable company. = 我们不会与这样不可靠的公司合作。
