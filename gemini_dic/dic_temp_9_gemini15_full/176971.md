realm:/relm/| n.|领域，范围，王国|a kingdom; a field of study or activity; a sphere of influence or activity|例句：The realm of science is vast and ever-expanding. 科学的领域是广阔且不断发展的。

例句：She ruled over a vast and powerful realm. 她统治着一个广阔而强大的王国。|近义词：kingdom, domain, sphere, region|反义词：point, detail

This painting belongs to the realm of classical art. 这幅画属于古典艺术的领域。
The scientist's research falls within the realm of theoretical physics. 科学家的研究属于理论物理学的范畴。

The king's realm extended far beyond the river. 国王的领土延伸到河的远方。

过去完成时:

The artist had already established himself in the realm of abstract painting. 艺术家已经在抽象画领域确立了自己的地位。

虚拟语气:
