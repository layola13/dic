maharanees:/ˌmɑːhəˈreɪniːz/| n.|马哈拉尼（印度王公的妻子）|The word "maharani" refers to the wife or queen consort of a maharaja (a king or prince in India).  It is a title of respect and indicates high social standing.  The term is derived from Sanskrit and reflects the hierarchical structure of historical Indian royalty.|The Maharani of Jodhpur was known for her philanthropy.|近义词：queen, princess (in context)|反义词：commoner
