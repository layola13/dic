preredeem:/ˌpriːriˈdiːm/| v.|预先赎回，提前赎回|To redeem (a security, etc.) before its scheduled maturity date.|The company allowed investors to preredeem their bonds.|近义词：redeem early, prepay|反义词：defer, postpone


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He preredeems his bonds every year. = 他每年都提前赎回债券。
The company preredeems its shares to reduce capital. = 公司提前赎回股份以减少资本。


一般过去时:
They preredeemed their investment last month. = 他们上个月提前赎回了投资。
She preredeemed her certificate of deposit early to cover an emergency. = 她提前赎回了她的存款证明以应付紧急情况。


现在完成时:
We have preredeemed several bonds this quarter. = 本季度我们已经提前赎回了多笔债券。
They have not yet preredeemed their shares. = 他们尚未提前赎回股份。


过去完成时:
The investor had preredeemed his shares before the market crash. = 投资者在市场崩盘前已提前赎回股份。
Before the announcement, they had already preredeemed a significant portion of their holdings. = 在公告之前，他们已经提前赎回了一大部分持股。


将来时:
We will preredeem our investments if interest rates rise. = 如果利率上升，我们将提前赎回投资。
She will preredeem her mortgage if she receives a bonus. = 如果她收到奖金，她将提前偿还抵押贷款。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would preredeem my loan. = 如果我有更多钱，我就会提前偿还贷款。
I wish I could preredeem my bonds, but there are penalties. = 我希望我能提前赎回债券，但是有罚款。


被动语态:
The bonds were preredeemed by the issuer last week. = 债券在上周被发行人提前赎回。
The shares are being preredeemed to improve the company's balance sheet. = 股份正在被提前赎回以改善公司的资产负债表。


疑问句:
Can I preredeem my savings bonds? = 我可以提前赎回我的储蓄债券吗？
Will the company preredeem its debt before the deadline? = 公司会在截止日期前提前赎回债务吗？


否定句:
They did not preredeem their assets. = 他们没有提前赎回资产。
The bank does not allow preredemption of this type of account. = 银行不允许提前赎回这种类型的账户。


PHRASE->
preredemption penalty = 提前赎回罚款
preredemption request = 提前赎回申请
preredemption notice = 提前赎回通知
preredemption date = 提前赎回日期
preredemption option = 提前赎回选择权
