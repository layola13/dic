rubious:/ˈruːbiəs/| adj.|微红的，略带红色的；酒红色的|Slightly red; ruddy; reddish.  The word often implies a slightly unhealthy or flushed redness, suggesting a blush or sunburn.  It is less commonly used than its synonyms.|例句：Her cheeks were rubious after her run. 她跑步后脸颊微红。

例句：The sunset cast a rubious glow on the mountains. 夕阳给山峦披上了一层酒红色的光芒。


近义词：ruddy, reddish, rosy, flushed

反义词：pale, wan, ashen


词性：形容词
