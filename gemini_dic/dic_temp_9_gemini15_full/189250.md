saddest:/ˈsædəst/| adj.|最悲伤的，最令人伤心的 | Most sorrowful; causing the greatest sadness.  The saddest day of my life was when my grandmother passed away.  |近义词：most sorrowful, most mournful, most depressing |反义词：happiest, most joyful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The story is the saddest I've ever read. = 这个故事是我读过的最悲伤的故事。
This music sounds the saddest. = 这音乐听起来最悲伤。


一般过去时:
Yesterday was the saddest day of my life. = 昨天是我生命中最悲伤的一天。
The news made him the saddest. = 这个消息使他非常悲伤。


现在完成时:
That's the saddest thing I've ever heard. = 那是听过最悲伤的事情了。
This is the saddest movie I have ever seen. = 这是我看过的最悲伤的电影。


过去完成时:
It was the saddest moment he had ever experienced. = 这是他经历过的最悲伤的时刻。
She had never felt so saddest before. = 她以前从未感到如此悲伤。


将来时:
Tomorrow will likely be the saddest day. = 明天很可能会是最悲伤的一天。
The future seems saddest to me. = 未来对我来说似乎是最悲伤的。


虚拟语气:
If I knew it would be the saddest day, I would have stayed home. = 如果我知道那将是最悲伤的一天，我就待在家里了。
I wish it weren't the saddest day. = 我希望这不是最悲伤的一天。


被动语态:
The saddest story was selected for the award. = 最悲伤的故事被选中获奖了。

疑问句:

What is the saddest thing that's ever happened to you? = 你经历过的最悲伤的事情是什么？


否定句:
It wasn't the saddest movie I've ever seen. = 这不是我看过的最悲伤的电影。
The experience wasn't the saddest. = 那次经历并非最悲伤。
