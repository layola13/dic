reinstate:/ˌriːɪnˈsteɪt/| vt.|恢复职位；使恢复原状；恢复有效|To restore someone or something to their former position or state.|例句：The company reinstated him after he proved his innocence. 公司在他证明清白后恢复了他的职位。|例句：The judge reinstated the original verdict. 法官恢复了原判决。|近义词：restore, reestablish, reinstate|反义词：remove, dismiss, abolish


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reinstate employees after a period of leave. = 他们会在休假一段时间后恢复员工的职位。
The government reinstates the old policy. = 政府恢复了旧政策。


一般过去时:
The court reinstated his driving license. = 法院恢复了他的驾照。
The teacher reinstated the student after the suspension. = 教师在停学后恢复了学生的学籍。


现在完成时:
The company has reinstated its former CEO. = 公司已经恢复了前任首席执行官的职位。
They have reinstated the old rules. = 他们已经恢复了旧规。


过去完成时:
He had been reinstated before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他已经复职了。
The committee had reinstated the proposal before the final vote. = 在最终投票之前，委员会已经恢复了该提案。


将来时:
They will reinstate the service next week. = 他们下周将恢复这项服务。
The judge will reinstate the original sentence. = 法官将恢复原判。


虚拟语气:
If they reinstated him, the team would be stronger. = 如果他们恢复他的职位，球队就会更强大。
I wish they would reinstate the old system. = 我希望他们能恢复旧制度。


被动语态:
He was reinstated to his position after the investigation. = 调查后，他被恢复了职位。
The old law was reinstated by the parliament. = 议会恢复了旧法律。


疑问句:
Will they reinstate the program? = 他们会恢复这个项目吗？
Was the employee reinstated after the disciplinary action? = 纪律处分后，员工被恢复职位了吗？


否定句:
They did not reinstate the employee. = 他们没有恢复该员工的职位。
The government will not reinstate the tax. = 政府不会恢复这项税收。


PHRASE->
reinstate someone to their former position = 恢复某人以前的职位
reinstate someone in their job = 恢复某人的工作
reinstate a law = 恢复法律
reinstate a policy = 恢复政策
reinstate an agreement = 恢复协议
