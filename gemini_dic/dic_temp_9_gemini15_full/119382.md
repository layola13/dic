microcyte:/ˈmaɪkroʊsaɪt/| n. |微细胞|A microcyte is an abnormally small red blood cell.  This condition is often associated with various medical conditions, including iron deficiency anemia.  |例句：The blood test revealed a high number of microcytes, indicating a potential anemia.  血液测试显示大量的微细胞，表明可能有贫血。

The patient's microcytes were a significant finding in the diagnosis. 患者的微细胞是诊断中的重要发现。|近义词：small red blood cell |反义词：macrocyte
