overselling: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛlɪŋ/ | n. | 过度推销；吹嘘；言过其实 | The act of selling or promoting something too aggressively or exaggerating its qualities or benefits.  It can lead to disappointed customers and damage to reputation. | Example: The company's overselling of its new product led to a wave of customer complaints. 公司对新产品的过度宣传导致了客户投诉浪潮。| 近义词：hype, exaggeration, puffery | 反义词：underselling, downplaying


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The salesman oversells his products every day. = 销售员每天都过度推销他的产品。
They oversell the benefits of their new software. = 他们夸大了他们新软件的好处。


一般过去时:
He oversold the potential of the investment. = 他夸大了投资的潜力。
The marketing team oversold the features of the phone. = 营销团队夸大了手机的功能。


现在完成时:
They have oversold their capabilities to the client. = 他们已经向客户夸大了自己的能力。
The company has oversold its stock. = 公司已经超额发行股票了。


过去完成时:
The entrepreneur had oversold his vision to the investors. = 企业家曾向投资者夸大了他的远景。
They had oversold the tickets to the concert. = 他们已经超额售出了音乐会的门票。


将来时:
He will oversell the benefits of his new invention. = 他将夸大他的新发明的益处。
The company will likely oversell its products during the holiday season. = 公司很可能会在节日期间过度推销其产品。


虚拟语气:
If they hadn't oversold the product, they wouldn't have received so many complaints. = 如果他们没有过度推销产品，就不会收到这么多投诉。
I wish they hadn't oversold the experience; it was underwhelming. = 我希望他们没有夸大其词；体验令人失望。


被动语态:
The product was oversold by the marketing team. = 产品被营销团队过度推销了。
The benefits of the program were oversold in the brochure. = 该项目的益处在宣传册中被夸大了。


疑问句:

否定句:
The company did not oversell its services. = 公司没有过度推销其服务。
