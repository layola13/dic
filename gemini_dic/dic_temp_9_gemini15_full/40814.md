convolvulaceous:/ˌkɒnvɒlvjuˈleɪʃəs/| adj. |旋花科的，旋花状的|Belonging to or characteristic of the Convolvulaceae, a family of flowering plants that includes morning glories, bindweeds, and sweet potatoes.  The term often describes plants with twining or climbing stems and funnel-shaped flowers.|The convolvulaceous plants are known for their beautiful flowers.|近义词：twining, climbing|反义词：upright, erect
