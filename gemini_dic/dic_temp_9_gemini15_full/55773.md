diversion:/daɪˈvɜːʒən/ | n. |转移；消遣；转移注意力的事物 | A diversion is something that takes your attention away from something else, or a way of doing something different to avoid a problem or difficulty.  It can also refer to a turning aside from a course or path. |例句：The police set up a diversion to catch the robbers. = 警方设置了转移路线以抓住劫匪。

The comedian provided a welcome diversion from the serious speeches. = 喜剧演员提供了令人欣慰的轻松一刻，让人暂时忘掉严肃的演讲。

近义词：detour, distraction, pastime, entertainment |反义词：focus, concentration, straight path
