manioc:/ˈmænɪæk/| n. |木薯|A starchy tuberous root, Manihot esculenta, native to South America, that is a major source of carbohydrates in many tropical countries. It is also called cassava.  |Example:  Manioc is a staple crop in many parts of Africa.  木薯是非洲许多地区的 staple 作物。 |近义词：cassava, yuca |反义词：无 |名词
