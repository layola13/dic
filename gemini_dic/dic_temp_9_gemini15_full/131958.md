noncontrollably:/ˌnɒnkənˈtrəʊləbli/| adv. |不可控制地，不受控制地 |in a way that cannot be controlled; uncontrollably.  |The car skidded uncontrollably on the icy road. 汽车在结冰的路上不受控制地打滑。| uncontrollably, inevitably |controllably|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He laughs uncontrollably. = 他不受控制地大笑。
She cries uncontrollably. = 她不受控制地哭泣。

一般过去时:

The fire has spread uncontrollably. = 火势已经不受控制地蔓延了。

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
