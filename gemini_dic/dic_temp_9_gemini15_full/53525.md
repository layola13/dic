disatisfied:/ˌdɪsˈsætɪsfaɪd/| adj.|不满意的，不满足的|Feeling or expressing dissatisfaction.  It implies a lack of fulfillment or contentment with something. |例句：He was dissatisfied with his current job and decided to look for a new one. 他对目前的工作不满意，决定另找一份工作。

例句：The customers were dissatisfied with the poor quality of the product and demanded a refund.  顾客对产品的低劣质量感到不满，要求退款。


近义词：unhappy, displeased, disgruntled, discontent, frustrated| 反义词：satisfied, content, pleased, happy|词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is dissatisfied with his salary. = 他对自己的薪水不满意。
They are dissatisfied with the service they received. = 他们对收到的服务不满意。


一般过去时:
She was dissatisfied with the results of the exam. = 她对考试结果不满意。
We were dissatisfied with the hotel accommodations. = 我们对酒店住宿不满意。


现在完成时:
I have been dissatisfied with my life for a long time. = 我对我的生活很久都不满意了。
They have been dissatisfied with the management's decisions. = 他们一直对管理层的决定不满。


过去完成时:
He had been dissatisfied with his job for years before he finally quit. = 在他最终辞职之前，他已经对他的工作不满多年了。
She had been dissatisfied with her performance before receiving the feedback. = 在收到反馈之前，她对自己的表现一直不满意。


将来时:
I will be dissatisfied if they don't meet the deadline. = 如果他们没有按时完成，我会不满意。
He will be dissatisfied if the project fails to meet expectations. = 如果项目未能达到预期，他会感到不满。


虚拟语气:
I would be dissatisfied if I were in his shoes. = 如果我是他，我会感到不满。
She would have been dissatisfied if she had known the truth earlier. = 如果她早知道真相，她会不满意。


被动语态:
The management was dissatisfied with the employee's performance.  = 管理层对员工的表现不满意。（被动语态中，dissatisfied 作表语）
His request was met with dissatisfaction. = 他的请求遭到了不满。（被动语态， dissatisfaction 是名词形式）


疑问句:
Are you dissatisfied with your current situation? = 你对目前的情况不满吗？
Is he dissatisfied with the progress of the project? = 他对项目的进展不满吗？


否定句:
I am not dissatisfied with my job at all. = 我一点也不对我的工作不满。
He is not dissatisfied with the company's policy. = 他对公司的政策并不不满。

express dissatisfaction = 表达不满
