respectfully:/rɪˈspektfəli/ | adv. |恭敬地；尊敬地 | 表示以尊重和礼貌的方式；通常用于书信或正式场合。|例句：I respectfully disagree with your assessment.  我恭敬地不同意你的评价。|例句：I respectfully request your assistance. 我恭敬地请求你的帮助。|近义词：courteously, politely, deferentially |反义词：disrespectfully, rudely, insolently


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He respectfully requests a meeting. 他恭敬地请求会面。
She respectfully addresses the concerns. 她恭敬地处理这些担忧。

一般过去时:
He respectfully submitted his resignation. 他恭敬地递交了他的辞职信。
