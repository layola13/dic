outplacements:/ˌaʊtˈpleɪsmənts/| n.|人员安置；员工安置服务；解雇后的职业咨询|The process of assisting employees who have been made redundant or laid off in finding new jobs.  This often includes career counseling, resume writing assistance, and job search support.|例句：The company offered outplacement services to its laid-off employees. 这家公司为被解雇的员工提供人员安置服务。|例句：The outplacement firm helped her update her resume and refine her interviewing skills. 这家员工安置公司帮助她更新简历并提升面试技巧。|近义词：job placement, career transition services|反义词：employment, hiring
