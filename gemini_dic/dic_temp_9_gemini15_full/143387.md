outfights:/ˈaʊtfaɪts/| v.|打败，战胜，在战斗中胜过|To defeat or overcome in a fight or competition.  It implies a prolonged or superior fighting ability.|例句：The smaller boxer outfought his larger opponent with speed and agility.  较小的拳击手凭借速度和敏捷战胜了更大的对手。

例句：The experienced warrior outfought the younger, less skilled opponent.  经验丰富的战士战胜了年轻、技巧较差的对手。


近义词：defeat, overcome, outmaneuver, surpass


反义词：lose to, be defeated by, succumb to


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outfights his opponents with superior technique. = 他凭借高超的技术战胜对手。
She consistently outfights her rivals in the ring. = 她一直在拳台上战胜她的对手。


一般过去时:
The underdog outfought the champion in a stunning upset. = 黑马以令人震惊的优势战胜了冠军。
The seasoned fighter outfought his younger challenger. = 经验丰富的战士战胜了他的年轻挑战者。


现在完成时:
He has outfought every opponent he has faced this year. = 今年他战胜了遇到的所有对手。
She has outfought many opponents throughout her career.  = 她在职业生涯中战胜了许多对手。


过去完成时:
By the end of the match, he had outfought his rival in every aspect. = 比赛结束时，他在各个方面都战胜了他的对手。
She had outfought her opponent before the final round even began. = 在最后一轮开始之前，她就战胜了对手。


将来时:
I believe he will outfight his opponent and win the championship. = 我相信他会战胜对手赢得冠军。
She is expected to outfight her challenger and retain her title. = 预计她将战胜挑战者并保住她的头衔。


虚拟语气:
If he had trained harder, he might have outfought his opponent. = 如果他训练更刻苦，他或许就能战胜对手了。
If she had been in better condition, she would have outfought her rival. = 如果她的状态更好，她就能战胜对手了。


被动语态:
The champion was outfought by a determined underdog. = 冠军被一个意志坚定的弱者击败了。
The seasoned fighter was surprisingly outfought by the rookie. = 经验丰富的战士出人意料地被菜鸟击败了。


疑问句:
Did the smaller fighter really outfight the larger one? = 较小的拳击手真的战胜了较大的拳击手吗？
Will the challenger be able to outfight the champion? = 挑战者能够战胜冠军吗？


否定句:
He did not outfight his opponent; he simply got lucky. = 他并没有战胜对手；他只是运气好。
She failed to outfight her rival, losing the match. = 她未能战胜对手，输掉了比赛。


PHRASE->
outfight someone in a battle=在战斗中击败某人
outfight the competition=战胜竞争
outfight one's way to victory=凭借实力赢得胜利
outfight and outsmart=又打败又智取
outfight the odds=战胜逆境
