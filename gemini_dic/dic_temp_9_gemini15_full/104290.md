justifiable:/ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪəbl/| adj.| 可辩解的；合理的；有正当理由的 |  capable of being justified; defensible.  It refers to an action, decision, or belief that can be shown to be right or reasonable. |例句：His actions, while unconventional, were justifiable under the circumstances.  他的行为虽然非常规，但在当时的情况下是合理的。

例句：The judge found the defendant's actions justifiable self-defense.  法官认定被告的行为属于正当防卫。


近义词：legitimate, valid, reasonable, defensible, warranted

反义词：unjustifiable, inexcusable, unwarranted, indefensible


词源：源自动词 justify (使…显得有理；证明…是正当的)，加后缀 -able (可…的；能…的) 构成形容词。justify 最终源自拉丁语 *iustificare，由 ius (正义，权利) + facere (做)构成。
