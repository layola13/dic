ipad:/ˈaɪpæd/| n.|苹果平板电脑|Apple's tablet computer, a portable personal computer with a touchscreen interface.|I bought a new iPad Pro last week. 我上周买了一台新的iPad Pro。|近义词：tablet, slate|反义词：desktop computer, laptop


USAGE->
(iPad本身作为名词，没有动词形式，因此无法进行时态变化)

一般现在时:
I use my iPad every day. = 我每天都用我的iPad。
He reads ebooks on his iPad. = 他在iPad上阅读电子书。


一般过去时:

She used her iPad to take notes in class. = 她用iPad在课堂上记笔记。
