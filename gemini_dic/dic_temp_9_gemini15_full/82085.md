groan:/ɡroʊn/| /ɡrəʊn/ | n., v., int.|呻吟，抱怨；发出呻吟声|to make a deep, low sound expressing pain, unhappiness, or disapproval; a deep, low sound expressing pain, unhappiness, or disapproval|例句：He groaned in pain after falling. 他摔倒后痛苦地呻吟着。

例句：The old house groaned under the weight of the snow.  这栋老房子在雪的重压下发出呻吟声。


近义词：moan, sigh, grunt, lament|反义词：exult, rejoice, cheer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He groans every time he bends over. 他每次弯腰都会呻吟。
She groans about her job all the time. 她总是抱怨她的工作。


一般过去时:
He groaned with exhaustion after the marathon. 马拉松结束后，他疲惫地呻吟着。
She groaned when she heard the bad news. 听到坏消息时，她呻吟了一声。


现在完成时:
He has groaned with pain all night. 他整夜都在痛苦地呻吟。
She has groaned about the traffic for hours. 她已经抱怨交通堵塞好几个小时了。


过去完成时:
He had groaned in pain before the doctor arrived. 医生到达之前，他已经痛苦地呻吟了。
She had groaned about the project before the meeting even started. 会议还没开始，她就一直在抱怨这个项目。


将来时:
He will groan when he sees the bill. 他看到账单时会呻吟的。
She will groan if she has to work late again. 如果她不得不再次加班，她会抱怨的。


虚拟语气:
I would groan if I had to do that again. 如果我不得不再次做那件事，我会抱怨的。
If I knew how much it would hurt, I would have groaned before I started. 如果我知道会这么痛，我一开始就会呻吟了。


被动语态:
A groan was heard from the next room. 从隔壁房间传来了呻吟声。(很少用被动语态)
His complaints were often met with groans from his colleagues. 他的抱怨经常遭到同事们的抱怨。（groan作名词）


疑问句:
Did you groan when you lifted that weight? 你举起那个重量时呻吟了吗？
Why are they groaning? 他们为什么在呻吟？


否定句:
He didn't groan when he got the news. 他听到消息时没有呻吟。
She didn't groan, but her face showed her displeasure. 她没有呻吟，但她脸上露出了不满的表情。


PHRASE->
groan under the weight  在…的重压下呻吟
a groan of disapproval 不满的呻吟

a low groan 低沉的呻吟
with a groan 带着呻吟声
