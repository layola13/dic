calves:/kɑːvz/| n.|小牛；腿肚子|The plural of calf.  Calves are the young of cows.  The calf muscles are located in the back of the lower leg.|例句：The farmer is looking after his calves.  这位农民正在照看他的小牛。

例句：I pulled a muscle in my calves during the marathon.  我在马拉松比赛中拉伤了腿肚子肌肉。


近义词：young cows (for young calves),  muscles (for calf muscles)|反义词：None directly applicable.|词性: 名词
