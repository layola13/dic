nonconstituents:/ˌnɒnkənˈstɪtjʊənts/| n. |非成分；非构成成分|Elements in a sentence that are not part of the grammatical structure.  They are typically added for emphasis, stylistic effect, or as afterthoughts. They do not affect the grammatical correctness or meaning of the sentence when removed. |Example:  "The dog, a golden retriever, barked loudly."  "a golden retriever" is a nonconstituent.|近义词：interjection, aside, insertion|反义词：constituent
