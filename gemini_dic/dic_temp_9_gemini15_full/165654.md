premodelled:/ˌpriːˈmɒdəld/ | adj. | 预先建模的；预先设计的 |  Premodelled refers to something that has been created or designed in advance, often using a model or template.  It implies a process of planning and preparation before the final product is made.  This often applies to computer-generated models or simulations, but can also refer to other pre-designed items. | Example: The premodelled characters in the video game saved development time.  预先建模的游戏角色节省了开发时间。 The architect presented premodelled designs for the new building. 建筑师展示了新建筑的预先设计方案。| 近义词: pre-designed, pre-fabricated, pre-built | 反义词: custom-made, bespoke, improvised |  形容词
