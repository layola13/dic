hero-worshiping: /ˈhɪroʊ ˈwɜːrʃɪpɪŋ/ | n. | 英雄崇拜；盲目崇拜 |  The excessive admiration and imitation of a hero, often to the point of neglecting one's own judgment and individuality.  It often involves idealized and unrealistic views of the hero. |

例句：His hero-worshiping of the rock star led him to make many impulsive decisions.  他的对摇滚巨星的盲目崇拜导致他做了许多冲动决定。

例句：The culture of hero-worshiping can be detrimental to the healthy development of young people. 英雄崇拜的文化可能不利于年轻人的健康发展。


近义词：idolization, deification, veneration

反义词：criticism, skepticism, objectivity


词性：名词
