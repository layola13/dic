disappointers:/ˌdɪsæˈpɔɪntərz/ | n. |令人失望的人或事 |Things or people that cause disappointment.  Disappointers refer to something or someone that fails to meet expectations, leading to feelings of dissatisfaction or letdown.  |例句：The new movie was a major disappointer for fans of the book.  这部新电影让书迷们非常失望。

The constant delays in the project were a series of disappointers.  项目中不断的延误是一系列令人失望的事情。|近义词：letdowns, disappointments, failures |反义词：delights, successes, triumphs |名词
