cisgender:/ˈsɪsˌdʒɛndər/| adj.|顺性别的|An adjective describing a person whose gender identity aligns with the sex they were assigned at birth.  This term is used to describe individuals who identify as the gender that corresponds to their assigned sex at birth. It's often used in contrast to transgender.|例句：Many cisgender people take their gender identity for granted.  许多顺性别的人认为他们的性别认同是理所当然的。|近义词：gender-conforming (in some contexts)|反义词：transgender|
