retaliator: /rɪˈtælieɪtər/ | n. | 报复者，反击者 | A person who retaliates; someone who takes revenge.  | Example: The retaliator sought revenge for the injustices he had suffered.  报复者寻求报复他遭受的不公正待遇。 |近义词：avenger, revenger | 反义词：forgiver, pacifist |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The retaliator plans his revenge carefully. = 报复者仔细计划他的报复。
He retaliates against his enemies with fierce determination. = 他以强烈的决心报复他的敌人。


一般过去时:
The retaliator struck back swiftly and decisively. = 报复者迅速而果断地反击。
She retaliated against her attacker with a well-aimed blow.  = 她用精准的一击报复袭击者。


现在完成时:
The retaliator has exacted revenge on all those who wronged him. = 报复者已经向所有冤枉他的人报了仇。
He has retaliated multiple times for the initial offense. = 他多次报复了最初的冒犯。


过去完成时:
The retaliator had already prepared his counterattack before the enemy moved. = 在敌人行动之前，报复者已经准备好反击。
She had retaliated before realizing the full extent of the consequences. = 在意识到后果的全部严重性之前，她就报复了。


将来时:
The retaliator will not rest until he has had his revenge. = 报复者直到报仇雪恨才会罢休。
He will retaliate with a force that will surprise everyone. = 他将以一种让所有人惊讶的力量进行报复。


虚拟语气:
If he were a retaliator, he would have acted by now. = 如果他是报复者，他现在就会采取行动了。
I wish the retaliator would show mercy. = 我希望报复者能展现仁慈。


被动语态:
Revenge was retaliated upon the perpetrators. = 对肇事者进行了报复。
His actions were retaliated against by the authorities. = 他的行为受到了当局的报复。


疑问句:
Is he a retaliator? = 他是报复者吗？
Who is the retaliator in this situation? = 在这种情况下，谁是报复者？


否定句:
He is not a retaliator; he prefers forgiveness. = 他不是报复者；他更喜欢宽恕。
She did not retaliate against her critics. = 她没有报复她的批评者。
