relearn:/ˌriːˈlɜːrn/ | vt. |重新学习，再学习 |To learn something again, often after forgetting it or not understanding it fully the first time.  |例句：I need to relearn the basics of algebra before I start calculus. 我需要重新学习代数基础知识才能开始学习微积分。

例句：He decided to relearn Spanish after moving to Mexico. 他决定搬到墨西哥后重新学习西班牙语。


近义词：revise, review, restudy | 反义词：unlearn, forget


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I relearn new vocabulary every day. = 我每天都重新学习新的词汇。
She relearns the piano piece every week to improve. = 她每周都重新练习这首钢琴曲以提高水平。


一般过去时:
I relearned the song after forgetting the lyrics. = 我忘记歌词后重新学习了这首歌。
They relearned the safety procedures after the accident. = 事故发生后，他们重新学习了安全规程。


现在完成时:
I have relearned several languages throughout my life. = 我一生中重新学习了几种语言。
She has relearned how to ride a bike after her accident. = 事故后，她已经重新学会了骑自行车。


过去完成时:
I had relearned the material before the exam. = 我在考试前已经重新学习了这些材料。
He had relearned the dance routine before the performance. = 他在演出前已经重新学习了舞蹈套路。


将来时:
I will relearn French before my trip to Paris. = 我将在去巴黎旅行之前重新学习法语。
She will relearn the software before the project begins. = 项目开始前，她将重新学习这个软件。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would relearn how to play the guitar. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新学习弹吉他。
I wish I could relearn all the things I've forgotten. = 我希望我能重新学习所有我忘记的东西。


被动语态:
The lesson was relearned by the students after the teacher's explanation. = 在老师解释后，学生们重新学习了这节课。
The procedure needs to be relearned by all staff. = 所有员工都需要重新学习这个程序。


疑问句:
Will you relearn the material for the exam? = 你会为考试重新学习这些材料吗？
Have you ever relearned a skill you thought you'd lost? = 你有没有重新学习过你认为自己已经忘记的技能？


否定句:
I didn't relearn the material, so I failed the test. = 我没有重新学习这些材料，所以考试不及格。
She hasn't relearned the song yet, she's too busy. = 她还没重新学习这首歌，她太忙了。
