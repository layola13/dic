relight:/ˌriːˈlaɪt/| vt.|重新点燃；使…重新焕发活力|To light (something) again.  To renew or revive the energy or enthusiasm of something.|例句：He re-lit the candle after it had gone out. 他在蜡烛熄灭后又把它点燃了。

例句：The success of the new product has re-lit the company's enthusiasm. 新产品的成功重新点燃了公司的热情。|近义词：ignite, kindle, rekindle|反义词：extinguish, quench


USAGE->

He relights the candles every evening. = 他每天晚上都重新点燃蜡烛。
