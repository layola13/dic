overindulgently:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒəntli/ | adv. | 过度放纵地；纵情地 |  To behave in an excessively indulgent manner; to give way excessively to one's desires or appetites. | 例句：He ate overindulgently at the buffet, regretting it later. 他在自助餐上吃得太过放纵，后来后悔了。

He spent his inheritance overindulgently, leaving himself with nothing. 他过度挥霍了他的遗产，以至于一无所有。


近义词：excessively, immoderately, intemperately, profligately

反义词：sparingly, moderately, temperately, frugally

词性: 副词
