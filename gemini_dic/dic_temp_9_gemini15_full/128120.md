net:/net/| n.|网络，网状物；净值，净额|A net is a woven fabric of threads, used for catching things.  It can also refer to a network or a system of interconnected things. In finance, it refers to a value after deductions.|例句：The fisherman cast his net into the sea.  渔夫把渔网撒到海里。|例句：The company's net profit was $1 million. 公司的净利润为100万美元。|近义词：network, mesh, web|反义词：gross, total|名词
