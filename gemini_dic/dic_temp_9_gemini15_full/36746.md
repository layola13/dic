colloquialness:/kəˈloʊkwiəlˌnɛs/ | n. | 口语化；口语风格 | The quality or state of being colloquial; informal language.  It refers to the use of informal words, phrases, or expressions characteristic of everyday conversation. Colloquialness can add a sense of familiarity and approachability to writing, but it can also make it less formal and precise. |  The colloquialness of the email made it feel more personal.  这封邮件的口语化风格使它感觉更私人化。 The novel's colloquialness added to its charm. 这部小说的口语化风格增加了它的魅力。 | informality, familiarity | formality, precision | 名词
