half-pleasing:/ˌhɑːfˈpliːzɪŋ/ | adj. | 半令人愉悦的，不太令人满意的 | Partially pleasing; somewhat satisfying but not entirely so.  It implies a feeling of incompleteness or a lack of full satisfaction. |例句：The movie had a half-pleasing ending; it resolved the main plot but left some loose ends. 这部电影的结局有点令人满意；它解决了主要情节，但也留下了一些悬而未决的问题。

The painting was half-pleasing, with beautiful colors but a weak composition. 这幅画有点令人满意，色彩优美，但构图薄弱。|近义词：moderately pleasing, somewhat satisfying, partially agreeable |反义词：unpleasing, displeasing, unsatisfactory
