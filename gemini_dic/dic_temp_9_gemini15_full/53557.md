disbarred:/dɪsˈbɑːrd/| v.|吊销律师资格|To officially prevent someone from working as a lawyer.  This usually happens as a result of serious misconduct or unethical behavior.|The lawyer was disbarred for misappropriating client funds.  律师因挪用客户资金而被吊销律师资格。|The judge disbarred him after he was found guilty of perjury.  法官在他被判犯有伪证罪后吊销了他的律师资格。|barred, disqualified|licensed, permitted|动词
