refencing:/ˈrɛfərənsɪŋ/| n. | 再次引用；重新参考；(对文献等的)再次提及 | The act of referring to something again, especially a source of information or a previous statement.  It involves citing or mentioning something previously mentioned or discussed.  This is often done to support a point, add context, or clarify an idea. | Example: The essay demonstrates effective refencing by citing relevant studies throughout. 这篇文章通过在全文中引用相关的研究，展现了有效的参考文献引用。 |近义词：re-citation,  re-referencing, reiteration |反义词：omission, neglect
