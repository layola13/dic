micromanaging: /ˈmaɪkroʊˌmænɪdʒɪŋ/ | v. | 微观管理，事无巨细地管理 | To manage something or someone with excessive control or attention to minor details.  This often involves a manager closely supervising every aspect of their subordinates' work, stifling creativity and autonomy. | 例句：The CEO's micromanaging style stifled innovation within the company.  首席执行官事无巨细的管理风格扼杀了公司内部的创新。

The project manager's micromanaging drove the team to burnout. 项目经理的过度管理导致团队筋疲力尽。


近义词：overmanaging, overseeing excessively, controlling excessively, supervising excessively


反义词：delegating, empowering, trusting, giving autonomy


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He micromanages every aspect of his team's work. 他事无巨细地管理着团队的每一方面的工作。
She micromanages her employees, leaving them feeling stifled. 她事无巨细地管理她的员工，让员工感到压抑。


一般过去时:
He micromanaged the project, delaying its completion. 他对项目进行了过度管理，导致项目延期完成。
She micromanaged the team's presentation, making them nervous. 她对团队的演示进行了过度管理，使他们感到紧张。


现在完成时:
The manager has micromanaged the department for years.  经理多年来一直对部门进行过度管理。
They have micromanaged this project since its inception. 自项目启动以来，他们就一直在对该项目进行过度管理。


过去完成时:

She had micromanaged the team before, so they were prepared this time. 她以前曾对团队进行过过度管理，所以这次他们做好了准备。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

词源:  The word is a blend of "micro" (meaning small or tiny) and "managing" (the act of managing).  It implies a focus on extremely small details in management, often to the detriment of overall effectiveness.
