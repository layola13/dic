hereon:/ˌhɪrˈɒn/| adverb | 从此以后，此后 | From this point onward; from now on.  | Example:  Hereon, all communication will be conducted via email. 此后，所有沟通都将通过电子邮件进行。 |近义词：henceforth, hereafter, from now on | 反义词：previously, before
