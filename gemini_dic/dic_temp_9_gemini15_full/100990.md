invalidators:/ɪnˈvælɪdeɪtərz/ | n. |使无效者；使作废者 |Those who or that which invalidate something;  things that make something invalid or untrue.  The term often refers to factors, arguments, or events that undermine the validity or effectiveness of something else. |例句：The new evidence presented acted as invalidators of the original theory. 新提出的证据使原来的理论失效。 The inconsistent data points were significant invalidators of the study's conclusions. 不一致的数据点严重削弱了该研究的结论。|近义词：nullifier, disprover, refuter |反义词：validator, confirmer, verifier |名词
