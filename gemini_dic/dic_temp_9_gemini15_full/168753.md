prodding:/ˈprɒdɪŋ/| /ˈprɑːdɪŋ/| n. v.| 戳，驱使，激励 |  The act of poking or pushing something; persistent encouragement or stimulation. |例句：The farmer was prodding the reluctant cow.  农夫在戳那头不愿前进的牛。

例句：Her constant prodding finally got him to finish the report. 她不断的督促最终让他完成了报告。


近义词：goading, urging, pushing, stimulating | 反义词：discouraging, hindering, obstructing | 词性: noun, verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prods the sleeping dog. = 他戳了戳睡着的狗。
She prods her friend to work harder. = 她督促她的朋友更加努力工作。


一般过去时:
He prodded the sleeping dog gently. = 他轻轻地戳了戳睡着的狗。
She prodded her friend to apply for the job. = 她鼓励她的朋友申请那份工作。


现在完成时:
He has prodded the cow several times. = 他已经戳过那头牛好几次了。
She has prodded him to action many times. = 她已经多次敦促他采取行动了。


过去完成时:
He had prodded the sleeping dog before it woke up. = 在那只狗醒来之前，他已经戳了它。
She had prodded him all week to finish his project.  = 她整个星期都在督促他完成他的项目。


将来时:
He will prod the reluctant student to answer. = 他将催促那个不愿回答的学生。
She will prod him to make a decision soon. = 她将催促他尽快做出决定。


虚拟语气:
If I prodded him more, he might finish the work. = 如果我再催促他一下，他也许会完成工作。
I wish I had prodded him earlier. = 我真希望我早点催促他。


被动语态:
The sleeping dog was prodded by the farmer. = 那只睡着的狗被农夫戳了。
He was prodded into action by his boss. = 他被老板催促着采取行动。


疑问句:
Are you prodding the children to eat their vegetables? = 你在催促孩子们吃蔬菜吗？
Has he been prodded to make a decision yet? = 他是否已经被催促做出决定了？


否定句:
He didn't prod the sleeping dog. = 他没有戳那只睡着的狗。
She didn't prod him to study harder. = 她没有催促他更加努力学习。
