muskrats:/ˈmʌskræt/| n.|麝鼠|A muskrat is a semi-aquatic rodent with dark brown fur, webbed hind feet, and a long, scaly tail.  They are known for building lodges in marshes and along the edges of bodies of water.|例句：We saw several muskrats swimming in the pond. 我们看到几只麝鼠在池塘里游泳。|例句：The muskrat built a large lodge in the reeds. 那只麝鼠在芦苇丛中建造了一个很大的窝。|近义词：water rat|反义词:None.
