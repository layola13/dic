peritonitis:/ˌpɛrɪtəˈnaɪtɪs/ | n. |腹膜炎|Inflammation of the peritoneum, the serous membrane lining the abdominal cavity and covering the abdominal organs.  It's a serious condition that can be caused by infection, injury, or other underlying diseases. Symptoms can include abdominal pain, fever, nausea, and vomiting.  |Example: The patient was diagnosed with peritonitis after a ruptured appendix.  这位病人阑尾破裂后被诊断出患有腹膜炎。|近义词：peritoneal inflammation |反义词：None.
