philathea:/fɪˈleɪθiə/| n. |博爱；慈善；仁爱|  A love of and devotion to God and humankind; brotherly love; benevolence.  The word combines elements from Greek roots suggesting "love" and "God". It's less common than words like "philanthropy" but carries a similar sentiment with a stronger religious connotation.  |例句：The philathea of the community was evident in their tireless efforts to help the victims of the flood.  社区的博爱精神体现在他们不遗余力地帮助洪水受害者上。|近义词：philanthropy, benevolence, charity, love|反义词：misanthropy, malice, hatred|名词
