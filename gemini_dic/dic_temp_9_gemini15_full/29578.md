cate:/keɪt/| n. |猫；猫咪|A small domesticated carnivorous mammal with soft fur, a short snout, and retractile claws. It is widely kept as a pet.|例句：My cat loves to sleep in sunbeams. 我的猫喜欢在阳光下睡觉。  例句：She adopted a stray cate. 她收养了一只流浪猫。|近义词：kitten, feline|反义词：dog


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat sleeps all day. = 猫整天睡觉。
The cats chase mice. = 猫追老鼠。

一般过去时:
The cat slept on the mat. = 猫睡在垫子上。
The cats chased the birds. = 猫追赶鸟儿。

现在完成时:
The cat has slept for hours. = 猫已经睡了好几个小时了。
The cats have caught many mice. = 猫已经抓了很多老鼠。

过去完成时:
The cat had already eaten before I arrived. = 我到达之前猫已经吃过了。
The cats had chased the birds away before I saw them. = 我看到它们之前，猫已经把鸟儿赶走了。


将来时:
The cat will sleep in the sun tomorrow. = 猫明天将在阳光下睡觉。
The cats will chase the butterflies. = 猫将会追逐蝴蝶。


虚拟语气:
If the cat were hungry, it would meow. = 如果猫饿了，它会喵喵叫。
I wish the cat would stop scratching the furniture. = 我希望猫能停止抓挠家具。

被动语态:
The cat was petted by the child. = 猫被孩子抚摸。
The mice were chased by the cats. = 老鼠被猫追赶。

疑问句:
Is the cat sleeping? = 猫在睡觉吗？
Are the cats playing? = 猫在玩耍吗？

否定句:
The cat is not sleeping. = 猫没有睡觉。
The cats did not catch the mouse. = 猫没有抓住老鼠。
