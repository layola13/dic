impervious: /ɪmˈpɜːrviəs/ | adj. | 不可渗透的；不受影响的 | Not allowing fluid to pass through; impenetrable.  Also, not affected or influenced by something. | The raincoat was impervious to the heavy rain.  雨衣不透雨。| The politician remained impervious to criticism. 这位政治家对批评无动于衷。| 近义词：impermeable, resistant, unaffected | 反义词：permeable, penetrable, susceptible


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The material is impervious to water. =这种材料不透水。
He remains impervious to my suggestions. = 他对我的建议无动于衷。


一般过去时:
The old city walls were impervious to attack. =古老的城墙坚不可摧。
She was impervious to his charm. = 她不受他的魅力影响。


现在完成时:
The dam has remained impervious to the floodwaters. =水坝一直抵挡住了洪水。
His confidence has proved impervious to setbacks. = 他的信心经受住了挫折的考验。


过去完成时:
The ship's hull had been made impervious to damage. = 船体曾被制成不易损坏的。
Her composure had remained impervious to the chaos. = 她一直保持镇定，不受混乱影响。


将来时:
The new roof will be impervious to leaks. = 新屋顶将不会漏水。
He will likely remain impervious to reason. = 他很可能仍然不听劝。


虚拟语气:
If the material were impervious to heat, it would be ideal. = 如果这种材料耐高温，那就完美了。
If he were impervious to criticism, he would be a better leader. = 如果他不受批评的影响，他会成为更好的领导者。


被动语态:
The fabric is designed to be impervious to stains. =这种织物设计成防污的。
His reputation was impervious to scandal. = 他的名誉不受丑闻影响。


疑问句:
Is this material impervious to oil? = 这种材料不透油吗？
Are you impervious to persuasion? = 你是否固执己见？


否定句:
The soil is not impervious to water. = 土壤不防水。
He was not impervious to the flattery. = 他并非不受奉承的影响。
