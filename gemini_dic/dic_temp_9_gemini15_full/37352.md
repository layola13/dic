commender:/kəˈmɛndər/ | n. |推荐者，赞扬者，指挥官 | A person who commends or recommends someone or something.  |例句：The manager was a strong commender of her team's work.  经理强烈推荐她团队的工作。 |例句：The ship's commender gave orders to set sail. 船长下令起航。|近义词：advocate, supporter, proponent, officer |反义词：critic, detractor, opponent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He commends his employees regularly. = 他定期表扬他的员工。
She commends the new policy. = 她赞扬新的政策。


一般过去时:
The general commended the soldiers for their bravery. = 将军赞扬士兵们的英勇。
He commended her on her excellent performance. = 他称赞她优秀的表现。


现在完成时:
The CEO has commended the team for their hard work. = 首席执行官已经表扬了团队的辛勤工作。
She has commended the book to many friends. = 她已经向许多朋友推荐了这本书。


过去完成时:
He had commended her work before the meeting. = 他在会议之前就已经赞扬了她的工作。
They had commended the plan to the board. = 他们已将该计划推荐给了董事会。


将来时:
The professor will commend the student's research. = 教授将赞扬学生的科研成果。
He will commend the new strategy to the shareholders. = 他将向股东推荐新战略。


虚拟语气:
If he were to commend the project, it would boost morale. = 如果他赞扬这个项目，将会提高士气。
I wish he would commend my efforts. = 我希望他会赞扬我的努力。


被动语态:
Her work was commended by the judges. = 她的作品受到了评委的赞扬。
The plan was commended to the committee. = 该计划被推荐给了委员会。


疑问句:
Will the manager commend our performance? = 经理会赞扬我们的表现吗？
Who commended the new product? = 谁推荐了这个新产品？


否定句:
He did not commend her behavior. = 他没有赞扬她的行为。
The committee did not commend the proposal. = 委员会没有通过该提案。


PHRASE->
commend to = 推荐给...
commend oneself to = 使自己受...欢迎
commend for = 因为...而受到赞扬
commend itself to = 自荐给...
commend to the attention of = 特别推荐给...
