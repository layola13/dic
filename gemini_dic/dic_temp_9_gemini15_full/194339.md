self-planted:/ˌsɛlfˈplæntɪd/| adj.| 自种的，自行种植的 |  Describes plants that have grown without human intervention, typically from seeds that fell naturally.  | Example: The self-planted wildflowers added a burst of color to the meadow.  自种的野花给草地增添了一抹色彩。 | 近义词：wild, spontaneous, untended | 反义词：cultivated, planted (by humans)
