dojo:/ˈdoʊdʒoʊ/ | n. |道场；练习场；武术馆 | A dojo is a place where martial arts are practiced.  It can also refer to a place of learning or training in other disciplines. |例句：He trains at a karate dojo three times a week. 他每周在空手道道场训练三次。|例句：The online dojo offered courses in various coding languages.  这个在线道场提供各种编程语言的课程。|近义词：training center, studio, school |反义词：N/A
