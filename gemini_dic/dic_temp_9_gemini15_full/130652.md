nonadvancement:/ˌnɒnədˈvɑːnsmənt/| n. |缺乏进步；未获提升；停滞不前|The lack of advancement or promotion; stagnation.  It refers to a situation where there is no progress or improvement, particularly in a career or a project.  This word emphasizes the absence of movement forward. |例句：His persistent nonadvancement in the company led to frustration.  他的公司职位长期没有提升，这让他很沮丧。

His nonadvancement in the project was due to unforeseen circumstances.  项目进展停滞是因为不可预见的情况。|近义词：stagnation, lack of progress, standstill, failure to advance|反义词：advancement, promotion, progress, improvement
