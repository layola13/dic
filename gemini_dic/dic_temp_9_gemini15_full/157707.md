pixilating: /ˈpɪksəleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 像素化；使成像素 | To break an image or video into pixels, often to obscure or censor details.  This process reduces the image resolution and can make it appear blurry or blocky. |

The software pixilated the faces in the video to protect the identities of the individuals.  = 该软件将视频中的面部像素化，以保护个人的身份。

The image was heavily pixilated, making it difficult to discern any details. = 图片严重像素化，难以辨别任何细节。


近义词：pixelate, block out, obscure, blur

反义词：sharpen, clarify, define
