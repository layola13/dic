masters:/ˈmɑːstərz/| n.|主人；硕士；专家；大师|The word "masters" refers to individuals who possess significant skill or expertise in a particular field, often implying a high level of mastery and accomplishment. It can also refer to people who hold a Master's degree, or to people who own or control something.  |例句：He is a master of his craft. 他是技艺大师。The masters of the universe control the global economy.  宇宙的主宰者控制着全球经济。|近义词：experts, professionals, specialists, scholars, graduates|反义词：novices, beginners, apprentices


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The masters control the company. = 主人们控制着这家公司。
These masters teach advanced techniques. = 这些大师教授高级技巧。


一般过去时:
