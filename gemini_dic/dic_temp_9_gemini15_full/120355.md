minimization: /ˌmɪnɪmaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 最小化；减小；缩小 | The act of minimizing something; reducing something to the smallest possible size or amount.  | Example: The company's focus on cost minimization led to increased efficiency. 公司注重成本最小化，从而提高了效率。 |近义词：reduction, lessening, diminution |反义词：maximization, amplification


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company minimizes its carbon footprint. = 公司将碳足迹降到最低。
He minimizes the risks involved. = 他将所涉及的风险降到最低。


一般过去时:
They minimized the damage caused by the storm. = 他们将风暴造成的损失降到了最低。
She minimized her role in the project. = 她淡化了自己在项目中的作用。


现在完成时:
We have minimized our expenses this year. = 我们今年已经将支出降到了最低。
The government has minimized the impact of the crisis. = 政府已经将危机的冲击降到了最低。


过去完成时:
They had minimized their losses before the market crash. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经将损失降到了最低。
She had minimized her contact with him after the argument. = 吵架后，她尽量减少了与他的接触。


将来时:
We will minimize the disruption to services. = 我们将把对服务的中断降到最低。
They will minimize their environmental impact. = 他们将把对环境的影响降到最低。


虚拟语气:
If we minimized costs, we could achieve profitability. = 如果我们把成本降到最低，就能实现盈利。
I wish we could minimize the negative consequences. = 我希望我们能够将负面影响降到最低。


被动语态:
The risks were minimized by careful planning. = 通过仔细的计划，风险被降到了最低。
The impact of the changes was minimized by the company. = 公司将这些变化的影响降到了最低。


疑问句:
How can we minimize the waste? = 我们怎样才能减少浪费？
What steps can be taken to minimize the risks? = 可以采取哪些措施来降低风险？


否定句:
They did not minimize the importance of the issue. = 他们并没有轻视这个问题的重要性。
The company failed to minimize its environmental impact. = 公司未能将对环境的影响降到最低。


PHRASE->
risk minimization 风险最小化
cost minimization 成本最小化
damage minimization 损害最小化
error minimization 误差最小化
loss minimization 损失最小化
