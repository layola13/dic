一般现在时:
The war causes devastation in many countries. = 战争在许多国家造成破坏。
This policy brings about devastation to the environment. =这项政策给环境带来了破坏。


一般过去时:
The flood devastated the entire town. = 洪水摧毁了整个城镇。
The fire caused widespread devastation. = 大火造成了大范围的破坏。


现在完成时:
The storm has caused widespread devastation across the region. = 暴风雨给该地区带来了大范围的破坏。
Years of drought have led to devastation of the farmland. = 多年的干旱导致农田荒芜。


过去完成时:
By the time help arrived, the storm had caused complete devastation. = 当救援到来时，暴风雨已经造成了彻底的破坏。
Before the war, the region had never experienced such devastation. = 在战争爆发之前，这个地区从未经历过如此严重的破坏。


将来时:
The predicted earthquake will cause immense devastation. = 预计的地震将造成巨大的破坏。
If the dam breaks, it will lead to widespread devastation. = 如果大坝决堤，将导致大范围的破坏。


虚拟语气:
If the hurricane had hit the city, the devastation would have been unimaginable. = 如果飓风袭击了这座城市，造成的破坏将是难以想象的。
I wish the earthquake hadn't caused so much devastation. = 我真希望地震没有造成如此大的破坏。


被动语态:
The city was devastated by the bombing. = 这座城市被轰炸摧毁了。
The crops were devastated by the frost. = 庄稼被霜冻毁了。


疑问句:
What is the extent of the devastation caused by the fire? = 火灾造成的破坏程度有多大？
How can we prevent further devastation to the environment? = 我们如何才能防止环境进一步遭到破坏？


否定句:
The government is taking steps to prevent further devastation. = 政府正在采取措施以防止进一步的破坏。
The area was not completely devastated by the flood. = 该地区并没有被洪水完全摧毁。
