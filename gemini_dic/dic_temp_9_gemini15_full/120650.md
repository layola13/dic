mis-sell:/ˌmɪsˈsɛl/ | vt. | 错卖，误售 | To sell something incorrectly or deceptively, often involving providing inaccurate or misleading information to the buyer.  This can include selling a product that is faulty, damaged, or not as described, or misrepresenting its features or benefits. | Example: The car salesman mis-sold me a car with hidden mechanical problems.  这家汽车销售员向我错卖了一辆存在隐藏机械问题的汽车。 |近义词：misrepresent, deceive, defraud | 反义词：sell honestly, sell truthfully |动词
