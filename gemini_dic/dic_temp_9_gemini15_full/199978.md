skateboard:/ˈskeɪtˌbɔːrd/| n. |滑板|A skateboard is a board with four wheels, used for riding on.  It's a popular recreational activity.  |例句：He learned to skateboard when he was ten. 他十岁的时候学会了滑板。|例句：My son got a new skateboard for his birthday. 我儿子生日收到了一个新的滑板。|近义词：longboard,  |反义词:None.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He skateboards every weekend. = 他每个周末都玩滑板。
She skateboards in the park. = 她在公园里玩滑板。

一般过去时:
I skateboarded all afternoon yesterday. = 昨天我玩了一下午滑板。
They skateboarded down the hill. = 他们从山上滑板下来。

现在完成时:
He has skateboarded since he was a child. = 他从孩提时代就开始玩滑板了。
She has never skateboarded before. = 她以前从未玩过滑板。

过去完成时:
I had skateboarded many times before I finally landed a kickflip. = 在我最终学会了踢翻之前，我已经玩过很多次滑板了。
They had never skateboarded on such a steep hill. = 他们以前从未在如此陡峭的山坡上玩过滑板。

将来时:
I will skateboard tomorrow if the weather is good. = 如果天气好，我明天就去玩滑板。
We will be skateboarding at the skate park this weekend. = 这个周末我们将在滑板公园玩滑板。

虚拟语气:
If I had a skateboard, I would skateboard every day. = 如果我有一块滑板，我每天都会玩滑板。
I wish I could skateboard as well as he does. = 我希望我能像他一样擅长滑板。

被动语态:
The skateboard was broken in the accident. = 滑板在事故中坏了。
The new skate park is being built near the school. = 新的滑板公园正在学校附近建造。

疑问句:
Do you skateboard? = 你玩滑板吗？
Where do they skateboard? = 他们在哪里玩滑板？

否定句:
I don't skateboard very often. = 我不经常玩滑板。
She didn't skateboard yesterday because it was raining. = 昨天因为下雨她没玩滑板。
