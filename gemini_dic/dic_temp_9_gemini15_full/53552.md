disbands:/dɪsˈbændz/| v.|解散，解体|To formally dissolve (a group, organization, or assembly).  It implies a planned or official ending.|The band disbanded after their final concert. 乐队在最后一场音乐会后解散了。 The army unit disbanded after the war.  战争结束后，这个军队单位解散了。|近义词：dissolve, break up, disperse|反义词：form, assemble, unite


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team disbands after every game.  = 每次比赛后队伍都解散。
The club disbands at the end of the season. = 赛季结束时俱乐部解散。

一般过去时:
The group disbanded last year. = 这个团体去年解散了。
The parliament disbanded after the election. = 选举后议会解散了。

现在完成时:
The band has disbanded after 20 years. = 乐队20年后解散了。
The committee has disbanded due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，委员会已解散。

过去完成时:
The club had disbanded before I joined. = 我加入之前俱乐部就已经解散了。
The army had disbanded before the peace treaty was signed. = 在和平条约签署之前，军队已经解散了。

将来时:
The company will disband next month. = 公司下个月将解散。
The team will disband after the championship. = 比赛结束后队伍将解散。

虚拟语气:
If the project failed, the team would disband. = 如果项目失败，团队将解散。
I wish the committee hadn't disbanded so quickly. = 我希望委员会不要这么快解散。

被动语态:
The team was disbanded by the coach. = 球队被教练解散了。
The organization was disbanded due to financial problems. = 该组织因财务问题而被解散。

疑问句:
Did the club disband? = 俱乐部解散了吗？
Will the army disband soon? = 军队很快就会解散吗？

否定句:
The band did not disband. = 乐队没有解散。
The committee will not disband easily. = 委员会不会轻易解散。
