eternalist:/ɪˈtɜːnəlɪst/| adj. |永恒主义者；永恒论者|  A person who believes in eternalism, the philosophical position that all moments in time exist equally.  This contrasts with presentism (only the present exists) and eternalism (all times exist simultaneously).| Example:  The debate between presentist and eternalist viewpoints often centers on the nature of time itself.  永恒主义者和现在主义者之间的辩论往往集中在时间本身的本质上。|近义词：timeless advocate, believer in eternal recurrence|反义词：presentist
