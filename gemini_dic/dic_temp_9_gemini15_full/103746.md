词源:
The word "jottings" comes from the verb "jot," meaning to write something down quickly or briefly.  "Jot" itself has uncertain origins, possibly related to Old Norse words for "scratch" or "mark."  The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb into a noun, indicating the act or result of jotting.
