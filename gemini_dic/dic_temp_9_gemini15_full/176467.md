re-orientate:/ˌriːˈɔːriənˌteɪt/| vt.|重新定向；使重新定位；使改弦更张|To change someone's attitude or way of thinking, or to change the direction or purpose of something.|The company had to re-orientate its marketing strategy to appeal to a younger demographic. 公司不得不调整其营销策略以吸引更年轻的人群。|近义词：redirect, reposition, readjust|反义词：disorient, confuse|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-orientate their business strategy every year. = 他们每年都会调整他们的商业策略。
The teacher re-orientates the students' attention to the lesson. = 老师重新吸引学生的注意力到课程上。


一般过去时:
The company re-orientated its marketing campaign after the poor sales figures. = 公司在销售业绩不佳后重新调整了营销活动。
He re-orientated himself after getting lost in the woods. = 迷路后，他重新定位了自己的方向。


现在完成时:
The government has re-orientated its foreign policy towards closer ties with its neighbours. = 政府已经将其外交政策重新定位为与邻国建立更紧密的联系。
She has re-orientated her career goals after gaining new experience. = 在获得新的经验后，她已经重新调整了她的职业目标。


过去完成时:
They had re-orientated their plans before the unexpected change in circumstances. = 在情况意外变化之前，他们已经重新调整了他们的计划。
The pilot had re-orientated the aircraft before landing. = 飞行员在降落前已经重新调整了飞机的方向。


将来时:
The company will re-orientate its product line to meet the changing consumer demands. = 公司将重新调整其产品线以满足不断变化的消费者需求。
We will re-orientate the map to show the new highway. = 我们将重新调整地图以显示新的高速公路。


虚拟语气:
If they had re-orientated their approach earlier, they might have succeeded. = 如果他们早点调整方法，他们也许会成功。
I wish I had re-orientated my studies sooner. = 我希望我早点调整我的学习方向。


被动语态:
The company's marketing strategy was re-orientated to target a wider audience. = 公司的营销策略被重新调整以针对更广泛的受众。
His perspective was re-orientated by the experience. = 他的观点因这次经历而改变了。


疑问句:
Will the company re-orientate its focus on innovation? = 公司会将重点重新放在创新上吗？
How often do they re-orientate their training programs? = 他们多久重新调整一次培训计划？


否定句:
The government did not re-orientate its policy despite the protests. = 尽管有抗议，政府也没有调整其政策。
He refused to re-orientate his thinking on the matter. = 他拒绝改变他对这件事的看法。


PHRASE->
re-orientate oneself = 重新定位自己
re-orientate the discussion = 重新调整讨论方向
re-orientate the company's mission = 重新调整公司的使命
re-orientate the curriculum = 重新调整课程
re-orientate to a new market = 重新适应新的市场


词源:
re- (prefix indicating repetition or reversal) + orientate (from orient, ultimately from Latin *oriens "rising," the present participle of oriri "to rise," referring to the rising sun and thus the east)
