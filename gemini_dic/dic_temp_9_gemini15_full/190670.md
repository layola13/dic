sauger:/ˈsɔːɡər/ | n. |沙盖鱼，一种淡水鱼 | A sauger is a freshwater fish of the perch family, typically found in rivers and streams.  It is characterized by its relatively small size and distinctive markings. |Example: The fisherman caught a large sauger in the river.  钓鱼人从河里钓到了一条大沙盖鱼。|近义词：perch (广义上), walleye (近亲)|反义词：无
