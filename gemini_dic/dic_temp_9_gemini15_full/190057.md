sands:/sændz/| n.|沙子；沙滩；少量；细粒物质|The word "sands" refers to a large quantity of sand, typically found on beaches or deserts. It can also refer to something resembling sand in texture or appearance, or a small quantity or amount of something.  |例句：The golden sands stretched for miles along the coast.  金色的沙滩绵延数英里。 The sands of time are slipping away. 时间飞逝。|近义词：grains, particles, beach|反义词：rocks, stones, mountains
