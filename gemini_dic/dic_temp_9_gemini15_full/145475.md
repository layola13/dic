overcleaning: /ˌoʊvərˈkliːnɪŋ/ | n. |过度清洁| The act of cleaning something excessively, often to the point of damage or harm.  It refers to cleaning beyond what is necessary or beneficial. |例句：Overcleaning can strip away essential oils from your skin, leaving it dry and irritated. 过度清洁会剥夺皮肤中的必需油脂，导致皮肤干燥和刺激。

例句：The constant overcleaning of antique furniture damaged its finish.  不断过度清洁古董家具损坏了它的漆面。


近义词：excessive cleaning, hypercleaning | 反义词：neglect, insufficient cleaning
