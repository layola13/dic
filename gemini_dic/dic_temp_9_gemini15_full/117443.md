meditate:/ˈmɛdɪteɪt/| vt. vi.|沉思；冥想；考虑|To think deeply or focus on one's thoughts, often for religious or spiritual purposes, or to focus intently on a subject.|例句：I meditate every morning to clear my mind. 我每天早上冥想以清除杂念。|例句：He meditated on the problem for hours before finding a solution. 他花了几个小时思考这个问题才找到解决方法。|近义词：contemplate, contemplate, reflect, ponder|反义词：act, rush


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I meditate every day. = 我每天都冥想。
She meditates before making important decisions. = 她做重要决定之前都会冥想。

一般过去时:
I meditated for an hour yesterday. = 我昨天冥想了一个小时。
They meditated on the meaning of life. = 他们冥想了生命的意义。

现在完成时:
I have meditated regularly for years. = 我多年来一直定期冥想。
She has meditated on this problem for days. = 她已经思考这个问题好几天了。

过去完成时:
I had meditated for a while before I felt calm. = 我冥想了一会儿才感到平静。
He had meditated deeply before he gave his speech. = 他在发表演讲之前进行了深入的思考。

将来时:
I will meditate tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上会冥想。
She will meditate on the proposal before responding. = 她将在回复提案之前仔细考虑一下。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would meditate more often. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更频繁地冥想。
I wish I could meditate more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地冥想。

被动语态:
The problem was meditated upon by the committee. = 这个问题被委员会仔细考虑了。
The issue should be carefully meditated upon before a decision is made. = 在做出决定之前，应该仔细考虑这个问题。

疑问句:
Do you meditate regularly? = 你经常冥想吗？
Does she meditate before sleep? = 她睡前冥想吗？

否定句:
I don't meditate often enough. = 我冥想不够频繁。
He didn't meditate before making the decision. = 他在做决定之前没有冥想。


PHRASE->
meditate on = 沉思；思考
meditate upon = 仔细考虑
guided meditation = 指导冥想
mindful meditation = 正念冥想
transcendental meditation = 超越冥想
