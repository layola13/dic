exemplarity:/ˌɛɡzɛmpˈlærəti/| n. |典范；榜样；模范性 |The quality of serving as an example; the state of being exemplary.  It refers to the nature of something that is worthy of imitation or serves as a model of excellence. |The company's commitment to sustainability is a testament to its exemplarity. 这家公司对可持续发展的承诺证明了它的典范性。|近义词：model, archetype, paragon, ideal |反义词：mediocrity, inferiority
