prebelieves:/ˌpriːbɪˈliːvz/| v.|预先相信；预先认定|To believe something before having evidence or proof.  It often implies a strong prior belief that may be difficult to change, even in the face of contradictory evidence.|例句：He prebelieves that his team will win the championship. 他预先相信他的球队会赢得冠军。|例句：She prebelieves in the power of positive thinking, regardless of scientific studies. 她预先相信积极思维的力量，无论科学研究如何。|近义词：assume, presuppose, presume, anticipate|反义词：doubt, question, disbelieve|
