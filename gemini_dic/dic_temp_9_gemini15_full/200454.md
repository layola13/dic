slake:/sleɪk/| vt. n.| 满足；使平静；使减弱；止渴；（石灰）熟化|To satisfy (a thirst or appetite); to quench; to reduce the intensity of (something).|例句：He slaked his thirst with a cool drink. 他喝了杯凉饮解渴。  The firefighters slaked the flames with water. 消防员用水扑灭了火焰。|近义词：quench, satisfy, appease|反义词：intensify, aggravate, increase

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slakes his thirst with water every day. = 他每天都喝水解渴。
She slakes her hunger with a big breakfast. = 她用丰盛的早餐来满足饥饿。

一般过去时:
The cool breeze slaked the heat of the day. = 凉爽的微风减轻了白天的酷暑。
He slaked his thirst with a glass of lemonade. = 他喝了一杯柠檬水解渴。

现在完成时:
The heavy rain has slaked the drought. = 大雨缓解了旱情。
They have slaked their desire for knowledge through extensive reading. = 通过大量的阅读，他们已经满足了他们对知识的渴望。

过去完成时:
He had slaked his thirst before the long journey. = 长途旅行之前，他已经解渴了。
By the time they arrived, they had slaked their hunger with snacks. = 他们到达的时候，已经用零食填饱了肚子。

将来时:
The coming rain will slake the parched earth. = 即将来临的雨水将滋润干旱的土地。
We will slake our thirst with fresh coconut water at the beach. = 我们将在海滩上用新鲜的椰子水解渴。


虚拟语气:
If I had some water, I would slake my thirst. = 如果我有水，我就能解渴。
If she had a good meal, she would slake her hunger. = 如果她吃一顿好饭，她就能填饱肚子。


被动语态:
His thirst was slaked by a glass of iced tea. = 一杯冰茶解了他的渴。
The fire was eventually slaked by the firefighters. = 大火最终被消防员扑灭了。


疑问句:
Does this drink slake your thirst? = 这饮料能解渴吗？
Can anything slake this burning desire? = 有什么东西能满足这种强烈的欲望吗？


否定句:
The small amount of rain did not slake the drought. = 少量的雨水并没有缓解旱情。
Nothing could slake his anger. = 什么都不能平息他的怒火。
