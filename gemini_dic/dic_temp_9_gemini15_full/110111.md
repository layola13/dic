licks:/lɪks/| n. v.|舔；轻微打击|The act of licking; a quick or light stroke or blow.|例句：The dog licks its paws.  狗狗舔着它的爪子。|例句：The waves gave the boat a few licks. 海浪轻轻拍打着小船。|近义词：lap, swipe, tap|反义词：None specifically, depends on context.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He licks his ice cream. = 他舔他的冰淇淋。
The cat licks its fur. = 猫舔它的毛。

一般过去时:
She licked the stamp. = 她舔了邮票。
The dog licked my hand. = 狗舔了我的手。

现在完成时:
The baby has licked the bowl clean. = 宝宝把碗舔干净了。
He has licked his wounds and moved on. = 他舔舐了伤口并继续前进。

过去完成时:
She had licked the envelope before she sealed it. = 在她封信之前，她已经舔过信封了。
He had licked his ice cream before I arrived. = 我到之前他已经把冰淇淋舔完了。

将来时:
The dog will lick your face when you get home. = 你回家时，狗会舔你的脸。
I will lick my wounds after the defeat.  = 失败后我会疗伤。

虚拟语气:
If I were a cat, I would lick myself clean. = 如果我是一只猫，我会把自己舔干净。
I wish I could lick my worries away. = 我希望我能舔掉我的烦恼。

被动语态:
The stamp was licked and stuck to the envelope. = 邮票被舔湿并粘在信封上。
The ice cream was licked clean. = 冰淇淋被舔干净了。

疑问句:
Does the dog lick its paws often? = 狗经常舔它的爪子吗？
Did he lick the spoon? = 他舔勺子了吗？

否定句:
The cat didn't lick the milk. = 猫没有舔牛奶。
He didn't lick his fingers after eating. = 吃完饭后他没有舔手指。
