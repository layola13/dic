crawler: /ˈkrɔːlər/ | n. | 网络爬虫，爬行物 | A program or automated script that systematically browses the World Wide Web, typically to gather information for a search engine.  It can also refer to a creature that crawls. | Example: The search engine uses a crawler to index web pages. 搜索引擎使用爬虫来索引网页。  The baby crawler was exploring the room.  婴儿爬行着探索房间。|近义词：spider, bot, web spider |反义词：flyer, jumper
