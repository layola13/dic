osmometer:/ɒzˈmɒmɪtər/| n. |渗透压计|An instrument for measuring osmotic pressure.  It's used to determine the concentration of a solution by measuring the pressure required to prevent osmosis. |Example: The osmometer was used to measure the osmotic pressure of the blood sample.  渗透压计被用来测量血液样本的渗透压。|近义词：tonometer (if measuring pressure in a specific context)|反义词：None specifically, but instruments measuring opposite properties could be considered antonyms in a broad sense.|名词
