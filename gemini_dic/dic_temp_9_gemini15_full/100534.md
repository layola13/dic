intervolution:/ˌɪntərɪˈvɒljuːʃən/ | n. |内卷；内旋；内向演化 | A process of internal conflict or struggle within a system, often leading to stagnation or decline.  It describes a situation where increased effort does not lead to proportional improvements in outcome, often due to competition within a limited system.  This term is relatively new and its usage is still evolving. | Example: The intervolution within the company led to decreased productivity and morale. 公司内部的内卷导致生产力和士气下降。|近义词：involution, internal conflict, stagnation |反义词：evolution, progress, development |名词
