label:/ˈleɪbəl/ | n. v. |标签；标记；贴标签；分类 | to attach a label to something; to categorize or classify something. |例句：He carefully labeled each jar with its contents. 他仔细地给每个罐子贴上标签注明内容。  |例句：They labeled him a traitor. 他们把他贴上叛徒的标签。|近义词：tag, mark, brand, categorize |反义词：unlabel, mislabel


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I label all my files carefully. = 我仔细地给我的所有文件贴标签。
They label this product as organic. = 他们将此产品贴上有机标签。


一般过去时:
She labeled the boxes before shipping them. = 她装运之前给箱子贴上了标签。
The scientists labeled the cells for identification. = 科学家们给细胞贴上标签以便识别。


现在完成时:
He has labeled all the samples. = 他已经给所有样本贴上了标签。
We have labeled this project a success. = 我们已经将这个项目标注为成功。


过去完成时:
They had labeled the exhibits before the museum opened. = 博物馆开馆前他们已经给展品贴上了标签。
The committee had labeled the proposal unrealistic. = 委员会已经将该提案标注为不切实际。


将来时:
She will label the photographs tomorrow. = 她明天将给照片贴标签。
They will label the containers with the appropriate warnings. = 他们将用适当的警告标记容器。


虚拟语气:
If I labeled the boxes, it would be easier to find them. = 如果我给箱子贴上标签，会更容易找到它们。
I wish I had labeled the jars before putting them away. = 我真希望我把罐子收起来之前贴上标签。


被动语态:
The packages were labeled incorrectly. = 包裹贴错了标签。
The products are labeled with nutritional information. = 产品上标有营养信息。


疑问句:
Are these boxes labeled correctly? = 这些箱子贴的标签正确吗？
How are the different types of fruit labeled? = 不同种类的水果是如何贴标签的？


否定句:
He didn't label the boxes. = 他没有给箱子贴标签。
They haven't labeled the samples yet. = 他们还没给样本贴标签。
