slam-dancer: /ˈslæmˌdænsər/ | n. | 甩舞者，激进舞蹈者 | A person who performs slam dancing, a style of energetic dancing characterized by pushing and shoving.  | Example: The slam-dancers created a mosh pit in front of the stage. 甩舞者在舞台前形成了一个疯狂的舞台冲撞区。 | 近义词: mosh-pit dancer, aggressive dancer | 反义词: ballet dancer, graceful dancer
