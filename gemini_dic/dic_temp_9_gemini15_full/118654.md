mestizo:/meˈstiːzoʊ/| n. |混血儿（尤指欧洲人和印第安人的混血儿）|A person of mixed European and Native American ancestry, especially in Latin America.  Mestizos represent a significant portion of the population in many Latin American countries, resulting from centuries of intermarriage between European colonizers and indigenous peoples.|例句：Many people in Mexico are mestizo.  许多墨西哥人是混血儿。|例句：The painting depicts a mestizo family in traditional clothing. 这幅画描绘了一个穿着传统服装的混血家庭。|近义词：mulatto, half-breed |反义词：purebred, full-blooded
