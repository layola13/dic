exoskeletons:/ˌɛksoʊˈskɛlətənz/ | n. |外骨骼 | A rigid external covering for the body in some invertebrate animals, especially arthropods, providing both support and protection.  | Example:  Crabs and lobsters have hard exoskeletons.  螃蟹和龙虾有坚硬的外骨骼。

The insect shed its exoskeleton.  昆虫蜕掉了它的外骨骼。|近义词：external skeleton, carapace (for certain animals)|反义词：endoskeleton (internal skeleton)
