pestered:/ˈpɛstərd/| v.|纠缠，烦扰，打扰|to trouble or annoy (someone) persistently.|例句：The children pestered their mother for sweets.孩子们缠着妈妈要糖果。

The salesman pestered me for days to buy his product. 销售员纠缠了我好几天让我买他的产品。|近义词：annoy, bother, harass, importune|反义词：soothe, appease, calm


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pesters me every day. = 他每天都烦我。
They pester their teacher with questions. = 他们用问题烦他们的老师。

一般过去时:
She pestered him until he agreed. = 她一直缠着他直到他同意。
The dog pestered the cat all afternoon. = 这只狗一下午都在烦这只猫。

现在完成时:
I have been pestered by debt collectors all week. = 我整个星期都被讨债的人烦扰。
They have pestered the manager for a raise. = 他们一直在纠缠经理要求加薪。

过去完成时:
He had pestered her for weeks before she finally gave in. = 他纠缠了她几周后她才终于让步。
They had pestered the landlord for repairs for months. = 他们几个月来一直在纠缠房东要求修理。


将来时:
She will pester him again tomorrow. = 她明天还会再来烦他。
They will pester the authorities until they get a response. = 他们会一直纠缠当局直到得到回应。

虚拟语气:
If he pestered me again, I would call the police. = 如果他再次纠缠我，我会报警。
I wish he wouldn't pester me so much. = 我希望他不要再这么烦我了。

被动语态:
He was pestered by journalists all day. = 他一整天都被记者纠缠。
The manager was pestered with complaints. = 经理被投诉烦扰了。

疑问句:
Are you being pestered by anyone? = 你有没有被任何人纠缠？
Was he pestered by his creditors? = 他是否被债权人纠缠？

否定句:
I didn't pester him; he asked me for help. = 我没有烦他；是他求我帮忙的。
They didn't pester the waiter for service. = 他们没有纠缠服务员要求服务。


PHRASE->
pester someone into doing something = 纠缠某人做某事
pester someone for something = 纠缠某人要某物
pester someone incessantly = 不断地烦扰某人
pestered with requests = 受到请求的烦扰
be pestered by insects = 被昆虫骚扰
