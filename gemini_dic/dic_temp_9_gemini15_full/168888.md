profeminists: /proʊˈfɛmənɪsts/ | n. | 支持女权主义者 |  A profeminist is a man or other person who supports feminism and women's rights.  They actively advocate for gender equality and challenge patriarchal structures.  Profeminists often work alongside feminists to achieve common goals, but the term distinguishes those who are not themselves women. |  Many profeminists are involved in grassroots activism, working to end gender-based violence. | 近义词：feminist ally, supporter of women's rights | 反义词：misogynist, antifeminist | 名词
