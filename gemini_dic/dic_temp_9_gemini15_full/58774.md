dunned:/dʌnd/| vt.|催债，不断要求偿还债务|To demand payment of a debt repeatedly and persistently.  The word often implies an aggressive or insistent manner.|例句：The landlord dunned him repeatedly for the overdue rent. 房东反复催促他支付拖欠的房租。
例句：He was constantly dunned by his creditors.  他的债权人不断地向他催债。|近义词：press, urge, hound, badger|反义词：forgive, remit


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He duns his tenants for rent every month. = 他每个月都向他的房客催租。
She duns her friends for money. = 她向朋友们要钱。

一般过去时:
The creditor dunned him for the money he owed. = 债权人催他还钱。
My boss dunned me for the report. = 我的老板催我要报告。

现在完成时:
They have dunned me for payment several times. = 他们已经几次催我付款了。
The company has dunned its customers for outstanding invoices. = 公司已向客户催收未付发票。

过去完成时:
He had dunned her repeatedly before she finally paid. = 他在她最终付款之前反复催促她。
The bank had dunned the company for months before taking legal action. = 在采取法律行动之前，银行已经向该公司催收了几个月。

将来时:
The collector will dun him again next week. = 收款人下周还会再来催他。
I will dun him until he pays. = 我会一直催他还钱，直到他还清为止。

虚拟语气:
If I were him, I wouldn't wait to be dunned. = 如果我是他，我不会等到被催债。
I wish they wouldn't dun me so often. = 我希望他们不要这么频繁地催债。

被动语态:
He was dunned relentlessly by his creditors. = 他的债权人无情地催促他。
The debtor was dunned until he agreed to pay. = 债务人被催促，直到他同意付款。

疑问句:
Are you being dunned by your creditors? = 你的债权人正在催你吗？
Has he been dunned for the money? = 他是否因为钱被催债了？

否定句:
They did not dun him for the small debt. = 他们没有因为那笔小债而催促他。
She won't be dunned. = 她不会被催债。



PHRASE->
be dunned for sth = 因某事被催债
dun sb for sth = 催促某人支付某物
dun sb relentlessly = 持续不断地催促某人
dun sb into submission = 通过催促使某人屈服
get dunned = 被催债
