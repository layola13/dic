roomless:/ˈruːmləs/| adj.|无房间的；没有房间的|Lacking a room or rooms; without a room or rooms.  It often implies a lack of adequate living space.|例句：The refugees lived in a roomless shelter.难民住在没有房间的收容所里。
The family was roomless after the fire destroyed their home.  大火烧毁了他们的家后，这个家庭无家可归。|近义词：homeless, houseless, shelterless|反义词：roomy, spacious
