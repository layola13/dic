cadge's:/kædʒz/| n.|乞讨；骗取；搭便车|The word "cadge's" is the possessive form of the noun "cadge," referring to something belonging to or associated with cadging.  Cadging itself means to obtain something by begging or sponging.  It can also refer to informally obtaining a ride or lift.|例句：He tried to cadge a cigarette from me. 他试图向我讨一支烟。 The students tried to cadge a ride to the party. 学生们试图搭便车去参加派对。|近义词：beg, sponge, bum, scrounge|反义词：give, donate, provide
