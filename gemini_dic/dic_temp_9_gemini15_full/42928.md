coven:/ˈkʌvən/| n.|女巫集会；秘密组织；一群人|A coven is traditionally a group of witches, often thirteen in number, meeting for rituals.  The term can also refer to any secret gathering or group of people.  |例句：The witches gathered for their monthly coven. 女巫们聚集在一起参加她们每月的集会。

例句：A coven of spies worked to undermine the government. 一群间谍密谋破坏政府。


近义词：assembly, group, gathering, circle

反义词：None directly applicable,  consider antonyms of "secret" like "open" or "public" depending on context.

词性：名词
