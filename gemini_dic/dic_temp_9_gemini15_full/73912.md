forstraught:/fɔːrˈstrɔːt/| adj.| 焦虑的；极度不安的；苦恼的 | extremely worried, agitated, or upset.  |例句：She was forstraught with worry after her son's accident. 她儿子出事后，她焦虑万分。|例句：The news left him forstraught and unable to speak. 这个消息使他惊慌失措，说不出话来。|近义词：distraught, distressed, anguished, agitated |反义词：calm, composed, serene, tranquil
