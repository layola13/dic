job:/dʒɑːb/ /dʒɒb/| n.|工作，职业，任务|a piece of work, especially one that needs a lot of time and effort; a position of employment|例句：Finding a good job is difficult in this economy.在这个经济环境下，找到一份好工作很难。

例句：She applied for a job as a teacher. 她申请了一份教师的工作。


近义词：occupation, profession, employment, position, task, assignment|反义词：leisure, idleness, relaxation|词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has a job at the bank. = 他在银行工作。
She works a part-time job. = 她做一份兼职工作。


一般过去时:
I had a job interview yesterday. = 我昨天参加了一次工作面试。
He lost his job last month. = 他上个月丢了工作。


现在完成时:
I have had this job for five years. = 我这份工作已经做了五年了。
She has just finished her job. = 她刚刚完成了她的工作。


过去完成时:
He had already finished his job before I arrived. = 我到达之前他已经完成了他的工作。
She had held several jobs before finding this one. = 在找到这份工作之前，她已经做过几份工作了。


将来时:
I will start my new job next week. = 我下周将开始我的新工作。
She will apply for many jobs before she finds the right one. = 在找到合适的工作之前，她会申请很多工作。


虚拟语气:
If I had a job, I would be happier. = 如果我有一份工作，我会更快乐。
I wish I had a better job. = 我希望我有一份更好的工作。


被动语态:
The job was completed on time. = 工作按时完成了。
The position is being filled by a new employee. = 这个职位由一位新员工担任。


疑问句:
Do you have a job? = 你有工作吗？
What kind of job are you looking for? = 你在找什么类型的工作？


否定句:
I don't have a job right now. = 我现在没有工作。
She didn't get the job. = 她没有得到那份工作。
