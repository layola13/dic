pumped:/pʌmpt/| adj.|兴奋的，充满激情的|Feeling energetic and excited.|例句：I'm pumped for the concert tonight! 今晚的演唱会让我兴奋不已！|例句：She was pumped up after winning the race. 赢得比赛后她兴奋极了。|近义词：excited, thrilled, energized|反义词：depressed, unenthusiastic, apathetic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am pumped about the upcoming trip. = 我对即将到来的旅行感到兴奋。
He is pumped for the game. = 他对比赛感到兴奋。

一般过去时:
I was pumped after finishing the marathon. = 完成马拉松后我感到兴奋。
She was pumped to see her favorite band. = 看到她最喜欢的乐队后她兴奋极了。

现在完成时:
I have been pumped all day. = 我一整天都兴奋不已。
We have been pumped about the project's success. = 我们一直对项目的成功感到兴奋。

过去完成时:
I had been pumped for weeks leading up to the event. = 在活动开始前的几周里，我一直都非常兴奋。
They had been pumped about the opportunity. = 他们一直对这个机会感到兴奋。

将来时:
I will be pumped when I finally graduate. = 当我最终毕业的时候，我会非常兴奋。
They will be pumped to receive the award. = 他们将对获得这个奖项感到兴奋。

虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would be pumped! = 如果我中了彩票，我会非常兴奋！
I wish I were pumped for the exam. = 我希望我对考试感到兴奋。

被动语态:  (Pumped is rarely used passively.)  被动语态很少使用。


疑问句:
Are you pumped for the party? = 你对派对兴奋吗？
Is he pumped about the news? = 他对这个消息兴奋吗？

否定句:
I'm not pumped about going to work today. = 我今天上班并不兴奋。
She wasn't pumped for the presentation. = 她对演示并不兴奋。


PHRASE->
be pumped up =  兴奋起来
get pumped =  变得兴奋
pump someone up =  激励某人
pumped about/for = 对……感到兴奋
pumped to do something =  渴望做某事
