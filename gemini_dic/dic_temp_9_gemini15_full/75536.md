fub:/fʌb/| n. |(俚语)糟糕透顶的事物，令人讨厌的人或事 |A fub is something or someone extremely unpleasant or undesirable. It is a slang term.  |例句：That new policy is a real fub. = 那项新政策真是糟糕透顶。  That guy's a total fub. = 那家伙真是个讨厌鬼。 |近义词：disaster, mess, nuisance, bore|反义词：blessing, delight, joy|名词
