non-perception:/ˌnɒn pərˈsɛpʃən/| n. |非感知，无知觉|The lack of awareness or understanding of something.  It refers to a failure to perceive or understand something, often due to inattention, lack of sensory input, or cognitive impairment.|Example: His non-perception of the danger led to his accident.他的无知觉导致了他的事故。|近义词： unawareness, insensibility, obliviousness |反义词：perception, awareness, consciousness |名词
