nabbed:/næbd/| vt.|抓住，逮捕，夺取|To seize or arrest someone suddenly and unexpectedly.  It often implies a quick and skillful capture.|例句：The police nabbed the thief red-handed. 警察当场抓住小偷。
例句：She nabbed the last cookie before anyone else could. 她抢在其他人之前拿走了最后一块饼干。|近义词：seize, arrest, apprehend, grab, snatch|反义词：release, free, liberate|动词
