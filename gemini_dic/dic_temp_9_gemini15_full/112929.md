luger:/ˈluːɡər/| n. |鲁格手枪|A Luger is a type of semi-automatic pistol known for its distinctive design and history.  It's often associated with German military use in the early 20th century. |Example: The collector proudly displayed his rare Luger pistol.|近义词：Parabellum (referring to the 9mm Parabellum cartridge)|反义词：无
