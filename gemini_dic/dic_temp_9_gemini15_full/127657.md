neem:/niːm/| n. |楝树；印度楝树；印楝；印楝树的果实或种子|A tropical evergreen tree, Azadirachta indica, of the mahogany family, native to India and widely cultivated throughout the tropics for its medicinal and insecticidal properties.  Its fruit, seeds, and leaves are used to produce various products.|The neem tree is known for its medicinal properties.|近义词：Indian lilac|反义词：无
