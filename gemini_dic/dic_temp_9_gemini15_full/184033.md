resident:/ˈrezɪdənt/ | n. adj. |居民；居住者；住院医生；常驻的；居住的 | A resident is a person who lives in a particular place.  A resident doctor is a doctor who is training in a hospital. |例句：He is a resident of New York. 他是纽约居民。The resident physician examined the patient. 住院医生检查了病人。|近义词：inhabitant, occupant, domiciliary |反义词：nonresident, transient


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The residents enjoy the quiet neighborhood. = 居民们享受着宁静的社区。
She is a resident of this city. = 她是这个城市的居民。


一般过去时:
He was a resident of London for many years. = 他在伦敦住了很多年。
They were residents at the hospital during their training. = 他们在医院实习期间是住院医生。


现在完成时:
She has been a resident of this town for over twenty years. = 她在这个镇上住了二十多年了。
He has resided in this house since 1990. = 自1990年以来，他一直住在这所房子里。


过去完成时:
Before moving to Paris, she had been a resident of Rome. = 在搬到巴黎之前，她是罗马的居民。
They had resided in that apartment for five years before they moved. = 他们在搬家之前在那套公寓住了五年。


将来时:
He will be a resident of this building next year. = 他明年将住在这栋楼里。
She will remain a resident of this city for the foreseeable future. = 在可预见的未来，她将继续是这个城市的居民。


虚拟语气:
If I were a resident of that town, I would join the community garden. = 如果我是那个镇的居民，我会加入社区花园。
I wish I were a resident of a smaller town. = 我希望我是一个小镇的居民。


被动语态:
The apartment was occupied by a new resident. = 这套公寓被一位新居民占据了。
The hospital's resident program was highly regarded. = 医院的住院医生项目备受推崇。


疑问句:
Are you a resident of this country? = 你是这个国家的居民吗？
Is he a long-term resident of the area? = 他是这个地区的长期居民吗？


否定句:
He is not a resident of this state. = 他不是这个州的居民。
She was not a permanent resident of the country. = 她不是该国的永久居民。
