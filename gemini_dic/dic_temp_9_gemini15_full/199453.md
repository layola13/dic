simultaneousness:/ˌsɪmlˈteɪniəsnəs/| n. |同时性，同时发生|The state or quality of being simultaneous; the occurrence of two or more things at the same time.  It often refers to events happening at the exact same moment, but can also refer to overlapping events or processes.  |例句：The simultaneousness of the two explosions was remarkable.  两个爆炸同时发生，令人吃惊。

The simultaneousness of the symphony orchestra and the choir created a powerful effect. 交响乐团和合唱团同时演奏创造了强大的效果。|近义词：coincidence, concurrence, simultaneity|反义词：succession, sequence
