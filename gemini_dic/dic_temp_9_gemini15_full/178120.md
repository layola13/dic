recodifies:/ˌriːˈkoʊdɪfaɪz/| v.|重新编码，重新制定编码 |To change a code or system of codes.  This often involves restructuring or reorganizing information to achieve a new format or purpose.  It implies a more thorough revision than simply modifying an existing code. |例句：The company recodified its software to improve security. 公司重新编写了其软件以提高安全性。

The government recodified its tax laws to simplify the system. 政府重新制定了税法以简化体系。


近义词：recode, rewrite, restructure, reorganize |反义词：encode (in the sense of creating a code rather than changing it)
