clupeoid:/ˈkluːpiɔɪd/| n. |鯡形目魚類；鯡形類的|A clupeoid is a fish belonging to the order Clupeiformes, which includes herrings, sardines, anchovies, and shads.  These fish are typically small, schooling, and have laterally compressed bodies. They are an important food source for many marine animals and humans.|Example:  Many commercially important fish are clupeoids, such as herring and sardines.  许多重要的商业鱼类都是鯡形目鱼类，例如鲱鱼和沙丁鱼。|近义词：herring-like, sardine-like|反义词：无
