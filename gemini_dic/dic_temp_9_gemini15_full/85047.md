harbourless:/ˈhɑːbərləs/| adj. |无港口的；无庇护的；没有安全感的|Lacking a harbor or port; lacking shelter or protection; feeling insecure or unprotected.|The ship, caught in the storm, was left harbourless and at the mercy of the waves.  这艘船被困在暴风雨中，没有港口庇护，只能任凭风浪摆布。|sheltered, protected|exposed, vulnerable, unsafe|形容词
