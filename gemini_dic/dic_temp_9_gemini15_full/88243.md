hindsighted:/ˈhaɪndˌsaɪtɪd/| adj.|事后的，马后炮的|Having or showing the understanding of an event only after it has happened.|The decision seemed foolish in hindsight. 事后看来，这个决定很愚蠢。|近义词：retrospective, after-the-fact|反义词：foresighted, prescient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is hindsighted about the market crash. = 他对股市崩盘持有事后诸葛亮的看法。
Their hindsighted comments are unhelpful. = 他们事后评论毫无帮助。


一般过去时:
She was hindsighted in her criticism of his actions. = 她对他的行为进行了事后批评。
They were hindsighted about the risks involved. = 他们对所涉及的风险持有事后诸葛亮的看法。


现在完成时:
He has been hindsighted about many of his investment choices. = 他对许多投资选择都持有事后诸葛亮的看法。
We have been hindsighted about the impact of the pandemic. = 我们对疫情的影响持有事后诸葛亮的看法。


过去完成时:
She had been hindsighted about the consequences of her actions. = 她对行为的后果持有事后诸葛亮的看法。
They had been hindsighted about the potential for conflict. = 他们对潜在冲突持有事后诸葛亮的看法。


将来时:
He will be hindsighted about this situation in a few years. =  几年后，他会对这种情况持有事后诸葛亮的看法。
They will likely be hindsighted about this decision later. = 他们以后可能会对这个决定持有事后诸葛亮的看法。


虚拟语气:
If I had been less hindsighted, I would have acted differently. = 如果我不那么事后诸葛亮，我会采取不同的行动。
I wish I hadn't been so hindsighted about that opportunity. = 我真希望我没有对那个机会那么事后诸葛亮。


被动语态:
His actions were judged to be hindsighted. = 他的行为被认为是事后诸葛亮。
The decision was criticized as being hindsighted. =  这个决定因事后诸葛亮而受到批评。


疑问句:
Is he being hindsighted in his analysis? = 他在分析中是否事后诸葛亮？
Were their actions truly hindsighted? = 他们的行为真是事后诸葛亮吗？


否定句:
He wasn't hindsighted at all; he predicted the outcome accurately. = 他一点也不事后诸葛亮；他准确地预测了结果。
Their assessment wasn't hindsighted; it was based on sound reasoning. = 他们的评估不是事后诸葛亮；它是基于合理的推理。
