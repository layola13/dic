postexperience: /ˌpoʊstɪkˈspɪəriəns/ | n. |  经验之后；体验后；事后体验 |  Refers to the period of time, events, or feelings that occur after a significant experience. It can encompass reflection, processing, and the ongoing impact of the experience.  | Example:  The postexperience interviews revealed valuable insights into the participants' emotional responses. 事后访谈揭示了参与者情绪反应的宝贵见解。  | 近义词: aftermath, sequel, follow-up | 反义词: beforehand, prior to, anticipation |  名词
