ladles:/ˈleɪdlz/| n.|勺子；长柄勺|A ladle is a large deep spoon with a long handle, used for serving soup, stew, or other liquids.|例句：She ladled the soup into bowls. 她用勺子把汤盛到碗里。|例句：The cook used a large ladle to serve the stew. 厨师用一个大勺子盛炖菜。|近义词：spoon, dipper|反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ladles the soup carefully. = 他小心地舀汤。
She ladles the gravy over the potatoes. = 她把肉汁浇在土豆上。

一般过去时:
The chef ladled out generous portions of stew. = 厨师慷慨地舀出大份的炖菜。
He ladled the hot chocolate into mugs. = 他把热巧克力舀进马克杯里。

现在完成时:
She has ladled all the soup into the containers. = 她已经把所有的汤都舀进容器里了。
They have ladled out enough food for everyone. = 他们已经为每个人舀出了足够的食物。

过去完成时:
He had ladled the sauce before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他已经把酱汁舀好了。
She had ladled out all the ice cream by the time the party ended. = 派对结束时，她已经把所有的冰淇淋都舀光了。

将来时:
I will ladle the batter into the muffin tins. = 我将把面糊舀进松饼模具中。
The waiter will ladle the broth over the noodles. = 服务员会把肉汤浇在面条上。

虚拟语气:
If I had a larger ladle, I would ladle the soup more quickly. = 如果我有一个更大的勺子，我会更快地舀汤。
I wish I had ladled more of the stew for myself. = 我希望我给自己舀更多炖菜。


被动语态:

疑问句:
