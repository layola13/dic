charmed:/tʃɑːmd/| adj.|有魅力的；着迷的；施魔法于…的|Having or exhibiting charm; delightful; enchanted; magically influenced.  |例句：She was charmed by his wit and humor. 她被他机智幽默的谈吐所吸引。|例句：The magician charmed the snake into a basket. 魔术师用魔法把蛇引诱到篮子里。|近义词：enchanted, delighted, captivated, fascinated|反义词：disenchanted, repelled, disgusted


USAGE->

He is charmed by her beauty. = 他被她的美丽迷住了。
The audience is charmed by the performance. = 观众被演出迷住了。

过去完成时:
