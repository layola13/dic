corvettes:/kɔːˈvɛts/ | n. |轻型护卫舰 |A corvette is a small, fast warship, typically smaller than a frigate but larger than a patrol boat. They are used for a variety of roles, including anti-submarine warfare, escort duties, and coastal patrol.  |Example: The navy deployed several corvettes to protect the shipping lanes.  海军部署了几艘轻型护卫舰来保护航运线路。|近义词：frigate (larger), patrol boat (smaller) |反义词：battleship (much larger)
