normalizes:/ˈnɔːrməlaɪz/| vt.|标准化，使正常化 |to make something normal or standard|The software normalizes the data before analysis.  软件在分析之前对数据进行标准化。 The doctor worked to normalize her blood pressure. 医生努力使她的血压恢复正常。|近义词：standardize, regularize|反义词：disorganize, destabilize

The company normalizes its procedures. = 公司对其流程进行标准化。
