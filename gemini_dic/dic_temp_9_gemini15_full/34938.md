clobbering:/ˈklɒbərɪŋ/| n. v.|痛打；猛击；彻底击败|The act of hitting someone or something very hard; a severe defeat or beating.|例句：The boxer delivered a clobbering blow to his opponent. 拳击手给了对手重重一击。

例句：The team suffered a clobbering in the final game.  球队在决赛中惨败。


近义词：beating, thrashing, drubbing, pounding, trouncing|反义词：victory, win, triumph


USAGE->

He clobbers his opponents regularly. 他经常痛打他的对手。
She clobbers the competition. 她击败了竞争对手。
