re-engineer:/ˌriːˌɛndʒɪˈnɪr/| vt.|重新设计；改造；逆向工程|To redesign or restructure (a system, process, etc.) in order to improve efficiency or effectiveness.  It often involves a fundamental change to the way something works, rather than just minor adjustments.  The term can also refer to the process of analyzing a product or system to understand its workings, often with the goal of reproducing it (reverse engineering).|例句：The company decided to re-engineer its manufacturing process to reduce costs. 公司决定重新设计其制造流程以降低成本。

例句：The engineers re-engineered the software to improve its performance. 工程师们重新设计了软件以提高其性能。


近义词：redesign, revamp, restructure, overhaul, reverse engineer|反义词：maintain, preserve (in the context of not changing a system)|词性:verb
