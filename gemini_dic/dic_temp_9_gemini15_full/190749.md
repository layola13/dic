savants:/ˈsævənts/| n.|学者；专家；天才|A savant is a person of learning; especially : one with profound knowledge in a special field.  A savant is often used to describe someone with exceptional abilities in a specific area, sometimes in the context of autism or other developmental conditions. |例句：He's a savant in the field of astrophysics. 他是天体物理学领域的专家。

例句：The child, though autistic, is a savant when it comes to music. 这个孩子虽然患有自闭症，但在音乐方面却是个天才。


近义词：expert, scholar, genius, prodigy|反义词：ignoramus, dunce, amateur
