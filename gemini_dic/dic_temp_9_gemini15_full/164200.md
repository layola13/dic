prediverted:/ˌpriːdɪˈvɜːtɪd/| adj.|预先转移的；预先改道的|  Pre-existing or prior to diversion; diverted beforehand.  It implies a planned or intentional alteration of a course or plan before the usual or expected time.| The flight was prediverted due to the impending storm. 这趟航班由于即将到来的风暴而被提前改道。|近义词：rerouted, diverted, redirected|反义词：undiverted
