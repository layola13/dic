flipbook:/ˈflɪpbʊk/| n.| 动画书，翻页书 | A flipbook is a book with a series of pictures that, when rapidly flipped through, create the illusion of movement.  This is due to the persistence of vision.  | Example: The children were mesmerized by the flipbook of a running horse. 孩子们被奔跑的马的动画书迷住了。 |近义词：animated book, picture book |反义词：None.
