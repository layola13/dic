publican:/ˈpʌblɪkən/| n. |酒店老板；酒吧老板；税吏|A publican is the owner or keeper of a pub or tavern.  The term historically also referred to a tax collector, especially in the Roman Empire.|例句：The publican poured me a pint of his best ale.  酒店老板给我倒了一品脱他最好的啤酒。 The publican was known for his generous hospitality.  这位酒店老板以其慷慨的款待而闻名。|近义词：innkeeper, barkeeper, tavern keeper|反义词：customer, patron
