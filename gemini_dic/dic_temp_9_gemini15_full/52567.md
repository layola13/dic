diffuseness:/dɪˈfjuːsnəs/ | n. |弥漫性；散漫；模糊 |The quality or state of being diffuse; lack of clarity or precision; widespreadness.  It refers to something that is spread out over a large area, lacking focus or concentration. This can apply to light, sound, ideas, or even personality traits. |例句：The diffuseness of the light made it difficult to see clearly. 光线的弥漫使得难以看清。 The diffuseness of his explanation left me confused. 他解释的含糊不清让我感到困惑。|近义词：vagueness, ambiguity, indistinctness, dispersion |反义词：clarity, precision, focus, concentration
