heritable: /ˈhɛrɪtəbəl/ | adj. | 可遗传的，可继承的 |  capable of being inherited; transmissible from parent to offspring.  |  The trait is highly heritable.  这个特性具有高度遗传性。| inheritable, genetic, transferable | non-heritable, acquired
