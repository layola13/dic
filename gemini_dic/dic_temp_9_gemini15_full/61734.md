emove:/ɪˈmuːv/| vt.|移动，搬迁，使感动|to move something or someone from one place to another; to cause someone to feel strong emotion|例句：Please remove your shoes before entering.  请进门前脱鞋。|例句：The story removed me to tears. 这个故事让我感动得流泪。|近义词：shift, relocate, transfer, touch, stir|反义词：stay, remain, keep, leave untouched


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He removes his hat. = 他摘下帽子。
She removes the dishes from the table. = 她把盘子从桌子上拿走。


一般过去时:
They removed the obstacle. = 他们移除了障碍物。
He removed the stain from his shirt. = 他去除了衬衫上的污渍。


现在完成时:
I have removed all the files from the computer. = 我已经从电脑中删除了所有文件。
She has removed her makeup. = 她已经卸妆了。


过去完成时:
They had removed the furniture before the painting began. = 在开始绘画之前，他们已经搬走了家具。
She had removed the old wallpaper before putting up the new one. = 在贴上新的壁纸之前，她已经撕掉了旧的壁纸。


将来时:
He will remove the trash tomorrow. = 他明天将把垃圾扔掉。
They will remove the car from the driveway. = 他们将把车从车道上开走。


虚拟语气:
If he removed the obstacle, we could proceed. = 如果他移除了障碍物，我们就能继续进行。
I wish she had removed the files before the system crashed. = 我真希望她在系统崩溃之前就把文件删除了。


被动语态:
The car was removed by the tow truck. = 这辆车被拖车拖走了。
The furniture will be removed next week. = 家具将在下周被搬走。


疑问句:
Will you remove the boxes? = 你会把箱子搬走吗？
Did he remove the evidence? = 他销毁证据了吗？


否定句:
He didn't remove the files. = 他没有删除文件。
She couldn't remove the stubborn stain. = 她无法去除顽固的污渍。


PHRASE->
remove oneself from = 避开
remove doubt = 消除疑虑
remove a difficulty = 排除困难
remove temptation = 去除诱惑
remove from office =罢免
