incredulousness: /ɪnˈkrɛdʒələsnis/ | n. |  难以置信；怀疑；不相信 |  A feeling of disbelief or skepticism; the state of being incredulous.  It often involves a mixture of surprise and doubt. |


例句：

His incredulousness was evident in his wide, staring eyes.  = 他难以置信的表情显现在他睁大的眼睛里。

The incredulousness in her voice betrayed her lack of belief. = 她语气中的怀疑暴露了她不相信的态度。


近义词：disbelief, skepticism, doubt, suspicion, mistrust

反义词：belief, faith, acceptance, credence


词性：名词
