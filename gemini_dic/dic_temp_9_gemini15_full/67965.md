facilitator:/fəˈsɪlɪteɪtər/| n. | 促进者；协调者；辅导员 | A facilitator is a person who makes a process easier. They guide and support a group to achieve a common goal, often without imposing their own opinions or solutions.  | The workshop facilitator encouraged open discussion among participants.  研讨会的促进者鼓励参与者进行公开讨论。  | moderator, mediator, assistant, guide | obstructer, hinderer, impediment | 名词
