rhymer:/ˈraɪmər/ | n. | 押韵诗人；擅长押韵的人 | A rhymer is a person who writes or composes rhymes, often poetry.  They are skilled in using words that have similar sounds, especially at the end of lines. | Example: He's a talented rhymer, capable of crafting intricate and beautiful poems. 他是一位才华横溢的押韵诗人，能够创作出复杂而优美的诗歌。 |近义词：poet, versifier | 反义词：prose writer
