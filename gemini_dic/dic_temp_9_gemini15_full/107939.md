/ˌlærɪŋˈɡɒlədʒi/ /ˌlærɪŋˈɡɑːlədʒi/ | n. |喉科学|The branch of medicine that deals with the larynx (voice box) and its diseases.  It encompasses the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disorders affecting the larynx, including voice problems, swallowing difficulties, and airway obstructions. |The laryngologist examined the patient's vocal cords. 喉科医生检查了病人的声带。|近义词:laryngology (no true synonyms, as it's a specific field)|反义词:N/A
