mesial:/ˈmiːziəl/| adj. |近中的，近中线的| Situated near the middle or median plane of the body or a structure.  The term is often used in anatomy and dentistry to describe the surface of a tooth closest to the midline of the dental arch.  |例句：The mesial surface of the incisor is in contact with the adjacent tooth.  近中切牙面与邻牙接触。
The mesial drift of molars is a natural process. 磨牙的近中移动是一个自然过程。|近义词：medial, middle|反义词：distal
