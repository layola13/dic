prelocate:/ˌpriːˈloʊkeɪt/| vt.|预先安置；预先配置；提前布置|To move or place something in a position before it is needed.  This often involves preparation or planning.|例句：The company prelocated equipment to the new factory before the staff moved in. 公司在员工搬进新工厂之前就预先安置好了设备。

例句：They prelocated resources to ensure a smooth project launch. 他们预先配置了资源以确保项目顺利启动。

近义词：position beforehand, pre-place, prepare in advance

反义词：postlocate (this word is not commonly used)
