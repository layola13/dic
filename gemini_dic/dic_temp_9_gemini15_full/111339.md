livelier:/ˈlaɪvlɪər/| adj.|更活泼的，更热闹的，更生动的 |More lively; more full of life and energy.  It is the comparative form of the adjective "lively".|例句：The party became much livelier after he arrived.  派对在他到来之后变得热闹多了。|例句：Her livelier presentation kept the audience engaged. 她更生动的演讲吸引了观众的注意力。|近义词：more animated, more spirited, brisker|反义词：duller, quieter, less animated|形容词
