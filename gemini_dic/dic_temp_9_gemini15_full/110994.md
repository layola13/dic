liquidness:/ˈlɪkwɪdnəs/| n. |液态；流动性；流畅性 |The state or quality of being liquid.  It refers to the ability of a substance to flow easily and take the shape of its container, or, in a financial context, the ease with which an asset can be converted into cash. |Example: The liquidness of the honey made it easy to spread.  蜂蜜的流动性使其易于涂抹。The high liquidness of the market allowed for quick trades.  市场的流动性很高，可以快速交易。|近义词：fluidity, liquidity |反义词：solidity, viscosity
