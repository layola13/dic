comforted:/ˈkʌmfərtɪd/| v.|安慰，抚慰|To give comfort or consolation to (someone); to alleviate (someone's) distress or sorrow.|例句：Her mother comforted her after she failed the exam.  她考试不及格后，她母亲安慰了她。|例句：The warm blanket comforted him on the cold night.  温暖的毯子在寒冷的夜晚安慰了他。|近义词：consoled, soothed, reassured|反义词：distressed, upset, troubled


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He comforts his child every night. = 他每天晚上都安慰他的孩子。
She comforts her friend in times of need. = 她在她朋友需要的时候安慰她。

一般过去时:
My friend comforted me when I was sad. = 我难过的时候，我的朋友安慰了我。
The teacher comforted the crying student. = 老师安慰了哭泣的学生。

现在完成时:
She has comforted many people in her life. = 她一生中安慰过很多人。
He has comforted me many times. = 他安慰过我很多次。

过去完成时:
She had comforted him before he went to sleep. = 他睡觉前，她已经安慰过他了。
I had comforted my sister before she left for the hospital. = 我姐姐去医院之前，我已经安慰过她了。

将来时:
I will comfort her when she is feeling down. = 当她情绪低落时，我会安慰她。
He will comfort his son after the game. =  比赛结束后，他会安慰他的儿子。

虚拟语气:
If I could comfort her, I would. = 如果我能安慰她，我会的。
I wish I could have comforted him sooner. = 我希望我早点能安慰他。

被动语态:
The grieving family was comforted by the priest. = 悲伤的家庭受到了牧师的安慰。
The child was comforted by the soft music. =  孩子被轻柔的音乐所安慰。

疑问句:
How can I comfort my friend? = 我该如何安慰我的朋友？
What can I say to comfort him? = 我能说些什么来安慰他？

否定句:
I couldn't comfort her; her grief was too deep. = 我无法安慰她；她的悲伤太深了。
He did not comfort the child. = 他没有安慰孩子。
