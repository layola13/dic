reembarkation:/ˌriːɪmˌbɑːrˈkeɪʃən/| n. |重新登船；重新上船；再次启用|The act of embarking again; the process of getting back on a ship or aircraft after a temporary disembarkation.  It can also refer to the restarting of a project or undertaking after a pause.|例句：The reembarkation of the passengers was delayed due to bad weather.  乘客的重新登船由于恶劣天气而延误。

The reembarkation of the troops was a carefully planned operation.  部队的重新登船是一次精心策划的行动。|近义词：reboarding, resumption|反义词：disembarkation
