jerquer:/ˈdʒɜːrkər/ | n. | 猛地一拉的人；（尤指）粗暴地对待某人的人 | A person who gives a sudden, sharp pull or tug; someone who treats someone roughly, especially by giving sharp, sudden pulls. | Example: The jerker yanked the rope, almost pulling the horse over.  猛地一拉绳子的人差点把马拉倒了。  | 近义词：tugger, yanker, bully | 反义词：gentle person, kind person | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He jerks the rope. = 他猛地拉了一下绳子。
She jerks her head. = 她猛地一甩头。


一般过去时:
He jerked the rope suddenly. = 他突然猛地拉了一下绳子。
She jerked her head back. = 她猛地把头缩了回去。


现在完成时:
He has jerked the handle several times. = 他已经拉了好几次把手了。
She has jerked her knee in pain. = 她因为疼痛而猛地抽搐了一下膝盖。


过去完成时:
He had jerked the lever before the machine started. = 机器启动前，他已经猛地拉动了操纵杆。
She had jerked her hand away from the hot stove. = 她已经把手从滚烫的炉子上猛地缩回去了。


将来时:
He will jerk the string to start the kite. = 他将猛地拉动线来放风筝。
She will jerk her head in agreement. = 她将会猛地点头表示同意。


虚拟语气:
If he jerked the rope harder, it would break. = 如果他用力拉绳子，绳子就会断。
If she had jerked the steering wheel, the accident might have been avoided. = 如果她猛地打方向盘，事故可能就会避免了。


被动语态:
The rope was jerked violently. = 绳子被猛烈地拉动了。
Her arm was jerked back by the sudden movement. = 她的胳膊被突然的动作猛地拉了回去。


疑问句:
Did he jerk the door open? = 他猛地把门拉开了吗？
Why did she jerk her hand away? = 她为什么猛地把手缩回去了？


否定句:
He didn't jerk the handle. = 他没有拉把手。
She didn't jerk her head. = 她没有甩头。


PHRASE->
jerk reaction:  反射动作
muscle jerk: 肌抽动
jerk someone around: 粗暴对待某人
jerk someone's chain:  惹恼某人
jerk off: 自慰 (粗俗用语)
