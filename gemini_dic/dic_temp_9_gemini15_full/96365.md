indiscernible: /ˌɪndɪˈsɜːrnəbl/ | adj. | 难以辨别的；模糊不清的 |  Impossible to discern or distinguish; unclear.  | The speaker's words were indiscernible over the loud music.  说话声被嘈杂的音乐盖住了，难以辨别。|  clear, distinct, audible | discernible, clear, understandable |
