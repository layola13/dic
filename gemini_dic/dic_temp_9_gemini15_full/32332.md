child-proofs:/ˈtʃaɪldpruːf/| v.|使儿童安全；采取防儿童措施|To make something safe for children to use by preventing them from accessing or damaging it.  |例句：The cabinet was child-proofed with special locks.  橱柜安装了特殊的锁，以防止儿童接触。

例句：We need to child-proof the house before the baby arrives.  婴儿出生前我们需要给房子采取防儿童措施。


近义词：baby-proof, secure, protect


反义词： endanger, expose


词性:动词
