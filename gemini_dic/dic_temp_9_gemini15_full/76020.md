furfuration:/ˌfɜːrfjuˈreɪʃən/| n. |糠秕；麸皮；脱屑；皮屑|The shedding of scales or bran-like particles from the skin, especially the scalp.  This can be a symptom of various skin conditions.|Example:  The doctor noted the furfuration on the patient's scalp, suggesting a possible case of dandruff.  医生注意到病人头皮上的糠秕，这表明可能是头皮屑。|近义词： scaling, desquamation, exfoliation|反义词：  None directly opposite, but  *smooth skin* could be considered a contrasting state.|
