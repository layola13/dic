dradge:/drædʒ/| v. | 艰难地拖曳；费力地挖掘；|To pull or drag something heavy or difficult along the ground; to dredge up something unpleasant from the past.|例句：He had to dradge the heavy sack across the field. 他不得不费力地把沉重的麻袋拖过田地。
例句：They dredged up old scandals to smear the candidate's reputation. 他们挖出旧丑闻来抹黑候选人的名誉。|近义词：drag, haul, tow, excavate |反义词：lift, elevate

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drags the heavy box. = 他拖着沉重的箱子。
They dredge the harbor. = 他们疏浚港口。

一般过去时:
She dragged the suitcase up the stairs. = 她把行李箱拖上楼梯。
The workers dredged the riverbed. = 工人们疏浚了河床。

He has dragged himself to the finish line. = 他拖着疲惫的身躯到达了终点线。
They have dredged up some embarrassing secrets. = 他们挖出了一些令人尴尬的秘密。

过去完成时:
She had dragged the furniture all day. = 她已经拖了一整天的家具了。
They had dredged the canal before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经疏浚了运河。

被动语态:

疑问句:
