hfcs:/ˈeɪtʃɛfsiːz/| n. |高果糖玉米糖浆|High-fructose corn syrup (HFCS) is a sweetener made from corn starch. It's widely used in processed foods and beverages because it's cheaper than sugar and has a sweeter taste.|Many sodas and processed foods contain HFCS.|近义词：corn syrup, sucrose, glucose|反义词：None
