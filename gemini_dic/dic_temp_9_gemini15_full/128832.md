nightstand: /ˈnaɪtˌstænd/ | n. |床头柜| A small table placed beside a bed, used for holding a lamp, books, and other personal items.  |例句：I placed my book and glasses on the nightstand before going to sleep. 我睡觉前把书和眼镜放在床头柜上。

例句：She kept a glass of water and a small alarm clock on her nightstand. 她的床头柜上放着一杯水和一个小闹钟。


近义词：bedside table, night table| 反义词：无
