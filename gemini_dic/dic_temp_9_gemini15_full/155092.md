petrography:/pɪˈtrɒɡrəfi/| n. |岩石学，岩石描述学|The study of rocks, their description, classification, and origin.  It involves detailed examination of rock samples using microscopes and other techniques.|例句：Petrography is essential for understanding the geological history of a region. 岩石学对于理解一个地区的地理历史至关重要。|例句：The petrography of the sample revealed the presence of quartz and feldspar. 样品的岩石学显示存在石英和长石。|近义词：lithology, petrology|反义词：无
