flushest:/ˈflʌʃɪst/| adj.|最红润的；最充裕的；最繁荣的|Most flushed; most abundant; most prosperous.  The word describes a state of being intensely red in the face, or a state of great abundance or prosperity.|例句：Her cheeks were flushest after the long run.  她长跑后脸颊最红润。 The company is in its flushest period, with record profits. 这家公司正处于最繁荣的时期，利润创下纪录。|近义词：ruddiest, reddest, most abundant, most prosperous|反义词：palest, most impoverished
