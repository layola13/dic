preponderant:/prɪˈpɒndərənt/| adj. | 主要的；占优势的；压倒一切的 |  Signifying something that is greater in number, power, or influence; outweighing others in importance.  | Example: The preponderant opinion among experts is that the climate is changing.  专家们主要的意见是气候正在变化。  |近义词：dominant, predominant, prevailing, surpassing | 反义词：minor, insignificant, subordinate

Example: His preponderant influence in the company was undeniable. 他在公司中占主导地位是不可否认的。


词源：

From the Latin word *praeponderare*, meaning "to outweigh".  The prefix *prae-* means "before" or "in front of", and *pondus* means "weight".  Therefore, the original meaning literally referred to something that weighed more than something else.
