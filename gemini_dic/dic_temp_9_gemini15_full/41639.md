corrections:/kəˈrekʃənz/ | n. | 校正；修正；改正 | The corrections to the manuscript were minor. |近义词：amendments, adjustments, rectifications |反义词：errors, mistakes


英文解析：The plural form of "correction," referring to the act of correcting something or the changes made to correct something.  It can refer to changes made to written work, spoken words, behavior, or any inaccuracies.

例句：

The teacher made several corrections on my essay.  老师在我的论文上做了几处修改。

He needed to make some corrections to his calculations. 他需要对他的计算结果做一些修正。
