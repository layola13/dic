pixilate:/ˈpɪksəleɪt/| vt.|使像素化；使画面模糊|To break an image down into pixels, often resulting in a loss of detail and a blocky appearance.  This is often done intentionally to obscure an image, usually a face, for privacy reasons or to censor content.|The police pixilated the suspect's face in the security footage.  警方在安全录像中将嫌疑人的脸部像素化了。|blur, obscure, pixelate|sharpen, clarify|动词
