leapin': /ˈliːpɪn/ | adj. | 飞跃的；跳跃的；精力充沛的 |  A word formed by adding -in to "leap", often used informally to describe something energetic, exciting, or fast-paced. It doesn't have a formal dictionary definition, but its meaning is derived from the action of leaping.  | 例句：The music was leapin' and the crowd went wild.  音乐节奏飞快，人群疯狂起来。  |近义词：bounding, jumping, energetic, lively | 反义词：slow, sluggish, lethargic | 形容词
