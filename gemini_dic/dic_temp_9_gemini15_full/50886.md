deserter:/dɪˈzɜːrtər/|/ˈdɛzɚtɚ/| n. |逃兵；脱党者；叛逃者 |A deserter is a person who has abandoned their duties or responsibilities, especially a soldier who has left their military unit without permission.  |The deserter was captured and court-martialed.  这个逃兵被抓获并接受了军事法庭的审判。|renegade, runaway, defector|loyalist, patriot

The soldier deserted his post during the battle.  士兵在战斗中擅离职守。

现在完成时:
He has deserted his family. 他已经抛弃了他的家人。

过去完成时:
The soldiers had deserted before the enemy attacked.  敌人进攻之前，士兵们已经逃跑了。

将来时:
