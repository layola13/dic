grappler:/ˈɡræplər/| n. | 抓住者；角斗者；擒抱者；抓斗 | A person or thing that grapples.  A grappler in wrestling is someone skilled in grappling techniques.  A grappling hook is a tool used for grappling. |例句：The experienced grappler easily subdued his opponent.  经验丰富的角斗者轻松制服了他的对手。

The powerful grappler secured the heavy object with its claw.  强大的抓斗用爪子牢牢地抓住了重物。

|近义词：wrestler, fighter, contender, hook |反义词：releaser, liberator |名词
