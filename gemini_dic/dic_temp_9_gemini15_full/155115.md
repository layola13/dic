petromyzont:/ˌpɛtrəˈmaɪzɒnt/| n. |七鳃鳗；八目鳗|A petromyzont is a jawless fish belonging to the order Petromyzontiformes.  They are characterized by their eel-like body, circular mouth with rasping teeth, and seven gill openings. They are parasitic, attaching themselves to other fish to feed on their blood and bodily fluids. |Example: The researcher studied the parasitic behavior of the petromyzont in the river. 研究人员研究了河里七鳃鳗的寄生行为。|近义词：lamprey (more common)|反义词：无
