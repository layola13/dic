invisible:/ɪnˈvɪzəbl/| adj. | 看不见的，无形的 | Not able to be seen.  Something invisible is not visible to the eye. | Example: The magician made the rabbit invisible. 魔术师让兔子消失了。The air is invisible. 空气是看不见的。|近义词：unseen, imperceptible, hidden |反义词：visible, apparent, obvious

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ghost is invisible to the naked eye.  鬼眼无法看见。
This problem is invisible to many people.  这个问题对许多人来说是看不见的。


一般过去时:
The plane became invisible in the clouds. 飞机在云层中消失了。
The ship was invisible in the fog. 船在雾中消失了。


现在完成时:
The flaws in the system have remained invisible until now. 系统中的缺陷直到现在才暴露出来。
The truth has remained invisible for many years. 真相多年来一直隐藏着。


过去完成时:
The thief had made himself invisible before the police arrived. 警察到达之前，小偷已经藏了起来。
The danger had been invisible until it was too late. 危险直到为时已晚才显现出来。


将来时:
The spaceship will become invisible as it enters hyperspace. 宇宙飞船进入超空间后将变得隐形。
The new technology will make the object invisible. 新技术将使该物体隐形。


虚拟语气:
If the device worked, the car would be invisible. 如果设备有效，汽车将隐形。
I wish my mistakes were invisible. 我希望我的错误是看不见的。


被动语态:
The details were made invisible by the encryption.  细节被加密隐藏了。
The problem is often made invisible by those in power. 这个问题常常被当权者隐瞒。


疑问句:
Is the writing invisible under ultraviolet light? 在紫外线下文字是否看不见？
Are the flaws invisible to the casual observer? 这些缺陷对于粗心的观察者来说是否看不见？


否定句:
The damage is not invisible; it's clearly visible.  损坏并非看不见；它清晰可见。
The solution to the problem is not invisible; it's quite clear.  解决这个问题的方法并非看不见；它非常清楚。
