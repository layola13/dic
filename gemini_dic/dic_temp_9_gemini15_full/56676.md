doodling:/ˈduːdlɪŋ/| n.|涂鸦，乱画|The act of drawing or scribbling something without thinking about it, often absentmindedly.|例句：I was doodling in my notebook during the boring lecture. 我在无聊的讲座上在笔记本上乱涂乱画。|近义词：scribbling, sketching (informal)|反义词：drawing (precise), painting (detailed)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I doodle when I'm on the phone. = 我打电话的时候会乱涂乱画。
He doodles intricate patterns. = 他乱涂乱画复杂的图案。

一般过去时:
She doodled a funny face on her notepad. = 她在她记事本上涂鸦了一个滑稽的脸。
They doodled during the meeting. = 他们在会议期间乱涂乱画。

现在完成时:
I have doodled all over my margins. = 我在我的页边空白处到处乱涂乱画。
She has doodled since she was a child. = 她从小就喜欢乱涂乱画。


过去完成时:
I had doodled a whole page before I realized it. = 我在意识到之前已经涂鸦了一整页。
He had doodled many designs before settling on one. = 他在确定一个设计之前已经涂鸦了很多设计。

将来时:
I will doodle while I listen to the presentation. = 我将在听报告时涂鸦。
She will doodle something creative. = 她将会涂鸦一些有创意的东西。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would doodle more often. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更频繁地涂鸦。
I wish I could doodle as well as he can. = 我希望我的涂鸦能力也能像他一样好。


被动语态:
The margins were doodled on. = 页边空白处被涂鸦了。
A whole page was doodled full of hearts. = 一整页都被涂满了心形图案。


疑问句:
Do you often doodle? = 你经常涂鸦吗？
What are you doodling? = 你在涂鸦什么？


否定句:
I don't doodle very often. = 我不经常涂鸦。
He didn't doodle during the important meeting. = 他在重要的会议期间没有涂鸦。


PHRASE->
doodle on = 在...上涂鸦
doodle away =  消磨时间（通过涂鸦）
start doodling = 开始涂鸦
stop doodling = 停止涂鸦
enjoy doodling = 喜欢涂鸦
