poirier:/pɔɪˈriːeɪ/| n. |梨树；杜梨；(姓氏)波里埃|A pear tree; a type of pear.  The word can also be a surname.|例句：The orchard contained many poirier trees bearing sweet fruit.  这片果园里有很多结着甜果的梨树。

例句：Many famous people have the surname Poirier. 许多名人姓波里埃。


近义词：pear tree (if referring to the tree),  pyrus (genus name)|反义词：无|词性：名词


词源:  The word "poirier" comes from the Old French word "poiriers," meaning "pear tree."  This, in turn, derives from the Latin word "pirus," also meaning "pear tree."
