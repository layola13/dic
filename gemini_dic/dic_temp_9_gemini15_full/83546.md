hadron:/ˈhædrɒn/ | n. | 强子 | A hadron is a composite particle made of two or more quarks held together by the strong force.  Protons and neutrons are examples of hadrons. | Example: Protons and neutrons are both hadrons, fundamental components of atomic nuclei.  质子和中子都是强子，是原子核的基本组成部分。 |近义词：baryon, meson (depending on quark composition) |反义词：lepton
