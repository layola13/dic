cross-border: /ˈkrɒsˌbɔːrdər/ | adj. |跨境的，跨国界的| Relating to or involving activities or transactions that take place across national or regional borders.  |例句：The company is expanding its cross-border e-commerce business. 这家公司正在拓展其跨境电子商务业务。

例句：The agreement aims to facilitate cross-border trade between the two countries.  该协议旨在促进两国之间的跨境贸易。


近义词：transnational, international, interregional


反义词：domestic, internal, local


词性:形容词
