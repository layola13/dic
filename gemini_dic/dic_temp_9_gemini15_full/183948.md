resetting: /ˌriˈsɛtɪŋ/ | v. | 重置；重新设置 | The act of returning something to its original state or a default setting.  It often implies a process of restoring something to a previous condition or configuration. |

例句：
The computer is resetting after a system error. = 计算机在系统错误后正在重置。

The factory is resetting its production line. = 工厂正在重置其生产线。


近义词：restarting, rebooting, initializing

反义词：maintaining, preserving
