chews:/tʃuːz/| v.|咀嚼；反复考虑；|To bite and grind with the teeth.|The cow chews its cud.  母牛咀嚼着它的反刍食物。|He chewed on the problem for days. 他反复思考这个问题好几天。|bites, gnaws|swallows|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chews his food carefully. 他细嚼慢咽。
She chews gum all day. 她整天嚼口香糖。

一般过去时:
The dog chewed up my shoes. 狗把我的鞋子咬坏了。
I chewed on the idea for a while. 我考虑了一下这个主意。

现在完成时:
He has chewed through the rope. 他已经咬断绳子了。
She has been chewing her nails all afternoon. 她整个下午都在咬指甲。

过去完成时:
The dog had chewed the bone before I got home. 我回家之前，狗已经把骨头啃完了。
They had chewed over the contract for hours before signing. 他们在签合同之前已经讨论了好几个小时。

将来时:
I will chew this over before I decide. 我会在决定之前好好考虑一下。
She will chew the gum until it loses its flavor. 她会一直嚼口香糖直到它失去味道。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would chew over the proposal more carefully. 如果我有更多时间，我会更仔细地考虑这个提案。
I wish I hadn't chewed gum during the meeting. 我真希望我没有在会议上嚼口香糖。

被动语态:
The food was chewed thoroughly. 食物被彻底咀嚼了。
The problem has been chewed over by the committee. 这个问题已被委员会反复讨论。

疑问句:
Does he chew with his mouth open? 他张着嘴咀嚼吗？
What are you chewing on? 你在嚼什么？

否定句:
He doesn't chew his food properly. 他没有好好咀嚼食物。
She didn't chew the gum because it was stale. 她没有嚼口香糖，因为它不新鲜了。
