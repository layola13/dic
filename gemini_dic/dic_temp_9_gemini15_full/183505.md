repumps:/rɪˈpʌmpz/| v. |再次抽水；重新灌注；重新推动(政治运动等)|To pump again; to fill or refill something by pumping.  The term often implies a renewed effort or a revitalization of something that had previously slowed or stopped.|例句：The firefighters repumped the flooded basement. 消防队员再次抽干了被淹的地下室。
例句：The campaign manager tried to repump enthusiasm into the flagging political movement.  竞选经理试图重新燃起逐渐衰落的政治运动的热情。|近义词：replenish, refill, reinvigorate |反义词：drain, deplete
