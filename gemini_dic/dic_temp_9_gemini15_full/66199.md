excision:/ɪkˈsɪʒən/| n. |切除；删除；删节|The surgical removal of tissue.  The act of cutting something out or removing it. |例句：The excision of the tumor was successful.  肿瘤切除手术很成功。

例句：The editor made several excisions to shorten the article. 编辑删节了几处以缩短文章。


近义词：removal, resection, deletion, expurgation |反义词：insertion, addition, inclusion

词源:  From Old French *excisioun, from Latin *excisio, from excisus, past participle of excidere "to cut out," from ex- "out" + caedere "to cut."
