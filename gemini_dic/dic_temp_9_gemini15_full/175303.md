rainstorm:/ˈreɪnstɔːrm/|/ˈreɪnstɔːrm/| n. |暴雨；大雨 |A rainstorm is a storm with heavy rain.  It often involves strong winds and sometimes thunder and lightning. |例句：The rainstorm flooded the streets.  暴雨淹没了街道。

例句：We were caught in a sudden rainstorm. 我们遭遇了一场突如其来的暴雨。|近义词： downpour, deluge, cloudburst |反义词：sunny day, dry spell|名词

A rainstorm often occurs in summer. = 夏季经常发生暴雨。
The rainstorm brings much needed water to the parched land. = 暴雨给干旱的土地带来了急需的水分。


一般过去时:
A severe rainstorm hit the city last night. = 昨晚一场严重的暴雨袭击了这座城市。
The rainstorm delayed our flight. = 暴雨延误了我们的航班。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

The river had overflowed its banks before the rainstorm subsided.  = 暴雨消退之前，河水就已经泛滥成灾了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:
