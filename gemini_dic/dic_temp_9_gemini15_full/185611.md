revengers:/rɪˈvɛndʒərz/| n.|复仇者，报仇者|Those who seek revenge; people who avenge a wrong.  The word refers to a group of individuals united by a common goal of revenge.|例句：The revengers plotted their attack meticulously. 复仇者精心策划了他们的袭击。

例句：The film follows the story of a group of revengers seeking justice for a loved one. 这部电影讲述了一群为亲人寻求正义的复仇者。


近义词：avengers, retaliators, punishers

反义词：forgivers, pardoners


词性:名词
