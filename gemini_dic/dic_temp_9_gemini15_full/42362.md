counterassassinations:/ˌkaʊntərəˌsæsɪneɪʃənz/ | n. | 反暗杀行动；反暗杀 | The act of preventing or retaliating against assassinations.  It involves measures taken to protect a target from assassination attempts, or to eliminate those planning or carrying out such attempts. | Example: The government launched a series of counterassassinations to thwart the terrorist group's plans.  政府发起了一系列反暗杀行动来挫败恐怖组织的计划。|近义词: anti-assassination measures, preventative security, protective measures |反义词: assassination, murder, homicide |名词
