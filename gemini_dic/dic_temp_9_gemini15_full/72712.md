focuses:/ˈfoʊkəsɪz/| v. | 集中；专注；关注 | To direct one's attention or efforts towards a particular object or activity.  | The project focuses on improving education. 这个项目专注于改善教育。| concentrates, centers, emphasizes | diffuses, scatters, distracts | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He focuses on his studies. = 他专注于他的学习。
The company focuses on customer satisfaction. = 公司专注于客户满意度。

一般过去时:
She focused on the details of the report. = 她专注于报告的细节。
The speaker focused on the main points of his presentation. = 演讲者专注于演讲的主要内容。

现在完成时:
They have focused on renewable energy sources. = 他们一直专注于可再生能源。
We have focused our efforts on finding a solution. = 我们一直努力寻找解决方案。

过去完成时:
He had focused on his career before starting a family. = 在组建家庭之前，他一直专注于他的事业。
She had focused her attention on the problem for hours.  = 她几个小时都把注意力集中在问题上。

将来时:
We will focus on expanding our business into new markets. = 我们将专注于将业务扩展到新的市场。
They will focus their research on a new type of medicine. = 他们将把研究重点放在一种新型药物上。

虚拟语气:
If he focused more, he would achieve better results. = 如果他更专注，他会取得更好的成绩。
I wish I had focused more on my studies in college. = 我希望我在大学里能更专注于学习。

被动语态:
Attention was focused on the victim. = 注意力集中在受害者身上。
The discussion was focused on the budget. = 讨论的重点是预算。

疑问句:
What does the article focus on? = 这篇文章的重点是什么？
Where does your company focus its resources? = 贵公司将资源集中在哪里？

否定句:
He doesn't focus on the task at hand. = 他没有专注于手头的任务。
The report doesn't focus enough on the key findings. = 这份报告没有足够关注关键发现。


PHRASE->
focus on = 专注于
focus group = 焦点小组
bring into focus = 使聚焦；使清晰
out of focus = 模糊的；不清楚的
in focus = 清晰的；集中的
