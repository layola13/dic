lutenists:/ˈluːtənɪsts/| n. |鲁特琴演奏家们|Players of the lute, a plucked stringed instrument.  The word refers to a group or collective of lute players. |The lutenists performed a beautiful rendition of a Renaissance piece. 鲁特琴演奏家们演奏了一首美丽的文艺复兴时期的作品。| musicians, instrumentalists |  (no direct antonym,  but perhaps "vocalists" could be considered a contrasting term)|noun
