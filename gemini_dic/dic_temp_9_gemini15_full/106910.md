lacerating: /ˈlæsəreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 撕裂的；痛苦的；尖刻的 |  causing deep emotional pain; severely critical;  tearing or ripping apart |

英文解析:  The word "lacerating" describes something that causes deep emotional pain or distress, often through harsh criticism or a violent act. It can also refer to a physical act of tearing or ripping something apart.

例句:

His lacerating criticism left her in tears. = 他尖刻的批评让她泪流满面。

The accident left him with several lacerating wounds. = 那次事故导致他身上多处撕裂伤。


近义词:  wrenching, agonizing, agonizing, searing, scathing, harsh, critical, tearing, ripping


反义词: soothing, comforting, gentle, kind, healing


词性: adjective (形容词)
