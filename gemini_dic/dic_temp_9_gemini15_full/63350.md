entombed:/ɪnˈtuːmd/ | v. | 埋葬；被埋葬 |  To place (a body) in a tomb; to bury.  Also, to enclose or encase as if in a tomb; to imprison or confine. | 例句：The pharaoh was entombed with all his treasures.  法老和他的所有财宝一起被埋葬了。

The ancient city was entombed under tons of volcanic ash.  这座古城被数吨火山灰掩埋了。


近义词：bury, inter, encase, immure | 反义词：exhume, unearth
