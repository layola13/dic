querying:/ˈkwɪəriŋ/| v.| 查询；询问；质询 | The act of asking questions or seeking information.  It implies a thorough investigation or a search for answers. |例句：The police are querying the suspect's alibi. 警方正在盘问嫌疑人的不在场证明。
例句：She spent hours querying databases for information on the topic. 她花了几个小时查询数据库以获取有关该主题的信息。|近义词：asking, questioning, investigating, inquiring |反义词：answering, responding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am querying the database for relevant information. = 我正在查询数据库以获取相关信息。
They are querying the witnesses about the accident. = 他们正在询问目击者关于事故的情况。

一般过去时:
The detective queried the suspect's story. = 侦探盘问了嫌疑人的说法。
She queried the company about their return policy. = 她向公司询问了他们的退货政策。

现在完成时:
I have queried the system multiple times but haven't received a response. = 我已经多次查询了系统，但没有收到回复。
They have queried the data and found some inconsistencies. = 他们已经查询了数据，并发现了一些不一致之处。

过去完成时:
He had queried the accounts before realizing the error. = 他在意识到错误之前已经查询了账户。
She had queried the database several times without success. = 她已经多次查询数据库，但没有成功。

将来时:
I will query the server for updated information. = 我将查询服务器以获取更新的信息。
They will query the residents about their opinions on the new project. = 他们将询问居民对新项目的意见。

虚拟语气:
If I queried the database correctly, I would have found the answer. = 如果我正确地查询了数据库，我就能找到答案。
I wish I had queried the system earlier. = 我希望我早点查询系统。

被动语态:
The database is being queried by the user. = 数据库正在被用户查询。
The system was queried for information. = 系统被查询以获取信息。

疑问句:
Are you querying the data properly? = 你在正确地查询数据吗？
Is he querying the database for specific information? = 他是在查询数据库以获取特定信息吗？

否定句:
I am not querying the system at the moment. = 我目前没有在查询系统。
He did not query the database before making the decision. = 他在做决定之前没有查询数据库。
