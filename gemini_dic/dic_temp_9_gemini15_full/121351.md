misgovernor:/ˌmɪsˈɡʌvərnər/| n. |不称职的统治者；不良的管理者 | A misgovernor is a person who governs badly or unfairly, often characterized by corruption, incompetence, or tyranny.  They fail to effectively manage their responsibilities, leading to negative consequences for those under their rule. | Example: The misgovernor's reign was marked by widespread poverty and social unrest.  这个不称职的统治者的统治时期以普遍的贫困和社会动荡为特征。 |近义词：tyrant, despot, oppressor, bad administrator |反义词：good governor, just ruler, wise leader |名词
