pop-the-question:/pɑːp ðə ˈkwɛstʃən/| phrasal verb|求婚|To propose marriage to someone.  This idiom refers to the act of asking someone to marry you. The "pop" suggests a sudden, perhaps slightly informal, way of asking the question. |例句：He finally popped the question on their anniversary. 他终于在他们的周年纪念日那天求婚了。

He nervously popped the question during a romantic dinner. 他在浪漫的晚餐中紧张地提出了求婚。


近义词：propose, propose marriage, ask for someone's hand in marriage |反义词：N/A

PHRASE->
pop the question to someone: 向某人求婚
pop the question unexpectedly: 意外地求婚
pop the question in a romantic setting: 在浪漫的环境中求婚
pop the question after dating for several years: 约会几年后求婚
pop the question and get rejected: 求婚被拒绝


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He pops the question every Valentine's Day. 他每年情人节都求婚。
She usually pops the question after a romantic getaway. 她通常在浪漫的短途旅行后求婚。


一般过去时:
He popped the question last night. 他昨晚求婚了。
She popped the question during their trip to Paris.  她在他们去巴黎的旅行期间求婚了。


现在完成时:
He has popped the question three times already. 他已经求婚三次了。
She has popped the question, but he hasn't answered yet. 她已经求婚了，但他还没回答。


过去完成时:
He had popped the question before she even realized he was serious. 他在她意识到他是认真的之前就已经求婚了。
She had popped the question several times before he finally agreed. 她求婚好几次他才终于同意。


将来时:
He will pop the question on her birthday. 他将在她的生日那天求婚。
She will pop the question after she graduates. 她毕业后会求婚。


虚拟语气:
If he popped the question, she would say yes. 如果他求婚，她会答应的。
I wish he had popped the question sooner. 我希望他早点求婚。


被动语态:
The question was popped during a beautiful sunset.  求婚是在美丽的日落时分进行的。(This usage is less common)
The proposal was carefully planned before the question was popped.  在求婚之前，求婚计划被精心策划。(This usage is less common)


疑问句:
When will he pop the question? 他什么时候求婚？
Has she popped the question yet? 她已经求婚了吗？


否定句:
He didn't pop the question. 他没有求婚。
She hasn't popped the question yet. 她还没求婚。
