cass:/kæs/| n.|盒子，箱子；保险箱|A container, typically made of wood or metal, with a lid and used for storage.  |例句：He kept his valuables in a strong cass. 他把贵重物品放在一个坚固的箱子里。|例句：The cass was filled with old photographs. 箱子里装满了旧照片。|近义词：box, chest, container|反义词：None
