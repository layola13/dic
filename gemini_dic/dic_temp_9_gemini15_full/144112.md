outscoop:/ˌaʊtˈskuːp/| vt.| 超过；胜过（尤指在获取信息方面）|to get or find out something (especially news or information) before someone else does |例句：The rival newspaper managed to outscoop ours on the story.  竞争对手的报纸在报道这条新闻上抢在我们前面。

例句：The investigative journalist outscooped all her competitors, breaking the story first.  这位调查记者胜过所有竞争对手，第一个报道了这条新闻。


近义词：outdo, surpass, beat, outstrip|反义词：be beaten by, be outdone by|词性:动词
