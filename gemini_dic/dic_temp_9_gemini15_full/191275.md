scattier:/ˈskætər/| adj.|分散的，散乱的|more scattered than before.  The meaning refers to something being spread over a wide area in a disorganized or irregular way.  It can describe physical objects or abstract concepts.|例句：The toys were scattered all over the floor.  玩具散落在地板上。

The papers were scattered across the desk. 文件散落在桌子上。|近义词：dispersed, spread, strewn, diffuse|反义词：concentrated, clustered, gathered
