reschooling:/ˌriːˈskuːlɪŋ/ | n. | 再教育；重新培训 | The process of educating someone again, often to provide them with new skills or knowledge, or to address learning deficiencies.  It can refer to individuals or groups undergoing retraining for a new career, or students needing additional support to catch up academically. | Example: The company offered reschooling to its employees to adapt to the changing market demands.  这家公司为员工提供再培训以适应不断变化的市场需求。  The school provided intensive reschooling for students who fell behind. 学校为落后的学生提供了强化再教育。| 近义词：retraining, re-education, further education | 反义词：de-schooling
