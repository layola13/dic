scummer:/ˈskʌmə(r)/| n. |卑鄙的人，败类，渣滓 | A scummer is a person considered to be despicable or morally reprehensible.  The term often implies a lack of integrity, dishonesty, or exploitation of others.  It carries a strong negative connotation and is often used informally and in a derogatory manner. | Example: He's a scummer who cheated his employees out of their wages. 他是个卑鄙的人，克扣了员工的工资。|近义词：villain, scoundrel, rat, creep, lowlife |反义词：saint, hero, paragon, angel
