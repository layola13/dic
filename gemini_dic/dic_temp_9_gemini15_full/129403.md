nominalistic: /ˌnɒmɪˈnælɪstɪk/ | /ˌnɑːmɪˈnælɪstɪk/ | adj. | 名义上的；唯名论的 |

英文解析：Relating to or characteristic of nominalism, the philosophical doctrine that denies the existence of universals and abstract concepts.  It can also describe something that exists only in name or is insignificant in reality.

例句：

The company's nominalistic growth is deceptive; their profits are actually declining.  公司的名义增长具有欺骗性；他们的利润实际上正在下降。

He held a nominalistic position on the board, with little real power. 他在董事会中担任一个名义上的职位，几乎没有实际权力。


近义词： titular, nominal, superficial, insignificant, theoretical

反义词： real, substantial, significant, concrete

词性: adjective
