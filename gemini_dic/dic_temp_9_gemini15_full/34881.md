clip-ons:/ˈklɪpɑːnz/| n. |夹式眼镜,  夹式耳环|Clip-ons are a type of eyewear or earrings that clip onto existing frames or piercings, rather than being permanently attached. They offer a convenient and versatile way to change one's look.|例句：She wore clip-on earrings to match her dress. 她戴着夹式耳环来搭配她的衣服。  He switched to clip-on sunglasses when the sun came out. 太阳出来后，他换上了夹式太阳镜。|近义词：attachments, add-ons|反义词：permanently attached items
