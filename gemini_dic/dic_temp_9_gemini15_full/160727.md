portraying:/pɔːrˈtreɪɪŋ/| v.|描绘，扮演，刻画 |The act of representing something or someone, typically in a work of art.  It involves depicting the character or qualities of something. |例句：The painting portrays a serene landscape. 这幅画描绘了一幅宁静的风景。

The actor is portraying Hamlet with great intensity.  这位演员正在以极大的强度刻画哈姆雷特。

|近义词：depicting, representing, presenting, showing, illustrating |反义词：misrepresenting, distorting, concealing|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He portrays the character convincingly. = 他令人信服地刻画了这个角色。
The artist portrays nature's beauty in his paintings. = 艺术家在画作中描绘了大自然的美丽。


一般过去时:
She portrayed a strong female lead in the movie. = 她在电影中扮演了一个坚强的女主角。
The book portrayed the harsh realities of war. = 这本书描绘了战争的残酷现实。


现在完成时:
The author has portrayed the protagonist's struggles effectively. = 作者有效地刻画了主人公的挣扎。
The media has portrayed the situation inaccurately. = 媒体对局势的描述不准确。


过去完成时:

The artist had already portrayed the subject several times before. = 艺术家之前已经多次描绘过这个主题。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
