preincentive:/ˌpriːɪnˈsɛntɪv/| n. |激励措施（尤指在主要激励措施之前提供的）|A preincentive is a reward or benefit offered before the main incentive to encourage a particular behavior or action.  It's often used to build anticipation or to prime individuals for the larger reward to come.  For example, a company might offer a small bonus before a major project launch to keep employees motivated.|近义词：advance payment, preliminary reward, early bonus|反义词：postincentive, delayed reward|名词
