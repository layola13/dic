restless:/ˈrɛstlɪs/| adj.|不安的；焦躁的；坐立不安的|Unable to relax or be still; fidgety.|例句：The children were restless after a long journey. 长途旅行后，孩子们坐立不安。|例句：He felt restless and irritable. 他感到焦躁易怒。|近义词：uneasy, fidgety, agitated, anxious|反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil, serene


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is restless tonight. = 他今晚坐立不安。
She is always restless in class. = 她上课总是坐立不安。


一般过去时:
The baby was restless all night. = 宝宝整晚都睡不安稳。
I was restless and couldn't sleep. = 我辗转反侧，睡不着觉。


现在完成时:
I have been restless since I heard the news. = 自从听到这个消息后，我一直很不安。
She has been restless all day. = 她整天都坐立不安。


过去完成时:
I had been restless for days before the exam. = 考试前几天我一直都坐立不安。
He had been restless all week, waiting for the results. = 他等了一周的结果，一直都坐立不安。


将来时:
I will be restless until I hear from him. = 在收到他的消息之前，我会一直很不安。
She will probably be restless if she has to stay indoors all day. = 如果她必须整天待在室内，她可能会坐立不安。


虚拟语气:
I would be restless if I were in his shoes. = 如果我是他，我会坐立不安。
If I knew the truth, I wouldn't be so restless. = 如果我知道真相，我就不会这么不安了。


被动语态:
(Restless is rarely used in the passive voice.  A more natural phrasing would be needed to express the passive idea.)  The situation made him restless. = 这种情况使他不安。
His actions were described as restless by the observers. = 观察者将他的行为描述为不安的。


疑问句:
Are you restless? = 你不安吗？
Is the baby restless again? = 宝宝又不安了吗？


否定句:
He is not restless at all. = 他一点也不不安。
She wasn't restless; she was simply bored. = 她不是不安，只是无聊。


PHRASE->

feel restless = 感到不安
