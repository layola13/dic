name-callings: /ˈneɪmˌkɔːlɪŋz/ | n. |辱骂；人身攻击 |The act of insulting someone by using offensive or derogatory names.  Name-calling is a common form of bullying and can be very hurtful.  |近义词：insults, verbal abuse, taunts, slurs |反义词：praise, compliments


例句：

The children engaged in name-calling during the playground argument. 孩子们在操场争吵时互相辱骂。

His constant name-callings created a hostile work environment. 他不断的辱骂造成了一个充满敌意的工作环境。
