priorities:/praɪˈɒrətiz/ /praɪˈɔːrətiz/ | n. |优先级；优先考虑的事 |Things that are considered more important than others and need to be dealt with first.  Priorities are often ranked in order of importance.| 例句：My priorities are my family and my health. 我的首要任务是我的家人和健康。

例句：
The company's priorities are to increase profits and expand into new markets. 公司的当务之急是增加利润和拓展新市场。


近义词：preferences, necessities, imperatives
反义词：non-essentials, trivialities


词源：
From Middle French priorité, from Old French priorité, from Latin prioritās "priority, precedence," from prior "former, earlier," from pro "before" + -or (suffix denoting agent or state).


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are your priorities for this project? = 你这个项目的优先级是什么？
Her priorities are always her children. = 她的首要任务永远是她的孩子。

一般过去时:
What were your priorities last year? = 你去年的首要任务是什么？
His priorities shifted after the accident. = 事故发生后，他的优先级发生了变化。

现在完成时:
What have your priorities been this month? = 这个月你的优先事项是什么？
She has always prioritized her career. = 她一直把事业放在首位。

过去完成时:
What had been his priorities before the emergency? = 紧急情况发生之前，他的优先事项是什么？
They had prioritized the urgent tasks. = 他们已经优先处理了紧急任务。

将来时:
What will your priorities be next year? = 你明年的优先事项是什么？
His priorities will change once he retires. = 他退休后，他的优先级将会改变。

虚拟语气:
If I had known my priorities, I would have finished the report earlier. = 如果我知道我的优先级，我会早点完成报告。
I wish I had prioritized my health more in the past. = 我希望过去更重视我的健康。

被动语态:
The tasks were prioritized according to their urgency. = 任务按照紧急程度进行了优先排序。
The budget has been prioritized based on the company's goals. = 预算已根据公司目标确定了优先级。

疑问句:
What are your top three priorities? = 你最重要的三个优先事项是什么？
Which priorities should we focus on? = 我们应该关注哪些优先事项？

否定句:
I don't know what my priorities are. = 我不知道我的优先级是什么。
They did not prioritize the issues correctly. = 他们没有正确地确定问题的优先级。
