facading:/fəˈsɑːdɪŋ/ | n. | 门面；虚饰；外观 | The act of creating a facade;  the process of presenting a false or superficial appearance.  It can refer to the physical facade of a building or a metaphorical facade concealing something else. |例句：The company's recent success is just facading; their financial reports show a different story.  这家公司最近的成功只是表面现象；他们的财务报表显示了不同的情况。 |近义词：veneer, pretense, sham, front |反义词：authenticity, genuineness, reality
