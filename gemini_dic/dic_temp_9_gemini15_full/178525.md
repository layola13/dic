reconverges:/ˌriːkənˈvɜːrdʒəz/| v.|重新汇聚，重新聚合|To come together again; to converge again.|例句：The scattered beams of light reconverge at a focal point. 散射的光束在焦点处重新汇聚。  The separated paths of the river reconverge downstream. 分离的河流路径在下游重新汇合。|近义词：recombine, reunite, coalesce|反义词：diverge, separate, disperse|动词
