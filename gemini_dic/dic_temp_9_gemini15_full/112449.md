loudest: /ˈlaʊdəst/ | adj. | 最响亮的，最大的 |  The superlative form of "loud," describing the highest degree of loudness or volume. It indicates the greatest intensity of sound among a group or within a context. | 例句：The loudest sound came from the construction site. 最响亮的声音来自建筑工地。

The band played their loudest song during the encore. 乐队在返场演出中演奏了他们最响亮的歌曲。


近义词：most intense, most powerful (referring to sound) | 反义词：quietest, faintest, softest | 词性：形容词
