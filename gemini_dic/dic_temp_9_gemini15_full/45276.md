/kʌlˈtɪveɪtɪd/ | adj. |  耕作的；培养的；有教养的 | Cultivated refers to land that has been prepared for growing crops, or to someone who is refined and well-educated. It can also describe the process of improving or developing something. |

The farmer cultivated the field before planting the seeds.  = 农民在播种前耕种了田地。

She has a cultivated taste in art. = 她对艺术有着高雅的品味。


近义词：refined, polished, cultured, sophisticated | 反义词：uncultivated, unrefined, crude, rustic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cultivate their garden every weekend. = 他们每个周末都耕种他们的花园。
He cultivates a positive attitude. = 他培养积极的态度。


一般过去时:
The ancient Egyptians cultivated wheat and barley. = 古埃及人种植小麦和大麦。
She cultivated a friendship with her neighbor. = 她和邻居培养了友谊。


现在完成时:
Farmers have cultivated this land for centuries. = 农民们已经耕种这块土地几个世纪了。
He has cultivated a reputation for honesty. = 他培养了诚实的声誉。


过去完成时:
They had cultivated the soil before the rain came. = 雨来之前他们已经耕耘了土壤。
She had cultivated her singing voice for years before her debut. =  在她首次亮相之前，她已经培养了多年的歌声。


将来时:
They will cultivate more crops next year. = 他们明年将种植更多的农作物。
She will cultivate a new skill in her spare time. = 她将在业余时间培养一项新技能。


虚拟语气:
If they cultivated the land properly, they would have a better harvest. = 如果他们正确地耕种土地，他们将会有更好的收成。
If I cultivated patience, I would be a better person. = 如果我培养耐心，我会成为更好的人。


被动语态:
The land was cultivated by the farmers. = 这块土地是由农民耕种的。
His talent was cultivated by his teacher. = 他的天赋是由他的老师培养的。


疑问句:
Do they cultivate organic vegetables? = 他们种植有机蔬菜吗？
Has she cultivated her musical talent? = 她培养了她的音乐天赋吗？


否定句:
They don't cultivate that type of flower. = 他们不种植那种花。
He hasn't cultivated a strong work ethic. = 他没有培养强烈的职业道德。
