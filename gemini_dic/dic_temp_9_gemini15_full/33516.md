/ˈsɪsəli/| n. |西塞莉（女子名）|Cicely is a female given name, a variant of Cecilia.  It's of Roman origin, derived from the Roman family name Caecilius, which is believed to have come from the Latin word *caecus*, meaning "blind".  The name is associated with Saint Cecilia, the patron saint of music. |My sister's name is Cicely.  我的妹妹名叫西塞莉。|Cecilia, Cecily|  (no direct antonym, as it's a proper noun)|名词
