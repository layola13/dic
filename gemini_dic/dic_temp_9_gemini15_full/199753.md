sirenize:/ˈsaɪrənaɪz/| vt.|使…发出警报声；用警报声警告|to make a sound like a siren; to warn with a siren|The factory sirenized loudly at the end of the shift.|工厂在轮班结束时响起了响亮的警报声。| 例句2: The approaching storm sirenized the coastal residents.|即将来临的风暴用警报声警告了沿海居民。|近义词：alarm, alert|反义词：silence, hush|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They sirenize the city every night at 10 pm. = 他们每天晚上10点用警报声警告城市。
The fire alarm sirenizes when it detects smoke. = 当火灾警报探测到烟雾时，它会发出警报声。


一般过去时:
The police sirenized to warn drivers of the accident. = 警察鸣响警报以警告司机发生事故。
The factory sirenized at noon to signal lunch break. = 工厂在中午鸣响警报以表示午休时间。


现在完成时:
The city has sirenized several times today due to severe weather. = 由于恶劣天气，该市今天已经多次发出警报。
They have sirenized the area to warn of the approaching danger. = 他们已经向该地区发出警报以警告即将到来的危险。


过去完成时:
The residents had sirenized before the earthquake struck. = 在地震发生之前，居民们已经发出了警报。
The system had sirenized repeatedly, but no one had heeded the warnings. = 系统已经反复发出警报，但没有人理会警告。


将来时:
The authorities will sirenize the public if a tsunami warning is issued. = 如果发布海啸预警，当局将向公众发出警报。
The school will sirenize at the end of the day to dismiss students. = 学校将在一天结束时发出警报以让学生放学。


虚拟语气:
If the system sirenized correctly, the accident could have been prevented. = 如果系统正确发出警报，事故本来是可以避免的。
I wish the warning system had sirenized sooner to give us more time to evacuate. = 我希望预警系统能早点发出警报，给我们更多的时间疏散。


被动语态:
The city was sirenized at dawn to announce the curfew. = 城市在黎明时分发出警报以宣布宵禁。
The area was sirenized repeatedly to alert residents of the impending threat. = 该地区反复发出警报以提醒居民即将到来的威胁。


疑问句:
Did they sirenize the neighborhood last night? = 他们昨晚有没有向该街区发出警报？
Will the factory sirenize at the usual time today? = 工厂今天会在通常的时间发出警报吗？


否定句:
The siren did not sirenize during the power outage. = 在停电期间，警报器没有发出警报。
They didn't sirenize the people quickly enough. = 他们没有及时向人们发出警报。
