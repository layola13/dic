plot:/plɑːt/| /ˈplɒt/| n. vt.|情节；密谋；绘图；标绘|

英文解析:
As a noun, "plot" refers to the plan or main story of a narrative or dramatic work.  It can also mean a small piece of land, or a secret plan to do something harmful or illegal. As a verb, "plot" means to make a secret plan, usually to do something illegal or harmful, or to mark or draw on a map or chart.

例句:
The plot of the novel was intricate and suspenseful.  = 这部小说的情节错综复杂，扣人心弦。

The villain plotted to overthrow the king. = 恶棍密谋推翻国王。


近义词:
(n.) scheme, plan, story, storyline, conspiracy
(v.) scheme, conspire, plan, design, map

反义词:
(n.)  resolution (in the context of a story),  chance (in the context of a plan)
(v.)  reveal (opposite of plotting something secretly)


词性:
Noun, Verb
