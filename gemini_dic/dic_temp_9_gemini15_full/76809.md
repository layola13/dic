gamogenesis:/ˌɡæməˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/| n. |配子生殖；两性生殖|The process of sexual reproduction involving the fusion of gametes (sex cells) to form a zygote.  It is characterized by the union of male and female gametes, resulting in offspring with genetic material from both parents.|Example: Gamogenesis is the primary mode of reproduction in most animals and plants.|近义词：sexual reproduction|反义词：apomixis, asexual reproduction
