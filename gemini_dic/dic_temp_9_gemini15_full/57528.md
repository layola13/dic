drags:/dræɡz/ | n./v.|拖，拉；沉闷的人或事 | to pull (someone or something) along forcefully, often with difficulty; something tedious or boring |例句：He drags his feet when it comes to doing his homework. 他做作业拖拖拉拉。The meeting dragged on for hours. 会议冗长乏味地持续了好几个小时。|近义词：pull, haul, tow, bore, tedious|反义词：push, propel, exciting, stimulating|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drags the heavy box across the floor. = 他把沉重的箱子拖过地板。
The story drags on endlessly. = 故事冗长乏味地持续下去。

一般过去时:
She dragged the suitcase behind her. = 她把行李箱拖在身后。
The movie dragged and I almost fell asleep. = 电影很沉闷，我差点睡着。

现在完成时:
They have dragged the investigation out for months. = 他们已经把调查拖延了好几个月。
This project has dragged on longer than expected. = 这个项目比预期的持续时间更长。

过去完成时:
He had dragged himself to the finish line, exhausted. = 他筋疲力尽地把自己拖到了终点线。
The negotiations had dragged on for weeks with no resolution. = 谈判已经持续了数周，但没有结果。

将来时:
I will drag myself out of bed tomorrow morning. = 明天早上我将勉强起床。
The meeting will drag on if we don't stick to the agenda. = 如果我们不遵守议程，会议将会冗长乏味地持续下去。

虚拟语气:
If I had to drag myself through that again, I'd quit. = 如果我不得不再次经历那件事，我会辞职。
I wish this meeting wouldn't drag on so long. = 我希望这次会议不会持续这么久。

被动语态:
The heavy furniture was dragged into the house. = 沉重的家具被拖进了房子。
The case was dragged through the courts for years. = 这起案件在法庭上拖延了好几年。

疑问句:
Why are you dragging your feet? = 你为什么拖拖拉拉？
Does the movie drag? = 这部电影沉闷吗？

否定句:
He didn't drag the chair across the room. = 他没有把椅子拖过房间。
The conversation didn't drag; it was lively and engaging. = 会话并不沉闷；它生动有趣。
