nonrhymes:/ˈnɒnraɪmz/ | n. | 不押韵的词语 | Words or lines of verse that do not rhyme.  The poem is characterized by its use of nonrhymes. | 近义词：unrhymed words, blank verse  | 反义词：rhymes


USAGE->
This word is a noun, and as such does not conjugate through tenses like verbs.  It describes a characteristic of words or verses and therefore doesn't have a tense.  Its usage is primarily descriptive.


例句:
The poet deliberately used nonrhymes to create a sense of dissonance. = 诗人故意使用不押韵的词语来营造一种不和谐感。

His free verse poem relies heavily on nonrhymes. = 他的自由体诗歌很大程度上依赖于不押韵的词语。
