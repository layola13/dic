gigo:/ˈɡiːɡoʊ/|/ˈdʒaɪɡoʊ/| n. |垃圾进，垃圾出|Garbage In, Garbage Out;  refers to the fact that the quality of the output of a process is directly dependent on the quality of the input.  If you put bad data into a system, you will get bad data out. |Example: The analysis was flawed because of the gigo principle; the data used was unreliable.  垃圾进，垃圾出原则导致分析存在缺陷；所用数据不可靠。|近义词：erroneous output, flawed results |反义词：accurate output, reliable results |名词
