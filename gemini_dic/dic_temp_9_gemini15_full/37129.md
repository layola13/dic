come clean:/kʌm ˈkliːn/| v.|坦白；招供；彻底打扫|To confess to wrongdoing; to tell the truth about something you have been hiding.  It can also mean to thoroughly clean something.|例句：He finally came clean about his involvement in the crime. 他最终坦白了他参与犯罪的事实。
例句：It's time to come clean about your finances. 该坦白你的财务状况了。|近义词：confess, admit, reveal|反义词：conceal, hide, deny|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He comes clean about his mistakes every time. = 他每次都会坦白自己的错误。
They always come clean after a big fight. = 他们每次大吵一架后都会坦白。


一般过去时:
She came clean to her parents about the accident. = 她向父母坦白了事故的真相。
The thief came clean to the police after days of hiding. = 小偷躲藏几天后向警察坦白了。


现在完成时:
He has come clean about his gambling debts. = 他已经坦白了他的赌债。
She has finally come clean about her past. = 她终于坦白了自己的过去。


过去完成时:
He had come clean to his boss before the meeting. = 他在会议之前已经向老板坦白了。
They had come clean about their plans before we left. = 在我们离开之前，他们已经坦白了他们的计划。


将来时:
I think he will come clean eventually. = 我认为他最终会坦白的。
She promises she will come clean about everything tomorrow. = 她承诺明天会把一切都坦白。


虚拟语气:
If he came clean, he would feel much better. = 如果他坦白了，他会感觉好得多。
I wish he had come clean sooner. = 我真希望他早点坦白。


被动语态:
The truth was eventually come clean about by the suspect. =  真相最终被嫌疑人坦白了。（被动语态略显不自然，come clean 通常用于主动语态）
His lies were uncovered; he had to come clean. = 他的谎言被揭穿了；他不得不坦白。(并非come clean 的被动语态，而是结果)


疑问句:
Will he ever come clean about what happened? = 他会坦白发生的事情吗？
Did she come clean to the authorities? = 她向当局坦白了吗？


否定句:
He didn't come clean about his role in the incident. = 他没有坦白他在事件中扮演的角色。
She refused to come clean, even under pressure. = 即使在压力下，她也不肯坦白。


PHRASE->
come clean about something =  坦白某事
come clean to someone = 向某人坦白
come clean with the truth =  说出真相
come clean and confess = 坦白并认罪
come clean with everything = 全盘托出
