overattenuate: /ˌoʊvərəˈtɛnjuˌeɪt/ | vt. |过度衰减；过度减弱| To weaken or reduce the strength of something excessively.  This term is often used in technical contexts, particularly in fields like electronics and signal processing, referring to the excessive reduction of amplitude or intensity of a signal. | Example: The amplifier overattenuated the signal, resulting in a weak output.  放大器过度衰减了信号，导致输出信号微弱。|近义词: excessively weaken, overdamp, excessively reduce | 反义词: amplify, enhance, boost |
