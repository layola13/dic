overblow:/ˈoʊvərbloʊ/ | vt. |夸大；吹嘘；(风)猛吹；(乐器)吹得太响 | To exaggerate or make something seem more important than it is.  It can also refer to strong wind or playing a musical instrument too loudly. |例句：He overblew the importance of his role in the project. 他夸大了他在项目中的作用。

例句：The wind overblew the small boat. 风把小船吹翻了。


近义词：exaggerate, inflate, magnify, hype | 反义词：understate, downplay
