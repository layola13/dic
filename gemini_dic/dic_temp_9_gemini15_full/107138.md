ladylikeness:/ˈleɪdiˌlaɪknəs/ | n. |淑女风范，端庄，优雅 |The quality or state of being ladylike;  refined and elegant behavior characteristic of a lady.  It encompasses grace, politeness, and decorum. |例句：Her ladylikeness was undeniable, evident in her poised demeanor and gentle speech.  她的淑女风范不容置疑，体现在她优雅的举止和温柔的谈吐中。


例句：The ballet dancer possessed a remarkable ladylikeness on and off stage.  这位芭蕾舞演员台上台下都展现出非凡的淑女风范。


近义词：elegance, grace, refinement, gentility, decorum


反义词：vulgarity, crudeness, coarseness, indelicacy, boorishness


词性: 名词
