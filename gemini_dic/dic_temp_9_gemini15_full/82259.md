groundhog:/ˈɡraʊndhɔːɡ/|/ˈɡraʊndhɑːɡ/| n. |土拨鼠|A rodent of the family Sciuridae, similar to a large woodchuck, that lives in burrows and hibernates in winter.  It is known for its supposed ability to predict the arrival of spring, as observed in the annual Groundhog Day tradition.|The groundhog emerged from its burrow to check the weather. |土拨鼠从洞穴里出来查看天气。|woodchuck, marmot| (no direct antonym, but perhaps "spring")|名词
