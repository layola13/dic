diffusible:/dɪˈfjuːzəbl/| adj. |可扩散的，易扩散的 | capable of being diffused or spread out;  easily spread or transmitted.  |Example:  The aroma of freshly baked bread was diffusible throughout the house. 新烤面包的香味弥漫在整个房子里。|近义词：permeable, dispersible, spreadable |反义词：impermeable, confined, localized

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scent is diffusible throughout the room. = 气味弥漫在整个房间。
This gas is diffusible through the membrane. = 这种气体可以透过膜扩散。


一般过去时:
The dye was diffusible in the water. = 染料在水中易扩散。
The information was diffusible among the employees. = 信息在员工中很容易传播。


现在完成时:
The virus has been diffusible across the region. = 病毒已在该地区广泛传播。
The rumor has become diffusible throughout the town. = 谣言已在全镇传播开来。


过去完成时:
The pollutants had been diffusible into the groundwater before the cleanup. = 在清理之前，污染物已经扩散到地下水中。
The news had become diffusible before the official announcement. = 在官方宣布之前，消息就已经传开了。


将来时:
The technology will be diffusible to other industries. = 这项技术将推广到其他行业。
The knowledge will be diffusible to a wider audience. = 知识将传播给更广泛的受众。


虚拟语气:
If the substance were diffusible, it would spread rapidly. = 如果该物质易扩散，它就会迅速蔓延。
I wish the solution were more diffusible in the solvent. = 我希望该溶液在溶剂中更容易扩散。


被动语态:
The substance is easily diffused, therefore it is diffusible. = 该物质易于扩散，因此它是可扩散的。
The information was diffusible, and it was rapidly spread. = 信息易于传播，并且迅速传播开来。


疑问句:
Is this substance diffusible? = 这种物质易扩散吗？
Are the ideas in the paper diffusible to a broader audience? = 论文中的观点能否传播给更广泛的受众？


否定句:
The material is not diffusible in water. = 该物质在水中不易扩散。
The information was not diffusible due to security restrictions. = 由于安全限制，信息无法传播。


PHRASE->
highly diffusible = 高度扩散性
readily diffusible = 易扩散的
slowly diffusible = 缓慢扩散的
poorly diffusible = 难扩散的
easily diffusible = 容易扩散的

词源:
diffusible comes from the verb "diffuse," which originates from the Latin word "diffundere," meaning "to pour out, spread, scatter."  The suffix "-ible" indicates capability or possibility.
