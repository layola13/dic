kill:/kɪl/ | vt. |杀死，弄死，扼杀，破坏| to cause the death of (a person, animal, etc.)|例句：The hunter killed a deer.  猎人杀了一只鹿。

例句：The disease killed thousands.  这种疾病夺去了成千上万人的生命。


近义词：murder, assassinate, slay, execute, destroy | 反义词：save, rescue, revive, heal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kills spiders. = 他杀蜘蛛。
She kills time by reading. = 她通过阅读消磨时间。

一般过去时:
They killed the enemy soldiers. = 他们杀死了敌军士兵。
He killed the engine. = 他熄火了。

现在完成时:
She has killed two birds with one stone. = 她一箭双雕。
The frost has killed all the plants. = 霜冻杀死了所有的植物。

过去完成时:
He had killed the snake before I arrived. = 我到达之前他已经杀死了那条蛇。
They had killed all the rats in the house. = 他们已经杀死了房子里所有的老鼠。

将来时:
I will kill him if he tries to hurt my family. = 如果他试图伤害我的家人，我会杀了他。
The cold weather will kill the crops. = 寒冷的天气会冻死庄稼。

虚拟语气:
If I killed him, I would go to jail. = 如果我杀了他，我会进监狱。
I wish I hadn't killed that spider. = 我真希望我没有杀死那只蜘蛛。

被动语态:
The lion was killed by the hunter. = 这只狮子被猎人杀死了。
Many innocent people were killed in the war. = 许多无辜的人在战争中丧生。

疑问句:
Did he kill the dog? = 他杀死了那条狗吗？
Who killed the president? = 谁杀死了总统？

否定句:
I would not kill a fly. = 我不会杀一只苍蝇。
He didn't kill anyone. = 他没有杀任何人。
