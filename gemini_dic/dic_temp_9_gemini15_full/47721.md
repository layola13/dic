decadentism:/ˌdɛkəˈdɛntɪzəm/ | n. |颓废主义；衰落；堕落 | The philosophical and artistic movement emphasizing the decay of Western civilization.  Decadentism often features themes of weariness, disillusionment, and the exploration of the darker aspects of human nature. It is characterized by a rejection of conventional morality and a fascination with the grotesque and perverse. | Example: The painting is a prime example of decadentism, with its depiction of decay and moral corruption. 这幅画是颓废主义的典型例子，它描绘了衰败和道德败坏。|近义词：decadence, decline, degeneration | 反义词：renewal, renaissance, resurgence
