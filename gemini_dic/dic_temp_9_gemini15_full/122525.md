modable:/ˈmɒdəbəl/| adj.| 可修改的，可调整的 | Capable of being modified or adjusted.  Something that is modable can be altered, changed, or adapted.  |例句：The software is modable, allowing users to customize its features.  该软件是可修改的，允许用户自定义其功能。

例句：This game's difficulty is modable; you can make it easier or harder. 这个游戏的难度是可以修改的；你可以让它更容易或更难。


近义词：adjustable, customizable, alterable, modifiable


反义词：immutable, unchangeable, fixed, inflexible


词性：形容词
