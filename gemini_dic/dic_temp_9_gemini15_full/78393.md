get-along:/ˌɡɛt əˈlɒŋ/ | v. |相处，进展，过活 |To be friendly and have a good relationship with someone; to manage or progress satisfactorily. |例句：They get along well together. 他们相处得很好。|例句：The project is getting along nicely. 项目进展顺利。|近义词：get on, agree, progress |反义词：clash, disagree, fail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They get along very well. = 他们相处得很好。
We get along fine. = 我们相处得很好。

一般过去时:
They got along well in college. = 他们在大学里相处得很好。
The project got along slowly. = 项目进展缓慢。

We have gotten along well since we met. = 自从我们见面以来，我们相处得很好。
How has the project been getting along? = 项目进展如何？

They expect the project to get along smoothly. = 他们期望项目顺利进行。
