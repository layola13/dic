simplified:/ˈsɪmplɪfaɪd/| adj.|简化的，简易的|Simplified means made easier to understand or do; reduced to its most basic form. It often refers to a process or system that has been made less complex.|例句：The instructions were simplified for beginners.  初学者说明书经过简化。|例句：The simplified version of the software is easier to use.  软件的简化版本更容易使用。|近义词：abridged, shortened, streamlined|反义词：complicated, complex, elaborate
