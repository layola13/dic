hemiopsia:/ˌhɛmiˈɒpsiə/|/ˈhɛmiˌɒpsɪə/| n. |半视野盲, 半盲|Loss of vision in half of the visual field.  This can affect either the right or left half of the visual field in either or both eyes. The loss of vision can be complete or partial. Hemianopsia is often caused by damage to the brain, specifically to the visual cortex or the optic tract.|The stroke caused him to experience hemianopsia, leaving him blind in his left visual field.|近义词：visual field defect, half-blindness|反义词：normal vision
