psychostimulant: /ˌsaɪkoʊˈstɪmjələnt/ | n. |精神兴奋剂 | A psychostimulant is a drug that stimulates the central nervous system, increasing alertness, attention, and energy levels.  They are often used to treat conditions like ADHD and narcolepsy, but can also be misused for recreational purposes.  | Example:  Amphetamines and methylphenidate are examples of psychostimulants. 兴奋剂和哌甲酯是精神兴奋剂的例子。


The doctor prescribed a psychostimulant to help manage his ADHD. 医生开了精神兴奋剂来帮助控制他的注意力缺陷多动障碍。

近义词： stimulant, psychomotor stimulant, CNS stimulant | 反义词：depressant, sedative
