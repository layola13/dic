counterclaims:/ˈkaʊntə(r)kleɪmz/| n. |反驳；反诉；对抗性主张 |A counterclaim is a claim made to oppose another claim, especially in a legal context.  It's a statement or argument presented in response to an opposing argument.  In a debate or legal case, it challenges the validity of the original claim. |Example:  The defendant presented a counterclaim alleging breach of contract by the plaintiff.  被告提出反诉，指控原告违反合同。

The plaintiff's claim of negligence was met with a strong counterclaim. 原告的疏忽指控遭到了强烈的反驳。|近义词：rebuttal, refutation, rejoinder |反义词：confirmation, corroboration |名词
