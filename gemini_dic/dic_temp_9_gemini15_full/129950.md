non-manipulative: /ˌnɒn.məˈnɪpjʊlətɪv/ | adj. | 不操纵的，不控制的 | Not attempting to influence or control somebody unfairly or dishonestly.  This adjective describes actions, behaviors, or people who are free from manipulation. |


例句：

He offered a non-manipulative suggestion, simply stating his opinion without pressure. 他提出了一个不带有操控性的建议，只是简单地陈述了自己的观点，没有施加压力。


She appreciated his non-manipulative approach to the problem; he listened and offered help without trying to control the situation. 她欣赏他处理问题时不带有操控性的方式；他倾听并提供帮助，而没有试图控制局面。


近义词：honest, fair, sincere, genuine, straightforward

反义词：manipulative, controlling, coercive, deceitful, scheming


词性: 形容词
