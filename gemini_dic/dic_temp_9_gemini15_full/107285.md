/læm/ | n. | 羔羊；小羊；温柔的人 | A young sheep, especially one less than a year old.  The word can also be used metaphorically to describe someone gentle or meek. | Example: The farmer led the lamb to the pasture.  农夫把小羊带到牧场。 The lamb was gentle and trusting. 这只小羊温顺而信任人。 | 近义词：sheep, lambkin | 反义词：old sheep, goat


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lamb eats grass. = 小羊吃草。
The lambs are playing in the field. = 小羊们在田野里玩耍。

一般过去时:
The lamb drank milk. = 小羊喝奶。
The lambs slept soundly last night. = 小羊们昨晚睡得很香。

现在完成时:
The shepherd has lost a lamb. = 牧羊人丢了一只小羊。
The lambs have grown considerably since spring. = 自从春天以来，小羊们长得相当大了。

过去完成时:
The farmer had already sheared the lambs before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，农夫已经把小羊剪了毛。
We had seen the lambs many times before. = 我们之前见过很多次小羊了。

将来时:
The lamb will be weaned soon. = 这只小羊很快就要断奶了。
The lambs will graze in the meadow tomorrow. = 小羊们明天将在草地上吃草。

虚拟语气:
If the lamb were stronger, it could escape the wolf. = 如果小羊更强壮一些，它就能逃脱狼的魔爪。
I wish the lambs were here with us. = 我希望小羊们能和我们在一起。

被动语态:
The lamb was found by the farmer. = 小羊被农夫找到了。

疑问句:
Is that a lamb? = 那是一只小羊吗？

否定句:
