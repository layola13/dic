economists: /ɪˈkɒnəmɪsts/ /ɪˈkɑːnəmɪsts/ | n. | 经济学家 | Experts who study and analyze economic systems, theories, and policies. They often work in academia, government, or the private sector, offering insights into economic trends and advising on economic strategies. | Example: Many economists predict a recession next year. 许多经济学家预测明年将会出现经济衰退。  |近义词：economic experts, financial analysts | 反义词： N/A
