heritages:/ˈherɪtɪdʒɪz/ | n. |遗产；传统；继承物 |  The word "heritages" refers to something that is inherited from the past, typically cultural traditions, practices, objects, or historical sites. It emphasizes the collective and enduring nature of these inheritances.  | The rich heritages of many cultures are threatened by globalization.  许多文化的丰富遗产正受到全球化的威胁。 | legacies, traditions, inheritances |  nothingness, destruction | 名词
