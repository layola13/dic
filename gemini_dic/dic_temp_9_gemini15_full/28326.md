carrageen:/ˌkærəˈɡiːn/| n. |卡拉胶|A family of linear sulfated polysaccharides that are extracted from red seaweed.  They are used as thickening agents, gelling agents, and stabilizers in food and other industries. Carrageenan is a common food additive used to improve the texture and consistency of various products.|Example:  This ice cream uses carrageenan to give it a smooth, creamy texture.  这款冰淇淋使用了卡拉胶来使其口感顺滑，奶油味十足。|近义词：agar, alginate, gum|反义词：无
