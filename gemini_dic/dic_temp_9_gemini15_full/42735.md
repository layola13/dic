counties:/ˈkaʊntiːz/ | n. | 县；郡 | Counties are administrative divisions of a state or country.  县是州或国家的行政区域。| The United States is divided into many counties. 美国分为许多县。 | districts, shires |  (反义词较难直接对应，因为是行政划分，没有绝对的反义词) | 名词

The map shows all the counties in this state. = 这张地图显示了该州的所有县。
Many counties rely heavily on tourism. = 许多县严重依赖旅游业。

虚拟语气:
If I were a county official, I would address this issue immediately. = 如果我是县官员，我会立即解决这个问题。
