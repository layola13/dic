neighboured: /ˈneɪbərd/ | adj. | 邻近的，与…接壤的 | Situated near or next to; bordering.  | The neighboured countries share a common history.  邻近的国家有着共同的历史。  | adjacent, adjoining, neighboring | distant, remote | 形容词
