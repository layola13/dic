consoled:/kənˈsoʊld/ | vt. | 安慰，抚慰 | To comfort someone who is feeling sad or worried. |例句：She consoled her friend after he lost his job. 她朋友失业后安慰了他。|例句：I tried to console her, but she was inconsolable. 我试图安慰她，但她无法安慰。|近义词：comfort, soothe, solace |反义词：distress, upset


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He consoles his daughter every day. = 他每天都安慰他的女儿。
She consoles her friends when they are sad. = 她朋友伤心的时候会安慰他们。

一般过去时:
My mother consoled me when I failed the exam. = 我考试不及格时，妈妈安慰了我。
He consoled her with kind words. = 他用好言好语安慰了她。

现在完成时:
I have consoled many people in my life. = 我一生中安慰过很多人。
She has consoled him after many setbacks. = 她在他经历多次挫折后安慰了他。

过去完成时:
She had consoled him before he went on stage. = 他上台前，她已经安慰过他了。
They had consoled each other after the accident. = 事故发生后，他们互相安慰对方。

将来时:
I will console her when she hears the news. = 当她听到这个消息时，我会安慰她。
He will console his friend after the loss. = 他会在朋友遭受损失后安慰他。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would console her. = 如果我是你，我会安慰她。
I wish I could have consoled him better. = 我希望我能更好地安慰他。

被动语态:
She was consoled by her friends. = 她受到了朋友们的安慰。
He was consoled by the news. = 他被这个消息安慰到了。

疑问句:
Did anyone console her? = 有人安慰她了吗？
Who consoled him? = 谁安慰了他？

否定句:
I couldn't console her. = 我无法安慰她。
They didn't console him at all. = 他们根本没有安慰他。
