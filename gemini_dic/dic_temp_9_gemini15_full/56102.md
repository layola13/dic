doctoring:/ˈdɒktərɪŋ/| v.|篡改，伪造，治疗，修补|The act of altering or falsifying something, often for deceptive purposes; also, the act of treating an illness or injury, or repairing something.|例句：He was accused of doctoring the accounts to hide the losses. 他被指控篡改账目以掩盖损失。

例句：The mechanic spent hours doctoring the old engine to get it running again.  技工花了数小时修理旧引擎使其重新运转。

近义词：falsifying, tampering, altering, treating, repairing

反义词：authenticating, verifying, leaving untouched


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are doctoring the photographs to make them look better. = 他们在篡改照片，让它们看起来更好。
He doctors his car every weekend. = 他每个周末都修理他的车。

一般过去时:
She doctored the document before submitting it. = 她提交文件之前篡改了文件。
The surgeon doctored the patient's wound. = 外科医生治疗了病人的伤口。

现在完成时:
They have doctored the evidence to support their case. = 他们已经篡改了证据来支持他们的说法。
He has doctored many cars in his career. = 他在他的职业生涯中修理过许多汽车。

过去完成时:
She had doctored the records before the audit. = 审计之前，她已经篡改了记录。
The mechanic had doctored the engine several times before it finally broke down. = 技工在引擎最终坏掉之前已经修理过几次了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
