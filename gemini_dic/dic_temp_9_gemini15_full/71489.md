flanch: /flæn(t)ʃ/ | n. |法兰，凸缘 | A flange is a projecting rim, rib, or edge, for strengthening or attaching something.  It's often used to connect pipes, tubes, or other components.  |例句：The plumber used a flange to connect the two pipes securely.  水管工用法兰将两根管道牢固地连接起来。


The flange on the wheel prevented it from slipping. 车轮上的法兰防止它打滑。| 近义词：rim, collar, brim | 反义词： (无直接反义词，但可以考虑与其功能相反的连接方式)

词源:
The word "flange" comes from Middle Dutch *vlange, meaning "flat, broad edge".  This ultimately derives from a Proto-Germanic root, related to words meaning "flat" or "broad".
