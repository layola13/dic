precative:/ˈprɛkətɪv/| adj.|祈求的；恳求的；请求的|Relating to or characterized by entreaty or prayer; supplicatory.  The precative mood in grammar is used to express a wish, request, or command.|例句：His precative tone suggested a desperate plea for help. 他祈求的语气暗示了他绝望地求助。例句：The precative nature of the letter indicated the writer's humility. 这封信恳求的性质表明了作者的谦卑。|近义词：supplicatory, imploring, pleading|反义词：demanding, peremptory, commanding
