commends:/kəˈmɛndz/| vt.|称赞；推荐；委托；提交|to express approval of or admiration for; to entrust or commit to someone's care; to submit (something) formally|例句：The teacher commended the student for his hard work.老师称赞学生努力学习。  The general commended the soldier for bravery. 将军称赞士兵的勇敢。|近义词：praise, applaud, recommend, entrust|反义词：criticize, condemn, blame|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He commends her for her kindness. = 他称赞她的善良。
The manager commends the new employee's work ethic. = 经理称赞新员工的工作态度。

一般过去时:
The judge commended the lawyer's skillful argument. = 法官称赞律师技巧高超的辩论。
She commended her team for their successful project. = 她称赞她的团队成功完成了项目。

现在完成时:
The company has commended its employees for their dedication. = 公司已经表彰员工的奉献精神。
He has commended the book to all his friends. = 他已经向他所有的朋友推荐了这本书。

过去完成时:
The principal had commended the student's achievement before the ceremony. = 校长在仪式之前已经表扬了学生的成就。
They had commended the plan to the board before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已将计划提交给了董事会。

将来时:
The president will commend the outstanding workers at the annual dinner. = 总统将在年度晚宴上表彰优秀员工。
She will commend the new policy to the committee. = 她将向委员会推荐这项新政策。

虚拟语气:
If he worked harder, his boss would commend him. = 如果他工作更努力，老板就会表扬他。
I wish my teacher had commended my efforts. = 我希望我的老师能肯定我的努力。

被动语态:
His work was commended by the critics. = 他的作品受到评论家的好评。
The proposal was commended to the council for approval.  = 该提案被提交给理事会以供批准。

疑问句:
Will the committee commend the proposal? = 委员会会推荐该提案吗？
Does the manager commend the team regularly? = 经理经常表扬团队吗？

否定句:
The boss did not commend the employee's performance. = 老板没有称赞员工的表现。
They did not commend the plan to the higher authorities. = 他们没有向高级主管推荐该计划。


PHRASE->
commend to = 推荐给
commend oneself to = 使自己受人欢迎
commend itself to = 自行推荐
commend...to...= 将…委托给…
highly commend= 大力推荐
