climatologist:/ˌklaɪməˈtɒlədʒɪst/| n. |气候学家|A climatologist is a scientist who studies climates.  They analyze long-term weather patterns, climate change, and the effects of climate on the environment and human society.  Climatology involves understanding atmospheric processes, collecting and interpreting climate data, and modeling future climate scenarios.|例句：The climatologist presented compelling evidence of global warming.  这位气候学家提出了令人信服的全球变暖证据。|例句： Leading climatologists are warning of the severe consequences of inaction on climate change.  领先的气候学家正在警告对气候变化不采取行动的严重后果。|近义词：meteorologist (though climatology focuses on long-term trends, meteorology on short-term weather)|反义词：None directly applicable|词性:名词
