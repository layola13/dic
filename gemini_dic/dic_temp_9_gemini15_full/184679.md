restudies:/ˈriːstʌdiːz/| n.|再学习，复习|The act of studying something again; a renewed or repeated study.|例句：The restudies of the ancient texts revealed new insights.  再研究古代文本揭示了新的见解。|近义词：review, revision, rereading|反义词：initial study, first study|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He restudies the material before the exam. 他在考试前复习材料。
She restudies the difficult chapter. 她复习那章难懂的内容。


一般过去时:
They restudied the data to verify their findings. 他们重新研究了数据以验证他们的发现。
He restudied the map before embarking on the journey. 他在开始旅程之前重新研究了地图。


现在完成时:
I have restudied the subject several times. 我已经复习过这个科目好几次了。
She has restudied the notes for the upcoming test. 她已经为即将到来的考试复习了笔记。


过去完成时:
They had restudied the blueprints before starting construction. 他们在开始施工前已经重新研究过蓝图。
She had restudied the historical context before writing her essay.  在她写论文之前，她已经复习过历史背景。


将来时:
I will restudy the topic next week. 我下周将重新学习这个话题。
He will restudy the case files before the hearing. 他将在听证会前重新研究卷宗。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would restudy the entire course. 如果我有更多时间，我会重新学习整门课程。
It would have been helpful if we had restudied the instructions. 如果我们重新研究一下说明书就好了。


被动语态:
The material was restudied by the committee.  该材料被委员会重新研究了。
The case will be restudied by the authorities.  该案将由当局重新调查。


疑问句:
Will you restudy the chapter? 你会复习那一章吗？
Have you restudied the rules? 你复习过规则了吗？


否定句:
I did not restudy for the quiz. 我没有复习小测。
They will not restudy the documents. 他们不会重新研究文件。
