resumeed:/rɪˈzuːmd/| v.|恢复，重新开始|To begin again after a pause or interruption.|The meeting resumed after a short break.  会议在短暂休息后恢复了。|The project resumed after a delay. 项目在延误后恢复了。|近义词：recommence, restart,reinstate|反义词：suspend, cease, discontinue|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resumes his work every morning. = 他每天早上都恢复工作。
She resumes her studies after a long break. = 她长时间休息后恢复学业。


一般过去时:
The game resumed after the rain stopped. = 雨停后比赛恢复了。
They resumed their conversation after a long silence. = 他们长时间沉默后恢复了谈话。


现在完成时:
They have resumed their partnership. = 他们已经恢复了伙伴关系。
She has resumed her running routine. = 她已经恢复了跑步的习惯。


过去完成时:
After the storm, the workers had resumed construction.  = 暴风雨过后，工人们恢复了施工。
Once the power was restored, they had resumed their work. = 一旦电力恢复，他们就恢复了工作。


将来时:
We will resume our journey tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上将继续我们的旅程。
The conference will resume at 2 pm. = 会议将于下午两点恢复。


虚拟语气:
If the weather cleared, we would resume our hike. = 如果天气放晴，我们将继续我们的徒步旅行。
I wish I could resume my old hobbies. = 我希望我能恢复我以前的爱好。


被动语态:
The meeting was resumed after a short delay. = 会议在短暂延误后恢复了。
The project will be resumed next week. = 项目将于下周恢复。


疑问句:
When will the service resume? = 服务什么时候恢复？
Has the train service resumed yet? = 火车服务恢复了吗？


否定句:
The meeting did not resume until the next day. = 会议直到第二天才恢复。
They haven't resumed their relationship. = 他们还没有恢复关系。
