cwt:/kʌt/| n.|担; 112磅|A cwt is a unit of weight equal to 112 pounds (50.8 kilograms). It is an abbreviation of hundredweight.|The farmer weighed the sack of potatoes, finding it to be about a cwt.|近义词：hundredweight|反义词：无
