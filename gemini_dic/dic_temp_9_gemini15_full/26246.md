buttery:/ˈbʌtəri/| adj.|黄油般的，涂有黄油的，像黄油一样的|Having the characteristics of butter; smooth, rich, and creamy like butter.  It can also refer to something having the color or consistency of butter.|例句：The cake had a buttery texture and rich flavor. 这蛋糕口感像黄油一样，味道浓郁。

例句：He spread buttery cream cheese on the bagel. 他在百吉饼上涂抹了黄油般的奶油奶酪。


近义词：creamy, smooth, rich, oily

反义词：dry, hard, crumbly

词性：形容词
