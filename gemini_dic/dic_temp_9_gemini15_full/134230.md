nonindustry: /ˌnɑːnˈɪndəstri/ | adj. |非行业的；非工业的 |  Not related to or part of an industry; outside the industrial sector.  This adjective describes things, activities, or people that are not involved in industrial production or manufacturing. |

例句：

The nonindustry sector of the economy is growing rapidly. = 经济的非工业部门正在快速增长。

He pursued a career in the nonindustry field of education. = 他在教育这个非工业领域追求事业。


近义词：extra-industrial, non-manufacturing, outside industry


反义词：industrial, manufacturing


词性：形容词
