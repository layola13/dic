in terms of:/ɪn tɜːmz əv/ | prep. | 就…而言；从…方面来说 |  This prepositional phrase indicates the perspective or aspect from which something is being considered or described. It introduces a standard of measurement, a point of view, or a system of classification. | 例句：In terms of cost, the first option is better. 就成本而言，第一个方案更好。

In terms of efficiency, this new software is amazing. 就效率而言，这款新软件很棒。


近义词：regarding, concerning, with respect to, in relation to | 反义词：None (as it's a prepositional phrase, not a single word with a direct opposite) | 词性：介词短语
