dignifying: /ˈdɪɡnɪfaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 使尊严的；使高贵的；提升身份的 | Giving dignity or respect to someone or something; making something seem more important or impressive. |The ceremony was a dignified and moving occasion, dignifying the lives of those lost.  仪式庄严而感人，提升了逝者生命的价值。|近义词：honoring, elevating, ennobling |反义词：demeaning, degrading, humiliating |


USAGE->

This award dignifies her years of hard work. = 这个奖项肯定了她多年的辛勤工作。
