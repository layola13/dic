sci-fi: /ˈsaɪ faɪ/ | n. | 科幻小说；科幻电影 | Science fiction; a genre of fiction dealing with imaginative and futuristic concepts such as advanced science and technology, space travel, time travel, parallel universes, and extraterrestrial life.  It often explores the potential consequences of scientific and technological advancements on society and humanity. | Example:  I love watching sci-fi movies. 我喜欢看科幻电影。

Example:  The novel is a classic example of sci-fi literature. 这部小说是科幻文学的经典之作。


近义词: speculative fiction, science fantasy


反义词:  realistic fiction, historical fiction


词性: noun
