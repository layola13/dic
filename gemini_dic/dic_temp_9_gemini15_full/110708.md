linebacker:/ˈlaɪnˌbækər/| n. |外线后卫，后卫|A linebacker is a defensive player in American football who lines up behind the defensive line.  They are responsible for tackling running backs, covering receivers, and rushing the quarterback.|例句：The linebacker made a crucial tackle to prevent a touchdown.  外线后卫做出了关键擒抱，阻止了一次达阵。


The star linebacker led the team in tackles this season. 这名明星外线后卫本赛季在擒抱次数上领先全队。|近义词：defender, backer|反义词：offensive player
