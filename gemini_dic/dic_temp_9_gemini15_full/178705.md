recruit:/rɪˈkruːt/ | vt. n. | 招募；新兵；新成员 | To enlist (someone) in the armed forces or other organization; a newly enlisted person |  The army is trying to recruit more soldiers. 军队正在努力招募更多士兵。 They are looking for talented recruits to join their company. 他们正在寻找有才华的新成员加入他们的公司。 | enlist, enroll, engage | discharge, dismiss |  动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company recruits new graduates every year. = 公司每年都招聘应届毕业生。
He recruits volunteers for the charity. = 他为慈善机构招募志愿者。


一般过去时:
The army recruited thousands of soldiers during the war. = 战争期间，军队招募了数千名士兵。
She recruited her friends to help with the project. = 她招募了她的朋友来帮助这个项目。


现在完成时:
They have recruited a new CEO for the company. = 他们已经为公司招聘了一位新首席执行官。
The organization has recruited over 100 volunteers this year. = 今年该组织已经招募了100多名志愿者。


过去完成时:
They had recruited all the staff before the project started. = 项目开始前，他们已经招募了所有员工。
The company had recruited several interns by the time the summer began. = 夏天开始的时候，公司已经招募了几名实习生。


将来时:
We will recruit more staff next year. = 我们明年将招聘更多员工。
The university will recruit new students in the fall. = 大学将在秋季招募新生。


虚拟语气:
If they recruited more experienced staff, they would have better results. = 如果他们招募更有经验的员工，他们就会有更好的结果。
I wish they had recruited me for the position. = 我希望他们能聘用我担任这个职位。


被动语态:
New employees are recruited through online applications. = 新员工通过在线申请被招聘。
Many young people are recruited into the military every year. = 每年都有许多年轻人被招募入伍。


疑问句:
How many people do you recruit each year? = 你们每年招募多少人？
Where do they recruit their employees? = 他们在哪里招聘员工？


否定句:
The company did not recruit anyone last month. = 上个月公司没有招聘任何人。
We will not recruit any more staff this year. = 今年我们不会再招聘任何员工了。
