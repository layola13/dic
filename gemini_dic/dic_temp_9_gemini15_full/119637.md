middle-of-the-road:/ˌmɪdl ʌv ðə ˈroʊd/| adj. | 中庸的；温和的；折衷的 |  Moderate; avoiding extremes of opinion or action.  It refers to something that is neither extreme nor radical, but rather occupies a central position.  | Example: His political views are quite middle-of-the-road. 他的政治观点相当中庸。|近义词：moderate, centrist, average, compromising |反义词：extreme, radical, partisan, uncompromising
