prejudicant:/ˈprɛdʒʊdɪkənt/ | adj. | 有偏见的；有害的；不公平的 |  Prejudicant refers to something or someone that causes prejudice or is characterized by prejudice. It suggests a harmful or unfair bias, often leading to unjust treatment.  | Example: The prejudicant remarks made by the judge during the trial were highly inappropriate.  法官在审判期间发表的有偏见言论极其不当。 | 近义词：biased, unfair, partial, discriminatory | 反义词：impartial, fair, unbiased, just
