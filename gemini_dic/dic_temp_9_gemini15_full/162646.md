prearrest: /ˌpriːəˈrɛst/ | adj. |逮捕前的；逮捕之前的|  Relating to the period of time before an arrest is made.  This term often refers to the investigative phase leading up to an arrest, including gathering evidence and interviewing witnesses. |例句：Prearrest investigations are crucial for building a strong case. 逮捕前的调查对于建立强有力的案件至关重要。

例句：The police conducted extensive prearrest surveillance.警方进行了广泛的逮捕前监控。


近义词：prior to arrest, before arrest, preceding arrest

反义词：postarrest (逮捕后)

词性：形容词
