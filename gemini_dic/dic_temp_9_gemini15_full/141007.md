oneself:/wʌnˈsɛlf/ | pron. | 自己，自身 |  oneself is a reflexive pronoun, referring back to the subject of the sentence. It emphasizes that the action is performed by the subject on themselves.  |  She hurt herself playing tennis. 她打网球时伤到了自己。  He taught himself to play the guitar. 他自学了吉他。 | himself, herself, itself | other people  | pron.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I treat myself to a nice dinner. = 我请自己吃一顿丰盛的晚餐。
She prepares herself for the exam. = 她为考试做准备。


一般过去时:
He blamed himself for the accident. = 他责备自己造成了事故。
They enjoyed themselves at the party. = 他们在派对上玩得很开心。


现在完成时:
She has convinced herself that she is right. = 她已经说服自己她是对的。
He has hurt himself badly. = 他伤得很重。


过去完成时:
I had prepared myself for the worst. = 我已经做好了最坏的打算。
She had dressed herself quickly. = 她迅速地打扮好了自己。


将来时:
I will reward myself with a vacation. = 我将用假期来奖励自己。
They will find themselves in trouble. = 他们会发现自己陷入困境。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would look after myself better. = 如果我是你，我会更好地照顾自己。
I wish I had looked after myself better. = 我希望我以前能更好地照顾自己。


被动语态:
(Oneself is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
(Oneself is rarely used in questions directly.  It's more common in a statement that implies a question, like "Did you hurt yourself?")


否定句:
He didn't hurt himself. = 他没有伤到自己。
She couldn't bring herself to tell him the truth. = 她无法鼓起勇气告诉他真相。


PHRASE->
by oneself = 独自地
of oneself = 自动地，自然而然地
to oneself = 自言自语地
for oneself = 亲自，独立地
in oneself = 本身，就其本身而言
