dmt:/ˌdiːɛmˈtiː/| n. |二甲基色胺|Dimethyltryptamine, a naturally occurring psychedelic compound found in various plants and animals, known for its potent hallucinogenic effects.  It's used in some shamanistic rituals and is a subject of ongoing scientific research. |例句：DMT is a powerful psychedelic substance. = 二甲基色胺是一种强效致幻剂。|例句：Researchers are studying the effects of DMT on the brain. = 研究人员正在研究二甲基色胺对大脑的影响。|近义词：psychedelic, hallucinogen |反义词：None specifically, but substances with opposite effects could include sedatives or tranquilizers. |名词
