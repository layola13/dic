local:/ˈloʊkəl/| adj.|当地的，地方的；局部的| relating to or occurring in a particular area or place.|例句：The local news reported on the accident. 当地新闻报道了这起事故。|近义词：regional, provincial, native|反义词：national, international, global


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The local bakery makes delicious bread. = 当地的面包店做面包很好吃。
This local shop offers a wide variety of goods. = 这家当地商店提供各种各样的商品。


一般过去时:
The local government held a meeting yesterday. = 当地政府昨天举行了一次会议。
We visited the local museum last week. = 我们上周参观了当地的博物馆。


现在完成时:
They have lived in this local community for many years. = 他们在这个当地社区住了很多年。
The local council has improved the park significantly. = 当地议会大大改善了公园的面貌。


过去完成时:
The local farmers had already harvested their crops before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，当地农民已经收割了庄稼。
She had worked at the local library for ten years before retiring. = 在退休之前，她在当地图书馆工作了十年。


将来时:
The local authorities will announce their decision tomorrow. = 当地政府部门将于明天宣布他们的决定。
We will visit the local market next Saturday. = 我们下周六将去当地的市场。


虚拟语气:
If I lived in a local community, I would feel more connected. = 如果我住在当地社区，我会感觉更有归属感。
It is important that the local government support small businesses. = 当地政府支持小企业很重要。


被动语态:
The local roads are being repaired. = 当地道路正在维修中。
The local library was closed for renovations. = 当地图书馆因装修而关闭。


疑问句:
Is there a local market nearby? = 附近有当地市场吗？
What is the local specialty of this region? = 这个地区的当地特色是什么？


否定句:
The local restaurant wasn't open late. = 当地餐馆没有开到很晚。
He didn't support the local candidate. = 他不支持当地候选人。
