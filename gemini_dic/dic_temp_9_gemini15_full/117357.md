medians: /ˈmiːdiənz/ | n. | 中位数，中线 | The median is the middle value in a data set when the values are arranged in order.  中位数是在将值按顺序排列后数据集中的中间值。| The median income in this city is $60,000. 这个城市的中间收入是6万美元。| average, mean | extreme, outlier | 名词
