illuminate:/ɪˈluːmɪneɪt/ | vt. | 照明；阐明；照亮 | to light up; to make something clear or understandable | The moon illuminated the path. 月亮照亮了道路。  The professor's explanation illuminated the complex topic. 教授的解释阐明了这个复杂的话题。 | clarify, enlighten, elucidate | darken, obscure | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun illuminates the sky. = 太阳照亮天空。
The artist illuminates the canvas with vibrant colors. = 艺术家用鲜艳的色彩照亮画布。

一般过去时:
The candles illuminated the room. = 蜡烛照亮了房间。
He illuminated the problem with a simple analogy. = 他用一个简单的比喻阐明了这个问题。


现在完成时:
The new streetlights have illuminated the neighborhood. = 新的路灯照亮了这个街区。
Her speech has illuminated the issue for many people. = 她的演讲为许多人阐明了这个问题。


过去完成时:
The fireworks had illuminated the night sky before the rain started. = 在下雨之前，烟花照亮了夜空。
The historical context had illuminated the meaning of the poem. = 历史背景阐明了这首诗的意义。


将来时:
The spotlights will illuminate the stage during the performance. = 演出期间，聚光灯将照亮舞台。
His presentation will illuminate the key challenges. = 他的演示将阐明关键挑战。


虚拟语气:
If the moon were full, it would illuminate the landscape beautifully. = 如果月亮是满月，它就会美丽地照亮风景。
If only he had illuminated the instructions more clearly. = 但愿他把说明解释得更清楚些。


被动语态:
The room was illuminated by the fireplace. = 房间被壁炉照亮了。
The subject was illuminated by the expert's analysis. = 这个主题被专家的分析阐明了。


疑问句:
Does the lamp illuminate the entire room? = 灯能照亮整个房间吗？
Will his explanation illuminate the situation for us? = 他的解释会为我们阐明情况吗？


否定句:
The darkness did not illuminate the path. = 黑暗没有照亮道路。
His speech failed to illuminate the core issues. = 他的演讲未能阐明核心问题。
