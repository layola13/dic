electro-osmotic:/ˌɪˌlɛktroʊɒzˈmɑːtɪk/| adj.|电渗的；电渗析的 | Relating to or denoting the movement of a fluid through a porous material under the influence of an electric field.  This phenomenon is caused by the interaction of an electric field with the charged surface of the material and the surrounding ions in the fluid. |例句：Electro-osmotic flow is used in various microfluidic devices. 电渗流用于各种微流控设备。

例句：The researchers investigated the electro-osmotic properties of the nanochannel. 研究人员调查了纳米通道的电渗特性。


近义词：electrokinetic (更广义，包含电渗)


反义词：无直接反义词，但可以考虑与“非电渗的”相关描述


词性:形容词
