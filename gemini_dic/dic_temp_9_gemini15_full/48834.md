defoliates:/dɪˈfoʊliˌeɪts/| vt.|使落叶；使脱叶|To remove leaves from (a tree or plant), either naturally or artificially.  The process often results in a significant reduction in foliage.  This can be caused by natural factors like disease or drought, or by human intervention such as herbicide use or defoliation campaigns.|The disease defoliated the entire forest.  这种疾病使整片森林的树叶都脱落了。 The farmer defoliated the cotton plants to improve harvesting. 农民摘除了棉花植株的叶子以方便采摘。|strip, denude|retain leaves,  leaf out|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The disease defoliates the trees every year. = 这种病每年都会使树木落叶。
The farmer regularly defoliates his grapevines. = 这位农民定期给他的葡萄藤除叶。

一般过去时:
The storm defoliated the trees in the park. = 暴风雨使公园里的树木落叶。
The herbicide defoliated the fields in a short time. = 除草剂在短时间内使田地里的植物落叶。

现在完成时:

将来时:
