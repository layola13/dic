precurricula:/ˌpriːkəˈrɪkjʊlə/| n. |课程预备；入学前课程|Courses or learning experiences that precede or prepare for a formal curriculum.  Precurricula often focus on foundational skills or knowledge necessary for success in a specific program or field of study.  They might include preparatory classes, workshops, or online modules. |Example: The university offers a precurricula program to help students improve their math skills before starting their engineering degree. 大学提供入学前课程帮助学生在开始工程学位学习之前提高数学技能。|近义词：preparatory courses, preliminary studies, foundational learning|反义词：post-curriculum, follow-up courses|名词
