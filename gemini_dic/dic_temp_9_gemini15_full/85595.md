hatted:/ˈhætɪd/| adj.|戴帽子的；(尤指)戴着礼帽的|Wearing a hat, especially a top hat.  The word often implies a more formal or stylish hat.|例句：The hatted gentleman tipped his hat to the ladies.  戴着礼帽的绅士向女士们致意。

例句：She looked elegant in her hatted attire. 她穿着戴帽子的服装显得优雅。


近义词：capped (if wearing a cap),  bonneted (if wearing a bonnet)


反义词：hatless, bareheaded


词性：形容词
