gastropubs:/ˈɡæstrəʊpʌbz/| n. |美食酒吧；高级酒吧|A gastropub is a pub that serves high-quality food, often with a focus on locally sourced ingredients and innovative dishes, in addition to alcoholic beverages.|例句：Many gastropubs are now offering tasting menus alongside their traditional pub fare. 许多美食酒吧现在除了传统的酒吧菜肴外，还提供品尝菜单。|例句：The gastropub's reputation for excellent food and craft beer attracted a large crowd. 这家美食酒吧以其优质的食品和精酿啤酒而闻名，吸引了众多顾客。|近义词：restaurant, pub, bar|反义词：None
