pseudoministry:/ˌsuːdoʊˈmɪnɪstri/ | n. |伪政府部门；虚设的部委；冒牌政府机构 | A pseudoministry is a government department or agency that exists in name only, lacking real power or authority, often created for show or to deceive.  It might have a title and staff but little actual function or budget.  | Example: The newly elected dictator established several pseudoministries to give the impression of a functioning government, while real power remained concentrated in his hands. |近义词：sham ministry, facade ministry, bogus department |反义词：legitimate ministry, genuine department
