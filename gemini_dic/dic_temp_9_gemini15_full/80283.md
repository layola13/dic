going on:/ˈɡoʊɪŋ ɑːn/|/ɡəʊɪŋ ɒn/| n. phr.|正在发生的事情；进展；活动|The phrase "going on" describes something that is happening or in progress.  It can refer to events, activities, or situations. |例句：What's going on here? 这儿发生什么事了？

What's going on with your project? 你的项目进展如何？


近义词：happening, event, activity, progress, development|反义词：stillness, inactivity, cessation|n. phr.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is going on in the classroom? = 教室里正在发生什么？
What is going on with the construction project? = 建设项目的进展如何？


一般过去时:
What was going on during the power outage? = 停电期间发生了什么事？
What was going on at the party last night? = 昨晚派对上发生了什么？


现在完成时:
What has been going on since I left? = 我离开后发生了什么事？
What has been going on with your health lately? = 你最近身体状况如何？


过去完成时:
What had been going on before the accident happened? = 事故发生前发生了什么事？
What had been going on in the city before the earthquake? = 地震前城市里发生了什么事？


将来时:
What will be going on during the holiday season? = 假期期间会发生什么？
What will be going on at the conference next week? = 下周会议上会发生什么？


虚拟语气:
I wish I knew what was going on. = 我希望我知道发生了什么。
If I had known what was going on, I would have acted differently. = 如果我知道发生了什么，我会采取不同的行动。


被动语态:
It is not clear what was going on. = 不清楚发生了什么。
The situation was being closely monitored, although nobody knew exactly what was going on. = 局势正受到密切监控，尽管没有人确切知道发生了什么。


疑问句:
What's going on? = 发生了什么事？
What's going on with that noise? = 那噪音是怎么回事？


否定句:
Nothing much is going on. = 没什么事发生。
I don't know what's going on. = 我不知道发生了什么事。


PHRASE->
What's going on? = 发生了什么事？

Keep going on. = 继续努力。
Let's go on. = 我们继续吧。
