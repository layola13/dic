compliments:/ˈkɒmplɪmənts/ /ˈkɑːmplɪmənts/| n., v.|赞美；恭维；称赞；问候|

英文解析：
As a noun, "compliment" refers to an expression of praise, admiration, or congratulation.  As a verb, it means to pay someone a compliment; to praise or express admiration for someone or something.

例句：
n.  He paid her many compliments on her cooking. 他称赞她的厨艺。

n.  I received many compliments on my new dress.  我收到了很多关于我新裙子的赞美。


v.  He complimented her on her intelligence. 他称赞她的聪明才智。

v.  Let me compliment you on your excellent work. 让我赞扬你优秀的业绩。


近义词：
praise, flattery, commendation, congratulations, accolades


反义词：
insults, criticisms, condemnations


词性：
noun, verb
