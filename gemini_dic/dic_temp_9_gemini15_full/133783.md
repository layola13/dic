nongenerating: /ˌnɑːnˈdʒɛnəreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |不发电的，不产电的 | Not producing or generating electricity or power.  The term usually refers to equipment or systems. | 例句：The nongenerating assets of the utility company include transmission lines and substations.  这家公用事业公司的非发电资产包括输电线路和变电站。


The aging power plant was deemed nongenerating after repeated failures.  这间老化的发电厂在多次故障后被认定为不发电的。


近义词：inactive, unproductive, non-productive, idle | 反义词：generating, productive, active
