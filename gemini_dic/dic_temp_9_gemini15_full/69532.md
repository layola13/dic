feller:/ˈfɛlə(r)/| n.|家伙，小伙子，伐木工人|A feller is a man, often used informally.  It can also refer to a lumberjack or a person who fells trees.|例句：That feller over there is a real character.  那边那个家伙是个真性情的人。

例句：He's a strong feller, good at felling trees. 他是个壮汉，很擅长伐木。|近义词：guy, chap, man, fellow|反义词：N/A
