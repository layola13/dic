chloroplastid:/ˈklɔːrəˌplæstɪd/| n. |叶绿体|A chloroplast is a plastid that contains chlorophyll and in which photosynthesis takes place.  It's a membrane-bound organelle found in plant cells and other eukaryotic organisms that conduct photosynthesis.|Example: The chloroplasts in plant leaves capture sunlight to produce energy.|近义词:plastid|反义词:None
