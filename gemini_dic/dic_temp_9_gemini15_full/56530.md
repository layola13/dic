domina:/ˈdɒmɪnə/ | n. | 女主人；女统治者；支配者 | A woman who dominates or controls; a mistress.  It can also refer to a powerful female figure, often in a sexual or submissive context. | Example:  She was the domina of the household, making all the decisions. 她家里的女主人，所有决定都由她做。


The word "domina" originates from the Latin word *domina*, the feminine form of *dominus*, meaning "master" or "lord".  Therefore, its root signifies power and control.
