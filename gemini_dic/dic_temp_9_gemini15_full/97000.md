infantryman:/ˈɪnfəntrimæn/| n. |步兵，步兵士兵|A soldier who fights on foot as part of an infantry unit.  Infantrymen are typically equipped with rifles and other small arms.|例句：The infantryman bravely charged across the open field.  这位步兵勇敢地冲过开阔的田野。

例句：Experienced infantrymen are crucial for any successful military operation. 经验丰富的步兵对于任何成功的军事行动都至关重要。


近义词：foot soldier, private, GI (informal)


反义词：airman, sailor, pilot


词性：名词
