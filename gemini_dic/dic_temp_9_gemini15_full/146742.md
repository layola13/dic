英文解析:Overhugeness refers to the state or quality of being excessively large, immense, or enormous. It emphasizes the overwhelming scale or size of something, often to the point of being difficult to comprehend or manage.  It's a less common word, often used for emphasis or stylistic effect.


例句:

The overhugeness of the universe is difficult to grasp. 宇宙的巨大令人难以理解。

The overhugeness of the project led to its eventual failure.  项目的庞大最终导致了它的失败。
