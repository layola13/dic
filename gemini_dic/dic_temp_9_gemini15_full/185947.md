revved:/rɛvd/| v.|  加速；兴奋；刺激 | To increase the speed of (an engine) by increasing the speed of its rotation; to increase the speed of (something, especially a vehicle engine) by pressing the accelerator pedal.  Also, to cause (someone or something) to become excited or stimulated. | 例句：He revved the engine and sped off. 他加大油门迅速驶离。

例句：The crowd revved up when their favorite band took the stage.  乐队主唱上台时，人群兴奋起来。


近义词：accelerate, speed up, excite, stimulate

反义词：slow down, decelerate, calm, soothe

词性:动词
