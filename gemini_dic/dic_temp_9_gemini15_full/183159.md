repotted:/ˌriːˈpɑːtɪd/| v.|重新栽种，换盆|To plant (a plant) in a new pot.  This usually implies moving a plant from a smaller pot to a larger one to accommodate its growth. |例句：I repotted my orchids this spring.  今年春天我给我的兰花换了盆。

例句：The gardener repotted the struggling tomato plant into a larger container. 园丁把那棵快要枯萎的番茄苗移栽到一个更大的容器里。


近义词：transplant, replant

反义词：uproot (in the context of removing a plant from a pot)


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I repot my plants regularly. = 我定期给我的植物换盆。
She repots her herbs every spring. = 她每年春天都给她的香草换盆。


一般过去时:
I repotted my fern last week. = 我上周给我的蕨类植物换了盆。

现在完成时:
I have repotted all my houseplants. = 我已经给所有的室内植物换了盆。
