lustrous:/ˈlʌstrəs/| adj.|光亮的；有光泽的；华丽的 |Shining brightly; having a sheen or gloss.  The lustrous surface of the polished wood gleamed in the sunlight.  |例句：The lustrous silk dress shimmered in the candlelight.  这件光亮的丝绸连衣裙在烛光下闪闪发光。

The lustrous pearls were a family heirloom.  这些光泽的珍珠是传家宝。|近义词：shiny, glossy, radiant, brilliant, gleaming |反义词：dull, matte, tarnished, lackluster
