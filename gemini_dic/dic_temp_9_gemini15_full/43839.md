crepitate:/ˈkrɛpɪteɪt/ | v. |爆裂声；劈啪作响| To make a crackling or snapping sound, like that of fire or something burning.  |例句：The fire crepitated merrily in the hearth.  壁炉里的火愉快地噼啪作响。


The dry leaves crepitated underfoot. 干树叶在脚下发出噼啪声。|近义词：crackle, snap, sputter, fizz|反义词：silence, hush
