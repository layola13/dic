heterogamy:/ˌhɛtərˈɒɡəmi/ | n. | 异配生殖；异型配偶；异型结合 | The fusion of dissimilar gametes (sex cells) in sexual reproduction.  This contrasts with isogamy, where the gametes are similar in size and form.  Heterogamy is a common feature in many sexually reproducing organisms, including animals and plants. | Example:  Many flowering plants exhibit heterogamy, with distinct male and female flowers or separate male and female parts within the same flower.  许多开花植物表现出异配生殖，具有不同的雄性和雌性花朵，或在同一朵花内具有单独的雄性和雌性部分。|近义词：anisogamy, allogamy |反义词：isogamy
