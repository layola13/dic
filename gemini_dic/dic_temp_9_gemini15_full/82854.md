gullah:/ˈɡʌlə/| n.|盖拉语；盖拉人|A creole language spoken by the Gullah people, primarily on the Sea Islands of South Carolina, Georgia, and northeastern Florida. It is a blend of English with West African languages.  The term also refers to the people who speak this language.|例句：The Gullah people maintain a rich cultural heritage, including their unique language. 盖拉人保持着丰富的文化遗产，包括他们独特的语言。|近义词：Geechee (a related term)|反义词: N/A
