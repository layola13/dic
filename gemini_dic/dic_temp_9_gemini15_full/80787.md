gota:/ˈɡɒtə/ | v. |获得，得到，具有|Got is the past tense of the verb "get."  It means to obtain, receive, or acquire something. It can also mean to understand or comprehend something.  |例句：I gota new car. = 我得到了一辆新车。  I gota good understanding of the topic. = 我对这个话题有了很好的理解。|近义词：obtained, received, acquired, understood |反义词：lost, missed, failed to get


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I get a letter every week. = 我每周都收到一封信。
She gets good grades in school. = 她在学校取得好成绩。


一般过去时:
I got a new job last month. = 我上个月找到了一份新工作。
He got a present for his birthday. = 他收到了生日礼物。


现在完成时:
I have gotten used to the noise. = 我已经习惯了噪音。
She has gotten much better at playing the piano. = 她钢琴弹得进步很多了。


过去完成时:
I had gotten there before the rain started. = 我在雨开始下之前就到了那里。
They had gotten lost in the forest. = 他们在森林里迷路了。


将来时:
I will get a haircut tomorrow. = 我明天要去理发。
She will get the results next week. = 她下周会拿到结果。


虚拟语气:
If I got a million dollars, I would travel the world. = 如果我得到一百万美元，我会环游世界。
I wish I had gotten a better grade on the test. = 我希望我在考试中取得更好的成绩。


被动语态:
The problem got solved quickly. = 问题很快得到解决。
The message got delivered to the wrong person. = 信息被送到了错误的人手中。


疑问句:
Did you get my email? = 你收到我的邮件了吗？
What did you get for your birthday? = 你生日收到了什么？


否定句:
I didn't get the job. = 我没有得到那份工作。
She didn't get enough sleep last night. = 她昨晚睡眠不足。
