levering: /ˈliːvərɪŋ/ | n. v. |杠杆作用；利用；撬动| The act of using leverage; the process of using something to gain an advantage;  prizing or moving something with a lever. |例句：The company's success is due to its clever levering of existing technologies. 公司的成功归功于其巧妙地利用现有技术。


The company used financial levering to expand its operations. 公司利用财务杠杆来扩大业务。

近义词：exploitation, utilization, leverage,  prizing

反义词：neglect, ignoring, hindering


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are levering their expertise to gain a competitive advantage. = 他们正在利用他们的专业知识来获得竞争优势。
He levers his influence to help others. = 他利用他的影响力帮助他人。


一般过去时:
She leveraged her network to find a job. = 她利用她的人脉关系找到了工作。
They leveraged their assets to secure a loan. = 他们利用他们的资产获得了贷款。


现在完成时:
We have leveraged our resources effectively. = 我们已经有效地利用了我们的资源。
They have leveraged their brand to attract new customers. = 他们已经利用他们的品牌吸引了新的顾客。


过去完成时:
He had leveraged his connections before starting his business. = 他在创业之前已经利用了他的人脉关系。
The company had leveraged its market position to dominate the industry. = 公司已经利用其市场地位主导了该行业。


将来时:
They will lever their new technology to improve efficiency. = 他们将利用他们的新技术来提高效率。
We will lever our experience to guide the project. = 我们将利用我们的经验来指导这个项目。


虚拟语气:
If they leveraged their resources better, they would be more successful. = 如果他们更好地利用他们的资源，他们会更成功。
I wish they had leveraged their strengths more effectively. = 我希望他们能更有效地利用他们的优势。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
