relentlessly: /rɪˈlɛntlɪsli/ | adv. | 不屈不挠地；持续不断地；无情地 |  Continuously and without stopping; persistently; without weakening or yielding.  |  例句：The rain fell relentlessly for days.  = 雨持续不断地下了好几天。

例句：He relentlessly pursued his goals, never giving up despite setbacks. = 他不屈不挠地追求他的目标，尽管遇到挫折也从未放弃。


近义词：incessantly, persistently, constantly, continuously, relentlessly, steadily


反义词：intermittently, sporadically, occasionally


词性：副词
