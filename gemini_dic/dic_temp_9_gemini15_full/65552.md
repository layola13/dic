euthanizes:/ˈjuːθənɑɪzɪz/ | vt. | 安乐死；结束…的生命 | To kill (a person or animal) painlessly, especially to end suffering.  | The vet euthanized the dog to end its suffering. 兽医对狗实施了安乐死以结束它的痛苦。 The family decided to euthanize their terminally ill pet.  这个家庭决定对他们患有绝症的宠物实施安乐死。 |  put to sleep, dispatch |  prolong life, treat


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The vet euthanizes suffering animals. = 兽医对受苦的动物实施安乐死。
He routinely euthanizes animals with incurable diseases. = 他例行公事地对患有不治之症的动物实施安乐死。


一般过去时:
They euthanized the horse after its injury. = 他们在马受伤后对它实施了安乐死。
The doctor euthanized the patient peacefully. = 医生平静地对病人进行了安乐死。


现在完成时:
The clinic has euthanized hundreds of animals this year. = 诊所今年已经对数百只动物实施了安乐死。
They have never euthanized a healthy animal. = 他们从未对健康的动物实施过安乐死。


过去完成时:
The owner had already euthanized the cat before the vet arrived. = 在兽医到达之前，主人已经对猫实施了安乐死。
The family had decided to euthanize their dog before its condition worsened. = 在狗狗病情恶化之前，家人已经决定对它实施安乐死。


将来时:
The vet will euthanize the dog tomorrow. = 兽医明天将对这只狗实施安乐死。
They will euthanize the animal if its suffering continues. = 如果动物的痛苦持续下去，他们将对其实施安乐死。


虚拟语气:
If the animal were suffering, I would euthanize it. = 如果动物正在受苦，我会对它实施安乐死。
I wish I had euthanized the pet sooner. = 我希望我早点对宠物实施安乐死。


被动语态:
The animal was euthanized quickly and painlessly. = 动物被迅速而无痛地实施了安乐死。
Many animals are euthanized due to incurable diseases. = 许多动物因不治之症而被实施安乐死。


疑问句:
Will the animal be euthanized today? = 动物今天会被实施安乐死吗？
Should the cat be euthanized? = 猫应该被实施安乐死吗？


否定句:
They did not euthanize the bird. = 他们没有对这只鸟实施安乐死。
The vet will not euthanize the dog without the owner's consent. = 未经主人同意，兽医不会对这只狗实施安乐死。


PHRASE->
There are not many common phrases directly using "euthanizes," as it's a more formal and specific term.  However, related phrases include:

mercy killing = 仁慈的杀戮
assisted suicide = 协助自杀
end-of-life care = 生命终末期护理
compassionate euthanasia =  富有同情心的安乐死
veterinary euthanasia = 兽医安乐死


词源:

From Greek *euthanatos ("easy death"), from eu- ("good, well") + thanatos ("death").
