king-size: /ˈkɪŋsaɪz/ | adj. | 特大号的；国王尺寸的 |  Describes something, especially a bed, that is larger than standard size.  It implies a luxurious or ample size. |

例句：

We bought a king-size bed for our master bedroom. = 我们为我们的主卧室买了一张特大号床。

The hotel room had a king-size bed and a stunning view. = 酒店房间有一张特大号床和令人惊叹的景色。


近义词：extra-large, oversized, queen-size (slightly smaller than king-size), double (smaller than king-size)

反义词：single, small, standard, twin


词性：形容词


USAGE->

(由于king-size本身是形容词，没有动词或名词用法，因此不适用各种时态和语态的例句。)


PHRASE->

king-size duvet: 特大号羽绒被
