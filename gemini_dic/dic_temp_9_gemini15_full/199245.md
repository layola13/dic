silos:/ˈsaɪloʊz/| n. |筒仓；孤立部门；信息孤岛|A silo is a tall cylindrical structure used for storing grain or other bulk materials.  The term is also used metaphorically to describe departments or groups within an organization that operate in isolation from each other, hindering communication and collaboration. |例句：The company's marketing and sales departments operated in silos, resulting in missed opportunities.  这家公司的市场部和销售部各自为政，导致错失良机。

例句：We need to break down the silos between different teams to improve efficiency.我们需要打破不同团队之间的隔阂以提高效率。


近义词：compartment, division, isolation |反义词：integration, collaboration, communication |词性：名词
