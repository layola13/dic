fraternised:/ˈfrætənaɪzd/| v.|  fraternize | 亲密相处；友善交往；(尤指士兵与敌方)  fraternize with sb |The soldiers fraternized with the enemy.士兵和敌人亲密相处。|近义词：associate, socialize, mingle |反义词：estrange, alienate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fraternizes with his colleagues after work. = 他下班后和同事们一起相处得很融洽。
They fraternize regularly at the local pub. = 他们在当地酒吧经常一起聚会。


一般过去时:
The soldiers fraternized with the local villagers. = 士兵们与当地村民友好相处。
She fraternized with the enemy during the war. = 战争期间她与敌人来往密切。


现在完成时:
The two groups have fraternized since the merger. = 自从合并以来，这两个团体一直相处融洽。
He has fraternized with many people from different backgrounds. = 他与来自不同背景的许多人友好相处过。


过去完成时:
They had fraternized for years before the conflict broke out. = 在冲突爆发之前，他们已经友好相处多年了。
She had fraternized with the enemy before her capture. = 在被俘获之前，她与敌人来往密切。


将来时:
The two teams will fraternize after the game. = 比赛结束后，两支队伍将友好相处。
We will fraternize with our new neighbours soon. = 我们很快就会和新邻居们友好相处。


虚拟语气:
If they fraternized more, they would understand each other better. = 如果他们更友好相处，他们就能更好地理解对方。
I wish I had fraternized more with my classmates in college. = 我希望我在大学里能和我的同学们更友好相处。


被动语态:
The soldiers were fraternized with by the locals. = 当地居民与士兵们友好相处。
The enemy was fraternized with by the soldiers (This is less common and sounds unnatural). = 士兵们与敌人来往密切。(这句话不太常见，听起来不自然)。


疑问句:
Do they fraternize with their competitors? = 他们与竞争对手友好相处吗？
Did the soldiers fraternize with the enemy? = 士兵们与敌人来往密切吗？


否定句:
The officers forbade the soldiers from fraternizing with the enemy. = 军官禁止士兵与敌人来往。
She refused to fraternize with those she considered her enemies. = 她拒绝与她认为是敌人的人来往。


PHRASE->
fraternize with: 与…友好相处
fraternize openly: 公开地友好相处
forbid fraternization: 禁止来往
encourage fraternization: 鼓励来往
result of fraternization: 友好相处的结果
