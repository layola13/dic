enthusiastical: /ɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪkəl/ | adj. | 热情的；热心的 |  Characterized by or showing great enthusiasm.  It describes someone or something filled with or showing great excitement and interest. |


例句：

She gave an enthusiastical speech about her project. = 她热情洋溢地介绍了她的项目。

The crowd was enthusiastical about the band's performance. = 观众对乐队的表演热情高涨。


近义词：passionate, ardent, fervent, zealous, eager

反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic, lukewarm


词性：形容词
