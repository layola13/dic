precleaner:/ˈpriːˌkliːnər/| n. |预清洁器；预处理装置|A precleaner is a device or system used to remove larger particles or contaminants from a fluid or gas stream before it enters a more precise or sensitive cleaning or processing stage.  It's a preliminary step in a multi-stage cleaning process. |Example: The precleaner removed most of the large debris from the air intake before the HEPA filter took over. 预清洁器在HEPA过滤器接管之前去除了进气口中的大部分大型碎片。|近义词：pre-filter, primary filter |反义词：final filter, polisher
