incorporating:/ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪtɪŋ/| v. |包含；吸收；合并；体现|To include something as part of a whole; to combine or unite.  It can also refer to forming a corporation.|

The new software incorporates many improvements.  = 新软件包含了许多改进。

The artist incorporates natural elements into her sculptures. = 艺术家在她的雕塑中融入了自然元素。


近义词：including, integrating, combining, embracing, consolidating

反义词：excluding, separating, omitting, detaching


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are incorporating new features into the product. = 他们正在将新功能整合到产品中。
The company incorporates sustainable practices. = 这家公司采用可持续发展措施。


一般过去时:
The architect incorporated modern design elements into the building. = 建筑师将现代设计元素融入建筑中。
She incorporated all of his suggestions into the report. = 她将他的所有建议都纳入报告中。


现在完成时:
We have incorporated your feedback into the final version. = 我们已将您的反馈纳入最终版本。
The company has incorporated several smaller businesses. = 这家公司已经并购了几家小型企业。


过去完成时:
They had incorporated the changes before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经完成了更改。
She had incorporated all the data into the presentation before she began. = 她开始之前已将所有数据都纳入演示文稿中。


将来时:
We will incorporate your ideas into the next design. = 我们将在下一个设计中融入您的想法。
The company will incorporate new technologies into its products. = 公司将新技术融入其产品中。


虚拟语气:
If they incorporated more feedback, the product would be better. = 如果他们能吸收更多反馈，产品将会更好。
I wish they had incorporated my suggestion. = 我希望他们采纳了我的建议。


被动语态:
The changes were incorporated into the final draft. = 这些更改已纳入最终草案。
New safety measures are being incorporated into the factory. = 工厂正在融入新的安全措施。


疑问句:
Is the company incorporating new hiring practices? = 公司是否正在采用新的招聘措施？
Does the design incorporate sustainable materials? = 该设计是否包含可持续材料？


否定句:
The plan did not incorporate sufficient risk management. = 该计划没有充分纳入风险管理。
They did not incorporate my recommendations. = 他们没有采纳我的建议。



PHRASE->
incorporate into = 融入到...中
incorporate with = 与...合并
fully incorporate = 完全融合
incorporate by reference = 引用合并
incorporate a clause = 加入条款
