encrustations:/ˌɛnkrʌˈsteɪʃənz/ | n. | 结壳；附着物；堆积物 |  A hard layer formed on the surface of something.  Encrustations can be formed by minerals, shells, or other materials. They often indicate a build-up of substances over time.  | The ancient vase was covered in beautiful encrustations of precious stones.  古老的花瓶上覆盖着美丽的宝石结壳。

The old pipes were clogged with mineral encrustations. 老旧的管道被矿物结壳堵塞了。| incrustations, deposits, accretions |  decrustations (反义词，指去除结壳) | 名词
