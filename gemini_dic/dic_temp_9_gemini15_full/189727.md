salted:/ˈsɔːltɪd/| adj.| 腌制的；加盐的 | Containing or preserved with salt.  |例句：The salted fish was delicious.  腌制的鱼很好吃。|例句：She prefers salted butter to unsalted. 她喜欢加盐黄油而不是不加盐的。|近义词：seasoned,brined|反义词：unsalted,fresh


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The chef salts the soup. = 厨师给汤加盐。
This dish is salted perfectly. = 这道菜的盐放得刚刚好。


一般过去时:
He salted the meat before grilling it. = 他在烤肉之前给肉加了盐。
We salted the potatoes for the stew. = 我们给炖菜里的土豆加了盐。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
