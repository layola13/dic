healing:/ˈhiːlɪŋ/ | n. adj. v. | 愈合；康复；治疗；治愈的；有疗效的 | The process of becoming healthy or whole again, physically, mentally, or emotionally;  the act of treating an injury or illness. |近义词：recovery, mending, restoration, cure |反义词：injury, damage, deterioration


n.  愈合；康复；治疗
例句：The healing of the wound took several weeks.  伤口愈合花了几个星期。

例句：Spiritual healing can be a powerful tool for recovery. 精神疗愈可以成为强大的恢复工具。


adj. 治愈的；有疗效的
例句：The healing properties of this herb are well-known.  这种草药的疗效是众所周知的。

例句：She sought healing touch therapy to alleviate her pain. 她寻求治疗性触摸疗法来缓解疼痛。


v. 愈合；康复；治疗
例句：The broken bone is slowly healing.  断骨正在慢慢愈合。

例句：The doctor is trying to heal the patient's infection.  医生正在试图治愈病人的感染。
