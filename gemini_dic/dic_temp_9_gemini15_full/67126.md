exponents:/ˈɛkspəʊnənts/ | n. | 指数，幂；倡导者；拥护者 |  A number or symbol representing a power to which a given number or expression is to be raised.  Also, a person who speaks or writes in favor of something; a proponent. | Example 1: In the expression 2³, 3 is the exponent.  (在表达式2³中，3是指数。) Example 2:  She is a leading exponent of modern art. (她是现代艺术的主要倡导者。) | 近义词: index, power, advocate, proponent | 反义词: opponent, critic | 名词
