flashmobbed:/ˈflæʃmɒbd/| v. | 突袭式群体表演；闪拍 | To participate in or be the target of a flash mob.  Flash mob is a large group of people who assemble suddenly in a public place, perform an unusual and pre-arranged act for a brief time, then quickly disperse.  Being "flashmobbed" can refer to either participating in or being surprised by such an event. |例句：The unsuspecting shoppers were flashmobbed by a group of dancers.  毫无防备的购物者被一群舞者突袭式群体表演了。

The square was flashmobbed with singing and dancing.广场上进行了突袭式的歌舞表演。|近义词：surprise, ambush, overwhelm |反义词：plan, schedule, anticipate
