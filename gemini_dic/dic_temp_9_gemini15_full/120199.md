minehunting:/ˈmaɪnˌhʌntɪŋ/| n. |扫雷，排雷|The act of searching for and destroying mines, especially naval mines.  Minehunting is a crucial operation in naval warfare and also for clearing waterways for civilian use. |例句：The navy conducted a minehunting operation in the harbor before allowing ships to enter.  海军在允许船只进入港口之前进行了扫雷行动。

The minehunting team successfully cleared the area of all mines.  扫雷小组成功地排除了该区域的所有水雷。


近义词：mine clearance, demining |反义词：mine laying, mining
