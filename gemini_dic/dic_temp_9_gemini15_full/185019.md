retests:/ˌriːˈtɛsts/| n. |复测；再次测试|Repeated tests or examinations.  The study involved retests after a period of time to assess long-term effects.  这项研究包括一段时间后的复测，以评估长期影响。|近义词：repeated tests, re-examinations|反义词：initial tests, first tests|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor retests the patient's blood. = 医生再次检查病人的血液。
She retests the software for bugs. = 她再次测试软件是否存在错误。


一般过去时:
The scientist retested the hypothesis. = 科学家再次检验了这个假设。
They retested the engine after the accident. = 事故后他们对发动机进行了再次测试。


现在完成时:
He has retested the sample three times. = 他已经对样本进行了三次复测。
She has retested her knowledge for the exam. = 她已经复习了考试的知识。


过去完成时:
They had retested the product before releasing it to the market. = 他们在将产品投放市场之前已经对其进行了复测。
The engineer had retested the circuit several times. = 工程师已经多次对电路进行了复测。


将来时:
We will retest the materials next week. = 我们下周将对材料进行复测。
They will retest the system after the update. = 他们将在更新后对系统进行复测。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would retest the experiment. = 如果我有时间，我会重新做实验。
It would be best if they retested the results. = 最好他们能重新测试结果。


被动语态:
The data will be retested by an independent team. = 数据将由一个独立团队进行复测。
The software is being retested for compatibility issues. = 软件正在接受兼容性问题的复测。


疑问句:
Will the students be retested on the material? = 学生们会再次测试这个材料吗？
When will the software be retested? = 软件什么时候会再次测试？


否定句:
They did not retest the hypothesis. = 他们没有再次检验这个假设。
We will not retest the samples until next month. = 我们直到下个月才会再次测试样本。


PHRASE->
retest the hypothesis = 重新检验假设
retest the results = 重新测试结果
retest the engine = 重新测试发动机
retest the software = 重新测试软件
retest the system = 重新测试系统
