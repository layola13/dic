misstarted:/ˌmɪsˈstɑːrtɪd/ | adj. | 出师不利；开头不顺利的 |  Having a bad or unsuccessful beginning.  This describes a situation or process that began poorly, often implying a setback or difficulty early on. | Example: The project had a misstarted, with key personnel unavailable at the outset.  这个项目一开始就出师不利，关键人员一开始就无法到位。


The race was misstarted, requiring a restart. 比赛一开始就出现错误，需要重新开始。|近义词： ill-started, badly begun,  unsuccessful start | 反义词： well-started, successful start | 形容词
