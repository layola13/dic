dematerializing: /ˌdiːməˈtɪəriəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 去物质化；使非物质化 | The process of converting something from a physical form to a digital or virtual one, often involving the elimination of physical components or processes.  | Example: The company is dematerializing its operations by moving its files to the cloud and reducing its physical office space. 公司正在通过将文件转移到云端并减少实体办公空间来使运营去物质化。 | 近义词：virtualizing, digitizing | 反义词：materializing, solidifying
