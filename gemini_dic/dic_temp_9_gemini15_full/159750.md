polydipsia:/ˌpɒliˈdɪpsiə/| n. |多饮症，烦渴症 |Excessive thirst and drinking of fluids, often a symptom of underlying medical conditions such as diabetes.  |Example:  The patient presented with polydipsia and polyuria, leading the doctor to suspect diabetes. 患者出现多饮和多尿，导致医生怀疑是糖尿病。|近义词：excessive thirst |反义词：None specifically, but could be considered the opposite of dehydration.
