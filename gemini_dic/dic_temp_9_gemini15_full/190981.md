scaling:/ˈskeɪlɪŋ/ | n. v. |缩放；规模；比例；攀登 | The act of scaling something, or the process of being scaled.  This can refer to adjusting size (e.g., an image), adjusting a metric (e.g., scaling a business), or climbing something (e.g., a mountain). |例句：The architect used a precise scaling system for the building plans. 建筑师在建筑图纸中使用了精确的比例系统。

The climber successfully scaled the sheer cliff face. 登山者成功地攀登了陡峭的悬崖峭壁。|近义词：resizing, proportioning, climbing, enlarging, expanding |反义词：shrinking, reducing, diminishing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He scales the fish before cooking. = 他在烹饪前给鱼去鳞。
The company scales its operations to meet demand. = 公司按需调整运营规模。


一般过去时:
She scaled the mountain in record time. = 她创纪录地攀登了这座山。
They scaled down the project due to budget constraints. = 由于预算限制，他们缩减了项目规模。


现在完成时:
We have scaled the wall successfully. = 我们已经成功地翻过了墙。
The business has scaled rapidly in the past year. = 这家公司在过去一年中发展迅速。


过去完成时:
They had already scaled the peak before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他们已经登上了山顶。
The project had been scaled back significantly before the funding was secured. = 在获得资金之前，该项目已被大幅缩减。


将来时:
We will scale the mountain next week. = 我们下周将攀登这座山。
The company will scale up production to meet the increased demand. = 公司将扩大生产以满足日益增长的需求。


虚拟语气:
If we scaled the problem down, it would be easier to solve. = 如果我们缩小问题的规模，它就更容易解决。
I wish I had scaled the wall more carefully. = 我真希望我当时更小心地翻墙。


被动语态:
The image was scaled to fit the screen. = 图片被缩放以适应屏幕。
The project is being scaled back due to financial difficulties. = 由于财务困难，该项目正在缩减规模。


疑问句:
How do you scale the fish? = 你是怎么给鱼去鳞的？
How will the company scale its operations in the next quarter? = 公司下个季度将如何调整运营规模？


否定句:
He didn't scale the wall. = 他没有翻墙。
The project wasn't scaled up as planned. = 该项目没有按计划扩大规模。
