carbonaceous:/ˌkɑːrbəˈneɪʃəs/| adj. |含碳的；碳质的 |Containing carbon; relating to or composed of carbon.  The carbonaceous material was analyzed for its elemental composition.  |例句：The carbonaceous shale yielded significant amounts of natural gas.  含碳页岩产生了大量的天然气。

The carbonaceous deposits were studied to understand their origin. 对碳质沉积物进行了研究以了解其成因。|近义词：carbon-containing, carboniferous |反义词：non-carbon containing

词源:
From carbonaceous + -aceous.  "carbonaceous" comes from "carbon" (from Latin *carbo, "coal") + "-aceous," a suffix meaning "relating to" or "resembling."  Therefore, it literally means "relating to or resembling coal or carbon."
