coquina:/koʊˈkiːnə/| n. |科基纳石，贝壳岩|A sedimentary rock composed of shell fragments cemented together.  Coquina is a type of limestone that is relatively soft and porous. It's often found in coastal areas where shells accumulate.|The beach was made of coquina, a soft, easily eroded rock.|近义词：shell rock, coquinite|反义词：无
