damasked:/ˈdæmæskt/| adj.| 提花织的；大马士革式的 |  Describes a fabric with a pattern woven into it, often in a rich or intricate design.  The term often evokes images of luxury and elegance, drawing its name from the city of Damascus, known for its fine textiles. |例句：The damasked tablecloth added a touch of elegance to the dinner table.  这块提花桌布为餐桌增添了一丝优雅。

The curtains were made of heavy, richly damasked silk. 窗帘是用厚重的、华丽的提花丝绸制成的。


近义词：brocaded, figured, patterned|反义词：plain, unpatterned|词性: adjective
