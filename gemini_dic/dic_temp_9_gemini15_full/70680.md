finalise:/ˈfaɪnəlaɪz/| vt.| 使最终确定；完成；定稿 | To make something final or complete.  |例句：We need to finalise the contract before the end of the week. 我们需要在本周末前敲定合同。|例句：The architect finalised the blueprints for the new building. 建筑师完成了新建筑的蓝图。|近义词：complete, conclude, finalize (US spelling)|反义词：begin, start, initiate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They finalise the details every Friday. = 他们每个星期五敲定细节。
She finalises her reports before submitting them. = 她提交报告前会先定稿。

一般过去时:
He finalised the deal yesterday. = 他昨天完成了交易。

过去完成时:

I wish I had finalised the report earlier. = 我真希望我早点完成报告。

被动语态:
