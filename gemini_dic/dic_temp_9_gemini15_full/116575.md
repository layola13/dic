matress: /ˈmætrəs/ | n. | 床垫 | A mattress is a large, thick pad filled with soft material, used for sleeping on.  | Example: I need a new mattress; this one is too lumpy. 我需要一张新的床垫；这张太有疙瘩了。 | 近义词：bedding, pad | 反义词：None
