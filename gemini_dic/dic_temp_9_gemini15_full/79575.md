gloominess:/ˈɡluːminɪs/| n.|阴沉；忧郁；沮丧 |A state of being gloomy; darkness, sadness, or depression.  It refers to a feeling of sadness, depression, or pessimism, often associated with darkness or lack of light. |例句：The gloominess of the weather matched her mood.  天气阴沉沉的，和她当时的心情很相符。

The gloominess of the forecast dampened their spirits.  预报阴沉的天气打击了他们的情绪。


近义词：depression, sadness, dejection, melancholy, despondency |反义词：cheerfulness, gaiety, happiness, optimism, brightness

词性：名词
