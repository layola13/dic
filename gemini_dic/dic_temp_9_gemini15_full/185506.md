reutilized:/ˌriːˈjuːtəlaɪzd/ | v. | 再利用，循环利用 | To use something again, especially in a different way.  | Example: The old tires were reutilized to make playground surfacing. 旧轮胎被再利用来制作操场地面。The company reutilized its waste materials in a new product line.  这家公司将废料再利用到新的产品线上。| 近义词：recycled, repurposed, reused | 反义词：discarded, wasted, thrown away | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reutilize rainwater for irrigation. = 他们将雨水再利用用于灌溉。
We reutilize old glass bottles as vases. = 我们将旧玻璃瓶再利用作为花瓶。

一般过去时:
The factory reutilized its scrap metal. = 工厂将废金属再利用了。
She reutilized the fabric scraps to make a quilt. = 她将布头再利用做成了一床被子。

现在完成时:
The city has reutilized many brownfield sites. = 该城市已经将许多褐地重新利用了。
They have reutilized the old building for a community center. = 他们已经将旧建筑重新利用作为社区中心。

过去完成时:
They had reutilized the materials before the new project started. = 新项目开始前，他们已经将材料再利用了。
The artist had reutilized found objects in her previous works. = 在她之前的作品中，这位艺术家已经再利用了一些现成的物品。

将来时:
We will reutilize the plastic containers for storage. = 我们将把塑料容器再利用来储物。
The company will reutilize its waste water in its cooling system. = 公司将把废水再利用到冷却系统中。

虚拟语气:
If we reutilized more resources, we would reduce waste. = 如果我们更多地再利用资源，我们将减少浪费。
I wish they had reutilized the old equipment instead of discarding it. = 我希望他们能将旧设备再利用而不是丢弃它。

被动语态:
The old furniture was reutilized in the new office. = 旧家具在新办公室被再利用了。
The waste materials are being reutilized in a new manufacturing process. = 废料正在一个新的制造过程中被再利用。

疑问句:
How can we reutilize this material? = 我们如何才能再利用这种材料？
Will the company reutilize the old machinery? = 公司会再利用旧机器吗？

否定句:
They did not reutilize the packaging materials. = 他们没有再利用包装材料。
We cannot reutilize this type of plastic. = 我们无法再利用这种类型的塑料。


PHRASE->
reutilized resources = 再利用的资源
reutilized materials = 再利用的材料
reutilized energy = 再利用的能源
reutilized water = 再利用的水
reutilized waste = 再利用的废物

词源:  The word "reutilized" is formed from the prefix "re-" (again), the verb "utilize" (to make practical and effective use of), and the past participle suffix "-ed".  "Utilize" itself comes from the Latin "utilis" (useful).
