malignantly:/məˈlɪɡnəntli/| adv.|恶性地，恶意地，阴险地 |In a malignant manner; showing ill will; with a harmful intent.  |例句：The tumor grew malignantly.  肿瘤恶性生长。
The dictator ruled malignantly, oppressing his people. 独裁者残酷统治，压迫人民。|近义词：viciously, wickedly, malevolently, spitefully |反义词：benignly, kindly, gently
