compactors:/kəmˈpæktərz/| n. |压实机；压缩机|Machines or devices that compress or consolidate materials, such as garbage, waste, or soil.  They reduce the volume of the material, making it easier to handle and transport. |例句：The city uses large compactors to reduce the volume of trash before it goes to the landfill.  城市使用大型压实机来减少垃圾体积，然后再送往垃圾填埋场。

例句：We need to buy a new compactor for the recycling center. 我们需要为回收中心购买一台新的压实机。


近义词：compressors, consolidators |反义词：expanders


词性: 名词
