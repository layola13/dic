figurer:/ˈfɪɡjər/| vt. n.|想象；认为；计算；描绘；扮演角色；出现；重要人物|To form a mental image of; imagine. To calculate or compute. To represent or depict pictorially. To act or play the part of; to be a figure in. To appear or become visible.|例句：I can't figure out why she left. 我不明白她为什么离开。He figured prominently in the campaign. 他在竞选中扮演了重要角色。|近义词：imagine, calculate, depict, represent, understand|反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I figure that he will arrive soon. = 我认为他很快就会到了。
She figures the cost of the project. = 她计算项目的成本。


一般过去时:
They figured out the solution to the problem. = 他们找到了问题的解决办法。
He figured the total amount due. = 他计算了应付总额。


现在完成时:
I have figured out how to use this software. = 我已经弄清楚了如何使用这个软件。
She has figured the correct answer. = 她已经算出了正确的答案。


过去完成时:
We had figured that the meeting would be canceled. = 我们已经预料到会议会被取消。
They had figured the expenses before the trip. = 他们在旅行前已经计算了费用。


将来时:
I will figure out what to do next. = 我会想出下一步该怎么做。
She will figure the profits for the quarter. = 她将计算本季度的利润。


虚拟语气:
If I figured correctly, we are on the right track. = 如果我算得没错，我们就在正确的轨道上。
I wish I had figured out the puzzle earlier. = 我真希望我早点解开这个谜题。


被动语态:
The cost was figured by the accountant. = 成本由会计计算。
The solution was figured out by the team. = 解决方案由团队找到了。


疑问句:
How do you figure that? = 你是怎么算出来的？
Do you figure we should go? = 你认为我们应该去吗？


否定句:
I can't figure out this math problem. = 我算不出这道数学题。
He didn't figure on such a large crowd. = 他没料到会有这么多人。


PHRASE->
figure out = 想出；弄明白
figure on = 预料；估计
figure in = 包括；列入
figure for = 代表；象征
figure of speech = 比喻说法
