mits:/mɪts/ | n. |手套；拳击手套 | mittens are gloves without separate fingers.  Mittens are warm and cozy, perfect for cold weather. 手套是沒有分開手指的連指手套。手套很暖和舒適，非常適合寒冷的天氣。|近义词：gloves |反义词：bare hands
