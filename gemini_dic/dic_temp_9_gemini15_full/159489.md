/pɒliːz/ | n. |  多边形 | A polygon is a plane figure with at least three straight sides and angles.  Polygons are classified by the number of sides they have (e.g., triangle, quadrilateral, pentagon). | Example:  A hexagon is a polygon with six sides. 一个六边形是一个有六条边的多边形。  A square is a polygon with four equal sides and four right angles. 正方形是一个有四条相等边和四个直角的多边形。|近义词：geometric figure, many-sided figure |反义词：circle, ellipse | 名词
