pinchem:/ˈpɪntʃɛm/| n.| 捏；偷窃；少量 | A small amount; a pinch.  |例句：He gave me a pinchem of salt. 他给了我一小撮盐。|近义词：pinch, bit, smidgen |反义词: abundance, plenty.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pinches the dough. = 他捏面团。
She pinches a little from the cookie jar. = 她从饼干罐里偷了一点。


一般过去时:
He pinched the child's cheek. = 他捏了孩子的脸颊。
She pinched some money from her brother. = 她从她哥哥那里偷了一些钱。


现在完成时:
He has pinched the flowers from the garden. = 他已经从花园里偷了花。
She has pinched a few coins from my purse. = 她从我的钱包里偷了几枚硬币。


过去完成时:
He had pinched the candy before I got there. = 我到那里之前，他已经偷了糖果。
She had pinched the money from the till before the audit. = 审计之前，她已经从收银机里偷了钱。


将来时:
He will pinch the fabric to check its quality. = 他将捏捏布料以检查其质量。
She will pinch some money from her savings to buy a new dress. = 她将从她的积蓄中偷一些钱来买一件新衣服。


虚拟语气:
If he pinched the dough more carefully, it would be better. = 如果他更仔细地捏面团，效果会更好。
If she had not pinched the money, she would not be in trouble. = 如果她没有偷钱，她就不会有麻烦。


被动语态:
The dough was pinched into various shapes. = 面团被捏成各种形状。
Some money was pinched from the office safe. = 一些钱从办公室保险箱里被偷了。


疑问句:
Did he pinch the child's cheek? = 他捏了孩子的脸颊吗？
Did she pinch money from the till? = 她从收银机里偷钱了吗？


否定句:
He didn't pinch the dough hard enough. = 他没有用力捏面团。
She didn't pinch anything from the shop. = 她没有从商店里偷任何东西。
