misworshipping:/ˌmɪsˈwɜːʃɪpɪŋ/| v.|错误崇拜，误信|The act of worshipping incorrectly or inappropriately, often involving a misunderstanding of the deity or ritual.  It can also refer to placing undue reverence on something that does not deserve it.|例句：His misworshipping of power led him down a dark path.  他的权力崇拜使他走上了一条黑暗的道路。 The cult's misworshipping of ancient gods was a source of controversy.  该邪教对古代神明的错误崇拜引发了争议。|近义词：idolatry, misbelief, false worship|反义词：true worship, correct belief
