rejuvenatingly:/rɪˈdʒuːvənˌeɪtɪŋli/| adv. |令人焕发活力的；使人年轻的； |In a way that rejuvenates or makes one feel younger.  It describes the manner in which something makes someone or something feel renewed and revitalized.| The spa treatment left her feeling rejuvenatingly refreshed.  水疗护理让她感觉焕然一新。

The mountain air had a rejuvenatingly invigorating effect. 山间空气具有令人振奋的活力。


近义词：invigoratingly, refreshingly, revitalizingly
反义词：debilitatingly, agingly, wearily
