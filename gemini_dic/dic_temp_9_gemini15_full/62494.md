endosymbiosis:/ˌɛndoʊˌsɪmbaɪˈoʊsɪs/| n. |内共生 |The theory that eukaryotic cells evolved from a symbiotic relationship between prokaryotic cells, where one cell engulfed another and they formed a mutually beneficial relationship.|Example:  The endosymbiotic theory explains the origin of mitochondria and chloroplasts in eukaryotic cells.|近义词：symbiosis|反义词：parasitism
