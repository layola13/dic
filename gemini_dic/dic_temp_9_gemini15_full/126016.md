musquet:/ˈmʌskɪt/| n. |火枪|A musket is a type of firearm that was widely used from the 16th to the 19th centuries.  It is a muzzle-loading shoulder firearm, typically smoothbore, and fired a relatively large lead ball.  Muskets were crucial to the development of military tactics and significantly changed warfare.|The soldiers marched forward, their muskets held at the ready.  士兵们向前行进，他们的火枪准备就绪。|rifle, firearm|sword, bow and arrow|名词
