penny:/ˈpɛni/| n. |便士；少量钱|A penny is a British and US coin of low value.  It's also used figuratively to refer to a small amount of money.| I only have a few pennies in my pocket. 我口袋里只有几个便士。|  She earned a few extra pennies by babysitting. 她通过保姆工作赚了一些额外的钱。|  dollar, cent|  fortune, wealth|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A penny saved is a penny earned. = 省下来的每一分钱都是赚到的钱。
This candy costs one penny. = 这颗糖果值一便士。


一般过去时:
He spent his last penny on a lottery ticket. = 他把最后几个便士花在了彩票上。
I found a penny on the sidewalk yesterday. = 我昨天在人行道上找到一枚便士。


现在完成时:

She has spent all her pennies on new shoes. = 她把所有的钱都花在了新鞋上。


过去完成时:

They had saved every penny for their vacation. = 他们为了度假攒下了每一分钱。


将来时:
