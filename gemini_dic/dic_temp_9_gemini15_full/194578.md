self-studies:/ˈsɛlf ˈstʌdiz/ | n. | 自学；独立学习 |  The act of learning or studying independently, without the guidance of a teacher or formal instruction.  Self-studies are often undertaken to acquire specific skills or knowledge, or to pursue personal interests. | 例句：He improved his English through self-studies. = 他通过自学提高了他的英语水平。


She completed her self-studies in advanced calculus. = 她完成了高等微积分的自学。


近义词：independent study, private study, self-education | 反义词：formal instruction, classroom learning | 词性:noun
