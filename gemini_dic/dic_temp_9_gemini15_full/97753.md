initiate:/ɪˈnɪʃieɪt/ | vt. n. | 开始；发起；启动；介绍；接纳 | To begin or start something; to introduce someone into a group or organization. | 例句：The company initiated a new marketing campaign. 公司发起了一场新的营销活动。  | 例句：They initiated him into the secret society. 他们将他介绍到秘密社团。|近义词：begin, commence, start, introduce, inaugurate | 反义词：conclude, end, finish, terminate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He initiates new projects every year. 他每年都启动新的项目。
She initiates conversations with strangers easily. 她很容易就能和陌生人开始交谈。

一般过去时:
They initiated the meeting with a prayer. 他们以祈祷开始会议。
The teacher initiated the new student into the class. 老师将新生介绍给班级。

现在完成时:
The government has initiated several reforms. 政府已经启动了几项改革。
She has initiated contact with the potential client. 她已经与潜在客户取得了联系。

过去完成时:
They had initiated the process before we arrived. 在我们到达之前，他们已经启动了这个流程。
He had initiated the conversation before the others arrived. 他在其他人到达之前已经开始了谈话。

将来时:
The company will initiate a new product line next year. 公司明年将推出一个新的产品线。
She will initiate legal proceedings against the company. 她将对该公司提起诉讼。

虚拟语气:
If they initiated the project earlier, it would have been completed by now. 如果他们早点启动这个项目，它现在就完成了。
I wish I had initiated the conversation sooner. 我希望我早点开始谈话。

被动语态:
The plan was initiated by the CEO. 该计划是由首席执行官启动的。
The new member was initiated into the club last week. 新成员上周被介绍到俱乐部。

疑问句:
When will they initiate the new policy? 他们什么时候会启动这项新政策？
Who initiated the investigation? 谁发起了调查？

否定句:
He did not initiate the conversation. 他没有开始谈话。
They did not initiate the project on time. 他们没有按时启动项目。


PHRASE->
initiate a discussion 开始讨论
initiate a process 启动流程
initiate contact 建立联系
initiate an investigation 开始调查
initiate a lawsuit 提起诉讼


词源:
The word "initiate" comes from the Latin word "initiāre," meaning "to begin," "to introduce," or "to instruct."  This Latin word is related to the Latin word "initium," meaning "beginning."
