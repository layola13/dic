shortcuts: /ˈʃɔːrtkʌts/ | n. |捷径，快捷键，近路 | A shortcut is a quicker or easier way to do something, often bypassing the usual or standard method.  It can refer to a literal path, a method, or a key combination on a computer. | 例句：I found a shortcut through the woods. 我找到了一条穿过树林的捷径。
She uses keyboard shortcuts to work more efficiently. 她使用键盘快捷键来提高工作效率。|近义词：short cut, bypass, alternative, expediency |反义词：long way, roundabout route, conventional method
