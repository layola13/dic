decision:/dɪˈsɪʒən/| n. |决定，抉择 |The act of deciding or making up one's mind; a conclusion or resolution reached after consideration.  |例句：She made the difficult decision to leave her job. 她做出了离开工作的艰难决定。|例句：The final decision will be announced next week. 最终决定将于下周公布。|近义词：resolution, determination, conclusion |反义词：indecision, uncertainty


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He makes a decision quickly. = 他迅速做出决定。
She decides what to wear every morning. = 她每天早上都决定穿什么。

一般过去时:
They decided to go to the beach. = 他们决定去海滩。
He made a rash decision yesterday. = 他昨天做了一个草率的决定。

现在完成时:
I have made my decision. = 我已经做出了我的决定。
She has decided to move to a new city. = 她已决定搬到一个新的城市。

过去完成时:
They had already decided before I arrived. = 在我到达之前他们就已经决定了。
She had decided to quit her job months ago. = 她几个月前就决定辞职了。

将来时:
We will decide later. = 我们稍后再决定。
They will make a decision by the end of the week. = 他们将在本周末前做出决定。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would make a different decision. = 如果我是你，我会做出不同的决定。
I wish I had made a better decision. = 我希望我做出了更好的决定。

被动语态:
The decision was made by the committee. = 这个决定是由委员会做出的。
A decision is being made right now. = 现在正在做出决定。

疑问句:
What decision have you made? = 你做了什么决定？
Have they reached a decision yet? = 他们已经做出决定了吗？

否定句:
I haven't decided yet. = 我还没决定。
They didn't make a wise decision. = 他们没有做出明智的决定。



PHRASE->
make a decision = 做决定
reach a decision = 达成决定
come to a decision = 做出决定
final decision = 最终决定
crucial decision = 至关重要的决定
