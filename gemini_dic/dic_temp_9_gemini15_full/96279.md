indie:/ˈɪndi/ | adj. n. |独立的；独立制作的；独立音乐的 | Independent; relating to independent music, film, etc.  The term often implies a DIY (do-it-yourself) ethos and a rejection of mainstream culture. | 例句：The band released their indie album to critical acclaim.  乐队发行了他们的独立专辑，获得好评如潮。

例句：She prefers watching indie films to Hollywood blockbusters. 她更喜欢看独立电影而不是好莱坞大片。


近义词：independent, self-produced, underground

反义词：mainstream, commercial, mass-produced

词性: adjective, noun
