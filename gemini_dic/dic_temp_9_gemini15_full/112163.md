look-sees:/ˈlʊkˌsiz/| n.|窥探；察看；(非正式)观察 |A quick look or inspection; a brief viewing.  It often implies a casual or secretive observation.|例句：He took a few look-sees around the corner before crossing the street. 他在过街前在拐角处四处看了看。|例句：The police did a few look-sees in the neighborhood to find the suspect. 警察在附近四处查看以寻找嫌疑人。|近义词：glance, peek, survey, inspection|反义词：thorough examination


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He takes look-sees around the neighborhood every morning. = 他每天早上都在附近四处看看。
They always take look-sees before making a decision. = 他们做决定前总是先四处看看。


一般过去时:
She took a look-see at the price tag before putting the item back. = 她把商品放回去之前看了一眼价格标签。
They took several look-sees at the house before making an offer. = 他们在出价之前多次查看了这栋房子。


现在完成时:
He has taken many look-sees at the property. = 他多次查看了该房产。
They have taken look-sees at several different options. = 他们已经查看了几个不同的选择。


过去完成时:
She had taken a look-see at the contract before signing. = 她在签字之前查看过合同。
They had taken look-sees at the competition's products before launching theirs. = 他们在推出自己的产品之前查看过竞争对手的产品。


将来时:
I will take a look-see at the document later. = 我稍后会查看一下文件。
We will take look-sees at various proposals before deciding. = 我们将在决定之前查看各种提案。


虚拟语气:
If I had taken a look-see, I would have noticed the problem earlier. = 如果我事先查看一下，我就会早点发现问题。
I wish I had taken a look-see before I bought it. = 我希望在我买之前查看一下。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
