detainingly:/dɪˈteɪnɪŋli/| adv.| 延迟地；拘留地；扣押地 |  In a way that delays or detains;  keeping someone or something for a period of time.  The adverb emphasizes the act of delaying or keeping someone/something. | 例句：He looked at her detainingly, unwilling to let her go. 他意味深长地看了她一眼，不愿让她走。


例句：The police officer spoke detainingly to the suspect. 警官意味深长地对嫌疑人说话。


近义词： lingeringly, hesitantly, delaying | 反义词：quickly, swiftly, immediately


词性：副词
