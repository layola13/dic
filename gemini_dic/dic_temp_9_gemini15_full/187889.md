roster:/ˈrɒstər/ /ˈroʊstər/| n. |花名册，人员名单，值班表|A roster is a list of names, especially of people who work in a particular place or do a particular job, often showing when they are supposed to work.|The teacher posted the roster of students in her class.老师张贴了班上学生的名单。|The hospital uses a rotating roster for night shifts.医院使用轮班制值班表。|schedule, list, register |  |noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team roster is posted on the wall. = 球队名单贴在墙上。
This roster shows who is on duty today. = 这个花名册显示了今天谁值班。

一般过去时:
The company rostered a new employee last week. = 公司上周安排了一名新员工。
They checked the roster to see who was absent. = 他们查看花名册以查看谁缺席了。

现在完成时:
The school has rostered all the students for the field trip. = 学校已经为所有学生安排了校外旅行。
We have rostered additional staff for the busy season. = 我们为繁忙的季节安排了额外的员工。

过去完成时:
They had rostered him for the morning shift before he called in sick. = 他请病假之前，他们已经把他排在早班了。
The manager had rostered all the staff before the meeting. = 会议之前，经理已经安排好了所有员工。

将来时:
The coach will roster the players for the upcoming game. = 教练将为即将到来的比赛安排球员。
They will roster extra security personnel for the event. = 他们将为该活动安排额外的保安人员。

虚拟语气:
If they rostered more staff, the workload would be lighter. = 如果他们安排更多员工，工作量就会减轻。
I wish they had rostered me for the project. = 我希望他们把我安排到这个项目中。

被动语态:
The employees were rostered for different shifts. = 员工被安排在不同的班次。
He was rostered for the night shift next week. = 他被安排在下周值夜班。

疑问句:
Is your name on the roster? = 你的名字在花名册上吗？
Who is rostered for the weekend? = 谁周末值班？

否定句:
My name isn't on the roster. = 我的名字不在花名册上。
He wasn't rostered for that task. = 他没有被安排做这项任务。
