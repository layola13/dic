heterocosm:/ˈhɛtərəʊkɒzəm/| n. |异宇宙，异世界 |A heterocosm is a world or universe different from our own, often implying a fundamentally different set of physical laws or realities.  It is often used in science fiction and philosophical discussions to explore alternative realities and possibilities. |Example: In the novel, the protagonist travels to a heterocosm where gravity works in reverse.  在小说中，主人公前往一个重力反向的异世界。|近义词：otherworld, alternate universe, parallel universe |反义词：homeworld, this world
