pastorale:/ˌpæstəˈrɑːli/| adj. n.|田园的；牧歌；田园诗；田园剧|relating to the countryside or rural life, often in an idealized or romanticized way.  It can also refer to a work of art (music, literature, painting) that depicts or evokes such a setting and mood.|例句：The painting depicts a classic pastoral scene of shepherds tending their flocks. 这幅画描绘了一幅牧羊人照看羊群的经典田园场景。


例句：The composer's latest work is a delightful pastoral symphony.  这位作曲家的最新作品是一部令人愉悦的田园交响曲。


近义词：rural, idyllic, bucolic, rustic|反义词：urban, metropolitan, industrial


词性：adj., n.

词源:  From the Latin word *pastoralis*, meaning "of or relating to a shepherd".  This in turn derives from *pastor*, meaning "shepherd".  The word's evolution reflects the long-standing association of pastoral settings with shepherds and a simple, idyllic way of life.
