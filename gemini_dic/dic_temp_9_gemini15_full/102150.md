issuant:/ˈɪʃuənt/| adj.|发出的；起源于；由…产生的| Proceeding from; emanating from; originating in.  The word describes something that emerges or originates from a source. It often implies a sense of origin or derivation. |例句：Issuant from the mountain spring was a stream of crystal-clear water.  山泉流出一股清澈见底的水流。

The issuant rays of the sun warmed the earth.  从太阳发出的光线温暖了大地。|近义词：emanating, originating, proceeding, deriving, springing|反义词：incoming, converging, terminating, ending
