entrails:/ˈɛntreɪlz/| n. |内脏；肠子；内心深处|The internal organs of an animal, especially the intestines.  Entrails are often used in a metaphorical sense to refer to the deepest emotions or innermost being of a person or thing.|例句：The hunter carefully removed the entrails of the deer.  猎人仔细地取出鹿的内脏。

The butcher displayed the animal's entrails on a table.屠夫把动物的内脏摆在桌子上。|近义词：viscera, guts, innards |反义词：exterior, outside
