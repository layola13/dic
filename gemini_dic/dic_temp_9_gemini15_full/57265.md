downpipes: /ˈdaʊnpaɪps/ | n. |落水管，排水管 |A downpipe is a pipe that carries rainwater from a roof gutter to the ground.  They are typically made of metal (such as galvanized steel, aluminum, or copper) or plastic.  |例句：The downpipes were overflowing after the heavy rain.  大雨过后，落水管溢满了。

The downpipes on the house need replacing. 这栋房子的落水管需要更换。|近义词：drainpipe, rainwater pipe |反义词:  None directly applicable.
