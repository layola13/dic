designing:/dɪˈzaɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 设计；策划 | The act of creating plans or drawings for something, or the process of making something.  It involves conceiving, planning, and executing a design. | 例句：She is designing a new website. = 她正在设计一个新的网站。

They are designing a new strategy for the company. = 他们正在为公司设计一个新战略。


近义词：planning, creating, developing, drafting, scheming | 反义词：destroying, dismantling, ruining


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am designing a new dress. = 我正在设计一件新裙子。
He designs houses for a living. = 他以设计房子为生。

一般过去时:
She designed a beautiful garden. = 她设计了一个美丽的庭院。
They designed the building's interior. = 他们设计了建筑物的内部。

现在完成时:
I have designed several websites this year. = 今年我已经设计了几个网站。
She has designed many successful marketing campaigns. = 她已经设计了许多成功的营销活动。

过去完成时:
He had designed the logo before the meeting. = 他在会议之前已经设计好了标志。
They had designed the product before it went into production. = 他们在产品投产之前就已经设计好了它。

将来时:
I will be designing a new app next month. = 下个月我将要设计一个新的应用程序。
They will design a new car model next year. = 他们明年将设计一款新的汽车模型。

虚拟语气:
If I were designing the house, I would use natural materials. = 如果我设计这栋房子，我会使用天然材料。
I wish I had designed the software better. = 我希望我当初能把软件设计得更好。

被动语态:
The software was designed by a team of experts. = 这个软件是由一个专家团队设计的。
The building is being designed by a famous architect. = 这座建筑物正在由一位著名的建筑师设计。

疑问句:
Who is designing the new bridge? = 谁在设计这座新桥？
Are you designing anything at the moment? = 你现在正在设计什么吗？

否定句:
I am not designing a new house. = 我没有在设计新房子。
They did not design the entire system. = 他们并没有设计整个系统。


PHRASE->
web designing = 网页设计
interior designing = 室内设计
fashion designing = 服装设计
game designing = 游戏设计
graphic designing = 平面设计

词源:
The word "designing" comes from the Old French word "devising," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "dispositio," meaning "arrangement" or "disposition."  The sense of planning and creating something evolved from this root.
