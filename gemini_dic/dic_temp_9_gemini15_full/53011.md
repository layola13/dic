dinosaurs:/ˈdaɪnəˌsɔːrz/| n.|恐龙|Extinct reptiles that lived during the Mesozoic Era, characterized by their varied sizes, shapes, and adaptations.|例句：Dinosaurs roamed the Earth millions of years ago. 恐龙在数百万年前漫游地球。|近义词：reptiles, prehistoric animals|反义词: modern animals

一般现在时:
Dinosaurs are fascinating creatures. = 恐龙是迷人的生物。
Scientists study dinosaurs to understand prehistoric life. = 科学家研究恐龙以了解史前生命。

一般过去时:
Dinosaurs dominated the Earth for millions of years. = 恐龙统治地球数百万年。
The dinosaurs died out 65 million years ago. = 恐龙在6500万年前灭绝了。


现在完成时:
Scientists have discovered many new dinosaur species. = 科学家们已经发现了许多新的恐龙物种。
We have learned a lot about dinosaurs from fossils. = 我们从化石中学到了很多关于恐龙的知识。


过去完成时:
The dinosaurs had already become extinct before humans evolved. = 在人类进化之前，恐龙就已经灭绝了。
Scientists had long suspected that a meteor impact caused the dinosaur extinction. = 科学家们长期以来一直怀疑陨石撞击导致了恐龙的灭绝。


将来时:
