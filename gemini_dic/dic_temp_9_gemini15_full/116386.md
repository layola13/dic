mastodon:/ˈmæstədɒn/| n. |猛犸象，乳齿象 | A large, extinct elephant-like mammal with long, curved tusks and a shaggy coat.  |The mastodon roamed North America during the Pleistocene epoch.  猛犸象在更新世时期漫游北美洲。| mammoth, elephant |  extinct  |
