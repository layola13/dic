fragmentation:/ˌfræɡmənˈteɪʃən/| n.|碎片化；分裂；分割|The breaking of something into fragments or smaller parts.  It can refer to physical objects, data, or even social structures. |例句：The fragmentation of the hard drive resulted in data loss. 硬盘碎片化导致数据丢失。

例句：The political fragmentation of the country led to instability.  国家的政治分裂导致了不稳定。


近义词：division, shattering, disintegration, atomization

反义词：unification, integration, consolidation, wholeness

词性:名词
