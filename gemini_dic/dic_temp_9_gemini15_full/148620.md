overtheorize: /ˌoʊvərˈθiːəraɪz/ | vt. | 过度理论化；对…作过度理论解释 | To explain or interpret something in an excessive or overly theoretical way, often to the point of obscuring practical considerations or losing sight of the concrete details.  |  例句：He tends to overtheorize, losing sight of the practical implications of his ideas. 他倾向于过度理论化，忽略了他想法的实际意义。

例句：Don't overtheorize the problem; let's find a practical solution. 不要对这个问题过度理论化；让我们找到一个实际的解决方案。


近义词：overintellectualize, overanalyze, overinterpret


反义词：simplify, concretize,  practicalize


词性：动词
