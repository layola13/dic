multiperil:/ˈmʌltiˌpɛrəl/| n. |多险种，多种危险|An insurance policy that covers multiple perils or hazards.  A multiperil policy protects against several types of losses, such as fire, windstorm, and theft. |Example:  The homeowner purchased a multiperil insurance policy to protect against various risks.  = 房主购买了一份多险种保险单以防范各种风险。  The farmer obtained a multiperil crop insurance policy. = 农民获得了一份多险种作物保险单。|近义词：comprehensive insurance, all-risk insurance |反义词：monoline insurance, single-peril insurance|名词
