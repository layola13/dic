panada:/pəˈnɑːdə/| n.|糊状物；面包糊|A thick paste or batter, typically of bread crumbs or flour and water, used as a coating for food or as a base for other dishes.  |Example:  The chef used a panada to thicken the soup.  厨师用面包糊来浓稠汤汁。

|近义词：batter, paste, slurry|反义词：N/A|词性: 名词
