re-judged:/ˌriːˈdʒʌdʒd/| v.|重新审判，重新评判|To judge again; to reconsider a previous judgment.|例句：The case was re-judged after new evidence emerged.  案件在出现新的证据后被重新审判。|例句：The judges re-judged the competition after a scoring error was discovered.  在发现计分错误后，评委们重新评判了比赛。|近义词：reassess, reconsider, review|反义词：accept, confirm
