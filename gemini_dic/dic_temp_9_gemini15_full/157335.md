pip:/pɪp/| n.|  小颗粒；鸟叫声；（尤指枪声的）轻微爆裂声；（英国）软件包安装程序 |a small seed; a small, usually insignificant thing; the sound of a bird; a small explosive sound, especially of a gun; (UK) a package manager for installing software|例句：The bird sang a cheerful pip.  那只鸟唱着欢快的吱吱声。
The gunfire was a distant pip. 枪声是远处传来的轻微爆裂声。


例句：I installed the library using pip. 我用pip安装了这个库。


近义词：seed, pellet, chirp, pop


反义词：无


词性:名词
