gardening:/ˈɡɑːrdənɪŋ/| n. |园艺；园艺学|The cultivation of plants, typically in a garden.  It involves various activities like planting, watering, fertilizing, and maintaining plants. |例句：My grandmother's hobby is gardening. 我奶奶的爱好是园艺。

例句：He spends his weekends gardening in his backyard. 他周末都在自家后院进行园艺活动。


近义词：horticulture, cultivation |反义词：neglect (of plants)
