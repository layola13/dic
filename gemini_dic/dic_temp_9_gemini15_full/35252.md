clunker:/ˈklʌŋkər/| n.|破旧的汽车；无用之物|An old, unreliable, and often broken-down car or other machine.  It can also refer to something that is generally worthless or ineffective.|例句：His old clunker finally gave out on the highway. =他的旧车最终在高速公路上抛锚了。

例句：That software is a real clunker; it's constantly crashing. =那个软件是个真正的废物；它一直在崩溃。|近义词：junk, wreck, jalopy, lemon|反义词：new car, gem, treasure
