debased:/dɪˈbeɪst/| vt. adj.|贬低；降低；堕落；贬值的 |To reduce something in value or quality; to lower the moral or intellectual character of someone or something.  |例句：The constant advertising debased the once-respected magazine.  持续的广告宣传贬低了这份曾经受人尊敬的杂志。

The scandal debased the reputation of the politician. 这场丑闻败坏了这位政治家的名声。|近义词：degrade, corrupt, demean, depreciate |反义词：elevate, enhance, improve, uplift |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He debases the currency. 他贬低货币。
The constant negativity debases the team's morale.  持续的消极情绪降低了团队的士气。


一般过去时:
The emperor debased the coinage to pay his debts. 皇帝贬低货币来偿还债务。
They debased themselves by accepting bribes. 他们接受贿赂，从而自贬身份。


现在完成时:
Years of mismanagement have debased the company's image. 多年的管理不善已经损害了公司的形象。
The constant lies have debased his credibility.  持续的谎言已经损害了他的信誉。


过去完成时:
They had debased the currency before the crisis hit. 危机爆发前他们已经贬低了货币。
The artist felt his work had been debased by the poor reproduction. 艺术家觉得他的作品因复制质量差而被贬低了。


将来时:
They will debase the standards if we don't intervene. 如果我们不干预，他们将会降低标准。
Inflation will debase the value of our savings. 通货膨胀将会贬低我们储蓄的价值。


虚拟语气:
If they debased the currency further, the economy would collapse. 如果他们进一步贬低货币，经济就会崩溃。
I wish they hadn't debased the noble ideals. 我真希望他们没有贬低那些崇高的理想。


被动语态:
The currency was debased by the government. 货币被政府贬值了。
His reputation was debased by the false accusations. 他的名誉因虚假的指控而受损。


疑问句:
Has the government debased the currency? 政府是否已经贬低了货币？
Will the new policy debase the quality of education? 新政策会降低教育质量吗？


否定句:
The government did not debase the currency. 政府没有贬低货币。
They would not debase themselves by cheating. 他们不会通过作弊来贬低自己。
