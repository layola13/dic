preaddressed:/ˌpriːˈædrɛst/| adj. |预先写好地址的；预先贴好地址的| Already having an address written or printed on it.  This adjective describes something, usually an envelope or label, that has already had an address applied to it, saving the sender time and effort.|


例句：

I sent a preaddressed envelope with my donation. 我随捐款寄去了一封预先写好地址的信封。

The company sent out preaddressed postcards to gather customer feedback. 公司寄出预先贴好地址的明信片以收集客户反馈。


近义词：prelabeled, addressed in advance


反义词：unaddressed


词性：形容词
