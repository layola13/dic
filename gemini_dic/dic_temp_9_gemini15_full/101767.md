irretraceable: /ˌɪrɪˈtreɪsəbl/ | adj. | 不可追溯的，无法追查的 |  Impossible to trace or follow back to an origin or source.  The path was irretraceable after the heavy snow.  |近义词：untraceable, undetectable, inexplicable | 反义词：traceable, trackable


例句：

The origins of the rumour were irretraceable.  谣言的起源无法追溯。

The thief left no trace, making his movements irretraceable. 小偷没有留下任何痕迹，使他的行动无法追查。


词源：

ir- (否定前缀) + retraceable (可追溯的)  retraceable 来自 retrace (追溯)  retrace  = re- (again) + trace (追踪)


用法说明：

irretraceable 主要用作形容词，修饰名词，表示某事物无法被追踪或追溯到其来源或起源。它通常用来形容事件、路径、行动或信息等。


PHRASE->

an irretraceable path 一条无法追溯的路径
irretraceable steps 不可追溯的步骤
irretraceable origins 不可追溯的起源
an irretraceable trail 一条无法追寻的踪迹
irretraceable evidence 不可追溯的证据
