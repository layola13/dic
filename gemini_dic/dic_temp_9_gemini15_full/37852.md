competitivity:/ˌkɒmpɛtɪˈtɪvɪti/| /ˌkɑːmpɛtɪˈtɪvɪti/ | n. |竞争力，竞争性 |The ability of a company, country, or person to compete successfully.  It often involves factors like price, quality, innovation, and efficiency. |例句：The company's competitiveness has improved significantly after restructuring. 公司重组后，竞争力显著提高。

例句：Increased productivity is essential for enhancing national competitiveness. 提高生产力对增强国家竞争力至关重要。

近义词：competitiveness, competitive edge, market strength |反义词：uncompetitiveness, weakness
