pseudoencephalitic: /ˌsuːdoʊˌɛnsɛfəˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. | 假性脑炎的 | Resembling or characteristic of encephalitis, but not caused by inflammation of the brain.  It describes symptoms that mimic those of encephalitis but have a different underlying cause.  | Example: The patient presented with pseudoencephalitic symptoms, but further testing revealed a different neurological disorder.  患者表现出假性脑炎症状，但进一步检查显示是另一种神经系统疾病。 | 近义词: encephalitic-like,  encephalopathy-associated (depending on context) | 反义词:  true encephalitic (describing actual brain inflammation)
