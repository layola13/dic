depositee:/dɪˌpɒzɪˈtiː/ | n. | 受托人，保管人 | A person or institution with whom something is deposited; a trustee.  | The bank acted as the depositee of the funds.  银行充当了这笔资金的保管人。 | trustee, custodian, depository |  (反义词较少，可考虑与“depositor”（存款人）相对) | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bank is the depositee of my savings. = 银行是我的存款保管人。
He is the depositee of the valuable documents. = 他是这些珍贵文件的保管人。


一般过去时:
She was the depositee of her grandmother's will. = 她曾是她祖母遗嘱的保管人。
The lawyer acted as the depositee for the settlement. = 律师充当了和解款项的保管人。


现在完成时:
The company has been the depositee of the assets for many years. = 这家公司多年来一直是这些资产的保管人。
They have acted as depositees for several high-profile cases. = 他们曾担任过几起引人注目的案件的保管人。


过去完成时:
The museum had been the depositee of the artifact before the war. = 在战争之前，博物馆一直是该文物的保管人。
The trust had been the depositee of the inheritance for decades. = 信托基金几十年来一直是遗产的保管人。


将来时:
The court will appoint a new depositee to handle the funds. = 法院将任命一位新的保管人来处理这些资金。
He will be the depositee for the charitable donation. = 他将成为慈善捐款的保管人。


虚拟语气:
If he were the depositee, he would be responsible for the safety of the items. = 如果他是保管人，他将对物品的安全负责。
If she had been the depositee, the situation might have been different. = 如果她是保管人，情况可能会有所不同。


被动语态:
The funds were deposited with a reputable depositee. = 资金存放在信誉良好的保管人那里。
The documents are being held by the appointed depositee. = 文件由指定的保管人保管。


疑问句:
Who is the depositee of the estate? = 谁是遗产的保管人？
Is the bank still the depositee of our assets? = 银行还是我们资产的保管人吗？


否定句:
He is not the depositee of the money. = 他不是这笔钱的保管人。
The organization was not a suitable depositee for such sensitive information. = 该组织不适合作为此类敏感信息的保管人。
