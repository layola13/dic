copestone:/ˈkɒpˌstoʊn/| n. |顶石；收尾工作；最终结论 |The final, crowning element or event; something that completes or finishes something else.  It often refers to a literal keystone in architecture, but also metaphorically to the concluding part of a project or process.|例句：The new bridge's copestone was finally put in place.  这座新桥的顶石终于就位了。|例句：The successful launch of the product was the copestone of years of hard work.  产品的成功发布是多年辛勤工作的最终成果。|近义词：culmination, apex, climax, capstone |反义词：beginning, commencement, start
