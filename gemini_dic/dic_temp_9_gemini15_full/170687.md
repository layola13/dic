pseudoaffectionately: /ˌsuːdoʊəˈfɛkʃənətli/ | adv. | 假装亲切地，虚情假意地 |  Seemingly affectionate but insincere.  The adjective "pseudoaffectionate" describes behavior that appears loving or caring but is actually false or pretended.  It implies a lack of genuine feeling behind the outward display of affection. | 例句：He smiled pseudoaffectionately, but his eyes betrayed his true indifference. 他假装亲切地笑了笑，但他的眼睛出卖了他真正的冷漠。

He patted her head pseudoaffectionately before delivering the bad news. 他在宣布坏消息之前假装亲切地拍了拍她的头。


近义词：feignedly, insincerely, affectedly, hypocritically | 反义词：genuinely, sincerely, truly, wholeheartedly | 词性: 副词
