cyanometer:/ˌsaɪəˈnɒmɪtər/ | n. |天色计，测天色仪 |An instrument for measuring the blueness of the sky, used to estimate the amount of water vapor in the atmosphere.  It typically consists of a series of colored glasses or a calibrated scale against which the sky's color is compared.  |Example: The scientist used a cyanometer to determine the atmospheric water vapor content. 科学家用天色计来测定大气中的水汽含量。|近义词：None  |反义词：None |名词
