portfolios: /pɔːrtˈfoʊliˌoʊz/ | n. | 作品集；投资组合；文件夹 | A portfolio is a collection of a person's work, often used by artists, designers, photographers, and writers to showcase their skills and experience. It can also refer to a collection of investments.  | Example:  She presented a stunning portfolio of her photography to the potential client. 她向潜在客户展示了她令人惊艳的摄影作品集。

The artist showed his portfolio to the gallery owner.  这位艺术家向画廊老板展示了他的作品集。


近义词：collection, compilation, folder, investment holdings | 反义词：N/A
