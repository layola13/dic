epitomizing: /ɪˈpɪtəmaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 作为…的典型；是…的缩影；完美地体现 |  To be a perfect example of something; to embody or represent the essential characteristics of something. | 例句：His work epitomizes the spirit of the times. 他的作品完美地体现了那个时代的精神。

His arrogance epitomizes everything I dislike about him. 他的傲慢体现了我讨厌他的一切。


近义词：embodying, exemplifying, representing, symbolizing | 反义词：misrepresenting, contradicting, defying


词性:动词 (verb)
