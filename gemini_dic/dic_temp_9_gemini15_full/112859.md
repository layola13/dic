lucklessness:/ˈlʌkləsnəs/| n. |不幸；霉运；不走运|The state or quality of being unlucky; lack of luck.  It often implies a persistent or habitual lack of good fortune, rather than just a single instance of bad luck.|例句：His lucklessness followed him throughout his career.  他的霉运伴随了他的整个职业生涯。


例句：Their lucklessness in finding a suitable house was frustrating.  他们找不到合适的房子，真是令人沮丧。


近义词：unluckiness, misfortune, ill fortune, adversity


反义词：good fortune, luck, success, prosperity


词性: 名词
