google's:/ˈɡuːɡəlz/ | pron. |谷歌的，谷歌公司所属的 | Google's search engine is very powerful. 谷歌的搜索引擎非常强大。| 近义词：Google's, belonging to Google | 反义词：无


USAGE->
This word is a possessive pronoun, and doesn't have verb conjugations like "is", "was", etc.  It's not used in tenses. Usage depends on the sentence structure.  Examples below show different sentence constructions using "Google's".

Example 1 (Simple possessive):
Google's headquarters are in Mountain View. = 谷歌的总部在山景城。

Example 2 (Possessive with a gerund):
Google's developing new AI technology is impressive. = 谷歌正在开发新的AI技术令人印象深刻。

Example 3 (Possessive with an adjective):
Google's innovative approach to search is remarkable. = 谷歌创新的搜索方法非常出色。

Example 4 (Possessive with a noun phrase):
Google's impact on the world is undeniable. = 谷歌对世界的影响是不可否认的。

Example 5 (Possessive within a longer sentence):
I read about Google's ambitious plans for the future in a recent article. = 我在最近的一篇文章中读到了谷歌雄心勃勃的未来计划。
