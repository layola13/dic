over-reservedness:/ˌoʊvər rɪˈzɜːvdnəs/ | n. |过度拘谨，过分矜持 |The excessive reluctance to express one's feelings or opinions, often to the point of seeming unfriendly or distant.  It can stem from shyness, insecurity, or a deliberate attempt to maintain a formal demeanor.  |例句：Her over-reservedness made it difficult to get to know her.  她的过度拘谨使得人们很难了解她。


例句：His over-reservedness at the party left many guests feeling uncomfortable. 他在聚会上的过度拘谨让许多宾客感到不舒服。


近义词：reticence, diffidence, shyness, standoffishness, coldness


反义词：extroversion, openness, sociability, warmth, friendliness


词性:名词
