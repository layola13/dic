cirrhous:/ˈsɪrəs/ | adj. | 卷云状的；卷发状的 | Resembling cirrus clouds; characterized by thin, wispy, or feathery formations.  The term often describes something with a tangled or curly appearance, similar to a cirrus cloud's structure. | Example: The cirrhous smoke billowed from the chimney.  卷曲的烟雾从烟囱里飘出。 The cirrhous tendrils of the plant reached for the sunlight. 植物卷曲的卷须伸向阳光。 | 近义词：feathery, wispy, filamentous, plumelike | 反义词：dense, compact, solid
