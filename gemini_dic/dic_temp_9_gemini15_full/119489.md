microorganisms:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈɔːrɡənɪzəmz/| n. |微生物| Microscopic organisms, including bacteria, viruses, fungi, and protozoa.  They are found everywhere in the environment and play vital roles in various ecological processes.  | Example: The study of microorganisms is crucial for understanding disease and developing new treatments.  微生物的研究对于理解疾病和开发新的治疗方法至关重要。|近义词：microbes, germs |反义词：macroscopic organisms
