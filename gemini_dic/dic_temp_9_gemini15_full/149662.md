pairment:/pɛərmənt/ | n. | 损害；损伤；障碍 | The impairment of his hearing made it difficult for him to communicate.  他的听力受损使他难以交流。| damage, injury, defect, handicap | improvement, enhancement | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
His hearing impairment affects his daily life. = 他的听力障碍影响了他的日常生活。
The software has a minor impairment. = 该软件存在轻微缺陷。


一般过去时:
The accident caused a significant impairment to his vision. = 那次事故严重损害了他的视力。
The storm caused impairment to the power lines. = 暴风雨损坏了电力线。


现在完成时:
The disease has caused considerable impairment to his cognitive functions. = 这种疾病已经严重损害了他的认知功能。
Years of smoking have resulted in lung impairment. = 多年的吸烟导致了肺部损伤。


过去完成时:
Before the surgery, he had already experienced some speech impairment. = 在手术之前，他已经出现了一些言语障碍。
The prolonged drought had led to significant crop impairment. = 长期的干旱导致了严重的农作物减产。


将来时:
Continued exposure to loud noises will likely cause hearing impairment. = 持续暴露在巨大的噪音中可能会导致听力损伤。
The pollution will cause further environmental impairment. = 污染将导致进一步的环境恶化。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't suffered that head injury, he wouldn't have experienced such cognitive impairment. = 如果他没有遭受那次头部受伤，他就不会有这样的认知障碍。
I wish the project hadn't suffered such severe financial impairment. = 我希望这个项目没有遭受如此严重的财务损失。


被动语态:
His ability to work was impaired by his illness. = 他的工作能力受到了疾病的影响。
The bridge's structure was impaired by the earthquake. = 桥梁的结构受到了地震的破坏。


疑问句:
What kind of impairment are you experiencing? = 你正在经历什么样的损伤？
Is there any impairment to his mental health? = 他的心理健康有任何问题吗？


否定句:
There was no impairment to the building's structural integrity. = 建筑物的结构完整性没有受到损害。
The test results showed no significant impairment of his liver function. = 检验结果显示他的肝功能没有明显损害。
