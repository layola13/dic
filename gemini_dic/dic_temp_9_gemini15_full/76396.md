gaffing:/ˈɡæfɪŋ/| n. v.|用鱼叉叉; 粗鲁的言语或行为|The act of using a gaff (hook) to land a fish, or a clumsy or embarrassing action.  It can also refer to crude or boorish behavior or speech.|例句：The fisherman expertly gaffed the large tuna. 渔夫熟练地用鱼叉叉住大金枪鱼。

例句：His gaffing remarks offended several guests. 他粗鲁的言辞冒犯了几位客人。|近义词：hooking (for the fishing aspect), blundering, clumsiness|反义词：delicate, graceful, refined|名词，动词
