pentoxide:/ˈpɛntaʊksaɪd/| n. |五氧化物|A chemical compound containing five oxygen atoms combined with one or more atoms of another element.  Pentoxides are often acidic oxides.|Phosphorus pentoxide is a powerful dehydrating agent. 五氧化二磷是一种强脱水剂。|近义词:oxide|反义词:无
