multigrains:/ˈmʌltiɡreɪnz/| adj.| 多谷物的；多种谷物的 |Containing or made from several types of grain.  This term typically refers to foods that combine different grains like wheat, oats, barley, rye, etc., offering a wider range of nutrients.| 例句：This bread is made with multigrains, giving it a richer flavor and texture.  这款面包是用多种谷物制成的，口感更丰富。|  例句：The multigrain cereal provides a good source of fiber and vitamins.  这种多谷物麦片富含纤维和维生素。|近义词：mixed-grain, whole-grain (if all grains are whole), various-grain |反义词：single-grain, refined-grain
