reorientate:/ˌriːˈɔːriənˌteɪt/| vt.|重新定向；使重新定位；使改观；使重新调整方向|To change the position or direction of something or someone; to change someone's attitudes or beliefs.|例句：The company had to reorientate its marketing strategy to appeal to a younger audience.  公司不得不重新调整其营销策略以吸引更年轻的受众。|例句：The experience reorientated his thinking about life.  这段经历改变了他对生活的看法。|近义词：redirect, reposition, realign, readjust|反义词：misalign, disorient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reorientates its marketing strategy annually. = 公司每年都会重新调整其营销策略。
He reorientates himself to the new environment quickly. = 他很快适应了新的环境。

一般过去时:
They reorientated their business plan after the market crash. = 市场崩盘后，他们重新调整了商业计划。
She reorientated her career goals after having children. = 她生孩子后重新规划了职业目标。

现在完成时:
The government has reorientated its education policy towards STEM subjects. = 政府已经将其教育政策重新调整为侧重于STEM学科。
He has reorientated his life priorities after his near-death experience. = 经历了濒死体验后，他重新调整了生活优先级。

过去完成时:
They had reorientated their resources before the project started. = 项目开始之前，他们已经重新分配了资源。
She had reorientated her beliefs after years of self-reflection. = 经过多年的自我反省后，她已经改变了自己的信仰。

将来时:
We will reorientate our approach to customer service next year. = 我们明年将调整我们的客户服务方法。
The company will reorientate its production line to meet the increasing demand. = 公司将重新调整生产线以满足日益增长的需求。

虚拟语气:
If we reorientated our strategy, we might see better results. = 如果我们调整策略，可能会看到更好的结果。
I wish I had reorientated my career path earlier. = 我真希望我早点改变职业道路。

被动语态:
The company's marketing strategy was reorientated last year. = 该公司的营销策略去年进行了重新调整。
His thinking was reorientated by the events of that day. = 他当天的经历改变了他的想法。

疑问句:
Will the company reorientate its operations to a more sustainable model? = 公司会将其运营转向更可持续的模式吗？
Have they reorientated their priorities since the merger? = 自合并以来，他们是否重新调整了优先级？

否定句:
The company did not reorientate its strategy despite the declining sales. = 尽管销售额下降，公司也没有调整其战略。
He refused to reorientate his perspective on the matter. = 他拒绝改变他对这件事的看法。
