printshop:/ˈprɪntʃɑːp/| n. |印刷厂；印刷公司|A printshop is a business that prints materials such as posters, flyers, and business cards.  It typically uses printing presses and other equipment to reproduce images and text onto various substrates.|Example:  He took his manuscript to the local printshop to get it printed.|近义词：printing press, printing house|反义词:None.
