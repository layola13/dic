overconfident:/ˌoʊvərˈkɑːnfɪdənt/| adj. | 过分自信的，自负的 | Having or showing excessive confidence; excessively self-assured.  | He was overconfident in his abilities and underestimated the challenge. 他对自己的能力过于自信，低估了挑战。She went into the exam overconfident and consequently didn't prepare adequately. 她考试过于自信，结果没有充分准备。| confident, self-assured, arrogant | humble, modest, self-doubting


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is overconfident in his abilities. = 他对自己的能力过于自信。
They are overconfident about their chances of winning. = 他们对赢得比赛的机会过于自信。


一般过去时:
She was overconfident before the presentation. = 她演讲前过于自信。
He was overconfident and made a lot of mistakes. = 他过于自信，犯了很多错误。


现在完成时:
He has been overconfident throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中都过于自信。
They have become overconfident after a string of victories. = 他们在一连串胜利后变得过于自信。


过去完成时:
She had been overconfident, which led to her downfall. = 她曾经过于自信，这导致了她的失败。
They had been overconfident and hadn't prepared sufficiently. = 他们曾经过于自信，没有充分准备。


将来时:
He will be overconfident in the upcoming negotiations. = 他在即将到来的谈判中将会过于自信。
They will likely be overconfident if they win the first game. = 如果他们赢得第一场比赛，他们可能会过于自信。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't so overconfident, he might succeed. = 如果他不那么自信，他可能会成功。
I wish he weren't so overconfident; it's a dangerous trait. = 我希望他不那么自信；这是一个危险的特质。


被动语态:
His overconfidence was clearly demonstrated in his actions. = 他的过度自信在他的行为中得到了清楚的体现。
The team's failure was partly attributed to their overconfidence. = 球队的失败部分归因于他们的过度自信。


疑问句:

否定句:
