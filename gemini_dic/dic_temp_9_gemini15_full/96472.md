individualizes: /ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 使个性化；使个别化 | To make something unique or specific to an individual.  It emphasizes the distinct qualities of a person or thing, setting it apart from others. | The teacher individualizes the curriculum to meet the needs of each student. 老师根据每个学生的需求个性化课程设置。| personalize, customize, differentiate | generalize, standardize | 动词


USAGE->

The company individualizes its marketing strategies for each customer segment. = 公司针对每个客户群体个性化其营销策略。
She individualizes her teaching methods to suit different learning styles. = 她调整教学方法以适应不同的学习风格。
