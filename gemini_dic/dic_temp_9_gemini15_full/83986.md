half-days:/ˈhɑːf deɪz/| n.|半天，半天假|Half-days refer to periods of time that are half a day long, typically a morning or afternoon session.  They are often used in the context of work schedules or school hours.|例句：She works half-days on Wednesdays. 她星期三只上半天班。

例句：The school offers half-days for kindergarten students.幼儿园的学生可以上半天学。|近义词：half-shifts, partial days|反义词：full days


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He works half-days on Fridays. = 他星期五只上半天班。
They take half-days during summer. = 他们在夏天只上半天班。


一般过去时:
She took half-days last week. = 她上周只上了半天班。
We worked half-days because of the heat. = 因为天气炎热，我们只上了半天班。


现在完成时:
He has worked half-days for months. = 他已经上半天班好几个月了。
They have taken half-days every Friday this semester. = 这个学期他们每个星期五都只上半天班。


过去完成时:
She had worked half-days for a year before she quit. = 她辞职前已经上半天班一年了。
They had taken half-days all summer before the new schedule started. = 新时间表开始前，他们整个夏天都只上半天班。


将来时:
He will work half-days next month. = 他下个月将只上半天班。
They will take half-days for the rest of the week. = 他们本周剩下的时间将只上半天班。


虚拟语气:
If I had known they offered half-days, I would have applied. = 如果我知道他们提供半天班，我就申请了。
I wish I could work half-days. = 我希望我能上半天班。


被动语态:
Half-days are offered to new parents. = 半天班提供给新父母。
Half-days were not offered last year. = 去年没有提供半天班。


疑问句:
Do they offer half-days? = 他们提供半天班吗？
Are you working half-days this week? = 你这周上半天班吗？


否定句:
He doesn't work half-days. = 他不上半天班。
They didn't take half-days yesterday. = 他们昨天没有上半天班。
