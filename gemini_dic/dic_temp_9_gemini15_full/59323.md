earing:/ˈɪəriŋ/| n.|耳环，耳饰|An earing is an ornament worn in the ear, typically a pendant hanging from a pierced earlobe.|例句：She wore beautiful diamond earrings to the party. 她戴着漂亮的钻石耳环去参加派对。|近义词：earring, pendant, jewelry|反义词：无
