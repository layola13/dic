rubbles:/ˈrʌbəl/| n.|瓦砾；碎石；废墟|Broken pieces of stone, brick, etc., especially as the result of destruction.|The earthquake left behind a landscape of rubble.|近义词：debris, wreckage, ruins|反义词：intactness, wholeness
