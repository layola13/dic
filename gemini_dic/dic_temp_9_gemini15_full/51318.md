detaining: /dɪˈteɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 拘留；扣留；耽搁 |  The act of keeping someone or something in one place.  It can refer to holding someone in custody by legal authority or simply delaying something. |

例句：
The police are detaining him for questioning. = 警方将他拘留接受问话。

The heavy traffic detained us for an hour. = 严重的交通堵塞使我们耽误了一个小时。


近义词：holding, arresting, delaying, detaining,  restraining

反义词：releasing, freeing, liberating


USAGE->

They are detaining the suspect. = 他们正在拘留嫌疑犯。
The bad weather is detaining the flight. = 恶劣天气正在延误航班。
