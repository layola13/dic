mischannel:/ˌmɪsˈtʃænəl/| vt.| 误导；错误地引导；歪曲；滥用|To direct or guide incorrectly or inappropriately; to divert from the proper course or purpose.|例句：The company mischanneled funds into dubious projects. 公司将资金错误地导入了可疑项目。
The protesters mischanneled their anger into violence. 抗议者将他们的愤怒错误地导向了暴力。|近义词：misdirect, misguide, pervert, misuse|反义词：direct, guide, channel properly
