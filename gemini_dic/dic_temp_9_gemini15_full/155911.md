photocollage: /ˈfoʊtoʊkɒlɑːʒ/ | n. |照片拼贴画|A photocollage is a work of art created by assembling various photographs to form a new image or design. It often uses a variety of photographic techniques and styles to create a unique and visually compelling piece.|例句：She created a beautiful photocollage for her grandmother's birthday.|近义词：photomontage, picture collage|反义词：single photograph
