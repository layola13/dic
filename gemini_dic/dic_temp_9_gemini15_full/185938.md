revues:/rəˈvjuːz/| n.|评论；评论集；文艺评论；(尤指)滑稽歌舞表演|A revue is a type of theatrical entertainment consisting of a series of short, unrelated acts, often including music, dance, and comedy.  It can also refer to a critical review or a periodical containing such reviews.|例句：The theatre group put on a dazzling revue featuring songs from the 1920s.  这个剧团上演了一场精彩的歌舞表演，其中包括20世纪20年代的歌曲。|例句：She wrote a scathing revue of the new film. 她写了一篇尖刻的影评。|近义词：review, spectacle, show|反义词：N/A|名词

词源:
The word "revue" comes from the French word "revue," meaning "review" or "inspection."  Its theatrical sense developed in France in the late 19th century, evolving into the type of varied entertainment we know today.
