memorialised:/məˈmɔːriəlaɪzd/| vt.|纪念，使不朽|To keep the memory of someone or something alive; to commemorate.|The fallen soldiers were memorialised in a moving ceremony.  阵亡士兵在一次感人的仪式上被纪念。|The museum memorialises the history of the city. 这个博物馆纪念了这座城市的历史。|commemorate, celebrate, immortalize|forget, ignore, neglect|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They memorialise the event annually. = 他们每年纪念这一事件。
We memorialise our ancestors with this monument. = 我们用这座纪念碑纪念我们的祖先。


一般过去时:
The city memorialised the victims of the disaster. = 该城市纪念了灾难的受害者。
The artist memorialised the beauty of the landscape in his paintings. = 艺术家在画作中纪念了风景的美丽。


现在完成时:
They have memorialised the signing of the treaty with a statue. = 他们用一座雕像纪念了条约的签署。
The town has memorialised its founders in a historical marker. = 这个城镇用历史标记纪念了它的创建者。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
