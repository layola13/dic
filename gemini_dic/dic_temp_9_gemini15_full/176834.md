readvising: /ˌriːˈædvɑɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 再劝告；重新考虑 |  The act of advising again or reconsidering a previous decision.  It implies a further consideration or a change in previous advice. |例句：After reviewing the new data, the board decided to readvise the investment strategy.  = 在审查了新的数据后，董事会决定重新考虑投资策略。

The company is readvising its marketing campaign after poor initial results. = 公司在最初业绩不佳后正在重新考虑其营销活动。


近义词：reconsidering, reviewing, reassessing | 反义词：confirming, finalizing


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are readvising their clients on the new tax laws. = 他们正在就新的税法再次向客户提供建议。
The committee is readvising its approach to the problem. = 委员会正在重新考虑其解决问题的方法。


一般过去时:
He readvised her to seek professional help. = 他再次建议她寻求专业帮助。
The lawyer readvised his client on the terms of the contract. = 律师再次就合同条款向他的委托人提供建议。

过去完成时:

The doctor had readvised the patient to rest for a week. = 医生曾再次建议病人休息一周。

词源:  The word "readvising" is derived from the verb "advise" which comes from the Old French word "aviser" meaning "to consider, to look at, to inform, to advise".  The prefix "re-" indicates a repetition or doing something again. Therefore, "readvising" means advising again or reconsidering.
