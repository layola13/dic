huckleberry:/ˈhʌkəlˌbɛri/| n. |越橘；美洲越橘|A small, dark-purple or black berry that grows on a low shrub, especially in North America.  It is often used in jams, pies, and other desserts.|例句：We went huckleberry picking in the woods. 我们去树林里采越橘。|例句：The pie was made with fresh huckleberry. 这馅饼是用新鲜的越橘做的。|近义词：blueberry, bilberry|反义词：无
