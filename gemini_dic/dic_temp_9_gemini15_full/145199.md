overbaking:/ˌoʊvərˈbeɪkɪŋ/| n. |烤过头；烘焙过度|The act of baking something for too long, resulting in dryness, hardness, or burning.  Overbaking is a common mistake in baking.  |例句：The cake suffered from overbaking and was dry.  蛋糕因为烤过头而变得干巴巴的。|例句：To avoid overbaking, use a thermometer to check the internal temperature.|为了避免烤过头，用温度计检查内部温度。|近义词：overcooking, burning|反义词：underbaking
