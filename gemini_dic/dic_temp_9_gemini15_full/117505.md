meek:/miːk/| adj.| 温顺的；谦逊的；懦弱的 |  Characterized by humility and submissiveness; gentle; mild.  It suggests a quiet and gentle nature, often lacking assertiveness.  |例句：He was a meek and mild-mannered man. 他是一个温和谦逊的人。

例句：She offered a meek apology for her mistake. 她为自己的错误道了歉。


近义词：gentle, mild, submissive, docile, humble

反义词：aggressive, assertive, domineering, forceful, bold


词源:  The word "meek" comes from the Old English word "mēok," meaning "soft," "gentle," or "mild." This in turn is related to the Proto-Germanic word "*mōkaz," which also had similar meanings.  The sense of submissiveness developed later.
