malison:/ˈmælɪsən/| n.|诅咒，咒骂|A malison is a curse; an invocation of evil or misfortune upon someone or something.  It often carries a stronger sense of malevolence than a simple curse.|The witch cast a malison upon the village, bringing a plague upon its people.  巫婆对村庄下了诅咒，给村民带来了瘟疫。|curse, imprecation, execration|blessing, benediction|名词
