forbears:/ˈfɔːrbeərz/| n.|祖先，前辈 |Those who came before, usually in a family lineage.  They are ancestors, predecessors, or forebears.|例句：He traced his lineage back to his forbears who settled in the New World in the 17th century. 他追溯了他的血统，追溯到17世纪定居在新世界的祖先。

例句：My forbears were farmers for generations. 我的祖先世世代代都是农民。


近义词：ancestors, predecessors, forefathers, foremothers


反义词：descendants, posterity


词性：名词
