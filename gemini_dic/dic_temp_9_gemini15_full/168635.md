procollectivist: /ˌproʊkəˈlɛktɪvɪst/ | adj. | 倾向集体主义的；支持集体主义的 |  Supporting or advocating for collectivism; emphasizing the importance of the group over the individual.  This term describes a belief system or political ideology that prioritizes the needs and goals of the collective over those of individual members.  It often contrasts with individualism. | Example: The procollectivist policies of the government prioritized communal farming over private land ownership.  政府支持集体主义的政策优先考虑集体耕作而非私人土地所有制。|近义词：collectivist, communitarian | 反义词：individualist, libertarian |  adj.
