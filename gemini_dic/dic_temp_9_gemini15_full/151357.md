parklands:/ˈpɑːrkˌlændz/| n. |公园绿地；绿地带 |Areas of land that are used as parks or are covered with grass and trees.  These areas are often publicly accessible and designed for recreation and enjoyment.  |例句：The city boasts extensive parklands, offering residents ample opportunities for outdoor activities.  这座城市拥有广阔的公园绿地，为居民提供了充足的户外活动机会。

The parklands surrounding the lake are a popular spot for picnics.  环绕湖泊的绿地是野餐的热门地点。|近义词：green spaces, recreational areas, public gardens |反义词：urban areas, built-up areas, industrial zones
