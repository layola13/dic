rover:/ˈroʊvər/ | n. | 漫游者；流浪者；探测车 | A rover is a person or thing that roams or travels freely.  It can also refer to a vehicle designed to explore a planet's surface, such as a Mars rover. | 例句：The Curiosity rover is exploring Mars. 好奇号探测车正在探索火星。

The desert rover wandered for days, lost and alone.  沙漠中的流浪者在荒野中迷失了方向，独自流浪了好几天。| 近义词：wanderer, explorer, traveler, rambler | 反义词：settler, homebody

词源:

The word "rover" comes from the Old English word "rofian," meaning "to rob" or "to plunder."  Over time, the meaning shifted to imply wandering or roaming, likely because robbers often traveled widely.  The modern sense of "rover" as a wanderer or explorer developed from this earlier meaning.
