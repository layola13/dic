inventorize:/ɪnˈvɛntəraɪz/ | vt. | 盘点；开列清单 | To make a list or inventory of (something).  This often involves systematically checking and recording items. | Example: We need to inventorize the warehouse before the audit. 我们需要在审计之前盘点仓库。  The company inventorized its assets at the end of the fiscal year.  该公司在财政年度结束时清点其资产。| 近义词：inventory, catalog, list | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->

They inventorize the stockroom weekly. = 他们每周都会盘点储藏室的库存。
The team inventorizes the equipment after each project. = 团队在每个项目之后都会盘点设备。
