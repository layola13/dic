reagitate:/ˌriːˈædʒɪteɪt/| vt.|重新激起；再次使激动；重新讨论|To stir up again; to excite anew; to discuss again.|例句：The news reagitated his grief. 这则新闻再次激起了他的悲伤。

例句：They decided to reagitate the issue at the next meeting. 他们决定在下一次会议上再次讨论这个问题。


近义词：revive, rekindle, rouse, stir up|反义词：calm, soothe, pacify|动词
