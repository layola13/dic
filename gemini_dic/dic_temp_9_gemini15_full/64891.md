essays:/ˈɛseɪz/| n.| 论文；散文；尝试 | A short piece of writing on a particular subject.  Essays can be formal or informal, and they often present the writer's own argument or perspective. | Example:  She wrote several essays for her college application. 她为大学申请写了几篇论文。  |近义词：composition, paper, treatise |反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He writes essays regularly. = 他定期写论文。
She often submits her essays on time. = 她经常按时提交论文。


一般过去时:
I wrote an essay about the French Revolution. = 我写了一篇关于法国大革命的论文。
They submitted their essays last week. = 他们上周提交了论文。


现在完成时:
I have written three essays this semester. = 这个学期我已经写了三篇论文。
She has not yet finished her essay. = 她还没完成她的论文。


过去完成时:
I had already written the essay before the deadline. = 我在截止日期之前就已经写完了论文。
They had submitted their essays before the professor left. = 教授离开之前，他们已经提交了论文。


将来时:
I will write an essay on Shakespeare. = 我将写一篇关于莎士比亚的论文。
She will submit her essay tomorrow. = 她明天将提交她的论文。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would write a longer essay. = 如果我有更多时间，我会写一篇更长的论文。
I wish I had written a better essay. = 我希望我写了一篇更好的论文。


被动语态:
The essay was graded by the professor. = 论文由教授批改。
The essays are being marked now. = 论文正在批改中。


疑问句:
Have you finished your essay? = 你写完论文了吗？
When will the essays be returned? = 什么时候退回论文？


否定句:
I did not write the essay. = 我没有写这篇论文。
She hasn't started her essay yet. = 她还没开始写论文。
