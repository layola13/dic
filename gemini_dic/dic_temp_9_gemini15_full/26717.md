cadetship:/kəˈdɛtʃɪp/| n.|见习生身份；学员资格；军校学生身份|The period of training as a cadet; the status of being a cadet.  It refers to the position or rank of a cadet, particularly in a military academy or similar institution,  and also encompasses the training and experience gained during this period. |Example: He completed his cadetship at the Royal Military Academy Sandhurst. 他完成了在桑赫斯特皇家军事学院的见习生训练。|近义词：apprenticeship, traineeship, studentship|反义词：graduation, completion of training
