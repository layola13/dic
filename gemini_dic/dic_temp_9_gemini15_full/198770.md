sicklebill:/ˈsɪkəlˌbɪl/| n. |镰嘴鸟|A bird with a long, curved bill resembling a sickle.  Sicklebills are typically found in tropical regions and are known for their distinctive beaks, which they use to probe for food in crevices and flowers.|例句：The brightly colored sicklebill was easily spotted amongst the dense foliage.  那只颜色鲜艳的镰嘴鸟很容易在茂密的树叶中被发现。
