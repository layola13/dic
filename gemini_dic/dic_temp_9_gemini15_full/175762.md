rapturous:/ˈræptʃərəs/| adj.|狂喜的；充满喜悦的；令人狂喜的| feeling or expressing great delight or joy|The rapturous applause lasted for several minutes.  热烈的掌声持续了几分钟。

The singer received a rapturous ovation after her performance.  歌手演出结束后受到了热烈的喝彩。| ecstatic, euphoric, joyful, blissful| apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic, miserable|
