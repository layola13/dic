recertify:/ˌriːˈsɜːrtɪfaɪ/ | vt. |重新认证，再认证 |To certify again; to re-validate a certification.  This involves a renewed assessment to ensure continued competence or compliance with standards. |例句：The company needs to recertify its safety procedures annually. 公司需要每年重新认证其安全程序。

例句：He had to recertify his pilot's license after a long break from flying. 他在长时间不飞行后必须重新认证他的飞行执照。


近义词：re-validate, renew, re-approve | 反义词：decertify (取消认证) | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company recertifies its products every year. = 公司每年对其产品进行重新认证。
They recertify their employees' skills regularly. = 他们定期对员工的技能进行重新认证。


一般过去时:
The pilot recertified his license last month. = 飞行员上个月重新认证了他的执照。
The organization recertified its quality management system. = 该组织重新认证了其质量管理体系。


现在完成时:
The factory has recertified its production line. = 工厂已经重新认证了其生产线。
We have recertified our software to meet the new standards. = 我们已经重新认证了我们的软件以符合新的标准。


过去完成时:
They had recertified the equipment before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经重新认证了设备。
She had recertified her professional credentials before applying for the job. = 她申请工作之前已经重新认证了她的专业资质。


将来时:
The engineer will recertify the bridge next week. = 工程师下周将重新认证这座桥梁。
We will recertify our ISO 9001 certification next year. = 我们明年将重新认证我们的ISO 9001认证。


虚拟语气:
If the system failed, we would have to recertify it immediately. = 如果系统出现故障，我们将不得不立即重新认证它。
It is essential that he recertify his skills before taking on this project. = 在承担这个项目之前，他必须重新认证他的技能，这一点至关重要。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
