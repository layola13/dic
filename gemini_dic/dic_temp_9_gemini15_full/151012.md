paranoically: /ˌpærənˈɔɪkəli/ | adv. | 妄想地，偏执地 |  In a manner characteristic of paranoia; excessively suspicious and distrustful.  | 例句：He acted paranoically, checking the locks multiple times. 他行为偏执，反复检查门锁。  | 例句：She paranoically believed everyone was plotting against her. 她妄想地认为每个人都在密谋对付她。 | 近义词：suspiciously, distrustfully, suspiciously, obsessively | 反义词：trustingly, confidently, calmly

词源:
From *paranoia* + *-ically*.  *Paranoia* comes from the Greek word *paranoia* (παρανοια), meaning "madness,"  from *para-* (παρα-), meaning "beside, beyond," and *nous* (νοῦς), meaning "mind."  The suffix *-ically* forms adverbs.
