historical:/hɪˈstɔːrɪkəl/| adj.|历史的；史实的；具有历史意义的|relating to history or past events.  The word often implies significance or importance.|例句：The historical significance of the event is undeniable.  这个事件的历史意义不容否认。

例句：This building is a historical landmark. 这座建筑是具有历史意义的地标建筑。


近义词：historic, past, ancient

反义词：modern, contemporary, current


词源: From Middle English *historik, from Old French historique, from Latin historicus, from historia "history," from Greek historia "inquiry, history," from histōr "one who knows, investigator," from hīstēmi "I stand, I cause to stand."
