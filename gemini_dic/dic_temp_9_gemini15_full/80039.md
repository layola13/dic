go-with:/ɡoʊ wɪð/ | v. | 搭配，与…相配，协调 | To be suitable or compatible with something else; to harmonize or match well.  | 例句：That shirt goes well with your trousers. = 那件衬衫和你的裤子很相配。  | 例句：Her personality doesn't go with her job. = 她的性格和她的工作不相符。 | 近义词：match, suit, complement, harmonize | 反义词：clash, conflict, mismatch | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This wine goes well with fish. = 这款酒和鱼很配。
That color goes with everything. = 那个颜色百搭。

一般过去时:
The dress didn't go with her shoes. = 那件衣服和她的鞋子不搭配。
His explanation didn't go with the facts. = 他的解释与事实不符。

现在完成时:
This trend has gone with the times. = 这种趋势已经过时了。
Everything has gone with the plan. = 一切都按计划进行。

过去完成时:
The music had gone with the movie perfectly. = 音乐和电影完美地融合在一起。
The evidence had gone with the theory. = 证据支持了这个理论。


将来时:
This style will go with the new season's fashion. = 这种风格将与新一季的时尚相符。
I think this design will go with the house style. = 我认为这个设计会符合房子的风格。

虚拟语气:
If that color went with my skin tone, I would buy it. = 如果那个颜色适合我的肤色，我会买它。
I wish this furniture went with my decor. = 我希望这套家具与我的装饰风格相配。

被动语态:
The color scheme was carefully chosen to go with the overall design. = 色彩方案经过精心挑选，以与整体设计相协调。
The new policy is designed to go with the existing regulations. = 新政策旨在与现有规章相符。

疑问句:
Does this tie go with this shirt? = 这条领带和这件衬衫搭配吗？
Does this furniture go with the room's style? = 这套家具与房间的风格相符吗？

否定句:
That hat doesn't go with your outfit. = 那顶帽子不适合你的衣服。
The two ideas don't go with each other. = 这两个想法不相容。
