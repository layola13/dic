cult:/kʌlt/| n.|邪教；崇拜；风尚；社会群体|A system of religious veneration and devotion directed towards a particular figure or object.  A relatively small group of people having religious beliefs or practices regarded by others as strange or dangerous.|例句：The Jonestown cult was responsible for many deaths.琼斯镇邪教导致许多人死亡。

例句：Her devotion to her work bordered on a cult.她对工作的奉献近乎狂热崇拜。

近义词：sect, sect, movement, craze

反义词：mainstream, orthodoxy
