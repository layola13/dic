jarred:/dʒɑːrd/ | /ˈdʒɑːrd/ | vt. n. | 使震动；使震惊；使不适；(食物)腌制；(玻璃等)裂纹 | To shake or jolt violently; to have a jarring effect on; to clash or be discordant; to preserve (food) in a jar; a jarring sound or effect; a crack or fissure (in glass, etc) |例句：The news jarred him deeply.  这个消息使他深受震动。 | The sudden noise jarred me. 突如其来的噪音使我震惊。|近义词：shocked, startled, jolted, cracked |反义词： soothed, calmed, pleased


USAGE->
