nonpositivistic: /ˌnɒnˌpɒzɪˈtɪvɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非实证主义的 | Not adhering to positivism; rejecting the view that only empirically verifiable statements are meaningful.  It emphasizes the subjective, interpretive, and value-laden aspects of knowledge.  | Example:  The study employed a nonpositivistic approach, prioritizing qualitative data and interpretive analysis over quantitative measurements.  = 这项研究采用了一种非实证主义的方法，优先考虑定性数据和解释性分析，而不是定量测量。| 近义词: interpretivist, constructivist, hermeneutic | 反义词: positivistic, empirical, objective
