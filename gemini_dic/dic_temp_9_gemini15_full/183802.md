resealing:/ˌriːˈsiːlɪŋ/| v.|重新密封；再次密封 |The act of sealing something again.  It implies a previous sealing that has been compromised or needs to be reinforced. |例句：The company specializes in resealing damaged packaging to ensure product freshness. 公司专门从事重新密封损坏的包装，以确保产品的新鲜度。

例句：After the leak, we needed to reseal the window to prevent further water damage. 漏水后，我们需要重新密封窗户以防止进一步的水损坏。


近义词：resealing, reseal, re-sealing


反义词：opening, unsealing


词性：动词
