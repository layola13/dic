scarps:/skɑːrps/| n.|陡坡；峭壁；断崖|A steep slope or cliff, especially one that is part of a larger geological formation.  Scarps are often formed by faulting or erosion.|例句：The hikers carefully navigated the steep scarps. 登山者小心翼翼地绕过陡峭的悬崖峭壁。

例句：The landscape was dramatic, punctuated by numerous scarps and canyons. 这片景色壮观，点缀着许多峭壁和峡谷。|近义词：cliff, escarpment, precipice|反义词：plain, flatland
