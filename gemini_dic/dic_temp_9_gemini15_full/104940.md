kelp:/kɛlp/ | n. | 海带；巨藻 |  Large brown seaweed that grows in underwater forests, often used as food or in other products.  | Example:  The divers explored the kelp forests off the coast of California.  潜水员探索了加利福尼亚海岸外的海藻林。

| Example:  We added kelp to the soup for a richer flavor. 我们在汤里加了海带以增加鲜味。|近义词：seaweed (泛指海藻)|反义词：None|词性：名词
