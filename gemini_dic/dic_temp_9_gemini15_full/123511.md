monotocous:/məˈnɒtəkəs/| adj. | 单胎的；一次只生一个的 |  Producing only one offspring at a time.  The term is typically used to describe animals, particularly mammals. | Example:  Horses are generally monotocous, meaning they usually give birth to a single foal.  马通常是单胎的，这意味着它们通常只产下一匹小马驹。| 近义词: uniparous | 反义词: polytocous
