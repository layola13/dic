nursing: /ˈnɜːrsɪŋ/ | n. | 护理，照料；护士职业 | The act of caring for someone, especially a sick or injured person; the profession of being a nurse. | 例句：She is studying nursing at university. = 她正在大学学习护理。

She provides excellent nursing care to her patients. = 她为她的病人提供优质的护理。


近义词：care, tending, ministration | 反义词：neglect, abandonment | 词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She nurses her sick mother. = 她照顾她生病的母亲。
He nurses his injured knee. = 他护理他受伤的膝盖。


一般过去时:
The nurse nursed the baby gently. = 护士轻轻地护理婴儿。
They nursed their sick friend back to health. = 他们照顾生病的朋友直到康复。


现在完成时:
She has nursed her grandmother for many years. = 她照顾祖母多年了。
The hospital has nursed countless patients over the years. = 这家医院多年来已经护理了无数的病人。


过去完成时:
She had nursed her grandfather before he passed away. = 她在她祖父去世前照顾了他。
They had nursed the injured bird until it could fly again. = 他们照顾受伤的小鸟直到它又能飞翔。


将来时:
She will nurse her newborn baby. = 她将会照顾她的新生儿。
He will nurse his wounds until they heal. = 他将护理他的伤口直到痊愈。


虚拟语气:
If I were a nurse, I would nurse the sick with compassion. = 如果我是一名护士，我会带着同情心护理病人。
I wish I could nurse my ailing father. = 我希望我能照顾我生病的父亲。


被动语态:
The patient was nursed back to health. = 病人被护理康复了。
The injured soldier was nursed by the Red Cross. = 受伤的士兵由红十字会护理。


疑问句:
Do you nurse your baby at night? = 你晚上喂奶吗？（此处nurse指哺乳）
Does she plan to pursue a career in nursing? = 她计划从事护理事业吗？


否定句:
He didn't nurse his wounds properly. = 他没有正确护理他的伤口。
She doesn't nurse patients in the hospital. = 她不在医院护理病人。


PHRASE->
breastfeeding/bottle-feeding (哺乳)
intensive care nursing (重症监护护理)
palliative care nursing (姑息性护理)
geriatric nursing (老年护理)
community nursing (社区护理)
