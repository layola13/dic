commanderies:/kəˈmændəriːz/ | n. |  司令部；管辖区；骑士团管辖区 |  A commandery is a district or territory under the jurisdiction of a commander, especially a military commander or the head of a religious or knightly order.  It often refers to a unit of land administered by a religious or military order. | The Knights Templar established numerous commanderies across Europe.  =  圣殿骑士团在欧洲建立了许多司令部。

The Hospitallers held several powerful commanderies in the Holy Land. =  医院骑士团在圣地拥有几个强大的管辖区。


近义词：prefecture, province, district, territory

反义词：None directly applicable; antonyms would depend on the context (e.g., if contrasting with a decentralized system).
