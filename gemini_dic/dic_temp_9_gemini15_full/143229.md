outdances:/ˌaʊtˈdænsɪz/| v.|胜过（在跳舞方面）|to surpass in dancing skill or grace|The young dancer outdanced all her competitors.|近义词：outshine, outdo|反义词：be outdanced by


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She outdances everyone in the class. = 她跳舞比班里每个人都好。
He outdances his partner with ease. = 他轻松地胜过他的舞伴。


一般过去时:
She outdanced all the other contestants. = 她胜过了所有其他的参赛者。
The lead dancer outdanced his rival in the final round. = 在决赛中，首席舞者胜过了他的对手。


现在完成时:
She has outdanced all her rivals in the competition. = 在比赛中，她已经胜过了所有对手。
He has outdanced many experienced dancers throughout his career. = 在他整个职业生涯中，他已经胜过了许多经验丰富的舞者。


过去完成时:
By the end of the performance, she had outdanced everyone on stage. = 表演结束时，她已经胜过了舞台上的每个人。
He had outdanced his opponent before the final judge's score. = 在最终评委打分之前，他已经胜过了他的对手。


将来时:
She will outdance her opponent in the upcoming competition. = 在即将到来的比赛中，她将会胜过她的对手。
He believes he will outdance all the other dancers. = 他相信他会胜过所有其他的舞者。


虚拟语气:
If she practiced more, she would outdance everyone. = 如果她多练习，她就能胜过所有人。
I wish I could outdance her; she's so graceful. = 我希望我能胜过她；她太优雅了。


被动语态:
He was outdanced by a younger, more agile competitor. = 他被一位更年轻、更灵活的竞争对手击败了。
The experienced dancer was surprisingly outdanced by the newcomer. = 令人惊讶的是，经验丰富的舞者被新人击败了。


疑问句:
Can she outdance her sister? = 她能胜过她姐姐吗？
Will he be able to outdance the champion? = 他能够胜过冠军吗？


否定句:
He didn't outdance his opponent. = 他没有胜过他的对手。
She couldn't outdance the reigning champion. = 她无法胜过卫冕冠军。
