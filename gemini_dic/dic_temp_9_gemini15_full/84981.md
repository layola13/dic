happing:/ˈhæpɪŋ/| n.|正在发生的事；偶然事件|An event or occurrence, especially one that is unplanned or unexpected.  It often refers to something happening at a particular time or in a particular place. |例句：There's a lot of happening at the festival. 节日上有很多活动。 The street was full of happenings. 街上热闹非凡。 |近义词：event, occurrence, incident |反义词：nothingness, stillness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is happening now? = 现在发生了什么？
What is happening in the news? = 新闻里发生了什么？

一般过去时:
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？
What happened at the party? = 晚会上发生了什么？

现在完成时:
What has happened since you left? = 你离开后发生了什么？
What has happened to the project? = 项目发生了什么情况？


过去完成时:
What had happened before the accident? = 事故发生前发生了什么？
What had happened before he arrived? = 他到达之前发生了什么？

将来时:
What will be happening tomorrow? = 明天会发生什么？
What will be happening at the conference? = 会议上会发生什么？

虚拟语气:
If something interesting were happening, I would go. = 如果有什么有趣的事情发生，我会去的。
I wish something exciting were happening. = 我希望有什么令人兴奋的事情发生。

被动语态:
What is being happened to the environment? = 环境正在发生什么？ (Note:  "is being happened" is grammatically questionable. A better phrasing would be "What is happening to the environment?")
What is being done about the situation? = 针对这种情况正在采取什么措施？

疑问句:
What is happening? = 正在发生什么？
What's happening there? = 那儿发生了什么？

否定句:
Nothing is happening. = 什么事也没有发生。
Nothing much is happening. = 没什么事情发生。


PHRASE->
happening now = 正在发生
happening place = 事件发生地
what's happening = 发生了什么
the happening = 事件
a happening = 一个事件
