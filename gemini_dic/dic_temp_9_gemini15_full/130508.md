nonaccommodable: /ˌnɒnəˈkɒmədeɪbəl/ | adj. | 不可容纳的，无法适应的 |  Unable to be accommodated; incapable of being adapted or adjusted to.  The inflexible scheduling made the event nonaccommodable to our needs. | 近义词：unadaptable, inflexible, unyielding | 反义词：accommodating, adaptable, flexible


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hotel is nonaccommodable to large groups. = 这家酒店无法容纳大型团队。
This software is nonaccommodable to older operating systems. = 这个软件无法兼容旧的操作系统。


一般过去时:
The previous arrangement was nonaccommodable to the changes in the project. = 之前的安排无法适应项目的变更。
Their rigid policies were nonaccommodable to our special request. = 他们僵化的政策无法满足我们的特殊要求。


现在完成时:
The company has been nonaccommodable to employee requests for flexible working hours. = 公司一直以来无法满足员工对弹性工作时间的请求。
The system has proven nonaccommodable to the increasing data volume. = 系统已被证明无法适应不断增长的数据量。


过去完成时:
The outdated technology had been nonaccommodable to the demands of the modern market. = 过时的技术已经无法满足现代市场的需求。
Their inflexible approach had been nonaccommodable to the evolving circumstances. = 他们僵化的做法已经无法适应不断变化的环境。


将来时:
The new regulations will be nonaccommodable to small businesses. = 新的法规将无法适应小型企业的需要。
The planned expansion will likely be nonaccommodable to the current infrastructure. = 计划中的扩张可能会无法适应现有的基础设施。


虚拟语气:
If the schedule were more flexible, it wouldn't be nonaccommodable. = 如果日程安排更灵活一些，它就不会无法适应。
I wish the system were more accommodating; it's currently nonaccommodable to our needs. = 我希望系统更能适应；它目前无法满足我们的需求。


被动语态:

Their concerns were found to be nonaccommodable within the existing framework. = 他们的担忧在现有框架内被发现是无法解决的。


疑问句:

否定句:
