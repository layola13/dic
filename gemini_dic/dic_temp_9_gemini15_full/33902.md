circumventable: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈvɛntəbl/ | adj. | 可规避的，可绕过的 |  capable of being circumvented or avoided.  Something that is circumventable can be gotten around or avoided. | The problem, while difficult, is circumventable with careful planning.  这个问题虽然棘手，但只要仔细规划就能规避。  |近义词：avoidable, evitable, escapable | 反义词：inevitable, unavoidable

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rule is circumventable. = 这条规则是可以规避的。
This problem is circumventable with a little effort. = 这个问题只要稍微努力一下就能解决。

一般过去时:
The security system was circumventable. = 安全系统是可以绕过的。
The obstacle was circumventable by taking a different route. = 通过选择不同的路线，障碍是可以绕过的。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

词源:
circumventable derives from the verb "circumvent," which comes from the Latin words "circum" (around) and "venire" (to come).  Therefore, it literally means "to come around."  Over time, its meaning evolved to imply going around an obstacle or difficulty.
