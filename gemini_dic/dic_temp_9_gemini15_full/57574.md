dramatized:/ˈdræməˌtaɪzd/| v.|戏剧化；使戏剧性；|to represent (something) in a dramatic way; to make something seem more exciting or important than it really is.|例句：The news report dramatized the events of the hostage crisis. 新闻报道戏剧化地描述了人质危机的事件。

例句：He dramatized his minor injury to gain sympathy. 他夸大了自己的轻伤以博取同情。


近义词：exaggerated, sensationalized, romanticized|反义词：understated, downplayed, minimized|词性：动词
