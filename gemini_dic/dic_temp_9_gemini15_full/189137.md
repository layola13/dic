sacerdotalism:/ˌsæsərˈdoʊtəlɪzəm/| n. |僧侣统治；祭司统治；神权政治|The system or practice of priestly government or rule; the dominance of the clergy in secular affairs.|The rise of sacerdotalism in the Middle Ages led to significant conflicts between Church and State.  中世纪神权政治的兴起导致了教会与国家之间严重的冲突。|The excessive influence of religious authorities in secular matters can be seen as a form of sacerdotalism. 世俗事务中宗教权威的过度影响可以被视为一种神权政治。|clericalism, theocracy|secularism, laicism|名词
