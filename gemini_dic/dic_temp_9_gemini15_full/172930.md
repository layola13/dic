qualifications:/ˌkwɒlɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |资格，资历，条件|The skills, knowledge, and experience that make someone suitable for a particular job or course of study.  It often refers to formal certificates or degrees, but also includes practical skills and experience. |例句：He lacks the necessary qualifications for the job. 他缺乏这份工作所需的资格。

例句：Her qualifications include a PhD in physics and five years of research experience. 她的资历包括物理学博士学位和五年的研究经验。


近义词：credentials, attributes, assets,  requirements

反义词：disqualifications, inadequacies


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What are your qualifications for this position? = 你担任这个职位的资格是什么？
What qualifications does she possess? = 她拥有哪些资格？


一般过去时:
What were his qualifications when he applied for the job? = 他申请这份工作时有什么资格？
What qualifications did the candidate have? = 候选人有什么资格？


现在完成时:
What qualifications have you gained since graduating? = 你毕业后获得了什么资格？
What qualifications has she acquired in her career? = 她职业生涯中获得了什么资格？


过去完成时:
What qualifications had he obtained before he started his business? = 他创业前获得了什么资格？
What qualifications had she already acquired by that time? = 到那时为止她已经获得了什么资格？


将来时:
What qualifications will you need for the advanced course? = 你需要什么资格才能参加高级课程？
What qualifications will she have by next year? = 到明年她将拥有哪些资格？


虚拟语气:
If I had the necessary qualifications, I would apply for the job. = 如果我有必要的资格，我会申请这份工作。
I wish I had the qualifications to become a doctor. = 我希望我有资格成为一名医生。


被动语态:
The applicant's qualifications were carefully reviewed. = 申请人的资格受到了仔细审查。
His qualifications are being considered by the selection committee. = 他的资格正在由遴选委员会考虑。


疑问句:
What qualifications are required for this role? = 这个角色需要什么资格？
What are the minimum qualifications for entry? = 入门的最低资格是什么？


否定句:
He doesn't have the right qualifications for the position. = 他没有担任这个职位的正确资格。
She lacked the qualifications needed for promotion. = 她缺乏晋升所需的资格。
