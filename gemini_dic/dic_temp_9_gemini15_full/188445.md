ruddy:/ˈrʌdi/| adj.|红润的；满脸通红的；粗俗的；糟糕的|Having a reddish complexion;  also used informally to express anger, annoyance, or emphasis. |例句：He had a ruddy complexion after a day in the sun. 他在阳光下晒了一天后脸色红润。|例句：That's a ruddy nuisance! 这真是个讨厌的东西！|近义词：red, flushed, rosy, damned (informal)|反义词：pale, wan
