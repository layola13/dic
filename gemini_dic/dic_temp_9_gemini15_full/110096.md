licitation:/ˌlɪsɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |引出；诱导；索取；招标 |The process of eliciting information or responses, often in a structured or formal way.  It can refer to drawing out information from someone, or the process of inviting bids for a project. |例句：The researcher conducted a licitation to gather data from participants. 研究人员进行了一项引出调查以收集参与者的数据。

例句：The government announced a licitation for the construction of a new highway. 政府宣布了一项关于新建高速公路的招标。

近义词：elicitation, solicitation, procurement, tendering

反义词：suppression, withholding, concealment

词性:名词
