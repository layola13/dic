rival:/ˈraɪvəl/ | n., v. |竞争对手；与…竞争 | A person or thing competing with another for the same objective or for superiority in the same field.  |例句：He's my main rival for the job. 他是我的主要竞争对手。

例句：She's a strong rival in the tennis tournament. 她在网球锦标赛中是一位强大的对手。


近义词：competitor, opponent, contender | 反义词：ally, supporter


词性：名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rivals his brother in intelligence. = 他在智力方面与他兄弟不相上下。
My company rivals theirs in market share. = 我们公司在市场份额上与他们竞争。


一般过去时:
She rivaled her sister in beauty. = 她的美貌与她姐姐不相上下。
The two teams rivaled each other in skill and determination. = 这两支球队在技巧和决心方面互相竞争。


现在完成时:
He has rivaled many competitors in the industry. = 他在这个行业中已经与许多竞争对手竞争过了。
Our sales have rivaled those of our biggest competitor. = 我们的销售额已经与我们最大的竞争对手不相上下。


过去完成时:
They had rivaled each other for years before finally settling their differences. = 在最终解决分歧之前，他们已经互相竞争多年了。
The company had rivaled its competitors in innovation for decades. = 这家公司在几十年里一直在创新方面与竞争对手竞争。


将来时:
She will rival her predecessors in success. = 她将在成功方面与她的前任们竞争。
The new product will likely rival existing ones in the market. = 新产品可能会在市场上与现有产品竞争。


虚拟语气:
If he were to rival her in skill, he would have a better chance of winning. = 如果他在技巧上能与她竞争，他将有更大的获胜机会。
I wish I could rival her achievements. = 我真希望我能与她的成就相媲美。


被动语态:
He was rivaled by many other applicants for the position. = 许多其他申请者都在竞争这个职位。
Their performance was rivaled by no other team. = 他们的表现没有其他球队能与之匹敌。


疑问句:
Does he rival anyone in his field? = 他在这个领域里与任何人竞争吗？
Who rivals him in strength? = 谁的力气能与他匹敌？


否定句:
He doesn't rival her in artistic talent. = 他在艺术天赋上不如她。
The smaller company couldn't rival the larger one's resources. = 小公司无法与大公司的资源竞争。


PHRASE->
close rival = 强劲的竞争对手
main rival = 主要竞争对手
fierce rival = 激烈的竞争对手
potential rival = 潜在的竞争对手
a bitter rival = 死对头
