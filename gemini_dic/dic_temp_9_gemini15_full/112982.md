luller:/ˈlʌlə(r)/| n., v.| 催眠曲；使平静的人或事物；使平静的动作 |Something or someone that soothes or calms; a person or thing that lulls.  A lullaby. |例句：The gentle rocking of the boat was a luller.  摇晃的小船是一种催眠。 The soft music acted as a luller for the baby. 轻柔的音乐对婴儿起到了催眠的作用。|近义词：soother, calmer, pacifier |反义词：exciter, stimulant, agitator |名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music lulls the baby to sleep. = 音乐使婴儿入睡。
The rocking chair lulls her into a peaceful state. = 摇椅使她平静下来。

一般过去时:
The story lulled me to sleep. = 这个故事让我睡着了。
The gentle breeze lulled the waves. = 柔和的微风使波浪平静下来。

现在完成时:
The constant noise has lulled me into a state of apathy. = 持续不断的噪音让我变得冷漠。
The repetitive task has lulled her into a sense of boredom. = 重复性的工作让她感到厌烦。

过去完成时:
The quiet atmosphere had lulled them into a false sense of security. = 安静的气氛让他们产生了一种虚假的安全感。
The long, peaceful day had lulled the city into a quiet sleep. = 漫长而平静的一天让城市沉睡了。

将来时:
The soft music will lull the baby to sleep. = 轻柔的音乐会使婴儿入睡。
The gentle waves will lull them into a relaxing state. = 温柔的波浪会使他们放松身心。

虚拟语气:
If the music were louder, it wouldn't lull the baby. = 如果音乐更大点，它就不会催眠婴儿了。
If the waves were calmer, they would lull me to sleep more effectively. = 如果波浪更平静，它们会更有效地让我入睡。

被动语态:
The baby was lulled to sleep by the music. = 婴儿被音乐催眠入睡。
The audience was lulled into a state of complacency by the speaker's words. = 听众被演讲者的言辞麻痹了。

疑问句:
Does the sound of rain lull you to sleep? = 雨声能让你入睡吗？
Will the gentle rocking of the hammock lull you into a relaxed state? = 吊床的轻柔摇晃会让你放松吗？

否定句:
The loud music didn't lull the baby to sleep. = 嘈杂的音乐没有让婴儿入睡。
The rough sea didn't lull the sailors. = 汹涌的大海没有使水手们平静下来。
