flat-footedly: /ˌflætˈfʊtɪdli/ | adv. |  直率地；坦率地；不拐弯抹角地 |  In a direct and straightforward manner, without hesitation or evasion.  It suggests a lack of subtlety or diplomacy. |

例句：

He flat-footedly denied any involvement in the crime. 他直截了当地否认参与了这起犯罪。

She flat-footedly refused his offer of help. 她断然拒绝了他提供的帮助。


近义词： bluntly, frankly, openly, directly, forthrightly

反义词： subtly, indirectly, cautiously, diplomatically


词性：副词
