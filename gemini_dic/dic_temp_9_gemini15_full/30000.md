cavort:/kəˈvɔːrt/| v.|嬉戏，欢跃，雀跃|To move about in a lively and playful way.  It often implies a sense of exuberance and freedom.|例句：The children cavorted joyfully in the park. 孩子们在公园里快乐地嬉戏。
The horses cavorted in the field. 马儿在田野里欢跃。|近义词：frolic, romp, gambol, prance|反义词：lag, slump, mope

They cavort on the beach every summer. = 他们每年夏天都在海滩上嬉戏。
