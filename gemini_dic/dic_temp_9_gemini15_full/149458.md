paddled:/ˈpædəld/| v.|划桨；涉水；|to propel (a boat, etc.) through water using a paddle or paddles; to move or travel through water by using a paddle or paddles.|例句：He paddled the canoe across the lake. 他划着独木舟穿过湖泊。

例句：The children paddled in the shallows. 孩子们在浅水区涉水玩耍。|近义词：row, propel, wade|反义词：sail (in the context of boat propulsion)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He paddles the kayak every weekend. = 他每个周末都划皮划艇。
They paddle across the river. = 他们划船过河。

一般过去时:
She paddled her canoe down the river. = 她划着独木舟顺流而下。
We paddled to the island. = 我们划船到岛上。

现在完成时:
He has paddled across the English Channel. = 他已经划过英吉利海峡。
They have paddled for hours. = 他们已经划了好几个小时了。

过去完成时:
She had paddled for miles before she reached the shore. = 她划了好几英里才到达岸边。
They had paddled all day before finding a campsite. = 他们在找到营地之前划了一整天。

将来时:
We will paddle along the coast tomorrow. = 我们明天将在海岸边划船。
They will paddle to the next island. = 他们将划船到下一个岛屿。


虚拟语气:
If I had a kayak, I would paddle across the lake. = 如果我有一艘皮划艇，我会划过湖泊。
I wish I could paddle like a professional. = 我希望我能像专业人士一样划船。

被动语态:
The canoe was paddled across the lake by a skilled paddler. = 这艘独木舟由一位熟练的划桨者划过湖泊。
The river is often paddled by tourists. = 这条河经常被游客划船。

疑问句:
Did you paddle to the island? = 你划船到岛上去了吗？
Have they ever paddled a canoe before? = 他们以前划过独木舟吗？

否定句:
He didn't paddle far today. = 他今天没有划得很远。
They couldn't paddle against the strong current. = 他们无法逆着强流划船。


PHRASE->
paddle a canoe = 划独木舟
paddle upstream = 逆流而上
paddle down the river = 顺流而下
paddle your own canoe = 自力更生
go for a paddle = 去划船


词源:
The word "paddle" comes from the Old English word "paddan," meaning "to dabble or splash in water."  This reflects the action of the paddle in the water.  The verb form evolved from this root.
