congregated:/ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪtɪd/| v.|聚集，集合|To come together in a group or crowd.|The students congregated in the auditorium before the lecture. 学生们在演讲前聚集在大礼堂。|近义词：gathered, assembled, collected|反义词：dispersed, scattered, separated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
People congregate in the town square every Sunday. = 人们每个星期天都在城镇广场聚集。
Birds congregate near the lake during the migration season. =候鸟在迁徙季节聚集在湖边。


一般过去时:
The crowd congregated to watch the parade. = 人群聚集起来观看游行。
The family congregated for their annual reunion. =  家人聚集在一起参加他们每年的团聚。


现在完成时:
A large number of people have congregated outside the stadium. =  许多人已聚集在体育场外。
The protesters have congregated in front of the government building. = 抗议者们已经聚集在政府大楼前。


过去完成时:
The villagers had congregated before the storm hit. =  村民们在暴风雨来临之前就已经聚集在一起了。
The birds had congregated in the trees before sunset. = 鸟儿们在日落之前就聚集在树上了。


将来时:
The fans will congregate outside the concert hall. = 粉丝们将在音乐厅外聚集。
The delegates will congregate for the international summit. = 代表们将为国际峰会而聚集。


虚拟语气:
If they had congregated earlier, they would have avoided the rain. = 如果他们早点聚集，他们就能避免淋雨了。
I wish the crowd had congregated more peacefully. = 我希望人群聚集得更和平一些。


被动语态:
The people were congregated by the police. = 人们被警察聚集起来。
The animals were congregated in the zoo. = 动物们被聚集在动物园里。


疑问句:
Where did the people congregate? = 人们在哪里聚集？
Why did the birds congregate there? = 为什么鸟儿在那里聚集？


否定句:
The people did not congregate in the usual place. = 人们没有在通常的地方聚集。
The students did not congregate for the meeting. = 学生们没有为会议而聚集。


PHRASE->
congregate together = 聚集在一起
congregate around = 聚集在...周围
congregate in = 聚集在...里面
congregate for = 为了...而聚集
congregate with = 与...聚集
