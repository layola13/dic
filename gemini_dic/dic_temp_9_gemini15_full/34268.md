claptrap:/ˈklæptræp/| n.|空话；废话；胡说八道|Nonsense; empty talk; insincere or pretentious talk.|例句：His speech was nothing but claptrap. 他的演讲全是空话。|近义词：balderdash, bosh, twaddle, hogwash|反义词：truth, sense, wisdom
