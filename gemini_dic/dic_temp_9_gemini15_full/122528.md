modalist: /ˈmoʊdəlɪst/ | n. | 模式论者；唯模式论者 | A person who adheres to modalism, a theological doctrine that identifies the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit as three modes or manifestations of one God.  Modalism denies the distinct persons of the Trinity. |  He was a staunch modalist, arguing vehemently against the trinitarian view. 他是一个坚定的模式论者，强烈反对三位一体的观点。| trinitarian, unitarian | trinitarian (in theological context) |
