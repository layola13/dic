columbarium:/ˌkəˌlʌmˈberiəm/| n. |骨灰存放室；骨灰龛；鸽舍|A columbarium is a structure containing niches for storing urns of ashes.  It is often found in cemeteries or within larger religious buildings. The word's origins relate to its resemblance to a dovecote.|例句：The family visited the columbarium to pay their respects at their father's niche.  家人去骨灰存放室向父亲的骨灰龛致敬。|近义词：urn niche, mausoleum (for larger structures)|反义词：N/A|名词
