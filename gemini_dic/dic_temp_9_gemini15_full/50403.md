deposition:/ˌdɛpəˈzɪʃən/| n. |沉淀；证词；罢免 |The act of depositing something;  the material deposited;  a formal statement made under oath.  It can refer to geological processes, legal proceedings, or the removal of someone from a position. |例句：The deposition of sediment formed the delta.  沉积物的沉积形成了三角洲。

例句：The lawyer questioned the witness about their deposition. 律师就证词讯问证人。


近义词：sedimentation (for geological sense), testimony, statement, affidavit (for legal sense), removal, dismissal (for removal from office) |反义词：elevation (opposite of geological deposition),  reinstatement (opposite of removal from office) |词性 n.
