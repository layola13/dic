line:/laɪn/| n. |线条，线；排，行；路线；诗行；电话线；(商品的)种类；|A straight line is the shortest distance between two points. 直线是两点之间最短的距离。  The cars were lined up in a long line. 车子排成一长排。|近义词：row, stripe, streak |反义词：curve, bend


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The line is long today. = 今天的队伍很长。
This line of work is very demanding. = 这行工作非常费力。


一般过去时:
The line broke down during the storm. = 电线在暴风雨中断了。
He drew a straight line across the page. = 他在纸上画了一条直线。


现在完成时:
They have waited in line for hours. = 他们已经排队等了几个小时。
She has crossed the finish line. = 她已经过了终点线。


过去完成时:
The phone line had been cut before we called. = 我们打电话之前电话线已经被切断了。
They had already crossed the line before the rain started. = 下雨开始之前他们已经越线了。


将来时:
The new product line will launch next month. = 新产品系列将于下月推出。
Will you be in line by then? = 到那时你会排队吗？


虚拟语气:
If the line were shorter, I would have gone to the store. = 如果队伍短一点，我就去商店了。
I wish this line would move faster. = 我希望这条队伍能快一点。


被动语态:
The line was drawn with a ruler. = 这条线是用尺子画的。
The phone lines were down for several hours. = 电话线路中断了好几个小时。


疑问句:
What line of work are you in? = 你从事什么行业？
Is there a line at the bank? = 银行排队吗？


否定句:
There was no line at the post office. = 邮局没有排队。
This drawing doesn't have a straight line. = 这幅画没有直线。
