howitzers:/ˈhaʊɪtsərz/| n. |榴弹炮，臼炮|A howitzer is a type of artillery gun that fires shells at high angles, allowing it to reach targets behind cover or over obstacles.  Howitzers are characterized by their relatively short barrel and high trajectory compared to other artillery pieces like guns. They are used for both direct and indirect fire. |The army deployed its howitzers to provide supporting fire.|近义词：cannon, artillery piece|反义词：无
