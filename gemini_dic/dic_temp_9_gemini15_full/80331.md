goldest:/ˈɡoʊldɪst/| adj.|最金色的，最像黄金的|Most golden; most like gold in color or quality.  The word is a superlative form of "golden," and while grammatically correct, it's rarely used in modern English because more descriptive alternatives exist.|例句：The goldest sunset I've ever seen painted the sky in vibrant hues. 我见过的最金色的日落用鲜艳的色彩染红了天空。The goldest honey had a rich, deep flavor. 最金色的蜂蜜有着浓郁而深沉的香味。|近义词：most golden, most gilded, goldenest|反义词：dullest, darkest
