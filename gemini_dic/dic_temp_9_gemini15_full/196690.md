severed:/ˈsɛvərd/| vt.|切断；断绝；解雇|To cut off or separate, especially by force.  It can refer to physical objects or relationships.  |例句：The surgeon severed the artery. = 外科医生切断了动脉。
The phone line was severed by the storm. = 电话线被暴风雨切断了。|近义词：cut, separate, break, dismiss|反义词：join, connect, unite


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He severs the bread. = 他切面包。
She severs ties with her past. = 她与过去断绝关系。

一般过去时:
The accident severed his leg. = 事故切断了他的腿。
They severed their business partnership. = 他们解除了他们的商业伙伴关系。

现在完成时:
The storm has severed power to the entire town. = 暴风雨已经切断了整个城镇的电力供应。
She has severed all contact with her family. = 她已经与家人断绝了一切联系。

过去完成时:
The line had been severed before we could call for help. = 在我们能求助之前，线路就已经被切断了。
They had severed their relationship months ago. = 他们几个月前就已经断绝了关系。

将来时:
The surgeon will sever the tumor from the surrounding tissue. = 外科医生将把肿瘤与周围组织切除。
They will sever their contract next month. = 他们下个月将终止合同。

虚拟语气:
If he severed the wire, the alarm would sound. = 如果他切断电线，警报就会响起。
If she had severed ties earlier, she would have been happier. = 如果她早点断绝关系，她会更快乐。

被动语态:
The artery was severed during the operation. = 动脉在手术中被切断了。
His employment was severed due to misconduct. = 由于行为不端，他被解雇了。

疑问句:
Was the connection severed? = 连接中断了吗？
Have they severed all ties with the company? = 他们已经与公司断绝一切联系了吗？

否定句:
The surgeon did not sever the nerve. = 外科医生没有切断神经。
They have not severed their friendship. = 他们没有断绝友谊。
