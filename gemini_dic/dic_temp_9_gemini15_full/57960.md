drippiness:/ˈdrɪpɪnɪs/| n. |滴答声；湿漉漉的状态；过于伤感；过于感伤|The quality or state of being drippy; excessive wetness or moisture;  excessive sentimentality or emotionalism.  It can refer to the sound of dripping water, the feeling of something being excessively wet, or an overly sentimental or emotional manner.|例句：The drippiness of the leaky faucet drove me crazy. 漏水的龙头滴答滴答的声音把我逼疯了。

The drippiness of his farewell speech was embarrassing. 他告别演讲中过于感伤的语气令人尴尬。|近义词：dampness, sogginess, sentimentality, mawkishness|反义词：dryness, aridity, stoicism, indifference|
