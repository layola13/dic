self-opened: /ˌsɛlfˈoʊpənd/ | adj. | 自动开启的，自行打开的 |  Describes something that opens by itself, without external intervention.  It implies a mechanism or design that allows for automatic opening. | Example: The self-opened door startled her.  自动开启的门吓了她一跳。 The self-opened umbrella protected him from the sudden downpour. 自动开启的雨伞保护他免受突如其来的倾盆大雨。 | 近义词：automatic, self-acting, spontaneously opening | 反义词： manually opened, closed
