cleanses:/ˈklɛnzɪz/| vt.|清洗；净化；涤清|To make something clean; to purify something, often from impurities or negative influences.|例句：The ritual cleanses the body and soul.  这个仪式净化身心。|例句：She cleanses her face twice a day. 她每天洗脸两次。|近义词：purify, cleanse, purify, wash|反义词：dirty, pollute, contaminate|动词


USAGE->

The river cleanses itself naturally. = 这条河自然地进行自我净化。
She cleanses her skin with a gentle cleanser. = 她用温和的洁面乳清洁肌肤。
