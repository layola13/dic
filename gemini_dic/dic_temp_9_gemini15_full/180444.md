regardful:/rɪˈɡɑːrdfəl/| adj.| 体贴的；留意的；关心的 | Attentive and considerate; mindful. Showing consideration for the feelings of others. |例句：He was always regardful of other people's feelings. 他总是很体贴别人的感受。

例句：She showed a regardful attitude towards her colleagues' needs. 她对同事的需求表现出关心的态度。


近义词：considerate, thoughtful, mindful, attentive | 反义词：inconsiderate, thoughtless, careless, negligent


词源：源自regard (v.) + -ful (suffix)  regard本身来自古法语"regard," "regard,"  表示“注意，看”。"-ful"是形容词后缀，表示"充满…的"。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is regardful of his promises. = 他很重视自己的承诺。
She is regardful of the environment. = 她很关心环境。


一般过去时:
He was regardful of her feelings. = 他很体贴她的感受。
They were regardful of the dangers involved. = 他们很留意所涉及的危险。


现在完成时:
She has been regardful of her duties. = 她一直认真履行职责。
We have been regardful of your suggestions. = 我们一直很重视你的建议。


过去完成时:
He had been regardful of his health before the illness. = 他生病前一直很注意身体健康。
They had been regardful of the warnings. = 他们一直很重视警告。


将来时:
She will be regardful of her responsibilities. = 她将会认真负责。
He will be regardful of the time constraints. = 他将会注意时间限制。


虚拟语气:
If he were more regardful, he wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果他更细心的话，就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish she were more regardful of other people's opinions. = 我希望她能更重视别人的意见。


被动语态:
His feelings were not regardful by the company. = 公司没有重视他的感受。
The environment should be regardful by all. = 环境应该受到所有人的重视。


疑问句:
Is he regardful of the consequences? = 他考虑过后果吗？
Are they regardful of the rules? = 他们遵守规则吗？


否定句:
He is not regardful of his appearance. = 他不注意自己的外表。
She was not regardful of the consequences of her actions. = 她没有考虑到自己行为的后果。


PHRASE->
be regardful of = 注意；重视
show regard for = 表示尊重
have regard for = 顾及；重视
pay regard to = 注意；重视
with regard to = 关于；至于
