rightful:/ˈraɪtfl/| adj. | 正当的；合法的；应得的 |  Just and lawful; conforming to law or morality.  It refers to something that is morally or legally correct and belongs to someone. |例句：He is the rightful heir to the throne. 他是王位的合法继承人。


例句：She fought for her rightful place in the company. 她为她在公司中应得的地位而奋斗。


近义词：legitimate, legal, just, proper, due


反义词：illegitimate, unlawful, unjust, improper, undue


词性:形容词
