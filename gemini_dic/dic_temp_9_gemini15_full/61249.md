emanators:/ˈɛməneɪtərz/| n. |散发者，发射器|Something or someone that emits or sends out something, especially a smell, gas, or radiation.  Examples include radioactive materials, fragrant flowers, or even people who project a certain aura.|The old pipes were emanators of a foul odor.  那些旧管道散发着恶臭。|The radioactive materials were powerful emanators.  那些放射性物质是强大的发射器。|近义词：emitter, radiator, source|反义词：absorber
