mesolite:/ˈmɛzoʊlaɪt/| n. | 中沸石 | A fibrous mineral, a variety of natrolite, occurring in radiating aggregates and often forming delicate, needle-like crystals. It is a hydrous sodium calcium aluminum silicate.  | Example: The sample contained beautiful, radiating aggregates of mesolite, a testament to the hydrothermal activity in the region.  The mesolite specimens displayed stunning needle-like crystals. | natrolite, scolecite |  (no direct antonym) | 名词
