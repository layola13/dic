regularised:/ˈrɛɡjʊləraɪzd/ | adj. | 正规化的；规范化的 |  To make something conform to rules or standards; to make something more regular or systematic.  It implies a process of making something more consistent and predictable. | Example: The company regularised its accounting procedures after the audit. 公司在审计之后规范了其会计程序。The government regularised the informal settlements by providing them with basic services. 政府通过提供基本服务使非正式定居点规范化。|近义词：standardized, normalized, formalized | 反义词：irregular, haphazard, chaotic |  adj.
