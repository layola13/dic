entomophilous:/ˌɛntəˈmɒfɪləs/| adj. |虫媒的；昆虫传粉的|Insect-pollinated; relating to or denoting pollination by insects.|Many flowering plants are entomophilous, relying on insects for the transfer of pollen.|近义词：insect-pollinated|反义词：anemophilous, hydrophilous
