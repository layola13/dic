injector:/ɪnˈdʒɛktər/ | n. |喷射器，注射器 |A device that injects a fluid, such as fuel or medicine, under pressure.  |例句：The fuel injector delivers fuel to the engine.  燃油喷射器将燃油输送到发动机。

例句：The doctor used an injector to administer the vaccine. 医生用注射器注射疫苗。|近义词：syringe, nozzle |反义词：extractor
