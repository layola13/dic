chilopod:/ˈkɪləpɒd/| n. |蜈蚣，少足纲动物|A chilopod is any member of the class Chilopoda, a group of elongated, segmented invertebrates characterized by a pair of legs on each segment and a pair of venomous forcipules (modified legs) on the head.  They are carnivorous and primarily nocturnal. | Example:  The centipede, a type of chilopod, scuttled across the floor. 这只蜈蚣，一种少足纲动物，在楼板上快速爬行。|近义词：centipede (though not all chilopods are centipedes)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with other invertebrate groups like insects or arachnids.
