entomostraca: /ˌɛntəʊˈmɒstrəkə/ | n. | 介形亚纲动物 |  Crustaceans of the subclass Entomostraca, characterized by their small size and often having a bivalve carapace.  They encompass a diverse group of mostly small crustaceans, including water fleas, copepods, and ostracods, playing crucial roles in aquatic ecosystems. | Example:  Many species of entomotraca are important food sources for fish.  许多介形亚纲动物是鱼类的重要食物来源。 | 近义词:  small crustaceans, microcrustaceans | 反义词:  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with larger crustaceans like crabs or lobsters)  | 词性: 名词
