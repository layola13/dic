callused:/ˈkɔːlʌst/ | adj. |长茧的；冷漠的；无情的 | Having skin thickened and hardened through constant pressure or friction; showing or having a lack of feeling and sympathy; emotionally hardened. |例句：His hands were callused from years of manual labor.  他的双手因多年的体力劳动而长满老茧。|例句：She had a callused heart after years of betrayal.  多年背叛之后，她变得冷酷无情。|近义词：hardened, toughened, insensitive, unfeeling |反义词：soft, tender, sensitive, compassionate
