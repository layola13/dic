forded:/ˈfɔːrdɪd/| v.|涉水而过；强行通过；克服（困难）|To wade across (a river or other body of water). To force one's way through or over (an obstacle).|例句：The pioneers forded the river on horseback.  拓荒者骑马涉水过河。
They forded the difficult terrain. 他们强行通过了艰难的地形。|近义词：wade, traverse, overcome |反义词：bypass, avoid
