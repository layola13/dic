existential:/ˌɛɡzɪˈstɛnʃəl/| adj. |存在主义的；存在论的；与存在有关的 | Relating to existence; concerned with existence.  Existential questions deal with fundamental issues of life, death, meaning, and freedom.  |例句：The novel explores existential themes of alienation and despair. 这部小说探讨了疏离和绝望的存在主义主题。
例句：His existential crisis led him to question his purpose in life. 他的存在主义危机使他开始质疑自己的人生目标。


近义词：ontological, phenomenological | 反义词：nonexistent, unreal

词源:
