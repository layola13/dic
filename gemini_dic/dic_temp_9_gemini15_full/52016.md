diagrams:/ˈdaɪəɡræmz/| n. |图表；图解|Visual representations of information, often using lines, symbols, and text to illustrate relationships or processes.  Diagrams can simplify complex ideas and make them easier to understand.|The engineer used diagrams to explain the design of the bridge. 工程师用图表解释了桥梁的设计。  The textbook contained many helpful diagrams to illustrate the concepts. 这本教科书包含许多有用的图表来说明这些概念。|charts, illustrations, schematics|text, descriptions|名词
