pseudo-japanese:/ˈsuːdoʊˌdʒæpəˈniːz/|/ˈsjuːdəʊˌdʒæpənˈiːz/| adj.|伪日式；貌似日式的|Apparently Japanese in style or appearance, but not genuinely so.  It often refers to something that imitates or appropriates aspects of Japanese culture superficially, without a deep understanding or respect for its origins.|例句：The restaurant's decor was pseudo-Japanese, a mishmash of cherry blossoms and anime characters. 这家餐馆的装饰是伪日式的，樱花和动漫人物混杂在一起。|例句：Her clothing style, while inspired by Japanese fashion, was ultimately described as pseudo-Japanese due to its lack of authenticity.|她的服装风格虽然受到了日本时尚的启发，但由于缺乏真实性，最终被形容为伪日式的。|近义词：faux-Japanese, imitation Japanese, Japanesque (if aesthetically pleasing)|反义词：authentically Japanese, genuine Japanese|adj.
