dingiest:/ˈdɪndʒiɪst/| adj.|最肮脏的，最污秽的|Most dirty or grimy; extremely unclean.  The word implies a long-standing, ingrained dirtiness.  It suggests a lack of cleanliness that is unpleasant and perhaps even repulsive.|例句：The dingiest alley in the city was where he found his lost cat.  这座城市最肮脏的小巷是他找到他丢失的猫的地方。

The dingiest room in the house was the basement.  房子里最脏的房间是地下室。


近义词：filthiest, dirtiest, grimiest, muckiest|反义词：cleanest, purest, spotless
