elviate: /ˈɛlvieɪt/ |  v. | 使轻松；减轻；缓和 | To make something less severe, serious, or painful.  |The doctor prescribed medication to elviate her pain.  医生开了药来减轻她的疼痛。| alleviate, mitigate, relieve | aggravate, worsen | 动词
