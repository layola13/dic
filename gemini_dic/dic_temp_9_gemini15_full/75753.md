fulminant:/ˈfʊlmɪnənt/| adj.|暴发性的；迅雷不及掩耳的；剧烈的|characterized by sudden onset and rapid progress.  It often describes a disease or symptom that develops quickly and severely.|例句：The patient presented with a fulminant case of hepatitis.  病人出现了暴发性肝炎。|例句：The fulminant onset of the storm caught everyone by surprise.  风暴的突然爆发让所有人都措手不及。|近义词：rapid, sudden, acute, explosive|反义词：gradual, slow, chronic
