recentrifuges:/ˌriːˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒɪz/| n. |再离心机；再次离心处理|A recentrifuge is a device used to further separate components of a substance after an initial centrifugation process. It's often used in laboratories for more precise separation of materials with similar densities.  |例句：The researchers used a recentrifuge to isolate the rare protein from the sample. 研究人员使用再离心机从样品中分离出稀有蛋白质。

The laboratory technician performed a recentrifugation to obtain a purer sample. 实验室技术员进行了再离心处理以获得更纯净的样品。|近义词：ultracentrifuge, centrifuge (for the initial process)|反义词：None specifically;  the antonym would relate to the opposite of separation, such as mixing or combining. |名词
