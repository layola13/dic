planetoid:/ˈplænɪtɔɪd/| n. |小行星，类行星|A planetoid is a celestial body that is smaller than a planet but larger than an asteroid, orbiting a star.  It's often used interchangeably with "minor planet," but the term "planetoid" emphasizes the body's planetary-like characteristics. |Example:  Ceres, once considered an asteroid, is now classified as a dwarf planet, a type of planetoid.|近义词：minor planet, dwarf planet|反义词：planet, star
