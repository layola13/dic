densimeter:/dɛnˈsɪmɪtər/| n. |密度计|A densimeter is an instrument used to measure the density of liquids.  It typically works by measuring the buoyancy of the instrument in the liquid.  Different designs exist depending on the liquid's properties and the desired accuracy.|The technician used a densimeter to check the density of the engine oil.|hydrometer, areometer|dilatometer (measuring expansion, opposite effect)|名词
