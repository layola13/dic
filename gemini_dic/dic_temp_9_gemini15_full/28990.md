cartesian:/kɑːrˈtiʒən/|adj.|笛卡尔的；笛卡尔坐标系的；直角坐标系的|Relating to the philosopher and mathematician René Descartes or his ideas, especially his system of philosophy based on the principle of doubt and the assertion that the only thing one can be certain of is one's own existence ("I think, therefore I am").  Also relating to Cartesian coordinates, a system of plotting points in space using three mutually perpendicular axes.|The Cartesian coordinate system is essential in mathematics and physics.|近义词：rectangular (in the context of coordinates)|反义词：polar (in the context of coordinates)
