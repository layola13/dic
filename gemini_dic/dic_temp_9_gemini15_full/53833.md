disconsideration: /ˌdɪskənˌsɪdəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 不顾；漠视；忽视 | The act of disregarding or ignoring something.  It implies a lack of consideration or thoughtfulness.  This word is relatively rare and often replaced by synonyms. | Example:  The disconsideration shown by the company towards its employees' concerns led to a strike. 公司对员工担忧的漠视导致了罢工。 |近义词：neglect, disregard, oversight, inattention |反义词：consideration, attention, heed
