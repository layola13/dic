nobilitate:/ˌnoʊbɪˈleɪt/ | vt. |使贵族化；使高尚；提升…的地位| To ennoble; to raise to the rank of nobility; to make noble or illustrious.  |例句：The king nobilitated his loyal subjects. 国王封他的忠诚臣民为贵族。 |例句：His generous act nobilitated his character. 他的慷慨行为提升了他的品格。|近义词：ennoble, elevate, dignify |反义词：debase, degrade, demean |动词
