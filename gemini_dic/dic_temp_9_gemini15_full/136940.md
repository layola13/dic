nonspeaker:/ˈnɒnˌspiːkə(r)/| n. |非说话者，不说话的人|A nonspeaker is someone who does not speak, either because they cannot or choose not to. This term can refer to individuals with communication disorders, those who are silent by nature, or those who are temporarily unable to speak.  The context is crucial to understanding the precise meaning.|例句：The meeting was attended by several nonspeakers, who communicated through sign language.  会议上有几位不会说话的人参加，他们通过手语交流。|近义词：mute, silent, non-verbal communicator|反义词：speaker, talker
