saccharate:/ˈsækəreɪt/| n.|糖化物，糖酸盐|A salt or ester of a saccharic acid.  Saccharates are formed by the reaction of a saccharic acid with a base. They are often used as sweeteners or in food processing.|例句：Calcium saccharate is used as a food additive.  钙糖酸盐用作食品添加剂。|近义词：sugar salt,  sweetener|反义词：N/A
