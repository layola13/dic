populations:/ˌpɒpjʊˈleɪʃənz/ /ˌpɑːpjʊˈleɪʃənz/ | n. |人口（复数）|The populations of many cities are growing rapidly. |例句：The populations of many cities are growing rapidly.  许多城市的人口都在迅速增长。
The global populations are facing numerous challenges. 全球人口面临诸多挑战。|近义词：inhabitants, citizenry|反义词：depopulation

USAGE->

The population of this city increases every year. = 这个城市的人口每年都在增长。
The populations of these countries are diverse. = 这些国家的人口构成多样。

将来时:
