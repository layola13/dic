dancers:/ˈdænsərz/| n. |舞蹈演员，舞者 |People who perform dances, professionally or otherwise.  Dancers often train extensively to develop their skills and artistry. They may specialize in various dance styles, such as ballet, jazz, hip-hop, or contemporary dance. |例句：The dancers performed a graceful ballet. 舞蹈演员表演了一段优美的芭蕾舞。

The dancers' costumes were dazzling. 舞蹈演员的服装令人眼花缭乱。


近义词：performers, artists, choreographers (if they create their own dances) |反义词：spectators, audience |名词
