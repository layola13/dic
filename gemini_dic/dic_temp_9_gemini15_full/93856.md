immobilizer:/ɪˈməʊbɪlaɪzər/| n. |防盗装置，防盗器|A device that prevents a vehicle from being started without the correct key or code.  It's a security system designed to deter theft. |Example: The car has an immobilizer fitted as a standard security feature. 这辆车标配了防盗装置。


The car's immobilizer prevented thieves from driving it away.  这辆车的防盗装置阻止了小偷将其开走。|近义词：theft deterrent, anti-theft device |反义词：ignition system (without security features)
