revetment:/rɪˈvɛtmənt/ | n. |护岸工程；护坡；护面 |A revetment is a structure built to protect a slope or bank from erosion.  It is typically made of materials like stone, concrete, or vegetation.  |例句：The riverbank was stabilized with a strong stone revetment.  河岸用坚固的石质护岸工程加固了。

The engineers designed a gabion revetment to prevent further soil erosion. 工程师设计了一种格宾护岸工程来防止进一步的水土流失。


近义词：facing, retaining wall, seawall |反义词：erosion, degradation
