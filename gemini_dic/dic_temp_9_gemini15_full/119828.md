/ˈmaɪlɪdʒ/ | n. | 英里数，里程；（尤指汽车的）耗油量；效用，成效 |  Mileage refers to the total number of miles traveled by a vehicle, or the distance traveled in general. It can also refer to the fuel efficiency of a vehicle (miles per gallon) or the effectiveness or usefulness of something. |  例句：My car has high mileage.我的车里程数很高。The mileage on that car is over 100,000 miles. 那辆车的里程数超过100,000英里。| 近义词：distance, fuel economy, effectiveness | 反义词：ineffectiveness
