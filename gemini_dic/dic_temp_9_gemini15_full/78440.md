get-wise:/ɡɛtˈwaɪz/ | adj. |变得精明；变得聪明 | To become more aware, knowledgeable, or shrewd; to become wiser.  |例句：He quickly got wise to their scheme. 他很快识破了他们的诡计。  |近义词：become shrewd, wise up, catch on | 反义词：remain naive, stay ignorant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gets wise to the company's tricks. = 他识破了公司的伎俩。
She gets wise to her boyfriend's lies. = 她识破了男友的谎言。


一般过去时:
He got wise to their plan quickly. = 他很快识破了他们的计划。
She got wise to the scam before it was too late. = 在为时已晚之前，她识破了骗局。


现在完成时:
They have gotten wise to our strategy. = 他们已经识破了我们的策略。
I have gotten wise to his manipulative tactics. = 我已经识破了他的操纵手段。


过去完成时:
She had gotten wise to his lies before he confessed. = 在他坦白之前，她就识破了他的谎言。
He had gotten wise to the hidden agenda long ago. = 他很久以前就识破了隐藏的议程。


将来时:
They will get wise to the deception eventually. = 他们最终会识破这个骗局。
She will get wise to his true nature soon enough. = 她很快就会识破他的真实面目。


虚拟语气:
If I had gotten wise to his plan earlier, I could have stopped him. = 如果我早点识破他的计划，我就能阻止他了。
I wish I had gotten wise to the danger sooner. = 我希望我早点察觉到危险。


被动语态:  (The phrase "get wise" is rarely used passively.)


疑问句:
Did he get wise to the fraud? = 他识破了欺诈吗？
Will they get wise to our secret? = 他们会识破我们的秘密吗？


否定句:
He didn't get wise to their scheme. = 他没有识破他们的诡计。
She didn't get wise to his deception until it was too late. = 直到为时已晚，她才识破了他的欺骗。


PHRASE->
get wise to something = 识破某事
get wise to someone = 识破某人
get wise to the fact = 意识到事实
get wise to the game = 识破游戏规则
wise up = 变聪明
