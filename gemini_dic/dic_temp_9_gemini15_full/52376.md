dictum:/ˈdɪktəm/| n. |格言；声明；断言 |A dictum is a short statement that expresses a general truth or principle.  It's often a formal or authoritative statement.  |例句：His dictum was "Always be prepared."  他的格言是“时刻准备着”。
例句：The judge's dictum settled the matter.  法官的裁决解决了这个问题。|近义词：maxim, proverb, saying, aphorism |反义词：refutation, contradiction |词性：名词
