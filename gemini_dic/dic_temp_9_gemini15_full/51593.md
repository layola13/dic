devanagari:/dɛvənəˈɡɑːri/| n. |梵文书写文字|The Devanagari script is an abugida writing system used to write Sanskrit and many other modern Indian languages.  It is characterized by its distinctive cursive style and the use of a horizontal line above the letters. |例句：Many ancient Indian texts are written in the Devanagari script. 许多古代印度文本都是用梵文书写文字书写的。|例句：Learning Devanagari allows access to a rich literary tradition. 学习梵文书写文字可以接触到丰富的文学传统。|近义词：Sanskrit script |反义词：Latin alphabet, Cyrillic alphabet |名词
