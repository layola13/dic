carter:/ˈkɑːrtər/ | n. | 卡特；马车夫；搬运工 | A carter is a person who transports goods using a cart or wagon.  The word can also refer to a person who makes or repairs carts. | Example: The carter delivered the goods to the market.  这个马车夫把货物送到了市场。 He was a skilled carter, known for his strong horses and sturdy cart. 他是一位熟练的马车夫，以其强壮的马和结实的马车而闻名。|近义词：driver, carrier, transporter | 反义词：recipient, receiver | 名词
