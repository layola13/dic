overcompress:/ˌoʊvər kəmˈprɛs/ | vt. |过度压缩|To compress something beyond its optimal or safe level, often resulting in a loss of quality or data.  This often applies to digital data like images or audio files, but can also refer to physical compression.| 例句：Overcompressing the image resulted in significant loss of detail.  过度压缩图像导致细节大量丢失。

例句：The engineer warned against overcompressing the gas cylinders. 工程师警告不要过度压缩气瓶。


近义词：overburden, overpack, excessively compress

反义词：undercompress, decompress


词性：动词
