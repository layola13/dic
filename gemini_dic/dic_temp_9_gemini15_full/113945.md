magazines:/ˌmæɡəˈziːnz/ | n. |杂志 |A periodical publication containing articles, stories, photographs, etc., often on a particular subject or for a particular readership.  | I subscribe to several magazines on gardening. 我订阅了几本关于园艺的杂志。  | periodicals, journals | newspapers, books | n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reads magazines every week. 他每周都读杂志。
She buys fashion magazines regularly. 她定期购买时尚杂志。

一般过去时:
I read a magazine on the plane yesterday. 我昨天在飞机上读了一本杂志。
They bought several magazines at the bookstore. 他们在书店买了数本杂志。

现在完成时:
I have read this magazine already. 我已经读过这本杂志了。
She has subscribed to that magazine for five years. 她已经订阅那本杂志五年了。

过去完成时:
I had already read the magazine before he asked me about it. 在他问我关于这本杂志之前，我已经读过了。
She had finished reading all the magazines before the trip. 旅行前她已经读完了所有的杂志。

将来时:
I will buy a new magazine tomorrow. 我明天会买一本新杂志。
He will read the magazines after dinner. 他晚饭后会读杂志。

If I had more time, I would read more magazines. 如果我有更多时间，我会读更多杂志。
I wish I had time to read all those magazines. 我希望我有时间读完所有那些杂志。

The magazines are delivered every month. 杂志每月送达。
