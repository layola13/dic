price-tag: /ˈpraɪs tæɡ/ | n. |价格标签；代价 | A small tag attached to an item showing its price.  It can also refer to the cost or consequences of something. | 例句：The price tag on that dress was shockingly high. 那件裙子的价格标签高的令人吃惊。

The project's price tag exceeded our budget.  这个项目的代价超过了我们的预算。| 近义词：label, sticker, cost, expense | 反义词：free, priceless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price tag shows the cost of the item. = 价格标签显示商品的成本。
This product has a high price tag. = 这个产品价格很高。
