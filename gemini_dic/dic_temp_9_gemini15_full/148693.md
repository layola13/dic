overtipping:/ˌoʊvərˈtɪpɪŋ/| n.|给小费过多|The act of giving excessively large tips.  It often implies a gratuity that is significantly more than customary or expected.|例句：Overtipping is a common problem in tourist areas.  在旅游区，给小费过多是一个常见的问题。|例句：He felt embarrassed by his overtipping of the waiter. 他因为给服务员小费过多而感到尴尬。|近义词：excessive tipping, generous tipping (in a negative context)|反义词：undertipping, stinginess|名词

The word "overtipping" is a compound word formed by combining the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") with the noun "tipping" (the act of giving a tip).
