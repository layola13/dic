hawkers:/ˈhɔːkəz/ | n. |小贩，叫卖者 | A hawker is a person who sells goods, especially food, in a public place, often by shouting or calling out. They usually carry their goods with them and may not have a fixed place of business.  |The street hawkers sold delicious snacks. 街头小贩卖着美味的小吃。 | vendor, peddler, street seller |  (无明显反义词) | 名词
