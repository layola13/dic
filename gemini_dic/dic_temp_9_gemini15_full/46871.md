dares:/deərz/ | v. | 敢，胆敢；挑战 | To have the courage to do something, especially something that is risky or difficult; to challenge someone to do something. | 例句：He dares to speak truth to power. 他敢于向当权者说实话。  He dares me to jump off the roof. 他挑战我从屋顶跳下去。 | 近义词：challenges, defies, provokes | 反义词：fears, hesitates


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dares to climb the mountain. = 他敢于攀登这座山。
She dares to express her opinion. = 她敢于表达自己的意见。


一般过去时:
He dared to speak his mind. = 他敢于说出自己的想法。
They dared each other to swim in the cold water. = 他们互相挑战在冰冷的水中游泳。


现在完成时:
She has dared to challenge the status quo. = 她敢于挑战现状。
He has never dared to ask her out. = 他从未敢约她出去。


过去完成时:
He had dared to defy the rules before. = 他以前就敢于违反规定。
She had never dared to imagine such a thing. = 她从未敢想象这样的事情。


将来时:
I will dare to ask him for a raise. = 我将敢于向他要求加薪。
They will dare to face their fears. = 他们将敢于面对他们的恐惧。


虚拟语气:
I wish I dared to tell him the truth. = 我希望我敢告诉他真相。
If he dared to disobey, he would face punishment. = 如果他敢于不服从，他将面临惩罚。


被动语态:
He was dared to jump over the fence. = 他被别人怂恿跳过栅栏。
The plan was dared to be tried.  = 人们敢于尝试这个计划。


疑问句:
Do you dare to try it? = 你敢尝试吗？
Dare he speak his mind? = 他敢说出自己的想法吗？


否定句:
I don't dare to go near him. = 我不敢靠近他。
She didn't dare to tell her parents. = 她不敢告诉她父母。


PHRASE->
dare to do sth. = 敢做某事
dare say = 我想，大概
I dare you to... = 我敢你...
how dare you! = 你好大的胆子！
not dare to = 不敢
