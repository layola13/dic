right-thinker: /ˈraɪt θɪŋkər/ | n. | 正确思考者，思想正确者 | A person who adheres to or promotes officially approved ideology or beliefs, often in a rigid or unquestioning manner.  This term often carries a negative connotation, implying conformity and a lack of critical thinking.  |  例句：He was branded a right-thinker for blindly supporting the government's policies. 他因为盲目支持政府政策而被贴上“思想正确者”的标签。


例句：The regime suppressed any dissent, rewarding only the right-thinkers.  这个政权压制任何异见，只奖励那些思想正确的人。


近义词：conformist, ideologue, true believer, yes-man


反义词：dissenter, heretic, nonconformist, critical thinker


词性：名词
