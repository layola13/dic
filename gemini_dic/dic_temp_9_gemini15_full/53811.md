disconformably:/ˌdɪskənˈfɔːrməbli/ | adv. | 不整合地，不协调地 | In geology, it describes a surface separating two rock strata where the upper layer lies on an eroded or otherwise uneven surface of the lower layer, indicating a significant time gap in deposition.  It implies a lack of conformity or continuity between the strata. | Example: The younger sedimentary rocks rested disconformably upon the eroded surface of the older metamorphic rocks.  年轻的沉积岩不整合地覆盖在被侵蚀的老变质岩表面上。|近义词：incongruously, discordantly | 反义词：conformably
