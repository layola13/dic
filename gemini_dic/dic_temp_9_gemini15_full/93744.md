immature:/ˌɪmɪˈtʃʊr/| adj.|不成熟的，幼稚的|lacking the mental, emotional, or social maturity considered appropriate for one's age.|The boy's immature behavior was a source of concern for his parents.  这个男孩的不成熟行为让他的父母很担心。|He acted in an immature way during the argument. 他在争论中表现得很幼稚。|mature, developed|mature, adult, sophisticated


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is immature in his thinking. 他在思维上还不成熟。
She is often immature in her responses to criticism. 她对批评的回应往往很幼稚。


一般过去时:
He was immature when he was younger. 他年轻的时候很幼稚。
Her immature reaction surprised everyone. 她的幼稚反应让每个人都感到惊讶。


现在完成时:
He has remained immature throughout his life. 他一生都保持着幼稚的状态。
She has shown herself to be immature in handling this situation.  她在处理这件事上表现得非常不成熟。


过去完成时:
He had been immature in his decision-making process. 他在做决定的时候一直很幼稚。
They had been immature in their approach to the problem. 他们解决问题的方法一直很幼稚。


将来时:
He will likely remain immature unless he changes his ways. 除非他改变自己的方式，否则他可能会一直幼稚下去。
She will probably become more mature with age. 她可能会随着年龄的增长而变得更加成熟。


虚拟语气:
If he were more mature, he wouldn't have made that mistake. 如果他更成熟一些，就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish she were more mature in her dealings with others. 我希望她在与他人交往时能更成熟一些。


被动语态:
His behavior was considered immature by his peers. 他的行为被同龄人认为是不成熟的。
The project was criticized for its immature design.  该项目因其不成熟的设计而受到批评。


疑问句:
Is his behavior immature? 他的行为幼稚吗？
Are her responses immature? 她的回应幼稚吗？


否定句:
His actions were not immature; they were simply misguided. 他的行为并不幼稚，只是方向错了。
Her approach to the problem was not immature; it was innovative. 她解决问题的方法并不幼稚；它是创新的。
