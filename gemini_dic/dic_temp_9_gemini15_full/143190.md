outcompetes:/ˌaʊt kəmˈpiːts/ | vt. |胜过；竞争获胜| To surpass or defeat in competition.  This verb implies a superior competitive ability resulting in victory.|例句：Our new product outcompetes all others on the market. 我们的新产品胜过市场上的所有其他产品。
Our company outcompetes its rivals by offering superior customer service.  我们的公司通过提供卓越的客户服务来胜过竞争对手。|近义词：surpass, outstrip, outperform, excel |反义词：lose to, be defeated by
