sizeableness:/ˈsaɪzəblənəs/| n. |大小；规模；显著性 |The sizeableness of the task overwhelmed him.  这项任务的规模令他难以承受。|近义词：magnitude, extent, significance |反义词：insignificance, triviality


英文解析：Sizeableness refers to the quality or state of being sizable; the extent or importance of something, often in terms of size, amount, or impact.  It implies a noticeable or considerable degree.


例句：

The sizeableness of the donation surprised everyone.  捐款的数额让每个人都感到惊讶。

The sizeableness of the problem requires immediate attention.  问题的严重性需要立即关注。


词源：

Derived from the adjective "sizable," which itself comes from "size" + "-able" (suffix indicating capability or possibility).  The addition of "-ness" creates a noun form indicating the quality or state of being sizable.
