forfeited:/ˈfɔːrfɪtɪd/| v. |丧失；被没收；放弃 | To lose or be deprived of (something) as a penalty for wrongdoing or failure to fulfill an obligation.  It implies a loss due to a breach of rules or conditions. |例句：He forfeited his right to the inheritance by failing to meet the conditions of the will. 他因为没有满足遗嘱的条件而丧失了继承权。

例句：She forfeited her bail by failing to appear in court.  她因为没有出庭而被没收保释金。


近义词：lose, surrender, relinquish, sacrifice |反义词：gain, acquire, retain, keep
