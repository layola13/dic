guillotined:/ˈɡɪlətɪnd/| vt.|断头台处死；突然终止|To behead (someone) with a guillotine.  Also, to abruptly end or terminate something.|例句：The condemned man was guillotined at dawn.  = 那个被判刑的人在黎明时被送上了断头台。 The project was guillotined due to lack of funding. =  由于缺乏资金，该项目被突然终止。|近义词：behead, execute, terminate, end, abolish|反义词：start, begin, initiate, preserve
