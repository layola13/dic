reliquidations:/ˌriːlɪkwɪˈdeɪʃənz/| n. |再清算；再次变现|The reliquidations of assets were necessary to cover the company's debts.  再清算资产对于弥补公司的债务是必要的。|近义词：revaluation, reassessment, liquidation |反义词：accumulation, accretion

The company reliquidates its assets annually. = 公司每年都会对资产进行再清算。
Reliquidations are a common practice in volatile markets. = 在动荡的市场中，再清算是一种常见的做法。
