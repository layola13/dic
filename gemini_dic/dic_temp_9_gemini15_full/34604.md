cleavelandite:/ˌkliːv.ləndˈaɪt/| n. |透闪石-钠长石|A variety of albite, a sodium feldspar mineral, that displays a pearly to subvitreous luster and often exhibits a characteristic lamellar structure.  It's named after its discoverer, Parker Cleaveland.|The cleavelandite in the specimen showed a beautiful pearly luster.  这块标本中的透闪石-钠长石展现出美丽的珍珠光泽。|近义词：albite (钠长石)|反义词：无
