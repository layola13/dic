downpouring:/ˈdaʊnˌpɔːrɪŋ/ | adj. |倾盆大雨的，暴雨的 |  Describes heavy rain falling rapidly and intensely.  |例句：The downpouring rain made it impossible to go outside.  倾盆大雨让我们无法出门。
例句：We were caught in a downpouring rain on our way home. 回家的路上我们遇到了倾盆大雨。|近义词：torrential, heavy, intense |反义词：drizzling, light


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain is downpouring. = 雨正在倾盆而下。
It's downpouring cats and dogs. =  雨下得很大(谚语)。

一般过去时:
It downpoured all night. =  雨整夜下个不停。
The rain downpoured heavily yesterday. = 昨天雨下得很大。

现在完成时:
It has been downpouring for hours. = 雨已经下了好几个小时了。
It has downpoured since morning. = 从早上开始就一直下大雨。

过去完成时:
It had been downpouring before we left. = 我们离开之前一直在下大雨。
The ground was soaked because it had downpoured all day. = 地面湿透了，因为一整天都在下大雨。

将来时:
It will downpour tomorrow. = 明天将会下大雨。
They predict it will downpour later this evening. = 他们预测今天晚上晚些时候会下大雨。

虚拟语气:
If it downpoured tomorrow, we would cancel the picnic. = 如果明天下大雨，我们会取消野餐。
I wish it hadn't downpoured during the game. = 我真希望比赛期间没有下大雨。

被动语态:
(Downpouring is rarely used in the passive voice.  A sentence like "The street was downpoured on" is ungrammatical.)

疑问句:
Is it downpouring outside? = 外面在下大雨吗？
Was it downpouring when you left? = 你离开的时候在下大雨吗？

否定句:
It wasn't downpouring when we arrived. = 我们到达的时候没有下大雨。
It doesn't usually downpour this heavily here. = 这儿通常不会下这么大的雨。
