half-profiles:/ˈhæf ˈproʊfaɪlz/| n.|半侧面像，半剖面图|A half-profile is a portrait or image showing a subject's face at a three-quarter view, rather than a full face or profile.  It's often used in art and design to create a more dynamic and engaging composition.|例句：The artist captured the subject's essence in a striking half-profile portrait.  艺术家在一幅引人注目的半侧面肖像中捕捉到了主题的精髓。|The painting featured a half-profile of a woman, her gaze directed slightly away from the viewer. 这幅画以一个女人的半侧面为特色，她的目光略微偏离了观看者。|近义词：three-quarter view, profile (partially)|反义词：full-face portrait, full profile|名词
