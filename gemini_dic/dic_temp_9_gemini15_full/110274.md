lifts:/lɪfts/| n. v.|电梯；举起；提升|An elevator or a device for raising or lowering something.|The elevator lifts people to the upper floors. 电梯把人们送往高层。  He lifts weights every day. 他每天举重。|raises, elevates, hoists|lowers, drops

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lifts the box. = 他举起箱子。
She lifts her hand. = 她举起手。

一般过去时:
They lifted the heavy furniture. = 他们搬起了沉重的家具。
He lifted the car. = 他把车抬了起来。

现在完成时:
I have lifted this many times. = 我已经举起过很多次了。
She has lifted the weight effortlessly. = 她毫不费力地举起了重物。

过去完成时:
He had lifted the package before I arrived. = 我到达之前他已经把包裹抬起来了。
They had lifted the stones for the new house construction. = 他们已经为新房建设搬运了石头。

将来时:
We will lift the anchor tomorrow. = 我们明天将起锚。
They will lift the ban on imports. = 他们将取消进口禁令。

虚拟语气:
If he lifted the weight, he would win the competition. = 如果他举起那个重量，他就会赢得比赛。
I wish I could lift that heavy stone. = 我真希望我能举起那块大石头。

被动语态:
The box was lifted by the machine. = 箱子被机器举了起来。
The ban will be lifted next week. = 禁令将于下周解除。

疑问句:
Does she lift weights regularly? = 她经常举重吗？
Can you lift this heavy suitcase? = 你能搬动这个沉重的行李箱吗？

否定句:
He didn't lift a finger to help. = 他没有伸出一根手指帮忙。
I couldn't lift the piano. = 我搬不动钢琴。
