mastership: /ˈmɑːstəʃɪp/ | n. | 掌握；精通；大师地位；船长职位 | Mastery or skill in a particular field; the position or authority of a master.  |  例句：His mastership of the violin was evident in his performance.  他的小提琴造诣在他的演奏中显而易见。


例句：The ship's mastership was transferred to a more experienced captain. 船长职位移交给一位更有经验的船长。


近义词：expertise, mastery, proficiency, command,  authority


反义词：incompetence, inexperience, ignorance


词性：名词
