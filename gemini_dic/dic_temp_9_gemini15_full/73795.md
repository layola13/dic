formats:/ˈfɔːrmæts/ | n. |格式，样式|The way in which something is arranged or presented.  It refers to the structure or organization of data, text, images, or other information.|例句：The document is available in several formats.  这个文档有几种格式。|例句：Please ensure the report follows the correct format. 请确保报告遵循正确的格式。|近义词：style, layout, design|反义词：chaos, disorder
