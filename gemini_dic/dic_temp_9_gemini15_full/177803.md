receivers:/rɪˈsiːvərz/| n. |接收器；收件人；受话器|A receiver is a device or person that receives something.  This could be a radio receiver picking up signals, a person receiving a letter, or a telephone receiver used to listen to a call. |例句：The radio receivers picked up a faint signal. 收音机接收到了微弱的信号。

The receivers of the stolen goods were arrested.  赃物的接收者被捕了。


近义词：recipient, acceptor, listener (depending on context)|反义词：sender, transmitter, dispatcher (depending on context)|名词
