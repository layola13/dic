nonmetro:/ˈnɑːnˌmɛtroʊ/| adj.|非都市的，非大都市的|Not relating to or characteristic of a large city or metropolitan area.  It often refers to areas outside of major cities, typically rural or suburban.|例句：The nonmetro counties experienced slower economic growth.  非都市郡县的经济增长较慢。

例句：Nonmetro areas often face challenges in attracting and retaining young professionals. 非都市地区在吸引和留住年轻专业人士方面往往面临挑战。


近义词：rural, suburban, exurban, country

反义词：metropolitan, urban

词性：adjective
