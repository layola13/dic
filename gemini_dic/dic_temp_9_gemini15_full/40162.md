contending:/kənˈtɛndɪŋ/| v. |争论；竞争；对抗 | To struggle to overcome a difficulty or opponent; to compete for something. |例句：The two teams are contending for the championship.  两个队正在争夺冠军。


例句：She contended with a difficult situation. 她与困境作斗争。


近义词：competing, struggling, vying, battling |反义词：agreeing, cooperating, yielding

词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He contends that the Earth is flat. = 他认为地球是平的。
They are contending for the top spot in the league. = 他们正在争夺联赛的榜首位置。

一般过去时:
She contended with her illness bravely. = 她勇敢地与疾病作斗争。
The two armies contended for control of the city. = 两支军队争夺对该城市的控制权。

现在完成时:
He has contended with many challenges in his life. = 他一生中克服了许多挑战。
They have contended for the championship title for years. = 多年来他们一直在争夺冠军头衔。

过去完成时:
She had contended with similar problems before. = 她以前曾遇到过类似的问题。
The team had contended for the cup several times before winning. = 这支球队在夺冠之前曾几次争夺过冠军杯。

将来时:
He will contend for the presidency next year. = 他明年将竞选总统。
They will contend with strong opposition in the election. = 他们在选举中将面临强烈的反对。

虚拟语气:
If he contended harder, he might have won. = 如果他更努力地争取，他也许会赢。
I wish I had contended more effectively in that argument. = 我希望我在那场争论中更有效地据理力争。

被动语态:
The issue is being contended by both sides. = 双方都在争论这个问题。
The championship was contended for fiercely. = 冠军头衔被激烈争夺。

疑问句:
Is he contending for the job? = 他在争夺这份工作吗？
Are they still contending with the technical difficulties? = 他们还在与技术难题作斗争吗？

否定句:
He didn't contend with the issue directly. = 他没有直接处理这个问题。
They didn't contend for the prize. = 他们没有争夺奖品。


PHRASE->
contend with = 与...竞争；与...作斗争
contend for = 争夺；竞争
contend against = 与...对抗
contend that = 主张；认为
contend over = 为...而争论
