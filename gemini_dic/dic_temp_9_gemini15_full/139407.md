observed:/əbˈzɜːvd/| /ɑːbˈzɜːrvd/| v. |观察到的；被观察到的；遵守；庆祝|To have noticed or perceived (something) and to have paid attention to it.  To comply with (a rule, law, etc.). To celebrate (a day or event).|例句：I observed a bird building a nest. 我观察到一只鸟在筑巢。 The students observed the rules of the classroom. 学生们遵守了教室的规章制度。 We observed Christmas with our family. 我们和家人一起庆祝了圣诞节。|近义词：noticed, watched, seen, noted, celebrated, kept, followed|反义词：ignored, neglected, disobeyed, violated
