punctuate:/ˈpʌŋktʃueɪt/| vt.| 加标点符号；强调；使…显得突出 | To add punctuation marks to (text).  To interrupt or emphasize (something) at intervals. |例句：Please punctuate this essay correctly. 请正确地给这篇论文加标点符号。

The lecturer punctuated his speech with anecdotes. 讲师用轶事来强调他的演讲。|近义词：interject, interrupt, emphasize |反义词： leave unmarked,  de-emphasize
