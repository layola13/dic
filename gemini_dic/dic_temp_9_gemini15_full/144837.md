over-loving: /ˌoʊvərˈlʌvɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度溺爱的，溺爱的 | Excessively loving; showing excessive affection, often to the point of being detrimental.  |  例句：Her over-loving parents shielded her from the realities of life.  她的过度溺爱的父母把她保护起来，让她远离了生活的现实。

例句：His over-loving nature sometimes made people uncomfortable. 他过度溺爱的性格有时会让人感到不舒服。


近义词：doting, overly affectionate, smothering, excessively caring | 反义词：neglectful, indifferent, uncaring | 词性：形容词
