gassed:/ɡæst/| v. adj.| 煤气中毒；兴奋；吸毒；|英文解析：To be gassed can refer to several things, primarily being overcome by a poisonous gas,  experiencing intense euphoria (often from drugs), or being subjected to tear gas. As an adjective, it describes someone affected by any of these situations. |例句：The soldiers were gassed during the attack.  士兵在袭击中吸入了毒气。 He felt gassed after the concert. 音乐会后他感觉很兴奋。|近义词：intoxicated, high, drugged, overcome|反义词：sober, clear-headed
