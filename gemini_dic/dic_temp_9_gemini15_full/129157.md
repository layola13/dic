no-noes:/ˈnoʊnoʊz/| n. |禁忌，不允许的事|Things that are unacceptable or forbidden.  No-noes are typically informal and refer to actions or behaviors considered inappropriate or taboo within a specific context.  They are often used in a playful or lighthearted manner.|

Examples:
Wearing white after Labor Day is a no-no in some circles.  在某些圈子里，劳动节后穿白色是禁忌。

Using your phone at the dinner table is a major no-no in our family. 在我们家，吃饭时使用手机是大忌。


Near synonyms: taboo, faux pas, impropriety, forbidden, unacceptable

Antonyms: acceptable, permissible, allowed
