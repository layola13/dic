raveler:/ˈrævələr/| n. |旅行者，流浪者 |A traveler is someone who is traveling, especially on a long journey.  |Example: The weary traveler finally reached his destination.  疲惫的旅行者终于到达了他的目的地。|近义词：traveler, tourist, voyager |反义词：homebody, stay-at-home

词源:
The word "raveler" derives from the verb "to travel," ultimately from the Old French word "travailler," meaning "to work" or "to toil."  The sense of "to journey" developed from the idea of working one's way across a distance.  The "-er" suffix indicates an agent or doer of the action.
