firefish: /ˈfaɪərˌfɪʃ/ | n. | 火鱼 | A small, brightly colored fish, typically found in coral reefs, known for its striking appearance and often venomous spines.  Many species are in the family *Apogonidae*.| 例句：The firefish darted among the coral, its vibrant colors flashing.  火鱼在珊瑚丛中穿梭，鲜艳的色彩一闪一闪的。

The firefish is a popular aquarium fish, but its venomous spines require careful handling. 火鱼是一种受欢迎的水族箱鱼类，但其有毒的刺需要小心处理。


近义词：cardinalfish (some species),  | 反义词：无
