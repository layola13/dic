diaphragmatically:/ˌdaɪəˈfræɡmætɪkli/| adj. |隔膜地；用隔膜的|Relating to or involving the diaphragm.  The word describes actions or processes that utilize or affect the diaphragm, a major muscle involved in breathing.  |例句：He breathed diaphragmatically, deeply inhaling and exhaling. 他用隔膜呼吸，深深地吸气和呼气。

例句：The singer's diaphragmatic control allowed for powerful and sustained notes.  这位歌手的隔膜控制使他能发出有力而持久的音符。


近义词：diaphragmatic (adj.)


反义词：None directly opposite, but words describing shallow breathing could be considered contextual opposites.
