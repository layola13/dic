ghostwrites:/ˈɡoʊstˌraɪts/ | vt. |代笔；捉刀；幕后代写 | To write something, such as a book or article, for someone else, who is then credited as the author.  | Example: She ghostwrites biographies for several famous actors. 她为几位著名演员代写自传。 | 近义词: write for, author for | 反义词:  self-write, openly write


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ghostwrites articles for a living. = 他靠代写文章为生。
She ghostwrites novels for a popular author. = 她为一位畅销书作家代写小说。

一般过去时:
The celebrity ghostwrote his autobiography last year. = 这位名人在去年代写了他的自传。
They ghostwrote the politician's speech. = 他们为这位政客代写了演讲稿。

现在完成时:
He has ghostwritten over fifty books in his career. = 他职业生涯中已经代写了五十多本书。
She has ghostwritten for numerous high-profile clients. = 她已经为许多知名客户代笔。

过去完成时:
Before the deadline, he had ghostwritten three chapters of the book. = 在截止日期之前，他已经代写了这本书的三章。
The author had ghostwritten several articles before publishing her own novel. = 在出版她自己的小说之前，这位作者已经代笔撰写了几篇文章。

将来时:
She will ghostwrite a new memoir next year. = 她明年将代写一本新的回忆录。
He will ghostwrite the screenplay for the upcoming movie. = 他将为即将上映的电影代写剧本。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
