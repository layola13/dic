colorers:/ˈkʌlərərz/| n.|着色者，染色者|People who apply color, usually to fabrics or other materials.  They might be artists, textile workers, or others involved in coloring processes.|例句：The colorers carefully applied the dye to the silk fabric.  着色者小心地将染料涂抹在丝绸织物上。

例句：Experienced colorers can achieve subtle shades through blending different dyes. 经验丰富的着色者可以通过混合不同的染料来获得微妙的色调。


近义词：dyers, colorists, stainers|反义词：None specifically antonymous, but could consider "bleachers" (if removing color)|词性：名词
