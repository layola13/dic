fibreglass:/ˈfaɪbərɡlæs/| n. |玻璃纤维；玻璃钢|A composite material consisting of glass fibers embedded in a resin matrix.  It is strong, lightweight, and resistant to corrosion. |The boat hull is made of fibreglass. 这艘船的船体是用玻璃纤维制成的。|composite material, reinforced plastic|wood, metal|
