quickstep:/ˈkwɪkˌstɛp/ | n. |快步舞 | A lively ballroom dance characterized by quick, short steps.  It is a fast-paced and energetic dance typically performed in competitions and social settings. | Example:  She effortlessly executed a dazzling quickstep across the dance floor. 她轻松地在舞池中跳了一段令人眼花缭乱的快步舞。 |近义词：foxtrot (though slower), waltz (though smoother and slower) |反义词：slow dance, tango (though both can be fast-paced, they differ in style) |名词
