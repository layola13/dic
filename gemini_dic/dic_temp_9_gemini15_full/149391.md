pack's:/pæks/| n.|包裹；背包；一群；一组|The plural possessive form of "pack," indicating something belonging to or associated with a pack.|例句：The wolf pack's territory was vast. 狼群的领地很广阔。|近义词：group's, team's, bunch's|反义词: none
