rediscovered:/ˌriːdɪˈskʌvərd/ | v. |重新发现；再次发现 | To discover something again after it has been lost, forgotten, or unknown for a period of time.  |例句：The ancient city was rediscovered after being buried for centuries.  这座古城在被掩埋了几个世纪后被重新发现。

例句：Scientists rediscovered the lost manuscript in a dusty attic. 科学家在一个布满灰尘的阁楼里重新发现了丢失的手稿。


近义词：re-find, recover, unearth, bring to light | 反义词：lose, misplace
