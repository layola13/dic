It can also refer to someone who is closely involved in or well-informed about a particular activity or situation.|例句：Many ringsiders were impressed by the boxer's agility. 许多拳击场边观众都被拳击手的敏捷身手所折服。  He's a ringsider in the political world, always knowing what's happening behind the scenes. 他是政界的圈内人，总是知道幕后发生的事情。|近义词：spectator, observer, insider|反义词：outsider
