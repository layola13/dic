loomed:/luːmd/| v.| 隐约出现；迫近；显得巨大 | To appear impressively large or great.  To appear as a vague or indistinct form, especially a threatening one. To appear suddenly and impressively large. |例句：A dark cloud loomed on the horizon.  乌云笼罩着地平线。 The deadline loomed large. 最后期限迫在眉睫。|近义词：appeared, threatened, towered, impended |反义词：shrunk, diminished, receded


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mountain looms large in the distance. = 山在远处显得很大。
Danger looms over the city. = 危险笼罩着这座城市。

一般过去时:
A figure loomed out of the shadows. = 一个身影从阴影中出现。
Trouble loomed over our heads. = 麻烦临头了。

现在完成时:
The problem has loomed large in our minds for weeks. = 这个问题几周来一直困扰着我们。
The threat of war has loomed over the region for years. =  战争的威胁多年来一直笼罩着这个地区。

过去完成时:
The storm had loomed on the horizon all day. =  暴风雨一整天都隐约出现在地平线上。
After the accident, the possibility of legal action had loomed large. 事故之后，采取法律行动的可能性越来越大。

将来时:
A crisis will loom if we don't act now. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，危机将会出现。
More challenges will loom in the future. = 未来将面临更多挑战。

虚拟语气:
If the storm had loomed sooner, we would have stayed home. = 如果风暴早点出现，我们就待在家里了。
I wish that the consequences hadn't loomed so large in my mind.  我希望那些后果没有在我的脑海中显得如此巨大。

被动语态:
The building loomed over the small houses. = 这栋大楼高耸在那些小房子之上。(主动语态，被动语态表达不自然)
The threat of war was looming. = 战争的威胁迫在眉睫。(更自然的被动表达)


疑问句:
Does the future loom large in your mind? = 你是否对未来感到担忧？
Does any danger loom on the horizon? = 地平线上有什么危险吗？

否定句:
The threat didn't loom as large as we had feared. = 威胁并没有像我们担心的那样严重。
No immediate danger loomed. = 没有迫在眉睫的危险。


PHRASE->
loom large =显得重要；构成严重威胁
loom over = 笼罩；威胁
loom up = 出现；显现
loom in the distance = 远处隐约出现
loom on the horizon =  在地平线上出现


词源:
The word "loom" in this sense ultimately derives from Old English *lūman, meaning "to shine," "to gleam," or "to loom," referring to something shining brightly or appearing large and distinct.  The modern sense of "to appear large and threatening" developed from this earlier meaning over time.
