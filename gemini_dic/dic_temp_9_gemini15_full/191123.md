scaphoid:/ˈskeɪfɔɪd/| n. |舟状骨|A scaphoid bone is one of the eight carpal bones in the wrist. It's a small, somewhat boat-shaped bone located on the thumb side of the wrist.  It's crucial for wrist stability and movement.  Fractures of the scaphoid bone are common injuries, often resulting from a fall onto an outstretched hand.|The scaphoid bone is easily fractured.  = 舟状骨很容易骨折。|navicular|cuboid|
