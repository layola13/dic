delusion:/dɪˈluʒən/| n. |妄想；错觉；谬见 |A delusion is a false belief that is held despite strong evidence to the contrary.  It is often a symptom of a mental illness. |例句：He suffers from the delusion that he is wealthy. 他患有自以为富有的妄想症。

例句：Her delusion that she can fly led her to jump off the roof. 她相信自己会飞的错觉导致她从屋顶跳了下去。


近义词：fantasy, hallucination, illusion, misconception |反义词：reality, truth, fact |词性:名词


词源:  From Middle French *delusion, from Latin *deludere "to deceive, mock, delude", from de- "away, from" + ludere "to play".
