preentitle: /ˌpriːɪnˈtaɪtəl/ | v. | 预先给…称号；提前授予称号 | To give a title or right to something or someone in advance or before it is officially due.  |  The company preentitled the new software "Project Phoenix" before its official launch.  公司在正式发布之前就将新软件预先命名为“凤凰计划”。 |  assign, bestow, grant |  deny, revoke | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They preentitle the upcoming film "The Lost City". = 他们预先为即将上映的电影命名为“迷失之城”。
The professor preentitles his new research "Quantum Entanglement". = 教授预先为他的新研究命名为“量子纠缠”。

虚拟语气:

疑问句:
Has the company preentitled the new software yet? = 公司是否已经为新软件预先命名？
Will they preentitle the book before sending it to the publisher? = 他们会在送交出版商之前为这本书预先命名吗？


否定句:
They did not preentitle the presentation. = 他们没有为演示文稿预先命名。
The committee did not preentitle the initiative. = 委员会没有为该倡议预先命名。
