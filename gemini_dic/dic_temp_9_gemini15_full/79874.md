gnarly:/ˈnɑːrli/| adj.| 复杂的；极好的；令人毛骨悚然的；扭曲的 |  Describes something intricate, excellent, frightening, or twisted. It can be used ironically to mean "awesome" or sincerely to describe something unpleasant. | 例句：That's a gnarly wave! = 那是一个很刺激的海浪！(positive connotation)

That gnarly tree root tripped me. = 那根扭曲的树根绊倒了我。(negative connotation)


近义词：awesome, amazing, twisted, complex, intricate, frightening, scary| 反义词：simple, easy, smooth, straightforward
