photointerpreter:/ˈfoʊtoʊɪnˈtɜːrprətər/ | n. |照片判读员，图像解释员 | A person who examines photographs or other images (such as satellite imagery) to extract information and interpret its meaning.  Photointerpreters use their knowledge of geography, geology, and other relevant fields to analyze the images and draw conclusions. | Example: The military relies on skilled photointerpreters to analyze aerial reconnaissance images.  军事依赖熟练的照片判读员来分析空中侦察图像。

The photointerpreter carefully examined the satellite imagery to identify potential targets. 照片判读员仔细检查卫星图像以识别潜在目标。


近义词：image analyst, image interpreter, aerial photograph analyst
