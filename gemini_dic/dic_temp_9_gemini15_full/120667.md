misadaptation:/ˌmɪsˌædəpˈteɪʃən/| n.| 错适应；不良适应；适应不良 | The failure of an organism or species to adapt adequately to its environment, resulting in reduced fitness or survival.  This can be due to various factors, including rapid environmental change, genetic limitations, or flaws in the adaptation process itself. | Example: The rapid climate change led to the misadaptation of many species, resulting in population decline.  这种快速的的气候变化导致许多物种适应不良，导致种群数量下降。|近义词：maladaptation,  inadequate adaptation |反义词：adaptation,  successful adaptation
