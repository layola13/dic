conisor:/ˈkɒnɪsɔːr/|/kɑːnɪˈsɔːr/| n. |鉴赏家，行家|A connoisseur is a person who is knowledgeable and enthusiastic about a particular subject, especially art, food, or wine.|例句：He is a connoisseur of fine wines. 他是一位葡萄酒鉴赏家。|例句：She's a connoisseur of Impressionist painting. 她是一位印象派绘画的行家。|近义词：expert, specialist, aficionado |反义词：amateur, novice, ignoramus

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a connoisseur of art. = 他是一位艺术鉴赏家。
She is a connoisseur of French cuisine. = 她是法国美食的行家。

一般过去时:
He was a connoisseur of old maps. = 他以前是古地图鉴赏家。
She was a connoisseur of jazz music. = 她以前是爵士乐的行家。

现在完成时:
He has become a connoisseur of single malt scotches. = 他已经成为苏格兰单一麦芽威士忌的鉴赏家了。
She has been a connoisseur of classical music for many years. = 她多年来一直是古典音乐的鉴赏家。

过去完成时:
He had already become a connoisseur of wine before he turned thirty. = 他三十岁之前就已经成为葡萄酒鉴赏家了。
She had been a connoisseur of antiques for decades. = 她几十年来一直是古董鉴赏家。

将来时:
He will be a connoisseur of vintage cars one day. = 他将来有一天会成为老爷车的鉴赏家。
She will become a connoisseur of opera in due time. = 她最终会成为歌剧鉴赏家。

虚拟语气:
If he were a connoisseur of art, he would recognize that painting. = 如果他是艺术鉴赏家，他会认出那幅画。
If she were a connoisseur of tea, she would know the difference. = 如果她是茶叶鉴赏家，她会知道其中的区别。

被动语态:
He was considered a connoisseur of modern dance. = 他被认为是现代舞的鉴赏家。
She is often called a connoisseur of fine jewelry. = 她经常被称为高级珠宝的鉴赏家。

疑问句:
Is he a connoisseur of classical literature? = 他是古典文学鉴赏家吗？
Is she a connoisseur of cheese? = 她是奶酪鉴赏家吗？

否定句:
He is not a connoisseur of wine. = 他不是葡萄酒鉴赏家。
She is not a connoisseur of abstract art. = 她不是抽象艺术的鉴赏家。
