proatheism:/ˈproʊˌeɪθiɪzəm/| n. |公开支持无神论|The active promotion and advocacy of atheism.  It involves not just a lack of belief in God but also a positive assertion of atheism and a willingness to defend it publicly.|Example:  His proatheism was evident in his frequent debates with religious leaders.  他的公开支持无神论在他与宗教领袖的频繁辩论中显而易见。|近义词: atheism advocacy, antitheism|反义词: theism, religious belief|名词
