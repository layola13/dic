centimetre: /ˈsɛntɪmiːtə(r)/| n. |厘米|A unit of length equal to one hundredth of a metre.  It is a unit of length in the metric system.|The table is 120 centimetres long.|近义词：centimeter|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ruler measures 30 centimetres. = 这把尺子长30厘米。
This box is 15 centimetres wide. = 这个盒子宽15厘米。

一般过去时:
The plant grew 5 centimetres last week. = 上周植物长了5厘米。
The child's height was measured at 90 centimetres. = 孩子的身高测量为90厘米。

现在完成时:
The tree has grown 10 centimetres this year. = 这棵树今年长了10厘米。

虚拟语气:
