gazettes:/ɡəˈzɛts/| n.|官方公报；政府公报；杂志；期刊|An official publication containing announcements, laws, or other governmental information.  It can also refer to a newspaper or magazine, especially one focused on a particular field.|The government gazette announced the new tax laws. 政府公报宣布了新的税法。|The university gazette published research findings. 大学公报发表了研究成果。|journal, bulletin, periodical|newsletter, informal publication|名词

词源:  The word "gazette" ultimately derives from the Italian word "gazzetta," a small coin.  In Venice, a small handwritten news sheet was sold for this coin, and thus the name stuck.  The word then spread into other languages.
