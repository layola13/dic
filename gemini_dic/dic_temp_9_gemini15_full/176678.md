reacquaint:/ˌriːəˈkweɪnt/| vt.|使重新认识，使再熟悉|To make someone familiar again with something they have forgotten or are unfamiliar with.|例句：I need to reacquaint myself with the software before the presentation. 我需要在演示文稿之前重新熟悉一下这个软件。

例句：After years apart, they reacquainted themselves with each other's lives.  多年不见后，他们重新了解了彼此的生活。


近义词：refamiliarize, reintroduce


反义词：estrange, alienate


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reacquaints himself with the city every year. = 他每年都重新熟悉这座城市。
She reacquaints her children with their grandparents. = 她让她的孩子们重新认识他们的祖父母。


一般过去时:
They reacquainted themselves after a long separation. = 他们经过长时间的分离后重新熟悉了彼此。
He reacquainted himself with his old college friends. = 他重新认识了他以前的大学朋友们。


现在完成时:
I have reacquainted myself with my old hobby. = 我已经重新拾起了我的老爱好。
She has reacquainted herself with the local customs. = 她已经重新熟悉了当地的风俗习惯。


过去完成时:
They had reacquainted themselves with the project before the meeting. = 他们会议前已经重新熟悉了这个项目。
She had reacquainted herself with the material before the exam. = 她在考试前已经重新熟悉了材料。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
