preemploy:/ˌpriːɪmˈplɔɪ/| adj.| 预先雇用；提前雇佣 |relating to or involving the employment of someone before they officially start a job.  This often involves background checks, training, or other preparatory activities.| 例句：The company has a robust preemployment screening process. 这家公司有一个强大的招聘前筛选流程。  The preemployment training program helped new hires quickly integrate into the team.  招聘前的培训项目帮助新员工快速融入团队。 |近义词：pre-hire, beforehand employment|反义词：postemployment
