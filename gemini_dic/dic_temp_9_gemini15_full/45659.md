curtailing:/kɜːˈteɪlɪŋ/ | v. | 缩减；削减；限制 |  The act of making something shorter or less in extent.  It often implies a reduction in something that is considered excessive or undesirable. |

例句：

The government is curtailing spending on social programs. = 政府正在削减社会福利项目的开支。

The company curtailed its operations due to the economic downturn. = 由于经济衰退，公司缩减了业务。


近义词：reducing, shortening, limiting, restricting, abridging, cutting back on

反义词：extending, prolonging, expanding, increasing


词源:

From Middle English *curtaylien, from Anglo-French *curteler, from Old French curteler ("to shorten, dock (a tail)"), from curt ("short").  The -ing suffix indicates the present participle form, meaning "the act of curtailing".
