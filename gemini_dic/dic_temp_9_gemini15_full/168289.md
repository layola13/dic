pro-Mexicans:/ˌproʊˈmɛksɪkənz/| adj. n.|亲墨西哥的；支持墨西哥人的；墨西哥裔美国人（集合名词）|Supportive of Mexicans or Mexican Americans.  The term can be used to describe people who advocate for Mexican interests or those who identify with Mexican culture. However, the term is often used in a political context and can carry a strong positive or negative connotation depending on the speaker and the context.|例句：Some pro-Mexicans organized a protest against the new immigration laws. 一些亲墨西哥人士组织了抗议新移民法的示威活动。  The group consisted largely of pro-Mexicans who felt their heritage was under attack. 这个团体主要由那些感到自己的传统受到攻击的亲墨西哥人组成。|近义词：pro-Mexican, Mexican-American, Chicanos (depending on context), supporters of Mexico|反义词：anti-Mexican, anti-immigrant
