crustacean: /krʌˈsteɪʃən/ | n. | 甲壳类动物 | An invertebrate animal of the large phylum Arthropoda, having a segmented body, typically covered with a hard exoskeleton, and jointed limbs.  Crustaceans include crabs, lobsters, shrimps, and barnacles. | Example: Crabs, lobsters, and shrimps are all examples of crustaceans.  螃蟹、龙虾和虾都是甲壳类动物的例子。|近义词：shellfish, arthropod | 反义词：vertebrate
