collectives:/kəˈlɛktɪvz/ | n. |集体；集体名词 |A collective is a group of individuals considered as a single unit.  Collectives can refer to groups of people, animals, or things.  The term is also used in grammar to describe nouns that represent a group of things as a single unit (e.g., "family," "team").|例句：The family is a strong collective. 这个家庭是一个强大的集体。

The birds formed a tight collective to protect their young.  这些鸟类结成紧密的群体来保护它们的幼鸟。


近义词：groups, aggregates, assemblies, associations |反义词：individuals, units (in the sense of single entities) |词性：名词
