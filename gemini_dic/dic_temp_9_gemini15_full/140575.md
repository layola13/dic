oligochaeta:/ˌɒlɪɡəˈkiːtə/| n. |寡毛类，环节动物 |Oligochaeta is a class of annelid worms, characterized by their relatively small size and the lack of parapodia (lateral appendages). They are typically found in freshwater, marine, and terrestrial environments.  They play a crucial role in nutrient cycling and soil aeration. |Example:  Earthworms are a well-known example of oligochaetes.  蚯蚓是寡毛类的一个众所周知的例子。|近义词：annelid (more general term) |反义词：polychaeta (possessing many chaetae)
