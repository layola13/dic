poach:/poʊtʃ/| /pəʊtʃ/| vt. n.|偷猎；水煮（食物）|to illegally hunt or catch (animals, fish, etc.) on someone else's property or in a protected area; to cook (food, especially eggs) gently in almost boiling water. |例句：They were caught poaching deer on private land.他们被抓到在私人的土地上偷猎鹿。  The eggs were poached perfectly. 蛋煮得恰到好处。|近义词：hunt illegally, pilfer, pilfer, coddle|反义词：protect, conserve
