palaeography:/ˌpeɪliˈɒɡrəfi/|/ˌpeɪliˈɑːɡrəfi/| n. |古文字学|The study of ancient and historical handwriting.  It involves the analysis of writing systems and scripts to determine their age, origin, and authorship.  Palaeographers examine the physical characteristics of writing, including the style of letters, the materials used, and the tools employed, to decipher and interpret texts. |Example: The palaeographer carefully examined the faded manuscript, attempting to determine its date and origin.  =  这位古文字学家仔细检查了褪色的手稿，试图确定它的年代和来源。

The analysis of an ancient inscription requires expertise in palaeography. = 分析古代铭文需要古文字学的专业知识。


近义词：paleography, codicology (study of codices/manuscripts)


反义词：无


词性：名词
