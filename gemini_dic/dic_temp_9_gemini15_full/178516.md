recontracts:/ˌriːˈkɒntrækt/| v.|重新签订合同；再次签约 |To enter into a contract again; to renew or revise a contract.  |例句：The company recontracted with its suppliers for a lower price. 公司以较低的价格与供应商重新签订了合同。|例句：After negotiations, they recontracted the lease for another year. 经过协商，他们将租赁合同续签了一年。|近义词：renew, revise, renegotiate |反义词：terminate, cancel


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They recontract with their suppliers annually. = 他们每年都与供应商重新签订合同。
The company recontracts its insurance policy every year. = 公司每年都重新签订保险合同。

一般过去时:
We recontracted the project last month. = 我们上个月重新签订了该项目的合同。
The government recontracted the construction work after the initial bid was rejected. = 在最初的投标被拒绝后，政府重新签订了施工合同。

现在完成时:
They have recontracted the services for the next quarter. = 他们已经重新签订了下一季度的服务合同。
The firm has recontracted with a new marketing agency. = 该公司已经与一家新的营销机构重新签订了合同。

过去完成时:
They had recontracted the building lease before the previous one expired. = 在之前的租赁合同到期之前，他们已经重新签订了建筑租赁合同。
The company had recontracted with the vendor before the price increase took effect. = 在价格上涨生效之前，该公司已与供应商重新签订了合同。

将来时:
We will recontract the agreement next year. = 我们明年将重新签订协议。
The team will recontract the software license after the trial period. = 试用期结束后，团队将重新签订软件许可证。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would recontract the deal. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会重新签订协议。
I wish we had recontracted the services earlier. = 我希望我们早点重新签订服务合同。

被动语态:
The contract was recontracted under new terms. = 合同以新的条款重新签订。
The agreement will be recontracted by the end of the month. = 协议将在月底重新签订。

疑问句:
Will they recontract the project? = 他们会重新签订这个项目吗？
Have they recontracted with the same supplier? = 他们是否与同一家供应商重新签订了合同？

否定句:
They did not recontract the lease. = 他们没有续签租赁合同。
We will not recontract with that unreliable company. = 我们不会与那家不可靠的公司重新签订合同。
