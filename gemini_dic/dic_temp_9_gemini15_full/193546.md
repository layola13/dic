self-admiring:/ˌself ədˈmaɪərɪŋ/| adj. |自恋的，自我陶醉的 |Self-admiring refers to someone who has an excessive admiration for themselves and their own qualities or achievements.  They may be boastful, narcissistic, and preoccupied with their own image and accomplishments. |例句：He gave a self-admiring speech about his accomplishments. 他发表了一篇自我吹嘘成就的演讲。

例句：Her self-admiring behavior alienated many of her friends. 她自恋的行为疏远了她许多朋友。|近义词：narcissistic, vain, egotistical, self-centered |反义词：humble, modest, self-effacing
