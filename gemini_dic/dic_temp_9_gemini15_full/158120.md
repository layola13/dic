plate:/pleɪt/| n.|盘子，板块，镀层|A flat, usually round dish used for serving food.  A flat piece of something, often forming part of a larger structure. A thin layer of metal applied to a surface.|例句：She put the plate on the table. 她把盘子放在桌子上。
例句：The tectonic plates shift and cause earthquakes. 地壳板块移动导致地震。|近义词：dish, platter, tray|反义词：bowl (in some contexts)
