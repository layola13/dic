metamerically:/ˌmɛtəˈmɛrɪkli/| adv. | 偏分割地；同分异构地 |  Describes something that is arranged in a repeating segmental pattern, like the segments of an annelid worm or the repeating units of a polymer.  It implies a structural similarity between repeating units or segments, but not necessarily identicality.  | The body plan of many segmented worms is metamerically arranged. = 许多环节动物的身体结构是偏分割排列的。 The polymer chain was constructed metamerically. = 聚合物链是偏分割构建的。 | segmentally, repeatedly |  non-metamerically | 副词
