permittedly:/pərˈmɪtɪdli/ | adv. |许可地；允许地 | Adverb meaning "in a way that is permitted or allowed".  It indicates that something is done with permission or authorization. | Example: Permittedly, the use of the park after dark is restricted.  许可使用公园在晚上是有限制的。 |近义词：allowably, lawfully, legally |反义词：illegally, unlawfully, forbiddenly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb,  so it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It doesn't have tenses.  Its usage is solely about modifying a verb or clause to indicate that the action is permitted.

Example 1:  He permissably entered the building. 他被允许进入大楼。
Example 2: She permissably accessed the confidential files. 她被允许访问机密文件。


PHRASE->
There aren't common phrases specifically using "permittedly".  The word itself is relatively formal and less frequently used in everyday conversation compared to simpler alternatives like "allowedly" or phrases like "it's permitted to...".
