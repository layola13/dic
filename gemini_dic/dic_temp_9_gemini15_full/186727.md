rigid:/ˈrɪdʒɪd/| adj.|僵硬的；严格的；死板的；不易弯曲的|Characterized by stiffness, inflexibility, or unyieldingness; not easily bent or changed.  It can refer to physical objects, rules, or people's attitudes.|例句：The rigid structure of the building withstood the earthquake. 这座建筑物坚固的结构经受住了地震。|例句：He adhered to a rigid schedule, allowing no deviations. 他坚持严格的时间表，不容许任何偏差。|近义词：stiff, inflexible, inflexible, unbending, strict, firm|反义词：flexible, pliable, supple, lenient, lax
