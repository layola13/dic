overview:/ˈoʊvərˌvjuː/| n. |概述，概览，综述 |A general description or summary of a subject.  It provides a broad understanding without going into great detail. |例句：The professor gave a brief overview of the course before starting the first lesson. 教授在上第一课之前对课程做了简要概述。

例句：The report provides an overview of the company's financial performance. 报告概述了公司的财务业绩。


近义词：summary, synopsis, survey, general view, outline |反义词：detail, minutiae, specifics |词性:名词
