chromate:/ˈkroʊmeɪt/| n.|铬酸盐；铬酸酯|A salt or ester of chromic acid. Chromates are typically yellow or orange crystalline solids.  They are used in various industrial applications, including pigments, corrosion inhibitors, and leather tanning.|例句：Many chromates are brightly colored and used as pigments in paints. 许多铬酸盐颜色鲜艳，用作油漆颜料。|例句：The use of chromates as wood preservatives is declining due to their toxicity. 由于铬酸盐的毒性，其作为木材防腐剂的使用正在减少。|近义词：dichromate|反义词：无
