dowries:/ˈdaʊriːz/| n.|嫁妆；陪嫁物|Property or money brought by a bride to her husband on their marriage.|The bride's family gave a generous dowry, including a house and several acres of land.|近义词：bridewealth, dower|反义词：None


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Her family provides a substantial dowry. = 她家提供丰厚的嫁妆。
The dowry includes jewelry and valuable antiques. = 嫁妆包括珠宝和贵重的古董。
