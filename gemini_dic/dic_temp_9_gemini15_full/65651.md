evaporating: /ɪˈvæpəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 蒸发；消失 | The process of a liquid turning into a gas.  It can also refer to something gradually disappearing or fading away. | Example: The water in the puddle was evaporating quickly in the hot sun.  太阳下水洼里的水很快就蒸发了。 The tension in the room was slowly evaporating.  房间里的紧张气氛慢慢消散了。 | 近义词：vaporizing, disappearing, fading | 反义词：condensing, materializing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water is evaporating. = 水正在蒸发。
The evidence is evaporating. = 证据正在消失。


一般过去时:
The puddle evaporated quickly. = 水洼很快蒸发了。
His hopes evaporated. = 他的希望破灭了。


现在完成时:
The lake has evaporated significantly. = 湖水已经显著蒸发了。
Most of the snow has evaporated. = 大部分的雪已经融化蒸发了。


过去完成时:
The water had evaporated before we arrived. = 我们到达之前水已经蒸发了。
All the excitement had evaporated by the time the meeting ended. = 到会议结束时，所有的兴奋都消失了。


将来时:
The dew will evaporate as the sun rises. = 太阳升起后露水会蒸发。
His anger will evaporate eventually. = 他的怒气最终会消散。


虚拟语气:
If the liquid evaporated faster, the experiment would be finished sooner. = 如果液体蒸发得更快，实验就能更快完成。
If the problem evaporated, we would have no need to worry. = 如果问题消失了，我们就不用担心了。


被动语态:
The water is being evaporated by the heat. = 水正在被高温蒸发。
The mystery was slowly being evaporated by the police investigation. = 这个谜团正被警方的调查慢慢解开。


疑问句:
Is the water evaporating? = 水在蒸发吗？
Is the tension evaporating? = 紧张气氛在消散吗？


否定句:
The water isn't evaporating fast enough. = 水蒸发得不够快。
The problem isn't evaporating; it's getting worse. = 问题没有消失；它正在恶化。
