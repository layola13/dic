occluding: /əˈkluːdɪŋ/ | v. | 阻塞；遮蔽；闭塞 |  To block or obstruct (an opening, passage, etc.); to shut off from view. |

例句：

The tumor was occluding the artery. = 肿瘤阻塞了动脉。

The clouds were occluding the sun. = 云彩遮蔽了太阳。


近义词：blocking, obstructing, shutting off, closing, masking

反义词：opening, clearing, unblocking, exposing


词性：动词 (present participle of occlude)


USAGE->

The valve occludes the flow of blood. = 阀门阻塞了血流。
The building occludes the view of the mountain. = 这栋建筑物遮挡了山景。
