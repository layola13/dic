laf:/læf/| v.|大声笑，狂笑|to laugh loudly and heartily|例句：The children laughed loudly at the clown. 孩子们看着小丑大声笑了起来。|例句：She laughed so hard she cried. 她笑得都哭了。|近义词：chuckle, guffaw, roar|反义词：cry, weep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He laughs at my jokes. = 他嘲笑我的笑话。
They laugh every day. = 他们每天都笑。


一般过去时:
She laughed at the funny movie. = 她看着这部有趣的电影笑了。
We laughed until our stomachs hurt. = 我们笑到肚子疼。


现在完成时:
I have laughed so much today. = 我今天笑得太多了。
They have laughed at the same joke many times. = 他们多次嘲笑同一个笑话。


过去完成时:
He had laughed before he saw the punchline. = 他在看到妙语之前就已经笑了。
We had laughed heartily before the show started. = 演出开始前我们已经开怀大笑。


将来时:
They will laugh when they hear the news. = 当他们听到这个消息时，他们会笑。
I will laugh at your jokes. = 我会嘲笑你的笑话。


虚拟语气:
If I knew the joke, I would laugh. = 如果我知道这个笑话，我会笑的。
I wish I had laughed more when I was younger. = 我希望我年轻的时候能多笑一笑。


被动语态:
His jokes are laughed at by everyone. = 他的笑话被每个人都嘲笑。
The funny video was laughed at by millions. = 数百万人在观看这部搞笑的视频时笑了。


疑问句:
Why are you laughing? = 你为什么笑？
Do you laugh often? = 你经常笑吗？


否定句:
He didn't laugh at my joke. = 他没有嘲笑我的笑话。
They don't laugh easily. = 他们不容易笑。


PHRASE->
laugh at = 嘲笑
laugh out loud = 大笑
burst out laughing = 突然大笑起来
have a good laugh = 开怀大笑
laugh it off =一笑而过
