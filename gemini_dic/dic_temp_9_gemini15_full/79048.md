giveaways:/ˈɡɪvəweɪz/| n.|赠品，免费赠送；泄露，暴露； giveaway (比赛)|免费赠送活动，赠品活动|  Things given away free, especially as a promotion.  Also, something that reveals a secret or truth.|The company is having a giveaway of its new product. |近义词：freebies, handouts, prizes|反义词：purchases, sales


例句：

The radio station had a giveaway of concert tickets.  无线电台赠送了演唱会门票。

The subtle clues in the painting were a giveaway of the artist's identity.  画作中细微的线索暴露了艺术家的身份。
