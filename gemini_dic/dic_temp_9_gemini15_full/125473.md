multiply:/ˈmʌltɪplaɪ/ | vt. |乘；繁殖；增加|To increase in number or amount; to perform multiplication. |例句：Multiply 6 by 4.  = 把6乘以4。

例句：The rabbits multiplied rapidly. = 兔子迅速繁殖。

近义词：increase, augment, expand | 反义词：divide, decrease, reduce | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He multiplies the numbers quickly. = 他很快地计算乘法。
We multiply our efforts to achieve the goal. = 我们加倍努力以实现目标。

She multiplied the two numbers correctly. = 她正确地计算了这两个数的乘积。
They multiplied their investment in the company. = 他们增加了在公司的投资。


现在完成时:
