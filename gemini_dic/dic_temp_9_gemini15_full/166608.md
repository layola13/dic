presells:/ˈpriːsɛlz/| v.|预售，提前销售|To sell (something) before it is officially released or available.|The band pre-sold 10,000 tickets for their upcoming concert.乐队预售了1万张他们即将举行的演唱会的门票。|近义词：pre-market, advance sale|反义词：post-sale


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They presell their products online. 他们在线预售他们的产品。
The company presells tickets for the event.  公司预售活动的票。


一般过去时:
The artist pre-sold all his paintings before the exhibition.  艺术家在展览前预售了他所有的画作。
We pre-sold the limited edition books quickly. 我们迅速预售了限量版书籍。


现在完成时:
They have pre-sold over half of the concert tickets. 他们已经预售了超过一半的演唱会门票。
The company has pre-sold a significant number of units. 公司已经预售了相当数量的单元。


过去完成时:
They had pre-sold most of the inventory before the launch.  他们在发布之前就已经预售了大部分库存。
The organizers had pre-sold all the VIP tickets by the deadline.  组织者在截止日期前已经预售了所有VIP门票。


将来时:
They will presell the new phone next month. 他们下个月将预售新款手机。
The developer will presell apartments in the new building. 开发商将预售新楼盘的公寓。


虚拟语气:
If they pre-sold more tickets, they would make more profit. 如果他们预售更多门票，他们将获得更多利润。
I wish we had pre-sold more merchandise. 我希望我们预售了更多商品。


被动语态:
The tickets were pre-sold within hours. 门票在几小时内就被预售一空。
The album was pre-sold to great anticipation. 专辑在巨大的期待中被预售。


疑问句:
Have they pre-sold many tickets? 他们预售了很多门票吗？
Will the new game be pre-sold online? 新游戏会在网上预售吗？


否定句:
They didn't presell enough copies of the book. 他们没有预售足够的书。
The tickets were not pre-sold to the public. 门票没有预售给公众。
