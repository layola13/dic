interfere:/ˌɪntərˈfɪr/| vt.|干涉，干扰，妨碍|to get involved in a situation where you are not wanted or needed, or to prevent something from happening properly|例句：It's not my business, I won't interfere.  这不是我的事，我不会干涉。

例句：The noise interfered with my concentration. 噪音干扰了我的注意力。


近义词：meddle, intrude, impede, obstruct|反义词：assist, help, facilitate|动词
