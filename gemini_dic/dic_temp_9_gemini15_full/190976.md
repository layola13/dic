USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scales the fish before cooking. = 他在烹饪前给鱼去鳞。
She scales the mountain every weekend. = 她每个周末都爬山。


一般过去时:
The artist scaled down the drawing. = 艺术家缩小了图画。
They scaled the cliff face with ropes. = 他们用绳索攀登了悬崖峭壁。


现在完成时:
She has scaled Mount Everest. = 她已经攀登了珠穆朗玛峰。
He has scaled the business plan several times. = 他已经多次调整了商业计划。


过去完成时:
They had scaled the wall before the guards arrived. = 警卫到达之前，他们已经翻过了墙。
She had scaled back her expectations. = 她已经降低了她的期望。


将来时:
He will scale the building to fix the antenna. = 他将攀爬建筑物以修理天线。
They will scale up production to meet demand. = 他们将扩大生产以满足需求。


虚拟语气:
If I could scale the mountain, I would. = 如果我能爬山，我会去。
I wish I could scale the problem down more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地缩小问题规模。


被动语态:
The model was scaled to fit the blueprint. = 模型按比例缩放到与蓝图相符。
The project has been scaled back due to budget cuts. = 由于预算削减，项目规模已被缩小。


疑问句:
How do you scale the fish? = 你怎么给鱼去鳞？
Does the map scale accurately? = 地图比例精确吗？


否定句:
The fish doesn't scale easily. = 这条鱼不容易去鳞。
He didn't scale the wall successfully. = 他没有成功翻过墙。


PHRASE->
on a large scale = 大规模地
on a small scale = 小规模地
to scale = 按比例
out of scale = 不成比例
to scale down/up = 缩小/扩大规模
