lead-out: /ˈliːdˌaʊt/ | n. | 引出；导线；引线 | A lead-out is something that leads to something else, often a cable or wire. It can also refer to a concluding part of a performance or event. |例句：The lead-out cable connected the amplifier to the speakers.  导线将放大器连接到扬声器。

The band's lead-out was a powerful rendition of their hit song. 乐队的结束表演是他们热门歌曲的强力演绎。|近义词：extension, connection, outro, conclusion |反义词：introduction, beginning, commencement | 词性：名词
