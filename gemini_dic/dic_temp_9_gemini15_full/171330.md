USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't have verb or adjective forms with the same meaning.  Therefore, tense conjugations are not applicable.  The usage is primarily descriptive within anatomical contexts.


PHRASE->

词源:
From Latin *pubes, meaning "adult," "grown-up," referring to the time when pubic hair appears.  The anatomical term developed from the association of this bone with the onset of puberty.
