predecay:/ˌpriːdɪˈkeɪ/| n.| 预衰变；先期衰变 |The process occurring before the main decay of an unstable atomic nucleus.  It refers to a series of events leading up to the primary decay process, often involving isomeric transitions or other nuclear rearrangements. |例句：The predecay processes significantly influence the final decay products.  预衰变过程会显著影响最终的衰变产物。  |The study of predecay phenomena helps us understand nuclear structure.  对预衰变现象的研究有助于我们理解原子核结构。|近义词：preliminary decay, antecedent decay|反义词：postdecay
