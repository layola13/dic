misbefitting:/ˌmɪsbɪˈfɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不合适的；不相称的 |  Unfitting; unsuitable; inappropriate.  It describes something that is not appropriate or suitable for a particular situation, person, or purpose. | Example: Her flamboyant dress was misbefitting for a funeral.  她的艳丽服装不适合葬礼。  The punishment seemed misbefitting for such a minor offense.  对如此轻微的罪行来说，这种惩罚似乎不相称。| 近义词：unsuitable, inappropriate, unbecoming, incongruous | 反义词：suitable, appropriate, fitting, becoming
