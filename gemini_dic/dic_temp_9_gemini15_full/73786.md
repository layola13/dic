formalizes:/ˈfɔːrməlaɪzɪz/| v.| 使正式化；使制度化 | To give a definite or systematic form to (something); to make something official or established. |例句：The agreement was formalized in a written contract.  协议已正式写入合同。|例句：The company formalized its procedures to improve efficiency. 公司使其流程制度化以提高效率。|近义词：systematize, codify, institutionalize |反义词：informalize, casualize
