overdoubting: /ˌoʊvərˈdaʊtɪŋ/ | v. | 过度怀疑，疑神疑鬼 |  The act of doubting excessively or unreasonably. It implies a level of suspicion that goes beyond healthy skepticism and can be detrimental to one's mental well-being and relationships.  | 例句：His overdoubting of her intentions caused a rift in their relationship.  他的过度怀疑她的意图导致了他们关系破裂。

His constant overdoubting led to him missing many opportunities. 他不断的过度怀疑导致他错过了很多机会。


近义词：overthinking, hypervigilance, excessive skepticism, mistrust, paranoia | 反义词：trust, faith, confidence, belief


词性：动词
