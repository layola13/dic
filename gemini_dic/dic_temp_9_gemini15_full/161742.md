powerbomb: /ˈpaʊərbɒm/ | n. v. | 力量炸弹；(摔跤)力量炸弹式摔跤动作 |

英文解析:As a noun, a powerbomb refers to a powerful wrestling move where one wrestler lifts another wrestler up and slams them to the mat.  As a verb, it describes the act of performing this move.  The term combines "power" and "bomb," reflecting the force and impact of the maneuver.

例句 (noun):
The wrestler executed a perfect powerbomb, silencing the crowd. = 摔跤手完美地执行了一个力量炸弹，让全场鸦雀无声。

例句 (verb):
He powerbombed his opponent onto the steel steps. = 他将他对手用力量炸弹摔到钢制台阶上。


近义词: (as a noun)  slam, piledriver,  bomb (in wrestling context)
反义词:  (There isn't a direct antonym, but) gentle throw, lift


词性: noun, verb
