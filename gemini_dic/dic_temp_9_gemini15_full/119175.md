mezereon:/ˈmɛzəriən/| n. |芫花|A low-growing shrub with poisonous berries and fragrant pink, purple, or white flowers, often grown as an ornamental plant.  It is also known as Daphne mezereum.  The name comes from the Old French word "mezereon".|The mezereon bloomed early in the spring, its delicate pink flowers a welcome sight after a long winter.  芫花在春天早早地开花，它娇嫩的粉色花朵在漫长的冬季之后令人欣慰。|近义词：Daphne|反义词：无
