reedification:/ˌriːˌɛdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |重建；复兴；改造|The act of rebuilding or restoring something, especially a building or a system.  It implies a significant restoration or improvement, going beyond simple repair. |例句：The reedification of the old town center involved meticulous restoration of historical buildings.  旧城中心的重建涉及对历史建筑的精心修复。


例句：The government is committed to the reedification of the country's infrastructure. 政府致力于重建国家的基础设施。


近义词：reconstruction, rehabilitation, restoration, renewal


反义词：destruction, demolition, ruin


词性：名词
