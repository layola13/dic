hypothesised:/haɪˈpɒθəsaɪzd/| /haɪˈpɑːθəsaɪzd/ | v. | 假设；假设存在 | To suggest an explanation for something that has not yet been proved to be true.  It is the past tense of "hypothesize". |  Scientists hypothesised that the extinction of the dinosaurs was caused by a meteor impact. 科学家们假设恐龙的灭绝是由陨石撞击造成的。
The researcher hypothesised a link between diet and disease. 研究人员假设饮食和疾病之间存在联系。 | hypothesize, postulate, presume, assume | confirm, prove | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hypothesizes a connection between the two events. 他假设这两件事之间存在联系。
They hypothesize that climate change is accelerating. 他们假设气候变化正在加速。

一般过去时:
She hypothesised that the results were due to chance. 她假设结果是由于偶然造成的。
The team hypothesised a new model for understanding the phenomenon.  团队假设了一个理解这种现象的新模型。


现在完成时:
Scientists have hypothesised about the origins of life for centuries. 科学家们几个世纪以来一直在假设生命的起源。
They have hypothesised a new theory to explain the data. 他们已经假设了一个新的理论来解释这些数据。


过去完成时:
Before conducting the experiment, they had hypothesised the likely outcome. 在进行实验之前，他们已经假设了可能的结果。
Having hypothesised a correlation, they then sought to prove it. 在假设了相关性之后，他们随后试图证明它。


将来时:
Researchers will hypothesise about the long-term effects of the policy. 研究人员将假设这项政策的长期影响。
We will hypothesise different scenarios to test the model's robustness. 我们将假设不同的情景来测试模型的稳健性。


虚拟语气:
If they had hypothesised correctly, the experiment would have been successful. 如果他们假设正确，实验就会成功。
I wish I had hypothesised a different approach. 我真希望我一开始就假设了一种不同的方法。


被动语态:
It was hypothesised that the drug would have side effects.  人们假设这种药物会有副作用。
A correlation between stress and illness was hypothesised by the researchers. 研究人员假设压力和疾病之间存在相关性。


疑问句:
What did the researchers hypothesise about the cause of the disease? 研究人员对这种疾病的成因做了什么假设？
What would you hypothesise if you were presented with this data? 如果你拿到这些数据，你会假设什么？


否定句:
The study did not hypothesise any significant relationship. 这项研究没有假设任何显著的关系。
They did not hypothesise a causal link between the two variables. 他们没有假设这两个变量之间存在因果关系。
