epiphyllospermous:/ˌɛpɪˈfaɪləʊspɜːrməs/| adj. |叶生的；叶上生殖的 | Relating to or characterized by seeds borne on the surface of leaves.  This term describes plants that produce seeds directly on their leaves, rather than in fruits or other structures.  | Example:  The *epiphyllospermous* nature of this plant is a fascinating adaptation to its environment.  这种植物叶上生殖的特性是对其环境的一种迷人的适应。|近义词：foliose (in the context of seed bearing) |反义词：fruit-bearing (in the context of seed production)
