epencephalon:/ˌɛpənˈsɛfəlɒn/| n. |后脑|The hindbrain; the posterior part of the brain, comprising the metencephalon and myelencephalon.  It develops from the caudal part of the neural tube and controls vital functions such as breathing, heart rate, and balance.|Example: The epencephalon plays a crucial role in maintaining homeostasis and coordinating essential bodily functions.|近义词：hindbrain|反义词：forebrain
