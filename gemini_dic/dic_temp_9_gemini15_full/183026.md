replicates:/ˈrɛplɪkeɪts/| v. |复制；重复；再现|To make or produce an exact copy of something.  It can also refer to the act of repeating or reproducing a process or experiment.|

The scientist replicates the experiment to verify the results. = 科学家重复实验以验证结果。

The software replicates the functionality of the older system. = 该软件复制了旧系统的功能。

近义词：duplicate, copy, reproduce, imitate

反义词：destroy, erase, delete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine replicates data automatically. = 机器自动复制数据。
She replicates the success of her colleagues. = 她复制了同事的成功经验。

一般过去时:
He replicated the study using a different sample. = 他使用不同的样本重复了这项研究。
The company replicated its successful marketing campaign. = 公司复制了其成功的营销活动。

现在完成时:
They have replicated the experiment three times. = 他们已经重复了三次实验。
Scientists have replicated the results of the initial study. = 科学家们已经复制了初步研究的结果。

过去完成时:
Before the presentation, she had replicated the slides several times. = 在演示之前，她已经多次复制了幻灯片。
The team had replicated the error in their previous experiment. = 团队在之前的实验中已经复制了错误。

将来时:
We will replicate the process next week. = 我们下周将重复这个过程。
The researchers will replicate the experiment under different conditions. = 研究人员将在不同的条件下重复实验。

虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we could replicate the study more effectively. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们可以更有效地重复这项研究。
It would be beneficial if they could replicate the experiment. = 如果他们能够重复这个实验，将会有益。

被动语态:
The results were replicated by another research team. = 结果被另一个研究团队复制了。
The experiment is being replicated in several laboratories. = 该实验正在多个实验室中被重复。

疑问句:
Can you replicate the process? = 你能复制这个过程吗？
Have they replicated the findings? = 他们已经复制了这些发现吗？

否定句:
They could not replicate the experiment. = 他们无法重复该实验。
The results were not replicated in subsequent studies. = 在随后的研究中，结果没有被复制。


PHRASE->

词源:
From Late Latin *replicāre, from Latin plicare "to fold," "to plait," "to bend," from PIE *plek- "to weave," "to plait."  The prefix "re-" indicates repetition or again.  The sense evolved from the idea of "folding back" or "repeating a fold" to the modern meaning of making an exact copy.
