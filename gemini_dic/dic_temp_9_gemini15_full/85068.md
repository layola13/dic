hard-headed:/ˌhɑːrdˈhedɪd/| adj.|顽固的；倔强的；实际的；精明的|Practical and determined; not easily persuaded to change one's opinions or plans.  It can also imply someone is shrewd and pragmatic, focusing on practical results rather than sentiment. |例句：He's a hard-headed businessman who always gets what he wants. 他是一个精明的商人，总是能得到他想要的东西。

例句：Despite the evidence, she remained hard-headed and refused to change her mind.  尽管有证据，她仍然固执己见，拒绝改变主意。


近义词：stubborn, obstinate, determined, pragmatic, shrewd, tough-minded

反义词：yielding, pliable, flexible, sentimental, naive


词性:形容词
