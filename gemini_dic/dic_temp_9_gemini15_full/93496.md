illywhacker:/ˈɪliˌwækər/| n. |骗子，江湖骗子，无赖|A person who is dishonest or unreliable; a swindler or con artist.  The term often implies a more flamboyant or theatrical type of con artist.  It's primarily used in Australian English.|例句：He's a bit of an illywhacker, always trying to get something for nothing. 他有点像个骗子，总是想不劳而获。

例句：Don't trust that illywhacker; he'll probably try to rip you off.  别相信那个骗子；他可能会试图敲诈你。


近义词：swindler, con man, sharper, rogue, scoundrel, cheat


反义词：honest person, trustworthy person, upstanding citizen


词性: 名词
