mesoscutum:/ˌmɛsoʊˈskjuːtəm/| n. |中盾片（昆虫胸部背板的一部分）|The mesoscutum is the dorsal sclerite of the mesothorax in insects. It is often the largest and most prominent part of the insect's thorax.  |Example: The mesoscutum of this beetle species is brightly colored. 这只甲虫的中盾片颜色鲜艳。|近义词：mesonotum (部分同义，mesonotum包含mesoscutum)|反义词：无
