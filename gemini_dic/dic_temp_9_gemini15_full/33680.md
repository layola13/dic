cinnamic:/ˈsɪnəmɪk/| adj.|肉桂色的；桂皮酸的；|Cinnamon-like; relating to cinnamic acid.  The word derives from "cinnamon," referring to the spice. |例句：The candle had a cinnamic fragrance. 这支蜡烛有一种肉桂香味。|例句：Cinnamic acid is a component of cinnamon oil. 桂皮酸是肉桂油的成分。|近义词：aromatic, spicy|反义词：None specifically, but could be considered opposite to bland or odorless.
