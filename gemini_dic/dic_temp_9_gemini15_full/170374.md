provisos:/prəˈvaɪzoʊz/| n.| 附款；条件；限制 | A proviso is a condition or qualification attached to an agreement or statement.  It's something that must be met or considered before the agreement is valid or the statement is true.  | Example: The contract was signed with several provisos concerning liability. 这份合同签署时附带了几个关于责任的附款。 The donation was accepted with the proviso that it be used for educational purposes only. 捐赠被接受，但前提是只能用于教育目的。 |近义词：condition, stipulation, qualification, caveat |反义词：unconditional, unrestricted
