readded:/ˌriːˈædɪd/| v.|重新添加，再添加|To add something again; to add something that had been removed.  |例句：He readded the deleted paragraph to the essay. 他把删除的段落重新添加到文章中。

例句：The chef readded the spices to enhance the flavor.  厨师重新添加香料以增强风味。


近义词：readmit, reinstate, restore, append


反义词：remove, delete, subtract


词性：动词
