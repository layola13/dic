sentenced:/ˈsɛntənst/| adj.|被判刑的；被判处…刑罚的|Having been given a sentence in a court of law.  This adjective describes someone who has received a judicial punishment.|The sentenced man was taken to prison.  被判刑的男子被带进了监狱。|The judge sentenced the criminal to ten years in prison.  法官判处罪犯十年监禁。|convicted, condemned|innocent, acquitted|形容词

一般现在时:
He is sentenced to life imprisonment. 他被判处无期徒刑。
The criminals are sentenced to ten years in jail. 罪犯们被判处十年监禁。

一般过去时:
She was sentenced to five years in prison. 她被判处五年监禁。
The thief was sentenced to community service. 小偷被判处社区服务。

现在完成时:
He has been sentenced for fraud. 他因诈骗罪被判刑。
The judge has sentenced her to a fine. 法官判处她罚款。

过去完成时:
The defendant had been sentenced before the appeal. 上诉前被告已被判刑。
By the time the trial ended, the guilty party had already been sentenced.  审判结束时，罪犯已被判刑。

将来时:
He will be sentenced next week. 他下周将被判刑。
The jury will sentence the defendant after deliberation. 陪审团将经过商议后对被告进行判决。

虚拟语气:
If he were sentenced, he would appeal. 如果他被判刑，他会提出上诉。
I wish he hadn't been sentenced so harshly. 我希望他没有被判处如此严厉的刑罚。

被动语态:
The sentence was handed down yesterday. 判决书昨天宣判了。
The criminal will be sentenced by the judge. 罪犯将由法官判刑。

疑问句:
Has he been sentenced yet? 他已经被判刑了吗？
When will the accused be sentenced? 被告什么时候会被判刑？

否定句:
He has not yet been sentenced. 他还没有被判刑。
She was not sentenced to jail time. 她没有被判处监禁。
