cariole:/ˈkæriəʊl/| n. |轻便双轮马车|A small, light, two-wheeled carriage, especially one drawn by a single horse.  It was popular in the 18th and 19th centuries.|例句：The couple took a romantic ride in a cariole through the countryside.  一对夫妇在乡间乘坐轻便双轮马车进行了一次浪漫的旅行。


The cariole, a nimble vehicle, was ideal for navigating narrow, winding roads. 这辆轻便灵活的双轮马车非常适合在狭窄蜿蜒的道路上行驶。|近义词：gig, trap, chaise|反义词：coach, omnibus
