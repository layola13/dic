retransferring: /ˌriːˈtrænsˌfɜːrɪŋ/ | v. | 再转移；重新转让 | The act of transferring something again;  the process of moving something from one place or person to another, a second time.  It implies a previous transfer has already occurred. |例句：The company is retransferring the funds to the original account.  公司正在将资金重新转回原账户。

The data is being retransferred to a more secure server. 数据正在被重新传输到一个更安全的服务器。

近义词：relocating, re-routing, transferring again, reassigning

反义词：receiving, keeping, retaining

词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They retransfer the files every week.  他们每周都重新传输文件。
The bank retransfers funds automatically. 银行自动重新转账。

一般过去时:
He retransferred the ownership last year. 他去年重新转让了所有权。
She retransferred the call to her supervisor. 她把电话重新转接给了她的主管。

现在完成时:
We have retransferred all the necessary documents. 我们已经重新传输了所有必要的文档。
They have retransferred the patient to a different ward. 他们已将病人重新转到不同的病房。

过去完成时:
They had retransferred the money before the deadline. 他们在截止日期前已重新转账。
The system had retransferred the data before the failure. 系统在故障前已重新传输了数据。

将来时:
We will retransfer the funds next week. 我们下周将重新转账。
They will retransfer the responsibility to a new team. 他们将把责任重新转交给一个新的团队。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would retransfer the files. 如果我们有更多时间，我们会重新传输文件。
