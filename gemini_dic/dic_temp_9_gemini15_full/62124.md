enchoric: /ɛnˈkɔːrɪk/ | adj. |  古代的；古代文字的 | Relating to or characteristic of ancient inscriptions or writings, especially those found in the Levant.  The term often refers to scripts that are not yet fully deciphered or understood.  | 例句：The enchoric inscription on the sarcophagus defied all attempts at translation. 古 sarcophagus 上的古代铭文让所有翻译尝试都失败了。  | 近义词：ancient, archaic, epigraphic | 反义词：modern, contemporary |  词性: adjective
