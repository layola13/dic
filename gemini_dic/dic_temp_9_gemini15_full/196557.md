sesquisalt:/ˈsɛskwɪsɔːlt/| n.|倍半盐，1.5倍盐|A sesquisalt is a salt containing a metal cation and a polyatomic anion in a 2:3 ratio or a 3:2 ratio.  For example,  Al₂(SO₄)₃ is a sesquisalt because the ratio of aluminum (Al) to sulfate (SO₄) is 2:3.|例句：Aluminum sulfate, Al₂(SO₄)₃, is a common example of a sesquisalt. 硫酸铝Al₂(SO₄)₃是倍半盐的常见例子。|近义词：salt (general term)|反义词：N/A
