printout:/ˈprɪntˌaʊt/| n. |打印输出，打印件 |A printout of the document is needed for the meeting.  会议需要这份文件的打印件。|近义词：hard copy, output |反义词：digital copy


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The printer produces a printout every few seconds. = 打印机每隔几秒钟就会打印出一份输出。
This software generates a detailed printout of your expenses. = 这个软件会生成一份详细的支出打印清单。


一般过去时:
I received a printout of my bank statement yesterday. = 我昨天收到了我的银行对账单打印件。
