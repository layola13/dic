labiatifloral:/ˌleɪbiˌeɪtɪˈflɔːrəl/| adj. |唇形花卉的；唇形花科的|Relating to or characteristic of labiate flowers or the Labiatae family (now Lamiaceae).  The term describes plants with flowers having a two-lipped corolla, a characteristic feature of the mint family. |The labiatifloral plants in the garden attracted many bees.|近义词： labiate,mint-like|反义词：无
