reminding:/rɪˈmaɪndɪŋ/| v. |提醒；使想起|The act of reminding someone of something.  It often involves bringing something back to their attention or memory.|例句：He kept reminding me of my promise. 他不断提醒我我的承诺。|例句：The smell of baking bread reminded her of her childhood. 烤面包的味道让她想起了童年。|近义词：prompting, reminding, recalling|反义词：forgetting, overlooking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I remind him to take his medicine every day. = 我每天提醒他吃药。
She reminds me of my aunt. = 她让我想起了我的阿姨。


一般过去时:
He reminded me about the meeting yesterday. = 他昨天提醒了我会议的事。
I reminded her to lock the door before she left. = 我提醒她在离开前锁门。


现在完成时:
I have reminded him several times, but he still forgets. = 我已经提醒他好几次了，但他还是忘记了。
She has reminded me of my appointment this morning. = 她今天早上提醒了我我的约会。


过去完成时:
I had reminded him once before, but he still forgot. = 我之前已经提醒过他一次了，但他还是忘记了。
She had reminded me of the deadline the previous week. = 她在上周已经提醒了我截止日期。


将来时:
I will remind him again tomorrow. = 我明天会再次提醒他。
She will remind me of the meeting before it starts. = 会议开始前她会提醒我。


虚拟语气:
I wish I had reminded him earlier. = 我真希望我早点提醒他。
If I had reminded her, she wouldn't have missed the train. = 如果我提醒了她，她就不会错过火车了。


被动语态:
He was reminded of his responsibilities. = 他被提醒了他的责任。
She was reminded to submit her report on time. = 她被提醒要按时提交报告。


疑问句:
Did you remind him to call? = 你提醒他打电话了吗？
What reminded you of that old song? = 什么让你想起了那首老歌？


否定句:
I didn't remind him about the deadline. = 我没有提醒他截止日期。
She didn't remind me to bring my book. = 她没有提醒我带书。



PHRASE->
a constant reminder = 不断的提醒
a gentle reminder = 友好的提醒
a timely reminder = 及时的提醒
a painful reminder = 痛苦的提醒
a stark reminder = 严峻的提醒
