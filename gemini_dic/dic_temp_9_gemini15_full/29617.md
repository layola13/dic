USAGE->
一般现在时:
He categorizes his emails by sender. 他按发件人分类他的电子邮件。
She categorizes her expenses into different categories. 她把她的支出分成不同的类别。

一般过去时:
The teacher categorized the students by ability. 老师按能力对学生进行了分类。
The scientist categorized the specimens according to their characteristics. 科学家根据其特征对样本进行了分类。

现在完成时:
She has categorized all the data for the report. 她已经为报告整理好所有数据了。
They have categorized the products into different price ranges. 他们已将产品分类到不同的价格范围内。

过去完成时:
He had categorized the files before the meeting. 他在会议前已对文件进行了分类。
The researcher had categorized the research papers before writing her review. 研究人员在撰写评论之前已经对研究论文进行了分类。

将来时:
They will categorize the applicants based on their qualifications. 他们将根据申请人的资格对他们进行分类。
She will categorize the images before uploading them to the website. 她会在上传图片到网站之前对它们进行分类。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would categorize all my photos. 如果我有更多的时间，我会把我的所有照片分类。

被动语态:
