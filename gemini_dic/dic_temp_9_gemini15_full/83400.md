haaf:/hæf/| n.|海夫（苏格兰语中指一种捕鱼方法或渔船）|A haaf is a type of fishing in Shetland and Orkney, involving fishing from a boat in open water, often at night.  It's also used to refer to the boat used in this type of fishing.|The fishermen set out for the haaf at dusk. = 渔民在黄昏时出发去海夫捕鱼。|  The haaf boat was battered by the storm. = 海夫渔船被暴风雨袭击。|近义词：fishing (general term), netting|反义词：无
