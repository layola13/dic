postcapitalist: /ˌpoʊstˈkæpɪtəlɪst/ | adj. | 后资本主义的 | Describing a societal or economic system that follows or succeeds capitalism, often characterized by a move away from traditional capitalist structures and ideologies.  This term is often used to describe systems that attempt to mitigate the negative effects of capitalism, incorporate elements of socialism, or explore alternative economic models. The precise meaning can vary widely depending on the context and the author's perspective. | Example:  Many theorists propose a postcapitalist society focused on sustainability and social justice.  许多理论家提出了一个以可持续性和社会正义为重点的后资本主义社会。  |近义词: post-industrial, alternative economic model | 反义词: capitalist
