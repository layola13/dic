despoil:/dɪˈspɔɪl/ | vt. |掠夺；破坏；剥夺 | To rob a place of its valuable possessions; to strip something of its beauty or value.  |The invading army despoiled the city of its treasures. 侵略军洗劫了这座城市的财宝。|The factory despoiled the landscape. 工厂破坏了风景。| pillage, plunder, ravage, loot | enhance, enrich, beautify


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They despoil the land of its resources. = 他们掠夺土地资源。
He despoils the ancient building of its artifacts. = 他掠夺古建筑的文物。


一般过去时:
The vandals despoiled the monument. = 故意破坏者毁坏了纪念碑。
The storm despoiled the garden of its flowers. = 暴风雨摧毁了花园的花朵。


现在完成时:
The miners have despoiled the mountainside. = 矿工们已经破坏了山坡。
They have despoiled the river of its fish. = 他们已经把河里的鱼都捕捞光了。


过去完成时:
The thieves had despoiled the museum before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，小偷已经洗劫了博物馆。
The invaders had despoiled the countryside before retreating. = 侵略者撤退前已经破坏了乡村。


将来时:

The developers will despoil the coastline with their buildings. = 开发商将会用他们的建筑破坏海岸线。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:
