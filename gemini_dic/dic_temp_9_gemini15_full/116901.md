mayhaps:/meɪˈhæps/| adv. |也许，可能 |Perhaps, possibly; used to express uncertainty or a possibility.  It is an archaic or literary word, adding a slightly whimsical or old-fashioned tone.|例句：Mayhaps we shall meet again someday.  = 也许我们将来某天还会再见。
例句：Mayhaps the rain will stop soon. = 也许雨很快就会停。|近义词：perhaps, possibly, maybe, perchance |反义词：certainly, definitely
