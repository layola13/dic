ichnolite:/ˈɪknəlaɪt/| n. |遗迹化石|A fossil that is a trace of an organism, rather than the organism itself; a type of fossil that shows evidence of the activity of an organism, such as footprints, burrows, or trails.|The ichnolite revealed a dinosaur trackway, indicating the direction and gait of the ancient creature.|近义词: trace fossil, biogenic structure|反义词: body fossil
