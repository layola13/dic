realpolitikers: /ˌriːəlˈpɒlɪtɪkərz/ | n. |现实政治家，务实政治家 | Those who practice realpolitik, prioritizing practical considerations over ideological or moral ones in politics.  They focus on power, national interest, and tangible results, often disregarding ethical concerns. |例句：The realpolitikers in the government prioritized securing a trade deal, even if it meant compromising on environmental regulations.  政府中的现实政治家们优先考虑达成贸易协议，即使这意味着要在环境法规上做出妥协。


例句：Many criticized the realpolitikers for their willingness to make deals with unsavory regimes. 许多人批评现实政治家们愿意与不受欢迎的政权达成协议。


近义词： pragmatists, strategists, power brokers |反义词：idealists, moralists


词性：名词
