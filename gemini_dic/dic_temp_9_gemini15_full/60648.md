electing:/ɪˈlɛktɪŋ/| v.|选举；推选|The act of choosing someone for a position by voting.|例句：The country is currently electing a new president. 这个国家目前正在选举新总统。  The committee is electing a new chairperson. 委员会正在选举新主席。|近义词：choosing, selecting, nominating|反义词：rejecting, dismissing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are electing a new mayor. = 他们正在选举一位新市长。
We elect our representatives every four years. = 我们每四年选举一次我们的代表。

一般过去时:
The voters elected him to office. = 选民们选举他担任公职。
She was elected class president. = 她被选为班长。


现在完成时:
They have elected a new leader. = 他们已经选举了一位新领导。
Have you ever elected someone to a position? = 你有没有选举过某人担任某个职位？


过去完成时:
The board had elected a new CEO before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，董事会已经选举了一位新的首席执行官。
They had elected him twice before he resigned. = 他辞职前已被选举过两次。


将来时:
We will be electing a new school board member next month. = 我们下个月将要选举一位新的学校董事会成员。
They will elect a new president in November. = 他们将在十一月选举一位新总统。


虚拟语气:
If they elected a more competent leader, the company would prosper. = 如果他们选举一位更有能力的领导人，公司就会繁荣。
I wish they had elected a different candidate. = 我希望他们当时选举了不同的候选人。


被动语态:
A new president was elected last week. = 上周选举了一位新总统。
The candidate was elected by a landslide. = 该候选人以压倒性优势当选。


疑问句:
Are they electing a new chairman today? = 他们今天选举新主席吗？
Who was elected to the Senate? = 谁当选参议员？


否定句:
They did not elect him to the position. = 他们没有选举他担任这个职位。
The committee did not elect a new secretary. = 委员会没有选举新秘书。


PHRASE->
electing officials = 选举官员
electing a representative = 选举代表
electing a committee = 选举委员会
electing a president = 选举总统
electing a leader = 选举领导人
