physiologists:/ˌfɪziˈɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. |生理学家 | Scientists who study the functions and processes of living organisms.  Physiologists investigate how the body works at all levels, from molecules to organs and systems.  They use various techniques, including experiments, observations, and modeling, to understand the mechanisms underlying life processes. | Example:  Many physiologists are currently researching the effects of exercise on the human body.  许多生理学家目前正在研究运动对人体的影響。

  The study of physiology is crucial for understanding health and disease.  生理学的研究对于理解健康和疾病至关重要。 |近义词: biologists, life scientists |反义词: N/A
