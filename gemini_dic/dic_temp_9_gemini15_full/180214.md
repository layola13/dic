refounding:/ˌriːˈfaʊndɪŋ/| v.|重建，重新创建，改革|The act of establishing something again, often with significant changes or improvements.  It implies a complete or substantial overhaul of a previous structure or system.|例句：The refounding of the company involved a complete restructuring of its management. 公司的重建包括对其管理层的彻底重组。|例句：The refounding of the political party led to a shift in its ideology.  该政党的重建导致其意识形态发生了转变。|近义词：rebuilding, restructuring, reforming, reinventing|反义词：destruction, demolition, dismantling|动词
