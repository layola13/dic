gemmule:/ˈdʒɛmjuːl/| n. |芽球；生殖芽 |A small, asexual reproductive unit in some invertebrates, capable of developing into a new individual.  It is essentially a collection of cells that can bud off from the parent organism and grow into a new, genetically identical organism.  |Example:  Sponges reproduce asexually by producing gemmules that can survive harsh environmental conditions. 海绵通过产生能够在恶劣环境条件下生存的芽球进行无性繁殖。|近义词：bud, propagule |反义词：gamete
