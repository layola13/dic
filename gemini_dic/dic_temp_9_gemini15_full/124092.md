morse-alphabets:/mɔːrs ˈælfəbɛts/ | n. |莫尔斯电码字母表 |Morse code is a method of transmitting textual information as a series of on-off tones, lights, or clicks that can be directly understood by a skilled listener or observer without special equipment.  Morse alphabets refer to the specific code assignments for each letter of the alphabet in this system.  Each letter is represented by a unique combination of dots (short signals) and dashes (long signals).|The message was sent using Morse alphabets.|近义词：Morse code, telegraph code |反义词：None
