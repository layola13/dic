hydropically:/haɪˈdrɒpɪkli/| adj.|水肿地；水状地 | Characterized by or relating to dropsy (edema); resembling or pertaining to water.  The term describes a condition or process involving an abnormal accumulation of fluid, typically in body tissues. |Example: The patient's legs were hydropically swollen.  患者的腿严重水肿。|近义词：edematously, swollenly |反义词：dryly, dehydratedly
