shebeens:/ʃɪˈbiːnz/| n.|私酒馆，非法酒吧|A shebeen is an unlicensed bar or pub, often associated with illegal activities.  The term is particularly common in Southern Africa and Ireland.|例句：The police raided the shebeen and arrested several people.警方突击搜查了那家私酒馆，逮捕了几个人。|近义词：speakeasy, illegal bar, dive|反义词：licensed bar, pub
