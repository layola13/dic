foremean:/fɔːrˈmiːn/| n.|工头，领班|A foreman is a skilled worker who supervises other workers.|例句：The foreman instructed the workers to start the project immediately. 工头指示工人立即开始项目。|近义词：supervisor, supervisor, superintendent|反义词：worker, subordinate.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The foreman oversees the daily work. = 工头监督日常工作。
He checks the quality of the work. = 他检查工作的质量。


一般过去时:
The foreman supervised the construction project. = 工头监督了这个建筑项目。
He inspected the finished product yesterday. = 他昨天检查了成品。


现在完成时:
The foreman has completed many projects successfully. = 工头已经成功完成了许多项目。
He has ensured the safety of the workers. = 他确保了工人的安全。


过去完成时:
The foreman had already finished the inspection before the meeting. = 工头在会议之前就已经完成了检查。
He had addressed the workers' concerns. = 他已经解决了工人们的担忧。


将来时:
The foreman will lead the team to the new site. = 工头将带领团队前往新的工地。
He will assign tasks to the workers tomorrow. = 他明天将分配任务给工人。


虚拟语气:
If the foreman were here, he would solve this problem. = 如果工头在这里，他会解决这个问题。
I wish the foreman had more experience. = 我希望工头更有经验。


被动语态:
The work was overseen by the foreman. = 工作由工头监督。
The safety regulations were enforced by the foreman. = 安全规章由工头执行。


疑问句:

否定句:
