immuring: /ɪˈmjʊərɪŋ/ | v. | 砌墙封闭；监禁；埋藏 | To enclose or embed within a wall; to imprison; to bury. |

例句：

The prisoners were immured in the dungeon.  = 囚犯们被关押在地牢里。

They immured the time capsule in the cornerstone of the building. = 他们把时间胶囊砌在建筑物的基石里。


近义词：entomb, entomb, wall in, incarcerate, imprison

反义词：release, free, liberate

词性：动词

They immure the prisoners in the tower. = 他们把囚犯关在塔里。
He immures himself in his work. = 他埋头工作。
