scrubber:/ˈskrʌbər/| n. |擦洗器；磨光器；洗涤器；（尤指）烟气净化器 |A scrubber is a device or tool used for cleaning or polishing a surface, or for removing impurities from a gas or liquid.  It can refer to a simple household cleaning tool or a complex industrial system. |例句：She used a scrubber to clean the burnt pan. 她用擦洗器清洗了烧焦的平底锅。

例句：The power plant uses a scrubber to remove pollutants from its emissions.  这家发电厂使用洗涤器去除其排放物中的污染物。


近义词：cleaner, polisher, purifier, filter |反义词：polluter

词源:
The word "scrubber" comes from the verb "scrub," meaning to rub hard to clean.  The suffix "-er" indicates an agent or tool used for performing the action.  The earliest recorded usage of "scrubber" in its modern sense dates back to the late 19th century, in reference to devices used in industrial processes.
