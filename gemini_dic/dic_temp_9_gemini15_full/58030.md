drogue:/druːɡ/| n.|阻力伞；拖曳伞；阻力装置|A drogue is a device, typically a parachute or similar device, used to slow down or stabilize a moving object, such as a spacecraft, aircraft, or boat.  It is often deployed during landing or descent to reduce speed and ensure a safe landing.  It can also be used to stabilize a moving object in water.|The spacecraft deployed its drogue parachute to slow its descent.|近义词：parachute, drag chute, decelerator|反义词：accelerator|
