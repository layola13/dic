retrofit:/ˈrɛtrəʊfɪt/ | vt. n. |改装，改造，翻新 | To modify (something) by adding new or different features or components.  |例句：We retrofitted the old building with modern energy-efficient windows. 我们用现代节能窗户改造了旧建筑。
例句：The car's retrofit kit improved its fuel efficiency. 这辆车的改装套件提高了燃油效率。|近义词：modify, upgrade, renovate, overhaul |反义词：deteriorate, damage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They retrofit the old cars with new engines. = 他们用新发动机改装旧车。
We retrofit our homes with solar panels. = 我们为我们的家安装太阳能电池板。


一般过去时:
The company retrofitted the factory with new safety equipment. = 公司用新的安全设备改造了工厂。
He retrofitted his bike with a new set of gears. = 他给他的自行车安装了一套新的齿轮。


现在完成时:
They have retrofitted all the buildings in the complex. = 他们已经改造了整个建筑群的所有建筑物。
She has retrofitted her old computer with a faster processor. = 她已经用更快的处理器升级了她的旧电脑。


过去完成时:
They had retrofitted the plane before the flight. = 他们在那次飞行之前已经改装了飞机。
The engineers had retrofitted the bridge with stronger supports. = 工程师们已经用更坚固的支架加固了桥梁。


将来时:
We will retrofit the entire system next year. = 我们明年将改造整个系统。
They will retrofit the old power plant with cleaner technology. = 他们将用更清洁的技术改造旧电厂。


虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would retrofit the entire building. = 如果我们有更多钱，我们会改造整栋建筑。
It would be better if they retrofitted their factory with modern equipment. = 如果他们用现代设备改造工厂会更好。


被动语态:
The house is being retrofitted with new insulation. = 这栋房子正在进行隔热改造。
The cars were retrofitted with catalytic converters. = 汽车安装了催化转换器。


疑问句:
Will the building be retrofitted to meet safety standards? = 这栋建筑物是否会进行改造以符合安全标准？
Have they retrofitted the engines yet? = 他们已经改装发动机了吗？


否定句:
They did not retrofit the older models of the car. = 他们没有改装旧款汽车。
The company will not retrofit the outdated equipment. = 公司不会改装过时的设备。
