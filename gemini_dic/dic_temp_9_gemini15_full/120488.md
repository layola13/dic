minting:/ˈmɪntɪŋ/ | v. |铸造；创造；发明 |The act of creating something new, especially coins or money, or of producing something in large quantities.  It can also refer to the process of creating something fresh or innovative. |例句：The Royal Mint is responsible for minting coins for the UK.  英国皇家造币厂负责铸造英国硬币。

The government is minting new economic policies. 政府正在制定新的经济政策。

近义词：coining, striking, creating, producing | 反义词：destroying, abolishing
