painted:/ˈpeɪntɪd/| adj. v. |油漆过的；绘画的；描绘的|Covered with paint; depicted in a painting or drawing.|例句：The walls were painted a bright yellow. 墙壁漆成了亮黄色。
The artist painted a beautiful landscape.  艺术家画了一幅美丽的风景画。|近义词：varnished, coated, depicted, portrayed|反义词：unpainted, bare


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He paints landscapes every weekend. 他每个周末都画风景画。
She paints her nails a different color each week. 她每周都把指甲涂成不同的颜色。


一般过去时:
They painted the house last summer. 他们去年夏天粉刷了房子。
She painted a stunning portrait of her grandmother. 她为祖母画了一幅令人惊叹的肖像。


现在完成时:
I have painted several portraits this year. 今年我已经画了几幅肖像画。
They have painted the entire building. 他们已经粉刷了整栋楼。


过去完成时:
He had painted the mural before the festival began. 节日开始前他已经完成了壁画。
She had painted the entire room before I arrived. 我到达之前她已经把整个房间都粉刷好了。


将来时:
We will paint the kitchen next week. 我们下周将粉刷厨房。
He will paint a new picture tomorrow. 他明天将画一幅新的画。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would paint a masterpiece. 如果我有更多时间，我会画出一幅杰作。
I wish I had painted that beautiful sunset. 我真希望我画下了那美丽的日落。


被动语态:
The house was painted white. 房子被漆成白色。
The mural was painted by a famous artist. 这幅壁画是一位著名的艺术家创作的。


疑问句:
Did you paint the fence? 你粉刷了篱笆吗？
Have they painted the living room yet? 他们粉刷了客厅了吗？


否定句:
I didn't paint the door. 我没有粉刷门。
They haven't painted the bedroom yet. 他们还没有粉刷卧室。
