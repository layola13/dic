rabbin:/ˈræbɑɪn/|/ˈræbaɪn/| n. |犹太教拉比|A rabbi is a Jewish religious leader and teacher.  Rabbis interpret Jewish law, lead religious services, and provide spiritual guidance to their congregations. They are often highly educated in Jewish texts and traditions.|例句：The rabbi delivered a powerful sermon on the importance of community.  这位拉比发表了一篇关于社区重要性的精彩布道。

例句：My family attends services at a progressive rabbi's synagogue. 我的家人在一个进步派拉比的犹太教堂参加宗教仪式。

近义词：priest, minister, cleric|反义词：None (no direct antonym)|词性:名词
