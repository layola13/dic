netted:/ˈnɛtɪd/ | adj. v. | 网状的；用网捕获的；赚得 |  Having a net-like structure or pattern; caught in or as if in a net; obtained as profit. |例句：The fisherman netted a large haul of fish. 渔民捕获了大量的鱼。  The company netted a profit of $1 million. 公司赚取了100万美元的利润。 |近义词：trapped, ensnared, gained, earned |反义词：lost, wasted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nets a lot of fish every day. 他每天捕获很多鱼。
The company nets a substantial profit each quarter.  这家公司每个季度都能获得可观的利润。

一般过去时:
She netted a beautiful butterfly in her net. 她用网捕到一只美丽的蝴蝶。
They netted a significant amount of money from the sale. 他们从这次销售中赚取了可观的金额。

现在完成时:
We have netted over 1000 applications for the job. 我们已经收到了1000多份工作申请。
The project has netted a considerable return on investment.  该项目带来了可观的投资回报。

过去完成时:
Before the end of the year, they had netted a record profit. 在年底之前，他们已经获得了创纪录的利润。
By the time the storm hit, the fishermen had netted most of their catch.  暴风雨来临之前，渔民们已经捕获了大部分渔获。

将来时:
They will net a bigger profit next year. 他们明年将获得更大的利润。
We expect to net a large number of new customers with this campaign. 我们预计这次活动将吸引大量新客户。

虚拟语气:
If we had used a better net, we would have netted more fish. 如果我们使用更好的网，我们将捕获更多的鱼。
If they had invested wisely, they would have netted a higher return. 如果他们投资明智，他们将获得更高的回报。

被动语态:
A large amount of money was netted from the auction. 拍卖会赚取了大量的钱。
Many fish were netted by the trawler. 拖网渔船捕获了许多鱼。

疑问句:
How many fish did you net today? 你今天捕获了多少鱼？
What profit did the business net last year?  这家企业去年赚取了多少利润？

否定句:
They didn't net any fish yesterday. 他们昨天没有捕到任何鱼。
The company didn't net a profit this quarter.  这家公司本季度没有盈利。


PHRASE->
net income: 净收入
net profit: 净利润
net worth: 净资产
net weight: 净重
net effect: 净效应


词源:
源自古英语“nett,”意为“网”，最终来自原始日耳曼语根"*net-",意为“网，结”。  作为动词，其含义从“用网捕捉”演变到“获得（利润）”，体现了捕获猎物与获得利益的隐喻联系。
