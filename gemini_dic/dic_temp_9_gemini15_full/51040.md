desmog:/ˈdɛzˌmɒɡ/| n. |除雾器；除霾器|A desmog is a device used to remove smog or other pollutants from the air.  It typically involves a filtration system to trap particulate matter and potentially other technologies to address gaseous pollutants.  The term is a blend of "de-" (meaning "to remove") and "smog."|例句：The city is installing new desmogs to improve air quality.  这个城市正在安装新的除霾器以改善空气质量。|近义词：air purifier, smog filter|反义词：smog producer, pollutant emitter|名词
