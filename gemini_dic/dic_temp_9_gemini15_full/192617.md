scur:/skɜːr/| v.|疾驰，飞奔，迅速移动|To move quickly or hurriedly.|The children scurried across the road to avoid the oncoming car. 孩子们飞快地穿过马路以避开迎面而来的汽车。|近义词：dash, hurry, rush, scamper|反义词：crawl, creep, dawdle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scurries to catch the bus every morning. 他每天早上都飞快地赶公共汽车。
The mice scurry across the floor when they hear a noise. 老鼠听到声音时就飞快地穿过地板。

一般过去时:
She scurried home when it started to rain.  下雨的时候她赶紧跑回家。
The dog scurried under the table when it saw the vacuum cleaner. 那条狗看到吸尘器就跑到桌子底下去了。

现在完成时:
The rabbit has scurried into its burrow. 兔子已经窜进了它的洞穴。
They have scurried to complete the project before the deadline. 他们已经赶紧完成项目以赶上截止日期。

过去完成时:
The thief had scurried away before the police arrived. 警察到达之前，小偷已经逃跑了。
She had scurried to get ready before the important meeting. 重要的会议之前，她已经赶紧准备好。

将来时:
He will scurry to the airport to catch his flight. 他将飞奔到机场赶飞机。
The children will scurry to hide when they hear their mother's footsteps. 孩子们听到母亲的脚步声就会赶紧躲起来。

虚拟语气:
If I were a mouse, I would scurry away at the slightest noise. 如果我是老鼠，我会在听到一点声音就赶紧跑掉。
I wish I could scurry away from all my responsibilities. 我希望我能逃避掉我所有的责任。

被动语态: (Scurry is rarely used in the passive voice)
*It's difficult to construct a natural passive voice sentence with "scurry."*


疑问句:
Does the cat scurry when it sees a mouse? 猫看到老鼠的时候会不会飞快地跑？
Will they scurry away if they see us? 如果他们看到我们，他们会跑掉吗？

否定句:
He didn't scurry; he walked calmly. 他没有跑；他平静地走着。
The children didn't scurry, they strolled leisurely. 孩子们没有跑，他们悠闲地散步。


PHRASE->
scurry about: 忙乱地跑来跑去
scurry around: 匆忙地四处走动
scurry off: 匆匆离开
scurry away: 赶紧跑开
scurry to: 赶紧去
