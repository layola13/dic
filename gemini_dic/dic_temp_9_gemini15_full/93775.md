immen:/ˈɪmən/| adj.|巨大的，无限的，广阔的|extremely large in size or extent; immeasurable.|The immenseness of the universe is awe-inspiring. 宇宙的广阔令人敬畏。|近义词：vast, immense, enormous, huge|反义词：tiny, minute, insignificant


USAGE->
