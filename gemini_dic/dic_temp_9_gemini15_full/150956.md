param:/ˈpærəm/ | n. | 参数，变量 | A parameter is a variable or value that is passed to a function, subroutine, or other procedure.  It's a piece of information that the function needs to perform its task. | Example: The function `calculate_area` takes two parameters: `length` and `width`.  函数`calculate_area` 接受两个参数：`length` 和 `width`。|近义词：argument, variable, input | 反义词: output, result | 名词
