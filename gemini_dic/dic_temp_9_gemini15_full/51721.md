It can also refer to a departure from a norm, rule, or standard.|例句：The deviation from the planned route caused a delay.  偏离计划路线导致延误。

The large deviation in test scores suggests a problem with the exam. 测试分数的大偏差表明考试存在问题。|近义词：departure, divergence, aberration, variation|反义词：conformity, adherence, compliance|名词
