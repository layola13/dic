cetological:/ˌsiːtəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/| adj.|鲸类的；鲸鱼学的| Relating to cetology, the scientific study of whales, dolphins, and porpoises.  | Example:  The cetological research team spent months studying the migration patterns of humpback whales. 鲸类研究小组花了几个月时间研究座头鲸的迁徙模式。|近义词：whale-related,  delphinic (in relation to dolphins)|反义词：None.
