date:/deɪt/| n.|日期，日子，约会，枣子 | a particular day of the year, or a particular time | 例句：What's the date today? 今天几号？| 例句：We have a date tonight. 我们今晚有约会。| 近义词：day, appointment | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The date today is October 26th. = 今天是十月二十六号。
My birthday is on the 15th of May. = 我的生日在五月十五号。


一般过去时:
The date of the meeting was last Tuesday. = 会议日期是上周二。
We went on a date last night. = 我们昨晚约会了。


现在完成时:
I haven't had a date in months. = 我已经好几个月没约会了。
The date of the announcement has not yet been determined. = 公告日期尚未确定。


过去完成时:
The project was already late by the due date. = 到截止日期时，项目已经延误了。
They had already set the date before I arrived. = 我到达之前，他们已经确定了日期。


将来时:
The concert will be held on the 10th of next month. = 音乐会将在下个月的十号举行。
We will plan our date later. = 我们稍后会计划我们的约会。


虚拟语气:
If the date were earlier, I could attend. = 如果日期早一点，我就能参加了。
I wish the date was sooner. = 我希望日期早一点。


被动语态:
The date of the exam has been decided. = 考试日期已经确定。
The date was announced on the website. = 日期已在网站上公布。


疑问句:
What is the date of the next meeting? = 下次会议的日期是什么时候？
What date are you free? = 你哪天有空？


否定句:
The date is not yet confirmed. = 日期尚未确定。
I don't know the exact date. = 我不知道确切的日期。



PHRASE->
set a date = 确定日期
due date = 截止日期
on this date = 在此日期
a date with = 与...约会
go on a date = 约会
