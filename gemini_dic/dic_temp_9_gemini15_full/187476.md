rollback:/ˈroʊlˌbæk/|/ˈrəʊlˌbæk/| n. vt.|回滚；恢复；撤销|The act of returning to a previous state or condition, typically in a computer system or software application.  It involves reversing changes made to a system to a prior state.  This can involve undoing transactions, restoring files, or reverting code changes.|例句：The database administrator performed a rollback to recover from the corrupted data. 数据库管理员执行回滚操作以恢复损坏的数据。

例句：We had to do a rollback of the software update after discovering several bugs.  在发现几个错误后，我们不得不回滚软件更新。


近义词：revert, undo, reverse, restore|反义词：advance, proceed, progress|动词
