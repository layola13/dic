high-hatting:/ˈhaɪˌhætɪŋ/| v.| 摆架子，装腔作势 | To behave in a superior or condescending manner; to act snobbishly.  | 例句：He was high-hatting everyone at the party, refusing to speak to anyone he deemed beneath him. 他在派对上对每个人都摆架子，拒绝与他认为不如自己的人说话。

例句：She's been high-hatting me ever since I got the promotion.自从我升职以来，她一直对我摆架子。


近义词：snobbing, condescending, patronizing, supercilious


反义词：humble, modest, unassuming


词性:动词
