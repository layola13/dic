derricks:/ˈdɛrɪks/| n.|起重机；井架|A derrick is a tall structure, typically with a boom (a long arm), used for lifting and moving heavy objects.  It is often used in construction, oil drilling, and salvage operations. |例句：The construction workers used a derrick to lift the steel beams into place. 建筑工人用起重机将钢梁吊装到位。

例句：The oil derrick stood tall against the desert sky.  石油井架高耸在沙漠的天空下。

近义词：crane, hoist, lift
