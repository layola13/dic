phytogeography:/ˌfaɪtoʊdʒiˈɑːɡrəfi/| n. |植物地理学 |The branch of botany that deals with the geographical distribution of plants.  It examines the factors that influence plant distribution, such as climate, soil, topography, and biotic interactions.  |Example: Phytogeography helps us understand the evolutionary history and adaptation of plant species across different regions.  植物地理学帮助我们了解不同地区植物物种的进化史和适应性。|近义词：geobotany, plant geography |反义词：N/A
