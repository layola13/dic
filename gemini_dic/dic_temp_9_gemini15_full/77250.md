gastronomer:/ˈɡæstrənəʊmər/ | n. |美食家；烹饪专家 |A gastronomer is a person who is an expert in gastronomy, the art and science of good eating.  They are knowledgeable about food, its preparation, and its cultural significance. They often appreciate fine dining and have a refined palate. |Example:  He is a renowned gastronomer, known for his insightful critiques of Michelin-starred restaurants. 他是一位著名的美食家，以其对米其林星级餐厅的深刻评论而闻名。|近义词：gourmet, epicure, gourmand |反义词：None specifically, but someone with a simple palate could be considered an antonym.
