hypersomnolence:/ˌhaɪpərsɒmnələns/ | n. |嗜睡症，过度的嗜睡 |Excessive sleepiness, characterized by prolonged or excessive sleep, often despite sufficient sleep.  It's a symptom, not a disease itself, and can be caused by various underlying conditions. |Example:  His hypersomnolence was so severe that he struggled to stay awake during the day.  他的嗜睡症非常严重，以至于白天很难保持清醒。|近义词：somnolence, excessive sleepiness, sleepiness, lethargy|反义词：insomnia, wakefulness
