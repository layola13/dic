committable: /kəˈmɪtəbəl/ | adj. | 可提交的，可委托的 | Capable of being committed; able to be entrusted or delegated.  This word often refers to something that can be officially submitted or given over to someone's care or responsibility. | 例句：The case is committable to the grand jury.  这个案子可以提交给大陪审团。

The evidence is committable to the court.  证据是可以提交给法庭的。


近义词：submittable, transferable, assignable | 反义词：unsubmittable, irretrievable
