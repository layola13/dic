frustrater: /frʌˈstreɪtər/ | n. | 令人沮丧的人或事物 | A person or thing that causes frustration.  |  例句：The constant delays were a major frustrater for the project team.  持续的延误是项目团队的主要障碍。|近义词：obstacle, impediment, annoyance, irritation |反义词：help, aid, assistance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The traffic frustrates me every morning. = 早上的交通让我很沮丧。
That machine is frustrating the workers. = 那台机器让工人们很沮丧。


一般过去时:
The test frustrated him greatly. = 那次考试让他非常沮丧。
The long wait frustrated the customers. = 漫长的等待让顾客们很沮丧。


现在完成时:
The project has frustrated us for months. = 这个项目已经让我们沮丧了好几个月了。
This problem has frustrated many attempts at a solution. = 这个问题已经让许多尝试解决的努力都失败了。


过去完成时:
They had been frustrated by repeated failures. = 他们因为反复的失败而感到沮丧。
I had already been frustrated by similar problems. = 我已经被类似的问题弄得很沮丧了。


将来时:
The new rules will frustrate many people. = 新规会让很多人沮丧。
This delay will likely frustrate our plans. = 这次延误可能会打乱我们的计划。


虚拟语气:
If only the system weren't so frustrating! = 要是系统没那么令人沮丧就好了！
I wish this software wasn't so frustrating to use. = 我希望这个软件用起来没那么令人沮丧。


被动语态:
The students were frustrated by the difficult exam. = 学生们被那场困难的考试弄得很沮丧。
He was frustrated by the lack of progress. = 他因为缺乏进展而感到沮丧。


疑问句:
What frustrates you the most about this job? = 你在这份工作中最沮丧的是什么？
Does anything frustrate you about this project? =  关于这个项目，有什么事情让你沮丧吗？


否定句:
The easy instructions didn't frustrate him at all. = 简单的说明一点也没让他沮丧。
Nothing frustrated her more than incompetence. = 没有什么比无能更让她沮丧了。
