calculation:/ˌkælkjuˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 计算，估算，推算 | The process of using mathematics to find an answer to a problem; a mathematical operation or process.  It can also refer to a careful plan or consideration of the possible results of something. | Example: The calculation of the rocket's trajectory was complex. 火箭轨迹的计算非常复杂。


Example:  His business success was due to careful calculation and planning.  他的商业成功是由于仔细的计算和计划。 |近义词：computation, reckoning, estimation |反义词：guess, approximation |词性:名词
