diphthongising: /ˈdɪpθɒŋaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 双元音化；使成双元音 | The process of changing a simple vowel sound into a diphthong.  This often happens in language evolution. For example, the sound in the word "price" in some dialects has diphthongised to become a sound similar to "proice". | The pronunciation of the word "fire" is diphthongising in some regions, resulting in a longer, more drawn-out sound. = 在某些地区，“fire”一词的发音正在双元音化，导致声音更长，更拖沓。 The long 'a' sound in some words is diphthongising in modern English. = 现代英语中某些词语中的长元音“a”正在双元音化。| 近义词：vocalizing, diphthongization | 反义词：monophthongizing
