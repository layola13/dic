incorporation:/ˌɪnkɔːrpəˈreɪʃən/ | n. |合并；公司；结合 |The act of incorporating or the state of being incorporated.  This can refer to the formation of a company, the inclusion of something into a larger whole, or the combining of different things into a single entity. |例句：The incorporation of new technology improved efficiency. 新技术的融入提高了效率。

例句：The incorporation of the small business into the larger company was completed last week. 小企业并入大公司已于上周完成。


近义词：combination, merger, integration, consolidation | 反义词：separation, division, segregation | 词性：n.
