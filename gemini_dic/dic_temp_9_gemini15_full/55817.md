divest:/daɪˈvɛst/ | vt. |剥夺；脱去；使…摆脱；|To deprive someone of something, especially power, rights, or possessions; to rid oneself of something.|例句：The company divested itself of its unprofitable subsidiaries.  公司剥离了其无利可图的子公司。|例句：He divested himself of his winter coat. 他脱掉了冬衣。|近义词：deprive, strip, rid, relinquish |反义词：invest, acquire, endow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company divests itself of its non-core assets. = 公司剥离其非核心资产。
He divests his anger slowly. = 他慢慢地平息怒火。


一般过去时:
She divested herself of her responsibilities. = 她卸下了自己的责任。
The government divested the company of its license. = 政府吊销了该公司的执照。


现在完成时:
They have divested themselves of their old beliefs. = 他们已经放弃了旧有的信仰。
The corporation has divested many of its holdings. = 该公司已经剥离了许多资产。


过去完成时:
He had divested himself of all his worldly possessions before joining the monastery. = 在加入修道院之前，他已经放弃了他所有的世俗财产。
The country had divested itself of its colonial past. = 这个国家已经摆脱了它的殖民过去。


将来时:
The company will divest its failing product lines. = 公司将剥离其亏损的产品线。
She will divest herself of her old habits. = 她将改掉自己的坏习惯。


虚拟语气:
If the government divested the company of its monopoly, competition would increase. = 如果政府剥夺该公司的垄断地位，竞争将会加剧。
I wish I could divest myself of this burden. = 我真希望我能摆脱这个负担。


被动语态:
The company was divested of its assets by the court. = 该公司被法院剥夺了其资产。
He was divested of his power. = 他被剥夺了权力。


疑问句:
Will the company divest itself of its overseas operations? = 公司会剥离其海外业务吗？
Has the government divested the company of its license? = 政府是否吊销了该公司的执照？


否定句:
The company did not divest itself of its unprofitable division. = 公司没有剥离其无利可图的部门。
He wouldn't divest himself of his principles. = 他不会放弃自己的原则。


PHRASE->
divest oneself of = 摆脱；放弃
divest...of = 剥夺…；使…摆脱
divest in = 撤资
divest from = 剥离；撤资
divest of power = 夺权
