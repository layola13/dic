overmortgage:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrɡɪdʒ/| n. |过度抵押，超额抵押 |The act of borrowing more money on a property than its worth.  This can lead to financial difficulties if the property value decreases. |例句：He was facing foreclosure after an overmortgage left him owing more than his house was worth. 他在过度抵押后面临着丧失抵押品赎回权的风险，因为他欠的钱比房子的价值还要多。|近义词：excessive mortgage, over-leveraging |反义词：undermortgage (not a common word)
