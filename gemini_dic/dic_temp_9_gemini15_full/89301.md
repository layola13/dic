homoeostases:/ˌhoʊmiəˈsteɪsɪz/| n. |体内平衡状态（复数）|The maintenance of a stable internal environment within an organism despite changes in the external environment.  Homeostasis refers to the ability of an organism to maintain internal stability.  Homoeostases is the plural form, indicating multiple instances of this stable state.  |Example:  The body's homeoestases are crucial for survival. 人体的体内平衡状态对生存至关重要。 The various homeoestases of the human body work together to maintain overall health. 人体各种体内平衡状态共同作用以维持整体健康。|近义词：equilibrium, balance, stability|反义词：disequilibrium, instability
