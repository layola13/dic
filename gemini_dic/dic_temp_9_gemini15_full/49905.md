demystifier: /dɪˈmɪstɪfaɪər/ | n. | 解释者；使易于理解的人或事物 |  Someone or something that makes something easier to understand, especially by explaining complex or mysterious concepts in a simple way. | The book serves as a demystifier of complex scientific concepts. 这本书充当了复杂科学概念的解释者。|近义词：explainer, elucidator, clarifier |反义词：obscurer, confuser


USAGE->

The scientist demystifies the complex experiment for the students. = 科学家为学生解释了复杂的实验。
This website demystifies the process of applying for a visa. = 这个网站解释了申请签证的过程。


一般过去时:
