ganger:/ˈɡæŋər/| n.|监工；工头；一队工人|A ganger is a person who supervises a group of workers, especially in road construction or other manual labor.  The term is primarily British English.|例句：The ganger directed the workers to lay the paving stones. 监工指挥工人铺设铺路石。|例句：A team of gangers was needed to complete the project on time. 需要一个监工团队才能按时完成项目。|近义词：foreman, supervisor, overseer|反义词：worker, laborer|名词
