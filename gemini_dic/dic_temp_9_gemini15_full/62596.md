enfeeblish:/ɪnˈfiːblɪʃ/| vt.|使衰弱；使虚弱|To weaken or enervate (someone or something).  The long illness had enfeeblished him.|The prolonged drought enfeeblished the crops. 长期干旱使庄稼枯萎。|近义词：weaken, debilitate, enervate|反义词：strengthen, invigorate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The illness enfeebles him. = 这场病使他衰弱。
Hard work enfeebles the body. =  努力工作会使身体虚弱。


一般过去时:
The heat enfeeblished the soldiers. = 炎热使士兵们虚弱。
The long journey enfeeblished the travelers. = 漫长的旅程使旅行者们疲惫不堪。


现在完成时:
The drought has enfeeblished the land. = 干旱已经使这片土地贫瘠。
Years of overwork have enfeeblished his health. = 多年的过度工作已经损害了他的健康。


过去完成时:
The prolonged starvation had enfeeblished the refugees. = 长期的饥饿使难民们虚弱不堪。
He had been enfeeblished by years of poor diet. = 多年不良的饮食习惯使他体弱多病。


将来时:
Lack of exercise will enfeeblish his muscles. = 缺乏运动会使他的肌肉无力。
The continued stress will enfeeblish her immune system. = 持续的压力会削弱她的免疫系统。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't been so ill, the surgery wouldn't have enfeeblished him so much. = 如果他不那么病重，手术就不会让他虚弱到如此程度。
If she had eaten properly, the illness wouldn't have enfeeblished her so quickly. = 如果她吃得好，病就不会这么快就让她虚弱了。


被动语态:
He was enfeeblished by the disease. = 他被疾病折磨得虚弱不堪。
The crops were enfeeblished by the frost. = 庄稼被霜冻损害了。


疑问句:
Has the illness enfeeblished him significantly? = 这场病是否严重地削弱了他？
Will the treatment enfeeblish his already weakened heart? = 治疗会进一步削弱他本已虚弱的心脏吗？


否定句:
The medicine did not enfeeblish him; instead, it strengthened him. = 这药并没有使他虚弱，反而使他强壮了。
The healthy diet didn't enfeeblish her; it made her stronger. = 健康的饮食并没有使她虚弱，反而让她更强壮了。

enfeeblish the will = 削弱意志
