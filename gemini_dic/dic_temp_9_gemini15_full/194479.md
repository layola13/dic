self-restricted:/ˌsɛlf rɪˈstrɪktɪd/| adj.|自我限制的，自我约束的|Something that is self-restricted is limited or controlled by the person or thing itself, rather than by external forces.  It implies a voluntary limitation or constraint.|例句：His self-restricted diet consisted only of fruits and vegetables.  他的饮食自我限制，只吃水果和蔬菜。|例句：The company's self-restricted growth strategy prevented it from expanding too rapidly.  公司的自我限制增长战略阻止了它过快扩张。|近义词：self-imposed, voluntary, limited, constrained|反义词：unrestricted, unlimited, free, unrestrained|形容词
