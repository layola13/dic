nonimpulsiveness:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpʌlsɪvnəs/| n. |非冲动性，克制；自制力 |The absence of impulsiveness; the tendency to think before acting.  It is a personality trait characterized by careful consideration and planning before making decisions or taking actions. |Example:  Her nonimpulsiveness saved her from making a costly mistake.  她的克制使她避免犯下代价高昂的错误。|近义词：self-control, restraint, deliberation, forethought |反义词：impulsiveness, recklessness, rashness
