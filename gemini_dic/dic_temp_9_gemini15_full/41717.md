corroboree:/ˌkɒrəˈbɔːriː/| n. |土著歌舞表演；集会|A corroboree is a type of ceremony or gathering of Aboriginal Australians, often involving dancing, singing, and storytelling, typically performed for ceremonial, social, or religious purposes. It is a significant part of Aboriginal culture and traditions.|The tribe held a corroboree to celebrate the harvest.  这个部落举行了一场歌舞表演来庆祝丰收。|gathering, ceremony, festival|dispute, argument
