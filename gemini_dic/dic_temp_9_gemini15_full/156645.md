picture-book:/ˈpɪktʃəbʊk/ | n. |图画书 | A book for children with many pictures and a simple story.  |Example: My daughter loves reading picture books before bedtime. 我的女儿喜欢睡前读图画书。|近义词：illustrated book, storybook |反义词：novel, textbook
