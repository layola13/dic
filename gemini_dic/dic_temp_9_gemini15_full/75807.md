fumerell:/ˌfjuːməˈrɛl/ | n. | 喷气孔；小烟囱；火山喷气孔 | A small vent or opening in the ground from which steam or gases escape.  Often associated with volcanic activity. | 例句：The fumerelle emitted a plume of sulfurous steam.  喷气孔喷出一股硫磺蒸汽。

The fumerelles on the volcano's slopes were a spectacular sight. 火山山坡上的喷气孔景象壮观。| 近义词：steam vent, fumarole | 反义词：无
