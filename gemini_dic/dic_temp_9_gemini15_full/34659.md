clergies:/ˈklɜːrdʒi/| n.|神职人员；牧师阶层|The body of ordained clergy in a religion.  It refers to the collective group of people who are ordained to perform religious duties.|例句：The local clergies organized a charity event. 当地神职人员组织了一场慈善活动。

例句：The clergies gave a sermon on Sunday. 神职人员在周日做了一场布道。


近义词：ministers, priests, pastors, rabbis|反义词：laypeople, laity
