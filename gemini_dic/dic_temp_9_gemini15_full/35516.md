coag:/koʊˈæɡ/ | v. | 凝结；凝固 | To change from a liquid to a solid or semi-solid state.  This typically refers to the process of blood clotting or the thickening of a liquid. | Example: The blood coagulated quickly after the injury.  血液受伤后迅速凝固。The milk began to coagulate in the heat. 牛奶在高温下开始凝结。|近义词：clotting, thickening, congealing | 反义词：liquefying, thinning, dissolving | 动词
