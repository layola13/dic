phytochemistry:/ˌfaɪtoʊˈkɛmɪstri/ | n. | 植物化学 | The scientific study of phytochemicals, which are chemicals produced by plants.  It encompasses the identification, quantification, and biological activity of these compounds. | Example:  Phytochemistry research has led to the discovery of many new drugs derived from plants. 植物化学研究导致了许多源于植物的新药的发现。 | 近义词：plant biochemistry, botanical chemistry | 反义词：无
