captivation: /ˌkæptɪˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 迷恋；吸引；着迷 | The state of being captivated; the act of captivating.  The power to fascinate or charm. | 例句：The captivation of the audience was evident in their rapt attention. 观众全神贯注，可见他们被深深吸引。

The captivating performance left a lasting impression on the audience.  扣人心弦的表演给观众留下了深刻的印象。| 近义词：fascination, enchantment, charm, allure | 反义词：boredom, repulsion, indifference | 名词
