exhibitive:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪtɪv/| adj.|炫耀的；卖弄的；展览的 | Tending to exhibit or show off; characterized by exhibitionism.  It can also describe something related to or used for exhibition. |例句：His exhibitive behavior at the party made everyone uncomfortable. 他在派对上炫耀的行为让每个人都感到不舒服。

例句：The museum's exhibitive collection of ancient artifacts was truly impressive. 博物馆展出的古代文物收藏令人印象深刻。


近义词：showy, ostentatious, demonstrative, display | 反义词：modest, reserved, unassuming, retiring | 词性：形容词


词源:  From exhibit + -ive.  "Exhibit" comes from the Latin *exhibere, meaning "to hold forth," "to offer," "to present," from *ex- "out" + *habēre "to have, hold."  The suffix "-ive" creates adjectives.
