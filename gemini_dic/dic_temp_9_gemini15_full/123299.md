monogeny:/məˈnɒdʒəni/ | n. | 单系发生；单一起源 | Monogeny refers to the evolutionary hypothesis that all members of a species are descended from a single common ancestor. This contrasts with polygeny, which suggests multiple origins.  | Example:  The monogeny of humankind is a long-debated topic in anthropology. 人类的单一起源是人类学中长期争论的话题。 | 近义词：unigeniture, single origin | 反义词：polygeny, multiple origins | 名词
