monsoon:/ˈmɑːnsuːn/ /ˈmɒnˌsuːn/| n.|季风|A monsoon is a seasonal prevailing wind in the region of South and Southeast Asia, blowing from the southwest between May and September, and from the northeast between October and April.|例句：The monsoon season brings heavy rains to India.  季风季节给印度带来了大量的降雨。|例句：Farmers rely on the monsoon rains for their crops. 农民依靠季风带来的雨水种植庄稼。|近义词：seasonal wind, rainy season|反义词：dry season
