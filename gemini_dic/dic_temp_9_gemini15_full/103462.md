job-hunting:/ˈdʒɒbˌhʌntɪŋ/| n.|求职，找工作|The act of searching for a job.  It involves activities like writing resumes, applying for positions, and attending interviews.|例句：Job-hunting can be a stressful but rewarding process. 求职可能是一个压力大但有回报的过程。|例句：He's been job-hunting for months without success. 他已经找了几个月的工作，但没有成功。|近义词：job searching, employment seeking|反义词：employment, being employed


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is job-hunting actively. = 他正在积极求职。
She job-hunts online every day. = 她每天都在网上找工作。


一般过去时:
I job-hunted extensively last year. = 我去年广泛地寻找工作。
They job-hunted in several cities. = 他们在几个城市找工作。


现在完成时:
She has been job-hunting since graduation. = 自毕业以来，她一直在找工作。
He has job-hunted for many companies. = 他已经为许多公司求职了。


过去完成时:
I had job-hunted for six months before I found this position. = 在找到这个职位之前，我已经找了六个月的工作。
They had job-hunted unsuccessfully before they decided to start their own business. = 在他们决定自己创业之前，他们已经不成功地求职了一段时间。


将来时:
I will be job-hunting after I finish my studies. = 我学业结束后会去找工作。
She will job-hunt in a different field next year. = 她明年将在不同的领域求职。


虚拟语气:
If I were job-hunting, I would focus on networking. = 如果我在求职，我会专注于人脉拓展。
I wish I hadn't wasted so much time job-hunting. = 我真希望我没有浪费那么多时间找工作。
