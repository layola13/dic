numpad:/ˈnʌmpæd/| n. |数字小键盘|A numeric keypad is a section of a keyboard that contains only number keys, typically arranged in a 3x4 grid, and is used for quick numerical input.  It often includes arithmetic operators (+, -, *, /) and sometimes other keys like Enter, Tab, and decimal point.  It's commonly found on desktop keyboards and is used for tasks requiring frequent numerical input, such as data entry, accounting, and programming.| Example:  I prefer using the numpad for entering large numbers quickly. 我更喜欢用数字小键盘快速输入大数字。|近义词：numeric keyboard, number pad|反义词：alphabetic keyboard
