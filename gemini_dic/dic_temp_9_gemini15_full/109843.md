lexicons:/ˈlɛksɪkɑːnz/ | n. | 词汇表；词典；词汇集 | A lexicon is a dictionary or vocabulary of a language. It can also refer to the vocabulary of a particular field or subject.  | Example:  The lexicon of linguistics is quite extensive.  语言学的词汇量相当大。 The software's lexicon included thousands of words. 该软件的词汇表包含数千个单词。 | 近义词：vocabulary, glossary, dictionary | 反义词：N/A
