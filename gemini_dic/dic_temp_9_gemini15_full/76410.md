gagged:/ɡæɡd/| v. | 阻止说话；塞住嘴；使窒息 | to prevent someone from speaking by stuffing their mouth; to stifle or suppress |The kidnappers gagged the victim to prevent him from screaming. 绑匪用东西堵住受害者的嘴以防止他尖叫。  He gagged on a piece of food. 他被一块食物噎住了。| stifled, silenced, muzzled |  unmuzzled, freed | 动词


USAGE->

He gags his opponents in every debate. 他在每次辩论中都压制他的对手。
They often gag their children when they are misbehaving.  当孩子们行为不当时，他们经常会阻止他们说话。
