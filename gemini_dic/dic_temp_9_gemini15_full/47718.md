decadences:/ˈdɛkədənsɪz/| n. |颓废；衰落；堕落 |The decadences of the late Roman Empire led to its eventual downfall.  罗马帝国晚期的颓废导致了它最终的衰落。|近义词：decline, decay, degeneration |反义词：progress, advancement, improvement

The city shows signs of decadence. = 这个城市显示出颓废的迹象。

过去完成时:
