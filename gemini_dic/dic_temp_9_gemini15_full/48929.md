defuel:/diːˈfjuːəl/| vt.| 去除燃料；排空燃料 | To remove fuel from something, especially an aircraft or vehicle.  |例句：The mechanics defueled the plane before maintenance.  机械师在维护飞机之前排空了飞机的燃料。

例句：The car needs to be defueled before it can be repaired. 这辆车需要排空燃油才能修理。


近义词：drain, empty


反义词：fuel, refuel


词性：动词
