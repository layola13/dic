diprotodon:/ˌdaɪprəˈtoʊdɒn/| n. |双门齿兽|An extinct genus of large marsupials belonging to the family Diprotodontidae,  characterized by two large lower incisors.  They were herbivores and lived in Australia during the Pleistocene epoch. |The diprotodon was one of the largest marsupials ever to exist.  |近义词：giant marsupial (broad term)|反义词：无
