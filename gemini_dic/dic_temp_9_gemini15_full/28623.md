caretaking:/ˈkɛərteɪkɪŋ/| n.|看护；照料；管理|The act of taking care of something or someone.  It often implies responsibility for the well-being or maintenance of something.|例句：The caretaking of the elderly is a demanding but rewarding job.  老年人的看护工作既费力又令人欣慰。|例句：The building's caretaking is handled by a professional management company.  大楼的管理由一家专业的管理公司负责。|近义词：supervision, maintenance, guardianship|反义词：neglect, abandonment|名词
