green-manures:/ˈɡriːnˈmænʊərz/| n.|绿肥|Plants grown specifically to be plowed under to enrich the soil.  Green manures improve soil fertility, structure, and water retention. They are a sustainable agricultural practice that reduces the need for chemical fertilizers.|例句：Farmers often use green manures like clover and alfalfa to improve soil health.|近义词：cover crops, soil enrichers|反义词：chemical fertilizers
