seeya:/ˈsiːjə/| int.|再见|A shortened, informal version of "see you."  Used as a casual farewell.|例句：Seeya later!  回头见！|近义词：bye, later, farewell, goodbye|反义词:hello


USAGE->
This word is an interjection and doesn't conjugate like verbs or nouns.  Therefore, grammatical tenses are not applicable.


PHRASE->
Seeya soon =  很快再见
Seeya around =  回头见
Seeya later =  回头见
Seeya tomorrow = 明天见
Catch ya later =  回头见 (similar meaning)
