reversionary: /rɪˈvɜːʒənəri/ | adj. | 可回复的；返还的；逆转的 | Relating to or of the nature of reversion; capable of reverting.  This adjective describes something that can return to a previous state or ownership, or that relates to the act of reverting.  | The reversionary interest in the property will pass to his heirs.  这处房产的可回复权益将转交给他的继承人。 | reverting, reverting,  returnable | irreversible, permanent
