reboiled:/ˌriːˈbɔɪld/ | v. | 再煮沸；重新煮沸 | To boil again.  This typically refers to boiling something that has already been boiled once, often to further cook it or to improve its consistency. | Example:  The chef reboiled the sauce to thicken it.  厨师再次煮沸酱汁以使其变浓。 |近义词：reheat, simmer again | 反义词：cool, freeze
