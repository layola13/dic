canonizations:/ˌkænənaɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n.|封圣；宣告为圣徒|The act of officially declaring someone a saint, typically by the Catholic Church.  This process involves a rigorous investigation into the candidate's life and miracles.|例句：The canonizations of several new saints took place in St. Peter's Square.  圣彼得广场举行了几位新圣徒的封圣仪式。|例句：The long-awaited canonizations brought joy to many devout Catholics.  期待已久的封圣仪式给许多虔诚的天主教徒带来了喜悦。|近义词：saint-making, beatification (a step towards canonization)|反义词：damnation, condemnation|名词
