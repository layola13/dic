needful:/ˈniːdful/| adj.|必要的，必需的|necessary, required, essential; something that is needed.|The needful preparations have been made.  必要的准备工作已经完成。


They did the needful to ensure the project's success. 他们做了必要的事情以确保项目的成功。


近义词：necessary, essential, requisite, required|反义词：unnecessary, superfluous, redundant
