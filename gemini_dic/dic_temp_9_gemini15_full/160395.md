popliteal:/pɒpˈlaɪtiəl/| adj.|腘的；膝弯处的| Relating to the popliteal space, the hollow at the back of the knee.  The popliteal artery and popliteal vein are located in this area.|例句：The doctor examined the popliteal fossa for any signs of swelling. 医生检查了腘窝是否有任何肿胀迹象。|例句：He felt a sharp pain in his popliteal region after the fall. 他摔倒后感觉膝盖后方剧烈疼痛。|近义词：posterior (to the knee), back-of-knee|反义词：anterior (to the knee), front-of-knee


词源：From Late Latin *popliteus, from Latin poples "ham, hollow of the knee".
