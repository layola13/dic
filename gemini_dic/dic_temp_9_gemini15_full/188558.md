ruinates:/ˈruːɪneɪts/| v.|毁坏；破坏；使破产 |To destroy or damage something severely; to bring to ruin.|例句：The storm ruined the crops.  暴风雨毁坏了庄稼。|例句：His gambling ruined him financially. 他的赌博使他破产了。|近义词：destroy, devastate, wreck, spoil |反义词：build, construct, repair, restore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain ruins the picnic every year. = 雨每年都会毁了野餐。
He constantly ruins his chances with his arrogance. = 他总是因为他的傲慢而毁掉自己的机会。

一般过去时:
The earthquake ruined the city. = 地震摧毁了这座城市。
The heavy snow ruined the harvest. = 大雪毁了收成。

现在完成时:
The scandal has ruined his reputation. = 这场丑闻毁了他的名声。
Years of neglect have ruined the building. = 多年的忽视毁了这栋建筑。

过去完成时:
The war had ruined the country before the peace treaty was signed. = 在和平条约签署之前，战争已经摧毁了这个国家。
He had ruined his health through years of overwork. = 他多年来过度劳累，毁了自己的健康。

将来时:
The flood will ruin the crops if it continues. = 如果洪水继续下去，将会毁掉庄稼。
If he keeps spending money like that, he will ruin himself. = 如果他继续那样花钱，他会毁掉自己。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't gambled, he wouldn't have ruined his life. = 如果他不赌博，他就不会毁掉自己的生活。
I wish the storm hadn't ruined our vacation. = 我真希望暴风雨没有毁了我们的假期。

被动语态:
The city was ruined by the earthquake. = 这座城市被地震摧毁了。
His career was ruined by his own mistakes. = 他的事业被他自己的错误毁了。

疑问句:
Will this rain ruin the party? = 这场雨会毁了派对吗？
Has the damage ruined the building beyond repair? = 损坏是否已经严重到无法修复这栋建筑物？

否定句:
The rain didn't ruin the picnic. = 雨并没有毁了野餐。
His actions didn't ruin his chances; he still has hope. = 他的行为并没有毁掉他的机会；他仍然有希望。


PHRASE->
ruin one's life = 毁掉某人的一生
ruin one's reputation = 毁掉某人的名声
ruin someone's chances = 毁掉某人的机会
go to ruin = 毁坏；衰败
beyond repair/ruin =  无法修复
