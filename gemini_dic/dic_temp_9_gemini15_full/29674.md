cathedral:/kəˈθiːdrəl/| n. |大教堂|A large and important church, typically one that is the official seat of a bishop or archbishop.|例句：The cathedral was a magnificent example of Gothic architecture.  这座大教堂是哥特式建筑的宏伟典范。|例句：We visited the Notre Dame Cathedral in Paris. 我们参观了巴黎的巴黎圣母院。|近义词：church, basilica, temple|反义词：chapel (smaller)
