grasp:/ɡræsp/ | vt. n. |抓住；理解；掌握；企图 | To take hold of something firmly with one's hand or hands. To understand something completely. |例句：He grasped the rope firmly. 他紧紧抓住绳子。She grasped the meaning of the poem. 她理解了这首诗的意思。|近义词：seize, grip, understand, comprehend |反义词：release, let go, misunderstand, misinterpret |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grasps the concept easily. = 他很容易理解这个概念。
I grasp the opportunity. = 我抓住机会。

一般过去时:
She grasped my hand tightly. = 她紧紧抓住我的手。
He grasped the situation quickly. = 他很快地把握住了局势。

现在完成时:
I have grasped the main points of the lecture. = 我已经掌握了讲座的主要内容。
They have grasped the importance of teamwork. = 他们已经抓住了团队合作的重要性。

过去完成时:
I had grasped the significance of the event before it happened. = 在事情发生之前，我已经意识到了事件的重要性。
She had grasped the opportunity to study abroad.  = 她抓住了出国留学的机会。

将来时:
He will grasp the chance to speak to the president. = 他将抓住机会与总统讲话。
I will grasp the new technology as soon as possible. = 我将尽快掌握这项新技术。

虚拟语气:
If I grasped the problem, I would solve it. = 如果我理解了这个问题，我就能解决它。
I wish I had grasped the opportunity earlier. = 我真希望我早点抓住那个机会。

被动语态:
The concept was easily grasped by the students. = 学生们很容易理解这个概念。
The opportunity was grasped by him. = 他抓住了机会。

疑问句:
Do you grasp the meaning of this sentence? = 你理解这句话的意思吗？
Did he grasp the significance of the situation? = 他理解了情况的重要性吗？

否定句:
I didn't grasp the instructions clearly. = 我没有清楚地理解指示。
He didn't grasp the opportunity to advance his career. = 他没有抓住提升事业的机会。
