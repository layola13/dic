overapprehensively: /ˌoʊvəræprɪˈhɛnsɪvli/ | adv. | 过分担忧地；过度焦虑地 | excessively apprehensive; with excessive worry or anxiety. |

例句：He overapprehensively checked the locks on all the doors and windows before going to bed. 他上床睡觉前过度焦虑地检查了所有门窗的锁。

例句：She overapprehensively awaited the doctor's diagnosis, imagining the worst possible outcome. 她过度焦虑地等待着医生的诊断，想象着最坏的结果。


近义词：nervously, anxiously, worriedly, fearfully, timidly

反义词：calmly, confidently, assuredly, serenely


词源:

* **over-**:  prefix indicating excess or to a great extent. (表示过度的、过分的)
* **apprehensive**: adjective meaning anxious or fearful that something bad or unpleasant will happen. (形容词，意思为焦虑的或害怕将会发生不好的或令人不愉快的事情)  This word itself comes from the Latin "apprehendere," meaning "to seize" or "to grasp,"  reflecting the sense of being mentally "grasped" by worry. (这个词本身来自拉丁语“apprehendere”，意思是“抓住”或“掌握”，反映了被焦虑“抓住”的感觉。)


用法说明：

overapprehensively is an adverb modifying a verb, describing how an action is performed.  It emphasizes the excessive nature of the apprehension. (overapprehensively是一个副词，修饰动词，描述一个动作是如何执行的。它强调了焦虑的过度性质。)  It's not a commonly used word, and more descriptive alternatives often exist. (这不是一个常用的词，通常存在更具描述性的替代词。)  Using simpler words like "nervously" or "anxiously" might be preferable in most writing. (在大多数写作中，使用“紧张地”或“焦虑地”等更简单的词可能更好。)
