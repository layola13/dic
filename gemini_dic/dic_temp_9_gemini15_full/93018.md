idiotical:/ɪˈdɪɒtɪkəl/| adj. |愚蠢的，白痴的，傻的 |Extremely foolish or stupid.  It suggests a lack of intelligence and common sense, often to a degree that is considered absurd or amusing. |例句：His idiotical plan to climb Mount Everest in flip-flops was quickly dismissed. 他的穿着人字拖攀登珠穆朗玛峰的愚蠢计划很快就被否决了。

例句：That was an idiotical thing to say.  那是句蠢话。


近义词：stupid, foolish, silly, asinine, imbecilic |反义词：intelligent, clever, sensible, wise
