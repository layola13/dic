defensible:/dɪˈfɛnsəbl/| adj.| 可辩护的；可防御的；站得住脚的 | Justifiable or able to be defended against attack or criticism.  |例句：His actions, while questionable, were ultimately defensible. 他的行为虽然值得怀疑，但最终是站得住脚的。

例句：The castle's position on the hill made it easily defensible. 这座城堡位于山顶，易守难攻。


近义词：justifiable, justifiable, valid, arguable, supportable

反义词： indefensible, unjustifiable, untenable
