handbrake:/ˈhændbreɪk/| n. |手刹，驻车制动器 |A handbrake is a lever or pedal in a vehicle that is used to lock the wheels and prevent the vehicle from rolling. It is typically used to hold the vehicle stationary on a hill or when parked.  |例句：The car rolled down the hill because the handbrake wasn't engaged.  这辆车因为手刹没有拉上而滚下了山。

The handbrake failed, causing the car to roll into a ditch. 手刹失灵，导致汽车滚入沟中。|近义词：parking brake, emergency brake |反义词：accelerator, gas pedal
