non-stick: /ˌnɒnˈstɪk/ | adj. | 不粘的；不沾的 |  Not allowing substances, especially food, to adhere to a surface.  This adjective describes surfaces treated to prevent sticking.  | Example: The non-stick pan made cooking eggs much easier.  不粘锅让煎蛋容易多了。  |近义词：nonstick, stick-resistant | 反义词：sticky, adhesive
