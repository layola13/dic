nonsuccessfully:/ˌnɒn.səkˈsɛsfʊli/ | adv. | 不成功地，未成功地 |  The adjective "unsuccessful" indicates a lack of success in achieving a desired outcome.  The adverb "nonsuccessfully" modifies a verb, describing how an action was performed without achieving its intended goal. |  例句：He tried nonsuccessfully to open the jammed door. 他试图打开卡住的门，但没有成功。

例句：The experiment was conducted nonsuccessfully, leading to further investigations. 实验进行得不成功，导致了进一步的调查。


近义词：unsuccessfully, without success, to no avail


反义词：successfully, triumphantly
