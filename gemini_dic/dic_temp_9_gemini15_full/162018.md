prayer:/ˈpreər/| n. |祈祷，祷告|A humble request made to God or a god, or a solemn act of communicating with God or a god, typically involving petition or thanksgiving.|例句：She offered a prayer for her sick child. 她为她生病的孩子祈祷。|例句：The priest led the congregation in prayer. 牧师带领会众祈祷。|近义词：supplication, invocation, entreaty |反义词：curse, blasphemy


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He prays every day. = 他每天祈祷。
She prays before meals. = 她饭前祈祷。

一般过去时:
They prayed for rain. = 他们祈祷下雨。
He prayed silently. = 他默默祈祷。

现在完成时:
I have prayed for guidance. = 我一直在祈祷寻求指引。
She has prayed for years for a cure. = 她多年来一直在祈祷能治愈。

过去完成时:
They had prayed for peace before the war started. = 战争开始前，他们曾祈祷和平。
She had prayed all night for her son's safety. = 她整夜为儿子的安全祈祷。


将来时:
We will pray for your success. = 我们会为你祈祷成功。
They will pray for a miracle. = 他们会祈祷奇迹发生。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would pray for forgiveness. = 如果我是你，我会祈祷得到宽恕。
I wish I had prayed harder. = 我希望我当时祈祷得更虔诚。


被动语态:
Prayers were offered for the victims. = 为受害者进行了祈祷。
A prayer was said before the meal. = 饭前作了祈祷。


疑问句:
Do you pray regularly? = 你定期祈祷吗？
What do you pray for? = 你祈祷什么？


否定句:
He doesn't pray often. = 他不常祈祷。
She didn't pray that night. = 她那天晚上没有祈祷。


PHRASE->

say a prayer = 做一个祈祷
