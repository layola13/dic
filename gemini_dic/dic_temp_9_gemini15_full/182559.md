reobligation:/ˌriːˌɒblɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 再承担义务；重新承诺 | The act of obligating oneself again; renewing a commitment or responsibility.  It often implies a previous obligation that has lapsed, been broken, or needs reaffirming. |例句：The company's reobligation to its environmental promises was met with public approval. 公司再次承诺其环保承诺受到了公众的赞扬。

The reobligation of the loan agreement requires both parties' signatures.  贷款协议的重新承诺需要双方签字。

近义词：recommitment, renewal, reaffirmation | 反义词：breach, abrogation, cancellation
