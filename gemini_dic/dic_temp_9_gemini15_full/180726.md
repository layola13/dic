reground:/riːˈɡraʊnd/| vt.|重新研磨；重新磨削；再次研磨|To grind again; to grind or mill a second time.  It often refers to the process of re-processing something that has already been processed, particularly in the context of coffee beans, spices, or other materials that need to be finely ground.|例句：The coffee beans were reground to achieve a finer consistency. = 咖啡豆被再次研磨以达到更细的稠度。
The old grinding wheel was used to regrind the dull knife. = 旧的磨轮被用来重新磨钝了的刀。|近义词：re-mill, reprocess|反义词：grind initially
