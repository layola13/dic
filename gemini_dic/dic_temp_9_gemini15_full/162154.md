pre-Hellenic: /ˌpriː hɛˈlɛnɪk/ | adj. | 希腊化之前的；前希腊的 |  Relating to the period in the Aegean region before the arrival of the Greeks, typically referring to the Cycladic, Minoan, and Mycenaean civilizations.  It describes cultures and artifacts predating the classical Greek era. | Example: Pre-Hellenic pottery often features distinctive geometric designs.  前希腊陶器通常具有独特的几何图案。 The archaeological findings suggest a rich pre-Hellenic culture flourished in this region. 考古发现表明，该地区曾繁荣存在着丰富的希腊化前文化。 | 近义词: pre-Greek, antecedent | 反义词: Hellenic, Greek
