fibril:/ˈfaɪbrɪl/| n.|原纤维；纤丝|A fibril is a small, thread-like structure, especially one found in muscle tissue or in nerve cells.|Example:  Muscle fibers are composed of numerous myofibrils.  肌肉纤维由许多肌原纤维组成。|近义词：filament, fiber|反义词：None.
