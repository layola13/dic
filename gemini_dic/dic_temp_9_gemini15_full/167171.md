pretasting: /priːˈteɪstɪŋ/ | v. | 预先品尝；预先体验 |  To taste or experience something beforehand, often in a small or symbolic way, to anticipate the full experience.  | Example:  The chef pre-tasted the sauce before serving it to the guests.  厨师在把酱汁端给客人之前先尝了尝。  | 近义词:  sampling, previewing, foretaste | 反义词:  aftertasting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pretastes the soup before adding more salt. 他在加盐之前先尝尝汤。
She pretastes the wine to assess its quality. 她先品尝一下葡萄酒以评估其质量。

一般过去时:
The baker pre-tasted the bread to check for doneness. 面包师预先品尝了面包以检查是否烤熟。
They pre-tasted the cake before deciding on the frosting. 他们在决定用什么糖霜之前先尝了尝蛋糕。


现在完成时:
She has pre-tasted all the dishes on the menu. 她已经预先品尝了菜单上的所有菜肴。
He has pre-tasted the coffee and adjusted the strength. 他已经预先品尝了咖啡并调整了浓度。


过去完成时:
They had pre-tasted the dessert before the party started. 派对开始前他们已经预先品尝了甜点。
She had pre-tasted the wine and determined it needed more time to breathe. 她已经预先品尝了葡萄酒，并确定它需要更多时间醒酒。


将来时:
The sommelier will pre-taste the wine before recommending it. 酒侍将在推荐葡萄酒之前先品尝一下。
I will pre-taste the chili to see if it needs more spice. 我将预先品尝辣椒，看看是否需要加更多香料。


虚拟语气:
If I had pre-tasted the food, I would have known it was undercooked. 如果我事先品尝了食物，我就知道它没煮熟。
I wish I had pre-tasted the curry, it was too spicy. 我真希望我事先尝过咖喱，它太辣了。


被动语态:
The sauce was pre-tasted by the head chef. 酱汁由主厨预先品尝。
The new recipe was pre-tasted by several volunteers. 新菜谱由几位志愿者预先品尝。


疑问句:
Did he pre-taste the food before serving it? 他在端上食物之前是否预先品尝过了？
Have you pre-tasted the cake batter? 你已经预先品尝过蛋糕糊了吗？


否定句:
They did not pre-taste the soup before serving it. 他们在上汤之前没有预先品尝。
She didn't pre-taste the cookies before putting them in the oven. 她把饼干放进烤箱之前没有先尝尝。
