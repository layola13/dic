reversal:/rɪˈvɜːrsəl/| n. |逆转；反转；颠倒|The change of something to its opposite or reverse.  A reversal of fortune can refer to a sudden change from good luck to bad luck, or vice versa.  It can also describe a change in direction or a process that reverses something previously done. |例句：The company experienced a dramatic reversal of fortune after the new CEO took over. 公司在新任CEO接手后经历了戏剧性的命运逆转。

例句：The court ordered a reversal of the original verdict. 法院命令推翻原判。


近义词：inversion, overturn, reversal of fortune, turnaround|反义词：continuation, progression, affirmation|词性：名词
