feasting:/ˈfiːstɪŋ/| n. |盛宴；宴会；狂欢 |The act of eating and drinking in a celebratory or abundant manner; a large meal, often with many guests.  It can also refer to a period of abundant eating and drinking. |例句：The wedding feast was a lavish affair, with food and drink in abundance. 婚礼盛宴是一场奢华的活动，食物和饮料非常丰富。

The holiday season is a time of feasting and merriment. 节假日是盛宴和欢乐的时节。


近义词：banqueting, revelry, carousing |反义词：fasting, starvation
