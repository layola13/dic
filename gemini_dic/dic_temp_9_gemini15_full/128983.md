nipperkin:/ˈnɪpərkɪn/| n. |小杯；少量|A nipperkin is a small cup, typically used for drinking ale or other alcoholic beverages.  The exact size varied historically, but it was generally understood to be a small measure. |The old tavern served ale in nipperkins. 老酒馆用小杯斟啤酒。 He only drank a nipperkin of wine. 他只喝了一小杯酒。| gill, noggin |  quart, pint | 名词
