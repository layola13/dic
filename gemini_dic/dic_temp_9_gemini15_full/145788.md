/ˌoʊvərˈdeɪt/ | v. | 过度争论；喋喋不休地辩论 | To engage in excessive or unnecessary debate or argument.  It often implies a fruitless or unproductive discussion. |  The committee overdebated the minor details, delaying the final decision.  委员会对细枝末节进行了过度争论，延误了最终决定。  They overdebated the issue until everyone was exhausted. 他们喋喋不休地争论这个问题，直到每个人都筋疲力尽。|  argue excessively, debate endlessly | agree, concur | 动词
