participant:/pɑːrˈtɪsɪpənt/| n. |参与者，参加者 |A participant is a person who takes part in something.  |例句：All participants in the study were over 65 years old.  所有参与这项研究的人都超过65岁。|例句：She was an active participant in the debate.  她是辩论中积极的参与者。|近义词：member, attendee, player, involved party|反义词：observer, spectator


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The participant completes the questionnaire. = 参与者填写问卷。
He is a regular participant in the marathon. = 他是马拉松赛的常客。


一般过去时:
The participant answered all the questions. = 参与者回答了所有问题。
She was a participant in the last election. = 她参加了上次选举。


现在完成时:
The participant has finished the task. = 参与者已经完成了任务。
He has been a participant in many projects. = 他已经参与了许多项目。


过去完成时:
The participant had already left before the meeting started. = 在会议开始之前，参与者已经离开了。
They had been participants in similar studies before. = 他们之前曾参与过类似的研究。


将来时:
The participant will present their findings tomorrow. = 参与者明天将展示他们的研究结果。
She will be a participant in the upcoming conference. = 她将参加即将举行的会议。


虚拟语气:
If he were a participant, he would have a different perspective. = 如果他是参与者，他会拥有不同的视角。
I wish I had been a participant in that exciting event. = 我真希望我参加了那场令人兴奋的活动。


被动语态:
The participant was asked to leave. = 参与者被要求离开。
The data from each participant was carefully analyzed. = 每个参与者的数据都被仔细分析了。


疑问句:
Is he a participant in this project? = 他是这个项目的参与者吗？
How many participants are there in the experiment? = 实验有多少参与者？


否定句:
She was not a participant in the crime. = 她不是犯罪的参与者。
He did not become a participant in the discussion. = 他没有参与讨论。


PHRASE->
