redesign:/ˌriːˈdɪzaɪn/| vt.|重新设计，改型|To design something again or in a different way.|例句：We need to redesign the website to make it more user-friendly. 我们需要重新设计网站，使其更易于用户使用。|例句：The architect redesigned the building to incorporate more natural light. 建筑师重新设计了建筑，以融入更多的自然光。|近义词：remodel, revamp, reconstruct|反义词：maintain, preserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They redesign the logo every year. = 他们每年都重新设计标志。
We redesign our marketing strategies regularly. = 我们定期重新设计我们的营销策略。


一般过去时:
She redesigned her garden last spring. = 她去年春天重新设计了她的花园。
The company redesigned its product packaging. = 公司重新设计了其产品包装。


现在完成时:
They have redesigned the website three times this year. = 他们今年已经三次重新设计了网站。
We have redesigned our entire product line. = 我们已经重新设计了我们的整个产品线。


过去完成时:
They had redesigned the software before the launch. = 他们在发布之前已经重新设计了软件。
The artist had redesigned the painting several times. = 艺术家已经几次重新设计了这幅画。


将来时:
They will redesign the app next month. = 他们下个月将重新设计应用程序。
We will redesign the office space next year. = 我们明年将重新设计办公空间。


虚拟语气:
If they redesigned the system, it would be more efficient. = 如果他们重新设计系统，它将会更高效。
I wish they had redesigned the website sooner. = 我希望他们早点重新设计网站。


被动语态:
The website was redesigned last year. = 网站去年被重新设计了。
The product is being redesigned to improve its functionality. = 该产品正在被重新设计以改进其功能。


疑问句:
Will the building be redesigned? = 建筑物会被重新设计吗？
Have they redesigned the curriculum? = 他们重新设计了课程吗？


否定句:
They did not redesign the car. = 他们没有重新设计汽车。
We haven't redesigned the brochure yet. = 我们还没有重新设计小册子。


PHRASE->
redesign a website = 重新设计一个网站
redesign a process = 重新设计一个流程
redesign a system = 重新设计一个系统
redesign a product = 重新设计一个产品
redesign a strategy = 重新设计一个策略
