game:/ɡeɪm/| n., v.|游戏，比赛；猎物；计划；骗局|A form of play or competition, often involving rules and a goal;  an instance of playing a game;  wild animals hunted for sport or food; a scheme or plan, often deceitful; to gamble or play a game.|例句：The children are playing a game of hide-and-seek.孩子们正在玩捉迷藏游戏。

例句：He won the game by a large margin. 他以很大的优势赢得了比赛。|近义词：match, contest, sport, play, trick|反义词：work, seriousness
