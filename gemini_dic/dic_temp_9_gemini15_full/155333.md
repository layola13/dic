pharmaceutically: /ˌfɑːrməˈsuːtɪkli/ | adj. | 制药的；药物的 | Relating to pharmacy or pharmaceuticals.  This adjective describes something related to the practice of pharmacy, the preparation and dispensing of medications, or the pharmaceutical industry itself.


例句：

The drug was tested pharmaceutically before being released to the public. = 该药物在公开发售前进行了药学测试。

Pharmaceutically active compounds are carefully regulated. = 药效化合物受到严格监管。


近义词：pharmaceutical, medicinal


反义词：None directly opposite, but words implying lack of medicinal properties could be considered contrasting, such as:  non-medicinal, inert.


词性: adjective
