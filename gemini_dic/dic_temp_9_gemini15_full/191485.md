schlepped:/ʃlɛpt/| v.|拖，搬运，费力地携带|To carry or drag something heavy or cumbersome, often implying difficulty or weariness. It often suggests a long or arduous journey.  |例句：I schlepped all the groceries home in one trip. = 我一次把所有杂货都拖回家了。
I schlepped my luggage across the airport. = 我费力地把行李拖过了机场。|近义词： lug, haul, drag, carry, transport|反义词：float, glide


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He schlepps his heavy backpack to school every day. = 他每天背着沉重的背包去上学。
She schlepps groceries home from the market. = 她从市场把杂货拖回家。


一般过去时:
We schlepped the furniture up three flights of stairs. = 我们费力地把家具搬上了三层楼。
He schlepped the boxes to the attic. = 他把箱子拖到阁楼上。


现在完成时:
I've schlepped this suitcase all over Europe. = 我把这个行李箱拖遍了整个欧洲。
She's schlepped her kids to every soccer practice this season. = 这个赛季她把她的孩子们拖到每一次足球训练。


过去完成时:
They had schlepped all the equipment to the campsite before nightfall. = 他们在夜幕降临之前把所有设备都拖到了营地。
She had schlepped the heavy books to the library before the deadline. = 她在截止日期之前把沉重的书拖到了图书馆。


将来时:
We will schlep the presents to the party tomorrow. = 我们明天要把礼物送到聚会上。
He will schlep the equipment to the construction site. = 他要把设备搬到建筑工地。


虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I wouldn't have to schlep all these bags. = 如果我有车，我就不用拖这么多包了。
I wish I didn't have to schlep all these boxes. = 我真希望不用拖这么多箱子。


被动语态:
The luggage was schlepped to the hotel by a porter. = 行李由搬运工搬到了酒店。
The heavy equipment was schlepped across the field. = 沉重的设备被拖过了田野。


疑问句:
Did he schlep the piano himself? = 他自己搬钢琴吗？
Will you schlep the groceries or shall I? = 你要搬杂货还是我搬？


否定句:
I didn't schlep the bags myself; I got help. = 我没有自己搬包，我找了人帮忙。
They didn't schlep the furniture; they hired movers. = 他们没有搬家具，他们雇了搬家公司。
