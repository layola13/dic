consolidates:/kənˈsɑːlɪdeɪts/| v.|巩固；加强；合并 |To make something stronger or more solid; to combine things into a single more effective unit.|The company consolidates its position in the market by acquiring its main competitor.  这家公司通过收购其主要竞争对手来巩固其在市场上的地位。|The government is attempting to consolidate its power. 政府正试图巩固其权力。|strengthen, reinforce, unify|weaken, divide, separate|动词
