rereviewing:/ˌriːrɪˈvjuːɪŋ/| v.|再次审查；重新审核|The act of reviewing something again.  It implies a more thorough or critical second look than a simple rereading.|例句：The committee is rereviewing the budget proposal. 委员会正在再次审查预算提案。
例句：After the initial rejection, the author decided to rereviewing his manuscript. 初稿被拒后，作者决定重新审核他的手稿。|近义词：reexamining, reconsidering, re-evaluating|反义词：accepting, approving

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are rereviewing the evidence. = 他们正在再次审查证据。
We rereview the project plan every month. = 我们每月都会重新审核项目计划。

一般过去时:
The judge rereviewed the case files. = 法官再次审查了案件文件。
She rereviewed her notes before the exam. = 考试前她重新复习了笔记。

现在完成时:
They have rereviewed the contract three times. = 他们已经三次重新审查了合同。
We have rereviewed the data and found a mistake. = 我们已经重新审核了数据，发现了一个错误。

过去完成时:
The lawyer had rereviewed the documents before the meeting. = 律师在会议前已经重新审查了文件。
They had rereviewed the proposal several times before submitting it. = 他们在提交提案之前已经多次重新审核了它。

将来时:
We will rereview the results next week. = 我们下周将重新审核结果。
The board will rereview the application. = 董事会将重新审核申请。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would rereview the entire project. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们会重新审核整个项目。
I wish they had rereviewed the data more carefully. = 我希望他们能更仔细地重新审核数据。

被动语态:
The proposal was rereviewed by the committee. = 提案被委员会重新审核了。
The evidence is being rereviewed by the investigators. = 证据正在被调查人员重新审查。

疑问句:
Will you rereview the document? = 你会重新审查这份文件吗？
Have they rereviewed the plan yet? = 他们已经重新审核计划了吗？

否定句:
They did not rereview the evidence properly. = 他们没有正确地重新审查证据。
We will not rereview the decision. = 我们不会重新审查这个决定。
