payrolled: /ˈpeɪroʊld/ | adj. | 上了工资单的；领工资的 |  Included on a company's payroll; employed.  | Example:  He's payrolled by a large multinational corporation. 他受雇于一家大型跨国公司。 | 近义词：employed, salaried, on the payroll | 反义词：unemployed, jobless


USAGE->

The company payrolls over 500 employees. = 这家公司雇佣了500多名员工。
He is payrolled by a tech firm. = 他受雇于一家科技公司。


一般过去时:
She was payrolled by the bank for ten years. = 她曾在银行工作十年。
They were payrolled at different times. = 他们受雇的时间不同。

He has been payrolled by the same company since graduation. = 自毕业以来，他一直在这家公司工作。
