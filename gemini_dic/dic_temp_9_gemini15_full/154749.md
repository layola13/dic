persuasions:/pərˈsweɪʒənz/| n. |劝说；说服；信仰；见解 |The act of persuading someone or of being persuaded; the state of being persuaded.  It can also refer to the beliefs or opinions that someone holds. |例句：His persuasions were strong enough to change her mind.  他的劝说足以改变她的主意。

例句：Her religious persuasions are deeply held.她的宗教信仰根深蒂固。


近义词：convictions, beliefs, arguments, inducements |反义词：discouragement, dissuasion


词源：源自古法语 *persuasion，来自拉丁语 persuasio,  persuadere 的名词形式，意思是“劝说，说服”。persuadere 由 per- (完全地) + suadere (劝说)构成。
