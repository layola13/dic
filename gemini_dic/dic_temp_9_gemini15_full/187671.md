rooftop:/ˈruːftɑːp/| n. |屋顶；屋顶平台|A rooftop is the top surface of a building, often used as a terrace or for other purposes.  It's the surface forming the upper covering of a building.|例句：We had a party on the rooftop. 我们在屋顶上开了个派对。

例句：The rooftop garden was full of beautiful flowers. 屋顶花园里开满了漂亮的花。


近义词：roof, top, terrace


反义词：basement, foundation


词性:名词
