gangsta:/ˈɡæŋstə/| n. adj.|匪徒；歹徒；黑帮成员；（非正式）非常酷的；炫酷的|A gangsta is a member of a criminal gang, often involved in violent and illegal activities.  The term is often used in hip-hop culture to refer to someone who is tough, cool, and rebellious, even if they are not actually involved in criminal activity. |例句：He portrayed a ruthless gangsta in the movie. 他在电影中扮演了一个冷酷无情的匪徒。|例句：That outfit is so gangsta! 那套衣服太酷了！|近义词：thug, gangster, hoodlum, criminal|反义词：law-abiding citizen, goody-two-shoes
