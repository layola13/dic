cassino:/kəˈsiːnoʊ/ | n. |卡西诺（纸牌游戏）|A card game played by two or more players, using a standard deck of cards. The objective is to be the first player to get rid of all their cards.  | Example: We played a game of cassino last night.  昨晚我们玩了一局卡西诺。 | 近义词：canasta (similar card game) | 反义词：无 | 名词
