self-deceives:/ˌsɛlf dɪˈsiːvz/ | v. | 自欺欺人 | To deceive oneself; to believe something false about oneself.  This involves a conscious or unconscious effort to avoid unpleasant truths about oneself. | Example: He self-deceives himself into believing he's a great musician, despite a lack of talent. 他自欺欺人地认为自己是一位伟大的音乐家，尽管他缺乏天赋。|近义词：delude, mislead, fool oneself | 反义词：be honest with oneself, face reality | 动词
