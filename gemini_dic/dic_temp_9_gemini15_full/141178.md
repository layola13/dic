ooh-and-aah:/uːændɑː/| int.|啧啧称奇声，赞叹声|表示对某事物感到惊奇或赞叹的声音，通常用于非正式场合。|例句：The audience went "ooh-and-aah" at the magician's trick. 观众对魔术师的戏法发出了“啧啧称奇”的声音。|近义词：wow, amazing, incredible|反义词：blah, boring


USAGE->
This word is an interjection, so it doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".  Interjections don't follow typical grammatical tense structures.


PHRASE->
ooh and aah over something: 对某事啧啧称奇
ooh and aah at something: 对某事赞叹不已
the ooh-and-aah factor:  令人惊叹的因素
to elicit oohs and aahs:  博得赞叹
full of oohs and aahs:  充满赞叹声
