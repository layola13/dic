communicator: /kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtər/ | n. |交流者；沟通者；传播者 | A person who communicates effectively;  a device or system that facilitates communication.  |例句：He's a skilled communicator, able to explain complex ideas simply. 他是一位熟练的沟通者，能够简单地解释复杂的想法。

例句：This new software is a powerful communicator, allowing instant messaging across continents.  这款新软件是一款强大的沟通工具，允许跨大陆的即时通讯。

近义词：speaker, envoy, messenger, transmitter

反义词：isolator, obstructer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He communicates effectively with his team. = 他有效地与他的团队沟通。
She communicates her ideas clearly. = 她清晰地表达她的想法。


一般过去时:
He communicated the news to his family. = 他把这个消息告诉了他的家人。
She communicated her concerns to her boss. = 她向老板表达了她的担忧。


现在完成时:
She has communicated effectively throughout the project. = 在整个项目中，她一直有效地进行沟通。
He has communicated his intentions to the stakeholders. = 他已经向利益相关者传达了他的意图。


过去完成时:
They had communicated their plans before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经沟通了他们的计划。
She had communicated her dissatisfaction several times. = 她已经多次表达了她的不满。


将来时:
He will communicate the results tomorrow. = 他明天将公布结果。
She will communicate her decision soon. = 她很快就会宣布她的决定。


虚拟语气:
If he communicated more clearly, people would understand him better. = 如果他沟通更清晰，人们会更好地理解他。
I wish she had communicated her needs earlier. = 我希望她早点表达她的需求。


被动语态:
The information was communicated to the public. = 信息已向公众公布。
The message was communicated clearly and concisely. = 信息清晰简洁地传达了。


疑问句:
How does he communicate his ideas? = 他如何表达他的想法？
What methods does she use to communicate with clients? = 她使用什么方法与客户沟通？


否定句:
He didn't communicate his feelings adequately. = 他没有充分表达他的感情。
She failed to communicate the importance of the deadline. = 她未能传达截止日期的重要性。
