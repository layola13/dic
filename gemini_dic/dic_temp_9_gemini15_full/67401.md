externalised:/ˈɛkˌstɜːrnəlaɪzd/| adj.|外部化的；外部处理的|Transferred or shifted outside of a system or organization.|The company externalised its IT department, hiring a third-party provider.  这家公司将其IT部门外包给了一个第三方供应商。|近义词：outsourced, offshored|反义词：internalised, in-sourced|形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company externalises its manufacturing. = 公司将制造业外包。
They externalise their accounting processes. = 他们将会计流程外包。


一般过去时:
We externalised the project to a consultant. = 我们将项目外包给了一位顾问。
The firm externalised its customer service. = 该公司将其客户服务外包。


现在完成时:
They have externalised many of their operations. = 他们已经将许多业务外包。
The organization has externalised its data storage. = 该组织已将数据存储外包。


过去完成时:
They had externalised their IT before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们已经将其IT外包。
The company had externalised its research and development. = 该公司已将其研发外包。


将来时:
We will externalise the data management next year. = 我们明年将把数据管理外包。
They will externalise their marketing campaign. = 他们将把他们的营销活动外包。


虚拟语气:
If we externalised the project, we would save money. = 如果我们将项目外包，我们将节省资金。
It would be better if they externalised the difficult tasks. = 如果他们把困难的任务外包出去会更好。


被动语态:
The tasks were externalised to a specialized agency. = 任务被外包给了一个专门的机构。
The production process is being externalised to a cheaper location. = 生产过程正在被外包到一个更便宜的地方。


疑问句:
Has the company externalised its logistics? = 公司是否将其物流外包？
Will they externalise the development of the software? = 他们会将软件开发外包吗？


否定句:
We did not externalise the project; we did it ourselves. = 我们没有将项目外包；我们自己做的。
They will not externalise their core business functions. = 他们不会将其核心业务职能外包。


PHRASE->
externalised costs 外包成本
externalised risk 外包风险
highly externalised 高度外包的
externalised services 外包服务
externalised production 外包生产

词源:

The word "externalised" is derived from the verb "externalise" which combines the prefix "extern-" (meaning "outside" or "external") with the suffix "-ise" (a verb-forming suffix indicating to make or cause to be).  The word therefore literally means "to make external" or "to move outside."
