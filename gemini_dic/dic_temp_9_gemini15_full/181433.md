rejectable:/rɪˈdʒɛktəbəl/| adj.| 可拒绝的；不可接受的 |  Something that is rejectable is considered unacceptable or unsatisfactory and can be refused or rejected.  It implies a lack of suitability or quality that makes it worthy of rejection. | Example: His proposal was rejectable due to its unrealistic budget. 他的提案因预算不切实际而被拒绝。

The design was rejectable because it didn't meet safety standards.  这个设计因为不符合安全标准而被拒绝。|近义词：unacceptable, undesirable, unsatisfactory, refutable |反义词：acceptable, desirable, satisfactory
