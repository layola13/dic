reactivate:/ˌriˈæktɪveɪt/| vt.|重新启动；使重新活跃；使复活|To make something active again; to start something again after it has been stopped or inactive.|例句：The company reactivated its marketing campaign after a period of inactivity. 公司在一段时间的停滞后重新启动了其营销活动。
例句：They reactivated the dormant volcano monitoring system. 他们重新启动了休眠的火山监测系统。|近义词：restart, revive, renew|反义词：deactivate, inactivate, terminate|动词
