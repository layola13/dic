intoxicating: /ɪnˈtɒksɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |令人陶醉的；使人兴奋的；醉人的 |  causing a feeling of great happiness and excitement; causing intoxication. | 例句：The intoxicating aroma of freshly baked bread filled the kitchen.  令人陶醉的新鲜烘焙面包的香味充满了厨房。

The intoxicating rhythm of the music made everyone want to dance.  音乐令人陶醉的节奏让每个人都想跳舞。


近义词：exhilarating, captivating, enthralling, heady, stimulating | 反义词：sobering, dull, boring, monotonous
