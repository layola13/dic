food-cycle:/ˈfuːdˌsaɪkəl/ | n. |食物循环，食物链 | The food cycle describes the flow of energy and nutrients through an ecosystem, from producers to consumers to decomposers.  食物循环描述了能量和营养物质在生态系统中的流动，从生产者到消费者再到分解者。|近义词：nutrient cycle, food chain, ecological cycle |反义词：None
