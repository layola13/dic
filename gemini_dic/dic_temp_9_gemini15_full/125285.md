multiflagellated:/ˌmʌltiˈflædʒəleɪtɪd/| adj.|多鞭毛的|Having many flagella.  Flagella are whip-like appendages that enable motility in certain cells and microorganisms.| Example:  Many types of bacteria are multiflagellated, allowing them to move rapidly through their environment.  许多种细菌都是多鞭毛的，这使它们能够快速地在环境中移动。|近义词：polyflagellate|反义词：monoflagellated, aflagellate
