howlers:/ˈhaʊlərz/| n.|严重的错误，大笑，（尤指）令人尴尬的错误| a serious mistake, especially one that is obvious and embarrassing.  |例句：His essay was full of howlers.他的文章里充满了低级错误。

例句：The speaker made a howler when he mispronounced the president's name. 演讲者误读了总统的名字，犯了一个令人尴尬的错误。


近义词：blunder, mistake, error, gaffe
