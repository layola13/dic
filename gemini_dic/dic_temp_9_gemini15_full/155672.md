phloem:/ˈfloʊɛm/| n. |韧皮部|The phloem is the living tissue in vascular plants that transports sugars and other organic nutrients from the leaves to other parts of the plant.|例句：The phloem transports sugars from the leaves to the roots. 韧皮部将糖分从叶子运输到根部。|近义词：bast|反义词：xylem


USAGE->
This word is a noun and does not conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, typical tense examples are not applicable.  Usage examples will focus on different sentence structures and contexts.

Example Sentences:


Simple Sentence:
The phloem is essential for plant growth.  韧皮部对植物生长至关重要。


Complex Sentence:
Because the phloem was damaged, the plant couldn't transport nutrients effectively. 因为韧皮部受损，植物无法有效运输养分。


Compound Sentence:
The xylem transports water, and the phloem transports sugars. 木质部运输水分，韧皮部运输糖分。


Passive Voice:
The sugars are transported throughout the plant by the phloem.  糖分由韧皮部输送到植物的各个部分。
