fluency:/ˈfluːənsi/ | n. |流利，流畅 |The ability to speak or write a language easily and smoothly.  It often implies not only grammatical correctness but also a natural rhythm and flow.  |例句：Her fluency in Spanish impressed everyone.  她的西班牙语说得非常流利，给每个人留下了深刻印象。

例句：He spoke with remarkable fluency, considering he'd only been studying the language for a year. 考虑到他只学习了一年的语言，他的口语表达非常流畅，令人惊叹。


近义词：facility, smoothness, eloquence |反义词：hesitation, stammering, halting
