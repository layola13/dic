consequence:/ˈkɒnsɪkwəns/ /ˈkɑːnsɪkwɛns/| n. |结果，后果，影响 |The natural consequence of this policy is unemployment.这项政策的自然结果是失业。  The consequences of his actions were severe. 他行为的后果很严重。|result, outcome, effect |cause, reason |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The consequence of his actions is clear. = 他行为的结果很清楚。
What are the consequences of this decision? = 这个决定的后果是什么？


一般过去时:
The consequences of the storm were devastating. = 暴风雨的后果是毁灭性的。
What were the consequences of the war? = 战争的后果是什么？


现在完成时:
The consequences of climate change have been far-reaching. = 气候变化的后果是深远的。
What consequences have your actions had? = 你的行为带来了什么后果？


过去完成时:
The consequences of his earlier mistakes had caught up with him. = 他早先错误的后果已经追上他了。
What consequences had they foreseen before the project began? = 项目开始前他们预见了什么后果？


将来时:
The consequences of this decision will be felt for years to come. = 这个决定的后果将在未来几年内感受到。
What consequences will this policy have on the environment? =这项政策对环境会产生什么后果？


虚拟语气:
If he had been more careful, the consequences would have been less severe. = 如果他更小心点，后果就不会那么严重。
I wish the consequences weren't so dire. = 我希望后果不要那么可怕。


被动语态:
The consequences were felt by everyone in the community. = 后果是社区中的每个人都感到的。
The consequences are being carefully considered. = 后果正在被仔细考虑。


疑问句:
What are the consequences of ignoring this warning? = 忽视这个警告的后果是什么？
What consequences might arise from this situation? =这种情况可能会产生什么后果？


否定句:
There were no serious consequences. = 没有严重的后果。
His actions did not have any negative consequences. = 他的行为没有任何负面后果。
