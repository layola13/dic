perennially:/pəˈreniəli/ | adv. | 长期地；反复地；永久地 |  Continuously or repeatedly over a long period; year after year. | 例句：The garden is perennially popular with visitors. 花园长期以来一直深受游客欢迎。
例句：He perennially complains about the weather. 他总是抱怨天气。| 近义词：constantly, continually, perpetually, incessantly | 反义词：occasionally, intermittently, sporadically | 词性：副词

词源:
