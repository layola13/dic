reimplant:/ˌriːˈɪmplænt/| vt.|再植；重新种植|To plant or set again; to implant again.  The procedure involves surgically reattaching a detached body part, such as a tooth or limb, to its original location.|例句：The surgeons successfully reimplanted the patient's severed finger.  外科医生成功地为病人再植了被割断的手指。

例句：The dentist carefully reimplanted the child's knocked-out tooth. 牙医小心地为孩子再植了被打掉的牙齿。


近义词：replant, reinsert|反义词：remove, extract|词性:动词
