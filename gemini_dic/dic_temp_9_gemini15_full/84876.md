词源：

The word "hangman" is a compound word. It combines "hang," meaning to execute by hanging, with "-man," a suffix indicating a person who performs a particular task. The verb "hang" itself has Germanic origins, tracing back to Old English *hōn and Proto-Germanic *hōnan. The "-man" suffix is also of Old English origin.  Therefore, the word's origins reflect its function and historical context.
