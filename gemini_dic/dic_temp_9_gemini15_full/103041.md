jello:/ˈdʒɛloʊ/| n. |果冻，布丁|A gelatin dessert, typically sweet and fruit-flavored.  It's a jiggly, wobbly food made with gelatin, water, and sweeteners, often containing fruit, juice, or other flavorings.|例句：We had jello for dessert. = 我们吃果冻当甜点。

例句：The jello trembled slightly when she touched it. = 当她触摸果冻时，它微微颤抖。


近义词：gelatin dessert, jelly (in some regions), wobbly dessert|反义词：solid food, firm food|名词
