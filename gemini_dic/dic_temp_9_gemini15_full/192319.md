scribbler:/ˈskrɪblər/| n. |涂鸦者；潦草书写者；拙劣的作家 | A scribbler is someone who writes quickly and carelessly, often producing messy or poorly written work.  They may also be a person who writes prolifically but without much skill or refinement. | Example: He was a prolific scribbler, churning out novels at an astonishing rate, but their quality was often questionable. 他是一位多产的潦草作家，以惊人的速度创作小说，但其质量常常令人质疑。|近义词：scrivener, scrawler, hack |反义词：skilled writer, meticulous writer
