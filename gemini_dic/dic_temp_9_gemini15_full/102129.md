isotropy:/aɪˈsɒtrəpi/| n. |各向同性；均匀性|The quality of being isotropic; having the same properties in all directions.|Example:  The material exhibits isotropy in its thermal conductivity.  这种材料的导热性具有各向同性。


Example: The amorphous structure of the glass leads to its isotropy. 玻璃的非晶态结构导致其各向同性。


近义词：homogeneity, uniformity


反义词：anisotropy


词性：名词
