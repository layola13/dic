sarcologic:/ˌsɑːrkəˈlɑːdʒɪk/| adj.| 肌肉学的；肉体的；与肌肉有关的 | Relating to or characteristic of the flesh or muscles. The term is often used in medical contexts to describe conditions, processes, or treatments related to the muscular system.  | Example:  Sarcologic studies are crucial for understanding muscular dystrophy.  肌肉学研究对于理解肌肉萎缩症至关重要。| 近义词：myologic, muscular | 反义词：non-muscular, skeletal (in some contexts)
