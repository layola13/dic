leafleting:/ˈliːflɪtɪŋ/| n. |散发传单；分发传单|The act of distributing leaflets, often for advertising or political purposes.  It involves handing out or posting printed materials to a wide audience.|例句：The political party engaged in extensive leafleting before the election.  这个政党在大选前进行了大规模的传单散发活动。|例句：Leafleting in the city center proved to be an effective way to reach potential customers. 在市中心散发传单被证明是接触潜在客户的有效途径。|近义词：handbilling, distributing, disseminating|反义词：N/A|名词

一般现在时:
They are leafleting the neighborhood. = 他们在附近散发传单。
We leaflet the town every Saturday. = 我们每个星期六都在镇上散发传单。

一般过去时:
They leafleted the streets yesterday. = 他们昨天在街上散发传单。
She leafleted the campus during lunch break. = 她在午休时间在校园里散发传单。

现在完成时:
They have leafleted the entire town. = 他们已经散发了整个镇的传单。
We have leafleted this area for three weeks. = 我们在这个区域散发传单已经三个星期了。

过去完成时:
They had leafleted the area before the rally. = 他们在集会前已经散发了该地区的传单。
She had leafleted all the houses on the street before it got dark. = 天黑之前，她已经散发了这条街上所有房子的传单。

将来时:
