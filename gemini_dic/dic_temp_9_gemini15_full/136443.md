nonreverent:/ˌnɒnˈrevərənt/| adj. |不敬的，无礼的 | Lacking reverence; disrespectful.  Showing a lack of respect for something considered sacred or important. |例句：His nonreverent remarks about the national anthem angered many.  他关于国歌的不敬言论激怒了许多人。

例句：The comedian's nonreverent jokes about religion offended some members of the audience.  这位喜剧演员关于宗教的不敬笑话冒犯了部分观众。


近义词：irreverent, disrespectful, impious, sacrilegious, blasphemous

反义词：reverent, respectful, pious, devout


词源:  The word is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "reverent" (meaning "feeling or showing deep respect").  "Reverent" ultimately derives from the Old French word "reverent," which comes from the Latin "revereri" meaning "to stand in awe of, to revere."
