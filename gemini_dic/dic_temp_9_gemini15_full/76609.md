gallivat:/ˈɡælɪvæt/| n.|轻型帆船，小艇|A gallivat is a type of small, flat-bottomed sailing vessel, often used in shallow waters or rivers.  It's typically propelled by sails but may also have oars. The term is now largely archaic.|例句：The merchant used a gallivat to transport goods along the coastal waterways. 商人用轻型帆船沿沿海水道运输货物。
The pirates attacked the merchant ship with their smaller, faster gallivats. 海盗用他们更小、更快的轻型帆船袭击了商船。|近义词：lighter, shallop, barge|反义词：large ship, ocean liner|名词
