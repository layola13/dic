merchandises:/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzɪz/| n. |商品，货物|Goods or wares; articles of trade.|The store merchandises a wide variety of products.|近义词：goods, wares, commodities, products|反义词：services


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store merchandises clothing and accessories. = 这家商店销售服装和配饰。
He merchandises his artwork online. = 他在网上销售他的艺术品。


一般过去时:
They merchandised their new line of toys successfully. = 他们成功地销售了他们新系列的玩具。
The company merchandised its products through television commercials. = 公司通过电视广告销售其产品。


现在完成时:
The company has merchandised its products globally. = 这家公司已在全球销售其产品。
They have merchandised their brand effectively. = 他们有效地推销了他们的品牌。


过去完成时:
Before the launch, they had merchandised similar products for years. = 在发布之前，他们已经销售类似产品多年了。
The retailer had merchandised that item before, but with little success. = 该零售商以前销售过该商品，但收效甚微。


将来时:
We will merchandise the new collection next month. = 我们下个月将销售新的系列产品。
The company will merchandise its products on social media platforms. = 公司将在社交媒体平台上销售其产品。


虚拟语气:
If they merchandised their products differently, they might have sold more. = 如果他们以不同的方式销售产品，他们可能会卖出更多。
I wish we had merchandised the product more aggressively. = 我希望我们当时能更积极地销售该产品。


被动语态:
The products were merchandised through various channels. = 产品通过各种渠道销售。
The new collection is being merchandised heavily this season. = 本季正在大力销售新系列产品。


疑问句:
How are the new products being merchandised? = 新产品是如何销售的？
Where are the products merchandised? = 产品在哪里销售？


否定句:
The company did not merchandise its products effectively. = 公司没有有效地销售其产品。
They didn't merchandise the product in the right market. = 他们没有在正确的市场销售该产品。
