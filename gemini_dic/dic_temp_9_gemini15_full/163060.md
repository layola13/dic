preclude: /prɪˈkluːd/ | vt. |阻止；排除；妨碍 | To prevent something from happening or someone from doing something. |例句：The heavy rain precluded any possibility of a picnic.  大雨使得野餐成为不可能。

例句：His age precluded him from joining the army.  他的年龄使他无法参军。


近义词：prevent, avoid, hinder, impede, obstruct | 反义词：allow, permit, enable


词源:  From Latin *praecludere, from prae- "before" + claudere "to shut".


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bad weather precludes a picnic. = 恶劣的天气阻止了野餐。
This rule precludes any further discussion. = 这条规则排除了进一步讨论的可能性。

一般过去时:
The storm precluded our travel plans. = 暴风雨打乱了我们的旅行计划。
His illness precluded his participation in the game. = 他的疾病使他无法参加比赛。

现在完成时:
The snow has precluded us from going outside all day. = 雪让我们整天都无法出门。
Recent events have precluded any chance of reconciliation. = 最近发生的事件排除了任何和解的可能性。

过去完成时:
The previous commitments had precluded her from attending the meeting. = 之前的承诺使她无法参加会议。
His earlier mistake had precluded him from winning the competition. = 他之前的错误使他无法赢得比赛。

将来时:
The expected strike will preclude the delivery of the goods. = 预计的罢工将阻止货物的交付。
A lack of funding will preclude the project from moving forward. = 缺乏资金将阻止项目的推进。

虚拟语气:
If the rain hadn't precluded the game, we would have won. = 如果雨没有阻止比赛，我们本该赢了。
If only my injury hadn't precluded my participation! = 要不是我的伤势阻止了我参加就好了！

被动语态:
Further progress was precluded by a lack of resources. = 由于缺乏资源，进一步的进展被阻止了。
Their plans were precluded by unforeseen circumstances. = 他们的计划由于不可预见的情况而被阻止了。

疑问句:
Does the contract preclude us from making changes? = 合同是否阻止我们进行更改？
Would this decision preclude any other options? = 这个决定是否会排除其他选择？

否定句:
Nothing could preclude him from achieving his goal. = 没有什么能够阻止他实现目标。
The agreement does not preclude future negotiations. = 该协议并不排除未来的谈判。


PHRASE->
preclude from: 排除…的可能性
preclude the possibility of: 排除…的可能性
preclude someone from doing something: 阻止某人做某事
preclude further action: 阻止进一步行动
preclude any other outcome: 排除任何其他结果
