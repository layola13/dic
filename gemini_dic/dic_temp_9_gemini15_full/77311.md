gatewise:/ˈɡeɪtwaɪz/| adv.| 门户方式地；通过网关|  Describing something done or occurring through a gateway or a similar access point, often in a network context. It implies a process or action mediated by a central point of access.|例句：The data was routed gatewise to the server. 数据通过网关路由到服务器。|例句：The system operates gatewise, controlling access to all network resources. 系统以网关方式运行，控制对所有网络资源的访问。|近义词：via, through, by means of|反义词：directly, independently
