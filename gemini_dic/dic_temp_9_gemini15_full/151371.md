parlay:/ˈpɑːrleɪ/| /ˈpɑːrli/| n., v.| 谈判；充分利用；吹嘘；闲聊 | To discuss, negotiate, or convert (something) into something else, especially a greater advantage.  |例句：He parlayed his initial success into a thriving business. 他凭借最初的成功创办了一家兴隆的企业。|近义词：negotiate, discuss, convert, leverage |反义词：abandon, forfeit


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They parlay their differences every week. = 他们每周都会协商他们的分歧。
He parleys with the enemy. = 他与敌人谈判。


一般过去时:
The diplomats parlayed for hours. = 外交官们进行了数小时的谈判。
She parlayed her winnings into a larger investment. = 她把赢来的钱变成了更大的投资。


现在完成时:
They have parlayed their small startup into a multinational company. = 他们已将他们的小型初创公司发展成为一家跨国公司。
He has parlayed his fame into a lucrative career. = 他已将自己的名气转化为一份利润丰厚的事业。


过去完成时:
They had parlayed their savings into a down payment on a house. = 他们已将他们的积蓄用于房屋的首付。
She had parlayed her knowledge into a successful consulting business. = 她已将自己的知识转化为一家成功的咨询公司。


将来时:
They will parlay their new technology into a competitive advantage. = 他们将利用他们的新技术获得竞争优势。
He will parlay his connections to secure the contract. = 他将利用自己的人脉关系来确保合同。


虚拟语气:
If they parlayed their resources effectively, they would succeed. = 如果他们有效地利用他们的资源，他们就会成功。
I wish I had parlayed my opportunity more wisely. = 我真希望我当时能更明智地利用我的机会。


被动语态:
The dispute was parlayed into a mutually beneficial agreement. = 争议通过协商达成了一项互利的协议。
The initial investment was parlayed into a substantial return. = 初始投资带来了可观的回报。


疑问句:
How will they parlay this new discovery? = 他们将如何利用这一新发现？
Did they successfully parlay their negotiations? = 他们的谈判成功了吗？


否定句:
They did not parlay their advantage effectively. = 他们没有有效地利用他们的优势。
He failed to parlay his talent into a successful career. = 他没能将自己的才能转化为成功的事业。



PHRASE->
parlay into:  转化为...

parlay an advantage: 利用优势
parlay a success:  将成功...
parlay one's skills:  充分发挥技能
