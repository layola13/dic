glom:/ɡlɒm/| vt.|粘贴；团聚；抓住；迅速抓住|to stick or cling together; to grab or seize quickly; to gather or collect hastily|例句：The children glommed onto their mother.孩子们紧紧地抱住他们的母亲。
例句：He glommed onto the idea immediately. 他立刻抓住了这个主意。|近义词：stick, cling, grab, seize, snatch|反义词：separate, detach, release
