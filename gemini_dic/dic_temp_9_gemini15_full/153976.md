perfections:/pərˈfɛkʃənz/| n. |完美；完美的事物；极致 |The state of being free from all flaws or defects; flawlessness.  It can also refer to the highest degree of excellence or skill. |例句：He strives for perfection in all his endeavors. 他努力在所有努力中追求完美。

例句：The sculptor achieved a level of perfection rarely seen.  这位雕塑家达到了很少见的完美水平。

近义词：excellence, flawlessness, impeccability, faultlessness

反义词：imperfection, flaw, defect, blemish

词性：名词
