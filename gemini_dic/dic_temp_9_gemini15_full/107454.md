lampoons:/læmˈpuns/| n. vt.|讽刺文章；讽刺诗；(用讽刺文章或诗歌)讽刺|A lampoon is a written satire usually aimed at a particular person or group.  It uses ridicule, irony, and sarcasm to expose flaws or criticize behavior.|例句：The magazine published a lampoon of the president's new policy. 这本杂志发表了一篇讽刺总统新政策的文章。|例句：He was lampooned in the newspapers for his foolish remarks. 他因其愚蠢的言论而遭到报纸的讽刺。|近义词：satire, parody, caricature|反义词：praise, eulogy|名词，动词
