honed:/hoʊnd/ | v. | 磨练；使完美；加强 | To sharpen or make more effective.  |  He honed his skills through years of practice. 他通过多年的练习磨练了他的技能。  | sharpened, refined, perfected | dulled, blunted, weakened | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hones his writing skills daily. = 他每天磨练他的写作技巧。
She hones her presentation skills before every meeting. = 她每次会议前都会磨练她的演示技巧。

一般过去时:
The blacksmith honed the blade to a razor sharpness. = 铁匠将刀刃磨得锋利无比。
He honed his argument until it was irrefutable. = 他反复打磨自己的论点，直到无可辩驳。

现在完成时:
She has honed her public speaking abilities over the years. = 多年来，她已经磨练了自己的公开演讲能力。
They have honed their strategy to perfection. = 他们已经将他们的策略完善到了极致。

过去完成时:
He had honed his skills before entering the competition. = 他在参加比赛之前就已经磨练了自己的技能。
She had honed her presentation before the big meeting. = 她在大型会议之前就已经磨练了自己的演示文稿。

将来时:
He will hone his skills further with more practice. = 他将通过更多的练习进一步磨练他的技能。
She will hone her skills in negotiation for the upcoming deal. = 她将为即将到来的交易磨练她的谈判技巧。

虚拟语气:
If he had honed his skills earlier, he would have won the competition. = 如果他早点磨练自己的技能，他就会赢得比赛。
If she had honed her presentation, she would have impressed the client more. = 如果她磨练了她的演示文稿，她会给客户留下更深刻的印象。

被动语态:
His skills were honed through rigorous training. = 他的技能是通过严格的训练磨练出来的。
The software was honed over several years of development. = 该软件经过多年的开发才得以完善。

疑问句:
How did he hone his skills to such a high level? = 他是如何将自己的技能磨练到如此高的水平的？
How can she hone her skills in a short period? = 她如何在短期内磨练自己的技能？

否定句:
He did not hone his skills enough for the competition. = 他没有为比赛充分磨练自己的技能。
She didn't hone her arguments sufficiently before the debate. = 她在辩论之前没有充分磨练自己的论点。
