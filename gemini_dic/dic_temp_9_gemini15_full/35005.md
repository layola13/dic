clone:/kləʊn/| /kloʊn/ | n. v. |克隆，复制品 |A clone is an exact copy of a plant or animal created by scientists.  Cloning is the process of producing such a copy. |例句：Scientists created a clone of the sheep Dolly. 科学家克隆了多利羊。|例句：They are trying to clone extinct animals. 他们试图克隆灭绝的动物。|近义词：copy, duplicate, replica |反义词：original, unique |名词，动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Scientists clone many plants.  科学家克隆许多植物。
He clones his father's behaviour. 他模仿他父亲的行为。


一般过去时:
They cloned a rare flower. 他们克隆了一种稀有的花。
