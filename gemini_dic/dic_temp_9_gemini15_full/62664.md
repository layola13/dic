engages:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪz/| vt.| 从事；吸引；雇佣；使忙碌；参与；约定 | To occupy, attract, or involve someone's interest or attention; to employ someone; to become involved in something. |例句：She engages in many charitable activities. 她从事许多慈善活动。|例句：The movie engages the audience from beginning to end. 这部电影从头到尾都吸引着观众。|近义词：occupy, attract, involve, employ, participate |反义词：disengage, detach, ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He engages in regular exercise. = 他定期锻炼。
She engages her students with interesting activities. = 她用有趣的活动吸引她的学生。

一般过去时:
They engaged a lawyer to represent them. = 他们雇了一位律师来代表他们。
He engaged in a heated debate. = 他参与了一场激烈的辩论。

现在完成时:
She has engaged herself in this project for years. = 她多年来一直致力于这个项目。
The company has engaged several new employees. = 公司聘请了几名新员工。

过去完成时:
They had engaged a caterer before the party started. = 他们早在派对开始前就雇佣了餐饮承办商。
He had engaged in similar research previously. = 他以前做过类似的研究。

将来时:
We will engage a professional cleaning service. = 我们将雇佣专业的清洁服务。
The company will engage in new market exploration. = 公司将进行新的市场探索。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would engage in painting. = 如果我有更多时间，我会从事绘画。
I wish I could engage more with my family. = 我希望我能更多地与家人相处。

被动语态:
The lawyer was engaged by the company. = 律师是被公司雇佣的。
The audience was fully engaged by the performance. = 观众被演出深深吸引住了。

疑问句:
Does he engage in any sports? = 他从事任何运动吗？
Does this topic engage your interest? = 这个话题能引起你的兴趣吗？

否定句:
He doesn't engage in politics. = 他不参与政治。
The story didn't engage me at all. = 这个故事一点也没吸引我。


PHRASE->
engage in = 从事；参与
engage with = 与…互动；参与
be engaged to = 与…订婚
get engaged = 订婚
engaged in = 忙于
