heapiest:/ˈhæpiːɪst/| adj.|最快乐的，最幸福的|Happiest is the superlative form of happy, meaning the most happy or joyful. It describes the highest degree of happiness. |例句：She was the happiest person I knew. 她是我认识的最快乐的人。
例句：The happiest day of my life was my wedding day.  我生命中最快乐的一天是我的婚礼。|近义词：most joyful, most cheerful, most delighted|反义词：saddest, most miserable, most unhappy

USAGE->
This word is an adjective, and therefore doesn't conjugate like a verb.  It only changes in comparative and superlative forms (happier, happiest).  It's used to describe nouns.  The tense of the sentence it is used in will determine the tense of the whole sentence.


一般现在时:
He is the happiest person in the room. = 他是房间里最快乐的人。
The flowers make her the happiest. = 这些花让她最快乐。


一般过去时:
She was the happiest she had ever been. = 她是她有史以来最快乐的时候。
Yesterday was the happiest day of my life. = 昨天是我生命中最快乐的一天。


现在完成时:
He has been the happiest since he met her. = 自从他遇到她以来，他一直是最快乐的。
I have never felt happier. = 我从未感到如此快乐。(Note: This uses the comparative, but illustrates the context of present perfect)


过去完成时:
She had been the happiest before the accident. = 事故发生前，她一直是最快乐的。
I had never been happier in my life. = 我一生中从未如此快乐。(Note: This uses the comparative, but illustrates the context of past perfect)


将来时:
I hope I will be the happiest on my birthday. = 我希望在我生日那天我会是最快乐的。
He will be the happiest man alive if she says yes. = 如果她说"是的"，他将成为世界上最快乐的人。


虚拟语气:
If I were the happiest person in the world, I would still want more. = 即使我是世界上最快乐的人，我也仍然想要更多。
I wish I were the happiest person alive. = 我希望我是世界上最快乐的人。


被动语态:
The happiest person is often described as carefree. = 最快乐的人通常被描述为无忧无虑的。 (Note:  "happiest" is the subject here, not the object of a passive verb)


疑问句:
Who is the happiest person you know? = 你认识的最快乐的人是谁？
Is he the happiest man in the world? = 他是世界上最快乐的人吗？


否定句:
She is not the happiest person I know. = 她不是我认识的最快乐的人。
He was definitely not the happiest at the party. = 他在派对上肯定不是最快乐的。
