overdebated:/ˌoʊvərˈdeɪtɪd/ | adj. |  过度争论的；被过度讨论的 |  This is a word formed by adding the prefix "over-" to the past participle "debated". It describes something that has been discussed or argued about excessively, to the point of being unproductive or tiresome.  | The issue of gun control has become overdebated, with little progress made.  |  much-discussed, overanalyzed |  underdebated | adj.
