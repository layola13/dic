cosplay:/ˈkɒspleɪ/|/ˈkɑːspleɪ/| n. v.|角色扮演；扮演；动漫角色扮演|The act of dressing up as a character from a video game, movie, book, or other fictional work, often involving elaborate costumes and makeup.  It's a popular hobby and subculture. |例句：She's going to cosplay as Sailor Moon at the convention. 她打算在动漫展上扮演美少女战士。

例句：Many people cosplay their favorite characters from anime and manga. 许多人扮演他们最喜欢的动漫和漫画角色。


近义词：role-playing, impersonation, enacting|反义词：None specifically.  The opposite would be not dressing up or acting as a character.|词性:noun, verb
