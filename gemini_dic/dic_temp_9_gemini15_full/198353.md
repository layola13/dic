showcase: /ˈʃoʊkeɪs/ | n. vt. | 展示，橱窗，展台 | 展示，显现；作为…的展示 |  The museum showcases the artist's best works. | 近义词：display, exhibit, demonstrate | 反义词：hide, conceal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gallery showcases contemporary art. = 画廊展出当代艺术。
This event showcases the talents of young musicians. = 此次活动展示了年轻音乐家的才华。


一般过去时:
The company showcased its new product at the trade show. = 公司在贸易展会上展示了其新产品。
The film showcased the beauty of the Scottish Highlands. = 这部电影展现了苏格兰高地的美丽。


现在完成时:
The artist has showcased his work in numerous galleries. = 该艺术家已在众多画廊展出过他的作品。
They have showcased their commitment to environmental sustainability. = 他们已经展示了他们对环境可持续性的承诺。


过去完成时:
Before the launch, the company had showcased the product to potential investors. = 在发布之前，公司已向潜在投资者展示了该产品。
The museum had showcased the ancient artifacts for over a decade. = 该博物馆已经展出这些古代文物超过十年了。


将来时:
The company will showcase its new technology at the conference. = 公司将在会议上展示其新技术。
The festival will showcase a diverse range of musical styles. = 音乐节将展示各种各样的音乐风格。


虚拟语气:
If they showcased their products more effectively, they would sell more. = 如果他们更有效地展示他们的产品，他们会卖出更多。
I wish they would showcase the local artists' work more prominently. = 我希望他们能更突出地展示当地艺术家的作品。


被动语态:
The product was showcased to great acclaim. = 该产品受到好评如潮。
The artist's work is being showcased in a major exhibition. = 该艺术家的作品正在一个大型展览中展出。


疑问句:
Will the exhibition showcase any new works by the artist? = 展览会展出该艺术家的任何新作品吗？
Does the store showcase locally made goods? = 这家商店是否展示本地制造的商品？


否定句:
The company did not showcase its new product at the conference. = 公司没有在会议上展示其新产品。
The presentation did not showcase the strengths of the proposal effectively. = 演示文稿并没有有效地展示该提案的优势。
