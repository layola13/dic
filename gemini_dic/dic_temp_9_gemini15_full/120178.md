mindfully:/ˈmaɪndfʊli/| adj. | 专注地；用心；留心地 | Paying close attention to one's thoughts, feelings, and sensations in the present moment.  It emphasizes awareness and intentionality in actions and experiences. |例句：She mindfully ate her meal, savoring each bite. 她专心地吃饭，细细品味每一口。|例句：He approached the problem mindfully, considering all the implications. 他用心对待这个问题，考虑到了所有的影响。|近义词：attentively, consciously, deliberately |反义词：carelessly, thoughtlessly, absentmindedly
