caribous:/ˈkærɪbuːz/| n.|北美驯鹿；加拿大驯鹿|The plural of caribou.  Caribou are large deer found in North America and are known for their ability to migrate long distances. They are herbivores and play a significant role in their ecosystems.| 例句：The herd of caribou migrated across the tundra. 这群驯鹿迁徙到苔原上。  The caribou population has been affected by climate change.  驯鹿种群受到气候变化的影响。|近义词：reindeer (though technically a different species)|反义词：无|名词
