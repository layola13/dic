fall-outs: /ˈfɔːlˌaʊts/ | n. | 后果；冲突；争吵；（核爆炸的）放射性尘埃 |

英文解析：The consequences or repercussions of an event, especially an unpleasant or unexpected one.  It can also refer to a quarrel or dispute, or the radioactive dust and particles produced by a nuclear explosion.

例句：

The fall-out from the scandal damaged his reputation.  这场丑闻的后果损害了他的名誉。

The two countries experienced a serious fall-out over trade disputes.  这两个国家因为贸易纠纷而发生了严重的冲突。


近义词：consequences, repercussions, aftermath, fallout (as a noun meaning radioactive dust), quarrel, dispute, disagreement

反义词：agreement, harmony, accord, resolution


词性：名词
