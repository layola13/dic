mistakenly:/mɪˈsteɪkənli/ | adv. | 错误地，误以为 |  The statement was mistakenly attributed to the wrong person.  这句话错误地归咎于错误的人。|近义词：erroneously, wrongly, incorrectly | 反义词：correctly, accurately


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mistakenly believes that the earth is flat. = 他错误地认为地球是平的。
She mistakenly dials the wrong number. = 她错误地拨打了错误的号码。


一般过去时:
I mistakenly deleted the important file. = 我错误地删除了重要的文件。
They mistakenly took my bag for theirs. = 他们错误地把我的包当成他们的了。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

They had mistakenly believed the rumor. = 他们错误地相信了那个谣言。


将来时:
I will mistakenly forget his birthday. = 我可能会错误地忘记他的生日。

虚拟语气:

否定句:
