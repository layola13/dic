presswork:/ˈprɛsˌwɜːrk/ | n. |印刷品；印刷工作；冲压件|Printed matter or the work of printing;  metal pieces shaped by stamping or pressing.  |例句：The quality of the presswork was excellent.  印刷品的质量非常好。
The presswork for the car body is done by a high-speed stamping machine. 汽车车身的冲压件是由高速冲压机完成的。|近义词：printing, stamping, forging|反义词：handwork, handcrafting|名词
