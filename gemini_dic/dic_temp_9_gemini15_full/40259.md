contingency:/kənˈtɪndʒənsi/ | n. |意外事件；可能性；偶然性 | An event that may occur but that is not expected or intended.  A possible event or outcome. |例句：We need to plan for all possible contingencies. 我们需要为所有可能出现的意外情况做好计划。

例句：The company has a contingency plan in case of a fire. 公司制定了火灾应急预案。


近义词：possibility, chance, eventuality, incident

反义词：certainty, inevitability


词性:名词
