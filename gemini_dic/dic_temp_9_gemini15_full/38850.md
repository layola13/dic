confectioning: /kənˈfɛkʃənɪŋ/ | v. | 糖果制作；甜食制作 | The process of making confectionery, sweets, or similar food items. The small bakery specialized in artisan confectioning, creating unique and beautiful sweets. 这家小面包店专门从事手工糖果制作，创造出独特而美丽的甜点。
