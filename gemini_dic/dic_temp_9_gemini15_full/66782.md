It often involves romanticized or idealized representations of foreign cultures. |例句：The restaurant's decor emphasized exoticism, with vibrant colors and unusual spices.|近义词：foreignness, strangeness, otherness |反义词：familiarity, ordinariness

词源:
Exoticism comes from the word "exotic," which originates from the Greek word "exōtikós," meaning "foreign" or "external."  The suffix "-ism" denotes a doctrine, practice, or characteristic.  Therefore, exoticism refers to the characteristics or qualities associated with foreign or unusual cultures.
