inter-pollinating: /ˌɪntərˈpɒləneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 交叉授粉 | The act of transferring pollen between different plants, especially of different varieties or species, to enhance genetic diversity or produce hybrid offspring.  | Example:  Farmers often practice inter-pollinating their crops to improve yield and disease resistance.  农民经常对他们的农作物进行异花授粉以提高产量和抗病性。 |近义词: cross-pollination, hybridization | 反义词: self-pollination

They inter-pollinate their apple trees every spring. = 他们每年春天都会给他们的苹果树进行异花授粉。
Bees inter-pollinate many flowers in the garden. = 蜜蜂给花园里的许多花进行授粉。
