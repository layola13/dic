salinity:/səˈlɪnəti/| n. |盐度，含盐量 |The salinity of seawater varies depending on location and depth. 海水的盐度因地而异，也取决于深度。|近义词：saltiness |反义词：freshness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The salinity of the ocean is generally high. = 海洋的盐度通常很高。
This lake has a surprisingly low salinity. = 这个湖泊的盐度出奇地低。


一般过去时:
The salinity of the river decreased after the rain. = 雨后河水的盐度下降了。
Scientists measured the salinity of the sample. = 科学家测量了样本的盐度。


现在完成时:
Scientists have studied the effects of salinity on marine life. = 科学家们已经研究了盐度对海洋生物的影响。
The salinity of the soil has increased due to irrigation. = 由于灌溉，土壤的盐度增加了。


过去完成时:
Before the dam was built, the salinity of the river was much lower. = 在大坝建成之前，河水的盐度要低得多。
They had already analyzed the salinity data before the presentation. = 他们在演示文稿之前就已经分析了盐度数据。


将来时:
The salinity of the bay will likely increase due to climate change. = 由于气候变化，海湾的盐度可能会增加。
We will monitor the salinity levels regularly. = 我们将定期监测盐度水平。


虚拟语气:
If the salinity were lower, more plants would grow. = 如果盐度更低，更多的植物会生长。
It would be beneficial if the salinity could be controlled. = 如果能够控制盐度，那将是有益的。


被动语态:
The salinity was measured using a specialized instrument. = 盐度是用专门的仪器测量的。
The salinity levels are being monitored continuously. = 盐度水平正在持续监测中。


疑问句:
What is the salinity of this water sample? = 这个水样的盐度是多少？
How does salinity affect plant growth? = 盐度如何影响植物生长？


否定句:
The salinity is not high enough for this type of salt marsh. = 这个类型的盐沼的盐度不够高。
This area doesn't have a high salinity problem. = 这个地区没有严重的盐度问题。
