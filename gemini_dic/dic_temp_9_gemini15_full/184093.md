resiliently:/rɪˈzɪliəntli/ | adv. | 具有弹性地；顽强地；适应力强地 |  In a resilient manner; showing resilience.  The ability to recover quickly from difficulties; toughness. | 例句：The economy rebounded resiliently after the crisis.  经济在危机后强劲反弹。

The small business adapted resiliently to the changing market conditions. 这家小企业适应了不断变化的市场环境。


近义词：tenaciously, stubbornly, strongly, flexibly

反义词：weakly,frailly,fragilely
