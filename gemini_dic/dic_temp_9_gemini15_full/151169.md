parcel:/ˈpɑːrsəl/| n.|包裹，小包，批，份额|A parcel is a package that is sent by post or delivered by a courier.|例句：I received a parcel from my mother yesterday. 昨天我收到我母亲寄来的一个包裹。|例句：The land was divided into several parcels. 这块土地被分成好几块。|近义词：package, bundle, packet|反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sends parcels every week. = 他每周都寄包裹。
She often receives parcels from overseas. = 她经常收到来自海外的包裹。

一般过去时:
I sent a parcel to my friend last week. = 我上周寄了一个包裹给我的朋友。
They received a large parcel yesterday. = 他们昨天收到一个大包裹。

现在完成时:
She has sent several parcels this month. = 这个月她已经寄了好几个包裹了。
He has received many parcels recently. = 他最近收到了很多包裹。

过去完成时:
I had already sent the parcel before he called. = 他打电话之前我已经寄出包裹了。
They had received the parcel before the deadline. = 截止日期之前他们已经收到了包裹。

将来时:
I will send a parcel to my sister tomorrow. = 我明天会寄一个包裹给我姐姐。
They will receive the parcel next week. = 他们下周会收到包裹。

虚拟语气:
If I had sent the parcel earlier, it would have arrived on time. = 如果我早点寄出包裹，它就能准时到达了。
I wish I had sent the parcel by express delivery. = 我真希望我用快递寄出包裹。

被动语态:
The parcel was sent yesterday. = 包裹是昨天寄出的。
The parcel is being delivered right now. = 包裹正在送达中。

疑问句:
Did you send the parcel? = 你寄包裹了吗？
Has she received the parcel yet? = 她收到包裹了吗？

否定句:
I didn't send the parcel. = 我没有寄包裹。
He hasn't received the parcel yet. = 他还没收到包裹。
