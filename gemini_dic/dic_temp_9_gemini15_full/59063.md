dwelt:/dwel/| v.|居住，沉思，详述|to live in a place; to think or talk a lot about something.|例句：They dwelt in a small cottage by the sea.他们住在海边的一间小别墅里。They dwelt on the details of the accident.他们详细讲述了事故的细节。|近义词：resided, lived, pondered, mused, elaborated|反义词：departed, left, ignored, neglected


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He dwells in a small apartment. = 他住在一间小公寓里。
She dwells on her past mistakes. = 她总是沉湎于过去的错误。


一般过去时:
They dwelt in that house for many years. = 他们在那栋房子里住了很多年。
He dwelt on the topic for quite some time. = 他花了相当长的时间谈论这个话题。


现在完成时:
We have dwelt in this city for over a decade. = 我们在这个城市住了十多年了。
She has dwelt on this problem for days. = 她已经考虑这个问题好几天了。


过去完成时:
They had dwelt in the countryside before moving to the city. = 他们在搬到城市之前住在乡下。
He had dwelt on the possibility before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前已经考虑过这种可能性。


将来时:
They will dwell in a new house next year. = 他们明年将住进新房子。
She will dwell on the matter further later. = 她以后会进一步考虑这件事。


虚拟语气:
If I dwelt in a castle, I would be happy. = 如果我住在一座城堡里，我会很高兴。
I wish I had dwelt on that point earlier. = 我真希望我早点考虑那一点。


被动语态:
The issue was dwelt on extensively in the report. = 报告中对这个问题进行了广泛的讨论。
The matter will be dwelt upon during the meeting. = 会议期间将会讨论这个问题。


疑问句:
Where do they dwell? = 他们住在哪里？
What will you dwell on in your speech? = 你在演讲中会着重讨论什么？


否定句:
He did not dwell on the negative aspects. = 他没有过多地关注消极方面。
They did not dwell long in that town. = 他们没有在那座小镇上住多久。


PHRASE->

dwell on = 详述；细想
dwell in = 居住于
dwell among = 居住在...之中
dwell upon = 仔细考虑
dwell with = 与...同住
