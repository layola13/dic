gift:/ɡɪft/ | n. | 礼物，天赋 | A gift is something given willingly to someone without payment; a natural talent or ability.  | Example: She received a beautiful gift for her birthday. 她收到了一个美丽的生日礼物。 | Example: He has a gift for music. 他有音乐天赋。|近义词：present, talent, aptitude |反义词：curse, burden


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He gives her a gift every year. = 他每年都送她礼物。
They often give gifts to charity. = 他们经常向慈善机构捐赠。


一般过去时:
She gave me a lovely gift. = 她送给我一件可爱的礼物。
He gifted his daughter a car. = 他送给他女儿一辆车。


现在完成时:
I have given him many gifts over the years. = 多年来，我已经送给他许多礼物了。
They have gifted their entire collection to the museum. = 他们已经将他们的全部收藏捐赠给了博物馆。


过去完成时:
She had already given him a gift before he arrived. = 他到达之前，她已经送给他礼物了。
They had gifted the painting to the gallery before the exhibition. = 展览前，他们已经将这幅画赠送给画廊了。


将来时:
I will give him a gift for his graduation. = 我将送给他一件毕业礼物。
They will be gifting the proceeds to a local charity. = 他们将把收益捐赠给当地的一家慈善机构。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would give him a more expensive gift. = 如果我有更多钱，我会送给他一件更贵的礼物。
I wish I could give her the gift of time. = 我希望我能送给她时间这件礼物。


被动语态:
The gift was given to her anonymously. = 这份礼物是匿名赠送的。
The painting was gifted to the museum by a private collector. = 这幅画是由一位私人收藏家捐赠给博物馆的。


疑问句:
What gift did you receive? = 你收到了什么礼物？
Have they received any gifts yet? = 他们收到礼物了吗？


否定句:
He didn't give her a gift. = 他没有送她礼物。
They haven't gifted anything to the cause yet. = 他们还没有为这个事业捐赠任何东西。
