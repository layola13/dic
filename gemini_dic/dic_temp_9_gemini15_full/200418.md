slabs:/slæbz/| n.|厚板；石板；平板|A slab is a thick, flat piece of a hard material, such as stone, concrete, or wood.  It is usually rectangular or square in shape.|例句：The countertop is made of marble slabs.  台面是用大理石板做的。

例句：They used concrete slabs to build the foundation. 他们用混凝土板建造地基。


近义词：plate, panel, section|反义词：fragment, splinter, crumb|名词
