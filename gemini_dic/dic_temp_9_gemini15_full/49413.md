delivery:/dɪˈlɪvəri/ | n. |递送，交付；分娩；演讲；投递 | The act of delivering something; the process of bringing something to a recipient.  It can also refer to the manner or style of speaking. |例句：The delivery of the package was delayed.包裹的投递延误了。

例句：She had a difficult delivery. 她分娩很艰难。


近义词：conveyance, shipment, presentation, childbirth | 反义词：receipt, collection


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company delivers packages daily. = 公司每天递送包裹。
He delivers a compelling speech. = 他发表了一篇引人入胜的演讲。


一般过去时:
They delivered the furniture yesterday. = 他们昨天送来了家具。
She delivered a baby boy. = 她生了一个男婴。


现在完成时:
We have delivered all the orders. = 我们已经完成了所有的订单。
The doctor has delivered hundreds of babies. =这位医生接生过数百个婴儿。


过去完成时:
They had delivered the goods before the deadline. = 他们已在截止日期前交付了货物。
She had delivered a powerful speech before the audience. = 她之前已向观众发表了一篇精彩的演讲。


将来时:
We will deliver the goods next week. = 我们下周将交付货物。
She will deliver her baby next month. = 她下个月将分娩。


虚拟语气:
If they delivered the package on time, we would be happy. = 如果他们准时送达包裹，我们会很高兴。
If she had delivered the speech earlier, more people would have attended. =如果她早点发表演讲，会有更多人来参加。


被动语态:
The package was delivered this morning. = 包裹今天早上送到了。
The speech was delivered with great passion. = 这篇演讲充满激情地发表了。


疑问句:
When will the goods be delivered? = 货物什么时候交付？
How was the baby delivered? = 宝宝是怎么出生的？


否定句:
They didn't deliver the package on time. = 他们没有准时送达包裹。
She didn't deliver her speech as planned. = 她没有按照计划发表演讲。


PHRASE->

free delivery: 免费送达
