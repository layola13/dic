hemoptysis:/ˈhɛməptɪsɪs/| n. |咯血；咳血|The coughing up of blood from the respiratory tract.  It can be a symptom of various lung diseases, such as bronchitis, pneumonia, or tuberculosis, as well as other conditions affecting the lungs or circulatory system.  The amount of blood coughed up can vary from a small streak to a large amount.| 例句：The patient presented with hemoptysis and shortness of breath.  病人出现咯血和呼吸困难。

The doctor suspected tuberculosis after examining the patient's hemoptysis. 医生在检查病人的咯血后怀疑是肺结核。


近义词：bloody cough, expectoration of blood|反义词：None (It's a symptom, not a condition with a direct opposite)|词性:名词


词源:
From the Greek words  "haima" (αἷμα), meaning "blood," and "ptysis" (πτύσις), meaning "spitting."  Therefore, the term literally means "spitting blood."
