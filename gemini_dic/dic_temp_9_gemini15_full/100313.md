intersocietal:/ˌɪntərsoʊˈsaɪətəl/| adj. |跨社会的，社会间的| Relating to or occurring between different societies or social groups.  The study focused on intersocietal communication patterns.  |近义词：intersocial, transnational|反义词：intrasocietal


例句:

The research explored intersocietal conflict resolution strategies.  研究探讨了跨社会冲突解决策略。

Intersocietal cooperation is crucial for addressing global challenges. 跨社会合作对于应对全球性挑战至关重要。


词源:

* **inter-**:  A prefix meaning "between" or "among."  源自拉丁语，意为“在…之间”或“在…之中”。
* **societal**:  Derived from "society," referring to a community of people.  源于“society”（社会），指一群人组成的社群。  "Societal" itself is formed with the suffix "-al," indicating an adjective form.  “-al”后缀表示形容词形式。


用法说明:

"Intersocietal" is primarily used as an adjective to describe things that involve interactions, relationships, or processes between different societies or social groups.  It emphasizes the cross-societal nature of the phenomenon being described.  “Intersocietal”主要用作形容词，用来描述涉及不同社会或社会群体之间互动、关系或过程的事物。它强调所描述现象的跨社会性质。


因为“intersocietal”是一个相对较少使用的词，所以没有专门的语法练习。  但是，它可以用于各种句法结构中，例如：

* **主语**: Intersocietal cooperation is essential. （跨社会合作至关重要。）
* **宾语**:  We are studying intersocietal relations. （我们正在研究跨社会关系。）
* **定语**:  Intersocietal conflicts are complex. （跨社会冲突很复杂。）
