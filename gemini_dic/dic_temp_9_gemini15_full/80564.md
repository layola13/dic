词源:
The word is a reduplication of "goodie,"  meaning something good or pleasant, particularly food. "Goodie" itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to words implying sweetness or pleasantness.  The "-y" suffix adds an endearing or playful quality.  The repetition emphasizes abundance and a childlike sense of delight.
