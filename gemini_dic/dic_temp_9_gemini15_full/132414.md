nondeodorants:/ˌnɒnˈdiːəʊdərənts/| n. |非除臭剂|Products that do not mask or eliminate body odor.  These may be natural products or simply products that do not contain odor-masking ingredients.|Example:  Some people prefer nondeodorants to antiperspirants because they don't block sweat.|近义词:natural deodorant, unscented deodorant|反义词:deodorant, antiperspirant|名词
