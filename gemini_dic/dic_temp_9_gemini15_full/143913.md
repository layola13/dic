outpreaches:/ˌaʊtˈpriːtʃɪz/ | v. |超过（在布道或演讲方面）|To surpass in preaching or speaking; to preach more effectively or extensively than.| The visiting preacher outpreached all the local clergy.  这位来访的传教士的布道比所有当地牧师都更有效。|近义词：outshine, surpass, excel |反义词：underperform, fail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outpreaches everyone in the congregation. = 他在会众中比任何人都更擅长布道。
The pastor outpreaches his predecessor. =这位牧师比他的前任更擅长布道。


一般过去时:
She outpreached her opponent in the debate. =她在辩论中超过了她的对手。
The young minister outpreached the veteran in eloquence. =这位年轻的牧师在雄辩方面胜过了资深牧师。


现在完成时:
He has outpreached many renowned speakers throughout his career. =在他整个职业生涯中，他已经超过了许多著名的演讲者。
The evangelist has outpreached all others in the region. =这位传福音者在该地区比其他所有人都更擅长布道。


过去完成时:
By the end of the tour, he had outpreached every other speaker on the circuit. =巡回演讲结束时，他已经超过了巡回演讲中的其他所有演讲者。
Before the final sermon, she had outpreached all her rivals. =在最后一次布道之前，她已经超过了所有竞争对手。


将来时:
She will outpreach her opponent in the upcoming debate. =她将在即将到来的辩论中超过她的对手。
He believes he will outpreach all others at the conference. =他相信他将在会议上超过其他所有人。


虚拟语气:
If he had prepared better, he might have outpreached the other speaker. =如果他准备得更好，他可能会超过另一位演讲者。
I wish I could outpreach the renowned theologian. =我希望我能超过那位著名的神学家。


被动语态:
He was outpreached by the visiting missionary. =他被来访的传教士超过了。
The local preacher was outpreached in terms of passion and delivery. =当地牧师在热情和表达方面被超过了。


疑问句:
Did he outpreach the other speakers at the conference? =他在会议上是否超过了其他演讲者？
Will she outpreach the established leader in the field? =她会在该领域超过已建立的领导者吗？


否定句:
He did not outpreach his colleague in the sermon. =他在布道中没有超过他的同事。
She failed to outpreach the experienced orator. =她未能超过经验丰富的演说家。


PHRASE->
outpreach the competition = 超越竞争对手
outpreach the opposition = 超越反对派
outpreach in eloquence = 在雄辩方面胜过
outpreach in passion = 在热情方面胜过
outpreach in persuasiveness = 在说服力方面胜过
