lysis:/ˈlaɪsɪs/| n.|溶解；分解；破坏|The breakdown of a cell caused by damage to its membrane.  This process can be caused by various factors, including disease, injury, or chemical exposure.  |例句：Cellular lysis is a crucial step in many biological processes. 细胞溶解是许多生物过程中至关重要的一步。

例句：The virus caused lysis of the red blood cells.  病毒导致红细胞溶解。|近义词：destruction, disintegration, breakdown |反义词：synthesis, formation, creation|名词
