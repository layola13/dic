self-correction:/ˌsɛlf kəˈrɛkʃən/| n. |自我纠正；自动校正|The act of correcting one's own mistakes or errors, often automatically or unconsciously.  It can refer to processes in language learning, writing, or even biological systems.|例句：The software has a built-in self-correction function.  该软件具有内置的自动校正功能。


例句：Her self-correction of the grammatical error showed her attentiveness to detail. 她对语法错误的自我纠正显示了她对细节的关注。


近义词：amendment, rectification, adjustment


反义词：perpetuation, continuation of error


词性：名词
