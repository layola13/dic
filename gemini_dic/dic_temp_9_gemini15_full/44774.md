crumples:/ˈkrʌmpəlz/ | v. |弄皱；弄褶；坍塌；萎缩 |To become wrinkled or folded; to make something wrinkled or folded; to collapse or fall down.  |例句：The old man crumples into a chair.  老汉无力地瘫坐在椅子上。
The fabric crumples easily.  这布料很容易起皱。

近义词：wrinkle, crease, crumple up, collapse, shrink | 反义词：smooth, straighten, iron
