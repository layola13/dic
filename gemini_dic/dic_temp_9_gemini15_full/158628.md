plicated:/ˈplɪkeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 复杂的，难懂的 | Having many parts or aspects that are difficult to understand or deal with.  It implies intricacy and often suggests a problem or challenge. | The convoluted plot of the novel left readers feeling plicated. 这部小说的情节错综复杂，让读者感到困惑。 | complex, intricate, involved, convoluted | simple, straightforward, easy, uncomplicated | 形容词


USAGE->

词源:
From Latin *plicātus, past participle of plicāre "to fold," from PIE *plek- "to weave, braid."  The sense evolved from "folded" to "complex" due to the association of folded things with intricate arrangements.
