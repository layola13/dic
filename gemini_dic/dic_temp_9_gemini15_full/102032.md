isolate:/ˈaɪsəleɪt/ | v. n. adj. |隔离；孤立；分离 | to separate something or someone from other things or people; to be alone or separated |例句：The patient was isolated to prevent the spread of the disease.  病人被隔离以防止疾病传播。
The island is geographically isolated. 这个岛屿在地理上与世隔绝。

近义词：separate, segregate, quarantine | 反义词：integrate, connect, unite

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They isolate the virus for study. = 他们分离病毒以进行研究。
He isolates himself from society. = 他把自己与社会隔绝开来。

一般过去时:
Scientists isolated the gene responsible for the disease. = 科学家们分离出了导致这种疾病的基因。
She isolated herself in her room after the argument. = 争吵后，她把自己锁在房间里。

现在完成时:
Researchers have isolated several new strains of the virus. = 研究人员已经分离出几种新的病毒菌株。
He has isolated himself from his family for years. = 他多年来一直与家人隔绝。

过去完成时:
They had isolated the suspect before he could escape. = 他们在嫌疑人逃跑之前就将他隔离了。
She had isolated herself from her friends before the incident. = 事发前她就与朋友们疏远了。

将来时:
We will isolate the variable to test its effect. = 我们将隔离变量以测试其影响。
He will isolate himself in the mountains for a month. = 他将独自一人在山上待一个月。

虚拟语气:
If we isolated the problem, we could solve it easily. = 如果我们能找到问题的症结所在，就能很容易地解决它。
I wish I had isolated myself from that toxic relationship sooner. = 我真希望我早点从那段有毒的关系中抽身出来。

被动语态:
The infected area was isolated by the authorities. = 受感染区域被当局隔离了。
The experiment was carefully designed to isolate the effects of different variables. = 实验被仔细设计，以隔离不同变量的影响。

疑问句:
Did they isolate the cause of the accident? = 他们是否查明事故的原因？
Have you ever isolated yourself from your work for a while? = 你有没有试过暂时远离工作？

否定句:
They did not isolate the virus successfully. = 他们没有成功分离出病毒。
She didn't isolate herself from the negativity. = 她没有把自己从消极情绪中隔离出来。
