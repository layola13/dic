putrid:/ˈpʌtrɪd/ | adj. |腐烂的；恶臭的；极坏的 | Extremely unpleasant; decayed or rotting and emitting a foul smell.  The word implies a state of decay that is advanced and offensive.  It often refers to something organic, like food or flesh. |例句：The putrid smell of rotting fish filled the air.  腐烂的鱼腥味充满了空气。

例句：The putrid meat was unfit for consumption.  腐烂的肉不适合食用。


近义词：rotten, decaying, decomposed, foul, fetid, rancid, sickening


反义词：fresh, sweet-smelling, wholesome, pristine


词性:形容词
