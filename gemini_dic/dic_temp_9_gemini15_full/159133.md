USAGE->
This word is an adjective, and thus doesn't have tenses like verbs.  The usage is straightforward: it describes something as being shaped like a cup or goblet.

Example 1: The poculiform structure of the mushroom was striking.  蘑菇的杯状结构非常引人注目。
Example 2:  The geologist noted the poculiform depression in the rock. 地质学家注意到岩石上的杯状凹陷。
