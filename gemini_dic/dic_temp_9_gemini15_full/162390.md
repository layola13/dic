preactivity:/ˌpriːækˈtɪvɪti/| n. |预活动；前期活动；准备活动|Activity that precedes or prepares for a main activity or event.  It often refers to preparatory actions or exercises undertaken before a more significant task or performance.|例句：The preactivity involved reviewing the project proposal and gathering necessary resources.  前期活动包括审查项目建议书和收集必要的资源。|例句：The dancers engaged in rigorous preactivity before their performance. 舞蹈演员在演出前进行了严格的准备活动。|近义词：preparation, preliminary, groundwork, prelude|反义词：postactivity, aftermath, consequence
