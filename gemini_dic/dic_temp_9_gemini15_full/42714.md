/ˌkaʊntərjuːnɪˈvɜːrsɪti/ | n. | 反大学；对大学的抵制；批判大学 | A counteruniversity is an informal, often underground, educational setting that operates in opposition to or as a critique of traditional universities.  It may offer alternative teaching methods, curricula, or philosophies.  The term suggests a rejection of established academic norms and hierarchies. | Example: The Occupy movement's informal learning circles could be seen as a form of counteruniversity.  反占领运动中的非正式学习小组可以被视为一种反大学形式。 | 近义词：alternative educational institution, underground academy | 反义词：traditional university, established academy | 名词
