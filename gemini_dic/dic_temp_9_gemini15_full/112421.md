lotos:/ˈloʊtəs/| n. |莲花，睡莲 |A lotus is a type of aquatic plant with large, showy flowers.  The lotus flower is a symbol of purity and enlightenment in many cultures. |例句：The pond was filled with beautiful pink and white lotuses.  池塘里盛开着美丽的粉色和白色的莲花。  The lotus flower is a symbol of purity. 莲花是纯洁的象征。|近义词：water lily (某些种类)|反义词：无|名词
