queendom:/ˈkwiːndəm/| n. |王国，女王统治的时期|The realm or territory ruled by a queen; the period of a queen's reign.  The term often implies a feminine and possibly romanticized vision of power and authority, often emphasizing elegance and sophistication.|例句：The queendom was renowned for its beautiful gardens and thriving arts scene.  这个王国以其美丽的庭院和繁荣的艺术景象而闻名。

例句：Elizabeth's queendom was a golden age for England. 伊丽莎白的统治时期是英格兰的黄金时代。|近义词：kingdom, realm, reign|反义词：republic, democracy|名词
