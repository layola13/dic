ineptitude:/ɪˈnɛptɪtuːd/ | n. |无能；笨拙；不称职 | Lack of skill or ability; incompetence.  |His ineptitude as a manager led to the company's downfall.  他的管理无能导致公司倒闭。| incompetence, incapability, clumsiness | competence, skill, ability | 名词
