end-run: /ˈɛndrʌn/ | n., v. | 绕过；规避；巧妙地避开 | To circumvent or bypass something, especially a rule or procedure; a maneuver to avoid a direct confrontation or obstacle. | 近义词：circumvent, bypass, sidestep, outflank | 反义词：confront, directly address

They used an end-run to gain a competitive advantage.  他们用巧妙的策略获得了竞争优势。
