interavailability: /ˌɪntərəˌveɪləˈbɪləti/ | n. |交互可用性，互用性 | The degree to which different systems or components can work together or share resources.  It often refers to the ability of different parts of a system to communicate and exchange data or services seamlessly.  | Example:  The high interavailability of the modules allows for flexible system configuration.  高模块间的交互可用性允许灵活的系统配置。 | 近义词: compatibility, interoperability,  interchangeability | 反义词: incompatibility, isolation
