pseudoapologetically:/ˌsuːdoʊəˌpɒləˈdʒɛtɪkli/|/ˌsjuːdəʊəpɒləˈdʒɛtɪkli/| adj. |伪道歉地，貌似道歉地 | seeming to apologize, but not sincerely; offering a half-hearted or insincere apology.  It suggests a pretense of apology, often to deflect criticism or avoid responsibility. |He mumbled pseudoapologetically about being late for the meeting. 他含糊不清地貌似道歉说开会迟到了。|近义词：insincerely, halfheartedly, superficially apologetically|反义词：sincerely apologetically, genuinely repentant
