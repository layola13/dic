siege:/siːdʒ/| n. |围攻，包围 |The siege of the city lasted for months.  这座城市的围攻持续了数月。|近义词：besiegement, blockade |反义词：liberation, relief


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army besieges the city. = 军队包围了这座城市。
A long siege is underway. = 一场漫长的围攻正在进行。


一般过去时:
The city endured a long siege. = 这个城市经受了长期的围攻。
They lifted the siege after negotiations. = 谈判后他们解除了围攻。


现在完成时:
The city has been under siege for weeks. = 这座城市已经被围攻了好几个星期了。
They have successfully resisted the siege. = 他们成功地抵抗了围攻。


过去完成时:
The defenders had endured several sieges before this one. = 在这次围攻之前，守军已经经受了几次围攻。
The city had already fallen before reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达之前，这座城市已经沦陷了。


将来时:
The enemy will lay siege to the castle. = 敌人将要围攻城堡。
We will withstand the siege. = 我们将抵挡住围攻。


虚拟语气:
If they had not lifted the siege, the city would have fallen. = 如果他们没有解除围攻，这座城市就会沦陷。
I wish the siege were over. = 我希望围攻早点结束。


被动语态:
The city was besieged for many months. = 这座城市被围攻了数月。
The castle is being besieged by the enemy. = 城堡正被敌人围攻。


疑问句:
Was the siege successful? = 围攻成功了吗？
How long did the siege last? = 围攻持续了多久？


否定句:
The siege did not last long. = 围攻没有持续很久。
They did not break the siege. = 他们没有打破包围。


PHRASE->
lay siege to:  围攻
under siege:  处于围攻状态
lift the siege: 解除围攻
break the siege: 打破包围
end the siege: 结束围攻
