overthrown:/ˌoʊvərˈθroʊn/| v.|推翻；颠覆；打倒|To overthrow something means to violently remove it from power or destroy it completely.  It implies a forceful and often sudden removal. |例句：The rebels successfully overthrew the dictator.  反叛者成功推翻了独裁者。

The government was overthrown in a bloody coup. 政府在一场血腥的政变中被推翻了。|近义词：overturn, subvert, depose|反义词：establish, uphold, institute|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rebels overthrow the government.  反叛者推翻政府。
They overthrow the existing social order. 他们推翻了现有的社会秩序。

一般过去时:
The king was overthrown by his own son. 国王被自己的儿子推翻了。
The people overthrew the corrupt regime. 人民推翻了腐败的政权。

现在完成时:
The dictator has been overthrown. 独裁者已被推翻。
The old system has been overthrown and replaced by a new one. 旧制度已被推翻，取而代之的是一个新制度。

过去完成时:
The revolution had overthrown the monarchy before he arrived.  在他到达之前，革命已经推翻了君主制。
The people had overthrown the tyrant before the army could intervene.  人民在军队干预之前已经推翻了暴君。

将来时:
The people will overthrow the oppressive government. 人民将会推翻压迫的政府。
They believe the current system will be overthrown soon. 他们相信目前的制度很快就会被推翻。

虚拟语气:
If the people had been united, they would have overthrown the regime. 如果人民团结一致，他们就会推翻政权。
If the army had not intervened, the revolution would have overthrown the government. 如果军队没有干预，革命就会推翻政府。

被动语态:
The government was overthrown by the revolutionaries. 政府被革命者推翻了。
The old order was overthrown and a new one established. 旧秩序被推翻，新的秩序建立起来了。

疑问句:
Was the government overthrown peacefully? 政府是被和平推翻的吗？
Has the corrupt leader been overthrown yet? 腐败的领导人已经被推翻了吗？

否定句:
The rebels failed to overthrow the government. 反叛者未能推翻政府。
They did not overthrow the established order. 他们没有推翻既定的秩序。


PHRASE->
be overthrown = 被推翻
overthrow a government = 推翻政府
overthrow a regime = 推翻政权
overthrow a system = 推翻一个制度
overthrow tyranny = 推翻暴政
