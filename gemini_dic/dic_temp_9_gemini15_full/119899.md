militarized:/ˈmɪlɪtəraɪzd/| adj.|军事化的；武装化的|Having been equipped for or converted to military use; given military characteristics.|The border region has become increasingly militarized in recent years. 近年来，边境地区日益军事化。|The government has militarized the police force. 政府已经将警察部队军事化了。|近义词：armed, fortified, weaponized|反义词：demilitarized, pacifist

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The country militarizes its border regions. = 这个国家将其边境地区军事化。
This company militarizes its security protocols. = 这家公司将其安全协议军事化。


一般过去时:
They militarized the area after the conflict. = 冲突后，他们将该地区军事化。
The government militarized the police force last year. = 政府去年将警察部队军事化。


现在完成时:
The region has been heavily militarized since the war. = 自战争以来，该地区已被严重军事化。
The government has militarized its response to the crisis. = 政府已经将其对危机的应对军事化。


过去完成时:
They had militarized the island before the invasion. = 他们在入侵之前已经将岛屿军事化。
The country had militarized its economy in preparation for war. = 该国为了准备战争已经将其经济军事化。


将来时:
They will militarize the airspace if necessary. = 必要时，他们将使空域军事化。
The government will militarize its approach to cybersecurity. = 政府将使其网络安全方法军事化。


虚拟语气:
If they militarized the area, it would escalate tensions. = 如果他们将该地区军事化，将会加剧紧张局势。
I wish they hadn't militarized the police. = 我真希望他们没有将警察军事化。


被动语态:
The police force was militarized by the government. = 警察部队被政府军事化了。
The border region is being militarized at an alarming rate. = 边境地区正在以惊人的速度军事化。


疑问句:
Has the government militarized the internet? = 政府是否将互联网军事化了？
Will they militarize the space program? = 他们会将太空计划军事化吗？


否定句:
The country did not militarize its response to the protests. = 该国没有将其对抗议的回应军事化。
They have not yet militarized the entire region. = 他们尚未将整个地区军事化。
