invited:/ɪnˈvaɪtɪd/ | v. |邀请，恳请 | To ask (someone) to come to a social event.  |例句：They invited us to their wedding. 他们邀请我们参加他们的婚礼。|例句：She invited her friends to a party. 她邀请她的朋友参加聚会。|近义词：asked, summoned |反义词：excluded, banned |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I invite you to my party. = 我邀请你参加我的派对。
They invite guests to their home regularly. = 他们定期邀请客人到家里做客。


一般过去时:
He invited me to dinner last night. = 他昨晚邀请我吃晚餐。
She invited all her classmates to the graduation ceremony. = 她邀请了她所有的同学参加毕业典礼。


现在完成时:
I have invited all my relatives to the celebration. = 我已经邀请了我所有的亲戚参加庆祝活动。
They have invited a famous speaker to the conference. = 他们已经邀请了一位著名的演讲者参加会议。


过去完成时:
They had invited all the guests before the party started. = 派对开始前他们已经邀请了所有客人。
She had invited him to the meeting, but he couldn't make it. = 她已经邀请他参加会议了，但他没能来。


将来时:
I will invite you to my birthday party next month. = 我下个月会邀请你参加我的生日派对。
We will invite the professor to give a lecture. = 我们将邀请教授来做讲座。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would invite him. = 如果我是你，我会邀请他。
I wish I had invited her to the concert. = 我真希望我邀请她参加了音乐会。


被动语态:
We were invited to the royal wedding. = 我们被邀请参加皇室婚礼。
He was invited to speak at the conference. = 他被邀请在会议上发言。


疑问句:
Were you invited to the party? = 你被邀请参加聚会了吗？
Have they been invited to the meeting? = 他们被邀请参加会议了吗？


否定句:
I didn't invite him to the party. = 我没有邀请他参加派对。
They haven't invited her yet. = 他们还没有邀请她。


PHRASE->
be invited to = 被邀请参加
invite sb to do sth = 邀请某人做某事
invite sb over = 邀请某人过来
invite sb out = 邀请某人出去
a warm invitation = 热情的邀请


词源:
invite 源自古法语 inviter，最终源自拉丁语 invitare，意思是“to call, summon, invite”。  其词根是 vocare（to call）。
