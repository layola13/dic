fixtureless: /ˈfɪkstʃərləs/ | adj. | 无固定装置的；无设备的 | Lacking fixtures or fittings.  This term typically describes a space or structure that is devoid of permanently installed equipment or furnishings. | Example: The new office space was completely fixtureless, requiring us to install everything from desks to light fixtures.  新建的办公室完全没有固定装置，我们需要安装从办公桌到灯具的一切东西。 | 近义词: unfurnished, bare, empty | 反义词: furnished, equipped
