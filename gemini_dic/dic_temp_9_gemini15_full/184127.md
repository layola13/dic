resistances:/rɪˈzɪstənsɪz/| n.|抵抗，阻力，反抗|The act of resisting, opposing, or withstanding something.  It can refer to physical opposition, such as pushing against a force, or to opposition to an idea, plan, or authority.|例句：The army encountered strong resistances during the invasion.  军队在入侵期间遇到了强烈的抵抗。|例句：There was considerable resistance to the proposed changes.  对拟议的变革存在相当大的阻力。|近义词：opposition, defiance, resistance|反义词：compliance, acceptance, submission|名词
