paschals:/ˈpæskəlz/| n.|逾越节的；复活节的|Relating to Passover or Easter.  The word derives from the Greek word "Pascha," meaning Passover, which itself is derived from the Hebrew word "Pesach" (פֶּסַח) with the same meaning.|The Paschal lamb is a central symbol of Passover.  Many Christian traditions celebrate Easter as a Paschal feast.|近义词：Easterly, Passover, Resurrection|反义词：None
