resprinkled:/ˌriːˈsprɪŋkld/| v. |再撒上；重新撒上|To sprinkle again; to sprinkle something over something else a second time.|例句：He resprinkled the cake with powdered sugar. 他又在蛋糕上撒了一层糖粉。

例句：She resprinkled the lawn with water after the first sprinkling dried. 她第一次洒水干了之后，又给草坪洒了水。


近义词：re-scatter, redistribute


反义词：collect, gather


词性：动词
