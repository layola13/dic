homey:/ˈhoʊmi/| adj.|舒适的，家常的；朴实的|Having a comfortable, cozy, and familiar atmosphere, like home; simple and unpretentious.|例句：The cabin had a homey atmosphere, with a crackling fireplace and comfortable armchairs. 小屋有着舒适温馨的氛围，壁炉噼啪作响，舒适的扶手椅环绕其间。

例句：Her cooking is simple but homey, just the way I like it. 她的烹饪简单却家常，正合我意。


近义词：cozy, comfortable, snug, familiar, unpretentious|反义词：uncomfortable, cold, austere, pretentious|词性：形容词
