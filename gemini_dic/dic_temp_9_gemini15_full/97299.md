infoldment: /ɪnˈfoʊldmənt/ | n. | 包裹；包含；融入；内卷 | The act or process of enfolding or being enfolded; the state of being enfolded.  It often suggests a gradual or subtle process of integration or enclosure. | 例句：The poem explores the infoldment of personal experience into universal themes. 这首诗探讨了个人经验融入普遍主题的过程。

The slow infoldment of the night brought a sense of peace. 夜幕的缓缓降临带来一种平静感。


近义词：envelopment, embrace, inclusion, integration | 反义词：unfolding, exclusion, separation


词源：源于动词"infold"，由前缀"in-" (进入，内部) + "fold" (折叠，包容)构成。  "fold"的词源追溯到古英语"fealdan"，意思是"to fold"。因此，"infoldment"字面意思是"折叠进去的状态"，引申为各种形式的包裹、包含和融入。
