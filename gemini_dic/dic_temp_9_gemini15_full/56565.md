dominicide: /ˈdɒmɪnɪsaɪd/ | n. | 杀害统治者；弑君；篡位 | The killing of a dominant individual within a group, typically a leader or ruler.  This term is primarily used in zoology and political science to describe the violent overthrow and death of a leader, often within a social hierarchy.  It differs from assassination in that it emphasizes the social context and power dynamics involved. | Example: The dominicide of the alpha male resulted in a period of instability within the wolf pack.  =  首领雄狼的被杀导致狼群一段时间内不稳定。 | 近义词：regicide, tyrannicide, assassination | 反义词：deification, enthronement |  词性：名词
