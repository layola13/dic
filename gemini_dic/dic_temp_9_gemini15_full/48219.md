deconsecrating: /ˌdiːˈkɒnsɪkreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 撤圣，取消圣化 | The act of removing the sacred character from something previously consecrated; to deprive of sanctity.  | Example: The church was deconsecrated after years of disuse.  这座教堂因多年闲置而被取消圣化。 |近义词: desecrating, profaning, violating | 反义词: consecrating, sanctifying
