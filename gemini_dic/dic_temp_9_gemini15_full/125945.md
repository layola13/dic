musicale:/ˌmjuːzɪˈkɑːl/|/ˈmjuːzɪkəl/| n. adj.|音乐剧；音乐的；悦耳的|A musicale is a performance of music, often informal or private.  It can also refer to a piece of music or a musical work, especially one that is light and entertaining. As an adjective, it means relating to or characteristic of music.  |例句：They hosted a small musicale in their living room. 他们在客厅里举办了一场小型音乐会。  The film features a beautiful, sweeping musicale. 这部电影以优美动听的音乐为特色。|近义词：concert, recital, musical performance, melodious|反义词：silent, noisy, discordant|名词，形容词
