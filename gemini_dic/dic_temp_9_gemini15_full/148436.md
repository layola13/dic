overstimulating: /ˌoʊvərˈstɪmjuleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度刺激的 |  Excessively stimulating; causing an overabundance of stimulation, often leading to negative consequences such as anxiety, stress, or sensory overload.  | The overstimulating environment of the city left her feeling exhausted. = 城市过度刺激的环境让她感到筋疲力尽。The overstimulating music made it difficult to concentrate. = 过度刺激的音乐使人难以集中注意力。|近义词：overwhelming, intense, excessive, hyper-stimulating | 反义词：calming, soothing, relaxing, understimulating
