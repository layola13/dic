sativa:/səˈtiːvə/| n. |大麻（一种植物）|A tall, slender plant that is a variety of cannabis, typically grown for its fiber and seeds, but also known for its psychoactive effects when consumed.  The term often contrasts with "indica," referring to different strains with varying effects.| Example:  The farmer cultivated a large field of sativa for its hemp fiber.  农民种植了一大片大麻以获取其纤维。|近义词：cannabis, hemp (when referring to the non-psychoactive variety)|反义词：indica (in the context of cannabis strains)|名词
