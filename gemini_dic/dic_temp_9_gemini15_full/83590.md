haematoglobulin:/ˌhiːmətoʊˈɡlɒbjʊlɪn/| n. |血红蛋白球蛋白|A globulin protein found in red blood cells that is responsible for carrying oxygen.  It is a complex protein consisting of a heme group and a globin protein. Hemoglobin is the most important component of haematoglobulin.|The study focused on the role of haematoglobulin in oxygen transport.  这项研究侧重于血红蛋白球蛋白在氧气运输中的作用。|hemoglobin, globulin|—|名词
