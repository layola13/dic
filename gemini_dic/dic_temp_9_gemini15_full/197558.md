shellwork:/ˈʃɛlˌwɜːrk/| n.|贝壳工艺品；贝壳镶嵌|Decorative work made from shells, often used to adorn boxes, pictures, or other objects.  It involves arranging and gluing shells of various shapes, sizes, and colors to create intricate designs.|例句：The antique box was decorated with exquisite shellwork. 这只古董盒上装饰着精美的贝壳工艺品。|例句：She spent months creating a stunning piece of shellwork for her mother's birthday. 她花了几个月的时间为母亲的生日制作了一件令人惊叹的贝壳工艺品。|近义词：shell craft, shell mosaic|反义词：无
