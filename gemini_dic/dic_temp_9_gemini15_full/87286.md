hermeneutist: /ˌhɜːrməˈnjuːtɪst/ | n. | 解释学家；诠释学家 | A hermeneutist is a scholar who studies hermeneutics, the theory and practice of interpretation, especially of texts.  Hermeneuticists are concerned with understanding the meaning and significance of texts, taking into account their historical context, authorial intent, and the reader's own perspective. |  The renowned hermeneutist offered a new interpretation of the ancient text.  著名的诠释学家对古代文本提供了新的解释。 | interpreter, exegete, commentator |  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "misinterpreter" could be considered a contextual antonym) | 名词
