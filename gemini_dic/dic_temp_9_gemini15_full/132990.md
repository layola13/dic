USAGE->
This word is an adverb,  so it doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".  It modifies verbs, describing how an action is performed.

Example Sentences:

He moved nonenergetically across the room. 他无精打采地穿过房间。

She responded nonenergetically to his questions. 她无精打采地回答他的问题。
