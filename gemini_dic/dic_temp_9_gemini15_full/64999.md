estrangement:/ɪˈstreɪndʒmənt/| n. |疏远；隔阂；离间|The state of being estranged; a feeling of being alienated or separated from someone or something.|例句：Their estrangement lasted for many years. 他们的疏远持续了很多年。|例句：The increasing estrangement between the two countries is a matter of concern.  两国关系日益疏远，令人担忧。|近义词：alienation, separation, distance, rift|反义词：closeness, intimacy, reconciliation|名词
