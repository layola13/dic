physiogony:/ˌfɪziˈɒɡəni/| n. |面相学；体质学|The study of the face and its features, especially in relation to character or personality.  It also refers to the origin or formation of the physical body. |Example:  The physiognomy of the suspect suggested a troubled past.  嫌疑犯的面相暗示着他不光彩的过去。|近义词：physionomy,  somatology|反义词：N/A
