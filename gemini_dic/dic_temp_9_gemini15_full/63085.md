ensconcing: /ɪnˈskɒnsɪŋ/ | v. | 安置；使舒适地安顿；隐藏 |  The act of settling or placing someone or something snugly and securely.  It implies a sense of comfort and security, often in a hidden or sheltered place. |

例句：

She ensconced herself in a comfortable armchair with a good book. = 她舒服地坐在扶手椅上，拿着一本好书。

The bird ensconced its eggs in a hidden nest. = 鸟儿把蛋藏在隐蔽的巢穴里。


近义词：nestling, settling, sheltering, concealing

反义词：exposing, revealing, uncovering


词性：动词
