resurface:/ˌriːˈsɜːrfɪs/| vt. vi. n.|再浮出水面；重新出现；重新使用；重新装修表面|To appear again after being hidden or submerged, or to bring something to the surface again.  It can also refer to the act of repairing or refinishing a surface.|例句：The old memories resurfaced during the reunion.  在重逢期间，旧的记忆又浮出水面了。

The road needs resurfacing after the heavy rain.  大雨过后，这条路需要重新铺面。

近义词：reappear, reappear, renovate, refurbish|反义词：submerge, sink, disappear


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The old painting resurfaces in the auction. = 这幅旧画在拍卖会上重新出现。
He resurfces his old ideas in the meeting. = 他在会议上重新提出他的旧想法。

一般过去时:
The submarine resurfaced after several hours underwater. = 潜艇在水下几小时后浮出水面。
They resurfaced the damaged section of the highway. = 他们修复了高速公路受损的路段。

现在完成时:
The issue has resurfaced in the recent debate. = 这个问题在最近的辩论中再次出现。
The company has resurfaced its marketing strategy. = 公司已经重新调整了其营销策略。

过去完成时:
The memory had resurfaced just as she was falling asleep. = 正当她快要睡着的时候，那段记忆又浮现了出来。
The problem had resurfaced after they thought it was solved. = 他们以为问题解决了，但它又再次出现了。

将来时:
The topic will resurface in the next meeting. = 这个话题将在下次会议上再次被提及。
The government will resurface the pothole-ridden streets. = 政府将修复坑坑洼洼的街道。

虚拟语气:
If the evidence resurfaced, the case would be reopened. = 如果证据重新出现，案件将被重新审理。
I wish the old feelings would not resurface. = 我希望旧日的情感不会再次浮现。

被动语态:
The road is being resurfaced this week. = 这条路本周正在重新铺设。
The old furniture was resurfaced with a new coat of paint. = 旧家具重新刷了一层油漆。

疑问句:

否定句:
The problem didn't resurface after the initial fix. = 问题在最初修复后没有再次出现。
She tried to forget him, but his memories refused to resurface. = 她试图忘记他，但他的记忆却挥之不去。
