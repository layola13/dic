crews:/kruːz/| n.| 全体船员；工作人员；全体人员；剧组 | A group of people working together, especially on a ship, aircraft, or in a sports team.  | Example: The ship's crew worked tirelessly to save the passengers. 这艘船的船员们夜以继日地工作以拯救乘客。  The film crew worked long hours to complete the project.  电影剧组为了完成项目而长时间工作。|近义词：team, gang, staff, company |反义词：None specifically, but could be considered opposite to individuals working alone.


USAGE->

The film crew works long hours. = 电影剧组工作时间很长。
The ship's crew sails the ocean. = 船上的船员在航行在大海上。
