predetecting:/ˌpriːdɪˈtɛktɪŋ/| v. |预先检测，预先发现|  The act of detecting something before it happens or becomes apparent.  It involves using predictive methods and technologies to identify potential problems or events in advance.|例句：The system is designed for predetecting potential equipment failures.  该系统旨在预先检测潜在的设备故障。

例句：Predetecting cyberattacks is crucial for maintaining online security. 预先检测网络攻击对于维护网络安全至关重要。


近义词：foreseeing, anticipating, predicting, preempting


反义词：ignoring, overlooking, neglecting


词性：动词
