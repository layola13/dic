fruitarians:/fruːˈtɛəriən/| n. |水果食者；果食主义者 | A fruitarian is a person who follows a fruitarian diet, which consists primarily or exclusively of fruit.  This diet often includes nuts and seeds,  but typically excludes other plant-based foods like vegetables, grains, and legumes. Fruitarians believe that consuming fruit is ethically superior to consuming other plants because fruits are considered naturally self-propagating and their consumption does not harm the plant itself.  | Example:  Many fruitarians believe their diet is more ethical and sustainable.  许多水果食者认为他们的饮食更道德、更可持续。|近义词：vegetarian (but with stricter limitations), vegan (but with even stricter limitations)|反义词：carnivore, omnivore
