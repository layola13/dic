cruciate:/ˈkruːʃieɪt/| adj.|十字形的；交叉的|Cross-shaped; intersecting like a cross.  The cruciate ligaments in the knee are crucial for stability.|例句：The cruciate ligaments in the knee are crucial for stability.  膝盖的交叉韧带对稳定性至关重要。

例句：The design of the window featured a cruciate pattern.  窗户的设计采用十字形图案。


近义词：cross-shaped, intersecting, decussate|反义词：linear, straight


词源:  From Latin *cruciatus, past participle of cruciare "to torture on the cross," from crux (genitive crucis) "cross." The anatomical sense developed from the cross-like shape of the ligaments.
