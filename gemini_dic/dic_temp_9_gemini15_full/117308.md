medalist:/ˈmɛdəlɪst/| n. |奖牌获得者|A medalist is a person who has won a medal, especially in a sporting competition or the Olympics.|例句：She's a gold medalist in the 100-meter dash.  她是一百米短跑的金牌获得者。|例句：He is a former Olympic medalist. 他是一位前奥运会奖牌获得者。|近义词：winner, champion |反义词：loser
