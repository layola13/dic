epithite:/ˈɛpɪθeɪt/| n. |形容词；修饰语；称号；雅号|An adjective or descriptive phrase expressing a quality characteristic of the person or thing mentioned.  It can also be a descriptive title or epithet. |例句：The epithet "The Great" was often applied to rulers.  “大帝”这一称号常用于统治者。|近义词：adjective, descriptor, appellation, title|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be words representing opposite qualities depending on the epithet.|名词
