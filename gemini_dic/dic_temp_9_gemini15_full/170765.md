pseudocoele: /ˈsuːdoʊsiːl/ | n. | 假体腔 | A body cavity that is not a true coelom (a body cavity lined with mesoderm), but rather a space between the endoderm and the body wall.  It is formed by the blastocoel persisting into the adult stage.  | Example:  Rotifers and nematodes are examples of animals with pseudocoeloms.  轮虫和线虫是具有假体腔动物的例子。 | 近义词：blastocoel (in early development) | 反义词：coelom (true body cavity)
