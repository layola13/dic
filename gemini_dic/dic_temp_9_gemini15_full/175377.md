ralliers:/ˈræliərz/| n.|集会者；支持者；恢复元气的人|People who gather together to support a cause or person; those who rally.  Those who regain strength or recover from a setback.|例句：The political ralliers marched through the streets.  政治集会者们游行穿过街道。

The exhausted runners were ralliers in the final stretch of the marathon. 精疲力尽的跑步者在马拉松的最后一段路程中恢复了元气。

近义词：supporters, demonstrators, participants, recoverers

反义词：opponents, protesters (depending on context), detractors


词性: 名词
