oversolidifying: /ˌoʊvərˈsɑːlɪdəfaɪɪŋ/ | v. |过度固化，过度凝固 |The process of becoming excessively solid or firm, beyond the desired or optimal level.  This term is often used in contexts involving materials science, food science, and medicine.  |例句：The concrete mixture was oversolidifying, making it difficult to pour.混凝土混合物过度凝固，难以浇筑。

The prolonged baking time led to the cake oversolidifying.长时间烘烤导致蛋糕过度凝固。

近义词：overhardening, oversetting


反义词：undersetting, liquefying


词性:动词
