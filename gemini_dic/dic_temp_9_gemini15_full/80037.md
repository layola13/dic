go viral:/ɡoʊˈvaɪrəl/| adj. v.| 病毒式传播的；迅速走红的 |  Something "goes viral" when it spreads rapidly and widely through a network, especially the internet,  often through social media sharing.  It gains immense popularity in a short period. | 例句：The video of the cat playing the piano went viral overnight.  这只猫弹钢琴的视频一夜之间爆红。  The news of the scandal quickly went viral across all social media platforms. 这则丑闻的消息迅速在所有社交媒体平台上疯传。 | 近义词：spread rapidly, become a sensation, trend | 反义词：remain obscure, be ignored


USAGE->

The video goes viral every day. = 这个视频每天都在病毒式传播。
