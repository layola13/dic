literalist:/ˈlɪtərəlɪst/| n. |逐字解读者；拘泥字面意思的人 |A literalist interprets words in their most basic, literal sense, without considering context or implied meaning.  They often miss the nuances and figurative language in communication. |He's such a literalist; he took my joke completely seriously. 他是个十足的逐字解读者；他把我的笑话当真了。| interpreter, precisian | figurative speaker, metaphorical thinker | 名词
