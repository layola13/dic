multi-articulated: /ˌmʌltiˈɑːrtikjuleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 多关节的，多节的 | Having many joints or articulations; composed of many interconnected parts capable of independent movement.  | Example:  The robot arm is multi-articulated, allowing for a wide range of motion.  这个机器人手臂是多关节的，允许其具有广泛的运动范围。| 近义词：jointed, segmented, articulated | 反义词：single-jointed, unsegmented
