nonsectionally:/ˌnɒnˈsɛkʃənəli/ | adv. |非按章节地，非部分地 |Not in sections or parts; as a whole.  This adverb indicates a lack of division or compartmentalization. |The report was written nonsectionally, presenting a holistic view of the project.  这份报告并非按章节撰写，而是对项目进行了全面的概述。|holistically, comprehensively, integrally |sectionally, partially, fragmentarily


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and as such, it doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It doesn't have tenses like past, present, or future.  Its usage is consistent regardless of the grammatical tense of the sentence it modifies.


Example Sentences:

The data was analyzed nonsectionally, revealing unexpected correlations. 数据被整体分析，揭示了意想不到的相关性。

The committee considered the proposal nonsectionally, avoiding a piecemeal approach. 委员会整体考虑了提案，避免了零散的做法。


PHRASE->
There are no common idiomatic phrases directly using "nonsectionally."  Its usage is typically within a specific context of holistic or comprehensive analysis or treatment.
