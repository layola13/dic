gasify:/ˈɡæsɪfaɪ/| vt.|气化；使气体化|To convert (a solid or liquid substance) into a gas.|The coal was gasified to produce fuel gas.  煤被气化以生产燃料气。|They gasified the biomass for energy production. 他们将生物质气化以产生能量。|近义词：vaporize, volatilize|反义词：liquefy, solidify|动词
