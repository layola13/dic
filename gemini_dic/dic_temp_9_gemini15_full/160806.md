posology:/pɒˈzɒlədʒi/| n. |剂量学，用药学|The study of dosages of medicines and drugs, including their administration, distribution, metabolism, and excretion.  It focuses on determining the appropriate dose of a drug to achieve the desired therapeutic effect while minimizing adverse effects. |例句：The posology of this medication requires careful attention to the patient's age and weight.  此药物的剂量学需要仔细注意患者的年龄和体重。

例句：Accurate posology is crucial for effective treatment and patient safety.  精确的剂量学对于有效的治疗和患者安全至关重要。


近义词：pharmacology (更广义的药物学), therapeutics (治疗学)
反义词：None (没有直接的反义词)
词性：名词
