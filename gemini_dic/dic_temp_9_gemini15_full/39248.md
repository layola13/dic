congregate:/ˈkɒŋɡrɪɡeɪt/ /ˈkɑːŋɡrɪɡeɪt/| v.|聚集，集合|To come together in a group or crowd.|The students congregated in the auditorium before the lecture. 学生们在演讲前聚集在大礼堂。  The birds congregated on the power lines. 鸟儿们聚集在电线上。|gather, assemble, collect|disperse, scatter, separate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
People congregate in the town square every Sunday. = 人们每个星期天都在城镇广场聚集。
Birds congregate near the lake during migration season. = 在迁徙季节，鸟类聚集在湖边。

一般过去时:
The crowd congregated outside the stadium. = 人群聚集在体育场外。
The family congregated for Thanksgiving dinner. =  家人聚在一起吃感恩节晚餐。

现在完成时:
A large crowd has congregated to protest the new law. = 一大群人聚集起来抗议新法律。
Many people have congregated in the city center. = 许多人聚集在市中心。

过去完成时:
The protesters had congregated before the speech began. = 演讲开始前，抗议者们已经聚集了。
The animals had congregated before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，动物们已经聚集在一起了。


将来时:
The fans will congregate outside the concert hall. = 粉丝们将在音乐厅外聚集。
People will congregate to celebrate the festival. = 人们将聚集在一起庆祝节日。


虚拟语气:
If they congregated earlier, they would have better seats. = 如果他们早点聚集，他们就会有更好的座位。
I wish the crowd had congregated more peacefully. = 我希望人群能更和平地聚集。

被动语态:
The people were congregated by the police. = 人们被警察聚集起来。 (This usage is less common and often implies forced gathering.)
The protesters were congregated in a designated area. = 抗议者们被聚集在一个指定区域。


疑问句:
Where do the birds congregate in winter? = 冬天鸟儿们在哪里聚集？
Will the fans congregate before or after the game? = 粉丝们会在比赛前还是比赛后聚集？


否定句:
The students did not congregate in the library. = 学生们没有在图书馆聚集。
The animals did not congregate near the river. = 动物们没有在河边聚集。


PHRASE->
congregate together = 聚集在一起
congregate around = 聚集在...周围
congregate for = 为...而聚集
congregate in = 聚集在...
congregate to = 为了...而聚集
