combative:/kəmˈbætɪv/ | adj. | 好斗的；好战的；挑衅的 |  Inclined or eager to fight; aggressively defiant.  It describes someone who is readily inclined to argue or fight. | 例句：He became combative when questioned about his past.  他被问及过去时变得好斗起来。

例句：Her combative stance during the debate surprised everyone. 她辩论中好斗的姿态让每个人都感到惊讶。


近义词：aggressive, belligerent, pugnacious, contentious, hostile | 反义词：peaceful, docile, submissive, compliant, amiable

词源：源自combate (拉丁语)，意思是“战斗”。  The word comes from the Latin word "combattere," meaning "to fight."  It reflects the inherent nature of conflict and aggression.
