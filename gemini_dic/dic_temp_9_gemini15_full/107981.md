lashing:/ˈlæʃɪŋ/| n., v.|捆绑；鞭打；猛烈抨击；一阵|The act of lashing something or someone; a sudden, violent, or forceful action.|例句：The boat was secured by lashing the ropes to the posts.  船是用绳子捆绑在柱子上固定住的。|例句：The storm lashed the coast with fierce winds.暴风雨以狂风袭击海岸。|近义词：binding, whipping, thrashing, castigating|反义词：loosening, unbinding, praising
