self-disapprobation:/ˌsɛlfˌdɪsæprəˈbeɪʃən/| n. |自我厌恶，自我谴责|Dislike or disapproval of oneself; self-contempt.  It often arises from a perceived failure to meet one's own standards or expectations. |Example:  Her self-disapprobation was evident in her slumped posture and downcast eyes. 她垂头丧气，眼神低落，显然是对自己感到厌恶。  She felt deep self-disapprobation after failing the exam. 考试不及格后，她感到深深的自我厌恶。|近义词：self-condemnation, self-reproach, self-criticism, shame, guilt|反义词：self-acceptance, self-love, self-esteem|名词
