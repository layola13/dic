overreact:/ˌoʊvərɪˈækt/ | v. |反应过度，过激反应 | To react in an excessive or disproportionate way to something.  |例句：He overreacted to the minor criticism. 他对小小的批评反应过度了。

例句：She overreacted to the news, bursting into tears. 她对这个消息反应过度，哭了起来。


近义词：overrespond, exaggerate, overestimate, go overboard


反义词：underreact, remain calm, stay composed


USAGE->

He often overreacts to small problems. = 他经常对小问题反应过度。
They overreact to any criticism. = 他们对任何批评都反应过度。
