colting:/ˈkɒltɪŋ/| n. |小马驹；初次生驹；（马的）生驹时期|The act of a mare giving birth to a foal, or the period when a mare is giving birth to foals.  It can also refer to the young foals themselves, especially when considered collectively. |例句：The farm experienced a good colting season this year. 今年农场的小马驹出生季很顺利。|例句：The field was full of colts and fillies, all products of the recent colting.|田野里充满了小公马和小母马，都是最近出生的小马驹。|近义词：foaling, foaling season|反义词：无|名词
