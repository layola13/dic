leukaemias:/luːˈkiːmiəz/| n.|白血病（复数）|A group of cancers that usually begin in the bone marrow and result in the production of abnormal white blood cells.  These abnormal cells prevent the bone marrow from making healthy blood cells, leading to various health problems.|例句：The doctor confirmed that she had acute leukaemias. 医生确认她患有急性白血病。

例句：Research into new treatments for leukaemias is ongoing.  白血病的新疗法研究正在进行中。
