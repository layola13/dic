coordinates:/koʊˈɔːrdɪnəts/ | n. |坐标，座标 |  Numbers that show the position of a point or place, especially on a map or graph.  Coordinates are usually expressed as a set of numbers, such as latitude and longitude. | Example: The map shows the coordinates of the treasure. 这张地图显示了宝藏的坐标。 The GPS gave us the coordinates of our destination. GPS为我们提供了目的地的坐标。 |近义词：position, location |反义词：None |名词
