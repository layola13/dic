praters:/ˈpreɪtərz/| n. |喋喋不休的人；空谈家|A prater is someone who talks excessively and often about unimportant things. They may be seen as boastful or lacking in substance.  |例句：He's a terrible prater, always boasting about his nonexistent achievements. 他是个可怕的夸夸其谈者，总是吹嘘他根本不存在的成就。|例句：The prater droned on and on about his travels, ignoring everyone else's attempts to join the conversation. 那个喋喋不休的人没完没了地谈论他的旅行，无视其他人试图加入谈话的尝试。|近义词：babbler, chatterbox, windbag, gossip|反义词：listener, silent person|名词
