precharted:/priːˈtʃɑːrtɪd/| adj.|预先规划的；预先绘制的|Already planned or mapped out in advance.  It implies a prior process of planning and charting, often for navigation or a detailed course of action.|例句：The ship followed a precharted course across the ocean. 这艘船沿着预先规划的海上航线行驶。

例句：We followed a precharted itinerary during our trip to Europe. 我们在欧洲旅行期间遵循了预先规划的行程。


近义词：planned, mapped out, prearranged, predetermined


反义词：improvised, unplanned, spontaneous

词性：形容词
