deplorable:/dɪˈplɔːrəbəl/| adj. | 可悲的；令人惋惜的；极坏的 | Extremely bad or unsatisfactory; deserving strong condemnation.  |例句：The living conditions in the slum were deplorable. 贫民窟里的居住条件极其恶劣。

例句：The government's response to the crisis was deplorable. 政府对危机的应对令人遗憾。


近义词：terrible, awful, dreadful, shameful, regrettable, lamentable

反义词：admirable, praiseworthy, excellent, commendable


词源：源自法语“déplorable”，由déplorer（谴责，哀悼）+ -able（可…的）构成。最终源于拉丁语“deplorare”（哀悼，谴责）。
