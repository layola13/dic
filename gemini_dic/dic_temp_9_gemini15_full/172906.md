quake:/kweɪk/| n. vi. |地震；震动；颤抖| a sudden shaking of the ground caused by the movement of earth's plates; to shake violently | The earthquake caused widespread destruction. 这场地震造成了广泛的破坏。The ground quaked violently. 地面剧烈震动。| tremor, temblor, shudder | calm, stillness | n. vi.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The ground quakes during an earthquake. = 地震时地面会震动。
He often quakes with fear. = 他经常害怕得发抖。

一般过去时:
The city quaked after the explosion. = 爆炸后城市震动了。
She quaked at the sight of the snake. = 她看到蛇后浑身颤抖。

现在完成时:
The region has quaked several times this year. = 该地区今年已经震动了几次。
My hands have quaked since I heard the news. = 自从听到这个消息后，我的手一直在颤抖。

过去完成时:
The building had quaked violently before collapsing. = 建筑物剧烈震动后倒塌了。
She had quaked with fear before she spoke. = 她说话前害怕得发抖。

将来时:
The volcano is likely to quake again soon. = 火山很可能很快再次震动。
I think I will quake with nervousness during the presentation. = 我想我在演示期间会紧张得发抖。


虚拟语气:
If the ground quaked, we would run for cover. = 如果地面震动，我们会躲起来。
I wish the earth wouldn't quake so often. = 我希望地球不会这么频繁地地震。

被动语态:
The house was quaked by the tremor. = 房子被震动震动了。(这个被动语态略显不自然，quake 作不及物动词用得更多)
The city was shaken by the earthquake. = 城市被地震震动了。(使用更自然的动词)


疑问句:
Did the earth quake last night? = 昨晚地震了吗？
Does he quake at the thought of public speaking? = 他一想到公开演讲就害怕吗？

否定句:
The ground didn't quake during the storm. = 暴风雨期间地面没有震动。
He didn't quake in the face of danger. = 他面对危险时没有颤抖。
