/nɒnˈvjuːə(r)/| n. |非观看者，非观众 |A nonviewer is someone who does not watch or view something, such as a television program or a movie.  This term often implies a deliberate choice not to watch, rather than simply being unable to. |Example:  The study showed that nonviewers of the documentary were less informed about the issue.  这项研究表明，没有观看这部纪录片的观众对这个问题了解较少。|近义词：non-watcher, non-spectator |反义词：viewer, spectator |名词
