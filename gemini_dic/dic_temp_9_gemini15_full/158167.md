platinocyanide:/ˌplætɪnəʊˈsaɪənaɪd/| n. |铂氰化物|A salt of platinocyanic acid, containing the anion [Pt(CN)₄]²⁻.  Platinocyanides are known for their intense luminescence and were historically used in fluorescent screens and other applications. They are coordination compounds containing platinum(II) coordinated to four cyanide ligands.|The beautiful luminescence of barium platinocyanide was once used in early X-ray screens. |近义词：platinum cyanide complex (less precise)|反义词:无
