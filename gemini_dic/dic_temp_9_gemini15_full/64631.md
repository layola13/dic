erythric:/ɪˈrɪθrɪk/| adj.|赤红色的，红血球的| Relating to or resembling red blood cells or their color.  The term often refers to compounds derived from or related to erythrose, a four-carbon sugar.| Erythric acid is a rare compound found in lichens.  赤藓酸是一种在苔藓中发现的稀有化合物。|近义词：reddish, ruby, crimson|反义词：pale, colorless
