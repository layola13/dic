raskolnik:/ˈrɑːskɔːlnɪk/|/ˈræskɒlnɪk/| n. |分裂者；拉斯柯尔尼科夫（人物名）|A person who commits a crime, especially one motivated by ideological or philosophical considerations;  Specifically, the protagonist of Fyodor Dostoevsky's novel *Crime and Punishment*.  The word can also refer to a member of a schismatic religious group.|Example: Raskolnikov's crime stemmed from his nihilistic beliefs.|近义词：criminal, offender, dissenter |反义词：conformist, law-abider|名词
