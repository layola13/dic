cantaloupe:/ˈkæntəloup/| n. |甜瓜，香瓜|A type of sweet melon with a hard rind and orange flesh.  It is typically round or oval-shaped and has a sweet, musky flavor. |例句：I bought a delicious cantaloupe at the farmer's market. 我在农贸市场买了一个美味的甜瓜。|例句：The salad included chunks of cantaloupe and honeydew.沙拉里包含甜瓜和哈密瓜块。|近义词：muskmelon, melon|反义词：无
