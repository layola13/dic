purgatory:/ˈpɜːrɡətɔːri/| n. |炼狱；净化；痛苦的经历 |A state after death believed by some Christians to be one of purification or punishment before going to heaven.|例句：In some religious beliefs, purgatory is a place where souls are purified before entering heaven. 在某些宗教信仰中，炼狱是灵魂在进入天堂之前得到净化的场所。

例句：He went through a purgatory of self-doubt and anxiety before finally achieving his goal. 在最终实现目标之前，他经历了一段自我怀疑和焦虑的痛苦时期。|近义词：intermediate state, limbo, torment |反义词：heaven, paradise
