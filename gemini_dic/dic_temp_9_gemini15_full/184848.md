resynchronizes:/ˌriːˈsɪŋkrənaɪzɪz/| v.|重新同步，使重新同步|To bring back into synchronization; to restore to a synchronized state.  The process involves adjusting timing or phase to match a reference signal or system.|例句：The software automatically resynchronizes the clocks on all the computers in the network.  软件会自动重新同步网络中所有计算机上的时钟。

The system resynchronizes itself after a power outage. 系统在断电后会自动重新同步。|近义词：realign, synchronize, coordinate|反义词：desynchronize, misalign
