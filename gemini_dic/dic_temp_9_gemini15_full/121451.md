misinterprets:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɜːrprɪts/ | vt. |误解，曲解 | To understand or explain incorrectly.  |例句：He misinterpreted my silence as agreement. 他误解了我的沉默为同意。

例句：The media often misinterprets the politician's statements. 媒体经常曲解这位政治家的言论。


近义词：misunderstand, misconstrue, misjudge | 反义词：understand, interpret correctly


USAGE->

She misinterprets the instructions regularly. 她经常误解说明。
