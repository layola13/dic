rumored:/ˈruːmɚd/|/ˈruːməd/| adj.| 盛传的，谣传的 |Said to be true but not yet proven; reported as a rumour.  |例句：A rumored merger between the two companies sent their stock prices soaring.  两家公司即将合并的消息传出后，它们的股价飙升。
例句：The rumored scandal involving the politician quickly became the top news story.  涉及那位政治家的丑闻传闻迅速成为头条新闻。|近义词：reported, whispered, speculated, alleged |反义词：confirmed, proven, verified|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new product is rumored to be revolutionary. = 据说这款新产品具有革命性。
It is rumored that the CEO will resign. = 据说首席执行官将辞职。


一般过去时:
The actress was rumored to be dating a famous musician. = 据说这位女演员正在与一位著名的音乐家约会。
It was rumored that the company was facing financial difficulties. = 据说该公司正面临财务困难。


现在完成时:
The singer has been rumored to have secretly married. = 据说这位歌手秘密结婚了。
It has been rumored that a new species has been discovered. = 据说发现了一种新的物种。


过去完成时:
It had been rumored that the building was unsafe before the collapse. = 在倒塌之前，据说这座建筑物不安全。
The project had been rumored to be cancelled before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，据说该项目已被取消。


将来时:
The new phone is rumored to be released next month. = 据说这款新手机将于下个月发布。
It is rumored that the election results will be delayed. = 据说选举结果将会延迟。


虚拟语气:
If it were rumored that I was leaving, I would be upset. = 如果有传言说我要离开，我会很不安。
I wish it weren't rumored that the company was going bankrupt. = 我希望没有传言说公司要破产了。


被动语态:
The story was rumored to be untrue. = 这个故事据说是不真实的。
The details of the agreement were rumored to be kept secret. = 据说协议的细节被保密。


疑问句:
Is it rumored that the company is hiring? = 据说公司正在招聘吗？
Is it rumored that the weather will be bad tomorrow? = 据说明天的天气会很糟糕吗？


否定句:
It is not rumored that the president will step down. = 并没有传言说总统将下台。
The allegations were not rumored to be true. = 这些指控并没有传说是真的。


PHRASE->
be rumored to be = 据说...是...
be rumored to have = 据说...已经...
rumored to be involved = 据说参与...
widely rumored = 广泛传言
rumor has it that = 传言说...
