cephalitis:/ˌsɛfəˈlaɪtɪs/| n. |脑炎|Inflammation of the brain, typically caused by a viral infection but can also be caused by bacteria, fungi, or parasites.  Symptoms can include headache, fever, stiff neck, confusion, seizures, and even coma.  The severity varies depending on the cause and location of the inflammation.| Example: The child was diagnosed with encephalitis after experiencing several days of high fever and severe headache.  孩子在经历几天高烧和剧烈头痛后被诊断为脑炎。|近义词：encephalitis|反义词：N/A
