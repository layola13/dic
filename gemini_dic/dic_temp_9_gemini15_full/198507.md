shrinkage:/ˈʃrɪŋkɪdʒ/ | n. | 收缩；缩水；减少 | The reduction in size, number, or value of something.  This can refer to physical dimensions (like clothes shrinking after washing), quantity (like a decrease in workforce), or value (like a decline in the value of an asset).| Example: The shrinkage of the wool sweater after washing was significant. 洗后羊毛衫的缩水很明显。
The shrinkage in the company's profits this quarter is alarming.  本季度公司利润的减少令人担忧。|近义词：reduction, decrease, contraction |反义词：expansion, increase, growth |名词
