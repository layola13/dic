guarantee:/ˌɡærənˈtiː/|/ˌɡærənˈtiː/| n., vt.|保证；担保；保证书 |  A guarantee is a promise or assurance that something will happen or be done.  It can also refer to something that provides this assurance. |例句：The company offers a one-year guarantee on all its products. 这家公司对其所有产品提供一年的保修。

The store gave me a guarantee that the TV would work. 商店向我保证电视机能用。|近义词：warranty, assurance, promise |反义词：disclaimer, denial


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The company guarantees customer satisfaction. = 公司保证客户满意。
We guarantee the quality of our products. = 我们保证我们产品的质量。


一般过去时:
They guaranteed a quick delivery. = 他们保证快速送达。
He guaranteed me a job. = 他向我保证有一份工作。


现在完成时:
They have guaranteed the safety of the building. = 他们已经保证了建筑物的安全。
We have guaranteed that the project will be completed on time. = 我们已保证项目将按时完成。


过去完成时:
She had guaranteed the loan before the meeting. = 她曾在会议前担保了贷款。
He had guaranteed their success in the past. = 他过去曾保证过他们的成功。


将来时:
We will guarantee to fix any problems. = 我们将保证解决任何问题。
The government will guarantee the stability of the economy. = 政府将保证经济的稳定。


虚拟语气:
If they guaranteed the loan, I would invest. = 如果他们担保贷款，我会投资。
I wish they had guaranteed the quality. = 我希望他们当时保证了质量。


被动语态:
The product is guaranteed for one year. = 产品保修一年。
The success of the project was guaranteed by the team's hard work. = 项目的成功有赖于团队的辛勤工作。


疑问句:
Does the manufacturer guarantee this product? = 制造商是否保证这款产品？
Do you guarantee the delivery time? = 你能保证送货时间吗？


否定句:
They didn't guarantee the outcome of the election. = 他们没有保证选举的结果。
The warranty doesn't guarantee against accidental damage. = 保修不包括意外损坏。


PHRASE->

a money-back guarantee:退款保证
a lifetime guarantee:终身保修
a written guarantee:书面保证
under guarantee:在保修期内
give a guarantee:提供担保
