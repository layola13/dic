necrotizing:/ˈnɛkroʊtaɪzɪŋ/| adj.|坏死的；组织坏死的|causing the death of living tissue.  It describes a process where body tissue dies due to lack of blood supply or infection.|例句：The patient presented with necrotizing fasciitis, a severe bacterial infection. 患者出现坏死性筋膜炎，这是一种严重的细菌感染。

例句：The surgeon removed the necrotizing tissue to prevent further infection. 外科医生切除了坏死组织以防止进一步感染。


近义词：gangrenous, mortifying, putrefying|反义词：healing, regenerating|词性:形容词
