plonked:/plɒŋkt/ | vt. | 砰地放下；猛地坐下；粗鲁地放置 | To put or drop something down heavily and without care.  | 例句：He plonked the heavy suitcase on the floor. 他把沉重的行李箱砰地扔在地上。

例句：She plonked herself down on the sofa. 她猛地坐到沙发上。


近义词：drop, dump, thud, fling | 反义词：place carefully, gently put


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plonks his keys on the table. = 他把钥匙砰地放在桌子上。
She plonks the bag down. = 她把包砰地放下。

一般过去时:
He plonked the book onto the desk. = 他把书砰地扔到桌子上。
She plonked herself down in the chair. = 她猛地坐在了椅子上。

现在完成时:
He has plonked the dirty dishes in the sink. = 他把脏盘子砰地扔进了水槽。
She has plonked the letter on the mantelpiece. = 她把信砰地放在壁炉台上。

过去完成时:
He had plonked the bag down before he went inside. = 他进去之前已经把包砰地扔下了。
She had plonked herself down before the announcement began. = 在宣布开始之前，她已经猛地坐下了。

将来时:
He will plonk the parcel on the doorstep. = 他会把包裹砰地放在门口。
She will plonk the remote down on the coffee table. = 她会把遥控器砰地放在咖啡桌上。

虚拟语气:
If he were tired, he would plonk himself down. = 如果他累了，他会猛地坐下。
I wish she hadn't plonked the vase on the edge of the table. = 我真希望她没有把花瓶砰地放在桌边。

被动语态:
The parcel was plonked on the doorstep. = 包裹被砰地放在了门口。
The cake was plonked unceremoniously on the table. = 蛋糕被粗鲁地砰地放在桌子上。

疑问句:
Did he plonk the suitcase on the floor? = 他是不是把行李箱砰地扔在地上了？
Will she plonk the cup down without a care? = 她会不在乎地把杯子砰地放下吗？

否定句:
He didn't plonk the letter on the table; he placed it carefully. = 他没有把信砰地扔在桌子上；他小心地放下了。
She didn't plonk the child down; she gently sat him on the floor. = 她没有把孩子砰地放下；她轻轻地把他放在地上。
