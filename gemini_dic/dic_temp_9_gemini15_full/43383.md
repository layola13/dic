crank:/kræŋk/| n., v.|曲柄；怪人；脾气暴躁的人；启动；使旋转|

英文解析:
As a noun, "crank" refers to a bent piece of metal used to rotate a machine, a person who holds eccentric or strange beliefs, or someone who is irritable and bad-tempered.  As a verb, it means to start or operate a machine by turning a crank, or to forcefully turn something.

例句:
He used a crank to start the old engine. = 他用曲柄启动旧引擎。

The old car needed a good crank to get going. = 这辆旧车需要用力摇动曲柄才能启动。


近义词:
Noun (for the person): eccentric, oddball, crankcase,  grump, curmudgeon
Verb: rotate, turn, start

反义词:
Noun (for the person):  normal, reasonable, agreeable
Verb: stop


词性:
Noun, Verb
