gemelliparous:/dʒɛmɛˈlɪpərəs/| adj.| 双胞胎的，孪生的 | Able to bear twins; producing twins.  This adjective describes the ability or tendency to give birth to twins.  | Example:  The breed of sheep is known for its gemelliparous nature.  这个羊种以其孪生习性而闻名。


The rabbit is a gemelliparous animal, often producing litters of multiple offspring. 这只兔子是双胞胎动物，经常产下多胎后代。| 近义词：bigenital, twin-bearing | 反义词：uniparous (producing only one offspring at a time)


词源:

* **gemelli-:** From Latin "gemelli," meaning "twins."
* **-parous:** From Latin "-parus," a suffix meaning "producing" or "bearing."  This suffix is commonly used in biological terms to describe the ability to produce offspring of a certain type.


用法说明：

gemelliparous is primarily used in a biological context, referring to animals capable of producing twins.  It's less common in everyday language compared to simpler terms like "twin-bearing."  The word itself is descriptive rather than evaluative; it simply states the fact of twin birth capability.  There are no common idiomatic expressions or phrases involving "gemelliparous".

由于gemelliparous是一个较为专业的形容词，没有对应的时态变化。以下列出其他语法范例，以展示其在不同句法结构中的用法：

肯定句:
The species is gemelliparous.  这个物种是双胞胎的。
That breed of cattle is highly gemelliparous.  那个牛种非常容易产双胞胎。


否定句:
The animal is not gemelliparous; it typically gives birth to single offspring.  这种动物不是双胞胎的；它通常只产下一只后代。
This particular species is not known to be gemelliparous.  这个物种并非已知会产双胞胎。


疑问句:
Is this breed of dog gemelliparous?  这个犬种是双胞胎的吗？
Are these animals known to be gemelliparous?  这些动物已知会产双胞胎吗？


比较级和最高级:  gemelliparous本身并非一个可以进行比较等级修饰的形容词，因为其描述的是一种生物学特性，而非程度。  你可以说一个物种比另一个物种更可能产双胞胎（more likely to produce twins），但不能说一个物种比另一个物种更“gemelliparous”。
