firm-up: /fɜːrm ʌp/ | vt. | 使稳固；使坚实；加强；明确；变得坚定 | to make something stronger or more solid; to become stronger or more definite | 近义词：strengthen, consolidate, solidify, reinforce | 反义词：weaken, undermine, destabilize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is firming up its plans for economic recovery. = 政府正在巩固其经济复苏计划。
They are firming up the deal with the new supplier. = 他们正在与新的供应商敲定交易。


一般过去时:
The team firmed up their strategy before the competition. = 团队在比赛前巩固了他们的策略。
He firmed up his resolve to quit smoking. = 他下定决心戒烟。


现在完成时:
The company has firmed up its position in the market. = 公司已经巩固了其在市场上的地位。
She has firmed up her ideas for the project. = 她已经明确了她的项目想法。


过去完成时:
They had firmed up their agreement before the deadline. = 他们在大限前已经敲定了协议。
We had firmed up our plans for the trip before the weather turned bad. = 天气变坏之前，我们已经确定了旅行计划。


将来时:
The construction company will firm up the foundation before building the house. = 建设公司将在建房前夯实地基。
They will firm up the details of the contract next week. = 他们下周将敲定合同细节。


虚拟语气:
If the government firmed up its policies, the economy would improve. = 如果政府加强政策，经济就会好转。
I wish they would firm up their commitment to the project. = 我希望他们能坚定对项目的承诺。


被动语态:
The plans were firmed up after several revisions. =  经过多次修改后，计划得到了巩固。
The agreement will be firmed up by the end of the month. = 协议将在月底敲定。


疑问句:
Have they firmed up the date for the meeting yet? = 他们已经确定会议日期了吗？
Will the government firm up its stance on immigration? = 政府会坚定其在移民问题上的立场吗？


否定句:
The company hasn't firmed up its marketing strategy yet. = 公司尚未确定其营销策略。
He didn't firm up his decision until the last minute. = 他直到最后一刻才下定决心。


PHRASE->

firm up the details: 细化细节
firm up the evidence: 加强证据
