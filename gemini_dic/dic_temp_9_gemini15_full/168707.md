procrustesian:/proʊkrʌsˈtiːziən/| adj.| 粗暴强求一致的；强行扭曲的；墨守成规的 |  Relating to or characterized by Procrustes, a figure in Greek mythology who stretched or shortened his victims to fit his bed.  Figuratively, it describes something that is forced to conform to a preconceived standard, often in a brutal or unnatural way.  It implies a disregard for individual differences or natural variations. | Example: The procrustean approach to education ignored the diverse learning styles of the students.  教育中这种粗暴强求一致的方法忽略了学生不同的学习风格。 | 近义词：rigid, inflexible, inflexible, doctrinaire, authoritarian | 反义词：flexible, adaptable, accommodating, tolerant
