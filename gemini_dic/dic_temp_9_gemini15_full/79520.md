globs:/ɡlɒbz/| n. |团块；团状物；大量；(非正式)一堆东西|A glob is a small, shapeless lump or mass of a viscous substance, such as mud, paint, or blood.  It can also refer to a large, undefined quantity of something.|例句：A glob of paint splattered on the canvas. 一团颜料溅到画布上。

例句：He wiped a glob of grease from his hands. 他擦去了手上的油污。


近义词：lump, blob, mass, clump
反义词：scatter, disperse


词性:名词
