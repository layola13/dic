deallocate:/ˌdiːˈæləkeɪt/ | vt. |释放（内存、资源等）|To release or make available for other use (especially computer memory or other resources) that has been previously allocated.|The program deallocated the memory after it was finished processing the data.  程序处理完数据后释放了内存。|allocate, release, free|allocate, reserve, retain|动词
