decession:/dɪˈsɛʃən/| n. |脱离；退出；(尤指正式的)退位|The formal withdrawal of a state from a federation or body.  It often involves a formal declaration and process.  This word is less common than "secession."|例句：The decession of the southern states from the Union led to the American Civil War. 南方各州脱离联邦导致了美国内战。|近义词：secession, withdrawal, departure|反义词：accession, joining, adhesion


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The state is considering decession. = 该州正在考虑脱离。
This group plans decession from the organization. = 这个团体计划退出该组织。


一般过去时:
The colony declared its decession from the empire. = 该殖民地宣布脱离帝国。
Several members voted for decession from the union. = 几名成员投票赞成退出联盟。


现在完成时:
Several regions have completed their decession from the larger nation. = 几个地区已经完成了从大国中的脱离。
The party has announced its decession from the coalition. = 该党宣布退出联盟。


过去完成时:
Before the war, several states had already planned their decession. = 在战争爆发前，一些州就已经计划脱离了。
The group had finalized its decession before the public announcement. = 在公开宣布之前，该团体已经完成了退出。


将来时:
The region will likely pursue decession in the coming years. = 该地区很可能会在未来几年寻求脱离。
The organization will announce its decession next month. = 该组织将在下个月宣布退出。


虚拟语气:
If the people voted for decession, the country would be divided. = 如果人民投票赞成脱离，国家就会分裂。
I wish the country had not undergone decession. = 我希望国家没有经历过分裂。


被动语态:
Decession was declared by the rebellious province. = 脱离是由叛乱省份宣布的。
The decession was finalized after weeks of negotiations. = 脱离在几周的谈判后最终确定。


疑问句:
Will the state pursue decession? = 该州会寻求脱离吗？
Has the group made a decision about decession? = 该团体是否已就脱离问题做出决定？


否定句:
The province did not support decession. = 该省不支持脱离。
The party refused to consider decession. = 该党拒绝考虑脱离。
