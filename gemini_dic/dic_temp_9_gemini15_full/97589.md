inhabitate:/ɪnˈhæbɪteɪt/|/ˈɪnˌhæbɪˌteɪt/| vt.|居住，栖息|To live in or occupy (a place).  It also refers to the living organisms that occupy a particular area.|例句：Many different species inhabit the rainforest.  许多不同的物种栖息在雨林中。

例句：The birds inhabit the tall trees. 这些鸟类栖息在高大的树木上。


近义词：occupy, populate, dwell in, reside in|反义词：evacuate, vacate, desert|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They inhabit the coastal areas. = 他们居住在沿海地区。
Birds inhabit the forest. = 鸟类栖息在森林里。

一般过去时:
Ancient civilizations inhabited this region. = 古代文明居住在这个地区。
Dinosaurs inhabited the Earth millions of years ago. = 恐龙在数百万年前居住在地球上。

现在完成时:
Humans have inhabited this planet for thousands of years. = 人类已经在这个星球上居住了几千年了。
Many species have inhabited this island for centuries. = 许多物种已经在这个岛屿上栖息了几个世纪。

过去完成时:
Before the flood, the village had inhabited the riverbank for generations. = 在洪水泛滥之前，这个村庄世世代代居住在河岸边。
The explorers discovered that the island had been inhabited by a tribe for many years. = 探险家们发现这个岛屿已经被一个部落居住多年了。

将来时:
We will inhabit Mars in the future. = 我们将来会居住在火星上。
They believe that humans will inhabit other planets one day. = 他们相信人类总有一天会居住在其他星球上。

虚拟语气:
If we had better technology, we could inhabit the moon. = 如果我们有更好的技术，我们就能居住在月球上了。
I wish we could inhabit a planet with more resources. = 我希望我们能居住在一个资源更丰富的星球上。

被动语态:
The area was inhabited by nomadic tribes. = 这个地区被游牧部落居住。
The island is inhabited by a small community. = 这个岛屿被一个小社区居住。

疑问句:
Does anyone inhabit that remote island? = 有人居住在那个偏远的岛屿上吗？
What kinds of animals inhabit this cave? = 什么样的动物居住在这个洞穴里？

否定句:
No one inhabits the abandoned building. = 没有人居住在废弃的建筑物里。
The area was not inhabited until the 18th century. = 直到18世纪，这个地区才有人居住。
