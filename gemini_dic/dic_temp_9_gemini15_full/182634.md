reordered:/ˌriːˈɔːrdərd/| adj.|重新排序的，重新安排的|Having been put in a new order.|The reordered list is now alphabetically arranged.  重新排序的列表现在按字母顺序排列。|近义词： rearranged, reorganized, reshuffled |反义词：unchanged, original


USAGE->

The schedule is reordered every week. = 时间表每周都被重新安排。
