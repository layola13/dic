oarfish:/ˈɔːrfɪʃ/| n. |皇带鱼|A deep-sea fish characterized by its long, ribbon-like body and dorsal fin running the length of its back.  It is known for its unusual appearance and is rarely seen alive.|Example: The divers were astonished to encounter an oarfish during their deep-sea expedition.  潜水员在深海探险中偶然遇到皇带鱼，感到非常惊讶。|近义词：ribbonfish|反义词：无

词源:
The name "oarfish" is a descriptive term combining "oar" (referring to the long, oar-like shape of its fins) and "fish".  The scientific name *Regalecus glesne* reflects its regal appearance.
