kloof:/kluːf/| n.|峡谷，峡；裂缝|A kloof is a narrow, steep-sided ravine or gorge, typically found in mountainous or hilly terrain.  It is a characteristic feature of the landscape in certain regions, particularly in South Africa. |例句：We hiked through a deep kloof in the Drakensberg mountains. 我们徒步穿越了德拉肯斯堡山脉中的一条深深的峡谷。

例句：The kloof was so narrow that the sunlight barely reached the bottom. 峡谷如此狭窄，阳光几乎照不到底部。|近义词：gorge, ravine, chasm, canyon|反义词：plain, plateau
