reaggravation:/ˌriːæɡrəˈveɪʃən/| n. |复发；恶化；加重|The worsening or intensification of a problem or condition, especially a disease or injury.  It implies a previous occurrence of the problem that has returned or become more severe.|例句：The reaggravation of his knee injury forced him to withdraw from the competition.  他的膝伤复发迫使他退出了比赛。

例句：Her asthma suffered a reaggravation due to the polluted air.  她的哮喘因空气污染而加重了。


近义词：exacerbation, worsening, intensification, recurrence


反义词：improvement, alleviation, remission


词性:名词
