obtainable:/əbˈteɪnəbl/ | adj. | 可获得的，可得到的 | attainable, available, achievable | unattainable, unavailable | 形容词

英文解析：Able to be obtained; achievable.  Something that is obtainable is something that can be acquired or achieved.

例句：

The information is readily obtainable online. = 这些信息很容易在网上获得。

Tickets for the concert are obtainable through the box office. = 音乐会的门票可通过售票处获得。


近义词：attainable, available, achievable, procurable

反义词：unattainable, unavailable, unachievable, inaccessible


词性：形容词
