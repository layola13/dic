contemplated:/ˈkɒntɛmpleɪtɪd/ | /ˈkɑːntɛmpleɪtɪd/ | v. | 沉思； contemplate的过去式和过去分词 | To think deeply and at length; to meditate.  | 例句：He contemplated the meaning of life. 他沉思生命的意义。  | 例句：She contemplated her options carefully before making a decision.  她在做决定之前仔细考虑了她的选择。 | 近义词：ponder, meditate, reflect, muse | 反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I contemplate the vastness of the universe. = 我沉思宇宙的浩瀚。
She contemplates her future career path. = 她思考她未来的职业道路。

一般过去时:
He contemplated his mistakes for hours. = 他花了几个小时思考他的错误。
They contemplated the proposal before voting. = 他们在投票前仔细考虑了这个提案。

现在完成时:
I have contemplated this problem for days. = 我已经考虑这个问题好几天了。
She has contemplated moving to a new city. = 她一直在考虑搬到一个新的城市。

过去完成时:
He had contemplated quitting his job for months before he finally did. = 在他最终辞职之前，他已经考虑辞职好几个月了。
They had contemplated all the possibilities before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前已经考虑了所有可能性。

将来时:
I will contemplate the offer before I respond. = 我会在回复之前仔细考虑这个提议。
She will contemplate the consequences of her actions. = 她将思考她行为的后果。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would contemplate the matter further. = 如果我有更多时间，我会进一步思考这个问题。
I wish I had contemplated the risks more carefully. = 我希望我当时更仔细地考虑了风险。

被动语态:
The problem was contemplated by the committee. = 这个问题由委员会进行了仔细考虑。
The proposal is being contemplated by the board. = 董事会正在考虑这个提案。

疑问句:
Have you contemplated the possibility of failure? = 你有没有考虑过失败的可能性？
What are you contemplating now? = 你现在在思考什么？

否定句:
He did not contemplate the consequences. = 他没有考虑后果。
She did not contemplate the long-term effects. = 她没有考虑长远的影响。

contemplate the future = 展望未来
contemplate a purchase = 考虑购买
contemplate suicide = 考虑自杀
