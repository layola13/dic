overhelpful:/ˌoʊvərˈhɛlpfəl/| adj. | 过度热心的；多管闲事的 | Excessively helpful; interfering.  Often implies that the help is unwanted or unhelpful in its excessive nature. |例句：He was overhelpful, constantly interrupting and offering unsolicited advice. 他过度热心，不断打断并提供不请自来的建议。


例句：Her overhelpful nature often annoyed her colleagues.  她过度热心的天性常常惹恼她的同事们。


近义词： meddlesome, interfering, officious, intrusive


反义词： unhelpful, uncooperative, indifferent
