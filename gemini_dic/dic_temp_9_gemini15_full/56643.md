donnish:/ˈdɒnɪʃ/| adj.| 书生气的；迂腐的；学究气的 | Relating to or characteristic of a don (a university teacher, especially at Oxford or Cambridge).  It often implies pedantry, formality, and a detachment from the concerns of ordinary life. | His donnish mannerisms made him seem aloof and unapproachable. 他书生气十足的举止使他显得冷漠而难以接近。|近义词：pedantic, scholarly, academic, professorial |反义词：worldly, practical, informal
