president: /ˈprezɪdənt/ | n. |总统，会长，校长 | A person who holds the highest position in a country or organization.  |例句：The president addressed the nation. 总统向全国发表讲话。|例句：The university president announced a new policy. 大学的校长宣布了一项新政策。|近义词：leader, chief, chairman, head |反义词：subject, subordinate
