lunching:/ˈlʌntʃɪŋ/ | v. | 午餐；吃午饭 | The act of having lunch; the process of eating lunch.  |  例句：We are lunching at a new restaurant today. = 我们今天在一个新餐厅吃午饭。
例句：She's lunching with her clients. = 她正在和她的客户共进午餐。 | 近义词：dining, eating (lunch) | 反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to the opposite of eating, such as fasting. | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are lunching at the cafe. = 他们在咖啡馆吃午饭。
We lunch together every Friday. = 我们每周五一起吃午饭。

一般过去时:
I lunched with my friend yesterday. = 我昨天和我朋友一起吃午饭了。
They lunched early to catch the train. = 他们为了赶火车早早地吃了午饭。

现在完成时:
We have lunched already. = 我们已经吃过午饭了。
She has not lunched yet. = 她还没吃午饭。


过去完成时:
They had lunched before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他们已经吃过午饭了。
I had lunched quickly to make the appointment on time. = 我为了按时赴约快速地吃了午饭。

将来时:
We will be lunching with the mayor tomorrow. = 我们明天将与市长共进午餐。
I will lunch at my desk today. = 我今天将在办公桌前吃午饭。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would lunch with you. = 如果我有时间，我会和你一起吃午饭。
I wish I could lunch at a nicer restaurant. = 我真希望能在更好的餐厅吃午饭。

被动语态:
Lunch is being served now. = 现在正在供应午餐。 (This is less common with "lunching")
The meeting was being interrupted by those who were lunching. = 会议正被那些正在吃午饭的人打断。


疑问句:
Are you lunching today? = 你今天吃午饭吗？
Where are you lunching? = 你在哪里吃午饭？

否定句:
I am not lunching today. = 我今天不吃午饭。
They did not lunch together. = 他们没有一起吃午饭。



PHRASE->
lunching out = 外出吃午饭

power lunch =  重要的商务午餐
