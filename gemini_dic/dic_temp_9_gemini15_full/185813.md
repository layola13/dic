revisits:/ˌriːˈvɪzɪts/ | v. | 再访问；重温；重新审视 | To visit again; to reconsider or review something. |例句：He revisits his childhood home every summer. 他每年夏天都会重访他的童年故居。

例句：She revisits the events of that day in her mind, trying to understand what went wrong. 她反复回想起那天发生的事情，试图弄明白哪里出了错。


近义词：revisits, revises, reviews, re-examines | 反义词：ignores, neglects


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He revisits his family every Christmas. = 他每年圣诞节都会去看望家人。
She revisits her notes before the exam. = 考试前她会复习笔记。


一般过去时:
They revisited the museum last week. = 他们上周又去了趟博物馆。
He revisited his decision after careful consideration. = 他仔细考虑后重新审视了他的决定。


现在完成时:
I have revisited that old house several times. = 我已经多次重访过那栋老房子。
She has revisited her childhood memories in her new novel. = 她在新小说中重温了她童年的回忆。


过去完成时:
They had revisited the topic many times before reaching a conclusion. = 他们在得出结论之前已经多次重新讨论过这个话题。
Before making the final decision, he had revisited the project proposal several times. = 在做出最终决定之前，他多次重新审视了项目提案。


将来时:
We will revisit this issue at the next meeting. = 我们将在下次会议上重新讨论这个问题。
She will revisit her strategy if the initial plan fails. = 如果最初的计划失败，她将重新调整策略。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would revisit my old hobby. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重拾我的旧爱好。
I wish I could revisit my youth. = 我真希望可以重回我的青春时代。


被动语态:
The issue will be revisited at a later date. = 这个问题将在稍后时间重新讨论。
The old building is being revisited by many tourists. = 这座旧建筑正在被许多游客重新参观。


疑问句:
Will you revisit your decision? = 你会重新考虑你的决定吗？
Have you ever revisited your old school? = 你曾经重返你的旧学校吗？


否定句:
He did not revisit the subject again. = 他没有再次讨论这个话题。
She will not revisit her mistakes. = 她不会重蹈覆辙。


PHRASE->
revisit the past = 回顾过去
revisit a decision = 重新考虑一个决定
revisit a topic = 重新讨论一个话题
revisit a plan = 重新制定计划
revisit a memory = 回忆往事
