outperform:/ˌaʊtpərˈfɔːrm/| vt.|胜过，超越|To do or perform better than (someone or something else).  It implies exceeding expectations or achieving a higher level of success.|例句：Our sales team outperformed all expectations this quarter.  本季度我们的销售团队超额完成了所有预期。|例句：The new model outperforms its predecessor in terms of speed and efficiency.这款新车型在速度和效率方面都优于其前代产品。|近义词：surpass, exceed, excel, top|反义词：underperform, lag behind|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new software outperforms the old one. = 新软件比旧软件性能更好。
This company consistently outperforms its competitors. = 这家公司始终优于其竞争对手。

一般过去时:
Last year, our investment portfolio outperformed the market average. = 去年，我们的投资组合跑赢了市场平均水平。
She outperformed all the other candidates in the interview. = 她在面试中胜过所有其他候选人。

现在完成时:
Our team has outperformed its sales targets for three consecutive quarters. = 我们的团队连续三个季度超额完成了销售目标。
The fund has consistently outperformed its benchmark index. = 该基金一直跑赢其基准指数。

过去完成时:
By the end of the year, the company had outperformed all projections. = 到年底，该公司已超出了所有预期。
He had outperformed his rivals in the race, securing a decisive victory. = 他在比赛中胜过对手，取得了决定性的胜利。


将来时:
Analysts predict that the company will outperform its rivals next year. = 分析师预测该公司明年将胜过其竞争对手。
We expect our new product to outperform the competition in the market. = 我们预计我们的新产品将在市场上胜过竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If we had invested in that company, we would have outperformed the market. = 如果我们投资那家公司，我们的投资回报率就会超过市场平均水平。
I wish I had outperformed my expectations on that project. = 我希望我在那个项目上能够超额完成预期。


被动语态:
The company's performance was outperformed by its main competitor.  = 该公司的业绩被其主要竞争对手超越。
The expectations were outperformed by the actual results. = 实际结果超出了预期。


疑问句:
Does this model outperform the previous one? = 这个型号的性能比之前的更好吗？
Will this strategy outperform the others? = 这个策略会比其他的策略更好吗？


否定句:
The company failed to outperform its competitors. = 该公司未能超越其竞争对手。
His performance did not outperform the others. = 他的表现没有超过其他人。


PHRASE->
outperform the market = 跑赢市场
outperform expectations = 超出预期
outperform competitors = 胜过竞争对手
significantly outperform = 明显优于
consistently outperform = 一直优于
