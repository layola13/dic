reavow:/ˌriːəˈvaʊ/| vt.|重新声明；再次保证；重申|To state or affirm again; to renew one's declaration of.  |例句：He reavowed his commitment to the cause. 他再次承诺支持这项事业。|例句：After initial hesitation, she reavowed her love for him. 在最初的犹豫之后，她重申了她对他的爱。|近义词：reaffirm, reaffirm, reiterate, reassert|反义词：renounce, deny, retract|动词
