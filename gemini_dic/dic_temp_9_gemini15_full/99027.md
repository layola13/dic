interagreement: /ˌɪntərəˈɡriːmənt/ | n. | 协议；相互协议 | An agreement or understanding between two or more parties.  It often implies a mutual understanding or compromise reached through discussion and negotiation.  | Example: The interagreement between the two companies facilitated a smooth merger.  两家公司之间的协议促成了顺利的合并。 |近义词：agreement, accord, treaty, understanding |反义词：disagreement, discord, conflict
