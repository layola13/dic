regoverned:/ˌriːˈɡʌvənd/ | v. |重新治理；再次管理 | To govern or rule again; to subject to governmental control once more.  | The newly elected officials regoverned the city after a period of instability. 新当选的官员在一段时间的动荡后重新治理了这座城市。 |  govern, administer, rule | anarchy, chaos | 动词

They regovern the company after the scandal. = 丑闻之后，他们重新管理公司。
