floribunda:/ˌflɒrɪˈbʌndə/ | adj. | 多花的；丰花的 | Abundantly flowering;  referring to a type of rose that produces many flowers in clusters. | 例句：The floribunda rose bushes were a riot of color.  多花月季灌木丛一片色彩缤纷。  | 例句：Her garden boasts several varieties of floribunda roses. 她的花园里拥有几种不同品种的多花月季。|近义词：profuse, abundant, prolific | 反义词：sparse, scanty

词源:
From Latin *floribundus, from *flori- (from flos "flower") + -bundus ("-full").  The word combines the Latin word "flos" (flower) with the suffix "-bundus," meaning "abundant" or "full of."  Therefore, "floribunda" literally means "full of flowers."
