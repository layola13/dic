petroleur:/pɛtʁɔlœʁ/| n.|石油工人，石油贩子|A petroleur is a worker in the petroleum industry, often associated with the extraction, transportation, or sale of petroleum products.  The term can also refer to a person who deals in or sells petroleum.  The term carries a historical connotation, often associated with the early days of the oil industry.|例句：The petroleurs worked long hours in challenging conditions.  石油工人每天工作时间很长，工作环境艰苦。
The old photograph showed a group of grizzled petroleurs around a newly discovered well. 老照片上显示一群满脸皱纹的石油工人围在一口新发现的油井旁。|近义词：oil worker, petroleum worker, oilman|反义词：无
