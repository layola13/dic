hazard:/ˈhæzərd/| n. v.|危险，危害；冒险|A hazard is something that could be dangerous or cause damage.  It can also refer to a risk or chance of something unpleasant happening.|例句：Driving in icy conditions is a hazard. 在结冰的路况下开车很危险。
例句：The company assessed the hazards of the new chemical. 公司评估了这种新化学品的危害。|近义词：danger, risk, peril|反义词：safety, security|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The old building presents a fire hazard. = 这座旧建筑存在火灾隐患。
This project has several safety hazards. = 这个项目存在几个安全隐患。


一般过去时:
The storm posed a significant hazard to coastal communities. = 暴风雨对沿海社区构成了重大危险。
He ignored the hazards and continued working. = 他无视危险继续工作。


现在完成时:
They have identified all potential hazards in the factory. = 他们已经查明了工厂里所有潜在的危险。
We have mitigated many of the environmental hazards. = 我们已经减轻了许多环境危害。


过去完成时:
The engineers had underestimated the hazards of the project. = 工程师们低估了该项目的危险性。
They had already encountered several hazards before reaching the summit. = 他们在到达山顶之前就已经遇到过好几次危险了。


将来时:
The rising sea levels will pose a greater hazard to low-lying areas. = 海平面上升将对低洼地区构成更大的危险。
Future climate change will create new hazards for global populations. = 未来气候变化将为全球人口创造新的危害。


虚拟语气:
If they had been aware of the hazards, they would have taken precautions. = 如果他们知道这些危险，他们就会采取预防措施。
I wish I had known about the hazards before I started the job. = 我希望在我开始工作之前就知道这些危险。


被动语态:
The hazards were carefully assessed before the construction began. = 在施工开始之前，对危险进行了仔细评估。
Many hazards are associated with this type of work. = 许多危险与这种类型的工种相关。


疑问句:
What are the main hazards associated with this job? = 与这项工作相关的主要危险是什么？
Are there any known hazards in this area? = 这个地区有任何已知的危险吗？


否定句:
There were no apparent hazards during the operation. = 在手术过程中没有明显的危险。
The company claimed there were no hazards associated with the product. = 公司声称该产品没有任何危害。

PHRASE->

safety hazard = 安全隐患
fire hazard = 火灾隐患
potential hazard = 潜在危险
