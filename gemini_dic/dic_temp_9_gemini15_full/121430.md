misinstruct:/ˌmɪsɪnˈstrʌkt/| vt. |错误指导，误导|To give someone the wrong instructions or information, leading them to do something incorrectly or inappropriately.  |例句：The map misinstructed us, and we ended up lost in the woods. 地图误导了我们，结果我们在树林里迷路了。

例句：He misinstructed his assistant, causing a significant delay in the project. 他错误地指导了他的助手，导致项目严重延误。


近义词：mislead, misguide, confuse, deceive

反义词：instruct, guide, direct, clarify

词性：动词
