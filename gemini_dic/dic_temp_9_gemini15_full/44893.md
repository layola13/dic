cryogenist:/ˈkraɪəʊdʒənɪst/| n. |低温学家，冷冻学家|A cryogenist is a scientist who specializes in cryogenics, the study and application of very low temperatures.  They work with materials and processes at temperatures below -150°C (-238°F).|The cryogenist carefully monitored the temperature of the liquid nitrogen.|近义词：cryophysicist, low-temperature physicist|反义词：无
