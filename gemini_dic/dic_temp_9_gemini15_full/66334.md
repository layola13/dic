excubation:/ˌɛkˈskjuːbeɪʃən/ | n. |孵化（尤指非鸟类动物的）, 潜伏|The process of incubation, especially in animals other than birds.  It refers to the period during which eggs or young are kept warm and protected until they hatch or are able to fend for themselves. This term often emphasizes the care and protection provided during this period. |Example: The meticulous excubation of the reptile eggs ensured a high hatching rate.  爬行动物蛋的细致孵化确保了较高的孵化率。|近义词：incubation, brooding, hatching |反义词：abandonment
