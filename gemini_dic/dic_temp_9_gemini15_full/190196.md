santorini:/ˌsæn(t)əˈriːni/| n. |圣托里尼（希腊爱琴海岛屿）|Santorini is a volcanic island group in the southern Aegean Sea, about 200 km (120 mi) northeast of Crete, and 110 km (68 mi) south of Naxos.  It is the remnants of a volcanic caldera. The largest island is also called Santorini. It is known for its stunning white-washed buildings, blue-domed churches, and breathtaking sunsets.|例句：We spent a week on Santorini, enjoying the beautiful beaches and charming villages. 我们在圣托里尼度过了一周，享受着美丽的沙滩和迷人的村庄。

例句：The iconic sunsets of Santorini are world-renowned. 圣托里尼标志性的日落闻名世界。
