details:/ˈdiːteɪlz/ | n. |细节，详情 |Details are important in this case.  在这个案例中，细节很重要。| specifics, particulars | generalities


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The report details the company's financial performance. = 这份报告详细说明了公司的财务业绩。
He details his travel plans to his family. = 他向家人详细说明了他的旅行计划。


一般过去时:
She detailed her experiences in the Amazon rainforest. = 她详细描述了她亚马逊雨林的经历。
The witness detailed the events leading up to the accident. = 目击者详细描述了导致事故发生之前的事件。


现在完成时:
He has detailed all the necessary steps in his instruction manual. = 他已在他的说明书中详细说明了所有必要的步骤。
She has detailed the challenges faced by the team. = 她已经详细说明了团队面临的挑战。


过去完成时:
The historian had detailed the life of the emperor in his previous book. = 这位历史学家在他之前的书中已经详细介绍了这位皇帝的一生。
They had detailed their concerns to the management before the meeting. = 会议前他们已向管理层详细说明了他们的担忧。


将来时:
The architect will detail the design of the new building next week. = 建筑师下周将详细说明新建筑的设计。
She will detail her findings in her upcoming presentation. = 她将在即将到来的演示文稿中详细说明她的发现。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would detail every aspect of the project. = 如果我有更多时间，我会详细说明项目的各个方面。
I wish I could detail the beauty of the sunset. = 我希望我能详细描述日落的美丽。


被动语态:
The plan was detailed in the meeting. = 计划在会议上得到了详细说明。
The process is detailed in the user manual. = 该过程在用户手册中已详细说明。


疑问句:

What details do you need to know? = 你需要知道哪些细节？


否定句:
He didn't detail the reasons for his resignation. = 他没有详细说明他辞职的原因。
The report didn't detail the impact of the policy change. = 该报告没有详细说明政策变化的影响。
