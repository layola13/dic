contagiously:/kənˈteɪdʒəsli/ | adv. |  具有传染性地；容易传染地 |  In a way that is likely to spread rapidly from one person to another.  | Example: The laughter spread contagiously throughout the room.  房间里笑声传染般地蔓延开来。  The disease spread contagiously through the population. 疾病在人群中迅速蔓延。 | 近义词：infectiously, communicably | 反义词：incommunicably

He laughs contagiously. = 他笑得很传染。
The rumor spreads contagiously. = 谣言迅速传播。
