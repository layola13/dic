overcorrects:/ˌoʊvər kəˈrɛkts/| v.|矫枉过正，过度纠正|To correct something to an excessive degree, often resulting in a worse outcome than the initial problem.|例句：He overcorrects his steering wheel when driving, leading to swerving. 他开车时过度纠正方向盘，导致车辆摇摆不定。


例句：The teacher overcorrects her students' grammar, making them afraid to speak. 老师过度纠正学生的语法，导致他们不敢说话。


近义词：overcompensate, overdo, overreact|反义词：undercorrect, neglect


词性：动词
