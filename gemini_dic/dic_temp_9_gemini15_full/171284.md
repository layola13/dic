pterylography:/ˌtɛrɪˈɡræfi/| n. |羽枝学，羽羽纹理学|The study of the arrangement, distribution, and pattern of feathers on a bird's body.  It involves detailed descriptions and comparisons of feather tracts and apteria (featherless areas).  Pterylography provides valuable information for avian taxonomy, phylogeny, and evolutionary biology.|Example:  The pterylography of the newly discovered bird species was meticulously documented. =  对新发现的鸟类物种的羽枝学进行了细致的记录。|近义词:feather arrangement study, avian feather pattern analysis|反义词:None
