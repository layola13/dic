contingence:/kənˈtɪndʒəns/|/ˈkɒntɪndʒəns/| n. |偶然事件；可能性；意外事件|An event that may occur but that is not certain.  It often refers to a possible future event that must be planned for, but which might not actually happen.|The contingency plan was put into action when the power failed.  |意外情况；应急计划被启动是因为电力故障了。|possibility, chance, eventuality|certainty, inevitability|名词
