chorea:/ˈkɔːriə/| n. |舞蹈；舞蹈病；舞蹈症|A neurological disorder characterized by involuntary, jerky movements.|例句：She suffers from chorea, a neurological disorder that causes uncontrolled movements. 她患有舞蹈症，这是一种导致不受控制的运动的神经系统疾病。|近义词：dyskinesia, athetosis |反义词：paralysis, stillness
