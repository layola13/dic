posh:/pɒʃ/|ˈpɑːʃ/| adj.| 豪华的；高雅的；讲究的；上流社会的 |  Posh describes something or someone that is elegant, stylish, and expensive, often associated with upper-class society. It can refer to things like clothing, cars, hotels, or even a person's mannerisms. |例句：They live in a posh neighborhood. 他们住在富人区。

例句：She was wearing a posh dress. 她穿着一件昂贵的连衣裙。


近义词：elegant, luxurious, upscale, stylish, refined, upper-class


反义词：cheap, shabby, dowdy, unrefined, common


词性：形容词


词源：The origin of "posh" is uncertain, but a popular theory links it to the abbreviations "Port Out, Starboard Home" used by British travelers on P&O liners.  These abbreviations indicated the most desirable cabins on the ships, implying luxury and comfort.  Another theory connects it to Romany words.
