presidence:/ˈprɛzɪdənsɪ/| n. |总统职位；总统任期；优先；优先权；先例|The period during which someone is president; the office or position of president.  It also refers to precedence or priority given to something.|例句：The presidence of Abraham Lincoln was marked by the Civil War. 亚伯拉罕·林肯的总统任期以内战为标志。|例句：The company gave presidence to safety concerns. 公司优先考虑安全问题。|近义词：presidency, term, tenure, priority, precedence|反义词：subordination, posteriority
