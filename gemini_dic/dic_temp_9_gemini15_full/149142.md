oxidizer:/ˈɑːksɪdaɪzər/| n.|氧化剂|A substance that oxidizes another substance, especially by providing oxygen.|例句：Hydrogen peroxide is a powerful oxidizer. 过氧化氢是一种强氧化剂。|例句：The rocket fuel contains a powerful oxidizer and a fuel. 火箭燃料包含强氧化剂和燃料。|近义词：oxidant|反义词：reducer, reducing agent
