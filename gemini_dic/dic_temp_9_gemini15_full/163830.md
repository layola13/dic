predepreciating:/ˌpriːdiːˈpriːʃieɪtɪŋ/| v.| 预先贬值；提前贬值 |  The act of decreasing the value of something before its official or expected devaluation.  This often involves actions taken to anticipate a future drop in value, such as selling assets before a currency devaluation. | Example: The company was accused of predepreciating its assets to reduce its tax burden.  公司被指控预先贬值其资产以减少税负。| 近义词：devaluing, undervaluing, discounting | 反义词：appreciating, revaluing
