growing:/ˈɡroʊɪŋ/| adj. | 生长的；增长的；越来越…的 |  Increasing in size, amount, or intensity.  |例句：The growing popularity of smartphones is undeniable.  智能手机日益普及是不可否认的。|例句：He has a growing concern about his son's future. 他越来越担心儿子的未来。|近义词：increasing, developing, expanding |反义词：decreasing, shrinking, dwindling |形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plant is growing rapidly. = 植物正在快速生长。
The company's profits are growing steadily. = 公司的利润稳步增长。

一般过去时:
The child was growing taller each day. = 孩子每天都在长高。
The economy was growing at a slow pace. = 经济增长缓慢。

现在完成时:
The population has grown significantly. = 人口显著增长。
The business has grown into a large corporation. = 这家企业已发展成为一家大型公司。

过去完成时:
The tree had grown much taller before the storm. = 在暴风雨来临之前，这棵树已经长得高多了。
The city had grown considerably since the last census. = 自上次人口普查以来，这座城市已经发展壮大了不少。

将来时:
The company will be growing its market share next year. = 公司明年将扩大市场份额。
The town will grow significantly in the coming decades. = 在未来几十年里，这个城镇将会显著发展。

虚拟语气:
If the economy were growing faster, we would have more jobs. = 如果经济增长更快，我们将会有更多就业机会。
I wish my business were growing more quickly. = 我希望我的生意能更快地发展。

被动语态:
The garden is being grown organically. = 花园正在进行有机种植。
The crops are being grown in a controlled environment. = 农作物在受控环境下生长。

疑问句:
Is the plant growing well? = 植物长得好吗？
Are your sales growing this year? = 你们今年的销售额增长了吗？

否定句:
The plant isn't growing as quickly as I'd hoped. = 植物的生长速度不如我预期的那样快。
The economy isn't growing at all. = 经济根本没有增长。


PHRASE->

growing evidence 越来越多的证据
