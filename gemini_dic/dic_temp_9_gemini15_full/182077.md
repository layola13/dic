remigrate:/ˈriːmaɪɡreɪt/| v.|重新移民；回迁 |To migrate again, especially to return to one's native country or region after having lived elsewhere.  |例句：Many refugees remigrated to their homeland after the war ended. 许多难民在战争结束后重新移民回他们的祖国。 |例句：After spending ten years abroad, she decided to remigrate to her childhood town. 在国外待了十年后，她决定搬回童年的小镇。|近义词：return, repatriate |反义词：emigrate, immigrate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They remigrate to their homeland every year. = 他们每年都回故乡。
Birds remigrate to warmer climates during winter. = 鸟类在冬天会迁徙到气候温暖的地方。


一般过去时:
He remigrated to his country of origin after retirement. = 他退休后回到了他的祖国。
My grandparents remigrated to Europe after living in America for decades. = 我的祖父母在美国生活了几十年后又回到了欧洲。


现在完成时:
She has remigrated to Canada after living in the UK for five years. = 她已经在英国生活了五年后搬回了加拿大。
They have remigrated to their ancestral home following the family reunion. = 家族聚会后，他们已经搬回了祖先的家园。


过去完成时:
They had remigrated to the countryside before the city became too crowded. = 在城市变得过于拥挤之前，他们已经搬回了农村。
The family had remigrated to Ireland before the outbreak of the war. = 在战争爆发之前，这家人已经搬回了爱尔兰。


将来时:
We will remigrate to Australia once we save enough money. = 一旦攒够钱，我们就会移居澳大利亚。
He plans to remigrate to the Philippines next year. = 他计划明年回到菲律宾。


虚拟语气:
If I had the chance, I would remigrate to my birthplace. = 如果我有机会，我会回到我的出生地。
I wish I could remigrate to the mountains and live a simpler life. = 我希望我能搬到山里过一种更简单的生活。


被动语态:
Many people were remigrated to their villages during the reconstruction. = 在重建期间，许多人被遣返回他们的村庄。
The refugees were remigrated back to their home country by the government. = 难民被政府遣返回国。


疑问句:
Will they remigrate after the election? = 选举后他们会搬回去吗？
Have they remigrated to the south yet? = 他们已经搬到南方了吗？


否定句:
They did not remigrate after the flood. = 洪水过后他们没有搬回去。
She chose not to remigrate to her hometown despite her family's wishes. = 尽管家人希望她回去，但她还是选择不搬回她的家乡。
