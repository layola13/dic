foreappoint:/fɔːrəˈpɔɪnt/| vt.|预先任命，预先指定|To appoint or designate beforehand; to predetermine.|The committee foreappointed a chair before the meeting began. 委员会在会议开始前预先任命了一位主席。|近义词：preappoint, predestine|反义词：postpone, delay|动词


USAGE->

They foreappoint a new manager every year. = 他们每年都预先任命一位新经理。
The company foreappoints its top executives. = 公司预先任命其高管。
