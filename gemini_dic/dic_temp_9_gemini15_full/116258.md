massacred:/ˈmæsəkərd/| vt.| 大屠杀；残杀；严重破坏 | To kill a large number of people indiscriminately, especially in a cruel or violent way.  |例句：The soldiers massacred the civilians. 士兵们屠杀了平民。
例句：The storm massacred the crops. 暴风雨摧毁了庄稼。|近义词：slaughter, butcher, kill, destroy |反义词：save, rescue, protect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They massacre innocent people.  = 他们屠杀无辜的人。
The company massacres its competitors with aggressive marketing. = 这家公司用积极的营销策略残酷地打压竞争对手。

一般过去时:
The rebels massacred the village. = 叛军屠杀了村庄。

现在完成时:
The regime has massacred thousands of its opponents. = 该政权已经屠杀了数千名反对者。
The drought has massacred the harvest. = 干旱毁掉了收成。


过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

If the measures had been taken earlier, the crop failure could have been prevented and a massacre of the harvest avoided. = 如果早些采取措施，就可以防止歉收，避免对收成的严重破坏。
