myrica:/ˈmɪrɪkə/| n. |杨梅属植物|A genus of flowering plants in the family Myricaceae, comprising shrubs and trees, many of which bear edible berries.  Myrica species are known for their aromatic leaves and often grow in diverse habitats, including coastal areas and mountains.| Example:  The *Myrica rubra*, also known as the Chinese bayberry, produces a delicious, tart berry.  杨梅，也叫中国杨梅，结出美味酸甜的浆果。|近义词：bayberry (for specific species)|反义词：无
