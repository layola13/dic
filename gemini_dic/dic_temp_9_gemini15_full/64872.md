espying:/ɪˈspaɪɪŋ/| v.| 侦察；窥探；偶然发现 | To catch sight of something or someone unexpectedly; to see or discover something secretly. | 例句：He was espying on his neighbours through his binoculars. 他正用望远镜窥探他的邻居。  | 例句：While walking in the woods, I espied a rare bird. 在树林里散步时，我偶然发现了一只珍稀鸟类。| 近义词：discovering, sighting, spying, observing | 反义词：ignoring, overlooking
