retrocedent:/ˌrɛtroʊˈsiːdnt/| adj.|逆行的；倒退的；让渡的 |Relating to or denoting a transfer of rights or property;  giving something back or transferring it.| The retrocedent agreement transferred the responsibility to the new company.  这个反向转让协议将责任转给了新公司。|近义词：ceding, transferring, assigning|反义词：ceding, receiving

The retrocedent clause is clear in this contract. = 这份合同中的反向转让条款很清楚。

一般过去时:
The company retroceded its assets last year. = 这家公司去年将其资产转让了出去。
