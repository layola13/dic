henogenesis: /ˌhɛnəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ | n. |孤雌生殖；單性生殖| The development of an egg into an embryo without fertilization.  It is a form of asexual reproduction. | Example:  Henogenesis is a common reproductive strategy in some species of aphids. 孤雌生殖是某些蚜虫物种中常见的繁殖策略。 | 近义词：parthenogenesis, apomixis | 反义词：sexual reproduction | 词性:名词
