dreamed:/driːmd/| v.| 做梦；梦想 | dreamed is the past tense and past participle of the verb "to dream". It signifies having experienced something in a dream or having imagined or envisioned something. | I dreamed I was flying. 我梦见我在飞。|She dreamed of becoming a doctor. 她梦想成为一名医生。| imagined, envisioned, fantasized | reality, wakefulness | 动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
I dream of a better future. = 我梦想一个更美好的未来。
He dreams vividly every night. = 他每晚都做着栩栩如生的梦。

一般过去时:
I dreamed about you last night. = 我昨晚梦见你了。
She dreamed of winning the lottery. = 她梦想着赢得彩票。

现在完成时:
I have dreamed of this moment for years. = 我多年来一直梦想着这一刻。
He has dreamed of traveling the world. = 他一直梦想环游世界。

过去完成时:
I had dreamed of becoming a musician since childhood. = 从小我就梦想着成为一名音乐家。
She had dreamed of going to Paris before she finally went. = 在她最终去巴黎之前，她一直梦想着去那里。

将来时:
I will dream of you tonight. = 今晚我会梦见你。
She will dream big dreams. = 她将怀揣伟大的梦想。

虚拟语气:
If I dreamed it, it must be significant. = 如果我梦见它，那一定意义重大。
I wish I dreamed more often. = 我希望我能更频繁地做梦。

被动语态:
(The verb "dream" is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Did you dream last night? = 你昨晚做梦了吗？
What did you dream about? = 你梦见了什么？

否定句:
I didn't dream last night. = 我昨晚没做梦。
She didn't dream of giving up. = 她从未想过放弃。


PHRASE->
