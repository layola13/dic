edelweiss:/ˈeɪdəlvaɪs/| n. |雪绒花|A small, white, woolly-leaved mountain flower, especially one of the species *Leontopodium alpinum*, found in the Alps and other mountain ranges.|The edelweiss is a symbol of the Alps and is often associated with courage and purity.|近义词：mountain flower, alpine flower|反义词：无
