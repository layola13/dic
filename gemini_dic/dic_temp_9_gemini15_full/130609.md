nonadhering: /ˌnɒnədˈhɪərɪŋ/ | adj. | 不粘的，不粘连的 | Not sticking or adhering.  This describes a substance or material that doesn't stick to another surface. | Example: The nonadhering bandage allowed for easy removal without causing pain.  不粘贴的绷带可以轻松去除，不会造成疼痛。


近义词：unstuck, non-sticky, free from adhesion


反义词：adhesive, sticking, clinging

词性：形容词
