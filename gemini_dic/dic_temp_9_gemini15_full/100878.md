intrusional:/ɪnˈtruʒənəl/| adj.|侵入的；侵入性的|Relating to or characterized by intrusion; formed or emplaced by intrusion.  It often refers to igneous rocks that have forced their way into pre-existing rock formations.|例句：The intrusional rock formations were studied by geologists. 地质学家研究了侵入性岩层。|例句：The intrusional igneous rocks show evidence of high temperatures. 侵入性火成岩显示出高温的证据。|近义词：intrusive, invasive, encroaching|反义词：extrusive, effusive
