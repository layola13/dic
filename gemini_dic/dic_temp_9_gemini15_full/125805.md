murkness:/ˈmɜːknəs/| n. |黑暗；阴霾；模糊|The state or condition of being dark, obscure, or unclear; a dimly lit or gloomy atmosphere.  It often refers to a lack of clarity or understanding, as well as physical darkness.|例句：The murkness of the fog prevented visibility.  浓雾的阴霾阻碍了视线。


例句：The murkness of the political situation made it difficult to predict the outcome.  政治局势的模糊不清使得难以预测结果。


近义词：darkness, gloom, obscurity, dimness, fogginess


反义词：clarity, brightness, light, illumination


词性:名词
