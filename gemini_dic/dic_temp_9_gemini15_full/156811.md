pigeontoed: /ˈpɪdʒəntoʊd/ | adj. | 鸽子脚的；内八字的 | Having the feet turned inward.  This refers to a condition where the feet turn inward at the ankles, causing the heels to point outward.  The term derives from the way pigeons stand with their feet turned inward. | Example: The child's pigeontoed gait made walking difficult.  这个孩子的内八字步态使走路很困难。 |近义词：in-toed, pigeon-breasted (though this refers to the chest)|反义词：out-toed, bowlegged
