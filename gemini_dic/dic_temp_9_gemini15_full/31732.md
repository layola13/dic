cheaping:/ˈtʃiːpɪŋ/| n.|廉价出售；讨价还价|The act of buying or selling at a low price; bargaining.  It can also refer to the sound of birds chirping.|例句：The market was bustling with cheaping and haggling. 市场里人声鼎沸，都在讨价还价。

例句：The constant cheaping of the birds annoyed the early risers. 鸟儿不停的鸣叫声扰乱了早起的人们。|近义词：bargaining, haggling, bartering|反义词：overpricing, exorbitant pricing|词性：名词
