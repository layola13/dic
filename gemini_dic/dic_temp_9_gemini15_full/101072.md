inventiveness:/ɪnˈvɛntɪvnəs/ | n. |创造力；发明才能；独创性 |The ability to create new things or ideas.  It often involves imagination, resourcefulness, and the ability to solve problems in innovative ways.|例句：His inventiveness led to the development of several groundbreaking products. 他的创造力导致了几种突破性产品的开发。


例句：The company’s success is largely due to its employees' inventiveness and dedication. 这家公司的成功很大程度上归功于员工的创造力和奉献精神。


近义词：creativity, originality, innovation, resourcefulness |反义词：unoriginality, unimaginativeness
