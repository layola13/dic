prerecognition:/ˌpriːˌrekəɡˈnɪʃən/| n. |预先识别，预先认知|The act of recognizing something before it is fully apparent or before it officially happens.  It often implies an anticipation or a premonition. |例句：The prerecognition of the market downturn allowed the company to take preventative measures.  公司对市场低迷的预先识别使公司能够采取预防措施。

例句：His prerecognition of the danger saved his life. 他对危险的预先识别挽救了他的生命。


近义词：anticipation, premonition, foreshadowing, preawareness

反义词：postrecognition, belated recognition


词性: 名词
