multicolumn:/ˈmʌltiˌkɒlʌm/| adj.|多栏的，多列的|Having or arranged in multiple columns.  This adjective describes something that is spread across more than one column, typically in a document or table.|例句：The table is displayed in a multicolumn format. 这张表以多栏格式显示。
例句：The newspaper article used a multicolumn layout to fit more text on the page. 这篇报纸文章使用了多栏布局以容纳更多文本。|近义词：multi-columned|反义词：single-column, one-column|形容词


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what" does.  Therefore, tense variations are not applicable.  The usage revolves around describing the structure or layout of something.
