megs:/mɛɡz/| n.|兆字节（百万字节）|缩写形式，指million bytes,  表示计算机存储容量单位。|例句：The file is about 10 megs. 这个文件大约10兆字节。|近义词：MB (megabytes)|反义词:无


USAGE->
This word is an abbreviation and doesn't have verb conjugations or other grammatical variations like "what" does.  It functions solely as a noun.  Therefore, typical tense conjugations, passive voice, etc., are not applicable.
