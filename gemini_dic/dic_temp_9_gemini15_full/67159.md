expositions:/ˌɛkspəˈzɪʃənz/ | n. | 展览会；阐述；说明 | A public display of art or manufactured products.  A comprehensive explanation or description of an idea or theory. |例句：The museum is hosting a major exposition of Impressionist paintings. = 博物馆正在举办一个重要的印象派绘画展览。

The professor's exposition of quantum physics was quite enlightening. = 教授对量子物理学的阐述非常有启发性。|近义词：exhibition, display, explanation, presentation |反义词：concealment, obscurity, mystification | 名词
