fix:/fɪks/| vt. n.|修理，固定；解决；确定，安排|To repair or mend something that is broken or damaged.  To make something stable or firm. To determine or arrange something.|例句：I need to fix the leaky faucet. 我需要修理漏水的龙头。

例句：He fixed the broken chair. 他修理了坏掉的椅子。


近义词：repair, mend, remedy, resolve, settle|反义词：break, damage, ruin, destroy|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fixes cars for a living. = 他以修理汽车为生。
She fixes her hair every morning. = 她每天早上都梳理头发。

一般过去时:
The mechanic fixed my car quickly. = 技工很快地修好了我的车。
They fixed the problem with the computer. = 他们解决了电脑的问题。

现在完成时:
I have fixed the broken window. = 我已经修好了破窗户。
She has fixed the appointment for tomorrow. = 她已经预约了明天的约会。

过去完成时:
He had fixed the bike before his journey. = 他在旅行前已经修好了自行车。
They had fixed the price before the negotiation. = 他们已经在谈判前确定了价格。

将来时:
I will fix the dinner tonight. = 我今晚将准备晚餐。
They will fix the roof tomorrow. = 他们明天将修理屋顶。

虚拟语气:
If I had a hammer, I would fix the shelf. = 如果我有一把锤子，我会修理架子。
I wish I could fix the situation. = 我希望我能解决这种情况。

被动语态:
The car was fixed yesterday. = 车昨天修好了。
The problem will be fixed soon. = 这个问题很快就会解决。

疑问句:
Can you fix this for me? = 你能帮我修理这个吗？
Will you fix the date for the meeting? = 你会确定会议日期吗？

否定句:
I can't fix the computer. = 我修不好电脑。
They didn't fix the leak. = 他们没有修理漏水的地方。


PHRASE->
fix up: 修理，改善

fix with: 用…固定
get fixed: 被修理
