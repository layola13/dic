sieges:/ˈsiːdʒɪz/| n.|包围，围攻|The act of surrounding a place with military forces to force its surrender; a prolonged period of attack or assault.  |例句：The city endured a long and brutal siege.  这座城市经受了漫长而残酷的围攻。

例句：The siege of Troy lasted for ten years. 特洛伊围城战持续了十年。


近义词：besiegement, blockade, assault, attack|反义词：relief, liberation|名词
