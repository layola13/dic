rendezvous:/ˈrɑːndɪvuː/ (美式)/ˈrɒ̃deɪvuː/ (英式)| n. v.|约会；集合地点；会合|A meeting at an agreed time and place, typically between two people.|例句：We arranged a rendezvous at the park. 我们约定在公园见面。They had a secret rendezvous in a small cafe. 他们在一家小咖啡馆秘密会面。|近义词：meeting, appointment, assignation|反义词：separation, parting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We rendezvous at the cafe every Friday. = 我们每周五都在咖啡馆碰面。
They rendezvous near the old oak tree. = 他们在古老的橡树附近会合。

一般过去时:
We rendezvoused at the agreed-upon time. = 我们在约定的时间会面了。
The spies rendezvoused in a dark alley. = 间谍们在一个黑暗的小巷里会合。

现在完成时:
We have rendezvoused here many times before. = 我们以前在这里多次会面过。
They have rendezvoused secretly for years. = 他们多年来一直秘密会面。

过去完成时:
We had already rendezvoused before the others arrived. = 在其他人到达之前，我们已经会面了。
The soldiers had rendezvoused at dawn. = 士兵们在黎明时分会合了。

将来时:
We will rendezvous at the airport tomorrow. = 我们明天将在机场会合。
They will rendezvous at the designated location. = 他们将在指定地点会合。

虚拟语气:
If we could rendezvous tonight, it would be wonderful. = 如果我们今晚能见面就好了。
I wish we could rendezvous more often. = 我希望我们能更经常地见面。

被动语态:
The meeting was to be a secret rendezvous. = 会议将是一个秘密约会。
The rendezvous point was carefully chosen. = 会合地点经过精心挑选。


疑问句:
Where did you rendezvous with him? = 你和他在哪里会面的？
When will we rendezvous tomorrow? = 我们明天什么时候会合？


否定句:
We did not rendezvous at the appointed place. = 我们没有在指定地点会合。
They could not rendezvous due to the bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，他们无法会合。


PHRASE->
secret rendezvous 秘密约会

chance rendezvous 偶然相遇
prearranged rendezvous 预先约定的会合
fixed rendezvous  固定的会合地点
