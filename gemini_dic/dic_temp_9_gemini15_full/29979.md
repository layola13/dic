cavilingly:/ˈkævəlɪŋli/| adv.|吹毛求疵地，挑剔地 |  characterized by or given to caviling; finding fault; raising trivial objections.  |例句：He cavilingly criticized every detail of the proposal. 他吹毛求疵地批评了提案的每一个细节。


例句：She listened to his complaints cavilingly, offering no encouragement. 她不耐烦地听着他的抱怨，没有给予任何鼓励。


近义词：critically, faultfindingly, quibblingly, hypercritically
