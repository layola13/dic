reincreasing: /ˌriːɪnˈkriːsɪŋ/ | v. | 重新增加；再次增加 |  The act of increasing something again after a previous increase or decrease.  It implies a continuation or resumption of an upward trend. |


例句：

The company is reincreasing its marketing budget after a temporary reduction. = 公司在暂时削减后再次增加了营销预算。

Sales figures are reincreasing following a period of decline. = 在经历了一段时间的下降之后，销售额正在再次增长。


近义词：reinforcing, boosting, augmenting, escalating

反义词：decreasing, reducing, diminishing


词性：动词
