jacent: /ˈdʒeɪsnt/ | adj. | 邻近的，毗连的 | Adjacent means next to or adjoining something else.  It implies close proximity and often a shared boundary. | The houses are adjacent to each other.  这些房子彼此相邻。|  nearby, neighboring, contiguous | distant, remote, separate | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The building is adjacent to the park. = 这座建筑物紧邻公园。
The two countries are adjacent. = 这两个国家相邻。


一般过去时:
The hotel was adjacent to the beach. = 这家酒店靠近海滩。
My house was adjacent to a busy street. = 我的房子紧挨着一条繁忙的街道。


现在完成时:
The new development has become adjacent to the old town. = 新开发项目已与旧城毗邻。
The library has always been adjacent to the school. = 图书馆一直与学校相邻。


过去完成时:
The factory had been adjacent to the river before it moved. = 工厂搬迁前一直靠近河边。
The shop had been adjacent to the bakery for many years. = 这家商店多年来一直与面包店相邻。


将来时:
The new stadium will be adjacent to the train station. = 新体育场将与火车站相邻。
The shopping mall will be adjacent to the residential area. = 购物中心将与住宅区相邻。


虚拟语气:
If the house were adjacent to the school, it would be more convenient. = 如果房子靠近学校，那就更方便了。
I wish my office were adjacent to the cafeteria. = 我希望我的办公室靠近自助餐厅。


被动语态:
The land was found to be adjacent to a protected wetland. = 发现这块土地与受保护的湿地相邻。
The property is being developed adjacent to the highway. = 正在开发靠近高速公路的房产。


疑问句:
Is your house adjacent to the park? = 你的房子靠近公园吗？
Are the two properties adjacent? = 这两处房产相邻吗？


否定句:
The hospital is not adjacent to the supermarket. = 医院不挨着超市。
The two buildings are not adjacent. = 这两座建筑不相邻。


PHRASE->
adjacent to = 邻近...
adjacent areas = 邻近地区
mutually adjacent = 互相邻接
adjacent sides = 相邻的边
adjacent angles = 邻角
