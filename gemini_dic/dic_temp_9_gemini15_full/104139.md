junctures:/ˈdʒʌŋkʃərz/ | n. |关键时刻；连接点；结合点 | A juncture is a point in time, especially one where a change or decision is important.  It can also refer to a place where things meet or join. |例句：We are at a critical juncture in the peace process. 我们正处于和平进程的关键时刻。

The two pipes meet at a juncture. 这两根管道在一个连接点汇合。


近义词：point, moment, crossroads, intersection, connection | 反义词：separation, disconnection | 词性：名词
