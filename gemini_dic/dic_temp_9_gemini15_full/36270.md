cohabitation:/ˌkoʊˌhæbɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |同居；共同居住 |The state of two people, usually of the opposite sex, living together without being married.  It often implies a romantic relationship but not necessarily. Cohabitation is increasingly common in many societies. |例句：They are considering cohabitation before marriage. 他们正在考虑婚前同居。

例句：Cohabitation rates have risen significantly in recent years. 近年来同居率显著上升。


近义词：living together,  domestic partnership


反义词：separate living, living apart


词性：名词
