nonthirstiest: /ˌnɒnˈθɜːrstɪɪst/ | adj. | 最不渴的，最不需要水的 |  This word is a superlative form, combining "non-" (not) and the superlative of "thirsty". It describes the state of being least thirsty among a group or in a comparison.  It's relatively rare and often found in specific, comparative contexts. | Example: "Among the marathon runners, he was the nonthirstiest, perhaps due to his meticulous hydration strategy." | 近义词: least thirsty,  most hydrated | 反义词: thirstiest, most dehydrated
