combs:/koʊmz/| n. |梳子；蜂巢|A comb is a toothed implement used to untangle or style hair, or a similar structure found in nature, such as a honeycomb.  |例句：She brushed her hair with a comb. 她用梳子梳理头发。|例句：The bees built their combs in the hollow of the tree. 蜜蜂在树洞里建造蜂巢。|近义词：brush, hairbrush |反义词：无
