diversifiers:/daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪərz/| n. |多样化因素；多元化工具|Things that introduce variety or different elements.  Diversifiers are used in various contexts, such as investment portfolios to reduce risk, or in a business strategy to expand into new markets. |例句：The fund manager added several diversifiers to the portfolio to mitigate risk.  基金经理向投资组合中添加了一些多元化工具以降低风险。

例句：To increase its revenue streams, the company implemented several business diversifiers. 为增加收入来源，公司实施了几项业务多元化策略。


近义词：varied assets, spread, hedging tools |反义词：concentrators, homogeneous assets


词性:名词
