piranha:/pɪˈrɑːnə/| n. |食人鱼|A piranha is a small, fierce freshwater fish with sharp teeth, found in South American rivers.  They are known for their aggressive feeding habits and powerful jaws.  |Example:  The tourists were warned to stay away from the river because of the piranhas.|近义词：killer fish, caribe|反义词：None specifically, but words implying gentleness or harmlessness could be considered antonyms.
