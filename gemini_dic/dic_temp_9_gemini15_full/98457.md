inspired:/ɪnˈspaɪrd/| adj.|受启发的；灵感迸发的；充满灵感的|Feeling or showing great enthusiasm or excitement.  It suggests a strong influence that motivates or stimulates creativity and action.|例句：Her inspiring speech moved the audience to tears.  她的鼓舞人心的演讲使听众感动落泪。|例句：The artist was inspired by the beauty of nature.这位艺术家受到自然美的启发。|近义词：motivated, stimulated, enthused,  enlivened|反义词：uninspired, unmotivated, unenthusiastic


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The music inspires me. = 这音乐激励着我。
The view inspires his paintings. = 景色激发了他的绘画灵感。

一般过去时:
The sunset inspired a beautiful poem. = 日落激发了一首美丽的诗歌。
The teacher's words inspired her students. = 老师的话语激励了她的学生们。

现在完成时:
His work has inspired many artists. = 他的作品启发了众多艺术家。
This book has inspired me to travel the world. = 这本书启发我去环游世界。

过去完成时:
I had been inspired by his earlier work. = 我曾受到他早期作品的启发。
She had already been inspired to write a novel. = 她那时已经受到启发去写一部小说。

将来时:
This experience will inspire future generations. = 这段经历将激励后代。
I believe this project will inspire many people. = 我相信这个项目会激励很多人。

虚拟语气:
If I were inspired, I would write a song. = 如果我有灵感，我会写一首歌。
I wish I were inspired to paint. = 我希望我能有灵感作画。

被动语态:
He was inspired by a dream. = 他受到梦境的启发。
Many artists have been inspired by her work. = 许多艺术家都受到她的作品的启发。

疑问句:
What inspires you? = 什么激励你？
What inspired this painting? = 这幅画的灵感来源是什么？

否定句:
I wasn't inspired by his speech. = 我并没有受到他的演讲的启发。
The design wasn't inspired. = 这个设计缺乏灵感。


PHRASE->
be inspired by = 受...启发
draw inspiration from = 从...中汲取灵感
an inspiring speech = 一场鼓舞人心的演讲
an inspired choice = 一个充满灵感的抉择
feel inspired = 感觉受到启发


词源:

The word "inspired" comes from the Latin word "inspirare," meaning "to breathe into."  In its original religious context, it referred to the divine breath giving life or inspiration to something. Over time, it evolved to encompass the broader sense of being creatively stimulated or motivated.
