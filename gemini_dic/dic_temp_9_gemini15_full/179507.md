reedy:/ˈriːdi/| adj.|芦苇似的；细长的；声音尖细的|having a thin, high-pitched sound, like that of a reed instrument; tall and slender like reeds.  |例句：Her voice was reedy and slightly trembling.  她的声音又细又有点颤抖。

例句：The reedy plants grew tall along the riverbank.  芦苇般的高大的植物沿着河岸生长。


近义词：thin, slender, shrill, piping

反义词：thick, robust, deep, booming


词性：形容词
