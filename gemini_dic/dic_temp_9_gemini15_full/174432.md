quilly:/ˈkwɪli/| adj.|多刺的；带刺的；笔迹潦草的|Covered with quills; prickly; characterized by a spiky or bristly texture; also, written in a hurried or careless manner.  |例句：The hedgehog has a quilly back.  刺猬的背上长满了刺。

The writer's quilly handwriting was difficult to decipher.  作者潦草的笔迹很难辨认。|近义词：prickly, spiny, bristly, scratchy |反义词：smooth, hairless
