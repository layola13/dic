词源:
The word "fettuccine" is derived from the Italian word "fettuccia," meaning "little ribbon."  It's a diminutive form, indicating smallness.  The plural "-ine" is a standard Italian plural ending. Therefore, fettuccine literally means "little ribbons" of pasta.
