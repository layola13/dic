fust:/fʌst/ | v. | 发霉；腐坏；使…腐坏 | To decay or rot, especially of organic matter.  It can also refer to becoming stale or moldy. | Example: The fruit began to fust in the heat. 水果在高温下开始腐烂。 The bread fust quickly in humid weather. 面包在潮湿的天气里很快就会发霉。 | 近义词：rot, decay, decompose, spoil | 反义词：preserve, conserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bread fusts in the humid weather. = 面包在潮湿的天气里会发霉。
The fruit fusts quickly in the summer heat. = 水果在夏日的酷暑中很快就会腐烂。

一般过去时:
The old book fusted in the attic. = 那本旧书在阁楼里发霉了。
The food fusted before we could eat it. = 食物在我们能吃之前就腐败了。

现在完成时:
The apples have fusted in the basket. = 苹果已经在篮子里腐烂了。
The milk has fusted because it was left out. = 牛奶因为放在外面而变质了。

过去完成时:
The meat had fusted before we even noticed. = 肉在我们注意到之前就已经腐败了。
The vegetables had fusted due to improper storage. = 蔬菜由于储存不当而腐烂了。


将来时:
The cheese will fust if it's not refrigerated. = 如果不冷藏，奶酪会发霉。
The leftovers will fust if they are not properly stored. = 如果剩菜没有妥善保存，它们会腐败。


虚拟语气:
If the food had been refrigerated, it wouldn't have fusted. = 如果食物被冷藏，它就不会腐烂了。
I wish the flowers hadn't fusted so quickly. = 我真希望花不会这么快就枯萎了。


被动语态:
The fruit was fusted by the heat. = 水果被高温腐蚀了。
The bread was fusted by the dampness. = 面包被潮湿弄坏了。


疑问句:
Has the meat fusted? = 肉变质了吗？
Did the fruit fust during the journey? = 水果在运输途中变质了吗？


否定句:
The fruit didn't fust because it was stored properly. = 水果因为保存得当所以没有腐烂。
The bread didn't fust because we ate it quickly. = 面包因为我们很快吃掉了所以没有发霉。
