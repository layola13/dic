nonlibrarian:/ˌnɑːnlaɪˈbrɛəriən/ | adj. n. |非图书馆员的；非图书馆工作人员的；外行 | A person who is not a librarian.  Someone outside of the library profession.  The term often implies a lack of familiarity with library systems or resources. |例句：The nonlibrarian struggled to navigate the library's online catalog. =  这位非图书馆员难以使用图书馆的在线目录。  The presentation was geared towards a nonlibrarian audience. = 这场演示面向的是非图书馆员的观众。|近义词：layperson, outsider, amateur |反义词：librarian
