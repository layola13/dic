hasn't:/ˈhæzənt/ | aux. v. | 没有， hasn't got 没有 | hasn't is a contraction of "has not," used as an auxiliary verb in the present perfect tense to form negative sentences. It is used with singular third-person subjects (he, she, it, and singular nouns).|

例句：

He hasn't finished his work yet. = 他还没完成他的工作。

She hasn't been to Paris before. = 她以前没去过巴黎。


近义词：has not


反义词：has


词性：助动词
