nonubiquitous: /ˌnɒnjuːˈbɪkwɪtəs/ | adj. | 非普遍存在的；不无处不在的 | Not found everywhere; not ubiquitous.  The nonubiquitous nature of the species makes its conservation challenging.  | 近义词：rare, scarce, uncommon, infrequent | 反义词：ubiquitous, common, widespread


例句：

The nonubiquitous nature of the species makes its conservation challenging.  =  该物种并非普遍存在，这使得它的保护工作极具挑战性。

The use of this technology is still nonubiquitous in developing countries. = 在发展中国家，这项技术的应用仍然不普遍。
