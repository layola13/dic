futurism:/ˈfjuːtʃərɪzəm/ | n. | 未来主义 | A radical artistic and literary movement that originated in Italy in the early 20th century, characterized by its glorification of speed, technology, youth, violence, and industrialization.  Futurism rejected tradition and embraced the dynamism of the modern world. |  The Futurist movement celebrated the energy of the machine age. 未来主义运动赞扬了机器时代的能量。 |  Modernism, avant-garde | Traditionalism, classicism | 名词
