kiss-offs:/ˈkɪsˌɒfs/| n.|轻蔑的拒绝；断然拒绝；甩掉；解雇|A definitive rejection; a dismissal; a snub.  It can refer to a romantic rejection, a job dismissal, or a general brush-off.|例句：He gave her the kiss-off after she insulted him. 他在她侮辱他之后给了她一个断然拒绝。


例句：The company gave him the kiss-off without any explanation. 公司没有解释就解雇了他。


近义词：rejection, dismissal, snub, brush-off, sacking, firing


反义词：acceptance, approval, embrace


词性:名词
