cannier:/ˈkæniər/| adj.|更狡猾的；更精明的 | More shrewd or cunning than others; showing greater wisdom or skill in practical matters. |例句：He was cannier than he looked, quickly spotting the flaw in their plan. 他比看起来更精明，很快就发现了他们计划中的缺陷。

例句：The cannier investors avoided the risky venture.  更精明的投资者避开了风险投资。


近义词：shrewder, more astute, more cunning, more clever, wiser

反义词：naive, gullible, foolish, simple, unintelligent


词源:  Derived from "canny," ultimately from Old Norse "kunningr" meaning "knowledge, skill."  The suffix "-er" adds a comparative sense.
