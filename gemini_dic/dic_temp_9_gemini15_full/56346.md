dogtooth:/ˈdɒɡtuːθ/ /ˈdɔːɡtuːθ/| n. |犬齿；狗牙；(某些植物的)尖状突起|A dogtooth is a pointed canine tooth, especially of a dog.  The term can also refer to sharp, pointed projections on certain plants or minerals.|例句：The dog was missing a dogtooth. 这条狗少了一颗犬齿。

例句：The sharp dogtooth on the plant scratched my arm. 植物上尖锐的尖状突起划伤了我的胳膊。

近义词：canine, tusk (for larger teeth) |反义词：incisor, molar

词性：名词
