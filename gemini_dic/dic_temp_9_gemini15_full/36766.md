collum:/ˈkɒləm/| n.|柱子，专栏|A vertical support, typically cylindrical, often part of a building or other structure.  It can also refer to a regularly appearing column in a newspaper or magazine.|例句：The ancient temple boasted magnificent columns supporting its roof.  这座古老的寺庙以支撑屋顶的宏伟柱子而自豪。|例句：She writes a weekly fashion column for the newspaper. 她每周为报纸撰写一个时尚专栏。|近义词：pillar, post,  section|反义词：无
