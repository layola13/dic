remble:/ˈrɛmbəl/| v.|颤抖，震动|To shake or tremble slightly.  It often implies a slight, involuntary shaking, perhaps due to cold, fear, or weakness. |例句：My hands trembled as I held the fragile vase.  我的手颤抖着，手里拿着易碎的花瓶。|例句：The ground trembled slightly before the earthquake. 地震前地面轻微震动。|近义词：shake, quiver, shiver, vibrate|反义词：be still, be steady


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The bird's wings tremble in the wind. = 鸟的翅膀在风中颤抖。
His voice trembles with emotion. = 他的声音因激动而颤抖。


一般过去时:
Her hands trembled as she signed the contract. = 她签合同时双手颤抖。
The old house trembled during the storm. = 老房子在暴风雨中摇晃。


现在完成时:
My hands have trembled ever since I heard the news. = 从我听到这个消息以来，我的手一直在颤抖。
The earth has trembled several times this week. = 本周地球已经震动了几次。


过去完成时:
My knees had trembled before I even started the speech. = 我甚至还没开始演讲，膝盖就已经开始颤抖了。
The building had trembled violently before collapsing. = 建筑物剧烈摇晃后倒塌了。


将来时:
I think my hands will tremble when I meet her. = 我想当我见到她时，我的手会颤抖。
The ground will tremble if there is an earthquake. = 如果发生地震，地面将会震动。


虚拟语气:
If I knew what was happening, I wouldn't tremble so much. = 如果我知道发生了什么，我就不会这么颤抖。
If I were you, I would tremble with fear. = 如果我是你，我会害怕得发抖。


被动语态:
The building was trembled by the explosion. = 建筑物被爆炸震动了。(被动语态用得较少，通常用主动语态)
The picture was nearly trembled off the wall. = 画几乎被震落墙。


疑问句:
Does your hand tremble when you are nervous? = 你紧张的时候手会抖吗？
Why did the building tremble? = 为什么建筑物会震动？


否定句:
My hands didn't tremble at all. = 我的手一点也不颤抖。
The ground didn't tremble noticeably. = 地面没有明显震动。
