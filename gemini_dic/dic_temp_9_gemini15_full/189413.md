said:/sɛd/| v.|说，讲|To utter words; to express something in speech.|He said he would be late. 他说他会迟到。She said nothing. 她什么也没说。|told, stated, mentioned, declared|kept silent, remained mum|动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
He says he is happy. = 他说他很快乐。
She says she will come. = 她说她会来。

一般过去时:
He said he was happy. = 他说他很快乐。
She said she would come. = 她说她会来。

现在完成时:
He has said that many times. = 他已经说过很多次了。
She has said nothing about it. = 她对此什么也没说。


过去完成时:
He had said he would be there. = 他说过他会去那里。
She had said she didn't want to go. = 她说过她不想去。


将来时:
He will say it again tomorrow. = 他明天会再说一遍。
She will say what she thinks. = 她会说出她的想法。


虚拟语气:
He said he would go if he had time. = 他说如果他有时间就会去。
She said she would help if she could. = 她说如果她能帮忙就会帮忙。


被动语态:
It was said that he was innocent. = 据说他是无辜的。
It has been said that the project is delayed. = 据说该项目延期了。


疑问句:
What did he say? = 他说了什么？
Did she say anything? = 她说了什么吗？


否定句:
He didn't say anything. = 他什么也没说。
She said she wouldn't go. = 她说她不会去。



PHRASE->
said to oneself = 自言自语
