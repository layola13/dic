matchstick:/ˈmætʃˌstɪk/| n. |火柴棒|A small wooden stick with a head that is easily ignited to produce a flame.  It is used to light fires, cigarettes, etc.|例句：He struck a matchstick to light the candle. 他划了一根火柴点燃蜡烛。

例句：I need a matchstick to start the campfire. 我需要一根火柴来生篝火。

近义词：splint (in some contexts)

反义词：None
