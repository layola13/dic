indigitation: /ˌɪndɪdʒɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 指认；指控 | The act of pointing out or accusing someone.  It often implies a formal or official accusation. | Example: The witness's indigitation of the suspect led to his arrest.  证人的指认导致了嫌疑人的逮捕。 | 近义词：identification, accusation, denunciation | 反义词：exoneration, acquittal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The witness indigitates the thief. = 证人指认小偷。
She indigitates him as the culprit. = 她指认他为罪犯。


一般过去时:
The police indigitated the suspect in the crime. = 警方指认了犯罪嫌疑人。
He indigitated his brother as the liar. = 他指认他哥哥是说谎者。


现在完成时:
The jury has indigitated the defendant as guilty. = 陪审团已认定被告有罪。
She has indigitated several people as witnesses. = 她已经指认了几个人作为证人。


过去完成时:
The court had indigitated him before the trial. = 法院在审判前就已指认了他。
They had indigitated the wrong person before realizing their mistake. = 他们指认错了人，之后才意识到自己的错误。
