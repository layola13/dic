chloridization:/ˌklɔːrɪdaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |氯化作用；氯化处理|The process of treating something with chlorine or a chlorine compound. This often involves the conversion of a metal into its chloride.  Chloridization is used in various industries, including metallurgy and waste treatment. |例句：The chloridization of gold ore is a crucial step in its extraction.  金矿石的氯化作用是其提取的关键步骤。

例句：The waste water treatment plant uses chloridization to remove heavy metals. 污水处理厂使用氯化处理来去除重金属。|近义词：chlorination|反义词：dechlorination
