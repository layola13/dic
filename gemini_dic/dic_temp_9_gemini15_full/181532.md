reknitted:/ˌriːˈnɪtɪd/| adj.|重新编织的；重新连接的|Reknitted refers to something that has been knitted again, often after being damaged or unraveled. It implies a process of repair or reconstruction through knitting.|例句：The reknitted sweater looked as good as new.  重新编织的毛衣看起来和新的一样。

The reknitted fishing net was strong enough for another season.  重新编织的渔网足够坚固，可以再用一个季节。|近义词：rewoven, repaired, mended|反义词：unravelled, damaged, broken|

词源:
The word "reknitted" is formed by adding the prefix "re-" (meaning "again") to the past participle "knitted" of the verb "knit".  "Knit" originates from Old English *cnittan,  meaning "to knot; to tie".
