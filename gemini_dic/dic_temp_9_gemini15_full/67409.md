externalized:/ˌɛkˈstɜːrnəlaɪzd/|/ˈɛkˌstɜːrnəlaɪzd/ | adj. |外部化的；外部的|Transferred or shifted outside; made external.  The process of moving something from an internal state to an external one.  This often refers to data, processes, or resources. |例句：The company externalized its IT infrastructure to a cloud provider. 公司将其IT基础设施外包给了云服务提供商。

例句：The software's configuration is fully externalized, allowing for easy customization. 该软件的配置完全外部化，便于自定义。


近义词：outsourced, offshored, delegated

反义词：internalized, integrated


词性：形容词 (也可作及物动词的过去分词)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company externalizes its data storage. = 公司将数据存储外包。
He externalizes his emotions through art. = 他通过艺术表达自己的情感。


一般过去时:
They externalized the manufacturing process last year. = 他们去年将制造流程外包了。
She externalized her anxieties by talking to a therapist. = 她通过与治疗师交谈来疏解焦虑。


现在完成时:
We have externalized many of our business operations. = 我们已经将许多业务运营外包了。
The project has been successfully externalized. = 项目已成功外部化。


过去完成时:
They had externalized their customer support before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们已经将客户支持外包了。
The company had already externalized its IT department. = 公司已经将IT部门外包出去了。


将来时:
We will externalize our marketing efforts next quarter. = 我们将在下个季度将营销工作外包。
They will externalize the risk by purchasing insurance. = 他们将通过购买保险来转移风险。


虚拟语气:
If we externalized the project, we could save money. = 如果我们将项目外包，我们可以节省资金。
I wish we had externalized the development process earlier. = 我希望我们早点将开发流程外包。


被动语态:
The problem was externalized by the team. = 这个问题由团队外部化处理了。
The data is being externalized to a separate server. = 数据正在被外部化到一个单独的服务器上。


疑问句:
Has the company externalized its accounting department? = 公司是否已将会计部门外包？
Will they externalize the software development? = 他们会将软件开发外包吗？


否定句:
The company did not externalize its research and development. = 公司没有将其研发工作外包。
We have not yet externalized the production process. = 我们尚未将生产流程外包。
