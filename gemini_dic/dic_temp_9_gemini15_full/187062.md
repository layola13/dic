It is depicted as a beautiful and peaceful place, a haven for elves and other beings.  The name itself evokes a sense of mystery and ancient beauty.|例句：The Fellowship of the Ring rested in Rivendell before continuing their quest.  （魔戒现身一行人在继续他们的任务之前在瑞文戴尔休息。）|近义词：haven, sanctuary, refuge |反义词：battlefield, wasteland
