parential:/pəˈrɛntəl/| adj. |父母的；像父母的；家长式的|Relating to or characteristic of parents or parenthood.  It often implies a concern for the welfare and guidance of children or those under one's care.  The term can also carry connotations of overprotectiveness or authoritarianism, depending on the context.|例句：He showed great parental concern for his children's education. 他对孩子的教育表现出极大的父母般的关怀。|例句：The parental controls on the computer restricted access to certain websites. 电脑上的家长控制限制了对某些网站的访问。|近义词：paternal, maternal, protective, tutelary|反义词：childlike, independent, rebellious
