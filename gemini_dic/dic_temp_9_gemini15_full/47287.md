de-deputizing: /ˌdiːˈdɛpjʊtaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 取消副职；解除代理职务 | The process of removing someone from a deputy or acting role.  It implies a formal revocation of authority or responsibility. | Example: The sheriff is de-deputizing several officers due to budget cuts. = 由于预算削减，警长正在取消几名警官的副职。 | 近义词：discharging, dismissing, removing | 反义词：appointing, deputing
