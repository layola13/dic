jer:/dʒɜːr/| n.| 猛然一拉；猛烈撞击；|a sudden pull or jerk; a sudden, sharp movement.|例句：He gave the rope a jerk. 他猛地拉了一下绳子。|例句：The car came to a sudden jerk. 车突然猛地一颠。|近义词：tug, yank, jolt|反义词：smooth, gentle


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The dog jerks on its leash. = 狗猛拉牵引绳。
She often jerks her head when she's surprised. = 她惊讶时经常猛甩头。

一般过去时:
He jerked the door open. = 他猛地拉开了门。
The train jerked to a stop. = 火车猛地停了下来。

现在完成时:
Have you ever jerked your back? = 你曾经扭伤过背吗？
The car has jerked several times during the journey. = 汽车在旅途中几次猛地颠簸。

过去完成时:
He had jerked the steering wheel before the accident. = 事故发生前他已经猛打方向盘了。
She had jerked her hand away before the hot pot touched her skin. = 热锅碰到她的皮肤之前，她已经猛抽回了手。

将来时:
He will jerk the fishing rod when he feels a bite. = 他感觉到鱼上钩时会猛拉鱼竿。
The rollercoaster will jerk violently at the turns. = 过山车在转弯处会剧烈颠簸。

虚拟语气:
If he had jerked the rope harder, he might have caught the fish. = 如果他用力拉绳子，也许就能抓住鱼了。
I wish I hadn't jerked my knee so badly. = 我真希望我没有把膝盖弄得那么严重。

被动语态:
The rope was jerked violently. = 绳子被猛烈地拉动。
The car was jerked off the road. = 汽车被猛地甩出了路面。

疑问句:
Did he jerk the door open? = 他是猛地拉开门吗？
Why did the car jerk so suddenly? = 为什么汽车会突然猛地一颠？

否定句:
He didn't jerk the handle. = 他没有猛拉把手。
The machine didn't jerk during operation. = 机器在运行过程中没有颠簸。
