laparotomy:/læpəˈrɒtəmi/|/læpəˈrɑːtəmi/| n. |剖腹手术|Surgical incision into the abdominal cavity.  Laparotomies are performed for various reasons, including diagnosis and treatment of abdominal conditions.| Example: The surgeon performed a laparotomy to remove the appendix.  外科医生进行了剖腹手术以切除阑尾。|近义词：abdominal surgery, celiotomy |反义词：无
