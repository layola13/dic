grandmas:/ˈɡrænmɑːz/ | n. |奶奶们，外婆们 |The plural form of "grandma," referring to multiple grandmothers.  It can refer to a collection of one's own grandmothers, or a group of grandmothers in a general sense. | Example: My grandmas used to tell me wonderful stories. 我的奶奶们过去常常给我讲精彩的故事。  The grandmas in the park were knitting. 公园里的奶奶们在织毛衣。 |近义词: grandmothers |反义词:  (无直接反义词)

一般现在时:
My grandmas bake delicious cookies every week. = 我的奶奶们每周都烤美味的饼干。
The grandmas at the nursing home enjoy playing bingo. = 养老院的奶奶们喜欢玩宾果游戏。

一般过去时:
My grandmas visited me last summer. = 我的奶奶们去年夏天来看我了。
The grandmas told us stories about their childhoods. = 奶奶们给我们讲了她们童年的故事。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

The grandmas had told the children many bedtime stories before they fell asleep. = 奶奶们在孩子们睡着之前已经给他们讲了很多睡前故事。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
