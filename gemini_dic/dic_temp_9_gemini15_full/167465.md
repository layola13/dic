preventative: /prɪˈvɛntətɪv/ | adj. | 预防性的，预防的 | Relating to or tending to prevent something, especially disease.  It emphasizes the action of preventing something from happening. |

例句：
She took preventative measures to avoid getting sick. = 她采取了预防措施以免生病。

The doctor prescribed a preventative medication. = 医生开了预防性药物。


近义词：prophylactic, precautionary, preventive


反义词：curative, remedial


词性：形容词
