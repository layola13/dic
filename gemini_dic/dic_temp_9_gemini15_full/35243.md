clumsiness:/ˈklʌmzɪnəs/| n. |笨拙；笨手笨脚；粗鲁|The noun "clumsiness" refers to a lack of skill or grace in movement or action. It suggests awkwardness and a tendency to make mistakes or cause damage due to a lack of coordination or dexterity.  It can also refer to something that is poorly done or designed.|例句：His clumsiness caused him to knock over a vase.他的笨拙导致他打翻了一个花瓶。

例句：The clumsiness of the design made the product difficult to use.  这个设计笨拙，使得产品难以使用。|近义词：awkwardness, ungainliness, ineptitude|反义词：grace, dexterity, skill|词性:名词
