existentialistic: /ˌɛɡzɪstənˈʃælɪstɪk/ | adj. | 存在主义的 | Relating to or characteristic of existentialism.  Existentialistic philosophy emphasizes individual existence, freedom, and responsibility. It often explores themes of meaning, purpose, and the absurdity of existence. |  The existentialistic themes in the novel explore the protagonist's struggle with meaninglessness. 存在主义小说中的主题探讨了主人公与虚无的斗争。 | 近义词：existential, phenomenological | 反义词：deterministic, materialistic
