peece:/piːs/| n.|一片，一块；和睦；平静；乐曲|a portion of something larger; a feeling of calm; a musical composition|例句：He ate a piece of cake. 他吃了一块蛋糕。  He longed for a piece of mind. 他渴望心灵平静。|近义词：part, portion, section, tranquility, calm |反义词：chaos, turmoil, discord


USAGE->

This piece of music is beautiful. = 这首乐曲很美。
