non-depletable: /ˌnɒn dɪˈpliːtəbl/ | adj. | 不可枯竭的，用之不竭的 | Not capable of being depleted or exhausted; inexhaustible.  This adjective describes resources or sources that are not diminished or used up by consumption.  |例句：Solar energy is a non-depletable resource. 太阳能是一种不可枯竭的资源。

例句：The ocean's vastness makes it seem like a non-depletable source of fish, but overfishing threatens this perception. 海洋的广阔无垠让人觉得它似乎是取之不尽用之不竭的鱼类资源，但是过度捕捞却威胁着这种认知。


近义词：inexhaustible, unlimited, unending, boundless, infinite


反义词：depletable, exhaustible, finite, limited


词性:形容词
