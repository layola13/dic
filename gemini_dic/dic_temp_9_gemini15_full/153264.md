penalised:/ˈpɛnəlaɪzd/| v.|处罚，惩罚|To impose a penalty on (someone) for a fault or offence.|The driver was penalised for speeding.  司机因超速而受罚。|He was penalised five yards for a false start. 他因假跑被罚五码。|punish, fine, sanction|reward, benefit|

一般现在时:
The team is penalised for fouls frequently.  这个队经常因为犯规而受罚。
They penalise late submissions.  他们会惩罚迟交作业。


一般过去时:
The player was penalised for unsportsmanlike conduct.  这位运动员因不文明行为而受罚。
