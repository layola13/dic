/ˈʌðərweɪz/ | adv. | 否则；不然；用别的方法 | Otherwise, we'll be late.  否则，我们就迟到了。|近义词：alternatively, else, differently |反义词：similarly, likewise


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I go to school every day; otherwise, I would be expelled. = 我每天都去上学；否则，我会被开除。
He works hard otherwise he would fail the exam. = 他努力工作，否则考试会不及格。


一般过去时:
She studied hard; otherwise, she wouldn't have passed the test. = 她学习努力；否则，她就不会通过考试了。
They arrived on time otherwise they would have missed the train. = 他们准时到达，否则他们就会错过火车了。


现在完成时:
I have finished my homework; otherwise, I would have been in trouble. = 我已经完成了我的作业；否则，我就麻烦了。
She has saved enough money otherwise she couldn't afford the trip. = 她已经存够了钱，否则她负担不起这次旅行。


过去完成时:
I had already eaten; otherwise, I would have been hungry. = 我已经吃过了；否则，我就饿了。
They had finished the project; otherwise, they would have been fired. = 他们已经完成了项目；否则，他们会被解雇。


将来时:
We will leave early; otherwise, we will miss the flight. = 我们将早点离开；否则，我们会错过航班。
He will study hard otherwise he won't pass the exam. = 他会努力学习，否则他考试不及格。


虚拟语气:
I would have gone to the party, otherwise I would have been bored at home. = 我本来会去参加聚会的，否则我就会在家无聊。
She would have helped me, otherwise I would have failed. = 她会帮助我的，否则我就失败了。


被动语态:
The project would have been delayed otherwise more people would have been assigned to it. = 项目会被延迟，否则就会有更多人被分配到这个项目。
The meeting would have been cancelled otherwise adequate preparations would have been made. = 会议会被取消，否则就会做好充分的准备。


疑问句:
How else could we solve this problem otherwise? = 否则我们还能用什么方法解决这个问题？
What would you have done otherwise? = 否则你会怎么做？


否定句:
It wasn't raining; otherwise, we wouldn't have gone for a walk. = 天没下雨；否则，我们就不会去散步了。
She didn't study hard; otherwise, she would have passed the exam. = 她学习不努力；否则，她就能通过考试了。


PHRASE->
act otherwise 行为表现相反
think otherwise 想得不一样
do otherwise  采取其他行动
say otherwise  说相反的话
be otherwise 情况不同
