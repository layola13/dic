reprogramming:/ˌriːˈprəʊɡræmɪŋ/ | n. |重新编程，程序再编写，重新规划 | The act of changing or altering a computer program, or a biological system.  It often involves modifying existing code or instructions to achieve a new or different function.  | Example: The engineers performed reprogramming on the robot's software to improve its efficiency.工程师对机器人的软件进行了重新编程以提高其效率。 The scientists are exploring methods of cellular reprogramming to treat diseases. 科学家们正在探索细胞重编程治疗疾病的方法。|近义词：recoding, restructuring, reconfiguration |反义词：initial programming, original programming
