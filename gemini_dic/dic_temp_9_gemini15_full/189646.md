sallied:/ˈsæliːd/ | v. | 突然袭击；出击；飞奔；(尤指)突然说出；|To make a sudden attack or sortie; to rush or dart forth, especially to make a sudden attack.  |例句：The soldiers sallied forth from their trenches. 士兵们从战壕里冲了出来。

例句：He sallied out into the storm. 他冲进暴风雨中。


近义词：attack, sortie, rush, dart, burst forth | 反义词：retreat, withdraw, recede


词源:  源自古法语 *salir，意为“跳跃，跳出”，最终源自拉丁语 *salīre，意为“跳跃”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sallies forth every morning for a run. = 他每天早上都出去跑步。
They sally from their hiding place. = 他们从藏身处冲出来。

一般过去时:
The knight sallied forth to battle. = 骑士出征作战。
The comedian sallied forth with a string of jokes. = 喜剧演员突然讲了一连串笑话。

现在完成时:
The army has sallied forth to reclaim the lost territory. = 军队已经出击以收复失地。
She has sallied out to buy some groceries. = 她已经出去买些杂货了。

过去完成时:
The rebels had sallied forth before the reinforcements arrived. = 增援部队到达之前，叛军已经出击了。
Before the speech, he had sallied forth with a few practice jokes. = 演讲之前，他试着讲了几个笑话。

将来时:
The troops will sally forth at dawn. = 部队将在黎明时分出击。
He will sally forth with a new business idea next week. = 他下周将带着一个新的商业创意出击。

虚拟语气:
If the general had not sallied forth, they would have lost the battle. = 如果将军没有出击，他们就会输掉战斗。
If I were braver, I would sally forth and face my fears. = 如果我更勇敢，我会勇敢地面对我的恐惧。

被动语态:
The city was sallied against by the enemy. = 城市受到了敌人的攻击。
A new marketing strategy was sallied forth by the company. = 公司推出了一个新的营销策略。

疑问句:
Will they sally forth into the unknown? = 他们会冒险进入未知领域吗？
Did he sally forth with a plan in mind? = 他出击时心里有计划吗？

否定句:
The soldiers did not sally forth that night. = 士兵们那天晚上没有出击。
He did not sally forth with an answer to her question. = 他没有回答她的问题。


PHRASE->
sally forth = 出击；出发
sally out = 冲出去；突然出现
sally into = 突然进入；开始从事
make a sally = 发起攻击
a sally of wit = 一句妙语
