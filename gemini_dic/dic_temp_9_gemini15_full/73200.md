forceable:/ˈfɔːrsəbl/| adj.| 可强制的，可强迫的；强烈的 | Capable of being forced; able to be compelled or achieved by force.  It can also describe something done with great force or intensity. |例句：The lock was forceable with a crowbar.  撬棍可以撬开这把锁。

The police made a forceable entry into the building. 警方强行进入大楼。


近义词：compellable, coercible, forcible, powerful, strong

反义词：impossible, unachievable, weak, gentle


词性:形容词
