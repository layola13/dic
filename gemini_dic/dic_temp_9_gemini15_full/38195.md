compulsively:/kəmˈpʌlsɪvli/ | adv. | 强迫性地；不由自主地 | done repeatedly without the ability to stop | He compulsively checked his phone for messages. 他不由自主地反复查看手机是否有信息。 |  obsessively, uncontrollably | voluntarily, willingly | 副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He compulsively checks his email every five minutes. = 他每五分钟就强迫性地检查一次电子邮件。
She compulsively buys shoes, even though she has plenty. = 她不由自主地买鞋，即使她已经有很多了。


一般过去时:
He compulsively ate sweets all day yesterday. = 昨天他一整天都在强迫性地吃糖果。
She compulsively cleaned her house for hours. = 她强迫性地打扫房子几个小时。


现在完成时:
He has compulsively gambled away all his savings. = 他已经强迫性地把所有的积蓄都赌掉了。
She has compulsively collected stamps for twenty years. = 她已经强迫性地收集邮票二十年了。


过去完成时:
He had compulsively lied to his parents for years before confessing. = 在坦白之前，他已经强迫性地对父母撒谎多年了。
She had compulsively avoided social situations for months. = 她几个月来一直强迫性地避免社交场合。


将来时:
He will compulsively check his phone as soon as he wakes up. = 他醒来后会不由自主地检查手机。
She will compulsively overeat during the holidays. = 节日期间她会强迫性地暴饮暴食。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't so compulsively neat, his apartment would be more livable. = 如果他不那么强迫性地整洁，他的公寓会更宜居。
I wish I weren't so compulsively worried about everything. = 我希望我不那么强迫性地担心所有事情。


被动语态:  (该词为副词，一般不用于被动语态)
The task was completed compulsively.  = 任务是被迫完成的。(虽然语法上可以，但意义上不自然)
His actions were described as compulsively driven. = 他的行为被描述为受强迫驱使的。(更自然的用法)


疑问句:
Does he compulsively check his phone? = 他是不是强迫性地检查手机？
Is she compulsively spending money? = 她是不是强迫性地花钱？


否定句:
He doesn't compulsively clean his room. = 他不会强迫性地打扫房间。
She doesn't compulsively overthink every little thing. = 她不会强迫性地过度思考每一件小事。
