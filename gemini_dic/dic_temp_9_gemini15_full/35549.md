coalesces:/koʊəˈlɛsɪz/ | v. | 结合；合并；融合 | To come together and form one mass or whole.  | The small droplets of water coalesced to form a larger drop. 小水滴结合成了一大滴。The different factions coalesced into a single political party. 不同的派系合并成一个政党。 | merge, combine, fuse, unite | separate, divide, disperse | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The liquid coalesces slowly. 液体慢慢地融合。
Many small businesses coalesce into larger corporations. 许多小型企业合并成大型公司。

一般过去时:
The clouds coalesced into a storm. 云层汇集成暴风雨。
The two companies coalesced last year. 这两家公司去年合并了。


现在完成时:
The protesters have coalesced into a powerful movement. 抗议者已经联合成一个强大的运动。
The evidence has coalesced into a compelling case. 证据已经汇集成一个令人信服的案例。


过去完成时:
The ingredients had coalesced into a smooth paste.  配料已经融合成一种光滑的糊状物。
The group had coalesced before the march began.  队伍在游行开始前就已经集合完毕。


将来时:
The particles will coalesce under pressure.  粒子将在压力下融合。
The different opinions will hopefully coalesce into a consensus.  不同的意见有望融合成共识。


虚拟语气:
If the two groups coalesced, they would be a powerful force. 如果这两个群体合并，他们将成为一股强大的力量。
It is essential that the different elements coalesce harmoniously.  至关重要的是，不同的元素和谐地融合在一起。


被动语态:
The ingredients were coalesced into a smooth paste. 配料被融合成一种光滑的糊状物。
The different opinions were carefully coalesced into a single statement.  不同的意见被仔细地融合成一份声明。


疑问句:

否定句:
The two groups did not coalesce. 这两个群体没有合并。
The ideas failed to coalesce into a unified theory.  这些想法未能融合成一个统一的理论。


PHRASE->

coalesce into:  融合成…
coalesce around:  围绕…而团结
coalesce with: 与…合并
coalesce from: 从…融合而来
coalesce to: 融合到…


词源:

The word "coalesce" comes from the Late Latin word *coalescere*, a combination of *co-* (together) and *alescere* (to grow, to nourish).  This reflects the sense of things growing together to form a unified whole.
