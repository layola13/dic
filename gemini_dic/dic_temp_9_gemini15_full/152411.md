pavlovas:/pævˈləʊvəz/| n. | 泡芙|A pavlova is a meringue-based dessert named after the Russian ballerina Anna Pavlova.  It typically consists of a crisp meringue base, topped with whipped cream and fresh fruit. |例句：We had a delicious pavlova for dessert at the party.  我们在派对上吃了美味的泡芙作为甜点。|近义词：meringue pie, meringue cake |反义词：无
