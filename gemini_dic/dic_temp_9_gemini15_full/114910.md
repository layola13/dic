manacled:/məˈnækl/| adj. v.|上镣铐的；束缚的|Chained or restrained with manacles.  The prisoner was manacled to the wall.|The manacled suspect was led away by police.|近义词：shackled, chained, fettered|反义词：freed, liberated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He manacles the prisoner. = 他给囚犯戴上手铐。
They manacle the suspect to the chair. = 他们把嫌疑犯铐在椅子上。


一般过去时:
The police manacled the thief. = 警察给小偷戴上手铐。
They manacled him to the wall. = 他们把他铐在墙上。


现在完成时:
The police have manacled the suspect. = 警察已经给嫌疑犯戴上手铐了。
They have manacled him and taken him away. = 他们已经把他铐起来带走了。


过去完成时:
The prisoner had been manacled before the interrogation. = 审问之前，囚犯已经被戴上手铐了。
They had manacled him before transporting him. = 他们在押送他之前已经把他铐起来了。
