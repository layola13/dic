effecting:/ɪˈfektɪŋ/| v.| 产生；实现；引起；生效 | To bring about or accomplish (something); to cause (something) to happen; to make (something) effective or operative. | 例句：The new law is effecting significant changes in the industry.  新法律正在对该行业产生重大影响。 | 例句：They are effecting repairs to the damaged bridge. 他们正在对损坏的桥梁进行维修。 | 近义词：causing, producing, accomplishing, implementing | 反义词：neglecting, hindering, preventing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are effecting positive changes in the community. = 他们在社区中正在产生积极的变化。
The new policy is effecting improvements in efficiency. = 新政策正在提高效率。


一般过去时:
The government effected major reforms in the education system. = 政府对教育制度进行了重大改革。
The medicine effected a rapid cure. = 这药很快治好了病。


现在完成时:
The changes have effected a significant improvement in our sales. = 这些变化使我们的销售额有了显著提高。
The new regulations have effected a reduction in pollution. = 新规已减少了污染。


过去完成时:
They had effected repairs before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经完成了维修。
The treaty had effected peace between the two nations for many years. = 这项条约使这两个国家和平相处多年。


将来时:
The new technology will effect a revolution in the industry. = 新技术将给行业带来一场革命。
We will effect the changes as soon as possible. = 我们将尽快实施这些改变。


虚拟语气:
If they effected the changes earlier, the situation would be better now. = 如果他们早点做出改变，现在的情况就会更好。
If only they had effected a better plan. = 但愿他们制定了一个更好的计划。


被动语态:
Significant changes are being effected in the company's structure. = 公司的结构正在发生重大变化。
The repairs were effected quickly and efficiently. = 维修工作迅速高效地完成了。


疑问句:
What changes are being effected by this new law? = 这部新法律带来了哪些变化？
How will they effect the necessary changes? = 他们将如何实施必要的改变？


否定句:
The new policy did not effect any significant changes. = 新政策没有产生任何重大变化。
Their efforts did not effect a solution to the problem. = 他们的努力并没有解决问题。



PHRASE->
effect a change = 带来改变
effect a cure = 治愈
effect a compromise = 达成妥协
effect an arrest = 逮捕
effect a rescue = 营救
