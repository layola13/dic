footbridges:/ˈfʊtˌbrɪdʒɪz/| n.|人行天桥；步行桥|A footbridge is a bridge designed for pedestrians to cross over a road, river, or other obstacle.  It is typically narrower and simpler in design than a road bridge.|例句：The new footbridge provides a safe way for pedestrians to cross the busy highway. 新建的人行天桥为行人提供了一种安全穿越繁忙高速公路的方式。

例句：Many tourists enjoy walking across the charming footbridge to reach the opposite bank of the river. 许多游客喜欢走过迷人的步行桥到达河的对岸。


近义词：pedestrian bridge, walkway, overpass|反义词：road bridge, vehicle bridge
