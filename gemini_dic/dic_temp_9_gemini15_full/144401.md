outstript:/ˌaʊtˈstrɪpt/ | v. |超过，胜过，超越 | To surpass or exceed (someone or something) in performance or achievement.  |例句：Her success outstripped all expectations.  她的成功超出了所有人的预期。
例句：The demand for the product outstripped the supply.  产品的需求超过了供应。|近义词：surpass, exceed, transcend, eclipse |反义词：underperform, lag behind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company's growth outstrips its competitors. = 这家公司的增长超过了它的竞争对手。
His talent outstrips that of his peers. = 他的天赋超过了他的同龄人。


一般过去时:
Last year's sales outstripped those of the previous year. = 去年销售额超过了前一年。
The athlete outstripped his rivals in the final race. = 运动员在决赛中超过了他的对手。


现在完成时:
The team has outstripped all expectations this season. = 这个赛季球队已经超出了所有人的预期。
Technological advancements have outstripped our ability to regulate them. = 技术进步已经超过了我们监管它们的能力。


过去完成时:
By the end of the project, his contribution had outstripped everyone else's. = 到项目结束时，他的贡献已经超过了其他所有人。
The army had outstripped the enemy's forces before they could regroup.  = 敌军还没来得及重整旗鼓，军队就已经超过了他们。


将来时:
We expect that demand will outstrip supply in the coming months. = 我们预计未来几个月需求将超过供应。
The company's innovative products will outstrip the competition. = 这家公司的创新产品将超越竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If only I had worked harder, I could have outstripped my competitors. = 如果我当时更努力工作，我就能超过我的竞争对手了。
If the technology were to advance further, it would quickly outstrip our capabilities. = 如果技术进一步发展，它将很快超过我们的能力。


被动语态:
The company was outstripped by its competitors in the market share. = 这家公司在市场份额上被竞争对手超越了。
His achievements were outstripped by those of his younger brother. = 他的成就被他的弟弟超越了。


疑问句:
Will our production capacity outstrip the demand? = 我们的生产能力能否超过需求？
Does his performance outstrip the expectations of the coach? = 他的表现是否超过了教练的预期？


否定句:
The team's performance did not outstrip their rivals'. = 球队的表现没有超过他们的对手。
His efforts did not outstrip those of his colleagues. = 他的努力没有超过他的同事。


PHRASE->
outstrip the competition: 超越竞争对手
outstrip expectations: 超出预期
outstrip the demand: 超出需求
outstrip the supply: 超出供应
outstrip one's rivals: 超越对手


词源:
The word "outstrip" comes from the Old English "ūt-" (out) + "strīpan" (to strip). The original meaning involved exceeding something by stripping it away.  Over time, this evolved to its current meaning of surpassing in performance or achievement.
