cloned:/kloʊnd/| /kləʊnd/ | adj. v. | 克隆的；复制的 |  Describes something that is an exact copy of something else, especially a living organism, created by cloning.  | Example: The cloned sheep was a scientific breakthrough.  克隆羊是科学的突破。 The scientist cloned the gene. 科学家克隆了这个基因。 |近义词：copied, duplicated, replicated | 反义词：original, unique |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Scientists clone genes in the lab. = 科学家在实验室里克隆基因。
He clones images for his artwork. 他为他的艺术作品克隆图像。

一般过去时:
Dolly the sheep was cloned in 1996. = 多利羊于1996年被克隆。
They cloned the plant successfully. 他们成功地克隆了这种植物。


现在完成时:
Researchers have cloned many different species. = 研究人员已经克隆了许多不同的物种。
The company has cloned the software for wider distribution. 公司已经克隆了该软件以便更广泛地分发。


过去完成时:
They had cloned the DNA before the experiment began. = 他们在实验开始前已经克隆了DNA。
Scientists had cloned several plants before achieving success with mammals. 在成功克隆哺乳动物之前，科学家已经克隆了几种植物。


将来时:
They will clone more animals in the future. = 他们将来会克隆更多动物。
We will have cloned the data by tomorrow. 我们明天将完成数据的克隆。


虚拟语气:
If they cloned a human, it would be controversial. = 如果他们克隆一个人，那将是具有争议性的。
I wish they hadn't cloned that animal. 我真希望他们没有克隆那只动物。


被动语态:
The plant was cloned using tissue culture. = 这种植物是用组织培养克隆的。
Many animals have been cloned for research purposes. 许多动物已被克隆用于研究目的。


疑问句:
Has the gene been cloned successfully? = 基因克隆成功了吗？
Were the plants cloned from the same source? 这些植物是从同一个来源克隆的吗？


否定句:
They did not clone the entire genome. = 他们没有克隆整个基因组。
The experiment failed to clone the animal. 实验未能克隆出该动物。
