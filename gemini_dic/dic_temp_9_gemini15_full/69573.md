felons:/ˈfɛlənz/| n.|重罪犯，罪犯|A felon is a person who has been convicted of a serious crime, typically one punishable by imprisonment for more than one year.|例句：The police arrested several felons involved in the bank robbery.  警方逮捕了几名参与银行抢劫案的重罪犯。|例句：He was labeled a felon after being convicted of grand theft auto.  因犯下重罪汽车盗窃罪而被贴上重罪犯的标签。|近义词：criminals, offenders, convicts|反义词：law-abiding citizens
