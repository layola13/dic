saled:/seɪld/| v.|出售，销售|Past tense of "sell". It describes the act of selling something.  |例句：The company saled all its products quickly. 公司迅速售出了所有产品。
例句：They saled the house last week. 他们上周卖掉了房子。|近义词：sold, disposed of|反义词：bought, purchased|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The store sells many items. = 这家商店卖很多东西。
He sells cars for a living. = 他靠卖车为生。

一般过去时:
She sold her old car last month. = 她上个月卖掉了她的旧车。
They sold out of tickets quickly. = 他们很快就把票卖光了。

现在完成时:
We have sold all the cakes. = 我们已经把所有的蛋糕都卖掉了。
They have sold more than 100 units this week. = 他们本周已经卖出了超过100件产品。

过去完成时:
They had sold their house before they moved. = 他们在搬家之前已经卖掉了房子。
The company had sold all its stock by the end of the year. = 到年底，该公司已将其所有库存全部售出。

将来时:
We will sell our old furniture next week. = 我们下周将出售我们的旧家具。
The company expects to sell a million units next year. = 公司预计明年将销售一百万件产品。

虚拟语气:
If I sold my car, I could buy a new one. = 如果我卖掉我的车，我就能买一辆新车。
I wish I had sold my stocks earlier. = 我真希望我早点卖掉我的股票。

被动语态:
Many products are sold online. = 许多产品都在网上销售。
The house was sold for a high price. = 这栋房子以高价售出。

疑问句:
Did you sell your bike? = 你卖掉你的自行车了吗？
How many books did they sell? = 他们卖了多少本书？

否定句:
I didn't sell my car. = 我没有卖掉我的车。
The shop didn't sell that item. = 这家店没有卖那种商品。


PHRASE->
sell out = 卖光
sell off = 廉价出售
sell short = 做空
sell oneself = 推销自己
sell like hotcakes = 热销
