moldable: /ˈmoʊldəbəl/ | adj. | 可塑的，易于塑造的 | Easily shaped or molded; malleable; adaptable.  |例句：Clay is a moldable material. 粘土是一种可塑的材料。

The young minds are moldable and receptive to new ideas. 年轻的头脑易于接受新思想。

近义词：malleable, pliable, ductile, adaptable | 反义词：rigid, inflexible, unyielding, intractable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The clay is moldable. = 这粘土是可塑的。
This plan is moldable to our needs. = 这个计划可以根据我们的需要进行调整。

一般过去时:
The metal was moldable when hot. = 金属在热的时候是可塑的。
Her personality was surprisingly moldable in her youth. = 她年轻时的性格出人意料地易于塑造。

现在完成时:
The sculptor has found the material moldable. = 雕塑家发现这种材料是可塑的。
His opinion has become more moldable after the discussion. = 讨论之后，他的观点变得更易于改变。

词源:
From "mold" (to shape) + "-able" (capable of being).  The word "mold" itself has Old English roots, ultimately related to words meaning "earth" or "soil," reflecting its early use in shaping earth or clay.
