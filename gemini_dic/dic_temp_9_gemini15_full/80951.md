grace-notes:/ɡreɪsˌnoʊts/| n.|装饰音，倚音|Short, usually unaccented notes added to a melody to embellish it. They are typically played quickly and softly.|Example: The pianist added delicate grace notes to the melody, enhancing its beauty.  钢琴家在旋律中加入了精美的装饰音，增强了它的美感。|近义词：ornament, embellishment|反义词：main note, principal note
