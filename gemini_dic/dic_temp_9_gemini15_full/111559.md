lobe:/loʊb/| n.|叶，瓣；脑叶；肺叶|A lobe is a roundish projection or division of an organ or part.  It is often used to describe parts of the brain, lungs, or liver.|The occipital lobe of the brain processes visual information.大脑的枕叶处理视觉信息。|The right lung has three lobes, while the left lung has two. 右肺有三叶，左肺有两叶。|section, division, segment|  (no direct antonym, depends on context)|名词
