morisco:/məˈrɪskoʊ/| adj. n.|摩尔人的；摩尔人后裔；摩尔式风格的 |Of or relating to the Moors or their culture.  The term often refers to Muslims of Iberian descent who remained in Spain after the Reconquista, or to their descendants. It can also describe art, architecture, or other cultural elements associated with them. |例句：The Alhambra palace is a stunning example of Morisco architecture. 阿罕布拉宫是摩尔式建筑的惊人例子。

The intricate details of the Morisco tilework are breathtaking. 摩尔式瓷砖的复杂细节令人叹为观止。|近义词：Moorish|反义词：None specifically, but terms suggesting a contrasting culture could be used in context.
