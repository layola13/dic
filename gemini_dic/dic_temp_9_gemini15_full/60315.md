ego-tripped:/ˈiːɡoʊtrɪpt/ | adj. | 自我膨胀的，自大的 |  Characterized by excessive pride or self-importance; behaving in a way that shows off one's perceived superiority.  | 例句：He became ego-tripped after his promotion and started treating everyone condescendingly.  他升职后变得自大，开始居高临下地对待所有人。


例句：Her ego-tripped Instagram posts were filled with photos of her luxury lifestyle and achievements. 她的Instagram帖子充满了炫耀她奢华生活方式和成就的自大内容。


近义词：narcissistic, conceited, arrogant, self-important, full of oneself


反义词：humble, modest, unassuming, self-effacing


词性: Adjective (形容词)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is ego-tripped and refuses to listen to criticism. 他很自大，拒绝听取批评。
She acts ego-tripped, always boasting about her accomplishments. 她表现得很自大，总是吹嘘自己的成就。

一般过去时:
He was ego-tripped after winning the award. 他在获奖后变得自大。
She was ego-tripped about her intelligence, ignoring others' opinions. 她对自己的智力过于自负，无视其他人的意见。

现在完成时:
He has become ego-tripped since he started his own business. 自从他开始创业以来，他就变得自大了。
She has been ego-tripped for years, and it's starting to affect her relationships. 她多年来一直很自大，这开始影响到她的关系。

过去完成时:
He had been ego-tripped for so long that no one dared to challenge him. 他自负了那么久，以至于没有人敢挑战他。
She had become ego-tripped after years of success and recognition. 在多年的成功和认可之后，她变得自大了。

将来时:
He will be ego-tripped if he wins the election. 如果他赢得选举，他会变得自大的。
She will likely become ego-tripped if she gets the promotion. 如果她得到晋升，她很可能会变得自大。

虚拟语气:
If he weren't so ego-tripped, he would have more friends. 如果他不那么自大，他就会有更多朋友。
If she hadn't been so ego-tripped, she would have listened to her advisor's advice. 如果她不那么自大，她就会听取她顾问的建议。

被动语态:  (This adjective is rarely used in passive voice.)  这个形容词很少用于被动语态。
His behavior is perceived as ego-tripped by many. 许多人认为他的行为是自大的。
His success was seen as having made him ego-tripped. 他的成功被认为使他变得自大了。

疑问句:
Is he always so ego-tripped? 他总是这么自大吗？
Was she ego-tripped before her promotion? 她升职前自大吗？

否定句:
He is not ego-tripped; he's just confident. 他并不自大；他只是自信。
She wasn't ego-tripped; she was simply proud of her accomplishments. 她并不自大；她只是为自己的成就感到自豪。
