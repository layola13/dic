filtration: /ˌfɪlˈtreɪʃən/ | n. |过滤；过滤作用 |The process of separating solids from a liquid or gas by passing the mixture through a porous material.  This process removes unwanted substances.  |例句：The filtration of water removes impurities.  水过滤去除了杂质。

例句：The coffee maker uses a paper filter for filtration. 咖啡机使用纸质滤网进行过滤。


近义词：purification, screening, sieving |反义词：contamination, pollution
