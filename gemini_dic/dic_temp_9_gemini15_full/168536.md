procaryotes:/proʊˈkæriˌoʊts/ | n. |原核生物 |Prokaryotes are organisms whose cells lack a nucleus and other membrane-bound organelles.  They are typically unicellular, although some form colonies.  Examples include bacteria and archaea. |Example: Bacteria and archaea are examples of prokaryotes.  细菌和古细菌是原核生物的例子。|近义词:monera (less common now)|反义词:eukaryotes
