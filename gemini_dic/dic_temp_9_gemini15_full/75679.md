fulfilment:/fʊlˈfɪlmənt/ | n. | 履行；实现；满足；成就 | The act of fulfilling or being fulfilled; the achievement of something desired, promised, or expected.  It can also refer to a feeling of satisfaction and happiness resulting from achieving one's goals. |  例句：The fulfilment of a long-held ambition brought her great joy.  实现长久以来的抱负给她带来了极大的快乐。 |  例句：He found fulfilment in his work as a teacher. 他在作为一名教师的工作中找到了满足感。 | 近义词：achievement, accomplishment, realization, satisfaction | 反义词：failure, disappointment, frustration |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is fulfilling its objectives. = 项目正在实现其目标。
Her work brings her a sense of fulfilment. = 她的工作带给她一种满足感。

一般过去时:
They fulfilled their promise to donate to charity. = 他们履行了向慈善机构捐款的承诺。
The concert fulfilled all my expectations. = 音乐会满足了我所有的期望。

现在完成时:
She has fulfilled her dream of becoming a doctor. = 她已经实现了成为医生的梦想。
He has found fulfilment in his family life. = 他在家庭生活中找到了满足感。

过去完成时:
They had fulfilled their contract before the deadline. = 他们已在截止日期前完成了合同。
She had found fulfilment in her career before retiring. = 她在退休前已经在事业上找到了满足感。

将来时:
We will fulfil our obligations to our customers. = 我们将履行对客户的义务。
I believe this plan will fulfil your needs. = 我相信这个计划会满足你的需求。

虚拟语气:
If only I could fulfil my potential. = 如果我能发挥我的潜力就好了。
I wish I could fulfil my parents' expectations.  = 我希望我能满足父母的期望。

被动语态:
The contract was fulfilled on time. = 合同按时完成了。
All requirements were fulfilled to the highest standards. = 所有要求都以最高标准完成了。

疑问句:
Has the company fulfilled its promises? = 公司是否履行了承诺？
Will this action fulfil our goals? = 这个行动能实现我们的目标吗？

否定句:
The company failed to fulfil its obligations. = 公司未能履行其义务。
He didn't fulfil his potential. = 他没有发挥他的潜力。
