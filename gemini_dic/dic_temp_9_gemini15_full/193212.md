seductive:/səˈdjuːsɪv/ | adj. | 诱惑的；有魅力的；迷人的 |  Attractive or tempting in a way that entices or excites.  It often implies a certain danger or risk associated with the attraction. |

例句：Her seductive smile captivated everyone in the room.  她的迷人微笑迷住了房间里的每个人。

例句：The seductive aroma of freshly baked bread filled the kitchen. 新鲜烘焙面包的诱人香味充满了厨房。


近义词：alluring, tempting, enticing, captivating, charming, attractive

反义词：repulsive, unattractive, unappealing, repellent


词源：  Comes from the verb "seduce," which itself derives from the Old French "seduire," meaning "to lead astray, mislead, entice."  The ultimate origin is the Latin "ducere," meaning "to lead."  The prefix "se-" implies "away from oneself," adding a sense of being drawn away or tempted.
