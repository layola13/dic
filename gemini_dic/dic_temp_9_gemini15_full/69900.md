fertilizabilities:/ˌfɜːrtɪlaɪzəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |受精能力，可育性|The ability of an ovum or sperm to be fertilized.  It refers to the capacity of gametes (sperm and egg) to successfully unite and initiate the development of a new organism. This capacity can be influenced by various factors, including the health and maturity of the gametes, the environment, and genetic factors.|例句：The study investigated the fertilizabilities of different sperm samples. 这项研究调查了不同精液样本的受精能力。

The researchers assessed the fertilizabilities of eggs after cryopreservation. 研究人员评估了冷冻保存后卵子的受精能力。|近义词：fecundity, fertility|反义词：sterility, infertility|名词
