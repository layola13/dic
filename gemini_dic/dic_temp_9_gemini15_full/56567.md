dominion:/dəˈmɪnjən/ | n. |统治；支配；领地；所有权 |  supreme authority; control; territory ruled; ownership |The British Empire once held dominion over vast territories.  英国帝国曾经统治着广阔的领土。

The company asserted its dominion over the market. 这家公司宣称其对市场的统治地位。


近义词：rule, reign, sovereignty, sway, authority, control | 反义词：subjection, servitude, dependence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He holds dominion over his kingdom. 他统治着他的王国。
This company exercises dominion over the market. 这家公司控制着市场。


一般过去时:
The Roman Empire held dominion over much of Europe. 罗马帝国统治着欧洲大部分地区。
The king's dominion extended to many lands. 国王的统治范围延伸到许多土地。


现在完成时:
They have asserted their dominion over the region. 他们已经确立了对该地区的统治。
The family has held dominion over the estate for centuries. 这个家族几个世纪以来一直拥有这片庄园的所有权。


过去完成时:
Before the revolution, the monarch had held dominion for decades. 革命之前，君主已经统治了几十年。
The empire had lost much of its dominion before its collapse. 这个帝国在其崩溃之前已经失去了大部分领土。


将来时:
She will claim dominion over her inheritance. 她将宣称对她的遗产拥有所有权。
The new leader will likely establish his dominion quickly. 新领导人可能会迅速确立他的统治地位。


虚拟语气:
If he had absolute dominion, he would change everything. 如果他拥有绝对的权力，他会改变一切。
I wish I had the dominion to make such decisions. 我希望我有权力做出这样的决定。


被动语态:
The land was once under the dominion of a powerful king. 这片土地曾经在一个强大的国王的统治之下。
The market is increasingly being dominated by large corporations. 市场正日益被大型公司所控制。


疑问句:
Who holds dominion over this territory? 谁统治着这片领土？
Does the company have dominion over its competitors? 这家公司是否控制着它的竞争对手？


否定句:
He does not claim dominion over others. 他不声称统治别人。
The government lacks dominion over certain regions. 政府对某些地区缺乏控制权。


PHRASE->
dominion over: 对…的统治
under the dominion of: 在…的统治下
within the dominion of: 在…的管辖范围内
lose dominion: 失去统治
assert dominion: 宣称统治
