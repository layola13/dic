falsifier:/ˈfɔːlsɪfaɪər/| n. |伪造者；篡改者|A falsifier is someone who creates or presents something false or inaccurate, often intentionally to deceive.  They might forge documents, alter evidence, or fabricate information. |例句：The police caught the falsifier red-handed, forging signatures on important documents.  警察当场抓获了伪造重要文件签名的伪造者。|近义词：forger, counterfeiter, fabricator |反义词：truth-teller, verifier
