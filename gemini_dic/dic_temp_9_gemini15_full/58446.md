ducks: /dʌks/ | n. | 鸭子；鸭肉 | A waterbird with a broad flat bill, short legs, and webbed feet.  Ducks are known for their swimming ability and often found in ponds, lakes, and rivers. | 例句：The ducks were swimming peacefully on the pond.  鸭子们正在池塘里平静地游泳。

The roast duck was delicious. 烤鸭真好吃。

近义词： waterfowl, bird | 反义词：N/A
