samoyedes:/ˌsæməˈjɛdiz/| n. |萨摩耶德人；萨摩耶德语；萨摩耶犬|Samoyedes refers to several things: a group of indigenous peoples of northern Russia and Siberia, their languages, and a breed of dog originating from Siberia.  The term encompasses various ethnic groups with shared cultural and linguistic traits.|The Samoyedes are known for their resilience in harsh climates. |近义词:  (for people)  Uralic peoples, Siberian peoples; (for language) Uralic languages; (for dogs) Siberian husky,  Alaskan malamute |反义词:  N/A
