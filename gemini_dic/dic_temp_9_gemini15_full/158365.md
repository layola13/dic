pleach:/pliːtʃ/| vt.|编织；交错；修剪（树枝）|To interweave (branches) so as to form a dense hedge or screen; to bend or weave together.  |例句：The gardener pleached the young trees to create a living wall. 园丁将幼树编织在一起，形成一道绿墙。|例句：The branches were pleached together to form a shady bower. 枝条交错编织在一起，形成凉爽的凉亭。|近义词：interweave, interlace, intertwine, braid|反义词：separate, disentangle
