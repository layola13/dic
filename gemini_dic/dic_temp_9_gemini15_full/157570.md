pitcherful: /ˈpɪtʃərˌful/ | n. | 一大壶的；一罐的 |  A pitcherful is a quantity filled to the capacity of a pitcher. It refers to the amount a pitcher can hold.  The word combines "pitcher" and the suffix "-ful," indicating "full of." | 例句：She made a pitcherful of lemonade for the party. 她为派对做了一大壶柠檬水。


The gardener watered the plants with a pitcherful of water. 园丁用一罐水浇灌植物。


近义词：pitcher's worth, a full pitcher | 反义词：a little, a small amount

词性: 名词
