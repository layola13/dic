inoculates:/ˈɪnɒkjʊleɪts/ | vt. |接种疫苗；灌输；使免疫 |To introduce a vaccine or antiserum into (a person or animal) to produce immunity against a disease; to introduce (an idea, belief, etc.) into the mind of someone. |例句：The doctor inoculated the child against measles.  医生给孩子接种了麻疹疫苗。
The teacher tried to inoculate her students with a love of learning. 老师试图给学生灌输对学习的热爱。|近义词：vaccinate, immunize, implant |反义词：infect, contaminate


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The doctor inoculates patients against the flu every year. = 医生每年都给病人接种流感疫苗。
She inoculates her children with positive thinking. = 她给她的孩子们灌输积极的思维。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:

She has inoculated herself against cynicism. = 她已经让自己免受愤世嫉俗的影响。

虚拟语气:

If I were to inoculate myself against failure, I would focus on perseverance.  = 如果我要让自己免受失败的影响，我会专注于坚持不懈。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
