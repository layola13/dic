radar: /ˈreɪdɑːr/ | n. | 雷达 | Radio Detection and Ranging; a system for detecting the presence, direction, distance, and speed of aircraft, ships, or other objects by sending out radio waves that are reflected back by the object.  | The airport uses radar to monitor incoming flights.  机场使用雷达监测进港航班。 | sonar, lidar |  (no direct antonym) | 名词
