diy:/ˌdiːaɪˈwaɪ/| n. /v.|自己动手做；自己动手制作|Do it yourself;  the activity of making or repairing things yourself, rather than paying someone else to do it. |例句：I prefer DIY to hiring a professional. 我更喜欢自己动手而不是雇佣专业人士。

例句：She enjoys DIY and has renovated her entire house. 她喜欢自己动手，已经翻新了她的整栋房子。


近义词：self-made, homemade, handcrafted |反义词：professional, commissioned


词性:n./v.
