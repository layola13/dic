hailstones:/ˈheɪlstəʊnz/| n.|冰雹|Pieces of ice that fall from the sky like rain. Hailstones vary greatly in size, from small pellets to balls of ice several inches in diameter.  They are formed in cumulonimbus clouds and often associated with thunderstorms.|The hailstones damaged the car's windshield.  冰雹损坏了汽车挡风玻璃。The children were amazed by the large hailstones falling from the sky. 孩子们对从天而降的大冰雹感到惊讶。|ice pellets, ice balls|rain, sunshine|名词
