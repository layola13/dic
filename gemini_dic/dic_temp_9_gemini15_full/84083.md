half-informed:/ˌhɑːfɪnˈfɔːrmd/| adj. | 半知情的；了解不全的 |  Having or showing only partial or incomplete knowledge or understanding of a subject.  This adjective implies a dangerous level of confidence despite the lack of complete information.  |  He made a half-informed decision based on limited research. 他根据有限的研究做出了一个半知情的决定。  | partially informed, insufficiently informed | fully informed, well-informed | 形容词


USAGE->

He is half-informed about the project. = 他对这个项目了解不全。
They are half-informed regarding the new policy. = 他们对新政策了解不全。


一般过去时:
