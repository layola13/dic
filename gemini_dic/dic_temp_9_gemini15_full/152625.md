peaching:/ˈpiːtʃɪŋ/| v.|告密；检举；泄露；|To inform on someone or something, especially to the authorities.|例句：He was peaching on his friends to the teacher. 他向老师告发了朋友们。|例句：The newspaper article was peaching on the company's illegal activities. 报纸文章揭露了该公司的非法活动。|近义词：informing, betraying, snitching, tattling|反义词：protecting, concealing, shielding


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He peaches on his classmates regularly. = 他经常告发他的同学。
She is peaching to the manager about the safety hazard. = 她正在向经理举报安全隐患。


一般过去时:
The boy peached on his brother for breaking the vase. = 男孩告发他哥哥打碎了花瓶。
She peached on the thief to the police. = 她向警方告发了小偷。


现在完成时:
He has peached on his colleagues several times this year. = 他今年已经几次告发他的同事了。
She has peached on the company's illegal practices to the authorities. = 她已经向当局举报了公司的违规行为。


过去完成时:
He had peached on his friend before the truth came out. = 在真相大白之前，他已经告发了朋友。
She had peached on the conspiracy before the police could arrest anyone. = 在警方逮捕任何人之前，她已经告发了阴谋。


将来时:
I will peach on him if he continues to bully me. = 如果他继续欺负我，我会告发他。
They will peach on the illegal activities to the relevant authorities. = 他们会向相关部门举报非法活动。


虚拟语气:
If he peached on them, they would be in serious trouble. = 如果他告发他们，他们将面临严重麻烦。
I wish I hadn't peached on my friend. = 我真希望我没有告发我的朋友。


被动语态:
He was peached on by his own brother. = 他被自己的兄弟告发了。
The company's illegal activities were peached on by a whistleblower. = 该公司的非法活动被一名举报人揭露了。


疑问句:
Is he peaching on anyone? = 他在告发任何人吗？
Was she peaching on the suspect? = 她告发嫌疑犯了吗？


否定句:
He didn't peach on his friend, even though he knew the truth. = 即使他知道真相，他也没有告发他的朋友。
She didn't peach on the accident to avoid further trouble. = 她没有举报事故以避免进一步的麻烦。


PHRASE->
peach on someone = 告发某人
peach to the authorities = 向当局举报
peach about something = 泄露某事
to peach an incident = 举报一起事件
a peaching incident = 一个需要举报的事件
