outtwine:/ˌaʊtˈtwaɪn/ | v. |解开，松开；使纠缠不清 | To separate or untwist something that is tangled or intertwined.  |例句：He carefully outtwined the wires. 他小心地解开了电线。

例句：The gardener outtwined the rose bushes that had become entangled. 园丁解开了纠缠在一起的玫瑰花丛。


近义词：untangle, unravel, disentangle | 反义词：entangle, intertwine | 词性:动词
