pre-Syrian:/ˌpriːˈsɪəriən/| adj.| 前叙利亚的，叙利亚之前的 |  Describing something that existed or occurred before the emergence of Syria as a distinct political entity.  The term is generally used in historical or archaeological contexts. | Example:  Pre-Syrian settlements in the region have yielded significant archaeological finds.  前叙利亚时期的定居点在该地区出土了重要的考古发现。|近义词：antecedent, prior, preceding |反义词：post-Syrian
