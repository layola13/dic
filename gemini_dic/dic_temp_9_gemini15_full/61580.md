emergencies:/ɪˈmɜːrdʒənsiz/| n. |紧急情况，突发事件 |Unexpected events requiring immediate action.  For example, a fire, a serious accident, or a sudden illness are all emergencies. |例句：The hospital is well-equipped to handle medical emergencies. 医院设备齐全，能够处理医疗紧急情况。

例句：We need a plan to deal with potential emergencies.我们需要一个计划来应对潜在的紧急情况。


近义词：crises, critical situations, urgent situations |反义词：routine, normalcy
