confirmational: /ˌkɒnfɜːmeɪʃənəl/ | /ˌkɑːnfɚˈmeɪʃənəl/ | adj. |构象的；确认的 | Relating to or denoting the spatial arrangement of atoms in a molecule.  This term is primarily used in chemistry and biochemistry to describe the three-dimensional structure of a molecule.  In a broader sense, it can also refer to the act of confirming or verifying something. |


例句：

The confirmational analysis revealed a significant difference in the folding patterns of the two proteins.  构象分析揭示了两种蛋白质折叠模式的显著差异。

The witness's testimony provided confirmational evidence for the prosecution's case.  证人的证词为检方的案情提供了确凿的证据。


近义词：conformational (in the context of chemistry), corroborative, supportive

反义词：disconfirming, refuting, contradictory


词性: 形容词
