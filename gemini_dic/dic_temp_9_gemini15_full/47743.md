decalcify:/ˌdiːˈkælsɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 脱钙；使脱钙 | To remove calcium salts from (something), especially bone tissue.  |例句：The dentist recommended a procedure to decalcify the affected tooth. 牙医建议进行一项程序来去除受影响牙齿的钙质。

例句：The doctor prescribed medication to help decalcify the patient's arteries. 医生开了药，以帮助病人动脉脱钙。


近义词：demineralize  | 反义词：calcify
