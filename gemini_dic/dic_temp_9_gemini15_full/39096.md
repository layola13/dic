confrier:/kənˈfriər/| n. |同僚，同行；兄弟|A confrier is a fellow member of a profession, guild, or religious order.  It implies a bond of shared experience and purpose.|例句：He considered his fellow lawyers to be his confriers in the pursuit of justice. 他认为他的律师同事是在追求正义方面的同僚。|近义词：colleague, comrade, brother|反义词：opponent, adversary, enemy
