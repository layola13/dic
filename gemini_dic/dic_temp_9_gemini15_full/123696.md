moonblink: /ˈmuːnblɪŋk/ | n. | 月光闪烁；月光微光 | A fleeting glimpse of moonlight, often through clouds or foliage.  It refers to the momentary illumination caused by moonlight breaking through an obstruction. |例句：The moonblink briefly illuminated the path through the trees. 月光透过树林短暂地照亮了小路。

例句：A sudden moonblink revealed a hidden cave.  突然的月光闪烁显露出一个隐藏的洞穴。


近义词：moon gleam, moon flash, momentary illumination


反义词：darkness, shadow, obscurity


词性: 名词
