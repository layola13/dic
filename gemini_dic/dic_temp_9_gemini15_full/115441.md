mantras:/ˈmɑːntrəz/ | n. | 咒语；真言；格言 | A mantra is a sound, syllable, word, or phrase repeated to aid concentration in meditation or prayer.  It can also refer to a statement or slogan repeated frequently to reinforce a particular belief or idea. | Example:  She repeated her personal mantra, "I am strong and capable," to overcome her fear. 她重复着她的个人真言：“我坚强而有能力”，以克服恐惧。 |近义词：incantation, affirmation, slogan |反义词：None particularly applicable.
