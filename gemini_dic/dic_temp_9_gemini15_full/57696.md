drawnet:/ˈdrɔːnɛt/| n. |拖网，张网|A drawnet is a fishing net that is drawn through the water to catch fish.  It is typically used in shallow water and is often operated by hand or with a small boat.|例句：The fishermen used a drawnet to catch the salmon. 渔民用张网捕捞鲑鱼。 The drawnet was heavy with the day's catch. 拖网装满了当天的渔获。|近义词： seine, trawl, dragnet |反义词：None specifically.
