inflamer:/ɪnˈfleɪmər/ | n. |煽动者，激怒者 | A person or thing that inflames or excites strong feelings.  |Example:  The politician's speech acted as an inflamer, sparking protests across the city.  这位政治家的演讲起到了煽动作用，在全市引发了抗议。|近义词：agitator, instigator, firebrand |反义词：peacemaker, conciliator


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The journalist inflames the public with his biased reporting. = 记者用他带有偏见的报道煽动公众。
That song inflames my passion for dancing. = 那首歌点燃了我对舞蹈的热情。


一般过去时:
His words inflamed the already tense situation. = 他的话使本来就紧张的局势更加恶化。
The incident inflamed the conflict between the two countries. = 此事件加剧了这两个国家之间的冲突。


现在完成时:
His actions have inflamed the situation beyond repair. = 他的行为使局势恶化到无法挽回的地步。
The media has inflamed public opinion against the company. = 媒体煽动公众舆论反对该公司。


过去完成时:
They had inflamed the crowd with their nationalist rhetoric before the riot. = 暴乱前，他们用民族主义言论煽动了人群。
The rumors had inflamed tensions between the two families for years. = 多年来，谣言加剧了这两个家庭之间的紧张关系。


将来时:
His speech will inflame passions and cause unrest. = 他的演讲将激起激情并导致动荡。
The upcoming election will inflame political divides. = 即将到来的选举将加剧政治分歧。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't spoken, he wouldn't have inflamed the situation. = 如果他不说话，就不会激化事态。
I wish he hadn't inflamed the argument with his aggressive comments. = 我真希望他不要用咄咄逼人的言辞来激化争论。


被动语态:
The public was inflamed by the news of the scandal. = 公众被丑闻的消息激怒了。
The situation was inflamed by irresponsible reporting. = 不负责任的报道加剧了事态。


疑问句:
What inflamed the conflict? = 什么激化了冲突？
What will inflame the public's anger? = 什么会激起公众的愤怒？


否定句:
He did not intend to inflame the controversy. = 他无意激化这场争议。
The speaker tried not to inflame the audience. = 演讲者试图不激怒听众。


PHRASE->
inflamed passions = 激烈的热情
inflamed rhetoric = 煽动性的言辞
highly inflamed = 剧烈发炎的
inflamed skin = 发炎的皮肤
an inflamed response = 过激的反应

词源:
From Middle English *inflamen, from Old French inflamer, from Latin inflammare "to set on fire," from in- "in, into" + flamma "flame."  The sense evolution is from "set on fire" to "excite strong feelings."
