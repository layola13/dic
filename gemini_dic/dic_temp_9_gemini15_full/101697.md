irreparability:/ˌɪrɛpərəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不可修复性，无法弥补|The state or quality of being irreparable; inability to be repaired or mended.  It often refers to damage that is so extensive or fundamental that it cannot be fixed or restored to its original condition. |The irreparable damage to the ecosystem is a serious concern. 生态系统的不可修复性损害令人担忧。 The irreparability of the broken vase saddened her. 花瓶的无法修复让她很难过。|  repairability, recoverability |  repairability, fixability
