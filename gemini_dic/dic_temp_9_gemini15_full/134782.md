nonmetaphorically:/ˌnɑːnˌmɛtəˈfɔːrɪkli/| adv. |非比喻地；字面地| Literally; without using metaphors or figurative language.  The word emphasizes a direct, literal interpretation, excluding any symbolic or implied meaning.|

例句：He described the situation nonmetaphorically, avoiding any colourful language. 他非比喻地描述了这种情况，避免使用任何生动的语言。

例句：The instructions were given nonmetaphorically to ensure complete understanding.  说明书是用字面意思写的，以确保完全理解。


近义词：literally, directly, plainly, explicitly

反义词：figuratively, metaphorically, symbolically


词源:

* **non-**:  prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of"
* **metaphor-**: from Greek "metaphora," meaning "transfer," "carrying over," referring to the transfer of meaning in figurative language.
* **-ically**: adverbial suffix
