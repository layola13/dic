cashier:/kæˈʃɪər/| n.|收银员|A cashier is a person who handles financial transactions in a retail store, bank, or other establishment.  They receive payments from customers, operate cash registers, and balance accounts.|Example: The cashier counted the money and gave me my change.  收银员数了钱并找给我零钱。|近义词：teller, clerk|反义词：customer, shopper
