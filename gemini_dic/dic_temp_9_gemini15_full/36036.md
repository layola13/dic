codiscovering: /ˌkoʊdɪˈskʌvərɪŋ/ | v. | 共同发现，合作发现 | The act of discovering something together or simultaneously.  It emphasizes the collaborative nature of the discovery. | Example:  Scientists are co-discovering new species in the Amazon rainforest. 科学家们正在亚马逊雨林中共同发现新的物种。 | Near synonyms: jointly discovering, collaboratively discovering | Antonyms: independently discovering, individually discovering | verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are co-discovering a new treatment for cancer. = 他们在共同发现一种新的癌症治疗方法。
We are co-discovering the secrets of the universe. = 我们正在共同探索宇宙的奥秘。


一般过去时:
The two researchers co-discovered a new planet. = 这两位研究人员共同发现了一颗新的行星。
