sinoper:/ˈsɪnəpər/| n. |赭石，红赭石 |A red or reddish-brown earthy pigment consisting of hydrated iron oxide.  It has been used as a paint pigment since antiquity. |The ancient Romans used sinoper extensively in their frescoes. 古罗马人在壁画中广泛使用赭石。  The artist mixed sinoper with linseed oil to create a rich, earthy tone.  艺术家将赭石与亚麻籽油混合以创造出丰富的土质色调。|近义词：red ochre,  hematite |反义词：无
