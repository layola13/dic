loitered:/ˈlɔɪtərd/| v.|闲逛；徘徊；磨蹭|To spend time idly or without purpose; to linger.|例句：The teenagers loitered on the street corner.  青少年在街角闲逛。

例句：He loitered in the park, enjoying the sunshine. 他在公园里闲逛，享受着阳光。

近义词：linger, dawdle, hang around, tarry|反义词：hurry, rush


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They loiter near the shop. = 他们在商店附近闲逛。
He often loiters in the park. = 他经常在公园里闲逛。

一般过去时:
We loitered by the river. = 我们在河边闲逛。
She loitered outside the cinema. = 她电影院外面徘徊。

现在完成时:
They have loitered here for hours. = 他们在这里闲逛了好几个小时。
She has loitered around the mall all afternoon. = 她整个下午都在商场里闲逛。

过去完成时:
They had loitered there before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们一直在那里闲逛。
We had loitered for a while before deciding to go home. = 我们闲逛了一会儿才决定回家。

将来时:
They will loiter around the town square tomorrow. = 他们明天会在城镇广场附近闲逛。
I will loiter on the beach until sunset. = 我会在海滩上闲逛到日落。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would loiter in the bookstore. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会在书店里闲逛。
I wish I could loiter longer, but I have to go. = 我希望我能再闲逛一会儿，但我必须走了。


被动语态:
(Loiter is rarely used in the passive voice.  A more natural phrasing would be needed to express the passive idea.)  很少用被动语态。


疑问句:
Were they loitering near the school? = 他们在学校附近闲逛吗？
Did you loiter on the way home? = 你回家的路上有闲逛吗？


否定句:
They did not loiter; they walked quickly. = 他们没有闲逛；他们走得很快。
We didn't loiter, we went straight home. = 我们没有闲逛，直接回家了。



PHRASE->
loiter around  = 在...附近闲逛
loiter about = 在...附近闲逛
loiter away = 虚度（时间）
loiter on = 继续闲逛
loiter near = 在...附近徘徊


词源:
The word "loiter" comes from the Middle English word "loitren," meaning "to delay" or "to linger."  This word likely has Germanic origins.
