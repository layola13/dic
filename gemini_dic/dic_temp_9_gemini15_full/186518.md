rickshaw:/ˈrɪkʃɔː/| n. |人力车；黄包车 |A rickshaw is a two-wheeled cart, typically pulled by a person, used for transportation, especially in parts of Asia.  |例句：He took a rickshaw to the market. 他坐人力车去了市场。|近义词：jinrikisha, cycle rickshaw |反义词:car, taxi
