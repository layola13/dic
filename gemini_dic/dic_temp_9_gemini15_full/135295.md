nonpassionateness: /ˌnɒnˈpæʃənɪtnəs/ | n. | 冷漠；缺乏热情；无动于衷 | Lack of passion or enthusiasm; apathy.  It describes a state of being unemotional, uninterested, or indifferent. |


例句：

The nonpassionateness of his delivery made the speech rather dull.  他的演讲缺乏激情，使得演讲相当乏味。

Her nonpassionateness towards her work was a cause for concern. 她对工作的冷漠令人担忧。


近义词：apathy, indifference, coldness, detachment, impassiveness, disinterest


反义词：passion, enthusiasm, zeal, fervor, ardor, excitement


词性：名词
