dramaturgist:/ˈdræməˌtɜːrdʒɪst/| n. |剧作家，戏剧顾问|A dramaturgist is a literary advisor or editor who works with playwrights, theatre companies, and other artists to develop and refine theatrical productions. They are experts in dramatic literature, theatre history, and dramatic theory.  They often assist in research, script development, and the overall artistic vision of a play. |The dramaturgist helped the playwright refine the dialogue and structure of the play. 剧作家帮助编剧修改了戏剧的对话和结构。|literary advisor, script doctor|  (no direct antonym)|名词
