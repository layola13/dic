overrichness:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪtʃnəs/| n. |过分富有；极度富有；过于丰富| Excessive wealth or abundance.  The overrichness of the dessert made it sickly sweet. |近义词：opulence, affluence, superabundance |反义词：poverty, scarcity, dearth |名词


英文解析:Overrichness refers to an excessive degree of richness, wealth, or abundance, often to the point of being excessive or undesirable.  It suggests a surplus beyond what is necessary or pleasant.


例句：

The overrichness of the tapestry overwhelmed the eye.  = 挂毯过于华丽，让人眼花缭乱。

The overrichness of the sauce overpowered the delicate flavor of the fish. = 酱汁过于浓厚，盖过了鱼的鲜美。


近义词：

Opulence:  Suggests great wealth and luxuriousness.
Affluence:  Indicates a state of having a great deal of money; wealth.
Superabundance:  Implies an excessive amount; more than is needed.


反义词：

Poverty:  A state of being extremely poor.
Scarcity:  The state of being scarce or in short supply.
Dearth:  A scarcity or lack of something.
