nestor:/ˈnɛstər/| n.|涅斯托尔；老前辈；经验丰富的人|A Nestor is a very old man, especially one who is respected for his wisdom and experience.  The name comes from Nestor, a wise and old warrior in Homer's Iliad.|例句：He was the Nestor of the company, having worked there for over fifty years. 他是公司的 Nestor，在那里工作了五十年以上。|例句：The team looked to their Nestor for guidance during the crisis.  团队在危机期间向他们的老前辈寻求指导。|近义词：veteran, elder, sage|反义词：youth, novice
