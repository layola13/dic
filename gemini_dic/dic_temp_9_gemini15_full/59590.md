ebullience:/ɪˈbʌljəns/| n. |热情奔放；兴高采烈；沸腾|The quality of being full of life, vigor, and enthusiasm; exuberant cheerfulness.  It can also refer to the state of bubbling or boiling. |例句：Her ebullience was infectious, and soon everyone was laughing and dancing.  她的热情奔放极具感染力，很快大家都笑了起来，跳起了舞。

例句：The ebullience of the champagne fountain added to the celebratory atmosphere. 香槟喷泉的沸腾增添了庆祝的气氛。


近义词：exuberance, enthusiasm, effervescence, vivacity, zest|反义词：apathy, lethargy, depression, sluggishness|词性：名词
