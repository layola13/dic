placements:/ˈpleɪsmənts/ | n. |职位；位置；安置；实习；布置|The act of placing someone or something in a particular position or place; a job or position, especially a temporary one; the arrangement of items in a particular place or order.  |例句：The placements of the furniture were carefully considered. 家具的摆放经过仔细考虑。|例句：She secured a placement at a top law firm. 她在一个顶级律师事务所获得了一个职位。|近义词：positions, appointments, assignments, postings, locations|反义词：removal, dismissal, evacuation
