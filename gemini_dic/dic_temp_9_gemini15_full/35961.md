codecs:/ˈkoʊdeks/ | n. | 编解码器 | A codec (coder-decoder) is a device or computer program capable of encoding and decoding a digital data stream or signal.  Codecs are used for compressing and decompressing audio and video files, as well as other types of data. | Example:  The video player uses a specific codec to play the MP4 file.  这个视频播放器使用特定的编解码器播放MP4文件。  |近义词: encoder, decoder, transcoder | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，但可以考虑其功能的反面，例如：uncompressed data)
