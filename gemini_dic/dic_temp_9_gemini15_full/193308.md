seepier:/ˈsiːpiər/| adj.|渗透的，渗漏的|  Describes something that allows liquids or gases to pass through slowly.  It suggests a gradual and often imperceptible process of leakage or infiltration.| The old roof was seepier than we thought, leading to water damage in the attic.  |近义词：permeable, porous, leaky |反义词：impermeable, airtight
