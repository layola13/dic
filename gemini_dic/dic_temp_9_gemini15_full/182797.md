repaving: /ˌriːˈpeɪvɪŋ/ | v. | 重新铺设；翻修路面 | The act of covering a road or other surface again with paving material.  It implies a complete or extensive replacement of the existing surface, not just minor repairs. |例句：The city is undertaking a major repaving project on Main Street this summer.  今年夏天，市政部门将在主街上进行一项重大的道路重新铺设工程。

The repaving of the highway is expected to be completed by next month.  高速公路的重新铺设预计将在下个月完成。


近义词：resurfacing, relaying, repavement (n.) | 反义词：destruction, demolition

They repave the streets every ten years. = 他们每十年重新铺一次街道。
The council is repaving the road outside my house. = 委员会正在重新铺设我家外面的道路。

现在完成时:
