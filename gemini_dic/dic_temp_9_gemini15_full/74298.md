fractionate:/ˈfrækʃəneɪt/| vt.|分离，分馏|To separate or divide into fractions or parts, especially by distillation.  The process often involves separating components of a mixture based on their different boiling points or other physical properties.|例句：The crude oil was fractionated to obtain gasoline, kerosene, and other products. 原油被分馏以获得汽油、煤油和其他产品。
例句：Scientists fractionated the blood sample to analyze its different components. 科学家们分离了血液样本以分析其不同的成分。|近义词：separate, divide, split, distill|反义词：combine, unite, merge|动词
