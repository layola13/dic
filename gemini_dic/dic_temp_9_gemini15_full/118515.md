mesocephalic:/ˌmɛsoʊsɛˈfælɪk/ | adj. | 中头型的 | Having a head of medium breadth; neither brachycephalic nor dolichocephalic.  This term is used in physical anthropology to classify skulls based on their cephalic index (the ratio of head breadth to head length).  | Example: The skull was classified as mesocephalic, indicating a head of average breadth.  | 近义词: medium-headed | 反义词: brachycephalic (broad-headed), dolichocephalic (long-headed)


词源:

* **meso-**:  From Greek *mesos*, meaning "middle," "intermediate."
* **-cephalic**: From Greek *kephalē*, meaning "head."

因此，mesocephalic字面意思是“中间的头”。


用法说明:

这个词主要用于人类学和医学领域，用于描述头型的分类。  它并不常用在日常会话中。


例句：

1.  Anthropologists measured the cephalic index to determine if the skull was brachycephalic, dolichocephalic, or mesocephalic. 人类学家测量头指数以确定头骨是短头型、长头型还是中头型。

2.  The study found that the population showed a range of cranial shapes, including both brachycephalic and mesocephalic individuals.  这项研究发现，人口显示出一系列颅骨形状，包括短头型和中头型个体。


需要注意的是，mesocephalic的定义可能因研究和测量方法而略有差异，因此在不同文献中可能存在细微差别。
