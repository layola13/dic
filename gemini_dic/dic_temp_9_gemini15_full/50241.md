depended:/dɪˈpendɪd/ | v. |依靠，取决于 | depended is the simple past tense and past participle of the verb "depend." It indicates that a reliance or condition was in effect at a specific point in the past.  |The success of the project depended on the weather.  项目的成功取决于天气。|relied on, rested on, hinged on|independent, self-sufficient|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He depends on his parents for financial support. = 他依靠父母的经济支持。
The outcome depends on several factors. = 结果取决于几个因素。

一般过去时:
The decision depended on the available evidence. = 决定取决于现有证据。
His happiness depended on her love. = 他的幸福取决于她的爱。

现在完成时:
The success of the project has depended on many people's hard work. = 项目的成功取决于许多人的努力工作。
Everything has depended on your timely intervention. = 一切都取决于你及时的干预。

过去完成时:
The outcome had depended on a single vote. = 结果取决于一票。
My decision had depended on what he said. = 我的决定取决于他所说的话。


将来时:
The success of the mission will depend on good weather. = 任务的成功将取决于好天气。
The final result will depend on the judge's decision. = 最终结果将取决于法官的决定。

虚拟语气:
If it depended on me, I would have done it differently. = 如果取决于我，我会做得不一样。
I wish the outcome didn't depend on such unreliable sources. = 我希望结果不依赖于如此不可靠的来源。

被动语态:
The schedule is depended on by many. = 很多人都依赖这个时间表。
The outcome was depended upon by all participants. = 结果为所有参与者所依赖。

疑问句:
Does the success of the plan depend on us? = 计划的成功取决于我们吗？
Does your decision depend on external factors? = 你的决定取决于外部因素吗？

否定句:
The result did not depend on luck. = 结果并不取决于运气。
His success didn't depend on his family background. = 他的成功并不取决于他的家庭背景。
