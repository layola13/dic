noncodified: /ˌnɒnkəʊdɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 未编纂的；不成文的；非法的 | Not officially written down or recorded in a system of laws or rules.  It refers to customs, practices, or laws that exist but are not formally documented. |  The customary law in this region is largely noncodified. 这个地区的风俗法在很大程度上是没有成文法的。 |近义词：uncodified, unwritten, informal | 反义词：codified, written, formal

一般现在时:
The customary law remains largely noncodified. = 风俗法在很大程度上仍然未编纂。
This area's legal system is mostly noncodified. = 这个地区的法律体系大多不成文。


一般过去时:
The rules were noncodified before the new legislation. = 在新的立法之前，这些规则是没有成文的。
Their traditions remained largely noncodified for centuries. = 他们的传统几个世纪以来都基本上没有成文。


现在完成时:
Many aspects of their culture have remained noncodified. = 他们的文化许多方面仍然未编纂。
The tribal laws have not yet been codified; they remain noncodified. = 部落法尚未编纂；它们仍然未编纂。


过去完成时:

The legal system had remained largely noncodified up to that point. = 直到那时，法律制度在很大程度上仍然没有成文。


将来时:
Some traditions will likely remain noncodified for the foreseeable future. = 在可预见的未来，某些传统可能会仍然未编纂。
They plan to codify some of the currently noncodified rules. = 他们计划将一些目前未编纂的规则编纂成文。


虚拟语气:
If the laws were codified, it would be easier to enforce them. = 如果法律被编纂成文，执行起来就会更容易。
It would be better if those unwritten customs were noncodified.  = 如果那些不成文的习俗被编纂成文就好了。


被动语态:
Many aspects of the legal system are kept noncodified intentionally. = 法律体系的许多方面是故意保持未编纂的。
These informal agreements were considered noncodified by the court. = 法院认为这些非正式协议是未编纂的。


疑问句:
Are all the rules and regulations codified, or are some noncodified? = 所有规则和规章都已编纂成文，还是有些未编纂？

否定句:
The legal system is not entirely noncodified; there are some written laws. = 法律体系并非完全未编纂；也有一些成文法。
This is not a noncodified agreement; it is a legally binding contract. = 这不是一份未编纂的协议；这是一份具有法律约束力的合同。
