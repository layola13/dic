fondants:/ˈfɑːndænts/| n.|软糖，糖衣|A fondant is a type of candy made from sugar, water, and often other ingredients such as butter, cream, or flavorings. It has a smooth, creamy texture and is often used as a coating or filling for confections. |Example: The cake was decorated with beautiful fondant flowers.  这款蛋糕装饰着漂亮的糖花。|近义词：candy, confection, sweet|反义词：N/A
