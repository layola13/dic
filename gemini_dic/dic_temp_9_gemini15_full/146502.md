overgeneralise: /ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛnərəlaɪz/ | /ˌəʊvəˈdʒɛnərəlaɪz/ | v. | 过度概括；以偏概全 | To make generalizations that are too broad or that do not apply in all cases.  It often implies drawing conclusions based on limited or insufficient evidence. |


例句：

He overgeneralizes about women based on his experiences with a few. = 他根据与几个女人的经历，对女人做出了过度概括。

Don't overgeneralize from one bad experience; not all restaurants are bad. = 不要因为一次不好的经历就过度概括；并非所有餐馆都不好。


近义词：generalize excessively, oversimplify, stereotype, make sweeping statements


反义词：be specific, be precise, nuance, differentiate


词性: 动词
