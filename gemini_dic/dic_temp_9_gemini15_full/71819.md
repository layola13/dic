fleer:/flɪər/| vt. vi.| 嘲笑；讥笑；冷笑 | To mock or jeer at someone or something, often in a scornful or contemptuous way.  |例句：He fleered at her clumsy attempt to dance. 他嘲笑她笨拙的舞蹈尝试。

例句：The bully fleered at the smaller boy.  那个恶霸嘲笑那个更小的男孩。
