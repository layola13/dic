overcuriously: /ˌoʊvərˈkjʊriəsli/ | adv. | 过分好奇地，过于好奇地 |  Excessively curious; showing too much curiosity. | 例句：He overcuriously peered into the neighbor's window. 他过分好奇地窥视着邻居的窗户。


例句：She overcuriously questioned the witness, jeopardizing the investigation. 她过于好奇地盘问证人，危及了调查。


近义词：inquisitively, pryingly, unduly curiously


反义词：indifferently, apathetically


词性：副词
