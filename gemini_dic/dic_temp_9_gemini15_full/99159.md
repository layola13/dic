interchanger:/ɪnˈtʃeɪndʒər/| n. |交换机；兑换处；交换者|A device or place where things are exchanged, especially currency.  It can also refer to a person who facilitates exchange.|例句：The airport has a convenient currency interchanger. 机场有一个方便的货币兑换处。

例句：He acted as an interchanger between the two warring factions. 他充当了两个交战派系之间的调解人。

近义词：exchanger, converter, mediator

反义词：receiver (in the sense of only receiving, not exchanging)
