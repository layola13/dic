pluma:/ˈpluːmə/|/ˈpluːmɑː/| n. |羽毛；笔；翎毛|A pluma is a feather, especially a large or showy one, or a pen made from a feather.  It can also refer to a plume of smoke or gas.|例句：The bird of paradise has magnificent plumas.  (极乐鸟拥有华丽的羽毛。)

The writer used a quill pluma to write the letter. (作家用羽毛笔写信。)
