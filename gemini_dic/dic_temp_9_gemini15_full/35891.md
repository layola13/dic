coconut:/ˈkoʊkənʌt/ /ˈkəʊkənʌt/| n. |椰子；椰子肉；椰子油|A coconut is a tropical fruit with a hard shell and white flesh.  The fruit's flesh, milk, and oil are all used in various foods and products.|例句：She cracked open the coconut and drank the sweet milk. 她敲开椰子喝了甜椰汁。

例句：We used coconut oil to make the cake. 我们用椰子油做蛋糕。

近义词：palm nut, cocoanut

反义词：无
