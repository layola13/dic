optimise:/ˈɒptɪmaɪz/ /ˈɑːptɪmaɪz/| vt.|优化；使最优|To make the best or most effective use of (a situation, resource, or system).  It involves making something as good or functional as possible.|The company is working to optimise its production processes to reduce costs and increase efficiency. 公司正在努力优化其生产流程，以降低成本并提高效率。|improve, enhance, refine|worsen, impair, deteriorate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We optimise our website regularly. = 我们定期优化我们的网站。
They optimise their marketing strategies. = 他们优化他们的营销策略。

一般过去时:
The engineers optimised the engine design. = 工程师们优化了发动机设计。
She optimised her workout routine. = 她优化了她的锻炼计划。

现在完成时:
We have optimised our supply chain. = 我们已经优化了我们的供应链。
They have optimised their code for better performance. = 他们已经优化了他们的代码以获得更好的性能。

过去完成时:
They had optimised the software before the launch. = 他们在发布之前已经优化了软件。
The team had optimised the process to increase efficiency. = 团队已经优化了流程以提高效率。

将来时:
We will optimise the system next week. = 我们下周将优化系统。
They will optimise their algorithms for faster processing. = 他们将优化他们的算法以加快处理速度。

虚拟语气:
If we optimised the workflow, we would save time. = 如果我们优化工作流程，我们将节省时间。
It would be better if they optimised their resources. = 如果他们优化资源会更好。

被动语态:
The system is being optimised for mobile devices. = 该系统正在被优化以适应移动设备。
The algorithm was optimised for speed. = 该算法被优化以提高速度。

疑问句:
Have you optimised your database? = 你优化了你的数据库吗？
How can we optimise the energy consumption? = 我们如何优化能源消耗？

否定句:
They did not optimise the design. = 他们没有优化设计。
We haven't optimised the process yet. = 我们还没有优化这个流程。


PHRASE->
