interferons:/ˌɪntərˈfɪərɒnz/ | n. | 干扰素 |  Glycoproteins produced by the body's cells in response to viral infection or other immune stimuli; they have antiviral, antitumor, and immunomodulatory activities.  | Example: Interferons are crucial components of the innate immune system, helping to combat viral infections. 干扰素是先天免疫系统的重要组成部分，有助于对抗病毒感染。  | 近义词：cytokines (a broader category) | 反义词：None directly applicable.  | 名词
