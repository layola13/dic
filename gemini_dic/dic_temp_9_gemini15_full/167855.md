primped:/prɪmpt/| vt.|打扮；梳妆打扮；|To make oneself or someone else look neat and tidy, especially in preparation for a special occasion.|例句：She spent hours primping before the party. 她花了几个小时在派对前打扮自己。|例句：He primped his hair and adjusted his tie. 他打扮了自己的头发并整理了一下领带。|近义词：groom, preen, doll up,  fix up|反义词：neglect, disregard
