self-assessments:/ˌsɛlf əˈsɛsmənts/ | n. | 自我评估，自我评价 | The act of judging one's own abilities, performance, or character.  It often involves reflecting on one's strengths and weaknesses and identifying areas for improvement. | Example:  The teacher encouraged self-assessments to help students understand their learning process.  老师鼓励学生进行自我评估，以帮助他们了解自己的学习过程。


Example:  Regular self-assessments are crucial for professional development. 定期进行自我评估对于职业发展至关重要。| 近义词：self-evaluation, self-appraisal, introspection | 反义词：external evaluation, peer review |  词性：名词
