circumlocution:/ˌsɜːrkəmloˈkjuːʃən/ | n. | 婉言；迂回说法；绕圈子说话 | The act or an instance of talking around a subject; indirect or evasive speech. It often involves using more words than necessary to express an idea, making the communication less clear and efficient. |  He avoided a direct answer, resorting to circumlocution. 他避免直接回答，采取了婉转的表达方式。| periphrasis, evasiveness, indirectness | directness, frankness | 名词
