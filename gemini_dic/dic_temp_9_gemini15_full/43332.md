crampedness:/ˈkræmptnəs/ | n. |局促；狭窄；拥挤 |The feeling or state of being cramped; a lack of space.  It often refers to a feeling of confinement or restriction, not just physical lack of space.  |例句：The crampedness of the apartment made it difficult to move around freely.  公寓的狭窄使得自由活动变得困难。
The crampedness of the bus made the journey uncomfortable.  公共汽车的拥挤使旅程变得不舒服。|近义词：confinement, tightness, constriction, narrowness |反义词：spaciousness, roominess, breadth
