paging:/ˈpeɪdʒɪŋ/| n.|寻呼，分页，翻页|The act of calling someone via a paging system or the process of displaying information page by page.|例句：The nurse did paging for the doctor. 护士给医生打了寻呼。|例句：The software supports paging to handle large datasets. 该软件支持分页以处理大型数据集。|近义词：calling, pagination, flipping|反义词：continuous display
