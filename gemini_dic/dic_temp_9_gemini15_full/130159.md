non-resistant: /ˌnɒn rɪˈzɪstənt/ | adj. | 不抵抗的，顺从的 | Not offering resistance; yielding passively.  |  The non-resistant policy of the government led to the occupation of their territory. 政府的不抵抗政策导致了他们领土的沦陷。 |  passive, submissive, compliant | resistant, defiant, rebellious |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is non-resistant to authority. 他对权威不抵抗。
The material is non-resistant to water.  这种材料不防水。


一般过去时:
The soldiers were non-resistant during the invasion.  入侵期间士兵们没有抵抗。
The plant was non-resistant to the disease.  这种植物对这种疾病没有抵抗力。


现在完成时:
They have been non-resistant to the pressure. 他们一直屈服于压力。
The community has remained non-resistant to change.  这个社区一直顺从变化。


过去完成时:
The people had been non-resistant for many years before the rebellion.  在起义之前，人民多年来一直顺从。
The crop had been non-resistant to the pest for generations.  这种作物几代以来一直对害虫没有抵抗力。


将来时:
The army will likely be non-resistant to the superior force.  军队很可能无法抵抗强大的力量。
The new building materials will be non-resistant to fire. 新建材将不耐火。


虚拟语气:
If he were non-resistant, he would have avoided the conflict. 如果他不抵抗，他就能避免冲突。
If the city had been non-resistant, the siege would have ended sooner. 如果城市不抵抗，围城就会更快结束。


被动语态:
Their non-resistant attitude was exploited by the invaders.  他们的顺从态度被侵略者利用了。
The weakness of the non-resistant structure was exposed by the storm.  不抗力的结构弱点被风暴暴露了。


疑问句:
Is the population non-resistant to the new policies?  民众对新政策是否顺从？
Are these plants non-resistant to frost?  这些植物不抗霜冻吗？


否定句:
The people were not non-resistant; they fought back bravely.  人民并没有顺从；他们勇敢地反击了。
The building is not non-resistant to earthquakes; it's designed to withstand them.  这座建筑物不抗震；它是设计成能够承受地震的。
