overelaborations:/ˌoʊvərɪˌlæbəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度阐述；过于详细的解释 |  The essay suffered from overelaborations, obscuring its central argument.  |近义词：overexplanations, excessive detail, wordiness |反义词：brevity, conciseness, succinctness


英文解析：The plural noun "overelaborations" refers to instances of excessive detail or explanation, often to the point of being unclear or distracting from the main point.  It implies that something has been explained or described in far more detail than necessary.


例句：

His presentation was filled with overelaborations, making it difficult to follow the key takeaways.  他的演讲充满了冗长的解释，使得难以抓住要点。

The report was criticized for its overelaborations; the core findings were lost in a sea of unnecessary detail. 报告因其过于详细的解释而受到批评；核心发现淹没在一堆不必要的细节中。


词源：

* **over-**: prefix indicating excess or too much.
* **elaborate**:  verb meaning to explain or describe something in detail.  This word has roots in Late Latin *elaboratus*, past participle of *elaborare*, meaning "to work out, finish, perfect," from *e-* (out) + *laborare* (to work).
