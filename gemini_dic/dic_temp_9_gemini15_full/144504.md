outtonguing: /ˌaʊtˈtʌŋɪŋ/ | v. |  伸出舌头超过；胜过 |  To extend the tongue beyond; to surpass in length of tongue.  This word is relatively rare and primarily used in a descriptive, often figurative, sense. It suggests a competitive or surpassing aspect related to speech or expression, not necessarily literally sticking out one's tongue. | 例句：The debate became heated, with each speaker seemingly outtonguing the other. = 辩论变得激烈，每位发言者似乎都在试图压倒对方。  The young orator outtongued his experienced opponent. = 年轻的演说家以其雄辩胜过了经验丰富的对手。 | 近义词：outspeak, outtalk, surpass, excel | 反义词：be outdone, be surpassed
