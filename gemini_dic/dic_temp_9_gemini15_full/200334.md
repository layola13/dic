skunk:/skʌŋk/| n.|臭鼬|A skunk is a small, black and white mammal known for its ability to spray a foul-smelling liquid as a defense mechanism.  They are nocturnal and primarily insectivores.|Example: The dog ran away when it encountered a skunk.|近义词：polecat|反义词：无
