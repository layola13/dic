latecomers:/ˈleɪtˌkʌmər/| n.|迟到者，后到者|People who arrive after the expected or scheduled time.  They are often characterized by their tardiness.|例句：The latecomers missed the beginning of the concert. 迟到者错过了音乐会的开始。

例句：Many latecomers had to stand at the back of the hall. 许多迟到者不得不站在大厅的后面。


近义词：tardy arrivals, stragglers


反义词：early birds, prompt arrivals


词性:Noun
