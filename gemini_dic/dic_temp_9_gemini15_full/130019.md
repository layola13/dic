non-operational:/ˌnɒnˌɒpəˈreɪʃənəl/|/ˌnɑːnˌɑːpəˈreɪʃənəl/| adj. |不运行的；非运作的；闲置的 |Not in operation; not functioning.  This term often refers to equipment, systems, or personnel that are not currently active or available for use.  |例句：The factory was non-operational due to the power outage. =由于停电，工厂无法运作。

The server became non-operational after the software update. = 软件更新后，服务器停止运行。
