includ:/ɪnˈkluːd/ | vt. | 包括，包含 | To contain or comprise as part of a whole; to keep within limits.  | Example: The price includes tax. (价格包含税款。)  The tour includes a visit to the museum. (旅游包括参观博物馆。) |近义词：contain, comprise, encompass | 反义词：exclude


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The package includes a free gift. = 这个包裹包含一份免费礼物。
The menu includes several vegetarian options. = 菜单包括几种素食选择。


一般过去时:
The report included all the necessary data. = 报告包含所有必要的数据。
The meeting included a discussion of future plans. = 会议包括对未来计划的讨论。


现在完成时:
The exhibition has included works by many famous artists. = 此展览已包含许多著名艺术家的作品。
She has included all the relevant information in her presentation. = 她已在她的演示文稿中包含所有相关信息。


过去完成时:
They had included a map in the package before sending it. = 他们在寄送包裹之前已在里面包含了一张地图。
We had included all the necessary documents in the application. = 我们已在申请中包含所有必要的文档。


将来时:
The next update will include several new features. = 下一个更新将包含几个新功能。
The conference will include a keynote speech by a renowned expert. = 会议将包括一位知名专家的主题演讲。


虚拟语气:
If the project included more funding, we could achieve more. = 如果项目包含更多资金，我们可以取得更多成就。
I wish the proposal included a clearer timeline. = 我希望提案包含更清晰的时间表。


被动语态:
Many different perspectives were included in the study. = 研究中包含了许多不同的观点。
Tax is included in the final price. = 税款包含在最终价格中。


疑问句:
Does the price include shipping? = 价格包含运费吗？
What does the package include? = 包裹里包含什么？


否定句:
The contract does not include any clauses about liability. = 合同不包含任何关于责任的条款。
The original plan did not include a backup strategy. = 原计划不包含备用策略。
