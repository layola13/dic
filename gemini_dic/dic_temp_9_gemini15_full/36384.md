coinstantaneous: /ˌkoʊɪnˈsteɪniəs/ | adj. |同时发生的，同时存在的 | Happening or existing at the same time.  The word suggests a close and often inseparable connection between the events or things that are simultaneous. | Example: The two events were coinstantaneous, making it difficult to determine causality. 两个事件同时发生，难以确定因果关系。|近义词：simultaneous, concurrent, coincident |反义词：separate, distinct, successive
