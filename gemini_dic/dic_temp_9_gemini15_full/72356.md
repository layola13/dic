flowerlessness: /ˈflaʊərləsnəs/ | n. | 无花；不长花 | The absence of flowers; the state of not having flowers.  It often refers to a plant's inability to produce flowers, possibly due to environmental factors or genetic traits. | Example: The prolonged drought resulted in the flowerlessness of many plants in the region.  长期干旱导致该地区许多植物不開花。 | 近义词：sterility (in the context of plants), barrenness | 反义词：floweriness, blossoming
