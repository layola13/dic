registrant:/ˈrɛdʒɪstrənt/| n. |注册人，登记人|A person who has registered or enrolled in something.  |例句：The registrants for the conference are expected to arrive by Friday.  会议的注册人员预计将在周五之前到达。

例句：All registrants will receive a confirmation email. 所有注册者都将收到确认邮件。


近义词：enrollee, subscriber, applicant|反义词：None directly applicable|词性：名词
