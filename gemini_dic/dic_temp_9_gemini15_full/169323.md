This word is less common and may be considered informal. |Example:  His promotabilities were high after landing the key account.  他的晋升可能性很高，因为他拿下了这个关键客户。
The company assessed the promotabilities of its employees. 公司评估了其员工的晋升可能性。|近义词：promotion prospects, chances of advancement, career advancement potential|反义词：lack of opportunity, stagnation, dead-end job
