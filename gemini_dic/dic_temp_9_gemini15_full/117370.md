mediating:/ˈmiːdieɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 调解；斡旋；促成 |  To intervene between parties to reconcile differences.  |例句：The judge is mediating the dispute between the two neighbors.  法官正在调解这两个邻居之间的纠纷。

例句：The UN is mediating peace talks between the warring factions.  联合国正在调解交战各派之间的和平谈判。


近义词：arbitrating, conciliating, interceding, negotiating | 反义词：aggravating, exacerbating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mediates disputes between employees. = 他调解员工之间的纠纷。
She mediates conflicts in her community. = 她调解社区里的冲突。


一般过去时:
The diplomat mediated the peace treaty. = 外交官促成了和平条约。
He mediated a deal between the two companies. = 他促成了两家公司之间的交易。


现在完成时:
She has mediated numerous conflicts this year. = 她今年已经调解了许多冲突。
They have mediated a settlement in the lawsuit. = 他们已经调解了这场诉讼的和解。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, he had mediated several disagreements. = 会议前，他已经调解了几次分歧。
The mediator had mediated several disputes before this one. = 在这次调解之前，调解员已经调解过几次纠纷。


将来时:
The council will mediate the upcoming election dispute. = 理事会将调解即将到来的选举纠纷。
She will mediate the conflict between the two families. = 她将调解这两个家庭之间的冲突。


虚拟语气:
If he mediated the conflict, the situation would improve. = 如果他调解了冲突，情况就会好转。
I wish he had mediated the argument before it escalated. = 我希望他在争论升级之前就调解了。


被动语态:
The dispute was mediated by a neutral party. = 纠纷由中立方调解。
The agreement was mediated through intensive negotiations. = 协议是通过密集谈判促成的。


疑问句:
Who is mediating the negotiations? = 谁在调解谈判？
Will someone mediate the disagreement? = 会有人调解分歧吗？


否定句:
He did not mediate the conflict. = 他没有调解冲突。
They refused to mediate the dispute. = 他们拒绝调解纠纷。


PHRASE->
mediate a dispute = 调解纠纷
mediate a conflict = 调解冲突
mediate an agreement = 促成协议
mediate between parties = 在双方之间调解
mediate a settlement = 调解和解
