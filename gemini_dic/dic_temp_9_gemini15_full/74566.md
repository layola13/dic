fraught:/frɔːt/| adj.|充满…的；焦虑不安的；充满危险的 | Filled with or likely to result in (something undesirable);  Characterized by or full of (something specified, typically something undesirable).|例句：Their marriage was fraught with difficulties. 他们的婚姻充满了困难。
The atmosphere was fraught with tension.  气氛紧张。|近义词：burdened, laden, filled, rife, charged |反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil, serene


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The situation is fraught with danger. = 情况充满了危险。
The air is fraught with tension. = 空气中充满了紧张气氛。


一般过去时:
The journey was fraught with peril. = 这段旅程充满了危险。
The meeting was fraught with disagreement. = 会议充满了分歧。


现在完成时:
The project has been fraught with delays. = 这个项目一直被延误所困扰。
Their relationship has been fraught with conflict. = 他们的关系一直充满冲突。


过去完成时:
The negotiations had been fraught with difficulty before the breakthrough. = 在取得突破之前，谈判充满了困难。
The investigation had been fraught with obstacles. = 调查充满了障碍。


将来时:
The mission will be fraught with risk. = 这项任务将充满风险。
The upcoming election will be fraught with uncertainty. = 即将到来的选举将充满不确定性。


虚拟语气:
If the mission were fraught with danger, they would still proceed. = 即使任务充满危险，他们仍然会继续进行。
If the negotiations had been fraught with fewer problems, a deal would have been reached sooner. = 如果谈判中问题少一些，协议就会早些达成。


被动语态:
The process was fraught with complications. = 这个过程充满了复杂的问题。
The atmosphere was found to be fraught with suspicion. = 发现气氛充满了猜疑。


疑问句:
Is the situation fraught with peril? = 情况充满危险吗？
Is the path ahead fraught with difficulties? = 前方的道路充满困难吗？


否定句:
The meeting wasn't fraught with tension. = 会议并不紧张。
The journey wasn't fraught with problems. = 这段旅程并没有问题。
