camaraderie:/ˌkæməˈrɑːdəri/ | n. |同志情谊；友情；团结精神 | A feeling of friendship and good fellowship, especially among people who share a common interest or experience.  |The team showed great camaraderie, supporting each other through thick and thin.  这个团队展现了极高的团队精神，互相扶持，共同克服困难。|friendship, companionship, solidarity | antagonism, hostility, alienation| 名词

词源:

The word "camaraderie" comes from the French word "camarade," meaning "comrade."  This, in turn, is derived from the Spanish word "camarada," ultimately having roots in the Latin word "camera," meaning "chamber" or "room," referring to the shared living space of soldiers.
