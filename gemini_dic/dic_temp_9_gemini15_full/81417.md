grasswards:/ˈɡræsˌwɔːrdz/| n.|草地；牧场|Areas of land covered with grass, typically used for grazing.  This word is relatively uncommon and often used in a literary or poetic context to evoke a sense of open space and pastoral life.|例句：The sheep grazed contentedly in the sun-drenched grasswards.  绵羊在阳光普照的草地上心满意足地吃草。  The wind whispered secrets through the tall grasses of the vast grasswards. 风在广阔草地的高草丛中低语着秘密。|近义词：pasture, meadow, grassland|反义词：city, urban area
