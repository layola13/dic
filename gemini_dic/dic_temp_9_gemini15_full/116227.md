masoretic:/ˌmæsəˈrɛtɪk/| adj.|马所拉的；马所拉文本的 | Relating to the Masoretes, their system of vocalization and punctuation of the Hebrew Bible, or the text produced by them.  The Masoretic Text is considered the most reliable extant Hebrew text of the Hebrew Bible. |The Masoretic Text is the standard for most modern translations of the Hebrew Bible.  马所拉文本是大多数现代希伯来圣经译本的标准。|近义词：canonical (in the context of the Hebrew Bible) |反义词：None specifically, but terms suggesting less reliable or earlier versions could be considered opposites in context.


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations or other grammatical inflections like "what".  Its usage is descriptive.

Example 1:  The Masoretic text shows a different reading here. 马所拉文本在这里显示不同的解读。
Example 2: Scholars debate the accuracy of the Masoretic vocalization in this passage. 学者们争论这段经文中马所拉语音的准确性。
