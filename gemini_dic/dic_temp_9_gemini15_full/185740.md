revibration:/ˌriːvaɪˈbreɪʃən/| n. |再振动，再颤动 |The act or process of vibrating again; a second or repeated vibration.  This word is less common and often used in specialized contexts like physics or acoustics, describing a repeated or secondary vibration after an initial one.
