escalatory: /ˈɛskəleɪtəri/ | adj. | 升级的；逐步升级的 |  Increasing in intensity, seriousness, or extent.  Describes a process or situation that is becoming more intense, serious, or widespread. |

例句：

The escalatory rhetoric between the two countries is worrying.  = 两国之间升级的言辞令人担忧。

The escalatory nature of the conflict led to increased casualties. = 冲突的升级性质导致了伤亡人数的增加。


近义词：intensifying, escalating, heightening, mounting, growing

反义词：de-escalating, decreasing, diminishing, subsiding


词源:

Derived from the verb "escalate," which ultimately comes from the French word "escalier" meaning "staircase" or "ladder." The metaphorical sense of "gradually increasing" developed from the image of climbing a staircase or ladder.
