cinephile:/ˈsɪnɪfaɪl/| n. |电影爱好者，影迷 |A cinephile is a person who loves and is knowledgeable about films.  They often watch a wide variety of films, from classic to contemporary, and are interested in the technical and artistic aspects of filmmaking. |例句：He's a true cinephile; he attends film festivals and watches independent films religiously. 他是个真正的影迷；他虔诚地参加电影节并观看独立电影。

例句：Many cinephiles consider "Citizen Kane" to be one of the greatest films ever made. 许多影迷认为《公民凯恩》是有史以来最伟大的电影之一。

近义词：film buff, movie enthusiast, film lover |反义词：None  |词性：名词

词源:
The word "cinephile" is a blend of "cine-" (from "cinema," ultimately from Greek kinein, "to move") and "-phile" (from Greek philos, "loving").  It literally means "lover of cinema."
