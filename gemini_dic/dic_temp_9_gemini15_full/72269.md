flosser:/ˈflɒsər/ | n. |牙线使用者；牙线 | A person who uses dental floss to clean their teeth.  A tool used to clean between teeth. |例句：She's a meticulous flosser, making sure to clean every space between her teeth.  她是一个细心的牙线使用者，确保清洁牙齿之间的每一个缝隙。  He bought a new flosser with a built-in handle. 他买了一个带内置手柄的新牙线器。|近义词：dental floss user, interdental cleaner |反义词：N/A
