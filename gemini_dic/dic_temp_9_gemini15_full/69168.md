fax's:/fæksɪz/| n. |传真的复数形式|The plural form of "fax," referring to multiple faxes.  It indicates more than one transmitted document via facsimile.|Example: I received several faxes this morning. 我今天早上收到了几份传真。|近义词: facsimiles |反义词: None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The office sends many faxes daily. = 办公室每天发送许多传真。
He receives faxes from clients regularly. = 他定期收到客户的传真。


一般过去时:
She sent faxes to all the branches yesterday. = 她昨天给所有分公司发了传真。
They received important faxes last week. = 他们上周收到了重要的传真。


现在完成时:
I have sent all the necessary faxes. = 我已经发送了所有必要的传真。
They have received numerous faxes concerning the project. = 他们收到了许多关于该项目的传真。


过去完成时:
We had already sent the faxes before the meeting. = 我们在会议前已经发送了传真。
She had received several faxes from him before she called. = 在她打电话之前，她已经收到了他几份传真。


将来时:
I will send the faxes tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上会发送传真。
He will receive the faxes by the end of the day. = 他今天结束之前会收到传真。


虚拟语气:
If I had a fax machine, I would send the faxes immediately. = 如果我有传真机，我会立即发送传真。
I wish I had sent the faxes earlier. = 我希望我早点发送了传真。


被动语态:
The faxes were sent yesterday afternoon. = 传真是在昨天下午发送的。
The faxes are being processed now. = 传真正在处理中。


疑问句:
Have you sent the faxes? = 你发送传真了吗？
Did they receive all the faxes? = 他们收到所有传真了吗？


否定句:
I didn't send the faxes. = 我没有发送传真。
They haven't received the faxes yet. = 他们还没有收到传真。


PHRASE->
send faxes = 发送传真
receive faxes = 收到传真
process faxes = 处理传真
reply to faxes = 回复传真
forward faxes = 转发传真
