outweigh:/ˌaʊtˈweɪ/| vt.|超过，胜过，比…重要|To exceed in weight, value, or importance.|The benefits of the project outweigh the costs.  这个项目的益处超过了成本。|The advantages outweigh the disadvantages. 优点超过缺点。|近义词：exceed, surpass, preponderate|反义词：underweigh


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The advantages outweigh the disadvantages. = 优点超过缺点。
His happiness outweighs his sorrows. = 他的快乐胜过他的悲伤。


一般过去时:
The benefits of the program outweighed its costs. = 该项目的益处超过了它的成本。
Her desire for freedom outweighed her fear of the unknown. = 她对自由的渴望超过了她对未知的恐惧。


现在完成时:
The positive aspects have outweighed the negative ones. = 积极方面已经超过了消极方面。
His contributions have outweighed those of his colleagues. = 他的贡献超过了他的同事。


过去完成时:
The potential risks had outweighed the potential rewards. = 潜在的风险已经超过了潜在的回报。
By then, the evidence had outweighed the initial suspicions. = 到那时，证据已经超过了最初的怀疑。


将来时:
The benefits will outweigh the initial investment. = 收益将超过最初的投资。
The long-term gains will outweigh the short-term losses. = 长期收益将超过短期损失。


虚拟语气:
If the benefits outweighed the risks, we would proceed. = 如果好处超过风险，我们将继续进行。
I wish the positive aspects outweighed the negative ones. = 我希望积极方面超过消极方面。


被动语态:
The risks were outweighed by the potential rewards. = 风险被潜在的回报超过了。
The concerns were outweighed by the excitement of the project. = 对项目的担忧被兴奋感所超过。


疑问句:
Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages in this case? = 在这种情况下，优点是否超过缺点？
Does the potential profit outweigh the risk involved? = 潜在的利润是否超过了所涉及的风险？


否定句:
The negative aspects did not outweigh the positive ones. = 消极方面并没有超过积极方面。
The risks involved did not outweigh the potential benefits. = 所涉及的风险并没有超过潜在的益处。


PHRASE->
outweigh the risks = 超过风险
outweigh the benefits = 超过益处
outweigh the importance = 超过重要性
outweigh the drawbacks = 超过缺点
outweigh the consequences = 超过后果
