outdwelling:/ˈaʊtˌdwelɪŋ/ | n. | 外宅，附属建筑 | A secondary building located on the same property as a main dwelling.  It is often smaller and used for purposes other than primary living, such as a guest house, workshop, or studio. | Example: The outdwelling served as a guesthouse for visiting relatives.  附属建筑用作招待来访亲戚的宾馆。

The outdwelling was converted into a home office.  附属建筑被改造成家庭办公室。


近义词：annex, dependency, addition, outbuilding


反义词：main house, primary dwelling


词性：名词
