exhumers:/ɪɡˈzjuːmɚz/| n. |掘尸者；挖掘者|Those who exhume bodies, often for forensic or investigative purposes.  Exhumers carefully remove remains from burial sites, ensuring the process is respectful and documented.|The exhumers worked diligently to recover the remains. 掘尸者勤奋地工作以恢复遗体。 The team of exhumers carefully documented each step of the process. 掘尸者的团队仔细记录了过程的每个步骤。|grave robbers, body retrievers|embalmers(反义词，并非完全对立，而是指处理遗体不同阶段的人)|名词
