focusses:/ˈfoʊkəsɪz/ | v. | 集中；聚焦；专注 | To direct one's attention or efforts toward a particular object or activity.  | He focusses on his studies. 他专注于他的学习。  She focusses the camera on the subject. 她将相机镜头对准拍摄对象。 | concentrate, center, zero in on | diffuse, scatter, distract | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He focusses on his work every day. = 他每天都专注于他的工作。
She focusses her energy on her career. = 她把精力集中在她的事业上。


一般过去时:
He focussed on the problem and found a solution. = 他专注于这个问题并找到了解决办法。
She focussed her attention on the speaker. = 她把注意力集中在演讲者身上。


现在完成时:
He has focussed on this project for months. = 他几个月来一直专注于这个项目。
She has focussed her efforts on improving her skills. = 她一直致力于提高自己的技能。


过去完成时:
He had focussed on his studies before the exam. = 他在考试前一直专注于他的学习。
She had focussed her energy on the task before she got interrupted. = 在被打扰之前，她一直把精力集中在任务上。


将来时:
He will focus on his new business venture. = 他将专注于他的新商业投资。
She will focus her attention on the details of the contract. = 她将把注意力集中在合同的细节上。


虚拟语气:
If he focussed more, he would succeed. = 如果他更专注，他就会成功。
I wish she had focussed on her studies instead of partying. = 我希望她专注于学习而不是参加派对。


被动语态:
The camera was focussed on the main character. = 相机对准了主角。
Attention was focussed on the crisis. = 注意力集中在危机上。


疑问句:
What are you focussing on right now? = 你现在专注于什么？
Where is the camera focussed? = 相机对准哪里？


否定句:
He doesn't focus on details. = 他不关注细节。
She didn't focus on the instructions. = 她没有关注说明。


PHRASE->
focus on = 集中于
bring into focus = 使清晰
in focus = 清晰的
out of focus = 模糊的
focus group = 焦点小组
