catalog:/ˈkætəlɒɡ/ /ˈkætəlɔːɡ/| n. |目录，产品目录，商品目录|A catalog is a complete list of items, typically with descriptions and prices, often used for marketing or reference.  It can be a physical book or a digital document.|例句：The department store's catalog featured their new fall collection.  这家百货公司的目录展示了他们新的秋季系列。|例句：I ordered the new computer directly from the manufacturer's online catalog. 我直接从制造商的在线目录订购了新电脑。|近义词：list, inventory, index, register |反义词：无
