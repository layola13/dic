reenthronement:/ˌriːɪnˈθroʊnmənt/| n.|复位；重新登基|The act of restoring someone to a throne or position of power.  It implies a previous removal or dethronement followed by a return to power.|例句：The reenthronement of the king was met with jubilation throughout the land. 国王的复位在全国受到了热烈庆祝。

The reenthronement ceremony was a lavish affair, attended by dignitaries from across the globe.  重新登基仪式盛大隆重，来自世界各地的显要人物都出席了。|近义词：reinstatement, restoration, enthronement|反义词：dethronement, deposition, removal
