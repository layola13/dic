happiest: /ˈhæpiɪst/ | adj. | 最快乐的，最幸福的 |  The superlative form of "happy," indicating the greatest degree of happiness or joy. It describes a state of intense contentment, joy, or pleasure. | 例句：She had the happiest childhood imaginable. 她拥有可以想象到的最快乐的童年。

That's the happiest day of my life. 那是我的生命中最快乐的一天。

近义词：most joyful, most blissful, most delighted | 反义词：saddest, most miserable, most unhappy | 词性：形容词
