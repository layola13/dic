mall:/mɔːl/| n.|购物中心，商场|A large shopping center, usually with a covered walkway.|例句：We spent the afternoon at the mall. 我们在购物中心待了一下午。|近义词：shopping center, plaza|反义词: None

USAGE->

The mall opens at 10 am. = 购物中心上午10点开门。
This mall has a wide variety of stores. = 这个购物中心有各种各样的商店。

I went to the mall yesterday. = 我昨天去了购物中心。
