lemme:/ˈlɛmiː/| pron.|让我，让我来|Let me is a colloquial contraction of "let me". It's an informal way of saying "allow me" or "permit me" to do something.  It's primarily used in spoken English and informal writing.|例句：Lemme help you with that.  = 让我来帮你。

Lemme see what I can do. = 让我看看我能做什么。|近义词：let me, allow me, permit me|反义词:  None (it's not a descriptive word with a direct opposite).|pronoun
