pepin:/ˈpɛpɪn/| n. |果核；种子；核心；(尤指)苹果核 |A small seed or core, especially of an apple or similar fruit.  The word often refers to the core of a fruit containing seeds. |例句：He carefully removed the pepin from the apple before eating it. 他在吃苹果之前小心地去掉了果核。|例句：The apple's pepin was hard and woody. 苹果的果核又硬又木质。|近义词：core, seed, kernel |反义词：无
