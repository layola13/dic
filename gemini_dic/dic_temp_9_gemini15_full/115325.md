manning-up: /ˈmænɪŋ ʌp/ | v. | 变得男子汉；表现出男子气概 |  To behave in a way considered typical of a man, especially by showing courage and accepting responsibility.  This phrase often implies overcoming fear or weakness. | 例句：He needs to man up and face his problems. 他需要勇敢地面对自己的问题。 He finally manned up and apologized for his mistake. 他最终勇敢地为自己的错误道歉了。 | 近义词：growing up, toughening up, becoming a man | 反义词：being childish, shirking responsibility, being cowardly | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mans up to his responsibilities. = 他勇敢地承担起责任。
She mans up and confronts the bully. = 她勇敢地面对那个恶霸。

一般过去时:
He manned up and confessed his feelings. = 他勇敢地承认了自己的感情。
She manned up and spoke her mind at the meeting. = 她在会议上勇敢地表达了自己的意见。

现在完成时:
He has manned up and started taking care of himself. = 他已经变得成熟起来，开始照顾自己了。
She has manned up and overcome her fear of public speaking. = 她已经变得勇敢起来，克服了公开演讲的恐惧。

过去完成时:
He had manned up and faced the challenge before. = 他之前就曾勇敢地面对过挑战。
She had manned up and finished the project on time. = 她之前就已勇敢地按时完成了项目。

将来时:
He will man up and ask her out. = 他将会鼓起勇气约她出去。
She will man up and stand up for what she believes in. = 她将会勇敢地为自己的信仰而站出来。

虚拟语气:
If he manned up, he would have a better chance. = 如果他勇敢一点，他会更有机会。
If she had manned up sooner, things would have been different. = 如果她早点勇敢起来，情况就会不一样了。

被动语态: (This phrase is rarely used passively)  The situation needed to be manned up to. =  这种情况需要有人勇敢面对。(较为生硬的表达)

疑问句:
Will he man up and admit his mistake? = 他会勇敢地承认错误吗？
Is she going to man up and fight for her rights? = 她会勇敢地为自己的权利而战吗？

否定句:
He didn't man up and run away. = 他没有表现出男子气概，而是逃跑了。
She didn't man up and tell the truth. = 她没有勇敢地说出真相。


PHRASE->
man up and face it = 勇敢面对
man up and deal with it = 勇敢处理
man up and own it = 勇敢承担责任
man up and do it = 勇敢去做
man up or shut up = 要么勇敢点，要么闭嘴
