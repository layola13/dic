encloses:/ɪnˈkloʊzɪz/| vt.|包围；包含；附上；装入|To surround or shut in on all sides; to contain within; to include as part of something; to send something in an envelope or package.|例句：The fence encloses the garden.  围栏围住了花园。

例句：The letter enclosed a photograph. 信中附带一张照片。


近义词：surround, encompass, contain, include|反义词：open, expose, exclude|动词
