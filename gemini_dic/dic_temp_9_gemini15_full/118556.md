mesophyllum:/ˌmɛzoʊˈfɪləm/| n. |中生藻类|A type of calcareous red algae that forms a significant component of many shallow-water marine ecosystems.  These algae are characterized by their encrusting or branching growth forms and their ability to deposit calcium carbonate in their tissues, contributing to reef formation. |Example:  Studies have shown that mesophyllum species are sensitive to changes in water temperature and acidity.  研究表明，中生藻类对水温酸度的变化很敏感。|近义词：coralline algae (珊瑚藻)|反义词：N/A
