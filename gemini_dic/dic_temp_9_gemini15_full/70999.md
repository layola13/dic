firewoods:/ˈfaɪərwʊdz/ | n. | 柴火，木柴 |  Pieces of wood prepared for burning as fuel.  Firewood is essential for keeping a fire going, especially in fireplaces or wood-burning stoves. |例句：We gathered firewood for the campfire. 我们收集柴火准备生营火。

例句：The fireplace was stacked high with firewood. 壁炉里堆满了柴火。

近义词：fuelwood, kindling, logs

反义词：None (no direct antonym, but opposites could be electricity, gas etc. as alternative fuels)

词性：名词
