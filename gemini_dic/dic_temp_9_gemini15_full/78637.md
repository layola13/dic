gibor:/ˈɡɪbɔːr/| n. |吉博尔（一种货币单位）|Gibor is an acronym for "Government of Israel Bond Rate," representing the average rate on government bonds in Israel. It's used as a benchmark interest rate for various financial instruments.  |The bank uses the GIBOR rate to calculate interest on loans.  银行使用吉博尔利率来计算贷款利息。|benchmark interest rate, prime rate |  |名词
