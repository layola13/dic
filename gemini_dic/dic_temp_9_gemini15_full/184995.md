retender:/riːˈtɛndər/| v.|再次招标；重新投标|To submit a bid or proposal again, often after an initial bid has been rejected or deemed unsatisfactory.  It implies a renewed attempt to secure a contract or opportunity.|例句：The company decided to retender the project after receiving unsatisfactory bids.  公司在收到令人不满意的投标后决定重新招标该项目。


例句：The government decided to retender the contract due to concerns about the original bidder's qualifications. 政府由于担心最初投标人的资质问题而决定重新招标合同。


近义词：rebid, resubmit, re-offer|反义词：award, accept


词性:动词
