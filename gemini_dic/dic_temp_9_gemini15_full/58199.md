drubbing:/ˈdrʌbɪŋ/| n. v.|痛打；惨败；严厉批评 | a severe beating or defeat; a harsh criticism |例句：The team suffered a drubbing in the final game.  球队在决赛中惨败。 |近义词：thrashing, beating, defeat, trouncing |反义词：victory, win, triumph |名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often gives his students a drubbing for their mistakes. 他经常因为学生的错误而严厉批评他们。
The coach is giving the team a drubbing for their poor performance. 教练正在严厉批评球队糟糕的表现。


一般过去时:

The opposition party suffered a drubbing in the recent election. 反对党在最近的选举中惨败。

虚拟语气:

疑问句:
