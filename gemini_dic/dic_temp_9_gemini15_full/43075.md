cowpokes:/ˈkaʊpoʊks/| n. |牛仔，牧童|A cowpoke is a cowboy, especially one who is considered unskilled or inexperienced.  The term often carries a slightly humorous or derogatory connotation.|例句：Those cowpokes were always getting into trouble. 那些牛仔总是惹麻烦。|例句：He called himself a cowpoke, but he couldn't even ride a horse properly. 他自称是牛仔，但他连骑马都不会。|近义词：cowboy, rancher, buckaroo|反义词：city dweller, sophisticated person
