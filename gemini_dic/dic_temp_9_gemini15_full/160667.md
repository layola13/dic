portgreve:/ˈpɔːrtɡriːv/| n. |港口长，港务官 | An official in charge of a port or harbor.  The term is now largely obsolete, having been replaced by more modern titles.  Historically, the portgreve held significant power and responsibility within port communities. | Example: In medieval times, the portgreve oversaw the loading and unloading of ships and the collection of port dues.  在中世纪，港口长负责监督船只的装卸和港口税的征收。|近义词：harbormaster, port warden |反义词：None directly applicable.
