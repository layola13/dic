saheb:/ˈsɑːhɛb/| n. |先生，老爷，主人|A term of respect used in some South Asian countries, particularly India and Pakistan, to address or refer to a man, especially one of higher social status.  It is often used by those of lower social standing to those of higher standing. |He addressed the shopkeeper as "Saheb." 他称呼店主为“先生”。| sir, master, gentleman | servant, subordinate |名词
