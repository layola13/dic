reelectrify:/ˌriːɪˈlɛktrɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 使再次通电；重新电气化 | To supply (something) with electricity again; to modernize or upgrade something by using electricity. |例句：The old railway line is being reelectrified to improve the service. 老铁路正在重新电气化以改善服务。

例句：The city plans to reelectrify its entire public transportation system.  城市计划对其整个公共交通系统进行重新电气化。


近义词：repower, revitalize (in the context of electrification) | 反义词：de-electrify, de-energize
