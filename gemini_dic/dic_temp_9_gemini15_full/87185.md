herds:/hɜːrdz/| n.|兽群；牧群；人群|A herd is a large group of animals, especially hoofed mammals, that live and feed together.  The term often implies a sense of unity and movement as a single entity.  It can also be used figuratively to describe a large group of people.|Example:  A herd of elephants crossed the savanna.  一群大象穿过热带草原。

The cattle were herded into a corral. 牛群被赶进畜栏。|近义词：flock, drove, crowd|反义词：individual, single animal
