flys:/flaɪz/ | n.  |苍蝇复数|The plural form of "fly," referring to multiple flies.|Example:  There are many flies buzzing around the garbage can.  垃圾桶周围有很多苍蝇在嗡嗡叫。|近义词: insects|反义词: N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fly lands on the food. = 苍蝇落在食物上。
Many flies are buzzing around the picnic. = 许多苍蝇在野餐周围嗡嗡叫。


一般过去时:
The fly landed on my sandwich. = 苍蝇落在我的三明治上。
Several flies flew into the house. = 几只苍蝇飞进了屋子。


现在完成时:
A fly has landed on my cake. = 一只苍蝇落在了我的蛋糕上。
Many flies have been attracted by the smell. = 许多苍蝇被气味吸引了。


过去完成时:
The fly had already landed before I could swat it. = 我还没来得及拍它，苍蝇已经落下来了。
The flies had been swarming around the trash can all day. = 苍蝇一整天都在垃圾桶周围乱飞。


将来时:
The fly will land on the window. = 苍蝇将落在窗户上。
More flies will come if we leave the food out. = 如果我们把食物放在外面，将会来更多的苍蝇。


虚拟语气:
If the fly were larger, I would be more afraid. = 如果苍蝇更大，我会更害怕。
I wish the flies would go away. = 我希望苍蝇能飞走。


被动语态:
The food was covered with flies. = 食物上爬满了苍蝇。
The garbage was infested with flies. = 垃圾里爬满了苍蝇。


疑问句:
Are there any flies in the kitchen? = 厨房里有苍蝇吗？
How many flies did you see? = 你看见几只苍蝇？


否定句:
There aren't any flies in this room. = 这个房间里没有苍蝇。
I didn't see any flies. = 我没有看到任何苍蝇。
