milice:/mɪˈlɪʃə/| n. |民兵组织；民兵；义勇军 |A militia is an army composed of ordinary citizens rather than professional soldiers, often raised for temporary service.  It's typically organized by local communities or regions and is distinct from a standing army. |The local militia was called up to defend the town against the invaders. 当地民兵被召集起来保卫城镇免受侵略者袭击。 The governor authorized the formation of a state militia to maintain order. 州长批准组建州民兵以维持秩序。|army, national guard|standing army, professional army|名词
