effluxion:/ɪˈflʌkʃən/| n. |流出；外流；流出物|The outward flow of a fluid, especially blood.  It refers to the process of flowing out, or the thing that flows out.  The term often implies a gradual or continuous outflow.|例句：The effluxion of blood from the wound was alarming.  伤口大量出血令人担忧。

The slow effluxion of water from the reservoir depleted its supply. 水库缓慢流失的水耗尽了它的供应。|近义词：outflow, effluence, discharge |反义词：influx, influxion
