cyberspaces:/ˈsaɪbərˌspeɪsɪz/ | n. |网络空间，赛博空间 |The virtual world created by interconnected computer networks.  It encompasses online interactions, data, and digital environments.  |例句：Many people spend hours each day in cyberspace. 许多人每天在网络空间花费数小时。

例句：The company's new virtual reality game allows users to explore a vast and immersive cyberspace.  这家公司的新虚拟现实游戏允许用户探索一个广阔而身临其境的网络空间。


近义词：virtual world, digital realm, online world |反义词：physical world, real world |词性：名词
