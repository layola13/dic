perceivedly:/pərˈsiːvdli/ | adv. | 看来；据认为；表面上 | Apparently or seemingly; in a way that is perceived.  The word suggests that the perception might not be entirely accurate or complete. |例句：Perceivedly, the company is in trouble, but their financial reports show otherwise.  看来这家公司遇到了麻烦，但他们的财务报告却显示并非如此。


例句：The situation, perceivedly chaotic, was actually under control.  情况表面上看来很混乱，实际上却掌控得很好。


近义词：apparently, seemingly, ostensibly, outwardly


反义词：actually, really, truly, demonstrably


词性：副词
