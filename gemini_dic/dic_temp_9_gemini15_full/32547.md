chipped:/tʃɪpt/| adj. v.|有缺口的；碎裂的；削过的；|Having a small piece broken off; damaged with small breaks or cracks.  |例句：The cup was chipped.  杯子有缺口。
The paint was chipped in several places.油漆好几处都剥落了。|近义词：damaged, cracked, broken, fractured|反义词：intact, undamaged, whole


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chips away at the ice. 他一点一点地凿掉冰块。
The birds chip at the seeds. 鸟儿啄食着种子。


一般过去时:
She chipped a tooth while eating an apple. 她吃苹果时崩掉了一颗牙。
The car chipped the paint on the wall. 汽车刮掉了墙上的油漆。


现在完成时:
He has chipped away at the problem for weeks. 他已经处理这个问题好几个星期了。
The constant wind has chipped away at the stone.  持续不断的风把石头磨损了。


过去完成时:
She had chipped a nail before the interview. 面试前她已经弄断了指甲。
The old building had chipped away at the foundations.  这座旧建筑已经损坏了地基。


将来时:
He will chip away at the debt slowly but surely. 他将慢慢但肯定地还清债务。
The strong waves will chip away at the coastline. 强烈的海浪将会侵蚀海岸线。


虚拟语气:

疑问句:

否定句:
