reinsisting: /ˌriːɪnˈsɪstɪŋ/ | v. | 再次坚持；反复坚持 |  To insist again; to repeatedly insist.  |  He kept reinsisting on his innocence despite the evidence against him. 他尽管有证据对他不利，却一直坚持自己的清白。  |  insist, persist, urge |  give up, concede, yield | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reinsists on his opinion. = 他再次坚持他的意见。
They reinsist that the plan is feasible. = 他们再次坚持这个计划是可行的。

一般过去时:
She reinsited on seeing the manager. = 她再次坚持要见经理。
He reinsited that I was wrong. = 他再次坚持我错了。

现在完成时:
They have reinsited on their demands. = 他们已经再次坚持他们的要求。
She has reinsited that the meeting be postponed. = 她已经再次坚持会议应该推迟。

过去完成时:
He had reinsited on his position before the meeting. = 会议前他已经再次坚持自己的立场。
She had reinsited that she was not involved. = 她此前已经再次坚持自己没有参与其中。

将来时:
He will reinsist on his right to appeal. = 他将再次坚持他上诉的权利。
They will reinsist that the project be completed on time. = 他们将会再次坚持这个项目必须按时完成。

虚拟语气:
If he reinsited, I would have to reconsider. = 如果他再次坚持，我将不得不重新考虑。
I wish he hadn't reinsited on that point. = 我真希望他没有再次坚持那一点。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
