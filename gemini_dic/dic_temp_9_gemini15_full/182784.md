repatrolling:/ˌriːpəˈtroʊlɪŋ/| v.|再次巡逻；重新巡逻|The act of patrolling again or anew.  It implies a return to a previous patrol area or a resumption of patrolling after an interruption.|例句：After the incident, the police initiated repatrolling of the neighborhood.  = 事件发生后，警方对该街区进行了再次巡逻。

例句：The security guards are repatrolling the building following the recent break-in. = 最近发生入室盗窃案后，保安正在对大楼进行重新巡逻。

近义词：re-patrolling, patrolling again, resuming patrol

反义词：ceasing patrol, abandoning patrol
