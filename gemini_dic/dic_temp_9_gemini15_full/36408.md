cojoining:/kəˈdʒɔɪnɪŋ/ | v. |连接，联合|The act of joining or uniting two or more things.  It implies a close connection or combination, often suggesting a merging or integration. | Example: The cojoining of the two companies created a stronger market presence.  两个公司的合并创造了更强大的市场地位。|近义词：joining, uniting, combining, merging, integrating |反义词：separating, disconnecting, dividing
