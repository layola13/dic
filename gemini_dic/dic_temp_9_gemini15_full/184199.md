resodding:/rɪˈsɒdɪŋ/| v.|重新铺设草皮|To replace old or damaged turf with new turf.  This usually involves removing the old sod, preparing the soil, and then laying down new sod.|例句：The football field needs resodding after the recent heavy rains. 最近的大雨过后，足球场需要重新铺设草皮。 The gardener is resodding the lawn this weekend. 园丁这周末正在重新铺设草坪。|近义词：returfing, relaying turf|反义词：removing sod, letting the lawn go to seed.
