reassociates:/ˌriːəˈsoʊʃieɪts/ | v. | 再联想；重新结合；重新联系 | To associate again; to reconnect or re-establish a connection or relationship.  |例句：After the break-up, they reassociated only briefly. 分手后，他们只短暂地重新联系过。

例句：The therapist helped the patient reassociate positive memories with the trauma. 治疗师帮助病人将积极的记忆与创伤重新联系起来。


近义词：reconnect, reunite, rejoin, re-establish


反义词：disconnect, separate, disassociate


词性：动词
