hemorrhoid:/ˈhɛmərɔɪd/| n. |痔疮|A swollen vein or group of veins in the anus and rectum that can cause pain, itching, and bleeding.  Hemorrhoids are a common problem, affecting many people at some point in their lives. They can be internal (inside the rectum) or external (outside the anus).|例句：He suffers from painful hemorrhoids. 他患有痛苦的痔疮。

例句：The doctor diagnosed her with hemorrhoids and recommended some lifestyle changes.医生诊断她患有痔疮，并建议她改变一些生活方式。
