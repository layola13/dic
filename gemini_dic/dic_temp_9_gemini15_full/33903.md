circumvented:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌvɛntɪd/ | v. |规避；绕过；避开 | To avoid something, especially cleverly or illegally.  |例句：They circumvented the problem by using a different approach.他们用不同的方法规避了这个问题。|例句：The company circumvented environmental regulations to save money.该公司为了省钱而规避了环境法规。|近义词：evade, bypass, avoid, outmaneuver |反义词：confront, face, encounter |动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They circumvent the rules. = 他们规避规则。
He circumvents the traffic jam every morning. = 他每天早上都绕开交通堵塞。


一般过去时:
She circumvented the security system. = 她绕过了安全系统。
We circumvented the obstacle with ease. = 我们轻松地绕过了障碍物。


现在完成时:
They have circumvented the law many times. = 他们多次规避法律。
He has circumvented the issue altogether. = 他完全避开了这个问题。


过去完成时:
She had circumvented the problem before we even noticed it. =在我们注意到之前，她就已经解决了这个问题。
They had circumvented the blockade successfully. =他们成功地绕过了封锁。


将来时:
We will circumvent the difficulty by working together. = 我们将通过合作来克服困难。
He will circumvent the regulations if he can. =如果可以，他会规避这些规章制度。


虚拟语气:
If they had circumvented the problem earlier, they would have saved more time. =如果他们早点解决这个问题，他们就能节省更多的时间。
I wish I had circumvented that argument. =我希望我当时能避免那场争论。


被动语态:
The problem was circumvented by using a new method. =这个问题通过使用一种新方法得到了解决。
The security system was circumvented by hackers. =安全系统被黑客绕过了。


疑问句:
How did they circumvent the issue? =他们是如何规避这个问题的？
Can we circumvent this road closure? =我们能绕过这条封路的道路吗？


否定句:
They did not circumvent the regulations. =他们没有规避规章制度。
We could not circumvent the obstacle. =我们无法绕过障碍物。


PHRASE->
circumvent the law = 逃避法律
circumvent a problem = 绕过问题
circumvent an obstacle = 绕过障碍
circumvent the system = 绕过系统
circumvent the rules = 规避规则

词源:
From Latin *circumvenire, from circum- "around" + venire "to come".  The original meaning implied "to come around" or "to surround," evolving to its current meaning of cleverly avoiding something.
