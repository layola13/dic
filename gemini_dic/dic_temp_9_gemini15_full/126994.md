nasalities:/næˈsælɪtiz/| n. |鼻音；鼻音特征|The quality of speech sounds produced with nasal resonance; the use of nasal sounds.  It refers to the resonance of sound in the nasal cavity during speech, which is often perceived as a "nasal" quality.  This can be a characteristic of certain sounds or a feature of a speaker's overall voice. |Example: The singer's nasalities were quite pronounced.  这位歌手的鼻音很重。  The recording clearly captured the subtle nasalities of the dialect.  录音清晰地捕捉到了方言细微的鼻音。|近义词：nasal resonance, nasal quality|反义词：oral quality (lack of nasal resonance) |名词
