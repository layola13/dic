They are known for their sleight of hand and ability to create illusions that seem impossible.  |Example: The prestidigitator amazed the audience with his incredible card tricks.  魔术师以他不可思议的纸牌戏法让观众惊叹不已。 The prestidigitator's nimble fingers made the rabbit seemingly disappear. 魔术师灵巧的手指让兔子仿佛凭空消失了。|近义词：magician, illusionist, conjurer |反义词：None specifically, but words like "amateur" or "inexperienced" could be considered antonyms in the context of skill.|名词
