interregional:/ˌɪntərˈriːdʒənəl/| adj.|区域间的，跨地区的|Relating to or connecting two or more regions.  It often implies interaction, cooperation, or conflict between different geographical areas.|例句：The interregional trade agreement boosted economic growth in participating countries.  区域间的贸易协定促进了参与国的经济增长。

例句：The study examined the interregional differences in income levels. 这项研究考察了地区间收入水平的差异。


近义词：transregional, inter-regional, cross-regional


反义词：local, intraregional


词性: adjective
