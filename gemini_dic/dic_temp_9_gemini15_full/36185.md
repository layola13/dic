cofinance:/koʊˈfaɪnæns/| vt.|共同融资，联名融资 |To jointly finance something.  It implies a sharing of financial responsibility and often a collaborative effort between two or more parties.|例句：Several banks cofinanced the large infrastructure project. =几家银行共同为大型基础设施项目融资。|例句：The government and private investors cofinanced the new research facility. =政府和私人投资者共同为新的研究设施提供资金。|近义词：jointly finance, co-fund|反义词：solely finance, individually fund


USAGE->
