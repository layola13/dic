lexicologists:/ˌlɛksɪˈkɑːlədʒɪsts/ | n. |词汇学家|Scholars who study lexicology, the branch of linguistics concerned with the meaning, history, and use of words. Lexicologists analyze word formation, meaning changes, and the relationships between words in a language. They often contribute to the creation and improvement of dictionaries and thesauruses. |例句：Lexicologists are currently debating the etymology of the word "okay". 词汇学家目前正在争论“okay”一词的词源。 |近义词：linguists, semanticists |反义词: N/A
