leucoma:/ljuːˈkoʊmə/| n. |白斑；角膜白斑|A leucoma is an opaque white spot on the cornea of the eye, often resulting from scarring or inflammation.  It can significantly impair vision.  The severity depends on the size and location of the opacity.|The patient presented with a central leucoma, severely affecting his vision.  这位病人出现中央性角膜白斑，严重影响了他的视力。|scar, opacity|clear cornea, healthy cornea|名词
