endorsed:/ɛnˈdɔːrst/ | vt. |背书；认可；赞同；支持 |To give support or approval to someone or something.  |例句：The mayor endorsed the new park plan. 市长支持新的公园计划。|例句：Many celebrities endorsed the product. 许多名人都为该产品代言。|近义词：approve, support, sanction, advocate |反义词：reject, oppose, condemn, disapprove |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He endorses the new policy. = 他支持这项新政策。
The company endorses ethical business practices. = 这家公司支持合乎道德的商业行为。

一般过去时:
She endorsed the candidate for president. = 她支持这位总统候选人。
They endorsed the proposal unanimously. = 他们一致赞同该提案。

现在完成时:
The organization has endorsed several environmental initiatives. = 该组织已经支持了几项环保倡议。
He has endorsed this product for years. = 他多年来一直为该产品代言。

过去完成时:
The board had endorsed the project before the funding was secured. = 在资金到位之前，董事会已经批准了该项目。
She had endorsed the idea long before it became popular. = 在这个想法流行很久之前，她就表示赞同了。

将来时:
They will endorse the agreement next week. = 他们下周将批准该协议。
I believe she will endorse my application. = 我相信她会批准我的申请。

虚拟语气:
If the committee endorsed the plan, we would proceed. = 如果委员会批准该计划，我们将继续进行。
I wish the government would endorse renewable energy. = 我希望政府能支持可再生能源。

被动语态:
The proposal was endorsed by the committee. = 该提案得到了委员会的批准。
His ideas were widely endorsed by the public. = 他的想法得到了公众的广泛支持。

疑问句:
Does he endorse the new law? = 他支持这项新法律吗？
Will the bank endorse the loan application? = 银行会批准贷款申请吗？

否定句:
The organization did not endorse the controversial proposal. = 该组织没有支持这个有争议的提案。
He refused to endorse the candidate's platform. = 他拒绝支持该候选人的政纲。


PHRASE->
endorse a check = 背书支票
endorse a product = 为产品代言
endorse a candidate = 支持候选人
endorse a policy = 支持政策
endorse a bill = 认可法案


词源:
From Old French endosser, from Vulgar Latin *in- + dorsum ("back").  The original meaning related to writing on the back of something, which evolved to the current meaning of giving support or approval.
