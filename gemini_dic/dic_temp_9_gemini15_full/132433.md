nondependent:/ˌnɑːnˈdɪpɛndənt/| adj. |非依赖的，独立的 |Not dependent; independent.  The term often refers to individuals or entities that are self-supporting and not reliant on others for financial or other essential support.  |例句：He is a nondependent adult, financially supporting himself. 他是一个经济独立的成年人，靠自己养活自己。

例句：The nondependent clauses in the sentence are grammatically complete.  这个句子中的非限定性从句在语法上是完整的。


近义词：independent, autonomous, self-reliant, self-sufficient

反义词：dependent, reliant, subordinate


词性:形容词
