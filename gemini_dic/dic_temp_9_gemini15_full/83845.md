halal:/hɑːˈlɑːl/ | adj. | 清真的；符合伊斯兰教规的 | Permissible or lawful under Islamic law, especially relating to food.  |  This restaurant serves only halal meat. 这家餐馆只供应清真肉类。 | kosher, permissible | haram, forbidden | 形容词
