nonconviction: /ˌnɒnkənˈvɪkʃən/ | n. | 无罪判决；未定罪 | The absence of a criminal conviction; a finding of not guilty.  It refers to a situation where someone has been accused of a crime but has not been found guilty. This can be due to various reasons, including insufficient evidence, dismissal of charges, or acquittal. |  The defendant celebrated his nonconviction after a lengthy trial.  被告在漫长的审判后庆祝他的无罪判决。


His nonconviction doesn't necessarily mean he's innocent; it just means the prosecution failed to prove his guilt beyond a reasonable doubt. 他的无罪判决并不一定意味着他是无辜的；这仅仅意味着检方未能证明他有罪。|近义词：acquittal, exoneration |反义词：conviction, guilt
