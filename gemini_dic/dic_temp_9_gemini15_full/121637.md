mispend:/mɪsˈpend/| vt.|滥用；浪费；错用|To use (money, time, etc.) unwisely or wastefully.|例句：He misspent his youth. 他虚度了青春。|例句：Don't misspend your energy on unimportant things. 不要把精力浪费在不重要的事情上。|近义词：waste, squander, fritter away|反义词：save, conserve, utilize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mispends his money on gambling. = 他把钱浪费在赌博上。
She mispends her time watching TV all day. = 她整天看电视，浪费时间。


一般过去时:
They misspent their inheritance on a foolish venture. = 他们把遗产浪费在一个愚蠢的冒险事业上。
He misspent his youth pursuing fleeting pleasures. = 他虚度青春，追求转瞬即逝的快乐。


现在完成时:
I have misspent many hours trying to fix this computer. = 我花了许多时间试图修理这台电脑，却浪费了时间。
She has misspent her savings on useless gadgets. = 她把积蓄浪费在无用的电子产品上。


过去完成时:
He realized he had misspent his years chasing an unattainable dream. = 他意识到他多年来一直追逐一个无法实现的梦想，浪费了时间。
They discovered they had misspent a significant sum on faulty equipment. = 他们发现他们在有缺陷的设备上浪费了一大笔钱。


将来时:
If he continues this way, he will misspend his entire fortune. = 如果他继续这样下去，他会挥霍掉全部家产。
She fears she will misspend her retirement years doing nothing productive. = 她担心她会虚度退休时光，无所事事。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't misspent my youth, I would be in a better position now. = 如果我没有虚度青春，我现在的情况会更好。
I wish I hadn't misspent so much money on that useless course. = 我真希望我没有在那门无用的课程上浪费那么多钱。


被动语态:
His inheritance was misspent by his irresponsible son. = 他不负责任的儿子挥霍了他的遗产。
A significant portion of the budget was misspent on unnecessary projects. = 一大笔预算被浪费在不必要的项目上。


疑问句:
How much money have you misspent this month? = 这个月你浪费了多少钱？
Do you think he is misspending his talent? = 你认为他在浪费他的天赋吗？


否定句:
He did not misspend his time; he used it wisely. = 他没有浪费时间；他利用得很明智。
She made sure not to misspend the company's funds. = 她确保没有浪费公司的资金。
