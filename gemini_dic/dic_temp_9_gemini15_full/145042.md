overaggravating: /ˌoʊvərˈæɡrəveɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 加剧；使恶化；过度强调 |  This word is the present participle of the verb "overaggravate". It describes the action of making something worse or more serious than it already is, often to an excessive degree.  It implies an intensification of a negative situation or feeling. |

例句：
His constant complaining was overaggravating the already tense situation. = 他不断的抱怨加剧了本已紧张的局势。

The doctor warned that ignoring the symptoms would only overaggravate the condition. = 医生警告说，忽视症状只会加重病情。


近义词：exacerbate, worsen, aggravate, intensify, inflame

反义词：alleviate, soothe, mitigate, improve, ameliorate

词性：动词
