corrasive: /kəˈroʊsɪv/ | adj. | 腐蚀性的，侵蚀性的 |  Highly corrosive substances can eat away at or destroy materials through chemical reaction.  It refers to the ability of a substance to damage or destroy other materials by chemical action.  | 例句：Sulfuric acid is a highly corrosive substance.  硫酸是一种强腐蚀性物质。

The concentrated solution proved corrosive to the metal pipes.  浓缩溶液证明对金属管道具有腐蚀性。


近义词：erosive, caustic, destructive, abrading | 反义词：protective, harmless, inert | 词性：形容词
