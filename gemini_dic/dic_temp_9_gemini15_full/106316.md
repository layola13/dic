koch:/kɔːtʃ/| n. |科赫；教练；马车|A coach is a person who trains an athlete or sports team.  The word also refers to a type of enclosed carriage.  It can also refer to Robert Koch, a famous German physician.|例句：He's a soccer coach. 他是一名足球教练。|例句：They took a long journey in a horse-drawn coach. 他们乘坐马车进行了长途旅行。|近义词：trainer, instructor, mentor, carriage|反义词：player, athlete (in the context of a coach vs. player)|名词
