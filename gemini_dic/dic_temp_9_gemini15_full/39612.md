consent:/kənˈsɛnt/| n. v.|同意，赞成；准许|Agreement or permission; to agree to something or give permission.|例句：She gave her consent to the marriage. 她同意了这桩婚事。|近义词：agreement, approval, permission|反义词：refusal, objection, disapproval


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He consents to the plan. = 他同意这个计划。
They consent to our proposal. = 他们同意我们的建议。


一般过去时:
She consented to the operation. = 她同意了手术。
The board consented to his resignation. = 董事会同意了他的辞职。


现在完成时:
Have you consented to the terms of the contract? = 你是否同意合同条款？
They have consented to participate in the study. = 他们已经同意参与这项研究。


过去完成时:
He had consented to the terms before the meeting. = 他在会议之前就已同意了条款。
She had consented to help before realizing the full extent of the work. = 她是在意识到工作的全部内容之前才同意帮忙的。


将来时:
Will he consent to the interview? = 他会同意接受采访吗？
I believe they will consent to our request. = 我相信他们会同意我们的请求。


虚拟语气:
If he consented, we could proceed. = 如果他同意，我们就可以继续了。
I wish he would consent to our marriage. = 我希望他能同意我们的婚事。


被动语态:
His request was consented to by the committee. = 他的请求得到了委员会的批准。
The proposal was consented to after much deliberation. = 在经过大量的讨论之后，该提案得到了批准。


疑问句:
Does he consent to the changes? = 他同意这些改变吗？
Will they consent to our request? = 他们会同意我们的请求吗？


否定句:
He did not consent to the search. = 他不同意搜查。
She did not consent to the terms of the agreement. = 她不同意协议的条款。


PHRASE->
give consent = 同意
with one's consent = 经某人同意
without consent = 未经同意
obtain consent = 获得同意
parental consent = 家长同意


词源:
From Old French consentir, from Latin consentire, from com- "together" + sentire "to feel."  The word's evolution reflects the idea of shared feeling or agreement.
