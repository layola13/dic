disprovement:/dɪsˈpruːvmənt/| n. |驳斥；反驳；证明为假|The disprovement of the theory was a major setback for the research team.  理论的驳斥对研究团队来说是一个重大挫折。|近义词：refutation, denial, disproof|反义词：proof, confirmation, verification|名词


USAGE->
This word is relatively uncommon and doesn't readily lend itself to the full range of grammatical tenses and structures demonstrated in the example.  Its usage is primarily in the simple present and past tenses, and occasionally in passive voice.

一般现在时:
The scientist's new data provides a disprovement of the old hypothesis. 科学家的新数据驳斥了旧的假设。
His arguments offer a strong disprovement of the defendant's claims.  他的论点有力地驳斥了被告的主张。


一般过去时:
The experiment resulted in the disprovement of the initial theory. 实验结果驳斥了最初的理论。
The court heard evidence that led to the disprovement of the witness's testimony. 法庭听取了导致驳斥证人证词的证据。


现在完成时:
Recent studies have provided a disprovement of the long-held belief. 最近的研究已经驳斥了长期以来存在的信念。
Their research has led to the disprovement of several earlier findings.  他们的研究导致驳斥了几个早期的发现。


过去完成时:
Before the new evidence emerged, there had been no disprovement of the suspect's alibi. 在新的证据出现之前，并没有驳斥嫌疑人的不在场证明。
By the end of the debate, a disprovement of the opponent's argument had been presented. 在辩论结束时，已经提出了驳斥对方论点的论据。


将来时:
Further research will likely lead to the disprovement of this outdated model.  进一步的研究很可能会驳斥这个过时的模型。
We expect that upcoming experiments will result in the disprovement of the current assumptions. 我们预计即将进行的实验将驳斥目前的假设。


被动语态:
The theory was disproven by new evidence.  该理论被新的证据驳斥了。
His claims were disproven during the cross-examination.  他的说法在盘问过程中被驳斥了。
