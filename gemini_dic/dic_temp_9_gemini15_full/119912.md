militiaman:/ˌmɪlɪʃəˈmæn/ | n. |民兵；民兵队员 | A member of a citizen army that supplements a regular army, especially in times of emergency.  | Example: The militiaman stood guard at the bridge.  这位民兵在桥上站岗。 |近义词：soldier, volunteer, guardsman | 反义词：professional soldier
