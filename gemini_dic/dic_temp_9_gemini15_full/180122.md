refocusing:/ˌriːˈfoʊkəsɪŋ/ | v. | 重新聚焦；重新集中注意力 | The act of directing one's attention or efforts toward a new or different focus.  It implies a shift from a previous focus. | 例句：After a long break, she needed some time for refocusing before tackling the project.  重新休息后，她需要一些时间重新集中精力才能着手这个项目。


例句：The company is refocusing its efforts on its core business. 公司正在将其精力重新集中在其核心业务上。


近义词：refocusing, refocus, reorienting, redirecting


反义词：distracting, diverting


词性：动词 (gerund)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is refocusing his energy on his studies. 他正在把精力重新集中到学习上。
She refocuses her attention every hour. 她每小时都重新集中注意力。


一般过去时:
They refocused their marketing strategy. 他们重新调整了营销策略。
He refocused his camera on the bird. 他把相机重新对准了那只鸟。


现在完成时:
I have refocused my priorities. 我已经重新调整了我的优先级。
She has refocused her efforts on finding a new job. 她已经将精力重新集中在寻找新工作上。


过去完成时:
They had refocused their resources before the crisis. 危机发生前，他们已经重新分配了资源。
The team had refocused its goals before the deadline. 团队在截止日期前已经重新调整了目标。


将来时:
We will refocus our efforts next quarter. 我们将在下个季度重新集中精力。
He will refocus his attention on the details. 他将把注意力重新集中到细节上。


虚拟语气:
If I had refocused earlier, I would have succeeded. 如果我早点重新集中精力，我就成功了。
If I were to refocus, I would start by eliminating distractions. 如果我要重新集中精力，我会先消除干扰。


被动语态:
The project's goals were refocused by the management. 项目的目标由管理层重新调整了。
The company's strategy is being refocused. 公司的战略正在重新调整中。


疑问句:
Has he refocused his efforts yet? 他已经重新集中精力了吗？
Are they refocusing their resources on research and development? 他们是否正在将资源重新集中在研发上？


否定句:
He didn't refocus his attention on the lecture. 他没有把注意力重新集中到讲座上。
The company has not yet refocused its marketing strategy. 公司尚未重新调整其营销策略。
