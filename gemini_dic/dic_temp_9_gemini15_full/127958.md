neoprene:/ˈniːpriːn/| n. |氯丁橡胶；氯丁橡胶制品|A synthetic rubber, polychloroprene, that is resistant to oil, chemicals, and extreme temperatures. It is used in wetsuits, gloves, and other products requiring flexibility and durability.|The diver wore a neoprene wetsuit to stay warm in the cold water.  潜水员穿着氯丁橡胶潜水衣在冰冷的水中保持温暖。|synthetic rubber, rubber|  |
