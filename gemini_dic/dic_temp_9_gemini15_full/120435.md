minks:/mɪŋks/| n. |水貂；水貂毛皮|A mink is a small, carnivorous mammal of the weasel family, known for its valuable fur.  Minks are often farmed for their fur, which is used to make coats and other garments. |例句：The coat is made of mink. 这件外套是用水貂皮做的。

例句：She admired the luxurious mink stole. 她羡慕那条奢华的水貂披肩。


近义词：mustelid (a more general term), weasel (related species)
反义词：无


词性:名词
