felloe:/ˈfɛloʊ/| n. |轮辐；同伴|A felloe is one of the curved pieces of wood or metal that form the rim of a wheel.  It can also refer to a companion or associate, though this usage is archaic and rare.|例句：The wheelwright carefully replaced the broken felloe. = 车匠仔细地更换了破损的轮辐。|近义词：spoke (in the context of a wheel), comrade (in the archaic sense)|反义词：hub (opposite part of a wheel)
