mason:/ˈmeɪsən/| n. |石匠，泥瓦匠|A mason is a skilled worker who builds structures using stone, brick, or other materials. Masons are often involved in construction projects of all sizes, from small residential buildings to large-scale public works. They use various tools and techniques to cut, shape, and place the materials, ensuring the stability and aesthetic appeal of the finished structure.|例句：The mason carefully placed each stone, ensuring a perfect fit.  这位石匠小心翼翼地放置每一块石头，确保完美契合。|例句：Experienced masons are in high demand for the new cathedral project. 新大教堂项目对经验丰富的石匠需求量很大。|近义词：bricklayer, stonemason|反义词：architect (in a contrasting role)
