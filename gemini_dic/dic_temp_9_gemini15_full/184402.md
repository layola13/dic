responses:/rɪˈspɒnsɪz/ /rɪˈspɑːnsɪz/| n. |回应，反应，答案| Answers or reactions to something.|The teacher carefully considered the students' responses to the exam questions.  老师仔细考虑了学生对考试问题的回答。

The survey gathered a wide range of responses on public opinion.  这项调查收集了关于公众舆论的各种回应。| answers, replies, reactions | questions, inquiries | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He gives thoughtful responses to every question. = 他对每一个问题都给出深思熟虑的回答。
Their responses to the crisis were swift and effective. = 他们对危机的反应迅速而有效。


一般过去时:
Her responses to the interview questions were impressive. = 她对面试问题的回答令人印象深刻。
The company's responses to customer complaints were unsatisfactory. = 公司对客户投诉的回应令人不满意。


现在完成时:
We have received many positive responses to our new product. = 我们已经收到许多对我们新产品的积极回应。
The government has yet to provide adequate responses to the concerns raised. = 政府尚未对提出的担忧做出充分回应。


过去完成时:
By the time the emergency services arrived, there had been few responses to the distress call. = 当紧急服务到达时，对求救电话的回应很少。
They had already drafted their responses before the debate began. = 辩论开始前他们已经起草好了他们的回应。


将来时:
We will await their responses to our proposal with interest. = 我们将饶有兴致地等待他们对我们提议的回应。
The authorities will give a detailed responses to the media inquiry next week. = 当局下周将向媒体详细回应相关调查。


虚拟语气:
If they had given more thoughtful responses, the situation might have been different. = 如果他们给出更深思熟虑的回应，情况可能会有所不同。
I wish their responses had been more helpful. = 我希望他们的回应更有帮助。


被动语态:
The questions were answered with carefully considered responses. = 这些问题得到了经过仔细考虑的回答。
The proposal was met with overwhelmingly positive responses. = 该提案受到了压倒性的积极回应。


疑问句:
What were their responses to the survey questions? = 他们对调查问题的回答是什么？
How many responses did you receive to your advertisement? = 你收到了多少对你的广告的回应？


否定句:
The company gave no responses to our repeated requests. = 公司对我们多次的要求没有任何回应。
There were no immediate responses to the alarm. = 警报没有立即得到回应。
