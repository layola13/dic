shabrack:/ˈʃæbræk/| n.|沙布拉克（一种马鞍垫）|A shabrack is a decorative pad placed under a saddle on a horse's back.  It is typically made of quilted fabric and serves both a protective and aesthetic purpose. |Example:  The rider adorned her horse with a beautiful, embroidered shabrack. 骑手为她的马装饰了一个漂亮绣花的沙布拉克。|近义词：saddle pad, saddle cloth|反义词：无|名词
