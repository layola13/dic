reticency: /ˈrɛtɪsənsɪ/ | n. | 沉默寡言；不爱说话；含糊其辞 | Reluctance or unwillingness to speak about something.  A tendency to be silent or secretive. |  Her reticence on the subject surprised everyone.  她对这个话题的沉默寡言让每个人都感到惊讶。 | taciturnity, reserve, silence | loquacity, garrulity
