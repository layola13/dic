jackpot:/ˈdʒækˌpɑːt/|/ˈdʒækˌpɒt/| n. |头奖；意外之财；意外收获 |A jackpot is the biggest prize in a contest, especially a lottery or gambling game. It can also refer to a sudden, unexpected gain or success.  |例句：He hit the jackpot in the lottery and won a million dollars. 他中了彩票头奖，赢了一百万美元。

例句：The new marketing campaign was a jackpot, exceeding all sales expectations. 新的营销活动大获成功，超出了所有销售预期。


近义词：windfall, bonanza, stroke of luck |反义词：failure, disappointment, loss
