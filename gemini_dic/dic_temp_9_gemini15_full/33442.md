churn:/tʃɜːrn/|/tʃɝːn/| n. vt.|剧烈搅动；翻腾；动荡；大量生产；(客户的)流失率| to move or cause to move with a swirling or rotating motion; to produce something rapidly and in large quantities; the rate at which customers stop using a product or service|例句：The ocean churned violently during the storm.暴风雨期间，大海剧烈翻腾。The factory churns out thousands of cars every day.这家工厂每天生产数千辆汽车。Customer churn is a major concern for the company.客户流失率是该公司主要关注的问题。|近义词：stir, agitate, produce, turnover|反义词：calm, still, stability


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The machine churns the milk. = 机器搅动牛奶。
The company churns out new products constantly. = 公司不断推出新产品。

一般过去时:
The river churned with muddy water after the rain. = 雨后河水浑浊翻滚。
They churned the butter by hand. = 他们手工搅拌黄油。

现在完成时:
The storm has churned the sea into a frenzy. = 暴风雨已将大海搅成一片狂乱。
The company has churned out several bestsellers this year. = 公司今年已推出几部畅销书。

过去完成时:
The wind had churned the leaves into a swirling vortex before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临前，风已将树叶卷成一个旋转的漩涡。
The factory had churned out millions of units before the new model was released. = 新型号发布前，工厂已生产出数百万件产品。

将来时:
The mixer will churn the ingredients together smoothly. = 搅拌机将把配料充分搅拌均匀。
They will churn out more than 100,000 units next year. = 他们明年将生产超过10万件产品。

虚拟语气:
If we churned the batter longer, it would be smoother. = 如果我们把面糊搅拌得更久一些，它会更顺滑。
I wish the sea wouldn't churn so violently. = 我希望大海不要这么剧烈地翻腾。

被动语态:
The milk was churned into butter. = 牛奶被搅拌成黄油。
Thousands of widgets are churned out daily by the factory. = 工厂每天生产数千个小部件。

疑问句:
Does the ocean churn often in this area? = 这个海域经常翻腾吗？
How many units does the factory churn out weekly? = 工厂每周生产多少件产品？

否定句:
The water didn't churn much in the calm bay. = 平静的海湾里水流并不怎么翻腾。
The company didn't churn out enough products to meet demand. = 公司生产的产品不足以满足需求。
