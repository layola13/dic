ethical:/ˈɛθɪkəl/ | adj. | 道德的；伦理的 | Relating to moral principles or the branch of knowledge dealing with these.  It concerns the principles of right and wrong conduct. | 例句：It is ethically wrong to exploit workers.  这是对工人进行剥削，在道德上是错误的。


例句：The company's ethical policy prohibits bribery.  公司的道德政策禁止行贿。


近义词：moral, righteous, virtuous | 反义词：immoral, unethical, unprincipled | 词性：形容词
