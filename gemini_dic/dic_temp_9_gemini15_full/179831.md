reexpands:/ˌriːɪkˈspændz/| v. |重新膨胀；再次扩张 | To expand again after having been compressed or contracted.  This word is a less common verb formed by combining "re-" (again) and "expands" (the present tense of the verb "expand"). It implies a renewed or repeated expansion. | Example: The balloon reexpands after being squeezed. 气球被挤压后重新膨胀。The economy reexpands after a period of recession. 经济在一段时间的衰退后再次扩张。 |近义词：reinflate, swell again, enlarge again |反义词：contract, shrink, deflate |动词
