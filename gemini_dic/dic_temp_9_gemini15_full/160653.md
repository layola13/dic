porterhouse:/ˈpɔːrtərhaʊs/| n. |牛排(一种上等牛排)|A porterhouse steak is a large, flavorful cut of beef from the short loin, containing both the tenderloin and top loin muscles. It's known for its rich marbling and tender texture.  |例句：I ordered a porterhouse steak, medium-rare. 我点了一份五分熟的牛排。

例句：The restaurant is famous for its perfectly cooked porterhouse steaks. 这家餐厅以其烹调完美的牛排而闻名。|近义词：T-bone steak, strip steak|反义词：无|词性:名词
