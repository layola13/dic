ordinariest:/ˈɔːrdənɛriəst/| adj.| 最普通的；最平凡的 | Most common or commonplace; lacking distinction or originality.  The word is the superlative form of "ordinary".|例句：He led the most ordinariest life imaginable. 他过着最普通不过的生活。

例句：The ordinariest of tasks can bring unexpected joy.  最普通不过的任务也能带来意想不到的快乐。


近义词：commonest, most commonplace, most usual, most typical,  banal, pedestrian


反义词：most extraordinary, most exceptional, most unusual, unique, remarkable


词性：形容词
