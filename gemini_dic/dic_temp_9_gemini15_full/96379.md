indiscreet:/ˌɪndɪˈskriːt/| adj. |不谨慎的；轻率的；不适当的 | lacking prudence or good judgment in speech or action; tactless.  |例句：It was indiscreet of him to discuss her private affairs in public. 他在公共场合讨论她的私事是不明智的。|例句：Her indiscreet remarks caused a lot of embarrassment. 她轻率的言论引起了很多尴尬。|近义词：imprudent, tactless, careless, thoughtless |反义词：discreet, prudent, cautious, careful |
