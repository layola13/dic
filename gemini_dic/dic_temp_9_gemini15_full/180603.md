registry: /ˈrɛdʒɪstri/ | n. | 注册表；登记处；注册；记录 | A registry is a database that stores information.  It can refer to a centralized database in a computer system (like the Windows Registry), a record of births, deaths, or marriages, or a formal list of something. | Example: The Windows registry contains crucial system information.  Windows注册表包含重要的系统信息。  The birth registry showed she was born in 1980. 出生登记显示她出生于1980年。 | 近义词：database, record, register, index | 反义词：N/A | 名词

词源:
From Old French *registre, from Latin *registrum, from re- + gerere "to carry, bear, manage".  The sense development is from "something that is carried or borne" to "a record or register".
