orology:/ɔːˈrɒlədʒi/ | n. |气象学|The scientific study of the atmosphere and weather.  It encompasses various aspects such as temperature, humidity, wind, precipitation, atmospheric pressure, and cloud formation.  Orology involves observation, measurement, and analysis of atmospheric phenomena to understand weather patterns and predict future weather conditions. |Example:  She studied orology in college and now works as a meteorologist. 她大学学习气象学，现在是一名气象学家。|近义词：meteorology|反义词：无
