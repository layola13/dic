schematize:/ˈskiːmətaɪz/| vt.|将…系统化；将…图解；概述|To represent something in a schematic or simplified form; to reduce to a scheme or system.  It involves simplifying complex information by focusing on key elements and relationships.|例句：The professor schematized the complex historical process into three major phases.  教授将复杂的歷史过程简化为三个主要阶段。  The engineer schematized the design for easier understanding. 工程师将设计图示化以便更容易理解。|近义词：diagram, outline, summarize, simplify|反义词：elaborate, detail
