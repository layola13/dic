preservations:/ˌprɛzərˈveɪʃən/| n. |保护；保存；腌制；防腐|The act of preserving something.  It can refer to keeping something safe from damage or decay, or to the process of preparing food to prevent spoilage. |例句：The preservation of historical buildings is important.  历史建筑的保护很重要。

The preservation of our natural resources is crucial for future generations. 保护我们的自然资源对子孙后代至关重要。|近义词：conservation, protection, safeguarding |反义词：destruction, ruin, decay |名词
