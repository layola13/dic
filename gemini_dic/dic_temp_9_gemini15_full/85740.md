hawking:/ˈhɔːkɪŋ/| v. | 兜售，叫卖，（尤指）沿街叫卖 | To hawk something means to try to sell it by shouting about it in a public place.  This often involves walking around and calling out to potential customers. |The street vendor was hawking his wares loudly. 街头小贩大声叫卖他的商品。|  He spent the afternoon hawking newspapers. 他下午一直在叫卖报纸。| peddle, vend, sell | buying, purchasing | v.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He hawks his goods in the market every day. 他每天都在市场上叫卖他的货物。
She hawks newspapers on the corner. 她在街角叫卖报纸。


一般过去时:
They hawked their products at the fair. 他们在集市上叫卖他们的产品。
He hawked his wares until late into the night. 他一直叫卖到深夜。


现在完成时:
He has been hawking his paintings for years. 他已经多年叫卖他的画作了。
She has successfully hawked her business ideas to investors. 她成功地向投资者兜售了她的商业理念。


过去完成时:
Before the festival, they had already hawked all their goods. 节日前，他们已经把所有的货物都卖完了。
They had been hawking their produce for hours before anyone bought anything. 他们叫卖农产品几个小时后才有人买东西。


将来时:
He will hawk his new invention at the tech expo. 他将在科技博览会上兜售他的新发明。
They will be hawking their services at the upcoming job fair. 他们将在即将到来的招聘会上推销他们的服务。


虚拟语气:
If he hawked his products more aggressively, he would make more sales. 如果他更积极地叫卖他的产品，他会卖出更多。
I wish I could hawk my skills as easily as he does. 我真希望我能像他一样轻松地推销我的技能。


被动语态:
His goods were hawked throughout the city. 他的货物在全城叫卖。
The new product is being hawked on television. 新产品正在电视上热卖。


疑问句:
Is he still hawking his wares? 他还在叫卖他的货物吗？
Where are they hawking their products? 他们在哪里叫卖他们的产品？


否定句:
He doesn't hawk his goods on Sundays. 他星期天不叫卖他的货物。
They didn't hawk their products effectively. 他们没有有效地叫卖他们的产品。


PHRASE->
hawk one's wares: 叫卖商品
hawk one's services: 推销服务
hawk the streets: 在街上叫卖
hawk a product: 推销一种产品
hawk a message: 传播信息
