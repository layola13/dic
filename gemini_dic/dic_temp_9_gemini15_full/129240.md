noctuary:/nɒkˈtʃuəri/ | n. |夜间住所；夜间活动场所 |A noctuary is a place where one can spend the night, often associated with temporary or unconventional lodging.  It can also refer to a place of nocturnal activity.  The word is relatively rare and often used in a literary or evocative context. |Example: The old lighthouse served as a makeshift noctuary for the shipwrecked sailors.  古老的灯塔成了遇难水手的临时住所。 The abandoned warehouse became a noctuary for illicit activities.  废弃的仓库变成了进行非法活动的场所。|近义词：shelter, refuge, haven |反义词：daylight dwelling, daytime activity
