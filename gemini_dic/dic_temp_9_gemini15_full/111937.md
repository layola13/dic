loller:/ˈlɒlə(r)/| n. |懒散的人，游手好闲的人；（尤指）闲逛的人|A loller is someone who spends their time lazily lounging around, often without any particular purpose or occupation.  They might be seen as idle or unproductive. The term often carries a slightly negative connotation, suggesting a lack of ambition or drive.|He's become a bit of a loller since he retired. 他退休后变得有点懒散了。|idler, lounger, slacker |hard worker, diligent person|名词
