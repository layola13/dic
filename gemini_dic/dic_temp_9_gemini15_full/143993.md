outrates:/ˌaʊtˈreɪts/| vt.|胜过，超过|To surpass or exceed in rate, speed, or amount.|例句：The new model outrates its predecessor in fuel efficiency. 新款车型在燃油效率方面胜过其前代产品。|例句：His anger outrates his reason. 他的愤怒超过了他的理智。|近义词：exceed, surpass, outstrip, outpace|反义词：underperform, lag behind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This engine outrates all competitors in horsepower. = 这款引擎的马力超过所有竞争对手。
The growth of the company outrates the industry average. = 公司的增长速度超过了行业平均水平。

一般过去时:
Last year's sales outrated our projections. = 去年销售额超过了我们的预期。
The storm outrated the previous record for rainfall. = 暴雨的降雨量超过了之前的记录。

现在完成时:
The technology has outrated all expectations. = 这项技术已经超出了所有预期。
Our team has outrated all other teams in productivity. = 我们的团队在生产力方面已经超过了所有其他团队。

过去完成时:
By the end of the year, the project had outrated its initial budget. = 到年底，该项目已经超过了最初的预算。
The athlete had outrated his personal best time. = 该运动员已经超过了他的个人最好成绩。

将来时:
We anticipate that this year's profits will outrate last year's. = 我们预计今年的利润将超过去年。
The demand for this product is expected to outrate supply. = 对该产品的需求预计将超过供应。

虚拟语气:
If the economy continued to grow at this rate, it would outrate all predictions. = 如果经济继续以这个速度增长，它将超过所有预测。
I wish my income would outrate my expenses. = 我希望我的收入能超过我的支出。

被动语态:
The old system was outrated by the new one in terms of speed. = 旧系统在速度方面被新系统超越。
His performance was outrated by his colleague's. = 他的表现被同事超越。

疑问句:
Does this computer outrate the previous model? = 这台电脑性能超过之前的型号吗？
Does her intelligence outrate his? = 她的智力超过他吗？

否定句:
The company's performance did not outrate its competitors. = 该公司的业绩没有超过竞争对手。
His efforts did not outrate the challenges he faced. = 他的努力没有克服他面临的挑战。
