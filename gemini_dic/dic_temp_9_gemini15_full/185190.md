retotaling: /ˌriːˈtoʊtəlɪŋ/ | v. |重新计算，重新合计 | The act of calculating a total again, often after an error has been discovered or new information has been added.  It implies a correction or refinement of a previous calculation. |例句：After discovering a mistake in the initial figures, the accountant began retotaling the accounts. 会计发现初始数字有错误后，开始重新合计账目。

The retotaling of the election results revealed a significant shift in voter preferences. 大选结果的重新计算揭示了选民偏好的大幅转变。 |近义词：recalculating, recomputing, reviewing |反义词：accepting (an incorrect total)
