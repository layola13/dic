infinitely:/ɪnˈfaɪnɪtli/ | adv. |无限地，无穷地 |Extremely or to an unlimited extent.  It suggests something without bounds or limits, continuing forever. |例句：The universe is infinitely large. 宇宙无限大。

例句：The possibilities are infinitely varied.  可能性无限多样。


近义词：boundlessly, endlessly, limitlessly, without limit


反义词：finitely, limitedly
