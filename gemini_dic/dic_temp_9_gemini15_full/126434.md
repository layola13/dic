mysticism:/ˈmɪstɪsɪzəm/| n. |神秘主义；神秘体验|The belief in the existence of realities beyond human understanding, often involving direct experience of the divine or ultimate reality.  It emphasizes intuition, inner experience, and spiritual revelation over logic, reason, and empirical evidence.  Different mystical traditions exist across various religions and cultures.|例句：His interest in Eastern religions led him down a path of mysticism.  他的东方宗教兴趣使他走上了神秘主义的道路。

例句：The painting evokes a sense of profound mysticism. 这幅画唤起了一种深刻的神秘主义感。


近义词：esotericism, occult, spiritualism|反义词：materialism, rationalism, empiricism
