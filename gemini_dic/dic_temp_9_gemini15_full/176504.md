re-probes:/ˌriːˈproʊbz/ | v. |重新探测，再次调查 | To investigate or examine something again.  |例句：The police re-probed the crime scene after new evidence emerged.  警方在出现新的证据后重新调查了犯罪现场。|例句：The scientists re-probed the data to confirm their initial findings. 科学家们重新探测了数据以确认他们的初步发现。|近义词：reinvestigate, re-examine, review |反义词：ignore, neglect |动词
