lacertilia:/ˌlæsəˈtɪliə/| n. |蜥蜴类；蜥蜴目|The term "lacertilia" refers to the order of reptiles that includes lizards.  It is characterized by their elongated bodies, four limbs (though some species are limbless), and typically scaly skin.  The order is quite diverse, encompassing a vast range of species with varying sizes, habitats, and behaviors. |Example:  Many species within the Lacertilia order exhibit remarkable camouflage abilities. 许多蜥蜴目物种展现出非凡的伪装能力。|近义词：lizards (less formal)|反义词：无
