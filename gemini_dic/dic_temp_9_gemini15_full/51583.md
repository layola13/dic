deutschmark:/ˈdɔɪtʃmɑːrk/| n. |德国马克|The deutschmark (DM or DMk) was the currency of Germany from 1948 to 1999.  It was replaced by the euro.  It was a relatively strong and stable currency, known for its low inflation.|例句：Before the euro, the deutschmark was the dominant currency in Germany.  在欧元之前，德国马克是德国的主要货币。

例句：Many Germans still remember the deutschmark with fondness. 许多德国人仍然怀念德国马克。|近义词：mark (as a general term for currency)|反义词：euro (its replacement)|词性：名词
