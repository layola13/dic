sanctified:/ˈsæŋktɪfaɪd/| adj.|神圣的；被视为神圣的|Made holy; consecrated; set apart as sacred.|例句：The church was sanctified ground. 这座教堂是神圣的土地。The priest sanctified the bread and wine. 牧师将面包和葡萄酒圣化了。|近义词：consecrated, hallowed, blessed|反义词：profane, unholy, desecrated


USAGE->

The priest sanctifies the bread. = 牧师将面包圣化。
This land is sanctified. = 这片土地是神圣的。
