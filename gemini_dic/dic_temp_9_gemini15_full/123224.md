monocormic:/ˌmɒnəˈkɔːrmɪk/ | adj. | 单茎的；单轴的 | Having a single stem or axis.  Mono- indicates one, and -cormic refers to a corm (a short, thickened underground stem).  The term is primarily used in botany to describe plants with a single corm. | Example:  Many gladiolus varieties are monocormic, producing a single corm each year.  许多剑兰品种都是单茎的，每年只长出一个球茎。| 近义词：unbranched, single-stemmed | 反义词：polycormic, multi-stemmed
