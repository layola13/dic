nontaxation:/ˌnɒnˌtæksˈeɪʃən/| n. |免税；不征税|The absence of taxation; the state of not being taxed.  It often refers to a policy or situation where certain goods, services, or individuals are exempt from taxes.|Example:  The proposed legislation advocates for nontaxation of essential goods during the economic crisis.  这项拟议的立法主张在经济危机期间对必需品免税。

Example: The island enjoyed a period of nontaxation before the new government took power.  这个岛屿在新政府上台之前享受了一段时间的免税期。


近义词：tax exemption, tax relief, immunity from taxation
反义词：taxation
