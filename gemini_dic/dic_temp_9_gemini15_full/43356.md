crane:/kreɪn/| n. vt.|鹤；起重机；伸长脖子|A crane is a large bird with long legs and a long neck.  A crane is also a machine used for lifting and moving heavy objects. |例句：The crane lifted the heavy steel beams into place. 起重机将沉重的钢梁吊装到位。

The graceful crane flew overhead.  优雅的鹤飞过头顶。|近义词：derrick, hoist,  (for bird) heron, stork|反义词：N/A|名词，动词

词源:

The word "crane" as a bird comes from Old English *cran, related to Old High German *cranih, and ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kranō. The word "crane" as a machine is a later development, likely borrowing the bird's imagery of a long neck reaching upward.
