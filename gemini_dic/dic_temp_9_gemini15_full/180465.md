regasification:/ˌriːɡæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. | 再气化；重新气化 | The process of converting a substance from a solid or liquid state back into a gaseous state.  This is often applied to liquefied natural gas (LNG) which is converted back to gaseous natural gas for use. | Example: The regasification plant is crucial for supplying natural gas to the city.  再气化工厂对于向城市供应天然气至关重要。The regasification process involves heating the LNG to a high temperature. 再气化过程包括将液化天然气加热到高温。| 近义词：gasification, vaporization | 反义词：liquefaction, condensation | 名词
