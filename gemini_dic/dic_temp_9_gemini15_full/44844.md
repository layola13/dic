crusted:/ˈkrʌstɪd/| adj.|有硬皮的；结硬皮的；陈腐的|Covered with or resembling a crust; hardened or solidified into a crust.  The word often implies a layer that is hard, dry, and possibly unpleasant.|例句：The bread was crusted and hard. 这面包结了硬皮，变硬了。

例句：The old cheese had a thick, crusted rind. 那块老奶酪有一层厚厚的硬皮。


近义词：encrusted, coated, hardened, solidified

反义词：soft, fresh, moist


词性：形容词
