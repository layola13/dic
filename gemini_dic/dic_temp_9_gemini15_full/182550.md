renunciatory: /rɪˈnʌnsiətɔːri/ | adj. | 宣布放弃的；放弃的；拒绝的 | Relating to or characterized by renunciation; implying or expressing renunciation.  It suggests a formal or deliberate act of giving something up. |


例句：

The renunciatory statement from the CEO shocked the shareholders. = 首席执行官的放弃声明震惊了股东们。

His renunciatory letter to the organization was brief but firm. = 他给该组织的辞职信简短而坚定。


近义词：repudiatory, abjuring, rejecting

反义词：affirmative, accepting, endorsing


词性：形容词
