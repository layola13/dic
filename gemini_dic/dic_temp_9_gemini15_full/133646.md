nonformative: /ˌnɒnˈfɔːrmətɪv/ | adj. | 非构成性的，非造词的 | Not forming or contributing to the formation of a word or morpheme.  This term is primarily used in linguistics.  It refers to elements within a word that don't contribute to its core meaning or grammatical function.  For instance, prefixes or suffixes can be formative (changing the meaning) or nonformative (adding no meaning). | The prefix "un-" in "unhappy" is formative, changing the meaning from "happy" to "not happy," while a purely phonetic element might be considered nonformative. | 近义词: non-productive, unproductive | 反义词: formative, productive |
