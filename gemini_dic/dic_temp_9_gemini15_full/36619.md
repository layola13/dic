collections:/kəˈlɛkʃənz/ | n. |收藏；收集；系列 | A collection of items gathered together.  A collection can refer to a group of things of the same kind, or things with a common theme.  |例句：He has a large collection of stamps. 他有很多邮票收藏。

例句：The museum boasts a stunning collection of Impressionist paintings. 这个博物馆拥有令人惊叹的印象派绘画收藏。


近义词：assemblage, gathering, accumulation | 反义词：dispersion, scattering


词源:  The word "collections" originates from the Old French word "colleccioun," which itself comes from the Latin word "collecta," meaning "a gathering" or "a collection." The Latin word is formed from the verb "colligere," meaning "to gather together."


用法说明:

单数形式：collection


复数形式：collections


常用搭配：

a collection of: 一系列的...
art collection: 艺术收藏
stamp collection: 邮票收藏
clothing collection: 服装系列
book collection: 图书收藏


例句补充：

1. Her collection of antique dolls is quite valuable.  她的古董娃娃收藏非常有价值。
2. The new clothing collection features vibrant colors and bold patterns. 新的服装系列以鲜艳的色彩和大胆的图案为特色。
3.  The company's marketing strategy involves releasing new collections seasonally. 该公司的营销策略包括按季节发布新的系列。
