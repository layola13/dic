interoceanic:/ˌɪntərəʊˈsiːnɪk/| adj.|洲际的；横跨海洋的；连接两大洋的| Relating to or connecting two or more oceans.  The Panama Canal is an interoceanic waterway.|例句：The Panama Canal is a significant interoceanic waterway, connecting the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans. 巴拿马运河是重要的洲际水道，连接着大西洋和太平洋。

The proposed interoceanic canal would drastically reduce shipping times.  拟议中的洲际运河将大大缩短航运时间。|近义词：transoceanic, intercontinental|反义词：intracoastal, inland
