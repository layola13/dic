pseudology:/ˈsuːdəlɒdʒi/| n. |伪科学；谎言学|The study of lies and falsehoods, especially as a systematic or deliberate practice.  It can also refer to a field of study that presents itself as scientific but lacks scientific rigor.  |例句：His pseudology was evident in his constant fabrication of stories. 他的谎话连篇显而易见。

例句：The paper exposed the pseudology behind the company's claims. 这篇文章揭露了该公司说法背后的伪科学。

近义词：mendacity, fabrication, deception, hoax, pseudo-science

反义词：truth, veracity, reality, fact, science


词性：名词
