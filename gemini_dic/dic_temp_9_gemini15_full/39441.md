connecting: /kəˈnɛktɪŋ/ | v. adj. |连接的；联系的 |  Describing the act of joining or linking things together; also used as an adjective to describe something that joins or links. |

例句：
The connecting flight was delayed. = 转机延误了。

The connecting rod transmits power from the piston to the crankshaft. = 连杆将动力从活塞传递到曲轴。


近义词：linking, joining, uniting, associating

反义词：disconnecting, separating, isolating

词性：动词，形容词


词源：
The word "connecting" comes from the verb "connect," which originates from the Old French word "connecter," meaning "to join together."  This in turn derives from the Latin word "con," meaning "together," and "nectere," meaning "to bind" or "to tie."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is connecting to the Wi-Fi. = 他正在连接WiFi。
The bridge connects two cities. = 这座桥连接着两座城市。


一般过去时:
She connected the dots on the worksheet. = 她连接了工作表上的点。
They connected the two pipes. = 他们连接了两根管道。


现在完成时:
I have connected the printer to my computer. = 我已经把打印机连接到我的电脑上了。
They have connected successfully to the server. = 他们已经成功连接到服务器了。


过去完成时:
He had connected the wires before the power was turned on. = 在电源开启之前，他已经连接好了电线。
She had connected all the pieces of the puzzle before realizing it was a cat. = 在意识到那是一只猫之前，她已经连接好了拼图的所有碎片。


将来时:
We will connect the new system to the existing network. = 我们将把新系统连接到现有网络。
He will connect the call soon. = 他很快就会接通电话。


虚拟语气:
If he connected the wires correctly, the machine would work. = 如果他正确地连接了电线，机器就能工作了。
I wish I had connected with him earlier. = 我希望我早点和他联系上。


被动语态:
The two buildings are connected by a walkway. = 两座建筑物由一条人行道连接。
The internet connection was disconnected by the technician. = 网络连接被技术人员断开了。(被动语态更常用的是断开连接的表达)


疑问句:
Does this wire connect to the power supply? = 这根电线连接到电源了吗？
Are you connected to the meeting? = 你已经连接到会议了吗？


否定句:
The two devices are not connecting. = 这两个设备无法连接。
He couldn’t connect to the internet. = 他无法连接互联网。
