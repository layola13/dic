consortia:/kənˈsɔːrʃə/|/kənˈsɔːrsiə/| n. |财团；协会；联合体 |A consortium is an association of two or more individuals, companies, organizations or governments that is formed to pursue a common goal.  Consortia are often used for large projects that require significant resources or expertise. |Example: Several universities formed a consortium to bid on the research grant.  几个大学组成了一个财团来竞标研究拨款。|近义词：group, alliance, coalition |反义词：individual, single entity
