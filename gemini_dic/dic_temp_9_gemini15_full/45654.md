curtail:/kɜːˈteɪl/|/kərˈteɪl/| vt. |缩减；削减；缩短 |To cut short or reduce; to lessen the extent of. |例句：The government has decided to curtail spending on education.  政府已决定削减教育支出。 |例句：We had to curtail our holiday because of the bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，我们不得不缩短假期。|近义词：abridge, shorten, reduce, lessen |反义词：extend, lengthen, prolong, increase |动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They curtail their expenses every month. = 他们每个月都缩减开支。
The company curtails its production due to low demands. = 公司由于需求低迷而减少产量。

一般过去时:
The project was curtailed due to funding issues. = 由于资金问题，该项目被缩减了。
She curtailed her speech to fit the time limit. = 她缩短了演讲以符合时间限制。

现在完成时:
They have curtailed their travel plans because of the pandemic. = 由于疫情，他们已经缩减了他们的旅行计划。
The government has curtailed several social programs. = 政府已经削减了几项社会福利项目。

过去完成时:
They had curtailed their spending before the economic downturn. = 经济衰退之前，他们就已经削减了开支。
The company had curtailed its operations before the merger. = 公司在合并之前就已经缩减了业务。

将来时:
The company will curtail its workforce next year. = 公司明年将裁员。
We will curtail our vacation if the weather turns bad. = 如果天气变坏，我们将缩短假期。

虚拟语气:
If they curtailed their spending, they would save money. = 如果他们削减开支，他们就能省钱。
I wish they had curtailed the meeting earlier. = 我希望他们早点结束会议。

被动语态:
The budget was curtailed by the government. = 预算被政府削减了。
The program has been curtailed due to lack of funding. = 该项目由于缺乏资金而被缩减了。

疑问句:
Will the company curtail its operations? = 公司会缩减业务吗？
Did they curtail the meeting? = 他们缩短了会议吗？

否定句:
The government did not curtail the project. = 政府没有削减该项目。
They will not curtail their spending plans. = 他们不会削减他们的支出计划。
