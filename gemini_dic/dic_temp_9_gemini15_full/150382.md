The term is often used in medical contexts, particularly in ophthalmology and cardiology to describe changes in tissues.  | Example: Pannose formation in the cornea can impair vision. 角膜的纤维增生可能会影响视力。The patient presented with pannose-like changes in the cardiac valves. 病人出现心脏瓣膜类似泛白的变化。| 近义词：cloudy, opaque, hazy, fibrotic | 反义词：clear, transparent, translucent
