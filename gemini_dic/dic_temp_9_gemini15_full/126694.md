name-droppers:/ˈneɪmˌdrɑːpərz/ | n. | 炫耀认识名人的人 | People who casually mention the names of famous or important people they know, often to impress others.  They may or may not actually have a close relationship with these individuals.  | Example:  He's such a name-dropper; he keeps bringing up his connection to Spielberg. 他是个十足的炫耀认识名人的人；他总是提起他和斯皮尔伯格的关系。|近义词：braggart, show-off,boaster|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming|名词
