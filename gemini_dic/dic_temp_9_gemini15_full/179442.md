reductions:/rɪˈdʌkʃənz/ | n. | 减少；缩减；简化 |  The reductions in staff led to increased workloads for the remaining employees.  员工人数的减少导致剩余员工的工作量增加。 | increases, decreases, diminutions | augmentations, amplifications | 名词


例句：

The company announced significant reductions in its workforce. 公司宣布大幅裁员。

Price reductions are common during the holiday season. 节日期间价格下调很常见。


词源:

源自古法语“reduction”，最终源自拉丁语“reducere”，由“re-”(再次)和“ducere”(引导)组成，表示“引导回来，减少”。


用法说明:

reductions 通常指数量、大小、程度或价格的减少。它可以指具体的减少量，也可以指减少的过程或结果。


近义词：

decreases, diminutions, cuts, lessenings


反义词：

increases, augmentations, amplifications, expansions
