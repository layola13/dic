overprize:/ˈoʊvərpraɪz/ | vt. | 过分重视；估价过高 | To value or estimate something too highly.  |例句：The company overprized its new product, leading to disappointing sales. 公司对新产品估价过高，导致销售业绩令人失望。|近义词：overvalue, overrate, exaggerate |反义词：undervalue, underrate

He overprizes the importance of this meeting. = 他过高估计了这次会议的重要性。
