nonvascular:/ˌnɒnˈvæskjələr/|/ˌnɑːnˈvæskjʊlər/| adj. |无维管束的；非维管的 |Lacking vessels or ducts for the conduction of fluids, especially sap in plants.  This term is primarily used to describe plants that lack xylem and phloem, the tissues responsible for transporting water and nutrients.  These plants typically rely on diffusion and osmosis for water and nutrient transport. | Example: Mosses and liverworts are examples of nonvascular plants.苔藓和地钱是无维管束植物的例子。 |近义词：avascular |反义词：vascular

词源:
The word "nonvascular" is a combination of "non-" (meaning "not") and "vascular" (relating to vessels or ducts).  "Vascular" itself derives from the Latin word "vasculum," meaning "small vessel."  Therefore, "nonvascular" literally means "not having small vessels" for fluid transport.
