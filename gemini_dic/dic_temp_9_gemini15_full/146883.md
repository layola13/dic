overingeniously:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʒiːnjəsli/ | adj. | 过于巧妙地；过于精巧地 | Excessively ingenious; cleverly to a fault.  It implies an excess of ingenuity that may be ineffective or even counterproductive.  | The architect overingeniously designed a structure that was both unstable and impractical. = 建筑师设计了一个过于精巧的建筑，既不稳定也不实用。 | cleverly, skillfully, ingeniously | simply, plainly, directly | 形容词

词源:
