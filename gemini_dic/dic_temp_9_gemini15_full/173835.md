词源:

The word "quasi-probable" is a compound word.

* **quasi-**:  From Latin *quasi*, meaning "as if," "almost," "seemingly."  It indicates a resemblance or approximation.

* **probable**: From Old French *probable*, ultimately from Latin *probabilis*, meaning "provable," "likely."  This derives from *probare*, "to prove," "to test."
