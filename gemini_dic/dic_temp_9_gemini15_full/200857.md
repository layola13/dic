slimmest: /ˈslɪmɪst/ | adj. | 最苗条的，最纤细的 |  The superlative form of "slim," describing something or someone that is extremely thin or slender.  It emphasizes the highest degree of thinness. |例句：She wore the slimmest dress in the room. 她穿着房间里最苗条的裙子。

例句：That is the slimmest chance of success. 那是成功的几率最小。


近义词：thinnest, leanest, slenderest | 反义词：fattest, stoutest, plumpest
