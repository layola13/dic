heteropter:/ˈhɛtərɒptər/| n. |异翅亚目昆虫|A heteropteran is an insect belonging to the suborder Heteroptera, characterized by having forewings that are partially hardened at the base (the corium) and membranous at the apex (the membrane). They are commonly known as true bugs.  Many species are plant feeders, while others are predatory. |Example:  The assassin bug is a well-known example of a heteropteran. 刺杀虫是一个众所周知的异翅亚目昆虫的例子。Many heteropterans use their piercing-sucking mouthparts to feed on plants or other insects. 许多异翅亚目昆虫用它们的刺吸式口器来吸食植物或其他昆虫。|近义词:true bug|反义词:None specifically.
