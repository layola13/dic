nonfundamentalists: /ˌnɒnˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪsts/ | adj. |非原教旨主义者；非原教旨的 |  Those who do not adhere strictly to fundamentalist beliefs or principles, particularly in religion or politics.  They often emphasize a more liberal or moderate interpretation of doctrines or ideologies. |

例句：
The nonfundamentalists in the church advocated for a more inclusive approach to faith. = 教堂里的非原教旨主义者提倡一种更包容的信仰方式。

The political landscape is increasingly divided between fundamentalists and nonfundamentalists. = 政治格局日益分裂为原教旨主义者和非原教旨主义者。


近义词：liberal, moderate, progressive, open-minded

反义词：fundamentalist, orthodox, conservative, traditional


词性：形容词
