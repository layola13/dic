reformation's:/ˌrɛfərˈmeɪʃənz/ | n. | 改革；宗教改革 | The possessive form of "reformation," referring to something belonging to or associated with a reformation, typically the Protestant Reformation.  It signifies the actions, effects, or characteristics of a period of reform or the process of reforming something. | Example: The reformation's impact on European society was profound.  改革对欧洲社会的影响是深远的。The reformation's ideals spread throughout Europe. 改革的理想在欧洲传播开来。 | 近义词：reform's, revolution's, renewal's | 反义词：stagnation's, decline's | 名词
