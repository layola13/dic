shreds:/ʃredz/| n. |碎片，碎屑；少量 |  Pieces or fragments of something torn or cut; a small amount.  |The old letter was torn into shreds. |近义词：fragments, bits, scraps |反义词：whole, intact |名词


例句：

The wind tore the poster into shreds.  = 风把海报撕成了碎片。

He only ate shreds of the chicken. = 他只吃了几块鸡肉碎片。


词源：

源自古英语 *scrēad, 意思是“剪裁，碎片”。  This word has connections to other Germanic languages and ultimately shares a root with words related to cutting and tearing.


用法说明：

shreds 通常作不可数名词使用，表示“碎片”的总体概念。  当表示“少量”时，通常用复数形式。  它也可以用作可数名词，指具体的碎片。


PHRASE->

in shreds:  支离破碎的
tear to shreds:  撕成碎片
cut to shreds:  切成碎片
shredded cheese:  碎芝士
shredded wheat:  麦片
