off-centre: /ˌɒfˈsɛntə(r)/ /ˌɑːfˈsɛntər/ | adj. |偏心的；不在中心的；不平衡的 |Not in the centre; away from the centre.  Describes something that is not aligned with the center or is unevenly distributed. |例句：The painting was slightly off-centre.  这幅画略微偏离了中心。

The vase was off-centre on the table. 花瓶在桌子上放得不正。


近义词：uncentred, asymmetrical, unbalanced, skewed

反义词：centred, central, balanced, symmetrical

词性：形容词
