ostentatious: /ˌɒsənˈteɪʃəs/ | /ˌɑːstənˈteɪʃəs/ | adj. | 炫耀的；卖弄的；夸夸其谈的 | Characterized by or given to ostentation; showy; pretentious.  It suggests an excessive display intended to impress others, often implying a lack of genuine substance. |


例句：

Her ostentatious jewelry was a clear sign of her newfound wealth. = 她炫耀的珠宝清楚地显示了她新获得的财富。

The ostentatious display of wealth at the party was quite off-putting. = 聚会上炫耀财富的行为相当令人反感。


近义词：showy, flamboyant, pretentious, pompous, extravagant, conspicuous


反义词：modest, humble, unassuming, understated


词性：形容词
