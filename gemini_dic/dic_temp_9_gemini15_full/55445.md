distinguish-between:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ biˈtwiːn/| v. |区分，辨别|To recognize or treat (someone or something) as different.|The twins were so alike it was hard to distinguish between them. 双胞胎长得太像了，很难区分他们。|differentiate, discriminate|confuse, blend|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I can distinguish between right and wrong. = 我能明辨是非。
She distinguishes between fact and opinion. = 她能区分事实和观点。


一般过去时:
He distinguished between the two paintings. = 他区分了这两幅画。
The judge distinguished between the two defendants' accounts. = 法官区分了两个被告的说法。


现在完成时:
Scientists have distinguished between several types of viruses. = 科学家们已经区分了几种类型的病毒。
She has distinguished between her professional and personal life. = 她已经区分了她的职业生活和个人生活。


过去完成时:
He had distinguished between the genuine and the fake before the auction. = 拍卖前他已经区分了真伪。
They had distinguished between the two proposals before making a decision. = 他们做出决定前已经区分了两个提案。


将来时:
We will distinguish between the different species of birds. = 我们将区分不同种类的鸟。
The researchers will distinguish between the control group and the experimental group. = 研究人员将区分对照组和实验组。


虚拟语气:
If I could distinguish between the sounds, I would have understood them. = 如果我能区分这些声音，我就明白了。
I wish I could distinguish between the subtle differences in their arguments. = 我希望我能区分他们论点中的细微差别。


被动语态:
The two styles were easily distinguished between by the experts. = 两种风格很容易被专家区分开来。
The different shades of color were distinguished between in the presentation. = 不同的颜色色调在演示中被区分开来。


疑问句:
Can you distinguish between these two wines? = 你能区分这两种葡萄酒吗？
How do you distinguish between a genuine and a fake product? = 你如何区分真伪产品？


否定句:
I cannot distinguish between the two voices. = 我无法区分这两个声音。
He didn't distinguish between the important and unimportant details. = 他没有区分重要和不重要的细节。


PHRASE->
distinguish oneself =  使自己与众不同，表现突出
distinguish A from B = 把A和B区分开来
distinguish between A and B = 区分A和B
to be distinguished = 杰出的，卓越的
easily distinguished = 易于辨别的
