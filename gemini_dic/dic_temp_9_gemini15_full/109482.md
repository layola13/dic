leprosy:/ˈlɛprəsi/| n. |麻风病|A chronic infectious disease caused by the bacterium Mycobacterium leprae, affecting the skin, nerves, mucous membranes, and sometimes the bones.  It is characterized by disfiguring skin lesions and neurological damage.  Early diagnosis and treatment with multi-drug therapy (MDT) can prevent disability.|The patient was diagnosed with leprosy and immediately began treatment.|近义词：Hansen's disease|反义词：无
