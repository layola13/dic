Word Origin:

The word "nonflexibilities" is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the noun "flexibilities" (the plural of "flexibility").  "Flexibility" itself comes from the word "flexible," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "flexibilis," meaning "capable of being bent."
