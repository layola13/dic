shavuoths:/ʃəˈvuːθs/| n. |收割节（犹太教节日）|Shavuoth, also spelled Shavuot, is a Jewish harvest festival celebrating the giving of the Torah on Mount Sinai.  It falls on the 6th of Sivan, 50 days after Passover.  It is a time of feasting and celebration, often involving dairy products. |The Shavuoth celebrations included feasting, reading from the Torah, and staying up all night studying.  收割节的庆祝活动包括盛宴、朗读摩西五经和通宵学习。|近义词：harvest festival, Pentecost (Christian equivalent)|反义词：无
