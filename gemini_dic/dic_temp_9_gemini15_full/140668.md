omena:/ˈoʊmənə/| n.|预兆，征兆|An omen is a sign or event that is believed to foretell the future, often something bad.|例句：The dark clouds were an omen of the storm to come.  乌云是即将到来的暴风雨的预兆。|例句：The broken mirror was considered a bad omen. 打碎镜子被认为是不祥之兆。|近义词：sign, portent, foreboding|反义词：good fortune, blessing

一般现在时:
The dark clouds are an omen of rain. = 乌云是下雨的预兆。
This dream is an omen of good fortune. = 这个梦是好运的预兆。

一般过去时:
The crow was an omen of death in ancient times. = 在古代，乌鸦是死亡的预兆。
The earthquake was an omen of worse to come. = 地震预示着更糟糕的事情即将到来。

现在完成时:
Many people believe that this event has been an omen of change. = 许多人相信这次事件是变革的预兆。
Recent market drops have been an omen of economic downturn. = 最近的市场下跌是经济衰退的预兆。

过去完成时:
The strange noises had been an omen of the intruder's arrival. = 奇怪的声音是入侵者到来的预兆。
The falling barometer had been an omen of the approaching typhoon. = 气压计下降是即将到来的台风的预兆。

将来时:
Some say the comet will be an omen of disaster. = 有人说彗星将是灾难的预兆。
The upcoming election results will be an omen for the future of the country. = 即将到来的选举结果将预示着国家的未来。

虚拟语气:
If only the dream hadn't been an omen of the accident. = 要不是那个梦预示着那场事故就好了。
If that strange noise were an omen of danger, we should leave immediately. = 如果那个奇怪的声音是危险的预兆，我们应该立即离开。

被动语态:
The storm was seen as an omen of disaster. = 暴风雨被视为灾难的预兆。
The unusual silence was interpreted as an omen of trouble. = 不寻常的沉默被解读为麻烦的预兆。

疑问句:
Is this a bad omen? = 这是不祥之兆吗？
Could this be an omen of something worse? = 这会不会是更糟糕事情的预兆？

否定句:
The bright sunshine was not an omen of rain. = 明媚的阳光并非下雨的预兆。
This event is not necessarily an omen of failure. = 这件事并不一定是失败的预兆。
