plopped:/plɑːpt/| vt.|咚地落下；扑通一声掉下|To fall or drop heavily and suddenly, often with a soft thud.  It implies a sudden, somewhat clumsy movement.|The bird plopped onto the branch.  鸟扑通一声落到树枝上。
He plopped down onto the sofa. 他扑通一声坐到沙发上。|近义词：dropped, fell, slumped, thudded|反义词：rose, ascended, floated
