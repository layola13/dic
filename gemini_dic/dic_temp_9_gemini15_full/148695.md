overtire:/ˌoʊvərˈtaɪr/ | vt. |使过度疲劳；使筋疲力尽 | to tire someone or something out excessively.  |例句：The long journey overtired the children. 长途旅行使孩子们筋疲力尽。 |例句：He overtired himself by working too hard. 他工作太辛苦，把自己累坏了。|近义词：exhaust, fatigue, wear out |反义词：rest, refresh
