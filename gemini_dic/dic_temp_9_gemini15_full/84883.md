hango:/ˈhæŋoʊ/| n.|宿醉；残留物|The unpleasant aftereffects of drinking too much alcohol, characterized by headache, nausea, dizziness, and general malaise.  It can also refer to the lingering effects of something unpleasant.|例句：He had a terrible hango after the party. 他派对后宿醉得很厉害。|例句：The hango of the war continued to affect the country's economy.战争的余波继续影响着国家的经济。|近义词：hangover, aftereffects, residue|反义词：None specifically, but could be considered opposite to feeling refreshed or well.
