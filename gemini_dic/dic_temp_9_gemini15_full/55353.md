distally:/ˈdɪstəli/| adv.|远端地，末梢地| Situated away from the center of the body or from the point of attachment.  It typically refers to the part of a limb that is farthest from the torso or point of origin.|例句：The fracture was located distally in the tibia.  胫骨远端发生骨折。|例句：The nerve damage was distally located in the hand. 手部远端神经受损。|近义词：peripherally, terminally|反义词：proximally
