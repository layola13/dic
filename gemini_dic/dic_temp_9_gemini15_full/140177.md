offeree:/ˌɒfəˈriː/| n. |受要约人，受盘人 |The person to whom an offer is made.  The offeree can accept, reject, or counter the offer.  |例句：The offeree carefully considered the terms of the contract before signing.  受要约人仔细考虑了合同条款后才签字。|例句：The offeree rejected the initial offer but made a counteroffer. 受要约人拒绝了最初的报价，但提出了还价。|近义词：recipient, receiver |反义词：offeror
