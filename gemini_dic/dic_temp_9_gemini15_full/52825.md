dilutes: /daɪˈluːts/ | vt. | 稀释；冲淡；削弱 | to make a liquid less concentrated; to make something weaker or less effective |

例句：

The rain diluted the paint on the wall. = 雨水冲淡了墙上的油漆。

Adding water dilutes the juice, making it less sweet. = 加水稀释果汁，使其不那么甜。


近义词：weaken, thin, water down

反义词：concentrate, strengthen
