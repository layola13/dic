seersucker:/ˈsɜːrsʌkər/| n. adj.|细条棉布；皱纹布|A lightweight fabric with a puckered surface, typically striped, used for clothing in warm weather.  It is created by a special weaving technique that causes the fabric to wrinkle naturally.|例句：The seersucker suit was perfect for the hot and humid summer day.  这件细条棉布西装非常适合炎热潮湿的夏天。 The seersucker dress was cool and comfortable. 这件皱纹布连衣裙凉爽舒适。|近义词：gingham, madras|反义词：heavy fabric, smooth fabric|名词，形容词
