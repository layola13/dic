petrochemically: /ˌpɛtroʊˈkɛmɪkli/ | adj. | 石油化学的；用石油化学方法制造的 | Relating to or denoting processes or products derived from petroleum or natural gas.  |例句：Petrochemically derived plastics are widely used in packaging. 石油化工衍生的塑料广泛用于包装。

例句：Many petrochemically produced materials are non-biodegradable and contribute to pollution. 许多石油化工生产的材料不可生物降解，并导致污染。


近义词：petroleum-based, hydrocarbon-based | 反义词：bio-based, naturally derived


词源:  The word is a combination of "petro" (from "petroleum," ultimately from Greek πέτρα (petra) meaning "rock") and "chemically," referring to chemical processes.  The "-ically" suffix indicates in a manner characteristic of.
