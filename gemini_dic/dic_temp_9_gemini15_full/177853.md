receptions:/rɪˈsɛpʃənz/ | n. |招待会；接待；接受 |The act of receiving or being received; the state of being received.  It can refer to a formal social gathering, the act of welcoming guests, or the process of accepting something. |例句：The wedding reception was held in a beautiful garden. 婚礼招待会在美丽的庭院举行。

The hotel offers a warm reception to all its guests. 这家酒店热情款待所有宾客。


近义词：welcome, party, gathering, acceptance

反义词：rejection, refusal


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hotel reception usually welcomes guests with a smile.  酒店接待处通常面带微笑地欢迎宾客。
Many people attend the university's annual reception. 许多人参加了大学的年度招待会。
