reconceals:/ˌriːkənˈsiːlz/| vt.|重新隐藏；再次隐瞒|To hide again; to conceal a second time.  The act of hiding something again after it has already been hidden.|例句：He reconcealed the evidence after the police searched his house. 他在警察搜查了他的房子后再次隐藏了证据。

例句：She reconcealed her feelings, pretending everything was fine. 她再次掩饰了自己的感情，假装一切安好。


近义词：rehide, mask, secrete, obscure|反义词：reveal, uncover, disclose, expose|动词
