carburetor: /ˈkɑːrbjʊreɪtər/ | n. |化油器| A device in an internal combustion engine that mixes air and fuel before it enters the cylinders.  It uses a venturi effect to draw fuel into the airstream.  Modern vehicles largely use fuel injection systems instead. |The old car's carburetor needed cleaning. 这辆旧车的化油器需要清洗。  |The mechanic adjusted the carburetor to improve the engine's performance. 机械师调整了化油器以提高发动机的性能。|fuel injector, fuel injection system|  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific part;  "fuel injection system" is a functional opposite)|名词
