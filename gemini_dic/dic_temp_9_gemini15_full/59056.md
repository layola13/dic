dwell:/dwel/| v.|居住，沉思，详述|to live in a place; to think or talk about something for a long time|例句：They dwelt in a small cottage by the sea.他们住在一间靠海的小屋里。|例句：He dwelt on the injustice of the situation. 他详述了这件事的不公平。|近义词：reside, inhabit, contemplate, ponder|反义词：depart, leave, ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dwells in a small apartment. = 他住在一间小公寓里。
She dwells on her past mistakes. = 她总是沉湎于过去的错误。

一般过去时:
They dwelt in that house for many years. = 他们在那所房子里住了很多年。
He dwelt on the details of the accident. = 他详述了事故的细节。

现在完成时:
We have dwelt in this city for ten years. = 我们在这个城市住了十年了。
She has dwelt on that problem for too long. = 她在这个问题上纠结太久了。

过去完成时:
They had dwelt in the village before the war. = 战争前他们住在那个村庄。
He had dwelt on the subject for hours before he finally understood. = 他花了几个小时思考这个问题才最终理解。

将来时:
They will dwell in a new house next year. = 他们明年将住进新房子。
She will dwell on the positive aspects of the situation. = 她将会专注于事情积极的一面。

虚拟语气:
If I dwelt in a castle, I would be very happy. = 如果我住在一座城堡里，我会非常快乐。
I wish I hadn't dwelt on his criticism so much. = 我真希望我没有那么在意他的批评。

被动语态:
The issue was dwelt upon extensively in the meeting. = 会议上对这个问题进行了广泛的讨论。
The problem should not be dwelt upon. = 不应该在这个问题上过多纠结。

疑问句:
Where do they dwell? = 他们住在哪里？
What does he dwell on? = 他在思考什么？

否定句:
They do not dwell in that house anymore. = 他们不再住在那所房子里了。
She did not dwell on the negative aspects. = 她没有过多关注消极方面。


PHRASE->
dwell on/upon:  详述；细想
dwell in: 居住于
dwell among: 住在…中间
dwell together: 同住
dwell with: 与…同住
