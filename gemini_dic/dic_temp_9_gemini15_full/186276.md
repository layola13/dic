rhizopod:/ˈraɪzəpɑːd/| n. |根足虫；肉足虫 |A single-celled organism of the phylum Rhizopoda, characterized by pseudopodia (temporary projections of cytoplasm) used for locomotion and feeding.  Rhizopods are found in various aquatic and terrestrial environments. |Example: Amoebas are a well-known example of rhizopods.  变形虫是根足虫的一个众所周知的例子。|近义词：protozoan (原生动物)|反义词：N/A
