deferred:/dɪˈfɜːrd/ | adj. v. | 延迟的；推迟的；缓期的 | Delayed or postponed to a later time.  |  The meeting has been deferred until next week. 会议推迟到下周举行。  |  postponed, delayed, adjourned | immediate, prompt | adjective, verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They defer the decision until next week. = 他们将决定推迟到下周。
We defer to his expertise on this matter. = 在这件事上我们尊重他的专业知识。


一般过去时:
The judge deferred sentencing until next month. = 法官将宣判推迟到下个月。
She deferred to her father's wishes. = 她顺从了父亲的意愿。


现在完成时:
They have deferred the project due to budget constraints. = 由于预算限制，他们已经推迟了项目。
He has deferred his college application. = 他已经推迟了他的大学申请。


过去完成时:
They had deferred the announcement until after the election. = 他们在选举之后才宣布。
She had deferred to her supervisor's judgment. = 她一直以来都尊重她主管的判断。


将来时:
They will defer the payment until the next quarter. = 他们将付款推迟到下一季度。
We will defer to the experts' opinions. = 我们将尊重专家的意见。


虚拟语气:
If they deferred the deadline, we could finish the project on time. = 如果他们推迟截止日期，我们就能按时完成项目。
I wish they had deferred the decision until more evidence was available. = 我希望他们能在有更多证据的情况下再做决定。


被动语态:
The decision was deferred until further notice. = 决定被推迟到另行通知。
The project has been deferred due to unforeseen circumstances. = 项目因不可预见的情况而被推迟。


疑问句:
Was the payment deferred? = 付款被推迟了吗？
Will the meeting be deferred? = 会议会被推迟吗？


否定句:
They did not defer the meeting. = 他们没有推迟会议。
The project was not deferred. = 项目没有被推迟。


PHRASE->
deferred maintenance = 延期维护
deferred compensation = 递延补偿
deferred gratification = 延迟满足
deferred prosecution agreement = 延期起诉协议
deferred action = 缓期执行


词源:
From Old French *deferrer, from Latin differre "to carry apart, differ, postpone," from dis- "apart" + ferre "to carry."
