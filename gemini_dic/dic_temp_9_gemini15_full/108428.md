lawsuits:/ˈlɔːsuːts/| n. |诉讼，诉讼案件|A lawsuit is a legal action started in a court of law by a person or entity (the plaintiff) against another person or entity (the defendant).  It seeks to resolve a dispute or to obtain a legal remedy, such as damages or an injunction.|例句：The company is facing numerous lawsuits over its faulty products. 这家公司因其有缺陷的产品而面临多起诉讼。|例句：She filed a lawsuit against her former employer for wrongful dismissal. 她以不正当解雇为由起诉了她的前雇主。|近义词：litigation, action, case|反义词：settlement (out-of-court agreement)
