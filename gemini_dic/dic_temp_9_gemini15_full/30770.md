cesspit:/ˈsɛspɪt/| n.|污水坑；粪坑；罪恶渊薮|A cesspit is an underground container for the collection of sewage or other waste.  It is often built of brick or stone and is typically covered.  The term can also be used figuratively to refer to a place or situation that is morally corrupt or unpleasant.|The old house had a cesspit overflowing with sewage.|近义词：sewer, latrine, pit, cesspool|反义词：cleanliness, purity
