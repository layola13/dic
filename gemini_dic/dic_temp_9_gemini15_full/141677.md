optogram:/ˈɒptəɡræm/ | n. |视网膜影像；光学影像|A latent image formed on the retina of the eye by the action of light, which can be made visible under certain conditions.  It's a temporary image retained briefly after exposure to light. |The researcher observed the optogram of the test subject after exposure to a bright light. 研究人员观察了受试者在暴露于强光后的视网膜影像。|  photographic image, afterimage | (no direct antonym)|名词
