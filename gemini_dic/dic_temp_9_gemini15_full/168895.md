profession: /prəˈfɛʃən/ | n. |职业，专业 | a job that needs special training or education |  He chose teaching as his profession. 他选择教书作为他的职业。| 近义词：occupation, vocation, career | 反义词：amateurism, hobby


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What profession are you in? = 你从事什么职业？
His profession is medicine. = 他的职业是医生。


一般过去时:
What profession did he have? = 他以前从事什么职业？
Her profession was nursing. = 她以前的职业是护士。


现在完成时:
What profession has she pursued? = 她从事过什么职业？
He has changed profession several times. = 他换过几次职业。


过去完成时:
What profession had he held before retirement? = 他退休前从事过什么职业？
She had already established herself in her profession. = 她已经在自己的职业领域站稳脚跟了。


将来时:
What profession will you choose? = 你将来会选择什么职业？
He will pursue a profession in law. = 他将从事法律职业。


虚拟语气:
If I had chosen a different profession, I would be happier. = 如果我选择一个不同的职业，我会更快乐。
I wish I had chosen a more stable profession. = 我真希望我选择了一个更稳定的职业。


被动语态:
The profession is highly respected. = 这一职业备受尊敬。
This profession is often overlooked. = 这一职业经常被忽视。


疑问句:
What profession are you interested in? = 你对什么职业感兴趣？
What profession does he plan to enter? = 他计划进入哪个行业？


否定句:
That's not a suitable profession for him. = 那不是适合他的职业。
She didn't pursue a profession in science. = 她没有从事科学职业。
