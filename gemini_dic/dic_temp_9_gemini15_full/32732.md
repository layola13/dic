chloroformic:/ˌklɔːrəˈfɔːrmɪk/| adj.|氯仿的；含氯仿的 | Relating to or containing chloroform.  Chloroform is a colorless, sweet-smelling, highly volatile liquid that was once used as an anesthetic.
