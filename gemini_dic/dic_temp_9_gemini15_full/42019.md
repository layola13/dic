costings:/ˈkɒstɪŋz/| n.|成本计算；成本估算|The process of calculating and estimating the costs involved in a project or activity.  It often includes material costs, labor costs, overhead costs, and other relevant expenses.|例句：The project costings were significantly underestimated. 项目成本计算被严重低估了。|例句：Accurate costings are crucial for successful project management. 准确的成本计算对于成功的项目管理至关重要。|近义词：estimation, budgeting, pricing|反义词：N/A
