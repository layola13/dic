retrofitting:/ˌrɛtroʊˈfɪtɪŋ/ | n. |改造，改装，追溯改装 | The act of adding or modifying something to an existing system or structure.  It often refers to adding features or capabilities that were not originally included in the design.  |例句：The building underwent extensive retrofitting to meet modern safety standards. 这座建筑物进行了大规模的改造以符合现代安全标准。

例句：The company is retrofitting its older vehicles with new emission control systems.  该公司正在对其旧车进行改装，安装新的排放控制系统。


近义词：modification, upgrade, renovation, modernization | 反义词：new construction, original design
