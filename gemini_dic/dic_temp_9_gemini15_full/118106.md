menispermic:/ˌmɛnɪsˈpɜːrmɪk/| adj. |月形体的；新月形的；（指植物）有月形种子的| Relating to or characteristic of the genus Menispermum (moonseed), especially having seeds that are crescent-shaped.  The term often describes the shape of something resembling a crescent moon. |The menispermic fruit was easily identifiable by its distinctive curved shape.|近义词：crescent-shaped, lunate|反义词：circular, spherical
