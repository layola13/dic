masquerade:/ˌmæskəˈreɪd/| n. v.| 化妆舞会；伪装；掩饰 | A masquerade is a social gathering where people wear costumes and masks.  It can also refer to the act of concealing one's true identity or nature. |例句：The annual masquerade ball is always a highlight of the social calendar.  每年的化妆舞会都是社交日历上的亮点。

The criminal masterminded a masquerade to evade the police. 罪犯策划了一场伪装来躲避警察。|近义词：costume party, disguise, pretense |反义词：authenticity, genuineness
