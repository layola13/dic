realigns:/ˌriːəˈlaɪnz/ | v. |重新调整，重新排列|To adjust or arrange something again, often to improve its effectiveness or alignment.|例句：The company realigned its marketing strategy to target a younger demographic. 公司重新调整了其营销策略以瞄准更年轻的人群。The therapist helped the patient realign their spine. 治疗师帮助病人重新调整脊椎。|近义词：reorganize, readjust, realign|反义词：disorganize, misalign


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company realigns its strategy every year. = 公司每年都会调整其战略。
He realigns his priorities after a major life event. = 他在经历人生重大事件后会重新调整自己的优先级。

一般过去时:
They realigned the furniture to make more space. = 他们重新摆放了家具以腾出更多空间。
The government realigned its policies after the election. = 政府在大选后重新调整了政策。

现在完成时:
The team has realigned its goals to better meet market demands. = 团队已经重新调整了目标以更好地满足市场需求。
She has realigned her career path after gaining new skills. = 她学习新技能后重新规划了职业道路。

过去完成时:
He had realigned his business plan before presenting it to investors. = 他在向投资者展示商业计划之前已经对其进行了调整。
They had realigned their resources to support the new project. = 他们已经重新分配资源来支持新项目。

将来时:
The company will realign its resources next quarter. = 公司将在下个季度重新调整资源。
We will realign our efforts to achieve our goals. = 我们将重新调整我们的努力以实现我们的目标。

虚拟语气:
If the company realigned its strategy, it might be more successful. = 如果公司重新调整战略，可能会更成功。
I wish I had realigned my priorities earlier. = 我真希望我早点重新调整我的优先级。

被动语态:
The marketing strategy was realigned to focus on digital platforms. = 营销策略已重新调整，重点放在数字平台上。
The budget was realigned to accommodate unexpected expenses. = 预算已重新调整以应付意外支出。

疑问句:

Has the team realigned its objectives? = 团队是否重新调整了其目标？

否定句:
The company did not realign its policies. = 公司没有调整其政策。
He hasn't realigned his focus on the project yet. = 他还没有将注意力重新放在项目上。
