humpbacks:/ˈhʌmpˌbæks/| n. |座头鲸；驼背；隆起部分|A humpback whale is a large baleen whale known for its distinctive humped back and long pectoral fins.  The word can also refer to a physical hump or a raised, uneven surface.|例句：We saw a humpback whale breaching near the coast. 我们在海岸附近看到了一只座头鲸跃出水面。

例句：The road had several humpbacks that made driving difficult. 这条路上有几个驼峰使得驾驶困难。


近义词：whale (for the animal),  protuberance, bulge,  elevation


反义词：concave, depression, flat
