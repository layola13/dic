easy-as-pie:/ˌiːzi æz ˈpaɪ/| adj. |轻而易举的，非常容易的|extremely easy; requiring little effort or skill.  This idiom compares the ease of a task to the ease of eating a pie.|例句：The exam was easy-as-pie.  考试轻而易举。

例句：I thought the puzzle would be difficult, but it was easy-as-pie. 我以为这个谜题很难，但它却轻而易举。


近义词：a piece of cake, simple, effortless, child's play

反义词：difficult, hard, challenging, arduous


词性：形容词
