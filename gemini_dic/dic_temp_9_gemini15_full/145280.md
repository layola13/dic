overborrows:/ˌoʊvərˈbɒroʊz/| v.|过度借贷，借贷过多 |To borrow more money than is prudent or sustainable.  It implies exceeding reasonable limits and potentially leading to financial difficulties. |例句：The company overborrowed and now faces bankruptcy.  这家公司过度借贷，现在面临破产。

例句：He overborrowed on his credit cards and is now deeply in debt. 他信用卡透支太多，现在负债累累。


近义词：overextend, overindebt,  excessively borrow

反义词：underborrow, save, conserve
