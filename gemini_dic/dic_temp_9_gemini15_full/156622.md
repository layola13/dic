pics:/pɪks/ | n. | 图片，照片 | Pictures are visual representations of something.  They can be photographs, drawings, paintings, etc.  | Example: I saw some amazing pics of your trip. 我看到了你旅行的一些很棒的照片。|近义词：photos, images, illustrations |反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The website displays many pics. = 网站上显示许多图片。
These pics show the damage. = 这些图片显示了损坏情况。


一般过去时:
He took some pics of the sunset. = 他拍了一些日落的照片。
She showed me the pics from her vacation. = 她给我看了她度假的照片。


现在完成时:
I have posted all the pics on Instagram. = 我已经把所有的照片都发到Instagram上了。
They have seen the pics already. = 他们已经看过这些照片了。


过去完成时:

将来时:
We will take more pics tomorrow. = 我们明天会拍更多照片。
He will send the pics later today. = 他今天晚些时候会发送这些照片。


虚拟语气:
If I had a better camera, I would take better pics. = 如果我有一个更好的相机，我会拍更好的照片。
I wish I had taken more pics at the party. = 我真希望在派对上多拍些照片。


被动语态:
The pics were taken by a professional photographer. = 这些照片是由一位专业摄影师拍摄的。
The pics are being processed now. = 照片正在处理中。


疑问句:

否定句:
