physiographic:/ˌfɪzioʊˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|自然地理的，地貌的| relating to the physical geography of a region, including its landforms, climate, and vegetation.  The physiographic features of the region include mountains, valleys, and rivers.  这个区域的自然地理特征包括山脉、山谷和河流。|近义词：geographical, topographic |反义词：None (no direct antonym)|形容词
