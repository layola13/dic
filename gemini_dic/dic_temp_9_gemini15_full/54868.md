displeases:/dɪsˈpliːzɪz/| vt.|使不高兴；使不悦|To cause someone to be unhappy or dissatisfied.|His behavior displeases his parents. 他的行为使他的父母不高兴。|The news displeased everyone. 这个消息让每个人都不高兴。| pleases, gratifies | pleases, satisfies


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His attitude displeases me. = 他的态度让我不高兴。
The cold weather displeases many people. = 寒冷的天气让许多人不高兴。

一般过去时:
The results displeased the manager. = 结果让经理不高兴。
Her silence displeased him greatly. = 她的沉默让他非常不高兴。

现在完成时:
His actions have displeased many people. = 他的行为已经让很多人不高兴了。
The delay has displeased the customer. = 延误让顾客不高兴。

过去完成时:
The bad news had displeased her before she even received the call. =  坏消息在她接到电话之前就已经让她不高兴了。
I had displeased my teacher by not handing in my homework on time. = 我因为没有按时交作业而惹老师生气了。


将来时:
I fear that the news will displease them. = 我担心这个消息会让他们不高兴。
The decision will displease many voters. = 这个决定会让许多选民不高兴。

虚拟语气:
I wish my behavior hadn't displeased you. = 我希望我的行为没有让你不高兴。
If I knew my actions would displease him, I wouldn't have done it. = 如果我知道我的行为会让他不高兴，我不会那样做的。

被动语态:
He was displeased by the criticism. = 他对批评感到不高兴。
The company was displeased with the low sales figures. = 公司对低销售额感到不高兴。

疑问句:
Does my request displease you? = 我的请求让你不高兴吗？
Does this outcome displease the stakeholders? = 这个结果让利益相关者不高兴吗？

否定句:
His work doesn't displease his boss. = 他的工作并没有让老板不高兴。
The decision didn't displease everyone. = 这个决定并没有让所有人都不高兴。


PHRASE->
be displeased with = 对...不满意
feel displeased = 感到不快
express displeasure = 表达不满
show displeasure = 表现出不满
cause displeasure = 引起不满
