shaw:/ʃɔː/| n. |肖（姓氏）; 围巾；披肩 |A shaw is a type of garment worn around the shoulders or head, often made of wool or silk.  It's typically lightweight and can be used for warmth or fashion.  The word also functions as a surname. |例句：She wrapped a soft shaw around her shoulders to ward off the chill. 她披上一条柔软的围巾来抵御寒冷。
