glossist:/ˈɡlɒsɪst/| n. |释义学家；注释者|A glossist is a scholar who studies and interprets glosses, which are brief explanatory notes or translations of words or phrases.  They often work with ancient texts or languages, providing context and understanding for difficult or obscure passages. |例句：The glossist meticulously analyzed the marginal notes in the medieval manuscript.  释义学家仔细分析了中世纪手稿中的旁注。|近义词：commentator, annotator, interpreter |反义词：N/A
