lusitanian:/ˌluːsɪˈteɪniən/| adj.|卢西塔尼亚的；葡萄牙的|Of or relating to Lusitania, a Roman province in the Iberian Peninsula that roughly corresponds to modern Portugal.  The term is also used to describe things associated with Portugal.|The Lusitanian language was the ancestor of modern Portuguese. |近义词：Portuguese|反义词：None
