saliency:/ˈseɪliənsi/| n. |显著性，突出性，重要性 |The saliency of an object refers to its prominence or noticeable quality in relation to its surroundings.  It can be determined by various factors, including color, size, contrast, and motion.  In cognitive psychology, saliency plays a crucial role in attention and visual perception, as it determines which objects or features capture our attention first. |The red car stood out due to its saliency against the gray background.  红色汽车在灰色背景下因其显著性而脱颖而出。 The saliency of the problem prompted immediate action. 问题的突出性促使立即采取行动。| prominence, importance, significance, conspicuousness | insignificance, obscurity, triviality |名词
