king:/kɪŋ/| n.|国王，君主|A male monarch; the ruler of a kingdom.  Kings typically inherit their position through hereditary succession, though some may ascend to the throne through other means.|Example: The king ruled the kingdom with an iron fist. 国王铁腕统治王国。|Example:  King Henry VIII was a powerful and controversial figure in English history.  亨利八世是英国历史上一个强势且有争议的人物。|近义词：monarch, sovereign, ruler|反义词：subject, peasant
