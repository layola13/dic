omniparous:/ɒmˈnɪpərəs/| adj.| 杂食的，什么都吃的 |  Eating or feeding on both animal and vegetable substances.  |Example: Pigs are omniparous animals.  猪是杂食动物。|近义词：omnivorous|反义词：herbivorous, carnivorous|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bear is omniparous and eats both fish and berries. = 熊是杂食动物，既吃鱼也吃浆果。
Pigs are omniparous animals and will eat almost anything. = 猪是杂食动物，几乎什么都吃。


一般过去时:
Dinosaurs were omniparous in many cases. = 恐龙在很多情况下是杂食性的。
That species was considered omniparous until recent studies. = 那个物种直到最近的研究才被认为是杂食性的。


现在完成时:
Scientists have discovered that this bird species has become omniparous. = 科学家们已经发现这个鸟类物种已经变成了杂食性。
Researchers have proven that the panda's diet is more omniparous than previously thought. = 研究人员已经证明，熊猫的饮食比以前认为的更杂食性。

Before the study, it had been assumed the animal was strictly herbivorous, but it turned out to be omniparous. = 在研究之前，人们认为这种动物是严格的食草动物，但事实证明它是杂食性的。
