jugata:/dʒuˈɡɑːtə/| n.|策略，策略性行动|A jugata is a strategic move or series of moves in a game, especially chess, designed to achieve a specific advantage or goal.  It often involves a complex interplay of pieces and positions, and requires foresight and planning.|Example:  The grandmaster's jugata involved sacrificing a pawn to gain control of the center of the board.  大师的策略包括牺牲一个兵来控制棋盘中心。|近义词：strategy, tactic, maneuver, gambit|反义词：randomness, haphazardness|名词
