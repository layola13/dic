self-begotten:/ˌselfˈbɛɡətən/| adj.| 自生的；自行产生的 |  Originating or produced independently of any external cause or influence; self-created.  The term often implies a divine or supernatural origin. |例句：In some mythological systems, the world is described as self-begotten, springing forth from chaos.  在某些神话体系中，世界被描述为自生的，从混沌中涌现出来。

例句：The theory posits a self-begotten universe, originating from a singularity. 这个理论假设了一个自生的宇宙，起源于一个奇点。


近义词：self-generated, spontaneous, innate, inherent | 反义词：created, begotten, manufactured, external
