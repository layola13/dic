maternalism:/ˈmætərnlɪzəm/| n. |母性；母爱；母权制；母性统治|The belief in or practice of protecting or advancing the interests of women, especially by the state.  It often involves policies aimed at supporting mothers and children.  The term can be used positively to describe caring and protective actions, or negatively to imply excessive control or paternalism.|例句：The government's policies demonstrate a strong commitment to maternalism. 政府的政策体现了对母权制的强烈承诺。

The rise of maternalism in the early 20th century led to significant social reforms. 20 世纪初母权制的兴起导致了重大的社会改革。|近义词：motherhood,  protectionism (in a social context), nurturing|反义词：neglect, abandonment, patriarchy
