shapers:/ˈʃeɪpərz/| n.|塑造者；形成者；造型师|People or things that shape something; those who influence or mold something.  Examples include individuals, events, or even ideas that significantly affect the form, nature, or development of something else. |例句：The early pioneers were important shapers of the American West. 早期拓荒者是美国西部重要的塑造者。


The economic forces are powerful shapers of modern society. 经济力量是现代社会强大的塑造者。


近义词：formers, molders, influencers, designers|反义词：destroyers, dismantlers|名词
