chariotlike:/ˈtʃæriətlaɪk/| adj.|战车似的，像战车的|Resembling a chariot; having characteristics of a chariot, such as grandeur, speed, or power.  The word evokes images of ancient warfare, swift movement, and sometimes opulent design.|例句：The massive, ornate carriage looked chariotlike in its grandeur.  这辆巨大的华丽马车在其宏伟中显得像战车一样。

The general's swift and decisive advance was described as a chariotlike charge.将军迅速而果断的推进被形容为战车般的冲锋。|近义词：chariot-shaped, majestic, powerful, swift|反义词： pedestrian, slow, humble, insignificant
