revere:/rɪˈvɪər/| vt.|尊敬，敬畏|To regard with respect, reverence, or awe.|例句：People revere the Dalai Lama for his wisdom and compassion.  人们敬畏达赖喇嘛的智慧和慈悲。|例句：She is revered as a saint. 她被尊为圣人。|近义词：honor, venerate, adore, respect|反义词：despise, scorn, disdain|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They revere their ancestors. = 他们尊敬他们的祖先。
We revere the national flag. = 我们敬畏国旗。

一般过去时:
The people revered the king. = 百姓敬畏国王。
She revered her grandmother's wisdom. = 她敬畏她祖母的智慧。

现在完成时:
We have revered this tradition for centuries. = 我们几个世纪以来一直尊崇这个传统。
They have revered him as a hero since his death. = 自他去世以来，人们一直把他尊为英雄。

过去完成时:
They had revered the old gods before the new religion arrived. = 在新宗教到来之前，他们一直敬畏旧神。
We had revered him as a great leader. = 我们曾把他尊为伟大的领袖。

将来时:
Future generations will revere his achievements. = 后代将敬仰他的成就。
People will continue to revere his memory. = 人们将继续敬仰他的记忆。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would revere the power of nature. = 如果我是你，我会敬畏大自然的力量。
I wish people would revere the truth more. = 我希望人们更敬畏真理。

被动语态:
He was revered by all his followers. = 他受到所有追随者的敬仰。
The ancient temple was revered by the local community. = 这座古老的寺庙受到当地社区的敬仰。

疑问句:
Do they revere their leaders? = 他们尊敬他们的领导人吗？
Why do people revere this historical figure? = 为什么人们敬仰这位历史人物？

否定句:
They do not revere empty promises. = 他们不敬畏空洞的承诺。
He did not revere authority. = 他不敬畏权威。


PHRASE->
