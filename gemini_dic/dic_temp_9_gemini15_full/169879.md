prosocoelia: /ˌproʊsoʊˈsiːliə/ | n. | 前腔|  A type of insect wing venation characterized by the presence of a single, closed cell in the anterior portion of the wing. This cell is typically large and prominent. The term is used primarily in entomological classifications and studies. | The prosocoelia of the butterfly wing was particularly striking.  蝴蝶翅膀上的前腔特别醒目。|  (暂无近义词，该词较为专业) | (暂无反义词，该词较为专业) | 名词
