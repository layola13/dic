paradise:/ˈpærədaɪs/ | n. |天堂；乐园；极乐世界 |A paradise is a place or state of perfect happiness, beauty, or peace.  It often refers to Heaven in religious contexts, but can also describe any idyllic or delightful place. |例句：The tropical island was a paradise.  热带岛屿是人间天堂。

例句：She described her childhood home as a paradise. 她把她童年的家形容成天堂。


近义词：heaven, Eden, utopia, Elysium |反义词：hell, nightmare, dystopia |词性：名词
