exorable:/ˈɛksərəbəl/| adj.|不可避免的；不可阻挡的；恳求无效的 |  Relentless; unstoppable; incapable of being persuaded or moved by entreaty.  The inexorable march of time cannot be stopped. 时间的无情流逝无法阻止。  The inexorable rise of the sea level poses a threat to coastal communities. 海平面上升的不可阻挡之势对沿海社区构成威胁。|近义词：inevitable, unavoidable, relentless, implacable |反义词：avoidable, preventable, persuadable
