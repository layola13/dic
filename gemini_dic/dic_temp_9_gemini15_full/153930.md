peregrinate:/ˈpɛrəɡrɪneɪt/| v.|周游，旅行 | To travel from place to place, especially on foot; to wander.  |例句：He spent his youth peregrinating across Europe. 他在欧洲各地游历了他的青春岁月。
例句：The monk peregrinated through the countryside, spreading his message of peace.  这位僧侣游历乡村，传播他的和平信息。|近义词：travel, journey, roam, wander, ramble |反义词：stay, remain, settle
