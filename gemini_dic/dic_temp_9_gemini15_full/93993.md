impairment's:/ɪmˈpɛərmənts/| n.|损伤；损害；缺陷|The plural form of "impairment," referring to a weakening or damage, especially in physical or mental abilities.  It denotes a reduction or loss of function.|例句：His visual impairments made reading difficult.  他的视力障碍使阅读变得困难。

例句：The accident resulted in several long-term impairments. 这起事故导致了几项长期的损伤。


近义词：deficiency, disability, handicap, defect, damage

反义词：improvement, enhancement, strengthening


词性：名词(复数)
