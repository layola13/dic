mobilizes:/ˈmoʊbəlaɪzɪz/ | vt. | 动员；调动；组织；使…活动 | To assemble or prepare (as troops) for action or use; to rouse to action.  | The general mobilized his troops for the attack. 将军动员了他的军队准备进攻。The government mobilized resources to fight the epidemic. 政府动员资源来抗击疫情。| rally, muster, activate | demobilize, disband | 动词
