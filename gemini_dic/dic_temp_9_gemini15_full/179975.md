refile:/riːˈfaɪl/ | vt. |重新提交；再次归档 |To file again; to submit (a document, etc.) again for consideration or processing.  |例句：The judge ordered the lawyer to refile the motion with the correct supporting documents.  法官命令律师用正确的辅助文件重新提交动议。

例句：We need to refile the tax return because of an error. 我们需要因为一个错误重新提交纳税申报单。


近义词：resubmit, re-submit, re-present


反义词：discard, withdraw


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refiles his documents every month. 他每月都重新整理他的文件。
They refile the reports after review. 他们审查后重新提交报告。


一般过去时:
She refiled the application after corrections. 她修改后重新提交了申请。
He refiled the lawsuit last week. 他上周重新提起诉讼。


现在完成时:
I have refiled the documents three times already. 我已经重新提交了三次文件了。
They have refiled their appeal. 他们已经重新提出上诉。


过去完成时:
Before the deadline, she had refiled all the necessary paperwork. 在截止日期前，她已重新提交所有必要的文书工作。
They had refiled the claim several times without success. 他们已经多次重新提出索赔，但没有成功。


将来时:
We will refile the appeal next week. 我们下周将重新提出上诉。
He will refile the complaint if necessary.如有必要，他会重新提出投诉。


虚拟语气:
If I had known about the error, I would have refiled the report immediately. 如果我知道这个错误，我会立即重新提交报告。
I wish I had refiled my taxes earlier. 我希望我早点重新报税。


被动语态:
The documents were refiled after being reviewed. 文件在审查后被重新提交。
The case will be refiled in the higher court. 此案将在高级法院重新提起诉讼。


疑问句:
Has the application been refiled? 申请已经重新提交了吗？
Will the documents be refiled today? 文件今天会重新提交吗？


否定句:
He did not refile the lawsuit. 他没有重新提起诉讼。
The company refused to refile the claim.  公司拒绝重新提出索赔。
