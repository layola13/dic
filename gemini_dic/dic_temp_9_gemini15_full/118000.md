memorials:/məˈmɔːriəlz/| n.|纪念物，纪念活动|Things that serve as reminders of a person, event, or thing, especially one that is no longer alive or no longer exists.  They can be physical objects, ceremonies, or other forms of remembrance.|例句：The war memorial stands as a tribute to those who lost their lives.  战争纪念碑是为了纪念那些失去生命的人而设立的。

例句：The family held a memorial service to celebrate the life of their deceased grandmother.  这个家庭举行了一场追悼会来纪念他们去世的祖母。


近义词：monuments, tributes, commemorations|反义词：forgetfulness, oblivion|词性:名词
