personifiant:/pɜːˈsɒnɪfaɪənt/| adj.|拟人化的；赋予人格的|Giving human qualities or characteristics to something that is not human, such as an object, animal, or idea.  It often involves representing abstract concepts or inanimate objects as having human-like emotions, thoughts, and actions.|例句：The poem uses personifiant language to describe the wind as a playful child. 这首诗用拟人化的语言将风描述成一个顽皮的孩子。|近义词：anthropomorphic, personified|反义词：dehumanized, depersonalized
