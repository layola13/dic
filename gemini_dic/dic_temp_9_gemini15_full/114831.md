mammalian:/mæˈmeɪliən/| adj. n.|哺乳动物的；哺乳动物|Belonging to or characteristic of mammals.  A mammal.|例句：Humans are mammalian species. 人类是哺乳动物物种。

The study of mammalian behavior is fascinating.  对哺乳动物行为的研究很有趣。

近义词：mammal (as a noun),  乳汁哺育的

反义词：oviparous (egg-laying),  无乳腺动物的


词源:
From Middle English *mammalian, from Late Latin mammāliānus, from Latin mamma ("breast, udder").  The suffix -ian indicates "relating to" or "characteristic of".

用法说明:

作为形容词:  The term "mammalian" describes something related to or characteristic of mammals. It can modify nouns to indicate a connection to the class Mammalia.

作为名词:  While less common, "mammalian" can be used as a noun, referring to a mammal.  This usage is less formal than simply using the word "mammal".
