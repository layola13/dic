ventilation:/ˌvɛntɪˈleɪʃən/| n. |通风，换气|The process of replacing or exchanging air within a space.  This is typically done to remove foul air and introduce fresh air.  Good ventilation is essential for health and safety.|例句：The building has excellent ventilation. 这栋建筑通风良好。|例句：Poor ventilation can lead to the buildup of harmful gases. 通风不良会导致有害气体积聚。|近义词：airing, aeration, draft|反义词：suffocation, stagnation
