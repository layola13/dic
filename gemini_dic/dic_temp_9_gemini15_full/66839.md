expatriate:/ˈɛkspætrieɪt/| n. v. |侨民；移居国外者；驱逐；使移居国外 |A person who lives in a foreign country.  To banish or exile someone. |例句：Many expatriates live in Paris. 许多侨民住在巴黎。 He was expatriated for his political views.  他因政治观点而被驱逐出境。|近义词：emigrant, exile, migrant |反义词： repatriate, immigrant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He expatriates himself to avoid political persecution. 他移居国外以逃避政治迫害。
She expatriates to find better job opportunities. 她移居国外寻找更好的工作机会。


一般过去时:
The government expatriated the dissidents. 政府驱逐了异议人士。
He expatriated himself to Canada after the revolution.  革命后，他移居加拿大。


现在完成时:
She has expatriated to several countries in her career.  在她职业生涯中，她已移居多个国家。
He has expatriated himself from his homeland for over a decade. 他离开祖国移居国外已经超过十年了。


过去完成时:
They had expatriated themselves before the war broke out.  战争爆发前他们就已经移居国外了。
She had expatriated to the US before she met her husband. 在她遇到丈夫之前，她已经移居美国了。


将来时:
He will expatriate to Australia next year. 他明年将移居澳大利亚。
She will expatriate herself to escape the harsh winter. 她将移居国外以逃避严酷的冬天。


虚拟语气:
If I were to expatriate, I would choose a country with a warm climate. 如果我要移居国外，我会选择一个气候温暖的国家。
I wish I could expatriate to a peaceful island. 我希望我能移居到一个宁静的岛屿。


被动语态:
Many political activists were expatriated during the regime.  在那个政权统治期间，许多政治活动家被驱逐出境。
He was expatriated for his involvement in the coup. 他因参与政变而被驱逐出境。


疑问句:
Will he expatriate to another country? 他会移居到另一个国家吗？
Has she expatriated yet? 她已经移居国外了吗？


否定句:
He did not expatriate himself despite the threats.  尽管受到威胁，他也没有移居国外。
She will not expatriate to a country where she doesn't speak the language. 她不会移居到一个她不会说当地语言的国家。
