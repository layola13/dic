owl:/aʊl/| n.|猫头鹰|A nocturnal bird of prey with a large head, forward-facing eyes, and a hooked beak.|例句：The owl silently flew through the night.  猫头鹰悄无声息地飞过夜空。|例句：We heard the hooting of an owl in the forest. 我们在森林里听到了猫头鹰的叫声。|近义词：bird of prey, raptor|反义词：无
