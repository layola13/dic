hypogeum:/haɪˈpɒdʒiəm/| n. |地下墓穴；地下室|An underground chamber, especially one used as a tomb.  Hypogeums are often characterized by their subterranean nature and their use for burial or other sacred purposes. They can vary greatly in size and complexity, ranging from simple rock-cut chambers to elaborate structures with multiple rooms and intricate carvings. |The Hypogeum of Hal-Saflieni in Malta is a fascinating example of a prehistoric underground complex.  马耳他的哈尔·萨夫列尼地下墓穴是一个迷人的史前地下建筑群的例子。|catacomb, tomb, sepulchre|aboveground, surface structure|名词
