sequels:/ˈsiːkwəlz/ | n. |续集，后续篇 | A sequel is a book, film, play, or other piece of work that continues the story of a previous one.  It often features the same characters and setting. | Example:  The movie was a huge success, so they decided to make a sequel. 这部电影非常成功，所以他们决定拍续集。

The sequel to the popular novel is eagerly awaited.  备受期待的热门小说续集即将问世。|近义词：continuation, follow-up |反义词：prequel (前传)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The author is writing sequels to his popular series. 作者正在创作他热门系列的续集。
Many sequels are produced after a successful film.  许多成功的电影之后都会制作续集。

一般过去时:
The studio released three sequels to the original movie.  制片厂发行了这部原创电影的三部续集。
They produced several sequels during the 1990s.  他们在20世纪90年代制作了几部续集。


现在完成时:
The writer has completed two sequels to her best-selling novel.  这位作家已经完成了她畅销小说的两部续集。
They have announced plans for several more sequels. 他们已经宣布了制作更多续集的计划。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, they had finished filming the sequels. 到年底，他们已经完成了续集的拍摄。
Before the new movie, they had released two successful sequels. 在新电影上映之前，他们已经发行了两部成功的续集。


将来时:
The director will film the sequels next year.  导演将在明年拍摄续集。
They will probably release the sequels in the coming months.他们可能会在接下来的几个月里发行续集。


虚拟语气:
If they made another sequel, I would definitely watch it. 如果他们再拍一部续集，我一定会去看。
I wish they would make a sequel to that fantastic movie. 我希望他们能拍那部精彩电影的续集。


被动语态:
