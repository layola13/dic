cyclostyle:/ˈsaɪkləʊstaɪl/| n.|轮转印刷机；轮转印刷|A cyclostyle is a method of duplicating documents using a stencil and a rotating drum.  It was a common method of reproduction before the widespread use of photocopying.|The teacher used a cyclostyle to create copies of the exam for her students.|近义词：mimeograph|反义词：None.
