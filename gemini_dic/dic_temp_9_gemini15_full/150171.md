例句：Her performance was full of panache and flair. 她的表演充满了炫耀和天赋。|近义词：flair, style, dash, verve|反义词：dullness, drabness, mediocrity

词源:
From French *panache*, meaning "plume," "tuft of feathers," ultimately from a Late Latin word of similar meaning.  The meaning evolved from a literal feather plume to a metaphorical sense of showy style and flair.
