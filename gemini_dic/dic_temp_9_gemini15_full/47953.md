decided:/dɪˈsaɪdɪd/| adj. v.|已决定的；决定了的|past tense and past participle of decide|例句：The committee decided to postpone the meeting.  委员会决定推迟会议。

The jury decided he was guilty. 陪审团裁定他有罪。


近义词：determined, resolved, settled|反义词：undecided, unsettled|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He decides quickly. 他决定得很快。
She decides what to wear every morning. 她每天早上决定穿什么。

一般过去时:
They decided to go to the beach yesterday. 他们昨天决定去海滩。
I decided against buying the car. 我决定不买那辆车。

现在完成时:
We have decided to move. 我们已经决定搬家。
She has decided on her college major. 她已经决定了她的大学专业。

过去完成时:
He had decided to quit his job before he received the promotion.  在他升职之前，他已经决定辞职了。
They had decided to leave before the storm hit.  暴风雨来临之前，他们已经决定离开。

将来时:
I will decide later. 我稍后会决定。
They will decide on a date for the wedding next week. 他们下周将决定婚礼日期。

虚拟语气:
If I had decided differently, my life would be very different now. 如果我当时决定不同，我的生活现在就会大不一样了。
I wish I had decided to go to the party. 我真希望我当时决定去参加聚会。

被动语态:
It was decided that the project would be cancelled.  项目被决定取消。
The date of the meeting was decided last week. 会议日期上周就决定了。

疑问句:
Have you decided yet? 你决定了吗？
What did they decide to do? 他们决定做什么？

否定句:
I haven't decided what to do. 我还没决定做什么。
They didn't decide to come. 他们决定不来了。
