lapsing:/ˈlæpsɪŋ/ | v. |失效；终止； lapsed |  停止；失效；流逝 |The insurance policy will lapse if the premiums aren't paid. = 如果不支付保费，保险单将失效。|His membership lapsed due to non-payment. = 由于未付款，他的会员资格失效了。|cease, expire, terminate | renew, continue, maintain | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The software license lapses next month. = 软件许可证下个月到期。
He lapses into silence after each question. = 每个问题之后，他都沉默不语。


一般过去时:
The agreement lapsed after five years. = 协议五年后失效。
She lapsed into unconsciousness after the accident. = 事故后她昏迷不醒。


现在完成时:
My subscription has lapsed.  = 我的订阅已经过期了。
His concentration has lapsed several times during the meeting. = 会议期间，他几次都走神了。


过去完成时:
The warranty had lapsed before the product malfunctioned. = 产品出现故障之前，保修期已经过了。
He had lapsed into a coma before the doctors arrived. = 医生到达之前，他已经陷入昏迷。


将来时:
The contract will lapse at the end of the year. = 合同将在年底到期。
He will likely lapse back into his old habits. = 他很可能会回到他的老习惯。


虚拟语气:
If the deadline lapses, we'll be in trouble. = 如果截止日期过了，我们将陷入困境。
I wish my memory didn't lapse so often. = 我希望我的记忆力不会这么差。


被动语态:
The project was lapsed due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，该项目被终止。
His membership was lapsed for non-payment. = 他的会员资格因未付款而失效。


疑问句:
Has your driver's license lapsed? = 你的驾照过期了吗？
When does the offer lapse? = 此优惠何时失效？


否定句:
The agreement didn't lapse until next year. = 协议直到明年才失效。
His attention didn't lapse during the presentation. = 他在演示过程中没有走神。


PHRASE->

lapse into:  陷入（某种状态）
lapse of time: 时间的流逝
lapse in judgment: 判断失误
lapse from grace: 失宠；堕落
a lapse in security: 安全漏洞
