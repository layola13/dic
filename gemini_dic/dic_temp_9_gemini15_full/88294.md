hip-joints:/ˈhɪpˌdʒɔɪnts/| n.|髋关节|The hip joints are the ball-and-socket joints connecting the thigh bone (femur) to the pelvis.  They allow for a wide range of motion, including flexion, extension, abduction, adduction, internal rotation, and external rotation.  Problems with the hip joints can cause pain and limited mobility.|例句：The doctor examined his hip joints to determine the source of his pain.医生检查了他的髋关节以确定他疼痛的来源。

例句：  She injured her hip joints during a fall. 她摔倒时伤到了髋关节。


近义词：pelvic joints, coxal joints|反义词：无|词性：名词
