pluralistic: /ˌplʊərəlˈɪstɪk/ | adj. |多元化的；多元主义的 |  Characterized by the acceptance or inclusion of many different viewpoints or systems of belief.  It emphasizes the existence of multiple perspectives and the value of diversity. |


例句：

Our society is becoming increasingly pluralistic, with a wide range of cultures and beliefs represented.  我们的社会正变得越来越多元化，代表着各种各样的文化和信仰。

The university promotes a pluralistic approach to education, encouraging students to explore diverse ideas and perspectives. 这所大学提倡多元化的教育方法，鼓励学生探索不同的思想和观点。


近义词：multicultural, diverse, inclusive, eclectic

反义词：homogeneous, uniform, monolithic


词性：形容词
