rebanish:/rɪˈbænɪʃ/| vt.|重新放逐；再次驱逐|To banish again; to exile a second time.  This word is archaic and rarely used in modern English.|例句：The king, after a period of leniency, decided to rebanish the traitor. 国王在一段宽容时期后，决定再次放逐叛徒。

例句：After his initial exile, he was rebanished for further offenses.  在他最初流放之后，由于进一步的罪行，他又被放逐了。


近义词：re-exile, expel again, banish anew
反义词：welcome back, reinstate, pardon


词性：动词
