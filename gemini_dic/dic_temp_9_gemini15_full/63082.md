ensconce:/ɪnˈskɒns/| vt.|安置；使安顿；隐藏；安坐|To settle (someone or something) securely or snugly in a place.  To hide or conceal.|例句：She ensconced herself in a comfortable armchair. 她舒服地坐在扶手椅里。

例句：The family ensconced themselves in their new home.  这个家庭安顿在新家。


近义词：settle, establish, install, lodge, hide, conceal

反义词：expose, reveal, dislodge, unsettle


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ensconces himself in his study every evening. 他每天晚上都把自己安置在书房里。
They ensconce the jewels in a secure vault. 他们把珠宝安置在一个安全的保险库里。


一般过去时:
She ensconced herself in a warm blanket. 她用温暖的毯子把自己裹了起来。
He ensconced the painting behind a curtain. 他把画藏在窗帘后面。


现在完成时:
They have ensconced themselves comfortably in their new lives. 他们已经舒适地安顿在新的生活中。
She has ensconced the secret in her heart. 她把秘密藏在心里。


过去完成时:
He had ensconced himself in his chair before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前，他已经安坐在椅子上了。
She had ensconced the documents in a waterproof bag. 她已将文件藏在一个防水袋里。


将来时:
They will ensconce the baby in its crib for the night. 他们晚上会把婴儿放在婴儿床里。
She will ensconce herself in a quiet corner to read. 她会找个安静的角落看书。


虚拟语气:
If I were to ensconce myself in this matter, I could solve the problem. 如果我能全身心地投入到这件事中，就能解决这个问题。
I wish I could ensconce myself from all this noise. 我希望我能远离所有的噪音。


被动语态:
The treasure was ensconced deep within the mountain. 宝藏被深藏在山中。
The documents were ensconced in a fireproof safe. 文件被安放在一个防火保险箱里。


疑问句:

否定句:

They couldn't ensconce the evidence before the police arrived. 警察到达之前，他们没能藏匿证据。

词源:
The word "ensconce" comes from the Old French word "esconser," meaning "to hide, to shelter."  This in turn derives from the Old French "escon," meaning "secret place, hiding place."  The prefix "en-" adds an intensifying effect.
