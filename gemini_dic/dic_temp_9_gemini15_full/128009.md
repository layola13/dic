nephritis:/nəˈfraɪtɪs/| n. |肾炎|Inflammation of the kidney or kidneys.  Nephritis can be caused by various factors, including infections, autoimmune diseases, and certain medications.  Symptoms can vary but may include pain in the flank, fever, swelling, and changes in urination.  |Example:  The doctor diagnosed him with nephritis after several tests.  医生在做了几次检查后诊断他患有肾炎。

The patient's acute nephritis required immediate hospitalization. 患者的急性肾炎需要立即住院治疗。|近义词：kidney inflammation, renal inflammation |反义词：healthy kidneys, normal kidney function
