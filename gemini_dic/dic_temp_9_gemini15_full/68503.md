fan:/fæn/| n. v.|扇子；狂热爱好者；风扇|A fan is a device used to create airflow, often to cool something or someone.  It can also refer to an enthusiastic admirer of a particular person, team, or activity. |例句：She cooled herself with a hand fan. 她用一把扇子给自己降温。  He's a big fan of the Beatles. 他是披头士乐队的忠实歌迷。|近义词：admirer, enthusiast, devotee, blower|反义词：critic, opponent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fan blows cool air. = 电扇吹出凉风。
He is a big fan of basketball. = 他是篮球的超级粉丝。

一般过去时:
