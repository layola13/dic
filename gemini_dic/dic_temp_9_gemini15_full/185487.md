reup:/riːˈʌp/| v.|重新开始，再次使用，续订|To start or use something again; to renew a subscription or agreement.|例句：We need to reup our gym membership. 我们需要续订健身房会员资格。|例句：Let's reup the contract for another year. 让我们再续签一年的合同。|近义词：renew, restart, resume|反义词：cancel, terminate, end


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reup their magazine subscription every year. = 他们每年都续订杂志订阅。
He reups his commitment to the project. = 他重申了他对项目的承诺。

一般过去时:
She re-upped her contract last week. = 她上周续约了。
We re-upped our supply of coffee beans. = 我们补充了咖啡豆的供应。

现在完成时:
They have re-upped their software licenses. = 他们已经续签了他们的软件许可证。
I have re-upped my commitment to fitness. = 我已经重申了我对健身的承诺。

过去完成时:
She had re-upped her membership before the price increase. = 价格上涨前，她已经续费了。
They had re-upped their supplies before the long trip. = 长途旅行前，他们已经补充了物资。


将来时:
We will reup our insurance policy next month. = 我们下个月将续保。
They will reup their contract with the supplier. = 他们将与供应商续签合同。


虚拟语气:

I wish I had re-upped my gym membership before it expired. = 我真希望我的健身房会员资格到期前我已经续费了。
