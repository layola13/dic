passingly:/ˈpæsɪŋli/| adv.|顺便地，偶然地，短暂地 |In a passing manner; briefly or incidentally.  It suggests something is done without much attention or significance. |例句：He passingly mentioned the project during the meeting. 他在会议上顺便提到了这个项目。  He passingly glanced at the newspaper. 他随意地看了一眼报纸。|近义词：briefly, casually, incidentally, superficially|反义词：thoroughly, extensively, deliberately
