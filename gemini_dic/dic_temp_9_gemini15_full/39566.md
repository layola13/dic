consciousnesses:/ˈkɒnʃəsnɪsɪz/ | n. |意识状态；觉知；知觉 | The state of being aware of and responsive to one's surroundings.  It encompasses self-awareness, thoughts, feelings, and sensations. |例句：Her consciousness returned slowly after the accident. 事故后她的意识慢慢恢复了。|例句：The drug altered her state of consciousness.  这种药物改变了她的意识状态。|近义词：awareness, sentience, perception |反义词：unconsciousness, insensibility
