resubmitted:/ˌriːsəbˈmɪtɪd/| v. |重新提交|The document was resubmitted after the necessary corrections were made.  重新提交了文件，因为已经做了必要的修改。|submit, re-present|withdraw, reject|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They resubmit their applications every year. = 他们每年都重新提交申请。
He resubmits the report if it's not approved. = 如果报告没有被批准，他会重新提交。


一般过去时:
She resubmitted the assignment after the professor's feedback. = 教授给出反馈后，她重新提交了作业。
The company resubmitted the proposal with the updated figures. = 公司用更新的数据重新提交了提案。


现在完成时:
They have resubmitted the proposal twice already. = 他们已经重新提交了提案两次。
She has resubmitted her application for the scholarship. = 她已经重新提交了奖学金申请。


过去完成时:
He had resubmitted the form before the deadline. = 他在截止日期之前已经重新提交了表格。
They had resubmitted their tax returns after discovering an error. = 他们在发现错误后重新提交了纳税申报单。


将来时:
We will resubmit the document once the changes are complete. = 一旦修改完成，我们将重新提交文件。
She will resubmit the application if it is rejected. = 如果申请被拒绝，她将重新提交。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would resubmit my application. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新提交我的申请。

被动语态:

疑问句:
