postencephalitic: /ˌpoʊstɛnsɛfəˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. | 脑炎后的；继发于脑炎的 |  Relating to or denoting the consequences or symptoms that persist after an attack of encephalitis.  This typically refers to the long-term neurological and psychiatric effects that can follow an encephalitis infection.  These effects can be wide-ranging and debilitating. |


例句：

The patient exhibited postencephalitic Parkinsonism, a common sequela of viral encephalitis.  = 病人表现出脑炎后帕金森病，这是病毒性脑炎的常见后遗症。

Postencephalitic symptoms, such as memory loss and behavioral changes, can significantly impact a person's quality of life. = 记忆力丧失和行为改变等脑炎后症状会严重影响一个人的生活质量。


近义词：post-encephalitic (less common variant)


反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑与“pre-encephalitic”(脑炎前的)相对，但意义关联性较弱。


词性：形容词
