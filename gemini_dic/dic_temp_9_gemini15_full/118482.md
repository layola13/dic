mesmerize:/ˈmɛzməraɪz/| vt.|使着迷；使神魂颠倒|to hold the attention of (someone) to the point of fascination|例句：The magician mesmerized the audience with his amazing tricks. 魔术师以他精湛的技巧迷住了观众。
例句：The beautiful scenery mesmerized me.美丽的风景让我着迷。|近义词：hypnotize, captivate, fascinate|反义词：bore, repel, disgust|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music mesmerizes me. = 这音乐让我着迷。
The speaker mesmerizes the audience with his charisma. = 演讲者以其魅力吸引了观众。


一般过去时:
The movie mesmerized us. = 这部电影让我们着迷。
His performance mesmerized the judges. = 他的表演迷住了评委。


现在完成时:
The beauty of nature has mesmerized me for years. = 自然之美多年来一直让我着迷。
His storytelling has mesmerized countless listeners. = 他的故事吸引了无数听众。


过去完成时:
I had been mesmerized by his charm before I realized his true nature. = 在我意识到他的真实本性之前，我已经被他迷住了。
The audience had been mesmerized by the performance before the lights went out. = 灯光熄灭之前，观众已经被表演迷住了。


将来时:
The new exhibit will mesmerize visitors. = 新展览将吸引参观者。
His magic show will mesmerize the children. = 他的魔术表演将让孩子们着迷。


虚拟语气:
If the story were more engaging, it would mesmerize the readers. = 如果故事更引人入胜，它就能吸引读者。
I wish the painting could mesmerize me like it did yesterday. = 我希望这幅画能像昨天一样让我着迷。


被动语态:
The audience was mesmerized by the singer's voice. = 观众被歌手的歌声迷住了。
The viewers were mesmerized by the breathtaking scenery. = 观众被令人叹为观止的景色迷住了。


疑问句:
Does the painting mesmerize you? = 这幅画让你着迷吗？
What mesmerizes you about this book? = 这本书什么地方吸引了你？


否定句:
The movie didn't mesmerize me. = 这部电影没有让我着迷。
The lecture failed to mesmerize the students. = 讲座未能吸引学生。


PHRASE->
