reshoes: /ˌriːˈʃuz/ | vt. |给…换鞋底| To put new soles on (shoes).  This verb refers to the act of replacing the worn-out soles of shoes. It implies a repair or refurbishment rather than simply buying new shoes. |例句：I need to reshoe my hiking boots; the soles are completely worn. = 我需要给我登山靴换鞋底；鞋底完全磨损了。 The cobbler reshoed my favorite shoes, extending their life. = 鞋匠为我心爱的鞋子换了鞋底，延长了它们的寿命。|近义词：resole, repair (shoes)|反义词：discard, throw away (shoes)|动词
