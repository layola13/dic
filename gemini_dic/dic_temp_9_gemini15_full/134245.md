noninfectiousness: /ˌnɒnɪnˈfɛkʃəsnəs/ | n. |非传染性，非感染性 | The state or quality of not being infectious or contagious; the inability to spread from one individual to another.  | Example: The noninfectiousness of the condition was confirmed by several studies.  这项疾病的非传染性已被多项研究证实。 | 近义词: noncontagiousness, noncommunicability | 反义词: infectiousness, contagiousness
