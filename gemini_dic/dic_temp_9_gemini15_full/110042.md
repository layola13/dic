licensee:/ˌlaɪsənˈsiː/| n. |被许可人，受让人 |A person or entity granted a license to use something, such as intellectual property or a business model.  |Example: The licensee is responsible for adhering to the terms and conditions outlined in the license agreement.  被许可人有责任遵守许可协议中规定的条款和条件。|近义词：grantee, permit holder |反义词：licensor
