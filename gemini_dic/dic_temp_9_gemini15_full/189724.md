saltbush:/ˈsɔːltˌbʊʃ/| n. |盐灌木，盐柴|A shrub or small tree that grows in salty or alkaline soil, often having succulent leaves.  Many species are found in arid and semi-arid regions of Australia, and some are used for fodder or have other economic uses.|例句：Many Australian sheep farmers rely on saltbush as a crucial source of fodder for their flocks.  许多澳大利亚养羊的农民依靠盐灌木作为羊群的重要饲料来源。

例句：The saltbush plants thrived in the harsh, salty environment of the coastal plain. 盐灌木植物在沿海平原严酷的盐碱环境中茁壮成长。
