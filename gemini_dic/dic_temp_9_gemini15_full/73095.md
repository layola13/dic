footy:/ˈfʊti/| n.| 足球，澳式足球 | Australian English: a shortened form of "football," often referring specifically to Australian rules football or, less commonly, association football (soccer).  | Example:  He loves playing footy on weekends. 他喜欢在周末踢足球。 |近义词:football,soccer(less common in Australian English)|反义词:None.
