preferred: /prɪˈfɜːrd/ | adj. | 首选的；更喜欢的 |  preferred means more liked or wanted than another or others; favored.  It describes something that is chosen above other options because it is considered better or more suitable. | 例句：His preferred method of transport is cycling. = 他首选的交通方式是骑自行车。  The preferred option is to negotiate a settlement. = 首选方案是协商解决。 | 近义词：favored, chosen, selected, desired, liked best | 反义词：least preferred, disliked, unwanted
