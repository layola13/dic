keesh:/kiːʃ/| n.|基斯犬|A Keeshond is a breed of dog known for its distinctive fluffy, grey coat and expressive face.  They are intelligent, loyal, and make good family companions.  Keeshonds are relatively large dogs, with a thick double coat that requires regular grooming.|Example: My neighbor has a beautiful Keeshond; its coat is so fluffy!  我的邻居有一只漂亮的基斯犬；它的毛非常蓬松！|近义词：Spitz (general term)|反义词：无|名词
