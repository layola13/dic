decaffeinated:/ˌdiːkæfɪˈneɪtɪd/| adj.| 脱咖啡因的；去咖啡因的 |  Having had caffeine removed. This adjective describes substances, especially beverages like coffee or tea, from which caffeine has been extracted.  | Example: I prefer decaffeinated coffee in the evening to avoid sleep disturbances.  晚上我更喜欢喝脱咖啡因咖啡，以免影响睡眠。|近义词： caffeine-free | 反义词：caffeinated
