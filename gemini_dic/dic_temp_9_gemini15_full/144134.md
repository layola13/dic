outsee:/ˌaʊtˈsiː/| v.|  超越，胜过；看得更远 |To surpass in seeing; to have a wider or more extensive view than.  |例句：From the mountaintop, we could outsee the valley below. 从山顶上，我们可以看到山谷下面的景色。|近义词：outdo, surpass, exceed, excel |反义词：undersee (非标准词，表示视野受限)
