go:/ɡoʊ/| /ɡəʊ/ | v. | 去，走；进行；运转；变成 | to move from one place to another; to proceed; to function; to become |

例句：
I go to school every day. = 我每天都去上学。

They go to the park on weekends. = 他们周末去公园。


近义词：proceed, travel, move, depart

反义词：stay, remain, stop


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I go to the gym every Tuesday. = 我每周二都去健身房。
She goes to work by bus. = 她乘公共汽车上班。

一般过去时:
We went to the beach yesterday. = 我们昨天去了海滩。
He went to see a doctor last week. = 他上周去看医生了。

现在完成时:
I have gone to London several times. = 我去过伦敦好几次了。
She has gone to the market. = 她去市场了。

过去完成时:
They had gone home before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经回家了。
He had gone to bed when the phone rang. = 电话响的时候他已经睡觉了。

将来时:
I will go to the concert tomorrow. = 我明天要去看音乐会。
We are going to travel to Paris next year. = 我们明年要去巴黎旅行。

虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would go to the museum. = 如果我有时间，我会去博物馆。
I wish I could go to the party. = 我希望我能去参加派对。

被动语态: (Go本身很少用被动语态，通常用其他动词代替)
The meeting was postponed; it won't go ahead. = 会议被推迟了；它不会继续进行。
The car is going to be repaired. = 这辆车将要被修理。


疑问句:
Where do you go on vacation? = 你去哪里度假？
When does the train go? = 火车几点开？

否定句:
I don't go to the movies often. = 我不常去看电影。
She doesn't go to that restaurant anymore. = 她不再去那家餐馆了。


PHRASE->
go ahead = 开始；继续
go away = 走开
go back = 返回
go out = 出去
go on = 继续
