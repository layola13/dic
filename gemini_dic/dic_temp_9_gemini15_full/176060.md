ravine:/rəˈviːn/| n.|峡谷，沟壑|A ravine is a deep, narrow valley with steep sides, often formed by erosion.  Ravines are typically found in areas with softer rock or soil. |例句：The hikers carefully navigated the narrow ravine.  徒步旅行者小心翼翼地穿过狭窄的峡谷。

例句：A raging river carved a deep ravine into the landscape.  奔腾的河流在景观中刻出了一条深深的峡谷。


近义词：gorge, gully, canyon, chasm|反义词：plain, plateau
