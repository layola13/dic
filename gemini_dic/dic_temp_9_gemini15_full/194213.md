self-love:/sɛlfˈlʌv/| n. |自爱，自我关爱 |The act of loving and accepting oneself, including one's strengths and weaknesses. It involves prioritizing one's well-being and happiness.  Self-love is not selfishness; it's about treating oneself with kindness and respect. |例句：Practicing self-love is crucial for mental health.  练习自爱对心理健康至关重要。

例句：She learned to prioritize self-love after a difficult breakup.  在一段艰难的分手后，她学会了优先考虑自爱。


近义词：self-acceptance, self-esteem, self-compassion |反义词：self-hate, self-loathing, self-deprecation |词性:名词
