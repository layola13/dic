non-agrarian:/ˌnɒnˈeɪɡreəriən/| adj.|非农业的；非耕作的|Not relating to or connected with agriculture.  This term describes things, systems, or societies that are not based on farming or agriculture as their primary economic activity.|The city's economy is largely non-agrarian, relying instead on manufacturing and technology.  这个城市的经济很大程度上是非农业的，而是依靠制造业和技术。|近义词：industrial, urban, non-agricultural|反义词：agrarian, agricultural
