outvoice:/ˌaʊtˈvɔɪs/| vt.|大声说出；压过…的声音|to speak louder than someone else, or to speak so loudly that someone else cannot be heard|例句：He outvoiced the protesters with his powerful microphone. 他用他强大的麦克风压过了抗议者的声音。|例句：She tried to outvoice her doubts, but they still lingered. 她试图大声说出她的疑虑，但它们仍然挥之不去。|近义词：overshadow, drown out|反义词：whisper, murmur|动词
