rambo:/ˈræmboʊ/| n. |兰博，好战的人|A fictional character, a highly skilled and violent soldier, created by David Morrell in his novel "First Blood" and popularized in the subsequent film series.  The name has become synonymous with a brutal, relentless warrior.  The word can also be used to refer to anyone who is excessively aggressive or violent.|例句：He acted like a real rambo, charging into the fight without a plan. 他像个真正的兰博一样，不顾计划地冲进战斗。


例句：The soldiers were criticized for their rambo-style tactics.  士兵们因其兰博式的战术而受到批评。

近义词：warrior, fighter, soldier, brute

反义词：coward, pacifist
