customises:/ˈkʌstəmaɪzɪz/ | vt. |定制，个性化 | To modify (something) to suit a particular individual or task.  |例句：The company customises its software to meet the specific needs of each client.  该公司根据每个客户的具体需求定制其软件。 |例句：She customises her own clothes, adding unique details and embellishments. 她自己定制衣服，添加独特的细节和装饰。|近义词：personalize, tailor, adapt, modify |反义词：standardize, generalize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They customise their products to meet customer demands. = 他们根据客户需求定制产品。
We customise our marketing strategies for each campaign. = 我们为每个活动定制营销策略。


一般过去时:
The artist customised the guitar with intricate carvings. = 艺术家用复杂的雕刻定制了吉他。
They customised the car's interior with leather seats and a sound system. = 他们用真皮座椅和音响系统定制了汽车内饰。


现在完成时:
She has customised her phone with a unique ringtone and wallpaper. = 她已经用独特的铃声和壁纸定制了她的手机。
The company has customised thousands of software packages for its clients. = 该公司已经为其客户定制了数千个软件包。


过去完成时:
He had customised his bike before the race. = 他在比赛前已经定制了他的自行车。
They had customised the website with new features before the launch. = 他们在发布前已经用新功能定制了网站。


将来时:
We will customise the website to improve user experience. = 我们将定制网站以改善用户体验。
They will customise the software to integrate with our existing system. = 他们将定制软件以与我们现有的系统集成。


虚拟语气:
If they customised the product, it would sell better. = 如果他们定制产品，它会卖得更好。
I wish I had customised my resume before applying for the job. = 我希望我在申请工作之前就定制了我的简历。


被动语态:
The software is customised to meet the client's specifications. = 该软件是根据客户的规格定制的。
The website was customised by a professional designer. = 网站是由一位专业设计师定制的。


疑问句:
Do they customise the software for each user? = 他们是否为每个用户定制软件？
Will the car be customised before delivery? = 汽车在交付前会进行定制吗？


否定句:
They do not customise their products for individual customers. = 他们不为个人客户定制产品。
The company did not customise the software as requested. = 公司没有按要求定制软件。
