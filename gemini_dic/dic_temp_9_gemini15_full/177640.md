rebuttable:/rɪˈbʌtəbl/| adj.| 可反驳的，可驳斥的 | Capable of being refuted or disproven.  Something that is rebuttable is not necessarily false, but it can be challenged and potentially shown to be incorrect. | Example: The judge ruled that the evidence presented was rebuttable.  法官裁定提交的证据是可以反驳的。 The defendant's claim of self-defense was rebuttable.  被告的自卫说法是可以反驳的。 |近义词：refutable, challengeable, disputable |反义词：irrefutable, undeniable, incontrovertible
