gothicity:/ɡɒˈθɪsɪti/| n. |哥特式风格；哥特式特征 |The quality or state of being Gothic; the characteristic features of Gothic architecture, literature, or art.  It often evokes a sense of mystery, darkness, and romance. |例句：The novel is infused with a palpable gothicity, evident in its gloomy setting and macabre themes. 这部小说充满了明显的哥特式风格，这在其阴郁的背景和骇人的主题中显而易见。|例句：The building's pointed arches and gargoyles are clear examples of its gothicity. 这座建筑的尖拱和怪兽是其哥特式风格的明显例子。|近义词：Gothicism, darkness, gloom |反义词：Classicism, brightness, cheerfulness |名词
