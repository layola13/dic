only-so-many:/ˈoʊnliˌsoʊˈmɛni/| adj. phrase|仅此而已；有限的|This phrase indicates a limited quantity or number of something. It suggests that there is a restriction or constraint on the available resources or options.  The "only" emphasizes the limited nature, and "so many" specifies a quantity, although not a precise one. |例句：There are only-so-many hours in a day.  一天只有这么多小时。

There are only-so-many seats available for the concert.  音乐会的座位只有这么多。|近义词：limited, restricted, few, scarce|反义词：unlimited, abundant, plentiful, numerous|词性：形容词短语
