nonlegumes:/ˈnɑːnˈliːɡjuːmz/| n.|非豆科植物|Plants that are not legumes; plants belonging to families other than the Fabaceae (Leguminosae).  These plants do not have the characteristic fruit of legumes (pods containing seeds).|Example:  Many nonlegumes rely on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.  许多非豆科植物依靠菌根真菌来吸收养分。|近义词:non-legume plants,  other plants|反义词:legumes, leguminous plants
