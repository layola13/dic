irresistible: /ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəbl/ | adj. | 不可抗拒的，难以抵挡的 |  Unable to be resisted; too tempting to be refused.  |The cake was so irresistible that I ate the whole thing.  这蛋糕太诱人了，我把它全部吃光了。| tempting, alluring, captivating | resistible, resistable, avoidable |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chocolate cake is irresistible. = 这块巧克力蛋糕令人无法抗拒。
His charm is irresistible to women. = 他的魅力对女人来说是无法抗拒的。

一般过去时:
The offer was irresistible. = 这个提议令人无法拒绝。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
