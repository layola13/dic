/ˌpriːdɛprɪˈveɪʃənz/ | n. | 剥夺前，预先剥夺 | The study of predeprivations focuses on the conditions and factors that precede periods of deprivation, aiming to understand their contribution to the subsequent negative consequences.  It examines the vulnerabilities and pre-existing circumstances that might increase susceptibility to the harmful effects of deprivation. |  例句：The research explored the predeprivations experienced by children before entering foster care, such as parental neglect and instability.  研究探讨了儿童进入寄养家庭之前所经历的剥夺前经历，例如父母忽视和不稳定。| 近义词：antecedents, precursors, forerunners | 反义词：post-deprivations, aftermaths
