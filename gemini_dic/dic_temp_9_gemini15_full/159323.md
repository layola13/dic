pointy:/ˈpɔɪnti/| adj.|尖的；尖锐的；尖头的|Having a sharp point or end.  It refers to something that comes to a sharp point,  either literally or figuratively.|例句：The pencil has a pointy tip. 这支铅笔笔尖很尖。

The mountain peaks are pointy and jagged. 山峰尖尖的，参差不齐。|近义词：sharp, pointed, acute, peaked|反义词：blunt, rounded, dull, obtuse
