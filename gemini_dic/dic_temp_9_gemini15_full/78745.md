gigot:/ʒɪˈɡoʊ/| n.|羊腿；（羊腿肉）|A gigot is a leg of mutton (or sometimes lamb).  It's a large cut of meat, typically roasted. |The chef prepared a delicious roasted gigot of lamb.  厨师准备了一道美味的烤羊腿。|leg of lamb, leg of mutton|—|n.


USAGE->

词源:
The word "gigot" comes from Old French "gigot," which means "leg" or "shank."  It ultimately has roots in the Vulgar Latin word *gēnū, meaning "knee." The association with a leg of meat stems from the resemblance of the leg shape to a bent knee.
