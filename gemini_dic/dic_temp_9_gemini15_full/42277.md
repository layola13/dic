counter-earth:/ˈkaʊntərɜːrθ/| n.| 对地球| A hypothetical celestial body on the opposite side of the sun from Earth, sometimes proposed in various cosmological theories.  It's often associated with ancient myths and pseudoscientific ideas, and there is no scientific evidence of its existence.|Example: The idea of a counter-earth was once popular in some ancient cultures, but it's not supported by modern astronomy.|近义词：anti-earth (less common)|反义词：Earth
