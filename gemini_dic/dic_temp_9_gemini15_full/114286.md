mainly:/ˈmeɪnli/ | adv. | 主要地，大体上 | chiefly, primarily, mostly |  The project is mainly focused on improving customer service.  这个项目主要集中在改善客户服务上。  The children mainly played outside. 孩子们主要在外面玩耍。 |  secondarily, minimally |  less importantly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mainly works from home. 他主要在家工作。
She mainly speaks English. 她主要说英语。

一般过去时:
They mainly traveled by train. 他们主要乘火车旅行。
He mainly ate vegetables last week. 他上周主要吃蔬菜。

现在完成时:
We have mainly focused on our studies. 我们主要专注于学习。
She has mainly worked on this project. 她主要从事这个项目。

过去完成时:
They had mainly finished the work before the deadline. 他们在截止日期前主要完成了工作。
He had mainly relied on his parents for support. 他主要依靠父母的支持。

将来时:
I will mainly be working on the report next week. 我下周主要会处理报告。
They will mainly use public transport. 他们主要会使用公共交通工具。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would mainly focus on my hobbies. 如果我有更多时间，我会主要专注于我的爱好。
I wish I could mainly spend my time reading. 我希望我主要能把时间花在阅读上。

被动语态:
The work is mainly done by volunteers. 这项工作主要由志愿者完成。
The decisions are mainly made by the management team. 这些决定主要由管理团队做出。

疑问句:
What does he mainly do for a living? 他主要靠什么谋生？
What do they mainly discuss in their meetings? 他们在会议上主要讨论什么？

否定句:
He doesn't mainly work with computers. 他不是主要用电脑工作。
She doesn't mainly shop online. 她不是主要在网上购物。
