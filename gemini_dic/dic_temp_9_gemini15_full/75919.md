funfair:/ˈfʌnfeə(r)/| n. |游乐场，娱乐场 |A funfair is a temporary fair providing entertainment and amusements, typically including rides, games, and food stalls.  It's often associated with festive occasions. |例句：The children had a wonderful time at the funfair.孩子们在游乐场玩得很开心。 The annual town funfair draws large crowds.  一年一度的城镇游乐场吸引了大量人群。|近义词：fairground, carnival, amusement park |反义词：None specifically.
