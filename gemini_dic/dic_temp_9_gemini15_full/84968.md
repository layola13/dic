happed:/ˈhæpənd/| v.|发生，碰巧|To occur; to take place; to come to pass by chance.  |例句：Something unexpected happened.  发生了一些意想不到的事情。

例句：It happened that I was there at the time.  碰巧那时我在那里。


近义词：occurred, transpired, took place| 反义词：planned, designed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Something happens every day. =每天都会发生一些事情。
It often happens that he is late. = 他经常迟到。


一般过去时:
What happened yesterday? = 昨天发生了什么事？
The accident happened quickly. = 事故发生得很快。


现在完成时:
Many things have happened since I last saw you. =自从我上次见到你以来，发生了很多事情。
Nothing much has happened. = 没有发生什么大事。


过去完成时:
I had just arrived when it happened. = 我刚到就发生了那件事。
Before I knew it, it had already happened. = 我还没反应过来，事情就已经发生了。


将来时:
What will happen next? =接下来会发生什么？
I think something bad will happen. = 我认为将会发生不好的事情。


虚拟语气:
If it happened again, I would be very angry. = 如果它再次发生，我会非常生气。
I wish that hadn't happened. = 我希望那件事没有发生。


被动语态:
The accident was happened near the bridge. =事故发生在桥附近。(注意：happen通常不用于被动语态，此句语法略有不妥，更自然的表达是 The accident happened near the bridge.)
It is said that a miracle happened. =据说发生了奇迹。


疑问句:
What happened? = 发生了什么事？
When did it happen? = 它是什么时候发生的？


否定句:
Nothing happened. = 什么事也没发生。
It didn't happen as planned. =事情没有按计划进行。


PHRASE->
as it happened = 碰巧
what happened to…? = …怎么了？
it so happened that… = 碰巧…
happen to… = 偶然…
by chance/happenstance = 偶然，碰巧
