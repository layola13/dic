nonpercipient: /ˌnɒnˈpɜːsɪpɪənt/ | adj. | 无感知的，无知觉的 | Lacking perception or awareness; unable to perceive.  The word highlights a lack of sensory awareness or understanding. | Example:  The nonpercipient machine could not register the subtle changes in temperature. 这台无感知的机器无法记录温度的细微变化。|近义词:unaware, insensitive, unconscious, unfeeling | 反义词:percipient, aware, sentient, perceptive
