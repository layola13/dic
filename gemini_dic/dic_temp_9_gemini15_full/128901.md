nimbus:/ˈnɪmbəs/| n.|光环；雨云；气氛|A nimbus is a rain cloud, or a luminous cloud or radiance surrounding a deity or saint, often depicted in art.  It can also refer to a distinctive atmosphere or aura surrounding a person or thing.|例句：A dark nimbus hung over the mountains.  乌云笼罩着群山。|例句：The saint was depicted with a nimbus around his head. 圣人画像中头顶环绕着光环。|近义词：halo, aura, atmosphere|反义词：clarity, brightness
