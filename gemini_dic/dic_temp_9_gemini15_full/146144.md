overencouraged: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈkɜːrɪdʒd/ | adj. | 过度鼓励的，士气过高的 |  Excessively encouraged; overly stimulated or motivated, often to the point of being detrimental.  This implies an encouragement that has gone beyond a healthy or productive level, potentially leading to unrealistic expectations, recklessness, or even failure. |

例句：

The overencouraged team took unnecessary risks, ultimately leading to their defeat.  =  过度自信的团队冒了不必要的风险，最终导致了他们的失败。

The overencouraged child believed he could achieve anything without effort. =  过度被鼓励的孩子相信自己不费吹灰之力就能做到任何事。


近义词：overconfident, overly optimistic, inflated,  exuberant (in a negative context)


反义词：discouraged, undermotivated, pessimistic


词性：形容词
