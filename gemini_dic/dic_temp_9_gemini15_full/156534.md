pick-me-ups: /ˈpɪk mi ʌps/ | n. |提神的东西；令人振奋的事物 |Something that improves your mood or gives you more energy.  It refers to something that temporarily boosts one's spirits or energy levels, often after feeling down or tired. |例句：A cup of coffee is my morning pick-me-up.  一杯咖啡是我早晨的提神饮品。

例句：After a long day, a hot bath is a great pick-me-up. 漫长的一天后，洗个热水澡是很好的放松方式。


近义词：boost, stimulant, tonic, refresher, energizer

反义词：depressant, downer

词性: 名词
