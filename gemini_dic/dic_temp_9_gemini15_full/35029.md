/kləʊz/ | /kloʊz/ | adj. | 关闭的；靠近的；密切的 | Closed means not open.  It can also describe things that are near or have a close relationship. | 例句：The door is closed. 门是关着的。 The shops are closed on Sundays. 周日商店关门。|近义词：shut, near, intimate | 反义词：open, distant, far

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shop is closed. = 商店关门了。
The flowers are closed at night. = 花晚上闭合了。

一般过去时:
The restaurant closed early. = 餐厅早早关门了。
He closed the book and went to bed. = 他合上书去睡觉了。

现在完成时:
They have closed the factory. = 他们已经关闭了工厂。
The wound has closed nicely. = 伤口已经很好地愈合了。

过去完成时:
The shop had closed before we arrived. = 我们到达之前商店已经关门了。
She had closed her eyes before the impact. = 撞击发生前她已经闭上了眼睛。

将来时:
The school will close for the holidays next week. = 学校下周将因假期而关闭。
They will close the deal tomorrow. = 他们明天将达成协议。


虚拟语气:
If the weather were closed in, we wouldn't go hiking. = 如果天气很糟糕，我们就不去远足了。
I wish the store was closed later in the evening. = 我希望商店晚上关门更晚一些。


被动语态:
The road was closed due to the accident. = 道路因事故而关闭。
The case will be closed soon. = 此案很快就会结案。


疑问句:
Is the bank closed? = 银行关门了吗？
Are the borders closed to tourists? = 边境对游客关闭了吗？


否定句:
The door isn't closed properly. = 门没有关好。
The deal isn't closed yet. = 这笔交易还没有完成。
