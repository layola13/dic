determent:/dɪˈtɜːrmənt/| n. |威慑；阻止；制止|The act of deterring or the state of being deterred; something that deters.  Determent refers to the action or process of discouraging someone from doing something, often through the threat of negative consequences. It can also refer to the thing that discourages that action.|例句：The heavy prison sentences served as a determent to potential criminals.  严厉的监禁判决对潜在罪犯起到了威慑作用。

The high cost of the project proved a significant determent.  项目的巨额成本证明是一个重要的制止因素。|近义词：discouragement, prevention, inhibition|反义词：encouragement, incitement, stimulation|
