couped:/kuːpt/| v.|政变，突然罢免|To overthrow or depose suddenly and forcibly, especially a ruler.|The rebels couped the dictator and established a new government.  反叛者发动政变推翻了独裁者，建立了一个新政府。|近义词：overthrow, oust, depose|反义词：install, enthrone|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He coups the government. 他发动政变推翻政府。
They coup the president. 他们发动政变推翻总统。


一般过去时:
The general couped the king. 将军发动政变推翻了国王。
The rebels couped the government. 反叛者发动政变推翻了政府。


现在完成时:
The army has couped the president. 军队已经发动政变推翻了总统。
They have couped the ruling party. 他们已经发动政变推翻了执政党。


过去完成时:
The people had couped the emperor before the revolution.  人民在革命前已经发动政变推翻了皇帝。
The rebels had couped the prime minister before the announcement. 反叛者在宣布之前已经发动政变推翻了首相。


将来时:
The opposition will coup the government. 反对派将发动政变推翻政府。
They will coup the current leader. 他们将会发动政变推翻现任领导人。


虚拟语气:
If they couped the dictator, peace might return. 如果他们推翻独裁者，和平可能会回归。
I wish they had couped the corrupt regime sooner. 我希望他们早点推翻腐败政权。


被动语态:
The president was couped in a military takeover. 总统在军事政变中被推翻。
The government was couped overnight. 政府在一夜之间被推翻。


疑问句:
Was the king couped by his own advisors? 国王是被他自己顾问推翻的吗？
Will the government be couped? 政府会被推翻吗？


否定句:
The rebels did not coup the government. 反叛者没有发动政变推翻政府。
They could not coup the established power.  他们无法推翻既定的权力。
