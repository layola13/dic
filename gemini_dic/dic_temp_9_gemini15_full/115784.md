marionette:/ˌmærɪəˈnet/| n. |木偶；傀儡；受操纵的人|A marionette is a puppet controlled by strings or wires.  It is often used figuratively to describe someone who is manipulated by others. |The puppeteer skillfully manipulated the marionette, making it dance and leap.|puppet, doll|independent, autonomous|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The puppeteer controls the marionette. = 木偶师操纵着木偶。
That marionette dances beautifully. = 那只木偶跳得很漂亮。


一般过去时:
He pulled the strings of the marionette. = 他拉动木偶的线。
The children watched the marionette show. = 孩子们观看了木偶戏。


现在完成时:
They have made several marionettes. = 他们已经制作了好几个木偶。
She has never seen such a lifelike marionette. = 她从未见过如此栩栩如生的木偶。


过去完成时:
The show had already begun when we arrived.  = 我们到达时演出已经开始了。(This example uses "show" but can be applied to a marionette show.)
The child had broken his favorite marionette. = 孩子已经弄坏了自己最喜欢的木偶。


将来时:
He will make a new marionette next week. = 他下周将做一个新的木偶。
The theater will present a marionette performance. = 剧院将上演木偶戏。


虚拟语气:
If I had a marionette, I would put on a show. = 如果我有一个木偶，我会上演一场戏。
I wish I could operate that marionette. = 我真希望我能操纵那个木偶。


被动语态:
The marionette was controlled by strings. = 木偶由细线控制。
The marionette is being repaired. = 木偶正在修理。


疑问句:
Is that a marionette? = 那是木偶吗？
Where did you buy the marionette? = 你在哪里买的木偶？


否定句:
That is not a marionette; it's a puppet.  = 那不是木偶；那是木偶。
He didn't like the marionette show. = 他不喜欢木偶戏。


PHRASE->
marionette theater = 木偶剧院
string marionette = 线控木偶
shadow marionette = 影子木偶
water marionette = 水偶
glove marionette = 手套木偶


词源:
The word "marionette" comes from the French word "marionnette,"  ultimately derived from the name "Marie," a common name often given to puppets in the 17th and 18th centuries.  The diminutive suffix "-ette" indicates smallness or a reduced form.
