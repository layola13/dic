sensualism:/ˈsɛnʃuəlɪzəm/| n. |感觉论；感官享乐主义|The doctrine that all knowledge is derived from sensory experience.  It emphasizes the importance of sensory experience in understanding the world and forming beliefs.  Sensualism can also refer to excessive indulgence in sensual pleasures. |例句：The painting was criticized for its blatant sensualism. 这幅画因其赤裸裸的感官享乐主义而受到批评。

例句：His philosophy was a form of sensualism, believing that all knowledge came from the senses. 他的哲学是一种感觉论，认为所有知识都来自感官。


近义词：hedonism, pleasure-seeking |反义词：asceticism, puritanism


词源：From  Latin *sensualis (“of the senses”), from sensus (“sense”).  The suffix "-ism" denotes a doctrine, system, or practice.
