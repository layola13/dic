firebomber: /ˈfaɪərˌbɒmbər/ | n. |纵火犯；燃烧弹投掷者 | A person who uses firebombs to commit arson or other acts of violence.  A firebomber is someone who deliberately sets fires using firebombs. They are often involved in acts of terrorism, vandalism, or protest. |例句：The firebomber was apprehended by the police after setting fire to the government building.  = 纵火犯在纵火焚烧政府大楼后被警方逮捕。

The police are investigating the motive of the firebomber. = 警方正在调查纵火犯的动机。|近义词：arsonist, incendiary, terrorist (depending on context)|反义词：firefighter, rescuer
