portrayer:/pɔːrˈtreɪər/ | n. | 画像者；描绘者；扮演者 | A person who portrays something or someone, such as an actor portraying a character or an artist portraying a scene.  | Example: The actor was a masterful portrayer of Shakespearean characters.  这位演员是莎士比亚戏剧人物的杰出扮演者。  |近义词:actor, artist, interpreter, depictor | 反义词: N/A | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The actor portrays the king in the play. = 演员在剧中扮演国王。
The artist portrays the landscape beautifully. = 艺术家美丽地描绘了风景。


一般过去时:
He portrayed the villain convincingly. = 他令人信服地扮演了反派。
She portrayed a strong female character. = 她扮演了一个坚强的女性角色。


现在完成时:
The artist has portrayed many famous people. =这位艺术家已经描绘了许多名人。
He has portrayed a variety of roles throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中扮演过各种各样的角色。


过去完成时:
The painter had portrayed the scene before the storm. = 画家在暴风雨来临之前已经描绘了这个场景。
She had portrayed the character many times before. = 她之前已经扮演过很多次这个角色了。


将来时:
The actress will portray a complex character in the new film. = 女演员将在新电影中扮演一个复杂的角色。
He will portray the hero's journey in his next novel. = 他将在他的下一部小说中描绘英雄的旅程。


虚拟语气:
If he were to portray Hamlet, he would need extensive training. = 如果他要扮演哈姆雷特，他需要大量的训练。
I wish I could portray such a powerful character. = 我希望我能扮演这样一个强大的角色。


被动语态:
The character was masterfully portrayed by the actor. = 演员出色地扮演了这个角色。
The scene was portrayed with great detail. = 场景被细致地描绘出来。


疑问句:
Who will portray the lead role? = 谁将扮演主角？
How will the artist portray the subject's emotions? = 艺术家将如何描绘主体的感情？


否定句:
He did not portray the character accurately. = 他没有准确地扮演这个角色。
The painting did not portray the landscape realistically. = 这幅画没有真实地描绘风景。


PHRASE->
portray a character = 扮演一个角色
portray a scene = 描绘一个场景
