messes up: /ˈmɛsɪz ʌp/ | v. |搞砸，弄糟，弄乱 | To make something untidy or disordered; to spoil or ruin something; to make a mistake. | 例句：He messed up the project by missing the deadline. 他因为错过最后期限而搞砸了项目。  He messed up his chances of getting the job by being late for the interview. 他因为面试迟到而毁掉了得到这份工作的机会。|近义词：ruin, spoil, botch, screw up | 反义词：improve, fix, perfect |
