butting:/ˈbʌtɪŋ/| v.|碰撞；顶撞；干扰；插嘴|The act of striking or pushing with the head or horns, or of forcefully interrupting or interfering.  It can also refer to the act of repeatedly hitting something.|例句：The rams were butting heads in a fierce contest.  公羊们在激烈的争斗中互相顶撞。

例句：He kept butting into our conversation. 他不断插嘴我们的谈话。|近义词：ramming, jostling, interrupting, interfering|反义词：yielding, cooperating, assisting
