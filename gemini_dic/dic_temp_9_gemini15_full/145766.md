overcuring:/ˌoʊvərˈkjʊrɪŋ/| n.|过度硫化，过度固化|The process of curing something for too long, resulting in undesirable changes in its properties.  This is often seen in the context of rubber or plastics, where excessive curing can lead to brittleness and loss of elasticity.|例句：Overcuring the rubber led to cracks appearing in the finished product. 过度硫化橡胶导致成品出现裂纹。|例句：The paint was overcured, resulting in a brittle and easily chipped finish. 油漆过度固化，导致漆膜变脆，容易剥落。|近义词：overheating, overprocessing|反义词：undercuring
