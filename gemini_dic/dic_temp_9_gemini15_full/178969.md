rededications:/ˌriːˌdɛdɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n.|重新奉献；再献身；重新致力于|The act of rededicating something; renewing a commitment or dedication.  It often implies a renewed sense of purpose or commitment after a period of inactivity or waning enthusiasm. |例句：The ceremony included rededications of several historical landmarks.  重新奉献典礼包括对几个历史地标的重新奉献。

The rededication of the park was attended by hundreds of people. 公园的重新启用仪式吸引了数百人参加。|近义词：recommitment, reaffirmation, reconsecration|反义词：abandonment, neglect, desecration|名词
