rationed:/ˈræʃənd/| vt.|配给，限量供应|To restrict the amount of something that can be used or obtained.|During the war, sugar was rationed.  战争期间，糖是配给的。|The government rationed fuel during the crisis. 政府在危机期间对燃料实行配给。|allocate, allot, distribute|unrestricted, unlimited|verb


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The government rations fuel in times of shortage. = 政府在燃料短缺时实行配给。
They ration food to the refugees. = 他们向难民配给食物。

一般过去时:
They rationed gasoline during the oil crisis. = 他们在石油危机期间对汽油实行配给。
The authorities rationed bread to prevent shortages. = 当局配给面包以防止短缺。

现在完成时:
The country has rationed essential goods for months. = 这个国家已经对必需品实行配给数月了。
We haven't rationed anything yet, but we might have to. = 我们还没有对任何东西实行配给，但我们可能不得不这样做。


过去完成时:
They had rationed food for weeks before the supplies arrived. = 在物资到达之前，他们已经对食物实行配给数周了。
The city had already rationed water before the drought. = 在干旱发生之前，城市就已经对水实行配给。


将来时:
The government will likely ration electricity if the power plant fails. = 如果发电厂发生故障，政府可能会对电力实行配给。
We will ration food if the storm lasts longer. = 如果暴风雨持续时间更长，我们将对食物实行配给。


虚拟语气:
If the government rationed resources more effectively, there would be less waste. = 如果政府更有效地配给资源，浪费就会减少。
I wish they had rationed the tickets better, so many people missed out. = 我希望他们能更好地配给门票，这样就不会有那么多人错过了。


被动语态:
Fuel was rationed throughout the country. = 全国各地都对燃料实行配给。
Food supplies were rationed to ensure fair distribution. = 对食品供应实行配给以确保公平分配。


疑问句:
Were goods rationed during the last war? = 上次战争期间是否有对商品实行配给？
Will water be rationed this summer? = 今年夏天会对水实行配给吗？


否定句:
They didn't ration sugar during peacetime. = 和平时期他们没有对糖实行配给。
The government has not yet decided whether to ration gasoline. = 政府尚未决定是否对汽油实行配给。
