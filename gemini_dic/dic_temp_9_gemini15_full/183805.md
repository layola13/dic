research:/rɪˈsɜːrtʃ/| n. v.| 研究；调查；探究 | The systematic investigation into and study of materials and sources in order to establish facts and reach new conclusions.  |例句：The scientist conducted extensive research on the effects of climate change.  这位科学家对气候变化的影响进行了广泛的研究。

例句：His research has earned him international recognition. 他的研究为他赢得了国际认可。


近义词：investigation, study, inquiry, exploration | 反义词：neglect, ignorance


词性：名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I research the history of ancient civilizations. = 我研究古代文明的历史。
They research new materials for construction. = 他们研究用于建筑的新材料。


一般过去时:
She researched the topic thoroughly before writing her paper. =  她在写论文之前彻底研究了这个主题。
We researched various options before making a decision. = 我们在做决定之前研究了各种方案。


现在完成时:
Scientists have researched the causes of the disease for years. = 科学家们多年来一直在研究这种疾病的病因。
He has researched extensively in the field of artificial intelligence. = 他在人工智能领域进行了广泛的研究。


过去完成时:
They had researched the market before launching their product. = 他们在推出产品之前已经对市场进行了调查。
She had researched the subject extensively before giving her presentation. =  她在做报告之前已经广泛研究了这个主题。


将来时:
I will research the effects of pollution on marine life. = 我将研究污染对海洋生物的影响。
They will research the feasibility of the project before starting it. = 他们将在启动项目之前研究项目的可行性。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would research the topic further. = 如果我有更多时间，我会进一步研究这个主题。
I wish I had researched the market before investing. = 我希望在投资之前我已经对市场进行了调查。


被动语态:
The experiment was researched by a team of scientists. = 这个实验是由一个科学家团队进行研究的。
The new drug is being researched by a pharmaceutical company. =  这种新药正在由一家制药公司进行研究。


疑问句:
What are you researching at the moment? = 你现在正在研究什么？
Have they finished researching the data yet? = 他们是否已经完成了数据的分析？


否定句:
They did not research the problem adequately. = 他们没有充分研究这个问题。
She hasn't researched the subject properly. = 她没有正确地研究这个主题。
