preinvests: /ˌpriːɪnˈvɛsts/ | v. | 预先投资，提前投资 | To invest in something before it is fully developed or before it is needed.  This often involves taking on some risk in anticipation of future returns. | 例句：The company preinvests heavily in research and development to maintain its competitive edge. = 这家公司大力预先投资研发以保持其竞争优势。

The venture capitalist preinvests in promising startups, hoping for significant returns later. = 风险投资家预先投资有前景的初创企业，希望以后获得可观的回报。


近义词：invest beforehand, prefund, frontload


反义词：post-invest, delay investment


词性：动词
