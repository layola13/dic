pertinate:/ˈpɜːrtɪneɪt/| v.| 坚持；固执； pertinacity |  坚持不懈; 固执; 执着 |To persist in a course of action, belief, or feeling, especially in the face of opposition or difficulty.  |例句：He pertinated in his refusal to cooperate, despite the warnings. 他坚持拒绝合作，尽管受到了警告。 |例句：Despite the evidence against her, she pertinated in her claim of innocence.  尽管有证据反对她，但她坚持声称自己无罪。|近义词：persist, persevere, insist |反义词：yield, give in, relent
