recantingly:/rɪˈkæntɪŋli/| adv.| 悔过地；撤回声明地；勉强地 |  In a manner that shows repentance or a withdrawal of a statement.  It implies reluctance or hesitation. | Example: He recantingly admitted his involvement in the crime. 他悔过地承认了他参与了犯罪。

Example: She recantingly agreed to help, clearly unhappy about the decision. 她勉强同意帮忙，显然对这个决定很不高兴。


近义词:reluctantly, unwillingly, hesitantly, grudgingly

反义词:willingly, eagerly, enthusiastically

词性:副词
