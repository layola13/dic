indices:/ɪnˈdaɪsiːz/| n.|索引；指标；指数|The plural of index.  Indices are used to represent a collection of things, such as data points or entries in a database. They can also refer to mathematical indices, such as exponents or subscripts. |例句：The indices of the stock market fluctuated wildly. 股市指数剧烈波动。|例句：The book has a detailed index and indices of names and subjects. 这本书有详细的索引以及姓名和主题索引。|近义词：indexes, indicators, exponents|反义词：无
