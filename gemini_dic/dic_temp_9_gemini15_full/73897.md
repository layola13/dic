forsaker:/fərˈseɪkər/| n. |抛弃者；遗弃者 |One who forsakes; one who abandons or deserts.  A forsaker is someone who has left someone or something behind, often in a way that is considered to be heartless or irresponsible. |Example: He was a forsaker of his family and his responsibilities. 他是抛弃家庭和责任的人。|近义词: deserter, abandoner, leaver |反义词: supporter, protector, caregiver |名词

词源:
The word "forsaker" comes from the verb "forsake," which is of Old English origin.  "Forsake" combines "for-" (meaning "completely") and "sake" (meaning "to leave behind," related to the word "seek").  Therefore, "forsaker" literally means "one who completely leaves behind."
