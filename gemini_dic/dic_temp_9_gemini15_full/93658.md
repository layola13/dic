imbrue:/ɪmˈbruː/| vt.|弄湿；浸透；沾染|to wet or soak thoroughly; to stain or dye deeply; to saturate or imbue (with a feeling, quality, etc.)|例句：The rain imbrued his clothes. 雨水把他的衣服淋湿了。

The battlefield was imbrued with blood. 战场血流成河。|近义词：soak, saturate, stain, dye|反义词：dry, dehydrate

USAGE->

The rain imbrues the ground. = 雨水浸湿了地面。
He imbrues his hands in the paint. = 他把双手浸在油漆里。
