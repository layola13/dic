inaffected:/ˌɪnəˈfɛktɪd/| adj.|未受影响的；自然的；不矫揉造作的|Unaffected means not influenced or changed by something; natural and sincere; not artificial or pretentious.  It often describes a person's behavior or style as genuine and straightforward, lacking affectation.|例句：He maintained an unaffected manner despite the pressure. 他尽管承受着压力，却保持着自然的态度。

例句：Her unaffected charm won everyone over. 她毫不做作的魅力赢得了所有人的好感。

近义词：natural, genuine, sincere, unaffected, artless, unpretentious|反义词：affected, pretentious, artificial, contrived


词源:  The word "unaffected" is formed from the prefix "un-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "affected," which derives from the past participle of the verb "affect" (to influence or produce a change in).  The root of "affect" ultimately traces back to the Latin verb "afficere," meaning "to do to, to affect."
