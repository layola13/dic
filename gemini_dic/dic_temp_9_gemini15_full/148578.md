overtalking:/ˌoʊvərˈtɔːkɪŋ/ | n. | 喋喋不休；抢话；打断别人说话 | The act of talking excessively or interrupting others.  It can be a sign of nervousness, excitement, or a lack of awareness of others' conversational space.  |例句：Her overtalking made it difficult for others to contribute to the meeting. 她的喋喋不休使得其他人很难在会议上发言。


例句：The constant overtalking during the debate made it hard to follow the arguments.  辩论中不断的抢话使得很难跟上论点的进展。


近义词：interrupting, dominating the conversation, rambling, chattering, babbling |反义词：listening attentively, being silent, letting others speak


词性：名词
