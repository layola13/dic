hangworthy: /ˈhæŋˌwɜːrði/ | adj. | 值得吊死的；应受绞刑的 | Deserving of hanging; worthy of death by hanging.  This word is rarely used in modern English and carries a strong connotation of extreme disapproval and punishment. It implies a crime so severe that the death penalty is justified. |  The sentence was so harsh, some considered it hangworthy.  = 判决如此严厉，一些人认为罪犯应受绞刑。  |  capital, deserving death |  innocent, pardonable |  形容词
