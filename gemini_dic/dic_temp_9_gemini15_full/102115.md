isotonically:/ˌaɪsoʊˈtɑːnɪkli/ | adv. | 等渗地；等张地 |  Maintaining the same osmotic pressure as the surrounding solution.  This term is primarily used in the context of solutions, particularly in medicine and biology, describing a solution that has the same solute concentration as another solution, preventing osmosis.  | Example: The intravenous fluid was administered isotonically to avoid cell damage.  输液以等渗的方式进行，以避免细胞损伤。 |近义词:isosmotically | 反义词: hypertonically, hypotonically
