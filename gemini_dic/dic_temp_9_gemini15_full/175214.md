raided:/ˈreɪdɪd/ | vt. | 袭击；搜查；突然袭击 | To make a sudden attack on (a place) , especially by a group of people, typically in order to steal something or cause damage.  |例句：The police raided the house and found drugs.  警察突袭了那栋房子并找到了毒品。

例句：Bandits raided the village and stole all the food.  土匪袭击了村庄，偷走了所有的食物。


近义词：attack, assault, invade, search


反义词：defend, protect


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police raid suspicious locations regularly. = 警察定期突袭可疑地点。
He raids the refrigerator every night. = 他每晚都突袭冰箱。


一般过去时:
The rebels raided the town last night. = 反叛军昨晚袭击了该镇。
They raided the museum and stole a priceless painting. = 他们洗劫了博物馆，偷走了一幅无价的画。


现在完成时:
The thieves have raided several houses in the neighborhood. = 小偷已经洗劫了附近几户人家。
The army has raided the enemy's base multiple times. = 军队已经多次袭击了敌人的基地。


过去完成时:
The police had raided the building before the suspect arrived. = 嫌犯到达之前，警方已经搜查了那栋建筑。
They had raided the bank just before the alarm went off. = 警报响起前他们刚刚抢劫了银行。


将来时:
The government will raid the suspected terrorist hideout tomorrow. = 政府将在明天突袭可疑的恐怖分子藏匿处。
We will raid the cake for dessert after dinner. = 晚饭后我们将会“袭击”蛋糕作为甜点。


虚拟语气:
If they had raided the warehouse earlier, they would have caught the smugglers. = 如果他们早点突袭仓库，就能抓住走私犯了。
I wish the police had raided the building sooner. = 我真希望警察早点搜查那栋楼。


被动语态:
The building was raided by the police. = 那栋建筑被警方突袭了。
The bank was raided last night. = 银行昨晚被抢劫了。


疑问句:
Was the house raided by the police? = 那栋房子被警察搜查了吗？
Have they raided the office yet? = 他们已经搜查了办公室了吗？


否定句:
The police didn't raid the building. = 警察没有搜查那栋建筑。
They haven't raided the warehouse yet. = 他们还没有突袭仓库。


PHRASE->
raid on = 对…的袭击
