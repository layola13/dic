geometrician:/ˌdʒiˈɒmɪtrɪʃn/|/ˈdʒiːɒmɪtrɪʃən/| n. |几何学家|A geometrician is a mathematician specializing in geometry.  They study the properties and relationships of points, lines, surfaces, solids, and higher-dimensional analogs.  Their work can involve both theoretical research and practical applications in fields like computer graphics, engineering, and architecture.|Example: Euclid is considered one of the most important geometricians in history.  欧几里德被认为是历史上最重要的几何学家之一。|近义词:geometer, mathematician|反义词:None
