outdriving:/ˌaʊtˈdraɪvɪŋ/| v. |超越；胜过；超过|The act of surpassing or exceeding something, typically in terms of performance or achievement. It implies a significant degree of superiority.|例句：His outdriving on the golf course was remarkable. 他在高尔夫球场上的击球距离非常出色。

例句：The new car's performance outdrives its predecessor in every aspect. 这款新车的性能在各个方面都超越了其前代产品。


近义词：outshining, surpassing, exceeding, outperforming | 反义词：underperforming, lagging behind
