mosis:/ˈmoʊsɪs/| n. |渗透；透过|The process by which molecules of a solvent tend to pass through a semipermeable membrane from a less concentrated solution into a more concentrated one, thus equalizing the concentrations on each side of the membrane.|例句：Osmosis is a crucial process in plant cells, allowing them to absorb water.  渗透作用是植物细胞中至关重要的过程，使它们能够吸收水分。|例句：Reverse osmosis is a technology used to purify water. 反渗透技术用于净化水。|近义词：diffusion, permeation |反义词：active transport
