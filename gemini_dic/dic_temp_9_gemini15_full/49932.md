dendriform: /ˈdɛndrɪfɔːrm/ | adj. |树枝状的，树状的 | Resembling the branching form of a tree; tree-like in form.  The term is used to describe structures that have a branching pattern similar to that of a tree's branches, often with many small branches extending from larger ones. |例句：The dendriform crystals were beautiful and intricate.  树枝状的晶体美丽而复杂。

The lightning's dendriform pattern etched itself into the night sky.  闪电的树枝状图案刻印在夜空中。

近义词：arborescent, treelike, ramified, branched | 反义词：unbranched, linear, simple
