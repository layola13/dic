keenly:/ˈkiːnli/| adv. |热切地；敏锐地；强烈地 |Intensely; eagerly; sharply.  It describes the manner in which something is done or felt, emphasizing intensity and focus.  |例句：She keenly awaited the results of the exam. 她热切地等待着考试结果。

例句：He keenly felt the loss of his friend. 他强烈地感受到了失去朋友的痛苦。


近义词：eagerly, intensely, sharply, avidly

反义词：indifferently, apathetically, sluggishly


词源:  Derived from the adjective "keen," ultimately from Old English *cēne "bold, brave, sharp," related to Old High German *cheini "brave, bold," and ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kauniz-.  The adverbial suffix "-ly" was added to create "keenly."
