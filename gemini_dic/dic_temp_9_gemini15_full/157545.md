pit-a-patted:/pɪt ə pætɪd/| v. | 轻轻地、快速地跳动；怦怦乱跳 | To beat or flutter rapidly and lightly, as a heart does.  It is often used to describe the sound of something tapping lightly and repeatedly. |例句：My heart pit-a-patted when I saw him.  我的心跳加速当我看到他。
The rain pit-a-patted against the windowpane. 雨点轻轻地敲打着窗玻璃。|近义词：flutter, throb, patter |反义词：thump (a heavier, more forceful sound) |动词
