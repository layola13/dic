nonbelligerents:/ˌnɒn bəˈlɪdʒərənts/| n. |非交战国，中立国 |People or nations that are not involved in a war.  Non-belligerents remain neutral during times of conflict. |Example: Switzerland remained a nonbelligerent throughout both World Wars. 瑞士在两次世界大战中都保持中立。  Many countries declared themselves nonbelligerents during the Cold War. 许多国家在冷战期间宣布自己是中立国。|近义词：neutrals, unengaged parties|反义词：belligerents, combatants|名词
