outgaze:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪz/| vt.|凝视，注视|To look at someone or something for a long time, especially with a particular feeling or expression.|例句：She outgazed the setting sun, lost in thought. 她凝视着落日，陷入沉思。

例句：He outgazed her with an expression of disbelief. 他难以置信地凝视着她。


近义词：gaze at, stare at, contemplate, regard|反义词：ignore, disregard|动词
