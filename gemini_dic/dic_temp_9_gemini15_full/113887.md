madrases:/məˈdrɑːsəz/| n.| मदरसा (阿拉伯语) | Islamic schools, especially in the Middle East and South Asia.  These schools typically provide religious instruction alongside secular subjects. |例句：Many madrasas in Pakistan offer a blend of religious and secular education. 巴基斯坦许多 मदरसा 提供宗教和世俗教育的融合。|近义词：Islamic school, religious school, seminary |反义词：secular school

Many madrasas offer religious instruction. = 许多 मदरसा 提供宗教指导。
The madrasa teaches students Arabic. = 这所 मदरसा 教学生阿拉伯语。
