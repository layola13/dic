damp:/dæmp/| adj., n.|潮湿的；湿气|slightly wet, often in a way that feels unpleasant or unhealthy.  It can refer to a state of being slightly wet or to the moisture itself.|例句：The basement was damp and smelled musty.  地下室潮湿，闻起来很霉。

The air was damp with the morning mist.  空气中弥漫着清晨的薄雾，潮湿湿的。|近义词：moist, humid, soggy|反义词：dry, arid
