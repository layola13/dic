pachacuti:/ˌpɑːtʃəˈkuːti/| n. |帕查库特克（印加帝国的统治者）|Pachacuti Inca Yupanqui (c. 1438 – 1471) was the ninth Sapa Inca of the Inca Empire.  He is credited with transforming the Inca from a small regional power into the vast empire that conquered much of the Andes region. His reign was characterized by significant military expansion, administrative reforms, and infrastructural developments.| 例句：Pachacuti's reign marked a turning point in Inca history. 帕查库特克的统治标志着印加历史的转折点。| 近义词：Inca emperor, ruler, sovereign | 反义词：subject, ruled
