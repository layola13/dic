extumescence:/ɛkstuˈmɛsns/| n. |肿胀；膨胀；突出 |The swelling or protrusion of a part of the body, often due to inflammation or congestion.  It can also refer to the act of swelling or protruding.  |例句：The extumescence of his ankle was a clear sign of a sprain.  他的脚踝肿胀是扭伤的明显迹象。

The extumescence of the volcano threatened nearby villages. 火山的隆起威胁着附近的村庄。
