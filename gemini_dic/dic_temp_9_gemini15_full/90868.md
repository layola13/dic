hunk:/hʌŋk/| n. |大块；大块头；英俊男子 |A hunk is a large piece of something, especially food. It can also refer to a large, strong, and attractive man.  |例句：He carved a hunk of cheese. 他切了一大块奶酪。
He's a real hunk. 他真是个大帅哥。|近义词：chunk, lump, Adonis, stud |反义词：crumb, little, unattractive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cuts a hunk of meat. = 他切了一大块肉。
She eats a hunk of bread. = 她吃了一大块面包。

一般过去时:

将来时:
