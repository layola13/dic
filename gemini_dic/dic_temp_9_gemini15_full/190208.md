sapid:/ˈseɪpɪd/| adj.|美味的，可口的，有趣的|Having an agreeable taste or flavor; savory; pleasing to the palate.  It can also refer to something intellectually stimulating or interesting.|例句：The chef prepared a sapid dish that delighted all the guests.  厨师准备了一道美味佳肴，让所有客人都很高兴。

The sapid lecture captivated the audience.  那场引人入胜的演讲迷住了听众。|近义词：delicious, savory, flavorful, piquant, stimulating|反义词：tasteless, bland, insipid, dull

词源:
From Latin *sapidus ("savory, tasteful"), from sapere ("to taste").  The word ultimately derives from Proto-Indo-European *sap- ("to taste").
