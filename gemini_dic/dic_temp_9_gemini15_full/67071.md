exploder:/ɪkˈspləʊdər/| n. |爆炸装置；爆炸者|A device or person that causes an explosion.  An exploder can refer to a detonator, a person who sets off explosives, or a device that causes a sudden and dramatic increase in something.|例句：The bomb squad carefully deactivated the exploder. 爆炸物处理小组小心地拆除了爆炸装置。  The accidental exploder was found to be a careless worker. 事故的肇事者被发现是一个粗心的工人。|近义词：detonator, blaster, saboteur|反义词：deactivator, neutralizer
