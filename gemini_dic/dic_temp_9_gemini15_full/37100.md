combining:/kəmˈbaɪnɪŋ/ | v.ing |结合；联合；混合 |The act of uniting or merging things.  It implies bringing different elements together to form a whole. |Example: The combining of different cultures enriched the city's identity.  = 不同文化的融合丰富了这座城市的文化内涵。The artist's work shows a remarkable combining of traditional and modern techniques. = 艺术家的作品展现了传统和现代技法的完美结合。|近义词：merging, uniting, integrating, blending |反义词：separating, dividing, segregating
