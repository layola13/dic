scorchers:/ˈskɔːrtʃərz/| n.|酷暑，极热天气，极快的人或物|A scorcher is something extremely hot, such as a very hot day or a very fast car.  It can also refer to a person who is exceptionally good at something.|例句：It's a scorcher today; I'm melting!  今天天气太热了，我快融化了！

例句：He's a real scorcher on the tennis court. 他在网球场上真是个高手。|近义词：heatwave, blaze, burner, hotshot |反义词：cold snap, freeze
