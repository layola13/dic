incrystallizable: /ˌɪnkrɪˈstælɪzəbl/ | adj. | 不可结晶的 | Unable to be crystallized; not capable of forming crystals.  This term describes substances that do not readily form a crystalline structure upon solidification or cooling.  They remain amorphous or in a non-crystalline state. |


例句：

The amorphous polymer is incrystallizable at room temperature.  =  这种无定形聚合物在室温下不可结晶。

Some glasses are incrystallizable due to their complex molecular structure. =  一些玻璃由于其复杂的分子结构而不可结晶。


近义词：amorphous, non-crystalline


反义词：crystallizable


词性:形容词
