palms:/pɑːmz/| n.|手掌；棕榈树；棕榈叶；（建筑物的）掌状装饰|The word "palms" refers to several things: the fleshy inner surface of the hand, the tropical trees of the family Arecaceae, and sometimes the leaves of these trees. It can also refer to decorative elements resembling palm leaves.  |例句：He opened his palms to show his empty hands. 他张开手掌以展示他的空手。

例句：The resort boasted beautiful palm trees swaying in the breeze. 度假村拥有美丽的棕榈树在微风中摇曳。

近义词：hands (for the part of the body),  palmettes (for architectural decoration)|反义词：无|词性：名词
