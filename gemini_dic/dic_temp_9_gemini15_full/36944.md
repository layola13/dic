coloured: /ˈkʌləd/ /ˈkʌlərd/ | adj. | 有色的，彩色的 | Having colour; not white, black, or gray.  |例句：She wore a coloured dress. 她穿了一件彩色的裙子。

例句：The artist used coloured pencils to create the drawing. 艺术家用彩色铅笔创作了这幅画。


近义词：pigmented, tinted, hued | 反义词：colourless, uncoloured, monochrome


词源:  Comes from the Old English word "colōred,"  a past participle of the verb "colōrian," meaning "to colour."  The word "colour" itself has roots in the Latin word "color," meaning "appearance, complexion."
