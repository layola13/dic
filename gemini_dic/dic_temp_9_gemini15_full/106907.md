lacerate:/ˈlæsəreɪt/| vt.|撕裂；割破；使伤痛；严厉批评|to tear or cut (something) roughly; to wound or injure severely; to criticize severely|例句：The barbed wire lacerated his arm.  带刺的铁丝划破了他的胳膊。

例句：The critic lacerated the author's new novel in his review. 批评家在他的评论中严厉批评了作者的新小说。

近义词：tear, rip, gash, wound, mangle, maim, criticize harshly

反义词：heal, mend, repair, soothe, praise


词源:  From Latin *lacerāre, frequentative of *lacīnāre "to tear," from *lacertus "flank, side," related to *lacer "torn."  The sense development is from "tear flesh" to the figurative sense of "tear apart reputation, etc."
