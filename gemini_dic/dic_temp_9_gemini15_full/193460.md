select:/səˈlɛkt/| vt. |选择，挑选|To choose someone or something from a group.|例句：Please select your preferred payment method. 请选择您喜欢的付款方式。|近义词：choose, pick, opt for |反义词：reject, refuse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I select the best apples from the basket. = 我从篮子里挑选最好的苹果。
She selects her clothes carefully. = 她仔细挑选衣服。


一般过去时:
He selected a red car from the showroom. = 他从展厅里选择了一辆红色的汽车。
They selected a new chairman last week. = 他们上周选了一位新的主席。


现在完成时:
We have selected the winning entries. = 我们已经选出了获奖作品。
She has selected a university to attend. = 她已经选择了一所大学就读。


过去完成时:
They had selected the team before the game started. = 比赛开始前他们已经选好了队伍。
I had selected my dress for the party a week before. = 我在一周前就选好了参加聚会的衣服。


将来时:
I will select a new phone next month. = 我下个月会选择一部新手机。
They will select the finalists tomorrow. = 他们明天将选出决赛选手。


虚拟语气:
If I were to select a career, I would choose medicine. = 如果我要选择一个职业，我会选择医学。
I wish I had selected a different path in life. = 我真希望我人生中选择了不同的道路。


被动语态:
The winner was selected by a panel of judges. = 获胜者是由评审团选出的。
A new CEO will be selected soon. = 一位新的首席执行官很快就会被选出来。


疑问句:
Which candidate did you select? = 你选择了哪位候选人？
Have you selected a date for the wedding yet? = 你已经选定婚礼日期了吗？


否定句:
I did not select that option. = 我没有选择那个选项。
They did not select him for the team. = 他们没有选中他加入球队。
