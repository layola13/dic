self-abuses:/ˌsɛlf əˈbjuːzɪz/ | n. | 自虐；自我摧残 | The act of harming oneself, either physically or emotionally.  This can encompass a wide range of behaviors, from cutting and burning to substance abuse and self-neglect.  It's often a symptom of underlying mental health issues. | Example:  His self-abuses stemmed from deep-seated feelings of inadequacy. 他自虐的行为源于根深蒂固的自卑感。  |近义词: self-harm, self-destruction, masochism | 反义词: self-care, self-love | 名词
