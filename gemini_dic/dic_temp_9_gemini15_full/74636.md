free-based:/friːˈbeɪst/| adj.|游离碱的，非盐酸盐的|Free-based refers to a substance, usually a drug, that is in its pure, un-salted form.  This contrasts with a salt form, which is often created to improve stability, solubility, or other pharmaceutical properties.  The term is most commonly used in the context of illicit drugs.|例句：The free-based cocaine was more potent and dangerous. 游离碱可卡因效力更强，也更危险。  The study compared the effects of free-based and salt forms of the medication.  这项研究比较了药物的游离碱形式和盐形式的效果。|近义词：unsalted, pure|反义词：salted, hydrochloride
