meadows:/ˈmɛdoʊz/| n.|草地，牧场|Open areas of grassland, typically covered with wildflowers and grasses.  Meadows are often found in valleys or other low-lying areas and provide habitat for various plants and animals.|例句：We went for a walk in the sun-drenched meadows. 我们在阳光普照的草地上散步。

例句：The sheep grazed peacefully in the green meadows.  羊群在绿色的草地上安静地吃草。


近义词：pasture, grassland, field


反义词：forest, desert, jungle


词性:名词

词源:
The word "meadow" comes from the Old English word "mæd,"  meaning "pasture" or "grassland."  This word is related to other Germanic words with similar meanings, reflecting its ancient origins in the language.
