nonsacredness:/ˈnɒnˈseɪkrɪdnəs/| n. |非神圣性，世俗性 |The absence of sacred qualities; profanity or irreverence.  It refers to the quality or state of being not sacred or holy; lacking religious or spiritual significance.  |例句：The nonsacredness of the modern world is often lamented by religious thinkers. 现代世界的非神圣性经常被宗教思想家们所惋惜。

例句：The artist's work explores the themes of nonsacredness and the everyday.  这位艺术家的作品探讨了非神圣性和日常生活的主题。


近义词：profane, secular, worldly, unholy, irreverence

反义词：sacredness, holiness, divinity, piety
