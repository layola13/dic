decaying:/dɪˈkeɪɪŋ/| adj.|腐烂的；衰败的；衰退的 | decaying describes something that is rotting or breaking down, losing its strength or quality over time.  It can refer to physical objects, social structures, or even abstract concepts. |例句：The decaying leaves smelled strongly of damp earth.  腐烂的树叶散发着潮湿泥土的气味。

The decaying empire lost its power and influence.  衰败的帝国失去了权力和影响力。


近义词：rotting, rotting, decomposing, deteriorating, disintegrating, crumbling

反义词：flourishing, thriving, prospering
