shastra:/ˈʃɑːstrə/ | n. | 论著；圣典；经典；学说 | A shastra is a treatise or manual, particularly one dealing with a branch of Hindu philosophy or religion, or with a system of law or a science.  They are often considered authoritative texts.  | Example: The ancient shastras contain vast knowledge about Ayurveda.  古代的圣典包含了关于阿育吠陀的丰富知识。 | 近义词：treatise, manual, scripture, text | 反义词：fiction, fabrication
