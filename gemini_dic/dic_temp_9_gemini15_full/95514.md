incloister:/ɪnˈklɔɪstər/| vt.|使隐居；使与世隔绝；|to shut up in a cloister; to seclude from the world; to confine within the walls of a monastery or convent.|例句：The nuns were incloistered in the remote mountain monastery. 修女们被幽禁在偏远山区的修道院里。|例句：He incloistered himself in his study for days, working on his novel. 他把自己关在书房里好几天，埋头写作。|近义词：seclude, isolate, confine, sequester |反义词：expose, liberate, release
