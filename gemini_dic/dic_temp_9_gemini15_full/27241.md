caloric:/kəˈlɒrɪk/|/kəˈlɔːrɪk/| adj. |卡路里的；热量的 | Relating to or measured in calories.  The word refers to the energy content of food. |Example:  The caloric content of this meal is quite high. 这顿饭的卡路里含量很高。

The caloric intake of athletes is often significantly higher than that of sedentary individuals. 运动员的卡路里摄入量通常比久坐不动的人高得多。|近义词：energetic, high-energy |反义词：low-calorie, calorie-free


词源:  From "calorie," ultimately from Latin *calor ("heat").
