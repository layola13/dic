词源:

* **post-**:  Latin prefix meaning "after" or "behind".
* **axial**: From Latin "axis," meaning "axle" or "central line."  Relating to the body's axis.

Therefore, "postaxial" literally means "after or behind the axis," referring to the side of a limb further from the midline.
