outreached:/ˌaʊtˈriːtʃt/| v.|延伸；接触；帮助；伸出援手 |To extend to or reach beyond a certain limit; to make contact with (someone), especially in order to provide help or information; to extend a helping hand.  |例句：The organization outreached to the homeless community providing food and shelter.  该组织向无家可归的社区伸出援手，提供食物和住所。|例句：The tree's branches outreached over the fence. 树枝伸出篱笆。|近义词：extend, reach, contact, assist |反义词：withdraw, neglect, ignore |动词
