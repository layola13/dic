kits:/kɪts/| n.|工具包，成套工具，成套用品|A kit is a set of tools or supplies for a particular purpose.  It often contains everything needed to complete a specific task or project.|例句：He bought a fishing kit before his trip. 他在旅行前买了一套钓鱼工具。

例句：The first-aid kit contained bandages, antiseptic wipes, and pain relievers. 急救箱里装着绷带、消毒湿巾和止痛药。


近义词：set, outfit, collection|反义词：None|词性:n.
