caretakes:/ˈkerˌteɪks/| n. |看护者，临时管理者|A person who takes care of something or someone, especially temporarily.  It often implies a responsibility for overseeing or managing something.|例句：The neighbor acted as caretaker for the house while the owners were on vacation.  邻居在房主度假期间担任房屋的看护人。|例句：The caretaker of the museum oversaw the daily operations.|博物馆的看护人负责日常运营。|近义词：custodian, guardian, supervisor, manager |反义词：abandoner, neglecter
