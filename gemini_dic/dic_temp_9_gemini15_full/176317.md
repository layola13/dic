re-emerge:/ˌriːɪˈmɜːrdʒ/|/ɪˈmɜːrdʒ/| v.|重新出现；再次涌现|To appear again after a period of time.|The sun re-emerged from behind the clouds. 太阳从云后重新出现。The issue of climate change has re-emerged as a major political concern. 气候变化问题再次成为主要的政治关注点。|emerge, reappear, resurface|submerge, disappear, vanish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plant re-emerges every spring. = 这株植物每年春天都会重新生长。
He re-emerges into the public eye after years of seclusion. = 他在隐居多年后再次进入公众视野。

一般过去时:
The economy re-emerged stronger after the recession. = 经济在衰退后更加强劲地复苏。
The lost dog re-emerged several days later. = 失踪的狗几天后又出现了。

现在完成时:
The band has re-emerged with a new album. = 乐队带着一张新专辑重新出现了。
Hope has re-emerged after the devastating news. = 在令人震惊的消息之后，希望又出现了。

过去完成时:
After the storm had subsided, the sun re-emerged. = 风暴平息后，太阳重新出现了。
Once the dust had settled, the truth re-emerged. = 一切尘埃落定后，真相浮出水面。

将来时:
The flowers will re-emerge in the spring. = 花会在春天重新开放。
The company expects to re-emerge as a market leader. = 公司预计将重新成为市场领导者。

虚拟语气:
If the economy were to re-emerge strongly, we would invest more. = 如果经济强劲复苏，我们将进行更多投资。
I wish the lost painting would re-emerge someday. = 我希望丢失的画作有一天能重现。

被动语态:
The evidence was re-emerged after a thorough investigation. = 在彻底调查后，证据重新出现。
The problem is re-emerged after a temporary solution. = 问题在临时解决方案之后再次出现。

疑问句:
Will the lost civilization re-emerge? = 失落的文明会重新出现吗？
When will the bird re-emerge from its nest? = 小鸟什么时候会从巢中出来？

否定句:
The symptoms did not re-emerge after treatment. = 治疗后症状没有再次出现。
He vowed that the problem would never re-emerge. = 他发誓这个问题永远不会再次出现。
