connive:/kəˈnaɪv/| v.|纵容；默许；暗中勾结|To secretly allow or assist in an activity that is wrong or dishonest.|The principal connived at the cheating students by ignoring their actions. 校长对作弊的学生视而不见，等于纵容了他们。| collude, conspire, abet | oppose, confront, object |动词

一般现在时:
He connives at his son's misdeeds. = 他纵容他儿子的恶行。
They connive to overthrow the government. = 他们密谋推翻政府。


一般过去时:
The manager connived at the fraud. = 经理对欺诈行为睁一只眼闭一只眼。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:
