litotes:/ˈlaɪtəʊtiːz/ | n. | 委婉说法；低调说法；反语；轻描淡写 |  A figure of speech consisting of an understatement in which an affirmative is expressed by the negative of its contrary.  It's a way of saying something by denying its opposite.  This often creates emphasis or irony. |  He's not the sharpest tool in the shed. (meaning he's stupid)  The weather isn't exactly balmy. (meaning the weather is cold) | understatement, downplaying | hyperbole, exaggeration | 名词
