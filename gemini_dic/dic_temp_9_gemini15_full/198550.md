shrubbery:/ˈʃrʌbəri/| n. |灌木丛；灌木植物 |A shrubbery is a collection of shrubs, typically planted together to form a hedge or border.  It refers to a dense growth of shrubs. |例句：The garden boasted a beautiful shrubbery bordering the lawn. 花园里有一片美丽的灌木丛环绕着草坪。|例句：They carefully pruned the shrubbery to maintain its shape. 他们仔细修剪灌木丛以保持其形状。|近义词：bushes, undergrowth, thicket |反义词：open space, grassland
