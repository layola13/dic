lag-behind:/læɡ bɪˈhaɪnd/| v. |落后，滞后|To fall behind; to fail to keep up with others or with a schedule.  It implies a slower pace or progress compared to a standard or expectation.|例句：The project is lagging behind schedule. 项目进度落后了。|例句：He's lagging behind in his studies. 他的学习落后了。|近义词：fall behind, trail, delay|反义词：lead, surpass, excel


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The students are lagging behind in math. = 学生们数学学习落后了。
He lags behind his colleagues in terms of promotion. = 他在晋升方面落后于同事。


一般过去时:
The runner lagged behind the others. = 那名跑步者落后于其他人。
The company lagged behind its competitors in innovation. = 这家公司在创新方面落后于竞争对手。


现在完成时:
The country has lagged behind in economic development. = 这个国家在经济发展方面一直落后。
Our team has lagged behind in completing the project. = 我们的团队在完成项目方面落后了。


过去完成时:
The smaller company had lagged behind in adopting new technology. = 这家较小的公司在采用新技术方面已经落后了。
They had lagged behind in their preparations for the exam. = 他们在考试准备方面已经落后了。


将来时:
If we don't speed up, we will lag behind. = 如果我们不加快速度，我们将落后。
The less developed countries might lag behind in the global race for technological advancement. = 欠发达国家可能在全球科技进步的竞争中落后。


虚拟语气:
If he had studied harder, he wouldn't have lagged behind. = 如果他学习更努力，他就不会落后了。
I wish I hadn't lagged behind in my exercise routine. = 我真希望我没有在锻炼计划中落后。


被动语态:
The team was lagged behind by the other team. = 这个团队落后于另一个团队。 (This usage is less common and may sound unnatural.  Active voice is preferred.)


疑问句:
Is the project lagging behind? = 项目进度落后了吗？
Are you lagging behind in your work? = 你的工作落后了吗？


否定句:
The team isn't lagging behind. = 这个团队没有落后。
He didn't lag behind during the marathon. = 他在马拉松比赛中没有落后。
