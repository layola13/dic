recontemplations:/ˌriːkənˌtɛmpleɪʃənz/| n.|再思考，重新考虑|The act of reconsidering or thinking about something again;  a renewed contemplation.  It implies a deeper or more thorough consideration than a simple rethinking.|例句：His recontemplations of the past led him to a new understanding.他的再次思考过去让他对过去有了新的理解。

例句：After careful recontemplations, she decided to change her plans.经过仔细的重新考虑，她决定改变计划。


近义词：reconsideration, re-evaluation, review, reflection


反义词：impulsiveness, rashness


词性：名词
