enclosed:/ɪnˈkloʊzd/| adj.|封闭的；随函附上的|surrounded on all sides; contained within something else; sent with a letter|例句：The garden is enclosed by a high wall. 花园被一堵高墙围着。

The contract was enclosed with the letter. 合同随信附上。|近义词：contained, included, attached |反义词：open, exposed
