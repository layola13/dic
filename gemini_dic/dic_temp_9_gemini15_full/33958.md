cis:/sɪs/| adj.|顺式；顺位的|Situated on the same side (of a double bond or ring structure in organic chemistry).  It describes the spatial arrangement of atoms or groups in a molecule.|例句：The cis isomer has a different melting point than the trans isomer.  顺式异构体的熔点与反式异构体不同。|近义词：同侧的(same-side),  同面的(coplanar)|反义词：trans (反式)|形容词
