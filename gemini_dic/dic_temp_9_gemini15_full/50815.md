/dɪˈskraɪbərz/ | n. | 描述者，形容词 | People or things that describe something.  Those who or that which characterize or delineate. | The author's describers painted a vivid picture of the bustling city. 作者的描述者描绘了一幅繁华都市的生动景象。 | narrators, depicters, characterizers |  (无明确反义词，可考虑：obscurers, concealers) | 名词
