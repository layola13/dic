distending: /dɪˈstɛndɪŋ/ | v. |膨胀；扩张；使…膨胀|  The act of stretching or enlarging something, causing it to become swollen or inflated.  It often implies a stretching to the point of tension or strain. | 例句：The balloon was distending with air. 气球正在充气膨胀。

The doctor noticed the distending abdomen of the patient. 医生注意到病人腹部膨胀。


近义词：expanding, swelling, inflating, enlarging

反义词：contracting, shrinking, deflating

词源:
