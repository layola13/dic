curarize:/ˈkʊərəraɪz/| vt.|用箭毒木毒素麻醉|To treat (a person or animal) with curare, a neuromuscular blocking agent derived from plants.  Curare causes paralysis by blocking nerve impulses to muscles. This is often used in surgery to relax muscles. |例句：The surgeon decided to curarize the patient before the operation to ensure muscle relaxation. 外科医生决定在手术前用箭毒木毒素麻醉病人以确保肌肉放松。The veterinarian curarized the animal to facilitate a difficult examination. 兽医用箭毒木毒素麻醉动物以方便进行困难的检查。|近义词：paralyze, immobilize|反义词：stimulate, energize|动词
