darner:/ˈdɑːrnər/| n. |缝补者；（蜻蜓的）一种；|A person who mends holes, especially in clothes;  a type of dragonfly.|例句：My grandmother was a skilled darner, patching up all our torn clothes.  我的祖母是一位熟练的缝补者，她缝补了我们所有破损的衣服。|例句：The darner zipped past, its wings a blur of iridescent color.  蜻蜓飞驰而过，翅膀呈现出彩虹般的色彩。|近义词：mender, repairer, dragonfly (in the second meaning)|反义词：ripper, tearer|名词
