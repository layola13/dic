courtierly:/ˈkɔːrtlɪ/| adj.|有礼貌的；彬彬有礼的；宫廷的|Having the manners or characteristics of a courtier; polite and refined.|例句：He greeted her with a courtierly bow. 他以宫廷式的鞠躬向她致意。
例句：Her courtierly demeanor impressed everyone at the ball.  她在舞会上彬彬有礼的风度给每个人留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：polite, courteous, refined, elegant|反义词：rude, impolite, boorish
