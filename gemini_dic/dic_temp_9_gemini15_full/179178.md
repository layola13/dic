redirection:/ˌriːdaɪˈrɛkʃən/| n. |重定向，转向，改道 |The process of sending data or a request to a different location or destination than originally intended.  It's commonly used in computing, networking, and even in fluid dynamics.  |例句：The website automatically performs a redirection to its secure server.  网站会自动重定向到其安全服务器。

例句：The traffic was redirected due to the accident. 交通因事故而改道。


近义词：rerouting, diversion, channeling |反义词：straightforward transmission, direct access |词性：名词
