pie:/paɪ/| n.|馅饼|A baked dish consisting of a filling of fruit, meat, or vegetables enclosed in a pastry crust.  Examples include apple pie, chicken pot pie, and shepherd's pie.|例句：My grandmother's apple pie is the best I've ever tasted.  我奶奶做的苹果派是我吃过最好吃的。|例句：He ordered a chicken pot pie for lunch. 他午餐点了一份鸡肉馅饼。|近义词：tart,pastry|反义词：None
