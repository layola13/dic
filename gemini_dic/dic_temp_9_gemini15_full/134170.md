nonincarnate:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkɑːrneɪt/| adj.|非化身的，非肉身的|Not having a physical body; existing only in spirit or as a concept.  This term is primarily used in theological contexts, referring to a divine being or spirit that does not possess a physical form.|例句：The doctrine of the nonincarnate Logos is central to some theological traditions.  一些神学传统中，非化身逻各斯的教义是核心。|近义词：immaterial, incorporeal, spiritual|反义词：incarnate, embodied
