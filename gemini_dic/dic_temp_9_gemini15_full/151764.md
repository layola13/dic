pasigraphic:/ˌpæzəˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|记号的，用记号表示的|Relating to or denoting a system of writing in which symbols represent words or ideas rather than sounds;  using a system of symbols to represent words or ideas.  It differs from phonetic writing systems which represent sounds.| Example:  The ancient script was pasigraphic, with each symbol representing a concept rather than a sound.  古代文字是记号文字，每个符号代表一个概念而不是一个声音。|近义词：ideographic, symbolic|反义词：phonetic, alphabetic|
