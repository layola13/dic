renminbi:/rɛnˈmɪnbiː/| n. |人民币|The official currency of the People's Republic of China.  It is also known as the yuan (元).  The renminbi is subdivided into 10 jiao (角), and each jiao is further subdivided into 10 fen (分).|例句：The price of this item is 100 renminbi. 这件商品的价格是100元人民币。

例句：I need to exchange some US dollars for renminbi before my trip to China. 我需要在去中国旅行之前兑换一些美元成人民币。


近义词：yuan (元), RMB (abbreviation)|反义词：None.  (Currency doesn't have direct antonyms)|词性：Noun

词源:
Renminbi (人民币) is a compound word.  "Renmin" (人民) means "people," and "bi" (币) means "currency." Therefore, renminbi literally translates to "people's currency."
