misencoding:/ˌmɪsˈɛnkoʊdɪŋ/ | n. | 误编码，错误编码 | The process of encoding information incorrectly, leading to data corruption or loss.  This can occur in various contexts, including computer systems, data transmission, and genetic information. | Example:  The misencoding of the genetic code resulted in a non-functional protein.  基因密码的误编码导致蛋白质功能失调。 |近义词：incorrect encoding, faulty encoding, encoding error | 反义词：correct encoding, accurate encoding | 名词
