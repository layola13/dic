showgirl:/ˈʃoʊɡɜːrl/ | n. |歌舞女郎，表演女郎 | A showgirl is a female performer, typically in a revue or similar entertainment, who performs dances and often wears elaborate costumes.  They are often associated with casinos and nightclubs. |  The showgirls' costumes were dazzling. 歌舞女郎的服装令人眼花缭乱。 The Las Vegas showgirl captivated the audience with her performance.  拉斯维加斯的歌舞女郎以她的表演迷住了观众。| dancer, performer, entertainer |  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with) office worker, teacher | 名词
