precomplicates: /ˌpriːˈkɒmplɪkeɪts/ | v. | 预先使复杂化；提前使复杂 | To make something more complicated before it is necessary or before it even begins.  This word is a relatively rare neologism, formed by combining "pre-" (before) and "complicates."  It implies unnecessary complexity added beforehand. | 例句：He precomplicates every project by overthinking the initial stages. 他在项目初期过度思考，预先使每个项目复杂化。


The unnecessary paperwork precomplicates the application process. 不必要的文书工作使申请流程预先复杂化。


近义词：pre-complicate, overcomplicate, complicate beforehand, unnecessarily complicate


反义词：simplify, streamline, clarify


词性：动词
