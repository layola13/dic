lootable: /ˈluːtəbəl/ | adj. | 可掠夺的，可抢劫的 | Able to be looted or plundered; containing items of value that can be stolen.  |例句：The ancient tomb was lootable, filled with gold and jewels.  古代陵墓里装满了金银珠宝，是可以被掠夺的。The abandoned village was lootable, with many houses containing valuable artifacts.  被遗弃的村庄里有很多房屋存放着珍贵的文物，是可以被掠夺的。|近义词：pillageable, spoilable, plunderable |反义词：unlooted, secure, protected
