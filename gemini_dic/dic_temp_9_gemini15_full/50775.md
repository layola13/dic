descaler:/dɪˈskeɪlər/| n.|除垢剂，除垢器|A descaler is a substance or device used to remove scale (a hard deposit of minerals) from surfaces, typically in kettles, coffee machines, or other appliances.  It can be a chemical solution or a mechanical device.|例句：I used a descaler to clean the limescale from my kettle. 我用除垢剂清洗了水壶里的水垢。

例句：The coffee machine needed a thorough descaling. 咖啡机需要彻底除垢。|近义词：scale remover, limescale remover|反义词：scaler (in the sense of something that *causes* scale)
