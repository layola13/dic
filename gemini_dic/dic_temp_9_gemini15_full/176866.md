reaffixing: /ˌriːˈæfɪksɪŋ/ | v. | 再加词缀；重新加词缀 | The act of adding affixes again to a word, often after a previous affixation process. This can involve adding a new prefix or suffix, or changing an existing one.  | Example:  The linguist analyzed the process of reaffixing in the evolution of the word "unbreakable." (语言学家分析了“unbreakable”一词演变过程中再加词缀的过程。) | 近义词: re-derivation, re-affixation | 反义词:  deaffixation (去除词缀) | 动词
