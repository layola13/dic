pavement:/ˈpeɪvmənt/| n. |人行道，路面 |A pavement is a paved surface for pedestrians, typically alongside a road.  |例句：I walked along the pavement. 我沿着人行道走。|例句：The pavement was wet from the rain.  雨水打湿了人行道。|近义词：sidewalk, walkway|反义词：roadway, carriageway|名词
