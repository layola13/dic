obliterate:/əˈblɪtəreɪt/| vt.|抹去；消除；摧毁；使湮没|to destroy something completely so that it no longer exists or cannot be seen:|The building was completely obliterated by the bomb.  炸弹彻底摧毁了这座建筑物。The town was obliterated by the earthquake.  地震摧毁了那个城镇。|destroy, erase, wipe out, annihilate|preserve, save, protect, conserve|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm obliterates the small village.暴风雨摧毁了那个小村庄。
He obliterates his past mistakes.他抹去了他过去的错误。

一般过去时:
The earthquake obliterated the city. 地震摧毁了这座城市。
The fire obliterated all evidence of the crime. 大火烧毁了所有犯罪证据。


现在完成时:
The war has obliterated the ancient city.战争已经摧毁了这座古城。
The years have obliterated much of the evidence. 岁月已经抹去了许多证据。


过去完成时:
The flood had obliterated the village before the rescue team arrived.  救援队到达之前，洪水已经摧毁了村庄。
They had obliterated all traces of their presence.  他们已经抹去了他们存在的一切痕迹。


将来时:
The tsunami will obliterate the coastal towns. 海啸将摧毁沿海城镇。
The new technology will obliterate the old methods. 新技术将淘汰旧方法。


虚拟语气:
If the bomb had exploded, it would have obliterated the entire building. 如果炸弹爆炸，它会摧毁整栋建筑物。
I wish I could obliterate the memory of that day. 我真希望我能忘记那天。


被动语态:
The ancient city was obliterated by the earthquake.  这座古城被地震摧毁了。
The evidence was obliterated by the fire. 证据被大火烧毁了。


疑问句:
Will the new policy obliterate the old system? 新政策会淘汰旧制度吗？
Can technology obliterate the need for human labor? 科技能消除对人力劳动的需求吗？


否定句:
The rain did not obliterate the evidence. 雨水没有冲刷掉证据。
The army couldn't obliterate the enemy completely.  军队无法完全消灭敌人。
