shutterbug:/ˈʃʌtərˌbʌɡ/| n. |狂热摄影爱好者，摄影迷|An enthusiastic amateur photographer who takes many photographs.  A person who is passionate about photography and takes lots of pictures.|例句：He's a real shutterbug; he always has his camera with him.|近义词：photography enthusiast, camera buff|反义词：None  |名词
