moralized:/ˈmɔːrəlaɪzd/|/ˈmɒrəlaɪzd/| v.| 道德化；说教；赋予道德意义 | To interpret or explain something in terms of morality; to give a moral lesson or lecture.  | The teacher moralized about the importance of honesty. 老师对诚实的重要性说教了一番。|He moralized the story, making it a lesson about good versus evil. 他对故事进行了道德解读，使其成为善恶之争的教训。| preach, lecture, sermonize | amoralized, de-moralized | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He moralizes frequently. = 他经常说教。
She moralizes about the news. = 她对新闻说教。


一般过去时:
The priest moralized on the Ten Commandments. = 牧师就十诫进行了说教。
He moralized the events of the day. = 他对当天发生的事件进行道德解读。


现在完成时:
She has moralized every story she's told. = 她讲述的每一个故事都带有说教意味。
They have moralized the situation excessively. = 他们对局势进行了过度的道德解读。


过去完成时:
He had moralized the entire play before the discussion began.  = 在讨论开始之前，他已经对整部戏剧进行了道德解读。
She had moralized extensively in her previous lectures. = 在她之前的讲座中，她已经做了大量的道德说教。


将来时:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
