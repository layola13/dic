catch-all:/ˈkætʃɔːl/| adj. n.|包罗万象的；万能的；总括性的；万金油|A catch-all term or category is designed to include anything that doesn't fit into other, more specific categories.  It's often used when there's a need for broad classification or when precise categorization is difficult or unnecessary.|例句：The "other" category on the form is a catch-all for anything not specifically listed.  表格上的“其他”类别是一个包罗万象的选项，用于填写未明确列出的任何内容。|近义词：general, comprehensive, inclusive, omnibus|反义词：specific, particular, exclusive
