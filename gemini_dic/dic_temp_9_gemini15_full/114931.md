managerial:/ˌmænəˈdʒɪəriəl/| adj.|管理的；经理的；管理人员的 | Relating to or characteristic of managers or management.  It describes things connected with the work of managing or being a manager. |例句：He showed strong managerial skills during the crisis. 他在危机期间展现出很强的管理能力。

例句：The company is looking for someone with excellent managerial experience. 这家公司正在寻找一位拥有丰富管理经验的人。


近义词：administrative, executive, supervisory, directorial

反义词：subordinate, non-managerial


词源:  Derived from "manager," ultimately from the Old French *managier,  meaning "to manage, handle, control."  The suffix "-ial" creates an adjective.
