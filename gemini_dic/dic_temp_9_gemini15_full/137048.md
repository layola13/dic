nonstimulable: /ˌnɒnˈstɪmjʊləbl/ | adj. | 不能被刺激的，无反应的 | Not capable of being stimulated or excited.  Describes something that doesn't respond to stimulation, whether physical, chemical, or emotional. |  例句：The nerve fibers were found to be nonstimulable after the injury.  受伤后，发现神经纤维无法被刺激。

The patient presented with nonstimulable reflexes.  病人表现出无法被刺激的反射。


近义词：unresponsive, inert, unresponsive to stimuli, insensitive,  unreactive

反义词：stimulable, responsive, reactive, sensitive


词性：形容词
