USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and as such, doesn't have tense variations like verbs do. Its usage is always modifying a verb, adjective or another adverb.


Example 1: He reacted nonintellectually to the news. 他对这个消息的反应很不理性。
Example 2:  She responded nonintellectually, driven by emotion. 她非理性地回应，受情绪驱使。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "nonintellectually".  Its use is primarily as a standalone adverb to describe actions or qualities.
