halibut:/ˈhælibət/| n. |大比目鱼|A large flatfish of the family Pleuronectidae, highly prized as food.  Halibut are typically found in cold, deep waters of the North Atlantic and Pacific oceans.|例句：The chef prepared a delicious grilled halibut. 主厨准备了一道美味的烤大比目鱼。|例句：We caught a huge halibut on our fishing trip. 我们在钓鱼旅行中钓到了一条巨大的大比目鱼。|近义词：turbot, flounder (though these are generally smaller)|反义词:None|名词
