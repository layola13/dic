conscientize:/ˈkɒnʃənˌtaɪz/ | vt. |使具有责任感；使认真负责；教育|To make someone aware of their moral obligations or responsibilities; to make someone more conscientious.|例句：The teacher tried to conscientize her students about the importance of honesty. 老师试图让她的学生明白诚实的的重要性。|例句：The experience conscientized him to the plight of the homeless.  这次经历使他认识到无家可归者的困境。|近义词：educate, enlighten, sensitize |反义词：desensitize, disregard
