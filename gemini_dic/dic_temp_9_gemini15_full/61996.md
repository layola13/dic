enambush:/ɛmˈbʊʃ/| v.|埋伏；伏击|To attack someone by surprise from a hidden place.  |例句：The soldiers ambushed the enemy troops. 士兵们伏击了敌军。

例句：The guerrillas ambushed the convoy, causing significant damage. 游击队员伏击了车队，造成了重大损失。


近义词：surprise attack, ambush, waylay, trap


反义词：open attack, frontal assault


词性：动词
