gest:/dʒɛst/ | n. |姿势，手势；姿态；表示；举动 | A gesture is a movement of the body, especially of the hands, to express an idea or feeling.  | 例句：He made a gesture of dismissal. 他做了个打发人的手势。
He gave a small wave as a gesture of farewell. 他轻轻挥了挥手表示告别。 | 近义词：movement, motion, sign, signal | 反义词：N/A
