gusts:/ɡʌsts/ | n. |一阵（风），一阵（雨），突发的一阵 | A sudden strong rush of wind.  |例句：Strong gusts of wind blew down the trees. 强烈的阵风吹倒了树木。|例句：The rain came in gusts. 雨一阵一阵地下。|近义词：blast, flurry, squall |反义词：calm, breeze


USAGE->

Gusts of wind often blow through this area. = 这片区域经常刮阵风。
Strong gusts regularly disrupt the flight schedule. = 强风经常扰乱航班时刻表。


一般过去时:

虚拟语气:
