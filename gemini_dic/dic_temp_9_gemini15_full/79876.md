gnashed:/ɡnæʃt/| v.|磨碎；咬牙切齿|To strike or grind (the teeth) together, especially forcefully or angrily.|例句：He gnashed his teeth in frustration. 他气得磨牙。|例句：The dog gnashed its teeth at the intruder.  那条狗对着入侵者磨牙。|近义词：grind, grit, crunch|反义词：None
