intergalactically:/ˌɪntərɡəˈlæktɪkli/| adv.|星际地，银河系地 |Across or throughout many galaxies.|例句：The spaceship traveled intergalactically, exploring distant star systems.  这艘宇宙飞船进行星际旅行，探索遥远的星系。

例句：The effects of the supernova were felt intergalactically. 超新星的影响在整个星系间都能感受到。


近义词：cosmically, universally (in a broad sense)


反义词：locally, terrestrially
