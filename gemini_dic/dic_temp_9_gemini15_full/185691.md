reverser:/rɪˈvɜːrsər/| n. v.|反向器；反转者；使反转的人或物|A reverser is a device or person that reverses something.|The mechanic used a reverser to change the direction of the engine.  机械师用反向器改变发动机的方向。|The gearshift is a type of reverser.  变速杆是一种反向器。|近义词：inverter, overturner|反义词：forwarder, advancer|名词，动词
