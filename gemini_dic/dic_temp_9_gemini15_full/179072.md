redeployment:/ˌriːdɪˈplɔɪmənt/ | n. |重新部署，重新配置 | The act of moving something or someone to a new position or location, often for strategic or tactical reasons.  It implies a planned and organized shift, not just a random relocation. | Example: The company announced a redeployment of its resources to focus on its core business. 公司宣布重新部署资源，以专注于核心业务。

The army's redeployment to the southern border was swift and efficient.  军队向南部边境的重新部署迅速而有效。|近义词：relocation, repositioning, reassignment |反义词：withdrawal, removal
