overintensely: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈtɛnsli/ | adv. | 过度强烈地；过于紧张地 |  Intensely to an excessive degree.  It describes an action or feeling that is too strong or extreme. |

例句：

He reacted overintensely to the criticism, becoming visibly upset. = 他对批评反应过度强烈，变得明显不安。

She studied overintensely for the exam, neglecting her health and social life. = 她为考试学习过度紧张，忽略了健康和社交生活。


近义词：excessively, intensely, extremely, immoderately

反义词：mildly, calmly, gently, moderately

词性：副词
