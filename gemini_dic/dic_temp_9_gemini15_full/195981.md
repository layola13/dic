sententiosities:/ˌsɛn.tən.ʃiˈɒs.ə.tiz/ | n. | 充满警句的言语；富于格言的言辞；夸夸其谈 |  Sententiosities are statements that are weighty or profound, often expressed in a concise and memorable way, sometimes to the point of being pompous or affected.  They can be insightful or merely pretentious. | The professor's lecture was filled with sententiosities, leaving the students confused rather than enlightened.  教授的讲座充满了夸夸其谈的言辞，让学生们感到困惑而不是启发。 |  proverbs, aphorisms, maxims |  banality, triviality | 名词
