discombobulated:/ˌdɪskəmˈbɒbjuleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 混乱的；心烦意乱的；不知所措的 | Confused, disoriented, and generally flustered.  The word suggests a state of being thrown into disarray, both mentally and physically. | 例句：After the car accident, she felt completely discombobulated.  车祸后，她感到完全不知所措。

The unexpected news left him feeling utterly discombobulated.  这个意外的消息让他感到完全心烦意乱。


近义词：confused, bewildered, disoriented, flustered, rattled, upset


反义词：calm, composed, collected, organized, serene


词性:形容词
