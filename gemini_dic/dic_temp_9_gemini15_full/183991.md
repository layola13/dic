reshoed:/ˌriːˈʃuːd/| v.|给…换鞋掌|To put new shoes on (something, especially the bottom of a horse's hoof or a piece of equipment).  This word is a past participle form of the verb "reshoe".|例句：The farrier reshoed the horse after its race. 比赛后，蹄铁匠为马换上了新蹄铁。

例句：The mechanic reshoed the tractor after the tire wore out. 轮胎磨损后，技工为拖拉机换上了新轮胎。


近义词：re-sole, refit (in the context of equipment)|反义词：un-shoe (though rarely used)|动词
