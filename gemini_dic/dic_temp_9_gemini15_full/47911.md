decentralization: /ˌdiːˌsɛntrəlʌɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 分权；权力下放 | The process of distributing or dispersing functions, powers, people, or things away from a central location or authority.  It involves delegating authority and responsibility to lower levels within an organization or system.  | Example: The company underwent decentralization, granting more autonomy to its regional offices.  公司进行了分权，赋予其地区办事处更大的自主权。 |近义词：decentralisation, distribution, devolution |反义词：centralization, concentration
