manipulatives:/məˈnɪpjuleɪtɪvz/ | n. |操作物；教具；可操作的材料 | Objects or materials used to help someone learn, especially in mathematics or science.  These are often hands-on items used to represent abstract concepts.  | Example: The teacher used manipulatives, such as blocks and counters, to teach addition.  老师使用积木和计数器等教具来教授加法。 |近义词：teaching aids, learning tools, concrete materials |反义词：abstract concepts, theoretical explanations |名词
