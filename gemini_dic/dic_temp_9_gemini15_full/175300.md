rainproofing: /ˈreɪnprʊfɪŋ/ | n. | 防水处理；防雨措施 |  The process of making something resistant to rain, typically by applying a waterproof coating or sealant.  It involves treating a surface or structure to prevent water penetration and damage. | 例句：The rainproofing of the roof is essential to prevent leaks. 屋顶的防水处理对于防止漏水至关重要。

The rainproofing of the tent kept us dry during the storm.  帐篷的防水处理让我们在暴风雨中保持干燥。


近义词：waterproofing, weatherproofing | 反义词：water-permeable

词源:
The word "rainproofing" is a combination of "rain" and "proofing."  "Rain" refers to precipitation in the form of water droplets. "Proofing" means to make something resistant or impervious to something, in this case, rain.  The suffix "-ing" indicates the process or act of doing something.
