nomad:/ˈnoʊmæd/| n.|游牧民；流浪者|A member of a people who travels from place to place to find fresh pasture for their animals and does not usually stay in one place for long.|例句：The nomadic tribes migrated with the seasons. 游牧部落随着季节迁移。|近义词：wanderer, itinerant, vagrant|反义词：settler, resident
