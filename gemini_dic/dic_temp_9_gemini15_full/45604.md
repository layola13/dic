curriculum:/kəˈrɪkjʊləm/| n. |课程；课程表；教学大纲 |A curriculum is the set of courses that are offered by an educational institution.  It outlines the subjects, topics, and skills students are expected to learn. |Example: The school's curriculum includes math, science, and English. 这所学校的课程包括数学、科学和英语。The new curriculum emphasizes practical skills. 新的课程强调实践技能。|近义词：syllabus, course of study |反义词：None

词源:

The word "curriculum" comes from the Latin word "curriculum," meaning "a running," "a course," or "a race."  It originally referred to the course or path a student would follow in their studies.  Over time, its meaning evolved to encompass the entire range of courses and subjects offered by an educational institution.
