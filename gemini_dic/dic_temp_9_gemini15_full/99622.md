interjurisdictional: /ˌɪntərˌdʒʊrɪsˈdɪkʃənəl/ | adj. | 跨司法管辖区的；涉及多个司法管辖区的 | Relating to or involving more than one jurisdiction or area of legal authority.  | 例句：The case involved complex interjurisdictional issues, requiring cooperation between multiple states.  这个案件涉及复杂的跨司法管辖区问题，需要多个州的合作。


The agreement on environmental protection has significant interjurisdictional implications.  这项环境保护协议具有重大的跨司法管辖区影响。


近义词：transjurisdictional, multijurisdictional | 反义词：intrajurisdictional


词源:

* **inter-**:  A prefix meaning "between," "among," or "reciprocally."  (来自拉丁语 "inter")
* **juris-**: From Latin "juris," meaning "of law," "legal."
* **-diction-**: From Latin "dictio," meaning "speaking," "saying," "pronouncement,"  related to the concept of legal pronouncements and authority.
* **-al**: A suffix forming adjectives, meaning "relating to," "pertaining to."


因此，interjurisdictional字面意思是“在法律管辖区之间”的。
