electroplating:/ɪˌlekˈtroʊpleɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 电镀 | The process of coating a metal object with a thin layer of another metal using electricity.  Electroplating is used to improve the appearance, corrosion resistance, or wear resistance of the metal object.  | Example: The car parts were electroplated with chromium to enhance their shine and durability.  汽车零件镀铬以增强光泽和耐用性。 | 近义词：metallization, plating | 反义词：None
