channel:/ˈtʃænəl/ | n., v. | 频道；渠道；通道；海峡；引导；开导 |

英文解析：A channel can refer to a narrow passage of water connecting two larger bodies of water, a path or route for something to move along, a means of communication or distribution, or a groove or furrow. As a verb, it means to direct or guide something along a specific course.


例句：
The television channel showed a documentary about wildlife.  电视台频道播放了一部关于野生动物的纪录片。

We need to find a new channel for distributing our products. 我们需要找到一条新的渠道来分销我们的产品。


近义词：route, conduit, passage, way, medium


反义词：barrier, obstruction


词性：n., v.
