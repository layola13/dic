confirm:/kənˈfɜːrm/| vt. |确认；证实；批准 |To state or show that something is definitely true or correct.  |例句：Please confirm your booking by email. 请通过电子邮件确认您的预订。

例句：The experiment confirmed his theory. 实验证实了他的理论。


近义词：verify, ratify, validate, endorse | 反义词：deny, refute, invalidate | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He confirms the appointment. 他确认了约会。
They confirm the news. 他们证实了这个消息。

一般过去时:
She confirmed her reservation yesterday. 她昨天确认了她的预订。
The police confirmed the identity of the suspect. 警方确认了嫌疑人的身份。

现在完成时:
We have confirmed the details of the contract. 我们已经确认了合同的细节。
They have confirmed their participation in the event. 他们已经确认参加此次活动。

过去完成时:
He had already confirmed his attendance before the email arrived.  邮件到达之前，他已经确认参加了。
She had confirmed the flight before she left for the airport.  她去机场之前已经确认了航班。

将来时:
I will confirm the date with you tomorrow. 我明天会和你确认日期。
They will confirm the delivery time shortly. 他们很快就会确认送货时间。

虚拟语气:
If he confirmed the story, we'd know the truth. 如果他证实了这个故事，我们就会知道真相。
I wish he would confirm his arrival time. 我希望他能确认他的到达时间。

被动语态:
The booking was confirmed by email. 预订已通过电子邮件确认。
The date has been confirmed. 日期已确定。

疑问句:
Can you confirm the information? 你能确认一下信息吗？
Have they confirmed the order yet? 他们确认订单了吗？

否定句:
He didn't confirm the rumors. 他没有证实那些谣言。
She couldn't confirm the address. 她无法确认地址。
