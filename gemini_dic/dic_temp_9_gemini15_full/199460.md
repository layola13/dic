sinaitic:/saɪˈneɪtɪk/| adj.|西奈的；西奈半岛的；西奈山文化的|Of or relating to Mount Sinai or the Sinai Peninsula.  The term often refers to the early alphabetic script believed to have originated in the Sinai region.|The Sinaitic inscriptions are among the earliest examples of alphabetic writing.|近义词：Sinaitic Peninsula, Sinaitic script|反义词：无
