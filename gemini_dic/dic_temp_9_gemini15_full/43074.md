cowpoke's: /ˈkaʊpoʊks/ | n. |牛仔的，牧牛人的 |  A cowpoke is a cowboy, especially one who is unskilled or inexperienced. The term often carries a slightly humorous or derogatory connotation.  The possessive 's' indicates ownership or association.  | 例句：The cowpoke's hat was dusty from days on the trail. = 牛仔的帽子在小路上经过好几天后满是灰尘。  The cowpoke's rope was tangled. = 牛仔的绳子缠住了。 | 近义词：cowboy's, rancher's, buckaroo's | 反义词：city dweller's, office worker's | 名词所有格
