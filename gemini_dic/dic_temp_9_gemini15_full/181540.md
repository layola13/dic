relabeled:/ˌriːˈleɪbəld/| v. |重新贴标签；重新标记| To label something again, often with a different label.  This implies a change in the label, not just the act of labeling. |例句：The products were relabeled to reflect the new ingredients. 产品重新贴上了标签以反映新的成分。

The old jars were relabeled with updated expiration dates. 旧罐子贴上了更新的有效期标签。

|近义词：re-tagged, re-marked, relabeled|反义词：unlabeled|动词
