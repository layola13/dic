droppingly: /ˈdrɒpɪŋli/ | adv. | 下垂地，低落地，逐渐下降地 |  Describes something that is happening in a gradually decreasing or falling manner; often used to describe a downward trend, a falling tone of voice, or a declining state. | 例句：The temperature dropped droppingly throughout the day.  = 温度在一天中逐渐下降。


The sales figures dropped droppingly after the marketing campaign ended. = 营销活动结束后，销售额逐渐下降。

近义词：gradually, progressively, steadily (for downward trends); dejectedly, dispiritedly (for tone/mood)

反义词：increasingly, soaringly, rising,  upwardly (for opposite trends); cheerfully, enthusiastically (for opposite tone/mood)

词性: 副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, therefore it doesn't have verb conjugations like "is", "was", etc.  Its usage is always modifying a verb or adjective.  Examples follow:


一般现在时:
The stock prices are dropping droppingly. = 股票价格正在逐渐下降。
He speaks droppingly to avoid conflict. = 他说话低落地以避免冲突。

否定句:

PHRASE->
This word doesn't form common phrases in the same way as common words like "what".  Its usage is primarily to modify the verb describing the downward trend.  Examples of phrases using "droppingly" would be context-dependent, for example:


droppingly low  = 极低的
droppingly slow = 极慢的
droppingly quiet = 极安静的
droppingly sad = 极度悲伤的
droppingly fast = 急剧加快(注意反义)
