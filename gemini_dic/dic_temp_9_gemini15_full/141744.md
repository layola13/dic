orangeisms:/ˈɒrɪn(d)ʒɪzəm/| n. |橘子主义；橙色政治；（尤指荷兰）橘色革命相关言行|Orangeism refers to the beliefs and practices associated with the Orange Order, a Protestant fraternal organization primarily in Northern Ireland and parts of Scotland. It's characterized by strong Loyalist sentiments, celebrating William of Orange's victory at the Battle of the Boyne and often involving parades and demonstrations which can be highly contentious.|The Orange Order's marches often spark controversy and clashes with nationalist communities. |近义词：Loyalism, Protestantism (in this context)|反义词：Nationalism (in this context), Republicanism (in this context)|名词
