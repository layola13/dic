fade-away:/feɪd əˈweɪ/ | v. |逐渐消失；衰退；(声音、光线等)逐渐减弱 |to gradually disappear or become weaker |The music faded away. 音乐逐渐消失。|The pain slowly faded away. 疼痛慢慢地消失了。|appear, vanish | intensify, strengthen |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun fades away slowly at sunset. = 太阳在日落时慢慢地消失。
The sound of the music fades away gradually. = 音乐的声音逐渐消失。


一般过去时:
The image faded away after a few seconds. = 图像几秒钟后消失了。
His anger faded away after he calmed down. = 他冷静下来后，怒气消散了。


现在完成时:
The old photograph has faded away with time. = 这张旧照片随着时间的推移已经褪色了。
My memories of childhood have gradually faded away. = 我对童年的记忆逐渐淡忘了。


过去完成时:
The feeling of excitement had faded away by the time the event ended. = 活动结束时，兴奋感已经消失了。
The evidence had faded away long before the trial. = 在审判开始很久之前，证据就已经消失了。


将来时:
The paint will fade away in the sun. = 油漆会在阳光下褪色。
His hopes will fade away if he doesn't work hard. = 如果他不努力，他的希望将会破灭。


虚拟语气:
If the pain didn't fade away, I would see a doctor. = 如果疼痛没有消失，我会去看医生。
I wish my worries would fade away. = 我希望我的担忧能够消失。


被动语态:
The colors in the painting were faded away by the sun. = 画上的颜色被太阳晒褪了。
The memories were slowly faded away by time. = 记忆随着时间的推移慢慢地消失了。


疑问句:
Does the pain ever fade away completely? = 疼痛会完全消失吗？
Will the feeling of sadness ever fade away? = 悲伤的感觉会永远消失吗？


否定句:
The smell didn't fade away easily. = 气味不容易散去。
The memories didn't fade away, they stayed with me. = 记忆并没有消失，它们一直伴随着我。


PHRASE->
fade away into nothing = 完全消失
fade away into silence = 逐渐归于寂静
fade away from memory = 从记忆中消失
fade away with time = 随着时间推移而消失
fade away into the distance = 消失在远处
