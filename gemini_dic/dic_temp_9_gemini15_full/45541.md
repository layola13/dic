curiosity: /ˌkjʊəriˈɒsəti/ /ˌkjʊəriˈɑːsəti/ | n. |好奇心，求知欲 | a strong desire to know or learn something.  It often involves a feeling of wonder or fascination. |例句：Her curiosity about the mysterious package was overwhelming.  她对那个神秘包裹的好奇心非常强烈。

例句：The children's curiosity led them to explore the abandoned house. 孩子们的好奇心驱使他们去探索那座废弃的房子。


近义词：inquisitiveness, inquisitiveness, interest, wonder | 反义词：apathy, indifference


词源:  From Middle English *curiosité, from Old French curiosité, from Latin curiosus "inquisitive," from cura "care, concern."  The root is also related to words like "cure" and "curious."
