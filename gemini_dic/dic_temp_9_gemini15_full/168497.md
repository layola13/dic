problema:/prɒˈbliːmə/|ˈprɑːbləm| n. |问题，难题| a matter or situation regarded as unwelcome or harmful and needing to be dealt with and overcome.  A problem can be a difficult question, a situation requiring a solution, or a source of distress. |例句：We have a serious problem with pollution. 我们有一个严重的污染问题。

例句：The company is facing many financial problems. 这家公司面临着许多财务问题。


近义词：issue, difficulty, challenge, predicament, trouble |反义词：solution, answer


词源:  From Middle English *probleme, from Old French probleme, from Latin problēma, from Greek problēma "a thing put forth", from probalein "to throw forward, propose", from pro- "before" + ballein "to throw".
