quasi-Greek:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ɡriːk/| adj.|似希腊的；半希腊的|Appearing to be or resembling something Greek, but not genuinely or fully so.  It often refers to something that incorporates Greek elements but isn't strictly or purely Greek in origin or style.|例句：The building's architecture had a quasi-Greek style, with some Roman influences. 这座建筑的建筑风格类似希腊风格，但也有一些罗马风格的影响。

例句：His vocabulary contained many quasi-Greek words, derived from Latin but sharing etymological roots with Greek. 他的词汇包含许多似希腊语的词，它们源自拉丁语，但与希腊语有着词源上的联系。


近义词：pseudo-Greek, Greco-Roman (when referring to style), semi-Greek


反义词：purely Greek, authentically Greek


词性：形容词
