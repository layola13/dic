integrate:/ˈɪntɪɡreɪt/| vt. vi. n.|整合；使结合；融入；积分|To combine (one thing) with another so that they become a whole; to become part of a larger group or system.  |例句：The new software integrates seamlessly with our existing system. 新软件与我们现有的系统无缝集成。|例句：She successfully integrated into the new school community. 她成功地融入新的学校社区。|近义词：combine, unify, incorporate, amalgamate |反义词：segregate, separate, isolate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They integrate new ideas into their work. = 他们将新思想融入他们的工作中。
The company integrates sustainability into its business model. = 这家公司将可持续性融入其商业模式。

一般过去时:
He integrated the data from multiple sources. = 他整合了来自多个来源的数据。
The school integrated students from diverse backgrounds. = 学校融合了来自不同背景的学生。


现在完成时:
We have integrated the latest security features. = 我们已经集成了最新的安全功能。
She has successfully integrated into her new role. = 她已成功融入新的角色。


过去完成时:
They had integrated the feedback before releasing the product. = 他们在发布产品之前已经整合了反馈。
The team had integrated the design changes before the deadline. = 团队在截止日期前已经整合了设计更改。


将来时:
We will integrate the new module next week. = 我们下周将集成新模块。
The company will integrate AI into its operations. = 公司将人工智能整合到其运营中。


虚拟语气:
If they integrated the data properly, the results would be more accurate. = 如果他们正确地整合数据，结果将更准确。
It is essential that we integrate all perspectives. = 至关重要的是，我们必须整合所有观点。


被动语态:
The new system was integrated into the network. = 新系统已集成到网络中。
The data will be integrated by the end of the week. = 数据将在本周末整合完毕。


疑问句:
How do they integrate different cultures? = 他们如何融合不同的文化？
Will the two companies be integrated successfully? = 两家公司能否成功整合？


否定句:
They did not integrate the changes into the final version. = 他们没有将更改整合到最终版本中。
The system failed to integrate with the other applications. = 系统未能与其他应用程序集成。


PHRASE->
integrate into: 融入
integrate with: 与...集成
fully integrate: 完全整合
horizontally integrate: 横向整合
vertically integrate: 纵向整合
