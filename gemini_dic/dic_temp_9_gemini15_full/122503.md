词源:

The word "mockbird" is a compound word.  "Mock" comes from the Old French "mocquer," meaning "to scoff, jeer, or mimic."  "Bird" has its origins in Old English "bird,"  with Germanic roots.  Thus, "mockbird" literally means a bird that mocks or mimics others.
