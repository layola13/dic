nonstimulant:/ˈnɑːnˈstɪmjələnt/ | adj. |非兴奋剂的；无刺激性的 | Not causing stimulation or excitement;  lacking a stimulating effect.  This term often refers to medications or substances that do not increase alertness or energy levels in the same way stimulants do. | 例句：Many people prefer nonstimulant medications for ADHD because they cause fewer side effects. 许多人更喜欢非兴奋剂的ADHD药物，因为它们引起的副作用较少。

Many doctors recommend nonstimulant treatment options for children who don't respond well to stimulants. 许多医生建议对对兴奋剂反应不好的儿童采取非兴奋剂治疗方案。

近义词：calming, soothing, sedative (in some contexts) | 反义词：stimulating, energizing, excitatory | 词性：adj.
