nip-off:/nɪp ˈɔːf/| vt.| 摘除；掐断；迅速结束；突然停止 | To remove something by pinching or cutting it off; to quickly end or stop something. |例句：He nipped off the dead flower heads. 他摘掉了枯萎的花头。

例句：The meeting was abruptly nipped off due to the emergency. 会议因紧急情况而突然中止。

近义词：remove, cut off, terminate, end, stop | 反义词：continue, prolong, extend | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nips off the excess fabric. = 他剪掉多余的布料。
She nips off the top of the herbs. = 她掐掉香草的顶部。


一般过去时:
The gardener nipped off the wilted leaves. = 园丁摘掉了枯萎的叶子。
They nipped off the negotiations prematurely. = 他们过早地终止了谈判。


现在完成时:
She has nipped off all the weeds in the garden. = 她已经除掉了花园里所有的杂草。
He has nipped off the conversation before it got awkward. = 他在谈话变得尴尬之前就中断了谈话。


过去完成时:
They had nipped off the discussion before the boss arrived. = 他们在上司到达之前就结束了讨论。
She had nipped off a piece of the cake before anyone else could. = 她在她人之前就偷吃了一块蛋糕。


将来时:
He will nip off the extra branches tomorrow. = 他明天将剪掉多余的树枝。
They will nip off the project if it becomes too costly. = 如果项目成本过高，他们将终止该项目。


虚拟语气:
If I had a pair of scissors, I would nip off the loose thread. = 如果我有剪刀，我会剪掉松散的线头。
If she had been more decisive, she would have nipped off the problem in its early stages. = 如果她更果断一些，她就会在早期解决这个问题。


被动语态:
The dead leaves were nipped off by the gardener. = 枯叶被园丁摘掉了。
The negotiations were nipped off by the sudden news. = 谈判因突发新闻而中止。


疑问句:
Will you nip off the extra string? = 你会剪掉多余的绳子吗？
Did he nip off the conversation before it became a fight? = 他在谈话变成争吵之前就中断了谈话吗？


否定句:
He didn't nip off the extra branches. = 他没有剪掉多余的树枝。
They didn't nip off the project despite the high cost. = 尽管成本很高，他们也没有终止项目。

词源:
The word "nip" comes from the Middle English word "nippen," meaning "to bite" or "to pinch."  The "-off" suffix indicates separation or removal.  Therefore, "nip-off" literally means "to pinch or bite off."
