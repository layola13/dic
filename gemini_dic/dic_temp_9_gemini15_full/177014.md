reams:/riːmz/| n.|大量，许多；一刀纸(500张)|A ream is a large quantity of something, typically paper.  It usually refers to 500 sheets of paper.|例句：We need reams of paper for the printer. 我们需要大量的纸用于打印机。

例句：She wrote reams about her travels. 她写了大量的关于她旅行的文章。


近义词：abundance, mass, quantity, stacks

反义词：scarcity, dearth, paucity


词源：From Middle English *reme, from Old French rame, from Vulgar Latin *rama, from Latin ramus "branch," referring to the way paper was originally stacked.  The sense of "large quantity" developed later.
