double-parkers:/ˈdʌbəl ˈpɑːrkərz/ | n. | 双排停车的人 | People who park their vehicles in a way that blocks other vehicles from passing or parking.  Double-parkers often cause traffic congestion and inconvenience to others. | Example: The double-parkers on this street make it almost impossible to drive through.  这条街上的双排停车的人使得几乎无法通行。|近义词: illegally parked drivers, inconsiderate drivers | 反义词: considerate drivers, lawful drivers
