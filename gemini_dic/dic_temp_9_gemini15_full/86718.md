helminthes:/ˈhɛlmɪnθiz/| n. |蠕虫动物；寄生虫|Parasitic worms, a diverse group of invertebrate animals including flatworms, roundworms, and other related species.  They are often characterized by their parasitic lifestyle and simple body structures.  The study of helminthes is called helminthology.|Example:  The doctor prescribed medication to eliminate the helminthes infecting the patient. 医生开了药来消除感染病人的蠕虫。|近义词:parasites, worms|反义词:None specifically, but could be contrasted with non-parasitic animals.
