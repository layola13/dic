managing: /ˈmænɪdʒɪŋ/ | v. | 管理；经营；控制 | To control or be in charge of something, especially a business or organization. |例句：She's managing a team of ten people. 她管理着一个十人团队。

例句：He is managing the family business successfully. 他成功地经营着家族企业。


近义词：administering, directing, overseeing, running | 反义词：neglecting, ignoring, abandoning | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He manages the project efficiently. 他有效地管理着这个项目。
She manages a large department store. 她经营着一家大型百货公司。


一般过去时:
They managed the crisis effectively. 他们有效地处理了这场危机。
He managed to finish the report on time. 他设法按时完成了报告。


现在完成时:
She has managed the company for ten years. 她管理这家公司十年了。
He has managed to overcome many challenges. 他已经克服了许多挑战。


过去完成时:
They had managed to secure funding before the deadline. 他们在截止日期前已设法获得了资金。
She had managed the department for five years before her promotion. 在晋升之前，她管理该部门五年了。


将来时:
He will manage the new branch office. 他将管理新的分公司。
She will manage to find a solution. 她将设法找到解决办法。


虚拟语气:
If he managed the team better, they would be more productive. 如果他更好地管理团队，他们的生产力会更高。
I wish I had managed to finish the project earlier. 我希望我早点完成这个项目。


被动语态:
The project is managed by a team of experts.  这个项目由专家团队管理。
The company is being managed efficiently. 公司正在被有效地管理。


疑问句:
Who manages this department? 谁管理这个部门？
Does she manage to balance work and family life? 她能平衡工作和家庭生活吗？


否定句:
He doesn't manage his time well. 他没有很好地管理时间。
They didn't manage to reach an agreement. 他们没能达成协议。
