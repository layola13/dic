sailor:/ˈseɪlər/ | n. | 水手，海员 | A sailor is a person who works on a ship, especially as a member of the crew.  Sailors are skilled in navigation and seamanship. | Example: The sailor skillfully navigated the turbulent waters.  这位水手熟练地驾驭着汹涌的海浪。  |近义词： seaman, mariner | 反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with landlubber.
