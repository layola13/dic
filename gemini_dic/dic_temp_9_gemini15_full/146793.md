/ˌoʊvərˌɪləˈstreɪʃən/ | n. | 过度说明；插图过多 | The excessive use of illustrations, often to the detriment of clarity or effectiveness.  Overillustration can be distracting and overwhelm the reader. | The textbook suffered from overillustration; the numerous pictures detracted from the text's readability.  | Underillustration |  No direct antonym, but could be considered the opposite of "underillustration" or "lack of illustration".
