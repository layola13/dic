centralised:/ˈsɛntrəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 集中的，中央集权的 | Relating to or denoting a system in which things are controlled from a central place.  This describes a system where power, control, or administration is concentrated in one place or authority. | The company has a centralised management structure. 这家公司采用集中管理体制。 | Decentralised | decentralised
