resigns:/rɪˈzaɪnz/| v.|辞职；放弃；顺从；听任|To give up a job or position formally.|The CEO resigned from his post due to health concerns.  首席执行官因健康问题辞职了。|He resigned himself to his fate. 他听天由命。|quit, abandon, surrender|continue, persist, embrace|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resigns from his job every year. 他每年都辞职。
She resigns herself to her fate. 她听天由命。


一般过去时:
He resigned from his position last week. 他上周辞职了。
She resigned herself to the inevitable. 她认命了。


现在完成时:
He has resigned from the company. 他已经离开了公司。
She has resigned herself to living alone. 她已经习惯了独自生活。


过去完成时:
He had resigned before the scandal broke.  丑闻爆发前他就已经辞职了。
She had resigned herself to failure before even trying.  她甚至还没尝试就认定自己会失败。


将来时:
He will resign next month. 他下个月将辞职。
She will resign herself to whatever happens. 她将顺其自然。


虚拟语气:
If he resigned, the company would be in trouble. 如果他辞职，公司就会陷入困境。
I wish she had resigned sooner. 我希望她早点辞职。


被动语态:
His resignation was accepted. 他的辞职请求被接受了。
The matter was resigned to fate. 这事听天由命了。


疑问句:
Will he resign? 他会辞职吗？
Has she resigned herself to the situation? 她对这种情况认命了吗？


否定句:
He did not resign. 他没有辞职。
She did not resign herself to defeat. 她没有认输。
