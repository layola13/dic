overtheorizes:/ˌoʊvərˈθiəriˌzaɪzɪz/ | v. | 过度理论化；过度解释 | To interpret or explain something in an excessively theoretical or abstract way, often to the point of losing sight of practical considerations or empirical evidence.  | 例句：He overtheorizes the simple act of handshaking, seeing hidden power dynamics where none exist. 他过度解读简单的握手行为，在其中看到了根本不存在的权力动态。


The professor overtheorizes the impact of social media on young people, neglecting the lived experiences of actual users. 教授过度理论化了社交媒体对年轻人的影响，忽略了实际用户的真实体验。| 近义词：overintellectualize, overanalyze, overinterpret | 反义词：simplify,  understate,  ignore
