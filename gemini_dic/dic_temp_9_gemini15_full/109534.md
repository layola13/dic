lessee:/lɛˈsiː/ | n. |承租人，承租方 |A lessee is a person or entity that rents property from a lessor.  Lessees are bound by the terms of a lease agreement. |Example: The lessee was responsible for maintaining the property in good condition.  承租人有责任保持房产状况良好。  The lessee failed to pay rent on time. 承租人没有按时支付租金。|近义词：tenant, renter |反义词：lessor, landlord |名词
