jiggering:/ˈdʒɪɡərɪŋ/ | v. | 晃动；摇动；欺骗；作弊 | The act of moving something with quick, jerky movements; to cheat or swindle.  |例句：The boat was jiggering violently in the rough sea.  船在汹涌的大海上剧烈地摇晃。

例句：He was jiggering the accounts to hide his embezzlement. 他伪造账目以掩盖挪用公款的行为。|近义词：shaking, rocking, swindling, cheating |反义词：stabilizing, steadying, honest, fair |动词
