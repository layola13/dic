likably:/ˈlaɪkəbli/| adj.|讨人喜欢的，可爱的|Pleasing in manner or appearance; amiable.  It suggests a person is easy to like and get along with.  The word emphasizes the positive qualities that make someone appealing.|例句：He was a likably awkward young man. 他是一个讨人喜欢的笨拙年轻人。

例句：She had a likably mischievous smile. 她有一个讨人喜欢的淘气笑容。


近义词：lovable, amiable, pleasant, agreeable, charming

反义词：unlikeable, unpleasant, disagreeable, repulsive

词性:形容词
