hanker:/ˈhæŋkər/ | v. |渴望，热望，想念 |To have a strong desire or longing for something.  |He hankered after his childhood home. 他渴望回到他的童年之家。| yearn, long, crave, desire |  disdain, dislike


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He hankers after fame and fortune. 他渴望名利。
She hankers for a simpler life. 她渴望过一种更简单的生活。

一般过去时:
I hankered after a quiet life in the countryside. 我曾渴望在乡下过平静的生活。
They hankered for the days of their youth. 他们想念年轻时的日子。

现在完成时:
She has hankered after that painting for years. 她多年来一直渴望得到那幅画。
He has hankered for a chance to travel the world. 他一直渴望有机会周游世界。

过去完成时:
I had hankered after a career in music before I changed my mind. 在我改变主意之前，我一直渴望从事音乐事业。
They had hankered for a reconciliation before their final falling out. 在他们最终决裂之前，他们一直渴望和解。


将来时:
I will always hanker after the taste of my grandmother's apple pie. 我将永远怀念我祖母的苹果派的味道。
She will continue to hanker after a peaceful retirement. 她将继续渴望一个平静的退休生活。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would hanker after a luxurious yacht. 如果我有钱，我会渴望拥有一艘豪华游艇。
I wish I didn't hanker after things I can't have. 我希望我不渴望我得不到的东西。


被动语态:
(The verb "hanker" is rarely used in the passive voice because it is an intransitive verb that doesn't take a direct object.)


疑问句:
Do you hanker after anything in particular? 你特别渴望什么吗？
Does he still hanker after his lost love? 他还渴望他的失恋吗？


否定句:
He doesn't hanker after material possessions. 他不渴望物质财富。
She doesn't hanker after power or influence. 她不渴望权力或影响力。


PHRASE->

hanker after: 渴望，热望
hanker for: 渴望，想念
hanker to:  渴望做某事 (less common)
hanker after something lost: 渴望失去的东西
hanker for the past: 怀念过去
