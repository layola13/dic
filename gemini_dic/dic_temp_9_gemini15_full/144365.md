outstep:/ˌaʊtˈstɛp/| v.|超过，超越，胜过|To go beyond or exceed (something or someone) in extent, amount, or degree.|例句：He outstepped his authority by making that decision without consulting his superiors. 他未经上级同意就做出了那个决定，超过了他的权限。|例句：Her performance outstepped all expectations. 她的表现超出了所有人的预期。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, transcend|反义词：underperform, lag behind


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outsteps his colleagues in terms of efficiency. = 他在效率方面超过了他的同事。
The company outsteps its competitors in innovation. = 这家公司在创新方面超越了它的竞争对手。


一般过去时:
She outstepped her brother in the race. = 她在比赛中超过了她的哥哥。
The team outstepped their rivals by a significant margin. = 该队以显著的优势超过了他们的对手。


现在完成时:
He has outstepped his previous best score. = 他已经超过了他之前的最高分。
The project has outstepped its initial budget. = 这个项目已经超过了最初的预算。


过去完成时:
They had outstepped their original plan by a long shot. = 他们远远超出了最初的计划。
The explorers had outstepped their supplies and were facing starvation. = 探险家们已经用完了他们的补给，面临着饥饿。


将来时:
She will outstep her current limitations with hard work. = 通过努力工作，她将克服目前的局限性。
The new technology will outstep the capabilities of the old system. = 新技术将超越旧系统的功能。


虚拟语气:
If he worked harder, he would outstep his rivals. = 如果他更努力工作，他就会超过他的对手。
If she had trained more, she might have outstepped the champion. = 如果她训练更多，她可能就会超过冠军。


被动语态:
His authority was outstepped by the junior manager. = 他的权威被一位初级经理超越了。
The company's expectations were outstepped by the unexpected success. = 公司的预期被意想不到的成功所超越。


疑问句:
Does his ambition outstep his ability? = 他的野心超过了他的能力吗？
Will this technology outstep current market limitations? = 这项技术会超越当前的市场局限性吗？


否定句:
He did not outstep his boundaries. = 他没有越界。
The plan did not outstep the available resources. = 该计划没有超过可用的资源。


PHRASE->
outstep one's authority = 滥用职权
outstep the bounds of = 超越...的界限
outstep the competition = 超越竞争对手
outstep expectations = 超出预期
outstep oneself = 胜过自己
