shiver:/ˈʃɪvər/| v. n.|颤抖，哆嗦| to shake slightly because you are cold, frightened, or ill |The dog shivered in the cold wind.  那条狗在寒风中瑟瑟发抖。|I shivered with fear. 我吓得发抖。|tremble, shake, shudder|sweat, calm
