cleanser:/ˈklɛnzər/| n.|清洁剂，洁肤剂|A cleanser is a substance used to clean something, especially the skin.  It can be a liquid, cream, or gel.|例句：I use a gentle facial cleanser every morning. 我每天早上都用温和的洁面乳。

例句：She applied the cleanser to her face and then rinsed it off. 她将洁面乳涂在脸上，然后冲洗干净。


近义词：cleaner, detergent, purifier|反义词：pollutant, contaminant|名词
