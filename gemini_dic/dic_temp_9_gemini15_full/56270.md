dogfighting:/ˈdɒɡˌfaɪtɪŋ/| n. |狗斗；飞机格斗；（尤指）斗狗|The act of two or more aircraft engaging in close-range combat; also, the practice of pitting dogs against each other in a fight.|例句：The pilot skillfully executed a series of maneuvers during the dogfight.  飞行员在空战中巧妙地执行了一系列机动动作。

例句：Dogfighting is illegal in many countries.  在许多国家，斗狗是违法的。|近义词：air combat, aerial battle, dogfight (as a verb)|反义词：peaceful cooperation, friendly competition|词性:noun
