corrupt:/kəˈrʌpt/| vt. adj. n.|腐败的；堕落的；败坏的；贿赂；使腐败；使堕落；|to make someone or something morally bad or dishonest; to damage or destroy the purity or integrity of something. |例句：The scandal corrupted the reputation of the company.  这场丑闻败坏了公司的名誉。

The politician was corrupted by bribery.  这位政客被贿赂腐蚀了。


近义词：debauch,  pervert,  contaminate,  degrade,  vitiate|反义词：purify,  reform,  rectify,  improve,  cleanse|动词
