non-Islamic: /ˌnɒnɪˈzlæmɪk/ | adj. | 非伊斯兰教的 | Not relating to or connected with Islam.  This adjective describes things, people, or ideas that are outside the scope or influence of Islam.


英文解析:The word is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "Islamic" (relating to Islam).  It indicates a lack of association or adherence to Islamic principles, beliefs, or practices.

例句:

He follows a non-Islamic faith. = 他信仰非伊斯兰教。

The government implemented non-Islamic laws in certain regions. = 政府在某些地区实施了非伊斯兰教法。


近义词:un-Islamic, non-Muslim (referring to people), secular, irreligious (in a broader context)


反义词:Islamic, Muslim (referring to people)


词性:adjective
