sahibdom:/ˈsɑːhɪbdəm/| n. | 统治；统治权；（尤指）印度统治者的统治 | The rule or dominion of a sahib, especially in India; the social and political system under the rule of sahibs.  This term carries a strong historical and often negative connotation, reflecting the colonial power structure in India.  | Example: The sahibdom exerted its control through a complex system of laws and administration.  印度统治者的统治通过一套复杂的法律和行政系统来行使权力。|近义词：rule, dominion, empire, reign | 反义词：subjugation, servitude, colonialism (as an antonym to the power structure itself)
