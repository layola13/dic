parapsoriasis:/ˌpærəpˌsoʊˈraɪəsɪs/| n. |副银屑病|A group of skin conditions characterized by scaly, itchy patches that resemble psoriasis but lack the characteristic histological features.  It's often a chronic condition with variable symptoms and  no known cause. Several subtypes exist. |Example: The dermatologist diagnosed her with parapsoriasis lichenoides, a type characterized by small, reddish-brown papules.  皮肤科医生诊断她患有苔藓样副银屑病，其特征是小而红棕色的丘疹。|近义词：psoriasiform dermatitis, scaly skin condition|反义词：None specifically, but conditions without scaly patches.
