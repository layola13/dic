merlins:/ˈmɜːrlɪnz/| n.|小猎鹰；（尤指）灰林鸮|A merlin is a small falcon, often associated with its speed and hunting prowess.  It is also sometimes used to refer to a type of owl, specifically the short-eared owl.|例句：The merlin swooped down and caught its prey in a flash.  这只小猎鹰俯冲而下，闪电般抓住了猎物。

例句：We saw a merlin hunting near the river.我们在河边看到一只小猎鹰在捕猎。


近义词：falcon, kestrel|反义词：None|词性:名词
