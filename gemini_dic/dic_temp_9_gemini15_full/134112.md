nonimmunized:/ˌnɒnˈɪmjuːnaɪzd/| adj. |未免疫的，未接种疫苗的 |Not protected against infectious disease by vaccination or previous infection.  This term refers to individuals who have not received the necessary vaccinations or have not developed immunity through natural infection.  |例句：The nonimmunized children are at greater risk of contracting measles. 未接种疫苗的孩子更容易感染麻疹。
The study focused on the health outcomes of nonimmunized individuals. 该研究的重点是非免疫个体的健康结果。

近义词：unvaccinated, unprotected
反义词：immunized, vaccinated
