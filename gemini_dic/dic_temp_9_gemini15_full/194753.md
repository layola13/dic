seljukian: /sɛlˈdʒuːkiən/ | adj. |塞尔柱的；塞尔柱王朝的 | Relating to the Seljuk Turks or their empire.  The adjective refers to the Seljuk dynasty, a powerful Turkic empire that dominated much of the Middle East and parts of Central Asia from the 11th to the 13th centuries.  The term is often used to describe the art, architecture, and culture of this period. | Example: Seljukian architecture is characterized by its intricate ornamentation and use of domes.  塞尔柱的建筑以其复杂的装饰和圆顶的使用为特征。|近义词: Seljuk, Turkic (in the context of the empire)|反义词: N/A
