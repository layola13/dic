pantries:/ˈpæn(t)riːz/ | n. | 食品储藏室；餐具室 | A pantry is a room or cupboard for storing food, dishes, and other kitchen supplies.  | Example:  The pantry was stocked with enough food for a week. 食品储藏室里储存着足够一周的食物。  The new house has a large walk-in pantry. 新房子有一个很大的步入式食品储藏室。|近义词：larder, storeroom |反义词：None

词源:
The word "pantry" ultimately comes from the Old French word "paneterie," which means "bread room."  This in turn derives from "pain," meaning "bread."  The evolution reflects the pantry's original function as a place for storing bread and other baked goods.
