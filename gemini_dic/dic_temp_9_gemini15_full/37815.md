compensating: /ˈkɒmpənseɪtɪŋ/ | /ˈkɑːmpənseɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 补偿；赔偿；抵消；弥补 | To make up for something that is lacking or unsatisfactory.  | 例句：The company is compensating employees for the lost wages. 公司正在补偿员工因工资损失造成的损失。 | 例句：The warm weather is compensating for the lack of sunshine.  温暖的天气弥补了阳光的不足。 | 近义词：reimbursing, offsetting, making up for | 反义词：depleting, worsening


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company compensates its employees well. = 公司给员工的薪水很高。
He compensates for his lack of experience with hard work. = 他用努力工作来弥补经验不足。


一般过去时:
They compensated the victims for their losses. = 他们赔偿了受害者的损失。
She compensated me for the damage to my car. = 她赔偿了我汽车的损失。


现在完成时:
The government has compensated the farmers for their crop failures. = 政府已经赔偿了农民的歉收损失。
We have compensated for the delay by working overtime. = 我们通过加班来弥补延误。


过去完成时:
They had compensated the workers before the strike began. = 罢工开始前，他们已经赔偿了工人的损失。
The company had compensated for the lost revenue by cutting costs. = 公司通过削减成本弥补了收入损失。


将来时:
The insurance company will compensate you for the stolen goods. = 保险公司将赔偿你被盗物品的损失。
They will compensate the employees for their extra hours. = 他们将补偿员工的加班时间。


虚拟语气:
If they compensated us fairly, we would be satisfied. = 如果他们公平地赔偿我们，我们会满意。
I wish they had compensated the victims properly. = 我希望他们已经妥善赔偿了受害者。


被动语态:
The employees will be compensated for their overtime. = 员工的加班将得到补偿。
The damage was compensated for by the insurance company. = 损失由保险公司赔偿。


疑问句:
Will the company compensate us for the inconvenience? = 公司会赔偿我们因不便造成的损失吗？
How will they compensate for the lost time? = 他们将如何弥补损失的时间？


否定句:
The company did not compensate the employees for their hard work. = 公司没有因为员工的辛勤工作而给予补偿。
They refused to compensate for the damage they caused. = 他们拒绝赔偿他们造成的损失。


PHRASE->
compensate for = 弥补
compensate somebody for something = 赔偿某人某物
compensate someone's loss = 补偿某人的损失
fully compensate = 全额赔偿
compensating control = 补偿控制
