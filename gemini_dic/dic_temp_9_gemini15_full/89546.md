honored:/ˈɑːnəd/ | adj. | 荣幸的，受尊敬的 | Feeling or showing great respect for someone or something.  |例句：I am honored to receive this award. 我很荣幸获得这个奖项。

例句：She felt honored to be invited to the president's dinner. 她很荣幸被邀请参加总统的晚宴。


近义词：privileged, respected, esteemed, lauded | 反义词：disgraced, dishonored, disrespected


词源:源自honor（荣誉，尊敬），加上-ed后缀，变成形容词。 honor 来自古法语 honour，最终源自拉丁语 honor（荣誉）。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am honored to meet you. = 我很荣幸见到你。
He is honored to be a part of this project. = 他很荣幸能参与这个项目。


一般过去时:
She was honored by the invitation. = 她很荣幸收到邀请。
They were honored to have her speak at their event. = 他们很荣幸请她来他们的活动上发言。


现在完成时:
I have been honored by your kindness. = 我一直因为你的好意而感到荣幸。
We have been honored to serve our community. = 我们一直很荣幸能为我们的社区服务。


过去完成时:
She had been honored many times before. = 她以前曾多次获得表彰。
They had been honored to work with such talented individuals. = 他们很荣幸能与如此有才华的人一起工作。


将来时:
I will be honored to assist you. = 我将荣幸地协助你。
She will be honored to accept the award on behalf of the team. = 她将很荣幸代表团队接受这个奖项。


虚拟语气:
I would be honored if you would accept my invitation. = 如果你接受我的邀请，我将不胜荣幸。
It would have been an honor if they had attended. = 如果他们能来就好了（这将是一种荣幸）。


被动语态:
He was honored with a medal for bravery. = 他因勇敢而获得一枚勋章。
The team was honored for their outstanding achievements. = 该团队因其杰出成就而受到表彰。


疑问句:
Would you be honored to speak at our conference? = 你愿意在我们的会议上发言吗？（这将对你来说是莫大的荣幸）
Are you honored to be chosen for this role? = 你被选中担任这个角色感到荣幸吗？


否定句:
I was not honored by his actions. = 我并不因为他的行为而感到荣幸。
They were not honored to be associated with such a dishonest organization. = 他们并不荣幸与这样一个不诚实的组织有关系。


PHRASE->
feel honored = 感到荣幸
be honored to = 很荣幸能...
highly honored = 非常荣幸
deeply honored = 深感荣幸
to feel honored = 感觉荣幸
