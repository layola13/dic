mirrored:/ˈmɪrərd/ | adj. v. |反射的；镜像的；反映的 |  Reflecting or showing an image like a mirror; resembling a mirror.  |例句：The lake mirrored the mountains.  湖面映照着群山。

The polished floor mirrored the lights.  抛光的地板反射着灯光。|近义词：reflected, duplicated, imitated |反义词：original, unique


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lake mirrors the sky. = 湖面映照着天空。
The glass mirrors my face. = 玻璃镜子里映出我的脸。


一般过去时:
The still water mirrored the sunset. = 静止的水面映照着日落。
The old building mirrored the architecture of the time. = 这座古老的建筑反映了当时的建筑风格。


现在完成时:
The artist has mirrored the emotions of the subject in his painting. = 艺术家在他的画作中反映了人物的情绪。
The success of this project has mirrored the company's overall growth. = 这个项目的成功反映了公司的整体增长。


过去完成时:
The painting had mirrored the artist's inner turmoil before he found peace. = 在他找到平静之前，这幅画反映了艺术家内心的骚动。
The earlier events had mirrored the current crisis, showing a pattern of negligence. = 早期事件反映了当前的危机，显示出一种疏忽的模式。


将来时:
The new building will mirror the design of the old one. = 新建筑将模仿旧建筑的设计。
The exhibition will mirror the artist's life and career. = 展览将反映艺术家的生平和事业。


虚拟语气:
If the surface were smoother, it would mirror the image more clearly. = 如果表面更光滑，它就能更清晰地反射图像。
I wish my life mirrored the happiness I see in others. = 我希望我的生活能像我看到别人的幸福一样。


被动语态:
The scene was mirrored in the still water. = 场景被映照在平静的水面上。
The artist's style was mirrored by many of his contemporaries. = 许多同时代的艺术家都模仿了这位艺术家的风格。


疑问句:
Does this painting mirror the artist's emotions? = 这幅画是否反映了艺术家的情感？
Does the behavior of these children mirror the parenting style they received? = 这些孩子的行为是否反映了他们所接受的养育方式？


否定句:
The reflection did not accurately mirror the object's shape. = 反射并没有准确地反映物体的形状。
His actions did not mirror his words. = 他的行为与他的话语不符。
