re-fixing:/ˌriːˈfɪksɪŋ/| n.|重新固定；再次固定|The act of fixing something again.  It implies a previous fixing attempt that was unsuccessful or needs improvement.|例句：The re-fixing of the loose tile required more adhesive.  重新固定松动的瓷砖需要更多粘合剂。
例句：The re-fixing of the broken fence took the entire afternoon.  重新修理损坏的栅栏花了一下午的时间。|近义词：refastening, repairing, mending|反义词：unfixing, loosening, detaching|名词
