nondiscursive:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈskɜː.sɪv/| adj. |非论述性的，非议论性的 |Not involving or relating to discourse or reasoned argument; not discursive.  It often refers to things that are presented visually or through other non-verbal means, rather than through a logical progression of ideas. | Example:  The artist's work was nondiscursive, relying more on emotional impact than on narrative.  这位艺术家的作品是非论述性的，更依赖于情感冲击而不是叙事。|近义词：non-argumentative, nonverbal, visual, imagistic |反义词：discursive, argumentative, verbal
