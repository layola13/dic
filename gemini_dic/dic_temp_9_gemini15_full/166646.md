presenting:/prɪˈzentɪŋ/ | v. |介绍；展示；提出；赠送 | Presenting information clearly is crucial for effective communication. 清晰地呈现信息对于有效的沟通至关重要。|近义词：showing, displaying, introducing, offering |反义词：concealing, hiding, withdrawing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is presenting a new product today. 他今天正在展示一款新产品。
She presents her research findings at the conference. 她在会议上展示她的研究成果。

一般过去时:
The professor presented a compelling argument. 教授提出了一个令人信服的论点。
They presented the award to the winner. 他们把奖颁给了获胜者。

现在完成时:
He has presented his case to the jury. 他已经向陪审团陈述了他的案情。
She has presented numerous papers on this topic. 她已经就此主题发表了许多论文。

过去完成时:
Before the meeting, she had presented her proposal to the committee. 会议之前，她已向委员会提交了她的提案。
They had presented their findings before the deadline. 他们在截止日期之前已经提交了他们的研究结果。

将来时:
The CEO will be presenting the company's financial results next week.  首席执行官下周将公布公司财务业绩。
She will present her work at the international symposium. 她将在国际研讨会上展示她的作品。

虚拟语气:
If he presented more evidence, the judge might believe him. 如果他提供更多证据，法官可能会相信他。
I wish I had presented my ideas more clearly. 我希望我当初能更清晰地表达我的想法。

被动语态:
The award was presented to the deserving candidate. 奖项颁给了应得的候选人。
The data is being presented in a variety of formats. 数据正在以各种格式呈现。

疑问句:
Is he presenting at the conference? 他会在会议上做报告吗？
Who will be presenting the keynote speech? 谁将发表主题演讲？

否定句:
He didn't present any evidence to support his claim. 他没有提供任何证据来支持他的说法。
She wasn't presenting the data accurately. 她没有准确地呈现数据。


PHRASE->
presenting oneself:  展现自我
