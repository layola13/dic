monophyllous:/ˌmɒnəˈfɪləs/| adj.|单叶的；单瓣的|Having only one leaf or petal.  The term is often used in botany to describe plants with a single leaf per node. |例句：The plant exhibits monophyllous leaves, unlike its relative with compound leaves. 这株植物的叶子是单叶的，不同于其具有复叶的近亲。  The flower boasts a monophyllous corolla, creating a simple, elegant appearance. 这朵花拥有单瓣花冠，呈现出简洁优雅的外观。|近义词：unifoliate|反义词：polyphyllous
