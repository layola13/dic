nonproblematical: /ˌnɒnˌprɒbləˈmætɪkəl/ | adj. | 不成问题的，简单的，容易解决的 |  Not causing or presenting problems; easy to deal with.  The project proved to be surprisingly nonproblematical.  |  例句：The transition to the new software was surprisingly nonproblematical.  新软件的过渡出乎意料地顺利。

The implementation of the new policy was relatively nonproblematical. 新政策的实施相对顺利。


近义词：easy, simple, straightforward, uncomplicated, hassle-free | 反义词：problematic, difficult, challenging, complex, troublesome


词源:  The word is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") + "problematical" (meaning "relating to or involving problems").  "Problematical" itself derives from "problem" + "-atic" (a suffix forming adjectives).  The ultimate root is likely the Greek word "problema," meaning "something thrown forward" or "a question proposed for solution."
