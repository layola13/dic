curmudgeons:/ˈkɜːrmʌdʒənz/| n.|脾气暴躁的人，抱怨的人|A curmudgeon is a bad-tempered, surly, and usually old person.  They are often grumpy and unwilling to compromise. |例句：He's a grumpy old curmudgeon who complains about everything. 他是个脾气暴躁的老家伙，什么都抱怨。

例句：The curmudgeons in the neighborhood constantly complained about the noise from the children's playground.  邻居里那些脾气暴躁的人总是抱怨孩子们操场上发出的噪音。


近义词：grump, grouch, crab, cynic, misanthrope|反义词：sweetheart, darling, angel
