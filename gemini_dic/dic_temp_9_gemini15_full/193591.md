self-assemblies:/ˌsɛlf əˈsɛmbliz/| n.|自组装体|The spontaneous organization of components into larger, ordered structures without external guidance or control.  This process is driven by inherent properties of the components, such as chemical interactions or physical forces.|例句：Nanomaterials often exhibit remarkable self-assemblies, forming complex structures with unique properties. 纳米材料经常表现出显著的自组装特性，形成具有独特性能的复杂结构。

例句：The researchers observed the self-assemblies of proteins into intricate filaments. 研究人员观察到蛋白质自组装成复杂的细丝。


近义词：self-organization, spontaneous organization |反义词：external assembly, directed assembly|名词
