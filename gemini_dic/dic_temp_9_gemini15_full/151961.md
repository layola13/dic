pastoring:/ˈpɑːstərɪŋ/| v.|牧养；担任牧师；指导|The act of serving as a pastor; providing spiritual guidance and leadership to a congregation.  It also implies nurturing and caring for the spiritual well-being of a group of people.|例句：He dedicated his life to pastoring the small rural church. 他将自己的一生奉献给了乡村小教堂的牧养工作。|例句：The pastor's pastoring style emphasized community engagement.  这位牧师的牧养风格强调社区参与。|近义词：shepherding, ministering, guiding|反义词：neglecting, abandoning
