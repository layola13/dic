pseudoanthropoid:/ˌsuːdoʊænθrəˈpɔɪd/| adj.|似人的，类人猿的| seeming like a human being or an ape;  resembling a human being in certain respects but not truly human.  |例句：The creature, though exhibiting pseudoanthropoid features, lacked the intelligence of a human being.  虽然该生物表现出类人特征，但缺乏人类的智力。


例句：Some scientists debate whether certain fossils represent true hominids or merely pseudoanthropoid primates. 一些科学家争论某些化石代表真正的人类还是仅仅是类人灵长类。


近义词：anthropoid-like, hominid-like, human-like


反义词：non-human, inhuman, subhuman


词性：形容词
