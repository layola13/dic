roundabout:/ˈraʊndəbaʊt/ | n. adj. adv. | 环状交叉路口；迂回的；绕圈子的；大约 |  A roundabout is a circular road intersection where traffic flows around a central island.  It can also describe something indirect or circuitous. |例句：We took a roundabout route to avoid the traffic. =为了避开交通拥堵，我们绕了一条路。

The roundabout was confusing to navigate. = 这个环状交叉路口很难通行。


近义词：traffic circle, rotary, circuitous, indirect | 反义词：direct, straight, straightforward

词源:
