pregenerating: /ˌpriːˈdʒɛnəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 预先生成；预先产生 |  The act of generating something beforehand, typically data, resources, or content, to improve efficiency or availability.  | 例句：The system is pregenerating thumbnails to speed up page load times. = 系统正在预先生成缩略图以加快页面加载速度。

例句：The server pregenerates reports overnight to ensure they are ready for the morning. = 服务器在夜间预先生成报表以确保它们在早上准备好。


近义词：precomputing, pre-calculating, preparing in advance | 反义词：generating on demand, improvising


词性：动词
