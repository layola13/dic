overprotected:/ˌoʊvərprəˈtɛktɪd/ | adj. |过度保护的，溺爱的| Excessively protected; shielded from the normal risks and challenges of life.  This often leads to a lack of independence and resilience in the person being protected. |例句：The overprotected child struggled to adapt to college life.  过度保护的孩子难以适应大学生活。

例句：She felt overprotected by her parents and longed for more independence. 她感觉父母过度保护她，渴望拥有更多独立性。


近义词：sheltered, coddled, pampered, shielded


反义词：neglected, abandoned, exposed


词源:

* **over-**:  prefix indicating "too much," "excessive," or "beyond."  源自古英语 *ofer-, *of-,  表示“过度”、“超过”。

* **protect**: from Old French *proteger, ultimately from Latin *protegere ("to cover, shelter, defend"). 源自古法语 *proteger，最终源自拉丁语 *protegere（“覆盖、庇护、保护”）。

* **-ed**: past participle suffix, forming adjectives.  过去分词后缀，构成形容词。
