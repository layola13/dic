foregrounded:/ˈfɔːrˌɡraʊndɪd/| adj. | 突出的，显著的；被强调的 |  Given prominence or emphasis; made the focus of attention.  The report foregrounded the urgent need for action.  | 近义词：emphasized, highlighted, prominent, salient | 反义词：backgrounded, understated, downplayed

例句：

The artist foregrounded the figure in the painting, making it the central focus.  | 艺术家将画中的人物放在前景，使其成为中心焦点。

The speech foregrounded the importance of education reform. | 这篇演讲强调了教育改革的重要性。
