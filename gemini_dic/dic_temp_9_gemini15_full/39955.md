construct: /kənˈstrʌkt/ | vt. n. | 建造；构建；建造物；结构 | To build or create something, typically something complex.  |例句：They are constructing a new bridge across the river. 他们正在建造一座横跨河流的新桥。  |近义词：build, erect, fabricate, form |反义词：destroy, demolish, deconstruct |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They construct new houses every year. = 他们每年都建造新房子。
The company constructs software solutions for businesses. = 这家公司为企业构建软件解决方案。


一般过去时:
The ancient Egyptians constructed magnificent pyramids. = 古埃及人建造了宏伟的金字塔。
We constructed a detailed plan for the project. = 我们为这个项目制定了一个详细的计划。


现在完成时:
They have constructed a new stadium in the city center. = 他们在市中心建造了一个新的体育场。
The team has constructed a successful marketing campaign. = 该团队已经策划了一个成功的营销活动。


过去完成时:
They had constructed the building before the deadline. = 他们已在截止日期前建好大楼。
The artist had constructed a complex narrative in her artwork. = 艺术家在她的作品中构建了一个复杂的叙事。


将来时:
They will construct a new highway next year. = 他们明年将建造一条新的高速公路。
The government will construct affordable housing for low-income families. = 政府将为低收入家庭建造经济适用房。


虚拟语气:
If they had constructed the bridge properly, it wouldn't have collapsed. = 如果他们当初正确地建造了桥梁，它就不会坍塌。
I wish they had constructed the building with more sustainable materials. = 我希望他们用更环保的材料建造这座建筑物。


被动语态:
The new library is being constructed by the city council. = 新图书馆正在由市议会建造。
The software was constructed using the latest technology. = 该软件是使用最新技术构建的。


疑问句:

否定句:
They did not construct the building according to the plan. = 他们没有按照计划建造大楼。
The company will not construct a new factory in this area. = 这家公司不会在这个地区建造新工厂。
