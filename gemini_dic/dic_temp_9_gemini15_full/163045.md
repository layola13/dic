preclearing: /ˌpriːˈklɪəriŋ/ | n. | 预先清理；预先结算 | The act of clearing something beforehand, typically debts or accounts.  It often involves preparing or settling something in advance to avoid future complications or delays. | 例句：The preclearing of customs documents saved us hours at the border. 预先清理海关文件为我们在边境节省了数小时。


The company's preclearing of its debts improved its financial standing.  公司的债务预先清理改善了其财务状况。| 近义词：advance settlement, prior clearance, preliminary clearance | 反义词：post-clearance, delayed settlement | 名词
