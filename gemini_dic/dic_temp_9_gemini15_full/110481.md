lily-like: /ˈlɪliˌlaɪk/ | adj. |百合花似的，像百合花的；纯洁的，美丽的 | Resembling a lily in appearance, especially in its delicate beauty and purity.  It often implies grace, elegance, and sometimes fragility. | 例句：Her skin had a lily-like pallor.  她的皮肤有着百合花般苍白的颜色。

Her lily-like gown was stunning. 她的百合花似的礼服令人惊艳。


近义词：dainty, delicate, elegant, graceful, pure, pristine | 反义词：coarse, rough, unrefined, ugly, soiled


词性：形容词
