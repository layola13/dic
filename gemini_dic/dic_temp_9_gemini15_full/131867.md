nonconsumption: /ˌnɒnkənˈsʌmpʃən/ | n. |非消费；未消费 |The act or state of not consuming something; the opposite of consumption.  It can refer to goods not purchased, services not utilized, or resources left unexploited.  This term is often used in economic contexts to describe unsold goods or unused resources. |例句：The high price led to a significant level of nonconsumption of luxury goods. 高价导致奢侈品消费量显著下降。

The increasing nonconsumption of fossil fuels is a positive sign for the environment.  化石燃料的消耗减少对环境来说是一个积极的信号。|近义词： abstention, avoidance, forgoing |反义词： consumption, utilization, expenditure |
