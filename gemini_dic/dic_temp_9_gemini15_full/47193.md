daydreamed:/ˈdeɪˌdriːmd/| v.| 白日梦；幻想 | To indulge in daydreams; to have one's thoughts wander idly.  |例句：I daydreamed about winning the lottery. 我做着中彩票的白日梦。
I daydreamed of a life on a tropical island. 我幻想着在热带岛屿上的生活。|近义词：fantasized, pondered, mused |反义词：focused, concentrated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He daydreams in class. = 他上课时做白日梦。
She often daydreams about her future. = 她经常幻想她的未来。

一般过去时:
I daydreamed during the boring meeting. = 在无聊的会议上我做着白日梦。
They daydreamed about their vacation. = 他们幻想着他们的假期。

现在完成时:
I have daydreamed about this moment for years. = 我多年来一直梦想着这一刻。
She has daydreamed of becoming a doctor since she was a child. = 从小她就梦想成为一名医生。

过去完成时:
I had daydreamed of meeting him before we actually met. = 在我们真正见面之前，我就梦想着见到他。
She had daydreamed about traveling the world before she got the job. = 在她得到这份工作之前，她就梦想着环游世界。

将来时:
I will daydream about my upcoming trip. = 我会幻想着我即将到来的旅行。
They will probably daydream during the long flight. = 他们很可能会在漫长的飞行中做白日梦。


虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would daydream more often. = 如果我有时间，我会更频繁地做白日梦。
I wish I could daydream without feeling guilty. = 我希望我可以不用感到内疚地做白日梦。


被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice because it is an intransitive verb.)  他的白日梦被打断了。(His daydream was interrupted.)


疑问句:
Do you often daydream? = 你经常做白日梦吗？
What do you daydream about? = 你在幻想什么？


否定句:
I didn't daydream during the test. = 我考试时没有做白日梦。
She doesn't daydream as much as she used to. = 她不像以前那样经常做白日梦了。


PHRASE->
daydream away:  消磨时间做白日梦 (to spend time daydreaming)
daydream about:  幻想... (to imagine or fantasize about)
lose oneself in daydreams:  沉浸在白日梦中 (to become completely absorbed in daydreams)
pleasant daydreams:  美好的白日梦 (happy and enjoyable daydreams)
idle daydreams:  漫无目的的白日梦 (daydreams without a clear purpose)


词源:
The word "daydream" is a compound word formed from "day" and "dream."  It emerged in the late 19th century as a natural combination reflecting the activity of dreaming during waking hours.
