nonhostilely:/ˌnɑːnˈhɑːstaɪlli/| adv. |不怀恶意地，友善地|In a manner that is not hostile; in a friendly or peaceable way.  It implies a lack of aggression or antagonism. |例句：He approached the situation nonhostilely, seeking a peaceful resolution. 他以不怀恶意的态度处理这件事，寻求和平解决。  The police officer spoke to the suspect nonhostilely, hoping to gain cooperation. 警官和嫌疑人说话很友善，希望能获得合作。|近义词：peaceably, amicably, benignly, kindly|反义词：hostilely, aggressively, antagonistically|词性:副词
