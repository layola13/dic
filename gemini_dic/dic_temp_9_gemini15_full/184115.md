resinning: /ˈrɛzɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 树脂处理；涂树脂；再涂树脂 | The act of applying resin to a surface, often to seal, protect, or strengthen it. It can also refer to the process of reapplying resin to a surface that has already been treated. |  例句：The boat needs resinning to seal the cracks.  = 这艘船需要涂树脂来封住裂缝。

The antique furniture required careful resinning to restore its original luster. = 这件古董家具需要仔细地涂上树脂才能恢复其原来的光泽。


近义词：varnishing, coating, sealing | 反义词：removing resin, stripping

词源:  The word "resinning" is derived from the noun "resin" (a sticky substance secreted by some plants) plus the suffix "-ing," indicating the act of doing something.  The origin of "resin" itself goes back to Old French "resine" and ultimately to Latin "resina".
