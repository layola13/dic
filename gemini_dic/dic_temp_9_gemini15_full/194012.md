self-exultations:/ˌsɛlf ɪɡˈzʌlˈteɪʃənz/| n. |自我陶醉；得意洋洋；自我吹嘘 |Self-exultations are feelings of excessive pride and satisfaction in oneself or one's achievements.  It often implies an inflated sense of self-importance and can be seen as arrogant or boastful. |例句：His self-exultations were evident in his pompous speech.  他的自我吹嘘在他的夸夸其谈中显而易见。

例句：Her constant self-exultations alienated her friends. 她不断地自我陶醉疏远了她的朋友们。


近义词：self-satisfaction, gloating, boasting, self-aggrandizement, pride (in excessive amounts)

反义词：humility, modesty, self-deprecation


词性：名词 (plural)
