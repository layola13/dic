conciliatorily:/kənˈsɪliəˌtɔːrɪli/ | adj. | 调解地；抚慰地；劝慰地 | In a way that seeks to reconcile or appease.  It suggests an attempt to soothe anger or resentment and restore harmony. | He spoke conciliatorily, hoping to end the argument. 他和解地说话，希望能结束争论。|  appeasingly, pacifically, placatingly | antagonistically, aggressively, belligerently | 形容词
