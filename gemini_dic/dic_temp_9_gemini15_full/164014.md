prediplomatic: /ˌpriːˌdɪpləˈmætɪk/ | adj. |  外交前的，准外交的 | Relating to the period or activities before formal diplomatic relations are established.  It describes actions, negotiations, or communications that occur before full diplomatic recognition or the establishment of an embassy. |


例句：

The prediplomatic contacts between the two countries laid the groundwork for a future treaty.  = 两国外交前的接触为未来的条约奠定了基础。

Prediplomatic discussions focused on confidence-building measures. = 外交前的讨论集中在建立信任措施上。


近义词： preliminary, initial, preparatory


反义词： diplomatic, official


词性：形容词
