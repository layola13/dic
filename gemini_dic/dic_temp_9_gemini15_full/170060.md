protest:/ˈprəʊtest/| /ˈproʊtest/| n., v.|抗议，反对；声明；断言|To express strong disapproval of or opposition to something.  A statement or action expressing disapproval of or opposition to something.|例句：They protested against the war.  他们抗议这场战争。

例句：He protested his innocence. 他声明自己是无辜的。|近义词：demonstration, objection, complaint, dissent|反义词：acceptance, approval, consent|词性: Noun, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They protest the government's policies. = 他们抗议政府的政策。
Students protest tuition increases every year. = 学生们每年都抗议学费上涨。


一般过去时:
The workers protested their low wages. = 工人们抗议他们低廉的工资。
She protested her innocence to the police. = 她向警方声明自己是无辜的。


现在完成时:
They have protested against the new law for months. = 他们已经抗议这项新法律好几个月了。
Activists have protested tirelessly for social justice. = 激进分子为社会正义不知疲倦地抗议。


过去完成时:
They had protested the decision before it was implemented. = 在这项决定实施之前，他们就已经抗议过了。
The community had protested the factory's pollution for years. = 社区多年来一直在抗议该工厂的污染。


将来时:
They will protest the unfair treatment at the upcoming rally. = 他们将在即将到来的集会上抗议这种不公平的待遇。
We expect that many people will protest the proposed changes. = 我们预计很多人会抗议拟议中的变化。


虚拟语气:
If they protested louder, maybe they would be heard. = 如果他们抗议的声音更大一些，也许他们就会被听到。
I wish they had protested earlier. = 我希望他们早点抗议。


被动语态:
The proposed changes were protested by many citizens. = 许多市民抗议了拟议中的变化。
The demonstration was violently protested by the authorities. = 这次示威活动受到了当局的暴力镇压。


疑问句:
Will they protest the new regulations? = 他们会抗议新的规章制度吗？
Did anyone protest the decision? = 有人抗议这个决定吗？


否定句:
They did not protest the decision. = 他们没有抗议这个决定。
He didn't protest when his rights were violated. = 当他的权利受到侵犯时，他并没有抗议。


PHRASE->
protest against = 反对
lodge a protest = 提出抗议
file a protest = 提出抗议
under protest = 在抗议下
in protest = 以示抗议

词源:
源自拉丁语“protestari”，意为“公开声明，公开宣称”。  This reflects the core meaning of publicly expressing strong disapproval or opposition.
