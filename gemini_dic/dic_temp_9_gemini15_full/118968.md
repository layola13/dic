meters:/ˈmiːtərz/ | n. |米（复数）|The plural form of "meter," a unit of length equal to 100 centimeters or one thousandth of a kilometer.  It is used to measure distance.|The room measures five meters in length.  这房间长五米。 The athletes ran a 100-meter race. 运动员们跑了一百米赛跑。|kilometers, centimeters, yards|inches, feet|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
These meters measure the water flow. = 这些仪表测量水流。
How many meters of fabric do you need? = 你需要多少米布？


一般过去时:
The race was 100 meters long. = 比赛长100米。
The builders measured the room in meters. = 建造者用米来测量房间。


现在完成时:

将来时:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
