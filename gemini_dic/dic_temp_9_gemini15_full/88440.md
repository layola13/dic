hisser:/ˈhɪsər/| n.|嘶嘶声发出嘶嘶声者|A person or thing that hisses.  A hisser is something that makes a hissing sound, like a snake or a leaky pipe.|例句：The snake was a hisser, warning of its potential strike.  那条蛇发出嘶嘶声，警告它可能要攻击。 The old radiator was a constant hisser throughout the night.  旧暖气片整夜都在发出嘶嘶声。|近义词：sizzler, whistler (depending on context)|反义词：None specifically;  antonyms depend on the context (e.g., if referring to a quiet animal, the antonym would be something quiet).|名词
