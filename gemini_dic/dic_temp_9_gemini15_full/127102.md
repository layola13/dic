nationalists: /ˈnæʃənəlɪsts/ | n. |民族主义者|People who advocate for national independence or the interests of their nation, often emphasizing national unity and identity. They may support policies that prioritize the nation's interests above others.  |例句：Many nationalists believe their country is superior to others. 许多民族主义者认为他们的国家优于其他国家。
例句：The rise of nationalism led to increased tensions between countries.民族主义的兴起导致了国家之间紧张局势的加剧。|近义词：patriots, ultranationalists|反义词：internationalists, globalists|名词
