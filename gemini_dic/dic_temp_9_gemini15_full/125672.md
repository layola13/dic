munched:/mʌntʃt/| v.|咀嚼，嚼碎|To chew (food) noisily.  |例句：He munched on an apple. 他大口地啃着苹果。
She munched her sandwich during the meeting.她在会议期间吃着三明治。|近义词：chew, gnaw, masticate|反义词：swallow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He munches on carrots every day. 他每天都吃胡萝卜。
She munches her food quietly. 她静静地吃东西。


一般过去时:
The dog munched on the bone happily.  狗高兴地啃着骨头。
I munched popcorn during the movie. 我在看电影时吃爆米花。


现在完成时:
The children have munched all the cookies. 孩子们已经把所有饼干都吃掉了。
She has munched through a whole bag of chips. 她已经吃掉了一整袋薯片。


过去完成时:
They had munched all the snacks before the party started.  派对开始前他们已经吃掉了所有零食。
He had munched his lunch before the meeting. 他在开会前已经吃过午饭了。


将来时:
I will munch on some nuts while I watch TV. 我看电视的时候会吃一些坚果。
They will munch their way through the entire pizza.他们会把整个披萨都吃掉。


虚拟语气:
If I were hungry, I would munch on a granola bar. 如果我饿了，我会吃一根格兰诺拉麦片棒。
I wish I could munch on some chocolate right now. 我真希望现在能吃些巧克力。


被动语态:
The apple was munched by the rabbit.  苹果被兔子吃掉了。
(Note:  Passive voice is less common with "munch".)


疑问句:
Did he munch the entire cake? 他把整个蛋糕都吃了吗？
What did you munch on for lunch? 午餐你吃了什么？


否定句:
He didn't munch on anything all day. 他一整天什么都没吃。
She didn't munch the cookies because she wasn't hungry. 她因为不饿所以没吃饼干。


PHRASE->
munch on: 吃……
munch down: 狼吞虎咽地吃
munch through: 吃光……
happily munch: 高兴地吃
quietly munch: 安静地吃
