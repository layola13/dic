disengagements:/ˌdɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənts/ | n. |脱离；脱节；解脱；解除；断开连接 | The act of disengaging; the state of being disengaged.  It often refers to the separation of something from something else, the release from an engagement or commitment, or a break in connection or involvement. |例句：The disengagement of the troops was a carefully planned operation.  部队的撤离是一次精心策划的行动。

The sudden disengagement from the project left the team scrambling.  突然退出项目让团队手忙脚乱。


近义词：separations, detachments, disconnections, releases | 反义词：engagements, connections, involvements | 词性：名词
