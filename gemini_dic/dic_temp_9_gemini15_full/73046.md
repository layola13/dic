footless:/ˈfʊtlɪs/| adj.|无足轻重的；无用的；无目标的 |lacking purpose or effectiveness; useless; without feet. |例句：His arguments were footless and unconvincing. 他的论点毫无根据且令人难以信服。

例句：The plan was footless from the start.  这个计划从一开始就毫无意义。


近义词：pointless, purposeless, futile, ineffective, baseless

反义词：purposeful, effective, useful, meaningful


词源：源自古英语*fōtles，由fōt（脚）和-lēas（无）构成。
