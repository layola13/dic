magnetize:/ˈmæɡnətaɪz/ | vt. | 使磁化；吸引；迷住 | To cause (a body) to become magnetic; to attract strongly; to fascinate. | 例句：The earth's magnetic field magnetizes the needle of a compass. 地球的磁场使指南针的指针磁化。 | 例句：His charisma magnetized the audience. 他的魅力吸引了观众。 | 近义词：attract, fascinate, charm | 反义词：demagnetize, repel


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He magnetizes metal objects. = 他使金属物体磁化。
The speaker magnetizes the crowd with his speech. = 演讲者用他的演讲吸引了人群。


一般过去时:
