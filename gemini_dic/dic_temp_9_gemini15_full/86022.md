healer:/ˈhiːlər/| n. |治疗者，治疗师，治愈者 |A healer is a person who treats illnesses or injuries, often using alternative or traditional methods.  Healers may use a variety of techniques, including herbal remedies, acupuncture, massage, and spiritual practices. |例句：She's a healer who uses a combination of massage and aromatherapy. 她是一位使用按摩和芳香疗法相结合的治疗师。

例句：Many people believe that faith healers can cure diseases. 许多人相信信仰治疗师可以治愈疾病。


近义词：therapist, physician, curer, practitioner |反义词：harmer, destroyer, injurer |词性:名词
