punch-in:/pʌntʃ ɪn/| vt.|打卡上班；录入；(拳击)猛击|To record one's arrival at work using a time clock or similar system.  It can also refer to entering data into a system. |例句：I punch in at 8:00 am every morning. = 我每天早上8点打卡上班。

例句：The manager asked me to punch in the new sales data. = 经理让我录入新的销售数据。|近义词：clock in, register, record|反义词：punch out, clock out
