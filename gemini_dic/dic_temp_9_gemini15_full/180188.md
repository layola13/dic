reformists:/rɪˈfɔːrmɪsts/| n. |改革者，改良主义者|Advocates of reform; those who favor gradual or incremental changes within a system rather than radical upheaval.  Reformists often work within existing structures to bring about change.|例句：The reformists proposed a series of amendments to the constitution. 改革者们提出一系列宪法修正案。|例句：Many reformists believe that gradual change is more effective than revolution. 许多改革者认为循序渐进的改变比革命更有效。|近义词：reformer, revisionist, progressive|反义词：reactionary, conservative, revolutionary|名词
