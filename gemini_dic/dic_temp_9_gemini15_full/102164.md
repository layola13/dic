isthmuses:/ˈɪzθməsɪz/| n. |地峡（复数）|Narrow strips of land connecting two larger landmasses, often with water on either side.  Isthmuses are important geographical features, sometimes serving as strategic locations for trade and defense. |例句：The Panama Canal cuts across the Isthmus of Panama, connecting the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans.巴拿马运河横穿巴拿马地峡，连接大西洋和太平洋。

The Isthmuses of Corinth and Suez are historically significant waterways. 科林斯地峡和苏伊士地峡在历史上具有重要的航运意义。|近义词：narrow strip, neck of land |反义词：wide expanse, open sea
