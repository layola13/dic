preclassifications: /ˌpriːklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. |预分类，初步分类 | Preliminary classifications or categorizations, often done before a more detailed or refined classification.  These are often used to quickly sort large amounts of data or items into broad groups. |Example: The initial preclassifications of the archaeological finds allowed for a more efficient later analysis.  初步的考古发现预分类使得后期的分析更加有效率。

|近义词: preliminary classifications, initial categorizations, rough sorting |反义词: final classifications, definitive categorizations |词性: noun
