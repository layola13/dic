nonunited:/ˌnɒnjuˈnaɪtɪd/| adj. | 未统一的，分裂的 | Not united; separated or divided.  The word implies a lack of unity or cohesion, often in a political or social context, suggesting a state of fragmentation or disharmony. | Example: The nonunited factions struggled to reach a consensus.  未统一的派系努力达成共识。 The country remains politically nonunited after the civil war.内战之后，这个国家仍然政治上分裂。|近义词：divided, fragmented, disunited, separated |反义词：united, unified, consolidated, cohesive
