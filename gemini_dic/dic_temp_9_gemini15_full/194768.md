sellers:/ˈsɛlərz/| n.|销售员；卖家；售货员|People or organizations that sell goods or services.|例句：Online sellers are becoming increasingly common. 在线卖家越来越普遍了。
例句：The sellers of these products are all reputable companies. 这些产品的卖家都是信誉良好的公司。|近义词：vendors, merchants, retailers|反义词：buyers, purchasers


USAGE->
