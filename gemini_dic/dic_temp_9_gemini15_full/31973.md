chemistry:/ˈkɛmɪstri/ | n. |化学 |The scientific study of the composition, structure, properties, and reactions of matter.  Chemistry explores the interactions between atoms and molecules and how these interactions lead to the formation of different substances. |Example:  Organic chemistry is the study of carbon-containing compounds. 有机化学是研究含碳化合物的学科。|  Example:  She excelled in her chemistry studies. 她在化学学习中表现出色。|近义词：chemical science |反义词： (lack of) physics, biology
