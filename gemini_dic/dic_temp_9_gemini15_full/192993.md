second-guessed:/ˌsekəndˈɡɛst/| v.|事后质疑，对…妄加评断|To question or doubt (a decision or action) that has already been made, often with the implication of criticizing it.  It suggests reconsidering a choice, often with a negative or critical tone.|例句：I second-guessed my decision to quit my job after I realized how much I missed the work. 我在意识到自己有多怀念工作后，开始质疑自己辞职的决定。

例句：Don't second-guess yourself; you made the right choice. 不要怀疑自己，你做出了正确的选择。


近义词：question, doubt, criticize, re-evaluate, reconsider|反义词：affirm, endorse, support|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He second-guesses every decision I make. = 他对我做的每一个决定都进行事后质疑。
They second-guess the coach's strategy after every game. = 每一场比赛后，他们都会对教练的策略进行事后质疑。


一般过去时:
I second-guessed my investment strategy after the market crashed. = 市场崩盘后，我开始质疑自己的投资策略。
She second-guessed her choice of university after her first semester. = 她大一第一学期结束后，开始质疑自己大学的选择。


现在完成时:
He has second-guessed himself countless times about this project. = 关于这个项目，他已经无数次地质疑自己了。
We have second-guessed our marketing plan, and we're making changes. = 我们已经重新考虑了我们的营销计划，并且正在做出改变。


过去完成时:
She had second-guessed her decision to move to the city before even arriving. = 甚至在到达城市之前，她就对自己搬到城市的决定产生了质疑。
They had second-guessed the initial design after several prototypes failed. = 在几个原型失败后，他们已经对最初的设计进行了质疑。


将来时:
I will second-guess my decision to buy this car if it keeps breaking down. = 如果这辆车一直出故障，我将质疑自己购买它的决定。
They will likely second-guess the policy change if it causes significant problems. = 如果这项政策变更导致重大问题，他们很可能会质疑这项变更。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't second-guessed my instincts, I would have been more successful. = 如果我没有怀疑自己的直觉，我会更成功。
I wish I hadn't second-guessed my initial plan. = 我真希望我没有怀疑我最初的计划。


被动语态:
His decision was second-guessed by many of his colleagues. = 他的决定受到了许多同事的质疑。
The team's strategy was constantly second-guessed by the media. = 球队的策略不断受到媒体的质疑。


疑问句:
Do you ever second-guess your decisions? = 你会质疑自己的决定吗？
Will they second-guess the government's response to the crisis? = 他们会质疑政府对危机的回应吗？


否定句:
I don't second-guess my decisions often. = 我很少质疑自己的决定。
They didn't second-guess the captain's orders during the emergency. = 在紧急情况下，他们没有质疑船长的命令。
