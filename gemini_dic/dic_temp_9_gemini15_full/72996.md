foolscaps:/ˈfuːlzˌkæp/| n.| foolscap纸；便笺簿 |A type of paper, originally a large size, now typically around 13 x 16 inches (33 x 41 cm).  The name derives from a watermark of a jester's cap (fool's cap) that was often used on this type of paper. |Example: The artist used foolscap paper for his sketches.  艺术家用便笺纸画素描。 The report was printed on foolscap. 报告印在便笺纸上。|近义词： writing paper, notepaper |反义词:  None specifically, but could be contrasted with smaller paper sizes.
