compliancy:/kəmˈplaɪənsi/ | n. | 顺从；服从；依从 | The state of being compliant; readiness to comply with rules, regulations, or requests.  It often implies a passive acceptance rather than enthusiastic agreement. | Example: The company's compliancy with environmental regulations was praised by the inspectors.  这家公司的环境法规遵从性受到了检查员的赞扬。|近义词：obedience, compliance, conformity, acquiescence |反义词：defiance, resistance, disobedience, rebellion |
