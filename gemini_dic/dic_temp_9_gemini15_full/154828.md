perula:/ˈpɛrʊlə/| n.|小鳞片，小薄片|A small scale, especially one of the overlapping scales on a cone or bud.  It can also refer to a small, thin, flat piece of something.|例句：The perulae protected the developing buds from frost.  小鳞片保护着正在发育的芽免受霜冻。
例句：The geologist examined the perulae of the rock sample. 地质学家检查了岩石样本上的小薄片。|近义词：scale, flake, lamella|反义词：large scale, large flake
