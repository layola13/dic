hillwalking: /ˈhɪlˌwɔːkɪŋ/ | n. |徒步登山，登山健行| The activity of walking in hilly areas, often for leisure or exercise. It can range from gentle walks on low hills to challenging climbs in mountainous terrain.  |例句：He enjoys hillwalking in the Scottish Highlands. 他喜欢在苏格兰高地徒步登山。

例句：We went hillwalking last weekend and saw some amazing views. 我们上周末去登山健行，看到了令人惊叹的景色。


近义词：hiking, rambling, mountaineering (for more challenging climbs)


反义词：None directly opposite, but the antonym could be considered sedentary activities like watching TV or playing video games.


词性：名词


USAGE->

They hillwalk every weekend. = 他们每个周末都去登山健行。
He hillwalks in the mountains. = 他在山里登山健行。
