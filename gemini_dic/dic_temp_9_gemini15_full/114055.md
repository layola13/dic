magnetism:/ˈmæɡnɪtɪzəm/| n. |磁性，磁力；吸引力；魅力|The force of attraction or repulsion between magnetic poles.  It's also used to describe a compelling charm or attractiveness.|例句：The magnet's magnetism lifted the iron filings.  磁铁的磁力吸起了铁屑。

例句：Her charisma and magnetism drew people to her. 她的魅力和吸引力吸引了人们。|近义词：attraction, charm, allure|反义词：repulsion
