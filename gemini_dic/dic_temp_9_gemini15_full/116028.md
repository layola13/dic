marsupials: /mɑːrˈsuːpiəlz/ | n. |有袋动物|  Animals characterized by having a pouch (marsupium) in which the young are carried and suckled.  They are mostly found in Australia and surrounding islands, but some species exist in the Americas. | Example: Kangaroos and koalas are examples of marsupials. 袋鼠和考拉是有袋动物的例子。|近义词：pouched mammals |反义词：placental mammals
