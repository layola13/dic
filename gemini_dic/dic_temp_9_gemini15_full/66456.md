exempting:/ɪɡˈzɛmptɪŋ/| v.|免除；豁免|The act of freeing or releasing someone or something from an obligation, rule, or requirement.|例句：The new law is exempting small businesses from certain taxes.  新法律免除了小型企业某些税款。|例句：He was exempting himself from the rules. 他把自己从规则中豁免了。|近义词：excluding, freeing, releasing|反义词：including, subjecting, obligating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are exempting certain goods from the import tax. = 他们正在免除某些商品的进口税。
The company exempts its employees from paying parking fees. = 公司免除其员工支付停车费。


一般过去时:
The government exempted farmers from the new regulations. = 政府将农民从新的规章制度中豁免。
The judge exempted the witness from testifying. = 法官免除了证人的作证义务。


现在完成时:
They have exempted us from the usual paperwork. = 他们已经免除了我们通常的文书工作。
The school has exempted her from the final exam. = 学校已经免除了她的期末考试。


过去完成时:
He had exempted himself from the competition before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已将自己从比赛中豁免。
The company had exempted its employees from the mandatory training. = 公司已免除了其员工的强制性培训。


将来时:
They will exempt students from the tuition fee. = 他们将免除学生的学费。
The council will exempt the residents from paying the property tax. = 理事会将免除居民支付房产税。


虚拟语气:
If they exempted us from the test, we would be very happy. = 如果他们免除我们的考试，我们会非常高兴。
I wish they exempted me from this tedious work. = 我希望他们能免除我这项繁琐的工作。


被动语态:
Certain goods were exempted from the import tax. = 某些商品被免除了进口税。
He was exempted from military service due to his health condition. = 由于健康原因，他被免除了服兵役。


疑问句:
Are they exempting anyone from the rules? = 他们是否有人被免除规则？
Will we be exempted from the upcoming project? = 我们会被免除即将到来的项目吗？


否定句:
They are not exempting anyone from the rules. = 他们没有免除任何人的规则。
The company does not exempt its employees from paying taxes. = 公司没有免除其员工的纳税义务。


PHRASE->
be exempted from = 被免除...
exempt from = 免除...
exempt somebody/something from something = 免除某人/某事于某事
exempt oneself from = 将自己从...中豁免
grant an exemption from = 授予免除...的权利
