rethought:/ˌriːˈθɔːt/| v.|重新思考；再考虑|To think about something again, often in a different way.|例句：He rethought his decision after speaking to his wife. 他和妻子谈话后重新考虑了他的决定。|近义词： reconsider, rethink, re-evaluate|反义词：accept, confirm


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I rethink my strategies every week. = 我每周都会重新思考我的策略。
They regularly rethought their marketing plans. = 他们定期重新思考他们的营销计划。


一般过去时:
She rethought her answer after a moment of silence. = 她沉默片刻后重新考虑了她的答案。
He rethought the entire project after the initial failure. = 首次失败后，他重新思考了整个项目。


现在完成时:
I have rethought my approach to the problem. = 我已经重新考虑了解决这个问题的方法。
We have rethought our business model several times. = 我们已经多次重新思考了我们的商业模式。


过去完成时:
She had rethought her career path before accepting the new job. = 在接受这份新工作之前，她已经重新考虑了自己的职业道路。
They had rethought their investment strategy before the market crash. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经重新考虑了他们的投资策略。


将来时:
I will rethink my options before making a final decision. = 我会在做出最终决定之前重新考虑我的选择。
We will rethink the design after receiving customer feedback. = 我们将在收到客户反馈后重新考虑设计。


虚拟语气:
If I had rethought my actions, I might have avoided the mistake. = 如果我重新考虑过我的行为，我本来可以避免这个错误。
I wish I had rethought my investment before it plummeted. = 我真希望我的投资暴跌之前，我重新考虑过。


被动语态:
The plan was rethought after several complaints. = 在收到几项投诉后，该计划被重新考虑了。
The strategy has been rethought by the management team. = 该策略已被管理团队重新考虑。


疑问句:
Have you rethought your position on this issue? = 你是否重新考虑了你对这个问题的立场？
Will they rethink their decision in the light of new evidence? = 鉴于新的证据，他们会重新考虑他们的决定吗？


否定句:
He did not rethink his decision, despite the warnings. = 尽管有警告，他并没有重新考虑他的决定。
They haven't rethought their approach to the problem yet. = 他们还没有重新考虑他们解决这个问题的方法。


PHRASE->
rethought the situation = 重新思考了情况
rethought the plan = 重新考虑了计划
rethought the approach = 重新思考了方法
rethought the strategy = 重新考虑了策略
rethought the implications = 重新思考了影响
