nonsympathizers:/ˌnɑːnˈsɪmpəθaɪzərz/ | n. |不同情者，不支持者 |Those who lack sympathy or support for a particular cause or group.  They often remain indifferent or even hostile towards the plight of others. |Example: The nonsympathizers remained silent during the victim's testimony.  不同情者在受害者作证期间保持沉默。|近义词：opponents, adversaries, antagonists |反义词：supporters, sympathizers, advocates
