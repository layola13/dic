porphyrite:/ˈpɔːrfəraɪt/| n. |斑状岩|A porphyritic igneous rock; a volcanic rock containing larger crystals (phenocrysts) embedded in a fine-grained matrix.  Porphyrite is distinguished from porphyry by the composition of its matrix. While porphyry has a felsic matrix, porphyrite's matrix is mafic or intermediate. |Example:  The geologist identified the sample as a porphyrite due to its distinct phenocrysts within a dark-colored matrix.  地质学家根据其在深色基质中明显的斑晶，将样本鉴定为斑状岩。|近义词：porphyry (but with important compositional distinction)|反义词：无|名词
