dungan:/ˈdʌŋɡən/| n. |东干人；东干语|A member of a Turkic-speaking Muslim people living primarily in China's Gansu and Ningxia provinces.  They are descendants of Hui people who migrated from Central Asia.|The Dungan people are known for their unique culture and traditions.|近义词：Hui (in some contexts)|反义词：无
