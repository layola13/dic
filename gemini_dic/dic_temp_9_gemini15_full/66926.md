expender:/ɪkˈspɛndər/| n. |支出者；花费者；消耗者| A person or thing that expends something, especially money or energy.  Someone who spends a lot of money or uses a lot of energy. |The company's biggest expender of resources is its marketing department. 这家公司最大的资源消耗者是其市场营销部门。|近义词：spender, consumer |反义词：saver, accumulator |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a big expender of funds. = 他是个大手笔花钱的人。
She expends a lot of energy at work. = 她在工作中消耗了很多精力。


一般过去时:
He was a lavish expender in his youth. = 他年轻时花钱大手大脚。
They expended considerable effort on the project. = 他们在项目上付出了相当大的努力。


现在完成时:
She has expended all her savings on the trip. = 她把所有的积蓄都花在了旅行上。
The company has expended significant resources on research and development. = 公司已将大量资源投入研发。


过去完成时:
They had expended all their ammunition before the enemy surrendered. = 敌人投降之前，他们已经用完了所有的弹药。
She had expended much energy trying to convince him. = 她已经耗费了很多精力试图说服他。


将来时:
He will expend all his energy to finish the marathon. = 他将耗尽全部精力完成马拉松比赛。
The government will expend significant funds on infrastructure projects. = 政府将在基础设施项目上投入大量资金。


虚拟语气:
If he were a wiser expender, he wouldn't be in debt. = 如果他更会花钱，他就不会负债了。
If she had expended less energy arguing, she might have finished her work. = 如果她少花点精力争论，她可能已经完成了工作。


被动语态:
A lot of money was expended on the new building. = 在新建大楼上花费了很多钱。
Much time was expended in preparing the report. = 在准备报告上花费了很多时间。


疑问句:
Who is the biggest expender of resources in this company? = 这家公司最大的资源消耗者是谁？
How much energy will be expended in this experiment? = 在这个实验中将消耗多少能量？


否定句:
He is not a wasteful expender of resources. = 他不是一个浪费资源的人。
They did not expend unnecessary effort on the task. = 他们没有在任务上花费不必要的努力。
