calorific:/ˌkæləˈrɪfɪk/| adj.|卡路里的，产热量的 | Relating to or producing heat.  The word refers to the amount of heat produced by the combustion of a substance, usually measured in calories or kilocalories.  | Example: The calorific value of this fuel is very high. 这燃料的卡路里值很高。 The calorific content of the food is quite low. 这食物的卡路里含量相当低。 |近义词：heat-producing, heat-generating |反义词：cold-producing
