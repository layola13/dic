inquisitive: /ɪnˈkwɪzɪtɪv/ | adj. | 好奇的，爱打听的 | Curious; eager to learn or know.  Characterized by a desire to investigate and inquire. | 例句：The children were inquisitive about the strange noise. 孩子们对奇怪的声音很好奇。

例句：Her inquisitive nature led her to explore every corner of the old house. 她好奇的天性让她探索了老房子的每一个角落。


近义词：curious, inquiring, questioning, prying, nosy

反义词：indifferent, apathetic, unconcerned


词源:  From Middle English *inquisitif, from Old French *inquisitif, from Latin inquisitīvus, from inquisītus, past participle of inquirere "to inquire," from in- "in" + quaerere "to seek."
