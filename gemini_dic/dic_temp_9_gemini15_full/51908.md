dhooties:/ˈduːtiːz/| n. |裹身布，纱笼| A dhoti is a type of traditional garment worn by men in the Indian subcontinent. It's a long piece of unstitched cloth wrapped around the waist and legs.  | Example: He wore a white dhoti for the ceremony. 他在仪式上穿了一件白色裹身布。|近义词：lungi, sarong |反义词：pants, trousers
