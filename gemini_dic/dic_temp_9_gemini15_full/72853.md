folklore:/ˈfoʊkˌlɔːr/| n.|民间传说，民俗|The traditional beliefs, customs, and stories of a community, passed through generations by word of mouth.|例句：The folklore of the region is rich in tales of mythical creatures.  这个地区的民间传说充满了关于神话生物的故事。|近义词：tradition, legend, myth, lore|反义词：fact, reality

Folklore preserves the cultural heritage of a community. = 民间传说保存着一个群体的文化遗产。
We study folklore to understand past societies. = 我们研究民间传说以了解过去的社会。
