shammers:/ˈʃæmər/| n.|骗子，假冒者|A shammer is someone who pretends to be something they are not, often to deceive others for personal gain.  They might fake expertise, qualifications, or experiences. |例句：He was exposed as a shammer after his fake credentials were discovered. 他被揭露为骗子，因为他伪造的证书被发现了。|近义词：fraud, imposter, charlatan, deceiver|反义词：truth-teller, honest person|名词
