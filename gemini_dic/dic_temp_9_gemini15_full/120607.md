mirthlessness:/ˈmɜːθləsnəs/ | n. |缺乏欢乐；无趣；沉闷 |The utter mirthlessness of the occasion was palpable.  这次场合的完全缺乏欢乐是显而易见的。| 近义词：gloom, dreariness, solemnity | 反义词：mirth, gaiety, merriment


词源:

* **mirth:**  源自古英语 *myrþ*,  意思是“欢乐，快乐”。这词根与古高地德语 *murþi* 和古诺尔斯语 *myrðr*  有关，都表示类似的意思。

* **-less:**  这是一个否定后缀，表示“缺乏…的”、“没有…的”。


英文解析:

Mirthlessness is the state or quality of being without mirth; lacking in joy, amusement, or merriment. It suggests a serious, somber, or depressing atmosphere devoid of laughter or lightheartedness.


例句:

Her face was devoid of expression, reflecting the mirthlessness of the situation.  她的脸上没有任何表情，反映出局势的沉闷。

The heavy silence in the room spoke volumes about the gathering's mirthlessness.  房间里沉重的寂静充分说明了聚会的沉闷。


近义词:

* Gloom:  A state of depression or low spirits.
* Dreariness:  The quality or state of being dreary; dullness and lack of interest.
* Gravity:  Seriousness or solemnity of manner or appearance.
* Solemnity:  The state or quality of being solemn; seriousness.


反义词:

* Mirth:  Happiness and laughter.
* Gaiety:  The state or quality of being gay or merry.
* Merriment:  Noisy or boisterous fun or celebration.
* Lightheartedness:  A cheerful and carefree disposition.


词性: 名词
