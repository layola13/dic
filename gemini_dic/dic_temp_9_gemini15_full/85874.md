headbanger:/ˈhɛdbæŋər/| n. |甩头的人；(尤指)摇滚乐迷| A person who repeatedly bangs their head to music, especially heavy metal music.  Often used to refer to a fan of heavy metal music known for this behavior. |例句：The headbanger in the front row was losing his mind to the music.  前排那个甩头狂热地随着音乐摇摆。

The lead singer encouraged the headbangers in the audience to mosh. 主唱鼓励台下的甩头族们进行疯狂的舞蹈。|近义词：metalhead, headbanging enthusiast|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who passively listens to music.
