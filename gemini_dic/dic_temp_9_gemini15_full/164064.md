/prɪˈdɪskaʊnts/| n. |预扣款，预先折扣|A prediscount refers to a discount applied before the final price is calculated.  It's often used in situations where multiple discounts are applied sequentially, or where a discount is applied to a base price before additional charges are added. |例句：The prediscount price was $100, but after adding tax and shipping, the final price was $115. 预扣款价格为100美元，但在加上税和运费后，最终价格为115美元。|近义词：advance discount, pre-discount |反义词：post-discount, surcharge|名词
