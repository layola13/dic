non-Swedes:/ˌnɑːnˈswiːdz/| n.|非瑞典人|People who are not Swedish.  This term refers to individuals who do not belong to the Swedish nationality or ethnicity.|Example: The conference attracted attendees from many countries, including a large number of non-Swedes.|近义词：foreigners, non-Scandinavians|反义词：Swedes
