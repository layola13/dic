overwintering:/ˈoʊvərˌwɪntəriŋ/ | v. n. adj. | 越冬；冬眠；过冬的 |  The act or process of surviving the winter.  It can refer to animals, plants, or even humans surviving harsh winter conditions. | 例句：Many birds overwinter in warmer climates. 许多鸟类在温暖的气候中过冬。 The overwintering population of monarch butterflies is declining.  帝王蝶的越冬种群数量正在下降。  This plant is known for its excellent overwintering hardiness.  这种植物以其极强的越冬抗寒性而闻名。 | 近义词：hibernation (for animals), surviving the winter | 反义词：summering (spending the summer somewhere)
