/ˈpluːpɜːrfɛkts/ | adj. | 过去完成时 |  The pluperfect tense is a verb tense used to describe an action that was completed before another action in the past.  It's formed using the past participle of the auxiliary verb "to have" ("had") plus the past participle of the main verb.  It emphasizes the sequence of past events. |

例句：
She had finished her work before he arrived. = 她在他到达之前已经完成了她的工作。

They had eaten dinner when the phone rang. = 电话响的时候，他们已经吃过晚饭了。


近义词： prior, antecedent (referring to time) | 反义词： future, subsequent (referring to time) | 词性: adjective
