indecisive:/ˌɪndɪˈsaɪsɪv/| adj. |优柔寡断的，犹豫不决的 | Unable to make decisions easily; hesitant.  Characterized by a lack of firmness or resolution. |例句：He was indecisive about which job offer to accept. 他优柔寡断，不知道该接受哪个工作机会。

例句：Her indecisive nature often led to missed opportunities.  她优柔寡断的天性常常导致她错过机会。


近义词： irresolute, hesitant, wavering, vacillating, uncertain, ambivalent


反义词： decisive, resolute, determined, firm


词性：形容词


词源：
The word "indecisive" is derived from the prefix "in-" (meaning "not") and the word "decisive."  "Decisive" comes from the Latin word "decidere," meaning "to decide" or "to cut off."  Therefore, "indecisive" literally means "not able to decide."
