re-feeling:/ˌriːˈfiːlɪŋ/| n.|再次感受，重新体验|The act of experiencing a feeling again, often with renewed intensity or a different perspective.  It can refer to revisiting past emotions or experiencing a similar emotion in a new context.|例句：Her re-feeling of grief after seeing the old photographs was intense.  她看到旧照片后再次感受到的悲伤非常强烈。|近义词：reliving, re-experiencing, recalling|反义词：forgetting, suppressing, ignoring|名词
