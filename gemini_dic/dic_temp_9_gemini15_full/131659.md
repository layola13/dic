nonconcentrated: /ˌnɒnkɒnˈsɛntreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 不集中的；稀释的；未浓缩的 |  Not concentrated; diluted.  This refers to a substance that has been diluted or not brought to its fullest strength or potency. It can also describe something that lacks focus or intensity. | 例句：The nonconcentrated juice was very watery.  = 未浓缩的果汁很稀。 The nonconcentrated effort resulted in poor performance. =  不集中的努力导致了糟糕的表现。 | 近义词：diluted, weak, unconcentrated, diffuse | 反义词：concentrated, strong, intense
