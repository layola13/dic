name-droppings:/ˈneɪmˌdrɒpɪŋz/ | n. | 炫耀式地提及名人 | The act of casually mentioning famous people's names in conversation to impress others, often without a genuine connection to them.  | Example: Her constant name-droppings about her supposed friendship with celebrities made everyone uncomfortable.  她的不断炫耀与名人的所谓友谊让每个人都感到不舒服。 | 近义词：showing off, boasting, bragging | 反义词：humility, modesty | 名词
