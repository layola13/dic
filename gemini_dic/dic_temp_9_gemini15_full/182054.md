remenant: /ˈremnənt/ | n. | 残余；剩余部分；遗迹 | A small remaining quantity of something.  A fragment or trace of something that is disappearing or has disappeared. |

例句：

The remnants of the storm passed quickly.  暴风雨的残余很快过去了。

Only remnants of the old building remain.  只有那座旧建筑的残余部分留了下来。


近义词：remainder, residue, leftover, vestige, fragment


反义词：whole, entirety, totality


词源：

From Old French *remanant, present participle of remaner "to remain", from re- "again" + maner "to remain".  Ultimately from Latin manere "to remain".


用法说明：

remenant通常作名词使用，指某事物剩下的少量部分或痕迹。它可以指物质上的残余，也可以指抽象的概念的残余。


例句补充：


复数形式：remnants


The remnants of the feast were cleared away.  宴席的残余被清理掉了。


The ancient civilization left behind only a few remnants.  那个古老的文明只留下了一些残余。
