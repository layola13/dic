fluencies:/ˈfluːənsiz/ | n. |流利，流畅；熟练 |The ability to speak or write a language easily and accurately.  It can also refer to the skill or ease with which something is done. |例句：Her fluencies in several languages impressed the interviewers.  她的多门外语流利程度给面试官留下了深刻印象。
例句：The pianist displayed remarkable fluencies in his performance.  这位钢琴家在演奏中展现了非凡的技巧。 |近义词：proficiency, fluency, mastery, facility |反义词：hesitation, stammer, stumbling
