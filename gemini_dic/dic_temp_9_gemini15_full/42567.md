counterparty: /ˈkaʊntəpɑːrti/ | n. |交易对方，合同对方 | A party to a transaction, contract, negotiation, lawsuit, or other agreement.  It refers to the other person or entity involved in a deal. |例句：We need to carefully review the contract with our counterparty before signing. 我们需要在签署合同之前仔细审查与交易对方的合同。|例句：The counterparty to the agreement failed to meet its obligations.  协议的对方未能履行其义务。|近义词：opposite party, other party, counterpart|反义词：self, oneself


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bank carefully vets each counterparty before lending money. = 银行在贷款之前会仔细审查每个交易对方。
Our counterparty consistently delivers high-quality products. = 我们的交易对方始终提供高质量的产品。


一般过去时:
The previous counterparty defaulted on the loan. = 上一个交易对方未能偿还贷款。
We negotiated a favorable deal with our former counterparty. = 我们与以前的交易对方达成了一个有利的交易。


现在完成时:
We have worked with this counterparty for many years. = 我们与这个交易对方合作多年了。
The company has successfully completed many transactions with its various counterparties. = 该公司已成功地与其多个交易对方完成了许多交易。


过去完成时:
Before the merger, the counterparty had already acquired a significant market share. = 在合并之前，交易对方已经获得了相当大的市场份额。
We had thoroughly vetted the counterparty before entering into the agreement. = 在签订协议之前，我们已经彻底审查了交易对方。


将来时:
We will negotiate the terms of the contract with our new counterparty next week. = 我们下周将与新的交易对方协商合同条款。
Our counterparty will likely increase its order volume next quarter. = 我们的交易对方可能会在下个季度增加订单量。


虚拟语气:
If we had known more about our counterparty, we would have structured the deal differently. = 如果我们对交易对方了解得更多，我们会以不同的方式安排交易。
I wish we had a more reliable counterparty for this project. = 我希望我们这个项目有一个更可靠的交易对方。


被动语态:
The counterparty was carefully selected by our risk management team. = 交易对方是由我们的风险管理团队仔细挑选的。
The contract was reviewed by both parties and their respective counterparties. = 合同由双方及其各自的交易对方进行了审查。


疑问句:
Who is our counterparty in this transaction? = 这笔交易中我们的交易对方是谁？
What is the creditworthiness of our counterparty? = 我们交易对方的信用度如何？


否定句:
The counterparty did not fulfill its contractual obligations. = 交易对方没有履行其合同义务。
We were not satisfied with the performance of our counterparty. = 我们对交易对方的表现不满意。
