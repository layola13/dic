excellencies:/ˈɛksələnsiz/| n.|阁下们，各位尊贵的人|A formal term of address used to refer to a group of high-ranking officials, especially ambassadors or other dignitaries.  It is the plural form of "excellency."|Example:  His Excellencies, the Ambassadors of France and Germany, addressed the assembly.  阁下们，法国和德国的大使们向大会致辞。|近义词：your honors, your excellencies, distinguished guests|反义词：N/A
