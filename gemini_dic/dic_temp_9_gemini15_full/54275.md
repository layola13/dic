disguise:/dɪsˈɡaɪz/| n. vt.|伪装；掩饰；假扮|To conceal the identity or true nature of; mask.|The spy used a disguise to infiltrate the enemy base. 间谍使用伪装潜入敌方基地。He disguised himself as a beggar. 他伪装成乞丐。|近义词：conceal, mask, camouflage|反义词：reveal, expose|动词,名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disguises his feelings well. = 他很擅长掩饰自己的感情。
They disguise their intentions. = 他们掩饰他们的意图。


一般过去时:
She disguised herself as a waitress. = 她伪装成女服务员。
The thief disguised his tracks. = 小偷掩盖了他的踪迹。


现在完成时:
He has disguised his true identity for years. = 他多年来一直隐藏着自己的真实身份。
They have disguised the building's age with a new facade. = 他们用新的外观掩盖了建筑物的年代。


过去完成时:
She had disguised her voice before making the call. = 她打电话之前已经伪装了她的声音。
They had disguised the evidence before the police arrived. = 警察到达之前，他们已经伪装了证据。


将来时:
He will disguise himself as a doctor. = 他将伪装成医生。
They will disguise the truth with clever wording. = 他们将用巧妙的措辞来掩盖真相。


虚拟语气:
If he disguised himself, he might be able to sneak past. = 如果他伪装自己，他或许就能偷偷溜过去。
I wish I could disguise my accent. = 我希望我能掩饰我的口音。


被动语态:
His identity was disguised by the make-up. = 他的身份被化妆掩盖了。
The true nature of the problem was disguised by superficial solutions. = 问题的真正性质被表面上的解决方案掩盖了。


疑问句:
How did he disguise himself? = 他是如何伪装自己的？
Did they disguise the package before shipping it? = 他们在发货前是否伪装了包裹？


否定句:
He didn't disguise his anger. = 他没有掩饰他的愤怒。
They didn't disguise the fact that they were lying. = 他们没有掩盖他们正在撒谎的事实。


PHRASE->
in disguise: 伪装的
under the disguise of: 打着...的幌子
under disguise: 伪装着
to disguise oneself as: 假扮成...
a disguise of: 一种...的伪装
