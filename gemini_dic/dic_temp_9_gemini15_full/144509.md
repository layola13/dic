outtravel:/ˌaʊtˈtrævəl/| vt.|旅行得比…远；超过…的行程|To travel farther or more extensively than.  The verb implies surpassing someone or something in terms of distance covered or the breadth of travel experiences.|例句：He outtraveled his companions, exploring regions they had only dreamed of. 他比他的同伴们走得更远，探索了他们只在梦中见过的地方。


例句：Their journey outtraveled any previous expedition to the Antarctic. 他们的旅程超过了以往任何一次南极探险。


近义词：outskip, surpass, exceed


反义词：undertravel


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outtravels his friends every summer. = 他每年夏天都比他的朋友们旅行得更远。
She outtravels her colleagues in terms of international experience. = 就国际经验而言，她的旅行比同事们更多。


一般过去时:
They outtraveled the previous record by a significant margin. = 他们比之前的记录远超出一大截。
The explorers outtraveled all previous expeditions into the Amazon. = 探险家们的旅程超过了之前所有进入亚马逊地区的探险。


现在完成时:
She has outtraveled most of her family. = 她的旅行比大多数家人更多。
He has outtraveled his peers in exploring remote locations. = 他在探索偏远地区方面比同龄人走得更远。


过去完成时:
By the time they reached the summit, they had outtraveled all other climbers. = 当他们到达山顶时，他们已经超过了所有其他登山者。
Before the competition, she had outtraveled all her competitors in training miles. = 比赛前，她在训练里程方面已经超过了所有竞争对手。


将来时:
We will outtravel our previous adventures this year. = 我们今年的探险将会超过之前的探险。
They believe they will outtravel their rivals in the race to the South Pole. = 他们相信在前往南极点的竞赛中，他们将会超过对手。


虚拟语气:
If he had better planning, he would have outtraveled the other teams. = 如果他的计划更好，他本可以超过其他队伍。
I wish I had outtraveled more during my younger years. = 我希望我年轻的时候旅行更多。


被动语态:
The previous record was outtraveled by the new expedition. = 新探险队超过了之前的记录。
His achievements in exploring the Himalayas were outtraveled by his subsequent journey across the Sahara. = 他在探索喜马拉雅山脉方面的成就被他随后横穿撒哈拉沙漠的旅程所超越。


疑问句:
Did he outtravel his brother during his summer vacation? = 他暑假期间旅行比他哥哥远吗？
Will they outtravel their expectations on this expedition? = 这次探险他们会超过预期吗？


否定句:
They did not outtravel their original plan. = 他们的行程没有超过最初的计划。
We did not outtravel the other groups. = 我们没有超过其他小组。
