qualms:/kwɑːmz/| n.|疑虑；顾虑；不安|A feeling of doubt or uncertainty about whether something is morally right or a feeling of uneasiness.|I had no qualms about telling him the truth. 我毫无顾虑地告诉了他真相。|doubts, reservations, scruples|certainty, confidence, assurance|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has no qualms about his decision. 他对自己的决定毫无顾虑。
She has some qualms about the project's feasibility. 她对项目的可行性有一些疑虑。


一般过去时:
I had qualms about lying to my parents. 我对向父母撒谎感到不安。
They had serious qualms about the safety of the bridge. 他们对桥梁的安全非常担忧。


现在完成时:
I have had no qualms about helping him. 我一直乐于助他，毫无顾忌。
She has had qualms about her career choice for years. 多年来，她一直对自己的职业选择感到不安。


过去完成时:
I had had qualms about going alone, but I went anyway. 我曾对独自一人前往感到不安，但我还是去了。
They had had qualms about the plan before, but they agreed to it in the end. 他们之前对这个计划有过疑虑，但最终还是同意了。


将来时:
I will have no qualms about reporting him. 我将毫不犹豫地举报他。
She will likely have qualms about accepting the offer. 她很可能会对接受这个提议感到犹豫。


虚拟语气:
If I had no qualms, I would have told him everything. 如果我没有顾虑，我会告诉他一切。
I wish I didn't have qualms about asking for a raise. 我希望我不必为要求加薪而感到不安。


被动语态:
His actions were not met with qualms from the public. 他的行为没有遭到公众的质疑。
The plan was approved without qualms. 该计划未经任何异议就获得批准。


疑问句:
Do you have any qualms about this plan? 你对这个计划有什么疑虑吗？
Does he have qualms about taking on this responsibility? 他对承担这项责任有什么顾虑吗？


否定句:
I have no qualms about speaking my mind. 我毫不犹豫地表达我的想法。
He had no qualms about breaking the rules. 他毫不犹豫地违反了规则。
