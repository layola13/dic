penitents:/ˈpɛnɪtənts/| n.|悔罪者；忏悔者|People who feel regret for their sins and are seeking forgiveness.  They are engaged in the process of repentance. |例句：The penitents knelt before the altar, seeking absolution. 悔罪者跪在祭坛前，寻求赦免。
The penitents confessed their sins to the priest. 悔罪者向牧师忏悔了他们的罪过。|近义词：repenters, confessors|反义词：sinners, unrepentant
