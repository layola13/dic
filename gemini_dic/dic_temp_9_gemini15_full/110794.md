linkable: /ˈlɪŋkəbl/ | adj. | 可链接的，可连接的 | Able to be linked or connected.  This adjective describes something that can be easily joined or associated with something else, often in a digital or online context.  It implies a capacity for connection, either physically or virtually. | Example:  The website features many linkable resources for further learning.  这个网站有很多可链接的资源以供进一步学习。|近义词: connectable, joinable, integrable | 反义词: unlinkable, unconnected, isolated |
