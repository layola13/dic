carabiner:/ˌkærəˈbiːnər/| n. |登山扣；活动挂钩|A carabiner is a metal loop with a spring-loaded gate, used for attaching climbing equipment, such as ropes and harnesses, to other pieces of equipment or anchor points.  It's crucial for safety in climbing, mountaineering, and other activities.|Example:  He clipped the carabiner to his harness before rappelling down the cliff. 他在从悬崖速降之前将登山扣扣到他的安全带上。|近义词：snap hook, locking link|反义词:None
