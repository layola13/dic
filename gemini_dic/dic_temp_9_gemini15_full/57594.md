drank:/dræŋk/| v.|喝(过去式)|The past tense of "drink".  It indicates that someone consumed a liquid at some point in the past.|例句：I drank a glass of water. 我喝了一杯水。
I drank too much wine last night. 我昨晚喝了太多酒。|近义词：imbibed, quaffed, guzzled|反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I drink water every day. = 我每天喝水。
She drinks tea in the morning. = 她早上喝茶。

一般过去时:
He drank all the milk. = 他把所有的牛奶都喝光了。
They drank coffee after dinner. = 他们晚饭后喝了咖啡。

现在完成时:
I have drunk three cups of coffee today. = 我今天已经喝了三杯咖啡。
She has drunk all the juice. = 她已经把所有的果汁都喝完了。

过去完成时:
He had drunk the entire bottle before I arrived. = 我到之前他已经喝完了一整瓶。
They had drunk several beers at the party. = 他们在派对上已经喝了几瓶啤酒。

将来时:
I will drink plenty of water during the hike. = 我徒步旅行期间会喝很多水。
She will drink a toast to the happy couple. = 她将为这对幸福的夫妇敬酒。

虚拟语气:
If I were thirsty, I would drink some water. = 如果我渴了，我会喝点水。
I wish I had drunk less wine last night. = 我希望昨晚少喝点酒。

被动语态:
The entire bottle of wine was drunk quickly. = 整瓶酒很快就喝完了。
The water was drunk by the thirsty traveler. = 口渴的旅行者喝掉了水。

疑问句:
Did you drink your milk? = 你喝牛奶了吗？
Have you ever drunk sake? = 你喝过清酒吗？

否定句:
I didn't drink anything. = 我什么也没喝。
She didn't drink alcohol at the party. = 她在派对上没喝酒。
