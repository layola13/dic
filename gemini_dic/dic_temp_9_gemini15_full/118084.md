menfolk:/ˈmɛnfəʊk/| n. |男人们，男同胞们 |The term "menfolk" refers to men collectively, often used in a slightly archaic or informal way. It emphasizes the group aspect of men and can sometimes carry a connotation of masculinity and traditional roles.  |The menfolk were gathered around the fire, telling stories.  男人们围坐在火堆旁，讲故事。|men, fellows, chaps|womenfolk, children
