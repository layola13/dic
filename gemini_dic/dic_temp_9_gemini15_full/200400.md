skyrocketed:/ˈskaɪˌrɒkɪtɪd/ | v. | 骤增，猛涨 | To increase rapidly and dramatically.  | Example:  House prices skyrocketed after the announcement of the new infrastructure project.  房价在新基础设施项目公布后暴涨。|近义词：soared, surged, shot up | 反义词：plummeted, crashed, declined | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The price of gas skyrockets during peak season. = 油价在旺季飙升。
Profits skyrocket when the market is strong. = 市场强劲时利润会暴涨。


一般过去时:
Stock prices skyrocketed after the good news was released. = 好消息发布后，股价暴涨。
The company's sales skyrocketed last quarter. = 公司上季度的销售额猛增。


现在完成时:
The value of the cryptocurrency has skyrocketed this year. = 今年加密货币的价值已经暴涨。
Inflation has skyrocketed since the war began. = 自战争开始以来，通货膨胀率已飙升。


过去完成时:
By the end of the year, the company's profits had skyrocketed. = 到年底，公司利润已大幅增长。
Before the announcement, the stock price had already skyrocketed. = 在公告之前，股价已经飙升了。


将来时:
Experts predict that housing prices will skyrocket next year. = 专家预测明年的房价将会暴涨。
If demand increases, prices will likely skyrocket. = 如果需求增加，价格可能会暴涨。


虚拟语气:
If the economy improved, the stock market would skyrocket. = 如果经济好转，股市将会暴涨。
I wish the value of my investment had skyrocketed. = 我希望我的投资价值已经暴涨了。


被动语态:
The stock price was skyrocketed by the unexpected news. = 股价因意外消息而暴涨。
Sales were skyrocketed by the successful marketing campaign. = 成功的营销活动使销售额猛增。


疑问句:
Did the company's profits skyrocket last year? = 公司去年的利润是否暴涨？
Will housing prices skyrocket in the next few months? = 未来几个月房价会暴涨吗？


否定句:
Sales did not skyrocket as expected. = 销售额并未像预期那样暴涨。
The company's performance did not skyrocket despite the new strategy. = 尽管有新战略，但公司的业绩并未大幅增长。
