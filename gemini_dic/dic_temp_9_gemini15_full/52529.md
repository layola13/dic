difficult:/ˈdɪfɪkʌlt/| adj. |困难的，艰难的 |difficult describes something that is hard to do, understand, or deal with. It implies a significant challenge or obstacle.  |The exam was incredibly difficult.  这次考试非常难。|
The problem is difficult to solve.  这个问题很难解决。|easy, challenging, arduous |easy, simple |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The task is difficult. = 这项任务很难。
This problem is difficult to understand. = 这个问题很难理解。

一般过去时:
The journey was difficult. = 这段旅程很艰难。
