occurrence:/əˈkʌrəns/ | n. |事件；发生；出现 | An occurrence is something that happens, an event, or the fact of something happening.  It can refer to a single event or a repeated event. | Example: The police are investigating the occurrence of several burglaries in the neighborhood.  警方正在调查该街区发生的几起入室盗窃案。  The frequent occurrence of errors in the software needs to be addressed.  软件中频繁出现的错误需要解决。 |近义词：event, incident, happening, instance |反义词：absence, nonexistence
