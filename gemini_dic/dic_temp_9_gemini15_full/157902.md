plait:/pleɪt/| n. v.|辫子；编织；褶皱|A plait is a braid of hair or other material.  It can also refer to a fold or crease in fabric.|例句：She wore her hair in a long plait. 她留着长长的辫子。

例句：The baker carefully plaited the dough before baking. 面包师在烘烤前仔细地将面团编织起来。|近义词：braid, tress, weave|反义词：unbraid, unravel
