hagiology:/ˌheɪdʒɪˈɒlədʒi/ | n. |圣徒传记学；圣徒列传；颂圣史 |The study of the lives and writings of saints.  It often involves analyzing hagiographies (accounts of saints' lives) for historical accuracy and theological significance.  |Example:  The hagiology of the early Church is full of inspiring stories, but also contains elements of legend and myth.  圣徒传记学充满了鼓舞人心的故事，但也包含了传说和神话的成分。|近义词：saintly biography, lives of the saints |反义词：critical biography, secular history |名词
