quinizarin:/ˈkwɪnɪzærɪn/| n. | 茜素; 1,4-二羟基蒽醌 | A reddish-brown crystalline organic compound, C₁₄H₈O₄, used as a dye and in the manufacture of other dyes.  It is a dihydroxyanthraquinone. | The fabric was dyed with quinizarin, giving it a rich, deep red hue.  这块布料用茜素染色，呈现出浓郁深红的色调。| alizarin, purpurin |  (no direct antonym, but less intense colors could be considered opposites)| 名词
