pleurisy:/ˈplʊrɪsi/| n.|胸膜炎|Inflammation of the pleura, the membranes surrounding the lungs, causing sharp chest pain, especially during breathing.  The inflammation may be caused by infection (viral, bacterial, or fungal), autoimmune disease, or injury.|例句：The doctor diagnosed her with pleurisy after hearing her describe the sharp chest pain.|近义词：pleuritis|反义词：None.
