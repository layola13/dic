easement:/ˈiːzmənt/ | n. |地役权；便利；轻松 | An easement is a right to use someone else's land for a specific purpose.  It's a non-possessory interest in land.  |例句：The neighbor has an easement across our property to access their land. = 邻居有权穿越我们的土地去往他们的地。

The new software provides ease of use. = 新软件使用方便。 |近义词：right-of-way, privilege, convenience |反义词：burden, restriction
