perturbations:/ˌpɜːr.təˈbeɪ.ʃənz/| n. |扰动；微扰；烦扰 |A perturbation is a disturbance or deviation from a normal state or condition.  It can refer to a slight change or irregularity in a system, or to a state of anxiety or mental disturbance. |例句：The slight perturbations in the Earth's orbit are caused by the gravitational pull of other planets. 地球轨道的轻微扰动是由其他行星的引力造成的。

The unexpected news caused considerable perturbations in his usually calm demeanor.  这个意外的消息扰乱了他通常平静的态度。|近义词：disturbances, disruptions, fluctuations, anxieties |反义词：calmness, tranquility, stability
