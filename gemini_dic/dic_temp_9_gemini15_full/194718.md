self-worth:/ˌsɛlfˈwɜːrθ/| n. |自我价值，自尊 |Self-worth refers to the subjective value a person places on himself or herself.  It's a feeling of being worthy of love, respect, and happiness.  It's influenced by many factors, including past experiences, relationships, and societal expectations. |例句：Building self-worth takes time and effort, but it's a worthwhile investment.  建立自尊需要时间和精力，但这笔投资是值得的。

例句：Her low self-worth stemmed from years of criticism and neglect.  她的低自尊源于多年的批评和忽视。


近义词：self-esteem, self-respect, confidence |反义词：self-doubt, low self-esteem, insecurity |词性:名词
