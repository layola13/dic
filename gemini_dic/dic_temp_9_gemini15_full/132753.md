nondomesticated:/ˌnɑːnˌdəˈmɛstɪkeɪtɪd/| adj. |未驯化的；野生的|Not domesticated; wild or untamed.  The term refers to plants and animals that have not been selectively bred or tamed by humans for human purposes.  It emphasizes the absence of human influence in their development and behavior.|例句：The nondomesticated cat roamed freely in the forest.  这只未驯化的猫在森林里自由漫游。

The nondomesticated dog was wary of humans. 这只野狗对人类很警惕。|近义词：wild, untamed, feral |反义词: domesticated, tamed, cultured
