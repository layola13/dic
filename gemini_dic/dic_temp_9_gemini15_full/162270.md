preabundance:/ˌpriːəˈbʌndəns/| n. |过剩，过量；过多的存在|The state or quality of existing in excessive abundance; superfluity.  Preabundance refers to a condition where something exists in significantly greater quantity than is needed or usual. This often implies a surplus or oversupply, potentially leading to waste or devaluation.| Example: The preabundance of cheap labor led to a decrease in wages.  过剩的廉价劳动力导致工资下降。|近义词：superabundance, superfluity, excess, surplus |反义词：scarcity, deficiency, shortage |名词
