semicylindric:/ˌsɛmiˈsɪlɪndrɪk/| adj.|半圆柱形的；半圆筒形的|Having the shape of half a cylinder.  It describes an object that is cylindrical but only extends halfway around its central axis.  |Example: The tunnel had a semicylindrical roof.  隧道有一个半圆柱形的屋顶。 The semicylindrical structure was surprisingly stable.  这个半圆柱形的结构令人惊讶地稳定。|近义词：half-cylindrical|反义词：cylindrical, spherical
