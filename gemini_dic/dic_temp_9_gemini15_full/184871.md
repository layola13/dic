retackled:/ˌriːˈtækld/| v.|重新处理；再次努力解决；重新开始对付|To tackle something again; to deal with a problem or situation again after a previous attempt.|例句：After the initial attempt failed, the team retackled the problem with a new strategy.  在第一次尝试失败后，团队用新的策略重新处理了这个问题。
例句：He retackled the difficult project, determined to succeed this time. 他再次努力解决那个困难的项目，决心这次成功。|近义词：readdress, revisit, reconsider|反义词：abandon, neglect
