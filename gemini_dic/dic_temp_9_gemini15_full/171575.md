pulley:/ˈpʊli/| n.|滑轮，滑车|A simple machine consisting of a wheel with a grooved rim around which a rope, chain, or belt passes. It is used to change the direction of a force and/or to gain a mechanical advantage.|例句：The pulley system made lifting the heavy object much easier.  滑轮系统使抬起重物容易得多。|近义词：block, sheave|反义词:None
