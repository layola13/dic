regilt:/rɪˈɡɪlt/ | vt. |镀金；再镀金|To cover (something, especially metal) with a thin layer of gold again.|The antique mirror was regilted to restore its former glory. 这面古董镜子重新镀金以恢复其昔日的辉煌。|近义词：replate, gild|反义词：degild
