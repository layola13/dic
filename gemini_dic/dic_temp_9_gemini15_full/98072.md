inquire:/ɪnˈkwaɪr/| vt. vi.|询问；打听；调查|To ask for information; to make an inquiry.|例句：I inquired about the price of the tickets. 我询问了票价。|例句：She inquired into the matter thoroughly. 她彻底调查了此事。|近义词：ask, investigate, question|反义词：ignore, neglect|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I inquire about the weather forecast every morning. = 我每天早上都询问天气预报。
They inquire about the job openings regularly. = 他们定期询问职位空缺。

一般过去时:
He inquired about your health yesterday. = 他昨天问候你的健康。
She inquired whether the train was on time. = 她询问火车是否准时。

现在完成时:
I have inquired about the flight schedule several times. = 我已经多次询问航班时刻表了。
They have inquired into the cause of the accident. = 他们已经调查了事故的原因。

过去完成时:
I had inquired about the hotel before making a reservation. = 我在预订酒店之前已经询问过了。
She had inquired about the job before applying. = 她申请工作之前已经询问过相关信息了。

将来时:
I will inquire about the new product launch next week. = 我下周将询问新产品发布的情况。
He will inquire about the availability of the tickets tomorrow. = 他明天将询问门票是否有货。

虚拟语气:
If I inquired further, I might have found the answer. = 如果我进一步询问，也许就能找到答案了。
I wish I had inquired about the deadline earlier. = 我希望我早点询问截止日期。

被动语态:
The matter was inquired into by the police. = 这件事由警方进行了调查。
Information was inquired about by many people. = 许多人都询问了相关信息。


PHRASE->
inquire after: 询问（某人的健康状况）
inquire into: 调查
inquire of: 向...询问
inquire about: 询问关于...
make inquiries: 进行询问
