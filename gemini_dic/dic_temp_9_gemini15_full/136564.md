nonsale: /ˈnɑːnˌseɪl/ | adj. | 非销售的；不打折的 | Not offered for sale; not on sale.  This usually refers to items that are not part of a promotional sale or discount event. | 例句：These items are nonsale; they are always at full price. 这些商品不打折；它们总是全价。  | 例句：The nonsale items are located on the second floor. 非打折商品位于二楼。 | 近义词：regular-priced, full-price, undiscounted | 反义词：sale, discounted, on sale | 词性：形容词
