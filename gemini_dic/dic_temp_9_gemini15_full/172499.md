pyrazine:/ˈpɪrəziːn/| n. |吡嗪|A heterocyclic aromatic organic compound with the formula C₄H₄N₂.  It is a colorless crystalline solid with a slight nutty or earthy odor. Pyrazine is found in various foods and is used in some industrial applications.|Pyrazine is a key component in the aroma of roasted coffee and nuts.  The compound is also used as a solvent and in the production of pharmaceuticals and pesticides.|近义词：None (unique chemical structure)|反义词：None (no direct antonym)|名词
