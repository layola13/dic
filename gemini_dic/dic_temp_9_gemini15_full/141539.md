opportunism:/ˌɒpəˈtjuːnɪzəm/| n. |机会主义；投机取巧|The practice of taking advantage of opportunities, regardless of ethical considerations;  Exploiting circumstances to one's own advantage, often in a shrewd or unprincipled way. |例句：His success was attributed to opportunism rather than genuine talent. 他的成功归因于机会主义而不是真正的才能。

例句：The company's rapid expansion was fueled by aggressive opportunism in the market.  公司的快速扩张得益于在市场上的积极进取的机会主义。


近义词：expediency, self-interest, pragmatism,  grabbing,  shrewdness


反义词：idealism, principle, integrity, scruples


词性：名词
