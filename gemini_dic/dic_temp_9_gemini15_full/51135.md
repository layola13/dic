despoiled:/dɪˈspɔɪld/| vt.|掠夺；破坏；糟蹋|To deprive (someone) of something valuable or desirable; to damage, spoil, or ruin something.|例句：The invading army despoiled the city of its treasures.  侵略军洗劫了这座城市的宝藏。|例句：Years of neglect had despoiled the once-beautiful garden.  多年的忽视使曾经美丽的庭院变得破败不堪。|近义词：plunder, loot, ravage, ruin, destroy|反义词：preserve, protect, restore, enhance|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The vandals despoil the park every night. = 那些破坏分子每晚都破坏公园。
They despoil the environment with their careless actions. = 他们粗心大意的行为破坏了环境。


一般过去时:

虚拟语气:
