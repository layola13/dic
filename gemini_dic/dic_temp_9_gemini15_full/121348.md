misgoverning:/ˌmɪsˈɡʌvərnɪŋ/| adj. | 施政不力的；管理不善的 |  Describes a system or person that governs badly or unfairly, often leading to chaos or oppression.  It implies a lack of competence, fairness, or effectiveness in governing. | Example: The misgoverning regime led to widespread poverty and unrest.  这个施政不力的政权导致了普遍的贫困和动荡。The misgoverning of the company resulted in significant financial losses.  公司的管理不善导致了巨大的经济损失。 |近义词：maladministering, mismanaging, badly governing |反义词：governing well, administering effectively, managing properly |
