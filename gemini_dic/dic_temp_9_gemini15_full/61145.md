eloquently:/ˈɛləkwəntli/| adv. |雄辩地；流畅地；有口才地 |In a fluent and persuasive manner; with eloquence.  |例句：She eloquently defended her position in the debate. 她雄辩地捍卫了她在辩论中的立场。

例句：He eloquently expressed his gratitude to the audience. 他流畅地表达了他对观众的感谢。


近义词：fluently, persuasively, articulately, expressively

反义词：inarticulately, incoherently, stammeringly,umblingly


词源:  The adverb "eloquently" derives from the adjective "eloquent," which comes from the Middle English word "eloquent," ultimately tracing back to the Latin word "eloquens," meaning "fluent of speech, eloquent."  The Latin word is a present participle of "eloqui," meaning "to speak out," formed from "e-" (out) and "loqui" (to speak).
