conventional:/kənˈvɛnʃənəl/| adj.|传统的，常规的，习俗的 |  Following what is traditional or commonly accepted; conforming to accepted standards.  |例句：He prefers conventional medicine to alternative therapies. 他更喜欢传统医学而不是替代疗法。

例句：This is a conventional approach to problem-solving. 这是一个解决问题的常规方法。


近义词：traditional, customary, orthodox, standard, usual

反义词：unconventional, unorthodox, radical, revolutionary, avant-garde


词源:  From Middle French conventionel, from convenir "to agree, suit," from Latin convenire "to come together," from com- "together" + venire "to come."  The sense evolved from "agreed upon" to "conforming to established practice."
