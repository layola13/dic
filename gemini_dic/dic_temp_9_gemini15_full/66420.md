executory:/ˈɛksɪkjuˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 可执行的；待履行的；尚未履行的 |  Relating to an agreement or contract that is not yet fully performed by one or both parties.  It implies that there are still obligations to be fulfilled by at least one party. |

例句：

The contract is executory, meaning both parties still have obligations to perform. = 合同是待履行的，这意味着双方仍有义务履行。

The executory phase of the agreement involves the delivery of goods. = 该协议的待履行阶段涉及货物交付。


近义词：unperformed, pending, unfinished

反义词：executed, performed, completed
