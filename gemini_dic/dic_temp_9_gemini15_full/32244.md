chickenshit: /ˈtʃɪkənʃɪt/ | adj., n. | 胆小鬼的；懦弱的；卑鄙的；微不足道的 |

英文解析：A highly offensive and informal term used to describe someone who is cowardly, lacking courage, or behaving in a despicable manner. It can also refer to something insignificant or worthless.  The term is considered vulgar and should be avoided in formal settings.

例句：

He's such a chickenshit; he wouldn't even stand up to his boss.  = 他是个十足的胆小鬼；他甚至不敢顶撞他的老板。

That's chickenshit reasoning; it doesn't hold any water. = 那简直是懦弱的理由；它站不住脚。


近义词：cowardly, pusillanimous, craven, gutless, spineless,  meek, insignificant, paltry

反义词：brave, courageous, valiant, bold, dauntless, heroic, significant, substantial

词性：形容词(adj.)，名词(n.)
