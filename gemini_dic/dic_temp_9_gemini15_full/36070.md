coefficacy: /ˌkoʊɪˈfɪkəsi/ | n. |协同功效，协同作用 |The combined effect of two or more substances or agents, especially drugs, when they are used together.  It refers to the enhanced effect achieved when two or more treatments are used together, exceeding the sum of their individual effects.  This is different from simple additive effects, where the combined effect is just the sum of the individual effects. | Example: The study demonstrated the coefficacy of the two drugs in treating hypertension.  这项研究证明了这两种药物在治疗高血压方面的协同功效。The coefficacy of exercise and a healthy diet in weight loss is well-established.  运动和健康饮食在减肥方面的协同作用已被广泛证实。|近义词：synergy, synergism, combined effect |反义词：antagonism, counteraction |名词
