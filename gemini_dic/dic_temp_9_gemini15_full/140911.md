oncest:/ˈwʌns/| adv.|曾经，一度|Once, it was a common practice.  曾经，这是一种常见的做法。|近义词：formerly, previously, once upon a time|反义词：never, always


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I sometimes go to the park. = 我有时去公园。
She rarely eats meat. = 她很少吃肉。


一般过去时:
He once lived in London. = 他曾经住在伦敦。
They once went to Paris. = 他们曾经去过巴黎。


现在完成时:
I have once visited that museum. = 我曾经参观过那个博物馆。
She has once met the president. = 她曾经见过总统。


过去完成时:
I had once seen that movie before. = 我以前曾经看过那部电影。
They had once traveled to Asia before they moved. = 他们搬家之前曾经去过亚洲。


将来时:
I will once again visit my grandmother. = 我将再次去看望我的祖母。
We will once more try to contact them. = 我们将再次尝试联系他们。


虚拟语气:
If I had once known, I would have done it differently. = 如果我曾经知道，我会做得不一样。
I wish I had once traveled to Italy. = 我希望我曾经去过意大利。


被动语态:
It was once said that... = 曾经有人说过……
The rule was once followed strictly. = 这条规则曾经被严格遵守。


疑问句:
Have you ever seen a whale? = 你曾经见过鲸鱼吗？ (Note:  While "once" isn't directly used, the meaning is similar)
Did you once live in the countryside? = 你以前住在乡下吗？


否定句:
I have never been to Japan. = 我从未去过日本。(Note:  "Never" is the direct opposite)
He did not once mention the incident. = 他一次也没有提及此事。

PHRASE->
once upon a time = 从前
once in a while = 偶尔
once and for all = 一劳永逸地
at once = 立刻；同时
all at once = 突然；同时
