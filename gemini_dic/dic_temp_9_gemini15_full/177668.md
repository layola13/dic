recalculated:/ˌriːˈkælkjuleɪtɪd/| v. adj.|重新计算的，再计算的|To calculate again; to make a new calculation.|例句：The engineer recalculated the bridge's load-bearing capacity after the initial assessment proved insufficient. 工程师在初步评估证明不足后重新计算了桥梁的承重能力。|近义词：recomputed, recomputed, recalibrated|反义词：calculated, estimated


USAGE->

They recalculate the budget every quarter. = 他们每季度重新计算预算。
We recalculate the risk assessment annually. = 我们每年重新评估风险。
