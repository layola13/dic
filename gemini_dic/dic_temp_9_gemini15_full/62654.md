engaged:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒd/ | adj., v. | 忙碌的；订婚的；从事…的；雇佣；吸引；使参与 |


英文解析:As an adjective, "engaged" means busy or occupied; betrothed (in marriage); or actively involved in something. As a verb, it means to occupy or attract someone's attention; to hire or employ someone; to participate in or start something; or to become betrothed.


例句:
He is engaged in a very important project. = 他正从事一个非常重要的项目。

She is engaged to be married. = 她订婚了。


近义词:
busy, occupied, betrothed, involved, employed, hired, absorbed, captivated


反义词:
free, unoccupied, available, disengaged, unemployed, idle, uninterested


词性:
Adjective, Verb
