dissertation:/ˌdɪsərˈteɪʃən/| n. |论文，学位论文 |A long piece of writing on a particular subject, especially one that is done for a university degree.  |例句：She wrote her dissertation on the causes of the American Civil War. 她撰写了关于美国内战起因的论文。 |例句：His dissertation was highly praised by his professors. 他的论文受到了教授们的高度赞扬。|近义词：thesis, treatise, essay |反义词：brief, summary
