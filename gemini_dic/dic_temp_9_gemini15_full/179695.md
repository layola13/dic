reenslave:/ˌriːˈɪnˌsleɪv/| vt.|重新奴役；再次奴役|To enslave again or anew; to subject again to slavery.|例句：The escaped slaves feared being reenslaved.  逃脱的奴隶害怕再次被奴役。

例句：The oppressive regime attempted to reenslave the recently freed population.  压迫性政权试图重新奴役最近获得自由的人口。


近义词：re-enslave, subjugate again, re-subjugate|反义词：liberate, emancipate, free|动词
