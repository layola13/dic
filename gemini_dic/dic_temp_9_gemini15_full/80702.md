gorgonzola:/ˌɡɔːrɡənˈzoʊlə/| n. |戈尔冈佐拉奶酪|A strong-flavored, Italian blue cheese with a creamy, crumbly texture.  It is made from cow's milk and has a distinctive, pungent aroma and taste.  |例句：The pasta dish was topped with a generous amount of gorgonzola cheese. 这道意面菜上面放了大量的戈尔冈佐拉奶酪。

例句：I prefer the sharp, salty taste of gorgonzola to milder cheeses. 我更喜欢戈尔冈佐拉奶酪那种辛辣咸的味道，胜过那些味道较淡的奶酪。
