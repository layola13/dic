cephalotribe:/ˈsɛfələˌtraɪb/| n. |颅钳，脑钳|A cephalotribe is a surgical instrument used in obstetrics to crush the fetal head during difficult childbirth.  It is rarely used now due to advancements in obstetric care and the risks associated with its use.|Example: The cephalotribe was used as a last resort to deliver the baby.  = 颅钳被用作最后手段来分娩婴儿。|近义词：cranioclaste|反义词：None (No direct antonym)|名词
