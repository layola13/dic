pled:/plɛd/ | vt. n. | 保证；誓言；抵押；许诺 | To solemnly promise or declare; to give as a guarantee or security; to commit oneself to a course of action. |例句：He pledged his support for the project. 他保证支持这个项目。
He pledged his loyalty to the king. 他向国王宣誓效忠。


近义词：promise, vow, guarantee, swear, commit | 反义词：break (a promise), renege, violate


词性：动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pledges his allegiance to the country. 他向国家宣誓效忠。
She pledges to donate to charity. 她保证捐款给慈善机构。

一般过去时:
They pledged their support to the campaign. 他们承诺支持这场运动。
He pledged his house as collateral for the loan. 他以房子作抵押贷款。

现在完成时:
She has pledged to finish the project by the end of the year. 她已承诺在年底前完成项目。
They have pledged their support for the new initiative. 他们已经承诺支持这项新举措。

过去完成时:
He had pledged his entire savings to the cause. 他已经把全部积蓄捐献给了这项事业。
She had pledged her support before the meeting even started.  会议还没开始她就承诺支持了。

将来时:
They will pledge their assistance to the victims. 他们将向受害者提供援助。
He will pledge his life to the pursuit of justice. 他将为追求正义而献身。

虚拟语气:
If he pledged his support, we would have a better chance of success. 如果他承诺支持，我们成功的几率就会更大。
I wish he had pledged his support earlier. 我希望他早点承诺支持。

被动语态:
His support was pledged to the cause. 他的支持已承诺给这项事业。
The land was pledged as security for the debt. 这块土地被抵押为债务担保。

疑问句:
Did he pledge his loyalty? 他宣誓效忠了吗？
Has she pledged any money to the fund? 她向基金捐款了吗？

否定句:
He did not pledge his support. 他没有承诺支持。
She has not pledged to attend the meeting. 她没有承诺参加会议。
