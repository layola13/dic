claims:/kleɪmz/| n. vt.|声称；索赔；断言|The act of stating that something is true or is a fact, although it has not been proved.  A demand for something as one's due or right. |例句：He claims to have met the president. 他声称见过总统。

例句：The insurance company is processing his claims for damages. 保险公司正在处理他的损害赔偿请求。


近义词：assertions, allegations, demands, requests|反义词：denials, refutations


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He claims he is innocent. = 他声称自己是无辜的。
She claims to be a doctor. = 她声称自己是一名医生。


一般过去时:
They claimed they saw a UFO. = 他们声称看到一个不明飞行物。
He claimed responsibility for the accident. = 他对事故承担责任。


现在完成时:
She has claimed her prize. = 她已经领取了她的奖品。
They have claimed several victims. = 他们已经声称有几名受害者。


过去完成时:
He claimed he had already finished the work. = 他声称他已经完成了工作。
She claimed she had never seen him before. = 她声称以前从未见过他。


将来时:
They will claim compensation for their losses. = 他们将索赔以弥补他们的损失。
He will claim the throne. = 他将继承王位。


虚拟语气:
If he claimed to be a genius, I wouldn't believe him. = 如果他声称自己是个天才，我不会相信他。
I wish he hadn't claimed that he was right. = 我真希望他没有声称自己是正确的。


被动语态:
It was claimed that the election was rigged. = 有人声称选举舞弊。
The prize was claimed by the winner. = 奖品被获奖者领取了。


疑问句:
What do you claim to be able to do? = 你声称自己能做什么？
Does he claim ownership of this land? = 他声称拥有这块土地吗？


否定句:
He didn't claim to know anything about it. = 他声称对此一无所知。
She denied all the claims against her. = 她否认了针对她的一切指控。


PHRASE->
lay claim to = 声称拥有
stake a claim = 提出要求；宣称所有权
false claims = 虚假主张
groundless claims = 无根据的主张
insurance claims = 保险索赔
