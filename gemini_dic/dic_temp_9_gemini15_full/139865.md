oculary:/ˈɑːkjʊləri/ | n. | 目镜；眼状物 | An ocular is a lens or system of lenses used to view an image, especially in a microscope or telescope.  It is the lens closest to the eye of the observer. | Example: The microscope's ocular magnified the specimen significantly.  显微镜的目镜显著地放大了标本。 |近义词：eyepiece, lens |反义词：objective (in the context of microscopes)
