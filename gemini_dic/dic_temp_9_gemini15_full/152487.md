payee:/peɪˈiː/| n.| 受款人；收款人 | The person or entity to whom a payment is made.  |例句：Please make the check payable to the payee listed on the invoice. 请将支票开给发票上列出的收款人。|例句：The payee of the check was surprised to receive such a large sum. 这张支票的收款人收到这么一大笔钱感到很惊讶。|近义词：recipient, beneficiary |反义词：payer, payor
