combust:/kəmˈbʌst/ | v. |燃烧；使燃烧；激动；燃烧殆尽 | To burn rapidly with a flame.  The process of burning something. |例句：The wood quickly combusted in the fire. 木头很快在火中燃烧起来。  The engine combusted fuel to produce energy. 引擎燃烧燃料产生能量。|近义词：burn, ignite, inflame |反义词：extinguish, quench


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The candle combusts slowly. = 蜡烛慢慢地燃烧。
The engine combusts fuel efficiently. = 引擎高效地燃烧燃料。

一般过去时:
The building combusted rapidly after the explosion. = 爆炸后建筑物迅速燃烧起来。
The mixture combusted violently. = 混合物剧烈燃烧。

现在完成时:
The forest has combusted due to the heatwave. = 森林由于热浪已经燃烧起来。
The fuel has almost combusted. = 燃料几乎燃烧殆尽。

过去完成时:

The paper had already combusted completely. = 纸张已经完全燃烧殆尽。

将来时:
The rocket fuel will combust upon ignition. = 火箭燃料将在点火后燃烧。

虚拟语气:
