kosmos:/ˈkɒzmɒs/| n. |宇宙，世界，秩序|The word "kosmos" refers to the universe as an ordered and harmonious system, often implying a sense of beauty and completeness. It's derived from the ancient Greek word meaning "order," "harmony," and "ornament."  The modern scientific usage often overlaps with "universe," but it can also retain a more philosophical or aesthetic connotation.  |例句：The vast kosmos unfolded before their eyes, a breathtaking spectacle of stars and galaxies.  宇宙在他们眼前展开，是一幅令人叹为观止的星系奇观。|近义词：universe, cosmos, world, macrocosm |反义词：chaos, disorder
