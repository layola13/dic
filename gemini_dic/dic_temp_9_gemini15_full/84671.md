handcrafting:/ˈhændˌkræftɪŋ/| v. |手工制作，精工细作 |The act of making something by hand, typically with great skill and care.  It emphasizes the individual skill and artistry involved in the creation process. |例句：She's handcrafting beautiful ceramic bowls. 她正在手工制作精美的陶瓷碗。

例句：He handcrafts bespoke furniture for discerning clients. 他为眼光独到的客户手工制作定制家具。


近义词：handmade, crafting, artisanal, custom-made

反义词：mass-produced, factory-made, industrialized
