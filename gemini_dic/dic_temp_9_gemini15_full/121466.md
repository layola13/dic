misjudgments: /ˌmɪsˈdʒʌdʒmənts/ | n. |误判，错误判断 |  An incorrect judgment or evaluation; a mistake in assessing a situation or person.  |例句：His misjudgments in handling the crisis led to the company's downfall.  他的危机处理失误导致公司倒闭。

例句：The jury's misjudgments resulted in a wrongful conviction. 陪审团的误判导致了错误的定罪。


近义词：mistakes, errors, miscalculations, misinterpretations

反义词：accurate judgments, correct assessments, sound decisions


词源：mis- (wrongly) + judgment (the ability to make considered decisions or come to sensible conclusions)  源于古法语juggement，最终源于拉丁语judicium (judgment)。
