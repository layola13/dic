overcostly: /ˌoʊvərˈkɒstli/ | adj. | 过于昂贵的；成本过高的 | Excessively expensive; costing too much.  | The project proved overcostly due to unforeseen complications.  这个项目由于不可预见的问题而证明成本过高。 | expensive, pricey, costly | inexpensive, affordable, cheap |  形容词


USAGE->

The project is overcostly. = 这个项目成本过高。
This car is overcostly for a student. = 这辆车对一个学生来说太贵了。

一般过去时:
The renovation was overcostly. = 装修费用过高。
The holiday proved overcostly for our budget. = 假期对我们的预算来说太贵了。

现在完成时:
The new system has proved overcostly. = 新系统已被证明成本过高。
Their efforts have been overcostly and yielded little result. = 他们的努力成本过高且收效甚微。

过去完成时:
We realized the project had become overcostly before it was finished. = 我们在项目完成之前就意识到项目成本过高了。

否定句:

词源:

The word "overcostly" is a combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") and the adjective "costly" (meaning "expensive").  The word is a relatively modern formation, likely emerging as needed to express the idea of excessive cost.
