crustiest: /ˈkrʌstiɪst/ | adj. | 最粗糙的；最刻薄的；最坚硬的 | Most crusty;  extremely tough, hard, or brittle.  It can also describe someone who is grumpy, bad-tempered, or cynical. |


例句：

The crustiest bread is often the tastiest. = 最硬的面包往往是最美味的。

He had a crusty exterior, but a kind heart underneath. = 他外表粗鲁，但内心善良。


近义词：

roughest, toughest, hardest, grumpiest, surliest, most cynical


反义词：

softest, gentlest, kindest, most pleasant, most amiable


词源:

From "crust," meaning a hard outer layer, ultimately from Old French *croute, from Latin crusta "hard outer shell, crust." The figurative sense of "grumpy" developed later.
