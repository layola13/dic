poller:/ˈpɒlər/| n. |民意调查员；轮询程序；检察员|A person who conducts polls or surveys to gather information from a sample of people.  Also refers to a program or system that regularly checks for updates or changes. |例句：The poller interviewed residents about their opinions on the new park. 民意调查员就居民对新公园的意见进行了采访。

The system uses a poller to check for new email every five minutes. 系统使用轮询程序每五分钟检查是否有新邮件。|近义词：surveyor, interviewer, monitor, checker|反义词：N/A
