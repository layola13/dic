murmurish:/ˈmɜːrmərɪʃ/| adj.|低声抱怨的；含糊不清的；低语的|Characterized by murmuring or muttering; indistinct or low in sound.  It often implies discontent or complaint.|例句：The crowd became murmurish as the speaker droned on.  人群在演讲者冗长乏味的演讲中开始低声抱怨。

例句：Her murmurish response was barely audible. 她的低声回应几乎听不见。


近义词：muttering, grumbling, whispering, indistinct


反义词：clear, loud, articulate, distinct

词性:形容词
