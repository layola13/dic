overskilled: /ˌoʊvərˈskɪld/ | adj. | 技术过剩的；资历过高的 | Possessing skills and experience that exceed the requirements of a job or position.  This often leads to boredom, frustration, and a feeling of being underutilized. |例句：He felt overskilled in his current role, longing for more challenging projects. 他觉得目前的工作对他来说技术过剩，渴望更有挑战性的项目。|近义词：overqualified, highly skilled, excessively qualified |反义词：underqualified, unskilled, inexperienced
