ripost:/rɪˈpɒst/| n. v.| 反驳；机智的回答；迅速的反击 | A quick, clever, and often witty reply.  A quick retaliatory action. |例句：Her riposte was so sharp that he had no answer. 她的反驳如此犀利，他无言以对。

He launched a swift riposte. 他进行了迅速的反击。|近义词：reply, retort, rejoinder, comeback |反义词：acceptance, concession


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ripostes with a witty remark. = 他以一句俏皮话进行反驳。
She ripostes to every criticism. = 她对每一个批评都进行反驳。


一般过去时:
He riposted with a well-aimed jab. = 他以精准的一击进行了反击。
She riposted sharply to his accusations. = 她尖锐地反驳了他的指控。


现在完成时:
He has riposted effectively to all challenges. = 他有效地应对所有挑战。
She has riposted to every attempt to undermine her. = 她反驳了所有试图破坏她的企图。


过去完成时:
He had riposted before she could finish her sentence. = 在她说完话之前，他就已经反驳了。
She had riposted several times before he finally gave up. = 在他最终放弃之前，她已经反驳了好几次。


将来时:
He will riposte with force. = 他将有力地反驳。
She will riposte at the next opportunity. = 她将在下一次机会反驳。


虚拟语气:
If he were to riposte, it would be devastating. = 如果他要反驳，那将是毁灭性的。
I wish she had riposted more forcefully. = 我希望她能更强有力地反驳。


被动语态:
His argument was riposted with factual evidence. = 他的论点被事实证据驳斥了。
Her statement was riposted by her opponent. = 她的陈述被她的对手反驳了。


疑问句:

否定句:
He didn't riposte to the insult. = 他没有反驳侮辱。
