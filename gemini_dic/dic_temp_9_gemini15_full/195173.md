semidestructive:/ˌsemiːdɪˈstrʌktɪv/| adj.|半破坏性的，部分破坏性的|Partially destructive; causing some damage but not complete destruction.  The process is semidestructive, altering the data but not erasing it completely.|近义词：partially destructive, somewhat destructive|反义词：completely destructive, non-destructive, harmless


例句：

The earthquake caused semidestructive damage to the city. = 地震对城市造成了一定程度的破坏。

The semidestructive test helped engineers understand the material's breaking point. = 半破坏性测试帮助工程师了解了材料的断裂点。
