microcomputer:/ˈmaɪkroʊkəmˈpjutər/ | n. |微型计算机，微电脑 | A small computer, typically a desktop or laptop, as opposed to a mainframe or supercomputer.  It is characterized by its relatively small size, lower processing power compared to larger systems, and its self-contained nature. | Example: My microcomputer is powerful enough for my daily tasks. 我的微型计算机足以应付我的日常工作。  I bought a new microcomputer last week. 我上周买了一台新的微型计算机。|近义词：personal computer (PC), desktop computer, laptop |反义词：mainframe, supercomputer |名词
