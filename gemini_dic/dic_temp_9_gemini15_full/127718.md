negligee:/ˌnɛɡlɪˈʒeɪ/| n. |轻便睡衣；不拘礼节的服装|A négligée is a lightweight, usually loose-fitting garment worn by women, typically as a dressing gown or informal attire around the house.  It is often made of silk or a similar luxurious fabric.  The term can also imply a careless or negligent manner or style.|She wore a silk négligée while sipping her morning coffee. 她穿着丝绸睡衣啜饮着早晨的咖啡。|dressing gown, robe, housecoat|formal wear, tailored suit|名词

词源:
