pustules:/ˈpjʊsˌtʃuːlz/ | n. |脓疱，小丘疹 | Small, pus-filled bumps on the skin.  They are often inflamed and may be itchy or painful. Pustules are a common symptom of various skin conditions, including acne, eczema, and infections. |Example: The child's face was covered in small pustules.  孩子脸上长满了小脓疱。

The rash on her arm was characterized by numerous pustules. 她手臂上的皮疹的特点是许多脓疱。|近义词：blisters, pimples, zits |反义词：smooth skin, clear skin
