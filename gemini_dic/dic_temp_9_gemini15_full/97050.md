infeoffment:/ɪnˈfiːfmənt/| n. |授封；封地；土地转让|The act of investing someone with a fief or fee; the granting of a feudal estate.  It also refers to the legal instrument by which such a grant was made.  Infeoffment involved a formal ceremony and the delivery of symbolic possession of the land.|例句：The ceremony of infeoffment was witnessed by the entire village. = 整个村庄都见证了授封仪式。

The historical significance of infeoffment lay in its role in establishing feudal land tenure. = 授封在确立封建土地所有制方面具有历史意义。

|近义词：feoffment, grant, conveyance|反义词：dispossession, forfeiture|无
