pedobaptism:/ˌpiːdoʊˈbæptɪzəm/| n. |幼儿洗礼；婴儿洗礼 |The practice of baptizing infants or young children.  This practice is a central tenet of many Christian denominations, particularly those within the Catholic, Orthodox, and some Protestant traditions.  The theological justifications and interpretations vary widely among different groups. |Example:  The debate over pedobaptism versus credobaptism has been ongoing for centuries. 幼儿洗礼与信徒洗礼的争论已经持续了几个世纪。|近义词：infant baptism, child baptism |反义词：credobaptism (believer's baptism)
