rotundas:/roʊˈtʌndəz/| n.|圆形大厅；圆形建筑|A rotunda is a round building or room, usually with a dome.  It often features a circular arrangement of columns or other architectural elements.  The term is derived from the Latin word "rotundus," meaning "round."|例句：The museum's main hall is a magnificent rotunda with a glass dome.  博物馆的主厅是一个带有玻璃圆顶的宏伟圆形大厅。

The ancient Roman baths featured several rotundas for different purposes. 古罗马浴场设有几个用于不同用途的圆形大厅。|近义词：dome, rotunda, circular hall|反义词：angular structure, rectangular building
