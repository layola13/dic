self-renouncements:/ˌsɛlf rɪˈnaʊnsmənts/| n.|自我放弃；自我否定|The act of rejecting or denying oneself something, often a desire, privilege, or belief. It can involve giving up personal ambitions, pleasures, or comforts for a higher purpose or ideal.  This can be a voluntary act or a consequence of circumstances.|例句：His self-renouncements for the sake of his family were admirable. 他为了家庭而做出的自我牺牲令人钦佩。

例句：Her self-renouncements in pursuit of spiritual enlightenment were profound. 她为了追求精神启蒙而做出的自我放弃是深刻的。|近义词：self-denial, self-sacrifice, abnegation, renunciation|反义词：self-indulgence, selfishness, egotism|名词
