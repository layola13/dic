overpromote:/ˌoʊvərprəˈmoʊt/ | vt. | 过度提拔；提拔过高 | To promote someone to a position for which they are not sufficiently qualified or prepared.  This often leads to inefficiency and dissatisfaction. |例句：He was overpromoted and couldn't handle the responsibilities of his new role. 他被过度提拔，无法胜任新职位的职责。

The company overpromoted several junior employees last year, leading to several project failures.  去年公司过度提拔了几名初级员工，导致多个项目失败。|近义词：elevate excessively, prematurely promote, advance too quickly |反义词：demote, downgrade
