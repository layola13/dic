failing:/ˈfeɪlɪŋ/ | n. adj. v. |失败；缺点；不足；不及格；衰退 | failing as a noun means a weakness or fault, while as an adjective it means  unsuccessful or not functioning. As a verb, it means to not succeed. |例句：His only failing is his impatience.他的唯一缺点是缺乏耐心。  The engine is failing.发动机正在故障。He failed the exam. 他考试不及格。 |近义词：(n.) shortcoming, flaw, defect; (adj.) deficient, inadequate, unsuccessful; (v.)  to fail, to fall short |反义词：(n.) strength, virtue, success; (adj.) successful, adequate, proficient; (v.) to succeed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is failing his classes. = 他各科考试都不及格。
The business is failing to meet its targets. = 这家公司未能达到目标。


一般过去时:
The plan failed miserably. = 计划惨败。
The engine failed during the race. = 赛车过程中发动机出现了故障。


现在完成时:
The project has failed to deliver the expected results. = 项目未能取得预期成果。
My health has been failing recently. = 我最近身体状况不佳。


过去完成时:
The bridge had failed before the rescue team arrived. = 在救援队到达之前，桥梁已经垮塌了。
They had failed to anticipate the consequences. = 他们没有预料到后果。


将来时:
If we don't act now, the company will fail. = 如果我们现在不采取行动，公司将会倒闭。
The crops will fail if there's no rain. = 如果不下雨，庄稼就会歉收。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't failed that exam, I would be in university now. = 如果我没有考试不及格，我现在就在大学里了。
I wish I hadn't failed to tell him the truth. = 我真希望我没有忘记告诉他真相。


被动语态:
The test was failed by many students. = 许多学生考试不及格。
The mission was deemed to have failed. = 任务被认为失败了。


疑问句:
Is the system failing? = 系统出故障了吗？
Are your efforts failing to produce results? = 你的努力没有产生结果吗？


否定句:
The system is not failing. = 系统没有故障。
He did not fail to impress the audience. = 他成功地给观众留下了深刻印象。


PHRASE->

failing memory = 记忆力衰退
