reprieves:/rɪˈpriːvz/| n. vt.|缓刑；暂缓；赦免；延期；|The temporary postponement of a punishment.  A respite or delay. |例句：The judge granted him a reprieve. = 法官判给他缓刑。

The prisoner received a last-minute reprieve. = 囚犯在最后一刻获得赦免。


近义词：delay, postponement, respite, pardon, stay

反义词：execution, punishment, condemnation
