chronics:/ˈkrɒnɪks/ /ˈkrɑːnɪks/| adj.|慢性的；长期的；习惯性的|lasting a long time or constantly recurring.|Chronic pain can be debilitating. 慢性疼痛可能使人衰弱。His chronic lateness is a problem. 他总是迟到是个问题。|近义词：persistent, persistent, long-standing, habitual|反义词：acute, short-term


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Chronic diseases are a major public health concern. = 慢性病是主要的公共卫生问题。
His chronic cough bothers him daily. = 他每天都被慢性咳嗽所困扰。


一般过去时:
She suffered from chronic headaches throughout her pregnancy. = 她怀孕期间一直遭受慢性头痛的折磨。
He had chronic back pain for years. = 他多年来一直患有慢性背痛。


现在完成时:
He has had chronic fatigue syndrome for over a decade. = 他患慢性疲劳综合征已有十多年了。
She has experienced chronic insomnia since the accident. = 自事故发生以来，她一直遭受慢性失眠的困扰。


过去完成时:
Before the treatment, he had experienced chronic stomach problems for many years. = 在治疗之前，他多年来一直遭受慢性胃病的困扰。
She had suffered from chronic depression before seeking professional help. = 在寻求专业帮助之前，她一直遭受慢性抑郁症的折磨。


将来时:
His chronic illness will likely require ongoing medical attention. = 他的慢性病很可能需要持续的医疗护理。
The effects of chronic stress will manifest later in life. = 慢性压力的影响将在以后的生活中显现。


虚拟语气:
If he didn't have chronic allergies, he would enjoy the outdoors more. = 如果他没有慢性过敏症，他会更享受户外活动。
I wish I didn't have this chronic condition. = 我希望我没有这种慢性病。


被动语态:
Chronic diseases are often underdiagnosed. = 慢性病往往被诊断不足。
Chronic pain is sometimes treated with medication. = 慢性疼痛有时用药物治疗。


疑问句:

否定句:

The pain is not chronic; it's only temporary. = 疼痛不是慢性的；只是暂时的。
