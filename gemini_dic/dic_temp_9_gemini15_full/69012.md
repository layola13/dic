fattener:/ˈfætənər/| n.|增肥剂；催肥者|A substance or method used to increase the weight or fat content of animals, typically livestock.  It can also refer to a person or thing that causes something to increase in size or weight.|例句：The farmer used a special fattener to increase the weight of his pigs.  这个农民使用了一种特殊的增肥剂来增加他猪的重量。  The rich diet served as a fattener for the athlete.  富含营养的饮食对运动员来说是一种增肥剂。|近义词：feeder, improver|反义词：reducer, leaner
