ramping:/ræmpɪŋ/| n. v.|斜坡；陡坡；增加；逐步提高|The act of increasing or intensifying something gradually.|The construction crew is ramping up the road to make it accessible to vehicles.|近义词：escalation, increase, acceleration|反义词：decrease, reduction, decline|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are ramping up production. = 他们正在提高产量。
The company is ramping up its marketing efforts. = 公司正在加大市场营销力度。

一般过去时:
They ramped up production last quarter. = 上个季度他们提高了产量。
The government ramped up security measures after the attack. = 袭击发生后，政府加强了安全措施。

现在完成时:
They have ramped up their investment in renewable energy. = 他们已经增加了对可再生能源的投资。
The hospital has ramped up its COVID-19 testing capacity. = 医院已经提高了新冠病毒检测能力。

过去完成时:
They had ramped up production before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前提高了产量。
The company had ramped up its marketing campaign before the product launch. = 公司在产品发布前加大了营销力度。

将来时:
They will ramp up production next year. = 他们明年将提高产量。
The factory will ramp up its output to meet the growing demand. = 工厂将提高产量以满足日益增长的需求。

虚拟语气:

I wish they had ramped up the security earlier. = 我希望他们早点加强安保。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

词源:
The word "ramping" originates from the noun "ramp," meaning an inclined plane.  The verb sense of "ramping up" developed from the idea of gradually increasing something, like gradually increasing the incline of a ramp.  The figurative use extended to describe a gradual increase in activity, intensity, or production.
