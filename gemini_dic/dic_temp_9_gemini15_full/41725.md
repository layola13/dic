corroder:/kəˈroʊdər/| v.|腐蚀者，侵蚀者 |To gradually wear away (a material, especially metal) by chemical action; to destroy or damage slowly.|例句：Acid rain corroded the stone statues. 酸雨腐蚀了石像。 The metal pipes were corroded by rust. 金属管道被锈蚀了。|近义词：erode, wear away, eat away|反义词：protect, preserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The acid corrodes the metal. = 酸腐蚀金属。
Rust corrodes iron pipes. = 锈腐蚀铁管。

一般过去时:
The salt water corroded the ship's hull. = 盐水腐蚀了船体。
The old bridge corroded over time. = 老桥随着时间的推移而腐蚀。

现在完成时:
The statue has been corroded by the elements. = 这座雕像已被风雨侵蚀。
The pipes have corroded significantly. = 管道已经严重腐蚀。

过去完成时:
The engine had corroded before they could repair it. = 发动机在他们能修理之前就已经腐蚀了。
The metal components had corroded badly due to neglect. = 由于疏忽，金属部件严重腐蚀。

将来时:
The pipeline will corrode if not properly maintained. = 如果维护不当，管道将会腐蚀。
Exposure to the elements will corrode the fence. = 暴露在自然环境中会腐蚀栅栏。

虚拟语气:
If the pipes were made of stronger material, they wouldn't corrode so easily. = 如果管道采用更坚固的材料制成，它们就不会那么容易腐蚀。
I wish the car's body hadn't corroded so badly. = 我真希望汽车的车身没有腐蚀得这么严重。

被动语态:
The metal was corroded by the chemicals. = 金属被化学物质腐蚀。
The bridge is being corroded by sea water. = 桥梁正在被海水腐蚀。

疑问句:
Is the pipeline corroding? = 管道正在腐蚀吗？
Has the metal corroded yet? = 金属已经腐蚀了吗？

否定句:
The plastic doesn't corrode easily. = 塑料不容易腐蚀。
The coating prevents the metal from corroding. = 涂层防止金属腐蚀。
