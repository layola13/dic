isokinetically:/ˌaɪsoʊkaɪˈnɛtɪkli/| adv.| 等速地；等张地 |  Moving with a constant speed. This term is often used in the context of muscle contractions or exercise where the speed of movement is controlled and remains constant throughout the range of motion.  | Example: The patient performed isokinetic exercises to strengthen their knee.  患者进行等速运动以增强膝盖力量。|近义词: at a constant speed, uniformly |反义词: anisokinetically
