redelegations: /ˌriːˌdɛlɪˈɡeɪʃənz/ | n. | 再委派；重新分配 | The act of delegating something again, or assigning a task or responsibility to someone else after it has already been delegated once.  It often implies a shift in responsibility or a correction of a previous delegation. | Example: The redelegation of tasks proved necessary after the initial assignments proved inefficient. =  在最初的分配被证明效率低下后，重新分配任务被证明是必要的。|近义词：reassignment, reassignment of duties, reallocation | 反义词：centralization, concentration
