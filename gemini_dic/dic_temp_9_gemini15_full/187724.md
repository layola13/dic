rooster:/ˈruːstər/| n. |公鸡|An adult male chicken. Roosters are known for their crowing, especially at dawn. They are also used for breeding and sometimes for meat.|例句：The rooster crowed at sunrise.  公鸡在日出时啼叫。|例句：The farmer kept several roosters for breeding purposes. 这位农民养了几只公鸡用于繁殖。|近义词：cock, chanticleer|反义词：hen

PHRASE->

rooster comb (鸡冠): The fleshy crest on a rooster's head.
rooster tail (鸡尾): The long feathers of a rooster's tail.
rooster fight (斗鸡): A contest between roosters.
rooster feathers (鸡毛): The feathers of a rooster.
