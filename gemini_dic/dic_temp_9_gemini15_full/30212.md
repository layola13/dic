cellarer:/ˈsɛlə(r)ər/| n. |管膳员，财务主管|A cellarer is an official in a monastery or other religious community responsible for managing the supplies and finances, often including the cellar or storeroom.|The cellarer ensured the monks had enough food and wine for the winter.  管膳员确保僧侣们有足够的粮食和酒过冬。| steward, bursar |  |名词
