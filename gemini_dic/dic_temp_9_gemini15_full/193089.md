sectionals:/ˈsɛkʃənəlz/ | n. |组合沙发，分体式沙发 |A sectional is a type of sofa that is made up of multiple separate pieces that can be arranged in different configurations.  This allows for flexibility in adapting the sofa to different room sizes and layouts. |Example: We bought a new sectional for our living room, which allows us to rearrange the pieces to suit different needs. 我们为客厅买了一套新的组合沙发，这样我们可以根据不同的需求重新安排沙发的位置。|近义词：modular sofa, sofa set |反义词：single sofa, loveseat
