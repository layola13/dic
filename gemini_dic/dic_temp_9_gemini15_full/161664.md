pour-out:/pɔːr aʊt/ | vt. |倾倒；倾诉；大量涌出 |To cause (a liquid) to flow from a container; to express one's feelings freely and intensely.|例句：He poured out a glass of wine. 他倒了一杯酒。|例句：She poured out her heart to her friend. 她向朋友倾诉衷肠。|近义词：spill, gush, outpour, vent |反义词：retain, suppress, hold back |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pours out the milk every morning. = 他每天早上都倒牛奶。
She pours out her feelings in her diary. = 她把她的感情倾泻在日记里。

一般过去时:
They poured out the water from the bucket. = 他们把水从桶里倒了出来。
He poured out his frustrations to his therapist. = 他向他的治疗师倾诉了他的苦闷。

现在完成时:
I have poured out all the tea. = 我已经把所有的茶都倒出来了。
She has poured out her heart to me several times. = 她已经几次向我倾诉衷肠了。

过去完成时:
They had poured out the wine before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经把酒倒出来了。
He had poured out his soul in his last letter. = 他在最后一封信中倾诉了他的心声。

将来时:
I will pour out a glass of juice for you. = 我会为你倒一杯果汁。
She will pour out her story to the journalist tomorrow. = 她明天会向记者讲述她的故事。

虚拟语气:
If I poured out my feelings, I would feel better. = 如果我倾诉我的感情，我会感觉更好。
I wish I could pour out my problems to someone. = 我希望我能向某人倾诉我的问题。

被动语态:
The water was poured out of the container. = 水从容器中倒了出来。
His feelings were poured out in his paintings. = 他的感情倾泻在他的绘画中。

疑问句:
Will you pour out the coffee? = 你要倒咖啡吗？
Did she pour out her troubles to him? = 她向他倾诉她的烦恼了吗？

否定句:
He didn't pour out the entire bottle. = 他没有倒出整瓶酒。
She didn't pour out her secrets to anyone. = 她没有向任何人倾诉她的秘密。


PHRASE->

pour out one's anger = 发泄怒火
pour out tea/coffee = 倒茶/咖啡
