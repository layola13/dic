gastroscopic:/ˌɡæstrəˈskɑːpɪk/| adj.|胃镜的；胃镜检查的|Relating to or performed using a gastroscope.  A gastroscopic examination is a procedure that uses a gastroscope to view the inside of the stomach.|例句：The doctor recommended a gastroscopic examination to investigate the cause of his stomach pain.医生建议进行胃镜检查以调查他胃痛的原因。 The gastroscopic images revealed a small ulcer. 胃镜图像显示了一个小溃疡。|近义词：endoscopic (if referring to the procedure)|反义词：N/A
