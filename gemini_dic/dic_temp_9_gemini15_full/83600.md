haematoma:/ˌhiːməˈtoʊmə/| n. |血肿|A collection of blood outside blood vessels, usually in tissues or organs.  It is caused by a break in a blood vessel that allows blood to leak into surrounding tissues.|例句：The boxer suffered a haematoma on his forehead after a heavy blow. 拳击手遭到重击后，额头出现血肿。|例句：A large haematoma formed after the surgery. 手术后形成一个大的血肿。|近义词：bruise, contusion, hematoma|反义词：N/A

USAGE->

The bruise is a haematoma. = 淤青是血肿。
A haematoma forms when blood vessels break. = 当血管破裂时会形成血肿。
