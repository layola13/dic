self-suppressions:/ˌsɛlf səˈprɛʃənz/| n. |自我压制；自我抑制|The act of suppressing or inhibiting one's own thoughts, feelings, or behaviors.  It often involves consciously or unconsciously preventing the expression or awareness of certain aspects of oneself. This can manifest in various ways, from minor self-censorship to severe psychological repression. |例句：Her self-suppressions led to a build-up of resentment and anxiety.  她的自我压制导致了怨恨和焦虑的累积。


例句：The constant self-suppressions of his emotions eventually took a toll on his mental health. 他持续不断的自我情绪压制最终损害了他的心理健康。


近义词：self-restraint, inhibition, repression, suppression


反义词：self-expression, openness, release


词性：名词
