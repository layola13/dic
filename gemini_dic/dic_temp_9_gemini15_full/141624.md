opticians:/ɒpˈtɪʃənz/| n. |眼镜商；验光师|Professionals who examine eyes, test vision, and prescribe or sell corrective lenses.  They are trained to detect eye diseases and refer patients to ophthalmologists when necessary. |I went to the opticians to get a new pair of glasses. 我去看眼镜商配一副新眼镜。|ophthalmologists, optometrists|n/a
