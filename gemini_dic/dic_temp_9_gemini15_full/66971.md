experimentators:/ɪkˌspɛrɪmənˈteɪtərz/| n. |实验者，实验人员 |People who conduct experiments.  They design, carry out, and analyze experiments to test hypotheses or explore phenomena. |The experimentators carefully recorded the results of each trial. 实验人员仔细记录了每次试验的结果。  The experimentators were meticulous in their data collection. 实验人员在数据收集方面非常细致。|researchers, scientists, investigators|observers, theorists|(无明确反义词，可考虑non-experimenters 或 theorists)
