hajj:/hɑːʒ/| n.|朝觐，麦加朝圣|The Hajj is an annual Islamic pilgrimage to Mecca, Saudi Arabia, and a mandatory religious duty for Muslims that must be carried out at least once in their lifetime if they are physically and financially capable.  It is one of the five pillars of Islam.|例句：Millions of Muslims perform the Hajj every year. 数百万穆斯林每年都进行朝觐。

例句：He is planning to go on the Hajj next year. 他计划明年去朝觐。
