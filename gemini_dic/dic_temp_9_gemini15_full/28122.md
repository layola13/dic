capitan:/ˈkæptən/ | n. |船长；队长；首领 | A capitan is the leader of a team or a ship.  It can also refer to a person holding a high rank or position of authority. | 例句：The captain steered the ship through the storm. 船长带领船只度过了暴风雨。| 例句：The football captain led his team to victory. 足球队队长带领他的球队取得了胜利。| 近义词：leader, commander, chief | 反义词：follower, subordinate | 名词
