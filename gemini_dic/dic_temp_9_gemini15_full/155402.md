phases:/ˈfeɪzɪz/| n.|阶段，时期，相位|Different stages or periods in a process, activity, or development.  It can also refer to a particular aspect or stage in the moon's cycle.|例句：The project is in its final phase. 这个项目正处于最后阶段。

例句：The moon goes through different phases. 月亮经历不同的月相。


近义词：stage, period, step, aspect, cycle|反义词：completion, culmination, end


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is in its initial phase. = 项目处于初始阶段。
This phase of the moon is particularly bright. = 这个月相特别明亮。


一般过去时:
The first phase of the project lasted six months. = 项目的第一阶段持续了六个月。
That phase of my life was very challenging. = 我生命中的那个阶段非常具有挑战性。


现在完成时:
We have completed the first two phases of the research. = 我们已经完成了研究的前两个阶段。
The company has gone through many phases of growth. = 这家公司经历了许多发展阶段。


过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, they had already completed the first phase. = 我到达时，他们已经完成了第一阶段。
They had finished the preparatory phase before starting the main project. = 他们在开始主要项目之前已经完成了准备阶段。


将来时:
The next phase of the project will begin next month. = 项目的下一阶段将于下个月开始。
We will enter a new phase in our relationship. = 我们将进入一段新的关系阶段。


虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we could have completed this phase sooner. = 如果我们有更多时间，我们本可以更快地完成这个阶段。
I wish I had known about this phase earlier. = 我真希望我早点知道这个阶段。


被动语态:
The project phases are being carefully managed. = 项目阶段正在被仔细管理。
The different phases were explained in detail in the report. = 报告详细解释了各个阶段。


疑问句:
What phase are we in right now? = 我们现在处于哪个阶段？
What are the different phases involved in this process? = 这个过程中涉及哪些不同的阶段？


否定句:
The project is not yet in its final phase. = 项目尚未处于最后阶段。
We haven't finished the first phase yet. = 我们还没有完成第一阶段。


PHRASE->
in the initial phase = 在初始阶段
in the final phase = 在最后阶段
a critical phase = 一个关键阶段
a transitional phase = 一个过渡阶段
a new phase = 一个新阶段
