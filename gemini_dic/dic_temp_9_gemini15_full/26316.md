buy the farm:/baɪ ðə fɑːrm/| v. phr.|去世，死亡|This idiom refers to dying, particularly in a violent or sudden manner, often associated with accidents, especially in farming or military contexts. The origin is uncertain, but likely relates to the transfer of property upon death, where a farmer's death would lead to the "buying" of their farm by others (through inheritance or sale).|例句：He bought the farm in a tragic farming accident. 他在一次悲惨的农业事故中去世了。|例句：The pilot bought the farm when his plane crashed.  飞行员飞机失事身亡。|近义词：kick the bucket, bite the dust, pass away, die|反义词：be born, live
