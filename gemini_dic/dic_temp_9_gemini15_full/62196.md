encorporating:/ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 包含；吸收；合并；使并入 | Including or combining something into a whole.  |The new design incorporates many innovative features. 新设计融合了许多创新功能。| integrate, combine, unify | separate, exclude | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is incorporating new ideas into his project. 他正在将新的想法融入他的项目中。
The company is incorporating sustainable practices into its operations. 这家公司正在将其业务中纳入可持续发展实践。


一般过去时:
She incorporated several different styles into her painting. 她将几种不同的风格融入到她的绘画中。
They incorporated my suggestions into the final report. 他们将我的建议纳入了最终报告。


现在完成时:
We have incorporated feedback from users into the latest update. 我们已将用户的反馈纳入最新更新中。
The artist has incorporated elements of nature into her sculptures.这位艺术家已将自然元素融入她的雕塑中。


过去完成时:
They had incorporated all the necessary data before submitting the proposal. 他们在提交提案之前已经纳入了所有必要的数据。
She had incorporated several revisions before sending the manuscript to the publisher.  在她将手稿寄给出版商之前，她已经做了几次修改。


将来时:
We will incorporate your comments into the next version of the software. 我们将在软件的下一个版本中加入您的评论。
They will be incorporating new technology into their manufacturing process.他们将把新技术纳入他们的制造流程中。


虚拟语气:
If they incorporated my suggestions, the project would be much better. 如果他们采纳我的建议，项目将会好得多。
I wish they had incorporated more visual aids into the presentation. 我希望他们在演示文稿中加入更多直观的辅助工具。


被动语态:
New features were incorporated into the latest software release. 最新软件版本中包含了新功能。
Sustainable practices are being incorporated into the company's operations. 可持续发展实践正在被纳入公司的运营中。


疑问句:
Are you incorporating any new techniques into your work? 你在工作中融入了什么新技术吗？
Will they be incorporating customer feedback into their product design? 他们会将客户反馈纳入他们的产品设计中吗？


否定句:
He did not incorporate any changes into the document. 他没有在文档中加入任何更改。
They are not incorporating user feedback into their design process. 他们没有将用户反馈纳入他们的设计流程中。
