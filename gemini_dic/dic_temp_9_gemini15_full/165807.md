preobtain:/priːəbˈteɪn/| vt.|预先获得，事先取得|To obtain something before it is needed or before a particular time.  This implies securing something in advance to avoid future difficulties or delays.|例句：The company preobtained all necessary permits before starting construction. 公司在开工前预先取得了所有必要的许可证。
例句：He preobtained a visa to ensure a smooth trip. 他提前取得签证以确保行程顺利。|近义词：secure beforehand, acquire in advance, get ahead of time|反义词：postpone, delay
