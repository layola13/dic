illustratively:/ɪˈlʌstrətɪvli/ | adv. |  举例说明地；作为例证地 |  In a way that serves as an example or illustration; by way of illustration.  It provides examples to clarify or explain a point. | 例句：Illustratively, the teacher used several diagrams to explain the concept.  老师用几个图表来举例说明这个概念。


例句：The author illustratively showed the effects of pollution with several compelling case studies. 作者用几个引人注目的案例研究举例说明了污染的影响。


近义词：exemplificatory, for instance, for example, by way of example


反义词：abstractly, generally, theoretically


词性: 副词
