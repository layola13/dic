ependymis: /ɪˈpɛndɪmɪs/ | n. | 室管膜 |  The ependyma is a thin membrane that lines the ventricles of the brain and the central canal of the spinal cord. It is composed of specialized epithelial cells called ependymal cells, which are involved in the production and circulation of cerebrospinal fluid (CSF).  |  The ependymal cells lining the ventricles actively produce cerebrospinal fluid. | 近义词：choroid plexus (partially, as it contributes to CSF production) | 反义词：无

词源:
