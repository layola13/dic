ripienist:/ˌrɪpiəˈnɪst/| n. |  伴奏歌手；齐奏乐手 | A ripienist is a singer or instrumentalist who performs in the ripieno section of an orchestra or chorus.  They provide the background or supporting sound, as opposed to the soloists or concertino section.  | The ripienists provided a rich, full sound to the orchestra’s performance.  伴奏歌手为乐团的演出提供了丰富饱满的声音。| supporting musician, chorus member | soloist, principal player | 名词
