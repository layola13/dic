notifiable:/ˈnoʊtɪfaɪəbl/| adj.|应报告的；必须申报的|Something that is notifiable is required by law to be reported to the authorities.  It often refers to diseases, incidents, or events that need official notification due to public health or safety concerns. |例句：Cholera is a notifiable disease.  霍乱是一种必须申报的疾病。
例句：The accident was a notifiable incident under the Health and Safety at Work Act. 根据《工作场所健康与安全法》，该事故是必须申报的事故。|近义词：reportable, reportable, mandatory, compulsory|反义词：unreported, undisclosed
