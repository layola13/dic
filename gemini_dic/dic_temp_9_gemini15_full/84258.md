half-starved:/ˌhɑːfˈstɑːrvd/| adj.|半饿死的，极度饥饿的 | Extremely hungry; suffering from lack of food.  The adjective describes a state of severe hunger, often implying a prolonged period without sufficient nourishment. |例句：The half-starved refugees were desperate for food and water.  半饿死的难民们急需食物和水。

The half-starved dog looked pathetically at the scraps of food.  半饿死的狗可怜巴巴地看着食物残渣。|近义词：famished, starving, ravenous, undernourished |反义词：well-fed, satiated, replete
