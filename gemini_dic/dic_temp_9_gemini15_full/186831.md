ringbolt:/ˈrɪŋbəʊlt/| n. |环形螺栓|A ringbolt is a type of bolt with a ring or eye at one end, used for attaching ropes, chains, or other items.  It is typically made of metal and is designed to withstand significant tensile loads.|Example: The life raft was secured to the deck using a sturdy ringbolt.|近义词：eye bolt, shackle|反义词：None
