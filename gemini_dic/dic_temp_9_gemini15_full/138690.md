nuked:/nukt/| vt.|用微波炉加热；摧毁；|to cook (food) in a microwave oven; to destroy something completely by bombing or attacking it with nuclear weapons.|例句：I nuked a leftover pizza for lunch. 我用微波炉加热了剩下的比萨当午餐。|例句：The city was nuked during the war.  这个城市在战争中被核武器摧毁了。|近义词：microwaved, zapped, destroyed, obliterated|反义词：preserved, protected, saved
