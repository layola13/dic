landholding: /ˈlændˌhoʊldɪŋ/ | n. | 土地所有权；土地持有；地产 | The ownership or possession of land.  It often refers to the amount of land owned or controlled by an individual or entity.  |例句：The family's landholding has been passed down for generations.  这个家族的土地所有权已经传承了好几代。

The government implemented new policies to reform landholding patterns. 政府实施了新的政策来改革土地所有权模式。|近义词：estate, property, possession |反义词：landlessness

过去完成时:
