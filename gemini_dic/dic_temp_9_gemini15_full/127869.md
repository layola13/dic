neocolonialists: /ˌniːoʊkəˈloʊniəlɪsts/ | n. |新殖民主义者|Those who practice or support neocolonialism, a system where powerful nations exert economic, political, or cultural influence over less developed countries, often mimicking the methods of traditional colonialism but without direct political control.|例句：The neocolonialists exploited the country's resources for their own benefit. 新殖民主义者为了自己的利益剥削了这个国家的资源。|近义词：imperialists, exploiters |反义词：anti-imperialists, liberationists
