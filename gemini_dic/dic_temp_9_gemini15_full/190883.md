saylor:/ˈseɪlər/| n. |水手，海员 | A sailor is a person who works on a ship, especially as a member of the crew.  Sailors are skilled in navigation and seamanship. | Example: The sailor expertly navigated the stormy seas.  这位水手熟练地驾驭着暴风雨的海浪。  The old sailor spun tales of his adventures at sea.  这位老水手讲述了他海上冒险的故事。|近义词： seaman, mariner |反义词：landlubber
