outdeliver:/ˌaʊtˈdɪlɪvər/| vt.|胜过；超越；比…做得更好|To surpass or exceed in performance or delivery.|例句：Our team outdelivered all expectations, exceeding our sales targets by 20%. 我们的团队超出了所有预期，销售目标超出20%。|例句：The new software outdelivers its predecessor in terms of speed and efficiency.  这款新软件在速度和效率方面都超越了它的前代产品。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outperform, outstrip|反义词：underperform, fail, disappoint|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company outdelivers its competitors in customer service. = 这家公司在客户服务方面胜过其竞争对手。
He consistently outdelivers his colleagues in terms of productivity. = 他在生产力方面总是胜过他的同事。


一般过去时:
Last quarter, our sales team outdelivered their targets. = 上个季度，我们的销售团队超额完成了目标。
The athlete outdelivered his rivals in the final race. = 运动员在决赛中战胜了他的对手。


现在完成时:
The new marketing campaign has outdelivered all previous efforts. = 新的营销活动已经超越了所有之前的努力。
She has outdelivered expectations throughout her career. = 她在整个职业生涯中都超出了预期。


过去完成时:
By the end of the project, they had outdelivered the initial estimations. = 到项目结束时，他们已经超过了最初的估计。
The team had outdelivered their own previous records. = 该团队已经超过了他们之前的记录。


将来时:
We expect the new product to outdeliver its competitors in the market. = 我们预计新产品将在市场上胜过竞争对手。
With this strategy, we will outdeliver our sales goals for the year. = 通过这个策略，我们将超额完成今年的销售目标。


虚拟语气:
If we worked harder, we could outdeliver our rivals. = 如果我们更努力工作，我们就能胜过我们的竞争对手。
I wish our team had outdelivered the competition. = 我希望我们的团队能战胜竞争对手。


被动语态:

疑问句:

Does the new model outdeliver the old one? = 新型号是否优于旧型号？


否定句:

They failed to outdeliver their competitors in the market. = 他们未能超越市场上的竞争对手。
