capoeira:/ˌkæpʊˈeɪrə/| n.|卡波耶拉（巴西的一种格斗舞）|A Brazilian martial art that combines elements of dance, acrobatics, and music. It is known for its fluid movements and deceptive techniques.|例句：He learned capoeira in Brazil. 他在巴西学习卡波耶拉。|例句：The capoeira performance was mesmerizing. 卡波耶拉表演令人着迷。|近义词：martial art, fighting style|反义词：无
