mucigen:/ˈmjuːsɪdʒən/| n. |粘液原，粘液素|A glycoprotein that is a precursor to mucus.  It is secreted by goblet cells and other glandular cells and forms mucus upon hydration.| 例句：The goblet cells in the lining of the respiratory tract secrete mucigen, which hydrates to form mucus.| 近义词：mucus precursor | 反义词：无
