extravagant:/ɪkˈstrævəɡənt/ | adj. |奢侈的；浪费的；过分的 | characterized by excessive extravagance or luxury; exceeding what is reasonable or necessary.  |例句：Her extravagant spending habits landed her in debt.  她的奢侈消费习惯使她负债累累。

例句：His extravagant claims were met with skepticism. 他过分的言论受到了怀疑。


近义词：lavish, prodigal, sumptuous, excessive, immoderate | 反义词：frugal, economical, thrifty, modest, restrained

词源:  From Middle French *extravagant, from Latin *extravagans, present participle of *extravagari "to wander outside," from extra- "outside" + vagari "to wander."  The sense development is from "wandering outside the bounds of reason" to "excessive," then to "lavish."
