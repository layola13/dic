/ˌpriːˈdeɪnˈeɪʃənz/| n. |预先捐款；提前捐赠|Pre-donations refer to donations made in advance of an event or for a future purpose.  This practice allows organizations to secure funding before they need it, facilitating better planning and resource allocation. |Example:  The charity received several pre-donations before launching their fundraising campaign.  慈善机构在启动筹款活动之前收到了几笔预先捐款。|近义词：advance contributions, early donations |反义词：post-donations, late donations


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Pre-donations help organizations plan effectively. = 预先捐款帮助组织有效地进行规划。
The company accepts pre-donations for their annual event. = 公司接受他们年度活动的预先捐款。


一般过去时:
They received many pre-donations last year. = 他们去年收到了很多预先捐款。
The project benefited from pre-donations from several sponsors. = 该项目受益于几位赞助商的预先捐款。

We have already received several pre-donations for the project. = 我们已经收到了该项目的几笔预先捐款。
