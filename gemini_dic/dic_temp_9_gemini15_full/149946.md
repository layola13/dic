pallie:/ˈpæli/| n. v.|伙伴，朋友；闲聊|A pallie is a close friend or companion.  The verb form means to spend time in a friendly way, often chatting.|例句：He's my oldest pallie. 他是我最老的朋友。  They were pallieing around in the park. 他们在公园里闲聊。|近义词：friend, chum, mate, buddy|反义词：enemy, foe
