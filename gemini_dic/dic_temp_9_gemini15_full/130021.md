non-opposition:/ˌnɒnˌɒpəˈzɪʃən/| n. | 无反对，不反对 | The lack of opposition or objection to something.  It implies a passive acceptance rather than active support. | Example: The proposal passed with non-opposition from the board.  董事会未表示反对，该提案得以通过。|近义词：acquiescence, consent, approval |反义词：opposition, objection, dissent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There is non-opposition to the new policy. = 对这项新政策没有人反对。
Non-opposition usually means passive acceptance. = 无反对通常意味着被动接受。


一般过去时:
There was non-opposition to the budget cuts. = 对预算削减没有异议。
The committee's decision passed with non-opposition. = 委员会的决定未遭反对而通过。


现在完成时:
There has been non-opposition to the changes so far. = 到目前为止，对这些变化没有人反对。
The project has proceeded with non-opposition from stakeholders. = 该项目在利益相关者的默许下得以推进。


过去完成时:
There had been non-opposition to the previous proposals. = 之前的提案没有遭到反对。
The company had anticipated non-opposition to the merger. = 公司预计合并不会遭到反对。


将来时:
There will likely be non-opposition to the upcoming election. = 即将到来的选举很可能不会遭到反对。
We expect non-opposition to our revised plan. = 我们预计我们修订后的计划不会遭到反对。


虚拟语气:
If there were non-opposition, the project would proceed smoothly. = 如果没有反对，项目将会顺利进行。
I wish there had been non-opposition to my suggestion. = 我希望我的建议没有遭到反对。


被动语态:
Non-opposition was expected by the management team. = 管理团队预计不会遭到反对。
The plan was passed due to non-opposition from the public. = 该计划由于公众未表示反对而得以通过。


疑问句:
Is there non-opposition to the current proposal? = 对目前的提案是否没有人反对？
Was there non-opposition to the previous amendment? = 对之前的修正案是否没有人反对？


否定句:
There was not non-opposition to the plan; several members voiced concerns. = 对该计划并非没有反对，一些成员表达了担忧。
The lack of active support doesn't equate to non-opposition. = 缺乏积极支持并不等于没有反对。
