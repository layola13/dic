predevelopment:/ˌpriːdɪˈvɛləpmənt/| n. |前期开发；预先开发；开发前期阶段|The activities and processes that occur before the main development phase of a project, including planning, feasibility studies, and securing permits.  This stage often involves significant research and analysis to mitigate risks and ensure the project's success.|The predevelopment phase of the new stadium included extensive environmental impact assessments.  前期开发新体育场包括广泛的环境影响评估。|development, planning, preparation|completion, abandonment|名词
