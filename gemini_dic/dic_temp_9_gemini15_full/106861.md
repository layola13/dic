labrador:/ˈlæbrədɔːr/| n. |拉布拉多犬|A breed of large, powerfully built dog, originally from Newfoundland, typically having a short, dense coat and being used as a working dog (e.g., for retrieving game).  |例句：My neighbour has a beautiful chocolate Labrador.  我的邻居有一只漂亮的巧克力拉布拉多犬。

例句：Labrador Retrievers are known for their friendly and intelligent nature. 拉布拉多犬以其友好和聪明的性格而闻名。


近义词：retriever (泛指寻回犬)


反义词：无


词性:名词
