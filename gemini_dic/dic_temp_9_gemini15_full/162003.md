prawn:/prɔːn/| n.|对虾，明虾|A small shellfish with a long body, ten legs, and a shell that is often pink or reddish-brown.  They are a popular seafood. |例句：We had grilled prawns for dinner.  我们晚餐吃了烤虾。

例句：The prawns were fresh and delicious. 这虾很新鲜美味。


近义词：shrimp (though there are subtle differences in size and species)|反义词：无


词源:  The word "prawn" comes from the Old English "prawn," which in turn is likely of Germanic origin, possibly related to words meaning "to crawl" or "to creep."
