reroof:/ˌriːˈruːf/| vt.|重新铺设屋顶|To put a new roof on (a building).  This involves removing the old roofing materials and installing new ones.|例句：We need to reroof the house before winter. 我们需要在冬天来临之前重新铺设房子的屋顶。

例句：The old roof was leaking badly, so we decided to reroof the entire building. 旧屋顶漏得很厉害，所以我们决定重新铺设整栋楼的屋顶。


近义词：re-roof, re-cover (a roof)
反义词：无
