catchword:/ˈkætʃwɜːrd/| n. |口号；流行语；关键词；主题词|A catchword is a word or phrase that is often repeated and becomes associated with a particular idea, person, or group.  It can be a slogan, a buzzword, or a keyword used to summarize or represent something. |例句：The campaign's catchword was "Hope for the future." 这场运动的口号是“对未来的希望”。|例句： "Sustainability" is a popular catchword in the business world. “可持续性”是商界流行的口号。|近义词：slogan, motto, buzzword, keyword, theme word |反义词：N/A
