pace:/peɪs/ | n., v. | 步伐；速度；节奏；步调；缓步走；踱步 |

英文解析:
As a noun, "pace" refers to the speed at which someone or something moves or progresses. It can also refer to a single step in walking or running, or a particular speed or rhythm, such as the pace of life or work. As a verb, "pace" means to walk with steady steps, often back and forth, or to set the speed or rhythm of something.

例句:
He walked at a slow pace. 他以缓慢的速度行走。

She set a fast pace for the race. 她为比赛设定了快速的节奏。


近义词:speed, tempo, rhythm, gait, stride

反义词:slowness, sluggishness, stagnation


词性:
Noun and Verb
