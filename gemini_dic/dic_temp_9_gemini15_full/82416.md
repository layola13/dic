grueling:/ˈɡruːəlɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人筋疲力尽的；费力的；艰苦的 | Extremely tiring and demanding.  | 例句：The grueling hike left us exhausted.  令人筋疲力尽的徒步旅行让我们筋疲力尽了。

The grueling training schedule pushed the athletes to their limits.  艰苦的训练计划将运动员逼到了极限。| 近义词：exhausting, strenuous, arduous, laborious | 反义词：easy, relaxing, effortless
