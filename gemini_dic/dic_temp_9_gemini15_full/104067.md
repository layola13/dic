jumbler:/ˈdʒʌmblər/| n.|弄乱者；混合器|A person or thing that jumbles things.  Someone or something that mixes things up in a chaotic manner.|例句：The dog was a real jumbler, scattering toys all over the room.  这只狗真是个捣蛋鬼，把玩具扔得满屋都是。|近义词：mixer, shuffler, disorganizer|反义词：organizer, arranger
