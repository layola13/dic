gain-over: /ɡeɪnˈoʊvər/ | phrasal verb | 获得，赢得，克服 |  This phrasal verb implies acquiring something, usually an advantage or control, often after overcoming an obstacle.  It suggests a process of struggle and eventual success. | 例句：The team gained over the opponent in the final seconds. = 球队在最后几秒钟赢得了对手。

The company gained over the market share after launching a successful marketing campaign. = 公司在推出成功的营销活动后获得了市场份额。

近义词：obtain, acquire, win, overcome, conquer | 反义词：lose, fail, surrender


词性: phrasal verb
