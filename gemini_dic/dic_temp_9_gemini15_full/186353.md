rhymers:/ˈraɪmərz/| n. |押韵者；说唱歌手|People who create rhymes, especially poets or rappers.  Rhymers often use techniques like assonance, consonance, and alliteration to create musicality and emphasis in their work. They might write poetry, lyrics, or even just engage in playful rhyming games.|Example:  Many famous poets are considered skilled rhymers. 许多著名的诗人被认为是熟练的押韵者。 The young rhymers impressed the audience with their fast-paced rap performance. 年轻的说唱歌手们以他们快节奏的说唱表演给观众留下了深刻的印象。|近义词: poets, rappers, lyricists |反义词: prose writers (in the context of poetry)
