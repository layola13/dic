creeps:/kriːps/ | v. n. | 缓慢移动；令人毛骨悚然的人或事物；令人毛骨悚然的感觉 | to move slowly and quietly, often in a way that is slightly frightening; a person who is strange and slightly frightening; a feeling of unease or fear | 例句：The spider creeps across the floor.  蜘蛛缓慢地爬过地板。


例句：The feeling of being watched creeps me out.  被监视的感觉让我毛骨悚然。


近义词：crawls, slithers, creeps me out, creeps under my skin | 反义词：rushes, dashes


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The spider creeps across the floor. = 蜘蛛缓慢地爬过地板。
He creeps up the stairs slowly. = 他慢慢地爬上楼梯。

一般过去时:
The cat crept silently into the room. = 猫悄悄地溜进了房间。
She crept away unnoticed. = 她偷偷溜走了，没有被人注意到。

现在完成时:
The cold has crept into my bones. = 寒意已经渗透到我的骨子里了。
Fear has crept into her heart. = 恐惧已经爬上了她的心头。

过去完成时:
He had crept into bed before his parents noticed. = 他在他父母注意到之前已经偷偷溜上床了。
The thief had crept away before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，小偷已经偷偷溜走了。

将来时:
The shadows will creep across the walls as the sun sets. = 太阳下山时，阴影会爬过墙壁。
He will creep into her room to look for his toy. = 他会偷偷溜进她的房间寻找他的玩具。

虚拟语气:
If I crept into his room, I could get his toy. = 如果我偷偷溜进他的房间，我就能拿到他的玩具。
I wish I hadn't crept up on him like that. = 我真希望我没有那样偷偷靠近他。

被动语态:
The thief was crept upon by the police. = 小偷被警察偷偷靠近了。(较少用被动语态)
The sound of footsteps was crept across the empty street. = 空荡荡的街道上传来了蹑手蹑脚的脚步声。(较少用被动语态)

疑问句:
Does the cat creep up the tree? = 猫是爬上树的吗？
Is he creeping behind the house? = 他是不是躲在房子后面偷偷摸摸的？

否定句:
The snail didn't creep across the path. = 蜗牛没有爬过小路。
He didn't creep into the room; he walked in openly. = 他没有偷偷溜进房间；他是大大方方走进去的。


PHRASE->
creep up on someone = 偷偷接近某人
creep into something = 偷偷溜进…
creep out = 使毛骨悚然
creep into (one's mind) = 潜入（某人的脑海）
creep along = 缓慢地移动
