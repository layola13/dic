recline:/rɪˈklaɪn/ | v. | 躺下；斜倚；放松；倾斜 | To lean or lie back in a relaxed position.  |例句：He reclined in his armchair, enjoying the warmth of the fire. 他斜倚在扶手椅上，享受着炉火的温暖。

例句：She reclined on the sofa, reading a book. 她斜躺在沙发上，看书。


近义词：lean back, lie back, lounge, sprawl | 反义词：stand, sit upright, straighten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reclines on the couch every evening. = 他每天晚上都躺在沙发上。
I usually recline my seat on long flights. = 我通常在长途飞行中把座位放倒。

一般过去时:
She reclined gracefully on the chaise lounge. = 她优雅地斜躺在躺椅上。
He reclined his head against the pillow. = 他把头靠在枕头上。

现在完成时:
I have reclined too much today and my back aches. = 我今天躺得太久了，背疼。
They have reclined by the pool all afternoon. = 他们整个下午都躺在游泳池边。

过去完成时:
She had reclined for hours before finally falling asleep. = 在最终睡着之前，她已经躺了几个小时了。
He had reclined on the beach, completely relaxed. = 他躺在沙滩上，完全放松了。

将来时:
I will recline by the pool tomorrow. = 我明天将在游泳池边躺着。
We will recline in comfortable chairs during the performance. = 我们将在演出期间躺在舒适的椅子上。

虚拟语气:
If I had a comfortable chair, I would recline and relax. = 如果我有一把舒适的椅子，我会躺下休息。
I wish I could recline and read a book right now. = 我真希望我现在能躺下看书。

被动语态:
The chair can be reclined to a comfortable angle. = 椅子可以倾斜到一个舒适的角度。
The seats in the theater are designed to be reclined easily. = 剧院的座位设计成易于放倒。

疑问句:
Does he often recline in front of the television? = 他经常躺在电视机前吗？
Can this chair be reclined? = 这把椅子可以放倒吗？

否定句:
He did not recline; he sat upright. = 他没有躺下；他直坐着。
She couldn't recline comfortably because the seat was too hard. = 她不能舒服地躺下，因为座位太硬了。
