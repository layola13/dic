rewarm:/ˌriːˈwɔːrm/| vt.|再次加热，重新加热|To heat something again, especially food that has already been cooked.  |例句：I need to rewarm the leftovers for dinner. 我需要把剩菜重新加热一下当晚餐。

例句：Let's rewarm the soup before we eat.  让我们在吃饭前把汤重新加热一下。


近义词：heat up, warm up|反义词：cool down, chill


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rewarms the milk before giving it to the baby. 他在给婴儿喝奶之前会把奶重新加热。
She rewarms the coffee every morning. 她每天早上都会把咖啡重新加热。


一般过去时:
I rewarmed the pizza slices last night. 我昨晚把披萨重新加热了。
They rewarmed the stew before serving it. 他们在上菜之前把炖菜重新加热了。

过去完成时:
