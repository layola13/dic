overlegislate:/ˌoʊvərˈlɛdʒɪsleɪt/| vt.|立法过度；制定过多法律|To make too many laws, often resulting in confusing or contradictory regulations.  The government is accused of overlegislating in an attempt to control every aspect of citizens' lives.|近义词：overregulate, overburden with laws|反义词：underregulate, deregulate
