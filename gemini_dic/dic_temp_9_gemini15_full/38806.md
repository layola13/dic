conduits:/ˈkɒnduɪts/ /ˈkɑːnduɪts/| n. |管道；渠道；导管 |A conduit is a pipe, tube, or channel through which something can pass.  It can refer to physical structures like pipes for water or electricity, or metaphorical channels for information or influence. |例句：The water flowed through the conduit. 水流经管道。

The government used the media as a conduit for its propaganda. 政府利用媒体作为其宣传的渠道。|近义词：channel, duct, pipe, tube |反义词：barrier, obstruction
