chronostratigraphical: /ˌkrənoʊˌstrætəˈɡræfɪkəl/ | adj. | 年代地层学的；时间地层学的 | Relating to chronostratigraphy, the branch of stratigraphy that deals with the relative time relationships of rock strata.  It focuses on the age and time relationships of rock formations, rather than their lithological characteristics. | The chronostratigraphical analysis revealed the sedimentary layers were deposited over millions of years.  年代地层学分析表明沉积层沉积了数百万年。 |  geochronological, geochronologic |  (no direct antonym, but contrasting terms could include lithostratigraphic, biostratigraphic) |  形容词
