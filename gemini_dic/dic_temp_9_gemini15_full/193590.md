self-assembles:/ˌsɛlf əˈsɛmbəlz/ | v. | 自组装，自行组装 |  The verb "self-assembles" describes the process where components spontaneously organize themselves into a more complex structure without external guidance or control. This is a common phenomenon in nature and is being increasingly exploited in nanotechnology and materials science. | Example:  The nanoparticles self-assemble into a highly ordered structure.  纳米粒子自组装成高度有序的结构。

The protein molecules self-assemble to form a functional enzyme. 蛋白质分子自组装形成功能性酶。


近义词: self-organizes, spontaneously assembles, aggregates


反义词: manually assembles, externally assembled


词性: verb
