pro bono:/ˌproʊ ˈboʊnoʊ/| adj.|公益的，免费的|for the public good; done or given free of charge.|The lawyer provided pro bono services to the indigent client.  律师为贫困客户提供公益服务。|近义词：charitable, gratuitous, free|反义词：paid, for-profit


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They offer pro bono legal advice. = 他们提供免费法律咨询。
The organization provides pro bono services to the community. = 该组织为社区提供公益服务。


一般过去时:
She volunteered her time for a pro bono project last year. = 她去年志愿参与了一个公益项目。
The firm completed a pro bono case last month. = 该律师事务所上个月完成了一起公益案件。


现在完成时:
We have completed several pro bono projects this year. = 我们今年已经完成了几个公益项目。
The charity has received many pro bono contributions. = 该慈善机构收到了许多公益捐款。


过去完成时:
They had already finished the pro bono work before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经完成了公益工作。
The artist had donated many paintings for pro bono exhibitions. = 该艺术家已经为公益展览捐赠了许多绘画作品。


将来时:
We will offer pro bono services to those in need. = 我们将为有需要的人提供公益服务。
The company will continue its pro bono program next year. = 公司明年将继续其公益项目。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would take on more pro bono cases. = 如果我有更多时间，我会承担更多公益案件。
It would be wonderful if more professionals offered pro bono services. = 如果更多专业人士提供公益服务就好了。


被动语态:
Pro bono services are offered to the underprivileged. = 为弱势群体提供公益服务。
Many pro bono projects are funded by donations. = 许多公益项目由捐款资助。


疑问句:
Are you involved in any pro bono work? = 你参与过任何公益工作吗？
Does your company have a pro bono program? = 贵公司有公益项目吗？


否定句:
He doesn't accept paid cases, only pro bono work. = 他不接受付费案件，只做公益工作。
The firm does not have the capacity for additional pro bono projects. = 该律师事务所没有能力承担额外的公益项目。


PHRASE->
pro bono publico = for the public good (公益的)
pro bono services = free services (免费服务)
pro bono work = voluntary work (志愿工作)
pro bono legal aid = free legal assistance (免费法律援助)
pro bono consultation = free consultation (免费咨询)
