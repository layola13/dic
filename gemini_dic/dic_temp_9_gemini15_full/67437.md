extinguishes:/ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃɪz/ | vt. |熄灭；使无效；压制|To put an end to something, such as a fire, light, or feeling.  It can also mean to make something invalid or ineffective.|

The firefighter quickly extinguished the blaze. | 消防员迅速扑灭了大火。

He extinguished the candle before going to bed. | 他睡觉前吹灭了蜡烛。


近义词：put out, quench, suppress

反义词：ignite, kindle, inflame


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He extinguishes the candles every night. = 他每天晚上都熄灭蜡烛。
The company extinguishes all competition. = 这家公司压制所有竞争对手。


一般过去时:
She extinguished the fire with a fire extinguisher. = 她用灭火器扑灭了火。

现在完成时:

将来时:
