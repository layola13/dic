reintroduces: /ˌriːɪntrəˈduːsɪz/ | vt. |重新介绍；重新引进；再次提出 | To introduce again; to bring back into use or existence.  |例句：The government is reintroducing the death penalty. 政府正在重新启用死刑。 |例句：The zoo is reintroducing the endangered species back into the wild. 动物园正在将濒危物种重新引入野外。|近义词：reinstate, restore, revive |反义词：remove, abolish, eliminate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company reintroduces its flagship product every year. = 公司每年都会重新推出其旗舰产品。
He reintroduces himself to the new team members. = 他向新的团队成员重新介绍自己。


一般过去时:
They reintroduced the old tradition last year. = 他们去年重新引进了古老的传统。
She reintroduced the bill to the legislature. = 她向立法机关再次提交了该法案。

过去完成时:
