earbud:/ˈɪrbʌd/| n. |耳塞，耳芽|A small device that fits inside the ear and is used to listen to audio, typically from a smartphone or other portable device.  Earbuds are usually connected to the audio source by a wire, although wireless earbuds are also increasingly common.|例句：I lost one of my earbuds. 我丢了一个耳塞。

例句：She prefers earbuds to over-ear headphones because they are more portable. 她更喜欢耳塞而不是头戴式耳机，因为耳塞更便携。


近义词：earphone, earpiece|反义词：headphone, headset
