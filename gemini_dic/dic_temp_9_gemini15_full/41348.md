corinthian:/kəˈrɪnθiən/| adj. |科林斯式的；豪华的；精致的|Of or relating to Corinth, a city in ancient Greece, known for its wealth and artistic achievements.  The term is often used to describe a style of architecture characterized by elaborate ornamentation and decorative detail.  It also carries a connotation of luxury and refinement.|例句：The building featured Corinthian columns, adding to its grandeur. 这座建筑物饰有科林斯式柱子，增添了它的宏伟气势。|例句：He led a Corinthian lifestyle, indulging in luxury and pleasure. 他过着奢华的生活，沉溺于享乐之中。|近义词：elegant, ornate, luxurious, lavish|反义词：plain, simple, austere
