olivine:/ˈɑːlɪviːn/| n. |橄榄石|A magnesium-iron silicate mineral, (Mg,Fe)2SiO4,  commonly found in igneous and metamorphic rocks. It is usually green to olive-green in color, hence the name.  Olivine is a major constituent of the Earth's mantle and is also found in some meteorites.|例句：The gemstone peridot is a variety of olivine.  橄榄石宝石是一种橄榄石的变种。|  例句：Olivine's high melting point makes it resistant to weathering. 橄榄石的高熔点使其耐风化。|近义词：peridot (when referring to the gemstone)|反义词：无|名词
