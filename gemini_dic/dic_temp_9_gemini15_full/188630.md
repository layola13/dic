ruminating:/ˈruːmɪneɪtɪŋ/| v. |沉思，反复思考 |To think deeply and at length; to ponder.  It often implies a repetitive or anxious thought process. |例句：She spent hours ruminating on the meaning of life. 她花了几个小时反复思考人生的意义。
例句：He was ruminating over his mistakes. 他一直在反复思考自己的错误。|近义词：ponder, contemplate, meditate, muse |反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am ruminating on the problem. = 我正在反复思考这个问题。
They are ruminating over their options. = 他们在反复考虑他们的选择。


一般过去时:
She ruminated on the events of the day. = 她反复思考当天发生的事情。
He ruminated for hours before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前沉思了几个小时。


现在完成时:
I have been ruminating on this for days. = 我已经反复思考这件事好几天了。
She has ruminated over the implications of her actions. = 她一直在反复思考自己行为的含义。


过去完成时:
He had ruminated on the matter for weeks before he spoke. = 他在说话之前已经考虑了这个问题好几个星期了。
They had ruminated for hours before reaching a consensus. = 他们在达成共识之前已经反复思考了几个小时。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would ruminate more thoroughly on the issue. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更彻底地思考这个问题。
I wish I hadn't ruminated so much on his criticisms. = 我真希望我没有对他的批评想太多。


被动语态:
The problem was ruminated upon by the committee. = 这个问题被委员会反复思考了。
The proposal is being ruminated over by the board. = 董事会正在反复考虑这个提案。


疑问句:

否定句:
I didn't ruminate on the details. = 我没有仔细考虑细节。
He didn't ruminate on the consequences of his actions. = 他没有考虑自己行为的后果。
