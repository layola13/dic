givebacks:/ˈɡɪvˌbæks/| n.|让步；回馈；返还；(尤指工资或福利的)让步|Something given back, especially concessions made by employers to employees, often involving pay or benefits.  It can also refer to the return of something previously taken or lost.|例句：The company offered significant givebacks to avoid a strike. 公司为了避免罢工做出了重大的让步。

例句：The environmental group celebrated the government's givebacks on pollution control.  环保组织庆祝政府在污染控制方面的让步。


近义词：concessions, compromises, returns, reimbursements|反义词：demands, increases, takebacks|名词
