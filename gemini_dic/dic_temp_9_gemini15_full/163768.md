predeliberation:/ˌpriːdɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən/| n. |预先考虑，预先商议|The act of considering or discussing something beforehand. It implies careful thought and planning before a decision is made or action is taken.  |例句：The predeliberation among the committee members ensured a smooth and efficient meeting.  委员会成员的预先商议确保了会议顺利有效地进行。

例句：After much predeliberation, the team decided on a new marketing strategy.经过大量的预先考虑，团队决定了一个新的营销策略。


近义词：premeditation, forethought, consideration, deliberation (but less emphasis on the "beforehand" aspect) |反义词：impulsiveness, spontaneity, rashness |词性:名词
