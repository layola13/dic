cultivar:/ˈkʌltɪvɑːr/| n.|栽培品种，人工培育品种|A cultivar is a plant variety that has been produced in cultivation by humans and is distinguished by specific characteristics that are maintained through propagation.  It's not a naturally occurring variant. |Example:  The 'Granny Smith' apple is a popular cultivar known for its tartness.  'Granny Smith'苹果是一个以其酸味而闻名的受欢迎的栽培品种。|近义词：variety, breed (in the context of plants)|反义词：wild type
