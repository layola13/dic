shakers:/ˈʃeɪkərz/| n.|摇动者；（尤指）盐瓶；震动器；（宗教）震颤教徒；|A shaker is a person or thing that shakes something.  It can refer to a person who practices shaking as a religious ritual, a container for salt or pepper, or a device that produces vibrations.|例句：She used a cocktail shaker to mix the drinks. 她用鸡尾酒调酒器调制饮料。


例句：The earthquake was a powerful shaker.  那场地震威力巨大。


近义词：vibrator, agitator,  (for religious sect)  Quakers


反义词：stabilizer,  fixer


词性:名词
