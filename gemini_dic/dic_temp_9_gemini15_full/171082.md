psoas:/ˈsoʊæs/| n. |腰大肌|The psoas major is a long fusiform muscle that originates from the transverse processes of the lumbar vertebrae and the bodies of the 12th thoracic and all lumbar vertebrae. It plays a crucial role in hip flexion, lumbar spine stabilization, and overall core strength.  It's often implicated in lower back pain. |例句：Tight psoas muscles can contribute to lower back pain.  紧张的腰大肌会导致下背痛。|例句：Regular stretching can help to release tension in the psoas. 定期拉伸有助于放松腰大肌。|近义词：iliopsoas (iliopsoas muscle is the combined psoas major and iliacus muscle)|反义词：N/A|名词
