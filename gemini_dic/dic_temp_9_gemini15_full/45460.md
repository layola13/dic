curative:/ˈkjʊərətɪv/| adj.|治疗的；治病的；有疗效的|Having the power to cure; remedial.  It refers to something that has the ability to heal or cure a disease or condition.  |例句：The doctor prescribed a curative treatment for his infection. 医生为他感染开了一种疗效的治疗方法。

例句：This new drug shows great curative potential for the disease. 这种新药对这种疾病显示出巨大的治疗潜力。


近义词：healing, remedial, therapeutic, restorative|反义词：ineffective, unhelpful, palliative (providing relief but not a cure)
