corollary:/ˈkɔːrəleri/| n.|推论，必然结果，系理|A corollary is a proposition that follows from (and is often considered self-evident when compared to) a previously proven proposition.  It's a consequence or result that naturally follows from something else. |Example:  The corollary of increased productivity is higher profits. 推论是生产力提高必然导致更高的利润。

Example:  A corollary to the theorem is that all squares are rectangles.  这个定理的推论是所有正方形都是长方形。|近义词：consequence, result, inference, deduction|反义词：antecedent, premise, cause
