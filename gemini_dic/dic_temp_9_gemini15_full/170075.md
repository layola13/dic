protester:/proˈtestər/| n. |抗议者，示威者|A person who participates in a protest or demonstration.  Someone who publicly expresses disapproval or opposition to something. |例句：The protesters marched through the streets, chanting slogans. 抗议者们游行穿过街道，高喊口号。

例句：Many protesters gathered outside the government building. 许多抗议者聚集在政府大楼外。


近义词：demonstrator, activist, dissident |反义词：supporter, conformist |词性：名词
