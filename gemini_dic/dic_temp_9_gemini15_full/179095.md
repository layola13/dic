redesignates:/ˌriːˈdɪzɪɡneɪts/| vt.|重新指定；重新分配|To assign or appoint to a new or different position, role, or function.|The company redesigned the roles of its employees after the merger.  公司在合并后重新分配了员工的职责。|The government redesignated the area as a national park. 政府将该地区重新指定为国家公园。|reassign, reassign, reallocate|—|


USAGE->

The committee redesigns the project plan every year. = 委员会每年都会重新设计项目计划。
He redesignates his priorities based on new information. = 他根据新的信息重新安排他的优先事项。

The manager redesigned the team structure last month. = 经理上个月重新设计了团队结构。
