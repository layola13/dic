pasturing: /ˈpæstʃərɪŋ/ | n. |放牧；牧场；牲畜放牧 | The act of grazing livestock on pasture.  It refers to both the action of allowing animals to feed on grass in a field and the land itself used for this purpose. |例句：The sheep were enjoying the lush pasturing.  绵羊正在享受着茂盛的牧场。

The farmer rotates his cattle through different pasturing areas. 这位农民轮换着让他的牛群在不同的牧场放牧。


近义词：grazing, herding (in the sense of managing livestock on pasture)


反义词：None directly opposite, but confinement (keeping animals in enclosed spaces) could be considered a contrasting concept.


词性:名词


词源:源自古法语 *pasturage，最终源自拉丁语 *pastus，意思是“食物”，与“pasture”（牧场）同源。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cattle are pasturing in the field. = 牛群正在田里吃草。
He pastures his sheep on the hillside. = 他让他的羊群在山坡上吃草。


一般过去时:
The farmers pastured their sheep in the valley last week. = 农民们上周把羊群放在山谷里吃草。
They pastured their horses near the river. = 他们把马群放在河边吃草。


现在完成时:
The sheep have been pasturing in this field all summer. = 这些羊整个夏天都在这个田里吃草。
We have pastured our cows in different fields to improve the grass. = 我们把奶牛放在不同的田里吃草，以改善草地质量。


过去完成时:
The cows had been pasturing in the meadow before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，奶牛一直在草地上吃草。
They had already pastured the sheep before the sun rose. = 太阳升起之前，他们已经把羊放牧了。


将来时:
We will pasture the goats on the mountain next week. = 我们下周将在山上放牧山羊。
The farmer will pasture his cattle in the new field. = 农民将在新的田地里放牧他的牛群。


虚拟语气:
If we had more land, we would pasture more animals. = 如果我们有更多的土地，我们会放牧更多的动物。
I wish we could pasture our sheep in that green valley. = 我希望我们能在那个绿色的山谷里放牧我们的羊。


被动语态:
The land is pastured by many sheep. = 这块土地被许多羊群放牧。
The fields are being pastured by the farmer's cattle. = 田地正在被农民的牛群放牧。


疑问句:
Where are the horses pasturing? = 马群在哪里吃草？
Are they still pasturing the cattle near the river? = 他们还在河边放牧牛群吗？


否定句:
The sheep weren't pasturing in the field yesterday. = 羊昨天不在田里吃草。
They don't pasture their animals near the highway. = 他们不在高速公路附近放牧他们的动物。
