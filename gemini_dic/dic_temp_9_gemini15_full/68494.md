famous:/ˈfeɪməs/ | adj. | 著名的，出名的 | Well-known and admired by many people.  |  The Mona Lisa is a famous painting.  蒙娜丽莎是一幅著名的画作。|  renowned, celebrated, illustrious, noted | obscure, unknown, anonymous | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is famous for his novels. = 他因其小说而闻名。
She is famous all over the world. = 她世界闻名。


一般过去时:
He was famous as a musician. = 他作为音乐家而闻名。
The band was famous in the 1980s. = 这个乐队在20世纪80年代很有名。


现在完成时:
She has become famous overnight. = 她一夜成名。
He has been famous for many years. = 他已经成名多年了。


过去完成时:
He had already become famous before he released his first album. = 在他发行第一张专辑之前就已经成名了。
The actress had been famous for her roles in Shakespearean plays. =这位女演员因在莎士比亚戏剧中的角色而闻名。


将来时:
He will be famous one day. = 他总有一天会成名。
The new technology will make him famous. = 这项新技术会让他成名。


虚拟语气:
If he were famous, he would have more opportunities. = 如果他很有名，他就会有更多机会。
I wish I were famous. = 我希望我能成名。


被动语态:
He was made famous by his latest book. = 他因其最新著作而声名鹊起。
She is widely known and famous for her kind heart. = 她以善良的心肠而广为人知。


疑问句:
Is he famous? = 他有名吗？
Is that artist famous? = 那位艺术家有名吗？


否定句:
He is not famous. = 他不出名。
She isn't famous yet. = 她还没出名。


PHRASE->
become famous = 成名
be famous for = 以……而闻名
a famous actor = 一位著名的演员
famous last words = 临终遗言
world-famous = 世界闻名的
