expunging: /ɪkˈspʌndʒɪŋ/ | v. | 删除；清除；涂抹 |  The act of removing or erasing something completely.  This often refers to removing information or records, especially from official documents. |

例句：

The judge ordered the expungement of his criminal record. = 法官下令删除他的犯罪记录。

They are working to expunge all traces of the virus from the system. = 他们正在努力从系统中清除所有病毒痕迹。


近义词：erase, delete, remove, obliterate, cancel

反义词：add, insert, include, retain

词性：动词
