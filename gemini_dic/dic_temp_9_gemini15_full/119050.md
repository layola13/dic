metiers:/ˈmeɪtijeɪz/ | n. |职业；技艺；行业 |A métier is a craft, trade, or profession, especially one that requires skill and training.  It often implies a high level of expertise and artistry. |The chef demonstrated his mastery of his métier.  这位厨师展示了他对烹饪技艺的掌握。| professions, crafts, trades | unskilled labor, amateur work |名词
