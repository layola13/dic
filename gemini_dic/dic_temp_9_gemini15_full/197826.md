shining:/ˈʃaɪnɪŋ/| adj.|闪光的；闪亮的；杰出的|Shining describes something that emits or reflects light brightly.  It can also refer to something that is excellent or outstanding.|例句：The sun was shining brightly.  太阳明亮地照耀着。

The shining star guided the lost sailors. 闪耀的星辰指引着迷失的水手。|近义词：bright, lustrous, radiant, brilliant, excellent|反义词：dull, dim, lackluster, mediocre
