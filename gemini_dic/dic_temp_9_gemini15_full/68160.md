failure:/ˈfeɪljər/| n.|失败；故障；失败者 |The inability to accomplish an objective; lack of success.  It can refer to a person, a thing, or an event. |例句：His business venture ended in failure. 他的商业冒险以失败告终。

例句：The engine failure caused the plane to crash. 发动机故障导致飞机坠毁。

近义词：defeat, setback, flop, breakdown, fiasco |反义词：success, triumph, achievement, accomplishment |词性:名词

Failure is a part of life. = 失败是人生的一部分。
His attempts at cooking always end in failure. = 他做饭的尝试总是以失败告终。

一般过去时:

The experiment was a failure. = 实验失败了。

现在完成时:
He has experienced many failures in his career. = 他在事业上经历了许多失败。
She has had a series of failures recently. = 她最近经历了一系列失败。

过去完成时:

I realized that my previous attempt had been a failure. = 我意识到我之前的尝试失败了。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
