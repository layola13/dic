monothalaman:/ˌmɒnəθæləˈmæn/| n. |单室的；单心室的 |Relating to or having only one chamber or locule, especially in a plant ovary or a heart.  The term is primarily used in botany and anatomy. |例句：The botanist described the flower as having a monothalaman ovary. 植物学家将这种花描述为具有单室子房。

The monothalaman heart structure is rare in mammals. 单心室心脏结构在哺乳动物中很少见。|近义词：unicameral, unilocular |反义词：polythalaman, multilocular
