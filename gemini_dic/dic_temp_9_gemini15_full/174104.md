quatuor:/kwɑːˈtɔːr/|/ˈkwɑːtɔːr/| n. |四重奏；四重奏乐队；四 |A quatuor is a musical composition for four instruments or voices, typically a string quartet. It can also refer to a group of four musicians performing such a piece.  |Example: The string quatuor played a beautiful piece by Beethoven.  这支弦乐四重奏演奏了一首贝多芬的优美乐曲。|近义词：quartet |反义词：无
