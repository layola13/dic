lately:/ˈleɪtli/ | adv. | 最近；近来 | Recently, in the recent past.  It typically refers to a period of time that is not precisely defined but is understood to be relatively short. | Example: I haven't been feeling well lately.  最近我感觉不太舒服。  I've been working late lately. 最近我一直在加班。| 近义词：recently, recently, of late | 反义词：long ago, formerly

She often calls her mother lately. 她最近经常打电话给她的母亲。
