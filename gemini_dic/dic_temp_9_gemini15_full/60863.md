elegant:/ˈɛlɪɡənt/ | adj. |优雅的，精致的，简洁的 |  Elegant describes something or someone that is pleasingly graceful and stylish in appearance or manner. It suggests a refined and sophisticated quality, often characterized by simplicity and good taste.  | 例句：She wore an elegant gown to the ball. 她穿着一件优雅的礼服去参加舞会。

The design of the building is both elegant and functional. 这座建筑的设计既优雅又实用。


近义词：graceful, refined, stylish, sophisticated, polished, exquisite

反义词：clumsy, awkward, crude, unrefined, gaudy


词源：源自于古法语 *elegant，最终源自于拉丁语 *ēlegāns，意为“选择性的，挑选的”。  这与优雅的含义联系起来，是因为优雅的东西通常是经过精心挑选和设计的。
