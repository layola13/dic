poloists:/ˈpoʊloʊɪsts/| n. |马球运动员|A poloist is a person who plays polo.  They are skilled equestrian athletes who compete in the sport of polo, riding horses and using mallets to hit a ball into a goal.  Poloists require significant training and expertise in horsemanship and strategic gameplay.|The poloists displayed impressive teamwork during the final match.|equestrians, riders|non-poloists
