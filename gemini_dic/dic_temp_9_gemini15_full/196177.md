sepulchering: /ˈsɛpəlˌtʃərɪŋ/ | v. | 埋葬；放入坟墓 | The act of placing something, typically a body, in a sepulcher or tomb.  It implies a solemn and often ritualistic burial. |例句：They were sepulchering the ancient artifacts in a specially designed vault. = 他们正在将古代文物埋葬在一个专门设计的地下室里。


The family sepulchered their patriarch with great ceremony. = 这个家庭隆重地安葬了他们的族长。

近义词：entombing, interring, burying, inurning | 反义词：exhuming, unearthing


词源:  The word "sepulchering" comes from the noun "sepulcher," which ultimately derives from the Latin word "sepulcrum," meaning "tomb" or "grave."  The "-ing" suffix transforms the noun into a present participle, functioning as a verb.
