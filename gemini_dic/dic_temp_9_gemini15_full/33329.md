chuffy:/ˈtʃʌfi/| adj.|丰满的；圆胖的；粗壮的；|  Describes a person or animal that is plump or chubby, often in a pleasing way.  It suggests a soft, rounded fullness. |The baby is so chubby and cute.|近义词：plump, chubby, stout, stocky|反义词：thin, skinny, slender
