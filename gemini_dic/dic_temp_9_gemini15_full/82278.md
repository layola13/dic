groundsheets:/ˈɡraʊndʃiːts/| n. |地席，防潮垫|A groundsheet is a large sheet of waterproof or water-resistant material used to protect something (typically a tent or sleeping bag) from the ground.  It provides insulation, protection from moisture, and a clean, dry surface to sleep or sit on. |Example: We laid down a groundsheet before pitching our tent to keep it dry. 我们在搭帐篷之前铺了一块地席以保持帐篷干燥。

Example: The groundsheet prevented the sleeping bag from getting damp. 地席防止了睡袋受潮。|近义词：tarp, waterproof sheet, ground cloth|反义词：None specifically
