darkroom:/ˈdɑːrkruːm/|/ˈdɑːkrʊm/| n. |暗房 |A darkroom is a room that is completely dark or has very little light, used for developing and printing photographs.  |Example:  He spent hours in the darkroom, carefully developing his film. 他在暗房里花了数小时仔细冲洗胶卷。|近义词：photography lab, developing room |反义词：bright room, light room
