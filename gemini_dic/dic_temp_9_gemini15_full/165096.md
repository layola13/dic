preimporting:/ˌpriːɪmˈpɔːrtɪŋ/| v.|预先进口，提前进口 |The act of importing something before it is officially needed or available.  It often involves strategic planning to secure goods or resources ahead of time to avoid potential shortages or price increases. |例句：The company engaged in preimporting essential components to ensure uninterrupted production. 公司提前进口必要的零部件以确保生产不中断。 The government initiated a preimporting program for medical supplies in anticipation of a pandemic. 政府启动了医疗用品提前进口计划，以应对即将到来的疫情。|近义词：advance importing, prior importing |反义词：delayed importing, late importing |动词
