skill:/skɪl/ | n. |技能，技巧，熟练 |the ability to do something well; expertise |例句：He possesses excellent communication skills. 他拥有极好的沟通技巧。|例句：It takes years of practice to develop such a high level of skill.  掌握如此高超的技艺需要多年的练习。|近义词：ability, proficiency, expertise, talent |反义词：inability, incompetence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He possesses excellent cooking skills. = 他拥有精湛的烹饪技巧。
She demonstrates great leadership skills. = 她展现出卓越的领导才能。

一般过去时:
He showed remarkable problem-solving skills. = 他展现出非凡的解决问题的能力。
She lacked the necessary technical skills. = 她缺乏必要的技术技能。

现在完成时:
I have developed my writing skills significantly. = 我已经显著提高了我的写作能力。
They have mastered several musical skills. = 他们已经掌握了几种乐器技能。

过去完成时:
She had honed her artistic skills before the exhibition. = 在展览之前，她已经磨练了自己的艺术技巧。
He had acquired considerable carpentry skills by then. = 到那时，他已经获得了相当的木工技能。

将来时:
I will improve my programming skills next year. = 我明年将提高我的编程技能。
She will need strong communication skills for this job. = 这份工作需要她具备强大的沟通能力。

虚拟语气:
If I had better painting skills, I would enter the competition. = 如果我的绘画技巧更好，我会参加比赛。
I wish I had more technical skills. = 我希望我有更多技术技能。

被动语态:
His skills were highly praised by the judges. = 他的技能受到了评委的高度赞扬。
These skills are often required in this field. = 这些技能在这个领域经常被要求。

疑问句:
What skills are required for this position? = 这个职位需要什么技能？
What skills do you have that are relevant to this job? = 你有什么技能与这份工作相关？

否定句:
He lacks the essential skills for this task. = 他缺乏完成这项任务的必要技能。
She doesn't possess the necessary interpersonal skills. = 她不具备必要的人际交往能力。
