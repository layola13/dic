reicing: /ˌriˈaɪsɪŋ/ | n. | 再结冰；重新结冰 | The process of ice forming again on a surface after it has been previously melted or removed.  This often refers to the refreezing of precipitation on aircraft surfaces or roads. | Example:  The reicing of the runway caused significant delays.  = 跑道的再次结冰造成了严重的延误。 The pilot reported severe reicing conditions. = 飞行员报告了严重的再次结冰情况。|近义词：refreezing, icing (in the context of refreezing)|反义词：deicing, thawing|名词
