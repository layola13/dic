pin-money:/ˈpɪnˌmʌni/| n. |零花钱，少量零用钱 |A small amount of money given to a woman, typically a wife, for personal expenses.  It traditionally refers to money for small, non-essential purchases. |She used her pin-money to buy a new hat. 她用她的零花钱买了一顶新帽子。|pocket money, spending money |  (无明确反义词，可以考虑large sums of money)|名词
