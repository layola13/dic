osteoporosis:/ˌɒstiːoʊpəˈroʊsɪs/ | n. |骨质疏松症 |A condition in which bones become fragile and prone to fractures due to loss of bone mineral density.  It is characterized by decreased bone mass and microarchitectural deterioration of bone tissue, leading to increased bone fragility and a consequent increase in fracture risk.|例句：Her grandmother suffers from osteoporosis and has to be careful not to fall.  她祖母患有骨质疏松症，必须小心别摔倒。|近义词：osteopenia (a less severe form)|反义词：bone density, strong bones |名词
