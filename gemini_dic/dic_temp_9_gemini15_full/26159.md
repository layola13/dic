busywork:/ˈbɪziwɜːrk/| n. |无谓的工作；繁琐的琐事 | Unimportant or unnecessary work that fills time. It often involves tasks that are tedious, repetitive, and do not contribute significantly to a larger goal.  | Example:  The intern spent most of the day doing busywork, like filing papers and making copies. 实习生大部分时间都在做无谓的工作，例如整理文件和复印文件。

|近义词：chores,  menial tasks,  routine tasks,  paperwork,  red tape |反义词：productive work, meaningful work,  challenging work |词性:名词
