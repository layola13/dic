fluctuating:/ˈflʌktʃueɪtɪŋ/| adj.| 波动的；起伏不定的 | fluctuating refers to something that is constantly changing, rising and falling irregularly.  It implies a lack of stability or consistency. | Example: The price of oil has been fluctuating wildly in recent weeks. 近期油价波动剧烈。The fluctuating economy made it difficult to plan for the future. 波动的经济使得未来规划变得困难。 |近义词：wavering, varying, oscillating, shifting |反义词：stable, constant, steady, unchanging


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The stock market is fluctuating today. = 股市今天波动。
The temperature is fluctuating between 20 and 25 degrees. = 温度在20到25度之间波动。


一般过去时:
The exchange rate fluctuated dramatically last year. = 汇率去年剧烈波动。
His mood fluctuated throughout the day. = 他一整天情绪波动。


现在完成时:
The value of the property has fluctuated significantly since we bought it. = 自从我们买了这处房产以来，它的价值已大幅波动。
The company's profits have fluctuated over the past five years. = 公司的利润在过去五年里一直在波动。


过去完成时:
The stock price had fluctuated considerably before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，股价已大幅波动。
The river level had fluctuated significantly before the flood. = 洪水来临之前，河水水位已大幅波动。


将来时:
The price of gold will likely fluctuate in the coming months. = 黄金价格在未来几个月可能会波动。
The weather is expected to fluctuate throughout the week. = 本周天气预计将会波动。


虚拟语气:
If the exchange rate hadn't fluctuated so much, we would have made more money. = 如果汇率没有波动这么大，我们会赚更多钱。
If the temperature fluctuated less, it would be easier to plan outdoor activities. = 如果温度波动较小，计划户外活动会更容易。


被动语态:
The data was found to be fluctuating. = 发现数据在波动。
The results of the experiment were found to be fluctuating significantly. = 实验结果发现波动很大。


疑问句:
Is the water level fluctuating? = 水位在波动吗？
Are the prices fluctuating significantly? = 价格波动剧烈吗？


否定句:
The temperature isn't fluctuating much today. = 今天的温度变化不大。
The data doesn't fluctuate significantly. = 数据没有大幅波动。
