funnel:/ˈfʌnəl/ | n. v. |漏斗；引导；集中 | A cone-shaped utensil with a tube at the apex for conducting liquid or other substances into a container with a small opening.  Also, the act of guiding or channeling something into a specific direction or outcome. |例句：She poured the batter into a funnel before filling the cupcake tins. 她把面糊倒进漏斗里，然后装满纸杯蛋糕模具。

The company funneled millions of dollars into the project. 这家公司向该项目注入了数百万美元。|近义词：conduit, chute, channel, guide |反义词：disperse, scatter
