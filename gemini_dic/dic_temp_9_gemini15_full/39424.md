connect:/kəˈnɛkt/ |/kəˈnɛkt/| vt. vi. n.|连接，联系；结合；沟通|To join or link together; to establish a relationship or connection.|例句：Please connect the two wires. 请连接这两根电线。
例句：The two cities are connected by a railway. 这两座城市由一条铁路连接。|近义词：link, join, unite, combine|反义词：disconnect, separate, divide|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He connects the dots to form a picture. 他连接这些点来形成一幅图画。
They connect regularly through video calls. 他们定期通过视频电话联系。


一般过去时:
She connected the printer to her computer. 她把打印机连接到她的电脑上。
We connected over our shared love of music. 我们因为共同热爱音乐而建立了联系。


现在完成时:
I have connected with many people online. 我已经通过网络与许多人建立了联系。
They have connected the new building to the power grid. 他们已经将新建筑连接到电网。


过去完成时:
He had connected the wires before the power was turned on.  他在电源接通之前已经连接好电线了。
She had connected with her long-lost cousin before the reunion.  她在重聚之前已经联系上了她失散多年的表兄妹。


将来时:
I will connect the speakers to the amplifier tomorrow. 我明天会把音箱连接到放大器上。
We will connect with our clients through email. 我们将通过电子邮件与客户联系。


虚拟语气:
If I had connected the wires correctly, the machine would have worked. 如果我正确连接了电线，机器就会工作了。
I wish I had connected with him sooner. 我希望我早点和他联系。


被动语态:
The two buildings are connected by a bridge. 这两座建筑由一座桥连接。
The internet was connected yesterday.  互联网昨天连接上了。


疑问句:
How do you connect to the Wi-Fi? 你如何连接到Wi-Fi？
Does this cable connect to the TV? 这根电缆连接到电视机吗？


否定句:
He couldn't connect to the server. 他无法连接到服务器。
The phone wouldn't connect. 电话无法连接。
