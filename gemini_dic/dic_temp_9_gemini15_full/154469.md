perogue:/pəˈroʊɡ/| n.|独木舟；小船 | A small, usually flat-bottomed boat, often made from a single log or dugout.  |例句：He paddled his perogue across the still lake. 他划着他的独木舟穿过平静的湖泊。
例句：The old perogue was nearly submerged by the rising tide. 那艘旧独木舟几乎被涨潮淹没了。|近义词：canoe, pirogue, dugout |反义词：large ship, ocean liner
