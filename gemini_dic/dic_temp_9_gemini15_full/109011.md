leftism:/ˈlɛftɪzəm/| n. |左翼主义，左倾思想|  A political ideology or position characterized by support for social equality and egalitarianism, often in opposition to traditional hierarchies and capitalist systems.  Leftism encompasses a wide range of views, from moderate social democracy to more radical revolutionary socialism and communism. | Example:  His strong belief in social justice and economic redistribution marked him as a staunch leftist. 他对社会正义和经济再分配的坚定信念将他标记为坚定的左翼人士。|近义词：radicalism, socialism, communism, progressivism|反义词：rightism, conservatism, reactionarism|名词
