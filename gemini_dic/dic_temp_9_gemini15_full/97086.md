infertileness:/ˌɪnfɜːrˈtɪlətnəs/ | n. |不育；不肥沃|The inability to conceive or produce offspring.  It can refer to both women and men.  Infertileness can be caused by various factors, including hormonal imbalances, genetic issues, and lifestyle choices. |例句：The couple sought medical help for their infertileness. 这对夫妇因不育寻求医疗帮助。

例句：Years of intensive farming had led to the infertileness of the soil. 多年的集约化耕作导致土壤贫瘠。


近义词：sterility, barrenness, infecundity

反义词：fertility, fecundity

词性：名词
