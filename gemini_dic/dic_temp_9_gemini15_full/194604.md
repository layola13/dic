self-suspended: /ˌsɛlf səˈspɛndɪd/ | adj. | 自行暂停的，自我中止的 |  Describes something that has stopped or ceased functioning on its own accord, without external intervention.  This often implies a temporary state, with the possibility of resuming later. | Example: The self-suspended animation program will resume after the power outage is resolved.  自行暂停的动画程序将在停电解决后恢复。


近义词：automatically paused, temporarily halted, self-terminating

反义词：continuously running, permanently stopped


词性: 形容词
