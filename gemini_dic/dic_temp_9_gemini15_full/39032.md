conflict:/ˈkɒnflɪkt/ /ˈkɑːnflɪkt/| n., v.|冲突，矛盾；抵触；争执 | A serious disagreement or argument, typically a protracted one.  A state of opposition between incompatible ideas or actions. |例句：The conflict between the two countries lasted for many years. 这两个国家之间的冲突持续了许多年。

The family is in conflict over how to spend the inheritance.  这个家庭因为如何分配遗产而发生冲突。


近义词：dispute, clash, disagreement, contention, antagonism |反义词：agreement, harmony, accord, concord, peace


USAGE->

名词用法:

一般现在时:
The conflict between the two nations continues. = 两国之间的冲突仍在继续。
There is a conflict of interest in this situation. = 这种情况存在利益冲突。


一般过去时:
A major conflict erupted in the region last year. = 去年该地区爆发了一场重大冲突。
The conflict ended after years of fighting. = 经过多年的战斗，冲突结束了。


现在完成时:
The warring factions have finally resolved their conflict. = 交战各派终于解决了他们的冲突。
Several conflicts have occurred in the region this decade. = 本十年来，该地区发生了几起冲突。


过去完成时:
The peace treaty was signed after the conflict had ended. = 冲突结束后签署了和平条约。
By the time the ceasefire was announced, the conflict had claimed thousands of lives. = 在停火令宣布时，冲突已经夺去了数千人的生命。


将来时:
We fear that a new conflict will erupt soon. = 我们担心很快就会爆发新的冲突。
The negotiations aim to prevent future conflicts. = 谈判旨在防止未来的冲突。


虚拟语气:
If there were no conflict, the world would be a much better place. = 如果没有冲突，世界将会好得多。
I wish there were a peaceful solution to the conflict. = 我希望冲突有一个和平的解决方案。


被动语态:
The conflict was resolved through diplomacy. = 冲突通过外交手段得到了解决。

疑问句:
Is there a conflict of interest here? = 这儿有利益冲突吗？

否定句:

动词用法:

一般现在时:
Their ideas conflict with each other. = 他们的想法相互冲突。
His account of events conflicts with hers. = 他对事件的描述与她的相冲突。


一般过去时:
His statement conflicted with the evidence. = 他的陈述与证据相矛盾。
Their plans conflicted with mine. = 他们的计划与我的计划相冲突。


现在完成时:
The witness's testimony has conflicted with previous statements. = 目击证人的证词与之前的陈述相矛盾。
My schedule has conflicted with his for the past month. = 我的日程表上个月以来一直与他的冲突。


过去完成时:
It turned out that their schedules had conflicted. = 结果他们的时间安排发生了冲突。
The evidence had conflicted with the initial assumptions. = 证据与最初的假设相矛盾。


将来时:
The new policy will conflict with existing regulations. = 新政策将与现有规章相冲突。
I suspect their plans will conflict with ours. = 我怀疑他们的计划将与我们的计划冲突。


虚拟语气:
If the two plans didn't conflict, we could proceed more efficiently. = 如果这两个计划不冲突，我们可以更有效地进行。
I wish their schedules didn't conflict so often. = 我希望他们的时间安排不要经常冲突。


被动语态:
His actions were interpreted as conflicting with company policy. = 他的行为被解释为与公司政策相冲突。
The project timeline is likely to be conflicted by unforeseen circumstances. = 项目时间表可能会因不可预见的情况而冲突。


疑问句:
Does this decision conflict with our overall strategy? = 这个决定与我们的整体战略相冲突吗？
Does the new software conflict with the old version? = 新软件与旧版本冲突吗？


否定句:
Their stories didn't conflict in any significant way. = 他们的故事并没有发生任何重大冲突。
The two versions of the story do not conflict. = 这两个版本的故事情节并不冲突。
