regale:/rɪˈɡeɪl/| vt.|款待；使高兴；讲述；|to entertain someone with stories or anecdotes|He regaled us with stories of his adventures in the Amazon. 他给我们讲述了他亚马逊探险的故事。|delight, entertain, amuse|bore, disappoint|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He regales his friends with funny stories every week. = 他每周都用有趣的故事款待他的朋友们。
She regales the audience with tales of her travels. = 她用旅行的故事来款待观众。


一般过去时:
The old sailor regaled us with tales of the sea. = 这位老水手给我们讲述了大海的故事。
He regaled the children with exciting adventures. = 他给孩子们讲述了激动人心的冒险故事。


现在完成时:
She has regaled her colleagues with anecdotes from her recent trip. = 她已经用她最近旅行的趣闻款待了她的同事们。
He has regaled the listeners with fascinating accounts of his life. = 他已经用他生活中迷人的故事款待了听众们。


过去完成时:
They had regaled themselves with stories before falling asleep. = 他们睡前已经用故事来取乐自己了。
She had regaled the guests with humorous tales before dinner. = 晚饭前她已经用幽默的故事款待了宾客们。

虚拟语气:
If I had the time, I would regale you with my travel stories. = 如果我有时间，我会用我的旅行故事来款待你。
I wish I could regale you with a humorous anecdote right now. = 我真希望现在能用一个幽默的轶事来款待你。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
