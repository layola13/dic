journeyed:/ˈdʒɜːrnid/ | v. | 旅行；行程；经历 | To travel from one place to another, often over a long distance.  It can also imply a metaphorical journey, such as a journey through life or a difficult experience. |例句：They journeyed across the vast desert. = 他们横穿了广阔的沙漠。

They journeyed through many hardships to achieve their goals. = 他们经历了许多磨难才实现了自己的目标。|近义词：traveled, voyaged, trekked |反义词：remained, stayed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He journeys to work every day. = 他每天都去上班。
She journeys through life with grace. = 她优雅地走过人生。

一般过去时:
We journeyed to the mountains last summer. = 我们去年夏天去了山里。
He journeyed far and wide in search of adventure. = 他为了寻找冒险而远走高飞。

现在完成时:
They have journeyed extensively throughout Europe. = 他们已经广泛游历了欧洲。
She has journeyed through many trials and tribulations. = 她经历了许多艰难困苦。

过去完成时:
Before settling down, he had journeyed to many countries. = 在定居之前，他去过许多国家。
They had journeyed for days before reaching the oasis. = 他们在到达绿洲之前已经走了好几天了。

将来时:
We will journey to the coast next week. = 我们下周要去海边。
She will journey to the ends of the earth to find him. = 她将走遍天涯海角去寻找他。

虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would journey to Japan. = 如果我有钱，我会去日本旅行。
I wish I could journey back in time. = 我真希望可以回到过去。

被动语态:
The goods were journeyed across the ocean by ship. = 货物由船只运送过海。 (Note:  The passive voice is less common with "journeyed".)

疑问句:
Have you ever journeyed abroad? = 你曾经出国旅行过吗？
Where did they journey to? = 他们去哪里旅行了？

否定句:
He did not journey alone. = 他没有独自旅行。
They haven't journeyed far enough yet. = 他们还没走够远。


PHRASE->
a long journey = 一段漫长的旅程
a spiritual journey = 一段心灵之旅
a difficult journey = 一段艰难的旅程
a journey of discovery = 一次发现之旅
a life journey = 一生的旅程
