collies:/ˈkɒliz/| n. |柯利牧羊犬|A collie is a type of herding dog, known for its intelligence, loyalty, and herding abilities.  Collies are often used to herd sheep and other livestock. They come in various colors and coat types.|Example: The farmer used his collies to gather the sheep.|近义词：sheepdog, herding dog|反义词:无
