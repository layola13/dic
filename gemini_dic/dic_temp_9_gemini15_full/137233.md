nonsurvivals:/nɒn.səˈvaɪvəlz/ | n. |未幸存者；未生存者；淘汰者| Those who did not survive a disaster or competition.  It refers to individuals, groups, or entities that failed to persist or endure.  The term often implies a selection process where only some succeed and others fail.|


例句：

The nonsurvivals of the harsh winter were numerous.  =  严冬的未幸存者数量众多。

The nonsurvivals of the business competition were forced to close down. =  商业竞争中的淘汰者被迫关闭。


近义词：casualties, victims, failures, losses


反义词：survivors, successes


词性：名词
