live up to:/ˌlɪv ˈʌp tuː/| phrasal verb|符合；不辜负；达到…的标准|To meet or achieve expectations; to fulfill a promise or expectation.  |例句：He lived up to his reputation as a brilliant musician. 他不负盛名，是一位杰出的音乐家。

例句：The new product lived up to all the hype.  新产品不负众望。


近义词：fulfill, satisfy, meet, achieve, attain

反义词：fail, disappoint, let down


词性：phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They live up to their promises. = 他们信守承诺。
The company lives up to its high standards. = 公司达到其高标准。


一般过去时:
The team lived up to expectations. = 球队不负众望。
He lived up to his potential. = 他发挥了自己的潜力。


现在完成时:
She has lived up to her parents' hopes. = 她不负父母的期望。
We have lived up to the challenge. = 我们已经克服了挑战。


过去完成时:
They had lived up to the agreement. = 他们已经履行了协议。
She had lived up to her reputation for honesty. = 她一直保持着诚实的声誉。


将来时:
He will live up to the challenge. = 他将迎接挑战。
The project will live up to our expectations. = 项目将达到我们的预期。


虚拟语气:
If only he lived up to his potential! = 但愿他能发挥自己的潜力！
I wish the film lived up to the hype. = 我希望这部电影不负众望。


被动语态:  (This phrasal verb is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Does he live up to his responsibilities? = 他是否尽到了自己的责任？
Will the product live up to the advertising claims? = 产品是否会达到广告宣传的承诺？


否定句:
The film didn't live up to expectations. = 这部电影没有达到预期。
He failed to live up to his promise. = 他没有兑现承诺。


PHRASE->
live up to expectations = 达标
live up to the hype = 不负众望
live up to one's name = 名副其实
live up to the occasion = 应对自如
live up to one's responsibilities = 尽责
