mittens:/ˈmɪtənz/| n. |手套（指无指分开的套袖式手套）|A pair of mittens are warm hand coverings without separate finger sections. They are typically made from wool, fleece, or other warm materials.|例句：My grandma knitted me a pair of warm mittens for winter.  奶奶为我织了一双暖和的冬天手套。|例句：He pulled on his mittens before going outside in the snow. 他在雪地里出去之前戴上手套。|近义词：gloves (but with a key distinction: mittens lack separate fingers)|反义词：gloves (with separate fingers)
