pastoral:/ˈpɑːstərəl/| adj. n.|田园的；牧歌的；乡村的| relating to shepherds or rural life; idyllically rustic;  characterized by or suggesting the simplicity and tranquility of rural life. |例句：The painting depicts a pastoral scene of shepherds tending their flocks. 这幅画描绘了牧羊人照料羊群的田园景色。

例句：She longed for a pastoral life, far away from the city's noise and bustle. 她渴望过一种远离城市喧嚣的田园生活。


近义词：rural, rustic, idyllic, bucolic|反义词：urban, metropolitan, industrial


词源:  From Late Latin *pastoralis, from Latin pastor ("shepherd").  The word's development reflects the strong association between shepherds and the idealized image of rural life.
