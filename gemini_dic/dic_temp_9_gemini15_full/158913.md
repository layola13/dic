plunkers:/ˈplʌŋkərz/| n. |笨蛋，傻瓜，讨厌鬼|A plunker is a slang term for a clumsy or foolish person.  It can also be used to describe someone annoying or unpleasant.  The origin is uncertain, but likely relates to the sound of something heavy and clumsy being dropped ("plunk").|例句：He's such a plunker; he tripped over his own feet again. 他真是个笨蛋，他又绊倒了自己的脚。

He's a real plunker for always forgetting things. 他真是个讨厌鬼，总是忘记事情。|近义词：klutz, oaf, goofball, dope |反义词：genius, expert, skilled person
