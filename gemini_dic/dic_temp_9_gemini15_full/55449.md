distinguishable: /dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbl/ | adj. | 可区分的，可辨别的 |  Able to be recognized or identified as different or distinct.  | 例句：The twins were barely distinguishable. 这对双胞胎几乎难以区分。


例句：The two fabrics are easily distinguishable by their texture. 这两种织物很容易通过质地来区分。


近义词：perceptible, discernible, identifiable, differentiable


反义词：indistinguishable, identical, undifferentiated


词性：形容词
