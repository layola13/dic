healthful:/ˈhɛlθfəl/ | adj. | 有益健康的，健康的 | Promoting health; beneficial to the body or mind.  | Example:  A healthful diet is essential for good health.  健康的饮食对健康至关重要。

A healthful lifestyle includes regular exercise and a balanced diet. 健康的生活方式包括规律的运动和均衡的饮食。|近义词：healthy, wholesome, salutary |反义词：unhealthy, harmful, deleterious | 词性：形容词
