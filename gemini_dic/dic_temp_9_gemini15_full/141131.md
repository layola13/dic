onwardly:/ˈɒnwədli/| adv.|继续地，向前地|Moving or directed toward a point further ahead in space or time.  It implies continuous and progressive movement.|例句：The army marched onwardly towards the enemy.  军队继续向敌人挺进。

例句：He continued onwardly with his work, despite the setbacks. 他不顾挫折，继续向前工作。


近义词：forward, ahead, progressively, continuously|反义词：backward, reversely, regressively


词性：副词
