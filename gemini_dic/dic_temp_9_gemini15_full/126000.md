muslims:/ˈmʌzlɪmz/| n.|穆斯林；伊斯兰教徒|Muslims are followers of Islam, a monotheistic religion centered on the belief in one God, Allah, and the teachings of the Prophet Muhammad as revealed in the Quran.  They adhere to the Five Pillars of Islam: the declaration of faith (Shahada), prayer (Salat), charity (Zakat), fasting during Ramadan (Sawm), and pilgrimage to Mecca (Hajj).|例句：Many Muslims observe Ramadan by fasting from dawn till dusk. 许多穆斯林通过从黎明到黄昏禁食来遵守斋月。

例句：The mosque is a place of worship for Muslims. 清真寺是穆斯林的礼拜场所。
