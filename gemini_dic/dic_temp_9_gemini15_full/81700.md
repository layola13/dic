greenhorn:/ˈɡriːnhɔːrn/| n. |菜鸟；新手；乳臭未干的小子 |A greenhorn is a person who is new to a job or situation and therefore lacks experience.  They are often naive and easily fooled. |例句：He's a greenhorn at this job, so be patient with him. 他是这份工作的新手，所以对他耐心点。

例句：Don't trust him; he's a greenhorn and easily manipulated. 不要相信他；他是个新手，很容易被操纵。


近义词：novice, beginner, rookie, tyro |反义词：veteran, expert, professional |词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a greenhorn in the stock market. = 他是股市新手。
She is a greenhorn at programming. = 她是编程新手。


一般过去时:
He was a greenhorn when he started his first job. = 他开始第一份工作时还是个新手。
She was a greenhorn at driving. = 她刚开始开车时是个新手。


现在完成时:
He has been a greenhorn in this field for only a few months. = 他在这个领域才做了几个月的新手。
She has become less of a greenhorn since she started the training. = 自从她开始培训以来，她已经不再那么新手了。


过去完成时:
He had been a greenhorn for years before he finally mastered the skill. = 在他最终掌握这项技能之前，他已经做了多年的新手。
She had been a greenhorn at public speaking before she gained confidence. = 在她获得自信之前，她一直害怕公开演讲。


将来时:
He will be a greenhorn in his new role for a while. = 在他的新角色中，他将有一段时间是新手。
She will likely be a greenhorn at first, but she'll learn quickly. = 她一开始可能会很菜，但她会学得很快。


虚拟语气:
If he were not a greenhorn, he would have handled the situation better. = 如果他不是新手，他会更好地处理这种情况。
If she hadn't been such a greenhorn, she wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果她不是那么新手，她就不会犯那个错误了。


被动语态:
He was considered a greenhorn by his colleagues. = 他被同事们认为是新手。
She was treated like a greenhorn by her senior colleagues. = 她被资深同事像新手一样对待。


疑问句:
Is he a greenhorn in this industry? = 他在这个行业是新手吗？
Are you a greenhorn at this game? = 你玩这个游戏是新手吗？


否定句:
He is not a greenhorn anymore; he's become quite experienced. = 他不再是新手了；他已经变得很有经验了。
She is not a greenhorn in this business; she's been working here for years. = 她在这个行业不是新手；她在这里工作多年了。
