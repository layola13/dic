manipulating: /məˈnɪpjuleɪtɪŋ/ | v. |操纵；控制；操作；篡改 |

英文解析：To manipulate something means to control or influence it in a skillful way, often in a way that is unfair or dishonest.  It can also refer to the skillful handling or use of something.

例句：

The magician skillfully manipulated the cards.  魔术师熟练地操控着扑克牌。

He was accused of manipulating the stock market for personal gain. 他被指控操纵股市以谋取私利。


近义词：control, influence, manage, operate, exploit, handle

反义词：leave alone, ignore, neglect


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He manipulates the data to support his claims. 他操纵数据以支持他的说法。
She manipulates the puppets with great skill. 她熟练地操纵木偶。

一般过去时:
They manipulated the evidence to convict the innocent man. 他们篡改证据以定无辜者的罪。
The artist manipulated the clay to create a beautiful sculpture. 艺术家操纵粘土创造了一个美丽的雕塑。

现在完成时:
He has manipulated the system for years to his advantage. 他多年来一直操纵系统以利于自己。
She has manipulated her way to the top of the company. 她通过手段爬到了公司的顶端。

过去完成时:
They had manipulated the accounts before the audit. 他们在审计之前就已经操纵了账目。
The con artist had manipulated the victim into giving him all his money. 骗子已经操纵受害者让他交出所有钱财。

将来时:
He will manipulate the situation to his benefit. 他将操纵局面以利于自己。
She will manipulate the controls to start the machine. 她将操作控制装置启动机器。

虚拟语气:
If he manipulated the evidence, he would be in trouble. 如果他篡改证据，他就会有麻烦。
I wish I hadn't manipulated the situation. 我真希望我没有操纵局面。

被动语态:
The data was manipulated to fit the desired outcome. 数据被操纵以符合预期结果。
The machine is manipulated by a complex system of levers and gears. 这台机器由一套复杂的杠杆和齿轮系统操纵。

疑问句:
Who manipulated the evidence? 谁篡改了证据？
How did he manipulate the machine to work? 他是如何操纵机器使其工作的？

否定句:
He did not manipulate the results of the experiment. 他没有操纵实验的结果。
She did not manipulate the situation to her advantage. 她没有利用局面来为自己谋利。
