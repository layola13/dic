chalazion:/kəˈleɪziən/| n. |睑板腺囊肿|A chalazion is a small, usually painless lump that forms on the eyelid. It's caused by a blocked meibomian gland, which is an oil-producing gland in the eyelid.  The blockage leads to inflammation and the formation of a cyst filled with fatty material.|  He developed a chalazion on his upper eyelid. 他上眼睑长了一个睑板腺囊肿。|  eyelid cyst, stye (although a stye is usually more painful and acutely inflamed)|  (no direct antonym, but the absence of a chalazion could be considered the opposite)|名词
