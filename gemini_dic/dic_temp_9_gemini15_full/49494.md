dem:/dɛm/ | pron. | 他们，那些人 |  "Dem" is a nonstandard contraction of "them." It is primarily used in informal spoken English, particularly in certain dialects.  It functions as a pronoun, referring to a group of people or things previously mentioned. | Example: I saw dem at the store. 我在商店看到他们了。 |近义词: them, those guys | 反义词: None. | pronoun

PHRASE->
There are few common phrases specifically using "dem" due to its informal nature and limited usage in formal writing.  The usage is primarily within sentences as demonstrated above.  Attempts to create phrases feel contrived.
