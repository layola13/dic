minimizes:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪzɪz/| v.|最小化；使减到最少 | to reduce something to the smallest possible amount or degree | The company is trying to minimize its losses. 公司正试图将损失降到最低。 |近义词：reduce, lessen, decrease |反义词：maximize, increase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He minimizes his mistakes. = 他尽量减少自己的错误。
The software minimizes the risk of errors. = 该软件最大限度地降低了出错的风险。


一般过去时:
She minimized the impact of the bad news. = 她尽量减少了坏消息的影响。
They minimized the damage caused by the fire. = 他们将火灾造成的损失降到了最低。


现在完成时:
He has minimized his involvement in the project. = 他已经尽量减少了他对这个项目的参与。
We have minimized our carbon footprint. = 我们已经将我们的碳足迹降到了最低。


过去完成时:
They had minimized their expenses before the trip. = 他们在旅行前已经尽量减少了开支。
She had minimized the risks before starting the experiment. = 在开始实验之前，她已经将风险降到了最低。


将来时:
They will minimize the disruption to services. = 他们将把对服务的干扰降到最低。
We will minimize the use of plastic bags. = 我们将尽量减少塑料袋的使用。


虚拟语气:
If we acted quickly, we could minimize the damage. = 如果我们迅速采取行动，就能将损失降到最低。
I wish I could minimize the stress in my life. = 我希望我能减少生活中的压力。


被动语态:
The impact of the crisis was minimized by the government's actions. = 政府的行动将危机的冲击降到了最低。
The risks are minimized through careful planning. = 通过周密的计划，风险被降到了最低。


疑问句:
How can we minimize the environmental impact? = 我们怎样才能将对环境的影响降到最低？
What measures are being taken to minimize costs? = 正在采取什么措施来降低成本？


否定句:
He didn't minimize the seriousness of the situation. = 他没有轻视局势的严重性。(这里的minimize表示"轻视"，与减少的含义略有不同)
They did not minimize their efforts to help. = 他们并没有减少他们的帮助努力。


PHRASE->
minimize the risk = 最小化风险
minimize the damage = 最小化损失
minimize the impact = 最小化影响
minimize the cost = 最小化成本
minimize the effort = 最小化努力
