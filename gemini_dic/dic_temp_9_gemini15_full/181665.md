releasing:/rɪˈliːsɪŋ/| v. |释放；发行；公布；解脱|The act of setting something free or making something available.  It can refer to physical objects, information, emotions, or legal restrictions.|例句：The company is releasing a new product next month. 公司下个月将发布一款新产品。

例句：He felt a sense of releasing after confessing his secret.  他坦白秘密后感到如释重负。


近义词：unleashing, liberating, discharging, issuing, publishing|反义词：retaining, withholding, confining, imprisoning|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is releasing the bird from its cage. 他正在把鸟从笼子里放出来。
The company releases new software updates regularly. 公司定期发布新的软件更新。


一般过去时:
She released the ball into the air. 她把球抛向空中。
They released a statement to the press. 他们向媒体发布了一份声明。


现在完成时:
They have released three albums this year. 他们今年已经发行了三张专辑。
The government has released a report on the economy. 政府已经发布了一份关于经济的报告。


过去完成时:
The prisoner had been released before the trial. 犯人在审判前已被释放。
She had already released the information to the media before we could stop her. 在我们阻止她之前，她已经向媒体发布了信息。


将来时:
They will release the film next summer. 他们将在明年夏天上映这部电影。
The company will release its financial results next week. 公司将于下周发布其财务业绩。


虚拟语气:
If they released the information, the public would be informed. 如果他们发布信息，公众就会被告知。
I wish they had released the report sooner. 我希望他们早点发布报告。


被动语态:
The hostages were released unharmed. 人质被平安释放。
The new product will be released in the spring. 新产品将于春季发布。


疑问句:
When will they be releasing the new album? 他们什么时候发行新专辑？
Has the company released a statement yet? 公司是否已经发表声明了？


否定句:
They did not release any details about the accident. 他们没有透露任何关于事故的细节。
The prisoner was not released until after the trial. 犯人直到审判后才被释放。
