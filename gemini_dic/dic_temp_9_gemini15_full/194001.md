self-exposures: /ˌsɛlfɪkˈspoʊʒərz/ | n. |自我暴露；自我揭露；(个体或群体的)暴露行为| Acts of revealing oneself or one's thoughts and feelings, often in a vulnerable or intimate way.  This can refer to individual acts, or patterns of behavior.  It can also refer to the exposure of a group to risk or harm. |

例句：

His self-exposures in his memoir were surprisingly candid. | 他在回忆录中的自我暴露令人惊讶地坦率。

The artist's work is a series of self-exposures, exploring themes of identity and vulnerability. |这位艺术家的作品是一系列自我揭露，探索身份和脆弱的主题。


近义词：revelations, disclosures, confessions, unveilings

反义词：concealment, secrecy, privacy, suppression


词性：名词
