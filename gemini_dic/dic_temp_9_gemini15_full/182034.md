remelts:/ˌriːˈmelt/| v.|重熔；再熔化 |To melt again something that has already been melted.|The goldsmith remelts the scrap gold to make new jewelry.  金匠重熔废金以制作新首饰。 The factory remelts the aluminum cans to create new products. 工厂重熔铝罐以制造新产品。|melt, fuse|solidify
