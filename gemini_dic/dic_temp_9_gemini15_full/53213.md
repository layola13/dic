directed:/daɪˈrektɪd/| adj. |被指导的；导演的；指向的；针对的|Directed refers to something that is guided, managed, or controlled;  it can also describe something aimed or pointed at a specific target or goal.  It is often used to describe films or plays, indicating who is the director.|例句：The film was directed by a renowned filmmaker. 这部电影是由一位著名的电影导演执导的。|The arrow pointed in a directed path towards the target. 箭头指向目标的特定方向。|近义词：guided, controlled, aimed, targeted|反义词：undirected, uncontrolled, random, haphazard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project is directed by a talented team. = 这个项目由一个才华横溢的团队负责。
The camera is directed at the main character. = 摄像机对准主角。

一般过去时:
The play was directed by Shakespeare. = 这部戏剧是由莎士比亚执导的。
The letter was directed to the wrong address. = 这封信寄错了地址。

现在完成时:
The efforts have been directed towards finding a solution. = 努力一直集中在寻找解决方案上。
The funds have been directed to the charity. = 资金已经捐给了慈善机构。

过去完成时:
The investigation had been directed by the police chief. = 调查是由警察局长负责的。
The blame had been directed at the wrong person. = 责任归咎于错误的人。

将来时:
The resources will be directed to the most needy areas. = 资源将用于最贫困的地区。
The project will be directed by a new manager. = 项目将由一位新的经理负责。

虚拟语气:
If the resources were directed efficiently, the project would succeed. = 如果资源得到有效利用，项目就会成功。
I wish the efforts had been directed more effectively. = 我希望努力能够更有效率。

被动语态:
The funds were directed by the board of directors. = 资金由董事会调拨。
The criticism was directed at the government's policy. = 批评针对的是政府的政策。

疑问句:
Is the work directed by a single person? = 这项工作是由一个人负责的吗？
Is the laser beam directed accurately? = 激光束瞄准精确吗？

否定句:
The project is not directed by anyone yet. = 这个项目还没有人负责。
The criticism was not directed at me. = 批评不是针对我的。
