词源:
The word "outwrestles" is formed from the prefix "out-" (meaning "to a greater extent" or "surpass") and the verb "wrestles" (from "wrestle," meaning to engage in a grappling contest).  Therefore, it literally means "to wrestle better than" or "to surpass in wrestling."
