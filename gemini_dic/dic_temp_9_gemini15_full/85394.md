harts:/hɑːrts/| n.|雄鹿；鹿心；勇气；|The word "harts" refers to male deer, specifically red deer stags. It can also be used metaphorically to represent courage or spirit. |例句：The hunter stalked the majestic harts in the forest. 猎人潜伏在森林中，追踪雄鹿。  The brave knight showed a hart of a lion. 勇敢的骑士展现出狮子的勇气。|近义词：stags, deer|反义词：doe(雌鹿)
