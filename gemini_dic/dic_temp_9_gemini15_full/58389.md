dubbed:/dʌbd/ | vt. |配音；称作；授予称号 |to provide a voice-over for a film or television program; to give something a particular name or description; to confer a title or honor upon someone |例句：The film was dubbed into several languages. 这部电影被配音成多种语言。

The director dubbed the actor "the next Marlon Brando."  导演称这位演员为“下一个马龙·白兰度”。

近义词：titled, named, called, christened | 反义词：muted (in the context of sound)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dubs all the foreign films for the studio. 他为制片厂配音所有的外国电影。
They dub him a genius. 他们称他为天才。


一般过去时:
The movie was dubbed into Spanish. 这部电影被配音成西班牙语。
