horseman:/ˈhɔːsmən/ /ˈhɔːrsmən/| n. |骑手，骑士|A person who rides a horse, especially one skilled in riding.|The horseman rode across the plains with great skill.  这位骑手以高超的技巧驰骋在平原上。


A skilled horseman can easily control his horse.  一位熟练的骑手可以轻松地控制他的马。|近义词：rider, cavalier, equestrian|反义词：pedestrian|名词
