ordinals:/ˈɔːrdɪnəlz/ | n. |序数，序数词 | Ordinals are numbers that indicate the position of something in a sequence, such as first, second, third, etc. They are used to show rank or order.  |Example:  She finished the race in third place. 她获得了第三名。 The ordinals are used to organize the chapters of a book. 序数词用于组织书籍的章节。|近义词：rank, order, sequence |反义词：cardinals (基数词)
