foundational:/ˌfaʊnˈdeɪʃənəl/| adj. | 基础的；根本的；奠基的 | Forming or serving as a base, foundation, or groundwork; fundamental.  It refers to something that is essential and provides a basis for further development or understanding. |  The foundational principles of democracy are freedom and equality.  民主的根本原则是自由和平等。

The foundational research for this project took years to complete.  这个项目的奠基性研究花了数年时间才完成。| fundamental, basic, essential, primary | superficial, advanced, derivative | 形容词
