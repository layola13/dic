USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't conjugate like verbs.  It describes how an action is performed.  There are no tense variations.


例句1: He spent his inheritance nonprovidently, leaving himself with nothing in his old age. 他挥霍了他的遗产，老年时一无所有。
例句2:  The government's nonprovident policies led to a severe economic crisis. 政府缺乏远见的政策导致了严重的经济危机。


PHRASE->
There are no common set phrases using "nonprovidently".  The word itself functions as a standalone adverbial modifier.
