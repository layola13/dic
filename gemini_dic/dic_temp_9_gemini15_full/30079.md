cdrom:/ˈsiːdiːˈrɒm/| n. |光盘只读存储器|Compact Disc Read-Only Memory; a type of optical disc used to store data, typically software or multimedia.  It's read-only, meaning data cannot be written or erased.|例句：I installed the software from the CD-ROM. 我从光盘安装了软件。
例句：The CD-ROM drive is malfunctioning. 光盘驱动器出现故障了。|近义词：optical disc, data disc|反义词：CD-R, CD-RW
