pleura:/ˈplʊərə/| n.|胸膜|A thin serous membrane which folds back upon itself to form a closed sac.  The pleura lines the chest cavity and covers the lungs. It secretes a lubricating fluid that allows the lungs to move smoothly during breathing.|例句：The pleura is a vital part of the respiratory system, allowing for smooth lung expansion and contraction.|近义词：pleural membrane|反义词: N/A
