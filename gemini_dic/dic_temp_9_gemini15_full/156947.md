pilfers:/ˈpɪlfərz/| vt.|偷窃（少量东西）, 窃取|to steal things of little value, typically in small quantities.|例句：He was caught pilfering stationery from the office. 他被抓到偷办公室的文具。|例句：The thief pilfered small amounts of money from the cash register. 窃贼从收银机里偷走少量钱。|近义词：steal, thieve, rob, filch|反义词：donate, give, contribute|动词
