on-the-run: /ˌɒn ðə rʌn/ | adj. | 逃亡中的；在逃的 |  Fleeing from the law or some other danger; escaping.  |例句：The bank robber is still on the run.  这个银行抢劫犯仍在逃亡中。


例句：The escaped prisoner has been on the run for three weeks. 这个越狱的囚犯已经逃亡三周了。


近义词：fugitive, escaped, at large


反义词：captured, apprehended, in custody


词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The suspect is on the run. = 嫌疑犯在逃亡中。
He is always on the run. = 他总是很匆忙。


一般过去时:
The thief was on the run after the robbery. = 抢劫之后，小偷逃跑了。
She was always on the run, trying to meet deadlines. = 她总是忙忙碌碌，试图赶上最后期限。


现在完成时:
The criminal has been on the run for months. = 这个罪犯已经逃亡数月了。
He has been on the run since the scandal broke. = 自从丑闻爆发以来，他一直在逃亡。


过去完成时:
The police knew that the suspect had been on the run for a long time. = 警察知道嫌疑犯已经逃亡很久了。
Before the accident, she had been on the run, working day and night. = 事故发生前，她一直忙于工作，日夜兼程。


将来时:
The police believe the fugitive will be on the run for many more months. = 警方认为逃犯还会逃亡数月。
She will be on the run for the next few weeks. = 在接下来的几周里，她将非常忙碌。


虚拟语气:
If he were on the run, he would have a hard time. = 如果他在逃亡，他会很难过。
If she had been on the run, she would have been caught by now. = 如果她一直在逃亡，她现在已经被抓住了。


被动语态:
The criminal was said to be on the run. = 据说罪犯在逃亡。
It was reported that the fugitive had been on the run for years. = 据报道，逃犯已经逃亡多年了。


疑问句:
Is the murderer still on the run? = 凶手还在逃亡吗？
Is he always on the run like this? = 他总是这么匆忙吗？


否定句:
The suspect is not on the run; he is in custody. = 嫌疑犯没有在逃，他已被拘留。
She is not on the run; she's relaxing on vacation. = 她没有在逃亡；她正在度假放松。


PHRASE->
on the run from the law = 逃避法律
on the run from creditors = 逃避债权人
on the run from the police = 逃避警察
on the run for his life = 为了生命而逃亡
on the run with his lover = 和他的情人私奔


词源:
The phrase "on the run" originally referred to someone who is constantly busy and active, always rushing from one thing to another.  Later, the meaning extended to describe someone actively fleeing from the authorities or danger.  The "run" here refers to a continuous state of flight or escape.
