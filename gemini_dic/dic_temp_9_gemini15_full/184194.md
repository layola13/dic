resocialized:/ˌriːˈsoʊʃəlaɪzd/| v. | 再社会化；重新融入社会 | To reintegrate (someone, especially a former prisoner or patient in a mental institution) into society.  | 例句：The program aims to resocialize offenders and help them become productive members of society.  该项目旨在使罪犯重新融入社会，并帮助他们成为社会的有生产力的一员。


The prison system needs to focus on resocializing inmates, not just punishing them. 监狱系统需要关注罪犯的再社会化，而不仅仅是惩罚他们。

| 近义词：rehabilitate, reintegrate, reform | 反义词：isolate, marginalize | 动词
