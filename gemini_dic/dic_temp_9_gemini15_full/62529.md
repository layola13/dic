endurer:/ɛnˈdʊərər/| n. |忍耐者；耐久的人或物| A person or thing that endures; someone who can withstand hardship or difficulty.  |例句：He was a true endurer, facing every challenge with unwavering determination. 他是一位真正的忍耐者，以坚定不移的决心面对每一个挑战。

例句：The old bridge is an endurer, having withstood countless storms. 这座古老的桥梁经久耐用，经受住了无数的风暴。


近义词：perseverer, survivor, stalwart| 反义词：quitter, weakling |词性:noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The old house endures the harsh weather. = 这栋老房子经受住了恶劣的天气。
He endures the pain bravely. = 他勇敢地忍受着痛苦。


一般过去时:
She endured years of hardship. = 她忍受了多年的苦难。
The building endured the earthquake. = 这座建筑物经受住了地震。


现在完成时:
They have endured much suffering. = 他们忍受了很多苦难。
The company has endured many economic downturns. = 这家公司经受住了许多经济低迷时期。


过去完成时:
He had endured countless setbacks before achieving success. = 在取得成功之前，他忍受了无数的挫折。
The city had endured a long siege. = 这个城市经历了长期的围困。


将来时:
We will endure whatever challenges lie ahead. = 我们将克服未来面临的任何挑战。
The monument will endure for centuries. = 这座纪念碑将屹立数百年。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would endure the pain and finish the marathon. = 如果我是你，我会忍受痛苦并完成马拉松比赛。
I wish I could endure the heat better. = 我希望我能更好地忍受高温。


被动语态:
The hardship was endured with courage. = 困难是勇敢地忍受的。
The criticism was endured silently. = 批评是默默忍受的。


疑问句:
Can you endure this pain? = 你能忍受这种痛苦吗？
How long can the bridge endure? = 这座桥能承受多久？


否定句:
He could not endure the pressure any longer. = 他再也无法承受压力了。
They did not endure the harsh conditions for long. = 他们没有长时间忍受恶劣的条件。



PHRASE->
endure hardship = 忍受苦难
endure pain = 忍受痛苦
endure suffering = 忍受痛苦
endure temptation = 经受诱惑
endure the test of time = 经受时间的考验


词源:
源自古法语endurer，来自拉丁语ēndūrāre，意思是“to harden, make firm”。  其核心含义是“to last, to remain”，这与现代英语中的“忍耐”含义有所演变，但两者之间存在关联，即忍耐的过程往往需要坚韧和持久。
