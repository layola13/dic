phanerogamic:/ˌfænəroʊˈɡæmɪk/| adj.|显花植物的；有胚珠的|  Relating to or characteristic of phanerogams (seed plants that produce flowers or cones).  The term refers to plants that produce seeds enclosed within an ovary (angiosperms) or exposed on the surface of cone scales (gymnosperms), in contrast to cryptogams (plants that reproduce by spores).| Example:  Phanerogamic plants are characterized by their visible reproductive structures. = 显花植物的特征在于其可见的繁殖结构。  The study focused on the evolution of phanerogamic species. = 这项研究的重点是显花植物物种的进化。|近义词：flowering, seed-producing|反义词：cryptogamic
