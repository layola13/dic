retrier:/rɪˈtraɪər/ | n. |重试者，反复尝试者 | A person or thing that retries something.  Someone who persistently attempts something again and again after failures.  |例句：The dedicated retrier finally succeeded in climbing the mountain after many attempts.  那位坚持不懈的重试者经过多次尝试后最终成功登上了山峰。|近义词：perseverer, persistent one,  |反义词：quitter,  give-upper | 名词
