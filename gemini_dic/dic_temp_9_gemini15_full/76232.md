futureless:/ˈfjuːtʃərləs/| adj.|没有前途的；没有希望的；无望的|Lacking prospects or hope for the future; hopeless.  The adjective "futureless" describes something or someone without a promising or hopeful future. It implies a sense of stagnation, despair, or lack of potential for progress or improvement.|例句：His futureless job left him feeling unmotivated. 他的工作没有前途，这让他感到缺乏动力。

例句：The bleak landscape seemed futureless and desolate. 荒凉的景色显得毫无希望，一片荒凉。

近义词：hopeless, bleak, despairing, despondent, grim

反义词：promising, hopeful, bright, optimistic, encouraging
