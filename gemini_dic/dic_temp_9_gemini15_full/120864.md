misbeliever:/ˌmɪsbɪˈliːvər/| n. |不信者，异教徒|A person who does not believe in a particular religion or system of beliefs.  Someone who holds beliefs contrary to what is considered orthodox or accepted. |Example:  He was branded a misbeliever and ostracized by the community. 他被贴上不信者的标签，并被社区排斥。
|近义词：unbeliever, heretic, infidel |反义词：believer, adherent
