interesse:/ɪnˈtɛrɛsə/ | n. |兴趣，爱好；利息|Interest is a feeling of wanting to learn more about something or someone.  It can also refer to the money earned from an investment.|例句：My interest in history began in childhood. 我的历史兴趣始于童年。|例句：The bank pays 3% interest on savings accounts. 银行活期存款利率为3%。|近义词：hobby, fascination, liking, curiosity |反义词：disinterest, apathy, boredom |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shows great interest in art. = 他对艺术表现出极大的兴趣。
What are your interests? = 你的兴趣爱好是什么？


一般过去时:
Her interest in music faded after college. = 她大学毕业后对音乐的兴趣减退了。
I had a strong interest in learning foreign languages. = 我对学习外语有浓厚的兴趣。


现在完成时:
My interest in photography has grown over the years. = 我对摄影的兴趣多年来一直在增长。
She has lost interest in her job. = 她对工作失去了兴趣。


过去完成时:
By the time he retired, his interest in golf had waned. = 到他退休的时候，他对高尔夫的兴趣已经减弱了。
They had already lost interest in the project before it started. = 项目开始之前，他们就已经对它失去了兴趣。


将来时:
I think I will develop an interest in gardening next year. = 我想明年我会对园艺产生兴趣。
He will likely lose interest in the game soon. = 他很可能会很快对游戏失去兴趣。


虚拟语气:
If I had more free time, I would pursue my interest in painting. = 如果我有更多空闲时间，我会追求我绘画的兴趣。
I wish I could rekindle my interest in reading. = 我希望我能重新燃起我对阅读的兴趣。


被动语态:
His interest in science was sparked by a documentary. = 他对科学的兴趣是由一部纪录片激发的。
The project's success was fueled by the team's interest. = 项目的成功是由团队的兴趣推动的。


疑问句:
What are his main interests? = 他的主要兴趣是什么？
Does she have any interest in politics? = 她对政治有兴趣吗？


否定句:
He showed no interest in the offer. = 他对这个提议没有表现出任何兴趣。
She has lost all interest in her studies. = 她对学习完全失去了兴趣。



PHRASE->
interest rate: 利率
compound interest: 复利
vested interest:既得利益
self-interest: 自私自利
in the interest of: 为了…的利益
