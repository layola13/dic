padres:/ˈpædreɪz/| n. |圣父；教父；神父；（美国职棒大联盟球队）圣地亚哥教士队 |The word "padres" is the plural form of "padre," which is Spanish for "father."  It is commonly used to refer to Catholic priests or, in a more informal sense, to any older male figure. In the context of baseball, it refers specifically to the San Diego Padres Major League Baseball team.|例句：The Padres won the game last night. 圣地亚哥教士队昨晚赢得了比赛。

The local priest, a kind padre, helped the community. 当地的神父，一位慈祥的教父，帮助了社区。 |近义词：fathers, priests, clergymen |反义词：无
