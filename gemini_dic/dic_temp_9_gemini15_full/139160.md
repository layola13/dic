obfuscatory: /ɒbˈfjʊːskeɪtəri/ | adj. | 使模糊的；使费解的；含糊其辞的 | tending to confuse, bewilder, or obscure; intentionally unclear or confusing.  | The obfuscatory language of the legal document made it difficult to understand. 这份法律文件的含糊不清的语言使得它难以理解。 |近义词：obscure, confusing, ambiguous, unclear, evasive | 反义词：clear, transparent, lucid, unambiguous
