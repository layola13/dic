multidentate:/ˌmʌltiˈdɛnteɪt/| adj.|多齿的；多齿合的|Having or involving more than two points of attachment.  A multidentate ligand is a molecule that can form bonds with a central metal atom using more than one atom in the molecule.  This contrasts with monodentate ligands which only bond at one point.|例句：EDTA is a multidentate ligand used in many chemical applications. EDTA是一种在许多化学应用中使用的多齿配体。|近义词：polydentate|反义词：monodentate
