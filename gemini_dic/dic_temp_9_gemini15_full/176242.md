re-christianize:/ˌriːˈkrɪstʃənaɪz/| vt.|重新基督教化；使再次皈依基督教|To convert (a person or place) back to Christianity, or to reintroduce Christian values or practices into (a society or culture).|例句：The government attempted to re-christianize the country after a period of secular rule.  政府在一段世俗统治后试图重新基督教化这个国家。

例句：After decades of atheism, the movement sought to re-christianize the nation's moral compass. 数十年的无神论之后，该运动试图重新基督教化国家的道德准则。


近义词：reconvert, re-evangelize, evangelize anew


反义词：secularize, dechristianize


词性:动词
