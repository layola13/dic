chambers:/ˈtʃeɪmbərz/| n.|房间；会议室；腔；议院|A chamber is a room, especially a large room used for formal meetings or ceremonies, or a room in a house that is used for a particular purpose.  It can also refer to a natural cavity or hollow space within a body or structure.|例句：The Senate chamber was filled with reporters. 参议院会议室挤满了记者。

The judge's chambers were located down a long corridor.  法官的办公室位于一条长走廊的尽头。|近义词：room, hall, compartment, cavity|反义词：open space
