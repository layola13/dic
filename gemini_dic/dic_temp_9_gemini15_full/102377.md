jaan:/dʒɑːn/ | n. |亲爱的，生命，爱人 |  A term of endearment, often used to express love, affection, or intimacy. It's primarily used in South Asian languages, particularly Urdu, Hindi, and Punjabi.  |  "My jaan, I love you so much."  = “我的爱人，我非常爱你。” | darling, sweetheart, beloved |  enemy, foe | 名词


USAGE->
This word is not a verb, adjective, or other part of speech that would allow for conjugation in the way “what” is.  Therefore, grammatical tenses, voice, and mood are not applicable.  The word "jaan" functions as a noun, primarily as a term of endearment.
