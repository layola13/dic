hepatology:/ˌhiːpəˈtɒlədʒi/| n. |肝脏学，肝病学 |The study of the liver and liver diseases.  It encompasses the structure, function, and diseases of the liver. |Hepatology is a rapidly advancing field, constantly discovering new treatments for liver diseases. 肝病学是一个快速发展的领域，不断发现治疗肝脏疾病的新方法。| gastroenterology,  internal medicine |  (无明确反义词，可考虑与特定肝脏疾病相关的术语作对比)|名词

词源:
The word "hepatology" is derived from the Greek words "hēpar" (ἧπαρ), meaning "liver," and "-logia" (-λογία), meaning "study of."  Therefore, hepatology literally means "the study of the liver."
