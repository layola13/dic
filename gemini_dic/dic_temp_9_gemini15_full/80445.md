goners:/ˈɡɑːnərz/ | n. |完蛋的人；注定失败的人；即将死亡的人| People or things that are doomed, ruined, or about to die.  It often implies a sense of finality and irreversibility. |例句：After that last mistake, they're goners.  = 犯了最后一个错误后，他们完蛋了。

例句：With that injury, he's a goner. = 受了那样的伤，他活不成了。


近义词：doomed, finished, ruined, dead, lost causes |反义词：survivors, winners, successful


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Those plants are goners without more water. = 那些植物没有更多水的话就完蛋了。
The company's goners if they don't get a new CEO. = 如果公司得不到一位新CEO，那它就完蛋了。
