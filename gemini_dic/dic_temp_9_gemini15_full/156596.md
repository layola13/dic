pickup: /ˈpɪkʌp/ | n., v. | 拾起；搭载；接收；(俚语)搭讪；(车辆)皮卡车 |

英文解析:As a noun, "pickup" refers to the act of picking something up, a vehicle (pickup truck), or a radio signal reception. As a verb, it means to lift something, to collect someone or something, to learn something quickly, or to start a romantic relationship casually.

例句:

(Noun):

The pickup of the guitar was excellent.  = 吉他拾音器效果极佳。

He drove his pickup truck to the farm. = 他开着他的皮卡车去了农场。


(Verb):

Could you help me pickup the toys? = 你能帮我捡起玩具吗？

I'll pickup my dry cleaning later. = 我晚点去取我的干洗衣服。

She quickly picked up Spanish during her travels. = 在旅行中，她很快学会了西班牙语。

He tried to pickup girls at the bar. = 他试图在酒吧搭讪女孩。


近义词: (Noun) collection, gathering, selection; (Verb) lift, collect, gather, acquire, glean

反义词: (Verb) drop, put down, leave, discard


词性: Noun, Verb
