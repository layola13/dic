interfaith:/ˈɪntərfeɪθ/| adj.|宗教间（的）；跨宗教（的）|Relating to or involving different religions.  It describes activities, organizations, or dialogues that bring together people of various faiths. |例句：The interfaith council promotes understanding and cooperation among different religious communities. 宗教间议会促进不同宗教社群之间的理解与合作。  The interfaith dialogue aimed to bridge the gap between different religious beliefs. 宗教间对话旨在弥合不同宗教信仰之间的差距。|近义词：ecumenical, multifaith|反义词：intrafaith (within one faith)
