no-show: /ˈnoʊ ʃoʊ/ | n. |爽约者；未露面者；缺席者 | A person who fails to keep an appointment or attend an event without giving prior notice.  |例句：He was a no-show at the meeting, leaving us short-handed. 他没参加会议，导致我们人手不足。|例句：The restaurant had several no-shows for dinner reservations last night. 昨晚这家餐厅好几桌预订晚餐的人都没来。|近义词：absent, defaulter |反义词：attendee, participant |名词
