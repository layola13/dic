mummy:/ˈmʌmi/| n. |木乃伊；（口语）妈妈|A mummy is a dead body that has been preserved, especially by the ancient Egyptians.  In informal speech, "mummy" is a child's word for "mother".|例句：The museum displayed an ancient Egyptian mummy.  博物馆展示了一具古埃及木乃伊。


例句：The little boy called his mother "mummy".  小男孩叫他妈妈“妈妈”。


近义词： (as a preserved body) corpse, cadaver; (as a term of endearment) mom, mama, mother


反义词：  None directly applicable.  The antonyms would depend on the context (e.g.,  "alive" is the opposite of "mummy" when referring to a preserved body).


词性:名词
