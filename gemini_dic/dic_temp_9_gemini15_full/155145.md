petting:/ˈpetɪŋ/| n. v.|爱抚，抚摸；轻抚|The act of gently stroking or caressing someone or something.|例句：The cat enjoyed the petting.  猫喜欢被抚摸。|例句：She gave her dog some petting before bed. 她睡前抚摸了一下她的狗。|近义词：caressing, stroking, fondling|反义词：hitting, striking, abusing|动词


USAGE->

He pets the cat every day. = 他每天都抚摸猫。
They pet their dog gently. = 他们轻轻地抚摸他们的狗。
