preguidance:/ˌpriːˈɡaɪdəns/| n. |预先指导，预先引导 | Guidance given before an event or activity.  It often involves preparation and planning to ensure a smooth and successful outcome.  Preguidance can be given in many forms, such as instructions, training, or briefings. |Example: The preguidance session prepared the team for the challenges of the project. = 预先指导会议让团队为项目的挑战做好了准备。

The company provided preguidance to its employees on the new safety regulations. = 公司就新的安全条例向员工提供了预先指导。|近义词：advance guidance, prior instruction, preliminary briefing |反义词：post-guidance, follow-up instruction|名词
