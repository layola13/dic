olefine:/ˈoʊlɪfiːn/ | n. |烯烃；烯类|An olefin is an unsaturated hydrocarbon containing at least one carbon-carbon double bond.  They are also known as alkenes.  Olefins are highly reactive due to the presence of the double bond, readily undergoing addition reactions. |例句：Ethylene is the simplest olefin. = 乙烯是最简单的烯烃。|例句：The polymerization of olefins produces plastics. = 烯烃的聚合反应产生塑料。|近义词：alkene |反义词：alkane (saturated hydrocarbon)
