lemon-squeezer: /ˈlɛmənˌskwizər/ | n. | 榨柠檬器 | A kitchen tool used to extract juice from lemons.  It typically consists of a container with a conical or bowl-shaped top and a handle that presses down on the lemon to squeeze out the juice. |  例句：She used a lemon squeezer to add fresh lemon juice to her tea. 她用榨柠檬器往茶里加新鲜柠檬汁。

例句：The restaurant uses a commercial-grade lemon squeezer to make large quantities of lemonade. 这家餐馆使用商用级榨柠檬器来制作大量的柠檬水。


近义词：citrus juicer, lemon press


反义词：N/A  (no direct antonym)


词性：noun
