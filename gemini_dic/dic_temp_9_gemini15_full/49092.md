deification:/ˌdiːɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |神化；奉为神；神格化 |The act of elevating someone or something to the status of a god or goddess; the process of being treated as a god or goddess.  It often involves attributing divine qualities or powers to a person or thing. |例句：The deification of the emperor led to unquestioning loyalty and obedience.  皇帝的神化导致了毫无疑问的忠诚和服从。

例句：The deification of athletes in modern society is a complex phenomenon. 现代社会对运动员的崇拜是一个复杂的现象。


近义词：divinization, apotheosis, idolization |反义词：denigration, vilification, degradation |词性：名词
