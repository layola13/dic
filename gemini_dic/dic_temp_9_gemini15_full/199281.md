silverfin:/ˈsɪlvərˌfɪn/| n.|银鳍鱼；银鳍鲑鱼|A type of fish, often referring to a specific species of salmonid known for its silvery fins.  The term can also be used more generally for any fish with silvery fins.|例句：The silverfin is a prized catch for many anglers. 银鳍鱼是许多钓鱼者梦寐以求的收获。

例句：We saw schools of silverfin flashing in the shallows. 我们看到成群的银鳍鱼在浅水区闪闪发光。


近义词：whitefish (in some contexts), salmon (depending on the specific species)
反义词：无


词性:名词
