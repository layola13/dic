homotonous: /ˈhɒmətənəs/ | adj. | 单调的，无变化的 | Monotonous means lacking in variety or interest; tediously uniform. It describes something that is repetitive and boring due to a lack of change or variation.  | Example: The monotonous drone of the speaker's voice lulled the audience to sleep.  演讲者单调乏味的说话声使听众昏昏欲睡。|近义词：monotonal, unvaried, uniform, tedious, dull, boring |反义词：varied, diverse, interesting, exciting
