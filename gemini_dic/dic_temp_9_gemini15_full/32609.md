chirpers:/ˈtʃɜːrpərz/ | n. | 鸣叫者；（尤指）蟋蟀 |  People or things that chirp, especially crickets. The term often evokes a sense of smallness, numerousness, and perhaps a slightly annoying or persistent sound. | Example:  The summer night was filled with the chirpers in the grass.  夏夜里草丛中充满了蟋蟀的鸣叫声。|近义词：crickets, insects, tweeters|反义词：silent creatures

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The birds chirpers merrily in the morning. = 鸟儿在早晨快乐地鸣叫。
Many chirpers are singing in the field. = 田野里有很多虫子在鸣叫。

一般过去时:
The crickets chirped loudly last night. = 蟋蟀昨晚大声鸣叫。
The birds chirped sweetly during sunrise. = 日出时分，鸟儿婉转地鸣叫。

现在完成时:
The crickets have chirped all night. = 蟋蟀整夜都在鸣叫。
The birds have chirped a cheerful song this morning. = 鸟儿今天早上唱了一首快乐的歌。

过去完成时:
The crickets had chirped before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，蟋蟀就已经鸣叫了。
The birds had chirped their morning song before the sun rose. = 太阳升起之前，鸟儿就已经唱完了晨歌。

将来时:
The crickets will chirp loudly tonight. = 今晚蟋蟀会大声鸣叫。
The birds will chirp a welcome song for the coming day. = 鸟儿将为即将到来的一天唱一首欢迎的歌。

虚拟语气:
If the crickets chirped louder, we could hear them better. = 如果蟋蟀叫得更响亮，我们就能听得更清楚。
I wish the birds chirped more often. = 我希望鸟儿能更频繁地鸣叫。

被动语态:
The chirping was heard throughout the night. = 整个晚上都能听到鸣叫声。（Chirping is a gerund,  a verb acting like a noun.  It is the subject of the passive sentence.）
A sweet song was chirped by a small bird. = 一只小鸟唱了一首甜美的歌。

疑问句:
Are the crickets chirping tonight? = 今晚蟋蟀在鸣叫吗？
Do you hear the chirpers? = 你听到鸣叫声了吗？

否定句:
The crickets didn't chirp last night. = 蟋蟀昨晚没有鸣叫。
The birds weren't chirping this morning. = 今早鸟儿没有鸣叫。
