chloropeptic:/ˌklɔːroʊˈpɛptɪk/| adj.|氯消化性的；氯肽的|Relating to or involving both chlorine and peptic digestion.  The term is rarely used independently and usually appears in compound words or phrases describing substances or processes involving both chlorine and the digestive enzyme pepsin. There is no established common usage or widely accepted definition beyond this core meaning.|Example:  A study on the chloropeptic effects of a new compound on gastric function is underway.  一项关于一种新型化合物对胃功能的氯消化作用的研究正在进行中。|近义词:chlorinated, peptic (partially overlapping)|反义词:None readily available, as it's a specific combination of characteristics.
