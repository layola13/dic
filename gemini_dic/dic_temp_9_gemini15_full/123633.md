montoir: /mɒ̃ˈtwɑːr/ | n. | 马鞍架；马鞍垫 | A montoir is a small, portable step or stool used to mount a horse. It is typically made of wood or metal and is designed to provide a stable platform from which to easily mount a horse.  | Example:  The rider used a montoir to mount her horse more gracefully.  骑手用马鞍架更优雅地骑上马。 | 近义词：mounting block, stepping stone | 反义词：无 | 名词
