nonfeverishly: /ˌnɑːnˈfiːvərɪʃli/ | adv. | 不发烧地，平静地，不狂热地 |  The word "nonfeverishly" is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the adverb "feverishly" (meaning "in a feverish or excited manner").  Therefore, "nonfeverishly" describes something done without fever or excitement; calmly and steadily. | Example: He worked nonfeverishly on the project, completing each task methodically. 他平静地工作，有条不紊地完成每一个任务。 | 近义词：calmly, quietly, steadily, deliberately, methodically | 反义词：feverishly, excitedly, frantically, wildly
