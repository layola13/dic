relais:/rɪˈleɪ/| n. |继电器；中继；接力赛；传递|A relay is an electromechanical switch that uses a small electrical current to control a larger current.  It's also a race where each member of a team runs a part of the distance, and a process of passing something on.|例句：The electrical circuit uses a relay to switch the power on and off.  这个电路使用继电器来开关电源。

The relay team won the race. 接力队赢得了比赛。|近义词：switch, repeater, transfer|反义词：None specifically.
