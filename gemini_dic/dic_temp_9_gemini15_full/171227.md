psychotic:/saɪˈkɑːtɪk/| adj.|精神病的；精神错乱的| Relating to or characteristic of psychosis; suffering from psychosis.  The term refers to a severe mental disorder characterized by a loss of contact with reality, often involving delusions, hallucinations, disorganized thinking, and/or grossly disorganized or abnormal motor behavior. |例句：His psychotic break led to hospitalization. 他的精神崩溃导致住院治疗。

例句：The patient exhibited classic psychotic symptoms, including hallucinations and delusions.  这位病人表现出典型的精神病症状，包括幻觉和妄想。


近义词：insane, deranged, mad, demented, delusional

反义词：sane, rational, lucid, normal

词性：形容词
