footpath:/ˈfʊtpɑːθ/| n. |人行道，小路|A path intended for pedestrians, often alongside a road.|例句：I walked along the footpath to the park. 我沿着人行道走到公园。|例句：The footpath was muddy after the rain. 雨后人行道很泥泞。|近义词：path, walkway, trail|反义词：roadway, carriageway
