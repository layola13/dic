sewin:/ˈsoʊɪn/ | n. |缝纫；缝制|The act of sewing; stitching together fabric or other materials using a needle and thread.  Sewing is a useful skill for making clothes and repairing garments.|例句：She enjoys sewing as a hobby. 她喜欢缝纫作为一种爱好。|例句：The seamstress is skilled in sewing intricate designs.  这位女裁缝擅长缝制复杂的花纹。|近义词：stitching, needlework |反义词：ripping, tearing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I sew every Saturday. = 我每个星期六都缝纫。
She sews beautiful dresses. = 她缝制美丽的连衣裙。

一般过去时:
I sewed a button on my shirt. = 我在我的衬衫上缝了一个纽扣。
They sewed the tear in the fabric. = 他们缝补了布料上的裂缝。


现在完成时:
I have sewed three dresses this month. = 这个月我已经缝了三件连衣裙。
She has sewed many quilts for her family. = 她已经为她的家人缝制了很多棉被。


过去完成时:
I had sewed the dress before the party. = 我在派对之前已经缝好了裙子。
They had sewed all the costumes by the time the play began. = 在戏剧开始的时候，他们已经缝制好了所有的戏服。


将来时:
I will sew a new curtain tomorrow. = 我明天将会缝制一个新的窗帘。
She will sew a patch on her jeans. = 她将在她的牛仔裤上缝一个补丁。


虚拟语气:
