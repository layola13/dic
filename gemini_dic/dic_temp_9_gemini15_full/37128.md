come:/kʌm/| /kəm/ | vi. & vt. | 来；来到；出现；发生 | To move or travel toward the speaker or a specified place.  |例句：Come here, please. 请到这边来。

例句：The bus is coming. 公车来了。

近义词：arrive, approach, appear | 反义词：go, leave, depart | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I come to school every day. 我每天都来学校。
They come to visit us every weekend. 他们每个周末都来看望我们。


一般过去时:
He came to my party last night. 他昨晚参加了我的派对。
She came home late yesterday. 她昨天回家很晚。


现在完成时:
I have come to realize the importance of this. 我已经意识到这件事的重要性。
We have come a long way since then. 从那时起，我们已经走了很长一段路。


过去完成时:
I had come to understand her feelings by then. 到那时我已经理解了她的感受。
They had come to an agreement before the meeting. 会议之前他们已经达成了协议。


将来时:
I will come to see you tomorrow. 我明天来看你。
She will come back soon. 她很快就会回来。


虚拟语气:
If you came with me, it would be more fun. 如果你和我一起来，会更有趣。
I wish he would come to the party. 我希望他会来参加派对。


被动语态:
The news came as a surprise. 这个消息令人意外。(被动含义，news本身不能主动come)
This decision was come to after much deliberation. 这个决定是在经过深思熟虑之后做出的。(被动含义，decision本身不能主动come)


疑问句:
When will you come? 你什么时候来？
How did you come to know that? 你是怎么知道那件事的？


否定句:
He didn't come to the meeting. 他没有来参加会议。
She won't come to my house tonight. 她今晚不会来我家。


PHRASE->
come about: 发生
come across: 偶然遇见
come along: 一起去；进展
come back: 回来
come up with: 想出 (主意、计划等)

词源:
The word "come" originates from the Old English word "cuman," which itself has roots in Proto-Germanic and further back to Proto-Indo-European.  The various meanings and uses have evolved over centuries, reflecting the fundamental nature of movement and arrival.
