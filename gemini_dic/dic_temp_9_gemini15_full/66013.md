examining:/ɪɡˈzæmɪnɪŋ/| v.|检查；调查；仔细观察|The act of carefully looking at something or someone to understand it better.  It involves a thorough and detailed inspection.|例句：The doctor is examining the patient. 医生正在检查病人。|例句：The detective is examining the crime scene.  侦探正在检查犯罪现场。|近义词：inspecting, scrutinizing, investigating|反义词：ignoring, overlooking

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is examining the evidence. = 他正在检查证据。
She examines patients every day. = 她每天都检查病人。

一般过去时:
