negate:/nɪˈɡeɪt/| vt.|否定；取消；使无效|To make ineffective or nonexistent;  to deny the truth or existence of something.|例句：The increase in pay negated the effect of inflation. 加薪抵消了通货膨胀的影响。

例句：His evidence negated her claims. 他的证词否定了她的说法。


近义词：contradict, deny, invalidate, annul, nullify|反义词：affirm, confirm, validate, support|动词
