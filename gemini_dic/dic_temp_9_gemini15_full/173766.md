quasi-oriental:/ˈkwɑːziˌɔːriˈɛntəl/ | adj. |似东方的，准东方的 |  Appearing to be or suggesting something from the Orient, often in a superficial or stereotypical way.  It implies an imitation or incomplete representation of Eastern cultures.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting a romanticized or exoticized view of the East. | Example:  The restaurant's décor was quasi-oriental, featuring vaguely Asian-inspired motifs. 这家餐厅的装饰是似东方的，带有含糊不清的亚洲风格图案。 | 近义词：pseudo-oriental, orientalizing | 反义词：authentically Eastern, truly Oriental
