lose it: /luːz ɪt/ | phrasal verb | 发疯；失去控制；崩溃；忍无可忍 | To lose one's composure or self-control; to go crazy or insane; to become extremely angry or upset.  |例句：He completely lost it when he heard the news. 他听到这个消息后彻底崩溃了。|  She almost lost it during the stressful exam.  她在压力巨大的考试中差点崩溃。 |近义词：snap, crack up, freak out, go ballistic, fly off the handle | 反义词：remain calm, keep one's cool


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He loses it whenever he sees a spider. = 他一看到蜘蛛就失去控制。
They lose it easily under pressure. = 他们在压力下很容易崩溃。


一般过去时:
I lost it when my phone broke. = 我的手机坏了，我当时崩溃了。
She lost it at the party and started shouting. = 她在派对上失去控制，开始大喊大叫。


现在完成时:
He's lost it completely since his wife left him. = 自从妻子离开他后，他彻底崩溃了。
I've never lost it like that before. = 我以前从未像那样失去控制过。


过去完成时:
She had lost it before I even arrived. = 我到之前她就崩溃了。
He had lost it completely by the time the meeting ended. = 到会议结束时，他已经彻底崩溃了。


将来时:
I think I'll lose it if I have to wait much longer. = 我想如果我还要等更久的话，我会崩溃的。
He'll probably lose it if he fails the exam again. = 如果他再次考试不及格，他可能会崩溃。


虚拟语气:
If I were in his shoes, I think I'd lose it. = 如果我处在他的位置，我想我会崩溃的。
I wish I hadn't lost it in front of everyone. = 我真希望我没有当着所有人的面崩溃。


被动语态:  (This phrasal verb is rarely used passively)  


疑问句:
Did he lose it when you told him? = 你告诉他时，他崩溃了吗？
Will she lose it if she doesn't get the promotion? = 如果她没有得到晋升，她会崩溃吗？


否定句:
He didn't lose it, even though he was very angry. = 尽管他非常生气，但他并没有失去控制。
She tried to stay calm and didn't lose it. = 她试图保持冷静，并没有崩溃。



PHRASE->
lose it completely = 完全崩溃
lose one's cool = 失去冷静
lose it over something = 为某事而崩溃
lose one's mind = 发疯
lose it big time = 彻底崩溃
