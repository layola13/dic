nonsuit:/ˈnɒnˌsuːt/| n. v.|驳回诉讼；自行撤诉；放弃诉讼|A nonsuit is a judgment in favor of the defendant.  It is entered when the plaintiff fails to appear in court, or fails to make a case.  As a verb, it means to discontinue or withdraw a legal action.|例句：The judge granted a nonsuit. = 法官批准了驳回诉讼。

The plaintiff was nonsuited for lack of evidence. = 原告因缺乏证据而被驳回诉讼。|近义词：dismissal, withdrawal, abandonment|反义词：prosecution, continuation
