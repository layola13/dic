orthographies:/ɔːrˈθɒɡrəfiz/ | n. | 正字法；拼写系统 | The study of spelling and writing systems.  It encompasses the rules and conventions for writing a language, including letter combinations, syllable structures, and word formation. Different orthographies can exist for the same language, reflecting historical developments or regional variations. | Example: The orthographies of English and French differ significantly.  英语和法语的正字法差异很大。 |近义词：spelling systems, writing systems | 反义词：None
