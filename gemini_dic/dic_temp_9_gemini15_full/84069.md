half-hearted:/ˈhæfˈhɑːtɪd/|/ˈhæfˈhɑːtɪd/| adj. |不热心的；敷衍的；三心二意的 |Showing little enthusiasm or interest.  It suggests a lack of commitment or effort. |例句：He gave a half-hearted attempt at the exam. 他考试敷衍了事。

He made a half-hearted apology. 他敷衍地道歉了。


近义词：lackadaisical, unenthusiastic, apathetic, perfunctory, lukewarm


反义词：enthusiastic, passionate, committed, dedicated, zealous


词源:  The word "half-hearted" is a compound word.  "Half" indicates a portion or incomplete nature, while "hearted" refers to the heart, symbolizing feelings, enthusiasm, and commitment. The combination literally means "having only half a heart" in the endeavor, implying a lack of full engagement.
