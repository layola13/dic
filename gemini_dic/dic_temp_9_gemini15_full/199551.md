single-minded:/ˈsɪŋɡəlˈmaɪndɪd/| adj.|一心一意的；专注的；顽固的|Having or showing a strong determination to achieve something, even if it involves ignoring other things.  It often implies a lack of flexibility or openness to other perspectives.|例句：He was single-minded in his pursuit of wealth. 他一心追求财富。|例句：Her single-minded devotion to her work impressed everyone. 她对工作的全神贯注给每个人留下了深刻印象。|近义词：devoted, determined, focused, resolute, tenacious|反义词：indecisive, fickle, wavering, distracted, unfocused


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is single-minded in his efforts. = 他在努力中一心一意。
They are single-minded in their pursuit of success. = 他们一心追求成功。

一般过去时:
She was single-minded in her approach to the problem. = 她处理问题的方式非常专注。
He was single-minded in his refusal to compromise. = 他坚决拒绝妥协。


现在完成时:
He has been single-minded in his dedication to the project. = 他一直对这个项目全心全意。
They have remained single-minded in their beliefs. = 他们一直坚定地坚持自己的信仰。


过去完成时:
She had been single-minded in her efforts to help him, even before he asked. = 甚至在他开口之前，她就一心一意地帮助他。
They had been single-minded in their opposition to the plan for years. = 多年来，他们一直坚定地反对这项计划。


将来时:
He will be single-minded in his attempt to win the competition. = 他将全力以赴赢得比赛。
She will remain single-minded in her pursuit of knowledge. = 她将继续一心追求知识。


虚拟语气:
If he were single-minded, he would have achieved his goal long ago. = 如果他一心一意，早就达到目标了。
I wish I were more single-minded in my studies. = 我希望我的学习更专注一些。


被动语态:
His efforts were seen as single-minded and effective. = 他的努力被认为是专注且有效的。
The approach was criticized for being too single-minded. = 这种方法因过于专注而受到批评。


疑问句:
Is he single-minded in his pursuit of this goal? = 他追求这个目标是否一心一意？
Are they single-minded enough to overcome the obstacles? = 他们是否足够专注以克服障碍？


否定句:
He was not single-minded in his approach; he was easily distracted. = 他的方法并不专注；他很容易分心。
They were not single-minded in their loyalty to the company. = 他们对公司的忠诚并不坚定。
