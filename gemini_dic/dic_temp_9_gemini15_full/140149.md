offed:/ɒft/| vt.| 提供；杀死；摆脱；|to offer something, especially in a casual or informal way; to kill someone; to get rid of something.|例句：He offed me a ride home. 他主动提出送我回家。|近义词：offered, provided, killed, disposed of|反义词：kept, retained, saved


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He often offers help to others. = 他经常帮助别人。
She offers a wide range of services. = 她提供各种各样的服务。

一般过去时:
They offered me a job. = 他们给我提供了一份工作。
He offered a prayer before the meal. = 饭前他做了一个祈祷。

现在完成时:
She has offered her support to the project. = 她已经为这个项目提供了支持。
They have offered many suggestions. = 他们已经提出了很多建议。

过去完成时:
He had offered to help before I asked. = 我还没开口他就主动提出帮忙了。
She had already offered her resignation. = 她已经辞职了。

将来时:
I will offer you a cup of tea. = 我会给你一杯茶。
They will offer a discount for early bookings. = 他们会为提前预订提供折扣。

虚拟语气:
If he offered me the job, I would accept it. = 如果他给我这份工作，我会接受。
I wish he had offered me some advice. = 我希望他当时给我一些建议。

被动语态:
A reward was offered for the return of the stolen goods. = 悬赏找回失窃的物品。
Many opportunities were offered to the graduates. = 毕业生们得到了很多机会。

疑问句:
Did he offer any explanation? = 他有解释吗？
What did they offer you? = 他们给你提供了什么？

否定句:
He didn't offer any resistance. = 他没有反抗。
They didn't offer any help. = 他们没有提供任何帮助。
