madreporic:/ˌmædrəˈpɒrɪk/| adj.|海星的筛板的；多孔的|Pertaining to or characteristic of the madreporite, a porous calcareous plate in echinoderms (such as starfish) that allows water to enter the water vascular system.  |The madreporic plate is crucial for the starfish's locomotion and feeding.  海星的筛板对于海星的运动和摄食至关重要。|porous, perforated|impervious, sealed
