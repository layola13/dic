sightseeing:/ˈsaɪtsiːɪŋ/| n. |观光，游览 |The act of visiting places of interest, especially as a tourist.  Sightseeing involves visiting and exploring places of interest for leisure or educational purposes. It often includes visiting historical sites, natural landmarks, museums, and other attractions. |例句：We spent the afternoon sightseeing in the old town. 我们在老城区观光度过了下午。

例句：Their sightseeing trip included visits to several castles and cathedrals. 他们的观光旅行包括参观了几座城堡和大教堂。


近义词：tourism, touring, travel, excursion |反义词：None (Antonyms are contextual and depend on the specific aspect of sightseeing being contrasted) |词性：名词
