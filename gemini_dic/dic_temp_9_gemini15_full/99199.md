interclasping:/ˌɪntərˈklæspɪŋ/ | v. | 交叉紧握；互相紧扣 | The act of clasping or holding together, especially in a way that interlocks or crosses.  It often describes limbs or other elongated objects. | Example: The lovers were interclasping hands as they walked along the beach.  恋人沿着海滩散步时，十指紧扣。


The dancers' arms were interclasping during the final pose. 舞蹈演员在最后的姿势中双臂交叉紧握。

近义词：interlocking, entwining, interweaving


反义词：separating, unclasping, disengaging


词性:动词
