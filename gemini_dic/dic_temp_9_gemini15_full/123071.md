moneyball:/ˈmʌniˌbɔːl/ | n. | 金钱球；数据分析决策法 | A system of evaluating baseball players based on statistical analysis rather than traditional scouting methods.  This approach, popularized by the Oakland A's general manager Billy Beane, focuses on identifying undervalued players who can deliver high performance at a lower cost. | Example: The team's success was attributed to their innovative use of moneyball.  球队成功的秘诀在于他们创新地使用了金钱球方法。 | 近义词: sabermetrics, statistical analysis | 反义词: traditional scouting, intuition
