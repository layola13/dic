lifter:/ˈlɪftər/| n. |举重者；起重机；提升装置|A person who lifts weights; a machine that lifts heavy objects.|例句：He's a strong lifter and can easily lift that weight. 他是一位强壮的举重运动员，可以轻松举起那个重量。|例句：The construction workers used a lifter to move the heavy beams. 建筑工人使用起重机搬运重型梁。|近义词：weightlifter, crane, hoist|反义词：lowerer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lifts weights every day. = 他每天举重。
The crane lifts heavy objects. = 起重机起吊重物。

一般过去时:
He lifted the box onto the shelf. = 他把箱子抬到了架子上。
They lifted the car with a jack. = 他们用千斤顶把车抬了起来。

现在完成时:
He has lifted more weight than ever before. = 他举起的重量比以往任何时候都多。
The company has lifted its sales target. = 公司已提高了销售目标。

过去完成时:
He had lifted the heavy stone before he felt the pain. = 他举起那块石头后才感到疼痛。
They had lifted the roof before the rain started. = 下雨前他们已经把屋顶抬起来了。

将来时:
He will lift the car tomorrow. = 他明天将把车抬起来。
They will lift the ban on imports next week. = 他们下周将解除进口禁令。

虚拟语气:
If he were stronger, he could lift that heavier weight. = 如果他更强壮，他就能举起更重的重量。
I wish I could lift that heavy box. = 我真希望我能搬起那个沉重的箱子。

被动语态:
The heavy box was lifted by the crane. = 重箱子由起重机吊起。
The sales target was lifted by the board of directors. = 销售目标由董事会提高。

疑问句:
Can you lift this heavy suitcase? = 你能提起这个沉重的行李箱吗？
How much weight can the lifter lift? = 起重机能举起多少重量？

否定句:
He cannot lift that heavy rock. = 他不能搬动那块巨石。
The crane couldn't lift the damaged car. = 起重机无法吊起损坏的汽车。
