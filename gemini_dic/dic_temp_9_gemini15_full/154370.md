permalinks:/ˈpɜːrməˌlɪŋks/ | n. |永久链接|A permalink is a permanent link to a specific piece of online content, such as a blog post or a web page.  It remains valid even if the content's URL changes or is reorganized. |Example:  This blog post has a permalink so you can easily share it with others. 这篇博客文章有一个永久链接，因此您可以轻松地与他人分享它。|近义词：permanent URL, stable URL |反义词：temporary link, dynamic URL |名词
