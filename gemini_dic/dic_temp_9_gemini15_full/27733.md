cannibal:/ˈkænɪbəl/ | n. |食人族；食人者 | A cannibal is a person who eats human flesh.  Cannibalism is the practice of eating human flesh. |Example:  The tribe was known for its cannibalistic practices.  这个部落以其食人习俗而闻名。 |近义词：man-eater, anthropophagist |反义词：vegetarian, herbivore
