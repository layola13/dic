predoctorate: /ˌpriːˈdɒktərɪt/ | adj. | 博士前的；博士预科的 | Relating to the period of study before a doctoral degree is awarded.  It refers to the studies and work undertaken by a student before they become a doctoral candidate. |例句：She completed her predoctorate research on the impact of social media on political polarization. 她完成了关于社交媒体对政治两极分化影响的博士前研究。

例句：He is currently in the predoctorate program, working towards his PhD. 他目前正在攻读博士预科课程，努力争取获得博士学位。


近义词：pre-doctoral


反义词：postdoctoral


词性：形容词
