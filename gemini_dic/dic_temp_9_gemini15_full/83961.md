half-contemptuously:/ˌhæf kənˈtɛmptʃuəsli/ | adj. | 半带轻蔑地，带着几分轻蔑地 | Expressing a mixture of contempt and something else, often indifference or amusement.  It suggests a restrained or partial expression of contempt, not full-blown scorn. |例句：He looked at her half-contemptuously, a slight curl to his lip. 他半带轻蔑地看了她一眼，嘴唇微微上扬。

He dismissed her concerns half-contemptuously, waving his hand dismissively. 他轻蔑地挥了挥手，不以为然地打发了她的担忧。


近义词：disdainfully, scornfully, superciliously, condescendingly | 反义词：respectfully, admiringly, approvingly


词性：形容词
