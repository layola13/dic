resynthesize:/ˌriːˈsɪnθəsaɪz/| vt.|重新合成，再合成|To synthesize again; to combine elements to create something new, often from previously separated or broken-down components.|例句：The chemist attempted to resynthesize the complex molecule after its initial breakdown. 化学家试图在其最初分解后重新合成该复杂分子。

例句：The artist resynthesized his old paintings into a new, unified artwork. 艺术家将他的旧画重新合成了一件新的、统一的艺术品。


近义词：recreate, recombine, reconstruct|反义词：decompose, break down, analyze|动词
