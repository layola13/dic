gasiform:/ˈɡæsɪfɔːrm/| adj. |气态的，气形的|Existing in the form of a gas.  A gasiform substance is one that exists in the gaseous state. |Example:  The gasiform substance escaped from the container. 气态物质从容器中逸出。 The transformation of a solid directly to a gas is called sublimation; the reverse is called deposition.  固体直接转化为气体的过程称为升华；反之称为凝华。|近义词：gaseous, vaporous|反义词：solid, liquid
