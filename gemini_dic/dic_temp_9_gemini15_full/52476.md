dietetic:/ˌdaɪəˈtɛtɪk/| adj.|饮食的；营养的；治疗饮食的|Relating to or used in the treatment of disease or improvement of health by diet.|The dietetic department provides nutritional counseling.|近义词：nutritional, dietary|反义词：unhealthy, malnutritional

词源:
From  Greek *diaita* (diet), from *diaita*  "manner of living," from *diaita* "a way of life, mode of living," from *diaitan* "to lead one's life, live," from *dia* "through, between" + *aitein* "to ask, seek," from Proto-Indo-European  *(h₁)ei-/* "to go."  The suffix *-etic* indicates "relating to" or "pertaining to."
