ejects:/ɪˈdʒɛkts/ | vt. |喷射；弹出；驱逐 | To force or propel (something) out or away from something.  |例句：The volcano ejected a massive cloud of ash.  火山喷发出巨大的火山灰云。|例句：The machine automatically ejects the finished product. 机器自动弹出成品。|近义词：expel, discharge, eject  |反义词：insert, absorb, receive |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine ejects the coins. = 机器弹出硬币。
He ejects the smoke from his lungs. = 他从肺里排出烟雾。


一般过去时:
The pilot ejected from the plane. = 飞行员从飞机上弹射出来。
The volcano ejected lava and ash. = 火山喷出了熔岩和火山灰。


现在完成时:
The rocket has ejected its boosters. = 火箭已经抛弃了助推器。
She has ejected the unwanted files from her computer. = 她已经从电脑中删除了不需要的文件。


过去完成时:
The pilot had ejected before the plane crashed. = 飞机坠毁之前，飞行员已经弹射出来了。
The volcano had ejected a large amount of ash before the eruption ceased. = 火山在喷发停止之前已经喷出了大量的火山灰。


将来时:
The capsule will eject the astronauts into space. = 胶囊将把宇航员送入太空。
The system will automatically eject the disc when finished. = 系统完成后会自动弹出光盘。


虚拟语气:
If the pilot had ejected earlier, he might have survived. = 如果飞行员早点弹射，他可能就活下来了。
If the system ejected the disc immediately, there would be no error. = 如果系统立即弹出光盘，就不会出现错误。


被动语态:
The passengers were ejected from the vehicle during the accident. = 事故中乘客被从车里甩了出来。
The waste products are ejected into the atmosphere. = 废物被排放到大气中。


疑问句:
Does the printer automatically eject the paper? = 打印机是否会自动送出纸张？
Will the seat eject if I pull this lever? = 如果我拉动这个拉杆，座椅会弹射出来吗？


否定句:
The machine didn't eject the paper. = 机器没有送出纸张。
The pilot failed to eject from the plane. = 飞行员未能从飞机上弹射出来。



PHRASE->
eject from = 从...弹出/驱逐
eject a projectile = 发射弹丸
eject a disc = 弹出光盘
eject a passenger = 将乘客驱逐
eject oneself = 弹射自己
