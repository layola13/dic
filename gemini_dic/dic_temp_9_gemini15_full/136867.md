nonskiers:/ˈnɑːnˌskiːərz/ | n. |非滑雪者 |People who do not ski.  This word is a compound noun formed from "non-" (meaning "not") and "skiers" (people who ski). It refers to a group of people who do not participate in the activity of skiing. |Example: The ski resort also caters to nonskiers with ice skating and sleigh rides.  这个滑雪胜地也为非滑雪者提供滑冰和雪橇等活动。|近义词：non-skiing individuals, people who don't ski |反义词：skiers |名词
