hypothesist:/haɪˈpɒθəsɪst/ | n. | 假说者，假设提出者 | A person who formulates hypotheses.  Someone who proposes or suggests a hypothesis. | Example: The hypothesist presented her findings to the scientific community.  假说提出者向科学界展示了她的研究结果。|近义词：theorist, speculator |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who deals only in proven facts.
