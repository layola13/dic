coproduction:/ˌkoʊprəˈdʌkʃən/ | n. |合作制作，联合制作 |The joint production of a film, television program, or other media product by two or more companies or organizations.  This often involves sharing costs, resources, and creative control. | 例句：The movie was a coproduction between a Hollywood studio and a French film company. 这部电影是好莱坞制片厂和一家法国电影公司合作制作的。

The BBC and HBO entered into a coproduction agreement for the new series.  英国广播公司和HBO就新剧集达成了合作制作协议。


近义词：joint production, collaboration, partnership | 反义词：sole production, independent production | 词性：名词
