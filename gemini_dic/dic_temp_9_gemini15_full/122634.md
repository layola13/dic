modification:/ˌmɒdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| /ˌmɑːdəfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |修改，变更；修饰；改变 |The act of modifying something.  It involves making changes or alterations to something existing, often to improve it, adapt it, or correct it.  This can apply to physical objects, processes, systems, or even ideas. |例句：The car requires some modifications before it can be used on the road. 这辆车需要一些改装才能上路。

The software update includes several bug modifications. 软件更新包含一些错误修改。


近义词：alteration, amendment, adjustment, revision, change |反义词：preservation, maintenance, conservation |词性:n.


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The engineer makes modifications to the design. = 工程师对设计进行修改。
She frequently makes modifications to her recipes. = 她经常修改她的食谱。


一般过去时:
They made significant modifications to the building. = 他们对建筑物进行了重大修改。
He made a modification to his presentation. = 他修改了他的演示文稿。


现在完成时:
We have made several modifications to the process. = 我们已经对流程进行了几处修改。
She has made a few modifications to her schedule. = 她已经对她的日程安排做了一些修改。


过去完成时:
They had already made modifications to the contract before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就已经修改了合同。
He had made a modification to the plan before presenting it. = 他在提交计划之前已经修改了计划。


将来时:
We will make necessary modifications to the system next week. = 我们下周将对系统进行必要的修改。
She will make modifications to her essay before submission. = 她将在提交论文前修改她的论文。


虚拟语气:
If I had the tools, I would make the necessary modifications. = 如果我有工具，我会进行必要的修改。
It would be better if you made a few modifications to your approach. = 如果你对你的方法做一些修改会更好。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
