grozing: /ˈɡroʊzɪŋ/ | n. |  修剪(树枝等)；粗糙的表面；（非正式）粗鲁的行为 |  The act of trimming or pruning, especially of trees or shrubs; a rough or uneven surface; (informal) rude behavior. | 例句：The gardener spent the afternoon grozing the overgrown hedges. 园丁花了一下午修剪杂草丛生的树篱。 | 近义词：pruning, trimming, clipping, roughening | 反义词：smoothing, polishing
