patently:/ˈpeɪtəntli/| adv.|明显地；显而易见地|Clearly and obviously; in a way that is easily seen or understood.  It emphasizes the obviousness and undeniable nature of something.|例句：His lies were patently false. 他的谎言显然是假的。

例句：The solution to the problem was patently obvious.  这个问题的解决方法显而易见。


近义词：obviously, clearly, evidently, manifestly, plainly


反义词：subtly, secretly, obscurely, vaguely


词性:副词
