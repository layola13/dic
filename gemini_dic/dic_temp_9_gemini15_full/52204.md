diastema:/daɪˈæstiːmə/| n. |牙缝，齿隙|A space or gap between two teeth.  This is often found between the two upper central incisors. |例句：She has a noticeable diastema between her two front teeth. 她两颗门牙之间有明显的缝隙。 |例句：The orthodontist closed the diastema with braces. 正畸医生用牙套关闭了牙缝。|近义词：gap, space, interval|反义词：closeness, contact
