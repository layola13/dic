ravager:/ˈrævɪdʒər/| n. |破坏者，蹂躏者，劫掠者 |Someone or something that causes great damage or destruction.  A ravager can be a person, animal, or even a natural force. |例句：The hurricane was a ravager, leaving behind a trail of destruction.  飓风是一个破坏者，留下一条破坏的痕迹。

The locust swarm was a terrible ravager of the crops. 蝗虫群是庄稼可怕的破坏者。


近义词：destroyer, plunderer, devastator, wrecker |反义词：protector, preserver, savior |名词
