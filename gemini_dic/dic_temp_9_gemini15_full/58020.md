drizzly:/ˈdrɪzli/| adj.|毛毛雨的，细雨蒙蒙的|Slightly raining; characterized by a fine, light rain.  |例句：It's a drizzly day, perfect for staying inside and reading. 今天细雨蒙蒙，非常适合待在家里看书。

例句：The drizzly weather made the hike a little unpleasant.  细雨绵绵的天气使得徒步旅行有点不愉快。


近义词：misty, showery, sprinkling

反义词：sunny, dry, arid

词性:形容词
