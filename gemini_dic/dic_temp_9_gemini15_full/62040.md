encamp:/ɪnˈkæmp/| vt. vi. n.|扎营；宿营；|To set up camp; to live in a camp.|例句：The scouts encamped by the river. 侦察兵在河边扎营。 The army encamped on the plain.  军队在平原上扎营。|近义词：camp, pitch camp|反义词：depart, leave


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They encamp in the forest every summer. = 他们每年夏天都在森林里扎营。
We encamp near the lake for a week. = 我们在湖边扎营一周。

一般过去时:
The soldiers encamped on the hilltop. = 士兵们在山顶上扎营。
The travelers encamped by the roadside. = 旅行者们在路边扎营。

现在完成时:
They have encamped in this area for many years. = 他们在这个地区扎营多年了。
We have encamped by the river twice this month. = 这个月我们已经在河边扎营两次了。

过去完成时:
The army had encamped before nightfall. = 军队在天黑之前就扎营了。
The hikers had encamped near the waterfall before the storm. = 徒步旅行者在暴风雨来临之前就在瀑布附近扎营了。

将来时:
We will encamp under the stars tonight. = 我们今晚将在星空下扎营。
They will encamp near the mountain pass tomorrow. = 他们明天将在山口附近扎营。

虚拟语气:
If we had more time, we would encamp for a longer period. = 如果我们有更多的时间，我们会扎营更长时间。
I wish we could encamp in a more scenic location. = 我希望我们能在风景更优美的地方扎营。

被动语态:
The campsite was encamped by the tourists.  = 营地被游客们占领了。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，更常用的表达是：The tourists encamped at the campsite.)
The area was encamped by the army for a month. = 这个地区被军队占领了一个月。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，更常用的表达是：The army encamped in the area for a month.)

疑问句:
Where did they encamp last night? = 他们昨晚在哪里扎营？
Will they encamp near the village? = 他们会在村庄附近扎营吗？

否定句:
They did not encamp in the valley. = 他们没有在山谷里扎营。
We will not encamp near the river because of the mosquitoes. = 我们不会在河边扎营，因为那里有很多蚊子。
