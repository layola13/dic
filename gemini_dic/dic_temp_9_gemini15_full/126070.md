musters:/ˈmʌstərz/| vt. n.|集合；召集；鼓起；勉强；积聚；|To bring together, assemble; to summon or call forth; to gather or collect; to summon up (courage, strength, etc.);|例句：The general mustered his troops before the battle. 将军在战斗前集合了他的军队。

例句：She mustered the courage to speak to her boss. 她鼓起勇气和她老板说话。


近义词：gather, assemble, collect, summon, rally|反义词：disperse, scatter, dissipate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sergeant regularly musters his troops. = 中士定期集合他的部队。
He musters his strength to lift the heavy box. = 他鼓起力气搬起那个沉重的箱子。


一般过去时:
The farmer mustered his crops for the market. = 农民把他的庄稼收集起来准备去市场卖。
She mustered her courage and confessed her feelings. = 她鼓起勇气承认了自己的感情。


现在完成时:
The general has mustered his forces for the final assault. = 将军已经集合了他的部队准备进行最后的攻击。
He has mustered the courage to face his fears.  = 他已经鼓起勇气去面对他的恐惧。


过去完成时:
They had mustered all their resources before the project began. = 他们在项目开始前已经集合了他们所有的资源。
She had mustered the strength to finish the race. = 她已经积攒了足够的力气完成比赛。


将来时:
The company will muster its employees for a meeting next week. = 公司下周将召集员工开会。
He will muster all his skills for the competition. = 他将为比赛展现出他所有的技能。


虚拟语气:
If he mustered enough courage, he would confront his opponent. = 如果他鼓起足够的勇气，他就会面对他的对手。
I wish I could muster the energy to clean the house. = 我希望我能有足够的精力打扫房子。


被动语态:
The troops were mustered at dawn. = 部队在黎明时分被集合起来。
His resources were mustered for the relief effort. = 他的资源被集合起来用于救济工作。


疑问句:
Can you muster the support you need? = 你能召集到你需要的支持吗？
Will they be able to muster the necessary funds? = 他们能够筹集到必要的资金吗？


否定句:
He couldn't muster the energy to go out. = 他提不起劲儿出去。
They failed to muster enough votes to win the election. = 他们未能获得足够的选票赢得选举。
