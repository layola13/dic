semi-occasional:/ˌsɛmi əˈkeɪʒənəl/| adj.| 间或的，偶尔的 |  Relatively infrequent; occurring or done from time to time but not regularly.  It implies a frequency somewhere between "rare" and "regular".|

例句：

He makes semi-occasional visits to his parents. = 他偶尔去看望父母。

She engages in semi-occasional charity work. = 她间或做一些慈善工作。


近义词：infrequent, occasional, sporadic, intermittent

反义词：frequent, regular, constant, habitual


词性:形容词
