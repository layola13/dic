poses:/ˈpoʊzɪz/| n.|姿势，姿态；摆姿势；提出（问题等）|The word "poses" is the plural form of the noun "pose," referring to the position of the body or the way someone holds themselves. It can also refer to the act of striking a particular position, especially for a photograph or painting, or to the act of presenting something, such as a question or problem.  |例句：The model struck several elegant poses for the photographer.  模特为摄影师摆了几个优雅的姿势。

The professor posed a challenging question to the class. 教授向课堂提出了一个具有挑战性的问题。|近义词：attitudes, postures, stances, propositions|反义词：naturalness (lack of posing), spontaneity (lack of posing)|名词
