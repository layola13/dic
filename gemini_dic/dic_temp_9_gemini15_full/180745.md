reguided:/ˌriːˈɡaɪdɪd/| v. |重新引导，再指导 |To guide again or anew; to provide further guidance or direction.  |例句：The lost hikers were re-guided to safety by the experienced ranger.  迷路的徒步旅行者在经验丰富的护林员的带领下重新回到了安全地带。|例句：The teacher re-guided the students through the complex problem. 老师再次引导学生们解决这个复杂的问题。|近义词：redirect, reroute, reorient, reinstruct |反义词：misguide, mislead
