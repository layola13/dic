circumnavigates:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡeɪt/| vt.|环绕…航行；绕…航行|To sail or travel all the way around (something, especially a large body of water).  The verb implies a complete circuit.|例句：Magellan was the first person to circumnavigate the globe. 麦哲伦是第一个环球航行的人。

例句：The explorer spent years trying to circumnavigate the treacherous Arctic waters. 探险家花了数年时间试图绕过危险的北极水域。


近义词：encircle, go around, sail around, navigate around

反义词：cross directly, traverse directly
