computerize:/kəmˈpjuːtəraɪz/ | vt. |使电脑化；用电脑控制 | To control or operate (a process or system) by means of a computer.  |例句：The company decided to computerize its accounting system. 公司决定将其会计系统电脑化。

例句：They have computerized the entire factory floor. 他们已经将整个工厂车间电脑化了。


近义词：automate, digitize | 反义词：manualize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They computerize their inventory management. = 他们用电脑管理库存。
The company computerizes its payroll system. = 公司用电脑管理工资系统。


一般过去时:
We computerized our billing system last year. = 我们去年将账单系统电脑化了。
The factory computerized its production line in 2020. = 工厂在2020年将生产线电脑化了。


现在完成时:
They have computerized many of their operations. = 他们已经将许多业务电脑化了。
The bank has computerized its customer service. = 这家银行已经将其客户服务电脑化了。


过去完成时:
The company had computerized its database before the merger. = 公司在合并之前已经将其数据库电脑化了。
We had computerized our records before the audit. = 我们在审计之前已经将记录电脑化了。


将来时:
We will computerize our marketing department next year. = 我们明年将把市场部电脑化。
The school will computerize its library system soon. = 学校很快就会将其图书馆系统电脑化。


虚拟语气:
If we computerized the system, it would be more efficient. = 如果我们把系统电脑化，效率会更高。
I wish we had computerized our accounting years ago. = 我希望几年前我们就将会计工作电脑化了。


被动语态:

疑问句:
