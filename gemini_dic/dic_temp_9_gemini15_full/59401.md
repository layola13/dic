earthbag: /ˈɜːrθbæɡ/ | n. |土袋；装土的袋子 | A bag filled with earth used in earthbag construction.  Earthbag construction is a building technique using filled bags as a building material.  |例句：They built their house using earthbags and barbed wire.  他们用土袋和铁丝网建造了他们的房子。
|近义词：filled sack, earth-filled bag |反义词：无 |词性: 名词
