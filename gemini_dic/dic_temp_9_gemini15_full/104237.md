jurisdictions:/ˌdʒʊərɪsˈdɪkʃənz/| n. |司法权；管辖权；权限|The area of authority or control; the right to administer justice.  It can refer to a geographical area, a subject matter, or a group of people.|例句：The court lacked jurisdiction over the case because the crime occurred outside its territory.  这个法院对该案没有管辖权，因为犯罪发生在其管辖区域之外。

例句：The matter falls under the jurisdiction of the environmental protection agency.  此事属于环境保护机构的管辖范围。


近义词：authority, purview, competence, control|反义词：lack of authority, powerlessness|词性：noun
