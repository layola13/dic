flyest:/ˈflaɪɪst/| adj.|最酷的，最棒的，最时尚的 |The word "flyest" is a slang term, primarily used in African American Vernacular English (AAVE), meaning "the most stylish, fashionable, or cool." It's the superlative form of "fly," which itself is slang meaning stylish or cool.  |例句：His outfit was the flyest I've ever seen. 他的衣服是我见过的最酷的。
