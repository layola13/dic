opinionator:/əˈpɪnjəneɪtər/ | n. |好为人师者；固执己见者；专断的人 | A person who is opinionated and expresses their views forcefully and frequently, often regardless of others' opinions.  | He's a real opinionator; he never listens to anyone else's point of view. 他是个十足的好为人师者；他从来不听别人的观点。|  近义词：dogmatist, authoritarian, bossy, know-it-all | 反义词：listener, open-minded person
