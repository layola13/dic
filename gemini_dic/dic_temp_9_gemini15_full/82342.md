grow-a-pair:/ɡroʊ ə pɛər/| phrasal verb|  振作起来；拿出勇气；勇敢点 | This informal idiom encourages someone to be brave, courageous, and assertive, especially in a challenging or frightening situation. It implies that the person lacks the necessary courage or confidence. |例句：You need to grow a pair and tell him you're not interested. 你需要鼓起勇气告诉他你对他没兴趣。


例句：Just grow a pair and ask her out!  勇敢点，去约她吧！


近义词：man up, be brave, be courageous, show some guts, toughen up


反义词：cower, be timid, be cowardly, be faint-hearted


词性：phrasal verb

USAGE->
(由于grow a pair是一个习语，而不是一个可以进行各种时态变化的动词，因此无法按传统语法时态进行例句分析。)


PHRASE->
grow a pair and face it: 振作起来面对它
grow a pair and tell him: 振起勇气告诉他
grow a pair and stand up for yourself: 振起勇气为自己挺身而出
grow a pair and do what's right: 振起勇气做正确的事
He needs to grow a pair and take responsibility: 他需要勇敢承担责任
