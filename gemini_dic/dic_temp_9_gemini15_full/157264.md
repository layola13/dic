pinny:/ˈpɪni/| n.|别针；胸针；围嘴|A pinny is a small, usually decorative pin, often used to fasten clothing or as a brooch.  It can also refer to a bib, especially one worn by a baby.  |例句：She fastened her shawl with a pretty pinny. 她用一个漂亮的别针系上了她的披肩。
She put a pinny on the baby to protect its clothes. 她给婴儿戴上围嘴以保护它的衣服。|近义词：brooch, pin, bib|反义词：N/A
