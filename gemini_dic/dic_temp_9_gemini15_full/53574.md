disbend:/dɪsˈbɛnd/| vt.|解散；遣散|To officially end the existence of (a group or organization); to dismiss or disperse (a group of people).|例句：The army was disbanded after the war.  战争结束后，军队被解散了。|例句：The protesters were quickly disbanded by the police.  抗议者很快被警方驱散了。|近义词：dismiss, disperse, dissolve, break up|反义词：assemble, gather, convene|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government disbands the committee every year. = 政府每年解散该委员会。
The teacher disbands the class after the bell rings. = 铃响后老师解散了班级。

一般过去时:
They disbanded the club after several members left. = 几个成员离开后，他们解散了俱乐部。
The company disbanded its research team due to budget cuts. = 由于预算削减，该公司解散了其研究团队。

现在完成时:
The band has disbanded after their final concert. = 乐队在最后一场音乐会后解散了。
The organization has disbanded due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，该组织已经解散了。

过去完成时:
The team had disbanded before the championship game. = 在冠军赛之前，球队就已经解散了。
The parliament had disbanded before the new elections. = 在新选举之前，议会就已经解散了。

将来时:
The school will disband the drama club next year. = 学校明年将解散戏剧俱乐部。
The army will disband after the peace treaty is signed. = 和平条约签署后，军队将解散。

虚拟语气:
If the project failed, they would disband the team. = 如果项目失败，他们就会解散团队。
I wish they hadn't disbanded the project so quickly. = 我真希望他们不要那么快就解散了项目。

被动语态:
The club was disbanded due to low membership. = 由于会员人数过少，俱乐部被解散了。
The protest was quickly disbanded by the authorities. = 抗议活动很快被当局驱散了。

疑问句:
Will the committee be disbanded next month? = 委员会下个月会被解散吗？
Was the team disbanded because of poor performance? = 球队是因为表现不佳而被解散的吗？

否定句:
They did not disband the group despite the internal conflicts. = 尽管存在内部冲突，他们并没有解散该团体。
The government will not disband the police force. = 政府不会解散警力。
