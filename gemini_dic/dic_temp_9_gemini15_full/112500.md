loury:/ˈlaʊri/| adj.|阴沉的，忧郁的|Gloomy, sullen, and irritable.  The word suggests a persistent, somewhat brooding unhappiness.|例句：He was in a loury mood after the argument.  他争吵后心情阴沉。|例句：The loury weather matched his disposition.  阴沉的天气与他的心情相符。|近义词：gloomy, sullen, morose, grumpy, surly|反义词：cheerful, happy, jovial, sunny
