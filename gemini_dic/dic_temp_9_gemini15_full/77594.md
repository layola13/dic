geek-out:/ɡiːk aʊt/| v.|极度兴奋；狂热；沉迷于|To become extremely enthusiastic or excited about something, often related to a specific interest or topic.  This usually involves intense discussion or demonstration of knowledge.|例句：He totally geeked out when he saw the new Star Wars trailer. 他看到新的星球大战预告片时非常兴奋。

例句：We geeked out over the latest developments in artificial intelligence. 我们对人工智能的最新发展感到非常兴奋。


近义词：get excited, enthuse, be passionate about

反义词：be indifferent to, be apathetic towards
