remapping:/ˌriːˈmæpɪŋ/| n. |重新映射，重新规划|The process of changing the way something is mapped or represented, often in a computer or electronic system.  It can involve reassigning functions, adjusting parameters, or altering the structure of a map or data representation. |例句：The engineers performed a remapping of the engine's control unit to improve fuel efficiency. 工程师对发动机的控制单元进行了重新映射以提高燃油效率。

例句：The software update included a remapping of the keyboard shortcuts. 软件更新包括键盘快捷键的重新映射。


近义词：reconfiguration, reassignment, restructuring |反义词：original mapping, default mapping |名词
