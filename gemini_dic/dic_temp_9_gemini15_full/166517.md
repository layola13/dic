presatisfies:/ˌpriːˈsætɪsfaɪz/ | v. |预先满足；提前满足 |To satisfy beforehand or in advance.  This word is formed by combining "pre-" (meaning before) and "satisfies" (the third-person singular present tense of the verb "satisfy"). It implies fulfilling a requirement or condition before it's officially needed or expected. |Example: The company presatisfies all its customer's needs before launching a new product. 公司在推出新产品之前预先满足了所有客户的需求。|近义词：pre-fulfill, anticipate, fulfill in advance |反义词：postpone, delay, neglect
