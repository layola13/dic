heightened:/ˈhaɪtnd/ | adj. | 增强的；加剧的；提高的 |  Increased in intensity, amount, or degree.  It describes something that has become more intense, noticeable, or significant. |例句：The heightened security measures made everyone feel safer.  高度戒严措施使每个人都感到更安全。

例句：Her heightened sense of anxiety kept her awake at night. 她高度焦虑的情绪让她整夜睡不着觉。


近义词：intensified, amplified, elevated, increased, aggravated | 反义词：diminished, lessened, reduced, decreased | 词性:形容词
