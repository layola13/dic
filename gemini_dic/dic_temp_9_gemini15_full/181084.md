reindictments:/ˌriːɪnˈdaɪtmənts/| n.|再次起诉；重新提起公诉|The act of indicting someone again after a previous indictment has been dismissed or overturned.  It often happens when new evidence emerges or there are procedural errors in the first indictment.|例句：The reindictments came after the original charges were dismissed due to insufficient evidence.  再次起诉是在原先的指控因证据不足而被驳回后发生的。|近义词：reinstatement of charges, renewed indictments|反义词：acquittal, dismissal of charges|名词
