overplotted:/ˌoʊvərˈplɑːtɪd/ | adj. | 过度绘制的，信息过载的 |  Describes a graph or chart where too much data has been added, making it difficult to interpret or understand.  It implies a cluttered or confusing visual representation due to excessive information. | The graph was overplotted with so many data points that it was impossible to discern any trends.  这幅图表绘制了过多的数据点，以至于无法辨别任何趋势。 | cluttered, congested, overloaded | simplified, clarified  | 形容词
