rabelaisian:/ˌræbəˈleɪziən/| adj.| 狂饮的；放荡不羁的；粗俗滑稽的 | Relating to or characteristic of François Rabelais, his works, or their style, which is marked by exuberant humor, bawdy language, and grotesque imagery.  The term often implies a hearty, uninhibited, and sometimes vulgar enjoyment of life's pleasures. | The novel contained several rabelaisian scenes of drunken revelry and sexual exploits. 这部小说包含了几处狂饮作乐和性行为的粗俗滑稽场景。| 近义词：bawdy, ribald, licentious, earthy | 反义词：refined, demure, prudish
