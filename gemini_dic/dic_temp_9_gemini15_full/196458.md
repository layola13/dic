serrulation:/ˌsɛrjuˈleɪʃən/| n. |细齿状，细锯齿|The minute teeth or notches on the edge of a leaf or other structure, resembling a saw.  It refers to a series of small, closely spaced teeth along a margin.|例句：The leaf margin showed a distinct serrulation.  叶缘显示出明显的细锯齿状。

例句：The fine serrulation of the plant's leaves helped identify the species. 植物叶片的细微锯齿状有助于鉴定物种。


近义词：dentation, toothing, serration|反义词：smooth, entire

词性：名词
