rethreading:/ˌriːˈθredɪŋ/| n. |重新穿线；重新启动线程；|The act of threading something again, or restarting a thread in a computer program.  It can refer to the process of putting thread back through a needle, sewing machine, or other device, or to the act of restarting a computer process that has been interrupted. |例句：The tailor spent hours on the rethreading of the delicate silk fabric.  裁缝花了数小时重新穿针引线这件精细的丝绸面料。

例句：The programmer had to perform a rethreading operation to fix the software bug. 程序员不得不执行重新启动线程的操作来修复软件错误。|近义词：rethreading, restarting (in computing context)|反义词：untreading (in a literal sense, the opposite of threading)
