reknock:/riːˈnɒk/| v.|再敲，重新敲打|To knock again; to knock a second time.|例句：He reknocked on the door after a moment of silence.  片刻的沉默后，他又敲了敲门。|例句：I'll reknock if nobody answers the first time. 如果第一次没人应答，我会再敲一次。|近义词：knock again, tap again|反义词：open, enter.


USAGE->
