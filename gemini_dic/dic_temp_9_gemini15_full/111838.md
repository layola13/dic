logic:/ˈlɒdʒɪk/ /ˈlɑːdʒɪk/| n. |逻辑，逻辑学，推理|The study of the principles of valid reasoning.  It involves analyzing arguments to determine their soundness and validity.  Logic is essential for clear thinking and effective communication.|Example:  His argument lacked logical consistency.  他的论证缺乏逻辑一致性。


Example: The professor taught a course in formal logic.  教授开设了一门形式逻辑课程。|近义词：reasoning, rationality, coherence|反义词：illogic, irrationality, inconsistency|名词
