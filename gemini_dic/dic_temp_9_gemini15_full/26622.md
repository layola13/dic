cacique:/kəˈsik/| n. |酋长，首领 | A cacique is a native chief or leader, especially in the West Indies.  The term is often associated with pre-Columbian societies.  |Example: The cacique ruled his tribe with an iron fist.  这位酋长铁腕统治他的部落。|近义词：chief, chieftain, leader |反义词：follower, subject |名词
