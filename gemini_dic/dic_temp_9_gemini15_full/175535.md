ranchero:/rænˈtʃɛroʊ/| n. adj.|牧场主；牧场风格的；乡村风格的|A ranchero is a rancher, a person who owns or works on a ranch, especially in the American Southwest.  The term can also describe a style of music, clothing, or food associated with ranches and rural life. |例句：He's a seasoned ranchero, having spent his whole life raising cattle. 他是一位经验丰富的牧场主，一生都在养牛。

The term can also refer to a style of Mexican-American music and a type of hat. |例句：The restaurant played ranchero music while we ate. 我们吃饭的时候，餐馆播放着牧场风格的音乐。


近义词：rancher, cowboy (for the person), rustic, rural (for the style)|反义词：urban, metropolitan (for the style)


词性:名词，形容词
