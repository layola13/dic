mastitis:/mæsˈtaɪtɪs/| n. |乳腺炎|Inflammation of the breast tissue, usually caused by a bacterial infection.  It can range from mild discomfort to a severe illness requiring hospitalization. Symptoms can include pain, swelling, redness, warmth, and fever. |例句：She was diagnosed with mastitis after experiencing severe breast pain and swelling. 她经历了严重的乳房疼痛和肿胀后被诊断为乳腺炎。

例句：The doctor prescribed antibiotics to treat her mastitis. 医生开了抗生素来治疗她的乳腺炎。


近义词：breast infection, mammary gland inflammation |反义词：None.  (It's a condition, not something with a direct opposite.)
