neuroscientists:/ˌnʊroʊˈsaɪəntɪsts/| n. |神经科学家|Scientists who study the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves.  They investigate the structure, function, development, genetics, biochemistry, physiology, pharmacology, and pathology of the nervous system. |例句：Neuroscientists are making significant advances in understanding the human brain. 神经科学家们在理解人脑方面取得了重大进展。

例句：Many neuroscientists are currently researching the effects of stress on the brain. 许多神经科学家目前正在研究压力对大脑的影响。


近义词：brain researchers, neurobiologists, neurologists (although neurologists focus more on clinical applications)
反义词：无


词性：名词
