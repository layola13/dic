pickpockets:/ˈpɪkˌpɒkɪts/| n.|扒手，窃贼|People who steal from others' pockets, typically in crowded places.  They are skilled at distracting their victims and quickly removing valuables.|例句：Beware of pickpockets in crowded tourist areas.  在拥挤的旅游区要小心扒手。
例句：Several pickpockets were arrested at the festival.  几个扒手在节日庆典上被捕。|近义词：thief, robber, pilferer|反义词：police officer, security guard
