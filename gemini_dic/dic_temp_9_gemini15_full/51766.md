devote:/dɪˈvoʊt/| vt.  |奉献；致力于；专心于 |To give one's time, effort, or attention completely to something.  |He devoted his life to helping the poor. 他毕生致力于帮助穷人。| dedicate, commit, consecrate | neglect, ignore |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He devotes his weekends to gardening. = 他把周末都用来园艺。
She devotes her time to studying. = 她把时间都用来学习。

一般过去时:
They devoted the entire afternoon to the project. = 他们把整个下午都花在了这个项目上。
She devoted years to researching the topic. = 她花了数年时间研究这个课题。

现在完成时:
He has devoted his whole career to environmental protection. = 他毕生致力于环境保护。
She has devoted much of her life to charity work. = 她把她生命的大部分时间都奉献给了慈善事业。

过去完成时:
She had devoted many years to the company before retiring. = 在退休之前，她为公司奉献了许多年。
He had devoted himself to his studies before achieving success. = 在取得成功之前，他全身心地投入到学习中。

将来时:
I will devote more time to exercise next year. = 明年我将投入更多的时间去锻炼。
They will devote their resources to developing new technology. = 他们将把资源用于开发新技术。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would devote it to writing. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把它用来写作。
I wish I could devote more time to my family. = 我希望我能有更多时间陪伴家人。

被动语态:
Much time was devoted to the discussion. = 很多时间都花在了讨论上。
All his energy was devoted to his work. = 他所有的精力都奉献给了他的工作。

疑问句:
How much time do you devote to your hobby? = 你有多少时间花在你的爱好上？
What aspects of your life do you devote the most time to? = 你生命中哪些方面你投入了最多时间？

否定句:
He didn't devote enough time to the project. = 他没有为这个项目投入足够的时间。
She doesn't devote herself to her career anymore. = 她不再全身心地投入她的事业了。


PHRASE->
devote oneself to = 致力于…
devote time to = 将时间投入到…
devote energy to = 将精力投入到…
devote resources to = 将资源投入到…
devote attention to = 将注意力集中到…
