presume:/prɪˈzuːm/|/prɪˈzjuːm/| v. |假定；推测；擅自；冒昧|To believe something to be true without proof.  To take something for granted. To dare to do something. |例句：I presume you've already eaten. 我想你已经吃过了。

例句：He presumed to tell me what to do. 他竟敢指使我。|近义词：assume, suppose, suspect, infer|反义词：know, be certain, prove


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I presume you are familiar with this topic. = 我想你对这个话题很熟悉。
They presume that the project will be completed on time. = 他们假定项目会按时完成。


一般过去时:
I presumed he was telling the truth. = 我以为他说的是实话。
She presumed to enter without knocking. = 她未经敲门就擅自进去了。


现在完成时:
We have presumed him innocent until proven guilty. = 在证明他有罪之前，我们一直假定他是无辜的。
They have presumed upon my kindness. = 他们滥用了我的好意。


过去完成时:
I had presumed that he would be there, but he wasn't. = 我以为他会在那儿，但他没来。
She had presumed on his friendship for too long. = 她依赖他的友谊太久了。


将来时:
I presume he will be happy with the results. = 我想他会对结果感到满意。
We presume that the meeting will start on time tomorrow. = 我们预计会议明天会准时开始。


虚拟语气:
I would presume to suggest a different approach. = 我斗胆建议一种不同的方法。
If I were to presume, I would say he's guilty. = 如果我要推测的话，我会说他是有罪的。


被动语态:
It is presumed that the letter was lost in the mail. = 据推测，信件在邮寄途中丢失了。
His innocence was presumed until evidence was presented. = 在证据出示之前，他被假定为无罪。


疑问句:
May I presume to ask a question? = 我可以冒昧地问一个问题吗？
Do you presume that I am wrong? = 你认为我错了吗？


否定句:
I don't presume to know everything. = 我不敢说自己无所不知。
We did not presume to interfere in their affairs. = 我们没有胆量干涉他们的事务。


PHRASE->
presume upon/on: 滥用；冒昧
presume to: 敢于；冒昧
presumed innocent: 被假定为无辜的
it is presumed that: 据推测
on the presumption that: 基于...的假设
