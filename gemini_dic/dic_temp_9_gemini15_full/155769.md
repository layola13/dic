phonodisc:/ˈfoʊnoʊdɪsk/| n. |留声唱片 |A phonodisc is a disc record that stores sound in the form of grooves.  It's played on a phonograph.  The term is often used to distinguish it from other types of sound recording media. |Example: My grandfather had a large collection of old phonodiscs. 我的祖父收藏了很多旧留声唱片。 The antique phonodisc played beautifully despite its age. 这张古董留声唱片尽管年代久远，但播放得非常好。|近义词： gramophone record, record, disc |反义词：digital recording,  cassette tape
