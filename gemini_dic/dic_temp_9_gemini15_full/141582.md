oppression:/əˈprɛʃən/| n.|压迫；压制；压抑|The unjust or cruel exercise of authority or power.  It often involves the systematic subjugation of a group of people based on factors like race, religion, gender, or sexual orientation.  The act of oppressing or the state of being oppressed.|例句：The oppression of minorities is a serious human rights violation. 少数民族的压迫是严重的人权侵犯。

例句：Centuries of oppression have left deep scars on the community. 数百年来的压迫给这个社区留下了深深的伤痕。|近义词：suppression, persecution, tyranny, subjugation|反义词：liberation, freedom, emancipation

一般现在时:
The government oppresses its citizens. = 政府压迫其公民。
He feels the oppression of his demanding job. = 他感到他要求严格的工作带来的压抑。

一般过去时:
The people endured years of oppression under the dictator. = 人民在独裁统治下忍受了多年的压迫。
She recalled the oppression she felt in her childhood. = 她回忆起童年时期感受到的压抑。


现在完成时:
The system has oppressed marginalized communities for decades. = 几十年来，这个系统一直压迫边缘化社区。
They have overcome years of oppression and achieved equality. = 他们克服了多年的压迫并实现了平等。


过去完成时:
Before the revolution, the people had suffered generations of oppression. = 在革命之前，人民遭受了几代人的压迫。
She realized that she had internalized the oppression she had experienced. = 她意识到她已经将自己经历过的压迫内化了。


将来时:
Without intervention, the regime will continue to oppress its people. = 如果不干预，该政权将继续压迫其人民。

虚拟语气:
If there were no oppression, everyone would be free. = 如果没有压迫，每个人都将自由。

被动语态:

The minority group has been oppressed for centuries. = 少数群体几个世纪以来一直受到压迫。


疑问句:
Is this oppression justified? = 这种压迫是正当的吗？
What forms of oppression are present in our society? = 我们社会中存在哪些形式的压迫？


否定句:
The government does not oppress its people. = 政府没有压迫其人民。
They refused to accept the oppression they faced. = 他们拒绝接受他们面临的压迫。

词源:
源自拉丁语 *oppressus，是opprimere 的过去分词，意思是“压下，压垮”。  opprimere 由ob-（加强意义）和premere（压）构成。
