conduct:/ˈkɒndʌkt/ /ˈkɑːndʌkt/| n., v.|行为；实施；引导；传导|

英文解析:
As a noun, "conduct" refers to a person's behavior or the way in which someone behaves, especially in a particular place or situation.  As a verb, it means to organize and carry out something, such as a test or an investigation; to lead or guide someone or something; or to transmit or allow the passage of something, such as electricity or heat.

例句：
His conduct at the party was appalling.  = 他在派对上的行为令人震惊。

The police are conducting an investigation into the murder. = 警方正在对这起谋杀案进行调查。

She conducted the orchestra with skill and passion. = 她娴熟而充满激情地指挥乐队。

Copper conducts electricity well. = 铜导电性能良好。


近义词: (n.) behavior, demeanor, performance; (v.) manage, carry out, direct, lead, transmit

反义词: (n.) misconduct, misbehavior; (v.) obstruct, impede


词性:Noun, Verb
