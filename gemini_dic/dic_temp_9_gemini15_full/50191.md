deoxidize:/ˌdiːˈɒksɪdaɪz/ | /ˌdiːˈɑːksɪdaɪz/ | vt. |脱氧；除氧；还原|To remove oxygen from a substance.  The process often involves a chemical reaction that reduces the oxidation state of the substance.  |例句：The metal was deoxidized before being used in the construction. 金属在用于建筑之前进行了脱氧处理。

例句：The process deoxidizes the blood, improving its oxygen-carrying capacity.  该过程使血液脱氧，从而提高其携氧能力。


近义词：reduce, desaturate

反义词：oxidize, oxygenate

词性：动词
