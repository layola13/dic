head-restraint: /ˈhed rɪstreɪnt/ | n. |头部约束装置 | A device designed to limit the movement of a person's head, often used in vehicles to prevent whiplash injuries or in medical settings to stabilize the head.  | Example: The car's head-restraint prevented serious neck injury in the accident.  汽车的头枕在事故中防止了严重的颈部受伤。 |近义词: headrest, neck restraint | 反义词: None (antonyms would describe the absence of restraint, not a direct opposite device)
