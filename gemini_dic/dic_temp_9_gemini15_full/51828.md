dewired:/dɪˈwaɪərd/ | adj. | 脱线的；失去联系的；混乱的 |  Describes something that has been disconnected from its power source or network, or figuratively, someone who is disoriented or confused.  | Example: The computer was dewired during the storm.  暴风雨期间，电脑的电源线被拔掉了。 The argument left him feeling completely dewired. 那场争论让他感觉完全迷失了方向。|近义词：disconnected, disjointed, unhinged | 反义词：wired, connected, organized
