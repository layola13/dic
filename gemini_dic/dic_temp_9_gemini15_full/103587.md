join in:/dʒɔɪn ɪn/| v.|参加，加入，参与|To become involved in an activity with other people.  |例句：I'd love to join in the game. 我很乐意参加游戏。

例句：Let's all join in and sing a song.  让我们一起唱首歌吧。|近义词：participate, take part, engage in|反义词：withdraw, abstain, refrain from


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They join in the dance every year. = 他们每年都参加舞蹈。
We join in the conversation. = 我们加入谈话。


一般过去时:
He joined in the singing. = 他参加了唱歌。
She joined in the discussion. = 她加入了讨论。


现在完成时:
They have joined in many community events. = 他们已经参加了许多社区活动。
I have never joined in such a crazy party before. = 我以前从未参加过如此疯狂的派对。


过去完成时:
They had already joined in the celebration before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，他们已经加入了庆祝活动。
She had joined in the project before the deadline. = 截止日期之前，她已经加入了这个项目。


将来时:
I will join in the marathon next month. = 我下个月将参加马拉松比赛。
They will join in the meeting tomorrow. = 他们明天将参加会议。


虚拟语气:
If I were younger, I would join in the hiking trip. = 如果我还年轻，我会参加徒步旅行。
If he had more time, he would join in the game. = 如果他有更多时间，他会参加游戏。


被动语态:
He was joined in the project by his friend. = 他的朋友加入了他的项目。
The song was joined in by many people. = 许多人一起唱这首歌。（这句被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用）


疑问句:
Will you join in the party? = 你会参加聚会吗？
Would you like to join in the discussion? = 你想加入讨论吗？


否定句:
I didn't join in the game because I was tired. = 我因为累了所以没有参加游戏。
He refused to join in the argument. = 他拒绝加入争论。


PHRASE->
join in the fun = 参加娱乐
join in the activity = 参加活动
join in the celebration = 参加庆祝活动
join in the conversation = 参加谈话
join in the game = 参加游戏
