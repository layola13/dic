nonexploitation: /ˌnɒnˌɛksploɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 非剥削；不剥削 | The absence of exploitation; a state or system in which people are not exploited.  It often refers to fair labor practices, equitable resource distribution, and the absence of oppression. | Example: The company prides itself on its commitment to nonexploitation of its workers. 这家公司为其致力于不剥削其员工而自豪。  The principles of nonexploitation are central to fair trade.  非剥削的原则对公平贸易至关重要。| 近义词：fairness, equity, justice | 反义词：exploitation, oppression, abuse | 名词
