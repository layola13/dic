rafters:/ˈræftərz/| n.|椽子，屋椽|The structural members of a roof that support the roof covering.  They typically slope downwards from the ridge to the walls.|例句：The old house had exposed rafters in the attic.  这栋老房子阁楼里的椽子是裸露的。|例句：The weight of the snow caused some of the rafters to crack.  雪的重量导致一些椽子破裂。|近义词：beams, joists|反义词：foundation, base
