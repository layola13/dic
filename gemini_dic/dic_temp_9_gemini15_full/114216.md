maidservants:/ˈmeɪdˌsɜːrvənts/ | n. |女佣们，女仆们 | Maid servants are female domestic workers who perform household tasks.  The term is now largely considered outdated and potentially offensive. |The maidservants cleaned the entire house before the party. 女佣们在派对之前打扫了整个房子。| domestic servants, housemaids |  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with employers or mistresses)|名词
