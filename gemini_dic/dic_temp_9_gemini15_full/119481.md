micronodular:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈnɑːdjʊlər/| adj.| 微结节性的 |  Describing a condition characterized by the presence of many small nodules.  It's often used in medical contexts to describe the appearance of organs or tissues under microscopy.  | Example: The patient's liver showed a micronodular cirrhosis on biopsy.  患者肝脏活检显示为微结节性肝硬化。|近义词：nodular, granular|反义词：smooth, homogenous
