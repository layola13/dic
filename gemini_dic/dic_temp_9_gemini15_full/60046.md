educative:/ˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪv/ | adj. |教育性的，有教育意义的 |  Something that is educative is designed to teach people something.  It provides instruction or learning opportunities. | Example: The museum offers many educative exhibits for children.  博物馆为孩子们提供了许多具有教育意义的展览。  The documentary was highly educative, teaching me a lot about the history of the region. 这部纪录片极具教育意义，让我了解了很多关于该地区的历史。| 近义词：instructive, informative, enlightening | 反义词：uninformative, unenlightening

词源:
educative源自动词educate，其词根来自拉丁语educare，意思是“培养，训练”。  educare  本身由  e-  (表示“出”的意思) 和 ducere ("引导，带领") 构成，因此educate的本义是“引导出来”，引申为教育。  "-ative"是一个形容词后缀，表示“有…性质的”。
