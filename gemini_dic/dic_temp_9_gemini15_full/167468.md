preventers:/prɪˈvɛntərz/| n.| 预防物；防止装置 |  Something that prevents something else from happening or existing.  It often refers to a device or mechanism designed to prevent a specific event or condition. | Example: The safety preventers on the machine stopped it from overheating.  安全装置阻止了机器过热。 | 近义词：inhibitors, safeguards, deterrents | 反义词：promoters, catalysts
