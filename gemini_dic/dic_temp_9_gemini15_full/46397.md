daemons:/ˈdiːmən/| n.|守护神；精灵；后台进程|A daemon is a process that runs in the background of an operating system, performing tasks without direct user interaction.  It can also refer to a spirit or demon in mythology. |例句：The system daemons are responsible for managing various system functions. 系统守护进程负责管理各种系统功能。
The ancient Greeks believed in benevolent daemons that guided human affairs. 古希腊人相信有仁慈的守护神引导人类事务。|近义词：process, service, spirit, demon|反义词：user process, foreground process
