preconveyance: /ˌpriːkənˈveɪəns/ | n. | 预先交付；预先转让 |  A prior conveyance or transfer of property or rights.  It often refers to a transfer of property made before a main conveyance to create a specific legal effect, such as securing a loan or creating a trust. | The preconveyance of the land ensured the construction loan was secured.  预先转让土地确保了建筑贷款的安全。


The preconveyance agreement was crucial for the project's financing. 预先转让协议对项目的融资至关重要。 |  conveyance, transfer, assignment |  retention, withholding  |  n.
