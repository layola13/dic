ludification:/ˌluːdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. | 游戏化；把…变成游戏 | The process of turning something into a game, often to make it more engaging or enjoyable.  It can also refer to the trivialization or debasement of something serious by treating it as a game.  | Example: The ludification of politics has led to increased cynicism among voters.  政治的游戏化导致选民越来越愤世嫉俗。|近义词：gamification, trivialization |反义词：seriousness, solemnization | 名词
