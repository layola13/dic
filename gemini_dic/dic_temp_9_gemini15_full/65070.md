eternity:/ɪˈtɜːrnɪti/|/əˈtɜːrnəti/| n. |永恒；不朽；漫长时期 |A state or period of time without end.  It can refer to a limitless time, or a very long period. |例句：The soul is believed by some to live for eternity.  一些人相信灵魂会永生。

例句：He felt as if he had waited an eternity for the bus to arrive. 他感觉好像等公交车等了无穷无尽的时间。

近义词：perpetuity, immortality, timelessness

反义词：fleetingness, transience, brevity

词性：名词
