pedology:/pɪˈdɑːlədʒi/ | n. |土壤学，儿童心理学 | The scientific study of soil formation, classification, and mapping; also, the scientific study of the development of children. |例句：He is an expert in pedology, specializing in soil erosion. 他是土壤学专家，专门研究土壤侵蚀。  |例句：The pedology of this area reveals a rich history of agricultural use. 这个地区的土壤学揭示了其丰富的农业利用历史。 |近义词：soil science, child psychology, developmental psychology |反义词：N/A
