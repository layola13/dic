diarthrodial:/ˌdaɪ.ɑːrˈθroʊ.di.əl/ | adj. |关节的；活动关节的 |  Diarthrodial refers to a type of freely movable joint in the body, characterized by a joint cavity filled with synovial fluid and allowing for a wide range of motion.  These joints are crucial for locomotion and manipulation. | Example: The knee is a diarthrodial joint, allowing for flexion, extension, and some rotation.  膝盖是一个关节，允许屈曲、伸展和一些旋转。 |近义词：synovial, freely movable | 反义词：synarthrodial, fibrous, cartilaginous
