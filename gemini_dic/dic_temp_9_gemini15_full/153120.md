pejoratively:/pɪˈdʒɔːrətɪvli/| adv. |贬义地，轻蔑地 |Used to describe something said or done in a way that expresses disapproval or criticism.|例句：He pejoratively referred to the new policy as "a joke". 他贬义地称这项新政策为“笑话”。|近义词：derogatorily, disparagingly, contemptuously|反义词：complimentarily, flatteringly

He pejoratively describes his opponent's ideas. 他贬损地描述了他对手的想法。
She pejoratively labels the artwork as "childish". 她贬低地将这件艺术品称为“幼稚”。
