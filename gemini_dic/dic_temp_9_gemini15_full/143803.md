outorganizing: /ˌaʊtˈɔːrɡənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |  超越组织能力，胜过组织 |  To surpass or outdo someone in terms of organization or organizational skills. This is not a standard dictionary word; it's a newly coined word formed by combining "out-" (meaning to surpass) and "organizing". It implies a superior level of planning, efficiency, or resource management. |例句：The new CEO outorganized his predecessor, streamlining the entire company structure and boosting productivity. 新任首席执行官在组织能力上超越了他的前任，简化了整个公司结构并提高了生产力。


The marketing team outorganized the sales team by creating a more effective campaign. 市场团队通过策划更有效的活动，在组织能力上胜过了销售团队。


近义词: outmaneuver, outsmart, outperform, outdo | 反义词:  disorganize, mismanage


词性: 动词
