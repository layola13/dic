celebrate:/ˈsɛləbreɪt/ | vt. |庆祝，赞美|To mark or observe (a significant day or event) with festivities or special ceremonies.  It also means to praise or honor someone or something.|例句：We celebrated our anniversary with a romantic dinner. 我们用浪漫的晚餐庆祝了我们的周年纪念日。|例句：The city celebrated its victory with a huge parade.  城市以盛大的游行庆祝胜利。|近义词：commemorate, observe, honor |反义词：mourn, grieve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We celebrate Christmas every year. = 我们每年都庆祝圣诞节。
They celebrate their birthdays with a party. = 他们用派对庆祝他们的生日。

一般过去时:
We celebrated our wedding last year. = 我们去年庆祝了我们的婚礼。
They celebrated their success with champagne. = 他们用香槟庆祝他们的成功。

现在完成时:
We have celebrated many holidays together. = 我们一起庆祝了许多节日。
She has celebrated her graduation with her family. = 她和家人一起庆祝了她的毕业典礼。

过去完成时:
They had celebrated their son's graduation before they went on vacation. = 在他们去度假之前，他们已经庆祝了儿子的毕业典礼。
We had celebrated the New Year before the power went out. = 在停电之前，我们已经庆祝了新年。

将来时:
We will celebrate our tenth anniversary next year. = 我们明年将庆祝十周年纪念日。
They will celebrate the new product launch with a big event. = 他们将举办大型活动来庆祝新产品的发布。

虚拟语气:
If I won the lottery, I would celebrate with a trip around the world. = 如果我中了彩票，我会环游世界来庆祝。
I wish I could celebrate my birthday with all my friends. = 我希望我能和所有朋友一起庆祝我的生日。

被动语态:
The victory was celebrated with fireworks. = 胜利以烟花庆祝。
The anniversary will be celebrated with a grand banquet. = 周年纪念日将以盛大的宴会庆祝。

疑问句:
How will you celebrate your birthday? = 你将如何庆祝你的生日？
How did they celebrate the festival? = 他们是如何庆祝这个节日的？

否定句:
We didn't celebrate Thanksgiving this year. = 我们今年没有庆祝感恩节。
They won't celebrate the holiday due to the bad weather. = 由于天气不好，他们不会庆祝这个节日。


PHRASE->
celebrate a victory = 庆祝胜利
celebrate an achievement = 庆祝成就
celebrate a birthday = 庆祝生日
celebrate an anniversary = 庆祝周年纪念日
celebrate a holiday = 庆祝节日


词源:
From Old French *celebrar, from Latin celebrare ("to frequent, to celebrate, to perform, to make famous"), from celeber ("frequented, numerous, famous").  The Latin root is related to the word "celebrity".
