cagey:/ˈkeɪdʒi/| adj.| 谨慎的；遮遮掩掩的；闪烁其词的 |  Reluctant to give information; secretive.  Characterized by evasiveness or unwillingness to reveal information. | 例句：He was cagey about his plans for the future. 他对未来的计划守口如瓶。

He gave a cagey answer to the reporter's question. 他对记者的提问闪烁其词。

| 近义词：secretive, evasive, reticent, close-mouthed, tight-lipped | 反义词：open, frank, candid, forthright |  形容词
