grindstone:/ˈɡraɪndˌstoʊn/| n.|磨刀石；试金石|A rough, usually cylindrical or conical stone used for grinding or sharpening tools or other objects.|例句：He sharpened his axe on the grindstone. 他在磨刀石上磨他的斧头。
例句：The new job proved to be a real grindstone, testing his limits. 这份新工作证明是一块真正的磨刀石，考验了他的极限。|近义词：whetstone, hone|反义词：polisher (in the sense of making smooth)|词性:名词
