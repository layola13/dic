digest's: /daɪˈdʒɛst/ | n. |摘要，概要；文摘|  the condensed version of something | The editor prepared a digest of the lengthy report. 编辑准备了一份冗长报告的摘要。|近义词：summary, abstract, précis |反义词：original, full text |名词
