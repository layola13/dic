mistreating: /ˌmɪsˈtriːtɪŋ/ | v. |虐待；粗暴对待 | Treating someone or something badly or cruelly.  |例句：The animals were mistreating in the factory farm.  这些动物在工厂化农场里受到了虐待。
The children were mistreating their toys. 孩子们在虐待他们的玩具。|近义词：abuse, ill-treat, maltreat, mishandle |反义词：cherish, care for, nurture
