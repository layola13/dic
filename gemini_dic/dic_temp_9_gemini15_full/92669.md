/ˈaɪˌsiːbiːˈɛmz/| n. |洲际弹道导弹|Intercontinental ballistic missile (ICBM) is a guided ballistic missile with a minimum range of 5,500 kilometers (3,400 miles).  ICBMs are capable of delivering nuclear, chemical, biological, or conventional warheads to targets across intercontinental distances.
