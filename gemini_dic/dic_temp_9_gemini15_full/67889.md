fabulous:/ˈfæbjələs/| adj.|极好的；极棒的；难以置信的|extremely pleasing, delightful, or impressive; wonderful.  The adjective "fabulous" is used to describe something that is exceptionally good, impressive, or delightful. It often implies a sense of wonder or amazement.|例句：The party was fabulous!  派对太棒了！
例句：She has a fabulous collection of antique jewelry. 她收藏了令人惊叹的古董珠宝。|近义词：wonderful, amazing, fantastic, marvelous, splendid|反义词：terrible, awful, dreadful, poor
