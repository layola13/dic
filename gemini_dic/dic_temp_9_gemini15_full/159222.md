poet:/ˈpoʊɪt/ | /ˈpəʊɪt/ | n. |诗人 |A person who writes poems.  Poets use language creatively to express emotions, ideas, or experiences.  They often employ figurative language, such as metaphors and similes, to create vivid imagery and evoke strong feelings in the reader. |Example: Shakespeare is considered one of the greatest poets in the English language.  莎士比亚被认为是英语中最伟大的诗人之一。

Example:  Maya Angelou was a celebrated poet and activist. 玛雅·安杰卢是一位著名的诗人和活动家。


近义词:verse-writer, lyricist, bard


反义词:None directly applicable, but could consider terms implying lack of poetic ability, such as prose writer (in contrast to poetic writing).
