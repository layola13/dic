patience:/ˈpeɪʃəns/ | n. | 耐心，忍耐 | The ability to accept delay or suffering without complaint or anger.  It often involves perseverance and tolerance. |例句：He showed great patience in dealing with the difficult child. 他在应付那个难缠的孩子时表现出了极大的耐心。

例句： Her patience finally wore thin after waiting for three hours.  她在等了三个小时后，耐心终于耗尽了。


近义词： forbearance, tolerance, endurance, perseverance | 反义词：impatience, intolerance


词源:  The word "patience" originates from the Old French word "pacience," which itself comes from the Latin word "patientia," meaning "suffering," "endurance," or "forbearance." The Latin root is "pati," meaning "to suffer" or "to endure."
