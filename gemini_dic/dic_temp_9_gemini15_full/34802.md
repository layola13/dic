climbed:/klaɪmd/| v.|攀登；爬；上升|To ascend or move upwards, often using hands and feet; to increase gradually.|例句：He climbed the mountain. 他爬上了山。|例句：The price of oil climbed steadily. 油价稳步上涨。|近义词：ascended, scaled, mounted|反义词：descended, dropped


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He climbs mountains every weekend. 他每个周末都爬山。
She climbs the stairs slowly. 她慢慢地爬楼梯。


一般过去时:
They climbed the highest peak last year. 他们去年攀登了最高峰。
He climbed the tree to get the cat. 他爬上树去救猫。


现在完成时:
She has climbed Mount Everest. 她已经攀登了珠穆朗玛峰。
They have climbed that mountain many times. 他们已经爬过那座山很多次了。


过去完成时:
He had climbed the wall before the police arrived.  警察到达之前他已经翻过了墙。
We had climbed for hours before we reached the summit. 我们攀登了几个小时才到达山顶。


将来时:
They will climb the Matterhorn next month.他们下个月将攀登马特洪峰。
We will climb that hill tomorrow morning. 我们明天早上将爬上那座小山。


虚拟语气:
If I were stronger, I would climb that cliff. 如果我更强壮，我会攀登那悬崖。
I wish I could climb that mountain. 我真希望我能爬上那座山。


被动语态:
The mountain was climbed by many people last year. 那座山去年被很多人攀登过。
The tree was climbed by a mischievous monkey. 那棵树被一只顽皮的猴子爬上了。


疑问句:
Did he climb the tree? 他爬树了吗？
Have you ever climbed a mountain? 你曾经爬过山吗？


否定句:
They didn't climb the mountain because of the bad weather. 因为天气不好，他们没有爬山。
She hasn't climbed that difficult peak yet. 她还没有攀登那座困难的山峰。


PHRASE->
climb the ladder  往上爬（指事业）
climb the walls  快发疯了
climb down  下来；让步
climb aboard  上车；登机
climb out of  摆脱（困境）
