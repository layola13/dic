headwater:/ˈhɛdˌwɔːtər/| n.|源头；上游；源流|The source of a river or stream, especially its upper reaches.|The headwaters of the Amazon River are in the Andes Mountains. 亚马逊河的源头在安第斯山脉。|近义词：source, origin, upper reaches|反义词：mouth, delta, outflow
