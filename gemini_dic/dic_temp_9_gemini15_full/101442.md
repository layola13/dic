irascibly:/ˈɪræsɪbli/| adj.|易怒地；暴躁地 |easily provoked to anger; irritable.  |例句：He irascibly dismissed my suggestion. 他暴躁地驳回了我的建议。  |近义词：irritably, angrily, testily, cholerically |反义词：calmly, patiently, serenely |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He irascibly responds to criticism. = 他易怒地回应批评。
She irascibly refuses to cooperate. = 她暴躁地拒绝合作。

一般过去时:
He irascibly slammed the door. = 他怒气冲冲地摔上门。
She irascibly answered the question. = 她粗暴地回答了这个问题。

现在完成时:
He has irascibly reacted to every setback. = 他对每一次挫折都反应激烈。
She has irascibly treated her staff for years. = 她多年来一直粗暴地对待她的员工。

过去完成时:
He had irascibly interrupted the speaker several times. = 他曾多次粗暴地打断发言者。
She had irascibly refused to listen to reason. = 她曾暴躁地拒绝听取道理。

将来时:
He will irascibly react if you challenge him. = 如果你挑战他，他会勃然大怒。
She will irascibly refuse any help. = 她会暴躁地拒绝任何帮助。

虚拟语气:
If he were less irascible, he would have more friends. = 如果他不那么易怒，他就会有更多朋友。
I wish she weren't so irascible. = 我希望她不要那么易怒。

被动语态:  (The adverb "irascibly" is rarely used in passive voice constructions.)  
His suggestions were irascibly dismissed.  = 他的建议被粗暴地驳回了。
Her complaints were irascibly ignored. = 她的抱怨被粗暴地忽视了。


疑问句:
Does he always respond so irascibly? = 他总是这么易怒地回应吗？
Why is she acting so irascibly today? = 她今天为什么表现得如此暴躁？


否定句:
He didn't respond irascibly; he was quite calm. = 他并没有易怒地回应；他很平静。
She didn't treat him irascibly; she was very kind. = 她并没有粗暴地对待他；她非常善良。


PHRASE->

词源:
From irascible + -ly.  Irascible comes from Latin *irascibilis, from irasci "to be angry," from ira "anger, wrath."
