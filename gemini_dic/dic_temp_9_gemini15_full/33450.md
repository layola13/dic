churning:/ˈtʃɜːrnɪŋ/| v. adj. n.|搅拌；剧烈翻滚；动荡；混乱|The process of vigorously mixing or agitating something, often creating a swirling or turbulent effect. It can also refer to a state of constant change, turmoil, or unrest.|例句：The churning sea threatened to capsize the small boat.  汹涌的大海威胁着要使小船倾覆。The churning of the crowd made it difficult to move forward. 人群的涌动使得难以前进。|近义词：agitating, stirring, mixing, swirling, tumultuous, turbulent, chaotic|反义词：calm, still, peaceful, tranquil

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine is churning the butter. = 机器正在搅拌黄油。
The river is churning with rapids. = 河流湍急，水流翻滚。

一般过去时:

过去完成时:
