peewit:/ˈpiːwɪt/| n. |蛎鹬；田凫|A peewit is a type of bird, specifically the Eurasian lapwing (Vanellus vanellus), known for its distinctive cry.  It's a wading bird with a distinctive crest and long legs.|例句：We saw a peewit foraging in the mudflats. 我们看到一只蛎鹬在泥滩上觅食。 The peewit's call echoed across the marsh.  蛎鹬的叫声在沼泽地回荡。|近义词：lapwing, green plover|反义词: N/A
