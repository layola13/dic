formalization:/ˌfɔːrməlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 正式化；形式化；公式化 | The process of expressing something in a formal system, typically a mathematical or logical system.  It involves representing concepts, ideas, or processes using precise and unambiguous symbols and rules.  This can be applied to various fields like mathematics, logic, linguistics, and computer science. | Example: The formalization of the theory of relativity required the development of new mathematical tools.  相对论的正式化需要发展新的数学工具。  The formalization of grammar rules helps in creating computer programs that can analyze and generate language. 语法规则的形式化有助于创建能够分析和生成语言的计算机程序。 | 近义词：systematization, structuring, codification | 反义词：informalization, simplification
