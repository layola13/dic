scorpions:/ˈskɔːrpiənz/| n. |蝎子|Scorpions are arachnids belonging to the order Scorpiones. They are characterized by their eight legs, a segmented body, and a venomous tail with a stinger.  Scorpions are nocturnal and primarily feed on insects and other small invertebrates.  They are found in diverse habitats worldwide, particularly in warm and arid regions.|例句：Many species of scorpions are harmless to humans, but some possess venom that can be dangerous.  许多种类的蝎子对人类无害，但有些蝎子的毒液可能很危险。

例句：We saw several scorpions hiding under rocks during our night hike. 在我们夜间徒步旅行中，我们看到几只蝎子躲在岩石下面。
