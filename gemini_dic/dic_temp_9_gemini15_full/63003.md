enough:/ɪˈnʌf/| adj., pron., adv.|足够的，充分的；足够了|sufficient, adequate; a sufficient amount or number|

例句：

We have enough food for everyone.  = 我们有足够的食物供大家食用。

She's old enough to drive. = 她已经够年龄开车了。


近义词：sufficient, adequate, plenty, ample

反义词：insufficient, inadequate, scarce


词性：形容词，代词，副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There is enough time. = 时间足够。
We have enough money. = 我们有足够的钱。


一般过去时:
There was enough food. = 食物足够。
They had enough time to finish. = 他们有足够的时间完成。


现在完成时:
We have had enough rest. = 我们已经休息够了。
They have earned enough money. = 他们已经赚够钱了。


过去完成时:
She had had enough of his complaints. = 她已经受够了他的抱怨。

将来时:

There will be enough seats for everyone. = 将会有足够的座位供所有人使用。


虚拟语气:
If we had enough money, we would buy a house. = 如果我们有足够的钱，我们会买一栋房子。
I wish I had enough time to travel. = 我希望我有足够的时间去旅行。


被动语态:

否定句:

词源:

The word "enough" originates from Old English "genōh," which combines "ge-" (a prefix indicating completeness) and "nōh" (meaning "necessary" or "sufficient").  The word's development has been relatively consistent throughout the centuries, maintaining its core meaning of sufficiency.
