parle:/ˈpɑːrleɪ/| n. v.|谈话；会谈；商谈|A formal discussion or negotiation between people.|例句：The two countries held a parle to discuss trade relations.  两个国家举行会谈以讨论贸易关系。|近义词：conversation, discussion, negotiation, conference|反义词：silence, fight


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They parle regularly about their work. = 他们经常谈论他们的工作。
We parle with our clients to understand their needs. = 我们与客户交谈以了解他们的需求。


一般过去时:
The diplomats parleyed for hours before reaching an agreement. = 外交官们会谈了几个小时才达成协议。
They parleyed late into the night. = 他们一直谈到深夜。


现在完成时:
They have parleyed with the rebels several times. = 他们已经与叛军谈判过几次。
The two sides have parleyed unsuccessfully to find common ground. = 双方多次谈判都未能找到共同点。


过去完成时:
They had parleyed for days before the ceasefire was announced. = 在宣布停火之前，他们已经谈判了几天。
The negotiators had parleyed extensively before the final agreement. = 在最终协议达成之前，谈判代表们进行了广泛的谈判。


将来时:
The leaders will parle next week to resolve the conflict. = 领导人下周将进行会谈以解决冲突。
We will parle with the suppliers to negotiate better prices. = 我们将与供应商谈判以争取更好的价格。


虚拟语气:
If they had parleyed earlier, the conflict might have been avoided. = 如果他们早点谈判，冲突本可以避免。
I wish they would parle and find a peaceful solution. = 我希望他们能进行谈判并找到和平的解决办法。


被动语态:
The issue was parleyed extensively by both sides. = 双方对这个问题进行了广泛的讨论。
A solution will be parleyed out soon. = 一种解决方案很快就会被讨论出来。


疑问句:
Will they parle before taking any action? = 他们会在采取任何行动之前进行谈判吗？
Did they parle to each other before making the decision? = 在做出决定之前，他们彼此之间进行过会谈吗？


否定句:
They did not parle with the enemy. = 他们没有与敌人谈判。
The two sides refused to parle. = 双方拒绝谈判。


PHRASE->
parle with = 与...交涉
enter into a parle = 开始谈判
break off a parle = 中断谈判
a secret parle = 秘密会谈
a fruitless parle = 无效的会谈
