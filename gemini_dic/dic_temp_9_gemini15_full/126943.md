narrating:/nəˈreɪtɪŋ/| v. |叙述；讲述；作解说 |The act of telling a story or giving an account of something.  It often involves describing events in chronological order and providing context and details to make the story clear and engaging. |例句：He was narrating the events leading up to the accident. 他正在叙述导致事故发生的事件。

例句：The documentary narrates the struggles of refugees. 这部纪录片讲述了难民的挣扎。


近义词：relating, recounting, describing, reporting, explaining

反义词：concealing, hiding, suppressing, omitting

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He narrates stories to his children every night. 他每天晚上都给孩子们讲故事。
She narrates her experiences in her travel blog. 她在她的旅游博客中讲述她的经历。


一般过去时:
The author narrated the story from the protagonist's perspective. 作者以主人公的视角讲述了这个故事。
He narrated the film's plot to his friend. 他向朋友讲述了这部电影的情节。


现在完成时:
She has narrated several successful audiobooks. 她已经为几部成功的有声读物做过旁白。
He has narrated the entire history of the family. 他已经讲述了整个家族的历史。


过去完成时:
Before the trial, the witness had narrated the events to the police. 在审判之前，证人已经向警方讲述了事件经过。
She had narrated the story many times before, but this time it felt different. 她以前已经讲过很多次这个故事了，但这次感觉不一样。


将来时:
He will narrate the upcoming documentary about climate change. 他将为即将上映的关于气候变化的纪录片做旁白。
She will narrate her adventures in her new book. 她将在她的新书中讲述她的冒险经历。


虚拟语气:
If he narrated the story differently, it would have a different impact. 如果他以不同的方式讲述这个故事，它就会产生不同的影响。
I wish she had narrated the events more clearly. 我希望她能更清晰地讲述这些事件。


被动语态:
The story was narrated in a captivating way. 故事以一种引人入胜的方式讲述。
The events of the war were narrated by several eyewitnesses. 战争的事件由几位目击者讲述。


疑问句:
Who narrated that documentary? 谁为那部纪录片做旁白？
Will she narrate the audiobook herself? 她会自己为有声读物做旁白吗？


否定句:
He did not narrate the story accurately. 他没有准确地讲述这个故事。
She did not narrate the events in chronological order. 她没有按时间顺序叙述事件。
