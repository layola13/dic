entrusts:/ɪnˈtrʌsts/| vt.|委托；托付；信托|to commit or give something to someone with confidence, expecting it to be returned or dealt with responsibly.|例句：She entrusted her children to the care of her sister. 她把孩子们托付给姐姐照顾。|例句：The company entrusted the project to a new team. 公司将项目委托给了一个新的团队。|近义词：commit, confide, consign|反义词：retain, keep, withhold|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He entrusts his car to the mechanic every year. = 他每年都把他的车托付给技工。
She entrusts her secrets to her diary. = 她把她的秘密托付给她的日记。


一般过去时:
They entrusted their valuables to the bank. = 他们把贵重物品托付给了银行。
He entrusted the task to his most capable employee. = 他把任务委托给了他最能干的员工。


现在完成时:
I have entrusted my financial affairs to a financial advisor. = 我已将我的财务事务委托给了一位理财顾问。
She has entrusted the painting to a reputable art gallery. = 她已将这幅画委托给了一家信誉良好的画廊。


过去完成时:
They had entrusted the documents to a lawyer before leaving the country. = 他们离开国家之前已将文件委托给了一位律师。
She had entrusted her savings to a risky investment scheme. = 她之前已将积蓄委托给了一个高风险的投资计划。


将来时:
He will entrust the management of the company to his son. = 他将把公司的管理权托付给他的儿子。
They will entrust the delivery of the goods to a reliable courier service. = 他们将把货物的交付委托给一家可靠的快递服务公司。


虚拟语气:
If I were to entrust him with my money, I would need a guarantee. = 如果我要把钱托付给他，我需要一份担保。
I wish I had entrusted the project to a more experienced team. = 我希望我把项目委托给一个更有经验的团队。


被动语态:
The project was entrusted to a team of experts. = 该项目被委托给了一个专家团队。
The children were entrusted to the care of their grandparents. = 孩子们被托付给了他们的祖父母照顾。


疑问句:
To whom will you entrust this important mission? = 你将把这个重要的任务委托给谁？
Who did she entrust her jewelry to? = 她把她的珠宝托付给了谁？


否定句:
He did not entrust his secrets to anyone. = 他没有把他的秘密告诉任何人。
They would not entrust their business to an unreliable partner. = 他们不会把他们的生意托付给一个不可靠的伙伴。

PHRASE->
entrust someone with something = 把某物托付给某人
entrust a task to someone = 把任务委托给某人
entrust one's care to someone = 把自己的照顾托付给某人
entrust something to someone's care = 把某物托付给某人的照顾
entrust one's future to someone = 把自己的未来托付给某人
