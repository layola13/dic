overrealistic: /ˌoʊvərˌriːəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 过于现实的；过于逼真 | Excessively realistic;  more realistic than is necessary or desirable, often to the point of being artificial or unnatural.  It suggests a level of realism that surpasses what is natural or expected, sometimes feeling contrived. | The movie's special effects were overrealistic, making the violence almost unbearable. 这部电影的特效过于逼真，使暴力场面几乎难以忍受。|近义词: hyperrealistic, excessively realistic | 反义词: unrealistic, idealistic, fantastical
