pulmotor:/ˈpʌlməʊtər/ | n. |人工呼吸器|A device used to assist or force breathing, particularly in cases of respiratory failure or drowning.  It typically consists of a bellows-like mechanism that pushes air into the lungs. |The pulmotor was used to resuscitate the drowning victim.  人工呼吸器被用来抢救溺水者。|近义词：respirator, ventilator |反义词：None
