comember:/ˈkoʊˌmɛmbər/| n. |共同成员，同会员 |A comember is a member who shares membership with another in a group or organization.  This term emphasizes the shared aspect of membership. For example, in a joint venture, the representatives from each participating company could be considered comembers of the venture's governing body. |Example:  The two companies are comembers of the industry association. 这两家公司是该行业协会的共同成员。


近义词： fellow member, joint member
反义词：  sole member, individual member
