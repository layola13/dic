prepatents:/ˈpriːˌpætənts/| n. |预专利，申请中的专利|Documents or actions taken before a patent is officially granted, such as provisional patent applications, research notes, or design sketches.  These materials can provide evidence of prior invention or conception, which may be important in patent disputes.|Example: The company's prepatents showed they had been working on the technology for years before their competitor filed their patent application.  这家公司的预专利文件显示，他们在竞争对手申请专利之前就已经研发这项技术多年了。|近义词：prior art, preliminary patent applications |反义词：patents
