discomfortingly:/dɪsˈkʌmfərtɪŋli/ | adv. |令人不安地，令人不舒服地 |  In a way that causes discomfort or unease.  It describes a manner or situation that creates a feeling of unease or awkwardness. |例句：The silence stretched on discomfortingly.  令人不安的沉默持续着。

The news was discomfortingly familiar.  这个消息令人不安地熟悉。


近义词：uncomfortably, uneasily, awkwardly, disturbingly | 反义词：comfortably, reassuringly | 词性: 副词
