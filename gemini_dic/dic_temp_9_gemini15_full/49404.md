delivered:/dɪˈlɪvərd/| v. |递送；发表；交付；履行；分娩|To bring (something) to a recipient; to give a speech or presentation; to perform or fulfill a promise or task; to give birth to a baby.|例句：The postman delivered the package this morning.  邮递员今天早上送来了包裹。|例句：She delivered a powerful speech at the conference.她在会议上发表了一篇精彩的演讲。|近义词：conveyed, presented, fulfilled, accomplished|反义词：received, withheld, failed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He delivers newspapers every morning. = 他每天早上送报纸。
She delivers speeches with great passion. = 她充满激情地发表演讲。


一般过去时:
They delivered the furniture yesterday. = 他们昨天送来了家具。
The doctor delivered the baby safely. = 医生安全地接生了婴儿。


现在完成时:
He has delivered all the packages. = 他已经送完了所有的包裹。
She has delivered many inspiring speeches throughout her career. = 在她职业生涯中，她发表了许多鼓舞人心的演讲。


过去完成时:
They had delivered the goods before the deadline. = 他们已在截止日期前交付了货物。
The nurse had delivered several babies before her shift ended. = 护士在下班前已经接生了好几个婴儿。


将来时:
They will deliver the pizza in 30 minutes. = 他们将在30分钟内送来比萨饼。
She will deliver a keynote speech at the summit. = 她将在峰会上发表主旨演讲。


虚拟语气:
If he delivered the speech earlier, he would have felt less nervous. = 如果他早点发表演讲，他会感觉不那么紧张。
If she had delivered the baby at home, it would have been very risky. = 如果她在家里分娩，那就非常危险了。


被动语态:
The package was delivered yesterday. = 包裹昨天送到了。
The speech was delivered with great clarity. = 演讲表达得非常清晰。


疑问句:
Was the package delivered? = 包裹送到了吗？
When will the goods be delivered? = 货物什么时候送达？


否定句:
He didn't deliver the message. = 他没有传达信息。
The company failed to deliver on its promises. = 公司未能履行承诺。
