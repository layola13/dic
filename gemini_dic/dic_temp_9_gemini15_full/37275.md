commandant:/kəˈmændænt/| n. |指挥官，司令|A commandant is the person in command of a military unit, institution, or place.  They are responsible for the administration, discipline, and training of those under their command. |例句：The commandant of the military academy addressed the cadets.  军事学院的司令向学员们讲话。

例句：The commandant of the prison was known for his strict rules. 监狱的指挥官以其严格的规章制度而闻名。


近义词：commander, officer, superior

反义词：subordinate, follower
