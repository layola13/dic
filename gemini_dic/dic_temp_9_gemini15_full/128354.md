neutralness:/ˈnuːtrələnəs/| n. |中立性，中性，客观性 |The state or quality of being neutral; impartiality.  It refers to a lack of bias, preference, or involvement in a dispute or conflict.  |例句：The judge maintained strict neutralness throughout the trial.  法官在整个审判过程中保持严格的中立。

例句：His neutralness on the issue surprised everyone. 他在这个问题上的中立态度让每个人都感到惊讶。


近义词：impartiality, objectivity, fairness, indifference


反义词：bias, prejudice, partisanship


词性：名词
