nonparties:/ˈnɑːnˌpɑːrtiːz/| n.|非当事人，局外人|Persons or entities not directly involved in a legal case or dispute.  This term often appears in legal contexts, referring to individuals or organizations who are not plaintiffs, defendants, or otherwise directly named participants in a lawsuit or other legal proceedings.|例句：The judge considered the concerns of the nonparties before making a decision.  法官在做出决定之前考虑了非当事人的担忧。|例句：Nonparties are generally not bound by the outcome of a court case.  非当事人通常不受法院案件结果的约束。|近义词：outsiders, bystanders, uninvolved parties|反义词：parties, participants, stakeholders|名词
