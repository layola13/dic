nonspacious:/nɒnˈspeɪʃəs/ | adj. | 不宽敞的，狭小的 | Not spacious; cramped or confined.  The apartment, while charming, was nonspacious. |The tiny room felt nonspacious and suffocating.|近义词：cramped, confined, cramped, narrow, small |反义词：spacious, roomy, vast


例句：

The nonspacious cabin barely accommodated four adults.  狭小的船舱几乎容不下四个成年人。

Their nonspacious living room made entertaining guests difficult.  他们狭小的客厅使得招待客人很困难。
