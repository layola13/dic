inadequate:/ɪnˈædɪkwət/| adj. | 不充分的；不足的；不够好的 | Insufficient to meet a need or requirement.  It lacks the qualities or quantity required. |例句：The funding for the project is inadequate.  该项目的资金不足。

例句：His explanation was inadequate; it didn't answer my question. 他的解释不足，没有回答我的问题。


近义词：deficient, insufficient, lacking, meager, scanty

反义词：adequate, sufficient, enough, ample


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The resources are inadequate for the task. = 这些资源不足以完成这项任务。
His skills are inadequate for the job. = 他的技能不胜任这份工作。

一般过去时:
The response to the crisis was inadequate. = 对危机的回应不足。
Her preparation for the exam was inadequate. = 她对考试的准备不足。

现在完成时:
The measures taken have proved inadequate. = 已采取的措施已被证明不足。
His efforts have been inadequate to solve the problem. = 他的努力不足以解决这个问题。

过去完成时:
The previous security measures had been inadequate. = 之前的安全措施不足。
We realized that our planning had been inadequate. = 我们意识到我们的计划不足。

将来时:
The current plan will be inadequate in the long term. = 当前的计划从长远来看将不足。
The funding will be inadequate to complete the project. = 资金不足以完成项目。

虚拟语气:
If the funding were adequate, we could finish the project on time. = 如果资金充足，我们就能按时完成项目。
I wish the information provided were adequate.  我希望提供的信息足够充分。

被动语态:
The existing system was considered inadequate. = 现有的系统被认为是不够的。
The proposal was deemed inadequate by the committee. = 该提案被委员会认为是不够的。

疑问句:
Is the current infrastructure adequate? = 当前的基础设施是否足够？
Are the resources provided adequate for the research? = 提供的资源是否足以进行研究？

否定句:
The evidence is not inadequate; it's quite compelling. = 证据并非不足；它相当令人信服。(否定否定等于肯定)
The budget is not inadequate; it's more than enough. = 预算并非不足；它绰绰有余。(否定否定等于肯定)



PHRASE->

词源:
inadequate 源自拉丁语单词 *in- (否定前缀) + *adequatus (足够，适当)。  *adequatus 本身来自 *ad- (to) + *aequus (equal)。  因此，inadequate 字面意思是“不平等的，不足的”。
