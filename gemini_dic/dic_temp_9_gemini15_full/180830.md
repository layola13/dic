rehash:/ˌriːˈhæʃ/ | vt. n. |  重新处理；炒冷饭；重复；改写 | To discuss or use again material that has already been used or discussed, often without significant changes.  |例句：The band rehashed their old hits for the reunion tour. 乐队为重聚巡演重新演绎了他们的老歌。

例句：He keeps rehashing the same old arguments. 他一直在重复那些老掉牙的论点。


近义词：recycle, reiterate, repeat, revamp (as a noun), rework | 反义词：innovate, originate, create


词性：动词，名词


词源：re- (again) + hash (to chop finely; to mix or combine)  最初指对食物的再加工，后引申为对信息的再利用。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rehash the same old jokes every year. = 他们每年都重复那些老掉牙的笑话。
He constantly rehashes his past successes. = 他不断地重复他过去的成功经历。


一般过去时:
She rehashed the story for a younger audience. = 她为年轻的观众改编了这个故事。
The committee rehashed the proposal without making significant changes. = 委员会重新审议了提案，但没有做出重大更改。


现在完成时:
They have rehashed the same ideas several times. = 他们已经多次重复同样的想法。
He has rehashed his old blog posts into a new book. = 他已将他的旧博客文章改编成一本新书。


过去完成时:
They had rehashed the script multiple times before the final rehearsal. = 在最后一次排练之前，他们已经多次修改了剧本。
She had rehashed the data several times before presenting it. = 在演示数据之前，她已经多次重新处理了数据。


将来时:
They will rehash the topic at the next meeting. = 他们将在下一次会议上再次讨论这个话题。
He will rehash the information for the presentation. = 他将为演示文稿重新整理信息。


虚拟语气:
If they rehashed the material, it might be more engaging. = 如果他们重新处理这些材料，它可能会更引人入胜。
I wish they hadn't rehashed the same old plot. = 我真希望他们没有重复同样的老套情节。


被动语态:

否定句:
