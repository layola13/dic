oxymuriate:/ˌɒksɪˈmjuəriˌeɪt/| n. adj.|次氯酸盐；含氧氯化物|A compound containing oxygen and chlorine, especially one that is a salt of oxymuriatic acid (obsolete term for hypochlorous acid).  The term is largely obsolete in modern chemistry. |Example:  Early chemists referred to bleaching powder as an oxymuriate of lime. 早期化学家将漂白粉称为次氯酸钙。|近义词：hypochlorite|反义词：N/A
