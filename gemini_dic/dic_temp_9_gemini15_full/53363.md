disaffiliate: /ˌdɪs.əˈfɪl.i.eɪt/ | vt. |使脱离关系；使脱会；取消隶属关系 | To end an affiliation or association with a group or organization.  |例句：The college decided to disaffiliate from the athletic conference.  大学决定退出该体育联盟。

The university disaffiliated itself from the research project after discovering ethical violations.  大学在发现道德违规行为后，取消了与该研究项目的隶属关系。


近义词：sever ties with, withdraw from, separate from | 反义词：affiliate, associate with | 词性: verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company disaffiliates itself from any unethical practices. = 这家公司与任何不道德行为划清界限。
They disaffiliate members who violate the rules. = 他们会取消违反规定的会员的资格。


一般过去时:
The club disaffiliated from the national organization last year. = 该俱乐部去年退出了全国性组织。
He disaffiliated himself from the political party. = 他退出了该政党。


现在完成时:
The organization has disaffiliated several chapters due to financial mismanagement. = 由于财务管理不善，该组织已取消了几个分会的隶属关系。
She has disaffiliated herself from the group after a disagreement. = 因为意见不合，她已经退出了该组织。


过去完成时:
They had disaffiliated from the association before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他们就已经退出了该协会。
The church had disaffiliated itself from the denomination years ago. = 该教会几年前就已经脱离了该教派。


将来时:
The school will disaffiliate from the governing body next year. = 该学校明年将脱离管理机构。
He will disaffiliate himself from the project if the funding is cut. = 如果资金被削减，他将退出该项目。


虚拟语气:
If the group disaffiliated, they would lose funding. = 如果该组织脱离关系，他们将会失去资金。
I wish the university had disaffiliated sooner. = 我希望大学早点脱离关系。


被动语态:
The branch was disaffiliated due to lack of participation. = 由于缺乏参与，该分会资格被取消。
The member was disaffiliated for violating the code of conduct. = 该会员因违反行为准则而被取消资格。


疑问句:
Will the organization disaffiliate from the parent company? = 该组织会脱离母公司吗？
Has the church disaffiliated from the diocese? = 该教会已经脱离教区了吗？


否定句:
The union did not disaffiliate from the national organization. = 该工会没有脱离全国性组织。
The group will not disaffiliate despite the controversies. = 尽管存在争议，该组织也不会脱离。



PHRASE->
disaffiliate from:  脱离...
disaffiliate oneself from:  使自己脱离...
disaffiliate a chapter: 取消分会资格
disaffiliate a member: 取消会员资格
formally disaffiliate: 正式脱离


词源:
dis- (否定前缀) + affiliate (使隶属；加入)  The word "affiliate" comes from the Latin "affiliatus," meaning "adopted son" or "connected."
