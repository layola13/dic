draftsperson:/ˈdræftsˌpɜːrsən/| n.|绘图员，制图员|A person who makes detailed drawings or plans, especially of buildings or other structures.|The draftsperson meticulously prepared the blueprints for the new bridge.  绘图员一丝不苟地准备了新桥的蓝图。|architect, designer|  (无明显的反义词)|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The draftsperson draws the blueprints. = 绘图员绘制蓝图。
He drafts plans for new buildings. = 他起草新建筑的计划。


一般过去时:
The draftsperson drew the plans yesterday. = 绘图员昨天画了图纸。
She drafted the specifications last week. = 她上周起草了规范。


现在完成时:
The draftsperson has completed the drawings. = 绘图员已经完成了图纸。
He has drafted several designs for the project. = 他已经为该项目起草了几种设计方案。


过去完成时:
The draftsperson had finished the blueprints before the meeting. = 绘图员在会议前完成了蓝图。
She had drafted the initial proposal by Friday. = 她星期五之前已经起草了初步提案。


将来时:
The draftsperson will draft the final plans next week. = 绘图员下周将起草最终计划。
