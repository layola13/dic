curdler:/ˈkɜːrdlə(r)/| n. |凝乳剂；使凝结者 | A curdler is a substance that causes milk or cream to curdle.  It can also refer to someone or something that causes a similar change or disruption. | Example:  Lemon juice acts as a curdler in cheese making.  柠檬汁在奶酪制作中充当凝乳剂。The sudden news acted as a curdler of his already fragile optimism.突如其来的消息使他原本脆弱的乐观情绪破裂。|近义词：coagulant, thickener |反义词：thinning agent
