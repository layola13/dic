outpushing: /ˌaʊtˈpʊʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 向外推的；超越的 |  Describes something that pushes outwards or surpasses others.  It suggests a forceful or exceeding action.  This word is relatively uncommon and often used in specific technical contexts. | Example: The outpushing force of the expanding gas caused the container to burst.  向外膨胀的气体压力导致容器爆裂。The company's outpushing marketing campaign secured a significant market share. 公司积极的营销活动获得了显著的市场份额。| 近义词: protruding, projecting, exceeding, surpassing | 反义词:  inward-pushing, receding, lagging |  adj.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The expanding gas is outpushing the container walls. =膨胀的气体正在向外推容器壁。
His achievements are outpushing those of his peers. = 他的成就超越了他的同龄人。


一般过去时:
The piston outpushed the air from the cylinder. =活塞把气缸里的空气推了出去。
Her performance outpushed all expectations. =她的表现超出了所有人的预期。


现在完成时:
The company has outpushed its competitors in the market. =该公司在市场上已经超越了竞争对手。
The growth of the city has outpushed the capacity of its infrastructure. =城市的增长已经超过了其基础设施的承受能力。


过去完成时:
By the end of the race, he had outpushed all other runners. =比赛结束时，他已经超过了所有其他跑步者。
The pressure had outpushed the limits of the safety valve. =压力已经超过了安全阀的极限。


将来时:
The new technology will outpush the current limitations. =新技术将克服目前的局限性。
We believe our product will outpush the competition. =我们相信我们的产品将超越竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If the pressure were higher, it would outpush the seal. =如果压力更高，它就会冲破密封。
I wish my skills could outpush those of the experts. =我希望我的技能能够超越专家。


被动语态:
The container was outpushed by the expanding gas. =容器被膨胀的气体撑破了。
Their achievements were outpushed by the team's collective effort. =他们的成就被团队的集体努力所超越。


疑问句:
Is the force outpushing the resistance? =力是否超过了阻力？
Does the new model outpush the old one in terms of performance? =新模型在性能方面是否超越了旧模型？


否定句:
The pressure did not outpush the safety valve. =压力没有超过安全阀。
His efforts did not outpush his limitations. =他的努力没有克服他的局限性。
