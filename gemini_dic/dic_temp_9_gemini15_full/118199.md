menus:/ˈmenjuːz/| n. |菜单，目录|A list of dishes available at a restaurant or other place serving food.  It can also refer to a list of options or choices.|例句：The restaurant menus offered a wide variety of seafood. 这家餐馆的菜单提供各种各样的海鲜。

例句：Could you please bring me the dessert menus? 请您把甜点菜单拿给我好吗？


近义词：bill of fare, menu card |反义词：None
