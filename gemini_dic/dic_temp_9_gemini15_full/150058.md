pals:/pælz/| n.|好朋友，伙伴| close friends; companions.  |例句：He's been pals with Tom since childhood. 他从小就是汤姆的朋友。|例句：The two pals went on an adventure together. 这两个朋友一起冒险。|近义词：friends, buddies, companions, mates|反义词：enemies, adversaries


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My pals are always there for me. = 我的朋友们总是支持我。
They are pals with the neighbors. = 他们和邻居们是朋友。

一般过去时:
We were pals in high school. = 我们高中时是朋友。
They were pals despite their differences. = 尽管他们有差异，他们仍然是朋友。

现在完成时:
I have been pals with her for ten years. = 我和她已经做了十年的朋友。
They have been pals through thick and thin. = 他们风雨同舟，是好朋友。

过去完成时:
We had been pals for years before the argument. = 在争吵之前，我们已经做了多年的朋友。
They had been pals since kindergarten. = 从幼儿园开始他们就是朋友。

将来时:
We will still be pals even after graduation. = 毕业后我们仍然会是朋友。
I think we will be pals for a long time. = 我想我们会成为很长时间的朋友。

虚拟语气:
If only I had more pals! = 要是我有更多朋友就好了！
I wish I had more pals to hang out with. = 我希望我有更多朋友一起出去玩。
