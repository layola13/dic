miladis:/ˌmɪləˈdiːs/| n. |米拉迪斯（女子称呼，源于法语）|A term of address for a woman, derived from French.  It is a formal and somewhat archaic or playful term of endearment or respect, similar to "my lady" or "madam".  It's not commonly used in modern English.|Example: "Miladis, may I assist you?"  = "米拉迪斯，我可以为您效劳吗？" |近义词:my lady, madam, mistress|反义词: (no direct antonym)|名词
