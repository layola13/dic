restrictions:/rɪˈstrɪkʃənz/| n.|限制，约束|Limitations or conditions that govern or control something.|There are restrictions on the amount of alcohol you can bring into the country. 这个国家对你能带入境的酒类数量有限制。  There are restrictions on visiting hours at the hospital. 医院探视时间有限制。|limitations, constraints, regulations|freedom, liberty, openness|名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
There are many restrictions on the use of this software. = 这个软件的使用有很多限制。
What restrictions apply to this visa? = 这个签证有什么限制？


一般过去时:
There were fewer restrictions on travel in the past. = 过去旅行的限制较少。
The government imposed strict restrictions on public gatherings. = 政府对公众集会实施了严格的限制。


现在完成时:
The company has imposed new restrictions on employee expenses. = 公司对员工的开支施加了新的限制。
They have removed some of the restrictions on data sharing. = 他们取消了一些数据共享的限制。


过去完成时:
Before the pandemic, there had been few restrictions on international travel. = 大流行之前，国际旅行几乎没有限制。
The court had already imposed restrictions on his movements. = 法院已经对他的人身自由进行了限制。


将来时:
New restrictions will be introduced next year. = 明年将出台新的限制措施。
They will likely impose further restrictions if the situation worsens. = 如果情况恶化，他们可能会实施进一步的限制。


虚拟语气:
If there were no restrictions, we could travel anywhere. = 如果没有限制，我们就可以去任何地方旅行。
I wish there were fewer restrictions on my work. = 我希望我的工作限制少一些。


被动语态:
Restrictions are being placed on water usage during the drought. = 干旱期间对用水进行了限制。
Many restrictions were placed on imports last year. = 去年对进口商品施加了许多限制。


疑问句:
Are there any restrictions on bringing food into the stadium? = 带食物进入体育场有什么限制吗？
What restrictions apply to this area? = 这个区域有什么限制？


否定句:
There are no restrictions on the number of entries. = 参加人数没有限制。
There were no restrictions on access to the building. = 没有限制进入大楼。
