fanaticism:/fəˈnætɪsɪzəm/| n. |狂热；盲信；宗教狂热|Excessively zealous or enthusiastic;  an excessive zeal, especially for a religious or political cause.|例句：His fanaticism led him to make rash decisions.  他的狂热使他做出轻率的决定。

例句：The religious fanaticism of the cult was frightening.  该邪教的宗教狂热令人恐惧。


近义词：zealotry, extremism, obsession, fervor


反义词：indifference, apathy, tolerance, moderation


词源：源于拉丁语 *fanaticus，意思是“受神灵影响的，狂热的”。  The word ultimately derives from the Latin word *fanum ("temple"), suggesting a connection to religious ecstasy or fervor.
