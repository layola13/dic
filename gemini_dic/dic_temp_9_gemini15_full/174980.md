radically:/ˈrædɪkəli/| adv. |根本地；彻底地；激进地 | fundamentally; completely; in a radical way.  It implies a thorough and often extreme change. |例句：The company radically changed its marketing strategy.  这家公司彻底改变了它的营销策略。

例句：He radically altered his lifestyle after the accident. 他在事故后彻底改变了他的生活方式。

近义词：fundamentally, thoroughly, completely, drastically, extremely

反义词：slightly, marginally, superficially, minimally


词源:  From Middle English *radical, from Old French radical, from Late Latin radicalis, from Latin radix "root".  The original sense referred to something fundamental or basic, like a root.  The modern sense of "extreme" or "revolutionary" developed later.
