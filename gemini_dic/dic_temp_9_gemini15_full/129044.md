nitre:/ˈnaɪtər/| n. |硝石，硝酸钾 |A naturally occurring potassium nitrate, a crystalline salt used in gunpowder and as a fertilizer.  It is also known as saltpeter. |Example:  Gunpowder is a mixture of charcoal, sulfur, and nitre.  火药是木炭、硫磺和硝石的混合物。|近义词：saltpeter, potassium nitrate |反义词：无 |词性:名词
