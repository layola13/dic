remounts: /ˌriːˈmaʊnts/ | v. |重新安装；重新登上；再次骑上 | To mount again; to put back in place; to get back on (a horse, etc.). |

例句：

The mechanics remounted the engine after the repair.  = 技工修理后重新安装了发动机。

He remounted his horse and rode away. = 他重新骑上马离开了。


近义词：reinstall, remount, reinstate

反义词：dismount, remove


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They remount the artwork after cleaning. = 他们清洁后重新装裱艺术品。
He remounts his horse every morning. = 他每天早上都重新骑上他的马。


一般过去时:
The soldiers remounted their horses after the brief rest. = 士兵们短暂休息后重新骑上了马。
She remounted the picture on the wall. = 她把画重新挂在了墙上。


现在完成时:
They have remounted the exhibition after the fire. = 他们在大火之后重新展出了展览。
He has remounted the tire several times this month. = 这个月他已经几次重新安装轮胎了。


过去完成时:
They had remounted the engine before the test. = 他们在测试前已经重新安装了发动机。
She had remounted the painting before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经重新挂好了画。


将来时:
They will remount the play next season. = 他们将在下个季度重新上演这部戏。
He will remount the computer after the upgrade. = 他将在升级后重新安装电脑。


虚拟语气:
If they had more time, they would remount the entire exhibit. = 如果他们有更多时间，他们会重新安装整个展览。
I wish I could remount that old bicycle. = 我真希望我能重新组装那辆旧自行车。


被动语态:
The engine was remounted by the mechanics. = 发动机由技工重新安装。
The artwork is being remounted in the gallery. = 艺术品正在画廊重新装裱。


疑问句:
Will they remount the sculpture after the restoration? = 他们修复后会重新安装雕塑吗？
Has the software been remounted successfully? = 软件已经成功重新安装了吗？


否定句:
They did not remount the tires after the puncture. = 他们没有在轮胎扎破后重新安装轮胎。
He couldn't remount the hard drive after the crash. = 电脑崩溃后他无法重新安装硬盘。


PHRASE->
remount a horse = 再次骑上马
remount an exhibition = 重新举办展览

词源:

re- (prefix indicating again or anew) + mount (verb meaning to place or fix in position)  来自古法语monter，最终来自拉丁语montare "to climb a mountain," from mons (genitive montis) "mountain."
