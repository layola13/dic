enrages:/ɪnˈreɪdʒɪz/| vt.|激怒；使狂怒|To make someone extremely angry.|His constant complaining enrages me.  他不断的抱怨让我很生气。|The news enrages the public. 这个消息激怒了公众。|infuriates, provokes, maddens|calm, soothe, please|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His behavior enrages me. = 他的行为让我很生气。
The noise enrages the neighbors. = 噪音激怒了邻居们。

一般过去时:
The news enrages him yesterday. = 这个消息昨天让他很生气。
Her rudeness enraged her boss. = 她的粗鲁激怒了她的老板。


现在完成时:
His actions have enraged many people. = 他的行为激怒了许多人。
The delay has enraged the passengers. = 延误激怒了乘客们。


过去完成时:
The insult had enraged him before the meeting. = 在会议之前，侮辱已经激怒了他。
The lost documents had enraged the manager. = 丢失的文件激怒了经理。


将来时:
The announcement will enrage many voters. = 这一声明将激怒许多选民。
His actions will enrage his family. = 他的行为将会激怒他的家人。


虚拟语气:
If he knew the truth, it would enrage him. = 如果他知道真相，他会非常生气。
I wish that wouldn't enrage her. = 我希望那不会激怒她。


被动语态:
He was enraged by their comments. = 他被他们的评论激怒了。
The audience was enraged by the performance. = 观众被演出激怒了。


疑问句:
Does his attitude enrage you? = 他的态度让你生气吗？
What enrages you about this situation? =  关于这种情况，什么让你生气？


否定句:
The news did not enrage her. = 这个消息没有激怒她。
His apology didn't enrage his wife. = 他的道歉没有激怒他的妻子。
