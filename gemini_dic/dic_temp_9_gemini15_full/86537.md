held on:/held ˈɑːn/| v.|坚持，抓住，继续进行|To maintain a grip on something; to persevere; to continue despite difficulties.|例句：He held on to the railing as the boat rocked violently. 他紧紧抓住栏杆，因为船剧烈摇晃。|例句：Despite the setbacks, she held on to her dream. 尽管遇到挫折，她仍然坚持自己的梦想。|近义词：cling to, grasp, persevere, persist|反义词：let go, give up, abandon


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He holds on tightly to the rope. = 他紧紧抓住绳子。
They hold on to their beliefs. = 他们坚持自己的信仰。

一般过去时:
She held on to her purse during the rush. = 她在上车高峰期紧紧抓住她的钱包。
He held on to the hope of success. = 他坚持着成功的希望。


现在完成时:
I have held on to this photograph for years. = 我已经保存这张照片好多年了。
They have held on to their principles despite the pressure. = 尽管面临压力，他们仍然坚持自己的原则。


过去完成时:
She had held on to the secret for months before revealing it. = 在揭露秘密之前，她已经保守了几个月。
They had held on to their positions until the very end. = 他们一直坚持到最后。


将来时:
He will hold on to his promise. = 他将信守承诺。
We will hold on until help arrives. = 我们将坚持到救援到来。


虚拟语气:
If I held on a little longer, I might have succeeded. = 如果我坚持久一点，我可能就成功了。
I wish I had held on to that opportunity. = 我真希望我当时抓住了那个机会。


被动语态:
The rope was held on to firmly. = 绳子被牢牢抓住。
The belief was held on to by many people. = 许多人坚持这一信念。


疑问句:
Will you hold on to this for me? = 你能帮我保管这个吗？
How long can he hold on? = 他还能坚持多久？


否定句:
He didn't hold on to the opportunity. = 他没有抓住这个机会。
She didn't hold on to her anger for long. = 她没有长时间地生气。



PHRASE->
hold on tight = 紧紧抓住
hold on a minute = 等一下
hold on to something = 保持，坚持
hold on to your hat = 准备吃惊吧
hang on = 等一下 (与hold on同义)
