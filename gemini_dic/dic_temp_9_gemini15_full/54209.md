disfavours:/dɪsˈfeɪvərz/ | v. | 不喜欢；不赞成；反对 | To regard with disfavor; to oppose or disapprove of.  | The committee disfavours the proposed changes. 委员会不赞成拟议的更改。|近义词：oppose, object to, disapprove of | 反义词：favour, approve of, endorse |

They disfavour immediate action. 他们反对立即采取行动。

将来时:
The government will disfavour any further delay. 政府将反对任何进一步的拖延。
