samples:/ˈsæmpəlz/ | n. |样本，样品，例子 |Examples, specimens, or illustrations representing a larger group.  |例句：The lab technician analyzed several samples of blood.  实验室技术员分析了几份血样。|例句：She gave us samples of her new perfume. 她给了我们她新香水的一些样品。|近义词：specimens, examples, instances |反义词：whole, entirety
