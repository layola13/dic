manageable: /ˈmænɪdʒəbl/ | adj. | 可管理的，易处理的 |  Something that is manageable is not too difficult to control or deal with.  It implies a degree of control and ease in handling a situation, task, or problem. | Example: The workload is manageable, even with the added project.  工作量是可以应付的，即使增加了新的项目。 The problem, while complex, is still manageable. 这个问题虽然复杂，但仍然是可以处理的。 |近义词：controllable, tractable, workable | 反义词：unmanageable, intractable, overwhelming


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The project is manageable. = 这个项目是可以管理的。
The workload is manageable for her. = 工作量对她来说是可以应付的。


一般过去时:
The situation was manageable before the crisis. = 危机发生前，情况是可以控制的。
The task was manageable with the help of my colleagues. = 在同事的帮助下，这项任务是可以完成的。


现在完成时:
The team has made the project manageable. = 团队已经使这个项目变得易于管理。
We have found a way to make the problem manageable. = 我们已经找到了一种方法来使这个问题易于处理。


过去完成时:
The situation had been manageable until the new regulations were introduced. = 直到新规章出台之前，情况一直是可以控制的。
The problem had been manageable before the unexpected surge in demand. = 在需求意外激增之前，这个问题是可以处理的。


将来时:
The situation will be manageable with the new software. = 使用新的软件后，情况将是可以控制的。
The project will be manageable once we get more staff. = 一旦我们得到更多的人员，项目就可以管理了。


虚拟语气:
If the budget were larger, the project would be more manageable. = 如果预算更大，项目将更容易管理。
I wish the deadline were less tight, then the workload would be more manageable. = 我希望截止日期宽松一些，那样工作量就会更容易处理。


被动语态:
The problem can be made manageable through careful planning. = 通过仔细的计划，这个问题是可以得到有效控制的。
The situation was made manageable by the quick response of the team. = 团队的快速反应使情况得到了控制。


疑问句:
Is the workload manageable? = 工作量是可以应付的吗？
Is this project manageable with the current resources? = 用目前的资源这个项目是可以管理的吗？


否定句:
The situation is not manageable at the moment. = 目前情况无法控制。
The project is not manageable without additional funding. = 没有额外的资金，这个项目是无法管理的。


PHRASE->

highly manageable: 非常容易管理的
relatively manageable: 相对容易管理的
easily manageable: 容易管理的
barely manageable: 勉强可以管理的
just manageable: 刚好可以管理的
