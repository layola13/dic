inseminations:/ˌɪnsɛmɪˈneɪʃənz/| n. |人工授精；受精；播种|The process of introducing semen into the vagina or uterus for the purpose of fertilization.  It is commonly used in assisted reproductive technology (ART) to help people conceive.|例句：The doctor explained the procedure for artificial inseminations.  医生解释了人工授精的流程。

例句：Several attempts at inseminations were unsuccessful before the couple finally conceived.  在夫妇最终受孕之前，几次人工授精尝试都失败了。|近义词：fertilizations, implantations|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be a lack of fertilization.|名词
