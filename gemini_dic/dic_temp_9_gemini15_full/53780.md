discommonize:/dɪsˈkɒmənʌɪz/ | vt. |使不再普遍；使不流行；使不再常见 | To make something less common or usual; to render something uncommon.  | The government's new policy aims to discommonize the use of plastic bags.  政府的新政策旨在减少塑料袋的使用。 | 近义词：depopularize,  rarify | 反义词：popularize, commonize
