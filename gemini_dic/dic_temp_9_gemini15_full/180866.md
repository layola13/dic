rehired:/ˌriːˈhaɪərd/| v. |重新雇用，再次聘用 |To hire someone again after they have left a job.  |例句：After a brief period of unemployment, he was rehired by his old company.  在短暂的失业期后，他被他的老公司重新雇用了。
例句：The company rehired her after realizing their mistake in letting her go. 公司意识到解雇她是错误的，于是又重新雇佣了她。|近义词：reemploy, reinstate |反义词：fire, dismiss, lay off |动词
