glamourized:/ˈɡlæməraɪzd/| v.|使魅力化；使迷人；美化|To make something seem more glamorous or attractive than it really is, often by exaggerating its positive aspects or ignoring its negative ones.|The film glamourized the life of a drug dealer, ignoring the violence and destruction it caused.  这部电影美化了毒贩的生活，忽略了它造成的暴力和破坏。|近义词：romanticized, idealized, embellished|反义词：denigrated, vilified, exposed
