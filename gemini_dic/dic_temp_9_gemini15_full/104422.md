kafilah:/kæfɪˈlɑː/ | n. |商队；旅行队；队伍|A kafilah is a group of people, especially traders or pilgrims, traveling together, often across a desert or difficult terrain.  It is a large caravan, often with camels or other pack animals carrying goods and supplies.|例句：The kafilah slowly made its way across the vast desert.  这支商队缓慢地穿过广袤的沙漠。

The kafilah, protected by armed guards, reached the oasis safely. 这支由武装警卫保护的商队安全到达了绿洲。|近义词：caravan, convoy, procession |反义词：single traveler, lone individual |名词
