give way to: /ɡɪv weɪ tuː/ | phrasal verb | 让位于；被…取代；屈服于 | To yield or surrender to something else; to be replaced by something else.  |The old traditions are gradually giving way to modern influences.  古老的传统正逐渐让位于现代的影响。|The small shops have given way to large supermarkets. 小商店已经被大型超市取代。|近义词：yield to, succumb to, surrender to, be replaced by |反义词：resist, withstand, prevail over


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The old system gives way to a new one. = 旧系统让位于新系统。
His anger gives way to sadness. = 他的愤怒让位于悲伤。


一般过去时:
The old building gave way to a new skyscraper. = 旧建筑让位于一座摩天大楼。
His confidence gave way to doubt. = 他的自信让位于怀疑。


现在完成时:
Many traditional customs have given way to modern practices. = 许多传统习俗已被现代习俗所取代。
His determination has given way to despair. = 他的决心已被绝望所取代。


过去完成时:
His initial enthusiasm had given way to weariness. = 他最初的热情已被疲惫所取代。
The old bridge had given way under the weight of the heavy truck. = 旧桥在重型卡车的重量下坍塌了。


将来时:
The old factory will give way to a residential complex. = 旧工厂将被住宅区取代。
I think my fear will give way to excitement. = 我认为我的恐惧将会让位于兴奋。


虚拟语气:
If only my anger would give way to reason! = 但愿我的愤怒能被理智所取代！
I wish my fear would give way to courage. = 我希望我的恐惧能够让位于勇气。


被动语态:
The old houses were given way to a new park. = 旧房子被拆除，建成了一个新的公园。(注意：被动语态在此处略显不自然，通常用主动语态)
The old regime was given way to a new one. = 旧政权被新政权取代。(注意：被动语态在此处略显不自然，通常用主动语态)


疑问句:
Will the old traditions give way to modern ones? = 古老的传统会让位于现代的传统吗？
Does his anger ever give way to compassion? = 他的愤怒会让位于同情吗？


否定句:
The protesters did not give way to the police. = 抗议者没有向警察屈服。
The old customs did not give way to modernization easily. = 旧习俗并没有轻易地被现代化所取代。


PHRASE->

give way to temptation = 屈服于诱惑
give way to grief = 陷入悲伤
give way to panic = 陷入恐慌
give way to despair = 陷入绝望
