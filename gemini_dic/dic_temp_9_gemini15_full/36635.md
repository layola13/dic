collectivizations:/ˌkəˌlɛktɪvaɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |集体化；集体所有制|The process of transforming a privately owned agricultural system into a system of collective farms.  This often involves the consolidation of land and resources under state control.  The process can be voluntary or forced and often has significant social and economic consequences.|例句：The forced collectivizations of the Soviet Union led to widespread famine.  苏联的强制集体化导致了大范围的饥荒。|例句：The collectivizations of agriculture in China had a profound impact on rural life. 中国农业的集体化对农村生活产生了深远的影响。|近义词：nationalization, socialization, communization|反义词：privatization, individualization|名词
