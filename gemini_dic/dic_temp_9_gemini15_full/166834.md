presser:/ˈprɛsər/| n. |熨斗；压烫机；压制机；按压者|A presser is a machine or person that presses something.  It can refer to a person who operates an ironing press, a machine used for pressing clothes, or a mechanical device used for pressing or shaping materials.|例句：She used a steam presser to smooth out the wrinkles in her dress. 她用蒸汽熨斗熨平了衣服上的褶皱。

The factory uses a powerful presser to shape the metal parts. 工厂使用一台强大的压机来成型金属部件。|近义词：iron, ironer, press,  compactor|反义词：loosener, stretcher|名词
