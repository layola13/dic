pokey:/ˈpoʊki/ | adj.| 缓慢的，迟钝的；狭窄的，局促的 |  Slow-moving; cramped or confined.  |例句：The bus was pokey and uncomfortable. 这辆公共汽车又慢又不舒服。

例句：The pokey little room barely had enough space for a bed.  这个狭小的房间几乎没有足够的空间放一张床。


近义词：slow, sluggish, cramped, confined, small

反义词：fast, speedy, spacious, roomy, large

词性:形容词
