rented:/ˈrɛntɪd/| adj.|出租的；租来的|Rented refers to something that is leased or hired for a period of time in exchange for payment.  It describes the state of being rented or obtained through rental.|例句：We live in a rented apartment. 我们住在一间租来的公寓里。

例句：She drove a rented car to the airport. 她开着一辆租来的车去了机场。


近义词：leased, hired, let


反义词：owned


词性：形容词
