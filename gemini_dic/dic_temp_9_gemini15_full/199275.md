silverberry: /ˈsɪlvərˌbɛri/ | n. |沙棘果，银莓 | A small, usually silver-colored berry, often referring to species of Elaeagnus, especially Elaeagnus commutata (silverberry) and Elaeagnus angustifolia (Russian olive).  These berries are edible, though sometimes astringent, and are used in jams, jellies, and other food products.  The plant itself is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing properties and use in landscaping. | The silverberry bushes were laden with fruit, ripe for picking.  沙棘树上挂满了果实，成熟可采摘了。|  Elaeagnus, Russian olive |  (No clear antonym exists; perhaps "non-berry" could be considered a contextual antonym)| noun
