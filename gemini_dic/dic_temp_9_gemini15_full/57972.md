drivable:/ˈdraɪvəbəl/ | adj. | 可驾驶的，适于驾驶的 |  Able to be driven; suitable for driving.  This refers to roads, vehicles, or conditions that allow for driving. | Example: The road is drivable, despite the recent rain. 这条路尽管最近下了雨，但仍然可以通行。  The car is finally drivable after the repairs. 修理后，这辆车终于可以开了。|近义词：passable, navigable | 反义词：impassable, unnavigable
