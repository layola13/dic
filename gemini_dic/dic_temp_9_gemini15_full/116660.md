matthew:/ˈmæθjuː/ | /ˈmæθjuː/ | n. |马修（男子名） | Matthew is a common English masculine given name, derived from the Hebrew name Mattityahu.  It means "gift of Yahweh" or "Yahweh is gift."  The name has biblical origins, being associated with the Gospel of Matthew in the New Testament. | My brother's name is Matthew.  我的兄弟名叫马修。 |近义词：Matt (short form) |反义词：无
