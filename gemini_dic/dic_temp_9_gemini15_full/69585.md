felt:/felt/| v. n. adj.|感觉，触摸；毛毡；毛绒的 |to experience a particular emotion;  a type of fabric; having a soft, fluffy texture |例句：I felt a sharp pain in my leg. 我感到腿部一阵剧痛。  The blanket is made of felt. 这条毯子是用毛毡做的。 The cat has felt fur. 这只猫的毛很柔软。|近义词：sensed, touched, perceived, wool |反义词:numb, insensitive

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I feel happy today. = 我今天很快乐。
He feels the cold wind. = 他感到寒冷的风。

一般过去时:
I felt sad yesterday. = 我昨天很伤心。
She felt the fabric carefully. = 她仔细地触摸了布料。

现在完成时:
I have felt better since taking the medicine. = 服药后我感觉好多了。
He has felt a strange presence in the house. = 他感觉房子里有一种奇怪的存在。

过去完成时:
I had felt relieved after hearing the news. = 听到这个消息后我感到如释重负。
She had felt the texture before buying the cloth. = 在买布之前她摸过它的质地。

将来时:
I will feel better tomorrow. = 我明天会感觉好些。
We will feel the effects of the medicine soon. = 我们很快就会感受到药物的效果。

虚拟语气:
If I felt better, I would go to work. = 如果我感觉好些，我就去上班。
I wish I felt more confident. = 我希望我更有信心。

被动语态:
The material is felt to be soft. = 这材料感觉很柔软。(比较少用被动语态)
The pain was felt intensely. = 疼痛感非常强烈。(比较少用被动语态)

疑问句:
How do you feel today? = 你今天感觉怎么样？
Do you feel the vibration? = 你感觉到震动了吗？

否定句:
I don't feel well. = 我感觉不舒服。
He didn't feel the change in temperature. = 他没有感觉到温度的变化。
