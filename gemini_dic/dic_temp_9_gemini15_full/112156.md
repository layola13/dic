look-in-on:/lʊkˈɪnɑːn/| v. |顺便拜访，探望 | To make a brief visit to someone, especially to see if they are well or need anything.  |例句：I'll look in on Grandma on my way home. 我回家的时候会顺便去看看奶奶。|例句：Let's look in on the children before we leave. 我们离开前去看看孩子们。|近义词：visit, check on, drop by |反义词：ignore, neglect |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I look in on my neighbors regularly. = 我定期去看望我的邻居。
They look in on their elderly parents every weekend. = 他们每个周末都去看望他们的年迈父母。


一般过去时:
She looked in on her sick friend yesterday. = 她昨天去看望了她生病的朋友。
We looked in on the project to assess its progress. = 我们顺便查看了一下项目进展情况。


现在完成时:
I haven't looked in on them since last week. = 自从上周以来，我还没去看望他们。
Have you looked in on the baby recently? = 你最近看过宝宝吗？


过去完成时:
Before leaving for vacation, they had looked in on their pets. = 在度假之前，他们已经去看望了他们的宠物。
She had looked in on her mother several times that day. = 那天她已经好几次去看望她母亲了。


将来时:
I will look in on them tomorrow. = 我明天会去看望他们。
We will look in on the situation later this week. = 我们本周晚些时候会关注一下情况。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would look in on my aunt more often. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更经常去看望我的阿姨。
I wish I could look in on my friends more frequently. = 我希望我能更频繁地去看望我的朋友们。


被动语态:
The patient should be looked in on regularly. = 应该定期探望病人。
The project was looked in on by the manager. = 项目经理顺便查看了项目。


疑问句:
Will you look in on the children tonight? = 你今晚会去看孩子吗？
Did he look in on his grandmother yesterday? = 他昨天去看望他奶奶了吗？


否定句:
I didn't look in on her because I was busy. = 我因为忙所以没去看她。
They didn't look in on the progress of the project. = 他们没有关注项目的进展。
