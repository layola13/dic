misparsing: /ˌmɪsˈpɑːrsɪŋ/ | n. | 语法分析错误，曲解 | The act of incorrectly analyzing or interpreting something, especially a sentence or phrase.  This often occurs in natural language processing (NLP) when a computer program fails to understand the grammatical structure or intended meaning of a text. | Example: The software suffered from misparsing, leading to inaccurate translations.  软件存在语法分析错误，导致翻译不准确。 |近义词: misinterpretation, misunderstanding, misanalysis | 反义词: correct parsing, accurate interpretation |
