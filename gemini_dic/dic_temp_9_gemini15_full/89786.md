horizontalism: /ˌhɒrɪˈzɒntəlɪzəm/ | /ˌhɔːrɪˈzɑːntəlɪzəm/ | n. |水平主义；水平论；横向主义 |The principle or practice of emphasizing horizontal relationships and structures, often in contrast to hierarchical or vertical ones.  It can refer to various fields, including organizational structures, political systems, and artistic styles.  For example, in organizational theory, horizontalism promotes collaboration and communication across different departments or levels. In art, it might refer to a style that emphasizes flatness and avoids perspective. |例句：The company adopted a policy of horizontalism, encouraging collaboration between different departments. 公司采取了水平主义政策，鼓励不同部门之间的合作。

The rise of social media has fostered a kind of horizontalism in political discourse. 社交媒体的兴起在政治话语中助长了一种水平主义。


近义词：decentralization, egalitarianism, collaboration |反义词：hierarchism, verticality, authoritarianism

词性：名词
