egoistically: /ˌiːɡoʊˈɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 自私地；自我中心地 | Acting in a self-centered manner, prioritizing one's own needs and desires above others'. | 例句：He egoistically took all the credit for the team's success. 他自私地把团队成功的全部功劳都揽到了自己身上。

例句：She egoistically ignored her friend's pleas for help. 她自私地无视了朋友的求助。


近义词：selfishly, narcissistically, self-importantly, conceitedly

反义词：selflessly, altruistically, unselfishly
