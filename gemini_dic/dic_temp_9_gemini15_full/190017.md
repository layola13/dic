sanded:/ˈsændɪd/| adj.| 磨砂的；已打磨的 |  Having been treated with sandpaper to make smooth or even.  |例句：The tabletop felt sanded smooth to the touch.  桌子摸起来很光滑。|例句：The sanded wood was ready for staining. 打磨好的木材准备染色。|近义词：smoothed, polished, ground |反义词：rough, coarse, uneven


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The carpenter sands the wood every day. = 木匠每天都打磨木材。
She sands the beach before painting. = 她绘画前先打磨沙滩。


一般过去时:
He sanded the floor yesterday. = 他昨天打磨了地板。
They sanded the furniture before waxing it. = 他们在给家具打蜡之前先打磨了它。


现在完成时:
I have sanded all the chairs. = 我已经把所有的椅子都打磨好了。
She has sanded the deck and is ready to stain it. = 她已经打磨好了甲板，准备上色了。


过去完成时:
He had sanded the table before I arrived. = 我到达之前他已经打磨好了桌子。
They had sanded the walls before painting them. = 他们粉刷墙壁之前已经打磨好了墙壁。


将来时:
I will sand the countertop tomorrow. = 我明天将打磨台面。
We will sand the wood before applying the varnish. = 我们会在涂抹清漆之前打磨木材。


虚拟语气:
If I had sanded the wood properly, it wouldn't have chipped. = 如果我当时把木头打磨好，它就不会裂了。
I wish I had sanded the surface more smoothly. = 我真希望我把表面打磨得更光滑一些。


被动语态:
The wood was sanded by the craftsman. = 木材由工匠打磨。
The floor is being sanded right now. = 地板正在被打磨。


疑问句:
Has the wood been sanded yet? = 木材打磨好了吗？
Was the table sanded before painting? = 桌子在油漆之前打磨了吗？


否定句:
I didn't sand the furniture. = 我没有打磨家具。
He hadn't sanded the boards before applying the paint. = 他在涂漆之前没有打磨木板。
