dr:/ˈdɑːr/| /ˈdɒr/ |abbr.|缩写|Doctor 医生；博士；驱动器|The abbreviation "Dr." stands for "Doctor," a title used before the name of a physician or someone with a doctorate degree.  It can also stand for "drive" in computing contexts.|例句：Dr. Smith is a renowned cardiologist. 史密斯医生是一位著名的心脏病专家。|例句：She earned her PhD and is now addressed as Dr. Lee. 她获得了博士学位，现在被称为李博士。|近义词：physician, MD, professor|反义词：patient, student


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a doctor. = 他是一位医生。
She works as a doctor at the hospital. = 她在医院当医生。

一般过去时:
He was a doctor before he retired. = 他退休前是一位医生。
She worked as a doctor for many years. = 她做了很多年的医生。

现在完成时:
He has been a doctor for twenty years. = 他做了二十年的医生。
She has worked as a doctor since graduation. = 她毕业后就一直当医生。

过去完成时:
He had been a doctor for five years when he moved. = 他搬家的时候已经做了五年的医生了。
She had worked as a doctor in that hospital before she transferred. = 在她调到另一家医院之前，她在那家医院做过医生。

将来时:
He will be a doctor next year. = 他明年将成为一名医生。
She will work as a doctor in a new clinic. = 她将在一个新的诊所当医生。

虚拟语气:
If I were a doctor, I would help more people. = 如果我是一名医生，我会帮助更多的人。
I wish I were a doctor and could cure this disease. = 我希望我是一名医生，能够治愈这种疾病。

被动语态:
He was treated by the doctor. = 他接受了医生的治疗。
The patient was examined by the doctor. = 病人接受了医生的检查。

疑问句:
Is he a doctor? = 他是医生吗？
Are you a doctor? = 你是医生吗？

否定句:
He is not a doctor. = 他不是医生。
She is not a doctor, she's a nurse. = 她不是医生，她是护士。


PHRASE->
Dr. Seuss (苏斯博士): A famous children's author.
Doctor's orders:  Instructions from a doctor.
Doctor's appointment: A scheduled visit to a doctor.
Doctor's note: A written statement from a doctor.
Doctor's degree:  A doctorate.
