reisolating: /ˌriːˈaɪsəleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 再隔离；重新隔离 |  The act of isolating again;  to isolate something or someone a second time. This usually implies a previous period of isolation followed by a return to a non-isolated state, and then a subsequent re-isolation.  | Example: After a brief period of freedom, the city was reisolated due to a new outbreak.  = 在短暂的自由之后，由于新的疫情爆发，这座城市再次被隔离。  | 近义词: re-quarantine, reconfinement | 反义词: releasing, liberating | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are reisolating the infected patients. = 他们正在对感染的病人进行再次隔离。
The government is reisolating the affected area. = 政府正在对受影响的地区进行再次隔离。


一般过去时:
They reisolated the building after another case was found. = 在发现另一个病例后，他们再次隔离了那栋楼。
The school reisolated the class following a positive COVID test. = 在出现COVID阳性病例后，学校再次隔离了这个班级。


现在完成时:
They have reisolated the patient due to a relapse. = 由于复发，他们已经再次隔离了这位病人。
The country has reisolated its borders to prevent the spread of the virus. = 为防止病毒传播，该国已经再次关闭了其边境。


过去完成时:
They had reisolated the area before the storm hit. = 在暴风雨来临之前，他们已经对该地区进行了再次隔离。
The authorities had reisolated the village after the flood receded. = 洪水退去后，当局对村庄进行了再次隔离。


将来时:
They will reisolate the ward if more cases appear. = 如果出现更多病例，他们将再次隔离病房。
The company will reisolate the server if the security breach continues. = 如果安全漏洞持续存在，公司将再次隔离服务器。


虚拟语气:
If another outbreak occurred, they would reisolate the city immediately. = 如果再次爆发疫情，他们会立即再次隔离城市。
It would have been better if they had reisolated the patients sooner. = 如果他们早点再次隔离病人就好了。


被动语态:
The affected area was reisolated as a precaution. = 受影响的地区作为预防措施被再次隔离。
The suspected case was reisolated pending further tests. = 疑似病例在等待进一步检测期间被再次隔离。


疑问句:
Will the government reisolate the city? = 政府会再次隔离这座城市吗？

否定句:
They did not reisolate the building despite the risk. = 尽管存在风险，他们并没有再次隔离那栋楼。
The school did not reisolate the class, which was a mistake. = 学校没有再次隔离这个班级，这是一个错误。
