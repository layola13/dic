nonenteric: /ˌnɒnɪnˈtɛrɪk/ | adj. |非肠道的，非肠胃的 | Not affecting or occurring in the intestines.  This term is primarily used in medical and biological contexts to describe substances, organisms, or processes that do not involve or affect the intestines. For example, a nonenteric coating on a medication prevents it from dissolving in the stomach, allowing it to reach the intestines intact. | Example: The nonenteric-coated tablet is designed to release its medication in the small intestine.  非肠衣包衣的药片设计使其药物在小肠中释放。|近义词:intestinal, extraintestinal|反义词:enteric
