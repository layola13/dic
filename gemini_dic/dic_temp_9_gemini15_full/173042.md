quarrying:/ˈkwɒriɪŋ/| v.|采石，采矿|The extraction of stone, rock, or other minerals from the earth by digging.  This process often involves blasting and heavy machinery to break up the material and remove it from the quarry.|例句：The quarrying of granite has significantly altered the landscape.  采石场开采花岗岩已大大改变了地貌。

例句：The company is facing legal challenges due to its irresponsible quarrying practices.  该公司因其不负责任的采石作业而面临法律挑战。


近义词：mining, extraction, excavation|反义词：restoration, conservation

词源:
From the Old French *quarriere, from the Old North French quarrière ("quarry"), from the Old French quarer ("to quarry"), from the Late Latin quadrare ("to square, to make square").  The word ultimately reflects the practice of extracting stones in square or rectangular blocks.
