governmentalized: /ˌɡʌvərnmɛntəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 政府化的；由政府控制的 |  Made subject to government control or influence; characterized by government control.  The process of bringing something under the control or influence of the government. |


例句：

The healthcare system has become increasingly governmentalized in recent years. 近年来，医疗体系日益政府化。

The once-private industry has been completely governmentalized.  曾经的私营产业已经被完全政府化了。


近义词：nationalized, statized, bureaucratized

反义词：privatized, decentralized


词源:

The word "governmentalized" is derived from the noun "government" and the suffix "-alized".  "Government" comes from Old French "gouvernement," ultimately from Latin "gubernare" (to steer, govern). The suffix "-alized" indicates the process of making something characteristic of or subject to government.
