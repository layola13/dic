reframes:/ˌriːˈfreɪmz/ | vt. |重新构建；重新诠释；改变…的框架|To present or interpret something in a new or different way.|例句：He reframed the argument to make it more persuasive. 他重新构建了论点，使其更有说服力。  例句：The company reframed its mission statement to reflect its new goals. 公司重新诠释了其使命宣言以反映其新目标。|近义词：rephrase, reinterpret, recast |反义词：maintain, preserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She reframes her experiences to find meaning. = 她重新诠释自己的经历以找到意义。
They reframe the project goals regularly. = 他们定期重新构建项目目标。

一般过去时:
He reframed the painting after damaging it. = 他在损坏画作后重新为其装裱。
The artist reframed the issue of climate change in her artwork. = 艺术家在她的作品中重新诠释了气候变化的问题。

现在完成时:
She has reframed her understanding of the situation. = 她已经重新构建了她对局势的理解。
They have reframed the debate several times. = 他们已经多次重新构建了这场辩论。

过去完成时:
He had reframed the photo before showing it to his friends. = 他在向朋友展示照片之前已经重新为其装裱。
The politician had reframed her stance on the issue before the election. = 在选举之前，这位政治家已经重新调整了她对这个问题的立场。

将来时:
She will reframe her approach to the problem. = 她将重新调整她解决这个问题的方法。
They will reframe the narrative to appeal to a wider audience. = 他们将重新构建叙事以吸引更广泛的受众。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would reframe my response. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新构建我的回应。
I wish I could reframe the situation more positively. = 我希望我能更积极地重新诠释这种情况。

被动语态:
The painting was reframed by a professional. = 这幅画由专业人士重新装裱。
The argument was reframed to emphasize the benefits. = 论点被重新构建以强调好处。

疑问句:
How can we reframe this negative situation? = 我们如何才能重新构建这种负面局面？
Will they reframe the questions for the next interview? = 他们会在下一次采访中重新构建问题吗？

否定句:
He did not reframe the statement, leaving it open to misinterpretation. = 他没有重新构建声明，使其容易被误解。
They refused to reframe their position on the matter. = 他们拒绝重新构建他们在该问题上的立场。
