shrivels:/ˈʃrɪvəlz/| v.| 枯萎；皱缩；萎缩 |To become wrinkled and smaller, especially due to dryness or loss of moisture.  |例句：The leaves shrivelled in the intense heat. 叶子在酷暑中枯萎了。|例句：The fruit shrivelled up and became inedible. 水果干瘪了，变得不能吃了。|近义词：wither, shrink, wrinkle |反义词：flourish, expand, swell


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fruit shrivels in the sun. = 水果在阳光下枯萎。
The skin shrivels with age. = 皮肤随着年龄增长而起皱。


一般过去时:
The plant shrivelled after the drought. = 干旱过后，植物枯萎了。
My skin shrivelled in the hot bath. = 我在热水浴中皮肤起皱了。


现在完成时:
The grapes have shrivelled on the vine. = 葡萄藤上的葡萄已经干枯了。
The leaves have shrivelled and fallen. = 叶子已经枯萎掉落了。


过去完成时:
The apple had shrivelled before I could eat it. = 我还没来得及吃，苹果就已经干瘪了。
The flowers had shrivelled after days of neglect. = 几天无人照料后，花朵已经枯萎了。


将来时:
The tomatoes will shrivel if we don't water them. = 如果我们不浇水，西红柿就会枯萎。
The fruit will shrivel in the dry air. = 水果会在干燥的空气中干瘪。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
