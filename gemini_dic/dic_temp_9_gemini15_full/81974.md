grinder:/ˈɡraɪndər/| n. v.|研磨机；磨床；折磨者；用力磨；磨碎|A grinder is a machine used for grinding, such as a coffee grinder or a tool grinder. It can also refer to a person who grinds or someone who is relentlessly harsh or oppressive.  |例句：He used a grinder to sharpen his knives. 他用磨刀器磨刀。  The constant criticism was a grinder to his spirit.  持续的批评折磨着他的精神。|近义词：mill, crusher, tormentor|反义词：polisher, smoother
