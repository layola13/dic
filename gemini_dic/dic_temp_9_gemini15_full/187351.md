rockwell:/ˈrɒkwɛl/| /ˈrɑːkwɛl/ | n. adj. | 洛氏硬度；洛克威尔（姓氏） | Rockwell refers to a scale of hardness for materials, especially metals,  named after its inventor, Stanley P. Rockwell.
