crowns:/kraʊnz/| n.|王冠；顶峰；牙冠|A crown is a decorative headdress, often made of precious metals and jewels, worn by monarchs or other rulers as a symbol of their authority and power.  It can also refer to the topmost part of something, or to a dental crown.|例句：The queen wore a magnificent crown at the coronation. 女王在加冕典礼上戴着一顶华丽的王冠。

The mountain peaks were like crowns against the sky. 山峰像王冠一样耸立在天空。|近义词：diadem, coronet|反义词：base, bottom|名词
