osteologer:/ˌɒstiˈɒlədʒər/| n. |骨骼学家|A scientist who studies bones.  Osteologists specialize in the structure, function, and development of the skeletal system. They may work in museums, universities, or medical settings.|例句：The osteologer carefully examined the ancient bones, hoping to determine the cause of death. 骨骼学家仔细检查了古代骨骼，希望能确定死因。|近义词：bone specialist, skeletal anthropologist|反义词：N/A
