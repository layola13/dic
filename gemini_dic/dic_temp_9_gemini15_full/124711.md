moxies:/ˈmɑːksiz/| n.|胆量；勇气；魄力；本事|Audacity, nerve, and courage.  The ability to cope with difficult situations.  Often used in the plural.|例句：She has the moxie to stand up to her boss. 她有胆量对抗她的老板。

例句：He showed real moxie in facing down the bully. 他面对欺凌者时展现了真正的勇气。


近义词：courage, nerve, audacity, grit, guts, spirit, spunk


反义词：cowardice, timidity, fear


词性：名词
