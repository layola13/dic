distillate:/ˈdɪstɪleɪt/| n.|蒸馏物，馏出物|A liquid that has been purified by distillation.  It is the product collected after a liquid has been vaporized and condensed.|例句：The distillate from the fermentation process is then purified further. 发酵过程中的馏出物随后被进一步纯化。|例句：This particular whisky is known for its smooth, complex distillate.这种威士忌以其顺滑复杂的蒸馏物而闻名。|近义词：condensed liquid, purified liquid|反义词：residue, impure substance|名词
