purgatorios:/ˌpɜːrɡəˈtɔːriəʊz/|/n.|炼狱（复数）|The plural of purgatorio, referring to places or states of purification or temporary punishment before entering heaven.  It often refers to the Catholic concept of purgatory, a state of purification after death for those who die in God's grace but still have temporal punishment to undergo.|例句：Many different theological interpretations exist regarding the nature of these purgatorios.  许多关于这些炼狱性质的神学解释都存在差异。|近义词：purgatories, intermediate states|反义词：heaven, paradise
