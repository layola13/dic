redoubled:/riːˈdʌbld/| v. |加倍；加剧；重新开始 |To make something twice as great; to increase greatly in intensity or strength; to begin again with renewed effort.  |例句：He redoubled his efforts to finish the project on time. 他加倍努力以按时完成项目。

例句：The storm redoubled in intensity, causing widespread damage.  暴风雨加剧，造成大范围破坏。


近义词：intensify, increase, augment, reinforce | 反义词：decrease, lessen, diminish | 词性：动词

词源:
The word "redoubled" comes from the Old French "redoubler," which is a combination of the prefix "re-" (again) and "doubler" (to double).  Therefore, it literally means "to double again."
