over-drink:/ˌoʊvərˈdrɪŋk/ | vt. | 饮酒过量 | To drink more alcohol than is considered healthy or safe.  This usually implies exceeding a recommended limit or engaging in excessive drinking. | Example: He over-drank at the party and had a terrible headache the next day. 他在派对上喝得烂醉，第二天头痛欲裂。 |近义词：binge drink, overindulge in alcohol | 反义词：abstain from alcohol, drink moderately


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He over-drinks every weekend. = 他每个周末都喝得烂醉。
They over-drink at every family gathering. = 他们在每次家庭聚会上都喝得太多。


一般过去时:
She over-drank at the New Year's Eve party. = 她在除夕夜派对上喝多了。
We over-drank last night and missed the movie. = 我们昨晚喝多了，错过了电影。


现在完成时:
He has over-drank several times this month. = 这个月他已经好几次喝多了。
She has over-drank and regrets it. = 她喝多了，现在后悔了。


过去完成时:
I realized I had over-drank when I started feeling sick. = 当我开始感到恶心时，我意识到我喝多了。
They had over-drank before they got into the car accident. = 他们在发生车祸之前喝多了。


将来时:
I think he will over-drink if he goes to that bar. = 我认为如果他去那个酒吧，他会喝多的。
We won't over-drink tonight; we have an early start tomorrow. = 我们今晚不会喝太多，明天我们要早起。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't over-drank, I wouldn't have missed the flight. = 如果我没有喝太多，我就不会错过航班了。
I wish I hadn't over-drank last night.  = 我真希望我昨晚没有喝太多。


被动语态: (This verb is rarely used passively.)  
The problem wasn't that he was over-served; he over-drank himself. =  问题不在于他被过度劝酒；是他自己喝太多了。 (This example uses "over-served" for a passive construction, relating to being given too much alcohol.)


疑问句:
Did he over-drink again last night? = 他昨晚又喝多了吗？
Do you think she will over-drink at the wedding? = 你认为她会在婚礼上喝太多吗？


否定句:
He didn't over-drink; he just had a couple of beers. = 他没有喝太多；他只喝了几瓶啤酒。
We didn't over-drink at the party; we were very careful. = 我们在派对上没有喝太多；我们非常小心。
