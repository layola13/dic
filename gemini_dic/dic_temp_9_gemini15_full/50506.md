deprivation:/ˌdeprɪˈveɪʃən/| n. |剥夺；缺乏；匮乏 |The state of lacking or being deprived of something; especially the lack of the necessities of life.  It often refers to a serious lack of basic necessities such as food, water, shelter, or healthcare.  It can also refer to a lack of emotional, social, or intellectual stimulation. |例句：The deprivation of basic human rights is a serious issue.  基本的权利被剥夺是一个严重的问题。

The children suffered from severe nutritional deprivation. 孩子们遭受了严重的营养不良。

|近义词：lack, want, scarcity, deficiency |反义词：abundance, plenty, affluence
