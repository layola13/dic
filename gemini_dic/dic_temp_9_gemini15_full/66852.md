expectant:/ɪkˈspɛktənt/ | adj. | 期待的；怀孕的 |  Feeling or showing that something exciting or pleasant is going to happen; pregnant.  |例句：The expectant parents eagerly awaited the arrival of their baby.  期待中的父母们急切地等待着他们的宝宝到来。

例句：She looked expectant, her eyes wide with anticipation. 她看起来很期待，眼睛里充满了期待。


近义词：expecting, anticipating, hopeful | 反义词：unconcerned, apathetic, resigned


词源:  From Middle English *expectant, from Old French *expectant, from Latin *expectans, present participle of expecto (“I await, expect”).  The ultimate origin is from Latin ex- ("out") + specto ("I look").
