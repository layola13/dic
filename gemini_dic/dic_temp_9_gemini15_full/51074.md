despairing:/dɪˈsperɪŋ/| adj.|绝望的，令人绝望的|Feeling or showing despair.|The despairing cry echoed through the empty streets. 绝望的哭喊声在空荡荡的街道上回荡。|近义词：hopeless, despondent, discouraged|反义词：hopeful, optimistic, encouraged


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is despairing about his future. = 他对未来感到绝望。
She is despairing over her lost love. = 她为失去的爱而绝望。

一般过去时:
He was despairing after the accident. = 事故发生后他感到绝望。
They were despairing of ever finding a solution. = 他们对找到解决办法感到绝望。

现在完成时:
She has been despairing since her husband died. = 自从她丈夫去世后，她一直很绝望。
They have despaired of ever reaching their goal. = 他们已经对实现目标绝望了。

过去完成时:
He had despaired of success before he finally achieved it. = 在他最终成功之前，他曾对成功绝望过。
She had been despairing for weeks before she sought help. = 在她寻求帮助之前的几周里，她一直很绝望。

将来时:
I fear I will be despairing if this project fails. = 我担心如果这个项目失败了，我会绝望。
They will be despairing if they lose the election. = 如果他们输掉选举，他们将会绝望。

虚拟语气:
If I were in his shoes, I would be despairing. = 如果我处于他的位置，我也会绝望。
I wish I weren't despairing, but I am. = 我希望我不绝望，但我就是绝望了。

被动语态:
His situation is often described as despairing. = 他的处境经常被形容为绝望的。
The news was found to be despairing by many people. = 许多人发现这个消息令人绝望。

疑问句:
Is he despairing about his job? = 他对他的工作感到绝望吗？
Are they despairing of their chances of winning? = 他们对获胜的机会感到绝望吗？

否定句:
He is not despairing, he's just tired. = 他并没有绝望，他只是累了。
She was not despairing; she was determined. = 她并没有绝望；她下定决心了。
