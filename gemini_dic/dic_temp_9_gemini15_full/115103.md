manganite:/ˈmæŋɡənaɪt/| n. |锰矿石，软锰矿|A mineral consisting of manganese oxide hydroxide, MnO(OH).  It is a black or brownish-black mineral often found in sedimentary deposits. It is an important ore of manganese.|The manganite sample showed a distinctive black luster.|近义词：pyrolusite, psilomelane|反义词：无
