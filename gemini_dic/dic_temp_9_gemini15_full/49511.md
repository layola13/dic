demagoguing:/ˈdɛməɡɑːɡɪŋ/| v.|煽动，蛊惑，利用民粹主义|The act of appealing to the emotions and prejudices of the common people to gain power.  It involves using rhetoric to manipulate and exploit the fears and desires of the masses, often by spreading misinformation or making false promises. |例句：The politician was accused of demagoguing the public into supporting his unpopular policies.  这位政治家被指控煽动公众支持其不受欢迎的政策。

The candidate's speech was pure demagoguing, filled with empty promises and fear-mongering.  候选人的演讲纯粹是煽动，充满了空洞的承诺和危言耸听。|近义词：agitating, inciting, rabble-rousing, rabble-rouser |反义词：reasoning, persuading, educating

一般现在时:
He is demagoguing the crowd with his inflammatory rhetoric. 他正在用煽动性的言辞煽动人群。
They demagogue the public with false promises every election cycle. 他们每届选举周期都用虚假承诺来蛊惑公众。


一般过去时:
The speaker demagogued the audience with his passionate but misleading arguments. 演讲者用他热情但具有误导性的论点煽动了听众。
The populist leader demagogued the nation into a frenzy of nationalistic fervor. 民粹主义领导人煽动全国陷入民族主义狂热之中。


现在完成时:
He has been demagoguing the people for years, exploiting their anxieties. 他多年来一直在煽动民众，利用他们的焦虑。
The media has demagogued the public into a state of fear and uncertainty. 媒体煽动公众处于恐惧和不确定状态。


过去完成时:
Before the election, he had demagogued the voters into believing his opponent was corrupt. 大选前，他已煽动选民相信他的对手腐败。
They had demagogued the masses for decades, skillfully manipulating public opinion. 他们几十年来一直在煽动群众，巧妙地操纵舆论。


将来时:
She will demagogue the electorate with divisive rhetoric to win the election. 她将用分裂的言辞来煽动选民赢得选举。
He will continue demagoguing the public until he achieves his aims. 他将继续煽动公众，直到实现他的目标。


虚拟语气:
If he demagogued the people less, they might see his true intentions. 如果他不那么煽动人民，他们可能会看到他的真实意图。
I wish he hadn't demagogued the nation into such a state of division. 我真希望他没有煽动国家陷入如此分裂的状态。


被动语态:
The public was demagogued into supporting the war. 公众被煽动支持战争。
The voters were demagogued by promises of quick solutions. 选民被快速解决问题的承诺所煽动。


疑问句:
Is he demagoguing the people for his own gain? 他是否为了自己的利益而煽动人民？
Are they demagoguing the public to distract from real issues? 他们是否正在煽动公众以转移对真正问题的注意力？


否定句:
He did not demagogue the audience; he presented reasoned arguments. 他没有煽动观众；他提出了合理的论点。
They did not demagogue the crowd; they appealed to their sense of justice. 他们没有煽动人群；他们诉诸于他们的正义感。
