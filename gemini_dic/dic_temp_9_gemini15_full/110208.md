life-raft:/ˈlaɪfˌræft/| n. |救生筏 |A life-raft is an inflatable raft designed to keep people afloat in the event of a shipwreck or other emergency at sea. It typically includes supplies such as food, water, and first-aid equipment.  |The passengers were able to escape the sinking ship and survive in the life-raft.|  近义词：lifeboat, liferaft |反义词: None
