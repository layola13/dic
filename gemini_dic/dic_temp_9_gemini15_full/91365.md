hydrospheres:/ˈhaɪdrəsfɪrz/| n. |水圈|The hydrosphere is the total amount of water on a planet.  It includes water that is on the surface of the planet, underground, and in the air.  The hydrosphere is a crucial component of the Earth's system, playing a vital role in climate regulation, weather patterns, and the support of life.  |Example: The study focused on the impact of climate change on the Earth's hydrosphere.  这项研究关注气候变化对地球水圈的影响。


Example: The ocean is the largest part of the Earth's hydrosphere. 海洋是地球水圈中最大的部分。


近义词:water systems, aquatic sphere


反义词:atmosphere, lithosphere, biosphere
