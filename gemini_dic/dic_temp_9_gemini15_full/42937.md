covenants:/ˈkʌvənənts/| n.|契约；盟约；协议|A covenant is a formal agreement or promise, especially one that is made legally binding.  It often involves a binding agreement between two or more parties, outlining specific obligations and responsibilities.|例句：The two countries signed a covenant promising peace.  这两个国家签署了一份承诺和平的盟约。|例句：The developer entered into a covenant restricting the use of the land. 开发商签订了一份限制土地用途的契约。|近义词：agreement, contract, pact, treaty|反义词：breach, violation|名词
