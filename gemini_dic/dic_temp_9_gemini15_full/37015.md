columnar:/ˈkɑːlʌmnər/| adj.|柱状的；列状的；圆柱形的|Relating to or resembling a column; arranged in columns.  The term describes something having a column-like shape or arrangement, often tall and slender with a cylindrical or prismatic form.|例句：The basalt columns formed a spectacular columnar landscape.  玄武岩柱形成了壮观的柱状景观。  The data is presented in a columnar format for easy reading. 数据以列状格式呈现，便于阅读。|近义词：columnlike, pillar-shaped|反义词：flat, planar
