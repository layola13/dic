deepen:/ˈdiːpən/ | vt. |加深；变深；使加剧|To make or become deeper or more intense.  |例句：The river deepened as it approached the sea. 河水流向大海时变深了。|例句：The crisis deepened as more people lost their jobs. 随着更多人失业，危机加剧了。|近义词：intensify, aggravate, exacerbate |反义词：shallow, lessen, diminish |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pond deepens in the rainy season. = 雨季时池塘变深了。
He deepens his understanding of the subject through research. = 他通过研究加深了对这个主题的理解。

一般过去时:
The color of the dye deepened over time. = 染料的颜色随着时间推移而加深了。
The relationship between the two countries deepened after the summit. = 两国关系在峰会后加深了。

现在完成时:
My understanding of the problem has deepened significantly. = 我对这个问题的理解已经大大加深了。
The hole has deepened considerably due to erosion. = 由于侵蚀，洞穴已经加深了很多。

过去完成时:
The mystery had deepened after the new evidence emerged. = 新证据出现后，谜团更加深了。
His anxiety had deepened as the deadline approached. = 随着截止日期的临近，他的焦虑加剧了。

将来时:
The river will deepen after the heavy rainfall. = 大雨过后，河水会变深。
The economic crisis will deepen if no measures are taken. = 如果不采取措施，经济危机将会加剧。

虚拟语气:
If we deepened our cooperation, we could achieve more. = 如果我们加深合作，就能取得更多成就。
I wish the understanding between us had deepened sooner. = 我希望我们之间的了解能早点加深。

被动语态:
The well was deepened by the workers. = 井被工人们加深了。
The plot of the novel is deepened by the author's skillful use of foreshadowing. = 小说的情节因作者巧妙地运用预兆而加深了。

疑问句:
Does the meaning of the poem deepen with repeated readings? = 这首诗的含义随着反复阅读会加深吗？
Will this experience deepen your understanding of life? = 这段经历会加深你对生活的理解吗？

否定句:
The wound did not deepen despite the impact. = 尽管受到撞击，伤口并没有加深。
He did not deepen his involvement in the project. = 他并没有加深他对这个项目的参与。
