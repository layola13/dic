read:/riːd/ /red/ | v. | 阅读，朗读，理解 | To look at and comprehend the meaning of (written or printed matter) by interpreting the characters or symbols of which it is composed.  | I read a book last night. 我昨晚读了一本书。 |He read the letter aloud. 他大声朗读了这封信。| understand, comprehend, peruse | misunderstand, misinterpret | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I read a newspaper every morning. = 我每天早上读报纸。
She reads novels in her free time. = 她空闲时间读小说。


一般过去时:
I read a fascinating article yesterday. = 我昨天读了一篇引人入胜的文章。
He read the entire book in one sitting. = 他一口气读完了整本书。


现在完成时:
I have read that book twice. = 我已经读过那本书两次了。
She has read many classic novels. = 她读过许多经典小说。


过去完成时:
I had read the instructions before I started the project. = 我在开始项目之前已经读过说明书了。
They had read the report before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经读过报告了。


将来时:
I will read that new bestseller soon. = 我很快就会读那本畅销书。
She will read to the children tonight. = 她今晚会给孩子们读书。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would read more books. = 如果我有更多时间，我会读更多书。
I wish I had read that book earlier. = 我真希望我早点读那本书。


被动语态:
The book is read by many students. = 这本书被许多学生阅读。
The instructions were read carefully before the experiment. = 在实验前仔细阅读了说明书。


疑问句:
Do you read newspapers regularly? = 你经常读报纸吗？
What are you reading? = 你在读什么？


否定句:
I did not read the email. = 我没有读那封邮件。
She doesn't read much anymore. = 她现在不怎么读书了。
