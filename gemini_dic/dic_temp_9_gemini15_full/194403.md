self-punishing: /ˌsɛlfˈpʌnɪʃɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我惩罚的，自我摧残的 |  Describing actions or behaviors that inflict harm or suffering on oneself, often unconsciously or as a form of self-destructive behavior.  It implies a pattern of behavior where the individual actively causes themselves negative consequences. |

例句：

His self-punishing behavior, such as constantly working overtime despite exhaustion, worried his family.  = 他自我惩罚的行为，例如即使筋疲力尽也持续加班，让他的家人很担心。

Her self-punishing tendencies manifested in her sabotaging her own success. = 她的自我惩罚倾向表现在她破坏自己成功的事情上。


近义词：self-destructive, masochistic, self-harming, self-defeating


反义词：self-rewarding, self-caring, self-nurturing


词性：形容词
