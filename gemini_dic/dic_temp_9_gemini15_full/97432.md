infuriated:/ɪnˈfjʊəriˌeɪtɪd/| adj. | 激怒的，盛怒的 | Extremely angry; enraged.  |例句：He was infuriated by their constant interruptions. 他被他们不断的打断弄得很生气。

例句：The news infuriated her to the point of tears.  这个消息把她气哭了。


近义词：enraged, angered, furious, livid, irate | 反义词：calm, peaceful, serene, tranquil
