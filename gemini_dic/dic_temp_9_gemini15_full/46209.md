/sɜːrtoʊstaɪl/ | n. |弯曲风格，弓形风格 |  Relating to or characterized by a curved or arched style, particularly in architecture or design.  The term often refers to a style exhibiting a graceful, flowing curvature. | The building's cyrtostyle columns gave it an elegant, almost whimsical appearance.  这座建筑的弯曲柱式使其外观优雅而略带奇特。| 近义词: curved style, arcuate style | 反义词: rectilinear style, straight style
