resignationist: /ˌrɛzɪɡneɪʃənɪst/ | n. |辞职者；顺从者 |A person who resigns or submits to something.  Someone who readily accepts a situation, often without protest. |Example: The resignationist simply handed in his notice and left without a word. 辞职者只是递交了辞职信，一言不发地离开了。|近义词：resigner, quitter, conformist |反义词：rebel, protester, activist
