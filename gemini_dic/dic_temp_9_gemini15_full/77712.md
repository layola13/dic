gemmated:/ˈdʒɛmeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 芽生的；发芽的；生芽的 |  Having produced buds or shoots; budding.  The term often refers to the process of asexual reproduction in certain organisms, particularly fungi and some invertebrates, where new individuals develop as buds or outgrowths from the parent organism. | Example: The fungus exhibited gemmated growth, producing numerous daughter colonies.  真菌表现出芽生生长，产生了大量的子菌落。 | 近义词：budded, sprouting, budding | 反义词：senescent, withered
