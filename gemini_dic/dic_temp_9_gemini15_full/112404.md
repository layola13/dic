lot:/lɒt/ /lɑːt/| n. |许多；大量；签；命运；地块|A lot means a large quantity or number of something. It can also refer to a piece of land, a drawing of lots, or fate.  |例句：I have a lot of work to do. 我有很多工作要做。

There are lots of apples in the basket.  篮子里有很多苹果。


近义词：many, much, a great deal, quantity, parcel, destiny|反义词：little, few, a small amount, none

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has a lot of friends. = 他有很多朋友。
There are a lot of cars on the road. = 路上有很多车。

一般过去时:
She bought a lot of clothes yesterday. = 她昨天买了很多衣服。
We ate a lot of food at the party. =我们在派对上吃了很多食物。


现在完成时:
I have seen a lot of movies this year. = 今年我看过很多电影。
They have traveled a lot in their lives. = 他们一生中旅行了很多。


过去完成时:
He had already done a lot of work before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前他已经做了很多工作。
We had eaten a lot before we went to the restaurant. = 我们去餐馆之前已经吃得很饱了。


将来时:
I will buy a lot of presents for my family. = 我将为我的家人买很多礼物。
They will have a lot of fun at the concert. = 他们将在音乐会上玩得很开心。


虚拟语气:
If I had a lot of money, I would travel the world. = 如果我有钱，我会环游世界。
I wish I had a lot of time to relax. = 我希望我有足够的时间放松。


被动语态:
A lot of effort was put into the project. =  这个项目投入了大量精力。
A lot of damage was caused by the storm. = 暴风雨造成了很大的破坏。


疑问句:
Do you have a lot of homework? = 你有很多家庭作业吗？
Is there a lot of traffic today? = 今天交通拥堵吗？


否定句:
I don't have a lot of patience. = 我没有很多耐心。
She doesn't have a lot of free time. = 她没有很多空闲时间。


PHRASE->
a lot of = 许多
lots of = 许多
a whole lot of = 非常多
quite a lot = 相当多
