profitable: /ˈprɒfɪtəbl/ /ˈprɑːfɪtəbl/ | adj. | 有利可图的，赚钱的 |  Yielding or capable of yielding a profit; advantageous.  |  例句：The new marketing campaign proved highly profitable. 新的营销活动证明非常有利可图。

例句：Investing in real estate can be a very profitable venture.  投资房地产可能是一项非常有利可图的投资。


近义词：lucrative, remunerative, gainful, advantageous | 反义词：unprofitable, losing, uneconomical


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The business is profitable this year. = 今年这家公司盈利。
This investment strategy is profitable for many investors. = 这种投资策略对许多投资者来说都是有利可图的。

一般过去时:
The project was profitable last quarter. = 上个季度这个项目盈利。
The company's performance was not profitable last year. = 该公司去年的业绩没有盈利。

现在完成时:
The company has been profitable for many years. = 这家公司多年来一直盈利。
Our investment has been quite profitable. = 我们的投资相当有利可图。

过去完成时:
The venture had been profitable until the market crashed. = 在市场崩盘之前，这项投资一直是盈利的。
The business had already become profitable before they expanded. = 在他们扩张之前，公司就已经开始盈利了。

将来时:
The new product line will be very profitable. = 新产品线将非常有利可图。
We expect the investment to be profitable in the long run. = 我们预计这项投资从长远来看将是有利可图的。

虚拟语气:
If we invested more, the business would be more profitable. = 如果我们投资更多，生意将会更赚钱。
If only we had known, we would have made a profitable investment. = 如果我们早知道，我们就会进行有利可图的投资了。

被动语态:
The investment is considered to be very profitable. = 这项投资被认为非常有利可图。
The project was proven to be profitable after the first year. = 该项目在第一年后被证明是盈利的。

疑问句:
Is this venture profitable? = 这项投资有利可图吗？
Are these changes profitable for the business? = 这些变化对企业有利可图吗？

否定句:
The restaurant is not profitable at the moment. = 餐馆目前没有盈利。
This strategy proved to be unprofitable. = 这个策略被证明是无利可图的。


PHRASE->
highly profitable = 高度盈利的
moderately profitable = 适度盈利的
marginally profitable = 微利
become profitable = 开始盈利
remain profitable = 保持盈利


词源:
From *profit* + *-able*.  The word "profit" originates from Old French *profit*, from Vulgar Latin *profectus*, past participle of *proficere*, meaning "to advance, make progress."  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or suitability.
