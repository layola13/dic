frescoed:/frɛˈskoʊd/| adj.|壁画装饰的；湿壁画绘制的|Decorated with frescoes.  The adjective describes something that has been adorned with frescoes, a type of painting technique done on wet plaster.|例句：The walls of the villa were frescoed with scenes from mythology.  别墅的墙壁上绘有神话场景的壁画。 The church was beautifully frescoed, showcasing vibrant colors and intricate details. 这座教堂壁画精美，色彩鲜艳，细节精巧。|近义词：painted, decorated, muraled|反义词：bare, undecorated
