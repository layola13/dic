dividedly:/dɪˈvaɪdədli/| adv.| 分裂地；矛盾地；犹豫地 |  Describes a state of being split, uncertain, or conflicted in opinion or feeling. It suggests a lack of unity or consistency. |例句：The committee voted dividedly on the proposal. 委员会对该提案进行了分裂投票。
The family felt dividedly about the move.  这个家庭对搬家犹豫不决。|近义词：ambivalently, uncertainly, hesitantly, irresolutely |反义词：unanimously, decisively, resolutely
