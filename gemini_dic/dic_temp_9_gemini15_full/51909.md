dhootis:/ˈduːtiːz/| n.|裹身布，宽松长袍|A dhoti is a traditional garment worn by men in the Indian subcontinent. It is a long piece of unstitched cloth wrapped around the waist and legs.  |例句：He wore a white dhoti and kurta for the ceremony. 他在仪式上穿着一件白色裹身布和库尔塔。|近义词：lungi, sarong|反义词：None.
