occulting:/ɒˈkʌltɪŋ/| v.|遮蔽，掩盖，隐藏|To hide or obscure something from view.  The action of occulting something involves preventing it from being seen or known.|The eclipse occulted the sun. 日食遮蔽了太阳。
The clouds occulted the moon. 云彩遮蔽了月亮。|obscuring, concealing, masking|revealing, uncovering, exposing|动词
