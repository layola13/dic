schoolless:/ˈskuːlləs/| adj.|无学校的；未受教育的；不上学的 |Without a school; lacking education; not attending school.  |例句：The remote village was schoolless for many years.  偏远村庄多年来都没有学校。 |例句：Many schoolless children are forced to work. 许多不上学的儿童被迫工作。|近义词：uneducated, illiterate, untaught |反义词：educated, schooled, literate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is schoolless and works in the fields. = 他没上过学，在田里干活。
The village remains schoolless. = 这个村庄仍然没有学校。


一般过去时:
She was schoolless until she was ten. = 她直到十岁才上学。
The region was largely schoolless during the war. = 战争期间，该地区大部分地区没有学校。


现在完成时:
He has been schoolless his whole life. = 他一生都没上过学。
The government has made efforts to eliminate schoolless areas. = 政府一直在努力消除无学校地区。


过去完成时:
They had been schoolless for generations. = 他们世世代代都没有学校。
The community had been schoolless before the new school was built. = 在新建学校之前，这个社区没有学校。


将来时:
He will remain schoolless unless he gets a scholarship. = 除非他获得奖学金，否则他将继续不上学。
The project aims to ensure no child will be schoolless by next year. = 该项目旨在确保到明年为止，没有孩子不上学。


虚拟语气:
If he were schoolless, he would find it difficult to get a job. = 如果他没有受过教育，他将很难找到工作。
I wish I hadn't been schoolless during my childhood. = 我真希望我童年时期没有没上学。


被动语态:
Many children are left schoolless due to poverty. = 许多儿童由于贫穷而辍学。
The area was declared schoolless after the earthquake. = 地震后，该地区被宣布为无学校地区。


疑问句:
Is he schoolless? = 他没上学吗？
Are there any schoolless children in this town? = 这个镇上有没上学的儿童吗？


否定句:
He is not schoolless; he graduated from university. = 他不是没上过学；他大学毕业了。
The situation is not schoolless; there are several schools in the area. = 情况并非如此；该地区有几所学校。
