词源:
The word "sandal" comes from the Old French "sandale," which in turn originates from the Late Latin "sandalium."  This ultimately derives from the Greek "sandálion" (σανδάλιον), a diminutive of "sandalon" (σανδάλιον), meaning "sandal."  The Greek word itself likely comes from a Semitic source.
