corporations:/ˌkɔːrpəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. | 公司；法人团体 |  A large company or group of companies authorized to act as a single entity (legally a person) and regarded as independent from its owners.  Corporations are typically characterized by limited liability for their owners, meaning that the owners are not personally responsible for the debts or liabilities of the corporation. | Example: Many large corporations are headquartered in New York City. 许多大型公司总部设在纽约市。


Example:  The environmental impact of multinational corporations is a growing concern.  跨国公司的环境影响日益受到关注。


近义词：companies, firms, enterprises, businesses


反义词：sole proprietorships, partnerships (in the sense of organizational structure)


词性：名词
