dendrometer:/dɛnˈdrɒmɪtər/ | n. |树木直径测量仪 | An instrument used to measure the diameter of trees.  It typically uses a set of calipers or a tape measure to accurately determine the girth of a tree trunk at breast height (DBH). Dendrometers are essential tools in forestry, arboriculture, and ecological research for assessing tree growth, health, and overall forest inventory. | Example: The forester used a dendrometer to measure the diameter of the ancient oak tree.  林务员用树木直径测量仪测量了这棵古老橡树的直径。 |近义词: tree diameter gauge, girth tape |反义词: None
