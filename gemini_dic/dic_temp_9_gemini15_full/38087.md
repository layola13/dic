composters:/kəmˈpoʊstərz/ | n. | 堆肥者；堆肥机 | A person or machine that composts.  Composters are used to break down organic waste into compost. |例句：Many gardeners use composters to enrich their soil. 许多园丁使用堆肥机来改良土壤。

例句：The city provides composters to residents for free.  城市免费向居民提供堆肥机。


近义词：composting units, decomposition machines | 反义词：None (No direct antonym exists) | 词性：名词
