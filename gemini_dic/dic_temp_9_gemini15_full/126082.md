musts:/mʌsts/| n.|必需品，必须做的事|Things that are necessary or required.  It often refers to a list of essential items or actions.|例句：The musts for a successful camping trip include a tent, sleeping bag, and food. 露营成功的必备物品包括帐篷、睡袋和食物。

例句：My musts for this weekend are to finish my report and go grocery shopping.  这个周末我必须完成我的报告和去杂货店购物。


近义词：essentials, necessities, requirements|反义词：non-essentials, optional
