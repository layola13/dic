mahjong:/ˈmɑːdʒɒŋ/ /ˈmæʒɒŋ/| n.|麻将|A tile-based game of Chinese origin, played by four players with 136 tiles, each bearing a suit, number, or character.  The goal is generally to form specific sets of tiles (e.g., pungs, kongs, chows) and a winning hand. |例句：We played mahjong until late into the night. 我们打麻将打到深夜。

例句：Learning to play mahjong can be a fun and challenging experience. 学习打麻将是一次有趣且具有挑战性的经历。


近义词：tile game (泛指)


反义词：无


词性：名词
