draped:/ˈdreɪpt/ | adj. | 悬垂的；覆盖着的 | Covered or adorned with something hanging loosely; hanging in folds.  |The walls were draped with velvet curtains.  墙壁上垂挂着天鹅绒窗帘。| draped, hung, covered | stiff, erect | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fabric drapes beautifully. = 这布料垂坠得很好看。
The curtains drape elegantly from the rod. = 窗帘优雅地从杆子上垂下。


一般过去时:
She draped the shawl around her shoulders. = 她披着披肩。
He draped the cloth over the table. = 他把布盖在桌子上。


现在完成时:
The artist has draped the model in silk. = 艺术家已用丝绸为模特儿披上衣服。
The room has been draped in festive decorations. = 房间里已经装饰了节日的装饰品。


过去完成时:
She had draped the flowers along the mantlepiece before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已将鲜花沿着壁炉架摆放。
They had draped the stage with beautiful fabrics for the performance. = 他们为了演出，已经用美丽的布料装饰了舞台。


将来时:
She will drape the dress on the mannequin. = 她将把衣服披在模特儿身上。
They will drape the hall with banners for the celebration. = 他们将为庆祝活动在大厅里悬挂横幅。


虚拟语气:
If she draped the fabric differently, it would look better. = 如果她把布料垂挂得不一样，看起来会更好。
I wish I had draped the tablecloth more carefully. = 我真希望我更仔细地铺好桌布。


被动语态:
The statue was draped in a flowing robe. = 雕像披着飘逸的长袍。
The stage will be draped with colorful lights. = 舞台将被五彩缤纷的灯光装饰。


疑问句:
How should I drape this material? = 我应该如何悬挂这种材料？
Is the table draped properly for the dinner? = 这张桌子为晚餐摆放妥当了吗？


否定句:
The curtains didn't drape well; they looked uneven. = 窗帘垂挂得不好；看起来不均匀。
She didn't drape the scarf correctly; it slipped off her shoulder. = 她没有正确地披上围巾；它从她的肩膀上滑落了。



PHRASE->
draped in (something):  穿着…；披着…
draped with (something): 用…装饰；覆盖着…
heavily draped:  厚重地垂挂着
loosely draped: 松松垮垮地垂挂着
elegantly draped:  优雅地垂挂着

词源:
源自古法语 *drapier，意为“覆盖，遮盖”，最终源自拉丁语 *drappus，意为“布料”。
