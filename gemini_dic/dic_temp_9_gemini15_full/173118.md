quasi-actively:/ˈkwɑːzaɪˈæktɪvli/ | adv. | 准主动地，似主动地 |  Describes an action or process that resembles active behavior but isn't fully or genuinely active.  It suggests a partial or incomplete level of activity, often implying a degree of passivity or dependence on external factors. | The system operates quasi-actively, responding to stimuli but not initiating actions independently.  系统准主动运行，对刺激作出反应，但不独立启动动作。|近义词：partially actively, seemingly actively | 反义词：passively, inactively
