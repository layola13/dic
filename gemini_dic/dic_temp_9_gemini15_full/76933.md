gaper:/ˈɡeɪpər/| n. |张嘴的人；傻瓜；裂缝| A person who gapes; a foolish or stupid person; a wide opening or crack.|例句：The gaper stared at the accident with his mouth open. 张嘴的人目瞪口呆地看着事故。 The old building had several gapers in its walls. 这座老建筑的墙上有一些裂缝。|近义词：gawker, fool, simpleton |反义词：attentive, intelligent, shrewd


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He gapes at the television all evening. 他整个晚上都对着电视发呆。
The crack gapes wide open. 裂缝张得大大的。

一般过去时:
She gaped at the unexpected sight. 她对这意外的景象张大了嘴。
The wound gaped horribly. 伤口骇人地裂开了。

现在完成时:
He has gaped at the screen for hours. 他对着屏幕发呆好几个小时了。
The earth has gaped open, revealing a chasm. 大地裂开了，露出一个深渊。

过去完成时:
She had gaped in astonishment before she could react. 她惊呆了张大了嘴，还没来得及反应。
The crack had gaped wider after the earthquake. 地震后裂缝裂得更大了。

将来时:
He will gape at the beautiful scenery. 他将会对着美丽的风景发呆。
The ground will gape open if the dam breaks. 如果水坝决堤，地面将会裂开。

虚拟语气:
If he had seen it, he would have gaped in disbelief. 如果他看到了，他会惊讶得张大嘴巴。
I wish I hadn't gaped so rudely at her. 我真希望我没有那么粗鲁地盯着她看。

被动语态:
The chasm was gaped at by the onlookers. 旁观者们看着深渊。
The wound was gaped at by the doctor. 医生看着伤口。

疑问句:
Why are you gaping at me like that? 你为什么那样盯着我看？
Does the crack gape wide enough to see through? 裂缝张得够大能看透吗？

否定句:
He didn't gape at the accident. 他没有盯着事故看。
The door didn't gape open. 门没有裂开。
