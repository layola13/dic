/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈdʒɛstɪŋ/ | v. | 消化不良，过度消化 | The act of digesting food too slowly or incompletely, often resulting in discomfort or digestive issues.  It can also refer to mentally overanalyzing something to the point of exhaustion. | Example:  He experienced overdigesting after eating a large, greasy meal. 他在吃了一顿油腻的大餐后消化不良。  She was overdigesting the details of the project, leading to stress. 她对项目的细节过度分析，导致压力过大。|近义词：indigestion, dyspepsia, overthinking |反义词：easy digestion, efficient digestion |动词
