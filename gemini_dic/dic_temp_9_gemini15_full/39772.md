consolingly:/kənˈsoʊlɪŋli/ | adv. | 安慰地，抚慰地 | In a way that gives comfort or consolation.  It describes the manner in which something is done, offering comfort or solace. | Example: She looked at him consolingly. 她安慰地看着他。 He spoke consolingly to the grieving widow. 他安慰地对悲伤的寡妇说话。 | 近义词：comfortingly, soothingly, reassuringly | 反义词：harshly, cruelly, unsympathetically
