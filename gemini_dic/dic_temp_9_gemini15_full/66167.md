excess:/ˈɛksɛs/| n. adj.|过量；过剩；过度；过分的| exceeding what is necessary or usual; more than is needed or wanted.|The excess baggage fee was expensive.  行李超重费很贵。|  There was an excess of enthusiasm at the meeting. 会议上热情高涨。| surplus, superfluity | deficiency, shortage | 名词，形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
There is an excess of food. = 食物过剩。
He shows excess caution. = 他表现出过分的谨慎。


一般过去时:
There was an excess of rain last year. = 去年雨水过多。
She displayed excess emotion. = 她表现出过多的情绪。


现在完成时:
We have had an excess of problems this month. = 这个月我们遇到了很多问题。
He has shown excess confidence in his abilities. = 他对自己的能力表现出过度的自信。


过去完成时:
They had already consumed an excess of alcohol before the accident. = 事故发生前他们已经喝了过量的酒。
She had displayed excess aggression in the past. = 她过去曾表现出过度的侵略性。


将来时:
There will be an excess of applications for the job. = 这份工作会有过多的申请。
He will likely show excess anxiety before the exam. = 他考试前可能会表现出过度的焦虑。


虚拟语气:
If there were an excess of resources, we could solve the problem. = 如果资源充足，我们就能解决这个问题。
If he hadn't shown excess arrogance, he might have been promoted. = 如果他不那么傲慢，他可能就升职了。


被动语态:
Excess energy is often wasted. = 多余的能量经常被浪费。
Excess weight can be harmful to health. = 超重对健康有害。


疑问句:
Is there an excess of sugar in this recipe? = 这个食谱里糖分过量了吗？
Does he show any excess of vanity? = 他有表现出过分的虚荣心吗？


否定句:
There is no excess of information. = 信息并不过剩。
He doesn't show any excess of aggression. = 他没有表现出任何过分的侵略性。


PHRASE->

in excess of = 超过
excess capacity = 剩余产能
to excess = 过度地
excess weight = 超重
excess profit = 超额利润
