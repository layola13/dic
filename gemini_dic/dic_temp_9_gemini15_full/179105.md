redetermines: /ˌriːdiˈtɜːrmɪnz/ | v. | 重新决定；再确定 | To determine again; to establish or fix again.  The process involves reconsidering and potentially altering a previous decision or conclusion. |


例句：

The committee redetermined the budget after reviewing new data. = 委员会在审查了新的数据后重新确定了预算。

The court redetermined the sentence, considering mitigating circumstances. = 法院考虑到减轻处罚的情况，重新确定了判决。


近义词：reassess, redefine, revise, reconsider

反义词：confirm (in the sense of not changing a previous decision)


词性：动词
