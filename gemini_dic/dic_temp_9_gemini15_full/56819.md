dorm:/dɔːrm/| n. |宿舍，寝室 |A dorm is a room or a building where students live, especially at a college or university.  |Example: I live in a dorm on campus. 我住在校园里的宿舍里。|近义词：dormitory, residence hall |反义词：private room, apartment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lives in a dorm. = 他住在宿舍里。
The dorm provides laundry facilities. = 宿舍提供洗衣设施。

一般过去时:
I lived in a dorm during my freshman year. = 我大一的时候住在宿舍里。
They shared a dorm room. = 他们合住一个宿舍房间。

现在完成时:
She has lived in this dorm for two years. = 她在这个宿舍住了两年了。
Many students have experienced dorm life. = 许多学生都体验过宿舍生活。

过去完成时:
I had lived in a dorm before I moved into my apartment. = 在我搬进公寓之前，我住在宿舍里。
They had shared a dorm room before they had a fight. = 他们吵架之前是合住一个宿舍房间的。

将来时:
He will live in a dorm next semester. = 下学期他将住在宿舍里。
They will move into a new dorm building. = 他们将搬进新的宿舍楼。

虚拟语气:
If I lived in a dorm, I would save money. = 如果我住在宿舍里，我会省钱。
I wish I had lived in a dorm during college. = 我真希望大学时住在宿舍里。

被动语态:
The dorm rooms are cleaned weekly. = 宿舍房间每周打扫一次。
The dorm is managed by the university. = 宿舍由大学管理。

疑问句:
Do you live in a dorm? = 你住在宿舍里吗？
Is the dorm air-conditioned? = 宿舍有空调吗？

否定句:
He doesn't live in a dorm anymore. = 他不再住在宿舍里了。
She didn't like living in the dorm. = 她不喜欢住在宿舍里。
