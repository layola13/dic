microexpressions:/ˌmaɪkroʊɪkˈsprɛʃənz/| n. |微表情| Extremely brief, involuntary facial expressions that reveal true emotions, often contradicting consciously controlled expressions. They are fleeting and subtle, lasting only a fraction of a second.  | Example:  Detecting microexpressions can be a valuable skill for law enforcement and in lie detection.  微表情的侦测对执法和测谎来说是一项宝贵的技能。 |近义词：facial expressions, involuntary expressions |反义词：macroexpressions, posed expressions |名词
