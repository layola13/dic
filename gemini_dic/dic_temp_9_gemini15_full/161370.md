postscholastic: /ˌpoʊstskəˈlæstɪk/ | adj. |  学业结束后的；毕业后的 |  Relating to the period of time after formal schooling has ended.  It often implies a continued engagement with intellectual pursuits or a focus on activities beyond the scope of formal education. |


例句：

His postscholastic years were marked by extensive travel and independent study.  他的学业结束后，他进行了广泛的旅行和独立学习。

She pursued a career in art after her postscholastic period.  她在学业结束后从事艺术事业。


近义词：postgraduate, post-educational, after-school (less formal)


反义词：scholastic, pre-college, undergraduate


词性：形容词
