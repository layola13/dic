nonattention:/ˌnɒnəˈtɛnʃən/| n. |非注意；不注意；忽略 | The lack of attention; the state of not paying attention.  It often refers to a cognitive process where one's attention is not actively focused on a specific stimulus or task, but rather passively or diffusely directed.  This can be due to various factors including distraction, fatigue, or lack of interest.  |Example: The driver's nonattention to the road caused the accident.  司机对路况的不注意导致了事故。|近义词：inattention, disregard, neglect |反义词：attention, focus, concentration | 名词
