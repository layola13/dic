connoisseurs:/ˌkɒnəˈsɜːrz/| n.|鉴赏家，行家|A connoisseur has expert knowledge and keen appreciation in a particular field, especially in the fine arts, food, or wine.|例句：He is a connoisseur of fine wines. 他是一位葡萄酒鉴赏家。|近义词：expert, specialist, aficionado|反义词：novice, amateur, beginner


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a connoisseur of art. = 他是一位艺术鉴赏家。
She is a connoisseur of fine food. = 她是一位美食鉴赏家。

一般过去时:
He was a connoisseur of classical music. = 他是一位古典音乐鉴赏家。
She was a connoisseur of antique furniture. = 她是一位古董家具鉴赏家。

现在完成时:
She has become a connoisseur of Japanese tea ceremonies. = 她已经成为一位日本茶道鉴赏家。
He has cultivated his palate and become a connoisseur of wines. = 他培养了自己的味觉，并成为一位葡萄酒鉴赏家。

过去完成时:
Before his travels, he had already been a connoisseur of French cuisine. = 在旅行之前，他已经是法国美食的鉴赏家了。
They had become connoisseurs of vintage cars before they moved to the countryside. = 在他们搬到乡下之前，他们就已经是老爷车的鉴赏家了。

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
