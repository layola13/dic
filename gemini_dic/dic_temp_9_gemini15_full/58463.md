ducting:/ˈdʌktɪŋ/| n. |管道工程；管道系统；导管；风管|The process of conveying something through a duct or ducts; a system of ducts.  It often refers to the installation and arrangement of air ducts in a building's HVAC system. |例句：The building's ductwork needs to be inspected for leaks. 这栋建筑的管道系统需要检查是否有泄漏。|例句：The duct system was designed to efficiently distribute air throughout the house.  这个管道系统的设计是为了有效地将空气输送到房屋的各个地方。|近义词：conduiting, channeling, tubing |反义词：None directly applicable.
