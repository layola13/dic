overindulged:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌldʒd/ | adj. |纵容的；过度放纵的 |  Having indulged excessively; excessively gratified.  |例句：He felt overindulged and spoiled by his parents. 他感觉被父母过度溺爱和宠坏了。
例句：After a week of overindulged eating, she felt quite unwell.  在一周的暴饮暴食后，她感觉很不舒服。|近义词：spoiled, pampered, coddled |反义词：deprived, neglected, disciplined


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The child is overindulged by his grandparents. = 这个孩子被他的祖父母过度溺爱。
He overindulges in sweets. = 他沉迷于甜食。

一般过去时:
She overindulged at the buffet. = 她在自助餐上吃得太多。
They overindulged in alcohol at the party. = 他们在派对上喝了过多的酒。

现在完成时:
I have overindulged recently and need to cut back. = 我最近放纵过度，需要节制一下。
She has overindulged in rich foods and now feels sick. = 她吃了太多油腻的食物，现在感觉不舒服。

过去完成时:
He had overindulged the night before, so he felt sluggish. = 他前一天晚上纵容过度，所以感觉很迟钝。
They had overindulged in the sun and got sunburnt. = 他们过度晒太阳，结果晒伤了。

将来时:
I will not overindulge at the holiday feast. = 我不会在节日盛宴上放纵自己。
They will likely overindulge if given the chance. = 如果有机会，他们很可能会放纵自己。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't overindulged, I wouldn't feel so ill. = 如果我没有放纵自己，我就不会感觉这么难受。
I wish I hadn't overindulged yesterday. = 我真希望我昨天没有放纵自己。

被动语态:
The children were overindulged by their parents. = 孩子们被他们的父母过度溺爱。
His desires were overindulged to a fault.  他的欲望被过度满足，以至于过分了。

疑问句:
Were the guests overindulged at the party? = 派对上的宾客们是不是吃喝太多了？
Has he been overindulged his whole life? = 他一生都被过度溺爱了吗？

否定句:
The parents did not overindulge their children. = 父母没有过度溺爱他们的孩子。
She did not overindulge in dessert. = 她没有吃太多的甜点。
