kicker:/ˈkɪkər/| n. v.|踢球者；（新闻等的）结尾；（非正式）令人震惊的事；|英文解析：A kicker is primarily a person who kicks a ball, especially in football or soccer.  It can also refer to the concluding part of something, often a news story or a speech, designed to be impactful or memorable. In informal usage, it can mean something surprising or shocking.  As a verb, it means to kick. |例句：He's a powerful kicker for the team. 他是球队里一个强壮的踢球手。The article ended with a surprising kicker about the CEO's resignation. 这篇文章以关于首席执行官辞职的令人震惊的结尾而告终。|近义词：placekicker (n.),  conclusion (n.),  shocker (n.),  twist (n.)|反义词：beginning (n.),  introduction (n.)
