re-cabling:/ˌriːˈkeɪbəlɪŋ/ | n. |重新布线 |The process of replacing or reinstalling cables.  It often involves removing old, damaged, or inadequate cabling and installing new cabling to improve performance, reliability, or meet new requirements. |Example: The re-cabling of the entire building took several weeks.  整个建筑物的重新布线花费了数周时间。|近义词：rewiring, cable replacement |反义词：cable installation (if referring to the initial installation)
