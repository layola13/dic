immedi:/ɪˈmiːdi/ | adj. | 立即的，直接的，紧迫的 | immediate means happening or done without delay. It also refers to something that is next to or very close to something else.  |例句：The immediate cause of the fire was a faulty wire.  火灾的直接原因是电线故障。

The house next door is my immediate neighbour.  隔壁的房子是我的近邻。

近义词：instant, direct, prompt, urgent | 反义词：distant, remote, delayed, postponed
