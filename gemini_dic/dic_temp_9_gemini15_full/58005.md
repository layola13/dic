driveway:/ˈdraɪvweɪ/ | n. | 车道 | A hard surface area, usually made of concrete or asphalt, leading from a street or road to a garage or house.  It is used for vehicles to drive on. | Example:  We parked our car in the driveway. 我们把车停在了车道上。  The driveway is long and winding. 这条车道又长又弯曲。 | 近义词：access road, approach | 反义词：N/A
