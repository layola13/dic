justifications:/ˌdʒʌstɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |辩解；理由；正当理由 |Explanations or reasons given to justify a decision, action, or belief.  They aim to show something is right, proper, or reasonable. |例句：His justifications for his actions were unconvincing.  他的行为理由令人难以信服。

例句：The company needs to provide strong justifications for its pricing strategy. 这家公司需要为其定价策略提供充分的理由。


近义词：reasons, explanations, rationales, grounds, excuses


反义词：refutations, contradictions, denials


词性：名词
