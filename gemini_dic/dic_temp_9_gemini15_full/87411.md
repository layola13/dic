herself:/hərˈsɛlf/ | pron. | 她自己 | herself is a reflexive pronoun, referring back to a female subject. It indicates that the subject performs the action upon itself.  | She dressed herself quickly. 她迅速地穿好了衣服。She blamed herself for the accident. 她为这次事故责备自己。 | herself | himself, itself, myself, yourself, themselves |  

USAGE->
一般现在时:
She teaches herself French. = 她自学法语。
She prepares herself for the exam. = 她为考试做准备。


一般过去时:
She hurt herself playing sports. = 她运动时弄伤了自己。
She dressed herself in a hurry. = 她匆匆忙忙地穿好了衣服。


现在完成时:
She has taught herself to play the piano. = 她自学了钢琴。
She has convinced herself that she is right. = 她已经说服自己她是对的。


过去完成时:
She had already prepared herself for the worst. = 她已经为最坏的情况做好了准备。
She had dressed herself before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经穿戴好了。


将来时:
She will reward herself after finishing the project. = 项目完成后，她将犒劳自己。
She will teach herself coding next year. = 她明年将自学编程。


虚拟语气:
If she were to help herself, she would feel better. = 如果她能帮助自己，她会感觉更好。
She wishes she could control herself better. = 她希望自己能够更好地控制自己。


被动语态:
(Reflexive pronouns are rarely used in the passive voice.)  The passive voice doesn't generally work with reflexive pronouns because the action is done to the subject.


疑问句:
Did she hurt herself? = 她伤到自己了吗？
Does she blame herself? = 她责备自己吗？


否定句:
She didn't hurt herself. = 她没有伤到自己。
She doesn't believe in herself. = 她不相信自己。


PHRASE->
by herself = 单独地，独自地
for herself = 为她自己
to herself = 自言自语地
in herself = 本身
of herself = 自发地
