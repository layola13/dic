reconfront:/ˌriːkənˈfrʌnt/| vt.|再次面对；重新对抗|To confront again; to face or deal with (something) again.|例句：The police decided to reconfront the suspect with new evidence.  警方决定用新的证据再次质问嫌疑人。
例句：The author reconfronted his past traumas in his latest novel. 作者在他的最新小说中再次面对了他过去的创伤。|近义词：re-engage, challenge, face|反义词：avoid, ignore, evade|动词
