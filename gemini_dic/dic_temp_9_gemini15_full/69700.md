fenland:/ˈfenlænd/| n.|芬兰低地；沼泽地 |Low-lying, marshy land, especially in eastern England.  The term often refers specifically to the fertile lowlands of eastern England, characterized by peat bogs and historically drained for agriculture.|例句：The fenland is a unique ecosystem, home to diverse flora and fauna. 芬兰低地是一个独特的生态系统，是多种动植物的家园。|The East Anglian fenland has been extensively cultivated for centuries.东安格利亚的芬兰低地几个世纪以来一直被广泛耕种。|近义词：marsh, bog, wetland, moor|反义词：upland, highland|名词
