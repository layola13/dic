moult:/moʊlt/| /mɒlt/| v.|换毛；蜕皮；脱落|To shed or cast off (a covering, such as feathers, skin, or horns), typically periodically.  |例句：Birds moult their feathers annually. 鸟类每年换羽。|例句：The snake moulted its skin. 蛇蜕皮了。|近义词：shed, cast off, slough|反义词：grow, retain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird moults its feathers every year. = 这只鸟每年换羽。
He moults his skin regularly. = 他定期蜕皮。


一般过去时:
The snake moulted its skin last night. = 蛇昨晚蜕皮了。
The caterpillar moulted before it became a butterfly. = 毛毛虫在变成蝴蝶之前蜕皮了。


现在完成时:
The bird has moulted its feathers already. = 这只鸟已经换羽了。
The snake has moulted three times this year. = 蛇今年已经蜕皮三次了。


过去完成时:
The bird had moulted before winter arrived. = 冬天到来之前，鸟已经换羽了。
The snake had moulted several times before it reached maturity. = 蛇在成熟之前已经蜕皮好几次了。


将来时:
The bird will moult its feathers next spring. = 这只鸟明年春天将换羽。
The snake will moult again in a few weeks. = 蛇几周后将再次蜕皮。


虚拟语气:
If the bird moulted its feathers earlier, it might have survived the winter. = 如果鸟早点换羽，它可能就能熬过冬天了。
If the snake had moulted properly, it would not be so weak. = 如果蛇正常蜕皮，它就不会这么虚弱了。


被动语态:
The feathers were moulted by the bird in autumn. = 秋天，鸟的羽毛脱落了。(被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The skin is being moulted by the snake. = 蛇正在蜕皮。(被动语态略显不自然，主动语态更常用)


疑问句:
Does the bird moult its feathers every year? = 这只鸟每年都换羽吗？
When will the snake moult again? = 蛇什么时候会再次蜕皮？


否定句:
The bird didn't moult its feathers this year. = 这只鸟今年没有换羽。
The snake hasn't moulted yet. = 蛇还没蜕皮。


PHRASE->
moult feathers = 换羽

pre-moult = 换羽前
