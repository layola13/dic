shepherdship: /ˈʃɛpərdʃɪp/ | n. | 牧养；监护；领导；指导 | The act or practice of shepherding;  the care and guidance of a group of people or things.  It implies responsibility, protection, and leadership. |例句：His shepherdship of the company through difficult times was commendable. 他在公司度过难关时的领导才能值得称赞。


例句：The church appreciated the shepherd's careful shepherdship of his flock. 教堂赞赏牧师对羊群的细心照料。


近义词：guidance, leadership, supervision, care, guardianship | 反义词：neglect, abandonment, mismanagement
