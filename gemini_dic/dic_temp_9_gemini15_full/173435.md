quasi-essential:/ˈkwɑːziːˈɛsənʃəl/ | adj. | 准必需的，近乎必要的 |  Almost essential; necessary but not absolutely vital.  It implies a degree of importance that falls short of absolute necessity but is still significant. | Example:  Regular exercise is quasi-essential for maintaining good health.  规律的锻炼对于保持健康来说几乎是必要的。  | 近义词:  nearly essential, almost indispensable, highly desirable | 反义词:  unnecessary, dispensable, optional |
