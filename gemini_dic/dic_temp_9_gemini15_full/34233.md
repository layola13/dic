clanless: /ˈklæn.ləs/ | adj. | 无氏族的；没有宗族的；没有家族的 | Lacking a clan or tribe; without tribal affiliations.  The term often implies a sense of isolation or rootlessness. | 例句：The orphan felt clanless and adrift in the vast city.  这个孤儿在广阔的城市里感到无依无靠。  He lived a clanless existence, detached from any social group or family ties. 他过着没有家族的生活，与任何社会群体或家庭关系脱节。 | 近义词：rootless, unattached, isolated, unaffiliated | 反义词：tribal, clannish, affiliated
