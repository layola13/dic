lobbed:/lɒbd/ /lɑːbd/| vt.| 投掷，高高抛出 | To throw (something) in a high arc.  It often implies a gentle or casual throw, not a forceful one. |例句：He lobbed the ball over the net. 他把球高高抛过网。

例句：She lobbed a question to the speaker. 她向演讲者抛出一个问题。


近义词：toss, pitch, hurl, fling| 反义词：catch, receive
