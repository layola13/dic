eulogistical:/ˌjuːləˈdʒɪstɪkəl/ | adj. |赞美的；颂扬的；悼词的 |  Characterized by or expressing eulogy; praising.  It refers to speech or writing that praises someone or something highly, often in a formal setting like a funeral eulogy. | Example: The eulogistical speech at the memorial service was both moving and inspiring.  赞美纪念仪式上的悼词既感人又鼓舞人心。| 近义词：laudatory, commendatory, panegyrical | 反义词：critical, condemnatory, derogatory
