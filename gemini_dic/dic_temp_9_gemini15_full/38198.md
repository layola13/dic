compulsives:/kəmˈpʌlsɪvz/ | n. | 强迫症患者；有强迫行为的人 | People with compulsive behaviors, often characterized by repetitive actions or thoughts that they feel compelled to perform.  | Example: The therapist worked with several compulsives struggling with obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD).  治疗师与几位患有强迫症的强迫症患者一起工作。|近义词: addicts, obsessive people | 反义词:  relaxed individuals, people with self-control | 名词
