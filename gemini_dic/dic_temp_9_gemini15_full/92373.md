hypochondria: /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndrɪə/| /ˌhaɪpəˈkɑːndrɪə/ | n. | 疑病症，臆想病 |  An excessive preoccupation with one's health and a tendency to believe that one has or is about to develop a serious illness, often despite medical reassurance to the contrary.  | Example:  Her hypochondria led her to seek medical attention for every minor ache and pain.  她的疑病症使她对每一次轻微的疼痛都寻求医疗救助。 |近义词：health anxiety, illness anxiety disorder | 反义词：None specifically, but could be considered opposite to robust health and wellbeing. | 名词
