illusion: /ɪˈluːʒən/ | n. | 错觉；幻觉；假象 | A false perception of reality; something that is not what it seems.  |例句：The magician created the illusion of a disappearing rabbit. 魔术师创造了兔子消失的错觉。

例句：His wealth was merely an illusion; he was deeply in debt. 他的财富只是个假象；他负债累累。


近义词：delusion, hallucination, fantasy, mirage

反义词：reality, truth, fact

词源:
From Middle French *illusion, from Latin illudere "to mock, deceive," from in- "in, on" + ludere "to play."  The meaning evolved from "mocking" to "deception" and then to "false perception."
