headlock:/ˈhɛdˌlɒk/ | n. | 颈部锁喉；抱头锁 | A wrestling hold in which one person locks their arm around another person's neck and head, restricting their movement.  | Example: The wrestler applied a headlock, forcing his opponent to submit.  摔跤手使出抱头锁，迫使对手认输。 |近义词：head hold, stranglehold (in a more violent context) | 反义词：release, free
