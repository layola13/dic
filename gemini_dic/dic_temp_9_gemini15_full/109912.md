libations:/laɪˈbeɪʃənz/| n.|祭酒；酒宴；饮酒|The act of pouring a liquid offering to a deity; a drink, especially an alcoholic one.|例句：The priest offered libations to the gods.  祭司向众神献上了祭酒。
例句：We enjoyed a variety of libations at the party.我们在派对上享用了各种各样的酒。|近义词：offerings, drinks, beverages|反义词：abstinence, sobriety
