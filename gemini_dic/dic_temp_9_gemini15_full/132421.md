nondependable: /ˌnɒn.dɪˈpɛn.də.bəl/ | adj. |不可靠的，不可信赖的 | Not reliable; likely to fail or not perform as expected.  This word describes something or someone that cannot be depended upon consistently to function correctly or fulfill obligations.  |  例句：The old car is nondependable; it breaks down frequently.  这辆旧车不可靠；它经常抛锚。

例句：His testimony was deemed nondependable due to inconsistencies. 由于前后矛盾，他的证词被认为不可靠。


近义词：unreliable, undependable, untrustworthy, fickle, erratic, capricious


反义词：reliable, dependable, trustworthy, consistent, stable
