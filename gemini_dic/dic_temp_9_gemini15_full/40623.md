convening:/kənˈviːnɪŋ/| v.|召集，召开；集合|The act of assembling or bringing people together, typically for a meeting or other formal gathering.  It implies a formal or official nature to the gathering. |例句：The committee is convening to discuss the new budget. 委员会正在召开会议讨论新的预算。

例句：The president is convening a press conference to address the crisis. 总统正在召开新闻发布会来处理这场危机。


近义词：summoning, assembling, calling together, gathering


反义词：dispersing, adjourning, dismissing


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The chairman convenes the meeting every Monday. 主席每周一召开会议。
She convenes a team meeting to discuss project updates. 她召集团队会议讨论项目更新。


一般过去时:
The judge convened the court to hear the case. 法官召集法院审理此案。
They convened a special meeting to address the emergency. 他们召集了一次特别会议来处理紧急情况。


现在完成时:
The committee has convened three times this month. 委员会本月已经召开了三次会议。
The organization has convened numerous workshops on the topic. 该组织已经就该主题召开了多次研讨会。


过去完成时:
The jury had convened before the evidence was presented. 在证据提交之前，陪审团已经集结完毕。
After the incident, the board had convened an emergency session. 事故发生后，董事会召开了一次紧急会议。


将来时:
The council will convene next week to vote on the proposal. 理事会下周将召开会议对提案进行投票。
The leaders will convene a summit to discuss international relations. 领导人们将召开峰会讨论国际关系。


虚拟语气:
If the president convened a meeting, the issue might be resolved. 如果总统召开会议，这个问题或许就能解决。
I wish the committee had convened sooner to address the problem. 我希望委员会早点召开会议来解决这个问题。


被动语态:
The meeting was convened by the CEO. 会议是由首席执行官召开的。
A special session was convened to discuss the budget.  一次特别会议被召开以讨论预算。


疑问句:
When will the committee convene? 委员会什么时候召开？
Has the board convened yet? 董事会已经召开了吗？


否定句:
The meeting did not convene on time. 会议没有按时召开。
The group has not yet convened to discuss the plan.  该小组尚未召开会议讨论该计划。

词源:
From Old French convenir, from Latin convenire "to come together," from com- "together" + venire "to come."
