slightest:/ˈslaɪtɪst/| adj.|最轻微的；最小的|The smallest or least degree of something.  It emphasizes the minimum amount or degree.|例句：I didn't have the slightest idea what he was talking about. 我一点也不知道他在说什么。

例句：There wasn't the slightest chance of rain.  一点下雨的可能性都没有。


近义词：minimal, infinitesimal, trifling, negligible|反义词：significant, considerable, substantial, major|词性：形容词
