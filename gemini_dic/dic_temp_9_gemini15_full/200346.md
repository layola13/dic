skunky:/ˈskʌŋki/| adj.|臭鼬的；有臭鼬气味的；难闻的；|Having a strong, unpleasant odor like that of a skunk.  The word often implies a pungent, musky smell, not just simply "bad smelling".|例句：The garbage smelled skunky. 垃圾闻起来有臭鼬味。

The air in the alley was skunky and thick with smoke.  小巷里的空气又臭又浓烟滚滚。|近义词：musky, pungent, fetid, rank, foul|反义词：fragrant, sweet-smelling, aromatic
