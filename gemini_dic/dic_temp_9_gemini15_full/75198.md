frock-coat:/frɒk.kəʊt/| n. |长礼服；燕尾服|A frock coat is a long, usually dark-colored coat with tails, worn by men in the 19th century.  It was a formal garment, often worn for occasions such as formal dinners or official events.|The gentleman wore a long frock coat to the ball.|近义词：tailcoat, morning coat|反义词：None specifically, but informal clothing like jeans or t-shirts could be considered opposites.
