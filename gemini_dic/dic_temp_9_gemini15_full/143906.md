outpraising:/ˌaʊtˈpreɪzɪŋ/| v.|过分赞扬，吹捧|Excessively praising someone or something; to praise beyond what is deserved or reasonable.  |例句：His outpraising of the new policy seemed insincere.  他的过度赞扬新政策显得不真诚。


例句：The critics' outpraising of the film made many viewers skeptical. 批评家对这部电影的过分赞扬让许多观众感到怀疑。|近义词：overpraising, exaggerating, flattering, extolling|反义词：criticizing, belittling, downplaying, condemning|词性:动词
