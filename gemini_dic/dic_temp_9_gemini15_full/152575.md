peace-keeper:/ˈpiːsˌkiːpər/| n. |维和人员，维持和平者 | A peacekeeper is a person or group that works to maintain peace and prevent conflict, often through diplomatic means or by providing security in conflict zones.  They may be part of a peacekeeping force authorized by an international organization like the United Nations. | Example: UN peacekeepers are deployed to many conflict areas around the world to protect civilians and promote stability.  联合国维和人员被部署到世界各地的许多冲突地区，以保护平民和促进稳定。|近义词：mediator, arbitrator, peacemaker |反义词：aggressor, warmonger
