navigation:/ˌnævɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. |导航；航行；导航系统 |The act or process of navigating, especially the planning or plotting of a course; the science or art of navigation.  Navigation involves determining one's position and plotting a course to a destination.  |例句：The ship's navigation system failed during the storm. 这艘船的导航系统在暴风雨中失灵了。

例句：Good navigation is essential for a successful sea voyage.  良好的航海导航对于一次成功的航海至关重要。


近义词：guidance, piloting, steering | 反义词： aimlessness, wandering
