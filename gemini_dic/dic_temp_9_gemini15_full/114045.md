magnetically:/mæɡˈnɛtɪkli/| adv.| 磁性地；有吸引力地 |  In a way that relates to or uses magnetism.  The word describes something done or occurring through the force of magnetism, or in a way that is powerfully attractive or compelling. |例句：The particles were magnetically attracted to each other.  这些粒子被磁力互相吸引。

The compass needle moved magnetically towards the north. 指南针被磁力吸引指向北方。|近义词：attractively, compellingly |反义词：repulsively, unattractively
