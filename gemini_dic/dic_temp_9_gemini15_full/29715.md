catholicness:/ˌkæθˈɒlɪknəs/ | n. |天主教性；普遍性；包容性 | The quality of being catholic; broadness of mind or outlook; inclusiveness.  It often refers to the universality and comprehensiveness of the Catholic Church, but can also be used more broadly to describe a wide-ranging or inclusive nature.  | Example: The museum's collection showcased the catholicness of its artistic vision, spanning centuries and diverse styles.  博物馆的藏品展示了其艺术视野的包容性，跨越了几个世纪和多种风格。  |近义词：universality, comprehensiveness, inclusiveness, breadth | 反义词：narrow-mindedness, exclusiveness, sectarianism | 名词
