significatory:/sɪɡˈnɪfɪkətəri/| adj.|具有意义的；象征性的；预示的 | conveying or suggestive of meaning; symbolic; indicative.  |The ritual was significatory of the tribe's deep-seated beliefs.  这个仪式象征着部落根深蒂固的信仰。|significant, symbolic, representative|insignificant, meaningless|形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The painting is significatory of the artist's inner turmoil. = 这幅画象征着艺术家内心的骚动。
This symbol is significatory of peace and harmony. = 这个符号象征着和平与和谐。

The dream was significatory of future events. = 这个梦预示着未来的事情。
Her silence was significatory of her disapproval. = 她的沉默表明了她不同意。

现在完成时:
The events have been significatory of a shift in power. = 这些事件预示着权力发生了转移。
His actions have been significatory of his true intentions. = 他的行为已经表明了他的真实意图。

过去完成时:
The clues had been significatory of a larger conspiracy. = 线索已经暗示了一个更大的阴谋。
The signs had been significatory of an impending storm. = 迹象已经预示着一场即将到来的风暴。

将来时:
The upcoming election will be significatory of the country's future direction. = 即将到来的选举将预示着国家的未来方向。
His words will be significatory of his commitment to the cause. = 他的话语将预示着他对事业的承诺。

虚拟语气:
If the sign were significatory of danger, we would have heeded it. = 如果这个标志预示着危险，我们会注意到的。
If his gestures were more significatory, we would understand his intentions better. = 如果他的手势更有意义，我们会更好地理解他的意图。

被动语态:
The painting was considered significatory of the artist's emotional state. = 这幅画被认为象征着艺术家的情感状态。
The results were interpreted as significatory of a successful experiment. = 结果被解释为一次成功的实验的象征。

疑问句:
Is this gesture significatory of agreement or disagreement? = 这个手势象征着同意还是不同意？
Are these symbols significatory of anything important? = 这些符号象征着什么重要的事情吗？

否定句:
The symbol was not significatory of anything negative. = 这个符号并不象征着任何负面的东西。
His words were not significatory of a change of heart. = 他的话语并不预示着他的想法改变了。


PHRASE->
highly significatory = 非常有意义的
deeply significatory = 深度象征的
particularly significatory = 特别具有象征意义的
clearly significatory = 明显象征的
potentially significatory = 潜在地具有象征意义的


词源:
significatory 源自于拉丁语 "significare" (to signify)，意思是“表示”、“象征”或“意味着”。  "-tory" 是一个形容词后缀，表示“有…性质的”或“与…有关的”。
