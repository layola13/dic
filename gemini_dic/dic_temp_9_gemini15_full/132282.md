nondefendants:/ˌnɑːnˈdɪfɛndənts/| n. |非被告人，非当事人|Those who are not involved in a lawsuit or legal case as defendants.  Nondefendants are individuals or entities not named as parties to a legal action.|例句：The judge addressed the nondefendants present in the courtroom regarding the case's implications.|近义词：bystanders, uninvolved parties|反义词：defendants, parties to the suit|
