patreon:/ˈpeɪtriɒn/| n.|赞助人；资助者；庇护人|A patron is a person who gives financial or other support to a person, organization, cause, or activity.|例句：He is a patron of the arts. 他是一位艺术赞助人。|例句：Many patrons support the local theatre. 许多赞助人支持当地剧院。|近义词：sponsor, supporter, benefactor|反义词：recipient, beneficiary


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is a patron of the arts. = 他是一位艺术赞助人。
She regularly supports her favorite creators on Patreon. = 她定期在Patreon上支持她最喜欢的创作者。


一般过去时:
