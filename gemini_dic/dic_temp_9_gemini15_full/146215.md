overexpenditure:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspɛndɪtʃər/ | n. | 超支，过度支出 | The act of spending more money than was budgeted or planned.  It often implies a significant excess beyond the allocated funds. |例句：The company's overexpenditure on marketing led to financial difficulties. 公司在市场营销上的过度支出导致了财务困难。

例句：Their overexpenditure on the new project resulted in a significant budget deficit.  他们在新项目上的超支导致了严重的预算赤字。


近义词：excess spending, extravagance, profligacy |反义词：underspending, frugality, economy |词性：名词
