catfight:/ˈkætfaɪt/| n.|猫打架；女人间的争吵|A catfight is a fight between women, typically involving shouting, insults, and sometimes physical violence, often characterized by a petty and dramatic nature.  It often implies a spiteful and noisy quarrel.|例句：The two women got into a vicious catfight over a man. 这两个女人为了一个男人大打出手。|例句：The reality show was full of catfights and drama. 这档真人秀充满了女人间的争吵和戏剧性冲突。|近义词：brawl, squabble, spat, altercation|反义词：agreement, harmony, peace
