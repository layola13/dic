punished:/ˈpʌnɪʃt/| v. |惩罚，处罚 |to inflict a penalty on (someone) for a wrongdoing.  |例句：The thief was punished for stealing. 小偷因偷窃而受到惩罚。|例句：He was punished severely for his mistake. 他因犯错而受到严厉惩罚。|近义词：penalized, chastised, disciplined |反义词：rewarded, commended


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They punish wrongdoers. = 他们惩罚做错事的人。
The school punishes students for cheating. = 学校惩罚学生作弊。

一般过去时:
The judge punished the criminal. = 法官惩罚了罪犯。
My parents punished me for lying. = 父母因为我撒谎而惩罚了我。

现在完成时:
The court has punished many criminals this year. = 法院今年已经惩罚了许多罪犯。
She has been punished repeatedly for her bad behavior. = 她因不良行为而屡次受罚。

过去完成时:
He had already been punished before the new evidence emerged. = 在新的证据出现之前，他已经受到了惩罚。
The teacher had punished the student for disrupting the class before the principal arrived. = 校长到达之前，老师已经因学生扰乱课堂而惩罚了他。

将来时:
The court will punish the defendant if he is found guilty. = 如果被告被判有罪，法院将对其进行惩罚。
They will be punished for their actions. = 他们将为他们的行为付出代价。

虚拟语气:
If he had studied harder, he wouldn't have been punished. = 如果他学习更努力，他就不会受到惩罚。
I wish I hadn't lied; I wouldn't have been punished. = 我真希望我没有撒谎；我就不会被惩罚了。

被动语态:
The criminal was punished severely. = 罪犯受到了严厉的惩罚。
The students were punished for their misbehavior. = 学生们因为他们的不良行为而受到了惩罚。

疑问句:
Will he be punished for his actions? = 他会因为他的行为而受到惩罚吗？
How were the offenders punished? = 罪犯是如何受到惩罚的？

否定句:
He wasn't punished for his mistake. = 他没有因为他的错误而受到惩罚。
The children weren't punished, only warned. = 孩子们没有受到惩罚，只是受到了警告。


PHRASE->
punished by = 被…惩罚
punished for = 因…而受罚
severely punished = 严厉惩罚
punishment for = 对…的惩罚
capital punishment = 死刑
