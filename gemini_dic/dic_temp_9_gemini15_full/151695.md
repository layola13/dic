partnered:/ˈpɑːrtnərd/| v. adj.|合作的；成为伙伴的|Combined or working together; having a partner.|例句：They partnered to create a successful business.他们合作创建了一家成功的企业。  例句：She is partnered with a talented musician. 她与一位才华横溢的音乐家合作。|近义词：collaborated, teamed, allied |反义词：separated, alone, unassociated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They partner on many projects. = 他们在许多项目上合作。
We partner with local businesses. = 我们与当地企业合作。

一般过去时:
The two companies partnered last year. = 这两家公司去年建立了合作关系。
He partnered with her on the dance. = 他和她一起跳舞。

现在完成时:
They have partnered successfully for five years. = 他们成功合作了五年。
She has partnered with several charities. = 她与几家慈善机构合作过。

过去完成时:
They had partnered before on similar projects. = 他们之前在类似的项目上合作过。
The artists had partnered on several exhibitions. = 这些艺术家曾在几个展览上合作。

将来时:
We will partner with a new technology company. = 我们将与一家新的科技公司合作。
They will partner on a new research project. = 他们将合作一个新的研究项目。

虚拟语气:
If they partnered with us, we would succeed. = 如果他们与我们合作，我们会成功。
I wish they had partnered with a more experienced team. = 我希望他们与一个更有经验的团队合作。

被动语态:
The project was partnered by a major corporation. = 该项目由一家大型公司参与合作。
The research was partnered by several universities. = 这项研究由几所大学合作完成。

疑问句:
Are they partnered with anyone else? = 他们还与其他人合作吗？
Who is she partnered with on this project? = 她在这个项目中与谁合作？

否定句:
They did not partner with the competitor. = 他们没有与竞争对手合作。
She was not partnered with anyone on the assignment. = 她在这个任务中没有与任何人合作。
