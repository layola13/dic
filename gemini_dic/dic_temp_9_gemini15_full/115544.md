mappable:/ˈmæpəbəl/| adj.| 可映射的，可绘制地图的 | Able to be mapped; suitable for mapping.  It refers to something that can be represented or transformed into a map or a similar structure. This could be data, territory, or a process. |例句：The terrain is mappable using satellite imagery.  使用卫星图像可以绘制该地形图。|  例句：This data set is mappable onto a geographical coordinate system. 这组数据可以映射到地理坐标系上。|近义词：representable, chart-able|反义词：unmappable, uncharted
