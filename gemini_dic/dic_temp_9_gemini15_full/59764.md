economist:/ɪˈkɒnəmɪst/ /ɪˈkɑːnəmɪst/| n. |经济学家| A person who studies or teaches economics.  An economist analyzes economic systems, policies, and theories to understand how economies function and how to improve them. |The economist predicted a recession next year.  这位经济学家预测明年将出现经济衰退。|  financial expert, economic analyst |  (no direct antonym, but perhaps) lay person, non-expert
