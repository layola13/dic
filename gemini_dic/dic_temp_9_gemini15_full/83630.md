haemodromograph:/ˈhiːməʊˈdrɒməɡræf/| n. |血流描记器|A haemodromograph is an instrument used to record the rate of blood flow. It measures the velocity of blood flow in a vessel.  | Example: The researchers used a haemodromograph to study the effects of the drug on blood flow in the arteries.  研究人员使用血流描记器来研究该药物对动脉血流的影响。|近义词：blood flow meter, flowmeter|反义词：无|名词
