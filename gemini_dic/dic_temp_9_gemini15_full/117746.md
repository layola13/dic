melanomata:/ˌmɛləˈnoʊmətɑː/ | n. | 黑色素瘤（复数） | Malignant tumors of melanocytes, the cells that produce melanin.  They are often darkly pigmented and can occur anywhere on the body but are most common on sun-exposed areas.  | Example:  The dermatologist diagnosed multiple melanomata on the patient's back.  皮肤科医生诊断出病人背部有多处黑色素瘤。|近义词：melanomas (singular: melanoma) |反义词：benign skin lesion
