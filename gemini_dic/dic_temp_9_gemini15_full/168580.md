processional:/prəˈseʃənəl/| n. |行列；队伍；宗教仪式上的行列；（尤指）婚礼或葬礼的队伍|A processional is a group of people moving together in a formal or ceremonial way, often in a line or procession.  It often refers to the procession of people entering or leaving a religious service, a wedding, or a funeral. |Example: The wedding processional was beautiful, with the bridesmaids walking down the aisle in elegant gowns. 婚礼的队伍很漂亮，伴娘们穿着优雅的礼服走过通道。

The funeral processional moved slowly and solemnly through the town. 葬礼的队伍缓慢而庄严地穿过小镇。|近义词：procession, parade, cortege|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with terms implying disorder or lack of formality.
