restabilizes:/ˌriːˈsteɪbəlaɪzɪz/| v.|使重新稳定；使恢复稳定 |To restore stability to something; to make something stable again.  |例句：The government implemented new policies to restabilize the economy. 政府实施新政策以稳定经济。|例句：After the earthquake, engineers worked tirelessly to restabilize the damaged bridge. 地震后，工程师们夜以继日地努力使受损的桥梁恢复稳定。|近义词：stabilize, re-establish, consolidate |反义词：destabilize, upset, disrupt |动词
