fame:/feɪm/| n. |名声，名望 | Widely known and admired; renown. Fame can be achieved through talent, accomplishment, or notoriety.  |例句：She rose to fame after winning the singing competition. 她赢得歌唱比赛后名声大噪。|例句：His fame preceded him.  他的名声先于他而至。|近义词： renown, reputation, celebrity, notoriety |反义词： obscurity, anonymity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enjoys great fame. = 他享有盛名。
Her fame spreads rapidly. = 她的名声迅速传播。


一般过去时:
The actor achieved fame in his youth. = 这位演员年轻时就成名了。
The author's fame faded after his death. = 作者死后名声逐渐消退。


现在完成时:
She has gained considerable fame in recent years. = 近年来她获得了相当大的名声。
He has maintained his fame throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中都保持着名声。


过去完成时:
Before the scandal, he had enjoyed immense fame. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他享有巨大的名声。
By the time she retired, her fame had already spread worldwide. = 她退休时，她的名声已经传遍世界各地。


将来时:
He will achieve worldwide fame with his new invention. = 他将凭借他的新发明获得世界性的名声。
She will likely lose her fame if she continues this behavior. = 如果她继续这种行为，很可能会失去名声。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't acted in that movie, he wouldn't have achieved such fame. = 如果他不出演那部电影，就不会获得如此大的名声。
I wish I had the fame and fortune of a movie star. = 我真希望拥有电影明星的名气和财富。


被动语态:
His fame was built on years of hard work. = 他的名声是多年辛勤工作的成果。
Her fame is being threatened by recent events. = 她最近的名声受到了威胁。


疑问句:
What is the source of his fame? = 他名声的来源是什么？
Does he desire fame more than anything else? = 他是否比其他任何东西都更渴望名声？


否定句:
He doesn't crave fame. = 他不渴望名声。
She wasn't interested in fame or fortune. = 她对名利不感兴趣。
