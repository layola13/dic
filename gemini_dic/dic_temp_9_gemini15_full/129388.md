nomenclator:/ˈnɒmɛnkleɪtə(r)/| n. |命名者；命名表；（古罗马的）奴隶贩子|A nomenclator is a person who assigns names, especially in a systematic way, or a list of names, such as a glossary or index.  In ancient Rome, a nomenclator was a slave who reminded his master of the names of people they met.|例句：The botanist acted as a nomenclator for the newly discovered species. 植物学家为新发现的物种命名。|例句：The book's nomenclator helped me understand the terminology. 这本书的术语表帮助我理解了术语。|近义词：namer, indexer, glossary, catalog|反义词：N/A|名词
