haemacyanin:/ˈhiːməˌsaɪənɪn/| n. |血蓝蛋白|A blue respiratory protein containing copper, found in the hemolymph of many arthropods and mollusks. It transports oxygen throughout the body.  | Example:  The hemocyanin in the blood of the octopus allows it to survive in low-oxygen environments.  章鱼血液中的血蓝蛋白使它能够在低氧环境中生存。|近义词：hemocyanin (same word, different spelling)|反义词：hemoglobin (carries oxygen in vertebrates)|名词
