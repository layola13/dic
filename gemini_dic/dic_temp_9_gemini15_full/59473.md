easeless:/ˈiːz.ləs/| adj. |不知疲倦的；不停的；无休止的 | unceasing; constant; without pause; relentless.  |例句：The ceaseless noise from the construction site drove me crazy.  施工现场无休止的噪音把我逼疯了。
