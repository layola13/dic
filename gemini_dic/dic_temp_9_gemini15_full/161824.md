practicalize:/ˈpræktɪkəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使实用化；使切实可行；付诸实践 | To make something practical or applicable; to put a theory or idea into practice.  | 例句：They aimed to practicalize their research findings by developing a new product. = 他们旨在通过开发新产品来使他们的研究成果实用化。


例句：The company is working to practicalize its innovative ideas. = 这家公司正在努力将它的创新理念付诸实践。


近义词：implement, apply, realize, effectuate


反义词：theorize, idealize


词性：动词
