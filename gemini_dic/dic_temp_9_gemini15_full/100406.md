interteam: /ˌɪntərˈtiːm/ | adj. n. |跨团队的；团队间的；跨团队合作| Relating to or involving more than one team.  The term often refers to collaboration, communication, or conflict between different teams. |例句：Interteam communication is crucial for successful project completion. 跨团队沟通对于项目的成功完成至关重要。  The interteam conflict delayed the product launch. 团队间的冲突延误了产品发布。|近义词：cross-team, multi-team |反义词：intra-team, single-team|形容词，名词
