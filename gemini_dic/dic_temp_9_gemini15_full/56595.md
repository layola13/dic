donates:/ˈdoʊneɪts/ | vt. |捐赠；捐献 |To give something, especially money or goods, to a person or an organization that needs them.  |例句：He donates a large sum of money to charity every year. 他每年都向慈善机构捐款很多。|例句：The company donates a portion of its profits to environmental protection. 这家公司将部分利润捐赠给环保事业。|近义词：contribute, give, bestow |反义词：receive, accept, keep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She donates blood regularly. = 她定期献血。
He donates his time to volunteering. = 他将时间奉献给志愿工作。

一般过去时:
They donated generously to the relief fund. = 他们慷慨地捐款给救济基金。
She donated her old clothes to the homeless shelter. = 她把旧衣服捐给了收容所。

现在完成时:
We have donated over $10,000 to the hospital. = 我们已经向医院捐款超过一万美元。
He has donated his entire collection of stamps to the museum. = 他已将他的全部邮票收藏捐赠给了博物馆。

过去完成时:
They had donated all their savings before they realized the need was greater. = 在他们意识到需求更大之前，他们已经捐出了所有的积蓄。
She had donated several times before the recent appeal. = 在最近的呼吁之前，她已经捐过几次了。

将来时:
I will donate to the cause next month. = 我下个月将捐款给这个事业。
They will donate their old furniture to a local charity. = 他们将把旧家具捐赠给当地一家慈善机构。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would donate to that organization. = 如果我有更多钱，我会捐给那个组织。
I wish I could donate more to help the victims. = 我希望我能捐更多钱来帮助受害者。

被动语态:
A significant amount of money was donated to the project. = 大笔资金被捐赠给了该项目。
The books are being donated to the school library. = 书籍正在被捐赠给学校图书馆。

疑问句:
Will you donate to the campaign? = 你会捐款给这个活动吗？
How much did he donate to the cause? = 他为这项事业捐了多少钱？

否定句:
He did not donate anything to the fundraiser. = 他没有向筹款活动捐款。
She doesn't donate regularly, but she does give occasionally. = 她不定期捐款，但她确实偶尔会捐款。


PHRASE->

make a donation = 捐款
donate time = 捐献时间
