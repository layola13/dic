ombrometer:/ɒmˈbrɒmɪtə(r)/| n. |雨量计，测雨器|An ombrometer is an instrument used to measure rainfall.  It typically consists of a container with a known cross-sectional area that collects rainwater, allowing the amount of rainfall to be determined by measuring the volume of water collected.|The ombrometer recorded 2 inches of rainfall overnight.  雨量计记录下昨晚下了两英寸的雨。|rain gauge, pluviometer|—|
