exercising:/ˈɛksərˌsaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 运动；练习；运用 | The act of using or engaging in physical activity for the purpose of improving health, fitness, or skill.  It also refers to the act of practicing or utilizing a skill, ability, or right. | 例句：He is exercising in the gym. 他正在健身房锻炼。

例句：She is exercising her right to vote.  她在行使她的投票权。


近义词：training, practicing, utilizing, working out

反义词：resting, idling, neglecting

词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I exercise every morning. = 我每天早上锻炼。
He exercises his authority. = 他行使他的权力。


一般过去时:
I exercised yesterday. = 我昨天锻炼了。
They exercised their options. = 他们行使了他们的选择权。


现在完成时:
I have exercised regularly for years. = 我多年来一直定期锻炼。
She has exercised her influence wisely. = 她明智地运用她的影响力。


过去完成时:
I had exercised before I went to work. = 我上班前已经锻炼过了。
He had exercised his right to remain silent. = 他已经行使了他保持沉默的权利。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

I wish I exercised regularly. = 我希望我定期锻炼。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
