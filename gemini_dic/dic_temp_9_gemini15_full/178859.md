red-headed:/ˈrɛdˌhɛdəd/| adj.|红头发的|Having red hair.|例句：She is a red-headed girl with freckles. 她是一个长着雀斑的红头发女孩。
例句：The red-headed boy was very shy.  那个红头发的男孩非常害羞。


近义词：ginger-haired, auburn-haired|反义词：black-haired, brown-haired, blonde-haired


词源:  The word is a compound adjective formed from "red" (meaning the color red) and "headed" (referring to hair).  "Headed" in this context is an older usage meaning "having" or "possessing".  The origin of "red" traces back to Proto-Germanic *raudaz and ultimately to Proto-Indo-European *h₂rud-.
