plo:/plɒ/| n. |砰的一声；(突然的)爆炸声 | A sudden, explosive sound.  | 例句：The gunshot produced a loud plo.  枪声发出一声巨响。  | 例句：The balloon made a plo when it burst. 气球爆裂时发出砰的一声。 | 近义词：bang, pop, thump | 反义词：silence, whisper


USAGE->

The balloon makes a plo when it bursts. = 气球爆裂时发出砰的一声。
A gunshot produces a loud plo. = 枪声发出一声巨响。
