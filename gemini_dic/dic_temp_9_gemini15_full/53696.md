disclaimant: /dɪsˈkleɪmənt/ | n. | 免责声明者；放弃权利者 | A person who disclaims something, especially a right or interest.  Someone who formally renounces a claim or right.  | Example: The disclaimant relinquished his inheritance.  免责声明者放弃了他的继承权。 | 近义词: renouncer, repudiator | 反义词: claimant, asserter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The disclaimant denies any responsibility. = 免责声明者否认任何责任。
He disclaims any knowledge of the event. = 他否认对该事件有任何了解。


一般过去时:
The disclaimant renounced his claim to the property. = 免责声明者放弃了他对该房产的索赔。
She disclaimed all responsibility for the accident. = 她否认对事故负有任何责任。


现在完成时:
The company has disclaimed liability for damages. = 公司已否认对其造成的损害负责。
He has disclaimed any connection with the organization. = 他否认与该组织有任何联系。


过去完成时:
The witness had disclaimed knowledge of the crime before the trial. = 在审判之前，证人已经否认了解犯罪事实。
They had disclaimed their ownership of the land long ago. = 他们很久以前就放弃了对这块土地的所有权。


将来时:
The defendant will disclaim any involvement in the conspiracy. = 被告将否认参与阴谋。
She will disclaim all responsibility for the debt. = 她将否认对债务负有任何责任。


虚拟语气:
If he were the disclaimant, he would have acted differently. = 如果他是免责声明者，他会采取不同的行动。
I wish the disclaimant had been more forthcoming with information. = 我希望免责声明者能提供更多信息。


被动语态:
Responsibility was disclaimed by the manufacturer. = 制造商否认了责任。
All claims were disclaimed by the company. = 公司否认所有索赔。


疑问句:
Who is the disclaimant in this case? = 此案中的免责声明者是谁？
Is the disclaimant truly unaware of the situation? = 免责声明者真的不知道情况吗？


否定句:
The disclaimant did not deny his involvement. = 免责声明者并没有否认他的参与。
She was not a disclaimant; she actively pursued her rights. = 她不是免责声明者；她积极维护自己的权利。
