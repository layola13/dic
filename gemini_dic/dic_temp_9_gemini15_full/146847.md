/ˌoʊvərˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlɪzəm/ | n. |过度个人主义|Excessive individualism; a societal condition where individual needs and desires are prioritized above the needs of the community or society as a whole.  It often manifests as selfishness, a lack of social responsibility, and disregard for collective well-being.  |The rise of overindividualism is leading to increased social fragmentation and a decline in civic engagement. 过度个人主义的兴起导致社会分裂加剧和公民参与度下降。|近义词：egoism, selfishness, narcissism, atomism |反义词：collectivism, altruism, communitarianism
