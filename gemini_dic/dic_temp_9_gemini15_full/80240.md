goes-around:/ɡoʊz əˈraʊnd/| v.|流传，传播；巡回演出；四处走动|The rumor goes around quickly.  谣言迅速传播开来。|近义词：circulates, spreads, travels|反义词：stays, remains


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The news goes around the town. = 这个消息在镇上流传。
The flu goes around every winter. = 每年冬天流感都会流行。

一般过去时:
The story went around the school. = 这个故事在学校里传开了。
The band went around the country on tour. = 乐队在全国巡回演出。

现在完成时:
The rumors have gone around for weeks. = 这些谣言已经流传了好几个星期了。
The singer has gone around the world on tour. = 这个歌手已经周游世界巡演了。

过去完成时:
The news had gone around before I arrived. = 我到达之前，这个消息就已经传开了。
The team had gone around the stadium before the game. = 比赛前，球队已经在体育场周围走了一圈。

将来时:
The invitation will go around next week. = 请柬下周将发出。
The comedian will go around the country performing his new act. = 喜剧演员将到全国各地演出他的新节目。

虚拟语气:
If the news went around, everyone would know. = 如果消息传开了，每个人都会知道。
I wish the truth went around instead of the rumors. = 我希望真相而不是谣言传开。

被动语态:
The information was gone around by email. = 信息通过电子邮件传播。
The petition is being gone around for signatures. = 正在征集签名。


疑问句:
Does this story go around often? = 这个故事经常流传吗？
Where did the rumor go around? = 谣言是从哪里传出来的？

否定句:
The news didn't go around as quickly as expected. = 消息没有像预期的那样迅速传播。
The politician didn't go around the country much during his campaign. = 这位政治家在竞选期间并没有在全国各地走动太多。

PHRASE->
go around the bend = 发疯
go around in circles = 兜圈子
go around with = 与...交往
go around the clock = 昼夜不停地
go all around the world = 周游世界
