overexploiting:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsplɔɪtɪŋ/| v.|过度开发；过度利用|The act of exploiting something to an excessive degree, resulting in depletion or damage.  It implies using a resource beyond its sustainable capacity.|例句：Overexploiting the fishing grounds has led to a decline in fish populations. 过度捕捞导致鱼类数量下降。

例句：The company was criticized for overexploiting its workers. 公司因过度压榨员工而受到批评。|近义词：overusing, depleting, exhausting, abusing|反义词：conserving, preserving, protecting|词性：动词

USAGE->

They are overexploiting the forest for timber.  = 他们在过度砍伐森林以获取木材。
We overexploit our natural resources. = 我们过度开发自然资源。
