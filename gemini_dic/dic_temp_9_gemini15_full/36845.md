colonizes:/ˈkɑːlənaɪzɪz/ | v. |殖民；定居；散布；扩张 | To establish a colony or colonies in (a place); to spread or become established in a new place, often in a way that is considered invasive or harmful. |例句：The European powers colonized much of the world. 欧洲列强殖民了世界大部分地区。 |例句：The bacteria quickly colonized the wound. 细菌迅速在伤口上繁殖。|近义词：settle, populate, invade, infest |反义词：decolonize, vacate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fungus colonizes the plant's roots. = 真菌寄生在植物的根部。
Bacteria colonize the gut. = 细菌在肠道中定殖。

一般过去时:
The British colonized India. = 英国殖民了印度。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
