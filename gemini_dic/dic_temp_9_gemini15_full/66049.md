exasperated:/ɪɡˈzɑːspəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 恼怒的；气恼的；厌烦的 | Extremely annoyed and frustrated.  |例句：He was exasperated by their constant complaining. 他被他们没完没了的抱怨弄得很生气。

例句：I'm exasperated with his lack of responsibility.  我对他的不负责任感到非常恼火。


近义词：irritated, frustrated, annoyed, infuriated, irked

反义词：calm, patient, composed, serene


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is exasperated by the traffic. = 他对交通堵塞感到恼火。
She is constantly exasperated by her children's behavior. = 她不断地被孩子们的行为激怒。


一般过去时:
I was exasperated by his rudeness. = 我被他粗鲁的态度激怒了。
They were exasperated with the delay. = 他们对延误感到非常生气。


现在完成时:
I have been exasperated by this problem for weeks. = 我已经被这个问题困扰了好几周了。
She has been exasperated by his incompetence. = 她一直被他无能而感到恼火。


过去完成时:
He had been exasperated by the constant interruptions. = 他一直被不断的打断而感到恼火。
We had been exasperated by the lack of progress. = 我们对缺乏进展感到非常生气。


将来时:
I will be exasperated if they don't finish on time. = 如果他们不能按时完成，我会很生气。
She will be exasperated by the long wait. = 漫长的等待会让她很生气。


虚拟语气:
I would be exasperated if I were in his shoes. = 如果我是他的话，我会很生气。
If I knew how to fix it, I wouldn't be so exasperated. = 如果我知道怎么修理，我就不会这么生气了。


被动语态:
He was exasperated by the situation. = 他被这种情况激怒了。(被动语态在此处较少用)
The customers were exasperated by the poor service. = 顾客们对糟糕的服务感到非常生气。(被动语态在此处较少用)


疑问句:
Are you exasperated by the noise? = 你被噪音弄烦了吗？
Is he exasperated by her behavior? = 他被她的行为激怒了吗？


否定句:
I am not exasperated by minor inconveniences. = 我不会因为一些小麻烦而生气。
He wasn't exasperated by the criticism. = 他没有因为批评而生气。


PHRASE->
