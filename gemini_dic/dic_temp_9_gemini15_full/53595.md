disburthen:/dɪsˈbɜːðən/| vt.|卸下（负担）；解除；摆脱|To remove or unload a burden from something or someone.  The word is largely archaic and rarely used in modern English.|例句：He disburthened himself of his heavy backpack. 他卸下了沉重的背包。|例句：She disburthened her heart to her friend. 她向朋友倾诉了心事。|近义词：unload, relieve, unburden|反义词：burden, load, oppress|动词
