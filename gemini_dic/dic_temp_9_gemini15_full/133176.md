nonevangelical:/ˌnɒnˌɛvænˈdʒɛlɪkəl/| adj.|非福音派的；非基督教福音派的|Not belonging to or connected with evangelical Christianity.  This term describes individuals, groups, or movements that do not adhere to the beliefs and practices typically associated with evangelical Christianity.  It can also refer to things that are not characteristic of or associated with evangelical Christianity.|例句：The survey showed a significant portion of the population identified as nonevangelical.  这项调查显示，相当一部分人口认为自己是非福音派的。

The nonevangelical perspective on social issues often differs from that of evangelical Christians.  非福音派人士对社会问题的看法通常与福音派基督徒不同。|近义词：secular, nonreligious, non-Christian|反义词：evangelical, Christian (in the evangelical sense)
