domebook:/ˈdoʊmbʊk/| n. |穹顶书；(古代)编年史|A domebook, also known as a dom-book, is a type of historical record, particularly from the medieval period, often detailing land ownership, laws, or other significant events.  It was sometimes literally kept in a domed structure or chest.|The Winchester Domebook contains a wealth of information about the estates of Winchester Cathedral.|近义词：chronicle, record, annals|反义词：N/A
