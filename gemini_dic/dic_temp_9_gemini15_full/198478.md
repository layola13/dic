shrilling:/ˈʃrɪlɪŋ/| adj.|尖锐刺耳的，刺耳的 | 发出尖锐刺耳的声音；令人不悦地尖锐 | The child's shrilling cry pierced the quiet of the night.  孩子的尖锐哭声刺破了夜晚的宁静。|The teacher's shrilling voice made the students uncomfortable. 老师尖锐的声音让学生们感到不舒服。| 近义词：piercing, strident, shrill | 反义词：soft, gentle, muted |

USAGE->

The siren is shrilling loudly. = 警笛声刺耳地响着。
His voice shrills when he gets angry. = 他生气时声音变得尖锐。
