coyotillo: /ˌkoɪoʊˈtɪjoʊ/ | n. |  豆科植物，羊蹄甲属的一种植物 |  A small tree or shrub, *Erythrina americana*, of the pea family, with scarlet flowers, also called  coral bean.  It is native to the Americas and is often found in dry areas.
