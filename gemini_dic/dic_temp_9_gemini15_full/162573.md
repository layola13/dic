prealphabet:/ˌpriːˈælfəbɛt/| adj.|前字母表的，字母表之前的| Relating to or denoting a stage of language development before the invention of an alphabet, typically characterized by the use of pictograms or other non-alphabetic symbols.  |Example:  The prealphabet markings on the cave walls are thought to be some of the earliest forms of human communication.  前字母表的洞穴壁画被认为是最早的人类交流形式之一。 |近义词：proto-alphabetic, preliterate |反义词：alphabetic, literate
