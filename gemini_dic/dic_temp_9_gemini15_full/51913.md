dhow:/daʊ/| n.| 单桅三角帆船 | A dhow is a traditional sailing vessel used in the Indian Ocean and the Red Sea.  It is characterized by its distinctive triangular sails and its often-ornate decoration.  Dhows have been used for centuries for trade, fishing, and transportation. |例句：The ancient dhow gracefully sailed across the turquoise waters. 这艘古老的单桅三角帆船优雅地驶过碧绿的海水。
