practically:/ˈpræktɪkli/| adv.|实际上，几乎，差不多 |In practice; almost; virtually.  It describes something that is almost true or almost completely true. |例句：Practically everyone agreed with the proposal.  几乎每个人都同意这项提案。|例句：The work is practically finished. 工作几乎完成了。|近义词：virtually, almost, nearly, substantially|反义词：theoretically, ideally, impossibly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He practically lives at the gym. = 他几乎住在健身房里。
She practically knows everything about that topic. = 她几乎对那个话题无所不知。

一般过去时:
They practically ignored me. = 他们几乎无视我。
The meeting practically ran itself. = 会议几乎是自动进行的。

现在完成时:
I've practically finished the book. = 我几乎读完了这本书。
We've practically lost all hope. = 我们几乎失去了所有希望。

过去完成时:
They had practically completed the project. = 他们几乎完成了那个项目。
He had practically given up. = 他几乎放弃了。

将来时:
We will practically be neighbours. = 我们将来几乎就是邻居了。
The project will practically be finished by next week. = 项目下周几乎就能完成了。

虚拟语气:
If it were practically possible, I would help. = 如果实际上可能的话，我会帮忙。
If they were practically ready, the show would begin. = 如果他们实际上准备好了，演出就会开始。

被动语态:
The task was practically completed. = 任务几乎完成了。
The problem was practically solved. = 问题几乎解决了。

疑问句:
Is it practically impossible? = 这实际上是不可能的吗？
Are we practically bankrupt? = 我们实际上破产了吗？

否定句:
It's not practically feasible. = 这实际上不可行。
That's not practically possible. = 那实际上是不可能的。
