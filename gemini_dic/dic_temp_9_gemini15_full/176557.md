re-shortens:/ˌriːˈʃɔːrtənz/ | v. |重新缩短；再次缩短 | To shorten something again or to a further extent.  This word is a compound verb, formed by adding the prefix "re-" (meaning "again") to the verb "shortens" (the present tense third-person singular of "shorten"). |例句：The tailor re-shortened the trousers after the first alteration.  裁缝在第一次修改后又重新缩短了裤子。

The editor re-shortened the article to meet the deadline. 编辑为了赶截止日期再次缩短了文章。


近义词：abbreviate, curtail, reduce, condense | 反义词：lengthen, extend, prolong | 词性：动词
