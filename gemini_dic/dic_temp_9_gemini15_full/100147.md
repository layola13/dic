interradiate:/ɪntərˈeɪdieɪt/| vt.|互相辐射，相互影响|To affect or influence each other mutually; to radiate or spread throughout.|The two cultures interradiated, resulting in a unique blend of traditions.  两种文化相互影响，形成了独特的传统融合。|近义词：interpenetrate, interact, influence|反义词：isolate, separate|动词
