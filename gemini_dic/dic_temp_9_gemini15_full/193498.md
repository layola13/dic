selenite:/ˈsɛlənaɪt/| n. |月光石；亚硒酸盐；透闪石|A mineral consisting of a variety of gypsum, usually transparent or translucent and occurring in large crystals.  It is often used in jewelry and decorative objects.|例句：The selenite crystals shimmered under the lamplight. 月光石晶体在灯光下闪闪发光。

例句：She wore a beautiful necklace made of selenite. 她戴着一串美丽的月光石项链。


近义词：gypsum (in its crystalline form)


反义词：无


词性:名词
