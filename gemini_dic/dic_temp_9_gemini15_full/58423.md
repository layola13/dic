duces:/ˈdjuːsɪz/| n.|  引进；生产；结果；作品 | The word "duces" is the plural form of the Latin word "dux," meaning "leader" or "conductor". In English, it's primarily used in specific contexts related to the production or introduction of something, often in academic or formal settings.  It's not a common word in everyday conversation. |Example: The company's new product line introduces several innovative duces to the market. 这家公司的新产品线向市场引进了几款创新产品。 |近义词:productions, introductions, outputs, results|反义词:consumptions, removals
