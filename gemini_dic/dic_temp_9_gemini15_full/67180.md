expounder:/ɪkˈspaʊndər/ | n. |阐述者，解释者 | One who expounds or explains; an interpreter or commentator.  |The professor was a renowned expounder of ancient Greek philosophy.  这位教授是著名的古希腊哲学阐述者。 | interpreter, commentator, expositor | obfuscator, confuser | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He expounds his theories clearly. = 他清晰地阐述了他的理论。
She expounds on the benefits of exercise regularly. = 她经常阐述运动的好处。


一般过去时:
The speaker expounded upon the importance of education. = 演讲者详细阐述了教育的重要性。
He expounded his views at the meeting. = 他在会议上阐述了他的观点。


现在完成时:
She has expounded this topic in several articles. = 她已在几篇文章中阐述了这个主题。
He has expounded on the subject for years. = 他多年来一直在阐述这个主题。


过去完成时:
They had expounded their ideas before the debate began. = 他们在辩论开始之前已经阐述了他们的想法。
She had expounded the theory extensively in her previous work. = 她之前的工作中已经广泛地阐述了这个理论。


将来时:
He will expound on the new policy at the conference. = 他将在会议上阐述这项新政策。
She will expound her findings in the upcoming report. = 她将在即将发布的报告中阐述她的发现。


虚拟语气:
If he were to expound his views, I'm sure many would disagree. = 如果他要阐述他的观点，我相信很多人会不同意。
I wish he would expound on the matter more clearly. = 我希望他能更清晰地阐述这件事。


被动语态:
His theories were expounded in detail in the book. = 他的理论在书中得到了详细的阐述。
The new policy will be expounded by the CEO next week. = 这项新政策将于下周由首席执行官阐述。


疑问句:
Will the lecturer expound on the topic further? = 讲师会进一步阐述这个话题吗？
Who will expound the findings of the research? = 谁将阐述这项研究的结果？


否定句:
He did not expound his ideas adequately. = 他没有充分阐述他的想法。
They refused to expound on the sensitive issue. = 他们拒绝阐述这个敏感问题。


PHRASE->
expound on/upon sth: 详细解释某事
expound a theory: 阐述一个理论
expound one's views: 阐述某人的观点
expound the meaning: 解释含义
expound the facts: 阐述事实
