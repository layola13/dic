centreboard:/ˈsɛntəbɔːrd/ | n. |中板（船的）|A retractable keel, usually made of wood or metal, fitted to a sailing boat to provide stability and prevent capsizing.  It is lowered into the water when sailing and raised when not needed.|The centreboard on our sailboat was stuck, preventing us from sailing.|keel, fin|—|
