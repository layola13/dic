serotonin:/ˌsɛrəˈtoʊnɪn/| n. |血清素，5-羟色胺|A neurotransmitter involved in mood regulation, sleep, appetite, and other bodily functions.  It is also called 5-hydroxytryptamine (5-HT).|例句：Low levels of serotonin are often associated with depression.  低水平的血清素通常与抑郁症有关。

例句：Serotonin plays a crucial role in regulating mood and emotions. 血清素在调节情绪方面起着至关重要的作用。


近义词：5-hydroxytryptamine (5-HT)


反义词：无直接反义词，但其功能缺乏可视为反义。


词性：名词
