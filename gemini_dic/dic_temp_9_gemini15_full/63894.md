epineurium:/ˌɛpɪˈnʊəriəm/| n. |神经外膜|The outermost layer of connective tissue surrounding a peripheral nerve, enclosing the entire nerve bundle. It provides protection and support to the nerve fibers within.|例句：The epineurium protects the nerve fascicles from external damage. 神经外膜保护神经束免受外部损伤。|近义词：perineurium (神经束膜), endoneurium (神经内膜)|反义词: None
