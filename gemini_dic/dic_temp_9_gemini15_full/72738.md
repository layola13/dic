foetus: /ˈfiːtəs/ | n. | 胎儿 | An unborn offspring of a mammal, in the later stages of development before birth.  | Example: The ultrasound showed a healthy foetus.  超声波显示胎儿很健康。 |近义词: fetus (US spelling), unborn child |反义词:  newborn, infant
