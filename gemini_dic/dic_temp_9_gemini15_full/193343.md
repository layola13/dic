segment:/ˈseɡmənt/ | n. | 部分；片段；节；部分；区段 | A part of something that is separate from other parts. |例句：The company is divided into several segments.  这家公司被分成几个部门。|例句：She read a segment of the book. 她读了一部分书。|近义词：section, portion, part, piece |反义词：whole, entirety


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This segment of the road is under construction. = 这段路正在施工。
The marketing segment is performing well. = 市场营销部门表现良好。

一般过去时:
That segment of the film was my favorite. = 电影的那一部分是我最喜欢的。
The final segment of the race was very exciting. = 比赛的最后一段非常精彩。

现在完成时:
We have completed the first segment of the project. = 我们已经完成了项目的第一个阶段。
They have already analyzed that segment of the data. = 他们已经分析了那部分数据。

过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, they had finished the first segment of the performance. = 我们到达时，他们已经完成了演出的第一部分。

将来时:
We will address that segment of the problem tomorrow. = 我们明天将解决这个问题的一部分。
The next segment of the journey will be longer. = 旅程的下一段将更长。

虚拟语气:
If this segment were longer, it would be more interesting. = 如果这部分更长，它会更有趣。
I wish that segment of the concert had been longer. = 我希望音乐会的那个部分能再长一些。

被动语态:
This segment is being analyzed by the research team. = 这部分数据正在由研究团队进行分析。
The damaged segment was repaired quickly. = 受损的部分很快得到修复。

疑问句:

否定句:
