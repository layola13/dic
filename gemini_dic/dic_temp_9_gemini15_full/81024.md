graduate:/ˈɡrædʒuət/| n., v.|毕业生；研究生；毕业；获得学位|

英文解析:As a noun, "graduate" refers to a person who has completed a course of study, especially at a college or university. As a verb, it means to complete a course of study and receive a degree or diploma; or to progress to a higher level of education.


例句:
He is a graduate of Oxford University. = 他是牛津大学的毕业生。

She graduated with honors in physics. = 她以优异成绩获得了物理学学位。


近义词: (n.) alumnus, alumna,  (v.) complete, finish


反义词: (n.) undergraduate,  (v.) dropout, fail


词性: Noun, Verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He graduates next month. = 他下个月毕业。
She graduates from high school this year. = 她今年高中毕业。


一般过去时:
I graduated from college in 2010. = 我2010年大学毕业。
He graduated with a degree in engineering. = 他获得了工程学学位。


现在完成时:
She has graduated from university and is now working. = 她大学毕业后现在正在工作。
They have graduated from the same school. = 他们毕业于同一所学校。


过去完成时:
He had graduated before he got the job. = 他毕业后才找到工作。
She had already graduated when her brother started university. = 她弟弟上大学的时候，她已经毕业了。


将来时:
I will graduate next year. = 我明年毕业。
She will graduate with distinction. = 她将以优异成绩毕业。


虚拟语气:
If I had graduated earlier, I would have found a better job. = 如果我早点毕业，我就能找到更好的工作。
I wish I had graduated with honors. = 我真希望我以优异成绩毕业。


被动语态:
The students will be graduated next week. = 学生们下周将毕业。 (Note:  This usage is less common and often sounds unnatural.  It's preferable to use active voice.)


疑问句:
When did you graduate? = 你什么时候毕业的？
Has he graduated yet? = 他毕业了吗？


否定句:
She didn't graduate from that university. = 她没有从那所大学毕业。
He hasn't graduated yet. = 他还没毕业。
