dormitory:/ˈdɔːrmɪtəri/ | n. | 宿舍；寝室 | A building or room containing a number of separate sleeping rooms, especially for students or those in a boarding school.  | Example:  Many university students live in dormitories. 许多大学生住在宿舍里。  | 近义词：dorm, residence hall | 反义词：private room, apartment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dormitory provides laundry facilities. = 宿舍提供洗衣设施。
Students often socialize in the dormitory common room. = 学生经常在宿舍公共休息室社交。


一般过去时:
He lived in a dormitory during his first year of college. = 他大学一年级住在宿舍里。
The dormitory was renovated last year. = 宿舍去年进行了翻修。


现在完成时:
They have lived in that dormitory for three years. = 他们已经在那间宿舍住了三年了。
The university has recently upgraded its dormitories. = 大学最近升级了它的宿舍。


过去完成时:
She had already moved out of the dormitory before the inspection. = 检查之前她已经搬出宿舍了。

将来时:
He will move into the dormitory next week. = 他下周将搬进宿舍。
The new dormitory will open next semester. = 新宿舍将在下个学期开放。


虚拟语气:
