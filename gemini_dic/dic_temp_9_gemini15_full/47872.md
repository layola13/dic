decelerate: /dɪˈsɛləreɪt/ | vt. | 减速；减缓 | To reduce the speed of something; to slow down. | 例句：The car decelerated as it approached the intersection.  = 汽车在接近十字路口时减速了。 | 例句：Economic growth has decelerated in recent months. = 近几个月经济增长速度放缓了。 | 近义词：slow down, reduce speed, brake | 反义词：accelerate, speed up | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The driver decelerates smoothly. = 司机平稳地减速。
The economy decelerates gradually. = 经济逐渐减速。

一般过去时:
The train decelerated before the station. = 火车在车站前减速了。
He decelerated his pace to avoid falling. = 他放慢了脚步以避免摔倒。

现在完成时:
The car has decelerated significantly. = 汽车已经大幅减速了。
The project has decelerated due to funding issues. = 由于资金问题，项目进展放缓了。

过去完成时:
The plane had decelerated before landing. = 飞机在着陆前已经减速了。
They had decelerated the production line before the inspection. = 他们在检查前已经放慢了生产线速度。

将来时:
The rocket will decelerate upon re-entry. = 火箭在重返大气层时将减速。
The market will decelerate in the next quarter. = 市场在下个季度将会放缓。

虚拟语气:

If the project had decelerated less, it would have been finished on time. = 如果项目进展没有那么慢，它就能按时完成了。
