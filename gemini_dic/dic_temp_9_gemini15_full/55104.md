disrobe:/dɪsˈroʊb/ | vt. | 脱衣；使脱光衣服 | To remove one's clothing, or to remove the clothing of someone else.  | Example:  He disrobed quickly before entering the shower. 他迅速脱衣然后进了淋浴间。  She disrobed the child and put him in his pajamas. 她给孩子脱了衣服，然后给他穿上睡衣。 | 近义词：undress, strip | 反义词：dress, clothe | 动词
