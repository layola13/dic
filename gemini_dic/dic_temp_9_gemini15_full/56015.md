do justice:/duː ˈdʒʌs.tɪs/|/ v. | 秉公处理；公平对待；公正地表现 |To treat someone or something fairly or in a way that is appropriate.  It can also mean to represent something accurately or fully. |例句：The film doesn't do justice to the beauty of the scenery. 这部电影没有公正地展现风景之美。

例句：The judge did justice to the case, ensuring a fair trial for all parties involved.  法官公正地审理了此案，确保所有相关方都能得到公平审判。


近义词：represent fairly, treat fairly, give a fair account of, vindicate

反义词：misrepresent, distort, wrong, be unfair to

词性:动词
