showy:/ˈʃoʊi/| adj.|炫耀的；华而不实的；引人注目的| ostentatious or flamboyant in appearance or style.  It suggests an attempt to attract attention, often in an excessive or vulgar way. |例句：Her showy dress attracted unwanted attention.  她的炫耀的衣服吸引了不必要的注意。

例句：The showy plumage of the male bird helps it attract a mate.雄鸟炫耀的羽毛有助于它吸引配偶。


近义词：ostentatious, flamboyant, flashy, gaudy, conspicuous, pretentious

反义词：subtle, understated, modest, plain, unassuming


词源:源于"show"加上后缀"-y"，表示具有某种特性。  The suffix "-y" transforms the verb "show" into an adjective describing something that exhibits a lot of show or display.
