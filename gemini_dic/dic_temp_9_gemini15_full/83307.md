gyneolatry:/ˌɡaɪniˈɒlətri/| n. |女性崇拜；对女性的崇拜|The worship of women;  a form of religious veneration or devotion directed towards women. This can manifest in various ways, from the elevation of goddesses to the reverence of specific women in society.  The term often implies an excessive or unbalanced focus on female figures.|The ancient civilization practiced gyneolatry, evident in their numerous statues and temples dedicated to female deities.  古代文明奉行女性崇拜，这从他们为女性神祇建造的众多雕像和寺庙中可以看出。|近义词：femininism(在特定语境下),  female veneration|反义词：misogyny
