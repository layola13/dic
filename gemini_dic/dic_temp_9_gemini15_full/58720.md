dunces:/ˈdʌnsɪz/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A dunce is a stupid or foolish person.  The word often implies a lack of intelligence or ability, especially in academic settings.  It can be used in a humorous or derogatory way.|例句：He was called a dunce by his teacher for consistently failing his exams. 他因为考试总是不及格而被老师称为笨蛋。

例句：The children were acting like a bunch of dunces, running around and making a mess.孩子们像一群傻瓜一样跑来跑去，弄得一团糟。


近义词：fool, idiot, simpleton, blockhead|反义词：genius, prodigy, intellect

词性：名词
