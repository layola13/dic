dual-purpose: /ˈduːəl ˈpɜːrpəs/ | adj. | 双用途的，两用的 | Having two purposes or uses.  |  This is a dual-purpose tool that can be used as a saw and a hammer. 这是一款双用途工具，可以用作锯子和锤子。 |近义词：multipurpose, versatile |反义词：single-purpose, specific-purpose


词源:

* **dual:**  From Latin *dualis*, meaning "two," "double," or "twofold."  This traces back to the Proto-Indo-European root *dwo-,* meaning "two."

* **purpose:** From Old French *purpos*, from Latin *propositum*, the past participle of *proponere*, meaning "to put forth," "to propose." This combines the prefix *pro-* ("forward") and the verb *ponere* ("to place").


用法说明:

dual-purpose 主要用作形容词，修饰名词，表示该名词具有双重用途或功能。


例句:

* This dual-purpose room serves as both a bedroom and a study.  这个双用途的房间既可以作为卧室，也可以作为书房。

*  The software offers a dual-purpose functionality: data analysis and report generation.  该软件具有双重功能：数据分析和报告生成。
