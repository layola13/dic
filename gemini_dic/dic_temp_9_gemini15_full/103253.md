jewellery:/ˈdʒuːəlri/| n. |珠宝；首饰|Jewellery refers to ornaments worn for personal adornment, typically made from precious metals and gemstones.  It encompasses a wide range of items, including necklaces, bracelets, earrings, rings, and brooches. |She received a beautiful set of jewellery as a wedding gift. 她收到了一套漂亮的结婚礼物首饰。|  She has a stunning collection of jewellery. 她有一套令人惊叹的首饰收藏。|jewelry (American English)|
