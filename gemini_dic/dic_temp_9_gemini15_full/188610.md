rumblingly:/ˈrʌmblɪŋli/ | adv. | 隆隆地；低沉地；含糊不清地 |  Describes an action or sound that is made with a low, continuous, and vibrating sound, like thunder or a distant engine. It can also suggest something that is unclear or indistinct. | 例句：The distant thunder rumbledly announced the approaching storm. 远处隆隆的雷声预示着暴风雨即将来临。

The old engine rumbledly chugged along the track. 旧引擎沿着轨道隆隆地发出 chug 的声音缓慢行驶。


近义词：noisily, heavily, indistinctly, vaguely


反义词：quietly, softly, clearly, distinctly


词性：副词
