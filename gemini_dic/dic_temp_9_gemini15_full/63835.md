epigenesis:/ˌɛpɪˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/| n. |表观遗传学；后成论|The biological process by which a cell or organism develops from a relatively unspecialized state to a more specialized state.  This process is influenced by both genetic and environmental factors.|Example: Epigenetic modifications can alter gene expression without changing the underlying DNA sequence.  表观遗传修饰可以改变基因表达，而不会改变潜在的DNA序列。|近义词：epigentic inheritance, developmental biology|反义词：preformationism
