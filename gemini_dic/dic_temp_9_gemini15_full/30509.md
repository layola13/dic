centurions:/senˈtʃʊriən/| n. |百夫长，罗马军队中的一百人队队长|A centurion was a senior officer in the Roman army, commanding a century of about 80 men.|例句：The centurion led his men into battle. 百夫长带领他的士兵们冲入战场。

例句：  The brave centurion earned the respect of his soldiers.  这位勇敢的百夫长赢得了士兵们的尊敬。


近义词：captain, commander, officer|反义词：private, soldier


词源：源自拉丁语“centurio”，由“centum”（一百）和“-urio”（表示人或职务的后缀）构成，字面意思是“一百人的长官”。
