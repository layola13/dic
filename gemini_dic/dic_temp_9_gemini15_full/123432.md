monopolistic:/ˌmɒnəˈpɒlɪstɪk/|/ˌmɑːnəˈpɑːlɪstɪk/| adj.|垄断的；独占的 | Relating to or characterized by a monopoly; having exclusive control over a commodity or service.  A monopolistic market is one dominated by a single seller. |例句：The company's monopolistic practices were challenged in court. 这家公司的垄断行为受到了法庭的挑战。

例句：The government is trying to prevent the formation of monopolistic industries. 政府正试图阻止垄断性产业的形成。


近义词：monopoly-based, dominating, controlling, exclusive


反义词：competitive, open, free-market


词性：形容词
