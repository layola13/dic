coinvestigator:/koʊɪnˈvɛstɪɡeɪtər/| n. |共同研究者，合作者 |A coinvestigator is a researcher who collaborates with the principal investigator on a research project. They share responsibility for the project's design, execution, and dissemination of findings.  |Example:  She served as a coinvestigator on a multi-year study of childhood obesity. 她担任了一项为期多年的儿童肥胖症研究的共同研究者。

The lead investigator and her coinvestigator published their findings in a prestigious journal.  首席研究员和她的共同研究员在权威期刊上发表了他们的研究成果。|collaborator, co-worker, associate |opponent, adversary
