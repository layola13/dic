/ɪˈlɛktər/| n. |选举人；选择者|A person who has the right to vote in an election.  An elector is someone who chooses or selects something.|The electors cast their votes for the president.  选举人投票选总统。|voter, chooser, selector|—|noun


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The electors choose the president. = 选举人选择总统。
He elects to stay home. = 他选择待在家里。


一般过去时:
