madison:/ˈmædəsən/| n. |麦迪逊（女子名，地名）|Madison is a popular given name for girls, and also the name of several places, most notably the capital of Wisconsin.  The name is of English origin, derived from the surname which ultimately derives from the place name Maddison, in Yorkshire, England. This in turn came from a personal name, Maddo, a pet form of the name Matthew.|例句：My friend's name is Madison. 我的朋友名叫麦迪逊。

例句：Madison, Wisconsin is a beautiful city. 威斯康星州的麦迪逊市是一个美丽的城市。|近义词：无直接近义词，取决于语境。|反义词：无直接反义词，取决于语境。|词性:名词
