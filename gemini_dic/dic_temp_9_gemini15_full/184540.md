restinction:/rɪˈstrɪkʃən/| n. |限制，约束|The restriction of movement is a necessary precaution. 行动限制是必要的预防措施。|近义词：limitation, constraint, restriction|反义词：freedom, liberty|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There is a speed restriction on this road. = 这条路上有速度限制。
The government imposes restrictions on imports. = 政府对进口商品实行限制。

一般过去时:
The company imposed restrictions on employee travel. = 公司对员工的差旅实行限制。
There were restrictions on access to the building. = 进入大楼受到限制。

现在完成时:
They have placed restrictions on water usage. = 他们已经对用水进行了限制。
Restrictions have been in place for several weeks. = 限制措施已经实施了几周了。

过去完成时:
Before the pandemic, there had been few restrictions on international travel. = 在疫情爆发之前，国际旅行几乎没有限制。
They had already imposed restrictions on public gatherings. = 他们已经对公众集会实施了限制。

将来时:
They will impose stricter restrictions next week. = 他们下周将实施更严格的限制。
There will be restrictions on parking during the event. = 活动期间将对停车进行限制。

虚拟语气:
If there were no restrictions, we could travel freely. = 如果没有限制，我们就可以自由旅行。
I wish there were fewer restrictions on our work. = 我希望我们工作上的限制少一些。

被动语态:
Access to the site was restricted. = 对该地点的进入受到限制。
Further restrictions may be imposed by the government. = 政府可能会实施进一步的限制。

疑问句:
Are there any restrictions on bringing food into the stadium? = 带食物进入体育场有什么限制吗？
What are the restrictions on using this software? = 使用此软件有什么限制？

否定句:
There are no restrictions on the number of participants. = 参加人数没有限制。
The rule has no restrictions on age. = 该规则对年龄没有限制。
