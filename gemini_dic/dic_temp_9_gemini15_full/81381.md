grasped:/ɡræspt/| vt.|抓住；理解；掌握|To take hold of something firmly with one's hand. To understand something completely.|例句：She grasped the rope tightly. 她紧紧抓住绳子。|例句：I finally grasped the meaning of the poem. 我终于理解了这首诗的意思。|近义词：seized, clutched, comprehended, understood|反义词：released, let go, misunderstood, missed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He grasps the concept easily. = 他很容易理解这个概念。
She grasps the railing as she walks down the stairs. = 她下楼时扶着栏杆。


一般过去时:
I grasped the opportunity to speak to the CEO. = 我抓住了与CEO交谈的机会。
He grasped my hand and pulled me to safety. = 他抓住我的手把我拉到了安全的地方。


现在完成时:
I have grasped the main points of the lecture. = 我已经掌握了讲座的主要内容。
She has grasped the techniques needed to succeed. = 她已经掌握了成功所需的技巧。


过去完成时:
I had grasped the significance of his words only after he had left. = 直到他离开后，我才理解了他话语的含义。
She had grasped the situation before anyone else did. = 在其他人之前，她就掌握了情况。


将来时:
I will grasp every opportunity to improve myself. = 我将抓住每一个提升自己的机会。
He will grasp the chance to travel abroad. = 他将会抓住出国旅行的机会。


虚拟语气:
If I had grasped the problem earlier, I could have prevented it. = 如果我早点抓住问题，我就能阻止它了。
I wish I had grasped the opportunity to study abroad. = 我真希望我抓住了出国留学的机会。


被动语态:
The concept was easily grasped by the students. = 学生们很容易理解这个概念。
The situation was grasped by the police quickly. = 警方迅速掌握了情况。


疑问句:
Do you grasp the meaning of this sentence? = 你理解这句话的意思吗？
Has he grasped the importance of this project? = 他掌握了这个项目的重要性吗？


否定句:
I didn't grasp the instructions properly. = 我没有正确理解指示。
He didn't grasp the opportunity to invest in the company. = 他没有抓住投资这家公司的好机会。


PHRASE->

lose one's grasp on = 失去对…的控制
