deordination:/ˌdiːˌɔːrdɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |失调；紊乱；不协调|Lack of coordination; a state of being uncoordinated.  It often refers to a lack of order or proper arrangement, a disruption of normal function or system.  It can also refer to a lack of coordination between different parts of a system or body.|例句：The deordination of their efforts led to project failure.  他们的努力不协调导致了项目的失败。

The deordination of his muscles made walking difficult. 他肌肉的失协调使得行走困难。|近义词：disorder, disorganization, incoordination, chaos |反义词：order, coordination, organization
