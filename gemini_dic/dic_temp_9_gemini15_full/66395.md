executed:/ˈɛksɪkjuːtɪd/ | v. | 执行，处死；完成 | To carry out or put into effect; to kill as a legal punishment. | 例句：The plan was executed flawlessly.  计划完美地执行了。 The prisoner was executed for murder.  囚犯因谋杀被处死。 | 近义词：carried out, implemented, performed, put to death | 反义词：failed, abandoned, halted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government executes its policies. = 政府执行其政策。
She executes her duties diligently. = 她勤勤恳恳地履行职责。

一般过去时:
They executed the plan last night. = 他们昨晚执行了计划。
The court executed the convicted man. = 法庭处决了罪犯。

They have executed the order. = 他们已经执行了命令。

过去完成时:
The project had been executed before the deadline. = 项目已在截止日期前完成。
He had executed the task successfully. = 他已成功完成任务。
