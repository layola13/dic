radiograph:/ˈreɪdiəɡræf/| n. |X光照片；射线照片 |An image produced on a photographic film or a digital sensor by X-rays, gamma rays, or other forms of radiation.  It is used in medical diagnosis and other fields to visualize internal structures. |The radiograph clearly showed the fracture in his leg.  X光照片清晰地显示了他腿部的骨折。 The dentist used a radiograph to examine the patient's teeth. 牙医用X光照片检查病人的牙齿。| X-ray, roentgenogram |  |
