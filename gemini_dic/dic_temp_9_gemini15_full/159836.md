polyhedrical:/ˌpɒliˈhɛdrɪkəl/| /ˌpɑːliˈhɛdrɪkəl/ | adj. |多面体的；多方面的|Having many sides or faces; multifaceted.  The term refers to objects with many flat surfaces,  often used in geometry to describe three-dimensional shapes. It can also be used metaphorically to describe something complex or multifaceted.|例句：The crystal displayed a polyhedrical structure.  这个水晶呈现多面体结构。

The problem requires a polyhedrical approach, considering many different aspects.  这个问题需要多方面的方法，考虑许多不同的方面。|近义词： multifaceted, multisided, many-sided|反义词：single-sided, simple, unidimensional

词源:
From poly- + hedron (Greek *hedra, "base," "seat") + -al.  The Greek word *hedra relates to the faces of a solid.  "Poly-" means many.  Therefore, polyhedrical means "having many faces (or sides)."
