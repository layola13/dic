recoated:/ˌriːˈkoʊtɪd/| adj.| 再涂层的；重新涂覆的 | Having been coated again.  The term implies a previous coating has been applied and a new layer has been added.  |例句：The recoated bridge showed no signs of rust.  重新涂层的桥梁没有生锈的迹象。

The recoated pan was as good as new.  重新涂覆的平底锅跟新的一样好。


近义词：re-covered, re-surfaced, refinished | 反义词：uncoated, bare


词性：形容词
