quicklimes:/ˈkwɪklaɪmz/| n.| 生石灰 | Quicklime is calcium oxide, a white, caustic, crystalline solid. It is produced by heating limestone to a high temperature, driving off carbon dioxide.  Quicklime is used in many industrial processes, including the production of cement, steel, and paper. |例句：The construction workers used quicklime to set the mortar. 建筑工人用生石灰来凝固砂浆。|近义词：calcium oxide, unslaked lime |反义词：slaked lime, hydrated lime
