controversially:/ˌkɒntrəˈvɜːʃəli/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈvɜːrʃəli/| adv.|有争议地；引起争议地|In a way that causes disagreement or argument.  The decision was controversially made without public consultation.  这个决定是有争议地做出的，没有进行公众咨询。|arguably, debatably, disputably|uncontroversially, unarguably


例句：

The proposed new law was controversially passed by a narrow margin.  拟议中的新法律以微弱优势有争议地获得通过。

His controversial remarks sparked a heated debate. 他有争议的言论引发了一场激烈的辩论。
