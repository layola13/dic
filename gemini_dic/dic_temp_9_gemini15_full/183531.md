repurposed:/ˌriːˈpɜːrpəst/| adj.| 重新利用的；改作他用的 | Having been given a new purpose; adapted for a new use.  |例句：The old factory building has been repurposed as a trendy art gallery.  旧工厂建筑已被改造成一个时尚的艺术画廊。 |例句：The company repurposed its marketing strategy to target a younger demographic. 公司调整了营销策略以针对更年轻的人群。|近义词：reused, recycled, adapted, converted |反义词：discarded, abandoned, wasted


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company repurposes old materials for new products. = 公司将旧材料重新利用来制造新产品。
They repurpose their marketing strategies each year. = 他们每年都会调整他们的营销策略。

一般过去时:
The artist repurposed discarded objects into stunning sculptures. = 艺术家将废弃的物品改造成令人惊叹的雕塑。
We repurposed the old barn into a guesthouse. = 我们把旧谷仓改造成了一家宾馆。

现在完成时:
The city has repurposed many derelict buildings. = 这个城市已经重新利用了许多废弃的建筑物。
She has repurposed her old jeans into a stylish bag. = 她已将旧牛仔裤改造成一个时尚的包包。


过去完成时:
They had repurposed the space before the event started. = 他们在活动开始之前已经重新利用了这个空间。
The organization had repurposed its funds to support disaster relief. = 该组织已将其资金重新用于支持救灾工作。


将来时:
We will repurpose the leftover fabric for crafts. = 我们将把剩余的布料用于手工制作。
The developers will repurpose the abandoned mine into a museum. = 开发商将把废弃的矿井改造成博物馆。


虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we would repurpose the entire building. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们将重新利用整栋建筑物。
I wish they had repurposed the old equipment instead of throwing it away. = 我希望他们能重新利用旧设备而不是扔掉它。


被动语态:
The old tires were repurposed into playground equipment. = 旧轮胎被改造成操场设备。
The building is being repurposed into a community center. = 这栋建筑正在被改造成社区中心。


疑问句:
How can we repurpose this material? = 我们如何才能重新利用这种材料？
Will the old school be repurposed? = 旧学校会被重新利用吗？


否定句:
They didn't repurpose the old furniture. = 他们没有重新利用旧家具。
The company hasn't decided how to repurpose its waste. = 公司还没有决定如何处理其废物。


PHRASE->
