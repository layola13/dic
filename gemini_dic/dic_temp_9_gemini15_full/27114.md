/kəˈlɪɡrəfi/| n. |书法；优美的书写|The art or skill of forming beautiful lettering with a pen or brush.  Calligraphy is often used in formal documents and artistic creations.|例句：Her calligraphy is exquisite; each stroke is precise and elegant.  她的书法非常精美，每一笔都精准而优雅。|例句：The invitation featured beautiful calligraphy in gold lettering. 请柬上以金色字体书写着美丽的书法。|近义词：penmanship, hand lettering|反义词：illegible writing, scribbling
