displeasingly:/dɪsˈpliːzɪŋli/ | adv. | 不愉快地；令人不快地 | In a way that is unpleasant or unsatisfactory.  It describes the manner in which something is done or said. |  He looked displeasingly at the messy room. 他不悦地看着凌乱的房间。  She spoke displeasingly about her neighbor's dog. 她不高兴地谈论她邻居的狗。 | unpleasantly, unhappily, disagreeably | pleasingly, agreeably
