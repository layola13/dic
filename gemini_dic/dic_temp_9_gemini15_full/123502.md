monotheistically:/ˌmɒnəθiˈɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 一神论地；相信一神论地 |  Adverb describing something done in a manner consistent with monotheism, the belief in a single god.  It indicates actions, beliefs, or systems reflecting this belief. |例句：Many societies have functioned monotheistically for centuries. 许多社会已经信奉一神论几个世纪了。|例句：The kingdom was ruled monotheistically, with the king considered a divine representative. 这个王国奉行一神论统治，国王被视为神的代表。|近义词：theistically, religiously (in a religious manner, but not necessarily monotheistically) |反义词：polytheistically
