half-frowning: /ˈhæf ˈfraʊnɪŋ/ | adj. | 半皱眉的，略带皱眉的 |  Describes a facial expression that is partly a frown, suggesting slight displeasure, displeasure, or concentration. It implies a less intense frown than a full frown.  | Example:  He gave a half-frowning look at the messy room. 他半皱眉地看了一眼凌乱的房间。


近义词: slightly frowning,  with a hint of a frown,  with a furrowed brow (but less intense)

反义词: smiling, beaming, cheerful
