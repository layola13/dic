moment:/ˈmoʊmənt/| /ˈməʊmənt/ | n. |瞬间，片刻；时机，时刻；重要时刻 |A moment is a very short period of time.  It can also refer to a particular point in time or a significant event. |例句：I'll be with you in a moment.  我一会儿就来。


例句：That was a truly significant moment in history. 那是一个真正重要的历史时刻。


近义词：instant, second, juncture, occasion |反义词：eternity, age, forever |词性:名词
