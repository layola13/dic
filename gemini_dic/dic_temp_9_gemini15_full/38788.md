conducting:/kənˈdʌktɪŋ/ | v. | 传导；指挥；引导；进行 |  The act of leading, guiding, or managing something; the transmission of electricity, heat, or sound.  | 例句：The orchestra is conducting a new symphony.  乐队正在指挥一首新的交响乐。

例句：The wire is conducting electricity. 这根电线正在导电。


近义词：directing, managing, leading, transmitting | 反义词：insulating, obstructing, hindering | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is conducting the experiment. 他正在进行实验。
She conducts research on climate change. 她进行气候变化研究。

一般过去时:
They conducted a survey last week. 他们上周进行了一项调查。
He conducted the orchestra with passion. 他充满激情地指挥乐队。

现在完成时:
We have conducted several experiments this year. 今年我们已经进行了几次实验。
She has conducted numerous interviews for the job. 她已经为这份工作进行了多次面试。

过去完成时:
They had conducted the research before publishing the paper. 他们在发表论文之前已经完成了研究。
The team had conducted a thorough investigation.  团队已经进行了彻底的调查。

将来时:
We will be conducting a training session tomorrow. 我们明天将进行一次培训。
The scientists will conduct further tests. 科学家们将进行进一步的测试。

虚拟语气:
If he were conducting the meeting, it would be more efficient. 如果是他主持会议，效率会更高。
I wish I were conducting the orchestra. 我希望是我在指挥乐队。

被动语态:
The experiment was conducted under strict conditions. 实验是在严格的条件下进行的。
The survey is being conducted by a professional agency. 调查由一家专业机构进行。

疑问句:
Who is conducting the class today? 今天谁在授课？
Are they conducting any research on this topic? 他们对这个话题进行任何研究吗？

否定句:
The experiment was not conducted properly. 实验没有进行得当。
He did not conduct himself appropriately. 他表现得不得体。

词源:
源自拉丁语 *conducere，由 *com-（表示“一起”）和 *ducere（表示“引导”）构成。  因此，其核心含义是“引导在一起”，引申出“传导”、“指挥”、“引导”等含义。
