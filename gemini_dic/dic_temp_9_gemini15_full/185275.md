retransplanting: /ˌriːtrænzˈplæntɪŋ/ | n. | 再移植；重新种植 | The act of planting something again, especially after it has been removed or disturbed.  This term often refers to plants, but can also be used metaphorically. | 例句：The gardener is undertaking the replanting of the damaged flowerbeds. 园丁正在对受损的花坛进行重新种植。

The replanting of the saplings after the storm was a laborious task.  暴风雨过后，重新种植树苗是一项费力的工作。


近义词：replanting, transplanting again, re-establishment


反义词：uprooting, removal, eradication


词性:名词


USAGE->

The gardener retransplants the seedlings regularly. 园丁定期对幼苗进行再移植。
They replant the herbs after harvesting. 他们收割后重新种植香草。
