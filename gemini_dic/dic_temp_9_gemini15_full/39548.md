conscience-stricken: /ˈkɒnʃənsˌstrɪkən/| adj. |良心不安的，悔恨的|Feeling guilt or remorse for having done something wrong.|The conscience-stricken thief returned the stolen goods.  良心不安的小偷归还了赃物。|近义词：guilty, remorseful, repentant |反义词：blameless, guiltless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is conscience-stricken about lying to his parents. 他对向父母撒谎感到良心不安。
She is conscience-stricken by her actions.  她为自己的行为感到良心不安。


一般过去时:
He was conscience-stricken after he cheated on the exam. 考试作弊后，他感到良心不安。
She was conscience-stricken about breaking her promise. 她违背诺言后感到很内疚。


现在完成时:
He has been conscience-stricken ever since he hurt his friend. 从他伤害朋友以来，他就一直良心不安。
She has been conscience-stricken about her hurtful words. 她一直为自己的伤害性言辞感到内疚。


过去完成时:
He had been conscience-stricken for weeks before he finally confessed. 在最终坦白之前，他已经几周都良心不安了。
She had been conscience-stricken about the incident for months.  她对这件事已经内疚了几个月。


将来时:
He will be conscience-stricken if he doesn't tell the truth. 如果他不说实话，他会良心不安的。
She will be conscience-stricken if she abandons her project.  如果她放弃她的项目，她会感到内疚的。


虚拟语气:
If he had told the truth, he wouldn't be conscience-stricken now. 如果他当时说实话，现在就不会良心不安了。
If she hadn't lied, she wouldn't be conscience-stricken. 如果她没有撒谎，她就不会良心不安。


被动语态:
His conscience was stricken by his actions. 他的良心因他的行为而受折磨。
Her conscience was stricken with guilt.  她的良心充满了罪恶感。(注意：被动语态使用较少)


疑问句:
Is he conscience-stricken about his behavior? 他对自己的行为感到内疚吗？
Was she conscience-stricken after the argument? 争吵后她感到内疚吗？


否定句:
He is not conscience-stricken about his decision. 他对自己的决定并不感到内疚。
She was not conscience-stricken at all. 她一点也不内疚。
