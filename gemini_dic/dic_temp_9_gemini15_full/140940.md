one-man: /ˌwʌnˈmæn/ | adj. | 单人操作的；由一人负责的 |  Describes something done or operated by a single person. It can refer to a business, a show, a job, or a task.  | 例句：He runs a one-man operation. = 他经营着一家单人企业。 He put on a one-man show. = 他独自一人表演了一场独角戏。 | 近义词：solo, single-handed, individual | 反义词：team-based, collaborative


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He runs a one-man business. = 他经营着一家单人企业。
The show is a one-man performance. = 这场演出是个人表演。


一般过去时:
He managed a one-man shop for ten years. = 他经营了一家单人商店十年。
She completed the project as a one-man team. = 她作为一人团队完成了这个项目。


现在完成时:
He has successfully run his one-man business for five years. = 他成功地经营他的单人企业五年了。
She has always preferred one-man projects. = 她总是更喜欢单人项目。


过去完成时:
He had already established his one-man business before he turned 30. = 他在30岁之前就已经建立了自己的单人企业。
She had completed several one-man projects before joining the team. = 在加入团队之前，她已经完成了几个单人项目。


将来时:
He will continue his one-man operation next year. = 他明年将继续他的单人经营。
She will start a one-man business after graduation. = 她毕业后将开办一家单人企业。


虚拟语气:
If he were to start a business, it would be a one-man operation. = 如果他要创业，那将是一家单人企业。
I wish I could handle this project as a one-man show. = 我希望我能把这个项目当作个人表演来处理。


被动语态:
The task was completed by a one-man team. = 这项任务是由一个单人团队完成的。
The project can't be handled by a one-man operation. = 这个项目无法由单人操作来处理。


疑问句:
Is this a one-man show? = 这是个人表演吗？
Was the company a one-man operation originally? = 这家公司最初是不是单人经营的？


否定句:
It's not a one-man job; it requires a team. = 这不是单人工作；它需要一个团队。
This task cannot be accomplished by a one-man effort. = 这项任务不能由单人完成。
