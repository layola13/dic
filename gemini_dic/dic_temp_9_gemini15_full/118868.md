metascientific:/ˌmɛtəˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪk/| adj.|元科学的；关于科学方法的 | Relating to the philosophical study of the methods and assumptions of science.  It examines the nature of scientific knowledge, its limitations, and its social and cultural context. | Example:  The article presents a metascientific analysis of the research methodology. 这篇文章对研究方法进行了元科学分析。 | 近义词：epistemological (regarding knowledge), philosophical (regarding science) | 反义词：empirical (based on observation), practical (focused on application)
