This can apply to physical objects, systems, or people's attitudes and behaviors. | 例句：The nonflexibleness of the company's policies led to its decline. 公司政策的不灵活导致了它的衰落。


The nonflexibleness of the old machine made it difficult to repair. 这台旧机器的不灵活使得修理它变得困难。
