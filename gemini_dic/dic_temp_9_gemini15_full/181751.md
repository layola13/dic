relieve:/rɪˈliːv/ | vt. n. | 解除，减轻；使轻松；接替 | To make a problem, feeling, or situation less severe.  |例句：The medicine relieved my headache. 这药减轻了我的头痛。

例句：The new manager relieved her of her duties. 新经理解除了她的职务。

近义词：alleviate, ease, lessen | 反义词：aggravate, worsen | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The medication relieves my pain. = 这药缓解了我的疼痛。
He relieves stress by exercising. = 他通过运动来缓解压力。

一般过去时:
The rain relieved the drought. = 雨水缓解了干旱。
She relieved her colleague at 5 pm. = 她下午5点接替了同事的工作。

现在完成时:
The new policy has relieved much of the burden. = 新政策减轻了许多负担。
He has relieved his anxiety by talking to a friend. = 他通过和朋友聊天来缓解焦虑。

过去完成时:
They had relieved the pressure before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前缓解了压力。
She had relieved her shift before going home. = 她下班回家之前已经换班了。

将来时:
This medicine will relieve your symptoms. = 这药会缓解你的症状。
The arrival of the reinforcements will relieve the troops. = 增援部队的到来将减轻部队的压力。

虚拟语气:
If I had taken the medicine, it would have relieved my headache. = 如果我吃了药，我的头痛就会减轻了。
I wish the rain would relieve the drought. = 我希望这场雨能缓解旱情。

被动语态:
His pain was relieved by the medication. = 他的疼痛被药物缓解了。
The pressure was relieved by the timely intervention. = 及时的干预减轻了压力。

疑问句:
Will this treatment relieve my symptoms? = 这种治疗会缓解我的症状吗？
Does this medicine relieve pain effectively? = 这药能有效缓解疼痛吗？

否定句:
The medicine didn't relieve my headache. = 这药没有减轻我的头痛。
The new policy hasn't relieved the economic pressure. = 新政策并没有减轻经济压力。


PHRASE->
relieve oneself  = 解手
relieve pressure = 减轻压力
relieve pain = 缓解疼痛
relieve symptoms = 缓解症状
relieve a situation = 缓和局势

词源:
relieve源自古法语relever，意为“举起，抬高”，由re-(再次) + lever(举起)构成。其现代含义演变自“减轻负担”的含义，逐渐扩展到“减轻痛苦、压力等”等方面。
