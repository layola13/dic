moonset:/ˈmuːnˌsɛt/ | n. | 月落 | The disappearance of the moon below the horizon.  Moonset is the time when the upper limb of the moon disappears below the horizon.  | Example: We watched the beautiful moonset over the ocean.  我们看着美丽的月亮沉入大海。  The moonset was spectacular tonight. 今晚的月落景象壮观。| 近义词：sunset, sundown | 反义词：moonrise
