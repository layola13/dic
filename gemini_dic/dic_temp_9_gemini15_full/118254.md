merchandisers:/ˈmɜːrtʃəndaɪzərz/| n. |商品推销员；商品策划人员|People or companies involved in the planning, promotion, and sale of goods. They work to increase sales and profits by managing product displays, promotions, and pricing strategies.  |Example: The merchandisers worked hard to create an attractive display for the new line of clothing.|近义词：retailers, salespeople, marketers|反义词：N/A
