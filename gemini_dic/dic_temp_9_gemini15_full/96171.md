The subject matter or expression may be too complex, abstract, or illogical to grasp.  |The professor's lecture was indeprehensible to most of the students.  教授的讲座对大多数学生来说难以理解。

The instructions were so indeprehensible that I couldn't follow them.  说明书太难懂了，我无法理解。
