pent:/pent/| adj.|幽闭的；闷闷不乐的；憋闷的|confined, shut in; sullen, gloomy; feeling stifled or trapped|例句：The pent-up frustration finally exploded.  积压的挫败感最终爆发了。

例句：He felt pent in the small room. 他感觉在那个小房间里很压抑。


近义词：confined, stifled, cramped, gloomy, sullen, depressed|反义词：open, airy, cheerful, liberated, free


词源:源自古英语 *penned，意为“关在围栏里”。
