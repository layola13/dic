dependableness:/dɪˈpɛndəblənəs/ | n. |可靠性，可依赖性 | The dependableness of the system is crucial for its success.  系统的可靠性对其成功至关重要。|近义词：reliability, trustworthiness, dependability | 反义词：unreliability, untrustworthiness


例句：

His dependableness made him a valuable asset to the team.  他的可靠性使他成为团队中宝贵的财富。

The company prides itself on the dependableness of its products.  这家公司以其产品的可靠性而自豪。


词源:

The word "dependableness" is derived from the verb "depend," which ultimately traces back to the Old French word "depender" (to hang down from).  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or suitability, and "-ness" transforms the adjective into a noun, signifying a quality or state.


用法说明：

Dependableness is a noun referring to the quality of being reliable and trustworthy.  It emphasizes the consistency and predictability of someone or something.  It's often used to describe people, systems, or processes that can be relied upon to function as expected.
