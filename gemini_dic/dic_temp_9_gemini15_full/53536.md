disavowal: /ˌdɪsəˈvaʊəl/ | n. |否认；拒绝承认；放弃；抛弃 | The act of denying or repudiating something;  a denial of responsibility or connection.  It implies a deliberate rejection of something previously acknowledged or associated with oneself. |


例句：

The company issued a public disavowal of the employee's actions.  公司公开否认了员工的行为。

His disavowal of the treaty angered many. 他否认该条约激怒了许多人。


近义词：denial, repudiation, renunciation, rejection

反义词：affirmation, acknowledgement, acceptance, endorsement
