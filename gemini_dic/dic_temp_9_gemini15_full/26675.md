cadaster:/ˈkædəstər/| n. |地籍；地籍册；土地登记簿|A cadaster is a register of land ownership, often including details of boundaries, area, and value.  It provides a comprehensive record of the land parcels within a specific area.  A cadaster is essential for property taxation, land administration, and planning. |The government is updating its cadaster to reflect recent land transactions.|近义词：land register, property register, land record |反义词：无
