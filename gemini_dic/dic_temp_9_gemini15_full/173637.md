quasi-interviewed:/ˈkwɑːziˌɪntərˌvjuːd/| adj.|准采访；类似采访的|The candidate was quasi-interviewed, meaning he wasn't formally interviewed but was informally assessed.  候选人进行了准采访，这意味着他没有正式接受采访，而是进行了非正式评估。|近义词：informally assessed, informally evaluated|反义词：formally interviewed

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He quasi-interviews potential employees over coffee. 他在咖啡馆里对潜在员工进行准面试。
She quasi-interviews candidates for the position. 她对该职位候选人进行准面试。


一般过去时:
The journalist quasi-interviewed the celebrity at a party. 记者在一个派对上对这位名人进行了准采访。
They quasi-interviewed several applicants before making a decision. 他们在做决定之前对几个申请者进行了准面试。


现在完成时:
The company has quasi-interviewed many applicants this month. 这个月公司已经对许多申请人进行了准面试。
She has quasi-interviewed him several times for various projects. 她已经为他做过几次不同项目的准面试。


过去完成时:
They had quasi-interviewed the candidate before the formal interview. 他们在正式面试之前已经对候选人进行了准面试。
The team had quasi-interviewed all the shortlisted candidates.  团队已经对所有入围的候选人进行了准面试。
