carping:/ˈkɑːrpɪŋ/|/ˈkɑːrpɪŋ/| |n. v.|挑剔；抱怨；批评|The act of finding fault or criticizing; persistent petty criticism.  It can refer to the act of complaining or criticizing, or the complaints themselves.|例句：Her constant carping about the weather got on everyone's nerves.  她不断抱怨天气，让每个人都心烦。

例句：His carping criticisms were ignored by his colleagues. 他的喋喋不休的批评被同事们忽视了。|近义词：criticism, complaint, fault-finding, nagging|反义词：praise, commendation, approval|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He carps about everything. = 他对一切事情都吹毛求疵。
She carps at her husband constantly. = 她不断地对她的丈夫唠叨。


一般过去时:
He carped about the service at the restaurant. = 他抱怨餐厅的服务。
She carped at her son for his messy room. = 她因为儿子的房间脏乱而责备他。


现在完成时:
They have carped about the project for weeks. = 他们已经抱怨这个项目几周了。
She has carped at me all day. = 她整天都在唠叨我。


过去完成时:
He had carped about the unfairness of the system for years. = 他多年来一直在抱怨制度的不公平。
She had carped at him before he finally admitted his mistake. = 在他最终承认错误之前，她一直在责备他。


将来时:
I know he will carp about the cost. = 我知道他会抱怨成本。
She will carp at him until he does it. = 她会一直唠叨他直到他做了为止。


虚拟语气:
If he carped less, people would like him better. = 如果他少抱怨一些，人们会更喜欢他。
I wish she wouldn't carp so much. = 我希望她不要抱怨那么多。


被动语态:
His work was carped about by his boss. = 他的工作受到了老板的批评。
The decision was carped at by many people. = 许多人对这个决定表示不满。


疑问句:
Does he often carp about his job? = 他经常抱怨他的工作吗？
Why is she always carping at everyone? = 她为什么总是对每个人吹毛求疵？


否定句:
He doesn't carp about trivial matters. = 他不会抱怨琐碎的事情。
She didn't carp at him this time. = 她这次没有责备他。
