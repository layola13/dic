drinker:/ˈdrɪŋkər/| n. |饮酒者，嗜酒者|A person who drinks alcoholic beverages, especially habitually.  |例句：He's a heavy drinker and needs to cut back. 他是个酒鬼，需要少喝点酒。|例句：She's a social drinker, only drinking occasionally with friends. 她是个社交型饮酒者，只偶尔和朋友一起喝酒。|近义词：alcoholic (for excessive drinking), tippler, imbiber |反义词：teetotaler, abstainer


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He drinks beer every day. = 他每天都喝啤酒。
She drinks coffee in the morning. = 她早上喝咖啡。


一般过去时:
He drank too much last night. = 他昨晚喝太多了。
She drank only water at the party. = 她在派对上只喝水。


现在完成时:
He has drunk three beers already. = 他已经喝了三瓶啤酒。
She has never drunk wine before. = 她以前从未喝过葡萄酒。


过去完成时:
He had drunk all the beer before I arrived. = 我到之前他已经喝完所有的啤酒了。
She had never drunk so much before that night. = 在那天晚上之前，她从未喝过这么多酒。


将来时:
He will drink less next week. = 他下周会少喝点。
She will drink champagne at the celebration. = 她将在庆祝活动上喝香槟。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't drink and drive. = 如果我是你，我不会酒后驾车。
I wish I hadn't drunk so much last night. = 我真希望昨晚我没有喝那么多酒。


被动语态:
(This word doesn't typically use passive voice in this context.  Passive voice is more suitable for describing actions done *to* the drinker, rather than actions *by* the drinker.)


疑问句:
Does he drink often? = 他经常喝酒吗？
Does she drink tea or coffee? = 她喝茶还是咖啡？


否定句:
He doesn't drink alcohol. = 他不喝酒。
She doesn't drink much. = 她不太喝酒。


PHRASE->
heavy drinker: 酗酒者
social drinker: 社交型饮酒者
problem drinker: 有酗酒问题的饮酒者
occasional drinker: 偶尔饮酒者
designated drinker: 代驾者（负责开车不喝酒的人）
