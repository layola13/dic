self-realization: /ˌsɛlfˌriːəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |自我实现，自我认知 | The process of fully developing one's potential and becoming the person one is capable of being. It involves understanding one's strengths, weaknesses, values, and beliefs, and then using this self-knowledge to live a more authentic and fulfilling life.  | Example:  Through years of therapy, she finally achieved self-realization and embraced her true self. 通过多年的治疗，她最终实现了自我认知，并接受了真实的自己。


Example:  His journey to self-realization involved overcoming many obstacles and challenges. 他通往自我实现的旅程中克服了许多障碍和挑战。|近义词：self-discovery, self-actualization, fulfillment |反义词：self-destruction, self-neglect
