have a handle on: /ˌhæv ə ˈhændəl ɒn/ | phrasal verb | 理解，掌握，控制 | To understand or manage something successfully.  | 例句：I think I finally have a handle on this new software. = 我想我终于掌握了这个新软件。

例句：She has a handle on the situation. = 她掌握了局势。


近义词：understand, grasp, manage, control, comprehend, get a grip on | 反义词：misunderstand, be clueless about, be out of control


词性：phrasal verb
