lives:/laɪvz/ | n. |生命；生活；居住；生物 |The lives of the poor are often hard. 穷人的生活通常很艰难。 |近义词：life, existence, livelihood |反义词：death


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lives in London. 他住在伦敦。
They live a simple life. 他们过着简朴的生活。

一般过去时:
She lived in Paris for five years. 她曾在巴黎住了五年。
We lived happily ever after. 我们从此过着幸福的生活。

现在完成时:
I have lived here for ten years. 我在这里住了十年了。
They have lived through many hardships. 他们经历了许多苦难。

过去完成时:
He had lived in New York before moving to Los Angeles. 他在搬到洛杉矶之前住在纽约。
She had lived a difficult life before finding success.  她在获得成功之前过着艰难的生活。

将来时:
I will live in the countryside one day. 我有朝一日会住在乡下。
They will live to see their grandchildren. 他们将活到看到孙子孙女的那一天。

虚拟语气:
If I had a million dollars, I would live a luxurious life. 如果我有百万美元，我会过上奢华的生活。
I wish I lived closer to my family. 我希望我住得离我的家人更近。

被动语态:  (Lives itself is rarely used in passive voice.  The passive voice is more commonly used with verbs related to *living*, such as *to be lived*.)
That life was lived to the fullest. 那生活过得非常充实。(This uses "lived" as a past participle)
The house has been lived in for generations. 这房子住了几代人了。(This is passive, but focuses on the house, not "lives")


疑问句:
Where do you live? 你住在哪里？
How long have you lived there? 你在那里住了多久？

否定句:
I don't live in a big house. 我不住在豪宅里。
He didn't live to see his children grow up. 他没活到看到他的孩子们长大。
