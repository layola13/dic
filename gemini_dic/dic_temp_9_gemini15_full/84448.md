haloes:/ˈheɪloʊz/| n. |光环；晕轮；圣光|A halo is a ring of light appearing around the sun or moon, or sometimes around a person's head in art.  It is often associated with holiness or divine power.  The plural form "haloes" is less common than the alternative plural "halos".|例句：The saint in the painting had a golden halo around his head.  这幅画中的圣人头上有一圈金色的光环。


The setting sun cast haloes around the mountain peaks. 落日给山峰投下了一圈圈的光环。|近义词：aureole, nimbus, corona|反义词：None specifically, but words implying darkness or shadow could be considered antonyms in a figurative sense.|名词
