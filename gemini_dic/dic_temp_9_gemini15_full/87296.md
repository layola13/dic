hermitess:/ˈhɜːrmɪtɪs/| n. |女隐士，隐居的女子 |A hermitess is a woman who lives in seclusion, often for religious reasons or to pursue a solitary life.  She may live alone in a remote area, dedicating herself to prayer, meditation, or other spiritual practices.  |Example: The hermitess lived a simple life in a small cabin in the woods, far from the distractions of the world.  这位女隐士在远离尘嚣的森林里的小木屋里过着简朴的生活。|近义词：recluse, solitary, anchoress |反义词：socialite, extrovert
