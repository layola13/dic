castigate:/ˈkæstɪɡeɪt/| vt.|严厉批评；惩罚|To criticize someone severely.  To punish someone severely.|例句：The teacher castigated the student for cheating on the exam.老师严厉批评了那个考试作弊的学生。

例句：The judge castigated the criminal for his heinous crimes. 法官严厉惩罚了罪犯的滔天罪行。

近义词：rebuke, reprimand, chastise, censure, upbraid|反义词：praise, commend, applaud|动词
