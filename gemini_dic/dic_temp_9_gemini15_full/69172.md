faxes:/ˈfæksɪz/| n.|传真；传真件|Faxes are a quick way to send documents.|传真是发送文件的一种快捷方式。|近义词：facsimiles|反义词：None.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He faxes his reports every week. = 他每周都传真他的报告。
She faxes the documents to the client. = 她把文件传真给客户。

一般过去时:
I faxed the contract yesterday. = 我昨天传真了合同。
They faxed the information to the office. = 他们把信息传真给了办公室。

现在完成时:
We have faxed all the necessary documents. = 我们已经传真了所有必要的文档。
She has faxed the application form. = 她已经传真了申请表。

过去完成时:
He had faxed the report before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经传真了报告。
They had faxed the documents before the deadline. = 他们早在截止日期前就已传真了文件。

将来时:
I will fax you the details later. = 我稍后会把细节传真给你。
She will fax the order to the supplier tomorrow. = 她明天会把订单传真给供应商。

虚拟语气:
If I had a fax machine, I would fax the document. = 如果我有一台传真机，我会把文件传真过去。
I wish I had faxed the report earlier. = 我真希望我早点把报告传真过去。

被动语态:
The documents were faxed to the wrong address. = 文件传真到了错误的地址。
The report is being faxed right now. = 报告正在被传真。

疑问句:
Did you fax the documents? = 你传真文件了吗？
Has she faxed the information yet? = 她已经传真信息了吗？

否定句:
I didn't fax the report. = 我没有传真报告。
He hasn't faxed the documents yet. = 他还没传真文件。


PHRASE->
send a fax = 发送传真
receive a fax = 收到传真
fax a document = 传真一份文件
fax over = 传真过来
fax something to someone = 把某物传真给某人
