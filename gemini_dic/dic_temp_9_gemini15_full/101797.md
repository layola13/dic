irrigating:/ˈɪrɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ | v. |灌溉；冲洗；清除（阻塞物）|The act of supplying water to land or crops to help growth.  It can also refer to flushing or cleaning something by using a fluid.|例句：Farmers are irrigating their fields.  农民们正在灌溉他们的田地。

例句：The doctor is irrigating the wound to remove debris. 医生正在冲洗伤口以清除碎屑。


近义词：watering, watering, flushing|反义词：dehydrating, drying


USAGE->

They irrigate their crops regularly. = 他们定期灌溉庄稼。
The system irrigates the entire field efficiently. = 这个系统高效地灌溉整个田地。


一般过去时:
The farmers irrigated their fields last week. = 农民们上周灌溉了他们的田地。
