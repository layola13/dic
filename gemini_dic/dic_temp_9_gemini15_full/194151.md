self-instructors:/ˌsɛlfɪnˈstrʌktərz/| n. |自学者；自主学习者 |People who learn independently, often using self-study materials or online resources. They are not guided by a formal instructor or teacher.  |Example: Many self-instructors find online courses a valuable resource. 许多自学者发现在线课程是一种宝贵的资源。 |近义词：independent learners, autodidacts |反义词：students (in a formal setting), pupils
