erysipelas:/ˌɛrɪsɪˈpɛləs/ | n. |丹毒，红斑性皮肤病 | Erysipelas is a type of cellulitis, a bacterial skin infection that causes inflammation and redness. It typically affects the face, legs, and arms.  | Example: The doctor diagnosed her condition as erysipelas and prescribed antibiotics. 医生诊断她的病情为丹毒，并开了抗生素。  |近义词: cellulitis, impetigo |反义词:  (无明确反义词，可考虑healthy skin) | 名词
