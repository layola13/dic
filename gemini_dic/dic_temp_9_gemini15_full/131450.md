/ˌnɒnkəʊˈɜːsɪvnəs/ | adj. | 非强制性；不自觉性 | The absence of coercion; lack of compulsion or force.  It describes a situation or process where there is no pressure or influence to act in a particular way.  This concept is often used in various fields like law, psychology, and sociology.  | Example: The noncoerciveness of the survey design ensured participants felt free to answer honestly.  调查设计的非强制性确保参与者能够自由地如实回答。 | 近义词： voluntariness, freedom, permissiveness | 反义词： coercion, compulsion, force
