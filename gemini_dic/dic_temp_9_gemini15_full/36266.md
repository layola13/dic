cohabit:/koʊˈhæbɪt/| v.|同居，共栖|To live together, especially as an unmarried couple.|例句：They decided to cohabit before getting married. 他们决定在结婚前同居。|例句：Many birds cohabit in the same tree. 许多鸟类栖息在同一棵树上。|近义词：live together, dwell together|反义词：live separately, live apart
