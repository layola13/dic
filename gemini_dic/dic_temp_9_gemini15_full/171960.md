puppyism:/ˈpʌpiˌɪzəm/| n.|幼犬行为；幼稚行为；天真烂漫|Behavior characteristic of a puppy; childish or immature behavior;  naivete.  It often refers to overly enthusiastic, playful, and sometimes annoying behavior.  The term carries a slightly negative connotation, suggesting a lack of maturity or seriousness.|例句：His puppyism was endearing at first, but it grew tiresome after a while.  他的幼犬般行为起初很讨人喜欢，但过了一段时间就让人厌烦了。


例句：Her constant giggling and playful antics were a symptom of her puppyism. 她持续的傻笑和顽皮的滑稽动作是她幼稚行为的体现。


近义词：immaturity, childishness, silliness, naiveté


反义词：maturity, seriousness, sophistication, worldliness


词性：名词
