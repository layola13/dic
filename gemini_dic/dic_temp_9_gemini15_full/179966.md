reffed:/rɛft/| v. |裁判；仲裁；(非正式)提及；参考|To officiate as a referee in a game or contest.  It can also informally mean to mention something or to refer to something.|例句：He reffed the soccer game last night. 他昨晚当了足球比赛的裁判。|例句：She reffed the incident in her report. 她在报告中提到了这一事件。|近义词：officiate, judge, arbitrate, mention|反义词：participate (as a player), ignore
