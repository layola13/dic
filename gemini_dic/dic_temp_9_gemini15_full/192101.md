scouted:/skaʊtɪd/| v.|侦察，寻找，物色|to search for and examine something, or to search for a suitable person or thing |The scouts scouted the area for enemy troops. 侦察兵侦察该地区是否有敌军。|They scouted a new location for the factory. 他们为工厂物色了一个新地点。|surveyed, explored, investigated| ignored, overlooked|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They scout the area regularly. = 他们定期侦察该地区。
She scouts for new talent. = 她物色新人才。


一般过去时:
We scouted the neighborhood before buying the house. = 我们在买房子之前勘察了附近街区。
He scouted several locations for the film shoot. = 他为电影拍摄勘察了几个地点。


现在完成时:
They have scouted several potential candidates for the job. = 他们已经物色了几位潜在的候选人。
The team has scouted the opposition's strengths and weaknesses. = 球队已经侦察了对手的优缺点。


过去完成时:
They had scouted the route before the race began. = 比赛开始前他们已经勘察过路线了。
She had scouted the market for the best deal. = 她已经调查过市场，寻找最划算的交易。


将来时:
We will scout the area tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上将侦察该地区。
They will scout for a suitable venue for the concert. = 他们将为音乐会物色合适的场地。


虚拟语气:
If I had scouted the terrain beforehand, I would have avoided that mishap. = 如果我事先勘察过地形，我就不会遇到那次事故了。
If they had scouted the market better, they would have made more profit. = 如果他们更好地调查市场，他们就会赚到更多利润。


被动语态:
The area was scouted by the military. = 该地区由军方侦察。
The new recruits were scouted by the company. = 新兵被公司选中。


疑问句:
Have they scouted the location yet? = 他们已经勘察过地点了吗？
Did you scout the area thoroughly? = 你彻底勘察过该地区了吗？


否定句:
They did not scout the area before starting the project. = 他们在开始项目之前没有侦察该地区。
We haven't scouted any new players this season. = 这个赛季我们还没有物色到任何新球员。
