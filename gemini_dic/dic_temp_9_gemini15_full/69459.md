feigns:/feɪnz/| vt.|假装，伪装|To pretend to have or feel something, typically to deceive.|He feigned illness to avoid work. 他假装生病以逃避工作。|She feigned surprise at the news. 她假装对这个消息感到惊讶。|simulate, affect, pretend|be genuine, be real, be honest|动词
USAGE->
一般现在时:
He feigns ignorance. = 他假装不知道。
She feigns interest in his stories. = 她假装对他的故事感兴趣。

一般过去时:
He feigned sleep to avoid the conversation. = 他假装睡觉以避免谈话。
She feigned surprise when she received the gift. = 她收到礼物时假装很惊讶。
