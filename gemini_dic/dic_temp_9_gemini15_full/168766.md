prodigate:/ˈprɒdɪɡeɪt/ /ˈprɑːdɪɡeɪt/| adj., n.|挥霍的；浪费的；放荡的；败家子|

英文解析:
adj.  Spending money or resources freely and recklessly; wasteful.  Also describes someone who is extravagant or immoral in their behavior.
n. A person who spends money in an extravagant, irresponsible way.


例句:
adj. His prodigal spending habits left him deeply in debt.  他的挥霍无度的消费习惯使他负债累累。

adj. The prodigal use of natural resources is unsustainable.  对自然资源的挥霍浪费是不可持续的。

n.  He was known as a prodigal son, squandering his inheritance. 他被称为败家子，挥霍了他的遗产。

n. The prodigal lifestyle of the wealthy was criticized by many.  富人的挥霍生活方式受到了许多人的批评。


近义词:
extravagant, wasteful, spendthrift, profligate, dissolute


反义词:
frugal, economical, thrifty, sparing, abstemious


词性:
adj., n.
