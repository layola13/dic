self-delights:/ˌsɛlf dɪˈlaɪts/| n.|自我陶醉，自得其乐|The act of finding pleasure or satisfaction in oneself or one's own actions, often without regard for others.  It implies a self-centered enjoyment, sometimes to the point of narcissism.|例句：He spent hours admiring his reflection, lost in self-delights. 他花了好几个小时欣赏自己的倒影，沉醉于自我陶醉之中。|例句：Her constant self-delights made her increasingly isolated from her friends. 她不断的自我陶醉让她越来越孤立于朋友之外。|近义词：self-indulgence, self-gratification, narcissism|反义词：self-deprecation, humility|名词
