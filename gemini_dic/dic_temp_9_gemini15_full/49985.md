denitration:/ˌdiːnaɪˈtreɪʃən/| n. |脱硝；反硝化作用|The removal of nitrogen or nitrogen compounds, especially nitrates, from a substance or environment.  This process is crucial in various applications, including wastewater treatment and environmental remediation.  It often involves biological or chemical methods.|例句：The power plant uses selective catalytic reduction for denitration of its exhaust gases. 这座电厂使用选择性催化还原法对其废气进行脱硝。|例句：Biological denitration in soil helps maintain a healthy nitrogen cycle. 土壤中的生物脱硝有助于维持健康的氮循环。|近义词：denitrification|反义词：nitration
