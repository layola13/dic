granite:/ˈɡrænaɪt/| n.|花岗岩|A very hard, granular, crystalline igneous rock consisting mainly of feldspar, quartz, and mica, often used as a building stone.|例句：The countertops are made of polished granite.  厨房台面是用抛光花岗岩制成的。

例句：The mountain was composed primarily of granite. 这座山主要由花岗岩构成。


近义词：basalt, gneiss|反义词：无


词性：名词
