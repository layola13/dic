remention:/ˌriːˈmɛnʃən/ | vt. |再次提及；重新提起|To mention something again; to bring something up again.|例句：He rementioned the incident during the meeting. 他在会议上再次提到了这件事。|例句：She rementioned her concerns about the project. 她再次表达了她对这个项目的担忧。|近义词：mention again, reiterate, recall|反义词：ignore, forget


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rementions the problem every time we meet. = 他每次见到我们都再次提起这个问题。
They remention the plan at each board meeting. = 他们在每次董事会上都会重新提及这个计划。

一般过去时:
She rementioned the story to her friends. = 她向她的朋友们再次提起了这个故事。
He rementioned his childhood dream last night. = 他昨晚再次提起了他儿时的梦想。

现在完成时:
I have rementioned the deadline several times. = 我已经多次提醒了截止日期。
They have rementioned the importance of safety protocols. = 他们已经再次强调了安全规程的重要性。

过去完成时:
She had rementioned the issue before the final decision. = 在最终决定之前，她已经再次提到了这个问题。
He had rementioned the contract terms several times. = 他已经多次再次提到了合同条款。

将来时:
I will remention the topic in my next presentation. = 我将在我的下一次演示中再次提及这个话题。
They will remention the proposal to the investors. = 他们将向投资者重新提出这个提案。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would remention the offer to the client. = 如果我是你，我会再次向客户提出这个报价。
I wish he had rementioned the details before leaving. = 我希望他在离开之前再次提到了细节。


被动语态:
The issue was rementioned by the manager. = 这个问题被经理再次提及。
The proposal was rementioned in the subsequent meeting. = 该提案在随后的会议上被再次提及。


疑问句:
Will you remention the incident to the police? = 你会向警方再次提及这起事件吗？
Did he remention his concerns to anyone? = 他有没有向任何人再次提起他的担忧？


否定句:
I didn't remention the embarrassing situation. = 我没有再次提及那个尴尬的局面。
She didn't remention her plans for the weekend. = 她没有再次提及她周末的计划。


PHRASE->
remention the subject = 再次提及主题
remention the matter = 再次提及此事
remention the details = 再次提及细节
remention the point = 再次提及要点
remention the proposal = 再次提及提案
