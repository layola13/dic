maudlinly:/ˈmɔːdlɪnli/| adv.|伤感的，多愁善感的| excessively sentimental; tearfully or weakly emotional.|She spoke maudlinly about her lost love. 她伤心地谈论着失去的爱人。|近义词：sentimentally, tearfully, emotionally|反义词：stoically, calmly, rationally


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He speaks maudlinly about his past. = 他伤感地谈论他的过去。
She often acts maudlinly after drinking wine. = 她喝酒后经常表现得很伤感。


一般过去时:
He spoke maudlinly about his childhood. = 他伤感地谈论他的童年。
She acted maudlinly at the funeral. = 她在葬礼上表现得很伤感。


现在完成时:
She has spoken maudlinly about her lost dog many times. = 她已经多次伤感地谈论她失去的狗。
He has acted maudlinly since the breakup. = 分手后，他一直表现得很伤感。


过去完成时:
He had spoken maudlinly before he passed out. = 他昏过去之前一直在伤感地说话。
She had acted maudlinly all evening. = 她整个晚上都表现得很伤感。


将来时:
He will speak maudlinly if he drinks too much. = 如果他喝太多酒，他会伤感地说话。
She will likely act maudlinly at the wedding. = 她很可能会在婚礼上表现得很伤感。


虚拟语气:
If he drank more, he would speak more maudlinly. = 如果他喝得更多，他会更伤感地说话。
If she were sadder, she would act more maudlinly. = 如果她更悲伤，她会表现得更伤感。


被动语态:  (Maudlinly is an adverb, it doesn't usually take a passive voice.)  The story was described as being maudlinly sentimental. = 故事被描述为过于伤感。
The situation was portrayed maudlinly in the film. = 电影中对局势的描绘过于伤感。


疑问句:
Does he often speak maudlinly? = 他经常伤感地说话吗？
Did she act maudlinly at the party? = 她在派对上表现得很伤感吗？


否定句:
He didn't speak maudlinly at all. = 他根本没有伤感地说话。
She didn't act maudlinly; she was simply tired. = 她并没有表现得很伤感；她只是累了。



PHRASE->
There aren't common phrases directly using "maudlinly" as an adverbial phrase.  Instead,  "maudlin" is used in phrases like:

maudlin sentimentality = 多愁善感的伤感
maudlin songs = 伤感的歌曲
maudlin poetry = 伤感的诗歌
maudlin behavior = 伤感的行为
become maudlin = 变得伤感
