mapping:/ˈmæpɪŋ/| n. |地图绘制；映射；对应；制图|The process of establishing a correspondence between two sets of data or objects.  It can refer to the creation of a map, a graphical representation of data, or a functional relationship between variables. |例句：The mapping of the human genome was a monumental achievement.  人类基因组图谱的绘制是一项了不起的成就。


例句：The software provides a visual mapping of the network's topology. 该软件提供了网络拓扑的视觉映射。


近义词：charting, representation, correspondence, correlation|反义词：misrepresentation, disconnect|词性：名词
