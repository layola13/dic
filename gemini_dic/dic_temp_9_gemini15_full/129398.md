nominalised:/ˈnɒmɪnəlaɪzd/| adj.| 名词化的；被名词化的 |  Converted into a noun or used as a noun.  The process of turning a verb, adjective, or other word class into a noun. | Example: The *nominalization* of "decide" into "decision" improves clarity.  名词化“决定”为“决定”提高了清晰度。|近义词：nouned, substantivized | 反义词：verbalized
