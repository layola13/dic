home-made:/ˌhoʊmˈmeɪd/ | adj. |自制的，家常的 |Made at home, not commercially produced.  It implies a personal touch and often suggests a simpler, more traditional method of preparation.|例句：She served us delicious home-made bread. 她给我们端上了美味的自制面包。

例句：This home-made cake is much better than store-bought ones. 这个家做的蛋糕比商店买的要好得多。

近义词：homemade, home-baked (for baked goods), handcrafted

反义词：store-bought, commercially made, factory-made

词性：形容词
