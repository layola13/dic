outdistances:/ˌaʊtˈdɪstənsɪz/| vt.|超越，超过，胜过|To go faster than and leave behind (someone or something).  It implies a significant lead.|The marathon runner outdistanced all his competitors.  这位马拉松运动员超越了所有竞争对手。

The new technology outdistances the old methods in efficiency.  这项新技术在效率上超越了旧方法。|outpace, surpass, exceed|fall behind, lag|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outdistances his rivals in the race. = 他在比赛中超越了他的对手。
The company outdistances its competitors in innovation. = 这家公司在创新方面超越了竞争对手。

一般过去时:
She outdistanced her opponent in the final sprint. = 她在最后的冲刺中超过了她的对手。
The team outdistanced the other teams by a large margin. = 该队以很大的优势超过了其他队伍。

现在完成时:
Our sales team has outdistanced all others this quarter. = 我们销售团队本季度业绩超越了所有其他团队。
The athlete has outdistanced all expectations with his performance. = 运动员的表现超出了所有人的预期。

过去完成时:
By the end of the race, he had outdistanced all the other runners. = 到比赛结束时，他已经超过了所有其他跑步者。
The company had outdistanced its rivals in market share by the time the merger happened. = 在合并发生时，该公司已经在市场份额上超过了竞争对手。

将来时:
She will outdistance her rivals if she continues to train hard. = 如果她继续努力训练，她将超越她的对手。
The company expects to outdistance its competitors in the next financial year. = 公司预计在下一个财政年度将超越其竞争对手。

虚拟语气:
If she had trained harder, she would have outdistanced her competitors. = 如果她训练更努力，她就会超越她的竞争对手。
I wish I could outdistance my anxieties. = 我希望我能克服我的焦虑。

被动语态:
He was outdistanced in the final meters of the race. = 他在比赛最后几米被超越了。
The old technology was quickly outdistanced by the new model. = 旧技术很快就被新模型超越了。

疑问句:

Did she outdistance her opponents in the last election? = 她在上届选举中超过了对手吗？

否定句:
