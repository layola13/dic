midge:/mɪdʒ/| n. |小蚊子，小蠓虫 | A small biting fly, especially one of the family Chironomidae.  Midges are often found in swarms near water. | Example:  The air was thick with midges at dusk.  傍晚时分，空气中充满了小蠓虫。  The lake was teeming with midges. 湖里挤满了小蠓虫。|近义词：gnat, mosquito (in some contexts)|反义词：None.
