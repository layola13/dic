sippingly:/ˈsɪpɪŋli/| adv.| 细细地啜饮着；慢慢地；小口地 |  In a sipping manner; slowly and delicately, as if sipping a drink.  | 例句：She sipped her tea sippingly, enjoying the warmth. 她细细地啜着茶，享受着温暖。

例句：He looked at the sunset sippingly, lost in thought. 他慢慢地望着日落，陷入沉思。


近义词：slowly, gradually, delicately, leisurely


反义词：quickly, rapidly, hastily, gulpingly


词性：副词
