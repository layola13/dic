frock:/frɒk/| /frɑːk/| n.|女装，连衣裙；工作服|A frock is a dress, typically one worn by a woman or girl.  It can also refer to a kind of working clothes.|例句：She wore a pretty floral frock to the party. 她穿着一件漂亮的花裙子去参加派对。

例句：The painter wore a paint-splattered frock while working. 画家工作时穿着溅满油漆的工作服。|近义词：dress, gown, garment|反义词：trousers, suit(depending on context)
