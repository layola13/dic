reorientated:/ˌriːˈɔːriənˌteɪtɪd/ | v. |重新定向，重新定位，使改观 |To change the direction or focus of something; to adjust or realign something to a new direction or purpose.  |The company reorientated its marketing strategy to target a younger demographic. 公司重新调整了其营销策略，以目标更年轻的人群。  The map helped reorientate him after he got lost. 地图帮助他在迷路后重新定位方向。 |realign, refocus, redirect |misorient, disorient|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reorientate their business plans annually. = 他们每年都会重新调整他们的商业计划。
The teacher reorientates the students' understanding of the topic. = 老师重新调整了学生对这个话题的理解。


一般过去时:
The pilot reorientated the aircraft after encountering strong winds. = 飞行员在遇到强风后重新调整了飞机的方向。
She reorientated her life after the divorce. = 离婚后，她重新调整了自己的生活。


现在完成时:
The government has reorientated its economic policies. = 政府已经重新调整了其经济政策。
He has reorientated his career goals. = 他已经重新调整了他的职业目标。


过去完成时:
They had reorientated their research priorities before the funding cut. = 在经费削减之前，他们已经重新调整了他们的研究重点。
She had reorientated her thinking before making the decision. = 在做出决定之前，她已经重新调整了自己的想法。


将来时:
The company will reorientate its production line next year. = 公司明年将重新调整其生产线。
We will reorientate our marketing efforts towards online platforms. = 我们将把我们的营销工作重新转向在线平台。


虚拟语气:
If they reorientated their approach, they might succeed. = 如果他们调整了方法，他们可能会成功。
I wish they had reorientated their strategy earlier. = 我希望他们早点调整策略。


被动语态:
The marketing strategy was reorientated to focus on social media. = 营销策略被重新调整以关注社交媒体。
His understanding of the situation was reorientated by the new evidence. = 他对局势的理解被新的证据所改变。


疑问句:
Has the company reorientated its goals? = 公司是否重新调整了其目标？
Will they reorientate their plans after the feedback? = 他们会在收到反馈后重新调整计划吗？


否定句:
They did not reorientate their business model. = 他们没有重新调整他们的商业模式。
The project was not reorientated despite the challenges. = 尽管面临挑战，项目也没有重新调整方向。
