chipolata:/ˌtʃɪpəˈləʊtə/| n.|小香肠|A small, thin, usually mild-flavored sausage, often made of pork.  Chipolatas are typically grilled, fried, or added to other dishes.|例句：We grilled some chipolatas for the barbecue. 我们烤了一些小香肠做烧烤。

例句：The chipolatas were served with roasted vegetables. 小香肠配烤蔬菜一起上桌。|近义词：sausage, frankfurter, little sausage|反义词：无
