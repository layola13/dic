slackness:/ˈslæk.nəs/ | n. |松懈；懈怠；疏忽；懒散 |Lack of effort, energy, or diligence; carelessness.  It can also refer to a looseness or lack of tension.|例句：The project's failure was due to the team's slackness.  项目的失败是由于团队的懈怠造成的。

The company's slackness in safety procedures led to a serious accident.  公司在安全程序上的疏忽导致了一起严重的事故。|近义词：negligence, carelessness, indolence, lethargy, laxity |反义词：diligence, diligence, assiduity, alertness,  perseverance

词源:
The word "slackness" originates from the Old English word "slaec," meaning "slow," "loose," or "weak."  This evolved through Middle English "slake" to its modern form.  The sense of "laxness" or "carelessness" developed later.
