sleeve:/sliːv/| n. |袖子，套管|A sleeve is the part of a garment that covers the arm.  It can also refer to a protective covering for something else.|例句：My shirt has long sleeves. 我的衬衫有长袖。

The sleeves of her dress were too short.  她的裙子袖子太短了。|近义词：covering, casing |反义词:body(of garment)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shirt has long sleeves. = 这件衬衫有长袖。
She wears short sleeves in summer. = 夏天她穿短袖。

一般过去时:
He rolled up his sleeves. = 他卷起了袖子。
The tailor sewed the sleeves onto the dress. = 裁缝把袖子缝在了裙子上。

现在完成时:
I have mended the sleeve of my coat. = 我已经缝补好了我的外套袖子。
She has worn that dress with torn sleeves for weeks. = 她已经穿了那件袖子破了的裙子好几个星期了。

过去完成时:
He had already removed his sleeves before the fight began. = 战斗开始前他已经脱掉了袖子。
The sleeves had been carefully embroidered before the dress was finished. = 在裙子完成之前，袖子已经被仔细地绣上了。

将来时:
I will replace the sleeves on my jacket. = 我将更换我夹克的袖子。
She will have her sleeves shortened. = 她会把袖子改短。

虚拟语气:
If the sleeves were longer, it would fit better. = 如果袖子更长，它会更合身。
I wish the sleeves were less tight. = 我希望袖子不要那么紧。

被动语态:
The sleeves were carefully stitched by the seamstress. = 袖子是由女裁缝仔细缝制的。
The sleeves are being repaired at the moment. = 袖子正在修理中。

疑问句:
Are the sleeves removable? = 袖子可以拆卸吗？
What colour are the sleeves? = 袖子是什么颜色？

否定句:
The jacket doesn't have sleeves. = 这件夹克没有袖子。
Her dress didn't have long sleeves. = 她的裙子没有长袖。
