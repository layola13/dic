reptilia:/repˈtɪliə/| n. |爬行动物|Reptilia is a class of cold-blooded vertebrates, including snakes, lizards, turtles, crocodiles, and extinct species like dinosaurs. They are characterized by scales or scutes, laying eggs (oviparity), and having three-chambered hearts (except for crocodiles which have four).|The study of reptilia is a fascinating field of zoology. |近义词：scaly creatures, reptiles|反义词：mammals, birds, amphibians
