preprovide:/ˌpriːprəˈvaɪd/ | vt. |预先提供，预先供应 | To provide something in advance or beforehand.  This is a relatively uncommon word, often used in formal or technical contexts. It emphasizes the act of providing something *before* it is needed or requested. | Example:  The company preprovided all the necessary equipment for the project.  公司为该项目预先提供所有必要的设备。 |近义词:  provide beforehand, supply in advance, prearrange |反义词:  postpone, delay
