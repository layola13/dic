premeasure: /ˈpriːˌmɛʒər/ | n. | 预先测量；预测试 | A preliminary measurement or test, often conducted to assess the feasibility or effectiveness of a subsequent, larger-scale study or experiment.  It helps to refine the methodology and reduce potential errors. |  The premeasure of student knowledge revealed a significant gap in understanding core concepts.  预先对学生知识的测量揭示了对核心概念理解上的显著差距。  The researchers conducted a premeasure to evaluate the effectiveness of their new drug. 研究人员进行了预先测量以评估他们新药的有效性。 | pilot study, preliminary test, trial run |  post-measurement |  n.
