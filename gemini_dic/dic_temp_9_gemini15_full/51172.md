desquamation:/ˌdɛskwəˈmeɪʃən/| n. |脱屑；脱皮；鳞屑形成|The shedding of epithelial cells from a surface.  This process is a normal part of skin cell turnover, but it can also be a sign of disease. |Example:  The psoriasis caused significant desquamation on his scalp. 牛皮癣导致他的头皮严重脱屑。  The patient presented with excessive desquamation of the skin. 病人出现皮肤过度脱屑。|近义词：exfoliation, scaling, shedding |反义词：retention, adhesion
