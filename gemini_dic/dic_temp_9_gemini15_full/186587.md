ridiculing: /rɪˈdɪkjʊlɪŋ/ | v. | 嘲笑；戏弄 |  To make fun of someone or something in a cruel or unkind way; to mock. |

例句：

He was ridiculed for his unusual clothes. 他因为穿着奇特而遭到嘲笑。

The children ridiculed the clumsy clown. 孩子们嘲笑笨拙的小丑。


近义词：mocking, scoffing, jeering, deriding, making fun of

反义词：praising, complimenting, admiring


词源:

The word "ridiculing" comes from the Middle English word "ridiculen," which itself derives from the Old French word "ridiculer."  The ultimate origin is the Latin word "ridiculus," meaning "laughable" or "absurd."
