exhaustively:/ɪɡˈzɔːstɪvli/ | adv. |彻底地；详尽地；穷尽地 | Thoroughly, completely, and in detail, leaving nothing out.  It implies a comprehensive and detailed examination of a subject or topic. |例句：The police investigated the case exhaustively. 警方彻底调查了此案。

The committee exhaustively reviewed all the proposals before making a decision. 委员会在做出决定之前详尽审查了所有提案。


近义词：thoroughly, completely, comprehensively, fully, in detail | 反义词：superficially, partially, incompletely
