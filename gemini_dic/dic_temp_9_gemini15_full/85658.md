haustorium:/ɔːˈstɔːriəm/| n. |吸器，吸收器|A haustorium is a specialized structure in certain plants and fungi that penetrates the tissues of a host organism to absorb nutrients.  It's essentially a modified root or hypha used for parasitic or symbiotic nutrition.|Example: The parasitic plant's haustorium pierced the host's stem, drawing out vital nutrients.  吸附植物的吸器穿透了寄主的茎，吸取了重要的营养物质。|近义词：sucker (in some contexts),  absorptive organ|反义词：none specifically, but words like "producer" or "photosynthetic organ"  imply the opposite function.
