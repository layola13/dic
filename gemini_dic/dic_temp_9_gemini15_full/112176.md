looking-on: /ˈlʊkɪŋ ɒn/ | v. |旁观；观看；袖手旁观 | Observing something without participating.  |例句：He stood looking on as the children played. 他站在那里看着孩子们玩耍。


例句：They just stood looking on while the building burned down.  他们只是站在那里看着建筑物烧毁。


近义词：observing, watching, witnessing | 反义词：participating, intervening
