overspent:/ˌoʊvərˈspent/| v. |超支；花费过多 |To spend more money than one has budgeted for or than one can afford. |例句：We overspent on our vacation and now we're in debt. 我们度假超支了，现在负债了。|例句：She overspent her monthly allowance on clothes. 她把这个月的零花钱都花在了衣服上。|近义词：exceed, squander, lavish |反义词：underspend, economize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overspend every month. = 他们每个月都超支。
We overspend on eating out. = 我们在外出就餐上花费过多。

一般过去时:
I overspent on Christmas gifts last year. = 我去年在圣诞礼物上超支了。
They overspent their budget for the project. = 他们超出了项目的预算。

现在完成时:
We have overspent this year. = 我们今年超支了。
She has overspent on her credit card. = 她的信用卡超支了。

过去完成时:
They had overspent before they realized it. = 他们在意识到之前就已经超支了。
We had overspent on the house renovations. = 我们在房屋装修上超支了。

将来时:
I think we will overspend on our wedding. = 我想我们会婚礼上超支。
They will overspend if they don't budget properly. = 如果他们不做好预算，他们将会超支。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't overspent, I wouldn't be in debt. = 如果我没有超支，我就不会负债了。
I wish I hadn't overspent on that trip. = 我真希望我没有在那次旅行中超支。

被动语态:
The budget was overspent by the team. = 预算被团队超支了。
The funds were overspent on unnecessary items. = 资金被花在了不必要的东西上。

疑问句:
Did we overspend on the holiday? = 我们假期超支了吗？
Have they overspent on their marketing campaign? = 他们的营销活动超支了吗？

否定句:
We didn't overspend this month. = 这个月我们没有超支。
They haven't overspent yet. = 他们还没有超支。
