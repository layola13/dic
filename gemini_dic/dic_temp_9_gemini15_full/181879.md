reluctate:/rɪˈlʌktənt/| v.| 勉强，不愿，抗拒 | To be unwilling or hesitant; to show reluctance.  |例句：He reluctantly agreed to help. 他勉强同意帮忙。|例句：She reluctantly gave up her dream. 她不情愿地放弃了她的梦想。|近义词：hesitate, resist, object, demur |反义词：eager, willing, enthusiastic
