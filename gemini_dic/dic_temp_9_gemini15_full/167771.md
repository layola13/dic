primate:/ˈpraɪmeɪt/| n. |灵长类动物|A primate is a mammal of the order Primates, which includes lemurs, monkeys, apes, and humans.  Primates are characterized by their relatively large brains, forward-facing eyes, and grasping hands and feet. | Example: Humans are primates. 人类是灵长类动物。  Example:  Many primate species are endangered. 许多灵长类物种濒临灭绝。|近义词：mammal, ape (for some primates)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with other mammal orders.
