raphides:/ˈræfɪdiz/ | n. |针状结晶体；针晶束| Bundles of needle-shaped crystals of calcium oxalate found in the cells of many plants.  These crystals can cause irritation if ingested. | Example:  Many plants, particularly in the Araceae family, contain raphides which can cause mouth irritation if consumed.  许多植物，特别是天南星科植物，含有会引起口腔刺激的针状结晶体。|近义词：crystals (general term), spicules |反义词：无
