circulars:/ˈsɜːrkjələrz/| n. |通告；传单；循环物 |Printed or electronic notices that are sent to many people.  Circulars are often used to advertise products, services, or events.  They can also be used to communicate important information to a large group of people. |例句：The company sent out circulars announcing the new product launch. 公司发布通告宣布新产品发布。

The school distributed circulars informing parents about the upcoming field trip. 学校分发了通告，告知家长即将到来的实地考察。


近义词：notices, bulletins, pamphlets, flyers |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with personal communication or direct announcements. |名词
