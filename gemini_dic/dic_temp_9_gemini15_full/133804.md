nongerminations:/ˌnɒnˌdʒɜːrmɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. |  非萌发；不发芽 |  The lack of germination; the failure of seeds or spores to sprout. This term often refers to a collective absence of germination in a group of seeds or spores.  | Example: The poor soil conditions resulted in high rates of nongerminations.  土壤条件差导致大量种子未发芽。 |近义词: lack of germination, failure to germinate, non-sprouting | 反义词: germination, sprouting | 名词
