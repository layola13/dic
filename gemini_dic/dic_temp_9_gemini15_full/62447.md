endorsable: /ɪnˈdɔːrsəbl/ | adj. | 可背书的；可认可的 | Capable of being endorsed; worthy of approval or support.  |  The contract is endorsable by the company's CEO. 这份合同可以由公司首席执行官背书。The athlete's achievements are highly endorsable.这位运动员的成就非常值得认可。 |  approvable, acceptable, justifiable | unendorsable, unacceptable, objectionable | 形容词
