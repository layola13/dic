diversify:/daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪ/| vt. |使多样化；使多样化；发展多种经营|To make or become more diverse or varied.  The company is diversifying its product line to reduce its reliance on a single product. 公司正在使其产品线多样化，以减少对单一产品的依赖。|近义词：vary, diversify, differentiate|反义词：homogenize, standardize|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company diversifies its investments. = 公司使其投资多样化。
She diversifies her skills to enhance career prospects. = 她使自己的技能多样化以增强职业前景。


一般过去时:
They diversified their portfolio last year. = 他们去年使投资组合多样化。
He diversified his business into new markets. = 他将他的业务扩展到新的市场。


现在完成时:
The farmer has diversified his crops this season. = 农民本季使他的农作物多样化。
We have diversified our sources of income. = 我们已经使我们的收入来源多样化。


过去完成时:
The company had diversified its operations before the economic downturn. = 在经济衰退之前，该公司已经使运营多样化。
She had diversified her skills before applying for the job. = 在申请工作之前，她已经使自己的技能多样化。


将来时:
We will diversify our marketing strategies next year. = 我们明年将使我们的营销策略多样化。
The government will diversify the economy to reduce dependence on oil. = 政府将使经济多样化以减少对石油的依赖。


虚拟语气:
If the company diversified its products, it would be less vulnerable to market fluctuations. = 如果公司使产品多样化，它将不太容易受到市场波动的影响。
I wish I had diversified my investments earlier. = 我希望我早点使我的投资多样化。


被动语态:
The company's portfolio was diversified by a professional advisor. = 公司的投资组合由专业顾问进行了多样化处理。
The economy needs to be diversified to ensure stability. = 为了确保稳定，经济需要多样化。


疑问句:

Will they diversify their product line? = 他们会使他们的产品线多样化吗？


否定句:
The company did not diversify its investments. = 公司没有使其投资多样化。
They haven't diversified their sources of funding yet. = 他们还没有使他们的资金来源多样化。
