self-doubts:/ˈsɛlf daʊts/| n.|自我怀疑，缺乏自信|Feelings of uncertainty or lack of confidence in one's own abilities, judgment, or worth.  These doubts can range from mild insecurities to crippling anxieties that significantly impact one's actions and decisions.|例句：Her self-doubts prevented her from applying for the promotion.  她的自我怀疑阻止了她申请晋升。

例句：Overcoming his self-doubts was a crucial step in his recovery. 克服他的自我怀疑是他康复的关键一步。|近义词：insecurities, uncertainties, anxieties, misgivings, lack of confidence|反义词：self-confidence, self-assurance, self-belief|名词
