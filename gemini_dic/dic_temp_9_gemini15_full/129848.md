non-ignorant:/ˌnɒnˈɪɡnərənt/| adj. |非无知的；有知识的；见多识广的 |Not ignorant; knowledgeable; well-informed.  This word emphasizes the possession of knowledge and understanding, contrasting with ignorance. It suggests a level of awareness and comprehension beyond simple lack of ignorance. |The non-ignorant professor easily answered the student's complex question.  这位见多识广的教授轻松地回答了学生复杂的问题。|knowledgeable, informed, educated, intelligent|ignorant, uneducated, uninformed, unintelligent
