devilhead: /ˈdɛvəlˌhɛd/ | n. | 魔鬼头，恶魔头，邪恶的人 | A devilhead is a term used to describe someone who is extremely evil, wicked, or malicious.  It is a less common and more informal term than "devil" or "demon."  The word combines "devil," referring to an evil supernatural being, with "head," suggesting the person is the leader or embodiment of evil. | Example: "He was a devilhead, scheming and plotting against everyone."  | 近义词：evil person, fiend, demon, villain, scoundrel | 反义词：saint, angel, good person, benefactor |  词性：名词
