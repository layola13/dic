millenarians:/ˌmɪlənˈeəriənz/| n. |千禧一代；相信千年论者| Believers in a millennium, or a thousand-year reign of Christ, as prophesied in the Book of Revelation; also, people born in the years around the turn of the millennium (roughly 1981-1996).  |例句：Some millenarians interpret the current events as signs of the approaching apocalypse.  一些相信千年论者将当前事件解释为末日临近的预兆。|例句：Millennials are known for their tech-savviness and entrepreneurial spirit. 千禧一代以其精通技术和创业精神而闻名。|近义词： millennial (for the generational sense), chiliasts |反义词：  None directly;  anti-millenarian (opposing millenarian beliefs) | 名词
