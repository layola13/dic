reenunciating:/ˌriːɪˈnʌnsieɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 重申；重新声明 |  The act of stating something again, often with more emphasis or clarity.  It implies a previous statement that needs clarification or reinforcement. | Example: The judge ordered the witness to reenunciate his testimony, as it was unclear. = 法官命令证人重述他的证词，因为证词不清楚。  The professor reenunciated his main points at the end of the lecture. = 教授在讲座结束时重述了他的要点。| 近义词:restating, reiterating, rephrasing | 反义词:withdrawing, retracting | 动词
