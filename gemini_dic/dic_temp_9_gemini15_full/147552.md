overpenalize: /ˌoʊvərˈpiːnəlaɪz/ | vt. | 过度惩罚 | To punish someone or something more severely than is necessary or fair.  |例句：The judge was accused of overpenalizing the defendant. = 法官被指控过度惩罚被告。

例句：He feels the company overpenalized him for a minor mistake. = 他觉得公司对他一个小错误处罚过重。


近义词：overpunish, excessively punish, unduly punish | 反义词：underpenalize, leniently punish

词性: 动词
