funy:/ˈfʌni/| adj.|滑稽的，好笑的，有趣的|Funny refers to something that causes amusement or laughter. It can describe a person, event, or situation.|例句：That joke was really funny.  那个笑话真搞笑。|例句：He has a funny way of walking. 他走路的样子很有趣。|近义词：humorous, comical, amusing, witty|反义词：serious, dull, boring, uninteresting
