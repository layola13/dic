freights:/freɪts/ | n. | 货运；运费；货物 | The cost of transporting goods; goods carried by a means of transportation.  |例句：The freights were delayed due to the storm.  暴风雨导致货物延误。

例句：The company charges high freights for international shipping. 这家公司对国际航运收取高昂的运费。


近义词：cargo, shipment, transportation costs | 反义词：N/A


词源:  From Old French *freit, from Vulgar Latin *fractum, past participle of frangere "to break," referring to the breaking down of large amounts of goods for transport.  The modern sense evolved from the earlier meaning of "freight" as "burden, load."
