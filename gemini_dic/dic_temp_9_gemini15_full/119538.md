microseconds:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌsɛkəndz/ | n. |微秒 |A microsecond is one millionth of a second.  It's a very small unit of time used in measuring extremely fast processes, like computer operations or electrical signals. |Example: The computer chip processed the data in just a few microseconds. 这块电脑芯片在短短几微秒内就处理了数据。|近义词：milliseconds (毫秒) |反义词:  millennia (千年)
