pepino:/pɛˈpinoʊ/| n. |小黄瓜，甜瓜|A small, sweet melon with a pale green skin and white flesh, often eaten raw.  It is also sometimes referred to as a "melon pear" due to its shape and texture. |例句：I bought a couple of pepinos at the farmers market. 我在农贸市场买了几个小黄瓜。

例句：The salad was topped with thinly sliced pepinos.沙拉上面放了薄薄的切片小黄瓜。


近义词：melon,  cucumis (less common) |反义词：None directly applicable.  Perhaps large melon or bitter melon could be considered indirect opposites. |词性：名词
