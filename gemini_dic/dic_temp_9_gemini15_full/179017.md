redeleting: /ˌriːdɪˈliːtɪŋ/ | v. | 重新删除；再次删除 |  The act of deleting something again, often after a previous deletion attempt.  This typically implies a mistake was made in the first deletion, or that further removal of information is needed. |


例句：

The administrator is redeleting the corrupted files to ensure complete removal. 管理员正在重新删除损坏的文件以确保完全删除。

After realizing he’d deleted the wrong document, he had to redeleting the correct one.  在他意识到自己删除了错误的文档后，他不得不重新删除正确的文档。


近义词：re-erasing, removing again, deleting anew


反义词：restoring, recovering, reinstalling


词性：动词
