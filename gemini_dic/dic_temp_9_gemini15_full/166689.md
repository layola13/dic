presettlement:/ˌpriːˈsɛtlmənt/| adj. n.| 殖民前的；定居前的；预先解决；预先协议| Relating to or existing before the settlement of a region or area, especially by Europeans;  a pre-arranged settlement or agreement. |例句：The presettlement landscape of the region was vastly different from what we see today.  该地区的殖民前景观与我们今天看到的截然不同。  The parties reached a presettlement agreement to avoid lengthy litigation. 各方达成预先和解协议以避免冗长的诉讼。|近义词：antecedent, prior, precolonial|反义词：postsettlement, subsequent
