cephalon:/ˈsɛfəlɒn/| n. |头，头部；脑 |The cephalon is the head region of an invertebrate, especially an arthropod. Cephalon refers to the head of an invertebrate, such as an arthropod.  It encompasses the brain and sensory organs. |Example: The trilobite's cephalon was heavily armored.|近义词：head, head region |反义词：caudal region (tail)
