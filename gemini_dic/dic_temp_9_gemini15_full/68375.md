falling-stars: /ˈfɔːlɪŋ stɑːrz/ | n. |流星，陨星 |  Refers to meteors, which are small pieces of rock or dust that burn up as they enter the Earth's atmosphere, creating a streak of light.  The term is often used poetically or figuratively. | Example: We lay on the grass and watched the falling stars. 我们躺在草地上看流星。  The falling stars symbolized hope for a brighter future. 流星象征着对更美好未来的希望。 | 近义词：meteors, shooting stars | 反义词：N/A
