skipping:/ˈskɪpɪŋ/ | v. n. |跳跃；略过；遗漏 |To move lightly and quickly, with leaps and bounds; to omit or leave out. |例句：The children were skipping happily down the street.孩子们高兴地沿着街道跳跃着。She skipped breakfast this morning.她今天早上没吃早餐。|近义词：hopping, leaping, omitting, missing |反义词：walking steadily, including, considering |动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He skips rope every morning. = 他每天早上跳绳。
She skips the introduction and goes straight to the main point. = 她跳过引言，直接进入主题。

一般过去时:
The rabbit skipped across the field. = 兔子跳过田野。
He skipped a few chapters in the book. = 他跳过了书中的一些章节。

现在完成时:
I have skipped lunch several times this week. = 我本周已经几次没吃午饭了。
She has skipped several meetings due to illness. = 由于生病，她缺席了几次会议。

过去完成时:
He had skipped school that day. = 那天他逃学了。
They had skipped several steps in the process, leading to errors. = 他们在过程中跳过了几个步骤，导致了错误。

将来时:
I will skip the dessert tonight. = 今晚我不吃甜点了。
They will skip the next meeting if the problem is solved. = 如果问题解决了，他们将跳过下一次会议。

虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would skip the meeting. = 如果我有时间，我会跳过会议。
I wish I could skip the boring lecture. = 我真希望我能跳过这无聊的讲座。

被动语态:
The meeting was skipped by several important members. = 几位重要成员缺席了会议。
Several steps in the process were skipped, causing problems. = 过程中跳过了几个步骤，导致了问题。

疑问句:
Are you skipping dinner tonight? = 你今晚不吃晚饭吗？
Did she skip her classes yesterday? = 她昨天逃课了吗？

否定句:
I didn't skip any steps in the recipe. = 我在食谱中没有跳过任何步骤。
He does not skip his daily exercise routine. = 他没有跳过他的日常锻炼。
