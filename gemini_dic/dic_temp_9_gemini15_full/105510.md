kilograms:/ˈkɪləˌɡræmz/| n.|千克，公斤|A kilogram is a unit of mass in the metric system, equal to 1000 grams.|例句：The bag weighs five kilograms. 这个袋子重五公斤。|例句：The package is too heavy; it weighs over 20 kilograms.  这个包裹太重了；它超过20公斤。|近义词：kilos|反义词：None.
