cleans:/klɛnz/ | vt. | 清洁；使干净 | To make something clean.  | 例句：She cleans the house every Saturday. = 她每个星期六都打扫房子。
She cleans her teeth twice a day. = 她每天刷两次牙。| 近义词：purify, sanitize | 反义词：dirty, soil | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cleans his room every day. = 他每天打扫他的房间。
She cleans the dishes after dinner. = 她晚饭后洗碗。


一般过去时:
I cleaned the car yesterday. = 我昨天洗了车。
They cleaned the windows last week. = 他们上周擦了窗户。


现在完成时:
I have cleaned the kitchen. = 我已经打扫干净了厨房。
She has cleaned all the dishes. = 她已经洗完了所有的碗碟。


过去完成时:
He had cleaned the house before his guests arrived. = 在客人到达之前，他已经打扫干净了房子。
They had cleaned the pool before the party started. = 在派对开始之前，他们已经打扫干净了游泳池。


将来时:
I will clean the bathroom tomorrow. = 我明天将打扫浴室。
She will clean the apartment after work. = 她下班后将打扫公寓。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would clean the garage. = 如果我有时间，我会打扫车库。
I wish I had cleaned the house earlier. = 我希望我早点打扫了房子。


被动语态:
The house is cleaned every week. = 这房子每周都打扫。
The dishes are cleaned by the dishwasher. = 碗碟是用洗碗机洗的。


疑问句:
Do you clean your room regularly? = 你定期打扫你的房间吗？
Does he clean the car himself? = 他自己洗车吗？


否定句:
I didn't clean the floor today. = 我今天没有擦地板。
They don't clean their apartment often. = 他们不经常打扫他们的公寓。


PHRASE->
clean up = 打扫干净
clean out = 清理干净
clean off = 擦掉
clean and tidy = 干净整洁
spot clean = 局部清洁

词源:
The word "clean" comes from the Old English word "clæne," meaning "pure," "free from dirt," or "holy."  This word has Germanic roots, related to words in other Germanic languages with similar meanings.  The verb form developed naturally from the adjective.
