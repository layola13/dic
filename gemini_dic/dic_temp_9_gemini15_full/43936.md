crewneck:/ˈkruːnek/| adj. | 圆领的 | A crewneck is a type of neckline on a shirt, sweater, or other garment that is round and close-fitting around the neck.  It is a simple, classic style. | Example:  She wore a simple crewneck sweater. 她穿着一件简单的圆领毛衣。  He prefers crewneck t-shirts to V-necks. 他更喜欢圆领T恤而不是V领T恤。 | 近义词：round neck, boat neck (loosely) | 反义词：V-neck, turtleneck, scoop neck
