rudely:/ˈruːdli/| adv.|粗鲁地，无礼地 |In a rude manner; impolitely.  |例句：He spoke rudely to the waiter. 他粗鲁地跟服务员说话。|例句：She rudely interrupted the conversation. 她粗鲁地打断了谈话。|近义词：abruptly, discourteously, impolitely|反义词：politely, respectfully, courteously


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and therefore doesn't have tense conjugations like verbs.  It modifies verbs, describing how an action is performed.


PHRASE->
act rudely = 行为粗鲁
speak rudely = 粗鲁地说
behave rudely = 行为粗鲁
treat rudely = 粗鲁对待
look rudely = 粗鲁地看
