continuous:/kənˈtɪnjuəs/ | adj. | 连续的；持续的 |  Uninterrupted in time or sequence; ongoing.  | Example: The continuous rain caused flooding. 持续的雨导致了洪水。  |近义词: uninterrupted, incessant, constant, sustained | 反义词: intermittent, sporadic, discontinuous |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine runs continuously. = 机器连续运转。
She continuously improves her skills. = 她不断提高自己的技能。


一般过去时:
The rain continued continuously throughout the night. = 雨整夜持续不断地下着。
He continuously complained about the noise. = 他不断抱怨噪音。


现在完成时:
It has been raining continuously for three days. = 雨已经持续下了三天了。
She has continuously supported the charity. = 她一直持续地支持该慈善机构。


过去完成时:
The engine had been running continuously for hours before it failed. = 引擎连续运转了几个小时才发生故障。
They had continuously worked on the project for months. = 几个月来他们一直在持续地从事这个项目。


将来时:
The project will continue continuously for another year. = 该项目将持续进行一年。
He will continuously update his blog. = 他将持续更新他的博客。


虚拟语气:
If the system were continuous, it would be more efficient. = 如果系统是连续的，它会更高效。
I wish the music were continuous instead of stopping and starting. = 我希望音乐是连续播放的，而不是断断续续的。


被动语态:
The process is continuously monitored. = 这个过程正在持续监控中。
The data is continuously updated. = 数据正在持续更新。


疑问句:
Is the signal continuous? = 信号是连续的吗？
Will the supply be continuous? = 供应会持续吗？


否定句:
The supply of electricity was not continuous. = 电力供应不连续。
The noise was not continuous; it came in bursts. = 噪音不是连续的；它是间歇性的。


PHRASE->

continuous learning 持续学习
