open-top:/ˈoʊpən tɑːp/|/ˈəʊpən tɒp/| adj.|敞篷的，开顶的|Having no roof or a removable roof.  This adjective describes something, typically a vehicle, that has a design where the top can be opened or is not present.|例句：He drives an open-top sports car. 他开着一辆敞篷跑车。

例句：The open-top bus gave us a great view of the city.  这辆敞篷巴士让我们欣赏到了城市的美景。


近义词：convertible, uncovered, roofless
反义词：closed-top, hardtop, roofed

词性：形容词
