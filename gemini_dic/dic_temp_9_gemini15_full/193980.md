It implies taking responsibility for the absence without formal permission.|例句：He was self-excused from the meeting due to a prior commitment. 他因为有之前的承诺而自行请假没有参加会议。  She submitted a self-excused absence form, explaining her reason. 她提交了一份自行请假申请表，解释了她的理由。|近义词：unauthorised absence, absence without leave (AWOL) |反义词：officially excused, approved absence|形容词
