leechcraft:/ˈliːtʃkræft/| n. |水蛭疗法；巫术；邪恶的魔法|The practice of using leeches for medicinal purposes, or, in a more archaic sense, any form of witchcraft or harmful magic.  It often implies a dark or sinister practice.|例句：The old woman was accused of leechcraft and banished from the village.  这位老妇人被指控使用巫术，并被逐出村庄。|例句：While leechcraft as a medical practice is largely outdated, its historical significance remains. 尽管水蛭疗法作为一种医疗手段已经过时，但它的历史意义依然存在。|近义词：black magic, sorcery, witchcraft, necromancy|反义词：healing, medicine (in the modern sense)
