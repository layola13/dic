reconviction:/ˌriːkənˈvɪkʃən/| n. |再定罪，再次判罪 |The act of convicting someone again, usually for a similar crime after a previous conviction.  This often implies a repeat offense or a failure to comply with previous sentencing conditions. |例句：His reconviction for drunk driving resulted in a longer prison sentence.他的酒驾再次定罪导致了更长的监禁时间。

例句：The judge considered the defendant's reconviction a serious breach of parole.  法官认为被告的再次定罪是对假释的严重违反。


近义词：repeated conviction, second conviction, subsequent conviction |反义词：acquittal, exoneration |词性:名词
