cochineal:/ˈkɒtʃɪniːl/| n.|胭脂红；胭脂虫；胭脂红染料|A red dye produced from the dried bodies of female cochineal insects.  It's a natural food coloring. |Example: The cake was dyed a vibrant red using cochineal extract.  这款蛋糕用胭脂红提取物染成了鲜艳的红色。|近义词：carmine, crimson|反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting pale colors like "pale," "white," or "beige" could be considered opposites in the context of color.|名词
