outpolitick: /ˌaʊtˈpɒlətɪk/ | vt. |  胜过…的策略；在策略上超过 | To outmaneuver or outsmart someone in political strategy or maneuvering.  |例句：The experienced politician outpoliticked his younger opponent.  这位经验丰富的政治家在策略上胜过了他的年轻对手。

The seasoned diplomat outpoliticked her adversaries, securing a favorable agreement.  这位经验丰富的外交官在策略上胜过了她的对手，达成了有利的协议。


近义词：outsmart, outmaneuver, outwit, outplay

反义词：be outmaneuvered, be outplayed, be outsmarted


词性：动词

词源:  The word is a combination of "out-" (meaning to surpass or exceed) and "politick" (relating to politics or political strategy).  "Politick" itself comes from "politics," ultimately derived from the Greek word "politikos," meaning "of or relating to citizens."
