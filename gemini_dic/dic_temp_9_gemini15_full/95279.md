incapacity:/ˌɪnkəˈpæsɪti/| n. |无能力；无力；不胜任|Lack of ability, power, or capacity.  It can refer to physical, mental, or legal inability.|例句：His incapacity to cope with stress led to a breakdown.他的应对压力的无能导致了崩溃。

例句：The company's incapacity to innovate resulted in its decline.  公司的缺乏创新能力导致了它的衰落。


近义词：disability, inability, incompetence, powerlessness|反义词：ability, capacity, competence
