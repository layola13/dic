deep-fry:/diːp fraɪ/ | vt. |油炸，油煎 | To cook (food) by immersing it in hot fat or oil.  |例句：I deep-fried the chicken until it was golden brown.我把鸡肉油炸至金黄色。|例句：We deep-fried the potatoes for our dinner. 我们把土豆油炸着当晚餐。|近义词：fry, sauté (if partially submerged) |反义词：boil, steam, bake


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They deep-fry the potatoes every day. = 他们每天都油炸土豆。
We deep-fry our fish in peanut oil. = 我们用花生油油炸鱼。
