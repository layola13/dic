corresponding:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɒndɪŋ/|/ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndɪŋ/| adj. |对应的；相应的；一致的 | Relating to or connected with something else.  It implies a close relationship or similarity between two things. | The corresponding figures for last year are much lower.  去年相应的数字要低得多。|  matching, equivalent, related | different, unrelated | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The corresponding chapters in both books cover similar topics. = 两本书中对应的章节涵盖了类似的主题。
His actions correspond to his words. = 他的行动与他的话语相符。


一般过去时:
The two events corresponded in time. = 这两件事在时间上是一致的。
The map corresponded closely to the actual terrain. = 这张地图与实际地形非常吻合。


现在完成时:
We have found corresponding evidence in several sources. = 我们已经在多个来源中找到了对应的证据。
The results correspond to our expectations. = 结果与我们的预期相符。


过去完成时:
The earlier findings had corresponded with the latest data. = 早期发现与最新数据相符。
The witness's statement had corresponded with the suspect's alibi. = 证人的证词与嫌疑人的不在场证明相符。


将来时:
The two events will correspond in time. = 这两件事将在时间上相符。
Future results will correspond to the current trend. = 未来的结果将与目前的趋势相符。


虚拟语气:
If the data corresponded to the model, the hypothesis would be supported. = 如果数据与模型相符，则假设将得到支持。
I wish the results corresponded more closely to the predictions. = 我希望结果更接近预测。


被动语态:
The figures were found to correspond with the initial estimates. = 发现这些数字与最初的估计相符。
The map is being updated to correspond more accurately with the latest survey data. = 地图正在更新，以更准确地与最新的测量数据相符。


疑问句:
Do the results correspond to the theory? = 结果与理论相符吗？
Does this action correspond to the company's policy? = 这一行为符合公司政策吗？


否定句:
The descriptions do not correspond to the reality. = 描述与现实不符。
His claims did not correspond with the facts. = 他的说法与事实不符。
