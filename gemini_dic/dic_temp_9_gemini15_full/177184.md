rearmouse: /ˈriːaʊs/ | n. |  蝙蝠 | A small nocturnal flying mammal belonging to the order Chiroptera, characterized by leathery wings formed from a membrane stretched between the elongated fingers and body.  Rearmouses are insectivores, feeding primarily on insects caught in flight.  They are known for their echolocation abilities, using sound waves to navigate and hunt in the dark. | The rearmouse flitted silently through the night sky. 这只蝙蝠悄无声息地飞过夜空。 |  bat, flying mammal |  (no direct antonym) | noun
