shaftman: /ˈʃæftmən/ | n. |矿井工人，井下工人 | A worker in a mine, especially one who works in a mine shaft.  | Example: The shaftman skillfully operated the mining equipment.  矿井工人熟练地操作采矿设备。 The shaftmen risked their lives daily to extract coal. 井下工人每天冒着生命危险采煤。|近义词：miner, collier, pitman |反义词：N/A
