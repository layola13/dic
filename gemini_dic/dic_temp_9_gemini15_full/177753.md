recast:/ˌriːˈkæst/| vt.|重铸；改写；重新塑造；重新分配角色|To shape or mold (something) again; to change the form or structure of something; to cast (an actor) in a different role.|例句：The sculptor recast the bronze statue.  雕塑家重铸了青铜雕像。


例句：The playwright recast the play, simplifying the plot. 剧作家改写了剧本，简化了情节。


近义词：reshape, reformulate, remake, refashion, retool


反义词：maintain, preserve, keep


词性:动词
