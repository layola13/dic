donate: /ˈdoʊneɪt/ | vt. |捐赠，捐献 | to give something, especially money, to a person or an organization that needs it  |例句：He donated a large sum of money to the charity. 他向慈善机构捐赠了一大笔钱。|近义词：contribute, give, offer |反义词：receive, take


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He donates blood regularly. = 他定期献血。
She donates to several charities. = 她向几家慈善机构捐款。

一般过去时:
They donated books to the school library. = 他们向学校图书馆捐赠了书籍。
We donated our old clothes to the homeless shelter. = 我们把旧衣服捐给了收容所。

现在完成时:
I have donated to this cause for many years. = 我多年来一直为这一事业捐款。
She has donated over $10,000 to the hospital. = 她已经向医院捐款超过一万美元。

过去完成时:
He had donated his entire savings before he died. = 他死前已经捐出了全部积蓄。
They had donated all their food to the victims of the hurricane. = 他们已经把所有的食物都捐给了飓风受害者。

将来时:
I will donate my old car to a charity. = 我将把我的旧车捐给慈善机构。
We will donate our time to volunteering at the soup kitchen. = 我们将把时间奉献给在施粥所做志愿者工作。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would donate to that organization. = 如果我有更多钱，我会捐给那个组织。
I wish I could donate more to help those in need. = 我希望我能捐更多钱来帮助那些需要帮助的人。

被动语态:
A large sum of money was donated to the research project. = 一大笔钱被捐赠给了该研究项目。
Many items are donated to the local museum each year. = 每年都有许多物品被捐赠给当地博物馆。

疑问句:
Will you donate to the fundraiser? = 你会捐款给筹款活动吗？
How much did they donate to the campaign? = 他们向竞选活动捐了多少钱？

否定句:
He did not donate to the political party. = 他没有向政党捐款。
She refused to donate any more money. = 她拒绝再捐钱了。


PHRASE->
donate blood = 献血
donate organs = 捐献器官
donate time = 捐献时间
make a donation = 捐款
charitable donation = 慈善捐款
