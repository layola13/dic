codevelop:/ˌkoʊdɪˈvɛləp/ | vt. |共同开发；合作开发 | To develop something jointly or in collaboration with others.  | Example:  The two companies decided to codevelop a new software application. 两家公司决定共同开发一个新的软件应用程序。

Example: The university and the private sector are codeveloping a new cancer treatment. 大学和私营部门正在共同开发一种新的癌症治疗方法。


近义词：collaborate on, jointly develop, co-create


反义词：develop independently, solely develop


词性:动词


USAGE->

We codevelop new products every year. = 我们每年都共同开发新产品。
They codevelop innovative solutions for their clients. = 他们为客户共同开发创新的解决方案。
