incompressibility: /ˌɪnkɒmprɛsəˈbɪləti/ | n. |不可压缩性|The property of a substance that resists compression; the inability to be compressed.|The high incompressibility of water makes it useful in hydraulic systems.水的不可压缩性使其在液压系统中非常有用。|近义词：rigidity, incompressibleness |反义词：compressibility


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense and voice are not applicable.  The usage examples will focus on different sentence structures and contexts.


Example Sentences:

Simple Sentence:  The incompressibility of the material is crucial for its application.  这种材料的不可压缩性对其应用至关重要。

Complex Sentence: Because of its high incompressibility, this fluid is ideal for use in high-pressure systems.  由于其高不可压缩性，这种流体非常适合用于高压系统。

Sentence with a prepositional phrase:  The experiment demonstrated the incompressibility of the liquid under extreme pressure.  实验证明了该液体在极端压力下的不可压缩性。

Sentence with an adjective clause:  The material, which exhibited remarkable incompressibility, was selected for the project.  表现出卓越不可压缩性的材料被选中用于该项目。

Sentence with a relative clause:  The incompressibility that the engineers sought was finally achieved. 工程师们寻求的不可压缩性最终实现了。
