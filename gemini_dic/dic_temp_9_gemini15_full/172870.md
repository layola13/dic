quaff:/kwɒf/| /kwɑːf/| vt.|痛饮，大口喝|To drink (a liquid) heartily or in large quantities.  |例句：He quaffed a large glass of wine. 他痛饮了一大杯葡萄酒。|例句：The thirsty traveler quaffed the cool water.  口渴的旅行者痛饮了凉水。|近义词：gulp, swig, guzzle|反义词：sip, savor
