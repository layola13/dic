investor:/ɪnˈvɛstər/ | n. |投资者，投资人 | A person or organization that invests money in something, especially a business.  | Example:  The investor made a significant profit from the startup.  这位投资者从这家初创公司获得了可观的利润。 |近义词：financier, shareholder, backer | 反义词：borrower


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The investor analyzes the market trends. = 投资者分析市场趋势。
She invests in renewable energy companies. = 她投资可再生能源公司。


一般过去时:
He invested heavily in the stock market last year. = 他去年大量投资股市。
The company attracted several investors. = 这家公司吸引了几位投资者。


现在完成时:
They have invested millions in research and development. = 他们已经投资数百万美元用于研发。
She has been an investor in this field for over a decade. = 她在这个领域已经投资超过十年了。


过去完成时:
Before the project started, they had already secured investors. = 项目启动前，他们已经获得了投资。
The entrepreneur had sought investors for months before receiving funding. = 企业家在获得资金之前已经寻找了数月的投资者。


将来时:
We will attract more investors by showcasing our innovative technology. = 通过展示我们的创新技术，我们将吸引更多投资者。
He will invest in real estate next year. = 他明年将投资房地产。


虚拟语气:
If I were an investor, I would diversify my portfolio. = 如果我是投资者，我会使我的投资组合多样化。
I wish I had more capital to invest. = 我希望我有更多资金可以投资。


被动语态:
The project was funded by several angel investors. = 该项目由几位天使投资者资助。
Millions of dollars are being invested in infrastructure development. = 数百万美元正在投资于基础设施建设。


疑问句:
Who are the main investors in this project? = 这个项目的主要投资者是谁？
What kind of investors are you looking for? = 你在寻找什么样的投资者？


否定句:
He did not invest in that company. = 他没有投资那家公司。
They haven't found the right investors yet. = 他们还没有找到合适的投资者。
