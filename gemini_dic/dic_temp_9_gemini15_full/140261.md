offshoring:/ˈɒfʃɔːrɪŋ/| n. |将业务外包到海外|The practice of basing some of a company's processes or services overseas, to take advantage of lower costs.|Many companies are offshoring their manufacturing operations to countries with lower labor costs.  许多公司正在将其制造业务外包到劳动力成本较低的国家。|outsourcing, relocation|onshoring, insourcing|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company offshores its customer service to India. = 这家公司将其客户服务外包给印度。
They offshore a significant portion of their software development. = 他们将很大一部分软件开发工作外包出去。


一般过去时:
We offshored our data entry to the Philippines last year. = 我们去年将数据录入工作外包给了菲律宾。
The firm offshored its call center operations to reduce costs. = 该公司将呼叫中心业务外包以降低成本。


现在完成时:
Many companies have offshored their manufacturing to China. = 许多公司已将制造业外包给了中国。
They have offshored their IT support for several years. = 他们已经将IT支持外包了几年。


过去完成时:
Before the merger, they had offshored most of their production. = 在合并之前，他们已经将大部分生产外包出去了。
The company had offshored its research and development before the economic downturn. = 在经济衰退之前，该公司已经将其研发工作外包出去了。


将来时:
We will offshore our accounting department next year. = 我们明年将把会计部门外包出去。
They plan to offshore more of their operations in the coming years. = 他们计划在未来几年将更多业务外包出去。


虚拟语气:
If we offshored our production, we could reduce costs significantly. = 如果我们将生产外包出去，我们可以大幅降低成本。
I wish we had offshored our customer service sooner. = 我希望我们早点将客户服务外包出去。


被动语态:
A significant portion of the work is offshored to lower-cost countries. = 一大部分工作被外包到低成本国家。
Their manufacturing processes were offshored several years ago. = 他们的制造流程几年前就被外包出去了。


疑问句:
Does your company offshore any of its operations? = 贵公司是否将任何业务外包？
Will they offshore their marketing team? = 他们会将营销团队外包吗？


否定句:
We do not offshore any of our sensitive data. = 我们不会将任何敏感数据外包出去。
The company decided not to offshore its research and development. = 公司决定不将其研发工作外包出去。
