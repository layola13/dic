fulgurant: /ˈfʌlgjərənt/ | adj. | 闪光的；突然的；迅速的 |  Brilliant; flashing; sudden; swift.  The word evokes a sense of intense, sudden brightness or speed. It often describes something that is both impressive and fleeting. | The fulgurant flash of lightning illuminated the night sky.  闪亮的闪电照亮了夜空。


The meteor left a fulgurant trail across the heavens.  流星在天上留下一道闪亮的轨迹。

近义词： dazzling, brilliant, sudden, swift, instantaneous, abrupt

反义词： gradual, slow, dull, dim


词源：From Latin *fulgurāns, present participle of fulgurāre "to lighten," from fulgur "lightning."
