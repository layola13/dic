funnest: /ˈfʌnɪst/ | adj. | 最有趣的，最令人开心的 |  The superlative form of "fun," indicating the highest degree of fun or enjoyment. It describes something or someone that provides the greatest amount of amusement or pleasure. | 例句：The funnest part of the trip was visiting the water park. 旅行中最有趣的部分是参观水上乐园。

The funnest game I've ever played is definitely "Among Us". 我玩过的最有趣的遊戲絕對是「Among Us」。


近义词：most amusing, most enjoyable, most entertaining | 反义词：most boring, most tedious, least enjoyable | 词性：形容词
