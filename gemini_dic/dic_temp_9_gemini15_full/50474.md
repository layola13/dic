depresses:/dɪˈprɛsɪz/ | vt. |使沮丧；使萧条；压抑；降低 |To cause (someone) to feel very unhappy and without hope. To make (something) lower, weaker, or smaller. |例句：The bad news depressed him.  坏消息使他沮丧。 The economic downturn depressed the housing market. 经济衰退导致房地产市场低迷。|近义词：discourages, saddens, discourages, depresses, lessens, lowers |反义词：cheers, elevates, uplifts, inspires


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rain depresses me. = 雨天让我感到沮丧。
This job depresses her. = 这份工作让她沮丧。

一般过去时:
The news depressed her deeply. = 这个消息让她非常沮丧。
The failure of the project depressed the team. = 项目的失败使团队士气低落。

现在完成时:
The constant criticism has depressed him. =  持续的批评让他沮丧。
This weather has depressed the whole family. =  这种天气让全家人都感到压抑。


过去完成时:
He had been depressed for weeks before he sought help. = 在寻求帮助之前，他已经沮丧了好几个星期。
The company's losses had depressed the stock price significantly. = 公司的亏损已大幅压低了股价。

将来时:
The bad weather will likely depress the harvest. = 恶劣天气可能会导致歉收。
I fear this news will depress him greatly. = 我担心这个消息会让他非常沮丧。

虚拟语气:
If I failed the exam, it would depress me terribly. = 如果我考试不及格，我会非常沮丧。
I wish this situation wouldn't depress me so much. = 我希望这种情况不会让我这么沮丧。

被动语态:
He was depressed by the rejection. = 他因为被拒绝而感到沮丧。
The economy was depressed by the war. = 战争使经济陷入低迷。

疑问句:
Does this situation depress you? = 这种情况让你沮丧吗？
What depresses you most about your job? = 你工作中最让你沮丧的是什么？

否定句:
The good news didn't depress her. = 好消息并没有让她沮丧。
The sunny weather didn't depress him at all. = 晴朗的天气一点也没有让他沮丧。
