fastened:/ˈfæsn̩d/ | vt. | 系紧；固定；扣住 | To attach or secure something firmly.  The verb "fasten" implies a secure connection, often using a mechanism like a buckle, button, or latch. |例句：He fastened his seatbelt before driving. 他开车前系好了安全带。

He fastened the door with a lock. 他用锁锁上了门。


近义词：attach, secure, tie, bind, connect | 反义词：unfasten, loosen, detach


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He fastens his shoes every morning. = 他每天早上系鞋带。
She fastens the curtains before going to bed. = 她睡觉前系上窗帘。


一般过去时:
