cancellate:/ˈkænsəleɪt/ | vt. |取消，作废|To officially end or stop something that has been planned or agreed to.|The flight was cancelled due to bad weather.  航班因恶劣天气而取消。|They cancelled the meeting at the last minute. 他们最后一分钟取消了会议。|近义词：void, annul, revoke |反义词：confirm, ratify, approve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cancels his appointments frequently. = 他经常取消约会。
She cancels her subscription every year. = 她每年都取消订阅。


一般过去时:
They cancelled the trip because of the storm. = 他们因为暴风雨取消了旅行。
He cancelled the order after he changed his mind. = 他改变主意后取消了订单。


现在完成时:
I have cancelled my plans for the weekend. = 我已经取消了周末的计划。
She has cancelled her membership. = 她已经取消了会员资格。


过去完成时:
They had cancelled the concert before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他们已经取消了音乐会。
She had cancelled the reservation hours ago. = 她几个小时前就已经取消了预订。


将来时:
We will cancel the project if the funding is not secured. = 如果资金没有到位，我们将取消项目。
They will cancel the game if it rains. = 如果下雨，他们将取消比赛。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would cancel the contract. = 如果我是你，我会取消合同。
I wish I had cancelled the flight earlier. = 我真希望我早点取消航班。


被动语态:
The meeting was cancelled due to unforeseen circumstances. = 会议因不可预见的情况而取消。
The order was cancelled because of a production error. = 订单因生产错误而被取消。


疑问句:
Will they cancel the show? = 他们会取消演出吗？
Did you cancel the reservation? = 你取消预订了吗？


否定句:
He did not cancel the appointment. = 他没有取消预约。
They would not cancel the contract under any circumstances. = 他们无论如何都不会取消合同。


PHRASE->
cancel a meeting = 取消会议
cancel a flight = 取消航班
cancel an order = 取消订单
cancel a subscription = 取消订阅
cancel a reservation = 取消预订
