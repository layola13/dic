empathist:/ˈɛmpəθɪst/| n. |移情者，共情者|A person who is able to understand and share the feelings of another.|例句：She is a natural empathist, always able to sense the emotions of those around her. 她是一个天生的移情者，总是能够感受到周围人的情绪。|近义词：empath,sympathizer|反义词：apathy, unfeeling person.
