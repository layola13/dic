perspicacity:/ˌpɜːrspɪˈkæsɪti/| n. |敏锐；洞察力；明智|The ability to have a ready insight into and understanding of things.  It implies a quickness and accuracy of judgment. |例句：Her perspicacity allowed her to see through the deception.  她的敏锐洞察力让她看穿了骗局。

例句：The detective's perspicacity was instrumental in solving the complex case.  侦探的敏锐洞察力对解决这起复杂案件至关重要。
