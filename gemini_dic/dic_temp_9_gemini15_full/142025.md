organizing:/ˈɔːrɡənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |组织；安排；筹备 |The act of putting things in order or arranging them systematically.  This involves planning, coordinating, and implementing tasks to achieve a specific goal.  |例句：She is organizing a fundraising event for the charity. = 她正在为慈善机构组织一场筹款活动。

例句：The team is organizing their work schedule to meet the deadline. = 团队正在组织他们的工作时间表以赶上截止日期。


近义词：arranging, structuring, coordinating, planning, systematizing

反义词：disorganizing, disrupting, confusing, disordering

词性：动词
