interlaminar:/ˌɪntərˈlæmɪnər/ | adj. |层间的，层层间的| Relating to or situated between layers or laminae.  The term often refers to the space or material between layers in a composite material, a stratified rock formation, or anatomical structures.|例句：Interlaminar shear strength is a crucial property of composite materials. 层间剪切强度是复合材料的关键特性。|例句：The interlaminar bonding was weak, leading to delamination. 层间粘合力较弱，导致分层。|近义词：interlayer, between-layer |反义词：intralaminar
