necromancies:/ˈnɛkrəˌmænsiz/| n.|死灵法术；招魂术 |The practice of communicating with the dead, typically through magic.  Necromancy is often associated with dark arts and the raising of the dead. |例句：The old woman was accused of practicing necromancies to curse her enemies.  这位老妇人被指控使用死灵法术诅咒她的敌人。

例句：Legends tell of powerful necromancies used to summon armies of the undead. 传说中强大的死灵法术被用来召唤亡灵大军。


近义词：black magic, sorcery, witchcraft, conjuration

反义词：holy magic, divine magic, benediction
