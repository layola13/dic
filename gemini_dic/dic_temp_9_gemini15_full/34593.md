clearwing:/ˈklɪərwɪŋ/| n.|透翅蛾；透明翅膀的昆虫|A clearwing is a moth of the family Sesiidae, characterized by their largely transparent wings.  Many species mimic wasps or bees, providing them with protection from predators.|例句：The clearwing moth hovered near the honeysuckle bush.  透翅蛾在忍冬灌木丛附近盘旋。  The gardener was fascinated by the clearwing's mimicry of a wasp. 园丁被透翅蛾模仿黄蜂的行为所吸引。|近义词：glasswing (less common)|反义词：N/A|名词
