depreciating:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪtɪŋ/ | v. |贬值；贬低；使…贬值；轻视 | To decrease in value over time; to belittle or undervalue something. |例句：The value of the car depreciates over time.  这辆车的价值随着时间推移而贬值。|例句：He depreciated her efforts, making her feel undervalued. 他贬低了她的努力，让她感到被低估。|近义词：devaluing, diminishing, belittling, undervaluing |反义词： appreciating, enhancing, valuing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dollar is depreciating against the euro.  美元兑欧元贬值。
He constantly depreciates his own accomplishments. 他总是贬低自己的成就。


一般过去时:
The property depreciated significantly during the recession.  房产在经济衰退期间大幅贬值。
She depreciated his intelligence in front of everyone.  她在大家面前贬低了他的智力。


现在完成时:
The car has depreciated considerably since we bought it.  自从我们买了这辆车以来，它的价值已经大幅贬值。
He has repeatedly depreciated her contributions to the project. 他一再贬低她对项目的贡献。


过去完成时:
The stock market had depreciated sharply before the government intervened.  在政府干预之前，股市已经大幅下跌。
By the time the meeting ended, he had already depreciated her ideas. 会议结束时，他已经贬低了她的想法。


将来时:
The equipment is expected to depreciate rapidly in the coming years.  预计该设备在未来几年内将迅速贬值。
He will likely depreciate her work again if given the chance. 如果有机会，他很可能会再次贬低她的工作。


虚拟语气:
If the currency depreciated further, the economy would suffer. 如果货币进一步贬值，经济将遭受损失。
I wish he hadn't depreciated my hard work. 我真希望他没有贬低我的辛勤工作。


被动语态:
The asset was depreciated over a period of five years. 该资产在五年内被贬值。
Her suggestions were depreciated by the management team. 她的建议被管理团队贬低了。


疑问句:
Is the value of the house depreciating? 房子的价值在贬值吗？
Does he often depreciate the efforts of his colleagues? 他经常贬低同事的努力吗？


否定句:
The value of the antique did not depreciate; it actually increased. 古董的价值并没有贬值；它实际上增加了。
He did not depreciate her talents; instead, he praised them. 他并没有贬低她的才能；相反，他赞扬了她。
