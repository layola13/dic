rewatched:/ˌriːˈwɒtʃt/ | v. | 重看，再看 | To watch something again.  | I rewatched my favorite movie last night.  我昨晚又看了一遍我最喜欢的电影。 | watched, viewed again |  (无明确反义词，可考虑unwatched,但语义不完全相反) | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I rewatch movies regularly. = 我定期重看电影。
He rewatches the game every week. = 他每周都重看这场比赛。

一般过去时:
I rewatched that documentary yesterday. = 我昨天重看了那部纪录片。
She rewatched the entire series in a week. = 她在一周内重看了整个系列。

现在完成时:
I have rewatched this film three times. = 我已经重看这部电影三次了。
They have rewatched the video several times. = 他们已经重看了好几次视频。

过去完成时:
I had rewatched the movie before he arrived. = 他来之前我已经重看了这部电影。
She had rewatched all the episodes before the finale. = 在结局之前她已经重看了所有剧集。

将来时:
I will rewatch that classic film next weekend. = 下周末我将重看那部经典电影。
We will rewatch the concert recording later. = 我们稍后将重看演唱会录像。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rewatch that series. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重看那个系列。
I wish I had rewatched that movie before it left theaters. = 我真希望在电影下映之前我就重看过它。

被动语态:
The movie was rewatched by millions of people. = 这部电影被数百万人重看。
The video was rewatched countless times online. = 这段视频在网上被无数次重看。


疑问句:

Will you rewatch this show next season? = 你下季还会重看这个节目吗？


否定句:
I didn't rewatch the movie because I was busy. = 我因为忙而没有重看这部电影。
She hasn't rewatched the documentary yet. = 她还没重看那部纪录片。
