sierra:/sɪˈɛrə/ | n. |塞拉利昂；山脉；锯齿形 | A sierra is a mountain range characterized by its jagged, saw-tooth peaks.  It can also refer to a specific mountain range in California, or to a type of saw with a serrated blade. |例句：The Sierra Nevada mountain range is a stunning natural wonder. = 塞拉内华达山脉是一个令人惊叹的自然奇观。

The Sierra Leone is a West African country. = 塞拉利昂是西非的一个国家。|近义词：range, chain, ridge |反义词：plain, valley
