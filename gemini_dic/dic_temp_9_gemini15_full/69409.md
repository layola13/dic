feel oneself: /fiːl wʌnˈsɛlf/ | v. |感觉良好；感觉自信；恢复健康 | To feel oneself implies a return to a state of well-being, both physically and mentally.  It suggests a feeling of being back to normal after an illness or a period of feeling unwell, or regaining confidence and self-assurance. |


例句：

After a week in bed with the flu, I'm finally starting to feel myself again. =  卧床一周患流感后，我终于开始感觉好起来了。

She felt herself again after a relaxing vacation. =  在轻松的假期之后，她感觉自己恢复了活力。


近义词：recover, regain strength, feel better, be oneself


反义词：feel unwell, feel weak, feel depressed, feel down


词性: 动词
