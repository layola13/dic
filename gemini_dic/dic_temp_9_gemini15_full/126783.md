nanop:/ˈnænɒp/| n.|纳米级粒子，纳米；纳米技术相关产品|A nanop is a nanoscale particle or material, often referring to a specific product related to nanotechnology.|Example:  The researchers developed a new nanop for targeted drug delivery. 研究人员开发了一种用于靶向药物递送的新型纳米粒子。|近义词：nanoparticle, nanomaterial|反义词：macroscopic material


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like a verb.  Therefore, standard verb tenses (present, past, future, etc.) are not applicable.  The usage revolves around its noun function in sentences.

Example 1: The properties of the nanop are being studied.  该纳米粒子的特性正在被研究。
Example 2:  They are developing a new type of nanop for solar cells. 他们正在开发一种用于太阳能电池的新型纳米粒子。
