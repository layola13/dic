scratch: /skrætʃ/ | n., v. | 抓，刮擦；乱涂；挠；擦伤；起步 |

英文解析:
As a noun, "scratch" refers to a mark made by scratching, a superficial injury, or a starting point. As a verb, it means to rub or scrape with one's nails or something sharp, to make a rough drawing or writing, to cancel or withdraw something, or to start from scratch.


例句:
The cat scratched the furniture. = 猫抓坏了家具。

There's a scratch on my car. = 我的车上有一道划痕。


近义词:
scrape, rub, scratch

反义词:
polish, smooth


词性:
noun, verb
