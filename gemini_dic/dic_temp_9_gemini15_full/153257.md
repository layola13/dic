pen-and-ink: /ˈpen ænd ɪŋk/ | n. | 钢笔画；钢笔素描 | Pen-and-ink refers to a drawing technique using ink and a pen, often featuring fine lines and details.  It is also used to describe the resulting artwork. | Example:  The artist created a beautiful pen-and-ink drawing of a cityscape.  艺术家创作了一幅美丽的城市景观钢笔画。 The book's illustrations were all done in pen-and-ink. 这本书的插图都是钢笔画的。| 近义词: ink drawing, pen drawing | 反义词:  watercolor, oil painting


USAGE->
(Pen-and-ink as a noun doesn't have verb conjugations.  The usage examples below are for the verbs commonly associated with creating pen-and-ink drawings.)

一般现在时:
I draw with pen and ink every day. = 我每天都用钢笔画画。
She sketches using pen and ink. = 她用钢笔素描。


一般过去时:
He drew a beautiful landscape with pen and ink yesterday. = 昨天他用钢笔画了一幅美丽的风景画。
They sketched the scene in pen and ink. = 他们用钢笔速写了这个场景。


现在完成时:
I have completed several pen-and-ink illustrations this month. = 这个月我已经完成了多幅钢笔画插图。
She has been working on a pen-and-ink portrait for weeks. = 她已经创作钢笔肖像画好几个星期了。


过去完成时:
He had finished the pen-and-ink drawing before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前完成了钢笔画。
They had already sketched the outline in pen and ink. = 他们已经用钢笔勾勒出轮廓了。

虚拟语气:

I wish I could master the art of pen-and-ink illustration. = 我希望我能掌握钢笔画插图的艺术。
