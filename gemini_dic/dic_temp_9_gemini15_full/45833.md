customize:/ˈkʌstəmaɪz/ | vt. |定制，个性化 | To modify or adapt something to suit a particular individual or group.  | 例句：I customized my phone with a new wallpaper and widgets. 我用新的壁纸和小部件定制了我的手机。 | 例句：The company allows customers to customize their orders. 公司允许客户定制他们的订单。|近义词： personalize, tailor, adapt | 反义词：standardize, generalize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They customize their products to meet customer needs. = 他们定制产品以满足客户需求。
We customize our services for each client. = 我们为每个客户定制服务。

一般过去时:
She customized her car with a racing stripe. = 她用赛车条纹定制了她的车。
He customized the software to fit his workflow. = 他定制了软件以适应他的工作流程。

现在完成时:
They have customized several websites for our business. = 他们已经为我们的业务定制了几个网站。
I have customized my keyboard layout for better typing efficiency. = 我已经定制了我的键盘布局以提高打字效率。

过去完成时:
They had customized the software before the launch. = 他们在发布之前已经定制了软件。
She had customized her presentation before delivering it. = 她在演示之前已经定制了她的演示文稿。

将来时:
We will customize the application according to your requirements. = 我们将根据您的要求定制应用程序。
They will customize the tour based on the clients' preferences. = 他们将根据客户的喜好定制旅游。

虚拟语气:
If I had the skills, I would customize my own computer. = 如果我有技能，我会自己定制我的电脑。
It would be great if they could customize the furniture for us. = 如果他们能为我们定制家具就好了。

被动语态:
The software is customized to meet specific needs. = 软件是根据特定需求定制的。
The report was customized for the board meeting. = 报告是为董事会会议定制的。

疑问句:
Can you customize the design to include my logo? = 你能定制设计以包含我的标志吗？
Do they customize their products for individual customers? = 他们是否为个人客户定制产品？

否定句:
They did not customize the product; they used a standard model. = 他们没有定制产品；他们使用了标准型号。
We cannot customize this software; it's a pre-built package. = 我们不能定制这个软件；它是一个预构建的包。
