immanity:/ɪˈmænɪti/| n.|内在性；内在神性；固有性|The inherent nature or essence of something, especially a divine or spiritual nature.  It often refers to the idea that God or a divine principle is inherent within the world or within humanity. |例句：The philosopher explored the immanity of God in the natural world.  哲学家探讨了上帝在自然界中的内在性。  The artist sought to capture the immanity of the human spirit in his paintings.艺术家试图在他的绘画中捕捉人类精神的内在性。|近义词：inherence, indwelling, intrinsic nature|反义词：transcendence, externality|名词
