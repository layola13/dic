paleous: /ˈpeɪliəs/ | adj. | 古代的，古老的 | Relating to or characteristic of a past or earlier time, especially prehistoric times.  | The paleous artifacts found at the site suggested a much older civilization than previously thought.  | ancient, archaic, primeval | modern, new, contemporary |  adj.
