jatrophic: /dʒæˈtrɒfɪk/ | adj. | 营养不良的；发育不良的 | Relating to or characterized by malnutrition or stunted growth.  The term often refers to plants exhibiting poor growth due to nutrient deficiencies. | Example: The jatrophic plants in the drought-stricken area showed signs of severe leaf chlorosis and reduced yield.  贫瘠地区的植物表现出严重的叶绿素缺乏和减产迹象。| 近义词: malnourished, stunted, undernourished, deficient | 反义词: well-nourished, thriving, robust | 词性:形容词
