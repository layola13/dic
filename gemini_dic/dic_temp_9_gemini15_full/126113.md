mute:/mjuːt/ | adj., n., v. |  沉默的；哑巴；使沉默；消音 |  Silent; unable to speak; to silence or suppress sound. |  例句：The baby was mute with surprise. (婴儿惊讶得说不出话来。)  The television is muted. (电视声音被消音了。) | 近义词：silent, speechless, dumb, voiceless | 反义词：vocal, articulate, loud


USAGE->

The dog remains mute after the incident. = 这只狗在事件发生后保持沉默。
