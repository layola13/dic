killjoy:/ˈkɪlˌdʒɔɪ/| n.|扫兴的人，破坏乐趣的人|A person who spoils the enjoyment of others; someone who is depressing or negative.|例句：He's such a killjoy; he always has to criticize everything. 他真是个扫兴的人，他总是要批评一切。  例句：Don't be such a killjoy and let's have some fun. 不要扫兴，让我们玩得开心点。|近义词：spoilsport, wet blanket, party pooper|反义词：life of the party, merrymaker|名词
