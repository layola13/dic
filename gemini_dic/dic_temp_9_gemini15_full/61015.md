/ɛˈlɪŋgwɪd/ | n. | 语言学家 | A linguist is a scholar who studies linguistics. Linguists study the structure, history, and social aspects of language.  | Example:  Dr. Smith is a renowned linguist specializing in historical linguistics. 史密斯博士是一位专门研究历史语言学的著名语言学家。  |近义词: philologist, semanticist | 反义词: N/A
