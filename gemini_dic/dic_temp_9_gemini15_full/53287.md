dirtcore:/ˈdɜːrtkɔːr/| n. |泥土核心；脏污核心音乐|A subgenre of hardcore punk characterized by its raw, abrasive sound, often incorporating elements of noise rock and experimental music.  It's known for its chaotic and aggressive nature, frequently dealing with themes of nihilism, societal decay, and personal struggles. |例句：The band's dirtcore sound was a cacophony of distorted guitars and guttural vocals.  这支乐队的泥土核心音乐是失真吉他与粗犷人声的嘈杂混合。|近义词：hardcore punk, noise rock|反义词：pop, easy listening
