focusing: /ˈfoʊkəsɪŋ/ | v. | 集中注意力，聚焦 |  The act of concentrating one's attention or directing one's efforts towards a particular activity, object, or person.  | 例句：She was focusing on her studies. = 她正在专心学习。
She is focusing the camera on the subject. = 她正在将摄像机对准拍摄对象。| 近义词：concentrating, centering, zeroing in on | 反义词：distracting, diverting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is focusing on his career. = 他正在专注于他的事业。
They are focusing their efforts on finding a solution. = 他们正将精力集中在寻找解决方案上。


一般过去时:
I focused on the speaker's words. = 我专注于演讲者的讲话。
We focused our attention on the details of the contract. = 我们把注意力集中在合同的细节上。


现在完成时:
She has focused on painting for many years. = 她多年来一直专注于绘画。
They have focused their resources on developing new technologies. = 他们已经将资源集中在开发新技术上。


过去完成时:
He had focused on his studies before the exam. = 他在考试前一直专注于学习。
They had focused their energy on the project before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前一直将精力集中在项目上。


将来时:
I will focus on completing this task first. = 我将首先专注于完成这项任务。
We will focus our marketing campaign on young adults. = 我们将把营销活动重点放在年轻人身上。


虚拟语气:
If I were focusing better, I would finish the work faster. = 如果我更专注，我会更快地完成工作。
I wish I had focused more on my studies. = 我真希望我当时更专注于学习。


被动语态:
The camera was focused on the stage. = 摄像机对准了舞台。
Attention was focused on the emergency. = 注意力集中在紧急情况上。


疑问句:
What are you focusing on right now? = 你现在专注于什么？
Is she focusing on her work properly? = 她工作专心吗？


否定句:
He wasn't focusing during the lecture. = 他在讲座期间没有专心听讲。
They didn't focus enough on the problem. = 他们没有足够关注这个问题。


PHRASE->
focus on = 集中于
focus group = 焦点小组
bring into focus = 使聚焦
out of focus = 模糊的
in focus = 清晰的
