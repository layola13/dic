photochromic:/ˌfoʊtəˈkroʊmɪk/ | adj. |变色的，感光变色的 |  Changing color when exposed to light, especially ultraviolet light.  This property is often used in lenses that darken in sunlight and lighten in shade. | Example: The photochromic lenses in his glasses automatically adjusted to the changing light conditions.  他的眼镜上的感光变色镜片会自动适应不断变化的光线条件。  |近义词：light-sensitive, photosensitive | 反义词：color-constant
