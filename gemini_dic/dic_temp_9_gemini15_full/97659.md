inhibitor: /ɪnˈhɪbɪtər/ | n. |抑制剂，抑制物 | A substance that slows down or prevents a process.  | Example:  Enzyme inhibitors are used to treat certain diseases.  酶抑制剂用于治疗某些疾病。 |近义词：suppressor, retardant, antagonist |反义词：activator, stimulant | 名词


USAGE->

The drug acts as an inhibitor of cell growth.  = 这种药物起着抑制细胞生长的作用。
This chemical is a potent inhibitor of the enzyme. =  这种化学物质是该酶的有效抑制剂。


一般过去时:

虚拟语气:

It is vital that we find a potent inhibitor to stop the reaction. = 至关重要的是，我们必须找到一种有效的抑制剂来阻止反应。

疑问句:

否定句:

词源:
The word "inhibitor" is derived from the Latin word "inhibere," meaning "to restrain" or "to hold back."  The suffix "-tor" indicates an agent or doer of the action.
