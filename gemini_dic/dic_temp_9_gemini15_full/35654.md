cob:/kɒb/| n. |圆木,土坯,玉米芯|A cob is a thick, short piece of wood, or a piece of earth or clay that is shaped and dried to be used as a brick.  It can also refer to the central core of an ear of corn.|例句：He used a cob of wood to mend the fence. 他用一根圆木修补篱笆。|The builders used dried cobs of earth to make the walls. 建筑工人用晒干的土坯砌墙。|近义词：log, chunk, brick|反义词：None specifically, depends on context.
