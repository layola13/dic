roars:/rɔːrz/| v.|咆哮；怒吼；轰鸣|To make a loud, deep sound, like a lion or engine.|The lion roared at the hunter.  狮子对着猎人咆哮。|The engine roared to life. 引擎轰鸣着启动了。|growls, bellows, thunders|whispers, murmurs|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lion roars loudly. = 狮子大声咆哮。
The engine roars constantly. = 引擎持续轰鸣。


一般过去时:
The crowd roared with laughter. = 人群爆发出笑声。
The thunder roared throughout the night. = 雷声整夜轰鸣。


现在完成时:
The volcano has roared back to life. = 火山已经重新喷发。
The car has roared down the street. = 车子呼啸着冲下了街道。


过去完成时:
The audience had roared its approval before the curtain fell. = 幕布落下前，观众已经爆发出赞许的喝彩声。
The storm had roared through the night before calming down. = 暴风雨在平静下来之前整夜肆虐。


将来时:
The crowd will roar their approval. = 人群将发出赞许的喝彩。
The plane will roar overhead. = 飞机将从头顶呼啸而过。


虚拟语气:
If the lion roared, I would run away. = 如果狮子咆哮，我会逃跑。
If the engine roared louder, I would cover my ears. = 如果引擎咆哮得更响，我会捂住耳朵。


被动语态:
The news was roared from the rooftops. = 这个消息被到处宣扬。
His name was roared by the crowd. = 他的名字被人群高喊。


疑问句:
Does the lion roar often? = 狮子经常咆哮吗？
Will the engine roar when it starts? = 引擎启动时会轰鸣吗？


否定句:
The lion didn't roar today. = 狮子今天没有咆哮。
The engine didn't roar as loudly as expected. = 引擎的轰鸣声不如预期的大。
