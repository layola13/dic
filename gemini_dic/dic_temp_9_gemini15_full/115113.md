mangers:/ˈmændʒərz/| n.|  饲料槽；马槽；经理们 | A manger is a trough or container, typically for feeding livestock, especially cattle or horses.  The term is also used figuratively, particularly in relation to the nativity scene where Jesus was placed in a manger. | Example: The sheep were fed from the stone manger.  羊群从石头做的饲料槽里吃草。 The company's managers met to discuss the new project. 公司的经理们开会讨论新项目。|近义词：trough, crib, feed box |反义词：N/A
