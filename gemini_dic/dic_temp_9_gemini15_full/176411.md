re-imaging:/ˌriːˈɪmɪdʒɪŋ/| v.|重新想象；重新塑造形象 |To form a new image or impression of something or someone; to revamp or reinvent the image of something.|例句：The company is re-imaging its brand to appeal to a younger audience.  公司正在重新塑造其品牌形象以吸引更年轻的受众。 The artist is re-imaging classic fairy tales with a modern twist. 艺术家正在以现代风格重新演绎经典童话故事。|近义词：reimagine, reinvent, revamp, rebrand|反义词：maintain, preserve, keep the same


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are re-imaging their marketing strategy. = 他们正在重新构思他们的营销策略。
The designer is re-imaging the logo for a more modern look. = 设计师正在重新设计标志，使其更现代化。


一般过去时:
She re-imaged the old house into a modern masterpiece. = 她把旧房子重新设计成现代杰作。
The company re-imaged its product line to increase sales. = 公司重新设计了产品线以增加销量。


现在完成时:
They have re-imaged their website to improve user experience. = 他们已经重新设计了他们的网站以改善用户体验。
The museum has re-imaged its exhibits to attract more visitors. = 博物馆已经重新设计了它的展品以吸引更多游客。


过去完成时:
The artist had re-imaged the painting before the exhibition. = 艺术家在展览前已经重新构思了这幅画。
They had re-imaged the brand several times before settling on the current design. = 在最终确定当前设计之前，他们已经多次重新设计了品牌。


将来时:
We will re-image the garden next spring. = 我们明年春天将重新设计花园。
The team will re-image the software interface for better usability. = 团队将重新设计软件界面以提高可用性。


虚拟语气:
If they re-imaged the product, it might sell better. = 如果他们重新设计产品，它可能会卖得更好。
I wish they had re-imaged the website earlier. = 我希望他们早点重新设计网站。


被动语态:
The brand was re-imaged by a renowned design agency. = 该品牌由一家著名的设计公司重新设计。
The old building is being re-imaged into a luxury hotel. = 这座旧建筑正在被改造成一家豪华酒店。


疑问句:
Will the company re-image its products this year? = 公司今年会重新设计其产品吗？
How will they re-image the outdated technology? = 他们将如何重新设计过时的技术？


否定句:
They did not re-image the logo. = 他们没有重新设计标志。
The company will not re-image its brand until next year. = 公司直到明年才会重新设计其品牌。


PHRASE->

re-imaging the future = 重新构想未来
re-imaging the past = 重新审视过去
re-imaging oneself =  重新塑造自我
