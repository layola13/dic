overgeneralizes:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɛnərəlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 过度概括；以偏概全 | To make generalizations that are too broad or that do not apply in all cases.  It implies drawing conclusions based on limited or insufficient evidence, leading to inaccurate or misleading statements. | He overgeneralizes about women based on his experiences with a few. 他根据与少数女性的经历对女性进行过度概括。  She overgeneralizes the findings of the study to the entire population. 她将研究结果过度概括到整个人群。 | generalize, extrapolate |  specialize, particularize
