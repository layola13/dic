regest:/ˈrɛdʒɛst/| n. |摘要；登记；注册| A brief summary or abstract of a document, especially a medieval charter or other official record.  The act of registering or recording something formally. |例句：The regest of the charter provided a concise overview of its contents.  = 这份宪章的摘要提供了其内容的简要概述。

The court clerk meticulously recorded the regest of the legal proceedings. = 法庭书记员仔细记录了法律程序的登记。|近义词：abstract, summary, register, record |反义词：full text, complete version
