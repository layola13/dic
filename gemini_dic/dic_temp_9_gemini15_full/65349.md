eulogising:/ˈjuːləˌdʒaɪzɪŋ/| v.|赞扬；颂扬；悼词|The act of delivering a eulogy; praising someone or something highly, often formally.  It often implies a respectful and admiring tone, particularly when referring to someone who has passed away.|例句：He was eulogising his late grandfather’s life and achievements. 他正在赞扬他已故祖父的一生和成就。|例句：The newspaper article eulogised the city's vibrant cultural scene.  这篇报刊文章赞扬了这座城市充满活力的文化景象。|近义词：praising, celebrating, commending, extolling|反义词：criticizing, condemning, denouncing|动词
