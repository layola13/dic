rapping:/ˈræpɪŋ/| n. v.|说唱；说唱音乐；急促而有节奏地说|The act of performing rap music;  a style of music involving rhythmic and rhyming speech.|例句：He's known for his incredible rapping skills. 他以其令人难以置信的说唱技巧而闻名。|例句：The rapping in that song is amazing. 那首歌的说唱太棒了。|近义词：rhyming, chanting, spitting|反义词：singing (in a melodic way)
