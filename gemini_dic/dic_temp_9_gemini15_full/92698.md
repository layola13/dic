icehouse:/ˈaɪshaʊs/| n. |冰库；冰窖；冷藏库 |A building or room used for storing ice.  |例句：The icehouse was filled to the brim with ice harvested from the frozen lake. 冰库里装满了从冰冻湖里收割的冰。
例句：They kept the fish fresh by storing them in the icehouse. 他们把鱼储存在冰库里以保持新鲜。|近义词：ice storage, refrigerator, freezer |反义词：furnace, oven
