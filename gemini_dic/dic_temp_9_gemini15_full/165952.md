preparable:/prɪˈpɛərəbəl/| adj. | 可准备的；可预备的 | Capable of being prepared; able to be made ready.  This word describes something that can be prepared or made ready for a particular purpose or event. | 例句：The ingredients for the cake are preparable in advance. 蛋糕的配料可以提前准备好。

The assignment is preparable within a week.  这份作业可以在一周内准备好。


近义词：preparatory, ready-made,  get-ready


反义词：unpreparable, unprepared


词性:形容词
