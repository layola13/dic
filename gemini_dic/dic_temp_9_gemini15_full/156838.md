piglet:/ˈpɪɡlət/| n. |小猪|A young pig.  |例句：The piglet squealed when its mother left. 小猪在妈妈离开时尖叫起来。|The farmer carefully tended to his piglets. 农民细心地照料他的小猪。|近义词：young pig, pigling|反义词：sow (成年母猪), boar (成年公猪)
