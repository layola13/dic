pinot:/ˈpinoʊ/| n. |黑皮诺（一种葡萄）|Pinot is a type of red wine grape known for its delicate flavor and high acidity.  Pinot Noir wines are often described as earthy, complex, and elegant.|This Pinot Noir has notes of cherry and spice.|近义词：Burgundy (referring to wines from the Burgundy region often made with Pinot Noir)|反义词：无
