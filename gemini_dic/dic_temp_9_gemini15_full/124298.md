motherboard:/ˈmʌðərˌbɔːrd/ | n. |主板，母板 |The motherboard is the main circuit board in a computer.  It connects all the other components, such as the CPU, memory, and graphics card. |The new motherboard significantly improved the computer's performance. 新的主板大大提高了电脑的性能。|circuit board, mainboard |  | noun
