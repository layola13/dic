hydrotherapy:/ˌhaɪdroʊˈθɛrəpi/ | n. | 水疗疗法，水疗 | The therapeutic use of water for pain relief and rehabilitation.  Hydrotherapy involves using water in various temperatures, pressures, and movements to treat a variety of conditions. | Example:  She underwent hydrotherapy to relieve the pain in her back. 她接受了水疗来缓解背痛。  |近义词：aquatherapy, balneotherapy | 反义词： (no direct antonym, but opposite treatment methods could be considered, such as dry heat therapy)
