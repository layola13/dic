nonmystics:/ˌnɑːnˈmɪstɪks/ | n. |非神秘主义者 |People who do not believe in or practice mysticism.  They are typically characterized by a focus on reason, logic, and empirical evidence, rather than spiritual or supernatural experiences. | Example:  Many scientists are considered nonmystics due to their reliance on scientific method.  许多科学家因为依赖科学方法而被认为是非神秘主义者。|近义词: rationalists, empiricists, materialists |反义词: mystics
