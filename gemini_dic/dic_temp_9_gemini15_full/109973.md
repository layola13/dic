liberta:/lɪˈbɜːrtə/| n.|自由；解放|Liberation from oppression or restraint.  The state of being free within society from oppressive restrictions imposed by authority on one's way of life, behavior, or political views.|例句：The people fought for their liberta.  人民为争取自由而战。|例句：The liberta of speech is a fundamental human right. 言论自由是一项基本人权。|近义词：freedom, liberty, emancipation|反义词：slavery, oppression, bondage|名词
