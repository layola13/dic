postiler:/ˈpɒstɪlə(r)/| n.|注释者；写注释的人|A postiler is a person who writes or adds notes, comments, or annotations to a text, especially a religious text.  They often provide explanations, interpretations, or reflections on the original material.|The postiler meticulously annotated the ancient manuscript, adding insightful commentary to each verse.|近义词：commentator, annotator, interpreter|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite would be someone who ignores or avoids adding notes. |名词
