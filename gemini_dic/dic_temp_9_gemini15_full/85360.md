harrower:/ˈhærəʊər/| n. |碎草机；痛苦的根源；折磨者 | A harrower is a machine used in agriculture to break up and smooth the surface of the soil after plowing, or to pull up weeds.  It can also refer to something or someone that causes great suffering or distress. |例句：The farmer used a harrower to prepare the field for planting.  = 农民用碎草机准备播种田地。


The harrowing experience left him deeply scarred. =  那次痛苦的经历给他留下了深深的伤疤。|近义词：cultivator, pulverizer, tormentor, affliction |反义词：comforter, solace, reliever
