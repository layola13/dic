disruptions:/dɪsˈrʌpʃənz/| n.|中断；扰乱；破坏|The act of disrupting something; an instance of being disrupted.  It refers to an interruption or disturbance that prevents something from continuing as expected.|例句：The snowstorm caused major disruptions to air travel. 大雪导致航班严重中断。
例句：The constant disruptions in the classroom made it difficult to concentrate. 教室里持续不断的干扰使人难以集中注意力。|近义词：interruptions, disturbances, interruptions, upsets|反义词：continuity, smoothness, order
