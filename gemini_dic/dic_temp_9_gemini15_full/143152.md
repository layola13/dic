outchatter:/ˌaʊtˈtʃætər/| v.|喧宾夺主地说话，喋喋不休地盖过别人 |To talk more loudly or continuously than someone else, thus preventing them from being heard.  |例句：He completely outchatted her at the meeting, making it impossible for her to get a word in edgewise. 他在会议上完全盖过了她的声音，让她一句话也插不上嘴。  |近义词：overshadow, drown out, outtalk |反义词：listen attentively, remain silent
