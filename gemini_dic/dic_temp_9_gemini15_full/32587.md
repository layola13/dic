chiromancy:/ˈkaɪrəʊmænsi/| n. |手相术|The practice of telling fortunes or predicting the future by examining the lines, mounts, and other features of the hand.  Chiromancy is considered a pseudoscience, lacking empirical evidence to support its claims.| Example:  She believes in chiromancy and had her palm read at the fair. 她相信手相术，在集市上让人看了她的手掌。|近义词：palmistry, palm reading|反义词：scientific prediction, empirical analysis|名词
