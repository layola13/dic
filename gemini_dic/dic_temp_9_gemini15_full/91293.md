hydrolyses:/haɪˈdrɒləsɪz/ | n. |水解；水解作用 |The process of chemically breaking down a compound into its constituents by reaction with water.  |例句：The hydrolysis of starch produces glucose.  淀粉的水解产生葡萄糖。

例句：Acid hydrolysis is used in the production of many sugars. 酸性水解被用于许多糖的生产。


近义词：decomposition, breakdown |反义词：synthesis, formation |词性:noun
