prefigurate:/ˌpriːˈfɪɡjʊreɪt/ | vt. |预示；预想；预先安排 |To represent or foreshadow something in advance; to prefigure something.  |例句：The ancient myths prefigurate many of the themes found in modern literature. 古代神话预示了现代文学中许多主题。

The artist's early sketches prefigured his later masterpiece.  这位艺术家的早期素描预示了他后来的杰作。


近义词：foreshadow, anticipate, prefigure, portend | 反义词： follow, succeed


词性：动词
