enviably:/ˈɛnviəbli/ | adj. |令人羡慕地，令人嫉妒地 |  Adjective describing something so desirable that it evokes envy. It emphasizes the strong feeling of wanting what someone else has. |例句：He lived an enviably comfortable life. 他过着令人羡慕的舒适生活。

例句：She had an enviably successful career. 她拥有令人羡慕的成功事业。


近义词：desirably, admirably, wonderfully


反义词：undesirably, unfavorably, deplorably


词性：形容词
