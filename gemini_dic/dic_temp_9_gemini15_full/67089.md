exploitation:/ˌɛksploɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. |剥削；开发；利用 |The act of using someone or something in an unfair or selfish way for one's own advantage.  It can refer to the unjust treatment of workers, the overuse of natural resources, or the manipulation of others. |例句：The exploitation of workers in sweatshops is a serious human rights issue. 工厂血汗工厂对工人的剥削是一个严重的人权问题。

例句：The exploitation of oil reserves has significantly impacted the environment. 石油储备的开发对环境造成了重大影响。|近义词：abuse, oppression, misuse, utilization |反义词：fair treatment, conservation, protection |词性：名词
