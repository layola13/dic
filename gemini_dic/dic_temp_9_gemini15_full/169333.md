promotions:/prəˈmoʊʃənz/| n. |晋升；促销；推广 |The act of raising someone to a higher position or rank; the activity of advertising or publicizing something to increase sales or public awareness. |例句：He received a promotion to senior manager. 他被提升为高级经理。|例句：The company launched a new promotion for its summer collection. 公司为夏季系列产品推出了一项新的促销活动。|近义词：advancements, upgrades, advertisements, campaigns |反义词：demotions, downgrades


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company runs many promotions throughout the year. = 公司全年都会进行许多促销活动。
Our team focuses on digital promotions. = 我们的团队专注于数字推广。


一般过去时:
They held a successful promotion last month. = 他们上个月举办了一次成功的促销活动。
She received a promotion after working hard for years. = 她努力工作多年后获得了晋升。


现在完成时:
The marketing team has implemented several successful promotions. = 营销团队已经实施了几项成功的促销活动。
He has received two promotions in the last five years. = 他在过去五年中获得了两次晋升。


过去完成时:
Before the promotion, they had already prepared marketing materials. = 促销活动开始前，他们已经准备好了营销材料。
She had worked hard for years before receiving her promotion. = 在获得晋升之前，她已经努力工作了很多年。


将来时:
We will launch a new promotion next quarter. = 我们将在下个季度推出新的促销活动。
They will likely receive a promotion after the project completion. = 项目完成后，他们很可能会得到晋升。


虚拟语气:
If they had worked harder, they might have received a promotion. = 如果他们工作更努力，他们可能会得到晋升。
I wish I had applied for that promotion. = 我真希望我申请了那次晋升。


被动语态:
The new product was promoted through various channels. = 新产品通过各种渠道进行推广。
Several employees were promoted to higher positions. = 几名员工被提升到更高的职位。


疑问句:

否定句:

He wasn't offered a promotion despite his hard work. = 尽管他努力工作，但他没有得到晋升。
