democratized:/dɪˈmɒkrətaɪzd/| /dɪˈmɑːkrətaɪzd/| v.| 使民主化；普及；使大众化 | To make something accessible to or usable by more people; to spread something widely.|例句：The internet has democratized access to information.  互联网使信息获取民主化了。|例句：The invention of the printing press democratized the spread of knowledge. 印刷术的发明使知识的传播民主化了。|近义词：popularized, equalized, disseminated|反义词：centralized, monopolized


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government democratizes the education system. = 政府正在使教育体系民主化。
They democratize access to healthcare. = 他们正在使医疗保健普及。


一般过去时:
The reforms democratized the political process. = 这些改革使政治进程民主化了。
The company democratized its product pricing. = 公司使产品的定价大众化了。


现在完成时:
Technology has democratized communication. = 技术已经使沟通民主化了。
The movement has democratized the art world. = 这个运动已经使艺术界大众化了。


过去完成时:
The revolution had democratized the country before the war ended. = 在战争结束之前，革命已经使这个国家民主化了。
They had democratized the voting system before the election. = 他们在选举之前已经使投票制度民主化了。


将来时:
The new policy will democratize access to housing. = 新政策将使住房获取民主化。
This technology will democratize data analysis. = 这项技术将使数据分析大众化。


虚拟语气:
If the government democratized the media, the public would be better informed. = 如果政府使媒体民主化，公众将会得到更好的信息。
I wish they had democratized the process earlier. = 我希望他们早些使这个过程民主化。


被动语态:
The education system was democratized by the reforms. = 教育体系通过改革得到了民主化。
Access to information is being democratized by the internet. = 信息获取正在通过互联网实现民主化。


疑问句:
Has the company democratized its hiring process? = 公司是否已经使招聘流程民主化了？
Will this technology democratize healthcare? = 这项技术会使医疗保健民主化吗？


否定句:
The government did not democratize the legal system. = 政府没有使法律体系民主化。
They have not yet democratized the distribution of wealth. = 他们尚未使财富分配民主化。
