perceivance:/pərˈsiːvəns/| n. |感知能力；洞察力|The ability to perceive or understand something.  It refers to the act of perceiving, often implying a deeper understanding or insight beyond simple sensory input.  The word is less common than its related verb "perceive".| Example: Her perceivance of the situation allowed her to defuse the conflict before it escalated.  她的洞察力使她能够在冲突升级之前将其化解。|近义词：perception, insight, understanding, discernment|反义词：obtuseness, unawareness, blindness|名词
