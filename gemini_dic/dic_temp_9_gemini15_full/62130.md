encircle:/ɪnˈsɜːrkl/ | vt. |环绕；包围；圈出 |To surround something completely; to draw a circle around something. |例句：The police encircled the building.  警察包围了那座建筑物。 |例句：Please encircle the correct answer. 请圈出正确的答案。 |近义词：surround, encompass, enclose |反义词：uncircle (uncommon), leave open


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The planets encircle the sun. = 行星环绕太阳运行。
The children encircle their teacher. = 孩子们围着老师。

一般过去时:
The troops encircled the enemy. = 部队包围了敌人。
She encircled the word with a red pen. = 她用红笔圈出了那个词。

现在完成时:

They have encircled the most important points on the map. = 他们在地图上圈出了最重要的几个点。
