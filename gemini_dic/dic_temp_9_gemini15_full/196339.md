sericin:/ˈsɛrɪsɪn/| n. |丝胶蛋白|A protein found in silk, forming a glue-like substance that binds the silk fibers together.  It's a complex mixture of glycoproteins with various biological activities.|Silk sericin is used in cosmetics and skincare products.|近义词：silk gum, sericine|反义词：无
