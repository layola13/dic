recultivates:/ˌriːˈkʌltɪveɪts/| vt.|重新耕作；重新培养；使复兴|To cultivate again; to restore to cultivation; to revive or renew.|例句：The farmer recultivates the exhausted land after a period of fallow.  = 农民在休耕一段时间后重新耕作贫瘠的土地。

例句：The government is trying to recultivate the lost art of traditional craftsmanship. = 政府正试图使失传的传统工艺复兴。


近义词：revive, regenerate, rehabilitate, restore


反义词：destroy, ruin, devastate


词性：动词
