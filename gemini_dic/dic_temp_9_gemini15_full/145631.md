overconstructing:/ˌoʊvər kənˈstrʌktɪŋ/ | v. |过度构建，过度设计|  The act of designing or building something with excessive complexity or detail, often leading to inefficiency or impracticality. It implies that the design or structure is unnecessarily complicated and may not serve its intended purpose effectively.  |例句：The software engineers were criticized for overconstructing the application, making it slow and difficult to maintain.  软件工程师因过度构建应用程序而受到批评，导致应用程序运行缓慢且难以维护。


例句：The architect's overconstructing of the bridge led to significant cost overruns and delays. 建筑师对桥梁的过度设计导致了大量的成本超支和延误。


近义词：overengineering, overcomplicating, overdesigning |反义词：simplifying, streamlining


词性:动词
