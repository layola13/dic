compressors:/kəmˈprɛsərz/| n.|压缩机|A machine that reduces the volume of a gas or vapor, typically by increasing its pressure.|例句：The air compressors are essential for inflating the tires.  空气压缩机对于充气轮胎至关重要。

例句：The refrigerator uses a compressor to cool the air inside.冰箱使用压缩机来冷却内部空气。


近义词：compressor pump, pressure machine |反义词：expander
