decouple:/ˌdiːˈkʌpəl/ | vt. | 解耦，分离 | To separate or disconnect something from something else.  It often refers to separating systems or processes so that changes in one do not affect the others. | 例句：We need to decouple the marketing department from the sales department to improve efficiency. 我们需要将市场部和销售部分开，以提高效率。

例句：The engineers decoupled the software from the hardware to make it more portable. 工程师们将软件与硬件分离，使其更易于移植。


近义词：separate, disconnect, disengage, isolate | 反义词：couple, connect, integrate, link | 词性:动词
