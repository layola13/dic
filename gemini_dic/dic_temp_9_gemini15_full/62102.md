enchanters:/ɪnˈtʃæntərz/| n. |魔法师；施法者|People who practice magic, especially those who use enchantments.  They often wield powerful spells and possess magical abilities.|The enchanters gathered in the ancient ruins to perform a powerful ritual.  魔法师们聚集在古老的废墟中进行强大的仪式。|magicians, sorcerers, wizards| (无明确反义词，可考虑non-magical people)|名词
