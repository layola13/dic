obdurate: /ˈɑːbdʒʊreɪt/ | adj. | 固执的；顽固的；冥顽不灵的 |  stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or course of action.  |  例句：Despite all our efforts, he remained obdurate in his refusal to cooperate. 尽管我们尽了最大努力，他还是顽固地拒绝合作。


例句：The obdurate defendant refused to plead guilty, even in the face of overwhelming evidence.  顽固的被告拒绝认罪，即使面对压倒性的证据也是如此。


近义词：stubborn, obstinate, inflexible, intractable, adamant | 反义词：yielding, compliant, flexible, amenable


词性：形容词


词源：源自拉丁语 *obdūrus,  来自 *ob- (表示“反对”，“对抗”) + *dūrus (“坚硬的”)。字面意思是指“难以屈服的”。
