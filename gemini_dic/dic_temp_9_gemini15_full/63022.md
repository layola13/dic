enrage:/ɪnˈreɪdʒ/| vt.|激怒，使愤怒|To cause (someone) to feel extreme anger.|His behavior enraged his parents.他的行为激怒了他的父母。The news enraged the public. 这则新闻激怒了公众。|近义词：infuriate, anger, provoke, irritate|反义词：calm, soothe, appease


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The news enrages him every time he hears it. = 他每次听到这个消息都会被激怒。
Loud noises enrage the baby. = 大声的噪音会激怒婴儿。


一般过去时:
The delay enraged the passengers. = 延误激怒了乘客们。
His rudeness enraged her. = 他的粗鲁无礼激怒了她。


现在完成时:
His constant complaints have enraged me. = 他不断的抱怨已经激怒我了。
This situation has enraged the community. = 这种情况激怒了整个社区。


过去完成时:
I had been enraged by his lies before he apologized. = 在他道歉之前，我已经被他那些谎话激怒了。
She had already been enraged by his behavior before the argument started. = 在争吵开始之前，她已经被他的行为激怒了。


将来时:
This news will enrage many people. = 这个消息将会激怒很多人。
His actions will surely enrage his boss. = 他的行为肯定会激怒他的老板。


虚拟语气:
If he knew the truth, it would enrage him. = 如果他知道真相，这会让他很生气。
I wish that wouldn't enrage him so much. = 我希望那不会让他如此生气。


被动语态:
He was enraged by her betrayal. = 他被她的背叛激怒了。
The public was enraged by the government's decision. = 公众被政府的决定激怒了。


疑问句:
What enraged you so much? = 是什么让你如此生气？
Does his behavior enrage you? = 他的行为激怒你了吗？


否定句:
The news did not enrage her. = 这则新闻并没有激怒她。
Nothing could enrage him. = 什么也无法激怒他。


PHRASE->
be enraged by = 被...激怒
enraged at = 对...感到愤怒
feel enraged = 感到愤怒
become enraged = 变得愤怒
utterly enraged = 极度愤怒
