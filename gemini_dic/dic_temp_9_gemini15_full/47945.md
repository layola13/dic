词源:
Decidability derives from the verb "decide," ultimately from the Latin word "decidere," meaning "to cut off," "to determine," or "to settle." The suffix "-ability" indicates the capacity or potential for something.  Therefore, decidability refers to the capacity to be decided or determined.
