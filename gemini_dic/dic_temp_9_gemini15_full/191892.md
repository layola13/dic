/skoʊliˈkɔːrməfə/| n. |寡毛纲|a subclass of annelids (segmented worms) characterized by having a clitellum (a thickened band of segments involved in reproduction) and typically lacking parapodia (lateral appendages).  They include earthworms and leeches.|Earthworms belong to the subclass Scolecormorpha.|近义词:Oligochaeta (often used interchangeably)|反义词:Polychaeta (possessing parapodia)
