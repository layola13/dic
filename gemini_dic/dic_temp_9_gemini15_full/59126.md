dynamisms:/ˈdaɪnəmɪzəm/| n. |活力；动力；动能；动态；发展变化|The inherent energy or force of a process or system; the quality of being dynamic.|例句：The dynamism of the city was evident in its bustling streets.  这座城市的活力在其繁华的街道上显而易见。

例句：The economic dynamism of the region attracted many investors.  该地区的经济活力吸引了许多投资者。


近义词：energy, force, vigor, momentum, activity, power|反义词：stagnation, inertia, inactivity, dormancy|词性：名词
