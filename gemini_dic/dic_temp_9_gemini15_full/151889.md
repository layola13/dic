passport:/ˈpɑːspɔːrt/ /ˈpæspɔːrt/| n. |护照|An official document issued by a government, certifying the holder's identity and citizenship, and entitling them to travel abroad.|例句：I need to renew my passport before my trip to Europe.  我要在去欧洲旅行之前续签护照。|例句：She showed her passport at the border control. 她在边境检查站出示了护照。|近义词：identification, visa|反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He needs a new passport. = 他需要一本新护照。
She shows her passport to the officer. = 她向警官出示护照。


一般过去时:
