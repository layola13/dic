fructuation:/fruːkˈteɪʃən/| n. |结果，结果实；结实；结果期|The process of bearing fruit; the production of fruit.  It can also refer to the period or season of fruit production.|例句：The fructuation of the apple trees was abundant this year. 今年苹果树的产量很丰富。

例句：The orchard experienced a period of high fructuation last autumn.  去年秋天果园经历了一段丰收期。


近义词：fruiting, bearing fruit, yield, harvest

反义词：barrenness, sterility, unproductiveness


词性：名词
