forwarded:/fɔːrˈwɔːrdɪd/ | v. | 转发的；转寄的 |  To send (something, such as a letter or email) to another person or address.  | 例句：I forwarded the email to my boss. 我把邮件转发给了我的老板。  | 例句：The message was forwarded to several recipients. 这条消息被转发给了几个收件人。 | 近义词：relayed, transmitted, passed on | 反义词：received, kept


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He forwards all his emails to his personal account. = 他把他所有的邮件都转发到他的私人账户。
She forwards important documents to the archive. = 她将重要文件转发到档案馆。


一般过去时:
I forwarded the email to John yesterday. = 我昨天把邮件转发给了约翰。
They forwarded the complaint to the manager. = 他们把投诉转给了经理。


现在完成时:
She has forwarded all the necessary documents. = 她已经转发了所有必要的文档。
He has forwarded the message to the relevant team. = 他已将消息转发给了相关团队。


过去完成时:
I had forwarded the email before he called. = 他打电话之前我已经转发了邮件。
They had forwarded the report before the meeting started. = 会议开始前他们已经转发了报告。


将来时:
I will forward the document as soon as I receive it. = 我一收到文件就转发。
He will forward the information to the client tomorrow. = 他明天会把信息转发给客户。


虚拟语气:
If I had known his email address, I would have forwarded the message. = 如果我知道他的邮箱地址，我就会把消息转发给他。
I wish I had forwarded that email sooner. = 我真希望我早点把那封邮件转发出去。


被动语态:
The email was forwarded to the wrong person. = 这封邮件被转发给了错误的人。
The message was being forwarded to multiple recipients. = 消息正在被转发给多个收件人。


疑问句:
Have you forwarded the document yet? = 你已经转发文件了吗？
Did he forward the email to the right person? = 他是否把邮件转发给了正确的人？


否定句:
I did not forward the email. = 我没有转发邮件。
She hasn't forwarded the report yet. = 她还没转发报告。


PHRASE->
forward a message = 转发信息
forward an email = 转发邮件
forward the request = 转发请求
forward the application = 转发申请
forward to someone = 转发给某人
