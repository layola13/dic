crawly:/ˈkrɔːli/| adj. |爬行的；令人毛骨悚然的|  Describing something that moves slowly by crawling, or something that is unpleasant and creepy.  |例句：The spider was a crawly creature. 那只蜘蛛是个爬行的生物。

例句：The old house had a crawly feeling. 那栋老房子有一种令人毛骨悚然的感觉。

近义词：creeping, creeping, creepy, creepy-crawly

反义词：smooth, sleek, swift


词性:形容词
