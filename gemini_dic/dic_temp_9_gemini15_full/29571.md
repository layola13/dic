catchwater: /ˈkætʃˌwɔːtər/ | n. |集水设施；排水沟；蓄水池 | A structure or system designed to collect and store rainwater or surface runoff.  It can refer to a ditch, reservoir, or other type of water management system. | Example: The farmer built a catchwater to irrigate his fields during dry spells.  农民建造了一个集水设施，以便在旱季灌溉他的田地。| 近义词：reservoir, drainage system, water collection system | 反义词：water dispersal system
