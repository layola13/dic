provocational:/ˌprɒvəˈkeɪʃənəl/| adj.|挑衅的；煽动的；刺激的 | tending to provoke or excite, especially to anger or violence; stimulating or exciting.  |例句：His provocative remarks ignited a heated debate.  他的挑衅性言论引发了一场激烈的辩论。
His provocative behavior led to a fight. 他的挑衅行为导致了一场争斗。|近义词：inflammatory, inciting, stimulating, agitating |反义词：soothing, calming, pacifying, conciliatory
