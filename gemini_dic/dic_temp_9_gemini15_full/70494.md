filariasis:/ˌfɪləˈraɪəsɪs/| n. |丝虫病|A disease caused by filarial worms, which are transmitted by mosquitoes and other insects.  The worms live in the lymphatic system and can cause swelling, particularly in the legs and arms (elephantiasis).| 例句：Millions of people worldwide are affected by filariasis. 全世界数百万人受到丝虫病的影响。| 例句：Lymphatic filariasis is a major public health problem in many tropical countries.  淋巴丝虫病是许多热带国家的主要公共卫生问题。| 近义词：elephantiasis (in reference to the swelling) | 反义词：None
