miniaturised:/ˌmɪniˈætʃəraɪzd/ | adj. | 小型化的；微型化的 |  Reduced in size; made smaller.  The process of making something smaller. | 例句：The company has miniaturised its new phone, making it more portable.  公司对其新款手机进行了小型化设计，使其更便携。

例句：The scientists miniaturised the experiment to fit inside a small container. 科学家们将实验小型化，以使其适合放置在一个小容器内。


近义词：downsized, reduced, shrunken

反义词：enlarged, magnified, amplified

词性: Adjective (形容词)


词源:  The word "miniaturised" is derived from the word "miniature," which ultimately comes from the Italian word "miniatura," referring to the art of illuminating manuscripts with small, detailed illustrations.  The suffix "-ised" (or "-ized" in American English) indicates the process of making something into a miniature.
