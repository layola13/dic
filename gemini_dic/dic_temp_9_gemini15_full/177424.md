rebandage:/ˌriːˈbændɪdʒ/| vt.|重新包扎；再包扎|To bandage again or anew.  The verb implies a previous bandaging that may have become loose, ineffective, or soiled.|例句：The nurse had to rebandage the patient's wound after it became soaked with blood. 护士不得不重新包扎病人的伤口，因为它被血浸透了。
例句：After the fall, he needed to rebandage his injured knee.  摔倒后，他需要重新包扎受伤的膝盖。|近义词：redress, rewrap|反义词：unbandage|动词
