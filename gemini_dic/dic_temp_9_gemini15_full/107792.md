lapping:/ˈlæpɪŋ/| v.|拍打；轻拍；重叠；舔舐|  To strike or move against something repeatedly with a gentle sound; to overlap; to lick repeatedly.| The waves were lapping gently against the shore.  海浪轻轻拍打着海岸。|The tiles on the roof were lapping over each other. 屋顶的瓦片互相重叠。| 近义词：splashing, washing, overlapping | 反义词：separating, detaching


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog is lapping up the spilled milk. = 狗正在舔舐洒出的牛奶。
The waves are lapping against the rocks. = 海浪拍打着岩石。

一般过去时:
The cat lapped up the cream. = 猫舔掉了奶油。
The water lapped at the base of the pier. = 水拍打着码头的底部。

现在完成时:
The waves have lapped away the sand. = 海浪已经冲走了沙子。
The dog has lapped up all the water in its bowl. = 狗已经舔掉了碗里所有的水。

过去完成时:
The tide had lapped over the sandcastle before we got there. = 我们到达之前，潮水已经漫过了沙堡。
The rain had lapped at the windowpane for hours. = 雨水已经拍打窗玻璃好几个小时了。

将来时:
The tide will lap against the seawall tomorrow. = 明天潮水会拍打防波堤。
The children will lap up the story. (Figurative use) = 孩子们会很喜欢这个故事。(比喻用法)

虚拟语气:
If the waves were lapping more gently, we could swim. = 如果海浪拍打得更轻柔一些，我们就能游泳。
I wish the rain would stop lapping against the window. = 我希望雨水能停止拍打窗户。

被动语态:
The shore was lapped by the gentle waves. = 温柔的海浪拍打着海岸。
The boat was lapped by the water during the storm. = 暴风雨期间，船被水拍打。

疑问句:
Is the water lapping at the edge of the boat? = 水是否拍打着船边？
Are the dogs lapping up the gravy? = 狗们是否在舔肉汁？

否定句:
The waves weren't lapping against the shore. = 海浪没有拍打海岸。
The cat didn't lap up all the milk. = 猫没有把所有的牛奶都舔掉。
