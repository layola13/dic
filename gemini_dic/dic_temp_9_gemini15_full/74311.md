fractural:/ˈfrækʃərəl/| adj.|断裂的；骨折的；裂缝的| Relating to or characterized by fracture or fractures.  The term often describes something broken or cracked, especially in a geological or medical context.|例句：The geologist examined the fractural surfaces of the rock. 地质学家检查了岩石的断裂面。

例句：The patient suffered a fractural injury to his leg. 病人腿部受了骨折伤。

近义词：broken, cracked, fractured, fissured

反义词：intact, unbroken, whole

词性:形容词
