hop:/hɑːp/ /hɒp/| n., v.|跳跃；(啤酒的)啤酒花；|to move by jumping on one leg; a short jump; the dried flower of the hop plant used in brewing beer. |例句：The rabbit hopped across the field. 兔子蹦蹦跳跳地穿过田野。|例句：She added hops to the beer. 她往啤酒里加了啤酒花。|近义词：jump, leap, skip, bound|反义词：walk, crawl


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He hops on one leg. = 他单腿跳。
The frog hops from lily pad to lily pad. = 青蛙从一个睡莲叶跳到另一个睡莲叶。


一般过去时:
The kangaroo hopped away. = 袋鼠跳走了。
I hopped over the fence. = 我跳过了栅栏。


现在完成时:
He has hopped over many hurdles in his life. = 他一生中克服了许多障碍。
The rabbit has hopped into the burrow. = 兔子跳进了洞穴。


过去完成时:
The bird had hopped onto the branch before I noticed it. = 我注意到之前，鸟已经跳到树枝上了。
She had hopped off the train before it came to a complete stop. = 火车完全停下来之前她就跳下车了。


将来时:
I will hop on the bus when it arrives. = 公共汽车来了我就上车。
He will hop over the puddle. = 他会跳过水坑。


虚拟语气:
If I were a grasshopper, I would hop all day long. = 如果我是蚱蜢，我会整天跳来跳去。
I wish I could hop as high as that kangaroo. = 我真希望我能像那只袋鼠一样跳得那么高。


被动语态:
The ball was hopped across the court. = 球在球场上被跳着传递。
(Hop is rarely used passively)


疑问句:
Can you hop on one leg? = 你能单腿跳吗？
Did the child hop across the road? = 孩子跳过了马路吗？


否定句:
He didn't hop over the wall. = 他没有跳过墙。
The frog didn't hop very far. = 青蛙没有跳得很远。


PHRASE->

hop in = 跳进去 (e.g., Hop in the car!)
hop on = 跳上 (e.g., Hop on the bus!)
hop off = 跳下 (e.g., Hop off the train!)
hop to it = 加油 (e.g., Hop to it, we're running late!)
hop over = 跳过 (e.g., Hop over the fence)
