epicene:/ˈɛpɪsiːn/| adj.| 中性的；雌雄同体的；兼指两性的 |  Referring to a word or name that can be used for both sexes, or to a person or animal exhibiting characteristics of both sexes.  It often implies a lack of clear gender distinction or a deliberate avoidance of gender specification. | Example:  The term "flight attendant" is often considered epicene, replacing the gender-specific terms "steward" and "stewardess."  The plant displayed epicene characteristics, exhibiting both male and female reproductive structures. | 近义词：neutral, unisex, androgynous | 反义词：masculine, feminine
