happen:/ˈhæpən/ | /ˈhæpən/ | v. |发生，出现|To take place; to occur; to come to pass.  It implies a lack of intention or planning. |例句：What happened at the party last night? 昨晚的派对发生了什么？|例句：An accident happened on the highway. 高速公路上发生了一起事故。|近义词：occur, take place, come about |反义词：plan, design


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Something unexpected happens every day. = 每天都会发生一些意想不到的事情。
It often happens that I forget my keys. = 我经常忘记带钥匙。


一般过去时:
What happened to your car? = 你的车怎么了？
The accident happened quickly. = 事故发生得很突然。


现在完成时:
Nothing much has happened since I last saw you. = 自从上次见到你以来，没什么大不了的事情发生。
Many changes have happened in the city. = 这个城市发生了很多变化。


过去完成时:
Before I knew it, the accident had already happened. = 在我意识到之前，事故已经发生了。
I realized that something bad had happened. = 我意识到发生了不好的事情。


将来时:
What do you think will happen next? = 你认为接下来会发生什么？
I hope nothing bad will happen. = 我希望不会发生什么不好的事情。


虚拟语气:
If it hadn't happened, I would be in trouble. = 如果这件事没有发生，我就会有麻烦了。
I wish that hadn't happened. = 我希望那件事没有发生。


被动语态:
The accident was reported to the police. = 事故已被报告给警方。
The meeting was unexpectedly happened. = 会议意外地发生了。


疑问句:
What happened? = 发生了什么？
What will happen if we fail? = 如果我们失败了会发生什么？


否定句:
Nothing happened. = 什么事都没发生。
The meeting didn't happen as scheduled. = 会议没有按计划进行。


PHRASE->
happen to = 碰巧
happen upon/on = 偶然发现
what happened to...? = ...怎么了？
as it happens = 碰巧
it so happens that = 碰巧
