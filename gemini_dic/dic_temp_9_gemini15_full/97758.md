initiations:/ˌɪnɪʃiˈeɪʃənz/| n.| 开始；入会仪式；启蒙；|The act of beginning something.  It can also refer to a ceremony or rite marking admission into a group or organization. |例句：The initiations into the secret society were shrouded in mystery.  秘密社团的入会仪式笼罩在神秘之中。

例句：The initiations for the new employees included a week-long training program. 新员工的入门培训包括为期一周的培训项目。


近义词： commencements, beginnings, introductions, enrollments

反义词： conclusions, endings, terminations


词性: 名词
