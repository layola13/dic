rarefaction:/ˌreərəˈfækʃən/| n. |稀疏；稀薄；减压|The process of becoming less dense; the state of being less dense.  It often refers to the decrease in the density of a gas or the thinning of a substance.|例句：The rarefaction of the air at high altitudes makes breathing difficult. 高海拔地区空气的稀薄使得呼吸困难。

例句：The rarefaction of the population in rural areas is a growing concern. 农村地区人口稀疏是一个日益严重的问题。


近义词：thinning, attenuation, dilution|反义词：condensation, compression, densification|词性：名词
