nonsolar: /ˈnɒnˌsoʊlər/ | adj. |非太阳能的，非太阳的|Not relating to, using, or produced by the sun.  This word is a combination of "non-" (meaning not) and "solar" (relating to the sun).|例句：The nonsolar energy sources are being explored to reduce our dependence on fossil fuels.  非太阳能资源正在被探索以减少我们对化石燃料的依赖。


The company is investing heavily in nonsolar power generation. 这家公司正在大力投资非太阳能发电。

近义词：non-solar, sunless (in some contexts)

反义词：solar


词性：形容词
