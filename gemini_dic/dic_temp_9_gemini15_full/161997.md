prattlers:/ˈprætələrz/| n. |喋喋不休的人，饶舌者|People who talk excessively and often about unimportant things.  They are characterized by their continuous and often frivolous conversation.|The prattlers at the party kept everyone else from enjoying the music.|近义词：chatterboxes, gossips, blabbermouths|反义词：listeners, quiet people
