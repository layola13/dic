projected:/prəˈdʒɛktɪd/|/ˈprɒdʒɛktɪd/| adj. v. |预计的；计划中的；投射的；凸出的|  Projected sales for next year are very optimistic.  预计明年的销售额非常乐观。 The image was projected onto the screen. 图像被投射到屏幕上。|近义词：anticipated, forecast, estimated, thrown, cast|反义词：actual, real, past, unprojected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They project the film onto a large screen. = 他们把电影投射到一个大屏幕上。
We project our hopes and dreams onto the future. = 我们把希望和梦想投射到未来。


一般过去时:
The company projected a loss for the quarter. = 公司预计本季度亏损。
The teacher projected the slide onto the wall. = 老师把幻灯片投射到墙上。


现在完成时:

Scientists have projected the path of the hurricane. = 科学家们已经预测了飓风的路径。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
