shelter:/ˈʃɛltər/| n. v.|住所，庇护；庇护，掩蔽|A shelter from the storm.  A place offering protection from weather, danger, or attack. |The hikers sought shelter from the sudden downpour.  徒步旅行者寻求躲避突如其来的暴雨。
The family found shelter in a nearby cave.  这家人在一个附近的洞穴里找到了庇护。|refuge, haven, sanctuary|exposure, danger|名词
