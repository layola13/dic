no-doubt:/ˈnoʊ daʊt/ | adj. adv. |毫无疑问地，确定无疑地 |  Certainly; undoubtedly.  It expresses strong confidence or certainty about something. |例句：He will no doubt succeed. 他毫无疑问会成功。

例句：No doubt he'll be late. 他肯定要迟到。


近义词：certainly, undoubtedly, surely, definitely, unquestionably

反义词：doubtfully, uncertainly, possibly, perhaps


词性：形容词和副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There is no doubt he is intelligent. = 他很聪明，这是毫无疑问的。
No doubt, the sun rises in the east. = 太阳从东方升起，这是毫无疑问的。


一般过去时:
There was no doubt he had lied. = 他撒谎了，这是毫无疑问的。
No doubt, she felt relieved after the exam. = 考试后她如释重负，这是毫无疑问的。


现在完成时:
There has been no doubt about his guilt. = 他有罪，这是毫无疑问的。
No doubt, they have finished the project. = 他们毫无疑问已经完成了项目。


过去完成时:
There had been no doubt that she would win. = 她会赢，这是毫无疑问的。
No doubt, they had already left before we arrived. = 我们到达之前他们已经离开了，这是毫无疑问的。


将来时:
There will be no doubt about his success. = 他会成功，这是毫无疑问的。
No doubt, she will pass the test. = 她毫无疑问会通过考试。


虚拟语气:
There would be no doubt about it if we had more evidence. = 如果我们有更多证据，那就毫无疑问了。
No doubt, he would have won if he had trained harder. = 如果他训练更努力，毫无疑问他会赢。


被动语态:
His guilt was no doubt established by the evidence. = 他的罪行已被证据确凿地证实。
The solution to the problem is no doubt being sought. = 问题的解决方案无疑正在被寻找。


疑问句:
Is there any doubt about his honesty? = 他的诚实还有疑问吗？（反问，表达肯定）
No doubt, you know the answer, right? = 你知道答案，对吧？（反问，表达肯定）


否定句:
There is some doubt about the accuracy of the report. = 报告的准确性有些疑问。
I doubt that he will come. = 我怀疑他会来。
