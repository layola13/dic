/ˈwɛrtɑː/| n.|果园；菜园；园圃|A huerta is a garden, orchard, or vegetable patch, especially one in Spain or Latin America, often irrigated and intensively cultivated.  It typically features a wide variety of crops grown close together.  Huertas are often associated with traditional farming methods and sustainable agriculture.| The huerta of Valencia is famous for its orange groves.  瓦伦西亚的园圃以它的橘子林而闻名。| orchard, garden, vegetable garden| desert, wasteland| 名词
