pyrethrine:/paɪˈriːθrɪn/ | n. |除虫菊酯|A natural insecticide derived from the flowers of certain chrysanthemum species.  It's commonly used in household insecticides and agricultural pest control. Pyrethrins are relatively safe for mammals, but toxic to insects.|例句：Many natural insecticides contain pyrethrine, a compound extracted from chrysanthemum flowers.  许多天然杀虫剂含有除虫菊酯，这是一种从菊花中提取的化合物。|例句：The effectiveness of pyrethrine as an insecticide depends on factors such as temperature and humidity. 除虫菊酯作为杀虫剂的有效性取决于温度和湿度等因素。|近义词：insecticide, pesticide |反义词：N/A
