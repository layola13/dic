gelatines:/ˈdʒɛlətiːnz/ | n. |明胶（复数）|A protein substance obtained from animal tissues by boiling, used in food and photography.  It's a colorless, flavorless, and nearly tasteless substance. |例句：The chef used gelatines to set the mousse.  厨师用明胶来凝固慕斯。|例句：These photographic plates are coated with a sensitive gelatine emulsion. 这些照相底片涂有灵敏的明胶乳剂。|近义词：collagen, gelatin |反义词：None  |名词
