silvering: /ˈsɪlvərɪŋ/ | n. v. |镀银；银色；变银色 |The act of covering something with silver or giving something a silvery appearance.  |例句：The silvering of the leaves indicated the onset of autumn. 秋叶泛银光表明秋天到来了。 The old mirror's silvering had worn away. 旧镜子的镀银层磨损了。 |近义词：plating, silver-plating,  coating |反义词：oxidizing, tarnishing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The craftsman is silvering the jewelry. = 工匠正在给首饰镀银。
The moon silvers the snow. = 月光使雪地泛着银光。


一般过去时:
They silvered the handles of the cutlery. = 他们给餐具把手镀了银。
The frost silvered the windowpanes. = 霜使窗玻璃泛着银光。
