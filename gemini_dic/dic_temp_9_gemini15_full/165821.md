preobvious:/ˌpriːˈɒbviəs/ | adj. |  显而易见的，过分明显的 |  Clearly obvious; excessively or unnecessarily obvious.  It suggests something is so obvious it is almost irritating or condescending to point it out. |The preobvious nature of his explanation made the audience restless.  他的解释过于明显，使得观众坐立不安。 | obvious, apparent, self-evident | subtle, obscure, inconspicuous | 形容词
