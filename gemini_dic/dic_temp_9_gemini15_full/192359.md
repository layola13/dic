scrip:/skrɪpt/| n.| 剧本；手稿；字迹；(股票)认购证 | A written text for a play, film, broadcast, etc.  A written or printed document. |例句：The script for the movie was excellent.  电影剧本写得很棒。

The doctor's handwriting on the prescription was barely legible; the script was messy. 医生处方上的字迹几乎难以辨认；字迹很乱。


近义词：manuscript, text, document | 反义词：improvisation, ad-lib


词源:  Middle English: from Old French *escript, past participle of escrire ‘to write’, from Latin scribere.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The playwright is writing a new script. = 剧作家正在写一个新的剧本。
This script needs more dialogue. = 这个剧本需要更多对话。


一般过去时:
He wrote the script in just three weeks. = 他只用了三个星期就写完了剧本。

现在完成时:
She has finished writing the script. = 她已经写完了剧本。
They have already revised the script several times. = 他们已经修改了剧本好几次了。


过去完成时:

By the time the director arrived, they had already reviewed the script. = 当导演到达时，他们已经审查了剧本。
