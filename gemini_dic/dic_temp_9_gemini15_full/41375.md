corkwood: /ˈkɔːrkˌwʊd/ | n. |软木树, 软木|  A light, soft wood, typically from trees of the genus *Leitneria*, characterized by its corky texture. It is used for various purposes, often related to its buoyancy and insulation properties.  |Example:  The raft was constructed from lightweight corkwood, ensuring it floated easily.  软木筏的制作使用了轻便的软木，确保其轻松漂浮。|近义词: balsa,  (depending on context)  |反义词:  hardwood, dense wood |名词
