escalade:/ˌɛskəˈleɪd/ | n. v. | 逐步升级；（尤指暴力或冲突的）加剧；攀登；强行通过 |  A gradual increase, especially in intensity or violence; the act of climbing or ascending; to force one's way through. |

例句：

The protests escalated into violence.  = 抗议活动升级为暴力事件。

The conflict escalated rapidly after the border incident. = 边境事件后冲突迅速升级。


近义词：intensify, escalate, heighten, aggravate, exacerbate

反义词：de-escalate, decrease, lessen, mitigate


词性：

名词和动词


词源：

源自法语 *escalader*,  来自 *escale* ("ladder, stairs"),  最终源自拉丁语 *scala* ("ladder").  因此，其本义与攀爬有关，引申出逐步升级的含义。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tension escalates daily. = 紧张局势每天都在升级。
The company escalates its marketing efforts. = 公司正在加大营销力度。

一般过去时:
The situation escalated quickly. = 局势迅速恶化。
The argument escalated into a fight. = 争吵升级为打斗。

现在完成时:
The conflict has escalated significantly. = 冲突已显著升级。
Tensions have escalated since the election. = 自选举以来，紧张局势有所升级。

过去完成时:

The dispute had escalated to a point of no return. = 争端已经升级到无法挽回的地步。

将来时:
The situation will likely escalate further. = 局势很可能会进一步恶化。

虚拟语气:

The situation is being escalated by both sides. = 双方都在加剧局势。
