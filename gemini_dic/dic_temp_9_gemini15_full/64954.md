esthetes:/ˈɛsθiːts/| n. |审美家；唯美主义者 |A person who is highly sensitive to beauty, especially in art and nature.  Someone who values and appreciates beauty, especially in art. |例句：He is an esthete who spends hours admiring the details of Renaissance paintings. 他是一位审美家，会花数小时欣赏文艺复兴时期绘画的细节。

例句：Many esthetes consider the architecture of the building to be a masterpiece.  许多审美家认为这栋建筑的建筑风格是一部杰作。


近义词：connoisseur, aesthete, art lover |反义词： philistine, barbarian, boor
