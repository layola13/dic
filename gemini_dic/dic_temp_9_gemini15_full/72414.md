fluffed:/flʌft/| vt. n.|弄乱；蓬松；失败；(表演)失败|to make something, especially hair or a piece of clothing, look soft and full of air; to fail to do something properly or successfully |例句：She fluffed her lines during the play. 她在那场戏中忘词了。  He fluffed his hair to make it look fuller. 他把头发弄蓬松，使它看起来更浓密。|近义词：mess up, botch, bungle|反义词：perfect, succeed
