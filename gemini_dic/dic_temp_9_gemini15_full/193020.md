seconding:/ˈsɛkəndɪŋ/ | v. | 秒表计时；支持；附议；同意；派遣；调动 | To express support for a proposal or motion already made; to support or agree with.  |例句：I second the motion. 我附议这项动议。

例句：Several council members seconded his proposal.  几个议员附议了他的提案。


近义词：supporting, endorsing, agreeing with, backing, advocating

反义词：opposing, disagreeing with, rejecting, vetoing


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He seconds every motion. 他总是附议每一个动议。
She seconds her colleague's ideas. 她赞同同事的想法。

一般过去时:
He seconded the amendment. 他附议了修正案。
She seconded his nomination. 她附议了他的提名。

现在完成时:
They have seconded the resolution. 他们已经附议了决议。
He has seconded many important proposals. 他已经附议了许多重要的提案。


过去完成时:
She had seconded the motion before the vote.  她在投票前就已附议了该动议。
They had seconded the plan before the meeting. 他们在会议前就已附议了该计划。

将来时:
I will second his motion tomorrow. 我明天将附议他的动议。
She will second the proposal at the next meeting. 她将在下次会议上附议该提案。

虚拟语气:
If I were there, I would second his proposal. 如果我在那里，我会附议他的提案。
I wish I could have seconded the motion. 我真希望我能附议该动议。

被动语态:
His motion was seconded by many members. 他的动议得到了许多成员的附议。
The resolution was seconded unanimously. 该决议获得了一致附议。

疑问句:
Will anyone second the motion? 有人会附议这项动议吗？
Did anyone second the amendment? 有人附议了修正案吗？

否定句:
No one seconded the proposal. 没有人附议该提案。
He did not second the motion. 他没有附议该动议。
