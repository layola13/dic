nonpreferences:/ˌnɒnˈprɛfərənsɪz/ | n. |非偏好；不受欢迎的事物 | Things that are not preferred; things that are disliked or unwanted.  They represent choices or options that are actively avoided or considered less desirable.  The concept often arises in contexts like market research, consumer behavior, and decision-making, where understanding what people *don't* want is as important as understanding what they *do* want. | Example: The market research revealed several nonpreferences among consumers, such as the product's high price and limited color options.  The study of consumer nonpreferences revealed a strong dislike for the new packaging design. | 近义词: dislikes, aversions, objections, dispreferences | 反义词: preferences, favorites, desires
