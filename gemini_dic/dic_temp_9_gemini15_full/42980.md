coveters:/ˈkʌvɪtərz/ | n. | 贪婪者；渴望者 | People who intensely desire something that belongs to someone else.  They are often driven by envy and a sense of entitlement. |例句：The coveters eyed the neighbor's new car with resentment. 贪婪的人们带着怨恨注视着邻居的新车。  The coveters of power often resort to underhanded tactics. 渴望权力的人常常采取卑鄙的手段。|近义词：enviers,  longers,  cravers |反义词：givers, sharers |名词
