lenslet:/ˈlɛnzlɪt/| n. |小透镜|A lenslet is a small lens, often one of many in an array.  They are used in various optical systems to focus light or images.  Lenslets are commonly found in micro-optical systems and imaging devices.| Example: The camera uses an array of lenslets to capture a wide field of view.  这个相机使用一系列小透镜来捕捉宽广的视野。|近义词：miniature lens, micro-lens |反义词：large lens, macro lens |名词
