mandating:/ˈmændˌeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 命令；授权；委托 |  The act of making something mandatory or officially required.  It implies an authoritative order or instruction. | 例句：The new law mandating seat belt use has significantly reduced traffic fatalities. = 新的法规强制使用安全带，大大减少了交通事故死亡人数。

例句：The company is mandating the use of new software for all employees. = 公司强制要求所有员工使用新软件。


近义词：requiring, ordering, directing, compelling, enforcing


反义词：prohibiting, forbidding, allowing, permitting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government mandates regular safety inspections. = 政府强制要求定期进行安全检查。
The school mandates uniforms for all students. = 学校强制要求所有学生穿校服。

一般过去时:
The court mandated the release of the prisoner. = 法院强制要求释放囚犯。
The doctor mandated bed rest for the patient. = 医生强制要求病人卧床休息。

现在完成时:
They have mandated a new set of rules for the competition. = 他们已经强制实施了一套新的比赛规则。
The company has mandated a new dress code. = 公司已经强制实施了新的着装规定。

过去完成时:
The judge had mandated a thorough investigation before the trial. = 在审判之前，法官已经强制要求进行彻底调查。
The principal had mandated that all students participate in the event. = 校长已经强制要求所有学生参加此次活动。


将来时:
The council will mandate stricter building codes. = 理事会将强制执行更严格的建筑规范。
The company will mandate the use of a new security system. = 公司将强制使用新的安全系统。


虚拟语气:
If the board mandated the changes, we would have to comply. = 如果董事会强制要求这些更改，我们就必须遵守。
I wish the government mandated a higher minimum wage. = 我希望政府强制执行更高的最低工资。


被动语态:
New regulations are being mandated by the state. = 新的法规正在由州政府强制实施。
The use of protective gear was mandated by the safety committee. = 安全委员会强制要求使用防护装备。


疑问句:
Is the company mandating overtime work? = 公司是否强制要求加班？
Will the government mandate stricter environmental controls? = 政府是否会强制执行更严格的环境控制措施？


否定句:
The law does not mandate the disclosure of that information. = 法律不要求披露该信息。
The school did not mandate the use of textbooks. = 学校没有强制要求使用教科书。


PHRASE->

mandate compliance = 强制遵守
mandate reforms = 推行改革
