colophon:/ˈkɒləfɒn/|/ˈkɑːləfɑːn/| n. |书末说明；出版说明；印记|A colophon is a statement at the end of a book giving details of its publication, such as the printer, publisher, date, and place of publication.  It can also include information about the typeface, paper, and binding.  In earlier times, colophons were often more elaborate and included artistic elements. |The colophon of the book proudly announced its printing by the Gutenberg press.  这本书的书末说明自豪地宣布它是由古腾堡印刷机印刷的。|imprint, publisher's note|  (近义词无明确反义词)|名词
