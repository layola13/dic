reverers:/rɪˈvɪərərz/| n. |敬畏者，崇拜者 |Those who deeply respect and admire someone or something.  They hold the object of their reverence in high esteem and often show devotion or worship. |Example: The reverers of the ancient gods left offerings at the temple.  敬畏古代神灵的人们在庙宇里留下供品。  The reverers of Gandhi continue to study his teachings. 甘地的崇拜者们继续学习他的教诲。|近义词：admirers, worshippers, devotees, venerators |反义词：despisers, scorners, critics

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The reverers praise the saint daily. = 敬畏者每天赞美圣徒。
Many people revere their ancestors. = 许多人敬畏他们的祖先。


一般过去时:
The ancient people reverenced the sun. = 古代人崇拜太阳。
The crowd revered the king's arrival. = 人群敬仰国王的到来。


现在完成时:
People have revered this mountain for centuries. = 人们几个世纪以来一直敬畏这座山。
She has revered her teacher's wisdom throughout her life. = 她一生都敬仰她老师的智慧。


过去完成时:
They had revered the emperor before the revolution. = 革命前他们敬仰皇帝。
The villagers had revered the old shaman for decades. = 村民们几十年来一直敬畏那位老萨满。


将来时:
Future generations will revere his contributions to science. = 后代将敬仰他对科学的贡献。
We will revere this moment in history forever. = 我们将永远铭记历史上的这一刻。


虚拟语气:
If I were a reverer of art, I would visit the museum more often. = 如果我是艺术的崇拜者，我会更经常去博物馆。
I wish I could have revered my grandfather more when he was alive. = 我希望在我爷爷在世的时候，我能更多地敬仰他。


被动语态:
The hero was revered by the entire nation. = 这位英雄受到全国人民的敬仰。
His work is revered by scholars worldwide. = 他的作品受到世界各地学者的敬仰。


疑问句:
Do you revere any historical figures? = 你敬仰任何历史人物吗？
Who did they revere in ancient times? = 古代人敬仰谁？


否定句:
They did not revere the new leader. = 他们不敬仰新领导。
I don't revere violence. = 我不崇尚暴力。
