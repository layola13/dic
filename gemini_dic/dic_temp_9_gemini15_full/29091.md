caryatides:/kæriˈætɪdiːz/| n. |女像柱，负重女像 |Sculptural representations of women serving as architectural supports, typically in classical Greek architecture.  They are often depicted draped in flowing robes and bearing the weight of an entablature. |The ancient Greek temple was adorned with magnificent caryatides supporting the roof.  古希腊神庙装饰着支撑屋顶的宏伟女像柱。|近义词：female figures, supporting columns|反义词：male figures, columns
