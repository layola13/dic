previsits:/ˈpriːvɪzɪts/| n.|预先访问；事先探访|Visits made in advance, typically to assess a situation or location before a more substantial visit.  It often implies a preliminary inspection or survey.|例句：The team conducted previsits to several potential project sites to evaluate their suitability.  团队对几个潜在的项目地点进行了预先访问，以评估其是否合适。


例句：The architect made previsits to the construction site to ensure everything was proceeding as planned. 建筑师对施工现场进行了预先访问，以确保一切按计划进行。


近义词：advance visits, preliminary visits, reconnaissance visits


反义词：follow-up visits, subsequent visits


词性:名词
