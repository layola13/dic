outflanked:/ˌaʊtˈflæŋkt/| v.|侧翼攻击，绕过；智胜 |To attack (an enemy) on the flank or sides, especially by a turning movement.|The general outflanked the enemy, leading to their defeat.将军侧翼攻击敌人，导致了他们的失败。|The clever lawyer outflanked his opponent in court.这位聪明的律师在法庭上智胜了他的对手。|近义词：outmaneuver, circumvent, bypass |反义词：be flanked, be outmaneuvered

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They outflank their competitors with innovative products. = 他们用创新产品超越竞争对手。
The army outflanks the enemy position. = 军队绕过敌军阵地。


一般过去时:
Napoleon outflanked the Austrian army at Austerlitz. = 拿破仑在奥斯特里茨战役中侧翼攻击了奥地利军队。
The rebels outflanked the government forces. = 反叛者绕过了政府军。


现在完成时:
Our marketing team has outflanked the competition this year. = 我们的营销团队今年已经超越了竞争对手。
The company has outflanked its rivals by adopting new technologies. = 这家公司通过采用新技术超越了竞争对手。


过去完成时:
The general realized that the enemy had outflanked his troops. = 将军意识到敌人已经绕过了他的部队。
They discovered that the enemy had outflanked their defenses before the attack. = 他们在袭击前发现敌人已经绕过了他们的防线。


将来时:
We will outflank our opponents by launching a new product line. = 我们将通过推出新的产品线来超越对手。
The company plans to outflank its rivals in the coming year. = 该公司计划在来年超越其竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If we had outflanked them earlier, we would have won the battle. = 如果我们早点绕过他们，我们就赢得了战斗。
If only we had outflanked the opposition, the deal would have been ours. = 如果我们能绕过反对派，这笔交易就属于我们了。


被动语态:
The enemy was outflanked by a clever tactical maneuver. = 敌人被一次巧妙的战术机动所超越。
Their strategy was outflanked by a sudden change in the market. = 他们的策略被市场的突然变化所超越。


疑问句:
How did they manage to outflank the enemy? = 他们是如何成功地绕过敌人的？
Can we outflank the opposition in this negotiation? = 我们能在这次谈判中超越对手吗？


否定句:
We did not outflank the enemy; they were too well-prepared. = 我们没有绕过敌人；他们准备得太充分了。
The company failed to outflank its competitors in the market. = 这家公司未能超越市场上的竞争对手。
