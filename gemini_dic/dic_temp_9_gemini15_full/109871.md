logic:/ˈlɒdʒɪk/ /ˈlɑːdʒɪk/| n. |逻辑，逻辑学，推理 |The study of the principles of valid reasoning. Logic helps us to think clearly and systematically.  |Example:  His argument lacked logic.  他的论点缺乏逻辑性。
The professor taught a course in formal logic. 教授教了一门形式逻辑课。|近义词：reasoning, rationality |反义词：illogic, irrationality |名词
