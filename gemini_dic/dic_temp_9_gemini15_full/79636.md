glossier:/ˈɡlɒsiər/| adj.|光滑的；有光泽的；有光彩的|  Describes something that has a smooth, shiny surface, reflecting light well. It can also refer to something that is impressive or attractive.  |例句：The glossier the paint, the more expensive it is. 光泽度越高的油漆越贵。

The new car had a glossier finish than the old one. 新车比旧车漆面更光亮。|近义词：shiny, lustrous, polished, gleaming|反义词：dull, matte, rough
