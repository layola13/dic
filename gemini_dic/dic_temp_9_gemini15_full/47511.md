dears:/dɪərz/| n.|亲爱的们，各位|A term of endearment used to address a group of people, often family members or close friends.  It's a plural form of "dear."|Example: "Come on, dears, dinner's ready!"  亲爱的们，晚饭准备好了！|近义词：loved ones, my friends, my darlings |反义词:N/A
