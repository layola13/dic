encolure:/ɑ̃kɔˈlʏʁ/ | n. |衣领；颈部；（服装的）领口 |The encolure is the neckline of a garment, the part that frames the face and neck.  It can vary widely in style and shape. |The dress had a beautiful, plunging encolure. 这件裙子有一个漂亮的低领口。 The high encolure of her blouse was quite conservative. 她衬衫的高领口相当保守。|neckline, collar |high neckline |名词
