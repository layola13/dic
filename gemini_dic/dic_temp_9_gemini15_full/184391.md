respondent: /rɪˈspɑːndənt/ | n. | 回答者；被告；受访者 | A person who responds to something, especially a survey, questionnaire, or interview.  Also, in legal contexts, the defendant in a lawsuit. |

例句：

The researchers analyzed the data from the respondents to the survey.  研究人员分析了受访者调查问卷的数据。

The respondent in the case denied all charges.  该案被告否认所有指控。


近义词：answerer, replyer, interviewee, defendant

反义词：questioner, plaintiff


词性：名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The respondent answers the questions. 回答者回答问题。
Many respondents complete the survey. 许多受访者完成了调查问卷。

一般过去时:
The respondent answered all the questions truthfully. 回答者如实回答了所有问题。
The respondent filed a counterclaim against the plaintiff. 被告对原告提出反诉。

现在完成时:
The respondent has provided all the necessary information. 回答者已提供所有必要信息。
Have all the respondents submitted their answers? 所有受访者都提交了他们的答案了吗？

过去完成时:
The respondent had already completed the survey before the deadline. 回答者在截止日期前已经完成了调查。
The judge had reviewed the respondent's testimony before making a decision. 法官在做出判决之前已经审查了被告的证词。

将来时:
The respondent will receive a follow-up questionnaire. 回答者将收到后续问卷。
The respondent will likely appeal the court's decision. 被告可能会对法院的判决提出上诉。

虚拟语气:
If the respondent were more cooperative, the investigation would progress faster. 如果被告更配合，调查就会进展得更快。
I wish the respondent had been more forthcoming with information. 我希望被告能提供更多信息。

被动语态:
The questions were answered by the respondent. 问题由回答者回答。
The survey data was collected from the respondents. 调查数据是从受访者那里收集的。

疑问句:
Who is the respondent in this case?  本案的被告是谁？
How many respondents participated in the study?  有多少受访者参与了这项研究？

否定句:
The respondent did not provide a satisfactory answer. 回答者没有提供令人满意的答案。
Not all respondents answered all the questions.并非所有受访者都回答了所有问题。

词源：

The word "respondent" comes from the Latin word "respondere," meaning "to answer" or "to reply."  The suffix "-ent" indicates an agent or doer of the action.
