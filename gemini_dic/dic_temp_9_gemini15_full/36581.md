collated:/kəˈleɪtɪd/ | v. | 对照；校对；整理；汇编 | To bring together different items into a single collection, often in an organized way.  |  例句：The researcher collated the data from multiple sources. 研究人员将来自多个来源的数据整理在一起。

例句：The librarian collated the books according to their subject matter. 图书管理员按主题整理书籍。


近义词：assemble, gather, compile, collect | 反义词：disperse, scatter


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He collates the documents every week. 他每周都整理文件。
She collates information for her reports. 她为她的报告收集信息。


一般过去时:
They collated the results of the survey. 他们整理了调查结果。
The team collated the data before publishing the report. 团队在发布报告之前整理了数据。


现在完成时:
I have collated all the necessary documents. 我已经整理好了所有必要的文档。
She has collated the research findings into a single report. 她已将研究结果汇编成一份报告。


过去完成时:
He had collated the evidence before presenting his case. 他在陈述案情之前已整理好证据。
They had collated the responses before analyzing the data. 他们在分析数据之前已整理好回复。


将来时:
We will collate the information next week. 我们下周将整理这些信息。
She will collate the data for the presentation. 她将为演示文稿整理数据。


虚拟语气:
If I had collated the data properly, the report would have been more accurate. 如果我正确地整理了数据，报告就会更准确。
I wish I had collated the information earlier. 我希望我早点整理好这些信息。


被动语态:
The data was collated by the research team. 数据由研究小组整理。
The documents are being collated for the upcoming meeting. 文件正在为即将举行的会议进行整理。


疑问句:
Have you collated the results yet? 你整理好结果了吗？
Will the team collate the data before the deadline? 团队会在截止日期前整理好数据吗？


否定句:
He did not collate the information correctly. 他没有正确地整理信息。
They have not yet collated all the responses. 他们还没有整理所有回复。


PHRASE->

词源:
From the Latin word “collatus,” past participle of “colligere,” meaning "to gather together."
