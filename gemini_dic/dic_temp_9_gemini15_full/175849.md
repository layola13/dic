rasterization:/ˌræstəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 光栅化；栅格化 | The process of converting vector graphics into a raster image (bitmap).  This involves sampling the vector data at regular intervals and assigning a color value to each sample point, creating a grid of pixels.  This is essential for displaying vector graphics on a screen or printer, which are raster devices. | Example:  The software performs rasterization to display the vector drawing on the screen.  软件执行光栅化以在屏幕上显示矢量图形。


|近义词:  pixelation, scan conversion |反义词: vectorization |
