pseudoarticulate:/ˌsuːdoʊɑːrˈtɪkjuleɪt/| adj. |似懂非懂地表达的，表达不清的 | seemingly articulate but actually lacking precision or coherence; superficially fluent but ultimately meaningless or nonsensical.  It suggests an attempt at articulation that falls short of genuine clarity or understanding. |例句：His pseudoarticulate ramblings failed to convey any meaningful information. 他的表达含糊不清，未能传达任何有意义的信息。


例句：The politician's speech was pseudoarticulate, filled with empty promises and jargon.  这位政治家的演讲词语不达意，充满了空洞的承诺和行话。


近义词：inarticulate, incoherent, rambling, confused, unclear, unintelligible


反义词：articulate, clear, coherent, lucid, precise


词性：形容词
