heading-off:/ˈhedɪŋ ɔːf/| v.|出发；阻止；转向；偏离|To start a journey; to prevent something from happening; to turn aside or away from a course.|例句：They headed off early in the morning. 他们一大早就出发了。|例句：The police headed off a potential riot. 警方阻止了一场潜在的暴乱。|近义词：starting, preventing, diverting, avoiding|反义词：arriving, allowing, continuing, following
