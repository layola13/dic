pizzeria:/ˌpiːtsəˈriːə/| n. |比萨饼店 |A pizzeria is an establishment that primarily sells pizzas.  It can range from a small, family-run business to a large chain restaurant.|例句：My favorite pizzeria is on Elm Street. 我最喜欢的比萨饼店在榆树街。|例句：They opened a new pizzeria downtown last week. 他们上周在市中心开了一家新的比萨饼店。|近义词：pizza place,  Italian restaurant (if specializing in pizza)|反义词：None|名词
