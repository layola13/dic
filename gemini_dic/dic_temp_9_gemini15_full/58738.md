dunes:/dʒuːnz/| n.|沙丘，沙岗|A dune is a hill of sand built by wind. Dunes are common in deserts and near beaches.|例句：The desert was dotted with towering dunes.  沙漠上点缀着高耸的沙丘。

The wind sculpted the dunes into fascinating shapes. 风雕琢出沙丘迷人的形状。|近义词：sand hill, ridge|反义词：valley, depression
