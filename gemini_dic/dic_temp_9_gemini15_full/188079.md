round-up:/ˈraʊnd ʌp/| n. vt.| 总结； roundup； 围捕； 收集；  |To bring together; gather; collect.  Also, a summary or report. |例句：The police conducted a round-up of suspected criminals.  警方对嫌疑犯进行了围捕。

例句：Let's do a round-up of the key points of the meeting. 让我们总结一下会议的要点。


近义词：summary, compilation, roundup, collection, gather, arrest

反义词：disperse, scatter, distribute


词性：noun, verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer rounds up his cattle every evening. = 农夫每天晚上都把他的牛赶到一起。
She rounds up the news for the morning bulletin. = 她为早间新闻简报收集新闻。

一般过去时:
The police rounded up the protesters. = 警方逮捕了抗议者。
He rounded up the stray dogs. = 他把流浪狗赶到一起。

现在完成时:
They have rounded up all the suspects. = 他们已经逮捕了所有嫌疑人。
She has rounded up all the necessary documents. = 她已经收集了所有必要的文档。

过去完成时:
The sheriff had rounded up the gang before dawn. = 警长在黎明前就逮捕了该团伙。
They had rounded up all the evidence before presenting their case. = 他们在陈述案情之前已经收集了所有证据。

将来时:
The cowboys will round up the cattle tomorrow. = 牛仔们明天将把牛赶到一起。
We will round up the volunteers for the project. = 我们将召集志愿者参与该项目。

虚拟语气:
If the police had rounded up the criminals sooner, the crime wave might have been stopped. = 如果警方早点逮捕罪犯，犯罪浪潮可能就会停止了。
I wish they had rounded up the stray animals before the storm. = 我希望他们在暴风雨来临之前已经把那些流浪动物赶到一起了。

被动语态:
The suspects were rounded up by the police. = 嫌疑犯被警方逮捕了。
The stray animals were rounded up by the animal control officers. = 流浪动物被动物管制员收容了。

疑问句:
Were the cattle rounded up successfully? = 牛群是否成功地被赶到一起？
Have all the participants been rounded up for the meeting? = 所有与会者都已集合了吗？

否定句:
The police didn't round up all the protesters. = 警方没有逮捕所有抗议者。
They haven't yet rounded up the necessary funds for the project. = 他们还没有为该项目筹集到必要的资金。
