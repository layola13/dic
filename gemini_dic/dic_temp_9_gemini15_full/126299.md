myoepithelial:/ˌmaɪoʊˌɛpɪˈθiːliəl/| adj.| 肌上皮的 |  Relating to or denoting cells that are both muscle and epithelial in nature. They are found in various glands and ducts, and contract to expel secretions. |  The myoepithelial cells surrounding the secretory acini contract to release saliva. 肌上皮细胞围绕着分泌腺泡收缩以释放唾液。|  epithelial, muscular |  non-myoepithelial
