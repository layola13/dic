dalali:/dəˈlɑːli/| n.|达拉利，中间人，经纪人|A dalali is a broker or intermediary, especially one who facilitates transactions in a specific market, often involving commodities or financial instruments.  The term is particularly associated with certain regions and industries.|例句：The dalali arranged the sale of the spices between the farmer and the exporter. 达拉利安排了农民和出口商之间的香料销售。|近义词：broker, intermediary, agent, middleman|反义词：principal, direct buyer/seller|名词
