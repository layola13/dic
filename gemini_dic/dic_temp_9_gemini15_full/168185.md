pro-congressional: /ˌproʊ kənˈɡreʃənəl/ | adj. | 亲国会的；支持国会的 |

英文解析:  Supporting or favoring the policies and actions of the Congress (of the United States).  This term implies a positive stance toward the legislative branch of government and its activities.  It can refer to individuals, groups, or even specific policies.

例句:

The pro-congressional lobbyist worked tirelessly to influence legislation.  亲国会的游说者努力工作以影响立法。

The senator's pro-congressional voting record pleased his constituents. 参议员亲国会的投票记录令他的选民满意。


近义词: congressionalist, pro-government (in a legislative context), supportive of Congress

反义词: anti-congressional, anti-government (in a legislative context), opposed to Congress
