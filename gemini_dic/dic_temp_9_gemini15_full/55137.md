dissatisfy:/ˌdɪsˈsætɪsfaɪ/| vt.|使不满意；使不满|To fail to satisfy; to cause dissatisfaction in.|The poor service dissatisfied the customers.  糟糕的服务让顾客很不满意。|The long wait dissatisfied him. 漫长的等待让他很不满意。|satisfy, disappoint|satisfy, please|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The slow service dissatisfies many customers. 缓慢的服务让许多顾客不满。
This explanation doesn't dissatisfy me. 这个解释并没有让我不满。


一般过去时:
The concert dissatisfied the audience. 音乐会让观众不满。
His performance dissatisfied the judges. 他的表现让评委不满。


现在完成时:
The results have dissatisfied the shareholders. 结果让股东们不满。
Nothing has dissatisfied me more than this.  没有什么比这更让我不满了。


过去完成时:
They had dissatisfied the board with their proposals. 他们用他们的提议让董事会不满。
The bad news had dissatisfied her greatly. 这个坏消息让她非常不满。


将来时:
The new policy will likely dissatisfy many people. 新政策可能会让很多人不满。
I fear that the changes will dissatisfy the workers. 我担心这些改变会让工人们不满。


虚拟语气:
If the product dissatisfied you, you could return it. 如果产品让你不满意，你可以退货。
I wish the outcome had not dissatisfied me so much. 我真希望结果不会让我这么不满意。


被动语态:
Many customers were dissatisfied by the delay. 许多顾客因为延误而不满。
The company was dissatisfied with the employee's performance. 公司对员工的表现不满意。


疑问句:
Does the new design dissatisfy you? 新的设计让你不满意吗？
What aspects of the service dissatisfied you? 服务的哪些方面让你不满意？


否定句:
The quality of the product did not dissatisfy me. 产品的质量并没有让我不满意。
This explanation doesn't dissatisfy me at all.  这个解释一点也不让我不满。


PHRASE->
