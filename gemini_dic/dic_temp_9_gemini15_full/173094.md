quartzes:/ˈkwɔːrtsɪz/| n.|石英(复数)|The word "quartzes" is the plural form of "quartz," referring to multiple crystals of quartz.  Quartz is a hard, crystalline mineral composed of silicon and oxygen atoms. It is the second most abundant mineral in Earth's continental crust.  Different varieties of quartz exist, distinguished by their color and other properties.|例句：The geologist identified several different types of quartzes in the rock sample.  地质学家在岩石样本中鉴定出几种不同类型的石英。
