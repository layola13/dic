compressingly:/kəmˈprɛsɪŋli/ | adv. |压缩地；浓缩地 |  Describes something that is done in a compressed or condensed manner; suggesting a feeling of being overwhelmed or pressured.  It implies a sense of intensity or urgency. |例句：He spoke compressingly, cramming years of experience into a single speech. 他说话简洁有力，将多年的经验浓缩在一篇演讲中。

例句：The deadline loomed, and she worked compressingly to finish the project.  截止日期迫在眉睫，她马不停蹄地努力完成项目。


近义词：concisely, succinctly, briefly, intensely, urgently

反义词：lengthily, expansively, ramblingly

词性: 副词
