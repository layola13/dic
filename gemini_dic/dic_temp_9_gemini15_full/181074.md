reindexing:/ˌriːˈɪndɛksɪŋ/ | n. |重新索引 | The process of creating a new index for data, often after changes have been made to the original data. This ensures that searches and other operations remain efficient.  It is commonly used in databases and search engines. | Example:  The database administrator performed a reindexing operation after a major data update. = 数据库管理员在一次重大数据更新后执行了重新索引操作。


The website required reindexing to improve search results. = 网站需要重新索引以改善搜索结果。


近义词：index rebuilding, data reorganization | 反义词：None (it's a process, not a state) | 词性：名词
