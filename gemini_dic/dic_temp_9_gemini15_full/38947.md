confidentness: /ˈkɒnfɪdəns/ /ˈkɑːnfɪdəns/ | n. |自信，自信心 |The feeling or belief that one can rely on someone or something; firm trust.  It also refers to a feeling of self-assurance. |例句：Her confidentness allowed her to succeed in her career.  她的自信使她在事业上取得成功。

例句：His confidentness in his abilities was evident in his presentation. 他对自身能力的自信在他的演讲中显而易见。


近义词：self-assurance, self-confidence, assurance, belief, trust |反义词：self-doubt, insecurity, diffidence, uncertainty

词性：名词
