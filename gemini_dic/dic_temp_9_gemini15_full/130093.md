non-profit:/ˌnɒnˈprɒfɪt/|adj.|非营利的；公益的|An organization that does not aim to make a profit and typically uses its income to pursue a social cause.|例句：Many non-profit organizations rely on donations. 许多非营利组织依靠捐款。|近义词：charitable, philanthropic|反义词：for-profit, commercial
