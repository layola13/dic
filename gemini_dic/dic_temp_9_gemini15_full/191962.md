scopula:/ˈskɒpjʊlə/| n.| 系词 |A word that connects the subject of a sentence to its predicate, such as "is," "are," "was," "were," "be," "being," and "been."  In simpler terms, it's the verb that links the subject to a description or state of being. |The copula in "The sky is blue" is "is," linking the subject "sky" to the predicate adjective "blue." |近义词：linking verb |反义词：None


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat is fluffy. = 猫毛茸茸的。
He is a teacher. = 他是一位老师。


一般过去时:
The dog was playful. = 那条狗很顽皮。
She was tired. = 她很累。


现在完成时:
They have been happy. = 他们一直很快乐。
It has been raining. = 一直在下雨。


过去完成时:
We had been friends for years. = 我们是多年的朋友了。
The show had been cancelled. = 演出已经被取消了。


将来时:
The weather will be sunny. = 天气将会晴朗。
They will be here soon. = 他们很快就会来。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would go. = 如果我是你，我会去。
It would be great if she came. = 如果她来就好了。


被动语态:
The book is being read. = 这本书正在被阅读。
The window was broken. = 窗户被打碎了。


疑问句:
Is he coming? = 他来了吗？
Are you ready? = 你准备好了吗？


否定句:
The answer is not correct. = 答案不正确。
She is not happy. = 她不开心。
