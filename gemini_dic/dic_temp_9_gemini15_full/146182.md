overexcites:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsaɪts/ | vt. |过度兴奋，使过度兴奋|To excite excessively or beyond a normal or healthy level.  It implies a state of being overly stimulated, often leading to negative consequences.|例句：The roller coaster overexcited the children, leaving them jittery and unable to sleep. 过山车让孩子们过度兴奋，导致他们紧张不安，无法入睡。


The loud music overexcited the crowd, leading to a stampede.  喧闹的音乐使人群过度兴奋，导致了踩踏事件。|近义词：overwhelm, overstimulate, hyper-excite |反义词：calm, soothe, relax |动词
