caesar:/ˈsiːzər/| n. |凯撒；皇帝；独裁者 | Caesar refers to Julius Caesar, a prominent Roman general and statesman, or any emperor subsequently.  It can also be used as a title or a general term for an autocratic ruler. | Example: Julius Caesar conquered Gaul.  朱利叶斯·凯撒征服了高卢。  He ruled like a Caesar. 他像个皇帝一样统治。 |近义词：emperor, autocrat, dictator |反义词：subject, commoner
