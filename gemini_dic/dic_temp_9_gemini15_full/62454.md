endorser:/ɪnˈdɔːrsər/| n. |背书人；认可者；代言人 |A person or entity that endorses something; someone who publicly approves or supports something.  |Example: The celebrity is a paid endorser for the new sports drink.  这位名人在为这款新的运动饮料做付费代言。 |近义词：supporter, sponsor, advocate, promoter |反义词：detractor, critic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The athlete endorses several brands. =这位运动员代言几个品牌。
She endorses the new policy. =她支持这项新政策。


一般过去时:
He endorsed the candidate publicly. = 他公开支持这位候选人。
They endorsed the agreement. = 他们认可了这份协议。


现在完成时:
The company has endorsed many charitable causes. =该公司已经支持了许多慈善事业。
She has endorsed this product for years. =她多年来一直为这款产品代言。


过去完成时:
The artist had already endorsed the project before the funding was secured. =在资金到位之前，这位艺术家就已经认可了这个项目。
They had endorsed the plan before the meeting. =他们在会议之前就已经认可了这个计划。


将来时:
He will endorse the book next month. =他下个月将为这本书作序/代言。
The company will endorse the new technology. =该公司将支持这项新技术。


虚拟语气:
If he endorsed the product, sales would increase. =如果他为该产品代言，销量就会增加。
I wish the company had endorsed my proposal. =我希望公司已经认可了我的提案。


被动语态:
The product was endorsed by a famous actor. =该产品由一位著名演员代言。
The proposal was endorsed by the committee. =该提案得到了委员会的认可。


疑问句:
Who endorsed the new initiative? =谁支持这项新举措？
Has the singer endorsed any political causes? =这位歌手是否支持过任何政治事业？


否定句:
He did not endorse the controversial bill. =他不支持这份有争议的法案。
The organization did not endorse the candidate. =该组织不支持这位候选人。


PHRASE->
brand endorser 品牌代言人
celebrity endorser 名人代言人
product endorser 产品代言人
political endorser 政治支持者
endorsement deal 代言协议
