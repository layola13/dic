cleansed:/klɛnzd/ | v. | 清洗；净化；清除 | To make something clean, pure, or free from something undesirable.  |例句：She cleansed the wound with antiseptic. 她用消毒剂清洗伤口。
The river needs to be cleansed of pollution. 这条河需要清除污染。|近义词：purify, sanitize, purge |反义词：contaminate, pollute, defile |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cleanses his skin every night. = 他每天晚上都清洁皮肤。
She cleanses her chakras regularly. = 她定期清洁她的脉轮。


一般过去时:
I cleansed the house thoroughly before the guests arrived. = 客人来之前，我把房子彻底打扫干净了。
The priest cleansed the temple. = 牧师净化了寺庙。


现在完成时:
They have cleansed the water supply. = 他们已经净化了供水系统。
She has cleansed her diet of processed foods. = 她已经清除了饮食中加工食品。


过去完成时:
He had cleansed the wound before applying the bandage. = 他在包扎伤口之前已经清洗过了。
The team had cleansed the data before analysis. = 团队在分析之前已经清洗了数据。


将来时:
We will cleanse the beach after the storm. = 暴风雨过后我们将清理海滩。
They will cleanse the air filters tomorrow. = 他们明天将清洗空气过滤器。


虚拟语气:
If I cleansed my diet, I would feel healthier. = 如果我净化我的饮食，我会感觉更健康。
I wish I had cleansed the data before running the analysis. = 我希望我在运行分析之前已经清洗了数据。


被动语态:
The wound was cleansed by the doctor. = 伤口被医生清洗了。
The data is cleansed before being used in the model. = 数据在用于模型之前会被清洗。


疑问句:

否定句:
