reinterred:/ˌriːɪnˈtɜːrd/ | vt. |重新下葬；重新安葬 | To bury (a body) again, especially after exhumation.  |例句：The remains were reinterred in a more appropriate location.遗体被重新安葬在一个更合适的地方。

例句：After the discovery of a new burial ground, the authorities decided to reinter the bodies of those mistakenly buried elsewhere. 在发现新的墓地后，当局决定将那些错误埋葬在其他地方的遗体重新安葬。


近义词：rebury, reburial

反义词：exhume, disinter


词性：动词
