erve:/ɜːrv/ | vt. | 服侍；服务；供应；招待 | to perform duties or provide a service for someone or something |  He served in the army for five years. 他在军队服役了五年。 | She served us dinner. 她招待我们吃晚饭。| assist, help | neglect, ignore | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He serves the community well. 他很好地服务于社区。
The restaurant serves delicious food. 这家餐馆供应美味的食物。

一般过去时:
She served tea to the guests. 她给客人端茶。
The waiter served us quickly. 服务员很快地为我们服务。

现在完成时:
He has served the company for twenty years. 他为公司服务了二十年。
They have served many customers today. 他们今天已经服务了很多顾客。

过去完成时:
The chef had served the appetizers before the main course. 主厨在主菜之前已经上了开胃菜。
She had served her apprenticeship before opening her own shop. 她完成学徒期后才开自己的店。

将来时:
They will serve dinner at seven o'clock. 他们将在七点钟供应晚餐。
He will serve his country with pride. 他将自豪地为国效力。


虚拟语气:
If I were a waiter, I would serve them with a smile. 如果我是一个服务员，我会面带微笑地为他们服务。
I wish I could serve you better. 我希望我能更好地为您服务。

被动语态:
The food was served promptly. 食物很快就上来了。
Customers are served on a first-come, first-served basis. 顾客按先到先得的原则服务。

疑问句:
Does this restaurant serve vegetarian food? 这家餐馆供应素食吗？
What kind of tea do they serve? 他们供应哪种茶？

否定句:
The hotel does not serve alcohol. 这家酒店不供应酒。
They didn't serve breakfast that morning. 那天早上他们没有供应早餐。
