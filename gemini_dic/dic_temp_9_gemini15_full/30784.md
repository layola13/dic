/sɛt/ |abbr.| 大学英语等级考试 | College English Test | CET refers to the College English Test, a standardized test in China to assess the English proficiency of college students.  It is widely used for college graduation requirements and job applications. | The CET results are crucial for many students seeking employment after graduation. |近义词: English proficiency test, English language test |反义词: N/A
