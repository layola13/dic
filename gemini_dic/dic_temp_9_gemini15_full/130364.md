non-union:/ˌnɑːnˈjuːnjən/| adj. |非工会的；非会员的|Not belonging to or affiliated with a labor union.  This term describes individuals, businesses, or workplaces that are not part of a labor union.  It can refer to workers who choose not to join a union, or companies that do not have a unionized workforce.|例句：The company employs a largely non-union workforce. 这家公司雇佣的大部分员工是非工会成员。

例句：He chose to work at a non-union shop because of the higher pay. 他选择在非工会商店工作是因为薪水更高。


近义词：unorganized, unaffiliated


反义词：unionized, union
