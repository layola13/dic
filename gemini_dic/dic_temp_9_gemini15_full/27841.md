canonically:/kəˈnɒnɪkli/|/ˌkænənɪkli/| adv. |规范地；正统地；按照正规方法|In a way that conforms to or is in accordance with established rules, principles, or traditions.  It often implies an authoritative or official standard.|The novel was canonically accepted as the author's best work.|近义词：officially, traditionally, regularly, conventionally|反义词：unofficially, informally, irregularly, unconventionally
