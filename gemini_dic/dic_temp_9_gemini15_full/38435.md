concertinas:/ˌkɒnsɜːtɪˈnɑːz/| n. |手风琴，手提风琴 |A concertina is a small, portable free-reed musical instrument.  It is played by squeezing and expanding the bellows, which causes the reeds to vibrate and produce sound.  |例句：He played a lively tune on his concertina. 他用手风琴演奏了一首轻快的曲子。

例句：The concertina player captivated the audience with his skillful performance.  手风琴演奏者以其精湛的技艺征服了观众。


近义词：accordion (though larger), melodeon|反义词：None.
