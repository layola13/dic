reining:/ˈreɪnɪŋ/| v.|统治；控制；驾驭；抑制|reigning means holding power or control, often in a supreme or dominant way. It can refer to a monarch's rule, or to control over a situation or one's emotions.  |例句：The queen is reining over the kingdom. 女王统治着王国。

The rider is reining in his horse. 骑手正在勒住他的马。|近义词：ruling, governing, controlling, dominating|反义词：yielding, surrendering, submitting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is reining in his temper. 他正在控制他的脾气。
She reigns over her company with an iron fist. 她铁腕统治着她的公司。


一般过去时:
The king reigned for fifty years. 国王统治了五十年。
He reigned in his emotions during the difficult conversation. 在那次艰难的谈话中，他控制住了自己的情绪。


现在完成时:
She has reigned supreme in the music industry for decades. 几十年来，她一直是音乐界的女王。
He has been reining in his spending habits lately. 他最近一直在控制他的消费习惯。


过去完成时:
Before the new leader emerged, the old regime had reigned for centuries. 在新领导人出现之前，旧政权已经统治了几个世纪。
He had reigned in his anger before speaking. 他在说话之前已经控制住了自己的怒火。


将来时:
The new queen will reign for many years to come. 新女王将统治许多年。
He will reign in his impulsive behavior. 他将控制住他冲动的行为。


虚拟语气:
If he reigned in his spending, he would have more money. 如果他控制住他的开支，他就会有更多的钱。
I wish he would reign in his arrogance. 我希望他能控制住他的傲慢。


被动语态:
The country was reigned over by a wise king. 这个国家由一位英明的国王统治。
His emotions were reigned in with difficulty. 他的情绪很难控制住。


疑问句:
Who is reining the country at present? 目前是谁在统治这个国家？
Is she successfully reining in her impulses? 她能成功地控制住她的冲动吗？


否定句:
He did not reign in his anger. 他没有控制住他的怒火。
The new leader failed to reign in the violence. 新领导人未能控制住暴力。


PHRASE->
reign supreme 至高无上
reign over 统治
reign in 控制
under the reign of 在…统治下
reign of terror 恐怖统治

词源:
From Old French *reigner, from Latin regnare "to rule as king," from rex (genitive regis) "king."  The meaning evolved to encompass broader control and dominance beyond just monarchy.
