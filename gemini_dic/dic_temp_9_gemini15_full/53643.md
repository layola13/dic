discerption:/dɪˈsɜːrpʃən/| n.|辨别；识别；洞察力|The act of discerning or distinguishing; the ability to judge well.|例句：His keen discernment allowed him to spot the flaw in the argument. 他敏锐的洞察力使他发现了论证中的缺陷。|近义词：discrimination, perception, insight|反义词：misunderstanding, misjudgment|名词
