connotate:/ˈkɒnəʊteɪt/ /ˈkɑːnoʊteɪt/ | vt. |暗示；隐含；联想|To imply or suggest (an idea or feeling) in addition to the literal or primary meaning.  Connotation refers to the associated or secondary meaning of a word or expression in addition to its explicit or primary meaning.|The word "home" connotes warmth and security.  这个词“家”暗示着温暖和安全。|The word "cheap" connotes low quality.  “便宜”这个词暗示着低质量。|suggest, imply, signify, indicate |denote (in the sense of directly signifying)|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The word "home" connotes warmth and security. =  “家”这个词暗示着温暖和安全。
This image connotes a sense of peace. = 这幅图像暗示着一种平静感。


一般过去时:
The painting connoted a feeling of melancholy. = 这幅画暗示了一种忧郁的感觉。
His silence connoted disapproval. = 他的沉默暗示着不满。


现在完成时:
The novel has connoted a new understanding of social issues. = 这部小说已经暗示了对社会问题的新的理解。
Her actions have connoted a lack of interest. = 她的行为已经暗示出缺乏兴趣。


过去完成时:
The old photograph had connoted a sense of nostalgia. = 那张旧照片曾暗示着一种怀旧的感觉。
The symbol had connoted power and authority in ancient times. = 在古代，这个符号暗示着权力和权威。


将来时:
This new policy will connote a shift in government priorities. = 这项新政策将暗示政府优先事项的转变。
The upcoming election will connote significant change. =  即将到来的选举将暗示重大的变化。


虚拟语气:
If this gesture connoted respect, I would be relieved. = 如果这个姿势暗示着尊重，我会感到宽慰。
If the song connoted hope, it would be more uplifting. = 如果这首歌暗示着希望，它就会更令人振奋。


被动语态:
The poem is connoted as a masterpiece. = 这首诗被认为是杰作。（暗示为杰作）
The symbol was connoted with religious significance. = 这个符号被赋予了宗教意义。（暗示具有宗教意义）


疑问句:
What does this symbol connote? = 这个符号暗示着什么？
Does this behavior connote guilt? = 这种行为暗示着内疚吗？


否定句:
The word does not connote any negative meaning. = 这个词没有任何负面含义的暗示。
His expression did not connote anger. = 他的表情没有暗示愤怒。


PHRASE->
connotate with:  与……有某种联系/暗示
connotate meaning: 暗示意义
connotate significance: 暗示重要性
connotate feeling: 暗示情感
connotate symbol: 暗示象征意义
