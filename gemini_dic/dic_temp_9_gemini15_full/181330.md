reinthrone:/ˌriːɪnˈθroʊn/ | vt. |重新加冕；使复位 | To restore to a throne; to reinstate in power or authority.  |例句：The exiled king was reinthroned after the revolution. 流亡的国王在革命后复位了。 |例句：The newly elected president was reinthroned with a grand ceremony. 新当选的总统举行了盛大的仪式重新就职。|近义词：restore, reinstate, re-establish |反义词：dethrone, depose |动词
