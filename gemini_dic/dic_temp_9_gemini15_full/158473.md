pledgor:/ˈplɛdʒɔːr/| n.| 抵押人，质押人 | A pledgor is a person who gives something as collateral (pledge) in a loan agreement.  They are the party providing the security for a debt. | Example: The pledgor agreed to use his house as collateral for the loan.  抵押人同意用他的房子作为贷款的抵押物。 |近义词: mortgagor, guarantor |反义词: pledgee


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The pledgor pays the interest regularly. = 抵押人定期支付利息。
He is the pledgor in this transaction. = 他是这笔交易中的抵押人。


一般过去时:
The pledgor defaulted on the loan last year. = 抵押人去年未能偿还贷款。
She was the pledgor in a previous business deal. = 她之前的一笔生意中是抵押人。


现在完成时:
The pledgor has fulfilled all his obligations. = 抵押人已经履行了他的所有义务。
The bank has confirmed the pledgor's identity. = 银行已确认抵押人的身份。


过去完成时:
The pledgor had already pledged the assets before the lawsuit. = 诉讼之前，抵押人已经抵押了资产。
Before the contract, the pledgor had checked the terms carefully. = 在签订合同之前，抵押人仔细检查了条款。


将来时:
The pledgor will be responsible for any losses. = 抵押人将对任何损失负责。
He will be the pledgor for the new project's financing. = 他将成为新项目融资的抵押人。


虚拟语气:
If he were the pledgor, he would have acted differently. = 如果他是抵押人，他会采取不同的行动。
I wish the pledgor had informed us earlier. = 我希望抵押人早点通知我们。


被动语态:
The assets were pledged by the pledgor to secure the debt. = 资产由抵押人抵押以担保债务。
The pledgor's identity was verified by the bank. = 抵押人的身份已由银行核实。


疑问句:
Who is the pledgor in this agreement? = 这份协议中的抵押人是谁？
Is he still the pledgor for that loan? = 他还是那笔贷款的抵押人吗？


否定句:
He is not the pledgor; he is the pledgee. = 他不是抵押人；他是受押人。
The company was not the pledgor in that deal. = 那笔交易中，该公司并非抵押人。
