nonadministrative:/ˌnɑːnædˈmɪnɪstrətɪv/| adj. |非行政的，非管理的 |Not related to or connected with administration or management.  It describes things, roles, or tasks that are not part of the administrative or managerial functions of an organization or system. |例句：The nonadministrative staff are responsible for the day-to-day operations. 非行政人员负责日常运营。

例句：His role is primarily nonadministrative, focusing on research and development. 他的角色主要是非行政的，专注于研发。


近义词：nonmanagerial, operational, executive  (depending on context)
反义词：administrative, managerial


词性：形容词
