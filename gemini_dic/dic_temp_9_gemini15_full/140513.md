The excessive oleaginousness of the sauce made the dish unpalatable. 这道菜的酱汁过于油腻，让人吃不下去。|近义词：oiliness, slipperiness, unctuousness, smoothness |反义词：dryness, roughness, honesty, sincerity |词性：名词
