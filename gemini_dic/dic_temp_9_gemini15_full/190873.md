say:/seɪ/ | vt. |说；讲；表达；表明|To utter words; to express in words; to declare or state.|例句：He said he would be late. 他说他会迟到。|例句：The sign says "No Parking".  标志上写着“禁止停车”。|近义词：speak, tell, utter, express|反义词：keep silent, remain mum


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He says he's busy. = 他说他很忙。
She says she understands. = 她说她明白了。

一般过去时:
They said they would help. = 他们说他们会帮忙。
He said nothing. = 他什么也没说。

现在完成时:
She has said she will attend. = 她说过她会参加。
He has said little on the subject. = 关于这个话题，他几乎没说什么。

过去完成时:
I had said I would be there. = 我说过我会在那儿。
She had said she wasn't interested. = 她说过她不感兴趣。

将来时:
He will say what he thinks. = 他会说出他的想法。
She will say yes. = 她会答应的。

虚拟语气:
If I said that, I would be lying. = 如果我说那样的话，就是在撒谎。
I wish I had said something. = 我真希望我说了些什么。

被动语态:
It is said that he is wealthy. = 据说他很有钱。
It was said that the meeting was cancelled. = 据说会议取消了。

疑问句:
What did you say? = 你说什么？
What does this mean? = 这是什么意思？

否定句:
I didn't say anything. = 我什么也没说。
He said he wouldn't go. = 他说他不会去。


PHRASE->
say something = 说点什么
say no = 拒绝
say yes = 同意
have your say = 发表你的意见
that is to say = 也就是说
