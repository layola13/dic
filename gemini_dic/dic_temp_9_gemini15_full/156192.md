/ˈfriːkər/| n. |电话黑客，电话怪杰|A person who illegally manipulates telephone systems, often for fun or to make free calls.  Phreakers are skilled in exploiting vulnerabilities in telephone networks to gain unauthorized access or services. They often possess deep technical knowledge of telecommunications systems.|  The phreaker bypassed the phone company's security system and made thousands of free calls.|近义词：hacker, phone hacker|反义词：law-abiding citizen
