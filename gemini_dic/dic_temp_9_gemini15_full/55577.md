disturbingly:/dɪˈstɜːrbɪŋli/ | adv. |令人不安地，烦扰地 |  The adverb "disturbingly" means in a way that causes anxiety, worry, or unease. It describes something that is unsettling or disquieting. | 例句：Disturbingly, the crime rate has increased by 20% this year.  令人不安的是，今年犯罪率增加了20%。  |  The news was disturbingly similar to what we had heard before.  这则新闻与我们之前听到的令人不安地相似。| unsettlingly, alarmingly, worryingly | calmingly, reassuringly, peacefully | adv.
