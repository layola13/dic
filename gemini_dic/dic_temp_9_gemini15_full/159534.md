politicians:/pɒləˈtɪʃənz/| n. |政治家们，政客们 |People who are actively involved in politics, especially those who hold public office. Politicians often work to influence policy and legislation.  |Example:  The politicians debated the new healthcare bill for hours.  政治家们就新的医疗法案辩论了数小时。  |近义词：statesmen, lawmakers, officeholders |反义词：private citizens
