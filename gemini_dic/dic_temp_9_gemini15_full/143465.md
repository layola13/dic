outgabbled:/ˌaʊtˈɡæbəld/ | adj. | 被喋喋不休地击败；被滔滔不绝地说服 |  Past participle of outgabble; defeated in a contest of rapid speech.  Describes a situation where someone has been overwhelmed or defeated by someone else's rapid and incessant talking. | 例句：He was completely outgabbled by the fast-talking salesman. 他被那个说话飞快的推销员彻底说服了。  She felt outgabbled in the debate, unable to get a word in edgewise.  她在辩论中感觉被滔滔不绝地说服了，插不上话。 | 近义词：outtalked, overwhelmed, silenced (in a verbal sense) | 反义词：out spoken, dominated the conversation
