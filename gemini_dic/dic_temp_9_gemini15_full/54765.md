dispathy:/dɪsˈpæθi/| n. |反感；厌恶；冷漠 |A feeling of dislike or aversion.  It often implies a lack of sympathy or empathy. |例句：He felt a deep dispathy towards his former employer. 他对他以前的老板感到深深的反感。

例句：The politician's dispathy for the press was evident in his curt responses.  这位政治家对新闻界的厌恶在他简短的回答中显而易见。


近义词：antipathy, aversion, dislike, repugnance, animosity

反义词：sympathy, empathy, fondness, affection

词性：名词
