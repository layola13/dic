expans:/ɪkˈspæns/| n.|扩展，扩张；广阔；膨胀 |The vast expanse of the desert stretched before them.  沙漠的广阔无垠展现在他们面前。|近义词：extension, expansion, breadth, width |反义词：contraction, shrinkage


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The universe constantly expands. = 宇宙不断膨胀。
The company expands its business into new markets. = 公司将其业务扩展到新的市场。


一般过去时:
The balloon expanded rapidly. = 气球迅速膨胀。
The empire expanded its territory through conquest. = 这个帝国通过征服扩张了它的领土。


现在完成时:
The city has expanded significantly in recent years. = 近年来，这座城市已大幅扩张。
The universe has been expanding since the Big Bang. = 自大爆炸以来，宇宙一直在膨胀。


过去完成时:
The ice had expanded, cracking the bottle. = 冰已经膨胀，把瓶子弄裂了。
Before the war, the country had expanded its military. = 在战争爆发前，这个国家扩大了它的军队。


将来时:
The company will expand its operations next year. = 公司明年将扩大其业务。
The balloon will expand if you add more air. = 如果你加更多气体，气球就会膨胀。


虚拟语气:
If the universe didn't expand, it would collapse. = 如果宇宙不膨胀，它就会坍缩。
I wish the company would expand its product line. = 我希望公司能扩大其产品线。


被动语态:
The territory was expanded by the conquering army. = 领土被征服的军队扩张了。
The business is being expanded into new regions. = 业务正在扩展到新的地区。


疑问句:
Does the universe expand at a constant rate? = 宇宙以恒定的速度膨胀吗？
How much will the company expand its workforce? = 公司将扩大多少员工？


否定句:
The metal did not expand when heated. = 金属加热时没有膨胀。
The company didn't expand its operations as planned. = 公司没有按照计划扩大其业务。
