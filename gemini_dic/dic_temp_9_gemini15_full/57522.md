It often refers to a system of forced recruitment or oppression, particularly associated with historical military practices.|例句：The dragoonages during the war were brutal, forcing unwilling men into military service.  战争期间强行征兵极其残酷，强迫不愿参军的人服役。
例句：He described the company's sales tactics as a form of dragoonages, pressuring customers into making unnecessary purchases. 他将公司的销售策略描述为一种胁迫，迫使顾客购买不必要的商品。|近义词：coercion, oppression, intimidation, pressganging |反义词：persuasion, encouragement, freedom
