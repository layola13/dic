conman:/ˈkɑːnmæn/| n.|骗子，江湖骗子|A conman is a person who cheats others out of money or property, typically through a confidence trick.|例句：The conman tricked the old woman out of her life savings.  这个骗子骗走了老太太一生的积蓄。|例句：He was a smooth-talking conman who preyed on the elderly. 他是一个油嘴滑舌的骗子，专门欺骗老年人。|近义词：swindler, fraudster, sharper, imposter|反义词：honest person, philanthropist


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The conman cons people out of their money. = 骗子骗取人们的钱财。
He regularly cons unsuspecting victims. = 他经常欺骗毫无戒心的受害者。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
He has conned many people out of their money. = 他已经骗取了许多人的钱财。
