prolongers: /prəˈlɒŋɡərz/ | n. | 延长者；延期者 |  Those who or that which prolong something.  Prolongers can refer to devices, substances, or individuals that extend the duration of something. For example,  a food preservative acts as a prolonger of shelf life. |例句：The new medication acts as a prolonger of life for patients with the disease.  这种新药延长了患病患者的寿命。

例句：  He is a prolonger of the meeting, always adding unnecessary details. 他是会议的拖延者，总是添加不必要的细节。


近义词：extenders, lengtheners, prolongations (as a noun) | 反义词：shorteners, curtailers


词性: 名词

词源:
