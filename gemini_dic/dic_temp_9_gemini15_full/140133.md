off-the-map:/ˌɔːf ðə mæp/ | adj. | 地图上找不到的；偏远的；不为人知的；极其重要的 | Extremely remote or isolated; unknown or uncharted; exceptionally important or significant.  |例句：That remote island is completely off-the-map.  那个偏远的岛屿完全在地图上找不到。

例句：His new business idea is completely off-the-map, innovative and potentially groundbreaking. 他的新商业理念非常超前，极具创新性，并可能具有开创性。

近义词：remote, secluded, obscure, unheard-of, exceptional, extraordinary

反义词：well-known, familiar, accessible, commonplace, ordinary


PHRASE->
off the map completely: 完全消失
off the beaten map: 远离人迹
put something on the map: 使某事出名
take something off the map: 从地图上抹去
a map of the world: 世界地图

USAGE->

一般现在时:
This remote village remains off-the-map. = 这个偏远的村庄仍然在地图上找不到。
Their groundbreaking research puts them off the map of competitors. = 他们开创性的研究使他们超越了竞争对手。


一般过去时:
Before the exploration, that region was off-the-map. = 在探险之前，那个地区在地图上找不到。
The small town was off the map until the new highway was built. = 直到修建了新的高速公路，这个小镇才为人所知。


现在完成时:
The new technology has put our company off the map of the competition. = 新技术使我们公司超越了竞争对手。
Many small businesses have been put off the map by the economic downturn. = 许多小型企业因经济低迷而倒闭。


过去完成时:
Before the discovery, the island had been off-the-map for centuries. = 在发现之前，这个岛屿已经在地图上找不到几个世纪了。
The ancient civilization had long been off the map until archaeologists found its ruins.  在考古学家发现其遗址之前，这个古代文明早已不为人知。


将来时:
With this new technology, our competitors will be off the map. = 凭借这项新技术，我们的竞争对手将被甩在后面。
The rising sea levels may put many coastal communities off the map in the future.  未来，海平面上升可能会使许多沿海社区消失。


虚拟语气:
If we had better marketing, we wouldn't be off the map. = 如果我们有更好的营销策略，我们就不会默默无闻。
I wish our small town wasn't so off the map. = 我希望我们的小镇不要那么偏僻。


被动语态:
That region was initially off the map, but now it's being developed. = 那个地区最初在地图上找不到，但现在正在开发中。
Many traditional crafts have been put off the map by mass production.  许多传统工艺已被大规模生产所淘汰。


疑问句:
Is this area still off the map? = 这个地区仍然在地图上找不到吗？
Are we off the map in terms of innovation? = 在创新方面，我们落后了吗？


否定句:
This company is definitely not off the map. = 这家公司绝对没有默默无闻。
Their achievements are far from off the map. = 他们的成就远非默默无闻。


词源:

The phrase "off the map" is a metaphorical extension of its literal meaning.  Originally, it referred to places not yet charted or recorded on maps. Over time, its meaning broadened to encompass anything unknown, remote, or insignificant.  The "map" is a metaphor for awareness or recognition.
