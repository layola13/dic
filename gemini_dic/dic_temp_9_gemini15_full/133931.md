nonhappening:/ˌnɒnˈhæpənɪŋ/| adj.|未发生的事情；不会发生的事情|Something that is not happening or will not happen;  a situation or event that is unlikely to occur.  It often implies a sense of disappointment or frustration that something expected or desired is not taking place.|例句：The nonhappening of the promised pay raise caused widespread discontent among the employees.  承诺的加薪没有兑现，导致员工普遍不满。 The nonhappening of the party was a real letdown.  派对没有举行，真是令人失望。|近义词：unrealized, unlikely, improbable, nonexistent|反义词：happening, occurring, event, reality
