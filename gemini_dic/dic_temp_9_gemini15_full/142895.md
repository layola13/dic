out-of-whack:/ˌaʊt əvˈwæk/| adj.|混乱的；失调的；出毛病的|Not functioning correctly; disordered; out of alignment.  The expression implies a state of disrepair or malfunction, often implying something is not working as it should.|例句：My bike is out of whack; the gears are slipping. 我的自行车出毛病了；齿轮打滑了。

例句：The company's finances are completely out of whack. 这家公司的财务状况完全混乱。


近义词：broken, malfunctioning, disordered, amiss, awry


反义词：working properly, in order, functioning correctly, in sync
