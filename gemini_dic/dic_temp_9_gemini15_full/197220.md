share:/ʃeər/ | n./v.| 分享；股份；份额；参与 | To divide and distribute among several people;  a part or portion of something divided among several. |例句：We shared the pizza. 我们一起分享了披萨。

They share a flat. 他们合住一套公寓。


近义词：distribute, divide, participate, portion | 反义词：keep, hoard, withhold, conceal | 词性: noun/verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They share the cost. = 他们分摊费用。
We share a common goal. = 我们有共同的目标。


一般过去时:
She shared her toys with her friends. = 她把玩具和朋友们分享了。
He shared his experience with us. = 他和我们分享了他的经历。


现在完成时:
I have shared my ideas with the team. = 我已经和团队分享了我的想法。
They have shared their profits with their investors. = 他们已经和投资者分享了他们的利润。


过去完成时:
He had already shared the news before I called. = 我打电话之前他已经分享了这个消息。
She had shared her concerns with her manager before the meeting. = 会议之前，她已经和她的经理分享了她的担忧。


将来时:
We will share the results of the study next week. = 我们下周将分享这项研究的结果。
They will share their findings with the scientific community. = 他们将与科学界分享他们的发现。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would share it with you. = 如果我有更多时间，我会和你分享。
I wish I could share my feelings with someone. = 我希望我能和某人分享我的感受。


被动语态:
The profits were shared equally among the partners. = 利润在合伙人之间平均分配。
The information was shared with all employees. = 信息已与所有员工共享。


疑问句:
Will you share your thoughts on the matter? = 你愿意分享你对这件事的想法吗？
Can we share this work between us? = 我们能分担这项工作吗？


否定句:
I didn't share your opinion. = 我不同意你的意见。
He refused to share his secrets. = 他拒绝分享他的秘密。
