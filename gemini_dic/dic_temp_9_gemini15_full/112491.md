louping:/ˈluːpɪŋ/| n.|跳跃；(苏格兰)羊瘙痒症|A leaping or jumping movement; in Scotland, louping ill is a viral disease affecting sheep.|例句：The lamb performed a series of playful loupings across the field. 小羊在田野里做了一系列嬉戏的跳跃。  The farmer was concerned about the outbreak of louping ill among his flock. 农民担心他的羊群中爆发羊瘙痒症。|近义词：leaping, jumping, hopping|反义词：stillness, inactivity|名词
