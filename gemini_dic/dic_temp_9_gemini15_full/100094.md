interposers:/ˌɪntərˈpoʊzərz/| n.| 干涉者；介入者 |Someone or something that interposes or intervenes.  An interposer is a person or thing that comes between others, often to mediate or obstruct. |例句：The peacekeepers acted as interposers between the warring factions.  维和人员充当交战各派之间的调停者。

例句：The thick curtain served as an interposer between the stage and the audience. 厚厚的幕布在舞台和观众之间起到阻隔作用。


近义词：mediator, intervener, intermediary, arbitrator

反义词：bystander, observer


词性：名词
