practising: /ˈpræktɪsɪŋ/ | v. |练习，实践 | Practising is crucial for mastering a skill. 练习对于掌握一项技能至关重要。|近义词：training, rehearsing, exercising |反义词：neglecting, ignoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am practising the piano every day. = 我每天都在练习钢琴。
He is practising his English speaking skills. = 他正在练习他的英语口语技能。

一般过去时:
I practised the guitar for two hours yesterday. = 昨天我练吉他练了两个小时。
She practised her presentation before the meeting. = 她在会议前练习了她的演讲。

现在完成时:
I have been practising yoga for three years. = 我练习瑜伽三年了。
They have practised hard for the upcoming competition. = 为了即将到来的比赛，他们刻苦练习。

过去完成时:
I had practised my speech several times before I gave it. = 我在演讲之前已经练习了好几次了。
She had practised the dance routine for weeks before the performance. = 在演出前，她已经练习了几个星期的舞蹈动作。

将来时:
I will be practising for the marathon next week. = 下周我将为马拉松比赛进行练习。
He will practise his driving skills before the test. = 他将在考试前练习他的驾驶技能。

虚拟语气:
If I practised more, I would play better. = 如果我练习更多，我会演奏得更好。
I wish I had practised harder for the exam. = 我真希望我为考试更加努力练习。

被动语态:
The techniques are being practised by many athletes. = 许多运动员都在练习这些技术。
The song was practised by the choir many times. = 这首歌被合唱团排练了很多次。

疑问句:
Are you practising for the upcoming exam? = 你在为即将到来的考试做准备吗？
What are they practising? = 他们在练习什么？

否定句:
I am not practising today. = 我今天不练习。
He didn't practise enough for the audition. = 他为试镜没有练习足够多。


PHRASE->
practise medicine = 从事医疗工作
practise law = 从事法律工作
practise regularly = 定期练习
practise makes perfect = 熟能生巧
practise what you preach = 言行一致


词源:
The word "practising" comes from the Old French word "practiser," which in turn comes from the Late Latin word "practicare."  These words ultimately derive from the Latin word "practica," meaning "practice" or "action".
