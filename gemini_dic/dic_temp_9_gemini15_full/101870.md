/ˌaɪsəˈtrɒpɪk/ | adj. | 各向同性的；等向性的 | Exhibiting properties with the same value when measured in all directions.  Isotropic materials have uniform properties in all directions. This is in contrast to anisotropic materials, which exhibit directionally dependent properties. | Example:  The material is isotropic, meaning its strength is the same in all directions.  这个材料是各向同性的，这意味着它的强度在各个方向上都是相同的。|近义词：homogeneous, uniform | 反义词：anisotropic
