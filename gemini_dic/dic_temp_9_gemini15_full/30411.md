centner:/ˈsɛntnər/| n.|百磅；担；（旧称）100公斤|A centner is a unit of weight, historically varying in value depending on location and time period.  Most commonly, it refers to 100 pounds (approximately 45.36 kilograms) in the United States and other countries using the avoirdupois system.  In some parts of Europe, it historically referred to 100 kilograms (approximately 220.46 pounds). The word's usage is now largely obsolete except in specific historical or regional contexts.|例句：The farmer sold a centner of wheat. = 农民卖了一百磅小麦。

The old scales measured weight in centners. = 旧秤以百磅为单位测量重量。|近义词：hundredweight (cwt)|反义词：None directly applicable, as it's a unit of measurement.
