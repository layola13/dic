prophesying:/ˈprɒfəsaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 预言；预示 |  The act of making a prophecy; foretelling future events.  | Example: The fortune teller was prophesying doom and gloom.  算命先生正在预言厄运。


The ancient oracle was prophesying the downfall of the kingdom.  古代神谕正在预言王国的灭亡。 | foretelling, predicting, forecasting | denying, refuting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is prophesying the end of the world. 他正在预言世界末日。
She prophesys good fortune for us. 她预言我们将会好运。


一般过去时:
The prophet prophesied a great famine. 先知预言了一场巨大的饥荒。
They prophesied victory for their team. 他们预言他们的球队会取得胜利。


现在完成时:
The seer has prophesied many things.  这位先知已经预言了很多事情。
She has been prophesying for over twenty years. 她已经预言了二十多年。


过去完成时:
He had prophesied the storm before it arrived. 他在暴风雨到来之前就预言了它。
They had prophesied the king's death many times. 他们已经多次预言了国王的死。


将来时:
The oracle will prophesy the future of the land.  神谕将会预言这片土地的未来。
They will prophesy great changes. 他们将会预言巨大的变化。


虚拟语气:
If I could prophesy, I would tell you your future. 如果我能预言，我会告诉你你的未来。
I wish I could prophesy the winning lottery numbers. 我希望我能预言中彩票的号码。


被动语态:
The future was prophesied by the ancient priest. 未来被古代祭司预言了。
The coming disaster was being prophesied by many. 即将到来的灾难正被许多人预言。


疑问句:
What is the prophet prophesying? 先知在预言什么？
Are they prophesying the arrival of a new age? 他们是在预言一个新时代的到来吗？


否定句:
The soothsayer did not prophesy accurately. 占卜者没有准确地预言。
They were not prophesying anything positive. 他们没有预言任何积极的事情。
