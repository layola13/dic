excavated:/ˈɛkskeɪveɪtɪd/| v.|挖掘；发掘；开凿|To remove earth carefully and systematically to uncover something hidden or buried, or to create a hole or space in the ground.  |例句：Archaeologists excavated the ancient city for many years. 考古学家们挖掘这座古城多年了。
例句：The workers excavated a new tunnel for the subway. 工人们为地铁开凿了一条新的隧道。|近义词：unearthed, unearthed, dug up, disinterred|反义词：buried, concealed, filled in|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Archaeologists excavate ancient sites. = 考古学家们挖掘古代遗址。
They excavate the foundations for the new building. = 他们挖掘新建筑的地基。


一般过去时:
The team excavated the Roman ruins last summer. = 团队去年夏天挖掘了罗马遗址。
They excavated a large pit in the garden. = 他们在花园里挖了一个大坑。


现在完成时:
They have excavated several important artifacts. = 他们已经挖掘出几件重要的文物。
Archaeologists have excavated much of the ancient city. = 考古学家们已经挖掘了大部分的古城。


过去完成时:
Before the construction, they had excavated the area. = 在施工之前，他们已经挖掘了该区域。
The workers had excavated the tunnel before the rain started. = 下雨之前工人们已经挖好了隧道。


将来时:
They will excavate the site next year. = 他们明年将挖掘该遗址。
The team will excavate further to find more evidence. = 团队将进一步挖掘以寻找更多证据。


虚拟语气:
If they excavated deeper, they might find more treasures. = 如果他们挖得更深，可能会发现更多宝藏。
I wish they had excavated the area more carefully. = 我希望他们当时更仔细地挖掘了该区域。


被动语态:
The ancient city was excavated by archaeologists. = 古城被考古学家挖掘。
The tunnel is being excavated at the moment. = 隧道正在被挖掘。


疑问句:
Have they excavated the entire area? = 他们已经挖掘了整个区域吗？
When will the site be excavated? = 什么时候挖掘该遗址？


否定句:
They did not excavate the site properly. = 他们没有正确地挖掘该遗址。
The workers haven't excavated the foundation yet. = 工人们还没有挖掘地基。


PHRASE->
