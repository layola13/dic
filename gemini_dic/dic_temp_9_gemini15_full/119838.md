milder:/ˈmaɪldər/| adj.|较温和的，较轻微的 | less severe, intense, or harsh; more gentle or temperate.  |例句：The weather today is milder than yesterday. 今天的天气比昨天温和。

例句：His symptoms are milder than expected. 他的症状比预期的轻微。


近义词：gentler, softer, less severe, temperate

反义词：harsher, severer, more intense, rigorous


词性：形容词
