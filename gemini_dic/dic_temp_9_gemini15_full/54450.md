disinheritances:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhɛrɪtənsɪz/ | n. | 剥夺继承权；被剥夺继承的财产 | The act of disinheriting someone, or the state of being disinherited;  the property or rights that have been lost through disinheritance.  | 例句：The old man's disinheritances of his son caused a family feud.  老人的儿子被剥夺继承权导致了家族纠纷。

The will detailed the disinheritances of his wayward daughter.遗嘱详细说明了他顽皮女儿被剥夺继承权的事宜。


近义词：disinheritance, deprivation of inheritance, exclusion from inheritance | 反义词：inheritance, bequest, legacy | 词性：名词
