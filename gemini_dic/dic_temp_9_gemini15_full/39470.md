connived:/kəˈnaɪvd/| v.|暗中勾结；纵容；默许|to secretly allow something bad to happen or to be done|The principal connived at the cheating students. 校长纵容那些作弊的学生。|近义词：collude, conspire, tolerate|反义词：oppose, prevent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He connives with his colleagues to embezzle company funds. 他与同事串通起来贪污公司资金。
They connive at the illegal activities in their neighborhood.  他们对他们邻居的非法活动视而不见。


一般过去时:
The manager connived at the employee's misconduct. 经理纵容了员工的不当行为。
She connived with her brother to deceive their parents. 她与弟弟串通起来欺骗父母。


现在完成时:
The government has connived at the corruption for years. 政府多年来一直纵容腐败。
They have connived to manipulate the market. 他们串通起来操纵市场。


过去完成时:
He realized that his friend had connived against him. 他意识到他的朋友曾经暗算过他。
It turned out they had connived to steal the jewels. 事实证明他们串通起来偷了珠宝。


将来时:
They will connive to get what they want. 他们将会暗中勾结以达到目的。
I suspect they will connive at the injustice. 我怀疑他们会纵容这种不公正行为。


虚拟语气:
If he had not connived with the others, he wouldn't be in this mess. 如果他不与其他人串通，他就不会陷入这种困境。
I wish they hadn't connived to ruin my reputation. 我真希望他们没有串通起来毁坏我的名声。


被动语态:
The plan was connived at by the higher-ups. 这个计划受到了上层的纵容。
The crime was connived at by several people. 这起罪行受到了几人的纵容。


疑问句:
Did they connive to overthrow the government? 他们是否串通起来推翻政府？
Do you think the police connived at the drug trade? 你认为警方是否纵容毒品交易？


否定句:
The judge did not connive at the defendant's lies. 法官没有纵容被告的谎言。
We will not connive at any illegal activities. 我们不会纵容任何非法活动。


PHRASE->
connive at something: 纵容某事
connive in something:  参与某事（通常指不好的事情）
connive with somebody: 与某人串通
conniving behavior:  阴谋诡计的行为
conniving look:  阴险的眼神
