inferiorities:/ɪnˌfɪəriˈɔːrətiz/| n.|劣势；缺点；自卑感|The plural of "inferiority," referring to instances or feelings of being lower in status, quality, or ability.  It can refer to objective shortcomings or subjective feelings of inadequacy. |例句：He overcame many personal inferiorities to achieve his goals. 他克服了许多个人缺点以实现他的目标。

例句：Her feelings of inferiority stemmed from a difficult childhood. 她的自卑感源于一个艰难的童年。

近义词：shortcomings, defects, weaknesses, flaws, handicaps

反义词：superiorities, advantages, strengths

词性：名词
