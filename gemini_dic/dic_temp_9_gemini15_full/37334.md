commencement:/kəˈmɛnsmənt/ | n. | 开始，毕业典礼 | The act of beginning something; a ceremony at which degrees or diplomas are conferred.  | Example: The commencement of the project was delayed by the bad weather. 项目的开始因恶劣天气而推迟。 | Example: The commencement ceremony was held in the university's main hall. 毕业典礼在大学的主礼堂举行。 |近义词：beginning, start, inauguration | 反义词：conclusion, end, termination |名词
