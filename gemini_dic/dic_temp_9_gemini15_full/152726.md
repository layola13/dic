peasantist: /ˈpɛzəntɪst/ | n. | 农民主义者；农民运动支持者 | A person who supports the rights and interests of peasants or farmers.  They advocate for policies and reforms that benefit rural populations.  This term is less common than "peasant" itself and often implies a political or activist stance. | Example: The peasantist leader rallied the farmers to protest the unfair land distribution policy.  农民主义者领袖团结农民抗议不公平的土地分配政策。|近义词：peasant advocate, agrarian reformer |反义词：landowner, aristocrat
