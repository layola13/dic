roustabout:/ˈraʊstəˌbaʊt/| n.|粗工，临时工，油田工人|A roustabout is a laborer who does unskilled work, especially in the oil fields or on a ranch.  They perform a variety of tasks, often involving heavy lifting and manual labor.|例句：He worked as a roustabout on an oil rig for several years. 他在油井钻台上做了几年的粗工。|例句：The ranch hired several roustabouts to help with the spring roundup.  牧场雇佣了几名粗工来帮忙春季赶牲口。|近义词：laborer, worker, hand|反义词：manager, executive
