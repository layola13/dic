fellowshipping:/ˈfɛloʊʃɪpɪŋ/| n. v.|  fellowship的动名词形式，友谊，交情；群体；分享；参与 |The act of engaging in fellowship; the state of being in fellowship.  It refers to the friendly association, companionship, or camaraderie between people.  It can also refer to the sharing of experiences, beliefs, or activities within a group or community. |例句：The church group enjoyed fellowship after the service.  教会小组在礼拜结束后享受了 fellowship。(n.)

The students found fellowship in their shared love of literature. 学生们在对文学的共同热爱中找到了 fellowship。(n.)


例句：They were fellowshipping together at the conference.他们在会议上互相交流。(v.)

We are fellowshipping with other believers. 我们正在与其他信徒交往。(v.)


近义词：communion, companionship, camaraderie, association, participation |反义词：isolation, estrangement, alienation |动词
