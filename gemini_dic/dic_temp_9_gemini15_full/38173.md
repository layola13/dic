compromit:/ˈkɒmprəmaɪt/ /ˈkɑːmprəmaɪt/| vt. n.|危及；损害；妥协；让步|To weaken or damage the strength or effectiveness of something; to endanger or jeopardize something.  To make a compromise. |例句：The scandal compromised the politician's reputation.  丑闻损害了这位政治家的名誉。

The leaking documents compromised national security.  泄露的文件危及国家安全。


近义词：jeopardize, endanger, impair, undermine, risk, threaten|反义词：strengthen, enhance, protect, safeguard|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He compromises his principles for money. 他为钱而牺牲自己的原则。
The government compromises with the protesters. 政府与抗议者妥协。


一般过去时:
The negotiations compromised both parties' interests. 谈判损害了双方的利益。
She compromised her position to reach an agreement. 她为了达成协议而让步。


现在完成时:
The company has compromised its data security. 公司已经损害了其数据安全。
They have compromised on several key issues. 他们在几个关键问题上达成了妥协。


过去完成时:
He had compromised his position before the meeting even began. 会议还没开始，他就已经妥协了自己的立场。
The hackers had compromised the system's security before anyone noticed. 黑客在任何人注意到之前就已经破坏了系统的安全。


将来时:
This decision will compromise our future plans. 这个决定将会危及我们的未来计划。
They will compromise on the price to close the deal. 他们将就价格进行妥协以达成交易。


虚拟语气:
If he compromised his values, he would regret it. 如果他放弃了自己的价值观，他会后悔的。
I wish I hadn't compromised my principles. 我真希望我没有放弃我的原则。


被动语态:
His reputation was compromised by the media. 他的名誉被媒体损害了。
The security of the network has been compromised. 网络安全已被破坏。


疑问句:
Will this action compromise our safety? 这一行动会危及我们的安全吗？
Has the deal been compromised? 这笔交易是否已经破裂？


否定句:
He refused to compromise his beliefs. 他拒绝妥协他的信仰。
The company didn't compromise on quality. 公司没有在质量上妥协。


PHRASE->
compromise with: 与…妥协
compromise on: 就…妥协
reach a compromise: 达成妥协
a compromised position: 危险的处境
seriously compromised: 严重受损
