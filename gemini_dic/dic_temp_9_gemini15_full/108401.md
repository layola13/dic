lawlessness:/ˈlɔːləsnəs/| n. |无法无天；违法；肆无忌惮|The state of being without law or control; disregard for or defiance of the law.  It often implies a widespread lack of respect for authority and social order.|例句：The city was plagued by lawlessness after the earthquake. 地震后，这座城市充斥着无法无天的情况。

例句：The increase in lawlessness is a serious concern for the police.  犯罪率的上升令警方深感担忧。


近义词：anarchy, disorder, chaos, mayhem

反义词：order, lawfulness, obedience, compliance

词性:名词
