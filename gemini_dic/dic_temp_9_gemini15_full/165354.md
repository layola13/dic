prejudged:/ˈpriːdʒʌdʒd/| adj.| 预先判断的；有偏见的 | Having been judged or decided beforehand, often unfairly or without sufficient knowledge.  It implies a negative judgment formed before having sufficient evidence. |例句：The case was prejudged by the media's biased reporting.  媒体的偏颇报道使此案未审先判。

例句：His prejudged opinion of the new employee proved to be incorrect. 他对新员工的先入为主的看法被证明是错误的。


近义词：preconceived, biased, predisposed, preordained, predetermined

反义词：unbiased, impartial, objective, open-minded


词性:形容词
