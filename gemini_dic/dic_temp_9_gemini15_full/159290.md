point-up: /pɔɪnt ʌp/ | v. | 指向上；指向；突出；强调 | To direct attention towards something, often upward; to emphasize or highlight something. |例句：The arrow points up to the north.  箭头指向北方。|例句：The teacher pointed up the most important points in the lecture. 老师强调了讲座中最重要的一些点。|近义词：indicate, highlight, emphasize, stress |反义词：point down, disregard, ignore


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He points up the flaws in the argument. = 他指出了论证中的缺陷。
The sign points up the direction to the beach. = 指路牌指示着去海滩的方向。

一般过去时:
She pointed up the inconsistencies in his story. = 她指出了他故事中前后矛盾的地方。
The speaker pointed up the need for reform. = 演讲者强调了改革的必要性。

现在完成时:
They have pointed up the risks associated with the project. = 他们已经指出了与该项目相关的风险。
We have pointed up the importance of early detection. = 我们已经强调了早期检测的重要性。

过去完成时:
He had pointed up the problem before the meeting. = 他在会议之前就已经指出了这个问题。
She had pointed up the dangers of the situation. = 她已经指出了情况的危险性。

将来时:
They will point up the key findings in their report. = 他们将在报告中突出重点发现。
The presentation will point up the benefits of the new technology. = 演示文稿将突出新技术的优势。

虚拟语气:
If he pointed up the issue earlier, the problem would have been avoided. = 如果他早点提出这个问题，问题本来是可以避免的。
I wish he had pointed up the potential risks. = 我真希望他早点指出潜在的风险。

被动语态:
The problem was pointed up by the consultant. = 这个问题是由顾问指出的。
The importance of teamwork was pointed up in the training session. = 培训课程中强调了团队合作的重要性。

疑问句:
Does the arrow point up or down? = 箭头是指向上还是向下？
What points did the speaker point up in his speech? = 演讲者在演讲中强调了哪些要点？

否定句:
The report didn't point up any significant problems. = 报告没有指出任何重大问题。
He didn't point up the difficulties involved. = 他没有指出其中涉及的困难。


PHRASE->
point up the importance of = 强调...的重要性
point up the fact that = 指出...的事实
point up a problem = 指出一个问题
point up the contrast between = 指出...之间的对比
point up the need for = 指出...的必要性
