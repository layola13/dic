jubilee:/ˈdʒuːbɪli/| n. |禧年，庆祝会 | A period of celebration or rejoicing, especially one marking a special anniversary.  Often refers to a 50th or other significant anniversary. |The Queen's Jubilee was a spectacular event. 女王的禧年是一个盛大的活动。|celebration, festivity, anniversary |mourning, sadness |名词
