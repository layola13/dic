north-west:/ˌnɔːrθˈwɛst/|/ˌnɔːrθˈwɛst/| adj. n. adv.|西北，西北方向的|North-west refers to the direction that is both north and west.  It can be used as an adjective to describe location or direction, as a noun to refer to a region, or as an adverb to indicate direction. |The wind is blowing from the north-west.  西北风正在吹。|northwestern, westerly|south-east, south-west
