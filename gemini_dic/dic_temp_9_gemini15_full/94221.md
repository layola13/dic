imperialisms:/ɪmˈpɪəriəlɪzəm/| n. |帝国主义；扩张主义|The policy of extending a country's power and influence through diplomacy or military force.|例句：European imperialism in Africa led to widespread exploitation and suffering.  欧洲在非洲的帝国主义导致了广泛的剥削和苦难。|例句：The legacy of imperialism continues to affect many nations today. 帝国主义的遗产至今仍影响着许多国家。|近义词：expansionism, colonialism, hegemonism|反义词：anti-imperialism, independence, self-determination|名词
