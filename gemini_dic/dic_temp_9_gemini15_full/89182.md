homeowner:/ˈhoʊmˌoʊnər/ | n. | 房主，业主 | A person who owns a home.  | Example:  He is a proud homeowner of a Victorian house. 他是拥有维多利亚式房屋的自豪房主。  |近义词：house owner, property owner | 反义词：renter, tenant | 名词

一般现在时:
The homeowner mows the lawn every week. = 房主每周都修剪草坪。
She pays her homeowner's insurance annually. = 她每年支付房屋保险费。


一般过去时:
The homeowner painted the house last summer. = 房主去年夏天粉刷了房子。
He sold his homeowner's property last year. = 他去年卖掉了他的房产。


现在完成时:
The homeowner has lived in this house for ten years. = 房主在这栋房子里住了十年了。
She has just paid her homeowner's association fees. = 她刚刚支付了她的业主协会费用。


过去完成时:
The homeowner had already paid the mortgage before the interest rate increased. = 在利率上涨之前，房主已经还清了抵押贷款。
They had owned the property for twenty years before they decided to sell. = 在他们决定出售之前，他们已经拥有该房产二十年了。


将来时:
The homeowner will renovate the kitchen next year. = 房主明年将翻新厨房。
He will likely sell his home in the spring. = 他很可能在春天出售他的房子。


虚拟语气:
If I were a homeowner, I would plant a garden. = 如果我是房主，我会种一个花园。
I wish I were a homeowner and could renovate my dream house. = 我希望我是房主，可以装修我的梦想之家。


被动语态:
The house was bought by the homeowner last month. = 这栋房子是房主上个月买的。
The property taxes are paid annually by the homeowner. = 房产税由房主每年支付。


疑问句:
Is the homeowner satisfied with their property? = 房主对他们的房产满意吗？
Does the homeowner plan to sell soon? = 房主计划很快出售吗？


否定句:
The homeowner did not attend the neighborhood meeting. = 房主没有参加邻里会议。
She is not responsible for maintaining the communal garden. = 她不负责维护公共花园。
