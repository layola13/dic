collude:/kəˈluːd/ | v. |共谋；串通；勾结 | To act together secretly or illegally in order to deceive or cheat someone.  |例句：The two companies colluded to fix prices. 这两家公司串通起来操纵价格。

例句：The politicians were accused of colluding to rig the election.  这些政客被指控串通舞弊选举。


近义词：conspire, plot, intrigue, cabal | 反义词：cooperate openly, act honestly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They collude to manipulate the market. = 他们串通起来操纵市场。
The companies collude to inflate prices. = 这些公司串通起来抬高物价。


一般过去时:
The executives colluded to hide the evidence. = 高管们串通起来隐瞒证据。
The two nations colluded against their common enemy. = 这两个国家串通起来对付他们的共同敌人。


现在完成时:
They have colluded to defraud investors for years. = 他们多年来串通起来欺骗投资者。
The evidence shows they have colluded to commit tax evasion. = 证据表明他们串通起来逃税。


过去完成时:
It turned out that they had colluded to steal the trade secrets. = 原来他们串通起来窃取了商业机密。
The investigation revealed that they had colluded before the crime. = 调查显示他们在犯罪之前就已经串通好了。


将来时:
They will collude to gain unfair advantage in the tender. = 他们将在招标中串通起来获得不公平的优势。
I suspect they will collude to suppress the truth. = 我怀疑他们会串通起来压制真相。


虚拟语气:
If they had not colluded, they wouldn't have gotten away with it. = 如果他们没有串通，他们就不会逍遥法外了。
I wish they hadn't colluded to ruin my business. = 我真希望他们没有串通起来毁掉我的生意。


被动语态:
The plan was believed to have been colluded in by several officials. = 人们相信这个计划得到了几位官员的串通。
It was revealed that the judges had been colluded against by the mafia. = 据透露，法官们受到了黑手党的串通。


疑问句:
Did they collude to achieve this outcome? = 他们是否串通起来达到这个结果？
Could the witnesses have colluded to give false testimony? = 证人们是否可能串通起来作伪证？


否定句:
They did not collude; they simply happened to agree. = 他们没有串通；他们只是碰巧意见一致。
The investigation found that the officials did not collude. = 调查发现官员们没有串通。


PHRASE->
collude with someone: 与某人串通
collude in something:  参与某事（串通）
collude to do something: 串通做某事
collude against someone:  串通反对某人
secretly collude: 秘密串通


词源:
The word "collude" comes from the Latin word "colludere," which means "to play together," or "to sport together".  Over time, the meaning shifted to imply a secret or deceitful collaboration.
