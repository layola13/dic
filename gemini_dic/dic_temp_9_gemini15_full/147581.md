overpessimistically: /ˌoʊvərˌpɛsɪˈmɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 过分悲观地 | Excessively or unduly pessimistic.  Characterized by extreme pessimism. | He overpessimistically predicted the company's failure. 他过分悲观地预测了公司的失败。  |  excessively pessimistic, unduly gloomy | optimistically, hopefully | 副词


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't have verb conjugations like "is," "was," "will be," etc.  It modifies verbs, describing how an action is performed.


例句1：She overpessimistically assessed the situation, leading to unnecessary anxiety. 她过分悲观地评估了局势，导致不必要的焦虑。

例句2：The team overpessimistically projected their sales figures for the quarter.  团队对本季度的销售额作出了过于悲观的预测。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "overpessimistically."  However, it can be used within phrases describing actions:

Phrase 1:  Overpessimistically evaluate = 过分悲观地评估
Phrase 2: Overpessimistically predict = 过分悲观地预测
Phrase 3: Overpessimistically anticipate = 过分悲观地预期
Phrase 4: Overpessimistically interpret = 过分悲观地解读
Phrase 5: Overpessimistically assume = 过分悲观地假设


词源:

* **over-**:  prefix meaning "too much," "excessively," or "beyond."
* **pessimistically**: adverbial form of "pessimistic," derived from "pessimist."
* **pessimist**: from Late Latin *pessimus* ("worst"), ultimately from Latin *pessimus* ("very bad").  A pessimist is someone who believes that bad things are more likely to happen than good things.
