scarcely:/ˈskɛrsli/| adv.|几乎不，简直不，刚刚|Hardly or barely; only just.|He scarcely ate anything all day. 他整天几乎没吃东西。| barely, hardly, just| abundantly, plentifully, copiously|副词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scarcely eats anything. 他几乎不吃任何东西。
She scarcely ever goes out. 她几乎不出门。


一般过去时:
I scarcely saw him yesterday. 我昨天几乎没看见他。
They scarcely believed their eyes. 他们简直不敢相信自己的眼睛。


现在完成时:
I have scarcely had time to breathe. 我几乎没有时间喘口气。
She has scarcely finished her work. 她几乎没完成她的工作。


过去完成时:
I had scarcely arrived when the phone rang. 我刚到电话就响了。
They had scarcely begun when it started to rain. 他们刚开始下起雨来了。


将来时:
I will scarcely have time to finish. 我几乎没有时间完成。
We will scarcely be able to manage. 我们几乎无法应付。


虚拟语气:
If I had scarcely any money, I would borrow some.如果我几乎没有钱，我会借一些。
I wish I scarcely had any work to do. 我希望我几乎没有工作要做。


被动语态:
The work was scarcely finished. 工作几乎没有完成。
The problem was scarcely understood. 这个问题几乎没有被理解。


疑问句:
Did you scarcely see anything? 你几乎什么也没看见吗？
Have you scarcely any time? 你几乎没有时间吗？


否定句:
I didn’t scarcely understand him. 我几乎没听懂他的话。
It was not scarcely possible. 这几乎是不可能的。
