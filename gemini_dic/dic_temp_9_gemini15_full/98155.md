insatisfaction:/ˌɪnsætɪsˈfækʃən/| n. |不满，不满意 |A feeling of dissatisfaction is a feeling of not being satisfied or content.  It often arises from unmet needs or expectations. |例句：Her dissatisfaction with her job led her to seek a new career.  她的工作不满让她寻求新的职业。

例句：The widespread dissatisfaction with the government's policies led to protests.  对政府政策的普遍不满导致了抗议。


近义词：discontent, displeasure, unhappiness, frustration

反义词：satisfaction, contentment, fulfillment, gratification

词性：名词
