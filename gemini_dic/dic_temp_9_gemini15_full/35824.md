cockle:/ˈkɒkəl/| n. |小海扇贝；(尤指)圣雅各贝；褶皱；(头发的)卷曲；|a small edible shellfish with a ribbed shell.  Also refers to a wrinkle or a curl (especially of hair).|The children loved collecting cockles on the beach.孩子们喜欢在海滩上收集小海扇贝。


The waves crashed against the cockleshells. 海浪拍打着贝壳。


近义词：mussel, clam, scallop | 反义词：smooth (in the sense of wrinkle)


词源:  From Middle English *cokel, from Old English *coccle, of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to the Old French word *coquille ("shell").
