outgrosses:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊsɪz/| v.|超过（票房收入）|To exceed in gross receipts, especially in box office revenue.|The new superhero movie outgrossed all expectations, earning over $1 billion worldwide.  这部新的超级英雄电影票房收入超过所有预期，在全球赚取了超过10亿美元。|surpass, exceed, outstrip|underperform, underachieve|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
That movie outgrosses all others in the market. = 那部电影的票房收入超过市场上所有其他电影。
This product consistently outgrosses its competitors. = 这款产品一直以来都比竞争对手卖得好。


一般过去时:
The concert outgrossed last year's event. = 这次演唱会的票房收入超过了去年的活动。
His latest novel outgrossed his previous works. = 他最新的这部小说比他之前的作品卖得好。


现在完成时:
The film has outgrossed all previous releases this year. = 这部电影今年的票房收入已经超过了之前所有上映的电影。
Their business has outgrossed its projections for the quarter. = 他们的公司本季度的营业额已经超过了预期。


过去完成时:
By the end of the week, the play had outgrossed its initial box office predictions. = 到周末为止，这部剧的票房收入已经超过了最初的预测。
The company had outgrossed its competitors for three consecutive years. = 这家公司连续三年超过了竞争对手的营业额。


将来时:
Analysts predict that the game will outgross its predecessor. = 分析师预测这款游戏将超过其前作的销量。
We expect the new album to outgross the last one. = 我们预计这张新专辑的销量将超过上一张。


虚拟语气:
If the marketing campaign had been better, the movie might have outgrossed its rivals. = 如果营销活动更好，这部电影的票房收入可能会超过竞争对手。
If only the product had been launched earlier, it could have outgrossed the competition. = 如果产品早点发布，它本可以超过竞争对手的销量。


被动语态:
The previous record was outgrossed by the new release. = 先前的纪录被新发行的作品打破了。
The sales target was easily outgrossed by the team. = 团队轻松地超过了销售目标。


疑问句:
Will this product outgross the previous version? = 这款产品会超过之前的版本吗？
Did the concert outgross the organizers' expectations? = 演唱会的票房收入是否超过了组织者的预期？


否定句:
The independent film didn't outgross the blockbuster. = 这部独立电影的票房没有超过大片。
The small business didn't outgross its larger competitor. = 这家小型企业没有超过其更大的竞争对手。
