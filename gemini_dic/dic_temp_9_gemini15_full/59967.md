edified:/ˈɛdəˌfaɪd/| adj.|教化的；启迪的；增进知识的 |To improve someone's mind or understanding; to instruct or enlighten.  |例句：The lecture edified the audience with its insightful analysis of the historical context.  讲座通过对历史背景的深刻分析，使听众受益匪浅。

例句：Reading classic literature can edify the mind and expand one's worldview. 阅读古典文学可以陶冶情操，开阔视野。


近义词：enlightened, instructed, educated, improved, cultured

反义词：debased, unenlightened, ignorant, benighted


词源:  From Middle English edifiën, from Old French edifier, from Latin ædificāre "to build," from ædes "building" + -ficāre (suffix forming verbs of making or doing). The sense shifted from "to build up (a structure)" to "to build up (knowledge or character)."
