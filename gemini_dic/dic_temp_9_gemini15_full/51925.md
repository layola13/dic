diabase:/ˈdaɪəbeɪs/| n. |辉绿岩|A dark-colored, fine-grained, intrusive igneous rock composed mainly of plagioclase feldspar and pyroxene.  It is often found as dikes and sills. |Example: The geologist identified the dark rock sample as diabase.  地质学家将黑色岩石样本鉴定为辉绿岩。The road cut revealed a significant diabase intrusion. 路堑露出了一个显著的辉绿岩侵入体。|近义词: basalt, dolerite|反义词: None specifically, but contrasting terms might include granite (felsic rock) or pumice (extrusive rock).|名词
