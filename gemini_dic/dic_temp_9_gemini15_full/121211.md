miseducate:/ˌmɪsˈɛdʒukeɪt/| vt.|教坏；误导；使受不正规教育|To educate badly or wrongly; to teach incorrectly or to instill wrong values or beliefs.|例句：The system miseducates children by focusing solely on standardized tests.  系统通过仅仅关注标准化考试来误导孩子。


例句：The biased history textbook miseducated generations of students.  这本有偏见的歷史教科書誤導了好幾代學生。


近义词：misinform, mislead, misguide|反义词：educate, enlighten
