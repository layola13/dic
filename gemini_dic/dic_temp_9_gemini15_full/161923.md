praise:/preɪz/| n., vt.|赞扬，表扬；赞美；祈祷|To express approval of or admiration for; glorify.  To offer prayers or thanks to a deity.|例句：The teacher praised her students for their hard work.  老师表扬了学生们的辛勤工作。

The choir sang praises to God.  唱诗班向上帝献唱赞美诗。|近义词：commend, applaud, acclaim, extol|反义词：criticize, condemn, blame, denounce|词性:名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He praises his team regularly. = 他经常赞扬他的团队。
She praises God every day. = 她每天都赞美上帝。

一般过去时:
They praised the performers after the show. = 演出结束后，他们称赞了表演者。
He praised her beautiful singing. = 他称赞她美丽的歌声。

现在完成时:
She has praised him many times for his courage. = 她多次称赞他的勇气。
We have praised the new policy for its effectiveness. = 我们已经称赞了这项新政策的有效性。

过去完成时:
They had praised the chef before the restaurant review was published. = 在餐厅评论发表之前，他们已经表扬了厨师。
She had praised his efforts throughout the project. = 在整个项目中，她一直称赞他的努力。

将来时:
He will praise her achievements at the ceremony. = 他将在仪式上赞扬她的成就。
The manager will praise the team's performance in the meeting. = 经理将在会议上赞扬团队的表现。

虚拟语气:
I wish he would praise my work more often. = 我希望他能更经常地表扬我的工作。
If she praised my efforts, I would be more motivated. = 如果她赞扬我的努力，我会更有动力。

被动语态:
His work was praised by his colleagues. = 他的工作受到了同事的称赞。
The new product was praised for its innovation. = 新产品因其创新而受到好评。

疑问句:
Will they praise the team's performance? = 他们会称赞团队的表现吗？
Did she praise your idea? = 她赞扬你的主意了吗？

否定句:
He didn't praise her work, even though it was excellent. = 尽管她的工作出色，他并没有表扬她。
They did not praise the design at all. = 他们根本没有称赞这个设计。


PHRASE->

beyond praise = 无法形容的赞扬
deserving of praise = 值得赞扬的
