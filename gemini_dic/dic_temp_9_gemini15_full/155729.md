phonecards:/ˈfoʊnˌkɑːrdz/ | n. |电话卡 |A phonecard is a prepaid card that allows you to make telephone calls.  You purchase the card and then use the PIN number to make calls from public phones or compatible devices. | I bought a phonecard to call my family overseas.  我买了一张电话卡给我的家人打越洋电话。| prepaid card, calling card |  (近义词无明显反义词，因为其功能性) | 名词
