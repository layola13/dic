overusing:/ˌoʊvərˈjuːzɪŋ/ | v. |过度使用，滥用 |The act of using something excessively or to an inappropriate degree.  It implies that the use is beyond what is necessary, reasonable, or beneficial. |例句：Overusing antibiotics can lead to antibiotic resistance.  过度使用抗生素会导致抗生素耐药性。|例句：He's overusing his phone and neglecting his studies. 他过度使用手机，忽略了学业。|近义词：misusing, abusing, exploiting, overworking |反义词：underusing, conserving, sparing
