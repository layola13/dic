ostensive:/ɒˈstɛnsɪv/| adj.| 明示的；明显的；炫耀的 | Serving to demonstrate or indicate something; demonstrative.  The ostensive definition of a word is the definition given by pointing to an example of the word's referent.  | 例句：The professor's ostensive teaching methods included many hands-on activities. 教授的直观教学方法包括许多实践活动。

The gesture was clearly ostensive, intended to show his displeasure.  这个手势显然是故意表现出来的，意在表明他的不满。 | 近义词：demonstrative, explicit, manifest, evident | 反义词：implicit, implied, tacit
