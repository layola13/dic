idea:/aɪˈdiːə/| n.|主意，想法，观念，概念|A plan or suggestion for doing something.  An understanding or belief. |例句：I have a great idea for a new business. 我有一个很棒的新生意主意。

例句：The idea of flying scares me.  飞行这个想法让我害怕。


近义词：concept, notion, thought, plan, scheme |反义词：reality, fact


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has a good idea. = 他有一个好主意。
What's your idea about this? = 你对此有什么想法？


一般过去时:
She had a brilliant idea yesterday. = 她昨天有了一个绝妙的主意。
I didn't have any ideas at the meeting. = 我在会议上没有任何想法。


现在完成时:
They have had many good ideas this year. = 他们今年已经有很多好主意了。
Have you had any new ideas recently? = 你最近有什么新想法吗？


过去完成时:
I had already had that idea before you mentioned it. = 在你提到之前我已经有那个想法了。
She had never had such a crazy idea before. = 她以前从未有过如此疯狂的想法。


将来时:
We will have a better idea tomorrow. = 我们明天会更有主意。
I think he will have a new idea soon. = 我认为他很快就会有新的想法。


虚拟语气:
If I had a good idea, I would tell you. = 如果我有一个好主意，我会告诉你的。
I wish I had a better idea. = 我希望我有一个更好的主意。


被动语态:
The idea was rejected by the committee. = 这个想法被委员会否决了。
His idea is being considered by the board. = 他的想法正在由董事会考虑。


疑问句:
Do you have any ideas? = 你有什么想法吗？
What is your idea? = 你的想法是什么？


否定句:
I don't have any good ideas right now. = 我现在没有任何好主意。
He doesn't seem to have many ideas. = 他似乎没有什么想法。
