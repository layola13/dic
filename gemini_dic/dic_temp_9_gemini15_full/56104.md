doctorize:/ˈdɒktəraɪz/| vt.|  使具有医生或医学的特征；篡改（文本等）；矫揉造作地治疗|To treat (something) in a way that is characteristic of a doctor; to tamper with (a text, etc.) in an officious or meddlesome way; to treat (an ailment) in an amateurish or excessive way.|例句：He tried to doctorize the report to make it look better. 他试图篡改报告使其看起来更好。|例句：She doctorized the wound with some questionable home remedies. 她用一些可疑的家庭疗法处理伤口。|近义词：tamper with, alter, modify, treat|反义词：leave alone, ignore
