coeditor: /koʊˈɛdɪtər/ | n. | 合编者，共同编辑 | A coeditor is a person who works jointly with another person or people to edit a publication.  They share the responsibilities and tasks involved in the editing process. | Example:  She served as coeditor of the journal for five years. 她担任该期刊的合编者五年。  The book's coeditors brought together a diverse range of perspectives. 这本书的合编者汇集了各种不同的观点。 |近义词：joint editor, associate editor | 反义词：sole editor
