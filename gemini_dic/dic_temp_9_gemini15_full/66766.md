exost:/ˈɛksoʊst/| n.|外骨骼；外生骨疣|A bony outgrowth from the surface of a bone.  Exostoses are benign (non-cancerous) tumors that can occur on various bones in the body.| 例句：The surgeon removed the exostosis from his ankle.  外科医生切除了他脚踝上的骨疣。  The patient presented with multiple exostoses on his skull. 病人颅骨上有多个骨疣。|近义词：bony growth, osteophyte|反义词：None.
