nonverticality: /ˌnɒnˌvɜːrtɪˈkælɪti/ | n. | 非垂直性，不垂直 | The state or quality of not being vertical; deviation from the vertical.  It often refers to a line, plane, or object that is not perpendicular to a horizontal plane. | Example: The nonverticality of the leaning tower of Pisa is its most striking feature.  比萨斜塔的不垂直性是其最显著的特征。 | 近义词：obliquity, slant, inclination | 反义词：verticality
