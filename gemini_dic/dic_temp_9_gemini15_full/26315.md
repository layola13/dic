buy-ins: /ˈbaɪɪnz/ | n. | 认同；买入；认购；预付款 |

英文解析:  "Buy-in" can refer to several things, primarily the act of purchasing something, especially a significant share or stake.  However, it also has a figurative meaning, indicating agreement, commitment, or acceptance of an idea, plan, or process.  The context determines the specific meaning.

例句:

The company secured a significant buy-in from major investors. = 公司获得了主要投资者的巨额投资。

The team needs a buy-in from all members to successfully complete the project. = 团队需要所有成员的认同才能成功完成项目。


近义词:  (depending on context)  agreement, commitment, acceptance, investment, purchase, subscription, participation

反义词:  (depending on context)  disagreement, rejection, opposition, withdrawal, divestment


词性: Noun
