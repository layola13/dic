interchangeable: /ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒəbl/ | adj. | 可互换的，可替换的 |

英文解析:Able to be exchanged or substituted for one another without causing a significant difference in the result.  Things that are interchangeable can be used in place of each other without affecting the outcome.

例句:

These two screws are interchangeable.  = 这两颗螺丝是可以互换的。

The terms "sofa" and "couch" are often interchangeable. =  “沙发”和“长沙发”这两个词经常可以互换使用。


近义词:replaceable, substitutable, equivalent, exchangeable

反义词:distinct, different, unique, irreplaceable


词源:

* **inter-**:  prefix meaning "between," "among," or "mutually."  源自拉丁语 "inter-"
* **change-**:  verb meaning "to alter," "to exchange." 源自古法语 "changier"  最终源自拉丁语 "cambiare"
* **-able**: suffix meaning "capable of," "able to be."
