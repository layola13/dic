slippingly:/ˈslɪpɪŋli/ | adv. | 滑溜地；偷偷地；不经意地 | In a smooth and effortless manner; stealthily or subtly.  | 例句：He moved slippingly through the crowd. 他在人群中偷偷地移动。

例句：The dancer moved slippingly across the stage. 舞者在舞台上轻盈地移动。


近义词：smoothly, stealthily, subtly, secretly

反义词：clumsily, obviously, openly, directly


词性:副词
