restitched:/ˌriːˈstɪtʃt/| v. |重新缝合；缝补|To sew something again, often to repair a tear or damage.|The tailor restitched the torn seam of my jacket.  裁缝重新缝合了我夹克衫撕裂的缝线。|The seamstress restitched the button that had fallen off.  女裁缝重新缝上了掉下来的纽扣。|近义词：repaired, mended, sewn again |反义词：unpicked, ripped, tore
