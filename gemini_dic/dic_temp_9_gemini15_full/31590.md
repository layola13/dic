chast: /tʃæst/ | v. | 惩戒；责备；使…悔改 | To punish or discipline (someone), especially as a means of correcting or improving their behavior.  |例句：The teacher chastened the student for talking in class. 老师因学生在课堂上讲话而责备了他。|  例句：He was chastened by the experience. 他从这次经历中吸取了教训。|近义词：rebuke, reprimand, scold, discipline |反义词：reward, praise, commend | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chastens his children regularly. = 他定期惩戒他的孩子们。
The teacher chastens students who misbehave. = 老师惩戒那些行为不端的学生。


一般过去时:
My father chastened me for breaking the vase. = 爸爸因为我打破花瓶而责备我。
She was chastened by her mistakes. = 她因自己的错误而悔过自新。


现在完成时:
He has chastened himself for his past actions. = 他因过去的行径而自责。
The experience has chastened her. = 这段经历使她变得成熟谨慎了。


过去完成时:
She had chastened him many times before he finally learned his lesson. = 在他最终吸取教训之前，她已经多次责备过他了。
They had chastened their son for his bad behavior repeatedly. = 他们曾多次因儿子的不良行为而责备他。


将来时:
I will chasten myself if I make a mistake. = 如果我犯了错，我会自责。
The judge will chasten the defendant for his lies. = 法官将因被告的谎言而责备他。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would chasten myself for my arrogance. = 如果我是你，我会为自己的傲慢而自责。
I wish I had chastened him sooner. = 我真希望我早点责备他。


被动语态:
He was chastened by the criticism. = 他受到了批评的惩戒。
The students were chastened by the principal for their disruptive behavior. = 学生们因扰乱秩序的行为而受到了校长的责备。


疑问句:
Will you chasten your child for his behavior? = 你会因为孩子的行为而责备他吗？
Should I chasten him for his mistake? = 我应该因为他的错误而责备他吗？


否定句:
I did not chasten him, even though he deserved it. = 即使他活该，我也没有责备他。
She will not chasten her son for such a small thing. = 她不会因为这么小的事情责备她的儿子。
