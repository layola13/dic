infection:/ɪnˈfɛkʃən/ | n. |感染；传染；侵染 |The invasion of the body by pathogenic microorganisms resulting in the development of disease.  Infection can be caused by bacteria, viruses, fungi, or parasites.  |例句：The doctor confirmed that the wound was a bacterial infection.医生确认伤口是细菌感染。

例句：The rapid spread of the infection overwhelmed the hospital's resources. 感染的迅速蔓延使医院的资源不堪重负。


近义词：contamination, infestation, contagion | 反义词：sterility, cleanliness | 词性：名词
