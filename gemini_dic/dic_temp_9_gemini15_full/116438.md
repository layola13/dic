matcher:/ˈmætʃər/| n. |匹配器，匹配者|A matcher is a person or thing that matches or compares items to find similarities or differences.  It can also refer to a piece of software or algorithm designed to find matches within data.|例句：The software uses a sophisticated matcher to identify similar images.  这个软件使用一个复杂的匹配器来识别相似的图像。|例句：He's a good matcher of wines and cheeses. 他很擅长搭配红酒和奶酪。|近义词：comparator, pairer, aligner |反义词：separator, differentiator
