equipollence:/ɪˈkwɪpələn(t)s/| n. |等值；同等；同等重要性 |The state of being equal in power, force, or value.  Equipollence often refers to the equivalence of two or more propositions or arguments, meaning they have the same logical weight or force.  It can also refer to the equivalence of different expressions or statements. |Example: The two arguments, while presented differently, showed equipollence in their support of the conclusion.  两个论点虽然表达方式不同，但在支持结论方面具有同等重要性。|近义词：equivalence, equality, parity |反义词：inequality, disparity, imbalance |名词
