micromanages:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌmænɪdʒɪz/ | vt. | 细致管理；过度干预；事无巨细地管理 | To manage something or someone with excessive control or attention to minor details.  | The CEO micromanages every aspect of the company, leaving little room for employee autonomy.  首席执行官事无巨细地管理公司的每一个方面，员工几乎没有自主权。 | supervise, oversee, control, direct | delegate, trust, empower | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He micromanages his team's work. = 他事无巨细地管理他的团队的工作。
She micromanages every detail of the project. = 她对项目的每一个细节都过度干预。


一般过去时:
He micromanaged the project to the point of frustration. = 他对项目的过度干预导致了大家的沮丧。
The manager micromanaged her employees' tasks last week. = 上周经理对员工的任务进行了过度干预。


现在完成时:
She has micromanaged her employees for years, stifling creativity. = 她多年来一直过度干预员工的工作，扼杀了他们的创造力。
He has micromanaged this project so much that it's delayed. = 他对这个项目的过度干预导致了项目的延误。


过去完成时:
They had micromanaged the previous project, leading to burnout. = 他们对之前的项目进行了过度干预，导致了团队的倦怠。
The team had micromanaged the campaign leading to its failure.  = 团队对该活动的过度干预导致了它的失败。


将来时:
He will micromanage the new team if he's not careful. = 如果他不小心，他会过度干预新团队的工作。
She will micromanage the budget, leaving no room for error. = 她会对预算进行过度干预，不容许出现任何错误。


虚拟语气:
If he didn't micromanage, his team would be more productive. = 如果他不过度干预，他的团队会更有生产力。
I wish he wouldn't micromanage every little thing. = 我希望他不必事无巨细地管理每一件小事。


被动语态:
The project was micromanaged, resulting in delays. = 项目受到了过度干预，导致了延误。
The employees felt micromanaged and undervalued. = 员工感到受到过度干预且不被重视。


疑问句:
Does he micromanage his employees? = 他是否过度干预他的员工？
Is she going to micromanage the new initiative? = 她会过度干预新的计划吗？


否定句:
He doesn't micromanage his team; he trusts them. = 他不干预他的团队；他信任他们。
She doesn't micromanage her staff; she empowers them. = 她不干预她的员工；她赋予他们权力。
