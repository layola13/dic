overoptimist: /ˌoʊvərˈɒptɪmɪst/ | n. | 过度乐观者 | An overoptimist is someone who is excessively or unrealistically optimistic; they tend to expect things to turn out better than is likely.  They may ignore potential risks or downsides. | Example:  He's such an overoptimist; he thinks he'll win the lottery even though he never buys tickets.  他是个过度乐观的人；他认为他会赢得彩票，尽管他从未买过彩票。| 近义词: Pollyanna, utopian,  unrealistic optimist | 反义词: pessimist, cynic, realist | 名词
