langoustine:/ˈlæŋɡəstiːn/| n. |龙虾;  挪威龙虾 |A langoustine is a type of marine crustacean, similar to a lobster but smaller and with a smoother shell.  It is highly prized as a delicacy. |例句：The restaurant is famous for its delicious langoustines. 这家餐厅以其美味的龙虾而闻名。

例句：We had grilled langoustines for dinner last night.  昨晚我们吃了烤龙虾。


近义词：prawn, lobster (though smaller than a true lobster), scampi
