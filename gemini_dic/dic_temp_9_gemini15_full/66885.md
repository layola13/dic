expede:/ɪkˈspiːd/|v.|加快，促进，迅速完成|To expedite something means to make it happen sooner or more quickly.|例句：The company expedited the shipping process to meet the customer's deadline. 公司加快了运输流程以满足客户的最后期限。例句：We need to expedite the repair of the damaged equipment. 我们需要加快损坏设备的维修速度。|近义词：accelerate, hasten, quicken, facilitate|反义词：delay, hinder, obstruct, impede


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They expedite the delivery process. = 他们加快了送货流程。
He expedites his work efficiently. = 他高效地完成了他的工作。

一般过去时:
The manager expedited the approval process. = 经理加快了审批流程。
She expedited the shipment to ensure timely arrival. = 她加快了运输速度以确保及时到达。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

She had expedited the visa application to avoid delays. = 她已经加快了签证申请以避免延误。

将来时:

否定句:
