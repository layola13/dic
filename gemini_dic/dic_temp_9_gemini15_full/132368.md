nondelinquent:/ˌnɑːnˈdɪlɪŋkwənt/| adj.|非罪犯的；未拖欠的；守规矩的|Not delinquent; not having committed a crime or offense; not overdue in payment.  This adjective describes someone or something that is not delinquent.  It implies a positive behavior or status, the opposite of delinquency.|

例句：

The nondelinquent youth participated in many community service projects. =  这位守规矩的青年参加了许多社区服务项目。

The company reported a record year with no nondelinquent accounts. =  该公司报告称，创纪录的一年中没有拖欠的账户。


近义词： law-abiding, compliant, punctual, up-to-date

反义词： delinquent, criminal, overdue, defaulting


词性: adjective
