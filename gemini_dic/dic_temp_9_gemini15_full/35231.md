clump:/klʌmp/| n., v.| 块；丛；笨重地走；| A clump is a small group of things, especially plants, growing or located close together.  As a verb, it means to walk heavily or awkwardly. |例句：There's a clump of trees near the river.河边有一丛树。 He clumped down the stairs. 他笨重地走下楼梯。|近义词：cluster, bunch, thud, stomp|反义词：scatter, disperse, tiptoe
