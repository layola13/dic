cardoon:/kɑːrˈduːn/ | n. |朝鲜蓟的一种，菜蓟 |A thistle-like plant (Cynara cardunculus) with large, edible stalks and leaves, closely related to the artichoke.  It is cultivated as a vegetable, particularly in the Mediterranean region. The stalks are typically peeled and cooked before being eaten. |例句：The cardoon plants thrived in the rich soil of the Mediterranean garden.  = 朝鲜蓟植物在地中海花园肥沃的土壤中茁壮成长。

例句：We enjoyed a delicious dish made with blanched cardoon stalks. = 我们享用了一道用漂白的朝鲜蓟茎做的美味佳肴。


近义词：artichoke (though technically distinct) | 反义词：无
