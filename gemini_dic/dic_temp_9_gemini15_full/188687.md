run-down:/ˈrʌnˌdaʊn/| adj. v.|破旧的；衰弱的；概述；| 英文解析：The adjective "run-down" describes something that is in poor condition, dilapidated, or neglected.  It can also describe someone who is weak and tired, often due to illness or exhaustion. As a verb, it means to summarize or give a brief account of something.|例句：The old house was run-down and needed repairs.  这栋老房子破旧不堪，需要修理。|近义词：dilapidated, neglected, shabby, worn-out, exhausted, weak|反义词：new, renovated, well-maintained, vigorous, healthy|词性：adjective, verb
