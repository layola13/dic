marshy:/ˈmɑːʃi/| adj.|沼泽的，湿地的|Of, relating to, or resembling a marsh; swampy.  Characterized by marshes or swamps.|例句：The marshy land was teeming with wildlife.  这片沼泽地里充满了野生动物。

例句：The path through the marshy area was muddy and difficult to navigate.  穿过沼泽地的小路泥泞不堪，难以通行。


近义词：swampy, boggy, miry, waterlogged, fenny

反义词：arid, dry, parched


词源:  From Middle English *marshy, from Old English *mersc (marsh) + -ig (suffix forming adjectives).  The root likely comes from Proto-Germanic *merkaz, and ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *mer-, meaning "lake" or "sea".
