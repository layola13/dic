extravascular:/ˌɛkstrəˈvæskjələr/ | adj. |血管外的，血管外渗的 | Outside or external to the blood vessels.  This term is frequently used in medical contexts to describe something located outside the vascular system.  | Example: Extravascular fluid accumulation can lead to edema. 血管外液体的积聚会导致水肿。 |近义词：extravascular, perivascular |反义词：intravascular
