calcinate:/ˈkælsɪneɪt/| vt.|煅烧；焙烧|To heat (a substance) to a high temperature, typically to drive off moisture or volatile matter or to cause decomposition.|The limestone was calcinated to produce quicklime. 石灰石被煅烧以生产生石灰。  The potter calcinated the clay to remove impurities. 陶工煅烧粘土以去除杂质。|近义词：roast, heat, burn|反义词：hydrate
