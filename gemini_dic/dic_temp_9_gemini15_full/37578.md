communalized:/kəˈmjuːnəlaɪzd/ | v. | 使公有化；使成为集体所有 | To make something communal or shared; to bring under communal ownership or control.  |The land was communalized after the revolution.  革命后土地被公有化了。|  nationalized, socialized | privatized, individualized | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They communalize the resources. = 他们使资源公有化。
The company communalizes its profits. = 公司将利润公有化。


一般过去时:
The government communalized the factories. = 政府将工厂公有化了。
They communalized the farmland last year. = 他们去年将农田公有化了。


现在完成时:
The village has communalized its water supply. = 村庄已经将其供水公有化了。
They have communalized many aspects of their lives. = 他们已经将生活的许多方面公有化了。


过去完成时:
The community had communalized its land before the war. = 在战争爆发前，社区已经将土地公有化了。
Before the reforms, they had communalized all the businesses. = 在改革之前，他们已经将所有企业公有化了。


将来时:
The council will communalize the park's facilities. = 理事会将把公园的设施公有化。
They will communalize the transportation system next year. = 他们明年将使交通系统公有化。


虚拟语气:
If they communalized the resources, it would benefit everyone. = 如果他们使资源公有化，将使每个人受益。
I wish they had communalized the utilities years ago. = 我希望他们几年前就把公用事业公有化了。


被动语态:
The resources were communalized by the government. = 资源由政府公有化。
The land is being communalized for the benefit of the community. = 土地正在被公有化，以造福社区。


疑问句:
Was the property communalized after the war? = 战争后财产是否被公有化？
Will the forest be communalized in the future? = 未来森林会被公有化吗？


否定句:
They did not communalize the forests. = 他们没有将森林公有化。
The company has not yet communalized its assets. = 公司尚未将其资产公有化。


PHRASE->
communalized resources 公共资源
communalized property 公共财产
communalized land 公共土地
fully communalized 完全公有化
partially communalized 部分公有化

词源:
From "communal" + "-ized".  "Communal" derives from the Latin word "communis," meaning "common."  The suffix "-ized" indicates the process of making something become something else.
