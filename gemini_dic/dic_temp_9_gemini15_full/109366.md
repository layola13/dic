lens:/lɛnz/| n. |透镜，镜头|A lens is a piece of transparent material, usually glass or plastic, that refracts light to form an image.  It's used in eyeglasses, cameras, telescopes, and microscopes. |Example: The camera lens captured a stunning sunset.  相机镜头捕捉到令人惊叹的日落景象。|近义词：optical element, objective|反义词：None.

词源:
The word "lens" comes from the Latin word "lens," meaning "lentil," a type of legume. This is because the shape of a convex lens resembles a lentil seed.
