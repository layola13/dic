neglectors:/nɪˈɡlektərz/ | n. |忽视者，疏忽者 |People who neglect their duties or responsibilities.  Those who consistently fail to provide necessary care or attention. |例句：The report criticized the neglectors who failed to provide adequate care for the elderly.  报告批评那些未能为老年人提供适当照顾的忽视者。

例句：Child neglectors face severe legal consequences.  虐待儿童者将面临严重的法律后果。


近义词：abandoners, disregarders, shirkers |反义词：carers, protectors, guardians |词性:名词
