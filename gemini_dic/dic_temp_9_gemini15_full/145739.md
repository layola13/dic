overcross:/ˈoʊvərkrɒs/ | v. | 交叉，横穿，超越 | To cross over or go beyond something; to intersect or overlap.  |例句：The two roads overcross in the city center. 这两条路在市中心交叉。
The climber overcrossed the treacherous ridge. 登山者越过了险峻的山脊。|近义词：intersect, traverse, transcend | 反义词：underpass, parallel


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The train overcrosses the river at this point. = 火车在这一点上跨过河。
The cables overcross each other several times. = 电缆彼此交叉了好几次。


一般过去时:
The hikers overcrossed the mountain pass yesterday. = 徒步旅行者昨天越过了山口。
The two lines overcrossed at a right angle. = 两条线成直角相交。


现在完成时:
The project has overcrossed its initial budget. = 项目已经超过了最初的预算。
They have overcrossed the boundaries of acceptable behavior. = 他们已经越过了可接受行为的界限。


过去完成时:
The bird had overcrossed the ocean before landing. = 这只鸟在着陆前已经飞越了海洋。
The negotiations had overcrossed several deadlocks before reaching an agreement. = 在达成协议之前，谈判已经克服了几个僵局。


将来时:
The bridge will overcross the highway next year. = 这座桥明年将跨越高速公路。
The plane will overcross the Atlantic in about six hours. = 飞机大约六小时后将飞越大西洋。


虚拟语气:
If the bridge had overcrossed the river further downstream, it would have been safer. = 如果桥在河的下游更远的地方跨过，它会更安全。
I wish the project hadn't overcrossed its budget. = 我真希望项目没有超过预算。


被动语态:
The river is overcrossed by several bridges. = 河流被几座桥梁跨越。
The deadline was overcrossed by several days. = 截止日期被超过了好几天。


疑问句:
Does the pipeline overcross any major waterways? = 管道是否跨越任何主要水道？
Where does the railway line overcross the mountain range? = 铁路在哪里跨越山脉？


否定句:
The road does not overcross the river at this point. = 这条路在这一点上没有跨过河。
The plan did not overcross the legal limits. = 这个计划没有超过法律限制。
