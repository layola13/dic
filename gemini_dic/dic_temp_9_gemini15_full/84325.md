halfbeak:/ˈhæfbiːk/| n. |半嘴鱼|A small, tropical fish with a short, beak-like mouth.  Halfbeaks are known for their ability to leap out of the water.|Example:  The halfbeak leaped from the water, catching a passing insect. 半嘴鱼跃出水面，抓住了一只飞过的昆虫。|近义词：needlefish (部分种类)|反义词：无
