mucivore:/ˈmjuːsɪvɔːr/| n. |黏液食者，食粘液动物|An animal that feeds on mucus or slime.  Mucivores often feed on the mucus secretions of other animals, or on slime molds.|Example:  Some species of snails are mucivores, consuming the mucus trails left by other snails.  某些蜗牛物种是黏液食者，它们以其他蜗牛留下的黏液痕迹为食。|近义词：slime-eater |反义词：无
