retrospection:/ˌretrəˈspɛkʃən/| n. |回顾；追溯；回想 |The act of looking back on or reviewing past events or experiences.  It often involves reflection and consideration of the significance of those events. |例句：His retrospection on his childhood was filled with both joy and sorrow. 他对童年岁月的回顾充满了喜悦和悲伤。

例句：Her retrospection revealed a pattern of missed opportunities. 她的回顾揭示了她错失良机的模式。

近义词：reflection, review, reminiscence, contemplation |反义词：prospection, anticipation
