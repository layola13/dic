reinvented:/ˌriːɪnˈvɛntɪd/| v. |重新发明；彻底改革；重新塑造|To create or develop something again in a new or different way.  It implies a significant change or improvement over the original.|例句：The company reinvented itself as a technology firm.  这家公司将自己改造成了一家科技公司。
例句：He reinvented the wheel with his new design. 他用他的新设计重新发明了轮子。|近义词：reimagine, revamp, transform, revolutionize |反义词：destroy, ruin, abolish

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reinvent their marketing strategies every year. = 他们每年都会重新设计他们的营销策略。
The company constantly reinvents its product line. = 这家公司不断地更新它的产品线。

一般过去时:
The artist reinvented himself after a long hiatus. = 在长时间的休整后，这位艺术家重新焕发了活力。
Ford reinvented the automobile with the Model T. = 福特用T型车彻底改变了汽车。

现在完成时:
They have reinvented their business model several times. = 他们已经几次重新设计了他们的商业模式。
She has reinvented herself as a successful entrepreneur. = 她已经成功地将自己塑造成一位成功的企业家。

过去完成时:
They had reinvented their approach before the project launched. = 在项目启动之前，他们已经改变了他们的方法。
The company had reinvented its image before the merger. = 在合并之前，该公司已经重塑了其形象。


将来时:
They will reinvent the way we communicate. = 他们将彻底改变我们的沟通方式。
The company plans to reinvent its flagship product next year. = 公司计划明年对其旗舰产品进行重新设计。

虚拟语气:
If they reinvented their process, they would be more efficient. = 如果他们改进流程，效率将会更高。
I wish they had reinvented the product before its release. = 我希望他们在产品发布前对其进行了重新设计。

被动语态:
The product was reinvented to appeal to a younger audience. = 该产品经过重新设计以吸引更年轻的受众。
The company's image is being reinvented through a new marketing campaign. = 公司的形象正在通过新的营销活动进行重塑。

疑问句:
Has the company reinvented itself successfully? = 这家公司是否成功地重塑了自己？
Will they reinvent the technology for better performance? = 他们会重新设计这项技术以获得更好的性能吗？

否定句:
They did not reinvent the wheel; they merely improved it. = 他们并没有重新发明轮子；他们只是改进它。
The company failed to reinvent its image effectively. = 该公司未能有效地重塑其形象。
