cramming:/ˈkræmɪŋ/ | v. | 临时抱佛脚；塞满；挤满 | To study intensively over a short period, especially just before an examination.  Also, to force or pack something tightly into a small space. |例句：I'm cramming for the history exam tomorrow. 我正在为明天的历史考试临时抱佛脚。


例句：The students crammed into the small classroom. 学生们挤进了狭小的教室。


近义词：overloading, stuffing, jamming, packing | 反义词：spaced, leisurely studying


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He crams for every exam. = 他每次考试都临时抱佛脚。
They cram their bags with souvenirs. = 他们把纪念品塞进包里。

一般过去时:
I crammed all night for the test. = 我为考试通宵达旦地复习。
She crammed her suitcase full of clothes. = 她把衣服塞满了她的手提箱。

现在完成时:
She has crammed for the exam all week. = 她为考试复习了一周。
We have crammed so much into our trip. = 我们在旅途中塞进了太多东西。

过去完成时:
He had crammed all the information into his head. = 他已经把所有信息都记在了脑子里。
They had crammed the boxes into the van. = 他们已经把箱子塞进了货车。

将来时:
I will cram for the exam this weekend. = 我这个周末将为考试临时抱佛脚。
They will cram all the books into the cupboard. = 他们将把所有的书塞进橱柜里。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I wouldn't cram. = 如果我有更多时间，我就不会临时抱佛脚了。

被动语态:
The information was crammed into the presentation. = 信息被塞进了演示文稿。

疑问句:

否定句:
