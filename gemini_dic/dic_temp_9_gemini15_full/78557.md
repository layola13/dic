ghostwriting:/ˈɡoʊstˌraɪtɪŋ/|/ˈɡəʊstˌraɪtɪŋ/| n. |代笔写作 |The act of writing something for someone else, who is then credited with the work.  This is often done for celebrities, busy executives, or those who lack the writing skills to express their ideas effectively. | Example:  Many famous authors use ghostwriters to help them meet deadlines or to write books in different genres.  许多著名的作家使用代笔来帮助他们赶上截止日期或创作不同类型的书籍。|近义词：writing for someone else, authorship by proxy |反义词：self-writing, independent writing
