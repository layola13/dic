dioeciousness:/ˌdaɪˈiːʃəsnəs/ | n. |雌雄异株；雌雄异体|The state of being dioecious; having separate sexes in different individuals.  Dioeciousness is a common reproductive strategy in plants and animals. |Example: The dioeciousness of holly trees means you need both male and female plants for berry production.  冬青树的雌雄异株特性意味着你需要雄性和雌性植株才能结果。|近义词：unisexuality, gonochorism |反义词：monoecy, hermaphroditism |名词
