rehandles: /ˌriːˈhændlz/ | n. |重新处理，重新操纵|The act of handling something again, often after a previous attempt or failure.  It can refer to physical objects or abstract concepts. |例句：The company had to rehandle the damaged goods before shipping them. 公司必须在发货前重新处理损坏的货物。


例句：The project manager decided to rehandle the budget after discovering several errors. 项目经理在发现几个错误后决定重新处理预算。


近义词：reprocess, rework, re-evaluate, reconsider |反义词：abandon, discard, leave
