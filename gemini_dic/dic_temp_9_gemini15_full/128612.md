nick:/nɪk/| n. v.|昵称；小缺口；轻敲；偷窃| A short form of a name; a small cut or notch; to make a small cut or notch; to steal. |例句：His nickname is Nick. 他的昵称是尼克。  The coin had a nick in its edge. 这枚硬币边缘有一个缺口。|近义词：nickname, dent, clip, pilfer|反义词：full name, complete, repair
