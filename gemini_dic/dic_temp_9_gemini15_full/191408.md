schemes:/skiːmz/ | n. |计划；阴谋；方案 |A plan or system for doing or achieving something, especially one that is scheming or underhand.  It can also refer to a systematic or organized plan of action. |例句：He devised a clever scheme to get rich quickly. 他想出一个快速致富的妙计。

例句：Their schemes to defraud the company were uncovered. 他们欺骗公司的阴谋被揭露了。

近义词：plans, plots, projects, strategies, designs | 反义词：accidents,chance, haphazardness | 词性: 名词
