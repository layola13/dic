rumba'd:/ˈrʌmbɑːd/| v. |跳伦巴舞；使…以伦巴舞步移动|  Past tense and past participle of "rumba". To dance the rumba. To move or proceed in a manner suggestive of the rumba. |例句：They rumba'd all night long. 他们整夜跳伦巴舞。 They rumba'd across the dance floor. 他们以伦巴舞步穿过舞池。|近义词：danced, swayed, moved|反义词：stood still, remained stationary|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He rumbas every Saturday night. = 他每个星期六晚上都跳伦巴舞。
She rumbas with grace and passion. = 她优雅而热情地跳伦巴舞。


一般过去时:
They rumba'd until dawn. = 他们跳伦巴舞跳到了黎明。
We rumba'd to the music of the band. = 我们随着乐队的音乐跳伦巴舞。


现在完成时:
I have rumba'd many times in my life. = 我一生中跳过很多次伦巴舞。
She has never rumba'd before. = 她以前从未跳过伦巴舞。


过去完成时:
They had rumba'd for hours before they got tired. = 他们在疲倦之前已经跳了几个小时的伦巴舞。
He had never rumba'd with such a skilled partner. = 他从未和如此熟练的舞伴跳过伦巴舞。


将来时:
We will rumba at the party tonight. = 我们今晚将在派对上跳伦巴舞。
They will rumba under the stars. = 他们将在星空下跳伦巴舞。


虚拟语气:
If I could dance, I would rumba. = 如果我会跳舞，我会跳伦巴舞。
I wish I could rumba as well as she does. = 我希望我能像她一样跳好伦巴舞。


被动语态:
The rumba was danced beautifully. = 伦巴舞跳得很美。
The steps of the rumba were explained clearly. = 伦巴舞的舞步解释得很清楚。(Note:  This is a less common usage for "rumba'd" in passive voice)


疑问句:
Did you rumba last night? = 你昨晚跳伦巴舞了吗？
Will they rumba at the wedding? = 他们会在婚礼上跳伦巴舞吗？


否定句:
I didn't rumba at the party. = 我在派对上没有跳伦巴舞。
They haven't rumba'd together before. = 他们以前从未一起跳过伦巴舞。
