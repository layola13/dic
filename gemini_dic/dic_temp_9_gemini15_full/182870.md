repenting:/rɪˈpɛntɪŋ/ | v. | 后悔；忏悔 |  expressing remorse for past actions or mistakes.  The act of repenting involves feeling sorrow and regret for wrongdoing and often includes a desire to change one's behavior. |例句：He spent his days repenting his past sins. 他终日悔恨过去的罪行。


He is repenting for his actions. 他正在为自己的行为忏悔。


近义词：regretting, atoning, feeling remorse | 反义词：celebrating, rejoicing


USAGE->

一般现在时:
I am repenting my decision. = 我正在后悔我的决定。
She repents her sins daily. = 她每天都忏悔她的罪过。


一般过去时:
He repented his hasty words. = 他后悔自己说过的话。
They repented their actions after the incident. = 事情发生后，他们后悔自己的所作所为。


现在完成时:
I have repented my mistakes and learned from them. = 我已经为我的错误忏悔并从中吸取教训。
She has repented her sins and found peace. = 她已经忏悔了自己的罪过并找到了平静。


过去完成时:
He had repented his decision before it was too late. = 在为时已晚之前，他已经后悔了自己的决定。
They had repented their actions long before anyone found out. = 在任何人发现之前很久，他们就已经后悔自己的所作所为。


将来时:
I will repent my actions if they cause harm. = 如果我的行为造成伤害，我会为此忏悔。
She will repent her sins and seek forgiveness. = 她会忏悔自己的罪过并寻求宽恕。


虚拟语气:
If I had known better, I would have repented sooner. = 如果我早知道，我就会早点后悔。
I wish I had repented earlier. = 我希望我早点忏悔。


被动语态:  (Repenting is rarely used passively)


疑问句:
Are you repenting your actions? = 你后悔你的行为吗？
Has she repented her past mistakes? = 她是否已经为过去的错误忏悔？


否定句:
He did not repent his actions. = 他没有为自己的行为忏悔。
She does not repent her decisions easily. = 她不容易后悔自己的决定。


PHRASE->

repent one's sins = 悔过自己的罪孽
repent in sackcloth and ashes = 穿麻衣披灰悔过 (表示极度悔恨)
repent at leisure = 从容悔过 (讽刺意味)
repent one's ways = 改过自新
deeply repent = 深深悔恨


词源:

源自古法语 *repentir,  最终源自拉丁语 *paenitere “to cause pain, to repent”.
