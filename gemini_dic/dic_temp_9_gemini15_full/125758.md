murdered:/ˈmɜːdəd/| v. |谋杀；杀害|To kill (a person) unlawfully and intentionally.|例句：The police investigated the murdered man's background.  警方调查了被谋杀男子的背景。|例句：He was murdered in cold blood. 他被残忍地杀害了。|近义词：killed, assassinated, slaughtered |反义词：saved, rescued


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He murders people for money. = 他为了钱杀人。
She murders the English language. = 她滥用英语。
