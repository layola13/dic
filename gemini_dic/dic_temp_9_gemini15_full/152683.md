peanut: /ˈpiːnʌt/ | n. | 花生 | A small, oval-shaped nut that grows underground, commonly eaten as a snack or used in cooking.  It is botanically a legume, not a true nut. | Example: I love eating roasted peanuts. 我喜欢吃烤花生。  He added some peanut butter to his sandwich. 他在他的三明治里加了一些花生酱。|近义词：groundnut |反义词:  (无直接反义词，可根据语境考虑反义词，例如：cashew, almond等其他坚果)|名词
