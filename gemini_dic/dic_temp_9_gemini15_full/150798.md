paradoxical:/ˌpærəˈdɒksɪkəl/| adj.|自相矛盾的，悖论的| seemingly contradictory but nonetheless possibly true.|The situation was paradoxical: the more he tried to save money, the less he seemed to have.  这种情况很矛盾：他越是想省钱，似乎就越没钱。| contradictory, inconsistent, anomalous|logical, consistent, straightforward
