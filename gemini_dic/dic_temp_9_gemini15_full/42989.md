covets:/ˈkʌvɪts/| vt.| 贪求；觊觎；渴望得到 |To wish for earnestly; to desire something belonging to another.|例句：She coveted her sister's beautiful necklace. 她贪求她姐姐美丽的项链。|例句：He coveted the power and influence of the CEO. 他觊觎首席执行官的权力和影响力。|近义词：desire, crave, yearn for, long for|反义词：disdain, reject, scorn


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He covets his neighbor's new car. = 他觊觎他邻居的新车。
They covet the success of their rivals. = 他们渴望获得像竞争对手一样的成功。


一般过去时:
She coveted that antique vase for years. = 她多年来一直渴望得到那个古董花瓶。
He coveted the position of department head. = 他一直渴望得到部门主管的职位。


现在完成时:
He has coveted that property since he first saw it. = 自从他第一次看到那处房产以来，他就一直觊觎它。
They have coveted the championship title for many seasons. = 他们已经觊觎冠军头衔很多个赛季了。


过去完成时:
She had coveted the painting long before she could afford it. = 在她买得起之前，她就一直渴望得到那幅画。
He had coveted that promotion for years, but never received it. = 他多年来一直渴望晋升，但从未得到。


将来时:
She will covet that designer dress once she sees it. = 一旦她看到那件设计师的衣服，她就会渴望得到它。
They will covet the award for their innovative work. = 他们将渴望获得他们创新工作的奖项。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would covet that diamond necklace. = 如果我有钱，我会渴望得到那条钻石项链。
I wish I didn't covet my friend's possessions so much. = 我希望我不那么渴望得到朋友的财产。


被动语态:
The prize was coveted by all the contestants. = 所有参赛者都渴望获得该奖项。
The position is coveted by many ambitious young professionals. = 许多雄心勃勃的年轻专业人士都渴望得到这个职位。


疑问句:
Does he covet anything in particular? = 他特别渴望得到什么吗？
What does she covet most in life? = 她一生中最渴望得到的是什么？


否定句:
He does not covet his brother's wealth. = 他并不贪图他兄弟的财富。
She did not covet the attention she received. = 她并不渴望得到她所受到的关注。


PHRASE->

to openly covet = 公开地觊觎
to secretly covet = 暗中觊觎
