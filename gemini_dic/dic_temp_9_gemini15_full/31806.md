checkouts:/ˈtʃɛkaʊts/ | n. |结账；结账处；退房 |The act of checking out of a store, hotel, or other establishment; the place where this occurs.  It can also refer to the process of completing a transaction. |例句：The checkouts were crowded during the lunch rush.  午餐高峰期收银台挤满了人。

There were long lines at the supermarket checkouts. 超市收银台排起了长队。


近义词：check-out counters, payment points, tills | 反义词：check-in


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The customer checks out at the counter. =顾客在柜台结账。
He always checks out at the self-service checkout. = 他总是使用自助结账。


一般过去时:
She checked out of the hotel yesterday. = 她昨天退了酒店的房。
We checked out quickly after dinner. = 我们吃完饭后迅速结账离开了。


现在完成时:
I have already checked out of my hotel. = 我已经退了酒店的房。
Have you checked out all the items you wanted? = 你已经结账买下所有想买的东西了吗？


过去完成时:
They had checked out before the manager arrived. = 经理到达之前他们已经结账离开了。
We had checked out all the books before the library closed. = 图书馆关门前我们已经把所有书都退还了。


将来时:
We will check out of the hotel tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上将要退酒店的房。
I will check out all the new products in the store. = 我将要查看商店里所有新产品。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would check out that new restaurant. = 如果我有更多时间，我会去那家新餐厅吃饭。
I wish I could check out that interesting antique shop. = 我真希望我能去那家有趣的古董店看看。


被动语态:
The items were checked out by the cashier. = 这些商品由收银员结账。
The hotel room will be checked out by the guest. = 酒店房间将由客人退房。


疑问句:
Have you checked out yet? = 你结账了吗？
Did you check out the new exhibit at the museum? = 你去博物馆看了新的展览了吗？（这里checkout指查看）


否定句:
I haven't checked out yet. = 我还没结账。
He didn't check out the details carefully. = 他没有仔细查看细节。（这里checkout指查看）
