expatiating:/ɪkˈspeɪtieɪtɪŋ/ | v. |详述；详谈；滔滔不绝地谈论 | To speak or write at length or in detail.  |例句：He expatiated on the virtues of his new invention. 他详细地阐述了他新发明的优点。

例句：The professor expatiated on the complexities of quantum physics for over an hour. 教授滔滔不绝地讲解了量子物理的复杂性，持续了一个多小时。


近义词：elaborate, enlarge, dwell on, discourse, descant

反义词：summarize, abridge, condense


词源:  From Latin *expatiari "to wander about," from ex- "out" + pati "to suffer, undergo, experience."  The sense evolved from "to wander freely" to "to speak or write at length."
