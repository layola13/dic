figments:/ˈfɪɡmənts/| n. |虚构的事物；幻想；臆想 |Something that is imagined or invented and is not real.  It refers to products of the imagination, often appearing in dreams or delusions. |例句：His fears were mere figments of his imagination. 他的恐惧只是他想象出来的东西。

例句：Those stories are just figments of his overactive imagination. 那些故事只是他过度活跃的想象力编造出来的。


近义词：illusions, fantasies, fabrications, inventions, chimeras


反义词：realities, truths, facts


词性:名词
