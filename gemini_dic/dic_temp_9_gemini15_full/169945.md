prostomial:/prɒˈstoʊmiəl/| adj.|前口区的；前体节的|Relating to the prostomium, the anteriormost region of the body in certain segmented animals, particularly annelids.  It often bears sensory organs.|例句：The prostomial appendages of the polychaete worm were highly sensitive to changes in water current.  多毛类蠕虫的前口区附属物对水流变化非常敏感。


例句：Scientists studied the prostomial sensory organs to understand the worm's behavior. 科学家研究了蠕虫的前口区感觉器官以了解其行为。

近义词：anterior, preoral (in some contexts)

反义词：posterior


词性:形容词
