herr:/hɛr/| n.|先生|German title of respect for a man, equivalent to "Mr." in English.  It is commonly used in German-speaking countries.  |Example: Herr Schmidt is a very kind man.  施密特先生是一位非常善良的人。|近义词：Mr., sir|反义词：Frau (Mrs.)


USAGE->
This word, being a title, doesn't have typical verb conjugations.  Its usage is restricted to its function as a title.


PHRASE->
Herr Doktor: Doctor (Mr. Doctor)
Herr Professor: Professor (Mr. Professor)
Herr Präsident: President (Mr. President)
Herr Bürgermeister: Mayor (Mr. Mayor)
Herr Oberst: Colonel (Mr. Colonel)
