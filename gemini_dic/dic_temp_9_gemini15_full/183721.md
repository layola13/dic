resamples:/ˈriːsæmpl/| v.|重新采样，再抽样|To sample again; to create a new sample from an existing dataset by randomly selecting elements with replacement.  This technique is often used in statistical analysis and machine learning to improve the accuracy and robustness of models.  |例句：We resampled the data to reduce the impact of outliers. 我们重新采样数据以减少异常值的影响。

例句：The researchers resampled the dataset multiple times to assess the stability of their findings. 研究人员多次重新采样数据集以评估其发现的稳定性。


近义词：resample, re-sample, bootstrap


反义词：无


词性：动词
