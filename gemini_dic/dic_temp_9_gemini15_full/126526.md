nabbers:/ˈnæbərz/| n.|抓捕者，窃贼|A nabber is a person who steals things, often informally.  It's a slang term.|例句：The police finally caught the nabbers who had been stealing cars from the neighborhood.  警察最终抓到了那些一直在从附近偷车的窃贼。|近义词：thief, robber, pilferer, crook|反义词：police, law enforcement


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The police nabbers criminals every day. = 警察每天抓捕罪犯。
He nabbers apples from the neighbor's tree. = 他从邻居的树上偷苹果。


一般过去时:
The detective nabbed the thief red-handed. = 侦探当场抓获了小偷。
They nabbed the suspect near the bank. = 他们在银行附近抓获了嫌疑人。


现在完成时:
The police have nabbed several suspects in the robbery. = 警方已在抢劫案中抓获了几名嫌疑人。
They have nabbed all the burglars involved in the case. = 他们已经抓获了参与此案的所有窃贼。


过去完成时:
The police had already nabbed the suspect before the witness arrived. = 证人到达之前，警方已经抓获了嫌疑人。
They had nabbed the culprit before he could escape. = 他们在罪犯逃跑之前就抓住了他。


将来时:
The police will nab the fugitive soon. = 警方很快就会抓住逃犯。
We will nab him if he tries to run away. = 如果他试图逃跑，我们会抓住他。


虚拟语气:
If the police had nabbed him earlier, the crime wouldn't have happened. = 如果警方早点抓住他，罪案就不会发生了。
I wish they had nabbed the thief before he escaped. = 我真希望他们在小偷逃跑前就抓住他。


被动语态:
The thief was nabbed by the police. = 小偷被警察抓住了。
The stolen goods were nabbed back by the police. = 赃物被警方追回。


疑问句:
Were the robbers nabbed? = 抢劫犯被抓住了吗？
Has the suspect been nabbed yet? = 嫌疑人已经被抓了吗？


否定句:
The police didn't nab the criminal. = 警察没有抓住罪犯。
They haven't nabbed the right person yet. = 他们还没抓到正确的人。
