ruinating: /ˈruːɪneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 毁坏；破坏；糟蹋 |  To ruin or spoil something completely.  |例句：His careless spending was ruining his family.  他的挥霍无度正在毁了他的家庭。
例句：The storm was ruining the crops. 暴风雨正在毁坏庄稼。|近义词： destroying, wrecking, spoiling, damaging |反义词：improving, enhancing, building, repairing | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The constant noise is ruining my concentration. = 持续的噪音正在毁掉我的注意力。
He is ruining his health by smoking. = 他抽烟正在毁掉他的健康。

一般过去时:
The heavy rain ruined the picnic. = 大雨毁了野餐。
The scandal ruined his reputation. = 丑闻毁了他的名誉。

现在完成时:
Years of neglect have ruined the building. = 多年的忽视已经毁掉了这座建筑。
His gambling has ruined his family financially. = 他的赌博已经让他的家庭破产了。

过去完成时:
They had ruined their chances of success by being careless. = 他们因为粗心大意而毁掉了他们成功的机会。
The company had ruined its reputation through unethical practices. = 这家公司由于不道德行为而毁掉了自己的声誉。

将来时:
The new policy will ruin the small businesses. = 新政策将毁掉小型企业。
If you continue this way, you will ruin your life. = 如果你继续这样下去，你将会毁掉你的生活。

虚拟语气:
If he hadn't been so careless, he wouldn't have ruined the project. = 如果他不那么粗心大意，他就不会毁掉这个项目了。
I wish I hadn't ruined our friendship. = 我真希望我没有毁掉我们的友谊。

被动语态:
The crops were ruined by the frost. = 庄稼被霜冻毁了。
His career was ruined by a single mistake. = 他的事业因一个错误而毁了。

疑问句:
Is this project ruining your weekend? = 这个项目是不是毁掉了你的周末？
Are your bad habits ruining your health? = 你的坏习惯是不是正在毁掉你的健康？

否定句:
The rain didn't ruin the party. = 雨没有毁掉聚会。
He didn't ruin his chances of getting the job. = 他没有毁掉他得到这份工作的机会。


PHRASE->

ruin the environment = 破坏环境
ruin the mood = 破坏气氛
