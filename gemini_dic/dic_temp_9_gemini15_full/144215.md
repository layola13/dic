outskates:/ˌaʊtˈskeɪts/| v. |胜过(尤指在滑冰方面)|To skate faster or better than (someone).  This verb implies a competition or comparison in skating skill.|例句：He outskated all his opponents in the final race. 他在决赛中滑冰速度超过了所有对手。

例句：The young skater outskated the veteran champion, surprising everyone.  年轻的滑冰选手滑冰速度超过了资深冠军，让所有人都大吃一惊。


近义词：outperform, surpass, exceed, outstrip


反义词：be outskated by


词性: 动词
