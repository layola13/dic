sistrum:/ˈsɪstrəm/| n. |圣器，拨浪鼓|A sistrum is an ancient Egyptian percussion instrument consisting of a U-shaped frame with metal bars or rods that jingle when shaken.  It was often used in religious ceremonies and associated with the goddess Isis. |The priestess shook the sistrum rhythmically during the ceremony.|  近义词：rattle, shaker |反义词：无
