plummeting:/ˈplʌmɪtɪŋ/| v.| 骤降，急剧下降 | 急剧下降；垂直落下 | The plane plummeted to the ground.  飞机坠落地面。|The price of oil plummeted after the news.  石油价格在消息公布后暴跌。|  plunging, diving, dropping | rising, soaring, climbing | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The value of the stock is plummeting. = 股票价值正在暴跌。
The temperature is plummeting rapidly. = 温度正在迅速下降。


一般过去时:
The bird plummeted from the sky. = 鸟从空中坠落。
Sales plummeted after the scandal broke. = 丑闻爆发后，销售额暴跌。


现在完成时:
The economy has plummeted since the crisis. = 自危机以来，经济已经暴跌。
The value of the property has plummeted recently. = 最近房产价值暴跌。


过去完成时:
The stock price had plummeted before the announcement. = 在公告发布之前，股价已经暴跌。
The temperature had plummeted overnight. = 温度一夜之间骤降。


将来时:
The company's profits are expected to plummet next quarter. = 预计该公司下季度的利润将暴跌。
If the trend continues, the population will plummet. = 如果这种趋势继续下去，人口将会骤降。


虚拟语气:
If the government didn't intervene, the economy would plummet. = 如果政府不干预，经济就会暴跌。
I wish the crime rate hadn't plummeted so drastically. = 我真希望犯罪率没有下降得这么厉害。


被动语态:

The company's reputation was plummeted by the negative publicity. = 该公司的声誉因负面宣传而受损。


疑问句:
Is the unemployment rate plummeting? = 失业率正在下降吗？

否定句:
The sales figures did not plummet as expected. = 销售额并没有像预期的那样暴跌。
The support for the government didn't plummet after the scandal. = 丑闻发生后，政府的支持率并没有下降。

词源:
The word "plummet" ultimately derives from the Old French word "plom," meaning "lead."  This is because plummets were originally weights made of lead used for sounding depths or for fishing. The sense of "falling rapidly" developed from the image of a lead weight falling quickly.
