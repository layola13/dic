词源:
The word "nonreclusive" is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the adjective "reclusive."  "Reclusive" comes from the Late Latin word "recludere," meaning "to shut back, withdraw."  Therefore, nonreclusive directly contrasts the meaning of withdrawing or isolating oneself.
