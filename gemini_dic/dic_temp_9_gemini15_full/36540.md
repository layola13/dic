collaborators:/kəˈlæbəreɪtərz/ | n. |合作者，协作者 |People who work together on a project or task.  Collaborators pool their resources and skills to achieve a common goal. |Example: The scientists and their collaborators published a groundbreaking paper on climate change.  科学家和他们的合作者发表了一篇关于气候变化的开创性论文。|近义词：coworkers, partners, associates |反义词：competitors, rivals

词源:
The word "collaborators" comes from the verb "collaborate," which originates from the Latin word "collaborare," meaning "to work together."  The Latin prefix "col-" means "together," and "laborare" means "to work."  Therefore, the word's roots directly indicate the essence of working jointly.
