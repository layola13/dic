infallible: /ɪnˈfæləbl/ | adj. | 无谬的；不会犯错的 | incapable of failure or error; certain to succeed or prove true.  | The system is not infallible, and errors can occur. 这个系统并非无懈可击，可能会发生错误。 |  unerring, certain, impeccable | fallible, erroneous, flawed | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is infallible in his judgment. = 他在判断上从不犯错。
His method is infallible. = 他的方法是万无一失的。

一般过去时:
The doctor's diagnosis was infallible. = 这位医生的诊断是准确无误的。
His prediction proved to be infallible. = 他的预言被证明是正确的。

现在完成时:
His advice has always proved infallible. = 他的建议一直被证明是正确的。
His guidance has been infallible throughout the project. = 在整个项目中，他的指导都非常可靠。


过去完成时:
They had believed his judgment to be infallible. = 他们曾认为他的判断是不会错的。
It had been thought that his method was infallible.  = 人们曾认为他的方法是不会出错的。


将来时:
This new technology will make the system virtually infallible. = 这项新技术将使该系统几乎不会出错。
His plan will infallibly lead to success. = 他的计划一定会成功。


虚拟语气:
If his judgment were infallible, we wouldn't have made this mistake. = 如果他的判断是不会错的，我们就不会犯这个错误了。
If the system were infallible, there would be no errors. = 如果系统是不会出错的，就不会有错误了。


被动语态:
His methods are considered infallible by many experts. = 他的方法被许多专家认为是不会出错的。
His predictions were believed to be infallible. = 人们相信他的预测是准确无误的。


疑问句:
Is his judgment really infallible? = 他的判断真的不会错吗？
Can we rely on a system that is not infallible? = 我们能依赖一个并非万无一失的系统吗？


否定句:
His judgment is not infallible; he makes mistakes sometimes. = 他的判断并非总是正确的；他有时会犯错。
No system is truly infallible. = 没有哪个系统是真正不会出错的。


PHRASE->
infallible truth = 不容置疑的真理
infallible method = 万无一失的方法
infallible guide = 可靠的向导
infallible instinct = 天生的直觉
infallible proof = 确凿的证据

词源:
From Middle English *infallibil, from Old French infallible, from Latin infallibilis, from in- "not" + fallibilis "liable to err," from falli "to err, deceive" + -bilis "-able".
