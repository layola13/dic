gabioned:/ˈɡæbiənd/| adj.|用石笼护坡的；用石笼固定的|Reinforced with gabions;  constructed using gabions.  The term describes structures or slopes that have been stabilized or reinforced using gabions (wire mesh containers filled with rocks or other materials).|例句：The riverbank was gabioned to prevent erosion. = 河岸用石笼加固以防止侵蚀。

The steep slope was gabioned to improve stability. =陡坡用石笼加固以提高稳定性。|近义词：reinforced, stabilized, buttressed|反义词：unprotected, unsupported
