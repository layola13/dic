preissue:/ˈpriːɪʃuː/| v.|预发行，预先发行 |To issue something before the official release date.|The company will preissue the software to beta testers.|近义词：precede, antecede|反义词：postpone, delay


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They preissue new software to select users. = 他们将新软件预发行给特定用户。
The bank preissues new debit cards monthly. = 银行每月预发行新的借记卡。


一般过去时:
The publisher preissued the book to reviewers. = 出版商将书预发行给评论员。
We preissued the report to the committee members. = 我们将报告预发行给委员会成员。


现在完成时:
They have preissued several versions of the game. = 他们已经预发行几个版本的这个游戏。
The government has preissued the disaster relief funds. = 政府已经预发行救灾资金。


过去完成时:
The team had preissued the tickets before the official sale. = 团队在正式销售前已经预发了门票。
She had preissued the invitations a week prior to the event. = 她在活动前一周已经预发了邀请函。


将来时:
We will preissue the album next week. = 我们下周将预发行专辑。
The company will preissue the update to its loyal customers. = 公司将把更新预发行给忠实客户。


虚拟语气:
If they preissued the information earlier, the crisis could have been avoided. = 如果他们早点预发行信息，危机本来是可以避免的。
I wish they had preissued the tickets, it would have saved me a lot of time. = 我希望他们预发了门票，那将会节省我很多时间。


被动语态:
The software was preissued to a select group of users. = 该软件预发行给了一组选定的用户。
The tickets were preissued to the VIP guests. = 门票预发行给了贵宾。


疑问句:
Will the tickets be preissued? = 门票会预发行吗？
Have they preissued the report yet? = 他们已经预发行报告了吗？


否定句:
They did not preissue the update. = 他们没有预发行更新。
The company will not preissue the product to the public. = 公司不会将产品预发行给公众。


PHRASE->

preissue a statement = 预先发表声明
preissue a warning = 预先发出警告
preissue a report = 预先发布报告
preissue an announcement = 预先发布公告
preissue a document = 预先发布文件
