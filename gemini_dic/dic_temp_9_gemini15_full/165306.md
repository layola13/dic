preinterviews:/ˌpriːˈɪntərvjuːz/ | n. |初步面试；预先面试 | Preliminary interviews conducted before the main interviews.  These screenings often involve phone calls or short online assessments to filter out unsuitable candidates. | Example: The preinterviews helped to narrow down the applicant pool to a manageable number.  初步面试帮助将申请人范围缩小到可管理的数量。 |近义词：screening, preliminary selection |反义词：final interview
