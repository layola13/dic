pressed:/prɛst/ | adj., v. | 压制的；压迫的；紧迫的；恳求；熨烫 |

英文解析：As an adjective, "pressed" means subjected to pressure or force; urgent or compelling. As a verb, it means to exert pressure on something; to urge or implore someone; to iron clothing.

例句：

The pressed flowers were beautifully preserved.  = 被压制的鲜花保存得很好。

He felt pressed for time to finish the project. = 他感到完成项目的时间紧迫。


近义词：compelled, urgent, squeezed, ironed, pleaded


反义词：relaxed, unhurried, loose, unpressed


词性：adjective, verb
