caked:/keɪkt/| adj. |覆盖着…的；结块的；凝固的|Covered or coated with a substance resembling cake; solidified or clumped together.|The cake batter is caked with flour.|近义词：coated, covered, encrusted|反义词：uncovered, clean


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mud is caked on his shoes. = 泥巴粘在他的鞋子上。
The floor is caked with dirt. = 地板上一层厚厚的灰尘。


一般过去时:
His hands were caked in mud after gardening. = 他园艺后，双手沾满了泥土。
The paint caked on the brush after drying. = 油漆干后在刷子上结块了。


现在完成时:
The snow has caked on the car's windshield. = 雪已经粘在汽车挡风玻璃上了。
The sugar has caked in the bottom of the bag. = 糖在袋底结块了。


过去完成时:
The ice had caked on the pipes before the plumber arrived. = 水管工到达之前，冰已经结在水管上了。
The grease had caked on the engine parts, making them difficult to clean. = 润滑油已经结在引擎部件上，很难清洗。


将来时:
The rain will cake the soil together. = 雨会把土壤粘在一起。
The icing will cake if it is stored improperly. = 如果储存不当，糖霜会结块。


虚拟语气:
If the batter were caked, it would be difficult to spread. = 如果面糊结块了，就很难涂抹。
I wish the mud weren't caked on my boots. = 我真希望泥巴没有粘在我的靴子上。


被动语态:
The road was caked with snow after the blizzard. = 暴风雪过后，道路被雪覆盖了。
The engine parts were caked with grease. = 引擎部件上沾满了油脂。


疑问句:
Is the chocolate caked on the sides of the pan? = 巧克力粘在平底锅侧面了吗？
Are your shoes caked with mud? = 你的鞋子沾满泥巴了吗？


否定句:
The paint wasn't caked on the wall. = 油漆没有粘在墙上。
The mixture wasn't caked, it was smooth. = 混合物没有结块，很顺滑。
