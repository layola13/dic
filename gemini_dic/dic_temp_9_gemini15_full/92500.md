hypothesis:/haɪˈpɒθɪsɪs/ /haɪˈpɑːθəsɪs/| n. |假设，假说|A hypothesis is a proposed explanation for a phenomenon. It's a testable statement that can be supported or refuted through observation and experimentation.  A good hypothesis is specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART).|The scientist formulated a hypothesis about the cause of the disease.|theory, supposition, conjecture|conclusion, fact|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hypothesis suggests a correlation between sleep and memory. = 这个假设表明睡眠和记忆之间存在关联。
His hypothesis explains the observed phenomena. = 他的假设解释了观察到的现象。


一般过去时:
The researchers tested their hypothesis in a controlled experiment. = 研究人员在一个受控实验中测试了他们的假设。
Einstein's hypothesis revolutionized our understanding of the universe. = 爱因斯坦的假设彻底改变了我们对宇宙的理解。


现在完成时:
Scientists have formulated several hypotheses to explain this anomaly. = 科学家们已经提出了几种假设来解释这种异常现象。
They have not yet tested the hypothesis adequately. = 他们尚未充分检验这个假设。


过去完成时:
Before conducting the experiment, they had carefully formulated their hypothesis. = 在进行实验之前，他们已经仔细地制定了他们的假设。
The data had already refuted his initial hypothesis. = 数据已经证伪了他的最初假设。


将来时:
We will test the hypothesis using a new set of data. = 我们将使用一组新的数据来检验这个假设。
The results will either support or refute the hypothesis. = 结果要么支持要么证伪这个假设。


虚拟语气:
If the hypothesis were true, we would observe different results. = 如果这个假设是正确的，我们将观察到不同的结果。
I wish the hypothesis were simpler to understand. = 我希望这个假设更容易理解。


被动语态:
The hypothesis was rigorously tested and proven correct. = 这个假设经过严格的检验并被证明是正确的。
The hypothesis is being investigated by several research teams. = 这个假设正在由几个研究小组进行调查。


疑问句:
What is the underlying hypothesis of your research? = 你研究的根本假设是什么？
What hypothesis are you trying to prove? = 你试图证明什么假设？


否定句:
The data does not support the hypothesis. = 数据不支持这个假设。
Their hypothesis was not proven. = 他们的假设没有被证明。


PHRASE->
formulate a hypothesis = 制定一个假设
test a hypothesis = 检验一个假设
support a hypothesis = 支持一个假设
reject a hypothesis = 拒绝一个假设
null hypothesis = 零假设
