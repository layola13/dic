shuddering:/ˈʃʌdə(r)ɪŋ/| v. adj. n.|颤抖，哆嗦；战栗|The act of shaking or trembling, often due to cold, fear, or revulsion.  It can also describe something that causes such a reaction. |例句：He shuddered at the thought of the upcoming surgery.  他一想到即将到来的手术就浑身颤抖。

The wind caused the old house to shudder. 风使得老房子颤抖。


近义词：trembling, shivering, quivering, shaking|反义词：calmness, stillness


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shudders at the sight of blood. 他看到血就发抖。
The old house shudders in the wind. 老房子在风中颤抖。


一般过去时:
She shuddered when she heard the scream. 她听到尖叫声时浑身颤抖。
The ground shuddered violently during the earthquake. 地震时地面剧烈震动。


现在完成时:
I have shuddered at the thought of it ever since. 从那以后，我一想到它就害怕。
The building has shuddered several times this week due to construction.  由于施工，这栋楼本周已经颤抖了几次。


过去完成时:
He had shuddered at the sight of the accident before he even knew what had happened.  在他甚至不知道发生了什么事之前，他就对事故的景象感到害怕。
The car had shuddered violently before finally coming to a stop. 车子剧烈震动后才最终停了下来。


将来时:
She will shudder when she sees the results. 她看到结果时会颤抖。
The bridge will shudder under the weight of the heavy truck. 桥梁将在重型卡车的重量下颤抖。


虚拟语气:
If I saw a snake, I would shudder. 如果我看到蛇，我会颤抖。
I wish I hadn't shuddered so much during the presentation. 我希望我在演示期间不要颤抖得那么厉害。


被动语态:
The building was shuddered by the explosion. 建筑物被爆炸震动了。 (This usage is less common)
The audience was not shuddered by his story. 观众并没有被他讲的故事所吓到。（被动语态，更侧重观众的感受）


疑问句:
Did you shudder when you saw the movie? 你看电影时害怕了吗？
Does the thought of spiders make you shudder?  想到蜘蛛会让你害怕吗？


否定句:
He didn't shudder at the news. 他听到这个消息并没有颤抖。
The building didn't shudder during the storm. 建筑物在暴风雨中没有震动。


PHRASE->

a shudder ran down her spine  她脊背上感到一阵寒意
with a shudder 她颤抖着
a shuddering breath  颤抖的呼吸
shuddering fear  战栗的恐惧
