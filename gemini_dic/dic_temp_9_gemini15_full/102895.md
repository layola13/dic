jaw-dropping: /ˈdʒɔːˌdrɒpɪŋ/ | adj. |令人震惊的；令人瞠目结舌的 | Extremely surprising or impressive; astonishing.  |例句：The magician's performance was jaw-dropping. 魔术师的表演令人震惊。

例句：The view from the mountaintop was absolutely jaw-dropping. 从山顶看到的景色绝对令人叹为观止。


近义词：astonishing, amazing, breathtaking, stunning, spectacular, impressive

反义词：unimpressive, underwhelming, boring, dull
