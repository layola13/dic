knolls:/noʊlz/| n. |小丘；土堆 | A knoll is a small, rounded hill or mound.  Knolls are typically smaller and less steep than hills or mountains. They are often found in gently rolling landscapes. |例句：The sheep grazed peacefully on the grassy knoll.  羊群平静地吃着草地上的小丘上的草。

The children played hide-and-seek amongst the gentle knolls. 孩子们在平缓的小丘之间玩捉迷藏。|近义词：hill, mound, hummock |反义词：valley, depression
