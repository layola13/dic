nonvariants: /ˈnɒnˈveəriənts/ | n. |不变体，不变量| A nonvariant is something that does not change or vary; it remains constant or uniform.  It's often used in scientific or technical contexts to describe things that stay the same under different conditions. | Example:  The researchers found several nonvariants in the DNA sequence across all samples. 研究人员在所有样本的DNA序列中发现了几种不变体。|近义词：constants, invariants|反义词：variants, variables|名词
