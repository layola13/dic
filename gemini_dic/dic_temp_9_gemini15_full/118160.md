menswear: /ˈmɛnzˌwɛr/ | n. | 男装 | Clothing designed for men.  Menswear encompasses a wide range of garments, from suits and shirts to casual wear like jeans and t-shirts.  It often reflects current fashion trends and can vary significantly in style and price. | Example:  He bought a new menswear outfit for the wedding. 他为婚礼买了一套新男装。
