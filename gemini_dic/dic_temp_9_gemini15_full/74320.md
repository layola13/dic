fraenum:/ˈfreɪnəm/| n.|系带；小带；膜|A fold of mucous membrane that connects two parts of the body.  It is commonly found in the mouth (frenulum of the tongue) and the prepuce (frenulum of the prepuce).|例句：The dentist checked the child's lingual fraenum.  牙医检查了孩子的舌系带。

例句：He had a circumcision to release the frenulum of his prepuce. 他做了包皮环切术以释放包皮系带。

近义词：ligament, membrane|反义词：无|词性：名词
