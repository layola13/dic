dimmish:/ˈdɪmɪʃ/| v.|使变暗淡；使变小；使减弱 |to make or become less bright, intense, or important |The lights dimmed, the music swelled. 灯光变暗，音乐声增强了。 The sun's rays diminished as the clouds rolled in.  随着乌云滚滚而来，阳光减弱了。| lessen, reduce, weaken |brighten, intensify, enhance |动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sun diminishes in the evening. = 太阳在傍晚变暗。
He diminishes his opponent's arguments. = 他削弱了对手的论点。


一般过去时:
The sound diminished as we walked away. = 当我们走开时，声音变小了。
Her influence diminished after the scandal. = 丑闻之后，她的影响力减弱了。


现在完成时:
The pain has diminished since I took the medicine. = 自从我吃了药后，疼痛已经减轻了。
The number of participants has diminished over time. = 参与者的数量随着时间的推移而减少了。


过去完成时:
The light had diminished before the storm arrived. = 在暴风雨来临之前，光线已经变暗了。
His confidence had diminished after several failures. = 连续几次失败后，他的信心已经减弱了。


将来时:
The effect of the medicine will diminish gradually. = 药物的效果会逐渐减弱。
The power of the king will diminish as his health deteriorates. = 国王的权力随着他的健康恶化而减弱。


虚拟语气:
If the sun diminished, the earth would become cold. = 如果太阳变暗，地球就会变冷。
If the noise didn't diminish, I would have to leave. = 如果噪音不减弱，我将不得不离开。


被动语态:
The problem was diminished by the new policy. = 新政策减轻了这个问题。
His reputation was diminished by the false accusations. = 虚假的指控损害了他的名誉。


疑问句:
Did the light diminish over time? = 光线随着时间的推移是否减弱了？
Will the effect of the drug diminish quickly? = 药物的效果会很快减弱吗？


否定句:
The problem did not diminish despite our efforts. = 尽管我们努力了，问题并没有减少。
The intensity of the light did not diminish throughout the night. = 整个晚上，光的强度都没有减弱。


PHRASE->

diminish in size = 尺寸减小
diminish in importance = 重要性降低
diminish in value = 价值下降
diminish to nothing = 减少到零
diminish the impact = 减轻影响
