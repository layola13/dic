deformer:/dɪˈfɔːrmər/ | n. |变形器，畸变器 | A device or process that alters the shape or form of something. The software's deformer tools allowed for precise manipulation of the 3D model. 该软件的变形工具允许对3D模型进行精确的操纵。 |近义词：modifier, distorter, warper |反义词：restorer, straightener
