词源:
The word "extinguisher" is derived from the verb "extinguish," which comes from the Old French word *estinguir*, meaning "to put out (a fire)."  The suffix "-er" is an agent suffix indicating a person or thing that performs the action.  Therefore, an extinguisher is a thing that extinguishes.
