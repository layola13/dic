resentive: /rɪˈzɛntɪv/ | adj. | 怨恨的；不满的 | Feeling or showing resentment; indignant.  |例句：He was resentful of his brother's success. 他对兄弟的成功感到不满。

例句：Her resentful glare showed her displeasure. 她怨恨的目光显示了她的不满。


近义词：bitter, indignant, angry, offended, aggrieved | 反义词：grateful, appreciative, pleased, content


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is resentful of their unfair treatment. 他对他们不公平的待遇感到愤恨。
She is always resentful towards authority figures. 她总是对权威人物怀有怨恨。


一般过去时:
He was resentful about the way he was dismissed. 他对他被解雇的方式感到愤恨。
She was resentful that she hadn't been given the promotion. 她很生气，因为她没有得到晋升。


现在完成时:
They have been resentful of the company's decision for months.  几个月来，他们一直对公司的决定不满。
I have been resentful of his constant criticism. 我一直对他不断的批评感到不满。


过去完成时:
He had been resentful of his father's favoritism for years. 多年来，他对父亲的偏袒一直耿耿于怀。
She had been resentful ever since she was overlooked for the project. 从她被忽视了那个项目以来，她就一直怀恨在心。


将来时:
He will be resentful if he doesn't get the job. 如果他得不到这份工作，他会感到不满。
She will likely be resentful if her suggestions are ignored. 如果她的建议被忽视，她可能会感到不满。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't be so resentful. 如果我是你，我不会这么怨恨。
I wish I weren't so resentful of his success. 我真希望我不那么嫉妒他的成功。


被动语态:
His behavior was seen as resentful by many. 许多人认为他的行为是怨恨的。
Her actions were interpreted as resentful and spiteful. 她的行为被解读为怨恨和恶意的。


疑问句:
Is he resentful about the changes? 他对这些改变不满吗？
Are you resentful of your colleague's promotion? 你对同事的晋升不满吗？


否定句:
He wasn't resentful at all; he was simply disappointed. 他一点也不怨恨；他只是失望。
She wasn't resentful; she just needed some time to process the situation. 她并没有怨恨；她只是需要一些时间来处理这种情况。
