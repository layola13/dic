procerite:/ˈproʊsərˌaɪt/| n. |长石；长石矿 | A procerite is a rare earth mineral, a variety of orthoclase feldspar.  It's characterized by its unique chemical composition containing significant amounts of rare earth elements. |Example: The geologist identified the sample as procerite due to its distinctive crystal structure and chemical analysis.  地质学家根据其独特的晶体结构和化学分析，将样品鉴定为长石。|近义词：feldspar (更广泛的术语)|反义词：无
