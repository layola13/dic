dazedly:/ˈdeɪzɪdli/| adv.|茫然地，恍惚地，迷糊地 |In a dazed or confused manner; as if one is dazed.|例句：He walked dazedly away from the scene of the accident. 他茫然地离开了事故现场。
He stared dazedly at the wreckage. 他茫然地盯着残骸。|近义词：stunnedly, blankly, confusedly|反义词：alertly, keenly, attentively
