reply:/rɪˈplaɪ/| v. n. |回复；回答；回应|To give a spoken or written answer to someone.  |例句：He replied to my email promptly. 他迅速回复了我的邮件。|例句：Her reply was brief but to the point. 她的回复简短但切中要害。|近义词：answer, response, retort|反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I reply to emails every morning. = 我每天早上回复邮件。
He replies to his friends' messages quickly. = 他很快回复朋友的信息。

一般过去时:
She replied to my question with a smile. = 她笑着回答了我的问题。
They replied that they would be there soon. = 他们回复说他们很快就会到。

现在完成时:
I have replied to all the emails I received today. = 我已经回复了今天收到的所有邮件。
She has replied to several letters this week. = 她本周已经回复了几封信。

过去完成时:
He had replied to the letter before he left for the trip. = 他在去旅行之前已经回复了那封信。
They had replied to my inquiry before I called again. = 在我再次打电话之前，他们已经回复了我的询问。

将来时:
I will reply to your email as soon as possible. = 我会尽快回复你的邮件。
She will reply to the customer's complaint tomorrow. = 她明天将回复客户的投诉。

虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have replied sooner. = 如果我知道的话，我会早点回复。
I wish I had replied to her message earlier. = 我真希望我早点回复她的信息。

被动语态:
My email was replied to quickly. = 我的邮件很快得到了回复。
The question was replied to in detail. = 这个问题得到了详细的解答。

疑问句:
Did you reply to his message? = 你回复他的信息了吗？
Will she reply to my email today? = 她今天会回复我的邮件吗？

否定句:
I didn't reply to his email. = 我没有回复他的邮件。
He hasn't replied to my letter yet. = 他还没回复我的信。



PHRASE->
reply to = 回复
reply in kind = 以牙还牙
quick reply = 快速回复
make a reply = 作出回应
in reply to = 作为对…的回应
