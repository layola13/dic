lernean:/ˈlɜːrniən/| adj.|勒耳涅亚的；(指)勒耳涅亚水怪的|  relating to Lerna, a swamp near Argos in ancient Greece, famous as the home of the Lernaean Hydra.  The adjective is primarily used in reference to the Hydra, a multi-headed serpent slain by Heracles (Hercules) in Greek mythology. | The Lernaean Hydra was a fearsome beast.  = 勒耳涅亚水怪是一种可怕的野兽。|  multi-headed, hydra-like, monstrous |  (无明确反义词，可根据语境使用"one-headed," "ordinary," 等)| adj.
