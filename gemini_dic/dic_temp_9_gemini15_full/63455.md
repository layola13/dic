entrepreneur:/ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːr/ | n. | 企业家，创业者 | A person who organizes and operates a business or businesses, taking on greater than normal financial risks in order to do so.  They are often innovative and willing to take risks to pursue opportunities. |  Elon Musk is a successful entrepreneur known for his ventures in space exploration and electric vehicles.  埃隆·马斯克是一位成功的企业家，以其在太空探索和电动汽车领域的冒险而闻名。 | businessman, businessperson, innovator | employee, worker | 名词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He runs a successful tech startup. 他经营着一间成功的科技初创公司。
She manages a team of entrepreneurs. 她管理着一个企业家团队。


一般过去时:
He founded a company last year. 他去年创立了一家公司。
She successfully launched her business. 她成功地推出了她的企业。


现在完成时:
She has built a multi-million dollar empire. 她已经建立了一个价值数百万美元的商业帝国。
He has overcome many challenges in his entrepreneurial journey.  他在创业过程中克服了许多挑战。


过去完成时:
Before starting her business, she had saved a significant amount of money. 在创业之前，她已经存了一大笔钱。
They had already developed a strong business plan before seeking investment. 在寻求投资之前，他们已经制定了一个强有力的商业计划。


将来时:
He will expand his business into new markets. 他将把他的业务扩展到新的市场。
She will launch a new product line next quarter. 她将在下个季度推出一个新的产品线。


虚拟语气:
If I were an entrepreneur, I would take more risks. 如果我是个企业家，我会承担更多风险。
I wish I had the resources to start my own business. 我希望我有资源来创办自己的企业。


被动语态:
The business was founded in 2010. 这家公司成立于2010年。
New opportunities are constantly being created by entrepreneurs.  企业家们不断创造新的机遇。


疑问句:
