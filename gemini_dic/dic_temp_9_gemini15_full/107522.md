landers:/ˈlændərz/| n.|登陆者；着陆器；移民 |People or things that land, especially spacecraft or aircraft that have landed.  It can also refer to people who settle in a new place.|例句：The Mars landers sent back incredible images.  火星登陆器发回了令人难以置信的图像。|例句：The early settlers were brave landers who faced many hardships. 早期定居者是勇敢的登陆者，他们面临着许多困难。|近义词：settlers, colonists, immigrants |反义词：None specifically.  The antonym depends on the context (e.g., if referring to spacecraft, "orbiters" might be considered a contrast).|名词
