invades:/ɪnˈveɪdz/| v.|入侵，侵略，侵犯，涌入 |To enter a place by force with an army; to affect something negatively and undesirably.|例句：The army invaded the country.  军队入侵了该国。
The weeds invaded the garden. 杂草蔓延到花园里。|近义词：attack, conquer, overrun, encroach|反义词：withdraw, retreat, leave


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army invades the country every year. 军队每年都入侵该国。
Noise invades my peaceful home. 噪音扰乱了我平静的家。


一般过去时:
The barbarians invaded Rome. 野蛮人入侵了罗马。
The virus invaded his body. 病毒侵入了他的身体。


现在完成时:
The insects have invaded the house.  昆虫已经入侵了这房子。
Doubt has invaded her mind. 怀疑充满了她的脑海。


过去完成时:
The enemy had invaded the city before the reinforcements arrived. 增援部队到达之前，敌人已经入侵了城市。
Fear had invaded her heart before she entered the dark room.  在她进入黑暗的房间之前，恐惧已经占据了她的内心。


将来时:
The enemy will invade our country next year. 敌人明年将入侵我们的国家。
The cold weather will invade the region soon.  寒冷的天气很快就会侵袭这个地区。


虚拟语气:
If the enemy invaded, we would fight back. 如果敌人入侵，我们将反击。
If only my thoughts hadn't invaded my mind during the exam.  但愿考试时我的思绪没有扰乱我。


被动语态:
The country was invaded by the enemy. 该国被敌人入侵了。
His privacy was invaded by the paparazzi. 他的隐私被狗仔队侵犯了。


疑问句:
Did the enemy invade the city? 敌人入侵了城市吗？
Has the disease invaded the community?  这种疾病已经蔓延到社区了吗？


否定句:
The army did not invade the country. 军队没有入侵该国。
The cold weather didn't invade the region this year.  今年寒冷的天气没有侵袭这个地区。
