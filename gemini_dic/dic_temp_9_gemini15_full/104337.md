juvenile diabetes:/ˈdʒuːvənʌɪl daɪəˈbiːtiːz/| n. |青少年糖尿病|Juvenile diabetes is a type of diabetes that usually begins in childhood or adolescence.  It is characterized by the body's inability to produce insulin, a hormone needed to convert sugar into energy.|My cousin was diagnosed with juvenile diabetes at age 10. = 我的表弟10岁时被诊断出患有青少年糖尿病。|Type 1 diabetes, childhood diabetes|adult-onset diabetes|名词
