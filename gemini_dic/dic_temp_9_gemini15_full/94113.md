impeccable:/ɪmˈpekəbəl/| adj. |无可挑剔的，完美的 |free from fault or flaw; perfect.  |例句：Her manners were impeccable. 她的举止无可挑剔。

例句：His performance was impeccable. 他的表演无可挑剔。


近义词：flawless, perfect, faultless, spotless, unblemished

反义词：faulty, flawed, imperfect, blemished


词源：

From Latin *impeccabilis, from in- "not" + peccare "to sin, offend".  The word entered English in the 17th century.
