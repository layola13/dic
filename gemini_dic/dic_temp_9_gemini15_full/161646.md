poundage:/ˈpaʊndɪdʒ/| n. |磅重；重量；费用；酬金|The weight or amount of something, often measured in pounds; a fee or charge, especially one based on weight.|例句：The poundage of the shipment was excessive. 这批货物的重量过大。

例句：They charged a high poundage for the delivery. 他们收取了高额的送货费。


近义词：weight, fee, charge, duty|反义词：lightness (in the sense of weight), exemption (in the sense of fee)|词性:名词


词源:源自古法语 *pondage，最终源自拉丁语pondus，意为“重量”。
