desertification:/ˌdɛzərtifiˈkeɪʃən/| n. |荒漠化；沙漠化 |The process by which fertile land becomes desert, typically as a result of drought, deforestation, or inappropriate agriculture.  This involves the loss of topsoil, vegetation, and biodiversity, leading to a decline in land productivity and ultimately rendering it unsuitable for agriculture or habitation. |例句：Desertification is a major environmental problem in many parts of the world.  荒漠化是世界许多地区的主要环境问题。

例句：The prolonged drought contributed significantly to the desertification of the region.  长时间的干旱严重促成了该地区的沙漠化。


近义词：desertization, aridity, land degradation

反义词：reforestation, afforestation, land reclamation

词性:名词
