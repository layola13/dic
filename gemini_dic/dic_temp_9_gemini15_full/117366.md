mediately:/ɪˈmiːdiətli/ | adv. | 立即，马上 | immediately means without delay; instantly.  It describes something happening or being done at once. |例句：I need to leave immediately. 我需要立刻离开。

例句：The police responded immediately to the call. 警察立即回应了电话。


近义词：instantly, directly, forthwith, at once, right away | 反义词：later, eventually, afterward
