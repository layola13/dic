discontinuing:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuɪŋ/| v. |停止；中断；废止 |The act of stopping something, such as a product, service, or activity, permanently or temporarily.  |The company is discontinuing its line of outdated products. 公司正在停产其过时的产品线。 |近义词：ceasing, stopping, ending, terminating |反义词：continuing, starting, commencing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are discontinuing the bus route next month. = 他们下个月将停运这条公交线路。
The factory is discontinuing production of that model. = 工厂正在停止生产那个型号的产品。


一般过去时:
The school discontinued the art program last year. = 学校去年取消了美术课程。
They discontinued the service after many complaints. = 他们在收到很多投诉后停止了这项服务。


现在完成时:
They have discontinued the product due to low sales. = 由于销量低，他们已经停产了该产品。
The government has discontinued funding for the project. = 政府已经停止了对该项目的资助。


过去完成时:
They had discontinued the service before I even knew about it. = 我甚至都不知道之前他们就已经停止了这项服务。
The company had discontinued the product line before the market downturn. = 在市场低迷之前，该公司就已经停产了该产品线。


将来时:
The airline will be discontinuing flights to that city. = 航空公司将停止飞往那个城市的航班。
They will discontinue the service next year. = 他们明年将停止这项服务。


虚拟语气:
If they discontinued the project, it would save money. = 如果他们停止这个项目，就能省钱。
I wish they had discontinued the noisy construction earlier. = 我真希望他们早点停止吵闹的施工。


被动语态:
The product line was discontinued due to lack of demand. = 该产品线由于缺乏需求而被停产。
The service is being discontinued next week. = 该服务将于下周停止。


疑问句:
Is the company discontinuing any products this year? = 公司今年有停产任何产品吗？
Are they discontinuing the train service? = 他们要停运火车服务吗？


否定句:
The company is not discontinuing its flagship product. = 公司没有停产其旗舰产品。
They are not discontinuing the program yet. = 他们还没有停止该项目。
