misprise:/mɪsˈpraɪz/ | vt. |误解，轻视，看错|To misunderstand; to undervalue or scorn.  The word implies a failure to understand something correctly, often leading to a negative judgment or action.|例句：He misprised her intentions, thinking she was trying to deceive him. 他误解了她的意图，以为她试图欺骗他。|近义词：misunderstand, misinterpret, underestimate, scorn, disdain |反义词：understand, appreciate, value, respect |动词
