calendars:/ˈkælɪndərz/| n. |日历，历法 |A calendar is a system of organizing days for social, religious, commercial, or administrative purposes.  It is also a physical record of such a system.  Calendars typically display the days, weeks, and months of a year, often including additional information such as holidays, moon phases, or astrological signs.|Example:  I need a new calendar to keep track of my appointments. 我需要一个新的日历来记录我的约会。|Example: The Gregorian calendar is the most widely used calendar system in the world. 格里高利历是世界上使用最广泛的历法。|近义词：almanac, schedule, diary |反义词：无
