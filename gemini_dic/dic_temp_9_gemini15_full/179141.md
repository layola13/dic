redialling:/ˌriːˈdaɪəlɪŋ/| v. |重新拨号|The act of dialing a telephone number again, usually after a failed attempt.  This often refers to using a feature on a phone that automatically re-dials the last number called.|例句：After the line went dead, I tried redialling but still couldn't get through.  线路中断后，我尝试重新拨号，但仍然无法接通。

例句：The phone automatically redials when the call is dropped. 电话在掉线时会自动重新拨号。|近义词：recalling, calling back |反义词：disconnecting, hanging up
