deigning:/deɪnɪŋ/| v. |屈尊；降贵纡尊；赏赐 |To condescend; to think oneself worthy to do something.  It implies a sense of superiority and reluctant agreement to do something beneath one's perceived status. |例句：She deigned to answer my question. 她屈尊回答了我的问题。

例句：The queen deigned to attend the peasant's wedding. 女王屈尊参加了农民的婚礼。


近义词：condescend, stoop, vouchsafe | 反义词：refuse, reject, scorn


词性:动词(现在分词)

词源:
