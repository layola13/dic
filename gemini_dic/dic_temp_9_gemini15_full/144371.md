outstandings:/ˌaʊtˈstændɪŋz/| n. |未偿债务，未付金额 | Outstanding amounts are debts or payments that are still due.  These can include invoices, loans, or other financial obligations.  |The company's outstanding balance was quite high.  这家公司的未付余额相当高。|  The outstanding invoices need to be paid immediately.  未付发票需要立即支付。| debts, arrears, dues | payments, settled accounts | n.
