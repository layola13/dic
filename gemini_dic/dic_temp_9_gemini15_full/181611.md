relativistic:/ˌrɛləˈtɪvɪstɪk/| adj.|相对论的；相对性的|Relativistic refers to something related to or based on the principles of Einstein's theory of relativity, which describes the relationship between space, time, and gravity.  It emphasizes that measurements of physical quantities, such as length and time, are relative to the observer's frame of reference and are not absolute. |例句：Einstein's theory of relativity provides a relativistic understanding of gravity. 爱因斯坦的相对论提供了对引力的相对论理解。

例句：Relativistic effects become significant at speeds approaching the speed of light.  接近光速时，相对论效应变得显著。


近义词：relative, comparative


反义词：absolute, invariant


词性：形容词
