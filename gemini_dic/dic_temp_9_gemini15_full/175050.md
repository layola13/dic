radiolaria:/ˌreɪdiəˈlɛəriə/| n. |放射虫；放射虫类|  A single-celled, usually microscopic, marine protist with a siliceous skeleton.  Radiolaria are zooplankton, meaning they drift in the ocean currents. Their skeletons, often intricate and beautiful, are important components of marine sediments. | Example:  Many species of radiolaria exhibit remarkable diversity in the structure of their siliceous skeletons.  许多种类的放射虫在其硅质骨骼结构上表现出显著的多样性。|近义词：protozoa (general term)|反义词：N/A
