knight:/naɪt/| n. |骑士，爵士|A knight is a person who in the Middle Ages was a mounted warrior serving a king or other superior.  In modern usage, it refers to a person who has been awarded a knighthood, a title of honor. |Example: King Arthur was a legendary knight.亚瑟王是一位传奇骑士。 He was knighted for his bravery. 他因勇敢而被封为爵士。|近义词：Sir, champion, warrior|反义词：peasant, commoner
