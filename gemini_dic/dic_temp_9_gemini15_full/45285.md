culture:/ˈkʌltʃər/| n.|文化|The customs, arts, social institutions, and achievements of a particular nation, people, or other social group.|例句：The museum showcases the rich culture of ancient Egypt.  这个博物馆展示了古埃及丰富的文化。|近义词：civilization, heritage, tradition|反义词：barbarity, savagery


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Their culture emphasizes community over individualism. = 他们的文化强调集体主义而非个人主义。
We study different cultures in this class. = 我们在这门课上学习不同的文化。

一般过去时:
The ancient Romans had a complex culture. = 古罗马人拥有复杂的文化。
Her family's culture influenced her career choices. = 她家族的文化影响了她的职业选择。

现在完成时:
Our society has absorbed many aspects of other cultures. = 我们的社会已经吸收了其他文化的许多方面。
They have studied the culture of several Asian countries. = 他们已经研究了几个亚洲国家的文化。

过去完成时:
Before the war, the city had a vibrant cultural scene. = 战争之前，这座城市拥有充满活力的文化景象。
By the time I arrived, they had already embraced the local culture. = 当我到达时，他们已经接受了当地文化。

将来时:
We will learn more about Japanese culture next semester. = 下学期我们将学习更多关于日本文化的知识。
The exhibition will explore the culture of the Amazon rainforest. = 这个展览将探索亚马逊雨林的文化。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would study different cultures. = 如果我有更多时间，我会学习不同的文化。
I wish I understood their culture better. = 我希望我能更好地理解他们的文化。

被动语态:
Their culture is influenced by many different traditions. = 他们的文化受到许多不同传统的的影响。
The exhibition was organized to promote cultural understanding. = 这个展览是为了促进文化理解而举办的。

疑问句:
What is the dominant culture in this region? = 这个地区的主要文化是什么？
What aspects of their culture are unique? = 他们文化的哪些方面是独特的？

否定句:
He didn't understand the nuances of their culture. = 他不理解他们文化的细微之处。
Their culture doesn't value competition as much as ours does. = 他们的文化不像我们那样重视竞争。
