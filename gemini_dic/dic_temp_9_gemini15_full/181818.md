relish:/ˈrɛlɪʃ/| vt. n.|喜欢，享受；佐料|to enjoy something very much; a condiment or relish |例句：I relished the challenge. 我喜欢这个挑战。She relished the spicy food. 她喜欢辛辣的食物。|近义词：enjoy, savor, appreciate, delight in|反义词：dislike, abhor, detest|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I relish the opportunity to travel. = 我喜欢旅行的机会。
They relish spicy food. = 他们喜欢辛辣的食物。

一般过去时:
She relished every moment of her vacation. = 她享受假期的每一刻。
He relished the taste of the wine. = 他回味着葡萄酒的味道。

现在完成时:
I have relished many delicious meals in my life. = 我一生中享受过许多美味佳肴。
We have relished the quiet evenings at home. = 我们享受了在家安静的夜晚。

过去完成时:
She had relished the idea of a quiet evening at home. = 她曾享受过在家安静度过夜晚的想法。
They had relished the opportunity to work together. = 他们曾享受过一起工作的机会。

将来时:
I will relish the challenge of this new job. = 我将享受这份新工作的挑战。
We will relish the opportunity to meet new people. = 我们将享受结识新朋友的机会。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would relish the opportunity to read more. = 如果我有更多时间，我会享受阅读的机会。
I wish I could relish the simple pleasures of life more often. = 我希望我能更频繁地享受生活中简单的快乐。

被动语态:
The opportunity to learn was relished by the students. = 学生们享受了学习的机会。
The delicious meal was relished by everyone. = 每个人都喜欢这顿美味的饭菜。

疑问句:
Do you relish the prospect of a new job? = 你喜欢新工作的预期吗？
Does she relish the challenge? = 她喜欢这个挑战吗？

否定句:
He didn't relish the idea of working late. = 他不喜欢加班的想法。
I don't relish the thought of public speaking. = 我不喜欢公开演讲的想法。
