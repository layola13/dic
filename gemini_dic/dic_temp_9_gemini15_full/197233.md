shareholder:/ˈʃɛərˌhoʊldər/ | n. | 股东 | An individual or institution that legally owns one or more shares of stock in a public or private corporation.  Shareholders are the owners of the company, and their ownership is proportional to the number of shares they hold. They have certain rights, including the right to vote on major corporate decisions and to receive dividends. | Example:  The shareholders voted to approve the merger.  股东们投票批准了合并。


The company's shareholders are expecting a healthy dividend this year.  这家公司的股东们期望今年获得丰厚的股息。


近义词：stockholder, equity holder


反义词：N/A (no direct antonym, but concepts like "debt holder" or "creditor" represent the opposite financial relationship)


词性：名词
