rollway:/ˈroʊlˌweɪ/ | n. |滚道；滚珠轴承；滚柱轴承 | A rollway is a grooved or channeled path or surface along which something rolls, typically a bearing.  It's commonly used in the context of mechanical engineering to describe the raceway of a bearing. | Example: The smooth rollway ensured the efficient operation of the bearing.  滚道确保了轴承的有效运行。The damaged rollway caused the wheel to wobble. 损坏的滚道导致车轮晃动。 | 近义词：raceway, track, path | 反义词：N/A
