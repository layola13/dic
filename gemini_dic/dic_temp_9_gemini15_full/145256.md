overboard:/ˈoʊvərˌbɔːrd/| adv.|从船上掉入水中；过分地；过度地|Beyond the side of a ship or boat; excessively; to an excessive degree.|例句：The sailor fell overboard during the storm.  暴风雨中水手落水了。
例句：He went overboard with the decorations. 他在装饰方面太过火了。|近义词：over the side, excessively, excessively, immoderately|反义词：onshore, moderately, insufficiently
