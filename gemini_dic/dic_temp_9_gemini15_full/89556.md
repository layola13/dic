honoring:/ˈɑːnərɪŋ/ | v. | 尊敬；纪念；表彰 | To show great respect for someone or something.  |例句：We are honoring our veterans today. 我们今天正在纪念我们的退伍军人。|近义词：respecting, celebrating, commemorating |反义词：disrespecting, dishonoring


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We are honoring our teachers this year. = 今年我们正在尊敬我们的老师。
They honor their ancestors with annual ceremonies. = 他们每年通过仪式来纪念他们的祖先。


一般过去时:
The company honored its employees with a bonus. = 公司给员工发了奖金以示表彰。
She honored her promise to visit her grandmother. = 她信守了去看望祖母的承诺。


现在完成时:
We have honored our commitment to the project. = 我们已经履行了对项目的承诺。
They have honored their parents' wishes. = 他们已经尊重了父母的意愿。


过去完成时:
They had honored the agreement before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经履行了协议。
She had honored her friend's request for help. = 她已经满足了朋友的求助请求。


将来时:
We will honor the agreement we signed. = 我们将遵守我们签署的协议。
They will honor the fallen soldiers with a memorial. = 他们将用纪念碑来纪念阵亡士兵。


虚拟语气:
If they honored the contract, we would be satisfied. = 如果他们遵守合同，我们会很满意。
I wish they had honored their word. = 我希望他们信守诺言。


被动语态:
The winners will be honored at the ceremony. = 获奖者将在仪式上受到表彰。
The agreement was honored by both parties. = 双方都遵守了协议。


疑问句:
Will the company honor its warranty? = 公司会履行保修责任吗？
How will we honor the victims of the tragedy? = 我们将如何纪念这场悲剧的受害者？


否定句:
He did not honor his debt. = 他没有偿还债务。
She failed to honor her commitment. = 她没有履行承诺。



PHRASE->
in honor of = 为了纪念…
honor roll = 光荣榜
honor system = 荣誉制度
court of honor = 荣誉法庭
word of honor = 诺言
