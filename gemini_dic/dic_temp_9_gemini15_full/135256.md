nonparallelism:/ˌnɒnˈpærəˌlɛlɪzəm/ | n. |非平行结构，不相等|The lack of parallelism in sentence structure;  a failure to maintain consistency in grammatical structure, especially in lists or comparisons.  It often results in awkward or unclear writing. | Example:  The teacher corrected the student's essay, pointing out the nonparallelism in the concluding paragraph.  老师修改了学生的文章，指出了结尾段落中不平行的结构。|近义词:inconsistency, lack of balance, disparity|反义词:parallelism
