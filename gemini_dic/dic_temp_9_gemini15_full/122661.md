module:/ˈmɒdʒuːl/ /ˈmɑːdʒuːl/| n. |模块；组件；单元；课程单元 |A module is a self-contained unit of a larger system or course.  It can be a separate piece of equipment or a section of a program. |例句：This computer program is made up of several modules. 这个电脑程序由几个模块组成。

例句：The course is divided into several modules, each focusing on a different aspect of the subject. 这门课程分为几个单元，每个单元都侧重于主题的不同方面。


近义词：unit, component, section, element

反义词：whole, entirety
