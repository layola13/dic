copyable:/ˈkɒpiəbl/ /ˈkɑːpiəbl/ | adj. | 可复制的，可拷贝的 | Capable of being copied or reproduced.  Something that is copyable can be easily duplicated or replicated.  | Example: This software is copyable, but its redistribution is prohibited.  这个软件可以复制，但是禁止重新分发。  |近义词：reproducible, duplicable | 反义词：uncopyable, unique
