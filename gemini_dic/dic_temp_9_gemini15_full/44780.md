crunch:/krʌntʃ/| n. v.|嘎吱声；紧要关头；费力咀嚼；处理；压碎|The sound of something being crushed or chewed noisily; a difficult or critical situation; to chew something noisily; to process or deal with something; to crush something.|例句：I heard a crunch of gravel under my feet. 我听到脚下有碎石的嘎吱声。

The company is facing a financial crunch. 这家公司正面临财务危机。


例句：He crunched the numbers to prepare his presentation. 他仔细计算数据来准备他的演示文稿。

The machine crunched the data quickly. 机器快速处理数据。


近义词：crush, grind, crunch, crisis, emergency|反义词：smooth sailing, ease
