glycyrrhiza:/ɡlɪˈsɪrɪzə/| n. |甘草 |Glycyrrhiza is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae, known for their licorice-flavored roots.  The genus includes several species, the most important of which is Glycyrrhiza glabra, the source of most commercially available licorice. |The root of glycyrrhiza is used in traditional medicine and as a flavoring agent. |近义词:licorice root|反义词: N/A
