ductible:/ˈdʌktɪbl/| adj.|可延展的，易延展的，可塑的|Easily drawn out or hammered thin; malleable.  Ductile materials can be deformed under tensile stress without fracturing. This property is important in many manufacturing processes, allowing metals to be shaped into wires or sheets.|例句：Gold is a highly ductile metal, which is why it can be easily shaped into jewelry.  金是一种非常延展的金属，这就是为什么它很容易被塑造成首饰。|例句：Copper is known for its high ductility and conductivity. 铜以其高延展性和导电性而闻名。|近义词：malleable, pliable, flexible|反义词：brittle, rigid
