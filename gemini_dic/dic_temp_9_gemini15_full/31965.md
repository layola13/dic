chemiotaxis:/ˌkiːmiˈoʊtæksɪs/ | n. |趋化性 |The movement of an organism or cell in response to a chemical stimulus.| 例句：Bacteria exhibit chemotaxis, moving towards nutrients and away from toxins.  细菌表现出趋化性，向营养物质移动，远离毒素。

The immune system uses chemotaxis to direct immune cells to sites of infection. 免疫系统利用趋化性将免疫细胞导向感染部位。


近义词：chemoattraction (for movement towards stimulus), chemorepulsion (for movement away from stimulus) | 反义词：random movement,  (lack of directed movement) | 词性: 名词
