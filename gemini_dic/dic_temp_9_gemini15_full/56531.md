dominance:/ˈdɒmɪnəns/ /ˈdɑːmɪnəns/ | n. |支配；统治；优势；主导地位 | The state or fact of being dominant; control or influence over others or a situation.  It can refer to a position of power, influence, or control; a superior position or status; or a noticeable or striking feature. |


例句：

His dominance in the sport is undeniable. = 他在该项运动中的统治地位是不可否认的。

The company's dominance of the market is threatened by new competitors. =  这家公司对市场的支配地位受到了新竞争对手的威胁。


近义词：supremacy, ascendancy, authority, control, mastery, predominance

反义词：subordination, subservience, inferiority
