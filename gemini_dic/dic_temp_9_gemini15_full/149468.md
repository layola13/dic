paddocking:/ˈpædəkɪŋ/| n.|围场饲养；（马匹的）围场训练|The act of keeping horses or other livestock in a paddock.  It often involves rotational grazing to manage pasture health and provide animals with fresh forage.  It can also refer to the training of horses within a paddock.|例句：The farmer rotates his cattle through different paddocks to prevent overgrazing.  农民轮流将他的牛群放到不同的围场里，以防止过度放牧。

例句：The trainer used paddocking to help the young horse develop its stamina and obedience. 训练员利用围场训练来帮助年轻的马匹发展耐力和服从性。|近义词：pasturing, grazing, field training|反义词：stabling (keeping in a stable)|名词
