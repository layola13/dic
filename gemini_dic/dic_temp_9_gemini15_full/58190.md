drowsinesses:/ˈdraʊzinɛsiːz/| n.|困倦状态(复数)|The plural form of drowsiness, referring to multiple instances or types of drowsiness.  It describes a state of feeling sleepy and lethargic.  The word is less commonly used than "drowsiness" itself.|例句：The effects of the medication included drowsinesses of varying intensity throughout the day.  (药物的影响包括全天不同程度的困倦状态。)


例句：His prolonged drowsinesses were a symptom of his underlying illness. (他长时间的困倦是潜在疾病的症状。)


近义词：sleepinesses, lethargies, somnolences|反义词：alertnesses, wakefulnesses|名词
