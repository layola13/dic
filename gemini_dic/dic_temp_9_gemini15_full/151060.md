paraphrastic: /ˌpærəˈfræstik/ | adj. |释义的，解释的，意译的 |  Relating to or involving paraphrase; expressing the meaning of something in different words.  It emphasizes rewording to convey the same meaning, often simplifying or clarifying complex ideas. |

例句：

The teacher provided a paraphrastic explanation of the complex scientific concept.  老师对复杂的科学概念作了释义性的解释。

His paraphrastic summary captured the essence of the long article.  他的意译总结抓住了长篇文章的要点。


近义词：explanatory, interpretative, rewording, summarizing

反义词：literal, verbatim


词性：形容词
