outduel: /ˌaʊtˈduːəl/ | vt. |胜过；技胜一筹| To surpass or defeat someone in a duel or competition, especially by superior skill or performance.  |例句：The seasoned swordsman outdueled his younger opponent with ease.  经验丰富的剑客轻松地击败了他的年轻对手。


The champion outdueled all challengers in the tournament.  冠军在锦标赛中击败了所有挑战者。|近义词：outshine, outmaneuver, surpass, defeat |反义词：be defeated by, lose to
