prestorages:/ˈpriːˌstɔːrɪdʒɪz/| n.|预存储；预先存储|The process of storing data or resources in advance of their actual need.  It's often used in computing and data management to improve performance or availability.  |例句：The system uses prestorages to speed up access to frequently used files. = 该系统使用预存储来加快对常用文件的访问速度。

例句：The company implemented prestorages of critical data to ensure business continuity during outages. = 公司实施了关键数据的预存储，以确保在停机期间业务连续性。


近义词：caching, buffering, pre-fetching |反义词：post-processing, on-demand processing|名词
