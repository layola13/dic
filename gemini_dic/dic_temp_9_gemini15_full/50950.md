designation: /ˌdeziɡˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 指定；名称；称号；职位；委任 | The act of designating or assigning something; a name, title, or position given to someone or something.  |例句：The designation of the new CEO was met with mixed reactions. 新任首席执行官的任命引起了褒贬不一的反应。

例句：His designation as team leader was well-deserved. 他被任命为团队领导是当之无愧的。


近义词：appointment, assignment, nomination, title, appellation | 反义词：removal, dismissal


词源:  From Old French *designaison, from désigner "to designate," from Latin designare "to mark out, point out," from de- "off, away" + signare "to mark."  The word's evolution reflects its core meaning of marking or setting something apart.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company designates a new marketing strategy every year. = 公司每年都会指定一个新的营销策略。
This document designates the responsibilities of each team member. = 此文件指定了每个团队成员的职责。


一般过去时:
The committee designated John as the project leader. = 委员会指定约翰为项目负责人。
They designated a specific area for the new building. = 他们为新建筑指定了一个具体的区域。


现在完成时:
The government has designated several areas as national parks. = 政府已将几个地区指定为国家公园。
She has been designated as the spokesperson for the organization. = 她已被指定为该组织的发言人。


过去完成时:
They had already designated the meeting location before the announcement. = 他们在宣布之前就已经指定了会议地点。
The board had designated him as the successor before his retirement. = 董事会在其退休前已指定他为继任者。


将来时:
The president will designate a new cabinet member next week. = 总统下周将任命一位新的内阁成员。
They will designate a specific budget for the project. = 他们将为该项目指定具体的预算。


虚拟语气:
If they designated a different location, we would have had better access. = 如果他们指定不同的地点，我们将有更好的通道。
I wish they had designated a clearer timeline for the project. = 我希望他们为项目指定一个更清晰的时间表。


被动语态:
The new policy was designated by the board of directors. = 新政策是由董事会指定的。
He was designated as the primary contact person for the project. = 他被指定为该项目的首要联系人。


疑问句:
What area has been designated for the construction? = 建设指定了哪个区域？
Who was designated to lead the team? = 谁被指定领导团队？


否定句:
The company did not designate a specific budget for the project. = 公司没有为该项目指定具体的预算。
They have not yet designated a replacement for the departing employee. = 他们尚未指定离职员工的替代者。
