eclipser:/ɪˈklɪpsər/| n. | 蚀掩体；遮蔽物；使黯然失色者 | Something that eclipses; something that overshadows or surpasses.  An eclipser is something that causes an eclipse, or more generally, something that obscures or diminishes something else. |The new skyscraper is an eclipser of the old buildings nearby. 新摩天大楼遮蔽了附近的老建筑。  The singer's new album is an eclipser of her previous work.  这位歌手的新专辑使其之前的作品黯然失色。 |近义词：obscurer, overshadower, surpasser |反义词：enhancer,illuminator |名词
