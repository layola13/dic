cnida:/ˈnaɪdə/| n.|刺细胞；刺丝囊|A cnida is an explosive cell containing a nematocyst, used by cnidarians (such as jellyfish and sea anemones) to capture prey or defend themselves.  It's a specialized stinging structure.  |例句：The jellyfish used its cnida to paralyze its prey.  水母用它的刺细胞麻痹猎物。|例句：The microscopic cnida were visible under the high-powered microscope.  在高倍显微镜下，肉眼看不见的刺细胞清晰可见。|近义词：nematocyst (more specific)|反义词：无|名词
