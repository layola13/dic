patients:/ˈpeɪʃənts/| n.|病人，患者|People receiving or requiring medical treatment.  The word refers to individuals under the care of a healthcare professional for an illness, injury, or condition.|例句：The doctor examined his patients carefully.医生仔细检查了他的病人。|例句：The hospital is struggling to cope with the influx of patients.医院正在努力应对涌入的病人。|近义词：clients, sufferers, invalids|反义词：doctors, healers
