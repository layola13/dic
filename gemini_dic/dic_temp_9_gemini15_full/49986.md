denitrification: /ˌdiːˌnaɪˈtrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |反硝化作用|The biological process where nitrates are reduced to nitrites and then to nitrogen gas, which is released into the atmosphere.|The denitrification process is crucial for the nitrogen cycle and maintaining the balance of nitrogen in the environment.|近义词：nitrogen reduction|反义词：nitrification
