parcae:/ˈpɑːrkeɪ/| n. |命运三女神 |The Parcae were the three goddesses of fate in Roman mythology.  They spun, measured, and cut the thread of life for every mortal.  Their names were Clotho (the spinner), Lachesis (the measurer), and Atropos (the cutter).|Example:  The Parcae, in Roman mythology, were believed to control the destiny of every human being.  命运三女神在罗马神话中被认为掌控着每个人的命运。|近义词：Fates (Greek equivalent)|反义词：None.
