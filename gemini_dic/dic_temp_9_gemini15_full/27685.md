candlemas:/ˈkændəlmæs/| n. |圣烛节|Candlemas is a Christian festival, also known as the Feast of the Presentation of Jesus Christ to the Temple or the Feast of Purification of the Blessed Virgin Mary.  It is celebrated on February 2nd and marks the presentation of Jesus at the Temple in Jerusalem forty days after his birth, as prescribed by Jewish law. The name comes from the traditional lighting of candles in churches during the celebration. |The church was beautifully decorated for Candlemas.  圣烛节那天教堂装饰得很漂亮。  The Candlemas procession was a moving sight. 圣烛节的游行场面感人。|近义词：Feast of the Presentation, Feast of Purification|反义词：无
