It suggests a high degree of accuracy but acknowledges a small margin of error or imperfection.  | Example: The quasi-exact solution to the differential equation provided a good approximation for practical purposes.  准精确的微分方程解为实际用途提供了良好的近似值。|近义词：approximate, near-exact, almost exact | 反义词：precise, exact, accurate
