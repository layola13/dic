defer:/dɪˈfɜːr/| /dɪˈfɝː/| vt. n.|推迟；延期；尊重；听从|To postpone something to a later time; to yield to someone's wishes or opinion.|例句：We have deferred the decision until next week. 我们已将决定推迟到下周。|例句：I defer to your judgment on this matter. 在这件事上，我听从你的判断。|近义词：postpone, delay, adjourn, submit|反义词：accelerate, hasten, expedite|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They defer the meeting until next week. = 他们将会议推迟到下周。
He defers to his father's opinion. = 他听从他父亲的意见。

一般过去时:
The company deferred the project launch. = 公司推迟了项目的启动。
She deferred to her boss's expertise. = 她尊重她老板的专业知识。

现在完成时:
They have deferred the payment. = 他们已经推迟了付款。
He has deferred to my judgment on numerous occasions. = 他多次听从我的判断。

过去完成时:
They had deferred the decision until they received more information. = 他们在收到更多信息之前已经推迟了决定。
She had deferred to her superior's instructions. = 她已经听从了上司的指示。

将来时:
They will defer the vote until next month. = 他们会把投票推迟到下个月。
He will defer to his wife's wishes. = 他将顺从他妻子的意愿。

虚拟语气:
If they deferred the project, they would have more time. = 如果他们推迟项目，他们将会有更多时间。
I wish I had deferred to his advice. = 我真希望我当时听从了他的建议。

被动语态:
The meeting was deferred due to unforeseen circumstances. = 会议由于不可预见的情况而被推迟。
His opinion was deferred to by everyone. = 每个人都尊重他的意见。

疑问句:
Will the game be deferred? = 比赛会延期吗？
Do you defer to authority easily? = 你容易服从权威吗？

否定句:
They did not defer the payment. = 他们没有推迟付款。
She did not defer to his unreasonable demands. = 她没有屈服于他无理的要求。
