retiredness: /rɪˈtaɪədnəs/ | n. |退休生活；退休状态| The state of being retired; the condition of having retired from one's job or profession.  It often encompasses the lifestyle and experiences associated with retirement.|例句：He enjoyed a comfortable retiredness, spending his days gardening and traveling. 他享受着舒适的退休生活，每天都忙着园艺和旅行。

例句：After years of hard work, she looked forward to the quiet retiredness of her later years.  多年辛苦工作后，她期待着晚年平静的退休生活。


近义词：retirement, leisure, inactivity

反义词：employment, activity, work
