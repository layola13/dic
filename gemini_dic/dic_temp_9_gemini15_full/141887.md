ordainers:/ɔːˈdeɪnərz/ | n. | 授权者；任命者；制定者 | Those who ordain or establish something, often laws, rules, or religious officials.  They are the ones who give authority or power. | The Church's ordainers debated the new canon law for hours. 教会的授权者们就新的教会法讨论了数小时。  The ordainers of the constitution ensured its longevity through careful design. 制定宪法的人们通过精心的设计确保了它的持久性。 | lawmakers, legislators, establishers |  - | 名词
