horse:/hɔːrs/|n.|马|A large, four-legged herbivorous mammal, domesticated for riding and work.|例句：My grandfather used to ride a horse every day. 我爷爷过去每天都骑马。|例句：The farmer uses horses to plow his fields. 这位农民用马耕地。|近义词：steed, pony, equine|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The horse runs in the field. = 马在田野里奔跑。
The horses eat hay. = 马吃干草。


一般过去时:
The horse jumped over the fence. = 马跳过了栅栏。
The horses ran away. = 马跑掉了。


现在完成时:
The horse has jumped the fence. = 马已经跳过了栅栏。
The horses have been fed. = 马已经被喂了。


过去完成时:
The horse had already run away before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，马已经跑掉了。
The horses had never been ridden before. = 这些马以前从未被骑过。


将来时:
The horse will run faster tomorrow. = 马明天会跑得更快。
The horses will be sold next week. = 马下周将被出售。


虚拟语气:
If I had a horse, I would ride it every day. = 如果我有一匹马，我每天都会骑它。
I wish I had a horse. = 我真希望我有一匹马。


被动语态:
The horse was ridden by a young boy. = 马被一个年轻男孩骑着。
The horses are often fed by the farmer. = 马经常被农民喂养。


疑问句:
Is that your horse? = 那是你的马吗？
How many horses are there? = 有多少匹马？


否定句:
That is not my horse. = 那不是我的马。
I don't have a horse. = 我没有马。


PHRASE->
dark horse: 黑马 (意指出人意料的赢家)
horse around: 胡闹，开玩笑
horse trading: 讨价还价
a horse of a different color: 完全不同的事
get on one's high horse: 趾高气扬
