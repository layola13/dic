choak:/tʃoʊk/| v.|哽住；窒息；阻塞；|To choke means to have difficulty breathing because your throat is blocked.  It can also refer to the act of something obstructing flow or progress.|The smoke choked me.  烟呛住了我。
He choked on a piece of food. 他被一块食物噎住了。|suffocate, strangle, stifle|breathe freely, clear

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He chokes on his food sometimes. 他有时会噎到食物。
The dust chokes the air. 灰尘使空气窒息。

一般过去时:
I choked on a piece of bread yesterday. 昨天我被一片面包噎住了。
The heavy smoke choked the firefighters. 浓烟呛住了消防员。

现在完成时:
The pollution has choked the river. 污染已经堵塞了河流。
She has choked back her tears. 她强忍住了眼泪。

过去完成时:
He had choked on a fish bone before the ambulance arrived. 救护车到达之前，他已经卡住鱼刺了。
The pipes had choked with debris. 管道已经被碎石堵塞了。

将来时:
The volcanic ash will choke the city.火山灰将使城市窒息。
I think the smoke will choke me. 我想烟会呛到我。


虚拟语气:
If I ate too fast, I might choke. 如果我吃得太快，我可能会噎住。
If he had not choked, he would be alive. 如果他没有窒息，他就会还活着。


被动语态:
The pipe was choked with mud. 管子被泥堵住了。
The engine was choked by the excessive fuel. 发动机被过多的燃料阻塞了。


疑问句:
Did the smoke choke you? 烟呛到你了么？
Is the pipe choked? 管子堵了吗？


否定句:
The food didn't choke him. 食物没有噎到他。
The air did not choke me. 空气没有让我窒息。

choke off:掐断；阻止
