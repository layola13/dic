genies:/ˈdʒiːniːz/| n. |精灵，妖怪 |Plural of genie.  A genie is a supernatural being, often depicted in folklore as having magical powers and being able to grant wishes.  The term often originates from Arabian Nights stories. | Example:  Many stories feature genies granting wishes to those who find their lamp.  许多故事都以精灵实现找到他们灯的人的愿望为特色。|近义词：jinn, spirit, demon |反义词：human, mortal
