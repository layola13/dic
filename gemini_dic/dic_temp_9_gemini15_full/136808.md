nonshedding: /ˈnɒnʃɛdɪŋ/ | adj. | 不掉毛的 | Not shedding hair or fur.  This term is typically used to describe animals, particularly pets, whose coats don't shed significantly. | Example:  She chose a nonshedding breed of dog because she's allergic to pet dander. 她选择了一种不掉毛的狗品种，因为她对宠物皮屑过敏。 | 近义词: low-shedding, minimal-shedding | 反义词:shedding
