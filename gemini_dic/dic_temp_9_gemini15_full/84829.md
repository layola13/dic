hang-fire:/ˈhæŋfaɪər/| v.| 延迟；搁置；停滞不前 | To delay or be delayed; to fail to go off (as a gun) or take effect | The project has been hanging fire for months.  这个项目已经搁置了几个月了。 The gun hung fire, and he missed his shot.  枪卡壳了，他错过了射击机会。 |  delay, postpone, stall | proceed, advance, expedite | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The negotiations hang fire. = 谈判陷入僵局。
The plan hangs fire due to lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，计划迟迟未能启动。


一般过去时:
The deal hung fire for weeks. = 这笔交易拖延了几周。
The engine hung fire before finally starting. = 发动机在最终启动前卡壳了。


现在完成时:
The project has been hanging fire since last year. = 该项目自去年以来一直搁置。
The decision has hung fire, causing much anxiety. = 决定迟迟未下，导致许多焦虑。


过去完成时:
The proposal had hung fire for months before it was finally rejected. = 该提案在最终被否决之前已经搁置了数月。
The contract had hung fire until the last minute. = 合同一直拖到最后一刻才敲定。


将来时:
The launch will hang fire until further notice. = 发布将无限期推迟。
The repairs will hang fire until the parts arrive. = 维修工作要等到零件到达才能进行。


虚拟语气:
If the funding didn't hang fire, the project would have been completed by now. = 如果资金没有拖延，项目现在就已经完成了。
I wish the decision wouldn't hang fire any longer. = 我希望决定不要再拖延了。


被动语态:
The agreement was hung fire by unexpected circumstances. = 协议因意外情况而被搁置。
The matter is being hung fire pending further investigation. = 此事正在进一步调查中，暂时搁置。


疑问句:
Is the project hanging fire? = 项目是否搁置了？
Will the deal hang fire again? = 这笔交易还会再次拖延吗？


否定句:
The negotiations didn't hang fire; they concluded successfully. = 谈判没有拖延；它们成功地完成了。
The plan didn't hang fire; it went ahead as scheduled. = 计划没有拖延；它按计划进行了。


PHRASE->

let something hang fire = 让某事搁置
