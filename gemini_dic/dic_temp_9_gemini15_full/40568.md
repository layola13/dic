contumelious:/ˌkɒntʃuˈmiːliəs/| adj. |轻蔑的；侮辱的；粗鲁无礼的 |Showing contempt or lack of respect; insulting.  The speaker's contumelious remarks offended many in the audience.  |例句：His contumelious tone during the debate alienated potential allies. 他在辩论中轻蔑的语气疏远了潜在的盟友。


例句：The critic's contumelious review caused the playwright significant distress. 批评家轻蔑的评论给剧作家带来了很大的痛苦。


近义词：insulting, abusive, scornful, contemptuous, disrespectful, rude, offensive


反义词：respectful, polite, courteous, deferential, complimentary


词源：From Late Latin *contumeliosus, from contumelia “insult, abuse,” from con- “together” + tumeo “I swell” (referring to swelling with anger or pride).  The sense development is from "swelling with anger/pride" to "expressing anger/pride through insult."
