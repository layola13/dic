prologues:/ˈproʊlɒɡz/ | n. |序言，开场白 |A prologue is an introductory section of a literary work, especially a play, or a speech introducing a play.  It often sets the scene, introduces characters, or provides background information. |例句：The play began with a long prologue that summarized the plot. 这出戏以一篇长的序言开始，概述了剧情。

例句：The novel's prologue hinted at the tragic events to come. 这部小说的序言暗示了即将发生的悲剧事件。


近义词：preface, introduction, preamble, prelude |反义词：epilogue, conclusion
