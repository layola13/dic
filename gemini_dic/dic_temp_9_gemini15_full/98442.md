inspects:/ɪnˈspɛkts/| vt.| 检查；视察；审视 | To examine something carefully in order to check its quality, condition, or to discover any problems. |例句：The engineer inspected the bridge for damage. 工程师检查桥梁是否有损坏。

例句：The teacher inspected each student's work carefully. 老师仔细检查了每个学生的作业。


近义词：examine, survey, scrutinize, review | 反义词：neglect, overlook | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He inspects the equipment daily. 他每天检查设备。
She inspects the goods before shipping them. 她发货前检查货物。

一般过去时:
The police inspected the crime scene. 警方检查了犯罪现场。
They inspected the house thoroughly before buying it. 他们在买房子之前彻底检查了房子。

现在完成时:
I have inspected all the documents. 我已经检查了所有文件。
She has inspected the factory twice this month. 这个月她已经检查过工厂两次了。

过去完成时:
He had inspected the engine before the flight. 他在飞行前检查了发动机。
They had inspected the building several times before it was declared safe. 在宣布该建筑物安全之前，他们已经检查过几次了。

将来时:
We will inspect the new facility next week. 我们下周将检查新设施。
The team will inspect the site tomorrow morning.  团队明天上午将视察现场。

虚拟语气:
If I were the manager, I would inspect the work myself. 如果我是经理，我会亲自检查工作。
It is essential that the inspector inspect the product carefully. 检查员仔细检查产品是必要的。

被动语态:
The building was inspected last week.  该建筑物上周进行了检查。
The products are inspected before they are shipped. 产品在发货前都会经过检查。

疑问句:
Will you inspect the report before submitting it? 你会在提交报告之前检查一下吗？
Did they inspect the equipment properly? 他们是否正确地检查了设备？

否定句:
He did not inspect the package carefully. 他没有仔细检查包裹。
They didn't inspect the car before they bought it. 他们买车前没有检查。
