fighter:/ˈfaɪtər/| n. |战士，战斗机，斗士 |A fighter is a person or thing that fights.  This can refer to a person involved in combat, a military aircraft designed for fighting, or someone who shows determination and resilience in facing challenges. |例句：He was a brave fighter in the war. 他是战争中一名勇敢的战士。|例句：The new fighter jet is equipped with advanced weaponry. 这架新型战斗机配备了先进的武器。|近义词：combatant, warrior, contender,  aircraft|反义词：peacemaker, pacifist


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He fights for justice. = 他为正义而战。
She fights for her rights. = 她为自己的权利而战。


一般过去时:
The soldiers fought bravely. = 士兵们英勇作战。
He fought against injustice. = 他与不公正作斗争。


现在完成时:
They have fought many battles. = 他们已经打过许多仗了。
She has fought for equality her whole life. = 她为平等奋斗了一生。


过去完成时:
They had fought hard before they won. = 他们在获胜之前已经努力战斗了。
He had fought many times before this battle. = 在这场战斗之前，他已经战斗过很多次了。


将来时:
We will fight for what we believe in. = 我们将为我们信仰的东西而战。
They will fight to the end. = 他们将战斗到最后。


虚拟语气:
If I were a fighter, I would fight for peace. = 如果我是一个战士，我会为和平而战。
I wish I had the strength to fight for what is right. = 我希望我有力量为正义而战。


被动语态:
The battle was fought fiercely. = 战斗进行得很激烈。
The war was fought over many years. = 战争持续了很多年。


疑问句:
Will you fight for your country? = 你会为你的国家而战吗？
Who will fight for us? = 谁会为我们而战？


否定句:
He did not fight in the war. = 他没有参加战争。
They will not fight unless they are attacked. = 除非受到攻击，否则他们不会战斗。
