heartshaped:/ˈhɑːrtsʃeɪpt/| adj.|心形的|Having the shape of a heart.  This adjective describes something that resembles the shape of a human heart, typically rounded at the bottom and pointed at the top.|例句：She received a heartshaped box of chocolates. 她收到了一盒心形巧克力。

例句：The lake was heartshaped, a romantic sight.  湖泊是心形的，景色很浪漫。


近义词：heart-shaped (identical meaning) ,cordate (botanical term)


反义词：square-shaped, round, rectangular, triangular


词性:形容词
