sinking:/ˈsɪŋkɪŋ/| v. adj. n.|下沉；使下沉；下沉物；消沉|The act or process of going down below the surface of water or another liquid, or of gradually losing value or importance.  Also used to describe a feeling of depression or discouragement.|例句：The ship is sinking rapidly. 这艘船正在迅速下沉。|例句：The economy is sinking into recession. 经济正在陷入衰退。|近义词：submerging, descending, declining, depressing|反义词：rising, floating, ascending, buoyant|动词，形容词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ship is sinking. = 船正在下沉。
He is sinking into despair. = 他正在陷入绝望。

一般过去时:
The Titanic sank in 1912. = 泰坦尼克号在1912年沉没。
The value of the property sank dramatically. = 房地产价值急剧下降。

现在完成时:
The ship has sunk. = 船已经沉没了。
His spirits have sunk since the accident. = 自从事故发生后，他的精神状态一直低落。

过去完成时:
The ship had sunk before help arrived. = 在救援到来之前，船已经沉没了。
The company's stock had sunk to a record low. = 公司的股票跌至历史低点。

将来时:
The boat will sink if it takes on too much water. = 如果船进水过多，它就会沉没。
The economy will sink further without intervention. = 如果不干预，经济将进一步下滑。

虚拟语气:
If the ship hadn't been overloaded, it wouldn't have sunk. = 如果船没有超载，它就不会沉没。
If I hadn't lost my job, my spirits wouldn't have sunk so low. = 如果我没有失业，我的精神状态就不会这么低落。

被动语态:
The boat was sunk by a torpedo. = 船被鱼雷击沉。
The proposal was sunk by the opposition. = 该提案被反对派否决了。

疑问句:
Is the boat sinking? = 船在下沉吗？
Is your morale sinking? = 你的士气低落吗？

否定句:
The ship didn't sink. = 船没有沉没。
The plan didn't sink. = 计划没有失败。
