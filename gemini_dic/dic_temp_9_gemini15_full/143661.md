outlaboring: /ˌaʊtˈleɪbərɪŋ/ | v. |  超过…的工作量；胜过…的工作能力 | To surpass in labor; to exceed in work done.  This word is relatively rare and often appears in more formal or literary contexts. It emphasizes exceeding someone in the amount or intensity of work performed.  |  例句：The small team outlabored the larger one, completing the project ahead of schedule. 小团队的工作量超过了大团队，提前完成了项目。

The diligent student outlabored her classmates, spending countless hours studying. 勤奋的学生比她的同学们付出了更多努力，花费了无数时间学习。| 近义词：outwork, surpass, exceed, outperform | 反义词：underwork, underperform
