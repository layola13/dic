resuscitating: /ˌriˈsʌsɪteɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 使复苏；使复活；使恢复生机 |  The act of reviving someone or something from a state of unconsciousness or apparent death, or restoring something to activity or vigor.  | 例句：Paramedics were working frantically, resuscitating the accident victim.  急救人员正在拼命地抢救车祸受害者。


例句：The doctor is resuscitating the patient after the heart attack. 医生正在心脏病发作后抢救病人。


近义词：reviving, reviving, restoring, revitalizing | 反义词：killing, destroying, ending
