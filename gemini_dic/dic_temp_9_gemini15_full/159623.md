pollute:/pəˈluːt/| vt.|污染；玷污|To make (something) dirty, unclean, or impure, especially with harmful chemicals or waste.|例句：The factory polluted the river with toxic chemicals.  这家工厂用有毒化学物质污染了河流。|例句：Don't pollute your mind with negative thoughts. 不要让消极的想法污染你的思想。|近义词：contaminate, taint, defile|反义词：purify, cleanse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory pollutes the river. = 工厂污染河流。
We pollute the environment with our actions. = 我们用我们的行为污染环境。

一般过去时:
They polluted the lake with sewage. = 他们用污水污染了湖泊。
The oil spill polluted the coastline. = 原油泄漏污染了海岸线。

现在完成时:
Industrial activities have polluted the air for decades. = 工业活动几十年来一直在污染空气。
We have polluted the ocean with plastic waste. = 我们用塑料垃圾污染了海洋。

过去完成时:
They had polluted the well before anyone noticed. = 他们在任何人注意到之前就已经污染了井水。
The river had been polluted for years before the cleanup began. = 在清理工作开始之前，这条河已经被污染多年了。

将来时:
The new factory will pollute the air if they don't install proper filters. = 如果他们不安装合适的过滤器，新工厂将污染空气。
If we continue to consume at this rate, we will pollute the planet even further. = 如果我们继续以这种速度消费，我们将进一步污染地球。

虚拟语气:
If they hadn't polluted the river, the fish would still be thriving. = 如果他们没有污染河流，鱼类仍然会茁壮成长。
I wish they hadn't polluted the beach with litter. = 我真希望他们没有把垃圾扔在海滩上污染海滩。

被动语态:
The river was polluted by industrial waste. = 河流被工业废物污染了。
The air is being polluted by car emissions. = 空气正在被汽车尾气污染。

疑问句:
Is the water polluted? = 水被污染了吗？
What is polluting the air in this city? = 这个城市是什么污染了空气？

否定句:
They didn't pollute the land. = 他们没有污染土地。
We don't want to pollute our beautiful countryside. = 我们不想污染我们美丽的乡村。
