saree:/ˈsɑːriː/ | n. | 纱丽 | A women's garment from the Indian subcontinent, consisting of a length of unstitched cloth, typically five to nine yards long, draped around the body.  | Example: She wore a beautiful silk saree to the wedding. 她穿着一条美丽的丝绸纱丽去参加婚礼。 |近义词：dhoti (for men),  dress, garment | 反义词：None
