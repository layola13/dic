nonhypnotics:/ˌnɒnɪpˈnɒtɪks/| n. |非催眠药|Substances or medications that do not induce hypnosis or sleep.  They are used to treat various conditions without causing drowsiness.  |Example:  Many nonhypnotics are used to treat anxiety disorders. 许多非催眠药用于治疗焦虑症。|近义词：sedative alternatives, non-narcotic sleep aids|反义词：hypnotics, sedatives|名词
