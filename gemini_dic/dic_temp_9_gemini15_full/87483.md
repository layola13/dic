heterarchy:/ˈhɛtərɑːki/| n. |异质等级制度，多中心系统| A system of organization where power is distributed across multiple centers, rather than concentrated in a single authority.  It contrasts with hierarchy, which features a single, centralized power structure. Heterarchies often involve complex interactions and feedback loops between different elements.  Examples include ecological systems, social networks, and some organizational structures. |Example: The company's organizational structure was a heterarchy, with power distributed among various teams and departments.  公司组织结构是一个异质等级制度，权力分散在各个团队和部门之间。|近义词：polyarchy, network, decentralized system |反义词：hierarchy, autocracy
