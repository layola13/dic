seposition:/ˌsɛpəˈzɪʃən/| n. |沉积作用；沉淀；析出|The geological process in which sediments, soil, and rocks are added to a landform or land mass.  This process can be caused by several natural forces such as wind, ice, and water.  It is the process of settling or being deposited. |例句：The deposition of sediment in the river delta created fertile farmland. 河流三角洲的沉积物沉积形成了肥沃的农田。

例句：The slow deposition of minerals formed the stalactites in the cave.  矿物质的缓慢沉积形成了洞穴中的钟乳石。


近义词：sedimentation, precipitation, accumulation |反义词：erosion, removal
