nondepressive:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈprɛsɪv/ | adj. |非抑郁的，无抑郁症状的 |Not characterized by or suffering from depression.  The term describes a state or condition free from the symptoms associated with depressive disorders.  |Example:  He maintained a nondepressive attitude despite facing numerous challenges. 他尽管面临诸多挑战，却保持着乐观的态度。|近义词：cheerful, upbeat, optimistic, positive |反义词：depressed, melancholic, gloomy
