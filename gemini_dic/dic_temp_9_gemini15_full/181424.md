rejacket:/ˌriːˈdʒækɪt/| v.|重新穿外套；重新包装|To put a jacket back on; to repackage something in a new jacket or container.|例句：He rejacketed the book after finding a better cover. 他在找到更好的封面后重新包装了这本书。|近义词：repackage, rewrap|反义词：unjacket, unpack
