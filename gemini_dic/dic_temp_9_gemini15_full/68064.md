fad:/fæd/| n.|一时流行的时尚；短暂的流行；狂热|A fad is a temporary fashion, an interest, or a practice that is very popular for a short time.  It is often characterized by rapid growth in popularity, followed by a rapid decline.|Example:  The fidget spinner was a fad that swept the nation a few years ago.  几年前，指尖陀螺风靡全国。|近义词：craze, craze, trend, vogue|反义词：classic, enduring, timeless|名词
