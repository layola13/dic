evangelicalism:/ˌiːvænˈdʒɛlɪkəlɪzəm/| n. |福音派；福音主义；福音运动 |The belief and practice of evangelical Christianity, emphasizing a personal relationship with Jesus Christ, the authority of the Bible, and the importance of evangelism (spreading the Christian message).  It often involves a strong emphasis on personal conversion, missionary work, and social activism informed by religious beliefs. |Example: The rise of evangelicalism in the United States has had a significant impact on American politics.  福音派在美国的兴起对美国政治产生了重大影响。|近义词：evangelism, revivalism |反义词：secularism, atheism
