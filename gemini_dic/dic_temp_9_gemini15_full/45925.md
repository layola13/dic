cutting-edge:/ˈkʌtɪŋˌɛdʒ/| adj.|最先进的；尖端的； |The most advanced stage of development in a particular field.  It refers to something that is at the forefront of innovation and technology.|例句：This new technology is cutting-edge. 这项新技术是最先进的。|例句：Her research is at the cutting edge of medical science. 她的研究处于医学科学的最前沿。|近义词：advanced, pioneering, innovative, state-of-the-art|反义词：outdated, obsolete, old-fashioned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is always at the cutting edge of technology. = 这家公司始终处于技术的最前沿。
This research is cutting-edge in its field. = 这项研究在其领域处于领先地位。

一般过去时:
Their product was cutting-edge ten years ago. = 他们的产品十年前是尖端的。
The design was cutting-edge for its time. = 那个设计在当时是领先的。

现在完成时:
The company has remained at the cutting edge of innovation. = 这家公司一直保持着创新的领先地位。
Scientists have made a cutting-edge discovery. = 科学家们取得了一项突破性发现。

过去完成时:
The technology had been cutting-edge before the new discovery. = 在这项新发现之前，这项技术已经是尖端的了。
The project had already implemented several cutting-edge solutions. = 该项目已经实施了几项尖端解决方案。

将来时:
This technology will be cutting-edge for the next decade. = 这项技术在未来十年内都将处于领先地位。
The new design will surely be cutting-edge in the market. = 新的设计肯定会在市场上处于领先地位。

虚拟语气:
If we had more funding, our research would be more cutting-edge. = 如果我们有更多的资金，我们的研究将会更尖端。
I wish our company were at the cutting edge of this industry. = 我希望我们的公司能够处于这个行业的领先地位。

被动语态:
The project was considered cutting-edge by many experts. = 该项目被许多专家认为是尖端的。
New methods are being developed to keep the technology cutting-edge. = 正在开发新的方法以保持技术的先进性。

疑问句:
Is this product truly cutting-edge? = 这个产品真的是尖端的吗？
Are they using cutting-edge techniques in their research? = 他们在研究中使用尖端技术了吗？

否定句:
The software is not cutting-edge anymore. = 这个软件不再是尖端的了。
Their approach wasn't exactly cutting-edge. = 他们的方法并不完全是尖端的。
