faces:/ˈfeɪsɪz/| n.|面孔，表情；表面；外观|The plural of "face".  Refers to multiple instances of a face, either literally or figuratively. It can refer to the physical features of a person's face, or to expressions shown on a face, or even to the outward appearance of something.|例句：The faces in the crowd were a mixture of excitement and anxiety.  人群中的面孔既兴奋又焦虑。

例句：The building has many faces, each with a unique architectural style. 这座建筑有很多面，每面都有独特的建筑风格。|近义词：countenances, visages|反义词：back, reverse.
