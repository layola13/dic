hamperedly:/ˈhæmpərdli/| adv.| 受阻碍地，妨碍地 | In a way that is hampered or hindered.  It describes an action or process that is slowed down or made difficult. |例句：The hikers proceeded hamperedly through the dense undergrowth.  登山者艰难地穿过茂密的灌木丛。

例句：The project progressed hamperedly due to a lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，项目进展缓慢。


近义词：hinderedly, obstructively, impededly


反义词：freely, smoothly, easily


词性：副词
