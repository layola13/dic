schoolboyishly:/ˈskuːlˌbɔɪɪʃli/ | adj. | 孩子气地；像小学生一样地 |  In a manner characteristic of a schoolboy; immaturely, playfully, or mischievously.  It suggests a lack of seriousness or sophistication, often with a hint of charm. |

He grinned schoolboyishly, clearly delighted with his prank. 他孩子气地咧嘴一笑，显然对自己的恶作剧很高兴。

She answered the question schoolboyishly, avoiding the deeper issues. 她像个小学生一样回答了这个问题，回避了更深层次的问题。


近义词: childishly, youthfully, impishly, playfully

反义词: maturely, seriously, gravely, solemnly


词性:形容词
