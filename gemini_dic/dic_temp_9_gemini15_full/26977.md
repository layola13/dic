calciner:/kælˈsaɪnər/| n.|煅烧炉；煅烧器|A calciner is a furnace or other vessel used for calcining, a process of heating a substance to a high temperature to bring about a physical or chemical change.  Calciners are used in various industries, including cement production, metal refining, and chemical processing.|例句：The cement plant uses a rotary calciner to heat limestone and produce quicklime.  水泥厂使用旋转煅烧炉加热石灰石并生产生石灰。|近义词：kiln, furnace|反义词：无
