happens:/ˈhæpənz/ | v. |发生，碰巧 | To occur; to take place; to come to pass.  It also implies a degree of chance or unexpectedness. |例句：Something unexpected happened.  = 发生了意想不到的事情。

例句：What happened to your car? = 你的车怎么了？


近义词：occur, take place, come about, transpire | 反义词：N/A | 词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What happens every day? = 每天发生什么？
It often happens that I forget my keys. = 我经常忘记带钥匙。


一般过去时:
What happened yesterday? = 昨天发生了什么？
The accident happened quickly. = 事故发生得很迅速。


现在完成时:
What has happened to him? = 他怎么了？
A lot has happened since we last met. = 自从我们上次见面以来，发生了很多事情。


过去完成时:
What had happened before the police arrived? = 警员到达之前发生了什么？
By the time I arrived, the party had already happened. = 我到达时，派对已经结束了。


将来时:
What will happen next? = 下一步会发生什么？
I think something interesting will happen tomorrow. = 我想明天会发生一些有趣的事情。


虚拟语气:
If it happened again, I wouldn't know what to do. = 如果它再次发生，我就不知道该怎么办了。
I wish this hadn't happened. = 我希望这件事没有发生。


被动语态:
The accident was reported to have happened yesterday. = 事故据报道发生在昨天。
It happened to be seen by a passerby. = 它碰巧被一个路人看到了。


疑问句:
What happened? = 发生了什么事？
How did it happen? = 这是怎么回事？


否定句:
Nothing happened. = 什么事也没发生。
It didn't happen as planned. = 它并没有按照计划进行。



PHRASE->
What happens if...? = 如果……会怎么样？
What happens next? = 接下来的事情会怎样？
It just so happens that... = 碰巧……
What happened to...? = ……怎么了？
So what happens now? = 那么现在怎么办？
