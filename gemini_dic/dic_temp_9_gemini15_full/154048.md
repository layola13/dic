perfuse:/pərˈfjuːz/| vt.|灌注；充满；弥漫|To fill or saturate something completely.  The term often implies a thorough and sometimes overwhelming saturation.|例句：The wound was perfused with blood.  伤口被血液灌注。|例句：His speech was perfused with anger. 他的讲话充满了愤怒。|近义词：saturate, flood, inundate|反义词：drain, deplete
