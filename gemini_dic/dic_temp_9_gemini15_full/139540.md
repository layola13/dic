obtruders:/əbˈtruːdərz/| n.|闯入者；强行介入者 |Those who intrude or force their way in without invitation or welcome.  They are unwelcome and often disruptive. |例句：The obtruders were quickly removed from the premises by security.  保安迅速将闯入者从场所中移走。


例句：The noisy obtruders disrupted the peaceful meeting.  吵闹的闯入者扰乱了平静的会议。


近义词：intruders, trespassers, invaders, interlopers


反义词：guests, invitees, welcome visitors


词性:名词
