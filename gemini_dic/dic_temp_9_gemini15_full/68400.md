falsehood:/ˈfɔːlsˌhʊd/| n.|虚假；谎言；谬误|An untrue statement; a lie.  It can also refer to something that is not genuine or authentic.|例句：He told a falsehood to protect his friend. 他撒谎来保护他的朋友。

例句：The article contained several falsehoods about the company's history. 这篇文章包含几处关于公司历史的错误信息。


近义词：lie, untruth, fabrication, deception, sham

反义词：truth, veracity, fact, reality

词性:名词
