daubed:/dɔːbd/| v.|涂抹；乱涂；敷衍了事|To cover (a surface) with a thick layer of something, especially paint or mud; to apply (something) in a careless or untidy way.|例句：He daubed the wall with paint. 他在墙上乱涂油漆。

例句：The children daubed their drawings with bright colours. 孩子们用鲜艳的颜色乱涂他们的画。


近义词：smear, slap, plaster, smudge|反义词：refine, polish, detail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He daubs the canvas with thick paint. = 他用厚厚的颜料涂抹画布。
She daubs her lips with lipstick. = 她用口红涂抹嘴唇。


一般过去时:
They daubed the fence with mud. = 他们用泥巴涂抹栅栏。
The artist daubed the colours onto the paper. = 艺术家将颜色涂抹到纸上。


现在完成时:
He has daubed the entire wall. = 他已经涂抹了整面墙。
