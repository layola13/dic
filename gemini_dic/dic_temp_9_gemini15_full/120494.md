minuet:/ˈmɪnjuːet/ | n. | 小步舞曲 | A minuet is a slow, graceful dance in 3/4 time, popular in the 17th and 18th centuries.  It is characterized by its elegant and stately movements.  | Example: The dancers performed a beautiful minuet in period costume.  舞者们身穿古装表演了一段优美的赋格舞。 | 近义词：dance, gavotte | 反义词：None (no direct antonym)

词源:
