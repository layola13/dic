halfpenny:/ˈhæpni/| n.| 半便士 | A former British bronze coin worth half a penny.  It ceased to be legal tender in 1984. | Example:  He only had a halfpenny in his pocket. 他口袋里只有一枚半便士。 |近义词: half-penny (alternative spelling) |反义词: pound, shilling (historically larger denominations)
