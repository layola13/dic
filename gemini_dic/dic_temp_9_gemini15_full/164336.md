preelects:/ˌpriːɪˈlɛkts/| vt.|预选；预先选举|To choose or select beforehand; to elect in advance.|例句：The committee preelects its officers for the coming year.  委员会预先选举了下一年的官员。

The party preelected their candidate before the official primaries.  该党在正式初选前预选了他们的候选人。

近义词：preselect, choose in advance, nominate beforehand|反义词：post-elect
