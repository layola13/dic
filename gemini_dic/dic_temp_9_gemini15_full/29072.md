carvel-built:/ˈkɑːrvəl bɪlt/ | adj. | 舯缝船体结构的；平缝船体的 |  Describes a type of boat construction where the planks are laid edge to edge and fastened together, with the seams caulked.  This is in contrast to clinker-built, where the planks overlap. |  The ship was carvel-built, giving it a smooth hull. 这艘船是平缝船体结构的，使船体非常光滑。|近义词：flush-planked, smooth-hulled |反义词：clinker-built
