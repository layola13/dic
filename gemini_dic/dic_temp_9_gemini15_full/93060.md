idolise:/ˈaɪdəlaɪz/| vt.| 崇拜；盲目敬仰 | To regard or treat (someone or something) as an idol; to admire excessively. |例句：She idolized her older sister. 她盲目崇拜她姐姐。|例句：Many young people idolize pop stars. 许多年轻人崇拜流行歌星。|近义词：worship, adore, venerate |反义词：despise, scorn, disdain |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He idolizes his football coach. = 他崇拜他的足球教练。
They idolize the singer's talent. = 他们崇拜这位歌手的才华。

一般过去时:
I idolized that movie star when I was young. = 我年轻的时候崇拜过那位电影明星。
She idolized her teacher for her wisdom. = 她因为老师的智慧而崇拜她。

现在完成时:
They have idolized this band for over a decade. = 他们崇拜这个乐队已经超过十年了。
He has idolized that athlete since childhood. = 他从小就崇拜那位运动员。

过去完成时:
I had idolized her for years before I finally met her. = 在我最终见到她之前，我已经崇拜了她多年。
They had idolized the musician before his controversial actions. = 在他做出有争议的行为之前，他们崇拜过这位音乐家。

将来时:
She will continue to idolize her favorite author. = 她将继续崇拜她最喜欢的作家。
He will idolize his older brother throughout his life. = 他将终生崇拜他的哥哥。

虚拟语气:
If I were a child again, I would idolize that superhero. = 如果我再次成为孩子，我会崇拜那个超级英雄。
I wish I hadn't idolized such a flawed person. = 我真希望我没有崇拜过这样一个有缺陷的人。

被动语态:
That singer is idolized by millions of fans. = 那位歌手受到数百万粉丝的崇拜。
The artist was idolized for his unique style. = 这位艺术家因其独特的风格而受到崇拜。

疑问句:
Do you idolize any particular person? = 你崇拜任何特定的人吗？
Did they idolize the leader blindly? = 他们盲目地崇拜领导人吗？

否定句:
He doesn't idolize anyone. = 他不崇拜任何人。
She didn't idolize him after learning about his past. = 在了解他的过去之后，她不再崇拜他了。


PHRASE->
idolize someone blindly = 盲目崇拜某人

idolize someone's work = 崇拜某人的作品
idolize a certain lifestyle = 崇拜某种生活方式
idolize a historical figure = 崇拜一个历史人物
