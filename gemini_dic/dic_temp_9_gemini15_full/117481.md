medley:/ˈmɛdli/| n.|混合物；（尤指音乐）联奏曲；杂烩|A medley is a mixture of different things, often musical pieces played or sung together.  It can also refer to a varied collection of things.|例句：The concert ended with a medley of popular songs.  这场音乐会以一首流行歌曲联奏曲结束。

The restaurant offered a medley of appetizers. 这家餐厅提供各种各样的开胃菜。|近义词：mixture, assortment, potpourri|反义词：uniformity, homogeneity|名词
