press-up: /ˈprɛsʌp/ | n. v. |俯卧撑；撑起|

英文解析:A press-up is an exercise where you lie on your stomach, push yourself up using your arms, and then lower yourself back down.  It's a common exercise used to strengthen the chest, shoulders, and triceps. As a verb, it means to perform this exercise.


例句:

He did twenty press-ups this morning.  = 他今天早上做了二十个俯卧撑。

I need to do some press-ups to build up my chest muscles. = 我需要做一些俯卧撑来锻炼我的胸肌。


近义词: push-up


反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑与放松相关的词语)  relaxation, rest


词性:  Noun (名词), Verb (动词)
