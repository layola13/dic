restrictors:/rɪˈstrɪktərz/| n.|限制因素，约束物|Something that restricts or limits something else.  They can be physical objects, rules, regulations, or even abstract concepts that prevent something from happening or developing fully.|例句：Budgetary restrictors prevented the project from expanding. 预算限制阻止了项目的扩张。|例句：The tight deadlines acted as restrictors on our creativity. 紧迫的期限限制了我们的创造力。|近义词：constraints, limitations, restraints, inhibitors|反义词：enablers, facilitators
