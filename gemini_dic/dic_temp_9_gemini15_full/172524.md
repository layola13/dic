pyrites:/ˈpaɪraɪts/| n. |黄铁矿|A brassy yellow mineral consisting of iron disulfide (FeS2), often found in cubic crystals. It is sometimes mistaken for gold because of its color and metallic luster. |The miners mistook the glittering pyrites for gold.  矿工们把闪闪发光的黄铁矿误认为黄金了。|gold (in appearance), fool's gold|  (无明显反义词)|名词
