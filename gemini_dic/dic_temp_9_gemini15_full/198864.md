sidesman:/ˈsaɪdzmən/| n.|陪祭员，（教堂）执事|A sidesman is a member of a church congregation who assists the clergy during a service, often by helping with the collection of offerings or by guiding worshippers.  |Example: The sidesman politely directed the latecomers to their seats.  陪祭员礼貌地引导迟到者到他们的座位。|近义词：usher, deacon (in some contexts)|反义词：N/A
