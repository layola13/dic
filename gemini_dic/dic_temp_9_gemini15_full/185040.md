retheorized:/ˌriːˈθiːəraɪzd/| v.|重新理论化，重新构建理论 |To reconsider and reformulate a theory or theories; to reinterpret existing theories in light of new evidence or perspectives.  |例句：The discovery of the new fossil forced paleontologists to retheorize the evolutionary path of the species.  新的化石的发现迫使古生物学家重新构建该物种的进化路径。


例句：The physicist retheorized his model of the universe after considering the latest cosmological data. 物理学家在考虑了最新的宇宙学数据后，重新构建了他的宇宙模型。


近义词：revise, reformulate, reinterpret, reconstruct


反义词：accept, confirm, maintain (as correct)


词性：动词
