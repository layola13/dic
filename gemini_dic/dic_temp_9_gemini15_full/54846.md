displacency:/dɪsˈpleɪsənsi/| n.|不悦；不满；不快|Displacency is a feeling of displeasure or dissatisfaction.  It suggests a mild to moderate level of unhappiness, often stemming from something specific rather than a general sense of unhappiness.  It is less intense than resentment or indignation.|例句：Her displacency was evident in her curt responses.  她的不悦从她简短的回答中显而易见。

例句：His displacency with the outcome of the meeting was palpable. 他对会议结果的不满显而易见。


近义词： displeasure, dissatisfaction, discontent, unhappiness, annoyance

反义词：pleasure, satisfaction, contentment, happiness
