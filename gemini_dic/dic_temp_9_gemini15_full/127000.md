nasalize:/ˈneɪzəlaɪz/ | vt. | 鼻音化；使带鼻音 | To produce (a sound) with the nasal passages open, as in the pronunciation of the English /m/, /n/, and /ŋ/.  It's also used to describe the process of making a vowel sound nasal. | Example:  He nasalized the vowel in that word, making it sound different. 他把那个词里的元音鼻音化了，听起来不一样了。The singer nasalized her vowels to give a distinctive quality to her voice. 歌手把她的元音鼻音化，以赋予她的声音独特的品质。|近义词：nasalize, vocalize |反义词:denasalize
