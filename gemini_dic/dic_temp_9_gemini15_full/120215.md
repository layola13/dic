minerals: /ˈmɪnərəlz/ | n. | 矿物，矿物质 | Naturally occurring inorganic solids with a definite chemical composition and a crystalline structure.  Minerals are the building blocks of rocks and are essential components of the Earth's crust. They are also vital for plant and animal life. |


例句：

Iron is a valuable mineral used in many industries.  铁是一种用于许多行业的宝贵矿物。

The soil is rich in minerals, making it ideal for farming. 这片土壤富含矿物质，非常适合耕作。


近义词：ores (if referring to economically valuable minerals)

反义词：None (no direct antonym, but "organic matter" could be considered a contrasting concept)


词性:名词
