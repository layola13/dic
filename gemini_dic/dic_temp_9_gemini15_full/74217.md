fourthly: /ˈfɔːθli/ | adv. |第四地，第四点|  The adverb "fourthly" is used to introduce the fourth point or item in a series or list. It is a formal word and is less common in everyday speech than "fourth."  | Example: Firstly, we need more staff. Secondly, we need more funding. Thirdly, we need better equipment. Fourthly, we need a new strategy.  首先，我们需要更多员工。其次，我们需要更多资金。第三，我们需要更好的设备。第四，我们需要一个新的战略。|近义词: fourth, in the fourth place |反义词:firstly, secondly, thirdly

词源:
