epiphora:/ɪˈpɪfərə/| n.|反复出现；(修辞手法)叠句|The repetition of a word or phrase at the end of successive clauses.  It is a figure of speech used to emphasize a point or create a rhythmic effect.|例句：He was tired, she was tired, everyone was tired.|近义词：repetition, reiteration|反义词：variation, diversity|名词
