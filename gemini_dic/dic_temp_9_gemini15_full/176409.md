re-imaged:/ˌriːˈɪmɪdʒd/| v. |重新塑造形象；重新构想；重新想象 |To conceive or represent something in a new or different way.| The company re-imaged its brand to appeal to a younger audience.  这家公司重新塑造了其品牌形象以吸引更年轻的受众。|近义词：reimagined, re-envisioned, reconceived |反义词：unchanged, static


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-image their products regularly. = 他们定期重新设计他们的产品。
The artist re-images the world through his paintings. = 艺术家通过他的绘画重新诠释世界。

一般过去时:
The museum re-imaged its exhibits last year. = 博物馆去年重新设计了它的展品。
She re-imaged the story for a modern audience. = 她为现代观众重新演绎了这个故事。

现在完成时:
The designer has re-imaged many classic styles. = 该设计师已经重新设计了许多经典款式。
We have re-imaged our marketing strategy. = 我们已经重新规划了我们的营销策略。

过去完成时:
They had re-imaged the building before the grand opening. = 他们在盛大开幕前已经重新设计了这座建筑。
The writer had re-imaged the character several times before settling on the final version. = 作家在最终确定人物形象之前已经多次重新构思了这个人物。

将来时:
We will re-image the website next month. = 我们下个月将重新设计网站。
The company will re-image its public image after the scandal. = 丑闻过后，公司将重新塑造其公众形象。

虚拟语气:
If they re-imaged the logo, it would look better. = 如果他们重新设计标志，它会看起来更好。
I wish they had re-imaged the website sooner. = 我希望他们早点重新设计网站。

被动语态:
The brand was re-imaged to attract a new customer base. = 品牌经过重新设计以吸引新的客户群体。
The old building is being re-imaged into a modern art gallery. = 旧建筑正在被改造成现代美术馆。

疑问句:
Will the product be re-imaged for the new market? = 产品会针对新市场重新设计吗？
Has the company re-imaged its brand successfully? = 公司是否成功地重新塑造了其品牌？

否定句:
They did not re-image the product. = 他们没有重新设计产品。
The company has not yet re-imaged its website. = 公司尚未重新设计其网站。
