sadhus:/ˈsɑːduz/| n.|圣人；苦行僧|A sadhu is a Hindu ascetic who renounces worldly possessions and practices extreme self-discipline and devotion.|例句：Many sadhus live in remote areas, dedicating their lives to spiritual practices.  许多苦行僧住在偏远地区，将他们的生命奉献给灵性修行。|例句：The sadhu sat silently, meditating under the banyan tree.  这位苦行僧静静地坐在菩提树下冥想。|近义词：ascetic, hermit, yogi|反义词：materialist, hedonist
