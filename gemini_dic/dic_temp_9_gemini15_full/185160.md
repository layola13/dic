retiring:/rɪˈtaɪərɪŋ/ | adj. | 退休的；不愿与人交往的；害羞的；隐退的 |  Retiring describes someone who is shy, quiet, and prefers to be alone, or it can refer to someone who has stopped working because of age or ill health.  It can also describe the act of withdrawing from a position or activity. |


例句：

He is a retiring person and doesn't like to socialize. 他是一个不愿与人交往的人，不喜欢社交。

She is retiring from her job next month.  她下个月就要退休了。


近义词：shy, withdrawn, reserved, secluded, quiet

反义词：outgoing, extroverted, sociable, gregarious


词源:

The word "retiring" comes from the verb "retire," which ultimately derives from the Old French word "retirer," meaning "to withdraw."  This, in turn, is composed of the prefix "re-" (again) and "tirer" (to draw).  The sense of withdrawing from active life or work developed over time.


USAGE->

一般现在时:

She is retiring from public life. 她将退出公众生活。


一般过去时:

He retired last year. 他去年退休了。

She retired from her position as CEO. 她辞去了首席执行官的职务。


现在完成时:

He has retired from his job. 他已经退休了。

She has retired to a quiet life in the countryside. 她已退休，在乡下过着平静的生活。


过去完成时:

He had retired before I met him.  在我遇到他之前，他已经退休了。

She had retired from the company before the scandal broke.  丑闻爆发前，她已经离开了公司。


将来时:

He will retire next year. 他明年将退休。

She will retire to Florida. 她将退休到佛罗里达州。


虚拟语气:

If he retired, he would have more time for his hobbies. 如果他退休了，他就会有更多时间从事他的爱好。

I wish I could retire early. 我希望我能早点退休。


被动语态:

He was retired by the company due to ill health.  由于健康原因，他被公司辞退了（退休了）。

The plan was retired after several years of use.  该计划在使用数年后被废弃了。


疑问句:

Is he retiring soon? 他很快就要退休了吗？

Are you planning to retire early? 你计划提前退休吗？


否定句:

He is not retiring yet. 他还没退休。

She did not retire until she was 65. 直到65岁她才退休。
