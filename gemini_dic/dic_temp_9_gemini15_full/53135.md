diplomacies: /dɪˈploʊməsiz/ | n. |外交（策略）；外交手腕；外交事务 | The art and practice of conducting negotiations between representatives of states. It involves skillful management of international relations, often aiming for peaceful resolutions and cooperation.  |  例句：The success of the peace talks depended heavily on the skillful diplomacies of the mediators. 和谈的成功很大程度上取决于调解人的巧妙外交。


例句：The country's recent diplomacies have improved its international standing. 该国最近的外交策略提升了其国际地位。


近义词：negotiations,  international relations, statecraft,  foreign policy


反义词：warfare, conflict, antagonism


词性: 名词
