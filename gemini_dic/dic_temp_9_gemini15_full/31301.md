词源：
From chaplain + -ship, a suffix forming nouns of state, rank, or occupation.  "Chaplain" itself derives from Old French *chapeleyn, from chapele "chapel," ultimately from Latin capella "chapel."  The suffix "-ship" indicates the state or condition of being a chaplain.
