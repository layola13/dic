shampooing:/ʃæmˈpuːɪŋ/| n. v. |洗发；洗头|The act of washing hair with shampoo.  This word can function as a noun referring to the act of shampooing or as a gerund (verb acting as a noun).|例句：She enjoys the relaxing feeling of shampooing her hair. 她喜欢洗头的放松感觉。

The shampooing left her hair feeling soft and clean. 洗发后她的头发感觉柔软干净。|近义词：hair washing, cleansing (hair)|反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to neglecting hair care.
