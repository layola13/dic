precopies:/ˈpriːkɒpiz/| n.|预印本；预先复制的副本|Precopies are copies made in advance of a final version.  预印本是在最终版本之前制作的副本。|例句：The precopies of the manuscript were distributed to the reviewers. 预印本手稿分发给了审稿人。|近义词：advance copies, preliminary copies|反义词：final copies

He usually prepares precopies for his presentations. = 他通常为他演讲准备预印本。

They made precopies of the report last night. = 他们昨晚制作了报告的预印本。
She distributed precopies to the team members before the meeting. = 她在会议前向团队成员分发了预印本。
