fees:/fiːz/| n. |费用，酬金|Money charged for a service or privilege.|The university fees are quite high this year.  今年大学的学费相当高。|The lawyer's fees were exorbitant.  律师的费用过高。|charges, costs, dues|free, complimentary


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The school charges high fees. = 这所学校收取高额学费。
What fees do you charge for this service? = 你这项服务的费用是多少？


一般过去时:
They paid the fees on time. = 他们按时支付了费用。
We paid hefty fees for the concert tickets. = 我们为演唱会门票支付了高额费用。


现在完成时:
I have already paid all my fees. = 我已经支付了所有的费用。
She has paid her fees in full. = 她已全额支付了费用。


过去完成时:
He had already paid the fees before the deadline. = 他在截止日期前已经支付了费用。
They had paid their fees before the registration closed. = 他们在注册截止前已经支付了费用。


将来时:
I will pay the fees next week. = 我下周将支付费用。
They will pay the fees by the end of the month. = 他们将在月底前支付费用。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would pay the fees without hesitation. = 如果我有更多钱，我会毫不犹豫地支付费用。
I wish I had paid the fees earlier. = 我真希望我早点付了费用。


被动语态:
The fees are paid annually. = 费用每年支付。
The fees were increased last year. = 费用去年上涨了。


疑问句:
What are the fees for this course? = 这门课程的费用是多少？
How much are the fees? = 费用是多少？


否定句:

I haven't paid the fees yet. = 我还没付钱。
