engage:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/ | vt. & vi. | 吸引；占用；从事；雇佣；使订婚 | To occupy, attract, or involve someone's attention or interest; to participate or become involved in something; to hire or employ someone; to become engaged to be married. |

例句：
The game engaged the children's attention for hours.  = 这游戏吸引了孩子们几个小时的注意力。

The company engaged a new marketing consultant. = 公司聘请了一位新的市场顾问。


近义词：occupy, involve, attract, employ, hire,  enlist,  fascinate

反义词：disengage, detach, release, ignore, neglect


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She engages in many extracurricular activities. = 她参加许多课外活动。
He engages his audience with compelling stories. = 他用引人入胜的故事吸引听众。

一般过去时:
They engaged a lawyer to handle the case. = 他们聘请了一位律师来处理此案。
I engaged myself in a challenging project. = 我投入了一个具有挑战性的项目。

现在完成时:
We have engaged a professional photographer for the wedding. = 我们已经为婚礼聘请了一位专业摄影师。
She has engaged herself in philanthropy for many years. = 她多年来一直从事慈善事业。

过去完成时:
They had engaged a caterer before the party. = 他们宴会前已经聘请了一位餐饮承办商。
He had engaged himself in the debate for weeks. = 他已经参与辩论几周了。

将来时:
We will engage a cleaning service next week. = 我们下周将聘请清洁服务。
They will engage in a friendly competition. = 他们将进行一场友好的比赛。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would engage in more volunteer work. = 如果我有更多时间，我会从事更多的志愿者工作。
I wish I could engage with the topic more effectively. = 我希望我能更有效地参与这个话题。

被动语态:
A new marketing strategy was engaged by the company. = 公司采用了新的营销策略。(被动语态中engage不太常用)
The audience was fully engaged by the speaker's presentation. = 听众被演讲者的陈述深深吸引。(被动语态中engage不太常用，更常用的是be engaged by)

疑问句:
Does this activity engage your interest? = 这项活动能引起你的兴趣吗？
Will you engage in the discussion? = 你会参与讨论吗？

否定句:
He didn't engage with the criticism. = 他没有回应批评。
The machine failed to engage properly. = 机器未能正确啮合。


PHRASE->
engage in: 从事；参与
engage with: 与……互动；参与……
be engaged in: 忙于；从事于
engaged to: 与……订婚
engage the enemy: 与敌人交战
