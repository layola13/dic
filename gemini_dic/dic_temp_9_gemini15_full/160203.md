ponding:/ˈpɒndɪŋ/ | /ˈpɑːndɪŋ/ | n. |积水，水浸 |The accumulation of water on a surface, typically due to inadequate drainage.  It often refers to standing water on the ground. |例句：The heavy rain caused extensive ponding on the roads. 大雨导致道路上积水严重。|例句：Poor drainage led to ponding in the basement after the storm. 暴雨过后，排水不良导致地下室积水。|近义词：waterlogging, flooding (in more severe cases) |反义词：drainage, dryness
