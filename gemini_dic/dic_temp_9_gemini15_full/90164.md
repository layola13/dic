hothousing: /ˈhɒthaʊzɪŋ/ | /ˈhɑːthaʊzɪŋ/ | n. | 加速培养；强化训练；温室栽培 | The rapid and intensive cultivation or development of something, often involving artificial means.  It often implies an unnatural or forced acceleration of growth.  | 例句：The company's hothousing of young talent led to some impressive results, but also a high burnout rate. 公司对年轻人才的强化培养取得了一些令人瞩目的成果，但也导致了高流失率。

The government's hothousing of the economy resulted in unsustainable growth. 政府对经济的加速发展导致了不可持续的增长。


近义词：intensive cultivation, forced growth, rapid development, acceleration, fast-tracking | 反义词：slow growth, natural development, gradual development |  名词
