pay-for:/peɪ fɔːr/| v.|付费，支付 |To give money in exchange for goods or services.  |例句：I paid for the groceries with my credit card. 我用信用卡支付了杂货费用。|例句： We need to pay for the damages. 我们需要赔偿损失。|近义词：purchase, settle, defray|反义词：receive, get


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They pay for their groceries weekly. = 他们每周支付杂货费用。
We pay for the service in advance. = 我们预付服务费用。


一般过去时:
She paid for the dinner last night. = 她昨晚付了晚餐的钱。
He paid for the taxi ride home. = 他付了回家的出租车费。


现在完成时:
I have paid for the flight tickets already. = 我已经支付了机票费用。
They have paid for the repairs. = 他们已经支付了修理费。


过去完成时:
They had paid for the hotel before they arrived. = 他们在到达之前已经支付了酒店费用。
She had paid for the course months ago. = 她几个月前就支付了课程费用。


将来时:
I will pay for the lunch tomorrow. = 我明天付午餐的钱。
He will pay for the new car next month. = 他下个月将支付新车的费用。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would pay for the concert tickets. = 如果我有更多钱，我会买演唱会门票。
I wish I could pay for a trip to Europe. = 我希望我能支付去欧洲旅行的费用。


被动语态:
The bills were paid for by the company. = 账单由公司支付。
The damages will be paid for by the insurance. = 损失将由保险公司支付。


疑问句:
How much did you pay for that dress? = 你那件衣服花了多少钱？
Will you pay for the drinks? = 你会付酒钱吗？


否定句:
I didn't pay for the parking. = 我没有支付停车费。
He hasn't paid for the subscription yet. = 他还没有支付订阅费用。


PHRASE->
pay for something in cash = 用现金支付
pay for something in installments = 分期付款
pay for something upfront = 预付费用
pay for something online = 在线支付
pay for something with a credit card = 用信用卡支付
