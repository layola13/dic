hyperventilates:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɛntɪleɪts/| v.|过度换气，呼吸急促|To breathe very rapidly and deeply. This often happens during periods of stress, anxiety, or panic. It can also be a symptom of certain medical conditions.|例句：She hyperventilated after hearing the bad news. 她听到坏消息后呼吸急促。|例句：The patient hyperventilated during the surgery. 病人在手术过程中过度换气。|近义词：overbreathe, pant, gasp|反义词：hypoventilate
