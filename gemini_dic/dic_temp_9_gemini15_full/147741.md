overproduced:/ˌoʊvərprəˈduːst/| adj.|生产过剩的，产量过多的| Excessively produced; exceeding demand or the capacity to consume or utilize.  It implies a surplus of goods or services, often leading to economic inefficiencies or waste.|例句：The market is currently overproduced with cheap smartphones. = 目前市场上廉价智能手机生产过剩。

The factory overproduced widgets last month, leading to significant storage costs. = 上个月工厂生产的部件过多，导致了大量的仓储成本。


近义词：surplus, excessive, abundant, overflowing

反义词：underproduced, scarce, deficient, insufficient


词性:形容词
