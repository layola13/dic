maxima:/ˈmæksɪmə/| n.|极大值；最大量；最大值点|The highest value or point reached by a function or curve.|The maxima of the function are at x = 1 and x = 5.  该函数的极大值出现在x = 1和x = 5处。|近义词：maximum, peak, apex|反义词：minima


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, typical verb tenses are not applicable.  However, we can show how it's used in different sentence structures.

单数句:
The maxima of the curve is clearly visible. 曲线的极大值清晰可见。

复数句:
The maxima are located at the intersections of the lines. 极大值位于线的交点处。


疑问句:
What are the maxima of this data set? 这个数据集的极大值是多少？

否定句:
There are no clear maxima in this graph.  这个图表中没有明显的极大值。


比较句:
The maxima of this function are greater than those of the previous one. 这个函数的极大值大于前一个函数的极大值。

强调句:
It is the maxima that determine the overall trend.  正是极大值决定了整体趋势。
