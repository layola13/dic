quasi-municipally:/ˈkwɑːziˌmjuːnɪsɪpəli/| adv.|准市政地，类似市政地 |  Describes something done or organized in a way that resembles municipal administration, but without the full legal status or powers of a municipality.  It suggests a partial or incomplete form of municipal governance. | The town was governed quasi-municipally for many years before achieving full municipal status.  这个镇在获得完全的市政地位之前，多年来一直实行准市政管理。  | near-municipally, semi-municipally |  fully municipally  | 副词
