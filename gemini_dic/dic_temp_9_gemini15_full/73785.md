formalizers:/ˈfɔːrməlaɪzərz/ | n. | 正式化者；形式化者 |Those who establish or enforce formal rules or systems.  People or things that give something a formal structure or character. |Example: The committee acted as formalizers of the new regulations, ensuring clarity and consistency.  委员会充当了新规章的正式制定者，确保其清晰性和一致性。|近义词：regulators, systematizers, codifiers |反义词：informalizers, casualizers
