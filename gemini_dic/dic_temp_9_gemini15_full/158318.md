playlet:/ˈpleɪlɪt/| n.|小戏剧，短剧|A short play, especially one performed by amateurs or as part of a longer program.  It's often less complex than a full-length play.|例句：The school children put on a charming playlet for their parents. 学校的孩子们为他们的父母上演了一出迷人的小戏剧。

例句：The theatre group performed a series of playlets based on local folklore.  剧团上演了一系列以当地民间传说为基础的短剧。


近义词：short play, skit, dramatic sketch|反义词：full-length play, opera, epic|词性:名词
