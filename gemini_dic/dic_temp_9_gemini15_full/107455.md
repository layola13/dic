lampost:/ˈlæmpˌpoʊst/| n. |路灯|A tall post, usually at the side of a road or path, that holds a lamp to light the way at night.  A lamppost is a vertical structure designed to support and house a light source for illuminating streets, roads, or pathways.|Example:  The old lamppost cast a long shadow on the pavement. 这盏旧路灯在人行道上投下长长的影子。
The children played hide-and-seek around the lamppost. 孩子们在路灯周围玩捉迷藏。|近义词：streetlight, lamp standard|反义词：None.
