immerser: /ɪˈmɜːrsər/ | n. | 浸渍器；沉浸者 | A device used for immersing something in a liquid, or a person who immerses themselves in an activity or experience.  | Example: The baker used an immerser to coat the pastries in chocolate.  面包师用浸渍器将糕点裹上巧克力。 The artist was a complete immerser in his work, spending hours lost in his creative process.这位艺术家完全沉浸在自己的工作中，花费数小时沉浸在创作过程中。| 近义词：dipper, submerger | 反义词：emerger, extractor |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He immerses the fabric in dye. = 他将织物浸入染料中。
She immerses herself in her studies. = 她专心致志地学习。


一般过去时:
They immersed the vegetables in boiling water. = 他们把蔬菜浸在沸水中。
He immersed himself in the book. = 他专心致志地看书。


现在完成时:
She has immersed herself in the project for weeks. = 她已经投入这个项目几周了。
They have immersed the components in the cleaning solution. = 他们已将组件浸入清洁溶液中。


过去完成时:
He had immersed himself in his work before the deadline. = 截止日期前，他已经全身心地投入工作了。
She had immersed the cloth in the dye for several hours. = 她已经将布料浸在染料中几个小时了。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
