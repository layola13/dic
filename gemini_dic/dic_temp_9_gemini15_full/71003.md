fireworm:/ˈfaɪərwɜːrm/| n. |火虫,  环节蠕虫|A fireworm is a type of marine polychaete worm that has bristles containing toxins that can cause a painful sting.  They are often brightly colored to warn predators. |例句：The diver accidentally touched a fireworm and experienced a burning sensation.  潜水员不小心碰到了火虫，感到灼热感。

例句：Several fireworms were observed crawling on the seabed.  观察到几条火虫在海底爬行。


近义词：None (no direct synonym, but other stinging marine creatures could be considered functionally similar)
反义词：None
