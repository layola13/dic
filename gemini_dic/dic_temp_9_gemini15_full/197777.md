shimmies:/ˈʃɪmiz/ | v. |颤抖，抖动；(非正式)扭动，扭动舞步 | To shake slightly and uncontrollably; to move one's body in a rhythmic way, especially in a suggestive or sensual manner. |例句：The leaves shimmied in the breeze.  树叶在微风中轻轻摇曳。

The dancer shimmied across the stage. 舞者在舞台上扭动着舞步。


近义词：tremble, quiver, shake, wiggle, jiggle | 反义词：remain still, stand rigid
