scratches:/ˈskrætʃɪz/| n. v.|抓痕；刮擦声；抓挠|The act of scratching or the marks left by scratching;  a rough or harsh sound.|例句：The cat left scratches on the furniture. 猫在家具上留下了抓痕。  The old record produced a series of scratches. 那张旧唱片发出了一连串的刮擦声。|近义词：marks, abrasions, scrapes, scratches|反义词：smoothness, polishing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scratches his head when he's confused. = 他困惑时会抓头。
The dog scratches at the door to be let in. = 狗抓门想进来。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:

将来时:
