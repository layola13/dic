It encompasses a wide range of religious practices and beliefs, varying greatly across cultures and time periods.  |Example: Ancient Greece was characterized by its vibrant polytheisms, with a pantheon of gods and goddesses representing various aspects of life. 古希腊以其充满活力的多神教为特征，众神和女神代表着生活的各个方面。|近义词：polytheism (polytheism is the singular form, polytheisms refers to multiple instances or systems of polytheism)|反义词：monotheism, atheism
