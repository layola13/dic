chemises:/ʃəˈmiːz/ | n. | 衬裙；女衬衫；睡衣 | A chemise is a woman's undergarment, typically a loose-fitting garment worn next to the skin.  It can also refer to a simple, loose-fitting shirt or dress, often made of soft material. | Example: She wore a simple cotton chemise under her dress. 她穿着一件简单的棉质衬裙在连衣裙里面。  |近义词：undergarment, slip, blouse, nightshirt |反义词：outerwear, overcoat
