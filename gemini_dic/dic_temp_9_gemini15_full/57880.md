driblet:/ˈdrɪblɪt/| n.|少量；涓涓细流；少量支付|A driblet is a small amount of something, especially liquid, that comes out slowly or in small quantities.  It can also refer to a small sum of money paid at a time.|例句：The water trickled out in a driblet. 水一滴一滴地流出来。

The company paid its employees their salaries in driblets. 公司分期少量支付员工工资。|近义词：trickle, drop, trickle, installment |反义词：flood, torrent, deluge
