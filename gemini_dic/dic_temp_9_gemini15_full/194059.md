self-hatred: /ˈsɛlfˈheɪtrɪd/ | n. | 自我厌恶 |  A feeling of intense dislike or disgust towards oneself. This can manifest in various ways, including negative self-talk, self-destructive behaviors, and a general lack of self-worth.  It's often rooted in deeper psychological issues and can be a symptom of depression, anxiety, or other mental health conditions. |

例句：Her self-hatred stemmed from years of bullying and criticism.  她的自我厌恶源于多年的欺凌和批评。

Her self-hatred manifested as self-harm.  她的自我厌恶表现为自残。


近义词：self-loathing, self-contempt, self-deprecation

反义词：self-love, self-acceptance, self-esteem
