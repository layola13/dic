enticed:/ɪnˈtaɪst/| vt.|引诱；怂恿；吸引|To persuade someone to do something by offering them something attractive.  It implies a subtle or indirect persuasion, often appealing to desires or emotions.|例句：The delicious smell of freshly baked bread enticed me into the bakery.  香喷喷的新鲜面包味引诱我走进了面包店。

The promise of a promotion enticed her to work extra hours.  晋升的承诺引诱她加班。


近义词：tempted, lured, attracted, charmed, allured|反义词：repulsed, discouraged, deterred|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sweet aroma entices customers into the shop. = 甜美的香味吸引顾客进入商店。
He entices his children with promises of candy. = 他用糖果的承诺来引诱他的孩子。


一般过去时:
The salesman enticed the customer into buying the expensive watch. = 销售员诱使顾客购买了昂贵的腕表。
The beautiful scenery enticed us to stay longer. = 美丽的景色吸引我们多待了一会儿。


现在完成时:
The marketing campaign has enticed many new customers. = 这场营销活动吸引了许多新顾客。
They have been enticed by the prospect of higher salaries. = 他们被更高的薪水前景所吸引。


过去完成时:
He had been enticed by the promise of riches. = 他曾被致富的承诺所吸引。
The advertisement had enticed many people to apply for the job. = 广告吸引了许多人申请这份工作。


将来时:
The new product will entice consumers with its innovative features. = 新产品将以其创新的功能吸引消费者。
I believe that this offer will entice many people to participate. = 我相信这个优惠将吸引许多人参与。


虚拟语气:
If the offer had been more enticing, I would have accepted it. = 如果提议更诱人，我就接受了。
I wish the prize had been more enticing. = 我希望奖品更诱人。


被动语态:
Many people were enticed into buying the product by the clever advertisement. = 许多人被巧妙的广告诱导购买了该产品。
She was enticed by the opportunity to travel abroad. = 她被出国旅行的机会所吸引。


疑问句:
What enticed you to apply for this job? = 什么吸引你申请这份工作？
What enticed him to leave his job? = 什么诱使他离开他的工作？


否定句:
Nothing enticed him to leave his comfortable home. = 没有什么能够诱使他离开他舒适的家。
The low salary didn't entice her to accept the position. = 低薪没有诱使她接受这个职位。
