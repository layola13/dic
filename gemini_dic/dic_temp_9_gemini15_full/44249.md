croft:/krɒft/| n.|小农场，小耕地|A small farm, especially one with a house and buildings attached, typically in Scotland and northern England.|例句：They lived on a small croft in the Highlands.  他们住在高地上的一个小农场里。|例句：The croft produced enough food for the family. 这个小农场生产的食物足够一家人的食用。|近义词：farmlet, smallholding|反义词：large estate, plantation
