intonement:/ɪnˈtoʊnmənt/ | n. | 吟诵；调子；语气；调谐 | The act of intoning; the manner or style of intonation.  It refers to the musical tone, pitch, and rhythm used in speaking or singing, often with a particular emphasis or feeling.  It can also refer to the act of tuning or adjusting something to the proper pitch. |例句：The priest's intonement of the prayer was deeply moving.  牧师吟诵祈祷的语气深深地打动了人们。 The intonement of the choir was perfectly harmonious. 合唱队的音调十分和谐。|近义词：chanting, recitation, modulation, tuning |反义词：mumbling, whispering
