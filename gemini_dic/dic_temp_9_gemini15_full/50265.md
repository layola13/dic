depersonalized:/ˌdiːˈpɜːrsənəlaɪzd/| adj.| 去个性化的；非人格化的；冷漠的 |  Describes something that has lost its personal qualities or characteristics, often feeling impersonal, detached, or lacking individuality. It can refer to a person's feelings, experiences, or even a system or environment. | 例句：The factory work left him feeling depersonalized and alienated. 工厂的工作让他感到去个性化和疏离。 The highly structured environment of the hospital felt depersonalized to the patients. 医院高度结构化的环境让病人感到冷漠。| 近义词：impersonal, detached, alienated, dehumanized, objective | 反义词：personalized, individual, intimate, empathetic, human
