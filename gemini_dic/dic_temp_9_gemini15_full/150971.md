parameterization:/ˌpəræmɪtəraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 参数化；参数设定 | The process of expressing something (such as a curve or surface) in terms of parameters.  It involves representing a system or object using parameters, which are independent variables that define its characteristics. For example, a circle can be parameterized using its radius and angle.  | Example: The parameterization of the curve allowed for easy calculation of its length.  参数化曲线使得计算其长度变得容易。 |近义词：parametrization, parameter setting | 反义词：N/A
