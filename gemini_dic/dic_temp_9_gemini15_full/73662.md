forgeman:/ˈfɔːrdʒmən/| n. |工头，锻工领班|A foreman is a skilled worker who supervises other workers.  They are responsible for overseeing the work of a team and ensuring that it is completed efficiently and safely.  In some contexts, it specifically refers to a leader of a group of blacksmiths.|例句：The foreman instructed the workers to take a break. 工头指示工人休息一下。

The foreman checked the quality of the welds. 工头检查了焊接的质量。|近义词：supervisor, supervisor, overseer|反义词：worker, subordinate
