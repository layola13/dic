/haɪˈiːnə/ | n. |鬣狗 |A hyena is a carnivorous mammal of the family Hyaenidae, characterized by its powerful jaws and distinctive laugh-like vocalizations. They are known for their scavenging habits and are often found in Africa and Asia.  |Example: The hyena scavenged the remains of the zebra.  鬣狗啃食斑马的残骸。|近义词:  jackal, wild dog |反义词:  N/A
