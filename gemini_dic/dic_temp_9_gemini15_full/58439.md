duckier:/ˈdʌkiər/| adj.|更像鸭子的；更可爱的|More like a duck; more cute or charming.  This word is a comparative form, implying a greater degree of the qualities associated with "ducky".|例句：The duckier of the two ducklings was the one with the fluffiest feathers.  两只小鸭子中，羽毛最蓬松的那一只更像鸭子。|近义词：cuter, sweeter, more adorable|反义词：less cute, less charming
