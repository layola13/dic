seated:/ˈsiːtɪd/ | adj. | 坐着的，就座的 | seated describes someone or something that is in a sitting position.  It is often used as a past participle of the verb "to seat". | Example:  The audience was seated quietly before the concert began.  观众在音乐会开始前安静地坐好。The guests were already seated when I arrived. 我到达时客人们已经就座了。|近义词：sitting, ensconced | 反义词：standing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is seated at the table. 他坐在桌子旁。
They are seated in the front row. 他们坐在前排。


一般过去时:
She was seated next to him. 她坐在他旁边。
The students were seated alphabetically. 学生们按字母顺序就座。


现在完成时:
He has been seated for an hour. 他已经坐了一个小时了。
We have been seated since the play began. 从戏剧开始我们就一直坐着。


过去完成时:
They had been seated before the speaker arrived. 演讲者到达之前他们已经就座了。
She had been seated comfortably before the meeting started. 会议开始前她已经舒适地坐好了。


将来时:
They will be seated in the VIP section. 他们将坐在贵宾区。
We will be seated soon. 我们很快就会就座。


虚拟语气:
If I were seated closer, I could hear better. 如果我坐得近一点，就能听得更清楚。
I wish I were seated more comfortably. 我希望我能坐得更舒服些。


被动语态:
The guests were seated by the ushers. 客人们由引座员安排座位。
The chairs were seated in a circle. 椅子被摆成一个圆圈。


疑问句:
Are you seated comfortably? 你坐得舒服吗？
Is everyone seated? 大家都坐好了吗？


否定句:
He was not seated. 他没有坐着。
They were not yet seated. 他们还没坐好。


PHRASE->
be seated 就座
remain seated 保持坐姿
seated area 就坐区
seated position 坐姿
seated capacity 座位容量
