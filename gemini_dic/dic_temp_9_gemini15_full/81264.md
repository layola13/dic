granitoid:/ˈɡrænaɪtɔɪd/| n. |花岗岩状岩石|A granitoid is an intrusive igneous rock that is similar in appearance and composition to granite.  Granitoids are typically light-colored and coarse-grained, and they are composed primarily of quartz, feldspar, and mica.
