preconferred:/ˌpriːkənˈfɜːrd/| adj.| 预先授予的，预先给予的 | Previously granted or conferred; given in advance.  | The preconferred award was a surprise to the recipient. 预先授予的奖项让获奖者感到意外。 |近义词：predetermined, prearranged, prior |反义词：postponed, delayed


USAGE->

The committee preconfers awards to deserving students. 委员会预先授予奖项给应得的学生。
He preconfers his blessing on the marriage. 他预先祝福这桩婚事。
