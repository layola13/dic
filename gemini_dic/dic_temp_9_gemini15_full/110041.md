licensed:/ˈlaɪsnsd/ | adj. | 已获许可的；有执照的 | Having received a license or authorization.  |例句：The software is licensed for use on only one computer.  该软件只允许在一台电脑上使用。 |例句：He's a licensed driver. 他持有驾驶执照。 |近义词：authorized, permitted, accredited |反义词：unauthorized, unlicensed, prohibited


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is licensed to practice law. = 他有资格执业律师。
The software is licensed for commercial use. = 该软件已获商业用途许可。


一般过去时:
The building was licensed last year. = 这栋建筑物去年获得了许可证。
She was licensed to drive at 16. = 她16岁时获得了驾照。


现在完成时:
They have been licensed to operate in this region. = 他们已获得在该地区运营的许可。
The company has licensed its technology to several firms. = 这家公司已将其技术授权给几家公司。


过去完成时:
The project had been licensed before the funding was secured. = 在资金到位之前，该项目已经获得了许可。
The drug had been licensed for several years before the side effects were discovered. = 该药物在发现副作用之前已经获准上市数年。


将来时:
The product will be licensed to international distributors. = 该产品将授权给国际经销商。
We will be licensed to sell this product in the new market. = 我们将获得在新的市场销售该产品的许可。


虚拟语气:
If the product were licensed, we could start production. = 如果产品获得许可，我们就可以开始生产。
I wish the software were licensed for multiple users. = 我希望该软件能被授权给多个用户。


被动语态:
The patent was licensed to a larger corporation. = 该专利被授权给一家更大的公司。
The technology is being licensed to several companies worldwide. = 该技术正在被授权给全球多家公司。


疑问句:
Is the software licensed for personal use? = 该软件是否允许个人使用？
Are you licensed to drive a truck? = 你有驾驶卡车的执照吗？


否定句:
The driver was not licensed. = 该司机没有驾照。
The restaurant is not licensed to sell alcohol. = 该餐厅无权销售酒精饮料。


PHRASE->

Licensed professional = 持证专业人士
Licensed copy = 正版副本
Licensed driver = 持照驾驶员
