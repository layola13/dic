preconveying:/ˌpriːkənˈveɪɪŋ/ | v. |预先传送，预先告知 | To convey or communicate something beforehand; to transmit or impart information in advance.  |例句：The company preconveyed its plans for expansion to its shareholders before the official announcement. 公司在正式宣布之前就已向股东预先告知了其扩张计划。


例句：The professor preconveyed the main points of the lecture in the introduction. 教授在引言中预先传达了讲座的主要内容。


近义词：pre-notify, forewarn, pre-inform,  give prior notice


反义词：conceal, withhold, suppress


词性：动词
