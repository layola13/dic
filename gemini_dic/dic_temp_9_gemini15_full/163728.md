predefense:/ˌpriːdɪˈfɛns/| n. |预答辩，预先答辩 | A predefense is a practice run of a dissertation defense, typically held before the actual defense. It allows the candidate to receive feedback and refine their presentation and research before the official event.  | Example:  The student gave a predefense of their dissertation to their advisor and committee members.  学生向导师和委员会成员进行了论文预答辩。|近义词：rehearsal, practice run |反义词：defense (final)
