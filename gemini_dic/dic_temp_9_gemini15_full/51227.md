destroy: /dɪˈstrɔɪ/ | vt. | 破坏；毁坏；消灭 | To damage something so badly that it no longer exists, works, or can be repaired.  |例句：The earthquake destroyed many buildings. 地震摧毁了许多建筑物。

例句：The fire destroyed the entire forest. 大火烧毁了整片森林。


近义词：ruin, demolish, devastate, wreck, annihilate | 反义词：create, build, construct, preserve, save | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The storm destroys coastal homes every year.  暴风雨每年都会摧毁沿海的房屋。
He destroys all evidence of his crimes. 他销毁了他犯罪的所有证据。


一般过去时:
The army destroyed the enemy's base.  军队摧毁了敌人的基地。
The hurricane destroyed the city's infrastructure.  飓风摧毁了城市的公共设施。


现在完成时:
The war has destroyed countless lives.  战争已经夺去了无数人的生命。
They have destroyed the old building to make way for a new one. 他们已经拆除了旧建筑为新建筑让路。


过去完成时:
The fire had destroyed the entire town before firefighters arrived.  消防员到达之前，大火已经摧毁了整个城镇。
They had destroyed the evidence before the police could find it.  他们在警察找到证据之前就销毁了它。


将来时:
The flood will destroy the crops.  洪水将会毁掉庄稼。
The new policy may destroy the small businesses. 新政策可能会摧毁小型企业。


虚拟语气:
If we continue polluting the environment, we will destroy the planet. 如果我们继续污染环境，我们将毁灭地球。
I wish they hadn't destroyed that beautiful old house. 我真希望他们没有毁掉那座美丽的旧房子。


被动语态:
The city was destroyed by the bombing.  这座城市被轰炸摧毁了。
Many homes were destroyed by the tornado.  许多房屋被龙卷风摧毁了。


疑问句:
What destroyed the bridge?  是什么摧毁了桥梁？
Will the project destroy the natural habitat?  这个项目会破坏自然栖息地吗？


否定句:
The fire did not destroy the entire building.  大火并没有摧毁整栋建筑物。
We will not destroy the forest. 我们不会破坏森林。


PHRASE->
destroy completely: 完全摧毁
destroy oneself: 自毁
destroy the enemy: 摧毁敌人
destroy evidence: 销毁证据
destroy a reputation: 毁坏名誉

词源:
The word "destroy" comes from the Old French word "destruire," which in turn comes from the Latin word "destruere."  "Des-" is a prefix meaning "to undo" or "away," and "-struere" means "to build."  Therefore, the literal meaning is "to unbuild" or "to pull down."
