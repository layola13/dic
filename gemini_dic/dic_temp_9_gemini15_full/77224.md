gastritis:/ɡæsˈtraɪtɪs/| n. |胃炎|Inflammation of the lining of the stomach.  Gastritis can be caused by various factors, including infection (e.g., Helicobacter pylori), excessive alcohol consumption, prolonged use of nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), and autoimmune disorders. Symptoms can range from mild discomfort to severe pain, nausea, vomiting, and bleeding.  |例句：He was diagnosed with gastritis after experiencing persistent stomach pain. 他在经历持续的胃痛后被诊断出患有胃炎。

例句：The doctor prescribed medication to reduce the inflammation caused by her gastritis. 医生开了药来减轻她胃炎引起的炎症。


近义词：stomach inflammation, gastric inflammation |反义词：N/A
