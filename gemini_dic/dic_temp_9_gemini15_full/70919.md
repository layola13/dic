fireboard:/ˈfaɪərˌbɔːrd/ | n. |防火板，炉面板 |A fireboard is a decorative board, often made of fire-resistant material, placed in front of a fireplace to protect the wall from heat and sparks.  It can also be a type of insulating board used in construction. |Example: The fireboard prevented the wall from being damaged by the fireplace's heat.  防火板防止了壁炉的热量损坏墙壁。  The old house used fireboards in the walls for insulation. 老房子在墙体中使用防火板进行隔热。|近义词：fire screen, hearth board |反义词：None specifically, but a material that is not fire-resistant would be the opposite.  |名词
