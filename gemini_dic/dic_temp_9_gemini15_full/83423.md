habilitate:/həˈbɪlɪteɪt/| vt.|使康复；使胜任；使有资格|To restore someone to health or normal life through training and therapy; to qualify or entitle someone.|The therapist worked hard to rehabilitate the stroke victim.  治疗师努力使中风患者康复。|The program aims to rehabilitate offenders and help them reintegrate into society. 该计划旨在使罪犯改过自新并帮助他们重新融入社会。|rehabilitate, restore, recover|disable, incapacitate, cripple|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The therapist rehabilitates patients every day. = 治疗师每天都让病人康复。
The program rehabilitates offenders. = 该项目使罪犯改过自新。

一般过去时:
