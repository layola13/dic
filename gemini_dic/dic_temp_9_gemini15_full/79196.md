glance-off:/ɡlæns ˈɔːf/ | v. | 擦过；掠过；轻触后反弹 | To strike a glancing blow against; to graze.  It implies a brief, light contact, often with a change in direction. | 例句：The ball glanced off the wall and into the street.  球擦着墙反弹到街上去了。

The bullet glanced off his shoulder. 子弹擦过他的肩膀。


近义词： graze, skim, brush, ricochet | 反义词：collide, crash, smash


词性：动词
