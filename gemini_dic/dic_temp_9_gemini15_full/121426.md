misinformer:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrmər/ | n. | 误导者，传播虚假信息者 | A person who deliberately spreads false or inaccurate information.  | Example: The misinformer spread rumors that caused significant damage to the company's reputation.  这个误导者散布谣言，严重损害了公司的声誉。 |近义词：liar, deceiver, fraud, propagandist | 反义词：truth-teller, informant (if providing accurate information)
