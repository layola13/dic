chideress:/ˈtʃaɪdəres/| n. |训斥者，责骂者（尤指女性）|A chideress is a woman who frequently scolds or reprimands others.  The term is archaic and rarely used in modern English. It emphasizes the female aspect of a scolding person. |Example: The old chideress constantly berated the children for their minor mishaps. 老妇人不断地训斥孩子们的小过失。|近义词：scolder, shrew, nag|反义词：comforter, encourager|名词
