renewal: /rɪˈnuːəl/ | n. | 更新；续订；复兴 | The act of renewing or the state of being renewed;  a return to a former state; restoration.  It can refer to the act of making something new again, extending something's validity, or the revival of something. | 例句：The renewal of their marriage vows was a deeply moving ceremony.  他们的结婚誓言的重申是一个非常感人的仪式。

The building's renewal involved extensive renovations. 这座建筑的翻新包括大规模的翻修工程。


近义词：renovation, restoration, revival, extension, resumption | 反义词：termination, cancellation, end, expiry
