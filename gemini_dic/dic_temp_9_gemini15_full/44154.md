criticaster:/ˈkrɪtɪkæstər/| n.|批评家（尤指好批评坏批评的无能之辈）|A criticaster is someone who offers criticism, especially in a petty, superficial, or incompetent manner. They often focus on minor flaws rather than the overall merit of a work.  They are often seen as less qualified or insightful than true critics.|例句：He's not a real critic, just a criticaster who finds fault with everything.|近义词：nitpicker, fault-finder, carper|反义词：expert, connoisseur
