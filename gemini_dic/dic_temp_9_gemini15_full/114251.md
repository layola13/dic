mails:/meɪlz/| n.|邮件，邮递|Letters or other documents sent by post.|I received several mails this morning.  今天早上我收到了几封邮件。|He sent the mails by express post. 他通过快递邮寄了邮件。|post, correspondence|hand delivery, personal delivery|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mails his letters every week. 他每周都邮寄信件。
She mails the package to her friend. 她把包裹寄给她朋友。


一般过去时:
They mailed the package yesterday. 他们昨天寄了包裹。
He mailed the letter last week. 他上周寄了信。


现在完成时:
I have mailed the documents. 我已经邮寄了文件。
She has mailed the package to her sister. 她已经把包裹寄给了她姐姐。


过去完成时:
He had mailed the letter before he left. 他离开之前已经寄了信。
They had mailed the package before the deadline. 他们在截止日期之前已经寄了包裹。


将来时:
I will mail the letter tomorrow. 我明天会寄信。
She will mail the package next week. 她下周会寄包裹。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would mail the letter today. 如果我有时间，我今天就会寄信。
I wish I had mailed the package earlier. 我真希望我早点寄了包裹。


被动语态:
The package was mailed yesterday. 包裹是昨天寄的。
The letter is being mailed now. 信件正在邮寄中。


疑问句:
Did you mail the letter? 你寄信了吗？
Has she mailed the package yet? 她寄包裹了吗？


否定句:
I didn't mail the letter. 我没有寄信。
She hasn't mailed the package yet. 她还没寄包裹。
