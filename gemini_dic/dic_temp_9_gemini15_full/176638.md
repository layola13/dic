reaccumulate:/ˌriːəˈkjuːmjuleɪt/ | vt. |重新积累，再次累积 | To accumulate again; to gather or acquire again.  |例句：The company had to reaccumulate its lost market share after the scandal. 公司在丑闻之后不得不重新积累失去的市场份额。


例句：After the storm washed away the topsoil, farmers had to reaccumulate the fertile soil.  暴风雨冲走了表层土壤后，农民们不得不重新积累肥沃的土壤。


近义词：regather, rebuild, restore, amass again


反义词：dissipate, deplete, exhaust


词性：动词
