compatriotic:/ˌkæmˈpeɪtriɒtɪk/ | adj. | 同胞的；爱国的 | Showing a feeling of patriotism; having shared feelings of national pride and loyalty with one's fellow citizens.  |  例句：The compatriotic fervor was palpable during the national holiday celebrations.  国庆节庆祝活动期间，爱国热情非常明显。

例句：Their compatriotic spirit united them during the time of national crisis.  在国家危难之际，他们的爱国精神将他们团结在一起。


近义词：patriotic, nationalistic, loyal

反义词：unpatriotic, disloyal, treasonous

词性：形容词
