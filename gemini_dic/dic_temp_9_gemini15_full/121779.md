misreceive:/ˌmɪsrɪˈsiːv/| vt.|误收，误领，错误接收|To receive something incorrectly or unintentionally.  This often implies a misunderstanding or a failure to correctly interpret the intended message or item.|例句：The package was misreceived by the wrong department.  包裹被错误地送到了错误的部门。|例句：I misreceived your email and responded inappropriately. 我误收了你的邮件并做出了不恰当的回复。|近义词：misunderstand, misinterpret, mishandle|反义词：receive correctly, understand, correctly interpret|动词
