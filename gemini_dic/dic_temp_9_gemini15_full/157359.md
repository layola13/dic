piperidge:/ˈpaɪpərɪdʒ/| n.|山茱萸,  山楂树|A small, thorny shrub or tree, typically having red berries. It's often found in hedgerows and woodland edges.  The name is also sometimes used for other plants in the same family.|例句：The children gathered piperidge berries in the autumn. 秋天孩子们采摘山茱萸果实。|例句：The hedgerow was thick with piperidge bushes.  树篱上长满了山楂树。|近义词：hawthorn, crab apple|反义词:|无
