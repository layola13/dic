resumption:/rɪˈzʌmpʃən/ | n. | 重启；恢复；再继续 | The act of beginning something again after it has been interrupted.  It refers to the act of starting something again after a pause or interruption. |例句：The resumption of peace talks is a positive sign.  和平谈判的重启是一个积极的信号。

例句：After a long break, the resumption of the project is crucial to meeting the deadline.  在长时间中断后，项目的重启对于按时完成至关重要。


近义词：reinstatement, recommencement, revival, renewal | 反义词：suspension, interruption, cessation | 词性：名词
