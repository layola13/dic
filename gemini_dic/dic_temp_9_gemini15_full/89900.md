horrified:/ˈhɔːrɪfaɪd/|/ˈhɒrɪfaɪd/| adj.| 恐惧的；惊骇的；感到震惊的 | Feeling extreme fear or shock.  |The audience was horrified by the gruesome scene in the movie.  观众被电影中可怕的场景吓坏了。|  terrified, shocked, appalled | calm, pleased, delighted | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is horrified by the news. 他对这个消息感到震惊。
I am horrified by her behavior. 我对她行为感到恐惧。


一般过去时:
She was horrified to see the accident. 她看到事故后非常震惊。
They were horrified by the violence.  他们被暴力吓坏了。


现在完成时:
I have been horrified by the recent events. 我对最近发生的事件感到非常震惊。
We have been horrified by what we saw. 我们被我们所看到的东西吓坏了。


过去完成时:
He had been horrified by the previous day's events. 他对前一天发生的事情感到非常恐惧。
She had been horrified to learn the truth. 她得知真相后感到非常震惊。


将来时:
They will be horrified when they hear the news. 当他们听到这个消息时，他们会吓坏的。
I will be horrified if that happens. 如果那样发生，我会非常害怕。


虚拟语气:
I would be horrified if I saw something like that. 如果我看到那样的事情，我会吓坏的。
She would have been horrified if she had known. 如果她知道的话，她会吓坏的。


被动语态:
He was horrified by the sight. 他被眼前的景象吓坏了。(主动语态更常用)
They were horrified by the results. 他们被结果吓坏了。(主动语态更常用)


疑问句:
Are you horrified by the situation? 你对这种情况感到震惊吗？
Was he horrified by what he saw? 他被他所看到的吓坏了吗？


否定句:
I was not horrified by the movie. 我并没有被这部电影吓到。
He wasn't horrified by the news. 他并没有被这个消息吓到。
