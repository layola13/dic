jacking:/ˈdʒækɪŋ/| n. vt.|撬动；顶起；劫车；欺诈|The act of raising or lifting something with a jack; the act of forcibly taking control of a vehicle; a fraudulent act.|例句：The mechanic used a jack to lift the car.  机械师用千斤顶顶起了汽车。

例句：The thieves committed a daring car jacking in broad daylight. 窃贼在光天化日之下实施了大胆的劫车行为。


近义词：hoisting, lifting, hijacking, swindling|反义词：lowering, dropping


词源:源自古法语 *jacque,  最终源于拉丁语 *jaccus（"jack," a type of lifting device）
