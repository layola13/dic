prop's: /prɑːps/ | n. | 道具 | Props are objects used on a stage or in a film.  They are essential for creating the setting and atmosphere of a performance. | The set designer meticulously arranged the props for the play.  舞台设计师精心布置了戏剧的道具。  The film crew carefully placed the props to enhance the scene's realism. 电影摄制组小心地摆放道具以增强场景的真实感。 |  stage equipment, set pieces |  (无明显反义词，可考虑：absence of props) | 名词
