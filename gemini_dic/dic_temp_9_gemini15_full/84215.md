half-rotted:/ˈhæfˈrɑːtɪd/| adj. |半腐烂的，部分腐朽的 |Partially decayed or decomposed.  The adjective describes something that is only partly rotten, indicating a state between being completely fresh and completely decayed.|The half-rotted apple was still usable for applesauce.|近义词：partially decayed, partly decomposed, decaying |反义词：fresh, intact, undecomposed
