jalopies:/ˈdʒælapiːz/| n. |破旧汽车，老爷车 |A jalopy is an old car that is in bad condition and not worth much.  It often implies a car that is unreliable and prone to breakdowns. |例句：He drives a rusty jalopy that constantly needs repairs. 他开着一辆经常需要修理的生锈老爷车。

例句：That jalopy barely made it to the finish line. 那辆老爷车勉强到达了终点线。


近义词：clunker, wreck, bucket, beater

反义词：new car, luxury car


词性:名词
