by-passer:/ˈbaɪˌpæsər/| n. |旁观者，路过者 |A by-passer is someone who passes by a place or event without stopping.  They are not directly involved. |例句：A by-passer witnessed the accident.一位路人目睹了事故。  The by-passers paid little attention to the street performer. 路人们对街头表演者不太关注。|近义词：onlooker, observer, passerby |反义词：participant, involved party |名词
