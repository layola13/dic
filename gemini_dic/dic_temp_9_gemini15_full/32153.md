chewers:/ˈtʃuːərz/| n. |咀嚼者，嚼碎者|People or animals that chew.  The word refers to those who habitually or actively chew something, often implying a certain intensity or persistence in the action.  It can also refer to machines or tools designed for chewing or grinding materials.|例句：The cows were busy chewers in the pasture.  那些牛在牧场上忙着咀嚼。

例句：The machine is a powerful chewer of rocks. 这台机器是强大的碎石机。


近义词：masticators, grinders|反义词：swallowers (in the context of food)


词性:名词
