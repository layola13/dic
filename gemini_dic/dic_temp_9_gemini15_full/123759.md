moorhen:/ˈmʊrhen/| n. |水鸡|A small, dark-colored marsh bird with a red frontal shield and long toes, belonging to the rail family.  Moorhens are known for their striking appearance and their ability to swim and walk on aquatic vegetation.|例句：We saw a moorhen swimming gracefully among the reeds. 我们看到一只水鸡优雅地在芦苇丛中游泳。

例句：The moorhen chicks were hidden amongst the dense aquatic plants. 水鸡雏鸟藏在茂密的沉水植物中。


近义词：waterhen (less common)|反义词：N/A


词源：The name "moorhen" comes from the Old English "mōr" (moor, meaning a marshy area) and "hen" (referring to its chicken-like appearance).  The bird's habitat and its resemblance to a hen contributed to its naming.
