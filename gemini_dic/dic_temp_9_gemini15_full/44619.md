crowdsourcing:/ˈkraʊdsɔːrsɪŋ/| n. |众包，大众外包 |The process of obtaining needed services, ideas, or content by soliciting contributions from a large group of people, and especially from an online community, rather than from traditional employees or suppliers.  众包是一种获取所需服务、创意或内容的过程，它通过向一大群人（尤其是在线社区）征集贡献来实现，而不是从传统的员工或供应商那里获取。|Example: The company used crowdsourcing to design its new logo. 这家公司使用众包来设计它的新标志。


Example:  The website relies on crowdsourcing to generate its content.  这个网站依靠众包来生成它的内容。


近义词：outsourcing, collaborative creation, community sourcing


反义词：insourcing, centralized production


词性：名词
