drearihead:/ˈdrɪərihed/| n. |忧郁的人，愁眉苦脸的人 |A drearihead is someone who is habitually gloomy and pessimistic.  They often express negativity and lack enthusiasm.  The term is informal and somewhat dated. |例句：He's such a drearihead; he never has anything positive to say. 他真是个忧郁的人，他从来不说一句积极的话。

例句：Don't be such a drearihead!  Cheer up! 不要这么忧郁！振作起来！


近义词：gloom, pessimist, downer, killjoy |反义词：optimist, enthusiast

词性：名词
