buzz-saws:/ˈbʌzˌsɔːz/| n. | 电锯，嗡嗡作响的锯子 | A buzz saw is a type of saw that uses a rapidly rotating blade to cut through wood or other materials, producing a characteristic buzzing sound.  | Example: The carpenter used a buzz saw to cut the lumber. 木匠用电锯切割木材。 |近义词：circular saw, power saw |反义词：hand saw
