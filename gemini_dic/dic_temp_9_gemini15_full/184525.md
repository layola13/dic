restfullest:/ˈrɛstfʊləst/| adj.|最宁静的；最安宁的；最令人放松的|Most peaceful, tranquil, and relaxing.  It is the superlative form of "restful."|例句：The restfullest place I know is a quiet beach at sunset. 我知道的 最宁静 的地方是日落时分安静的海滩。 The restfullest moments of my day are spent reading before bed. 我一天中最放松的时刻是睡前阅读。|近义词：most peaceful, most tranquil, most relaxing, serenest|反义词：most stressful, most chaotic, most turbulent
