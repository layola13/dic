self-procured:/ˌsɛlf prəˈkjʊrd/| adj.| 自购的；自行获得的 | Obtained or acquired by oneself without outside assistance.  | 例句：He showed off his self-procured antique furniture. 他炫耀着他自购的古董家具。

例句：The self-procured evidence was enough to convict him.  自行获得的证据足以定他的罪。


近义词：self-acquired, independently obtained, self-supplied

反义词：provided, supplied, given


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company self-procures its office supplies. = 这家公司自己采购办公用品。
She self-procures her reading materials. = 她自己购买阅读材料。


一般过去时:
He self-procured the necessary documents for the visa application. = 他自行取得了签证申请所需的证件。
They self-procured the materials for their project. = 他们自己筹集了项目所需的材料。


现在完成时:
She has self-procured all the ingredients for the cake. = 她已经自己准备好了蛋糕的所有配料。
He has self-procured a substantial amount of funding for his research. = 他已经为自己研究筹集了大量资金。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, she had self-procured all the data needed for the presentation. = 会议之前，她已经自行准备好了演示文稿所需的所有数据。
They had self-procured the necessary permits before starting construction. = 在开始施工之前，他们已经自行取得了必要的许可证。


将来时:
We will self-procure the equipment needed for the experiment. = 我们将自己采购实验所需的设备。
He will self-procure the tickets for the concert. = 他将自己购买演唱会的门票。


虚拟语气:
If he had self-procured the materials earlier, he would have finished the project on time. = 如果他早点自己准备材料的话，他就能按时完成项目了。
I wish I had self-procured the necessary information before the interview. = 我真希望在面试前我已经自己收集到了必要的信息。


被动语态:
The necessary funds were self-procured by the organization. = 必要的资金是由该组织自行筹措的。
The equipment was self-procured by the research team. = 设备是由研究团队自行采购的。


疑问句:
Did she self-procure the ingredients for the recipe? = 她是自己准备菜谱里的配料吗？
Were the materials self-procured by the students? = 材料是学生自己准备的吗？


否定句:
The company did not self-procure the software; they outsourced it. = 公司没有自己采购软件；他们外包了。
He did not self-procure his education; he received a scholarship. = 他没有自己支付学费；他获得了奖学金。
