purging:/ˈpɜːrdʒɪŋ/|/ˈpɝːdʒɪŋ/| n. v.|清洗；清除；净化|The act of removing something unwanted, especially impurities or undesirable elements.|例句：The purging of toxins from the body is essential for good health. 清除体内的毒素对健康至关重要。

The government initiated a purging of corrupt officials. 政府开始清除腐败官员。


近义词：cleansing, purifying, eliminating, expunging|反义词：adding, accumulating, retaining


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He purges his system regularly. = 他定期清洗身体系统。
She purges the data from the database. = 她从数据库中清除数据。


一般过去时:
They purged the contaminated water supply. = 他们净化了受污染的供水。
He purged his hard drive of sensitive files. = 他从硬盘中删除了敏感文件。


现在完成时:
She has purged all traces of the virus from her computer. = 她已经从电脑中清除所有病毒痕迹。
They have purged the ranks of the organization of undesirable members. = 他们已经清除组织中不受欢迎的成员。


过去完成时:
The company had purged all the outdated information before the audit. = 公司在审计前已清除所有过时信息。
He had purged the old files from his computer before installing the new software. = 他在安装新软件之前已从电脑中删除了旧文件。


将来时:
We will purge the database of duplicate entries. = 我们将从数据库中清除重复条目。
The company will purge all obsolete equipment next month. = 公司下个月将清除所有过时的设备。


虚拟语气:
If I purged my diet of sugar, I would feel healthier. = 如果我戒掉糖，我会感觉更健康。
I wish I had purged those old files months ago. = 我真希望几个月前就删除了那些旧文件。


被动语态:
The infected files were purged automatically. = 受感染的文件被自动清除。
The old data will be purged from the system soon. = 旧数据将很快从系统中清除。


疑问句:
Will the system automatically purge temporary files? = 系统会自动清除临时文件吗？
Have you purged the unnecessary files from your computer? = 你已经从电脑中清除不必要的文件夹了吗？


否定句:
He did not purge the files as instructed. = 他没有按照指示清除文件。
She will not purge the data without authorization. = 未经授权，她不会清除数据。


PHRASE->

purge oneself of = 从…中清除
purge the body of = 清除体内的…
purge the mind of = 清除心中的…
purge from memory = 从记忆中清除
bowel purge = 肠道清洗
