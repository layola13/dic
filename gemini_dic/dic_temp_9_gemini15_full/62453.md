endorsements:/ɪnˈdɔːrsmənts/ | n. |认可；背书；支持；代言 |The act of endorsing or the state of being endorsed;  public approval or support.  It can refer to written statements of approval,  the act of supporting a person or product, or a celebrity's support for a brand. |例句：The product received numerous endorsements from satisfied customers.  该产品收到了许多满意客户的认可。

例句：Her endorsement of the candidate boosted his campaign. 她对候选人的支持提振了他的竞选活动。


近义词：approvals,  sanctions,  support,  commendations |反义词：rejections,  condemnations,  disapprovals |词性:名词
