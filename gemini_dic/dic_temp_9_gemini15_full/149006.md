ovule:/ˈoʊvjuːl/ | n. |胚珠|A small body within the ovary of a seed plant that contains the female gametophyte and develops into a seed after fertilization.  It is the female reproductive cell of a flowering plant. | Example: The ovule contains the egg cell, which is fertilized by pollen to form a seed.  胚珠包含卵细胞，卵细胞受精后与花粉结合形成种子。|近义词:embryo (in early stages)|反义词:pollen
