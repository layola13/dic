chiffon:/ˈʃɪfɒn/| n.|雪纺绸，轻薄透明织物|A lightweight, sheer fabric, typically made of silk, nylon, or rayon.  It is known for its delicate and flowing drape.|例句：The dress was made of a beautiful, flowing chiffon. 这件裙子是用美丽的飘逸雪纺绸做的。|例句：She wore a chiffon scarf that danced in the breeze. 她戴着一条在微风中飘动的雪纺围巾。|近义词：silk, voile, georgette|反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting heavy or coarse fabrics could be considered opposites.|名词
