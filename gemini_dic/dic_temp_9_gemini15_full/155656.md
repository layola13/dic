phlegmasia:/flɛɡˈmeɪʒə/| n.| 肢体肿胀；炎症；淤血 | A condition characterized by swelling and inflammation of a limb, often accompanied by pain and discoloration.  It often refers to a severe form of deep vein thrombosis (DVT) resulting in limb swelling. | Example:  The patient presented with phlegmasia cerulea dolens, a severe complication of DVT.|近义词：edema, swelling, inflammation |反义词：None directly applicable, as it describes a condition.
