horse-trader:/ˈhɔːrsˌtreɪdər/ | n. | 马商；精明交易者 | A person who buys and sells horses, often implying shrewdness and a willingness to use trickery to get a good deal.  | He was a shrewd horse-trader, always getting the best price. 他是个精明的马商，总是能拿到最好的价格。 |  dealer, bargainer, negotiator |  naive, gullible | noun
