font:/fɒnt/ /fɑːnt/| n. |字体；字型；印刷字体|A typeface or style of type.  A font is a complete set of characters—letters, numbers, punctuation marks, and symbols—in a particular typeface and size.|例句：The document was printed in a bold font. 这份文件是用粗体字打印的。|例句：She changed the font to Times New Roman. 她把字体改成了Times New Roman。|近义词：typeface, style|反义词：N/A
