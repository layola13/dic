resolvable: /ˈrɛzəlʊvəbəl/ | adj. | 可解决的，可分解的 | Able to be solved or settled; capable of being resolved into constituent parts.  | The problem, though complex, is resolvable.  这个问题虽然复杂，但还是可以解决的。The compound is resolvable into its constituent elements.  该化合物可分解成其组成元素。 | solvable, soluble, manageable | insoluble, intractable, unmanageable | 形容词

词源:
The word "resolvable" is derived from the verb "resolve," which comes from the Old French word "resoudre," meaning "to loosen, untie, solve."  The prefix "re-" indicates a repetition or intensification of the action.  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or possibility.
