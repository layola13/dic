overlaps:/ˈoʊvərˌlæps/ | v. n. |重叠，部分重合 |

英文解析:To extend over and cover partly.  As a noun, it refers to the extent to which things overlap.

例句:
The two circles overlap significantly. = 这两个圆圈有很大的重叠部分。

The dates of the two conferences overlap. = 这两个会议的日期有重叠。


近义词:coincide, intersect, concur

反义词:disjoint, separate, distinct


词性:verb, noun

词源:
The word "overlap" comes from the Old English *oferlapian, meaning "to lap over."  The prefix "over-" signifies "above" or "beyond," and "lap" means to fold or lie over something else.  The word's structure reflects the visual image of one thing extending beyond and covering another.
