nonfluidity: /ˌnɒnfluːˈɪdəti/ | n. |非流动性，不流动性 | The lack of ease or smoothness of flow or movement; rigidity.  It describes a state or quality where something is not easily flowing or changing. This can refer to physical substances, ideas, or social structures. | Example: The nonfluidity of the lava made it difficult to predict its path.  熔岩的不流动性使得难以预测其路径。  The nonfluidity of political opinions hindered compromise.  政治观点的不流动性阻碍了妥协。 | 近义词：rigidity, stiffness, inflexibility, viscosity | 反义词：fluidity, flexibility, mobility
