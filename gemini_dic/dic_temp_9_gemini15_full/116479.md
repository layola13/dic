materialistic:/ˌmətɪəriˈælɪstɪk/ | adj. | 物质主义的，唯物主义的 |  Characterized by or emphasizing material possessions or physical comfort as opposed to spiritual values.  It focuses on the acquisition of wealth and material goods as the primary goal in life. |


例句：

His materialistic lifestyle is a stark contrast to his spiritual beliefs.  他的物质主义生活方式与他的精神信仰形成鲜明对比。

She criticized the materialistic values of modern society. 她批评了现代社会的物质主义价值观。


近义词：material, worldly, avaricious, covetous


反义词：spiritual, idealistic, ascetic


词性:形容词
