neonism:/ˈniːənɪzəm/| n. |霓虹主义，霓虹灯文化|Neonism is a cultural movement or aesthetic that emphasizes the use of neon lights and their vibrant, often artificial, colors in art, design, and urban landscapes.  It often evokes a sense of futuristic, retro, or even dystopian atmospheres.|Example: The city's neonism was breathtaking at night, a dazzling display of light and color.|近义词：electroculture, cyberpunk aesthetic|反义词：naturalism, minimalism
