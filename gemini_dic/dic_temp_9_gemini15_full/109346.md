leninist:/ˈlɛnɪnɪst/| adj. n.|列宁主义的；列宁主义者|Relating to or characteristic of the political and economic theories of Vladimir Lenin, especially as developed in the Soviet Union.  Leninism emphasizes a vanguard party of professional revolutionaries leading the proletariat in a socialist revolution, followed by a dictatorship of the proletariat to build a communist state.  It contrasts with other forms of Marxism in its focus on a centralized party and its emphasis on the importance of a revolution led by a small elite.|例句：His political views are considered to be Leninist. 他的政治观点被认为是列宁主义的。

The country adopted a Leninist economic model. 这个国家采用了列宁主义的经济模式。|近义词：Marxist, communist, socialist|反义词：capitalist, liberal, democratic
