ceilidhs:/ˈkeɪlɪðz/| n. |凯利舞会；盖里聚会|A ceilidh is a type of social gathering, typically in Scotland, Ireland, and other parts of the Celtic world, featuring traditional folk music, dancing, and socialising.  It's a lively and communal event.|Example: We attended a lively ceilidh with traditional Scottish music and dancing. 我们参加了一个充满活力的凯利舞会，有传统的苏格兰音乐和舞蹈。|近义词：gathering, party, social, dance|反义词：solitude, isolation
