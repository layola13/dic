makes:/meɪks/ | vt. |制造；使得；构成；赚得 | to create or produce something; to cause something to happen; to form or constitute something; to earn money |例句：She makes beautiful pottery. 她制作精美的陶器。|例句：The news makes me sad.  这个消息使我很悲伤。|近义词：creates, produces, causes, earns |反义词：breaks, destroys, ruins
