preamble:/ˈpriːæmbəl/| n. |序言，前言，导言 |A preamble is an introductory statement to a formal document, especially a constitution or statute, explaining its purpose and principles.  |Example: The preamble to the constitution outlines the fundamental rights of citizens.  序言阐述了公民的基本权利。|近义词：introduction, preface, prologue |反义词：conclusion, postscript
