skimp:/skɪmp/| vt. & vi.|吝啬；节省；敷衍；偷工减料|To be frugal or stingy; to use less of something than is needed or desirable; to do something in a careless or inadequate way.|例句：They skimped on the ingredients, resulting in a bland dish. 他们吝啬食材，导致菜肴寡淡无味。|例句：He skimped on the details of his report. 他敷衍了事地完成了报告。|近义词：scrimp, stint, economize|反义词：splurge, lavish, overdo


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He always skimps on his holiday plans. = 他总是对他的假期计划吝啬。
They skimp on the quality of their products. = 他们偷工减料，降低产品质量。


一般过去时:
She skimped on the decorations for the party. = 她对聚会的装饰很吝啬。
We skimped on sleep before the exam. = 考试前我们睡眠不足。


现在完成时:
He has skimped and saved all his life. = 他一生都省吃俭用。
They have skimped on maintenance, leading to major repairs. = 他们吝啬维护费用，导致需要进行大修。


过去完成时:
They had skimped on their training, so they weren't prepared for the competition. = 他们训练不足，所以没有准备好比赛。
She had skimped on her studies, which resulted in poor grades. = 她学习敷衍了事，导致成绩很差。


将来时:
We will skimp on the luxuries this year to save for a new car. = 今年我们将省去奢侈品开支，为买新车攒钱。
They will skimp on advertising to cut costs. = 他们将削减广告支出以降低成本。


虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we wouldn't skimp on food. = 如果我们有钱，我们就不会在食物上吝啬。
I wish I hadn't skimped on the preparation for the presentation. = 我真希望我没有敷衍准备演示文稿。


被动语态:
The project was skimped on, leading to its failure. = 项目偷工减料，导致失败。
The details were skimped over in the report. = 报告中对细节进行了敷衍处理。


疑问句:
Did they skimp on the materials used in the construction? = 他们在建筑中是否吝啬材料？
Are you going to skimp on your vacation this year? = 你今年假期打算节省开支吗？


否定句:
We didn't skimp on the food for the party. = 我们没有在聚会的食物上吝啬。
They wouldn't skimp on quality; their reputation is at stake. = 他们不会偷工减料；他们的声誉岌岌可危。


PHRASE->
skimp on sth = 在…方面吝啬
skimp out on sth = 逃避责任；偷懒
skimp and save = 省吃俭用
skimp through sth = 草率完成某事
don't skimp on = 不要吝啬…
