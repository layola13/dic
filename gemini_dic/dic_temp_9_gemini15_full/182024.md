remedies:/ˈrɛmədiz/ | n. | 治疗方法；补救措施 | Means of curing or alleviating a disease or condition; a solution to a problem.  | Example:  The doctor prescribed several remedies for the cough. 医生开了几种治疗咳嗽的药。

Example: The company implemented several remedies to address the financial crisis. 公司实施了几项措施来解决金融危机。


近义词： cures, treatments, solutions,  correctives

反义词： problems, diseases, ailments,  difficulties


词性: 名词
