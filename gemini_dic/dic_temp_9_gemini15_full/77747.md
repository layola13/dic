gender-bendings:/ˈdʒɛndə(r) bɛndɪŋz/| n.|性别转换，角色性别颠倒|The act of portraying a character of a different gender than one's own, often in performance or fiction.  This can involve changing the gender of an existing character or creating a new character with a reversed gender.|例句：The play featured a fascinating gender-bending performance of Hamlet by a female actor. 这部戏由一位女演员扮演哈姆雷特，呈现了令人着迷的性别转换表演。|近义词：gender swapping, sex-role reversal, cross-dressing (if referring to clothing)|反义词：gender conformity, traditional gender roles|名词
