middle-of-the-roader:/ˌmɪdl əv ðə ˈroʊdər/| n. |中间派，温和派，立场不明确的人 |A middle-of-the-roader is someone who avoids extremes in their opinions or actions, often seen as moderate or uncommitted.  They tend to take a centrist position, compromising between opposing viewpoints. |Example:  He's a middle-of-the-roader on economic policy, advocating for neither extreme cuts nor excessive spending. 他在经济政策上是个中间派，既不主张极端削减开支，也不主张过度支出。|近义词：moderate, centrist, pragmatist |反义词：extremist, radical
