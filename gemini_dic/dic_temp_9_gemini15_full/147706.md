overpreoccupies: /ˌoʊvərˈpriːɒkjʊpaɪz/ | v. | 使过度担忧；使过度占据心思 | To worry or think too much about something.  This word is a relatively rare and less common formation, combining "over-" (excessive) with "preoccupies" (to dominate one's thoughts). It suggests a state of being excessively burdened by a thought or worry. |  His anxieties overpreoccupies him to the point of paralysis. 他对焦虑的过度担忧让他陷入了瘫痪状态。  Her fear of failure overpreoccupies her, hindering her progress. 她对失败的过度担忧阻碍了她的进步。|近义词：obsesses, burdens, overwhelms | 反义词：calms, relaxes, soothes | 动词
