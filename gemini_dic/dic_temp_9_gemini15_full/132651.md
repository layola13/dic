nondiscursively:/ˌnɒndɪˈskɜːrsɪvli/ | adv. |非论证地，非推理地 |Not involving or characterized by discourse or rational argument; not discursive.  It often implies a reliance on intuition, feeling, or immediate experience rather than logical reasoning.|Example:  The artist approached the creation of the painting nondiscursively, letting intuition guide her brushstrokes.  艺术家以非论证的方式创作这幅画，让直觉引导她的笔触。|近义词:intuitively, instinctively, implicitly|反义词: discursively, rationally, logically |无
