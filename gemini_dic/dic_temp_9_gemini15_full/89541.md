honoraries:/ˈɑːnəreriz/ | n. |名誉职位；荣誉称号；免费赠送物 |Honorary positions or titles are given in recognition of someone's achievements or contributions, often without salary or formal duties.  Honorary gifts are given free of charge. |例句：He received an honorary doctorate from the university. 他获得了该大学的荣誉博士学位。

例句：The organization presented her with an honorary membership. 该组织授予她名誉会员资格。


近义词：awards, distinctions, accolades |反义词：salaried positions, paid appointments


词源:源自honorary,  itself derived from Old French honorarie and ultimately from Latin honorarius ("relating to honor").  The suffix "-ies" indicates a plural form.
