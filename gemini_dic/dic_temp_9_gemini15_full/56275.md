/dɒɡ/| n. |狗|A domesticated canine mammal that typically has a long snout, an acute sense of smell, and a barking, howling, or whining voice. Dogs are kept as pets, for guarding property, or for hunting.|例句：My dog is a golden retriever. 我的狗是金毛猎犬。|例句：The dog barked at the stranger.  那条狗对着陌生人叫。|近义词： canine, hound |反义词：cat
