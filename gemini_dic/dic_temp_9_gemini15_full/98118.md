insalubrious: /ˌɪnˈsæljuːbriəs/ | adj. | 不卫生的；有害健康的；恶劣的 | Unhealthy, unwholesome, or unpleasant.  It often refers to conditions or environments that are detrimental to health or well-being. |例句：The insalubrious living conditions contributed to the spread of disease.  不卫生的居住条件导致疾病蔓延。

The factory was situated in an insalubrious part of town.  这家工厂位于城镇不卫生的地方。|近义词：unhealthy, unwholesome, unhealthy, noxious, deleterious |反义词：healthy, wholesome, salubrious

词源:
