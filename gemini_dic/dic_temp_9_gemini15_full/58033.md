droid:/ˈdrɔɪd/| n.|机器人，仿生人|A robot, especially a fictional one, typically one that looks human or humanoid.  The term is frequently used in science fiction. |例句：The droids were essential to the success of the mission.  机器人对任务的成功至关重要。|例句：C-3PO is a well-known protocol droid from Star Wars. C-3PO是星球大战中著名的礼仪机器人。|近义词：robot, android, automaton|反义词：human, organic
