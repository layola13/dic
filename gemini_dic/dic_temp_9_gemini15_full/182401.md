renegotiate:/ˌriːnɪˈɡoʊʃieɪt/ | vt. |重新谈判；再协商 | To negotiate again; to discuss and alter the terms of an agreement or contract.  |例句：We need to renegotiate the contract terms. 我们需要重新协商合同条款。
例句：The two countries decided to renegotiate the trade agreement. 这两个国家决定重新谈判贸易协定。|近义词：revise, amend, modify |反义词：finalize, conclude


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They renegotiate the contract every year. = 他们每年都会重新协商合同。
We renegotiate the terms of our partnership regularly. = 我们定期重新协商合作伙伴关系的条款。

一般过去时:
The union renegotiated the workers' wages. = 工会重新协商了工人的工资。
They renegotiated the deal after discovering a flaw. = 他们发现缺陷后重新协商了协议。

现在完成时:
They have renegotiated the deal three times. = 他们已经三次重新协商了协议。
The company has renegotiated its debt obligations.  = 公司已经重新协商了债务义务。

过去完成时:
They had renegotiated the contract before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经重新协商了合同。
She had renegotiated the price before the final sale. = 她在最终销售前已经重新协商了价格。


将来时:
We will renegotiate the contract next month. = 我们下个月将重新协商合同。
The government will renegotiate the trade deal with its allies. = 政府将与盟友重新谈判贸易协定。


虚拟语气:
If we renegotiated the terms, they might agree. = 如果我们重新协商条款，他们可能会同意。
I wish we had renegotiated the contract sooner. = 我希望我们早点重新协商合同。


被动语态:
The contract was renegotiated last week. = 合同在上周重新协商了。
The terms of the agreement are being renegotiated. = 协议条款正在重新协商中。


疑问句:
Will they renegotiate the price? = 他们会重新协商价格吗？
Have they renegotiated the contract yet? = 他们已经重新协商合同了吗？


否定句:
We did not renegotiate the contract. = 我们没有重新协商合同。
They will not renegotiate the terms under any circumstances. = 他们无论如何都不会重新协商条款。


PHRASE->
renegotiate a contract = 重新协商合同
renegotiate the terms = 重新协商条款
renegotiate a deal = 重新协商一笔交易
renegotiate the price = 重新协商价格
renegotiate a settlement = 重新协商和解方案

词源:
re- (again) + negotiate (to discuss and arrive at an agreement)  重新 + 谈判
