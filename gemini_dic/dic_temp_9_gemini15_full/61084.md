elliptograph:/ɪˈlɪptəɡræf/| n. |椭圆仪|A device used to draw ellipses.  It typically consists of a mechanism with two pins fixed at the foci of the intended ellipse, and a pencil or pen that is constrained to move such that the sum of the distances from the pencil to each pin remains constant.|Example: The artist used an elliptograph to create the perfect oval shape for the painting's frame.  艺术家用椭圆仪为画框创造了完美的椭圆形。|近义词：ellipse-drawing device|反义词：无
