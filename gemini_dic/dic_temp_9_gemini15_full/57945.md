drip:/drɪp/| n., vi., vt.|滴，滴下；|The sound of dripping water kept me awake.  滴水的声音让我睡不着觉。|The roof is dripping. 屋顶在滴水。|近义词：fall, trickle, ooze|反义词：pour, gush


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The faucet drips constantly. 水龙头一直在滴水。
Water drips from the leaky roof.  水从漏雨的屋顶滴下来。

一般过去时:
The tap dripped all night. 水龙头整夜都在滴水。
Honey dripped from his chin.  蜂蜜从他的下巴滴下来。

现在完成时:
The roof has dripped all day. 屋顶已经滴水一整天了。
The paint has dripped down the wall. 油漆已经顺着墙壁滴下来了。

过去完成时:
The water had dripped onto the floor before I noticed it.  在我注意到之前，水已经滴到地板上了。
The candle wax had dripped onto the tablecloth. 蜡烛油已经滴到桌布上了。

将来时:
The melting ice will drip onto the ground. 融化的冰将会滴到地上。
The rain will drip from the leaves. 雨水将从树叶上滴落。

虚拟语气:
If the tap dripped, I would fix it. 如果水龙头滴水，我会修理它。
I wish the roof wasn't dripping.  我希望屋顶不要滴水。

被动语态:
The floor was dripped on by the leaky pipe. 地板被漏水的管道滴到了。
The cake was dripped with chocolate. 蛋糕上淋了巧克力酱。

疑问句:
Is the tap dripping again? 水龙头又滴水了吗？
Why is the water dripping? 为什么水在滴？

否定句:
The faucet doesn't drip anymore. 水龙头不再滴水了。
The roof didn't drip during the night. 夜间屋顶没有滴水。
