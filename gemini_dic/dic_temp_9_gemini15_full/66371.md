excusingly: /ɪkˈskjuːzɪŋli/ | adv. |  抱歉地，辩解地，藉口地 |  In a way that offers an excuse or apology; apologetically.  |例句：He mumbled excusingly, "I'm sorry, I didn't see you there." 他抱歉地说：“对不起，我没看到你在那里。”
例句：She spoke excusingly about her lateness, blaming the traffic. 她辩解说她迟到是因为交通堵塞。|近义词：apologetically, regretfully, sheepishly |反义词：boldly, confidently, defiantly
