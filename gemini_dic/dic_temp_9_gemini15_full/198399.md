showpieces:/ˈʃoʊpiːsɪz/ | n. | 杰作；展品；精华 | Showpieces are the best or most impressive examples of something, often displayed to attract attention.  They are items or creations meant to be showcased as examples of exceptional quality or artistry. |例句：The museum's showpieces include a collection of ancient artifacts.  博物馆的展品包括一系列古代文物。

The company's new car is a showpiece of engineering. 这家公司的新车是工程技术的杰作。


近义词：masterpieces, highlights, exhibits, specimens | 反义词：trash, junk, rubbish | 词性：名词
