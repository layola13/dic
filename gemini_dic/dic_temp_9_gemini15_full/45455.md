curatic:/kjʊˈreɪtɪk/| adj.|策展的；精选的；具有鉴赏力的|relating to curation or curators;  showing careful selection and good judgment.|例句：The curatic approach to the exhibition was highly praised. 策展人对展览的精心策划受到了高度赞扬。|近义词：curated, selected, chosen|反义词：random, haphazard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The museum curates a diverse collection of artifacts. = 博物馆收藏了各种各样的文物。
She curates a blog featuring independent artists. = 她运营着一个博客，专门介绍独立艺术家。

一般过去时:
The art gallery curated a successful exhibition last year. = 去年这家艺术画廊策划了一个成功的展览。
He curated a playlist of his favorite songs. = 他精心制作了一个包含他最喜欢的歌曲的播放列表。

现在完成时:
They have curated several impactful exhibitions this year. = 今年他们已经策划了几个有影响力的展览。
She has curated a collection of vintage photographs. = 她已经收藏了一批老照片。

过去完成时:
The team had curated the exhibit for months before the opening. = 团队在展览开幕前已经策划了几个月。
He had curated his online portfolio for years.  = 他已经精心打理他的在线作品集多年了。

将来时:
The museum will curate an exhibition on Impressionist paintings next year. = 博物馆明年将策划一个关于印象派绘画的展览。
She will curate a special event for the company anniversary. = 她将为公司周年庆策划一个特别的活动。

虚拟语气:
If they curated the exhibition differently, it would have been more successful. = 如果他们以不同的方式策划展览，它会更成功。
I wish I had curated that collection myself. = 我真希望我自己策划了那个收藏。

被动语态:
The exhibition was curated by a renowned art historian. = 这个展览是由一位著名的艺术史学家策划的。
The playlist has been carefully curated for your listening pleasure. = 这个播放列表已精心策划，以供您欣赏。

疑问句:
Who curated this impressive art collection? = 谁策划了这个令人印象深刻的艺术收藏？
Will the museum curate another exhibition soon? = 博物馆很快会策划另一个展览吗？

否定句:
The event wasn't curated professionally. = 这个活动没有专业地策划。
He didn't curate the website content effectively. = 他没有有效地策划网站内容。
