proposers:/prəˈpoʊzərz/| n. |建议者；提议者|Those who put forward proposals or suggestions.  People who propose something, such as a plan, idea, or marriage. |The proposers of the new bill met with the committee. 新法案的提案者会见了委员会。 The proposers of the marriage were very happy. 结婚的提议者非常开心。|suggestors, proponents, offerers|objectors, opponents|名词
