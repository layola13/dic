graze:/ɡreɪz/ | v., n. | 轻微擦伤；放牧；轻吃；浏览 | To touch or scrape lightly; to feed on grass; to eat small amounts of food; to look at something quickly and casually. |例句：The car grazed the wall.  汽车轻擦了墙壁。
The cattle graze in the pasture. 牛群在牧场上吃草。
I only grazed on a piece of toast for breakfast. 我早餐只吃了一小块烤面包。
I was just grazing through the magazine. 我只是浏览了一下杂志。|近义词：scratch, graze, browse, nibble | 反义词：devour, gorge


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cows graze in the field every day. 牛每天都在田里吃草。
He grazes his sheep on the hillside. 他在山坡上放羊。


一般过去时:
The car grazed the lamppost. 车子蹭到了路灯杆。
She grazed her knee in the fall. 她摔倒时擦破了膝盖。


现在完成时:
The sheep have grazed all the grass. 羊吃光了所有的草。
I have grazed my elbow. 我擦破了我的肘部。


过去完成时:
The cattle had grazed most of the pasture before the storm. 暴风雨来临之前，牛群已经吃掉了大部分牧场上的草。
They had grazed on the meager rations for days. 他们已经吃了几天的微薄口粮。


将来时:
The farmer will graze his herd in the new field next week. 农民下周将在新田里放牧他的牛群。
We will graze the horses near the river. 我们将在河边放马。


虚拟语气:
If I grazed my knee, I would go to the doctor. 如果我擦破了膝盖，我会去看医生。
If the sheep had grazed more carefully, there would have been more grass. 如果羊更仔细地吃草，就会有更多的草。


被动语态:
The field was grazed bare by the sheep. 田地被羊吃光了草。
The car was grazed slightly in the accident. 车子在事故中轻微擦伤。


疑问句:
Do the goats graze on this hillside? 山坡上的山羊吃草吗？
Did the deer graze in the forest last night? 鹿昨晚在森林里吃草了吗？


否定句:
The sheep didn't graze in that field. 羊没有在那块田里吃草。
The horse didn't graze because of the rain. 马因为下雨没有吃草。
