passions:/ˈpæʃənz/| n.|热情；强烈爱好；酷爱；激情|intense and often overwhelming feelings; strong enthusiasm or interest.|例句：His passions include music, art, and cooking.  他的爱好包括音乐、艺术和烹饪。|例句：Her passion for justice led her to become a lawyer. 她对正义的热情使她成为了一名律师。|近义词：enthusiasms, zeal, fervor|反义词：apathy, indifference


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His passions are music and sports. = 他的爱好是音乐和体育。
What are your passions in life? = 你人生中的热情是什么？


一般过去时:
Her passions consumed her entirely. = 她的激情完全吞噬了她。
His passions for art faded over time. = 他对艺术的热情随着时间的推移而消退了。


现在完成时:
His passions have shaped his career. = 他的热情塑造了他的职业生涯。
She has pursued her passions relentlessly. = 她一直不懈地追求自己的热情。


过去完成时:
He had pursued his passions before he settled down. = 在他安定下来之前，他一直追求着自己的热情。
They had long since abandoned their passions. = 他们早已放弃了他们的激情。


将来时:
He will pursue his passions with renewed vigor. = 他将以新的活力追求他的热情。
She will dedicate her life to her passions. = 她将把一生奉献给她的热情。


虚拟语气:
If I had followed my passions, I would be happier. = 如果我追随我的热情，我会更快乐。
I wish I could dedicate more time to my passions. = 我希望我能有更多的时间来追求我的爱好。


被动语态:
His passions were ignited by a chance encounter. = 他的热情因一次偶然的相遇而被点燃。
Her passions were not understood by her family. = 她的热情不被她的家人理解。


疑问句:
What are his passions? = 他的爱好是什么？
What passions do you share with your friends? = 你和你的朋友有什么共同的爱好？


否定句:
He doesn't have many passions in life. = 他生活中没有多少热情。
She didn't let her passions interfere with her work. = 她没有让她的热情影响她的工作。
