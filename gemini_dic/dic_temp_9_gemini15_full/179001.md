redefining:/ˌriːˈdiːfaɪnɪŋ/| v. |重新定义；改写定义 |The act of defining something again, often with a changed or updated meaning.  It implies a shift in understanding or interpretation. |例句：The company is redefining its brand image to appeal to a younger audience. 公司正在重新定义其品牌形象以吸引更年轻的受众。

例句：The discovery of new evidence is redefining our understanding of the historical event. 新证据的发现正在重新定义我们对这一历史事件的理解。


近义词：revising, reinterpreting, recasting, reformulating


反义词：defining (in its original sense), solidifying, confirming


词性:动词
