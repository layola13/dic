radian:/ˈreɪdiən/| n. |弧度|A unit of angle, equal to the angle at the center of a circle whose arc is equal in length to the radius.|例句：The angle is measured in radians.  这个角度是用弧度来衡量的。|例句：One radian is approximately 57.3 degrees. 一个弧度大约等于57.3度。|近义词：degree (as a unit of angle, but with different scale)|反义词：degree (in the sense of being a different unit)|词性:名词
