hematein:/ˈhɛmətiːn/| n. |苏木精；苏木素|A natural red dye extracted from the heartwood of the logwood tree (Haematoxylum campechianum).  It's used as a histological stain and in some inks.|例句：Hematein is a valuable stain in microscopy for visualizing cellular structures.|近义词：hematoxylin (though technically different)|反义词：无
