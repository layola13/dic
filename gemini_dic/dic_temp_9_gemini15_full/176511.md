re-proof:/ˌriːˈpruːf/ | v. | 再校对，复印校对 | To proofread again; to print a second proof of (something).  |例句：We need to re-proof the document before sending it to the client. 我们需要在发送给客户之前再次校对文档。|例句：The editor asked for a re-proof of the chapter after the revisions. 编辑在修改之后要求重新校对章节。|近义词：revise, check, review | 反义词：None specifically, but implies a lack of initial thoroughness.
