词源:
The term "footbinding" is a compound word combining "foot" and "binding."  "Foot" refers to the foot itself, and "binding" refers to the act of tightly wrapping the feet.  The practice originated in China and the term reflects the English understanding of the process.  There isn't a direct equivalent single word in Chinese, although descriptive phrases exist.
