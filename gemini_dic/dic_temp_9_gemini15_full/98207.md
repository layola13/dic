insectivore:/ˈɪnsektɪvɔːr/| n. |食虫动物|An insectivore is an animal whose diet consists mainly of insects.  Insectivores are found in various habitats across the globe and play a crucial role in regulating insect populations.|Example: The hedgehog is a classic example of an insectivore.|近义词：insect-eater|反义词：carnivore (for large animals), herbivore|名词
