principals:/ˈprɪnsəpəlz/| n.|校长；负责人；主要人物；原理；原则 |The most important people involved in a matter, or the main things involved.  |例句：The principals of the two schools met to discuss a student exchange program.  两所学校的校长会面讨论学生交流项目。|例句：The principals of democracy are freedom and equality. 民主的主要原则是自由和平等。|近义词：directors, leaders, heads, principles, fundamentals|反义词：minors, subordinates, details
