reversionist:/rɪˈvɜːʒənɪst/| n. |复归论者；恢复论者；逆转主义者|A reversionist is someone who advocates for a return to a previous state or system, often a more traditional or conservative one.  They believe that societal or political progress has gone too far and that a return to earlier practices or beliefs is necessary or desirable.  This term is often used in political and social contexts.|例句：The reversionist movement gained traction after the recent social reforms.  复归论者运动在最近的社会改革后获得了支持。

The rise of the reversionist party signals a shift in public opinion. 复归主义政党的兴起标志着公众舆论的转变。|近义词：traditionalist, conservative, reactionary |反义词：progressive, innovator, radical
