renidification:/ˌriːnɪdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 再筑巢；重新筑巢 | The process of building a new nest, often after a previous nest has been destroyed or abandoned.  This term is commonly used in ornithology (the study of birds) and refers to the rebuilding of a nest by birds. | Example:  The robins, after their nest was destroyed by a storm, engaged in renidification and successfully raised another brood.  =  知更鸟在巢穴被暴风雨摧毁后，进行了重新筑巢，并成功地抚养了另一窝雏鸟。 | 近义词：re-nesting, nest rebuilding | 反义词：nest abandonment, nest desertion | 名词
