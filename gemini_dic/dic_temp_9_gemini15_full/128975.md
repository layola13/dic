nip:/nɪp/| n./v.|轻咬；掐；少量；困境|To bite gently; to pinch; a small amount; a difficult situation. |例句：The dog gave me a little nip on the hand.  狗狗轻轻咬了我一下手。|例句：She nipped the flower bud off the stem. 她掐掉了花蕾。|近义词：bite, pinch, snack, predicament |反义词：large amount, ease

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He nips the buds off the roses. = 他掐掉玫瑰花蕾。
The dog often nips at my heels. = 狗经常咬我的脚后跟。

一般过去时:
She nipped the thread. = 她剪断了线。
The cold wind nipped at my cheeks. = 寒风吹得我脸颊生疼。

现在完成时:
He has nipped the problem in the bud. = 他已扼杀了问题于萌芽状态。
The frost has nipped the young plants. = 霜冻损坏了幼苗。

过去完成时:
She had nipped the flower before the frost arrived. = 霜冻来临之前，她已经掐掉了花。
They had nipped the plan in the bud.  = 他们已经扼杀了这个计划于萌芽状态。

将来时:
I will nip that rumour in the bud. = 我将会扼杀那个谣言于萌芽状态。
The gardener will nip off the dead leaves. = 园丁将会摘掉枯叶。

虚拟语气:
If I had nipped that problem in the bud, things would be better now. = 如果我当初扼杀了那个问题于萌芽状态，现在情况会更好。
I wish I had nipped that bad habit in the bud. = 我真希望当初能扼杀掉那个坏习惯。

被动语态:
The buds were nipped by the frost. = 花蕾被霜冻损坏了。
The problem should be nipped in the bud. = 这个问题应该扼杀在萌芽状态。

疑问句:
Did the dog nip you? = 狗咬了你吗？
Will the frost nip the plants? = 霜冻会损害植物吗？

否定句:
The dog didn't nip me. = 狗没有咬我。
The gardener didn't nip the flowers. = 园丁没有掐掉花。
