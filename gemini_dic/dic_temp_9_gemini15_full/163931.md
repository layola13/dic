predevotion:/priːdɪˈvoʊʃən/| n. |预备祈祷；事先的奉献|A period of preparation for worship or prayer, often involving private reflection and spiritual exercises.  It can also refer to something dedicated or given in advance.|例句：She engaged in a period of predevotion before the sermon, meditating on the scripture reading. 她在上教堂讲道前进行了一段时间的预备祈祷，默想经文。|例句：His predevotion to the cause was evident in his tireless fundraising efforts. 他对事业的预先奉献在他不知疲倦的筹款努力中显而易见。|近义词：preparation, contemplation, dedication |反义词：neglect, indifference, procrastination |名词
