chiropractors:/kaɪˈrɒprəktərz/| n. |脊椎按摩师（复数）|Practitioners of chiropractic, a form of alternative medicine that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of musculoskeletal disorders, primarily those affecting the spine.|例句：Many chiropractors use spinal manipulation to relieve back pain. 许多脊椎按摩师使用脊柱手法来缓解背痛。  Many chiropractors also offer advice on lifestyle changes to improve overall health. 许多脊椎按摩师还会就改善整体健康的生活方式改变提供建议。|近义词：spinal manipulators, osteopathic physicians (in some contexts)|反义词：medical doctors (in some contexts), allopathic physicians|名词
