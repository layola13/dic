dyslexias:/dɪsˈlɛksiəz/| n. |诵读困难（复数）|Learning disabilities that affect the ability to read, write, and spell.  Dyslexias encompass a range of difficulties, impacting different aspects of language processing. |Examples:  Many different types of dyslexias exist, affecting individuals in various ways. 许多不同类型的诵读困难症存在，以各种方式影响个人。The school provides support for students with dyslexias. 学校为患有诵读困难症的学生提供支持。|近义词:reading disabilities, language processing disorders |反义词:  (no direct antonym, but could be contrasted with exceptional reading abilities)|名词

词源:

The word "dyslexias" is the plural form of "dyslexia."  "Dyslexia" comes from the Greek words "dys-" (meaning "bad" or "difficult") and "lexis" (meaning "word" or "speech").  Therefore, "dyslexia" literally means "difficulty with words" or "difficulty with language."
