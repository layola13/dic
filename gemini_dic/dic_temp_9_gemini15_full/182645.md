reorganize:/ˌriːˈɔːrɡənaɪz/ | vt. |重新组织；改组；改编 | To restructure or rearrange something, often a system, organization, or group, in order to improve efficiency or effectiveness.  |例句：The company decided to reorganize its marketing department to boost sales. 公司决定重组其市场部以提高销售额。

例句：The library staff reorganized the books according to a new classification system. 图书馆工作人员根据新的分类系统重新整理了书籍。


近义词：rearrange, restructure, reformulate, revamp |反义词：disorganize, disarray


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reorganize the office every year. = 他们每年都会重新组织办公室。
The company reorganizes its operations frequently. = 这家公司经常重组其业务。

一般过去时:
The team reorganized its strategy after the loss. = 团队在失败后重新调整了策略。
