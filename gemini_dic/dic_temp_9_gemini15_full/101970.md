isobathythermic: /ˌaɪsoʊˈbæθɪθɜːrmɪk/ | adj. | 等深温的 | Of, relating to, or characterized by isobathytherms; having the same temperature at the same depth.  Isobaths are lines connecting points of equal depth in the sea. Therms relate to heat. Therefore, isobathythermic refers to areas or depths in the ocean that share the same temperature. | The researchers mapped the isobathythermic zones in the Atlantic to understand ocean currents.  研究人员绘制了大西洋等深温带的地图，以了解洋流。|  同义词: isothermic (at the same depth) | 反义词:  There isn't a direct antonym, but a contrasting term might be "bathyal" (relating to deep ocean depths with varying temperatures).
