mesa:/ˈmeɪsə/| n.|平顶山，台地|A mesa is an isolated, flat-topped elevation, hill, or mountain with steep sides, especially one with a cap of resistant rock.|例句：The mesa rose dramatically from the surrounding plains.  平顶山从周围的平原中陡然升起。|例句：We drove across the vast expanse of the mesa. 我们驱车穿越了广阔的平顶山。|近义词：plateau, butte, highland|反义词：valley, lowland
