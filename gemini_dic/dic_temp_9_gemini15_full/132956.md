nonemotiveness: /ˌnɒnɪˈməʊtɪvnəs/ | /ˌnɑːnɪˈmoʊtɪvnəs/ | n. |缺乏情感；冷漠；不动声色|The quality or state of being unemotional; lack of emotion or feeling; impassivity.  It describes a personality trait or a particular behavior characterized by the absence of emotional expression.|

例句：

Her nonemotiveness during the trial surprised everyone.  她的审判期间的冷漠让每个人都感到惊讶。

The doctor noted the patient's unsettling nonemotiveness. 医生注意到病人令人不安的冷漠。


近义词：apathy, impassivity, calmness, coolness, stoicism

反义词：emotionality, expressiveness, passion, feeling


词性：名词
