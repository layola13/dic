disappoints:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪnts/ | vt. |使失望；未能达到期望 | To fail to fulfill the hopes or expectations of (someone).  The film disappointed many critics.  | The movie disappointed me.  这部电影让我失望了。  The results disappointed the team.  结果让团队失望了。| pleases, satisfies | excites, pleases | v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disappoints his parents regularly. 他经常让他的父母失望。
The weather disappoints us today.  今天的天气让我们失望。


一般过去时:
The concert disappointed the audience. 音乐会让观众失望了。
His performance disappointed the judges.  他的表现让评委们失望了。


现在完成时:
The news has disappointed everyone.  这个消息让每个人都失望了。
She has disappointed me many times. 她已经让我失望很多次了。


过去完成时:
I had been disappointed many times before I finally gave up. 在我最终放弃之前，我已经失望过很多次了。
They had disappointed their coach with their lack of effort.  他们缺乏努力，让他们的教练失望了。


将来时:
The outcome will likely disappoint many voters.  结果可能会让许多选民失望。
I fear the results will disappoint us all. 我担心结果会让我们所有人失望。


虚拟语气:
I wish the results hadn't disappointed me.  我希望结果没有让我失望。
If the movie hadn't disappointed me, I would have recommended it. 如果电影没有让我失望，我会推荐它的。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
