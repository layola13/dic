non-porous: /ˌnɒnˈpɔːrəs/ | adj. | 无孔的，不透水的 | Not allowing fluids to pass through; impervious.  | Example: The non-porous surface of the countertop prevented spills from seeping in.  台面的不透水表面阻止了溢出的液体渗入。|近义词：impervious, impermeable, airtight | 反义词：porous, permeable
