geophysicist:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈfɪzɪsɪst/| n. |地球物理学家|A scientist who studies the physics of the Earth, including its composition, structure, and processes.  Geophysicists use a variety of techniques, such as seismic surveys, gravity measurements, and magnetic surveys, to study the Earth's interior and its interaction with the atmosphere and oceans. |例句：The geophysicist analyzed the seismic data to locate the earthquake's epicenter.  这位地球物理学家分析了地震数据以确定地震的震中。|近义词：geologist, earth scientist |反义词: N/A
