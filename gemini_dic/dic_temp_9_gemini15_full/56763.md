/dɒp/| /dɑːp/| n. v.|滴，滴下；落下；放弃；突然停止|to fall in drops; to let something fall; to give up something; to suddenly stop|The rain was dropping. 雨正在下。|The price of petrol has dropped recently.  汽油价格最近下降了。|rise, increase|increase, rise|

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water drops from the tap. 水从水龙头滴下来。
He drops his bag on the floor. 他把包扔到地上。


一般过去时:
She dropped her phone and broke the screen. 她掉了手机，摔坏了屏幕。
The temperature dropped sharply last night. 昨晚气温骤降。


现在完成时:
He has dropped out of the race. 他退出了比赛。
The company has dropped its prices. 公司已经降价了。


过去完成时:
They had dropped their plans to travel abroad. 他们已经放弃了出国旅行的计划。
She had dropped the subject before he could respond.  在她能回应之前，她已经不再谈论那个话题了。


将来时:
The price of oil will drop next month.  下个月油价将会下降。
They will drop him off at the station. 他们会在车站把他放下。


虚拟语气:
If I dropped my phone, I'd be very upset. 如果我掉了手机，我会非常沮丧。
I wish I hadn't dropped out of school. 我真希望我没有辍学。


被动语态:
The package was dropped off at the post office. 包裹已在邮局寄出。
The plan was dropped because of funding issues.  由于资金问题，该计划被放弃了。


疑问句:
Did you drop the ball? 你把球掉了？
Will they drop the charges? 他们会撤销指控吗？


否定句:
He didn't drop the secret. 他没有泄露秘密。
The government will not drop its efforts to combat crime. 政府不会放弃打击犯罪的努力。


PHRASE->
drop by:顺便拜访
drop out:退出
drop off:下降；送；睡着
drop in:顺便访问
drop a hint:暗示
