epitrochoid:/ˈɛpɪtrəˌkɔɪd/| n. |外摆线|A curve traced by a point on a circle that rolls around the outside of another circle.  It is a generalization of an epicycloid, where the tracing point is not necessarily on the circumference of the rolling circle.|The design of some gears utilizes epitrochoids.|近义词：epicycloid (when the tracing point is on the rolling circle)|反义词：hypotrochoid
