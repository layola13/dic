rejumble:/rɪˈdʒʌmbəl/| vt.|重新排列；打乱；弄乱|To rearrange (something) in a disorganized or haphazard manner.|例句：The children rejumbled the toys after playing with them. 孩子们玩完玩具后把它们弄乱了。  例句：He rejumbled the papers on his desk. 他把办公桌上的文件弄乱了。|近义词：scramble, jumble, muddle|反义词：organize, arrange, order|动词


USAGE->

He rejumbles the letters in a word game. 他在一个文字游戏中重新排列字母。
They rejumble the furniture in the living room. 他们重新排列客厅的家具。
