modernises: /ˈmɒdənaɪzɪz/ /ˈmɑːdərnʌɪzɪz/ | v. | 使现代化；现代化改造 | To make something more modern.  |The city is modernising its public transport system.  这个城市正在对其公共交通系统进行现代化改造。| upgrade, renovate, update |  traditionalize,  antiquate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company modernises its products regularly. = 公司定期对其产品进行现代化改造。
He modernises his teaching methods every year. = 他每年都更新他的教学方法。

一般过去时:
They modernised the factory last year. = 他们去年对工厂进行了现代化改造。
She modernised her kitchen with new appliances. = 她用新的电器使她的厨房现代化了。

现在完成时:
The government has modernised the infrastructure significantly. = 政府已经对基础设施进行了重大现代化改造。
They have modernised their website to improve user experience. = 他们已经对他们的网站进行了现代化改造以改善用户体验。

过去完成时:
They had modernised the building before the new tenants moved in. = 新租户搬进来之前，他们已经对大楼进行了现代化改造。
She had modernised her wardrobe before the fashion show. = 时装秀之前，她已经把衣橱更新换代了。

将来时:
The company will modernise its production line next year. = 公司明年将对其生产线进行现代化改造。
They will modernise the airport to handle more passengers. = 他们将对机场进行现代化改造以处理更多乘客。


虚拟语气:
If they modernised the system, it would be more efficient. = 如果他们对系统进行现代化改造，它将更高效。
I wish they had modernised the software sooner. = 我希望他们早点对软件进行现代化改造。

被动语态:
The building is being modernised. = 大楼正在进行现代化改造。
The software will be modernised next month. = 软件将于下个月进行现代化改造。

疑问句:
Will the company modernise its operations? = 公司会对其运营进行现代化改造吗？
Have they modernised the equipment yet? = 他们已经对设备进行现代化改造了吗？

否定句:
The company did not modernise its facilities. = 公司没有对其设施进行现代化改造。
They haven't modernised their processes yet. = 他们还没有对其流程进行现代化改造。
