chargesheet:/ˈtʃɑːrdʒʃiːt/| n. |指控书，控诉书|A formal document listing the charges against someone.  It's typically used in legal proceedings to outline the accusations and evidence against the accused. |例句：The chargesheet detailed the various allegations of fraud against the company director. 指控书详细列明了针对公司董事的各种欺诈指控。|例句：The police submitted the chargesheet to the court. 警方将起诉书提交给了法院。|近义词：indictment, accusation, complaint|反义词：acquittal, exoneration
