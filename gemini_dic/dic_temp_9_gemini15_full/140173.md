offensives:/əˈfɛnsɪvz/ | n. | 进攻；攻势；攻击行动 | A planned and coordinated military attack.  | 例句：The allied forces launched a series of offensives against the enemy. 盟军对敌人发起了系列进攻。

例句：The general planned a major offensive to recapture the lost territory.  将军计划发起一次大规模进攻以夺回失地。


近义词：attacks, assaults, campaigns, drives | 反义词：defenses, retreats


词源：源自法语“offensive”，意思是“进攻的”，来自拉丁语“offendere”，意思是“攻击，冒犯”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The army conducts regular offensives. = 军队进行例行进攻。
Their offensives target key enemy positions. = 他们的进攻目标是敌人的关键阵地。


一般过去时:
The general launched a surprise offensive. = 将军发起了突然袭击。
The enemy's offensives failed to achieve their objectives. = 敌人的进攻未能实现其目标。


现在完成时:
They have conducted numerous offensives this year. = 他们今年已经进行了多次进攻。
The rebels have launched several offensives against the government. = 反叛分子对政府发起了数次进攻。


过去完成时:
Before the main offensive, they had conducted several smaller attacks. = 在主要进攻之前，他们进行了几次较小的攻击。
The army had completed its preparations before the offensive began. = 在进攻开始之前，军队已经完成了准备工作。


将来时:
The military will launch a large-scale offensive next month. = 军方将于下个月发起大规模进攻。
We anticipate that the enemy will launch several offensives in the coming weeks. = 我们预计敌人将在未来几周内发起几次进攻。


虚拟语气:
If they had launched the offensive earlier, they might have been successful. = 如果他们早些发动进攻，他们可能会成功。
I wish we had launched a more effective offensive. = 我希望我们发动一次更有成效的进攻。


被动语态:
The city was targeted in several offensives. = 这个城市成为几次进攻的目标。
The offensive was planned meticulously. = 这次进攻计划周密。


疑问句:
When will the next offensive begin? = 下一次进攻什么时候开始？
What are the objectives of this offensive? = 这次进攻的目标是什么？


否定句:
The offensive did not achieve its intended goals. = 这次进攻没有达到预期的目标。
They did not launch an offensive this week. = 他们本周没有发起进攻。
