embarrassed:/ɪmˈbærəst/| adj. |尴尬的，窘迫的 |Feeling ashamed, shy, or awkward, especially because of a mistake or something unexpected.  |例句：I was embarrassed when I tripped and fell in front of everyone. 我当着所有人的面绊倒摔倒了，感到很尴尬。
例句：She felt embarrassed by her son's rude behavior. 她因为儿子的粗鲁行为感到尴尬。|近义词：ashamed, shy, awkward, humiliated |反义词：proud, confident, comfortable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is embarrassed by his mistake. 他为自己的错误感到尴尬。
I am embarrassed to ask for help. 我不好意思求助。


一般过去时:
She was embarrassed after spilling coffee on her boss. 她把咖啡洒在老板身上后感到很尴尬。
They were embarrassed by the unexpected guest. 他们因为意外的客人感到很尴尬。


现在完成时:
I have been embarrassed many times in my life. 我一生中多次感到尴尬。
He has never been so embarrassed before. 他以前从未如此尴尬过。


过去完成时:
I had already been embarrassed before he even started speaking.  在他开始说话之前我就已经感到尴尬了。
She had been embarrassed numerous times in her career.  在她职业生涯中，她多次感到尴尬。


将来时:
I will be embarrassed if I forget his name. 如果我忘记他的名字，我会很尴尬。
They will be embarrassed if their secret is revealed. 如果他们的秘密被揭露，他们会很尴尬。


虚拟语气:
I would be embarrassed if I were in his shoes. 如果我处在他的位置，我会很尴尬。
If I had known, I wouldn't have been embarrassed. 如果我知道的话，我就不会尴尬了。


被动语态:
He was embarrassed by his own clumsiness. 他因为自己的笨拙而感到尴尬。(被动语态较少用于此词)
She was embarrassed to be the center of attention. 她因为成为众人关注的焦点而感到尴尬。(被动语态较少用于此词)


疑问句:
Are you embarrassed by your actions? 你为你的行为感到尴尬吗？
Was he embarrassed by the situation? 他当时因为这种情况而感到尴尬吗？


否定句:
I wasn't embarrassed at all. 我一点也不尴尬。
He wasn't embarrassed to admit his mistake. 他毫不尴尬地承认了自己的错误。


PHRASE->
feel embarrassed 感到尴尬
be embarrassed about 对...感到尴尬
deeply embarrassed  非常尴尬
slightly embarrassed 有点尴尬
embarrassed silence 尴尬的沉默
