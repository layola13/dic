dissoluble:/dɪˈsɒljʊbəl/| adj. |可溶解的，可解散的 |Capable of being dissolved; able to be broken up or ended.  This refers to the ability of a substance to dissolve in a liquid or the ability of a group, organization, or agreement to be broken up or terminated. | Example: Sugar is dissoluble in water.  糖溶于水。Salt is dissoluble in most liquids. 盐在大多数液体中都可溶解。|近义词：soluble, solvable, dissolvable |反义词：insoluble, indissoluble


USAGE->

This sugar is dissoluble in hot water. 这糖在热水中可溶解。
The contract is dissoluble by mutual agreement.  这份合同可以通过双方协议解除。
