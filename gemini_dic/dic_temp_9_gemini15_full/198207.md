shortbreads:/ˈʃɔːrtˌbrɛdz/| n. |酥饼(复数)|Shortbreads are crumbly, buttery biscuits, typically sweet and made with flour, butter, and sugar. They are often served with tea or coffee.  |例句：These shortbreads are melt-in-your-mouth delicious. 这些酥饼入口即化，美味极了。

例句：She baked a batch of delicious shortbreads for the tea party. 她为茶话会烘焙了一批美味的酥饼。|近义词：biscuits, cookies, crackers|反义词：无
