skeuomorphic:/ˌskjuːəˈmɔːrfɪk/| adj.|拟态的；仿真的|  Relating to or denoting a design principle in which digital elements mimic their real-world counterparts in appearance.  This creates a familiar and intuitive user experience, but can be criticized for being inauthentic or outdated.|The app's skeuomorphic design used realistic-looking notebook paper for its interface.  这个应用程序的拟态设计在其界面中使用了逼真的笔记本纸。|近义词：realistic, imitative|反义词：flat, minimalist
