insconce:/ɪnˈskɒns/| vt.|安置；隐藏；使安全|To fix firmly or securely in place; to hide or shelter securely.|The painting was insconced in a protective case.  这幅画被安放在一个保护套里。

He insconced himself in his study for the afternoon. 他一下午都把自己关在书房里。


近义词：hide, conceal, embed, secure, install|反义词：expose, reveal, uncover|动词
