flowered:/ˈflaʊərd/ | adj. |开花的；繁茂的；装饰有花的 | Adorned with flowers; having many flowers; flourishing.  |例句：The flowered meadow was a sight to behold.  开满鲜花的草地令人叹为观止。

The garden was flowered with colorful blooms. 花园里开满了五颜六色的花朵。|近义词：blooming, blossoming, ornate |反义词：barren, desolate
