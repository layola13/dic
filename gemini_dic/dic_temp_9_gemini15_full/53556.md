disbarment: /dɪsˈbɑːrmənt/ | n. |律师除名；取消律师资格 | The act of officially removing a lawyer's right to practice law.  This typically happens as a result of serious misconduct or unethical behavior. | Example: His disbarment was a result of his fraudulent activities.  他的律师资格被取消是因为他的欺诈行为。  | 近义词：removal from the bar, disqualification | 反义词：admission to the bar
