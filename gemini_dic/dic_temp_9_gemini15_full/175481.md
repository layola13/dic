rampantly:/ˈræmpəntli/| adv.| 猖獗地；蔓延地；无约束地 |  In a violent or uncontrolled way; excessively.  It suggests a lack of restraint or control, often implying negative consequences. | 例句：Weed grew rampantly across the neglected garden.  杂草在被忽视的花园里疯狂生长。
The rumors spread rampantly throughout the town. 谣言在城镇中迅速蔓延。| 近义词：uncontrollably, wildly, profusely, excessively | 反义词：controlledly, moderately, sparingly
