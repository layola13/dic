expatriations:/ˌɛkspætrieɪʃənz/ | n. | 移居国外；流亡；侨居 | The act of leaving one's native country to live in another; the state of being an expatriate.  It often implies a voluntary choice, but can also refer to forced exile. |例句：The political unrest led to mass expatriations from the country.  政治动荡导致该国大规模外逃。

例句：His expatriations to France marked a new chapter in his life. 他移居法国标志着他人生的新篇章。


近义词：emigration, exile, migration


反义词：repatriation, immigration
