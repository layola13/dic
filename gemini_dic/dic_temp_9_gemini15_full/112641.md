lowball:/ˈloʊbɔːl/|/ˈləʊbɔːl/| vt. n.|压低价格；低价出价；低估|to offer a price far lower than is considered reasonable or appropriate,  or to underestimate something significantly.|例句：The contractor tried to lowball the price on the renovation. = 承包商试图压低装修价格。

例句：They lowballed the value of my antique chair. = 他们低估了我古董椅子的价值。


近义词：underbid, undervalue, underestimate, shortchange


反义词：overbid, overvalue, overestimate


词性：动词，名词
