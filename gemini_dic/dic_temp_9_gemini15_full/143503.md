outglowing:/ˌaʊtˈɡloʊɪŋ/ | adj. | 超过…发光的；比…更明亮的 | Shining or glowing more brightly than something else.  |The outglowing stars filled the night sky.  夜空中闪耀的星星布满了夜空。| brighter, more radiant | dimmer, less bright | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun outglows the moon. = 太阳比月亮更亮。
This diamond outglows all the others. = 这颗钻石比其他所有钻石都更闪亮。


一般过去时:
The fire outglowed the candles. = 火光比烛光更亮。
Her smile outglowed the darkness. = 她的笑容比黑暗更明亮。


现在完成时:
The city lights have outglowed the stars tonight. = 今晚城市灯光的光芒超过了星星。
His achievements have outglowed his expectations.  他的成就已超过了他的预期。(此处outglow引申为超越)


过去完成时:
The setting sun had outglowed the rising moon. = 落日的光芒曾比升起的月亮更亮。
The old painting had outglowed all the new ones. = 那幅旧画曾比所有新画都更鲜艳。(此处outglow引申为超越)


将来时:
The new stadium lights will outglow the old ones. = 新体育场灯光将比旧的更亮。
The new technology will outglow the previous versions. = 新技术将超越之前的版本。(此处outglow引申为超越)


虚拟语气:
If the sun were closer, it would outglow all other stars. = 如果太阳更近，它将比所有其他恒星都更明亮。
I wish my creativity could outglow my fears. = 我希望我的创造力能够战胜我的恐惧。(此处outglow引申为战胜)


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
The dim lamp didn't outglow the surrounding darkness. = 暗淡的灯无法照亮周围的黑暗。
His efforts didn't outglow his shortcomings. = 他的努力没有掩盖他的缺点。(此处outglow引申为掩盖)
