deemer:/ˈdiːmər/| n. |判定者；认为者；估价者 |One who deems; one who judges or considers.  A deemer is someone who makes a judgment or assessment, often based on limited information or inference.  |例句：The judge acted as the deemer of the case.  法官充当了此案的判定者。

例句：The appraiser acted as the deemer of the property's value. 评估师充当了房产价值的估价者。


近义词：judge, assessor, evaluator, arbiter |反义词：None directly opposite, but antonyms could include those who blindly accept without judgment. |词性：名词
