fructure:/ˈfrʌktʃər/| n. |骨折；断裂；破裂 |The breaking of a hard object or material.  A fracture is a break in a bone or other hard tissue. |例句：The accident resulted in a fracture of his leg.  事故导致了他的腿骨折。|例句：The earthquake caused numerous fractures in the earth's surface. 地震在地表造成了许多裂缝。|近义词：break, rupture, crack, fissure |反义词：integrity, wholeness


USAGE->

The bone fractures easily. = 骨头很容易骨折。
