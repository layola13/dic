rebalance:/ˌriːˈbæləns/ | vt. n. |重新平衡，再平衡 |To restore balance to something; to adjust the proportions of something to achieve a more desirable distribution.  |例句：The portfolio needs rebalancing to reduce risk.  投资组合需要重新平衡以降低风险。

The company is rebalancing its product line to focus on higher-margin items.  该公司正在调整其产品线，以专注于利润率更高的产品。


近义词： readjust, realign, adjust,  re-proportion | 反义词：imbalance, unbalance | 词性:动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rebalance their investment portfolio annually. = 他们每年都会重新平衡他们的投资组合。
The government rebalances the budget every fiscal year. = 政府每年财政年度都会重新平衡预算。


一般过去时:
The fund manager rebalanced the portfolio after the market crash. = 市场崩盘后，基金经理重新平衡了投资组合。
She rebalanced her life priorities after having children. = 她生孩子后重新平衡了她生活中的优先事项。


现在完成时:
They have rebalanced their portfolio several times this year. = 他们今年已经几次重新平衡了他们的投资组合。
The company has rebalanced its workforce to better meet customer demand. = 公司已经调整了员工队伍，以更好地满足客户需求。


过去完成时:
He had rebalanced his budget before the unexpected expense. = 在发生意外开支之前，他已经重新平衡了他的预算。
She had rebalanced her work-life balance before taking on the new project. = 在承担新的项目之前，她已经重新平衡了她的工作与生活。


将来时:
We will rebalance the portfolio next quarter. = 我们将在下个季度重新平衡投资组合。
The team will rebalance the website's design to improve user experience. = 团队将重新平衡网站的设计以改善用户体验。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would rebalance my investments immediately. = 如果我是你，我会立即重新平衡我的投资。
It is vital that we rebalance the budget to avoid a deficit. = 至关重要的是，我们必须重新平衡预算以避免赤字。


被动语态:
