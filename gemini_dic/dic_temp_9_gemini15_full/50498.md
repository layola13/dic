depressurizers: /ˌdiːˈprɛʃəraɪzərz/ | n. | 减压器，泄压器 | Devices or systems used to reduce pressure, often in enclosed spaces or systems.  They are crucial for safety and controlled pressure release in various applications. | Example: The aircraft's depressurizers malfunctioned, causing a rapid drop in cabin pressure.  飞机的减压器发生故障，导致客舱压力迅速下降。 The submarine uses depressurizers to safely release excess pressure during deep dives. 潜艇在深潜过程中使用减压器安全地释放多余压力。| 近义词: pressure relief valves, pressure regulators | 反义词: pressurizers

词源:
The word "depressurizers" is formed from "depressurize" (to reduce pressure) + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Depressurize" itself comes from "de-" (away from) + "pressure" + "-ize" (to make or become).  Therefore, the word's roots lie in the concepts of removing or reducing pressure.
