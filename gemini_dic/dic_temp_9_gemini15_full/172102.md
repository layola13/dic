purposed:/ˈpɜːrpəst/ | adj. | 有目的的；故意的；预定的 | Having a clear aim or purpose; intentional.  |例句：He made a purposed effort to be more friendly. 他特意努力变得更友善。|近义词：intentional, deliberate, designed | 反义词：accidental, unintentional, unplanned

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company purposed to expand its market share. = 公司计划扩大其市场份额。
His purposed actions show his determination. = 他有目的的行为显示了他的决心。

一般过去时:
They purposed to build a new school last year. = 他们去年计划建造一所新学校。
She purposed to finish the project by Friday. = 她打算在周五之前完成这个项目。

现在完成时:
We have purposed to donate to the charity. = 我们已经计划向慈善机构捐款。
They have purposed to change their business strategy. = 他们已经计划改变他们的商业战略。

过去完成时:
He had purposed to visit his parents, but he was too busy. = 他本来打算去看望父母，但他太忙了。
She had purposed to go to the concert, but she got sick. = 她本来打算去听音乐会，但她病了。


将来时:
They will purpose to launch a new product next month. = 他们计划下个月推出新产品。
We will purpose to address the issues at the next meeting. = 我们将在下一次会议上着手解决这些问题。


虚拟语气:
If they had purposed to act sooner, the outcome might have been different. = 如果他们早点行动，结果可能就不同了。
I wish they had purposed to help us. = 我希望他们本来打算帮助我们。


被动语态:
A new strategy was purposed by the management team. = 新战略是由管理团队制定的。
The changes were purposed to improve efficiency. = 这些变化是为了提高效率而制定的。


疑问句:

What was purposed to be achieved by this project? = 这个项目的预期目标是什么？


否定句:
His actions were not purposed; they were accidental. = 他的行为并非有意的；它们是偶然的。
The company did not purpose to harm anyone. = 公司并非有意伤害任何人。
