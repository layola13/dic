in-her-stocking-feet: /ɪn hɜːr ˈstɒkɪŋ fiːt/ | adj. phrase |光着脚，穿着袜子 |  Describes someone who is wearing only socks on their feet, without shoes.  This phrase emphasizes the lack of footwear. | Example: She tiptoed into the room in her stocking feet. 她光着脚尖着地走进房间。 | 近义词: barefoot (but barefoot implies no socks), sock-footed | 反义词: shod, in shoes


USAGE->
This phrase is primarily adjectival and doesn't have verb conjugations like "to be" or "to have". It describes a state of being. Therefore, tense conjugation examples are not applicable.


PHRASE->

dance in one's stocking feet = 光着脚跳舞
creep in one's stocking feet = 踮着脚走
