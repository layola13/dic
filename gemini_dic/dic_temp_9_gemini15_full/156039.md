photomontage:/ˈfoʊtoʊmɒ̃ˈtɑːʒ/|/ˈfəʊtəʊmɒnˈtɑːʒ/| n. |照片蒙太奇；照片拼贴画 |A photomontage is a composite photograph created by assembling, juxtaposing, and combining different photographs into a single image.  It often serves a narrative or artistic purpose. |The artist created a powerful photomontage depicting the horrors of war.  这位艺术家创作了一幅强大的照片蒙太奇，描绘了战争的恐怖。|collage, montage |(近义词无明确反义词)|名词
