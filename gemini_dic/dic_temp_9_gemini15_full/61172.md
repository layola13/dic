elucidator: /ɪˈluːsɪdeɪtər/ | n. |阐明者；解释者 | A person or thing that elucidates; someone or something that makes something clear.  | The professor served as an elucidator of complex scientific theories.  这位教授充当了复杂科学理论的阐明者。 | explainer, interpreter, clarifier | obfuscator, confuser |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The elucidator explains the concepts clearly. = 阐明者清晰地解释了这些概念。
This device elucidates the problem efficiently. = 这个装置有效地阐明了这个问题。


一般过去时:
The speaker elucidated the historical context. = 演讲者阐明了历史背景。
The article elucidated the author's intentions. = 这篇文章阐明了作者的意图。


现在完成时:
He has elucidated the mystery successfully. = 他已经成功地阐明了这个谜团。
She has elucidated many difficult points in the text. = 她已经阐明了文本中许多难点。


过去完成时:
They had elucidated the problem before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经阐明了这个问题。
The scientists had elucidated the mechanism of the disease. = 科学家们已经阐明了这种疾病的机制。


将来时:
The researcher will elucidate the findings in his presentation. = 研究人员将在他的报告中阐明这些发现。
The expert will elucidate the new policy to the public. = 专家将向公众阐明这项新政策。


虚拟语气:
If he were a better elucidator, more people would understand. = 如果他是一位更好的阐明者，更多的人就会理解。
I wish the instructions were more elucidatory; I would understand better. = 我希望说明更清晰一些；那样我就能更好地理解。


被动语态:
The theory was elucidated by the leading scientist. = 这一理论是由一位领先的科学家阐明的。
The meaning of the poem was elucidated in the introduction. = 这首诗的含义在引言中得到了阐明。


疑问句:
Who elucidated the mystery of the missing artifact?  = 谁阐明了失踪文物之谜？
What method will be used to elucidate the data? = 将采用什么方法来阐明这些数据？


否定句:
He did not elucidate his reasons for leaving. = 他没有阐明他离开的原因。
The document failed to elucidate the key issues. = 这份文件未能阐明关键问题。


PHRASE->
elucidator of facts = 事实的阐明者
elucidator of principles = 原则的阐明者
elucidator of the problem = 问题的阐明者
a clear elucidator = 一个清晰的阐明者
an effective elucidator = 一个有效的阐明者

词源:  From the verb "elucidate," which comes from Latin *ēlūcīdāre, from ē- "out" + lūcīdāre "to make light," from lūx, lūcis "light."  Therefore, the word's roots point to the act of bringing something out of darkness into the light, making it clear and understandable.
