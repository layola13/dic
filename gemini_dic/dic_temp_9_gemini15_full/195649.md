semirespectability:/ˌsɛmiˌrɛspɛktəˈbɪləti/| n. |半体面；勉强体面；似是而非的体面|The state or quality of being only partially respectable; a condition of being neither fully respectable nor entirely disreputable. It often implies a veneer of respectability masking a less savory reality.  This term is often used to describe social groups or individuals who attempt to conform to societal norms but fall short, or who achieve a level of success that is insufficient to fully erase their past or lower-class origins.|例句：Despite their attempts at respectability, their questionable business dealings hinted at a underlying semirespectability.|近义词：respectability, quasi-respectability, marginal respectability|反义词：disrepute, disrespectability
