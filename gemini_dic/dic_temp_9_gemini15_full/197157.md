shanked:/ʃæŋkt/| v.| 踢偏；用力踢；拙劣地踢；(俚语) 失败； |To kick (something) awkwardly or badly, often missing the intended target.  It can also refer to a failure or a badly executed action.|例句：He shanked the ball completely, sending it into the rough. 他把球踢得很糟糕，踢进了粗糙的地面。

He completely shanked his attempt at the free throw. 他完全把罚球踢偏了。


近义词： botched, muffed, mishit, flubbed |反义词：aced, nailed, mastered


USAGE->

He shanks the ball every time he tries to kick it long. = 他每次试图远距离踢球都会踢偏。
She shanks her golf shots consistently. = 她高尔夫球总是打偏。


一般过去时:

过去完成时:

They had shanked the plan so badly that it had to be completely overhauled. = 他们把计划搞砸了，以至于不得不彻底修改。
