caking:/ˈkeɪkɪŋ/| n.|结块，成块状|The formation of lumps or a solid mass, often due to the loss of moisture or the binding of particles.|例句：The flour showed signs of caking after being stored in a humid environment. 面粉在潮湿的环境中储存后出现了结块的迹象。  The caking of the sugar prevented the cake from baking properly. 糖结块导致蛋糕无法正常烘焙。|近义词：clumping, lumping, agglomeration|反义词：disintegration, crumbling

一般现在时:
The sugar is caking in the bowl. = 糖在碗里结块了。
This powder cakes easily. = 这种粉末容易结块。

一般过去时:
