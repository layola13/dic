roseal:/ˈroʊziəl/| adj.|玫瑰色的，玫瑰红的|Roseal refers to the color of a rose, a pinkish-red hue.  It is often used to describe something having a delicate, soft pink-red color.|The roseal sunset painted the sky in vibrant hues.|近义词：rosy, pinkish-red, rose-colored|反义词：pale, white, blue
