excambium:/ɪkˈskeɪmbiəm/| n.|交换；互换；(植物)形成层|The word "excambium" refers to the process of exchanging something, often goods or services.  In botany, it specifically denotes the cambium layer in a plant stem or root, responsible for secondary growth.|例句：The treaty facilitated an excambium of land between the two countries.  这项条约促进了两国之间的土地互换。|近义词：exchange, barter, swap, interchange|反义词：retention, keeping
