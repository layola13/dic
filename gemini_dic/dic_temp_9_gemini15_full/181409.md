reitemizing:/ˌriˈaɪtəmaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |重新列举，重新细分 | The act of listing items again, often with more detail or in a different order.  It implies a previous listing that is being revised or expanded upon. | Example:  The accountant is reitemizing the expenses to clarify the budget. 会计正在重新列举开支以明确预算。 | 近义词：relisting, reclassifying, detailing | 反义词：summarizing, consolidating


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reitemizes his expenses every month. = 他每月都重新列举他的开支。
The teacher reitemizes the students' scores for a clearer understanding. = 老师重新列举学生的成绩以便更清晰地理解。


一般过去时:
She reitemized the inventory after the audit. = 审计之后，她重新列举了库存清单。
They reitemized the contract terms to avoid any ambiguity. = 他们重新列举了合同条款以避免任何歧义。


现在完成时:
The company has reitemized its assets for the annual report. = 公司已为年度报告重新列举了其资产。
He has reitemized his travel expenses for reimbursement. = 他已经重新列举了他的差旅费以申请报销。


过去完成时:
After the merger, they had reitemized all the combined company's assets. = 合并之后，他们已经重新列举了合并公司所有的资产。
Before submitting the proposal, she had reitemized the project's milestones. = 在提交提案之前，她已经重新列举了项目的里程碑。


将来时:
The team will reitemize the project budget next week. = 团队下周将重新列举项目预算。
We will reitemize the survey results for a more comprehensive analysis. = 我们将重新列举调查结果以便进行更全面的分析。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would reitemize the data. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新列举数据。
It would be helpful if they reitemized the contributions by department. = 如果他们按部门重新列举捐款，将会有所帮助。


被动语态:
The expenses were reitemized by the finance department. = 开支由财务部门重新列举。

疑问句:

否定句:
