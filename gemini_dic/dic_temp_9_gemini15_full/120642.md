mis-practiced:/ˌmɪsˈpræktɪst/| adj.| 练习不足的；未熟练掌握的 |  Describes something that has been practiced incorrectly or insufficiently, leading to a lack of proficiency or skill. It implies a deficiency in the quality or effectiveness of the practice. |The pianist's mis-practiced scales were evident in her shaky performance.  钢琴家的音阶练习不足，在她颤抖的演奏中显而易见。 | poorly practiced, inadequately trained, unskilled | proficient, skilled, expert |adj.
