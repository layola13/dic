mendicate:/ˈmɛndɪkeɪt/| vt.|乞讨；讨要；请求|To beg; to ask for alms.  The word implies a persistent and often importunate manner of begging.|例句：He was forced to mendicate on the streets after losing his job. 他失业后被迫在街上乞讨。|例句：She mendicated for help from the charity organization. 她向慈善组织请求帮助。|近义词：beg, solicit, implore|反义词：give, donate, bestow|动词
