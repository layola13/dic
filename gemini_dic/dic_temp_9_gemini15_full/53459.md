disapproves:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːvz/ | v. | 不赞成；反对 | To have or express an unfavorable opinion of; to disapprove of something.  |例句：My parents disapproved of my choice of career. 我的父母不赞成我的职业选择。|例句：She disapproves strongly of smoking in public. 她强烈反对在公共场所吸烟。|近义词：object to, oppose, condemn, disfavor |反义词：approve, endorse, sanction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He disapproves of gambling. = 他不赞成赌博。
She disapproves of her son's girlfriend. = 她不赞成她儿子的女朋友。

一般过去时:
The teacher disapproved of the student's behavior. = 老师不赞成学生的行径。
They disapproved of the new policy. = 他们不赞成这项新政策。

现在完成时:
He has disapproved of this plan from the beginning. = 从一开始他就反对这个计划。
She has disapproved of his actions repeatedly. = 她一再反对他的行为。

过去完成时:
They had disapproved of the project before it even started. = 在项目开始之前，他们就已经反对了。
He had disapproved of her marriage long before the wedding. = 早在婚礼之前，他就反对她的婚姻了。

将来时:
I think he will disapprove of my decision. = 我认为他会反对我的决定。
She will disapprove of the changes to the curriculum. = 她会反对课程的更改。

虚拟语气:
If I told my parents, they would disapprove. = 如果我告诉我的父母，他们会反对。
I wish he didn't disapprove of my lifestyle. = 我希望他不反对我的生活方式。

被动语态:
His ideas were disapproved of by the committee. = 他的想法遭到委员会的反对。
The proposal was disapproved of by the board of directors. = 该提案遭到董事会的反对。

疑问句:
Does she disapprove of my new haircut? = 她不赞成我的新发型吗？
Do your parents disapprove of your relationship? = 你父母反对你的恋情吗？

否定句:
My boss doesn't disapprove of my working from home. = 我的老板不反对我远程工作。
They didn't disapprove of the suggestion. = 他们没有反对这个建议。
