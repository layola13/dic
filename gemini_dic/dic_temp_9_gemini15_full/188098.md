roundfish:/ˈraʊndfɪʃ/| n. |圆鱼，辐鳍鱼类|A roundfish is a fish that has a relatively deep, laterally compressed body, often with a rounded profile.  The term is not a strictly scientific classification, but rather a descriptive term often used for certain types of fish, particularly those that are commercially important.  Many, but not all, roundfish are members of the Perciformes order. |例句：Tuna and salmon are examples of roundfish.  金枪鱼和鲑鱼是圆鱼的例子。|例句：Many roundfish are popular choices for sushi. 许多圆鱼是寿司的热门选择。|近义词：finfish, bony fish |反义词：flatfish (in contrast to body shape)
