shafted:/ʃæftɪd/ | v. | 欺骗；剥削；破坏；(非正式)性交 | To treat someone unfairly or dishonestly, often by depriving them of something they deserve or have been promised; to damage or destroy something;  (informal) to have sexual intercourse with. | 例句：He felt shafted by the company after they reneged on their promise. 他在公司违反承诺后感觉被欺骗了。  They completely shafted the project with their incompetence. 他们因无能而彻底破坏了这个项目。| 近义词：cheat, swindle, defraud, screw (informal),  rip off (informal) | 反义词：help, assist, benefit
