impregn:/ɪmˈprɛɡn/ | vt. | 使怀孕；充满；使饱和 | To make (a woman or female animal) pregnant; to fill or saturate something completely. | 例句：The farmer impregnated his cows.  = 这位农民让他的母牛怀孕了。  例句：The air was impregnated with the scent of roses. = 空气中弥漫着玫瑰的香味。 | 近义词：fertilize, saturate, imbue | 反义词：empty, deplete | 动词
