heartbroken:/ˈhɑːrtˌbroʊkən/|/ˈhɑːtˌbrəʊkən/| adj. |心碎的；悲痛欲绝的 |Extremely sad because of loss, disappointment, or grief.  The adjective describes a state of intense emotional pain caused by a significant loss, often romantic but applicable to other losses as well. |例句：She was heartbroken after the death of her beloved pet. 她心爱的宠物去世后，她悲痛欲绝。

例句：He was heartbroken when his girlfriend left him.  他的女朋友离开他后，他心碎了。


近义词：devastated, grief-stricken, despairing, inconsolable, desolate

反义词：joyful, elated, happy, cheerful, delighted


词性：形容词
