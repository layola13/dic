inhalers:/ɪnˈheɪlər/| n. |吸入器|A device used to inhale medication, typically for respiratory conditions.  Inhalers deliver medication in the form of a fine mist or powder directly to the lungs.|例句：She uses an inhaler to treat her asthma. 她用吸入器治疗哮喘。|例句：The doctor prescribed a new inhaler for his bronchitis. 医生给他开了新的支气管炎吸入器。|近义词：nebulizer|反义词:N/A|名词
