schemer:/ˈskiːmər/ | n. | 阴谋家；策划者 | A schemer is a person who makes plans secretly, especially plans to do something dishonest or illegal.  They are often characterized by cunning and deceit. | Example: He was a ruthless schemer, always looking for ways to gain an advantage over others.  他是一个无情的阴谋家，总是寻找方法来胜过他人。 |近义词：conspirator, plotter, intriguer | 反义词：idealist, honest person | 名词
