minim:/ˈmɪnɪm/| n. |极少量；最小单位；（古）最小音符|A very small amount or quantity; the smallest unit of something.  In older music notation, a minim was a half note.|例句：He added just a minim of salt to the dish. 他只在菜里加了一点点盐。

例句：The composer used a lot of minims in the piece.  作曲家在这首乐曲中使用了许多最小音符。

近义词：drop, bit, iota, jot, trace,  (for musical note) half note

反义词：abundance, plenty, maximum, whole note (for musical note)

词性:名词
