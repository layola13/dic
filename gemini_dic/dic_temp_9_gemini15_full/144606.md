outweighing: /ˌaʊtˈweɪɪŋ/ | v. |  超过；胜过；比…重要 |  To be greater than something else in weight, importance, or value.  |例句：The benefits of the project outweigh the costs.  这个项目的益处超过了成本。

The potential risks outweigh the potential rewards.  潜在的风险超过了潜在的回报。

近义词：exceed, surpass, eclipse, preponderate | 反义词：underweigh


词源:

* **out-**:  prefix indicating "surpassing," "exceeding," or "beyond."  (源自古英语)
* **weigh**:  verb meaning "to measure the weight of,"  from Old English *wegan*, related to Old High German *wegan* "to carry, weigh," and ultimately from Proto-Germanic *weghan*.


用法说明：

Outweighing is the present participle of the verb "outweigh."  It's often used as an adjective or part of a verb phrase.
