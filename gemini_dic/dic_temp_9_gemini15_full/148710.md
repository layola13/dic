overtop:/ˌoʊvərˈtɑːp/ | vt. | 超过；盖过；胜过 | To extend beyond or rise above the top of; surpass or exceed.  |例句：The trees overtopped the house. 树木高过房屋。

The new skyscraper overtops all the other buildings in the city.  这座新摩天大楼超过了城市里所有其他的建筑物。

近义词：surpass, exceed, overshadow, outstrip | 反义词：underneath, below, subordinate


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The sunflowers overtop the fence. 向日葵高出篱笆。
The mountain overtops the surrounding hills. 这座山比周围的山丘高。


一般过去时:
The waves overtopped the seawall. 海浪冲过了海堤。
The floodwaters overtopped the riverbanks. 洪水淹没了河岸。


现在完成时:
The weeds have overtopped the young plants. 杂草已经长过了幼苗。
The vines have overtopped the trellis. 藤蔓已经爬过了棚架。


过去完成时:
The snow had overtopped the doorway before the snowplow arrived. 除雪车到达之前，积雪已经堆到了门口上方。
The river had overtopped its banks before the dam broke. 大坝决堤之前，河水已经漫过了河岸。


将来时:
The rising tide will overtop the beach. 上涨的潮水将漫过海滩。
The growing tree will eventually overtop the house. 这棵不断生长的树最终将超过房屋的高度。


虚拟语气:
If the river rose higher, it would overtop the bridge. 如果河水涨得更高，它就会漫过桥面。
If the plants grew faster, they would overtop the fence. 如果植物长得更快，它们就会超过篱笆。


被动语态:
The house was overtopped by the trees. 房屋被树木遮盖住了。
The small plants were overtopped by the taller weeds. 小植物被更高的杂草遮盖住了。


疑问句:
Does the building overtop the others nearby? 这栋建筑物比附近其他建筑物高吗？
Will the floodwaters overtop the levee? 洪水会漫过防洪堤吗？


否定句:
The shrubs did not overtop the hedge.灌木丛没有超过树篱。
The younger trees did not overtop the older ones. 年轻的树木没有超过老树木。
