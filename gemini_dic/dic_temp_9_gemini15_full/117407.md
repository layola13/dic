medicinal:/məˈdɪsɪnəl/ | adj. | 药用的；医学的 | Relating to the use of medicines or drugs to treat illness or injury.  |例句：Many plants have medicinal properties. 许多植物具有药用价值。|例句：He's undergoing medicinal treatment for his illness. 他正在接受药物治疗。|近义词：therapeutic, pharmaceutical, curative |反义词：harmful, toxic


USAGE->

This plant has medicinal properties. = 这种植物具有药用价值。
That medicine is medicinal. = 那药是药用的。
