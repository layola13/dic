defrosting: /dɪˈfrɒstɪŋ/ | v. | 解冻；除霜 | The process of thawing something frozen, or the removal of frost or ice from something.  |例句：I'm defrosting the chicken for dinner. 我正在解冻鸡肉准备晚餐。

例句：We need to defrost the freezer before we can restock it. 我们需要先除霜才能重新储存冰箱。


近义词：thawing, unfreezing | 反义词：freezing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The refrigerator is defrosting automatically. = 冰箱正在自动除霜。
She defrosts the vegetables before cooking them. = 她做菜前会先解冻蔬菜。


一般过去时:
I defrosted the meat overnight. = 我把肉放在冰箱里解冻了一夜。
He defrosted the car windscreen with hot water. = 他用热水除掉了汽车挡风玻璃上的霜。


现在完成时:

We have defrosted the freezer, and it's ready to use. = 我们已经把冰箱除霜了，现在可以使用了。
