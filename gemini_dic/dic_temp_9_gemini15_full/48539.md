defacing:/dɪˈfeɪsɪŋ/| v.|毁坏外观；涂污；玷污 |To spoil the surface or appearance of something, especially by writing or drawing on it.|The building was defaced with graffiti. 这座建筑物被涂鸦弄坏了。|近义词：mar, spoil, vandalize, damage |反义词：beautify, restore


USAGE->

He defaces public property regularly. = 他经常毁坏公共财产。
