labionasal: /ˌleɪbiˈoʊneɪzəl/ | adj. | 唇鼻的；唇鼻音的 | Relating to both the lips and the nose, especially in the production of speech sounds.  The term refers to sounds produced with both the lips and the nose involved in their articulation, such as the English /m/ and /n/. | Example: The /m/ sound in "man" is a labionasal consonant.  / “man”中的/m/音是一个唇鼻辅音。| Near synonyms: oro-nasal | Antonyms:  oral, lingual |
