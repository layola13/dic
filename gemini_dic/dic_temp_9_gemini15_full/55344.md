dissymmetry:/dɪˈsɪmətri/| n. |不对称，不对称性 |Lack of symmetry; asymmetry.  Dissymmetry refers to a lack of symmetry, often implying a more subtle or less obvious difference than a complete lack of symmetry.  It can describe various aspects, from the arrangement of atoms in a molecule to the overall shape of an object.|例句：The crystal structure exhibits a high degree of dissymmetry.  晶体结构呈现高度的不对称性。

The slight dissymmetry in the painting's composition adds to its artistic appeal.  绘画构图中细微的不对称性增强了其艺术魅力。|近义词：asymmetry, irregularity |反义词：symmetry
