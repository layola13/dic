nephelometer:/nɪˈfɛləˌmiːtər/| n. |浊度计，散射光度计 |An instrument for measuring the turbidity of a liquid by measuring the intensity of light scattered at right angles to the incident beam.  Nephelometers are used in various fields, including environmental monitoring, clinical chemistry, and food science. |Example: The researcher used a nephelometer to determine the turbidity of the water sample.  研究人员用浊度计测定水样的浊度。  The nephelometer readings indicated a significant increase in particulate matter in the air. 浊度计读数表明空气中颗粒物含量显著增加。|近义词：turbidimeter |反义词：无
