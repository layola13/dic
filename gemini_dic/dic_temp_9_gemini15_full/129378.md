nomady:/ˈnoʊmədi/ | n. |游牧生活；游牧；流浪 |The way of life of nomadic peoples, characterized by movement from place to place in search of food, water, or pasture.  It often involves living in temporary shelters and having a strong connection to their animals. |例句：The ancient tribes lived a life of nomady, following the herds across the vast plains.  古代部落过着游牧生活，追随着兽群穿越广阔的平原。 |近义词：migration, wandering, peregrination |反义词：sedentary lifestyle, settled life
