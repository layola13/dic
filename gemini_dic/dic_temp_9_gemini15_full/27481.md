campanologists:/ˌkæmpəˈnɒlədʒɪsts/| n.|钟铃学家|A campanologist is an expert in bells, particularly church bells, their history, and their ringing techniques. They study the design, construction, and musical properties of bells, and often participate in bell ringing themselves. |例句：The campanologists meticulously tuned the bells for the upcoming concert.  钟铃学家们精心调整了即将到来的音乐会的钟声。|近义词：bell ringer, bell expert|反义词：无
