gladdens:/ˈɡlædnz/| vt.|使高兴；使欣喜|To make someone feel happy and pleased.|The good news gladdened her heart.  好消息使她欣喜若狂。|The sunny weather gladdened everyone.  阳光明媚的天气使每个人都感到高兴。|delight, please, cheer|sadden, depress, displease|动词
