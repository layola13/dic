foredoom:/fɔːrˈduːm/| vt.|预先注定；预言厄运|To predetermine to a disastrous fate; to prophesy doom for.|例句：The oracle foredoomed the city to destruction.  先知预言这座城市将被摧毁。|例句：The witch foredoomed the prince to a life of sorrow.  女巫预言王子将悲惨一生。|近义词：predestine, doom, foretell|反义词：bless, save

虚拟语气:
