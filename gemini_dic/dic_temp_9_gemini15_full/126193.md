muzzles:/ˈmʌzəlz/| n. v.|口套；枪口；压制|A muzzle is a covering placed over the mouth of an animal, typically a dog, to prevent it from biting or barking. It can also refer to the end of a gun barrel.  As a verb, it means to fit a muzzle on (an animal) or to suppress or restrain.|例句：The dog wore a muzzle to prevent it from biting. 那条狗戴着口套以防止咬人。
The police officer muzzled the suspect's protests. 警察压制了嫌疑人的抗议。|近义词：gag, restraint, suppress|反义词：unleash, liberate
