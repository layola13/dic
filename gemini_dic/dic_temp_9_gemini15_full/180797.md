rehabilitating:/ˌriːhəˈbɪlɪteɪtɪŋ/| v.|康复；修复；恢复名誉|The process of restoring someone to health or normal life through training and therapy after imprisonment, addiction, or illness.  It also refers to the restoration of something to a good state of repair.|例句：The hospital is rehabilitating patients after strokes. 医院正在对中风后的病人进行康复治疗。

例句：The government is rehabilitating the old buildings in the city center. 政府正在修复市中心的旧建筑。


近义词：reclaiming, restoring, recovering, reforming|反义词：damaging, destroying, incapacitating|动词
