resynchronize:/ˌriːˈsɪŋkrənaɪz/ | vt. |重新同步；使再次同步 | To synchronize again; to bring back into synchronization.  The process involves adjusting the timing or phase of something to match another. |例句：The engineers had to resynchronize the clocks in the network. 工程师们不得不重新同步网络中的时钟。

例句：We need to resynchronize our watches before the race. 我们需要在比赛前重新校准手表。


近义词：resync, realign, coordinate | 反义词：desynchronize, misalign
