overwhip:/ˈoʊvərhwɪp/| vt.|过度搅拌；过度抽打|To whip (a substance, such as cream or egg whites) to the point that it becomes separated or curdled; to excessively beat or stir something.|例句：Don't overwhip the cream, or it will become grainy.  不要过度搅拌奶油，否则它会变得粗糙。

例句：He overwhipped the egg whites until they became dry and rubbery. 他过度搅拌蛋白，直到它们变得干巴巴且像橡胶一样。


近义词：overbeat, overmix


反义词：underwhip


词性：动词
