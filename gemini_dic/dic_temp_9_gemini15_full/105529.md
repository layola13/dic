kilowatt:/ˈkɪləˌwɒt/|/ˈkɪləˌwɑːt/| n. |千瓦|A kilowatt (kW) is a unit of power equal to 1,000 watts. It is commonly used to measure the power output of electrical devices and generators.  |The air conditioner is rated at 2.5 kilowatts.|近义词：kilowatts,kw|反义词:None.

USAGE->

The generator produces 10 kilowatts of power. = 发电机产生10千瓦的电力。
This appliance consumes 1.5 kilowatts when operating. = 这台电器工作时消耗1.5千瓦。
