dedications:/ˌdɛdɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |奉献；献词；题词|The act of dedicating something to a person or cause; a statement expressing such dedication; something dedicated.  It can refer to the act of devoting oneself to a task or purpose, or a formal inscription in a book or work of art. |例句：His life was a dedication to the cause of social justice.  他的生命献给了社会正义事业。


例句：The book contains a dedication to his wife. 这本书包含了他献给妻子的题词。


近义词：devotion, commitment, consecration, inscription


反义词：neglect, disregard, abandonment


词性: 名词
