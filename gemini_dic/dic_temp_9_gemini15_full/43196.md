crabbiness:/ˈkræbɪnɪs/| n. |脾气暴躁；易怒；坏脾气 |A state of being irritable, grumpy, or bad-tempered.  It often refers to a persistent or habitual mood. |例句：His crabbiness was legendary around the office.  他的坏脾气在办公室里是出了名的。

例句：The crabbiness of the old man was a result of his failing health.  老人的坏脾气是由于他的健康状况恶化造成的。


近义词：grumpiness, irritability, ill-temper, surliness, crotchetiness |反义词：good humor, cheerfulness, pleasantness, amiability


词源:源自crab (螃蟹)，因螃蟹横行霸道、易怒的特性而引申而来。The connection is likely based on the association of crabs with sideways movement and a perceived "crabby" disposition.  This is a figurative, not literal, connection.
