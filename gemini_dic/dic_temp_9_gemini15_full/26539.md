cabin:/ˈkæbɪn/| n.|小屋；船舱；机舱|A small, simple house or shelter, especially one made of wood.  Also refers to a small room on a ship, airplane, or train.|例句：We rented a cozy cabin in the woods. 我们租了一间舒适的森林小屋。|例句：The pilot announced that our cabin was experiencing some turbulence.  飞行员宣布我们的机舱正在经历一些颠簸。|近义词：hut, cottage, compartment|反义词：mansion, palace


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The cabin sleeps four people comfortably. = 这小屋可以舒服地睡下四个人。
He always keeps his cabin clean and tidy. = 他总是保持他的船舱干净整洁。

一般过去时:
We stayed in a lovely cabin last summer. = 去年夏天我们住在一个可爱的小屋里。
The passengers were asked to remain in their cabins during the storm. = 乘客被要求在暴风雨期间留在自己的船舱里。

现在完成时:
They have renovated their mountain cabin. = 他们已经翻修了他们的山间小屋。
We have never experienced such a bumpy flight before; the cabin shook violently. = 我们以前从未经历过如此颠簸的飞行；机舱剧烈摇晃。

过去完成时:
We had already reached the cabin before the rain started. = 我们在雨开始下之前就已经到达了小屋。
The pilot had warned the passengers about turbulence before the cabin shook violently. = 飞行员在机舱剧烈摇晃之前已经警告乘客注意颠簸。

将来时:
We will book a cabin near the lake for our vacation. = 我们将预订一个靠近湖边的小屋作为我们的假期住所。
The airline will provide blankets for each passenger in the cabin. = 航空公司将为机舱里的每位乘客提供毯子。

虚拟语气:
If we had booked the cabin earlier, we would have gotten a better price. = 如果我们早点预订小屋，我们会得到更好的价格。
If I were you, I would never fly in that old cabin. = 如果我是你，我永远不会乘坐那个旧机舱。


被动语态:
The cabin was built by my grandfather. = 这小屋是我爷爷建造的。
The cabin was thoroughly cleaned before the new guests arrived. = 新客人到达之前，小屋被彻底打扫干净了。


疑问句:
Is there enough space in the cabin for all of us? = 小屋里够我们所有人住吗？
Where is the nearest cabin to the ski resort? = 最靠近滑雪胜地的客舱在哪里？


否定句:
We did not stay in a cabin; we stayed in a hotel. = 我们没有住在小屋里；我们住的是酒店。
There was no air conditioning in the cabin. = 小屋里没有空调。
