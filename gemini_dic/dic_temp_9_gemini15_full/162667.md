preassert:/ˌpriːəˈsɜːrt/| vt.|预先声明；预先断言|To state or declare something beforehand; to assert something in advance.|例句：The company preasserted its commitment to environmental sustainability.  公司预先声明了其对环境可持续性的承诺。|例句：He preasserted his innocence before the trial even began. 他在审判开始之前就预先声明了自己的清白。|近义词：affirm beforehand, declare in advance, preclaim|反义词：deny beforehand, contradict, refute in advance|动词
