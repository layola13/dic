denotes:/dɪˈnoʊts/ | vt. |表示；指示；象征|To be a sign of; to indicate; to signify.  The word "denotes" means to represent something or be a sign of something else. It implies a clear and direct connection between the thing denoted and the thing it represents.|

例句：

The red flag denotes danger. = 红旗表示危险。

The symbol denotes a high voltage. = 这个符号表示高压。


近义词：indicates, signifies, represents, symbolizes, means

反义词：conceals, hides, obscures


词源:

From Old French *denoter, from Latin denotare "to mark out, distinguish," from de- "down from" + notare "to mark" (from nota "mark").
