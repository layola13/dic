parallelable: /ˈpærəleɪbəl/ | adj. | 可并行的，可并列的 | Capable of being done or existing in parallel; able to be parallelized.  This word describes tasks, processes, or systems that can be executed simultaneously or independently without interfering with each other.  |


例句：

The tasks are parallelable, allowing for faster processing. = 这些任务是可以并行的，从而可以加快处理速度。

This algorithm is highly parallelable, significantly reducing computation time. = 这种算法高度并行，大大减少了计算时间。


近义词：concurrent, simultaneous, parallel, independent

反义词：sequential, serial


词性：形容词
