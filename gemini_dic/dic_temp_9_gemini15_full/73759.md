form:/fɔːrm/ /fɔːrm/| n. v.|形式，形状；表格；构成；形成|The form of the poem is very unique. 这首诗的形式非常独特。  He filled out the application form. 他填写了申请表。|shape, structure, type, mold|amorphous, shapeless|noun, verb

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The clay forms a beautiful vase. = 黏土形成了一个美丽的瓶子。
This sentence forms a complete thought. = 这句话构成一个完整的思想。

一般过去时:
The sculptor formed the clay into a bird. = 雕塑家把黏土塑造成一只鸟。
They formed a new company last year. = 他们去年成立了一家新公司。

现在完成时:
The committee has formed a conclusion. = 委员会已经得出了结论。
They have formed a strong bond over the years. = 他们多年来建立了牢固的联系。

过去完成时:
The foundation had formed before the building began. = 建筑开始之前地基已经打好了。
They had formed their opinions before the debate. = 他们在辩论之前就已经形成了自己的观点。

将来时:
The ice will form a thick layer on the lake. = 冰会在湖面上形成一层厚厚的冰层。
They will form a new government after the election. = 他们将在选举后组建新政府。

虚拟语气:
If the clay were soft, it would form easily. = 如果粘土柔软，它就容易成型。
I wish I could form a clear opinion on this matter. = 我希望我能对这件事形成明确的意见。

被动语态:
The team was formed last month. = 这个团队是上个月组建的。
The dough is formed into rolls. = 面团被做成面包卷。

疑问句:
What form does the answer take? = 答案采取什么形式？
What forms the basis of your argument? = 你的论点依据是什么？

否定句:
The liquid didn't form into crystals. = 液体没有形成晶体。
They didn't form a clear plan. = 他们没有制定一个明确的计划。


PHRASE->

fill out a form = 填写表格

out of form = 状态不佳
