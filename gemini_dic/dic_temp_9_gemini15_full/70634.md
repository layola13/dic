filterer:/ˈfɪltərər/| n. |过滤器；过滤者|A filterer is a person or thing that filters something.  This could be a physical device that removes impurities, or a person who selects and screens information. |例句：The water filterer removed all the impurities from the water. 净水器去除了水中的所有杂质。 The editor acted as a filterer, selecting only the best articles for publication. 编辑充当了过滤器的角色，只选择最好的文章发表。|近义词：strainer, purifier, sieve, censor |反义词：polluter, contaminator
