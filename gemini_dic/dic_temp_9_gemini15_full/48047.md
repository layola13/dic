deckle:/ˈdɛkl/| n.|齿形边，毛边；纸边；(印刷)齿形滚筒|The uneven, ragged edge of handmade paper, resulting from the deckle (a frame used in papermaking).  It's also used to describe a similar edge on other materials or a device used to create such an edge. |例句：The deckle edge of the handmade paper gave the invitation a unique, luxurious feel.  手工纸的齿形边使请柬显得独特而奢华。

The deckle on the book’s pages was slightly damaged during shipping. 书页上的毛边在运输过程中略微受损。


近义词：rough edge, ragged edge, untrimmed edge|反义词：trimmed edge, smooth edge|词性:名词
