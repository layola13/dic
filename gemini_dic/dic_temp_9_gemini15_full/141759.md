orangutans:/ˌɒrænˈɡʊtæŋz/ | n. |猩猩| Orangutans are large, arboreal primates found in the rainforests of Borneo and Sumatra.  They are known for their intelligence and solitary nature. |  We saw a family of orangutans swinging through the trees. 我们看到一群猩猩在树林中荡秋千。 The orangutans at the zoo were very active. 动物园里的猩猩非常活跃。 | apes, primates |  (no direct antonym)| 名词
