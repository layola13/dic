slopingnesses:/ˈsloʊpɪŋnəsɪz/| n. |倾斜度；斜坡的特性|The multiple slopingnesses of the terrain made navigation difficult.  地形的多处倾斜使得导航变得困难。|近义词：inclinations, gradients, slants |反义词：flatness, levelness


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense and voice are not applicable.  The word itself describes a plural state of "slopingness".
