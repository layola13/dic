rampart: /ˈræmpɑːrt/ | n. |城墙；防御工事 | A rampart is a defensive wall or embankment, often part of a fortification.  It is typically raised and strengthened to provide protection against attack.  | Example: The medieval city was surrounded by a massive rampart.  中世纪城市被一道巨大的城墙环绕着。 | 近义词：wall, fortification, bulwark | 反义词：opening, breach
