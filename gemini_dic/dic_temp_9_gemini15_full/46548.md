dallasite:/ˈdæləsaɪt/| n. |达拉斯石|A rare mineral consisting of a hydrated copper arsenate sulfate.  It is usually found as a secondary mineral in oxidized copper deposits.  Its color ranges from deep green to bluish-green and it crystallizes in the orthorhombic system.  It's named after Dallas, Texas, where it was first discovered.|The dallasite sample showed a distinct bluish-green hue.|近义词: olivenite (in some respects, due to similar copper arsenate composition)|反义词: N/A|名词
