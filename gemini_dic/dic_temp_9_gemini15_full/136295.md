nonrepresentatives: /ˌnɒnˌreprɛzɛnˈteɪtɪvz/ | n. |非代表性人物；非典型样本|  Those individuals or things that do not represent the typical or average characteristics of a larger group. They lack representativeness.  |例句：The sample was criticized for containing too many nonrepresentatives, skewing the results.  该样本因包含过多非典型样本而受到批评，导致结果偏差。


The survey results are unreliable because the sample included too many nonrepresentatives.  调查结果不可靠，因为样本包含了太多的非典型代表。|近义词：atypical examples, unrepresentative samples|反义词：representatives, typical examples
