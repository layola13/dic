palatinate:/ˈpælətaɪneɪt/| n.|普法尔茨；普法尔茨公国|A palatinate is a territory governed by a count palatine, a high-ranking official with extensive powers.  Historically, palatinates were often semi-independent regions within larger kingdoms or empires.|The Palatinate was once a powerful and influential region in the Holy Roman Empire. |近义词：county, principality|反义词：无
