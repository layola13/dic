hoboing:/ˈhoʊboʊɪŋ/ | v. | 流浪；漂泊；以流浪为生 | The act of traveling and living as a hobo;  wandering from place to place without a permanent home, often relying on odd jobs and charity. |  He spent his youth hoboing across the country. 他年轻时在全国各地流浪。  They were hoboing through Europe. 他们在欧洲流浪。 | vagrancy, tramping, wandering | settled life, staying put |  动词
