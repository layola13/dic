optionalize:/ˈɑːpʃənəlaɪz/| vt.|使成为可选的；使具有选择性|To make something optional; to give someone the option to choose.|The company optionalized the use of the new software, allowing employees to continue using the old system if they preferred.  公司将新软件的使用设置为可选，允许员工如果愿意可以继续使用旧系统。|近义词：optionalize, make optional, allow for choice|反义词：mandatory, require, necessitate|动词
