nonrevocation: /ˌnɒnˌrevəˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |不可撤销，撤销无效| The act or instance of not being able to revoke or cancel something; the state of being irrevocable.  It typically refers to a promise, agreement, or right that cannot be taken back or withdrawn. |例句：The contract contains a clause guaranteeing nonrevocation of the license.  该合同包含一条保证许可证不可撤销的条款。

The guarantee of nonrevocation is crucial for the success of the project. 不可撤销的保证对于项目的成功至关重要。|近义词：irrevocability, finality, permanence |反义词：revocation, cancellation, annulment |名词

词源:
The word "nonrevocation" is a combination of the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the noun "revocation" (the act of revoking or canceling something).  "Revocation," in turn, comes from the Latin verb "revocare" (to call back, recall).
