fingernail: /ˈfɪŋɡərˌneɪl/ | n. | 指甲 | The hard keratin covering on the dorsal surface of the distal phalanx of a finger.  It protects the sensitive fingertip. |  She painted her fingernails a bright red. 她把指甲涂成了鲜红色。  He bit his fingernails nervously. 他紧张地咬着指甲。 | toenail, claw |  (no direct antonym)
