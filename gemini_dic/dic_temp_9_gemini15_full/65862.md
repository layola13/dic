evoke:/ɪˈvoʊk/ | /ɪˈvəʊk/ | vt. |唤起，引起，使人想起 |To evoke something means to bring a feeling, memory, or image into the mind.  It often involves a strong emotional response. |The music evoked a sense of nostalgia.  这音乐唤起了怀旧之情。|The painting evoked memories of her childhood. 这幅画唤起了她童年时的回忆。|arouse, elicit, summon, inspire |suppress, repress, ignore |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The smell of rain evokes pleasant memories in me. = 雨的味道在我心中唤起了美好的回忆。
That song evokes strong emotions in many people. = 那首歌在许多人心中唤起了强烈的情感。


一般过去时:
The photograph evoked a flood of memories. = 这张照片唤起了许多回忆。
Her words evoked a feeling of sadness. = 她的话语唤起了悲伤之情。


现在完成时:
This painting has evoked much debate among art critics. = 这幅画已在艺术评论家之间引发了激烈的讨论。
The film has evoked strong reactions from the audience. = 这部电影引起了观众强烈的反响。


过去完成时:
The old house had evoked a sense of mystery in her. = 那座旧房子在她心中唤起了一种神秘感。
His speech had evoked a standing ovation. = 他的演讲赢得了全场起立鼓掌。


将来时:
I hope the new campaign will evoke a positive response from consumers. = 我希望新活动能引起消费者的积极回应。
The concert is expected to evoke a wide range of emotions. = 这场音乐会预计会唤起各种各样的情感。


虚拟语气:
If the music evoked stronger emotions, the movie would be more effective. = 如果音乐能唤起更强烈的情感，这部电影就会更有效。
I wish the painting evoked more joy instead of sadness. = 我希望这幅画能唤起更多快乐而不是悲伤。


被动语态:
A sense of peace was evoked by the quiet atmosphere. = 安静的氛围唤起了宁静感。
Strong feelings were evoked by the powerful speech. = 充满力量的演讲唤起了强烈的情感。


疑问句:
Does this painting evoke any particular emotion in you? = 这幅画在你心中唤起了什么特殊的情感吗？
What memories does this scent evoke for you? = 这种气味在你心中唤起了哪些回忆？


否定句:
The movie failed to evoke any strong emotions in me. = 这部电影未能在我心中唤起任何强烈的情感。
The design did not evoke the intended feeling of luxury. = 这种设计未能唤起预期的奢华感。
