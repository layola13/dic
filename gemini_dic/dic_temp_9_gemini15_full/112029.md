long-stay:/lɒŋ steɪ/| adj. n.|长期居住的；长期逗留；长期住宿|Describes a stay of extended duration, typically referring to accommodation or a visit. It can be used as an adjective modifying a noun (e.g., long-stay visa) or as a noun referring to the stay itself (e.g., a long-stay in a resort).|例句：She applied for a long-stay visa. 她申请了长期签证。

They booked a long-stay apartment for their trip. 他们为他们的旅行预订了一套长期公寓。|近义词：extended stay, prolonged stay |反义词：short stay, brief stay
