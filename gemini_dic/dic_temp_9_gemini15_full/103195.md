jesus:/ˈdʒiːzəs/| n. |耶稣|Jesus Christ, the central figure of Christianity, believed by Christians to be the Son of God.  He is revered as the Messiah prophesied in the Old Testament and is considered by Christians to be divine. His life, teachings, death, and resurrection form the basis of Christian faith and theology.|例句：Many people believe in Jesus Christ and follow his teachings. 很多人相信耶稣基督并遵循他的教诲。|例句：The story of Jesus' life is told in the Gospels. 耶稣生平的故事记载在福音书中。|近义词：Christ, Messiah|反义词：无
