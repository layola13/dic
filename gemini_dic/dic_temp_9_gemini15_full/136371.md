nonresponsibilities: /ˌnɒnˌrɪˌspɒnsəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. |非责任；非义务 |Things that are not one's responsibility or duty.  The term often highlights tasks or areas that someone is explicitly *not* expected to handle.  |例句：Cleaning the communal areas is not within my nonresponsibilities; I'm responsible for my own apartment. 清洁公共区域不在我的非责任范围内；我负责自己的公寓。

The company's nonresponsibilities are clearly outlined in the contract. 公司的非责任在合同中明确列明。

|近义词：irresponsibilities, nonobligations, exemptions |反义词：responsibilities, duties, obligations |名词
