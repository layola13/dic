codder:/ˈkɒdə(r)/| n. |鳕鱼捕捞者；鳕鱼加工工人；（尤指）用鳕鱼肝油制药的人|A codder is someone who catches or processes cod, especially someone who makes medicine from cod liver oil.  The term is now largely historical, reflecting a time when cod fishing and cod liver oil production were major industries.|The old codder told tales of his days at sea catching cod.  老渔夫讲述了他捕捞鳕鱼的往昔岁月。|fisherman, fishmonger|---|(无明确反义词，可根据语境选择反义词，例如：landlubber (陆地人))
