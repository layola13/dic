outhunted:/ˌaʊtˈhʌntɪd/| adj.| 被猎杀殆尽的；被过度狩猎的 |  Overhunted to the point of near extinction.  Describes a population of animals that has been hunted to a severely depleted level. |例句：The once-thriving deer population is now outhunted, with only a few individuals remaining.  曾经繁荣的鹿群现在已经被过度狩猎，只剩下少数个体。


The outhunted wolves struggled to survive in the harsh winter. 被过度狩猎的狼群在严酷的冬天艰难求生。|近义词：overhunted, decimated, depleted, exhausted |反义词：abundant, plentiful, thriving
