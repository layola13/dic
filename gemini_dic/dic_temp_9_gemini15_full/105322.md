kiboshing:/ˈkaɪbɒʃɪŋ/ | v. | 阻止；扼杀；使泡汤 | To put a kibosh on something means to put a stop to it, to suppress it, or to prevent it from happening.  It is informal and often humorous. |  They kiboshed the plan to build a new highway. 他们阻止了修建新高速公路的计划。 The sudden rain kiboshed our picnic.  突如其来的雨水使我们的野餐泡汤了。 | 近义词：stopping, preventing, quashing, nixing, squashing | 反义词：enabling, promoting, furthering
