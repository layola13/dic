fasttracks: /ˈfæsttræks/ | vt. n. | 快速推进；优先处理；快速通道 | To accelerate the progress of something; to give something priority treatment; a system or process that allows for rapid advancement. |

例句：

The company fast-tracked the new product launch. = 公司快速推进新产品的发布。

They fast-tracked her promotion after her outstanding performance. = 她业绩突出后，公司优先考虑了她的晋升。


近义词：accelerate, expedite, prioritize, rush

反义词：delay, hinder, procrastinate, slow down


词性:动词(vt.)，名词(n.)
