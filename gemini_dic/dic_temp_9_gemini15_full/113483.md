lyricist:/ˈlɪrɪsɪst/| n. |作词家|A lyricist is a person who writes the lyrics of songs.  They are responsible for crafting the words and verses that convey the song's message and emotion.|Example:  Bob Dylan is a celebrated lyricist known for his poetic and socially conscious lyrics.  鲍勃·迪伦是一位以其诗意和社会意识的歌词而闻名的著名词作家。|近义词：song writer, wordsmith|反义词：composer (in the context of music creation)|词性:名词
