例句：The village benefited greatly from the construction of a minihospital.  这个村庄从小型医院的建设中受益匪浅。


近义词：small hospital, clinic, medical center (depending on services offered) | 反义词：large hospital, major medical center | 词性：名词

词源:  The word "minihospital" is a compound word formed by combining "mini," a diminutive prefix meaning "small," and "hospital," a word of Latin origin (from "hospitale," meaning "guest house").  The combining of these creates a term to describe a smaller version of a traditional hospital.
