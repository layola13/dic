crepe:/krep/| n.|薄饼，法式薄饼|A thin, delicate pancake, often made with buckwheat or other flours.  It can be sweet or savory.|例句：I ordered a crepe with Nutella and strawberries. 我点了一份涂有Nutella酱和草莓的法式薄饼。|例句：The savory crepe filled with cheese and ham was delicious.  夹着奶酪和火腿的咸味薄饼非常美味。|近义词：pancake, crêpe Suzette (a specific type)|反义词：无
