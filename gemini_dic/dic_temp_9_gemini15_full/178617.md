recourse:/ˈriːkɔːrs/| n. |求助；凭借；追索权；资源 | a way of getting help or dealing with a difficult situation | The only recourse was to appeal to the court.  唯一的办法是向法院上诉。|  appeal, resort, remedy |  neglect, indifference | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has recourse to prayer. = 他求助于祈祷。
She has recourse to her savings. = 她求助于她的储蓄。


一般过去时:
They had recourse to violence. = 他们诉诸暴力。
He had recourse to his lawyer for advice. = 他向他的律师寻求建议。


现在完成时:
We have had recourse to many different methods. = 我们已经尝试过许多不同的方法。
They have had recourse to legal action. = 他们已经采取了法律行动。


过去完成时:
She had had recourse to her family for support. = 她曾求助于家人寻求支持。
He had had recourse to deception in the past. = 他过去曾采取过欺骗手段。


将来时:
They will have recourse to arbitration if negotiations fail. = 如果谈判失败，他们将诉诸仲裁。
We will have recourse to drastic measures if necessary. = 必要时，我们将采取严厉措施。


虚拟语气:
If I had any recourse, I would use it. = 如果我有任何办法，我会用它。
If they had recourse to the police, the situation might have been different. = 如果他们向警方求助，情况可能会有所不同。


被动语态:
Recourse was had to extreme measures. = 采取了极端措施。
Recourse can be had to mediation. = 可以求助于调解。


疑问句:
What recourse do we have? = 我们有什么办法？
Is there any recourse available to us? = 我们有什么可用的办法吗？


否定句:
There was no recourse but to surrender. = 除了投降别无选择。
They had no recourse but to accept the decision. = 他们别无选择，只能接受这个决定。
