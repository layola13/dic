lay-up:/ˈleɪ ʌp/| n. v.| 篮球中的上篮；储存；搁置；|英文解析：As a noun, it refers to a shot in basketball where a player gets close to the basket and shoots the ball. As a verb, it means to put something away for storage or to postpone something.|例句：He made a beautiful lay-up. 他完成了一个漂亮的上篮。  He layed up his winter clothes. 他把冬衣收了起来。|近义词：(n.) shot, basket; (v.) store, save, postpone|反义词：(v.) retrieve, use, continue|词性：n. v.
