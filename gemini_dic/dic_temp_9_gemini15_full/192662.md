scutibranchiata:/ˌskuːtɪbrænˈkiːætə/| n. |盾鳃类动物|A subclass of gastropods (snails and slugs) characterized by a modified gill structure resembling a shield.  They are mostly marine and often have a reduced or absent shell.|Example:  Many species of *scutibranchiata* inhabit intertidal zones.|近义词:  (no direct synonyms, but related terms include: gastropods, opisthobranchs)|反义词: (no direct antonyms)|名词
