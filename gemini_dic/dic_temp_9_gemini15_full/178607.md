recoup:/rɪˈkuːp/| vt.|补偿，挽回，恢复|To regain or recover something lost or expended.|The company hopes to recoup its losses in the next quarter. 公司希望在下个季度弥补损失。|recover, regain, retrieve|lose, forfeit, squander|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recoups his losses through careful investment. 他通过谨慎的投资来弥补损失。
She recoups her energy after a good night's sleep.  她睡了一个好觉后恢复了精力。


一般过去时:
The farmer recouped his harvest after the storm.  暴风雨过后，农民收回了他的收成。
They recouped their travel expenses from the company. 他们从公司报销了差旅费。


现在完成时:
We have recouped most of the money we lost. 我们已经收回了大部分损失的钱。
She has recouped her strength after a long illness.  她经过长时间的疾病后恢复了体力。


过去完成时:
He had recouped his losses before the market crashed.  市场崩盘之前，他已经弥补了损失。
They had recouped their initial investment by the end of the year.  到年底，他们已经收回了最初的投资。


将来时:
We will recoup the cost of the repairs from the insurance company. 我们将从保险公司收回维修费用。
The company expects to recoup its investment within five years. 公司预计在五年内收回投资。


虚拟语气:
If we worked harder, we would recoup our losses faster. 如果我们更努力工作，就能更快地弥补损失。
I wish I could recoup the time I wasted. 我真希望我能弥补我浪费的时间。


被动语态:
The losses were eventually recouped by the company.  损失最终由公司弥补。
The cost of the damage will be recouped from the responsible party.  损坏的费用将从责任方收回。


疑问句:
Will the company be able to recoup its investment? 公司能够收回投资吗？
How long will it take to recoup the losses?  需要多长时间才能弥补损失？


否定句:
The company failed to recoup its losses. 公司未能弥补损失。
He couldn't recoup the money he had spent. 他无法收回他已花费的钱。


PHRASE->
recoup losses 弥补损失
recoup expenses 报销费用
recoup costs 收回成本

recoup strength 恢复体力
