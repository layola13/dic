gridironing:/ˈɡrɪdaɪərənɪŋ/| v. |用格栅烤制；严密组织；细致规划|The act of cooking food, typically meat, on a gridiron (a framework of parallel bars used for grilling).  It can also refer to the meticulous planning and organization of something, often implying a highly structured and detailed approach.|例句：He spent weeks gridironing the project plan, ensuring every detail was accounted for. 他花了几个星期仔细规划项目计划，确保每个细节都被考虑在内。|近义词：grilling, structuring, organizing |反义词：improvising, haphazardly planning
