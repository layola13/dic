fraternized:/ˈfrætərnaɪzd/ | v. | 友好相处；亲密交往；与…兄弟般相处 | To associate or behave in a friendly way, especially with someone of a different group or social standing.  | The soldiers fraternized with the local people.  士兵们与当地人友好相处。|  The opposing teams fraternized after the game.  比赛结束后，对阵的两支队伍友好地交流。|  associate, socialize, mingle |  estrange, alienate, isolate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fraternizes with his colleagues. = 他和同事们友好相处。
They fraternize at the company picnic. = 他们在公司野餐会上友好地交流。


一般过去时:
The soldiers fraternized with the civilians. = 士兵们与平民友好相处。
The two groups fraternized after the conflict. = 冲突后，这两个群体友好地相处了。


现在完成时:
The troops have fraternized with the local population. = 部队已经与当地居民友好相处了。
They have fraternized extensively since the peace agreement. = 自和平协议签订以来，他们进行了广泛的友好交流。


过去完成时:
They had fraternized before the war broke out. = 在战争爆发之前，他们就已经友好地相处了。
The soldiers had fraternized with the enemy, which was against regulations. = 士兵们曾与敌人友好相处，这是违反规定的。


将来时:
The diplomats will fraternize at the upcoming summit. = 外交官们将在即将举行的峰会上友好地交流。
We will fraternize with our new neighbors. = 我们将和新邻居友好相处。


虚拟语气:
If they had fraternized earlier, the conflict might have been avoided. = 如果他们早点友好相处，冲突或许就能避免。
I wish I had fraternized more with my classmates. = 我真希望我以前能和同学们多友好相处一些。


被动语态:

It is forbidden that soldiers be fraternized with the enemy. = 士兵与敌人友好相处是被禁止的。（被动语态在该词语使用中较少见）


疑问句:
Did the opposing teams fraternize after the match? = 比赛后，对阵的两支队伍友好相处了吗？
Do the soldiers fraternize with the local people? = 士兵们与当地人友好相处吗？


否定句:
The soldiers did not fraternize with the enemy. = 士兵们没有与敌人友好相处。
He refused to fraternize with those he considered his enemies. = 他拒绝与他认为是敌人的人友好相处。
