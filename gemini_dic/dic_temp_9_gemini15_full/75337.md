frostily:/ˈfrɒstɪli/ | adv. | 冷淡地；冰冷地；严寒地 |  In a cold, unfriendly, or distant manner.  |  She greeted him frostily. 她冷淡地迎接了他。 He spoke frostily to the reporter. 他冷冰冰地对记者说话。 | coldly, icily, stiffly | warmly, cordially, amiably
