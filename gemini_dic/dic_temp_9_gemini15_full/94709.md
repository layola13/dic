improper:/ɪmˈprɒpər/ | adj. | 不合适的；不正确的；不雅的 | Not conforming to accepted standards of behavior or morality; not suitable or appropriate.  |例句：His improper conduct led to his dismissal. 他的不当行为导致他被解雇。

例句：It's improper to interrupt someone while they are speaking. 打断别人说话是不合适的。

近义词：unsuitable, inappropriate, unseemly, indecorous, wrong, incorrect

反义词：proper, appropriate, suitable, correct, seemly, decorous
