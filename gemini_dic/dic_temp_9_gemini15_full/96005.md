incursion:/ɪnˈkɜːʒən/| n. |入侵；侵犯；袭击 |The sudden incursion of enemy troops caused panic. 敌军的突然入侵引起了恐慌。|近义词：invasion, raid, attack |反义词：withdrawal, retreat


USAGE->
一般现在时:
An incursion often happens in the border areas. 边境地区经常发生入侵事件。
Their incursion into our market is causing problems. 他们进军我们的市场正在造成问题。


一般过去时:
The barbarian incursion devastated the countryside.  野蛮人的入侵摧毁了乡村。
The company's incursion into the new market failed. 这家公司进军新市场的尝试失败了。


现在完成时:
There have been several incursions into the country this year. 今年已经发生了多次对该国的入侵。
The company's recent incursion into online sales has been successful. 这家公司最近进军在线销售取得了成功。


过去完成时:
Before the main battle, there had been several small incursions. 在主要战斗之前，已经发生了几次小规模入侵。
By the time the merger happened, the company had already made several incursions into the technology sector. 到合并发生时，该公司已经多次进军科技领域。


将来时:
We anticipate further incursions by the rebels. 我们预计叛军将进一步入侵。
The company will make an incursion into the European market next year. 该公司将于明年进军欧洲市场。


虚拟语气:
If there were an incursion, the army would respond immediately. 如果发生入侵，军队将立即做出反应。
It's important to be prepared in case there is an unexpected incursion.  做好准备以防发生意外入侵非常重要。


被动语态:
The town was devastated by a violent incursion. 该镇被猛烈的入侵摧毁了。
The market has been successfully penetrated by their incursion. 他们的入侵成功地渗透了市场。


疑问句:
Was there an incursion last night?  昨晚有入侵吗？
Have there been any significant incursions recently? 最近有发生过什么重大入侵吗？


否定句:
There was no incursion reported yesterday. 昨天没有报告任何入侵事件。
The company did not make an incursion into that market. 该公司没有进军那个市场。

词源:
in- (进入) + currere (跑，奔流)  ->  incursion (跑进来，入侵)
