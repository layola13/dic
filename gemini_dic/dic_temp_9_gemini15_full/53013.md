dinotherium:/ˌdaɪnəˈθɪəriəm/| n. |恐象属|An extinct genus of large, elephant-like mammals belonging to the family Deinotheriidae.  They were characterized by downward-curving tusks on their lower jaw and a relatively short trunk.  They lived from the Miocene to the Pleistocene epochs. |The dinotherium was a fascinating creature of the prehistoric world. |近义词: mastodon, gomphothere |反义词:  (no direct antonyms, as it refers to a specific extinct genus)|名词
