herbar:/ˈhɜːrbɑːr/| n.|植物标本室；植物标本；草药集|A herbar is a collection of preserved plant specimens and/or a place where such specimens are housed.  It serves as a valuable resource for botanical research, education, and conservation.|The university's herbar boasts a collection of over 100,000 specimens. = 这所大学的植物标本室拥有超过10万份标本。|The local herbar contains many rare and endangered plant specimens. = 当地的植物标本室包含许多稀有和濒危植物标本。|herbarium, botanical collection|
