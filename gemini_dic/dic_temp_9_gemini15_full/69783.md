fermenting:/fərˈmɛntɪŋ/ | v. | 发酵；酝酿；激动 |  The process of undergoing fermentation, involving the enzymatic breakdown of a substance, typically with the release of gas or a change in chemical composition.  It can refer to both chemical processes (like the production of alcohol or yogurt) and metaphorical processes (like brewing anger or ideas). | 例句：The grapes are fermenting in large vats.  葡萄正在大型容器中发酵。


The bread dough is fermenting nicely. 面团正在很好地发酵。


近义词：brewing, developing, maturing | 反义词：decaying (in a chemical context)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The yeast is fermenting the sugar. = 酵母正在发酵糖分。
They are fermenting the cabbage for kimchi. = 他们正在发酵卷心菜做泡菜。


一般过去时:
The mixture fermented quickly. = 混合物很快就发酵了。
We fermented the apples to make cider. = 我们发酵苹果来制作苹果酒。


现在完成时:
The beer has fermented for several weeks. = 啤酒已经发酵了几周了。

过去完成时:
The wine had fermented before it was bottled. = 酒在装瓶之前就已经发酵了。
The dough had fermented overnight. = 面团已经发酵了一夜。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
