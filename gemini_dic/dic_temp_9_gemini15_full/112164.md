look-sharp:/lʊk ʃɑːrp/ | adj. |精神抖擞的，衣着讲究的，敏捷的 |Looking sharp means appearing well-dressed, stylish, and attentive.  It can also imply being alert and quick-witted. |例句：He looked sharp in his new suit. 他穿着新西装显得精神抖擞。

He's a look-sharp young lawyer. 他是一位敏捷的年轻律师。|近义词：stylish, dapper, alert, astute |反义词：sloppy, disheveled, dull, obtuse
