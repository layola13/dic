hyperphosphataemia:/ˌhaɪpərfɒsfəˈteɪmiə/ (BrE) /ˌhaɪpərˌfɑːsfəˈteɪmiə/ (AmE)| n. |高磷血症|High levels of phosphate in the blood.  This condition can be caused by various factors, including kidney disease, hypoparathyroidism, and excessive intake of phosphorus.  It can lead to serious health problems if left untreated.|The patient presented with hyperphosphataemia, a common complication of chronic kidney disease.|近义词：hyperphosphatemia|反义词：hypophosphataemia
