platelets:/ˈpleɪtləts/| n. |血小板|Small, irregular, colorless bodies of the blood that are important in blood clotting. They are fragments of large cells called megakaryocytes found in bone marrow.|Example:  Low platelet counts can lead to excessive bleeding. 低血小板计数会导致出血过多。
Example: The doctor ordered a platelet count to assess the patient's clotting ability. 医生下令进行血小板计数以评估患者的凝血能力。|近义词：thrombocytes|反义词：无
