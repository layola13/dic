chibouque:/ʃɪˈbuːk/| n. |雪茄烟斗；烟斗 |A chibouque is a long-stemmed tobacco pipe, typically with a bowl made of clay or other materials, and often adorned with an amber mouthpiece.  It was traditionally used in the Middle East and parts of North Africa. |Example: He puffed contentedly on his ornate chibouque. 他心满意足地抽着他的华丽烟斗。|近义词：pipe, hookah (but hookah is water pipe)|反义词：None
