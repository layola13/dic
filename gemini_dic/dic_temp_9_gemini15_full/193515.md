self:/sɛlf/|pron.|自身，自我|The word "self" refers to a person's own being or identity, separate from others. It can also refer to one's own interests or desires.|例句：She needs to look after herself. 她需要照顾好自己。|近义词：oneself, ego|反义词:other, others


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I see myself in the mirror. = 我在镜子里看见我自己。
She loves herself very much. = 她非常爱自己。


一般过去时:
He hurt himself playing football. = 他踢足球时伤到了自己。
They enjoyed themselves at the party. = 他们在派对上玩得很开心。


现在完成时:
I have taught myself to play the guitar. = 我自学了吉他。
She has expressed herself clearly. = 她表达得很清楚。


过去完成时:
He had already prepared himself for the worst. = 他已经做好了最坏的打算。
They had lost themselves in the forest. = 他们在森林里迷路了。


将来时:
I will reward myself with a vacation. = 我会奖励自己一个假期。
She will find herself a new job. = 她会为自己找一份新工作。


虚拟语气:
If I were myself, I would do it differently. = 如果我是我自己，我会做得不一样。
I wish I could be myself around them. = 我希望我能在他/她们面前做真实的自己。


被动语态:
(Self is rarely used in the passive voice.  A more natural phrasing would be needed.)


疑问句:
Is this your true self? = 这是你真正的自我吗？
Do you know yourself well? = 你了解你自己吗？


否定句:
He didn't quite know himself. = 他不太了解自己。
She couldn't bring herself to tell him the truth. = 她无法鼓起勇气告诉他真相。


PHRASE->
by oneself = 独自地
of oneself = 自发地
to oneself = 自言自语
in oneself = 本身
for oneself = 为自己
