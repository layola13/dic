quasi-righteous:/ˈkwɑːzaɪˈraɪtʃəs/ | adj. |似是而非地正义的，假装正义的 | Seemingly or superficially righteous; self-righteously moralistic but actually insincere or hypocritical.  It suggests a pretense of righteousness, often masking ulterior motives or a lack of genuine moral conviction. | Example: His quasi-righteous indignation over the minor infraction was unconvincing. 他对这件小过错的义愤填膺显得毫无说服力。 | Near synonyms: self-righteous, sanctimonious, hypocritical, pharisaical | Antonyms: humble, unassuming, sincere, genuine | 词性:形容词
