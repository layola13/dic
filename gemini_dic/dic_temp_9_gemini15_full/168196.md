pro-Darwinism: /ˌproʊˈdɑːrwɪnɪzəm/ | n. | 达尔文主义拥护 | Support for or advocacy of Darwinism, the theory of evolution by natural selection.  It emphasizes the importance of natural selection as the primary mechanism for evolutionary change. |  The scientist's work was a strong statement of pro-Darwinism.  这位科学家的工作是对达尔文主义的有力论证。 | 近义词: Darwinianism, evolutionism | 反义词: creationism, anti-evolutionism
