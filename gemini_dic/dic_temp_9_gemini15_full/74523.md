fraternate:/ˈfrætərneɪt/| v.|兄弟般相处；友善交往；结为兄弟|To associate or behave in a friendly way, especially with members of a particular group.  It often implies a sense of camaraderie and brotherhood.|例句：The soldiers fraternized with the locals after the battle. 战后士兵们与当地人友好相处。|例句：The two rival companies fraternized at the industry conference. 两家竞争公司在行业会议上友好交往。|近义词：associate, socialize, mingle, comrade|反义词：estrange, alienate, isolate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They fraternize regularly. = 他们经常友好交往。
We fraternize with our neighbors. = 我们和邻居们友好相处。


一般过去时:
The troops fraternized with the civilians. = 部队与平民友好相处。
They fraternized after the meeting. = 会议后他们友好地交往。


现在完成时:
The teams have fraternized throughout the tournament. = 各队在整个锦标赛期间一直友好相处。
We have fraternized with many people. = 我们已经结识了很多朋友。


过去完成时:
They had fraternized before the war broke out. = 在战争爆发之前，他们已经友好地交往了。
The soldiers had fraternized with the enemy. = 士兵们曾与敌人友好相处。


将来时:
We will fraternize at the party. = 我们将在聚会上友好地交往。
They will fraternize with their colleagues. = 他们将与同事们友好相处。


虚拟语气:
If they fraternized more, they would be happier. = 如果他们更友好地交往，他们会更快乐。
I wish we had fraternized more often. = 我希望我们能更经常地友好相处。


被动语态:
(This verb is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes an action taken by the subject, not something done to the subject.)


疑问句:
Do they fraternize often? = 他们经常友好交往吗？
Will they fraternize with the opposing team? = 他们会和对方球队友好相处吗？


否定句:
They do not fraternize with their competitors. = 他们不和竞争对手友好交往。
We did not fraternize with the strangers. = 我们没有与陌生人友好相处。


PHRASE->
fraternize with = 与…友好相处
fraternize among = 在…之中友好相处
openly fraternize = 公开友好交往
secretly fraternize = 秘密友好交往
forcibly fraternize = 强迫友好相处
