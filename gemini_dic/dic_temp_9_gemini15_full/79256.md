glassily:/ˈɡlæsili/| adj.|玻璃般地；呆滞地；茫然地|Having a glassy or glazed appearance or expression; lacking animation or expression.  It often suggests a vacant or unfeeling stare.|例句：He stared glassily ahead, seemingly oblivious to his surroundings. 他呆滞地向前凝视着，似乎没有注意到周围的环境。

例句：Her eyes were glassily fixed on the distant horizon.  她的眼睛呆滞地注视着远方的地平线。


近义词：vacantly, blankly, dully, listlessly, apathetically

反义词：animatedly, keenly, brightly, vividly, alertly

词源:  Derived from "glassy,"  meaning resembling glass, especially in smoothness, shine, or transparency.  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective "glassy" into an adverb, describing the manner in which something is done.
