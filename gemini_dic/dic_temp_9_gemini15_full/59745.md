ecologically:/ˌiːkəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 生态地；从生态学的角度 | Relating to or concerned with the environment and the impact of human activity on it.  It emphasizes the interconnectedness of living organisms and their environment. | Example: The project was designed to be ecologically sustainable.  这个项目的设计是为了生态可持续性。  The new factory was built ecologically responsibly. 新工厂的建造对生态负责。|近义词：environmentally, sustainably | 反义词：unsustainably, irresponsibly
