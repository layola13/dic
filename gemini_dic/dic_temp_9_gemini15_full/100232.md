/ɪntərˈruːlz/ | n. |  规则之间的间隔；行距；(印刷术语) | The spacing between lines of type;  the distance between the lines of text.  | The interrules were too wide, making the text look sparse. 行距太宽，使文本看起来稀疏。 | 近义词：leading, spacing | 反义词：None specifically.
