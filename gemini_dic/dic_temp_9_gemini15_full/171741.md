pumpkins:/ˈpʌmpkɪnz/| n.|南瓜|A large, round orange or yellow fruit with thick flesh, used as food. Pumpkins are a type of squash and are commonly associated with autumn and Halloween.  They are versatile and can be used in pies, soups, breads, and other dishes. |例句：We carved pumpkins for Halloween. 我们在万圣节雕刻了南瓜。

例句：She used pumpkin puree in her bread recipe. 她在她面包食谱中使用了南瓜泥。
