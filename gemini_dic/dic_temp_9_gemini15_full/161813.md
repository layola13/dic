practicable:/ˈpræktɪkəbl/| adj.|可行的；切实可行的；实际的|feasible; capable of being put into practice; possible to do.|The plan seemed practicable at first, but later proved to be too expensive.  一开始这个计划看起来可行，但后来证明太贵了。|The scheme is perfectly practicable. 这项计划完全可行。|feasible, workable, viable|impracticable, impossible, unfeasible

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plan is practicable. = 这个计划是可行的。
This approach is practicable in many situations. = 这种方法在许多情况下都是可行的。


一般过去时:
The suggestion was practicable at that time. = 那时这个建议是可行的。
The method proved practicable in the field test. = 该方法在现场测试中被证明是可行的。


现在完成时:
The project has proven practicable. = 该项目已被证明是可行的。
They have found a practicable solution to the problem. = 他们已经找到了一个可行的解决方案。


过去完成时:
The idea had been practicable before the new regulations were implemented. = 在实施新规章之前，这个想法是可行的。
We had considered a practicable alternative. 我们曾考虑过一个可行的替代方案。


将来时:
The new system will be practicable next year. = 新系统明年将可行。
The technology will hopefully be practicable soon.  这项技术有望很快就能实现。


虚拟语气:
If the budget were larger, the plan would be practicable. = 如果预算更大，这个计划将是可行的。
It would be practicable if we had more time.  如果我们有更多的时间，这是可行的。


被动语态:
The proposal was deemed practicable by the committee. = 该提案被委员会认为是可行的。
The plan is considered practicable by the experts.  专家们认为该计划是可行的。


疑问句:
Is the proposal practicable? = 这个提案可行吗？
Is this approach practicable under these conditions? = 在这些条件下，这种方法可行吗？


否定句:
The plan is not practicable. = 这个计划不可行。
The solution is not currently practicable. = 目前这个方案不可行。
