misassignment:/ˌmɪs.əˈsaɪn.mənt/ | n. |错误分配，误派 |The incorrect allocation of a task, resource, or person.  It often results in inefficiency, delays, or errors. |例句：The misassignment of personnel led to project delays.  人员的错误分配导致项目延误。


The misassignment of funds resulted in a budget shortfall.  资金的错误分配导致预算短缺。


近义词：maldistribution, misallocation, wrong assignment |反义词：correct assignment, proper allocation |名词
