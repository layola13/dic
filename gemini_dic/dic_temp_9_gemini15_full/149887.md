palingenetic: /ˌpælɪn.dʒəˈnɛtɪk/ | adj. | 再生的；复生的；返祖的 | Relating to or denoting the process of regeneration or rebirth, especially in geology or biology.  It often refers to the reappearance of ancestral characteristics or the reformation of a landmass from previously existing material. |

例句：

The palingenetic theory suggests that continents are formed and reformed over geological time. = 再生理论认为大陆在地质时期经历了形成和重组的过程。

The artist's work shows a palingenetic interest in classical forms. = 艺术家的作品显示出对古典形式的再生兴趣。


近义词：regenerative, reborn, resurrected, cyclical

反义词：destructive, degenerative, extinct


词性：形容词
