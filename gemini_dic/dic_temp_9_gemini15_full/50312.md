depletory:/dɪˈpliːtəri/| adj.|消耗性的，减少的|Reducing or tending to reduce the amount of something.|The depletory effect of the drought on the water supply was severe. 干旱对供水造成的消耗性影响非常严重。|近义词：depleting, reducing, exhausting|反义词：augmentative, enriching


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The factory's operation is depletory to the local environment. = 工厂的运作正在消耗当地环境。
This medication has a depletory effect on potassium levels. = 这种药物对钾水平有消耗作用。


一般过去时:
The prolonged war had a depletory effect on the nation's resources. = 长期的战争对国家的资源产生了消耗性影响。
His excessive spending had a depletory effect on his savings. = 他过度的支出消耗了他的储蓄。


现在完成时:
Overuse has depleted the ozone layer. = 过度使用已经消耗了臭氧层。(This sentence uses "depleted" which is the verb form, but illustrates the meaning of "depletory")
Years of poor farming practices have had a depletory effect on soil fertility. = 多年糟糕的耕作方式对土壤肥力产生了消耗性影响。


过去完成时:
Before the crisis, the company's resources had been severely depleted by poor management. = 在危机爆发前，公司资源已被糟糕的管理严重消耗殆尽。
They had already experienced the depletory effects of the harsh winter on their crops. = 他们已经经历了严冬对农作物造成的消耗性影响。


将来时:
Continued deforestation will have a depletory effect on biodiversity. = 持续的森林砍伐将对生物多样性产生消耗性影响。
The new policy is expected to have a depletory effect on the national budget. = 新政策预计将对国家预算产生消耗性影响。


虚拟语气:
If we continue at this rate, our resources would be depleted. = 如果我们继续以这种速度，我们的资源将会被消耗殆尽。
If they had known the depletory effect of the drug, they wouldn't have taken it. = 如果他们知道这种药物的消耗性影响，他们就不会服用它。


被动语态:
The water supply was depleted by the drought. = 水源被干旱消耗殆尽。
The national reserves are being depleted by unsustainable practices. = 国家储备正在被不可持续的实践所消耗。


疑问句:
Is the new project depletory to the company's resources? = 新项目是否会消耗公司的资源？
Are there any depletory factors affecting the population growth? = 有哪些消耗性因素影响着人口增长？


否定句:
The policy is not depletory to the environment; instead, it promotes sustainability. = 这项政策不会消耗环境；相反，它促进了可持续性。
The government's actions are not depletory to the economy; they are intended to stimulate growth. = 政府的行动不会消耗经济；它们旨在刺激经济增长。


PHRASE->

depletory substance 消耗性物质
depletory rate 消耗率
