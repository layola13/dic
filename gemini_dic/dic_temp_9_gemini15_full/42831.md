cours:/kur/| n.|课程，过程，路线| a series of lectures or lessons on a particular subject; a process or period of time; a route or direction |例句：The course in history is very interesting. 历史课程非常有趣。  The course of the river is winding. 这条河的河道蜿蜒曲折。|近义词：class, lesson, program, pathway|反义词：end, conclusion


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The course begins next Monday. = 课程下周一开课。
This course covers a wide range of topics. = 这门课程涵盖了广泛的主题。

一般过去时:
The course lasted for ten weeks. = 这门课程持续了十周。
I took a cooking course last year. = 我去年参加了一个烹饪课程。

现在完成时:
I have completed the course. = 我已经完成了这门课程。
She has taken several courses in computer science. = 她已经修过几门计算机科学课程。

过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, the course had already started. = 当我到达时，课程已经开始了。
They had finished the course before the summer break. = 他们在暑假前完成了课程。

将来时:
I will take a photography course next semester. = 下学期我将参加摄影课程。
The course will be taught by Professor Smith. = 这门课程将由史密斯教授讲授。

虚拟语气:
If I had taken that course, I would be better at programming. = 如果我参加了那门课程，我的编程能力会更好。
I wish I had taken more courses during my undergraduate studies. = 我希望我在本科学习期间能多修几门课程。

被动语态:
The course is taught in English. = 这门课程用英语授课。
The course materials were distributed at the beginning of the semester. = 课程材料在学期初发放。

疑问句:
What course are you taking this semester? = 这个学期你修什么课？
Which course is more challenging, math or physics? = 数学和物理，哪门课更具挑战性？

否定句:
I am not taking any courses this summer. = 今年夏天我不参加任何课程。
He did not finish the course on time. = 他没有按时完成课程。


PHRASE->
of course = 当然
in the course of = 在...过程中
in due course = 适时；到一定时候
take a course = 参加课程
a course of action = 行动方针
