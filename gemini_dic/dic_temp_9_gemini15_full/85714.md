havocs:/ˈhævək/| n.|浩劫；破坏；混乱|The plural form of "havoc," referring to widespread destruction or devastation.  It is less common than the singular form.|例句：The storms wreaked havocs across the region, leaving many homes destroyed.  暴风雨给该地区带来了巨大的破坏，许多房屋被摧毁。The invading army's actions caused untold havocs in the countryside. 入侵军的行动在农村地区造成了无数的破坏。|近义词：destruction, devastation, ruin, mayhem, chaos|反义词：order, peace, tranquility, calm

The storms wreak havocs on the coast every year. = 每年暴风雨都会给海岸带来巨大的破坏。
These policies are wreaking havocs on the economy. = 这些政策正在严重损害经济。

虚拟语气:
