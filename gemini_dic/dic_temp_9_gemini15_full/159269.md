poikilocyte:/ˈpɔɪkɪləsaɪt/ | n. |畸形红细胞|An abnormally shaped red blood cell.  Poikilocytes are characterized by variations in size and shape, deviating from the normal biconcave disc form.  Their presence often indicates underlying medical conditions. |Example: The blood smear revealed a high number of poikilocytes, suggesting a possible hemolytic anemia.  血液涂片显示大量畸形红细胞，提示可能存在溶血性贫血。|近义词:abnormal erythrocyte, irregular erythrocyte|反义词:normocyte
