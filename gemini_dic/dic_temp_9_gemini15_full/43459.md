crash-test:/ˈkræʃˌtɛst/| n. |碰撞测试，撞击测试 |A crash test is a controlled experiment conducted to assess the structural integrity and safety features of a vehicle during a collision.  It involves deliberately crashing a vehicle into a barrier or another vehicle at a controlled speed to evaluate its performance in protecting occupants. | Example: The car passed the crash test with flying colors. 这辆车以优异的成绩通过了碰撞测试。 |近义词：impact test, collision test |反义词：无
