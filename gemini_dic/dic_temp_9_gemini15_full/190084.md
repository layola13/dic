sangaree:/ˌsæŋɡəˈriː/| n. |桑格里酒，一种加香料的葡萄酒|A spiced wine, typically made with red wine, brandy, and spices such as cinnamon, cloves, and nutmeg.  It is often served warm. |Example:  We enjoyed a mug of warm sangaree by the fire. = 我们在火炉旁享用了一杯热桑格里酒。  She prefers sangaree to regular wine. = 她更喜欢桑格里酒而不是普通的葡萄酒。|近义词：mulled wine, spiced wine|反义词：无
