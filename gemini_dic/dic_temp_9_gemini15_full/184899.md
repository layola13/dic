retaining:/rɪˈteɪnɪŋ/| v. |保持；保留；雇用；记住|To keep something or someone; to continue to have something; to employ someone.|例句：The dam is designed to retaining water. 这座水坝的设计目的是蓄水。

The company is retaining its top employees.  这家公司正在留住其顶尖员工。


近义词：keeping, preserving, maintaining, employing, remembering|反义词：losing, releasing, dismissing, forgetting|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He retains all the information. = 他记住所有信息。
They retain a lawyer for the case. = 他们为这个案子聘请了一位律师。

一般过去时:
She retained the old house. = 她保留了老房子。
The company retained him as a consultant. = 公司聘请他担任顾问。

现在完成时:
I have retained all my notes. = 我保留了我所有的笔记。
They have retained their market share. = 他们保持了他们的市场份额。

过去完成时:
He had retained a copy of the letter. = 他保留了一份信件的副本。
She had retained her composure throughout the ordeal. = 在整个磨难中，她一直保持镇定。

将来时:
We will retain the services of a professional. = 我们将保留一位专家的服务。
The company will retain its current strategy. = 公司将保留其当前的战略。

虚拟语气:
If we retained our composure, we would succeed. = 如果我们保持冷静，我们就会成功。
I wish I had retained more information. = 我希望我记住了更多信息。

被动语态:
The information was retained by the company. = 信息被公司保留了。
He was retained as a consultant for the project. = 他被聘为该项目的顾问。

疑问句:
Does he retain any memories of his childhood? = 他还记得他童年的任何事情吗？
Will they retain their position in the market? = 他们会在市场上保持自己的地位吗？

否定句:
He did not retain the information. = 他没有记住这些信息。
The company did not retain the services of an architect. = 公司没有聘请建筑师。
