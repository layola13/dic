mammalia:/məˈmeɪliə/| n. |哺乳动物|The class Mammalia includes animals that are characterized by the presence of mammary glands in females, which produce milk to nourish their young.  Mammals also typically have hair or fur, three middle ear bones, and a neocortex region in the brain.|例句：Whales, despite their aquatic lifestyle, belong to the class Mammalia. 鲸鱼尽管生活在水中，但属于哺乳动物纲。|例句：Cats, dogs, and humans are all examples of mammals within the class Mammalia. 猫、狗和人都属于哺乳动物纲的哺乳动物。|近义词：mammal (常用单数形式)|反义词：无直接反义词，可考虑与鸟类、爬行动物等其他动物类别对比|名词
