leucic:/ˈljuːsɪk/| adj. |亮白的，白化的| Relating to or resembling leukocytes (white blood cells); characterized by a whitish or pale color.  The term is less common in modern usage and often replaced by more specific terms.|例句：The leucic discharge from the wound was a cause for concern.  伤口流出的白色分泌物令人担忧。|近义词：whitish, pale, leucocytic|反义词：dark, pigmented
