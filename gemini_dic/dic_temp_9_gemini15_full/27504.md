campfire:/ˈkæmpfaɪər/ | n. |篝火；营火 | A fire built outdoors, especially for warmth or cooking, typically in a campsite.  | We sat around the campfire, roasting marshmallows. 我们围坐在篝火旁烤棉花糖。 | bonfire, fire |  (none explicitly opposite)
