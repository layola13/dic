drips:/drɪps/| v. n.|滴，漏，少量地落下|to fall in drops; to let fall in drops|例句：Water drips from the leaky roof.  水从漏雨的屋顶滴下来。
The paint drips onto the floor. 油漆滴到地板上了。|近义词：trickle, leak, ooze|反义词：pour, gush

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tap drips constantly. 水龙头不停地滴水。
Rain drips from the leaves. 雨水从树叶上滴落。

一般过去时:
The faucet dripped all night. 水龙头整夜都在滴水。
Honey dripped from his chin.  蜂蜜从他的下巴滴落。


现在完成时:
The roof has dripped all winter. 屋顶整个冬天都在漏水。
He has dripped paint all over his shirt. 他把油漆滴到了衬衫上。


过去完成时:
The pipe had dripped for days before we noticed it. 水管已经滴了好几天了，我们才注意到。
The roof had dripped during the storm, causing water damage.  暴风雨期间屋顶一直在漏水，造成了水灾。


将来时:
The melting snow will drip from the eaves. 融化的雪会从屋檐滴下来。
I think the roof will drip again if it rains. 我想如果下雨的话，屋顶还会漏水。


虚拟语气:
If the roof dripped, we would need to repair it. 如果屋顶漏水，我们就需要修理它。
I wish the faucet didn't drip so loudly. 我真希望水龙头不要滴得这么响。


被动语态:
The paint was dripped onto the canvas. 油漆被滴到画布上了。
The water was dripped slowly onto the seedling. 水被慢慢地滴到幼苗上。


疑问句:
Is the faucet dripping again? 水龙头又在滴水了吗？
Why is the roof dripping? 为什么屋顶会漏水？


否定句:
The water doesn't drip anymore. 水不再滴了。
The tap didn't drip last night. 水龙头昨晚没有滴水。
