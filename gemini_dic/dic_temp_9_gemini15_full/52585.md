dig up: /dɪɡ ʌp/ | vt. | 挖掘；掘出；找出；发现；揭露 | To remove something from the ground by digging.  To discover or find out something, often something hidden or forgotten. | 近义词：unearth, excavate, uncover, discover | 反义词：bury, conceal, hide


英文解析：The verb "dig up" literally means to use a tool to remove something from the ground. Figuratively, it means to discover or uncover information or facts that were previously hidden or forgotten, often with the implication of revealing something unpleasant or scandalous.


例句：

一般现在时:
The archaeologists are digging up ancient ruins. = 考古学家正在挖掘古代遗址。
They dig up potatoes in the fall. = 他们在秋天挖土豆。


一般过去时:
The dog dug up my garden. = 狗把我的花园挖了个遍。
She dug up some old photos from her childhood. = 她翻出了童年的一些旧照片。


现在完成时:
We have dug up all the weeds in the flowerbed. = 我们已经把花坛里的所有杂草都挖出来了。
They have dug up some interesting facts about the company's history. = 他们已经挖掘出一些关于公司历史的有趣事实。


过去完成时:
The thieves had dug up the treasure before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，小偷已经挖走了宝藏。
By the time the meeting started, she had dug up all the relevant documents. = 到会议开始的时候，她已经找到了所有相关的文件。


将来时:
The workers will dig up the road next week. = 工人们下周将要挖路。
I will dig up some more information for you. = 我会为你查找更多信息。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would dig up the old letters. = 如果我有更多时间，我会把那些旧信挖出来。
I wish I could dig up the truth about what happened. = 我真希望我能挖掘出事情真相。


被动语态:
The old foundations were dug up during construction. = 在施工过程中，旧地基被挖了出来。
The information was dug up by a journalist. = 这些信息是由一名记者挖出来的。


疑问句:
What are they digging up in the park? = 他们在公园里挖什么？
Have you dug up anything interesting about the case? = 你挖到关于这个案子的什么有趣的东西了吗？


否定句:
They didn't dig up any fossils. = 他们没有挖到任何化石。
I couldn't dig up any information about him online. = 我在网上找不到关于他的任何信息。
