nonmeasurablenesses:/ˌnɒnˌmɛʒərəˈbɪlnɪsəz/ | n. |不可测量性；无法衡量的方面 | The plural of "nonmeasurability," referring to the state or quality of being incapable of measurement.  It often refers to abstract concepts or quantities that lack a defined unit or scale for quantification.  | Example: The nonmeasurablenesses of happiness and sadness make it difficult to quantify their impact on human well-being.  不可测量性快乐和悲伤使得量化它们对人类福祉的影响变得困难。|近义词：unquantifiables, indeterminacies |反义词：measurabilities
