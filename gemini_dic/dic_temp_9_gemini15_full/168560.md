It often involves a sense of knowing something without a rational explanation.  Proception is a less common word and often used in contexts related to parapsychology or intuitive awareness.|例句：She had a strong proception of impending danger, a feeling that something bad was about to happen. 她强烈预感到危险即将来临，感觉有什么不好的事情即将发生。
