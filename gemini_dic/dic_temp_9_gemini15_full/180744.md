reguide:/ˌriːˈɡaɪd/| vt.|重新指导，再引导|To guide again or anew; to guide in a different direction.  It implies a previous guidance that was insufficient, incorrect, or needs updating.|例句：The teacher had to re-guide the student after he made a crucial mistake in his calculations.老师在学生计算中犯了关键性错误后不得不重新指导他。

例句：The navigation system re-guided the driver after he took a wrong turn. 导航系统在司机转错弯后重新为他指引路线。


近义词：redirect, reorient, reinstruct|反义词：misguide, mislead|词性:动词
