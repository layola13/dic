nonsinkable: /ˈnɒnsɪŋkəbl/ /ˈnɑːnsɪŋkəbəl/ | adj. | 不可沉没的 |  Incapable of being sunk;  impossible to sink.  The word implies a design or inherent quality that prevents sinking, rather than simply being afloat. | 例句：The Titanic was once considered nonsinkable, a belief tragically proven wrong.  泰坦尼克号曾经被认为是不可沉没的，这一信念被悲剧性地证明是错误的。

The ship's advanced design made it virtually nonsinkable. 这艘船先进的设计使其几乎不可沉没。


近义词：unsinkable, indestructible, impenetrable

反义词：sinkable, vulnerable, susceptible
