desisted:/dɪˈsɪstɪd/| v.|停止；放弃；终止|To cease or abstain from doing something; to stop.|例句：He desisted from his plan to climb the mountain after hearing the weather forecast. 他在听到天气预报后放弃了登山计划。

例句：The company desisted from its illegal activities after being threatened with legal action.  公司在面临法律行动的威胁后停止了非法活动。


近义词：ceased, stopped, abandoned, refrained, discontinued|反义词：continued, persisted, persevered|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He desists from smoking. = 他戒烟了。
She desists from gossiping. = 她不再说闲话了。

一般过去时:
He desisted from his bad habits. = 他改掉了坏习惯。
They desisted from their argument. = 他们停止了争吵。

现在完成时:
He has desisted from drinking. = 他已经戒酒了。
She has desisted from her complaining. = 她已经停止抱怨了。

过去完成时:
He had desisted from gambling before he lost everything. = 在他倾家荡产之前他已经戒赌了。
They had desisted from their protest before the police arrived. = 在警察到来之前他们已经停止了抗议。

将来时:
He will desist from interfering in our affairs. = 他将不再干涉我们的事务。
She will desist from making those comments. = 她将不再发表那些评论。

虚拟语气:
If he desisted from his bad habits, he would be healthier. = 如果他改掉坏习惯，他会更健康。
I wish he would desist from interrupting me. = 我希望他不要再打断我了。

被动语态:
His bad behavior was desisted from. = 他的不良行为被制止了。(This is less common)
The project was desisted from due to lack of funding. =  由于缺乏资金，该项目被停止了。(This is less common, often replaced by "The project was stopped...")

疑问句:
Will he desist from his actions? = 他会停止他的行动吗？
Did she desist from her demands? = 她放弃了她的要求了吗？

否定句:
He did not desist from his efforts. = 他没有放弃他的努力。
She did not desist from her criticism. = 她没有停止她的批评。


PHRASE->
desist from doing something = 停止做某事
desist from a course of action = 放弃某种行为
desist from harassment = 停止骚扰
desist from further action = 停止进一步行动
desist from illegal activities = 停止非法活动

词源:
From Old French *desister, from Latin desistere, from de- "away, off" + sistere "to stand, halt".  Essentially, "to stand away from," implying stopping or ceasing an action.
