circum:/ˈsɜːrkəm/| /ˈsɜːrkʌm/ |prefix|周围，环绕|around, about, surrounding|The prefix "circum" means "around" or "about". It is used to form compound words.|例句：Circumnavigate the globe. 环绕地球航行。|近义词：peri-,around,encircling|反义词：inside, within


USAGE->
This is a prefix, not a standalone word, so it doesn't have verb conjugations or other grammatical forms.  It's used to create new words. Examples are given below.


PHRASE->
circumference: 周长
circumspect: 谨慎的
circumstances: 环境
circumlocution: 迂回说法
circumscribe: 限制
