jambalayas:/ˌdʒæmbəˈlaɪəz/ | n. | 什锦饭(复数) | A dish of rice cooked with meat (often chicken, sausage, and shrimp), vegetables, and seasonings.  It's a Creole and Cajun dish from Louisiana. |  Example: We had delicious jambalayas at the New Orleans festival. 我们在新奥尔良的节日上吃了美味的什锦饭。  | 近义词：Creole rice, Cajun rice | 反义词：无
