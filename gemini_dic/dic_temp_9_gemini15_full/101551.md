ironist:/ˈaɪrənɪst/| n. |反讽者，讽刺家 |A person who uses irony or sarcasm.  An ironist often employs language to convey a meaning that is the opposite of the literal meaning, or to highlight a discrepancy between expectation and reality. |Example: The comedian was a master ironist, using witty remarks to expose the flaws in society.  那位喜剧演员是一位反讽大师，他用诙谐的言语揭露社会的缺陷。|近义词：satirist, cynic, wit |反义词：literalist, naïf
