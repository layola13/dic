interquarter:/ˌɪntərˈkwɔːrtər/| adj. |季度间的；季度的|  Relating to the period between two financial quarters.  This term is primarily used in financial contexts to describe data, reports, or events that occur between the official end of one financial quarter and the beginning of the next.|例句：The interquarter performance was surprisingly strong, exceeding expectations. 季度间业绩出人意料地强劲，超过预期。|近义词：between-quarter, mid-quarter|反义词：intraquarter
