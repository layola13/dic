decidabilities:/ˌdɪsɪdəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可判定性；可决定的性质|The study of decidabilities in formal languages focuses on whether algorithms exist to determine the truth or falsity of statements within those languages.  可判定性在形式语言的研究中关注的是是否存在算法来确定这些语言中语句的真假。|例句：The decidabilities of certain logical systems have been extensively investigated.  某些逻辑系统的可判定性已被广泛研究。|近义词：determinability, solvability |反义词：undecidability, unsolvability
