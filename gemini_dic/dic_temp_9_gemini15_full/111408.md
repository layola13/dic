livraison:/li.vʁɛ.zɔ̃/| n.|交付，递送，供应|The act of delivering something; the thing delivered.  It often refers to the delivery of goods, but can also refer to the delivery of information or a service.|例句：The livraison of the furniture is scheduled for next week. 家具的交付安排在下周。|例句：The livraison of the news was met with shock. 消息的公布令人震惊。|近义词：delivery, shipment, supply|反义词：receipt (in the sense of receiving)
