peached:/piːtʃt/| v. |告发，检举；泄露秘密；|to inform on someone; to reveal a secret;  to accuse someone of wrongdoing. |例句：He peached on his friends to the teacher. 他向老师告发了朋友。|例句：She peached about the surprise party. 她泄露了惊喜派对的秘密。|近义词：betray, inform on, denounce, squeal |反义词：conceal, protect, keep secret


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He peaches on his classmates regularly. = 他经常告发他的同学。
She peaches every secret she hears. = 她会泄露她听到的每一个秘密。


一般过去时:
He peached on his brother to their parents. = 他向父母告发了弟弟。
She peached to the police about the robbery. = 她向警方举报了抢劫案。


现在完成时:
He has peached on his friends many times. = 他已经多次告发他的朋友。
She has peached about the company's illegal activities. = 她已经泄露了公司非法活动的秘密。


过去完成时:
He had peached on his accomplice before the trial. = 他在审判前就告发了同伙。
She had peached about the plan, ruining everything. = 她已经泄露了计划，毁了一切。


将来时:
He will peach on anyone who crosses him. = 他会告发任何得罪他的人。
She will peach if she's forced to. = 如果她被迫，她会泄露秘密。


虚拟语气:
If he peached, he would face consequences. = 如果他告发，他将面临后果。
I wish I hadn't peached on him. = 我真希望我没有告发他。


被动语态:
He was peached on by his own brother. = 他被自己的兄弟告发了。
The secret was peached to the media. = 这个秘密被泄露给了媒体。


疑问句:
Did he peach on the criminals? = 他告发了罪犯吗？
Will she peach under pressure? = 她在压力下会泄露秘密吗？


否定句:
He didn't peach on his friend, despite the pressure. = 尽管面临压力，他并没有告发他的朋友。
She didn't peach about the meeting, keeping it secret. = 她没有泄露会议内容，保守了秘密。
