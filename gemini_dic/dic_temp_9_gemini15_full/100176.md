interregnum:/ˌɪntərˈreɡnəm/ | n. | 权力真空期；过渡时期；空位期 | A period of time between two reigns or regimes during which there is no ruler.  It often refers to a period of instability or uncertainty. | Example: The assassination of the king led to a period of interregnum, marked by civil strife and political maneuvering.  国王遇刺导致了一段权力真空期，期间充满了内乱和政治斗争。 | 近义词: interim, hiatus, gap | 反义词: reign, rule, governance |  名词
