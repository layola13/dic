go-aheads:/ˈɡoʊ əˈhɛdz/ | n. |许可，批准，同意 |A go-ahead is permission to start or continue with something.  It signifies approval or authorization.  The term is informal. |例句：We received the go-ahead to start the project. = 我们收到了启动项目的许可。

The project finally got the go-ahead after months of delays. = 在经历了几个月的延误后，该项目最终获得了批准。


近义词：approval, authorization, permission, consent, sanction |反义词：refusal, rejection, prohibition, veto |词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The manager gives the go-ahead to new projects frequently. = 经理经常批准新的项目。
They always need a go-ahead before making significant changes. = 他们在进行重大更改之前总是需要批准。


一般过去时:
The committee gave the go-ahead for the construction yesterday. = 委员会昨天批准了这项建设。
She received the go-ahead last week and started immediately. = 她上周得到了批准，并立即开始了。


现在完成时:
They have finally received the go-ahead for their research. = 他们终于获得了研究的批准。
We haven't yet received the go-ahead from the board of directors. = 我们尚未得到董事会的批准。


过去完成时:
Before starting the renovation, they had received the go-ahead from the landlord. = 在开始装修之前，他们已经得到了房东的批准。
The team had already gotten the go-ahead when the funding was secured. = 资金到位时，团队已经获得了批准。


将来时:
The CEO will give the go-ahead for the expansion next month. = 首席执行官下个月将批准扩张计划。
We expect to get the go-ahead for the new software launch next quarter. = 我们预计下个季度将获得新软件发布的批准。


虚拟语气:
If we had received the go-ahead earlier, we would have finished by now. = 如果我们早点得到批准，我们现在就完成了。
I wish they would give us the go-ahead soon so we can begin. = 我希望他们能尽快批准，以便我们开始。


被动语态:
The project was given the go-ahead after a thorough review. = 该项目经过彻底审查后获得了批准。
The proposal is currently being considered; the go-ahead is expected soon. = 提案目前正在审议中；预计很快就会获得批准。


疑问句:
Has the go-ahead been given for the marketing campaign? = 市场推广活动是否已经获得批准？
Will we get the go-ahead before the deadline? = 我们能在截止日期前获得批准吗？


否定句:
The team didn't receive the go-ahead due to budget constraints. = 由于预算限制，团队没有获得批准。
They were not given the go-ahead to proceed with the risky experiment. = 他们没有得到进行危险实验的批准。
