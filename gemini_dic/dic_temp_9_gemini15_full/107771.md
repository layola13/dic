lapidification:/ˌlæpɪdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |石化；化石化|The process by which organic matter is converted into stone or a stone-like substance.  This often involves the replacement of organic material with minerals. |The lapidification of the ancient tree resulted in a stunning petrified wood specimen.  古代树木的石化作用形成了一件令人惊叹的硅化木标本。|petrification, fossilization|decomposition, decay|名词
