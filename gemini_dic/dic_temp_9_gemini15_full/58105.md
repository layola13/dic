droplet:/ˈdrɒplət/ /ˈdrɑːplət/| n. |小滴，液滴 |A small drop of liquid.  |例句：A droplet of water clung to the leaf.  一滴水粘在叶子上。

例句：Rain fell in tiny droplets. 雨下起了细小的雨点。


近义词：drop, speck


反义词：None (Antonyms are not directly applicable, as it describes a quantity, not a quality)


词性:名词


USAGE->

The droplet falls to the ground. = 水滴落到地上。
A droplet of oil floats on the water. = 一滴油漂浮在水上。

一般过去时:
The droplet landed on my nose. = 水滴落在了我的鼻子上。

The droplet has already evaporated. = 水滴已经蒸发了。
