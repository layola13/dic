priory:/ˈpraɪəri/| n. |修道院；修道院院长管辖的寺院|A priory is a religious community of monks or nuns, typically headed by a prior. It is often, but not always, smaller than an abbey.  Priories were often founded as dependencies of larger monasteries.|例句：The old priory is now a museum.  这座古老的修道院现在是一座博物馆。

The priory was founded in the 12th century. 这座修道院建于12世纪。|近义词：monastery, abbey, convent |反义词：None specifically, but terms like "secular building" or "lay institution" could be considered contrasting.
