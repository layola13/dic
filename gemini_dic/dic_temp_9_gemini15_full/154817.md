perturbingly:/pərˈtɜːrbɪŋli/ | adv. |令人不安地，令人烦恼地 | In a way that causes anxiety or concern.  It describes something that is disturbing or unsettling. |例句：Perturbingly, the evidence suggests a cover-up.  令人不安的是，证据表明存在掩盖行为。
The silence was perturbingly long.  沉默得令人不安地长。|近义词：disturbingly, unsettlingly, worryingly, alarmingly |反义词：reassuringly, calmingly, comfortingly
