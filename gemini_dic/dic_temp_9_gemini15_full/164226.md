predominately:/prɪˈdɒmɪnətli/ | adv. | 主要地，大部分地 |  chiefly; mainly; for the most part.  It describes something that is the most noticeable or important feature.  | 例句：The city's population is predominantly Asian. = 这个城市的人口主要是亚洲人。| 例句：The team's success was predominantly due to their hard work. = 球队的成功主要归功于他们的努力工作。 | 近义词：mostly, mainly, chiefly, largely, primarily | 反义词：minimally, scarcely, rarely
