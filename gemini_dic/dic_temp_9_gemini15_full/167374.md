prettyism:/ˈprɪtiɪzəm/| n. |漂亮主义；以貌取人；注重外貌的倾向|The belief that physical attractiveness is the most important quality in a person or thing.  It often involves prioritizing appearances over other attributes such as intelligence, character, or talent.  This can manifest in various ways, from superficial judgments to discriminatory practices.|例句：The hiring manager's prettyism led to the rejection of highly qualified but less conventionally attractive candidates.  招聘经理的以貌取人导致了对许多高素质但外貌不够出众的候选人的拒绝。|近义词：superficiality, appearance-based bias, favoritism, aestheticism (in a negative context)|反义词：meritocracy, egalitarianism
