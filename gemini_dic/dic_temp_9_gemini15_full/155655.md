/ˈflɛɡməɡɔːɡ/| n. |祛痰药，排痰剂|A phlegmagogue is a medicine that promotes the expulsion of phlegm or mucus from the respiratory tract.  Phlegmagogues work by stimulating the secretion of mucus and facilitating its removal.  They are often used to treat respiratory conditions characterized by excessive mucus production, such as coughs and bronchitis.|例句：The doctor prescribed a phlegmagogue to help clear the patient's congested lungs.  医生开了祛痰药来帮助病人清理堵塞的肺部。|近义词：expectorant, mucolytic|反义词：N/A
