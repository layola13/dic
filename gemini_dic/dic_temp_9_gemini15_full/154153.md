perimysium:/ˌpɛrɪˈmɪziəm/| n. |肌束膜|A sheath of connective tissue enclosing a bundle of muscle fibers.  It surrounds groups of muscle fibers, called fascicles, providing support and structure to the muscle.  The perimysium contains blood vessels and nerves that supply the muscle fibers within the fascicles. |Example:  The perimysium plays a crucial role in transmitting force generated by individual muscle fibers to the tendon.|近义词：fascicular sheath|反义词：endomysium (surrounds individual muscle fibers), epimysium (surrounds the entire muscle)|名词
