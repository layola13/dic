migrated:/maɪˈɡreɪtɪd/ | v. | 迁移；移居；迁徙 | To move from one place to another, especially to a new country or area to live permanently.  | 例句：Many people migrated from the countryside to the city in search of work. 许多人为了找工作从农村迁移到城市。

Many birds migrate south for the winter. 许多鸟类冬天会向南迁徙。


近义词：emigrated, relocated, transferred, moved

反义词：immigrated (in the context of people moving into a country)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
Birds migrate south every winter. = 鸟类每年冬天都向南迁徙。
People migrate for various reasons. = 人们因为各种原因而迁移。


一般过去时:
My family migrated to America in 1990. = 我的家人在1990年移居美国。
The population migrated towards the coast. = 人口向海岸迁移。


现在完成时:
Millions have migrated from rural areas to urban centers. = 数百万的人口从农村地区迁移到城市中心。
They have migrated to a new country and started a new life. = 他们已经迁移到一个新的国家，并开始了新的生活。


过去完成时:
Before the war, many people had already migrated to safer areas. = 在战争爆发之前，许多人已经迁移到更安全的地方。
The birds had migrated before the first snow. = 在第一场雪来临之前，鸟类就已经迁徙了。


将来时:
We will migrate to a warmer climate next year. = 我们明年将迁移到气候更温暖的地方。
Many more people are expected to migrate to the cities in the coming years. = 预计未来几年将有更多的人迁移到城市。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would migrate to a tropical island. = 如果我有钱，我会迁移到一个热带岛屿。
I wish I could migrate to a country with better job opportunities. = 我希望我能迁移到一个就业机会更好的国家。


被动语态:
Many species have been migrated due to climate change. = 许多物种由于气候变化而被迫迁移。(注意：被动语态在此处略显不自然，主动语态更常用)
The population was migrated to new settlements after the flood.  = 洪水过后，人口被迁移到新的定居点。(同样，主动语态更常用)


疑问句:
Why did they migrate to this region? = 他们为什么迁移到这个地区？
Where will the family migrate next? = 这个家庭接下来会迁移到哪里？


否定句:
They did not migrate last year. = 他们去年没有迁移。
The birds haven't migrated yet this year. = 鸟类今年还没有迁徙。



PHRASE->
mass migration = 大规模迁移
seasonal migration = 季节性迁移
bird migration = 鸟类迁徙
human migration = 人类迁移
forced migration = 被迫迁移
