duettists:/djuˈɛtɪsts/| n. |二重唱者；二重奏演奏者 |People who perform duets.  Duettists are skilled musicians who collaborate to create harmonious musical performances. They often specialize in a particular instrument or vocal style. |Example: The duettists captivated the audience with their flawless performance. 二重唱者们以他们完美的表演迷住了观众。|近义词: duet performers, duo |反义词: soloist, orchestra
