self-restraint:/ˌsɛlf rɪˈstreɪnt/| n. |自制力，克制|The ability to control oneself, especially one's emotions and desires.|He showed remarkable self-restraint in the face of provocation. 他在面对挑衅时表现出了非凡的自制力。|近义词：self-control, moderation, discipline|反义词：impulsiveness, lack of control, indulgence|


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She practices self-restraint daily. = 她每天都练习自制力。
He demonstrates self-restraint in his spending habits. = 他在花钱习惯上表现出自制力。


一般过去时:
The child showed commendable self-restraint during the party. = 这个孩子在聚会上表现出了值得称赞的自制力。
She exercised self-restraint and didn't lash out at him. = 她克制住了自己，没有对他发火。


现在完成时:
He has shown great self-restraint throughout this difficult period. = 在这段艰难时期，他表现出了极大的克制力。
She has maintained self-restraint despite the many temptations. = 尽管有很多诱惑，她仍然保持着自制力。


过去完成时:
He had practiced self-restraint for years before he finally achieved his goal. = 在最终实现目标之前，他已经练习自制力很多年了。
She had exercised considerable self-restraint, but finally snapped.  = 她已经克制很久了，但最终还是爆发了。


将来时:

He will demonstrate self-restraint when he faces his opponent. = 当他面对对手时，他会表现出自制力。


虚拟语气:
If he had more self-restraint, he wouldn't have made that mistake. = 如果他更有自制力，他就不会犯那个错误了。
I wish I had more self-restraint when it comes to desserts. = 我希望我在甜点方面更有自制力。


被动语态:

疑问句:
