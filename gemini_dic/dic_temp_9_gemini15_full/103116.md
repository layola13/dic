jerkies:/ˈdʒɜːrkiːz/| n.|牛肉干，肉干|Dried, cured meat, typically beef, but also other meats like venison or turkey.  It's a preserved food, often high in protein and sodium.|例句：I bought a bag of beef jerky for the hike.  我买了一袋牛肉干去远足。
例句：They sell various kinds of jerky at the gas station.  加油站卖各种各样的肉干。|近义词：dried meat, biltong (South African jerky)|反义词：fresh meat
