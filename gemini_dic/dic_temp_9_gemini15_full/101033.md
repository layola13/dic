invection:/ɪnˈvɛkʃən/| n. |痛骂；猛烈批评；(尤指长篇的) 谴责；| vehement verbal abuse; a strong denunciation or condemnation, especially a lengthy one.  |Example:  His speech was filled with invective against the government's policies.  他的演讲充满了对政府政策的痛骂。


Example: The critic unleashed a torrent of invective against the author's latest novel.  评论家对作者的最新小说发起了猛烈的抨击。


近义词：abuse, denunciation, diatribe, philippic, vituperation
