intersectional:/ˌɪntərˈsɛkʃənəl/| adj.|交叉的；交集的；具有交叉性别的|Relating to or denoting the interconnected nature of social categorizations such as race, class, and gender, creating overlapping and interdependent systems of discrimination or disadvantage.|例句：Intersectionality is a crucial framework for understanding social justice issues. 交叉性是一个理解社会正义问题的关键框架。|近义词：overlapping, interconnected, multi-faceted|反义词：isolated, singular, unidimensional
