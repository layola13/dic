proletariat: /ˌproʊlɪˈtɛəriət/ | n. |无产阶级 |The working class, especially those who lack ownership of the means of production.  This term is often used in Marxist theory to describe the class of wage earners who are exploited by the bourgeoisie (the capitalist class).|例句：Marx believed the proletariat would eventually overthrow the bourgeoisie. 马克思相信无产阶级最终会推翻资产阶级。

例句：The struggles of the proletariat are central to many historical narratives.  无产阶级的斗争是许多历史叙事中的核心。


近义词：working class, laboring class, commoners |反义词：bourgeoisie, elite, aristocracy


词性：名词
