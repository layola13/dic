primrose:/ˈprɪmroʊz/| n. |报春花；淡黄色|A primrose is a small flowering plant with bright yellow flowers, often blooming early in the spring.  It is also used to describe a pale yellow color.|例句：The primroses were blooming in the garden. 花园里的报春花正在盛开。|例句：Her dress was a delicate primrose shade. 她的裙子是淡黄色的。|近义词：cowslip, yellow flower|反义词：N/A
