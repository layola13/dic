self-survivor: /ˌsɛlf səˈvaɪvər/ | n. | 自救者，依靠自身生存者 |  A person who manages to survive a difficult or dangerous situation through their own efforts, without outside help.  |  例句：After the shipwreck, she was a true self-survivor, finding food and shelter on her own.  在沉船事故后，她凭借自己的努力找到了食物和住所，是一位真正的自救者。


例句：The hiker, lost in the wilderness for days, proved to be a remarkable self-survivor.  在荒野迷路数日的徒步旅行者证明自己是一位非凡的自救者。


近义词： survivor, self-reliant person, independent person


反义词： dependent person, victim


词性: noun
