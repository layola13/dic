reclose:/ˌriːˈkloʊz/| vt.|重新关闭；再次关闭|To close something again after it has been opened.|The shop assistant reclosed the box after showing me the contents. 店员在我看完内容后又把盒子关上了。|close, shut|open|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recloses the door every night. 他每晚都重新关上门。
She recloses the window after airing the room. 她通风后重新关上了窗户。


一般过去时:
They reclosed the gate after the cattle passed through. 牛群通过后，他们重新关上了栅栏。
He reclosed the file after saving the document. 他保存文档后重新关闭了文件。


现在完成时:
She has reclosed the account after reviewing the balance. 她审查余额后已重新关闭了账户。
They have reclosed the deal after further negotiations. 经过进一步谈判后，他们已重新达成了协议。


过去完成时:
The manager had reclosed the office before the storm hit. 暴风雨来临之前，经理已重新关闭了办公室。
We had reclosed the windows before the rain started. 下雨前我们已经关上了窗户。
