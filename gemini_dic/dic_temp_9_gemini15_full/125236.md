multicuspid:/ˌmʌltiˈkʌspɪd/| adj.|多尖的；多尖头的|Having many cusps or points.  The term is often used in dentistry to describe teeth with multiple cusps. |例句：The molar had a multicuspid surface, making it ideal for grinding food. 这颗磨牙表面有多个尖头，非常适合磨碎食物。

The premolar's multicuspid crown efficiently processed the food. 这颗前磨牙的多尖牙冠有效地处理了食物。|近义词：multipointed, multifaceted|反义词：unicuspid
