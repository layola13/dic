papilio:/pəˈpɪli.oʊ/| n. |凤蝶属；蝴蝶|The word "papilio" refers to a genus of swallowtail butterflies, characterized by their long, tail-like projections on their hindwings.  These butterflies are often large and brightly colored, and many species are known for their striking patterns.  The genus is quite diverse, with numerous species found across the globe.| Example:  Many species of *Papilio* are popular among butterfly collectors.  许多凤蝶属的蝴蝶深受蝴蝶收藏家的喜爱。|近义词：swallowtail (as a common name, not a genus)|反义词：无
