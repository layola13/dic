forestal: /fɔːˈrestəl/ | v. | 预防；阻止；避免 | To prevent or hinder the occurrence of something.  |  The government implemented measures to forestal the spread of the disease. 政府实施措施以预防疾病蔓延。| prevent, avert, preclude | accelerate, encourage, promote | 动词

一般现在时:
They forestal potential problems by careful planning. = 他们通过仔细的计划来预防潜在的问题。
We forestal any misunderstandings by clear communication. = 我们通过清晰的沟通来避免任何误解。

一般过去时:
The quick action forestalled a major accident. = 快速的行动阻止了一场重大事故。
She forestalled criticism by apologizing in advance. = 她提前道歉以避免批评。

过去完成时:

They had forestalled bankruptcy by making wise investments. = 他们通过明智的投资避免了破产。

将来时:
We will forestal future issues with better management. = 我们将通过更好的管理来预防未来的问题。
They will forestal any attempts at sabotage. = 他们将阻止任何破坏企图。

虚拟语气:

I wish we had forestalled the conflict. = 我希望我们当时能够避免这场冲突。

被动语态:
