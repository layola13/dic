chillies:/ˈtʃɪliz/| n. |辣椒，红辣椒|Chillies are a type of pepper that are typically hot and spicy.  They are used in many cuisines around the world to add flavor and heat to dishes.|例句：I added some chillies to the stew to make it spicier. 我在炖菜里加了一些辣椒，让它更辣。|例句：These chillies are incredibly hot; handle with care. 这些辣椒非常辣；小心处理。|近义词：peppers, chilies, red peppers|反义词：sweet peppers
