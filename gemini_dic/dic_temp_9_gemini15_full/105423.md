kidnapping:/ˈkɪdnæpɪŋ/ | n. | 绑架；诱拐 | The act of unlawfully taking a person hostage, usually for ransom or other purposes.  Kidnapping is a serious crime with severe penalties. | The kidnapping of the heiress made headlines worldwide.  全世界都报道了这位继承人的绑架案。| abduction, seizure | release, freedom | 名词
