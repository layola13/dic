nonchastisement:/ˌnɒnˈtʃæstɪzmənt/| n. |免于惩罚；不惩罚|The absence of punishment or discipline.  It implies a lenient or forgiving approach.  This word is quite rare and may be found mostly in formal or specialized texts.|Example: The judge showed remarkable nonchastisement in sentencing the offender.|近义词：leniency, forbearance, clemency|反义词：punishment, chastisement, discipline|名词
