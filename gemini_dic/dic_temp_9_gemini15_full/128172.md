networked:/ˈnɛtwɜːkt/ | adj. |联网的；网络化的 | Connected together as a network; operating as part of a network.  This adjective describes something that is part of or interconnected with a network, often referring to computers, devices, or systems. |例句：The networked computers allow for easy data sharing.  联网的电脑方便数据共享。

The company's networked infrastructure enables efficient communication. 公司的网络基础设施实现了高效的沟通。


近义词： interconnected, online, linked, connected | 反义词：isolated, unconnected, offline


词性: adjective
