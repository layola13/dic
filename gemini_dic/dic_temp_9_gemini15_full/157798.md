placing:/ˈpleɪsɪŋ/| v.|放置；安置；摆放|The act of putting something in a particular position.  It can also refer to the position something is in, or the emphasis given to something.|例句：He was placing the books on the shelf. 他正在把书放在书架上。

例句：The placing of the statue was carefully considered.  雕像的摆放经过仔细考虑。


近义词：positioning, setting, putting, locating|反义词：removing, taking away, discarding
