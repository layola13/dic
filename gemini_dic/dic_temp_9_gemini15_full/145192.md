overbait:/ˈoʊvərbeɪt/| vt.|过度诱饵，使用过多的诱饵|To use more bait than is necessary or appropriate, often in fishing or hunting.|例句：The fisherman overbaited his hook, resulting in a tangled mess.  渔夫在鱼钩上放了过多的诱饵，导致鱼钩缠结在一起。|例句：They overbaited the trap, scaring away the animals they were trying to catch. 他们放了过多的诱饵，吓跑了他们想捕捉的动物。|近义词：overlure, overload|反义词：underbait
