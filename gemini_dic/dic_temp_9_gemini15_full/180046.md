reflectibilities: /ˌriˌflɛktəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 反射性；反射能力 |  The capacity of a substance or surface to reflect light, sound, or other radiation.  It is a less common word, often used in scientific or technical contexts.  The plural form emphasizes the diverse or multiple aspects of reflectivity. |


例句：

The reflectibilities of different paints were tested under various lighting conditions.  不同油漆的反射性在各种照明条件下进行了测试。

Studies on the reflectibilities of various materials are crucial for optimizing solar energy collection.  对各种材料反射性的研究对于优化太阳能收集至关重要。


近义词：reflectances,  reflective properties


反义词：absorptivities (吸收性)


词性：名词


词源：

* **reflect:**  From Latin *reflectere*, "to bend back," from *re-* "back" + *flectere*, "to bend."
* **-ibility:**  Suffix indicating capacity or capability, from Latin *-ibilitas*.
* **-ies:** Plural suffix.


Therefore, "reflectibilities" literally means "the capacities or capabilities of reflecting."
