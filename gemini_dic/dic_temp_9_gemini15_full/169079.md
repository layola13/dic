progression:/prəˈɡreʃən/| n. |进展；级数；递增；发展|The gradual or continuous advancement or development towards a better state.|例句：The progression of the disease was slow but steady.  疾病的进展缓慢但稳定。|例句：The musical piece features a fascinating progression of chords. 这首乐曲包含一组引人入胜的和弦进行。|近义词：advancement, development, progress, sequence|反义词：regression, decline, deterioration


USAGE->

The disease progression is slow. = 疾病进展缓慢。
His career progression is remarkable. = 他的职业发展令人瞩目。

The progression of the project was delayed. = 项目的进展被耽搁了。
