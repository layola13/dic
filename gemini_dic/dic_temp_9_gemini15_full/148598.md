overtechnically: /ˌoʊvərˈtɛknɪkli/ | adv. | 过于技术性地，技术性过强地 |  Excessively or unnecessarily technical in manner or style.  It implies that the technical details are overwhelming or obscure the main point. |例句：He explained the process overtechnically, losing the audience’s attention. 他解释过程过于技术化，失去了听众的注意力。

例句：The report was overtechnically written, making it difficult for non-experts to understand. 这份报告写得太技术化了，非专家难以理解。


近义词：technically, overly technically, excessively technical


反义词：simply, plainly, understandably
