harpist:/ˈhɑːrpɪst/| n. |竖琴演奏家|A harpist is a musician who plays the harp.  Harpists are often skilled in classical music, but some also play in other genres.|例句：The concert featured a renowned harpist from Ireland. 这场音乐会有一位来自爱尔兰的著名竖琴演奏家。|例句：She is a talented harpist, capable of playing both delicate and powerful melodies. 她是一位才华横溢的竖琴演奏家，能够演奏细腻而强劲的旋律。|近义词：harp player, harper|反义词：无
