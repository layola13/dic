gigues:/ʒiːɡz/| n.| 吉格舞 | A gigue is a lively, quick dance in 6/8 or 3/4 time, typically characterized by its fast tempo, jumping movements, and often a playful or virtuosic character. It's a type of dance and musical form that originated in France and became popular throughout Europe.  | Example: The final movement of the suite was a spirited gigue.  (这组曲的最后一部分是一支充满活力的吉格舞。) | 近义词：jig, reel, hornpipe | 反义词：slow dance, ballad
