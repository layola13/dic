calcivorous:/kælˈsɪvərəs/| adj.| 嗜钙的，食钙的 |  Relating to or denoting organisms that derive nutrients from calcium compounds, especially calcium carbonate.  | Example:  Many soil organisms are calcivorous, thriving in calcareous environments. 许多土壤生物是嗜钙的，在钙质环境中茁壮成长。  | 近义词:calciphilic | 反义词:calcifuge
