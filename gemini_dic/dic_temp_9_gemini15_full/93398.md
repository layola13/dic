illiquation:/ɪˌlɪkwˈeɪʃən/| n. |液化；熔化；变现|The process of converting something from a solid or semi-solid state to a liquid state, or the process of converting an asset into cash.  It often refers to the melting of a solid or the selling of an asset to obtain cash.| Example: The illiquation of the metal was achieved by heating it to its melting point. 金属的液化是通过加热到其熔点实现的。 The illiquation of their assets was necessary to pay off their debts.  他们变卖资产以偿还债务是必要的。|近义词：liquefaction, melting, fusion, monetization|反义词：solidification, freezing, crystallization
