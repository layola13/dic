preoblige:/ˌpriːˈɒblɪdʒ/| v.|预先强制；预先承担义务 |To impose an obligation on someone in advance; to bind someone beforehand to a course of action.  |例句：The contract preobliged the company to deliver the goods by the end of the month. = 合同预先强制公司在月底前交付货物。|近义词：obligate, bind, commit |反义词：release, free


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The contract preobliges the company to deliver on time. = 合同预先强制公司准时交付。
He preobliges himself to finish the project before the deadline. = 他预先承担义务在截止日期前完成项目。


一般过去时:

She preobliged herself to attend the meeting. = 她预先承担义务参加会议。
