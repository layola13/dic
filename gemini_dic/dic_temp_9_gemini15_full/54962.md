disprofitable:/ˌdɪspˈrɑːfɪtəbl/| adj.|无利可图的，亏本的 | Unprofitable; not yielding a profit.  The venture proved disprofitable, leading to significant financial losses.  | 近义词：unprofitable, losing, uneconomical | 反义词：profitable, lucrative, gainful |


例句：

The new product line proved disprofitable and was quickly discontinued. = 新产品线证明是无利可图的，很快就被停产了。

Investing in that failing company was a disprofitable decision. = 投资那家亏损的公司是一个亏本的决定。
