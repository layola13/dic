precluding: /prɪˈkluːdɪŋ/ | v. | 排除；阻止；妨碍 | Preventing something from happening or being possible.  |例句：His age precluded him from joining the army.  他的年龄使他无法参军。


例句：The heavy rain precluded any possibility of an outdoor picnic. 大雨使得户外野餐成为不可能。


近义词：preventing, hindering, obstructing, barring, debarring


反义词：allowing, permitting, enabling


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The contract precludes any further negotiation. = 合同排除了任何进一步的谈判。
His beliefs preclude him from accepting this job. = 他的信仰使他无法接受这份工作。

一般过去时:
The storm precluded our travel plans. = 暴风雨打乱了我们的旅行计划。
The noise precluded her from concentrating on her work. = 噪音使她无法集中精力工作。

现在完成时:
His injury has precluded him from participating in the competition. = 他的伤势使他无法参加比赛。
The bad weather has precluded us from going hiking. = 糟糕的天气使我们无法去远足。

过去完成时:
The earlier accident had precluded him from attending the meeting. = 此前的意外事故使他无法参加会议。
Her previous commitments had precluded her from accepting the invitation. = 她之前的承诺使她无法接受邀请。

将来时:
The expected snowstorm will preclude all flights. = 预计的暴风雪将导致所有航班取消。
A lack of funding will preclude the project from starting on time. = 资金不足将导致项目无法按时启动。

虚拟语气:
If the weather had been better, it wouldn't have precluded our trip. = 如果天气更好，它就不会阻止我们的旅行。
If he hadn't been ill, his illness wouldn't have precluded his participation. = 如果他不生病，他的病就不会阻止他的参与。

被动语态:
Further action was precluded by the lack of evidence. = 由于缺乏证据，进一步的行动被阻止了。
Our plans were precluded by unforeseen circumstances. = 我们的计划由于不可预见的情况而被阻止了。

疑问句:
Does his illness preclude him from working? = 他的病是否妨碍他工作？
Would a lack of resources preclude the success of the project? = 资源匮乏是否会阻碍项目的成功？

否定句:
Nothing could preclude her from achieving her goals. = 没有什么能够阻止她实现目标。
The agreement did not preclude further discussions. = 该协议并没有排除进一步的讨论。


PHRASE->
preclude from = 排除…；阻止…
preclude the possibility of = 排除…的可能性
preclude any chance of = 排除任何…的机会
preclude someone from doing something = 阻止某人做某事
preclude further action = 阻止进一步行动


词源:
From Latin *praecludere, from prae- "before" + claudere "to close, shut."  字面意思是“在前面关闭”，引申为“阻止，排除”。
