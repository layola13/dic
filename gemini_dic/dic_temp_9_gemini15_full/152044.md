patellas:/pəˈtɛləz/ | n. | 髌骨（复数） | The patellas are the kneecaps, the small, triangular bones that sit at the front of the knee joint. They protect the knee joint and help with the extension of the leg.  | Example: The impact fractured his patellas.  这次撞击导致他的髌骨骨折。 |近义词: kneecaps |反义词: N/A
