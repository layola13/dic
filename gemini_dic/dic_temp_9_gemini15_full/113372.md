lychnis:/ˈlɪknɪs/| n. |石竹属植物|Lychnis is a genus of flowering plants in the family Caryophyllaceae, commonly known as catchflies.  They are characterized by their showy flowers and often sticky stems.  Many species are popular garden ornamentals. |The lychnis flowers bloomed brightly in the summer garden. 石竹属植物在夏日的花园里盛开.|近义词：Silene (related genus)|反义词：无
