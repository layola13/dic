go halves:/ɡoʊ hævz/| v.|平分，对半分|To share something equally, especially the cost of something.|例句：Let's go halves on the pizza.咱们平分这个披萨吧。|例句：We decided to go halves on the taxi fare. 我们决定平摊出租车费。|近义词：split, share, divide|反义词：keep entirely, take all


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They go halves on the rent every month. = 他们每个月平分房租。
We go halves on the petrol. = 我们平摊汽油费。

一般过去时:
We went halves on the bill last night. = 昨晚我们平摊了账单。
They went halves on the cost of the gift. = 他们平摊了礼物的费用。


现在完成时:
We have gone halves on many projects together. = 我们一起合作过许多项目，都平分了收益/成本。
They have gone halves on the apartment’s expenses. = 他们已经平摊了公寓的费用。


过去完成时:
They had gone halves on the holiday before they realized how expensive it was. = 在他们意识到假期有多贵之前，他们已经平分了费用。
We had gone halves on the dinner before the argument started. = 在争吵开始之前，我们已经平分了晚餐费用。


将来时:
We will go halves on the drinks tonight. = 今晚我们将平分酒水费用。
They will go halves on the wedding costs. = 他们将平分婚礼的费用。


虚拟语气:
If we went halves, we could afford the concert tickets. = 如果我们平分费用，我们就能买得起演唱会门票。
I wish we had gone halves on the car repairs. = 我真希望我们当时能平分汽车修理费。


被动语态:
The cost of the meal was gone halves. = 餐费被平分了。(此用法较少见)
The expenses were gone halves between the partners. = 费用由合伙人平分。(此用法较少见)


疑问句:
Shall we go halves on this? = 我们平分这个怎么样？
Do you want to go halves on the dessert? = 你想平分甜点吗？


否定句:
They didn't want to go halves on the project. = 他们不想平分这个项目的费用。
We didn't go halves on the lunch bill. = 我们没有平分午餐的账单。


PHRASE->
go halves on the bill = 平摊账单

go halves on the expenses = 平摊支出
go halves on a meal = 平分一餐
go halves with someone = 和某人平分
