ribboned:/ˈrɪbənd/ | adj. | 用丝带装饰的；有丝带的 | Adorned with ribbons; having ribbons. |例句：The little girl wore a ribboned hat.  小女孩戴着一顶系着丝带的帽子。

The beautifully ribboned presents sat under the Christmas tree. 精美地系着丝带的礼物摆放在圣诞树下。|近义词：decorated, adorned, embellished |反义词：bare, undecorated
