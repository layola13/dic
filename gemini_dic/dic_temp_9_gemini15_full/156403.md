phytochemistry:/ˌfaɪtoʊˈkɛmɪstri/| n. |植物化学|The scientific study of phytochemicals, which are chemicals produced by plants.  It encompasses the identification, characterization, and quantification of these chemicals, as well as their biological activities and effects on humans and other organisms.  Phytochemistry is closely related to botany, biochemistry, and pharmacology. |Example:  Research in phytochemistry has led to the discovery of many valuable medicines derived from plants.  植物化学的研究导致了许多从植物中提取的宝贵药物的发现。|近义词：plant chemistry, botanical chemistry|反义词：无
