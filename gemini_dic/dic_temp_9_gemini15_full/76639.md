gallygaskins:/ˈɡæliɡæskɪnz/| n.|大步走；阔步走|A gallygaskins is a type of walking or gait characterized by long, swinging strides. The term is archaic and now largely humorous or whimsical.|例句：He strode along with a gallygaskins gait, his long legs eating up the ground. 他迈着大步走着，长腿飞快地跑过地面。|近义词：stride, swagger,  stroll|反义词：tiptoe, shuffle, creep|名词
