kiddy's:/ˈkɪdiz/| n. |孩子们的；小孩的 |Kiddy's is a possessive form of "kiddy,"  referring to something belonging to or associated with children. It's often used informally and is sometimes considered slightly childish or condescending.  |This is a kiddy's playground.|This is the kiddy's corner in the library.|近义词：children's, kids'|反义词：adult's
