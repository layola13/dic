rad:/ræd/ | adj. | 极好的；酷的；激烈的 | extremely good; excellent; cool; intense | 例句：That concert was rad! 那场音乐会太棒了！  That new bike is totally rad! 那辆新自行车真是酷毙了！| 近义词：awesome, great, fantastic, cool | 反义词：awful, terrible, lousy
