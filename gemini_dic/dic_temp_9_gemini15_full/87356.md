heroics:/hɪˈroʊɪks/| n.|英勇行为，壮举|Brave or daring actions.  Heroics often involve risk and courage.|例句：The firefighters displayed incredible heroics in rescuing the trapped children. 消防队员在营救被困儿童时表现出令人难以置信的英勇行为。

例句：Her heroics saved the lives of many people. 她的英勇行为挽救了许多人的生命。
