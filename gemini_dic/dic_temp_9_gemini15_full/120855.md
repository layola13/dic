misbehaver:/ˌmɪsbiˈheɪvər/| n. |不守规矩的人；捣蛋鬼；行为不端者 |A person who behaves badly or disobediently.  |例句：The misbehaver was sent to the principal's office.  那个不守规矩的孩子被送到了校长办公室。|例句：That mischievous misbehaver needs a time-out.  那个淘气的捣蛋鬼需要冷静一下。|近义词：troublemaker, delinquent, rascal, rogue |反义词：well-behaved person, angel, saint
