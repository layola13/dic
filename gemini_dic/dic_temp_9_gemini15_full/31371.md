charbroilers:/ˈtʃɑːrbroʊlər/| n.|炭火烧烤炉；炭烤架 |A charbroiler is a type of cooking appliance that uses high heat from charcoal or gas to cook food, often giving it a distinctive smoky flavor.  Charbroilers are commonly used in restaurants and homes for grilling meats, vegetables, and other foods. |例句：The restaurant’s charbroiler was sizzling with delicious steaks. 这家餐厅的炭火烧烤炉正发出美味牛排的滋滋声。

The chef expertly used the charbroiler to cook the salmon.  厨师熟练地使用炭火烧烤炉烹制鲑鱼。|近义词：grill, barbecue, broiler |反义词：oven, steamer
