misplanting:/ˌmɪsˈplæntɪŋ/| n.|误植，栽种错误|The act of planting something in the wrong place or in an unsuitable way.  It often refers to plants, but can also be used figuratively.|例句：The misplanting of the young saplings resulted in poor growth.  误植幼苗导致生长不良。

例句：The misplanting of the evidence led to the wrong person being convicted. 证据的误植导致错误的人被定罪。|近义词：improper planting, incorrect planting, wrong planting|反义词：correct planting, proper planting|名词
