/ɡɪlts/| n. |政府债券；劣质猪肉|Government securities issued by a national or local government.  Gilts are considered low-risk investments.  The term "gilts" is primarily used in the UK and other Commonwealth countries.|例句：The government issued new gilts to finance its infrastructure projects. = 政府发行新的政府债券来为其基础设施项目融资。

例句：Investing in gilts is often seen as a safe haven for investors. = 投资政府债券通常被视为投资者的安全港。

近义词：bonds, government securities, treasury bills|反义词：high-risk investments, equities|名词
