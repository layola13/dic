orbite:/ˈɔːrbɪt/ | n. |轨道，眼眶 |The path of a celestial body or artificial satellite as it revolves around another of greater mass.  The bony cavity in the skull containing the eye. |例句：The Earth orbits the Sun. 地球绕着太阳运行。

The satellite is in a stable orbit.  卫星处于稳定轨道。


近义词：trajectory, path, course | 反义词：None specifically.
