/ˈpɑːlənaɪz/ /ˈpɒlənaɪz/ | vt. | 传粉；授粉 | To transfer pollen to the stigma of a flower, thus enabling fertilization.  | The bees pollenized the apple blossoms. 蜜蜂给苹果花授粉了。 The farmer pollenized the corn plants by hand. 农民手工给玉米授粉。 | fertilize, pollinate |  deprive of pollen | v.
