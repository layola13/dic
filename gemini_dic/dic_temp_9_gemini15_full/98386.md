insolence: /ˈɪnsələns/ | n. | 傲慢；无礼；放肆 |  The quality or state of being insolent; rude or disrespectful behavior.  It often implies a defiant disregard for authority or propriety. |  例句：Her insolence towards her elders was shocking.  她的长辈面前如此傲慢无礼，令人震惊。

例句：His insolence led to his dismissal. 他的傲慢无礼导致了他被解雇。


近义词：impudence, audacity, impertinence, disrespect, rudeness, arrogance

反义词：respect, politeness, deference, humility, courtesy


词性：名词
