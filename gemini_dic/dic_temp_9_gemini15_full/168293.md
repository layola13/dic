pro-Moslem: /ˌproʊˈmɑːzləm/ | adj. |亲穆斯林的，支持穆斯林的 |  Supporting or favoring Muslims or Islam.  The term often implies a positive stance towards Muslims and their beliefs and practices. However, it can be perceived as biased or even controversial depending on the context.  | Example:  He was known for his pro-Moslem views and often spoke out against Islamophobia. 他以其亲穆斯林的观点而闻名，并经常公开反对伊斯兰恐惧症。
