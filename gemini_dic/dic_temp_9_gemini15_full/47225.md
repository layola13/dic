dazedness:/ˈdeɪzdnɪs/| n. |茫然；昏沉；恍惚 | A state of being dazed or confused; a feeling of mental disorientation.  It often follows a shock, injury, or overwhelming experience. |例句：The dazedness in his eyes showed the impact of the accident. 他眼神中的茫然显示了事故对他造成的冲击。

例句：After the marathon, a profound dazedness settled over me. 马拉松之后，我感到深深的茫然。


近义词：confusion, bewilderment, disorientation, stupor, numbness

反义词：clarity, alertness, awareness, lucidity
