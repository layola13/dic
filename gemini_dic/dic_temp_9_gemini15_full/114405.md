make ends meet:/ˌmeɪk ˈɛndz ˈmit/| v. | 使收支平衡；维持生计 | To earn just enough money to pay for your living expenses.  |例句：It's difficult to make ends meet on such a low salary.  靠这么低的薪水很难维持生计。|例句：We're just about making ends meet this month, thanks to the extra work.  多亏了额外的工作，这个月我们勉强能维持生计。|近义词：survive, subsist, get by |反义词：live luxuriously, prosper, thrive |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They make ends meet by working extra hours. = 他们通过加班来维持生计。
We make ends meet each month with careful budgeting. = 我们每个月通过精打细算来维持生计。

一般过去时:
They made ends meet by selling their old car. = 他们通过卖掉旧车来维持生计。
We made ends meet last month, but it was very tight. = 上个月我们勉强维持生计，但非常紧张。

现在完成时:
We have made ends meet for the past five years despite the economic downturn. = 尽管经济低迷，我们在过去五年里一直维持生计。
They have made ends meet through hard work and frugality. = 他们通过努力工作和节俭来维持生计。

过去完成时:
They had made ends meet for years before the business finally succeeded. = 在生意最终成功之前，他们已经维持生计多年了。
We had made ends meet by cutting back on expenses. = 我们通过削减开支来维持生计。

将来时:
We will make ends meet next month if we get the bonus. = 如果我们拿到奖金，下个月就能维持生计。
They will make ends meet by taking on additional part-time jobs. = 他们将通过承担额外的兼职工作来维持生计。

虚拟语气:
If they had a higher salary, they would easily make ends meet. = 如果他们的工资更高，他们就能轻松维持生计。
If we earned more money, we could make ends meet without struggling. = 如果我们赚更多的钱，我们就能轻松维持生计，而不用挣扎。

被动语态:  (This phrase is rarely used in the passive voice because it's intransitive.)


疑问句:
How do they make ends meet with such a large family? = 他们有这么大家庭是如何维持生计的？
Can you make ends meet on your current income? = 你目前的收入能维持生计吗？

否定句:
They cannot make ends meet on their current salaries. = 他们目前的工资无法维持生计。
We couldn't make ends meet last year without help from family. = 去年如果没有家人的帮助，我们就无法维持生计。
