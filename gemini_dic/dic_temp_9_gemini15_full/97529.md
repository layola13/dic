ingraff:/ɪŋˈɡræf/| v.|嫁接；移植|To ingraft is to insert a slip of a plant into a stock, or to graft.|例句：The gardener skillfully ingrafted the new rose variety onto the existing rootstock. 园丁熟练地将新品种玫瑰嫁接到现有的砧木上。


例句：The surgeon successfully ingrafted the patient's new skin. 外科医生成功地为病人移植了新的皮肤。|近义词：graft, implant|反义词：remove, excise
