do the honors:/duː ðiː ˈɒnərz/| v. |主持；担任礼仪性角色；先请；率先行动|To perform a task or act of courtesy that is traditionally or customarily performed by someone of a certain status, typically involving hospitality or a formal occasion.  It often implies offering something to someone or starting a process.|例句：Would you do the honors and carve the turkey? 你能主持一下，把火鸡切了吗？

例句：I'd be delighted to do the honors and introduce our keynote speaker. 我很乐意担任礼仪性角色，介绍我们的主题演讲嘉宾。


近义词：initiate, commence, preside over, officiate


反义词：decline, refuse, abstain


词性:动词
