parapodium:/pəˈræpədiəm/| n. |侧足，副足 |A parapodium is a paired, unjointed, fleshy outgrowth on the body of some invertebrates, such as polychaete worms, used for locomotion or respiration.  | Example: The polychaete worm used its parapodia to crawl along the seabed.  多毛蠕虫用它的侧足在海底爬行。|近义词:appendage, limb (in a broader sense)|反义词:None
