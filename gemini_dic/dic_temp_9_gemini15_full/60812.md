electrophorus:/ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒfərəs/| n. |静电起电机|A device for generating static electricity by friction.  It typically consists of a resinous plate and a metal disk.  The resinous plate is rubbed to create a static charge, which is then transferred to the metal disk when it is placed on the plate and briefly grounded.| Example: The scientist used an electrophorus to demonstrate the principles of static electricity.  科学家用静电起电机演示了静电的原理。|近义词：electrifying machine|反义词：无
