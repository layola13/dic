roan:/roʊn/| adj.|  栗色的，红褐色的；(马) 花色的 |  Describing a horse's coat, roan refers to a mixture of white and another color, usually red or bay, giving a mottled or speckled appearance.  It can also describe a reddish-brown color. |The roan horse stood proudly in the field. 这匹栗色马骄傲地站在田野里。  Her hair was a beautiful roan shade. 她的头发是美丽的红褐色。 |bay, red, chestnut| black, white|
