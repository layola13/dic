polygenesis:/ˌpɒliˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/| n. |多基因遗传；多起源说；多发生|The theory that a species or trait originated independently in more than one place or time.  It contrasts with monogenesis, which posits a single origin.|例句：The polygenesis of the Indo-European languages is a debated topic among linguists.  多源论是语言学家们争论不休的话题之一。|例句：Recent genetic studies suggest a polygenesis for certain human traits. 最近的基因研究表明某些人类特征的多起源。|近义词：multiple origins, independent origins |反义词：monogenesis
