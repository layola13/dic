reanalyzable:/ˌriːˈænəlaɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 可重新分析的 | Capable of being analyzed again; subject to reanalysis.  This word describes something that can be subjected to a second or further analysis, often implying a different perspective or new information is available.  | The data is reanalyzable, given the new evidence.  重新分析数据，考虑到新的证据。 | analyzable, examinable | unanalyzable, unexaminable | 形容词
