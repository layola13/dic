haddest:/ˈhædəst/| v. |过去完成时第二人称单数|拥有过；持有过；经历过|The word "haddest" is the second-person singular past perfect form of the verb "to have." It indicates that you possessed something or experienced something before a specific point in the past.  |You haddest seen the wonders of the world before your journey to the East.|had, possessed, experienced|lost, lacked, missed|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I have a car. = 我有一辆车。
You have a beautiful house. = 你有一栋漂亮的房子。


一般过去时:
I had a dog. = 我曾经养过一条狗。
She had a dream. = 她做了一个梦。


现在完成时:
I have had many experiences. = 我有很多经历。
He has had a tough life. = 他过着艰难的生活。


过去完成时:
I had had enough by then. = 到那时我已经受够了。
She had had a long day. = 她度过了漫长的一天。


将来时:
I will have a party. = 我将举办一个派对。
They will have finished by then. = 到那时他们就完成了。


虚拟语气:
If I had had more time, I would have finished. = 如果我有更多时间，我就会完成了。
I wish I had had the opportunity. = 我真希望我有这个机会。


被动语态:
The book had been had by someone. = 这本书曾经被某人拥有过。(This sentence is awkward and rarely used.)
The cake had been had before we arrived. = 蛋糕在我们到达之前已经被吃掉了。(This uses "had" in a passive sense of consumption)


疑问句:

Had she been there before? = 她以前去过那里吗？


否定句:
I had not seen him before. = 我以前没见过他。
She hadn't had breakfast. = 她还没吃早餐。
