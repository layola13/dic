exportation:/ˌɛkspɔːrˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 出口；输出 | The act of exporting goods or services from one country to another.  It involves the movement of goods across international borders for sale or trade.  | Example: The exportation of coffee beans is a major industry in many countries.  咖啡豆出口是许多国家的支柱产业。  The government implemented new policies to boost the exportation of manufactured goods. 政府实施了新的政策以促进制成品的出口。 |近义词：export, shipment, trade | 反义词：importation, import
