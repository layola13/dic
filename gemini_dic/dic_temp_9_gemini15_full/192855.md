seascapes:/ˈsiːskeɪps/| n. |海景；海景画|A seascape is a picture or view of the sea, especially one showing its dramatic qualities.  It often depicts the sea and the sky, along with elements such as ships, cliffs, or beaches. |例句：The artist painted a breathtaking seascape of a stormy sea.  这位艺术家画了一幅令人叹为观止的风暴大海景画。


The gallery features many stunning seascapes from the local coast. 这家画廊展出了许多当地海岸令人惊叹的海景画。|近义词：coastal view, marine view, ocean view |反义词：landscape (purely land-based view)
