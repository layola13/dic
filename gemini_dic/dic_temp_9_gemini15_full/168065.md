privatizes:/ˈpraɪvətaɪzɪz/ | v. | 私有化；将…私有化 | To transfer from state or public ownership to private ownership.  This often involves selling state-owned assets to private companies or individuals. |

例句：
The government privatizes the railway system. = 政府将铁路系统私有化了。

The company privatizes its operations to increase efficiency. = 公司将其业务私有化以提高效率。


近义词：denationalize, commercialize

反义词：nationalize, socialize
