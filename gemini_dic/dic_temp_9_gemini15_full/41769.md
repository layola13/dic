corruptingly:/kəˈrʌptɪŋli/ | adv. | 腐败地；堕落地 | In a corrupting manner; tending to corrupt.  This adverb describes actions or situations that cause corruption or moral decay.  | 例句：The system was corruptingly influenced by money and power. 这个系统受到了金钱和权力的腐蚀性影响。


The dictator ruled corruptingly, enriching himself while his people starved. 独裁者腐败地统治，自己富裕起来，而人民却挨饿。 | 近义词：debauchingly, depravingly, vitiatingly | 反义词：virtuously, honestly, ethically | 词性: 副词
