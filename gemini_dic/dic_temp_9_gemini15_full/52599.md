digest:/daɪˈdʒɛst/| n., vt.|摘要；消化；领会|To break down food in the stomach; to understand fully; a summary of something.|The dog quickly digested its food.  这只狗很快消化了它的食物。|He struggled to digest the complex information. 他努力消化这些复杂的信息。|absorb, assimilate, summarize|undigested, incomprehensible|noun, verb


USAGE->

I digest my food slowly. = 我慢慢消化食物。
She digests information quickly. = 她快速理解信息。

一般过去时:
I digested the entire chapter last night. = 我昨晚消化了整章内容。
