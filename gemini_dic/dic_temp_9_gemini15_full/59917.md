edentata:/ˌeɪdənˈteɪtə/| n.|贫齿类动物|A taxonomic order of mammals characterized by the absence or reduction of teeth.  They include anteaters, sloths, and armadillos.|Example:  Edentata is an order that includes several unique species adapted to various niches.  贫齿类包含几种适应不同生态位的独特物种。|近义词:xenarthra (a more modern and preferred term)|反义词:无
