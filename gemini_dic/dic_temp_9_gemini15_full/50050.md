denouncement: /dɪˈnaʊnsmənt/ | n. | 公开谴责；告发；揭露 | The act of denouncing someone or something publicly;  a strong condemnation.  It often implies a formal or official statement. | 例句：The denouncement of the dictator's regime was met with widespread applause.  = 对独裁政权的公开谴责受到了广泛的赞扬。

The denouncement of the corrupt officials led to their arrest. = 对腐败官员的揭露导致了他们的逮捕。


近义词：condemnation, criticism, rebuke, exposure, accusation, indictment

反义词：praise, commendation, approval, endorsement


词性: 名词
