emissive:/ɪˈmɪsɪv/| adj.| 发射的；辐射的 | Relating to the emission or capability of emitting radiation, especially light or heat.  | Example:  The emissive power of the sun is immense. 太阳的辐射能力巨大。 The lamp has a high emissive efficiency.  这盏灯的发射效率很高。|近义词：radiant, emitting |反义词：absorptive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun emits light and heat.  太阳发出光和热。
This material has a high emissive coefficient.  这种材料具有高的发射系数。

一般过去时:
The volcano emitted a large amount of ash. 火山喷发出大量的火山灰。
The star emitted a powerful burst of energy. 这颗恒星发出强大的能量爆发。

现在完成时:
The experiment has emitted unexpected results. 实验产生了意想不到的结果。(emitted 指的是实验的结果，而非实验本身)
The company has emitted a press release on the matter. 公司就此事发布了一份新闻稿。(emitted 指的是公司发布新闻稿的行为)

过去完成时:
The reactor had emitted dangerous levels of radiation before the shutdown.  反应堆在关闭之前已经释放出危险水平的辐射。
They had emitted a warning signal before the accident. 他们在事故发生前发出了警报信号。

将来时:
The satellite will emit signals back to Earth.  卫星将向地球发射信号。
The factory will not emit pollutants into the river. 工厂将不会向河流排放污染物。

虚拟语气:
If the object emitted more light, we could see it better. 如果物体发出更多光，我们就能更好地看到它。
If the source had emitted a stronger signal, we would have received it. 如果信号源发出更强的信号，我们就会接收到它。

被动语态:
Light is emitted by the sun. 光是由太阳发出的。
A warning was emitted by the control system. 控制系统发出警告。

疑问句:
What kind of radiation is emitted by this device?  这个装置发射什么类型的辐射？
Does this material emit infrared light?  这种材料是否发射红外光？

否定句:
The device does not emit any harmful radiation. 该装置不发射任何有害辐射。
The star is not currently emitting any visible light. 这颗恒星目前没有发出任何可见光。
