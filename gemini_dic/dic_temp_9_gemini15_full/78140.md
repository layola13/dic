geometrize:/dʒiˈɒmɪtraɪz/| vt.|使几何化；用几何图形表示|To represent or interpret something using geometric shapes or principles.  It often implies simplifying or abstracting a complex form into a geometric one. |例句：The artist geometrized the landscape, reducing the natural curves to sharp angles and planes.  艺术家将风景几何化，将自然曲线简化为锐角和平面。

例句：The architect geometrized the building's design, emphasizing clean lines and simple shapes. 建筑师使建筑物的设计几何化，强调简洁的线条和简单的形状。


近义词：abstract, simplify, schematize, formalize

反义词：naturalize, organicize


词性:动词
