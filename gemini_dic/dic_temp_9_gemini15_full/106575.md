kshatriya:/ˈʃætriə/| n. |刹帝利，武士阶层 |A kshatriya is a member of the second highest of the four traditional Hindu castes (varnas), typically associated with warriors, rulers, and administrators.  They were traditionally responsible for protecting society and upholding dharma (righteousness). |The kshatriya caste played a crucial role in ancient Indian society. |近义词：warrior, noble, ruler |反义词：shudra (laborer)
