organophosphates:/ˌɔːrɡənoʊˈfɒsfets/ | n. |有机磷酸酯|Organophosphates are chemical compounds containing phosphorus bonded to organic groups. They are widely used in various applications, including insecticides, herbicides, and nerve agents.  Their toxicity varies greatly depending on the specific structure.  |例句：Many organophosphates are highly toxic to insects and other invertebrates. 许多有机磷酸酯对昆虫和其他无脊椎动物具有剧毒。|例句：The improper use of organophosphates can lead to environmental contamination.  有机磷酸酯的不当使用会导致环境污染。|近义词：phosphorus esters, organophosphorus compounds|反义词：N/A
