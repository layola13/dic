shrewmouse:/ʃruːmaʊs/| n. |鼩鼱|A small, mouse-like insectivorous mammal with a long snout and tiny eyes.  Shrewmice are characterized by their aggressive behavior and high metabolism. They are often confused with mice, but belong to a different family.|Example: The shrewmouse scurried across the forest floor, searching for insects.  这只鼩鼱在森林地面上快速跑动，寻找昆虫。|近义词：shrew, insectivore |反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with larger mammals or herbivores.
