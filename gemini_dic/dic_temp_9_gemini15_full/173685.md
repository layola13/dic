quasi-madly: /ˈkwɑːzaɪˈmædlɪ/ | adv. | 近乎疯狂地，几乎疯狂地 |  Describes an action or state that is almost or seemingly mad, but not completely so. It suggests a degree of intensity and perhaps irrationality bordering on madness. | 例句：He worked quasi-madly to finish the project before the deadline. 他近乎疯狂地工作以赶在截止日期前完成项目。  She laughed quasi-madly at the absurdity of the situation. 她近乎疯狂地嘲笑着局面的荒谬。 | 近义词：frantically, wildly, feverishly, almost insanely | 反义词：calmly, rationally, sanely | 词性：副词
