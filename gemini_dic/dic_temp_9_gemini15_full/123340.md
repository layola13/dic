monolingualism: /ˌmɒnəˈlɪŋɡwəlɪzəm/ | /ˌmɑːnəˈlɪŋɡwəlɪzəm/ | n. | 只说一种语言；单语现象 | The state or fact of speaking only one language.  It often refers to the condition of a person, community, or nation speaking only one language.  | Example:  His monolingualism in English presented a challenge when he traveled to Spain. 他只会说英语，这使得他去西班牙旅行时遇到了挑战。 |近义词：unilingualism | 反义词：multilingualism, bilingualism
