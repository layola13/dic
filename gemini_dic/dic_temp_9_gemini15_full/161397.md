posttoxic: /ˌpoʊstˈtɑːksɪk/ | adj. | 后毒性的，毒性后期的 |  Relating to the period or effects following a toxic event or exposure.  This term often refers to the long-term consequences and lingering effects of a toxic substance or situation,  even after the initial exposure has ended. It can encompass environmental, social, or psychological impacts. | Example: The posttoxic effects of the chemical spill were devastating to the local ecosystem.  |近义词:  late-stage toxic, lingering toxic,  aftermath (of toxicity) | 反义词:  pretoxic, nontoxic | 词性: adjective
