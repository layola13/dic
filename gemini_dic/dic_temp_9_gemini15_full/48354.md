decubation:/ˌdɛkjuˈbeɪʃən/| n. |孵化；潜伏期；培养|The process of incubating or being incubated; the period of incubation.|例句：The decubation period for chicken eggs is typically 21 days.  鸡卵的孵化期通常为21天。


例句：The decubation of the virus before symptoms appear can last for several days.病毒潜伏期在症状出现前可能持续几天。


近义词：incubation, brooding, hatching|反义词：None specifically contrasting|词性:名词
