shrink-wrapped:/ʃrɪŋkˈræpt/| adj.|收缩包装的；热收缩包装的|Wrapped in plastic film that shrinks tightly when heated.  This protects the item from damage and tampering.|例句：The software came in a shrink-wrapped box.  软件装在一个热收缩包装的盒子里。|例句：The new phone was shrink-wrapped to prevent theft.  新手机用热收缩膜包装以防止被盗。|近义词：sealed, packaged, protected|反义词：unwrapped, open


USAGE->

The store shrink-wraps all its electronics. = 商店用收缩膜包装所有的电子产品。
He shrink-wraps his valuable paintings for shipping. = 他用收缩膜包装他珍贵的画作以便运输。

词源:

shrink (收缩) + wrapped (包裹的)  "shrink" 源自古英语 *scrincan，意为“皱缩，萎缩”。 "wrapped" 是 wrap (包裹) 的过去分词，wrap 的词源可追溯到古英语 *wrappan，意为“包裹，缠绕”。  因此，shrink-wrapped 指的是一种用收缩膜包裹的包装方式。
