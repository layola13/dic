nonsigners:/ˈnɒnˌsaɪnərz/ | n. | 未签字者，未签署者 | People who have not signed a document or agreement.  This term often refers to those who haven't signed a petition, contract, or other legal document. | Example: The organizers were disappointed by the high number of nonsigners to the petition.  组织者对请愿书上未签字的人数之多感到失望。 The report included a list of nonsigners to the treaty. 报告中列出了未签署条约的人员名单。 | 近义词:  unsigned parties, those who haven't signed | 反义词: signers, subscribers
