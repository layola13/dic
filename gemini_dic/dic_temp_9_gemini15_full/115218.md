manifestoed:/ˌmænɪˈfɛstoʊd/ | v. |公开声明；表明；显示 | To make evident or manifest; to show clearly.  |例句：His anger manifested itself in a loud outburst. 他的愤怒以大声的爆发表现出来。
His beliefs were clearly manifested in his actions. 他的信仰在他的行动中得到了清晰的体现。 |近义词：revealed, demonstrated, displayed, expressed |反义词：concealed, hidden, suppressed |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He manifests his support for the team every week. = 他每周都表现出对球队的支持。
The symptoms manifest themselves in various ways. = 症状以各种方式表现出来。


一般过去时:
The artist manifested his talent through his paintings. = 艺术家通过他的绘画展现了他的才能。
His fear manifested itself in trembling hands. = 他的恐惧表现为颤抖的双手。


现在完成时:
She has manifested a remarkable resilience in the face of adversity. = 面对逆境，她表现出了非凡的韧性。
The disease has manifested itself in several patients. = 这种疾病已经在几位患者身上显现出来。


过去完成时:
The problem had manifested itself before the project even began. = 这个问题甚至在项目开始之前就已经显现出来了。
His genius had manifested itself early in his life. = 他的天才在他早年就显现出来了。


将来时:
The effects of the medication will manifest themselves gradually. = 药物的效果将逐渐显现。
The results of the experiment will hopefully manifest themselves next week. = 我们希望实验结果下周能够显现出来。


虚拟语气:
If he manifested more courage, he would succeed. = 如果他表现出更多勇气，他就会成功。
I wish he would manifest more interest in his studies. = 我希望他对他的学习表现出更多兴趣。


被动语态:
His displeasure was manifested in a sharp letter. = 他的不满在一封措辞尖锐的信中得到了体现。
The symptoms were manifested differently in each patient. = 症状在每个患者身上表现不同。


疑问句:
How did his anger manifest itself? = 他的愤怒是如何表现出来的？
Does the disease manifest itself in any specific way? = 这种疾病是否以任何特定方式表现出来？


否定句:
His feelings did not manifest themselves outwardly. = 他的感情没有外露。
The problem did not manifest itself until much later. = 这个问题直到很久以后才显现出来。
