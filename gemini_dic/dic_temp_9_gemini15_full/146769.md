overhypes:/ˈoʊvərhaɪps/| vt.|过度宣传，炒作|To promote or publicize something excessively or with exaggerated claims.|例句：The media overhyped the new product, leading to disappointing sales. 媒体对这款新产品进行了过度宣传，导致销量令人失望。|近义词：hype, puff up, build up|反义词：downplay, undersell|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overhype their products every year. = 他们每年都过度宣传他们的产品。
The company overhypes its new technology. = 这家公司过度宣传其新技术。

一般过去时:
The movie was overhyped before its release. = 这部电影在上映前被过度宣传了。
They overhyped the concert, and many people were disappointed. = 他们过度宣传了这场音乐会，许多人都感到失望。

现在完成时:

虚拟语气:
