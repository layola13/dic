hospitalized:/ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪzd/ /ˈhɑːspɪtəlaɪzd/| adj.|住院的，被送进医院的|Being placed in a hospital for medical treatment or observation.|The injured athlete was hospitalized after the accident. 受伤的运动员在事故后被送进了医院。|近义词：admitted (to hospital), confined (to hospital)|反义词：discharged, released


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He is hospitalized for a heart condition. 他因心脏病住院。
She is hospitalized and recovering well. 她住院了，恢复得很好。


一般过去时:
My grandfather was hospitalized last week. 我爷爷上周住院了。
The child was hospitalized immediately after the car crash. 车祸后，孩子立即被送往医院。


现在完成时:
He has been hospitalized for three days. 他已经住院三天了。
She has been hospitalized since the surgery. 她自从手术后就一直住院。


过去完成时:
They had been hospitalized for a month before they were released. 他们在出院前已经住院一个月了。
He had been hospitalized several times before this incident. 在这次事件之前，他已经住院过好几次了。


将来时:
He will be hospitalized for further observation. 他将被送往医院进行进一步观察。
The doctor said she will likely be hospitalized next week. 医生说她可能下周住院。


虚拟语气:
If I were hospitalized, I would miss my vacation. 如果我住院了，我会错过我的假期。
I wish I hadn't been hospitalized last year. 我真希望去年我没有住院。


被动语态:
He was hospitalized against his will. 他被强迫住院。
Many patients are hospitalized due to the flu season. 许多病人因流感季节而住院。


疑问句:
Is he hospitalized? 他住院了吗？
Was she hospitalized after the accident? 她事故后住院了吗？


否定句:
He was not hospitalized; he was treated as an outpatient. 他没有住院；他作为门诊病人接受治疗。
She wasn't hospitalized because her injuries were minor. 她没有住院，因为她的伤势很轻微。
