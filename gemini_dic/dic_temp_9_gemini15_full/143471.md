outgains:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪnz/| vt.|胜过；超过|To surpass or exceed in gain, profit, or advantage.|例句：His investment outgained all expectations. 他的投资超过了所有预期。
例句：The company's new strategy outgained its competitors in market share.  公司的新的策略在市场份额上超过了竞争对手。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip, outperform|反义词：underperform, lag behind, fall short of|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new marketing campaign outgains the previous one. = 新的营销活动胜过之前的活动。
Our profits outgain those of our competitors. = 我们的利润超过了竞争对手。

一般过去时:
Last year's sales outgained our projections. = 去年销售额超过了我们的预期。
The team outgained their rivals in the final match. = 该队在决赛中胜过了对手。

现在完成时:
The stock market has outgained inflation this year. = 今年股市涨幅超过通货膨胀。
Our investment strategy has outgained all others. = 我们的投资策略胜过了所有其他策略。

过去完成时:
By the end of the project, our team had outgained the other teams in terms of efficiency. = 到项目结束时，我们的团队在效率方面已经超过了其他团队。
Before the merger, the smaller company had outgained the larger one in terms of innovation. = 在合并之前，规模较小的公司在创新方面已经超过了规模较大的公司。

将来时:
We expect our new product to outgain its competitors in the market. = 我们预计我们的新产品将在市场上胜过竞争对手。
Analysts predict that renewable energy will outgain fossil fuels in the coming decade. = 分析师预测，未来十年可再生能源将超过化石燃料。

虚拟语气:
If we had invested in that company, we would have outgained the market. = 如果我们投资那家公司，我们的收益就会超过市场。
I wish our efforts had outgained the challenges we faced. = 我希望我们的努力能克服我们面临的挑战。

被动语态:
The competitor was outgained by our innovative product. = 竞争对手被我们的创新产品超越了。
The old system was outgained by the new, more efficient one. = 旧系统被新的、更高效的系统超越了。

疑问句:
Did our team outgain the other teams in the competition? = 我们队在比赛中超过其他队伍了吗？
Will this strategy outgain our previous efforts? = 这一策略会比我们之前的努力更有效吗？

否定句:
Our efforts did not outgain the difficulties we encountered. = 我们的努力没有克服我们遇到的困难。
The company failed to outgain its competitors in the market. = 公司未能超过竞争对手。
