edentulous:/ɪˈdɛntəlʊs/| adj.|无齿的，缺牙的|Lacking teeth.  The term refers to the absence of teeth, either natural or artificial.  It can describe a person, animal, or even a jawbone. |例句：The elderly patient was edentulous and required dentures.  这位老年患者无牙，需要安装假牙。

例句：The edentulous skull provided clues about the animal's diet. 这具无牙的头骨为研究动物的饮食提供了线索。


近义词：toothless, lacking teeth,  without teeth

反义词：dentate, toothed


词性:形容词
