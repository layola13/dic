owners:/ˈoʊnərz/ | n. |所有者，业主 |People or organizations that own something.  The owners of the company decided to expand their business. 公司的所有者决定扩大他们的业务。|近义词：proprietors, possessors |反义词：tenants, renters


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The owners manage the property effectively. = 业主有效地管理着这处房产。
These owners contribute to the community. = 这些业主为社区做出了贡献。


一般过去时:
The previous owners neglected the building's maintenance. = 前任业主忽视了建筑物的维护。
The original owners sold the land to a developer. = 最初的业主将土地卖给了一位开发商。


现在完成时:
The owners have invested heavily in renovations. = 业主们已大量投资于翻新工程。
The new owners have already made significant changes. = 新业主已经做出重大改变。


过去完成时:
The previous owners had already installed new windows before selling the house. = 前任业主在卖房前已经安装了新的窗户。

将来时:
The owners will increase the rent next year. = 业主明年将提高租金。

虚拟语气:
