grenade:/ɡrəˈneɪd/| n.|手榴弹|A small, explosive weapon that is thrown by hand or launched from a weapon.|例句：The soldier threw a grenade at the enemy bunker.  士兵向敌人的掩体投掷了一枚手榴弹。|例句：The terrorist attack involved the use of grenades and firearms. 恐怖袭击涉及使用手榴弹和枪支。|近义词：bomb, missile|反义词：None.
