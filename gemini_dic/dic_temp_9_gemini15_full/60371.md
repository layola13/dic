egritude:/ˈɛɡrɪtjuːd/| n. |傲慢；自负；盛气凌人|The word "egritude" signifies a feeling of self-importance, arrogance, and a condescending attitude towards others. It suggests a haughty demeanor and a lack of humility.  |例句：His egritude was evident in the way he dismissed everyone's opinions. 他那傲慢的态度在他轻蔑地驳斥所有人的意见时表现得很明显。  |近义词：arrogance, haughtiness, superciliousness, pride, conceit|反义词：humility, modesty, humbleness|名词
