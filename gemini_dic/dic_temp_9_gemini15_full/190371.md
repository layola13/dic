sarees:/ˈsɑːriːz/| n.|纱丽（印度女子服装）|A saree is a traditional garment worn by women in the Indian subcontinent. It's a long piece of unstitched cloth, typically five to nine yards in length, draped over the body in various styles.  |She wore a beautiful silk saree to the wedding. 她穿着美丽的丝绸纱丽参加婚礼。|dresses, garments|trousers, pants|名词
