nasofrontal:/ˌneɪzoʊˈfrɒntəl/| adj.|鼻额的|Relating to both the nose and the forehead.  The term is primarily used in anatomy and refers to structures or regions that involve both the nasal and frontal bones or areas.  For example, the nasofrontal angle refers to the angle formed where the nasal and frontal bones meet.|The nasofrontal angle is an important feature in facial assessment.|近义词：fronto-nasal|反义词：None specifically.
