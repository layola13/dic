owes:/oʊz/ | vt. |欠；归功于 |To be obligated to pay or repay (a debt, etc.); to be indebted to; to be in debt to someone for something.|  I owe you $10. = 我欠你10美元。|She owes her success to hard work. = 她的成功归功于努力工作。|  owe, be indebted to |  own, possess | v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I owe him a favor. = 我欠他一个人情。
She owes money to the bank. = 她欠银行钱。


一般过去时:
I owed him money last year. = 去年我欠他钱。
He owed his life to the quick thinking of the paramedics. = 他把命都托付给了医护人员的快速反应。


现在完成时:
I have owed him money for months. = 我已经欠他钱好几个月了。
She has owed her success to her hard work for years. = 多年来，她的成功一直归功于她的努力工作。


过去完成时:
I had owed him money since college. = 从大学以来我就欠他钱。
She had owed her inspiration to her grandfather's stories. = 她一直以来都把她获得灵感的来源归功于她祖父讲的故事。


将来时:
I will owe him a big thank you. = 我将要好好感谢他。
They will owe their victory to teamwork. = 他们的胜利将归功于团队合作。


虚拟语气:
If I had worked harder, I wouldn't owe so much money. = 如果我以前更努力工作，我就不会欠这么多钱了。
I wish I didn't owe him anything. = 我希望我不欠他任何东西。


被动语态:
The money is owed to the bank. = 这笔钱欠银行。
The success of the project is owed to their dedication. = 项目的成功归功于他们的奉献。


疑问句:
How much do you owe him? = 你欠他多少钱？
To whom do you owe your success? = 你把你的成功归功于谁？


否定句:
I don't owe you anything. = 我不欠你任何东西。
She doesn't owe him an explanation. = 她不需要向他解释。


PHRASE->
owe someone a favor = 欠某人一个人情
owe someone an apology = 欠某人一个道歉
owe it to someone = 把…归功于某人
owe someone money = 欠某人钱
owe someone explanation = 欠某人一个解释
