draughtboards: /ˈdrɑːftbɔːrdz/ | n. |  跳棋盘；绘图板 | A draughtboard is a board used to play draughts (checkers).  It is typically checkered with alternating light and dark squares.  Draughtboards can be made of various materials, such as wood or plastic. |  例句：The children were engrossed in a game of draughts on the draughtboard. 孩子们全神贯注地在一张跳棋盘上玩跳棋。


The old draughtboard was worn but still playable.  那张旧跳棋盘已经磨损了，但仍然可以使用。


近义词：checkerboard, game board | 反义词：无


词源:  The word "draughtboard" is a compound word. "Draught" comes from the Middle English word "draught," meaning a game of draughts (checkers).  "Board" refers to the flat surface on which the game is played.  The etymology thus reflects the direct connection between the game and its playing surface.
