patrols:/pəˈtroʊlz/ | v. |巡逻，巡查 | To go around an area regularly in order to watch for crime or other problems.  |例句：The police patrols the streets every night. 警察每晚在街上巡逻。 |例句：The soldiers patrols the border area.士兵们巡逻边境地区。|近义词：巡查,巡视,监视 |反义词：stay, remain


USAGE->

The police patrols the streets every night. = 警察每晚在街上巡逻。
He patrols the building regularly to check for security breaches. = 他定期巡逻大楼，检查安全漏洞。
