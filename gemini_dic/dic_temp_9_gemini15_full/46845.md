dappled:/ˈdæpld/ | adj. | 有斑点的；斑驳的 | Marked with small spots or patches of color; mottled.  The word implies a pattern of light and shade, often created by sunlight filtering through leaves. |例句：The sunlight dappled the forest floor.  阳光斑驳地洒在森林地面上。

The afternoon light dappled the stream.  下午的阳光给小溪投下斑驳的光影。


近义词：mottled, speckled, spotted, variegated |反义词：uniform, plain, solid


词源:  From Middle English *dapelen, from Old English *dæppian ("to dab, spot").  The root likely relates to the concept of "dabbing" or applying something in small spots.
