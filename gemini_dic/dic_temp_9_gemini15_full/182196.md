remollify:/rɪˈmɒləfaɪ/| vt.|使平静；抚慰；缓和|To soothe or calm (someone) again; to appease or pacify.  It implies a previous state of agitation or anger that has been temporarily calmed but needs further soothing.|例句：The manager tried to remollify the angry customer with a discount. 经理试图通过打折来安抚生气的顾客。
例句：After the initial shock, they attempted to remollify their concerns with reassurances.  最初的震惊过后，他们试图通过保证来减轻他们的担忧。|近义词：soothe, appease, pacify, calm, mollify|反义词：aggravate, irritate, provoke, inflame
