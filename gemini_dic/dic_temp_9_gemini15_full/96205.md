index's:/ˈɪndɛksɪz/| n. |索引的；指标的|The plural form of "index," referring to multiple indexes or indices.  It indicates a list of names, subjects, etc., usually found at the back of a book or at the end of a database, allowing quick access to specific information.  It can also refer to a numerical indicator of economic or social trends.|例句：The book's indexes are incredibly detailed and helpful. 这本书的索引非常详细且有用。

The indexes of the stock market fluctuated wildly yesterday. 股市指数昨天剧烈波动。|近义词：indices, tables, registers|反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept would be a lack of organization or a system for finding information.
