psychotropics:/ˌsaɪkəʊˈtrɒpɪks/ | n. |精神药物，精神活性药物 |Substances that affect the mind, emotions, and behavior;  they are used to treat mental disorders.  These drugs alter brain chemistry and neurotransmission to affect mood, perception, or behavior. |例句：Many psychotropics are prescribed to treat depression and anxiety. 许多精神药物被用来治疗抑郁症和焦虑症。|例句：The side effects of some psychotropics can be significant.  一些精神药物的副作用可能很大。|近义词：psychoactive drugs, mind-altering drugs |反义词：placebo (in the context of treatment)
