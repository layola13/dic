make allowance for:/meɪk əˈlaʊəns fɔːr/| v. |考虑到；体谅；顾及；允许；容许|To consider or take into account a particular factor when making a judgment or decision.  It implies a degree of understanding or leniency.|


例句：

We need to make allowance for the fact that he's still quite young. = 我们需要考虑到他还很年轻这个事实。

You should make allowance for her inexperience. = 你应该体谅她的经验不足。


近义词：consider, take into account, accommodate, allow for, factor in

反义词：ignore, disregard, overlook


词性：动词 (Verb)
