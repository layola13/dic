nonopposition:/ˌnɒnɒpəˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 不反对，无异议 | The absence of opposition or objection.  It implies a passive acceptance rather than active support.  | Example: The proposal passed with nonopposition from the board members.  董事会成员未表示反对，该提案获得通过。|近义词：acquiescence, consent, acceptance | 反义词：opposition, objection, dissent |  名词
