nephew:/ˈnefjuː/| n. |侄子；外甥|A son or daughter of one's brother or sister, or of one's brother-in-law or sister-in-law.|My nephew is a talented musician. 我的侄子是一位有天赋的音乐家。|近义词：grandson, grandson, kinsman|反义词：aunt, uncle


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is my nephew. = 他是我的侄子。
My nephew plays the piano. = 我的侄子弹钢琴。


一般过去时:
He was my favorite nephew. = 他是我最喜欢的侄子。
My nephew visited us last week. = 我的侄子上周来看我们了。


现在完成时:
I have known my nephew for many years. = 我认识我的侄子很多年了。
My nephew has just graduated from college. = 我的侄子刚刚大学毕业。


过去完成时:

将来时:
