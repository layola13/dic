fearful:/ˈfɪrfəl/| adj.|可怕的；令人畏惧的；严重的；极度的|inspiring fear or apprehension; extremely bad or serious.  |例句：The storm was fearful.  暴风雨很可怕。

例句：He had a fearful accident. 他遭遇了一场可怕的事故。


近义词：terrible, dreadful, awful, frightening, alarming, scary

反义词：safe, secure, harmless, comforting, reassuring


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The dog is fearful of loud noises. = 这只狗害怕大声的噪音。
The situation is fearful indeed. =  情况确实很糟糕。


一般过去时:
I was fearful for his safety. = 我担心他的安全。
The journey was fearful and long. = 这段旅程又可怕又漫长。


现在完成时:
She has been fearful of flying since the accident. = 自从那次事故以来，她一直害怕飞行。
I have been fearful of the results. = 我一直很担心结果。


过去完成时:
He had been fearful of the consequences. = 他一直害怕后果。
They had been fearful that the project would fail. = 他们一直担心项目会失败。


将来时:
I will be fearful if he doesn't call. = 如果他不打电话，我会很害怕。
We will be fearful of the unknown. = 我们将害怕未知。


虚拟语气:
I would be fearful to go there alone. = 我不敢一个人去那里。
If I were you, I would be fearful of the repercussions. = 如果我是你，我会害怕后果。


被动语态:
The children were fearful of being left alone. = 孩子们害怕被独自留下。
The villagers were fearful of the approaching storm. = 村民们害怕即将到来的暴风雨。


疑问句:
Are you fearful of spiders? = 你怕蜘蛛吗？
Is he fearful of the future? = 他害怕未来吗？


否定句:
I am not fearful of heights. = 我不怕高。
She wasn't fearful of the challenge. = 她不怕挑战。
