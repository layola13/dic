firewire:/ˈfaɪəwaɪər/| n. |火线，IEEE 1394接口|A high-speed serial interface standard for connecting peripherals to computers.  It's known for its fast data transfer rates and hot-swapping capabilities.|例句：My digital camera uses FireWire to transfer images to my computer. 我的数码相机使用火线接口将图像传输到我的电脑。|例句：The FireWire port is located on the back of the computer. 火线接口位于电脑的背面。|近义词：IEEE 1394, i.LINK|反义词：USB (in terms of interface type, not speed)|名词
