preemployer: /ˌpriːɪmˈplɔɪər/ | n. |  潜在雇主；未来的雇主 | A preemployer is a person or organization that is considering hiring someone, but has not yet made a formal offer of employment.  It refers to the entity before the official employment contract is signed. | Example:  She carefully researched the preemployer's company culture before the interview. 她面试前仔细研究了潜在雇主的公司文化。 |近义词: prospective employer, potential employer | 反义词: current employer, former employer
