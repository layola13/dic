overshipment:/ˌoʊvərˈʃɪpmənt/ | n. | 超量装运；发货过多 | The act of shipping more goods than ordered or requested.  It often leads to increased storage costs and potential waste.  | Example: The overshipment of components caused a backlog in our warehouse.  超量装运的组件导致我们的仓库积压。|近义词：excess shipment, surplus shipment | 反义词：undershipment
