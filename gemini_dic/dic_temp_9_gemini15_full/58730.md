dunderheadednesses:/ˈdʌndərˌhedɪdnɪsəz/ | n. |愚蠢的行为；笨拙的行为 |The sheer dunderheadednesses of their plan was appalling.  |近义词：foolishness, stupidity, silliness |反义词：wisdom, intelligence, cleverness


英文解析:This word is a plural noun formed from the adjective "dunderheaded," meaning extremely stupid or foolish.  It refers to multiple instances or examples of such stupidity or foolishness.  It's a rather informal and emphatic term.


例句:

Their constant bickering and reckless decisions are prime examples of their dunderheadednesses. = 他们不断的争吵和鲁莽的决定是他们愚蠢行为的主要例子。


The report detailed a series of dunderheadednesses that led to the project's failure. = 报告详细描述了一系列愚蠢的行为，导致了项目的失败。


词源:

The word "dunderheaded" is a compound word.  "Dunder" is an archaic word meaning "stupid" or "dull," possibly of Dutch origin. "Headed" simply means having a head, implying the seat of intelligence.  The suffix "-nesses" forms a plural noun from an adjective.
