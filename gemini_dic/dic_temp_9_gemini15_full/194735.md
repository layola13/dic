selfism:/ˈsɛlfɪzəm/| n. |利己主义；自我中心；自我主义 |The excessive regard for one's own interests or welfare; selfishness.  Selfism is often characterized by a lack of empathy and consideration for others. |例句：His selfism prevented him from seeing the needs of his family.他的利己主义使他看不到家人的需求。

例句：Her actions, driven by selfism, caused significant harm to her colleagues.  她的行为，出于利己主义，给她的同事造成了很大的伤害。


近义词：egoism, selfishness, narcissism, self-centeredness |反义词：altruism, selflessness, empathy
