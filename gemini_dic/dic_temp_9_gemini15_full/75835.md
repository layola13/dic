fumish:/ˈfɜːnɪʃ/| vt.|供应，提供；布置（房间等）|to provide or supply something needed or wanted |The hotel furnishes towels and soap.  这家酒店提供毛巾和肥皂。|近义词：supply, provide, equip|反义词：deprive, remove


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company furnishes office supplies to its employees. = 公司为员工提供办公用品。
They furnish the apartment with modern furniture. = 他们用现代家具布置公寓。


一般过去时:
The caterer furnished the food for the wedding. = 宴会承办商提供了婚礼的食品。
My grandmother furnished her house with antique pieces. = 我祖母用古董家具布置了她的房子。


现在完成时:
They have furnished the new office building. = 他们已经装修了新的办公楼。
She has furnished her apartment beautifully. = 她已经漂亮地布置了她的公寓。


过去完成时:
He had furnished the room before his guests arrived. = 他的客人到达之前，他已经布置好了房间。
The company had furnished all the necessary documents. = 公司已经提供了所有必要的文档。


将来时:
They will furnish the report by the end of the week. = 他们将在本周末提供报告。
We will furnish the house with new appliances. = 我们将用新的电器布置房子。


虚拟语气:
If they furnished the necessary information, we could proceed. = 如果他们提供必要的信息，我们就可以继续进行。
I wish they had furnished me with better instructions. = 我希望他们当时给了我更好的指示。


被动语态:
The room was furnished with elegant antiques. = 房间里摆放着优雅的古董。
The report will be furnished to the board of directors. = 报告将提交给董事会。


疑问句:
Does the hotel furnish breakfast? = 酒店提供早餐吗？
Will the company furnish the necessary equipment? = 公司会提供必要的设备吗？


否定句:
The landlord did not furnish the apartment with a stove. = 房东没有为公寓配备炉子。
They did not furnish the required documentation. = 他们没有提供所需的文档。


PHRASE->
furnish with = 用…装备；用…布置
furnish information = 提供信息
furnish evidence = 提供证据
furnish a room = 布置房间
furnish a report = 提供报告
