miscontent:/ˌmɪskənˈtɛnt/| adj.|不满的，不满足的|Dissatisfied; unhappy with one's situation or circumstances.|He was discontent with his job and decided to look for a new one. 他对自己的工作不满意，决定另找一份工作。|近义词：dissatisfied, unhappy, disgruntled, displeased|反义词：content, satisfied, pleased


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is discontent with his current salary. = 他对目前的薪水不满意。
She is discontent with the slow pace of progress. = 她对缓慢的进展速度感到不满。

一般过去时:
They were discontent with the outcome of the election. = 他们对选举的结果感到不满。
He was discontent with his living conditions. = 他对自己的生活条件感到不满。


现在完成时:
We have been discontent with the service for months. = 我们对这项服务已经不满了好几个月了。
She has been discontent with her boss's management style for years. = 她多年来一直对她老板的管理风格不满。


过去完成时:
They had been discontent with the company's policies before they resigned. = 他们在辞职之前就对公司的政策感到不满。
She had been discontent with her marriage for a long time before she decided to separate. = 在她决定分居之前，她对她的婚姻已经不满很久了。


将来时:
I will be discontent if they don't meet my expectations. = 如果他们达不到我的预期，我会感到不满。
He will likely be discontent with the results of the test. = 他很可能会对考试的结果感到不满。


虚拟语气:
I would be discontent if I were in his shoes. = 如果我处于他的位置，我会感到不满。
She would have been discontent if she had known the truth earlier. = 如果她早些知道真相，她就会感到不满。


被动语态:
His discontent with the situation was widely known. = 他对这种情况的不满广为人知。
The company's poor performance was largely attributed to the employees' discontent. = 公司业绩不佳很大程度上是由于员工的不满。


疑问句:
Is he discontent with his job? = 他对他的工作不满吗？
Are they discontent with the proposed changes? = 他们对拟议的更改不满吗？


否定句:
He is not discontent with his life. = 他对他的生活并不不满。
They were not discontent with the decision. = 他们对这个决定并不不满。


PHRASE->

voice discontent = 发声不满
show discontent = 表现不满
cause discontent = 引起不满
