paroled:/pəˈroʊld/| v.|假释|To release (a prisoner) from prison before the completion of a sentence, on condition of good behavior.|The judge paroled him after he served five years of his sentence. 法官在他服刑五年后将他假释。|近义词：release, liberate|反义词：imprison, incarcerate


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The parole board paroles prisoners who meet certain criteria. = 假释委员会假释符合特定标准的囚犯。
He paroles inmates based on their rehabilitation progress. = 他根据囚犯的改造进度进行假释。


一般过去时:
The governor paroled the convicted murderer. = 州长假释了被判有罪的杀人犯。
They paroled him early due to good behavior. = 由于表现良好，他们提前将他假释。


现在完成时:
The prison has paroled many inmates this year. = 今年监狱已经假释了许多囚犯。
Several prisoners have been paroled under the new reform program. = 在新的改革计划下，已经有几名囚犯被假释。


过去完成时:
He had been paroled twice before this incident. = 在这起事件之前，他已经两次被假释。
The authorities had already paroled her when the new evidence emerged. = 当新的证据出现时，当局已经将她假释了。


将来时:
The prisoner will be paroled next month. = 这名囚犯将于下个月假释。
They will parole him if he completes the rehabilitation program.  = 如果他完成了改造计划，他们将会假释他。


虚拟语气:
If he had behaved better, he would have been paroled earlier. = 如果他表现更好，他本来可以更早被假释。
I wish they had paroled him sooner. = 我希望他们能早点假释他。


被动语态:
He was paroled after serving half his sentence. = 他服刑期满一半后被假释。
Many prisoners are paroled every year. = 每年都有许多囚犯被假释。


疑问句:
Will he be paroled? = 他会被假释吗？
When was he paroled? = 他什么时候被假释的？


否定句:
They did not parole him because of his poor behavior. = 由于他的行为不端，他们没有假释他。
He was not paroled despite his pleas. = 尽管他一再恳求，但他仍未被假释。
