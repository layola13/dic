hatchback:/ˈhætʃbæk/| n.|掀背车，掀背式汽车|A hatchback is a car with a rear door that opens upwards, like a lid, giving access to the luggage compartment.  This design often combines the advantages of a smaller car's maneuverability with the practicality of a larger car's cargo space.|例句：She bought a new hatchback for better fuel economy. 她买了一辆新的掀背车以获得更好的燃油经济性。  My hatchback is perfect for carrying groceries. 我的掀背车很适合装杂货。|近义词：liftback (BrE)|反义词：sedan, saloon (BrE)|名词
