neckweed:/ˈnekwiːd/| n.|颈草，水生植物|A colloquial term for various aquatic plants, especially those with long, trailing stems that resemble tangled neck hair.  It is not a formally recognized botanical term and the specific plant referred to can vary depending on region and context.|The pond was choked with neckweed, making it difficult to swim.|近义词：waterweed, pondweed|反义词：None specifically.
