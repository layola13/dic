hospitium: /hɒsˈpɪtiəm/ | n. | 宾馆；招待；款待 |  A hospitium was a place of hospitality or lodging, often associated with monasteries or religious institutions in ancient times. It provided shelter and sometimes food for travelers or guests.  The word carries a sense of welcome and generous treatment of visitors. | Example: The weary traveler sought refuge in the monastery's hospitium.  疲惫的旅行者在修道院的宾馆里寻求庇护。 The hospitium offered warmth and a simple meal to the passing pilgrims.  宾馆为路过的朝圣者提供温暖和简单的饭菜。| 近义词：inn, hostel, lodging, accommodation | 反义词：inhospitality, rejection
