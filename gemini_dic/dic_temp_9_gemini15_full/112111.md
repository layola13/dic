longships:/ˈlɒŋʃɪps/| n.|长船|Longships were large, narrow seagoing vessels used by the Vikings and other Norse peoples.  They were characterized by their length, shallow draft, and ability to sail in shallow waters.  |例句：The Vikings' longships allowed them to raid coastal settlements across Europe.  维京人的长船使他们能够袭击整个欧洲的沿海定居点。
The longships were remarkably fast and maneuverable for their size. 长船就其大小而言，速度和机动性都非常出色。|近义词：warship, galley, drakkar|反义词：small boat, dinghy
