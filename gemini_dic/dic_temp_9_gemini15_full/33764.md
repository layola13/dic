circulating: /ˈsɜːrkjuleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 流通的；循环的；传播的 |  Moving or being moved continuously around a system or area.  | 例句：The circulating air in the room was refreshing.  房间里循环的空气令人神清气爽。

例句：The circulating rumors caused widespread panic.  流传的谣言引起了广泛的恐慌。


近义词：circulatory, flowing, current, spreading | 反义词：stationary, stagnant, immobile


词源:  From Middle English *circulaten, from Old French circuler, from Latin circulare "to move in a circle," from circulus "circle" (see circle).

一般现在时:
The blood is circulating throughout the body. = 血液在全身循环。
The news is circulating quickly online. = 新闻在网上迅速传播。

一般过去时:
The rumors circulated widely before the election. = 谣言在选举前广泛流传。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
