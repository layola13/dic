devilish: /ˈdɛvəlɪʃ/ | adj. | 恶魔似的；极坏的；顽皮的 |  extremely wicked or bad; mischievous; playfully mischievous. |

英文解析:The word "devilish" suggests a quality or characteristic associated with devils or demons, implying wickedness, malice, or mischievousness. It can describe something extremely bad, unpleasant, or difficult, or it can refer to playful naughtiness.

例句:

His devilish grin made me uneasy.  他的邪恶笑容让我不安。

The devilish heat made it impossible to work outside. 炎热的天气让人无法在户外工作。


近义词: wicked, evil, fiendish, mischievous, naughty, impish, diabolic, malicious

反义词: angelic, good, kind, benevolent, virtuous, harmless

词性: adjective
