protocultural: /ˌproʊtoʊˈkʌltʃərəl/ | adj. | 原文化的，原始文化的 |  Relating to or characteristic of the earliest stages of culture or a particular culture.  It suggests a stage before a culture is fully developed or formalized, often characterized by rudimentary or nascent forms of social organization, belief systems, and practices.  The term often implies a process of development and evolution towards a more complex cultural form. |  The protocultural stage of human development is marked by simple tools and communal living. = 人类发展的原文化阶段的特点是简单的工具和群体生活。  The study focuses on the protocultural aspects of early human societies. = 这项研究侧重于早期人类社会的原文化方面。| 近义词：precultural, primordial, nascent | 反义词：advanced, developed, sophisticated
