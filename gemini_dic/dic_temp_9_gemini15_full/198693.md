siamese:/ˌsaɪəˈmiːz/| adj. n.|暹罗的；暹罗猫的；孪生的；|Of or relating to Siam (now Thailand).  Also referring to Siamese cats, which are known for their distinctive coloring and blue eyes.  Can also describe twins joined at birth.|例句：She owns a beautiful Siamese cat. 她养了一只漂亮的暹罗猫。

例句：The Siamese twins were successfully separated.  这对连体婴儿被成功分离了。


近义词：twinned (for the twin meaning), conjoined (for the twin meaning)
反义词：separate (for the twin meaning), single (for the twin meaning)


词源:From Siamese, referring to Siam (present-day Thailand), where this breed of cat originated.  The word "Siamese" itself is derived from the historical name of the country.
