inevitability:/ˌɪnˌɛvɪtəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可避免性，必然性 |The state or quality of being inevitable; something that is inevitable.  It refers to the fact that something is certain to happen and cannot be avoided.|例句：The inevitability of death is a fact of life.  死亡的不可避免性是人生事实之一。

例句：The inevitability of the project's failure was clear to everyone.  该项目失败的必然性对每个人来说都很明显。


近义词：certainty, unavoidability, inescapability |反义词：avoidability, chance, possibility |词性: noun
