playpen:/ˈpleɪpen/| n.|婴儿游戏围栏|A playpen is a safe, enclosed space for babies and toddlers to play in. It typically has mesh sides or bars to allow for ventilation and visibility, and a soft, padded floor.|例句：The baby is happily playing with his toys in his playpen. 宝宝正高兴地在他的游戏围栏里玩玩具。  The parents set up the playpen in the living room. 父母在客厅里搭建了游戏围栏。|近义词：play yard, crib (sometimes)|反义词：None.
