conure:/ˈkɒnjʊər/| n. |锥嘴鹦鹉|A conure is a small to medium-sized parrot belonging to the tribe Arini.  Conures are known for their vibrant plumage, often featuring a mix of bright greens, reds, yellows, and blues. They are also popular as pets due to their relatively small size and often playful personalities. However, they can be quite noisy and require significant attention and care.|Example:  My neighbor owns a sun conure; its cheerful squawks fill the air each morning.  我的邻居养了一只太阳锥嘴鹦鹉；每天早上它欢快的叫声都会充满空中。|近义词：parrot, parakeet|反义词：无
