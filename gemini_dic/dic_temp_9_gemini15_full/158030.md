plantigrada:/plænˈtɪɡrædə/| n.|跖行类动物|Plantigrada is a taxonomic order of mammals characterized by walking on the soles of their feet, with the heel and toes touching the ground.  This contrasts with digitigrade (walking on toes) and unguligrade (walking on hooves) locomotion.  The order includes bears, raccoons, and badgers, among others.|Example: Bears are a classic example of plantigrades.|近义词：none (it's a taxonomic term)|反义词：digitigrade, unguligrade|名词
