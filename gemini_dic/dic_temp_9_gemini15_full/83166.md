guttiform:/ˈɡʌtɪfɔːrm/| adj.| 滴状的；泪滴状的 | Resembling a gutta or drop; drop-shaped.  The term often describes shapes resembling a teardrop or a small drop of liquid.  |例句：The raindrops on the windowpane were guttiform.  窗玻璃上的雨滴呈滴状。The pendant had a guttiform design.  吊坠呈泪滴状设计。|近义词：drop-shaped, pear-shaped, lacrimiform|反义词：spherical, cylindrical


USAGE->
(由于guttiform是形容词，没有动词用法，以下列举形容词的用法)

比较级：
The first drop was more guttiform than the second. 第一颗水滴比第二颗更像水滴状。
This design is less guttiform than the previous one. 这个设计比之前的那个更不像水滴状。

最高级：
Of all the designs, this one is the most guttiform.  在所有设计中，这个是最像水滴状的。
This is the least guttiform shape I've ever seen. 这是我见过的最不像水滴状的形状。

用于名词前作定语：
The guttiform crystals shimmered in the light. 滴状晶体在光线下闪闪发光。
He admired the guttiform pearls. 他欣赏那些泪滴状的珍珠。

用于系动词be之后作表语：
The stain was guttiform. 污渍呈滴状。
The cloud formations were distinctly guttiform. 云的形状明显是滴状的。

用于介词短语中：
The design was in a guttiform shape. 设计是滴状的。
The droplets fell in a guttiform pattern. 水滴呈滴状模式落下。
