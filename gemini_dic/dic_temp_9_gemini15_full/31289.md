chaperones:/ˈʃæpəroʊnz/ | n. | 陪伴者；监护人 |  A chaperone is a person who accompanies and supervises young people, especially on social occasions, to ensure their proper behavior.  | Example:  The school arranged chaperones for the field trip. 学校为这次实地考察安排了陪同人员。

The parents hired a chaperone to supervise their teenage daughter at the dance.  父母雇了一位监护人来监督他们的十几岁的女儿参加舞会。|近义词：supervisor, escort, guardian |反义词：None specifically, but the opposite concept would be unsupervised or unaccompanied.


USAGE->

She chaperones the young girls at the dance. = 她陪着年轻女孩参加舞会。
He chaperones his younger siblings on outings. = 他陪着他的弟弟妹妹外出。
