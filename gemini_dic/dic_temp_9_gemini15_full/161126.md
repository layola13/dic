posterity: /pɒˈstɛrɪti/ | n. | 后代；子孙；后世 |  Posterity refers to all future generations of people.  It emphasizes the lasting impact of actions or events on those who will come after.  |  The monument was built to ensure the memory of the event would live on for posterity.  这个纪念碑是为了确保这一事件的记忆能流传于后世而建造的。|  descendants, successors, offspring |  ancestors, predecessors
