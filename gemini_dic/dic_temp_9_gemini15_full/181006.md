reimpregnating:/ˌriːɪmˈprɛɡneɪtɪŋ/| v. |再怀孕；再次浸渍|The act of impregnating something again. This can refer to literally impregnating something (like soaking a material again) or figuratively (like reintroducing an idea).|例句：The artist was reimpregnating the canvas with more paint.  艺术家正在用更多的颜料再次浸渍画布。
The company is reimpregnating the market with its new product. 公司正在用其新产品再次占领市场。|近义词：resaturate, re-soak, reinvigorate |反义词：dry, deplete
