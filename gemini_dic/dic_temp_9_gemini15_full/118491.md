mesmerist:/ˈmɛzmərɪst/| n. |催眠师，施催眠术者 | A mesmerist is a person who practices mesmerism, a technique of inducing a hypnotic state.  They use suggestion and other techniques to influence a subject's thoughts and behavior. | The mesmerist swayed his hands over the patient, attempting to induce a trance.  催眠师在他的病人身上挥舞着双手，试图诱导他进入恍惚状态。| hypnotist, hypnotizer |  (无明确反义词，可考虑 anti-hypnotist) | 名词
