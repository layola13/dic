jogtrot:/ˈdʒɒɡtrɒt/ | v. |慢跑；缓步慢跑 |To move at a jog trot is to move at a slow, steady pace, a pace that is neither fast nor slow, but somewhere in between.  It often implies a comfortable and continuous movement. |例句：The horse jogtrotted along the country lane. 这匹马沿着乡间小路慢跑。

例句：The hikers jogtrotted up the gentle slope.  徒步旅行者沿着缓坡慢跑。


近义词：amble,canter,trot


反义词：sprint,race,gallop


词性：动词
