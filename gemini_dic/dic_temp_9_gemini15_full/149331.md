pacesetter:/ˈpeɪsˌsɛtər/| n. |领跑者；步调制定者；先驱|A pacesetter is a person or thing that sets the pace or standard for others to follow.  They are often leaders in their field, setting the trend or establishing a benchmark for others to emulate. |例句：She is a pacesetter in the field of artificial intelligence. 她的人工智能领域是领跑者。

例句：The company's new product is a pacesetter in the market. 这家公司的新产品是市场上的领跑者。


近义词：leader, pioneer, innovator, trendsetter, vanguard


反义词：follower, laggard, straggler


词性:名词
