diademing:/ˈdaɪədiːmɪŋ/| v.|加冕，戴上王冠|To adorn or crown with a diadem. The act of placing a diadem on someone's head.|例句：The queen was diademing her son, signifying his ascension to the throne. 女王为她的儿子加冕，标志着他登基。|近义词：crowning, crowning, investing|反义词：uncrowning, discrowning
