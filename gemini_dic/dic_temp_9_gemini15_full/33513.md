cicatrization:/ˌsɪkətrɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |瘢痕形成；愈合|The process of scar tissue formation.  It is a natural part of the body's healing process after an injury. |The cicatrization of the wound took several weeks.  伤口愈合花了几个星期。|healing, scarring, repair|inflammation, injury|名词
