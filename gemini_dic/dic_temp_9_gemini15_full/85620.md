hauled:/hɔːld/| /hɔːl/| vt.|拖，拉，拽；拖运；（用力）拉；提起|to pull or drag something heavy with effort.|例句：They hauled the boat ashore.他们把船拖上岸。|例句：The farmer hauled the heavy cart. 农民用力拉着一辆重车。|近义词：pull, drag, tug, tow|反义词：push


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hauls the heavy boxes every day. 他每天都搬运沉重的箱子。
She hauls her luggage to the airport. 她把行李拖到机场。

一般过去时:
The workers hauled the timber out of the forest. 工人们把木材从森林里拖了出来。
We hauled the broken car to the repair shop. 我们把坏车拖到修理厂。

现在完成时:
They have hauled all the rubbish to the dump. 他们已经把所有的垃圾都运到垃圾场了。
She has hauled several tons of coal today. 她今天已经运了几吨煤。

过去完成时:
The horse had hauled the cart for miles before collapsing. 马在倒下之前已经拉了数英里的车。
They had hauled the supplies to the mountaintop before nightfall. 他们在夜幕降临前把物资运到了山顶。

将来时:
We will haul the boat to the lake tomorrow. 我们明天要把船运到湖边。
He will haul the logs to the sawmill. 他将把原木运到锯木厂。

虚拟语气:
If I had a truck, I would haul the furniture myself. 如果我有一辆卡车，我会自己运家具。
I wish I could haul that heavy piano upstairs. 我真希望我能把那架沉重的钢琴搬到楼上。

被动语态:
The heavy equipment was hauled to the construction site. 重型设备被运送到建筑工地。
The logs are being hauled out of the forest. 原木正在被从森林里拖出来。

疑问句:
Did they haul the boat to the dock? 他们把船拖到码头了吗？
Will you haul the garbage to the curb? 你会把垃圾拖到路边吗？

否定句:
They didn't haul the boat ashore. 他们没有把船拖上岸。
He couldn't haul the heavy weight alone. 他一个人搬不动那重物。


PHRASE->
haul in: 收获；逮捕
haul away: 拖走；运走
haul up: 提升；上拉；使停泊
haul down: 降下；扯下
haul off: 猛拉；突然离开
