chinche:/ˈtʃɪntʃeɪ/| n.|臭虫；床虱|A chinche is a small, wingless insect that feeds on the blood of humans and animals. They are known for their nocturnal habits and the itchy bites they leave behind.  They are often found in mattresses, bedding, and furniture.|例句：I woke up with itchy bites all over my arms – there must be a chinche in my bed. 我醒来时胳膊上到处都是痒的咬痕——我的床上肯定有臭虫。|例句：The hotel room was infested with chinches; we had to change rooms immediately.  酒店房间里到处都是臭虫；我们不得不立即换房间。|近义词：bedbug, insect|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用absence of bedbugs 或 clean bedding)|名词
