glossy:/ˈɡlɒsi/ /ˈɡlɑːsi/| adj.|有光泽的，光滑的；华丽的，吸引人的|Having a smooth, shiny surface; superficially attractive or impressive.|The glossy magazine featured stunning photography. 这本光泽的杂志刊登了令人惊叹的照片。|近义词：shiny, polished, sleek, lustrous|反义词：dull, matte, rough


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The car's paint is glossy. = 这辆车的油漆很光亮。
Her hair looks glossy and healthy. = 她的头发看起来光泽健康。


一般过去时:
The brochure was glossy and colorful. = 这本宣传册光滑鲜艳。
The magazine cover was glossy. = 杂志封面是光滑的。


现在完成时:
The furniture has become less glossy over time. = 随着时间的推移，家具变得不那么光亮了。
The sun has made the leaves glossy. = 太阳使树叶变得光亮。


过去完成时:
The car had been glossy when it was new. = 这辆车刚买的时候很光亮。
The photographs had a glossy finish. = 照片有光泽的表面处理。


将来时:
The new car will have a glossy finish. = 新车将会有光泽的表面处理。
The floor will be glossy after I wax it. = 我打蜡后地板会很光亮。


虚拟语气:
If the wood were glossy, it would look better. = 如果木材有光泽，看起来会更好。
I wish my hair were as glossy as hers. = 我希望我的头发像她的一样光亮。


被动语态:
The pictures were made glossy with special coating. = 图片用特殊的涂层处理得非常光亮。
The magazine pages are printed on glossy paper. = 杂志页面是用光滑的纸张印刷的。


疑问句:
Is the finish on this table glossy? = 这张桌子的表面光滑吗？
Are those shoes made of glossy leather? = 那些鞋子是用光滑的皮革做的吗？


否定句:
The surface is not glossy; it's dull. = 表面不光滑，而是暗淡的。
The finish on the old wood wasn't glossy anymore. = 旧木头的表面不再光滑了。
