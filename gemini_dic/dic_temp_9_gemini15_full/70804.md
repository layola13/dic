fingerprinting: /ˈfɪŋɡərˌprɪntɪŋ/ | n. | 指纹识别，指纹采样 | The process of identifying an individual or object using fingerprints. This can involve the actual taking of fingerprints, the analysis of existing fingerprints, or the use of fingerprint technology for identification purposes. | Example:  The police used fingerprinting to identify the suspect.  警方用指纹识别来辨认嫌疑人。

The suspect's fingerprinting was crucial to solving the case. 嫌疑人的指纹识别对破案至关重要。| 近义词：fingerprint identification, biometric identification | 反义词：None
