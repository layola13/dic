non-substantial:/ˌnɒn səbˈstænʃəl/| adj.|不重要的；非实质性的；无形的；微不足道的|lacking substance or significance; unimportant; insubstantial.  It refers to something that is not significant, important, or solid.  It often implies a lack of weight, value, or impact.|例句：His arguments were non-substantial and easily refuted. 他的论点毫无价值，很容易被驳倒。

例句：The meeting yielded only non-substantial results.  会议只产生了微不足道的成果。


近义词：unimportant, insignificant, trivial, inconsequential, negligible, flimsy, superficial

反义词：substantial, significant, important, weighty, material, solid


词性：形容词
