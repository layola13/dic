neology:/niˈɒlədʒi/| n. |新词创造；新词研究|The study of new words and their creation, including the processes by which new words enter a language.  It also refers to the new words themselves. | Example: The rapid development of technology has led to a surge in neology.  技术的快速发展导致了新词创造的激增。|近义词：word coinage, new word formation|反义词：archaism
