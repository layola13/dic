septuagenarian:/ˌsɛptuəˈdʒɛnəriən/| n. |七十岁的人|A septuagenarian is a person who is between 70 and 79 years old.  This word is derived from the Latin word "septuaginta," meaning "seventy."|My grandfather, a sprightly septuagenarian, still enjoys hiking in the mountains.|octogenarian, nonagenarian|infant, toddler|名词
