precludes:/prɪˈkluːdz/| vt.|阻止；排除；妨碍|To prevent something from happening or someone from doing something.|The bad weather precluded a picnic.|近义词：prevent, hinder, impede, obstruct|反义词：allow, permit, enable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
His busy schedule precludes him from joining us. = 他繁忙的日程安排使他无法加入我们。
The contract precludes any further negotiation. = 合同排除了任何进一步的谈判。


一般过去时:
The storm precluded our travel plans. = 暴风雨打乱了我们的旅行计划。
His illness precluded him from attending the meeting. = 他的疾病使他无法参加会议。


现在完成时:
His responsibilities have precluded him from taking a vacation. = 他的责任使他无法休假。
The new regulations have precluded the use of certain chemicals. = 新的法规禁止使用某些化学品。


过去完成时:
The previous agreement had precluded any further discussion. = 先前的协议已排除了任何进一步的讨论。
His previous commitments had precluded him from accepting the offer. = 他之前的承诺使他无法接受这份工作。


将来时:
The upcoming election will preclude any major policy changes. = 即将来临的选举将阻止任何重大政策变革。
A strike will preclude the timely completion of the project. = 罢工将阻止项目按时完成。


虚拟语气:
If the law hadn't precluded it, we would have done it differently. = 如果法律没有禁止，我们会采取不同的做法。
I wish the rules didn't preclude me from participating. = 我希望规则没有阻止我参加。


被动语态:
Further discussion was precluded by the agreement. = 协议排除了进一步的讨论。
The project was precluded by a lack of funding. = 该项目因缺乏资金而搁浅。


疑问句:
Does this rule preclude us from entering? = 这条规则是否禁止我们进入？
What factors preclude the success of this plan? = 哪些因素阻碍了这个计划的成功？


否定句:
Nothing precludes us from trying. = 没有什么能阻止我们尝试。
The agreement does not preclude future negotiations. = 该协议不排除未来的谈判。


PHRASE->
preclude from:  阻止…做…
preclude the possibility of: 排除…的可能性
preclude someone/something from: 阻止某人/某事
preclude any further: 排除任何进一步的
be precluded by: 被…阻止
