simulacher:/ˈsɪmjʊleɪkər/| n. |拟像；仿真物；模拟物 |A simulacrum is a copy that is not just imperfect, but that actively misrepresents the original. It is not simply a flawed representation, but a deliberate distortion.  Simulacra are often used to create a false sense of reality, or to obscure the truth. |The advertising campaign created a simulacrum of a perfect family life.  广告活动营造了一种完美的家庭生活的假象。|近义词：copy, imitation, representation, phantom|反义词：original, reality
