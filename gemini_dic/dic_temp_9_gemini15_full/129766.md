non-explosive:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspləʊsɪv/| adj.|非爆炸性的，不爆炸的|Not capable of exploding or causing an explosion.  It describes substances, materials, or events that do not possess explosive properties.|例句：The material used in the construction is non-explosive and safe to handle.  这种建筑材料是非爆炸性的，安全易于操作。

例句：The team ensured all the packages contained non-explosive items before shipment.  团队在发货前确保所有包裹内都含有非爆炸性物品。


近义词：safe, harmless, inert, stable

反义词：explosive, volatile, unstable


词性：形容词
