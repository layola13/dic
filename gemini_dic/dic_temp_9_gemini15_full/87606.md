heterotrophic:/ˌhɛtərəˈtrɒfɪk/| adj.|异养的；异养生物的| Obtaining nourishment from organic matter, as opposed to synthesizing it from inorganic sources like plants do.  Heterotrophic organisms consume other organisms or organic matter for energy and nutrients. |Example: Fungi are heterotrophic organisms, deriving their nutrients from decaying organic matter. 真菌是异养生物，从腐烂的有机物中获取营养。  Animals are also heterotrophic. 动物也是异养生物。|近义词：consuming, dependent (on organic matter)|反义词：autotrophic
