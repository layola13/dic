pruned:/pruːnd/| vt. adj.| 修剪；剪枝；删节；精简 |To cut off branches or stems from a plant, tree, or bush; to remove unnecessary parts or elements from something. |例句：The gardener pruned the rose bushes. 园丁修剪了玫瑰花丛。  The editor pruned the article to make it shorter. 编辑删节了文章使其更短。|近义词：trim, clip, cut back, edit |反义词：grow, cultivate, expand


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prunes the trees every spring. = 他每年春天都修剪树木。
She prunes her bonsai carefully. = 她仔细地修剪她的盆景。


一般过去时:
I pruned the hedges yesterday. = 我昨天修剪了树篱。
They pruned the grapevines last fall. = 他们去年秋天修剪了葡萄藤。


现在完成时:
The gardener has pruned all the fruit trees. = 园丁已经修剪了所有的果树。
She has pruned the manuscript several times. = 她已经修改了手稿好几次。


过去完成时:
He had pruned the roses before the storm. = 他在暴风雨来临之前修剪了玫瑰花。
The editor had pruned the article before submitting it. = 编辑在提交文章之前已经删节了文章。


将来时:
I will prune the bushes next week. = 我下周将修剪灌木丛。
They will prune the trees in the autumn. = 他们将在秋天修剪树木。


虚拟语气:
If I had pruned the plants earlier, they would be healthier. = 如果我早点修剪植物，它们会更健康。
I wish I had pruned the branches more carefully. = 我真希望我更仔细地修剪了树枝。


被动语态:
The trees were pruned last month. = 树木上个月被修剪了。
The article was pruned by the editor. = 文章被编辑删节了。


疑问句:
Have you pruned the roses yet? = 你修剪玫瑰了吗？
Will they prune the trees this year? = 他们今年会修剪树木吗？


否定句:
I didn't prune the shrubs this year. = 我今年没有修剪灌木。
They haven't pruned the vines yet. = 他们还没有修剪藤蔓。


PHRASE->
prune back = 修剪
prune down = 减少，精简
prune away = 除去
prune out = 删除
prune for = 为…修剪
