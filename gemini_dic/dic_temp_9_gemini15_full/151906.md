pasted:/ˈpeɪstɪd/ | adj. | 粘贴的；过去的；被粘贴的 |  Describes something that has been pasted or stuck to a surface. It can also refer to something that feels like it's been stuck there for a long time, implying a lack of freshness or originality.  | 例句：The poster was pasted to the wall. 海报被贴在了墙上。


The old news article felt pasted together, lacking coherence. 那篇旧新闻文章感觉拼凑在一起，缺乏连贯性。| 近义词：stuck, affixed, attached | 反义词：detached, unstuck, loose |  形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The poster is pasted to the wall. = 海报贴在墙上。
This paragraph feels pasted together. = 这段话感觉拼凑而成。


一般过去时:
He pasted the stamp on the envelope. = 他把邮票贴在信封上。
She pasted the picture in her scrapbook. = 她把照片贴在她的剪贴簿里。


现在完成时:
I have pasted all the documents together. = 我已经把所有文件粘贴在一起了。
They have pasted the notice on the board. = 他们已将通知贴在布告栏上。


过去完成时:
He had pasted the label on the jar before he put it on the shelf. = 他在把罐子放到架子上之前已经贴好了标签。
She had pasted the photos into her album before she showed it to her friends. = 在她向朋友展示之前，她已经把照片贴到她的相册中了。


将来时:
I will paste the picture into my scrapbook tomorrow. = 我明天会把照片贴到我的剪贴簿里。
She will paste the flyer on every door in the neighborhood. = 她会把传单贴在附近每家每户的门上。


虚拟语气:
If I had pasted the address label correctly, the package would have arrived on time. = 如果我正确地贴上地址标签，包裹就会准时到达。
I wish I had pasted the important documents together before losing them. = 我真希望在丢失重要文件之前就把它们粘贴在一起。


被动语态:
The advertisement was pasted on the bus stop. = 广告被贴在公交车站上。
The photos were pasted into the album by her. = 照片是她贴到相册里的。


疑问句:
Was the picture pasted properly? = 图片贴好了吗？
Has the poster been pasted on the wall? = 海报贴到墙上了吗？


否定句:
He didn't paste the address label on the package. = 他没有把地址标签贴在包裹上。
She hadn't pasted the photos in the album yet. = 她还没把照片贴到相册里。



PHRASE->
pasted-up: 拼贴的；粘贴好的
pasted together: 拼凑而成的
pasted on: 粘贴在...上
pasted over:  覆盖；贴在...上面
pasted image: 粘贴的图像
