enarthrodia:/ˌɛnɑːrˈθroʊdiə/| n. |球窝关节；臼状关节|A ball-and-socket joint, characterized by a rounded head fitting into a cup-like cavity, allowing for a wide range of motion.  This type of joint is found in the hip and shoulder.| Example: The hip joint is a classic example of an enarthrodia, providing flexibility and stability.  髋关节是球窝关节的典型例子，它既灵活又稳定。|近义词：ball-and-socket joint, spheroid joint |反义词：hinge joint, gliding joint
