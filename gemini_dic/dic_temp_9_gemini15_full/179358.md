redraw:/ˌriːˈdrɔː/| vt.|重新绘制，重画，重新设计|To draw again or differently; to alter a drawing.|例句：The architect had to redraw the plans after the client changed their mind. 建筑师不得不根据客户改变主意后重新绘制图纸。|例句：She redrew the map to show the new road. 她重新绘制地图以显示新路。|近义词：redesign, remake, re-sketch|反义词：erase, delete


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He redraws the picture every day. = 他每天都重画这幅画。
They redraw the map frequently. = 他们经常重绘地图。

一般过去时:
She redrew the diagram yesterday. = 她昨天重画了图表。
He redrew the contract after negotiations. = 谈判后，他重新起草了合同。


现在完成时:
I have redrawn the chart several times. = 我已经重画了几次图表。
They have redrawn the boundaries of the park. = 他们已经重新划定了公园的边界。


过去完成时:
She had redrawn the plans before the meeting. = 她在会议之前已经重新绘制了计划。
They had redrawn the map to include the new highway. = 他们已经重新绘制了地图，以包括新的高速公路。


将来时:
He will redraw the sketch tomorrow. = 他明天将重新绘制草图。
They will redraw the blueprint next week. = 他们下周将重新绘制蓝图。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would redraw the entire picture. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新绘制整幅图画。
I wish I had redrawn the graph before submitting it. = 我希望在提交之前已经重新绘制了图表。


被动语态:

否定句:
