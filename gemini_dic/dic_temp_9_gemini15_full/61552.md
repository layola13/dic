emceeing:/ˈɛmˌsiːɪŋ/| v. |担任司仪；主持；当节目主持人 |The act of serving as a master of ceremonies (MC) or host of an event, typically involving introducing speakers, performers, or segments and generally keeping the event running smoothly.|例句：He was excellent at emceeing the awards ceremony. 他在主持颁奖典礼方面非常出色。  The emceeing was lively and engaging.  主持很活跃也很吸引人。|近义词：hosting, presenting, moderating |反义词：spectating, attending
