heroines:/ˈhɪərəʊɪnz/| n.|女英雄，女主角|A heroine is a woman who is admired for her courage, outstanding achievements, or noble qualities.  The term often refers to a female protagonist in a story, possessing exceptional bravery and moral strength. |例句：She is a heroine of the resistance movement. 她是一位抵抗运动的女英雄。

例句：The novel's heroine is a strong, independent woman. 这部小说的女主角是一个坚强、独立的女性。|近义词：hero, champion, protagonist |反义词：villain, antagonist
