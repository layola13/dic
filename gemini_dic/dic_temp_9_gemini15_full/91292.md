hydrology:/haɪˈdrɒlədʒi/| n. |水文学|The science dealing with the properties, distribution, and circulation of water on and below the earth's surface and in the atmosphere.  It encompasses the occurrence, circulation, and distribution of water throughout the Earth, including its chemical and physical properties and its relationship with the environment and living organisms. |Example:  The hydrology of the Amazon basin is incredibly complex. 亚马逊河流域的水文学极其复杂。  |Example:  Understanding hydrology is crucial for effective water resource management. 了解水文学对于有效的水资源管理至关重要。|近义词：hydrography (more focused on mapping and charting water bodies)|反义词：N/A|名词
