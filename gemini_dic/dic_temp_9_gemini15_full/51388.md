determinates:/dɪˈtɜːmɪneɪts/| n. |决定因素，决定性事物 |Things that determine or influence a result or outcome.  They are factors that shape or fix something.|The jury's verdict was based on the determinates presented by the prosecution.  陪审团的裁决是基于检方提出的决定性证据。|近义词：factors,influences, determinants|反义词：uncertainties, variables


determinates:/dɪˈtɜːmɪneɪts/|v. |(第三人称单数和复数形式)|决定，确定 |To establish or fix definitely or authoritatively; to settle or decide.|The judge will determinate the sentence for the convicted man.  法官将决定对被判有罪男子的判决。|近义词：decide, determine, resolve, settle|反义词：undecided, unsettled
