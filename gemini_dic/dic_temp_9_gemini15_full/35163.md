clowned:/klaʊnd/| v.|扮小丑；滑稽地表演|to act like a clown; to behave in a silly or clumsy way. |例句：He clowned around during the meeting, disrupting the serious atmosphere. 他在会议期间扮小丑，扰乱了严肃的气氛。

例句：The comedian clowned his way through the entire routine.  这位喜剧演员以滑稽的方式完成了整个表演。


近义词：buffooned,  joked,  goofed around

反义词：behaved seriously, acted professionally
