In some contexts, it can also mean a chicken. |例句：She's a really cool chica.  = 她是一个很酷的女孩。  The chica in the red dress is very attractive. = 穿着红裙子的女孩非常有魅力。|近义词：girl, lass, maiden, chick (informal)|反义词：old woman, man|名词
