over-flow's:/ˈoʊvərfloʊz/ | v. | 溢出；泛滥；充满 | To exceed the capacity of; to become full and spill over.  |例句：The river overflowed its banks after the heavy rain.  暴雨过后，河水漫过了河岸。

The bathtub overflowed because I forgot to turn off the faucet. 浴缸溢出了，因为我忘了关水龙头。


近义词：flood, inundate, deluge | 反义词：underflow, drain


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The water overflows the cup. 水溢出了杯子。
The river often overflows in the spring. 春天河水经常泛滥。

一般过去时:
The milk overflowed the glass. 牛奶溢出了杯子。
The dam overflowed during the storm. 暴风雨期间，水坝决堤了。

现在完成时:
The river has overflowed several times this year.  今年这条河已经泛滥好几次了。
The bucket has overflowed; the water is everywhere. 水桶溢出来了，水到处都是。

过去完成时:
The river had overflowed before we could build a dam. 在我们建水坝之前，河水已经泛滥了。
The bowl had overflowed, leaving a mess on the counter. 碗溢出来了，在柜台上留下一堆烂摊子。

将来时:
The river will overflow if it rains heavily tonight. 如果今晚下大雨，河水就会泛滥。
The container will overflow unless we reduce the flow. 除非我们减少流量，否则容器会溢出。

虚拟语气:
If the dam broke, the river would overflow. 如果水坝决堤，河水就会泛滥。
I wish the river hadn't overflowed. 我真希望河水没有泛滥。

被动语态:
The streets were overflowed by the rising tide. 街道被上涨的潮水淹没了。
The container is being overflowed by the liquid. 容器正在被液体溢满。

疑问句:
Does the cup overflow easily? 杯子容易溢出来吗？
Will the tank overflow if we add more water? 如果我们加更多水，水箱会溢出来吗？

否定句:
The cup doesn't overflow easily. 杯子不容易溢出来。
The river didn't overflow last year.  去年河水没有泛滥。
