reexchange: /ˌriːɪksˈtʃeɪndʒ/ | v. | 再交换；重新兑换 | To exchange again; to exchange something for a second time, often implying a reversal or correction of a previous exchange.  |例句：The bank agreed to reexchange the damaged currency.  银行同意重新兑换损坏的货币。

The company decided to reexchange the faulty parts for new ones. 公司决定将有缺陷的零件重新兑换成新的。|近义词：re-exchange,  redeem, replace |反义词：retain, keep


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reexchange the gifts every Christmas. = 他们每年圣诞节都会重新交换礼物。
The shop reexchanges faulty goods without question. = 商店毫无疑问地会重新交换有缺陷的商品。


一般过去时:
We reexchanged the addresses after discovering a mistake. = 我们发现错误后重新交换了地址。
