hatcheted:/ˈhætʃɪtɪd/ | v. |用小斧砍；乱砍；粗暴地处理 | To chop or cut with a hatchet; to sever or destroy violently or roughly.  |例句：The woodsman hatcheted the branches from the fallen tree.  = 伐木工人用小斧砍掉倒树上的树枝。

The angry mob hatcheted their way through the barricades. = 愤怒的暴民用小斧砍破了路障。


近义词：chopped, hacked, cleaved, mangled | 反义词：carefully constructed, delicately handled


词源:源自古英语 *haccēte,  意为“小斧”。
