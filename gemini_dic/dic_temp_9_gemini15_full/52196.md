diasporas: /daɪˈæspərəz/ | n. | 散居；侨民；流亡 |  The dispersion of any people from their original homeland.  Diasporas often maintain a strong sense of cultural identity and connection to their ancestral lands, even across generations and geographical distances.  They can be caused by various factors, including war, persecution, famine, or economic hardship. |  The Jewish diaspora is a well-known example, spanning millennia and continents. The African diaspora resulted from the transatlantic slave trade. | 近义词: dispersion, scattering, exile, emigration | 反义词: repatriation, return, homecoming

The African diaspora maintains strong cultural traditions. = 非洲侨民保持着强大的文化传统。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
