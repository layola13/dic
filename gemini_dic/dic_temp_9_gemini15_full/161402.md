postulate:/ˈpɒstʃuleɪt/ /ˈpɑːstʃəleɪt/| v. n.|假定；假设；提出；要求|To postulate something is to suggest or assume it as a basis for reasoning or arguing.  As a noun, it refers to a statement that is assumed to be true without proof.|The scientist postulated a new theory to explain the phenomenon. 科学家提出了一个新的理论来解释这种现象。
