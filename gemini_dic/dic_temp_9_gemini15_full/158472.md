pledging:/ˈplɛdʒɪŋ/| v.|保证；许诺；抵押；誓言|The act of making a solemn promise or commitment; the act of giving something as security for a loan or debt.|例句：The company is pledging to reduce its carbon footprint.  公司承诺减少碳足迹。

例句：He pledged his support for the candidate. 他保证支持这位候选人。

近义词：promising, vowing, guaranteeing, committing

反义词：breaking (a promise), reneging, defaulting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pledges his allegiance to the country. 他宣誓效忠国家。
They are pledging support for the charity.  他们正在承诺支持慈善事业。

一般过去时:
She pledged her diamond necklace as collateral. 她用钻石项链作抵押。
He pledged to donate a large sum of money. 他承诺捐赠一大笔钱。

现在完成时:
They have pledged to complete the project by the end of the year. 他们已经承诺在年底前完成项目。
We have pledged our support to the cause. 我们已经承诺支持这项事业。

过去完成时:
They had pledged their loyalty before the war began. 战争开始前，他们已经宣誓效忠。
She had pledged to help him, but she forgot. 她曾承诺帮助他，但她忘了。

将来时:
He will pledge his life to protecting his family. 他将誓死保护家人。
The government will pledge more funding for education. 政府将承诺为教育提供更多资金。

虚拟语气:
If he pledged his support, we would have a better chance of success. 如果他承诺支持，我们将有更大的成功机会。
I wish he had pledged to help us earlier. 我希望他早点承诺帮助我们。

被动语态:
The land was pledged as security for the loan. 这块土地被抵押作为贷款的担保。
His support was pledged to the project. 他的支持被承诺给这个项目。

疑问句:
Has he pledged his support yet? 他是否已经承诺支持？
Will the company pledge to improve working conditions? 公司会承诺改善工作条件吗？

否定句:
He did not pledge to pay back the money. 他没有承诺偿还这笔钱。
The government refused to pledge any further funds. 政府拒绝承诺提供任何进一步的资金。


PHRASE->
pledge allegiance  宣誓效忠
pledge one's word 保证；承诺
pledge support  承诺支持
pledge to do something 承诺做某事
break a pledge 违背诺言

词源:
源自古法语“plegier”,意为“to guarantee, to give as surety”,  最终源自拉丁语“pignus”, 意为"pawn, pledge".
