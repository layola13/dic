demagogue:/ˈdɛməɡɑːɡ/| n.|煽动者；蛊惑民心的政客|A demagogue is a political leader who seeks support by appealing to the desires and prejudices of ordinary people rather than using rational argument.  They often use populist rhetoric to gain power and manipulate the masses. |Example: The demagogue used fear-mongering tactics to sway public opinion.  煽动者利用危言耸听的策略来左右民意。|近义词：agitator, rabble-rouser, firebrand|反义词：statesman, leader (positive connotation)|名词
