preoffer:/ˈpriːˌɒfər/| n. |初步报价；初步提议；预先报价|A preoffer is a preliminary offer made before a formal offer is presented.  It often involves initial negotiations and discussions to gauge interest and explore potential terms.  It's not legally binding. |Example: The company received a preoffer for the acquisition of their subsidiary.  这家公司收到了收购其子公司的初步报价。

The preoffer stage was crucial in setting the foundation for the final contract. 预先报价阶段对于最终合同的制定至关重要。|近义词：preliminary proposal, initial offer, tentative offer|反义词：final offer, formal offer|名词
