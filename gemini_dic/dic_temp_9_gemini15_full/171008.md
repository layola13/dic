pseudoscope:/ˈsjuːdəʊskoʊp/ | n. |伪立体镜|An optical device that reverses the normal perception of depth, making concave objects appear convex and vice versa.  It achieves this by presenting the image to each eye in a way that is the reverse of normal binocular vision. | Example:  Using a pseudoscope, a hollow mask appears as a three-dimensional face.  使用伪立体镜，空心的面具看起来像一个三维的脸。|近义词：stereoscope (in the sense of reversing depth perception),  反义词：stereoscope (in the sense of enhancing depth perception) |名词
