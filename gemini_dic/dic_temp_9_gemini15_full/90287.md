housemaids:/ˈhaʊsˌmeɪdz/ | n. |女佣，家庭女仆 | Female domestic servants who perform household chores.  The term often implies a lower level of domestic service than a housekeeper. |  The housemaids cleaned the entire mansion before the guests arrived.  女佣们在客人到达之前打扫了整座豪宅。  Several housemaids were needed to maintain the large estate.  维持这片广阔的庄园需要好几个女佣。 | maids, servants |  (反义词较少，可考虑与雇主相对) employers | 名词
