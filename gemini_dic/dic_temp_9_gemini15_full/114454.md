makeshifts:/ˈmeɪkʃɪfts/| n. v.|权宜之计；临时替代品；临时凑合|A makeshift is something that is made or done quickly and without great care, often because of a lack of resources or time.  It is a temporary solution to a problem.  As a verb, it means to create something makeshift.|例句：They built a makeshift shelter from branches and leaves.他们用树枝和树叶搭建了一个临时住所。

The refugees used makeshift tents.难民们使用了临时帐篷。


近义词：improvisation, expedient, substitute, temporary solution|反义词：permanent solution, carefully planned solution|名词，动词
