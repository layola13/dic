USAGE->
This word is an adjective, therefore it doesn't have tenses like verbs.  Its usage is primarily descriptive.

Example Sentences:

1.  He took a nonnautical approach to solving the problem, ignoring the tide charts completely. 他采取了非航海的方式来解决这个问题，完全忽略了潮汐图。
2.  The nonnautical aspects of the coastal town were more appealing to her than the harbor itself.  沿海城镇的非航海方面比港口本身更吸引她。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "nonnautically".  The word is generally used as a stand-alone adjective to modify nouns.  For example:  "nonnautical navigation", "nonnautical activities", "nonnautical equipment".
