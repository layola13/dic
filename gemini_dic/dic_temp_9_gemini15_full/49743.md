demographist:/dɪˈmɒɡrəfɪst/| n. |人口统计学家|A demographist is a social scientist who studies the characteristics of human populations, such as size, growth, density, distribution, and vital statistics.  They analyze data to understand population trends and their impact on society.|  The demographist presented her findings on the aging population to the government committee.  人口统计学家向政府委员会展示了她关于人口老龄化的调查结果。| sociologist, statistician |  (no direct antonym)|名词
