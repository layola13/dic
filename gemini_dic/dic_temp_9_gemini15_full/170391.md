provokement:/prəˈvoʊkmənt/ | n. | 挑衅；激怒；煽动 | The act of provoking or inciting someone; something that provokes or incites.  It often implies an intention to cause anger or a negative reaction. |例句：His provocative remarks led to a heated argument. 他的挑衅性言论导致了一场激烈的争吵。

例句：The constant provocations from the neighboring country finally led to war.  来自邻国的不断挑衅最终导致了战争。


近义词：provocation, incitement, instigation, stimulation | 反义词：pacification, appeasement, conciliation | 词性：名词


词源:  Derived from the verb "provoke," which comes from the Old French "provoquer" (to challenge, to summon), ultimately from the Latin "provocare" (to call forth, to challenge). The suffix "-ment" is a noun suffix indicating a result or action.
