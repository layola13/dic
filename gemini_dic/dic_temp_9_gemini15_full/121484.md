mislay:/mɪsˈleɪ/| vt.| 把…放错地方；丢失 | To put something somewhere and then forget where you put it. This implies a temporary loss, not necessarily a permanent one.  | I mislaid my keys; I can't find them anywhere. 我把钥匙放错地方了，哪儿也找不到。  | lose, misplace | find, locate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I often mislay my glasses. = 我经常把眼镜放错地方。
He mislays his phone every week. = 他每周都把手机放错地方。

一般过去时:
I mislaid my passport last year. = 我去年把护照放错地方了。
She mislaid the important document. = 她把重要的文件放错地方了。

现在完成时:
I have mislaid my wallet several times this month. = 这个月我已经好几次把钱包放错地方了。
They have mislaid the contract, delaying the project. = 他们把合同放错地方了，耽误了项目。

过去完成时:
I realized I had mislaid my notes before the presentation. = 我在演示文稿之前才意识到我把笔记放错地方了。
She was upset because she had mislaid her grandmother's ring. = 她很沮丧，因为她把祖母的戒指放错地方了。

将来时:
I'm afraid I'll mislay my ticket again. = 我担心我会再次把票放错地方。
He thinks he might mislay his phone at the party. = 他认为他可能在派对上把手机放错地方了。

虚拟语气:
If I hadn't mislaid the instructions, I would have finished the project on time. = 如果我没有把说明书放错地方，我就能按时完成项目了。
I wish I hadn't mislaid my camera during the trip. = 我真希望旅行期间我没有把相机放错地方。

被动语态:  (Mislay is rarely used in the passive voice because it requires a direct object)  The passive form is grammatically possible but uncommon.  For instance:  The documents were mislaid. 文件被放错地方了。

疑问句:
Did you mislay your phone again? = 你又把手机放错地方了吗？
Have you ever mislaid something important? = 你曾经把什么重要的东西放错地方过吗？

否定句:
I didn't mislay my keys; I just can't find them. = 我没有把钥匙放错地方；我只是找不到它们。
He said he hadn't mislaid the letter, but I doubt it. = 他说他没有把信放错地方，但我对此表示怀疑。
