词源:
The term "six-shooter" is a compound word.  "Six" refers to the number of cartridges the revolver holds, and "shooter" refers to a firearm.  The term emerged in the late 19th century alongside the popularity of the Colt Peacemaker and similar revolvers in the American Old West.
