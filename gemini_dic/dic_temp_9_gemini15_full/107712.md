lanthanum: /ˈlænθənəm/ | n. |镧| A silvery-white, soft, malleable, ductile, and highly reactive rare-earth metallic chemical element. It is relatively abundant in the Earth's crust and is used in various alloys and compounds.| Lanthanum is used in the production of certain types of glass, especially those used in camera lenses.|  The discovery of lanthanum revolutionized the field of rare-earth elements. | 近义词：rare-earth metal (广义)| 反义词：无
