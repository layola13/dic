eviscerators:/ɪˈvɪsərˌeɪtərz/| n.|掏空内脏者；去除内脏的人或物|Those who or that which removes the internal organs.|The eviscerators worked quickly and efficiently, preparing the fish for market. 掏空内脏者快速有效地工作，准备将鱼送往市场。|近义词： disemboweler, gutting machine |反义词：  None specifically.  (Antonyms would describe the opposite action, like preserving internal organs.)
