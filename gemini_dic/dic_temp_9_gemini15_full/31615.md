chastities:/ˈtʃæstɪtiz/| n.|贞洁；纯洁；禁欲|The state or practice of refraining from extramarital, or any, sexual intercourse.|例句：She vowed to uphold her chasities. 她发誓要保持贞洁。|例句：The monk's life of chastity was a testament to his faith.  这位僧侣禁欲的生活证明了他的信仰。|近义词：purity, abstinence, continence|反义词：lechery, debauchery, promiscuity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Her chastity is unquestionable. = 她的贞洁不容置疑。
He values chastity above all else. = 他把贞洁看得高于一切。


一般过去时:
Her chastity was admired by all. = 她的贞洁受到所有人的赞赏。

现在完成时:
She has maintained her chastity throughout her life. = 她一生都保持着贞洁。
He has vowed chastity since his ordination. = 自从他受戒以来就发誓要保持贞洁。


过去完成时:
They had preserved their chastity before marriage. = 他们婚前一直保持贞洁。
She had vowed chastity to her late husband. = 她曾向已故的丈夫发誓要保持贞洁。


将来时:
She will maintain her chastity. = 她将会保持贞洁。

被动语态:
