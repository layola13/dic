dipterygian:/ˌdɪptəˈrɪdʒiən/| adj.|双翅类的；具双翅的|Relating to or characteristic of the Dipteran order of insects, which have two wings.  The term often refers to the characteristic features of this order, such as the halteres (modified hindwings) and specialized mouthparts.|Example:  The dipterygian fly had iridescent wings. 这只双翅目苍蝇的翅膀闪闪发光。 The study focused on the dipterygian fauna of the rainforest. 这项研究的重点是雨林中的双翅目动物群。|近义词：two-winged|反义词：apterous (wingless)
