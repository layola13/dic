resources:/rɪˈsɔːrsɪz/| n.|资源，财力|Material or assets that can be used to achieve a goal.  Resources can be anything from natural resources like land and minerals to human resources like labor and skills, to financial resources like money and investments.|例句：The company's success depends on its human resources. 这家公司的成功取决于它的人力资源。|例句：We need to conserve our natural resources. 我们需要保护我们的自然资源。|近义词：assets, means, supplies|反义词：lack, shortage

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company uses its resources efficiently. = 公司有效地利用其资源。
Our team needs more resources to complete the project. = 我们的团队需要更多资源来完成项目。

一般过去时:
They depleted their resources during the war. = 战争期间他们耗尽了资源。
The company allocated its resources wisely last year. = 公司去年明智地分配了资源。

现在完成时:
We have exhausted our financial resources. = 我们已经耗尽了财政资源。
They have utilized their resources effectively. = 他们已经有效地利用了资源。

过去完成时:
They had already used up their resources before the crisis hit. = 在危机爆发之前，他们就已经用光了资源。
The country had mismanaged its resources for decades. = 这个国家几十年来一直对资源进行错误管理。

将来时:
We will need additional resources to expand the business. = 我们将需要额外资源来扩展业务。
The government will invest in renewable resources. = 政府将投资可再生资源。

虚拟语气:
If we had more resources, we could achieve our goals faster. = 如果我们有更多资源，我们可以更快地实现目标。
I wish we had access to more resources. = 我希望我们能够获得更多资源。

被动语态:
The resources were allocated unfairly. = 资源分配不公平。
The project's resources are being carefully managed. = 项目资源正在被仔细管理。

疑问句:

Where can we find the necessary resources? = 我们在哪里可以找到必要的资源？

否定句:
