nonoxidizable:/ˌnɒnˈɒksɪdaɪzəbl/| adj. |不易氧化的；耐氧化的|Not easily oxidized or capable of being oxidized.  This term describes materials that resist chemical reaction with oxygen,  meaning they don't readily rust, corrode, or tarnish.|Example: Platinum is a nonoxidizable metal, making it ideal for jewelry and high-temperature applications.  铂是一种不易氧化的金属，使其成为珠宝和高温应用的理想选择。|近义词：corrosion-resistant, oxidation-resistant, stable|反义词：oxidizable, readily oxidizable
