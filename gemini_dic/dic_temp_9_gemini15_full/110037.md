licensing: /ˈlaɪsənsiŋ/ | n. |许可证发放；许可；授权 |The granting of licenses or permits.  The process of obtaining a license.  The system or practice of licensing. |例句：The licensing of the new software is handled by a separate department. 新软件的许可证发放由一个独立的部门负责。


The company's licensing agreement allows for distribution in several countries. 该公司的许可协议允许在几个国家进行分销。|近义词：authorization, permission, granting |反义词：prohibition, ban, restriction |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government licenses many different types of businesses. = 政府许可许多不同类型的企业。
Our company is licensing its new technology to other firms. = 我们公司正在将其新技术授权给其他公司。


一般过去时:
They licensed the song for use in the film. = 他们许可这首歌用于电影中。
The authorities licensed the new restaurant last week. = 当局上周批准了这家新餐厅的营业许可。


现在完成时:
We have licensed several products this year. = 今年我们已经许可了几种产品。
The company has licensed its technology to numerous partners. = 该公司已将其技术许可给众多合作伙伴。


过去完成时:
They had already licensed the software before the project started. = 项目开始前他们已经取得了该软件的许可。
The band had licensed its music to several advertising campaigns. = 乐队已将其音乐授权给多个广告宣传活动。


将来时:
They will license the technology to a major corporation. = 他们将把这项技术许可给一家大型公司。
We will be licensing our software to companies worldwide. = 我们将把我们的软件授权给全球各地的公司。


虚拟语气:
If we licensed the technology, we could expand our market share. = 如果我们获得该技术的许可，我们可以扩大我们的市场份额。
I wish we had licensed the patent earlier. = 我真希望我们早点获得专利许可。


被动语态:
The software is licensed by a third-party company. = 该软件由第三方公司授权。
The restaurant was licensed to operate in this area. = 这家餐厅被许可在这个区域经营。


疑问句:
Is the software licensed for commercial use? = 该软件是否允许用于商业用途？
Has the company been licensed to distribute the product? = 该公司是否已获得分销该产品的许可？


否定句:
The software is not licensed for redistribution. = 该软件不允许重新分发。
They did not license the music for use in the commercial. = 他们没有许可将音乐用于商业广告。
