connoting:/kənˈnoʊtɪŋ/ | v. |暗示；意味着；隐含|To suggest or imply in addition to the literal or primary meaning.  The word "connoting" describes the action of a word, phrase, or image suggesting a secondary meaning beyond its explicit definition. It focuses on the implied or associated meanings rather than the dictionary definition.|The dark clouds connoting an impending storm.  乌云暗示着一场即将到来的暴风雨。|imply, suggest, signify, indicate|denote, explicitly state|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The word "home" connotes warmth and security. = “家”这个词暗示着温暖和安全。
This painting connotes a sense of loss and despair. = 这幅画暗示着一种失落和绝望的感觉。


一般过去时:
Her silence connoted disapproval. = 她的沉默暗示着不满。
The article connoted a bias against the minority group. = 这篇文章暗示了对少数群体的偏见。


现在完成时:
His actions have connoted a lack of responsibility. = 他的行为暗示了他缺乏责任感。
The author's style has connoted a deep understanding of human nature. = 作者的风格暗示了他对人性的深刻理解。


过去完成时:
The old house had connoted a history of family secrets. = 这栋旧房子暗示着家族秘密的历史。
His nervous laughter had connoted guilt. = 他紧张的笑声暗示了内疚。


将来时:
The upcoming election will connote significant political change. = 即将到来的选举将暗示着重大的政治变革。
His new book will connote a shift in his writing style. = 他的新书将暗示着他写作风格的转变。


虚拟语气:
If the image connoted hope, it would inspire us. = 如果这幅图像暗示着希望，它将会激励我们。
I wish the poem connoted more joy. = 我希望这首诗能暗示更多快乐。


被动语态:
Hope is connoted by the rising sun. = 升起的太阳暗示着希望。
A sense of urgency was connoted by the ticking clock. = 滴答作响的时钟暗示着紧迫感。


疑问句:
What does this symbol connote? = 这个符号暗示着什么？
Does this phrase connote anything negative? = 这个短语暗示着任何负面的东西吗？


否定句:
The picture doesn't connote anything specific. = 这幅图画没有暗示任何具体的东西。
The data does not connote a causal relationship. = 数据并没有暗示因果关系。
