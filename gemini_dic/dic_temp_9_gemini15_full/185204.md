retracement:/rɪˈtreɪsmənt/| n. |回溯；回落；倒退|The act of retracing one's steps or going back over something again.  It can refer to a return to a previous position or level, often in a financial or geographical context.  |例句：The hiker retraced his steps to find his lost wallet.  徒步旅行者沿原路返回寻找他丢失的钱包。

例句：After a sharp rise, the stock market experienced a significant retracement.  股市在急剧上涨后经历了大幅回调。


近义词：reversal, backtracking, regression, recovery


反义词：advancement, progression, surge


词性：名词
