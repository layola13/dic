nonhumus: /ˌnɒnˈhjuːməs/ | n. |非腐殖质的 |  Nonhumus refers to soil material that is not humus.  Humus is the dark organic matter formed by the decomposition of plant and animal matter. Therefore, nonhumus encompasses all the mineral and inorganic components of soil, excluding the decayed organic matter. |  The soil sample contained a high percentage of nonhumus material.  这土壤样本含有高比例的非腐殖质物质。  The researcher analyzed the nonhumus fraction of the soil to determine its mineral composition. 研究人员分析了土壤的非腐殖质部分以确定其矿物成分。 |  mineral soil, inorganic matter | humus, organic matter | 名词
