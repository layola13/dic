reoperating:/ˌriːˈɒpəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 再手术，重新操作 |  The act of performing a surgical operation again, or operating again on a machine or system.  This word implies a repetition of a previous operation, often due to complications or the need for further intervention. | Example:  The surgeon is reoperating on the patient due to complications. = 由于并发症，外科医生正在为病人再次手术。

The patient required reoperating after the initial surgery failed to resolve the issue. = 首次手术未能解决问题后，病人需要再次手术。


近义词：re-operating, operating again, performing a second operation


反义词：initial operation, first operation


词性：动词
