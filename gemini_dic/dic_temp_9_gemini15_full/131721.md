nonconferrable: /ˌnɒnkənˈfɜːrəbl/| adj. |不可转让的；不可授予的| Not able to be transferred or granted to another. This word describes something that cannot be passed on, given away, or delegated to someone else.  It often relates to rights, privileges, or properties. | Example: The CEO's special parking spot is nonconferrable; only the CEO can use it.  首席执行官的专用停车位是不可转让的；只有首席执行官才能使用它。|近义词：nontransferable, inalienable, unassignable |反义词：transferable, assignable
