nonfermenting: /ˌnɒnˈfɜːrmɛntɪŋ/ | adj. | 不发酵的 |  Describing bacteria that do not ferment carbohydrates; they obtain energy through oxidative metabolism.  These bacteria typically don't produce acid or gas during carbohydrate metabolism. |


例句：

Many nonfermenting Gram-negative bacilli are opportunistic pathogens. = 许多不发酵革兰氏阴性杆菌是机会致病菌。

The laboratory identified the isolate as a nonfermenting organism. = 实验室将分离物鉴定为不发酵微生物。


近义词：oxidative (in the context of metabolism)


反义词：fermenting


词性：形容词
