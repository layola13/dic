compulsion:/kəmˈpʌlʃən/ | n. | 强迫；冲动；强制 | An irresistible urge to behave in a certain way, especially against one's conscious wishes.  A compelling force, influence, or urge. | 例句：He felt a compulsion to tell her the truth. 他感到一种强烈的冲动要告诉她真相。

He acted under compulsion. 他被迫行事。


近义词：urge, impulse, drive, obsession | 反义词：freedom, choice, volition | 词性：名词
