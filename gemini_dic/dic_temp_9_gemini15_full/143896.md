outpowered: /ˌaʊtˈpaʊərd/ | v. | 胜过；功率超过；比…强大 | To surpass in power; to exceed in power or strength. | 例句：The new engine outpowered its predecessor.  新的引擎比它的前身动力更强。  | 例句：The larger company easily outpowered the smaller startup.  更大的公司轻松地胜过了较小的初创公司。 | 近义词：overpower, surpass, outmatch, exceed | 反义词：be outpowered by, be overpowered by


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The larger engine outpowers the smaller one. =  更大的引擎动力超过了较小的引擎。
He consistently outpowers his opponents in debates. = 他在辩论中总是胜过对手。


一般过去时:
Our team outpowered the opposing team in the final game. = 我们队在决赛中胜过了对方球队。
The storm outpowered the city's power grid. = 暴风雨的威力超过了城市的电力网。


现在完成时:
They have outpowered their rivals in the market. = 他们在市场上已经胜过了竞争对手。
The new technology has outpowered the old system. = 新技术已经超过了旧系统。


过去完成时:

The army had outpowered the rebels' defenses. = 军队已经突破了叛军的防线。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If they had a better engine, they could outpower their rivals. = 如果他们有更好的引擎，他们就能胜过对手。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

Our efforts did not outpower the challenges we faced. = 我们的努力没有克服我们面临的挑战。
