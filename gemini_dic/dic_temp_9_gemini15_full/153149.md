pelegrine: /ˈpɛləɡrɪn/ | n. | 游隼；旅人；外国侨民 | A peregrine falcon is a type of falcon known for its speed and hunting prowess.  It is also used to describe someone who travels or lives in a foreign country. | 例句：The peregrine falcon is the fastest bird in the world. = 游隼是世界上飞得最快的鸟。  He lived as a peregrine in Paris for many years. = 他在巴黎做了多年的侨民。 | 近义词：falcon (for the bird), traveler, expatriate, immigrant | 反义词：native (opposite of expatriate), sedentary (opposite of traveler)
