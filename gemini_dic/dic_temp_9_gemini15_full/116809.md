max:/mæks/ | /mæks/| n. adj. | 最大值；最大限度；最多的；极大的 | The maximum value; the greatest possible amount. |The maximum speed is 100 kilometers per hour.  最大速度是每小时100公里。|The maximum number of participants is 100. 参加者最多100人。|maximum, highest, utmost | minimum, least


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The maximum speed is 100 mph. = 最大速度是每小时100英里。
What is the maximum capacity of this container? = 这个容器的最大容量是多少？


一般过去时:
The maximum temperature yesterday was 35 degrees Celsius. = 昨天的最高温度是摄氏35度。
The maximum attendance at the concert was 5000. = 音乐会的最大出席人数是5000人。


现在完成时:
The maximum temperature this week has been 38 degrees. = 本周的最高温度是38度。
He has reached the maximum level in the game. = 他已经达到了游戏中的最高等级。


过去完成时:
The maximum amount they had spent before the project ended was $100,000. = 在项目结束之前，他们最多花费了10万美元。
Before the accident, the maximum speed of the train had been 150 km/h. = 事故发生前，火车的最高速度是每小时150公里。


将来时:
The maximum temperature will be 25 degrees tomorrow. = 明天的最高温度将是25度。
The maximum capacity of the stadium will be reached during the final match. = 在决赛期间，体育场的最大容量将达到。


虚拟语气:
If the maximum capacity were exceeded, there would be safety concerns. = 如果超过最大容量，就会有安全隐患。
I wish the maximum speed limit were higher. = 我希望最高速度限制更高一些。


被动语态:
The maximum load is specified by the manufacturer. = 最大负载由制造商规定。
The maximum number of entries was determined by the committee. = 最大参赛人数由委员会决定。


疑问句:
What is the maximum weight allowed? = 最大允许重量是多少？
What's the maximum number of students in a class? = 一个班级的学生最大人数是多少？


否定句:
The speed was not at its maximum. = 速度没有达到最大值。
The amount of data received did not reach the maximum. = 收到的数据量没有达到最大值。
