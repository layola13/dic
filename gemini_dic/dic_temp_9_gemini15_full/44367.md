cross-contamination:/ˌkrɒs kənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |交叉污染|The transfer of harmful bacteria or other microorganisms from one food, surface, or person to another.|Example:  Cross-contamination can occur when raw meat juices drip onto cooked vegetables. 交叉污染可能发生在生的肉汁滴到熟蔬菜上的时候。
Example: To prevent cross-contamination, always wash your hands thoroughly after handling raw meat. 为避免交叉污染，处理生肉后务必彻底洗手。|近义词：contamination, infection, transmission|反义词：sterilization, purification|名词
