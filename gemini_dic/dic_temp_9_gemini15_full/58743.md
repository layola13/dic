dungarees:/ˈdʌŋɡəˌriz/| n. |工装裤，牛仔连体裤 |Loose-fitting trousers, typically made of denim, with shoulder straps and bib.  They are often worn by workers, but are also popular casual wear. |例句：She wore dungarees and a t-shirt to the farm.  她穿着工装裤和T恤去农场。
例句：My son loves his new blue dungarees. 我的儿子喜欢他新的蓝色工装裤。|近义词：overalls, boiler suits|反义词：dress, skirt
