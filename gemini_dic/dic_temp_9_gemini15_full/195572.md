semiproductiveness:/ˌsɛmiˌprɑːdəkˈtɪvnəs/| n. |半生产力；部分生产力 |The state or quality of being only partially productive.  It suggests a level of output that falls short of full or ideal productivity, but is not entirely unproductive.  |例句：The factory's semiproductiveness was attributed to outdated equipment and insufficient training.|近义词：partial productivity, limited productivity, suboptimal productivity|反义词：high productivity, full productivity, maximum productivity
