discussing:/dɪˈskʌsɪŋ/| v. |讨论；议论|The act of talking about something in detail, typically with others, to exchange ideas or information or reach a decision.  |例句：They are discussing the merits of different investment strategies. 他们正在讨论不同投资策略的优劣。|例句：We were discussing the possibility of a new marketing campaign. 我们正在讨论一项新的营销活动的可行性。|近义词：debating, conversing, conferring, deliberating |反义词：ignoring, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are discussing the project. = 他们在讨论这个项目。
We discuss these issues regularly. = 我们定期讨论这些问题。

一般过去时:
They discussed the problem for hours. = 他们讨论了这个问题几个小时。
We discussed the proposal last week. = 我们上周讨论了这个提案。

现在完成时:
They have discussed the matter thoroughly. = 他们已经彻底讨论了这个问题。
We have discussed this many times before. = 我们以前多次讨论过这个问题。

过去完成时:
They had discussed the plan before the meeting. = 会议前他们已经讨论了这个计划。
We had discussed the options before making a decision. = 我们在做决定之前已经讨论了各种选择。

将来时:
They will be discussing the budget tomorrow. = 他们明天将讨论预算。
We will discuss the next steps at the next meeting. = 我们将在下次会议上讨论下一步行动。

虚拟语气:
If they were discussing the issue honestly, we might find a solution. = 如果他们诚实地讨论这个问题，我们或许能找到解决方案。
I wish they were discussing the problem more openly. = 我希望他们能更公开地讨论这个问题。

被动语态:
The proposal was discussed extensively. = 这个提案被广泛讨论了。
The issue is being discussed by the committee. = 这个问题正在由委员会讨论。

疑问句:
Are they discussing the new policy? = 他们正在讨论新政策吗？
What are they discussing? = 他们在讨论什么？

否定句:
They are not discussing the sensitive topic. = 他们没有讨论这个敏感话题。
We didn't discuss the financial details. = 我们没有讨论财务细节。


PHRASE->
discuss the matter = 讨论此事
discuss at length = 详细讨论
open to discussion = 可供讨论
under discussion = 正在讨论中
subject to discussion = 有待讨论
