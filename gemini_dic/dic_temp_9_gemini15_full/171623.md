pulpit:/ˈpʌlpɪt/| n.|讲坛；布道坛|A raised platform or lectern in a church or other place of worship, from which a preacher or speaker addresses the congregation.|例句：The preacher delivered a powerful sermon from the pulpit.  牧师在讲坛上发表了一篇有力的布道。
例句：The mayor stood at the pulpit to address the crowd. 市长站在讲坛上向人群发表讲话。|近义词：lectern, rostrum|反义词：None|名词
