eden:/ˈiːdn/| n.|伊甸园；乐园|A biblical paradise, the garden of Eden, often used metaphorically to represent a state of idyllic happiness, innocence, and perfection.  It is also used to describe a place of great beauty and peace.|例句：The lush garden was an earthly eden.  这片茂盛的花园宛如人间伊甸园。|例句：She remembered her childhood as a lost eden. 她回忆起童年，那是一个逝去的乐园。|近义词：paradise, utopia, haven|反义词：hell, dystopia
