protocols:/ˈproʊtəkɔːlz/ | n. |协议；规程；礼仪 |  A set of rules or standards for data transmission, network communication, or other processes.  Protocols define the format and order of messages exchanged between systems. | Example:  The company implemented new security protocols to protect sensitive data.  这家公司实施了新的安全协议来保护敏感数据。The diplomatic protocols were strictly adhered to during the visit. 在访问期间，外交礼仪得到了严格遵守。|近义词：procedures, conventions, rules, regulations |反义词：chaos, disorder
