carry-overs: /ˈkæriˌoʊvərz/ | n. | 结转；遗留问题；延续的事物 | Things that continue from one period to the next.  They are items, tasks, or situations that haven't been completed or resolved and are carried forward to a later time or period. |

例句：

The company is dealing with carry-overs from last year's budget.  = 公司正在处理去年预算结转的问题。

There are several carry-overs from the previous project that need addressing. =  有一些来自先前项目的遗留问题需要解决。


近义词：remainders, residuals, continuations, legacies

反义词：completions, resolutions, conclusions


词性：名词

The project has many carry-overs. = 这个项目有很多遗留问题。
There are several carry-overs from the previous meeting. = 上次会议遗留了好几个问题。
