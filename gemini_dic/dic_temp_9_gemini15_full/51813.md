dewberries:/ˈduːbɛriz/| n.|露莓，黑莓的一种|A dewberry is a type of blackberry, typically smaller and sweeter than the common blackberry, with a darker, more purplish-black color. They grow wild and are often found trailing along the ground.|例句：We picked a basket full of dewberries along the trail. 我们沿着小路摘了一篮子露莓。|近义词：blackberries, brambles|反义词：无
