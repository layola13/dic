goodiest:/ˈɡʊdiɪst/| adj.|最好的；最优秀的|The superlative form of "good," indicating the highest degree of goodness or quality.  It is often used informally or in a playful or ironic way.  |例句：This is the goodiest cake I've ever tasted! 这蛋糕是我吃过最好吃的！

例句：She claimed to have the goodiest collection of stamps in the town. 她声称拥有镇上最好的邮票收藏。


近义词：best, finest, top-notch, excellent

反义词：worst, poorest, inferior

词性:形容词
