nudgers:/ˈnʌdʒərz/| n.|轻推者；劝诱者；轻微推动者|People or things that subtly encourage or guide someone towards a particular decision or action, often without the person realizing they are being influenced.|Example:  The government implemented nudgers to encourage people to save more for retirement.  政府实施了轻推策略来鼓励人们为退休储蓄更多。|近义词：influencers, guides, prompters|反义词：obstructors, hinderers|名词
