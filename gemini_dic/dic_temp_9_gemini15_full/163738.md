predefine:/ˌpriːdɪˈfaɪn/| vt.|预定义，预先定义|To define (something) beforehand; to establish or specify (something) in advance.|The variables were predefined in the program.  程序中预定义了变量。
The rules of the game were predefined before we started playing. 游戏规则在我们开始玩之前就已预先定义。|近义词：specify, determine, set, establish|反义词：undefined, unspecified

We predefine the project parameters. 我们预定义项目参数。
