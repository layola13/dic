pyretic:/paɪˈrɛtɪk/| adj.|发热的，热病的|Relating to or characterized by fever.  The word describes a condition or symptom, specifically a feverish state. It often refers to the symptoms associated with an infection or illness.|例句：He exhibited pyretic symptoms, including high fever and chills. 他表现出发热症状，包括高烧和寒战。|例句：The doctor prescribed medication to reduce the patient's pyretic condition. 医生开了药来降低病人的发热状态。|近义词：febrile, feverish|反义词：afebrile
