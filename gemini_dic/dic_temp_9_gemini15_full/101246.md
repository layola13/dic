invocation:/ˌɪnvəˈkeɪʃən/| n.|祈求；恳求；调用；仪式开始时的祈祷|The act of invoking or calling upon; a prayer or invocation at the beginning of a ceremony or meeting; the act of calling upon a deity, spirit, or muse for inspiration or aid; in programming, the act of calling a subroutine or function.|例句：The ceremony began with an invocation to the gods.  仪式以对神明的祈求开始。

例句：The programmer used an invocation to the database function to retrieve data. 程序员使用对数据库函数的调用来检索数据。


近义词：prayer, supplication, appeal, call, summoning|反义词：rejection, dismissal, refusal|词性:名词


词源:  From Middle English *invocacioun, from Old French invocacion, from Latin invocatio, from invocare "to call upon," from in- "upon" + vocare "to call."
