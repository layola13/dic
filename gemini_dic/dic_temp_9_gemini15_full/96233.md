/ˈɪndiəz/| n.| 印度(复数)|The plural form of "India," often used to refer to the Indian subcontinent or its various regions.  It can also be used in historical or literary contexts.| Examples:  The spices of the Indies were highly sought after. (印度香料非常抢手。) The East Indies were a major source of trade for European powers. (东印度是欧洲列强的主要贸易来源。) |近义词: India (singular), subcontinent|反义词: None
