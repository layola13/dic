gluttons:/ˈɡlʌtənz/| n.|贪吃者，暴食者|A glutton is a person who eats and drinks excessively.  It often implies a lack of self-control and disregard for consequences.|例句：He's a glutton for punishment, always volunteering for the hardest tasks. 他是个喜欢自找苦吃的人，总是自愿承担最艰巨的任务。|例句：The children were absolute gluttons at the birthday party, devouring all the cake and sweets. 孩子们在生日派对上简直是贪吃鬼，把所有的蛋糕和糖果都吃光了。|近义词：glutton, gourmand, gormandizer, epicure (in the negative sense)|反义词：abstemious, ascetic

词源:
The word "glutton" comes from the Old French word "gluton," meaning "glutton, gourmand."  This, in turn, is derived from the Latin word "glutto,"  which may have an onomatopoeic origin related to sounds of swallowing or eating greedily.
