enterprises:/ˈɛntərpraɪzɪz/| n. |企业；事业；进取心 |Businesses or companies, often large and complex ones.  It can also refer to ambitious projects or undertakings.|例句：Many enterprises are struggling in the current economic climate. 许多企业都在当前的经济环境中苦苦挣扎。

例句：The new enterprise requires significant investment. 这项新事业需要大量的投资。|近义词：businesses, companies, ventures, undertakings|反义词：passivity, inaction


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Google's enterprises are expanding globally. = 谷歌的企业正在全球扩张。
Small enterprises often face challenges securing funding. = 小型企业通常面临融资方面的挑战。

一般过去时:
His enterprises failed due to poor management. = 他的企业由于管理不善而失败了。
Many enterprises participated in the trade fair. = 许多企业参加了贸易展览会。

现在完成时:
Several enterprises have already adopted the new technology. = 几家企业已经采用了这项新技术。
The company has undertaken many successful enterprises over the years. = 多年来，该公司已经开展了许多成功的企业。

过去完成时:

They had completed several major enterprises before launching this one. = 在启动这项企业之前，他们已经完成了几个大型项目。

将来时:
Our enterprises will benefit from the new trade agreement. = 我们的企业将从新的贸易协定中受益。
Many new enterprises will emerge in the coming years. = 未来几年将出现许多新的企业。

虚拟语气:
If the enterprises were better managed, they would be more profitable. = 如果这些企业管理得更好，它们就会更有利可图。
I wish our enterprises had been more prepared for the economic downturn. = 我希望我们的企业对经济衰退有更好的准备。

被动语态:
Many enterprises are being affected by the pandemic. = 许多企业正受到疫情的影响。
The new regulations are being carefully considered by various enterprises. = 各种企业正在仔细考虑新的规章制度。

否定句:
