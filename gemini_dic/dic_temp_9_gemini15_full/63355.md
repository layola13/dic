entomere:/ˈɛntəˌmɪər/| n. | 节肢动物体节|A segment of an arthropod body, particularly referring to a repeating unit in the body plan.  The term is often used in the context of comparative morphology and evolutionary biology to discuss the segmentation of arthropods.|例句：The entomere theory suggests that the arthropod body is composed of a series of repeating units.|近义词：segment, metamere|反义词：无|名词
