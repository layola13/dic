idolizations:/ˌaɪˈdɑːləzeɪʃənz/| n. |偶像崇拜；盲目崇拜|The act of idolizing someone or something; excessive admiration or reverence.  It often implies an unrealistic or uncritical view of the object of admiration.|例句：His idolizations of famous athletes led him to neglect his own studies. 他的偶像崇拜导致他忽视了自己的学业。

例句：The band's idolizations by their fans bordered on obsession.  乐队受到歌迷的狂热崇拜，几乎到了痴迷的程度。


近义词：worship, veneration, adoration, hero-worship

反义词：contempt, disdain, scorn, criticism


词性:名词
