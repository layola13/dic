fashioning:/ˈfæʃənɪŋ/| v.| 制作；塑造；形成 |The act of giving a particular form or shape to something; creating something.|例句：She was fashioning a clay pot. 她正在制作一个陶罐。

例句：He is fashioning a new career for himself. 他正在为自己塑造一个新的职业。


近义词：shaping, molding, forming, creating | 反义词：destroying, ruining, dismantling | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is fashioning a new strategy. = 他正在制定一个新的策略。
She fashions her own clothes. = 她自己缝制衣服。


一般过去时:
The sculptor fashioned the statue from marble. = 雕塑家用大理石雕刻了这尊雕像。
They fashioned a raft from logs. = 他们用原木制作了一只木筏。


现在完成时:
She has fashioned a successful business. = 她已经创建了一家成功的企业。
He has fashioned a new identity for himself. = 他已经为自己塑造了一个新的形象。


过去完成时:
They had fashioned a plan before the meeting. = 他们在会议前就已经制定了一个计划。
She had fashioned a beautiful dress for the party. = 她已经为聚会做了一件漂亮的衣服。


将来时:
They will fashion a new law to address the issue. = 他们将制定一项新法律来解决这个问题。
She will fashion a new life for herself in the city. = 她将在城市里为自己创造一种新的生活。


虚拟语气:
If I had the skills, I would fashion a beautiful sculpture. = 如果我有这项技能，我会雕刻一件美丽的雕塑。
I wish I could fashion a better future for my children. = 我希望我能为我的孩子们创造一个更好的未来。


被动语态:
The vase was fashioned by a skilled artisan. = 这个花瓶是由一位技艺精湛的工匠制作的。
The policy was fashioned to protect the environment. = 这项政策是为了保护环境而制定的。


疑问句:
How was the cake fashioned? = 蛋糕是怎么做成的？
What materials are being fashioned into the sculpture? = 正在用什么材料制作雕塑？


否定句:
He didn't fashion a response to the criticism. = 他没有对批评做出回应。
She couldn't fashion a solution to the problem. = 她无法找到解决这个问题的方法。



PHRASE->
fashion something from something: 用……制作……
fashion something into something: 将……塑造成……
fashion a plan: 制定计划
fashion a response: 拟定回应
fashion a career: 发展职业
