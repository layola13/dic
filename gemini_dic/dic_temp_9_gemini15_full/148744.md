overthrow:/ˈoʊvərθroʊ/|/ˈəʊvəθrəʊ/| vt.|推翻；打倒；颠覆|To remove (a person or government) from power, especially by force.|The rebels attempted to overthrow the government. 反叛者试图推翻政府。  The dictator was overthrown in a coup. 独裁者在一场政变中被推翻。|overturn, depose, subvert|uphold, establish, support|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overthrow the statue. = 他们推翻了雕像。
The people overthrow the corrupt government. = 人民推翻了腐败的政府。

一般过去时:
The king was overthrown by his own son. = 国王被自己的儿子推翻了。
The revolution successfully overthrew the monarchy. = 革命成功地推翻了君主制。

现在完成时:
The rebels have overthrown the dictator. = 反叛者已经推翻了独裁者。
The old system has been overthrown by the new one. = 旧制度已被新制度所取代。

过去完成时:
The people had overthrown the government before the army arrived. = 在军队到达之前，人民已经推翻了政府。
The coup had overthrown the president before anyone realized it. = 政变发生前，没有人意识到总统已经被推翻了。

将来时:
The opposition plans to overthrow the government next year. = 反对派计划明年推翻政府。
They will overthrow the tyrant and establish a democracy. = 他们将推翻暴君，建立民主制度。

虚拟语气:
If the people had been united, they could have overthrown the regime. = 如果人民团结一致，他们就能推翻政权。
I wish we could overthrow the oppressive laws. = 我希望我们能够推翻压迫性的法律。

被动语态:
The government was overthrown in a bloody revolution. = 政府在一场血腥的革命中被推翻了。
The old regime was overthrown by the forces of change. = 旧政权被变革的力量推翻了。

疑问句:
Will the rebels overthrow the government? = 反叛者会推翻政府吗？
Has the dictator been overthrown yet? = 独裁者已经被推翻了吗？

否定句:
The rebels failed to overthrow the government. = 反叛者未能推翻政府。
They did not overthrow the king by force. = 他们没有武力推翻国王。


PHRASE->
overthrow a government = 推翻政府
overthrow a regime = 推翻政权
overthrow a monarchy = 推翻君主制
overthrow a dictatorship = 推翻独裁统治
overthrow the established order = 推翻既定的秩序
