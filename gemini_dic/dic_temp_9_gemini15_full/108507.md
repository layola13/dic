layner:/ˈleɪnər/| n.|衬里，内衬|A liner is a layer of material used to line something, such as a garment or a container, to protect it or to give it a better appearance.  |例句：The coat has a warm quilted liner. 这件外套有一个温暖的绗缝内衬。|例句：She added a liner to her suitcase to protect her clothes.|她往她的手提箱里加了一个衬里来保护她的衣服。|近义词：lining, inner lining|反义词：outer layer
