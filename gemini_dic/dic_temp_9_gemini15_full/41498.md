由于“cornuto”是意大利语单词，并非英语单词，因此无法按照英语单词的格式进行解析。  以下提供意大利语单词“cornuto”的解析：


/korˈnuːto/ | n. m.|  cuckold,  戴绿帽的人 | A man whose wife is unfaithful.  A cuckold is a husband whose wife is having an affair. The term often carries a strong connotation of shame and ridicule. |  Example:  Lui è un cornuto. (He is a cuckold.)  | 近义词: tradito (betrayed), ingannato (deceived) | 反义词: fedele (faithful)


USAGE->

(由于“cornuto”是名词，没有动词的时态变化。以下列举一些例句，体现不同语境下的用法)

疑问句:

词源:

The word "cornuto" derives from the Italian word "corno," meaning "horn."  The metaphorical association stems from the image of a bull with horns, symbolizing virility and cuckoldry, as the horns are seen as a symbol of the wife's infidelity.

需要注意的是，"cornuto" 在意大利语中是一个带有强烈负面含义的词，使用时需谨慎。
