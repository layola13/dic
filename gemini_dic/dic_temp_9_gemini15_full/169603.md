prophylaxis:/ˌproʊfɪˈlæksɪs/| n.| 预防措施；预防性治疗 |The act of preventing disease; a preventative measure.  Prophylaxis aims to prevent disease from occurring in the first place, rather than treating it after it has developed. | Example:  The doctor recommended a course of antibiotics as a prophylaxis against infection. 医生建议服用抗生素以预防感染。

| Example:  Regular dental checkups are a form of prophylaxis against gum disease. 定期进行牙齿检查是预防牙龈疾病的一种预防措施。|近义词：prevention, precaution, safeguard |反义词：treatment, cure |名词
