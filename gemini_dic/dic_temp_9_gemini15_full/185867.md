revocation:/ˌrevəˈkeɪʃən/| n. |撤销；废除；取消 |The act of revoking something, such as a law, license, or privilege.  It implies the formal or official annulment of something previously granted or enacted.  |例句：The revocation of his driver's license followed his third DUI.  他的驾照因第三次酒驾而被吊销。

例句：The company announced the revocation of its earlier statement. 公司宣布撤回之前的声明。


近义词：cancellation, annulment, repeal, rescission

反义词：granting, confirmation, ratification

词性:名词
