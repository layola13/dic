magentas:/məˈdʒɛntəz/| n. |紫红色；洋红；品红色|A shade of purplish-red.  The word refers to a color, often used in printing and painting.  It's a vibrant, deep color.  |例句：The artist used magentas and blues to create a striking contrast.  艺术家用紫红色和蓝色创造出鲜明的对比。

The walls were painted a deep magenta. 墙壁漆成深紫红色。


近义词：crimson, purplish-red, fuchsia, rose

反义词：pale, light

词源:  The word "magenta" derives its name from the Battle of Magenta, fought in 1859 during the Second Italian War of Independence.  A new aniline dye, a purplish-red color, was discovered around that time and was named after the battle.
