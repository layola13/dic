prefacing:/ˈprɛfəsɪŋ/| v.|作序；作为…的开端；|to introduce (a book, speech, etc.) with a preface or introductory remarks.  To serve as a preface or introduction to something.|例句：He prefaced his remarks with a joke. 他以一个笑话开头他的讲话。

例句：The author prefaced the novel with a dedication to her family. 作者在小说开头献给她的家人。


近义词：introducing, introducing, beginning, commencing


反义词：concluding, ending


词性:动词
