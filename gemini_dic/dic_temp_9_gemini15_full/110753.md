lingoa:/ˈlɪŋɡwə/| n. |语言；方言|A particular language or dialect.|例句：Latin was once the lingua franca of Europe. 拉丁语曾经是欧洲的通用语。|近义词：language, dialect, tongue|反义词：silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The lingua franca of this region is English. = 这个地区的通用语是英语。
This dialect is a distinct lingua. = 这种方言是一种独特的语言。

一般过去时:
Latin was the lingua franca of the Roman Empire. = 拉丁语是罗马帝国的通用语。
Swahili served as a lingua franca in East Africa. = 斯瓦希里语在东非充当通用语。

现在完成时:
English has become the global lingua franca. = 英语已成为全球通用语。
Many lingua francas have developed throughout history. = 历史上发展出了许多种通用语。

过去完成时:
Before the rise of English, French had been a lingua franca in some areas. = 在英语兴起之前，法语在某些地区曾是通用语。
The traders had used a local lingua franca for centuries. = 商人们几个世纪以来一直使用一种当地的通用语。

将来时:
Mandarin might become a significant lingua franca in the future. = 普通话将来可能会成为一种重要的通用语。
Will a new lingua franca emerge in the coming decades? = 未来几十年会涌现新的通用语吗？


虚拟语气:
If Spanish were the lingua franca, communication would be easier. = 如果西班牙语是通用语，交流会更容易。
I wish there were a universal lingua franca for all nations. = 我希望所有国家都有一种通用的语言。

被动语态:
A new lingua franca is being developed for international trade. = 正在为国际贸易开发一种新的通用语。
Esperanto was designed to be a universal lingua franca, but it wasn't widely adopted. = 世界语的设计目的是成为一种通用的语言，但它并没有被广泛采用。

疑问句:
What is the lingua franca of this international conference? = 这个国际会议的通用语是什么？
Is there a widely accepted lingua franca in this industry? = 这个行业是否有广泛接受的通用语？

否定句:
There is no single lingua franca for all of humanity. = 人类没有单一的通用语。
This region doesn't have a well-established lingua franca. = 这个地区没有完善的通用语。
