ground-rents:/ˈɡraʊndˌrents/| n.|地租|Ground rents are the payments made by a leaseholder to a freeholder for the right to occupy land.|The building's ground rent is quite high, adding significantly to the monthly cost.|近义词：land rent, leasehold rent|反义词：freehold (ownership)|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The tenant pays ground rent annually. = 租户每年支付地租。
Ground rent is included in the total cost. = 地租包含在总成本中。

一般过去时:
They paid the ground rent on time last year. = 他们去年按时支付了地租。
The ground rent was significantly lower in the past. = 过去地租要低得多。

现在完成时:
We have paid all the ground rent due this year. = 我们已经支付了今年到期的所有地租。
The landlord has increased the ground rent several times. = 房东已经几次提高了地租。

过去完成时:
They had already paid the ground rent before the deadline. = 他们早在截止日期之前就已支付了地租。
The tenant had neglected to pay the ground rent for several months. = 租户已经好几个月没有支付地租了。

将来时:
The ground rent will increase next year. = 明年地租将会上涨。
We will pay the ground rent electronically from now on. = 从现在开始我们将通过电子方式支付地租。

虚拟语气:
If the ground rent were lower, we would consider buying the property. = 如果地租更低，我们会考虑购买该房产。
I wish the ground rent was more affordable. = 我希望地租更便宜一些。

被动语态:
