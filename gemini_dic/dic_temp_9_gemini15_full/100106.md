interpret:/ɪnˈtɜːrprɪt/ | /ɪnˈtɜːrprɛt/| vt.  |解释；理解；翻译 |To explain the meaning of something; to translate something from one language into another; to understand something in a particular way. |例句：He interpreted the poem beautifully. 他对这首诗做了精彩的诠释。|例句：Could you interpret this passage for me? 你能帮我翻译一下这段话吗？|近义词：explain, translate, decipher, construe |反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She interprets the data carefully. = 她仔细地解读数据。
He interprets the law differently. = 他对法律有不同的解读。
