hillsides:/ˈhɪlˌsaɪdz/| n.|山坡；山腰|The sides of hills;sloping land forming the sides of a hill or valley.|例句：Wildflowers bloomed profusely on the hillsides. 山坡上鲜花盛开。|例句：The sheep grazed peacefully on the green hillsides.羊群在绿色的山坡上平静地吃草。|近义词：slopes, declivities|反义词：summit, peak
