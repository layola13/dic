intermaxillary: /ˌɪntərˈmæksɪlɛri/ | adj. | 上颌间的；位于上颌骨之间的 | Relating to the area between the maxillae (upper jaw bones).  The term is used primarily in anatomy and dentistry. |  The intermaxillary suture is where the two maxillae join. 上颌间缝是两块上颌骨连接的地方。  |The intermaxillary space is sometimes used for dental implants. 上颌间隙有时用于种植牙。 |  supramaxillary, premaxillary | inframaxillary


USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations or tense changes like "What".  Therefore, verb tenses are not applicable.  It's used to describe location or relationship.
