preassumed:/ˌpriːəˈsjuːmd/| adj.| 预先假定的；预先假设的 | Already assumed or supposed before there is evidence; taken for granted.  |例句：The preassumed guilt of the suspect hampered the investigation.  嫌疑人的预先假定的罪责妨碍了调查。

例句：Based on the preassumed conditions, the experiment yielded unexpected results. 基于预先假定的条件，实验产生了意想不到的结果。


近义词：presumed, supposed, assumed, taken for granted

反义词：unassumed, questioned, doubted

词性：形容词
