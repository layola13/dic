disadvantagednesses:/ˌdɪsædˈvæntɪdʒɪznəsiz/ | n. |不利因素；劣势的众多方面 |The plural form of "disadvantagedness," referring to the multiple aspects or instances of disadvantage.  It describes the various ways in which someone or something might be at a disadvantage.  The word is relatively uncommon and often replaced by simpler phrasing. |The report highlighted the multiple disadvantagednesses faced by rural communities, including lack of access to healthcare, education, and employment.  这份报告强调了农村社区面临的多种不利因素，包括缺乏医疗保健、教育和就业机会。| disadvantages, drawbacks, impediments | advantages, benefits, strengths | 名词
