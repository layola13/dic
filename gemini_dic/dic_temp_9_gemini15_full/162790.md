prebreathed:/ˌpriːˈbrɛðt/| adj.|预先呼吸的，事先吸入的|Describes something that has been breathed in beforehand, often in a specific context like a pre-breath before a vocal performance or a deep breath before an action.|例句：The singer took a prebreathed to steady her nerves before the performance.  = 歌手在演出前深吸一口气来稳定情绪。|近义词：pre-inhaled, anticipatory breath|反义词：exhaled, post-breath
