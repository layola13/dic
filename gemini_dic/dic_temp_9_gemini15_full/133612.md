nonfluids:/ˈnɒnˌfluːɪdz/| n.|非流体，非液体|Substances that do not flow easily or at all.  They lack the characteristic properties of fluids, such as liquids and gases, which readily deform and change shape under applied stress.|Example:  Solids are examples of nonfluids.  固体是非流体的例子。|近义词：solids, nonliquids|反义词：fluids, liquids, gases
