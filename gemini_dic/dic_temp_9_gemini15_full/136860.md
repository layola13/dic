nonskaters:/ˈnɑːnˌskeɪtərz/ | n. |非滑冰者|People who do not skate.  This term refers to individuals who do not participate in the activity of ice skating, roller skating, skateboarding, or other forms of skating.  It's a descriptive term, not necessarily carrying a negative connotation. |Example: The park was divided into areas for skaters and nonskaters. 公园被划分为滑冰者和非滑冰者区域。|近义词:non-skaters, people who don't skate |反义词:skaters
