etches:/ˈɛtʃɪz/| v.|蚀刻；描绘；勾勒|To produce (a design or picture) on a surface by etching.  To make a line or lines on a surface by scratching or carving.|例句：The artist etches delicate lines into the copper plate.  艺术家在铜板上蚀刻出精细的线条。

The craftsman etched a beautiful pattern onto the glass. 工匠在玻璃上蚀刻了一个美丽的图案。


近义词：carve, engrave, inscribe, delineate, sketch|反义词：smudge, blur
