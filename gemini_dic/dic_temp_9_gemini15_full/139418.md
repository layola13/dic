obsesses:/əbˈsɛs/ | vt. |使痴迷；使困扰 |To preoccupy or fill the mind of (someone) to the exclusion of other thoughts and feelings.  It implies an excessive or unhealthy preoccupation. |例句：He obsesses over his weight. 他对自己的体重念念不忘。|例句：She's obsessed with finding the perfect dress. 她一心想找到一件完美的礼服。|近义词：haunt, preoccupy, fixate on |反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He obsesses over video games. = 他沉迷于电子游戏。
She obsesses about her appearance. = 她对自己的外表过于在意。

一般过去时:
He obsessed over his work last year. = 去年他沉迷于工作。
She obsessed about her health problems. = 她一直担心她的健康问题。

现在完成时:
He has obsessed over this problem for months. = 他已经为此问题苦恼了几个月。
She has obsessed over her diet since she was a teenager. = 自从十几岁起，她就一直对自己的饮食很在意。

过去完成时:
He had obsessed over the details before submitting the report. = 他在提交报告之前就一直纠结于细节。
She had obsessed over the exam results for days. = 她已经为考试结果苦恼了好几天了。

将来时:
He will obsess over his new project. = 他将会沉迷于他的新项目。
She will probably obsess over the wedding planning. = 她可能会沉迷于婚礼的筹划。

虚拟语气:
If he weren't so successful, he wouldn't obsess over his career so much. = 如果他不那么成功，他就不会如此沉迷于事业。
I wish she wouldn't obsess over every little thing. = 我希望她不要对每一件小事都过于在意。

被动语态:
He was obsessed by his fear of failure. = 他被对失败的恐惧所困扰。
She was obsessed with the idea of revenge. = 她被复仇的想法所困扰。

疑问句:
Does he obsess over anything in particular? = 他有什么特别痴迷的东西吗？
What does she obsess about these days? = 她最近在纠结什么？

否定句:
He doesn't obsess over his work anymore. = 他不再沉迷于工作了。
She doesn't obsess about her weight as much as she used to. = 她不像以前那样在意体重了。


PHRASE->
obsess over something = 对某事过于执着
obsess about something = 对某事过于担忧
be obsessed with something = 对某事痴迷
become obsessed with someone = 迷恋上某人
obsessed fan = 狂热粉丝
