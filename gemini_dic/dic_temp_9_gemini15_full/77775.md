general-purpose: /ˌdʒɛnərəlˈpɜːrpəs/ | adj. | 通用型；多用途的；通用的 |  Having many uses or applications; not specialized.  This adjective describes something that can be used for a variety of purposes, rather than being designed for a single, specific task. |

例句：

This tool is general-purpose and can be used for a variety of tasks.  这个工具是通用的，可以用于各种任务。

The software has a general-purpose design, making it suitable for various industries. 这款软件具有通用型设计，使其适用于各个行业。


近义词：versatile, multipurpose, all-purpose, universal


反义词：specialized, specific, single-purpose


词源:

* **general:**  From Old French *general*, from Latin *generalis*, from *genus* "kind, race, birth."  Meaning "common, widespread."

* **purpose:** From Old French *pourpos*, from Latin *propositum*, past participle of *proponere* "to put forward, propose," from *pro-* "forward" + *ponere* "to put."


USAGE->

(Note:  Since "general-purpose" is an adjective, it doesn't have verb conjugations like the example word "what".)  It modifies nouns.


Examples modifying different noun types:

General-purpose language: 通用语言。 Python is a general-purpose programming language. Python 是一种通用编程语言。

General-purpose robot: 通用机器人。 A general-purpose robot can perform a variety of tasks. 通用机器人可以执行各种任务。

General-purpose adhesive: 通用粘合剂。 This general-purpose adhesive bonds to most surfaces.这种通用粘合剂可以粘合大多数表面。
