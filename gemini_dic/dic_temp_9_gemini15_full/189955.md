sanctimoniousnesses:/ˌsæŋktɪˈmoʊniəsnəsɪz/| n. |伪善行为；假装虔诚的行为 |The plural form of "sanctimoniousness," referring to multiple instances or types of affected piety or moral superiority.  It describes the display of excessive or hypocritical piety.  |Example: His sanctimoniousnesses were evident in his constant pronouncements of virtue while secretly engaging in immoral activities.  他的伪善行为在他不断宣扬美德的同时秘密从事不道德活动中显而易见。|近义词：hypocrisies, affectations, pieties (when hypocritical) |反义词：genuineness, sincerity, authenticity |名词
