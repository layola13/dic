right-on:/ˌraɪtˈɒn/| adj.|完全正确的；极好的；酷的；赞的|Completely correct; excellent; trendy and fashionable; expressing approval or enthusiasm.|例句：That's right-on!  = 那是完全正确的！ That's a right-on idea. = 这是一个很棒的主意。|近义词：excellent, great, cool, fantastic, awesome|反义词：wrong, awful, terrible, bad

USAGE->
This word is primarily used as an adjective, so verb conjugations do not apply.  The usage is informal and largely dependent on context.

例句1: His assessment of the situation was right-on.  = 他对局势的评估完全正确。
例句2:  That band's music is totally right-on. = 那个乐队的音乐太酷了。
