goldbricking: /ˈɡoʊldˌbrɪkɪŋ/ | n. |偷懒；磨洋工；欺骗；以次充好 |  The act of pretending to work while avoiding actual work; shirking one's duties; deceiving someone. It can also refer to selling something inferior as if it were superior. |例句：He was caught goldbricking on the job and was subsequently fired. 他在工作中被发现偷懒，随后被解雇了。

例句：The antique dealer was accused of goldbricking, selling fake antiques as genuine items.  那个古董商被指控以次充好，将假古董当真品出售。

近义词：shirking, malingering, loafing, scamming, defrauding |反义词：diligence, hard work, honesty
