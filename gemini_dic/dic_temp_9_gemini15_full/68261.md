faiths:/feɪθs/| n. |信仰；信念；信条|The word "faiths" is the plural form of "faith," referring to different systems of religious belief or trust in something.  It encompasses a wide range of spiritual and philosophical perspectives.|例句：Many faiths coexist peacefully in this diverse city.  这个多元化的城市里，许多信仰和平共处。

例句：The world's major faiths include Christianity, Islam, and Buddhism. 世界主要宗教包括基督教、伊斯兰教和佛教。|近义词：beliefs, creeds, religions |反义词：disbelief, doubt, skepticism |词性:名词
