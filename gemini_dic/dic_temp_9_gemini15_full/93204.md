ignored:/ɪɡˈnɔːrd/ | adj. | 被忽略的，未被注意到的 |  Something ignored is not given attention or consideration.  It is overlooked or disregarded. | Example:  My suggestion was ignored. 我的建议被忽略了。  The problem has been ignored for too long.  这个问题被忽视太久了。 |近义词：overlooked, neglected, disregarded, dismissed | 反义词：heeded, noticed, attended to |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He ignores my calls. 他不接我的电话。
They ignore the warnings. 他们无视警告。

一般过去时:
She ignored his advice. 她忽略了他的建议。
I ignored the traffic light. 我无视红绿灯。

现在完成时:
They have ignored the evidence. 他们一直无视证据。
We have ignored the problem for too long. 我们忽视这个问题太久了。


过去完成时:
He had ignored all the signs. 他之前忽视了所有迹象。
She had ignored her doctor's advice. 她之前忽略了医生的建议。


将来时:
They will ignore the consequences. 他们将会无视后果。
He will ignore my pleas. 他将无视我的恳求。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I wouldn't have ignored it. 如果我知道的话，我就不会忽略它了。
I wish I hadn't ignored his warning. 我真希望我没有忽视他的警告。


被动语态:
My concerns were ignored. 我的担忧被忽略了。
His warnings were ignored. 他的警告被忽略了。


疑问句:
Was my request ignored? 我的请求被忽略了吗？
Why were his complaints ignored? 为什么他的抱怨被忽略了？


否定句:
I didn't ignore his advice. 我没有忽视他的建议。
She didn't ignore the danger signs. 她没有忽视危险信号。



PHRASE->
be ignored 被忽略

safely ignored 可以忽略
deliberately ignored 故意忽略
generally ignored 通常被忽略
