schoolmates:/ˈskulˌmeɪts/| n. | 同学，校友 | People who attend the same school.  Schoolmates often share similar experiences and may form lasting friendships. | Example: I haven't seen my old schoolmates in years.  多年来我都没见过我的老同学们了。|近义词： classmates, school friends | 反义词： N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
My schoolmates are studying hard for the upcoming exams. = 我的同学们正在努力学习即将到来的考试。
He often plays basketball with his schoolmates after school. = 他放学后经常和同学们一起打篮球。


一般过去时:
My schoolmates and I went on a field trip last week. = 我和我的同学们上周去了一次实地考察。
We celebrated our graduation with our schoolmates. = 我们和同学们一起庆祝了毕业。


现在完成时:
I have known many of my schoolmates since elementary school. = 我从小学就认识很多同学了。
She has made many good friends among her schoolmates. = 她和很多同学成为了好朋友。


过去完成时:
Before the party started, my schoolmates had already decorated the classroom. = 在派对开始之前，我的同学们已经装饰好了教室。
We had known each other for years before we became close friends in high school. = 在高中成为好朋友之前，我们已经认识好几年了。


将来时:
My schoolmates will attend the reunion next month. = 我的同学们下个月将参加聚会。
We will probably keep in touch with some of our schoolmates even after graduation. = 甚至毕业后，我们也可能会和一些同学保持联系。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would visit my old schoolmates more often. = 如果我有更多的时间，我会更经常去看望我的老同学们。
I wish I could still see my schoolmates every day. = 我希望我还能每天都看到我的同学们。


被动语态:
The awards were given to the best students among the schoolmates. = 奖项颁发给了同学们中最优秀的学生。
The school trip was planned by our schoolmates. = 学校郊游是由我们的同学们策划的。


疑问句:

否定句:
