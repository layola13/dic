prediscourses:/ˈpriːˌdɪsˌkɔːrsɪz/ | n. |预先论述；先前的论述；预先谈论 | Discussions or writings that precede and shape subsequent discourse.  Prediscourses set the stage for later conversations, influencing the terms of debate and the very possibility of certain arguments. They often operate implicitly, shaping assumptions and framing perspectives before explicit discussion begins.  | Example: The prediscourses surrounding climate change have significantly influenced public opinion and political action.  先前的关于气候变化的论述极大地影响了公众舆论和政治行动。 | 近义词：preliminary discussions, prior discourse, foundational arguments | 反义词：post-discourse, subsequent discussion | 名词
