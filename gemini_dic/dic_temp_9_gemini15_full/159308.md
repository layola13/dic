pointillism:/ˌpɔɪnˈtɪlɪzəm/| n. |点彩画法 |A painting technique in which small, distinct dots of color are applied in patterns to form an image.  The overall impression of color and form is created by the viewer's eye mixing the colors from a distance. |The artist's use of pointillism created a vibrant and textured surface.  这位艺术家的点彩画法创造了一个充满活力和纹理的表面。|Impressionism, Divisionism |(no direct antonym)|名词
