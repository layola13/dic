quadriga:/ˈkwɒdrɪɡə/ | n. |四轮马车；四驾马拉的战车 | A quadriga is a type of ancient Roman chariot pulled by four horses abreast.  They were often used in circuses and parades, and sometimes in warfare.  | Example: The victorious general rode through the streets in a magnificent quadriga.  胜利将军乘坐华丽的四轮马车穿过街道。|近义词:chariot (but with the specific detail of four horses)|反义词:None.
