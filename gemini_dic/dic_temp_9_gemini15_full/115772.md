mariner:/ˈmærɪnər/| n. |水手，海员；海军陆战队队员 |A mariner is a sailor or seaman.  The term can also refer to a member of the Marines. |例句：The old mariner spun tales of his adventures at sea.  这位老水手讲述了他海上冒险的故事。|例句：He served as a mariner in the Royal Navy. 他在皇家海军服役，是一名水手。|近义词：sailor, seaman, seafarer |反义词：landlubber
