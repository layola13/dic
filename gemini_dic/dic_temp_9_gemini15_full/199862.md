situated:/ˈsɪtʃueɪtɪd/ | adj. |位于…的；处于…境地的 |  situated means being in a particular place or position. It describes the location or condition of something. |例句：The house is situated on a hill overlooking the sea.  这所房子位于俯瞰大海的山丘上。|例句：She is situated in a difficult position at work. 她在工作中处于困境。|近义词：located, placed, positioned |反义词：unlocated, misplaced


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The school is situated near the park. = 学校位于公园附近。
The office is situated on the tenth floor. = 办公室位于十楼。

一般过去时:
The hotel was situated by the river. = 旅馆位于河边。
The cottage was situated in a quiet village. = 小屋坐落在一个安静的村庄里。

现在完成时:
The factory has been situated here for fifty years. = 这个工厂在这里建厂五十年了。
The monument has been situated in the city center for centuries. = 这座纪念碑在市中心屹立了几个世纪。

过去完成时:
The old church had been situated on that hill before the town was built. = 在小镇建立之前，那座古老的教堂就位于那座山上了。
The building had been situated closer to the river, but it was moved. = 这座建筑以前更靠近河边，但后来被搬走了。

将来时:
The new hospital will be situated outside the city. = 新医院将建在城外。
The statue will be situated in the town square. = 雕像将被安置在城镇广场。

虚拟语气:
If the house were situated in a better location, it would be worth more. = 如果这房子位于更好的位置，它会更值钱。
If the company were situated closer to the market, it would have better access to customers. = 如果公司更靠近市场，它就能更好地接触到客户。

被动语态:
The building was situated perfectly for its purpose. = 这座建筑的位置非常适合其用途。
The conference was situated in a beautiful hotel. = 会议在一个美丽的酒店举行。

疑问句:
Where is the hotel situated? = 酒店位于哪里？
Where is the statue situated? = 雕像位于哪里？

否定句:
The campsite isn't situated near the beach. = 营地不在海滩附近。
The library isn't situated in the city centre. = 图书馆不在市中心。
