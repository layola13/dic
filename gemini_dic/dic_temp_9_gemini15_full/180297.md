refrigerates:/rɪˈfrɪdʒəreɪts/ | vt. | 冷藏；使冷冻 | To make or keep something cold, especially food, using a refrigerator. | The restaurant refrigerates all its ingredients to maintain freshness. 这家餐馆冷藏所有食材以保持新鲜。 |近义词：chills, cools, freezes |反义词：heats, warms


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refrigerates the milk every day. = 他每天都把牛奶冷藏起来。
The shop refrigerates its produce to prevent spoilage. = 商店冷藏其农产品以防止腐坏。


一般过去时:
She refrigerated the leftovers after dinner. = 晚餐后她把剩菜冷藏起来了。
They refrigerated the cake to keep it fresh for the party. = 他们把蛋糕冷藏起来，以保持其在派对上的新鲜。


现在完成时:
We have refrigerated the ice cream since this morning. = 我们从今天早上就把冰淇淋冷藏起来了。
He has refrigerated the medicine according to the instructions. = 他按照说明冷藏了药物。


过去完成时:
She had refrigerated the soup before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，她已经把汤冷藏起来了。
They had refrigerated the meat for several hours before grilling it. = 在烤肉之前，他们已经冷藏了肉好几个小时。


将来时:
I will refrigerate the vegetables after I wash them. = 我洗完蔬菜后会把它们冷藏起来。
They will refrigerate the drinks for the picnic tomorrow. = 他们明天会为野餐冷藏饮料。


虚拟语气:
If I had a refrigerator, I would refrigerate the leftovers. = 如果我有冰箱，我会把剩菜冷藏起来。
I wish I had refrigerated the fish earlier. = 我希望我早点把鱼冷藏起来。


被动语态:
The food is refrigerated to maintain its quality. = 食物被冷藏以保持其质量。
The medicine must be refrigerated to remain effective. = 药物必须冷藏才能保持有效性。


疑问句:
Do you refrigerate your milk? = 你会把牛奶冷藏起来吗？
Does the restaurant refrigerate its seafood? = 这家餐馆会冷藏海鲜吗？


否定句:
They don't refrigerate their food. = 他们不冷藏食物。
She didn't refrigerate the juice before leaving for work. = 她上班前没有把果汁冷藏起来。
