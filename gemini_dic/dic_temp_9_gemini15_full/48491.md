deepens:/ˈdiːpənz/| v.|加深；变深；使加剧|To become or make something become deeper or more intense.|The mystery deepens.  这个谜团越来越深了。|The river deepens as it approaches the sea. 河水靠近大海时越来越深。|intensifies, aggravates|shallow, lightens|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The conflict deepens every day. = 冲突每天都在加剧。
His understanding of the subject deepens with each lesson. = 他对这个学科的理解随着每一节课而加深。


一般过去时:
The wound deepened overnight. = 伤口一夜之间加深了。
Her sadness deepened after she received the news. = 她收到消息后悲伤加剧了。


现在完成时:
The relationship has deepened significantly over the years. = 多年来，他们的关系已经有了显著的加深。
My appreciation for art has deepened since I visited the museum. = 自从参观了博物馆以来，我对艺术的欣赏更加深入了。


过去完成时:
By the time she arrived, the snow had deepened considerably. = 她到达时，雪已经积得很厚了。
The chasm between them had deepened irreparably before the reconciliation attempt. = 在试图和解之前，他们之间的裂痕已经无法弥补地加深了。


将来时:
The crisis will deepen if we don't take action. = 如果我们不采取行动，危机将会加剧。
The color of the dye will deepen as it dries. = 染料的颜色在干燥时会加深。


虚拟语气:
If he had apologized, the rift wouldn't have deepened. = 如果他道歉了，裂痕就不会加深了。
I wish the misunderstanding hadn't deepened to this point. = 我希望误解不会加深到这个地步。


被动语态:
The mystery was deepened by the new evidence. = 新证据使这个谜团更加扑朔迷离。
The hole was deepened by the continuous excavation. = 由于持续的挖掘，洞穴被挖深了。


疑问句:
Does the color deepen over time? = 颜色会随着时间推移而加深吗？
Does this experience deepen your understanding? =  这次经历是否加深了你的理解？


否定句:
The relationship didn't deepen as quickly as they had hoped. = 他们的关系并没有像他们希望的那样迅速加深。
The river doesn't deepen in this section. = 河流在这个区域没有变深。
