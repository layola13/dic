inquisitions:/ˌɪŋkwɪˈzɪʃənz/ | n. |宗教法庭；严厉盘问；调查 | Inquiries made, especially in a harsh or aggressive way.  The act or process of inquiring into something.  |例句：The Spanish Inquisition was infamous for its brutal methods. 西班牙宗教法庭因其残酷的手段而臭名昭著。

The inquisition into the company's accounting practices revealed widespread fraud. 对该公司会计行为的调查揭露了广泛的欺诈行为。|近义词：investigations, inquiries, interrogations, examinations |反义词：acceptance, toleration
