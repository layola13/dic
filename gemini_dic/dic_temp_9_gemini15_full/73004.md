football:/ˈfʊtbɔːl/|/ˈfʊtˌbɔːl/| n. |足球；橄榄球|A game played by two teams, typically of eleven players, who try to score by kicking or carrying a ball into the other team's goal.  In American English, it usually refers to American football; in British English, it usually refers to association football (soccer).|例句：He loves playing football. 他喜欢踢足球。

例句：The football game was exciting. 足球比赛很精彩。


近义词：soccer (BrE), gridiron football (AmE), association football (BrE)


反义词：无


词性:名词
