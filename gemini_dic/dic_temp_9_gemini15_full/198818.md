sideboard:/ˈsaɪdbɔːrd/ | n. | 餐具柜；侧板 | A sideboard is a piece of furniture, typically placed against a wall in a dining room, used for storing dishes, silverware, and other dining essentials.  It often has drawers and cupboards. | Example: We keep our best china in the sideboard. 我们把最好的瓷器放在餐具柜里。 |近义词：buffet, credenza | 反义词:None
