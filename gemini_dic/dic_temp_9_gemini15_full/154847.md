pervades:/pərˈveɪdz/ | v. | 弥漫；渗透；遍布 | To spread throughout and be perceived in every part of.  The scent of lavender pervades the entire house.  |  The feeling of sadness pervaded the room after the news was announced. |近义词：penetrate, permeate, imbue, saturate | 反义词：exclude, leave out


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The aroma of coffee pervades the kitchen. = 咖啡的香味弥漫在厨房里。
Fear pervades the hearts of the villagers. = 恐惧弥漫在村民的心中。

一般过去时:
A sense of unease pervaded the atmosphere before the storm. = 暴风雨来临前，不安的情绪弥漫在空气中。
Silence pervaded the empty classroom. = 空荡荡的教室里一片寂静。

现在完成时:
Disappointment has pervaded her since she failed the exam. = 自从考试不及格后，失望的情绪一直萦绕着她。
A feeling of despair has pervaded the nation after the tragedy. = 悲剧发生后，绝望的情绪笼罩着整个国家。

过去完成时:
A sense of dread had pervaded the city before the earthquake struck. = 地震发生前，恐惧感已弥漫整个城市。
Doubt had pervaded her mind about his honesty. = 她对他的诚实产生了怀疑。

将来时:
The smell of freshly baked bread will pervade the house soon. = 过一会儿，新鲜烘焙的面包香味将会弥漫整个房子。
Anxiety will pervade the market if the economic situation worsens. = 如果经济形势恶化，焦虑情绪将弥漫市场。

虚拟语气:
If only happiness could pervade the world. = 但愿快乐能够遍布世界。
I wish peace would pervade the troubled region. = 我希望和平能够弥漫在动荡不安的地区。

被动语态:
The air was pervaded by the sweet scent of flowers. = 空气中弥漫着鲜花的甜香。
The town was pervaded by a sense of quiet anticipation. = 小镇笼罩着一种平静的期待感。

疑问句:
Does the smell of smoke pervade the building? = 烟味是否弥漫在整栋楼里？
Does a sense of foreboding pervade your feelings? = 你是否感到一种不祥的预感？

否定句:
The joy did not pervade the entire family. = 这份快乐并没有感染到全家。
The positive energy didn't pervade the team meeting. = 积极的能量并没有弥漫在团队会议中。


PHRASE->
pervade the atmosphere = 弥漫在空气中
pervade the culture = 渗透文化
pervade society = 遍布社会
pervade every aspect = 渗透到各个方面
pervade the thoughts = 充满思想


词源:
From Latin *pervadere, from per- "through" + vadere "to go."
