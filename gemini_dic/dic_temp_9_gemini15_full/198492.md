shrimplet:/ˈʃrɪmplɪt/| n. |小虾，虾米|A small shrimp.  The word is a diminutive of "shrimp," formed by adding the suffix "-let," indicating smallness.  It is often used to refer to very young or small shrimps, especially those used in cooking.|例句：The soup contained numerous shrimplets. 这汤里有很多虾米。

The fisherman caught a bucket full of shrimplets.  渔夫捕了一桶小虾。|近义词：small shrimp, tiny shrimp|反义词：large shrimp, prawn
