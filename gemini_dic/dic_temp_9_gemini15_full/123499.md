monotheisms:/ˈmɒnəθiːɪzəm/| n. |一神教；一神论| The belief in and worship of only one god.  Monotheism is a major religious tradition found in many cultures around the world. |例句：Christianity, Islam, and Judaism are examples of monotheisms. 基督教、伊斯兰教和犹太教是一神教的例子。

例句：Many monotheistic religions emphasize a personal relationship with God. 许多一神教都强调与上帝的个人关系。


近义词：unitarianism (较狭义，特指一种特定的一神教派)

反义词：polytheism (多神教)


词源：源自希腊语"monos" (单一的) + "theos" (神) + "-ism" (表示…主义，…论)
