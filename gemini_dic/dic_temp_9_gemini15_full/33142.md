Christs:/kraɪsts/| n.|基督们，基督教徒们|The word "Christs" is the plural form of "Christ," referring to Jesus Christ or, more broadly, to followers of Christianity.|例句：Many different sects of Christians believe in Jesus Christ.  = 许多不同的基督教派都信仰耶稣基督。

例句：The early Christians faced persecution for their beliefs. = 早期的基督教徒因他们的信仰而遭受迫害。


近义词：Christians (基督徒), believers (信徒)


反义词：无


词性：名词
