saururae:/sɔːrʊˈreɪ/ | n. | 蜥蜴类 |  A group of extinct, mostly large, lizard-like reptiles from the Permian and Triassic periods.  They are characterized by their sprawling posture and often large size.  |  The saururae were a diverse group of reptiles, some of which were carnivorous and others herbivorous. |  archosaurs, diapsids |  mammals (in terms of evolutionary distance) | 名词
