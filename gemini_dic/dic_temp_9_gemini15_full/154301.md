peritoneum:/ˌpɛrɪtəˈniːəm/| n.|腹膜|A thin serous membrane that lines the abdominal cavity and covers most of the abdominal organs.  It plays a vital role in protecting and supporting the abdominal viscera.|例句：The surgeon carefully inspected the peritoneum for any signs of inflammation. 外科医生仔细检查了腹膜是否有任何炎症迹象。|例句：The peritoneum was damaged during the surgery, requiring repair. 手术中腹膜受损，需要修复。|近义词：mesentery (部分腹膜)|反义词：无
