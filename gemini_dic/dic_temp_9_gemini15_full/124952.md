muffled:/ˈmʌf(ə)ld/| adj.| 闷闷的；压抑的；模糊不清的|  Something that is muffled is quiet or unclear because it is covered or blocked. It can refer to sounds, voices, or even feelings. |例句：The music was muffled by the thick walls. 厚厚的墙壁使音乐听起来闷闷的。

例句：Her voice was muffled by the scarf around her mouth.  她被围巾捂住嘴，声音听起来模糊不清。


近义词：dampened, subdued, muted, indistinct

反义词：clear, distinct, loud, resonant

词性:形容词
