fishwife:/ˈfɪʃwaɪf/| n.|鱼贩妇人；泼妇|A woman who sells fish;  a loud, scolding, or shrewish woman.  The word often implies a harsh and unpleasant personality.|例句：The fishwife shouted at the customer for dropping a fish.  鱼贩妇人因为顾客掉了一条鱼而冲他大喊大叫。|例句：She behaved like a real fishwife, screaming and cursing at everyone.|她像个泼妇一样，冲着每个人又叫又骂。|近义词：hag, shrew, termagant|反义词：gentlewoman, lady
