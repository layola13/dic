por:/pɔːr/ | prep. | 为了；由于；凭借；关于 |  Indicates purpose, cause, means, or subject matter.  | He worked hard for his family. 他为了家人努力工作。 |  for, because of, owing to, regarding | against, despite |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He works for his family. = 他为他的家人工作。
She sings for her friends. = 她为她的朋友们唱歌。


一般过去时:
They worked for the company for ten years. = 他们为这家公司工作了十年。
He fought for his country. = 他为他的国家而战。


现在完成时:
I have worked for this company for five years. = 我为这家公司工作了五年。
She has prepared for the exam all week. = 她为考试准备了一周。


过去完成时:
He had worked for the company before he retired. = 他退休前为这家公司工作过。
She had studied for the test before she took it. = 她考试前已经复习过了。


将来时:
We will work for a better future. = 我们将为一个更美好的未来而努力。
They will prepare for the trip next week. = 他们下周将为旅行做准备。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would work for a better cause. = 如果我是你，我会为一个更好的事业而努力。
I wish I had worked for a bigger company. = 我希望我曾经为一家更大的公司工作过。


被动语态:
The work was done for the benefit of the community. = 这项工作是为社区的利益而做的。
The song was written for her late mother. = 这首歌是为她已故的母亲而写的。


疑问句:
What did you do that for? = 你为什么要那样做？
Who are you working for? = 你为谁工作？


否定句:
He didn't work for the money. = 他不是为了钱而工作。
She didn't study for the exam. = 她没有为考试而学习。


PHRASE->
for example = 例如
for instance = 例如
for now = 目前
for sure = 肯定地
for the sake of = 为了…的缘故
