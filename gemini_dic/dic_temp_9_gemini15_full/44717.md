cruelties:/ˈkruːəltiz/| n.|残酷行为；暴行|Cruelties are acts of extreme cruelty or wickedness.  The plural form of "cruelty."  It refers to multiple instances of inhumane or vicious actions.|例句：The historical records detail the cruelties inflicted upon the enslaved people. 历史记录详细描述了施加在被奴役人民身上的残酷行为。

例句：The war crimes tribunal investigated numerous acts of cruelties committed during the conflict. 战争罪法庭调查了冲突期间发生的许多暴行。


近义词：atrocities, barbarities, inhumanities, brutalities, enormities|反义词：kindnesses, compassions, mercies|名词
