natter:/ˈnætər/| v. n.|闲聊，唠叨|to talk casually and continuously, usually about unimportant things|例句：They nattered away happily for hours. 他们高兴地聊了好几个小时。|例句：The constant natter of the children was driving me mad. 孩子们没完没了的唠叨快把我逼疯了。|近义词：chatter, babble, gossip|反义词：silence, quiet


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They natter incessantly. = 他们没完没了地闲聊。
We natter about the latest news. = 我们在聊最新的新闻。

一般过去时:
They nattered about their holidays. = 他们聊着他们的假期。
We nattered for hours about nothing in particular. = 我们聊了好几个小时，什么也没聊。

现在完成时:
They have nattered away the whole afternoon. = 他们整个下午都在闲聊。
We have nattered about this topic many times. = 我们已经多次讨论过这个话题了。

过去完成时:
They had nattered for a while before I joined them. = 我加入他们之前，他们已经闲聊了一会儿了。
We had nattered about the project before the meeting. = 会议之前，我们已经讨论过这个项目了。

将来时:
They will natter away happily during the party. = 他们在聚会期间会快乐地闲聊。
We will natter about our plans for the weekend. = 我们会聊聊我们周末的计划。

虚拟语气:
If they nattered less, they would be more productive. = 如果他们少闲聊一些，他们的效率会更高。
I wish they would stop nattering so loudly. = 我希望他们能停止大声闲聊。

被动语态:
The topic was nattered about extensively. = 这个话题被广泛地讨论过。
The issue was nattered over for hours. = 这个问题被讨论了好几个小时。

疑问句:
What are they nattering about? = 他们在聊什么？
Were they nattering when you arrived? = 你到达的时候他们在闲聊吗？

否定句:
They didn't natter much at the meeting. = 他们在会议上没有怎么闲聊。
We didn't natter at all during the exam. = 考试期间我们一点也没闲聊。
