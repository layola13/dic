miscontinuance:/ˌmɪskənˈtɪnjuəns/| n. | 疏忽；中断；中止 |The failure to continue something; a lapse or interruption.  This is a relatively rare word, often found in legal contexts referring to the discontinuation of a legal process due to negligence or oversight. |Example: The miscontinuance of the case resulted in significant delays and additional costs.  该案的疏忽中断导致了严重的延误和额外费用。|近义词：discontinuance, interruption, lapse, omission |反义词：continuation, resumption
