exannulate:/ɪkˈsænjuleɪt/| v.|除去环状物；环状脱落|To remove a ring or ring-like structure, or to be removed in this way.  The term is primarily used in biological and botanical contexts, referring to the removal of rings or annuli from something.|例句：The biologist exannulated the segments of the earthworm to study their internal structure. 生物学家去除了蚯蚓的环状体来研究其内部结构。The petals exannulated from the flower, leaving a bare stem. 花瓣从花朵上脱落，留下光秃秃的茎。|近义词：remove, detach, shed|反义词：add, attach, grow
