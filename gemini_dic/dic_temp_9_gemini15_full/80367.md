goldilocks:/ˈɡoʊldɪˌlɑːks/| n. |金发姑娘| A fictional character in a fairy tale who seeks a comfortable balance between extremes.  She is known for trying three bowls of porridge, three chairs, and three beds before finding one that's "just right".|Example: The story of Goldilocks and the Three Bears is a classic children's tale.  金发姑娘和三只熊的故事是一个经典的儿童故事。|近义词：none|反义词：none
