dronepipe:/ˈdroʊnpaɪp/| n. |无人机管道；无人机输送管线|A dronepipe is a system of pipes used to transport goods or materials using drones.  It's a relatively new concept, combining drone technology with traditional pipeline infrastructure.  This allows for efficient and potentially automated delivery of items over long distances or difficult terrain.|Example: The company is developing a dronepipe system to deliver essential medical supplies to remote villages.|近义词：drone delivery system, automated delivery system|反义词：manual delivery system
