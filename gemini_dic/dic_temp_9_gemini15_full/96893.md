inexpensive:/ˌɪnɪkˈspɛnsɪv/| adj. |便宜的，价廉的| Inexpensive means not costing a lot of money; affordable.  It describes something that is relatively cheap compared to similar items. |That restaurant offers inexpensive and delicious food.|近义词：cheap, affordable, budget-friendly, economical|反义词：expensive, costly, pricey, dear
