denization:/ˌdɛnɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |归化；公民权授予；取得居住权 |The process of granting someone the rights and privileges of a citizen in a country they were not born in.  It involves becoming a denizen.  |Example: The denization of the immigrants was a lengthy process. 归化这些移民是一个漫长的过程。|近义词：naturalization, incorporation,  admission |反义词：expulsion, deportation, banishment |名词
