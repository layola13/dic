respirational:/ˌrɛspəˈreɪʃənəl/| adj.|呼吸的；与呼吸有关的| Relating to respiration or breathing.  The term encompasses the processes involved in taking in oxygen and expelling carbon dioxide.|例句：He experienced respiratory problems after the surgery. 手术后他出现了呼吸问题。|例句：The doctor ordered a respiratory test to assess lung function. 医生开具了呼吸测试以评估肺功能。|近义词：pulmonary, breathing|反义词：None specifically opposite, but could be considered opposite to circulatory in a broad sense.
