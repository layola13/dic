simplif:/ˈsɪmplɪfaɪ/| vt.|简化，使简易|To make something simpler or easier to understand or do.|例句：We need to simplify the instructions. 我们需要简化说明。|例句：The teacher simplified the complex problem for the students.老师为学生简化了复杂的问题。|近义词：simplify, ease, streamline|反义词：complicate, complexify
