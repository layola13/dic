double-checking: /ˈdʌbəlˌtʃɛkɪŋ/ | v. | 复查；再次检查 | The act of checking something a second time to ensure accuracy or completeness.  It involves verifying previously checked information or actions to eliminate errors. | 例句：I'm double-checking the figures before submitting the report. = 我在提交报告之前正在复查数据。

例句：She double-checked the address on the envelope before mailing the letter. = 她邮寄信件前再次核对了信封上的地址。


近义词：rechecking, verifying, reviewing, confirming | 反义词：neglecting, overlooking, ignoring | 词性：动词
