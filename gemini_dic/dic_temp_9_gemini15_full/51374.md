determinability: /dɪˌtɜːmɪnəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可确定性，可决定性 | The quality or state of being determinable; the ability to be determined or ascertained.  It refers to the possibility of knowing or finding out something with certainty.  | Example: The determinability of the outcome was questionable given the many unpredictable variables.  结果的可确定性由于许多不可预测的变量而令人怀疑。 |近义词: ascertainability, definability, predictability | 反义词: indeterminacy, uncertainty, randomness |  词性: 名词
