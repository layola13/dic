skit:/skɪt/ | n. |滑稽短剧；戏仿；玩笑 | A short, humorous play, often performed informally.  |例句：The students put on a skit for the school talent show. 学生们为学校才艺表演准备了一个滑稽短剧。
The comedian's skit satirized political figures.  这位喜剧演员的小品讽刺了政治人物。|近义词：sketch, act, parody |反义词：serious drama, lecture
