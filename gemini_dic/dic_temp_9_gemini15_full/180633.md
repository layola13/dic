regolith:/ˈrɛɡəˌlɪθ/| n. | 风化层；岩屑层 | The unconsolidated debris that covers bedrock, including dust, soil, broken rock, and other materials.  It's essentially the loose surface material of a planet or moon. | Example: The lunar regolith is composed of dust and fragmented rock. 月球风化层由尘土和碎石构成。  The Martian regolith presents challenges for robotic exploration.  火星风化层对机器人探测提出了挑战。| 近义词：mantle, overburden | 反义词：bedrock
