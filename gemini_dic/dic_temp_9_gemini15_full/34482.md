clayier:/ˈkleɪər/| adj.|更粘土的，更像粘土的 |More clay-like in texture or consistency.  It refers to something having a higher proportion of clay or exhibiting qualities more characteristic of clay, such as stickiness, plasticity, or the ability to retain moisture. |The clayier soil made it difficult to plant the seeds.  更粘的土壤使播种变得困难。 The clayier parts of the riverbank were constantly eroding. 河岸更粘土的部分一直在侵蚀。|claylike, clayey|sandy, gravelly|
