eradicable: /ɪˈrædɪkəbl/ | adj. | 可根除的，可消灭的 | Capable of being eradicated or completely destroyed.  | Example:  Polio is now largely eradicable thanks to vaccination programs.  脊髓灰质炎由于疫苗接种计划现在已基本可以根除。


The disease is eradicable with the right treatment.  这种疾病通过正确的治疗是可以根除的。|近义词：eliminable, removable, extinguishable |反义词：incurable, ineradicable

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The disease is eradicable. = 这种疾病是可以根除的。
This problem is eradicable with effort. = 这个问题只要努力是可以根除的。

一般过去时:
The problem was eradicable then. = 那个问题那时是可以根除的。
The disease was eradicable before the mutation. = 这种疾病在变异之前是可以根除的。

现在完成时:
The pest has been eradicable since the new pesticide. = 自从有了这种新的杀虫剂，这种害虫就能够被根除了。
This issue has been largely eradicable due to recent advancements. = 由于最近的进步，这个问题在很大程度上已经能够被根除了。

过去完成时:
The weeds had been eradicable before the heavy rain. = 在大雨之前，这些杂草是可以清除的。
The threat had been eradicable until the recent developments. = 直到最近的发展之前，这种威胁是可以消除的。

将来时:
This issue will be eradicable with new technology. = 这个问题将可以通过新技术得到根除。
The disease will be eradicable with further research. = 通过进一步的研究，这种疾病将能够被根除。

虚拟语气:
If the resources were available, the problem would be eradicable. = 如果有资源的话，这个问题是可以根除的。
If we had acted sooner, the problem would have been eradicable. = 如果我们早点采取行动，这个问题是可以根除的。

被动语态:

疑问句:
Is this problem eradicable? = 这个问题可以根除吗？

否定句:
The problem is not eradicable. = 这个问题是无法根除的。
The disease is not easily eradicable. = 这种疾病不容易根除。


PHRASE->
completely eradicable  完全可以根除的
almost eradicable  几乎可以根除的
easily eradicable  容易根除的
potentially eradicable  潜在地可以根除的
virtually eradicable 事实上可以根除的


词源:
eradicate (v.) + -able (suffix)

eradicate 源自拉丁语 *ēradicāre，由 ē-  (out) + rādīx (root) 组成，字面意思是"to root out"，即“根除”。
