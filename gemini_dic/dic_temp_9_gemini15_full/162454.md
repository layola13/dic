It encompasses all the steps and processes undertaken before the legal adoption is finalized. |  The preadoption home study was thorough and extensive.  领养前的家庭调查非常彻底和广泛。

The preadoption process included several meetings with social workers. 领养前的流程包括与社工的几次会面。


近义词: pre-placement, adoption preparation | 反义词: postadoption
