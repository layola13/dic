leveling: /ˈlevəlɪŋ/ | v. | 平整；使平坦；（使）达到同一水平；批评；贬低 |

英文解析:  The act of making something level or flat. It can also refer to the process of bringing something to the same level, or to the act of criticizing or belittling someone or something.

例句:

The farmer spent the afternoon leveling the field.  = 农民花了一下午的时间平整田地。

The government is leveling the playing field for small businesses. = 政府正在为小型企业创造公平的竞争环境。（使达到同一水平）


近义词:  flattening, smoothing, equalizing, criticizing, disparaging

反义词:  roughening, raising, elevating, praising, extolling


词源:  From Middle English *levellen, from Old French niveler, from niveau "level," from Latin lībellus "a little level."
