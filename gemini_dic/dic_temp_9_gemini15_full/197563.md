shelterer: /ˈʃɛltərər/ | n. | 庇护者；收容者 | A person or organization that provides shelter or protection, especially to the homeless or vulnerable.  | Example: The shelterer provided food and a warm bed for the homeless man.  庇护者为无家可归的男子提供了食物和温暖的床铺。 |近义词：protector, guardian, provider | 反义词：abuser, tormentor | 名词
