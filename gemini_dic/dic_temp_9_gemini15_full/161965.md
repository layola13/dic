pranks:/præŋks/| n. |恶作剧，玩笑| a playful trick or joke, often causing harmless mischief.  |例句：The children played pranks on their teacher.孩子们捉弄了他们的老师。|例句：He pulled a silly prank on his friend. 他对朋友开了一个愚蠢的玩笑。|近义词：tricks, jokes, shenanigans, hoaxes |反义词：seriousness, solemnity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plays pranks on his classmates. = 他捉弄他的同学。
They often prank each other. = 他们经常互相开玩笑。


一般过去时:
She played a prank on her brother. = 她捉弄了她哥哥。
We played pranks all day. = 我们整天都在恶作剧。


现在完成时:
He has played many pranks on me. = 他对我开过很多玩笑。
They have never played such a big prank before. = 他们以前从未开过这么大的玩笑。


过去完成时:
I had played a prank on him before he knew it. = 在他意识到之前，我已经捉弄了他。
They had played many pranks before they got caught. = 他们在被抓住之前已经做了很多恶作剧。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If I were to play a prank, I wouldn't do anything harmful. = 如果我要恶作剧，我不会做任何有害的事情。
I wish I hadn't played that prank on him. = 我真希望我没有捉弄他。

否定句:
