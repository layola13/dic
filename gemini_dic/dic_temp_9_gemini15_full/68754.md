farmyards:/ˈfɑːrmjɑːrdz/ | n. |农场院子；农家院 |The enclosed area around farm buildings, typically containing barns, sheds, and often livestock.  |例句：The children loved playing in the farmyards during their visit to the farm. 孩子们喜欢在参观农场时在农场院子里玩耍。|例句：The chickens were pecking around in the farmyards looking for food. 鸡在农场院子里啄食寻找食物。|近义词：farm, homestead, farmyard |反义词：city, urban area
