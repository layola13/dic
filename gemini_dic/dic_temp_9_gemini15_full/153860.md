perched:/pɜːtʃt/| adj. v.|栖息的；位于高处的；不安定的|  situated on a high place; precariously balanced;  to settle or rest (oneself) in a high position|The bird perched on a branch.|近义词：seated, positioned, balanced|反义词：grounded, low, stable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird perched on the branch. = 鸟栖息在树枝上。
He perches precariously on the edge of the cliff. = 他岌岌可危地栖息在悬崖边上。

一般过去时:
The eagle perched on the highest peak. = 老鹰栖息在最高的山峰上。
She perched herself on the edge of the chair. = 她把自己安置在椅子的边缘。


现在完成时:
The bird has perched there for hours. = 这只鸟在那里栖息了好几个小时。
He has perched himself on the fence, watching the game. = 他坐在栅栏上，看着比赛。


过去完成时:
The bird had perched there before the storm. = 在暴风雨来临之前，鸟儿已经在那里栖息了。
She had perched the vase carefully on the shelf before leaving. = 离开之前，她小心翼翼地把花瓶放在架子上。
