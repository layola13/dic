precleared:/ˌpriːˈklɪərd/| adj. |预先清除的；预先批准的 |Already cleared or approved in advance.  This typically refers to something that has undergone a process of vetting or approval before a specific event or action.  |例句：The precleared documents were submitted to the immigration officer. 预先审批的文件提交给了移民官。

The shipment of precleared goods arrived on time. 预先清关的货物准时到达。|近义词：approved, authorized, vetted |反义词：unapproved, unauthorized, uncleared |
