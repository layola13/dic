playing:/ˈpleɪɪŋ/ | v. | 玩耍；演奏；扮演 | The act of engaging in a recreational activity, performing music on an instrument, or acting a role. |例句：The children were playing in the park.孩子们在公园里玩耍。  The pianist was playing a beautiful sonata.钢琴家演奏了一首优美的奏鸣曲。|近义词：acting, performing, sporting, gaming |反义词：working, studying, resting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is playing football. 他正在踢足球。
She plays the piano every day. 她每天弹钢琴。

一般过去时:
They played basketball yesterday. 他们昨天打篮球。
I played the guitar at the party. 我在派对上弹吉他。

现在完成时:
We have been playing tennis for hours. 我们打网球已经几个小时了。
She has played the violin since she was five. 她从五岁起就开始拉小提琴。

过去完成时:
They had played the game before I arrived. 我到达之前他们已经玩过游戏了。
He had been playing video games all night. 他整夜都在玩电子游戏。

将来时:
I will be playing golf tomorrow. 我明天将打高尔夫球。
They will play a concert next week. 他们下周将举行一场音乐会。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would be playing the guitar. 如果我有更多时间，我会弹吉他的。
I wish I were playing in the orchestra. 我希望我在管弦乐队演奏。

被动语态:
The song is being played on the radio. 这首歌正在电台上播放。
The game was played fairly.  这场比赛进行得很公平。

疑问句:
Are you playing any games? 你玩什么游戏吗？
What are they playing? 他们在玩什么？

否定句:

They didn't play well in the match. 他们在比赛中表现不好。
