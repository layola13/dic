self-mistrusts:/ˌsɛlfˈmɪsˌtrʌsts/| n.|自我怀疑，缺乏自信|The noun "self-mistrusts" refers to a state of lacking confidence in oneself; it represents the plural form of "self-mistrust." It implies a lack of belief in one's own abilities, judgment, or worth.  This can manifest in various ways, from hesitancy to make decisions to a tendency to second-guess oneself constantly.|例句：Her self-mistrusts prevented her from applying for the promotion.  她的自我怀疑阻止了她申请晋升。


Her numerous self-mistrusts stemmed from a difficult childhood. 她许多的自我怀疑源于不幸的童年。|近义词：self-doubt, lack of confidence, insecurity, diffidence|反义词：self-confidence, self-assurance, self-belief|名词
