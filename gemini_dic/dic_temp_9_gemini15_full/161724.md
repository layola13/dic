power-base:/ˈpaʊə(r) beɪs/ | n. |权力基础，势力范围|A power base refers to the group or groups of people who support and provide strength to a person or organization, enabling them to exert influence or authority.  It can be based on various factors such as wealth, political connections, popular support, or control of resources. |Example: The mayor solidified his power base by securing endorsements from key community leaders. 市长通过获得主要社区领导人的支持巩固了他的权力基础。

The governor's power base eroded after the scandal. 州长的权力基础在丑闻后被削弱了。|近义词：foundation, support base, constituency, stronghold |反义词：weakness, vulnerability
