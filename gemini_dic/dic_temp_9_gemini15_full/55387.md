distemperment:/dɪsˈtɛmpərmənt/| n. |性情暴躁；脾气坏；精神错乱|A state of mental or emotional disturbance; a bad or disordered state of mind;  ill temper.  The word suggests a more profound disturbance than simple irritability. It implies a lack of self-control and potentially erratic behavior.|例句：His distemperment was evident in his erratic behavior and angry outbursts. 他的性情暴躁在他反复无常的行为和愤怒的爆发中显而易见。
例句：The doctor attributed his violent actions to a temporary distemperment caused by illness. 医生认为他的暴力行为是由于疾病引起的暂时性精神错乱。|近义词：malady, derangement, instability, irritability, ill temper|反义词：calmness, composure, equanimity, serenity|名词
