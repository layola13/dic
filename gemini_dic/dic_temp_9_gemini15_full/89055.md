holohemihedral:/ˌhɒləʊˈhɛmiˌhiːdrəl/| adj. |半完全的（指晶体）|Holohemihedral refers to a type of crystal symmetry where only half of the faces of a theoretically complete form are present.  It's a specific type of hemihedry, characterized by the presence of only half the faces expected based on a higher symmetry system. This results in a lower symmetry overall. |The mineral pyrite often exhibits holohemihedral forms.|近义词：hemihedral (more general term)|反义词：holohedral (complete form)
