词源:

The word "homeopath" is derived from the term "homeopathy," which itself is a combination of the Greek words "homoios" (similar) and "pathos" (suffering or disease).  Therefore, homeopathy literally means "similar suffering."  The suffix "-path" indicates a practitioner or specialist.
