roadlessness:/ˈroʊdləsnəs/| n. |缺乏道路；道路稀少；荒凉|The state or quality of being without roads; lack of roads, especially in a large area; remoteness.  It often implies a sense of isolation and difficulty in accessibility. |例句：The roadlessness of the region made transportation extremely challenging.  该地区的道路稀少使得交通运输极具挑战性。

例句：The vast roadlessness of the national park contributed to its wild beauty. 国家公园广阔的荒凉地带增添了它的自然野性之美。


近义词：remoteness, inaccessibility, isolation, wilderness
