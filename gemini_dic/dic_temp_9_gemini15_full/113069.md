lumps:/lʌmps/| n.|肿块，瘤；一堆；一团|A lump is a swelling under the skin, often caused by injury or infection. It can also refer to an irregular mass or a shapeless collection of something. |例句：The doctor examined the lump on her arm. 医生检查了她胳膊上的肿块。|例句：He added a lump of sugar to his tea. 他往茶里加了一块方糖。|近义词：bump, swelling, mass, nodule|反义词：smooth, flat
