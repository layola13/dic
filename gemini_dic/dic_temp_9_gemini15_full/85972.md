heads-up:/ˈhɛdz ʌp/ | n. | 预先警告，提醒 | A warning or notice given in advance.  | Example:  "Thanks for the heads-up about the traffic jam."  = "谢谢你提前提醒我交通堵塞。"

Example: "I'll give you a heads-up before I arrive." = "我到之前会提前通知你。"


近义词：warning, alert, notice, tip-off | 反义词：surprise, ambush


PHRASE->
give someone a heads-up = 提前告知某人
get a heads-up = 收到预先警告
a heads-up on something = 关于某事的预先警告
need a heads-up = 需要预先警告
without a heads-up = 没有预先警告


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He gives me a heads-up before every meeting. = 他每次会议前都会给我预先警告。
She always gives her team a heads-up on upcoming projects. = 她总是提前向她的团队通报即将开展的项目。


一般过去时:
He gave me a heads-up about the change in plans. = 他提前告知我计划的变更。
They gave us a heads-up before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们提前提醒了我们。


现在完成时:
I have given them a heads-up regarding the deadline. = 我已经就截止日期提前通知了他们。
She has received a heads-up about the potential problems. = 她已经收到了关于潜在问题的预先警告。


过去完成时:
I had given him a heads-up, but he still missed the meeting. = 我已经提前提醒过他了，但他还是错过了会议。
They had received a heads-up, so they were prepared for the inspection. = 他们已经收到了预先警告，所以他们为检查做好了准备。


将来时:
I will give you a heads-up as soon as I know the details. = 我一知道细节就会提前通知你。
They will give the employees a heads-up before implementing the new policy. = 他们将在实施新政策之前提前通知员工。


虚拟语气:
If I had received a heads-up, I would have been better prepared. = 如果我收到了预先警告，我会准备得更好。
I wish I had gotten a heads-up about the surprise party. = 我真希望我收到了惊喜派对的预先警告。


被动语态:
The team was given a heads-up about the budget cuts. = 团队收到了关于预算削减的预先警告。
The employees were not given a heads-up about the layoffs. = 员工们没有收到关于裁员的预先警告。


疑问句:
Did you get a heads-up about the meeting? = 你收到关于会议的预先警告了吗？
Have they given you a heads-up about the changes? = 他们已经就变更情况提前通知你了吗？


否定句:
I didn't get a heads-up about the exam. = 我没有收到关于考试的预先警告。
They didn't give us a heads-up about the delay. = 他们没有提前告知我们延误的情况。
