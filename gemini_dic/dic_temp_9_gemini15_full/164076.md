prediscovering:/ˌpriːdɪˈskʌvərɪŋ/| v. |预先发现；预见性发现 | Discovering something before it is officially or widely known.  It implies an element of foresight or anticipation. |例句：The researchers were prediscovering the effects of climate change on coastal ecosystems.  研究人员预先发现了气候变化对沿海生态系统的影响。

例句：She prediscovered a flaw in the design before the product launch. 她预先发现了产品发布前的设计缺陷。


近义词：anticipating, foreseeing, prefiguring


反义词：overlooking, missing, neglecting


词性：动词
