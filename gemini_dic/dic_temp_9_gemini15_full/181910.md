remains:/rɪˈmeɪnz/ | n. | 残余；遗迹；遗体 | The remains of the ancient city are still visible. 古城的遗迹仍然可见。|近义词： remnants, relics, vestiges | 反义词：whole, complete | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The building remains a symbol of hope. 这座建筑仍然是希望的象征。
What remains to be done?  还剩下什么要做？


一般过去时:
The remains of the meal sat untouched on the table.  饭菜的残羹剩饭 untouched地留在桌子上。
The evidence remains inconclusive.  证据仍然没有定论。


现在完成时:
The mystery remains unsolved.  这个谜团仍然未解。
Much of the city remains to be rebuilt.  这座城市的大部分地区还有待重建。


过去完成时:
After the battle, only a few soldiers remained.  战斗结束后，只有少数士兵幸存下来。
The question remained unanswered until the next meeting.  这个问题直到下次会议才得到解答。


将来时:
The problem will remain unsolved unless we find a solution.  除非我们找到解决办法，否则这个问题将依然无法解决。
The building will remain standing for many years to come. 这座建筑还会屹立很多年。


虚拟语气:
If the evidence remained hidden, we would never have solved the case. 如果证据一直隐藏着，我们就永远无法破案。
I wish the situation remained unchanged. 我希望情况保持不变。


被动语态:
The decision remains to be made.  决定尚未做出。
The matter remains to be discussed.  此事有待讨论。


疑问句:
What remains of the old house? 老房子还剩下什么？
How many soldiers remained after the battle? 战斗后还剩下多少士兵？


否定句:
Nothing remains of the ancient civilization. 古文明已荡然无存。
There remain no clues. 没有线索留存。
