martite:/ˈmɑːrtaɪt/| n. |赤铁矿；磁赤铁矿|A variety of hematite that is magnetic due to the presence of magnetite inclusions. It is an iron oxide mineral with the chemical formula Fe2O3.|Martite pseudomorphs after magnetite are common in many iron ore deposits.|近义词：hematite, magnetite|反义词：无
