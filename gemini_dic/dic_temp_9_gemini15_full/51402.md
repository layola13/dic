determines: /dɪˈtɜːrmɪnz/ | vt. |决定；确定；影响；限定 | to control or influence the nature or outcome of something; to find out exactly; to establish clearly |近义词：decides, governs, influences, dictates |反义词：undetermines


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weather determines what we do today. = 天气决定了我们今天做什么。
Your choices determine your future. = 你的选择决定你的未来。


一般过去时:
The judge determined the sentence. = 法官决定了判决。
The experiment determined the effectiveness of the drug. = 实验确定了药物的有效性。


现在完成时:
The results have determined the winner. = 结果已经决定了获胜者。
Scientists have determined the cause of the disease. = 科学家已经确定了这种疾病的病因。


过去完成时:
They had determined their course of action before the meeting. = 会议前他们已经确定了行动方案。
She had determined to leave before the storm hit. =暴风雨来临之前，她已决定离开。


将来时:
The board will determine the budget next week. = 董事会下周将确定预算。
This test will determine if you are eligible. = 此测试将决定您是否有资格。


虚拟语气:
If he determined to go, nothing could stop him. = 如果他决定去，什么也阻止不了他。
I wish I had determined to study harder earlier. = 我真希望我早点下定决心努力学习。


被动语态:
The winner will be determined by a lottery. = 获胜者将通过抽签决定。
The date of the meeting has been determined. = 会议日期已经确定。


疑问句:
What determines the price of gold? = 什么决定了黄金的价格？
Does your personality determine your career path? = 你的性格决定你的职业道路吗？


否定句:
The weather didn't determine our plans. = 天气并没有决定我们的计划。
His age didn't determine his ability. = 他的年龄并没有决定他的能力。


PHRASE->
self-determined: 自主的
predetermined: 预先确定的
determined effort: 坚定的努力
be determined to: 决心做...
determine the outcome: 决定结果
