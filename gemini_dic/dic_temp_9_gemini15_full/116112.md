marvelled:/ˈmɑːvəld/| v.|惊叹，惊奇，赞叹|to feel or express great surprise or wonder at something.|例句：I marvelled at the beauty of the sunset. 我惊叹于日落的美丽。|例句：We marvelled at her talent. 我们对她的才能感到惊叹。|近义词：wondered, amazed, astonished|反义词：indifference, apathy|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I marvel at his skill. = 我惊叹于他的技巧。
They marvel at the speed of the train. = 他们惊叹于火车的速度。


一般过去时:
I marvelled at the size of the building. = 我惊叹于建筑物的规模。
She marvelled at his quick thinking. = 她惊叹于他的快速思考。


现在完成时:
I have marvelled at many things in my life. = 我一生中对许多事情都感到惊叹。
We have marvelled at the progress made in technology. = 我们对科技进步感到惊叹。


过去完成时:
I had marvelled at his paintings before I met him. = 在我遇见他之前，我就对他的画作感到惊叹。
They had marvelled at the acrobatic show before it ended. = 在杂技表演结束之前，他们就对它感到惊叹。


将来时:
I will marvel at the stars tonight. = 今晚我将惊叹于星空。
We will marvel at the beauty of the landscape. = 我们将惊叹于这片美丽的风景。


虚拟语气:
If I saw that, I would marvel at its beauty. = 如果我看到那样的事物，我会惊叹于它的美丽。
I wish I could marvel at the Northern Lights. = 我希望我能看到北极光并为之惊叹。


被动语态:
His skill was marvelled at by everyone. = 他的技巧受到每个人的赞叹。

疑问句:

What are you marveling at? = 你在惊叹什么？


否定句:
I didn't marvel at his achievement. = 我对他的成就并不感到惊叹。
They didn't marvel at the complexity of the problem. = 他们对问题的复杂性并不感到惊叹。
