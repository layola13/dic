co-operation:/ˌkoʊˌɒpəˈreɪʃən/| n. |合作，协作|The act of working together with someone or something else to achieve a common goal. It involves mutual assistance and a shared purpose.|例句：Teamwork and co-operation are essential for success.  团队合作和协作对于成功至关重要。|例句：The project requires close co-operation between different departments.  这个项目需要不同部门之间的密切合作。|近义词：collaboration, teamwork, synergy|反义词：conflict, opposition, antagonism|名词
