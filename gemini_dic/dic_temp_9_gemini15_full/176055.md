raver:/ˈreɪvər/| n. |狂热者，狂欢者 |A raver is someone who attends rave parties, often characterized by their enthusiasm for electronic dance music and the party culture.  They are typically known for their energetic dancing and vibrant clothing. | Example:  The raver danced enthusiastically throughout the night.  狂欢者整夜热情地跳舞。|近义词：clubber, partygoer, enthusiast |反义词：homebody, recluse
