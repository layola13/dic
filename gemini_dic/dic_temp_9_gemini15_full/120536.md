miquelet:/mɪkəlɛt/| n.|米格列特式火绳枪|A miquelet lock is a type of firearm lock mechanism characterized by a serpentine spring and a pivoted pan cover that is released by the trigger.  It was particularly popular in the 16th and 17th centuries, especially in Spain and its colonies.  It was known for its relative simplicity and reliability, making it suitable for mass production and use by less experienced soldiers.|The Spanish conquistadors often used miquelet locks on their firearms. |近义词：matchlock (a less advanced type of firearm lock)|反义词：无
