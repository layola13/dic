orderlies:/ˈɔːrdərliz/| n.|勤杂工；（医院的）护工|People whose job is to do tasks such as cleaning and helping patients in a hospital or other institution.|The orderlies cleaned the hospital rooms efficiently.|近义词：attendants, assistants|反义词:doctors, nurses


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The orderlies clean the rooms every day. = 勤杂工每天打扫房间。
Orderlies assist patients with their daily needs. = 护工协助病人满足他们的日常需求。


一般过去时:
The orderlies cleaned the ward last night. = 勤杂工昨晚打扫了病房。
The orderlies assisted the doctor during surgery. = 护工在手术期间协助医生。
