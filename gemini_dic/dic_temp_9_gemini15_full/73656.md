forge:/fɔːdʒ/| /fɔːrʒ/| v., n.| 锻造；伪造；创造；建立 |to shape metal by heating it and hammering it; to make or create something, especially something new or original; to make a copy of something with the intent to deceive|例句：The blacksmith forged a new horseshoe.  铁匠锻造了一个新的马蹄铁。
The artist forged a new style of painting.艺术家创造了一种新的绘画风格。|近义词：hammer, fabricate, create, form, establish|反义词：destroy, demolish, break, ruin|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He forges swords in his workshop. 他在他的工作室里锻造宝剑。
She forges new business opportunities. 她正在创造新的商业机会。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

She has forged many friendships throughout her travels.  她在旅行中结交了许多朋友。

过去完成时:

将来时:
