hazier:/ˈheɪzɪər/| adj.| 模糊的；朦胧的；烟雾弥漫的 | More hazy than clear; lacking clarity or distinctness.  | The air became hazier as the day wore on.  空气随着一天的推移变得越来越模糊。The memory became hazier over time.  随着时间的推移，记忆变得越来越模糊。| clearer, sharper | clear, distinct | 形容词
