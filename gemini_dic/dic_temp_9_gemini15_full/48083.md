declassifiable: /ˌdiːˈklæsɪfaɪəbl/ | adj. | 可解密的，可以解除保密的 | Capable of being declassified; able to have its classification removed.  This adjective describes information or documents that can be downgraded from a higher security classification to a lower one, or even made public. |


例句：

The document was deemed declassifiable after a thorough review.  = 在彻底审查后，该文件被认为可以解密。

The information, initially classified top secret, is now declassifiable. =  最初被列为最高机密的信息，现在可以解密了。


近义词：unsecret, releasable


反义词：classified, secret, confidential


词性：形容词
