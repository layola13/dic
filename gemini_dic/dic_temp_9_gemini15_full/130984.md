nonatmospheric:/ˌnɒnˌætməsˈfɪrɪk/| adj. |非大气层的，非大气中的|Not relating to or occurring in the atmosphere.  The term describes phenomena, processes, or objects that exist or operate outside of Earth's atmosphere. |例句：The study focused on nonatmospheric sources of pollution, such as volcanic eruptions. 这项研究关注的是非大气污染源，例如火山喷发。


例句：Scientists are researching nonatmospheric effects on climate change. 科学家们正在研究对气候变化的非大气影响。


近义词：extraterrestrial, extra-atmospheric


反义词：atmospheric
