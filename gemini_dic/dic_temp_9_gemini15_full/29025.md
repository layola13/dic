cartoning:/ˈkɑːrtənɪŋ/| n. |装箱；纸盒包装|The process of packing goods into cartons.  It includes the selection of appropriate cartons, the filling and sealing of cartons, and often the labeling and palletizing of the filled cartons.|例句：The cartoning process is automated to increase efficiency.  装箱过程实现了自动化以提高效率。

例句：The new cartoning machine significantly reduced packaging time. 新的装箱机大大缩短了包装时间。


近义词：packaging, boxing

反义词：unpacking, unboxing


词性：名词
