greco:/ˈɡreɪkoʊ/| adj.|希腊的；希腊式的|Of or relating to Greece or its people, language, or culture.  The word often refers to aspects of Greek art, architecture, or culture, particularly those of classical antiquity.|例句：The museum boasts a stunning collection of Greco-Roman sculptures. 这个博物馆拥有令人惊叹的希腊罗马雕塑收藏。|近义词：Hellenic, Grecian|反义词：None specifically, but words indicating other cultures could be considered contrastive.
