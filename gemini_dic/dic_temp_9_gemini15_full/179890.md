refaces:/riːˈfeɪs/| vt.|重新面对；翻新表面；更换表面|To give a new face or surface to something; to renovate the surface of something.  |例句：The old building was refaced with modern materials.  这座旧建筑用现代材料重新装修了表面。|例句：The company refaced its marketing strategy after a series of setbacks.  公司在经历一系列挫折后重新调整了营销策略。|近义词：remodel, renovate, refurbish|反义词：destroy, demolish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He refaces old furniture. = 他重新装修旧家具。
They reface the building's exterior. = 他们翻新建筑的外墙。

一般过去时:
She refaced the kitchen cabinets. = 她重新粉刷了厨房的橱柜。
The artist refaced the painting. = 艺术家重新粉刷了这幅画。

现在完成时:
They have refaced the entire building. = 他们已经重新装修了整栋建筑物。
We have refaced our marketing campaign. = 我们已经重新调整了我们的营销活动。

过去完成时:
The contractor had refaced the house before we moved in. = 我们搬进去之前，承包商已经重新装修了房子。
They had refaced the damaged section of the wall. = 他们已经重新粉刷了墙上受损的部分。

将来时:
We will reface the fireplace next month. = 我们下个月将重新装修壁炉。
The company will reface its product line. = 公司将重新调整其产品线。

虚拟语气:
If they refaced the building, it would look much better. = 如果他们重新装修了建筑物，它看起来会好得多。
I wish they had refaced the old furniture. = 我希望他们已经重新装修了旧家具。

被动语态:
The building is being refaced. = 这栋建筑正在重新装修。
The kitchen cabinets were refaced last year. = 厨房橱柜去年重新装修了。

疑问句:
Will they reface the entire building? = 他们会重新装修整栋建筑物吗？
Have they refaced the damaged area? = 他们已经重新装修了受损区域了吗？

否定句:
They did not reface the house. = 他们没有重新装修房子。
We will not reface the countertops. = 我们不会重新装修台面。


PHRASE->
reface a wall = 粉刷墙壁
reface a building = 翻新建筑
reface a book =  重新装订书籍 (较少用，通常用rebind)
reface a decision = 重新考虑决定 (比喻用法)
reface a strategy = 重新制定策略
