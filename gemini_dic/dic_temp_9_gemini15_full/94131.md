impeders:/ɪmˈpiːdərz/ | n. |阻碍者，妨碍物 |Those who hinder progress or obstruct something are called impeders. Impeders can be people, things, or circumstances that create difficulties or delays. |例句：The main impeders to economic growth were high inflation and unemployment.  主要的经济增长阻碍因素是高通货膨胀和高失业率。

例句：Bureaucracy and red tape are often cited as major impeders to efficient business practices. 官僚主义和繁文缛节常常被认为是阻碍高效商业实践的主要障碍。


近义词：obstacles, hindrances, obstructions, barriers |反义词：facilitators, aids, helpers
