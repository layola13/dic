extuberant: /ɪkˈsjuːbərənt/ | adj. | 兴高采烈的；充满活力的；茂盛的 |

英文解析:  Characterized by or showing great energy and excitement; exuberantly happy, enthusiastic, or cheerful. Also describes something growing in great abundance or profusion.

例句:

The children were exuberant after receiving their presents.  孩子们收到礼物后兴高采烈。

His exuberant personality made him a popular figure. 他热情奔放的性格使他成为一个受欢迎的人物。


近义词: enthusiastic, lively, buoyant, ebullient, effusive, vigorous, abundant, prolific

反义词: subdued, depressed, lethargic, apathetic, meager, scarce


词源:  From Latin *exuberare "to overflow," from *ex- "out" + *uber "udder, breast," hence "fruitful, abundant."  The sense developed from the idea of overflowing abundance to one of overflowing emotion.
