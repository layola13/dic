cookmaid:/ˈkʊkmeɪd/| n. |女厨，女佣 | A cookmaid is a female servant who cooks and performs other household tasks.  This term is largely archaic and rarely used in modern English. | Example:  The cookmaid prepared a delicious meal for the family.  女佣为家人准备了一顿美味的饭菜。 | 近义词：housekeeper, maid, domestic servant | 反义词：employer, master
