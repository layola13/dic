furbisher:/ˈfɜːrbɪʃər/ | n. |擦亮者；修饰者；翻新者 | One who furbishes; one who renovates or restores something to its former glory.  A furbisher is someone who cleans, polishes, or restores something to a better condition. They often work with antique furniture, metalwork, or other valuable items. |例句：The antique dealer hired a furbisher to restore the damaged clock. = 古董商雇佣了一名擦亮者来修复损坏的钟表。


The museum employed a skilled furbisher to revitalize their collection of old silverware. = 博物馆雇佣了一位熟练的修饰者来使他们收藏的旧银器焕发生机。
