shysters:/ˈʃaɪstərz/ | n. | 骗子，江湖骗子 | A shyster is a lawyer or other professional person who uses dishonest or unethical methods.  They often engage in unscrupulous practices to gain an advantage, especially in legal matters. They are characterized by their cunning, manipulation, and disregard for ethical conduct. | Example: The shyster lawyer managed to get his client acquitted despite overwhelming evidence of guilt.  这个骗子律师设法让他的当事人被宣判无罪，尽管有压倒性的证据证明他有罪。


|近义词：fraud, swindler, crook, con artist, charlatan |反义词：honest professional, ethical practitioner |词性：名词
