involve:/ɪnˈvɒlv/ | vt. | 包含；涉及；使参与；牵涉 | To include or contain as a part; to have or create as a necessary consequence; to cause someone to become connected or involved in something. |例句：The job involves a lot of travel. 这份工作需要大量的出差。|例句：He was involved in a car accident. 他卷入了一起车祸。|近义词：include, encompass, engage, implicate |反义词：exclude, omit


with:/wɪð/ | prep. | 和；与；具有；用 |  Expressing accompaniment, association, or connection. |例句：I went to the party with my friends. 我和我的朋友们一起去了派对。|例句：She's a woman with great patience. 她是一位很有耐心的女人。|近义词：along with, together with, by means of |反义词：without, apart from


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project involves extensive research. = 这个项目涉及大量的研究。
This game involves two players. = 这个游戏需要两个玩家。

一般过去时:
The accident involved three cars. = 这起事故涉及三辆车。
She was deeply involved with the charity. = 她深深地参与了这个慈善机构。


现在完成时:
I have been involved in several projects this year. = 今年我参与了几个项目。
They have involved themselves in community service. = 他们参与了社区服务。


过去完成时:
By then, he had involved himself in too many things. = 到那时，他已经参与了太多的事情。
She had been involved with the company for ten years before she retired. = 在退休之前，她已经在这家公司工作了十年。


将来时:
The new policy will involve significant changes. = 新政策将带来重大变化。
We will involve all stakeholders in the decision-making process. = 我们将在决策过程中让所有利益相关者参与。


虚拟语气:
If I were involved, I would have done things differently. = 如果我参与其中，我会采取不同的做法。
I wish I hadn't been involved in that argument. = 我真希望我没有卷入那场争论。


被动语态:
Many people were involved in the planning. = 许多人都参与了规划。
He was deeply involved in the scandal. = 他深陷丑闻之中。


疑问句:
What is involved in this job? = 这份工作都包括什么？
Is he involved with that company? = 他和那家公司有关系吗？


否定句:
The decision does not involve you. = 这个决定与你无关。
He was not involved in the crime. = 他没有参与犯罪。


PHRASE->
be involved in = 参与...
involved with = 与...有关系
