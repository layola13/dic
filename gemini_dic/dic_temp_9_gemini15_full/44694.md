crude:/kruːd/ | adj. n. | 粗糙的；未加工的；粗俗的；原油 |

英文解析：
As an adjective, "crude" means lacking refinement or finesse; being in an unrefined or natural state.  As a noun, it primarily refers to petroleum extracted from the earth.

例句：
(Adjective):
The crude drawing showed a stick figure. = 粗糙的图画显示了一个简笔人物。

The crude remarks offended many people. = 粗俗的言论冒犯了许多人。


(Noun):
The price of crude oil fluctuates wildly. = 原油价格剧烈波动。

They refined the crude oil into gasoline. = 他们将原油提炼成汽油。


近义词：
(Adjective): rough, unrefined, primitive, vulgar, coarse, raw, unprocessed

(Noun): petroleum, oil


反义词：
(Adjective): refined, polished, sophisticated, elegant, cultured


词性: adjective, noun
