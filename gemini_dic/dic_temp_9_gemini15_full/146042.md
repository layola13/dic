overdrive:/ˈoʊvərˌdraɪv/ | n. v. |超速驱动；超速档；过度活跃|

英文解析:  Overdrive refers to a higher gear in a vehicle's transmission that allows the engine to run at a higher speed than normal for a given vehicle speed, resulting in increased power and speed.  It can also refer to a state of excessive activity or intensity.


例句:

The car shifted into overdrive, accelerating rapidly.  = 汽车挂入超速档，迅速加速。

He was working in overdrive to meet the deadline. = 他为了赶期限而拼命工作。


近义词:  high gear, acceleration, hyperactivity, overdrive gear

反义词:  low gear, deceleration, inactivity, underdrive


词性: Noun and Verb
