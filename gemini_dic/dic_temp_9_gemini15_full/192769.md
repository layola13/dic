sealants:/ˈsiːlənts/| n.|密封剂，填充剂|Substances used to seal or close something tightly to prevent leakage or entry.|The plumber used sealants to fix the leaky pipe. 水管工用密封剂修补了漏水的管道。The dentist applied sealants to my child's molars to prevent cavities. 牙医在我的孩子磨牙上涂抹了密封剂以防止蛀牙。|adhesives, caulks|openings, gaps|n.
