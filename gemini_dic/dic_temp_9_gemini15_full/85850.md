he'd:/hiːd/ | pron. | 他会，他会，他曾经 |  Contraction of "he would" or "he had".  It can represent the past tense, future conditional, or habitual past. |


例句：He'd go to the park every day. = 他每天都去公园。(habitual past)

例句：He'd help you if you asked. = 如果你问他，他会帮助你。(future conditional)


近义词:he would, he had (depending on context) | 反义词: (no direct antonyms, depends on context) | 词性:pronoun
