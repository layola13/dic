isotropic:/ˌaɪsoʊˈtrɒpɪk/ | adj. | 各向同性的；等向性的 | Having the same properties in all directions.  Isotropic materials have the same physical properties regardless of the direction of measurement. For example, an isotropic material will have the same strength and conductivity in all directions. | Example:  The material is isotropic, meaning its properties are consistent regardless of direction.  =  这种材料是各向同性的，这意味着它的特性与方向无关。  |近义词: homogeneous, uniform |反义词: anisotropic
