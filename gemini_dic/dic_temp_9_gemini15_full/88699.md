hobblebush:/ˈhɑːbəlˌbʊʃ/| n. |荚蒾属植物|A shrub or small tree of the genus Viburnum, typically having somewhat irregular, often drooping clusters of white flowers.  The name likely refers to the appearance of the branches, which can appear bent or twisted.  Many species are known for their attractive berries.|例句：The hobblebush's delicate white flowers contrasted beautifully with the deep green foliage.  荚蒾属植物的精致白色花朵与深绿色的树叶形成了美丽的对比。

The hobblebush, with its drooping branches, provided a shaded haven for the birds.  枝条下垂的荚蒾为鸟类提供了一个阴凉的庇护所。|近义词：viburnum, cranberrybush (some species)|反义词:无
