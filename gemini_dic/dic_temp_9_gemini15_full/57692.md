drawls:/drɔːlz/ | v. | 拖长语调地说；懒洋洋地说；缓慢地移动 | To speak slowly with prolonged vowels, often in a lazy or relaxed manner; to move slowly and languidly. |例句：He drawled his answer, making it difficult to understand. 他拖长语调地回答，让人难以理解。

例句：The caravan drawled along the dusty road. 那辆大篷车沿着尘土飞扬的道路缓慢地行驶。|近义词：mumble, drone, slur, drag |反义词：rush, hurry, articulate
