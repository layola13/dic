retroflexed:/ˈrɛtroʊflɛkst/ | adj. | 卷舌的，后卷舌的 | Turned or curled back; specifically, referring to a sound produced with the tongue curled back towards the palate.  This term is primarily used in phonetics and linguistics to describe certain consonant sounds. | Example:  The Hindi consonant "ṭ" is a retroflexed stop.  印地语辅音“ṭ”是一个卷舌塞音。| Near synonyms: retracted, curled | Antonyms: forward-facing, non-retroflex |  adj.
