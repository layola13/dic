prefund:/ˈpriːfʌnd/| v.|预先资助，预先拨款|to provide funding for something before it is needed or before it begins.|The project was prefunded by a generous donation.  这个项目由一笔慷慨的捐款预先资助。|The company prefunded its marketing campaign for the upcoming product launch. 公司为即将推出的产品发布预先资助了其营销活动。|fund, finance|deplete, exhaust|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They prefund their research projects annually. = 他们每年预先资助他们的研究项目。
The government prefunds several infrastructure projects. = 政府预先资助了一些基础设施项目。

一般过去时:
The organization prefunded the disaster relief efforts. = 该组织预先资助了救灾工作。
We prefunded the trip with savings from last year. = 我们用去年的积蓄预先资助了这次旅行。

现在完成时:
They have prefunded the development of a new software. = 他们已经预先资助了一种新软件的开发。
The company has prefunded its expansion into new markets. = 公司已经预先资助了其向新市场的扩张。

过去完成时:
They had prefunded the construction before the groundbreaking ceremony. = 在奠基仪式之前，他们已经预先资助了建设。
The investors had prefunded the startup before it secured any other funding. = 在这家初创公司获得任何其他资金之前，投资者已经预先资助了它。

将来时:
We will prefund the project once the proposal is approved. = 一旦提案获得批准，我们将预先资助该项目。
The foundation will prefund the scholarship program for the next academic year. = 该基金会将为下一个学年预先资助奖学金项目。

虚拟语气:
If they prefunded the project, it would have been completed sooner. = 如果他们预先资助了该项目，它本可以更快地完成。
I wish we had prefunded the repairs; now we are short of money. = 我希望我们已经预先资助了维修；现在我们缺钱了。

被动语态:
The project was prefunded by a private foundation. = 该项目由一个私人基金会预先资助。
The research was prefunded by a government grant. = 这项研究是由政府拨款预先资助的。

疑问句:
Will the company prefund the development of the new product? = 公司会预先资助新产品的开发吗？
Has the university prefunded the research project? = 大学已经预先资助该研究项目了吗？

否定句:
They did not prefund the marketing campaign. = 他们没有预先资助营销活动。
The investors chose not to prefund the risky venture. = 投资者选择不预先资助这项风险投资。
