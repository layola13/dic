paid-off:/peɪd ˈɔːf/ | adj. | 付清的；已还清的；成功的；有回报的 |  Having received full payment; successfully completed; yielding a profit.  |例句：He finally paid off his mortgage. 他终于还清了抵押贷款。

例句：Her years of hard work finally paid off. 她多年的努力终于得到了回报。


近义词：settled, cleared, successful, profitable, rewarding | 反义词：outstanding, unpaid, unsuccessful, unprofitable, unrewarding | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loan is paid off. = 贷款已还清。
The investment is paid off. = 投资已获得回报。


一般过去时:
They paid off their debts last year. = 他们去年还清了债务。
The project paid off handsomely. = 这个项目获得了丰厚的回报。


现在完成时:
She has paid off all her student loans. = 她已经还清了所有学生贷款。
His hard work has finally paid off. = 他的努力终于得到了回报。


过去完成时:
He had paid off his car loan before buying a new one. = 他在买新车之前已经还清了汽车贷款。
They had paid off their mortgage before retirement. = 他们在退休前已经还清了抵押贷款。


将来时:
We will pay off the house in five years. = 我们将在五年内还清房贷。
I believe this investment will pay off in the long run. = 我相信这项投资从长远来看会带来回报。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:
