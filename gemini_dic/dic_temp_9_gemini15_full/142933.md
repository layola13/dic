out-travel:/ˌaʊtˈtrævəl/| v.|超过…的行程；旅行范围超过 |to travel farther than someone or something else; to exceed in extent of travel|例句：His travels out-travelled those of any other explorer of his time. 他的旅程超过了他那个时代任何其他探险家的旅程。|例句：The range of the new car out-travels that of the old one. 新车的行驶范围超过了旧车。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip|反义词：undertravel (not a common word)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The explorers out-travel their predecessors. = 探险家们的旅程超过了他们的前辈。
This car out-travels all its competitors. = 这辆车的行驶范围超过了所有竞争对手。

一般过去时:
Their journey out-travelled all expectations. = 他们的旅程超出了所有预期。
The migrating birds out-travelled their usual route. = 迁徙的鸟类飞行的距离超过了它们通常的路线。

现在完成时:
We have out-travelled our original plans. = 我们已经超过了最初的旅行计划。
She has out-travelled her father in terms of distance covered.  = 就行程距离而言，她已经超过了她父亲。

过去完成时:
By the end of the year, they had out-travelled their annual quota. = 到年底，他们已经超过了他们每年的配额。
They had out-travelled their rivals before the competition ended. = 在比赛结束之前，他们已经超过了他们的竞争对手。

将来时:
We will out-travel the previous record by a significant margin. = 我们将大幅超过之前的记录。
The expedition will out-travel any previous attempt to reach the summit. = 探险队将超过任何先前到达顶峰的尝试。

虚拟语气:
If we had a better vehicle, we would out-travel them easily. = 如果我们有一辆更好的车，我们就能轻松超过他们。
I wish I could out-travel my current limitations. = 我希望我能克服我目前的限制。

被动语态:
The previous record was out-travelled by a young adventurer. =  之前的记录被一位年轻的冒险家打破了。
Their achievements were out-travelled by the next generation of explorers. = 他们的成就被下一代探险家所超越。

疑问句:
Does this model out-travel the previous one? = 这个型号的行程范围是否超过之前的型号？
Will their journey out-travel the planned itinerary? = 他们的旅程会超过计划行程吗？

否定句:
Their expedition did not out-travel the expectations. = 他们的探险没有超过预期。
The team failed to out-travel the established route.  = 该团队未能超过既定的路线。
