hypercautiously:/ˌhaɪpərˈkɔːʃəsli/| adv. |极其谨慎地，过度小心地 | Extremely cautious; excessively careful.  It implies an excessive level of caution, potentially hindering progress or efficiency. |例句：He hypercautiously checked the brakes of his car three times before driving. 他在开车前极其谨慎地检查了三次汽车的刹车。|例句：She approached the negotiation hypercautiously, fearing any misstep. 她非常谨慎地进行谈判，害怕任何失误。|近义词：carefully, cautiously, meticulously, scrupulously |反义词：recklessly, carelessly, rashly |词性:副词
