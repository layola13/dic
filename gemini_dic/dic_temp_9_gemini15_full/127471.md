necessitarian:/ˌnɛsɛsɪˈteəriən/| n. adj.| 必然论者；必然论的；|A necessitarian believes that all events are causally determined and therefore inevitable.  A necessitarian philosophy often implies a lack of free will.|例句：Many philosophers are necessitarians, believing that free will is an illusion. 许多哲学家是必然论者，认为自由意志是一种幻觉。|例句：The necessitarian argument suggests that our choices are predetermined. 必然论的论点表明我们的选择是预先决定的。|近义词：determinist, fatalist|反义词：libertarian, free will advocate|名词，形容词
