/ˈmɛdər/| v. |瞎搞，干预，管闲事 |To interfere in something that is not one's concern; to meddle.  |He's always meddling in other people's affairs. 他总是干涉别人的事。|  interfere, meddle, butt in | mind one's own business


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He meddles in my business. = 他干涉我的事情。
She meddles with my computer. = 她乱动我的电脑。
