schoolhouses:/ˈskuːlˌhaʊzɪz/ | n. | 校舍，学校建筑 |  A schoolhouse is a building used as a school, especially a small, often one-room building in a rural area.  |  The old schoolhouse was converted into a museum.  那所旧校舍被改建成了博物馆。| school building, one-room school |  home, residence |
