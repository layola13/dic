envied:/ˈɛnvid/| v.|嫉妒，羡慕|To feel or show envy towards (someone or something).  To wish that one had what another person has.|例句：I envied her beautiful house. 我羡慕她漂亮的房子。

例句：He envied his brother's success. 他嫉妒他哥哥的成功。


近义词：covet, crave, begrudge

反义词：admire (without envy),  congratulate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I envy her talent. = 我羡慕她的才能。
They envy his wealth. = 他们羡慕他的财富。


一般过去时:
I envied his promotion. = 我嫉妒他的升职。
She envied my new car. = 她羡慕我的新车。


现在完成时:
I have envied her success for years. = 多年来我一直嫉妒她的成功。
They have envied his position since he got it. = 自从他得到那个职位以来，他们就一直羡慕他。


过去完成时:
I had envied her intelligence before I met her. = 在我认识她之前，我就羡慕她的聪明才智。
He had envied her opportunities since childhood. = 他从小就羡慕她的机会。


将来时:
I will envy her if she wins the award. = 如果她获奖，我会羡慕她。
They will envy his achievements in the future. = 将来他们会羡慕他的成就。


虚拟语气:
I wish I didn't envy him so much. = 我希望我不那么嫉妒他。
If I were her, I wouldn't envy anyone else's life. = 如果我是她，我不会羡慕任何人的生活。


被动语态:
His success was envied by many. = 他的成功被许多人羡慕。
Her beauty is envied by all. = 她的美貌被所有人羡慕。


疑问句:
Do you envy her success? = 你羡慕她的成功吗？
Does he envy my new job? = 他羡慕我的新工作吗？


否定句:
I don't envy her at all. = 我一点也不羡慕她。
He didn't envy his brother's good fortune. = 他不嫉妒他哥哥的好运气。



PHRASE->
be envied by = 被...羡慕
feel envied = 感到被羡慕
green with envy = 妒火中烧
an object of envy = 令人羡慕的对象
the envy of =  是...羡慕的对象
