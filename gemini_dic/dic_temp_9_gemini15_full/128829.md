nightshirts:/ˈnaɪtʃɜːrts/| n. |睡衣|A nightshirt is a loose-fitting garment, typically made of cotton or other lightweight fabric, worn in bed at night.  It's usually longer than a pajama top and often resembles a long shirt.|Example: He pulled on his nightshirt before climbing into bed. 他穿上睡衣然后爬上床。|近义词：nightgown, sleeping shirt |反义词:day clothes
