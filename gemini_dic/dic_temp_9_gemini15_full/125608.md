multiverse:/ˈmʌltɪˌvɜːrs/| n. |多元宇宙，多重宇宙 |A theoretical concept in physics and philosophy that proposes the existence of multiple universes or realities beyond our own, each potentially with different physical laws and constants.  |Example: The concept of the multiverse is explored in many science fiction stories. 多元宇宙的概念在许多科幻小说中都有探讨。  |近义词：parallel universes, alternate realities |反义词：universe (in the singular sense implying only one universe)
