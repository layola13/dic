provocatives:/prəˈvɑːkətɪvz/ | n. |煽动性言论；挑衅行为；刺激物 |  Something that provokes or incites a reaction, often anger or controversy.  Provocatives can be statements, actions, images, or objects designed to elicit a strong response. |例句：His provocative remarks sparked a heated debate. 他的煽动性言论引发了一场激烈的辩论。

例句：The artist's provocative sculptures caused a stir among viewers. 艺术家的挑衅性雕塑在观众中引起了轰动。


近义词：instigations, incitements, stimulants, irritants | 反义词：soothing agents, pacifiers, conciliatory remarks


词性：名词
