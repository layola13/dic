gossiping:/ˈɡɑːsɪpɪŋ/ | v. |说闲话；八卦 | The act of spreading rumors or discussing other people's private lives, often in a casual and informal manner.  |例句：They were gossiping about their neighbors' new car.  他们在说他们邻居的新车闲话。|例句：Stop gossiping and get back to work! 别八卦了，回去工作！|近义词：whispering, chattering,  rumormongering |反义词：keeping secrets, being discreet
