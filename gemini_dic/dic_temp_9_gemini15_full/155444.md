phenix:/ˈfiːnɪks/| n. |不死鸟，凤凰|A mythical bird that cyclically regenerates or is otherwise born again. Associated with the sun, it symbolizes immortality, rebirth, and resurrection.  The phoenix is often depicted as a majestic bird with brilliant plumage, typically red and gold.|例句：The phoenix, rising from the ashes, symbolizes hope and renewal.  不死鸟从灰烬中升起，象征着希望和新生。|近义词：firebird|反义词：无
