firewarden:/ˈfaɪərwɔːdn/| n. |消防管理员，防火监督员 |A firewarden is a person responsible for fire prevention and safety in a specific area or building.  They inspect premises, ensure fire safety regulations are followed, and may be involved in emergency response. |例句：The firewarden checked the fire extinguishers and escape routes. 消防管理员检查了灭火器和逃生路线。


The building's firewarden conducted a thorough fire drill.  大楼的消防管理员进行了一次彻底的消防演习。|近义词：fire inspector, fire marshal|反义词：N/A
