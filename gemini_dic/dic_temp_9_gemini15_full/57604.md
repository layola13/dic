drapes:/dreɪps/| n. v.| 窗帘；披巾；布幔；垂下 |Drapes are heavy curtains that hang in folds.  Drapes can also refer to something hanging loosely.  As a verb, it means to hang or arrange something loosely.|例句：The velvet drapes hung elegantly in the grand ballroom. 天鹅绒窗帘优雅地悬挂在大舞厅里。She draped the scarf around her neck. 她把围巾披在脖子上。|近义词：curtains, hangings, folds|反义词：straight, taut
