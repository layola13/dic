glamourizing:/ˈɡlæməraɪzɪŋ/| v.|使迷人；美化；渲染；|To make something seem more attractive, exciting, or glamorous than it really is.|The film glamourizes the life of a drug dealer. 这部电影美化了毒贩的生活。|近义词：romanticizing, idealizing, romanticizing|反义词：denigrating, exposing, criticizing|动词

She glamourizes her past experiences. = 她美化了她过去的经历。
