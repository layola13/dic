overvalue:/ˌoʊvərˈvæljuː/|/ˈoʊvərvæljʊ/| vt. |过高评价；高估|To place too high a value on something.  It implies assigning a worth greater than its actual or objective value.|例句：The company overvalued its assets in its financial report. 公司在其财务报告中高估了其资产。

例句：He overvalued his own abilities and underestimated the difficulty of the task. 他高估了自己的能力，低估了任务的难度。


近义词：overestimate, exaggerate, inflate


反义词：undervalue, underestimate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They overvalue their skills. = 他们高估了自己的技能。
The market overvalues this stock. = 市场高估了这只股票。


一般过去时:
The appraiser overvalued the house. = 评估师高估了房子的价值。
Investors overvalued the company's potential. = 投资者高估了公司的潜力。


现在完成时:
The company has overvalued its inventory. = 公司已经高估了其库存的价值。
He has overvalued his contribution to the project. = 他高估了他对项目的贡献。


过去完成时:
They had overvalued the land before the market crash. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经高估了土地的价值。
She had overvalued her chances of success. = 她之前高估了她成功的几率。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
