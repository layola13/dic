harpies:/ˈhɑːrpiːz/| n.|哈耳庇厄，凶恶的女人|In Greek mythology, harpies are monstrous creatures, typically depicted as winged women with the faces of hags, foul-smelling breath, and a penchant for snatching food.  They represent a force of destruction and chaos. |例句：The myth of the harpies features prominently in Greek literature.  希腊文学中哈耳庇厄的神话非常突出。|近义词：hag, fury, shrew, monster|反义词：goddess, angel
