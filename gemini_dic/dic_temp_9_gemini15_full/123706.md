moonflower:/ˈmuːnˌflaʊər/| n. |月光花，夕颜 |A moonflower is a type of flowering plant that blooms at night and closes during the day.  The flowers are often white or pale-colored and have a strong, sweet fragrance.  Many types exist, with varying sizes and colors.|例句：The moonflower opened its petals as the sun set. 月光花在日落时展开花瓣。

The moonflowers filled the garden with their sweet scent at night. 月光花在夜间用其甜美的香味充满了花园。|近义词：night-blooming cereus, evening glory|反义词：sunflower (blooms during the day)
