cormorants:/ˈkɔːrmərænts/| n. |鸬鹚|A large, dark-colored diving bird with a long neck and a hooked bill, found worldwide except in the polar regions. Cormorants are known for their fishing abilities, often diving deep to catch fish.|例句：The cormorants were diving gracefully into the water to catch fish. 鸬鹚优雅地潜入水中捕鱼。

例句：We saw a flock of cormorants perched on the rocks. 我们看到一群鸬鹚栖息在岩石上。
