reason:/ˈriːzən/ | n. | 理由，原因，理性 |  A cause, explanation, or justification for an action, event, or belief.  It can also refer to the power of the mind to think, understand, and form judgments. |例句：The reason for the delay was unforeseen circumstances.  延误的原因是不可预见的情况。

例句：He gave no reason for his absence. 他没有说明缺席的原因。


近义词：cause, explanation, justification, motive, rationale | 反义词：irrationality, illogicality, nonsense | 词性:名词

His reason for leaving is unknown. = 他离开的原因不明。
