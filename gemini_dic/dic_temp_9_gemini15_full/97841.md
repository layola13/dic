inlaid:/ɪnˈleɪd/| adj.|镶嵌的；嵌入的|Something that is inlaid has been decorated by having pieces of another material fitted into its surface.  For example, a table with inlaid wood has pieces of different colored or textured wood fitted into the surface of the table.|例句：The antique box was inlaid with mother-of-pearl. 这只古董盒子镶嵌着珍珠母。

The floor was inlaid with marble. 地板镶嵌着大理石。|近义词：embedded, encrusted|反义词：plain, undecorated|
