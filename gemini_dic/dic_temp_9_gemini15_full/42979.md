coveter:/ˈkoʊvɪtər/| v.|渴望者，贪婪者|To yearn intensely; to desire something that belongs to another.|例句：She coveted her sister's beautiful necklace. 她渴望拥有她姐姐那条美丽的项链。|近义词：crave, yearn, long for, desire|反义词：disdain, scorn


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He covets his neighbor's new car. = 他渴望拥有他邻居的新车。
They covet power and influence. = 他们渴望权力和影响力。


一般过去时:
She coveted that painting for years. = 她多年来一直渴望得到那幅画。
He coveted the position of CEO. = 他渴望担任首席执行官的职位。


现在完成时:
I have coveted that antique clock since I first saw it. = 自从我第一次看到那座古董钟以来，我就一直渴望拥有它。
They have coveted the prize for many months. = 他们已经渴望获得这个奖项好几个月了。


过去完成时:
She had coveted the diamond ring before it was even displayed. = 在那枚钻石戒指展出之前，她就渴望拥有它了。
He had coveted the promotion for quite some time. = 他渴望晋升已经有一段时间了。


将来时:
I will covet that property once I have the money. = 我一旦有钱就会渴望拥有那处房产。
They will covet the success of their rivals. = 他们会渴望获得像他们的竞争对手一样的成功。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would covet that island. = 如果我有钱，我会渴望拥有那个岛屿。
I wish I didn't covet other people's possessions. = 我希望我不渴望别人的财产。


被动语态:
The prize was coveted by many competitors. = 许多竞争者都渴望获得这个奖项。
The position is coveted by many ambitious young professionals. = 许多有抱负的年轻专业人士都渴望获得这个职位。


疑问句:
Do you covet anything at the moment? = 你现在渴望得到什么吗？
What do they covet most in life? = 他们生活中最渴望的是什么？


否定句:
He does not covet material possessions. = 他不渴望物质财富。
She did not covet her friend's success. = 她不嫉妒朋友的成功。


PHRASE->
covet something greatly = 极度渴望某物
covet power/wealth/success = 渴望权力/财富/成功
openly covet = 公开地渴望
secretly covet = 秘密地渴望
covet someone's position = 垂涎某人的职位
