incend: /ɪnˈsɛnd/ | vt. | 激怒；点燃；使燃烧 | to make someone very angry; to set something on fire | 例句：His remarks incensed the audience. 他的言论激怒了听众。

The fire quickly incensed the dry grass. 大火迅速点燃了干草。

近义词：infuriate, enrage, ignite, inflame | 反义词：calm, soothe, appease
