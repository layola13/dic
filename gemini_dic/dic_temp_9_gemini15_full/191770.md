sciography:/saɪˈɒɡrəfi/| n. | 影像学；阴影描绘法 | The study or depiction of shadows, especially as used in art or science to represent three-dimensional objects.  It involves the use of light and shadow to create an illusion of depth and form. | Example: The artist used sciography to create a realistic depiction of the landscape.  艺术家运用影像学来创作逼真的风景画。|近义词：shadowgraphy,  photogrammetry |反义词：photorealism (in the sense of eliminating shadow effects)
