parquets:/pɑːˈkeɪz/| n. |镶木地板|Parquets are decorative floor coverings made of small pieces of wood arranged in geometric patterns.  They are often considered a luxurious flooring option.|The ballroom floor was covered in beautiful, polished parquets.|近义词：wood flooring, inlaid flooring|反义词：carpet, tile
