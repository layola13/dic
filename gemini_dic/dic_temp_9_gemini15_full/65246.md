etym:/ˈɛtɪm/ | n. | 词源；词源学 | Etymology is the study of the origin of words and the way in which their meanings have changed throughout history.  It traces the development of words from their earliest forms to their current usage, exploring their historical context and influences from other languages. |  The etymology of the word "nice" is surprisingly complex. |近义词：derivation, origin |反义词：无
