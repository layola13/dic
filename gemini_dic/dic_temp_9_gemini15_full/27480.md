campanologer:/ˌkæmpənˈɒlədʒər/| n. |钟楼管理员；撞钟人 |A campanologer is a person who rings bells, especially church bells, or manages a bell tower.  They are skilled in the art of change ringing and often have a deep understanding of bell-ringing techniques and the history of bells. |The campanologer skillfully played the carillon, filling the square with beautiful melodies.  钟楼管理员熟练地演奏了卡里永，广场上回荡着优美的旋律。|bell ringer, bellmaster |  | noun
