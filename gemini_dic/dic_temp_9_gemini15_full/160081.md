pomace:/ˈpoʊmɪs/| n. |果渣，酒渣 |The residue remaining after the pressing of fruit, especially grapes, to extract juice.  It is often used in animal feed, cosmetics, and other products. |Example: The pomace from the winemaking process is rich in antioxidants.  酿酒过程中的果渣富含抗氧化剂。


The pomace was composted and used to fertilize the vineyard. 果渣被堆肥并用于给葡萄园施肥。|近义词：residue, marc, pulp |反义词：juice, extract
