enterotomy:/ˌɛntəˈrɑːtəmi/ | n. |肠切开术|The surgical incision into the intestine.  Enterotomy is a procedure performed to access the intestinal lumen for various reasons, including the removal of foreign bodies, repair of injuries, or treatment of obstructions.|Example: The veterinarian performed an enterotomy to remove the swallowed toy from the dog's intestine.  兽医进行了肠切开术，以去除狗肠道中吞下的玩具。|近义词：intestinal incision, laparotomy (if involving the abdomen)|反义词：intestinal closure, intestinal repair (as the opposite actions)|名词
