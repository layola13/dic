curatrix: /kjʊˈreɪtrɪks/ | n. | 女馆长；女管理者 | A woman who is in charge of a museum, art gallery, or other similar institution.  | The curatrix of the museum unveiled the new exhibit.  博物馆的女馆长揭开了新展览的面纱。| curator (近义词，但 curator 是男女皆可) |  (无明确反义词，可根据语境使用如 "employee", "visitor") | 名词
