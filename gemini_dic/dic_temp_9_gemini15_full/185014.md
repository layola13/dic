retestify:/ˌriːˈtɛstɪfaɪ/| vt.|重新作证；再次作证|To testify again; to give evidence again in a court of law.  |例句：The witness was asked to retestify after new evidence emerged.目击者在出现新的证据后被要求再次作证。|例句：The defense attorney decided to retestify their key witness.  辩护律师决定让他们的关键证人再次作证。|近义词：re-testify, re-affirm, reiterate|反义词：withdraw testimony, retract statement|动词
