crinel:/ˈkrɪnəl/| n.|发辫；（尤指妇女的）长发|A crinel is a woman's long hair, especially when worn in a braid or other elaborate style.  The word is now largely archaic or used in a literary context.|例句：She wore her crinel elaborately braided down her back. = 她把长发编成复杂的辫子披在背上。|例句：The painting depicted a lady with a magnificent crinel. = 这幅画描绘一位女士，她有着华丽的长发。|近义词：hair, tresses, locks|反义词：baldness, short hair|名词
