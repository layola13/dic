nonmanagement: /ˌnɑːnˈmænɪdʒmənt/ | adj. |非管理层的；非管理人员的 |  Relating to or characteristic of employees who are not in management positions.  This term typically refers to staff, workers, or other personnel who do not have supervisory or leadership responsibilities. |

例句：

The nonmanagement employees received a smaller raise than their managers.  非管理层员工的加薪幅度低于他们的经理。

The company's nonmanagement staff participated in a training program. 公司的非管理层员工参加了一个培训项目。


近义词：non-managerial, rank-and-file, subordinate


反义词：management, managerial, supervisory


词性：形容词
