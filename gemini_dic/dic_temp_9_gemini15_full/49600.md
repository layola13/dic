domestic: /dəˈmɛstɪk/ | adj. |国内的；家庭的；驯养的 | Relating to the home, country, or a person's household.  |例句：Domestic flights are usually cheaper than international flights. 国内航班通常比国际航班便宜。

例句：She enjoys domestic life and raising her children. 她喜欢家庭生活和抚养孩子。


近义词：home, internal, national, tame, household

反义词：foreign, international, wild, external


词性: 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat is a domestic animal. = 猫是一种家养动物。
This company focuses on the domestic market. = 这家公司专注于国内市场。

一般过去时:

They had already established a strong domestic presence before expanding internationally. = 他们在国际扩张之前就已经在国内建立了强大的影响力。

将来时:
We will be purchasing more domestic products in the future. = 我们将来会购买更多国产产品。
The company predicts strong domestic growth next year. = 公司预测明年国内经济强劲增长。

虚拟语气:
