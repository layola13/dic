scalary: /ˈskeɪləri/ | adj. | 标量的；数量的 | Relating to or denoting a scalar quantity. A scalar quantity is a quantity that has magnitude only, such as mass, length, or speed, as opposed to a vector quantity, which has both magnitude and direction. | Example: The scalar product of two vectors is a scalar quantity.  标量积是两个向量的标量。 | 近义词：scalar, quantitative | 反义词：vector
