overexploited:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsplɔɪtɪd/ | adj. |过度开发的，被过度利用的 |  Describes a resource or situation that has been used or exploited to an excessive degree, often resulting in depletion or damage.  | Example: The overexploited fishing grounds are now barren. 过度捕捞的渔场现在已经荒芜了。 The overexploited forests are losing biodiversity at an alarming rate. 被过度开发的森林正在以惊人的速度丧失生物多样性。|近义词：depleted, exhausted, drained, misused | 反义词：conserved, preserved, protected, sustainably managed |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The land is overexploited. = 这片土地被过度开发了。
The resources are overexploited. = 资源被过度开发了。


一般过去时:
The forest was overexploited last year. = 去年森林被过度开发了。
The fishery was overexploited in the past decade. = 在过去的十年里，渔业资源被过度开发了。


现在完成时:
The oil fields have been overexploited. = 油田已经被过度开采了。
The aquifer has been overexploited for decades. = 蓄水层已经被过度开采了几十年了。


过去完成时:
The mine had been overexploited before it was closed. = 这座矿山在关闭之前就已经被过度开采了。
The water table had been overexploited long before the drought. = 在干旱发生很久以前，地下水位就已经被过度开采了。


将来时:
The coral reefs will be overexploited if regulations are not enforced. = 如果不执行法规，珊瑚礁将会被过度开发。
The grassland will be overexploited if grazing continues at this rate. = 如果以这种速度继续放牧，草地将会被过度开发。


虚拟语气:

I wish we hadn't overexploited the forests. = 我真希望我们没有过度开发森林。


被动语态:

否定句:
