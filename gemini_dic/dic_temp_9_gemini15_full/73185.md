forbore:/fɔːˈbɔːr/|/fɔːrˈbɛər/| v.| 克制；忍耐；避免 | To refrain from doing something; to abstain; to forbear from doing something means to avoid doing it.  | He forbore from criticizing her work, despite his reservations. 他克制住自己，没有批评她的作品，尽管他有所保留。  |She forbore to mention the embarrassing incident. 她忍住没提那件尴尬的事。 | restrain, abstain, refrain | indulge, give in | 动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He forbears to comment on the matter. = 他避免评论此事。
They forbear from interfering in our affairs. = 他们避免干涉我们的事务。

一般过去时:
She forbore to express her anger. = 她忍住没有表达自己的愤怒。
He forbore criticizing his friend's mistakes. = 他忍住没有批评朋友的错误。

现在完成时:
I have forborne to answer his rude remarks. = 我一直忍住没有回答他无礼的言辞。
They have forborne from taking any action so far. = 到目前为止，他们一直没有采取任何行动。

过去完成时:
She had forborne mentioning the problem until it was too late. = 她忍到太迟了才提起这个问题。
He had forborne to interrupt, waiting for his chance to speak. = 他一直忍住没打断，等待着自己说话的机会。


将来时:
I shall forbear to judge until I have all the facts. = 在我掌握所有事实之前，我将避免做出判断。
We will forbear from making any hasty decisions. = 我们将避免做出任何仓促的决定。

虚拟语气:
If only I had forborne to speak then! = 要是我当时忍住不说就好了！
I wish I had forborne to interfere. = 我真希望我当时没有干涉。

被动语态:
Criticism should be forborne in this delicate situation. = 在这种微妙的局势下，应该避免批评。
His anger was forborne with great difficulty. = 他费了好大的劲才忍住怒气。

疑问句:
Would you forbear to ask such a personal question? = 你能否避免问这样一个私人问题？
Did he forbear to tell the truth? = 他是否忍住没说实话？

否定句:
He did not forbear to express his opinions. = 他没有忍住表达自己的意见。
She could not forbear laughing at his joke. = 她忍不住笑了他的笑话。


PHRASE->
forbear to do something  = 避免做某事
forbear with someone = 对某人忍耐
forbearance of  = 对...的忍耐
forbearance and patience = 忍耐和耐心
forbearance toward = 对...的容忍
