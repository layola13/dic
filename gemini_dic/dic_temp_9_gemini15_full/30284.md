cements:/sɪˈmɛnts/ | n. vt. |水泥；胶合剂；巩固 |

英文解析:
As a noun, "cement" refers to a powdery substance that is mixed with water to form a paste, which hardens to become a strong binding material used in construction.  As a verb, "cement" means to unite or strengthen something, often figuratively.

例句:
The workers used cement to build the wall. = 工人们用水泥砌墙。

The agreement cemented their friendship. = 这项协议巩固了他们的友谊。


近义词:
noun: concrete, mortar, glue, adhesive
verb: consolidate, strengthen, bind, unite


反义词:
noun:  (no direct antonym, but opposites could be things that weaken or separate)
verb: weaken, separate, divide, dissolve


词性:
noun, verb
