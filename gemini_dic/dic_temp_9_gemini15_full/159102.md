pocket: /ˈpɒkɪt/ /ˈpɑːkɪt/ | n. v. | 口袋；钱；（暗中）偷窃 |

英文解析:
Noun: A small bag sewn into clothing or a larger bag, used to carry small items.  Also refers to a small, secluded area or space, or a sum of money.  As a verb, it means to steal something secretly.

例句:
He put his hands in his pockets. 他把手插进口袋里。

She had some money in her pocket. 她的口袋里有一些钱。


近义词: pouch, receptacle,  (for money) funds, cash


反义词:  (no direct antonyms, depending on context)


词性: Noun, Verb


USAGE->

He pockets the money. 他把钱装进口袋。
She always keeps her phone in her pocket. 她总是把手机放在口袋里。

She has pocketed a significant amount of money. 她已经赚了一大笔钱。(隐含了获得的意思)


过去完成时:
He had pocketed the key before leaving. 他离开前已经把钥匙装进口袋了。
She had pocketed the winnings before anyone noticed. 她在任何人注意到之前就收起了奖金。


将来时:
