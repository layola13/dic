microsporic:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈspɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 微孢子的；产生微孢子的 | Relating to or characteristic of microspores.  Microspores are the small spores produced by plants that develop into male gametophytes. | Example:  The microsporic development in this species is unique.  这个物种的微孢子发育是独特的。 |近义词:microspore-producing|反义词:megasporic
