decamped:/dɪˈkæmpt/| v.| 突然离开；撤离；搬迁 | To leave a place suddenly, especially in order to escape from a dangerous or unpleasant situation.  It often implies a hasty or secret departure. | 例句：The soldiers decamped in the middle of the night.  士兵们在半夜突然撤离了。

The family decamped to the countryside for the summer.  这家人夏天搬到乡下去住了。


近义词：depart, leave, evacuate, abscond, flee | 反义词：arrive, settle, stay
