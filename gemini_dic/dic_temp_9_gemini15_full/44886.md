cryo:/ˈkraɪoʊ/| adj. n.|低温的；冷冻的；冷冻疗法|Relating to or involving low temperatures, especially the use of low temperatures for preserving biological tissue or for medical treatment.|Cryogenic surgery is a minimally invasive procedure that uses extremely cold temperatures to destroy abnormal tissue. 冷冻手术是一种微创手术，它利用极低的温度来破坏异常组织。|近义词：frigid, frozen|反义词：hot, warm
