reaccreditation:/ˌriːæˌkrɛdɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |重新认证，复审认证 |The process of being accredited again after a period of time.  This often involves demonstrating continued compliance with standards and requirements.  Reaccreditation ensures that an organization or individual maintains the necessary qualifications and competence. |Example: The university underwent reaccreditation this year and successfully retained its status.  今年大学进行了重新认证，并成功地保持了其地位。|近义词：recertification, renewal of accreditation |反义词：deaccreditation, loss of accreditation
