inflationist: /ɪnˈfleɪʃənɪst/ | n. | 通货膨胀论者，主张通货膨胀的人 | A person who advocates or believes in inflation as an economic policy.  Someone who supports inflationary policies. | Example:  The government's policies were criticized by many inflationists.  政府的政策受到了许多通货膨胀论者的批评。 | 近义词: inflation proponent, expansionist (in a monetary context) | 反义词: deflationist, anti-inflationist |  名词
