pre-congress:/ˌpriːˈkɒŋɡrəs/|/ˈpriːkɑːŋɡrəs/| adj. |会前的，大会前的 |Occurring or existing before a congress or convention.  It typically refers to events, meetings, or preparations that take place in the period leading up to a formal congress. |例句：The pre-congress workshops were well-attended. 会前研讨会很受欢迎。


例句：The pre-congress meeting helped to coordinate the agenda. 会前会议有助于协调议程。


近义词：antecedent, preliminary, prior


反义词：post-congress, subsequent


词性：形容词
