ill-founded:/ˌɪlˈfaʊndɪd/| adj.|缺乏根据的，没有根据的，站不住脚的 |  Based on inadequate or incorrect information; lacking a sound basis.  |例句：His accusations were ill-founded and caused unnecessary distress. 他的指控毫无根据，造成了不必要的痛苦。
His theory is ill-founded and lacks empirical evidence.他的理论缺乏根据，缺乏经验证据。|近义词：groundless, baseless, unfounded, unsubstantiated |反义词：well-founded, justified, valid
