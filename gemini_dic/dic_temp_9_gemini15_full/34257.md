clapperboard:/ˈklæpərˌbɔːrd/|/ˈklæpərˌbɔːrd/| n. |拍板；场记板|A device used in filmmaking to synchronize picture and sound, typically consisting of a hinged slate with a large number and scene information.  It is clapped before a take to mark the beginning of a scene for later synchronization.|The director yelled "Action!" after the clapperboard slammed shut.|近义词：slate, film slate|反义词:  None.
