cautioner: /ˈkɔːʃənər/ | n. | 保证人；担保人 | A person who guarantees the fulfillment of another person's obligations.  |  The bank required a cautioner before granting the loan.  这家银行在发放贷款前要求提供担保人。| guarantor, surety, bondsman |  (无明确反义词，可考虑debtor, principal) | 名词

词源:
