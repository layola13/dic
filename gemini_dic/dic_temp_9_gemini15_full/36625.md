collectivised:/kəˈlɛktaɪzd/ | v. | 集体化；公有化 | To bring under collective ownership or control.  The process of transforming private property into collective or state ownership. | Example:  The government collectivised the farms.  政府将农场集体化了。 The land was collectivised after the revolution.  革命后土地被集体化了。 | 近义词：nationalised, socialised, communised | 反义词：privatised, individualised


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They collectivise the farms every year. = 他们每年都将农场集体化。
The government collectivises industries to control resources. = 政府将产业集体化以控制资源。


一般过去时:
The Soviet Union collectivised agriculture in the 1930s. = 苏联在20世纪30年代对农业进行了集体化。
They collectivised the factories during the war. = 他们在战争期间将工厂集体化了。


现在完成时:
Many countries have collectivised their industries in the past. = 许多国家过去已经将它们的工业集体化了。
The government has collectivised most of the land. = 政府已经将大部分土地集体化了。


过去完成时:
Before the revolution, they had not yet collectivised the land. = 革命之前，他们尚未将土地集体化。
The party had collectivised the means of production before the economic crisis. = 在经济危机之前，党已经将生产资料集体化了。


将来时:
Will the government collectivise the banking sector? = 政府会将银行业集体化吗？
They will likely collectivise more resources in the future. = 他们将来可能会将更多资源集体化。


虚拟语气:
If the government collectivised the healthcare system, it might improve efficiency. = 如果政府将医疗体系集体化，可能会提高效率。
I wish they hadn't collectivised the fishing industry. = 我真希望他们没有将渔业集体化。


被动语态:
The farms were collectivised by the state. = 农场被国家集体化了。
The resources will be collectivised under the new policy. = 根据新政策，资源将被集体化。


疑问句:
Was the land collectivised peacefully? = 土地是和平地集体化吗？
Have all the factories been collectivised? = 所有工厂都被集体化了吗？


否定句:
The company did not collectivise its operations. = 这家公司没有将它的业务集体化。
They haven't yet collectivised the transportation system. = 他们尚未将交通系统集体化。
