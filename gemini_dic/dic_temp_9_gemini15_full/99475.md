interferer:/ˌɪntərˈfɪrər/| n. | 干涉者，干扰者 | A person who interferes in the affairs of others.  Someone or something that causes problems or disruption. | Example: He became an interferer in his neighbor's family matters. 他成了他邻居家务事的干涉者。  The dog was a constant interferer during the recording session. 这只狗在录音过程中不断干扰。 |近义词： meddler, busybody, intruder |反义词：helper, bystander
