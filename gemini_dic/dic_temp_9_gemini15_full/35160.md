clowe:/kloʊ/| n.|丁香(植物)|A clove is a dried flower bud of a tropical evergreen tree.  It is used as a spice.|Example: I added a few cloves to the stew for extra flavor. 我在炖菜里加了几颗丁香以增加风味。|近义词：spice, aromatic|反义词：无|名词
