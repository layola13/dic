problematically:/ˌprɒbləˈmætɪkli/| /ˌprɑːbləˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 有问题地；令人费解地；麻烦地 |  In a way that causes problems or difficulties; in a way that is difficult to deal with or explain.  | 例句：The project is proceeding problematically.  = 这个项目进展得很不顺利。

例句：His explanation was problematically vague. = 他的解释含糊不清，令人难以理解。


近义词：troublesomely,  difficultly,  questionably,  dubiously


反义词：easily, simply, straightforwardly


词性：副词
