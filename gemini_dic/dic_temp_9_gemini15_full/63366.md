entomologists:/ˌɛntəˈmɒlədʒɪsts/| n. |昆虫学家|Scientists who study insects.  Entomologists research insect behavior, physiology, ecology, and classification. They may work in universities, museums, government agencies, or private companies. | Many entomologists are involved in pest control research. 许多昆虫学家参与害虫防治研究。| zoologists, biologists |  (no clear antonym)|名词
