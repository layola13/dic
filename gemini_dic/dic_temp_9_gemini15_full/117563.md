megadyne:/ˈmɛɡədaɪn/| n. |兆达因|A megadyne is a unit of force equal to one million dynes.  It's a relatively large unit rarely used in modern contexts, often replaced by the SI unit of force, the newton.  |例句：The impact force was measured in megadynes.  冲击力以兆达因测量。

The force exerted by a large rocket engine might be measured in megadynes. 大型火箭发动机的推力可以用兆达因来衡量。|近义词：newton (although much smaller)|反义词：dyne (one millionth of a megadyne)


词源：mega- (million) + dyne (unit of force)
