embracive:/ɪmˈbreɪsɪv/| adj. |包容的，包含的，全面的|  Comprehensive; including or covering many things. It suggests a welcoming and encompassing nature.  |例句：His philosophy is embracive of many different beliefs.他的哲学包容许多不同的信仰。

例句：The new policy has an embracive approach to solving the problem. 新政策采取了一种包容性的方法来解决这个问题。


近义词：inclusive, comprehensive, encompassing, broad, wide-ranging

反义词：exclusive, limited, narrow, restrictive


词源：来自动词embrace (拥抱，包含)，加后缀-ive (形容词后缀)形成形容词。  The root is the Old French embracer, from the Latin *inbracciare,  a compound of in- (“in”) + bracchium (“arm”).
