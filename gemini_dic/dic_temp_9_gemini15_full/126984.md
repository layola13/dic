narwhals:/ˈnɑːrwɔːlz/| n. |独角鲸|A narwhal is an Arctic whale with a long, spiraled tusk that projects from its upper jaw.  Narwhals are known for their distinctive tusk, which is actually an elongated tooth. They are social animals and live in pods. |例句：The researchers observed a pod of narwhals swimming beneath the ice. 研究人员观察到一群独角鲸在冰层下游泳。

例句：We saw a narwhal surfacing briefly before diving back down. 我们看到一只独角鲸短暂地浮出水面，然后又潜了下去。


近义词：Arctic whale, toothed whale |反义词：无
