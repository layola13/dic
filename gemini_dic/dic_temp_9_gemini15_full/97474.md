ingeneration:/ˌɪndʒɛnəˈreɪʃən/| n. |一代人；同代人|The process of regenerating or being regenerated.|The younger generation is more tech-savvy than the older generation. 年轻一代比老一代更精通科技。|近义词：generation, cohort |反义词:N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This generation prefers online communication. = 这代人更喜欢线上交流。
The younger generation values experiences over possessions. = 年轻一代更重视体验而不是物质。


一般过去时:
The previous generation faced many hardships. = 上一代人经历了许多苦难。
My parents' generation witnessed significant social changes. = 我父母那一代见证了重大的社会变革。


现在完成时:
Several generations have contributed to this technology. = 几代人共同促进了这项技术的进步。
This company has spanned multiple generations of family ownership. = 这家公司已经历了数代家族所有制。


过去完成时:
Before the war, several generations had lived peacefully in the village. = 在战争爆发前，几代人都和平地生活在这个村庄里。
By the time she retired, three generations had worked in the family business. = 等她退休时，三代人都曾在家族企业工作。


将来时:
Future generations will inherit the problems we create today. = 后代将继承我们今天创造的问题。
The next generation will likely use even more advanced technology. = 下一代人可能会使用更先进的技术。


虚拟语气:
If only previous generations had taken better care of the environment! = 但愿前几代人能更好地保护环境！
I wish my generation had been more proactive in addressing climate change. = 我希望我们这一代在应对气候变化方面更积极主动。


被动语态:
This issue has been debated by many generations. = 这个问题已经被几代人争论了。
The traditions were passed down through generations. = 这些传统代代相传。


疑问句:
How many generations have lived in this house? = 几代人住在这所房子里？
What are the key differences between the two generations? = 这两代人之间的主要区别是什么？


否定句:
This problem cannot be solved by one generation alone. = 这个问题不能仅靠一代人解决。
Not all members of that generation shared the same views. = 并非那一代的所有人都持有相同的观点。
