presurveying: /ˌpriːˈsɜːveɪɪŋ/ | v. | 预先勘测；预先调查 |  The act of conducting a survey before a main project or undertaking, often to gather preliminary information or assess feasibility.  This involves gathering data and information in advance to inform planning and decision-making. |  The construction company is presurveying the land to determine the best location for the new building.  建筑公司正在预先勘测土地，以确定新建建筑的最佳位置。


近义词：preliminary survey, reconnaissance survey, advance survey

反义词：post-survey, follow-up survey
