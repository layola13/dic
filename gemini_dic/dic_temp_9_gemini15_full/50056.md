denovo:/ˈdeɪnˈoʊvoʊ/| adj.| 从头开始的；全新的 | anew; afresh; from the beginning;  starting from scratch. The term signifies something is created or developed from scratch, without reliance on pre-existing materials or structures. It is often used in scientific contexts to describe processes or experiments that are designed and conducted without using any previous data or results.| 例句：The experiment was conducted de novo, using only newly synthesized materials.  该实验是从头开始进行的，只使用了新合成的材料。

The company decided to create a de novo business plan. 公司决定制定一个全新的商业计划。| 近义词：afresh, anew, from scratch| 反义词：pre-existing, derived, secondary
