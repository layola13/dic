hierolatry:/ˌhaɪəˈrɒlətri/| n. |圣物崇拜；神像崇拜 |The worship of sacred objects or images.  This practice often involves a belief that the object possesses inherent sacred power or is a conduit to the divine.  |Example:  The ancient civilization's hierolatry extended to the worship of natural elements, such as the sun and moon.  古代文明的圣物崇拜延伸到对自然元素，如太阳和月亮的崇拜。|近义词：idolatry, iconolatry |反义词：iconoclasm
