metaphysics:/ˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪks/| n. |形而上学，玄学|The branch of philosophy that deals with the fundamental nature of reality and existence, including concepts such as being, knowing, identity, time, and space.  It explores questions beyond the physical and empirical world, often concerning the nature of consciousness, free will, and the relationship between mind and matter. |Example:  He studied metaphysics in graduate school. 他在研究生院学习形而上学。

The study of metaphysics delves into the nature of reality.  形而上学的研究深入探讨了现实的本质。|近义词：ontology, cosmology, theology|反义词：physics, empiricism
