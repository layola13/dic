conscious-of: /ˈkɒnʃəs ɒv/ | adj. | 意识到，察觉到 |  Aware of; having knowledge or perception of.  This phrase emphasizes the awareness and understanding of something. |

例句：
She was conscious of his gaze. = 她意识到他在注视她。

He became conscious of a strange smell. = 他察觉到一股奇怪的气味。


近义词：aware of, mindful of, cognizant of, sensible of


反义词：unaware of, oblivious to, ignorant of


词性：形容词 (adjectival phrase)


USAGE->  (Since "conscious-of" is an adjectival phrase, it doesn't conjugate like a verb.  The tense and voice are determined by the verb in the main clause.)

一般现在时:
He is conscious of the risks involved. = 他意识到其中存在的风险。
She is conscious of her own limitations. = 她意识到自身的局限性。


一般过去时:
They were conscious of the ticking clock. = 他们意识到钟表的滴答声。
He was conscious of the growing tension in the room. = 他意识到房间里越来越紧张的气氛。


现在完成时:
I have been conscious of a change in his behavior. = 我已经意识到他行为上的变化。
She has always been conscious of her social responsibilities. = 她一直都很清楚自己的社会责任。


过去完成时:
They had been conscious of the danger for some time. = 他们已经意识到危险有一段时间了。
I had been conscious of a growing unease before the accident. = 事故发生前，我一直感到不安。


将来时:
We will be conscious of the need for caution. = 我们将会意识到谨慎的必要性。
He will be conscious of the consequences of his actions. = 他将意识到自己行为的后果。


虚拟语气:
If I were conscious of any wrongdoing, I would confess. = 如果我意识到任何不当行为，我会承认。
I wish I had been more conscious of the cultural differences. = 我希望我当时更能意识到文化差异。


被动语态: (The passive voice is less common with "conscious-of" because it focuses on the awareness of the subject, not what is being acted upon.)
The risks involved were consciously considered. = 其中存在的风险得到了谨慎的考虑。(This uses a different word, but demonstrates the idea of passive voice)
The need for caution was made apparent; all were conscious of it. = 谨慎的必要性变得显而易见；大家都意识到了。(This avoids passive voice on "conscious-of" itself)


疑问句:
Are you conscious of the implications? = 你意识到这些含义吗？
Was he conscious of the potential dangers? = 他意识到潜在的危险吗？


否定句:
He was not conscious of the error. = 他没有意识到这个错误。
They were unconscious of the seriousness of the situation. = 他们没有意识到情况的严重性。(Note: Uses "unconscious" as the direct opposite)
