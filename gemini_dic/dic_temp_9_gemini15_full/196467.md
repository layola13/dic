serv:/sɜːrv/| /sɝv/| vt. vi. n.|服务；侍候；供应；服役|to perform duties or provide a service for someone or something; to be of use or benefit to someone or something. |例句：He served in the army for ten years. 他在军队服役十年了。|例句：The restaurant serves delicious food. 这家餐馆供应美味的食物。|近义词：help, assist, aid, supply, minister|反义词：hinder, obstruct, impede|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He serves the community well. = 他很好地服务于社区。
She serves breakfast every morning. = 她每天早上供应早餐。

一般过去时:
They served in the war. = 他们参加了战争。
He served me a cup of tea. = 他给我倒了一杯茶。

现在完成时:
I have served this company for five years. = 我为这家公司服务了五年。
She has served many clients successfully. = 她成功地为许多客户服务过。

过去完成时:
He had served his country before retiring. = 他退休前为国家服务过。
They had served dinner before the guests arrived. = 客人们到达之前他们已经摆好了晚餐。

将来时:
We will serve dinner at 7 p.m. = 我们将在晚上7点供应晚餐。
She will serve on the committee next year. = 她明年将在委员会任职。

虚拟语气:
If he served his country, he would feel proud. = 如果他为国家服务，他会感到自豪。
I wish I had served more people in my life. = 我希望我一生中能为更多的人服务。

被动语态:
The food was served promptly. = 食物很快就上来了。
The customers are served by friendly staff. = 客户由友好的员工服务。

疑问句:
Does this restaurant serve vegetarian food? = 这家餐馆供应素食吗？
Will you serve on the jury? = 你会担任陪审员吗？

否定句:
He didn't serve in the military. = 他没有服过兵役。
The waitress didn't serve us for a long time. = 服务员很久才来为我们服务。
