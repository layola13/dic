oxyphenol:/ˌɒksiˈfiːnɒl/ | n. | 氧酚 | A chemical compound containing both a hydroxyl (-OH) group and an aromatic ring. Oxyphenols are a broad class of compounds with various properties and applications, often exhibiting antioxidant and antimicrobial activity. Many are found naturally in plants, while others are synthetic.
