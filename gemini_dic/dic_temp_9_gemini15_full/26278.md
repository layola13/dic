buttonweed: /ˈbʌtənˌwiːd/ | n. |纽扣草，马齿苋属植物 | A low-growing plant with small, round leaves resembling buttons.  It's often considered a weed. | Example:  The buttonweed spread rapidly across the lawn.  纽扣草迅速蔓延到草坪上。|近义词：chickweed, purslane (depending on the specific species)|反义词：无
