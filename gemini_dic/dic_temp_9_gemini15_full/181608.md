relatives:/ˈrelətɪvz/| n.|亲戚，亲属|People related by blood or marriage.|My relatives came to visit me for the holidays. 我的亲戚来我家过节了。|近义词：kin, family, relations|反义词：strangers


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My relatives live in a small town. = 我的亲戚住在一个小镇上。
His relatives visit him every Sunday. = 他的亲戚每周日去看望他。


一般过去时:
We visited our relatives last summer. = 我们去年夏天去看望了亲戚。
Her relatives helped her move house. = 她的亲戚帮她搬家。


现在完成时:
I haven't seen my relatives for a long time. = 我很久没见过我的亲戚了。
They have recently welcomed new relatives into their family. = 他们最近迎来了新的家庭成员。


过去完成时:
I had already contacted my relatives before the trip. = 我在旅行前已经联系过我的亲戚了。
She had met her relatives several times before the wedding. = 婚礼前她见过几次她的亲戚。


将来时:
I will visit my relatives next week. = 我下周要去看望我的亲戚。
We will be seeing our relatives during the Christmas holiday. = 圣诞节期间我们会去看望亲戚。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would visit my relatives more often. = 如果我有更多时间，我会更经常去看望我的亲戚。
I wish my relatives lived closer. = 我希望我的亲戚住得近一些。


被动语态:
