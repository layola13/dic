recoils:/rɪˈkɔɪl/| v.|畏缩；反冲；退却 | to suddenly move back from something dangerous or unpleasant; the sudden backward movement of a gun after it has been fired. |例句：He recoiled in horror at the sight of the blood. 他看到血后惊恐地退缩了。|The gun recoiled violently when he fired it. 他开枪时枪猛烈地后坐。|近义词：shrink, flinch, recoil from|反义词：advance, approach


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He recoils from violence. = 他厌恶暴力。
She recoils at the thought of spiders. = 她一想到蜘蛛就害怕。


一般过去时:
The horse recoiled from the loud noise. = 马匹因巨大的噪音而受惊后退。
He recoiled in disgust at the smell. = 他闻到那个味道恶心想吐。


现在完成时:
She has recoiled from public life since the scandal. = 自丑闻发生以来，她已经退出了公众生活。
He has recoiled from his past mistakes. = 他已经从过去的错误中吸取教训了。


过去完成时:
They had recoiled from the challenge before even trying. = 他们在尝试之前就已经退缩了。
She had recoiled at the sight of the snake before running away. = 在逃跑之前，她已经对蛇的出现感到害怕了。


将来时:
He will recoil from any responsibility. = 他将逃避任何责任。
She will recoil at the suggestion of marriage. = 她会对结婚的建议感到反感。


虚拟语气:
If I saw a snake, I would recoil in fear. = 如果我看到蛇，我会害怕地退缩。
I wish I hadn't recoiled from the opportunity. = 我真希望我没有错过那个机会。


被动语态:
The soldier was recoiled by the explosion. = 士兵被爆炸震退了。(This usage is less common)
The audience was recoiled by the actor's horrific performance.  (观众被演员可怕的表演吓退了。) (This usage is less common)


疑问句:
Does he recoil from confrontation? = 他会回避冲突吗？
Will she recoil at the truth? = 她会害怕真相吗？


否定句:
He didn't recoil from the challenge. = 他没有回避挑战。
She didn't recoil from her responsibilities. = 她没有逃避自己的责任。


PHRASE->

recoil from: 躲避；退避
recoil in horror: 惊恐地退缩
recoil in disgust: 厌恶地退缩
recoil with fear: 害怕地退缩
recoil at the thought of: 一想到...就害怕
