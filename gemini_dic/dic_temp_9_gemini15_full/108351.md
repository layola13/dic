laver:/ˈlævər/| n. |洗衣盆；洗涤槽；(尤指)洗脸盆| A laver is a basin, typically for washing.  It's often used in the context of washing faces or hands, but can also refer to a larger basin for washing clothes or other items. |例句：She splashed water on her face in the laver. = 她用洗脸盆里的水洗了脸。

The old house had a stone laver in the corner of the bathroom. = 老房子浴室的角落里有一个石头做的洗脸盆。|近义词：basin, washbowl, sink |反义词:  None specifically, but could be contrasted with a shower or bathtub.
