masai:/məˈsaɪ/| n.|马赛人；马赛语|The Maasai are a Nilotic ethnic group inhabiting southern Kenya and northern Tanzania.|例句：The Maasai people are known for their distinctive culture and traditions.  马赛人以其独特的文化和传统而闻名。|近义词：Maasai people, Maasai tribe|反义词：无


USAGE->
This word is a proper noun and doesn't have verb conjugations like "is," "are," "was," etc.  The grammar applies to phrases *using* the word.  For example:


一般现在时:
The Maasai live in Kenya and Tanzania. = 马赛人居住在肯尼亚和坦桑尼亚。
Many Maasai are pastoralists. = 许多马赛人是牧民。


一般过去时:
The Maasai migrated to this area centuries ago. = 马赛人几个世纪前迁移到这个地区。
My friend visited a Maasai village last year. = 我的朋友去年参观了一个马赛村庄。


现在完成时:
The Maasai have maintained their traditions for generations. = 马赛人世世代代保持着他们的传统。
Researchers have studied the Maasai culture extensively. = 研究人员对马赛文化进行了广泛的研究。


过去完成时:
Before the arrival of Europeans, the Maasai had controlled a vast territory. = 在欧洲人到来之前，马赛人控制着广阔的领土。
The Maasai had already moved their cattle before the drought hit. = 在干旱来临之前，马赛人已经转移了他们的牲畜。


将来时:
The Maasai will continue to face challenges to their way of life. = 马赛人将继续面临对其生活方式的挑战。
Scientists predict that the Maasai will need to adapt to climate change. = 科学家预测马赛人需要适应气候变化。


虚拟语气:
If the Maasai had more land, they would have more grazing for their cattle. = 如果马赛人有更多的土地，他们的牲畜就会有更多的牧场。
It is important that the Maasai retain their cultural identity. = 保持马赛人的文化认同非常重要。


被动语态:
The Maasai culture is being threatened by modernization. = 马赛文化正受到现代化的威胁。
Their land has been encroached upon by settlers. = 他们的土地受到了定居者的侵占。


疑问句:
Are the Maasai still practicing their traditional customs? = 马赛人还在实践他们的传统习俗吗？
What are the biggest challenges facing the Maasai today? = 今天马赛人面临的最大挑战是什么？


否定句:
The Maasai are not always welcoming to outsiders. = 马赛人并不总是欢迎外人。
The Maasai did not originally live in this region. = 马赛人最初并不居住在这个地区。
