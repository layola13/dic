regardless-of:/rɪˈɡɑːrdləs ɒv/| preposition|不管；不顾；无论|Regardless of the weather, we will go hiking.  This phrase indicates that something will happen irrespective of a particular factor.  It emphasizes that the main action is unaffected by the condition mentioned.|Regardless of the cost, we must finish the project. = 不管成本多少，我们必须完成这个项目。


He went ahead regardless of the warnings. = 他不顾警告继续前进。

近义词：despite, notwithstanding, irrespective of

反义词：considering, taking into account, depending on

词性:介词
