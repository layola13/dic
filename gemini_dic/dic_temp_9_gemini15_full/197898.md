shirk:/ʃɜːrk/ | vt. | 逃避；推卸；偷懒 | To avoid or neglect (a duty or responsibility). |例句：He shirked his responsibilities as a father. 他逃避了他作为父亲的责任。 |例句：She tried to shirk the work, but her boss noticed. 她试图逃避工作，但她的老板注意到了。 |近义词：evade, avoid, dodge, shun |反义词：face, confront, undertake
