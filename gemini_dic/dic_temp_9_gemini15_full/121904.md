missed:/mɪst/| v. |错过，想念，漏掉 |To fail to catch or attend; to feel the absence of someone or something; to fail to notice or include. |例句：I missed the bus this morning. = 我今天早上错过了公共汽车。
I miss my family very much. = 我非常想念我的家人。|近义词：lost, overlooked, neglected |反义词：attended, caught, included |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I miss my friends. = 我想念我的朋友们。
He misses the old days. = 他怀念过去的日子。

一般过去时:
I missed the train yesterday. = 我昨天错过了火车。
She missed the meeting because she was sick. = 她因为生病错过了会议。

现在完成时:
I have missed several calls today. = 我今天错过了好几个电话。
We have missed many opportunities. = 我们错过了许多机会。

过去完成时:
I had missed the deadline by a day. = 我错过截止日期一天了。
They had missed the chance to win the game. = 他们错过了赢得比赛的机会。

将来时:
I will miss the party if I don't hurry. = 如果我不快点，我会错过派对。
We will miss the sunset if we don't leave now. = 如果我们现在不走，我们会错过日落。

虚拟语气:
I wish I hadn't missed the concert. = 我真希望我没有错过这场音乐会。
If I hadn't missed the flight, I would be in Paris now. = 如果我没有错过航班，我现在就在巴黎了。

被动语态:
The meeting was missed by many people. = 很多人错过了会议。
The opportunity was missed due to lack of preparation. = 由于准备不足，机会错过了。

疑问句:
Did you miss the game? = 你错过比赛了吗？
What did you miss in class today? = 你今天在课堂上错过了什么？

否定句:
I didn't miss a single detail. = 我没有错过任何细节。
He didn't miss the chance to speak. = 他没有错过说话的机会。
