reassigned:/ˌriːəˈsaɪnd/| vt.|重新分配；重新指派|To assign (something) again or to a different person or group.|The project was reassigned to a different team.  项目被重新分配给另一个团队。|近义词：reassigned, reallocated, redistributed|反义词：assigned, allocated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reassign tasks regularly. = 他们定期重新分配任务。
The manager reassigns responsibilities as needed. = 经理根据需要重新分配职责。


一般过去时:
The teacher reassigned the seats. = 老师重新安排了座位。
He reassigned the file to a new folder. = 他将文件重新分配到一个新的文件夹。


现在完成时:
The company has reassigned several employees this year. = 今年公司已经重新分配了几名员工。
They have reassigned the budget for the project. = 他们已经重新分配了该项目的预算。


过去完成时:
She had reassigned the project before the deadline. = 她在截止日期之前已经重新分配了项目。
The boss had reassigned the work before the meeting. = 老板在会议之前已经重新分配了工作。


将来时:
They will reassign the resources next week. = 他们下周将重新分配资源。
The manager will reassign the project to a more experienced team. = 经理将把项目重新分配给一个更有经验的团队。


虚拟语气:
If they reassigned the budget, we could finish the project. = 如果他们重新分配预算，我们就能完成项目。
I wish they had reassigned me to a different department. = 我希望他们把我重新分配到不同的部门。


被动语态:
The tasks were reassigned to the new team. = 任务被重新分配给新团队。
The project was reassigned due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，项目被重新分配。


疑问句:
Were the responsibilities reassigned? = 责任是否被重新分配了？
Will the project be reassigned? = 项目会被重新分配吗？


否定句:
The work wasn't reassigned. = 工作没有被重新分配。
They didn't reassign the project. = 他们没有重新分配项目。
