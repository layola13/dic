peacing:/ˈpiːsɪŋ/| v.|  和平地离开；平静地结束；(非正式)溜走 | To leave peacefully; to bring something to a calm end; (informal) to leave quietly and secretly. | 例句：He peaced out of the party early. 他早早地离开了派对。  She peaced the argument by offering a compromise. 她通过提出妥协来平息争论。 | 近义词：departing, leaving, exiting, escaping, skedaddling | 反义词：arriving, entering, confronting, staying
