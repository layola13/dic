dealership:/ˈdiːlərʃɪp/| n.|经销商；代理权；经销店|A business that sells or distributes a particular product or service, especially cars.  It can also refer to the right or authority to sell a product.|例句：He works at a car dealership. 他在一家汽车经销店工作。

例句：The company has secured a dealership for its new line of products. 这家公司已经获得了其新产品线的经销权。


近义词：agency, distributorship, franchise

反义词：manufacture, production

The dealership sells a wide variety of cars. = 这家经销商销售各种各样的汽车。
