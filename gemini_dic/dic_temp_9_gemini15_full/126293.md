myocardium:/ˌmaɪəˈkɑːrdiəm/|/ˈmaɪəʊkɑːrdɪəm/| n. |心肌|The myocardium is the muscular tissue of the heart.  It is responsible for pumping blood throughout the body.  |例句：The heart attack damaged a significant portion of his myocardium. 这场心脏病发作损害了他很大一部分心肌。|例句：The doctor examined the patient's myocardium for any signs of disease.医生检查了病人心肌是否有任何疾病迹象。|近义词：heart muscle|反义词：无|名词
