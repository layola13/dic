markets:/ˈmɑːrkɪts/| n.|市场；集市；销路；市场需求|A market is a place where goods and services are bought and sold.  It can refer to a physical location like a farmers' market or a broader concept like the stock market.|例句：The farmers' market is bustling with activity every Saturday. 每周六农贸市场都热闹非凡。|例句：The company is trying to expand into new markets. 这家公司正试图拓展新的市场。|近义词：marketplace, exchange, trade|反义词：monopoly
