dashed-off:/dæʃt ˈɔːf/ | adj. |仓促完成的，草率的 |Quickly produced or done without much care or attention to detail.  The writing style is characterized by a lack of precision and polish, often reflecting the hasty nature of its creation. |例句：He dashed off a quick email before the meeting. 他在会议前匆匆写了一封邮件。

例句：The painting felt dashed off and lacked depth. 这幅画感觉很仓促，缺乏深度。

近义词：hasty, cursory, perfunctory, slapdash

反义词：meticulous, painstaking, thorough, deliberate

词性：形容词
