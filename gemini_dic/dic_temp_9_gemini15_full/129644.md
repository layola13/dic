non-count:/ˈnɑːnkaʊnt/| adj.|不可数的|Uncountable; referring to nouns that cannot be counted individually and do not have a plural form.  These nouns typically refer to substances, concepts, or abstractions.|Examples:  water, information, advice, furniture|近义词：uncountable, mass|反义词：countable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I have some information. = 我有一些信息。
There is enough water. = 有足够的水。


一般过去时:
He gave me good advice. = 他给了我一些好的建议。
She needed more furniture. = 她需要更多家具。


现在完成时:
We have learned a lot of information. = 我们已经学习了很多信息。
They have consumed much water. = 他们已经消耗了很多水。


过去完成时:
I had already given him the advice. = 我已经给他提过建议了。
She had bought all the furniture. = 她已经买齐了所有的家具。


将来时:
I will need more information. = 我将需要更多信息。
They will use less water. = 他们将使用更少的水。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would give you more advice. = 如果我有更多时间，我会给你更多建议。
I wish I had more furniture. = 我希望我有更多家具。


被动语态:
The information was shared with everyone. = 信息与大家共享。
The water is being purified. = 水正在被净化。


疑问句:
How much water do you need? = 你需要多少水？
How much information do you have? = 你有多少信息？


否定句:
I don't have any furniture. = 我没有任何家具。
There isn't enough water. = 水不够。


PHRASE->
a lot of = 许多
plenty of = 充足的
an amount of = 一定数量的
a piece of (advice, information, etc.) = 一条（建议，信息等）
a grain of (sand, salt, etc.) = 一粒（沙子，盐等）
