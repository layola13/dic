quitting: /ˈkwɪtɪŋ/ | v.ing| 停止；放弃；辞职；戒除 | The act of stopping or ceasing an activity, job, or habit.  | 例句：He's considering quitting his job. 他正在考虑辞职。  |近义词：stopping, ceasing, giving up, resigning | 反义词：continuing, persisting, starting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is quitting smoking. = 他正在戒烟。
She is quitting her job next week. = 她下周要辞职。


一般过去时:
I quit my job last year. = 我去年辞了职。
He quit smoking cold turkey. = 他一下子就戒了烟。


现在完成时:
She has quit drinking. = 她已经戒酒了。
He has quit his job and started his own business. = 他已经辞职，自己创业了。


过去完成时:
He had quit his job before he moved to the city. = 他搬到城里之前就已经辞职了。
She had quit smoking for a year before she started again. = 她戒烟一年后又开始抽了。


将来时:
I will quit my job when I find a new one. = 我找到新工作后就会辞职。
He will quit smoking next month. = 他下个月将戒烟。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would quit that job. = 如果我是你，我会辞掉那份工作。
I wish I could quit this bad habit. = 我真希望我能改掉这个坏习惯。


被动语态:
The project was quit due to lack of funding. = 由于资金不足，项目被终止了。
His application was quit without explanation. = 他的申请未作解释就被拒绝了。


疑问句:
Are you quitting your job? = 你要辞职吗？
Is he quitting smoking for good this time? = 他这次真的要戒烟了吗？


否定句:
I'm not quitting my job. = 我不会辞职。
He's not quitting smoking easily. = 他戒烟不容易。



PHRASE->
quit smoking = 戒烟
quit drinking = 戒酒
quit a job = 辞职
quit school = 退学
quit cold turkey =  立刻戒掉（瘾）
