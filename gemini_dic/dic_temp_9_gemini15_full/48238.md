decontaminations:/ˌdiːkənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃənz/| n. |净化；消毒；去污|The process of removing contaminants from something, making it clean and safe.  This often involves removing harmful substances such as bacteria, viruses, chemicals, or radiation. |例句：The decontaminations of the building after the chemical spill took several days.  建筑物在化学品泄漏后的净化工作花了几天时间。

例句：Thorough decontaminations are essential in hospital operating rooms. 医院手术室彻底的净化至关重要。


近义词：purification, sterilization, cleansing |反义词：contamination, pollution |词性：名词
