acknowledgment:/əkˈnɒlɪdʒmənt/ | n. |承认；感谢；确认 |The act of acknowledging something; recognition; admission.  It can also refer to an expression of gratitude. |例句：The company sent an acknowledgment of my order. 公司已发出我的订单确认函。|例句：He received an acknowledgment of his contribution to the project. 他收到了对他为项目做出的贡献的认可。|近义词：recognition, acceptance, appreciation, thanks |反义词：denial, disregard, ignorance
