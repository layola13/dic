intermarries: /ˌɪntərˈmæriz/ | v. |通婚；异族通婚 |  To marry between different groups or races.  The verb implies a reciprocal action, suggesting marriage between members of distinct social, ethnic, or religious groups. |

例句：
Many families in the village intermarry, creating a strong sense of community. = 村里许多家庭通婚，营造了强烈的社区意识。

The two tribes have a long history of intermarrying. = 这两个部落有着长期通婚的历史。


近义词：marry across groups, interbreed (in the context of social groups)

反义词：marry within one's own group, endogamy


词性: 动词
