The researchers found that the metabolizable energy of the new formula was significantly higher. 研究人员发现，新配方的可代谢能量显著提高。


近义词：digestible, usable (in a metabolic context) | 反义词：indigestible, unmetabolizable


词源:  The word "metabolizable" is derived from the verb "metabolize," which itself comes from the Greek words "metabole" (μεταβολή), meaning "change," and "ballein" (βάλλειν), meaning "to throw" or "to cast."  Therefore, the root meaning suggests a process of transformation or change within the body.  The suffix "-able" indicates capability or possibility.
