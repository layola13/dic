minimap:/ˈmɪnɪmæp/| n.|小地图，缩略图|A small map showing a reduced-scale view of a larger area, often used in video games and other applications to aid navigation.|例句：The minimap showed my character's location relative to the enemy base. 小地图显示了我的角色相对于敌方基地的位置。  The minimap helped me navigate the vast and complex world. 小地图帮助我在这广阔而复杂的世界中导航。|近义词：overview map, thumbnail map|反义词：full map, large-scale map
