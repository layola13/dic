noninvincibleness:/ˌnɒnɪnˈvɪnsɪblənəs/ | n. |不可战胜性，不败性 | The state or quality of being not invincible; inability to be conquered or overcome.  This word is quite rare and largely theoretical, focusing on the lack of invincibility.  It describes a situation where something that might seem invincible actually has vulnerabilities. | Example: Despite his initial success, the general's belief in his own noninvincibleness led to his eventual downfall.  尽管他最初取得了成功，将军对自身不可战胜性的信念最终导致了他的失败。|近义词：vulnerability, susceptibility, weakness |反义词：invincibility, invincibleness
