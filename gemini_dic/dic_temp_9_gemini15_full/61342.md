embedding:/ɪmˈbedɪŋ/ | n. |嵌入，植入；嵌入式系统|The process of representing discrete data objects in a continuous vector space.  This allows for capturing semantic relationships between the objects.  For example, in natural language processing, word embeddings represent words as vectors, where similar words have similar vectors.  In other contexts, it can refer to the physical act of embedding something within another object.|例句：Word embeddings are crucial for many natural language processing tasks. 词嵌入对于许多自然语言处理任务至关重要。

The image was embedded in the document. 图片被嵌入文档中。|近义词：insertion, implantation, integration |反义词：extraction, removal |名词
