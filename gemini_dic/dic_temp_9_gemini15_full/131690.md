noncondensable:/ˌnɒnkənˈdensəbl/| adj.|不凝结的，不冷凝的|A substance that does not condense into a liquid at a given temperature and pressure.  It refers to gases that remain in gaseous form even when cooled.|Example:  The noncondensable gases in the system need to be purged. 系统中的不凝结气体需要排出。|近义词：non-condensible, uncondensed|反义词：condensable
