endoscopies:/ɛnˈdɒskəpiz/ | n. | 胃镜检查；内窥镜检查 |The plural form of endoscopy, referring to multiple instances of using an endoscope to examine the interior of a body cavity or organ.  |Example: The patient underwent several endoscopies to investigate the source of his abdominal pain.  病人接受了几次内窥镜检查以调查其腹部疼痛的来源。 |近义词：internal examinations, visual inspections (of internal organs) |反义词：external examination

词源:
The word "endoscopies" is derived from the Greek words "endon" (within) and "skopein" (to look).  It's the plural of "endoscopy",  reflecting the act of looking inside the body.
