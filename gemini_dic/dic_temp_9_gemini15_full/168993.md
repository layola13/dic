profluvium:/proˈfluːviəm/| n. |奔流；大量涌出；洪流 |A profuse flow or outpouring of something, especially a liquid.  It often implies a sudden or overwhelming abundance.|例句：The profluvium of lava from the volcano destroyed the nearby village.  火山喷出的熔岩洪流摧毁了附近的村庄。

The profluvium of information overwhelmed the researchers. 信息的洪流淹没了研究人员。|近义词：torrent, deluge, flood, outpouring |反义词：trickle, dribble, scantiness


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations like "is", "are", "was", etc.  Therefore, tenses are not applicable.  The word itself describes a state of being.

PHRASE->

a profluvium of ideas:  灵感的涌现
a profluvium of words:  滔滔不绝的话语
a profluvium of emotions:  情感的奔涌
a profluvium of blood: 血流如注
