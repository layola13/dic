dupes:/dʒuːps/| n. |傻瓜，受骗者，复制品|英文解析：A dupe is a person who is easily tricked or deceived.  It can also refer to a copy or imitation of something.|例句：He was a dupe of the con artist. 他是那个骗子的受害者。  The painting was a clever dupe of a masterpiece. 这幅画是名作的巧妙复制品。|近义词：fool, victim, sucker, imitation, copy|反义词：expert, con artist, original
