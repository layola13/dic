/ɡloʊm/| n. |忧郁；昏暗；地球| Gloom is a feeling of pessimism or depression.  It can also refer to a state of darkness or dimness. The word can also be used figuratively to refer to the Earth. |例句：A gloom descended upon the room after the bad news was announced.  坏消息宣布后，房间里笼罩着一股忧郁的气氛。

The gloom of the forest was almost palpable. 森林的阴暗几乎令人感觉得到。|近义词：depression, sadness, darkness, obscurity |反义词：cheerfulness, brightness, light, optimism|名词
