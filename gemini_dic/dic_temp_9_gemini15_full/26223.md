butterflylike: /ˈbʌtərˌflaɪlaɪk/ | adj. |像蝴蝶一样的；轻盈飘逸的 | Resembling a butterfly in appearance or movement; light and airy.  |例句：Her dress fluttered butterflylike in the breeze.  她的裙子在微风中像蝴蝶一样飘动。


例句：The dancers moved with butterflylike grace. 舞者们轻盈飘逸地舞动着。

反义词：clumsy, heavy, awkward, ungainly
