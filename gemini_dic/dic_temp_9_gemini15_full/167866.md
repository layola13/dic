primulas:/ˈprɪmjʊləz/| n.|报春花属植物|Primulas are a genus of flowering plants in the family Primulaceae, known for their vibrant and often brightly colored flowers. They are popular garden plants and are often grown in pots or window boxes.  The name "primula" comes from the Latin word "primus," meaning "first," referring to the fact that many species bloom early in the spring.|例句：The garden was ablaze with color, thanks to the profusion of primulas blooming in the spring.  花园里鲜花盛开，五彩缤纷，这多亏了春天盛开的报春花。
