hydrocarbon:/ˈhaɪdrəʊkɑːrbən/| n. |碳氢化合物|A hydrocarbon is an organic chemical compound composed exclusively of hydrogen and carbon atoms.|Natural gas is a mixture of hydrocarbons. 天然气是碳氢化合物的混合物。|近义词：organic compound|反义词：无

词源:
hydro-  来自希腊语 "hýdōr" (ὕδωρ), meaning "water"
carbon 来自拉丁语 "carbo", meaning "coal"

The term combines the "hydro" referencing hydrogen and "carbon" referring to the carbon atoms, reflecting the chemical composition.
