quadcopters:/ˈkwɑːdkɒptərz/| n. |四轴飞行器|A quadcopter is an unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) that is lifted and propelled by four rotors.  They are often used for photography, videography, and surveillance.|例句：Many companies are now using quadcopters for delivering small packages. 许多公司现在正在使用四轴飞行器来递送小包裹。
例句：The police used a quadcopter to search for the missing child.  警方用四轴飞行器搜寻失踪儿童。|近义词：drone, UAV (unmanned aerial vehicle), multirotor|反义词：fixed-wing aircraft
