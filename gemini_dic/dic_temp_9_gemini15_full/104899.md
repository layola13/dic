keepsakes:/ˈkiːpseɪks/| n.|纪念品，留念物|An object kept as a reminder of a person, place, or event.  Keepsakes are items of sentimental value, often associated with cherished memories.|例句：She keeps a box of keepsakes from her childhood. 她保留着一个盒子，里面装着她童年时代的纪念品。

例句：I treasure the keepsakes my grandmother left me. 我珍藏着祖母留给我的纪念品。


近义词：souvenirs, mementos, reminders|反义词：None  (Antonyms are not applicable in this context)|词性:名词
