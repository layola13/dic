concentrations:/ˌkɒnsənˈtreɪʃənz/ /ˌkɑːnsənˈtreɪʃənz/ | n. | 集中；浓度；专心；浓缩物 | The act of concentrating or the state of being concentrated.  It can refer to the amount of a substance in a defined space, or the focus of one's attention. | 例句：The high concentrations of pollutants in the air are harmful to health.  空气中高浓度的污染物对健康有害。

例句：His concentrations on the problem led to a breakthrough solution. 他专注于这个问题，最终找到了突破性的解决方案。


近义词：focus, attention, density, intensity

反义词：distraction, dispersion, dilution


词性：名词
