preaffiliations:/ˌpriːæˌfɪliˈeɪʃənz/| n. |前期从属关系；之前的隶属关系 |Prior associations or memberships, typically before a more significant or current affiliation.  This term often refers to affiliations held before joining a particular organization, group, or institution.  For example, a university might ask about preaffiliations to understand a candidate's background and previous commitments.|The candidate listed several preaffiliations on their application, including previous volunteer work and professional memberships.|近义词：prior affiliations, previous memberships, earlier connections|反义词：current affiliations, present memberships
