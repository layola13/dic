prejustification:/ˌpriːdʒʌstɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |预先证明；事先辩护；预先为…辩解|The act of justifying something before it is questioned or criticized; providing a defense or explanation in advance.  It often implies a potential for future criticism or challenge. |例句：His prejustification of his actions seemed unconvincing.  他的事先辩护听起来令人难以信服。

例句：The company's prejustification for the price increase was based on rising production costs.  公司对价格上涨的事先辩解是基于生产成本的上升。


近义词：preemptive justification, anticipatory defense, prior explanation


反义词：postjustification, after-the-fact explanation


词性：名词
