facsimiled:/ˈfæksɪmɪld/ | v. | 传真发送；复制 | To send or transmit a document or image by fax.  | 例句：I facsimiled the contract to the client. 我通过传真给客户发送了合同。

例句：He facsimiled the report to his boss before the meeting. 他在会议前将报告传真给了他的老板。


近义词：faxed, transmitted, copied


反义词：received (in the context of receiving a fax)


词性：动词
