impressionable: /ɪmˈprɛʃənəbəl/ | adj. | 易受影响的；感情丰富的 | Easily influenced or affected by others; susceptible to suggestion.  |  The impressionable young minds absorbed the teacher's every word.  年轻而易受影响的孩子们吸收了老师的每一句话。 | susceptible, vulnerable, sensitive | insensitive, resistant, impervious | 形容词
