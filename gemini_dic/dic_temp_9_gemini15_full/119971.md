millepede:/ˈmɪləpɛd/| n. |千足虫，马陆|A myriapod with a long, segmented body and many legs.  It is often confused with a centipede, but differs in having two pairs of legs per segment. |例句：The children shrieked when they saw the millepede crawling across the path. 孩子们看到千足虫爬过小路时尖叫起来。|例句：The gardener gently removed the millepede from the flowerbed. 园丁轻轻地把千足虫从花坛里移走了。|近义词：millipede, myriapod |反义词：无
