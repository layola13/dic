USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "What is your name?".  The grammatical structures below are examples of how the adjective "infusible" can be used in a sentence.  It will be used in various sentence structures, illustrative of adjective usage.


肯定句:
The material is infusible. = 这种材料是不可渗透的。
The substance proved infusible under high heat. = 在高温下，这种物质证明是不可熔化的。


否定句:

词源:

The word "infusible" derives from the Latin prefix "in-" (not, without) and the verb "fundere" (to pour, to melt).  The suffix "-ible" indicates capability or possibility.  Therefore, "infusible" literally means "not capable of being poured or melted."
