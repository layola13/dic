resourcefully:/rɪˈsɔːrsfʊli/| adj.| 足智多谋的，机智的，富有资源性地 |  Showing ingenuity in finding ways to overcome difficulties.  It implies using available resources creatively and effectively to solve problems or achieve goals. |例句：She resourcefully used a hairpin to unlock the door. 她巧妙地用发夹打开了门。

例句：He resourcefully managed to fix the car with limited tools. 他凭借有限的工具巧妙地修好了汽车。


近义词：inventively, cleverly, ingeniously, imaginatively, skillfully


反义词：ineptly, clumsily, unresourcefully


词性:形容词
