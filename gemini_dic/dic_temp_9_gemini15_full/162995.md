precirculated:/ˌpriːˈsɜːrkjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 预先流通的，预先发行的 |  Already circulated or distributed before the main release or official distribution. This term is often used in the context of currency, securities, or marketing materials.  | Example: The bank received a shipment of precirculated banknotes.  这家银行收到了一批预先流通的钞票。

The company tested the precirculated marketing materials in a smaller market before a national launch. 公司在全国发行之前，在一个较小的市场测试了预先发行的营销材料。|近义词：pre-released, previously distributed | 反义词：newly issued, fresh
