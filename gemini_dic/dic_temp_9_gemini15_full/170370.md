provisor:/ˈprɒvɪzə(r)/| n. |临时负责人；暂定条款；预备人员|A provisor is a person who temporarily holds a position or performs a function, especially pending the appointment of a permanent holder.  It can also refer to a clause or condition that is temporary or conditional. |Example:  The school appointed a provisor until a new headmaster could be found. 学校任命了一名临时校长，直到找到新的校长为止。
The contract included a provisor allowing for termination under certain conditions. 合同中包含一项条款，允许在特定条件下终止合同。|近义词：interim, temporary, substitute |反义词：permanent, established
