fecundate:/ˈfjuːkʌndeɪt/| vt.|使受精；使肥沃；使多产|To make fertile or productive; to impregnate.  The verb fecundate refers to the act of fertilizing or making something fertile, often used in biological contexts but also applicable to metaphorical situations of enriching or making something more productive.|例句：The pollen fecundated the ovules, resulting in the formation of seeds.  花粉使胚珠受精，从而形成种子。|例句：The rich soil fecundated the crops, leading to a bountiful harvest. 肥沃的土壤使庄稼丰收。|近义词：fertilize, impregnate, enrich|反义词：sterilize, barren
