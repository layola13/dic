ecologies:/ɪˈkɒlədʒiz/ | n. |生态学（复数）；生态系统（复数） | The study of the relationships between organisms and their environments.  It encompasses various levels of organization, from individual organisms to entire ecosystems.  Ecologies can be studied at different scales, ranging from the microscopic level to the global biosphere. | 例句：The ecologies of different regions vary widely due to differences in climate, topography, and biodiversity. 不同地区的生态系统由于气候、地形和生物多样性的差异而差异很大。


例句：Researchers are studying the interconnected ecologies of the Amazon rainforest. 研究人员正在研究亚马逊雨林相互关联的生态系统。


近义词：ecosystems (单数形式为ecosystem), environments


反义词：无直接反义词，但可以考虑与“破坏的生态系统”或“非自然的系统”对比


词性:名词
