multinationalisms: /ˌmʌltiˈnæʃənəlɪzəmz/ | n. | 多元民族主义；多国主义 | The existence of multiple, and often competing, national identities within a single state or region.  It often involves the interplay of different national groups, languages, cultures, and aspirations for self-determination. | 例句：The rise of multinationalisms in the former Yugoslavia led to conflict and ultimately, the breakup of the country.  前南斯拉夫多元民族主义的兴起导致了冲突，最终导致了国家的解体。


例句：The study explores the complex dynamics of multinationalisms in contemporary Europe. 这项研究探讨了当代欧洲多元民族主义的复杂动态。


近义词：plural nationalisms, multi-nationalism (though technically a slightly different concept)


反义词：national unity, homogeneity,  mononationalism


词性: 名词
