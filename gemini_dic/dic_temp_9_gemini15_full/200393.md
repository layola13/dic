skylines:/ˈskaɪlaɪnz/| n.|天际线，轮廓线|The visible line where the earth's surface and the sky appear to meet.|例句：The city's skyline is dominated by skyscrapers.  这座城市的天际线以摩天大楼为主。|例句：The dramatic skyline was a breathtaking sight.  壮观的天际线令人叹为观止。|近义词：horizon, outline, profile|反义词：ground level
