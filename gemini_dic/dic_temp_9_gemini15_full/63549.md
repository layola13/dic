envelops:/ɪnˈvɛləps/ | v. | 包裹；包含；笼罩 | To enclose or surround completely; to involve or encompass.  | The fog enveloped the mountain.  雾笼罩着山峰。| The project enveloped him completely. 这个项目完全把他吞噬了。 | encompass, enclose, surround | uncover, reveal | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The blanket envelops the child. = 毛毯裹着孩子。
The mist envelops the valley. = 薄雾笼罩着山谷。

一般过去时:
The darkness enveloped the city. = 黑暗笼罩了城市。
Fear enveloped her. = 恐惧包围了她。

现在完成时:
Doubt has enveloped the entire team. = 怀疑笼罩了整个团队。
A sense of peace has enveloped her. = 一种平静的感觉笼罩着她。

过去完成时:
A thick fog had enveloped the village. = 浓雾已经笼罩了村庄。
Silence had enveloped the room. = 寂静已经笼罩了房间。

将来时:
A storm will envelop the coast. = 暴风雨将席卷海岸。
Anxiety will envelop them before the exam. = 考试前焦虑会包围他们。

虚拟语气:
If only a blanket could envelop me in warmth. = 但愿一条毛毯能温暖地包裹着我。
If the mist enveloped the town, visibility would be poor. = 如果雾气笼罩了小镇，能见度就会很低。

被动语态:
The package was enveloped in brown paper. = 包裹被棕色的纸包裹着。
The house was enveloped by flames. = 房子被火焰包围着。

疑问句:
Does the fog envelop the entire area? = 雾是否笼罩了整个区域？
Is the city enveloped in darkness? = 城市是否被黑暗笼罩？

否定句:
The sun did not envelop the earth in warmth.  太阳没有温暖地包裹着地球。
The feeling of joy did not envelop her. = 喜悦的感觉并没有包围她。
