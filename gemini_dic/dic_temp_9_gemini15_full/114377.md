mak:/mæk/ | v. | 做，制造；使得；获得 | To make something means to create or produce it.  It can also mean to cause something to happen or to achieve something. | Examples:  I'm making a cake. 我正在做蛋糕。 He made a lot of money. 他赚了很多钱。 | 近义词：create, produce, build, construct | 反义词：destroy, break, ruin |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I make breakfast every morning. = 我每天早上做早餐。
She makes beautiful pottery. = 她制作精美的陶器。


一般过去时:
He made a mistake. = 他犯了一个错误。
They made a great decision. = 他们做了一个很棒的决定。


现在完成时:
I have made several attempts. = 我已经尝试了好几次。
She has made significant progress. = 她已经取得了显著的进步。


过去完成时:
He had made his decision before I arrived. = 我到达之前他已经做出了决定。
They had made plans for the trip. = 他们已经为这次旅行制定了计划。


将来时:
I will make a phone call later. = 我稍后会打个电话。
We will make sure everything is ready. = 我们会确保一切准备就绪。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would make a better cake. = 如果我有更多时间，我会做一个更好的蛋糕。
I wish I had made a different choice. = 我真希望我做出了不同的选择。


被动语态:
The cake was made by my mother. = 蛋糕是我妈妈做的。
The decision was made quickly. = 决定很快就被做出了。


疑问句:
What are you making? = 你在做什么？
Can you make a reservation for us? = 你能为我们预订吗？


否定句:
I didn't make the mess. = 我没有弄乱。
She didn't make it to the meeting. = 她没有参加会议。



PHRASE->
make a difference =  产生影响
make up one's mind =  下定决心
make sense =  有意义
make an effort =  努力
make a living =  谋生
