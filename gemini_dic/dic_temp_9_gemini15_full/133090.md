noneradicative: /ˌnɒnɪˈrædɪkətɪv/ | adj. | 非根除性的，不能根除的 | Not capable of completely eliminating or destroying something, especially a disease or pest.  It implies a treatment or method that controls or reduces but doesn't entirely remove the target. | Example:  The current treatment is noneradicative; it manages the symptoms but doesn't cure the disease.  目前的治疗是非根除性的；它可以控制症状，但不能治愈疾病。| 近义词：suppressive, controlling, mitigating | 反义词：eradicative, curative, eliminative
