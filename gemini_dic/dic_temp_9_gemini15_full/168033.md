privacy:/ˈpraɪvəsi/| n. |隐私，私密性 |The state of being free from unwanted observation or intrusion.  It refers to the right of individuals to control access to their personal information and to be free from surveillance or intrusion. |例句：He values his privacy and doesn't like people dropping by unannounced. 他很重视自己的隐私，不喜欢别人不请自来。

例句：The company has a strict privacy policy to protect customer data. 这家公司有严格的隐私政策来保护客户数据。


近义词：secrecy, confidentiality, seclusion |反义词：publicity, exposure
