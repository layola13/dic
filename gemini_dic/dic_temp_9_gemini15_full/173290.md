quasi-conveniently:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ kənˈvinjəntli/ | adv. | 几乎方便地；差不多方便地 |  This word describes something that is almost but not entirely convenient. It implies a slight lack of complete convenience, often due to minor inconveniences or compromises.  | Example:  The location of the meeting was quasi-conveniently situated near the train station, but parking was a nightmare.  会议地点几乎方便地位于火车站附近，但停车却是一场噩梦。 | 近义词: relatively conveniently, fairly conveniently, moderately conveniently | 反义词: inconveniently, tremendously inconveniently | 副词
