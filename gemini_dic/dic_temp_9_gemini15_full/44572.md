/ˈkraʊtʃərz/| n.| 蹲伏者；弯腰的人 | People who crouch or bend low.  The term often carries negative connotations, implying subservience or a lack of confidence.  It can also refer to a group of people engaging in this posture. | The protesters, a group of crouchers, remained silent during the demonstration.  示威者，一群蹲伏的人，在示威期间保持沉默。 |  bowers, kneelers | standers, uprights | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat crouches before pouncing. = 猫蹲伏着准备扑过去。
They crouch behind the wall. = 他们躲在墙后蹲着。

一般过去时:
He crouched down to examine the plant. = 他蹲下检查植物。
The soldiers crouched low to avoid being seen. = 士兵们低着头蹲下以免被发现。

现在完成时:
She has crouched for so long, her legs are numb. = 她蹲了这么久，腿都麻了。

过去完成时:
He had crouched there for a while before I arrived. = 我到达之前他已经在那里蹲了一会儿了。
They had crouched in the bushes, waiting for the signal. = 他们藏在灌木丛中蹲着，等待信号。

将来时:
We will crouch down to avoid being seen by the guards. = 我们将蹲下以免被守卫看到。
She will crouch behind the tree to observe the animals. = 她将躲在树后观察动物。

虚拟语气:
If I were a cat, I would crouch and pounce. = 如果我是一只猫，我会蹲下然后扑过去。
I wish I had crouched lower; I would have been harder to see. = 我真希望我蹲得更低一些；那样我就更难被发现了。

被动语态:
The figure was crouched in the doorway. = 人影蜷缩在门口。
The evidence was found where he had crouched. = 证据在他蹲过的地方被发现。

疑问句:
Were they crouching in fear? = 他们是不是害怕地蹲着？
Is the dog crouching to attack? = 那狗是不是准备攻击而蹲着？

否定句:
He didn't crouch down; he stood up straight. = 他没有蹲下；他笔直地站着。
They didn't crouch to hide; they ran away. = 他们没有蹲下躲藏；他们跑掉了。
