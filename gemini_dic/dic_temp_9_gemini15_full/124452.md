motorway:/ˈməʊtəweɪ/ (BrE) /ˈmoʊtərˌweɪ/ (AmE)| n. |高速公路|A motorway is a high-speed, multi-lane road designed for fast-moving traffic, typically with controlled access and limited intersections.  Motorways are often part of a national network of roads. |Example:  We took the motorway to get to London quickly. 我们走高速公路以便快速到达伦敦。

The journey was much faster once we joined the motorway. 一旦我们上了高速公路，旅程就快多了。|近义词：expressway, freeway, highway|反义词：country lane, back road
