lunges:/lʌndʒ/| n. v.|弓步，猛冲；突刺 |A lunge is a sudden forward movement of the body, typically made while attacking or defending.  It can also refer to a type of exercise. |例句：The boxer unleashed a powerful lunge, knocking his opponent off balance. 拳击手使出有力的一击，打得对手失去平衡。

The fencer executed a swift lunge, aiming for her opponent's chest.  击剑运动员迅速刺出一剑，目标是对手的胸部。

近义词：plunge, thrust, jab, lunge (exercise)|反义词：retreat, withdraw|词性: noun, verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lunges at the ball. = 他猛扑向球。
She lunges forward to catch the falling vase. = 她向前猛扑去接住快要掉落的瓶子。

一般过去时:
The tiger lunged at its prey. = 老虎猛扑向它的猎物。
He lunged for the door handle. = 他猛扑向门把手。

现在完成时:
The cat has lunged at the mouse several times. = 猫已经几次猛扑向老鼠了。
She has lunged at opportunities to advance her career. = 她抓住机会来提升自己的事业。

过去完成时:
The thief had lunged at the guard before being apprehended. = 小偷在被抓住之前已经向警卫猛扑过去。
He had lunged forward, expecting to catch the ball, but missed. = 他向前猛扑，想要接住球，但没接住。

将来时:
The athlete will lunge powerfully during the competition. = 运动员将在比赛中强有力地弓步。
I will lunge for the phone if it rings. = 如果电话响了，我会猛扑过去接电话。

虚拟语气:
If he lunged, he might have injured himself. = 如果他猛扑过去，他可能会受伤。
I wish I hadn't lunged so aggressively. = 我希望我当时没有那么猛烈地冲过去。

被动语态:
The ball was lunged at by the player. = 球被运动员猛扑了过去。 (This is less common usage)
The opportunity was lunged at by many applicants.  = 许多申请者都抓住这个机会。(This is less common usage)


疑问句:
Will he lunge again? = 他还会再猛扑吗？
Did she lunge before falling? = 她摔倒之前有没有猛扑？

否定句:
He didn't lunge at the opponent. = 他没有猛扑向对手。
She did not lunge for the knife. = 她没有猛扑向刀子。


PHRASE->

词源:
The word "lunge" comes from the Old French word "longe," meaning "a long, narrow strip of leather," originally referring to a leather strap used in falconry.  Later, it developed its current meaning, likely due to the long, extended movement associated with the action.
