crushes:/ˈkrʌʃɪz/| n. v.|迷恋；压碎；挫折|

英文解析:
As a noun, "crush" refers to a strong but typically brief infatuation, especially one experienced by young people.  It can also refer to the act of crushing something, or a feeling of being overwhelmed or defeated. As a verb, it means to press or squeeze something forcefully, causing it to break or become damaged; to suppress or defeat something; or to have a strong infatuation for someone.

例句:
She has a huge crush on the new boy in class. = 她迷恋着班上新来的男孩。

He crushed the can in his hand. = 他用手把易拉罐压扁了。
