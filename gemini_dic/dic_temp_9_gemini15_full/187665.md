roofing:/ˈruːfɪŋ/| n. |屋顶工程；屋顶材料|The covering of a roof;  the materials used for this purpose.  It encompasses all aspects of roof construction, repair, and maintenance.|例句：The roofing on the old house needed replacing.  旧房子的屋顶需要更换。|例句：They used asphalt shingles for the roofing.他们用沥青瓦做屋顶。|近义词：roof covering, roof construction|反义词：无
