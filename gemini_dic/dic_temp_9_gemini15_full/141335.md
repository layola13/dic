opens:/ˈoʊpənz/ | v. | 打开；开放；开始；展现 | To move something so that it is no longer closed or covered; to become open or available; to begin or commence; to reveal or display. | 例句：He opens the door. 他打开了门。

The store opens at 9 am. 商店早上9点开门。


近义词：unfolds, starts, begins, reveals | 反义词：closes, shuts, ends


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The door opens easily. 门很容易打开。
He opens his presents on Christmas. 他在圣诞节打开他的礼物。


一般过去时:
She opened the window. 她打开了窗户。
The conference opened with a prayer. 会议以祈祷开始。


现在完成时:
They have opened a new restaurant. 他们开了一家新餐馆。
The museum has opened its doors to the public. 博物馆已经向公众开放。


过去完成时:
He had opened the letter before he realized it was addressed to someone else. 他在意识到信是写给别人的之前已经打开了它。
The shop had opened for only an hour when the power went out. 商店才开了一个小时，就停电了。


将来时:
They will open the gift tomorrow. 他们明天会打开礼物。
The exhibition will open next month. 展览会下个月开幕。


虚拟语气:
If I opened that box, I might find something interesting. 如果我打开那个盒子，我可能会发现一些有趣的东西。
I wish I had opened the window earlier. 我真希望我早点打开窗户。


被动语态:
The door was opened by the wind. 门被风吹开了。
The package was opened before it arrived. 包裹在到达之前就被打开了。


疑问句:
Does this door open inwards or outwards? 这扇门是向内开还是向外开？
When does the shop open? 商店几点开门？


否定句:
The window doesn't open. 窗户打不开。
He didn't open the letter. 他没有打开信。


PHRASE->
opens up: 打开；展现
opens wide: 张开；敞开
opens to: 通向；面对
opens the door to: 打开...的大门；为...创造机会
opens a new chapter: 开启新篇章
