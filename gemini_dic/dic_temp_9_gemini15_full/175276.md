raindrops:/ˈreɪndrɒps/| n. |雨滴|Small drops of rain.  Raindrops fall from the sky during a shower or storm. They can vary in size depending on the intensity of the rain.|例句：The raindrops splattered on the windowpane. 雨滴溅在窗玻璃上。

例句：We felt the cool raindrops on our skin. 我们感到凉凉的雨滴落在皮肤上。


近义词：drops of rain, rain droplets|反义词：sunbeams, sunshine|词性:Noun
