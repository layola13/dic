narcolepsy:/ˈnɑːrkəˌlepsi/ | n. |嗜睡症，发作性睡病|A neurological disorder characterized by excessive daytime sleepiness and sudden, irresistible attacks of sleep.|例句：His narcolepsy caused him to fall asleep unexpectedly during meetings.  他的嗜睡症导致他在会议期间意外地睡着了。

例句：The doctor diagnosed her with narcolepsy after several sleep studies. 医生在进行了几次睡眠研究后诊断她患有嗜睡症。


近义词：hypersomnia, excessive sleepiness|反义词：insomnia
