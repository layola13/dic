carnation:/kɑːrˈneɪʃən/ | n. |康乃馨|A carnation is a type of flower with many ruffled petals, often fragrant, and available in a wide variety of colors. They are popular as cut flowers and are often given as gifts.|The bouquet contained a dozen red carnations.|近义词：pink, flower|反义词：无
