sky:/skaɪ/| n.|天空，天，苍穹|The vast expanse above the earth, visible during the day as blue (or sometimes other colors) and at night as a dark expanse studded with stars.|例句：The sky is blue today. 今天的天空是蓝色的。
例句：We watched the sunset paint the sky with vibrant colors. 我们看着日落给天空涂上了鲜艳的色彩。|近义词：heavens, firmament|反义词：earth, ground


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sky is clear tonight. = 今晚的天空晴朗。
Birds fly in the sky. = 鸟儿在天上飞翔。

一般过去时:
The sky was cloudy yesterday. = 昨天天空多云。
The sky turned dark before the storm. = 暴风雨来临前天空变暗了。

现在完成时:
The sky has cleared up. = 天空已经放晴了。
The sky has been cloudy all day. = 天空一整天都阴云密布。

过去完成时:
The sky had been clear all day until the rain started. = 下雨前一整天天空都是晴朗的。
The sky had cleared before sunrise. = 日出前天空已经放晴了。

将来时:
The sky will be clear tomorrow. = 明天天空将是晴朗的。
The sky will probably be cloudy tomorrow. = 明天天空可能会有云。

虚拟语气:
If the sky were blue, we would go for a picnic. = 如果天空是蓝色的，我们就去野餐。
I wish the sky were clearer tonight. = 我希望今晚的天空更晴朗。

被动语态:
The sky was painted with beautiful colors by the sunset.  = 日落给天空涂上了美丽的颜色。
The sky is often polluted by smog. = 天空经常被烟雾污染。

疑问句:
Is the sky clear? = 天空晴朗吗？
What color is the sky? = 天空是什么颜色？

否定句:
The sky is not clear. = 天空不晴朗。
The sky wasn't blue this morning. = 今早天空不是蓝色的。
