re-qualify:/ˌriːˈkwɑːlɪfaɪ/ | v. | 重新获得资格；再次取得资格 | To qualify again; to regain or obtain qualifications.  | Example: He had to re-qualify as a teacher after a long break from the profession. 他在离开这个职业很久之后不得不重新获得教师资格。  | 近义词: requalification, recertify | 反义词: disqualify

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He re-qualifies as a pilot every year. = 他每年都要重新获得飞行员资格。
She re-qualifies for the competition. = 她重新获得了参加比赛的资格。


一般过去时:
They re-qualified for the playoffs last season. = 他们上赛季重新获得了季后赛资格。
He re-qualified as a doctor after completing further training. = 他在完成进一步培训后重新获得了医生的资格。


现在完成时:
She has re-qualified as a lawyer after a career change. = 她换职业后重新获得了律师资格。
He has re-qualified for the Olympics. = 他重新获得了参加奥运会的资格。


过去完成时:
They had re-qualified before the new rules were implemented. = 新规实施之前，他们已经重新获得了资格。
She had re-qualified as a nurse before returning to work. = 在重返工作岗位之前，她已经重新获得了护士资格。


将来时:
He will re-qualify for the next tournament. = 他将重新获得参加下一场锦标赛的资格。
She will re-qualify for her driving license next month. = 她下个月将重新获得驾照。


虚拟语气:
If he studied harder, he would re-qualify easily. = 如果他学习更努力，他会很容易重新获得资格。
I wish I could re-qualify for that scholarship. = 我希望我能重新获得那笔奖学金。


被动语态:
He was re-qualified after a successful appeal. = 在成功上诉后，他的资格得到了重新认定。
The athletes were re-qualified for the games. = 运动员们重新获得了参加比赛的资格。


疑问句:
Will she re-qualify for the competition? = 她会重新获得参加比赛的资格吗？
Has he re-qualified as a doctor yet? = 他已经重新获得医生资格了吗？


否定句:
He did not re-qualify for the finals. = 他没有获得决赛资格。
She didn't re-qualify for her professional license. = 她没有重新获得职业执照。
