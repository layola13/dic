edgy:/ˈɛdʒi/| adj.|尖锐的；不安的；新潮的；前卫的|  Describes something that is unconventional, exciting, and slightly dangerous or unsettling; can also refer to a sharp edge or feeling of anxiety.|例句：The film's edgy soundtrack perfectly matched its rebellious tone. 这部电影的尖锐的配乐完美地契合了其叛逆的基调。
The interview had an edgy atmosphere due to the controversial questions.  由于有争议的问题，采访的气氛很紧张。|近义词：sharp, tense, avant-garde, unconventional|反义词：smooth, calm, conventional, traditional
