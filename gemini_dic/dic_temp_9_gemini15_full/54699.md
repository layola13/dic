disorganized:/ˌdɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/| adj.| 杂乱无章的；混乱的；无组织的 | lacking order or planning; messy and untidy.  The disorganized papers were scattered across the desk.  |近义词：chaotic, messy, cluttered, untidy, disordered |反义词：organized, orderly, systematic, neat


例句:

His room was disorganized, with clothes strewn everywhere.  他的房间很乱，衣服到处乱扔。

The project suffered because of the disorganized planning.  由于计划混乱，项目受到了影响。
