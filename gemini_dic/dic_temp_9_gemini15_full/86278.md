heavinesses:/ˈhɛvinɪsɪz/| n.|沉重感（复数）|The plural form of heaviness, referring to multiple instances or types of weight, seriousness, or depression.|例句：The heavinesses of the world weighed heavily on his shoulders. 世界的沉重压在他的肩上。


例句：The cumulative heavinesses of grief, loss, and responsibility left her feeling utterly drained.  悲伤、失去和责任的累积沉重感让她感到筋疲力尽。


近义词：weights, burdens, oppressions, sorrows


反义词：lightness, ease, buoyancy, joy


词性:名词(复数)
