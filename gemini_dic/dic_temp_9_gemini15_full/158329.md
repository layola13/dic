playmate:/ˈpleɪmeɪt/| n. |玩伴，伙伴|A child who plays with another child.|例句：She and her playmate built a sandcastle together. 她和她的玩伴一起堆了一个沙堡。|近义词：companion, friend, pal|反义词:enemy, opponent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My son has a new playmate next door. = 我儿子隔壁新交了一个玩伴。
They are playmates at school. = 他们在学校是玩伴。


一般过去时:
He had a playmate when he was young. = 他小时候有个玩伴。
We were playmates in kindergarten. = 我们在幼儿园是玩伴。


现在完成时:
I've had many playmates throughout my childhood. = 我童年时期有很多玩伴。
She has been a good playmate to her younger brother. = 她一直是她弟弟的好玩伴。


过去完成时:
He had already found a new playmate before we moved. = 我们搬家之前他已经找到了一个新的玩伴。
They had been playmates for years before they drifted apart. = 他们成为玩伴多年后才疏远了。


将来时:

She will be a wonderful playmate for the new baby. = 她将成为新生儿很棒的玩伴。
