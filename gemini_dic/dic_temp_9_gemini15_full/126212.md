my:/maɪ/| pron.|我的|Possessive pronoun indicating ownership or belonging.  It shows that something belongs to the speaker.|例句：This is my book. = 这是我的书。|例句：My car is red. = 我的车是红色的。|近义词：mine|反义词:yours, his, hers, its, ours, theirs


USAGE->

一般现在时:
This is my house. = 这是我的房子。
That's my dog. = 那是我的狗。

一般过去时:
That was my idea. = 那是我想出来的。
My brother played football yesterday. = 我弟弟昨天踢足球了。

现在完成时:
I have lost my keys. = 我丢了我的钥匙。
I have finished my work. = 我完成了我的工作。

过去完成时:
I had already eaten my lunch. = 我已经吃过午饭了。
She had lost her wallet before she realised it. = 她意识到之前就已经丢了钱包。

将来时:
I will bring my camera tomorrow. = 我明天会带我的相机。
I'll finish my project by next week. = 我下周前会完成我的项目。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I'd keep my promise. = 如果我是你，我会信守承诺。
I wish I had my own car. = 我真希望我有自己的车。

被动语态:
My car was stolen last night. = 我的车昨晚被偷了。
My questions were answered by the professor. = 我的问题被教授解答了。

疑问句:
Is this your bag, or is it my bag? = 这是你的包还是我的包？
Whose car is that?  Is it my car? = 那是什么人的车？是我的车吗？

否定句:
That is not my pen. = 那不是我的钢笔。
This isn't my problem. = 这不是我的问题。

PHRASE->
