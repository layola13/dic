nonvagrancy: /ˌnɒnˈveɪɡrənsi/ | n. | 非流浪；非游荡；定居 | The state or condition of not being a vagrant; the act of not wandering or straying.  It emphasizes the settled nature of a person or group, contrasting with the lifestyle of a vagrant. | Example:  The city's initiatives aimed to reduce homelessness and promote nonvagrancy among its population.  这个城市的倡议旨在减少无家可归现象，并在其人口中促进非流浪状态。


近义词: settlement, residence, stability | 反义词: vagrancy, homelessness, wandering
