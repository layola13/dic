death-mask:/ˈdɛθmæsk/| n. |死人面具；遗容像 |A death mask is a cast or mold of a person's face made after their death.  It is often made to preserve the likeness of the deceased.  |Example:  The museum displayed a death mask of the famous poet.  这个博物馆展出了那位著名诗人的死人面具。

The death mask of Napoleon is a well-known example. 拿破仑的死人面具是一个著名的例子。|近义词：facial cast, memento mori |反义词：N/A
