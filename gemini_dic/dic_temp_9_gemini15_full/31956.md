chemical:/ˈkɛmɪkəl/ | n. adj. |化学物质；化学的 |  A substance obtained by or used in a chemical process.  |例句：Many cleaning products contain harsh chemicals.  许多清洁产品含有强效化学物质。

例句：The experiment involved a complex chemical reaction. 实验涉及复杂的化学反应。


近义词：substance, compound | 反义词：natural (in some contexts)


词源:  From Middle French *chimique, from Medieval Latin *alchimicus, from Arabic al-kīmiyā', from kīmiyā' "alchemy".  The word ultimately derives from ancient Egyptian words referring to the "black land" of Egypt, reflecting the origins of alchemy in the Nile valley.
