gynodioecious:/ˌdʒaɪnoʊˈdaɪʃəs/| adj.|雌雄同株异体|Having both female and hermaphrodite individuals within a population.  This means some plants in the species have only female flowers, while others have both male and female flowers on the same plant.  It's a type of breeding system found in plants.|例句：Many species of *Silene* are gynodioecious, exhibiting a mixture of female and hermaphrodite plants.  许多蝇子草属物种是雌雄同株异体的，表现出雌性和两性植物的混合。|近义词：subdioecious|反义词：dioecious, monoecious
