overmedicate:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛdɪkeɪt/| vt.|过度用药|To give someone or something more medicine than is needed or recommended.  This can lead to negative side effects and health complications.|例句：The doctor warned against overmedicating the patient, as it could lead to organ damage. 医生警告不要给病人过度用药，因为这可能会导致器官损伤。

例句：She was concerned that her child was being overmedicated for ADHD. 她担心她的孩子因注意力缺陷多动障碍而服用过量的药物。


近义词：polypharmacy, overdose (in the context of exceeding a recommended dose), medicate excessively


反义词：undermedicate


词性:动词

词源:
The word "overmedicate" is a combination of "over-" (meaning excessively or too much) and "medicate" (to treat with medicine).  "Medicate" itself comes from the Latin word "medicare," meaning "to heal."
