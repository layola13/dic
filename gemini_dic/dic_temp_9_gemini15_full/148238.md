oversimplifying: /ˌoʊvərˈsɪmplɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 过分简化；简单化处理 |  The act of making something simpler than it actually is, often to the point of inaccuracy or misrepresentation.  It implies a reduction in complexity that loses important details or nuances. | Example:  Oversimplifying the problem led to an ineffective solution.  过分简化问题导致解决方案无效。 | 近义词: simplifying, reducing, glossing over, trivializing | 反义词: elaborating, complicating, detailing, nuance
