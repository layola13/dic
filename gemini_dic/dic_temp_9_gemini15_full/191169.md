scaremonger:/ˈskerˌmʌŋɡər/| n. |危言聳聽者，造謠生事者 | A person who deliberately spreads frightening rumors and stories. |例句：He's just a scaremonger trying to create panic. 他只是个危言聳聽者，试图制造恐慌。 |近义词：alarmist, fearmonger, sensationalist |反义词：reassurer, truth-teller


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The newspaper scaremongers about the upcoming election. = 这家报纸在散布关于即将到来的选举的谣言。
He constantly scaremongers about the dangers of social media. = 他不断地危言聳聽社交媒體的危險。

一般过去时:
The politician scaremongered about the economic crisis. = 这位政治家危言聳聽经济危机。
They scaremongered to manipulate public opinion. = 他们散布谣言以操纵舆论。


现在完成时:
The media has scaremongered about the pandemic for months. = 媒体几个月来一直在散布关于疫情的谣言。
She has consistently scaremongered, damaging public trust. = 她一直散布谣言，损害了公众的信任。


过去完成时:
They had scaremongered for years before the truth emerged. = 在真相大白之前，他们已经散布谣言多年了。
The tabloids had scaremongered about the celebrity's health. = 小报一直在散布关于这位名人的健康谣言。


将来时:
He will scaremonger again if given the chance. = 如果有机会，他还会再次危言聳聽。
They will likely scaremonger to gain votes. = 他们很可能会散布谣言来争取选票。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't a scaremonger, people would trust him more. = 如果他不是个危言聳聽者，人们会更信任他。
I wish the media wouldn't scaremonger so much. = 我希望媒体不要散布那么多谣言。


被动语态:
The public was scaremongered into believing the false claims. = 公众被危言聳聽者欺骗，相信了虚假说法。
The issue was scaremongered by irresponsible reporting. = 这个问题由于不负责任的报道而被夸大。


疑问句:
Is he a scaremonger or a genuine expert? = 他是危言聳聽者还是真正的专家？
Are they intentionally scaremongering, or are they misinformed? = 他们是在故意危言聳聽，还是他们被误导了？


否定句:
He is not a scaremonger; he is simply concerned. = 他不是危言聳聽者；他只是很担心。
They did not scaremonger; they simply reported the facts. = 他们没有危言聳聽；他们只是报道了事实。
