intermediating:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdieɪtɪŋ/| v.|居中调解；做中间人；斡旋|Acting as a mediator or intermediary between two or more parties.  The process involves facilitating communication and negotiation to reach a mutually acceptable solution.  This verb emphasizes the active role of the intermediary in the process.|例句：The UN is intermediating in the peace talks. 联合国正在斡旋和平谈判。

例句：The bank is intermediating between borrowers and lenders. 这家银行充当借款人和贷款人之间的中间人。


近义词：mediating, arbitrating, negotiating, conciliating|反义词：obstructing, hindering, impeding|词性：动词
