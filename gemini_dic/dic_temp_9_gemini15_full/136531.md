nonruminating: /ˌnɒnˈruːmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非反刍的；不反刍的 | Not chewing the cud; not relating to ruminants.  The term refers to animals that do not have a multi-compartment stomach allowing them to regurgitate and re-chew their food.  It can also be used metaphorically to describe someone who doesn't dwell on things or overthink. | Example:  "Nonruminating animals, such as horses and pigs, have a simpler digestive system." = “非反刍动物，例如马和猪，具有更简单的消化系统。”  |  近义词: non-ruminant | 反义词: ruminating
