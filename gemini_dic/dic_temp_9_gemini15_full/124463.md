mottoes:/ˈmɒtoʊz/ | n. | 座右铭；格言；箴言 |  Plural of motto.  A motto is a short sentence or phrase that expresses a guiding principle or belief.  It often serves as a summary of a person's or an organization's aims or character. | Example: The company's mottoes emphasize innovation and customer satisfaction. 这家公司的座右铭强调创新和客户满意度。|近义词：slogans, maxims, principles, creeds |反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could be actions that contradict the motto's meaning. | 名词
