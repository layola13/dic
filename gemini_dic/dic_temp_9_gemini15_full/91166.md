hydraemia:/haɪˈdriːmiə/| n.|血水症，血液稀薄|An abnormal increase in the fluid content of the blood, reducing its viscosity.  This condition can be caused by various factors, including excessive fluid intake, kidney dysfunction, and certain medical treatments.| Example:  The patient presented with symptoms consistent with hydraemia, including edema and hypotension.  病人表现出与血水症一致的症状，包括水肿和低血压。|近义词：hypervolemia (though hypervolemia specifically refers to increased blood volume)|反义词：hemoconcentration|名词
