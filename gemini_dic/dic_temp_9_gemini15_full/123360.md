monologuists:/məˈnɒləɡɪsts/| n. |独白演员；擅长独白的人|People who deliver monologues, often in a theatrical or performance setting.  They are skilled in delivering speeches or narratives intended for a single speaker.  |Example: The renowned monologuists captivated the audience with their powerful performances. 著名的独白演员们以其精彩的表演征服了观众。 The competition showcased a diverse range of monologuists, each with their unique style.  比赛展示了一系列风格独特的独白演员。|近义词：solo performers, soloists, speakers|反义词：dialogue actors, conversationalists
