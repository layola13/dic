morian:/ˈmɔːriən/| adj.|摩尔人的；摩尔式的|Of or relating to the Moors or their culture.  The word often refers to the Muslim inhabitants of North Africa and Spain during the Middle Ages.  It can also describe things associated with their art, architecture, or culture.|例句：The Alhambra palace in Granada is a stunning example of Morian architecture. 格拉纳达的阿尔罕布拉宫是摩尔式建筑的惊人例子。|近义词：Moorish|反义词：None specifically, but words implying non-Moorish cultures could be considered antonyms depending on context.|形容词
