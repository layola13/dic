commendableness:/kəˈmendəb(ə)lnəs/| n. |值得称赞；可嘉；令人赞赏|The commendableness of his actions is unquestionable.  他的行为值得称赞，这是毋庸置疑的。|近义词：praiseworthiness, merit, admirability|反义词：blameworthiness, reprehensibility


英文解析:The quality or state of being commendable; deserving praise or approval.  It refers to something that is worthy of praise or admiration because of its moral, ethical, or other positive qualities.


例句:

His selflessness and dedication to the cause were of great commendableness. 他的无私和奉献精神非常值得赞扬。

The commendableness of her efforts was evident in the success of the project.  她的努力成果显著，充分体现了其价值。


词源:

Derived from the verb "commend," meaning to praise or recommend.  The suffix "-able" means "worthy of" or "capable of," and the further suffix "-ness" indicates a state or quality.  Therefore, "commendableness" literally means "the state of being worthy of commendation."
