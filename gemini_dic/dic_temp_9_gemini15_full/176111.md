rayon:/ˈreɪɑːn/| n. |人造丝，粘胶纤维|A manufactured fiber composed of regenerated cellulose, typically derived from wood pulp.  Rayon is known for its softness, drape, and ability to absorb moisture.|例句：That dress is made of rayon. 那件衣服是用人造丝做的。

例句：The shirt feels soft because it's made from rayon. 这件衬衫摸起来很柔软，因为它是由人造丝制成的。


近义词：artificial silk, viscose


反义词：natural fiber (e.g., cotton, silk, wool)


词性：名词
