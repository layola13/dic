power-walked:/ˈpaʊər wɔːkt/ | v. |快走，健步走 |To walk at a brisk pace, often for exercise.  This is the past tense of "power walk".|例句：I power-walked for an hour this morning.  今天早上我快步走了1个小时。

例句：She power-walked around the park to stay fit. 她绕着公园快步走以保持健康。


近义词：brisk-walked, speed-walked | 反义词：strolled, ambled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I power-walk every morning. = 我每天早上都健步走。
He power-walks to work. = 他健步走到上班。

一般过去时:
I power-walked three miles yesterday. = 我昨天健步走了三英里。
They power-walked along the beach. = 他们在海滩上健步走。

现在完成时:
I have power-walked every day this week. = 我这周每天都健步走。
She has power-walked consistently for years. = 她多年来一直坚持健步走。

过去完成时:
I had power-walked five miles before I felt tired. = 我健步走了五英里之后才感到疲倦。
They had power-walked for hours before reaching the summit. = 他们在到达山顶之前已经健步走了几个小时。

将来时:
I will power-walk tomorrow morning. = 我明天早上将要健步走。
We will power-walk in the park this weekend. = 我们这个周末将在公园里健步走。

虚拟语气:
