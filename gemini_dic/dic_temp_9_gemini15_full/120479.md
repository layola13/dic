minstrelsy:/ˈmɪnstrəlsi/| n.|吟游诗人艺术；吟游诗歌；（尤指）黑人滑稽表演|The art, practice, or performance of minstrels; specifically, the kind of entertainment associated with minstrel shows.  It often involved blackface makeup and stereotypical portrayals of African Americans. |例句：The minstrel show was a popular form of entertainment in the 19th century.  十九世纪，吟游诗人表演是一种流行的娱乐形式。|例句：The legacy of minstrelsy continues to be debated and analyzed for its racist implications.  吟游诗人表演的遗产因其种族主义意味而持续受到辩论和分析。|近义词：music, entertainment, performance|反义词：seriousness, reality
