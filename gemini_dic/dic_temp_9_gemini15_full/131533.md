noncommemorative: /ˌnɒnkəˈmɛmərətɪv/ | adj. | 非纪念性的；不值得纪念的 | Not intended to commemorate or remember a person, event, or thing.  It suggests a lack of significance or importance in terms of historical or personal remembrance. |


The stamp is noncommemorative; it's just a standard issue. 这枚邮票是非纪念性的；它只是一个标准的发行物。


The building is noncommemorative, lacking any plaques or markers to indicate its historical significance. 这座建筑是非纪念性的，没有任何牌匾或标记来表明其历史意义。


近义词：unremarkable, ordinary, insignificant, commonplace


反义词：commemorative, memorable, significant, momentous
