prestructures:/ˈpriːˌstrʌktʃərz/| n. |预先结构；预设结构| Structures or frameworks that exist before something else is built or developed;  underlying organizational patterns that influence later development.  These structures can be physical, conceptual, or social and often shape the direction and form of subsequent events or creations.| The prestructures of the building's design were based on ancient Roman architecture.  建筑设计的预先结构是基于古罗马建筑的。

The social prestructures of the community influenced the way disputes were resolved. 社区的社会预先结构影响了纠纷的解决方式。|近义词：framework, foundation, groundwork, underpinnings |反义词：randomness, improvisation, spontaneity |名词
