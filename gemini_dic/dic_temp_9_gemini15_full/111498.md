loafs:/loʊvz/| n.|面包块；(一条)面包；(一)块(东西)|Loaves is the plural form of loaf. It refers to multiple units of bread, typically baked as a single piece.  It can also refer to a mass or lump of something.|


例句：He bought two loaves of bread for the picnic. 他买了两个面包去野餐。

例句：The baker displayed several loaves of different kinds of bread. 面包师展示了几种不同类型的面包。


近义词：loaves, buns (in some contexts)


反义词：None directly applicable.


词性：名词
