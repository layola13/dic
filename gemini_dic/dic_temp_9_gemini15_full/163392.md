preconsidering:/ˌpriːkənˈsɪdəriŋ/ | v. |预先考虑，事先考虑 |  The act of considering something beforehand.  It implies thinking about something before making a decision or taking action. This word is less common than "considering" and often used in more formal contexts. |例句：Preconsidering the potential risks, the team developed a comprehensive safety plan. 预先考虑了潜在的风险，团队制定了一个全面的安全计划。

The company is preconsidering its options before making a final decision on the merger. 公司在就并购做出最终决定之前正在预先考虑各种选择。

近义词：considering, contemplating, pondering, deliberating | 反义词：Ignoring, neglecting, overlooking | 词性：动词


USAGE->

They are preconsidering the implications of the new policy. = 他们正在预先考虑这项新政策的影响。
