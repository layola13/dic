minimizations: /ˌmɪnɪmaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 最小化；减小；缩小 | The act of minimizing something; the state of being minimized.  It often refers to reducing something to the smallest possible size, amount, or degree, or to the process of doing so. |  例句：The company's efforts focused on the minimizations of costs and waste. 公司的努力集中在降低成本和减少浪费上。


例句：The aim of the project is the minimizations of environmental impact.  该项目的目的是最大限度地减少对环境的影响。


近义词：reductions, diminutions, lessenings


反义词：maximizations, amplifications, augmentations
