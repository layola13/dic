hornpipe:/ˈhɔːrnpaɪp/| n. | 船首饰; 海上号角舞曲 | A lively dance tune, originally associated with sailors, characterized by its quick tempo and rhythmic complexity.  It is often played on a pipe and drum.  The name likely refers to a type of pipe used in the music or to the association with ships (horns were sometimes used on ships).|The band played a lively hornpipe. 乐队演奏了一首欢快的船首饰舞曲。The sailors danced a hornpipe on deck. 水手们在甲板上跳起了船首饰舞曲。| jig, reel |  (无明确反义词)| 名词
