pulmocutaneous:/ˌpʌlmoʊkjuˈteɪniəs/| adj.|肺皮的；肺皮的；|Relating to both the lungs and the skin.|The frog's pulmocutaneous respiration allows it to breathe through both its lungs and its skin.  青蛙的肺皮呼吸允许它通过肺和皮肤呼吸。|近义词：respiratory (in the context of skin breathing)|反义词：None specifically, but  terms referring to exclusively lung or exclusively skin respiration could be considered antonyms in a limited sense.
