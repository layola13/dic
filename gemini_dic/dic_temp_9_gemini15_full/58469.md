ducts:/dʌkts/| n.|管道；导管；渠道|Tubes or channels that convey fluids or other substances.  Ducts can be natural, like the bile ducts in the body, or artificial, like air ducts in a building.|例句：The body uses ducts to transport various fluids. 身体利用管道输送各种体液。|例句：The air ducts in the building need cleaning. 建筑物的通风管道需要清洁。|近义词：channels, conduits, tubes|反义词：None specifically, but  "blockage" or "obstruction" could be considered antonyms in context.|名词
