kidneys:/ˈkɪdniz/| n. |肾脏|Two bean-shaped organs located in the abdominal cavity, responsible for filtering waste products from the blood and producing urine.|例句：Healthy kidneys are essential for maintaining overall health. 健康的肾脏对维持整体健康至关重要。  The doctor ordered a blood test to check his kidney function. 医生下令进行验血以检查他的肾功能。|近义词：renal organs|反义词：无

My kidneys filter waste products daily. = 我的肾脏每天过滤废物。
