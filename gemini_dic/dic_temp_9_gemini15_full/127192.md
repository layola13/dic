naturing:/ˈneɪtʃərɪŋ/| v.|培育；养成；使具有自然特性|The process of fostering or developing something, especially in a natural or inherent way.  It often implies a gradual and organic process.|例句：He was naturing his talent for music through years of practice. 他通过多年的练习培养了他对音乐的天赋。|例句：The gardener was naturing the young seedlings, ensuring they received adequate sunlight and water. 园丁正在培育幼苗，确保它们获得充足的阳光和水分。|近义词：cultivating, nurturing, fostering, developing|反义词：neglecting, destroying, hindering|动词
