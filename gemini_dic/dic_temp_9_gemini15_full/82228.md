grouch:/ɡraʊtʃ/| n. v.|脾气坏的人；抱怨 |A grouch is a person who is habitually grumpy and complains a lot.  They often express dissatisfaction and are generally unpleasant to be around. |例句：He's such a grouch; he complains about everything. 他是个脾气坏的人；他抱怨一切。|例句：Don't be such a grouch; it's a beautiful day. 别那么脾气坏；今天天气很好。|近义词：grumbler, complainer, curmudgeon |反义词：optimist, cheerful person |名词，动词
