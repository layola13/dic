greenings:/ˈɡriːnɪŋz/| n. |嫩芽；新叶；(尤指茶叶的)新芽；问候；祝贺|New growth of plants, especially the new shoots of tea plants.  It can also refer to greetings or congratulations.|例句：The greenings on the tea bushes promised a bountiful harvest. 茶树上的嫩芽预示着丰收。|The team exchanged greenings after their victory.  团队在胜利后互相祝贺。|近义词：shoots, sprouts, greetings, congratulations|反义词：withering, decay
