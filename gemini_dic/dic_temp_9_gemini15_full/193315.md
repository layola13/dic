seerfish:/ˈsɪrfɪʃ/| n.|旗鱼|A large, fast-swimming fish with a long, pointed bill, belonging to the family Istiophoridae.  They are prized for their meat and are popular game fish.|例句：The fisherman proudly displayed his catch, a magnificent seerfish. 渔夫自豪地展示了他的战利品，一条壮观的旗鱼。|例句：Seerfish are known for their speed and agility in the water. 旗鱼以其在水中的速度和敏捷而闻名。|近义词：swordfish, marlin|反义词:None.
