intershop:/ˈɪntərʃɑːp/| n.| 网上商店，在线商店 | An intershop is an online store, often referring to a specific e-commerce platform or system.  It's a portmanteau of "internet" and "shop". | Example:  Many businesses now operate their own intershop to reach a wider customer base.  许多企业现在运营自己的网上商店以覆盖更广泛的客户群体。 | 近义词：online store, e-commerce platform, web store | 反义词：brick-and-mortar store, physical store | 名词
