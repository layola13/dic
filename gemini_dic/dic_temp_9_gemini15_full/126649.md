The most common readings are Nakamura (中 村), Nakamaru (仲 丸), and Naka mura (仲 村).|My friend, Kenji Nakamura, is a talented musician.  我的朋友，中村健二，是一位有才华的音乐家。


The meaning of the kanji characters varies depending on the specific characters used.  Therefore there is no single definitive meaning for the surname beyond its use as a surname.
