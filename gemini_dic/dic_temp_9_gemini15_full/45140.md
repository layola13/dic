cuddles:/ˈkʌdəlz/| n. v.|拥抱；依偎；搂抱|The act of holding someone or something close for comfort or affection; a close embrace.|例句：The child cuddled up to their mother for warmth.  孩子依偎在母亲身边取暖。|例句：She cuddled her teddy bear before going to sleep. 她睡觉前抱着泰迪熊。|近义词：hug, embrace, snuggle|反义词：push away, reject


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cuddles his cat every night. = 他每晚都抱着他的猫。
They cuddle on the sofa and watch TV. = 他们依偎在沙发上看电视。

一般过去时:
She cuddled her baby to sleep. = 她抱着婴儿哄他睡觉。
We cuddled by the fire last night. = 我们昨晚依偎在火炉旁。

现在完成时:
I have cuddled with my dog all afternoon. = 我整个下午都抱着我的狗。
They have cuddled together for hours. = 他们已经依偎在一起好几个小时了。

过去完成时:
The child had cuddled his teddy bear before falling asleep. = 孩子在睡着之前抱着他的泰迪熊。
We had cuddled up on the couch before the movie started. = 电影开始之前，我们已经依偎在沙发上了。

将来时:
I will cuddle my baby after his bath. = 我将在宝宝洗完澡后抱着他。
They will cuddle together to keep warm. = 他们会依偎在一起取暖。

虚拟语气:
If I were tired, I would cuddle up with a good book. = 如果我累了，我会抱着好书依偎着。
I wish I could cuddle my pet right now. = 我希望我现在能抱着我的宠物。

被动语态:
The baby was cuddled by his mother. = 婴儿被他的母亲抱着。
The teddy bear was cuddled tightly by the child. = 泰迪熊被孩子紧紧地抱着。

疑问句:
Do you often cuddle your pets? = 你经常拥抱你的宠物吗？
Does he like to cuddle? = 他喜欢拥抱吗？

否定句:
He doesn't like to cuddle strangers. = 他不喜欢拥抱陌生人。
She didn't cuddle her doll last night. = 她昨晚没有抱着她的洋娃娃。
