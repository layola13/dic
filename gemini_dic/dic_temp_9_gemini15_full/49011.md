degrader:/dɪˈɡreɪdər/| n. |降解剂；破坏者|A substance or process that causes degradation or breakdown of another substance, often a pollutant or waste product.  It can also refer to someone or something that causes deterioration or decline. |例句：Enzymes are natural degraders of organic matter.  酶是天然的有机物降解剂。|例句：The constant criticism acted as a degrader of his self-esteem.  持续的批评损害了他的自尊心。|近义词：destructor, decomposer, destroyer|反义词：builder, preserver, enhancer|名词
