disadvantagedness:/ˌdɪsˌædvənˈtɪdʒnəs/| n. |不利地位；劣势；贫困|The state of being disadvantaged; lacking the resources or opportunities necessary for success or well-being.  It often refers to social, economic, or educational disadvantages.|例句：The study highlighted the disadvantagedness of children from low-income families. 这项研究强调了低收入家庭儿童的不利地位。|例句：His disadvantagedness stemmed from a lack of access to quality education. 他的不利地位源于缺乏优质教育的机会。|近义词：deprivation, disadvantage, hardship, poverty|反义词：advantage, privilege, affluence
