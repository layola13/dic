hippocrene:/ˈhɪpəkriːn/| n. |希波克里尼泉；灵感之泉|A spring on Mount Helicon in Greek mythology, sacred to the Muses and said to inspire poetic creativity.  It is often used metaphorically to represent a source of inspiration or creative energy.|The poet found his hippocrene in the quiet solitude of the countryside.|近义词：source of inspiration, muse|反义词：block, impediment
