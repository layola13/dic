gimlets:/ˈɡɪmlɪt/| n.|螺丝锥；钻孔器；鸡尾酒|A gimlet is a small hand tool used for boring holes, typically in wood.  It is characterized by a helical screw-like point that draws the tool into the material. As a cocktail, it's a mixed drink typically made with gin or vodka, lime juice, and simple syrup.|例句：He used a gimlet to make a pilot hole for the screw. 他用螺丝锥钻了一个导向孔以便上螺丝。

The bartender expertly crafted a classic gimlet. 调酒师熟练地调制了一杯经典的吉姆雷特鸡尾酒。|近义词：awl, drill (tool)|反义词：None specifically.  |词性:noun
