papers:/ˈpeɪpərz/ | n. | 文件；论文；报纸 | Documents, writings, or printed materials;  a scholarly article or essay; a newspaper or magazine. |例句：I need to file these papers before the end of the day. 我需要在今天结束之前把这些文件归档。
He published his research papers in a prestigious journal. 他在著名的期刊上发表了他的研究论文。

例句：
I read the morning papers every day. 我每天都读早报。


近义词：documents, articles, writings, publications
反义词：None (It depends on the context.  There isn't a direct antonym)
