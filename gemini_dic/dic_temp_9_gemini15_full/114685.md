malignly:/məˈlaɪnli/| adv. |恶毒地；恶意地；险恶地 |in a malicious or harmful way; with ill will or spite. |例句：He spoke malignly of his rivals, spreading false rumors about their business dealings. 他恶毒地谈论他的竞争对手，散布关于他们商业交易的谣言。|例句：The dictator ruled malignly, oppressing his people and silencing dissent. 独裁者恶毒地统治，压迫人民并压制异议。|近义词：viciously, spitefully, malevolently |反义词：kindly, benignly, charitably |副词
