confidentially:/ˌkɒnfɪˈdɛnʃəli/| adv. |秘密地，机密地 |In a confidential manner; privately.  It implies that information is shared in secret and should not be disclosed to others.|例句：He told me confidentially that he was resigning. 他秘密地告诉我他将辞职。|例句：The doctor spoke confidentially with the patient's family. 医生与病人家属私下谈话。|近义词：secretly, privately, discreetly |反义词：openly, publicly
