polypody:/ˈpɒlɪpɒdi/ /ˈpɑːlɪpɑːdi/| n. |多足蕨；多足类植物|A polypody is a fern of the genus Polypodium, typically having creeping rhizomes and simple, often pinnatifid fronds.  Many species are epiphytes, growing on trees or rocks.  |例句：The rock face was covered in a lush growth of polypody ferns.  岩石表面覆盖着茂盛的多足蕨类植物。
