what:/wʌt/ | pron. |什么，什么东西，多么 |  "What" is an interrogative pronoun used to ask for information about something. It can refer to things, actions, qualities, or situations. | 例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

What are you doing? 你在做什么？


近义词：which (用于选择), how (用于方式) | 反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？


一般过去时:
What did you say? = 你说什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么事？


现在完成时:
What have you done today? = 你今天做了什么？
What has she accomplished? = 她取得了什么成就？


过去完成时:
What had you eaten before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前吃了什么？
What had they decided before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们决定了什么？


将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like? = 天气会怎么样？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built there? = 那儿正在建造什么？


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What is the problem? = 问题是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
I didn't see what happened. = 我没看到发生了什么事。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What's new? = 有什么新鲜事吗？
What if...? = 如果……怎么样？
What for? = 为什么？
What about...? = ……怎么样？
