karma:/ˈkɑːrmə/| n.|因果报应；命运；业力|The principle of cause and effect, where one's actions determine future consequences.|例句：His bad karma caught up with him eventually.  他的恶报最终追上了他。

例句：She believes in the law of karma. 她相信因果报应的规律。


近义词：consequence, retribution, fate, destiny|反义词：luck, chance, coincidence|词性：名词
