pesahs:/ˈpɛsɑːz/| n. |逾越节(复数)|The plural form of "Pesach," the Jewish festival celebrating the liberation of the Israelites from slavery in ancient Egypt.  It commemorates God's deliverance of the Israelites and is observed with a Seder, a special ritual meal. |例句：During Pesahs, Jewish families gather for the Seder.  逾越节期间，犹太家庭会聚在一起举行逾越节晚餐。

例句：Many Jewish people celebrate Pesahs with traditional foods and rituals.  许多犹太人以传统食物和仪式来庆祝逾越节。
