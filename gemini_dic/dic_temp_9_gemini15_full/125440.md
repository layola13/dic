multiple:/ˈmʌltɪpl/| adj. |多样的，多个的，多重的| having or involving several or many things; numerous.  |例句：The project has multiple objectives.  这个项目有多个目标。|例句：He suffered multiple injuries in the accident. 他在事故中受了多处伤。|近义词：various, numerous, several, many |反义词：single, one, sole |


USAGE->

They face multiple challenges. = 他们面临多重挑战。
