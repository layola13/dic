pluss: /plʌs/ | n. | 加号，正号，优点 |  The symbol "+" indicating addition or a positive value.  It can also refer to advantages or benefits. | 例句：The plus sign (+) indicates addition. 加号（+）表示加法。  The plus side of this job is the flexible hours. 这份工作的优点是工作时间灵活。 | 近义词：positive, advantage, benefit | 反义词：minus, disadvantage, drawback | 名词
