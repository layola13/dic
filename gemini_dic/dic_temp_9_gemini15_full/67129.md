exportability: /ˌɛkspɔːrtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可出口性，易出口性 | The ability of a product or service to be exported successfully to other countries.  This involves considering factors such as demand in the target market, compliance with regulations, and competitive pricing. | The exportability of the new software is high due to its universal appeal and ease of integration. 新软件的可出口性很高，因为它具有普遍的吸引力和易于集成的特性。|  feasibility, viability | non-exportability, difficulty of export | 名词

词源:

The word "exportability" is derived from the verb "export" and the suffix "-ability," which indicates capacity or potential.  "Export" itself comes from the Old French "exporter," ultimately derived from Latin "exportare" (ex- + portare, "to carry out").
