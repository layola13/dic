elude:/ɪˈluːd/ | vt. |逃避；躲避；使...困惑 | to escape from someone or something, especially by being clever or quick; to avoid something unpleasant or difficult |例句：The criminal eluded the police for many years. 罪犯多年来一直逃避警方的追捕。

The meaning of the question eluded me.  这个问题的意思我搞不懂。


近义词：evade, avoid, escape | 反义词：encounter, confront


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The thief eludes the police. 小偷躲避警察。
He eludes all attempts to capture him. 他躲避了所有抓捕他的企图。


一般过去时:
The prisoner eluded his guards. 囚犯躲过了看守。
She cleverly eluded the reporters' questions. 她巧妙地避开了记者的提问。


现在完成时:
The truth has eluded investigators for years.  多年来，真相一直未能被调查人员查明。
He has eluded capture for months. 他已经潜逃数月了。


过去完成时:
The suspect had eluded capture until that day. 嫌疑犯直到那天都一直躲避抓捕。
She had eluded the police for weeks before her arrest.  她在被捕前已经躲避警察数周了。


将来时:
The suspect will likely elude capture for a while longer. 嫌疑人很可能还会潜逃一段时间。
He believes he will elude the consequences of his actions. 他相信他会逃避自己行为的后果。


虚拟语气:
If only I could elude my responsibilities!  如果我能逃避我的责任就好了！
I wish I could elude the pressures of work. 我希望我能逃避工作的压力。


被动语态:
The criminal was eluded by the police, even though they tried hard. 尽管警方竭尽全力，罪犯还是逃脱了。
The solution to the problem eluded him, despite his best efforts. 尽管他尽了最大努力，但他还是没能找到解决问题的办法。


疑问句:
Does he think he can elude justice? 他认为他能逃避法律制裁吗？
Will the truth ever elude discovery? 真相会永远不被发现吗？


否定句:
The police did not elude the criminal. 警方没有抓住罪犯。
He could not elude his fate. 他无法逃避自己的命运。


PHRASE->
elude capture: 逃避抓捕
elude detection: 逃避侦察
elude comprehension: 难以理解
elude memory: 记不清楚
elude definition: 难以定义

词源:
源自中古英语 *eluden，来自古法语 *eluder，来自拉丁语 *eludere，意为“戏弄，欺骗，逃避”。  其词根为 *lud-,  表示“游戏，玩耍”，与“ludicrous”（滑稽的）有词源联系。
