glowed:/ɡloʊd/| v.|发光；容光焕发|To shine with a soft light; to have a healthy, radiant appearance.|例句：The fire glowed warmly in the hearth.  壁炉里的火温暖地燃烧着。

例句：Her face glowed with happiness. 她的脸上容光焕发。

近义词：shone, radiated, beamed| 反义词：dull, dim

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The embers glow in the darkness. = 余烬在黑暗中发光。
The city glows at night. = 城市在夜晚闪闪发光。

一般过去时:
The candle glowed softly. = 蜡烛柔和地发出光芒。
His cheeks glowed with exertion. = 他的脸颊因用力而泛红。

现在完成时:
The sunset has glowed beautifully all evening. = 日落整个晚上都非常美丽地发光。
She has glowed with health since she started exercising. = 自从她开始锻炼以来，她一直容光焕发。

过去完成时:
The fire had glowed brightly before the wind blew it out. = 在风把它吹灭之前，火燃烧得很旺。
Her face had glowed with pride after her son's graduation. = 她儿子毕业后，她的脸上充满了骄傲。

将来时:
The setting sun will glow brilliantly tomorrow evening. = 明晚落日将发出耀眼的光芒。
I hope your face will glow with happiness on your wedding day. = 我希望你结婚那天脸上会洋溢着幸福。

虚拟语气:
If the sun glowed brighter, we would see better. = 如果太阳更亮，我们会看得更清楚。
I wish my skin glowed as much as hers. = 我希望我的皮肤能像她的一样光彩照人。

被动语态:
The room was glowed beautifully by the fire. = 房间被火光照亮得非常漂亮。(此用法较少见，通常用被动语态表达“被照亮”)
The city can be glowed by the lights of the buildings. = 城市可以被建筑物的灯光照亮。(此用法较少见，通常用被动语态表达“被照亮”)

疑问句:
Does the lamp glow brightly enough? = 灯光够亮吗？
Does her skin glow naturally? = 她的皮肤是自然光泽的吗？

否定句:
The star didn't glow as brightly as last night. = 星星没有昨晚那么亮。
The screen didn't glow at all. = 屏幕根本不亮。
