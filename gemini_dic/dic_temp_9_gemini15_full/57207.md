downbeat:/ˈdaʊnbiːt/| adj. n.|低沉的；沮丧的；弱拍；|adj.  Having or showing a gloomy or pessimistic outlook.  n. The first beat in a bar of music. |例句：The music had a downbeat tempo. 这音乐节奏低沉。

The news gave him a downbeat feeling. 这消息让他感到沮丧。|近义词：depressed, gloomy, pessimistic,  (n.) weak beat|反义词：upbeat, optimistic, cheerful, (n.) strong beat|形容词，名词
