contamination: /kənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 污染，玷污 | The act of contaminating or the state of being contaminated; the introduction of impurities or contaminants into something.  It refers to the presence of undesirable substances or microorganisms that make something impure, unsafe, or unfit for use. | 例句：The water supply was contaminated by industrial waste.  水源被工业废物污染了。

The soil contamination was extensive, requiring years of remediation. 土壤污染非常严重，需要多年的修复。


近义词：pollution, adulteration, infection, taint, defilement | 反义词：purification, cleansing, sterilization
