It's used in various applications, from sorting algorithms to analog-to-digital converters. |Example: The analog comparator in the circuit triggers an alarm when the voltage exceeds a preset threshold.  这个电路中的模拟比较器在电压超过预设阈值时会触发警报。  |近义词： comparer, differentiator |反义词：None (no direct antonym)|名词
