ouzos:/ˈuːzɒs/| n.|乌佐酒|A strong, anise-flavored aperitif distilled from grapes and herbs, particularly popular in Greece.| 例句：We enjoyed a glass of ouzo by the sea. 我们在海边享用了一杯乌佐酒。|近义词：aperitif, anise liqueur|反义词：无
