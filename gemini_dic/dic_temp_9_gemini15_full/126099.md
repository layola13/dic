mutate:/ˈmjuːteɪt/| /ˈmjuːteɪt/| vt. vi. n.| 突变；变化；变异 | To change, alter, or modify in form or nature; to undergo a change in genetic structure.  |例句：The virus mutated and became more dangerous.  病毒变异了，变得更危险了。
The plant mutated after exposure to radiation.  这种植物在接触辐射后发生了变异。|近义词：alter, change, transform, vary|反义词：remain the same, stay constant|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The virus mutates rapidly. = 病毒迅速变异。
The bacteria mutate under certain conditions. = 细菌在特定条件下会发生变异。

一般过去时:
The species mutated over millions of years. = 这个物种经过数百万年的进化而发生了变异。
The gene mutated during replication. = 基因在复制过程中发生了突变。

现在完成时:
The bacteria have mutated and become resistant to antibiotics. = 细菌已经变异并对抗生素产生了耐药性。
The plant has mutated significantly since the accident. = 自事故发生以来，这种植物发生了显著的变异。

过去完成时:
The virus had mutated before the vaccine was developed. = 在疫苗研制出来之前，病毒就已经发生了变异。
Scientists had already observed the mutation in the lab. = 科学家们已经在实验室观察到了这种突变。

将来时:
The virus will likely mutate further. = 病毒很可能会进一步变异。
Researchers predict the gene will mutate in the next generation. = 研究人员预测该基因将在下一代发生突变。

虚拟语气:
If the gene mutated, it could cause a serious disease. = 如果基因发生突变，可能会导致严重的疾病。
I wish the virus hadn't mutated so quickly. = 我真希望病毒没有这么快变异。

被动语态:
The bacteria were mutated by the chemicals. = 细菌被化学物质诱导发生了变异。
The gene was mutated through a spontaneous error. = 基因通过自发错误发生了突变。

疑问句:
Has the virus mutated significantly? = 病毒是否发生了显著变异？
Did the gene mutate during the experiment? = 基因在实验过程中是否发生了突变？

否定句:
The gene did not mutate. = 基因没有发生突变。
The virus hasn't mutated significantly yet. = 病毒目前还没有发生显著变异。
