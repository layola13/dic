resizes:/ˌriːˈsaɪzɪz/| v.|重新调整大小，调整尺寸|To change the size of something, typically an image or window.|例句：The software automatically resizes images to fit the screen.  软件会自动调整图像大小以适应屏幕。
例句：I need to resize this photo before printing it. 我需要在打印这张照片之前调整它的尺寸。|近义词：scale, adjust, alter|反义词：maintain, keep


USAGE->

The software resizes images automatically. = 软件自动调整图像大小。
He resizes photos for his website. = 他为他网站上的照片调整尺寸。

She resized the window to see more content. = 她调整了窗口大小以便看到更多内容。
