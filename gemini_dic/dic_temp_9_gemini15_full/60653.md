electioneering:/ɪˌlɛkʃəˈnɪrɪŋ/ | n. |竞选活动；拉选票 | The act of campaigning for election;  actively working to secure votes for a candidate or party in an election. | Example: The electioneering was intense in the final days before the vote.  竞选活动在投票前的最后几天非常激烈。|近义词：campaigning, canvassing |反义词：None specifically, but  "abstaining from politics" could be considered an antonym.
