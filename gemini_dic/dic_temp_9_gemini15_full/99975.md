例句：

The two rivers interosculate near the town. = 这两条河流在镇附近交汇。

近义词：intersect, interweave, intermingle, merge, blend

反义词：separate, diverge, disconnect


词源:

From Latin *inter- (between) + osculum (a little mouth, kiss).  The original sense involved a close connection or joining, often likened to a kiss or the meeting of lips. The modern usage extends this to a broader sense of intersection, merging, or intertwining.
