pollywogs:/ˈpɑːliˌwɒɡz/ | n. |蝌蚪| Tadpoles, the larval stage of a frog or toad.  They are small, aquatic, and have tails.  |例句：The children were fascinated by the pollywogs swimming in the pond.孩子们对池塘里游动的蝌蚪很着迷。


例句：We saw hundreds of pollywogs in the stream after the rain. 雨后我们在小溪里看到了数百只蝌蚪。
