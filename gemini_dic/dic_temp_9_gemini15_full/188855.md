russianize:/ˈrʌʃənaɪz/ | vt. |使俄国化；使具有俄国特色 |To cause (a country, people, etc.) to adopt Russian customs, language, or political system.  |例句：The occupying forces attempted to russianize the conquered territories.  占领军试图使被征服的地区俄国化。|例句：The government's policies seemed designed to russianize the education system. 政府的政策似乎旨在使教育体系俄国化。|近义词：russify |反义词：westernize
