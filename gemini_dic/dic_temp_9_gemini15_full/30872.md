chafe:/tʃeɪf/| v. n.|擦伤；磨损；激怒；烦躁|To make (skin) sore by rubbing; to irritate or annoy;  a sore or abrasion caused by rubbing.|例句：The rough wool chafed his skin.粗糙的羊毛擦破了他的皮。
The constant criticism chafed him.不断的批评使他恼火。|近义词：irritate, annoy, abrade, rub|反义词：soothe, please


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The collar chafes my neck. = 衣领磨擦我的脖子。
He chafes at the restrictions. = 他对限制感到苦恼。

一般过去时:
The saddle chafed the horse's back. = 马鞍磨擦了马的背部。
Her constant complaints chafed me yesterday. = 她昨天不断的抱怨让我很烦躁。


现在完成时:
The rope has chafed through. = 绳子磨断了。
His constant nagging has chafed my patience. = 他不断的唠叨磨光了我的耐心。


过去完成时:
The harness had chafed the horse's shoulders before the race. = 比赛前，马具已经磨擦了马的肩膀。
I had chafed under their unfair treatment for years. = 我多年来一直忍受着他们不公平的待遇而感到苦恼。


将来时:
The new shoes will chafe my feet if I don't break them in properly. = 如果我不把新鞋穿合脚，它们会磨坏我的脚。
The constant pressure will chafe his spirit. = 持久的压力会磨损他的意志。


虚拟语气:
If the rope were tighter, it would chafe the wood. = 如果绳子更紧，它就会磨损木头。
If I hadn't been so stubborn, I wouldn't have chafed at their suggestions. = 如果我不那么固执，我就不会对他们的建议感到恼火。


被动语态:
His skin was chafed by the rough fabric. = 他的皮肤被粗糙的布料擦伤了。
His pride was chafed by their condescending remarks. = 他的自尊心受到了他们居高临下的话语的伤害。


疑问句:
Does the new belt chafe your waist? = 新腰带勒你的腰吗？
Does his constant complaining chafe you? = 他不断的抱怨让你烦恼吗？


否定句:
The smooth fabric didn't chafe her skin. = 光滑的布料没有擦伤她的皮肤。
The kind words didn't chafe his feelings. = 这些好话并没有让他感到生气。
