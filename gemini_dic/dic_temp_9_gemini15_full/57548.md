drakes:/dreɪks/ | n. |雄鸭；(尤指)公鸭；|A drake is a male duck.  The term often implies a mature male. |例句：The drake proudly displayed his iridescent feathers.  这只雄鸭骄傲地展示着它闪亮的羽毛。

例句：We saw a pair of mallards, a hen and a drake, swimming on the pond. 我们看到一对绿头鸭，一只母鸭和一只公鸭，在池塘里游泳。


近义词：male duck,  cock (less common) | 反义词：duck (hen)


词源:  The word "drake" comes from the Old English word "draca," which referred to a dragon or serpent.  Over time, the meaning shifted to specifically refer to the male duck, likely due to a perceived similarity in the aggressive nature or appearance of some drakes to dragons.
