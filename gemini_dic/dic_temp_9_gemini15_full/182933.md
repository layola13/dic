rephotographing:/ˌriːˈfoʊtəɡræfɪŋ/| v.|重新拍照；翻拍|The act of taking photographs of something again, often with a different purpose or technique than the original photographs.  It implies a prior photographic record exists.|例句：The museum is rephotographing all its artifacts for its online database.  博物馆正在为其在线数据库重新拍摄所有文物。

例句：They are rephotographing the crime scene with enhanced lighting. 他们正在使用增强的灯光重新拍摄犯罪现场。


近义词：re-shooting, reshooting, rephotographing


反义词：first photographing, initial photographing


词性:动词
