overprove:/ˌoʊvərˈpruːv/ | v. | 过度证明；证明过多 | To prove something to an excessive degree; to provide more evidence than is necessary.  |The lawyer overproved the defendant's innocence, presenting so much evidence that the jury became bored and unconvinced.  律师过度证明了被告的清白，提供的证据太多以至于陪审团感到厌烦且不相信。 |  prove, demonstrate, substantiate | disprove, refute |动词
