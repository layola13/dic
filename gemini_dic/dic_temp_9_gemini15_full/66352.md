excursions:/ɪkˈskɜːrʒənz/| n.|远足；短途旅行；游览|A short journey or trip, especially one taken for pleasure.|例句：We took several excursions into the countryside during our holiday.  我们在假期期间进行了几次乡村游览。|例句：The school organized an excursion to the zoo for the children. 学校为孩子们组织了一次动物园的郊游。|近义词：trips, outings, journeys, tours|反义词：staying home, confinement


USAGE->

We take many excursions every year. = 我们每年进行许多次远足。
They often go on excursions to the mountains. = 他们经常去山区远足。

一般过去时:
We took an excursion to the coast last weekend. = 上周末我们去海边远足了。
They went on an excursion to the historical site. = 他们去历史遗址游览了。

现在完成时:
We have taken many exciting excursions this year. = 今年我们已经进行了许多令人兴奋的远足。

将来时:

词源:
The word "excursion" comes from the Old French word "excursion," which in turn comes from the Latin word "excurrere," meaning "to run out" or "to make a raid."  The meaning evolved to encompass journeys or outings, especially those that are short and pleasurable.
