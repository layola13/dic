expurgatorious: /ˌɛkspɜːrɡəˈtɔːriəs/ | adj. | 旨在净化或清除的；旨在删除令人反感的成分的 | Relating to or characterized by expurgation; designed to remove objectionable material.  | The expurgatorious edition of the novel removed all the sexually explicit scenes. 这部小说的净化版删除了所有性爱场景。 | 近义词：purifying, cleansing, censorious | 反义词：unabridged, uncensored
