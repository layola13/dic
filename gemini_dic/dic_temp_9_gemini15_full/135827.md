nonpropagative: /ˌnɒnˈprɒpəɡeɪtɪv/ | adj. |非传播的，不传播的 | Not spreading or tending to spread; incapable of propagation.  This term is often used in contexts related to waves, signals, or diseases.  It describes something that doesn't spread or transmit further. |

例句：

The nonpropagative nature of the defect limited its impact on the overall system. = 该缺陷的非传播性限制了其对整个系统的影响。

The researchers observed a nonpropagative electrical signal in the nerve cell. = 研究人员在神经细胞中观察到一个非传播的电信号。


近义词：localized, confined, restricted, stationary

反义词：propagative, spreading, transmitting, diffusive
