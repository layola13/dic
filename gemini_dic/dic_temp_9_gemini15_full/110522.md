limblike:/ˈlɪmblaɪk/| adj.|肢体似的，像肢体的|Resembling a limb or limbs in form or function.  The description emphasizes a similarity to the structure or movement of limbs. |例句：The robot had several limblike appendages extending from its central body.  这个机器人有几条从中央身体延伸出的肢体状附属物。

例句：The artist sculpted a creature with strangely limblike roots, reaching out from the earth. 艺术家雕刻了一个生物，它有着奇怪的像肢体一样的根，从泥土中伸出来。


近义词：appendage-like, member-like, extremity-like


反义词：trunklike (if referring to a contrasting body part)


词性:形容词
