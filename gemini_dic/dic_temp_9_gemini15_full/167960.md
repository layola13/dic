a priori:/eɪ praɪˈɔːri/| adj.| 先验的 |  Relating to or denoting reasoning or knowledge that proceeds from theoretical deduction rather than from observation or experience.  It refers to knowledge that is independent of experience. | Example: The existence of God is often considered an a priori truth by theologians.  神学家通常认为上帝的存在是先验的真理。|近义词：deductive, theoretical, innate |反义词：a posteriori, empirical
