menaceful:/ˈmɛnəsfəl/| adj.| 威胁的；险恶的；具有威胁性的 |  Suggestive of menace or threat; menacing.  It implies a feeling of impending danger or harm. |例句：The menacing silence of the forest was menaceful.  森林里令人不安的寂静充满了威胁。

The storm clouds gathered, creating a menaceful atmosphere.  乌云密布，营造出一种威胁性的氛围。


近义词：threatening, menacing, ominous, sinister, baleful

反义词：harmless, benign, safe, peaceful, innocuous

词性：形容词
