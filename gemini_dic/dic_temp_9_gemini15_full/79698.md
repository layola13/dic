glowy:/ˈɡloʊi/| adj.|发光的，容光焕发的|Having a soft, radiant light; shining with a soft, warm light.  It can also describe someone's complexion as having a healthy, radiant glow.|例句：The sunset cast a glowy light over the landscape.  日落给风景投下了一层柔和的光芒。

Her skin had a healthy, glowy complexion after her vacation. 假期后，她的皮肤呈现出健康而容光焕发的肤色。|近义词：radiant, luminous, shining, bright|反义词：dull, pale, lifeless


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The candle glows softly. = 蜡烛柔和地发出光芒。
The city lights glow in the distance. = 城市的灯光在远处闪耀。

一般过去时:
The fire glowed brightly in the hearth. = 火在壁炉里明亮地燃烧。
Her cheeks glowed with excitement. = 她的脸颊因兴奋而泛红。

现在完成时:
The embers have glowed for hours. = 余烬已经燃烧了好几个小时。
Her skin has glowed ever since she started using that new cream. = 自从她开始使用那种新面霜以来，她的皮肤就一直容光焕发。

过去完成时:
The sunset had glowed beautifully before the clouds covered it. = 在云层遮蔽之前，日落曾美丽地燃烧。
She had glowed with happiness after receiving the good news. = 在收到好消息后，她幸福地容光焕发。

将来时:
The stars will glow brightly tonight. = 今晚星星将会明亮地闪耀。
Her face will glow with pride when she wins the award. = 当她获奖时，她的脸上将会洋溢着骄傲。

虚拟语气:
If the sun were shining, the snow would glow. = 如果太阳在照耀，雪就会闪闪发光。
If she had eaten healthier, her skin would glow. = 如果她吃得更健康，她的皮肤就会容光焕发。

被动语态:
The room was glowed beautifully by the fireplace. = 房间被壁炉照亮得非常美丽。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，glow 通常为不及物动词)
The painting was made to glow by the artist's skillful use of light. = 画家巧妙地运用光线，使这幅画显得格外光彩照人。(更自然的被动表达)

疑问句:
Does the fire still glow? = 火还在燃烧吗？
Does her skin glow naturally? = 她的皮肤是自然光泽的吗？

否定句:
The screen didn't glow after I turned it on. = 我打开后屏幕没有亮。
Her face didn't glow with happiness today. = 她今天脸上没有洋溢着幸福。
