endoplasm:/ˈɛndoʊˌplæzəm/| n. |内质,细胞内质|The inner portion of the cytoplasm of a cell, often differing in consistency from the outer portion (ectoplasm).  It contains many organelles and is the site of many cellular processes.|例句：The endoplasm of the amoeba pulsed rhythmically.  变形虫的内质有节奏地搏动。|例句：Scientists studied the endoplasm's role in cell division. 科学家研究了内质在细胞分裂中的作用。|近义词：protoplasm (less specific)|反义词：ectoplasm|名词
