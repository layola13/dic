nonspeciously:/nɒnˈspiːʃəsli/ | adv. |非奸诈地；坦率地；真诚地 |Without deceit or trickery; honestly and frankly.  It implies an action or statement is genuine and free from any attempt to mislead or deceive. |例句：He spoke nonspeciously about his intentions. 他坦率地谈论了他的意图。

例句：She answered nonspeciously, revealing the truth without hesitation. 她坦率地回答，毫不犹豫地揭示了真相。


近义词：honestly, frankly, openly, sincerely, truthfully

反义词：deceitfully, deceptively, insincerely, dishonestly, cunningly
