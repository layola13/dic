riparian:/rɪˈpeəriən/| adj.| 河岸的；河边的 | Relating to or situated on the banks of a river.  |例句：Riparian habitats are crucial for biodiversity. 河岸栖息地对生物多样性至关重要。|例句：The riparian zone is rich in plant and animal life. 河岸带植物和动物生命丰富。|近义词：riverside, riverine |反义词：inland, upland
