cleanup:/ˈkluːnʌp/| n. vt.|清理，清除；整顿|The act of cleaning something thoroughly; the process of removing unwanted things.  It can also refer to a thorough reorganization or improvement.|例句：The cleanup after the party took hours. 派对后的清理工作花了几个小时。

例句：The police conducted a cleanup of the crime scene. 警方对犯罪现场进行了清理。


近义词：cleaning, tidying, sprucing up,  sanitation|反义词：messing up, soiling, polluting|词性:noun, verb

He cleans up his room every day. 他每天都打扫他的房间。
They cleanup the spill immediately. 他们立即清理了溢出物。

一般过去时:

现在完成时:
