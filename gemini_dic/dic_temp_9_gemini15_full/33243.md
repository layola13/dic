chronology:/krɒˈnɒlədʒi/| /krɑːˈnɑːlədʒi/ | n. |年表；年代学；时间顺序 |The arrangement of events in time sequence.  It involves the study of dating, ordering, and relating events in time. |例句：The chronology of events leading up to the war is complex and debated.  战争爆发前事件的年表复杂且存在争议。

例句：A detailed chronology of the author's life is included in the book's appendix.  该书附录中包含作者生平的详细年表。


近义词：timeline, sequence, time line, order


反义词：disorder, chaos, randomness


词性：名词
