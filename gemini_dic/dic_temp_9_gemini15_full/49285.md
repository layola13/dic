delibration:/dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃən/| n.|仔细考虑；商议；审议|The careful consideration of something, especially in order to make a decision.|例句：After much deliberation, they decided to postpone the project.  经过深思熟虑，他们决定推迟项目。|例句：The committee's deliberation lasted for several hours.  委员会的审议持续了几个小时。|近义词：consideration, discussion, consultation, reflection|反义词：impulsiveness, rashness|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The jury is deliberating on the verdict. = 陪审团正在商议判决结果。
She deliberates carefully before making any important decisions. =  她在做任何重要决定之前都会仔细考虑。


一般过去时:
The council deliberated for hours before reaching a decision. = 理事会经过数小时的商议才做出决定。
They deliberated the matter thoroughly before voting. = 他们在投票前彻底讨论了此事。


现在完成时:
The committee has deliberated extensively on the proposal. = 委员会已广泛讨论了该提案。
We have deliberated the pros and cons of the plan. = 我们已经仔细权衡了这项计划的利弊。


过去完成时:
They had deliberated the issue for weeks before the meeting. = 他们在会议前几周就讨论过这个问题。
The judge had deliberated for days before announcing the sentence. = 法官在宣判前考虑了几天。


将来时:
The board will deliberate on the merger next week. = 董事会将在下周讨论合并事宜。
They will deliberate the budget carefully before approving it. = 他们在批准预算之前会仔细审议。


虚拟语气:
If they had deliberated more carefully, they might have made a different decision. = 如果他们考虑得更仔细一些，他们可能会做出不同的决定。
I wish they had deliberated more thoroughly before acting. = 我希望他们在行动前能更彻底地考虑一下。


被动语态:
The matter was deliberated by the committee. = 此事由委员会审议。
The proposal is being deliberated by the board. = 该提案正在由董事会审议。


疑问句:
Is the jury still deliberating? = 陪审团还在商议吗？
Has the council finished deliberating? = 理事会商议结束了吗？


否定句:
They did not deliberate sufficiently before making their decision. = 他们在做决定之前没有充分考虑。
The committee did not deliberate the implications of the policy. = 委员会没有仔细考虑这项政策的影响。
