shoemaker:/ˈʃuːmeɪkər/| n. |制鞋匠，鞋匠|A shoemaker is a person who makes or repairs shoes.  They are skilled in crafting footwear from various materials, and often operate their own shop or workshop. |The old shoemaker fixed my worn-out boots. 老鞋匠修理了我磨损的靴子。|cobbler, bootmaker|Unskilled worker in shoe making.
