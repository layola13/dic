nondiaphanous: /ˌnɒn.daɪˈæfənəs/ | adj. |不透明的；不透光的 | Not allowing light to pass through; opaque.  The thick, nondiaphanous curtain blocked out all the sunlight.  | 近义词：opaque, translucent (反义词), solid | 反义词：diaphanous, transparent, translucent


英文解析:The word "nondiaphanous" is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the word "diaphanous," which means "light, delicate, and translucent." Therefore, "nondiaphanous" describes something that is not translucent or transparent; it is opaque or solid.


例句:

The nondiaphanous fabric was perfect for blocking out the harsh afternoon sun. = 不透光的布料非常适合遮挡强烈的午后阳光。


The nondiaphanous walls of the building prevented any light from entering. = 建筑物不透光的墙壁阻止了任何光线进入。


词源:

* **non-**:  A prefix meaning "not."  From Latin *non*.
* **diaphanous**: From Greek διαφανής (diaphanēs), meaning "transparent," from δια- (dia-) "through" + φαίνειν (phainein) "to show, appear."


用法说明：

由于nondiaphanous是一个相对少见的词，其用法主要局限于对材料或物质的描述，强调其不透光性。在文学作品中，也可能用于营造一种厚重、不透明的氛围。
