scripturalist:/ˈskrɪptʃərəlɪst/| n. |圣经主义者，经文解释者|A person who interprets and applies scripture literally and rigorously, often emphasizing specific passages or doctrines above others. They prioritize the Bible as the ultimate source of authority and guidance in all matters of faith and life.  |Example: Some scripturalists believe the Bible should be interpreted literally, leading to different interpretations of its meaning.  有些圣经主义者相信圣经应该逐字解释，导致对其含义的不同解释。|近义词：biblical literalist, fundamentalist |反义词：liberal theologian, contextualist |名词
