knock-downs: /ˈnɒkdaʊnz/ | n. |击倒；击落；（尤指拳击比赛中的）击倒；挫折；失败 |

英文解析:A knock-down refers to an instance where someone or something is knocked to the ground, often forcefully.  It can refer to a literal physical act, such as in boxing, or a metaphorical defeat or setback.  The plural "knock-downs" emphasizes multiple instances of such events.

例句:

The boxer suffered several knock-downs in the fight.  = 拳击手在比赛中遭受了几次击倒。

His business suffered a series of knock-downs due to the economic downturn. = 由于经济衰退，他的生意遭受了一系列挫折。


近义词:  knockouts, defeats, setbacks, failures, downfalls

反义词: victories, triumphs, successes, upswings
