enervators: /ɪˈnɜːrvəteɪtərz/ | n. |使衰弱者，使无力者 |  Things or people that weaken or debilitate.  Enervators drain energy and vitality.  The word refers to agents or factors that sap strength, both physical and mental. | 例句：Excessive stress can be a powerful enervator, leading to burnout.  过度压力可能是强大的使人衰弱的因素，导致倦怠。

The constant negativity of the news cycle acts as an enervator for many people.  新闻周期中持续不断的负面信息对许多人来说是一种衰弱因素。

近义词：weakeners, debilitators, drainers, depleters | 反义词：invigorators, strengtheners, energizers | 词性：n.
