psychokinesis:/ˌsaɪkoʊkɪˈniːsɪs/| n. |心灵致动；超心理运动|The ability to move objects by mental effort alone, without the use of any physical means.  This is a parapsychological phenomenon, and its existence is not scientifically proven. |例句：Some people believe in psychokinesis and claim to have moved objects with their minds.  有些人相信心灵致动，并声称他们用思想移动过物体。

例句：The alleged demonstration of psychokinesis was met with skepticism from the scientific community. 声称的心灵致动演示受到了科学界的怀疑。|近义词：telekinesis, mental influence |反义词：physical manipulation,  natural causation
