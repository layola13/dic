forewarn:/fɔːˈwɔːrn/| vt.|预先警告，预先通知|To warn beforehand; to give previous notice of.|例句：The captain forewarned his crew of the impending storm. 船长预先警告船员即将到来的暴风雨。
例句：We were forewarned about the dangers of the mountain trail. 我们事先被告知了山路上的危险。|近义词：warn, advise, alert, caution|反义词:mislead, deceive.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He forewarns his clients of potential risks. 他预先警告他的客户潜在的风险。
They forewarn the public about the approaching hurricane. 他们预先向公众警告即将到来的飓风。


一般过去时:
