epilogue:/ˈɛpɪlɒɡ/| n. |结语，尾声 |A short passage at the end of a book or play, often summarizing or commenting on the main events.  |例句：The epilogue revealed the characters' fates ten years later.  结语揭示了十年后人物的命运。|近义词：afterword, postscript, coda |反义词：prologue, preface


例句：The novel ended with a poignant epilogue reflecting on the characters' journeys. 这部小说以一段令人心酸的结语结束，回顾了人物的旅程。


The play's epilogue offered a glimmer of hope for the future, despite the tragic events.  该剧的尾声为未来提供了一线希望，尽管发生了悲剧。
