resituate:/ˌriːˈsɪtʃuˌeɪt/| vt.|重新安置；重新定位；重新考虑|To place or establish something again in a different position or context.  It implies a change of location, perspective, or understanding.|例句：The company decided to resituate its headquarters to a more central location. 公司决定将其总部迁至更中心的位置。

例句：After the earthquake, the government worked to resituate displaced families. 地震后，政府努力安置流离失所的家庭。


近义词：relocate, reposition, readjust, rethink, re-evaluate|反义词：leave, abandon, neglect|动词
