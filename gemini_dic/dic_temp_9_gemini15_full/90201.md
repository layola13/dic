houmous:/ˈhuːməs/ | n. |鹰嘴豆泥|A creamy dip or spread made from cooked chickpeas or other beans, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic. It is a popular food in the Middle East and Mediterranean region.| 例句：The hummus was smooth and flavorful, perfectly complementing the pita bread.  鹰嘴豆泥口感顺滑，味道鲜美，与皮塔饼完美搭配。

例句：I prefer my hummus with a little extra olive oil. 我喜欢在我的鹰嘴豆泥里加一点橄榄油。


近义词：chickpea dip, bean dip,  tahini dip| 反义词:| 无 | 词性:名词
