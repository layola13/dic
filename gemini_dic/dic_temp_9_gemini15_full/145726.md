overcredulousness:/ˌoʊvərˈkrɛdʒələsnəs/| n. |轻信；过分相信；过于相信|Excessive trustfulness; gullibility.  It refers to a tendency to believe things too readily, often without sufficient evidence.  This can lead to being easily deceived or manipulated.|The overcredulousness of the young investor led him to lose a significant amount of money in a fraudulent scheme.  年轻投资者的轻信让他在一个欺诈计划中损失了大量的钱。|近义词：gullibility, naivete, credulity|反义词：skepticism, cynicism, incredulity
