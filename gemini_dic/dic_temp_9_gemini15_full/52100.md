diametrical:/ˌdaɪəˈmɛtrɪkəl/| adj.|直径的；对径的；完全相反的|Completely opposite; differing radically.  The two approaches are diametrical opposites.  |The two politicians held diametrical views on the issue.|opposite, contrary, antithetical|similar, identical, alike|形容词


例句：

Their opinions were diametrically opposed. = 他们的意见截然相反。

The two concepts are diametrically different. = 这两个概念完全不同。


词源:

From dia- (through) + meter (measure) + -ical (relating to).  Originally referring to something relating to a diameter, it evolved to describe things that are completely opposite.


用法说明:

diametrical 主要用作形容词，用来描述完全相反或截然不同的观点、意见、方法等。它强调的是极端的对立，而不是简单的不同。


近义词：

* opposite
* contrary
* antithetical
* polar


反义词：

* similar
* identical
* alike
* congruent


需要注意的是，diametrical 通常用于描述抽象的概念，而不是具体的物体。  虽然它源于几何学中的直径概念，但在现代英语中，其主要含义是“完全相反的”。
