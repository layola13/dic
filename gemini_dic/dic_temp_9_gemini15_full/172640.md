pyrrhotite:/ˈpɪrəʊtaɪt/| n. | 磁黄铁矿 | A mineral consisting of iron sulfide (FeS), often containing nickel and cobalt. It is magnetic and is sometimes used as a low-grade ore of iron.  | Example: Pyrrhotite is a common mineral found in many igneous and metamorphic rocks. 磁黄铁矿是许多火成岩和变质岩中常见的一种矿物。| 近义词:  magnetic iron sulfide | 反义词:  (no direct antonym,  but minerals with opposite magnetic properties could be considered)
