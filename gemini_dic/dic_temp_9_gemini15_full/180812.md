rehanging:/ˌriːˈhæŋɪŋ/ | v. |重新悬挂；再悬挂 | The act of hanging something again.  This word is the present participle of the verb "rehang," indicating an ongoing action. It can refer to hanging something that has been previously removed, or to the process of re-hanging something that is not properly hung. | Example: The rehanging of the artwork was a painstaking process.  重新悬挂这幅艺术品是一个费力的过程。 The rehanging of the curtains improved the room's ambiance significantly. 窗帘重新悬挂后，房间的氛围明显改善了。|近义词：reinstalling, re-positioning | 反义词：removing, taking down | 动词
