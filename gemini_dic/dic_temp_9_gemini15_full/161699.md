powder:/ˈpaʊdər/| n.|粉末，火药|A fine, dry substance in the form of small grains or particles.|The cake is coated with a fine layer of icing sugar powder. 这个蛋糕上覆盖着一层细细的糖粉。|近义词：dust, grains|反义词：liquid, solid (in a non-powdered form)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The baker sifts the flour powder. = 面包师筛面粉。
She uses face powder every day. = 她每天都用散粉。


一般过去时:
He sprinkled pepper powder on the steak. = 他在牛排上撒了胡椒粉。
They used gunpowder in the fireworks. = 他们在烟花中使用了火药。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:
