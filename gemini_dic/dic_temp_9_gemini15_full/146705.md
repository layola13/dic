overhelpfulness: /ˌoʊvərˈhɛlpflnəs/ | n. | 过度热心；过分帮忙 | The act of being excessively helpful, often to the point of being intrusive or annoying.  It implies well-intentioned but ultimately unhelpful or counterproductive assistance. |例句：Her overhelpfulness made me feel incompetent.  她的过度热心让我感觉自己很无能。


例句：His overhelpfulness in the kitchen only slowed down the cooking process. 他在厨房里过度热心，反而减慢了做饭的速度。


近义词：intrusiveness, meddlesomeness, officiousness


反义词：indifference, apathy, neglect


词性：名词
