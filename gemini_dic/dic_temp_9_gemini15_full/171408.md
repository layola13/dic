pudding:/ˈpʊdɪŋ/| n. |布丁|A soft, sweet food, typically made with milk, eggs, sugar, and flour, often baked or steamed.  It can also refer to various other sweet or savory dishes depending on region and recipe.  |例句：For dessert, we had a delicious chocolate pudding.  |近义词：custard, dessert, sweet|反义词：None specifically, but perhaps "savory dish" could be considered an antonym in terms of flavor profile.
