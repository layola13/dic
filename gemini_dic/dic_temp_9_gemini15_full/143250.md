outdesigning: /ˌaʊt.dɪˈzaɪ.nɪŋ/ | v. |胜过设计，设计得比…更好 | To surpass in design; to design something better than.  | The new model outdesigned its predecessor in terms of efficiency and aesthetics. 新型号在效率和美学方面都胜过其前代产品。 | surpass, excel, exceed | underdesign, poorly design | 动词
