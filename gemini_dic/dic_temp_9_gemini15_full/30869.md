chaetopod:/ˈkiːtəpɒd/| n. |多毛类动物|A segmented worm with numerous bristles, belonging to the phylum Annelida.  Many chaetopods are marine, but some live in freshwater or moist terrestrial habitats. They are characterized by their parapodia, fleshy appendages bearing bristles (chaetae).|例句：Many polychaetes, a class of chaetopods, are important members of marine ecosystems.|近义词：annelid (more general)|反义词：None specifically.
