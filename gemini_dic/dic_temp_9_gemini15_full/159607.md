pollinator:/ˈpɑːləneɪtər/| n.|传粉者|An organism that transfers pollen from the anther of a flower to the stigma of another flower, enabling fertilization.|例句：Bees are important pollinators for many flowering plants. 蜜蜂是许多开花植物的重要传粉者。|近义词：fertilizer, pollen carrier|反义词:None

一般现在时:
The bee pollinates the flower. = 蜜蜂给花授粉。
The wind pollinates many grasses. = 风为许多草类授粉。


一般过去时:
