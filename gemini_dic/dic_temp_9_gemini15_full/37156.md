come-to-senses:/ˌkʌm tuː ˈsɛnsɪz/| phrasal verb| 恢复理性；醒悟；想通|To regain one's reason or sanity; to understand a situation realistically after a period of delusion or irrationality.|例句：He finally came to his senses and apologized for his behavior. 他终于恢复了理性，为自己的行为道歉了。|例句：After losing all her money, she finally came to her senses and started saving.|她输光了所有的钱后，终于醒悟过来开始存钱了。|近义词：come to one's senses, regain consciousness, recover one's senses|反义词：remain delusional, stay irrational, lose one's mind|词性:phrasal verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He comes to his senses eventually. = 他最终恢复了理性。
She comes to her senses after a long time. = 她过了很久才想通。


一般过去时:
He came to his senses and admitted his mistake. = 他恢复了理性并承认了自己的错误。
She came to her senses and decided to quit her job. = 她想通了，决定辞掉工作。


现在完成时:
She has come to her senses and apologized. = 她已经恢复理性并道歉了。
He has finally come to his senses about his spending habits. = 他终于意识到自己花钱的习惯有问题了。


过去完成时:
They had come to their senses before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经想通了。
She had come to her senses before making a major decision. = 她做出重大决定前已经想通了。


将来时:
He will come to his senses eventually. = 他最终会想通的。
She will come to her senses once she sees the consequences. = 一旦她看到后果，她就会想通的。


虚拟语气:
If he came to his senses, he would apologize. = 如果他恢复理性，他会道歉的。
If she had come to her senses earlier, things would be different. = 如果她早点想通，情况就会不一样了。


被动语态: (This phrase is not typically used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Has he come to his senses yet? = 他想通了吗？
Will she ever come to her senses? = 她会想通吗？


否定句:
He hasn't come to his senses yet. = 他还没想通。
She didn't come to her senses until it was too late. = 直到为时已晚她才想通。


PHRASE->
come to one's senses = 恢复理性

wake up to = 意识到
