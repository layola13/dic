nonsensation:/ˌnɒnˌsɛnˈseɪʃən/| n. |非感觉；无感觉；无知觉|The lack of sensation; absence of feeling.  It refers to the state of not experiencing any sensation, either physically or emotionally.|Example: The medication induced a state of nonsensation in his injured leg.  药物使他受伤的腿处于无感觉状态。|近义词：numbness, insensibility |反义词：sensation, feeling
