psychics:/ˈsaɪkɪks/| n.|超能力者，灵媒|People who claim to have psychic abilities, such as telepathy, clairvoyance, or precognition.  They believe they can perceive information beyond the known senses.|例句：Many people are skeptical of psychics and their claims. 很多人对超能力者及其说法持怀疑态度。

例句：The psychic predicted a major earthquake.  这位灵媒预言了一场大地震。
