monandrian:/ˌmɒnænˈdriən/| adj.| 单雄蕊的；单体雄蕊的 | Having only one stamen.  Relating to flowers with a single stamen. | Example:  The monandrian flower had a single, prominent stamen.  单雄蕊的花有一根突出的大雄蕊。|近义词:unisexual (in the context of having a single stamen)|反义词:polyandrous (having many stamens)
