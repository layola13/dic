overnumerously:/ˌoʊvərˈnjuːmərəsli/ | adv. | 过多地；过多地；数量过多地 | Excessively numerous; in excessive numbers.  The students were overnumerously enrolled in the popular class. | 近义词： excessively, superfluously, abundantly | 反义词： sparsely, scantily


USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and thus doesn't conjugate like verbs or decline like nouns.  Its usage is consistent across tenses and voices. It modifies verbs, describing how an action is performed in relation to quantity.


例句1: The plants grew overnumerously, overwhelming the garden.  植物长得过多，淹没了花园。

例句2:  The invitations were sent overnumerously, resulting in many extra copies. 请柬寄送过多，导致很多多余的副本。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "overnumerously".  The word itself is relatively uncommon and usually used independently.
