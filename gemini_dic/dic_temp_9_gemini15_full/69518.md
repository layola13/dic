fellah:/ˈfɛlə/| n. |老兄，家伙，农民|A fellah is a colloquial term, primarily used in North Africa and the Middle East, referring to a man or fellow.  It can be used in a friendly or slightly patronizing way, depending on context.  The term historically referred specifically to a peasant farmer.|例句：The fellah smiled and offered me some dates.  这位农民笑着给我一些枣子。


例句：He's a good fellah, always ready to help. 他是个好人，总是乐于助人。


近义词：man, guy, fellow, chap|反义词：None specifically, context dependent.|名词
