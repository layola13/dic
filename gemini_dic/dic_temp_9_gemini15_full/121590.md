misogamist:/mɪˈsɒɡəmɪst/| n. |厌恶结婚者，反婚者|A misogamist is a person who dislikes or is opposed to marriage.  They may hold various reasons for their aversion, ranging from personal experience to philosophical beliefs.  Misogamy, the noun form, refers to the hatred of marriage.  The term often carries a negative connotation, implying a deep-seated prejudice against the institution of marriage itself.|He's a confirmed misogamist, having declared his intention never to marry.|近义词：anti-marriage advocate, marriage-averse person|反义词：philogamist
