non-smoking: /ˌnɒnˈsmoʊkɪŋ/ | adj. | 不吸烟的；禁烟的 |  Describes a place, area, or habit where smoking is not permitted or allowed.  It indicates the absence of smoking. |

例句：

This is a non-smoking section. = 这是禁烟区。

The restaurant has a non-smoking area and a smoking area. = 这家餐厅有吸烟区和禁烟区。


近义词：smoke-free, tobacco-free


反义词：smoking


词性：形容词
