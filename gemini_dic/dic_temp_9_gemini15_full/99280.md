intercontinental:/ˌɪntərˌkɑːntɪˈnɛntl/| adj.|洲际的，跨洲的|Relating to or connecting two or more continents.  It describes something that spans or involves multiple continents.|例句：The airline offers intercontinental flights. 这家航空公司提供洲际航班。

例句：We are planning an intercontinental trip next year. 我们计划明年进行一次洲际旅行。


近义词：transcontinental, international


反义词：local, regional, national


词性:形容词
