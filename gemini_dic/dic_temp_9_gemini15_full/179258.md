It can also refer to the repeated examination or analysis of something to refine understanding or interpretation.|例句：The redistillations of the essential oil yielded a purer product.  再蒸馏精油得到了更纯净的产品。


The repeated analysis of the data led to several redistillations of the initial hypothesis. 对数据的反复分析导致了对初始假设的多次重新提炼。|近义词：reprocessing, refinement, purification |反义词：crudification, adulteration
