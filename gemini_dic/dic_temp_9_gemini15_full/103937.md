judgments:/ˈdʒʌdʒmənts/ | n. | 判断，判决，意见 | The act of forming an opinion or making a decision based on available information.  It can refer to a formal legal decision or a more informal opinion. |例句：The judge's judgments were fair and impartial.  法官的判决公平公正。

The jury delivered its judgments after a lengthy deliberation. 陪审团经过长时间的商议后作出了判决。


近义词：decisions, opinions, verdicts, assessments, conclusions | 反义词：ignorance, indifference, uncertainty | 词性：名词

词源:
The word "judgments" originates from the Old French word "juggement," which in turn comes from the Latin word "iudicium."  "Iudicium" is derived from "iudex," meaning "judge."  Therefore, the word's root signifies the act of judging or the result of a judge's decision.
