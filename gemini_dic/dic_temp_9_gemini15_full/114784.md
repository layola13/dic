maltose:/ˈmɔːltoʊs/|/ˈmɒltoʊs/| n. |麦芽糖|A disaccharide sugar produced by the action of diastase on starch, consisting of two glucose units linked together.  It is a white, crystalline, soluble substance obtained from germinating cereals and used in confectionery and brewing. |The baker used maltose to sweeten the bread. 面包师用麦芽糖来使面包变甜。  The enzyme amylase breaks down starch into maltose. 淀粉酶将淀粉分解成麦芽糖。| sucrose, glucose |  |noun|
