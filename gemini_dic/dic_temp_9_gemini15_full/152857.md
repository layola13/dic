pedagoguing:/ˈpɛdəɡɔːɡɪŋ/ | v. | 教学；教育；训导 | The act of teaching or instructing, often implying a somewhat didactic or overly instructive manner.  It can suggest an excessive focus on teaching methods rather than the subject matter itself. |  例句：His constant pedagoguing made the students resentful.  他的不断说教让学生们很反感。


例句：The professor's pedagoguing style bored many students. 教授的教学风格让许多学生感到厌烦。


近义词：teaching, instructing, lecturing, schooling, tutoring | 反义词：learning (as a contrasting activity), neglecting (opposite of actively teaching) | 词性：verb
