self-abnegatory: /ˌsɛlf ˈæbnɪɡeɪtɔːri/ | adj. | 自我否定的；自我牺牲的 | Denoting or characterized by self-denial or self-sacrifice.  It implies a renunciation of self-interest or personal desires for a higher purpose or ideal. |

例句：

His self-abnegatory lifestyle, marked by extreme asceticism, surprised many who knew him.  他的自我否定的生活方式，以极端的禁欲主义为标志，让许多认识他的人都感到惊讶。


Her self-abnegatory dedication to her family left her with little time for her own pursuits. 她对家庭的自我牺牲的奉献让她几乎没有时间追求自己的事业。


近义词：self-denying, self-sacrificing, ascetic, altruistic


反义词：self-indulgent, selfish, egoistic, hedonistic


词性：形容词


词源:

* **self-**:  Prefix indicating "of or relating to oneself."  源自古英语 *self*，意为“自身”。

* **abnegate**:  Verb meaning "to deny oneself something; to renounce; to reject."  源自拉丁语 *abnegare*，由 *ab-* (away from) 和 *negare* (to deny) 组成。

* **-tory**: Suffix forming adjectives meaning "of the nature of; relating to."  一个形容词后缀，表示“……的性质；与……有关的”。


因此，"self-abnegatory" 字面上理解为“自我否定的”，它描述的是一种行为或性格，其特征是放弃自身利益、欲望或需求。
