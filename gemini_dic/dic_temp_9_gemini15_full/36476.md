coleader:/ˈkoʊˌliːdər/| n. |共同领导者，协同领导者 |A coleader is someone who shares leadership responsibilities with another person or people.  They work together to guide and direct a group or project. |Example:  The project was successfully completed thanks to the efforts of the coleaders.  这个项目多亏了共同领导者的努力而成功完成。|近义词：co-chair, joint leader, partner in leadership|反义词：sole leader, dictator
