respectively: /rɪˈspektɪvli/ | adv. | 分别地，各自地 |  This adverb indicates that things are considered individually and in the order they are mentioned. It shows the separate application of something to each item in a list. | 例句：Tom and Mary received A and B grades respectively. 汤姆和玛丽分别获得了A和B的成绩。

例句：The colors of the flags are red, white, and blue, respectively.  旗帜的颜色分别是红色、白色和蓝色。


近义词：separately, individually, each, in turn | 反义词：together, collectively | 词性：副词
