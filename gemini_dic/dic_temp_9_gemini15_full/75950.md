funicular:/fjuːˈnɪkjʊlə(r)/| n. |缆车；索道|A funicular is a cable railway typically used on steep inclines, such as mountainsides, where a conventional railway would be impractical. It uses a cable to pull cars up and down the incline.  |例句：The funicular ride up the mountain offered breathtaking views.  缆车登山之旅提供了令人叹为观止的景色。

The funicular was out of service due to heavy snow.  由于大雪，缆车停运了。|近义词：cable railway, incline railway|反义词：None specifically, but terms like "staircase" or "road" could be considered opposites in the context of transportation on steep inclines.
