knee-jerk:/ˈniːdʒɜːrk/|/ˈniːdʒɜːrk/| adj., n.|膝跳反射的；下意识的；反应迟钝的|An automatic, unthinking response; a reflex action.  It can refer to a physical reflex or a figurative, automatic reaction to a situation.|例句：His knee-jerk reaction was to blame his colleague.他的下意识反应是责怪他的同事。
例句：The knee-jerk reflex is a common neurological test. 膝跳反射是常见的神经系统检查。|近义词：reflexive, automatic, instinctive, impulsive|反义词：deliberate, considered, thoughtful, premeditated|

词源:
The term "knee-jerk" originates from the physiological reflex action of the knee, where a tap below the kneecap causes the lower leg to extend involuntarily.  The figurative use extends this to any automatic, unthinking response.
