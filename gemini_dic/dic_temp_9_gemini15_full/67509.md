extracted:/ɪkˈstræktɪd/ | adj. |  提取的；摘录的 |  Taken or selected from a larger whole.  Something that has been extracted has been removed from something else. | Example: The extracted information was crucial to the investigation.  提取的信息对调查至关重要。|近义词：removed, selected, derived | 反义词：inserted, added


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The researcher extracts data from the database. = 研究人员从数据库中提取数据。
The company extracts oil from the wells. = 公司从油井中提取石油。

一般过去时:
They extracted the tooth yesterday. = 他们昨天拔了牙。
She extracted a key passage from the novel. = 她从小说中摘录了一段重要的文字。
