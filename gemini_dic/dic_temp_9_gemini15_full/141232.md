ooze:/uːz/| v. n.|渗出；慢慢流出；（液体、气体等）缓缓流动；|To flow or leak slowly; to gradually become noticeable or apparent.|The honey oozed from the broken jar.蜂蜜从破了的罐子里渗了出来。|The news oozed out slowly.消息慢慢传了出来。|drip, seep, trickle| solidify, harden|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mud oozes slowly. = 泥巴慢慢地渗出。
The water oozes from the crack. = 水从裂缝中渗出。

一般过去时:
The blood oozed from the wound. = 血从伤口渗出。
The liquid oozed out of the container. = 液体从容器里渗了出来。

现在完成时:
The oil has oozed into the soil. = 油已经渗透到土壤里了。
The truth has oozed out to the public. = 真相已经泄露给了公众。

过去完成时:
The sap had oozed out before we noticed. = 我们注意到之前，树液已经渗出来了。
The secret had oozed out long before the meeting. = 会议之前很久，秘密就已经泄露了。


将来时:
The lava will ooze down the mountainside. = 岩浆将会沿着山坡缓缓流下。
The information will surely ooze out eventually. = 信息最终肯定会泄露。

虚拟语气:
If the dam broke, water would ooze into the valley. = 如果大坝决堤，水就会渗入山谷。
I wish the news wouldn't ooze out so quickly. = 我希望消息不会这么快泄露。

被动语态:
The poison was oozed into the victim's drink. = 毒药被渗入受害者的饮料中。
The message was slowly oozed to the media. = 信息慢慢地泄露给了媒体。

疑问句:
Is the jelly oozing? = 果冻在渗出吗？
Does the fluid ooze easily? = 液体容易渗出吗？


否定句:
The water didn't ooze from the pipe. = 水没有从管道里渗出来。
The information didn't ooze out immediately. = 信息没有立即泄露。



PHRASE->
