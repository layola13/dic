shanty:/ˈʃænti/| n.|简陋小屋；民歌；棚屋 | A shanty is a roughly built hut or dwelling, typically of wood or other makeshift materials.  It can also refer to a type of sea shanty, a folk song traditionally sung by sailors. |例句：They lived in a dilapidated shanty on the edge of town. 他们在城郊一座破旧的棚屋里居住。|近义词：hut, shack, cabin|反义词：mansion, palace
