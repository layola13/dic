grease-heel:/ɡriːsˈhiːl/| n.|谄媚者，奉承者|A person who uses flattery and insincere praise to gain favor or advantage.  It implies a lack of integrity and a willingness to compromise one's principles for personal gain.|例句：He was known as a grease-heel, always sucking up to the boss for promotions. 他以谄媚者闻名，总是拍老板马屁以求升职。 |近义词：flatterer, sycophant, toady, yes-man|反义词：truth-teller, honest person|名词
