nonfatty:/ˌnɒnˈfæti/| adj. |脱脂的，不含脂肪的 |Containing little or no fat.  This term is commonly used to describe food products that have had most or all of their fat content removed. |Example:  She prefers nonfatty milk in her coffee. 她喜欢在咖啡里加脱脂牛奶。

Example:  The doctor recommended a nonfatty diet to improve his cholesterol levels. 医生建议他采用低脂饮食来改善胆固醇水平。


近义词：low-fat, fat-free, lean


反义词：high-fat, fatty


词性：形容词
