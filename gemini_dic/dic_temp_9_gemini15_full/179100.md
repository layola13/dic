redesigning:/ˌriːdɪˈzaɪnɪŋ/ | v. |重新设计，改头换面 | The act of planning or creating a new design for something.  It implies a significant change or improvement to an existing design. |例句：The company is redesigning its logo to attract a younger audience.  公司正在重新设计其标志以吸引更年轻的受众。

例句：We're redesigning the website to improve user experience. 我们正在重新设计网站以改善用户体验。


近义词：remodeling, revamping, renovating, overhauling

反义词：maintaining, preserving, keeping the same


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are redesigning the kitchen. = 他们在重新设计厨房。
The team is redesigning the software. = 团队正在重新设计软件。


一般过去时:
She redesigned the garden last year. = 她去年重新设计了花园。
They redesigned the product packaging. = 他们重新设计了产品包装。


现在完成时:
They have redesigned the website three times. = 他们已经三次重新设计了网站。
We have redesigned the marketing strategy. = 我们已经重新设计了营销策略。


过去完成时:
They had redesigned the building before the earthquake. = 地震发生前，他们已经重新设计了那栋建筑。
The company had redesigned its logo before the launch. = 公司在发布之前已经重新设计了其标志。


将来时:
We will redesign the office next month. = 我们下个月将重新设计办公室。
They will redesign the curriculum for next year. = 他们将为明年重新设计课程。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would redesign the entire system. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新设计整个系统。
It's time we redesigned our approach to marketing. = 我们该重新设计我们的营销方法了。


被动语态:
The website is being redesigned. = 网站正在被重新设计。
The logo was redesigned last year. = 标志去年被重新设计了。


疑问句:

Will the product be redesigned? = 产品会被重新设计吗？


否定句:
