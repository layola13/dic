relicensing: /ˌriːˈlaɪsənsiŋ/ | v. | 再许可；重新许可 | The act of granting a license again, often after an initial license has expired or been revoked.  It involves renewing or granting a new license for something previously licensed. | Example: The company is seeking relicensing of its software after a successful trial period.  该公司在试用期成功后正在寻求其软件的重新许可。


The company decided to undergo relicensing of its intellectual property to extend its usage rights. 公司决定对其知识产权进行重新许可，以延长其使用权。| 近义词：renewal, reauthorization | 反义词：revocation, cancellation | 动词

词源:
The word "relicensing" is formed by combining the prefix "re-" (meaning "again" or "anew") with the noun "licensing" (the process of granting licenses).  Therefore, it literally means "licensing again."
