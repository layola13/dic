pectin:/ˈpektɪn/| n.|果胶|A soluble gelatinous polysaccharide that is present in ripe fruits and is responsible for the formation of jellies. It is used commercially as a setting agent in jams and jellies.|例句：The pectin in apples helps to thicken the jam.|近义词：gelatin, gelling agent|反义词：None specifically.
