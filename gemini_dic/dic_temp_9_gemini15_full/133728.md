nonfugitively: /ˌnɒnfˈjuːdʒɪtɪvli/ | adv. |  非逃亡地；非潜逃地 | Not in a manner suggesting an attempt to evade capture or arrest.  It implies a willingness to remain and face consequences. |


例句：

He remained nonfugitively at his home, awaiting trial. = 他待在家里，等待审判，没有试图逃跑。

The suspect surrendered nonfugitively to the police. = 嫌疑犯主动向警方自首，没有逃跑。


近义词：willingly, voluntarily, openly, readily


反义词：fugitively, clandestinely, secretly, evasively


词源：

The word is formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "fugitive," which derives from the Latin word "fugitivus," meaning "having fled; runaway."  Therefore, "nonfugitively" means "not in a fugitive manner."


用法说明：

"Nonfugitively" is primarily used in legal and criminal contexts to describe someone's behavior. It specifically highlights the absence of any attempt to flee or evade authorities.  It is a relatively uncommon word but precise in its meaning.  The word emphasizes a deliberate choice to stay and confront the situation, rather than escape.
