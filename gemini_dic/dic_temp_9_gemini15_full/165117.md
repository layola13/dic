preinaugurating: /ˌpriːɪˈnɔːɡjʊreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 就职前期的；就职典礼前的 |  The act of preparing for or doing something before an inauguration.  This word is relatively rare and often used in a formal context, referring to actions or events taking place in the period leading up to an inauguration ceremony. |


例句：

The preinaugurating events included a series of meetings with advisors and cabinet nominees. = 就职典礼前的活动包括与顾问和内阁提名人举行的一系列会议。

Preinaugurating speeches often set the tone for the new administration's policies. = 就职典礼前的演讲通常会为新政府的政策定下基调。


近义词：antecedent, preliminary, preparatory


反义词：postinaugural


词性：动词 (verb)
