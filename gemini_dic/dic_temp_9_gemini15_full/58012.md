drizzle:/ˈdrɪzəl/ | n., v.|细雨，毛毛雨；下毛毛雨 | A light rain falling in very small drops.  |例句：The drizzle made the streets wet and slippery.  细雨使街道又湿又滑。

例句：It was drizzling when we left the house. 我们离开家的时候下着毛毛雨。|近义词：sprinkle, shower, mist |反义词：downpour, deluge


USAGE->
一般现在时:
It drizzles lightly in the morning. = 早上下着毛毛雨。
The rain drizzles steadily throughout the day. = 雨一整天都在淅淅沥沥地下着。

一般过去时:
It drizzled all afternoon yesterday. = 昨天一下午都在下毛毛雨。
We went for a walk even though it drizzled. = 即使下着毛毛雨，我们也去散步了。

现在完成时:
It has drizzled intermittently all week. = 整周都在断断续续地下着毛毛雨。
It has drizzled so much that the ground is saturated. = 下了这么多毛毛雨，地都湿透了。

过去完成时:
It had drizzled for hours before the sun finally came out. = 太阳最终出来之前，已经下了好几个小时的毛毛雨了。
They had drizzled the sauce over the chicken before baking it. = 他们在烤鸡之前淋上了酱汁。

将来时:
It will drizzle tomorrow according to the forecast. = 据预报，明天将会下毛毛雨。
They will drizzle the glaze over the cake before serving. = 他们在上菜之前会在蛋糕上淋上糖霜。

虚拟语气:
If it drizzled tomorrow, we would stay inside. = 如果明天下毛毛雨，我们就待在家里。
I wish it drizzled a little more to water my plants. = 我希望再下点毛毛雨来浇我的植物。

被动语态:
The cake was drizzled with chocolate syrup. = 蛋糕上淋着巧克力糖浆。
The streets were drizzled with a light rain. = 街道上洒着细雨。

疑问句:
Is it drizzling outside? = 外面下毛毛雨吗？
Was it drizzling when you left? = 你离开的时候下毛毛雨了吗？

否定句:
It wasn't drizzling when we arrived. = 我们到达的时候没有下毛毛雨。
It doesn't drizzle often in this region. = 这个地区很少下毛毛雨。
