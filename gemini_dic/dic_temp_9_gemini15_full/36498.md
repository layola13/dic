coleseed: /ˈkoʊlsiːd/ | n. |油菜籽；芸薹籽|  The small, round seeds of the colza plant, used to produce oil and animal feed. Coleseed is a crucial crop for biofuel production and provides various industrial applications.  | Example:  The farmer harvested a bountiful crop of coleseed this year.  今年农民收获了大量的油菜籽。|近义词：rapeseed, canola seed |反义词：None specifically.
