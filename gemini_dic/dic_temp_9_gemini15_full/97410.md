infringer:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒər/| n. |侵权者，违规者 |A person or entity that infringes on a right, law, or agreement.  Someone who violates a copyright, patent, or trademark. |例句：The company was sued by an infringer who copied their software. 这家公司被一个抄袭其软件的侵权者起诉了。|例句：He was identified as the infringer of the intellectual property rights. 他被认定为侵犯知识产权的侵权者。|近义词：violator, offender, transgressor |反义词：observer, upholder, respecter |名词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The infringer copies the design without permission. = 侵权者未经许可复制设计。
He regularly infringes on his neighbor's property. = 他经常侵犯邻居的财产。

一般过去时:
The court found the company guilty of infringing the patent. = 法院认定该公司侵犯专利罪名成立。
She infringed the copyright by using the image without permission. = 她未经许可使用图像，侵犯了版权。

现在完成时:
They have infringed upon our trademark multiple times. = 他们多次侵犯了我们的商标权。
The artist has infringed copyright laws in several countries. = 该艺术家在多个国家侵犯了版权法。

过去完成时:
He had infringed the contract before the dispute arose. = 在纠纷发生之前，他已经违反了合同。
The company had infringed on the patent for years before being sued. = 在被起诉之前，该公司多年来一直在侵犯专利。

将来时:
They will infringe on the agreement if they continue this behavior. = 如果他们继续这种行为，他们将违反协议。
She will likely infringe the law if she proceeds with this plan. = 如果她继续这个计划，她很可能会违反法律。


虚拟语气:
If he infringed the rules, he would be punished. = 如果他违反了规则，他将受到惩罚。
I wish he had not infringed on my privacy. = 我真希望他没有侵犯我的隐私。

被动语态:
The copyright was infringed by the unauthorized use of the song. = 该歌曲的未经授权使用侵犯了版权。
The patent was infringed by a competitor's product. = 一家竞争对手的产品侵犯了该专利。

疑问句:
Did the company infringe any laws? = 这家公司是否违反了任何法律？
Has he ever infringed on your rights? = 他曾经侵犯过你的权利吗？

否定句:
The company did not infringe the copyright. = 该公司没有侵犯版权。
He has never infringed on anyone's property. = 他从未侵犯过任何人的财产。
