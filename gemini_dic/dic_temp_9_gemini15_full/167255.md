pretest:/ˈpriːtest/| n. |预测试，预考|A test given before the actual test to assess a student's readiness or to identify areas needing improvement.  It's used to gauge the student's current level of understanding and to inform further instruction. |例句：The teacher gave a pretest to see what the students already knew about the topic. 老师进行了一次预测试，以了解学生们对这个主题已经了解多少。

The students took a pretest before the final exam. 学生们在期末考试前进行了一次预测试。|近义词：trial test, preliminary test, diagnostic test|反义词：post-test
