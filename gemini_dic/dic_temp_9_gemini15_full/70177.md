fibriform:/ˈfaɪbrɪfɔːrm/| adj.|纤维状的；纤维形的|Resembling a fiber or fibers in form or structure; threadlike.|例句：The tissue showed a fibriform appearance under the microscope. 显微镜下，该组织呈现纤维状外观。
例句：The roots of the plant were long and fibriform. 植物的根又长又细，像纤维一样。|近义词：filamentous, threadlike, fibrous|反义词：massive, bulky
