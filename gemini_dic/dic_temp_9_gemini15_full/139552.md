obtundent:/əbˈtʌndənt/| adj.|使迟钝的；使麻木的；减轻（疼痛）的|Something that obtunds dulls or reduces the sharpness or intensity of something, often sensations or mental faculties.  It can refer to a substance that numbs or a process that blunts. |例句：The medication had an obtundent effect on his pain.  药物对他疼痛有减轻作用。

例句：The constant noise had an obtundent effect on his concentration.  持续的噪音让他难以集中注意力。


近义词：blunting, numbing, dulling, deadening|反义词：sharpening, intensifying, stimulating

词性：形容词
