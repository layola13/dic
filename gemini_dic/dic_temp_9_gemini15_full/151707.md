departure:/dɪˈpɑːrtʃər/ | n. |离开，出发；背离；去世 |The act of leaving or going away from a place.  It can also refer to a deviation from a standard, rule, or course. |例句：The departure of the train was delayed. 火车晚点了。
The departure from tradition caused controversy.  背离传统引起了争议。|近义词：leaving, going, exit, divergence |反义词：arrival, return
