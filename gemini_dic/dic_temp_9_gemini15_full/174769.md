rabblers:/ˈræblərz/| n.|煽动者，闹事者，起哄的人|A rabble-rouser is someone who stirs up trouble or incites violence or disorder among a group of people.  They often use inflammatory language and actions to achieve their goals, which may be political, social, or personal.|例句：The rabblers incited the crowd to riot.  煽动者煽动人群暴动。The speaker's words were interpreted by some as rabble-rousing.  一些人认为演讲者的言辞是在煽动闹事。|近义词：agitators, troublemakers, incendiaries, instigators|反义词：peacemakers, conciliators
