extractive:/ɪkˈstræktɪv/| adj.|提取的；萃取的；摘录的|Relating to or denoting the process of extracting something.  It often refers to methods that directly select information from a source, without any transformation or inference.|例句：The study employed an extractive summarization method to condense the lengthy report. 这项研究采用了一种提取式摘要方法来压缩冗长的报告。


例句：The extractive process yielded a potent essential oil from the plant.  提取过程从植物中提取出高效的精油。


近义词：selective, derivative


反义词：synthetic, abstractive, inferential


词性：adj.
