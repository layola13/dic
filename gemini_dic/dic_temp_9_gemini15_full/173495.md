quasi-final:/ˈkwɑːzi faɪnəl/ | adj. |准决赛的；几乎最终的；半决赛的 |  Nearly final; not completely final but very close.  It suggests a stage that precedes the final stage but has significant implications for the final outcome. | 例句：The quasi-final match was incredibly intense. = 准决赛的比赛非常激烈。 The committee reached a quasi-final decision on the budget. = 委员会就预算做出了一个几乎最终的决定。 | 近义词：semifinal, penultimate, almost final | 反义词： preliminary, initial, introductory
