kins:/kɪnz/| n.|亲属，亲戚|Relatives; family members.  The word often refers to blood relatives, but can sometimes include close friends or those considered family.|例句：My kins are coming to visit this weekend.  我的亲戚们这周末要来拜访。|例句：He considered his close friends as kins.|他把他的密友视为亲人。|近义词：relatives, family, kinfolk|反义词：strangers, outsiders
