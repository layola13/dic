melodramas:/ˈmɛləˌdrɑːməz/| n. |情节剧；通俗剧 |A melodrama is a dramatic work that exaggerates plot and characters in order to appeal to the emotions.  It often features sensational events, stereotypical characters (e.g., virtuous heroes, villainous villains), and a clear-cut conflict between good and evil. |The soap opera was a classic melodrama, full of betrayal, romance, and improbable twists. 这部肥皂剧是一部典型的通俗剧，充满了背叛、浪漫和不可思议的转折。

The play was a real melodrama; the villain was clearly evil and the heroine was impossibly pure. 这出戏真是一个情节剧；反派明显是邪恶的，女主角则是不可能纯洁的。
