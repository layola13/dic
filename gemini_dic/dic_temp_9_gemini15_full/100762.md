intratropical: /ˌɪntrəˈtrɒpɪkəl/ | adj. | 热带内部的；位于热带的 |  Situated within the tropics; occurring or existing within the tropics.  The word refers to a region or phenomenon confined to the area between the tropics of Cancer and Capricorn. | Example: Intratropical convergence zones are areas of low pressure where trade winds converge. 热带辐合带是信风辐合的低压区。  | 近义词: tropical, intertropical | 反义词: extratropical
