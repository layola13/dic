halfsmiling:/ˈhæfˌsmaɪlɪŋ/| adj.|半笑着的，带着一丝微笑的|Partially smiling; showing a slight or hesitant smile.  It suggests a smile that is not full or genuine, perhaps indicating uncertainty, shyness, or a reserved emotion. |例句：She gave a halfsmiling glance at him before turning away. 她半笑着瞥了他一眼，然后转过身去。

例句：He offered a halfsmiling, slightly apologetic nod. 他带着一丝歉意，半笑着点了点头。


近义词：partially smiling, faintly smiling, slightly smiling

反义词：grinning, beaming, scowling
