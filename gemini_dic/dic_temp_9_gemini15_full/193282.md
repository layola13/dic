seeling:/ˈsiːlɪŋ/| n.|密封；销售；销售额|The act of sealing something, or the process of selling goods or services.  It can also refer to the amount of sales.|例句：The sealing of the envelope ensured the letter's privacy. 信封的密封确保了信件的隐私。 The company's sealing achieved record-high sales last quarter. 公司的销售额在上个季度达到了历史新高。|近义词：selling, closure, marketing|反义词：opening, purchase


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is sealing the packages for shipment. = 公司正在密封包裹以便运输。
He is sealing the deal with a handshake. = 他正在通过握手来敲定交易。


一般过去时:
They sealed the documents in a vault. = 他们将文件密封在保险库中。
The sales team sealed the biggest deal of the year. = 销售团队完成了今年最大的一笔交易。


现在完成时:
We have sealed all the cracks in the wall. = 我们已经封堵了墙上的所有裂缝。
The marketing campaign has sealed the brand's success. = 市场营销活动巩固了品牌的成功。


过去完成时:
They had sealed the container before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经封好了容器。
The manager had sealed the agreement before leaving for vacation. = 经理在休假前已经签署了协议。


将来时:
We will seal the envelope tomorrow. = 我们明天将封好信封。
The company will seal the deal next week.  = 公司下周将敲定交易。


虚拟语气:
If we sealed the deal, we would celebrate. = 如果我们达成协议，我们将庆祝。
I wish we had sealed the cracks sooner. = 我希望我们早点封堵裂缝。


被动语态:
The packages were sealed by the machine. = 包裹是由机器密封的。
The contract was sealed by both parties. = 合同由双方签署。


疑问句:
Has the package been sealed? = 包裹密封了吗？
Was the agreement sealed yesterday? = 协议昨天签署了吗？


否定句:
They didn't seal the container properly. = 他们没有正确地密封容器。
The deal wasn't sealed until the next day. = 直到第二天交易才达成。
