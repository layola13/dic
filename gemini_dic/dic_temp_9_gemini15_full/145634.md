overconsumed:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsuːmd/ | adj. |过度消费的；消耗过度的 | Excessively consumed; beyond the point of reasonable or sustainable use.  |例句：The overconsumed resources have led to environmental damage. 过度消耗的资源导致了环境破坏。  |近义词：exhausted, depleted, wasted |反义词：underutilized, conserved, preserved |
