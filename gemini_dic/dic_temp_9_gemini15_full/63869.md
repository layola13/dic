epilepsy:/ˈɛpɪlɛpsi/ | n. |癫痫病 |A neurological disorder marked by sudden recurrent episodes of excessive cortical neuronal discharge, resulting in seizures with various manifestations.  Epilepsy is characterized by a predisposition to recurrent seizures, which are transient disturbances of brain function. The specific symptoms of a seizure vary widely depending on the location and extent of the abnormal brain activity. |例句：His grandfather suffered from epilepsy.  他祖父患有癫痫病。|例句：The doctor diagnosed her with epilepsy after a series of seizures. 医生在她经历一系列癫痫发作后诊断她患有癫痫病。|近义词：seizure disorder, convulsive disorder |反义词：None specifically, but terms describing neurological normalcy could be considered antonyms.
