invariantly:/ɪnˈveriəntli/ | adv. | 不变地；总是；一定 | Always; without exception.  The adverb invariably emphasizes that something happens consistently and without variation. It suggests a lack of change or deviation. | 例句：He invariably arrives late. 他总是迟到。

例句：The sun invariably rises in the east. 太阳总是从东方升起。


近义词：always, constantly, consistently, regularly, uniformly

反义词：occasionally, sometimes, rarely, seldom, variably
