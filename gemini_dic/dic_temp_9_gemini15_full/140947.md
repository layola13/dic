/wʌnˈpiːsɚz/| n. |连体衣;一件式服装|One-piecers are garments that are made of a single piece of fabric, usually covering the entire body from the shoulders to the legs or ankles. They can be casual or formal.  They are often used to refer to swimsuits or jumpsuits.|例句：She wore a stylish one-piecer to the beach. 她穿着时尚的连体泳衣去海滩。|例句：The mechanic wore a practical one-piecer while working under the car. 机械师在车底下工作时穿着一件实用的连体衣。|近义词：jumpsuit, playsuit, swimsuit (depending on context)|反义词：two-piece, separates (opposite of a one-piece garment)
