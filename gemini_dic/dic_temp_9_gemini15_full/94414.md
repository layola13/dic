implicature:/ˈɪmplɪkətʃər/ | n. |蕴涵意义，暗示，含蓄意义 | The meaning that is implied by an utterance, but not explicitly stated.  It is something suggested or hinted at, rather than directly expressed.  Implicatures rely on shared knowledge and context between speakers. |  Her silence implied consent.  她的沉默暗示着同意。

The statement "It's cold in here" might implicature a request to close the window, depending on the context.  “这里很冷”这句话可能暗示着请求关窗，这取决于语境。

|近义词：implication, suggestion, insinuation | 反义词：explicit statement, direct statement |  名词
