redepreciate:/ˌriːdɪˈpriːʃieɪt/ | v. | 重新贬值；再次贬值 | To depreciate again; to lower the value of something a second time.  | The currency redepreciated sharply after the election.  货币在大选后急剧贬值。 | The company's stock redepreciated after the bad news was released. 公司的股票在坏消息发布后再次贬值。| appreciate, devalue | appreciate, revalue | 动词

词源:
The word "redepreciate" is formed by adding the prefix "re-" (meaning "again") to the verb "depreciate."  "Depreciate" itself comes from the Latin "depretiare," meaning "to lessen the price of."  Therefore, "redepreciate" literally means "to lessen the price of again."
