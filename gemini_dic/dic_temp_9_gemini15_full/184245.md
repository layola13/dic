resolvedness: /rɪˈzɑːlvɪdnəs/ | n. | 坚决；果断；坚定；决心 | Determination; firmness of purpose; the quality of being resolved.  It implies a strong and unwavering commitment to a course of action or belief. |


例句：

Her resolvedness in the face of adversity was inspiring.  | 她面对逆境时的坚定令人鼓舞。

The team showed remarkable resolvedness in pursuing their goal. | 这支团队在追求目标的过程中表现出非凡的决心。


近义词：determination, firmness, resolve, dedication, commitment


反义词：indecision, wavering, vacillation, weakness, irresolution


词性: 名词
