enragedly:/ɪnˈreɪdʒɪdli/| adv. |狂怒地，暴怒地 |in a furious or enraged manner| The dog barked enragedly at the intruder.  这只狗对着入侵者狂吠不止。| He spoke enragedly about the injustice. 他愤怒地谈论着不公正的事情。| angrily, furiously, wrathfully |calmly, peacefully


USAGE->
This word is an adverb,  and therefore doesn't conjugate like verbs or nouns.  The tense of the verb it modifies will determine the overall tense of the sentence.
