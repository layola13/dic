screamingly: /ˈskriːmɪŋli/ | adv. | 极其，极度地，令人震惊地 | Extremely, intensely, to a shocking degree;  in a screaming manner.  | 例句：The colors were screamingly bright.  = 颜色极其鲜艳。

The movie was screamingly funny. = 这部电影极其搞笑。


近义词：exceedingly, incredibly, outrageously, tremendously, wildly


反义词：subtly, faintly, mildly
