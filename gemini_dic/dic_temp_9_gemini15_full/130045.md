non-performing:/ˌnɒn pərˈfɔːmɪŋ/ | adj. | 不履行的；不良的；呆账的 | Not performing as expected or required; failing to generate income or meet obligations.  | The bank is concerned about the increasing number of non-performing loans.  这家银行担心不良贷款数量的增加。  | delinquent, defaulting, bad | performing, good


近义词：delinquent, defaulting, bad, substandard

反义词：performing, good, productive


例句:

The non-performing assets of the company are a major concern.  该公司的不良资产是一个主要问题。

The bank has a high percentage of non-performing loans. 这家银行的不良贷款比例很高。


词源：

* **non-** :  prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of"
* **performing** : present participle of the verb "perform," meaning "to carry out, accomplish, or fulfill."

The word combines the negative prefix "non-" with the present participle "performing" to describe something that is not fulfilling its expected function or obligation.
