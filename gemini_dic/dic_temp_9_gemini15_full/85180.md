harelike:/ˈhɛəlaɪk/| adj.|像野兔的；胆怯的；易受惊吓的|Resembling a hare; timid; easily frightened.|The harelike creature darted into the bushes when it heard a sound.  那只像野兔一样的生物听到声音就窜进了灌木丛。


The harelike rabbit hid in the tall grass.  那只像野兔的兔子藏在高高的草丛中。


近义词：rabbit-like, timid, fearful, shy

反义词：bold, brave, courageous
