nondocumentary:/ˌnɒndɒkjuˈmentəri/| adj. |非纪录片的|Not pertaining to or characteristic of a documentary film; fictional or otherwise not based on factual events.  It describes something that is not a documentary.  This term is primarily used to describe films or other media. |例句：The film, while visually stunning, is ultimately a nondocumentary work of fiction. 这部电影虽然视觉效果惊人，但最终是一部虚构的非纪录片作品。

例句：Many viewers mistook the nondocumentary film for a true account of events. 许多观众把这部非纪录片误认为是真实事件的报道。


近义词：fictional, nonfactual, imaginative, invented

反义词：documentary, factual, realistic


词性:形容词
