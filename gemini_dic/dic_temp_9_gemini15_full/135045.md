/ˌnɒnɒkˈtɜːnəl/ | adj. |非夜行的；昼行的 | Not active at night; active during the day.  This word describes animals or creatures that are active during the day rather than at night. It is the opposite of nocturnal. | Example:  Many birds are nonnocturnal, preferring to forage and hunt during daylight hours.  许多鸟类是非夜行的，它们更喜欢在白天觅食和狩猎。 | 近义词: diurnal, daytime | 反义词: nocturnal
