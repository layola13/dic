relational:/rɪˈleɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 关系的；相关的；关联的 | Relating to or involving relationships between people or things.  The word emphasizes the interconnectedness and interdependence among elements. | Example:  Relational databases store data in interconnected tables.  关系型数据库将数据存储在相互关联的表中。  The relational aspect of their work was crucial to success.  他们工作的关联性对成功至关重要。|近义词：interrelated, connected, interdependent | 反义词：independent, unrelated, disconnected |  形容词
