sloggy:/ˈslɒɡi/| adj.| 沉重的；泥泞的；懒散的；乏味的 |  Describes something heavy, difficult to move, or lacking energy and enthusiasm. It can refer to physical objects, conditions, or even people's behavior.  |例句：The mud was sloggy and difficult to walk through.  泥泞不堪，难以行走。

The sloggy pace of the meeting bored everyone.  会议节奏缓慢沉闷，让每个人都厌烦。|近义词：heavy, muddy, sluggish, lethargic, tedious, dull |反义词：light, nimble, energetic, lively, exciting, stimulating
