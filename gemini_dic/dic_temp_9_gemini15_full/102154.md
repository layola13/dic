issuer:/ˈɪʃuːər/| n. |发行人，签发人，债务发行者|The person or organization that issues something, such as securities or currency.  It typically refers to entities responsible for creating and distributing financial instruments.|Example: The issuer of the bond is a large corporation.  发行债券的是一家大公司。|近义词：emitter, distributor, originator|反义词：recipient, receiver


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bank issues credit cards. = 银行发行信用卡。
The government issues passports. = 政府签发护照。


一般过去时:
The company issued a press release. = 公司发布了一份新闻稿。
They issued a warning about the storm. = 他们发布了关于暴风雨的警告。


现在完成时:
The company has issued several bonds this year. = 公司今年已经发行了几笔债券。
The government has issued new regulations. = 政府已经发布了新的规章制度。


过去完成时:
The bank had already issued the loan before the deadline. = 在截止日期之前，银行已经发放了贷款。
They had issued a statement before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他们已经发表了一份声明。


将来时:
The company will issue its financial report next week. = 公司将在下周发布其财务报告。
The government will issue new guidelines soon. = 政府很快就会发布新的指导方针。


虚拟语气:
If the bank issued more loans, the economy would improve. = 如果银行发放更多贷款，经济就会好转。
I wish the company had issued a recall sooner. = 我希望公司早点发出召回通知。


被动语态:
The bonds were issued last month. = 债券是上个月发行的。
The new regulations are being issued by the government. = 新的规章制度正在由政府发布。


疑问句:
Who issued this certificate? = 谁签发了这张证书？
Which company issued this stock? = 哪家公司发行了这只股票？


否定句:
The company did not issue any new shares. = 公司没有发行任何新股。
The bank has not yet issued the loan. = 银行尚未发放贷款。
