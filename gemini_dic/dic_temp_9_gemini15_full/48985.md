deghosting:/diːˈɡoʊstɪŋ/ | v. | 去鬼影，去除重影 | To remove or eliminate ghosting, a visual artifact that appears as a faint, blurry image of a previously displayed image. This is often seen in displays with poor refresh rates or slow response times.  The process might involve adjusting display settings, using different hardware, or applying image processing techniques. | Example:  The new software successfully deghosted the video footage, making it much clearer.  新的软件成功地去除了视频画面中的重影，使其清晰得多。|近义词：de-ghost, remove ghosting, eliminate ghosting | 反义词：ghost, introduce ghosting
