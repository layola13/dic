nonamphibiously:/ˌnɒnæmˈfɪbiəsli/ | adj. |非两栖地；非两栖性地 | Not in an amphibious manner; not capable of living both on land and in water.  This word describes something that lacks the characteristics of being amphibious. | Example: The creature moved nonamphibiously, solely on land.  这个生物以非两栖的方式移动，只在陆地上。


近义词: terrestrially, exclusively on land

反义词: amphibiously
