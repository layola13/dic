non-radioactive: /ˌnɒnˌreɪdiəʊˈæktɪv/ | adj. |非放射性的| Not emitting or exhibiting radioactivity.  This adjective describes substances or materials that do not emit ionizing radiation.  It is the opposite of radioactive.|

例句：

The sample was confirmed to be non-radioactive after thorough testing.  测试后证实样品是非放射性的。

Non-radioactive tracers are often used in medical imaging.  非放射性示踪剂常用于医学影像。


近义词：inactive (in the context of radioactivity), inert (in some contexts)

反义词：radioactive
