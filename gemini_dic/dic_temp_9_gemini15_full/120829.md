misauthorizing: /ˌmɪsɔːθəˈraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 滥用权力；错误授权；误授权 |  The act of giving someone authority or power inappropriately or incorrectly, often leading to misuse or harm.  It implies a wrongful or improper delegation of authority. | 例句：The manager was accused of misauthorizing funds, diverting them to personal use. = 经理被指控滥用资金，将其挪作私用。

The CEO's misauthorizing of the project led to significant delays and cost overruns. = 首席执行官对项目的错误授权导致了严重的延误和成本超支。

近义词：misusing, abusing, misappropriating,  illegally authorizing | 反义词：authorizing properly, legitimately empowering
