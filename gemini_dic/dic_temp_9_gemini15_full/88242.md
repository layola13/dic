hindsight:/ˈhaɪndsaɪt/| n. |后见之明，事后诸葛亮 |Understanding things clearly only after they have happened.  |Example: With hindsight, I should have taken that job. 事后看来，我应该接受那份工作的。|近义词：retrospection, retrospect |反义词：foresight, premonition|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Hindsight often provides a clearer understanding of past events. = 后见之明通常能让人更清晰地理解过去的事件。
Their hindsight helps them make better decisions in the future. = 他们的后见之明有助于他们在未来做出更好的决策。


一般过去时:
Hindsight revealed the flaws in his original plan. = 后见之明揭示了他最初计划中的缺陷。
