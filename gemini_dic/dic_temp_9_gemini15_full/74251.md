foxhounds:/ˈfɒkshaʊndz/ | n. |猎狐犬| A foxhound is a type of hound dog specifically bred for hunting foxes.  They are known for their stamina, speed, and keen sense of smell.  Foxhounds typically work in packs, following the scent trail of a fox. | Example: The pack of foxhounds followed the scent across the fields.  一群猎狐犬穿过田野追逐气味。 The hunt ended when the foxhounds cornered the fox. 猎狐犬将狐狸逼入绝境时，狩猎结束了。|近义词：hounds, scent hounds|反义词：无
