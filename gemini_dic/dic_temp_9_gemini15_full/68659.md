farads:/ˈfærədz/ | n. |法拉（电容单位） |Farad is the SI unit of electrical capacitance.  It is defined as the capacitance of a capacitor which requires a charge of one coulomb to raise its potential by one volt.| 例句：The capacitor has a capacitance of 10 farads.  这个电容器的电容为10法拉。

The capacitance of this circuit is measured in farads. 这个电路的电容以法拉为单位测量。|近义词：capacitance (though capacitance is a property, not a unit)|反义词：无|
