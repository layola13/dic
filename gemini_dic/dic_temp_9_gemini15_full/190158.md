sankha:/ˈʃæŋkə/| n.|贝壳；螺壳；海螺；(印度教)法螺；计数器|A sankha is a type of shell, often a conch shell, that has religious and symbolic significance in Hinduism and Buddhism. It can also refer to a counting device.  |例句：The priest blew the sankha to announce the ceremony.  祭司吹响法螺宣布仪式开始。|例句：Ancient civilizations used sankhas as currency or counting tools. 古代文明使用贝壳作为货币或计数工具。|近义词：conch, shell, counter|反义词:|N/A
