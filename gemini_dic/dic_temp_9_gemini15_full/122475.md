moblogging:/ˈmoʊˌblɒɡɪŋ/ | n. | 手机博客；移动博客 | The act of creating and posting blog entries using a mobile device.  This often involves using a smartphone or tablet to write, edit, and publish blog posts on the go.  | Example:  Moblogging allows journalists to report breaking news from the field instantly. = 手机博客允许记者即时报道现场突发新闻。  | 近义词: mobile blogging, on-the-go blogging | 反义词:  None (antonyms are not directly applicable) | 名词
