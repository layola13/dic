envy:/ˈɛnvi/ | n.  v. | 嫉妒；羡慕 |  A feeling of discontented or resentful longing aroused by someone else's possessions, qualities, or luck.  |例句：I felt a pang of envy when I saw her new car.  我看到她的新车时感到一阵嫉妒。

例句：His success filled me with envy. 他的成功让我充满了嫉妒。


近义词：jealousy, covetousness | 反义词：contentment, satisfaction | 词性: 名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I envy her talent. = 我羡慕她的才能。
They envy his wealth. = 他们羡慕他的财富。


一般过去时:
I envied her success. = 我嫉妒她的成功。
He envied his brother's popularity. = 他嫉妒他兄弟的受欢迎程度。


现在完成时:
I have envied her since she was a child.= 我从小就羡慕她。
We have envied their lifestyle for years. = 多年来我们一直羡慕他们的生活方式。


过去完成时:
I had envied her beauty for years before I met her. = 在我遇到她之前，我已经羡慕她的美貌多年了。
They had envied his position long before he got it. = 在他获得那个职位很久以前，他们就一直羡慕他的职位了。


将来时:
I will envy her if she wins the award. = 如果她获奖，我会羡慕她。
They will envy his achievement in the future. = 他们将来会羡慕他的成就。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would not envy her. = 如果我是你，我不会羡慕她。
I wish I didn't envy his success so much. = 我真希望我不那么羡慕他的成功。


被动语态:
Her beauty is envied by many. = 她的美貌被许多人羡慕。
His achievements are envied by his colleagues. = 他的成就受到同事们的羡慕。


疑问句:
Do you envy her beauty? = 你羡慕她的美貌吗？
Does he envy my success? = 他羡慕我的成功吗？


否定句:
I don't envy her at all. = 我一点也不羡慕她。
He doesn't envy her new job. = 他不羡慕她的新工作。


PHRASE->

die of envy: 嫉妒得要死
the envy of someone:  某人的羡慕对象
