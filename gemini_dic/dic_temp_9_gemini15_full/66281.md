excommunicates:/ˌɛksˈkɒmjunɪkeɪts/ | vt. |逐出教籍；开除教籍 | To officially exclude (someone) from a religious community, especially the Christian Church, as a punishment.  | The church excommunicated him for heresy. 教会因其异端邪说而将其逐出教籍。 He was excommunicated from the society for violating its rules. 他因为违反社团规定而被开除。 | 近义词：expel, banish, ostracize | 反义词：reinstate, welcome
