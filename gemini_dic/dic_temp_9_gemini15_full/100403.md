intertalks:/ˈɪntərˌtɔːks/| n.|  内部对话；内部讨论；内部沟通 | Internal conversations or discussions, often within an organization or group.  |例句：The intertalks between the marketing and sales teams revealed a significant disconnect in their strategies.  市场部和销售部之间的内部讨论揭示了他们的战略存在重大脱节。|近义词：internal communications, internal discussions, deliberations |反义词：public discussions, external communications |名词
