pseudonarcotics:/ˌsuːdoʊˈnɑːrkɒtɪks/| n. |拟麻醉剂，假麻醉药|Substances that mimic the effects of narcotics but are not derived from opium or its derivatives.  They may be synthetic or derived from other sources and often have similar pharmacological effects to opiates, but with potentially different mechanisms of action and varying degrees of addictive potential.|例句：The study investigated the effects of various pseudonarcotics on pain perception. 这项研究调查了各种拟麻醉剂对疼痛感知的影响。|近义词：opioid agonist, narcotic mimetic|反义词：narcotic antagonist, analgesic
