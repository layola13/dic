sandbag: /ˈsændˌbæɡ/ | n., v. |沙袋；欺骗；使泄气 |

英文解析:
As a noun, a sandbag is a bag filled with sand, typically used for flood control, weight, or as a makeshift barrier.  As a verb, "to sandbag" means to deliberately underperform in order to gain an advantage later, or to deceive or trick someone.

例句:
(Noun) The workers filled hundreds of sandbags to protect the town from the rising river. = 工人们装满了数百个沙袋以保护小镇免受河水上涨的侵害。

(Noun)  He used a sandbag as a makeshift weight for his workout. = 他用沙袋作为他锻炼的临时配重。


(Verb)  The team sandbagged during the early rounds to conserve energy for the finals. = 该团队在早些轮次中故意表现不佳，以节省精力用于决赛。

(Verb) He tried to sandbag his boss into giving him a raise. = 他试图欺骗他的老板给他加薪。


近义词:
(Noun) sack, bag of sand
(Verb) deceive, trick, underperform, mislead

反义词:
(Verb)  overperform, honestly perform, openly compete
