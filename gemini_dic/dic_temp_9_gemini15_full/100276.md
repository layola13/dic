intersected:/ˌɪntərˈsɛktɪd/| v. |相交，交叉，横切| Past tense and past participle of "intersect".  It describes the point or area where two or more things cross or meet. |例句：The two roads intersected at a busy traffic light.  两条路在一个繁忙的交通灯处交叉。


The lines intersected at a right angle.  两条线成直角相交。


近义词：crossed, overlapped, met, joined |反义词：separated, diverged, parted |动词
