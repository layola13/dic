disciplines: /ˈdɪsəplɪnz/ | n. |学科；纪律；训练 |  The systematic study of a particular subject or field of study, or the training and control of oneself or others.  It can also refer to the act of punishing someone. |

例句：

The university offers a wide range of disciplines.  | 这所大学提供各种各样的学科。

She maintains strict disciplines in her daily routine. | 她日常生活中保持着严格的纪律。


近义词：subjects, fields of study, branches of knowledge, training, control, punishment

反义词：chaos, disorder, lawlessness
