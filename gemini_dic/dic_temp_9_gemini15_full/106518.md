kreosote:/ˈkriːəsoʊt/ | n. | 杂酚油；木馏油 | A dark, thick liquid obtained by the distillation of wood tar, consisting mainly of phenols and used as a preservative for wood and as a disinfectant.  | Example:  The fence posts were treated with kreosote to prevent rot.  这道篱笆柱用杂酚油处理过以防腐烂。  |近义词：coal tar, wood tar | 反义词：None  | 名词
