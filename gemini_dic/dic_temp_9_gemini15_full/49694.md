demo:/ˈdɛmoʊ/ | n. v. | 演示；样品；试制品 | a demonstration or exhibition of a product, typically to potential customers or investors.  |例句：The company gave a demo of their new software. 公司演示了他们的新软件。  |例句：We saw a demo of the car's new features. 我们看到了这款车新功能的演示。|近义词：demonstration, presentation, sample |反义词：N/A

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company demos its new product every week. = 公司每周都会演示其新产品。
They demo new software at the conference. = 他们在会议上演示新软件。

一般过去时:
The developer demoed the app to the investors. = 开发者向投资者演示了这个应用程序。
We demoed the product at the trade show. = 我们在贸易展上演示了该产品。

现在完成时:
They have demoed the software to several potential clients. = 他们已经向几个潜在客户演示了该软件。
We have demoed the product at multiple events. = 我们已经在多个活动中演示了该产品。

过去完成时:
They had demoed the prototype before the final product launch. = 他们在最终产品发布之前已经演示了原型。
The team had demoed the software to the board before the meeting. = 团队在会议之前已经向董事会演示了该软件。

将来时:
They will demo the new features next week. = 他们下周将演示新功能。
We will demo the product at the upcoming conference. = 我们将在即将举行的会议上演示该产品。

虚拟语气:
If they demoed the product effectively, they would have secured the deal. = 如果他们有效地演示了产品，他们就能达成交易。
I wish they had demoed the software more clearly. = 我希望他们能更清晰地演示该软件。

被动语态:
The new software was demoed at the conference. = 新软件在会议上进行了演示。
The product is being demoed to potential customers. = 该产品正在向潜在客户进行演示。

疑问句:
Will they demo the product tomorrow? = 他们明天会演示产品吗？
Have you ever demoed software before? = 你以前演示过软件吗？

否定句:
They didn't demo the product at the meeting. = 他们在会议上没有演示产品。
We won't demo the new features until next month. = 在下个月之前我们不会演示新功能。
