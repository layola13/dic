realism:/ˈriːəlɪzəm/ | n. |现实主义；逼真；实际情况 |The artistic or literary representation of things as they actually are, without idealization or romanticism.  Realism emphasizes the depiction of everyday life and ordinary people, often focusing on social issues and psychological realism. |例句：The painting is a striking example of realism. 这幅画是现实主义的杰出例子。

例句：Her novels are known for their unflinching realism. 她的小说以其毫不掩饰的现实主义而闻名。


近义词：naturalism, verisimilitude | 反义词：idealism, romanticism


词源：From Middle French réalisme, from réaliser "to realize," from re- + réaliser (to realize).  Ultimately derived from Latin realis "real," from res "thing."
