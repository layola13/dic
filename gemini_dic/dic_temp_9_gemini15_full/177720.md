recapitulating:/ˌriːˈkæpɪtʃʊleɪtɪŋ/| v.|概括；总结；重述|To summarize and restate the main points of something.|The professor recapitulated the key concepts before the exam. 教授在考试前概括了关键概念。|summarizing, summarizing, reiterating|detailing, expanding|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recapitulates the main points at the end of each lecture. 他在每次讲座结束时都会总结要点。
She recapitulates her argument in the conclusion. 她在结论中重述了自己的论点。

过去完成时:
