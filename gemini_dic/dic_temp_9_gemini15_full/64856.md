espousage:/ɪˈspaʊzɪdʒ/ | n. |拥护；支持；婚姻 |The act of espousing, or marrying; a betrothal; marriage.  It also refers to the act of adopting or supporting a cause, belief, or principle. |例句：The espousal of democracy is a key tenet of their political platform. = 拥护民主是他们政治纲领的关键原则。

例句：Their espousal solidified their bond. = 他们的婚姻巩固了他们的关系。


近义词：support, advocacy, adoption, marriage, betrothal | 反义词：rejection, opposition, repudiation


词源：源自古法语 *espouser，意为“娶；嫁”，最终源自拉丁语 sponsus，意为“被许配的人”。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He espouses conservative values. = 他拥护保守的价值观。
She espouses a new theory. = 她支持一种新的理论。


一般过去时:
They espoused the cause of human rights. = 他们拥护人权事业。
He espoused her in marriage. = 他娶了她。


现在完成时:
She has espoused many different causes throughout her life. = 她一生中支持过许多不同的事业。
They have espoused a new lifestyle. = 他们过上了新的生活方式。


过去完成时:
He had already espoused the principles of non-violence before the war. = 在战争爆发前，他已经拥护非暴力原则了。
They had espoused a new faith. = 他们已经皈依了一种新的信仰。


将来时:
He will espouse a new political party. = 他将加入一个新的政党。
She will espouse her beliefs publicly. = 她将公开宣扬她的信仰。


虚拟语气:
If he espoused those ideas, he would be ostracized. = 如果他支持那些观点，他会被排斥。
I wish she had espoused a more moderate approach. = 我希望她采取更温和的方式。


被动语态:
The policy was espoused by the government. = 政府支持这项政策。
His ideas were espoused by many intellectuals. = 他的思想被许多知识分子所拥护。


疑问句:
Does he espouse those controversial views? = 他是否支持那些有争议的观点？
What beliefs does she espouse? = 她信仰什么？


否定句:
He did not espouse the radical ideas presented. = 他不支持提出的激进思想。
She does not espouse violence as a means to an end. = 她不支持暴力作为达成目的的手段。


PHRASE->
espouse a cause = 支持一项事业
espouse a belief = 拥护一种信仰
espouse a theory = 支持一种理论
espouse marriage = 结婚
espouse a lifestyle = 过着…样的生活方式
