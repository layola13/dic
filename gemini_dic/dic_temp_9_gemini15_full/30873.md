chafed:/tʃeɪfd/| v. |擦伤；磨损；激怒；感到不适 |to make (skin) sore by rubbing; to become sore from rubbing; to feel annoyance or irritation; to cause irritation or annoyance |例句：The new shoes chafed my heels. 这双新鞋磨破了我的脚后跟。

The rough wool chafed against his skin.粗糙的羊毛摩擦着他的皮肤。


His constant complaints chafed her. 他不断的抱怨让她很烦躁。


The restrictions chafed at his spirit. 这些限制让他感到压抑。


近义词：irritate, annoy, irritate, gall, abrade |反义词：soothe, please, appease
