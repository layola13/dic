descanting:/dɪˈskæntɪŋ/ | v. |详述；滔滔不绝地谈论；高谈阔论 |  To speak or write at length or in detail; to discourse at length.  |例句：He spent the evening descanting on the merits of his new investment. 他花了整个晚上详述他新投资的优点。

例句：She descants endlessly on her travels. 她没完没了地夸夸其谈她的旅行经历。


近义词：discoursed, expatiated, elaborated, harangued | 反义词：summarized, abridged,condensed


词源:  From Old French *deschanter, from des- (from Latin dis-) + chanter "to sing".  Originally meaning "to sing a descant," a musical embellishment or countermelody, it later evolved to encompass lengthy and detailed speaking or writing.
