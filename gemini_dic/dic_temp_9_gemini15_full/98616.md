instructs:/ɪnˈstrʌkts/| vt.|指示；命令；教导|To give someone instructions or directions on what to do.  It also means to teach someone something.|例句：The teacher instructs her students to write an essay. 老师指示她的学生写一篇论文。|例句：The manual instructs users on how to operate the machine.  说明书指导用户如何操作机器。|近义词：directs, commands, teaches, guides|反义词：follows, obeys


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The teacher instructs the students daily. = 老师每天指导学生。
He instructs his team on the new project. = 他指导他的团队进行新的项目。


一般过去时:
She instructed me to wait outside. = 她指示我在外面等候。
The captain instructed the crew to abandon ship. = 船长命令船员弃船。


现在完成时:
The professor has instructed us on the exam format. = 教授已经指导我们考试的格式。
They have instructed the workers to wear safety gear. = 他们已经指示工人们穿戴安全装备。


过去完成时:
He had instructed his assistant before leaving the office. = 他在离开办公室前已经指示了他的助手。
She had instructed them to meet at the library. = 她已经指示他们在图书馆见面。


将来时:
The manager will instruct the employees on the new policy. = 经理将指示员工新的政策。
They will instruct the participants in the proper procedures. = 他们将指导参与者正确的程序。


虚拟语气:
If he instructed us properly, we wouldn't have made so many mistakes. = 如果他正确地指导我们，我们就不会犯那么多错误。
I wish he had instructed me more clearly. = 我希望他当时能更清楚地指导我。


被动语态:
The students were instructed to complete the assignment by Friday. = 学生们被指示在星期五之前完成作业。
The team was instructed on the new safety regulations. = 团队被指导新的安全规章。


疑问句:
Does the manual instruct how to assemble the furniture? = 说明书指导如何组装家具吗？
Who instructed you to do that? = 谁指示你那样做的？


否定句:
The boss didn't instruct us to work overtime. = 老板没有指示我们加班。
He didn't instruct them clearly enough. = 他没有足够清晰地指导他们。


PHRASE->
instruct someone to do something = 指示某人做某事
instruct someone in something = 在某方面指导某人
instruct someone on something =  关于某事指导某人
clearly instruct = 清晰地指导
fully instruct = 全面指导
