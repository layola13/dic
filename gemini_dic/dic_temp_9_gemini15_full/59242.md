e-cash:/ˈiːkæʃ/| n. |电子现金，电子货币 |Electronic cash, also known as digital cash or electronic money, refers to a digital form of money that is used for online transactions. It is a method of paying for goods and services over the internet without using physical cash or credit cards.  E-cash systems typically involve the use of digital wallets or other forms of electronic storage to hold and transfer funds.  |Example:  Many online retailers accept e-cash as a form of payment. 许多在线零售商接受电子现金作为付款方式。


The increasing popularity of online shopping has led to a surge in the use of e-cash. 在线购物的日益普及导致电子现金的使用激增。|近义词：digital cash, electronic money, online payment |反义词：physical cash, hard cash |名词
