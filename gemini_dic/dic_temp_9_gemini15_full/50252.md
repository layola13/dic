depender:/dɪˈpɛndər/| n. |依靠者，依赖者 |A depender is someone who relies on someone or something else for support, help, or resources.  They are not self-sufficient. |Example:  The elderly woman was a depender on her family for financial assistance.  这位老年妇女依靠家人提供经济援助。|近义词：dependent, reliant |反义词：independent, self-sufficient |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He depends on his parents for financial support. = 他依靠父母的经济资助。
She depends on her colleagues for help with the project. = 她依靠同事帮助完成项目。


一般过去时:
They depended on the rain to water their crops. = 他们依靠雨水灌溉庄稼。
He depended on his instincts to guide him. = 他依靠直觉来指引他。


现在完成时:
We have depended on this software for years. = 我们多年来一直依赖这个软件。
She has depended on her friends for emotional support. = 她一直以来都依靠朋友的情感支持。


过去完成时:
They had depended on the weather forecast, but it was wrong. = 他们依赖天气预报，但预报错了。
She had depended on him to finish the report, but he failed. = 她依赖他完成报告，但他失败了。


将来时:
We will depend on renewable energy sources in the future. = 未来我们将依赖可再生能源。
He will depend on his savings to fund his trip. = 他将依靠他的储蓄来资助他的旅行。


虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I wouldn't depend on public transport. = 如果我有车，我就不会依赖公共交通。
I wish I didn't depend so much on my phone. = 我希望我不那么依赖我的手机。


被动语态:
The success of the project depends on many factors. = 项目的成功取决于许多因素。(被动语态，depend on 本身不常用被动)
The children were depended on by their grandparents for love and care. = 孩子们依靠祖父母的爱和照顾。（被动形式强调被依靠）


疑问句:
Does the outcome depend on the weather? = 结果取决于天气吗？
Who do you depend on for advice? = 你依靠谁来寻求建议？


否定句:
He doesn't depend on anyone for help. = 他不依赖任何人帮助。
She doesn't depend on her parents anymore. = 她不再依赖她的父母了。


PHRASE->
depend on/upon = 依靠，依赖
depend for = 依靠…获得…
to depend on it = 可以相信，指望
it depends = 看情况
That depends. = 这得看情况
