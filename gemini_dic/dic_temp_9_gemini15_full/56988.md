double-dates:/ˈdʌbəl deɪts/| n.|双重约会；两对情侣的约会|A double date is a social occasion where two couples go out together.  It often involves a shared activity, such as dinner, a movie, or other entertainment.|Example: We're going on a double date with John and Mary this weekend.  这个周末我们要和约翰和玛丽一起进行一场双人约会。|近义词：group date, couples' outing|反义词：solo date, single date|名词
