retag:/riːˈtæɡ/| vt.|重新标记，重新标签|To tag (something) again with a different or updated tag.  This often involves changing the metadata or classification of something.|例句：We need to retag these photos with the correct location.|近义词：relabel, reclassify|反义词：untag


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They retag products regularly to reflect changes in pricing. = 他们定期重新标记产品以反映价格变化。
The librarian retagging the books according to the Dewey Decimal System. = 图书管理员按照杜威十进制系统重新标记图书。


一般过去时:
The company retagged all its products after the rebranding. = 公司在重新品牌化后重新标记了所有产品。
I retagged the photos after realizing I had used the wrong location. = 我意识到自己使用了错误的位置后重新标记了照片。
