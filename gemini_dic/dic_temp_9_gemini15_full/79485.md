globalistic:/ˌɡloʊbəˈlɪstɪk/| adj. |全球主义的；全球化的 | Relating to or characterized by globalism; advocating or supporting globalism.  The term often implies a perspective that prioritizes global interests over national or local interests.  |例句：His globalistic views often put him at odds with more nationalist colleagues. 他的全球主义观点常常使他和那些更民族主义的同事发生冲突。


例句：The company's globalistic approach to marketing has led to significant growth in international sales. 这家公司的全球化营销策略使其国际销售额大幅增长。


近义词：globalist, internationalist, cosmopolitan


反义词：nationalistic, protectionist, isolationist


词性：形容词
