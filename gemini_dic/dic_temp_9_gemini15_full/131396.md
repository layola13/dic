nonclaim:/ˈnɒnˌkleɪm/ | n. |未提出索赔；未声明；不认领|The absence of a claim; a failure to make a claim.  This term is often used in insurance contexts to describe a situation where a potential claimant has not yet filed a claim. |Example:  The insurance company noted a high rate of nonclaim for this particular type of policy.  这家保险公司注意到这种特定类型的保单的未提出索赔率很高。  The nonclaim of the missing package meant that we couldn't get a refund.  未认领丢失的包裹意味着我们无法获得退款。|近义词：lack of claim, absence of claim, unclaimed |反义词：claim, assertion
