half-proved:/ˌhɑːfˈpruːvd/| adj.| 半证实的；部分证明的 |Partially or incompletely proven;  suggesting a truth but lacking full confirmation.  It implies that there is some evidence supporting the claim, but not enough to definitively prove it. |例句：The theory is half-proved, needing further research to solidify its claims.  这个理论只得到了部分证实，需要进一步研究来巩固其论点。


The detective had a half-proved case against the suspect, lacking sufficient evidence for a conviction.  侦探对嫌疑人有一个半证实的案子，缺乏足够的证据进行定罪。|近义词：partially proven, tentatively established, partially substantiated|反义词：fully proved, disproved, refuted|
词性：形容词
