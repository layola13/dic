discipline:/ˈdɪsɪplɪn/|/n. vt.|纪律；学科；训练；惩罚|The noun "discipline" refers to a system of rules and methods of training that is used to control someone's behavior.  It can also refer to a branch of knowledge or study. As a verb, it means to train someone to obey rules or a code of behavior, often through punishment.|

例句：
The school has a strict discipline policy.  学校有一套严格的纪律政策。

The students need more discipline in their studies. 学生们需要在学习方面更有纪律。


近义词：order, control, training, subject, method

反义词：chaos, disorder, freedom


词性：n. vt.
