countervail:/ˌkaʊntərˈveɪl/| v.|对抗，抵消，制衡|to offset the effect of something; to counteract|例句：The government took measures to countervail the rise in inflation. 政府采取措施来对抗通货膨胀的上升。The company's strong marketing campaign countervailed its competitors' aggressive pricing. 该公司的强势营销活动抵消了其竞争对手的激进定价。|近义词：offset, counteract, neutralize|反义词：reinforce, support


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government countervails the rise in oil prices. = 政府对抗油价上涨。
This policy countervails the negative impact of globalization. = 这一政策抵消了全球化的负面影响。

一般过去时:
The company countervailed its competitor's actions. = 公司对抗了其竞争对手的行动。
They countervailed the effects of the storm. = 他们抵消了暴风雨的影响。

现在完成时:
The treaty has countervailed the threat of war. = 这项条约已经抵消了战争的威胁。
Her efforts have countervailed the negative publicity. = 她的努力抵消了负面宣传。


过去完成时:
The previous government had countervailed similar economic issues. = 上届政府已经对抗过类似的经济问题。
They had countervailed the effects of the drought before the harvest. = 在收割之前，他们已经抵消了干旱的影响。


将来时:
The new regulations will countervail unfair competition. = 新规将对抗不正当竞争。
We will countervail the negative effects of climate change. = 我们将抵消气候变化的负面影响。


虚拟语气:
If we acted now, we could countervail the crisis. = 如果我们现在采取行动，我们就能抵消危机。
It is important that we countervail the spread of misinformation. = 我们抵消虚假信息的传播非常重要。


被动语态:
The negative effects were countervailed by government intervention. = 负面影响被政府干预所抵消。
The rise in interest rates will be countervailed by increased investment. = 利率上升将被增加的投资所抵消。


疑问句:
How can we countervail the effects of pollution? = 我们如何才能抵消污染的影响？
Does this strategy countervail the risks involved? = 这种策略是否能抵消所涉及的风险？


否定句:
The measures did not countervail the economic downturn. = 这些措施并没有抵消经济衰退。
The plan failed to countervail the damage caused by the fire. = 该计划未能抵消火灾造成的损失。
