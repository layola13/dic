epigeum:/ɛˈpɪdʒiəm/| n. | 地上生长的；地面上的 |  Above ground; growing on the surface of the ground.  The term is often used in botany to describe plants that grow above the ground, as opposed to hypogeal plants which grow below ground. | Example: Many epigeal seeds germinate above the soil surface, relying on sunlight for energy. 许多地上种子在土壤表面上方发芽，依靠阳光获取能量。 | 近义词：terrestrial, aboveground | 反义词：hypogeal, subterranean | 名词
