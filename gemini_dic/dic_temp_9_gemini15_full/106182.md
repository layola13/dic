knocked-out: /ˌnɒktˈaʊt/ | adj. |  击昏的；筋疲力尽的；被淘汰的；(拳击)击倒的 |

英文解析：The adjective "knocked-out" describes someone or something that has been rendered unconscious, extremely tired, or eliminated from a competition.  It often refers to a state of being physically or mentally exhausted, or to being defeated in a knockout competition.

例句：

He was knocked-out cold after the punch.  他被那一拳打晕了。

The long journey left her completely knocked-out.  长途旅行让她筋疲力尽。

Their team was knocked-out in the first round.  他们的队伍在第一轮就被淘汰了。


近义词：unconscious, exhausted, eliminated, defeated,  out cold, wiped out

反义词：conscious, energetic,  victorious, alive

词性: Adjective
