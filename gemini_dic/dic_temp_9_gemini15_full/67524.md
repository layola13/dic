extracurricular:/ˌɛkstrəkəˈrɪkjələr/| adj.|课外的，业余的|relating to activities done at school or college that are not part of the usual course|例句：He participates in many extracurricular activities, such as sports and drama club. 他参加许多课外活动，例如体育运动和戏剧俱乐部。|近义词：non-curricular, outside-of-class|反义词：curricular, academic
