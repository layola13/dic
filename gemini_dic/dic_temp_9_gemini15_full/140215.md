officially:/əˈfɪʃəli/ | adv. | 正式地；官方地 | Officially, the meeting has been postponed.  | 近义词：formally, formally, officially | 反义词：informally, unofficially


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He officially opens the new building today. = 他今天正式启用新大楼。
They officially announce the winner this evening. = 他们今晚正式宣布获胜者。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
The project has officially been completed. = 项目已正式完成。
They have officially confirmed their engagement. = 他们已正式订婚。


过去完成时:
The deal had officially been signed before the news broke. = 这笔交易在消息传出之前就已经正式签署了。

将来时:
The new law will officially come into effect next month. = 新法律将于下个月正式生效。
