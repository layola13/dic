coelacanths:/ˌsiːləˈkænθs/| n. |腔棘鱼 |Coelacanths are lobe-finned fish that were thought to be extinct until 1938. They are considered living fossils because they have changed little over millions of years.  |例句：The discovery of living coelacanths was a major event in zoology.  腔棘鱼的发现是动物学领域的一件大事。

例句：Scientists continue to study coelacanths to learn more about their unique biology and evolutionary history. 科学家们继续研究腔棘鱼，以了解更多关于它们独特的生物学和进化史。|近义词：lobe-finned fish, living fossil |反义词：None.
