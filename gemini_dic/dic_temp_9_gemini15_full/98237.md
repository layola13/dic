insensible: /ɪnˈsensəbl/ | adj. | 无感觉的；麻木的；不省人事的；愚蠢的 |  Unconscious; unaware; unresponsive; without feeling; foolish or unfeeling. |

英文解析:Insensible means lacking feeling or sensitivity, either physically or emotionally.  It can describe someone who is unconscious or unresponsive to stimuli, or someone who is oblivious to the feelings or needs of others.  It can also refer to something that is not easily noticed or perceived.

例句:
He lay insensible after the accident.  = 他事故后不省人事。

She was insensible to his pleas for help. = 她对他的求助无动于衷。


近义词: unconscious, unresponsive, insensitive, numb, unfeeling, oblivious, unaware, stupid, foolish

反义词: conscious, responsive, sensitive, feeling, aware, perceptive, intelligent, sensible
