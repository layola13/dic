manzanita:/ˌmænzəˈniːtə/| n. |曼萨尼塔（一种灌木）|A shrub of the genus Arctostaphylos, native to western North America, characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and small, leathery leaves.  Manzanitas often have beautiful, bell-shaped flowers and attractive berries.|The manzanita bushes were ablaze with pink flowers in the spring. 春天，曼萨尼塔灌木丛开满了粉红色的花朵。|  chaparral,  Arctostaphylos |  |名词
