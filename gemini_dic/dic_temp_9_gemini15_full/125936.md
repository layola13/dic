mushroomed:/ˈmʌʃˌruːmd/| v.|迅速增长；激增|To increase rapidly in number, amount, or extent.|The small business mushroomed into a large corporation.  这家小企业迅速发展成为一家大公司。|The number of participants mushroomed after the announcement.  公告发布后，参与人数激增。|increased rapidly, exploded, boomed, proliferated |decreased, dwindled, shrank|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The city mushroomed in recent years. = 近年来，这座城市迅速发展。
New businesses are mushrooming in the tech industry. = 科技行业涌现出许多新企业。


一般过去时:
The population mushroomed after the discovery of gold. = 在发现黄金后，人口激增。
The company's profits mushroomed during the holiday season. = 公司的利润在节日期间激增。


现在完成时:
The number of online shoppers has mushroomed in the past decade. = 过去十年，网上购物者的数量激增。
The use of smartphones has mushroomed since their invention. = 自从发明以来，智能手机的使用量激增。


过去完成时:
By the time I arrived, the crowd had mushroomed. = 当我到达时，人群已经激增了。
Before the concert started, the audience had mushroomed. = 音乐会开始前，观众已经激增了。


将来时:
Experts predict that the demand for renewable energy will mushroom in the coming years. = 专家预测，未来几年对可再生能源的需求将激增。
It is expected that the use of AI will mushroom in various fields. = 预计人工智能的使用将在各个领域激增。


虚拟语气:
If the project succeeded, the company would have mushroomed. = 如果项目成功，公司就会迅速发展。
If the economy improved, the business would mushroom. = 如果经济好转，企业就会迅速发展。


被动语态:
The town was mushroomed by the influx of tourists. = 城镇因游客涌入而迅速发展。（此用法较少见，通常用主动语态）
The problem was mushroomed by a series of unfortunate events. = 这个问题因一系列不幸事件而迅速恶化。（此用法较少见，通常用主动语态）


疑问句:
Did the number of applications mushroom after the deadline extension? = 截止日期延长后，申请数量是否激增？
Will the company’s market share mushroom in the next quarter? = 公司的市场份额在下个季度会激增吗？


否定句:
The project didn't mushroom as expected. = 项目并没有像预期那样迅速发展。
The sales didn't mushroom despite the advertising campaign. = 尽管进行了广告宣传，销售额并没有激增。
