detassels: /diːˈtæsəlz/ | vt. | 去除玉米须；去除雄穗 | To remove the tassels from (corn).  The process of removing the tassels from corn plants, typically done to prevent pollination and improve the yield of kernels. | The farmer detasseled the corn field by hand.  农民手工去除玉米田的玉米须。|  remove tassels, de-tassel |  (无明显反义词，反义词可理解为“留下玉米须”) | 动词
