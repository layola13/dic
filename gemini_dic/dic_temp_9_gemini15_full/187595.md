romeine:/roʊˈmeɪn/| n. |罗马生菜|Romaine lettuce, also known as cos lettuce, is a variety of lettuce with long, crisp leaves. It is commonly used in salads and other dishes.  |Example: I added some romaine lettuce to my Caesar salad. 我在我的凯撒沙拉里加了一些罗马生菜。|近义词：cos lettuce, crisphead lettuce|反义词：无
