mediator: /ˈmiːdieɪtər/ | n. | 调解人，中间人，媒介 | A person who attempts to make people involved in a conflict come to an agreement; something that acts as a means of connection between two things. |例句：The judge acted as a mediator between the two feuding families.  法官充当了这两个不和家庭之间的调解人。

The treaty served as a mediator between the two warring nations. 这项条约充当了两个交战国家之间的调解人。

近义词：arbitrator, negotiator,intermediary, conciliator | 反义词：opponent, adversary


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The mediator facilitates communication between the parties. 调解人促进各方之间的沟通。
He mediates disputes in the community. 他调解社区纠纷。


一般过去时:
The mediator helped them reach a settlement. 调解人帮助他们达成和解。
She mediated the conflict between the two neighbours. 她调解了两个邻居之间的冲突。


现在完成时:
The mediator has successfully resolved many conflicts. 调解人已经成功地解决了許多冲突。
They have mediated several disputes this year. 今年他们已经调解了几起纠纷。


过去完成时:
Before the mediator arrived, the situation had escalated. 在调解人到达之前，情况已经升级。
The parties had already tried to negotiate before seeking a mediator. 在寻求调解人之前，各方已经尝试过谈判。


将来时:
The mediator will attempt to bring the parties together. 调解人将尝试让各方走到一起。
We will need a mediator to help us resolve this issue. 我们需要一个调解人来帮助我们解决这个问题。


虚拟语气:
If we had a mediator, the conflict might have been resolved sooner. 如果我们有调解人，冲突可能早就解决了。
It would be helpful if a neutral mediator were involved.  如果有一个中立的调解人参与进来，将会有所帮助。


被动语态:
The dispute was mediated by a neutral third party. 纠纷由中立的第三方调解。
A settlement was reached after mediation. 调解后达成了和解。


疑问句:
Will a mediator be needed to resolve this? 解决这个问题需要调解人吗？
Who will act as the mediator in this case? 在这种情况下谁将担任调解人？


否定句:
The parties refused to use a mediator. 各方拒绝使用调解人。
He did not want to involve a mediator in the disagreement. 他不想让调解人参与到这场争执中。

词源:

The word "mediator" comes from the Latin word "mediātor," which means "one who mediates."  The verb "mediāri" means "to be in the middle" or "to intervene."  The root is "medius," meaning "middle."
