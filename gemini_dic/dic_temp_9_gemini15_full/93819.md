immigrated:/ˈɪmɪɡreɪtɪd/| v.| 移民；移居 | To come to live permanently in a foreign country. |例句：My grandparents immigrated to the United States from Ireland in the 1950s.  我的祖父母在20世纪50年代从爱尔兰移民到美国。
例句：Millions of people immigrated to Australia during the gold rush.  淘金热期间，数百万的人移民到澳大利亚。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They immigrate to Canada every year. = 他们每年都移民到加拿大。
Many people immigrate to find better job opportunities. = 许多人移民是为了寻找更好的工作机会。


一般过去时:
My family immigrated to this country ten years ago. = 我的家人十年前移民到这个国家。
He immigrated to the US after finishing his studies. = 他完成学业后移民到美国。


现在完成时:
She has immigrated to Australia and started a new life. = 她已经移民到澳大利亚，并开始了新的生活。
They have immigrated to several countries before settling down. = 他们曾在几个国家移民，然后才定居下来。


过去完成时:
They had immigrated to Brazil before the war broke out. = 战争爆发前，他们已经移民到巴西。
She had immigrated to France before she met her husband. = 在她遇到丈夫之前，她已经移民到法国。


将来时:
We will immigrate to New Zealand next year. = 我们明年将移民到新西兰。
They are planning to immigrate to Spain when they retire. = 他们计划退休后移民到西班牙。


虚拟语气:
If I had the chance, I would immigrate to a warmer country. = 如果我有机会，我会移民到一个气候更温暖的国家。
I wish I had immigrated sooner. = 我希望我早点移民。


被动语态:
Many were immigrated to the country during the war. = 战争期间，许多人被移民到这个国家。(注意：被动语态在此处用法较少见，通常用主动语态)
Thousands of people were forced to immigrate due to the conflict. = 由于冲突，成千上万的人被迫移民。


疑问句:
Have they immigrated yet? = 他们已经移民了吗？
When did your parents immigrate? = 你父母什么时候移民的？


否定句:
We did not immigrate to the US. = 我们没有移民到美国。
They have not yet immigrated to their dream country. = 他们还没有移民到他们梦想的国家。


PHRASE->
legally immigrated = 合法移民
illegal immigration = 非法移民
immigrated to a new country = 移民到一个新的国家
immigrated population = 移民人口
skilled immigrants = 技术移民

近义词：emigrate (指从原籍国移居国外), migrate (指迁徙，范围更广，包括动物迁徙)

反义词：emigrate (从移民的角度看，是反义词，指离开原籍国)
