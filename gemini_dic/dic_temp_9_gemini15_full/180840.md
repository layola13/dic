rehearing:/ˌriːˈhɪərɪŋ/| n.|复审；再审；重听|The act of hearing a case again, typically after an appeal.  It involves a review of the original decision or judgment.  |例句：The court granted a rehearing of the case due to new evidence. 法院因新的证据而准许对该案进行复审。|例句：The musician requested a rehearing of the piece to perfect his performance.  音乐家要求重新演奏这首乐曲以完善他的演奏。|近义词：reconsideration, review, appeal|反义词：final judgment, dismissal

He requests a rehearing of the music. = 他请求重新演奏音乐。
