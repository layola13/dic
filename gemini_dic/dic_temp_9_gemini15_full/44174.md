critics:/ˈkrɪtɪks/ | n. | 批评家；评论员 | People who express judgments of literary or artistic works. Critics often analyze and evaluate the strengths and weaknesses of a work, offering insights into its meaning and impact.  | The critics praised the novel's innovative plot.  批评家们赞扬了这部小说新颖的情节。 | reviewers, commentators, judges | supporters, enthusiasts | 名词
