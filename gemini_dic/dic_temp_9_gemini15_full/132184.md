nondamnations:/ˌnɑːnˌdæmˈneɪʃənz/| n. |非谴责；未被谴责|The absence of condemnation or punishment; a state of not being condemned.  It is often used in a context suggesting a surprising or unexpected lack of negative judgment.|例句：The surprising nondamnations of the perpetrators shocked the public.  令人惊讶的是，肇事者没有受到谴责，这震惊了公众。 The court's decision resulted in a nondamnation of the defendant, raising questions about justice. 法院的判决导致被告未被定罪，这引发了人们对正义的质疑。|近义词：acquittal, absolution, exoneration |反义词：condemnation, damnation, denunciation|名词
