hr:/eɪtʃ ɑːr/|abbr.|小时，人力资源|缩写词，表示“hour”或“human resources”。|例句：The meeting will last 2 hrs.  会议将持续2小时。|例句：The HR department is responsible for hiring. 人力资源部门负责招聘。|近义词：hour (for time), personnel (for human resources)|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The meeting lasts two hours. = 会议持续两小时。
He works eight hours a day. = 他每天工作八小时。

一般过去时:
The meeting lasted two hours. = 会议持续了两小时。
She worked ten hours yesterday. = 她昨天工作了十个小时。

现在完成时:
I have worked here for three hours. = 我在这里工作了三个小时。
They have completed the project in record time. = 他们破纪录地完成了项目。


过去完成时:
I had already worked eight hours when he arrived. = 他到达时我已经工作了八个小时。
They had finished the report before the meeting. = 他们在会议前完成了报告。

将来时:
The meeting will last about an hour. = 会议将持续大约一小时。
I will work from home tomorrow. = 我明天将在家里工作。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would work on that project. = 如果我有更多时间，我会从事那个项目。
I wish I had more time to work on my hobby. = 我希望我有更多时间从事我的爱好。

被动语态:
The report was completed on time. = 报告按时完成了。
The meeting was postponed due to unforeseen circumstances. = 会议由于不可预见的情况而被推迟。

疑问句:
How many hours do you work per week? = 你每周工作多少小时？
What time does the meeting start? = 会议几点开始？

否定句:
The meeting did not last long. = 会议持续时间不长。
He doesn't work weekends. = 他周末不工作。
