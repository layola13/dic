deactivating:/ˌdiˈæktɪveɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 使失效的；使无效的 |  Describes the process or state of making something inactive or ineffective.  | Example: The deactivating process of the bomb was extremely delicate.  使炸弹失效的过程极其微妙。 |近义词：disabling, inactivating, deenergizing | 反义词：activating, enabling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The system is deactivating automatically. = 系统正在自动停用。
She is deactivating her social media accounts. = 她正在停用她的社交媒体账号。


一般过去时:
He deactivated his credit card. = 他停用了他的信用卡。
They deactivated the alarm system. = 他们停用了报警系统。


现在完成时:
I have deactivated my old email address. = 我已经停用了我的旧邮箱地址。
She has deactivated her Facebook profile. = 她已经停用了她的脸书个人资料。


过去完成时:
The engineer had deactivated the power supply before starting the repairs. = 工程师在开始维修之前已经切断了电源。
They had deactivated the emergency broadcast system. = 他们已经停用了紧急广播系统。


将来时:
We will deactivate the software next week. = 我们下周将停用该软件。
The company will deactivate all unused accounts. = 公司将停用所有未使用的帐户。


虚拟语气:
If I deactivated my account, I wouldn't receive any more emails. = 如果我停用我的账户，我就不会再收到任何邮件了。
It would be best if they deactivated the harmful website immediately. = 最好他们立即停用有害的网站。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
