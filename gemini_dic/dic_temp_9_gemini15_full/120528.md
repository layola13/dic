miocene:/ˈmaɪəˌsiːn/| n. |中新世|The Miocene Epoch is a geologic epoch that lasted from about 23 to 5.3 million years ago.  It is the fifth epoch of the Neogene Period in the current geologic timescale.|例句：The Miocene Epoch saw significant changes in climate and the evolution of many new species. 中新世时期见证了气候的重大变化和许多新物种的进化。|近义词：Neogene|反义词：Paleogene
