quilts:/kwɪlts/| n.|被子；棉被|A quilt is a bed covering made of two layers of fabric with a layer of filling between them, usually stitched together.|My grandmother made me a beautiful patchwork quilt.|近义词：comforter, bedspread|反义词：None


USAGE->
