non-condensing: /ˌnɑːn kənˈdensɪŋ/ | adj. |不凝结的；非凝结的| Not undergoing or causing condensation; referring to a process or system where water vapor does not condense into liquid water.  This term is often used in the context of boilers and heating systems. |例句：The non-condensing boiler is less efficient than a condensing one.  非凝结锅炉不如凝结锅炉高效。

The non-condensing furnace vented the exhaust gases directly to the outside.  非凝结炉直接将废气排出到室外。


近义词：non-condensate, uncondensed | 反义词：condensing
