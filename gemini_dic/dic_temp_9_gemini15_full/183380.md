reprogrammer's:/ˌriːˈproʊɡræmər/| n. |重新编程者；程序员（复数）|A reprogrammer is a person who modifies or creates computer programs.  The term often implies a focus on altering existing code rather than writing entirely new programs.  It can also refer to someone who rewrites or updates software for different platforms or systems. | Example: The company hired a team of reprogrammers to update their legacy software.  这家公司聘请了一组重新编程人员来更新他们的旧软件。  The reprogrammers worked tirelessly to fix the bugs in the system.  重新编程人员夜以继日地努力修复系统中的错误。|近义词：programmer, coder, software developer |反义词：N/A
