consummately:/ˈkɒnsəmeɪtli/| adj. |圆满地；完美地；极其地|Completely and perfectly; to the highest degree.  It implies a high level of skill, accomplishment, or expertise. |例句：He consummately executed the piano concerto, captivating the audience. 他完美地演奏了钢琴协奏曲，迷住了观众。  She consummately managed the crisis, preventing any major damage. 她圆满地处理了危机，避免了任何重大损失。|近义词：perfectly, flawlessly, expertly, masterfully|反义词：imperfectly, poorly, ineptly
