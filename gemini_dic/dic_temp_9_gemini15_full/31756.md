cheating:/ˈtʃiːtɪŋ/| n. v.|欺骗；作弊；舞弊|The act of deceiving or breaking rules to gain an advantage.  It can refer to academic dishonesty, infidelity in a relationship, or any situation where someone gains an unfair advantage through dishonesty.|例句：He was caught cheating on the exam. 他考试作弊被抓住了。

例句：She admitted to cheating on her husband. 她承认背叛了她的丈夫。


近义词：deceiving, defrauding, swindling, trickery, dishonesty|反义词：honesty, fairness, integrity|动词
