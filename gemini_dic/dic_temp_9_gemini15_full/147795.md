overprotective: /ˌoʊvərprəˈtɛktɪv/ | adj. | 过度保护的，溺爱的 | Excessively protective; showing excessive concern for the safety and well-being of someone, often to the point of being restrictive or hindering their independence.  |  例句：She's an overprotective mother who won't let her son go out at night. = 她是一位过度保护的母亲，不让儿子晚上出门。

例句：His overprotective father stifled his ambition. = 他过度保护的父亲扼杀了他的雄心壮志。


近义词：sheltering, possessive, smothering, solicitous | 反义词：neglectful, indifferent, unconcerned


词性：形容词
