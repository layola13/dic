enclave:/ˈɛŋkleɪv/| n. |飞地；被包围的领土；（文化、社会等的）聚集地 | A territory completely surrounded by the territory of another state or nation.  It can also refer to a distinct group or community within a larger society. |例句：The small town is an enclave of peace in a turbulent region.  这个小镇是动荡地区中的一片和平绿洲。

例句：The Italian enclave in New York City is known for its vibrant culture.  纽约市的意大利人聚居区以其充满活力的文化而闻名。


近义词：pocket, territory, community, colony


反义词：mainland, metropolis, surrounding area


词性：名词
