over-talkativeness: /ˌoʊvərˈtɔːkətɪvnəs/ | n. |喋喋不休；话多；健谈 | Excessive talkativeness; the tendency to talk excessively or more than is necessary or appropriate.  It often implies a lack of awareness of others' feelings or needs, or an inability to control one's speech. |


例句：

Her over-talkativeness made it difficult to have a meaningful conversation.  她的喋喋不休使得很难进行有意义的谈话。

His over-talkativeness sometimes overshadows his good ideas. 他的话多有时会掩盖他的好主意。


近义词：

garrulousness, loquacity, volubility, fluency (in a negative context), garrulity


反义词：

laconicism, taciturnity, reticence, silence


词性：

名词
