sampan:/ˈsæmpæn/| n.|舢板，小船|A small, flat-bottomed boat, usually propelled by oars or a sail, traditionally used in Asia, especially in rivers and canals.|例句：The fisherman steered his sampan through the narrow river.  渔夫驾驶着他的舢板穿过狭窄的河流。|例句：Many sampans were moored along the riverbank. 许多舢板停泊在河岸边。|近义词：boat, dinghy, skiff|反义词：large ship, ocean liner
