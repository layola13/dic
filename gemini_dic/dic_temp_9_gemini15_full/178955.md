redeclining:/ˌriːdɪˈklaɪnɪŋ/| adj.|重新下降；再次衰退|  Describes something that is declining again after a period of improvement or stability. It implies a renewed downward trend.  |例句：The economy, after a brief period of growth, is redeclining.  经济在短暂增长后再次衰退。 |近义词：receding, deteriorating, declining further |反义词：improving, recovering, rising


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The stock market is redeclining. = 股票市场正在再次下跌。
The patient's condition is redeclining. = 病人的病情正在再次恶化。

一般过去时:
The temperature redeclined after a brief warming. = 气温在短暂回暖后再次下降。
Sales redeclined sharply in the last quarter. = 上个季度的销售额急剧下降。

现在完成时:
The company's profits have redeclined since the merger. = 自从合并以来，公司的利润已经再次下降。
The population of the endangered species has redeclined significantly. = 濒危物种的数量已经大幅减少。

过去完成时:
The river's water level had redeclined before the heavy rain. = 大雨之前，河流水位已经再次下降。
The unemployment rate had redeclined before the new economic stimulus package. = 新的经济刺激计划出台之前，失业率已经再次下降。


将来时:
Experts predict that the housing market will redecline next year. = 专家预测房地产市场明年将再次下跌。
We fear that his health will redecline further. = 我们担心他的健康状况会进一步恶化。


虚拟语气:
If the government hadn't intervened, the economy would have redeclined further. = 如果政府不干预，经济将会进一步恶化。
If they had taken preventative measures, the situation would not have redeclined. = 如果他们采取预防措施，情况就不会恶化。

被动语态:
The proposal was redeclined by the committee. = 这个提议被委员会再次否决了。(Note:  "redecline" as a verb is uncommon and usually implies a decline in something measurable, not a rejection of a proposal. A better phrasing would be "The proposal was rejected again.")
It is feared that the value of the currency will be redeclined. = 人们担心货币价值会再次下跌。


疑问句:
Is the level of support redeclining? = 支持度是否正在下降？
Will the sales redecline next month? = 下个月销售额会下降吗？

否定句:
The value of the property is not redeclining. = 房产价值没有下降。
The number of students enrolled hasn't redeclined. = 注册的学生人数没有减少。



PHRASE->
There is no common fixed phrase using "redeclining".  The word itself functions as an adjective modifying nouns representing things that are declining again.  Examples of phrases would be built around the nouns being modified, such as:  "redeclining economic indicators", "redeclining health", "redeclining market share".
