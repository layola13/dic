overperformed:/ˌoʊvərpərˈfɔːrmd/ | v. | 超额完成；表现优异 | To perform better than expected or required.  |The company overperformed its sales targets for the quarter.  这家公司本季度超额完成了销售目标。| outperform, exceed, surpass | underperform, fail  | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The team overperforms consistently. = 这个团队始终表现优异。
He overperforms in his role. = 他在自己的角色中表现超群。

一般过去时:
The stock overperformed last year. = 该股票去年表现优异。
Our project overperformed its budget. = 我们的项目超支了预算。


现在完成时:
The fund has overperformed its benchmark. = 这只基金的表现超过了基准。
She has consistently overperformed in her studies. = 她在学习中一直表现优异。


过去完成时:
The company had overperformed expectations for several quarters. = 这家公司几个季度都超出了预期。
He had overperformed his duties, so he received a bonus. = 他超额完成了他的职责，所以获得了奖金。


将来时:
Analysts predict the market will overperform next year. = 分析师预测市场明年将表现优异。
We expect the new product to overperform its competitors. = 我们预计新产品将胜过竞争对手。


虚拟语气:
If the team had overperformed, they would have received a bonus. = 如果团队表现出色，他们就会得到奖金。
I wish the project had overperformed its budget. = 我希望这个项目能超额完成预算。


被动语态:
The expectations were overperformed by the team. = 团队超额完成了预期。
The sales targets were overperformed by a significant margin. = 销售目标被大幅超额完成。


疑问句:
Did the company overperform its projections? = 公司是否超额完成了预期？
Has the investment overperformed the market? = 这项投资的表现是否超过了市场？


否定句:
The product didn't overperform in the market. = 该产品在市场上的表现并不出色。
The team did not overperform its targets this quarter. = 本季度团队没有超额完成目标。


PHRASE->
