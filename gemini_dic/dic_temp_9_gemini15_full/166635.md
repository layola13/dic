presenter:/prɪˈzɛntər/| n. |主持人，演讲者，介绍者 |A person who introduces a television or radio program, or who presents information to an audience.  |The presenter introduced the next guest on the show.  这位主持人介绍了节目的下一位嘉宾。| host, speaker, announcer | audience member | 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The presenter explains the concept clearly. = 主持人清晰地解释了这个概念。
He presents the data in a compelling way. = 他以引人入胜的方式呈现数据。


一般过去时:
The presenter spoke eloquently. = 主持人雄辩地发言。

现在完成时:
The presenter has already introduced three speakers. = 主持人已经介绍了三位演讲者。
He has presented at numerous international conferences. = 他在许多国际会议上做过演讲。


过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If the presenter were more engaging, the audience would be more attentive. = 如果主持人更吸引人，观众就会更专心。
