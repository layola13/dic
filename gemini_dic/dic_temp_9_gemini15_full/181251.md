reinstalls:/ˌriːɪnˈstɔːlz/ | vt. |重新安装，恢复安装 | To install something again.  This usually implies that something was previously installed and then removed or uninstalled, before being installed again. |例句：The technician reinstalls the operating system after the virus attack. 技术人员在病毒攻击后重新安装了操作系统。

例句：We had to reinstalls the software after the power outage. 断电后我们不得不重新安装软件。


近义词：reinstall, restore


反义词：uninstall, remove


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reinstalls the software every month. 他每月重新安装软件。
The company reinstalls the security system regularly. 公司定期重新安装安全系统。


一般过去时:
I reinstalled the game after deleting it accidentally.我不小心删除了游戏后又重新安装了。
She reinstalled the application because it stopped working. 因为应用程序停止工作，她重新安装了它。


现在完成时:
They have reinstalled the printer several times this week.  他们本周已经多次重新安装了打印机。
We have reinstalled the operating system to fix the bug. 我们已经重新安装了操作系统以修复错误。


过去完成时:
The IT support had reinstalled the server before the meeting. IT支持在会议前已经重新安装了服务器。
She had reinstalled the driver before reporting the problem. 她报告问题之前已经重新安装了驱动程序。


将来时:
We will reinstall the program tomorrow. 我们明天将重新安装程序。
The team will reinstall the application after the update. 团队将在更新后重新安装应用程序。


虚拟语气:
If the computer crashed again, I would reinstall the whole system. 如果电脑再次崩溃，我会重新安装整个系统。
I wish I had reinstalled the software before the deadline. 我希望我在截止日期之前已经重新安装了软件。


被动语态:
