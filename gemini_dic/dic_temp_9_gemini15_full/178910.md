reddening:/ˈrɛdnɪŋ/| n. adj.|发红，变红；发炎|The process of becoming red or the state of being red.  It can refer to the physical act of something turning red, or a metaphorical reddening, such as anger causing a face to redden.|例句：The sunset caused a beautiful reddening of the sky.  日落使天空呈现美丽的红色。

The inflammation caused a reddening of his skin. 炎症导致了他的皮肤发红。|近义词：blushing, flushing, inflammation|反义词：paling, bleaching
