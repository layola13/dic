giggling: /ˈɡɪɡlɪŋ/ | v. |咯咯地笑，傻笑 |  The act of laughing in a light, repeated manner, often expressing amusement or nervousness.  |例句：The children were giggling uncontrollably during the story time. 孩子们在讲故事的时候忍不住咯咯地笑。

例句：She giggled nervously when he looked at her. 她被他看着的时候紧张地咯咯地笑。


近义词：chuckle, titter, snicker | 反义词：sobbing, weeping
