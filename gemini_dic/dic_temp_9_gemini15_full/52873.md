diminishings: /dɪˈmɪnɪʃɪŋz/ | n. | 减少；递减；衰减 |  The gradual diminishing of his influence.  |  reductions, decreases, declines | increases, augmentations | 名词


英文解析:The plural form of "diminishing," referring to instances or processes of something becoming less in size, amount, intensity, or importance.  It often describes a gradual or continuous reduction.


例句:

The diminishing returns on investment are a concern.  = 投资回报率的下降令人担忧。

The diminishing supply of fresh water is a serious problem. = 淡水供应的减少是一个严重的问题。


近义词: reductions, decreases, declines, lessenings, dwindling


反义词: increases, augmentations, growths, expansions


词性：名词 (plural noun)
