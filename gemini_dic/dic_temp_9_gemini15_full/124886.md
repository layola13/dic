muddish:/ˈmʌdɪʃ/| adj.|泥泞的，污秽的|Resembling mud; muddy; dirty.  The word suggests a less intense degree of muddiness than "muddy".|例句：The path was rather muddish after the rain. 雨后小路相当泥泞。

例句：His boots were muddish from walking through the fields. 他穿过田野后，靴子沾满了泥巴。


近义词：muddy, miry, slushy, soiled|反义词：clean, spotless, pristine
