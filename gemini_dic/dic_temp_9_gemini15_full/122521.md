mockups:/ˈmɑːkʌps/ | n. |样机，模型，模拟 | A mockup is a scale or full-size model of a design or device, often used for testing or demonstration purposes. It's a preliminary version of something, allowing for feedback and adjustments before the final product is created.  | Example: The design team presented a mockup of the new website to the client for feedback.  设计团队向客户展示了新网站的样机以征求反馈意见。|近义词：prototype, model, sample, draft |反义词：finished product, final version |名词
