malted:/ˈmɔːltɪd/ | adj. |麦芽的；用麦芽制成的 |Containing malt or made with malt.  Malt is grain, especially barley, that has been germinated and allowed to sprout, then dried, giving it a sweet, nutty flavor and making it useful in brewing and baking. |例句：The beer has a rich, malted barley flavor. 这啤酒有一种浓郁的麦芽大麦香味。

例句：This recipe calls for malted milk powder. 这个食谱需要麦芽奶粉。


近义词：barleyed (less common), germinated (referring to the process)


反义词：unmalted


词性：形容词
