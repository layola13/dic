degelled:/dɪˈdʒɛld/| adj.| 去胶的，脱胶的 |  Describes a substance, typically a food product like jam or jelly, that has had its gelling agent removed, resulting in a less firm or solid consistency.  | Example: The degelled jam was smoother and easier to spread. 去胶的果酱更顺滑，更容易涂抹。 |近义词：ungelled, liquefied |反义词：gelled, solidified | 形容词
