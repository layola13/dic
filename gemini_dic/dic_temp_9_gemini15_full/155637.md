phishings:/ˈfɪʃɪŋz/| n.|网络钓鱼行为（复数）|The act of attempting to acquire sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details (and sometimes indirectly, money) often for malicious reasons, by disguising as a trustworthy entity in an electronic communication.  Phishings are a common type of cybercrime.|例句：Recent phishings have targeted employees with fake login pages.|近义词：cyberattacks, online scams, internet fraud|反义词：N/A
