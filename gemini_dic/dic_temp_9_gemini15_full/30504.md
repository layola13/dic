centuriate:/sɛnˈtʃʊəriˌeɪt/| n. v.|百年；百年纪念；组成一百人的单位|A centuriate is a unit of 100, particularly used in the context of Roman military organization.  It refers to a group of 100 men, a century, or a period of 100 years.  As a verb, it means to divide into centuries or to last for a century.|例句：The Roman army was organized into centuriates. 罗马军队被编成百人队。 The centuriate celebrated its 100th anniversary. 这个百人队庆祝了它的100周年纪念日。|近义词：century, hundred, group|反义词：decade, millennium|名词，动词
