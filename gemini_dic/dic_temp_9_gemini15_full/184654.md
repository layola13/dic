restring:/riːˈstrɪŋ/ | vt. | 重新穿弦 (尤指乐器) | To replace the strings of (a musical instrument or similar object).  | 例句：I need to restring my guitar; the strings are worn out. 我需要重新为我的吉他换弦；琴弦磨损了。

The tennis player had to restring his racquet after the match.  网球运动员比赛后不得不重新穿线他的球拍。| 近义词：replace strings, re-string | 反义词：leave strings as they are


USAGE->

She restrings her violin regularly. 她定期为她的小提琴换弦。
