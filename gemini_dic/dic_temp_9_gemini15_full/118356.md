meritable:/ˈmɛrɪtəbl/ | adj. |值得称赞的；有功的；值得嘉奖的 |Deserving of merit; praiseworthy.  Something meritable is worthy of reward or praise because of its excellence or value. |例句：Her tireless work on the project was truly meritable. 她为项目付出的辛勤工作是真正值得称赞的。

例句：His contributions to the company were meritable and led to his promotion. 他对公司的贡献是值得称赞的，并因此得到了晋升。


近义词：laudable, commendable, praiseworthy, deserving

反义词：blameworthy, reprehensible, unworthy


词性：形容词
