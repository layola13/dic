observator: /ˌɒbzəˈveɪtə(r)/, /ˌɑːbzərˈveɪtər/ | n. |观察者，观测者 | A person or thing that observes.  Someone who watches or monitors something carefully. | 例句：The observator meticulously recorded the bird's migration patterns.  观察者一丝不苟地记录了鸟类的迁徙模式。

The observator noted a significant change in the patient's vital signs. 观察者注意到病人的生命体征发生了显著变化。


近义词：observer, watcher, monitor, viewer | 反义词：participant, actor


词源:  From the verb "observe" + "-or" (agent suffix).  "Observe" comes from the Latin "observare," meaning "to keep watching," from "ob-" (towards) + "servare" (to keep, preserve).
