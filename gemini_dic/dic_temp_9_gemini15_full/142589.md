osteogenetic:/ˌɒstiəʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/| adj. |成骨的；生骨的| Relating to bone formation or bone generation.  The term refers to the process of bone formation by osteoblasts.  |例句：Osteogenetic cells are crucial for bone healing. 成骨细胞对骨骼愈合至关重要。

例句：The researchers are investigating new osteogenetic therapies for fractures. 研究人员正在研究用于骨折治疗的新型成骨疗法。


近义词：bone-forming, ossifying


反义词：osteolytic (bone-resorbing)


词性:形容词
