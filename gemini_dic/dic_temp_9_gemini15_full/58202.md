drudger:/ˈdrʌdʒər/| n. |苦工，辛劳工作的人|A drudger is a person who does hard, monotonous, and often unpleasant work.  They are typically involved in laborious tasks requiring little skill or creativity. |Example:  He was a drudger in the factory, spending his days performing repetitive tasks. 他是工厂里的一名苦工，每天都在做重复性的工作。|近义词：toiler, laborer, worker, slave|反义词：leisure, idler
