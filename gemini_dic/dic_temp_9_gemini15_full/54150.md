disenrol: /ˌdɪsɪnˈroʊl/ | vt. | 退学；注销；除名 | To remove someone's name from a register or list, especially a list of students or members.  | 例句：He decided to disenrol from the university after failing several courses. 他因为几门课不及格决定退学。

She was disenrolled from the program for violating the rules.  她因为违反规定被取消了该项目的资格。


近义词：unregister, withdraw, deregister | 反义词：enrol, register, enroll
