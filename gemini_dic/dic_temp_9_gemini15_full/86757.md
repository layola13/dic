helping-out: /ˈhɛlpiŋ aʊt/ | v. | 帮忙，协助 | To give assistance or support to someone.  This is a gerund, functioning as a noun.  | 例句：I'm helping out at the soup kitchen tonight. = 我今晚在施粥所帮忙。

例句：  She's always happy to help out her neighbours. = 她总是乐于帮助她的邻居们。


近义词：assisting, aiding, supporting, lending a hand


反义词：hindering, obstructing, impeding


词性: Verb (gerund)
