pseudobiologically:/ˌsuːdoʊˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. |伪生物学地，似生物学地 |  Describing something that mimics or resembles biological processes but is not genuinely biological in nature. It often implies a superficial or artificial resemblance. |例句：The software simulates a biological ecosystem pseudobiologically, relying on algorithms rather than actual organisms.  软件以伪生物学的方式模拟生物生态系统，依赖的是算法而不是实际生物。


例句：The artist created a pseudobiologically inspired sculpture, using organic forms but employing non-biological materials.艺术家创作了一个受伪生物学启发的雕塑，使用了有机形态，但采用了非生物材料。


近义词：artificially, synthetically, quasi-biologically


反义词：naturally, organically, biologically


词性:副词
