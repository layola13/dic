sapphires:/ˈsæfaɪrz/ | n. |蓝宝石 |A sapphire is a precious stone, a variety of the mineral corundum, that is typically blue in color, but can also be found in other colors like pink, yellow, and green. Its intense color and hardness make it a highly valued gemstone.  |例句：Her engagement ring featured a stunning sapphire surrounded by diamonds.  她的订婚戒指镶嵌着一颗令人惊叹的蓝宝石，周围环绕着钻石。
