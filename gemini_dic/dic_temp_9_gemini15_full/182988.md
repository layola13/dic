repledge:/riːˈplɛdʒ/ | vt. | 再保证；重新承诺；再次抵押 | To pledge again; to reaffirm a pledge; to use something already pledged as collateral for a new loan.  | Example: The company had to repledge its assets to secure a new loan. 公司不得不再次抵押其资产以获得新的贷款。 |近义词：reassure, reaffirm, rededicate | 反义词：renege, retract, default | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He repledges his support for the project every week. 他每周都重申对该项目的支持。
The bank repledges assets to maintain liquidity. 银行再次抵押资产以维持流动性。


一般过去时:
They repledged their loyalty to the king. 他们再次向国王宣誓效忠。
She repledged the jewelry to cover her debts. 她再次抵押珠宝来偿还债务。
