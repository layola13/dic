re-blossomed:/ˌriːˈblɑːsəmd/ | v. |重新开花；复苏；再次兴盛 | To blossom again; to flourish or thrive anew after a period of decline or dormancy.  |例句：After a difficult winter, the garden re-blossomed in spring.  春回大地，经历了艰难的冬天后，花园又重新开花了。

例句：Her career re-blossomed after she took a sabbatical.  休假之后，她的事业再次兴盛。


近义词：reflourished, revived, renewed, resurfaced


反义词：withered, faded, declined


词性：动词
