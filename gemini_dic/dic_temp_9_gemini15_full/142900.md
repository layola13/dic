out-patients:/ˌaʊtˈpeɪʃənts/| n. |门诊病人；门诊患者 |Patients who receive medical treatment without being admitted to a hospital.  Outpatients are treated on an ambulatory basis, meaning they do not stay overnight. |The hospital sees hundreds of outpatients every day.  这家医院每天接诊数百名门诊病人。|  in-patients | in-patients |
