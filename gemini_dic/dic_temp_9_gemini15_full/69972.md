festoon:/fɛˈstuːn/| n. vt.|彩饰,饰带,花彩|A festoon is a chain or garland of flowers, leaves, or ribbons, hung in a decorative way.|The party was festooned with colorful balloons and streamers.  派对上装饰着五颜六色的气球和彩带。|decorate, adorn, embellish|plain, undecorated|名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The city is festooned with lights for the festival. = 城市为节日装饰着彩灯。
She festoons her garden with climbing roses. = 她用攀援玫瑰装饰她的花园。


一般过去时:
They festooned the hall with banners. = 他们用横幅装饰了大厅。
We festooned the wedding arch with flowers. = 我们用鲜花装饰了婚礼拱门。

The streets have been festooned for the parade. = 街道已经为游行装饰好了。

过去完成时:
