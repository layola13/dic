envoy:/ˈɛnˌvɔɪ/| n. |使节，特使；代表|A person sent as a representative on a special mission, especially a diplomatic one.  An envoy is typically sent to negotiate or represent their country's interests in another country. |例句：The envoy was sent to negotiate a peace treaty.  这位特使被派去谈判和平条约。|例句：The special envoy worked tirelessly to resolve the conflict.  这位特使为解决冲突而不知疲倦地工作。|近义词：ambassador, delegate, emissary, representative|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who acts against their country's interests. |名词
