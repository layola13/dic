scrophulariaceous:/ˌskrɒfjʊˈleəriəs/| adj. | 腺毛科的；玄参科的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Scrophulariaceae, a large family of flowering plants.  The family is characterized by typically irregular flowers, often with a two-lipped corolla. Many scrophulariaceous plants are herbs, but some are shrubs or small trees. | Example:  Many scrophulariaceous plants are known for their medicinal properties.  许多玄参科植物以其药用特性而闻名。|近义词：figwort (referring to the family as a whole)|反义词：无
