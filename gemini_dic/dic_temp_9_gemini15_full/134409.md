noninvestors: /ˌnɑːnɪnˈvɛstərz/ | n. | 非投资者 | People or entities that do not invest in a particular company, project, or market.  Those who do not allocate capital for the purpose of generating a financial return. | Example: The company's initial public offering (IPO) was undersubscribed, likely due to the low participation of noninvestors. 这家公司的首次公开募股(IPO)认购不足，这可能是由于非投资者的参与度低造成的。 | 近义词: nonparticipants, bystanders | 反义词: investors
