instigate:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪt/| vt.|煽动；激起；唆使|To provoke or stir up (a feeling, action, or event); to incite.|例句：He was accused of instigating violence. 他被指控煽动暴力。

例句：The rebels instigated a revolution. 叛乱者发起了革命。


近义词：provoke, incite, foment, stir up|反义词：appease, pacify, calm, quell|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He instigates trouble wherever he goes. 他走到哪里都煽动事端。
She instigates change within the company. 她公司内部推动变革。


一般过去时:
They instigated a riot in the city center. 他们在市中心煽动了一场暴乱。
The government instigated reforms to the education system. 政府对教育体系进行了改革。


现在完成时:
He has instigated numerous conflicts throughout his career. 他职业生涯中煽动过无数冲突。
The organization has instigated a campaign to raise awareness. 该组织发起了一场提高认识的运动。


过去完成时:
The police discovered that he had instigated the crime. 警方发现是他唆使了这起犯罪。
It turned out that she had instigated the whole argument. 原来是她挑起了整个争论。


将来时:
They will instigate legal proceedings against the company. 他们将对该公司提起诉讼。
She will instigate a thorough investigation into the matter. 她将对此事进行彻底调查。


虚拟语气:
If he had instigated the fight, he would have been arrested. 如果他煽动了这场打斗，他就会被逮捕。
I wish they hadn't instigated such a fierce debate. 我希望他们没有挑起如此激烈的辩论。


被动语态:
The riot was instigated by a small group of extremists. 暴乱是由一小撮极端分子煽动的。
The changes were instigated by the new management team. 这些变化是由新的管理团队促成的。


疑问句:
Who instigated the conflict between the two countries? 谁挑起了两国之间的冲突？
Did he really instigate the rumours? 他真的散布谣言了吗？


否定句:
He did not instigate the violence; he tried to stop it. 他没有煽动暴力；他试图阻止它。
The company denies instigating any illegal activities. 公司否认煽动任何非法活动。


PHRASE->
instigate a riot 煽动暴乱
instigate a conflict 挑起冲突
instigate a lawsuit 提起诉讼
instigate change 推动变革
instigate an investigation 发起调查


词源:
The word "instigate" comes from the Late Latin word "instigare," meaning "to goad, prick, or urge on."  This, in turn, is derived from the Latin "stingere," meaning "to prick." The sense of provoking or urging someone to action is central to the word's development.
