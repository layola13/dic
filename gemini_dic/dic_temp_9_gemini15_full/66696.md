exomoons:/ˈɛksoʊmuːnz/| n. |系外卫星|An exomoon is a moon that orbits a planet outside of our solar system (an exoplanet).  The existence of exomoons is currently theoretical; none have been definitively detected.  Scientists are actively searching for them using various methods. |Scientists are hopeful that future telescopes will be powerful enough to directly image exomoons.|近义词：extrasolar moon (less common)|反义词：moon (within our solar system)
