reinfluence:/ˌriːˈɪnflʊəns/| vt.|重新影响；再次影响|To influence again or anew; to exert influence once more.|The new evidence reinfluenced the jury's decision. 新证据再次影响了陪审团的决定。|influence, affect, impact|uninfluence, unaffected


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The new policy reinfluences the market. = 新政策正在重新影响市场。
His words reinfluence my decision. = 他的话语正在重新影响我的决定。

一般过去时:
The speech reinfluenced public opinion. = 这篇演讲重新影响了公众舆论。
The scandal reinfluenced the politician's career. = 丑闻再次影响了这位政治家的职业生涯。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:
If the evidence were stronger, it would reinfluence the judge's ruling. =如果证据更充分，它将重新影响法官的裁决。
I wish the teacher's feedback had reinfluenced my writing style. =我希望老师的反馈能重新影响我的写作风格。

被动语态:

否定句:
