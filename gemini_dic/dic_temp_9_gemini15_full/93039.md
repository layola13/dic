idles:/ˈaɪdəlz/ | v. | 懒散；无所事事；空转；消磨时间 | to spend time doing nothing; to be inactive or unproductive | 例句：He idled away the afternoon watching TV. 他下午无所事事地看电视。  He idled his car engine. 他让汽车发动机空转。| 近义词：loaf, dawdle, procrastinate | 反义词：work, hustle, strive | 动词
