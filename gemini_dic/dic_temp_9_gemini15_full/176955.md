realliance:/ˌriːˈaɪəns/| n.| 重组联盟；重新结盟 | A realliance is the formation of a new alliance, often after a previous one has dissolved or failed.  It implies a renewed commitment or a shift in alliances.  | Example: The two countries, after years of conflict, forged a realliance based on mutual economic interests.  两个国家在多年的冲突之后，基于共同的经济利益建立了新的联盟。 |近义词：realignment, renewed alliance|反义词：dissolution, breakup|名词
