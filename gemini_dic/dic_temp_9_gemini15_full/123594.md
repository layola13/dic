/mɒnˈteɪnəs/ | adj. | 山地的；多山的 | Of or relating to mountains; mountainous.  The word describes a region characterized by its numerous mountains.  It implies a rugged, often high-altitude landscape. | 例句：The montanos region is known for its breathtaking scenery.  山区以其令人叹为观止的景色而闻名。


The montanos climate is harsh and unforgiving.  山区气候恶劣而残酷。


近义词：mountainous, hilly, upland | 反义词：flat, plain, lowland

词源:  Derived from the Spanish word "montañoso," ultimately from the Latin "montanus," meaning "of or relating to mountains."  The suffix "-oso" in Spanish and "-ous" in English adds an adjectival quality, signifying abundance or characteristic of.

USAGE->
This word is primarily an adjective and doesn't have verb or noun forms in common usage.  Therefore, tense conjugation examples are not applicable.  Its usage is restricted to describing the characteristic of a place.
