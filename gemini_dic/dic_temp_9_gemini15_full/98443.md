insperse:/ɪnˈspɜːrs/ | vt. | 散布；点缀；插入 | To scatter or distribute among other things; to insert at intervals. | 例句：Wildflowers were interspersed among the grass. 野花散布在草丛中。  The author interspersed anecdotes throughout the book. 作者在书中穿插了轶事。| 近义词：scatter, sprinkle, intermingle, interweave | 反义词：concentrate, cluster
