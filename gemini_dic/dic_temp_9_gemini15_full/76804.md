gammoner:/ˈɡæmənər/ | n. | 腌制火腿肉贩；（尤指）欺诈者，骗子 | A gammoner is someone who sells gammon (smoked or cured ham). The term has also evolved to refer to a swindler or deceiver, possibly due to the association of gammon with something potentially deceptive or artificial.  | Example: He was known as a gammoner in the marketplace, always trying to cheat his customers. 他在市场上被称为骗子，总是试图欺骗他的顾客。 | 近义词：swindler, cheat, fraudster, sharper | 反义词：honest person, truthful person | 名词
