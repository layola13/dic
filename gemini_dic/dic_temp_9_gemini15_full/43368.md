craniofacial:/ˌkreɪniəʊˈfeɪʃəl/| adj. |颅面部的；颅面相关的| Relating to the cranium (skull) and the face.  The term refers to the structures and functions of the skull and face, encompassing bones, muscles, nerves, and blood vessels. | Example: The child was born with a craniofacial anomaly.  这个孩子出生时患有颅面畸形。  The surgeon specializes in craniofacial surgery.  这位外科医生专门从事颅面手术。|近义词：facial, skull-related |反义词：N/A

词源:
The word "craniofacial" is a compound word formed from:

* **cranio-**:  From Greek *kranion* meaning "skull".
* **facial**: From Latin *facies* meaning "face".

Therefore, "craniofacial" literally means "relating to the skull and face".
