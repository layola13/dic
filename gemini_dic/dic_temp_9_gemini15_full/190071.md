sandyish: /ˈsændiʃ/ | adj. |略带沙质的，沙一样的 | Slightly resembling sand in color or texture.  The term implies a less intense or complete resemblance to sand than "sandy." |例句：The beach had a sandyish hue. 海滩呈现出略带沙色的色调。

The desert landscape was sandyish in places.  沙漠景观在某些地方略带沙质。


近义词：slightly sandy, sandy-colored, gritty


反义词：smooth, sleek, polished


词性：形容词
