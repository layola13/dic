payment: /ˈpeɪmənt/ | n. | 支付，付款 | The act of paying someone or something; the money paid.  |例句：The payment is due on the 15th of the month.  这个月的15号到期付款。

例句：He made the payment online. 他在线支付。


近义词：pay, settlement, disbursement | 反义词：receipt


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company makes its payments on time. = 公司按时付款。
She receives payment for her services. = 她因其服务而获得报酬。


一般过去时:
He made a payment last week. = 他上周付款了。
They received payment for the completed project. = 他们收到了已完成项目的款项。


现在完成时:
We have made the payment. = 我们已经付款了。
She has not yet received payment for her work. = 她尚未收到她的工作报酬。


过去完成时:
They had made the payment before the deadline. = 他们已在截止日期前付款。
He had received payment before leaving the country. = 他在离开国家之前已经收到了付款。


将来时:
I will make the payment tomorrow. = 我明天付款。
They will receive payment next week. = 他们下周将收到付款。


虚拟语气:
If I had the money, I would make the payment immediately. = 如果我有钱，我会立即付款。
I wish I had made the payment earlier. = 我真希望我早点付款。


被动语态:
The payment was made promptly. = 付款及时完成。
The invoice is being paid. = 发票正在支付。


疑问句:
When will the payment be made? = 什么时候付款？
Has the payment been received? = 付款是否已收到？


否定句:
He didn't make the payment. = 他没有付款。
The payment was not received on time. = 付款没有按时收到。
