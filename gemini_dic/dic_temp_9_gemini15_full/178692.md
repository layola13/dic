recrosses:/ˌriːˈkrɒsɪz/| v.|再次横过；重新越过|To cross again; to cross over or through something a second time.  |例句：The river recrosses the valley further downstream. 这条河在河谷下游再次横越。

例句：After reaching the other side, he recrossed the bridge to retrieve his dropped wallet. 到达对面后，他又返回桥上取回掉落的钱包。


近义词：repass, cross again, recut


反义词：No direct opposite, but actions like remaining on one side or going in the opposite direction could be considered contrasting.


词性:动词
