pseudosolarization:/ˌsuːdoʊˌsoʊləraɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |假太阳化作用，伪太阳化|The process of increasing the apparent sharpness and contrast of a photographic image by using techniques that mimic the effects of solarization.| 例句：Pseudosolarization techniques are often used in digital image processing to enhance contrast and detail.  伪太阳化技术常用于数字图像处理以增强对比度和细节。

例句：The photographer achieved a dramatic effect by employing pseudosolarization in post-processing. 摄影师通过后期处理中的伪太阳化技术取得了戏剧性的效果。


近义词：Solarization (but with a crucial distinction: true solarization is a photographic effect caused by overexposure during development, while pseudosolarization is a simulated effect achieved through digital manipulation or other techniques.)  太阳化（但两者有关键区别：真正的太阳化是显影过程中过度曝光造成的摄影效果，而伪太阳化是通过数字处理或其他技术模拟产生的效果。）


反义词：None directly, as it describes a specific image processing technique. 没有直接的反义词，因为它描述的是一种特定的图像处理技术。


词性：名词


词源: pseudo- (from Greek ψευδής, meaning "false" or "sham") + solarization (from solarize, referring to the photographic effect).  伪-（源于希腊语 ψευδής，意为“虚假的”或“冒牌的”）+ 太阳化（源于solarize，指摄影效果）。
