conch:/kɒŋk/ /kɑːŋk/| n.|海螺；海螺壳|A conch is a large sea snail with a spiral shell.  The shell is often used as a decorative object or a musical instrument.|例句：He blew a conch shell to signal the start of the ceremony. 他吹响海螺壳，标志着仪式的开始。

例句：The beach was littered with beautiful conch shells. 海滩上散落着许多美丽的贝壳。
