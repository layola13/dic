/ˌoʊvərˈtʃærəti/ | n. |过度慈善；施舍过多 | Excessive charity; giving too much in charity.  It often implies a negative connotation, suggesting that the act of giving is done to an extent that is unhelpful, unproductive, or even harmful to the recipient or the giver.  |  His overcharity to the beggar only enabled his continued begging. 他的过度施舍只会助长乞丐继续乞讨。  The overcharity of the government led to widespread dependency. 政府的过度施舍导致了普遍的依赖。 | excessive generosity, undue benevolence | stinginess, miserliness | 名词
