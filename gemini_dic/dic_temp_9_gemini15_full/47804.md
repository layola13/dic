decarbonised:/ˌdiːˈkɑːrbənaɪzd/ | adj. | 脱碳的；已脱碳的 | Reduced or removed carbon dioxide emissions.  The process of reducing or removing carbon dioxide from the atmosphere or from a particular process or system. |  例句：The company has decarbonised its operations by switching to renewable energy.  这家公司通过转向可再生能源使其运营脱碳。 |例句：The government aims to achieve a fully decarbonised energy system by 2050. 政府的目标是在2050年实现完全脱碳的能源系统。| 近义词：carbon-neutral, low-carbon | 反义词：carbon-intensive
