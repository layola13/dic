champed:/tʃæmpt/| v.|用力咀嚼；急切地等待|To chew noisily or impatiently.|例句：The horse champed at the bit, eager to run. 这匹马用力地嚼着马嚼子，渴望奔跑。|例句：He champed at the bit, waiting for the meeting to start. 他焦急地等待着会议开始。|近义词：gnaw, chew, bite|反义词：swallow, gulp


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He champs his food noisily. = 他大声咀嚼食物。
The dog champs at its bone. = 狗用力啃咬着骨头。

一般过去时:
She champed her gum nervously. = 她紧张地嚼着口香糖。
The audience champed at the bit for the concert to begin. = 观众焦急地等待着音乐会开始。

现在完成时:
He has champed at the bit all morning. = 他整个上午都焦急地等待着。
The children have champed through their snacks. = 孩子们已经狼吞虎咽地吃掉了零食。

过去完成时:
They had champed their way through a mountain of cookies. = 他们已经吃掉了一大堆饼干。
She had champed at the bit all day, waiting for the news. = 她一整天都焦急地等待着消息。

将来时:
He will champ at the bit until he gets his promotion. = 他会一直焦急地等待，直到得到晋升。
They will champ through the entire buffet. = 他们会把自助餐上的所有食物都吃光。

虚拟语气:
If I were a horse, I would champ at the bit. = 如果我是一匹马，我会用力嚼着马嚼子。
I wish I hadn't champed so loudly during the meeting. = 我真希望在会议上不要嚼得那么大声。

被动语态:  (Champed is rarely used passively)
The food was champed noisily by the child.  =  食物被孩子大声地嚼着。


疑问句:
Is he champing at the bit? = 他是不是焦急地等待着？
Did they champ through all the food? = 他们是不是把所有的食物都吃掉了？

否定句:
He didn't champ at the bit; he was quite calm. = 他并不焦急地等待；他很平静。
She didn't champ her food; she ate politely. = 她没有大声咀嚼食物；她吃得很斯文。
