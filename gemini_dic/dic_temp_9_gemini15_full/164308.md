preeducated: /ˌpriːˈɛdʒukeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 预先教育的，事先受教育的 |  Having received education or training beforehand.  This word is rarely used and often considered informal or non-standard. It implies prior instruction or preparation before a specific event or task. | 例句：The preeducated group performed significantly better on the test. = 预先接受过教育的组在测试中表现明显更好。


The soldiers were preeducated in survival techniques before deployment. = 士兵们在部署前接受了生存技巧的预先培训。


近义词：pre-trained, prepared, briefed, indoctrinated | 反义词：unprepared, uninstructed, naive


词性:形容词
