dipterocarpus:/ˌdɪptərəˈkɑːrpəs/| n. |龙脑香属植物|A genus of tropical Asian trees in the family Dipterocarpaceae, known for their valuable timber and fragrant resin.  Many species are important sources of timber and resin, some producing valuable oils. |例句：Dipterocarpus trees are a significant component of Southeast Asian rainforests.  龙脑香属植物是东南亚雨林的重要组成部分。|例句：The fragrant resin from certain Dipterocarpus species is used in incense.  某些龙脑香属植物的芳香树脂用于香料。|近义词：Shorea (another genus in the Dipterocarpaceae family)|反义词：无|名词
