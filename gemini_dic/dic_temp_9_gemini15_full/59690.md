echometry:/ɛkˈɒmɪtri/ | n. |回声测深法；回声探测；回声测量|The measurement of distances or depths by means of reflected sound waves (echoes).  It's used extensively in various fields, such as oceanography for determining water depth, and in medicine for creating images of internal organs.|Example:  Echometry was used to map the seabed. 回声测深法被用来绘制海底地图。|近义词：sonar, echo sounding|反义词：None directly applicable.
