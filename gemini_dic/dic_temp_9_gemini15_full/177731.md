recapturable: /ˌriːˈkæptʃərəbl/ | adj. | 可重新捕获的，可收回的 | Capable of being recaptured or recovered.  Something that is recapturable can be caught or obtained again after having been lost or escaped. |  The escaped prisoner was considered recapturable due to his limited resources.  逃犯由于资源有限被认为是可以重新捕获的。

The lost data, while challenging to retrieve, was ultimately recapturable through data recovery software.  丢失的数据虽然难以检索，但最终可以通过数据恢复软件重新获取。


近义词：recoverable, retrievable

反义词：irrecoverable, irretrievable

词性：形容词
