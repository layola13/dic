simile:/ˈsɪməli/ | n. |明喻，比喻 |A figure of speech involving the comparison of one thing with another thing of a different kind, used to make a description more emphatic or vivid.  It usually uses "like" or "as". |例句：He is as brave as a lion. 他像狮子一样勇敢。|例句：Her voice was like silk. 她的声音像丝绸一样柔美。|近义词：metaphor, analogy |反义词：literal description
