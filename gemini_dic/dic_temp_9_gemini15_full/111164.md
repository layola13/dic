lithocarp:/ˈlɪθəˌkɑːrp/| n. |坚果树；栎属植物|A lithocarp is a type of tree belonging to the genus Lithocarpus,  which is  part of the beech family (Fagaceae) and known for its acorns.  Many species are found in Asia, with some in North America. They are often evergreen trees with leathery leaves.  |例句：Many species of lithocarps thrive in the humid climates of Southeast Asia.  许多栎属植物在东南亚潮湿的气候中茁壮成长。|例句：The forest was dominated by various species of lithocarps, their acorns scattered on the forest floor. 这片森林里主要生长着各种栎属植物，它们的橡子散落在森林地面上。|近义词：oak (in some contexts), evergreen tree|反义词：无|词性:noun
