separatist:/ˈsɛpəreɪtɪst/| n. adj.|分离主义者；分离主义的|A separatist is a person who supports the separation of a group of people from a larger body, especially a political state.  A separatist movement advocates for such separation.|例句：The separatist movement in the region has gained considerable support.  该地区的分裂主义运动获得了相当大的支持。The separatist leader called for a referendum on independence. 分裂主义领导人呼吁就独立举行公投。|近义词： secessionist, autonomist |反义词：unionist, integrationist
