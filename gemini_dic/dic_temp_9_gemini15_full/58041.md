drolled:/drōld/| v.|  傻笑，故作滑稽地说话 |To speak or behave in a playfully teasing or mocking way; to speak or act in a silly or amusing manner.  |例句：He drolled about his disastrous date. 他滑稽地谈论了他糟糕的约会。

例句：She drolled a witty anecdote about her boss. 她故作滑稽地讲了一个关于她老板的趣闻轶事。


近义词：jested, quipped,  capered,  clowned


反义词：serious, solemn, grave


词性:动词
