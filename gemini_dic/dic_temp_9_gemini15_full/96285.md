indifference:/ɪnˈdɪfərəns/| n. |冷漠；漠不关心；不重要 |A state of not caring; lack of interest or concern.  It can refer to a feeling, an attitude, or a lack of effect. |例句：Her indifference to his pleas was hurtful.  她的冷漠回应让他很受伤。

例句：The government's indifference to the plight of the refugees is shocking. 政府对难民困境的漠不关心令人震惊。


近义词：apathy, unconcern, insensibility, neutrality |反义词：concern, interest, enthusiasm, passion |词性:名词
