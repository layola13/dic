misfeasance:/ˌmɪsˈfiːzəns/| n. |不当行为；渎职；职务过失 |The improper performance of a lawful act.  This differs from malfeasance (doing something illegal) and nonfeasance (failing to do something required). Misfeasance involves doing something correctly but in the wrong way or at the wrong time, causing harm or damage. |例句：The doctor was accused of misfeasance for administering the wrong dosage of medication.  医生因服用错误剂量的药物而被指控犯有渎职行为。

The construction company's misfeasance in building the bridge led to its collapse. 建筑公司在桥梁建设中的不当行为导致了桥梁的倒塌。|近义词：malpractice, negligence, wrongdoing |反义词：nonfeasance, proper conduct
