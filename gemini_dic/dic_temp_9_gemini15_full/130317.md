non-temporary: /ˌnɒnˈtɛmpərəri/ | adj. |非临时的；永久的|  Not temporary; lasting or intended to last for a long time.  It emphasizes the enduring nature of something, contrasting with a temporary or short-lived existence. |例句：This is a non-temporary solution to the problem.  这是一个永久的解决方案。

例句：The non-temporary nature of the damage required extensive repairs.  损坏的持久性需要大规模的维修。


近义词：permanent, lasting, enduring, abiding, fixed

反义词：temporary, transient, ephemeral, fleeting, short-term


词性：形容词
