This word is rare and largely unused in modern English.|The judge's overmercifulnesses led to the release of several dangerous criminals.  法官的过度仁慈导致了几名危险罪犯被释放。|近义词：excessive compassion, undue clemency|反义词：harshness, severity, strictness
