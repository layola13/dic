/ɡæs/| n. |气体，煤气，汽油|A substance in a gaseous state, having no fixed shape or volume.  It can be a fuel, such as natural gas or propane, or a component of the atmosphere, such as oxygen or nitrogen.|例句：Natural gas is a common fuel source. 天然气是一种常见的燃料来源。|例句：The car runs on gasoline. 这辆车用汽油驱动。|近义词：gasoline, vapor, steam|反义词：solid, liquid|名词
