grandma:/ˈɡrænmɑː/|/ˈɡrænmɑː/| n. |奶奶；外婆|A grandmother; a mother's or father's mother.  The term is often used affectionately.|例句：My grandma knits beautiful sweaters.  我的奶奶织漂亮的毛衣。|例句：I spent the summer with my grandma at her beach house. 我在奶奶的海边别墅和她一起度过了夏天。|近义词：grandmother, granny, nana|反义词：grandchild


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My grandma bakes delicious cookies. = 我的奶奶烤美味的饼干。
Grandma always tells wonderful stories. = 奶奶总是讲精彩的故事。

一般过去时:
My grandma visited us last week. = 我的奶奶上周来看我们了。
Grandma made a delicious pie for my birthday. = 奶奶为我的生日做了一个美味的馅饼。
