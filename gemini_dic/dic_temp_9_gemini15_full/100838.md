introit:/ˈɪntroɪt/| n. |圣歌；开场白；序言 |An introit is a short anthem or chant sung at the beginning of a religious service, particularly in the Mass.  It often sets the tone for the service and may include verses from Scripture. |例句：The introit was sung beautifully by the choir.  = 合唱团优美地演唱了圣歌。

The introit for today's service is particularly moving. = 今天礼拜的圣歌尤其感人。


近义词：prelude, opening, introduction |反义词：conclusion, ending, finale |词性:noun
