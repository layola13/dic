purchase: /ˈpɜːrtʃəs/| /ˈpɝːtʃəs/| n., vt.|购买；采购；获得|The act of buying something; something that has been bought.|例句：I made a purchase at the mall. 我在商场买了一件东西。|例句：The purchase of a new car is a big decision. 购买一辆新车是一个重大的决定。|近义词：buy, acquire, obtain|反义词：sell, dispose of


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He purchases groceries every week. = 他每周都购买杂货。
She purchases her clothes online. = 她在线购买衣服。

一般过去时:
I purchased a new laptop last month. = 我上个月买了一台新笔记本电脑。
They purchased a house in the suburbs. = 他们在郊区买了一栋房子。

现在完成时:
She has purchased several books this year. = 她今年已经买了几本书。
He has purchased all the necessary equipment. = 他已经购买了所有必要的设备。

过去完成时:
I had purchased the tickets before the prices went up. = 价格上涨之前我已经买好了票。
They had purchased the land before building the house. = 他们在建房之前就买好了地。

将来时:
We will purchase a new car next year. = 我们明年将买一辆新车。
They will purchase the supplies tomorrow. = 他们明天将购买用品。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would purchase that painting. = 如果我有更多钱，我会买那幅画。
I wish I could purchase a beachfront property. = 我真希望我能买到海滨房产。

被动语态:
The tickets were purchased online. = 票是在网上购买的。
The house was purchased at a good price. = 这房子以一个好价格卖出。

疑问句:
Did you purchase anything at the store? = 你在商店里买东西了吗？
Where did he purchase the car? = 他在哪儿买的车？

否定句:
I did not purchase anything today. = 我今天什么也没买。
They did not purchase the property. = 他们没有买下那处房产。


PHRASE->

strategic purchase = 战略性收购
