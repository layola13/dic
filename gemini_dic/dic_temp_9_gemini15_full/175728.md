rappeled:/ræˈpɛld/ | v. | 绳降；用绳索下降 | To descend a steep cliff or rock face using ropes.  | Example:  He rappelled down the cliff face with practiced ease. 他熟练地沿悬崖峭壁下降。


Example: They rappelled into the canyon to explore the hidden waterfalls. 他们绳降到峡谷中去探索隐藏的瀑布。


近义词：abseiled, descended

反义词：ascended, climbed
