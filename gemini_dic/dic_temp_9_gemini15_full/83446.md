habitator:/ˈhæbɪteɪtər/| n. |居住者，栖息者 |A habitator is an inhabitant or resident of a particular place.  It refers to any living organism that dwells in a specific habitat.  The term often implies a more permanent or established presence than simply a visitor. |Example: The desert habitator must endure extreme temperatures.  沙漠中的居住者必须忍受极端的气温。The deep-sea habitator is adapted to the crushing pressure. 深海居住者已适应巨大的压力。|近义词：inhabitant, resident, dweller |反义词：visitor, migrant
