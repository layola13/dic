resale:/ˌriːˈseɪl/| n.|转售，再售|The act of selling something again, especially something that has been previously owned.  It refers to the selling of used goods or property. |例句：The resale value of the car is surprisingly high. 这辆车的转售价值出奇的高。

例句：They make a profit from the resale of second-hand clothing. 他们通过转售二手服装获利。


近义词：secondhand sale, re-selling, retail

反义词：original sale, first sale


词性：名词
