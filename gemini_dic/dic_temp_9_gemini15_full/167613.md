preyed:/preɪd/| v.|捕食；折磨；困扰|To hunt and kill for food; to have a harmful or negative effect on someone or something.|例句：The lion preyed on the zebra.  狮子捕食斑马。|例句：Doubt preyed on her mind. 疑虑困扰着她。|近义词：hunt, attack, harass, torment|反义词：protect, aid, comfort


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The cat preys on mice. = 猫捕食老鼠。
The worries prey on his mind. = 担忧困扰着他的思绪。

一般过去时:
The eagle preyed on the fish. = 老鹰捕食了鱼。
The news preyed heavily on his mind. = 这消息严重地困扰着他。

现在完成时:
The wolves have preyed on the sheep for weeks. = 狼群已经捕食羊群数周了。
Fear has preyed upon her ever since the accident. = 自从事故发生以来，恐惧一直困扰着她。


过去完成时:
The farmer had preyed on the land for generations. = 这位农民世世代代地耕耘这片土地。(此处prey on指过度开发，并非字面意义的捕食)
She had preyed on his kindness for too long. = 她利用他的善良太久了。(此处prey on指利用)


将来时:
The hawk will prey on smaller birds. = 鹰将会捕食更小的鸟类。
I fear that loneliness will prey on his spirit. = 我担心孤独会折磨他的心灵。


虚拟语气:
If lions preyed on humans more often, we would be more cautious. = 如果狮子更频繁地捕食人类，我们会更加谨慎。
If the doubt had not preyed on her mind, she would have succeeded. = 如果疑虑没有困扰她，她就会成功了。


被动语态:
The sheep were preyed upon by the wolves. = 羊被狼捕食了。
His mind was preyed upon by anxieties. = 他的思绪被焦虑所困扰。


疑问句:
What animals prey on rabbits? = 什么动物捕食兔子？
What kind of worries prey on your mind lately? = 最近是什么样的担忧困扰着你？


否定句:
The cat did not prey on the bird. = 猫没有捕食那只鸟。
The problem did not prey on her conscience. = 这个问题并没有困扰她的良心。


PHRASE->
prey on someone's mind = 困扰某人
prey upon one's fears = 利用某人的恐惧
a prey to = 受…支配；成为…的牺牲品
fall prey to = 成为…的牺牲品
prey to despair = 陷入绝望
