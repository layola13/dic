marchlike:/ˈmɑːrtʃlaɪk/| adj.|像三月一样的；像行军一样的|Resembling the month of March; characterized by the qualities typically associated with March, such as strong winds, sudden changes in weather, or the beginning of spring.|例句：The weather was marchlike, with blustery winds and occasional sunshine.  三月般的天气，狂风阵阵，间或阳光普照。  The soldiers marched with a marchlike precision. 士兵们以行军般的精确度行进。|近义词：springlike, stormy, blustery|反义词：calm, peaceful, still
