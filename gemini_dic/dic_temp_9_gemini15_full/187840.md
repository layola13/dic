roseola:/roʊˈziːələ/| n. |玫瑰疹，婴儿玫瑰疹|A viral infection, particularly common in young children, characterized by a distinctive rash.  The rash typically appears as small, pink spots that spread across the body. Roseola is usually mild and resolves on its own. |Example: My toddler developed roseola last week, but he's already feeling much better.  我的幼儿上周得了玫瑰疹，但他现在已经好多了。|近义词：exanthem, rash |反义词：None specifically.
