promoed:/prəˈmoʊd/ | v. |晋升，提升，宣传 | To advance to a higher rank or position; to promote or publicize. |例句：She was promoed to senior manager after years of hard work. 她多年努力后被提升为高级经理。 He promoed his new book on social media. 他在社交媒体上宣传了他的新书。 |近义词：promoted, advanced, elevated |反义词：demoted, downgraded

USAGE->
