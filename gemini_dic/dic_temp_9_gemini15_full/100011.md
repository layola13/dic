interpenetrates:/ˌɪntərˈpɛnɪtreɪts/| v.|相互渗透，互相交织|To penetrate or be penetrated mutually; to be interwoven or interconnected.|The two cultures interpenetrate, influencing each other's traditions and values.  两种文化相互渗透，互相影响彼此的传统和价值观。|The roots of the trees interpenetrate beneath the soil. 树根在地下相互交织。|interweave, permeate, blend|separate, divide|动词
