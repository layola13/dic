louisvillian:/ˌluːiˈvɪljən/| adj.|路易斯维尔人（的）；路易斯维尔风格的|Relating to or characteristic of Louisville, Kentucky.  It can refer to a person from Louisville or something that reflects the culture or style of the city.|例句：She's a Louisvillian, born and raised in the heart of the city. 她是个地道的路易斯维尔人，在市中心长大。|例句：The restaurant serves Louisvillian barbecue, a unique blend of spices and flavors. 这家餐馆供应路易斯维尔风味的烤肉，独特的香料和风味融合。|近义词：Kentuckian (if referring to someone from Kentucky)|反义词：无
