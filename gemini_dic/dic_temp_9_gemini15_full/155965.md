photogram:/ˈfoʊtəˌɡræm/ | n. |摄影图，照片图；影象图 |A photogram is a photograph made without a camera, by placing objects directly onto photosensitive material and exposing it to light.  The objects block the light, creating shadows and silhouettes on the developed print. |Example:  The artist created a stunning photogram of flowers and leaves.  这位艺术家创作了一幅令人惊叹的花叶摄影图。  The resulting photogram showed a delicate pattern of lace.  最终的摄影图显示出精致的花边图案。|近义词：photograph, image |反义词：N/A
