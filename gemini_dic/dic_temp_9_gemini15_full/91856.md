hyperimmunization:/ˌhaɪpərɪmjʊnɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 超免疫, 过度免疫 | The process of inducing a high level of immunity, often exceeding that achieved through natural infection or standard vaccination.  This is typically done by administering multiple doses of an antigen or using specific immune boosting techniques. | Example: Hyperimmunization with tetanus toxoid is recommended for individuals at high risk of exposure.  过度免疫破伤风类毒素对于高危人群来说是推荐的。 |近义词:  immunization,  immunoprophylaxis |反义词:immunosuppression
