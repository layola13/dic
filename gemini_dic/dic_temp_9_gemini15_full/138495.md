novices:/ˈnɑːvɪsɪz/| n. |新手，初学者 | Beginners or learners who are new to a field or activity.  They lack experience and expertise. |Example: The class was designed for novices in pottery.  这门课是为陶艺新手设计的。

The workshop is perfect for novices in photography.  这个工作坊非常适合摄影新手。


近义词：beginners, tyros, neophytes, apprentices |反义词：experts, veterans, professionals, masters |词性：名词


词源:
The word "novice" ultimately derives from the Latin word "novicius," meaning "new" or "newly arrived."  This term was frequently used to describe new members of religious orders.  Over time, its meaning broadened to encompass anyone new to a particular skill or field.
