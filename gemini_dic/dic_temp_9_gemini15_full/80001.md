go-missing:/ˌɡoʊ ˈmɪsɪŋ/| v. |失踪，不见了|To disappear unexpectedly or without explanation; to become lost or unavailable.|The child went missing from the playground.  孩子在操场上失踪了。|The documents have gone missing. 文件不见了。|disappear, vanish, be lost|be found, appear, be present|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He goes missing every weekend. 他每个周末都失踪。
The remote control goes missing frequently. 遥控器经常不见了。


一般过去时:
She went missing last night. 她昨晚失踪了。
My keys went missing after the party. 聚会后我的钥匙不见了。


现在完成时:
My wallet has gone missing. 我的钱包不见了。
Several valuable paintings have gone missing from the museum. 博物馆里几幅珍贵的画不见了。


过去完成时:
The police discovered that the jewels had gone missing before the alarm was raised.  警方发现警报发出之前珠宝就丢失了。
He realised his phone had gone missing when he tried to call his wife. 他想给他妻子打电话时才意识到手机不见了。


将来时:
If he keeps drinking like this, he will go missing again. 如果他继续这样喝下去，他还会再次失踪。
I fear my luggage will go missing on the flight. 我担心我的行李会在航班上丢失。


虚拟语气:
If she hadn't gone missing, we would be celebrating her birthday now. 如果她没有失踪，我们现在就会庆祝她的生日。
I wish my favorite book hadn't gone missing. 我希望我最喜欢的书没有丢失。


被动语态:
It was reported that a young girl had gone missing in the woods. 据报道，一个年轻女孩在树林里失踪了。
It is feared that more artifacts will go missing from the ancient ruins. 人们担心古代遗址中会有更多文物丢失。


疑问句:
Has anyone gone missing in this area recently? 最近这个地区有人失踪了吗？
Did your dog go missing last week? 你的狗上周失踪了吗？


否定句:
The police confirmed that no one had gone missing in the village. 警方证实村里没有人失踪。
Fortunately, none of our belongings went missing during the trip. 幸运的是，我们旅行期间没有任何东西丢失。
