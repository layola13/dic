commemorators:/kəˈmɛməreɪtərz/ | n. |纪念者，庆祝者 |Those who participate in commemorating an event or person.  People who remember and honor significant events or individuals. |例句：The commemorators laid wreaths at the war memorial. 纪念者在战争纪念碑前献上了花圈。  The commemorators gathered to celebrate the anniversary of the city's founding. 纪念者聚集在一起庆祝城市建城周年纪念。|近义词：celebrators, rememberers, mourners (in the context of remembrance)|反义词：forgetters (in the context of remembrance)
