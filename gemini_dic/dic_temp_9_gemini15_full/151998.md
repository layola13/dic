patagium:/pəˈteɪdʒiəm/| n.|飞膜；翼膜|A membrane of skin that stretches between the limbs of certain animals, enabling gliding flight.|Example:  Flying squirrels use their patagium to glide between trees.  飞鼠利用它们的飞膜在树木之间滑翔。|近义词：membrane, wing (in a gliding context)|反义词：无
