presbyterians:/ˌprɛzbɪˈtɪəriənz/| n. |长老会教徒；长老会信徒| Presbyterians are members of Presbyterian churches, which are Protestant churches governed by elders.  The term derives from the Greek word "presbyteros," meaning "elder."  Presbyterianism emphasizes the authority of scripture and the importance of both individual faith and congregational governance.| Many Presbyterians attend worship services on Sundays.  许多长老会教徒在星期天参加礼拜仪式。


The Presbyterian Church is known for its strong theological tradition.  长老会教堂以其强大的神学传统而闻名。|近义词：Protestants, Calvinists|反义词：无
