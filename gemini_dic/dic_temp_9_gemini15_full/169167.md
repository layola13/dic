proles:/ˈprəʊlz/| n.|无产阶级；平民；下层民众|The word "proles" refers to the working class, the common people, or the lower social strata, often characterized by their lack of political power and influence.  It's a term frequently associated with George Orwell's dystopian novel *Nineteen Eighty-Four*.|In Orwell's *Nineteen Eighty-Four*, the proles are the vast majority of the population, largely ignored by the Party.|近义词：common people, working class, masses, proletariat|反义词：elite, aristocracy, upper class|名词
