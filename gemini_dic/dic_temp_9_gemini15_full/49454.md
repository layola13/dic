deltic:/ˈdɛltɪk/| adj.|三角洲的；三角洲形的|Relating to or characteristic of a delta.  A delta is a landform created by the deposition of sediment that is carried by a river as the flow leaves its mouth and enters slower-moving or stagnant water.|例句：The deltic plain supported a rich ecosystem. 三角洲平原支撑着一个丰富的生态系统。|例句：The river formed a large deltic area. 这条河形成了一个很大的三角洲地区。|近义词：deltaic|反义词： upland, mountainous
