habergeon:/ˈhæbərdʒən/| n. |锁子甲|A type of medieval chain mail shirt, typically sleeveless, worn as body armor.  It was often made of interconnected metal rings.  |Example: The knight wore a habergeon under his plate armor for added protection.  骑士在板甲下穿着一件锁子甲以增加保护。|近义词：chain mail, mail shirt |反义词：None  |名词
