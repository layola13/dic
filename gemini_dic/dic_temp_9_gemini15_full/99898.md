international: /ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəl/ | adj. | 国际的，国际性的 | Relating to or involving more than one nation or country.  |例句：The company has international operations. 这家公司在国际上开展业务。|近义词：global, worldwide, transnational |反义词：domestic, national, local |


英文解析：The adjective "international" describes something that involves or affects more than one nation or country.  It signifies a scope or impact that extends beyond national borders.


例句：

The Olympics are an international sporting event. 奥运会是一项国际体育赛事。

She works for an international organization. 她在一个国际组织工作。


词源：

The word "international" is derived from the prefix "inter-" (meaning "between" or "among") and the word "national" (relating to a nation).  Therefore, it literally means "between nations" or "among nations."
