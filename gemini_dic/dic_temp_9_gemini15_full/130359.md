non-unanimous:/ˌnɒnjuˈnænɪməs/| adj. |非一致的，不一致的|Not unanimous; not showing complete agreement.  It implies a lack of complete agreement, with some dissenting opinions present. |The jury's verdict was non-unanimous, with one juror dissenting. 陪审团的裁决并非一致，有一名陪审员持异议。| unanimous, agreed, consentient | unanimous, agreed-upon |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The committee's decision is non-unanimous. = 委员会的决定并非一致。
The results of the survey are non-unanimous. = 调查结果并不一致。


一般过去时:
The vote was non-unanimous; several members opposed the motion. = 表决结果并非一致；几名成员反对该动议。
The jury's verdict in the trial was non-unanimous. = 审判中陪审团的裁决并非一致。


现在完成时:
The board has reached a non-unanimous decision on the merger. = 董事会就合并问题达成了非一致的决定。
The group has expressed a non-unanimous opinion on the proposal. = 该小组对该提案表达了不一致的意见。


过去完成时:
The team had already reached a non-unanimous conclusion before the new evidence emerged. = 在新的证据出现之前，团队已经得出了一个非一致的结论。
It had become clear that the previous agreement was non-unanimous. = 很明显，之前的协议并非一致。


将来时:
The upcoming vote is expected to be non-unanimous. = 预计即将进行的投票结果将不会一致。
Their decision on the matter will likely be non-unanimous. = 他们对这件事的决定很可能不会一致。


虚拟语气:
If the decision were non-unanimous, we would have to reconsider. = 如果决定并非一致，我们将不得不重新考虑。
I wish the committee's opinion were not non-unanimous. = 我希望委员会的意见不是不一致的。


被动语态:
A non-unanimous decision was reached by the council. = 理事会做出了一个非一致的决定。
A non-unanimous verdict was delivered by the jury. = 陪审团做出了一个非一致的裁决。


疑问句:
Was the decision non-unanimous? = 决定是否并非一致？
Is the outcome likely to be non-unanimous? = 结果是否可能不一致？


否定句:
The decision was not non-unanimous; everyone agreed. = 决定并非不一致；每个人都同意。
The vote was not non-unanimous; it was unanimous. = 投票并非不一致；它是全票通过的。
