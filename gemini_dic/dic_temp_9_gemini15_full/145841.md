overdemand:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈmænd/ | n. | 超额需求，过量需求 |  Excessive demand; a demand that exceeds supply or capacity.  This often leads to shortages, price increases, and potentially rationing. | The overdemand for electricity during the heatwave caused widespread power outages.  电力在热浪期间的超额需求导致了大范围的停电。 The overdemand for tickets resulted in many fans being unable to attend the concert.  门票的超额需求导致许多歌迷无法参加音乐会。 | high demand, excessive demand | undersupply, insufficient demand | 名词
