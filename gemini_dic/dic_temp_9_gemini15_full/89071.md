holosteric:/ˌhɒləˈstɛrɪk/| adj. |全位阻的，全位阻性的| Relating to or denoting the binding of a ligand to a receptor at a site other than the primary binding site, affecting the binding at the primary site. This type of binding often involves allosteric modulation, but can also affect the primary binding site directly.|  The holosteric inhibitor binds to a site distant from the active site, causing a conformational change that prevents substrate binding. 全位阻抑制剂结合在远离活性位点的位点，引起构象变化，从而阻止底物结合。|近义词：allosteric (in some contexts)|反义词：orthosteric
