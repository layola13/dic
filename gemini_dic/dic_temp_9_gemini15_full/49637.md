demilancer: /ˌdɛmiˈlɑːnsər/ | n. | 半枪骑兵；轻骑兵 | A demilancer was a type of light cavalry soldier, typically armed with a lance but less heavily armored than a full lancer.  The term is primarily historical, referring to a specific type of cavalry unit in the 16th and 17th centuries. | Example: The demilancers charged across the field, their lighter armor offering greater speed but less protection.  半枪骑兵横扫战场，他们较轻的盔甲提供了更快的速度，但也更缺乏保护。| 近义词：light cavalry, dragoon | 反义词：lancer (in the sense of a fully equipped lancer) | 名词
