readying:/ˈrediɪŋ/| v. |准备；使准备好|The act of making something or someone ready.  It often implies a process of preparation that is underway.|例句：She is readying the children for school. = 她正在为孩子们准备上学。

例句：The chef is readying the ingredients for the special dish. = 厨师正在准备特殊菜肴的配料。


近义词：preparing, getting ready, making ready|反义词：delaying, postponing|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is readying the boat for the race. = 他正在为比赛准备船只。
They are readying themselves for the exam. = 他们正在为考试做准备。

一般过去时:
She readied the house for the guests. = 她为客人准备好了房子。
He readied his presentation before the meeting. = 他在会议前准备好了他的演示文稿。

现在完成时:
They have readied all the materials for the project. = 他们已经为项目准备好了所有材料。
She has readied herself for the challenging task ahead. = 她已经为即将到来的艰巨任务做好了准备。

过去完成时:
He had readied the car before his journey. = 他在旅程开始前已经准备好汽车了。
They had readied the documents before the lawyer arrived. = 律师到达之前，他们已经准备好文件了。

将来时:
We will ready the equipment by tomorrow morning. = 我们将在明天早上准备好设备。
She will ready the food for the party by evening. = 她将在晚上准备好派对的食物。

虚拟语气:
If I had readied the documents earlier, I wouldn't be in this mess. = 如果我早点准备好文件，我就不会陷入这种困境。
I wish I had readied myself better for the interview. = 我真希望我为面试做了更好的准备。

被动语态:
The room was readied for the conference. = 会议室已准备好用于会议。
The documents are being readied for publication. = 文件正在准备出版。

疑问句:
Is she readying the car for a long trip? = 她正在为长途旅行准备汽车吗？
Are they readying the supplies for the camping trip? = 他们正在为露营旅行准备用品吗？

否定句:
He didn't ready himself properly for the marathon. = 他没有为马拉松做好充分的准备。
They haven't readied the venue for the concert yet. = 他们还没有为音乐会准备好场地。
