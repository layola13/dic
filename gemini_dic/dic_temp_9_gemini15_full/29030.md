cartooning:/kɑːˈtuːnɪŋ/| n.|卡通创作，漫画绘制|The art or process of creating cartoons.|例句：His cartooning skills are exceptional.他的漫画创作技巧非常出色。|近义词：comic drawing, animation, caricature|反义词：realistic painting, photography


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is cartooning a superhero. = 他正在创作一个超级英雄的漫画。
She cartoons for a popular magazine. = 她为一本流行杂志绘制漫画。

He cartooned a funny scene yesterday. = 他昨天画了一幅有趣的场景漫画。
