prestraighten:/ˌpriːˈstreɪtən/| v.|预先拉直；预先矫正|To straighten something beforehand, often to prepare it for a later process.  This is a relatively uncommon word, formed by combining "pre-" (before) and "straighten."|例句：The carpenter prestraightened the wood before gluing it to the frame. 木匠在把木头粘到框架上之前先把它拉直了。

The tailor prestraightened the fabric to prevent wrinkles in the finished garment.裁缝预先拉直了布料，以防止成品衣服起皱。


近义词：pre-align, prepare, adjust|反义词：pre-bend, pre-curve
