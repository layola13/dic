slink:/slɪŋk/| vi.|偷偷摸摸地走；潜行；萎靡；衰落|to move in a quiet, stealthy way, often because one is ashamed or guilty; to become weak or less effective|例句：The thief slinked away into the shadows. 小偷偷偷摸摸地溜进了阴影里。

The cat slinked through the tall grass. 猫在高高的草丛中潜行。


近义词：sneak, creep, skulk, slither|反义词：stride, swagger, strut|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He slinks away when he sees me. = 他看到我就偷偷溜走了。
The economy is slinking into recession. = 经济正在慢慢滑向衰退。

一般过去时:
She slinked out of the room after the argument. = 争吵后，她偷偷溜出了房间。
The company's market share slinked steadily downward. = 公司的市场份额稳步下降。

现在完成时:
He has slinked away from his responsibilities. = 他已经逃避了他的责任。
The once-powerful empire has slinked into obscurity. = 曾经强大的帝国已经默默无闻了。

过去完成时:
She had slinked off before anyone noticed. = 在任何人注意到之前，她就偷偷溜走了。
The project had slinked into failure after a series of setbacks. = 在经历了一系列挫折之后，该项目已经失败了。

将来时:
He will slink away when the time is right. = 时机成熟时，他会偷偷溜走。
The popularity of the product is expected to slink. = 该产品的受欢迎程度预计会下降。

虚拟语气:
If I were him, I would slink away and never return. = 如果我是他，我会偷偷溜走，永不再回来。
I wish my confidence wouldn't slink so easily. = 我希望我的信心不会这么容易就下降。

被动语态:
(Slink is an intransitive verb and does not have a passive voice.)  slink没有被动语态


疑问句:
Why does he always slink away when confronted? = 为什么他总是被质问时就偷偷溜走？
Is the project slinking towards failure? = 这个项目是不是正在走向失败？

否定句:
He didn't slink away; he walked out confidently. = 他没有偷偷溜走；他自信地走了出去。
The sales figures didn't slink; they actually increased. = 销售数字并没有下降；它们实际上增加了。


PHRASE->
slink away = 偷偷溜走
slink off = 偷偷离开
slink into = 偷偷溜进
slink out = 偷偷溜出
slink past = 偷偷走过

词源:
The word "slink" is of Old English origin, deriving from the word "slincan," meaning "to creep or glide."  Its meaning has evolved over time to incorporate the connotations of stealth and shame.
