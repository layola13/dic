cyanurate:/ˈsaɪənʊreɪt/| n. |氰尿酸盐|A salt or ester of cyanuric acid. Cyanurates are used in various applications, including flame retardants, herbicides, and disinfectants.  They are characterized by a cyclic structure containing three nitrogen atoms and three carbonyl groups.|例句：Melamine cyanurate is a common flame retardant used in plastics.  三聚氰胺氰尿酸盐是塑料中常用的阻燃剂。|近义词：暂无直接近义词，需根据具体语境选择|反义词：暂无直接反义词|名词
