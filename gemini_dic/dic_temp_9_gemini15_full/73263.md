fore-understanding:/ˌfɔːrˈʌndərˌstændɪŋ/| n. |预先理解，预想，事先了解|A prior understanding or comprehension of something before it is fully explained or experienced. It implies a level of anticipation or expectation based on prior knowledge or context.|例句：The fore-understanding of the project's complexity helped the team prepare adequately.  预先了解项目的复杂性帮助团队充分准备。


例句：With a fore-understanding of the potential risks, the investors made a cautious decision. 事先了解潜在风险后，投资者做出了谨慎的决定。


近义词：preconception, anticipation, expectation, prior knowledge|反义词：misunderstanding, ignorance, surprise|词性:名词
