disaffirmation: /ˌdɪsˌæfərˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 否认；取消；撤销 | The act of denying or repudiating; the rejection of a contract or agreement, especially by a minor.  It involves a clear expression of intent to not be bound by the agreement. | Example:  The minor's disaffirmation of the contract meant he was no longer obligated to pay.  这个未成年人的合同否认意味着他不必再付款。 | 近义词：repudiation, renunciation, cancellation | 反义词：affirmation, ratification, confirmation |  词性：名词
