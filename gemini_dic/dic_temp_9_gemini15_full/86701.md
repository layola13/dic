hellward:/ˈhɛl.wərd/| adv.|地狱般地，向地狱般的地方|Moving hellward implies a descent into a state of chaos, suffering, or moral depravity.  It suggests a journey towards ruin or destruction, both literally and figuratively.|例句：The army marched hellward, leaving a trail of destruction in its wake.  军队向地狱般的地方进军，在其身后留下了一片废墟。|The soldiers were driven hellward by the relentless enemy fire.  士兵们被无情的敌军炮火逼向毁灭.|近义词：downward, towards destruction, to perdition|反义词：heavenward, upward, towards salvation|副词
