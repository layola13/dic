centring: /ˈsɛntəriŋ/ | n. | 中心，居中；对中 | The act of placing something at the center or making it central.  It can also refer to the state of being centered or the process of centering something. | 例句：The photographer carefully adjusted the centring of the subject in the frame. 摄影师仔细调整了画面中主体的居中位置。

The potter achieved perfect centring on the wheel. 陶工在轮子上实现了完美的中心定位。| 近义词：centering, alignment, focusing | 反义词：decentralization, off-center | 名词
