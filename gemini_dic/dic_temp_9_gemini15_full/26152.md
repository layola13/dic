busybodied:/ˈbɪziˌbɑːdid/| adj.| 爱管闲事，多嘴的 |  Interfering in other people's affairs; meddling.  A busybody is someone who is excessively interested in the affairs of others. |例句：He's such a busybody, always sticking his nose into other people's business. 他是个爱管闲事的人，总是把鼻子伸到别人的事里。


例句：She's a busybody; she's always gossiping about her neighbors. 她是个爱管闲事的人，总是说邻居的闲话。


近义词：meddling, interfering, intrusive, nosy, gossipy |反义词：uninvolved, indifferent, unconcerned |词性:形容词
