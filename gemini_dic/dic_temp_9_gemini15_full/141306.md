open-neck: /ˈoʊpən nek/ | adj. | 开领的 |  Describes a garment, typically a shirt or blouse, with a neckline that is not closed or fastened, but rather open at the front.  | Example: He wore an open-neck shirt to the casual party. 他穿着一件开领衬衫参加休闲派对。 | 近义词: unbuttoned, open-collared | 反义词: closed-neck, high-necked


USAGE->

The shirt has an open neck. = 这件衬衫是开领的。
She prefers open-neck blouses. = 她更喜欢开领衬衫。

一般过去时:
He wore an open-neck shirt yesterday. = 他昨天穿了一件开领衬衫。

I have always worn open-neck shirts in summer. = 夏天我一直穿开领衬衫。
