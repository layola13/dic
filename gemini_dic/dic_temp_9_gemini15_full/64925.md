establishments:/ɪˈstæblɪʃmənts/ | n. |机构；企业；设施；制度 |An establishment is a business, organization, or institution.  It can refer to a place of business, a system, or an officially recognized body.  The word often carries a connotation of being well-established, reputable, or even powerful. |例句：The new restaurant is a popular establishment in town. 这家新餐厅是城里很受欢迎的场所。


例句：The government established many new programs to help the poor. 政府制定了许多新的扶贫项目。


近义词：institution, organization, company, firm, business, system


反义词：disestablishment (the process of ending an establishment)


词性：名词
