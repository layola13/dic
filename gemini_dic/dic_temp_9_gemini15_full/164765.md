preferential:/ˌprɛfəˈrɛnʃəl/ | adj. |优先的；优惠的；特惠的 |Giving or enjoying special advantages or treatment.  It implies a favoritism or advantage given to one person or group over others. |例句：The government offered preferential tax rates to new businesses.  政府为新企业提供优惠税率。

例句：She received preferential treatment because of her connections. 她因为关系而受到优待。


近义词： advantageous, favorable, privileged, preferential treatment


反义词：unfavorable, disadvantageous, equal, standard


词性：形容词
