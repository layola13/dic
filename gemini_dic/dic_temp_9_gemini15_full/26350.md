buzzworthy:/ˈbʌzwɜːrði/| adj.| 值得关注的；备受关注的；热门的 |  Generating a lot of excitement and interest; likely to be the subject of much discussion.  | The new movie is buzzworthy, generating considerable excitement among critics and audiences. 这部新电影很热门，在评论家和观众中引起了相当大的兴奋。|  trending, popular, hot, hyped |  unremarkable, obscure, unknown |


USAGE->

The new product is buzzworthy. = 这个新产品很热门。
This restaurant is always buzzworthy. = 这家餐厅总是很受关注。

过去完成时:
