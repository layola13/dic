elastin:/ˈɛlæstɪn/| n. |弹性蛋白|A protein that is the main component of elastic fibers. It gives tissues elasticity and allows them to return to their original shape after being stretched or compressed.|The elastin in your skin helps it to remain firm and youthful.|collagen,  fibrin|rigid, inflexible|名词
