chondrometer:/kɒnˈdrɒmɪtə(r)/| n. |软骨测量器|An instrument for measuring cartilage.  A chondrometer is used in medical and research settings to precisely measure the thickness, density, or other physical properties of cartilage samples. This is crucial for diagnosing and monitoring conditions affecting cartilage, such as osteoarthritis.|The doctor used a chondrometer to assess the thickness of the patient's articular cartilage.|近义词：cartilage gauge (less precise)|反义词：无|名词
