declassification's:/ˌdiːˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. |解密（的）；脱密（的）|The act of removing the classification from something, making it public.  Declassification is a crucial process for transparency and accountability in government. |例句：The declassification of those documents revealed shocking truths about the war.  这些文件的解密揭示了关于战争的令人震惊的事实。

The declassification's impact on national security is still being debated. 解密对国家安全的影响仍在争论中。|近义词：disclosure, release, revelation |反义词：classification, secrecy
