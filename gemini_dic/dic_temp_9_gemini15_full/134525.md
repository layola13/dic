nonlicensable: /ˌnɑːnˈlaɪsnsəbl/ | adj. | 不可许可的；不能授权的 | Unable to be licensed; not subject to licensing.  This word describes something that cannot be granted a license for use, reproduction, or distribution.  This often arises from legal restrictions, ethical considerations, or the nature of the thing itself. |


例句：

The algorithm is nonlicensable due to national security concerns.  该算法由于国家安全问题而不可许可。

The government declared the ancient artifact nonlicensable to preserve its cultural heritage. 政府宣布该古代文物不可许可以保护其文化遗产。


近义词：unlicensed, unauthorized, prohibited, restricted


反义词：licensable, permitted, authorized


词性：形容词
