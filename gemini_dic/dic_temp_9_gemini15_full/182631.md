reordaining:/ˌriːɔːrˈdeɪnɪŋ/| v. |重新任命；再次授予圣职 |The act of ordaining someone again, especially a priest or minister, after a period of suspension or following a previous ordination that is considered invalid.|例句：The bishop is reordaining several priests who were wrongly dismissed. 主教正在重新任命几位被错误解雇的牧师。

例句：After the scandal, the church decided to reordain the priest who had been wrongly accused. 丑闻过后，教会决定重新任命那位被错误指控的牧师。


近义词：reinstating, reappointing

反义词：deordaining, dismissing
