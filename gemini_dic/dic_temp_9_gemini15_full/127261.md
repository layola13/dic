USAGE->
This word is an adverb, and as such doesn't have tenses like verbs.  It describes how something is done, not an action itself.  Therefore, typical verb tense examples are not applicable.  Instead, here are examples demonstrating its usage in different sentence structures:


Affirmative Sentence:
The operation was conducted navally. =  行动是由海军进行的。


Negative Sentence:
The attack was not handled navally, but by air. =  这次袭击不是由海军，而是由空军来处理的。


Question Sentence:
Was the battle fought navally or by land forces? =  这场战役是在海军还是陆军作战中进行的？
