The term can also refer to the strings of a stringed instrument.  Figuratively, it can describe a harmonious combination of elements. | Example: The guitarist played a beautiful chord progression. 吉他手弹奏了一个优美的和弦进行。  The chords of the song were simple but effective. 这首歌的和弦很简单但很有效。 |近义词：harmony, consonance |反义词：discord, dissonance |名词
