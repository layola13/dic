foreknowable: /ˌfɔːrˈnoʊəbəl/ | adj. | 可预知的，可预见的 | Capable of being known in advance; predictable.  | The outcome of the experiment was entirely foreknowable given the existing data.  这个实验的结果，鉴于现有数据，完全是可以预知的。| predictable, foreseeable, anticipated | unpredictable, unforeseeable, unexpected |  形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The future is not always foreknowable. = 未来并非总是可预知的。
This outcome is easily foreknowable. = 这个结果很容易预测。


一般过去时:
The consequences were foreknowable. = 后果是可以预见的。
The danger was clearly foreknowable. = 危险是显而易见的。


现在完成时:
The results have been foreknowable for some time. = 结果在一段时间内是可以预见的。
Many experts have found the crisis foreknowable. = 许多专家认为这场危机是可以预见的。


过去完成时:
The failure had been foreknowable. = 失败是可以预见的。
It had been foreknowable that the project would fail. = 这个项目会失败是可以预见的。


将来时:
