resisted:/rɪˈzɪstɪd/| v.|抵抗，抵制，忍住|To withstand the force or effect of (something); oppose.  |例句：The soldiers resisted the enemy attack. 士兵们抵抗了敌人的进攻。
例句：She resisted the urge to eat the entire cake. 她忍住了吃掉整个蛋糕的冲动。|近义词：withstood, opposed, defied |反义词：yielded, succumbed, surrendered |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He resists change. 他抵制改变。
They resist temptation. 他们抵制诱惑。

一般过去时:
She resisted the pressure to conform. 她抵制了顺从的压力。
The army resisted the invasion.  军队抵抗了入侵。

现在完成时:
I have resisted the urge to buy that new phone. 我已经抵制住了购买那部新手机的冲动。
We have resisted all attempts to bribe us. 我们抵制了所有贿赂我们的企图。

过去完成时:
He had resisted the temptation for many years. 他多年来一直抵制着诱惑。
They had resisted the enemy for weeks. 他们已经抵抗敌人数周了。

将来时:
I will resist any attempt to control me. 我将抵制任何控制我的企图。
She will resist the pressure to quit her job. 她将抵制辞职的压力。

虚拟语气:
If I were stronger, I would have resisted his demands. 如果我更坚强，我就会抵制他的要求。
I wish I had resisted the temptation to eat that extra slice of pizza. 我真希望我没有抵制住吃那多余的一片披萨的诱惑。(这句话表达的是后悔没有抵制)

被动语态:
His demands were resisted by the workers. 工人们抵制了他的要求。
The invasion was bravely resisted by the citizens.  市民们勇敢地抵抗了入侵。

疑问句:
Did he resist arrest? 他反抗逮捕了吗？
Will they resist the new regulations? 他们会抵制新的规章制度吗？

否定句:
He didn't resist the temptation. 他没有抵制诱惑。
They didn't resist the changes. 他们没有抵制这些变化。
