rowdyish:/ˈraʊdiʃ/| adj. |有点吵闹的，有点粗鲁的 |Slightly rowdy; somewhat boisterous or unruly.  The party got a little rowdyish later in the evening. 晚会后来有点吵闹。|近义词： boisterous, unruly, turbulent, noisy |反义词：calm, quiet, peaceful, orderly |

词源:
