precalculating:/ˌpriːˈkælkjuleɪtɪŋ/| v.|预先计算，预估|To calculate or estimate something in advance.|The engineers were precalculating the stress on the bridge before construction began. 工程师们在桥梁施工前预先计算了桥梁的压力。|近义词：forecasting, estimating, projecting|反义词：postcalculating (not a common word)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They precalculate the costs of each project. = 他们预先计算每个项目的成本。
We precalculate the necessary materials for the construction. = 我们预先计算施工所需的材料。


一般过去时:
The team precalculated the trajectory of the rocket. = 团队预先计算了火箭的轨迹。
She precalculated the amount of paint needed for the room. = 她预先计算了房间所需的油漆量。


现在完成时:
They have precalculated the potential risks involved. = 他们已经预先计算了潜在的风险。
