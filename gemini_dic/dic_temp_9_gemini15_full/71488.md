flan:/flæn/| n.|法式布丁；蛋奶酥|A flan is a custard dessert with a sweet caramel topping, baked in a shallow dish with sloping sides.|例句：The flan was rich and creamy, with a perfectly caramelized top.  这道法式布丁浓稠顺滑，焦糖顶部完美无瑕。|例句：I made a delicious chocolate flan for dessert. 我做了一份美味的巧克力法式布丁作为甜点。|近义词：custard, crème caramel|反义词：无
