Resorters often seek relaxation, entertainment, and a change of scenery.|例句：The resort was filled with resorters enjoying the sunny weather.  这个度假胜地挤满了享受阳光的度假者。  |例句：Many resorters return to the same resort year after year. 许多度假者每年都回到同一个度假胜地。|近义词：vacationer, holidaymaker, tourist|反义词：homebody, hermit|名词
