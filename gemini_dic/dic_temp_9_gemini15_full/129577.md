non-citizen:/ˌnɒnˈsɪtɪzən/| n. |非公民 |A person who is not a citizen of a particular country.  Someone who does not hold citizenship in a given nation. |Example:  He is a non-citizen of the United States, holding a green card instead. 他不是美国公民，而是持有绿卡。|近义词：alien, foreigner |反义词：citizen
