caprylate:/ˈkæpriˌleɪt/| n.|辛酸酯|A caprylate is a salt or ester of caprylic acid.  Caprylic acid is a saturated fatty acid with eight carbon atoms. Caprylates are used in various applications, including cosmetics, food, and pharmaceuticals, often as preservatives or emulsifiers.| Example:  Many skincare products contain caprylate esters to help maintain their stability and texture. 许多护肤品含有辛酸酯以帮助保持其稳定性和质地。|近义词: caprylic acid ester (近义词较少，因为caprylate本身指代一类化合物)|反义词: N/A|名词
