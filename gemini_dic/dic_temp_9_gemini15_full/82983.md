gunnel:/ˈɡʌnəl/| n.|龙骨，舷边，小鱼|The gunnel is the top edge of a boat's side.  It is also a small, slender fish.|例句：The fisherman caught several gunnels in the shallows. 渔民在浅滩捕获了几条龙骨鱼。The boat's gunnel was reinforced with extra wood.  船的舷边用额外的木头加固了。|近义词：wale, strake (for boat's edge);  |反义词：keel (bottom of boat)|名词
