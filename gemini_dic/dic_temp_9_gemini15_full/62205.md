encourage:/ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒ/| vt.|鼓励，激励，促进|To give support, confidence, or hope to someone.|例句：Her parents encouraged her to pursue her dreams.  她的父母鼓励她追求梦想。|例句：The warm weather encouraged the plants to grow quickly.温暖的天气促使植物快速生长。|近义词：motivate, inspire, stimulate|反义词：discourage, deter, hinder|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He encourages his students to think critically. 他鼓励他的学生批判性思考。
The teacher encourages participation in class. 老师鼓励课堂参与。

一般过去时:
She encouraged me to apply for the job. 她鼓励我申请这份工作。
My mentor encouraged my artistic pursuits. 我的导师鼓励我的艺术追求。

现在完成时:
His support has encouraged me to persevere. 他的支持鼓励我坚持不懈。
The positive feedback has encouraged her to continue writing.积极的反馈鼓励她继续写作。

过去完成时:
She had encouraged him to study abroad before he left.在他离开之前，她已经鼓励他出国留学了。
They had encouraged her to apply, even though she doubted herself. 即使她对自己怀疑，他们也鼓励她申请。

将来时:
I will encourage him to join the team. 我将鼓励他加入团队。
We will encourage more people to participate in the event. 我们将鼓励更多的人参与此次活动。

虚拟语气:
If I encouraged him, he might try harder. 如果我鼓励他，他可能会更加努力。
I wish I had encouraged her more. 我真希望我当时多鼓励她一些。

被动语态:
He was encouraged by his friends to try again. 他受到朋友们的鼓励再次尝试。
The students were encouraged to ask questions. 学生们被鼓励提问题。

疑问句:
Does she encourage her children to read? 她是否鼓励她的孩子们阅读？
What encouraged you to take up painting?是什么鼓励你开始绘画的？

否定句:
He didn't encourage her reckless behavior. 他没有鼓励她鲁莽的行为。
They didn't encourage laziness in the workplace. 他们不鼓励在工作场所偷懒。
