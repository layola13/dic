photodetachment:/ˌfoʊtoʊdɪˈtætʃmənt/| n. |光电离, 光致脱附 |The process by which an electron is removed from an atom or molecule by the absorption of a photon.  This is a crucial process in various fields, including astrophysics and laser physics.| Example: Photodetachment of negative ions is an important process in the Earth's ionosphere. 光电离负离子是地球电离层中的一个重要过程。|近义词：photoionization (though photoionization usually refers to positive ions)|反义词：photoattachment |名词
