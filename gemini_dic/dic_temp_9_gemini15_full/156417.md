phytolite:/ˈfaɪtoʊlaɪt/| n. |植物硅酸体|Plant opal; microscopic silica structures found in plants.  Phytolites are durable remnants of plants that can survive long after the plant itself has decayed, providing valuable information about past vegetation. |The researchers analyzed the phytolites found in the sediment to determine the type of plants that grew in the area thousands of years ago.  研究人员分析了沉积物中发现的植物硅酸体，以确定几千年前该地区生长的植物类型。|fossil, microfossil |(无明确反义词，可考虑“organic remains”作为对比)|名词
