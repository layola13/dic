gadsman: /ˈɡædzmən/ | n. | 佃农，农场工人 | A gadsman is a farm laborer, especially one who travels from place to place seeking seasonal work.  The term is now largely archaic. | Example: In the old days, gadsmen would follow the harvests, moving from farm to farm.  = 过去，佃农们会跟着收割季节走，从一个农场搬到另一个农场。  | 近义词: farmhand, farm laborer, agricultural worker | 反义词: landowner, farmer (employer) |  名词
