photostating:/ˈfoʊtoʊˌsteɪtɪŋ/| v.|影印，复印|The act of making photographic copies, especially of documents.|例句：I need to photostate these documents before the meeting. 我需要在会议前把这些文件复印一下。
例句：She photostated the contract and gave me a copy. 她把合同复印了一份给我。|近义词：copying, xeroxing|反义词：None.
