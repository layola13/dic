eyes:/aɪz/| n.|眼睛|The organs of sight.  They are used to see.|例句：Her eyes are blue.  她的眼睛是蓝色的。|例句：He looked into her eyes and saw his reflection. 他看着她的眼睛，看到了自己的倒影。|近义词：optic, vision|反义词:blindness
