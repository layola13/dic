came:/keɪm/| v.|来，到达|Past tense of "come."  It indicates the past action of arriving or moving to a place.|例句：The bus came late.  公共汽车来晚了。
例句：She came to the party unexpectedly. 她意外地来到了派对。|近义词：arrived, appeared, showed up|反义词：left, departed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I come to school every day. 我每天都来学校。
He comes home late every night. 他每天晚上都回家很晚。

一般过去时:
I came to this city last year. 我去年来到这座城市。
They came to help us yesterday. 他们昨天来帮助我们。

现在完成时:
I have come to realize the truth. 我已经意识到真相了。
She has come a long way in her career. 她在事业上取得了长足的进步。

过去完成时:
I had come to a decision before he called.  在他打电话之前，我已经做出了决定。
She had come to understand his feelings by then. 到那时她已经理解了他的感受。

将来时:
I will come to see you tomorrow. 我明天来看你。
He will come to the meeting if he is available.如果他方便的话，他会来参加会议。


虚拟语气:
If she came earlier, she would have seen him. 如果她早点来，她就能见到他了。
I wish he had come to the party. 我希望他来参加了派对。


被动语态:
(The verb "come" is rarely used in the passive voice, as it is usually intransitive.  However, it can be used in some idiomatic expressions.)
The news came unexpectedly.  这个消息出乎意料地传来了。(被动含义，但结构并非标准被动)


疑问句:
When did he come? 他什么时候来的？
Did she come to the meeting? 她来参加会议了吗？


否定句:
He didn't come to the party. 他没有来参加聚会。
She did not come home until late. 她直到很晚才回家。


PHRASE->
come across: 偶然遇见
come along:  一起走，进展
come back: 回来
come out: 出版，公开
come up with: 想出 (主意、方案等)
