gymnasiums:/dʒɪmˈneɪziəmz/ | n. | 体育馆；健身房 | A gymnasium is a large room or building that is used for indoor sports and physical exercise.  It often contains equipment such as exercise machines, weights, and mats. |  例句：Our school gymnasium is large enough to host basketball games.  我们的学校体育馆足够大，可以举办篮球比赛。


例句：Many people use the local gymnasium to stay fit. 许多人使用当地的体育馆来保持健康。


近义词：gym, sports hall, fitness center

词源:
The word "gymnasium" comes from the Ancient Greek word "gymnasium" (γυμνάσιον), which itself is derived from "gymnos" (γυμνός), meaning "naked."  In ancient Greece, athletes often exercised nude in gymnasiums.  The word entered English via Latin.
