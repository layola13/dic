quarrelsomeness:/ˈkwɒrəlˌsəmnɪs/| n. |好争吵的特性；爱争吵；好斗；争吵的倾向|The state or quality of being quarrelsome; a tendency to quarrel.  It refers to the disposition or habit of engaging in arguments or disputes.|Their constant quarrelsomeness made it difficult to live with them.|近义词：contentiousness, disputatiousness, litigiousness|反义词：peacefulness, amiability, agreeableness

In a sentence showing cause and effect:
