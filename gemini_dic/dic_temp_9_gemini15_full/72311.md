flourishes:/ˈflʌrɪʃɪz/| v. n.|繁荣，茂盛；挥舞；炫耀 |To grow or develop successfully; to wave or make a sweeping gesture; to show off.|例句：The business is flourishing. 这家公司生意兴隆。  The artist flourished his brush.艺术家挥舞着他的画笔。|近义词：thrive, prosper, flourish, wave, display|反义词：decline, wither, fail


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The business flourishes. = 这家公司生意兴隆。
The plants flourish in the sun. = 植物在阳光下茁壮成长。

一般过去时:
The company flourished in the 1990s. = 这家公司在20世纪90年代蓬勃发展。
He flourished his sword. = 他挥舞着他的剑。

现在完成时:
The town has flourished since the new highway opened. = 自从新的高速公路开通以来，这个城镇蓬勃发展。
She has flourished her academic achievements. = 她炫耀她的学术成就。

过去完成时:
The empire had flourished before the invasion. = 在入侵之前，帝国曾繁荣昌盛。
He had flourished his weapon before the fight. = 在战斗开始前，他曾挥舞过他的武器。

将来时:
The economy will flourish if the government implements these policies. = 如果政府实施这些政策，经济将会繁荣。
She will flourish her new painting at the exhibition. = 她将在展览会上炫耀她的新画作。

虚拟语气:
If the conditions were better, the plants would flourish. = 如果条件更好，植物就会茂盛生长。
If I had more money, I would flourish my lifestyle. = 如果我有更多钱，我会过得更奢华。

被动语态:
The business is being flourished by the new manager. = 这家公司正在被新经理搞得欣欣向荣。(此用法较少见，通常用主动语态)
His talent was flourished through hard work.  = 他的天赋通过努力得到了充分展现。（更自然的表达）


疑问句:
Does the business flourish? = 这家公司生意兴隆吗？
Will your garden flourish this year? = 你的花园今年会茂盛吗？

否定句:
The plants didn't flourish in the shade. = 植物在阴凉处没有茂盛生长。
The economy isn't flourishing at the moment. = 目前经济并不景气。


PHRASE->
flourish and thrive = 蓬勃发展
flourish in = 在...中兴旺
flourish under = 在...的领导下兴旺
a flourishing business = 一家兴隆的企业
flourish one's talents = 充分发挥自己的才能
