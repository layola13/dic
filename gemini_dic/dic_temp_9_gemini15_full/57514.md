dragonism: /ˈdræɡənɪzəm/ | n. | 龙崇拜；对龙的迷信；以龙为主题的文化现象 | The worship of, or belief in, dragons, often incorporating elements of mythology, folklore, and symbolism.  It encompasses the cultural expressions, artistic representations, and societal impact related to dragons across various cultures. | Example:  The prevalence of dragon imagery in East Asian art demonstrates a long history of dragonism.  东亚艺术中龙的形象普遍存在，这表明龙崇拜有着悠久的历史。| 近义词: draconolatry (more formal and religious connotation) | 反义词:  None (antonyms would depend on the specific aspect of dragonism being considered) |  n.
