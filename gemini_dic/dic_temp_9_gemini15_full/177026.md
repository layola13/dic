reanchor:/ˌriˈæŋkər/| vt.|重新固定；重新锚定 |To secure or fasten something again, especially after it has become loose or detached.|例句：The boat needs to be reanchored in the deeper water.  船需要在更深的水域重新抛锚。 The surgeon had to reanchor the detached retina. 外科医生不得不重新固定脱落的视网膜。|近义词：refasten, resecure, reset|反义词：unanchor, detach
