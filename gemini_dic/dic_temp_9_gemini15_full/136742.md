nonsensory:/ˈnɑːnsɛnsəri/| adj.|非感觉的，非感觉器官的|Not involving or perceived by the senses.  It refers to things that are not related to or experienced through the five senses (sight, hearing, smell, taste, and touch).|Example: The nonsensory effects of the drug were significant, impacting cognitive functions.  这种药物的非感觉影响是显著的，影响了认知功能。|近义词：non-sensory, abstract, intellectual|反义词：sensory, perceptible, tangible
