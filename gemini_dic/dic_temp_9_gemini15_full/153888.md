percolation:/ˌpɜːrkəˈleɪʃən/ | n. |渗透；渗滤；过滤；慢慢扩散 | The process of a liquid slowly passing through a filter.  It also refers to the gradual spread of something, like an idea or feeling. |Example: The slow percolation of water through the coffee grounds produced a rich brew.  咖啡粉中水慢慢渗透，产生浓郁的咖啡。


The percolation of news through the town was surprisingly quick. 新闻在镇上传播得惊人地快。| infiltration, seepage, diffusion | stagnation, blockage |  n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The water percolates through the soil. = 水渗透到土壤中。
Coffee percolates slowly in the pot. = 咖啡在壶中慢慢渗透。


一般过去时:
The liquid percolated through the filter. = 液体通过过滤器渗透。
The rumor percolated throughout the office. = 谣言在整个办公室里流传。


现在完成时:
The rain has percolated deep into the ground. = 雨水已经渗透到地底深处。
The idea has percolated through many minds. = 这个想法已经在许多人的脑海中传播开来。


过去完成时:
The coffee had percolated before we arrived. = 我们到达之前咖啡已经过滤好了。
The news had percolated to the public before the official announcement. = 在官方宣布之前，新闻已经传到了公众那里。


将来时:
The water will percolate through the gravel. = 水将渗透到砾石中。
The message will percolate throughout the organization. = 这条信息将传遍整个组织。


虚拟语气:
If the water percolated faster, we would have more coffee. = 如果水渗透得更快，我们就会有更多的咖啡。
If the idea had percolated more widely, the project would have succeeded. = 如果这个想法更广泛地传播，这个项目就会成功。


被动语态:
The coffee was percolated for two hours. = 咖啡过滤了两个小时。
The information was percolated to the employees. = 信息被传达给了员工。


疑问句:
Does the water percolate easily through this soil? = 水能轻易地渗透到这片土壤中吗？
Will the new policy percolate through the company effectively? = 新政策能否有效地贯彻到公司？


否定句:
The water didn't percolate through the clay. = 水没有渗透到黏土中。
The message didn't percolate to all the staff. = 这条信息没有传达给所有员工。
