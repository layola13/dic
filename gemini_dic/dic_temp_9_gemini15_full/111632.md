localizations:/ˌloʊkəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |本地化；本地化版本 |The process of adapting a product or content to a specific local market, taking into account cultural, linguistic, and technical differences.  This includes translation, cultural adaptation, and sometimes technical adjustments. |Example: The game's localizations for the Japanese market included new voice acting and culturally relevant content.  游戏的日语本地化版本包括新的配音和与当地文化相关的內容。|近义词：adaptation, translation, internationalization |反义词：globalization
