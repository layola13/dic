preauthorizations:/ˌpriːˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |预授权；预先批准|Plural form of preauthorization.  It refers to the prior approval obtained for a service, treatment, or procedure before it is performed, usually to determine if it is covered by insurance. |Example: The patient needs preauthorizations from their insurance company before undergoing the expensive surgery.  患者在进行昂贵的手术前需要获得保险公司的预授权。|近义词：prior approval, advance approval, prior authorization|反义词：post-authorization

The hospital requires preauthorizations for all elective procedures. = 医院要求所有选择性手术都必须预先授权。
Many insurance plans necessitate preauthorizations for specialized medications. = 许多保险计划都需要为特种药物进行预授权。
