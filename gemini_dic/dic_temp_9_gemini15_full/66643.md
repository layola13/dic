exister:/ɛɡˈzɪstər/ | v. | 存在；生存 | To continue to live or exist.  |The ancient civilization no longer exists.  这个古老的文明不再存在。|  survive, endure | cease to exist, perish | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He exists. = 他存在。
The problem still exists. = 问题依然存在。

一般过去时:
The dinosaurs existed millions of years ago. = 恐龙在数百万年前存在。
The kingdom no longer existed. = 王国不再存在了。

现在完成时:
The company has existed for over a century. = 这家公司已经存在了一个多世纪。
This tradition has existed for generations. = 这个传统已经延续了好几代。

过去完成时:
The evidence suggested that the culture had existed long before the Roman Empire. = 证据表明这种文化早在罗马帝国之前就存在了。
Before the invention of the printing press, the book had existed only in manuscript form. = 在印刷术发明之前，书籍只以手稿形式存在。


将来时:
The project will exist for at least another five years. = 这个项目至少还会存在五年。
This species may not exist in the future. = 这个物种将来可能不存在。

虚拟语气:
If only he still existed! = 要是他还活着就好了！
I wish that peace existed everywhere. = 我希望和平存在于世界各地。

被动语态:
(The verb "exist" is rarely used in the passive voice because it is intransitive.)  (动词“exist”很少用于被动语态，因为它是不及物动词。)


疑问句:
Does life exist on other planets? = 其他星球上存在生命吗？
Does this opportunity still exist? = 这个机会还存在吗？


否定句:
The myth doesn't exist. = 这个神话不存在。
Such a thing does not exist. = 这样的事情不存在。


PHRASE->
in existence: 现存的
to bring into existence: 使...产生；使...存在
out of existence: 不存在
to cease to exist: 不复存在
come into existence: 产生；出现
