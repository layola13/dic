pronoun:/ˈproʊnaʊn/|pron./代词|A pronoun is a word that substitutes for a noun or noun phrase.  Pronouns are used to avoid repetition and make sentences more concise and natural. They can refer to people, places, things, or ideas.|例句：He went to the store. (He replaces the name of a person)|近义词：substitute, replacement|反义词：noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a doctor. = 他是一名医生。
She works in a bank. = 她在一个银行工作。


一般过去时:
They went to the movies last night. = 他们昨晚去看电影了。
I ate pizza for dinner. = 我晚饭吃了披萨。


现在完成时:
We have finished the project. = 我们已经完成了项目。
She has lived in London for five years. = 她已经在伦敦住了五年了。


过去完成时:
They had already left when I arrived. = 我到达时他们已经离开了。
I had never seen anything like it before. = 我以前从未见过这样的东西。


将来时:
I will call you tomorrow. = 我明天会给你打电话。
They will be here soon. = 他们很快就会到。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would apologize. = 如果我是你，我会道歉。
I wish I had more time. = 我希望我有更多时间。


被动语态:
The book was written by a famous author. = 这本书是一位著名作家写的。
The car is being repaired. = 汽车正在修理中。


疑问句:
Who is that? = 那是谁？
What did you say? = 你说什么？


否定句:
I am not going to the party. = 我不去参加聚会。
They didn't see the accident. = 他们没有看到事故。
