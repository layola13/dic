dramatised:/ˈdræmətaɪzd/ | v. |戏剧化；使戏剧性；改编成戏剧 |To represent or portray (something) in a dramatic manner; to adapt (a story, etc.) for dramatic presentation. |例句：The news report dramatised the events of the hostage crisis. 新闻报道戏剧化地描述了人质危机的事件。 |例句：The novel was dramatised for television. 这部小说被改编成了电视剧。 |近义词：exaggerate, sensationalize, stage |反义词：understate, downplay


USAGE->

He dramatizes every little problem. = 他把每一个小问题都戏剧化了。
She dramatizes her life experiences in her writing. = 她在写作中戏剧化地展现了她的人生经历。
