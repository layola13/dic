fumigating:/ˈfjuːmɪɡeɪtɪŋ/| v.|熏蒸；消毒|The act of applying gaseous pesticides to kill insects or other pests.  It involves the use of fumigants, which are chemicals that turn into gases. Fumigation is often used in large-scale pest control, such as in warehouses, ships, or buildings.|例句：The farmer is fumigating his fields to control pests.  这个农民正在给他的田地进行熏蒸以控制害虫。
They are fumigating the entire building to eliminate bed bugs. 他们正在对整栋建筑物进行熏蒸以消灭臭虫。|近义词： disinfect, sterilize |反义词：contaminate
