pre-position:/ˌpriːpəˈzɪʃən/ | n. |介词 |A word governing, and usually preceding, a noun or pronoun and expressing a relation to another word or element in the clause.  介词是一个词，它支配并通常位于名词或代词之前，表达与从句中另一个词或成分的关系。|例句：The book is on the table. (书在桌子上。) The cat jumped over the fence. (猫跳过了栅栏。) |近义词：adverbial phrase (某些情况下)|反义词：N/A|词性：n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The book is on the table. = 书在桌子上。
The cat sits under the tree. = 猫坐在树下。

一般过去时:
The car was in the garage. = 车在车库里。
The bird flew over the house. = 鸟飞过了房子。

现在完成时:
The package has been in the mail for a week. = 包裹已经寄了一个星期了。
She has lived in London since 2010. = 她自从2010年就住在伦敦了。

过去完成时:
The work had been completed before the deadline. = 工作在截止日期前已经完成了。
They had already left before I arrived. = 我到达之前他们已经离开了。

将来时:
The meeting will be in the conference room. = 会议将在会议室举行。
He will arrive after lunch. = 他将在午餐后到达。

虚拟语气:
If I were in your position, I would act differently. = 如果我是你，我会采取不同的行动。
It's time we were going home. = 我们该回家了。

被动语态:
The house was built on a hill. = 这房子建在山上。
The prize was awarded to the best student. = 奖品颁给了最好的学生。

疑问句:
What is your opinion on this matter? = 你对这件事有什么看法？
Where are you going? = 你要去哪里？

否定句:
The dog isn't in the garden. = 狗不在花园里。
He didn't go to the party. = 他没有去参加聚会。


PHRASE->
in addition to = 除…之外
on behalf of = 代表
in accordance with = 依照
with regard to = 关于
by means of = 通过
