preinspected:/ˌpriːɪnˈspɛktɪd/| adj.| 预先检查过的；事先检验过的 |  Having been inspected beforehand.  This term implies a thorough inspection before a certain event or process. |例句：The house was preinspected before we closed on the sale.  = 这栋房子在我们完成交易之前已经过预先检查。

The car underwent a preinspection to ensure its safety. = 这辆车经过了预先检查以确保其安全。


近义词：inspected beforehand, pre-examined, previously checked


反义词：uninspected, unchecked


词性：形容词
