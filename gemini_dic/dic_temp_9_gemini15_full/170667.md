pseudo-roman:/ˈsuːdoʊroʊmæn/| adj.|伪罗马的，似罗马的|Apparently Roman, but not genuinely so; resembling Roman style or characteristics but not authentically Roman.  The term often describes styles, architecture, or other cultural elements that imitate Roman forms but lack the historical or cultural authenticity of genuine Roman artifacts.|例句：The building's pseudo-Roman columns were made of concrete, not marble. 这座建筑物的伪罗马式柱子是用混凝土而不是大理石制成的。|近义词：pseudoclassical, imitation, mock|反义词：authentic, genuine, true
