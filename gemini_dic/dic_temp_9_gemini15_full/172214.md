push-up:/ˈpʊʃʌp/| n. vt.|俯卧撑；向上推；|A push-up is a common calisthenics exercise performed in a prone position by raising and lowering the body using the arms.|例句：He did twenty push-ups this morning. 他今天早上做了20个俯卧撑。|例句：The sales team needs a push-up to meet their targets. 销售团队需要努力一把才能达到目标。|近义词：press-up, uplift|反义词：push-down
