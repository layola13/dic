词源:

The word "nonprecious" is a combination of the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "precious" (meaning "of great value; highly valued").  "Precious" itself has roots in the Old French word "precieux," ultimately deriving from the Latin "pretiosus," meaning "valuable" or "costly."
