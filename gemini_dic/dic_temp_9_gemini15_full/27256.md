calorimetric:/ˌkæləriˈmɛtrɪk/| adj.|量热法的；测热法的|Relating to or involving calorimetry, the science or process of measuring the heat involved in a chemical reaction or other physical process.|The experiment used a calorimetric method to determine the heat of combustion.|近义词：thermometric|反义词：None


USAGE->
This word is an adjective and doesn't have verb conjugations like "what".  It describes a process or method.  Therefore, typical verb tenses are not applicable.  Instead, examples will show how it modifies nouns.


Example Sentences:

Calorimetric analysis was used to determine the reaction's enthalpy. = 使用量热分析法测定反应的焓值。

The calorimetric data revealed a significant heat release during the reaction. = 量热数据显示反应过程中有大量的热量释放。
