glair:/ɡleər/| n.| 蛋清；光泽；粘液 |The clear viscous part of an egg white; a glossy or shiny appearance; a slimy substance.|例句：The recipe calls for egg glair to bind the ingredients. 这个食谱需要用蛋清来粘合配料。
例句：The glair on the surface of the varnish gave it a beautiful sheen.  清漆表面的光泽使它呈现出美丽的色泽。


近义词： albumen, white of egg, sheen, gloss | 反义词： dullness, matte


词源:  From Old French *glaire, from Latin *clarus ("clear"). The meaning shifted from "clear liquid" to specifically "egg white" over time.
