non-veteran:/ˌnɒnˈvɛtərən/| adj. |非退伍军人，非老兵|A person who has not served in the military.  This term refers to individuals who lack experience in a particular field, often in comparison to veterans.  It can be used in a general sense or in specific contexts, such as referring to non-veteran employees in a company.|例句：The company is trying to attract more non-veteran applicants.|近义词：inexperienced, novice, newcomer|反义词：veteran, experienced
