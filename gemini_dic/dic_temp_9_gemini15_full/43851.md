crescence:/krɛˈsɛns/| n. |增长；增大；新月|The gradual increase in size or amount.  It often refers to a gradual growth or increase, particularly of something that is naturally occurring, like the moon's phases or a plant's growth. |例句：The crescence of the moon was visible to the naked eye. 月亮的增长用肉眼可见。

The crescence of the population in the city is putting a strain on resources.  城市人口的增长给资源带来了压力。|近义词：growth, increase, augmentation |反义词：decrease, diminution, decline |名词
