hough:/haʊ/| n. vt.| 踝；膝关节；砍断（动物的）腿筋 |The hough is the joint between the leg and the foot in animals, especially four-legged animals.  To hough an animal means to hamstring it by cutting the tendons at the hough. |The hunter houghed the deer, crippling it.  猎人砍断了鹿的腿筋，使它残废。| hamstring, disable | enable, heal | n., vt.
