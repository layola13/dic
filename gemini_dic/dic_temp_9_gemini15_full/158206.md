platters:/ˈplætərz/| n. |大浅盘，大盘子 |A platter is a large, flat dish, typically used for serving food.  It often has a wide, shallow shape, making it ideal for presenting a variety of items.|例句：She served the appetizers on a large silver platter. 她用一个大银盘盛放开胃菜。|例句：The buffet table was laden with platters of food.  自助餐桌上摆满了装满食物的大盘子。|近义词：tray, dish, serving plate|反义词：small dish, bowl
