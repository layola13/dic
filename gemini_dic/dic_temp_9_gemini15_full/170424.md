proxima:/ˈprɒksɪmə/| n. |比邻星，最近的|The closest star to the Sun, a red dwarf in the Alpha Centauri system.  Proxima Centauri is known for its orbiting exoplanet, Proxima b.|例句：Proxima Centauri is the closest star to our sun. 比邻星是离我们太阳最近的恒星。|例句：Scientists are studying Proxima Centauri b to see if it could support life. 科学家们正在研究比邻星b，看看它是否能够支持生命。|近义词：nearby star, nearest star |反义词：distant star, far star|名词
