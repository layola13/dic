roundel:/ˈraʊndəl/| n.|圆形装饰物；环形物；（诗歌体裁）轮韵诗|A roundel is a small, typically circular decorative object, often made of metal or other material.  In poetry, it refers to a specific verse form.|例句：The stained-glass window featured a beautiful roundel depicting a scene from the Bible. 彩绘玻璃窗上有一个美丽的圆形装饰物，描绘了圣经中的一个场景。|The artist used a series of intricately designed roundels to decorate the altar. 艺术家用一系列精心设计的圆形装饰物来装饰祭坛。|近义词：medallion, disc, circle|反义词:rectangle, square
