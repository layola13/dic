procidentia:/prɒsɪˈdɛntiə/ | n. | 器官脱垂；(尤指)子宫脱垂 |  Prolapse of an organ, especially the uterus. It refers to the downward displacement of an organ from its normal anatomical position.  | Example:  She suffered from uterine procidentia after childbirth. 她分娩后患有子宫脱垂。 |近义词：prolapse, descent,  lapse | 反义词：retention,  staying in place | 名词
