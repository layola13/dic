resounded:/rɪˈzaʊndɪd/| v.|回响，共鸣，大声地说|To make a loud, deep sound that continues for some time; to be filled with a particular sound.|The applause resounded through the hall.  掌声在礼堂回荡。

The singer's voice resounded with power and emotion. 歌手的歌声充满了力量和情感。|reverberate, echo, boom|silent, quiet|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The music resounds throughout the stadium. 音乐在整个体育场回荡。
His laughter resounds in the empty house. 他的笑声在空荡荡的房子里回荡。

一般过去时:
The news resounded across the nation.  这个消息传遍了全国。
Her words resounded in my ears.  她的言语在我耳边回响。

现在完成时:
The impact of his speech has resounded for years. 他演讲的影响已经持续多年。
Her achievements have resounded throughout the industry. 她的成就已在整个行业引起轰动。

过去完成时:
The victory had resounded throughout the kingdom before the king even knew. 国王甚至还没知道的时候，胜利的消息就已经传遍了整个王国。
The news of the disaster had resounded in every corner of the world. 灾难的消息已传遍世界各地。

将来时:
His name will resound throughout history. 他的名字将名垂青史。
The impact of this decision will resound for generations.  这项决定的影响将会持续几代人。


虚拟语气:
If only my voice could resound across the ocean.  但愿我的声音能传遍大海。
I wish my message could resound more clearly to all. 我希望我的信息能更清晰地传达给所有人。


被动语态:
His name was resounded throughout the land. 他的名字传遍了全国。
The victory was resounded by the entire city.  整个城市都在庆祝胜利。


疑问句:
Does his name resound in history? 他的名字在历史上响亮吗？
Will this discovery resound in the scientific community? 这一发现会在科学界引起轰动吗？


否定句:
His voice did not resound in the large hall. 他的声音在大的礼堂里听不见。
The message did not resound with the audience. 这条信息没有引起听众的共鸣。
