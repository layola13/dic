outside:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪd/ | adverb, preposition, noun | 外面，外部 |  Outside, the sun is shining.  外面阳光灿烂。 | 近义词：exterior, outwardly | 反义词：inside, interior


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He plays outside every afternoon. = 他每天下午都在外面玩。
The birds sing outside my window. = 鸟儿在我的窗外唱歌。

一般过去时:
We sat outside yesterday. = 我们昨天坐在外面。
They played outside all day long. = 他们整天都在外面玩。

现在完成时:
We have been outside for hours. = 我们在外面待了几个小时了。
They have never been outside this country. = 他们从未去过这个国家以外的地方。


过去完成时:
I had already been outside when it started to rain. = 下雨的时候我已经在外面了。
They had played outside before dinner. = 他们晚饭前在外面玩过。


将来时:
We will be outside tomorrow. = 我们明天将在外面。
I will go outside later. = 我过会儿会出去。


虚拟语气:
If it weren't raining, we would be outside. = 如果不下雨，我们就会在外面。
I wish I were outside right now. = 我真希望我现在在外面。


被动语态:
The trash was left outside. = 垃圾被扔在了外面。
The meeting was held outside. = 会议在户外举行。


疑问句:
Is it cold outside? = 外面冷吗？
What's going on outside? = 外面发生了什么事？


否定句:
It's not safe to play outside at night. = 夜晚在外面玩不安全。
I didn't go outside today. = 我今天没有出去。
