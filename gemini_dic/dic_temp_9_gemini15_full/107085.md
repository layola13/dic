laden:/ˈleɪdn/ | adj. | 装满的；负重的；沉重的 | heavily loaded or burdened; weighed down.  |例句：The ship was laden with cargo. 这艘船装满了货物。 |例句：He was laden with guilt. 他负罪感很重。|近义词：burdened, loaded, weighed down |反义词：empty, light, unburdened |形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The truck is laden with fruit. = 这辆卡车装满了水果。
The tree is laden with apples. = 这棵树上挂满了苹果。

一般过去时:
The donkey was laden with sacks of grain. = 驴子驮着几袋谷物。
The shelves were laden with books. = 架子上摆满了书。

现在完成时:
The ship has been laden with containers. = 这艘船已经装满了集装箱。
The table has been laden with delicious food. = 桌子上摆满了美味佳肴。

过去完成时:
The wagon had been laden with hay before the journey. = 旅行前，马车已经装满了干草。
The cart had been laden with goods before it broke down. = 手推车在坏掉之前已经装满了货物。

将来时:
The plane will be laden with passengers. = 飞机将载满乘客。
The truck will be laden with supplies tomorrow. = 卡车明天将装满物资。

虚拟语气:
If the ship were laden with more cargo, it would sink. = 如果船装载更多的货物，它就会沉没。
If the shelves were laden with books, it would be a great library. = 如果架子上摆满了书，那将是一个很棒的图书馆。

被动语态:
The wagon was laden with stones. = 货车装满了石头。
The table was laden with food and drink. = 桌子上摆满了食物和饮料。

疑问句:
Is the truck laden with heavy machinery? = 卡车装载的是重型机械吗？
Is the branch laden with ripe mangoes? = 树枝上挂满了成熟的芒果吗？

否定句:
The shelves weren't laden with books. = 架子上没有摆满书。
The truck wasn't laden with anything. = 卡车什么也没装。


PHRASE->
laden with: 装满…；负重…
heavily laden: 重载的
laden down: 压得喘不过气；负重累累
laden with guilt: 负罪感很重
laden with sorrow: 悲伤欲绝
