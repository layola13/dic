feasibilities:/ˌfiːzəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 可行性(复数) | The practical possibility of something |  例句：The report assessed the feasibilities of different approaches to the problem.  这份报告评估了不同方法解决这个问题的可行性。
例句：We need to carefully consider the economic feasibilities before starting the project.  在开始项目之前，我们需要仔细考虑经济上的可行性。


近义词：viabilities, possibilities, practicabilities | 反义词：impossibilities, impracticabilities | 词性:名词
