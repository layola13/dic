semi-industrially:/ˌsɛmiɪnˈdʌstriəli/| adj.|半工业化地，半工业规模地 |  Describes a process, production, or system that is partially industrialized, falling between fully handcrafted methods and fully automated mass production. It often implies a blend of manual labor and machinery, or a smaller scale of industrial operations. | Example: The furniture was semi-industrially produced, combining traditional craftsmanship with some automated processes.  这件家具是半工业化生产的，结合了传统的工艺和一些自动化流程。|近义词：partially industrialized,  moderately industrialized|反义词：fully industrialized, handcrafted, artisanal|词性：形容词
