pouncet:/ˈpaʊnsɪt/| v.|猛扑，突然袭击|To spring or swoop down suddenly upon something; to pounce.|例句：The cat pounced on the mouse. 猫猛扑向老鼠。|例句：The hawk pounced from the sky. 鹰从空中猛扑下来。|近义词：spring, swoop, lunge, leap|反义词：withdraw, retreat


USAGE->
