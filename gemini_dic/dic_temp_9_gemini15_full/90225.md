hourless:/ˈaʊərləs/| adj.|无小时的；无时间限制的；持续不断的|Without hours; unending; continuous.  The adjective "hourless" describes something that lacks a specific time limit or is continuous and without interruption. It implies a lack of structure or defined working hours.|例句：The factory operates on an hourless schedule, with workers rotating shifts around the clock. = 这家工厂实行无时间限制的轮班制，工人昼夜轮换工作。

例句：He dreamt of an hourless vacation, where time simply ceased to matter. = 他梦想着一个没有时间限制的假期，时间不再重要。


近义词： ceaseless, continuous, unending, perpetual, interminable

反义词： timed, limited, scheduled, regulated


词性：形容词
