polyvoltine:/ˌpɒliˈvɒltaɪn/| adj.|多代繁殖的（指昆虫）|Producing more than one brood in a year;  referring to insects with multiple generations per year. This term is primarily used in entomology (the study of insects).|例句：The silkworm is a polyvoltine insect, producing multiple generations annually.  桑蚕是一种多代繁殖的昆虫，每年产多代。

例句：Many species of aphids are polyvoltine, rapidly increasing their population in favorable conditions. 许多蚜虫种类是多代繁殖的，在有利条件下迅速增加种群数量。


近义词：multivoltine(虽然两者含义基本相同，但polyvoltine更常用)|反义词：univoltine (单代繁殖的)


词性：形容词
