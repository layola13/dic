cabled:/ˈkeɪbld/ | adj. v. | 用电缆传送的；用电缆连接的；发送电报 | Sent by cable; wired;  to send a message by cable. |例句：The news was cabled to London.  这条新闻是用电缆发往伦敦的。  The two computers were cabled together. 这两台电脑是用电缆连接起来的。| 近义词：wired, telegraphed, transmitted | 反义词：wireless, unwired


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cables his mother every week. = 他每周都给他母亲发电报。
They cable the data to the central server. = 他们通过电缆将数据传输到中央服务器。

一般过去时:
She cabled the news to her family. = 她给她家人发了电报。
