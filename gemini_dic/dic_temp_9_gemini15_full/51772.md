devotionalist: /dɪˈvoʊʃənəlɪst/ | n. |虔诚者，奉献者 | A person who is deeply devoted to a religion or cause; someone who practices or expresses devotion.  | Example:  She is a devotionalist who spends hours each day in prayer. 她是一位虔诚者，每天花数小时祈祷。  |近义词：devotee, enthusiast, adherent | 反义词：apostate, heretic
