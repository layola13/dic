shootout:/ˈʃuːtˌaʊt/ | n. |枪战，决斗 | A shootout is a gunfight, typically between law enforcement and criminals, or between rival gangs.  It often involves rapid and sustained gunfire. | 例句：The police engaged in a shootout with the armed robbers.  警察与持械抢劫犯发生了枪战。

The shootout lasted for over an hour, leaving several people injured. 这场枪战持续了一个多小时，造成多人受伤。 | 近义词：gunfight, firefight, exchange of fire | 反义词：peace, negotiation
