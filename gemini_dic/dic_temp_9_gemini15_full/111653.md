locatives:/ˈloʊkətivz/ | n. |方位词，表示地点的词 |Words or phrases indicating location or position.  Locatives are a grammatical category that specifies the location of an action or entity. They can be adverbs, prepositions, or other grammatical elements that express where something is or where something happens.  |例句： "Here" and "there" are common locatives.  此处和彼处是常见的方位词。

The book is on the table. (on is a locative preposition) 书在桌子上。(on 是一个方位介词)


近义词：place indicators, location words, spatial terms |反义词：N/A |词性：n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bird is in the tree. = 鸟在树上。
The shops are near the park. = 商店在公园附近。


一般过去时:
The cat was under the bed. = 猫在床底下。
They were at the cinema. = 他们当时在电影院。


现在完成时:
The car has been in the garage. = 车一直在车库里。
She has lived in London for ten years. = 她已经在伦敦住了十年。


过去完成时:
The dog had been in the garden. = 狗一直在花园里。
They had walked to the library. = 他们已经走到图书馆了。


将来时:
The meeting will be in room 101. = 会议将在101房间举行。
We will be at home tomorrow. = 我们明天会在家里。


虚拟语气:
If the party were in the park, it would be more fun. = 如果派对在公园里举行，会更有趣。
I wish the restaurant were closer to home. = 我希望餐馆离家更近些。


被动语态:
The ball was found near the river. = 球在河边被发现。
The books are kept in the library. = 书被放在图书馆里。


疑问句:
Where is the key? = 钥匙在哪里？
Where are my glasses? = 我的眼镜在哪里？


否定句:
The dog is not in the house. = 狗不在屋里。
The meeting wasn't at the office. = 会议不在办公室举行。


PHRASE->
Here and there = 到处
Over there = 在那边
In the vicinity = 在附近
All around = 周围
Far and wide = 远近
