reboarded:/ˌriːˈbɔːrdɪd/ | v. |重新登机；再次上船 |To board (a plane, ship, etc.) again after having disembarked.  This implies a previous boarding event. |例句：After a delay, the passengers reboarded the plane. = 延误之后，乘客重新登上了飞机。
例句：The crew reboarded the ship after inspecting the damage. = 船员检查完损坏后又重新登上了船。 |近义词：re-embark, re-enter |反义词：disembark, leave


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reboards the plane every time there is a delay. = 每当延误，他都会重新登机。
They reboard the train after a short break. = 他们在短暂休息后重新登上火车。


一般过去时:
She reboarded the flight after collecting her luggage. = 她取完行李后重新登上了航班。
The tourists reboarded the bus after visiting the museum. = 游客们参观完博物馆后重新登上了巴士。


现在完成时:
He has reboarded the ship three times today due to technical issues. = 今天由于技术问题，他已经三次重新登上船了。
They have reboarded the plane several times because of the bad weather. = 由于天气恶劣，他们已经多次重新登机了。


过去完成时:
They had reboarded the train before the conductor announced the departure. = 列车员宣布出发前，他们已经重新登上了火车。
The passengers had reboarded the plane before the announcement of the second delay. = 在宣布第二次延误之前，乘客们已经重新登上了飞机。


将来时:
They will reboard the plane after the storm passes. = 暴风雨过后，他们将重新登机。
He will reboard the ship once the repairs are complete. = 修理完成后，他将重新登船。


虚拟语气:
If the plane had not been delayed, they would not have had to reboard. = 如果飞机没有延误，他们就不必重新登机了。
If the ship were seaworthy, we would reboard immediately. = 如果船适航，我们将立即重新登船。


被动语态:
The passengers were reboarded after a thorough security check. = 乘客在经过彻底的安全检查后重新登机了。
The tourists were reboarded on the bus after receiving instructions. = 游客们在收到指示后重新上了巴士。


疑问句:
Will they reboard the plane soon? = 他们很快会重新登机吗？
Did the passengers reboard the train before the departure? = 乘客们在出发前重新登上了火车吗？


否定句:
The passengers did not reboard the plane after the emergency landing. = 乘客们在紧急着陆后没有重新登机。
He did not reboard the ship because the damage was too severe. = 他没有重新登船，因为损坏太严重了。
