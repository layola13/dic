faked:/feɪkt/| v. adj.|伪造的；假装的|To create something false or pretend; to imitate deceptively.  It can be used as a verb or an adjective describing something that is not genuine.|例句：He faked his signature on the check. 他伪造了支票上的签名。

The police discovered that the documents had been faked. 警方发现文件是伪造的。|近义词：counterfeited, forged, sham, simulated, pretended|反义词：genuine, authentic, real, true
