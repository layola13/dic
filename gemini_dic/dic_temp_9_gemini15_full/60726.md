/ɪˈlɛktrəʊslæɡ/ | n. | 电渣 |  Electro-slag refers to a metallurgical process that uses an electric arc to melt a slag bath, which is then used to refine and solidify metal.  The process is primarily used for producing large, high-quality castings of steel and other metals. | Example:  The electro-slag remelting (ESR) process significantly improved the quality of the titanium alloy.  电渣重熔（ESR）工艺显著提高了钛合金的质量。| 近义词：electroslag remelting (ESR), vacuum arc remelting (VAR) | 反义词：N/A |  名词
