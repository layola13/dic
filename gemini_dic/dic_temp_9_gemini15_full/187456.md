role:/roʊl/| /rəʊl/ | n. |角色；作用；任务 |The role of a teacher is to educate students. 教师的角色是教育学生。  He played a crucial role in the project. 他在这个项目中扮演了至关重要的角色。|近义词：part, function, position |反义词：inaction, inactivity |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What role does she play in the company? =她在公司扮演什么角色？
What role does education play in society? = 教育在社会中扮演什么角色？


一般过去时:
What role did he play in the war? = 他在战争中扮演什么角色？
What role did technology play in the development of the country? = 技术在国家发展中扮演了什么角色？


现在完成时:
What role has she played in the team's success? = 她对团队的成功起了什么作用？
What role has globalization played in the world economy? = 全球化对世界经济起了什么作用？


过去完成时:
What role had he played before he joined the company? = 他加入公司之前扮演过什么角色？
What role had social media played in the spread of information before the incident? = 事件发生前社交媒体在信息传播中扮演了什么角色？


将来时:
What role will AI play in the future? = 人工智能在未来将扮演什么角色？
What role will renewable energy play in solving the climate crisis? = 可再生能源将在解决气候危机方面扮演什么角色？


虚拟语气:
If I knew what role to play, I would be more confident. = 如果我知道该扮演什么角色，我会更有信心。
I wish I knew what role I should play in this situation. = 我希望我知道在这种情况下我应该扮演什么角色。


被动语态:
What role was assigned to him in the play? = 在这部戏中，他被分配了什么角色？
What role is being played by the government in this crisis? = 政府在这个危机中扮演着什么角色？


疑问句:
What role are you playing in this project? = 你在这个项目中扮演什么角色？
What role does the environment play in our lives? = 环境在我们生活中扮演什么角色？


否定句:
He didn't understand what role he was supposed to play. = 他不明白他应该扮演什么角色。
She didn't know what role to take in the discussion. = 她不知道在讨论中应该扮演什么角色。


PHRASE->
play a role = 扮演角色；发挥作用
take on a role = 承担一个角色
assume a role = 担任一个角色
fulfill a role = 履行职责
in the role of = 以……的身份
