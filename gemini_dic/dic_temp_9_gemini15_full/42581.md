counterplead:/ˌkaʊntəˈpliːd/ | vt. |反驳，抗辩 | To argue against or oppose (a plea or claim).  It involves presenting arguments or evidence that contradict or refute the original claim. |例句：The defendant's lawyer counterpleaded the prosecution's evidence of guilt.  被告的律师反驳了控方关于有罪的证据。

The plaintiff counterpleaded the defendant's motion to dismiss. 原告反驳了被告的驳回动议。|近义词：rebut, refute, contest, oppose |反义词：concede, admit, agree with |动词
