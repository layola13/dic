non-verbal:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːbəl/|adj.|非语言的，非言语的|Not involving or using words or speech.|例句：He gave me a non-verbal cue to leave. 他给了我一个非语言的暗示让我离开。|近义词：unspoken, implicit, nonverbal communication|反义词：verbal, spoken


non-verbal communication:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːbəl kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/|n.|非语言沟通|The communication of information through nonverbal cues such as gestures, facial expressions, and tone of voice.|例句：Nonverbal communication plays a crucial role in understanding others. 非语言沟通在理解他人方面起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：body language, unspoken communication|反义词：verbal communication
