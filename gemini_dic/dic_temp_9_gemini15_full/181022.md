rein's: /reɪnz/ | n. |缰绳(复数) | The reins of a horse or other animal are the straps that a rider holds to control it.  The word often figuratively refers to control or power. | 例句：He pulled on the reins to stop the horse. 他拉紧缰绳以阻止马。

The reins of government are in the hands of the new prime minister. 政府的统治权掌握在新任首相手中。 | 近义词：bridle, controls | 反义词：freedom, liberty
