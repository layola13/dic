dandriffy:/ˈdændrɪfi/| adj.|有头皮屑的，布满头皮屑的|Covered with or resembling dandruff.  The word suggests a significant and perhaps unsightly amount of dandruff.|例句：His hair was dandriffy and needed a good wash.  他的头发布满了头皮屑，需要好好洗一洗。

例句：The collar of his shirt was dandriffy from neglecting to wash his hair regularly. 他的衬衫领子因为经常不洗头而沾满了头皮屑。


近义词：scaly, flaky (referring to the dandruff itself), scurfy


反义词：clean (referring to the hair and scalp)


词性:形容词
