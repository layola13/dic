词源:
Derived from the adjective "deserted," meaning abandoned or empty.  The suffix "-ness" is added to create a noun, indicating a state or quality.  Ultimately, it traces back to the Old French word "deserter" (to abandon) and the Latin "desertus" (abandoned).
