novel:/ˈnɒvəl/ /ˈnɑːvəl/| n.|小说，长篇故事|A long fictional narrative, usually in prose, which describes fabricated events and characters.| 例句：She's writing a novel about the French Revolution. 她正在写一部关于法国大革命的小说。

例句：The novel explores themes of love, loss, and redemption. 这部小说探讨了爱、失去和救赎的主题。


近义词：story, tale, narrative| 反义词：nonfiction, factual account| 词性：名词
