quasi-ministerial: /ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəl/ | adj. |准部长的；类似部长的；具有部长职权的 |  Relating to or resembling a minister or a ministry; having powers or functions similar to those of a minister.  It suggests a level of authority and responsibility below that of a full minister but still significant. | The quasi-ministerial body was responsible for overseeing the implementation of the new policy.  这个准部长机构负责监督新政策的实施。 | near-ministerial, ministerial-like | non-ministerial |
