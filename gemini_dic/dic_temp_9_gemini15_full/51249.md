destructure: /dɪˈstrʌktʃər/ | vt. | 解构；破坏结构 | To take apart the structure of something; to analyze something by breaking it down into its constituent parts.  | 例句：The critic destructured the novel's narrative to reveal its underlying themes. 批评家解构了小说的叙事结构以揭示其潜在的主题。

例句：The programmer destructured the JSON data to access individual values. 程序员解构了JSON数据以访问单个值。


近义词：deconstruct, disassemble, analyze, break down | 反义词：construct, build, assemble, synthesize | 词性：动词
