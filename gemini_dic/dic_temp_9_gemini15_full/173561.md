It suggests a pretense of honesty, often masking ulterior motives or a lack of complete integrity. | Example: His quasi-honest explanation didn't quite convince the police.  他的似是而非的解释并没有完全说服警方。  |近义词：dishonest, deceitful, insincere,  hypocritical |反义词：honest, truthful, sincere, genuine
