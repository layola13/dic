renovation:/ˌrɛnəˈveɪʃən/| n. |整修；翻新；修缮|The act of repairing and improving something old, especially a building.  It often involves updating or modernizing the structure and its features.|例句：The house is undergoing extensive renovation. 这房子正在进行大规模的翻修。

例句：The renovation of the old factory into a modern art gallery was a success. 将旧工厂改造成现代美术馆的翻修工程很成功。


近义词：renewal, restoration, refurbishment, modernization|反义词：destruction, demolition


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is renovating the office building. = 公司正在翻新办公楼。
He renovates old houses and sells them for profit. = 他翻新旧房子然后高价卖出。


一般过去时:
They renovated their kitchen last year. = 他们去年装修了厨房。
The museum underwent a major renovation in 2010. = 该博物馆在2010年进行了大规模的翻修。


现在完成时:
We have renovated three houses this year. = 今年我们已经翻新了三栋房子。
She has just completed the renovation of her bathroom. = 她刚刚完成了浴室的装修。


过去完成时:
They had renovated the hotel before the grand opening. = 他们在大开幕之前已经翻修了酒店。
The builders had already completed the renovation when the owner arrived. = 业主到达时，建筑工人已经完成了翻修工作。


将来时:
We will renovate the garden next spring. = 我们明年春天将翻修花园。
They will begin the renovation project next month. = 他们下个月将开始翻修项目。


虚拟语气:
If we had more money, we would renovate the entire house. = 如果我们有钱的话，我们会把整栋房子都翻修一下。
I wish we could renovate the bathroom sooner. = 我希望我们能早点装修浴室。


被动语态:
The building is being renovated at the moment. = 这栋建筑正在进行翻修。
The old house was renovated by a skilled craftsman. = 这栋旧房子是由一位熟练的工匠翻新的。


疑问句:

否定句:
