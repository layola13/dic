sculling:/ˈskʌlɪŋ/| n. v.|划船；快速饮酒|The act of propelling a boat through the water using oars; also, the act of drinking quickly.  |例句：He is skilled in sculling. 他擅长划船。  She sculls a pint of beer in one go. 她一口气喝掉了一品脱啤酒。|近义词：rowing, gulping|反义词：sipping, savoring|动词，名词
