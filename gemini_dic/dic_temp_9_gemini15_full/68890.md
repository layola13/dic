fastforwarding:/ˈfæstˈfɔːrwɜːrdɪŋ/ | v. |快进；快速推进 |The act of quickly moving forward through something, especially a recording or a process.  It implies skipping over less important parts to reach a desired point. |例句：I fastforwarded through the boring parts of the movie. 我快进掉了电影中无聊的部分。

例句：We need to fastforward the negotiations to reach a deal before the deadline. 我们需要加快谈判进程，以便在截止日期前达成协议。


近义词：speeding up, accelerating, advancing


反义词：rewinding, slowing down, delaying
