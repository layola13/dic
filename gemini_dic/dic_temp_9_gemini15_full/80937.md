It represents a quest for something unattainable or the ultimate goal. |例句：The search for the Holy Grail became a metaphor for the pursuit of unattainable ideals. 寻找圣杯成为了追求无法实现的理想的隐喻。|近义词：holy grail, ultimate goal, aspiration |反义词：failure, disappointment |名词
