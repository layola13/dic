lust:/lʌst/| n. v.| 强烈的欲望；贪欲；性欲 |  A strong sexual desire; intense longing or craving.  |例句：He felt a lust for power. 他渴望权力。

例句：Her lust for adventure led her to travel the world.  她的冒险欲望使她周游世界。


近义词：craving, yearning, desire, passion, longing | 反义词：disgust, aversion, indifference | 词性: noun, verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He lusts after power. = 他渴望权力。
She lusts for adventure. = 她渴望冒险。


一般过去时:
He lusted after fame and fortune. = 他渴望名利。
She lusted for a life of luxury. = 她渴望奢华的生活。


现在完成时:
He has lusted after that car for years. = 多年来他一直渴望那辆车。
She has lusted after success in her career. = 她一直渴望在事业上取得成功。


过去完成时:
He had lusted after her for months before he finally confessed his feelings. = 在他最终表白之前，他已经渴望她好几个月了。
She had lusted after independence since her childhood. = 从小她就渴望独立。


将来时:
He will lust after even greater power once he achieves his current goals. = 一旦实现当前目标，他将渴望更大的权力。
She will lust after a peaceful retirement after years of hard work. =  经过多年的辛勤工作后，她将渴望平静的退休生活。


虚拟语气:
If he lusted less for material possessions, he would be happier. = 如果他不那么渴望物质财富，他会更快乐。
I wish I hadn't lusted after things I couldn't afford. = 我真希望我没有渴望我买不起的东西。


被动语态: (Lust is rarely used passively)  The position was lusted after by many ambitious individuals. = 许多有野心的人渴望得到这个职位。(此句较为罕见)
Power is often lusted after by those who feel inadequate. = 权力往往被那些感到力不从心的人所渴望。(此句较为罕见)


疑问句:
Does he lust after fame? = 他渴望名声吗？
What does she lust after? = 她渴望什么？


否定句:
He does not lust after money. = 他不渴望金钱。
She does not lust after power. = 她不渴望权力。
