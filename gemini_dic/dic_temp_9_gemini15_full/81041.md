graffer:/ˈɡræfər/| n. |涂鸦者，乱涂乱画者 |A person who creates graffiti.  A graffer is someone who illegally paints or draws pictures on walls, buildings, or other surfaces in public spaces.| 例句：The graffer was caught red-handed spraying his tag on the side of the train.  涂鸦者当场被抓住，正在火车侧面喷涂他的标签。
The police are trying to identify the graffer who defaced the historical monument.  警方正试图确认毁坏历史古迹的涂鸦者。|近义词：graffiti artist, vandal, tagger |反义词：artist (in a legitimate context), conservator |名词
