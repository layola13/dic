impeccability:/ˌɪmpekəˈbɪləti/| n. |无可挑剔；完美无缺|The state of being impeccable; faultlessness; perfection.  It refers to something being free from fault or defect. |例句：The impeccability of her performance was truly remarkable. 她表演的完美无缺，令人惊叹。

例句：The museum boasts the impeccability of its collection.  博物馆以其馆藏的完美无缺而自豪。


近义词：perfection, flawlessness, faultlessness, excellence

反义词：flawedness, imperfection, defectiveness


词性：名词
