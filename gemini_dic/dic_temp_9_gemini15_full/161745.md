powered: /ˈpaʊərd/ | adj. | 供电的；由…驱动的 |  Powered by electricity, the machine runs smoothly. | 近义词：energized, driven, propelled | 反义词：unpowered, manual


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car is powered by gasoline. = 这辆车由汽油驱动。
This device is powered by solar energy. = 这个设备由太阳能供电。


一般过去时:
The old mill was powered by water. = 那座旧磨坊由水力驱动。
The lights were powered by a generator. = 灯光由发电机供电。


现在完成时:
The project has been powered by grants from various organizations. = 该项目一直以来都得到来自各个组织的资助。
The city has been powered by renewable energy for five years. = 这个城市已经使用可再生能源供电五年了。


过去完成时:
The spaceship had been powered down before the docking procedure. = 太空船在对接程序之前已经关闭了电源。
The factory had been powered by coal before the switch to natural gas. = 在改用天然气之前，工厂一直使用煤炭供电。


将来时:
The new factory will be powered by wind turbines. = 新工厂将由风力涡轮机供电。
The electric car will be powered by a lithium-ion battery. = 这辆电动汽车将由锂离子电池供电。


虚拟语气:
If the machine were powered by solar energy, it would be more environmentally friendly. = 如果机器由太阳能供电，它将更环保。
I wish my house were powered by renewable energy. = 我希望我的房子能用可再生能源供电。


被动语态:
The city is powered by hydroelectric power. = 该城市由水力发电供电。
The computer was powered off accidentally. = 电脑意外地被关机了。


疑问句:
Is your laptop powered by a battery or an adapter? = 你的笔记本电脑是用电池还是适配器供电的？
How is this device powered? = 这个设备是怎么供电的？


否定句:
The device is not powered by electricity; it's manually operated. = 该设备不是由电力驱动的；它是手动操作的。
The car wasn't powered by gasoline; it was electric. = 这辆车不是汽油驱动的；它是电动的。
