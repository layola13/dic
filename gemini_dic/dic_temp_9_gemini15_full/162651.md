prearrests:/ˌpriːəˈrɛsts/ | n. |逮捕前的准备工作 |  The term "prearrests" refers to the investigative actions and preparations taken by law enforcement before making an arrest. This includes gathering evidence, identifying suspects, obtaining warrants (if necessary), and planning the arrest itself.  The goal is to ensure a safe and effective apprehension. |  The police conducted thorough prearrests, gathering sufficient evidence before apprehending the suspect. = 警方在逮捕嫌犯前进行了彻底的逮捕前准备工作。The prearrests involved surveillance and interviews with witnesses. = 逮捕前的准备工作包括监视和对证人的采访。 |  investigations, preparations, planning | postarrests | n.
