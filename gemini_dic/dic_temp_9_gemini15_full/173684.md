quasi-mad:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ mæd/| adj.|近乎疯狂的，有点疯狂的|Almost but not completely mad; exhibiting some characteristics of madness.  The term suggests a degree of eccentricity or unusual behavior that falls short of full-blown insanity.|例句：His quasi-mad scheme to build a time machine was ultimately abandoned due to lack of funding.  他的建造时光机的近乎疯狂的计划最终由于缺乏资金而被放弃了。|近义词：eccentric, unbalanced, slightly deranged, unhinged (mild)|反义词：sane, rational, composed, normal
