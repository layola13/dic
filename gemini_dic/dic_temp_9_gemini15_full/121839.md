misremembrance:/ˌmɪsrɪˈmɛmbrəns/| n. |错误的回忆，误记|The act of remembering something incorrectly; a false or inaccurate memory.  It often involves details being altered or events being conflated.|例句：His misremembrance of the incident led to a misunderstanding. 他的对事件的错误回忆导致了误解。

例句：The witness's misremembrance of the suspect's height hampered the investigation.  目击证人对嫌疑人身高记错妨碍了调查。


近义词：false memory, inaccurate recollection, misremembering, distorted memory |反义词：accurate recollection, correct memory |词性:名词


词源:

* **mis-**:  A prefix meaning "wrongly," "badly," or "ill."
* **remembrance**:  From Old French *remembrance*, ultimately from Latin *rememoratio*, a combination of *re-* (again) and *memoratio* (remembering, from *memor* - mindful).
