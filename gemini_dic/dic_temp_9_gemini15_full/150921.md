parallelling: /ˈpærəleɪlɪŋ/ | v. | 平行，与…相似 |  Describes the action of being parallel or running parallel to something else.  It can also imply similarity or correspondence between things. | 例句：The two roads were parallelling each other for miles.  两条路平行延伸数英里。

The artist's style paralleled that of the Impressionists.  这位艺术家的风格与印象派画家相似。 |近义词：running parallel to, resembling, corresponding, equalling | 反义词：diverging, contrasting, differing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The train is parallelling the river. = 火车正沿着河边行驶。
These two policies are parallelling each other. = 这两项政策彼此相似。

一般过去时:
The two lines paralleled each other on the graph. = 图表上的两条线彼此平行。
His career paralleled his brother's. = 他的职业生涯与他兄弟的相似。

现在完成时:
The government's actions have paralleled those of previous administrations. = 政府的行动与前任政府的行动相似。
Our research has paralleled theirs in many aspects. = 我们的研究在许多方面与他们的研究相似。


过去完成时:
By then, her musical style had paralleled that of her mentor. = 到那时，她的音乐风格已经与她的导师相似。
The development of the two technologies had paralleled each other for years.  这两种技术的研发多年来一直同步发展。


将来时:
The new highway will parallel the existing one. = 新高速公路将与现有高速公路平行。
His future plans will parallel his current goals. = 他未来的计划将与他目前的计划一致。


虚拟语气:
If the two paths paralleled each other, the journey would be easier. = 如果两条路径平行，旅程就会更容易。
I wish my progress paralleled his. = 我希望我的进步能与他一样快。


被动语态:
The new road is being paralleled by a cycle path. = 新建道路旁边正在修建一条自行车道。(被动语态中，paralleling本身较少用被动式)
The artist's work was paralleled by many critics with that of Picasso. = 许多评论家将这位艺术家的作品与毕加索的作品相提并论。(被动语态更常用被动形式的动词)


疑问句:
Does this line parallel that one? = 这条线与那条线平行吗？
Do your views parallel those of your colleagues? = 你的观点与同事的观点一致吗？


否定句:
The lines do not parallel each other. = 这两条线不平行。
His interests do not parallel hers at all. = 他的兴趣与她完全不同。


PHRASE->
run parallel to = 与……平行
in parallel with = 与……同时；与……平行地
parallel to = 平行于
parallel with = 与…平行；与…相似
draw a parallel between = 在…之间作比较
