myomancy:/maɪˈoʊmənsi/ | n. |肌肉占卜术|Divination by means of observing the movements or contractions of muscles.|The ancient practice of myomancy involved interpreting muscle spasms or twitches to predict the future or gain insight into a situation.  This practice, though obscure, highlights the historical attempts to find meaning in seemingly random bodily occurrences.|近义词：muscle divination,  (loosely)divination|反义词：None
