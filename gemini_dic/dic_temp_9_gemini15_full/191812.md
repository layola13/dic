sciuroid:/ˈsaɪərɔɪd/| adj. |松鼠类的；似松鼠的| Belonging to or characteristic of the Sciuridae family of rodents, which includes squirrels, chipmunks, and prairie dogs.  The term often describes features resembling those of squirrels.|例句：Many sciuroid species exhibit excellent climbing abilities. 许多松鼠类物种都表现出极佳的攀爬能力。
The sciuroid teeth are adapted for gnawing. 松鼠类的牙齿适合啃咬。|近义词：squirrel-like, rodent-like|反义词：N/A
