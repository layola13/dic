catalpa:/kəˈtælpə/| n. |梓树；梓树属植物|A catalpa is a type of tree known for its large leaves, showy flowers, and long, bean-like seed pods.  They are popular ornamental trees. |The catalpa tree in our yard is blooming beautifully.|  近义词：Indian bean tree, cigar tree| 反义词：无
