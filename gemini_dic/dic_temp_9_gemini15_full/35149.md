cloven:/ˈkloʊvən/| adj.| 分裂的；劈开的；蹄分叉的 | Split or divided into two parts; especially referring to a hoof divided into two parts.  |例句：The cloven hoof of the goat was clearly visible. 山羊分叉的蹄子清晰可见。|The path was cloven by a rushing river. 小路被奔腾的河流劈开了。|近义词：split, divided, cleft |反义词：undivided, whole, single
