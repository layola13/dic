modiolus:/ˈmɒdɪələs/| n. |蜗轴；耳蜗轴；基台|The modiolus is the conical central pillar of the cochlea in the inner ear, around which the spiral organ of Corti winds.  It contains the auditory nerve fibers.|例句：The modiolus is a crucial structure for hearing, as it houses the nerve fibers that transmit sound information to the brain. 蜗轴是听觉的关键结构，因为它容纳了将声音信息传递到大脑的神经纤维。|近义词：cochlear axis|反义词：无
