reengrossed:/ˌriːɪnˈɡroʊst/| v. |重新全神贯注，重新使…全神贯注|To become engrossed again; to become absorbed or deeply involved in something once more.  |例句：After a brief interruption, she reengrossed herself in her work.  在短暂中断后，她又全神贯注地投入工作。|例句：He reengrossed himself in the intricate details of the project. 他再次沉浸在项目错综复杂的细节中。|近义词：reabsorb, refocus, immerse|反义词：distract, divert
