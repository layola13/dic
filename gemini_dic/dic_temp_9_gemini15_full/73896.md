forsaken:/fɔːˈseɪkən/| adj.|被遗弃的；绝望的；破败的 |abandoned; desolate; hopeless;  describes something or someone that has been abandoned or left alone, often implying neglect and despair. It can also describe a place that is dilapidated and in a state of ruin.|例句：The forsaken house stood alone on the hill.  那座被遗弃的房子孤零零地矗立在山上。

The forsaken child cried for its mother. 被遗弃的孩子哭着喊着找妈妈。


近义词：abandoned, deserted, desolate, neglected, hopeless, ruined |反义词：cherished, loved, cared for, protected, thriving
