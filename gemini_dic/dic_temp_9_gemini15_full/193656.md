self-changing: /ˌsɛlfˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 自我改变的，自动改变的 |  Describing something that changes or alters itself without external intervention. This typically implies an inherent mechanism or process within the object or system causing the change. |

例句：

The self-changing color of the chameleon amazed the children.  = 变色龙自我改变的颜色让孩子们惊叹不已。

The self-changing display on the smart watch updated the time automatically. = 智能手表上自动改变的显示屏自动更新了时间。


近义词：automatic, self-adjusting, self-regulating, autonomous


反义词：static, unchanging, constant, fixed


词性：形容词
