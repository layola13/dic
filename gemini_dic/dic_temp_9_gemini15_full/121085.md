miscopying:/ˌmɪsˈkɒpiɪŋ/| n.|抄写错误，笔误|The act of copying something incorrectly.  It often implies a mistake made unintentionally.|例句：The miscopying of the address led to the delay in delivery.  抄写地址错误导致了送货延误。

例句：My miscopying of the formula resulted in an incorrect answer. 我抄写公式错误导致答案错误。


近义词：error, mistake, blunder, slip


反义词：accurate copying, correct transcription


词性:名词
