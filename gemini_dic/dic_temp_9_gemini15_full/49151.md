dejection:/dɪˈdʒɛkʃən/| n. |沮丧，消沉 | A state of low spirits caused by loss of hope or confidence.  It often involves feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and disappointment. |例句：His dejection was evident in his slumped posture and downcast eyes. 他的沮丧从他无精打采的姿势和低垂的眼睛中显而易见。

例句：After failing the exam, she fell into a deep dejection.  考试不及格后，她陷入了深深的沮丧。


近义词：despondency, depression, gloom, discouragement | 反义词：elation, joy, exhilaration, enthusiasm | 词性：名词
