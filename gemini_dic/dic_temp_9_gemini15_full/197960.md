shive:/ʃɪv/| n.|碎片；薄片；(尤指)木片|A shive is a thin, flat piece of something, especially wood, that has been broken off.  It often implies a sharp or pointed edge.|The sharp shive of wood pierced his finger.  这块尖利的木片刺伤了他的手指。| splinter, sliver, shard |  (无明确反义词，可以考虑whole, intact)| 名词
