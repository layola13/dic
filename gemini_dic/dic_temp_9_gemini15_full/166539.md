presbyterianly:/ˌprɛzbɪˈtɪəriənli/| adv. |长老会地，按长老会教义|In a manner characteristic of Presbyterians or the Presbyterian Church.  This adverb describes actions or beliefs aligning with Presbyterian theology and practices.|He lived presbyterianly, prioritizing community and faith.|近义词：devotedly, religiously|反义词：irreligiously, secularly


USAGE->
This word is an adverb and doesn't have verb conjugations like "is," "are," "was," etc.  It describes how something is done, not an action itself.  Therefore, typical verb tense examples are not applicable.  The word itself is rarely used in common speech.

例句1:  The congregation behaved presbyterianly during the service, showing respect and solemnity. = 会众在礼拜仪式中表现得虔诚庄重，展现了尊重和严肃。

例句2: He argued presbyterianly about the interpretation of scripture. = 他按照长老会的教义来论证经文的解释。


PHRASE->
This word doesn't typically form common phrases. Its use is limited to describing actions or characteristics consistent with Presbyterianism.
