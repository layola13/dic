despite:/dɪˈspaɪt/ | prep. |尽管，虽然 | in spite of; notwithstanding; regardless of.  It indicates that something happened or is true even though something else might have prevented it. |例句：Despite the rain, we went for a walk. = 尽管下雨，我们还是去散步了。|例句：He finished the race despite his injury. = 尽管受伤，他还是完成了比赛。|近义词：in spite of, notwithstanding, regardless of, even though |反义词：because of, due to, owing to

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He goes to work despite the bad weather. = 尽管天气不好，他还是去上班了。
She continues to study despite feeling tired. = 尽管感到疲倦，她还是继续学习。

一般过去时:
They went on a picnic despite the cold. = 尽管天气寒冷，他们还是去野餐了。
She sang beautifully despite her nervousness. = 尽管很紧张，她还是唱得很美。

现在完成时:
We have finished the project despite many challenges. = 尽管面临许多挑战，我们还是完成了项目。
She has maintained her optimism despite setbacks. = 尽管遭遇挫折，她仍然保持乐观。

过去完成时:
They had completed the task despite numerous obstacles. = 尽管有许多障碍，他们还是完成了任务。
She had succeeded despite her initial doubts. = 尽管她最初有所怀疑，但她还是成功了。

将来时:
We will go to the party despite the late hour. = 尽管时间很晚了，我们还是会去参加聚会。
He will continue his research despite the lack of funding. = 尽管缺乏资金，他仍将继续他的研究。

虚拟语气:
I would go to the concert despite the high ticket price. = 即使票价很高，我也会去看音乐会。
She would have helped him despite his rudeness. = 即使他很粗鲁，她也会帮助他。

被动语态:
The meeting was held despite the low attendance. = 尽管出席人数较少，会议还是召开了。
The plan was approved despite some objections. = 尽管有一些反对意见，计划还是得到了批准。

疑问句:
Would you still go despite the risk? = 即使有风险，你还会去吗？
Will they continue despite the difficulties? = 即使困难重重，他们还会继续吗？

否定句:
He didn't give up despite the failure. = 尽管失败了，他并没有放弃。
She didn't change her mind despite the criticism. = 尽管受到批评，她并没有改变主意。


PHRASE->
