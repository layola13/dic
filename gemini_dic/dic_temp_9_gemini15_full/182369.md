rendezvouses:/ˈrɑːn.deɪ.vuːz/| n.|约会；集合点；会合|The plural form of "rendezvous," referring to multiple meetings or meeting places.  It implies prearranged meetings, often of a clandestine or secretive nature, or those with a specific purpose.|例句：The spies had several secret rendezvouses throughout the city.间谍们在城市里进行了几次秘密会面。|例句：The military planned a series of rendezvouses for the troops.军方为部队计划了一系列的集合点。|近义词：meetings, appointments, gatherings|反义词：separations, partings
