clause:/klɔːz/| /klɔːz/| n.|从句，条款|A clause is a group of words containing a subject and a verb, forming part of a sentence.  A clause can be independent (a complete sentence) or dependent (requiring another clause to complete its meaning).|The clause "because it was raining" is dependent. |sentence, phrase, proviso|independent clause|
