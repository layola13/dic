megatherium:/ˌmɛɡəˈθɪəriəm/| n. |巨型地懒|A genus of extinct giant ground sloths that lived in South America during the Pleistocene epoch.  They were massive herbivores with powerful claws. |Example:  The *megatherium* was one of the largest mammals to ever walk the earth.  巨型地懒是曾经在地球上行走的最大的哺乳动物之一。|近义词：giant ground sloth|反义词：N/A
