ecmascript:/ˈɛk.məˌskrɪpt/ | n. |ECMAScript，ECMA-262标准定义的脚本语言 |ECMAScript is a standardized scripting language specification.  It is best known as the language of the web, as it is the foundation for JavaScript.  The specification defines syntax, semantics, and a standard library.  Different implementations of ECMAScript exist, with JavaScript being the most popular. |Example: Modern JavaScript engines mostly conform to the latest ECMAScript specifications.  现代的JavaScript引擎大多符合最新的ECMAScript规范。|近义词：JavaScript (in common usage)|反义词：无
