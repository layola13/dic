glasswort:/ˈɡlæsˌwɜːrt/| n. |玻璃草，碱蓬|A succulent plant of the genus Salicornia, growing in salt marshes and having jointed stems and leaves that are reduced to scales.  It was historically used to make soda ash. |Example:  The salt marshes were teeming with glasswort, its succulent stems a vibrant green.  盐沼里挤满了玻璃草，它多汁的茎是鲜艳的绿色。|近义词：samphire (similar plant)|反义词：无|名词
