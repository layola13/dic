link-up: /ˈlɪŋkʌp/ | n., v. |连接，联合；会合 |

英文解析:A link-up refers to the act of connecting or joining two or more things together, or the instance of people meeting or coming together.  It can be a physical connection, a metaphorical connection, or a meeting of people.

例句:

n.  The link-up between the two companies created a powerful synergy.  = 两家公司的合并产生了强大的协同效应。

n.  The police arranged a link-up with the local community to combat crime. = 警方与当地社区联手打击犯罪。


v.  The two teams will link up for a joint training session. = 两队将联合进行训练。

v.  We need to link up the databases to improve efficiency. = 我们需要连接数据库以提高效率。


近义词: connection, union, meeting, rendezvous, merger, combination

反义词: disconnection, separation, severance, disunity
