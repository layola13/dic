rumors:/ˈruːmərz/| n.|谣言，传闻|Unverified or unsubstantiated information spread informally, often by word of mouth.|例句：Rumors of a merger spread quickly through the company. 很快就有合并的谣言在公司里传开了。|近义词：gossip, hearsay, speculation, scuttlebutt|反义词：facts, truth


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Rumors spread like wildfire. = 谣言像野火一样蔓延。
There are always rumors circulating in this town. = 这个镇上总是有谣言在流传。


一般过去时:
Rumors circulated about the CEO's resignation. = 关于首席执行官辞职的谣言四起。
Many rumors followed the unexpected announcement. = 许多谣言伴随着这个意外的公告而来。


现在完成时:
Rumors have spread about a potential scandal. = 关于一起潜在丑闻的谣言已经传开了。
Have you heard any rumors about the upcoming election? = 你听说过关于即将到来的选举的任何谣言吗？


过去完成时:

By the time the truth came out, damaging rumors had already spread widely. = 当真相大白的时候，损害性的谣言已经广泛传播了。


将来时:
