ratepayings:/ˈreɪtpeɪɪŋz/| n.| 费率支付；税款支付 | Ratepayings refer to the payments made for services or goods based on a specific rate or tariff.  This often applies to local government taxes or utility bills. | Example:  The council increased ratepayings this year, causing a significant rise in household expenses.今年市议会提高了税率，导致家庭支出大幅增加。|近义词：tax payments, utility payments, assessments|反义词：none|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Ratepayings are due at the end of the month. = 税款应在月底支付。
Our ratepayings cover water and sewage services. = 我们的税款包括水费和污水处理费。


一般过去时:
Ratepayings were significantly lower last year. = 去年的税款要低得多。
We paid our ratepayings on time last quarter. = 我们上个季度按时缴纳了税款。


现在完成时:
The council has increased ratepayings this year. = 市议会今年提高了税款。
We have always paid our ratepayings promptly. = 我们一直按时缴纳税款。


过去完成时:
They had already paid their ratepayings before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经缴纳了税款。
The government had underestimated the impact of rising ratepayings. = 政府低估了税款上涨的影响。


将来时:
Ratepayings will increase next year due to inflation. = 由于通货膨胀，明年的税款将会增加。
We will pay our ratepayings online next month. = 我们下个月将在线支付税款。


虚拟语气:
If ratepayings were lower, more people could afford to live here. = 如果税款更低，更多的人就能负担得起住在这里。
I wish ratepayings weren't so high. = 我希望税款不要这么高。


被动语态:
Ratepayings are collected by the local council. = 税款由地方议会征收。
Ratepayings are often calculated based on property value. = 税款通常根据房产价值计算。


疑问句:
What are the current ratepayings for this property? = 这处房产目前的税款是多少？
When are ratepayings due? = 税款的截止日期是什么时候？


否定句:
