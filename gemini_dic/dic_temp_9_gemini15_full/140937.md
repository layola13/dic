one-for-the-road:/wʌn fɔː ðə roʊd/| n. phr.|最后一杯酒；临别赠言|An alcoholic beverage consumed as a final drink before leaving a place, often a bar or party. It's a farewell drink.  |例句：Let's have one for the road before we head home.  = 我们回家前再来一杯吧。

例句：He offered his friend one for the road as a parting gesture. = 他给朋友最后一杯酒作为临别的表示。


近义词：last drink, parting drink, final drink


反义词：first drink, initial drink


词性:名词短语
