czarist:/ˈzɑːrɪst/| adj.|沙皇的；与沙皇相关的|Relating to or characteristic of a tsar or the tsardom.  The term often implies the autocratic and oppressive aspects of Tsarist rule.|例句：The czarist regime was overthrown in 1917.  沙皇政权于1917年被推翻。|例句：He studied the czarist policies of the late 19th century. 他研究了19世纪末期的沙皇政策。|近义词：imperial, regal, autocratic|反义词：democratic, republican|
