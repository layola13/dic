idle away:/ˈaɪdl əˈweɪ/| v. |虚度；消磨；浪费(时间)|To spend time in a lazy, unproductive way.|例句：He idled away the afternoon watching television. 他下午看电视虚度时光。|例句：She idled away her summer vacation doing nothing worthwhile. 她无所事事地虚度了暑假。|近义词：waste, fritter away, dawdle, squander|反义词：utilize, employ, utilize productively
