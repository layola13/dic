oik:/ɔɪk/| n.|乡巴佬，粗人，乡下人 |A person, especially a man, from the country or a rural area, considered unsophisticated or uneducated.  The word often carries a derogatory connotation.|例句：He was considered an oik by the city folk. 他被城里人看作是乡巴佬。|近义词：bumpkin, yokel, peasant, rustic|反义词：sophisticate, cosmopolitan|名词
