myrtlewoods:/ˈmɜːrtlˌwʊdz/| n. |桃金娘树林；桃金娘丛生地|A place with many myrtle trees.  Myrtlewoods are often found in warm, humid climates.|例句：We hiked through dense myrtlewoods, the air thick with the scent of blossoms. 我们徒步穿越茂密的桃金娘树林，空气中弥漫着浓郁的花香。|例句：The birds built their nests in the sheltered branches of the myrtlewoods. 鸟儿们在桃金娘树林里庇护的树枝上筑巢。|近义词：myrtle grove, myrtle thicket|反义词：open field, desert
