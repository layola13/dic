comback:/ˈkʌmbæk/ | n. |卷土重来；东山再起；回归；反击 | A comeback is a return to a previous level of success or popularity, or a return to a place or situation after a period of absence.  It can also refer to a retort or a counterattack. |例句：His comeback album was a huge success. 他的复出专辑获得了巨大成功。

例句：After a long illness, she made a remarkable comeback to the tennis court.  大病初愈后，她令人瞩目地重返网球场。

近义词：return, revival, resurgence, recovery, retort

反义词：failure, defeat, downfall, decline


USAGE->

His comeback tour is selling out fast. = 他的复出巡演门票销售火爆。
The team is hoping for a comeback in the next game. = 球队希望在下场比赛中卷土重来。
