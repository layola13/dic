clamberer:/ˈklæmbərər/| n. |攀登者，爬行者 |A person who climbs or scrambles, often with difficulty.  Someone who clambers is typically moving upwards over rough or uneven terrain.|例句：The experienced clamberer scaled the treacherous cliff face with ease.  经验丰富的攀登者轻松地攀登了险峻的悬崖峭壁。

The agile clamberer navigated the rocky path without hesitation.  敏捷的攀登者毫不犹豫地走过了崎岖的小路。

近义词：climber, scrambler, mountaineer |反义词：None specifically, but words implying ease of movement could be considered antonyms, such as "walker," "stroller."|词性:名词
