clinger's: /ˈklɪŋərz/ | n. | 黏着的人（复数）|  指那些依恋性强，依赖性强的人，或者指紧紧抓住某物的人。This term often carries a slightly negative connotation, suggesting excessive dependence or unwillingness to let go. | 例句：The clingy behavior of the children made some of the other participants uncomfortable.  孩子们黏人的行为让其他一些参与者感到不舒服。


The teacher noticed the student's clingy attachment to his mother. 老师注意到这个学生对母亲的依恋。


近义词：dependents, adherers | 反义词：independent people, detached individuals


词性:名词 (复数)
