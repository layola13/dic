outspy:/aʊtˈspaɪ/| vt.| 胜过；暗中侦察；比…更胜一筹 | To surpass in skill or cunning; to secretly spy on or investigate. |例句：The seasoned detective managed to outspy the cunning thief.  经验丰富的侦探设法胜过了狡猾的小偷。
The agency sent agents to outspy the enemy's movements.  该机构派特工去暗中侦察敌人的行动。|近义词：outwit, surpass, spy on, investigate |反义词：be outspied by, be surpassed by
