foamed:/foʊmd/ | v. | 起泡沫；发泡；变得泡沫状 | To form or produce foam; to become covered with foam.  | The soap foamed nicely in the hot water. 肥皂在热水中产生了丰富的泡沫。The river foamed with white water after the storm. 暴风雨过后，河水泛起白色的泡沫。 | bubble, froth |  still, calm


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The beer foams when poured.  啤酒倒出来时会起泡。
He foams at the mouth when he's angry. 他生气时会口吐白沫。

一般过去时:
The cappuccino foamed beautifully. 卡布奇诺咖啡的奶泡很漂亮。
The sea foamed against the rocks. 海浪拍打岩石，激起泡沫。

现在完成时:
The detergent has foamed up the entire sink. 洗涤剂把整个水槽都弄满了泡沫。
The waves have foamed and crashed against the shore all day.  海浪整天都在岸边拍打起泡沫。

过去完成时:
The beer had already foamed over the top of the glass. 啤酒已经溢出杯子了。
The mixture had foamed excessively, causing a mess.  混合物泡沫过多，弄得一团糟。

将来时:
The soap will foam readily in warm water. 肥皂在温水中会很容易起泡。
The agitated sea will foam wildly during the storm. 暴风雨期间，汹涌的大海将会狂暴地起泡。

虚拟语气:
If the beer foamed less, it would be easier to drink. 如果啤酒泡沫少一些，喝起来就容易多了。
I wish the detergent would foam more effectively. 我希望洗涤剂能更有效地起泡。

被动语态:
The milk was foamed to create a rich texture. 牛奶被打成泡沫以创造丰富的质地。
The surface of the water was foamed by the strong wind. 水面被强风吹起了泡沫。

疑问句:
Does this shampoo foam well?  这款洗发水泡沫丰富吗？
Does the detergent foam effectively in cold water? 洗涤剂在冷水中能有效起泡吗？

否定句:
The soap didn't foam at all. 肥皂根本没有起泡。
The beer didn't foam properly. 啤酒没有充分起泡。
