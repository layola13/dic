go-fer:/ˈɡoʊfər/| n. |跑腿的人，杂役|A go-fer is a person, usually a junior employee, whose job is to run errands and perform minor tasks for others.  The term often implies a lack of significant responsibility or decision-making power. |Example:  He started as a lowly go-fer, fetching coffee and photocopying documents. 他最初只是一个卑微的跑腿，负责端咖啡和复印文件。|近义词:errand boy/girl, messenger, assistant|反义词:boss, manager, executive
