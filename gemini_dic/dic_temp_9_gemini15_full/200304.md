skringe:/skrɪndʒ/| v.|畏缩；退缩；蜷缩|To shrink or cringe; to recoil in fear or disgust.|例句：He skringed away from the spider. 他畏缩着躲开了蜘蛛。|例句：She skringed at the sight of the blood. 她看到血就吓得缩了起来。|近义词：cringe, recoil, shrink|反义词：stand up to, confront


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He skringes away from conflict. = 他回避冲突。
She skringes at the thought of public speaking. = 她一想到公开演讲就害怕。

一般过去时:
He skringed when he saw the snake. = 他看到蛇时吓了一跳。
She skringed away from the loud noise. = 她躲避了巨大的噪音。

现在完成时:
He has skringed at the sight of insects his whole life. = 他一辈子都害怕昆虫。
She has skringed from responsibility ever since she was young. = 从小她就逃避责任。

过去完成时:
He had skringed from the task for weeks before finally completing it. = 在最终完成任务之前，他几周都一直逃避这项任务。
She had skringed at the idea of confrontation prior to the meeting. = 在会议之前，她一直害怕对抗。
