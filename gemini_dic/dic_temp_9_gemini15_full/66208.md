excitant:/ɪkˈsaɪtənt/| n. |兴奋剂；刺激物 |A substance that excites or stimulates; an agent that increases the activity of a biological system.  It can refer to a drug or other substance that excites the nervous system, or a general stimulus. |Example: Caffeine is a common excitant. 咖啡因是一种常见的兴奋剂。 The news was such an excitant that I couldn't sleep.  这个消息太令人兴奋了，我睡不着觉。|近义词：stimulant, stimulant, energizer |反义词：depressant, sedative |名词
