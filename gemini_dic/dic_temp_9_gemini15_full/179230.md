redispersing:/ˌriːdɪˈspɜːrsɪŋ/| v.|重新分散；再分散|The act of dispersing something again after it has been concentrated or gathered.  It implies a previous dispersion followed by a reconcentration and then a subsequent redispersion. |例句：The paint needed redispersing before application.  涂料在使用前需要重新分散。

例句：After the initial mixing, the sediment required redispersing to ensure a uniform consistency. 初始混合后，沉淀物需要重新分散以确保均匀一致性。


近义词：redistributing, reshaping, respreading |反义词：concentrating, consolidating, aggregating
