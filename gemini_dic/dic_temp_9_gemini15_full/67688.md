exudate:/ˈɛksjuːdeɪt/| n. |渗出物，分泌物 |A fluid that oozes out of a wound or from a plant.  It often indicates infection or inflammation. |例句：The wound was producing a yellowish exudate.  伤口正在分泌黄色的渗出物。

例句：The plant's leaves showed signs of a gummy exudate. 植物的叶子显示出树胶状渗出物的迹象。

近义词：discharge, secretion, oozing, effusion |反义词：absorption, intake |词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wound exudates pus. = 伤口渗出脓液。
The plant exudates a sticky resin. = 植物分泌一种粘性的树脂。


一般过去时:
The infection exudated a foul-smelling fluid. = 感染渗出一种恶臭的液体。
The tree exudated sap after being cut. = 树木被砍伐后渗出树液。


现在完成时:
The lesion has exudated fluid for several days. = 病灶已经渗出液体好几天了。
The cut has exudated a clear liquid. = 伤口已经渗出清澈的液体。


过去完成时:
The wound had exudated blood before the bandage was applied. = 在包扎之前，伤口已经渗出血来了。
The plant had exudated sap all over its bark. = 植物的树皮上已经渗出了树液。


将来时:
The infection will likely exudate more pus. = 感染很可能会渗出更多的脓液。
The wound will exudate fluid for a few more days. = 伤口还会渗出液体几天。


虚拟语气:
If the wound were infected, it would exudate pus. = 如果伤口感染了，它就会渗出脓液。
If the plant were damaged, it might exudate resin. = 如果植物受损，它可能会分泌树脂。


被动语态:

疑问句:

PHRASE->

词源:
From Latin *exsudāre, from ex- "out" + sudāre "to sweat".  The word reflects the appearance of the fluid, often appearing to "sweat" out of the source.
