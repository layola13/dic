reawakes:/ˌriːəˈweɪks/| v.|重新唤醒；使复苏|To awaken again; to revive or renew.|例句：The old memories reawaken in her heart.  旧日的回忆在她心中苏醒。|例句：The spring rain reawakened the sleeping seeds. 春雨唤醒了沉睡的种子。|近义词：revive, rouse, awaken|反义词：extinguish, quell, suppress|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun reawakens the sleeping city. = 太阳唤醒了沉睡的城市。
Hope reawakens her spirit. = 希望重新点燃了她的斗志。

一般过去时:
The music reawakened her childhood memories. = 音乐唤起了她童年的回忆。
The warm sun reawakened the flowers. = 温暖的阳光使花朵重新绽放。

现在完成时:
The project has reawakened interest in sustainable energy. = 这个项目重新燃起了人们对可持续能源的兴趣。
His words have reawakened my passion for writing. = 他的话语重新点燃了我对写作的热情。

过去完成时:
The news had reawakened his old fears. = 这个消息重新唤起了他过去的恐惧。
She had reawakened her artistic talent after many years. = 许多年后，她重新唤起了自己的艺术天赋。

将来时:
The coming spring will reawaken the dormant plants. = 即将来临的春天将唤醒休眠的植物。
This treatment will reawaken his senses. = 这种疗法将使他恢复知觉。

虚拟语气:
If the music were louder, it might reawaken the sleeping child. = 如果音乐再响亮一些，或许就能唤醒睡着的孩子。
I wish this experience would reawaken my sense of adventure. = 我希望这段经历能重新唤起我的冒险精神。

被动语态:
His courage was reawakened by his family's support. = 他在家庭的支持下重拾了勇气。
The city was reawakened by the sounds of morning traffic. = 城市被清晨的交通声喧嚣着苏醒。

疑问句:
Will the warm weather reawaken the flowers this year? = 今年温暖的天气会唤醒花朵吗？
Does the smell of rain reawaken any memories for you? = 雨的味道是否唤起了你任何回忆？

否定句:

The cold weather didn't reawaken the hibernating bears. = 寒冷的天气并没有唤醒冬眠的熊。
