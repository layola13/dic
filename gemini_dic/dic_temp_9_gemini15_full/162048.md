pre-annexes:/ˌpriːˈæneksɪz/| v.|预先吞并；提前兼并|To annex something before it is officially annexed.  It implies a taking control or possession before the formal process is complete.|例句：The army pre-annexed the territory, securing vital resources before the official treaty was signed.  军队提前吞并了该领土，在正式条约签署之前就确保了重要的资源。|例句：The company pre-annexed the smaller firm's market share through aggressive marketing. 这家公司通过积极的营销手段提前兼并了小型公司的市场份额。|近义词：seize, occupy, usurp|反义词：cede, relinquish, surrender|动词
