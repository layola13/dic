clamorous: /ˈklæmərəs/ | adj. | 喧闹的；吵闹的；大声疾呼的 | Loud and confused noise; strongly expressing demands or complaints | The clamorous crowd demanded justice.  喧闹的人群要求正义。 The clamorous debate lasted for hours. 喧闹的辩论持续了几个小时。 | noisy, boisterous, vociferous | quiet, silent, hushed | 形容词


USAGE->

The children are clamorous for attention. = 孩子们吵着要关注。
The market is clamorous with the sounds of vendors. = 市场充斥着小贩的叫卖声。

一般过去时:
The protesters were clamorous in their demands. = 抗议者们大声疾呼他们的要求。
The audience was clamorous for an encore. = 观众们大声要求再来一首。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:
