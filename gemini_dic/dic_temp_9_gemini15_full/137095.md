nonsubconscious: /ˌnɒnˈsʌbkɒnʃəs/ | adj. |非潜意识的，有意识的|Not related to or arising from the subconscious mind; conscious.  The term is relatively rare and often used in contrast to "subconscious". It highlights actions or thoughts that are clearly within the realm of conscious awareness and deliberate control.|

Example:  His decision to quit his job was a nonsubconscious choice, made after careful consideration.  他辞职的决定是一个经过深思熟虑的有意识的选择。


近义词：conscious, deliberate, intentional, willful

反义词：subconscious, unconscious, involuntary, unintentional


词性: adjective
