kilometres:/kɪˈlɒmɪtəz/ (BrE) /kɪˈlɑːmɪtərz/ (AmE)| n. |千米，公里 |Kilometres is the plural form of kilometre, a unit of length equal to 1000 metres.  It is used to measure distances, especially longer ones. |The journey was 500 kilometres long. 这段旅程有500公里长。  We drove several kilometres before reaching the town. 我们开了几公里才到达镇上。|miles, meters|inches(反义词需考虑语境，并非完全反义)|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The distance is ten kilometres. = 这段距离是十公里。
How many kilometres do you run each day? = 你每天跑多少公里？


一般过去时:
We cycled 20 kilometres yesterday. = 我们昨天骑车跑了20公里。
They drove 100 kilometres to the beach. = 他们开车去了海滩，路程100公里。


现在完成时:
She has run over 50 kilometres this week. = 她这周已经跑了50多公里。
We have travelled many kilometres on this trip. = 在这次旅行中，我们已经走了很多公里。


过去完成时:
They had already run 15 kilometres before they stopped for a rest. = 他们在停下来休息之前已经跑了15公里。
We had driven over 300 kilometres by nightfall. = 到夜幕降临的时候，我们已经开了300多公里。


将来时:
We will cycle 30 kilometres tomorrow. = 我们明天将骑车30公里。
They will drive several hundred kilometres to reach their destination. = 他们将开车几百公里才能到达目的地。


虚拟语气:
If I had a car, I would drive 100 kilometres easily. = 如果我有车，我会轻松地开100公里。
I wish I had run 10 kilometres today, but I was too busy. = 我希望今天跑了10公里，但我太忙了。


被动语态:
Ten kilometres were covered in the first hour. = 在第一个小时内行驶了十公里。
Many kilometres are usually covered daily by the delivery truck. = 送货卡车每天通常行驶许多公里。


疑问句:
How many kilometres is it to the next town? = 到下一个镇还有多少公里？
What distance in kilometres did he run? = 他跑了多少公里？


否定句:
The journey was not 200 kilometres, it was shorter. = 这段旅程不是200公里，而是更短。
We didn't run 10 kilometres; we only managed 5. = 我们没有跑10公里，只跑了5公里。
