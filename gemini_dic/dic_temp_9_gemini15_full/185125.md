retinging:/riːˈtɪŋɪŋ/| v.|重新涂层，再镀层|The process of applying a new coating or layer to something, often a metal.  This could involve painting, plating, or other surface treatments.|例句：The old bridge is undergoing retinging to prevent further rusting.  老桥正在进行重新涂层以防止进一步生锈。

例句：The antique furniture requires careful retinging to restore its original color. 这件古董家具需要仔细地重新涂层以恢复其原来的颜色。


近义词：recoating, replating, refinishing

反义词：stripping (removing a coating)


词性:动词
