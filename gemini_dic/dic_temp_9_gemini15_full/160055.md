polyunsaturated: /ˌpɒliʌnˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/ /ˌpɑːliʌnˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 多不饱和的 | Containing more than one double or triple bond per molecule.  This term is commonly used to describe fats and fatty acids.  Polyunsaturated fats are generally considered healthier than saturated fats. |  Example:  Polyunsaturated fats are found in many plant-based oils, such as sunflower oil and soybean oil. 多不饱和脂肪存在于许多植物油中，例如葵花籽油和大豆油。  The benefits of a diet rich in polyunsaturated fatty acids are well documented. 富含多不饱和脂肪酸的饮食益处已被充分证明。| 近义词：unsaturated | 反义词：saturated, monounsaturated
