overaffirmative: /ˌoʊvərəˈfɜːrmətɪv/ | adj. | 过分肯定的，过于自信的 | Excessively or unnecessarily affirmative; expressing excessive certainty or confidence.  It suggests an overstatement of positivity or agreement, often to the point of being unrealistic or insensitive. |  例句：His overaffirmative responses made the interview feel disingenuous.  他的过分肯定的回答让面试感觉不真诚。

例句：Her overaffirmative attitude towards the project, despite obvious flaws, worried her colleagues. 她对这个项目过分肯定的态度，尽管存在明显的缺陷，还是让她的同事们担心。


近义词：overconfident, assertive, dogmatic, positive (in an excessive way),  unrealistic, overly optimistic


反义词：uncertain, doubtful, hesitant, pessimistic, negative, skeptical
