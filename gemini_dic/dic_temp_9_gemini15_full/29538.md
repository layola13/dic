catch:/kætʃ/| vt. n.|抓住；赶上；感染；理解；吸引；偷窃|To seize or capture something, especially after a chase.  To understand or grasp something quickly. To become affected by a disease. To attract someone's attention or interest.|例句：The police caught the thief. 警察抓住了小偷。

The child caught the ball. 孩子接住了球。


近义词：grab, seize, apprehend, understand, attract|反义词：miss, lose, fail to understand, repel|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I catch the bus every morning. = 我每天早上都乘公共汽车。
She catches the ball easily. = 她很容易接住球。


一般过去时:
He caught a cold last week. = 他上周感冒了。
I caught the thief red-handed. = 我当场抓住了小偷。


现在完成时:
I have caught three fish already. = 我已经钓到三条鱼了。
She has caught the flu.  = 她得了流感。


过去完成时:
He had caught the train before the announcement. = 他在公告发布前就已经赶上了火车。
They had caught several criminals before the investigation ended. = 在调查结束前，他们已经抓获了几名罪犯。


将来时:

We will catch up later. = 我们稍后再聊。


虚拟语气:

I wish I had caught the bus. = 我真希望我赶上了公共汽车。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
