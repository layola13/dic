enlumine:/ɪˈluːmɪn/ | vt. |照亮；阐明；启迪| to light up; to illuminate (mentally or spiritually); to brighten| The stained-glass window enlumined the cathedral. 彩绘玻璃窗照亮了大教堂。|The philosopher's words enlumined my understanding. 哲学家的言辞启迪了我的理解。|illuminate, brighten, enlighten|darken, obscure|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sun enlumines the sky. = 太阳照亮了天空。
His ideas enlumine the debate. = 他的想法阐明了这场辩论。


一般过去时:
The artist enlumined the manuscript. = 艺术家为手稿绘制插图（并使其更加明亮）。
The lecture enlumined my understanding of the topic. = 这次讲座使我对这个话题有了更清晰的理解。


现在完成时:
The new technology has enlumined many aspects of our lives. = 新技术已经照亮了我们生活的许多方面。
His teachings have enlumined countless souls. = 他的教诲启迪了无数人的心灵。


过去完成时:
The discovery had enlumined the scientific community. = 这一发现曾照亮了科学界。
The ancient writings had enlumined the history of the civilization. = 古代文献曾阐明了这个文明的历史。


将来时:
The new streetlights will enlumine the streets. = 新的路灯将照亮街道。
The upcoming conference will enlumine the current issues. = 即将来临的会议将阐明当前的问题。


虚拟语气:
If the moon were full, it would enlumine the night. = 如果月亮是满月，它就会照亮夜晚。
If only his words had enlumined my path earlier. = 但愿他的话早点启迪我的方向。


被动语态:
The room was enlumined by the fireplace. = 房间被壁炉照亮了。
The mystery was enlumined by the detective's keen observation. = 这个谜团被侦探敏锐的观察所阐明。


疑问句:
Does the lamp enlumine the entire room? = 这盏灯能照亮整个房间吗？
Will this explanation enlumine the situation? = 这个解释能阐明情况吗？


否定句:
The darkness did not enlumine the path. = 黑暗没有照亮道路。
His words did not enlumine the complex problem. = 他的话并没有阐明这个复杂的问题。


PHRASE->
enlumine the mind = 启迪心智
enlumine the soul = 净化心灵
enlumine the path = 指引方向
enlumine the future = 照亮未来
enlumine the darkness = 驱散黑暗

词源:
源自古法语 *enluminer，意为“照亮”，最终源自拉丁语 *illuminare，意为“照亮”。  这与 illuminate 的词源关系密切。
