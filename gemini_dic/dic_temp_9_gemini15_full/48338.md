decriminalizes:/ˌdiːˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪz/ | v. |使非犯罪化；取消…的犯罪性质 | To remove the criminal status of something; to make something no longer a crime. | 例句：Many countries have decriminalized the possession of small amounts of marijuana. 许多国家已经将持有少量大麻的行为非罪化。 | 例句：The government is considering decriminalizing prostitution. 政府正在考虑将卖淫行为非罪化。 | 近义词：legalize, legitimize | 反义词：criminalize
