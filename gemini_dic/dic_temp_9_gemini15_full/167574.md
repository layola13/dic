prewillingness:/ˌpriːˈwɪlɪŋnəs/| n.|预先的意愿，预先的准备|A predisposition or readiness to do something before it is officially requested or required.  It suggests a proactive and anticipatory attitude.|例句：His prewillingness to help was evident in his immediate offer of assistance.  他的乐于助人表现在他立即提供的帮助中。

例句：The company's prewillingness to negotiate saved time and resources.  公司预先愿意谈判节省了时间和资源。


近义词：proclivity, predisposition, readiness, preparedness

反义词：reluctance, unwillingness, hesitation, resistance

词性: 名词
