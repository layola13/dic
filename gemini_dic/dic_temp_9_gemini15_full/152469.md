pay-someone-back-in-his-own-coin: /ˌpeɪ ˈsʌmˌwʌn bæk ɪn hɪz ˈoʊn kɔɪn/ | v. | 以牙还牙；以其人之道还治其人之身 | To retaliate against someone using the same methods they used against you.  |例句：He paid her back in her own coin by spreading rumors about her. 他以散播她的谣言来以牙还牙。

例句：After years of bullying, the victim finally paid his tormentor back in his own coin.  多年受欺凌后，受害者终于以其人之道还治其人之身。


近义词：retaliate, revenge, get even with, reciprocate (in kind)

反义词：forgive, pardon, overlook


词性：动词 (verb)
