preexhausts: /ˌpriːɪɡˈzɔːst/ | vt. | 预先耗尽；提前疲惫 | To exhaust or tire out beforehand; to deplete beforehand.  |  例句：The intense training preexhausted the athletes before the competition.  = 剧烈的训练在比赛前使运动员筋疲力尽。

例句：The marathon runner preexhausted his energy reserves in the early miles. = 马拉松运动员在比赛初期就耗尽了能量储备。


近义词：exhaust, tire out, deplete, weaken | 反义词：energize, invigorate, strengthen | 词性：动词
