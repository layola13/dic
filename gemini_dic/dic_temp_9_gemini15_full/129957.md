non-Mediterranean: /ˌnɒnˌmɛdɪtəˈreɪniən/ | adj. | 非地中海地区的 | Not relating to or characteristic of the Mediterranean region.  This word describes something that is not located in, associated with, or typical of the Mediterranean Sea and its surrounding countries.  It signifies a geographical or cultural distinction.


例句：

The restaurant serves non-Mediterranean cuisine. = 这家餐厅供应非地中海菜系。

His architectural style is distinctly non-Mediterranean. = 他的建筑风格明显是非地中海风格的。


近义词：

extra-Mediterranean, non-Mediterranean


反义词：

Mediterranean


词性：形容词
