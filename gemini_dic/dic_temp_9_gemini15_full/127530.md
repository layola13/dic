necromancy:/ˈnɛkrəˌmænsi/| n. |死灵法术，招魂术 |The practice of communicating with the dead, typically through rituals and spells, to gain knowledge or power.  Necromancy is often associated with witchcraft and the supernatural. |例句：The villagers accused the old woman of necromancy after strange occurrences in the graveyard. 村民在墓地发生怪事后指责那个老妇人使用死灵法术。|例句：He studied ancient texts on necromancy, hoping to unlock the secrets of the afterlife. 他研究古代关于死灵法术的文本，希望能解开来世的秘密。|近义词：black magic, sorcery, witchcraft |反义词：prayer, blessing
