pressure-cookers: /ˈprɛʃərˌkʊkərz/ | n. |高压锅 |A pressure cooker is a sealed pot that cooks food faster than conventional methods by using steam pressure to raise the boiling point of water.  This allows food to cook at higher temperatures, significantly reducing cooking time. |例句：She uses a pressure cooker to make delicious and quick meals. 她用高压锅做美味又快捷的饭菜。|近义词：pressure pot, rapid cooker |反义词：conventional pot, slow cooker
