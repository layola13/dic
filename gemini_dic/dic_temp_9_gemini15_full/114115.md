magot:/ˈmæɡət/| n.|蛆，幼虫|A maggot is the larva of a fly, especially a blowfly.  Maggots are typically white or cream-colored and worm-like. They feed on decaying organic matter.|例句：The rotting meat was teeming with maggots.  腐烂的肉里爬满了蛆。

例句：We found several maggots in the garbage can.  我们在垃圾桶里发现了几条蛆。|近义词：larva, grub, worm|反义词:none.
