regarrisoned:/ˌriːˈɡærɪsənd/| v. |重新驻军；再次配置兵力 | To provide (a place) with a new garrison; to reinforce a garrison.  |例句：The fort was regarrisoned after the enemy attack.  敌军进攻后，要塞重新驻军了。  The city was regarrisoned with fresh troops.  这座城市用新兵重新驻防。|近义词：reoccupy, reinforce, man|反义词：evacuate, abandon|动词
