nimble: /ˈnɪmbəl/ | adj. | 敏捷的；灵活的 |  Characterized by quickness, lightness, and ease of movement; agile.  |  例句：The dancer's nimble movements captivated the audience.  舞者轻盈敏捷的动作迷住了观众。

例句：He had nimble fingers, perfect for intricate needlework. 他有一双灵巧的手指，非常适合做精细的针线活。


近义词：agile, quick, deft, spry | 反义词：clumsy, awkward, stiff, slow | 词性: adjective
