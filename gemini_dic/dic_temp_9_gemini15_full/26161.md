but:/bʌt/| conj.|但是，然而|It introduces a contrasting idea or fact.  It shows a difference or exception to what has just been said. |例句：I wanted to go to the party, but I was too tired. 我想参加派对，但是我太累了。|例句：She tried hard, but she still failed. 她努力尝试了，但她仍然失败了。|近义词：however, yet, nevertheless|反义词：and, therefore
