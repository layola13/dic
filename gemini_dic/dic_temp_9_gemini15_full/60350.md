egotistical: /ˌɛɡoʊˈtɪstɪkəl/ | adj. | 自我中心的，自大的 | excessively conceited or absorbed in oneself; self-centered.  |例句：His egotistical behavior alienated many of his colleagues. 他自大的行为疏远了他的许多同事。

例句：She's become increasingly egotistical since her promotion. 自从升职以来，她变得越来越自大了。


近义词：narcissistic, self-centered, vain, conceited, self-absorbed


反义词：humble, modest, selfless, unselfish


词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is egotistical and rarely listens to others. 他很自大，很少听取别人的意见。
She acts egotistical, always boasting about her achievements. 她表现得很自大，总是吹嘘自己的成就。


一般过去时:
He was egotistical in his youth, but matured later. 他年轻时很自大，但后来成熟了。
She was so egotistical that nobody wanted to work with her. 她太自大了，没有人想和她一起工作。


现在完成时:
He has been egotistical throughout his career. 他整个职业生涯都很自大。
She has become increasingly egotistical since winning the award. 自从获奖以来，她变得越来越自大了。


过去完成时:
He had been egotistical for years before anyone called him out on it.  多年来他一直很自大，直到有人指出这一点。
She had been so egotistical that her friends had abandoned her. 她一直如此自大，以至于她的朋友们都抛弃了她。


将来时:
He will likely remain egotistical, unless he undergoes significant self-reflection. 除非他进行深刻的自我反省，否则他可能会一直自大下去。
She will probably become even more egotistical if she continues to receive praise without criticism. 如果她继续受到赞扬而没有批评，她可能会变得更加自大。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't so egotistical, he would have more friends. 如果他不那么自大，他就会有更多朋友。
I wish she weren't so egotistical; it's hard to be around her. 我希望她不要那么自大；和她在一起很难相处。


被动语态:
His behavior is often described as egotistical. 他的行为常常被描述为自大的。
Her actions are perceived as egotistical by many. 许多人认为她的行为是自大的。


疑问句:
Is he always so egotistical? 他总是这么自大吗？
Are you sure she isn't just confident, and not egotistical? 你确定她不是自信，而是自大吗？


否定句:
He is not egotistical; he's just very confident. 他并不自大；他只是非常自信。
She is not at all egotistical; she's actually quite humble. 她一点也不自大；她实际上很谦虚。
