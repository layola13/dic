corrosiveness:/kəˈroʊsɪvnəs/| n. |腐蚀性，侵蚀性 |The corrosiveness of the acid damaged the metal pipe.  酸的腐蚀性损坏了金属管道。|近义词：erosiveness,destructiveness |反义词：inertness, harmlessness


英文解析：The quality or state of being corrosive; the ability to cause corrosion.  腐蚀的性质或状态；引起腐蚀的能力。


例句：

The high corrosiveness of seawater requires the use of special materials for marine structures. 海水的强腐蚀性要求对海洋结构使用特殊材料。

The manufacturer warned about the chemical's corrosiveness and recommended protective gear. 制造商警告了该化学品的腐蚀性，并建议使用防护装备。


词性：名词
