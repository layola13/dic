comma:/ˈkɑːmə/|ˈkɒmə/| n. |逗号 |A punctuation mark (,) indicating a pause between parts of a sentence.  It is also used to separate items in a list. |例句：Please use a comma after the introductory phrase. 请在引导词组后使用逗号。|例句：I bought apples, bananas, and oranges. 我买了苹果、香蕉和橙子。|近义词：separator |反义词：无
