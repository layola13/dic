leafing:/ˈliːfɪŋ/| v.| 翻阅；长出叶子 |  The act of turning over pages or the process of a plant producing leaves.  |例句：She was leafing through a magazine. 她正在翻阅杂志。  The trees are leafing out. 树木正在长出叶子。|近义词：browsing, perusing, sprouting|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is leafing through the book. = 他正在翻阅这本书。
The plants are leafing out beautifully. = 植物们正茂盛地长出叶子。

一般过去时:
She leafed through the report quickly. = 她快速地翻阅了报告。
The trees leafed out late this year. = 今年树木出叶较晚。

现在完成时:
I have leafed through several books this morning. = 我今天早上已经翻阅了几本书。
The trees have leafed out, signaling the arrival of spring. = 树木已经长出叶子，预示着春天的到来。

过去完成时:
He had leafed through the manuscript before the meeting. = 他在会议前已经翻阅过手稿了。
The leaves had leafed out before the unexpected frost. = 在意外的霜冻来临之前，树叶已经长出来了。

将来时:
She will be leafing through the photos later. = 她稍后会翻看照片。
The oak trees will leaf out in early May. = 橡树将在五月初长出叶子。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would leaf through the entire encyclopedia. = 如果我有更多时间，我会翻阅整本百科全书。
I wish I could leaf through the ancient scrolls. = 我希望我能翻阅那些古老的卷轴。

被动语态:
The book was being leafed through by the students. = 学生们正在翻阅这本书。
The leaves are being consumed by caterpillars.  = 叶子正在被毛毛虫啃食。(Note:  While not directly "leafing," this demonstrates the passive voice with a related action)


疑问句:
Are you leafing through that novel? = 你在翻阅那本小说吗？
Have the roses leafed out yet? = 玫瑰花长出叶子了吗？


否定句:
He didn't leaf through the contract carefully. = 他没有仔细翻阅合同。
The plants haven't leafed out yet due to the cold weather. = 由于天气寒冷，植物还没有长出叶子。
