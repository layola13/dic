epimera:/ɪˈpɪmərə/| n.|差向异构体|Diastereomers that differ in configuration at only one chiral center.  Epimers are a special type of diastereomer. They are stereoisomers that differ in the configuration at only one chiral carbon atom. |Example: D-glucose and D-mannose are epimers, differing only at the C-2 carbon.|近义词：diastereomer|反义词：enantiomer
