frappe:/fræˈpeɪ/ | n. |冰沙，冰冻咖啡|A frappe is a blended iced beverage, often made with coffee, milk, and ice.  It's typically sweet and refreshing.| 例句：I ordered a caramel frappe to cool off on the hot day.  我点了一杯焦糖冰沙来消暑。  The coffee shop offers a wide variety of frappes, including chocolate and vanilla. 这家咖啡店提供各种各样的冰沙，包括巧克力和香草口味。|近义词：smoothie, milkshake, iced coffee|反义词：hot coffee, hot chocolate|名词
