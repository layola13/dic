mangas:/ˈmæŋɡəz/| n.|漫画（日语漫画的复数形式）|  Manga refers to Japanese comics, typically published in serialized form in magazines or collected in bound volumes.  The term encompasses a wide range of genres and styles, from action and adventure to romance and horror. |例句：I love reading mangas, especially those with strong female leads. 我喜欢读漫画，尤其是那些以女性为主角的漫画。  I bought a whole collection of mangas at the anime convention.我在动漫展上买了一整套漫画。|近义词：comics, graphic novels|反义词：None.
