chest:/tʃest/| n.|胸部，胸腔；箱子，柜子|The broad upper part of the human body between the neck and the abdomen, containing the heart and lungs.  A container, typically made of wood or metal, with a lid and used for storage.|例句：He felt a sharp pain in his chest. 他感到胸部一阵剧痛。

例句：She keeps her jewelry in a beautiful wooden chest. 她把首饰放在一个漂亮的木箱里。|近义词：thorax, trunk, box, container|反义词：back

词源:

The word "chest" comes from the Old English word "cist," meaning "box" or "chest."  This word, in turn, has roots in the Latin word "cista," also meaning "box" or "basket." The meaning extended to refer to the human chest due to the shape similarity.
