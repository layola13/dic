messaged:/ˈmɛsɪdʒd/ | v. | 发送消息，留言 | To send a message to someone, typically via electronic means.  | Example: I messaged him on WhatsApp to ask about the meeting. 我在 WhatsApp 上给他发了消息问关于会议的事。 |近义词: texted, emailed, contacted | 反义词: N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I message my friends every day. = 我每天都给我朋友发消息。
She messages her boss regularly for updates. = 她定期给老板发消息以获取更新。

一般过去时:
He messaged me last night. = 他昨晚给我发了消息。
They messaged their parents after arriving safely. = 他们安全到达后给父母发了消息。

现在完成时:
I have messaged him twice, but he hasn't replied. = 我给他发了2次消息，但他还没回复。
She has messaged her colleagues about the project update. = 她已经给她的同事发了项目更新的消息。

过去完成时:
I had messaged her before the meeting started. = 会议开始之前我给她发了消息。
He had messaged his friends before he left for the trip. = 他出发旅行前已经给他的朋友们发了消息。

将来时:
I will message you when I arrive. = 我到了会给你发消息。
She will message him as soon as she gets the information. = 她一拿到信息就会给他发消息。

虚拟语气:
If I had his number, I would message him. = 如果我有他的号码，我会给他发消息。
I wish I could message him, but I don't have his contact information. = 我真希望我能给他发消息，但是我没有他的联系方式。

被动语态:
He was messaged by his mother about the family dinner. = 他收到他母亲关于家庭晚餐的消息。
The information was messaged to all employees yesterday. = 信息昨天发送给了所有员工。

疑问句:

Have they messaged you yet? = 他们给你发消息了吗？

否定句:
