pyrophone:/ˈpaɪrəfoʊn/ | n. |火声器；发声火器|A pyrophone is a musical instrument that produces sounds by igniting a mixture of gas and air.  The varying pressure of the explosions creates different musical notes.  It's also sometimes referred to as a flame organ. |Example: The concert featured an unusual instrument, a pyrophone, whose fiery melodies captivated the audience.  The inventor demonstrated his new pyrophone to a small group of investors.|近义词: flame organ |反义词: None readily available.
