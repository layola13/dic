pivots:/ˈpɪvəts/ | n. v. |支点；枢轴；中心点；关键；(使)旋转；(使)转变；|英文解析：A pivot is a central point, person, or thing on which something else turns or depends. As a verb, it means to turn or change direction. |例句：The company's success pivots on its new marketing strategy. 公司的成功取决于其新的营销策略。The dancer pivoted gracefully on one foot. 舞蹈演员优雅地单脚旋转。 |近义词：turn, rotate, revolve, hinge, fulcrum |反义词：remain static, stay fixed


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The door pivots smoothly on its hinges. = 门在其铰链上平稳地旋转。
The economy pivots around the tourism industry. = 经济围绕旅游业运转。

一般过去时:
He pivoted quickly to avoid the oncoming traffic. = 他迅速转身以避开迎面而来的车辆。
The company pivoted its strategy after the market downturn. = 市场低迷后，公司调整了战略。

现在完成时:
The company has pivoted its marketing efforts to focus on social media. = 公司已将其营销工作转向专注于社交媒体。
The government has pivoted its policy on immigration. = 政府已经改变了其移民政策。

过去完成时:
They had pivoted their business model several times before finding success. = 在获得成功之前，他们已经几次改变了他们的商业模式。
The team had pivoted its approach after realizing their initial strategy was flawed. = 团队在意识到其最初的策略有缺陷后改变了其方法。

将来时:
The company will pivot to a new market next year. = 公司明年将转向一个新的市场。
We will pivot our resources to address the urgent issue. = 我们将调整资源来解决紧急问题。

虚拟语气:
If the company had pivoted earlier, it might have avoided bankruptcy. = 如果公司早点转型，它可能就能避免破产。
I wish I had pivoted my career path sooner. = 我希望我早点改变我的职业道路。

被动语态:
The decision was pivoted on the results of the survey. = 该决定取决于调查结果。
The marketing campaign was pivoted to attract a younger demographic. = 营销活动进行了调整以吸引更年轻的人群。

疑问句:
Does the success of the project pivot on funding? = 项目的成功是否取决于资金？
Will the company pivot its strategy in response to the competition? = 公司是否会根据竞争对手的情况调整战略？

否定句:
The company did not pivot its strategy despite the falling sales. = 尽管销售额下降，公司并没有调整其战略。
The plan did not pivot towards sustainability.  = 计划没有转向可持续发展。
