The word is now largely archaic or used in literary contexts.|例句：The child was chidden for his bad behavior.  孩子因其不良行为而受到责备。|例句：Having been chidden by her mother, the girl quietly went to her room.  被母亲责备后，女孩静静地回到了自己的房间。|近义词：rebuke, scold, reprimand, reproach|反义词：praise, commend
