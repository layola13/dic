chrysalis: /ˈkrɪsəlɪs/ | n. |蛹；茧 | The chrysalis is the pupa stage of a butterfly, a resting stage between the larva and the adult.  A chrysalis is the stage in which a butterfly undergoes metamorphosis. The butterfly emerges from the chrysalis.  | 近义词：pupa | 反义词：无
