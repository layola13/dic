paleotechnic: /ˌpeɪliːoʊˈtɛknɪk/ | adj. | 古代技术的；旧技术的 | Relating to or characteristic of ancient technology or techniques.  The term combines "paleo" (ancient) and "technic" (technical or relating to technology). | 例句：The paleotechnic methods of irrigation were surprisingly effective. 古代的灌溉方法出奇地有效。

The museum showcased paleotechnic tools used by early civilizations. 博物馆展出了早期文明使用的古代工具。


近义词：archaic, antique, primitive, old-fashioned

反义词：modern, advanced, contemporary, futuristic


词性:形容词
