USAGE->

There is no verb conjugation for adverbs. Adverbs modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs.  The usage examples below show how "schematically" modifies the verb describing the action of presenting or explaining something.

Example 1:
The professor explained the complex theory schematically. 教授以图解的方式解释了复杂的理论。

Example 2:
The architect presented the building's design schematically. 建筑师以简图的方式展示了建筑的设计。

PHRASE->

There are no common phrases specifically using "schematically".  The word is used independently to describe the *manner* of presentation.  However, it frequently appears in phrases like:

schematically represented  图解表示
schematically shown  图示显示
schematically illustrated 图示说明
schematically described 概要描述
