orienter:/ɔːriˈentər/ | vt. | 使适应；确定…的方向；定向；使朝向 | To cause (someone or something) to become familiar with a new situation or environment;  To determine the position of (someone or something) in relation to the points of the compass or other fixed bearings; To adjust the direction or position of (something) | 例句：The program is designed to orient new employees to the company culture.  该计划旨在使新员工适应公司文化。  The map helped us orient ourselves in the unfamiliar city. 这张地图帮助我们在陌生的城市中确定方向。| 近义词：adapt, adjust, acclimate, guide, direct | 反义词：disorient, confuse, mislead | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He orients himself to the new job. = 他适应新的工作。
The compass orients the traveler. = 指南针为旅行者定向。


一般过去时:
She oriented herself quickly to the new environment. = 她很快适应了新的环境。
The captain oriented the ship towards the harbor. = 船长将船朝港口方向行驶。


现在完成时:
They have oriented themselves to the challenges of the project. = 他们已经适应了项目的挑战。
The hikers have oriented themselves using the sun. = 徒步旅行者用太阳确定了方向。


过去完成时:
He had oriented himself to the company's policies before starting work. = 他在开始工作之前已经熟悉了公司的政策。
The explorers had oriented themselves using the stars before setting out. = 探险家们出发前已利用星辰确定了方向。


将来时:
We will orient the new recruits tomorrow. = 我们明天将对新兵进行定向训练。
She will orient herself using the map. = 她将用地图确定方向。


虚拟语气:
If I had oriented myself better, I wouldn't have gotten lost. = 如果我方向感更好一些，我就不会迷路了。
I wish I had oriented myself before entering the building. = 我希望我在进入建筑物之前已经确定了方向。


被动语态:
The students were oriented to the school's rules. = 学生们接受了学校规章制度的培训。
The telescope was oriented towards the distant galaxy. = 望远镜被对准了遥远的星系。


疑问句:
How do you orient yourself in a new city? = 你如何在新的城市中确定方向？
Are the new employees being properly oriented? = 新员工正在接受适当的培训吗？


否定句:
He didn't orient himself properly and got lost. = 他没有正确地辨别方向，结果迷路了。
The pilot failed to orient the plane correctly. = 飞行员未能正确地操纵飞机方向。


PHRASE->
