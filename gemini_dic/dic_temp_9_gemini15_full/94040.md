imparted:/ɪmˈpɑːrtɪd/ | vt. | 传授；告知；赋予 | To impart something means to communicate it, share it, or bestow it upon someone else.  It often implies a transfer of knowledge, information, or a quality. | 例句：The teacher imparted her wisdom to her students.  老师把她的智慧传授给学生们。

The artist imparted a sense of mystery to the painting. 艺术家给这幅画赋予了一种神秘感。


近义词：communicate, convey, bestow, transmit, confer | 反义词：withhold, conceal, suppress | 词性：动词


USAGE->

The teacher imparts knowledge to her students. = 老师传授知识给她的学生们。
He imparts wisdom through his writings. = 他通过写作传授智慧。
