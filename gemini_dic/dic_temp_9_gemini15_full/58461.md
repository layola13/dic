ductilities:/dʌkˈtɪlətiz/| n.|展性；延展性|The ability of a solid material to undergo significant plastic deformation before rupture.  This property is important in many engineering applications, allowing materials to be drawn into wires or shaped into complex forms. |例句：The ductilities of different metals vary greatly.  不同金属的延展性差异很大。

例句：High ductility is essential for the manufacturing of thin wires. 高延展性对于细线的制造至关重要。


近义词：malleability, plasticity, workability


反义词：brittleness, fragility


词性:名词
