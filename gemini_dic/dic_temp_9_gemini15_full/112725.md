loyalties: /ˈlɔɪəltiz/ | n. |忠诚；忠心；忠贞 |  The feeling of being loyal to someone or something.  It often refers to a strong feeling of support and allegiance.  |例句：His loyalties lay with his family, not his company. 他的忠诚在于他的家人，而不是他的公司。

例句：Her unwavering loyalties to her friends were admirable. 她对朋友始终如一的忠诚令人钦佩。


近义词：allegiances, faithfulness, devotion, fidelity, commitment | 反义词：treachery, betrayal, disloyalty


词性: 名词
