parasols:/ˈpærəsɔːlz/| n. |阳伞；遮阳伞|A parasol is a lightweight umbrella used to provide shade from the sun.  It is typically smaller and more decorative than a rain umbrella.|She carried a pretty parasol to protect herself from the strong sun. 她拿着一把漂亮的阳伞来遮挡强烈的阳光。|umbrella, sunshade|raincoat (in terms of opposite function)|名词
