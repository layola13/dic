collidine:/ˈkɒlɪdiːn/| n. |2,4,6-甲基吡啶；3,5-二甲基吡啶|A heterocyclic aromatic organic compound, a type of pyridine with three methyl groups attached to the pyridine ring.  There are several isomers depending on the position of the methyl groups. The most common is 2,4,6-collidine. |例句：The researchers synthesized 2,4,6-collidine for use in their experiment. 研究人员合成了2,4,6-甲基吡啶用于他们的实验。

例句：The presence of collidine was detected in the sample. 样本中检测到甲基吡啶的存在。


近义词：2,4,6-trimethylpyridine (if referring specifically to the 2,4,6 isomer),  symmetrical collidine(if referring specifically to the 2,4,6 isomer)
反义词：无


词性:n.
