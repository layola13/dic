preservationisms:/ˌprɛzərˈveɪʃənɪzəm/ | n. | 保护主义；保存主义 | The ideologies and practices associated with the preservation of natural resources, cultural heritage, or historical artifacts.  It often involves active intervention to maintain or restore something in its original state.  Different preservationist movements may emphasize different aspects, such as ecological integrity, historical authenticity, or aesthetic value. | The preservationist movement successfully lobbied for the creation of national parks.  = 保护主义运动成功游说建立国家公园。  The museum’s preservationist efforts ensured the painting's survival. = 博物馆的保护工作确保了这幅画的保存。 | conservationism, conservation | exploitation, destruction |  名词
