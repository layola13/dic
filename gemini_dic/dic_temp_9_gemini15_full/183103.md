reporting:/rɪˈpɔːrtɪŋ/| n. |报告；报道；汇报|The act of giving a detailed account of an event, situation, or occurrence.  It can refer to the process of conveying information, or the information itself.|例句：The police are preparing a report on the accident. 警方正在准备一份关于事故的报告。|例句：Her reporting on the war won her several awards. 她关于战争的报道为她赢得了多个奖项。|近义词：account, narration, coverage, news|反义词：concealment, suppression, secrecy|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is reporting record profits this year. = 公司今年报告创纪录的利润。
She regularly reports to her supervisor. = 她定期向她的主管汇报。


一般过去时:
He reported the crime to the police immediately. = 他立即向警方报案。
The journalist reported on the election results. = 记者报道了选举结果。


现在完成时:
They have reported the findings of their research. = 他们已经报告了他们的研究结果。
She has not yet reported for duty. = 她尚未上班。


过去完成时:
The witness had already reported seeing the suspect. = 目击者已经报告说看到嫌疑人了。
Before the meeting, they had reported their progress to the manager. = 会议前，他们已向经理汇报了进度。


将来时:
We will report our findings at the conference. = 我们将在会议上报告我们的发现。
The team will report back next week. = 团队将在下周汇报。


虚拟语气:
If I had known, I would have reported it immediately. = 如果我知道的话，我会立即报告。
It is essential that he report the incident to his superiors. = 他必须向他的上司报告这一事件。


被动语态:
The incident was reported to the authorities. = 此事件已向当局报告。
The news was widely reported in the media. = 这条新闻在媒体上广泛报道。


疑问句:
Have you reported the problem yet? = 你已经报告这个问题了吗？
What did the investigation report find? = 调查报告发现了什么？


否定句:
He did not report the incident. = 他没有报告此事。
They failed to report the damage. = 他们未能报告损坏情况。
