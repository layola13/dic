nonvillager: /ˈnɑːnˌvɪlədʒər/ | n. | 非村民；村外人 | A person who does not live in a village.  This word is formed by combining "non-" (meaning "not") with "villager." It describes someone who resides outside of a village or rural community. | Example: The nonvillagers were surprised by the village's unique customs.  非村民们对村庄独特的习俗感到惊讶。 | 近义词: outsider, townsperson (if referring to someone from a town), city dweller (if referring to someone from a city) | 反义词: villager, resident (of a village)
