dandy's:/ˈdændiz/| n.| 花花公子；漂亮的男子；（尤指男子的）时髦服装；|A dandy is a man who gives a lot of attention to his clothes and appearance.  The term often implies a degree of affectation or foppishness.  The word "dandy's" is the possessive form of "dandy," indicating ownership or association.  For example, "the dandy's clothes" refers to the clothes belonging to or associated with a dandy.|例句：The dandy's cravat was exquisitely knotted.  那位花花公子的领结系得非常精致。
The dandy's flamboyant attire drew many admiring glances. 那位花花公子华丽的服装吸引了许多羡慕的目光。|近义词：fop, beau, dude,swell |反义词：sloven, slob
