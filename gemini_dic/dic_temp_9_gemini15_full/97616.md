inharmonical:/ˌɪnhɑːrˈmɒnɪkəl/| adj. |不和谐的，不协调的 |Lacking harmony; discordant.  It refers to a lack of agreement or consistency, often in sounds, colors, or ideas.  |例句：The colors in the painting were inharmonical, clashing violently with each other.  这幅画的颜色很不和谐，彼此之间强烈冲突。

The musical arrangement sounded inharmonical due to the clashing notes.  由于音符的冲突，这首乐曲的编排听起来很不和谐。


近义词：discordant, dissonant, incongruous, unharmonious |反义词：harmonious, concordant, consonant
