irritable:/ˈɪrɪtəbl/| adj.|易怒的，烦躁的，过敏的|easily annoyed or made angry.|He's been irritable all day. 他今天一整天都脾气暴躁。|近义词：grumpy, choleric, testy|反义词：calm, placid, patient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is irritable today. = 他今天很烦躁。
She is irritable when she's hungry. = 她饿的时候很烦躁。


一般过去时:
He was irritable yesterday. = 他昨天很烦躁。
She was irritable after the long flight. = 长途飞行后她很烦躁。


现在完成时:
He has been irritable all week. = 他整个星期都很烦躁。
She has been irritable since she lost her job. = 自从她失业以来，她就一直很烦躁。


过去完成时:
He had been irritable for days before he finally snapped. = 在他最终爆发之前，他已经烦躁了好几天了。
She had been irritable all morning before the meeting. = 会议前，她一上午都很烦躁。


将来时:
He will be irritable if he doesn't get enough sleep. = 如果他睡不够，他会很烦躁。
She will probably be irritable after the exam. = 考试后她可能会很烦躁。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't so irritable, we could get along better. = 如果他不那么烦躁，我们就能相处得更好。
I wish he weren't so irritable. = 我希望他不那么烦躁。


被动语态:  (形容词没有被动语态)


疑问句:
Is he irritable today? = 他今天脾气暴躁吗？
Is she usually so irritable? = 她通常这么暴躁吗？


否定句:
He is not irritable usually. = 他通常不暴躁。
She wasn't irritable at all this morning. = 今天早上她一点也不暴躁。
