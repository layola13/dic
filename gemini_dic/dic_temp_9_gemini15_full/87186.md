herdsman: /ˈhɜːrdzmən/ | n. | 牧人，牧羊人 | A person who takes care of a herd of animals, especially sheep or cattle. | 例句：The herdsman guided his flock across the mountain. 牧人带领他的羊群越过山脉。  The herdsman skillfully herded the cattle into the pen. 牧人熟练地将牛群赶进围栏。| 近义词：shepherd, drover, stockman | 反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with someone who doesn't work with livestock.
