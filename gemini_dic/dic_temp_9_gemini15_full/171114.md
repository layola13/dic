psychiatrists:/saɪˈkaɪətrɪsts/ | n. | 精神科医生 | Psychiatrists are medical doctors who specialize in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of mental illnesses.  They often work with patients experiencing a wide range of conditions, from depression and anxiety to schizophrenia and bipolar disorder. | 例句：Many psychiatrists prescribe medication as part of their treatment plans.  许多精神科医生在治疗计划中开药。

Many psychiatrists use a combination of therapy and medication to help their patients.  许多精神科医生结合治疗和药物来帮助他们的病人。


近义词：psychologists (in some contexts), psychotherapists | 反义词：N/A | 词性:noun
