nuggety:/ˈnʌɡɪti/| adj. |块状的；金块似的；结实的；矮胖的|  Characterized by or resembling nuggets; compact and solid.  The word evokes the image of something small, rounded, and potentially valuable, like a gold nugget.  It can also describe a person or object as being stout or stocky.|

例句：

The nuggety gold was difficult to extract from the rock. =  这些块状的金子很难从岩石中提取出来。

He was a nuggety, powerfully built man. = 他是一个矮胖结实的男人。


近义词：chunky, compact, stocky, sturdy

反义词：slender, thin, frail, delicate

词性：形容词
