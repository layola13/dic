curtseying: /ˈkɜːtsiɪŋ/ | v. | 行屈膝礼；屈膝致敬 |  The act of making a curtsy; a graceful lowering of the body by bending the knees.  It is typically performed by women as a gesture of respect or politeness. |

例句：

The young girl curtseying before the queen.  年轻女孩向女王行屈膝礼。

She curtseying to show her appreciation. 她屈膝致谢。


近义词：bobbing, dipping (informal),  kneeling (more formal and profound)


反义词：standing rigidly, bowing (men's equivalent)


词性：动词 (present participle of curtsy)
