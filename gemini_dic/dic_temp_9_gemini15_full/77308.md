gates:/ɡeɪts/ | n. |大门；门；闸门；水门 |The word "gates" refers to a structure, typically made of a framework and panels, that can be opened and closed to control access to an area.  It can be a single door or a pair of doors that swing open.  The term also applies to similar structures in other contexts, such as those controlling water flow.  |例句：The school gates were locked at 5 pm. 学校大门在下午5点锁上了。

The iron gates creaked open. 铁门吱呀一声开了。|近义词：doors, entrances, portals |反义词：exits, openings (in the sense of an unobstructed passage)
