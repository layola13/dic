reexpansion:/ˌriːɪkˈspænʃən/| n.|重新扩张，再膨胀|The act or process of expanding again after having been compressed or contracted.  It often refers to the restoration of a collapsed lung or other organ to its normal size and function.|例句：The reexpansion of the collapsed lung was successful. 塌陷肺的重新扩张是成功的。
The patient underwent a procedure to promote reexpansion of the lung. 病人接受了促进肺部重新扩张的手术。|近义词：expansion, inflation, resurgence|反义词：collapse, compression, deflation|名词
