capableness:/keɪˈpeɪbəlnəs/ | n. |能力；才能；胜任；可能性 |The ability to do something; potential.  It refers to the possession of the necessary skills, qualities, or resources to accomplish a task or achieve a goal. |例句：Her capableness in handling complex projects is impressive.  她的处理复杂项目的能力令人印象深刻。

例句：The team's capableness was evident in their swift completion of the task.  团队的能力在他们快速完成任务中显而易见。


近义词：ability, capacity, competence, capability, potential, proficiency |反义词：inability, incompetence, incapacity


词源:  Derived from the adjective "capable," which ultimately comes from the Old French "capable," meaning "having capacity."  The suffix "-ness" is added to form the noun, indicating a state or quality.  The root can be traced back to the Latin "capax," meaning "capable of holding," "spacious," or "roomy."
