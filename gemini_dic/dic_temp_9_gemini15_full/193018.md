seconders:/ˈsɛkəndərz/| n.|支持者；附议者|Those who support or endorse a proposal or motion.  They formally agree to a motion or proposition already made by another person.  |例句：The motion was passed, with several seconders speaking in its favor.  该动议获得通过，几位附议者也为此发言。|例句：Several council members acted as seconders to the mayor's proposal.  几位议员附议了市长的提案。|近义词：supporters, endorsers, proponents|反义词：opponents, objectors|名词
