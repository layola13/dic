error:/ˈɛrər/| n.|错误，差错，误差|A mistake or inaccuracy; something that is wrong.  It can refer to a flaw in reasoning, a factual inaccuracy, a technical malfunction, or a deviation from a standard.|例句：There was an error in the calculation. 计算中出现了一个错误。|例句：The software has a known error that causes it to crash. 该软件有一个已知的错误，会导致它崩溃。|近义词：mistake, fault, blunder, flaw, defect|反义词：accuracy, correctness, precision


USAGE->
一般现在时:
There is an error in the system. = 系统中有一个错误。
A grammatical error often leads to misunderstanding. = 语法错误常常导致误解。


一般过去时:
There was an error in the database. = 数据库中出现了一个错误。

现在完成时:
There have been several errors in this report. = 这份报告中出现了几处错误。
I have made an error in my calculations. = 我的计算中出现了一个错误。


过去完成时:

We had not noticed the error until it was too late. = 直到为时已晚我们才注意到这个错误。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

否定句:
