plunderers:/ˈplʌndərərz/| n. |掠夺者，抢劫者 |People who plunder or loot.  They steal goods, especially during war or violence. |例句：The plunderers ransacked the village, leaving behind only destruction. 掠夺者洗劫了村庄，只留下满目疮痍。

例句：The invading army consisted mainly of brutal plunderers.入侵军队主要由残暴的掠夺者组成。


近义词：looters, raiders, marauders, pillagers

反义词：benefactors, protectors, saviors


词性: 名词
