muffler:/ˈmʌflər/| n. |消音器；围巾；（尤指旧时）暖手筒 |A muffler is a device fitted to the exhaust pipe of an internal combustion engine to reduce the noise of the exhaust gases.  It can also refer to a scarf worn around the neck to keep warm, or an older-fashioned type of hand warmer. |例句：The car's muffler was damaged in the accident.  这辆车的消音器在事故中受损了。 The old lady wore a thick wool muffler around her neck.  这位老太太在脖子上围了一条厚厚的羊毛围巾。|近义词：silencer (for exhaust), scarf, shawl |反义词：amplifier (opposite function)
