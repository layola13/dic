/ˌaʊtˈdɛvəl/ | vt. |胜过；技高一筹；比…更邪恶 | To surpass or exceed in wickedness or evil; to outdo in devilish actions or qualities.  | The villain's cunning outdeviled even the seasoned detective.  反派人物的狡猾连经验丰富的侦探都比不过。| 近义词：outsmart, outwit, surpass, exceed | 反义词：be outdone by, be inferior to | 动词
