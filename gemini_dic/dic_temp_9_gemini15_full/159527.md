politicalization: /ˌpɒlɪtɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌpɑːlətɪsəˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |政治化 |The act of giving a political character to something; the process of something becoming political.  The process of something becoming subject to political influence or control. |例句：The increasing politicalization of the media is a cause for concern.  媒体日益政治化令人担忧。

例句：The environmental movement has seen a significant degree of politicalization in recent years. 近年来，环保运动经历了相当程度的政治化。


近义词：politicization, politicizing


反义词：depoliticization


词性：名词
