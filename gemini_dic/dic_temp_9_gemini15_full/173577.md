quasi-ignorantly:/ˈkwɑːzaɪ ˈɪɡnərəntli/ | adv. |近乎无知地，似懂非懂地 |  Describes an action or state of being performed or existing with a lack of full understanding or awareness; suggesting a partial or superficial knowledge.  It implies a degree of ignorance, but not complete obliviousness. |例句：He answered the question quasi-ignorantly, mumbling something about the subject. 他似懂非懂地回答了这个问题，含糊不清地说了些什么关于这个主题。 He approached the complex problem quasi-ignorantly, lacking the necessary expertise. 他近乎无知地处理这个复杂的问题，缺乏必要的专业知识。|近义词：uncertainly, vaguely, blindly, cluelessly |反义词：knowingly, intelligently, expertly, consciously |词性:副词
