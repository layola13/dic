gazel:/ɡəˈzɛl/| n. |瞪羚|A small, slender antelope with long, elegant legs and large, expressive eyes.  Gazelles are known for their graceful movements and speed.|The gazelle leaped across the savanna with effortless grace.|近义词：antelope, springbok|反义词：无
