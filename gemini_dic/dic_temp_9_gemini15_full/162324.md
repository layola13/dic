preaccustoming:/ˌpriːəˈkʌstəmɪŋ/| v.|预先习惯；预先适应|The process of gradually becoming accustomed to something beforehand.  It implies a proactive and anticipatory adaptation to a future situation or condition. |例句：Preaccustoming yourself to the cold weather will make your winter trip more enjoyable. 预先适应寒冷的天气会让你的冬季旅行更愉快。

The company's preaccustoming its employees to the new software before its official launch. 公司在正式发布新软件之前让员工预先适应。|近义词：preparing, conditioning, acclimating|反义词：surprising, shocking, unprepared
