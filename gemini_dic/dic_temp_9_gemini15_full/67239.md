expugner:/ɪkˈspʌɡnər/ | vt. |攻克；征服；攻破 | To overcome or conquer (a place, etc.) by force.  |例句：The army expugned the enemy fortress.  军队攻克了敌人的堡垒。|例句：They expugned the city after a long siege.  经过长期围攻后，他们攻下了这座城市。|近义词：conquer, subdue, vanquish |反义词：surrender, yield, cede |动词
