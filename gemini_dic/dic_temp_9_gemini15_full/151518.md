parse:/pɑːrs/| /pɑːrs/| vt.| 解析；分析；理解；解释 | To examine a sentence, text, etc. and determine the grammatical relationships of its components. To separate something into its constituent elements. To understand the meaning of something. |例句：The students parsed the sentence to identify the subject and verb. 学生们分析句子以识别主语和谓语。

例句：The computer program parsed the data into useful categories.  计算机程序将数据解析成有用的类别。


近义词：analyze, interpret, explain, dissect, break down | 反义词：synthesize, combine, obfuscate | 动词
