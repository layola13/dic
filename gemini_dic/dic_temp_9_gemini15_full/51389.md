determinating:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |决定性的，确定的，最终的 |  Having the power or tendency to decide something decisively.  It emphasizes the decisive and often irreversible nature of the outcome.|  The determining factor in his success was his perseverance.  他的成功决定性因素是他的毅力。

The determining factor in the election was the economy.  这次选举的决定性因素是经济。


近义词：decisive, crucial, definitive, conclusive | 反义词：inconclusive, uncertain, insignificant | 词性：形容词
