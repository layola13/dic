enfranchised:/ɪnˈfrænˌtʃaɪzd/| adj. |赋予特权的；享有公民权的；获得解放的 |Given the right to vote; granted freedom from oppression or hardship.  |例句：The newly enfranchised citizens celebrated their right to vote. 新获得选举权的公民庆祝他们投票的权利。

The enfranchised population rejoiced at the end of the war. 获得解放的人民在战争结束时欢欣鼓舞。|近义词：empowered, liberated, freed |反义词：disenfranchised, oppressed, enslaved
