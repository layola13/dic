repunctuation: /ˌriːpʌŋktʃuˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 重新标点 | The act of adding or changing punctuation marks in a text.  It often involves correcting errors, improving clarity, or altering the emphasis of a sentence or passage. Repunctuation can be a significant part of editing and proofreading. | Example: The repunctuation of the manuscript improved its readability considerably.  重新排版手稿大大提高了它的可读性。|近义词:re-punctuation, editing, proofreading | 反义词:None
