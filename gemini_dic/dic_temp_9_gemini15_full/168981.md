profits:/ˈprɒfɪts/| n.|利润，盈利|The financial gain resulting from a business activity after all expenses have been deducted.  Profits are the difference between revenue and costs.|例句：The company's profits increased by 10% last year.  去年这家公司的利润增长了10%。|例句：They reinvested their profits into new equipment.  他们把利润再投资到新设备上。|近义词：earnings, gains, returns|反义词：losses, deficits


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company makes large profits this year. = 今年公司获得了巨额利润。
Our small business earns modest profits each month. = 我们的小企业每月赚取适度的利润。

一般过去时:
The factory reported high profits last quarter. = 工厂上个季度报告利润很高。
The restaurant made little profit during the pandemic. = 在疫情期间，这家餐馆的利润微薄。

现在完成时:
We have made significant profits since launching the new product. = 自从推出新产品以来，我们已经获得了可观的利润。
The company has consistently reported strong profits over the past decade. = 在过去的十年里，该公司始终报告强劲的利润。

过去完成时:
They had already made a substantial profit before the market crashed. = 在市场崩溃之前，他们已经赚取了可观的利润。
The investors had lost their initial investment before they saw any profits. = 投资者在看到任何利润之前就已经损失了他们的初始投资。

将来时:
Analysts predict the company will generate even higher profits next year. = 分析师预测该公司明年将获得更高的利润。
We expect to see increased profits once the new marketing campaign launches. = 一旦新的营销活动启动，我们预计将看到利润增加。

虚拟语气:
If we had invested earlier, we would have made greater profits. = 如果我们早点投资，我们会获得更大的利润。
I wish we had seen such profits last year. = 我真希望去年也能看到这样的利润。

被动语态:
Large profits were reported by the corporation last year. = 去年公司报告了巨额利润。
Profits are being closely monitored by the management team. = 利润正受到管理团队的密切监控。

疑问句:
What were the company's profits last year? = 公司去年的利润是多少？
How much profit did the project generate? = 这个项目产生了多少利润？

否定句:
The business didn't make a profit this month. = 这个企业这个月没有盈利。
The investment didn't yield any profits. = 这项投资没有产生任何利润。
