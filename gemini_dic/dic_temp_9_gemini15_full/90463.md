hubba:/ˈhʌbə/| int.|  嗯，哎哟，(表示兴奋或赞赏)|An exclamation expressing excitement, pleasure, or approval; often used informally.|例句：Hubba hubba! That's a gorgeous car! 哇！那辆车真漂亮！|近义词：wow, wowzer,  ooh-la-la|反义词:  (无明确反义词，取决于语境)|感叹词


USAGE->
This word is primarily an interjection and doesn't have tenses or voices like verbs.  It's used to express a feeling, not to describe an action. Therefore, typical grammatical structures don't apply.


PHRASE->
Hubba hubba: (A repeated expression intensifying the feeling of excitement or admiration)
Hubba, hubba, hubba!: (Further intensifying the expression)
Look at that hubba hubba!: (Using the word to describe something attractive)
That's some hubba hubba!: (Expressing strong approval)
Man, what a hubba hubba!: (Expressing strong excitement or approval in a more informal setting)
