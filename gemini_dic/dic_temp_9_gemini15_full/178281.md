reconceived:/ˌriːkənˈsiːvd/| vt.|重新构想，重新设想|To think about or plan something again, often in a different way.|例句：The project was reconceived after the initial failure.  最初的失败后，项目被重新构想。|例句：The architects reconceived the building's design to incorporate more natural light. 建筑师重新设计了建筑，以融入更多自然光。|近义词：reimagine, rethink, revamp|反义词：maintain, preserve|动词


USAGE->

They reconceive the marketing strategy every year. = 他们每年都会重新构想营销策略。
We reconceive our approach to the problem. = 我们重新构想我们解决问题的方法。
