dilettantisms:/ˌdɪləˈtæntɪzəm/| n.|业余爱好者的作品；半瓶醋；浅尝辄止|The exhibition showcased a collection of charming dilettantisms, reflecting the artist's playful exploration of various styles.  这次展览展示了一系列迷人的业余作品，反映了艺术家对各种风格的轻松探索。|近义词：amateurishness, superficiality, dabblings|反义词：expertise, professionalism, mastery|名词


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate like verbs.  Therefore, tense and voice examples are not applicable.

例句1: His paintings, while enjoyable, revealed a certain dilettantism; a lack of serious training was evident. 他的画虽然赏心悦目，但却流露出某种业余气息；缺乏正规训练是显而易见的。

例句2: The critic dismissed the writer's latest work as mere dilettantism, lacking depth and originality. 批评家认为这位作家最新的作品不过是一种浅尝辄止的业余之作，缺乏深度和原创性。
