reconcentrated: /ˌriːˈkɒnsənˌtreɪtɪd/ | adj. |重新集中的；重新浓缩的 |  Having been concentrated again;  having undergone a second concentration.  The term implies a previous concentration that has been further intensified or refined. |


例句：

The reconcentrated orange juice had a richer flavor. = 重新浓缩的橙汁味道更浓郁。

The reconcentrated efforts yielded significant results. = 重新集中的努力取得了显著成果。


近义词：re-focused, intensified, strengthened

反义词：diluted, dispersed, weakened

词性：形容词
