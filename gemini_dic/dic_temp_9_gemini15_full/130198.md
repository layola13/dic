non-scientist: /ˌnɑːnˈsaɪəntɪst/ | n. |非科学家 | A person who is not a scientist; someone lacking scientific training or expertise.  | Example: The article was written in a way that was easily understandable for the average non-scientist. 这篇文章的写作方式易于普通非科学家理解。 |近义词：layperson, amateur, layperson, nonprofessional |反义词：scientist, expert, professional
