porpoise:/ˈpɔːrpəs/| n. |海豚|A porpoise is a small toothed whale that is found in most oceans of the world.  They are known for their intelligence and playful nature.  Porpoises are often mistaken for dolphins, but they have smaller, more rounded bodies and shorter snouts.| Example: We saw a pod of porpoises leaping and playing in the waves. 我们看到一群海豚在海浪中跳跃嬉戏。|近义词：dolphin (though technically different)|反义词：None.
