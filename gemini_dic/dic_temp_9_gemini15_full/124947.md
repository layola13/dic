muffins:/ˈmʌfɪnz/| n.|松饼|A small, sweet bread, often baked in a muffin tin, sometimes containing fruit, chocolate chips, or nuts.  Muffins are typically quick breads, meaning they don't require a long rising time.|例句：I baked a batch of blueberry muffins this morning.  今天早上我烤了一批蓝莓松饼。|例句：These corn muffins are delicious with butter. 这些玉米松饼配黄油很好吃。|近义词：cupcake, quick bread|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with  a loaf of bread or a cake. |名词
