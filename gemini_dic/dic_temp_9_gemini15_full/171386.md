puck-like:/pʌkˌlaɪk/| adj.|像冰球的；像顽童的|Resembling a puck, a flat, round disc used in ice hockey;  can also refer to something mischievous or playful, like a puckish elf.|例句：The small, flat stone was puck-like in shape. 这块小而扁平的石头形状像冰球。|The child had a puck-like grin on his face. 孩子脸上露出了淘气般的笑容。|近义词：disc-shaped, mischievous, playful, impish|反义词：serious, solemn, sedate
