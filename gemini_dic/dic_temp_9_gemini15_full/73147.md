foray:/fɔːreɪ/| n. v.|突袭；尝试；涉足|A foray is a sudden attack or raid, especially one made by a small group into enemy territory. It can also refer to a brief and often enthusiastic attempt to do something new or different. |例句：The company made a foray into the online market. 这家公司进军了在线市场。

The knights made a daring foray into enemy territory.  骑士们大胆地突袭了敌人的领地。


近义词：raid, incursion, attempt, venture|反义词：retreat, withdrawal|词性:Noun, Verb
