outwhirling: /ˌaʊtˈhwɜːrlɪŋ/ | v. |  超越；旋转超越；超过旋转 |  To surpass or exceed in whirling or rotating motion; to whirl or rotate faster or more forcefully than something else.  | 例句：The tornado outwhirled all previous storms in the area.  龙卷风超越了该地区所有之前的风暴。

The dancer's rapid spins outwhirled the swirling dervishes. 舞者的快速旋转超过了旋转的苦行僧。


近义词：outspin, outrotate, surpass, exceed | 反义词：underwhelm (in terms of rotational force)

词源:  The word "outwhirling" is a combination of the prefix "out-" (meaning "to a greater extent than") and the verb "whirling" (meaning "to rotate rapidly").  Therefore, it's a relatively modern word formed through compounding.
