legalize:/ˈliːɡəlaɪz/| vt.|使合法化；准许|To make something legal.  This usually refers to activities or substances that were previously illegal. |例句：The government legalized marijuana in several states.  政府在几个州将大麻合法化了。|例句：They are campaigning to legalize same-sex marriage. 他们在努力争取同性婚姻合法化。|近义词：authorize, permit, sanction |反义词：prohibit, ban, outlaw|动词
