feminizing: /ˈfɛmɪnaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使女性化；使具有女性特征 | To make or become more feminine in appearance or character.  | 例句：The artist was feminizing the male figure in his painting.  艺术家在他的画中使男性形象女性化。  | 例句：He's feminizing his style of dress. 他正在使他的穿着风格女性化。| 近义词：effeminizing, womanizing (in a specific context) | 反义词：masculinizing
