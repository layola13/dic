mayonnaises:/ˌmeɪəˈneɪzɪz/| n. |蛋黄酱（复数）|Mayonnaises are creamy sauces made with egg yolks, oil, and an acid, such as vinegar or lemon juice.  They are commonly used as condiments and in various dishes.|例句：The restaurant offers a variety of mayonnaises, from classic to spicy. 这家餐厅提供各种蛋黄酱，从经典款到辣味款都有。|例句：Many different types of mayonnaises are available in supermarkets. 超市里有很多不同种类的蛋黄酱。|近义词：salad dressings, sauces|反义词：无
