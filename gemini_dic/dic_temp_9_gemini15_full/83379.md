gyrostatic:/ˌdʒaɪroʊˈstætɪk/| adj.|陀螺的；回转的；具有陀螺效应的|Relating to or exhibiting gyroscopic properties;  characterized by the tendency to maintain orientation.  Describes the behavior of a spinning body resisting changes to its orientation.|The gyrostatic effect of the spinning top keeps it upright. 陀螺的回转效应使它保持直立。|The spinning wheel demonstrated gyrostatic stability.旋转的轮子展示了陀螺稳定性。|stable, steady|unstable, unsteady|形容词
