hotly:/ˈhɑːtli/ | adv. | 热烈地；激烈地；生气地 |  intensely; passionately; angrily | 例句：They hotly debated the issue. 他们激烈地争论了这个问题。

例句：He hotly denied the accusations. 他愤怒地否认了这些指控。


近义词：passionately, fervently, vehemently, angrily

反义词：calmly, coolly, mildly, indifferently


词源:  Derived from the adjective "hot," meaning having or producing a high temperature, or intense in feeling.  The adverbial "-ly" suffix is added to create the adverbial form.  The sense of "intensely" or "passionately" developed from the association of heat with strong emotions.
