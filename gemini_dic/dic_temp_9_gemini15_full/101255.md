invoices:/ˈɪnvɔɪsɪz/| n. |发票，账单 |A list of goods sent or services provided, with a statement of the sum due for these; a bill.  |例句：Please send me the invoices for the last three months. 请把过去三个月的发票寄给我。 |例句：We received the invoices and paid them promptly. 我们收到了发票并立即付款。|近义词：bill, statement, account |反义词：receipt (in the sense of a proof of payment)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company issues invoices to its clients monthly. = 公司每月向客户开具发票。
He sends invoices to his customers every week. = 他每周都向客户寄送发票。


一般过去时:
She paid all the invoices last Friday. = 她上周五支付了所有发票。
They received the invoices and checked the details. = 他们收到了发票并仔细核对细节。


现在完成时:
I have already sent the invoices to the client. = 我已经将发票发送给客户了。
We have paid all outstanding invoices. = 我们已经支付了所有未付的发票。


过去完成时:
They had already sent the invoices before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经发送了发票。
The client had paid the invoices before we contacted them. = 在我们联系客户之前，客户已经支付了发票。


将来时:
We will send the invoices at the end of the month. = 我们将在月底发送发票。
She will issue the invoices tomorrow morning. = 她明天早上会开具发票。


虚拟语气:
If I had the invoices, I would pay them immediately. = 如果我有发票，我会立即付款。
I wish I had received the invoices earlier. = 我希望我早点收到发票。


被动语态:
The invoices were sent last week. = 发票是上周寄出的。
All invoices are processed by the accounting department. = 所有发票都由会计部门处理。


疑问句:
Have you received the invoices yet? = 你收到发票了吗？
When will the invoices be sent? = 发票什么时候寄出？


否定句:
We haven't received the invoices yet. = 我们还没收到发票。
He didn't send the invoices on time. = 他没有按时发送发票。


PHRASE->
raise an invoice = 开具发票
issue an invoice = 开具发票
pay an invoice = 付清发票
process an invoice = 处理发票
outstanding invoice = 未付发票
