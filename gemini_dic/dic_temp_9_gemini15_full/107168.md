lager:/ˈlɑːɡər/| n.|淡啤酒|A type of beer that is brewed at low temperatures and is typically characterized by its light color, crisp taste, and lower alcohol content.  It is usually bottom-fermented, meaning the yeast settles at the bottom of the fermentation tank.|例句：He ordered a lager at the pub. 他在酒吧点了一杯淡啤酒。
例句：This lager has a refreshing, slightly bitter taste. 这款淡啤酒口感清爽，略带苦味。|近义词：beer, ale|反义词：stout, ale (in the sense of a heavier, darker beer)
