nondiscretionary: /ˌnɒn.dɪˈskreʃənəri/ /ˌnɑːn.dɪˈskreʃənəri/ | adj. |非自行决定的；强制性的；必须的|

英文解析:  Not left to one's own judgment; mandatory; obligatory.  It implies a lack of choice or freedom in the decision-making process.  The action or expenditure is required or predetermined by rules, regulations, or contracts.

例句:

The nondiscretionary spending on salaries and benefits constitutes a significant portion of the company's budget.  =  工资和福利的强制性支出构成了公司预算的很大一部分。

Nondiscretionary payments, such as mortgage payments, must be prioritized in a tight budget. =  像抵押贷款支付这样的强制性付款，在预算紧张的情况下必须优先考虑。


近义词: mandatory, obligatory, compulsory, required, imperative

反义词: discretionary, optional, voluntary


词性: adjective
