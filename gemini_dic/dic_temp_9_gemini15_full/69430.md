feelings:/ˈfiːlɪŋz/ | n. |感觉，情感 |Feelings are subjective experiences of emotions, sensations, and moods. They can range from joy and love to sadness and anger, and are influenced by both internal and external factors.  |例句：Her feelings were hurt by his harsh words.  她的感情被他刻薄的话语伤害了。

例句：I have mixed feelings about the upcoming trip.  我对即将到来的旅行有复杂的心情。


近义词：emotions, sensations, sentiments, affections | 反义词：N/A (feelings are not typically expressed as having direct opposites;  instead, they exist on a spectrum) | 词性：名词
