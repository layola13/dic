chancy:/ˈtʃɑːnsi/| adj.|冒险的；不确定的；靠不住的|Depending on chance; risky; uncertain.  It implies a significant element of risk or unpredictability, suggesting the outcome is far from certain. |例句：The weather forecast is chancy; it might rain. 天气预报不可靠；可能会下雨。

例句：Investing in the stock market is a chancy business. 投资股市是一项冒险的生意。
