pharmacodynamics:/ˌfɑːrməkəʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/| n. |药物动力学|The study of the biochemical and physiological effects of drugs and their mechanisms of action.  It examines what the drug does to the body. |Example: Understanding the pharmacodynamics of a drug is crucial for determining its efficacy and safety. 了解药物的药效学对于确定其疗效和安全性至关重要。|近义词：drug action, mechanism of action |反义词：pharmacokinetics
