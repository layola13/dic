scarcer:/ˈskɛrsər/| adj.| 稀少的，缺乏的，不足的 | More rare or less common than usual.  Something that is scarcer is in short supply and difficult to obtain. | 例句：Fresh water is becoming scarcer in many parts of the world.  许多地区淡水越来越稀缺。

例句：Due to the drought, food has become scarcer in the region. 由于干旱，该地区的食物变得更加稀缺。


近义词：rare, infrequent, uncommon, scarce, deficient

反义词：abundant, plentiful, copious, ample
