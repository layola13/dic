grow up:/ɡroʊ ʌp/| /ɡrəʊ ʌp/| v.|长大，成熟|To develop from a child into an adult; to become mature in behavior or understanding.|例句：He grew up in a small town. 他在一个小镇长大。|例句：She's grown up a lot since I last saw her. 自从我上次见到她以来，她成熟了很多。|近义词：mature, develop|反义词：degenerate, regress


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I grow up in a big city. = 我在一个大城市长大。
They grow up quickly. = 他们长得很快。

一般过去时:
He grew up in the countryside. = 他在乡下长大。
She grew up very fast. = 她长得很快。

现在完成时:
I have grown up a lot since I was a child. = 自从我还是个孩子以来，我已经长大很多了。
She has grown up into a beautiful young woman. = 她已经长成一位美丽的年轻女子了。

过去完成时:
By the time I was 18, I had grown up significantly. = 到我18岁的时候，我已经长大了很多。
Before the accident, she had grown up very carefree. = 事故发生前，她成长得非常无忧无虑。

将来时:
He will grow up to be a strong man. = 他将会成长为一个强壮的男人。
She will grow up soon. = 她很快就会长大。

虚拟语气:
If I had grown up in a different environment, I might be a different person. = 如果我在不同的环境中长大，我可能就是一个不同的人。
I wish I had grown up with more support. = 我希望我的成长有更多支持。

被动语态:  (grow up 不常用被动语态)
N/A

疑问句:
Where did you grow up? = 你在哪儿长大的？
How did he grow up so quickly? = 他怎么长得这么快？

否定句:
He didn't grow up in a wealthy family. = 他不是在一个富裕的家庭长大的。
She didn't grow up easily. = 她成长并非一帆风顺。


PHRASE->
grow up fast = 快速成长
grow up quickly = 快速长大
grow out of something = 脱离(不好的习惯)
grow into something =逐渐适应
grow on someone = 越来越喜欢
