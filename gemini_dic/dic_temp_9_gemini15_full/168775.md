prodigy's:/ˈprɑːdɪdʒiz/| n. |天才的；奇才的 |The extraordinary talent or abilities of a prodigy.  It's the possessive form of "prodigy".|例句：The prodigy's performance was breathtaking.  天才的表演令人叹为观止。|例句：The painting displayed the prodigy's unique style. 这幅画展现了天才独特的风格。|近义词：genius's, marvel's, wonder's |反义词：dunce's, idiot's, fool's |名词


USAGE->
This word is a possessive noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses.  It shows possession.  Therefore, no verb conjugations are applicable.

PHRASE->
a child prodigy: 神童
a musical prodigy: 音乐神童
a prodigy of nature: 自然奇观
a prodigy of learning: 学问天才
