cubit:/ˈkjuːbɪt/| n.|肘尺；古代长度单位|A cubit is an ancient unit of length, approximately equal to the length of a forearm from the elbow to the tip of the middle finger.  Different cultures had slightly varying cubit lengths.|The ancient Egyptians used cubits to measure the length of pyramids.|近义词：span, fathom|反义词：无
