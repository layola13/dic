shotgunning:/ˈʃɒtɡʌnɪŋ/ | n. v. |  同时饮用(多瓶啤酒等)；用猎枪射击；（俚语）粗略快速地完成 | The act of drinking from multiple containers simultaneously, typically beer bottles or cans, often as a drinking game or challenge; also the act of firing a shotgun.  |

例句：

He shotgunned three beers in under a minute. 他在一分钟内一口气喝掉了三瓶啤酒。

The hunter shotgunned the bird in flight. 猎人射杀了正在飞行的鸟。


近义词：downing (drinking), blasting (shooting)


反义词：sipping (drinking),  aiming carefully (shooting)


词性: 名词和动词
