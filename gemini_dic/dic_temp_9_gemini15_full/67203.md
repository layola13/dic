expressionists: /ɪkˈspreʃənɪsts/ | n. | 表现主义者 |  A person who practices or advocates expressionism, especially in art.  Expressionists believe that art should convey emotions and ideas rather than simply represent reality.  They often use bold colors, distorted forms, and exaggerated features to achieve this effect. |  The expressionists of the early 20th century challenged traditional artistic conventions.  |近义词: avant-gardists, modernists | 反义词:realists, classicists | 名词
