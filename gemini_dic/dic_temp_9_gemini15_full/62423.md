endomorphy:/ˌɛndoˈmɔːrfi/ | n. |内胚型；内胚层性 |The tendency to gain weight easily and have a soft, round body type.  This is one of the three somatotypes identified by William Sheldon.  It is characterized by a predisposition to fat accumulation, particularly around the abdomen and hips. |  He has a classic endomorphy body type, with a larger build and tendency towards weight gain. 他具有典型的内胚型体型，身材较胖，容易增重。 | mesomorphy, ectomorphy | ectomorphy |
