printing:/ˈprɪntɪŋ/| n. |印刷术；印刷品；印刷业|The process of producing text and images on paper or other materials using ink and a printing press or other methods.  It encompasses various techniques, from letterpress to digital printing.|例句：The printing of this book was delayed. 这本书的印刷被推迟了。|例句：The quality of the printing is excellent. 印刷质量非常好。|近义词：publication, impression|反义词：handwriting, typing
