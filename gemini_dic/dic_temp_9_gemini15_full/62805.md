enhance:/ɪnˈhɑːns/| vt.|提高，增强，改进|To improve the quality, amount, or strength of something.|The new software enhances the user experience. 新软件增强了用户体验。  The music enhances the mood of the film. 音乐增强了电影的气氛。|improve, amplify, boost|reduce, diminish, weaken|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He enhances his skills by practicing regularly. = 他通过定期练习来提高他的技能。
The company enhances its products with innovative features. = 公司通过创新的功能来改进其产品。


一般过去时:
She enhanced her presentation with compelling visuals. = 她用引人注目的视觉效果增强了她的演示文稿。
The renovation enhanced the value of the house. = 翻新提高了房子的价值。


现在完成时:
They have enhanced their security measures significantly. = 他们已经大大加强了安全措施。
We have enhanced our understanding of the problem. = 我们已经加深了对这个问题的理解。


过去完成时:
He had enhanced his resume before applying for the job. = 他在申请工作之前已经润色了他的简历。
The team had enhanced the design based on customer feedback. = 团队根据客户反馈改进了设计。


将来时:
They will enhance the website with a new design. = 他们将用新的设计来改进网站。
The update will enhance the performance of the system. = 更新将提高系统的性能。


虚拟语气:
If they enhanced their marketing strategy, they would attract more customers. = 如果他们改进他们的营销策略，他们就能吸引更多客户。
I wish I had enhanced my qualifications before applying. = 我希望我在申请之前已经提高了我的资格。


被动语态:
The image was enhanced using special software. = 图片是用特殊的软件增强的。
The security system is being enhanced to prevent future breaches. = 安全系统正在加强以防止未来的入侵。


疑问句:
How can we enhance the productivity of our team? = 我们如何提高团队的生产力？
What measures can be taken to enhance the quality of the product? = 可以采取什么措施来提高产品的质量？


否定句:
The poor lighting did not enhance the beauty of the painting. = 昏暗的灯光并没有增强绘画的美感。
He didn't enhance his argument with any evidence. = 他没有用任何证据来增强他的论点。
