nonmanipulatory: /ˌnɒnməˈnɪpjʊlətəri/ | adj. | 非操纵的，不操控的 | Not involving or intended to involve manipulation or control.  This adjective describes something that is free from manipulation, coercion, or undue influence. It suggests honesty, fairness, and respect for autonomy. |


例句：

The therapist employed nonmanipulatory techniques, focusing on empowering the client.  = 治疗师采用非操纵的技术，专注于增强客户的能力。

Her nonmanipulatory approach to negotiation resulted in a mutually beneficial agreement. = 她谈判中不耍手段的做法达成了互利的协议。


近义词：

unmanipulative, genuine, honest, straightforward, fair, impartial


反义词：

manipulative, controlling, coercive, deceptive, exploitative
