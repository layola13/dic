saleably:/ˈseɪləbli/| adj.| 可销售地；适于销售地 | Easily sold or likely to be sold; marketable.  | The product is saleably presented. 这个产品包装精美，容易销售。|近义词：marketable, merchantable, sellable |反义词：unsaleable, unmarketable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The products are saleably presented. = 产品包装精美，容易销售。
This item sells saleably in this market. = 这件商品在这个市场上很畅销。


一般过去时:
The goods were saleably packaged last year. = 去年商品包装精美，易于销售。
The house was saleably priced, so it sold quickly. = 房屋定价合理，因此很快就卖掉了。
