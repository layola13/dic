fruition:/fruːˈɪʃən/| n. |结果，实现，完成 |The successful completion of a plan or project; the point at which something reaches its peak or is fully realized.  |例句：After years of hard work, his plans finally came to fruition. 多年的努力之后，他的计划终于实现了。
例句：The fruition of her dream was a moment of pure joy.  她梦想的实现是无比快乐的时刻。|近义词：achievement, accomplishment, realization, fulfillment |反义词：failure, defeat, frustration
