lucubrator:/ˈluːkjuːbreɪtər/| n. |夜间工作者；勤奋的学者|A lucubrator is a person who works or studies at night, often implying diligent and scholarly work.  The term often carries a slightly archaic or literary feel.|例句：The weary lucubrator finally finished his manuscript at dawn.  疲惫的夜间工作者终于在黎明前完成了他的手稿。
The old professor, a lifelong lucubrator, continued his research even into his eighties.  这位年事已高的教授，一位终身的勤奋学者，即使到了八十多岁还在继续他的研究。|近义词：scholar, student, researcher, night worker|反义词：idler, loafer, slacker

词源:
