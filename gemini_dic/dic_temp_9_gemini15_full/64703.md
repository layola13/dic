escargatoire:/ɛskɑːrɡwɑːtʊər/| n. |蜗牛养殖场；蜗牛饲养箱|A place or container where snails are raised or kept.  It can refer to a farm dedicated to snail farming or a smaller container for keeping snails as pets.|例句：The escargatoire was filled with thousands of plump snails ready for harvest. 蜗牛养殖场里挤满了数千只肥美的蜗牛，准备收获。|例句：He kept his pet snails in a small escargatoire on his balcony. 他把宠物蜗牛养在他的阳台上一个小的蜗牛饲养箱里。|近义词：snail farm, snailery |反义词：无
