proffers:/ˈprɒfərz/| vt.|提供，提出；建议|To offer something for acceptance; to put forward a suggestion or proposal.|例句：The company proffered a generous severance package to the laid-off employees. 公司向被解雇的员工提供了丰厚的遣散费。|例句：He proffered a solution to the problem. 他提出了一个解决问题的方案。|近义词：offer, tender, suggest|反义词：reject, refuse, withhold

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He proffers his assistance regularly. = 他经常提供帮助。
She proffers insightful suggestions during meetings. = 她在会议上提出有见地的建议。

一般过去时:
The teacher proffered a helpful explanation. = 老师提供了有益的解释。
They proffered their apologies for the delay. = 他们为延误道歉。

现在完成时:
He has proffered many solutions to this problem over the years. = 多年来，他为这个问题提出了许多解决方案。
She has proffered her support to the charity. = 她一直支持该慈善机构。

过去完成时:
They had proffered their resignation before the scandal broke. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他们已经递交了辞呈。
He had proffered a warning, but it was ignored. = 他曾发出警告，但被忽略了。

将来时:
The company will proffer a new contract next week. = 公司下周将提供一份新合同。
She will proffer her expertise to the project. = 她将把她的专业知识贡献给这个项目。

虚拟语气:
If he proffered an apology, I would forgive him. = 如果他道歉，我会原谅他。
I wish he had proffered a more practical solution. = 我希望他提出更实际的解决方案。

被动语态:
A generous donation was proffered by an anonymous donor. = 一位匿名捐赠者慷慨捐款。
Several solutions were proffered to address the issue. = 提出了几个解决这个问题的方案。

疑问句:
What solutions did he proffer? = 他提出了什么解决方案？
Will the government proffer any financial aid? = 政府会提供任何财政援助吗？

否定句:
He did not proffer any explanation for his absence. = 他没有解释他为什么缺席。
The company refused to proffer any compensation. = 公司拒绝提供任何赔偿。


PHRASE->

proffer an excuse = 找借口
proffer a helping hand = 伸出援助之手
proffer a bribe = 行贿
