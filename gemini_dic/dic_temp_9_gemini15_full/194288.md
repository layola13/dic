self-opinionative:/ˌsɛlf əˈpɪnjənətɪv/| adj.|固执己见的，自以为是的|Having or expressing strong opinions; opinionated and assertive to the point of being annoying or obstructive.  It suggests a stubborn unwillingness to consider other viewpoints. |例句：He's become rather self-opinionative in his old age. 他上了年纪后变得相当固执己见。

例句：Her self-opinionative manner alienated many of her colleagues. 她自以为是的态度疏远了她许多同事。


近义词：opinionated, dogmatic, assertive, headstrong, self-assured, arrogant, conceited

反义词：open-minded, receptive, flexible, compromising, modest, humble

词性：形容词
