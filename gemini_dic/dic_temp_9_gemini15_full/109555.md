lest:/lest/| conj.|以免，唯恐|lest is a conjunction used to express a purpose or reason for doing something, usually to avoid a negative consequence.  It implies a fear or caution.  It is more common in formal writing and less frequent in everyday speech.|例句：He hurried lest he be late. 他赶紧赶路，以免迟到。

例句：She locked the door lest anyone should enter. 她锁上门，以免有人进来。


近义词：for fear that, in case, so that...not, in order that...not|反义词：so that (expressing a positive purpose)|词性:连词
