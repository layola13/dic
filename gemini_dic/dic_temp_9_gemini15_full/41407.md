cornel:/ˈkɔːrnəl/| n. |山茱萸；山茱萸树；深红色|A cornel is a shrub or small tree, typically with small, yellow flowers and red berries.  The wood is also very hard and is sometimes used for making tools.|The garden boasts a beautiful cornel tree, its branches laden with ripe red berries.|近义词：dogwood (某些种类)|反义词：无
