frictional:/ˈfrɪkʃənəl/| adj.|摩擦的；摩擦引起的；冲突的|Relating to or caused by friction.  Friction is the resistance that one surface or object encounters when moving over another.|The frictional force slowed the car down. 摩擦力减缓了汽车的速度。The frictional heat ignited the material. 摩擦热点燃了材料。|近义词：abrasive, rough, grating|反义词：smooth, effortless
