poetry: /ˈpoʊətri/ | n. |诗歌；诗意 |The art of writing poems, or a collection of poems.  Poetry often uses figurative language, such as metaphors and similes, to create imagery and evoke emotions. |例句：She writes beautiful poetry about nature. 她写关于自然的优美的诗歌。|例句：  The anthology contains poetry from various eras. 这部选集包含了各个时代的诗歌。|近义词：verse, rhymes, poetics |反义词：prose
