consenting:/kənˈsɛntɪŋ/| adj.| 同意的，赞成的 |  Giving consent; agreeing to something.  |例句：The consenting adults signed the contract.  同意参与的成年人签署了合同。  |近义词：agreeing, approving, assenting |反义词：objecting, dissenting, refusing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is consenting to the treatment. = 他同意接受治疗。
They are consenting to the terms of the agreement. = 他们同意协议条款。


一般过去时:
She consented to the marriage. = 她同意结婚了。
He consented to a blood test. = 他同意进行血液测试。


现在完成时:
Have they consented to the plan yet? = 他们是否已经同意这个计划了？
She has consented to participate in the study. = 她已经同意参与这项研究了。


过去完成时:
He had already consented to the interview before they called. = 他们在打电话之前，他已经同意接受采访了。
They had consented to the changes before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前已经同意了这些更改。


将来时:
Will they consent to the operation? = 他们会同意手术吗？
He will consent to the terms if we make some concessions. = 如果我们做出一些让步，他会同意这些条款。


虚拟语气:
If she consented, we could proceed. = 如果她同意，我们就可以继续了。
I wish he had consented earlier. = 我希望他早点同意。


被动语态:
Consent was not given by the patient. = 病人没有给予同意。
The proposal was consented to by the board. = 董事会同意了该提案。


疑问句:
Did he consent to the search? = 他同意搜查了吗？
Are you consenting to this course of action? = 你同意采取这种行动吗？


否定句:
The patient did not consent to the surgery. = 病人不同意进行手术。
She refused to consent to the terms. = 她拒绝同意这些条款。


PHRASE->
consenting adults =  成年人双方同意
mutually consenting =  双方同意
freely consenting = 自愿同意
without consenting = 未经同意
informed consenting = 知情同意
