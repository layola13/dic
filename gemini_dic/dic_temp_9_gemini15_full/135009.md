词源:
The word "nonnegligent" is formed by the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the adjective "negligent" (meaning "failing to take proper care").  Therefore, it literally means "not negligent."  "Negligent" ultimately derives from the Latin word "negligere," meaning "to neglect."
