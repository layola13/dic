horizontalization:/ˌhɒrɪzɒntəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/|/ˌhɔːrɪzɒntəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |水平化；水平扩展；横向扩展|The process of making something horizontal or flat;  in computing, the process of distributing tasks across multiple servers or machines to improve performance and scalability.  It can also refer to the flattening of organizational hierarchies.|例句：The company's horizontalization strategy involved distributing tasks across multiple teams. 公司的水平化战略包括将任务分配给多个团队。

The horizontalization of the database significantly improved query performance. 数据库的水平扩展显著提高了查询性能。|近义词：distribution, decentralization, parallelization|反义词：verticalization, centralization
