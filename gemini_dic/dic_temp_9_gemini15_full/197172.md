shantih:/ˈʃɑːntiː/| n. |平静；安宁；和平；梵语中的祝祷词|A Sanskrit word meaning peace, tranquility, or serenity.  It is often used as a benediction or a mantra for achieving inner peace.  |例句：The chanting of "shantih, shantih, shantih" brought a sense of calm to the room.  念诵“shantih, shantih, shantih”使房间里充满了平静。|近义词：peace, tranquility, serenity, calm, composure|反义词：turmoil, chaos, unrest, anxiety, disturbance|名词
