mummery:/ˈmʌməri/| n.|装腔作势；滑稽表演；闹剧|Meaning:  Mummery refers to actions or ceremonies that are meaningless or pretentious, often involving exaggerated or showy behavior. It can also refer to a boisterous or noisy performance, typically a traditional one involving masked figures. |Example: The whole coronation ceremony felt like a lot of mummery to me.  整个加冕仪式在我看来只是一场虚张声势的闹剧。The children's mummery play was full of loud singing and silly costumes. 孩子们的滑稽剧充满了大声的歌唱和滑稽的服装。|近义词：posturing, charade, pretense, buffoonery|反义词：sincerity, genuineness, authenticity
