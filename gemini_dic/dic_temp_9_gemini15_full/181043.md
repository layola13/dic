reinclined:/ˌriːɪnˈklaɪnd/| adj.|重新倾向的；再次倾斜的 | Having been inclined again; tilted or slanted anew.  This word is rarely used and is essentially a combination of "re-" and "inclined".  It describes something that has been tilted or slanted once and then tilted or slanted again. | Example: The already leaning tower became further reinclined after the recent earthquake.  最近的地震使已经倾斜的塔楼更加倾斜。| 近义词：re-inclined, re-tilted, re-slanted | 反义词：upright, vertical |  形容词
