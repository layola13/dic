dismembered:/dɪsˈmɛmbərd/| v.|肢解，分割；破坏（组织、系统等）|To cut or tear off the limbs of (a body); to separate the parts of (something).|例句：The body was found dismembered in the woods. 尸体在树林里被发现肢解了。

例句：The opposition party tried to dismember the government's reform plan. 反对党试图肢解政府的改革计划。


近义词：cut up, sever, mutilate, dissect

反义词：assemble, unite, integrate


词性:动词

词源:
