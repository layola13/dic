反义词：calm, quiet, stillness


词性:名词


词源：The word "fandango" originates from the Spanish word "fandango," which itself likely derives from the Portuguese "fandangueira," meaning a type of dance.  The ultimate origin is uncertain but may relate to words suggesting "noise" or "boisterous celebration".
