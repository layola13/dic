engorged:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒd/| adj.|充血的；肿胀的；胀满的|Extremely swollen or congested, typically with blood.  It implies an unnatural fullness or distension.|The engorged veins in his neck pulsed visibly.|近义词：swollen, congested, distended, bloated |反义词：deflated, empty
