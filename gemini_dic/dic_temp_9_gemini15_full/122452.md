mobilehomes: /ˈmoʊbəlˌhoʊmz/ | n. | 活动房屋，移动房屋 | A mobile home is a prefabricated structure designed to be transported to a location and placed on a permanent or semi-permanent foundation.  They are often used as affordable housing or recreational vehicles. |  例句：Many retirees live in comfortable mobile homes in retirement communities.  许多退休人员住在退休社区舒适的活动房屋里。

例句：We spent our summer vacation in a mobile home by the lake. 我们在湖边的活动房屋里度过了暑假。


近义词：trailers, manufactured homes, mobile houses | 反义词：permanent homes, stationary homes | 词性: noun
