palisade:/ˌpælɪˈseɪd/| n.|栅栏；防御工事；（植物的）栅栏状物|A palisade is a fence made of strong, pointed wooden stakes, often used for defense.  It can also refer to a natural formation resembling a fence, such as a row of closely spaced trees or a geological feature.|例句：The village was protected by a strong palisade.  这个村庄受到坚固栅栏的保护。

例句：The cliffs formed a natural palisade along the coast. 悬崖形成了一道沿着海岸线的天然屏障。|近义词：fence, barricade, stockade|反义词：opening, gap|名词
