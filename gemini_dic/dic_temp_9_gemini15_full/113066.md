lumpishly:/ˈlʌmpɪʃli/| adv.|笨拙地；迟钝地；懒散地 | in a clumsy or awkward way; in a slow and unhurried way.  |例句：He moved lumpishly across the room. 他笨拙地穿过房间。 He sat lumpishly in the chair. 他懒洋洋地坐在椅子上。|近义词：awkwardly, clumsily, heavily, sluggishly |反义词：gracefully, nimbly, swiftly


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He walks lumpishly. = 他走路很笨拙。
She speaks lumpishly. = 她说话很迟钝。

一般过去时:
He walked lumpishly to the car. = 他笨拙地走到车旁。
She responded lumpishly to the question. = 她迟钝地回答了这个问题。


现在完成时:
He has moved lumpishly all day. = 他一整天都行动笨拙。
She has reacted lumpishly to the news. = 她对这个消息反应迟钝。


过去完成时:
He had walked lumpishly before he fell. = 他摔倒之前走得很笨拙。
She had responded lumpishly throughout the meeting. = 她在整个会议上反应迟钝。


将来时:
