sieve-like:/ˈsɪvlaɪk/| adj. |筛子状的，多孔的|Resembling a sieve; full of holes or openings.  This describes something with many small holes, allowing things to pass through easily.  |例句：The porous rock had a sieve-like structure.  这块多孔岩石具有筛子状结构。

例句：The damaged filter had become sieve-like, allowing contaminants to pass through.  损坏的过滤器变得像筛子一样，允许污染物通过。


近义词：porous, perforated, honeycombed


反义词：impermeable, solid, dense


词性：形容词
