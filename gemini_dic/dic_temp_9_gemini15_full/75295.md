frontierism:/ˈfrʌntɪəriːzəm/| n. |拓荒主义；边疆精神|The ideology and practices associated with expansion into and settlement of new territories, often involving displacement or subjugation of indigenous populations.  It emphasizes self-reliance, individualism, and the taming of the wilderness.|Example: The westward expansion of the United States was fueled by a strong sense of frontierism.|近义词：expansionism, colonialism, westward expansion|反义词：conservationism, preservationism
