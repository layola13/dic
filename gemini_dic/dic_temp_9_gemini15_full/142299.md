orthoclastic:/ˌɔːrθəʊˈklæsɪk/| adj.| 正长石的；正长结构的 | Relating to or denoting a type of rock texture characterized by angular fragments that have not been significantly rounded by weathering or transport.  Orthoclastic rocks are formed by the accumulation of angular fragments of pre-existing rocks. |  The breccia showed a clear orthoclastic texture. 这块角砾岩显示出明显的正长结构。The geologist identified the orthoclastic fragments in the sandstone sample. 地质学家鉴定了砂岩样品中的正长石碎块。 | 近义词：angular, fractured | 反义词：clastic, rounded
