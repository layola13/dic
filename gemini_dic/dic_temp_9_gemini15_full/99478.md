interfering: /ˌɪntərˈfɪərɪŋ/ | adj. | 干涉的；妨碍的 |  Meddling; obstructing; causing problems.  | 例句：His interfering remarks annoyed everyone.  他的干涉性言论激怒了所有人。  | 例句：The interfering machine needs repair. 这台干扰的机器需要修理。 | 近义词：meddling, intrusive, obstructive | 反义词：helpful, supportive, unobtrusive |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is constantly interfering in other people's business. = 他总是干涉别人的事。
She interferes in her children's lives too much. = 她过多地干涉孩子的生活。


一般过去时:
He interfered in the argument and made it worse. = 他干涉了这场争论，结果使情况更糟了。
She interfered with my plans by arriving late. = 她迟到扰乱了我的计划。


现在完成时:
They have been interfering with the investigation. = 他们一直在干扰调查。
He has interfered in our affairs too many times. = 他已经多次干涉我们的事务了。


过去完成时:
I realized he had interfered with the experiment before I started. = 我意识到在我开始实验之前他已经干预过了。
She discovered that someone had interfered with her mail. = 她发现有人动过她的邮件。


将来时:
I predict he will interfere again if we don't stop him. = 我预测如果我们不阻止他，他会再次干涉。
They will interfere with the project unless we get their approval. = 除非我们得到他们的批准，否则他们会干扰这个项目。


虚拟语气:
If he hadn't interfered, the situation would have been much better. = 如果他没有干涉，情况会好得多。
I wish they hadn't interfered in my personal life. = 我希望他们不要干涉我的私生活。


被动语态:
The experiment was interfered with by an unknown factor. = 实验受到了未知因素的干扰。
The meeting was interfered with by a sudden power outage. = 会议因突然停电而被打扰。


疑问句:

Are they interfering with our signal? = 他们干扰了我们的信号吗？


否定句:
He didn't interfere in their relationship. = 他没有干涉他们的关系。
She didn't interfere with my work. = 她没有干扰我的工作。
