multitasks:/ˌmʌltiˈtæskz/| v.|多任务处理；同时处理多项任务|To perform multiple tasks simultaneously or in rapid succession.|例句：She multitasks effectively, managing her job, family, and social life with ease. 她有效地进行多任务处理，轻松地兼顾工作、家庭和社交生活。例句：The operating system allows you to multitask several applications at once.  操作系统允许你同时运行多个应用程序。|近义词：juggle, handle concurrently, manage simultaneously|反义词：monotask (处理单一任务)

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He multitasks efficiently at work. = 他在工作中高效地进行多任务处理。
She multitasks while cooking dinner. = 她在做晚饭的同时处理其他事情。


一般过去时:
I multitasked all day yesterday. = 我昨天一整天都在处理多项任务。
He multitasked during the meeting, checking emails and taking notes. = 他在会议期间处理多项任务，查看邮件并做笔记。


现在完成时:
She has multitasked throughout her career. = 她职业生涯中一直在处理多项任务。
I have multitasked so much today that I'm exhausted. = 我今天处理了太多任务，以至于精疲力尽。


过去完成时:
I had multitasked all morning before the deadline. = 截止日期前我整个上午都在处理多项任务。
She had multitasked for hours before finally taking a break. = 她连续工作几个小时处理多项任务后，终于休息了一下。

虚拟语气:
