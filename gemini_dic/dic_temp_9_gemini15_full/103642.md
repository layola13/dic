jokes:/dʒoʊks/ | n. |笑话，玩笑 |A joke is something said or done to cause laughter.  Jokes often rely on wordplay, irony, or unexpected twists. |He told a few jokes to lighten the mood. 他讲了几个笑话来活跃气氛。|puns, gags, witticisms |seriousness, truth |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He tells jokes every day. = 他每天都讲笑话。
She makes jokes about her job. = 她拿她的工作开玩笑。


一般过去时:
He told a funny joke last night. = 他昨晚讲了一个好笑的笑话。
