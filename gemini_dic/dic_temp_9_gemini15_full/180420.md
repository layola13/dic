regaling:/rɪˈɡeɪlɪŋ/ | v. | 款待；使快乐；使高兴 | To entertain or amuse someone with something.  |例句：He regaled us with stories of his adventures. 他给我们讲了他冒险经历的故事，让我们很开心。  |近义词：entertaining, amusing, delighting |反义词：boring, depressing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He regales his friends with funny anecdotes. = 他经常给朋友们讲有趣的轶事。
She regales the audience with her singing. = 她用歌声款待观众。

一般过去时:
The comedian regaled the audience with jokes. = 喜剧演员用笑话逗乐了观众。
They regaled their guests with a delicious meal. = 他们用美味佳肴款待了客人。

现在完成时:
He has regaled us with tales of his travels for hours. = 他给我们讲了他旅行的故事，已经讲了好几个小时了。
She has regaled the children with bedtime stories every night this week. = 这周每晚她都给孩子们讲睡前故事。

过去完成时:
He had regaled the guests with his wit before the main course was served. = 在上主菜之前，他已经用他的机智逗乐了客人。
They had regaled themselves with a lavish buffet before beginning the meeting. = 在开始会议之前，他们已经享用了一顿丰盛的自助餐。

将来时:
She will regale us with her experiences in Africa. = 她将会给我们讲述她在非洲的经历。
The band will regale the crowd with their new songs. = 乐队将用他们的新歌来娱乐人群。

虚拟语气:
If he regaled us with stories like that more often, we’d be much happier. = 如果他经常给我们讲这样的故事，我们会快乐得多。
I wish he would regale us with his adventures again. = 我希望他再给我们讲讲他的冒险经历。

被动语态:
The guests were regaled with a feast. = 客人们受到了盛宴的款待。
The audience was regaled with a spectacular performance. = 观众们欣赏了一场精彩的演出。


疑问句:
Will he regale us with his latest travel stories?  = 他会给我们讲他最新的旅行故事吗？
Does she often regale her colleagues with gossip? = 她经常向同事们八卦吗？


否定句:
He didn't regale us with any interesting stories. = 他没有给我们讲任何有趣的故事。
She didn't regale the children with stories last night. = 她昨晚没有给孩子们讲故事。
