The word "sepose" does not exist in standard English.  It is likely a misspelling of "suppose".  Therefore, I will provide the analysis for "suppose".


suppose:/səˈpoʊz/ | vt. | 假设；认为；料想 | To assume to be true or probable; imagine or assume. |  例句：I suppose you're right. 我想你是对的。  I suppose it'll rain tomorrow. 我想明天会下雨。|近义词：assume, presume, guess, imagine | 反义词：know, be certain, be sure | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I suppose you are tired. = 我猜你累了。
They suppose the project will be finished on time. = 他们认为项目会按时完成。

一般过去时:
I supposed he was joking. = 我以为他在开玩笑。
She supposed the letter had arrived. = 她以为信已经到了。

现在完成时:
We have supposed that the data is accurate. = 我们一直认为数据是准确的。
They have supposed the problem to be more complicated than it is. = 他们一直认为这个问题比实际情况更复杂。

过去完成时:
I had supposed that you would be there. = 我原以为你会在那儿。
He had supposed the meeting would be shorter. = 他原以为会议会短一些。

将来时:
I will suppose that this is true for the sake of argument. = 为便于论证，我将假设这是真的。
They will suppose the outcome to be favorable. = 他们将假设结果会是好的。

虚拟语气:
Suppose you were rich, what would you do? = 假设你很富有，你会做什么？
If I were you, I suppose I would leave. = 如果我是你，我想我会离开。

被动语态:
It is supposed that the earthquake caused the tsunami. = 人们认为地震引起了海啸。
The theory is supposed to be revolutionary. = 据说这个理论具有革命性。

疑问句:
Do you suppose he'll come? = 你认为他会来吗？
What do you suppose he wants? = 你认为他想干什么？

否定句:
I don't suppose he'll be happy about this. = 我想他不会对这事高兴。
They don't suppose the plan will work. = 他们认为这个计划不会成功。
