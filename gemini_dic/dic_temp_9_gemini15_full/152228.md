patrol:/pəˈtroʊl/ |/pəˈtrəʊl/| n., v.|巡逻；巡查|The act of going around an area to watch for problems or to protect it; to go around an area regularly in order to watch or guard it.|例句：The police patrol the streets at night.  警察在晚上巡逻街道。
The soldiers patrolled the border.士兵巡逻边境。


近义词：巡视，巡查，警戒，守卫|guard, watch, monitor, survey|反义词：撤离，放弃|abandon, leave, withdraw|词性：名词，动词
