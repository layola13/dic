overexcite:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsaɪt/ | vt. |过度兴奋；使过度兴奋 | to excite excessively; to stimulate beyond a normal or healthy level.  |例句：The loud music overexcited the children.  喧闹的音乐使孩子们过度兴奋。
The news of the win overexcited the team.  获胜的消息使球队过度兴奋。|近义词：hyper-stimulate, overstimulate |反义词：calm, soothe, tranquilize |动词

词源:
The word "overexcite" is a combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") and the verb "excite" (meaning "to stimulate or arouse").  "Excite" originates from the Latin word "excītāre," meaning "to rouse, stir up."
