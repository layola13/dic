person:/ˈpɜːrsən/ /ˈpɜːrsn/| n. |人，个人；人物；人称|A human being.  A person can be a man, woman, or child.  It can also refer to a character in a story or a specific individual.|例句：There were many people at the party.  派对上有很多人。|例句：She is a person of great integrity. 她是一个非常正直的人。|近义词：individual, human, being|反义词：nobody, nothing
