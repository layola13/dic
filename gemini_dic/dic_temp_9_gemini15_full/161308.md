postpectoral: /ˌpoʊstˈpɛktərəl/ | adj. |胸后部的，胸骨后的|  Relating to or situated behind the pectoral region (the chest).  The term is primarily used in anatomical contexts. | 例句：The postpectoral lymph nodes are located behind the chest muscles. = 胸后淋巴结位于胸肌之后。


The postpectoral approach is sometimes used in cardiac surgery. = 心脏手术有时会采用胸后入路。


近义词：retrosternal (referring to behind the sternum, a bone in the chest)


反义词：pectoral (referring to the chest region itself)


词性: adjective


词源:  The word is a combination of "post-" (meaning "behind" or "after") and "pectoral" (referring to the chest). "Pectoral" originates from the Latin word "pectus," meaning "breast" or "chest."
