overcharitable: /ˌoʊvərˈtʃærɪtəbl/ | adj. | 过分慈善的；过于慷慨的 | Excessively charitable; too generous to the point of being unwise or impractical.  It implies a generosity that is potentially harmful or detrimental due to its excess. | 例句：His overcharitable nature led him to give away all his savings.  他的过分慈善导致他捐光了所有的积蓄。

His overcharitable donation to the charity left him financially vulnerable. 他对慈善机构的过分慷慨的捐款使他的财务状况变得脆弱。


近义词：excessively generous, overly kind, improvidently charitable, prodigal

反义词：stingy, miserly, parsimonious, ungenerous


词性：形容词
