coigny:/ˈkɔɪni/| n.|墙角；壁角；（建筑）隅石|A coigny is a projecting stone at the angle of a building, often used as a decorative feature or to strengthen the corner.  It's a type of quoin. |例句：The old house had beautifully carved coign stones at each corner.  这座老房子每个角落都有精美雕刻的隅石。

The coigny of the building was reinforced with steel. 建筑的墙角用钢筋加固了。|近义词：quoin, corner, angle|反义词：center, middle
