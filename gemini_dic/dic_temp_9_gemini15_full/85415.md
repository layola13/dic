harvester:/ˈhɑːvəstər/ | n. | 收割机；收割者 | A machine used for harvesting crops, or a person who harvests crops.  |例句：The farmer used a large harvester to gather the wheat.  农民用大型收割机收割小麦。
|例句：The experienced harvester worked quickly and efficiently.  经验丰富的收割者工作又快又有效率。 |近义词：reaper, gatherer |反义词：sower (播种者)
