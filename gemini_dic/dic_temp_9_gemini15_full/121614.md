misorienting: /ˌmɪsˈɔːriɛntɪŋ/ | adj. | 使迷失方向的；使误解的 | causing someone to lose their way or to have a mistaken understanding of something.  | The misorienting map led them astray.  这幅令人迷失方向的地图使他们迷路了。The misorienting instructions confused the assembly team. 这些令人误解的指示使装配小组感到困惑。 | confusing, misleading, perplexing | orienting, clarifying, enlightening | 形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The confusing instructions are misorienting many users. = 这些令人困惑的说明使许多用户迷失方向。
This misleading advertisement is misorienting consumers. = 这则误导性广告正在误导消费者。


一般过去时:
