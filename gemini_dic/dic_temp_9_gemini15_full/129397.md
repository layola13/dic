nominalise:/ˈnɒmɪnəlaɪz/|/ˈnɑːmɪnəlaɪz/| vt.|名词化；使…成为名词|To convert (a word or phrase) into a noun.|The process of turning a verb, adjective, or other word class into a noun.  This often involves adding suffixes like "-tion," "-ment," "-ance," or "-ness."|

The verb "to analyze" was nominalised to the noun "analysis".  = 动词“分析”被名词化成了名词“分析”。

They nominalised the adjective "important" into the noun "importance". = 他们把形容词“重要的”名词化成了名词“重要性”。


近义词：substantivize, nounify

反义词：verbify, adjectivize


词性：动词
