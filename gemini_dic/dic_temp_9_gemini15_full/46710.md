danced:/dænst/| v.|跳舞|To move rhythmically to music, typically following a set of steps.|例句：The children danced joyfully at the party.孩子们在派对上快乐地跳舞。|例句：She danced gracefully across the stage.她优雅地走过舞台。|近义词：waltzed, twirled, swayed|反义词：stood still, remained motionless

USAGE->
一般现在时:
She dances every Saturday. = 她每个星期六都跳舞。
They dance beautifully. = 他们跳得很漂亮。

一般过去时:
He danced with her last night. = 他昨晚和她跳舞。
We danced until dawn. = 我们跳到黎明。

现在完成时:
I have danced many times in my life. = 我一生中跳过很多次舞。
They have danced together for ten years. = 他们一起跳舞十年了。

过去完成时:
She had danced before she met him. = 在她遇到他之前，她已经跳过舞了。
We had danced for hours before we got tired. = 我们跳了几个小时才累。

将来时:
I will dance at the wedding. = 我将在婚礼上跳舞。
They will dance the night away. = 他们将通宵跳舞。

虚拟语气:
If I could dance, I would. = 如果我会跳舞，我会跳。
I wish I could dance like her. = 我希望我能像她一样跳舞。

被动语态:
The dance was danced beautifully. = 这支舞跳得很美。
The waltz was danced by many couples. = 华尔兹舞由许多夫妇跳。

疑问句:
Did you dance last night? = 你昨晚跳舞了吗？
Can you dance the tango? = 你会跳探戈舞吗？

否定句:
I didn't dance at the party. = 我没有在派对上跳舞。
He cannot dance. = 他不会跳舞。
