perceptibleness:/pərˈsɛptəbəlnəs/| n. |可感知性，可觉察性 |The degree to which something can be perceived or noticed.  It refers to how easily something can be detected by the senses or the mind.  |The perceptibleness of the faint sound was questionable. 微弱的声音的可感知性值得怀疑。 The high perceptibleness of the error made it easy to correct. 错误的高可感知性使其易于纠正。|noticeability, detectability, discernibility |imperceptibility, invisibility, undetectability|名词
