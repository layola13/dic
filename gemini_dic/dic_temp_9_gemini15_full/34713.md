cliché:/ˈkliːʃeɪ/| n.|陈词滥调，老生常谈|A cliché is a phrase or opinion that is overused and betrays a lack of original thought.  It is a stereotyped expression, a hackneyed phrase, or a predictable idea.|例句：His speech was full of clichés about the American dream. 他的演讲充满了关于美国梦的老生常谈。

例句：Using clichés in your writing can make it sound unoriginal. 在你的写作中使用陈词滥调会使它听起来缺乏创意。


近义词：stereotype, platitude, banality, truism, commonplace

反义词：originality, novelty, innovation


词性：名词
