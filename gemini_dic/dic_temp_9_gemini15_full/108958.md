leering: /ˈlɪərɪŋ/ | adj. | 淫荡的，色迷迷的 |  Showing or expressing an offensive or lascivious gaze.  It suggests a suggestive and often disrespectful look, usually with sexual connotations. | 例句：He gave her a leering glance. 他对她投去一个色迷迷的眼神。

He leered at the women passing by. 他色迷迷地盯着路过的女人们。


近义词：ogling,  smirking,  lustful,  salacious


反义词：chaste, modest, demure


词性：形容词 (也可以作动名词，即leering作名词使用，指“色迷迷地看”的动作)
