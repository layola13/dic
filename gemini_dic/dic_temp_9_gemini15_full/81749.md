greeted:/ɡriːtɪd/ | v. | 迎接；问候；致意 | To address (someone) with an expression of welcome or greeting.  |例句：She greeted me warmly at the door. 她热情地在门口迎接我。

例句：He greeted his friends with a cheerful smile. 他面带笑容地向朋友们问好。


近义词：welcomed, saluted, hailed, addressed


反义词：ignored, snubbed


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He greets his colleagues every morning. 他每天早上都向同事们打招呼。
She greets visitors with a smile. 她笑着迎接访客。


一般过去时:
I greeted my teacher respectfully. 我恭敬地向老师问好。
They greeted each other with a handshake. 他们互相握手致意。


现在完成时:
I have greeted all the guests. 我已经向所有客人问候过了。
She has greeted the President. 她已经向总统致敬了。


过去完成时:
He had greeted the speaker before the speech began. 演讲开始前，他已经向演讲者问好。
They had greeted each other many times before. 他们之前已经见过很多次面，互相问候过了。


将来时:
I will greet them at the airport. 我将在机场迎接他们。
She will greet her friends warmly. 她将热情地迎接她的朋友们。


虚拟语气:
If I saw him, I would greet him politely. 如果我见到他，我会礼貌地向他问好。
I wish I had greeted her earlier. 我真希望我早点向她问好。


被动语态:
The guests were greeted warmly by the host. 客人们受到了主人的热情款待。
The visitors were greeted at the entrance. 访客们在入口处受到了迎接。


疑问句:
Did you greet your neighbors? 你有没有向你的邻居打招呼？
How did they greet the new student? 他们是如何迎接新生的？


否定句:
I didn't greet him because I didn't see him. 我没有向他打招呼，因为我没有看到他。
She didn't greet anyone at the party.  她在派对上没有向任何人打招呼。
