postulates:/ˈpɒstʃuleɪts/ /ˈpɑːstʃəleɪts/ | v. n. |假设；假定；提出…作为前提|To postulate something is to suggest or assume it as a basis for reasoning or argument, without necessarily having proof.  It can be a verb or a noun depending on the context.|

例句：

The theory postulates a flat earth. = 这个理论假设地球是平的。

Scientists postulate that the universe began with a Big Bang. = 科学家假设宇宙起源于大爆炸。


近义词：assume, hypothesize, presuppose, suggest, propose

反义词：prove, demonstrate, verify
