iterating:/ˈɪtəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. |重复，迭代 |反复地做某事；重复一个过程以获得改进的结果。|例句：The program is iterating through the list of names.  程序正在遍历姓名列表。|例句：We're iterating on the design, making small changes each time. 我们正在改进设计，每次都做一些小的改动。|近义词：repeating, reiterating, repeating, cycling |反义词：stopping, ceasing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is iterating the process until he gets the desired result. 他正在重复这个过程，直到得到想要的结果。
The computer is iterating through the database. 计算机正在遍历数据库。


一般过去时:
She iterated the experiment several times to confirm the findings. 她重复实验几次以确认结果。
They iterated the design based on user feedback. 他们根据用户反馈修改了设计。


现在完成时:
We have iterated on this project for months. 我们已经在这个项目上迭代了好几个月了。
They have iterated the software many times, improving its performance significantly. 他们已经多次迭代软件，显著提高了其性能。


过去完成时:
They had iterated the code several times before finding the bug. 他们在找到错误之前已经多次迭代了代码。
Before launching the product, they had iterated on the prototype many times. 在发布产品之前，他们已经多次迭代了原型。


将来时:
We will iterate on the design once we get the user feedback. 我们拿到用户反馈后会修改设计。
They will iterate the process until they reach the optimal solution. 他们将重复该过程，直到找到最佳解决方案。


虚拟语气:
If we iterated more often, we would have a better product. 如果我们更频繁地迭代，我们会得到更好的产品。
I wish we had iterated on the design earlier. 我希望我们早点修改设计。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
