rearrange:/ˌriːəˈreɪndʒ/| vt.|重新安排；改组|To change the arrangement or order of something.|例句：We need to rearrange the furniture in the living room. 我们需要重新安排客厅的家具。|例句：The teacher rearranged the seating plan.老师重新安排了座位。|近义词：reorganize, reorder, readjust|反义词：disorganize, disarrange


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They rearrange the books on the shelf every week. = 他们每周都会重新摆放书架上的书。
I rearrange my schedule to fit in more exercise. = 我重新安排我的日程表以腾出更多时间锻炼。

一般过去时:
She rearranged the flowers in the vase. = 她重新摆放了花瓶里的花。
He rearranged his priorities after the accident. = 事故后他重新安排了优先级。

现在完成时:
They have rearranged the office layout several times this year. = 他们今年已经几次重新安排了办公室的布局。
I have rearranged my travel plans due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，我已经重新安排了我的旅行计划。

过去完成时:
They had rearranged the furniture before the party. = 他们在派对之前已经重新安排了家具。
She had rearranged her schedule to attend the meeting. = 她为了参加会议已经重新安排了她的日程表。

将来时:
We will rearrange the meeting for next week. = 我们将把会议改到下周。
He will rearrange the files on his computer. = 他将重新整理电脑上的文件。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would rearrange my whole life. = 如果我有更多时间，我会重新安排我整个人生。
I wish I could rearrange my childhood memories. = 我希望我能重新安排我的童年记忆。

被动语态:
The furniture was rearranged for the party. = 家具为了派对被重新安排了。
The schedule has been rearranged due to the delay. = 由于延误，日程已被重新安排。

疑问句:
Have you rearranged the seating chart? = 你重新安排座位表了吗？
Will they rearrange the program? = 他们会重新安排节目吗？

否定句:
I didn't rearrange the files. = 我没有重新整理文件。
They haven't rearranged the furniture yet. = 他们还没有重新安排家具。
