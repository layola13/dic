/ˈkaʊntərəˌtæks/| n. |反击；反攻|A counterattack is an attack made in response to an attack. It's a defensive action that aims to regain lost ground or inflict damage on the attacker.  |例句：The army launched a fierce counterattack after being pushed back.  军队在被击退后发起了猛烈的反攻。

The rebels staged a surprise counterattack, recapturing the lost territory.  叛军发动了突然袭击，夺回了失地。|近义词：retaliation, reprisal, counteroffensive|反义词：surrender, retreat


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The army counterattacks the enemy forces regularly. = 军队定期反击敌军。
She counterattacks any criticism with well-reasoned arguments. = 她以合理的论据反驳任何批评。


一般过去时:
The general counterattacked with a surprise flanking maneuver. = 将军以出其不意的侧翼机动进行了反击。
He counterattacked his opponent's accusations with evidence. = 他用证据反驳了对手的指控。


现在完成时:
The team has counterattacked effectively throughout the match. = 这支球队在整场比赛中都进行了有效的反击。
They have counterattacked the spread of misinformation with factual information. = 他们用事实信息反击了虚假信息的传播。


过去完成时:
The soldiers had counterattacked before the enemy could consolidate their position. = 士兵们在敌人巩固阵地之前进行了反击。
She had counterattacked every argument he presented, leaving him speechless. = 她反驳了他提出的每一个论点，让他哑口无言。


将来时:

We will counterattack any attempt to undermine our efforts. = 我们将反击任何破坏我们努力的企图。


虚拟语气:
