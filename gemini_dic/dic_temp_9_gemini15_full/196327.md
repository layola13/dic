serializable:/ˌsɪəriəlaɪzəbəl/| adj.|可序列化的|Capable of being serialized; able to be converted into a sequence of bytes for storage or transmission.|This object is serializable, allowing for easy data persistence.  这个对象是可序列化的，方便数据持久化。|近义词：storable, transmittable|反义词：unserializable
