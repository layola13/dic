hirudine:/ˈhɪrjuːdiːn/| n. |水蛭素|A protein found in the saliva of leeches that prevents blood clotting. It is used medically as an anticoagulant. |Hirudin is a potent anticoagulant that inhibits thrombin.  水蛭素是一种有效的抗凝剂，它能抑制凝血酶。|近义词：anticoagulant|反义词：coagulant|名词
