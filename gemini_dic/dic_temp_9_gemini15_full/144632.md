outwitted:/ˌaʊtˈwɪtɪd/| v.|智胜，以智取胜|To defeat or trick someone using intelligence or cleverness.|The cunning fox outwitted the hunter by leading him on a wild goose chase.  狡猾的狐狸通过引诱猎人进行一场徒劳无功的追逐而智胜了他。|The clever detective outwitted the criminals and solved the case.  聪明的侦探智胜了罪犯并破案了。|outsmart, outmaneuver, trick|be outsmarted, be tricked|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He often outwits his opponents in chess. = 他经常在国际象棋比赛中智胜对手。
She outwits her rivals in business deals. = 她在商业交易中智胜她的竞争对手。

一般过去时:
The team outwitted their rivals in the final match. = 该队在决赛中智胜了他们的对手。
The clever magician outwitted the audience with his illusions. = 这位聪明的魔术师用他的幻觉戏法智胜了观众。

现在完成时:
The detective has outwitted many criminals in his career. = 这位侦探在他职业生涯中智胜了许多罪犯。
She has outwitted her opponents time and again. = 她一次又一次地智胜了她的对手。

过去完成时:
They had outwitted the security guards before entering the building. = 他们在进入大楼之前已经智胜了保安。
He had outwitted his enemies numerous times in the past. = 他过去多次智胜了他的敌人。

将来时:
We will outwit them with our clever plan. = 我们将用我们的巧妙计划智胜他们。
She will outwit her opponents in the upcoming debate. = 她将在即将到来的辩论中智胜她的对手。

虚拟语气:
If we had been more clever, we would have outwitted them. = 如果我们更聪明一些，我们本可以智胜他们。
I wish I had outwitted him in that argument. = 我真希望我在那场争论中智胜了他。

被动语态:
He was outwitted by his younger brother in the game. = 他在游戏中被他的弟弟智胜了。
The plan was outwitted by unforeseen circumstances. = 该计划被不可预见的情况破坏了。

疑问句:
Were you outwitted by the puzzle? = 你被这个谜题难住了吗？（智胜的被动）
Did they outwit the police? = 他们智胜了警察吗？

否定句:
He didn't outwit his opponent in the negotiation. = 他在谈判中没有智胜他的对手。
We couldn't outwit the security system. = 我们无法智胜安全系统。
