例句：

The penetrableness of the security system was a major concern.  = 安全系统的可渗透性是一个主要问题。

The penetrableness of his arguments convinced the jury. = 他论点的可信度（易于让人信服）说服了陪审团。


近义词：permeability, vulnerability, susceptibility

反义词：impermeability, impenetrability, resistance


词源：

From penetrable + -ness.  Penetrable comes from the verb "penetrate," which has Latin roots:  *penetrāre* (to penetrate, pierce).  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns of state or quality.
