otitic:/oʊˈtaɪtɪk/| adj.|耳的；耳部的；与耳有关的 | Relating to the ear.  The term often refers to infections or inflammation of the ear. | Example:  The doctor diagnosed an otitic infection.  医生诊断为耳部感染。


The patient presented with otitic symptoms. 病人出现耳部症状。|  近义词：aural, auricular | 反义词：None directly opposite, but words implying the absence of ear-related issues could be considered indirect antonyms (e.g., healthy, normal).
