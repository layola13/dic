nonvicariously: /ˌnɒnvaɪˈkɛəriəsli/ | adj. | 非间接地；非代理地 | Not experiencing something indirectly through another person; directly.  It emphasizes a direct, firsthand experience rather than a secondhand or empathetic one. |  Example:  He experienced the joy of parenthood nonvicariously, having raised his own children. 他非间接地体验到了为人父母的快乐，因为他自己抚养了孩子。


近义词：directly, firsthand, personally


反义词：vicariously, indirectly, secondhand


词性: adjective
