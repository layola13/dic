fossoria:/fɒˈsɔːriə/| adj.|穴居的；挖掘的；适于挖掘的|Relating to or adapted for digging or burrowing.  The term often describes animals that live underground. |例句：Many fossorial animals have strong claws and powerful forelimbs. 许多穴居动物长有强壮的爪子和强壮的前肢。
例句：The fossorial habits of badgers are well-known.  獾的穴居习性众所周知。|近义词：burrowing, subterranean |反义词：arboreal, aerial
