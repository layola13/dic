drugstores:/ˈdrʌɡˌstɔːrz/| n.|药店|A drugstore is a retail store that sells pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and other health and beauty products.  Many also sell a variety of other items, such as snacks, drinks, and stationery. | Example: I went to the drugstore to buy some aspirin and toothpaste. 我去药店买了些阿司匹林和牙膏。|近义词：pharmacy, chemist's (UK)|反义词：N/A
