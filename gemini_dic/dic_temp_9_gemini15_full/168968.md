profited:/ˈprɒfɪtɪd/| v.|获益，有利于|to gain an advantage or benefit from something.|例句：The company profited greatly from the new marketing campaign. 公司从新的营销活动中获益良多。|近义词：benefited, gained, reaped|反义词：lost, suffered


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He profits from his hard work. = 他从他的努力工作中获益。
The company profits from its innovative products. = 公司从其创新产品中获益。

一般过去时:
We profited from the experience. = 我们从这次经历中获益。
The farmers profited handsomely from the good harvest. = 农民们从丰收中获得了丰厚的利润。

现在完成时:
They have profited immensely from their investments. = 他们从投资中获得了巨大的收益。
The charity has profited from the generous donations. = 这家慈善机构从慷慨的捐款中获益。

过去完成时:
She had profited from the lessons learned in the past. = 她从过去吸取的教训中获益。
The business had profited significantly before the economic downturn. = 在经济衰退之前，这家企业已经获得了可观的利润。

将来时:
We will profit from this new technology. = 我们将从这项新技术中获益。
The country will profit from the increased trade. = 这个国家将从贸易增加中获益。

虚拟语气:
If we had invested wisely, we would have profited more. = 如果我们投资明智，我们会获得更多利润。
I wish I had profited from his advice earlier. = 我希望我早点从他的建议中获益。

被动语态:
The community was profited by the new park. = 社区从新的公园中获益。
Much profit was profited from the sale of the property. = 从房产销售中获得了巨额利润。(注意：此句略显冗余，更自然的表达是：Much profit was made from the sale of the property.)

疑问句:
Did you profit from the deal? = 你从这笔交易中获益了吗？
How much did they profit from the venture? = 他们从这项风险投资中获利多少？

否定句:
The company did not profit from the new product launch. = 公司从新产品的发布中没有获利。
We didn't profit as much as we had hoped. = 我们没有获得像我们希望的那样多的利润。
