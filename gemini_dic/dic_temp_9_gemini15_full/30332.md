censures:/ˈsɛnʃərz/| n. v.|谴责；非难；批评|The act of expressing strong disapproval or criticism.  It can refer to the act of criticizing harshly or the criticism itself.|例句：The committee censured the member for misconduct.  委员会因行为不端而谴责该成员。

例句：His actions drew sharp censures from the public. 他的行为招致公众的强烈谴责。


近义词：condemnation, rebuke, reprimand, reproach, criticism|反义词：praise, commendation, approval|名词，动词
