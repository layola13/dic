oligarchization:/ˌɒlɪɡˈɑːrkəzaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |寡头政治化；寡头统治； |The process by which a society or organization comes to be dominated by a small group of powerful individuals.  This process often involves the concentration of wealth and power in the hands of a few, leading to a system where the interests of the elite are prioritized over the needs of the general population. | Example: The rapid oligarchization of the media industry has led to a decline in diverse perspectives and critical reporting.  媒体行业的迅速寡头化导致了多元视角和批判性报道的下降。 The oligarchization of the country's political system resulted in widespread public discontent.  该国政治体制的寡头化导致了广泛的公众不满。|近义词：plutocratisation, monopolization |反义词：democratization
