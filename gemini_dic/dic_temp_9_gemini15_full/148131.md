overseers:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪər/| n. |监督者；管理者；监工|A person who supervises a particular activity or group of workers.|The factory overseers ensured that the workers followed safety regulations. 工厂监工确保工人们遵守安全规章。|supervisor, superintendent, foreman|subordinate, worker|名词
