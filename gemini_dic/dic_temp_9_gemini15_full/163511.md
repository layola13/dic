precopy:/ˈpriːkɒpi/ | v. |预先复制；预先拷贝|To make a copy of something before it is officially released or needed.  This often involves unauthorized copying.|例句：He precopied the files before the server crashed. 他在服务器崩溃之前预先复制了文件。
例句：They precopied the documents to ensure they had backups.他们预先复制了文件以确保有备份。|近义词：copy, duplicate, replicate|反义词：delete, erase|动词
