pullet:/ˈpʊlɪt/| n.|小母鸡，未成年母鸡|A young hen, especially one less than a year old.  |例句：The farmer raised several pullets for egg production.  这个农民养了几只小母鸡来产蛋。|例句：We bought some pullets from the local farm. 我们从当地农场买了几只小母鸡。|近义词：chick, hen (young)|反义词：cock, rooster
