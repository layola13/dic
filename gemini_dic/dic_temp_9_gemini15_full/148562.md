oversystematizes:/ˌoʊvərˌsɪstəˈmætaɪzɪz/ | v. | 过度系统化；使过于系统化 | To organize or arrange something in an excessively systematic or rigid way, often to the point of being counterproductive or inflexible.  | 例句：He oversystematizes his approach to problem-solving, losing sight of creative solutions. 他过度系统化地解决问题，忽略了创造性的解决方案。

The company oversystematizes its workflow, hindering innovation and employee morale. 这家公司过度系统化其工作流程，阻碍了创新和员工士气。


近义词：overorganize, overstructure, overformalize | 反义词：improvise, ad-hoc, informal


词源:

* **over-**:  prefix indicating excess or to a too great extent. (来自表示过度或过分的词缀)
* **system-**: from Greek "systema," meaning "organized whole." (来自希腊语"systema"，意为"有组织的整体")
* **-atize**: suffix meaning "to make or become." (来自表示“使…化”或“变得…”的后缀)

因此，“oversystematizes” 的字面意思是“使过度系统化”。
