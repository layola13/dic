corollaceous:/ˌkɔːrəˈleɪʃəs/ | adj. | 花冠状的；具花冠的 | Having a corolla; relating to a corolla.  The term refers to the structure of a flower, specifically the petals collectively forming a corolla. | Example: The corollaceous flower displayed vibrant colors.  这朵花冠状的花朵展现出鲜艳的色彩。| 近义词：petalled, floral | 反义词：apetalous (lacking petals)
