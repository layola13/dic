knocking:/ˈnɒkɪŋ/ | n. v. |敲击声；敲打 |The act of knocking, or the sound produced by it.  It can refer to the action of hitting a surface repeatedly, or the sound resulting from such action.  |例句：I heard a knocking at the door. 我听到有人敲门。
The persistent knocking annoyed the neighbors.  持续的敲击声惹恼了邻居们。|近义词：rapping, tapping, banging |反义词：silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He knocks on the door every morning. 他每天早上都敲门。
She knocks on wood for good luck. 她敲木头以求好运。

一般过去时:
They knocked loudly at the window. 他们大声敲窗户。
He knocked over the vase accidentally. 他不小心碰倒了花瓶。

现在完成时:
Someone has been knocking at the door for ages. 有人在门外敲门很久了。
The rain has been knocking against the windows all night. 雨整夜都在敲打着窗户。

过去完成时:
The thief had already knocked down the door before the police arrived.  警察到达之前，小偷已经把门撞开了。
She had knocked on every door in the building but no one answered. 她敲了楼里每一家的门，但都没人回应。

将来时:
They will knock on the door again in the morning. 他们明天早上还会敲门。
I will knock you out if you don't stop! 如果你不停下来，我就把你打晕！

虚拟语气:
If he knocked louder, they would have heard him. 如果他敲得更大声一点，他们就会听到他了。
I wish he hadn't knocked over my drink.  我希望他没有打翻我的饮料。

被动语态:
The door was knocked repeatedly. 门被反复敲打。
The vase was knocked over by the cat. 花瓶被猫打翻了。

疑问句:
Who is knocking at the door? 谁在敲门？
Is anyone knocking? 有人在敲门吗？

否定句:
He didn't knock before entering the room. 他没有敲门就进了房间。
Nobody knocked on the door last night. 昨晚没有人敲门。


PHRASE->

knocking spots off:  远远胜过
knocking down:  拆除；撞倒
knocking together: 快速拼凑
