sentimental: /ˌsɛn.tɪˈmɛn.təl/ | adj. | 多愁善感的；感伤的；伤感的；煽情的 |

英文解析:Characterized by or expressing tender, emotional feelings; showing or caused by tender feelings;  evoking or expressing sentiment.  It can also refer to something overly emotional or mawkish.


例句:

She became sentimental when she looked at the old photographs. = 她看着旧照片时变得多愁善感。

The movie had a sentimental ending that made many people cry. = 这部电影有一个感伤的结局，让很多人哭了。


近义词:emotional, tender, romantic, nostalgic, mawkish, mushy


反义词:unemotional, apathetic, hard-hearted, callous


词源:

Originating from the late Middle English word "sentiment," derived from the Old French "sentiment," ultimately from the Latin "sentire," meaning "to feel."  The -al suffix transforms the noun into an adjective.
