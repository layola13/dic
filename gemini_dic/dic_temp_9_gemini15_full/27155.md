call-out:/ˈkɔːlˌaʊt/| n. vt.|大声喊叫；点名批评；（对产品或服务的）宣传；(代码的)外部调用|A loud call-out for help.  大声呼救。|The manager gave him a call-out for his poor performance. 经理因其表现不佳而公开批评了他。|近义词：shout, yell, criticism, advertisement |反义词：silence, praise


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They call out the names of the winners. = 他们大声念出获胜者的名字。
We call out for help when we are in trouble. = 我们遇到麻烦时大声呼救。


一般过去时:
He called out my name during the meeting. = 他在会议上叫了我的名字。
The soldiers called out the enemy's position. = 士兵们喊出了敌人的位置。


现在完成时:
She has called out for help several times. = 她已经多次呼救了。
They have called out the names of all the participants. = 他们已经念出了所有参与者的名字。


过去完成时:
He had called out a warning before the accident happened. = 事故发生前他已经发出警告了。
They had called out the fire alarm. = 他们已经拉响了火警。


将来时:
They will call out the names of the finalists. = 他们将念出决赛选手的名字。
We will call out for help if we get lost in the woods. = 如果我们在森林里迷路了，我们会呼救。


虚拟语气:
If I were in trouble, I would call out for help. = 如果我遇到麻烦，我会呼救。
I wish he had called out the fire alarm sooner. = 我希望他早点拉响火警。


被动语态:
His name was called out last. = 他的名字最后被念到。
The problem was called out by the manager. = 这个问题被经理指出来了。


疑问句:
Did they call out your name? = 他们叫你的名字了吗？
Why did he call out his colleague? = 他为什么斥责他的同事？


否定句:
He didn't call out for help. = 他没有呼救。
They didn't call out my name. = 他们没有叫我的名字。


PHRASE->
call out for help = 呼救
