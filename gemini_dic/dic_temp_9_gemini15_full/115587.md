maraud:/məˈrɔːd/| v.| 抢劫；掠夺；四处劫掠 | To roam or raid in search of plunder; to go about pillaging.  |例句：The Vikings marauded the coastal villages. 维京人袭击了沿海村庄。

例句：Bands of robbers marauded through the countryside. 一伙强盗在乡间四处抢劫。


近义词：raid, plunder, loot, pillage, foray


反义词：protect, defend, safeguard


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They maraud the countryside for food. = 他们四处劫掠乡村以获取食物。
The army marauds the enemy territory. = 军队掠夺敌方领土。


一般过去时:
The bandits marauded the town last night. = 强盗昨晚洗劫了这座城镇。
The soldiers marauded the village after the battle. = 战斗结束后，士兵们洗劫了村庄。


现在完成时:
The rebels have marauded several villages this year. = 叛军今年已经洗劫了几个村庄。
The army has marauded the enemy's supply lines. = 军队已经破坏了敌人的补给线。


过去完成时:
The villagers had marauded the neighboring farms before the army arrived. = 在军队到达之前，村民们已经洗劫了邻近的农场。
The invaders had marauded the capital before the king surrendered. = 在国王投降之前，入侵者已经洗劫了首都。


将来时:
The pirates will maraud the shipping lanes tomorrow. = 海盗明天将袭击航运线路。
They will maraud the city if we don't defend it. = 如果我们不加以防御，他们将会洗劫这座城市。


虚拟语气:
If they marauded the city, there would be chaos. = 如果他们洗劫了这座城市，就会一片混乱。
I wish they hadn't marauded the village. = 我真希望他们没有洗劫那个村庄。


被动语态:
The village was marauded by bandits. = 村庄被强盗洗劫了。
The city was marauded last night. = 城市昨晚被洗劫了。


疑问句:
Did the soldiers maraud the town? = 士兵们洗劫了城镇吗？
Were the villages marauded by the invading army? = 村庄是被入侵军队洗劫的吗？


否定句:
The army did not maraud the civilian population. = 军队没有袭击平民。
They didn't maraud the villages, they only searched them. = 他们没有洗劫村庄，只是搜查了它们。
