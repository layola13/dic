chauvinisms:/ˈʃoʊvənɪzəmz/ | n. | 沙文主义；盲目爱国主义 |  Extreme or aggressive patriotism, often manifested in belief in the superiority of one's own nation or group. Chauvinisms are often characterized by intolerance towards other cultures or nationalities.  | Example:  His chauvinisms were evident in his constant denigration of foreign films and music.  他的沙文主义在他不断贬低外国电影和音乐中显而易见。

|近义词: jingoism, nationalism, xenophobia |反义词: cosmopolitanism, internationalism |
