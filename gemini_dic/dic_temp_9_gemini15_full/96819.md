inescapable:/ɪnˈɛskəpəbl/ | adj. | 不可避免的，无法逃避的 |  Impossible to avoid or escape.  | The truth is inescapable; we must face it. 真相是不可避免的；我们必须面对它。 | unavoidable, inevitable, certain | avoidable, escapable | 形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The consequences of his actions are inescapable. = 他行为的后果是不可避免的。
This problem seems inescapable to me. = 对我来说这个问题似乎无法避免。


一般过去时:
The feeling of loneliness was inescapable during the long journey. = 在漫长的旅途中，孤独感是无法逃避的。
His guilt was inescapable after the accident. = 事故发生后，他的内疚感是无法逃避的。


现在完成时:
The need for change has become inescapable. = 变革的需要已经变得不可避免。
The impact of climate change has become inescapable. = 气候变化的影响已经变得不可避免。


过去完成时:
By then, the truth had become inescapable. = 到那时，真相已经变得不可避免了。
It had become inescapable that they would lose the war. = 他们将输掉战争已经变得不可避免了。


将来时:
Further delays are inescapable. = 进一步的延误是不可避免的。
The eventual decline of the empire was inescapable. = 帝国最终的衰落是不可避免的。


虚拟语气:
If we continue down this path, disaster would be inescapable. = 如果我们继续沿着这条路走下去，灾难将是不可避免的。
Were we to ignore the warnings, the consequences would be inescapable. = 如果我们忽视警告，后果将是不可避免的。


被动语态:
The issue cannot be escaped; it is inescapable. = 这个问题无法逃避；它是不可避免的。
The problem was inescapable; there was no solution. = 这个问题是不可避免的；没有解决办法。


疑问句:
Is this feeling of despair inescapable? = 这种绝望的感觉是不可避免的吗？
Is there any way to avoid this inescapable fate? = 有什么办法可以避免这种不可避免的命运吗？


否定句:
The situation is not inescapable; we can find a solution. = 情况并非不可避免；我们可以找到解决方案。
The outcome is not necessarily inescapable; we can still fight for a better future. = 结果并非必然不可避免；我们仍然可以为更美好的未来而奋斗。
