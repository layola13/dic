pram:/præm/| n. |婴儿车，童车 |A pram is a small carriage, typically with four wheels, used for carrying babies.  |例句：She pushed the pram through the park. 她推着婴儿车穿过公园。|近义词：baby carriage, stroller, pushchair |反义词：无
