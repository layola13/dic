moratorium:/mɔːrəˈtɔːriəm/| n. |暂停；延期；中止；暂缓执行|A temporary suspension of an activity, especially of a law or a legal process.  A moratorium is usually put in place for a specific period of time to address a particular issue or problem. |例句：The government imposed a moratorium on logging in the rainforest.  政府对雨林的伐木活动实施了暂停令。

例句：A moratorium on new nuclear power plants was declared.  一项关于新建核电站的暂停令被宣布。


近义词：suspension, postponement, delay, stay, cessation|反义词：continuation, resumption, commencement|词性：名词


词源：源自拉丁语 *morari "to delay" + -torium (suffix forming nouns of place or purpose)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
A moratorium is in effect. = 现在正处于暂停状态。
The bank declares a moratorium on loan repayments. = 银行宣布暂停贷款偿还。


一般过去时:
There was a moratorium on building new houses. = 曾经有一段时期禁止建造新房子。
The government imposed a moratorium last year. = 政府去年实施了暂停令。


现在完成时:
They have declared a moratorium on fishing. = 他们已经宣布暂停捕鱼。
A moratorium has been in place for five years. = 暂停令已经实施了五年。


过去完成时:
A moratorium had already been implemented before the crisis worsened. = 在危机恶化之前，已经实施了暂停令。
They had lifted the moratorium before the new law was passed. = 在新法律通过之前，他们已经取消了暂停令。


将来时:
The committee will propose a moratorium next month. = 委员会下个月将提出一项暂停令。
A moratorium will be put in place to allow for further investigation. = 将会实施暂停令以进行进一步调查。


虚拟语气:
If we had a moratorium, the situation would improve. = 如果我们有暂停令，情况就会好转。
I wish there were a moratorium on the use of plastics. = 我希望塑料的使用能够暂停。


被动语态:
A moratorium was imposed by the court. = 法院实施了暂停令。
The moratorium is being reviewed by the council. = 暂停令正在由议会审查。


疑问句:
Is there a moratorium on the sale of that product? = 销售该产品是否暂停了？
Will a moratorium be implemented soon? = 暂停令很快就会实施吗？


否定句:
There is no moratorium on arms sales. = 军售并没有暂停。
The government did not impose a moratorium. = 政府没有实施暂停令。
