It can also refer to a spiral staircase or a coil of something.|例句：The garden was full of caracoles.  花园里满是蜗牛。|例句：The DNA molecule resembles a caracol. DNA分子像一个螺旋。|近义词：snail, spiral, helix |反义词：straight, linear
