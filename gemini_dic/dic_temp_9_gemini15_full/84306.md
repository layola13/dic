half-wickets: /ˌhɑːfˈwɪkɪts/ | n. | 半球门 |  Two areas on a cricket field, just outside the wickets, where a batsman can be dismissed.  These areas are considered less dangerous than the wickets themselves. | The batsman was dismissed by a run-out at the half-wickets.  击球手在半球门处被跑出局。  | 近义词:  (No direct synonyms, but could be described as areas outside the wickets) | 反义词: wickets (the main stumps) | 名词
