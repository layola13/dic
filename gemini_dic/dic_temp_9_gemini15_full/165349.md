preissues:/ˈpriːɪʃuːz/| n.|预发行物；预先发行 |Things issued or released before the official release.  Pre-issues can include beta versions of software, early access to a product or service, or advance copies of a publication. |例句：The company released preissues of the software to selected testers. 公司向选定的测试人员发布了该软件的预发行版。|例句：The preissues of the book generated significant buzz among readers. 这本书的预发行版在读者中引起了很大的轰动。|近义词：advance copies, previews, early releases |反义词：final releases, post-releases|名词
