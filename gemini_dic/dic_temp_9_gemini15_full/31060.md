chamferers:/ˈtʃæmfərərz/| n.| 倒角器，斜面加工机 | A chamferer is a tool or machine used to create a chamfer, a beveled edge or corner, on a workpiece.  It can be a hand tool or a more sophisticated machine used in industrial settings. | Example: The machinist used a precision chamferer to create a smooth, 45-degree chamfer on the metal part.  倒角工用精密倒角器在金属部件上加工出平滑的45度倒角。|近义词：beveler, edge breaker|反义词：无
