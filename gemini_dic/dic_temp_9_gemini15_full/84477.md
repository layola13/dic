halted:/ˈhɔːltɪd/| v. |停止；中断；阻碍|To bring or come to a stop; to cease or discontinue.|The train halted suddenly. 火车突然停了。|The negotiations were halted due to a disagreement. 由于意见分歧，谈判停止了。|stopped, ceased, arrested|continued, proceeded|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He halts his car at the red light. 他在红灯前停车。
She halts her work to answer the phone. 她停下手头的工作去接电话。


一般过去时:
The army halted its advance. 军队停止了前进。
He halted abruptly at the edge of the cliff. 他在悬崖边突然停了下来。


现在完成时:
The project has been halted due to funding issues.  由于资金问题，项目已经暂停了。
They have halted production until further notice.  他们已暂停生产，直至另行通知。


过去完成时:
The game had been halted by the heavy rain. 大雨使得比赛暂停了。
She had halted her studies to travel the world. 她曾暂停学业去环游世界。


将来时:
The government will halt the construction of the dam. 政府将停止大坝的建设。
They will halt the meeting if the situation worsens. 如果情况恶化，他们将暂停会议。


虚拟语气:
If the police had not halted the suspect, he would have escaped. 如果警方没有阻止嫌犯，他就会逃脱了。
I wish the rain would halt so we can continue our picnic.  我希望雨能停下来，这样我们就可以继续野餐了。


被动语态:
The traffic was halted by the accident. 交通事故导致交通中断。
The meeting was halted because of a power outage.  由于停电，会议被暂停了。


疑问句:
Did they halt the procession? 他们是否停止了游行？
Will the construction be halted? 建设将会停止吗？


否定句:
He did not halt his car despite the warning signs.  尽管有警告标志，他仍然没有停车。
The workers did not halt their strike despite the company's offer. 工人们尽管接受了公司的提议，但并没有停止罢工。
