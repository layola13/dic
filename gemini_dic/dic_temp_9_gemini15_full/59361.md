earnest:/ˈɜːrnɪst/| adj.| 认真的；诚挚的；严肃的；重要的 |Serious and sincere; showing deep feeling.  It can also refer to something done with great effort or determination. |例句：He spoke with earnest conviction. 他带着诚挚的信念说话。

例句：She approached the task with earnest effort. 她认真努力地完成了这项任务。

近义词：serious, sincere, genuine, heartfelt, dedicated

反义词：insincere, frivolous, casual, joking, superficial

词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is earnest in his beliefs. = 他对自己的信仰很认真。
She is earnest about her studies. = 她对学习很认真。


一般过去时:
He was earnest in his apology. = 他道歉很真诚。
She was earnest in her request. = 她请求很恳切。


现在完成时:
He has been earnest in his efforts to help. = 他一直认真努力地帮助别人。
She has been earnest in her pursuit of knowledge. = 她一直认真追求知识。


过去完成时:
He had been earnest in his warnings, but nobody listened. = 他曾经认真地警告过，但没有人听。
She had been earnest in her attempts to reconcile them, but failed. = 她曾认真地试图调解他们，但失败了。


将来时:
He will be earnest in his negotiations. = 他将在谈判中表现得很认真。
She will be earnest in her presentation. = 她将在演示中表现得很认真。


虚拟语气:
If he were more earnest, he would succeed. = 如果他更认真一些，他就会成功。
If she had been more earnest, she would have finished the project on time. = 如果她更认真一些，她就能按时完成项目了。


被动语态:
His commitment was viewed as earnest. = 他的承诺被认为是真诚的。
Her efforts were seen as earnest attempts to improve the situation. = 她的努力被视为改善局势的认真尝试。


疑问句:
Is he earnest in his intentions? = 他的意图是认真的吗？
Is she earnest about her promise? = 她对自己的承诺是认真的吗？


否定句:
He was not earnest in his approach. = 他在处理方法上并不认真。
She wasn't earnest in her denial. = 她否认时并不认真。


PHRASE->
in earnest: 认真地；严肃地
make earnest efforts: 认真努力
earnestly believe: 认真相信
earnestly desire: 渴望
with earnest attention: 专注地


词源:
The word "earnest" ultimately derives from the Old English word "earnest," meaning "money paid as a pledge."  This reflects the idea of something being serious and committed, like a serious pledge.  Over time, the meaning shifted to encompass the sense of sincerity and seriousness of purpose.
