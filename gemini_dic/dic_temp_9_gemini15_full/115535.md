mapendo:/mɑːˈpɛndoʊ/| n.|深爱，挚爱|A deep and abiding love; a profound affection, often romantic in nature.  It is a Swahili word expressing a love that is intense, passionate, and enduring.|例句：My mapendo for you knows no bounds. 我对你的爱是无边无际的。  She expressed her mapendo in a heartfelt letter. 她在一封充满深情的信中表达了她的爱意。|近义词：love, adoration, affection, devotion|反义词：hate, loathing, indifference|名词
