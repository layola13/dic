raceing:/ˈreɪsɪŋ/| v.|赛跑；竞赛；急速行进|The act of participating in a race or moving very quickly.  It can refer to various types of races, from horse racing to car racing to foot races.|例句：The horses were racing towards the finish line.  赛马正在冲向终点线。

例句：The car was racing down the highway. 这辆车正沿着高速公路飞驰。


近义词：competing, speeding, sprinting, dashing|反义词：crawling, dawdling, idling

词性: 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is racing his friends. 他正在和朋友们赛跑。
She races every weekend. 她每个周末都参加比赛。


一般过去时:
They raced their cars yesterday. 他们昨天赛车了。
He raced to the hospital. 他飞奔到医院。


现在完成时:
I have raced in many competitions. 我参加过许多比赛。
She has raced against the best. 她曾与最强者竞争。


过去完成时:
He had raced before, but this time was different. 他以前参加过比赛，但这次不一样。
They had raced to the scene before the police arrived. 警察到达之前，他们已经赶到了现场。


将来时:
We will race again next year. 我们明年还会再比赛。
They will be racing in the finals. 他们将在决赛中比赛。


虚拟语气:
If I were racing, I would win. 如果我参赛，我会赢。
I wish I were racing right now. 我真希望我现在正在比赛。


被动语态:
The race was raced fairly. 比赛是公平进行的。(此处被动语态略显不自然，通常不用被动)
The cars are being raced on the track. 赛车正在赛道上比赛。(此处被动语态略显不自然，通常不用被动)


疑问句:
Are they racing today? 他们今天比赛吗？
Is he racing in the Olympics? 他参加奥运会比赛吗？


否定句:
He isn't racing today. 他今天不比赛。
They didn't race last week. 他们上周没有比赛。
