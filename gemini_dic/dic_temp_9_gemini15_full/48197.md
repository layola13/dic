decompressions:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛʃənz/ | n. |减压；减压过程 | The process of reducing pressure, especially in a confined space or on a body.  It can refer to the gradual reduction of pressure on a diver ascending from depth, the release of pressure in a sealed container, or the lessening of emotional or psychological stress. |例句：The divers underwent a slow decompression after their deep-sea dive.  潜水员在深海潜水后进行了缓慢的减压。

The rapid decompression of the aircraft cabin caused serious injuries. 飞机客舱的快速减压造成了严重的伤害。|近义词：pressure release, depressurization |反义词：compression, pressurization |名词
