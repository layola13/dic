chronogrammatist: /krəˈnɒɡrəməˌtɪst/ | n. | 编年史家，纪年诗作者 | A person who composes chronograms.  A chronogrammatist skillfully incorporates dates into their writing, often using the numerical values of letters to spell out a significant year. |  The renowned chronogrammatist cleverly embedded the year of the battle in the poem's title.  著名的纪年诗作者巧妙地将战役年份嵌入诗歌标题。|  epigrammatist, poet |  (无明显反义词) | 名词
