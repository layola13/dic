landsliding:/ˈlændˌslaɪdɪŋ/| n. adj.|滑坡；山崩；（指选举中）压倒性胜利 |The process of land sliding down a slope, often due to gravity and weakened soil or rock. Landsliding can cause significant damage and loss of life. |例句：The heavy rains triggered a massive landsliding, burying several villages. 大雨引发了大规模山体滑坡，掩埋了几个村庄。  The landslide victory secured her position as the new party leader.  压倒性的胜利确保了她成为新党领袖的地位。|近义词：mudslide, avalanche, slump |反义词：stable, secure
