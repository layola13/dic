quire:/ˈkwaɪər/| n. |  一打（纸张）；合唱队；纸张；要求 | A quire is a unit of paper, traditionally consisting of 24 sheets. It can also refer to a choir or a group of singers.  |  The printer used a quire of paper to print the pamphlets.  打印机用一打纸张来印刷小册子。  The church choir sang beautifully. 教堂合唱团唱得非常优美。 |  dozen, sheaf |  single sheet (in the paper sense) | 名词
