epitomist: /ɪˈpɪtəmɪst/ | n. | 典型人物；缩影；代表 | A person or thing that embodies or exemplifies a particular quality or characteristic.  Someone who epitomizes something serves as a perfect example of it.  |  He is the epitomist of modern entrepreneurship. 他是现代企业家精神的典型代表。  |近义词：exemplar, embodiment, archetype | 反义词：antithesis, opposite
