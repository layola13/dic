importunate:/ɪmˈpɔːrtʃənət/| adj.| 纠缠不休的；急切恳求的；强求的 | excessively persistent in making demands; troublesomely urgent.  |例句：The importunate beggar would not leave him alone.  那个纠缠不休的乞丐不肯离开他。|例句：His importunate requests for a loan finally wore down his friend. 他不断恳求贷款，最终磨掉了朋友的耐心。|近义词：persistent, insistent, demanding, urgent, pressing |反义词：yielding, compliant, patient, unassuming

词源:
From Middle French importun, from Latin importunus, from in- ("not") + portunus ("convenient, opportune").  The original meaning implied "inconvenient" or "untimely," evolving to its current sense of persistent and bothersome demands.
