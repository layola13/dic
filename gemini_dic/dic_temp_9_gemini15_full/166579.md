prescriptions:/prɪˈskrɪpʃənz/ | n. |处方，规定，指示 |Instructions from a doctor that authorize the dispensing of medication.|The doctor wrote me a prescription for antibiotics.  医生给我开了抗生素处方。|  formula, order, mandate |cure, remedy |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The doctor writes prescriptions for her patients every day. = 医生每天都为她的病人开处方。
He often gives prescriptions for pain relief. = 他经常开止痛药的处方。

一般过去时:
The pharmacist filled the prescriptions yesterday. = 药剂师昨天配好了处方药。
She received prescriptions for her allergies last week. = 她上周拿到了过敏症的处方。


现在完成时:
The doctor has written many prescriptions this month. = 这个月医生已经开了很多处方。
Have you filled all your prescriptions yet? = 你所有的处方都配好了吗？


过去完成时:
He had already filled the prescriptions before the patient arrived. = 病人到达之前，他已经配好了处方药。
She had not received her prescriptions before the appointment. = 她预约之前还没有拿到处方。


将来时:
The doctor will write a new prescription tomorrow. = 医生明天会开一个新的处方。
They will need to fill the prescriptions next week. = 他们下周需要去配药。


虚拟语气:
If I had a prescription, I would take the medicine. = 如果我有处方，我会吃药。
I wish I had a prescription for this headache. = 我真希望我有治这种头痛的处方。


被动语态:
The prescriptions were filled quickly. = 处方很快就配好了。
The medications are prescribed by a doctor. = 药物是由医生开的。


疑问句:
Do you need a prescription for this medication? = 你需要这个药的处方吗？
How many prescriptions did you get today? = 你今天拿到了多少处方？


否定句:
This medicine doesn't require a prescription. = 这药不需要处方。
She didn't have a prescription for the strong painkillers. = 她没有强效止痛药的处方。
