quasi-desperate:/ˈkwɑːziˌdɛspərət/| adj.|近乎绝望的，非常绝望的 |Almost desperate; bordering on desperation.  It suggests a state of near desperation, but not quite fully reaching it.  The word emphasizes the proximity to complete desperation.|例句：He was in a quasi-desperate attempt to save his failing business. 他为了拯救他即将倒闭的生意而几乎绝望地尝试着。

He made a quasi-desperate plea for help. 他几乎绝望地恳求帮助。|近义词：near-desperate, almost desperate, bordering on desperate|反义词：calm, composed, hopeful, optimistic|词性:形容词
