signallers:/ˈsɪɡnələr/| n.|信号兵，信号员|People who send and receive signals, especially in military or railway contexts.  They are responsible for the accurate and timely transmission of information using various methods, such as flags, lights, or electronic devices.|例句：The signallers ensured clear communication between the two battalions. 信号兵确保了两个营之间的清晰沟通。

例句：Experienced railway signallers are crucial for safe train operations. 经验丰富的铁路信号员对于安全的火车运行至关重要。


近义词：signalmen, communicators, dispatchers|反义词：recipients (in a broad sense)|词性:n.
