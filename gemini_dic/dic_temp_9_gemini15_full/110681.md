line-caught: /ˈlaɪn kɔːt/ | adj. | 线钓的，用钓线钓到的 |  Describes fish or other seafood that have been caught using a fishing line and hook, as opposed to methods like netting or trapping.  It often implies a more sustainable and selective fishing practice. | Example:  The restaurant prides itself on serving only line-caught tuna. 这家餐厅以只供应钓获的金枪鱼而自豪。  The line-caught salmon was incredibly fresh.  这条用钓线钓到的鲑鱼非常新鲜。 | 近义词: hook-caught, rod-caught | 反义词: net-caught, trapped
