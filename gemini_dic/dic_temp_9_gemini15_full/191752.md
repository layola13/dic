scimitars:/ˈsɪmɪtɑːrz/| n.|弯刀；克力斯弯刀|A scimitar is a curved sword, typically of Middle Eastern or Asian origin, with a single-edged blade that curves toward the point.  They were historically used by cavalry and infantry. |例句：The warrior brandished his scimitar, ready for battle.  战士挥舞着他的弯刀，准备战斗。

The museum displayed a collection of ancient scimitars. 博物馆展出了一批古代弯刀。|近义词：curved sword, saber, tulwar|反义词：straight sword
