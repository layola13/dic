goitrogenous:/ˌɡɔɪˈtrɒdʒənəs/| adj.|致甲状腺肿的；产生致甲状腺肿物质的|Causing or producing goiter; relating to goitrogens.|Certain foods, like cruciferous vegetables, are goitrogenous and can interfere with thyroid function if consumed in large quantities.  某些食物，例如十字花科蔬菜，具有致甲状腺肿的特性，如果大量食用可能会干扰甲状腺功能。|近义词：thyroid-disrupting|反义词：thyroid-supporting
