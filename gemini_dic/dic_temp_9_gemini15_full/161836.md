practician:/prækˈtɪʃənər/| n.|从业者，实践者|A practician is a person who regularly practices a profession, skill, or art.  It emphasizes the practical application of knowledge and skill.|例句：He's a highly respected practician in the field of alternative medicine. 他是另类医学领域一位备受尊敬的从业者。|近义词：practitioner, professional, expert|反义词：amateur, novice, dilettante
