disputing:/dɪˈspjuːtɪŋ/| v. |争论；质疑；辩论 |The act of arguing or contesting something.  It implies a disagreement or conflict over a particular issue or point. |例句：They are disputing the terms of the contract. 他们正在就合同条款进行争论。
例句：He is disputing the accuracy of the report. 他正在质疑报告的准确性。|近义词：arguing, debating, contesting, challenging |反义词：agreeing, accepting, conceding |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are disputing the ownership of the land. = 他们正在争夺土地的所有权。
We are disputing the bill at the restaurant. = 我们正在和餐厅就账单进行争论。

一般过去时:
They disputed the election results. = 他们对选举结果提出异议。
She disputed his claim that he was innocent. = 她驳斥了他无罪的主张。

现在完成时:
They have been disputing the matter for weeks. = 他们已经就此事争论了几周了。
We have disputed this point several times. = 我们已经多次争论过这一点。

过去完成时:
They had disputed the issue before the meeting. = 他们在会议之前就已经就这个问题争论过了。
She had disputed the findings of the study before it was published. = 在研究结果发表之前，她就对其提出异议。

将来时:
They will dispute the decision in court. = 他们将在法庭上对该决定提出异议。
We will dispute the charges on our credit card bill. = 我们将对信用卡账单上的费用提出异议。

虚拟语气:
If they disputed the findings, the study would have to be redone. = 如果他们对研究结果提出异议，这项研究就必须重新进行。
I wish they hadn't disputed the plan so vehemently. = 我真希望他们不要那么激烈地反对这个计划。

被动语态:
The decision was disputed by many people. = 许多人都对这个决定提出异议。
The findings of the report are being disputed. = 报告的调查结果正在受到质疑。

疑问句:
Are they disputing the contract terms? = 他们是否正在就合同条款进行争论？
Is he disputing the evidence presented? = 他是否正在质疑所提供的证据？

否定句:
They did not dispute the facts. = 他们没有对事实提出异议。
She did not dispute his authority. = 她没有质疑他的权威。


PHRASE->
dispute over = 关于……的争论
dispute with = 与……争论
dispute about = 关于……的争执
dispute the claim = 对……的主张提出异议
dispute settlement = 纠纷解决
