panically: /ˈpænɪkli/ | adv. |惊慌地，恐慌地 |  Describes an action performed in a state of panic or fear.  It implies a sudden and uncontrolled feeling of fear or anxiety. |例句：He looked around panically. 他惊慌地环顾四周。

She screamed panically when she saw the snake. 她看到蛇时惊慌地尖叫起来。|近义词：frantically, wildly, hysterically |反义词：calmly, serenely, peacefully |副词
