familiarising: /fəˈmɪliəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使熟悉；使了解 |  The process of making someone or something familiar.  It implies a gradual process of becoming acquainted with something new. | 例句：The museum is familiarising visitors with the history of the city.  博物馆正在让游客熟悉这座城市的历史。

例句：He spent months familiarising himself with the software before launching the project. 他在启动项目之前花了几个月的时间熟悉软件。


近义词：acquainting, introducing, orienting, acclimating


反义词：estranging, alienating, distancing


词性：动词 (现在分词)


词源：来自familiar (熟悉的) + -ising (现在分词后缀)  familiar源于拉丁语familiaris，意思是“家庭的”，引申为“熟悉的”。
