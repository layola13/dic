maharishi:/məˈhɑːrɪʃi/| n. |圣人，大师，贤者 |A maharishi is a great sage, seer, or rishi, often considered to have attained enlightenment or a high level of spiritual realization.  The term is primarily used in Hinduism and related traditions.|例句：The maharishi gave a profound lecture on the nature of reality.  这位圣人作了一场关于现实本质的深刻演讲。|近义词：sage, seer, guru, saint, wise man |反义词：fool, ignoramus
