overaccelerates:/ˌoʊvəræˈsɛləreɪts/| v.|超速加速；过度加速|To accelerate beyond a safe or intended speed or rate.|The car overaccelerated on the icy road, causing it to skid.|近义词：speed up excessively, accelerate rapidly|反义词：decelerate, slow down
