postmenstrual: /ˌpoʊstˈmɛnstruəl/ | adj. | 绝经后的，月经后的 | Relating to the time after menstruation.  This term typically refers to the period after a woman's menstrual cycle has ceased, often associated with menopause but can also refer to the period immediately following menstruation.  | Example: Postmenstrual bleeding can sometimes indicate underlying health issues. 绝经后出血有时可能暗示潜在的健康问题。 |近义词: postmenopausal (for menopause specifically) | 反义词: premenstrual, menstrual
