discontentful: /ˌdɪskənˈtɛntfəl/ | adj. | 不满的；不安的 |  Feeling or expressing discontent; dissatisfied.  This word is less common than "discontented". |  例句：He felt discontentful about his low salary. 他对自己的低薪感到不满。

例句：Her discontentful expression showed her displeasure with the decision. 她不满的表情显示了她对这个决定的不悦。


近义词：discontented, dissatisfied, unhappy, disgruntled | 反义词：content, satisfied, happy, pleased | 词性: adjective


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is discontentful with his current job. = 他对目前的工作感到不满。
She is discontentful about the lack of progress. = 她对缺乏进展感到不满。


一般过去时:
He was discontentful with the outcome of the meeting. = 他对会议的结果感到不满。
She was discontentful after receiving the bad news. = 她收到坏消息后感到不满。


现在完成时:
He has been discontentful for many months. = 他已经不满好几个月了。
She has been discontentful since the company's restructuring. = 自从公司重组以来，她就一直感到不满。


过去完成时:
He had been discontentful for years before he finally quit his job. = 他在最终辞职之前已经不满多年了。
She had been discontentful with her life before she decided to make a change. = 在她决定改变之前，她对自己的生活一直感到不满。


将来时:
He will likely be discontentful if he doesn't get a promotion. = 如果他不升职，他很可能会感到不满。
She will probably be discontentful unless the situation improves. = 除非情况好转，否则她可能会感到不满。


虚拟语气:
If he were discontentful, he would complain. = 如果他感到不满，他会抱怨。
I wish I weren't discontentful with my life. = 我希望我不对我的生活感到不满。


被动语态:

The discontentful comments were addressed in the meeting. = 会议上解决了不满的评论。


疑问句:

否定句:

They were not discontentful with the results. = 他们对结果并不感到不满。
