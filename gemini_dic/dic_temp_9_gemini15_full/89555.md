honorifics:/ˌɑːnəˈrɪfɪks/ | n. |敬称，尊称 |Titles or expressions of respect used in addressing or referring to someone.  They are often used to show politeness, deference, or social status. Examples include "Mr.", "Mrs.", "Ms.", "Dr.", "Professor," and titles specific to certain cultures and professions. |例句：Using appropriate honorifics is crucial in many Asian cultures.  在许多亚洲文化中，使用合适的敬称至关重要。

例句：The email lacked proper honorifics, making it seem unprofessional. 这封邮件缺乏合适的敬称，显得不专业。
