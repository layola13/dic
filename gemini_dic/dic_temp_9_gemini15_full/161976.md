prate:/preɪt/| v.|喋喋不休地谈论；夸夸其谈|to talk continuously and at length about unimportant things; to boast or brag.|He was prating on about his latest business deal. 他喋喋不休地谈论着他最新的生意交易。|近义词：babble, chatter, gabble, ramble|反义词：be silent, be quiet

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He prates endlessly about politics. 他没完没了地大谈政治。
She prates about her accomplishments all day. 她整天夸夸其谈自己的成就。

一般过去时:
He prated on about his travels for hours. 他滔滔不绝地讲了他几个小时的旅行经历。
She prated incessantly about her new dress. 她喋喋不休地谈论她的新衣服。

现在完成时:
He has prated about his wealth since he won the lottery. 自从他赢得彩票以来，他就一直夸耀他的财富。
She has prated so much that everyone is tired of listening. 她说了太多，以至于每个人都听烦了。

过去完成时:
He had prated for so long that everyone had left the room. 他说了很久，以至于每个人都离开了房间。
She had prated about her problems before she finally asked for help. 在她最终寻求帮助之前，她一直在喋喋不休地抱怨她的问题。

将来时:
He will prate about his success at the party. 他将在派对上夸耀他的成功。
She will prate incessantly if you give her the chance. 如果你给她机会，她会喋喋不休地说话。

虚拟语气:
If he prated less, people would listen more. 如果他少说些话，人们就会多听一些。
I wish she wouldn't prate so much about herself. 我希望她不要再那么多地谈论自己了。

被动语态:
His achievements were prated about by everyone. 他的成就被每个人夸夸其谈。
Her problems were prated about to anyone who would listen. 她的问题被她告诉任何愿意倾听的人。

疑问句:
Does he prate so much all the time? 他总是那么多话吗？
Why does she prate about such trivial matters? 她为什么喋喋不休地谈论这些琐碎的事情？

否定句:
He didn't prate about his failures. 他没有夸耀他的失败。
She didn't prate, she listened attentively. 她没有喋喋不休，而是专心致志地听着。
