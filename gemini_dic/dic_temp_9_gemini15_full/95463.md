incitants:/ɪnˈsaɪtənts/ | n. |煽动者；刺激物 | Things that incite or stimulate;  agents that provoke or encourage a particular action or feeling.  Incitants can be people, events, or ideas. |例句：The inflammatory speeches acted as incitants to violence. 煽动性的演讲成了暴力事件的导火索。


The rise in unemployment was a major incitant to social unrest. 失业率上升是社会动荡的主要刺激因素。|近义词：provocateurs, stimulants, catalysts, instigators |反义词：deterrents, inhibitors, suppressants |名词
