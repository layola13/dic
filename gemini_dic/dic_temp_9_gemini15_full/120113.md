mimically:/ˈmɪmɪkli/| adv.| 模仿地，模拟地 | In a way that mimics or imitates.  It describes an action or behavior that copies or resembles something else. |例句：He mimicked the teacher's gestures mimically. 他模仿老师的手势，模仿得很像。

例句：She spoke mimically, copying the accent of the person she was describing. 她模仿着说话，模仿着她描述的那个人的口音。


近义词：imitatively, mockingly,  simulatively

反义词：authentically, genuinely, originally


词性：副词
