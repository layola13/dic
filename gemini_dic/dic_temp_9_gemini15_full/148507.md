oversubscription:/ˌoʊvər səbˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. | 超额认购，认购过度 | The state of having more subscriptions or applications for something than there are available resources or spaces.  This often occurs with popular events, investments, or services. | Example: The concert experienced oversubscription; tickets sold out within minutes.  这场音乐会的门票超额认购，几分钟内就售罄了。 | 近义词：overdemand, excess demand | 反义词：undersubscription
