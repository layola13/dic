interspace:/ˈɪntərˌspeɪs/| n. |间隔；中间空间；星际空间|The space between things;  the area between objects or points.  It can also refer to the space between stars in outer space.|例句：The interspace between the lines of text was too narrow.  文本行之间的间隔太窄了。|例句：Scientists are exploring the vast interspace between galaxies.科学家们正在探索星系之间的广阔星际空间。|近义词：gap, interval, space, void|反义词：contact, proximity
