girds:/ɡɜːrdz/| vt.|束紧；使…做好准备；包围；袭击|To fasten or secure (something) tightly; to prepare oneself or something for a difficult situation; to encircle; to attack. |例句：She girded herself for the difficult task ahead. 她为即将到来的艰难任务做好了准备。 |例句：The soldiers girded the city. 士兵们包围了这座城市。|近义词：prepare, brace, surround, encircle |反义词：unfasten, loosen, relax


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He girds his sword. = 他束紧他的剑。
She girds herself for the challenge. = 她为挑战做好准备。

一般过去时:
The knight girded on his armor. = 骑士穿上了他的盔甲。
They girded the wagon wheels with chains. = 他们用链条捆绑了马车车轮。

现在完成时:
She has girded herself with courage. = 她以勇气武装自己。
He has girded his loins for battle. = 他已准备好战斗。

过去完成时:
They had girded themselves for a long journey. = 他们已为长途旅行做好了准备。
The troops had girded the enemy's position. = 部队已经包围了敌人的阵地。

将来时:
She will gird herself for the upcoming presentation. = 她将为即将到来的演讲做好准备。
They will gird the city walls against attack. = 他们将加固城墙以防攻击。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would gird myself with patience. = 如果我是你，我会以耐心武装自己。
I wish I had girded myself better for the exam. = 我真希望我为考试做了更好的准备。

被动语态:
The city was girded by high walls. = 城市被高墙包围着。
The package was girded with rope for secure transport. = 包裹用绳子捆绑以便安全运输。

疑问句:
Did he gird his sword before the battle? = 他在战斗前系好剑了吗？
Have they girded themselves for the difficult negotiations? = 他们为艰难的谈判做好准备了吗？

否定句:
He did not gird himself for the unexpected difficulties. = 他没有为意想不到的困难做好准备。
She did not gird her belongings properly for the trip. = 她没有为旅行妥善打包她的行李。


PHRASE->
gird oneself for:  为…做好准备
gird up one's loins:  准备行动
gird against:  防范；抵抗
gird with: 用…束紧
gird at:  攻击；抨击
