exiled:/ˈɛɡzaɪld/| v. adj. n.|流放的；被放逐的；流亡者 |  to banish someone from their native country, typically for political reasons;  a person who has been forced to leave their country or home. |例句：He was exiled to Siberia for his political beliefs. 他因为政治信仰被流放到西伯利亚。  The exiled king plotted his return. 流亡的国王密谋回归。 |近义词：banished, deported, expatriated |反义词：repatriated, welcomed

They exile political dissidents. = 他们流放政治异议人士。
The government exiles anyone who opposes them. = 政府流放任何反对他们的人。


一般过去时:
