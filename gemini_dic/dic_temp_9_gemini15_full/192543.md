scuds:/skʌdz/| v. n.|疾驰，飞奔；碎云；少量雨雪|To move swiftly, especially in a hurried or uncontrolled manner; a small, detached cloud or mass of cloud; light rain or snow.|例句：The clouds scudded across the sky.  云快速地飘过天空。  The rain scudded down the street. 雨水飞快地冲刷着街道。|近义词：dash, hurry, flit, drift|反义词：crawl, meander, trickle

USAGE->

一般现在时:
The clouds scud across the sky. = 云快速地飘过天空。
He scuds down the street. = 他飞快地跑过街道。


一般过去时:
The ship scudded before the storm. = 船在暴风雨中疾驰。
The rain scudded against the window. = 雨水拍打着窗户。


现在完成时:
The clouds have scudded away. = 云已经飘走了。
The wind has scudded the leaves across the yard. = 风把树叶吹过院子。


过去完成时:
The rain had scudded past before we reached shelter. = 我们到达避难所之前，雨已经过去了。
The clouds had scudded across the moon before we saw it. = 我们看到月亮之前，云已经飘过了月亮。


将来时:
The storm will scud across the ocean. = 暴风雨将横扫海洋。
The clouds will scud across the sky tomorrow. = 明天云彩将飞速飘过天空。


虚拟语气:
If the wind blew harder, the clouds would scud faster. = 如果风更大，云就会飘得更快。
If the boat were lighter, it would scud more easily. = 如果船更轻，它就能更容易地疾驰。


被动语态:
The small boat was scudded before the strong wind. = 小船被强风吹得飞驰。
The clouds were scudded by the wind. = 云被风吹得飘过。


疑问句:
Are the clouds scudding across the sky? = 云彩正在快速飘过天空吗？
Does the rain scud down the street? = 雨水正在飞快地冲刷街道吗？


否定句:
The clouds are not scudding today. = 今天云彩没有快速飘过。
The boat did not scud easily in the calm water. = 船在平静的水面上并没有轻松地疾驰。
