fop:/fɑːp/| n.|花花公子，爱打扮的男子|A man who is excessively concerned with his clothes and appearance; a dandy.  He is often perceived as vain, affected, and foolish. |例句：He was a fop, always meticulously dressed and preening himself in the mirror. 他是个花花公子，总是穿着考究，在镜子前精心打扮自己。|例句：The fop strutted down the street, convinced of his own irresistible charm.  那个花花公子在街上昂首阔步，确信自己拥有无法抗拒的魅力。|近义词：dandy, coxcomb, beau|反义词：sloven, simpleton
