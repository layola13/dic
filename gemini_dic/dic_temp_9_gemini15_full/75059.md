frightens:/ˈfraɪtənz/| v.|使害怕；吓唬|To cause someone to feel fear or alarm.|The loud noise frightened the children.  大声的噪音吓坏了孩子们。|The sudden appearance of the dog frightened her.  狗的突然出现吓了她一跳。|terrifies, scares, alarms|calms, soothes, reassures|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The loud music frightens the baby. = 响亮的音乐吓到了婴儿。
That scary movie frightens me. = 那部恐怖电影吓到我了。


一般过去时:
The storm frightened the villagers. = 暴风雨吓坏了村民。
The sudden noise frightened the cat. = 突然的响声吓到了猫。


现在完成时:
Nothing has frightened me so much before. = 以前没有什么事情这么吓到过我。
The experience has frightened him off trying again. = 那次经历吓到他了，让他不敢再尝试了。


过去完成时:
I had never been so frightened before. = 我以前从未如此害怕过。
She had been frightened by a similar incident in the past. = 她以前曾被类似事件吓到过。


将来时:
The thunder will frighten the dog. = 雷声会吓到狗。
I think that will frighten him. = 我想那会吓到他。


虚拟语气:
If it frightened me, I wouldn't go. = 如果那吓到我了，我就不去了。
I wish the loud noises wouldn't frighten my pet. = 我希望大声的噪音不会吓到我的宠物。


被动语态:
The children were frightened by the fireworks. = 孩子们被烟火吓到了。
She was frightened by the unexpected visitor. = 她被意想不到的访客吓到了。


疑问句:
Does the dark frighten you? = 黑暗吓到你了吗？
What frightens you the most? = 什么最让你害怕？


否定句:
The dog didn't frighten me. = 那条狗没有吓到我。
The darkness doesn't frighten me anymore. = 黑暗不再吓到我了。


PHRASE->
be frightened of = 害怕…
be frightened to death = 吓死了
frighten away = 吓跑
frighten off = 吓跑
frighten somebody into doing something = 吓唬某人做某事
