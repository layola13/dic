microphonics:/ˌmaɪkrəˈfɒnɪks/| n. |麦克风效应，微音效应 |Sounds produced by a component or device, usually electronic, due to vibrations.  This is often unwanted noise in audio equipment. |例句：The amplifier exhibited significant microphonics when touched.  放大器在触摸时表现出明显的微音效应。

例句：The faulty capacitor caused microphonics in the audio circuit.  有故障的电容导致音频电路中出现微音效应。


近义词：electrical noise, interference, hum, feedback |反义词：silence, clarity
