horrifiedly:/ˈhɒrɪfaɪdli/ /ˈhɔːrɪfaɪdli/ | adv. | 恐惧地；惊恐地 |  The adverb "horrifiedly" describes an action performed in a state of horror or extreme fright. It emphasizes the intense fear and revulsion felt by the subject.  | She stared horrifiedly at the accident scene. 她恐惧地盯着事故现场。 He reacted horrifiedly to the news of the tragedy. 他对悲剧的消息作出了惊恐的反应。 |  terrifiedly, fearfully, frightfully | calmly, serenely, placidly | 副词
