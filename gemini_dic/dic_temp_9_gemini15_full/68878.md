fasten:/ˈfæsn/| vt.|系紧；固定；扎牢|To attach or secure something firmly.|例句：Please fasten your seatbelts. 请系好安全带。|例句：He fastened the rope to the tree. 他把绳子系在树上。|近义词：attach, secure, tie, bind|反义词：unfasten, loosen


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fastens his shoes every morning. = 他每天早上系鞋带。
She fastens her hair with a clip. = 她用发夹固定头发。

一般过去时:
I fastened the door before leaving. = 我离开前锁上了门。
They fastened the package with strong tape. = 他们用强力胶带捆扎包裹。

现在完成时:
She has fastened the curtains. = 她已经系好了窗帘。
He has fastened his seat belt. = 他已经系好了安全带。

过去完成时:
I had fastened my backpack before the hike. = 我远足前已经系好了背包。
They had fastened the boat to the dock. = 他们已经把船系在了码头上。

将来时:
I will fasten the button. = 我将会系上扣子。
She will fasten the painting to the wall. = 她将会把画固定在墙上。

虚拟语气:
If I had fastened my seatbelt, I wouldn't have been injured. = 如果我系好了安全带，我就不会受伤了。
If she fastened her hair properly, it would stay in place. = 如果她把头发好好固定住，它就不会散了。

被动语态:
The package was fastened securely. = 包裹被牢固地系好了。
The button will be fastened soon. = 扣子很快就会被系上。

疑问句:
Is the seatbelt fastened? = 安全带系好了吗？
Have you fastened your shoes? = 你系好鞋带了吗？

否定句:
He didn't fasten his shoes. = 他没有系鞋带。
She hasn't fastened the door. = 她没有锁门。
