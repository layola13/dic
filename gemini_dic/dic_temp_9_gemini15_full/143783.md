outmove:/ˌaʊtˈmuːv/ | vt. |超越，胜过，比…行动更快|To surpass or exceed in movement or action; to outmaneuver.  |例句：The chess player skillfully outmoved his opponent, forcing a checkmate.  国际象棋选手巧妙地超越了他的对手，迫使其将死。

例句：The nimble dancer outmoved the clumsy one with ease. 轻盈的舞者轻松地超越了笨拙的舞者。


近义词：outmaneuver, surpass, outstrip, excel, outdo | 反义词：be outmaneuvered, be surpassed, be outstripped

词性：动词
