panslavist:/ˈpænˌslɑːvɪst/| n. |泛斯拉夫主义者；泛斯拉夫主义拥护者 | A panslavist is a person who supports Panslavism, a political ideology advocating for the unity and cooperation of all Slavic peoples.  | Example:  Many intellectuals in the 19th century were ardent panslavists, dreaming of a unified Slavic nation.  许多19世纪的知识分子是狂热的泛斯拉夫主义者，梦想建立一个统一的斯拉夫国家。| 近义词：Slavophile (if focusing on cultural unity)| 反义词：anti-Slavic nationalist
