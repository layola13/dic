pelecaniformes:/ˌpɛləkəˈnɪfɔːrmiːz/| n. |鹈形目|Pelecaniformes is an order of large waterbirds that includes pelicans, boobies, gannets, frigatebirds, cormorants, and anhingas.  They are characterized by their webbed feet,  often having a pouch (like pelicans), and being skilled divers or surface feeders.  The order is currently undergoing taxonomic revision, with some species being reclassified.|Example:  The pelicans are a prominent family within the order Pelecaniformes.  鹈鹕是鹈形目中一个显著的科。|近义词：暂无完全同义词，可根据具体语境选择合适的鸟类目名称|反义词：暂无
