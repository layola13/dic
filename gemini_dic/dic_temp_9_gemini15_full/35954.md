code-naming:/ˈkoʊdˌneɪmɪŋ/| n.| 代码命名，代号|The act of giving a secret or unofficial name to something, typically a project, operation, or person, for the purpose of secrecy or convenience.  |例句：The code-naming of the project helped maintain secrecy during development.  该项目的代码命名有助于在开发过程中保持秘密。|近义词：covert designation, secret appellation|反义词：open naming, public identification|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They code-name their projects regularly. = 他们定期为项目命名。
The company code-names its new products before release. = 公司在发布新产品之前为其命名。


一般过去时:
The team code-named the operation "Nightingale". = 团队将行动命名为“夜莺”。
They code-named the spy "Cardinal". = 他们给间谍起了个代号“红衣主教”。


现在完成时:
They have code-named all the key participants. = 他们已经为所有关键参与者起了代号。
The agency has code-named several ongoing investigations. = 该机构已经为几项正在进行的调查起了代号。


过去完成时:
They had code-named the mission before the briefing. = 他们在简报会之前就为任务起了代号。
The government had code-named the program long before its launch. = 政府早在该项目启动很久之前就为其起了代号。


将来时:
They will code-name the next phase of the project soon. = 他们很快就会为项目的下一阶段命名。
The team will code-name the new software update "Phoenix". = 该团队将把新的软件更新命名为“凤凰”。


虚拟语气:
If they code-named the operation differently, it might have been more successful. = 如果他们对行动的命名不同，它可能会更成功。
I wish they had code-named the project something less obvious. = 我希望他们为这个项目起个不那么明显的代号。


被动语态:
The project was code-named "Project X". = 该项目被命名为“X 项目”。

疑问句:

否定句:

The document was not code-named. = 文件没有被命名。
