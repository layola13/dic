gorgelet:/ˈɡɔːrdʒlɛt/| n.|小峡谷；瓶颈；（酒瓶的）瓶颈|A gorgelet is a small, narrow gorge or ravine.  It can also refer to the neck of a bottle, particularly a wine bottle.  The term often implies a constricted passage or opening.|例句：The hikers squeezed through the narrow gorgelet.  徒步旅行者挤过狭窄的小峡谷。

The wine flowed smoothly from the bottle's gorgelet. 酒从酒瓶的瓶颈处流畅地流出。|近义词：ravine, gully, bottleneck, neck|反义词：wide opening, expanse
