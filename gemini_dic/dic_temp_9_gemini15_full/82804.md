guiro:/ˈɡɪroʊ/| n.|刮擦乐器|A guiro is a percussion instrument of Afro-Caribbean origin, typically made from a dried gourd or similar material. It has ridges or notches carved into its surface, which are scraped with a stick or other implement to produce a distinctive rattling sound.  |例句：The musician played a lively rhythm on the guiro.  演奏家用刮擦乐器演奏了轻快的节奏。|近义词：scraper, percussion instrument|反义词：无
