defendant:/ˈdɛfændənt/| n. |被告，被控方 |A defendant is a person or institution who is sued or accused in a court of law.  |例句：The defendant pleaded not guilty to the charges.  被告对指控不认罪。|例句：The defendant's lawyer presented a strong defense. 被告的律师提出了强有力的辩护。|近义词：accused, respondent |反义词：plaintiff, claimant
