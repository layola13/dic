resanctioned:/ˌriːˈsæŋkʃənd/ | adj. | 重新实施制裁的；再次受到制裁的 |  Having sanctions reimposed or reinstated.  The previously lifted sanctions against the country were resanctioned due to its continued human rights violations.  |  例句：The resanctioned country faced further economic hardship. 重新受到制裁的国家面临进一步的经济困难。

The company's resanctioned products were pulled from shelves.  再次受到制裁的公司产品被下架了。| 近义词：reimposed, reinstated | 反义词：lifted, removed, repealed |  词性：形容词
