mooc:/muːk/| n.|大型开放式在线课程|Massive Open Online Course,  a free or low-cost online course available to a large number of people.  They are often offered by universities and other institutions.|例句：I'm taking a MOOC on artificial intelligence this semester.  这个学期我正在上一个关于人工智能的慕课。|近义词：online course, e-learning course|反义词：in-person class, traditional course
