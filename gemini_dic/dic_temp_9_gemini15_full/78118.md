geomagnetism:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈmæɡnɪtɪzəm/| n. |地磁学；地磁现象|The study of the Earth's magnetic field, its origin, and its effects.  Geomagnetism encompasses the generation of the magnetic field within the Earth's core, its variations over time, and its interaction with the solar wind. |The study of geomagnetism helps us understand plate tectonics and the Earth's internal structure.  地磁学研究有助于我们了解板块构造和地球内部结构。|近义词：terrestrial magnetism|反义词：无
