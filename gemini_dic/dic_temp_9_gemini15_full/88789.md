hoe: /hoʊ/ | n., v. | 锄头；除草；辛勤工作 |

英文解析: A hoe is a gardening tool with a blade attached to a long handle, used for weeding, cultivating soil, and other gardening tasks.  As a verb, it means to cultivate or weed with a hoe, or to work diligently.

例句:
He used a hoe to loosen the soil around the plants. = 他用锄头松动植物周围的土壤。

She spent the afternoon hoeing the vegetable patch. = 她下午一直在菜地里除草。


近义词: (n.) cultivator, digging tool; (v.) cultivate, weed, till


反义词: (v.) neglect, idle


词性: Noun, Verb

词源:
The word "hoe" comes from the Middle English word "howe," which in turn derives from the Old English word "hō," meaning "a tool for digging."  The word's ultimate origins are likely Proto-Germanic.
