politically:/pəˈlɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 政治上地；从政治角度来看 |  In a political manner; relating to or concerned with politics.  |例句：The issue was handled politically rather than practically.  这个问题是政治性处理而不是实际处理。

例句：The candidate's stance on the economy was politically motivated.  这位候选人在经济问题上的立场是出于政治动机。


近义词：politically speaking, in a political context

反义词：impolitically, apolitically


词源：源自political (adj.) + -ly (adv. suffix)。political  来自古法语 politique，最终来自希腊语 politikos，“公民的，政治的”，来自 polis，“城市，城邦”。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
The decision was influenced politically by outside groups. = 这个决定受到了外部团体的政治影响。
The situation is being handled politically, not practically. = 情况正在以政治的方式处理，而不是以实际的方式处理。


疑问句:
Is this decision politically sound? = 这个决定在政治上是否明智？

否定句:
The situation was not handled politically; it was handled practically. = 情况并非政治性处理，而是实际处理。
He didn't speak politically at the conference; he focused on practical matters. = 他在会议上没有发表政治性言论；他专注于实际问题。
