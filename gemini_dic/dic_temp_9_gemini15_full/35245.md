clumsy:/ˈklʌmzi/| adj.|笨拙的，笨手笨脚的|Awkward in movement or handling things.  Characterized by lack of skill or grace. |例句：He's a bit clumsy and often bumps into things. 他有点笨拙，经常撞到东西。

例句：The clumsy waiter dropped the tray of drinks.  那个笨拙的服务员打翻了托盘里的饮料。


近义词：awkward, ungainly, maladroit, gauche


反义词：graceful, dexterous, skillful, adroit


词源:  The word "clumsy" ultimately derives from the Middle English word "clumsen," meaning "to be awkward or clumsy."  This, in turn, likely originates from a Germanic root related to words suggesting heaviness or dullness.
