jujuist:/ˈdʒuːdʒuːɪst/| n. |巫术师，伏都教信徒|A person who practices juju, a form of West African magic and religion.  Jujuists often use charms, fetishes, and rituals to achieve their goals, which can range from healing to causing harm.|例句：The villagers believed the jujuist had cursed their crops. 村民们相信巫师诅咒了他们的庄稼。|近义词：sorcerer, witch doctor, voodoo priest|反义词：skeptic, rationalist
