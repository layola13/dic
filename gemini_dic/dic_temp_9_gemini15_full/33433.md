churidar:/tʃʊrɪˈdɑːr/| n. |楚里达尔裤(一种紧身裤)|A churidar is a type of tight-fitting trousers, traditionally worn in South Asia, that are close-fitting from the waist to the ankles, often gathered at the ankles.  They are typically made of cotton, silk, or other fabrics, and are often worn with a kurta (a long shirt) or kameez (a tunic).|She wore a beautiful churidar and kurta to the wedding. 她穿着美丽的楚里达尔裤和库尔塔参加婚礼。|pyjama, salwar|trousers (loose-fitting)|名词
