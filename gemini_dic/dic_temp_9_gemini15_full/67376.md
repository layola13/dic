extents:/ɪkˈstɛnts/ | n. | 范围，程度，大小 | The extent of the damage is still unknown.  损坏的程度仍然未知。|近义词：scope, range, degree, magnitude |反义词：limit, restriction


英文解析：Extent refers to the area or length covered by something; the amount or degree to which something happens or is present.  It can also refer to the full length or range of something.


例句：

The extent of his injuries was shocking. 他伤势的严重程度令人震惊。

To what extent are you involved? 你参与的程度有多深？



词源：

Middle English: from Old French *estente, from extendre ‘to stretch out’ (see extend).
