legibility:/ˌlɛdʒəˈbɪləti/| n. |清晰度，易读性|The quality of being easy to read.  This refers to the clarity and understandability of written or printed text. It encompasses factors like font choice, spacing, and overall presentation.|The legibility of the document was greatly improved by using a larger font size.|clarity, readability|illegibility, unintelligibility
