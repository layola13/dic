marginate:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪneɪt/| vt.| 边缘化；使处于社会边缘 |To put or keep (someone or something) in a powerless or unimportant position within a society or group.  |例句：The government's policies have marginated many ethnic minorities. 政府的政策使许多少数民族边缘化了。|例句：His ideas were marginated by the scientific community. 他的想法被科学界边缘化了。|近义词：exclude, ostracize, sideline, marginalize |反义词：integrate, include, centralize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company marginates its less productive employees. = 公司边缘化效率较低的员工。
They marginate dissenting voices in the debate. = 他们在辩论中边缘化异议的声音。


一般过去时:
The previous administration marginated environmental concerns. = 上届政府忽视了环境问题。
The artist felt marginated by the art world. = 这位艺术家感觉被艺术界边缘化了。


现在完成时:
Social media has marginated traditional news sources. = 社交媒体已经边缘化了传统新闻来源。
The economic crisis has marginated many families. = 经济危机已经使许多家庭陷入困境。


过去完成时:
They had marginated her contributions before the project even started. = 他们在项目开始之前就已经边缘化了她的贡献。
The community had marginated him due to his past mistakes. = 由于他过去的错误，社区已经将他边缘化了。


将来时:
Automation will likely marginate many manual labor jobs. = 自动化很可能会使许多体力劳动工作边缘化。
Increased globalization may marginate smaller businesses. = 越来越多的全球化可能会边缘化小型企业。


虚拟语气:
If they continued this approach, they would marginate a significant portion of the population. = 如果他们继续这种做法，他们将会边缘化很大一部分人口。
I wish they hadn't marginated the younger generation's ideas. = 我真希望他们没有边缘化年轻一代的想法。


被动语态:
Many minority groups have been marginated throughout history. = 许多少数民族群体在历史上一直被边缘化。
His concerns were marginated by the committee. = 他的担忧被委员会忽视了。


疑问句:
Are marginalized communities receiving sufficient support? = 边缘化社区是否获得了足够的支援？
Why were these voices marginated in the discussion? = 为什么这些声音在讨论中被边缘化了？


否定句:
The company doesn't marginate its employees based on age. = 公司不会根据年龄来边缘化员工。
We won't allow them to marginate the vulnerable population. = 我们不会允许他们边缘化弱势群体。
