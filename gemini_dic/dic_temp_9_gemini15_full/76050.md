furls:/fɜːrlz/| n.|卷，缠绕；（尤指帆或旗的）收起|The act of furling; the state of being furled.  Specifically, the act of wrapping or rolling something up tightly, especially a sail or flag. |例句：The sailors furled the sails as the storm approached. 水手们在暴风雨来临之际收起了帆。

例句：The flag was furled neatly and stored away. 旗子被整齐地卷起来收好。


近义词：roll up, wrap up, coil


反义词：unfurl, unfurl


词性：名词
