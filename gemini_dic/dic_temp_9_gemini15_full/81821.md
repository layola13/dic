grew-up: /ɡruːˈʌp/ | adj. | 成年的；长大的 |  Describes someone who has reached adulthood and is no longer a child.  It can also refer to something that has developed or matured. | 例句：He's a grown-up now and should take responsibility for his actions. 他现在已经成年了，应该对自己的行为负责。

He's a grown-up now and can make his own decisions. 他现在已经成年了，可以自己做决定了。


近义词：adult, mature, developed | 反义词：childish, immature, undeveloped | 词性：adjective


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The plant grows up quickly in the sun. = 植物在阳光下长得很快。
He grows up so fast. = 他长得真快。


一般过去时:
The boy grew up in a small village. = 这个男孩在一个小村庄长大。
She grew up quickly after puberty. = 她青春期后长得很快。


现在完成时:
She has grown up a lot since I last saw her. = 自从我上次见到她以来，她长大了很多。
The company has grown up into a global enterprise. = 这家公司已经发展成为一家全球性企业。


过去完成时:
He had grown up before he knew his parents' secrets. = 在他了解父母的秘密之前，他已经长大了。
By the time she got married, she had grown up enough to handle family matters. = 到她结婚的时候，她已经足够成熟来处理家庭事务了。


将来时:
He will grow up to be a fine young man. = 他将会成长为一个优秀的年轻人。
The city will grow up to a great metropolis in future. = 未来这座城市将发展成为一个大都市。


虚拟语气:
If I had grown up in a different environment, I might be a different person. = 如果我在不同的环境中长大，我可能会成为一个不同的人。
I wish I had grown up with more support. = 我希望我在成长的过程中有更多支持。


被动语态:
The children are being grown up in a loving family. = 孩子们在一个充满爱的家庭中长大。(被动语态在此处略显不自然，很少使用)
The project is being grown up steadily. = 项目正在稳步发展。(指项目发展成熟)


疑问句:
Where did you grow up? = 你在哪里长大的？
Has he grown up much since the last time you saw him? = 自从你上次见到他以来，他长大了很多吗？


否定句:
He didn't grow up in a wealthy family. = 他不是在一个富裕的家庭长大的。
The plant didn't grow up well because of the lack of sunlight. = 因为缺乏阳光，植物长得不好。


PHRASE->
grow up and be responsible  长大并承担责任
grow up quickly  迅速成长
grow up in the country 在农村长大
grow up to be... 长大成为...
grow up to expectations 达到了预期
