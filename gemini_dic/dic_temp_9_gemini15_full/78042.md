genuflects:/ˈdʒɛnjuˌflɛkt/ | v. | 屈膝跪拜；(尤指表示敬意而) 弯下膝盖 | To bend one knee, especially in worship.  |例句：He genuflected before the altar. 他在祭坛前屈膝下跪。

例句：The priest genuflected before the crucifix.  神父在十字架前屈膝行礼。


近义词：kneel, bow, prostrate


反义词：stand, remain upright


USAGE->

He genuflects before the statue every day. 他每天都在雕像前屈膝礼拜。
She genuflects respectfully during the ceremony. 她在仪式上恭敬地屈膝行礼。


一般过去时:
He genuflected before the king. 他在国王面前下跪。

He has genuflected many times in his life. 他一生中屈膝礼拜过很多次。

过去完成时:
He had genuflected before he began his speech. 他在开始演讲之前已屈膝行礼。
