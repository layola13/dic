genders:/ˈdʒɛndərz/| n. |性别，性|The word "genders" refers to the categories of male and female, and sometimes also includes other categories such as non-binary or intersex.  It often relates to the social and cultural roles associated with biological sex, rather than solely biological sex itself. |例句：The survey asked respondents to specify their genders.  这项调查要求受访者说明他们的性别。|例句：Discussions about genders are becoming increasingly common in modern society.  关于性别的讨论在现代社会越来越普遍。|近义词：sexes, categories of sex |反义词：无
