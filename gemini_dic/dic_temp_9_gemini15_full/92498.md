hypothermias:/ˌhaɪpəˈθɜːrmiəz/| n.|低体温症(复数)|The state of having an abnormally low body temperature.|例句：Prolonged exposure to cold can lead to hypothermias. 长时间暴露在寒冷中会导致低体温症。  
例句：Several victims of the avalanche suffered from severe hypothermias. 雪崩的几个受害者患有严重的低体温症。|近义词：coldness, chill|反义词：hyperthermia
