rerecords:/ˌriːˈrɛkɔːrdz/| v.|重新录制，再次记录 |To record something again.|例句：The band rerecorded the song with a different producer. 乐队请了不同的制作人重新录制了这首歌。|近义词：re-record, re-register|反义词：erase, delete


USAGE->

We rerecord the audio if there are any mistakes. = 如果有任何错误，我们会重新录制音频。
