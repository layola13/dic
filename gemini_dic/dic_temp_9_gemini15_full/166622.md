presentation:/ˌprezenˈteɪʃən/| n.|展示；演示；陈述；介绍|The act of presenting something to someone or a group of people;  a formal talk or report given to an audience.|例句：He gave a presentation on the new product. 他就新产品做了一个演示。|例句：The presentation was well-received by the audience.  听众对这次演示反响很好。|近义词：demonstration, exposition, display, address|反义词：concealment, hiding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company gives a presentation every quarter. = 公司每个季度都会做一个演示。
He presents his ideas clearly. = 他清晰地表达了他的想法。


一般过去时:
She presented her findings at the conference. = 她在会议上展示了她的研究结果。
They gave a compelling presentation yesterday. = 他们昨天做了一个引人入胜的演示。


现在完成时:
He has presented at numerous international conferences. = 他在许多国际会议上做过演示。
We have completed the presentation for tomorrow's meeting. = 我们已经完成了明天会议的演示材料。


过去完成时:
They had prepared the presentation before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已经准备好了演示文稿。
She had already presented her ideas before the discussion began. = 在讨论开始之前，她已经陈述了她的想法。


将来时:
I will present my work next week. = 我下周将展示我的作品。
They will be presenting their findings at the seminar. = 他们将在研讨会上展示他们的研究结果。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would give a better presentation. = 如果我有更多时间，我会做一个更好的演示。
I wish I had presented my ideas more clearly. = 我希望我更清晰地表达了我的想法。


被动语态:
The presentation was delivered flawlessly. = 演示完美地完成了。
The new product will be presented at the trade show. = 新产品将在贸易展会上展出。


疑问句:
When will the presentation be held? = 演示将在什么时候举行？
Who will be presenting the findings? = 谁将展示研究结果？


否定句:
He didn't give a presentation at the meeting. = 他在会议上没有做演示。
They haven't finished preparing their presentation yet. = 他们还没有完成演示文稿的准备。
