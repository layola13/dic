frizz:/frɪz/ | v., n. | 卷曲；毛躁；(头发)蓬乱 | To become or cause to become curly or untidy.  |例句：Her hair frizzed in the humidity.  她的头发在潮湿的环境下变得蓬乱。The wool will frizz if you wash it in hot water. 如果你用热水洗羊毛，它会起球。|近义词：curl, kink, crinkle |反义词：straighten, smooth


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The wool frizzles easily. = 羊毛很容易起球。
My hair frizzles in the rain. = 我的头发在雨中变得蓬乱。

一般过去时:
The fabric frizzed after being washed. = 洗完后织物起球了。
Her hair frizzed up in the humid weather. = 她头发在潮湿的天气里蓬乱了。

现在完成时:
My hair has frizzed since I went swimming. = 我游泳后头发就蓬乱了。
The sweater has frizzed after several washes. = 这件毛衣洗了几次后就起球了。

过去完成时:
The wool had already frizzed before I even started to knit. = 我甚至还没开始织毛衣，羊毛就已经起球了。
Her hair had frizzed so much that it was impossible to style. = 她的头发蓬乱得无法打理。

将来时:
I think my hair will frizz if it rains. = 我想如果下雨的话我的头发会蓬乱。
That material will probably frizz in the dryer. = 那种材料在烘干机里可能会起球。

虚拟语气:
If I didn't use conditioner, my hair would frizz. = 如果我不使用护发素，我的头发就会蓬乱。
If the wool were washed in hot water, it would frizz. = 如果羊毛用热水洗，它就会起球。

被动语态:

The hair was frizzed by the humidity. = 头发被潮湿的天气弄得蓬乱了。

疑问句:

否定句:
