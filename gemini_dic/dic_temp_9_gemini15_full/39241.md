congratulations:/kənˌɡrætʃuˈleɪʃənz/| n. |祝贺，恭喜| Expressions of good wishes on the occasion of success or good fortune.  It is typically used to express joy and celebrate someone's achievement. |例句：Congratulations on your graduation! 恭喜你毕业！

例句：Congratulations on your new job! 恭喜你找到新工作！


近义词：felicitations, best wishes, accolades |反义词：condolences (in the context of expressing sorrow)


词性：名词
