prehesitated:/ˌpriːˈhɛzɪteɪtɪd/ | v. | 犹豫地做了，预先犹豫 | To hesitate before doing something; to show hesitation before taking action.  This word is a less common, more descriptive form emphasizing the prior hesitation before an action.  It combines "pre-" (before) and "hesitated" (past tense of hesitate).|


例句：He prehesitated, then finally stepped forward to speak. 他犹豫了一下，然后终于上前发言。

例句：She prehesitated, carefully weighing the pros and cons before signing the contract.  她在签合同之前犹豫了一下，仔细权衡了利弊。


近义词：hesitated, wavered, faltered, demurred

反义词：acted decisively, plunged in, jumped in

词性:动词
