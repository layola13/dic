/ˈmɔːstrɑː/| n.|展览，展示，样品|A mostra is a display or exhibition of something, often art or products.|例句：The museum is hosting a mostra of modern art.  博物馆正在举办一场现代艺术展览。|近义词：exhibition, display, show|反义词：concealment, hiding|名词

The museum hosts a mostra of Italian paintings. = 博物馆举办一场意大利绘画展览。
This shop displays a mostra of its latest products. = 这家商店展示其最新产品。
