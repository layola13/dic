objet:/ˈɒbʒeɪ/| /ɔbˈʒɛ/ | n. | 目标；物体；作品 | An objet is an object, especially one that is decorative or of artistic interest.  It often refers to a small, crafted item. | Example: The museum showcased a collection of exquisite objets d'art.  博物馆展出了许多精美的艺术品。  |近义词：object, item, article | 反义词：subject


USAGE->
This word is a noun and doesn't conjugate through tenses like verbs.  Therefore, tense examples are not applicable.  However, we can show how it's used in different sentence structures.

单数句:
The objet d'art was a stunning example of craftsmanship.  这件艺术品是工艺的绝佳典范。

复数句:
Several objets d'art adorned the mantelpiece. 几件艺术品装饰着壁炉架。

所有格:
The museum's objets d'art are world-renowned. 博物馆的艺术品享誉世界。

介词短语:
The book was about the creation of the objet. 这本书是关于这件物品的创作。

疑问句:
Is that objet a genuine antique? 那件物品是真正的古董吗？

否定句:
That objet is not for sale. 那件物品不出售。
