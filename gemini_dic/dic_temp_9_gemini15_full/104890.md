keepers:/ˈkiːpərz/| n.|看守人，保管人，饲养员|People or things that keep or maintain something.  They can be individuals responsible for guarding, protecting, or caring for something or someone, or they can be the parts of something that hold it together.|例句：The zookeepers carefully cleaned the lion's enclosure. 动物园管理员仔细地打扫了狮子的围栏。

例句：The keepers of the ancient castle ensured its preservation. 古堡的守护者确保了它的保存。


近义词：guardians, custodians, caretakers, wardens|反义词：destroyers, vandals, neglecters|词性:名词
