nonlubricious:/ˌnɒnluːˈbrɪʃəs/| adj. |不润滑的，不滑的 |Not slippery or lubricated; lacking lubrication.  The nonlubricious surface caused the machine parts to grind against each other.|近义词：dry, unlubricated, friction-producing|反义词：lubricious, slippery, oily


USAGE->
This word is an adjective, and thus doesn't have verb conjugations.  The usage is straightforward: it describes a surface or substance that lacks lubrication.

Example sentences:

The nonlubricious bearing seized due to lack of oil. = 由于缺乏润滑油，不润滑的轴承卡住了。

The nonlubricious nature of the material resulted in increased wear and tear. = 材料的不润滑特性导致磨损增加。


PHRASE->
There are no common phrases specifically using "nonlubricious".  The word is used to describe a characteristic of a material or surface.
