regroupment:/ˌriːˈɡruːpmənt/ | n. |重新组合；重组；集结 | The act of regrouping or the state of being regrouped; the formation of a new group.  It often refers to the reorganization of troops or forces after a defeat or setback, or the reorganization of a group or organization for greater efficiency or effectiveness. |例句：After the initial attack, the soldiers quickly regrouped and launched a counteroffensive.  在最初的攻击之后，士兵们迅速重组并发动了反攻。

例句：The company underwent a major regroupment to improve its market position. 公司进行了大规模的重组以改善其市场地位。

近义词：reorganization, reorganization, reconstitution, rally

反义词：dispersion, scattering, disintegration
