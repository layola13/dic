scenograph:/ˈsiːnəɡræf/ | n. |布景设计；舞台美术设计|The design and creation of the visual aspects of a theatrical production, film, or other performance.  It encompasses the setting, props, lighting, and overall visual style.|The scenographer created a breathtaking set for the opera.|近义词：set designer, stage designer|反义词：无
