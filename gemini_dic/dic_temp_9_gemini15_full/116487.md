materializer:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪzər/ | n. | 物质化者；实现者 | One who or that which materializes something;  someone or something that makes something real or concrete.  A device or process that creates a physical manifestation of something previously abstract or conceptual. | 例句：The artist acted as a materializer of her dreams, transforming abstract concepts into tangible artwork.  艺术家充当了她梦想的实现者，将抽象的概念转化为有形的艺术品。


The new software is a materializer of complex data, presenting it in an easily understandable format.  新的软件是一个复杂数据的实现者，以易于理解的格式呈现它。| 近义词：creator, implementer, manifester | 反义词：dematerializer, abstractor
