release:/rɪˈliːs/| vt. n.|释放，发布，发行|to set free; to make something available to the public; to let go of something.|例句：The prisoner was released from jail.  犯人被释放出狱了。
例句：The company released a new product last week. 公司上周发布了一款新产品。|近义词：liberate, discharge, launch, publish|反义词：capture, restrain, withhold, retain|动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He releases a new album every year. 他每年发行一张新专辑。
They release the balloons into the sky. 他们把气球放飞到空中。


一般过去时:
The police released the suspect. 警方释放了嫌疑人。
She released her grip on the rope. 她松开了绳子。


现在完成时:
They have released three albums this year. 他们今年已经发行了三张专辑。
The tension has been released. 紧张气氛已经缓和了。


过去完成时:
The prisoner had been released before the news broke.  消息传出之前，囚犯已被释放。
The pressure had been released slowly. 压力慢慢地释放了。


将来时:
They will release the movie next month. 他们下个月将上映这部电影。
The company will release its financial results soon. 公司很快就会公布财务业绩。


虚拟语气:
If they released the information earlier, the damage would have been less. 如果他们早点公布信息，损失就会小一些。
I wish they would release the new software soon. 我希望他们能尽快发布新软件。


被动语态:
The hostages were released unharmed. 人质被平安释放了。
The new software will be released next week. 新软件将于下周发布。
