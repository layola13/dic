infiltrators: /ˈɪnfɪltreɪtərz/ | n. |渗透者，潜入者 |People or things that secretly enter a place or organization to gather information or cause damage.  They often operate covertly and deceptively.|例句：The police arrested several infiltrators who were trying to disrupt the peace rally.  警方逮捕了几名试图破坏和平集会的渗透者。


例句：Government agents uncovered a network of infiltrators working for a foreign power.  政府特工发现了一个为外国势力工作的渗透者网络。


近义词：spies, saboteurs, undercover agents, intruders


反义词：open supporters, allies, collaborators (depending on context)


词性: 名词
