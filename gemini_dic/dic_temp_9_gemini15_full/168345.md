pro-Syrian: /ˌproʊˈsɪəriən/ | adj. | 亲叙利亚的，支持叙利亚的 | Supporting or favoring the Syrian government or people.  | Example:  The pro-Syrian demonstrations were met with a strong police response.  亲叙利亚的示威游行遭到了警方强烈的回应。  | 近义词: pro-Assad (如果指的是支持叙利亚政府),  supportive of Syria | 反义词: anti-Syrian


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is pro-Syrian in his political views. = 他在政治观点上亲叙利亚。
She shows pro-Syrian sentiment in her writings. = 她在著作中表达了亲叙利亚的观点。


一般过去时:
He was pro-Syrian even before the conflict began. = 即使在冲突开始之前，他也亲叙利亚。
Many countries were initially pro-Syrian during the early stages of the war. = 在战争初期，许多国家最初都支持叙利亚。


现在完成时:
The government has been consistently pro-Syrian throughout the crisis. = 在整个危机中，政府一直坚定地支持叙利亚。
Public opinion has shifted, becoming less pro-Syrian in recent years. = 近年来，公众舆论发生了转变，对叙利亚的支持减少了。


过去完成时:
They had been pro-Syrian for decades before changing their stance. = 在改变立场之前，他们几十年来一直支持叙利亚。
The media had portrayed a largely pro-Syrian narrative before the uprising. = 在起义之前，媒体描绘的主要是亲叙利亚的叙事。


将来时:
He will remain pro-Syrian regardless of international pressure. = 不管国际压力如何，他都会继续支持叙利亚。
It is unlikely that the country will become more pro-Syrian in the near future. = 该国在不久的将来不太可能变得更加亲叙利亚。


虚拟语气:
If he were pro-Syrian, he would have supported the government's actions. = 如果他亲叙利亚，他会支持政府的行动。
I wish more people were pro-Syrian and understood the complexities of the conflict. = 我希望更多的人支持叙利亚，并理解冲突的复杂性。


被动语态:
His pro-Syrian views were criticized by many. = 他亲叙利亚的观点受到许多人的批评。
The pro-Syrian stance of the country was questioned by international observers. = 该国的亲叙利亚立场受到国际观察员的质疑。


疑问句:
Are you pro-Syrian or anti-Syrian in your opinion on the conflict? = 你对这场冲突的看法是亲叙利亚还是反叙利亚？
Was the media outlet clearly pro-Syrian in its reporting? = 该媒体机构在其报道中是否明确地支持叙利亚？


否定句:
He is not pro-Syrian; he is strongly opposed to the regime. = 他并不亲叙利亚；他强烈反对该政权。
The newspaper article was not pro-Syrian; it presented a balanced view. = 这篇报纸文章并非亲叙利亚；它呈现了一个平衡的观点。
