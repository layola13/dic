flocks:/flɒks/| /flɑːks/| n.|兽群；人群；大量|A flock is a group of birds, sheep, or other animals that live together.  It can also refer to a large number of people or things.|例句：A flock of sheep grazed peacefully in the meadow.一群羊在草地上安静地吃草。

例句：A flock of tourists gathered around the ancient monument.一群游客聚集在古迹周围。


近义词：herd, group, crowd, multitude|反义词：individual, single


USAGE->

A flock of birds flies south for the winter. = 一群鸟飞到南方过冬。
Many flocks of sheep graze on this pasture. = 许多羊群在这个牧场上吃草。
