selvage:/ˈsɛlvɪdʒ/| n.| 布边，纬纱边 |The finished edge of a piece of woven fabric, formed by turning the weft threads back on themselves.  It is usually denser and stronger than the rest of the fabric.| 例句：The selvedge of the fabric is neatly finished. 这块布的布边很整齐。

The selvedge prevents the fabric from unraveling. 布边防止布料散开。|近义词：edge, selvage edge|反义词：raw edge
