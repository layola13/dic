prismatoidal:/ˌprɪzˈmeɪtɔɪdəl/| adj.|棱柱体状的；近似棱柱体的|Having the form of a prismatoid.  A prismatoid is a polyhedron whose vertices lie in two parallel planes.  The word combines "prism" and "oid," indicating a resemblance to a prism. |例句：The geological formation exhibited a prismatoidal shape.  这地质构造呈现棱柱体状。

The newly designed building has a prismatoidal structure. 这座新设计的建筑物具有棱柱体状的结构。|近义词：polyhedral, pyramidal(if the resemblance is closer to a pyramid)|反义词：spherical, cylindrical
