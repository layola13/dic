kyanophyll:/kaɪˈænəfɪl/| n. |蓝藻素，藻蓝蛋白|A blue pigment found in cyanobacteria (blue-green algae). It's a light-harvesting protein that absorbs light energy for photosynthesis.  |Example:  Kyanophyll is responsible for the blue-green color of many cyanobacteria.  蓝藻素是许多蓝藻呈现蓝绿色的原因。|近义词：phycocyanin (藻蓝蛋白，更常用的学术术语)|反义词：无|名词
