perkish:/ˈpɜːrkɪʃ/| adj.|活泼的；神气活现的；傲慢的|Lively and cheerful; smartly dressed and confident, sometimes to the point of being arrogant.|例句：She had a perkish smile and a confident stride. 她带着一丝俏皮的笑容和自信的步伐。

例句：He wore a perkish little hat. 他戴着一顶俏皮的小帽子。


近义词：cheeky, jaunty, self-assured, cocky|反义词：depressed, downcast, subdued, meek

词源:  The word "perkish" likely originated as a variant or diminutive of "perky," meaning lively and cheerful.  While a precise etymological trace is difficult to pinpoint, the connection to "perky" is clear, with "perkish" adding a nuance of stylishness or even a touch of arrogance to the spiritedness.  The "-ish" suffix adds a slightly informal or diminutive quality.
