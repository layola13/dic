rejoicingly:/rɪˈdʒɔɪsɪŋli/| adv.|欣喜地；高兴地|In a joyful or celebratory manner.  Expressing or characterized by great joy.|例句：They danced rejoicingly at their wedding. 他们在婚礼上欣喜若狂地跳舞。

例句：She accepted the award rejoicingly, tears in her eyes. 她欣喜地接受了奖项，眼里含着泪水。

近义词：joyfully, gladly, happily, exuberantly

反义词：sadly, sorrowfully, dejectedly, miserably

词性:副词
