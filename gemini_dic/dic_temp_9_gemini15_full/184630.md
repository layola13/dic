restress:/ˌriːˈstrɛs/ | vt. |再强调；重新强调；再次使紧张|To emphasize something again; to put stress on something again; to make something tense again.|例句：The teacher restressed the importance of studying hard.老师再次强调了努力学习的重要性。|例句：The recent events have restressed the need for better security measures. 近期发生的事件再次突显了加强安全措施的必要性。|近义词：reiterate, emphasize, re-emphasize|反义词：de-emphasize, downplay|动词


USAGE->

He restresses the key points in his presentation. 他在他的演示文稿中再次强调了要点。
She restresses the importance of punctuality every morning. 她每天早上都强调准时的重要性。
