conservers:/kənˈsɜːrvərz/| n.| 保护者；节约者；保存者 | People or things that conserve something.  They protect and preserve resources or prevent waste. | Example:  Energy conservers are crucial in reducing our carbon footprint. 能量节约者对于减少我们的碳足迹至关重要。  The government implemented new policies to encourage water conservers. 政府实施了新的政策来鼓励节约用水。|近义词：preservers, protectors, savers |反义词：wasters, squanderers |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The community conserves water resources. = 社区节约用水资源。
She conserves energy by using energy-efficient appliances. = 她通过使用节能电器来节约能源。

一般过去时:
They conserved food for the winter months. = 他们为冬季储存食物。

We have conserved a significant amount of energy this year. = 我们今年已经节省了大量的能源。
They have conserved the natural beauty of the area. = 他们已经保护了该地区的自然美景。

过去完成时:
They had conserved enough food to last through the harsh winter. = 他们储存了足够的食物以度过严冬。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
