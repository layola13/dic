lag:/læɡ/ | n. v. | 延迟；落后；迟缓；滞后 |  To fall behind; to move slowly; a delay or period of inactivity. | 例句：The project is lagging behind schedule. = 项目进度滞后。 The economy is lagging. = 经济发展缓慢。 | 近义词：delay,retard,slowdown | 反义词：advance, accelerate, hasten


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The project lags behind schedule. = 项目进度落后。
He lags behind the others in the race. = 他在比赛中落后于其他人。

一般过去时:
The train lagged behind schedule due to the heavy snow. = 由于大雪，火车晚点了。
She lagged behind her friends on the hike. = 她徒步旅行时落后于朋友们。

现在完成时:
The company has lagged in adopting new technologies. = 公司在采用新技术方面落后了。
The economy has lagged for several years. = 经济已经滞后了好几年。

过去完成时:
The team had lagged behind in the first half of the game. = 球队在上半场落后了。
He had lagged behind in his studies and failed the exam. = 他学习落后，考试不及格。

将来时:
The company expects to lag behind its competitors next year. = 公司预计明年将落后于竞争对手。
If we don't work harder, we will lag behind others. = 如果我们不更加努力，我们将落后于他人。

虚拟语气:
If the economy lagged further, there would be serious consequences. = 如果经济进一步下滑，将会产生严重的后果。
I wish the project hadn't lagged so much. = 我希望项目没有落后这么多。

被动语态:
The development of the product was lagged by the lack of funding. = 产品的开发由于缺乏资金而滞后。
The project was lagged behind due to unforeseen circumstances. = 项目由于不可预见的情况而落后。

疑问句:
Is the project lagging behind schedule? = 项目进度是否滞后？
Are you lagging behind in your studies? = 你的学习是否落后了？

否定句:
The company didn't lag behind in the market competition. = 公司在市场竞争中没有落后。
He didn't lag behind his classmates in any subjects. = 他在任何科目上都没有落后于同学。


PHRASE->
time lag: 时间延迟
lag behind: 落后
lag indicator:滞后指标
cognitive lag:认知迟滞
output lag: 输出滞后

词源:
The word "lag" ultimately derives from Old Norse *lag, meaning "layer" or "course".  Its meaning evolved through Middle English, where it took on the sense of "something that lies behind or is left behind."  The modern meaning reflecting delay or slowness developed over time.
