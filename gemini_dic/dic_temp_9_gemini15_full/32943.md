choppering:/ˈtʃɒpərɪŋ/ | v. | 砍，剁，搅碎 | The act of chopping repeatedly;  the sound of repeated chopping.  It can also refer to the act of using a helicopter repeatedly or the sound of a helicopter repeatedly flying overhead. | 例句：The butcher was choppering meat all morning. = 屠夫整个上午都在剁肉。 The constant choppering of the helicopter above was driving us mad. = 直升机在头顶上不断盘旋的声音快让我们发疯了。 | 近义词：hacking, mincing, hewing (for wood), whirring (for helicopter sound) | 反义词：assembling, joining, uniting
