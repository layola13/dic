indo-european:/ˌɪndoʊˈjʊərəpiən/| adj. n. |印欧语系的；印欧语系语言|Of or relating to the Indo-European family of languages.  The term refers to a large language family that includes most of the languages of Europe and parts of Asia.  |例句：English is an Indo-European language.  英语属于印欧语系。|例句：The Indo-European language family encompasses a vast geographical area. 印欧语系涵盖了广阔的地域。|近义词：Indo-European language|反义词：无
