chaetodont:/ˈkiːtədɒnt/| n. |毛齿鱼， щетинозубые|A chaetodont is a type of fish belonging to the family Chaetodontidae, characterized by their laterally compressed bodies, vibrant colors, and brush-like teeth used for feeding on corals and other invertebrates.  They are commonly known as butterflyfish. |例句：The coral reef was teeming with brightly colored chaetodonts.  珊瑚礁里挤满了色彩鲜艳的毛齿鱼。|例句：Many species of chaetodont are popular in the aquarium trade. 许多种类的毛齿鱼在水族馆贸易中很受欢迎。|近义词：butterflyfish|反义词：无
