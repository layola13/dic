signs:/saɪnz/| n.|迹象；征兆；符号；招牌|Signs can be physical manifestations, indications, symbols, or emblems that convey meaning or information.  They can be visible or subtle, and they often serve as clues or warnings.|例句：The dark clouds were signs of an approaching storm.  乌云是暴风雨即将来临的征兆。|例句：Traffic signs help drivers navigate safely. 交通标志帮助司机安全驾驶。|近义词：indications, symptoms, signals, markers|反义词：absence, lack
