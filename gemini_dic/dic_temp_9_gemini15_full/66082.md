excarnification:/ˌɛkskɑːrnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.| 剥肉；剔骨；除肉|The process of removing flesh from something, often in a ritualistic or symbolic context.  It can refer to the literal removal of flesh from bones, or a metaphorical removal of something essential or vital.|例句：The excarnification of the sacrificial animal was a crucial part of the ancient ceremony.  古代仪式中，祭祀动物的剥肉是一个关键部分。


例句：The artist's work depicts the excarnification of a human figure, symbolizing mortality. 艺术家的作品描绘了人体的剥肉，象征着死亡。


近义词：defleshing, disemboweling, evisceration


反义词：embodiment, incarnation


词性:n.
