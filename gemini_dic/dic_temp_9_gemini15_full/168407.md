proanarchism: /ˌproʊˈænərˌkɪzəm/ | n. |亲无政府主义|  Support for or advocacy of anarchism.  It represents a positive engagement with anarchist ideas and strategies, often distinguishing itself from mere "tolerance" of anarchism. | Example:  His writings reveal a strong proanarchism, advocating for decentralized social structures.  他的著作流露出强烈的亲无政府主义倾向，主张去中心化的社会结构。|近义词：anarchism sympathy, anarchist leanings |反义词：authoritarianism, statism
