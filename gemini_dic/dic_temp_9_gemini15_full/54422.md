disinfectant:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktənt/ | n. |消毒剂 |A substance that kills harmful bacteria or germs.  |Example:  We used a strong disinfectant to clean the wound. 我们用强效消毒剂清洗伤口。|近义词：antiseptic, germicide |反义词：contaminant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The disinfectant kills germs. = 消毒剂杀死细菌。
This disinfectant smells strongly of bleach. = 这种消毒剂有很浓的漂白剂气味。


一般过去时:
She disinfected the countertop with bleach. = 她用漂白剂消毒了台面。
They used a disinfectant to clean the spill. = 他们用消毒剂清理了溢出物。


现在完成时:
The hospital has used this disinfectant for years. = 这家医院已经使用这种消毒剂多年了。

过去完成时:
