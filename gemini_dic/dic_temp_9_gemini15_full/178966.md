rededicates: /ˌriːˈdɛdɪkeɪts/ | vt. |重新奉献；再献身；重新致力于 | To dedicate something again; to recommit oneself to a cause or purpose. |例句：The city rededicates the park to the memory of fallen soldiers.  城市重新将公园奉献给阵亡士兵的纪念。

The museum rededicates itself to preserving local history. 博物馆重新致力于保存当地历史。|近义词：recommit, reconsecrate, reaffirm |反义词：abandon, forsake, neglect
