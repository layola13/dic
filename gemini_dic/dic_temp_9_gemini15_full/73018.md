footbridge:/ˈfʊtbrɪdʒ/| n. |人行桥|A bridge designed for pedestrians to cross over a body of water, a road, or other obstacle.  It is usually narrower than a road bridge and may or may not have railings.|例句：The footbridge offered stunning views of the river.  人行桥提供了令人惊叹的河流景色。

例句：We crossed the footbridge to reach the other side of the park. 我们穿过人行桥到达公园的另一边。|近义词：pedestrian bridge, walkway, overpass|反义词：road bridge, highway|名词
