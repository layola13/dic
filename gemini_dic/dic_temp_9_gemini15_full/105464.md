kill-two-birds-with-one-stone: /kɪl tuː bɜːdz wɪð wʌn stəʊn/ | idiom | 一石二鸟；一举两得 | To achieve two things with a single action. This idiom originates from the idea of killing two birds with one stone thrown.  | 例句：By taking the highway, I killed two birds with one stone: I avoided traffic and got home early.  通过走高速公路，我做到了一举两得：既避开了交通拥堵，又早早到家了。


近义词：achieve two goals simultaneously, accomplish two things at once, solve two problems at once. | 反义词: to be inefficient, to waste time and effort. | 词性: idiom


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He kills two birds with one stone by exercising and spending time with his family. = 他通过锻炼和与家人共度时光，做到了一举两得。
She kills two birds with one stone by combining work and travel. = 她通过工作和旅行相结合，做到了一举两得。


一般过去时:
They killed two birds with one stone by buying a house in the countryside. = 他们通过在乡下买房，做到了一举两得。
He killed two birds with one stone by attending the conference and visiting his friend. = 他通过参加会议顺便拜访朋友，做到了一举两得。


现在完成时:
She has killed two birds with one stone by getting a promotion and a pay raise. = 她通过升职加薪，做到了一举两得。
We have killed two birds with one stone by fixing the roof and painting the house at the same time. = 我们通过同时修理屋顶和粉刷房子，做到了一举两得。


过去完成时:
He had killed two birds with one stone by finishing his homework and watching his favorite show. = 他通过完成作业并观看他最喜欢的节目，做到了两全其美。
They had killed two birds with one stone by buying the property and renting it out. = 他们通过购买房产并将其出租，做到了两全其美。


将来时:
I will kill two birds with one stone by visiting my parents and attending the family reunion. = 我将通过探望父母并参加家庭聚会，做到一举两得。
We will kill two birds with one stone by purchasing a new car and getting a good deal. = 我们将通过购买新车并获得优惠价，做到一举两得。


虚拟语气:
If I studied harder, I would kill two birds with one stone: improving my grades and learning new skills. = 如果我学习更努力，我就能做到一举两得：提高成绩并学习新技能。
I wish I could kill two birds with one stone by finding a job that is both enjoyable and well-paying. = 我希望我能找到一份既令人愉快又有好报酬的工作，做到一举两得。


被动语态:  (This idiom is rarely used in the passive voice because it describes an action actively taken.)


疑问句:
How can we kill two birds with one stone in this situation? = 在这种情况下，我们该如何做到一举两得？
Can we kill two birds with one stone by combining these two projects? = 通过合并这两个项目，我们能做到一举两得吗？


否定句:
He didn't kill two birds with one stone; he wasted time and resources. = 他并没有做到一举两得；他浪费了时间和资源。
We didn't kill two birds with one stone; we had to do each task separately. = 我们没有做到一举两得；我们不得不分别完成每个任务。
