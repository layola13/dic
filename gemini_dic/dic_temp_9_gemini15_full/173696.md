It suggests care, nurturing, and protection, but without the inherent biological connection. |例句：The teacher showed a quasi-maternal concern for her students' well-being.  老师对学生的福祉表现出一种类母性的关怀。|The older sister took on a quasi-maternal role for her younger siblings. 姐姐承担起了对弟弟妹妹的类母性角色。|近义词：motherly, nurturing, protective, caring |反义词：uncaring, neglectful, harsh, indifferent |
