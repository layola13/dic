recalibrates:/ˌriːˈkælɪbreɪts/| v.|重新校准；重新调整|To adjust or check the calibration of something again; to make a new calibration of.|例句：The technician recalibrates the instruments every month.  技师每月都会重新校准仪器。

例句：The GPS system automatically recalibrates itself to account for signal drift. GPS系统会自动重新校准自身以应对信号漂移。


近义词： readjust, readjust, re-evaluate, realign


反义词： miscalibrate,  maladjust


词性: 动词
