lyncher:/ˈlɪntʃər/| n. |私刑者，施以私刑的人 |A person who participates in lynching.  A lynching is an extrajudicial killing, often by a mob.  The term is usually associated with the historical racial violence in the United States against African Americans.|例句：The lynchers were never brought to justice. 私刑者从未受到法律制裁。|例句：He was accused of being a lynch mob leader. 他被指控是私刑暴徒的首领。|近义词：mob member, murderer, vigilante|反义词：law enforcer, judge, jury
