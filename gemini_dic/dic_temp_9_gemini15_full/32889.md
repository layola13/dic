chondrogenesis:/ˌkɒndrəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ | n. |软骨发生；软骨形成 | The process by which cartilage is formed.  It is a crucial stage in the development of the skeletal system.  | Example:  Disruptions in chondrogenesis can lead to skeletal abnormalities.  软骨发生中断会导致骨骼异常。| 近义词：cartilage formation | 反义词：chondrolysis (软骨溶解)
