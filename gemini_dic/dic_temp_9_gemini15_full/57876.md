dribbles:/ˈdrɪbəlz/| n. v.|滴，点滴；运球；|英文解析：To fall or flow in drops; to move a ball along a surface by repeatedly tapping or bouncing it.  As a noun, it refers to a small quantity of liquid that falls in drops.|例句：The water dribbled from the leaky faucet. 水从漏水的龙头滴下来。 The basketball player dribbled the ball down the court. 篮球运动员运球下场。|近义词：trickle, drip, dribble (v.), drippings (n.)|反义词：pour, gush, flood|词性：n. v.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The faucet dribbles constantly. = 水龙头不停地滴水。
He dribbles the basketball expertly. = 他熟练地运球。

一般过去时:
The milk dribbled down his chin. = 牛奶顺着他的下巴流了下来。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
