lascar:/ˈlɑːskɑːr/ | n. | 拉斯加尔人；（尤指印度或其他亚洲国家）水手；| A lascar is a person from South Asia, particularly an unskilled seaman. The term has historically been used in a derogatory manner.  | Example: Many lascars served on British ships during the 19th century. 许多拉斯加尔人在19世纪为英国船只服务。 | 近义词：seaman, sailor (in a neutral context) | 反义词：officer (in a nautical context)
