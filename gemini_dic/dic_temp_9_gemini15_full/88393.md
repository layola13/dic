hires:/ˈhaɪərz/| vt.|雇用，聘用|to employ someone|The company hires many graduates each year.  这家公司每年雇用许多毕业生。|They hired a new marketing manager. 他们聘请了一位新的市场经理。|employ, engage|fire, dismiss|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company hires new employees every month. = 公司每个月都雇用新员工。
He hires a car whenever he travels. = 他每次旅行都租车。


一般过去时:
They hired a contractor to renovate their house. = 他们雇了一名承包商来翻新他们的房子。
She hired a babysitter for the evening. = 她晚上雇了一个保姆。


现在完成时:
We have hired three new engineers this year. = 今年我们已经雇佣了三名工程师。
The firm has hired several interns for the summer. = 这家公司已经为暑假雇佣了几名实习生。


过去完成时:
They had already hired a replacement before he resigned. = 他辞职前他们已经聘请了替补人员。
The company had hired him for his expertise in AI. = 公司因为他在人工智能方面的专业知识而雇佣了他。


将来时:
We will hire more staff if the business expands. = 如果业务扩张，我们将雇佣更多员工。
They will hire a consultant to advise on the project. = 他们将聘请顾问为项目提供建议。


虚拟语气:
If they hired a better manager, the team would be more productive. = 如果他们雇佣一位更好的经理，团队的生产力将会更高。
I wish they had hired someone with more experience. = 我希望他们当初雇佣一个更有经验的人。


被动语态:
A new marketing specialist was hired last week. = 上周聘用了一位新的市场专家。
Many temporary workers are hired during the peak season. = 在旺季期间会雇用许多临时工。


疑问句:
Will you hire me for the job? = 你会雇用我做这份工作吗？
Have they hired anyone yet? = 他们已经雇用任何人了吗？


否定句:
They did not hire him because of his lack of experience. = 他们因为他的经验不足而没有雇用他。
We will not hire anyone without proper qualifications. = 我们不会雇佣任何没有适当资格的人。


PHRASE->
hire out = 出租
hire on = 雇佣
hire purchase = 分期付款购买
for hire = 出租的
hire somebody to do something = 雇佣某人做某事
