fritterers: /ˈfrɪtərərz/ | n. |挥霍者；浪费者 |Those who fritter away time or money on unimportant things. People who squander resources or opportunities.  |例句：The fritterers spent their inheritance on frivolous pursuits. 挥霍者把他们的遗产花在了毫无意义的事情上。|近义词：spendthrifts, wasters, squanderers |反义词：savers, misers


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fritters away his money on video games. 他把钱浪费在电子游戏上。
They fritter their time on social media. 他们把时间浪费在社交媒体上。


一般过去时:
She frittered away her savings on a useless gadget. 她把积蓄浪费在一个无用的装置上。
They frittered away the whole afternoon watching TV. 他们整个下午都浪费在看电视上。


现在完成时:
He has frittered away his entire fortune. 他已经挥霍了他的全部财产。
She has frittered away years of her life on unproductive endeavors. 她已经把几年的时间浪费在没有成效的事情上。


过去完成时:

They had frittered away the opportunity before they even considered it. 他们在甚至考虑之前就已经错过了机会。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
