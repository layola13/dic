replots:/riːˈplɒts/| v.|重新绘制；重新描绘|To draw or plot again; to redraw a graph or chart.|例句：The engineer replotted the data to clarify the trend. 工程师重新绘制了数据以阐明趋势。|近义词：redraw, replan, remake|反义词：erase, delete
