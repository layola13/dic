reconfuse: /ˌriːkənˈfjuːz/ | vt. | 使再次迷惑；使再次糊涂 | To confuse someone again or cause someone to be confused again after they had understood something.  | The new instructions completely reconfused me. 新的指示完全把我搞糊涂了。 | confuse, bewilder, perplex | clarify, enlighten | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reconfuses the issue with irrelevant details. 他用无关紧要的细节再次混淆了问题。
The teacher reconfuses the students with conflicting explanations.老师用相互矛盾的解释再次把学生们弄糊涂了。


一般过去时:
The change in plans reconfused everyone. 计划的改变再次让每个人都感到困惑。
The misleading information reconfused the investigators. 误导性信息再次让调查人员感到困惑。


现在完成时:
The conflicting reports have reconfused the public. 互相矛盾的报道已经再次让公众感到困惑。
Repeated explanations have reconfused the situation instead of clarifying it.  反复的解释非但没有澄清情况，反而再次使情况变得混乱。


过去完成时:
They had reconfused the issue by introducing a new variable. 他们通过引入一个新的变量再次使问题变得混乱。
She had reconfused herself by rereading the confusing passage. 她反复阅读那段令人困惑的段落，结果再次把自己弄糊涂了。


将来时:
The new policy will reconfuse many people. 新政策将会再次让很多人感到困惑。
Further delays will reconfuse the schedule. 进一步的延误将会再次打乱计划。


虚拟语气:
If he had explained it more clearly, he wouldn't have reconfused the audience. 如果他解释得更清楚一些，就不会再次让听众感到困惑了。
I wish he hadn't reconfused the matter with his contradictory statements.  我希望他没有用他自相矛盾的话再次使问题复杂化。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
