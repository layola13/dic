desktop-publishing: /ˈdɛskˌtɑp ˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ/ | n. |桌面排版|The process of using software to create high-quality documents, such as brochures, newsletters, and books, for printing or digital distribution.  It involves combining text and graphics to produce visually appealing layouts.|Example:  Desktop publishing software allows for greater control over the design and layout of documents. 桌面排版软件允许对文档的设计和布局进行更精细的控制。|近义词: page layout, typesetting |反义词:  hand-typesetting (traditional method)
