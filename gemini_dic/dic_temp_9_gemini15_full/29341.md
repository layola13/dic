castlebuilder:/ˈkæsəlˌbɪldər/ | n. |城堡建造者；（尤指）擅长建造城堡的人 | A castlebuilder is someone who builds castles, often implying skill and expertise in the construction of such structures.  The term can also be used figuratively to describe someone who creates ambitious or elaborate plans or structures, not necessarily physical castles. |Example:  He was a renowned castlebuilder, known throughout the land for his magnificent creations. 他是一位著名的城堡建造者，以其宏伟的建筑而闻名于世。


Example: She is a figurative castlebuilder, meticulously planning her career path. 她是一位比喻意义上的城堡建造者，一丝不苟地规划着她的职业道路。|近义词: castle architect, builder, constructor, architect (figurative use)|反义词: castle destroyer, deconstructor |名词
