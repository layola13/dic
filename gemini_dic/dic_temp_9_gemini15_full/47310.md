de-license:/ˌdiːˈlaɪs(ə)ns/| v.|吊销许可证；取消许可|To revoke or cancel a license.|The company was de-licensed for violating safety regulations.  这家公司因违反安全条例而被吊销了许可证。|The government de-licensed the radio station. 政府吊销了该广播电台的执照。|license, authorize|prohibit, forbid|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They de-license unsafe vehicles. = 他们吊销不安全车辆的许可证。
The authority de-licenses unqualified drivers. = 当局吊销不合格司机的驾照。


一般过去时:
The restaurant was de-licensed last year. = 这家餐馆的营业执照去年被吊销了。
His driving license was de-licensed after the accident. = 他在事故后驾照被吊销了。
