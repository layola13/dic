perverting:/pərˈvɜːrtɪŋ/ | v. | 歪曲；曲解；使堕落；滥用 | To corrupt, distort, or misuse something, especially morals, principles, or facts.  | 例句：He was accused of perverting the course of justice. 他被指控妨碍司法公正。  | 例句：The dictator perverted the truth to maintain power. 独裁者歪曲真相以维持权力。 | 近义词：distorting, corrupting, twisting, misrepresenting | 反义词：clarifying, rectifying, upholding, clarifying
