pigtail:/ˈpɪɡteɪl/| n. |辫子，小辫|A pigtail is a single braid of hair, typically worn at the back of the head.  It is often associated with children, but can also be a stylistic choice for adults. |例句：She wore her long hair in two pigtails. 她把长发扎成两个小辫子。|例句：The little girl's pigtails bounced as she ran. 小女孩跑起来的时候，她的辫子一晃一晃的。|近义词：braid, plait|反义词：loose hair (散开的头发)

词源:
The word "pigtail" originated in the 17th century.  The "pig" part likely refers to the resemblance of the tightly braided hair to a pig's tail, while "-tail" simply refers to the tail-like appendage.
