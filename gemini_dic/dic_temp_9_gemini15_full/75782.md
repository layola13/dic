fumado:/fuˈmɑːdoʊ/| adj.|熏制的，烟熏的|Smoked, typically referring to food that has been preserved or flavored by exposure to smoke.|The smoked salmon was delicious.  熏鲑鱼很好吃。|The restaurant offered a variety of smoked meats. 这家餐馆提供各种烟熏肉。|近义词：smoked, cured|反义词：fresh


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The chef smokes the fish every morning. = 厨师每天早上都熏鱼。
He smokes his own bacon. = 他自己熏培根。


一般过去时:
They smoked the meat yesterday. = 他们昨天熏肉。
She smoked the cheese for several hours. = 她熏制奶酪几个小时。


现在完成时:
He has smoked many types of fish. = 他熏制过许多种鱼。
We have smoked our own salmon for years. = 我们多年来一直在自己熏制鲑鱼。


过去完成时:
They had smoked the brisket before the guests arrived. = 客人到达之前，他们已经熏好了牛肉胸肉。
She had smoked the ham for twelve hours. = 她已经熏制火腿十二个小时了。


将来时:
We will smoke the trout tomorrow. = 我们明天将熏制鳟鱼。
They will smoke the cheese until it's golden brown. = 他们会一直熏制奶酪直到变成金棕色。


虚拟语气:
If I had a smoker, I would smoke my own meats. = 如果我有一个烟熏炉，我就会自己熏肉。
I wish I had smoked the fish longer. = 我希望我多熏制鱼一会儿。


被动语态:
The salmon was smoked over hickory wood. = 鲑鱼是用山核桃木熏制的。
The cheese is being smoked in a traditional way. = 奶酪正在以传统方式进行熏制。


疑问句:
Is the fish smoked or grilled? = 这鱼是熏的还是烤的？
Was the meat smoked properly? = 肉熏制得恰当吗？


否定句:
The meat wasn't smoked enough. = 肉没有熏够。
They didn't smoke the paprika. = 他们没有熏制红辣椒粉。
