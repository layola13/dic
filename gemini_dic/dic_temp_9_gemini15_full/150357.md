panir: /pəˈniːr/ | n. |印度奶酪| A fresh, unsalted cheese made from cow or buffalo milk, commonly used in Indian cuisine. It is a non-melting cheese, meaning it holds its shape well when cooked.  | Example:  The paneer tikka was delicious.  这道印度奶酪串非常美味。|近义词:Indian cheese, chenna (similar but not exactly the same)|反义词:None (no direct antonym)|名词
