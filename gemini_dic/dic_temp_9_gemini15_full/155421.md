pheasants:/ˈfɛzənts/| n.|雉鸡，环颈雉|A pheasant is a type of game bird known for its colorful plumage, particularly the males.  They are found in various parts of the world and are popular targets for hunting.|例句：The hunter shot a magnificent pheasant.  猎人射杀了一只雄伟的雉鸡。

The farmer raises pheasants on his land.  这个农民在他的土地上饲养雉鸡。|近义词：game bird,fowl|反义词:None specifically.
