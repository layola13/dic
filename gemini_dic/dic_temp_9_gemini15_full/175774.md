rarefy:/ˈrɛrɪfaɪ/ | vt. |使稀薄；使稀疏；使精炼 | To make or become less dense; to make thinner or less concentrated.  |例句：Heat will rarefy the air.  = 热会使空气稀薄。  The artist rarefied her palette, using only a few carefully chosen colors. =艺术家简化了她的调色板，只使用了少数精心挑选的颜色。|近义词：thin, dilute, attenuate |反义词：densify, condense, thicken |动词
