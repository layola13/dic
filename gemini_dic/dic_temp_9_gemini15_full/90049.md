hospitality:/ˌhɒspɪˈtælɪti/ /ˌhɑːspɪˈtælɪti/| n. |好客；殷勤待客；款待；热情；盛情款待|The quality of being friendly and welcoming to guests or strangers.  It often involves providing food, drink, and comfortable accommodation.|例句：The hotel is renowned for its excellent hospitality. 这家酒店以其卓越的待客之道而闻名。|例句：We were overwhelmed by the warmth of their hospitality.  他们的热情好客让我们感动不已。|近义词：generosity, kindness, welcome, warmth|反义词：inhospitality, coldness, rudeness|名词
