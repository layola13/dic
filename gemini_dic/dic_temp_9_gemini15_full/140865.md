on-the-other-hand:/ˌɒn ðiː ˈʌðə(r) hænd/ /ˌɑːn ðiː ˈʌðər hænd/ |adv.|另一方面，反之|This phrase introduces a contrasting point or argument. It signifies a shift in perspective or a presentation of an alternative viewpoint.|例句：He's very experienced; on the other hand, he's rather lazy. 他经验丰富；另一方面，他又很懒惰。|例句：The plan is ambitious; on the other hand, it's also very risky. 这个计划雄心勃勃；另一方面，风险也很大。|近义词：conversely, conversely, in contrast, by contrast, however|反义词：similarly, likewise, also, additionally


USAGE->
一般现在时:
On the other hand,  she is very kind. = 另一方面，她很善良。
On the other hand, he prefers tea. = 另一方面，他更喜欢茶。


一般过去时:
On the other hand, he didn't go to the party. = 另一方面，他没有去参加聚会。
On the other hand, she didn't finish her homework. = 另一方面，她没有完成作业。


现在完成时:
On the other hand,  they haven't seen each other for years. = 另一方面，他们多年没见面了。
On the other hand, she has already completed the project. = 另一方面，她已经完成了项目。


过去完成时:
On the other hand, he had already left when I arrived. = 另一方面，在我到达时他已经离开了。
On the other hand, they had finished eating before the guests arrived. = 另一方面，客人们到达之前他们已经吃完了。


将来时:
On the other hand, we will be having a meeting tomorrow. = 另一方面，我们明天将举行会议。
On the other hand, he will probably not come. = 另一方面，他可能不会来。


虚拟语气:
On the other hand, if I were you, I wouldn't do that. = 另一方面，如果我是你，我不会那样做。
On the other hand, I wish I had more time. = 另一方面，我希望我有更多的时间。


被动语态:
On the other hand, the problem is being addressed. = 另一方面，这个问题正在得到解决。
On the other hand, the decision was made without consultation. = 另一方面，这个决定是在没有协商的情况下做出的。


疑问句:
On the other hand, is there another solution? = 另一方面，还有其他解决方案吗？
On the other hand, wasn't that a better idea? = 另一方面，那不是更好的主意吗？


否定句:
On the other hand, it wasn't a complete failure. = 另一方面，它并非完全失败。
On the other hand,  I don't agree with his opinion. = 另一方面，我不同意他的观点。


PHRASE->
On the other hand, ... = 另一方面...

However, on the other hand,... = 然而，另一方面...
In contrast, on the other hand,... = 相比之下，另一方面...
But on the other hand,... = 但是另一方面...
