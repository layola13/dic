outstudies:/ˌaʊtˈstʌdiz/| vt.|超过…的学习；在学习方面胜过|To surpass someone in studies or academic achievement.|例句：He outstudied all his classmates and topped the exam. 他在学习上超过了所有同学，并取得了考试第一名。|近义词：surpass, excel, outperform|反义词：underperform, lag behind|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He outstudies his brother in math. = 他在数学方面超过了他的兄弟。
She outstudies her classmates in every subject. = 她在每一门学科上都超过了她的同学们。


一般过去时:
He outstudied his rivals and won the scholarship. = 他在学习上超过了他的竞争对手，并赢得了奖学金。
She outstudied me in preparation for the test. = 她为考试准备得比我好。


现在完成时:
He has outstudied everyone else in the class. = 他在班里已经超过了其他所有人。
She has outstudied her peers throughout her academic career. = 在整个学习生涯中，她都超过了她的同龄人。


过去完成时:
He had outstudied all his competitors by the time the competition began. = 当比赛开始时，他已经超过了所有竞争对手。
She had outstudied him in the previous semester. = 在上个学期，她就已经超过了他。


将来时:
He will outstudy his opponents and win the debate. = 他将超过他的对手并赢得辩论。
She will outstudy her classmates and secure a top grade. = 她将超过她的同学们并获得最高分。


虚拟语气:
If he had outstudied more diligently, he would have achieved a higher score. = 如果他学习更努力，他就会取得更高的分数。
If she had outstudied harder, she would have won the prize. = 如果她学习更努力，她就会赢得奖项。


被动语态:
He was outstudied by his diligent sister. = 他被勤奋的姐姐超过了。
She was outstudied in the competition. = 她在比赛中被超过了。


疑问句:
Does he outstudy his classmates regularly? = 他经常超过他的同学们吗？
Did she outstudy him on this particular assignment? = 在这项具体的作业中，她超过他了吗？


否定句:
He didn't outstudy his rival in that subject. = 他在那门学科上没有超过他的竞争对手。
She didn't outstudy him in the final examination. = 她在期末考试中没有超过他。
