commenders:/kəˈmɛndərz/| n. |推荐者，赞扬者 |Those who commend or praise.  People who express approval or admiration.  The word is the plural form of "commender". |例句：The commenders of the new policy were mostly senior managers. 新政策的支持者大多是高级管理人员。|例句：The book received glowing reviews from numerous commenders. 这本书收到了众多推荐者的热情评价。|近义词：praisers, approvers, supporters |反义词：critics, detractors
