fission:/ˈfɪʒən/| n. |裂变；分裂；分歧|The process by which the nucleus of an atom splits into two or more smaller nuclei, releasing a large amount of energy.  This process is used in nuclear power plants and atomic bombs.|例句：Nuclear fission is a powerful source of energy. 核裂变是强大的能源来源。|例句：The political party experienced a fission over policy disagreements.  该政党因政策分歧而分裂。|近义词：splitting, division, cleavage |反义词：fusion, union, merger
