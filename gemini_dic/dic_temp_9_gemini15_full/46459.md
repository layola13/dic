dahl:/dɑːl/| n.|扁豆；达尔（印度菜）|A lentil or a dish made from lentils.  Dahl is a type of lentil, often used in South Asian cuisine to make a stew or soup. |例句：I love to eat lentil dahl with rice.  我喜欢吃扁豆达尔配米饭。|例句：The restaurant is famous for its delicious dahl makhani. 这家餐馆以其美味的奶油扁豆达尔而闻名。|近义词：lentil, pulse|反义词：无
