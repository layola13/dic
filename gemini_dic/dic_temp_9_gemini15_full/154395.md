permeated:/ˈpɜːmieɪtɪd/ | vt. | 弥漫；渗透；充满 | To spread throughout (something); to penetrate or diffuse |  The aroma of freshly baked bread permeated the kitchen.  新鲜烘焙的面包的香味弥漫在厨房里。  His ideas permeated the entire organization. 他的想法渗透了整个组织。 |  penetrate, diffuse, infiltrate, imbue |  isolate, separate | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The scent permeates the air. = 气味弥漫在空气中。
His influence permeates the company. = 他的影响遍及公司。

一般过去时:
The rain permeated the soil. = 雨水渗透了土壤。
The dye permeated the fabric. = 染料渗透了织物。


现在完成时:
The new ideas have permeated the industry. = 新理念已经渗透到这个行业。
His philosophy has permeated his work. = 他的哲学思想贯穿了他的作品。


过去完成时:
The poison had permeated his system. = 毒素已经渗透了他的系统。
The rumor had permeated the town before the truth emerged. = 在真相大白之前，谣言已经传遍了整个城镇。


将来时:
The fragrance will permeate the room. = 香味将弥漫整个房间。
The new technology will permeate various industries. = 新技术将渗透到各个行业。


虚拟语气:
If the chemical permeated the soil, the plants would die. = 如果化学物质渗透到土壤中，植物就会死亡。
I wish the positive energy permeated our society more. = 我希望积极的能量更多地渗透到我们的社会中。


被动语态:
The fabric was permeated by the dye. = 织物被染料渗透了。
The city was permeated by a sense of unease. = 城市弥漫着不安的情绪。


疑问句:
Does the smell permeate the whole building? = 气味是否弥漫了整栋楼？
Will this policy permeate all levels of government? = 这一政策是否会渗透到政府的各个层面？


否定句:
The water did not permeate the ground. = 水没有渗透到地下。
The message failed to permeate the public consciousness. = 这条信息未能渗透到公众意识中。


PHRASE->
permeated with = 充满...的
permeated by = 被...渗透
permeated throughout = 遍布于...
deeply permeated = 深入渗透
permeated the culture = 渗透文化


词源:
From Latin *permeāre ("to go through"), from per ("through") + meāre ("to go").
