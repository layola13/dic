instigating: /ˈɪnstɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 煽动；激起；唆使 |  Provoking or stirring up (a disruptive or violent action or feeling).  |例句：He was accused of instigating the riot. 他被指控煽动暴乱。

例句：The protesters were instigating a revolution.  抗议者正在煽动一场革命。


近义词：provoking, inciting, stirring up, fomenting, agitating | 反义词：preventing, calming, appeasing, pacifying


词源:

The word "instigating" comes from the Latin word "instigare," which means "to goad," "to urge on," or "to incite."  The prefix "in-" intensifies the action, while the root "-stigare" is related to the word "sting," suggesting a sharp, provoking action.

一般过去时:

现在完成时:

过去完成时:

将来时:

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
