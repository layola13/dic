fascine:/ˈfæsɪn/ | v. | 使着迷；迷住；吸引 | To attract and hold the attention of someone completely; captivate. | The magician's tricks fascinated the audience. 魔术师的戏法迷住了观众。  | captivate, enthrall, enchant | repel, bore | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The vibrant colors fascinate me. =鲜艳的色彩吸引着我。
His stories fascinate his listeners. =他的故事吸引着听众。

一般过去时:
The ancient ruins fascinated the tourists. = 古代遗址吸引了游客。
The mystery of the disappearance fascinated the detective. = 消失的谜团吸引了侦探。

现在完成时:
The beauty of the landscape has fascinated me for years. = 这片风景的美景多年来一直吸引着我。
Her unique style has fascinated fashion critics. =她独特的风格吸引了时尚评论家。

过去完成时:
I had been fascinated by astronomy since childhood. = 从小我就对天文学很着迷。
They had already been fascinated by the performance before the intermission. = 中场休息前他们就已经被表演迷住了。

将来时:
The new exhibit will fascinate visitors. = 新展览会吸引游客。
I think this book will fascinate you. = 我认为这本书会吸引你。

虚拟语气:
If only the problem were simpler, it would fascinate me more. =如果问题更简单一些，我就会更感兴趣。
I wish the movie had fascinated me. =我希望这部电影能吸引我。


被动语态:
The audience was fascinated by the performance. = 观众被演出迷住了。
He was fascinated by her intelligence. = 他被她的智慧迷住了。


疑问句:
Does the topic fascinate you? = 这个话题吸引你吗？
What fascinates you about this subject? = 你对这个主题什么感兴趣？


否定句:
The lecture did not fascinate the students. = 讲座没有吸引学生。
The game didn't fascinate me at all. =这场比赛一点都没吸引我。


PHRASE->
be fascinated by = 对...着迷
be fascinated with = 对...着迷
absolutely fascinated = 完全着迷
utterly fascinated = 完全着迷
deeply fascinated = 深深着迷

词源:
源自拉丁语 *fascinare，意为“施魔法，迷惑”。  最终来自于拉丁语 *fascinum，意为“魔咒，邪恶影响”。
