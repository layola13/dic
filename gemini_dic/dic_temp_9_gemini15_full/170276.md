provable:/ˈpruːvəbl/| adj.| 可证明的；可证实的 | capable of being proved or demonstrated to be true.  |例句：The theory is provable through experimentation.  这个理论可以通过实验来证明。
例句：His guilt was provable beyond a reasonable doubt. 他的罪行已无可辩驳地得到证明。|近义词： demonstrable, verifiable, justifiable |反义词：unprovable, refutable, irrefutable


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The statement is provable. = 这句话是可以证明的。
This theorem is provable within the system. = 这个定理在这个系统内是可证的。

一般过去时:
The hypothesis was provable with the new data. = 用新的数据可以证明这个假设。

现在完成时:

Scientists have found the concept to be provable. = 科学家们发现这个概念是可证明的。

过去完成时:
It had been provable that the defendant was innocent. =  被告的清白是可以证明的。
