neven:/ˈnevən/ | adj. | 从未；决不 | Never is an adverb meaning "at no time in the past or future,"  used to emphasize a negative statement. It can also be used to mean "not ever." | 例句：I have never been to Paris. = 我从未去过巴黎。 He will never forget that day. = 他永远不会忘记那天。 | 近义词：nevertheless, ever | 反义词:always, ever
