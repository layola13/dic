organdy:/ˈɔːrɡændi/| n. |欧根纱|A thin, transparent, usually cotton fabric with a crisp texture.  It is often used for dresses, blouses, and other garments requiring a lightweight, sheer material.|例句：The bride wore a beautiful gown made of organdy.新娘穿着一件美丽的欧根纱礼服。|例句：She chose an organdy blouse for the summer heat.她选择了一件欧根纱衬衫来应对夏日的炎热。|近义词：batiste, voile, muslin|反义词：brocade, tweed
