roomful:/ˈruːmful/| n. |一屋子的人或物|A roomful is a noun referring to the contents of a room, typically a group of people or things that fill a room.  It implies a large quantity. |Example: There was a roomful of excited children waiting for Santa.  一屋子兴奋的孩子在等着圣诞老人。|近义词:room's contents, a crowd, a throng|反义词:empty room
