cockatiels:/ˈkɒkətiːlz/ | n. |葵花凤头鹦鹉 |A small, crested parrot native to Australia, known for its distinctive yellow and grey plumage and gentle temperament.  Cockatiels are popular pet birds due to their relatively easy care and affectionate nature. | Example:  My cockatiel sings a beautiful song every morning.  我的葵花凤头鹦鹉每天早上都会唱一首优美的歌。 |近义词:  nymphicus hollandicus (scientific name) |反义词: N/A
