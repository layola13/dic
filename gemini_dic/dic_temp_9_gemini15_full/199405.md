simplificatory: /ˌsɪmplɪfɪˈkeɪtəri/ | adj. | 简化的，使简化的 | Tending or intended to simplify; characterized by simplification.  This word describes something that aims to make things simpler or that is characterized by its simplicity.  |

例句：

The simplificatory approach to the problem proved effective. = 简化的方法证明是有效的。

His simplificatory explanations made the complex subject accessible to beginners. = 他简化的解释使这个复杂的问题对初学者来说易于理解。


近义词：simplifying, simplifying, streamlining,  reducing

反义词：complicating, complexifying


词性：形容词
