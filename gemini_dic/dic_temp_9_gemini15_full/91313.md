hydrometry:/haɪˈdrɒmətri/ | n. |测流法；水文测量 |The science or practice of measuring the flow of water in rivers, streams, or other bodies of water.  It involves techniques for determining the speed, volume, and discharge of water. |例句：The engineers used hydrometry to assess the river's flow rate before constructing the dam.  工程师们在建造水坝之前用测流法评估了河流的流速。

例句：Accurate hydrometry is crucial for managing water resources and predicting floods.  精确的测流法对于管理水资源和预测洪水至关重要。


近义词：stream gauging, river gauging, flow measurement |反义词：无
