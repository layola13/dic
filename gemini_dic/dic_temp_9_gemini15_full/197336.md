shawling:/ˈʃɔːlɪŋ/| n.| 围巾；披肩 | A shawling is a long piece of cloth, often made of wool or silk, worn around the shoulders or head for warmth or decoration.  |例句：She draped a beautiful shawling around her shoulders to ward off the evening chill. 她披上了一条美丽的披肩以抵御夜晚的寒意。|例句：The shawling was embroidered with intricate floral patterns. 这条披肩绣着精美的花卉图案。|近义词：scarf, wrap, stole |反义词：无 |名词
