pistazite:/ˈpɪstəzaɪt/| n. |绿帘石|A pistachio-green to emerald-green mineral, a calcium aluminum sorosilicate hydroxide with variable iron and manganese.  It is often found in metamorphic rocks. |Example: The geologist identified the specimen as pistazite due to its distinctive pistachio green color and crystalline structure.|近义词：epidote (a broader category)|反义词：N/A
