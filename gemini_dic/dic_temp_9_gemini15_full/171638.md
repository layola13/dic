pulps:/pʌlps/| n.|纸浆；果肉；牙髓|The soft, wet mass of plant fibres from which paper is made; the soft, juicy inner part of a fruit; the soft tissue within a tooth.|例句：The paper is made from wood pulp. 这张纸是用木浆做的。  例句：I love the pulp of the orange. 我喜欢橙子的果肉。|近义词：pulpit, mash|反义词：shell, rind

The machine pulps the wood. = 机器将木材制成纸浆。
He always pulps the oranges before eating them. = 他总是先把橘子剥了再吃。
