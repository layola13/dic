outwardly:/ˈaʊtwərdli/| adv.|表面上地，外表上地|Appearing on the surface; externally.  It describes something that is visible or apparent, but may not reflect the inner reality or truth. |例句：Outwardly, she seemed calm, but inside she was panicking.  表面上她看起来很平静，但内心却很恐慌。|例句：He outwardly supported the plan, but secretly he opposed it. 他表面上支持这个计划，但暗地里却反对它。|近义词：apparently, seemingly, superficially|反义词：inwardly, internally, secretly
