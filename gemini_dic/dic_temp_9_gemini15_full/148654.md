overthrows:/ˌoʊvərˈθroʊz/| vt.|推翻；颠覆；打倒|To overthrow something means to violently remove it from power or to destroy it completely.  The verb often refers to governments or systems of power.|例句：The rebels successfully overthrew the dictator.  叛军成功推翻了独裁者。
The earthquake overthrew several buildings. 地震摧毁了几栋建筑物。|近义词：overturn, subvert, topple|反义词：establish, uphold, support|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rebels overthrow the government. 叛军推翻政府。
He overthrows his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中击败了他的对手。


一般过去时:
The people overthrew the monarchy. 人民推翻了君主制。
The army overthrew the president. 军队推翻了总统。


现在完成时:
The revolution has overthrown the old regime. 革命已经推翻了旧政权。
They have overthrown the previous champion. 他们已经击败了之前的冠军。


过去完成时:
The revolutionaries had overthrown the king before the news reached the capital. 在消息传到首都之前，革命者已经推翻了国王。
The team had overthrown their rivals in the previous game.  该队在之前的比赛中击败了他们的对手。


将来时:
The opposition plans to overthrow the government next year. 反对派计划明年推翻政府。
He will overthrow his opponent with a decisive victory. 他将以决定性的胜利击败对手。


虚拟语气:
If the people had been more united, they might have overthrown the tyrant. 如果人民更加团结，他们也许就能推翻暴君。
I wish they would overthrow the corrupt government. 我希望他们能推翻腐败的政府。


被动语态:
The government was overthrown by the rebels. 政府被叛军推翻了。
The champion was overthrown in the final match. 冠军在决赛中被击败了。


疑问句:
Will the rebels overthrow the government? 叛军会推翻政府吗？
Can we overthrow this injustice? 我们能推翻这种不公正吗？


否定句:
The rebels did not overthrow the government. 叛军没有推翻政府。
He failed to overthrow his opponent. 他未能击败他的对手。
