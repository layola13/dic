similor:/ˈsɪmələr/| adj. |类似的，相似的|Similar things are alike in some way, but not identical.  They share some characteristics but have differences as well.|例句：His new car is similar to mine. 他的新车和我的很像。|例句：The two paintings are strikingly similar. 这两幅画惊人地相似。|近义词：alike, like, resembling, comparable|反义词：different, dissimilar, unlike


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The houses are similar in style. = 这些房子的风格相似。
These two plans are similar in their approach. = 这两个计划在方法上相似。

一般过去时:
Their opinions were similar on that issue. = 他们在那个问题上的观点相似。
The two experiments yielded similar results. = 这两个实验产生了相似的结果。

现在完成时:
We have found many similar cases. = 我们已经找到了许多类似的案例。
Scientists have discovered similar patterns in different species. = 科学家们在不同的物种中发现了相似的模式。

过去完成时:
They had already seen similar problems before. = 他们以前已经见过类似的问题了。
The researchers had encountered similar challenges in their previous study. = 研究人员在之前的研究中遇到过类似的挑战。

将来时:
The new model will be similar to the previous one. = 新型号将与之前的型号相似。
Future developments will likely be similar to current trends. = 未来的发展很可能与目前的趋势相似。

虚拟语气:
If the conditions were similar, the outcome would be the same. = 如果条件相似，结果将相同。
I wish my situation were similar to yours. = 我希望我的情况和你一样。

被动语态:
The two designs were considered similar by the experts. = 这两种设计被专家认为是相似的。
This approach is often considered similar to the traditional method. = 这种方法通常被认为与传统方法相似。

疑问句:
Are the two proposals similar in their objectives? = 这两个提案的目标相似吗？
Is your experience similar to mine? = 你的经历和我相似吗？

否定句:
The results were not similar across all groups. = 结果在所有组别中并不相似。
His attitude is not similar to hers.  = 他的态度与她不同。



PHRASE->
similar to = 与…相似
similar in = 在…方面相似
strikingly similar = 惊人地相似
broadly similar = 大致相似
quite similar = 相当相似
