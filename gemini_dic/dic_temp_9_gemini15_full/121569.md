misnavigates:/ˌmɪsˈnævɪɡeɪts/| vt.| 导航错误；误判航向；走错路 | To fail to navigate correctly; to make a mistake in navigation.  It implies a failure to understand or follow the correct course, often resulting in going the wrong way or reaching the wrong destination. | 例句：The captain misnavigated the ship, leading to a collision with an iceberg. 船长导航错误，导致与冰山相撞。

The hikers misnavigated the trail and ended up lost in the woods.  徒步旅行者走错了小路，最终迷失在树林里。| 近义词：err, stray, misdirect, go astray | 反义词：navigate correctly, guide accurately
