overlend:/ˌoʊvərˈlɛnd/ | vt. |过度贷款，过度放贷|To lend excessively or beyond prudent limits.  This often involves lending to borrowers who are considered high-risk or extending credit beyond the borrower's capacity to repay.|例句：The bank was criticized for overlending to subprime borrowers, contributing to the financial crisis.  银行因向次级抵押贷款借款人过度放贷而受到批评，这导致了金融危机。
例句：The government's policy of overlending to state-owned enterprises led to significant financial losses. 政府向国有企业过度放贷的政策导致了巨大的财务损失。|近义词：overextend credit, excessively lend |反义词：underlend, restrict lending

词源:
The word "overlend" is a combination of the prefix "over-" (meaning "too much" or "excessively") and the verb "lend" (meaning "to give something to someone with the understanding that it will be returned").  Therefore, it literally means "to lend too much."
