kissed:/kɪst/| v.|亲吻|To touch with the lips, especially as a sign of affection, greeting, or farewell.  It can also refer to a light touch or graze.|例句：He kissed her gently on the cheek. 他轻轻地吻了她的脸颊。

例句：The child kissed her mother goodnight.  孩子亲吻了母亲道晚安。

近义词：smooched, pecked, embraced

反义词：None directly opposite, but could be considered the opposite of ignoring or rejecting.

词性:动词
