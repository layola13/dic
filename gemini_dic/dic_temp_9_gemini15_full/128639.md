nicker:/ˈnɪkər/| n. v.|马嘶叫声；低声抱怨；(非正式)偷窃 | a horse's soft, gentle whinny; a low complaining sound; (informal) to steal something |例句：The horse let out a soft nicker.  那匹马发出一声轻柔的嘶鸣。  |近义词：whinny, neigh, murmur, grumble |反义词：roar, bellow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The horse often nickers to its foal. = 马经常对它的幼崽嘶鸣。
He constantly nickers about his low salary. = 他不断地抱怨他的低薪。


一般过去时:
The pony nickered softly as I approached. = 当我走近时，小马轻轻地嘶鸣着。
She nickered about the unfair treatment she received. = 她抱怨自己受到的不公平待遇。


现在完成时:
The mare has nickered several times since the foal was born. = 自从小马驹出生以来，母马已经嘶鸣了好几次。
He has been nickering all morning about the missed deadline. =  他整个上午都在抱怨错过的截止日期。


过去完成时:
The stallion had nickered anxiously before the race. = 在比赛之前，种马焦虑地嘶鸣着。
She had nickered repeatedly about the project's delays before the meeting. = 在会议之前，她已经反复抱怨项目的延误。


将来时:
The foal will nicker for its mother when it's hungry. = 小马饿了就会嘶鸣找妈妈。
He will probably nicker again about the workload tomorrow. = 他明天可能会再次抱怨工作量。


虚拟语气:
If the horse nickered, I would know it was nearby. = 如果马嘶鸣了，我就知道它在附近。
If she had nickered sooner, perhaps the problem could have been avoided. = 如果她早点抱怨，也许问题就可以避免了。


被动语态:
The nicker was barely audible above the wind. =  嘶鸣声几乎听不见，风声太大。
His complaints were nickered away and ignored by his boss. （This is a figurative use, not a true passive of "nicker")= 他的抱怨被老板敷衍了事并忽略了。


疑问句:
Did you hear the horse nicker? = 你听到马嘶鸣了吗？
Does he often nicker about trivial matters? = 他经常为琐事抱怨吗？


否定句:
The horse didn't nicker this morning. = 这匹马今天早上没有嘶鸣。
He didn't nicker about the issue once the solution was found. = 一旦找到解决方案，他就再也没有抱怨过这个问题。


PHRASE->
a soft nicker = 轻柔的嘶鸣
a nervous nicker = 紧张的嘶鸣
to nicker with discontent = 不满地低声抱怨
to nicker at someone = 向某人抱怨
to nicker and whinny = 嘶鸣和打响鼻
