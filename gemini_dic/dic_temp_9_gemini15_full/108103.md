lathing:/ˈleɪðɪŋ/| n.|木条条板，细木条；抹灰用的条状物|Thin strips of wood, metal, or other material used as a base for plaster or other finishes.  It can also refer to the process of applying these strips.|例句：The walls were prepared with lathing before the plaster was applied.  墙上先铺设了木条条板，然后再抹灰。

The ceiling needs re-lathing before we can paint it. 天花板需要重新铺设木条条板才能粉刷。|近义词：furring, sheathing|反义词：N/A
