manhunts:/ˈmænˌhʌnts/| n. |大规模搜捕；追捕；追猎 |The organized pursuit and capture of a person or group of people, typically a criminal or fugitive.  A manhunt often involves significant resources and personnel. |例句：The police launched a massive manhunt after the bank robbery.  警方在银行抢劫案发生后发起了大规模搜捕。

例句：The manhunt for the escaped prisoner lasted for several days. 对逃犯的追捕持续了好几天。


近义词：search, pursuit, hunt, chase, dragnet

反义词：release, freedom


词性：名词
