rechartering:/ˌriːˈtʃɑːrtərɪŋ/ | v. | 重新租赁；重新特许；重新授权 | The act of granting a new charter or extending an existing one.  This often applies to organizations, businesses, or vessels that need to renew their legal authorization or lease. | Example: The company is undergoing a rechartering process to expand its operations. 公司正在进行重新租赁流程以扩大其业务。  The ship's rechartering was delayed due to unforeseen circumstances.  由于不可预见的情况，船舶的重新租赁被推迟了。 | 近义词：renewal, extension, reauthorization | 反义词：cancellation, revocation | 动词
