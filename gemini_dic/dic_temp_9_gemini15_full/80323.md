goldener:/ˈɡoʊldənər/ | adj. | 金色的，黄金的 | Golden refers to something that is the color of gold, or having the qualities associated with gold, such as value, beauty, or excellence. It can also be used figuratively to describe something as perfect or ideal. |例句：The setting sun painted the sky in golden hues.  夕阳给天空涂上了金色的色彩。


The leaves turned golden in the autumn. 秋天树叶变成了金黄色。

近义词：golden-colored, gilded, yellow

反义词：dark, dull, silver


词源：源自古英语 *gold,  与古高地德语 *gult, 古弗里西亚语 *golt, 古撒克逊语 *gold,  古诺尔斯语 *gull,  古荷兰语 *golt 同源，最终源自原始日耳曼语 *gulþą
