haemolytic:/ˌhiːməˈlɪtɪk/| adj.|溶血的|Causing the destruction of red blood cells.  This adjective describes substances or processes that break down red blood cells, releasing hemoglobin into the surrounding medium.| Example:  A haemolytic disease can lead to anaemia.  溶血性疾病可导致贫血。  The snake venom had haemolytic properties. 蛇毒具有溶血特性。|近义词：blood-destroying, erythrolytic|反义词：non-haemolytic
