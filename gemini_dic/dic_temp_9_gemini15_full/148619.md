/ˌoʊvərˌθiːəriˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |过度理论化；过度解释 | The act of excessively theorizing or interpreting something, often to the point of being unrealistic or impractical.  It involves creating overly complex or elaborate explanations that go beyond the available evidence. |  His overtheorizations about the alien invasion were completely unfounded.  他的关于外星人入侵的过度理论化完全没有根据。|  Overinterpretation, overanalysis |  Simplicity, pragmatism | 名词
