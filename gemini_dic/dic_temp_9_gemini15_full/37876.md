complacency:/kəmˈpleɪsənsi/| n. |自满；自鸣得意；满足 | A feeling of smug or uncritical satisfaction with oneself or one's achievements.  It often implies a lack of awareness of potential dangers or problems.  |例句：His complacency about his performance led to his downfall.  他的自满导致了他的失败。

例句：The team's complacency after their early success proved to be their undoing.  球队早期的成功让他们沾沾自喜，最终导致了他们的失败。


近义词：self-satisfaction, smugness, self-contentment, complacency

反义词：anxiety, concern, humility, vigilance
