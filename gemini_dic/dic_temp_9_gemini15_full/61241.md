emanated:/ˈɛməneɪtɪd/| v.|散发，发出，起源于|To come out from a source; to originate.|例句：A sweet scent emanated from the bakery.  一股甜味从面包店飘散出来。|例句：The feeling of peace emanated from her.  平静的感觉从她身上散发出来。|近义词：originate, proceed, issue, radiate|反义词：absorb, cease


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The aroma emanates from the kitchen. = 香味从厨房散发出来。
Happiness emanates from her. = 幸福感从她身上散发出来。


一般过去时:
A strange smell emanated from the drain. = 一种奇怪的气味从排水沟散发出来。
Confidence emanated from his speech. = 自信从他的演讲中流露出来。


现在完成时:
A sense of unease has emanated from the public. = 一种不安的情绪已经从公众中蔓延开来。
Great works of art have emanated from this city. = 这座城市已经创作出许多伟大的艺术作品。


过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, a strong odor had emanated from the room. = 当我们到达时，房间里已经散发出一股浓烈的异味。
Before the announcement, fear had emanated throughout the company. = 在公告发布之前，恐惧已经蔓延至整个公司。


将来时:
A new wave of creativity will emanate from this generation. = 一股新的创造力浪潮将从这一代人中涌现。
The results of the research will soon emanate from the lab. = 研究成果将很快从实验室发布。


虚拟语气:
If only peace would emanate from the conflict. = 但愿冲突能够带来和平。
I wish that more positivity would emanate from the media. = 我希望媒体能多传递一些积极的信息。


被动语态:
The news was emanated by the government. = 消息是由政府发布的。(注意：emanate 通常不用于被动语态，此句略显不自然，更佳表达为The government emanated the news.)
The decision was announced, which emanated from the board of directors. = 决定已经宣布，该决定来自董事会。


疑问句:
Where does this strange noise emanate? = 这种奇怪的声音是从哪里发出的？
From where does the power emanate? = 权力从何而来？


否定句:
No light emanated from the window. = 窗户里没有光线透出来。
No warmth emanated from the fireplace. = 壁炉里没有散发温暖。


PHRASE->
