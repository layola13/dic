exclusives:/ɪkˈskluːsɪvz/ | n. | 独家新闻；独家报道；独家销售商品 | Items or information available only to a select group or through a specific source.  Exclusives are often associated with high-end products or privileged access to information. | Example: The magazine published several exclusives on the royal wedding. 这本杂志刊登了几篇关于皇室婚礼的独家新闻。 |近义词：scoops, specials, sole rights | 反义词：public information, common knowledge

This store offers exclusive designer clothing. = 这家商店提供独家设计师服装。


一般过去时:

虚拟语气:
