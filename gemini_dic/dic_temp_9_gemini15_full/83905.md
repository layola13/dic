half-barrel:/ˈhɑːfˌbærəl/| n. |半桶；半桶酒 |A half-barrel is a container that holds half the amount of a standard barrel.  It's often used in reference to alcoholic beverages, especially beer. |例句：The brewery delivered a half-barrel of their newest IPA.  这家酿酒厂送来半桶他们最新的印度淡啤酒。|例句：We need to order a half-barrel of whiskey for the party. 我们需要为派对订购半桶威士忌。|近义词：half- keg |反义词：full barrel
