extensive:/ɪkˈstɛnsɪv/ | adj. | 广泛的；大量的；广阔的 | covering a large area or range; having a wide scope or effect.  |例句：The museum boasts an extensive collection of ancient artifacts.  这个博物馆拥有大量的古代文物收藏。

例句：His knowledge of the subject is extensive. 他对这个主题的知识很广泛。


近义词：comprehensive, thorough, widespread, broad, far-reaching | 反义词：limited, restricted, narrow, confined, small


词源:  From Latin *extensus, past participle of extendere "to stretch out," from ex- "out" + tendere "to stretch."
