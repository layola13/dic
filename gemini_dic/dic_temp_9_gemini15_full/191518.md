schmoozer:/ʃmuːzər/| n. |爱逢迎的人，善于交际的人|A schmoozer is a person who is skilled at making contacts and cultivating relationships, often for personal gain.  They are typically charming and persuasive, using their social skills to achieve their goals.  Schmoozers are often seen as smooth talkers and networkers.|例句：He's a natural schmoozer; he can talk to anyone and make them feel comfortable. 他是个天生的爱逢迎的人；他能和任何人交谈，并让他们感到舒适。

He's a great schmoozer, but I'm not sure how much of it is genuine. 他很会逢迎，但我不知道有多少是真心的。|近义词：networker, glad-hander, smooth talker, wheeler-dealer |反义词：loner, recluse, introvert
