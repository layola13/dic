feministic: /ˌfɛmɪˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 女权主义的；女性主义的 | Relating to or supporting feminism. 例句：The novel explores complex feministic themes, challenging traditional gender roles. 这部小说探讨了复杂的女权主义主题，挑战了传统的性别角色。
