parapsychical:/ˌpærəˈsaɪkɪkəl/| adj.|超心理的，心灵的| relating to or denoting phenomena that appear to involve an interaction between mind and matter other than through the known physical senses.  Parapsychology studies these phenomena. |例句：The parapsychical experiments yielded inconclusive results.  超心理实验结果并不确定。

例句：He claimed to have parapsychical abilities, such as telepathy. 他声称拥有超心理能力，例如心灵感应。


近义词：psychic, paranormal, supernatural

反义词：natural, normal, physical


词源：para- (beyond, beside) + psychical (relating to the mind or soul)  源自希腊语para- (在…旁边，超过) 和psyche (灵魂，心灵).  "Psychical" itself is derived from the Greek word "psyche," meaning "soul" or "mind."
