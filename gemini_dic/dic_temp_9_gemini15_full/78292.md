germinates:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪts/ | v. |发芽；开始发展 | To begin to grow or develop.  |The seeds germinate in the spring. 春天种子发芽了。|The idea germinated slowly in his mind. 这个想法在他的脑海中慢慢地萌生。| sprout, bud, develop | decay, wither |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The seed germinates in spring. = 种子在春天发芽。
The plant germinates quickly in warm weather. = 植物在温暖的天气里迅速发芽。


一般过去时:
The seeds germinated after the rain. = 雨后种子发芽了。
The idea germinated in his mind during the meeting. = 这个主意在他开会的时候萌生了。


现在完成时:
The seeds have germinated and are showing small shoots. = 种子已经发芽，并长出小芽了。
The project has germinated from a simple idea. = 这个项目是由一个简单的想法发展而来的。


过去完成时:
The seeds had already germinated before the frost arrived. = 霜冻来临之前，种子已经发芽了。
The plan had germinated long before he spoke it out loud. = 这个计划在他大声说出来之前就已经酝酿很久了。


将来时:
The seeds will germinate once the temperature rises. = 温度升高后，种子就会发芽。
The business idea will germinate into a full-fledged company. = 这个商业创意将发展成为一家成熟的公司。


虚拟语气:
If the conditions were right, the seeds would germinate. = 如果条件合适，种子就会发芽。
If I had known earlier, the idea would have germinated sooner. = 如果我早点知道，这个想法就会早点萌生。


被动语态:
The seeds were germinated in a controlled environment. = 种子在受控环境下发芽。
The plan was germinated by the team leader. = 这个计划是由团队领导人提出的。


疑问句:
Do the seeds germinate easily? = 种子容易发芽吗？
Has the project fully germinated yet? = 这个项目已经完全启动了吗？


否定句:
The seeds did not germinate because of the drought. = 由于干旱，种子没有发芽。
The plan did not germinate because of lack of funding. = 由于缺乏资金，计划未能启动。
