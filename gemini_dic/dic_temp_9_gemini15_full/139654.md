occupancy:/ˈɑːkjuːpənsi/ /ˈɒkjuːpənsi/| n. |占用率；居住率；占用；占有 |The state of being occupied; the number of people or things occupying a place.  It can refer to the proportion of available space that is being used, or the number of people staying in a hotel or other accommodation. |例句：The hotel's occupancy rate is high during the summer months. 夏季酒店的入住率很高。

The occupancy of the building is limited to 50 people. 这栋建筑物的容纳人数限制为50人。


近义词：occupation, filling, usage, tenancy| 反义词：vacancy, emptiness |词性:名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The hotel's occupancy rate is high. = 酒店的入住率很高。
The office occupancy is currently at 90%. = 办公室的占用率目前为90%。


一般过去时:
Hotel occupancy was low last year. = 去年酒店入住率很低。
The building's occupancy decreased after the fire. = 火灾后建筑物的占用率下降了。


现在完成时:

The building has seen high occupancy since its renovation. = 自翻新以来，这栋建筑的入住率一直很高。


过去完成时:
Occupancy had already reached its peak before the conference began. = 会议开始前入住率就已经达到高峰。

虚拟语气:
If the occupancy rate were higher, we could afford to hire more staff. = 如果入住率更高，我们就能负担得起雇佣更多员工。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
