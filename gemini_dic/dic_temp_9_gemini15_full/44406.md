cross-legged:/ˈkrɒsˌlɛɡɪd/|/ˈkrɔːsˌlɛɡɪd/| adj.|盘腿的，交叠双腿的|Having legs crossed at the ankles or knees, typically while sitting.|She sat cross-legged on the floor.|近义词：legs crossed, with legs crossed|反义词：legs apart, legs straight


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sits cross-legged during meditation. = 他打坐时盘着腿。
They are cross-legged on the rug. = 他们盘腿坐在地毯上。

一般过去时:
She sat cross-legged throughout the long ceremony. = 在漫长的仪式中，她一直盘腿坐着。
The children sat cross-legged, listening to the story. = 孩子们盘腿坐着，听着故事。


现在完成时:
The yogi has sat cross-legged for hours. = 瑜伽士已经盘腿坐了几个小时了。
I have been cross-legged for so long, my legs are numb. = 我盘腿坐了这么久，腿都麻了。


过去完成时:
He had sat cross-legged for so long that his legs fell asleep. = 他盘腿坐了太久，以至于腿都睡着了。
They had been cross-legged before the teacher arrived. = 在老师到来之前，他们就已经盘腿坐着了。


将来时:
She will sit cross-legged during the concert. = 她将在音乐会期间盘腿坐着。
They will probably sit cross-legged on the floor. = 他们可能会盘腿坐在地板上。


虚拟语气:
If I were more flexible, I could sit cross-legged easily. = 如果我更灵活，就能轻松地盘腿坐。
I wish I could sit cross-legged comfortably. = 我希望我能舒服地盘腿坐着。


被动语态:
The position is often described as sitting cross-legged. = 这个姿势通常被描述为盘腿而坐。
(This adjective is rarely used in passive voice.)


疑问句:
Are you sitting cross-legged? = 你是盘腿坐的吗？
Was he sitting cross-legged when you saw him? = 你看到他时，他是盘腿坐的吗？


否定句:
She wasn't sitting cross-legged; she was sitting sideways. = 她不是盘腿坐的；她是侧着坐的。
They did not sit cross-legged; they sat in chairs. = 他们没有盘腿坐；他们坐在椅子上。
