sauba:/ˈsaʊbə/| n. |萨乌巴蚂蚁|Leafcutter ant of the genus *Atta* and *Acromyrmex*, known for their large size and agricultural activities. These ants cultivate fungi gardens within their nests, feeding on the fungus and using leaves as substrate.  They are found in Central and South America. |例句：The sauba ants built a massive nest under the tree. 萨乌巴蚂蚁在树下建了一个巨大的巢穴。|近义词：leafcutter ant, fungus-growing ant |反义词: N/A
