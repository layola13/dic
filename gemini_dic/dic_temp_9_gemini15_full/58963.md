duststorm:/ˈdʌstˌstɔːrm/| n. |沙尘暴 |A dust storm is a meteorological phenomenon common in arid and semi-arid regions.  It is a strong wind that carries large amounts of sand and dust, reducing visibility significantly and potentially causing damage. Dust storms are often associated with dry air masses and strong pressure gradients. |例句：The dust storm reduced visibility to near zero.  沙尘暴导致能见度几乎为零。

The dust storm caused significant damage to crops. 沙尘暴对农作物造成了重大损失。|近义词：sandstorm, haboob |反义词：calm, clear weather |名词
