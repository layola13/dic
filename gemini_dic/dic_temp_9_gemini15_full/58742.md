dungaree:/dʌŋɡəˈriː/| n. |粗棉布工作服；牛仔裤|A heavy, durable cotton twill fabric, typically blue, used for work clothes, especially overalls and jeans. The term is also often used to refer to the garments themselves, particularly jeans.|例句：He wore dungarees to work. 他上班穿牛仔裤。|近义词：jeans, overalls, denim|反义词:  (无明确反义词, 可考虑与正式服装相对比)|名词

词源:
The word "dungaree" originates from the Hindi word "dungri," referring to a coarse cotton fabric.  This fabric was originally produced in the Indian city of Dungri.  The word then entered English, initially referring to the fabric and later to the clothing made from it.
