peptidyl: /ˈpɛptɪdɪl/ | adj. | 肽基的 | Relating to or denoting a peptidyl group.  A peptidyl group is a group of atoms consisting of a peptide bond and the attached side chains.  It is the portion of a peptide chain that remains after the removal of a terminal amino acid. | Example: The peptidyl transferase center is crucial for peptide bond formation during protein synthesis.  肽基转移酶中心对于蛋白质合成过程中的肽键形成至关重要。|近义词：peptide-containing  |反义词：无
