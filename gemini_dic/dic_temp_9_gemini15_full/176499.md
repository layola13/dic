re-pressed:/ˌriːˈprɛst/| v. |再压制，重新压制；抑制（感情等）|To press again; to suppress (feelings, etc.).  The record label re-pressed the popular album due to high demand.  唱片公司由于需求量大而重新压制了这张流行专辑。|近义词：reissue, repress, suppress|反义词：release, express


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They re-press the vinyl records every month. = 他们每月都重新压制黑胶唱片。
She re-presses her emotions to maintain a professional demeanor. = 她抑制住自己的情绪以保持专业的举止。


一般过去时:
The company re-pressed the book after it sold out. = 这本书售罄后，公司又重新印刷了。
He re-pressed the urge to shout at his boss. = 他压制住了对老板大喊大叫的冲动。


现在完成时:
They have re-pressed the album three times this year. = 今年他们已经三次重新压制了这张专辑。
She has re-pressed her feelings for years, leading to emotional burnout. = 她多年来一直压抑着自己的感情，导致了情感倦怠。


过去完成时:
They had re-pressed the CD before the concert began. = 他们在音乐会开始之前就已经重新压制了CD。
He had re-pressed his anger for so long that it finally exploded. = 他压抑了这么久的怒火最终爆发了。


将来时:
The band will re-press their debut album next year. = 乐队明年将重新发行他们的首张专辑。
She will re-press her grief and continue with her life. = 她将压制她的悲伤，继续她的生活。


虚拟语气:
If they re-pressed the book, more people could read it. = 如果他们重新印刷这本书，更多人就能读到它。
If she hadn't re-pressed her feelings, she might have been happier. = 如果她没有压抑自己的感情，她可能会更快乐。


被动语态:
The album was re-pressed last month. = 这张专辑上个月重新压制了。
His emotions were re-pressed for so long that it became unhealthy. = 他的情绪被压抑了这么久，以至于变得不健康。


疑问句:
Will the record label re-press the album? = 唱片公司会重新压制这张专辑吗？
Was his anger re-pressed successfully? = 他的怒火成功压制了吗？


否定句:
They did not re-press the vinyl because of low sales. = 由于销量低迷，他们没有重新压制黑胶唱片。
She didn't re-press her emotions; she let them out. = 她没有压抑自己的情绪；她把它们发泄出来了。
