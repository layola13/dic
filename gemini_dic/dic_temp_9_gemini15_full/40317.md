contorts:/kənˈtɔːrts/ | vt. | 扭曲；使变形；曲解 | To twist or bend out of shape; to distort in meaning. |例句：The dancer contorted her body into a graceful pose. 舞蹈演员把身体扭曲成一个优美的姿势。

例句：He contorted the facts to suit his argument. 他歪曲事实以符合他的论点。


近义词：twist, distort, warp, wrench | 反义词：straighten, unbend, rectify


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He contorts his face in pain. = 他痛苦地扭曲着脸。
The artist contorts the figures in his paintings. = 艺术家在他的画作中扭曲了人物形象。


一般过去时:
She contorted her body to fit through the small opening. = 她扭曲身体以穿过狭小的开口。
The metal contorted under the intense heat. = 金属在高温下变形了。


现在完成时:
The wind has contorted the ancient trees. = 风已经扭曲了古老的树木。
He has contorted the meaning of the poem. = 他曲解了这首诗的意思。


过去完成时:
The accident had contorted the car beyond recognition. = 事故已使汽车变形得面目全非。
Before the earthquake, the ground had contorted slightly. = 地震前，地面略微变形了。


将来时:
The heat will contort the plastic. = 热量会使塑料变形。
The pressure will contort the pipes. = 压力会使管道变形。


虚拟语气:
If he tried to contort the truth, he would be caught. = 如果他试图歪曲事实，他会被抓住。
I wish he hadn't contorted my words. = 我真希望他没有曲解我的话。


被动语态:
His face was contorted by anger. = 他的脸因愤怒而扭曲。
The evidence was contorted to support a false narrative. = 证据被歪曲以支持虚假叙述。


疑问句:
Is his face contorted in pain? = 他的脸是不是因为疼痛而扭曲？
Were the facts contorted in the report? = 报告中是否歪曲了事实？


否定句:
He did not contort his features. = 他没有扭曲他的面部表情。
She did not contort the facts in her testimony. = 她没有在她的证词中歪曲事实。


PHRASE->
contort with pain = 痛苦地扭曲

contort oneself = 扭动身体
severely contorted = 剧烈扭曲


词源:
源于拉丁语 *con- (加强前缀) + torquēre (to twist).  字面意思是“扭转在一起”。
