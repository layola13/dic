It often refers to the selective attraction or affinity of cells, tissues, or organs for specific substances or environments. For example, certain cancer cells exhibit organotropism, showing a preference to metastasize to specific organs. |  The study revealed a surprising organophyly of the parasite, showing a preference for the liver over other organs.  这项研究揭示了这种寄生虫令人惊讶的器官嗜性，它更喜欢肝脏而不是其他器官。| 近义词：organotropism, tissue tropism | 反义词：organ aversion (this is not a common term)
