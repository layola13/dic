guesstimator:/ˈɡɛstɪmeɪtər/ | n. | 估计者；估算者 | Someone who makes estimations or educated guesses, especially in a quick or informal way.  A guesstimator combines elements of "guess" and "estimator." They don't aim for precise calculations but rather for a reasonable approximation. |  He's a good guesstimator; his estimates are usually within a 10% margin of error. 他是个很好的估算者；他的估计通常在误差范围的10%以内。 | estimator, approximator |  precision calculator, exact measurer |  n.
