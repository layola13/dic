retransplantations:/ˌriːtrænzˌplænˈteɪʃənz/| n. |再移植；再种植|The act of transplanting something, such as an organ or plant, a second time.  It often implies a previous transplantation that may have failed or required further intervention.|例句：The patient underwent multiple retransplantations due to organ rejection.  病人由于器官排斥反应经历了多次再移植。

The success rate of retransplantations is often lower than the initial transplantation. 再移植的成功率通常低于首次移植。|近义词：reimplantations, replantings|反义词：removal, excision
