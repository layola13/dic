/ˈsɜːrpənt/ | n. | 蛇 | A snake, especially a large or dangerous one. Serpents are often depicted in mythology and folklore.  | 例句：The serpent slithered through the tall grass.  这条蛇在高高的草丛中蜿蜒前行。 | 例句：Ancient myths often feature serpents as symbols of wisdom or evil.  古代神话中经常出现蛇作为智慧或邪恶的象征。 | 近义词：snake, viper, adder | 反义词：None specifically, but words like "bird" or "mammal" could be considered contrasting.
