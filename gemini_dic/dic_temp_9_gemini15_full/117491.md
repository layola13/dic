medulla:/mɛˈdʌlə/| n. |髓质；骨髓；延髓 | The medulla is the inner part of an organ or structure, especially the inner part of the kidney or the brainstem.  It refers specifically to the innermost part of the adrenal gland, the kidney, or the brainstem. In the context of the brainstem, the medulla oblongata controls vital functions like breathing, heart rate, and blood pressure. |例句：The medulla oblongata controls vital functions such as breathing and heart rate.  延髓控制着诸如呼吸和心跳等重要功能。

The adrenal medulla secretes adrenaline.  肾上腺髓质分泌肾上腺素。


近义词：core, pith, marrow | 反义词：cortex
