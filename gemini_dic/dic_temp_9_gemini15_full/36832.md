colonising: /ˈkɒlənaɪzɪŋ/ | v. |殖民；拓殖；占据| The act of establishing control over a territory and its people, often involving settling there and imposing one's culture and system of government.  It implies a power imbalance and often exploitation. |例句：European powers were colonising Africa in the 19th century.  欧洲列强在19世纪殖民了非洲。

例句：The company is colonising the market with its new product. 这家公司正在用它的新产品占领市场。


近义词：occupying, settling, appropriating, subduing

反义词：decolonising, liberating, emancipating


词性：动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are colonising new lands. = 他们在殖民新的土地。
We are colonising the market with our innovative products. = 我们正在用创新的产品占领市场。


一般过去时:
The British colonised India for many years. = 英国殖民印度多年。
Spain colonised much of South America. = 西班牙殖民了南美洲的大部分地区。


现在完成时:
They have colonised several islands in the Pacific. = 他们已经殖民了太平洋的几个岛屿。
Colonial powers have profoundly impacted the world. = 殖民列强深刻地影响了世界。


过去完成时:
The indigenous population had been colonised before the arrival of the Europeans. = 在欧洲人到来之前，当地居民就已经被殖民了。
They had already colonised the region before the war broke out. = 在战争爆发之前，他们已经殖民了该地区。


将来时:
They will colonise the moon in the future. = 他们将来会殖民月球。
Will they continue to colonise other countries? = 他们会继续殖民其他国家吗？


虚拟语气:
If they had not colonised the land, the people would still live freely. = 如果他们没有殖民这片土地，人民仍然会自由地生活。
I wish they hadn't colonised our homelands. = 我真希望他们没有殖民我们的家园。


被动语态:
The land was colonised by the invaders. = 这片土地被侵略者殖民了。
The resources were colonised by the foreign company. = 资源被外国公司占领了。


疑问句:
Were they colonising the area for its resources? = 他们殖民该地区是为了其资源吗？
Is this company colonising the market unfairly? = 这家公司是否在不正当地占领市场？


否定句:
They did not colonise the entire continent. = 他们没有殖民整个大陆。
The government will not allow them to colonise the island. = 政府不会允许他们殖民该岛屿。
