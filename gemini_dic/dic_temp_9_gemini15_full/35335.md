co-assume:/koʊ əˈsuːm/| v.|共同承担，共同假设|To assume or accept something jointly or together.  It implies a shared responsibility or belief.|例句：The partners co-assumed the risk of the new venture. 合作伙伴共同承担了这项新风险。|例句：Let's co-assume that the data is accurate for now. 让我们暂时共同假设数据是准确的。|近义词：jointly assume, mutually assume, share|反义词：deny, reject, disavow


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They co-assume the financial burden. = 他们共同承担经济负担。
We co-assume responsibility for the project. = 我们共同承担这个项目的责任。


一般过去时:
The team co-assumed the blame for the failure. = 团队共同承担了失败的责任。
They co-assumed the project's risks last year. = 他们去年共同承担了该项目的风险。


现在完成时:
We have co-assumed the debt since the merger. = 自合并以来，我们共同承担了债务。
They have co-assumed multiple roles in the organization. = 他们在组织中共同承担了多个角色。


过去完成时:
Before the trial, they had co-assumed the legal fees. = 在审判之前，他们已经共同承担了律师费。
By the deadline, they had co-assumed all project duties. = 到截止日期，他们已经共同承担了所有项目职责。


将来时:
We will co-assume the costs of the renovation. = 我们将共同承担装修费用。
They will co-assume leadership of the new department. = 他们将共同领导新的部门。


虚拟语气:
If they co-assumed the liability, the risk would be reduced. = 如果他们共同承担责任，风险就会降低。
I wish we had co-assumed the workload earlier. = 我希望我们早点共同承担工作量。


被动语态:
The responsibilities were co-assumed by the team members. = 责任由团队成员共同承担。
The risks are being co-assumed by both parties. = 风险正由双方共同承担。


疑问句:

Did they co-assume the responsibility? = 他们共同承担责任了吗？


否定句:
They did not co-assume the responsibility for the mistake. = 他们没有共同承担错误的责任。
We will not co-assume any further risks. = 我们不会再承担任何风险。
