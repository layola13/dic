nonfiction:/ˈnɑːnˈfɪkʃən/ | n. |非虚构文学；纪实文学 |  Writing that is based on facts, real events, and real people, as opposed to fiction.  It encompasses a wide range of genres, including biographies, memoirs, essays, historical accounts, and journalism. | Example:  The author's new nonfiction book details the history of the American Revolution. 这作者的新纪实文学书籍详细记述了美国革命的历史。

|近义词:factual writing, reality literature, true stories |反义词:fiction, fantasy, imaginative literature |词性:名词
