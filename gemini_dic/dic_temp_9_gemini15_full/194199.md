self-laudatory: /ˌsɛlfˈlɔːdəˌtɔːri/ | adj. | 自吹自擂的；自我赞扬的 |  Characterized by or given to self-praise; boastful.  |例句：His self-laudatory speech bored the audience.  他的自吹自擂的演讲让听众感到厌烦。

例句：The article was filled with self-laudatory statements about the author's achievements. 这篇文章充满了作者对自己成就的自吹自擂的陈述。


近义词：boastful, vainglorious, self-congratulatory, self-aggrandizing

反义词：self-deprecating, modest, humble


词源:

* **self-**:  Prefix meaning "of, by, or for oneself." (源自古英语 *self, *sylf)
* **laudatory**: Adjective meaning "expressing praise." (源自拉丁语 *laudatorius,  from *laudare "to praise")
