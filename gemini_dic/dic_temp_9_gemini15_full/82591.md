guardianage:/ˈɡɑːrdɪənɪdʒ/ | n. |监护期，监护权 |The period of time during which a guardian has legal responsibility for a person or their property.  It refers to the state or condition of being under guardianship. |Example:  The court extended her guardianage until she reached the age of 18. 法院将她的监护期延长到她18岁。|近义词：wardship, tutelage, custody |反义词：independence, self-reliance
