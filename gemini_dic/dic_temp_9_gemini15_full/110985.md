liquidity:/lɪˈkwɪdəti/| n. |流动性，变现能力|The ability to convert an asset into cash quickly without significant loss of value.  This is crucial for businesses and investors to meet short-term obligations and take advantage of opportunities.|例句：The company's high liquidity allowed it to weather the economic downturn. 这家公司的高流动性使其能够度过经济衰退。|例句：Investors are concerned about the liquidity of the market in the current situation. 投资者担心目前市场上的流动性。|近义词：solvency, fluidity, marketability|反义词：illiquidity, insolvency
