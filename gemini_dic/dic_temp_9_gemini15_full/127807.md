neither:/ˈnaɪðər/| pron. conj.|既不…也不…；两者都不|Neither of them is right.  他们俩都不对。|近义词：none, nor|反义词：both


USAGE->

一般现在时:
Neither of us likes spicy food. = 我们俩都不喜欢辣的食物。
Neither he nor she smokes. = 他和她都不抽烟。

一般过去时:
Neither of them came to the party. = 他们俩都没来参加聚会。
Neither my brother nor I went to school yesterday. = 我和我哥哥昨天都没去上学。

现在完成时:
Neither of them has finished their work. = 他们俩都没完成工作。
Neither you nor I have seen that movie. = 你和我都没看过那部电影。

过去完成时:
Neither of them had eaten before the meeting. = 他们俩在会议前都没吃东西。
Neither she nor he had heard the news. = 她和他都没听到这个消息。

将来时:
Neither of us will be there tomorrow. = 我们俩明天都不会在那儿。
Neither my friend nor I will attend the conference. = 我和我的朋友都不会参加会议。


虚拟语气:
Neither he nor she would have done that. = 他和她都不会那样做。
I wish neither my brother nor I were there. = 我希望我和我哥哥都不在那里。


被动语态:
Neither of the proposals was accepted. = 两个提案都没被接受。
Neither of the projects has been completed. = 两个项目都没完成。


疑问句:
Neither of you has a car, is that right? = 你们俩都没车，对吧？
Neither of them knows the answer, doesn't he? = 他们俩都不知道答案，对吗？


否定句:
It's not right that neither of you tried. = 你们俩都没尝试是不对的。
I don't believe that neither of them is capable. = 我不相信他们俩都没能力。


PHRASE->

neither...nor...:既不...也不...
neither here nor there:无关紧要
neither fish nor fowl:不伦不类
neither one nor the other:两者都不
neither more nor less:不多不少
