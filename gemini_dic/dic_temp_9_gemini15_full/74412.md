franchisable: /ˈfrænˌtʃaɪzəbəl/ | adj. | 可特许经营的 | Capable of being franchised; suitable for franchising.  This refers to a business, system, or product that can be successfully replicated and operated under a franchise agreement.  | 例句：The company's innovative business model is highly franchisable. 这家公司的创新商业模式极具特许经营潜力。  | 例句：This restaurant concept is franchisable, with proven success in multiple locations. 这个餐厅概念具有特许经营潜力，并在多个地方取得了成功。 | 近义词：licensable, transferable | 反义词：unsuitable for franchising, non-transferable
