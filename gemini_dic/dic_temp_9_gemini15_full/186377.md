rhythming:/ˈrɪðmɪŋ/ | v. |押韵；使押韵 | The act of making words or lines of verse rhyme.  It involves selecting words with similar-sounding endings to create a musical effect in poetry or song lyrics. | Example:  He spent hours rhythming the poem, trying to perfect the sound. 他花了几个小时为这首诗押韵，试图使声音完美。 |近义词: rhyming, riming | 反义词:unrhymed
