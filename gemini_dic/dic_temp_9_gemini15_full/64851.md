esplanades:/ˌɛspəˈneɪdz/| n. |海滨长廊；散步大道|A long, open, level area, especially one by the sea, used for walking or recreation.  It often features a promenade or walkway.|例句：The city boasts a beautiful esplanade overlooking the ocean. 这座城市拥有一个美丽的俯瞰大海的海滨长廊。|例句：We took a leisurely stroll along the esplanade in the evening. 我们晚上在海滨长廊上悠闲地散步。|近义词：promenade, walkway, boulevard, parade|反义词：None specifically, but words suggesting enclosed or confined spaces could be considered opposites.|名词
