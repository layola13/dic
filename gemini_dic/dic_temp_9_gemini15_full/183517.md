repunishment: /ˌriːˈpʌnɪʃmənt/ | n. | 再惩罚，二次处罚 | The act of punishing someone again for the same offense or a similar one.  It implies a previous punishment has already been given.  | 例句：The repunishment was deemed excessive and unjust. =  再次惩罚被认为是过分和不公正的。  The repunishment for his repeated tardiness was suspension. = 他因屡次迟到而受到停学的二次处罚。 | 近义词：reprimand, second punishment, repeated punishment | 反义词：reward, pardon, acquittal |  词性：名词
