paleface:/ˈpeɪlfeɪs/| n.|苍白脸；（尤指对美洲原住民的）白人|A derogatory term for a white person, especially by Native Americans.  It emphasizes the contrast in skin color between white people and people of color.|例句：The old stories spoke of the palefaces coming to their land.  老故事讲述了白人来到他们土地上的事情。|近义词：white person, Caucasian (less offensive)|反义词：Native American, Indigenous person
