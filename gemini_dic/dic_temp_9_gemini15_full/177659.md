recaged:/riˈkeɪdʒd/| v. |重新关进笼子；再次囚禁 | To put (someone or something) back into a cage.  This implies a prior confinement.  It suggests a return to a state of confinement or restriction.|例句：The escaped bird was recaged.  这只逃脱的鸟被重新关进了笼子。

例句：The escaped prisoner was recaged after a week on the run.  逃犯在逃亡一周后被重新关押。


近义词：reconfine, imprison again, cage again

反义词：release, liberate, free


词性:动词
