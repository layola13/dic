overproved:/ˌoʊvərˈpruːvd/| adj.| 过度证明的；证明过度了的 |  Describes something that has been proven to a greater extent than necessary or required.  It implies an excess of evidence or argumentation.  |Example:  His guilt was overproved; the jury had more than enough evidence to convict him.  他的罪行证据确凿；陪审团有足够的证据定罪。 |近义词: excessively proven, superfluously demonstrated |反义词: underproved, insufficiently proven
