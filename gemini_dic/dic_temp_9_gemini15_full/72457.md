flummery:/ˈflʌməri/| n. |糊涂话；废话；虚饰；甜点|Nonsense; insincere flattery; a sweet dessert made from flour, milk, and eggs.|The politician's speech was pure flummery, full of empty promises.  这位政治家的演讲全是废话，充满了空洞的承诺。

|His compliments were just flummery; he didn't really mean them.他的赞美只是虚情假意；他并非真心实意。

|近义词：nonsense, humbug, flattery, bosh|反义词：truth, sincerity, reality|
