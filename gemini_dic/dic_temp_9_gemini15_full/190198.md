sap:/sæp/| n. v.|树液；活力；智慧；损耗；使…精疲力竭| the liquid that flows through plants;  vitality; wisdom;  to weaken or exhaust; to undermine gradually.|The maple tree sap is used to make syrup. |枫树汁用来制作糖浆。|juice, fluid| exhaustion, depletion| n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The sap flows in the spring. = 春天树液流动。
This work saps my energy. = 这项工作耗尽我的精力。


一般过去时:
The tree sap dried up in the summer. = 夏天树液干涸了。
The long journey sapped their strength. = 漫长的旅程耗尽了他们的体力。


现在完成时:
The drought has sapped the land of its moisture. = 干旱已经耗尽了土地的水分。
Years of hard work have sapped his vitality. = 多年的辛勤工作已经耗尽了他的活力。


过去完成时:
The plants had been sapped of their nutrients by the pests. = 植物的养分已被害虫耗尽。
They had been sapped of their morale by the continuous defeats. = 连续的失败已经打击了他们的士气。


将来时:
