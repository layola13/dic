respected:/rɪˈspɛktɪd/ | adj. | 受尊敬的，受敬重的 |  The word "respected" means held in high regard; esteemed. It implies a deep sense of admiration and deference earned through actions, character, or achievements. |例句：He is a respected member of the community. 他是社区里一位受人尊敬的成员。

例句：The respected professor delivered a captivating lecture.  这位受人尊敬的教授做了一场引人入胜的演讲。


近义词：honored, esteemed, admired, revered, venerated | 反义词：disrespected, scorned, despised, contemned


词源:  The word "respected" is derived from the verb "respect," which ultimately comes from the Latin word "respectus," a past participle of "respectare," meaning "to look back at," "to look again," or "to consider."  Over time, the meaning evolved to encompass the idea of considering someone or something worthy of admiration and deference.

过去完成时:
