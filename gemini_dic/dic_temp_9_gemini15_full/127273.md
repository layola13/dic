nave:/neɪv/| n. |中殿，教堂正殿 |The nave is the central part of a church, extending from the entrance to the chancel.  The nave is typically the largest and most spacious part of the church, where the congregation gathers for worship. |例句：The soaring nave of the cathedral was breathtaking.  教堂高耸的中殿令人叹为观止。
The nave of the church was filled with worshippers. 教堂的中殿挤满了崇拜者。|近义词：aisle, body (of a church) |反义词：chancel, choir


Etymology:

The word "nave" comes from Old French "nave," meaning "ship," ultimately from Latin "navis," also meaning "ship."  The architectural term arose from a perceived resemblance between the long, central aisle of a church and the hull of a ship.
