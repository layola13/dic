innocuously:/ɪˈnɑːkjʊəsli/ | adj. |无害地；无恶意地；不起眼地 | harmlessly; in a way that does not cause harm or offense; seemingly insignificant.  | The snake slithered innocuously through the grass.  那条蛇无害地从草丛中滑过。He made an innocuous remark about the weather. 他随意地评论了一下天气。 | harmlessly, inoffensively, mildly | harmfully, offensively, significantly | 形容词

词源:
