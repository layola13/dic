nonintelligently:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəntli/ | adv. | 不明智地；愚蠢地 |  Acting or done in a way that shows a lack of intelligence or good judgment.  It implies a lack of thoughtfulness or consideration of consequences. | 例句：He nonintelligently spent all his savings on a useless gadget. 他愚蠢地把所有积蓄都花在一个无用的电子产品上。


He reacted nonintelligently to the crisis, making things worse. 他对危机反应迟钝，使情况更加恶化。| 近义词：foolishly, stupidly, unwisely, senselessly | 反义词：intelligently, wisely, cleverly, shrewdly
