lepidopterist:/ˌlɛpɪˈdɒptərɪst/| n. |鳞翅目昆虫学家；蝴蝶学家|A lepidopterist is a person who studies Lepidoptera, the order of insects that includes butterflies and moths.  They are often passionate about collecting, identifying, and researching these insects. |例句：My uncle is a renowned lepidopterist, his collection is breathtaking. 我的叔叔是一位著名的鳞翅目昆虫学家，他的收藏令人叹为观止。
例句：The lepidopterist spent years studying the migration patterns of monarch butterflies.  这位鳞翅目昆虫学家花了数年时间研究帝王蝶的迁徙模式。|近义词：entomologist (更广义的昆虫学家)|反义词：无
