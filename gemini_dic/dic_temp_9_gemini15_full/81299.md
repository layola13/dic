granulation:/ˌɡrænjʊˈleɪʃən/| n. |粒化；颗粒形成；结节形成 |The process of forming granules or grains.  It can refer to the formation of small particles, the appearance of a granular texture, or the development of small nodules or lumps.  |例句：The granulation of the sugar made it easier to dissolve.  糖的粒化使其更容易溶解。

The granulation of the wound tissue indicated healing. 伤口组织的颗粒形成表明正在愈合。|近义词：granularity,  cornification,  nodulation |反义词：homogeneity, smoothness
