meta-talk:/ˈmɛtɑːtɔːk/| n. |元谈话，元语言|Discussion about the process of communication itself, rather than the content of the communication.  It refers to communication about communication. For example, discussing the clarity of a message, the effectiveness of a communication strategy, or the appropriateness of language used in a particular context. |Example:  The team engaged in meta-talk to improve the clarity of their project reports.  团队进行了元谈话以提高项目报告的清晰度。  |近义词: communication about communication, discourse on discourse, reflection on communication |反义词:  object-level talk (referring to the content of communication)
