shoring:/ˈʃɔːrɪŋ/| n. v.|支撑；支撑物；护坡；加固|The act of supporting or strengthening something, typically a structure or excavation.  It can refer to the temporary supports used during construction or the permanent supports used to stabilize a structure. |例句：The shoring prevented the trench from collapsing. 支撑物防止了沟渠坍塌。

The builders used shoring to support the walls during construction. 建筑工人用支撑物在施工期间支撑墙壁。


近义词：support, bracing, underpinning, buttressing |反义词：destabilization, weakening


词性:名词，动词
