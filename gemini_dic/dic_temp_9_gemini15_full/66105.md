exceeds:/ɪkˈsiːdz/| vt.|超过，胜过，超出|To go beyond the limits of; surpass.  It implies exceeding a limit, standard, or expectation.|例句：His performance exceeds expectations.他的表现超出了预期。

例句：The cost exceeds the budget.  成本超过了预算。


近义词：surpasses, surpasses, outstrips, transcends, outdoes

反义词：falls short of, is less than


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The demand exceeds the supply. = 需求超过供应。
His achievements exceed those of his peers. = 他的成就超过了他的同龄人。


一般过去时:
The temperature exceeded 100 degrees. = 温度超过了100度。
The project's cost exceeded the initial estimate. = 项目的成本超过了最初的估计。


现在完成时:
The success of the project has exceeded all expectations. = 项目的成功超出了所有预期。
The book's popularity has exceeded even the author's wildest dreams. = 这本书的受欢迎程度甚至超出了作者最狂野的梦想。


过去完成时:
The damage had exceeded what we initially thought. = 损失超过了我们最初的估计。
The deadline had already been exceeded by the time we received the report. = 我们收到报告时，截止日期早已过期。


将来时:
The population is projected to exceed one billion by 2050. = 预计到2050年人口将超过十亿。
Demand will likely exceed supply in the coming months. = 未来几个月需求可能会超过供应。


虚拟语气:
If the project exceeds the budget, we'll have to cut costs. = 如果项目超出预算，我们就必须削减成本。
I wish my salary exceeded my expenses. = 我希望我的工资能够超过我的开支。


被动语态:
The speed limit was exceeded by the driver. = 司机超速了。
The expectations were exceeded by the team's performance. = 团队的表现超出了预期。


疑问句:
Does the amount exceed the limit? = 这个金额超过限额了吗？
Does your salary exceed 100,000 dollars? = 你的薪水超过10万美元吗？


否定句:
The profits did not exceed expectations this quarter. = 本季度利润没有超过预期。
The company's performance did not exceed its competitors. = 该公司的业绩没有超过其竞争对手。


PHRASE->
exceed the limit = 超过限制
exceed expectations = 超出预期
exceed the speed limit = 超速
exceed the capacity = 超过容量
exceed one's authority = 滥用职权

词源:
From Old French *exceder, from Latin excedere "to go out, depart, exceed," from ex- "out" + cedere "to go, yield."
