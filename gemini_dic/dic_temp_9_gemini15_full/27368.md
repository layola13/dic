camcorders:/ˈkæmˌkɔːrdərz/ | n. |便携式摄像机 | A portable device used to record moving images and sound.  Camcorders combine a video camera and a recording device into a single unit, making them convenient for filming various subjects. | Example: He used his camcorder to film his daughter's graduation ceremony. 他用他的摄像机拍摄了他女儿的毕业典礼。|近义词：video camera, film camera |反义词：None (no direct opposite)
