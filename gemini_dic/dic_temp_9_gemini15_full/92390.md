hypocrystalline:/ˌhaɪpəʊˈkrɪstəlɪn/| adj.|次晶质的，隐晶质的|Relating to or denoting rocks that have a partly crystalline structure, with some parts being glassy or amorphous.|The igneous rock exhibited a hypocrystalline texture, showing both crystalline and glassy components.|近义词：partly crystalline, microcrystalline|反义词：holocrystalline, glassy
