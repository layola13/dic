cropping:/ˈkrɒpɪŋ/| n. v.| 收获；裁剪；修剪 | The act of harvesting crops or trimming something.  |例句：The cropping of wheat was delayed by the rain.  小麦收割因雨水而延误。|例句：She used image-editing software to cropping the photo. 她用图像编辑软件裁剪照片。|近义词：harvesting, trimming, cutting |反义词：planting, growing

He crops his hair short every month. = 他每月都把头发剪短。
