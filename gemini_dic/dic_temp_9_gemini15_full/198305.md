shouts:/ʃaʊts/| v.|喊叫；大声说|To utter or express (something) loudly and forcefully.|He shouts at the top of his lungs. 他扯着嗓子喊叫。|近义词：yell, scream, cry out|反义词：whisper, murmur


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shouts instructions to his team. 他向他的团队大声喊叫指示。
She shouts across the room. 她大声地喊叫着穿过房间。


一般过去时:
The crowd shouted with excitement. 人群兴奋地欢呼。
He shouted for help. 他大声呼救。


现在完成时:
She has shouted at me several times today. 今天她已经对我大声喊叫好几次了。
They have shouted their approval. 他们已经大声表示赞同。


过去完成时:
He had shouted himself hoarse before anyone heard him.  在他被任何人听到之前，他已经喊哑了嗓子。
They had shouted warnings but no one listened. 他们已经大声警告过，但没有人听。


将来时:
I will shout if I see anything suspicious. 如果我看到任何可疑的东西，我会大声喊叫。
They will shout their support for the candidate.他们将大声疾呼支持候选人。


虚拟语气:
If I were in danger, I would shout for help. 如果我处于危险之中，我会大声呼救。
I wish I hadn't shouted at him. 我真希望我没有对他大喊大叫。


被动语态:
His name was shouted from the rooftops. 他的名字被大声地喊叫着传遍了各地。
Warnings were shouted but ignored. 警告声喊出去了，却被忽略了。


疑问句:
What are you shouting about? 你在喊什么？
Why did they shout? 他们为什么大喊大叫？


否定句:
He didn't shout at me. 他没有对我大喊大叫。
She didn't shout a word. 她一句话也没喊。


PHRASE->
shout down:  压制；喝倒彩 (e.g., The speaker was shouted down by the angry crowd. 演讲者被愤怒的人群喝倒彩.)
shout out: 大声喊出 (e.g., He shouted out the answer. 他大声喊出了答案。)
shout for joy:  高兴地欢呼 (e.g., The children shouted for joy. 孩子们高兴地欢呼。)
shout at the top of one's lungs: 扯着嗓子喊 (e.g., She shouted at the top of her lungs. 她扯着嗓子喊。)
let out a shout: 发出一声喊叫 (e.g., He let out a shout of surprise. 他发出一声惊讶的喊叫。)
