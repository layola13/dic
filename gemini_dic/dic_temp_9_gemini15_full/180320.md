refueled:/ˌriːˈfjuːld/| vt.|补充燃料；再次激发；重新供应燃料|To supply (something, especially a vehicle or machine) with more fuel.|The plane refueled in Denver before continuing to London.  飞机在丹佛加油后继续飞往伦敦。|The debate refueled the controversy.  这场辩论再次激化了争议。|近义词：replenish, refill|反义词：deplete, exhaust|动词


USAGE->

The car refuels at the gas station. = 汽车在加油站加油。
He refuels his body with healthy food. = 他用健康的食物补充能量。
