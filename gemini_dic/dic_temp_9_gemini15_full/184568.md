restocked:/ˌriːˈstɑːkt/| v. |重新进货；补充库存|To replenish a supply of goods that have been sold or used.  The verb "restock" implies the act of bringing in new items to replace those that are gone. |例句：The supermarket restocked its shelves with fresh produce.  超市用新鲜农产品补充了货架上的存货。

The store was restocked after the holiday rush. 节日购物高峰过后，商店进行了补货。|近义词：replenish, refill, replenish supplies|反义词：deplete, exhaust, sell out


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The store restocks its shelves every morning. = 商店每天早上都补充货架上的商品。
They restock the warehouse regularly. = 他们定期补充仓库的库存。

一般过去时:
The shop restocked the shelves yesterday. = 商店昨天补充了货架上的商品。
We restocked the office supplies after the meeting. = 会议后我们补充了办公用品。


现在完成时:
They have restocked the shelves twice this week. = 他们本周已经两次补充了货架上的商品。
The company has restocked its inventory to meet increased demand. = 公司已补充库存以满足日益增长的需求。


过去完成时:
They had restocked the shelves before the customers arrived. = 在顾客到达之前，他们已经补充了货架上的商品。
We had restocked the supplies before the project started. = 项目开始前，我们已经补充了物资。


将来时:
The store will restock its shelves tomorrow. = 商店明天将补充货架上的商品。
They will restock the warehouse next week. = 他们下周将补充仓库的库存。


虚拟语气:
If we had restocked earlier, we wouldn't have run out of supplies. = 如果我们早点补充库存，就不会缺货了。
I wish they had restocked the popular items sooner. = 我希望他们早点补充那些畅销商品。


被动语态:
The shelves were restocked this morning. = 货架今天早上已经补充了商品。
The warehouse is being restocked at the moment. = 仓库正在补充库存。


疑问句:
Has the shop been restocked yet? = 商店已经补充商品了吗？
Will the shelves be restocked before the sale? = 促销开始前货架会补充商品吗？


否定句:
The store didn't restock the shelves yesterday. = 商店昨天没有补充货架上的商品。
They haven't restocked the warehouse yet. = 他们还没有补充仓库的库存。
