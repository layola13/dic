looters:/ˈluːtərz/ | n. | 抢劫者，洗劫者 | People who steal goods, especially during a riot or time of civil disorder.  They take things from stores, homes, or other places without permission, often violently. |例句：The looters ransacked the shops after the earthquake. 地震过后，抢劫者洗劫了商店。

例句：Following the hurricane, looters were seen taking advantage of the chaos. 飓风过后，人们看到抢劫者利用混乱之机。


近义词：plunderers, thieves, robbers, marauders | 反义词：donors, benefactors


词性：名词
