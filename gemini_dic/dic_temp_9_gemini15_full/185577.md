revelation:/ˌrevəˈleɪʃən/ | n. |启示；揭露；展现 | The act of revealing something previously unknown or hidden; a sudden manifestation of something previously unknown.  It can refer to a divine or spiritual insight, or to the disclosure of something secret or scandalous. |例句：The scientist's discovery was a revelation in the field of medicine. 科学家的发现是医学领域的一项重大突破。

The book's final chapter held a shocking revelation about the main character's past.  这本书的最后一章揭露了主角过去令人震惊的秘密。|近义词：disclosure, unveiling, epiphany, discovery |反义词：concealment, secrecy, mystery
