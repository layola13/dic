scaliness:/ˈskeɪlinəs/| adj.|鳞状，鳞片状；多鳞的；有鳞片的|The quality or state of being scaly; having scales.  It can refer to the texture of a surface resembling fish scales, or to the abundance of scales on something.|例句：The lizard showed a remarkable scaliness on its back.  这只蜥蜴背上鳞片非常显著。

The snake's scaliness was evident in its shimmering skin. 这条蛇的鳞片在其闪闪发光的皮肤上很明显。|近义词：scaliness, scale-like, flaky|反义词：smooth, sleek
