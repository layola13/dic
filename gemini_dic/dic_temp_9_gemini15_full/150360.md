panislamism:/ˌpænˈɪzlæmɪzəm/| n. |泛伊斯兰主义|The political ideology advocating for the unity of all Muslim countries under a single Islamic government or caliphate.  It often involves a strong emphasis on Islamic identity and the rejection of Western influence.  |Example: The rise of panislamism in the 19th century was partly a reaction to European colonialism. 泛伊斯兰主义在19世纪的兴起部分是欧洲殖民主义的反应。|近义词：Islamic unity, Muslim unity |反义词：secularism, nationalism (in a competing sense)|
