jains:/dʒeɪnz/ | n. |耆那教徒；耆那教信徒 | The word "Jains" refers to the followers of Jainism, an ancient Indian religion.  Jainism emphasizes non-violence, self-discipline, and the pursuit of spiritual liberation through the eradication of karma.  | Example: Many Jains are vegetarian because of their strong belief in ahimsa (non-violence).  许多耆那教徒是素食主义者，因为他们坚信阿希姆萨（非暴力）。|近义词：Jain followers, Jains (plural of Jain)|反义词：无
