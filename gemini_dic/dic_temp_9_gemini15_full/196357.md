serio:/ˈsɪəriəʊ/| adj.|严肃的，认真的|Serious means showing a thoughtful or solemn attitude.  It can also refer to something that is important and requires attention.  |例句：He has a serious look on his face. 他脸上带着严肃的表情。|例句：This is a serious matter that requires immediate attention. 这是一个需要立即处理的严重问题。|近义词：grave, solemn, earnest, weighty|反义词：frivolous, lighthearted, casual, unserious


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is serious about his work. = 他对他的工作很认真。
She is serious when she speaks. = 她说话很认真。


一般过去时:
He was serious about his studies. = 他以前学习很认真。
She was serious when she gave the warning. = 她发出警告时很认真。


现在完成时:
They have been serious about their fitness goals. = 他们一直很认真地对待他们的健身目标。
He has always been serious about his commitments. = 他一直很认真地对待他的承诺。


过去完成时:
She had been serious about her career before she had children. = 在她生孩子之前，她对她的事业很认真。
They had been serious about finding a solution. = 他们之前一直很认真地寻找解决方案。


将来时:
He will be serious about his training for the marathon. = 他将认真对待马拉松训练。
She will be serious about her application to university. = 她将认真对待她的大学申请。


虚拟语气:
If he were serious, he would have finished the project by now. = 如果他认真一点，他现在应该已经完成了项目。
I wish she were more serious about her health. = 我希望她能更重视她的健康。


被动语态:
The matter was taken seriously by the authorities. = 当局认真对待了此事。
His concerns were not taken seriously enough. = 他的担忧没有得到足够的重视。


疑问句:
Are you serious about quitting your job? = 你认真考虑辞职吗？
Is she serious about her relationship? = 她对这段感情认真吗？


否定句:
He wasn't serious about his promise. = 他对他的承诺并不认真。
She wasn't serious when she said that. = 她这么说的时候并不认真。


PHRASE->
take something seriously = 认真对待某事
make a serious effort = 认真努力
a serious problem = 一个严重的问题

serious illness = 重病
