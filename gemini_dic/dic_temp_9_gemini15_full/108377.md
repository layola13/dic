law-abiding:/ˈlɔː əˈbaɪdɪŋ/ | adj. |守法的，遵纪守法的 |  Someone who is law-abiding obeys and respects the law.  They consistently act in accordance with legal and ethical standards. |例句：He has always been a law-abiding citizen. 他一直都是一个守法公民。

The company has a strict policy of employing only law-abiding individuals. 这家公司有严格的政策，只雇佣守法公民。


近义词：obedient, compliant, respectful of the law, upright

反义词：lawless, criminal, delinquent, rebellious
