shrimpiest: /ˈʃrɪmpiɪst/ | adj. | 最小的虾状的；最像虾的；最矮小的 | Most like a shrimp; smallest;  extremely small and insignificant.  The word combines "shrimp" with the superlative suffix "-iest," intensifying the qualities associated with shrimp, particularly small size. | 例句：The shrimpiest of the shrimp was barely visible. 最小的虾几乎看不见。  He felt like the shrimpiest person in the room. 他觉得自己是房间里最渺小的人。 | 近义词：tiniest, smallest, minutest, least | 反义词：largest, biggest, hugest
