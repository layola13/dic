gallivanters:/ˈɡælɪvæntərz/| n. |四处游荡的人；游手好闲的人|People who travel from place to place for pleasure, often without a fixed plan;  a person who goes on frivolous excursions.  They often engage in lighthearted and often irresponsible activities during their travels.|例句：The gallivanters spent their days exploring hidden beaches and charming villages. 那些四处游荡的人把他们的日子花在了探索隐秘的海滩和迷人的村庄上。


The group of gallivanters caused quite a stir at the local pub. 那群四处游荡的人在当地酒吧引起了不小的骚动。|近义词：vagabonds, wanderers, rovers, ramblers, pleasure-seekers |反义词：homebodies, stay-at-homes
