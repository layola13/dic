cayuse:/kaɪˈjuːs/| n.|凯尤斯马；（美国西北部印第安部落）|A small, hardy horse of the northwestern United States, especially one of those formerly used by the Nez Perce tribe.  The term is also sometimes used to refer to the Nez Perce people themselves.|例句：The cayuse were known for their endurance and agility. 凯尤斯马以其耐力和敏捷性而闻名。|The cayuse were renowned for their speed and stamina on the rugged terrain.凯尤斯马以其在崎岖地形上的速度和耐力而闻名。|近义词：pony, mustang|反义词：无
