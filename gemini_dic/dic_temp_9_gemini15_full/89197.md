homeschooling:/ˈhoʊmskuːlɪŋ/ | n. |家庭教育，在家上学 |The practice of educating children at home instead of sending them to school.  |例句：Homeschooling allows parents to tailor their children's education to their individual needs. 家庭教育允许父母根据孩子的个人需求定制教育。

例句：Many families choose homeschooling due to concerns about bullying or the quality of public schools. 许多家庭选择家庭教育是因为担心欺凌或公立学校的质量。


近义词：home education, private tutoring, unschooling | 反义词：public schooling, traditional schooling | 词性:名词
