reinforces:/ˌriːɪnˈfɔːrsɪz/| vt.| 加强；增强；强化 | To strengthen or support something, making it more effective.  |例句：The new evidence reinforces the theory. 新证据加强了这一理论。
The teacher's positive feedback reinforces the students' confidence. 老师的积极反馈增强了学生的信心。|近义词：strengthen, bolster, support |反义词：weaken, undermine


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government reinforces its policies. = 政府加强其政策。
She reinforces her arguments with facts. = 她用事实来加强她的论点。

一般过去时:
The coach reinforced the team's training. = 教练加强了球队的训练。
He reinforced his position with a strong statement. = 他用强烈的言辞来强化他的立场。

现在完成时:
The company has reinforced its security measures. = 公司已经加强了安全措施。
They have reinforced the walls of the castle. = 他们已经加固了城堡的城墙。

过去完成时:
She had reinforced her arguments before the debate. = 她在辩论之前已经加强了她的论点。
The soldiers had reinforced their defenses before the attack. = 士兵们在袭击之前已经加强了他们的防御。

将来时:
The engineer will reinforce the bridge structure. = 工程师将加固桥梁结构。
We will reinforce our commitment to sustainability. = 我们将加强我们对可持续性的承诺。

虚拟语气:
If they reinforced the team, they would win the game. = 如果他们加强球队，他们就能赢得比赛。
I wish they had reinforced the structure before the earthquake. = 我希望他们在地震前已经加固了结构。

被动语态:
The building was reinforced with steel beams. = 这座建筑用钢梁加固了。
The team's morale was reinforced by the victory. = 球队的士气因胜利而增强。

疑问句:
How can we reinforce our marketing strategy? = 我们如何加强我们的营销策略？
Does this evidence reinforce your hypothesis? = 这个证据是否加强了你的假设？

否定句:
The company did not reinforce its cybersecurity measures. = 公司没有加强其网络安全措施。
He didn't reinforce his argument with sufficient data. = 他没有用足够的数据来支持他的论点。
