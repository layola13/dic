outsprinted:/ˌaʊtˈsprɪntɪd/| v. |跑赢，超过（尤指速度）|To run faster than and overtake (someone). This is the past tense of the verb "outsprint". It implies a competitive race where one surpasses another through superior speed.|例句：In the final sprint, he outsrinted his rivals and won the race.  在最后的冲刺中，他超过了他的对手并赢得了比赛。


例句：She outsrinted all the other competitors in the 100-meter dash. 她在一百米短跑中跑赢了所有其他选手。


近义词：outpace, outrun, overtake


反义词：be outsprinted by, be beaten


词性：动词
