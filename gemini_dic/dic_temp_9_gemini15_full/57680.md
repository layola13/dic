drawlatch: /ˈdrɔːlætʃ/ | n. | 门闩；拉栓 | A drawlatch is a simple latch mechanism that is operated by pulling a handle or lever.  It typically consists of a bolt that slides into a keeper plate.  Drawlatches are commonly used on doors, gates, and chests. | Example: The old wooden chest was secured with a rusty drawlatch.  古老的木箱是用生锈的门闩锁住的。|近义词：latch, bolt | 反义词：None (Antonyms are not directly applicable as it describes a mechanism rather than a concept)
