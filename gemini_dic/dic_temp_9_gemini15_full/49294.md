delicatessen:/ˌdɛlɪkəˈtɛsən/| n. |熟食店；熟食；美味佳肴|A delicatessen is a shop that sells prepared foods, such as cheeses, meats, salads, and other gourmet items.  It often also sells imported foods and other specialty items. |例句：I stopped by the delicatessen to pick up some cheese and olives for the party. 我顺便去了熟食店，为派对买些奶酪和橄榄。

例句：The delicatessen offered a wide selection of gourmet sausages and pâtés. 这家熟食店提供各种各样的美味香肠和肉酱。


近义词：gourmet shop, specialty food store, food market|反义词：无|词性：名词
