empoison:/ɛ̃pwazɔ̃/| vt.| 毒害；使中毒；污染 | To contaminate or infect with poison; to corrupt or spoil.  |例句：The enemy tried to empoison the water supply. 敌人试图毒害水源。
The scandal empoisoned the atmosphere of the entire office.  丑闻败坏了整个办公室的气氛。|近义词：poison, contaminate, corrupt |反义词：purify, cleanse
