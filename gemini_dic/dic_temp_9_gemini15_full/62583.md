enerve:/ɪˈnɜːrv/ | vt. |使衰弱；使无力；使紧张|To weaken or destroy the strength or vitality of; to deprive of strength or vigor; to make nervous or irritable.|例句：The long journey enervated him. 长途旅行使他精疲力尽。|例句：The heat enervated the soldiers. 炎热的天气使士兵们精疲力尽。|近义词：weaken, debilitate, sap, exhaust|反义词：strengthen, invigorate, energize|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The constant pressure enervates her. =  持续的压力使她精疲力尽。
This heat enervates me. =  这热天让我无力。


一般过去时:
The long speech enervated the audience. =冗长的演讲使听众感到疲惫。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
