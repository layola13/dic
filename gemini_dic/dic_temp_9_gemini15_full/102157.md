issuing:/ˈɪʃuɪŋ/| v. |发行；发布；颁发；发出；开出；造成；引起 |The act of giving out or delivering something officially.  This verb often refers to documents, money, or statements. |例句：The bank is issuing new credit cards.  银行正在发行新的信用卡。

例句：The government is issuing a statement on the crisis. 政府正在就这场危机发表声明。


近义词：releasing, distributing, publishing, delivering, providing

反义词：receiving, collecting, recalling


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is issuing new shares. = 公司正在发行新股。
He is issuing instructions to his team. = 他正在向他的团队下达指示。


一般过去时:
The bank issued me a new debit card. = 银行给我发了一张新的借记卡。
She issued a warning about the dangers of smoking. = 她就吸烟的危害发出了警告。


现在完成时:
They have issued a press release. = 他们已经发布了一份新闻稿。
The government has issued several new regulations. = 政府已经发布了几项新规章。


过去完成时:
The court had issued a warrant for his arrest. = 法院已经对他签发了逮捕令。
Before the meeting, she had issued a detailed agenda. = 会议之前，她已经发布了一份详细的议程。


将来时:
They will issue a statement later today. = 他们今天晚些时候将发表声明。
The company will be issuing its annual report next month. = 公司将于下个月发布年度报告。


虚拟语气:
If the bank issued me a loan, I would start my business. = 如果银行给我贷款，我就会开始我的生意。
I wish they had issued a clearer explanation. = 我希望他们能给出更清晰的解释。


被动语态:
New shares are being issued by the company. = 新股正在由公司发行。
A statement was issued by the police. = 警方发表了一份声明。


疑问句:
Is the bank issuing new loans? = 银行正在发放新的贷款吗？
When will the tickets be issued? =  票什么时候发行？


否定句:
The bank did not issue me a loan. = 银行没有给我贷款。
They haven't issued any warnings yet. = 他们还没有发出任何警告。


PHRASE->
issue a statement  发布声明
issue a warning 发出警告
issue a license 发放许可证
issue an order 下达命令
issue a certificate 发放证书
