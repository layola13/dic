obstructer:/əbˈstrʌktər/| n. |阻碍者，妨碍者 |A person or thing that obstructs. Someone or something that blocks or hinders progress.|The fallen tree was a major obstructer on the road.  那棵倒下的树是路上的主要障碍物。The new regulations are seen by some as an obstructer to economic growth. 一些人认为新的规章制度阻碍了经济增长。|近义词：blocker,impediment,obstacle|反义词：facilitator,helper


USAGE->
