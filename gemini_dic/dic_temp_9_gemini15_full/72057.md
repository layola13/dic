flirting:/ˈflɜːrtɪŋ/ | n. |调情；卖弄风情 | The act of behaving in a way that shows romantic interest in someone, without being serious or committing.  It often involves playful or suggestive behavior. |例句：Her flirting with the waiter was obvious to everyone. 她与服务员调情，每个人都看得出来。

例句：The couple's playful flirting was endearing to watch. 这对情侣打情骂俏的样子很惹人喜爱。


近义词：coquetry,  teasing,  romancing,  wooing | 反义词：serious commitment,  ignoring
