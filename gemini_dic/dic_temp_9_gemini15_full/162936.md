prechill: /ˈpriːtʃɪl/ | v. | 预先冷却；提前冷却 | To cool something down beforehand, especially food or drinks, to prepare for later consumption or use.  | Example: I prechilled the champagne for the party. 我提前把香槟冰镇好了，为派对做准备。 | 近义词：chill, refrigerate, cool | 反义词：heat, warm


USAGE->

He prechills his beer before every game. = 他每次比赛前都会先把啤酒冰镇一下。
She prechills the ingredients for her smoothie. = 她会先把制作冰沙的食材冷藏一下。
