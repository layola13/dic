encryption:/ɪnˈkrɪpʃən/ | n. |加密，加 密|The process of converting information or data into a code, especially to prevent unauthorized access.|例句：Strong encryption is crucial for protecting sensitive data. 强加密对于保护敏感数据至关重要。|近义词：coding, scrambling, encoding |反义词：decryption


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Encryption protects sensitive information. = 加密保护敏感信息。
They use encryption to secure their communications. = 他们使用加密来保护他们的通信。

一般过去时:
The message was encrypted before transmission. = 消息在传输前进行了加密。
We encrypted the files to prevent unauthorized access. = 我们对文件进行了加密以防止未经授权的访问。

现在完成时:
They have encrypted all the important documents. = 他们已经加密了所有重要的文件。
We have implemented a new encryption system. = 我们已经实施了一个新的加密系统。

过去完成时:
The data had been encrypted before the hacker gained access. = 黑客获取访问权限之前，数据已被加密。
They had encrypted the files, but the key was lost. = 他们已经加密了文件，但是密钥丢失了。

将来时:
We will encrypt the data before sending it. = 我们将在发送数据之前对其进行加密。
They will implement stronger encryption next year. = 他们将在明年实施更强的加密。

虚拟语气:
It would be safer if we encrypted the data. = 如果我们对数据进行加密，会更安全。
I wish I had encrypted my files earlier. = 我希望我早点加密我的文件。

被动语态:

否定句:
