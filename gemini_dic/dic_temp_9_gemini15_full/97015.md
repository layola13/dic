infeasibility:/ˌɪnˌfiːzəˈbɪləti/| n.|不可行性，不可能性|The state or quality of being infeasible; impracticality.  It refers to something that is not capable of being done, achieved, or carried out successfully.|例句：The infeasibility of the project was clear from the outset.  这个项目的不可行性从一开始就显而易见。

例句：Due to the high costs and complex logistics, the infeasibility of the plan became apparent. 由于高昂的成本和复杂的物流，该计划的不可行性变得显而易见。


近义词：impracticability, impossibility, unfeasibility|反义词：feasibility, possibility|名词
