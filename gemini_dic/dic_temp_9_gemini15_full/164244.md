predooming:/priːˈduːmɪŋ/ | adj. | 预示厄运的；注定要失败的 |  Foretelling or suggesting an inevitable doom or failure; destined for ruin or destruction.  |The predooming atmosphere of the play left the audience feeling uneasy.  这部戏中预示厄运的氛围让观众感到不安。 The project, from its inception, seemed predooming to failure.  这个项目从一开始就似乎注定要失败。| ominous, ill-fated, doomed | auspicious, fortunate, promising | 形容词
