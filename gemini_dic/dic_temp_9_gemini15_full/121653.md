misphrase:/ˈmɪsfreɪz/| vt.| 措辞不当；曲解；误用词语 |To express something in an inaccurate or unclear way; to use the wrong words.|例句：He misphrased the question, leading to confusion. 他问话用词不当，导致了混淆。
例句：The journalist misphrased the politician's statement, making him seem more aggressive than intended.  记者曲解了这位政治家的声明，让他看起来比本意更咄咄逼人。|近义词：paraphrase incorrectly, misinterpret, misrepresent|反义词：phrase accurately, clarify
