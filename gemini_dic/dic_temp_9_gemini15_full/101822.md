irritatingly:/ˈɪrɪteɪtɪŋli/| adv. |令人恼怒地，烦人地 |In an irritating manner; annoyingly.  The word describes an action or situation that causes annoyance or frustration. |例句：He irritatingly hummed the same tune all day. 他整天令人恼火地哼着同一首歌。

例句：She irritatingly kept interrupting me. 她不断打断我，真让人烦。


近义词：annoyingly, frustratingly, exasperatingly, vexingly

反义词：pleasingly, agreeably, delightfully


词性：副词
