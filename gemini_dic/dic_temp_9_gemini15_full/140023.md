oem:/ˌoʊiˈɛm/| n. |原始设备制造商；贴牌生产|Original Equipment Manufacturer; a company that manufactures goods which are then sold under another company's brand name.|Example: Many electronics companies use OEMs to produce their products. 许多电子公司使用原始设备制造商来生产他们的产品。|近义词：contract manufacturer, subcontractor |反义词：brand owner, retailer

USAGE->
一般现在时:
This company is an OEM for several major brands. = 这家公司是几家主要品牌的原始设备制造商。
The OEM manufactures parts for various car companies. = 这家原始设备制造商为各种汽车公司生产零件。

一般过去时:
They were an OEM for a popular clothing line. = 他们曾是某知名服装系列的原始设备制造商。
Our company used an OEM for its computer components last year. = 我们公司去年使用了原始设备制造商来生产其电脑组件。


现在完成时:
This company has been an OEM for over 20 years. = 这家公司已经做了20多年的原始设备制造商。
We have successfully partnered with several OEMs on various projects. = 我们已经成功地与几家原始设备制造商在多个项目上合作。

过去完成时:

The company had already established relationships with several key OEMs. = 该公司已经与几家主要的原始设备制造商建立了关系。


将来时:
