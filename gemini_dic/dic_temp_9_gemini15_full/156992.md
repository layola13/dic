pillory:/ˈpɪləri/| n. | 辱骂; 嘲笑; 耻辱柱 | A pillory is a wooden structure with holes for the head and hands, in which offenders were formerly locked and exposed to public ridicule and abuse.  It can also refer to the act of publicly humiliating or criticizing someone. | Example:  He was subjected to a public pillorying in the newspapers. 他在报纸上受到了公众的公开谴责。 | 近义词：public shaming, ridicule, denunciation, rebuke | 反义词：praise, commendation, applause | 名词
