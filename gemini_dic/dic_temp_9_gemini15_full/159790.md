polygenism:/pɑːlɪˈdʒɛnɪzəm/| n. |多源论；多元论（指人类起源于多个祖先）|The theory that different races of humans originated from different ancestors.  This is a now-discredited theory that was used to justify racism and discrimination.  |例句：The proponents of polygenism argued that different races possessed distinct and unequal origins.|近义词：polyphyletism (in biology)|反义词：monogenism
