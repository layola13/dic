markupping:/ˈmɑːrkʌpɪŋ/ | n. |标记；标记法；加标记|The process of adding markup to a document, typically to specify its structure and formatting for display or processing.  This often involves using tags or other special symbols to indicate elements like headings, paragraphs, images, and links.  It's crucial for creating web pages, formatting documents in word processors, and other digital publishing tasks.|Example: The markupping of the manuscript was completed before it went to the printer.  = 手稿的标记工作在送交印刷厂之前就完成了。|近义词：markup, tagging, annotation |反义词：removal of markup, plain text
