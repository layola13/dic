nonromantically:/ˌnɒnˌroʊˈmænɪkəli/ | adv. |非浪漫地，非爱情地 |  Describes actions or relationships that lack romantic or amorous feelings or intentions.  It emphasizes the absence of romantic love or attraction. |例句：They've been friends for years, and they relate nonromantically. 他们多年来一直是朋友，他们之间的关系是非浪漫的。
They worked together nonromantically on the project. 他们在项目中非浪漫地一起工作。| 近义词：platonically, amicably,  | 反义词：romantically, amorously | 副词
