indignant:/ɪnˈdɪɡnənt/| adj.|愤慨的；义愤填膺的|Feeling or showing anger or annoyance at what is perceived as unfair treatment.  |例句：She was indignant at the suggestion that she had lied. 她对暗示她撒谎感到义愤填膺。

例句：He wrote an indignant letter to the newspaper. 他给报社写了一封义愤填膺的信。


近义词：outraged, offended, resentful, angry, furious

反义词：pleased, content, satisfied, unconcerned


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is indignant about the injustice. = 他对这种不公正感到愤慨。
She is indignant at their behavior. = 她对他们的行为感到愤怒。


一般过去时:
He was indignant at the unfair treatment. = 他对不公平的待遇感到愤慨。
They were indignant that their request was ignored. = 他们的请求被忽视了，他们对此感到愤怒。


现在完成时:
I have been indignant about this issue for years. = 多年来我一直对这个问题感到愤慨。
She has been indignant ever since the incident. = 从那件事发生以来，她就一直感到愤怒。


过去完成时:
He had been indignant for days before he finally spoke out. = 他闷闷不乐了好几天，才最终说出来。
They had been indignant about the decision long before the meeting. = 在会议很久之前，他们就已经对这个决定感到愤怒了。


将来时:
She will be indignant when she hears the news. = 当她听到这个消息时，她会很生气。
They will be indignant if their proposal is rejected. = 如果他们的提案被拒绝，他们会感到愤怒。


虚拟语气:
I would be indignant if I were in your shoes. = 如果我站在你的位置，我会很生气。
She would have been indignant if she had known the truth. = 如果她知道真相，她会很生气。


被动语态:
His behavior was considered indignant by many. = 许多人认为他的行为令人愤慨。
The response was found to be indignant and inappropriate. = 反应被认为是愤怒和不恰当的。


疑问句:
Are you indignant about the situation? = 你对这种情况感到愤慨吗？
Isn't he indignant at their actions? = 他对他们的行为难道不感到愤怒吗？


否定句:
He wasn't indignant at all. = 他一点也不生气。
She wasn't indignant, just disappointed. = 她不是生气，只是失望。


PHRASE->
feel indignant = 感到愤怒
express indignation = 表达愤怒
an indignant outburst = 愤怒的爆发
an indignant tone = 愤怒的语气
indignant silence = 愤怒的沉默
