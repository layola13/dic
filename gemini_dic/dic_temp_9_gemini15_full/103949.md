judicialize:/dʒuːˈdɪʃəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使司法化；将…提交法院 | To make something a matter for the courts or judges; to subject to judicial process.  | 例句：The government attempted to judicialize the dispute, referring it to the Supreme Court.  政府试图将争议司法化，将其提交最高法院。 | 例句：The case was judicialized, leading to a lengthy and costly trial. 此案被司法化，导致了一场漫长而昂贵的审判。 | 近义词：legalize, adjudicate | 反义词：ignore, settle out of court | 动词
