The word "excuse" does not exist.  I believe you meant "excuse".


excuse:/ɪkˈskjuːs/ | v. n. | 借口；原谅；免除 | to accept as valid; forgive; to remove the need for |  近义词：apology, pardon, justification | 反义词：blame, accusation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I excuse your behavior this time. = 这次我原谅你的行为。
He excuses himself from the meeting. = 他为自己缺席会议找借口。


一般过去时:
She excused his lateness. = 她原谅了他的迟到。
They excused themselves from the dinner party. = 他们为自己缺席晚宴找了借口。


现在完成时:
I have excused myself from the meeting. = 我已经为自己缺席会议找了借口。
He has excused her many times. = 他已经原谅了她很多次。


过去完成时:
She had excused him before, but this time she wouldn't. = 她以前原谅过他，但这次不行了。
They had excused themselves to the host before leaving. = 他们在离开之前向主人道别了。


将来时:
I will excuse you this once. = 我这次就原谅你一次。
He will excuse himself to go to the restroom. = 他会找个借口去洗手间。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would excuse myself. = 如果我是你，我会找个借口离开。
I wish I could excuse his rudeness. = 我真希望我能原谅他的粗鲁。


被动语态:
His absence was excused by the teacher. = 老师原谅了他的缺席。
His behavior was excused by the circumstances. = 他的行为因情况特殊而被原谅。


疑问句:
May I be excused? = 我可以离开一下吗？
Will you excuse my interruption? = 你能原谅我的打断吗？


否定句:
I will not excuse such behavior. = 我不会原谅这种行为。
She couldn't excuse his actions. = 她无法原谅他的行为。



PHRASE->
excuse me = 请原谅；对不起
make excuses = 找借口
under the excuse of = 以...为借口
with your excuse =  借你的名义
no excuse = 没有借口
