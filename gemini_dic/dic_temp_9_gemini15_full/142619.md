osteoplasty:/ˈɒstiəˌplæsti/| n.|骨成形术|Surgical reshaping of a bone.  It is a procedure used to correct bone deformities, fractures, or other bone issues.  The goal is to improve the function and/or appearance of the bone.|例句：The surgeon performed an osteoplasty to correct the patient's fractured tibia.  外科医生进行了骨成形术来矫正患者的胫骨骨折。

The osteoplasty on her femur improved her mobility significantly.  她股骨的骨成形术显著改善了她的活动能力。|近义词：bone shaping, bone grafting (depending on the specific technique)|反义词：bone resection (removal of bone)|名词
