filmless:/ˈfɪlm.ləs/| adj.| 无胶片的；数码的 |  Free from or not using photographic film.  This term is primarily used in the context of photography and cinematography, referring to systems that capture images digitally rather than using traditional film.  | Example:  The new camera is filmless, allowing for instant image review and editing.  = 这款新相机是无胶片的，允许立即查看和编辑图像。

The director chose a filmless approach for the movie, resulting in faster post-production. = 导演为这部电影选择了无胶片的方法，从而加快了后期制作。 | 近义词：digital, electronic | 反义词：film-based, traditional
