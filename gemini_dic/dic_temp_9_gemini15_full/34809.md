/ˈklaɪmən/| n. |气候|climate;  The word refers to the long-term weather patterns of a region, encompassing temperature, precipitation, humidity, wind, and other meteorological factors.  It's the average weather conditions over a long period, typically 30 years.|例句：The Mediterranean climate is known for its hot, dry summers and mild, wet winters.  地中海气候以其炎热干燥的夏季和温和潮湿的冬季而闻名。|近义词：weather, atmosphere |反义词：None. |名词
