dolphin:/ˈdɒlfɪn/ /ˈdɑːlfɪn/| n.|海豚|A marine mammal characterized by its streamlined body, intelligence, and playful behavior. Dolphins are known for their social nature and complex communication. They are found in oceans worldwide.|例句：We saw a pod of dolphins playing in the waves. 我们看到一群海豚在海浪中嬉戏。

例句：Dolphins are highly intelligent mammals. 海豚是高度智慧的哺乳动物。

近义词：porpoise (though technically distinct)


反义词：无


词性：名词
