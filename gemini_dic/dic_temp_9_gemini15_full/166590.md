presearching:/ˌpriːˈsɜːrtʃɪŋ/ | v. |预先搜索，预先调查 | To search or investigate something beforehand.  | Example:  I spent the morning presearching potential venues for the conference. 我花了整个上午预先搜索会议的潜在场地。 |近义词：preliminary research, advance investigation |反义词：post-searching (not a common word)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They presearch keywords before launching a marketing campaign. = 他们在启动营销活动之前预先搜索关键词。
We presearch flight options to find the best deal. = 我们预先搜索航班选项以找到最优惠的价格。

一般过去时:
She presearched the library catalog before her visit. = 她访问图书馆之前预先搜索了图书馆目录。
They presearched the internet for relevant information. = 他们预先在互联网上搜索相关信息。

现在完成时:
I have presearched several databases for relevant articles. = 我已经预先搜索了几个数据库以查找相关文章。
He has presearched the market for similar products. = 他已经预先调查了市场上类似的产品。

过去完成时:
Before starting the project, they had presearched all available resources. = 在开始项目之前，他们已经预先搜索了所有可用资源。
She had presearched the historical records before writing her paper. = 在撰写论文之前，她已经预先搜索了历史记录。

虚拟语气:
