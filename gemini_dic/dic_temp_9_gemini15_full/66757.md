exoskeleton:/ˌɛksoʊˈskɛlətən/| n. |外骨骼；外壳|A rigid external covering for the body in some invertebrate animals, especially arthropods, providing both support and protection.|例句：The beetle's exoskeleton protects it from predators.  甲虫的外骨骼保护它免受捕食者的侵害。|近义词：external skeleton, carapace, shell|反义词：endoskeleton
