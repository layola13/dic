disfeature:/dɪsˈfiːtʃər/ | n. |缺点，劣势，缺陷 | An undesirable or unattractive feature; a flaw.  |  The main disfeature of the plan is its high cost. 这个计划的主要缺点是成本太高。 The car's disfeature is its poor fuel economy. 这辆车的缺点是燃油经济性差。 |近义词：defect, flaw, blemish, shortcoming |反义词：advantage, merit, strength
