metamorphosed:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəʊzd/ | v. | 变形；蜕变；转化 | To undergo metamorphosis; to change in form or nature.  | The caterpillar metamorphosed into a beautiful butterfly. 毛毛虫蜕变成美丽的蝴蝶。 The city has metamorphosed into a bustling metropolis. 这个城市已经变成了一个繁华的大都市。 | transformed, changed, evolved | remained the same, stagnated | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The caterpillar metamorphoses into a butterfly. 毛毛虫变成蝴蝶。
He metamorphoses his ideas into art. 他把他的想法变成艺术。


一般过去时:
The frog metamorphosed from a tadpole. 青蛙由蝌蚪变来。
She metamorphosed her writing style. 她改变了她的写作风格。


现在完成时:
The city has metamorphosed dramatically in recent years. 近年来，这座城市发生了巨大的变化。
My understanding of the problem has metamorphosed since our last meeting. 从我们上次会议以来，我对这个问题的理解已经发生了变化。


过去完成时:
The artist had metamorphosed his earlier, simpler style into a more complex one.这位艺术家已经将他早期简单的风格转变为更复杂的风格。
By the time I finished the book, my perception of the characters had metamorphosed completely. 当我读完这本书时，我对人物的看法已经完全改变了。


将来时:
The project will metamorphose into a fully functional product by next year. 该项目将于明年转变为功能齐全的产品。
The company's strategy will metamorphose significantly in the coming months. 公司的战略将在未来几个月发生重大变化。


虚拟语气:
If the caterpillar had not metamorphosed, it would not have become a butterfly. 如果毛毛虫没有蜕变，它就不会变成蝴蝶。
I wish my understanding of the subject had metamorphosed sooner. 我希望我对这个主题的理解能早点改变。


被动语态:
The story was metamorphosed into a successful movie. 这个故事被改编成一部成功的电影。(注意：metamorphose本身的被动语态使用较少，通常用其他动词代替)
The raw materials are metamorphosed into finished products through a complex process. 原材料通过一个复杂的过程转化为成品。(注意：metamorphose本身的被动语态使用较少，通常用其他动词代替)


疑问句:
Has the caterpillar metamorphosed yet? 毛毛虫蜕变了没有？
Will the company’s image metamorphose after the scandal? 丑闻之后，公司的形象会改变吗？


否定句:
The caterpillar has not yet metamorphosed. 毛毛虫还没有蜕变。
The situation did not metamorphose as we had hoped. 情况并没有像我们希望的那样改变。


PHRASE->
metamorphosed into：转变为
undergo a metamorphosis：经历蜕变
a complete metamorphosis：彻底的改变
a gradual metamorphosis：逐渐的改变
metamorphosed from：从...转变
