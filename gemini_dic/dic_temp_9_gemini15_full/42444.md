counterdemonstrators:/ˈkaʊntərdɛmənstreɪtərz/| n. |反示威者，反对示威者|People who organize or participate in a demonstration against another demonstration.  They often hold opposing views or beliefs. |例句：The police had to separate the demonstrators and the counterdemonstrators to prevent violence.  警察不得不将示威者和反示威者分开以防止暴力。


例句：Counterdemonstrators gathered to protest the rally supporting stricter immigration laws. 反示威者聚集起来抗议支持更严格移民法的集会。


近义词：protestors, opponents, adversaries


反义词：demonstrators, supporters


词性:名词
