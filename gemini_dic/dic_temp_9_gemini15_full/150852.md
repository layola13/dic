paragons:/ˈpærəɡɑːnz/| n.|典范，模范；完美榜样|A paragon is a person or thing that is regarded as a perfect example of a particular quality.  It implies flawlessness or near-flawlessness.|He is a paragon of virtue. 他是美德的典范。|近义词：model, exemplar, ideal, archetype|反义词：failure, disappointment


USAGE->
一般现在时:
This athlete is a paragon of strength and skill. =这位运动员是力量和技巧的典范。
She is considered a paragon of beauty in her village. = 在她的村庄里，她被认为是美丽的典范。


一般过去时:
He was a paragon of honesty in his dealings. = 他在交易中是诚实的典范。
The old craftsman was a paragon of patience and dedication. =这位老工匠是耐心和奉献的典范。


现在完成时:
She has become a paragon of success in her field. = 她已成为她所在领域的成功典范。
The company has always strived to be a paragon of customer service. = 公司一直努力成为客户服务的典范。


过去完成时:
Before the scandal, he had been a paragon of integrity. = 在丑闻爆发之前，他一直是正直的典范。
The teacher had been a paragon of patience with her students for many years. = 多年来，这位老师一直对她的学生非常有耐心。


将来时:
With continued hard work, she will become a paragon of excellence. = 通过持续努力，她将成为卓越的典范。
He hopes to be a paragon of leadership in the future. = 他希望将来成为领导的典范。


虚拟语气:
If he were a paragon of virtue, he wouldn't have done that. = 如果他是美德的典范，他就不会那样做了。
I wish I were a paragon of self-discipline. = 我希望我是一个自律的典范。


被动语态:
He was considered a paragon of efficiency. = 他被认为是效率的典范。
She is often cited as a paragon of modern womanhood. = 她经常被誉为现代女性的典范。


疑问句:
Is he really a paragon of virtue, or is it just a facade? = 他真的是美德的典范吗，还是只是表面现象？
Can she be considered a paragon of success in such a short time? = 在这么短的时间内，她能被认为是成功的典范吗？


否定句:
He is not a paragon of health. = 他不是健康的典范。
The system is far from being a paragon of efficiency. = 这个系统远非效率的典范。
