impersonate:/ɪmˈpɜːrsənˌeɪt/| vt.|模仿；扮演；假冒|To assume the character or appearance of (another person); to pretend to be (another person).|例句：He impersonated the president on stage. 他在舞台上模仿总统。|例句：The con artist tried to impersonate a police officer. 那个骗子试图假扮警官。|近义词：mimic, imitate, simulate, pretend|反义词：be oneself, be authentic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He impersonates famous actors. = 他模仿著名的演员。
She impersonates her boss when she's feeling stressed. = 她感到压力大的时候会模仿她的老板。


一般过去时:
The comedian impersonated several celebrities. = 喜剧演员模仿了几位名人。
He impersonated a doctor to gain access to the hospital. = 他假扮医生以进入医院。


现在完成时:
She has impersonated numerous historical figures throughout her career. = 在她的职业生涯中，她模仿了许多历史人物。
He has never impersonated anyone before. = 他以前从未模仿过任何人。


过去完成时:
They had impersonated the security guards before the alarm went off. = 警报响起之前，他们已经假扮成保安。
She had impersonated her sister many times to avoid trouble. = 她为了避免麻烦，已经多次假扮她姐姐。


将来时:
He will impersonate Elvis Presley at the concert. = 他将在音乐会上模仿猫王。
She will impersonate a famous politician for the upcoming party. = 她将为即将到来的派对模仿一位著名的政治家。


虚拟语气:
If I could impersonate anyone, I would impersonate a superhero. = 如果我能模仿任何人，我会模仿超级英雄。
I wish I could impersonate a famous singer. = 我希望我能模仿一位著名的歌手。


被动语态:
Many famous people are impersonated by comedians. = 许多名人被喜剧演员模仿。
He was impersonated by a fraudster to steal money. = 他被一个骗子假扮来偷钱。


疑问句:
Does he impersonate anyone? = 他模仿任何人吗？
Who is she impersonating tonight? = 她今晚模仿谁？


否定句:
He doesn't impersonate anyone convincingly. = 他模仿任何人都没有说服力。
She didn't impersonate the character well. = 她没有把这个角色扮演好。


PHRASE->
