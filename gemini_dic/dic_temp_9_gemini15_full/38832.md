confab:/ˈkɒnfæb/| /ˈkɑːnfæb/| n., v.| 私下会谈；闲聊 |Informal conversation; a private discussion.  |例句：The two leaders held a brief confab before the press conference.  两位领导人在记者招待会前进行了简短的会谈。

They had a confab about the upcoming project.他们就即将到来的项目进行了私下会谈。


近义词：chat, discussion, colloquy, powwow | 反义词：formal meeting, silence


词性:  n., v.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They often confab in the break room. = 他们经常在休息室里闲聊。
We confab about work and personal matters. = 我们会谈论工作和私事。


一般过去时:
They confabbed about the problem last night. = 他们昨晚私下讨论了这个问题。
We confabbed for hours about our vacation plans. = 我们花了几个小时讨论我们的假期计划。


现在完成时:

We have confabbed with the client about the project's timeline. = 我们已经和客户就项目的进度表进行过私下会谈。


过去完成时:
They had confabbed before making the final decision. = 他们在做出最终决定之前已经私下讨论过了。
We had confabbed extensively before presenting our findings. = 我们在提交研究结果之前已经进行了广泛的私下讨论。

词源:
The word "confab" comes from the Latin "con" (together) and "fabula" (story, talk).  It's a shortened form of "confabulation," which originally referred to a fictional narrative or a fabricated story, but evolved to mean an informal conversation.
