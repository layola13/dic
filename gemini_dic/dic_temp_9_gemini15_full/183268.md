reprime:/ˌriːˈpraɪm/| vt.|再次强调；再灌输；重新启动|To emphasize or instill something again; to restart or reinvigorate.|例句：The teacher reprimed the students on the importance of following instructions.老师再次强调了遵守指令的重要性。|例句：The mechanic reprimed the engine after the repair. 机械师修理后重新启动了引擎。|近义词：reiterate, reinforce, reboot|反义词：neglect, ignore
