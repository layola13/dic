execute:/ˈɛksɪkjuːt/ | /ˈɛɡzɪkjuːt/ | vt. |执行；实行；处死；完成 |To carry out, accomplish, or fulfill (a task, plan, or instruction).  To put to death as a legal punishment. |例句：The government will execute its plans to improve the infrastructure. 政府将执行其改善基础设施的计划。|例句：He was executed for murder. 他因谋杀被处死。|近义词：perform, carry out, implement, enact, put to death |反义词：cancel, postpone, abort, pardon
