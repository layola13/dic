miscalculating: /ˌmɪskælˈkjuleɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 算错；误算；估计错误 |  To make a wrong calculation or estimate.  It implies an error in judgment or a lack of precision in the calculation. |

例句：
He was miscalculating the opponent's strength. = 他低估了对手的实力。

They were miscalculating the time it would take to complete the project. = 他们误判了完成项目所需的时间。


近义词：underestimating, overestimating, misjudging, erring


反义词：calculating accurately, correctly estimating


词性：动词 (verb)

词源:
mis- (错误的) + calculate (计算) + -ing (现在分词)
The prefix "mis-" indicates a wrong or bad action, while "calculate" refers to the process of making a mathematical computation.  The "-ing" suffix forms the present participle, indicating an ongoing action or state.
