illimitable:/ɪˈlɪmɪtəbl/| adj. |无限的；无限制的；不可估量的 |without limit; boundless; immeasurable |The universe is illimitable in its vastness. 宇宙无限广阔。His ambition seemed illimitable. 他的雄心壮志似乎无限大。|近义词：boundless, limitless, infinite, immeasurable |反义词：limited, finite, restricted
