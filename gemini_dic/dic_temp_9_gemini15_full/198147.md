shopped:/ʃɑːpt/| v.|购物，购买|To have gone to shops to buy things.|例句：I shopped for groceries this morning. = 我今天早上买了食品杂货。|例句：She shopped online for a new dress. = 她在网上买了一件新裙子。|近义词：bought, purchased|反义词：sold


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I shop at that store regularly. = 我经常在那家商店购物。
They shop for clothes online. = 他们在网上买衣服。

一般过去时:
We shopped for hours yesterday. = 我们昨天逛了几个小时的商店。
He shopped at the mall and bought a new phone. = 他在商场购物并买了一部新手机。

现在完成时:
I have shopped at many different stores. = 我在许多不同的商店购物过。
She has shopped online for years. = 她网上购物已经好多年了。

过去完成时:
We had shopped all day before we finally found what we needed. = 我们逛了一整天之后才最终找到我们需要的东西。
They had shopped at the market before coming to the picnic. = 他们去野餐之前已经在市场上买过东西了。

将来时:
I will shop for my birthday present tomorrow. = 我明天将去买我的生日礼物。
We will shop online next week. = 我们下周将在网上购物。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would shop at more expensive stores. = 如果我有更多的钱，我会在更贵的商店购物。
I wish I had shopped around before buying that car. = 我真希望在买那辆车之前多看看几家。

被动语态:
The goods were shopped carefully by the staff. = 商品由员工仔细挑选。  (This usage is less common, often replaced with "The goods were purchased...")
The items are being shopped for online by many customers. = 许多顾客正在网上选购商品。


疑问句:
Where did you shop for that dress? = 你在哪家商店买的这条裙子？
Do you shop online often? = 你经常网购吗？

否定句:
I didn't shop at all this week. = 我这周根本没购物。
They didn't shop at the farmers market. = 他们没去农贸市场购物。


PHRASE->
shop around:货比三家
shop till you drop:买到筋疲力尽
window shop:逛商店橱窗
shoplifter:商店扒手
online shopping:网上购物
