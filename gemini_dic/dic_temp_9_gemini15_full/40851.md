cooee:/ˈkuːiː/| int.| 喂；嘿；（招唤声）|A loud, prolonged call used to attract attention or signal someone at a distance.  It's often used in situations where other forms of communication are difficult. |Cooee! Over here!|近义词：hey, ho, yo|反义词：无


USAGE->

He cooees to his friend across the field. = 他对着田野对面的朋友大声呼喊。
She cooees to attract attention. = 她大声呼喊以引起注意。
