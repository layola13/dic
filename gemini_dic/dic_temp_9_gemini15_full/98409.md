insomnious:/ɪnˈsɒmniəs/| adj.|失眠的，睡不着的|suffering from insomnia; unable to sleep.|He's been insomnious for weeks, struggling to get even a few hours of rest.|近义词：sleepless, wakeful|反义词：somnolent, sleepy

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is insomnious tonight. = 他今晚睡不着。
She is always insomnious before big exams. = 她在大型考试前总是睡不着。

一般过去时:
I was insomnious last night. = 我昨晚睡不着。
He was insomnious after drinking too much coffee. = 他喝太多咖啡后睡不着。

现在完成时:
She has been insomnious for days. = 她已经失眠好几天了。
They have been insomnious since their child was born. = 自从他们的孩子出生以来，他们就一直睡不着。

过去完成时:
I had been insomnious for a week before I finally saw a doctor. = 在我最终去看医生之前，我已经失眠了一个星期。
She had been insomnious all her life before she found a solution. = 在她找到解决方法之前，她一生都睡不着。


将来时:
I think I will be insomnious tonight. = 我想我今晚会睡不着。
He fears he will be insomnious after that late-night coffee. = 他担心喝了夜咖啡后会睡不着。

虚拟语气:
If I were insomnious, I would try chamomile tea. = 如果我睡不着，我会尝试喝洋甘菊茶。
I wish I weren't insomnious tonight. = 我希望今晚我能睡着。

被动语态:
(This adjective is rarely used in the passive voice.  A better phrasing would focus on the subject's condition.)  例如：He is kept awake by stress. 他因压力而失眠。

疑问句:

Is he insomnious often? = 他经常睡不着吗？

否定句:
I am not insomnious tonight. = 我今晚睡得着。
She is not usually insomnious. = 她通常都能睡着。
