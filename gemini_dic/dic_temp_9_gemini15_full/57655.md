drawbar:/ˈdrɔːbɑːr/|/ˈdrɔːbɑːr/| n. |牵引杆；拉杆；制动杆|A drawbar is a bar or rod used to connect a tractor or other vehicle to a trailer or implement.  It's typically a strong, heavy-duty component designed to withstand significant pulling forces.|例句：The farmer attached the plow to the tractor's drawbar.  这位农民将犁连接到拖拉机的牵引杆上。|例句：The drawbar of the locomotive was heavily stressed during the climb.机车爬坡时牵引杆承受了巨大的压力。|近义词：tow bar, coupling bar, hitch|反义词：无
