hubbubs:/ˈhʌbʌbz/| n.|喧嚣；骚动；混乱|A hubbub is a situation in which there is a lot of noise and confusion.  It often refers to a loud, confused commotion. |例句：The announcement caused a hubbub in the crowd.  公告在人群中引起了一阵骚动。

例句：The market was filled with a hubbub of voices and activity. 市场里充满了喧闹的声音和活动。


近义词：commotion, uproar, turmoil, din, racket|反义词：silence, quiet, calm, peace


词性: 名词
