live-well:/laɪvˈwɛl/ | n. adj. |生活良好；过着舒适的生活；(尤指)健康长寿 |  A state of good health and well-being, often implying a comfortable and fulfilling life.  It can also refer to a philosophy or lifestyle focused on achieving this state. |例句：She enjoys a live-well retirement. = 她享受着舒适的退休生活。  They advocate for a live-well approach to aging. = 他们提倡一种健康长寿的养老方式。|近义词：well-being, healthy lifestyle, flourishing life |反义词：ill-health, suffering, poor lifestyle |名词，形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They live well. = 他们生活富裕。
He lives well despite his low income. = 尽管收入低，他生活得很好。


一般过去时:
They lived well in their youth. = 他们年轻时生活富裕。
She lived well despite many hardships. = 她尽管经历了许多苦难，但生活得很好。


现在完成时:
They have lived well for many years. = 他们多年来一直生活富裕。
We have lived well despite the economic downturn. = 尽管经济低迷，我们仍然生活得很好。


过去完成时:
They had lived well before the financial crisis. = 金融危机之前，他们生活富裕。
She had lived well, even during periods of poverty. = 她即使在贫困时期也生活得很好。


将来时:
They will live well in their new house. = 他们将在新房子里过上富裕的生活。
We will live well if we manage our finances wisely. = 如果我们明智地管理财务，我们将过上富裕的生活。


虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would live well. = 如果我有更多钱，我会过上富裕的生活。
I wish I could live well without working so hard. = 我希望不用那么努力工作也能过上富裕的生活。


被动语态:
(This word doesn't easily lend itself to passive voice.  A related phrase like "a life well-lived" could be used passively, but "live-well" itself is rarely used passively.)  A life well-lived is often envied. =  幸福美满的人生常常令人羡慕。


疑问句:
Do they live well? = 他们生活富裕吗？
How do they manage to live well on such a small income? = 他们靠这么少的收入如何过上富裕的生活？


否定句:
They don't live well. = 他们生活不富裕。
He didn't live well during his childhood. = 他童年时期生活不富裕。


PHRASE->
Live well, age well = 健康长寿地生活
Live well, laugh often = 活得精彩，常笑常乐
Live well, love much = 活得精彩，多多爱
To live well is to live healthily =  过得好就是健康地生活
Live well and prosper = 生活愉快，一切顺利
