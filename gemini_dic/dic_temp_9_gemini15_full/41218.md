coral:/ˈkɒrəl/ /ˈkɔːrəl/| n. |珊瑚，珊瑚虫|Coral is a hard, stony substance secreted by certain marine polyps.  It forms reefs and islands in tropical waters.|The Great Barrier Reef is the world's largest coral reef system.|近义词：reef, polyps|反义词：无
