perlitic:/pərˈlɪtɪk/ | adj. |珍珠状的，珍珠岩状的 |  Describing a texture in igneous rocks characterized by a glassy, concentrically fractured appearance resembling a string of pearls.  This texture forms during rapid cooling of lava, resulting in the fracturing. | The obsidian showed a perlitic texture. 这块黑曜岩呈现珍珠岩状结构。|  glassy, spherulitic | crystalline  |
