signboard:/ˈsaɪnbaʊrd/ | n. |招牌，路标 | A signboard is a board or structure on which signs are displayed, usually outdoors, to advertise or give directions.  | Example: The signboard advertised the local restaurant's daily specials.  这个招牌宣传了当地餐馆每日的特色菜。  |近义词：sign, billboard, notice board | 反义词：N/A
