luringly:/ˈlʊərɪŋli/| adv.|诱人地，吸引地，迷人地|In a way that is attractive and tempting.  It suggests something is appealing, often in a subtle or deceptive way.|例句：The aroma of freshly baked bread lured customers into the bakery luringly. 新鲜烘焙面包的香味诱人地吸引顾客进入面包店。

例句：The brightly colored flowers lured the butterflies luringly.色彩鲜艳的花朵诱人地吸引着蝴蝶。


近义词：attractively, temptingly, enticingly, captivatingly|反义词：repulsively, unattractively, repulsively


词性:副词
