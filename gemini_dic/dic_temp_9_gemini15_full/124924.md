mudsling:/ˈmʌdˌslɪŋɪŋ/ | n. v. | 诽谤；污蔑；造谣 | The act of spreading malicious rumors or making unfair criticisms about someone.  |例句：The campaign became a mudslinging match, with both candidates attacking each other's character. 这场竞选活动变成了互相诽谤的比赛，双方候选人都攻击对方的品格。

例句：He was accused of mudslinging against his opponent. 他被指控诽谤他的对手。


近义词：slander, vilify, defame, smear, calumniate | 反义词：praise, commend, flatter
