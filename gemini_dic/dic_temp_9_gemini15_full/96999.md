infantry:/ˈɪnfəntri/| n.|步兵，步兵部队|Foot soldiers; soldiers who fight on foot, as opposed to cavalry or armored forces.  The term often refers to a branch of the military. |例句：The infantry advanced towards the enemy lines. 步兵向敌军防线推进。
The infantry regiment suffered heavy casualties during the battle.  该步兵团在战斗中伤亡惨重。|近义词：foot soldiers, ground troops|反义词：cavalry, air force, navy
