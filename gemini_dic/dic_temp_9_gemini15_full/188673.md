rumpish:/ˈrʌmpɪʃ/| adj.|脾气暴躁的；倔强的；任性的|Characterized by or given to fits of anger or sulkiness; wayward or unruly.|例句：The child was rumpish and refused to eat his dinner.  这个孩子脾气暴躁，拒绝吃晚饭。

例句：Her rumpish behavior made it difficult to work with her. 她的任性行为使得和她一起工作变得困难。

近义词：irritable, petulant, fractious, wayward, unruly

反义词：docile, amiable, agreeable, compliant, well-behaved


词源：源自于 rump (臀部) ，暗示着像一头犟驴一样固执地扭动屁股。这个词的含义随着时间的推移发生了演变，从最初的“粗鲁的”、“不雅观的”发展到现在的“脾气暴躁的”、“任性的”。  The original meaning likely referred to a stubborn, obstinate behavior, like that of an animal stubbornly refusing to move.
