sermons:/ˈsɜːrmənz/ | n. |布道；说教 | A sermon is a religious discourse delivered as part of a worship service.  It typically involves interpretation of scripture and application of religious teachings to daily life.  | 例句：The pastor delivered a powerful sermon on forgiveness. 牧师发表了一篇关于宽恕的有力布道。

The priest's sermon lasted for over an hour.  这位牧师的布道持续了一个多小时。|近义词：preaching, lecture, discourse |反义词：None specifically, but antonyms could relate to the *content* of a sermon, such as  blasphemy, heresy. |名词
