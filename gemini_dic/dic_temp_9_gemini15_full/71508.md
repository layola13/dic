flankers:/ˈflæŋkərz/| n.|侧翼攻击者，侧翼产品|A flanker is a product or brand positioned to compete with established brands, often by offering a similar product at a lower price or with a different feature set.| 例句：The new flanker brand quickly gained market share. 新推出的侧翼品牌迅速获得了市场份额。|近义词：competitor, rival, challenger |反义词：leader, dominant brand
