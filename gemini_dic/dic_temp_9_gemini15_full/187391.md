rods:/rɒdz/ /rɑːdz/| n.|竿；杆状物；视杆细胞|A rod is a long, thin, straight piece of wood, metal, or other material.  It can also refer to a visual receptor cell in the retina.|例句：He used a fishing rod to catch a trout. 他用钓鱼竿钓到一条鳟鱼。

例句：The photoreceptors in the eye are called rods and cones. 眼睛中的感光细胞被称为视杆细胞和视锥细胞。


近义词：stick, pole, bar, shaft|反义词：None particularly fitting|词性：n.
