词源:
The word "chisel-like" is a compound adjective.  It combines the noun "chisel" (a tool used for shaping stone, wood, etc.) with the suffix "-like," indicating resemblance.  The word "chisel" itself has origins in Old French "ciseau" and ultimately from Latin "scissare" (to cut).
