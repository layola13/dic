infector:/ɪnˈfɛktər/ | n. |感染者，感染源 | A person or thing that infects.  Someone or something that transmits a disease or harmful program. | Example: The infected patient was quickly isolated to prevent him from becoming an infector.  感染的病人被迅速隔离以防止他成为感染源。  The malicious software acted as an infector, spreading rapidly across the network.  恶意软件充当感染源，迅速在网络上传播。|近义词：carrier, transmitter, agent |反义词：cure, remedy
