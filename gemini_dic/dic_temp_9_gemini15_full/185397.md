retrorse:/ˈrɛtrɔːrs/| adj.|反向的，向后的|Bent or directed backward or downward.  The term is often used to describe hairs,  scales, or spines that point backward. |例句：The hairs on the plant's stem are retrorse, pointing downwards.  植物茎上的毛是反向的，指向下方。

The leaves of this species exhibit retrorse hairs along their margins.  这个物种的叶子边缘有反向的毛。


近义词：backward, reversed, reflexed|反义词：protrorse, antrorse
