nostalgia:/nɒˈstældʒə/|n.|怀旧；乡愁|A sentimental longing or wistful affection for the past, typically for a period or place with happy personal associations.|例句：She felt a pang of nostalgia for her childhood home. 她对童年家园感到一阵怀旧之情。

例句：The old photograph evoked a flood of nostalgia. 这张旧照片唤起了汹涌的怀旧之情。

近义词：longing, yearning, wistfulness, reminiscence|反义词：present, contemporary

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He feels nostalgia for his college days. 他怀念他的大学时光。
She often experiences nostalgia when listening to old songs. 她听老歌时经常会感到怀旧。

一般过去时:
I felt a deep nostalgia for my hometown after moving abroad.  搬到国外后，我深深地怀念我的家乡。
They talked about their nostalgia for the carefree days of youth. 他们谈论起对无忧无虑青春岁月的怀念。


现在完成时:
I have had a lot of nostalgia for my childhood recently.  最近我非常怀念我的童年。
She has felt nostalgia for her old school since graduation.  毕业后她就一直怀念她的旧学校。


过去完成时:
By the time he returned, he had developed a strong nostalgia for his native land.  在他返回的时候，他已经对他的故乡产生了强烈的怀旧之情。
Before the trip, she had already been experiencing nostalgia for her family. 旅行之前，她就已经开始怀念她的家人了。


将来时:
I think I will feel nostalgia for this place when I leave. 我想离开这里的时候我会怀念这个地方的。
He believes he will have nostalgia for his current job once he retires. 他相信退休后他会怀念现在的工作。


虚拟语气:
If I had stayed longer, I would have felt more nostalgia. 如果我多待一段时间，我会更加怀念。
I wish I hadn't moved; I would not have experienced so much nostalgia. 我真希望我没有搬家；我就不会经历这么多的怀旧之情了。


被动语态:
Nostalgia is often triggered by sensory experiences.  怀旧之情常常是由感官体验触发的。
His feelings of nostalgia were powerfully evoked by the old music. 他强烈的怀旧之情是被古老的音乐唤起的。


疑问句:
Do you ever feel nostalgia for your childhood? 你曾经怀念过你的童年吗？
Does he experience nostalgia for his former life? 他怀念他以前的生活吗？


否定句:
I don't feel any nostalgia for my old job. 我对我的旧工作没有任何怀念之情。
She didn't express much nostalgia for her past. 她没有表达出多少对过去的怀旧之情。
