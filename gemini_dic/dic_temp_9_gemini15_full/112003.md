lonesomely:/ˈloʊnsəmli/ | adv. | 孤独地；寂寞地 |  In a lonely manner; feeling or showing loneliness.  | He sat lonesomely by the fire. 他孤独地坐在火炉旁。  She looked lonesomely out the window. 她孤独地望着窗外。 | sadly, forlornly, desolately | merrily, happily, cheerfully | 副词
