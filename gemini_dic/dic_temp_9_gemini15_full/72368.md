flows:/floʊz/| n. v.|流动；流畅；流程|The continuous movement of a liquid, gas, or electricity.|例句：The river flows swiftly. 河水流得很快。  The traffic flows smoothly this morning. 今早交通很流畅。|近义词：streams, currents, circulates|反义词：stagnates, stops


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The river flows constantly. = 河水不断地流动。
The blood flows through our veins. = 血液流经我们的血管。

一般过去时:
The water flowed rapidly after the rain. = 雨后水流得很快。
The lava flowed down the mountainside. = 岩浆顺着山坡流下。

现在完成时:
The river has flowed over its banks. = 河水已经漫过河岸。
The water has flowed into the reservoir. = 水已经流入水库。

过去完成时:
The river had flowed for miles before reaching the sea. = 河水在到达大海之前已经流淌了数英里。
The machine had flowed smoothly until the power outage. = 机器在停电之前一直运行平稳。

将来时:
The river will flow faster in the spring. = 春天河水会流得更快。
The water will flow through the pipes. = 水将流过管道。

虚拟语气:
If the river flowed faster, it would erode the banks more quickly. = 如果河水流得更快，它就会更快地侵蚀河岸。
If the electricity flowed constantly, the machine would run smoothly.=如果电力持续供应，机器就能平稳运行。

被动语态:
The water is flowed into the tank. = 水被流入水箱。(This is grammatically questionable.  Use "The water is being channeled into the tank" instead.)
The liquid is being flowed through the pipe. = 液体正在通过管道流动。(This is grammatically questionable. Use "The liquid is flowing through the pipe" instead.)


疑问句:
Does the river flow all year round? = 河水一年四季都流吗？
How does the current flow? = 电流是如何流动的？

否定句:
The river doesn't flow during the dry season. = 干旱季节河水不流。
The traffic didn't flow well because of the accident. = 因为事故，交通不畅。
