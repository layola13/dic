nonnationalizations: /ˌnɒnˌnæʃənəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非国有化；非民族化 | The absence or reversal of nationalization; the process of returning state-owned assets to private ownership or control.  It often involves privatization of industries or resources previously under government control. | Example: The government's policies promoted nonnationalizations across various sectors. 政府的政策促进了各个行业的非国有化。  The recent nonnationalizations in the telecommunications industry led to increased competition.  电信行业的近期非国有化导致了竞争加剧。 | 近义词：privatization, deregulation, denationalization | 反义词：nationalization, socialization
