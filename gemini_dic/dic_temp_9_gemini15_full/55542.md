distributives:/dɪˈstrɪbjʊtɪvz/| adj.| 分配的；分布的 |  Relating to or characterized by distribution or the act of distributing something.  Distributives are words or phrases that refer to members of a group individually. | 例句：The company's distributive network spans the globe.  这家公司的分销网络遍布全球。
例句：Each, every, and either are examples of distributive pronouns.  每个，每一个和两者都是分配代词的例子。|近义词：dispersing, apportioning, allocating |反义词：collective, centralized |词性:形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company distributes its products globally. = 公司在全球范围内分销其产品。
The system distributes the workload evenly. = 系统平均分配工作负载。


一般过去时:
They distributed flyers throughout the city. = 他们在全城散发传单。
The teacher distributed the tests to the students. = 老师把试卷分发给学生们。


现在完成时:
The organization has distributed aid to the victims. = 该组织已向受害者分发了援助物资。
We have distributed the marketing materials online. = 我们已在线分发营销材料。


过去完成时:
They had distributed all the packages before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前已分发所有包裹。
She had distributed the questionnaires before the meeting started. = 会议开始前，她已分发了问卷调查表。


将来时:
We will distribute the profits among the shareholders. = 我们将把利润分配给股东。
The government will distribute emergency supplies. = 政府将分发紧急物资。


虚拟语气:
If we distributed the resources more efficiently, we would achieve better results. = 如果我们更有效地分配资源，我们将取得更好的成果。
I wish they had distributed the information more clearly. = 我希望他们能更清晰地分发信息。


被动语态:
The leaflets were distributed across the town. = 传单在城里被分发。
The funds were distributed fairly among the participants. = 资金在参与者之间公平分配。


疑问句:
How will the resources be distributed? = 资源将如何分配？
Where were the invitations distributed? = 请柬在哪里分发？


否定句:
They did not distribute the reports on time. = 他们没有按时分发报告。
The company hasn't distributed the dividends yet. = 公司尚未分发股息。
