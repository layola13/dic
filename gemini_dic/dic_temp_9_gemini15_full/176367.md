re-form:/ˌriːˈfɔːrm/| vt. n.| 改革；重组；再形成；改造 | To change something to improve it; to reorganize something;  to form again. |例句：The government is planning to reform the healthcare system. 政府计划改革医疗体系。

The sculptor reformed the clay into a new shape. 雕塑家将黏土重新塑造成新的形状。

近义词：reorganize, restructure, revamp, improve,  renovate | 反义词：destroy, ruin, corrupt, worsen | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reform the education system every year. =他们每年都改革教育体系。
The company reforms its marketing strategies regularly. =公司定期改革其营销策略。

一般过去时:
The government reformed the tax laws last year. =政府去年改革了税法。
The artist reformed the painting after receiving criticism. =艺术家在受到批评后重新创作了这幅画。

现在完成时:
The country has reformed its constitution several times. =这个国家已经几次修改了宪法。
They have reformed their business practices to improve efficiency. =他们已经改革了业务流程以提高效率。

过去完成时:
They had reformed the company structure before the merger. =他们在合并之前已经改革了公司结构。
The committee had reformed its policies following the scandal. =在丑闻发生后，委员会已经改革了其政策。

将来时:
The president will reform the criminal justice system. =总统将改革刑事司法系统。
The company plans to reform its production processes next year. =公司计划明年改革其生产流程。

虚拟语气:
If they reformed their methods, they would see better results. =如果他们改革方法，他们会看到更好的结果。
I wish they would reform their wasteful spending habits. =我希望他们能改掉浪费的消费习惯。

被动语态:
The education system is being reformed. =教育体系正在改革中。
The company's structure was reformed last month. =公司结构在上个月进行了改革。

疑问句:
Will the government reform the social security system? =政府会改革社会保障体系吗？
Is the company planning to reform its operations? =公司计划改革其运营吗？

否定句:
The government did not reform the healthcare system. =政府没有改革医疗体系。
The company has not reformed its outdated policies. =公司没有改革其过时的政策。
