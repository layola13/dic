oats:/oʊts/| n.|燕麦，麦片|Oats are a type of cereal grain.  They are a staple food in many parts of the world and are used to make porridge, oatmeal, and other foods. | 例句：I had a bowl of oatmeal for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一碗燕麦粥。| 例句：Rolled oats are a good source of fiber. 卷燕麦是纤维的好来源。|近义词：grain, cereal|反义词：无
