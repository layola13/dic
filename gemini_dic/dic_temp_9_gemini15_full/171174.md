psychologic:/ˌsaɪkəˈlɒdʒɪk/ /ˌsaɪkəˈlɑːdʒɪk/| adj.|心理的；心理学的；精神的|Relating to the mind and its functions.  It often refers to mental or emotional processes rather than physical ones.  The term can be used interchangeably with "psychological" in many contexts.|例句：His psychologic state was deteriorating rapidly. 他的心理状态迅速恶化。

例句：The psychologic impact of the war on the soldiers was profound.  战争对士兵们的心理影响是深远的。


近义词：psychological, mental, emotional

反义词：physical, bodily


词性：形容词
