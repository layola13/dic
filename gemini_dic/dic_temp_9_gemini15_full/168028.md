prithee:/ˈprɪðiː/| int.|请，求你|A polite or archaic way of asking someone to do something.  It is an obsolete form of "I pray thee".| Prithee, lend me your ear. 请你听我说。|近义词：please, I beg you |反义词：None


USAGE->
(Prithee, as an interjection, doesn't have verb conjugations like "to be" or "to do".  It's used as a single word request.)


PHRASE->
(Prithee doesn't typically form common phrases. Its usage is as a standalone word.)
