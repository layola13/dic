meagerly:/ˈmiːɡərli/ | adv. | 贫乏地；少量地；可怜地 | In a meager manner; scantily; poorly.  It describes actions or states performed or existing with little quantity or quality. |例句：They meagerly supplied the refugees with food and water. = 他们可怜兮兮地向难民提供食物和水。


They ate meagerly, barely enough to survive. = 他们吃得很少，勉强够活。| 近义词：scantyly, sparingly, thinly, poorly | 反义词：abundantly, lavishly, generously | 词性：副词
