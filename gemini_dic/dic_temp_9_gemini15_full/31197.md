changet:/tʃeɪndʒ/ | n. vt.|改变，变化；零钱|To make or become different.|例句：The weather has changed. 天气变了。  The company is undergoing a major change. 公司正在经历重大变革。|近义词：alter, modify, transform|反义词：maintain, preserve


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He changes his mind frequently. = 他经常改变主意。
The color of the leaves changes in autumn. = 秋天树叶的颜色会变。


一般过去时:
She changed her clothes before going out. = 她出门前换了衣服。
The company changed its policy last year. = 公司去年改变了政策。


现在完成时:
They have changed a lot since I last saw them. = 自从我上次见到他们以来，他们变化很大。
The city has changed dramatically over the years. = 这些年来，这座城市发生了巨大的变化。


过去完成时:
He had changed his mind before I could persuade him. = 我还没来得及劝他，他就已经改变主意了。
The situation had changed significantly by the time we arrived. = 等我们到达时，情况已经发生了重大变化。


将来时:
The company will change its strategy next year. = 公司明年将改变战略。
I think the weather will change tomorrow. = 我想明天的天气会变。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would change my approach. = 如果我是你，我会改变我的方法。
I wish I could change the past. = 我希望我能改变过去。


被动语态:
The plan was changed at the last minute. = 计划在最后一刻被改变了。
The rules have been changed recently. = 规则最近被改变了。


疑问句:
Has the situation changed? = 情况改变了吗？
What has changed? = 发生了什么变化？


否定句:
Nothing has changed. = 什么都没变。
They didn't change their plans. = 他们没有改变他们的计划。


PHRASE->
change one's mind = 改变主意
change the subject = 转移话题
change gears = 改变策略
bring about change = 带来改变
undergo a change = 经历改变
