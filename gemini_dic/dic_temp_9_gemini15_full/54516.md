disjunction:/dɪsˈdʒʌŋkʃən/ | n. |析取；分离；脱节；不相容 | The logical operation that gives a true output if at least one of its inputs is true.  Disjunction can also refer to a break or separation between things. | Example: The disjunction of "A" and "B" is true if either "A" or "B" (or both) is true.  逻辑运算中，如果至少有一个输入为真，则输出为真。析取还可以指事物之间的断裂或分离。 例句：如果“A”或“B”（或两者）为真，则“A”和“B”的析取为真。|近义词：separation, division, disconnection, inconsistency |反义词：conjunction, connection, agreement, consistency |名词
