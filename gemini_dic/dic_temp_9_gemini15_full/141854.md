orchestras:/ˈɔːrkɪstrəz/ | n. |管弦乐队（复数） |A group of instrumental musicians playing together, typically including stringed instruments, woodwinds, brass, and percussion.| 例句：Many famous orchestras performed at the festival. 许多著名的管弦乐队都在音乐节上演出。  The city boasts several renowned orchestras. 这个城市拥有几支著名的管弦乐队。|近义词：bands, ensembles|反义词：soloists, individual musicians
