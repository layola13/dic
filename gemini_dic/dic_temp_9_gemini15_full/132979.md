nonendemic: /ˌnɒnɛnˈdɛmɪk/ | adj. | 非地方性的；非流行性的 | Not native or endemic to a particular region or population; not habitually present.  It describes something that is not regularly found in a specific area or group.  | 例句：The disease is nonendemic in this region, meaning it's not typically found here.  这种疾病在这个地区是非地方性的，这意味着它通常不会在这里发现。

The introduction of the nonendemic species disrupted the local ecosystem.  非本地物种的引入扰乱了当地生态系统。


近义词：exotic, introduced, alien, foreign | 反义词：endemic, native, indigenous

词性: adjective
