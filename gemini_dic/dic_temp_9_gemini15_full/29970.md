caviares:/kæviˈɑːrz/| n.|鱼子酱|Caviar is the processed roe of fish, most commonly the sturgeon.  It is known for its delicate flavor and luxurious nature, often served as a delicacy.|例句：The restaurant is famous for its exquisite caviar service. 这家餐厅以其精致的鱼子酱服务而闻名。|例句：She enjoyed a spoonful of the expensive caviar. 她享用了一勺昂贵的鱼子酱。|近义词：roe (鱼卵), delicacy (美味佳肴)|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with inexpensive foods.
