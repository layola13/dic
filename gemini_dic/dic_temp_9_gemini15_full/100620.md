intimidation: /ɪnˌtɪmɪˈdeɪʃən/ | n. | 恐吓；威胁；胁迫 | The act of intimidating or the state of being intimidated; the use of threats to influence someone's actions.  |例句：The intimidation tactics used by the bully caused many students to be afraid to speak up.  霸凌者使用的恐吓战术导致许多学生不敢大声说话。

例句：He used intimidation to get what he wanted from his employees. 他用恐吓手段从员工那里得到他想要的东西。


近义词：threat, coercion, bullying, harassment, pressure

反义词：encouragement, support, reassurance
