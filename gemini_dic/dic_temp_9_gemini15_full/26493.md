cabalistic:/ˌkæbəˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|神秘的；卡巴拉的；|relating to the Kabbalah or its mystical interpretations.  The term often implies secrecy and an esoteric system of beliefs or practices.|例句：The ancient text contained cabalistic symbols that baffled scholars for centuries.  古代文本包含令学者困惑几个世纪的神秘符号。

例句：He interpreted the poem's meaning in a cabalistic way, finding hidden layers of symbolism. 他以神秘的方式解读了这首诗的含义，发现了隐藏的象征层级。


近义词：mystical, esoteric, occult, cryptic

反义词：clear, straightforward, explicit
