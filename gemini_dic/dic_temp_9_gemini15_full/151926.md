pasteurizes:/ˈpæs.tʃə.raɪz/| vt.|巴氏消毒；使……经受巴氏消毒法处理|To heat (a liquid, such as milk) to a specific temperature for a specific period to destroy harmful microorganisms without significant alteration of the liquid's qualities.|例句：The milk is pasteurized to kill harmful bacteria. 这牛奶经过巴氏消毒杀死了有害细菌。

例句：The company pasteurizes all its juices before bottling them.  这家公司在装瓶前对其所有果汁进行巴氏消毒。


近义词：sterilize (although sterilization is a more complete process)|反义词：contaminate
