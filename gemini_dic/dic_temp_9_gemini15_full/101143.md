investment:/ɪnˈvɛstmənt/| n. |投资；投入；投资额 |The act of investing money or capital in something, with the expectation of achieving a profit or other benefit.  It can refer to money, time, or effort put into something with the hope of future gain. |例句：He made a wise investment in real estate. 他在房地产上做了一笔明智的投资。

例句：Her investment of time and energy finally paid off. 她投入的时间和精力最终得到了回报。|近义词：expenditure, outlay, placement |反义词：withdrawal, divestment |名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He invests in the stock market. = 他投资股市。
She invests her time in her children. = 她把时间花在孩子身上。

一般过去时:
They invested heavily in new technology. = 他们大量投资新技术。
He invested all his savings in the business. = 他把所有积蓄都投资到这个生意上了。

现在完成时:
We have invested a lot of money in research and development. = 我们已经投入大量资金用于研发。
She has invested wisely over the years. = 这些年来她的投资很明智。

过去完成时:
They had invested all their capital before the market crashed. = 在市场崩盘之前，他们已经投资了全部资本。
He had invested in that company for five years before selling his shares. = 在卖掉股份之前，他已经在那家公司投资了五年。

将来时:
We will invest more in renewable energy. = 我们将更多地投资可再生能源。
They will invest in a new factory next year. = 他们明年将投资建一个新工厂。

虚拟语气:
If I had more money, I would invest in a small business. = 如果我有更多钱，我会投资一家小企业。
I wish I had invested in that company earlier. = 我真希望我早点投资那家公司。

被动语态:
Millions of dollars were invested in the project. = 数百万美元投资于该项目。
The money is being invested in infrastructure development. = 这笔钱正在投资于基础设施建设。

疑问句:
Where should I invest my money? = 我应该把钱投资在哪里？
How much have you invested in this project? = 你在这个项目中投资了多少钱？

否定句:
He did not invest in the stock market. = 他没有投资股市。
They haven't invested any money in advertising. = 他们没有在广告上投资任何资金。
