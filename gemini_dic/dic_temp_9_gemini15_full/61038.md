The elixation of the rare ingredients resulted in a powerful potion. 稀有成分的提取物产生了一种强大的药剂。|近义词：preparation, concoction, extraction |反义词：None specifically, but words implying destruction or discarding of medicinal ingredients could be considered antonyms.  |词性 n.
