imperceptibly:/ɪmˈpɜːsɛptəbli/ | adj. | 难以察觉地，不知不觉地 |  In a way that is so subtle or gradual as to be almost impossible to notice.  | The temperature was imperceptibly rising.  气温在不知不觉中上升了。|gradually, subtly, insensibly | perceptibly, noticeably
