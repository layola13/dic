counterpetitioned: /ˌkaʊntərˈpɛtɪʃənd/ | v. | 反对请愿；提出反请愿 |  To file a counter-petition against a petition; to oppose a petition with a contrary petition.  | Example: The residents counterpetitioned the city council's proposal to build a new highway.  居民们对市议会建造一条新高速公路的提案提出了反请愿。 | 近义词：opposed, protested, counteracted | 反义词：petitioned, supported
