diaheliotropic: /ˌdaɪˌhiːliəˈtroʊpɪk/ | adj. | 向光性的；趋日的 |  Relating to or characterized by diaheliotropism, the movement of plant parts in response to the sun, specifically the orientation of leaves perpendicular to the sun's rays to minimize direct sunlight exposure during the hottest part of the day.  |  Many desert plants exhibit diaheliotropic leaf movements to reduce water loss. 许多沙漠植物表现出向光性叶片运动以减少水分流失。 | 近义词: heliotropic (less specific, encompassing both paraheliotropism and diaheliotropism) | 反义词: paraheliotropic
