eves:/iːvz/| n.|前夕；傍晚；(尤指圣诞节等的)前夜|The word "eves" is the plural form of "eve," meaning the evening or day before a special day or event, especially a religious holiday.  It often refers to the period of anticipation and preparation before a significant occasion.|例句：Christmas Eve is a time for family gatherings. 圣诞节前夕是家人团聚的时候。The eves of the election were filled with tension. 选举前夕充满了紧张气氛。|近义词：evenings, nights, vigils|反义词：mornings, days
