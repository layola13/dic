inevidence:/ˈɪnˈɛvɪdəns/ | adj. | 不明显的；未被发现的；未被证明的 | Not evident; not apparent; unproven.  | The detective found clues that were in evidence but seemingly insignificant.  侦探发现了些不明显的线索，但似乎微不足道。The in evidence nature of the crime made it difficult to solve. 犯罪的隐蔽性使得破案很困难。|  evident, apparent, manifest | evident, obvious, clear
