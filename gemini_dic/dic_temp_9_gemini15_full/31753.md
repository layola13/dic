cheater:/ˈtʃiːtər/| n. |作弊者；骗子|A person who cheats.  Someone who acts dishonestly in order to gain an advantage. |例句：He's a cheater; he cheated on the exam. 他是个作弊者；他在考试中作弊了。|例句：She's a cheater; she stole money from her company. 她是个骗子；她从公司偷了钱。|近义词：deceiver, fraud, swindler, trickster|反义词：honest person, truthful person|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cheats on tests. = 他考试作弊。
She cheats at cards. = 她打牌作弊。
