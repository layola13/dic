manifesto:/ˌmænɪˈfɛstoʊ/| n. |宣言；声明；纲领 |A public declaration of intentions, motives, or views, especially one issued by a political party or other group.  A manifesto is a written statement declaring publicly the intentions, motives, or views of an individual, group, or political party. It often outlines the principles and aims that will guide their actions.  |Example: The Communist Manifesto, written by Marx and Engels, outlined their vision for a classless society.  共产主义宣言，由马克思和恩格斯撰写，概述了他们对无阶级社会的愿景。|近义词：declaration, proclamation, program, platform |反义词：secrecy, concealment |名词
