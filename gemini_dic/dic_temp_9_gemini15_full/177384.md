reauthorize:/ˌriːˈɔːθəraɪz/| vt.|重新授权；再次授权|To authorize again; to give authority or power to someone or something once more.|The Congress will reauthorize the program next year.  国会明年将重新授权该项目。

The bill was reauthorized for another five years. 这项法案又获得五年授权。|近义词：renew, reinstate, revalidate|反义词：revoke, cancel, invalidate|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reauthorize the project annually. 他们每年都会重新授权该项目。
The committee reauthorizes the funding every two years. 委员会每两年重新授权一次资金。


一般过去时:
Congress reauthorized the spending bill last month. 国会在上个月重新授权了该支出法案。
The president reauthorized the military operation. 总统重新授权了军事行动。


现在完成时:
The government has reauthorized the emergency powers. 政府已经重新授权了紧急权力。
They have reauthorized the use of the technology. 他们已经重新授权使用这项技术。


过去完成时:
The board had reauthorized the contract before the scandal broke. 丑闻爆发前，董事会已重新授权了合同。
They had reauthorized the funding before the project started. 项目开始前，他们已经重新授权了资金。


将来时:
The legislature will reauthorize the program next year. 立法机构明年将重新授权该项目。
We expect they will reauthorize the treaty soon. 我们预计他们很快就会重新授权该条约。


虚拟语气:
If they reauthorized the funding, we could continue the research. 如果他们重新授权资金，我们就可以继续研究。
I wish they would reauthorize the program. 我希望他们能重新授权该项目。


被动语态:
The program was reauthorized by the Senate. 该项目经参议院重新授权。
The funds were reauthorized for another fiscal year. 资金被重新授权用于下一个财政年度。


疑问句:
Will the committee reauthorize the funding? 委员会会重新授权资金吗？
Has the president reauthorized the use of force? 总统是否重新授权使用武力？


否定句:
The agency did not reauthorize the permit. 该机构没有重新授权许可证。
Congress refused to reauthorize the program. 国会拒绝重新授权该项目。


PHRASE->
reauthorize a law  重新授权一项法律
reauthorize a program 重新授权一个项目
reauthorize a contract  重新授权一份合同
reauthorize spending  重新授权支出
reauthorize the use of  重新授权使用


词源:
re- (prefix indicating repetition or again) + authorize (from Old French autoriser, from Latin auctor "author, originator")
