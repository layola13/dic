meringue:/məˈræŋ/| n. |蛋白酥皮，蛋白霜|A meringue is a sweet meringue made from whipped egg whites and sugar, often baked until crisp.  It is commonly used as a topping for pies and desserts. | Example: The lemon meringue pie was the highlight of the dessert menu. 这份甜点菜单的亮点是柠檬蛋白酥皮派。|近义词：foam, froth|反义词：无
