invective:/ɪnˈvektiv/| n. | 痛骂；恶言；辱骂 | Abusive or venomous language used to attack or denounce someone.  | The politician's speech was filled with invective against his opponents.  这位政治家的演讲充满了对对手的辱骂。 | abuse, diatribe, vilification, denunciation | praise, compliment | 名词

His speech is full of invective. = 他的演讲充满了辱骂。
The article contains a lot of invective. = 这篇文章包含许多辱骂。


一般过去时:
He hurled invective at his critics. = 他向他的批评者猛烈抨击。
