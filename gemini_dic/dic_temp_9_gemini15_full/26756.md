caeca:/ˈsiːkə/| n.|盲肠（复数）|The caeca are blind pouches in the digestive system of many animals, including birds, reptiles, and some mammals. They play a role in the digestion of cellulose and other plant matter. | 例句：The chicken's caeca were examined for parasites. 鸡的盲肠被检查是否有寄生虫。


近义词：blind pouches, intestinal diverticula|反义词：无|词性:名词
