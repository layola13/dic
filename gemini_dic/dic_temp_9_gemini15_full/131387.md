noncitation: /ˌnɒnˌsaɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 未引证；未加注；未被引用 | The absence of a citation or reference to a source.  It refers to something presented without acknowledging its source.  This can be unintentional, due to oversight, or intentional, potentially constituting plagiarism. | Example:  The professor noted the noncitation of sources in the student's paper. 教授注意到学生论文中没有引用来源。 | 近义词：unreferenced, undocumented, unattributed | 反义词：citation, reference
