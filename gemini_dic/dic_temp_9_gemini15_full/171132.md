psychoanalyser:/ˌsaɪkəʊˈænəlaɪzər/| n. |精神分析师| A psychoanalyst is a therapist who practices psychoanalysis, a type of psychotherapy that aims to treat mental disorders by investigating the interaction of conscious and unconscious elements in the mind and bringing repressed fears and conflicts into the conscious mind by techniques such as dream interpretation and free association.  |Example:  The psychoanalyst helped her understand the root of her anxiety.  精神分析师帮助她理解她焦虑的根源。|近义词：psychiatrist, therapist, analyst |反义词: N/A
