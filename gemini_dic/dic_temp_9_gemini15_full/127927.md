neon:/ˈniːɑːn/| n. |霓虹灯；氖|A gas that glows brightly when an electric current is passed through it;  also refers to the bright light produced by this gas, often used in advertising signs.|例句：The neon sign illuminated the night street.  霓虹灯照亮了夜晚的街道。|近义词：sign, light|反义词：darkness
