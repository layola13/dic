clock:/klɒk/ /klɑːk/| n. |钟，计时器|A clock is a device used to tell time.  It can be mechanical, digital, or analog. |例句：The clock on the wall is slow.  墙上的钟慢了。|例句： My grandfather has a beautiful antique clock. 我爷爷有一个漂亮的古董钟。|近义词：timepiece, watch|反义词：N/A

USAGE->

The clock ticks every second. = 钟表每秒钟滴答作响。
The clock shows the correct time. = 钟表显示正确的时间。

一般过去时:
The clock stopped working yesterday. = 钟表昨天停止工作了。
The clock struck twelve at midnight. = 钟表在午夜敲响了十二下。

现在完成时:
The clock has been running for ten years. = 这钟表已经运行十年了。
The clock has just struck three. = 钟表刚刚敲响三点。

The clock had already stopped before we left. = 我们离开之前，钟表就已经停了。
The clock had chimed the hour before the earthquake. = 地震发生前，钟表已经报时了。

The clock will need repairing soon. = 这钟表很快需要修理。

I wish the clock would work again. = 我希望钟表能再次运转。
