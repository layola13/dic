mesogastrium:/ˌmɛsoˈɡæstrɪəm/| n. |中肠系膜|The mesogastrium is the portion of the peritoneum connecting the stomach to the posterior abdominal wall.  It is a double-layered membrane that helps support and suspend the stomach in the abdominal cavity.  The mesogastrium also contains blood vessels, nerves, and lymphatic vessels that supply the stomach.|The mesogastrium plays a crucial role in the development of the abdominal organs. |近义词: greater omentum, lesser omentum |反义词:  None directly applicable.
