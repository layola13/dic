jamborees:/ˈdʒæmbəriːz/| n.|大型集会；狂欢节|A jamboree is a large gathering, typically one that is lively and festive, often associated with scouting or similar youth organizations.  It can also refer to any large, exciting, and boisterous gathering.|例句：The Boy Scouts are planning a huge jamboree this summer.  今年夏天童子军计划举行一次大型集会。

例句：The city hosted a lively jamboree to celebrate its anniversary. 这个城市举办了一场热闹的狂欢节来庆祝它的周年纪念。


近义词：festival, celebration, rally, gathering


反义词：solitude, quietude


词性：名词
