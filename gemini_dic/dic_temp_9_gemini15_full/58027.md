drogman:/ˈdrɒɡmən/ | n. |通事；译员；向导 |A drogman was an interpreter, especially one employed by merchants or travelers in the East. The term is now largely obsolete, replaced by "interpreter" or "translator."  |Example: The merchant hired a drogman to facilitate communication with the local traders.  商人雇佣了一名通事来方便与当地商人的沟通。|近义词：interpreter, translator, guide |反义词：N/A
