microcline:/ˈmaɪkrəklaɪn/| n. |微斜长石|A potassium feldspar mineral, usually white or pale-colored, that is a common constituent of igneous and metamorphic rocks.  It is a polymorph of orthoclase and is distinguished by its characteristic cross-hatched twinning under a microscope.|Example: The geologist identified microcline in the sample of granite.|近义词：orthoclase, feldspar|反义词：N/A
