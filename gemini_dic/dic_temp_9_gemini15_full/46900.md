darkest:/ˈdɑːrkɪst/| adj.|最黑暗的，最阴暗的|The most extreme degree of darkness. It can refer to physical darkness, or metaphorical darkness representing things like despair, ignorance, or secrecy.|例句：The darkest hour is just before the dawn.  = 黑夜最深之时，便是黎明之前。
The darkest secret was finally revealed. = 最黑暗的秘密最终被揭露了。|近义词：blackest, deepest, most obscure|反义词：brightest, lightest, clearest
