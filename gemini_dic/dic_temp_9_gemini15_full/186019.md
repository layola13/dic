rewidening: /ˌriːˈwaɪdnɪŋ/ | v. | 重新加宽；再次拓宽 | The act of making something wider again.  This word is the present participle of the verb "rewiden," which itself is a combination of "re-" (again) and "widen." It describes a process of expanding something that had previously been narrowed or constricted. |例句：The road is undergoing rewidening to accommodate the increased traffic. 这条路正在重新拓宽以适应增加的交通流量。


The rewidening of the river after the flood caused significant erosion. 洪水过后河道重新加宽造成了严重的侵蚀。 |近义词：rebroadening, expanding, enlarging |反义词：narrowing, constricting, reducing
