except:/ɪkˈsɛpt/ | preposition, conjunction, verb | 除…之外；除了；反对；不包括 | 英文解析：Except means not including; other than.  As a preposition, it introduces a phrase that specifies an exception. As a conjunction, it introduces a clause that expresses an exception. As a verb, it means to exclude or object to something. | 例句：Everyone came to the party except John. =除了约翰，大家都来了派对。  I like all fruits except bananas. =除了香蕉，我喜欢所有水果。| 近义词：excluding, barring, save, but, besides | 反义词：including, inclusive


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I eat everything except meat. = 我吃所有东西，除了肉。
She likes all colors except green. = 她喜欢所有颜色，除了绿色。

一般过去时:
He went everywhere except the museum. = 他去了所有地方，除了博物馆。
They did everything except call me. = 他们做了所有事，除了打电话给我。

现在完成时:
I have read all the books except this one. = 我读了所有书，除了这本。
She has tried all the desserts except the chocolate cake. = 她尝试了所有甜点，除了巧克力蛋糕。

过去完成时:
He had done everything except finish his report. = 他做了所有事，除了完成他的报告。
They had visited all the cities except Paris. = 他们参观了所有城市，除了巴黎。

将来时:
I will eat anything except seafood. = 我会吃任何东西，除了海鲜。
She will go anywhere except the beach. = 她会去任何地方，除了海滩。

虚拟语气:
I would do anything except lie. = 除非说谎，我什么都愿意做。
She would go anywhere except that dangerous place. = 除非去那个危险的地方，她去哪儿都行。

被动语态:
Everything was discussed except the budget. = 除预算外，所有事情都讨论了。
All the guests were invited except him. = 除他以外，所有客人都被邀请了。

疑问句:
Is there anything you dislike except broccoli? = 除了西兰花，你还有什么不喜欢吗？
Did anyone object except him? = 除了他，还有谁反对吗？

否定句:
There is nothing I like except pizza. = 除了披萨，我什么都不喜欢。
I don't eat anything except vegetables. = 我不吃任何东西，除了蔬菜。
