flop-down:/ˈflɒpdaʊn/| adj. v.| 突然倒下；瘫倒；|  Describes something that falls or collapses heavily and suddenly, often in a relaxed or ungraceful manner. It can be used as an adjective to describe the action or the state of being in such a position, or as a verb describing the act itself. |例句：He flopped down onto the sofa, exhausted. 他筋疲力尽地瘫倒在沙发上。

The dog flopped down in the shade.  狗在阴凉处瘫倒了。


近义词：collapse, slump, tumble, crash|反义词：stand up, rise, erect

He flops down on the couch every evening. 他每天晚上都瘫倒在沙发上。
She flops down her bag onto the floor. 她把包重重地扔到地板上。


一般过去时:
He flopped down on the bed after a long day. 长途跋涉后，他倒在床上。

疑问句:
