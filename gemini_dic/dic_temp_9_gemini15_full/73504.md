foreseen:/fɔːˈsiːn/| adj.| 预见的，预料到的 |  Able to be foreseen or predicted; anticipated.  |The consequences were foreseen by experts.  专家们预见了后果。| anticipated, predicted, expected | unforeseen, unexpected | 形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
We foresee potential problems. = 我们预见到潜在的问题。
They foresee a bright future for the company. = 他们预见公司拥有光明未来。

一般过去时:
I foresaw the difficulty. = 我预见到了困难。
She foresaw the storm approaching. = 她预见到暴风雨即将来临。

现在完成时:
We have foreseen many challenges. = 我们已经预见到许多挑战。
They have foreseen this outcome for some time. = 他们已经预见到这个结果有一段时间了。


过去完成时:
I had foreseen the need for extra staff. = 我预见到需要额外的人员。
They had foreseen the potential risks before investing. = 他们在投资前已经预见到潜在的风险。

将来时:
We will foresee the impact of this decision. = 我们将预见这个决定的影响。
They will foresee the consequences of their actions. = 他们将预见他们行为的后果。


虚拟语气:
If I had foreseen the consequences, I would have acted differently. = 如果我预见了后果，我会采取不同的行动。
If we had foreseen the crisis, we would have prepared better. = 如果我们预见了危机，我们会做好更好的准备。


被动语态:
The difficulties were foreseen by the management. = 管理层预见了这些困难。
The potential risks were foreseen before the project started. = 项目开始前就预见了潜在的风险。


疑问句:
Could the consequences have been foreseen? = 后果是可以预见的么？
Were the problems foreseen before the launch? = 在发布之前是否预见了这些问题？


否定句:
The risks were not foreseen. = 风险没有被预见。
The problems were not foreseen at the outset. = 一开始没有预见到问题。
