loadings:/ˈloʊdɪŋz/ | n. |装载量；负荷；载重；(统计学)载荷；|The process or result of loading something.  It can refer to the amount of something loaded, the act of loading, or the weight or stress imposed.|例句：The truck's loadings exceeded the legal limit. 这辆卡车的载重量超过了法律限制。

例句：The engineer calculated the loadings on the bridge. 工程师计算了桥梁上的负荷。


近义词：cargo, freight, burden, weight, stress | 反义词：unloading, discharge


词源:源自动词load + -ing (名词后缀)，表示“装载”的动作或结果。  The suffix "-ing" transforms the verb "load" into a noun, referring to the action or the result of loading.
