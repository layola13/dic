nonsibilant:/ˌnɒnˈsɪbɪlənt/| adj. |非塞擦音的|Not involving or producing a sibilant sound; relating to a consonant sound that is not a sibilant.  A sibilant is a consonant sound, such as /s/, /z/, /ʃ/, /ʒ/, /tʃ/, and /dʒ/, that is produced with a hissing or buzzing sound.  Therefore, a nonsibilant consonant would be any consonant that doesn't make that sound. |Example: The voiceless alveolar plosive /t/ is a nonsibilant consonant.|近义词：non-hissing|反义词：sibilant
