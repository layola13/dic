medley:/ˈmɛdli/| n.|混合物；混合体；串烧|A medley is a mixture of different things, often in a disorganized or haphazard way. It can refer to a musical piece combining different tunes, a collection of diverse items, or a confused jumble.  |例句：The concert ended with a medley of popular songs.  这场音乐会以一首流行歌曲串烧作为结束。

The garden was a delightful medley of colors and scents. 花园里是色彩和香味的混合，令人愉快。|近义词：mixture, blend,potpourri|反义词：uniformity, homogeneity
