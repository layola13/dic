folkmote:/ˈfoʊkˌmoʊt/| n.|民众大会，人民会议|A folkmoot was an ancient Germanic assembly of free men, typically held outdoors.  It served as a court of justice and a forum for political discussion. The term is often used in historical contexts to describe early forms of governance and lawmaking in Germanic societies.|例句：The Anglo-Saxon folkmoot played a crucial role in the development of English law.  盎格鲁-撒克逊民众大会在英国法律的发展中发挥了至关重要的作用。|近义词：assembly, meeting, parliament (in a broader sense)|反义词：monarchy (as a contrasting form of governance)
