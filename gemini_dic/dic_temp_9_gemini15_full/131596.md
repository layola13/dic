noncompetitor:/ˌnɑːn kəmˈpɛtɪtər/| n. |非竞争者，非竞争对手|A noncompetitor is a company or individual that does not compete with others in a particular market or industry.  They may choose not to compete, or they may be excluded from competition due to various factors. |Example:  Due to its niche market, the small bookstore remained a noncompetitor to the large online retailers.|近义词：outsider, non-contender|反义词：competitor, rival


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The small bakery is a noncompetitor in the national bread market. = 这家小面包店在全国面包市场上没有竞争力。
That company remains a noncompetitor to ours in the software industry. = 那家公司在软件行业仍然不是我们的竞争对手。


一般过去时:
Before the merger, they were a noncompetitor. = 在合并之前，他们是竞争对手。
The startup remained a noncompetitor for several years. = 这家初创公司多年来一直没有竞争力。
