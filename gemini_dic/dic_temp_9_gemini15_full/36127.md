coestablishment:/ˌkoʊɪˈstæblɪʃmənt/ | n. | 共同建立；互惠共存；合作建立 | The act of establishing something jointly or mutually; the state of existing together in a mutually beneficial relationship.  This term often implies a reciprocal or symbiotic relationship where each entity contributes to the establishment and ongoing success of the other.  It is frequently used in ecological contexts, but can also apply to other areas. | Example: The coestablishment of the coral reef and its associated algae is crucial for the health of the ecosystem. 珊瑚礁及其相关藻类的共同建立对于生态系统的健康至关重要。  The coestablishment of a joint venture requires careful planning and negotiation.  合资企业的共同建立需要仔细的规划和谈判。|近义词：mutual establishment, joint establishment, symbiosis (in ecological contexts) |反义词：independent establishment, antagonism
