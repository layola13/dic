consolable:/kənˈsoʊləbl/ | adj. | 可安慰的，可抚慰的 | capable of being comforted; able to find comfort.  This adjective describes someone whose sadness or grief can be eased. |例句：After the accident, she was barely consolable. 事故后，她几乎无法得到安慰。|例句：Despite the loss, he eventually became consolable with the support of his friends. 尽管遭受了损失，在朋友们的支持下，他最终得到了安慰。|近义词：sootheable, comfort-able |反义词：inconsolable
