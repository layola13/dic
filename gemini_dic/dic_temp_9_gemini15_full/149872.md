palimony:/ˈpælɪmoʊni/| n.|  赡养费（非婚同居伴侣分手后一方支付给另一方的款项）|  Financial support paid by one partner to another after the termination of a non-marital relationship.  It's similar to alimony, but applies to unmarried couples.  | The couple lived together for 15 years before separating, and she sued him for palimony. 这对夫妇同居了15年才分手，她起诉他索要赡养费。|alimony, spousal support|child support,  (no direct antonym, but the opposite concept is the absence of such financial support)|名词
