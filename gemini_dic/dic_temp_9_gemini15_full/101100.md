inversion:/ɪnˈvɜːʒən/| n. |倒置；反转；反向；倒装|The reversal of the normal order, position, or sequence of something.  It can refer to a grammatical structure, a physical process, or an abstract concept.|例句：The inversion of the sentence made it more emphatic.  句子的倒装使其更具强调性。|例句：Temperature inversion trapped the smog in the valley.  温度逆温使烟雾滞留在山谷中。|近义词：reversal, inversion, overturn, reversal|反义词：normal order, direct order


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The cat sat on the mat. = 猫坐在垫子上。(Subject-Verb-Object)
On the mat sat the cat. = 猫坐在垫子上。(Inversion: Object-Verb-Subject)


一般过去时:
She went to the store. = 她去了商店。(Subject-Verb-Object)
To the store went she. = 她去了商店。(Inversion: Object-Verb-Subject - archaic)


现在完成时:
He has finished his work. = 他完成了他的工作。(Subject-Verb-Object)
Finished his work has he. = 他完成了他的工作。(Inversion: Object-Verb-Subject - archaic)


过去完成时:
They had eaten dinner. = 他们已经吃过晚饭了。(Subject-Verb-Object)
Eaten dinner had they. = 他们已经吃过晚饭了。(Inversion: Object-Verb-Subject - archaic)


将来时:
She will go to school. = 她将会去上学。(Subject-Verb-Object)
To school will she go. = 她将会去上学。(Inversion: Object-Verb-Subject - poetic/emphatic)


虚拟语气:
Were I you, I would apologize. = 如果我是你，我会道歉。(Inversion: Verb-Subject)
Should it rain, we will stay inside. = 如果下雨，我们就待在里面。(Inversion: Verb-Subject)


被动语态:
The ball was thrown by the boy. = 球被男孩扔了。(Passive voice)
By the boy was the ball thrown. = 球被男孩扔了。(Inversion: Prepositional phrase -Verb-Subject)


疑问句:
Do you like coffee? = 你喜欢咖啡吗？(Standard question formation)
Like coffee do you? = 你喜欢咖啡吗？(Inversion: Verb-Subject - archaic/emphatic)


否定句:
I don't understand. = 我不明白。(Standard negation)
Understand I do not. = 我不明白。(Inversion: Verb-Subject - archaic/emphatic)


PHRASE->

Sentence inversion = 句子倒装
Partial inversion = 部分倒装
Complete inversion = 完全倒装
