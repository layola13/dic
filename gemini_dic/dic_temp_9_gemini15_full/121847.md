misreporter:/ˌmɪsrɪˈpɔːrtər/ | n. | 错误报道者，歪曲事实者 | A person who gives a false or inaccurate account of something.  | The misreporter was later found to have fabricated the entire story.  这个错误报道者后来被发现编造了整个故事。| liar, falsifier, fabricator | truth-teller, accurate reporter | 名词

He misreports the news frequently. 他经常歪曲新闻报道。
The journalist misreports the facts consistently.  这位记者总是歪曲事实。


一般过去时:
She misreported the accident details. 她歪曲了事故细节。
