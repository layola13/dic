identified:/aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪd/ | v. | 识别出的；已鉴定的 | To have recognized and established the identity of (someone or something). | 例句：The police have identified the suspect. 警方已经确认了嫌疑人。

例句：The cause of the accident has been identified. 事故原因已被查明。


近义词：recognized, established, determined, ascertained | 反义词：misidentified, unrecognized

USAGE->
一般现在时:
They identify the problem quickly. = 他们很快地识别出了问题。
We identify ourselves with our work. = 我们通过工作来认同自己。

一般过去时:
The police identified the thief from the security footage. = 警方根据监控录像认出了小偷。
She identified her lost dog at the animal shelter. = 她在动物收容所认出了她丢失的狗。

现在完成时:
Scientists have identified a new species of plant. = 科学家们已经发现了一种新的植物物种。
They have identified the main causes of the issue. = 他们已经确定了该问题的主要原因。

过去完成时:
The detective had identified the killer before the trial. = 侦探在审判前已经找到了凶手。
By then, she had already identified the source of the leak. = 到那时，她已经找到了泄漏的来源。

将来时:
Experts will identify the painting as a forgery. = 专家们将认定这幅画是赝品。
We will easily identify the building from its unique architecture. = 我们很容易就能从它独特的建筑风格认出这座建筑。

虚拟语气:
If they identified the problem earlier, they could have fixed it easily. = 如果他们早点发现问题，就能很容易地解决它。
I wish I had identified the risk before investing. = 我希望在投资前我已经发现了风险。

被动语态:
The victim was identified by a witness. = 受害者被一名目击者认出。
The suspect has been identified through DNA evidence. = 嫌疑人通过DNA证据被确认身份。

疑问句:
Have they identified the culprit yet? = 他们已经找到罪犯了吗？
Can you identify the sound of that bird? = 你能辨认出那只鸟的叫声吗？

否定句:
The police couldn't identify the body. = 警方无法辨认尸体。
I didn't identify the man in the photo. = 我没认出照片里的那个人。


PHRASE->
identify with: 与...产生共鸣
identify oneself: 自我介绍
easily identified: 易于识别的
clearly identified: 清晰识别的
positively identified: 确切地识别
