classify:/ˈklæsɪfaɪ/| vt.|分类，归类|To arrange or organize things into groups based on shared characteristics.|例句：The librarian classified the books by subject. 图书管理员按主题对书籍进行了分类。|例句：We need to classify these documents according to their level of secrecy.我们需要根据这些文件的保密级别对它们进行分类。|近义词：categorize, sort, arrange|反义词：jumble, mix, randomize|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She classifies the emails by sender. = 她按发件人对电子邮件进行分类。
They classify the data according to age and gender. = 他们根据年龄和性别对数据进行分类。

一般过去时:
He classified the rocks by their mineral composition. = 他根据矿物成分对岩石进行了分类。
The scientist classified the new species of insect. = 科学家对这种新的昆虫物种进行了分类。

现在完成时:
She has classified all the documents for the project. = 她已经为项目对所有文件进行了分类。
They have classified the information into three categories. = 他们已将信息分为三类。


过去完成时:
The researchers had classified the data before they began their analysis. = 研究人员在开始分析之前就已经对数据进行了分类。
He had classified the books before the library opened. = 图书馆开馆前他已经把书分类了。


将来时:
We will classify the samples once the tests are complete. = 测试完成后，我们将对样本进行分类。
She will classify the images based on color and shape. = 她将根据颜色和形状对图像进行分类。


虚拟语气:
If we classified the data differently, the results might be different. = 如果我们对数据进行不同的分类，结果可能会有所不同。
I wish I had classified the files more systematically. = 我真希望我当时能更系统地对文件进行分类。


被动语态:
The documents were classified by subject matter. = 文件按主题进行了分类。
The data is being classified for analysis. = 数据正在被分类以进行分析。


疑问句:
How did they classify the different types of plants? = 他们是如何对不同种类的植物进行分类的？
How are the students classified in your school? = 在你们学校，学生是如何分班的？


否定句:
He didn't classify the information correctly. = 他没有正确地对信息进行分类。
They haven't yet classified all the responses. = 他们还没有对所有回复进行分类。
