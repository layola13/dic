corresponsive:/ˌkɒrəˈspɒnsɪv/ /ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːnsɪv/| adj.|相应的，对应的；互相对应的；有联系的|Corresponding; having a mutual relation; reciprocal.|The two countries maintain responsive diplomatic relations. 这两个国家保持着相应的外交关系。|The symptoms were responsive to the treatment. 症状对治疗有反应。|近义词：corresponding, reciprocal, related|反义词：unrelated, disparate


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The two events are responsive. = 这两个事件是相互关联的。
His actions are responsive to her needs. = 他的行为符合她的需求。


一般过去时:
Their answers were responsive to the questions. = 他们的回答与问题相符。
The market was responsive to the news. = 市场对这个消息做出了反应。


现在完成时:
The company has been responsive to customer feedback. = 公司一直对客户反馈做出了回应。
The government has been responsive to the public's concerns. = 政府一直对公众的担忧做出了回应。


过去完成时:
The team had been responsive to the crisis. = 团队对危机做出了及时的回应。
The economy had been responsive to the stimulus package. = 经济对刺激计划做出了反应。


将来时:
The system will be responsive to user input. = 系统将对用户的输入做出反应。
The company will be more responsive to customer complaints. = 公司将对客户投诉做出更积极的回应。


虚拟语气:
If the system were more responsive, we would have fewer problems. = 如果系统更灵敏，我们将遇到更少的问题。
I wish the government were more responsive to our needs. = 我希望政府能更积极地回应我们的需求。


被动语态:
The problem was responded to quickly. = 问题得到了迅速的回应。
The suggestions were responded to positively. = 建议得到了积极的回应。


疑问句:
Is the system responsive? = 系统灵敏吗？
Are the results responsive to the changes? = 结果对变化有反应吗？


否定句:
The system is not responsive. = 系统不灵敏。
The company was not responsive to our concerns. = 公司对我们的担忧没有做出回应。


PHRASE->

be responsive to: 对…有反应
highly responsive: 高度灵敏的
quickly responsive: 快速响应的
less responsive: 反应迟钝的
responsive design: 响应式设计
