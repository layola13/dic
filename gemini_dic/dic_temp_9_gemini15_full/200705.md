sleeps:/sliːpz/| v.|睡觉，睡眠|The verb "sleeps" is the third-person singular simple present tense form of the verb "to sleep," indicating that someone or something is currently sleeping.  It can also refer to a state of inactivity or dormancy.|He sleeps soundly every night. 他每晚睡得很香。|The baby sleeps peacefully in her crib. 婴儿在她的小床里安详地睡着。|rests, dozes, naps|wakes, is awake, is alert|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He sleeps eight hours a night. 他每晚睡八个小时。
She sleeps through the thunderstorm. 她睡过了雷雨。


一般过去时:
The dog slept all day. 狗睡了一整天。
I slept poorly last night. 我昨晚睡得很不好。


现在完成时:
He has slept well recently. 他最近睡得很好。
She has slept for ten hours straight. 她已经连续睡了十个小时。


过去完成时:
I had slept for only an hour when the phone rang. 电话响的时候，我只睡了一个小时。
They had slept soundly before the alarm went off. 闹钟响之前，他们睡得很香。


将来时:
I will sleep better tonight. 我今晚会睡得更好。
They will sleep in tomorrow. 他们明天会睡懒觉。


虚拟语气:
If I slept more, I would feel better. 如果我睡得更多，我会感觉更好。
I wish I slept better. 我希望我睡得更好。


被动语态: (被动语态较少用于sleep)
The baby is often slept with by her parents.  婴儿经常和父母一起睡觉。(This implies the parents put the baby to sleep in their bed)
The problem was slept on for too long. 这个问题被搁置太久了。(Figurative use)


疑问句:
Does he sleep well? 他睡得好吗？
Does the baby sleep through the night? 婴儿整夜睡吗？


否定句:
I don't sleep well with noise. 我有噪音睡不好。
He doesn't sleep enough. 他睡眠不足。


PHRASE->
sleep tight = 睡个好觉
fall asleep = 入睡
go to sleep = 去睡觉
sleep soundly = 睡得很香
sleep in = 睡懒觉
