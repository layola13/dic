lauraceous: /ˌlɔːrˈeɪʃəs/ | adj. | 月桂属的；月桂树的；似月桂的 | Relating to or characteristic of the laurel family (Lauraceae).  The term often describes plants having leaves resembling those of laurel trees, even if not botanically related. | The lauraceous trees of the rainforest provided shade and shelter.  月桂科的雨林树木提供了阴凉和庇护。|近义词:laurel-like, bay-like |反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可考虑与非月桂科植物相关的形容词)|形容词
