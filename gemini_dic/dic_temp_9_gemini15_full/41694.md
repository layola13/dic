corrigendum: /ˌkɒrɪˈdʒɛndəm/ | n. |勘误表；更正；订正 | A corrigendum is a statement correcting an error in a previously published document, such as a book, article, or official report. It usually lists the errors and provides the correct information.  | Example: The journal published a corrigendum to correct a minor error in the data analysis.  期刊发表了一份勘误表来更正数据分析中的一个小错误。|近义词：correction, amendment, erratum |反义词：error, mistake
