hootenanny:/ˈhuːtənænɪ/| n. |欢闹的聚会；喧闹的集会 |A hootenanny is a lively, informal gathering, typically featuring folk music and singing.  It often involves audience participation and a generally festive atmosphere. |Example: The college students threw a hootenanny to celebrate the end of exams.  大学学生们举办了一场欢闹的聚会来庆祝期末考试结束。|近义词：party, jamboree, sing-along |反义词：formal event, quiet gathering
