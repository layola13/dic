fogs:/fɒɡz/| n.|雾；迷雾|  A fog is a weather condition where tiny water droplets are suspended in the air near the ground, reducing visibility. Fogs can be caused by various factors, including cooling of the air, evaporation from water bodies, and air pollution.|例句：The morning fogs rolled in, obscuring the city.  早晨的雾滚滚而来，遮蔽了城市。
The dense fogs delayed all flights.  浓雾延误了所有航班。|近义词：mist, haze|反义词：clear skies, sunshine|名词
