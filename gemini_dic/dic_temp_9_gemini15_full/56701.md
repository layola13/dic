doomily:/ˈduːmɪli/| adj.|阴沉地；不祥地；沮丧地|In a gloomy or depressing manner, suggesting impending disaster or misfortune.  It implies a sense of foreboding and pessimism.|He spoke doomily of the future.|近义词：gloomily, drearily, despondently, pessimistically|反义词：cheerfully, optimistically, hopefully
