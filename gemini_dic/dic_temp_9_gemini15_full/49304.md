delict:/ˈdɛlɪkt/| n.|民事侵权行为；违法行为|A delict is a wrongful act that causes harm to another person, for which the law provides a remedy.  It's a civil wrong, as opposed to a crime.  Delicts are usually dealt with through civil lawsuits, where the injured party seeks compensation for their losses. |The company was found liable for delict in causing environmental damage. 这家公司被判犯有民事侵权行为，造成环境破坏。|tort, wrong, offense|crime, lawful act|名词
