despites:/dɪˈspaɪts/| preposition|尽管；虽然；即使|Despite the rain, we went for a walk.  尽管下雨，我们还是去散步了。|近义词：in spite of, notwithstanding, regardless of|反义词：because of, owing to|介词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Despite the cold weather, she wears a short skirt. = 尽管天气寒冷，她还是穿短裙。
Despite his efforts, he failed the exam. = 尽管他很努力，但他考试还是不及格。


一般过去时:
Despite the heavy traffic, we arrived on time. = 尽管交通拥堵，我们还是准时到达了。
Despite his illness, he went to work. = 尽管他生病了，他还是去上班了。


现在完成时:
Despite having studied hard, he didn't pass the test. = 尽管他学习很努力，但他考试还是没及格。
Despite having many problems, she remained optimistic. = 尽管有很多问题，她仍然保持乐观。


过去完成时:
Despite having finished the work, he felt unsatisfied. = 尽管完成了工作，他仍然感到不满意。
Despite having warned him, he ignored the danger. = 尽管警告过他，他还是无视了危险。


将来时:
Despite the forecast, we will go hiking tomorrow. = 尽管有预报，我们明天还是要去远足。
Despite possible difficulties, we will complete the project. = 尽管可能有困难，我们还是会完成这个项目。


虚拟语气:
Despite what you may think, I would do it again. = 尽管你可能认为，我还会再做一次。
Despite what might happen, I will support you. = 无论发生什么，我都会支持你。


被动语态:
Despite being warned, he continued to do it. = 尽管受到了警告，他还是继续这样做。
Despite being tired, the work was completed on time. = 尽管很累，工作还是按时完成了。


疑问句:
Despite the evidence, are you still unsure? = 尽管有证据，你仍然不确定吗？
Despite his claims, is he telling the truth? = 尽管他声称，他在说实话吗？


否定句:
Despite not having much money, he donated to charity. = 尽管钱不多，他还是捐给了慈善机构。
Despite not feeling well, she attended the meeting. = 尽管感觉不舒服，她还是参加了会议。


PHRASE->
despite everything = 尽管一切
despite the fact that = 尽管事实上
in spite of despite = 尽管（两者近义）
despite all odds = 尽管一切困难
despite oneself = 不由自主
