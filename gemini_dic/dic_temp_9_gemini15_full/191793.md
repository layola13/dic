scirrhous:/ˈsɪrəs/| adj.|硬癌的，癌肿的；坚硬的|Relating to or characterized by scirrhus, a hard cancerous tumor.  The term often describes a hard, firm, and usually cancerous growth or tumor, typically characterized by its fibrous and indurated nature. |例句：The biopsy revealed scirrhous carcinoma in the breast.  活检显示乳房内有硬癌。

The tumor was described as scirrhous, indicating a poor prognosis. 肿瘤被描述为硬癌，预后不良。|近义词：cancerous, malignant, indurated, hard |反义词：soft, benign


词源:
From Late Latin *scirrhosus, from Greek skīrrhos (σκιρρος) "hard swelling, scirrhus," of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to skīrō (σκιρῶ) "to harden."
