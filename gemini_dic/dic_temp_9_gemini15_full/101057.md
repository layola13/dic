invent:/ɪnˈvɛnt/| vt. |发明；创造；捏造|To create or produce (something) for the first time.| The Wright brothers invented the airplane.  莱特兄弟发明了飞机。|He invented a story about being lost. 他编造了一个关于迷路的谎话。|create, devise, fabricate |destroy, disprove|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She invents new recipes every week. = 她每周都会发明新的食谱。
They invent excuses to avoid work. = 他们编造借口来逃避工作。


一般过去时:
Edison invented the light bulb. = 爱迪生发明了灯泡。
The artist invented a new style of painting. = 这位艺术家发明了一种新的绘画风格。


现在完成时:
Scientists have invented many life-saving drugs. = 科学家们已经发明了许多救命的药物。
She has invented a new way to teach children. = 她发明了一种新的儿童教学方法。


过去完成时:
They had invented the machine before the patent was filed. = 他们在申请专利之前就已经发明了这台机器。
He had invented several stories to explain his absence.  = 他编造了好几个故事来解释他的缺席。


将来时:
They will invent a new type of battery next year. = 他们明年将发明一种新型电池。
I believe they will invent a cure for cancer soon. = 我相信他们很快就会发明出癌症的治疗方法。


虚拟语气:
If I had invented a time machine, I would travel to the future. = 如果我发明了一台时间机器，我就会去未来旅行。
I wish I had invented that amazing gadget. = 我真希望我发明了那个神奇的小玩意儿。


被动语态:
The telephone was invented by Alexander Graham Bell. = 电话是由亚历山大·格雷厄姆·贝尔发明的。
A new method of water purification has been invented. = 一种新的净水方法已经被发明出来了。


疑问句:
Who invented the printing press? = 谁发明了印刷机？
Did Leonardo da Vinci invent the helicopter? = 达芬奇发明了直升机吗？


否定句:
He did not invent the wheel. = 他并没有发明轮子。
They haven't invented a solution to this problem yet. = 他们还没有发明出这个问题的解决方案。


PHRASE->
invent a story = 编造故事
invent an excuse = 编造借口
invent a machine = 发明机器
invent a new method = 发明新方法
invent a solution = 发明解决方案
