nauseation:/ˌnɔːziˈeɪʃən/| n.|恶心；反胃；晕船|The feeling of sickness and the urge to vomit.  It can be caused by various factors, including illness, medication side effects, or motion sickness.|例句：The strong smell caused a wave of nausea and nausea. 强烈的味道引起一阵恶心和反胃。

The side effects of the medication included nausea and dizziness.  该药物的副作用包括恶心和头晕。|近义词：vomiting, sickness, queasiness|反义词：wellness, comfort
