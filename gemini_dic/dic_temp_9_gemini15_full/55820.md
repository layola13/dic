divi:/ˈdaɪvi/| n.| 分配；股息 | A divi is a dividend, a share of the profits of a company that is paid to its shareholders.  | Example: The company announced a generous divi this quarter.  这家公司宣布本季度将派发丰厚的股息。|近义词：dividend, distribution, share of profits | 反义词：N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company pays a divi every year. = 公司每年支付股息。
We receive a divi from our investments. = 我们从投资中获得股息。


一般过去时:
They received a large divi last year. = 他们去年收到一大笔股息。
The company didn't pay a divi last quarter. = 公司上个季度没有支付股息。


现在完成时:
The shareholders have received their divi. = 股东们已经收到了他们的股息。
I haven't received my divi yet. = 我还没有收到我的股息。


过去完成时:
They had already received their divi before the announcement. = 他们在公告之前就已经收到了他们的股息。
The investors hadn't received their divi before the bankruptcy. = 在破产之前，投资者们还没有收到他们的股息。


将来时:
The company will pay a divi next month. = 公司下个月将支付股息。
We expect to receive a divi in the spring. = 我们预计在春天收到股息。


虚拟语气:
If the company performed better, it would pay a larger divi. = 如果公司业绩更好，它将支付更高的股息。
I wish I had received a bigger divi. = 我真希望我收到了更多的股息。


被动语态:
The divi was paid out last week. = 股息在上周支付。
The divi is expected to be announced soon. = 股息预计很快就会公布。


疑问句:
When will the divi be paid? = 股息什么时候支付？
How much is the divi per share? = 每股股息是多少？


否定句:
The company did not pay a divi this year. = 公司今年没有支付股息。
They will not receive a divi this quarter. = 他们本季度将不会收到股息。
