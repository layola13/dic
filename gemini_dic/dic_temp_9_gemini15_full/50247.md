dependent:/dɪˈpɛndənt/ | adj., n. | 依赖的；依靠的；受约束的；（语法）从属的 |  relying on or subject to another; subordinate; conditional.  |例句：He is dependent on his parents for financial support. 他依赖父母的经济支持。

The children are dependent on their mother for care. 孩子们依靠母亲的照顾。


近义词：reliant, reliant on, subject to, contingent on | 反义词：independent, self-sufficient | 词性：adj., n.


词源：源自古法语 *dependent,  来自拉丁语 *dependens,  现在分词 dependere 的形式，意思是“悬挂；依靠”。


用法说明:


作为形容词：

1.  表示依赖：指某人或某事物依赖于其他人或事物。例如：

    The success of the project is dependent on the weather.  项目的成功取决于天气。

    His happiness seems dependent on her approval. 他的快乐似乎取决于她的认可。


2.  表示从属：指某事物从属于另一事物。例如：

    A dependent clause cannot stand alone as a sentence. 从句不能单独成句。

    The study found a correlation between dependent variables and independent variables. 研究发现因变量和自变量之间存在相关性。


作为名词：

1.  指依赖者：指依赖他人照顾或经济支持的人。例如：

    He is financially dependent on his wife. 他经济上依赖他的妻子。

    The elderly often become dependent on their children. 老年人经常变得依赖他们的孩子。


2.  （语法）指从属成分：指从句或其他从属成分。例如：

    The sentence contains a dependent clause. 这个句子包含一个从句。


例句补充：

* 一般现在时:
He is dependent on his parents. 他依靠他的父母。
She is dependent on medication. 她依赖药物。

* 一般过去时:
He was dependent on his parents for support. 他过去依靠父母的支持。
The country was dependent on foreign aid. 这个国家依赖外国援助。

* 现在完成时:
She has been dependent on her sister since her accident. 自从她出事以来，她一直依靠她姐姐。
They have become dependent on technology. 他们已经变得依赖科技。

* 过去完成时:
He had been dependent on alcohol for many years. 他多年来一直依赖酒精。
She had been dependent on her parents until she got married. 在她结婚之前，她一直依赖她的父母。

* 将来时:
He will be dependent on a wheelchair after the surgery. 手术后他将需要轮椅。
She will likely be dependent on others for help. 她很可能会依赖他人帮助。

* 虚拟语气:
If I were dependent on him, I would be in trouble. 如果我依赖他，我会陷入困境。
I wish I weren't dependent on caffeine. 我希望我不依赖咖啡因。

* 被动语态:
The outcome is dependent on many factors. 结果取决于许多因素。(被动语态已隐含在"is dependent on"中)
The children are dependent on for care. 孩子们需要被照顾。(被动语态)

* 疑问句:
Is he dependent on anyone? 他依赖任何人吗？
Are they dependent on government assistance? 他们依赖政府援助吗？

* 否定句:
He is not dependent on his parents anymore. 他不再依赖他的父母了。
She is not dependent on anyone for financial support. 她不需要依靠任何人来获得经济支持。
