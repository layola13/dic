pseudoheroical: /ˌsuːdoʊhɪˈroʊɪkəl/ | adj. | 似英雄的；冒牌英雄的；伪英雄的 |  Seeming or pretending to be heroic; falsely heroic.  It suggests a superficial or insincere display of heroism, often for self-aggrandizement. |


例句：

His pseudoheroical actions, motivated by self-promotion rather than genuine bravery, were quickly exposed.  他的伪英雄行为，是出于自我推销而不是真正的勇敢，很快就被揭露了。

The novel portrays a pseudoheroical protagonist who ultimately fails to live up to his self-proclaimed ideals. 这部小说描绘了一个伪英雄主角，最终未能达到他自诩的理想。


近义词：pretentious, affected, bombastic, theatrical, grandiose


反义词：genuine, authentic, humble, modest, unassuming


词性：形容词
