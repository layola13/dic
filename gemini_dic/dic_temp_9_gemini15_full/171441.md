/pjuːər/ | n. |男孩；童子；（尤指）少年|A boy, especially a young boy.  The word often implies a sense of innocence or youthfulness, sometimes with connotations of being immature or lacking experience. |Example: He was a puer who loved to play in the fields. 他是个喜欢在田野里玩耍的孩子。|近义词：boy, lad, youth |反义词：adult, man
