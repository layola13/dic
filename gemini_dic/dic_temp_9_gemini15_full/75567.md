fucoid:/ˈfjuːkɔɪd/| adj.|褐藻类的；墨角藻属的 |Relating to or characteristic of the brown algae, especially the genus *Fucus*.  Fucoid algae are a large group of brown seaweeds that are found in many parts of the world. They are characterized by their olive-brown color and their leathery or cartilaginous texture.  Many fucoid algae have gas bladders that help them float. | Example: The rocky intertidal zone was teeming with fucoid algae.  这片岩石潮间带充满了褐藻。|近义词：brown algal|反义词：无
