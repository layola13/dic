presignaled: /ˌpriːˈsɪɡnəld/ | v. | 预示；预告；提前发出信号 | To give a signal or warning in advance; to foreshadow.  | The dark clouds presignaled an approaching storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨即将来临。 The government's actions presignaled a change in policy. 政府的行动预示着政策的改变。| foreshadow, foretell, portend |  (无明确反义词，可根据语境使用如：conceal, hide等) | 动词
