feuded:/ˈfjuːdəd/| v. |世仇，长期争斗 |To engage in a prolonged and bitter quarrel or dispute.|The families have feuded for generations over land rights.  几代人以来，这两个家族一直因为土地权而争斗不休。|They feuded bitterly over the inheritance.  他们为了遗产而激烈争吵。|quarrel, dispute, conflict|reconcile, unite


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They feud over property rights. = 他们因为财产权而争斗。
The families feud constantly. =  这些家庭经常争吵。


一般过去时:
The two clans feuded for centuries. = 这两个家族争斗了几个世纪。
The neighbours feuded over a noisy dog. = 邻居们因为一条吵闹的狗而争吵。


现在完成时:
They have feuded since childhood. = 他们从孩提时代就开始争斗了。
The families have feuded for many years over a boundary dispute. =  多年来，这两个家庭一直因为边界纠纷而争吵。


过去完成时:
They had feuded for years before they finally reached a settlement. = 在他们最终达成协议之前，他们已经争斗多年了。
The brothers had feuded long before the inheritance was even discussed. = 在遗产问题被讨论之前，兄弟俩就已经争吵很久了。


将来时:
I predict they will feud again soon. = 我预测他们很快又会争吵。
If they don't compromise, they will feud endlessly. = 如果他们不妥协，他们将无休止地争吵。


虚拟语气:
If they hadn't feuded, they might have been friends. = 如果他们没有争吵，他们可能已经成为朋友了。
I wish they hadn't feuded so bitterly. = 我希望他们没有那么激烈地争吵。


被动语态:
The matter was feuded over for years.  = 这件事争论了好几年。
The inheritance was feuded over by the family members. = 遗产被家庭成员争夺。


疑问句:
Have they feuded for a long time? = 他们争吵很久了吗？
Why are they feuding? = 他们为什么争吵？


否定句:
They did not feud openly. = 他们没有公开争吵。
They haven't feuded since the agreement was reached. = 自从达成协议以来，他们就没有争吵过。


PHRASE->

feud with someone = 与某人结仇
a bitter feud = 一场激烈的争斗
family feud = 家庭纠纷
a long-standing feud = 长期争执
to settle a feud = 解决争端
