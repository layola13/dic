rustable:/ˈrʌstəbəl/| adj.|易生锈的，易腐蚀的|Susceptible to rusting or corrosion.  Something that is rustable is likely to develop rust, especially if it's made of iron or steel and exposed to moisture and air.|例句：The old car is rustable, due to years of neglect. 这辆旧车容易生锈，因为多年来无人照料。

例句：That rustable pipe needs replacing before it leaks. 那根易生锈的管道需要在漏水之前更换。


近义词：corrodible, oxidizable|反义词：rustproof, corrosion-resistant


词源：rust (名词，生锈) + able (形容词后缀，能够…的)  The word combines the noun "rust" indicating the process of oxidation and the suffix "-able" signifying the capacity or tendency to rust.
