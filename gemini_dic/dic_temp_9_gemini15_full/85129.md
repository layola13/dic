hardily:/ˈhɑːrdɪli/| adv. |大胆地；勇敢地；坚决地；勉强地；几乎不|Boldly; courageously; resolutely; with difficulty; scarcely.|例句：He hardily faced the challenges ahead. 他勇敢地面对了未来的挑战。

例句：She hardily denied the accusations. 她坚决否认了指控。


近义词：boldly, bravely, resolutely, stoutly|反义词：timidly, fearfully, hesitantly


词源:源自古英语 *hardelice，由harde（坚硬的，困难的）和-lice（副词后缀）构成。
