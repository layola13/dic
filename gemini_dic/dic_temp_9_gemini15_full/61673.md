emmenagogue: /ˈɛməˌnæɡɒɡ/ | n. | 通经药；催经药 | A substance that stimulates menstrual flow.  Emmenagogues are used to induce menstruation or to increase menstrual flow, often in cases of amenorrhea (absence of menstruation) or hypomenorrhea (scanty menstruation).  However, their use should be carefully considered due to potential side effects and the importance of underlying cause investigation. |  The doctor prescribed an emmenagogue to help regulate her menstrual cycle.  医生开了通经药来帮助她调节月经周期。


The herbal remedy acted as a potent emmenagogue, quickly inducing menstruation.  这种草药疗法起到了有效的通经作用，迅速诱发了月经。| 近义词：menstruum, stimulant (in this context) | 反义词：antimenorrhagic (substance that stops menstrual bleeding) | 名词
