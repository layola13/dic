recognisor:/ˈrekəɡnaɪzər/| n. |识别者，承认者|A person or thing that recognizes something or someone.  This term is often used in legal contexts to refer to the person who acknowledges or verifies a signature or document. |Example: The recognisor confirmed the authenticity of the signature on the contract.  识别者确认合同上的签字是真实的。|近义词：identifier, verifier, authenticator|反义词：denier, disbeliever
