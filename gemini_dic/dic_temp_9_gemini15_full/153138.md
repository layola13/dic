pelar: /pɛˈlɑːr/ | vt. | 剥皮；削皮；拔毛 | To remove the skin or outer covering of something, such as fruit, vegetables, or an animal. | 例句：He peeled the potatoes before boiling them. 他在煮土豆之前把土豆削了皮。 | 例句：She peeled the orange and ate it. 她剥了橙子然后吃了。| 近义词：pare, skin, shuck | 反义词：None specifically, but the opposite action would be to add a covering.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She peels apples every morning. = 她每天早上削苹果。
The chef peels the garlic before mincing it. = 厨师在剁蒜之前先剥蒜。


一般过去时:
I peeled the bananas yesterday. = 我昨天剥了香蕉。
He peeled the oranges before making the juice. = 他在榨果汁之前剥了橙子。


现在完成时:
I have peeled all the potatoes. = 我已经把所有的土豆都削皮了。
She has peeled many oranges today. = 她今天已经剥了很多橙子。


过去完成时:
I had already peeled the carrots before the soup was ready. = 汤准备好之前我已经把胡萝卜削皮了。
They had peeled all the onions before they started cooking. = 他们在开始做饭之前已经把所有的洋葱都剥好了。


将来时:
I will peel the carrots tomorrow. = 我明天会削胡萝卜皮。
