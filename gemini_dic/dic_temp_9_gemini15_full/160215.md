ponging:/ˈpɒŋɪŋ/ | v. | 砰砰作响；(球)来回弹击 | The sound of the ball ponging against the wall.  | 近义词：boinging, thudding | 反义词：silence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ball is ponging against the wall. = 球正在墙壁上砰砰作响。
He is constantly ponging the ball back and forth. = 他不断地把球来回击打。


一般过去时:
The ball ponged against the wall. = 球砰地一声撞在墙上。
They ponged the ping pong ball for hours. = 他们打了几个小时的乒乓球。


现在完成时:
The children have been ponging the ball in the garden all afternoon. = 孩子们整个下午都在花园里打球。
I have ponged this ball so many times, it is almost flat. = 我打了这么多次这个球，它几乎瘪了。
