overpumps:/ˌoʊvərˈpʌmpz/| vt.|过度抽取；过度使用|To pump or extract excessively, often to the point of depletion or damage.|例句：Overpumping groundwater can lead to land subsidence.  过度抽取地下水可能导致地层下陷。|例句：The oil company was criticized for overpumping the well. 石油公司因过度抽取油井而受到批评。|近义词：exhaust, deplete, overdraw|反义词：conserve, replenish
