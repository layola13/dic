constructability:/kənˌstrʌktəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可建造性，可施工性 | The ease with which a project can be built.  It considers factors like design, materials, site conditions, and available resources.  | Example: The high constructability of the prefabricated components reduced construction time significantly.  高预制构件的可建造性大大缩短了施工时间。 |近义词：feasibility, buildability | 反义词：infeasibility, unbuildability |名词
