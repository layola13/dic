hand-overs:/ˈhændˌoʊvərz/| n.|移交，交接|The act of handing something over to someone else; the transfer of control, responsibility, or possession.  |例句：The hand-over of the project to the new team went smoothly. 项目顺利移交给新团队。|例句：The hand-over of power after the election was peaceful.  选举后权力交接和平进行。|近义词：transfer, handover, conveyance, delivery|反义词：retention, withholding


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company hands over the project every quarter. = 公司每季度移交项目。
He hands over his work to his colleague before leaving. = 他离开前把工作移交给同事。


一般过去时:
They handed over the keys to the new owner. = 他们把钥匙交给了新房主。
The previous administration handed over the reins of power peacefully. = 前一届政府和平地移交了权力。


现在完成时:
She has handed over all the necessary documents. = 她已经移交了所有必要的文档。
The team has handed over the project to the client. = 团队已经将项目移交给客户。


过去完成时:
They had handed over the responsibility before the meeting. = 他们在会议前已经移交了责任。
He had handed over the evidence to the police. = 他已将证据移交给了警方。


将来时:
We will hand over the report tomorrow. = 我们明天将提交报告。
The CEO will hand over the company to his son next year. =  首席执行官明年将把公司移交给他的儿子。


虚拟语气:
If they handed over the information, we would solve the case. = 如果他们移交信息，我们会破案。
I wish they had handed over the documents earlier. = 我希望他们早点移交文件。


被动语态:
The report was handed over to the committee. = 报告已提交给委员会。
The power will be handed over in a formal ceremony. = 权力将在一个正式仪式上移交。


疑问句:
When will the documents be handed over? = 文件什么时候移交？
Has the responsibility been handed over yet? = 责任已经移交了吗？


否定句:
He didn't hand over the files on time. = 他没有按时移交文件。
They haven't yet handed over the property. = 他们尚未移交财产。
