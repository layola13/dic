emulatress:/ˈɛmjʊleɪtrəs/| n.|模拟者，效仿者|A woman who emulates or imitates someone or something.  It is a relatively rare word, primarily used in specific contexts where a female is explicitly mimicking another.|She was an emulatress of the great painters of the Renaissance, meticulously copying their techniques. 她是一位文艺复兴时期伟大画家的模仿者，一丝不苟地复制着他们的技法。|imitator, copycat, follower|original, innovator|名词
