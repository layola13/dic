inhospitality:/ˌɪnˌhɒspɪˈtælɪti/ | n. |不友好，冷淡，粗鲁 | The lack of warmth or friendliness; unwelcoming behavior.  It refers to a lack of hospitality, often manifesting as rudeness or discourtesy. |Example: The innkeeper's inhospitality drove the weary travelers away. 旅店老板的不友好赶走了疲惫的旅行者。|近义词：unfriendliness, rudeness, discourtesy, churlishness |反义词：hospitality, friendliness, warmth, welcome |名词
