happen-on:/ˌhæpənˈɒn/|/ˌhæpənˈɑːn/| v. |偶然发现；意外遇见 |To find or discover something unexpectedly or by chance.  |I happened on an antique shop while strolling through the town. 我在镇上闲逛时偶然发现了一家古董店。|  We happened on a beautiful beach during our road trip.我们在公路旅行中偶然发现了一个美丽的沙滩。| stumble upon, come across, encounter |  deliberately seek, plan | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I often happen on interesting articles online. = 我经常偶然在网上发现有趣的文章。
They happen on new ideas during brainstorming sessions. = 他们在头脑风暴会议中偶然想到新的主意。


一般过去时:
I happened on a lost puppy in the park yesterday. = 我昨天在公园里偶然发现了一只丢失的小狗。
She happened on an old photograph of her grandparents. = 她偶然发现了一张她祖父母的旧照片。

They have happened on some valuable information during their research. = 他们在研究过程中偶然获得了一些有价值的信息。


过去完成时:

By the time the meeting ended, they had happened on a solution to the problem. = 到会议结束时，他们已经偶然找到了解决问题的办法。

虚拟语气:
