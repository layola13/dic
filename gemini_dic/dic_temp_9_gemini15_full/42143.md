cotyledonal:/ˌkɒtɪˈliːdənəl/| adj. | 子叶的；双子叶的 | Relating to or characteristic of cotyledons.  The term often refers to plants with two cotyledons (dicots), as opposed to those with one (monocots).  | Example: The cotyledonal leaves of the bean plant provide initial nourishment for the seedling.  豆类植物的子叶为幼苗提供最初的营养。|近义词：embryonic (in the context of early plant development) |反义词：monocot (referring to plants with a single cotyledon)
