dismissals:/dɪsˈmɪsəlz/ | n. |解雇；开除；驳回；解散 |The act of dismissing someone from a job or position; the act of rejecting or disregarding something.  |例句：The recent dismissals have caused widespread unrest among the employees. 近期员工的解雇导致了员工中广泛的不满。 |例句：His arguments were dismissed as irrelevant.  他的论点被驳回为无关紧要。|近义词： firings, sackings, terminations,  rejections |反义词： hirings, appointments, acceptances |名词
