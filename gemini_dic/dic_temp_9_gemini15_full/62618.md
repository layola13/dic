词源:
The word "enfoldments" is derived from the verb "enfold," which combines the prefix "en-" (meaning "in" or "into") with the Old English word "fealdan" (meaning "to fold").  Therefore, "enfoldments" literally refers to the act or result of being folded in or enveloped.
