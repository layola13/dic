over-idealistic: /ˌoʊvərˌaɪdiˈælɪstɪk/ | adj. | 过于理想化的；空想的 | Excessively idealistic; impractical.  Characterized by or having ideals that are unrealistic or impractical.  |  His over-idealistic vision of a utopian society was ultimately unattainable. 他对乌托邦社会的过于理想化的设想最终是无法实现的。 | 近义词：utopian, unrealistic, impractical, visionary, romantic | 反义词：realistic, pragmatic, practical, down-to-earth


USAGE->

She is over-idealistic about the future of her company. = 她对公司未来过于理想化。
