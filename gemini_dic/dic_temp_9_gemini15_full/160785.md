positive:/ˈpɒzətɪv/ /ˈpɑːzətɪv/ | adj., n. |积极的；正面的；肯定的；阳性的 |

英文解析：
As an adjective, "positive" means expressing or feeling certainty or confidence; optimistic or cheerful;  showing or involving the presence of something.  As a noun, it refers to a positive statement, result, or image; or the presence of something, such as a disease.

例句：
She has a positive attitude towards life. = 她对生活抱有积极的态度。

He received a positive response to his job application. = 他收到了对他求职申请的积极回应。


近义词：optimistic, cheerful, affirmative, confident, certain, favorable, constructive


反义词：negative, pessimistic, doubtful, uncertain, unfavorable, destructive


词性：adj., n.
