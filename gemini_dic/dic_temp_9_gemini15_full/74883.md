freshener:/ˈfreʃənər/| n. |清新剂，空气清新剂|A substance or device used to make something smell fresher or cleaner.  It often refers to air fresheners, but can also apply to other products like fabric fresheners or laundry fresheners.|例句：She sprayed air freshener to mask the smell of pet odor. 她喷了空气清新剂来掩盖宠物的气味。
例句：The fabric freshener left my clothes smelling wonderfully clean.  织物清新剂让我的衣服闻起来非常干净。|近义词：deodorizer, odor eliminator |反义词:None specifically, but contextually it could be something that makes a space smell worse. |名词
