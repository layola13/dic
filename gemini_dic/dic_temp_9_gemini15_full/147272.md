overmining:/ˌoʊvərˈmaɪnɪŋ/ | v. |过度开采；过量采矿 |The act of mining beyond sustainable levels, leading to depletion of resources and environmental damage.  It often involves extracting resources faster than they can be replenished, resulting in long-term negative consequences. |例句：Overmining of coal has led to land subsidence and water pollution in many areas.  过度开采煤炭导致许多地区的土地沉降和水污染。

例句：The relentless overmining of the copper deposits has threatened the local ecosystem.  对铜矿的无情过度开采已经威胁到了当地的生态系统。


近义词：overexploitation, depletion, exhaustion |反义词：conservation, preservation, sustainable harvesting |词性：动词
