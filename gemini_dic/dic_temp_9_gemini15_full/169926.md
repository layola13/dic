It can cause pain, difficulty urinating, and other symptoms.  The condition can be acute (sudden onset) or chronic (long-lasting).|例句：He was diagnosed with prostatitis and prescribed antibiotics. 他被诊断出患有前列腺炎，并开了抗生素。|例句：Chronic prostatitis can be difficult to treat and may require long-term management. 慢性前列腺炎难以治疗，可能需要长期管理。|近义词：prostate infection, prostatic inflammation|反义词：N/A
