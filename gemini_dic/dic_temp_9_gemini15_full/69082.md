faunas:/ˈfɔːnəz/ | n. | 动物群；动物区系 | The faunas of the two regions are quite different.  两个地区的动物群系大相径庭。|近义词：animal life, zoology, wildlife |反义词：flora (植物群)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fauna of this rainforest is incredibly diverse. = 这个雨林的动物群极其多样化。
Scientists study the fauna of the Amazon. = 科学家们研究亚马逊的动物群。


一般过去时:
The local fauna was severely impacted by the drought. = 当地的动物群受到了干旱的严重影响。
Researchers documented the fauna of the island in the 1950s. = 研究人员在20世纪50年代记录了该岛的动物群。


现在完成时:
We have observed significant changes in the local fauna since the introduction of the invasive species. = 自引入入侵物种以来，我们已经观察到当地动物群的显著变化。
Scientists have compiled a comprehensive list of the region's fauna. = 科学家们已经编制了该地区动物群的完整清单。


过去完成时:
Before the dam was built, the river's fauna was much richer. = 在大坝建成之前，这条河的动物群要丰富得多。
They had already studied the fauna of the area extensively before starting the project. = 在开始项目之前，他们已经对该地区的动物群进行了广泛的研究。


将来时:
Conservation efforts will hopefully protect the unique fauna of the region. = 保护工作有望保护该地区独特的动物群。
Scientists predict that climate change will significantly alter the fauna of the Arctic. = 科学家预测，气候变化将极大地改变北极的动物群。


虚拟语气:
If the habitat were protected, the fauna would thrive. = 如果栖息地得到保护，动物群就会繁荣发展。
I wish the unique fauna of this island were better protected. = 我希望这个岛屿独特的动物群能够得到更好的保护。


被动语态:

The effects of climate change on the fauna are being studied. = 气候变化对动物群的影响正在被研究。


疑问句:
What kind of fauna is found in this ecosystem? = 在这个生态系统中发现了什么类型的动物群？

否定句:

They didn't find any endangered fauna during their expedition. = 他们在探险中没有发现任何濒危动物。

词源:
From Latin *fauna, from Fauna, the Roman goddess of fertility and the earth, equivalent to Greek Pan.  Ultimately, the word is related to the Greek word for "all," indicating a comprehensive listing of animal life.
