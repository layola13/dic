dioicous:/daɪˈaɪkəs/ | adj. |雌雄异株的；雌雄异体的| Having the male and female reproductive organs borne on separate individuals.  Dioecious plants, for example, have separate male and female plants.  The term is often used in botany and zoology to describe species where the sexes are separate. | Example: Holly is a dioecious plant, meaning there are separate male and female plants.  冬青是雌雄异株的植物，这意味着有单独的雄性和雌性植物。|近义词：unisexual|反义词：monoecious
