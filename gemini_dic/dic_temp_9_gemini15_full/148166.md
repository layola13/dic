overservice:/ˈoʊvərsɜːrvɪs/| n. |过度服务；服务过多|The act of providing more service than is necessary or desired, often leading to inconvenience or dissatisfaction.|例句：The overservice of the waiter made the customers feel uncomfortable.  服务员过度殷勤让顾客感觉不舒服。|近义词：excessive service, overattention|反义词：underservice, neglect

He overservices his clients. = 他过度服务他的客户。
