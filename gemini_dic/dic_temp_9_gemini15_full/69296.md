featuring: /ˈfiːtʃərɪŋ/ | v. | 以…为特色；由…主演；以…为主要内容 |  To include (someone or something) as a prominent part of something; to star in (a film, etc.). |  例句：The concert featured a performance by the renowned pianist.  这场音乐会以著名钢琴家的表演为特色。

The new album features several guest artists. 这张新专辑有几位客串艺术家。


近义词：showcasing, highlighting, starring in, presenting


反义词：excluding, omitting


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The film features a young actress. = 这部电影由一位年轻女演员主演。
The exhibition features works by local artists. = 展览展出了当地艺术家的作品。


一般过去时:
The show featured a spectacular dance routine. = 节目中有一个精彩的舞蹈表演。
The article featured the latest scientific discoveries. = 这篇文章介绍了最新的科学发现。


现在完成时:
The magazine has featured many successful entrepreneurs. = 这本杂志已经刊登过许多成功的企业家的故事。
This restaurant has featured a different menu every month. = 这家餐厅每个月都有不同的菜单。


过去完成时:
The museum had featured a dinosaur exhibit before the renovation. = 在翻修之前，博物馆曾展出过恐龙展览。
The newspaper had featured the politician's scandal prior to the election. = 在选举之前，报纸已经刊登了这位政治家的丑闻。


将来时:
The festival will feature a variety of musical performances. = 节日将会有各种各样的音乐表演。
The next issue will feature an interview with the author. = 下一期将刊登对这位作者的采访。


虚拟语气:
If the movie featured a better storyline, it would be more successful. = 如果这部电影的情节更好，它会更成功。
I wish the album featured more original songs. = 我希望这张专辑有更多原创歌曲。


被动语态:
The singer was featured on the album cover. = 歌手在专辑封面上。
The product was heavily featured in the advertisement. = 该产品在广告中被重点推荐。


疑问句:
Does the article feature any new research? = 这篇文章是否介绍了任何新的研究？
Which artists will be featured in the concert? = 音乐会上将有哪些艺术家演出？


否定句:
The menu doesn't feature any vegetarian options. = 菜单上没有任何素食选择。
The documentary didn't feature any interviews with the victims. = 这部纪录片没有采访任何受害者。
