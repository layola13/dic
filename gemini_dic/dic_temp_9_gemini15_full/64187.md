equestrian:/ɪˈkwɛstriən/| adj. n.| 骑马的；马术的；骑手 | Relating to horses or horse riding.  An equestrian is a person who rides horses, especially one who competes in equestrian sports. |例句：She is a skilled equestrian, winning many competitions. 她是一位技艺精湛的骑手，赢得了许多比赛。

例句：The equestrian statue of the general is impressive.  将军的骑马雕像令人印象深刻。


近义词：horsey, mounted

反义词：pedestrian (in the sense of not involving horses)


词性:形容词，名词
