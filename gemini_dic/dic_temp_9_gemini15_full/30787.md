cetaceans:/ˈsiːteɪʃənz/ | n. |鲸类动物 |Marine mammals that include whales, dolphins, and porpoises.  They are characterized by their streamlined bodies, powerful tails, and adaptations for aquatic life. |例句：Whales, dolphins, and porpoises are all examples of cetaceans. 鲸鱼、海豚和鼠海豚都是鲸类动物的例子。  The study of cetaceans is a fascinating field of marine biology.  研究鲸类动物是海洋生物学中一个引人入胜的领域。|近义词：whales, dolphins, porpoises (but this is not a perfect synonym as it is a broader category)|反义词：None (it's a broad category)|名词

词源:
The word "cetacean" comes from the Latin word "cetus," meaning "whale," and the suffix "-acean," which indicates belonging to a particular group or family.  Therefore, "cetacean" literally means "belonging to the whale family."
