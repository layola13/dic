reconsigned:/ˌriːkənˈsaɪnd/| vt. |重新托运；转运|To send (goods) again by a different means of transport or to a different destination.|The damaged goods were reconsigned to a different warehouse.|近义词：reship, forward, re-route|反义词：receive, accept


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reconsign the goods to a new location. = 他们将货物重新托运到一个新的地点。
The company reconsigns packages daily. = 公司每天都重新托运包裹。


一般过去时:
The shipper reconsigned the package after finding the wrong address. = 发货人发现地址错误后重新托运了包裹。
We reconsigned the shipment due to unforeseen circumstances. = 由于不可预见的情况，我们重新托运了货物。


现在完成时:
They have reconsigned the damaged goods to the manufacturer. = 他们已将损坏的货物重新托运给制造商。
The company has reconsigned several shipments this month. = 这个月公司已经重新托运了几批货物。


过去完成时:
The client had reconsigned the order before realizing the mistake. = 客户在意识到错误之前已经重新托运了订单。
The goods had been reconsigned several times before reaching their final destination. = 货物在到达最终目的地之前已经被重新托运了几次。


将来时:
They will reconsign the goods if the recipient is unavailable. = 如果收件人不可用，他们将重新托运货物。
The company will reconsign the damaged items next week. = 公司下周将重新托运损坏的物品。


虚拟语气:
If the address was incorrect, we would have reconsigned the package. = 如果地址不正确，我们将重新托运包裹。
It would be better if they reconsigned the goods via air freight. = 如果他们通过空运重新托运货物会更好。


被动语态:
