mailbombs:/ˈmeɪlbɒmz/| n. |邮件炸弹；大量邮件攻击|A mailbomb is a type of denial-of-service attack where the victim receives an overwhelming amount of email messages, often unsolicited and sometimes containing malicious content.  This flood of emails can overwhelm the recipient's email server, making it difficult or impossible to access their inbox or even causing the server to crash.|Example: The company was hit by a mailbomb, rendering their email system unusable for hours.  公司遭到邮件炸弹攻击，导致其电子邮件系统瘫痪数小时。|近义词：email bombing, email flood, spam flood|反义词：None|名词
