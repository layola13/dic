hairsplitter:/ˈheərsplɪtər/| n.|吹毛求疵者，墨守成规者|A person who makes very fine distinctions or who is excessively concerned with minor details.  Someone who focuses on trivial details and insignificant differences. |例句：He's such a hairsplitter; he spent an hour arguing about the placement of a single comma. 他真是个吹毛求疵的人；他花了一个小时争论一个逗号的位置。

例句：Don't be such a hairsplitter; the difference is negligible. 不要那么吹毛求疵；差别微不足道。


近义词：nitpicker, pedant, perfectionist, stickler


反义词：approximator, summarizer
