pelecan:/ˈpɛləkæn/| n. |鹈鹕|A large waterbird with a long beak and a pouch under the beak for catching fish.|例句：The pelican gracefully scooped up a fish with its pouch.  这只鹈鹕优雅地用它的喉囊舀起一条鱼。|例句：We watched a flock of pelicans soaring over the ocean. 我们看着一群鹈鹕在海洋上空翱翔。|近义词：bird,waterfowl|反义词: N/A
