meaningfully:/ˈmiːnɪŋfli/| adj.| 有意义地；意味深长地 |  In a way that is full of meaning or significance; purposefully.  It implies intention and impact. |例句：He looked at her meaningfully. 他意味深长地看了她一眼。

例句：She paused meaningfully before answering. 她停顿了一下，然后意味深长地回答。


近义词：significantly, purposefully, meaningfully, pointedly, expressively

反义词：pointlessly, meaninglessly, unimportantly, insignificantly
