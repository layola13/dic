entozoic:/ˌɛnˈtoʊzɔɪk/| adj.|内寄生的；内生的|Living within the body of an animal.  This term describes organisms that live within the body of another animal, often as parasites.  |例句：Many parasites are entozoic, meaning they live inside their host's body.  许多寄生虫是内寄生的，这意味着它们生活在宿主的体内。


例句：The researchers discovered a new species of entozoic protozoa in the intestines of the fish. 研究人员在鱼的肠道中发现了一种新的内寄生原生动物。


近义词：endoparasite (n.)内寄生虫,  parasitic (adj.) 寄生的


反义词：ectozoic (adj.)体外的，外寄生的


词性:形容词
