half-ripened: /ˌhɑːfˈraɪpənd/ | adj. | 半熟的，未完全成熟的 | Partially ripened; not fully mature.  The fruit is still slightly hard and may lack the full sweetness and aroma of a fully ripened fruit.  | 例句：The half-ripened mangoes were still a bit tart. 半熟的芒果仍然有点酸。

The half-ripened avocados were perfect for guacamole. 半熟的牛油果很适合做鳄梨酱。


近义词：underripe, partly ripe, immature | 反义词：ripe, overripe, mature

词性：形容词

词源:
The word "half-ripened" is a compound adjective formed by combining "half" (meaning "one of two equal parts") and "ripened" (the past participle of the verb "ripen," meaning "to become mature").  The word reflects a state of partial maturity.
