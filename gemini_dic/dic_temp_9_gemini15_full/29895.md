cauterization:/ˌkɔːtəraɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |灼烧术；烧灼法|The process of burning tissue to destroy it or to stop bleeding.  This is often done with a heated instrument or chemical substance. |例句：The surgeon used cauterization to stop the bleeding after the surgery. 外科医生在手术后用烧灼法止血。|例句：Cauterization of the wound prevented further infection.  烧灼伤口阻止了进一步感染。|近义词：cautery, burning, searing |反义词：healing, repair
