lettergram: /ˈlɛtərˌɡræm/ | n. |字母电报，字母图 | A lettergram is a message sent by telegraph, typically a short one,  consisting primarily of letters or words, rather than a longer, more detailed communication.  It’s essentially a telegram expressed using letters. | Example:  She sent a lettergram to her family announcing her arrival. 她给家人发了一封字母电报，宣布她已抵达。 | 近义词：telegram, cablegram | 反义词：long letter, detailed message |
