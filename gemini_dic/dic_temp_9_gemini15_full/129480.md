non-ambitious:/ˌnɒn æmˈbɪʃəs/| adj. |不雄心勃勃的，缺乏野心的|Lacking ambition or strong desire for achievement or advancement.|He led a non-ambitious life, content with his simple job and quiet routine. 他过着不追求上进的生活，对简单的工作和平静的日常生活感到满足。|近义词：unambitious, unaspiring, modest|反义词：ambitious, aspiring, driven

She remains non-ambitious despite her potential. = 尽管她很有潜力，但她仍然缺乏雄心。
