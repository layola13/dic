partite:/ˈpɑːrtaɪt/| adj.| 分部的；分成几部分的；多边的 |  Having or composed of parts; divided into parts.  It's often used to describe something that is divided into a specific number of parts, such as a musical composition or a building's structure.  |例句：The concerto is a three-partite work. 这首协奏曲是三段体的作品。

The building has a quadripartite structure. 这座建筑物具有四部分的结构。|近义词：divided, multipartite, sectional |反义词：undivided, single, whole |词性：形容词
