hagbut:/ˈhæɡbʌt/| n.|大型火炮，一种早期火炮|A hagbut was a type of early firearm, a large, heavy, matchlock hand cannon, typically used in the 15th and 16th centuries.  It was too large and cumbersome to be easily carried by a single soldier, often requiring a stand or rest.| Example: The soldiers positioned their hagbuts on the ramparts before the siege began.  士兵们在围攻开始前将他们的哈克步枪架在城墙上。|近义词：arquebus, hand cannon|反义词：无
