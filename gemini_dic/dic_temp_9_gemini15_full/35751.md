cochleare:/koʊˈkliːəri/| n. |耳匙；蜗牛壳状物| A cochlear is a spoon-shaped instrument, or a structure resembling a spoon, particularly one used in medicine or for dispensing small amounts of medicine.  It can also refer to something shaped like a snail shell. | The pharmacist measured out the medicine using a small cochlear. 药剂师用一个小耳匙量出药。The spiral structure of the inner ear is called the cochlea. 内耳的螺旋结构被称为耳蜗。|  spoon, scoop |  (no direct antonym)|名词
