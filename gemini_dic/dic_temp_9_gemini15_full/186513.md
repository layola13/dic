rickety:/ˈrɪkɪti/| adj. |摇摇欲坠的，不结实的，破旧的 |  Describing something that is likely to collapse or break because it is old, weak, or poorly made. It implies instability and a potential for danger. |例句：The rickety old bridge looked unsafe to cross.  那座摇摇欲坠的旧桥看起来不安全，无法通行。

The rickety chair nearly collapsed when he sat down. 他坐下时，那把摇摇欲坠的椅子差点倒塌。


近义词：shaky, unsteady, dilapidated, ramshackle, fragile


反义词：solid, sturdy, stable, strong, robust


词性：形容词
