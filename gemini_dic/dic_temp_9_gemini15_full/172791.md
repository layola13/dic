quadrilateral:/ˌkwɑːdrɪˈlætərəl/| n. |四边形 |A quadrilateral is a polygon with four sides and four angles.  It is a two-dimensional closed shape.  Examples include squares, rectangles, rhombuses, trapezoids, and kites. |例句：A square is a type of quadrilateral. 正方形是一种四边形。

The diagram shows a quadrilateral with unequal sides.  图表显示了一个不等边四边形。|近义词：quadrangle, tetragon |反义词：triangle (three-sided polygon), pentagon (five-sided polygon)
