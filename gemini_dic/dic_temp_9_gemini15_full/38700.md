condescendence:/ˌkɒndɪˈsɛndəns/| /ˌkɑːndɪˈsɛndəns/| n. | 屈尊；俯就；恩赐；高傲；傲慢 | The act of condescending; patronizing behavior showing a superior attitude.  It implies a feeling of superiority and a willingness to treat someone as inferior. |例句：Her condescendence towards her colleagues made her unpopular.  她的傲慢使得她在同事中不受欢迎。

例句：He treated the request with a hint of condescendence, implying it was beneath him. 他带着一丝屈尊的态度处理了这个请求，暗示这对他来说是微不足道的。


近义词：patronage, condescension, superciliousness, disdain

反义词：humility, respect, equality
