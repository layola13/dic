propagating:/ˈprɒpəɡeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 传播；繁殖；增殖 | Spreading or causing to spread; reproducing or multiplying.  | 例句：The fire quickly propagated through the dry forest.  大火迅速蔓延到干燥的森林中。 | 例句：The plant propagates by seeds. 这植物靠种子繁殖。| 近义词：disseminate, spread, multiply, reproduce | 反义词：suppress, extinguish, destroy

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is propagating its new product through social media. = 公司正在通过社交媒体推广其新产品。
Plants propagate themselves through seeds and spores. = 植物通过种子和孢子繁殖自身。


一般过去时:
The rumor propagated rapidly throughout the town. = 谣言迅速传遍了整个城镇。
The farmers propagated new varieties of wheat. = 农民繁殖了新的麦子品种。


现在完成时:
The government has propagated a new policy to combat poverty. = 政府已经推行了一项新的扶贫政策。
The gardener has successfully propagated several rare orchids. = 园丁成功地繁殖了几种稀有的兰花。


过去完成时:
Before the meeting, they had propagated the idea widely among the staff. = 会议之前，他们已经在员工中广泛宣传了这个想法。
The scientists had propagated the bacteria in the laboratory for several weeks. = 科学家们已经在实验室里繁殖细菌好几个星期了。


将来时:
The company will propagate its message through a series of advertisements. = 公司将通过一系列广告宣传其信息。
They will propagate the new strain of rice to increase food production. = 他们将繁殖这种新的水稻品种以增加粮食产量。


虚拟语气:
If they propagated the information more effectively, they would have achieved better results. = 如果他们更有效地传播信息，他们就能取得更好的结果。
I wish they had propagated the news more carefully. = 我希望他们更谨慎地传播这个消息。


被动语态:
The new technology was propagated by a team of engineers. = 这项新技术是由一个工程师团队推广的。
The virus was propagated through contaminated water. = 病毒是通过受污染的水传播的。


疑问句:
How does this plant propagate? = 这种植物是如何繁殖的？
How is the company propagating its brand? = 公司是如何推广其品牌的？


否定句:
The rumor did not propagate as quickly as expected. = 谣言传播的速度不如预期快。
They failed to propagate the new technique effectively. = 他们未能有效地推广这项新技术。
