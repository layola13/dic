clubs:/klʌbz/| n.|俱乐部；纸牌中的梅花|A club is an organization of people with a common interest, often social or recreational.  It can also refer to the suit of clubs in playing cards.|例句：He joined a golf club. 他加入了一个高尔夫俱乐部。|例句：She won the hand with a pair of clubs. 她用一对梅花赢得了这一局。|近义词：society, association, organization|反义词：None directly applicable.
