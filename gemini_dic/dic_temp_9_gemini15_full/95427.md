incinerates:/ɪnˈsɪnəreɪts/| vt.|焚烧；烧成灰烬|To burn something completely|The factory incinerates its waste. 工厂焚烧其废物。|近义词：burn, cremate|反义词：preserve, save


USAGE->

The factory incinerates waste daily. = 工厂每天焚烧垃圾。
He incinerates all his old documents. = 他焚烧了他所有的旧文件。

They incinerated the contaminated materials. = 他们焚烧了受污染的材料。

现在完成时:
The government has incinerated a large amount of hazardous waste. = 政府已经焚烧了大量的危险废物。
