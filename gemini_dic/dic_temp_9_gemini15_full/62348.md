endermatic: /ˌɛndərˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 内生的；内源性的 |  Relating to or denoting processes or phenomena that originate within a system, rather than being imposed from outside.  Endermatic processes are those that arise from within a particular system or body, as opposed to exodermatic processes, which are imposed from outside.  The term is frequently used in geology and biology. |

例句：

The formation of these crystals is endermatic, originating from within the magma chamber.  = 这些晶体的形成是内生的，起源于岩浆室内部。

The study showed that the disease had an endermatic origin, rather than being caused by an external infection. = 研究表明，这种疾病是内源性起源的，而不是由外部感染引起的。


近义词：intrinsic, inherent, internal, endogenous

反义词：exodermatic, extrinsic, external, exogenous

词性:形容词
