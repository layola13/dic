fatless:/ˈfætləs/| adj.|不含脂肪的，瘦的|Free from fat.  This term usually describes food that has had its fat removed or is naturally low in fat.|例句：This yogurt is fatless and low in sugar. 这款酸奶不含脂肪，而且低糖。|例句：She chose the fatless chicken breast over the fried chicken. 她选择了去皮鸡胸肉而不是炸鸡。|近义词：lean, low-fat, fat-free|反义词：fatty, high-fat
