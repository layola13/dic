carburizes:/ˈkɑːrbəraɪzɪz/ | vt. |使渗碳；使渗入碳 |To impregnate (iron or steel) with carbon, typically by heating it in the presence of a carbon-containing material, to increase its hardness.  |例句：The steel was carburized to increase its hardness and wear resistance.  钢材经过渗碳处理以提高其硬度和耐磨性。

The gears were carburized before being heat-treated.  齿轮在热处理之前进行了渗碳处理。

近义词： case-harden, cement | 反义词：decarburize
