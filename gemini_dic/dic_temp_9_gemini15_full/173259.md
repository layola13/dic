quasi-consciously:/ˈkwɑːziˈkɒnʃəsli/| adv. |似有意识地；半意识地|  Approaching or being in a state of consciousness but not fully aware; acting or occurring in a manner suggestive of consciousness but without full awareness or intention.  It implies a level of awareness that is less than full consciousness but more than unconsciousness. |例句：She quasi-consciously reached for the phone, driven by an instinct she couldn't explain. 她似有意识地伸手去拿电话，是被一种她无法解释的本能驱使。

例句：He quasi-consciously avoided eye contact, feeling shy and uncomfortable. 他似有意识地避免眼神接触，感到害羞和不舒服。


近义词：subconsciously, unconsciously (but with a slightly higher degree of awareness than "unconsciously"), semiconsciously

反义词：consciously, deliberately, intentionally


词性: 副词
