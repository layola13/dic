epiphanies:/ɪˈpɪfəniz/ | n. |顿悟，灵感，显现 | A sudden, profound realization or insight; a moment of clear understanding.  |例句：The solution to the problem came to him in a sudden epiphany.  解决问题的办法突然灵光一闪出现在他脑海中。

例句：She experienced an epiphany while meditating, understanding the true meaning of life.  她在冥想时顿悟了，理解了生命的真谛。


近义词：revelation, insight, realization, illumination, breakthrough

反义词：confusion, misunderstanding, ignorance


词性: 名词
