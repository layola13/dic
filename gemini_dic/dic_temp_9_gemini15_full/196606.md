seti:/ˈsɛti/| n.|搜寻地外文明计划|The Search for Extraterrestrial Intelligence, a program dedicated to searching for signs of intelligent life beyond Earth.  It involves using radio telescopes to listen for signals from other civilizations.|例句：SETI scientists are constantly scanning the skies for evidence of extraterrestrial communication.  SETI科学家们不断扫描天空，寻找地外通讯的证据。|近义词：alien search, extraterrestrial intelligence search|反义词：无
