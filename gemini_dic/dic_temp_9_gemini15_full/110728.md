linesmen:/ˈlaɪnzmen/| n. |助理裁判员；架线工人|A linesman is someone who assists a referee in sports like soccer or football, primarily by signaling whether the ball has gone out of bounds. In other contexts, a linesman is a worker who installs and maintains power lines.|例句：The linesman signaled a corner kick.  助理裁判示意角球。|例句：The linesmen were working high above the ground, installing new power lines. 架线工人在高空作业，安装新的电力线。|近义词：assistant referee, lineman (in American football)|反义词：referee (in the context of sports)|名词
