panegyrics:/pænəˈdʒɪrɪks/ | n. | 赞辞；颂词；颂扬 | Formal praise, especially in a public speech or written text.  A panegyric is a formal expression of praise, often celebratory in nature. It's typically delivered or written to honor someone or something. |  The mayor delivered a panegyric to the retiring fire chief, highlighting his decades of service. 市长向即将退休的消防队长发表了赞扬性讲话，强调了他几十年的服务。

The author wrote a lengthy panegyric on the virtues of democracy. 作者撰写了一篇冗长的文章来赞扬民主的优点。 | eulogy, encomium, tribute | criticism, condemnation, denunciation | 名词
