denigrate:/ˈdɛnɪɡreɪt/| vt.|贬低；诋毁；污蔑|To attack the reputation or integrity of someone or something.|例句：He denigrated her achievements, claiming they were due to luck rather than skill. 他贬低了她的成就，声称那是运气而不是技巧的结果。

例句：The politician tried to denigrate his opponent's character in the debate.  这位政治家试图在辩论中诋毁对手的品格。


近义词：belittle, disparage, defame, slander, vilify|反义词：praise, commend, extol, laud|动词


词源：源自于拉丁语“de-” (向下) + “niger” (黑色) ，字面意思是指“使变黑”，引申为玷污名誉。


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He denigrates his colleagues' work regularly. = 他经常贬低同事的工作。
She denigrates the value of traditional education. = 她贬低了传统教育的价值。


一般过去时:
The critic denigrated the author's latest novel. = 批评家贬低了作者的最新小说。
They denigrated the efforts of the volunteers. = 他们贬低了志愿者的努力。


现在完成时:
He has denigrated her reputation for years. = 他多年来一直诋毁她的名声。
The media has denigrated the company's image. = 媒体已经损害了公司的形象。


过去完成时:
They had denigrated the project before it even started. = 他们甚至在项目开始之前就贬低了它。
She had denigrated his abilities in front of everyone. = 她当着所有人的面贬低了他的能力。


将来时:
He will denigrate his rival in the upcoming election. = 他将在即将到来的选举中贬低他的竞争对手。
They will denigrate the opposing team's performance. = 他们将贬低对方球队的表现。


虚拟语气:
If he denigrated her again, I would intervene. = 如果他再次贬低她，我会介入。
I wish he hadn't denigrated my efforts. = 我真希望他没有贬低我的努力。


被动语态:
His work was denigrated by the critics. = 他的作品被评论家贬低了。
Her achievements were denigrated by the media. = 她的成就被媒体贬低了。


疑问句:
Did he denigrate her work intentionally? = 他故意贬低她的工作吗？
Why would anyone denigrate such a worthy cause? = 为什么有人会贬低如此有价值的事业？


否定句:
He did not denigrate her contributions to the project. = 他没有贬低她对项目的贡献。
They did not denigrate the importance of education. = 他们没有贬低教育的重要性。


PHRASE->
