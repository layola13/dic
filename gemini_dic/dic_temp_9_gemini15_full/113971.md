maggots:/ˈmæɡəts/| n.|蛆，幼虫|The larvae of a fly, especially a blowfly, that feeds on decaying organic matter.  Maggots are often found in decaying meat or garbage.|例句：The decaying apple was teeming with maggots.  腐烂的苹果里爬满了蛆。|例句：We found maggots in the leftover food.  我们在剩菜里发现了蛆。|近义词：larvae, grubs, worms|反义词:None.
