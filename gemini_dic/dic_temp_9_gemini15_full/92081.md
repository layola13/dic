hyperreactive: /ˌhaɪpərriˈæktɪv/ | adj. | 过度反应的，反应过敏的 | Excessively reactive; reacting in an exaggerated or extreme manner to a stimulus.  It often implies a disproportionate response to a relatively minor trigger. | 例句：He has a hyperreactive immune system, leading to frequent allergies. 他有一个过度活跃的免疫系统，导致频繁的过敏。


The child showed a hyperreactive response to criticism, bursting into tears easily.  这个孩子对批评反应过度，很容易哭起来。|近义词：oversensitive, overreactive, hypersensitive | 反义词：hyporeactive, unresponsive, indifferent
