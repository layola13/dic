epocha:/ˈɛpək/| n.|纪元，时代；时期|A period of time in history or a person's life, typically one marked by notable events or particular characteristics.  An epoch is a significant period of time, often distinguished by major events or changes.|例句：The Victorian epoch was a time of great industrial expansion. 维多利亚时代是工业大扩张的时期。
The discovery of penicillin marked a new epoch in medicine. 青霉素的发现标志着医学史上的一个新纪元。|近义词：era, period, age|反义词：moment, instant
