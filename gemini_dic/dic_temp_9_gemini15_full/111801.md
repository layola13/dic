log-out: /ˈlɒɡ aʊt/ | /ˈlɑːɡ aʊt/ | vt. | 注销，登出 | To end a session on a computer system or network.  | Example:  Remember to log out of your account before leaving.  记住在离开之前注销你的帐户。

| Example: I logged out of my email after finishing my work. 我完成工作后注销了我的邮箱。| 近义词：sign out, disconnect | 反义词：log in, sign in | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He logs out every night before going to bed. 他每天晚上睡觉前都会注销。
She logs out of her social media accounts regularly. 她定期注销她的社交媒体帐户。


一般过去时:
I logged out of the system after saving my work. 我保存工作后注销了系统。
They logged out of the game when the server crashed. 服务器崩溃时他们退出了游戏。


现在完成时:
He has logged out of his account already. 他已经注销了他的帐户。
She hasn't logged out yet. 她还没注销。


过去完成时:
I had logged out before the system went down. 系统瘫痪之前我已经注销了。
They had logged out before the power outage. 停电前他们已经注销了。


将来时:
I will log out when I finish this report. 我完成这份报告后就会注销。
She will log out before the meeting starts. 会议开始前她会注销。


虚拟语气:
If I had remembered to log out, my account wouldn't have been hacked. 如果我记得注销，我的帐户就不会被黑客入侵。
I wish I had logged out earlier. 我希望我早点注销。


被动语态:
The account was logged out automatically. 帐户已自动注销。
All sessions were logged out due to a system upgrade. 由于系统升级，所有会话都已注销。


疑问句:
Have you logged out yet? 你注销了吗？
Did he log out before leaving? 他离开之前注销了吗？


否定句:
I didn't log out last night. 我昨晚没有注销。
She hasn't logged out of her computer. 她还没有注销她的电脑。


PHRASE->
