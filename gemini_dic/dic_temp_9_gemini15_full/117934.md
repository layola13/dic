member:/ˈmɛmbər/| n. |成员，会员；部件；(身体)部位 |A member of a group or organization is one of the people who belong to it.  A part of something larger. |例句：He is a member of the committee. 他是委员会成员。|例句：The engine's a complex member of parts. 这台引擎是由复杂的部件组成的。|近义词：participant, component, element |反义词：non-member, outsider


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a member of the club. = 他是俱乐部的一员。
She is a valued member of our team. = 她是我们团队中宝贵的成员。


一般过去时:
He was a member of the parliament. = 他曾是议会成员。
She was a loyal member of the church. = 她曾是教会的忠实成员。


现在完成时:
He has been a member of the association for ten years. = 他已经加入该协会十年了。
She has been an active member of the community. = 她一直是社区的积极成员。


过去完成时:
He had been a member of the society before he moved. = 他搬家之前是该协会的成员。
She had been a member of the board for many years. = 她多年来一直是董事会成员。


将来时:
He will be a member of the board next year. = 他明年将成为董事会成员。
She will remain a member of the club. = 她将继续是俱乐部的成员。


虚拟语气:
If he were a member, he would have voting rights. = 如果他是会员，他将有投票权。
I wish I were a member of that exclusive club. = 我真希望我是那个高级俱乐部的会员。


被动语态:
He was elected a member of the council. = 他被选为理事会成员。
She was appointed a member of the committee. = 她被任命为委员会成员。


疑问句:
Is he a member of the team? = 他是团队成员吗？
Are you a member of this organization? = 你是这个组织的成员吗？


否定句:
He is not a member of the club. = 他不是俱乐部成员。
She is no longer a member of the group. = 她不再是该小组的成员了。


PHRASE->
