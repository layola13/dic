misconceives:/ˌmɪskənˈsiːvz/| vt.|误解，误认为|To have a mistaken understanding of something or someone.|He misconceives the meaning of the poem. 他误解了这首诗的意思。|近义词：misunderstand, misinterpret|反义词：understand, comprehend

She misconceives her own capabilities. = 她误解了自己的能力。
