normalise:/ˈnɔːrməlaɪz/ /ˈnɔːrməlaɪz/ | vt. |标准化；使正常化；使标准一致|To make something normal or standard.  It involves adjusting or conforming to a standard or norm. This can apply to various contexts, from data to social behavior.|例句：The government is trying to normalise relations with its neighbour. 政府正试图使其与邻国关系正常化。

例句：We need to normalise the data before we can analyze it. 我们需要先标准化数据才能进行分析。

近义词：standardize, regularize, normalize

反义词：disrupt, destabilize
