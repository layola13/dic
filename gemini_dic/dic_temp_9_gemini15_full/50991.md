desirer:/dɪˈzaɪərər/| n. |渴望者；热望者|A desirer is someone who has a strong longing or wish for something.  They are characterized by their intense desire and pursuit of a particular goal or object.|He was a fervent desirer of knowledge, spending countless hours in the library.|近义词：aspirant, seeker |反义词：indifferent person

The desirer seeks knowledge constantly. = 渴望者不断寻求知识。
She is a strong desirer of success. = 她是一位强烈渴望成功的渴望者。

一般过去时:

否定句:

词源:
源自desire (渴望，欲望) + -er (表示人)。  desire的词源追溯到古法语desirer，最终源于拉丁语desiderare (渴望，想要)。
