obtain:/əbˈteɪn/| vt.|获得，得到，取得|to get something, especially by making an effort|例句：She obtained a degree in law. 她获得了法律学位。
例句：He tried to obtain a visa. 他试图获得签证。|近义词：acquire, achieve, gain, secure|反义词：lose, forfeit, relinquish|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She obtains good grades. = 她取得好成绩。
They obtain information from various sources. = 他们从各种来源获取信息。

一般过去时:
He obtained a scholarship last year. = 他去年获得了一笔奖学金。
I obtained permission to leave early. = 我获得了提前离开的许可。

现在完成时:
She has obtained a lot of experience in her field. = 她在她所在领域获得了丰富的经验。
They have obtained all the necessary documents. = 他们已经获得了所有必要的证件。

过去完成时:
He had obtained the necessary funds before starting the project. = 他在开始项目之前已经获得了必要的资金。
They had obtained the information from a reliable source. = 他们是从可靠的来源获得的信息。

将来时:
She will obtain a new job soon. = 她很快就会找到一份新工作。
They will obtain the results of the test next week. = 他们下周将获得测试结果。

虚拟语气:
If he obtained a better education, he would have more opportunities. = 如果他接受了更好的教育，他将会有更多机会。
I wish I had obtained that position. = 我真希望我得到了那个职位。

被动语态:
The information was obtained from confidential sources. = 信息是从机密来源获得的。
The necessary permits must be obtained before construction begins. = 在开始建设之前必须获得必要的许可证。

疑问句:
How did she obtain such a high score? = 她怎么能取得这么高的分数？
Where did he obtain the materials for his project? = 他从哪里获得他的项目材料？

否定句:
He did not obtain the necessary funding. = 他没有获得必要的资金。
They failed to obtain a visa in time. = 他们未能及时获得签证。
