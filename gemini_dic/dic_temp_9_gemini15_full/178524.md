reconverged:/ˌriːkənˈvɜːrdʒd/| v.|重新汇聚，重新融合|The separated streams of data have reconverged into a single, unified flow.  数据流分离后重新汇聚成一个统一的流。|近义词：reunited, recombined, merged |反义词：diverged, separated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The rivers reconverge downstream. = 河流在下游重新汇合。
The company reconverges its resources on core businesses. = 公司将资源重新集中在核心业务上。

一般过去时:
The two groups reconverged after the meeting. = 会议结束后，两组人重新会合了。
The separated parts of the machine reconverged smoothly. = 机器分离的部分顺利地重新组合在一起。

过去完成时:
