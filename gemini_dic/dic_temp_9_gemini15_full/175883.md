rated:/ˈreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被评级的，被评估的；等级高的 |  Giving a rating or assessment of something or someone.  It can refer to something that has received a specific rating, or it can describe something of high quality or status. | 例句：This hotel is rated four stars. 这家酒店被评为四星级。

The movie is rated R for violence. 这部电影因暴力而被评为R级。


近义词：ranked, graded, assessed, appraised | 反义词：unrated, unranked


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The restaurant is rated highly by customers. = 这家餐馆受到顾客的高度评价。
This product is rated five stars on Amazon. = 这款产品在亚马逊上获得五星好评。


一般过去时:
The film was rated PG-13 for some suggestive content. = 这部电影因部分暗示性内容被评为PG-13级。
The teacher rated the student's essay as excellent. = 老师将学生的论文评为优秀。


现在完成时:
The restaurant has been rated the best in the city for three years running. = 这家餐厅连续三年被评为全市最佳餐厅。
The app has been rated highly by users for its ease of use. = 该应用程序因其易用性而受到用户的青睐。


过去完成时:
The book had been rated a bestseller before the movie adaptation. = 这本书在电影改编之前就被评为畅销书。
The project had been rated as high-risk before funding was approved. = 该项目在获得资金批准之前就被评为高风险项目。


将来时:
The new hotel will be rated highly once it opens. = 这家新酒店开业后将获得高度评价。
The product will be rated by consumers after its launch. = 产品发布后将由消费者进行评价。


虚拟语气:
If the restaurant were rated higher, it would attract more customers. = 如果这家餐厅的评价更高，它就会吸引更多顾客。
I wish my essay had been rated higher. = 我希望我的论文评分更高。


被动语态:
The movie was rated poorly by critics. = 这部电影受到评论家的差评。
The performance was rated as outstanding by the judges. = 评委们将此次演出评为杰出。


疑问句:
Is this movie rated for children? = 这部电影适合儿童观看吗？
How is this product rated by consumers? = 消费者对这款产品的评价如何？


否定句:
The restaurant is not rated highly on Yelp. = 这家餐厅在Yelp上的评价不高。
The product was not rated suitable for young children. = 该产品不适合幼儿使用。
