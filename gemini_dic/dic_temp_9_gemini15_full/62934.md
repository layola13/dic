enmities:/ˈɛnɪmɪtiz/| n. |敌意；仇恨；不和| a feeling of hatred and ill will; hostility; antagonism.  Enmities can exist between individuals, groups, or nations, often stemming from past conflicts or perceived injustices. |例句：The long-standing enmities between the two families finally erupted into violence.  = 这两个家庭之间长期存在的敌意最终爆发为暴力冲突。

The deep enmities between the two countries made peaceful negotiations impossible. = 两国之间根深蒂固的敌意使得和平谈判成为不可能。|近义词：hostility, antagonism, animosity, hatred, ill will|反义词：friendship, amity, concord, harmony|名词
