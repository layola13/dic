sentiment:/ˈsɛntɪmənt/ | n. | 情绪；观点；感情；情操 |  A feeling or opinion, especially one based on emotions.  Sentiment is a more general term than emotion, often referring to a general feeling or attitude rather than a specific, intense emotion. |例句：The general sentiment among the employees is one of optimism. 员工们普遍的情绪是乐观。

The film's sentimental ending brought tears to my eyes. 这部电影煽情的结局让我泪流满面。|近义词：feeling, emotion, opinion, attitude |反义词：indifference, apathy
