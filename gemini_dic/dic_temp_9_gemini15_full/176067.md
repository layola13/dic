ravioli:/ræviˈoʊli/| n. |意大利饺子|Small, filled pasta pockets, typically made of dough containing eggs and flour, and filled with cheese, meat, or vegetables. They are boiled or baked and often served with a sauce.|例句：I ordered the cheese ravioli for dinner. 我晚餐点了奶酪意大利饺子。|例句：The restaurant is famous for its spinach and ricotta ravioli. 这家餐厅以其菠菜和乳清干酪意大利饺子而闻名。|近义词：dumpling, pasta|反义词：无
