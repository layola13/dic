impurest: /ɪmˈpjʊərɪst/ | adj. | 最不纯的，最污秽的 | Most lacking in purity; most contaminated or polluted.  The adjective is the superlative form of "impure". It describes the highest degree of impurity. | 例句：The impurest water is unsafe to drink. 最不纯净的水不能饮用。 The air in the city was the impurest he had ever breathed.  这座城市里的空气是他呼吸过的最污浊的空气。| 近义词：dirtiest, most contaminated, most polluted, foulest | 反义词：purest, cleanest, most uncontaminated
