recuperate:/ˈrɛkjʊpəreɪt/| v.|恢复健康；恢复；重新获得|to recover from illness or injury; to regain or recover something lost or spent|例句：He is recuperating from a long illness. 他正在从一场大病中恢复。

例句：The company is trying to recuperate its losses. 公司正试图弥补损失。


近义词：recover, convalesce, rehabilitate|反义词：deteriorate, worsen

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He recuperates quickly after his workouts. 他锻炼后恢复得很快。
She recuperates from illness at home. 她在家里恢复健康。

一般过去时:
He recuperated quickly after the surgery. 他手术后恢复得很快。
She recuperated at a spa after her stressful job. 她工作压力大后在水疗中心休养。

现在完成时:
He has recuperated well since his accident. 自从事故发生后，他恢复得很好。
She has recuperated all her lost money. 她已经找回了所有损失的钱。

过去完成时:
He had recuperated sufficiently to return to work. 他已经恢复得足够好，可以回去工作了。
She had recuperated her strength before the marathon. 马拉松比赛前，她已经恢复了体力。

将来时:
He will recuperate fully within a few weeks. 他将在几周内完全康复。
She will recuperate her losses through hard work. 她将通过努力工作来弥补损失。

虚拟语气:
If he recuperated faster, he could return to work sooner. 如果他恢复得更快，他就能更快地回到工作岗位。
If she had recuperated more quickly, she would have entered the competition.如果她恢复得更快，她就会参加比赛。

被动语态:
His strength was recuperated slowly. 他的体力恢复得很慢。
The lost funds were recuperated by the police. 警方追回了丢失的资金。

疑问句:
Is he recuperating well? 他恢复得怎么样？
Has she recuperated from her injury yet? 她从伤病中恢复了吗？

否定句:
He did not recuperate quickly after the operation. 他手术后恢复得并不快。
She hasn't fully recuperated from the shock yet. 她还没从震惊中完全恢复过来。

词源:
Recuperate comes from the Latin word "recuperare," meaning "to regain, recover."  The prefix "re-" means "again," and "cupere" means "to desire" or "to seek."  Therefore, the original meaning points to the act of regaining something desired.
