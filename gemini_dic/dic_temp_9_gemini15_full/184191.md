resoaks:/ˌriːˈsoʊks/| v.|再次浸泡；重新浸湿|To soak again; to soak thoroughly.|例句：I resoaked the beans because they weren't soft enough. 我把豆子又泡了一遍，因为它们不够软。
例句：After the rain resoaked the ground, the plants revived. 雨后地面再次湿润，植物复苏了。|近义词：re-soak, soak again, steep again|反义词：dry, drain
