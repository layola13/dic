etching:/ˈɛtʃɪŋ/| n. & vt.|蚀刻；蚀刻术；蚀刻画|The process of making a design on a metal plate, typically using acid, and then printing from this plate.|例句：The museum has a wonderful collection of 17th-century etchings.  博物馆收藏了大量17世纪的蚀刻画。

The artist used a delicate etching technique to create the intricate details. 艺术家采用精细的蚀刻技术来创作复杂的细节。|近义词：engraving, printing|反义词：painting (in the sense of a contrasting artistic technique)

He etches beautiful landscapes onto copper plates. 他在铜板上蚀刻美丽的风景。
She etches designs into glass. 她把图案蚀刻到玻璃上。


一般过去时:
The artist etched the portrait with great precision. 艺术家精确地蚀刻了这幅肖像。
