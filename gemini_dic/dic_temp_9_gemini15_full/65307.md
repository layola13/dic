eudiometer:/juːdiˈɒmɪtər/| n. |气量计；测气计 |A eudiometer is a graduated glass tube used to measure the volume of gases, especially in chemical reactions.  It's often used to analyze the composition of gases or to study gas reactions.  |Example: The chemist used a eudiometer to determine the percentage of oxygen in the air sample.  化学家用气量计测定空气样本中氧气的百分比。|近义词：gas analyzer, volumetric analyzer |反义词：N/A
