modernistic:/ˌmɒdəˈnɪstɪk/ /ˌmɑːdəˈnɪstɪk/| adj.|现代主义的；现代风格的|Characterized by or adhering to the principles of modernism in art, architecture, literature, etc.; exhibiting a simplified, functional, or geometric style.  It emphasizes innovation and a break from traditional styles.|例句：The building's modernistic design is striking and unconventional. 这座建筑的现代主义设计引人注目且非常规。

例句：Her paintings are described as modernistic, characterized by bold colors and abstract forms. 她的画作被描述为现代主义的，其特点是大胆的色彩和抽象的形式。


近义词：modern, contemporary, avant-garde, futuristic

反义词：traditional, classical, conventional, antiquated


词性：形容词
