continual:/kənˈtɪnjuəl/| adj. |不断的；连续的；持续的 | happening again and again; repeated frequently;  characterized by frequent repetition or recurrence.  |例句：There were continual interruptions during the meeting. 会议期间不断被打扰。|例句：She faced continual criticism for her work. 她因工作而不断受到批评。|近义词：constant, incessant, persistent, uninterrupted |反义词：intermittent, occasional, sporadic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The machine makes a continual humming sound. = 机器发出持续的嗡嗡声。
He receives continual emails from his clients. = 他不断收到客户的邮件。


一般过去时:
There was continual rain throughout the trip. = 旅途中一直下着雨。
She experienced continual headaches last week. = 她上周持续头痛。


现在完成时:
We have had continual problems with the software. = 我们一直在这个软件上遇到问题。
He has faced continual challenges in his career. = 他在他的职业生涯中面临着持续的挑战。


过去完成时:
They had experienced continual setbacks before finally succeeding. = 在最终成功之前，他们经历了持续的挫折。
She had endured continual harassment for months before reporting it. = 在举报之前，她忍受了几个月的持续骚扰。


将来时:
We will have continual access to the online resources. = 我们将持续访问在线资源。
The project will require continual updates and adjustments. = 该项目将需要持续更新和调整。


虚拟语气:
If there were continual disruptions, the project would be delayed. = 如果持续中断，项目就会被推迟。
I wish I didn't have continual distractions at work. = 我希望我在工作中没有持续的干扰。


被动语态:
The system is being subjected to continual testing. = 该系统正在接受持续测试。
The building was plagued by continual leaks for years. = 这座建筑物多年来一直受到持续的渗漏困扰。


疑问句:
Is there continual noise coming from that direction? = 从那个方向传来持续的噪音吗？
Are you experiencing continual pain? = 你一直在持续疼痛吗？


否定句:
The progress was not continual; there were several delays. = 进度不是持续的；有几次延误。
The support was not continual; it stopped after a few weeks. = 支持并非持续的；几周后就停止了。
