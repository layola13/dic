localised:/ˈloʊkəlaɪzd/ /ˈləʊkəlaɪzd/| adj. |本地化的；局部的；定点的|Adapted or modified to suit a particular place or people.|The software has been localised for the Chinese market.  这个软件已经针对中国市场进行了本地化。|The pain was localised in his lower back. 疼痛局限在他的下背部。|globalised, centralized|widespread, generalized|形容词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The software is localised for different regions. = 这个软件针对不同的地区进行了本地化。
This problem is localised to a specific area. = 这个问题局限于一个特定区域。


一般过去时:
The game was localised for the European market. = 这款游戏针对欧洲市场进行了本地化。
The infection was localised to the arm.  感染局限于手臂。


现在完成时:
The company has localised its products for several countries. = 公司已经为多个国家本地化了其产品。
The damage has been localised to the engine compartment.  损坏已被限制在发动机舱内。


过去完成时:
The issue had been localised before the launch. = 问题在发布前已被定位。
The symptoms had been localised to the chest. 症状已局限于胸部。


将来时:
The website will be localised for Spanish speakers next year. = 网站明年将针对西班牙语使用者进行本地化。
The effects of the drug will be localised to the affected area.  药物的影响将局限于受影响的区域。


虚拟语气:
If the problem were localised, it would be easier to solve. = 如果问题是局部的，那么解决起来就会更容易。
I wish the damage had been localised to a smaller area. 我希望损坏局限在一个更小的区域。


被动语态:
The updates are being localised for multiple platforms. = 更新正在针对多个平台进行本地化。
The investigation is being localised to a specific department. 调查正局限于特定部门。


疑问句:
Is the problem localised to one specific area? = 这个问题是否局限于一个特定区域？
Has the software been localised for all target markets? 软件是否已针对所有目标市场进行了本地化？


否定句:
The damage wasn't localised; it spread throughout the system. = 损坏并非局部的；它扩散到了整个系统。
The effects of the virus weren't localised; they affected the whole body. 病毒的影响并非局部的；它影响了全身。
