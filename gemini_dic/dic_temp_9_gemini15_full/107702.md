lansquenet:/ˈlæŋskənɛt/| n.|朗斯基内特（十六世纪德国雇佣兵）|A landsknecht (plural landsknechte) was a type of German mercenary infantryman who served in large numbers during the late 15th and 16th centuries.  They were known for their distinctive colorful clothing,  fierce fighting style, and often brutal behavior. |The lansquenets were feared throughout Europe for their ruthlessness.  |近义词： mercenary, soldier,  retainer |反义词： civilian
