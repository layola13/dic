postlegitimation:/ˌpoʊstliˌdʒɪtɪˈmeɪʃən/| n. |婚后追认(子女身份)|The act of legitimizing a child born out of wedlock after the parents marry.  This process grants the child the legal rights and status of a legitimate child.  |例句：The couple's postlegitimation of their child gave him full inheritance rights.  这对夫妇婚后追认他们的孩子，赋予了他完全的继承权。|近义词：legitimization (of a child born out of wedlock)|反义词：illegitimation|名词
