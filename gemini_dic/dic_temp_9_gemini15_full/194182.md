self-isolation: /ˌsɛlf ˌaɪsəˈleɪʃən/ | n. |自我隔离，自我封闭 | The act of isolating oneself from others, typically to prevent the spread of a contagious disease.  This is often a voluntary action taken to protect oneself and others from illness. | 例句：Self-isolation is crucial during a pandemic to curb the spread of the virus.  自我隔离在疫情期间至关重要，以遏制病毒的传播。

He chose self-isolation to avoid infecting his family. 他选择自我隔离以避免感染家人。


近义词：quarantine, seclusion, confinement | 反义词：socialization, interaction
