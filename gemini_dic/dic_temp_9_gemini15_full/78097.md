geographer:/dʒiˈɒɡrəfər/| n. |地理学家|A geographer is a scientist who studies geography.  They study the Earth's physical features, climate, and human populations. Their work often involves fieldwork, data analysis, and mapmaking. | Example:  The geographer presented her findings on the impact of climate change on coastal communities.  地理学家展示了她关于气候变化对沿海社区影响的发现。
