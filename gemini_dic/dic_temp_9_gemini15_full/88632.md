hmm:/hʌm/| int.|嗯，哼；表示犹豫、思考或不确定|英文解析：An expression of hesitation, doubt, or thought.  Often used to fill a pause in conversation or to show that someone is thinking.|例句：Hmm, that's an interesting question.  嗯，这是一个有趣的问题。|例句：Hmm, I'm not sure about that. 嗯，我不确定。|近义词：um, er, uh|反义词：无
