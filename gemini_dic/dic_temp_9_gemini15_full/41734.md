corrosible:/kəˈroʊzəbl/| adj.| 可腐蚀的，易腐蚀的 | Easily damaged or destroyed by chemical action, especially oxidation.  | Example:  Iron is a corrosible metal.  铁是一种易腐蚀的金属。  The corrosible pipes needed replacing.  那些易腐蚀的管道需要更换。|近义词：erodible, perishable, susceptible to corrosion|反义词：incorruptible, indestructible, resistant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The acid corrodes the metal. = 酸腐蚀金属。
This material is corrosible in seawater. =这种材料在海水中易腐蚀。


一般过去时:
The pipe corroded over time. = 管子随着时间的推移而腐蚀了。
The old bridge was corroded by salt water. = 老桥被海水腐蚀了。


现在完成时:
The metal has corroded significantly. = 金属已经严重腐蚀了。
The structure has been corroded by years of neglect. = 由于多年的忽视，结构已经腐蚀了。


过去完成时:
The engine had corroded before we could repair it. = 我们还没来得及修理，发动机就已经腐蚀了。
The ship's hull had corroded extensively during its long voyage. = 在漫长的航行中，船体已经严重腐蚀。


将来时:
The pipes will corrode if not properly protected. = 如果没有得到妥善保护，管道将会腐蚀。
The monument will likely corrode over the centuries. = 这座纪念碑可能会在几个世纪中腐蚀。


虚拟语气:
If the metal were corrosible, it would have rusted. = 如果金属易腐蚀，它早就生锈了。
I wish the components weren't so corrosible. = 我希望这些部件不会这么容易腐蚀。


被动语态:

The equipment is being corroded by the chemicals. = 设备正在被化学物质腐蚀。


疑问句:

否定句:
