fosse:/fɒs/|n.|沟渠；壕沟；坑；（尤指剧院的）舞台下的通道|A long, narrow ditch or trench.  Especially in the context of a theater, it refers to the area beneath the stage.|例句：The soldiers dug a deep fosse around the castle.  士兵们在城堡周围挖了一条深沟。|例句：The dancers emerged from the fosse at the end of the performance. 舞蹈演员在演出结束时从舞台下的通道里出来。|近义词：ditch, trench, pit, channel|反义词：ridge, elevation
