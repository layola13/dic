recommence:/ˌriːkəˈmɛns/| v.|重新开始，继续|To begin something again after it has been interrupted or stopped.|例句：After the rain stopped, they recommenced their game. 雨停后，他们重新开始比赛。|例句：The meeting recommenced after a short break. 会议在短暂休息后重新开始。|近义词：restart, resume, begin again|反义词：end, cease, stop


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They recommence work after the break. = 他们休息后重新开始工作。
We recommence the project next week. = 我们下周重新启动这个项目。


一般过去时:
The construction recommenced after the storm. = 暴风雨过后，施工重新开始。
She recommenced her studies after a long illness. = 她大病初愈后重新开始学习。


现在完成时:
They have recommenced their discussions. = 他们已经重新开始讨论了。

过去完成时:

They had recommenced the negotiations after the initial failure. = 首次谈判失败后，他们已经重新开始谈判了。


将来时:
We will recommence the meeting tomorrow morning. = 我们明天早上将重新开始会议。
The project will recommence once funding is secured. = 一旦资金到位，项目就会重新启动。
