macro:/ˈmækroʊ/| n. adj. |宏，宏观；宏指令|A macro is a single instruction that expands into a set of instructions.  It's often used in computer programming to represent a sequence of actions or a larger piece of code with a simpler name.  Macros can also refer to something large-scale or overall, as opposed to something microscopic. |例句：He wrote a macro to automate the repetitive task. 他写了一个宏来自动化重复性任务。|近义词：command, instruction, large-scale, overall |反义词：micro, microscopic, detail
