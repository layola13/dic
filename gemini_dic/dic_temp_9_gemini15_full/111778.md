loess:/ˈloʊɛs/| n. |黄土|Loose, fertile, yellowish-brown soil deposited by wind.  It is typically found in semi-arid regions and is characterized by its fine texture and high porosity.|Example: The fertile loess plains of China support a large agricultural population.  中国的肥沃黄土高原养活了大量农业人口。|近义词：alluvium, silt|反义词：rock, clay
