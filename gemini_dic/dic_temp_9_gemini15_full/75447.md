fruiting:/ˈfruːtɪŋ/| adj.|结果的；结果实的；|Producing or bearing fruit.  The process of producing fruit. |例句：The fruiting season for apples is in the autumn. 苹果的果实成熟季节在秋季。

The plant is now in its fruiting stage.  这株植物现在正处于结果阶段。|近义词：bearing fruit, producing fruit, fructifying|反义词：barren, sterile
