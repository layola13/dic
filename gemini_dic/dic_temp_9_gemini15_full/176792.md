readjustable:/ˌriːəˈdʒʌstəbəl/| adj. | 可重新调整的，可重新校准的 | Capable of being readjusted or recalibrated.  It implies that something can be altered or modified to fit a new situation or requirement. | Example: The straps on this backpack are readjustable for a comfortable fit. 这款背包的肩带可调节，以获得舒适的佩戴体验。The settings on this machine are readjustable. 这台机器的设置是可以重新调整的。|近义词：adjustable, adaptable, modifiable | 反义词：fixed, inflexible, immutable
