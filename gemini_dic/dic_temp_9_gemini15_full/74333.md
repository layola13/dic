fragility:/frəˈdʒɪləti/ | n. |脆弱性，易碎性 | The fragility of the peace treaty was evident in the rising tensions between the two countries.  和平条约的脆弱性在两国之间日益紧张的关系中显而易见。|近义词：delicacy, weakness, vulnerability |反义词：strength, robustness, resilience


英文解析：The quality or state of being fragile; easily broken or damaged.  It can also refer to the weakness or instability of a system or situation.

例句：

The fragility of the vase made me nervous about handling it.  这花瓶的易碎性让我不敢碰它。

The economic fragility of the region is a major concern.  该地区的经济脆弱性是一个主要担忧。


词源：

From Late Middle English *fragilite, from Old French fragilité, from Latin fragilitās, from fragilis ("fragile").  Ultimately derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *bhreĝ- ("to break").
