cond:/kɒnd/|conj.|条件，假使|A conjunction used to introduce a subordinate clause stating a condition.  It implies that the main clause will only be true if the condition in the subordinate clause is met.|例句：I will go to the party if the weather is good.  如果天气好，我就去参加派对。|例句：He'll fail the exam unless he studies harder. 除非他更加努力学习，否则他会考试不及格。|近义词：provided that, given that, supposing that, on condition that|反义词：regardless of, irrespective of
