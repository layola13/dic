firecloud:/ˈfaɪərˌklaʊd/| n. |火云；火烧云| A firecloud is a cloud that appears fiery or reddish in color, often due to the scattering of sunlight at sunrise or sunset.  It's a descriptive term rather than a specific meteorological classification.|例句：The sunset painted the sky with streaks of vibrant orange and red, creating a breathtaking firecloud.|近义词：fiery cloud, burning cloud|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with a pale or grey cloud.
