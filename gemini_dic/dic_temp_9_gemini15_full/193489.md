selects:/səˈlɛkts/ | v. | 选择，挑选 | To choose or pick something from a group.  | The committee selects the best candidate. 委员会挑选最佳候选人。| chooses, picks, opts | rejects, ignores | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He selects the best apples from the basket. 他从篮子里挑选最好的苹果。
She selects her clothes carefully. 她仔细挑选衣服。

一般过去时:
They selected a new CEO last week. 他们上周选了一位新的首席执行官。
I selected the red car because it was faster. 我选了那辆红色的车，因为它更快。

现在完成时:
She has selected a dress for the party. 她已经为派对选了一件衣服。
He has selected his team for the tournament. 他已经为锦标赛选好了他的团队。

过去完成时:
They had selected their holiday destination before the prices increased. 在价格上涨之前，他们已经选好了度假目的地。
She had selected the winning lottery numbers earlier that day. 她当天早些时候就已经选好了中奖彩票号码。

将来时:
I will select a gift for my friend's birthday. 我将为朋友的生日挑选一份礼物。
They will select the winner of the competition tomorrow. 他们明天将选出比赛的获胜者。

虚拟语气:
If I were to select a new car, I would choose a hybrid. 如果我要选一辆新车，我会选择混合动力车。
I wish I had selected a different path in my career. 我希望我在职业生涯中选择了不同的道路。

被动语态:
The winner was selected by a panel of judges. 获胜者由评审团选出。
The books were carefully selected for their educational value. 这些书籍因其教育价值而被精心挑选。

疑问句:
Which candidate did you select? 你选择了哪个候选人？
What color did she select for the walls? 她为墙壁选择了什么颜色？

否定句:
He did not select the appropriate tools for the job. 他没有为这项工作选择合适的工具。
They did not select anyone for the position. 他们没有为这个职位选任何人。


PHRASE->
