skating:/ˈskeɪtɪŋ/| n. |滑冰；滑冰运动|The act of moving on skates, typically on ice or roller skates.  It can also refer to the sport or activity of skating.|例句：She enjoys ice skating in winter. 她喜欢在冬天滑冰。|例句：He's a professional figure skating champion. 他是专业的花样滑冰冠军。|近义词：ice-skating, roller skating, gliding|反义词：walking, running|名词
