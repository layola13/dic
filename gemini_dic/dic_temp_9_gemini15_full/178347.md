reconfiguration:/ˌriːkənˌfɪɡəˈreɪʃən/| n. |重新配置；重新构造；改组 |The act or process of reconfiguring something; changing the structure or arrangement of something.  This often involves reorganizing components or systems to achieve a different function or improve efficiency. |例句：The network administrator performed a reconfiguration of the server to improve performance. 网络管理员对服务器进行了重新配置以提高性能。

例句：The reconfiguration of the factory floor increased productivity by 15%.  工厂车间的重新布局使生产力提高了15%。


近义词：rearrangement, reorganization, restructuring, reorganisation (BrE) |反义词：configuration (original state) |词性：名词
