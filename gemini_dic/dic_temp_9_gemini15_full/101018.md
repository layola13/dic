invariably:/ɪnˈveriəbli/ | adv. |总是；不变地；一定地 | Always; without exception.  The adverb "invariably" emphasizes that something always happens or is always true, without any exceptions.  |例句：He invariably arrives late. 他总是迟到。

例句：The sun invariably rises in the east. 太阳总是从东方升起。


近义词：always, constantly, consistently, regularly, uniformly

反义词：occasionally, sometimes, rarely, seldom, infrequently

词性：副词
