It often implies a careful or deliberate recreation or representation of something. |例句：The reddition of the ancient manuscript was a painstaking process. 古代手稿的再现是一个费力的过程。

例句：The reddition of the prisoner to the enemy was a controversial decision. 将俘虏归还给敌人是一个有争议的决定。


近义词：reproduction, rendition, translation, version, return | 反义词：original, creation
