reswearing:/ˌriːˈswɛərɪŋ/| v.|重新宣誓，再次宣誓 | The act of swearing an oath again;  renewing a vow or promise under oath.  |例句：The judge ordered the reswearing of the witness after he admitted to initially lying.  法官命令证人再次宣誓，因为他承认最初撒谎了。|  例句：The newly elected officials engaged in a reswearing ceremony to reaffirm their commitment to public service. 新当选的官员举行了一次重新宣誓仪式，以重申他们对公共服务的承诺。 |近义词：re-affirmation, renewal of vows |反义词：repudiation, denial
