insuperably: /ɪnˈsuːpərəbli/ | adj. | 不可克服地，无法解决地 |  Incapable of being overcome or solved.  It describes a situation or problem that is extremely difficult or impossible to deal with. | The team faced insuperable obstacles in their attempt to climb the mountain.  团队在试图攀登这座山时面临着不可克服的障碍。  |近义词：insurmountable, unconquerable, invincible | 反义词：surmountable, conquerable
