on-spec:/ɒnˈspɛk/| adj. | 凭运气；投机；未经委托 |  Done or undertaken without prior agreement or commission, hoping for success.  It implies a risk-taking approach, where the outcome is uncertain. |例句：He submitted the proposal on spec, hoping to win the contract. 他未经委托提交了提案，希望能赢得合同。

例句：The writer submitted the article on spec to several magazines.  这位作家向几家杂志投递了未经约稿的文章。


近义词：speculative, unsolicited, uncommissioned


反义词：commissioned, contracted, agreed-upon


词性：adjectival phrase
