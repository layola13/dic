/mɛnz/ | n. | 男人，男装 | men refers to adult human males.  The word can also refer to clothing designed for men. | Example: The men went hunting.  男人们去打猎了。The men's clothing store had a great sale.  那家男装店搞大减价了。 |近义词：males, guys, fellows | 反义词：women
