overhunted:/ˌoʊvərˈhʌntɪd/| adj.|过度狩猎的，猎捕过度的|Excessively hunted; depleted by hunting.|The overhunted deer population is struggling to recover.  过度狩猎导致鹿群数量难以恢复。|The rabbit population is severely overhunted in this area. 这个地区的兔子数量因过度狩猎而严重减少。|underhunted, sustainably hunted|  well-populated, abundant
