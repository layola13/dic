legalising: /ˈliːɡəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使合法化；合法化 | The act of making something legal.  The process of removing legal restrictions or prohibitions. |

例句：
The government is considering legalising cannabis. = 政府正在考虑将大麻合法化。

The country is moving towards legalising same-sex marriage. = 这个国家正在朝着将同性婚姻合法化的方向发展。


近义词：
authorising, sanctioning, validating, ratifying, permitting

反义词：
outlawing, prohibiting, banning, forbidding


词源:
From "legal" (from Latin *lēgālis, from lex "law") + "-ising" (present participle suffix).
