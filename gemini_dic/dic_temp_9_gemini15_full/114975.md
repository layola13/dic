manchurian:/mænˈtʃʊəriən/| adj. n.|满洲的；满洲人；满洲话|Of or relating to Manchuria (a historical region in northeastern China).  The word can refer to the region itself, its people, or their language and culture. |例句：The Manchurian Plain is known for its fertile soil.  (满洲平原以其肥沃的土壤而闻名。)  He is a Manchurian by birth. (他出生于满洲。)近义词：Northeast Chinese (东北中国人) (as a regional descriptor)反义词：None.
