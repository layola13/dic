heteronymous:/ˌhetərˈɒnɪməs/ | adj. | 异名的；同形异义的 | Having different meanings despite identical spelling.  This term typically applies to words, but can be extended to other linguistic elements.  The difference in meaning is not simply a matter of context;  the meanings are distinct and unrelated. | Example:  The word "bank" can be heteronymous, referring to a financial institution or the side of a river.  The two meanings are completely unrelated. | 近义词：polysemous (but polysemous implies related meanings),  homographic | 反义词：homonymous (same spelling, same pronunciation, different meaning)
