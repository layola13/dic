hollys:/ˈhɑːliz/ | n. |冬青属植物；冬青树 | Holly is an evergreen shrub or tree with prickly leaves and bright red berries. 冬青是一种常绿灌木或乔木，叶子带刺，浆果鲜红。 |The garden is decorated with holly branches during Christmas. 圣诞节期间，花园里装饰着冬青树枝。| evergreen, shrub | deciduous (反义词，指落叶的) | 名词
