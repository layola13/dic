remodelled:/ˌriːˈmɒdəld/ | /ˌriːˈmɑːdəld/ | v. | 改建；重新塑造；改造 | To change the appearance, structure, or system of something, typically to improve it.  | The old house was remodelled to meet modern standards. 老房子经过了改建以符合现代标准。  The company remodelled its marketing strategy. 公司重新调整了其营销策略。 | renovate, refurbish, revamp | destroy, ruin | 动词


USAGE->

They remodel their kitchen every year. = 他们每年都重新装修他们的厨房。
