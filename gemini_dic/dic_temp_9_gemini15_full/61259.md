emancipators:/ɪˈmæn.sɪ.peɪ.tərz/| n.|解放者，废奴主义者|Those who liberate others from oppression or slavery, particularly those who fought to end slavery.|例句：Abraham Lincoln is considered one of the greatest emancipators in history. 亚伯拉罕·林肯被认为是历史上最伟大的解放者之一。

例句：The emancipators fought tirelessly for the freedom of enslaved people. 解放者为奴隶的自由而不知疲倦地奋斗。


近义词：liberators, freedom fighters, abolitionists|反义词：oppressors, enslavers|名词
