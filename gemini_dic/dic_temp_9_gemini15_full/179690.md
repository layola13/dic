reenlists: /ˌriːˈɛnlɪsts/ | v. | 重新入伍，再次应征 | To enlist again in the armed forces after a period of service.  |例句：After a five-year break, he reenlisted in the army.  五年休息后，他重新入伍。

例句：Many veterans choose to reenlist after their initial term of service. 许多退伍军人在服役期满后选择重新入伍。


近义词：re-enlist, rejoin  | 反义词：discharge, retire | 词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reenlists every five years. 他每五年重新入伍一次。
She reenlists in the air force next month. 她下个月将重新加入空军。


一般过去时:
He reenlisted in the navy last year. 他去年重新加入海军。
She reenlisted after a brief period of civilian life. 她短暂的平民生活后重新入伍。


现在完成时:
He has reenlisted three times. 他已经重新入伍三次了。
She has reenlisted and is currently serving overseas. 她已经重新入伍，目前正在海外服役。


过去完成时:
He had reenlisted before the war broke out.  战争爆发前他已经重新入伍了。
She had reenlisted twice before she finally retired.  在她最终退休前，她已经重新入伍两次了。


将来时:
He will reenlist next year. 他明年将重新入伍。
She will reenlist as soon as her current contract expires.  她的现役合同一到期，她就会重新入伍。


虚拟语气:
If he were younger, he would reenlist. 如果他年轻一些，他会重新入伍。
If she hadn't had family obligations, she would have reenlisted. 如果她没有家庭责任，她就会重新入伍。


被动语态:
He was reenlisted after a thorough medical examination.  在经过彻底的体检后，他重新入伍了。
Many soldiers are reenlisted each year. 每年都有许多士兵重新入伍。


疑问句:
Will he reenlist? 他会重新入伍吗？
Has she reenlisted yet? 她已经重新入伍了吗？


否定句:
He did not reenlist after his first tour. 他第一次服役结束后没有重新入伍。
She will not reenlist because of health concerns. 由于健康问题，她不会重新入伍。



PHRASE->
reenlist in the army:  重新加入军队
reenlist for another term: 再次服役
reenlist after retirement: 退休后重新入伍
reenlist with the same unit:  回到原来的部队重新入伍
reenlist under a new contract:  签署新合同后重新入伍
