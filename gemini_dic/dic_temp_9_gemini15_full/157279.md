pinprick:/ˈpɪnprɪk/| n. |针刺；微小的刺痛；微不足道的事物 | A pinprick is a small prick or puncture made by a pin.  It can also refer to something insignificant or minor. |例句：The mosquito bite was just a pinprick.  蚊子叮咬只是一个小小的针刺。

例句：His criticism was a mere pinprick compared to the larger issues.  与更大的问题相比，他的批评只是微不足道的小事。

近义词：prick, puncture, sting, slight

反义词：major blow, significant event

词性:名词
