humectant:/ˈhjuːmɛktənt/| n. |保湿剂|A humectant is a substance that attracts and retains moisture from the surrounding environment.  It is often used in cosmetics and skincare products to keep skin hydrated. |Example: Glycerin is a common humectant used in lotions and creams. 甘油是化妆水和面霜中常用的保湿剂。|近义词：moisturizer, emollient |反义词：dessicant
