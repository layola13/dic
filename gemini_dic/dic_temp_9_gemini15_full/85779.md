haylofts: /ˈheɪlɒfts/ | n. | 干草堆，阁楼 |  A hayloft is a loft or upper floor in a barn used for storing hay.  It's typically a large, open space under the roof. |  例句：The farmer climbed the ladder to the hayloft to check on the hay. 农民爬上梯子到干草堆检查干草。

The children played hide-and-seek in the dusty hayloft.孩子们在尘土飞扬的干草堆里玩捉迷藏。| 近义词：loft, attic (in some contexts) | 反义词：basement, cellar
