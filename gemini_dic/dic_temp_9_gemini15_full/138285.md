notarizing: /ˈnoʊtəraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 公证；认证 | The act of a notary public attesting to the authenticity of a signature or document.  This involves witnessing the signer's signature and affixing their official seal.  | 例句：I need to get this document notarized before submitting it. 我需要在提交这份文件之前对其进行公证。

例句：She is notarizing the contract for the property sale.  她在为房产出售合同进行公证。


近义词：authenticating, certifying, verifying | 反义词：invalidating, disproving
