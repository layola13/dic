rotten:/ˈrɒtən/ /ˈrɑːtən/| adj.|腐烂的；腐败的；极坏的|Decayed or decomposed, especially of organic matter; morally corrupt or thoroughly bad.|例句：The fruit is rotten.  这水果烂了。

The politician's rotten behaviour led to his resignation.  这位政治家的腐败行为导致了他辞职。


近义词：decayed, spoiled, corrupt, putrid, vile|反义词：fresh, sound, wholesome, good


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The apple is rotten. = 苹果烂了。
That meat smells rotten. = 那肉闻起来臭了。


一般过去时:
The milk went rotten. = 牛奶变质了。
The food was rotten. = 食物腐烂了。


现在完成时:
The vegetables have gone rotten. = 蔬菜已经腐烂了。
The fruit has rotted. = 水果已经腐烂了。


过去完成时:
The bread had gone rotten before we could eat it. = 我们还没来得及吃面包就坏了。
The meat had rotted in the heat. = 肉在高温下腐烂了。


将来时:
This fruit will be rotten by tomorrow. = 这水果明天就会烂了。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
