securement:/sɪˈkjʊərmənt/ | n. | 保护；固定；获得；担保 | The act of securing something; the state of being secured.  It refers to the process of making something safe, stable, or certain. This can involve physical securing (like fastening something down), or securing something abstract (like a financial agreement). | 例句：The securement of the bridge was a top priority after the storm.  暴风雨后桥梁的加固是重中之重。

The securement of the loan took several weeks of paperwork. 获得贷款花了几个星期的文书工作。


近义词：protection, fastening, acquisition, guarantee | 反义词：insecurity, endangerment, loss, risk | 词性：名词
