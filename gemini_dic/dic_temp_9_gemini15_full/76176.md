fussed:/fʌst/| v.|焦急的；大惊小怪的；过于讲究的|to be anxious or worried; to make a fuss; to be too concerned with minor details |例句：Don't get so fussed about the small things. 别为这些小事如此焦急。


例句：She fussed over the arrangements for the party. 她为晚会的安排忙得团团转。


近义词：worried, anxious, agitated, stressed


反义词：calm, relaxed, unconcerned


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fusses over his appearance. = 他很在意自己的外表。
She fusses about the house all day. = 她整天在家里忙忙碌碌。


一般过去时:
He fussed over his tie before the interview. = 面试前，他仔细地整理领带。
She fussed about the children's clothes. = 她为孩子们挑选衣服而忙乱。


现在完成时:
She has fussed over this project for weeks. = 她为这个项目忙了好几个星期了。
He has fussed about the details unnecessarily. = 他不必要地为细节操心。


过去完成时:
She had fussed over the dinner preparations all afternoon. = 她一下午都在忙着准备晚餐。
He had fussed about the seating arrangements before the guests arrived. = 客人到来之前，他一直在忙着安排座位。


将来时:
I know she will fuss over the wedding details. = 我知道她会为婚礼的细节操心。
He will fuss over the children when they are sick. = 孩子们生病时，他会非常担心。


虚拟语气:
If I didn't fuss so much, I would be less stressed. = 如果我不那么操心，我的压力会小一些。
I wish I hadn't fussed so much about the presentation. = 我真希望我不要为演示文稿操心那么多。


被动语态:
The details were fussed over by the committee. = 委员会非常仔细地处理了细节。
The arrangements were fussed over until they were perfect. = 一直忙活到安排完美无缺为止。


疑问句:
Does he fuss over his work? = 他对工作很在意吗？
Does she fuss over every little thing? = 她对每一件小事都过于操心吗？


否定句:
He didn't fuss about the delay. = 他没有为延误而焦虑。
She didn't fuss over her appearance for the casual meeting. = 为了参加非正式会议，她并没有刻意打扮。


PHRASE->
fuss over: 对…过于操心
fuss about: 为…而烦恼
make a fuss: 大惊小怪
fuss with: 弄乱；摆弄
fuss and bother: 忙乱不堪
