plumification: /ˌplʌmɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 羽毛化；长羽毛；长成羽毛状 | The process of developing feathers; becoming feathered.  The transformation of a structure or surface to resemble feathers. |例句：The plumification of the chick is a fascinating process.  雏鸟的羽毛化是一个迷人的过程。

The rapid plumification of the newly hatched bird allowed it to regulate its body temperature more effectively.  刚孵化的小鸟迅速长出羽毛，使其能够更有效地调节体温。


近义词：feathering, downing


反义词：deplumage (the process of losing feathers)

词源:
The word "plumification" is derived from the Latin word "pluma," meaning "feather," and the suffix "-ification," meaning "the act or process of."  Therefore, "plumification" literally means "the process of becoming feathered."
