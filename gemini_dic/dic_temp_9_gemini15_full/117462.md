meditweet:/ˈmɛdɪˌtwit/| n. |冥想推文，沉思推文 |A meditweet is a short message posted on social media, particularly Twitter, that expresses a thoughtful or reflective observation, often related to personal experiences or insights gained through meditation or mindfulness practices.  It's a blend of "meditation" and "tweet."|例句：I posted a meditweet about the peace I found during my morning meditation. 我发了一条关于我在晨间冥想中找到的宁静的冥想推文。
                                                                                                                                                                                             She shared a poignant meditweet reflecting on her recent mindfulness retreat. 她分享了一条发人深省的冥想推文，反思了她最近的正念静修。|近义词：reflective post, mindful tweet, contemplative message |反义词：superficial tweet, frivolous post|名词
