pen-pal:/ˈpɛn pæl/| n. |笔友 |A pen pal is someone you write letters to regularly, usually someone you have never met in person.  Pen pals are often located in different countries or regions, allowing for cultural exchange and the development of friendships across geographical boundaries. | I have a pen pal in Japan. 我有一个在日本的笔友。| friend, correspondent | enemy, foe |名词
