homeownership:/ˈhoʊmoʊnərʃɪp/| n. |房屋所有权；拥有住房|The state or right of owning a home.  It encompasses all aspects of owning a house, from purchasing it to maintaining it and eventually selling it. |例句：Achieving homeownership is a significant milestone for many young adults.  实现房屋所有权对许多年轻人来说是一个重要的里程碑。

例句：The high cost of housing is making homeownership increasingly difficult to attain. 高昂的住房成本使得拥有住房越来越困难。


近义词：house ownership, home possession|反义词：homelessness, renting
