overmedicating: /ˌoʊvərˈmɛdɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | v. |过度用药 | The act of giving or taking more medication than is necessary or recommended.  This can lead to adverse effects and health complications. | 例句：Overmedicating children can lead to serious health problems.  过度给孩子用药会导致严重的健康问题。

例句：The doctor warned against overmedicating the patient, suggesting alternative therapies instead. 医生警告不要过度用药，建议采用替代疗法。


近义词：polypharmacy (使用多种药物), drug overdose (药物过量)


反义词：undermedicating (用药不足), appropriate medication (适当用药)


词性：动词
