precipitousness:/prɪˌsɪpɪtəsnəs/| n. |陡峭；险峻；仓促 |The precipitousness of the cliff made climbing dangerous.  陡峭的悬崖使攀登变得危险。|steepness, abruptness, suddenness|gentleness, gradualness|名词

例句：

The precipitousness of the mountain slope was breathtaking.  山坡的陡峭令人叹为观止。

The precipitousness of their decision surprised everyone.  他们决定的仓促让每个人都感到惊讶。
