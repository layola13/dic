recreationally:/ˌrɛkriˈeɪʃənəli/| adv.|消遣地；娱乐地|Used to describe something done for leisure or enjoyment, not for work or profit.  It indicates an activity undertaken for fun or relaxation.|例句：He plays video games recreationally. 他玩电子游戏是为了消遣。|例句：She enjoys painting recreationally in her free time.  她在空闲时间喜欢消遣性地绘画。|近义词：leisurely, for fun, as a hobby|反义词：professionally, commercially, for profit
