pufferfish:/ˈpʌfərˌfɪʃ/| n. |河豚，气泡鱼|A pufferfish is a fish that can inflate its body to appear larger and deter predators.  They are known for their poisonous nature.  Many species are highly toxic and should not be eaten unless prepared by experienced professionals.| Example: The diver spotted a brightly colored pufferfish hiding amongst the coral.  潜水员发现一条色彩鲜艳的河豚藏在珊瑚丛中。  The chef carefully prepared the pufferfish, ensuring all the poisonous parts were removed.  厨师小心翼翼地准备河豚，确保所有有毒的部分都被去除。|近义词：blowfish, globefish|反义词：无
