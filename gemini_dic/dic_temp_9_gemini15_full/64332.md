eradicate:/ɪˈrædɪkeɪt/ | vt. | 根除；消灭；杜绝 | To completely destroy or get rid of something harmful or undesirable.  | 例句：We must eradicate poverty and disease. 我们必须根除贫困和疾病。  | 例句：The government is trying to eradicate corruption. 政府正在努力根除腐败。 | 近义词：eliminate, exterminate, abolish, destroy | 反义词：introduce, foster, cultivate, spread


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They eradicate pests from the farm. = 他们根除农场里的害虫。
We eradicate misinformation online. = 我们在网上根除虚假信息。

一般过去时:
The disease was eradicated in the 1950s. = 这种疾病在20世纪50年代被根除了。
Farmers eradicated weeds from the field. = 农民们从田里除去了杂草。


现在完成时:
Scientists have eradicated several diseases. = 科学家们已经根除了几种疾病。
They have eradicated the problem of homelessness in the community. = 他们已经根除了社区中无家可归的问题。


过去完成时:
The government had eradicated the threat before it escalated. = 政府在威胁升级之前就将其根除了。
Before the new policy, they had eradicated most of the illegal activities. = 在新政策出台之前，他们已经根除了大部分非法活动。


将来时:
We will eradicate hunger in our lifetime. = 我们将在有生之年消灭饥饿。
The company plans to eradicate all carbon emissions by 2030. = 公司计划在2030年前消除所有碳排放。


虚拟语气:
If we acted now, we could eradicate the problem. = 如果我们现在采取行动，就能根除这个问题。
I wish we could eradicate poverty entirely. = 我希望我们能够彻底消除贫困。


被动语态:
The disease was eradicated through vaccination. = 这种疾病通过疫苗接种被根除了。
The invasive species is being eradicated by the authorities. = 这种入侵物种正在被当局根除。


疑问句:
Can we eradicate this social injustice? = 我们能根除这种社会不公吗？
How can we eradicate the root cause of poverty? = 我们如何才能根除贫困的根本原因？


否定句:
We cannot eradicate poverty overnight. = 我们无法一夜之间消除贫困。
They failed to eradicate the weeds completely. = 他们未能完全清除杂草。
