manned-up: /mændˈʌp/ | v. |  变得男子汉气概；表现得勇敢；承担责任 | To behave in a way considered typical of a man, especially by showing courage or accepting responsibility.  |例句：He manned up and faced his fears. 他勇敢地面对了自己的恐惧。 He needed to man up and take responsibility for his actions. 他需要承担责任。|近义词：toughen up, be brave, take charge |反义词：cower, be cowardly, shirk responsibility |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mans up when faced with challenges. = 他在面对挑战时会变得勇敢。
She mans up and takes on the difficult task. = 她勇敢地承担了这项艰巨的任务。


一般过去时:
He manned up and confessed his mistake. = 他勇敢地承认了自己的错误。
She manned up and faced her critics. = 她勇敢地面对了她的批评者。


现在完成时:
He has manned up considerably since the incident. = 自那件事故以来，他变得坚强多了。
She has manned up and taken control of the situation. = 她已经变得勇敢并掌控了局面。


过去完成时:
He had manned up and apologized before I arrived. = 我到之前他已经勇敢地道歉了。
She had manned up and finished the project before the deadline. = 她已经勇敢地完成了项目，赶在了截止日期之前。


将来时:
He will man up and tell her the truth. = 他将会勇敢地告诉她真相。
She will man up and overcome this obstacle. = 她将会勇敢地克服这个障碍。


虚拟语气:
If he manned up, he would have a better chance of success. = 如果他勇敢一些，他成功的几率会更大。
If she had manned up earlier, things would have been different. = 如果她早点勇敢起来，情况就会有所不同。


被动语态:  (很少用被动语态)
The situation demanded that he man up.  =  情况要求他必须勇敢起来。(主动语态更常见)
It was expected that he would man up. =  人们期待他会变得勇敢起来。(主动语态更常见)


疑问句:
Will he man up and admit his fault? = 他会勇敢地承认错误吗？
Did she man up to the challenge? = 她勇敢地面对挑战了吗？


否定句:
He didn't man up and avoid the issue. = 他没有勇敢地面对问题。
She didn't man up and take the responsibility. = 她没有勇敢地承担责任。
