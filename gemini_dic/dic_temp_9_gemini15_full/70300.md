fidgeting:/ˈfɪdʒɪtɪŋ/ | v. n. | 烦躁不安；坐立不安 |  The act of making small, restless movements.  It often indicates nervousness, boredom, or discomfort. | 例句：She was fidgeting nervously in her seat. 她不安地在她座位上动来动去。

例句：The children were fidgeting during the long lecture. 孩子们在冗长的演讲中坐立不安。


近义词：restlessness, squirming, wriggling, fidgeting around


反义词：stillness, calmness, composure


词性：动词和名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He fidgets constantly. = 他不停地动来动去。
She fidgets with her pen. = 她不停地玩弄她的钢笔。


一般过去时:
He fidgeted throughout the meeting. = 他在整个会议期间都坐立不安。
She fidgeted with her necklace. = 她不停地摆弄她的项链。


现在完成时:
He has been fidgeting all morning. = 他整个上午都坐立不安。
She has fidgeted throughout the interview. = 她在整个面试过程中都坐立不安。


过去完成时:
He had been fidgeting for hours before the announcement. = 在宣布之前，他已经坐立不安了好几个小时了。
She had fidgeted nervously before her performance. = 在演出之前，她紧张地坐立不安。


将来时:
He will fidget during the presentation. = 他在演示过程中会坐立不安。
She will likely fidget if she's bored. = 如果她无聊的话，她可能会坐立不安。


虚拟语气:
If he were not nervous, he wouldn't fidget so much. = 如果他不紧张，他就不会这么坐立不安。
I wish she wouldn't fidget during the exam. = 我希望她在考试期间不要坐立不安。


被动语态:
His fidgeting was noticed by everyone. = 大家都注意到了他的坐立不安。
Her fidgeting was distracting to the speaker. = 她的坐立不安分散了演讲者的注意力。


疑问句:
Is he fidgeting because he's nervous? = 他是因为紧张才坐立不安的吗？
Why is she fidgeting so much? = 她为什么这么坐立不安？


否定句:
He didn't fidget during the quiet moment. = 在安静的时刻，他并没有坐立不安。
She tried not to fidget, but it was difficult. = 她试图不要坐立不安，但这很难。


PHRASE->
fidget with something:  摆弄某物
fidget around:  坐立不安地四处走动
fidget nervously:  紧张地坐立不安
stop fidgeting:  不要再动来动去了
fidgety behavior: 坐立不安的行为


词源:
The word "fidget" ultimately derives from the Middle Dutch word "fiddelen," meaning "to fiddle" or "to play with something."  This points to the root meaning of restless, aimless movement.  The "-ing" suffix creates the gerund form, acting as both a noun and a verb.
