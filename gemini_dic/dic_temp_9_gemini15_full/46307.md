daal:/dɑːl/| n.|扁豆；豆类汤|Lentil soup or stew, typically made from pulses such as lentils, chickpeas, or split peas.| 例句：I'm making daal for dinner tonight.  今晚我做扁豆汤当晚餐。| 例句：The daal was seasoned with cumin and coriander. 这道扁豆汤用孜然和芫荽调味。|近义词：pulse, lentil soup, stew|反义词：无
