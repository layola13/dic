rattle's: /ˈrætlz/ | n. | 拨浪鼓的；嘎嘎声；（尤指快速重复的）一系列短促响亮的声音 |  A rattle is a series of short, sharp sounds, especially a repeated series of sounds.  The word can also refer to a toy that makes a rattling sound.  The possessive form "rattle's" indicates that the rattle belongs to someone or something. | The baby's rattle fell under the table.  婴儿的拨浪鼓掉到桌子底下去了。 The old car made a terrible rattle as we drove down the road.  我们开车经过路面时，那辆旧车发出可怕的嘎嘎声。 | clatter, clattering,  jingle | silence, quiet | n.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car rattles constantly. = 车子一直在发出嘎嘎声。
The old window rattles in the wind. = 旧窗户在风中嘎嘎作响。

一般过去时:
The machine rattled loudly. = 机器发出很大的嘎嘎声。
The train rattled along the tracks. = 火车沿着轨道嘎嘎作响地行驶。

现在完成时:
The old house has rattled all night. = 这栋旧房子整夜都在嘎嘎作响。
My teeth have rattled from the cold. = 我的牙齿因为寒冷而打颤。

过去完成时:
The old building had rattled before the earthquake. = 地震发生前，这栋旧楼就一直在嘎嘎作响。
The car had rattled all the way home. = 车子一路嘎嘎作响地开回了家。

将来时:
The engine will rattle when it gets old. = 发动机老了就会发出嘎嘎声。
The loose window will rattle in the storm. = 松动的窗户会在暴风雨中嘎嘎作响。

虚拟语气:
If the engine rattled, we would stop. = 如果发动机发出嘎嘎声，我们就停车。
I wish the old chair didn't rattle so much. = 我希望这把旧椅子不要嘎嘎响得这么厉害。

被动语态:
The window was rattled by the wind. = 窗户被风吹得嘎嘎作响。
The old car is constantly being rattled by the rough road. = 这辆旧车不断地被崎岖不平的路面震得嘎嘎作响。

疑问句:
Does the car rattle a lot? = 这辆车嘎嘎响得很厉害吗？
Why is the window rattling? = 为什么窗户嘎嘎响？

否定句:
The machine doesn't rattle anymore. = 机器不再嘎嘎响了。
The new car doesn't rattle at all. = 新车一点也不嘎嘎响。
