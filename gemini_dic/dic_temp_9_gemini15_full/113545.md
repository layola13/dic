macacus:/məˈkeɪkəs/| n.|猕猴属|A genus of Old World monkeys, characterized by relatively short tails and often robust build.  Macacques are widely distributed across Asia and Africa, inhabiting a variety of habitats.  They are known for their intelligence and adaptability.|例句：The researcher studied the social behavior of *Macacus fascicularis*. 研究人员研究了长尾猕猴的社会行为。The rhesus macaque (*Macacus mulatta*) is a common subject in biomedical research.  恒河猴（猕猴属的一种）是生物医学研究中常见的实验对象。|近义词：monkey (general term)|反义词：无|
