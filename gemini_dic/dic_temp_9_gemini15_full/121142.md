misdemeanor:/ˌmɪsˈdiːmənər/| n. |轻罪；轻微的过失|A misdemeanor is a minor crime, less serious than a felony, typically punishable by a fine or jail sentence of less than one year.  It often involves less moral turpitude than a felony.|例句：Driving under the influence is considered a misdemeanor in most states. 在大多数州，酒后驾车被认为是轻罪。

例句：He was charged with a misdemeanor for public intoxication. 他因公开醉酒而被控犯有轻罪。


近义词：minor offense, petty crime, infraction|反义词：felony, crime
