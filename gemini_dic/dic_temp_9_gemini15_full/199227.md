sillier:/ˈsɪliər/| adj.|更愚蠢的，更傻的|More foolish or silly than usual.  It is the comparative form of "silly".|例句：His jokes were getting sillier and sillier.  他的笑话越来越傻了。


例句：That's a sillier idea than I've ever heard.  那是我听过最愚蠢的主意了。


近义词：more foolish, more absurd, more ridiculous|反义词：more sensible, more intelligent, more wise|词性：形容词
