re-election:/ˌriːɪˈlɛkʃən/| n. |再次当选；连任|The act of electing someone to the same office again.  It typically refers to a political office, but can apply to any elected position.|例句：The president's re-election campaign was highly contested.  总统的竞选连任活动竞争激烈。

例句：Her re-election to the board was a testament to her effective leadership. 她再次当选董事会成员证明了她有效的领导能力。


近义词：second term, reappointment | 反义词：defeat, dismissal | 词性：名词
