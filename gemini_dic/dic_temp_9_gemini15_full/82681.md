guestimate:/ˈɡɛstɪmeɪt/| v. n.| 粗略估计，大致估算 | To make a rough or approximate calculation or estimate.  It combines "guess" and "estimate".|例句：I guestimated that there were about 50 people at the party. 我粗略估计派对上有大约50个人。

例句：We guestimated the cost of the project to be around $10,000. 我们粗略估计该项目的成本约为10,000美元。


近义词：estimate, approximate, ballpark figure, guess, rough estimate


反义词：precise calculation, exact measurement


词性: verb, noun
