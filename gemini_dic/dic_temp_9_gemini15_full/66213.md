excitator: /ˈɛksɪteɪtər/ | n. | 激发器，激励器 | A device that excites or stimulates something, often used in technical contexts such as electronics or medicine.  It can refer to a part of a machine that provides the initial energy or signal to start a process. | Example: The excitator in the laser system provides the initial energy needed for lasing action. 激光系统中的激发器提供了激光作用所需的初始能量。 The excitator in the MRI machine generates the magnetic field.  核磁共振成像机中的激发器产生磁场。 | 近义词: stimulator, activator, initiator | 反义词: inhibitor, suppressor | 名词
