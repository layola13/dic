cut-down:/kʌt daʊn/| vt. n.|砍倒；削减；减少；降低|To cut down something means to cut it to a smaller size or to fell a tree.  It can also refer to reducing something in amount, number, or size.|例句：The lumberjack cut down the tall tree.  伐木工人砍倒了那棵高大的树。

例句：We need to cut down on our spending. 我们需要削减开支。|近义词：reduce, decrease, shorten, fell|反义词：increase, enlarge, expand|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They cut down trees every day. = 他们每天砍树。
We cut down on sugar in our diet. = 我们减少了饮食中的糖分。

一般过去时:
The farmer cut down the old apple tree. = 农民砍倒了那棵老苹果树。
She cut down her expenses significantly. = 她大幅削减了开支。

现在完成时:
They have cut down many trees in the forest. = 他们已经砍倒了森林里许多树木。
He has cut down on smoking recently. = 他最近减少了吸烟。

过去完成时:
They had cut down all the trees before the storm. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经砍倒了所有树木。
She had cut down her workload before the deadline. = 截止日期前，她已经减少了工作量。

将来时:
We will cut down the bushes tomorrow. = 我们明天将砍掉灌木丛。
They will cut down on their travel expenses next year. = 他们明年将减少差旅费。

虚拟语气:
If we cut down on pollution, the air would be cleaner. = 如果我们减少污染，空气就会更清洁。
I wish I had cut down on my caffeine intake. = 我真希望我减少了咖啡因的摄入。

被动语态:
Many trees were cut down to build the new road. = 为了修建新路，许多树木被砍倒了。
Spending will be cut down to control inflation. = 将削减开支以控制通货膨胀。

疑问句:
Will you cut down that old oak tree? = 你要砍掉那棵老橡树吗？
How much will we cut down on our energy consumption? = 我们将减少多少能源消耗？

否定句:
They didn't cut down any trees during the nesting season. = 繁殖季节期间他们没有砍伐任何树木。
We didn't cut down on our expenses enough. = 我们没有足够地削减开支。



PHRASE->
cut down on sth = 减少…
cut sb down = 砍倒某人（比喻意义，指打败某人）
cut down to size = 使…变得渺小；挫某人的锐气
cut sb down to size = 贬低某人；使某人自知之明
cut down the noise = 降低噪音
