inter-visited:/ˌɪntərˈvɪzɪtɪd/| v. |互相访问，彼此拜访|This word combines "inter-" meaning "between or among" and "visited," implying reciprocal visits.|例句：The two families inter-visited frequently throughout the summer.  两个家庭整个夏天频繁地互相拜访。

例句：The delegates from different countries inter-visited each other's booths at the trade fair.  来自不同国家的代表在贸易展览会上互相参观了彼此的展位。


近义词：mutually visited, reciprocally visited


反义词：avoided each other, remained isolated


词性：动词
