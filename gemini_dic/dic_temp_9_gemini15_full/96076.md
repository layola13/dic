indefatigabilities:/ˌɪnˌdɪfæˈtɪɡəbɪlətiz/| n. |不倦；不知疲倦；坚持不懈|The ability to persist tirelessly;  the quality of being indefatigable.  It refers to the state of being tireless and persistent in one's efforts.|例句：Her indefatigabilities in the face of adversity were truly inspiring.  她面对逆境时坚持不懈的精神着实令人鼓舞。

Her indefatigabilities allowed her to complete the marathon despite the extreme heat. 她坚持不懈的精神让她即使在酷暑中也完成了马拉松比赛。|近义词：perseverance, persistence, tenacity, tirelessnes|反义词：laziness, lethargy, indolence
