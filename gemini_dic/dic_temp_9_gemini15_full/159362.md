poked:/poʊkt/| v.|戳，刺，捅|To push or prod with a pointed object; to briefly touch or prod someone or something.|例句：He poked the fire to make it burn brighter. 他戳了戳火堆，让它烧得更旺。
The child poked her finger into the cake. 孩子用手指戳蛋糕。|近义词：prod, jab, nudge|反义词：pull, withdraw


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He pokes the bear with a stick. = 他用棍子戳熊。
She pokes her friend playfully. = 她开玩笑地戳了戳她的朋友。

一般过去时:
I poked the cake with a fork. = 我用叉子戳了戳蛋糕。
He poked his head around the corner. = 他探头看了看拐角。

现在完成时:
She has poked the sleeping cat. = 她戳了睡着的猫。
They have poked fun at him. = 他们取笑他。


过去完成时:
He had poked the fire before he went to bed. = 他睡觉前已经戳过火堆了。
She had poked around in the drawer before finding her keys. = 她在抽屉里翻找了一阵才找到钥匙。

将来时:
I will poke the hole in the wall. = 我将戳破墙上的洞。
He will poke the sleeping dog. = 他将戳戳睡着的狗。


虚拟语气:
If I poked the bear, it might attack me. = 如果我戳熊，它可能会攻击我。
I wish I hadn't poked him. = 我真希望我没有戳他。


被动语态:
The balloon was poked and burst. = 气球被戳破了。
The sleeping cat was poked by the child. = 睡着的猫被孩子戳了。


疑问句:
Did you poke the cake? = 你戳蛋糕了吗？
Has anyone poked the sleeping baby? = 有人戳过睡着的婴儿吗？


否定句:
I didn't poke the beehive. = 我没有戳蜂巢。
He didn't poke his nose into other people's business. = 他没有管闲事。
