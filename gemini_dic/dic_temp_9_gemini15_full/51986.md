diagenesis:/ˌdaɪəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/| n. |成岩作用；成岩过程|The process of physical and chemical changes that occur in sediments after deposition and during and after lithification.  This process transforms loose sediment into solid rock. Diagenesis includes compaction, cementation, recrystallization, and other changes. |例句：The diagenesis of these sediments resulted in the formation of sandstone.  这些沉积物的成岩作用导致了砂岩的形成。|近义词：lithification, petrification|反义词：weathering, erosion|
