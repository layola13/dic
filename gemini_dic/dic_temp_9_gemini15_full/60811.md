electrophone:/ɪˈlɛktrəfoʊn/| n. |电子电话；电子乐器|An electrophone is a musical instrument that produces sound using electronic means, as opposed to acoustic instruments which produce sound through the vibration of strings, air columns, or other physical means.  Electrophones encompass a vast range of instruments, from synthesizers and electric guitars to electric organs and theremins. |Example: The concert featured a dazzling array of electrophones, from vintage synthesizers to the latest digital instruments.  这个音乐会演奏了令人眼花缭乱的电子乐器，从老式合成器到最新的数字乐器。|近义词：electronic instrument, synthesizer|反义词：acoustic instrument
