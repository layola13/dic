in any case:/ɪnˈeni keɪs/| adv. |无论如何，不管怎样|In any case, we should be prepared for the worst. |近义词：anyway, regardless, nevertheless |反义词：in no case


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He goes to work in any case. = 他无论如何都去上班。
She attends the meeting in any case. = 她无论如何都参加会议。


一般过去时:
He went to work in any case. = 他无论如何都去上班了。
She attended the meeting in any case. = 她无论如何都参加了会议。


现在完成时:
He has gone to work in any case. = 他无论如何都去上班了。
She has attended the meeting in any case. = 她无论如何都参加了会议。


过去完成时:
He had gone to work in any case. = 他无论如何都已去上班了。
She had attended the meeting in any case. = 她无论如何都已参加了会议。


将来时:
He will go to work in any case. = 他无论如何都会去上班。
She will attend the meeting in any case. = 她无论如何都会参加会议。


虚拟语气:
Even if it rains, we will go to the park in any case. = 即使下雨，我们无论如何都会去公园。
I would go to the party in any case, even if I didn't know anyone. = 我无论如何都会去参加派对，即使我不认识任何人。


被动语态:
The decision will be made in any case. =  这个决定无论如何都会做出。
The meeting was held in any case. = 会议无论如何都举行了。


疑问句:

否定句:
