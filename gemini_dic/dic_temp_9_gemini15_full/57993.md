driven:/ˈdrɪvən/| adj. v. | 被驱动的；有动机的；努力工作的；|  driven describes something that is motivated, compelled, or forced into action; it can also describe something powered by an engine or other force. |例句：He is a driven individual, always striving for success. 他是一个有动力的人，总是努力争取成功。  The car is driven by a powerful engine. 这辆车由一台强劲的发动机驱动。|近义词：motivated, ambitious, impelled, powered|反义词：unmotivated, lazy, lethargic

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is driven by a strong desire to succeed. = 他被强烈的成功欲望所驱使。
The machine is driven by electricity. = 这台机器由电力驱动。

一般过去时:
She was driven to distraction by the noise. = 她被噪音弄得心烦意乱。
The old car was driven by a small engine. = 那辆旧车由一台小发动机驱动。

现在完成时:
They have been driven by a need for change. = 他们一直被改变的需要所驱使。
The project has been driven forward by a dedicated team. = 这个项目一直由一个敬业的团队推进。

过去完成时:
He had been driven to the edge of despair. = 他已经被逼到绝望的边缘。
The decision had been driven by political considerations. = 这个决定是由政治因素驱使的。

将来时:
The company will be driven by innovation in the future. = 未来，这家公司将以创新为驱动。
The car will be driven by a new type of battery. = 这辆车将由新型电池驱动。

虚拟语气:
If I were driven by greed, I would have taken the money. = 如果我贪婪驱使，我就拿了钱。
I wish I were not driven by fear. = 我希望我不被恐惧驱使。

被动语态:
The car is driven by a hybrid engine. = 这辆车由混合动力发动机驱动。
The decision was driven by economic factors. = 这个决定是由经济因素驱使的。

疑问句:
What is driving your decision? = 什么在驱动你的决定？
Is this machine driven by steam? = 这台机器是由蒸汽驱动的吗？

否定句:
He is not driven by ambition. = 他不被野心驱使。
The economy is not driven solely by consumer spending. = 经济并非仅仅由消费者支出驱动。


PHRASE->
