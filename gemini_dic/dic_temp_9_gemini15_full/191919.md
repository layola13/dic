scones:/skɒnz/| n.|烤饼|A scone is a small, quick bread, typically baked and served warm with butter or jam.  It can be sweet or savory, depending on the recipe.|例句：I love to have scones with clotted cream and jam for afternoon tea. 我喜欢下午茶时吃涂抹了凝脂奶油和果酱的烤饼。|例句：She baked a batch of cheese and onion scones for the picnic. 她烤了一批奶酪洋葱烤饼野餐食用。|近义词：biscuit (in some regions)|反义词：无
