proto-historian:/ˈproʊtoʊhɪstɔːriən/ | n. |史前史学家，研究史前时期的学者 | A proto-historian studies the period of human history before the existence of written records, relying on archaeological evidence and other non-written sources to reconstruct past societies and events.  | Example: The proto-historian presented compelling evidence of early human settlements in the region.  史前史学家提出了令人信服的证据，证明该地区早期人类定居点。|近义词: prehistorian, archaeologist (depending on focus) |反义词: historian (of recorded history)
