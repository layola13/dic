reoutlined:/ˌriˈaʊtlaɪnd/| v.|重新概述，重新勾勒|To outline again; to give a new outline to.  This implies a revision or a more detailed description of a previously outlined plan, topic, or subject.|例句：The professor reoutlined the lecture's main points to ensure clarity. 教授重新概述了讲座的主要要点以确保清晰度。
The architect reoutlined the building's design after receiving client feedback.  建筑师在收到客户反馈后重新勾勒了建筑物的方案。|近义词：revise, reshape, redefine|反义词：finalize, complete
