productize:/ˈprɒdʌktaɪz/ /ˈprɑːdʌktaɪz/| vt.|使产品化；将…商品化|To develop a product or service from an idea or concept, often involving processes of design, development, manufacturing, and marketing.|例句：The company is trying to productize its innovative technology. 公司正试图将其创新技术产品化。

例句：They productized their software and released it to the market.  他们将他们的软件产品化并投放市场。


近义词：commercialize, market,  monetize

反义词：de-productize (非正式用法)


词性:动词

USAGE->
