sauteing:/soʊˈteɪɪŋ/ | v. |  翻炒；煎； sauté 的现在分词 |  The act of sauteing involves cooking food in a small amount of fat over relatively high heat, stirring frequently.  This method is used to quickly brown and cook food while maintaining its texture and flavor. | 例句：I'm sauteing onions and peppers for the fajitas. 我正在为煎饼翻炒洋葱和青椒。

例句：The chef was expertly sauteing the shrimp in butter.  厨师熟练地用黄油煎虾。


近义词：stir-frying, pan-frying, frying


反义词：boiling, stewing, simmering


词性：动词
