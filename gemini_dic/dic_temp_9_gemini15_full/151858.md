passionately:/ˈpæʃənətli/ | adv. |热情地，充满激情地 |  The adverb "passionately" describes an action or state performed or experienced with intense emotion, zeal, or fervor. It suggests a strong feeling or belief driving the action. | 例句：He passionately defended his beliefs. 他热情地捍卫自己的信仰。 She passionately embraced her new life. 她热情地拥抱了她的新生活。 |近义词：ardently, fervently, intensely, zealously | 反义词：apathetically, indifferently, apathetically, coldly
