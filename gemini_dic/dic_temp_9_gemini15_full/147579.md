/ˌoʊvərˈpɛsɪmɪzəm/ | n. |过度悲观；悲观主义 | Excessive pessimism; an instance of being overly pessimistic.  It's characterized by an exaggeratedly negative outlook and expectation of unfavorable outcomes, often exceeding what is realistically justified.  |  His overpessimism prevented him from taking any risks in his business ventures.  他的过度悲观使他无法在商业冒险中承担任何风险。  The team's overpessimism before the game affected their performance.  比赛前球队过度悲观的情绪影响了他们的表现。|  pessimism, gloom, despondency | optimism, hopefulness, cheerfulness | 名词
