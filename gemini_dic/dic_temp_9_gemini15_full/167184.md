pretemperately: /ˌpriːˈtɛmpərətli/ | adv. |  过早地，过急地，仓促地 |  Acting or done before the proper or usual time; prematurely. It implies a lack of sufficient consideration or preparation. | 例句：He pretemperately accused her of stealing, without any evidence. 他过早地指责她偷窃，没有任何证据。


例句：She pretemperately ended the conversation, cutting him off mid-sentence. 她过早地结束了谈话，打断了他说话。


近义词：hasty, rash, premature, impetuous


反义词：deliberately, cautiously, carefully


词性：副词
