mandelate:/ˈmændəleɪt/| n. |扁桃酸盐，α-羟基苯乙酸盐|A salt or ester of mandelic acid. Mandelates are used in various applications, including pharmaceuticals and cosmetics.|例句：Calcium mandelate is sometimes used as a urinary antiseptic.  钙扁桃酸盐有时用作尿路防腐剂。|  例句：Many mandelate derivatives exhibit interesting biological activities. 许多扁桃酸酯衍生物表现出有趣的生物活性。|近义词：mandelic acid salt (扁桃酸盐)|反义词：无|名词


USAGE->
This word is primarily a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations like "hello".  The usage focuses on its chemical and pharmaceutical contexts.  Therefore, tense variations are not applicable in the same way as with verbs.
