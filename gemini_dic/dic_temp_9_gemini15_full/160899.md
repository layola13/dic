post-experimental: /ˌpoʊstɪkˈsperɪmɛntl/ | adj. | 实验后的，实验结束后的 | Relating to or denoting the period or observations following an experiment.  It refers to the phase after the main experimental manipulation has concluded.  | Example: Post-experimental interviews revealed unexpected insights into participant behavior.  实验后的访谈揭示了参与者行为的意外见解。| 近义词：post-test, follow-up | 反义词：pre-experimental
