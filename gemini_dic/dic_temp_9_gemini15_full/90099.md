hostship:/ˈhoʊstʃɪp/ | n. | 主办权；主人身份；款待 | The right or duty of being a host; the act of being a host.  It can refer to the role, responsibility, or position of a host. | Example:  The university took on the hostship of the international conference. 大学承担了这次国际会议的主办权。  The hostship of the event was given to a local charity.  此次活动的举办权授予了一个当地慈善机构。|近义词：hospitality,  hosting,  management | 反义词： guestship
