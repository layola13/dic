franchisement:/ˈfrænˌtʃaɪzmənt/| n. |特许经营权；公民权；解放|The granting of a franchise; the right or license granted to an individual or group to market a company's goods or services in a particular territory.  It can also refer to the act of granting or obtaining civil rights. |例句：The company expanded through franchising. 这家公司通过特许经营的方式扩张。

例句：The women fought hard for their franchisement.  这些妇女为争取自己的公民权而努力奋斗。


近义词：license, privilege, enfranchisement |反义词：disenfranchisement, suppression


词性：名词
