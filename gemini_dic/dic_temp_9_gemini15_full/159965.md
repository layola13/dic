polyporus:/pɒliˈpɔːrəs/| n. |多孔菌属；多孔菌 |A genus of fungi characterized by having pores instead of gills on the underside of the cap.  Many species are wood-decay fungi. |Example:  The researcher identified the mushroom as a species of *Polyporus*.  研究人员将这种蘑菇鉴定为多孔菌属的一种。  |近义词:bracket fungus, shelf fungus|反义词: N/A
