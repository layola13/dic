rebend:/riːˈbɛnd/ | vt. |重新弯曲；再次弯曲 | To bend something again or in a different direction.  |例句：The carpenter had to rebend the warped piece of wood. 木匠不得不重新弯曲那块翘曲的木头。
例句：She rebent the wire to create a new shape. 她重新弯曲了铁丝，创造了一个新的形状。|近义词：bend again, reshape, curve anew | 反义词：straighten
