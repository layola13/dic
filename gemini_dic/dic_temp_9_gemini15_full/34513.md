cleaned:/klɪnd/ | v. | 清洁的；打扫过的 |  The past tense and past participle of "clean". It describes something that has been made clean.  |  例句：She cleaned the house thoroughly. 她彻底打扫了房子。

例句：The car has been cleaned. 这辆车已经被清洗过了。


近义词：purified, washed, tidied


反义词：dirty, soiled, messy


词性：动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cleans his room every day. = 他每天都打扫他的房间。
She cleans the dishes after dinner. = 晚餐后她洗碗。


一般过去时:
I cleaned the kitchen yesterday. = 我昨天打扫了厨房。
They cleaned the park after the festival. = 节日后他们清理了公园。


现在完成时:
She has cleaned the windows. = 她已经擦过窗户了。
He has cleaned his car three times this week. = 他这周已经洗了三次车了。


过去完成时:
I had cleaned the house before they arrived. = 他们到达之前我已经打扫好房子了。
They had cleaned up the mess before the guests came. = 客人来之前他们已经清理了烂摊子。


将来时:
I will clean the bathroom tomorrow. = 我明天将打扫浴室。
She will clean the entire house this weekend. = 她这个周末将打扫整个房子。


虚拟语气:
If I had cleaned the room earlier, I wouldn't be so tired now. = 如果我早点打扫房间，现在就不会这么累了。
I wish I had cleaned my car yesterday. = 我真希望我昨天洗了我的车。


被动语态:
The floor was cleaned yesterday. = 地板昨天被清扫了。
The dishes are cleaned every day by the dishwasher. = 每天洗碗机都洗碗。


疑问句:
Have you cleaned the dishes yet? = 你洗碗了吗？
Did they clean the pool this morning? = 他们今天早上打扫游泳池了吗？


否定句:
I didn't clean my room this week. = 我这周没有打扫我的房间。
She hasn't cleaned her car yet. = 她还没洗车。


PHRASE->
clean up = 打扫干净
clean out = 清理干净
clean off = 擦掉
clean through = 彻底清理
spring clean = 大扫除
