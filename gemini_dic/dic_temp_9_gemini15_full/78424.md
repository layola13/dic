get the best of:/ɡɛt ðə bɛst ʌv/ | phrasal verb | 胜过；占…的便宜；战胜；从…中获得最大益处 | To defeat or surpass someone or something; to gain an advantage over someone or something; to derive the most benefit from something. |


例句：

He got the best of me in the argument. = 他在争论中胜过我。

The team got the best of their opponents in the final game. = 这支队伍在决赛中战胜了他们的对手。


近义词：defeat, outsmart, outwit, overcome, surpass, prevail over, triumph over

反义词：lose to, be defeated by, be surpassed by


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He gets the best of his brother in chess every time. = 他每次下棋都胜过他哥哥。
She gets the best of every situation. = 她总是能从各种境况中获得最大益处。


一般过去时:
The underdog got the best of the favorite in the race. = 黑马在比赛中战胜了热门选手。
I got the best of the deal by negotiating a lower price. = 我通过协商降低价格，从而在交易中占了便宜。


现在完成时:
He has gotten the best of me in three out of our five matches. = 在我们五场比赛中，他已经赢了我三场。
She has gotten the best of her illness and is feeling much better. = 她已经战胜了她的疾病，感觉好多了。


过去完成时:
Before the final round, he had gotten the best of his opponent in every round. = 在最后一轮之前，他每一轮都胜过了他的对手。
By the time the project finished, she had gotten the best of the difficult challenges. = 到项目结束时，她已经克服了那些艰难的挑战。


将来时:
I think he will get the best of his rival in the upcoming debate. = 我认为他会在即将到来的辩论中胜过他的对手。
With proper planning, we will get the best of this opportunity. = 通过适当的规划，我们将充分利用这次机会。


虚拟语气:
If I had trained harder, I might have gotten the best of him. = 如果我训练更努力，我或许就能战胜他。
If she had planned better, she could have gotten the best of the situation. = 如果她计划得更好，她就能从这场面中获得最大益处。


被动语态:
He was gotten the best of in the negotiation. = 他在谈判中吃了亏。
The company was gotten the best of by its competitors. = 这家公司被竞争对手击败了。


疑问句:
Did he get the best of you in the game? = 他在游戏中赢了你吗？
Will she get the best of this challenging project? = 她能成功完成这个具有挑战性的项目吗？


否定句:
He didn't get the best of me in the argument. = 他在争论中没有胜过我。
She didn't get the best of the situation; she made several mistakes. = 她没有充分利用这个机会；她犯了几个错误。


PHRASE->
get the best of both worlds  = 两全其美

get the best possible result = 获得最佳结果
get the best from someone = 从某人身上得到最好的东西
