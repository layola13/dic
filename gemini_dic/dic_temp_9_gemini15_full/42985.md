covetous:/ˈkʌvɪtəs/ | adj. | 贪婪的；渴望的 | Having or showing a great desire to possess something, typically something that belongs to someone else.  |例句：He cast a covetous eye on his neighbor's new car. 他贪婪地盯着邻居的新车。

例句：Her covetous nature led her to try and steal her friend's ideas.  她的贪婪本性导致她试图窃取朋友的想法。


近义词：greedy, avaricious, envious, jealous | 反义词：generous, unselfish, content


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is covetous of his brother's success. = 他渴望他兄弟的成功。
She is always covetous of other people's possessions. = 她总是贪婪地想要别人的财产。


一般过去时:
He was covetous of the prize. = 他渴望得到那个奖项。
She was covetous of her friend's beautiful dress. = 她垂涎她朋友那件漂亮的裙子。


现在完成时:
They have been covetous of power for years. = 他们多年来一直渴望权力。
He has been covetous of that painting since he first saw it. = 自从他第一次看到那幅画以来，他就一直渴望得到它。


过去完成时:
She had been covetous of her sister's engagement ring for months. = 她几个月来一直觊觎她姐姐的订婚戒指。
He had been covetous of his colleague's promotion for a long time. = 他很久以来就一直渴望得到他同事的晋升。


将来时:
She will be covetous of the new house. = 她将会渴望得到那栋新房子。
He will be covetous of his friend's new job. = 他将会渴望得到他朋友的新工作。


虚拟语气:
If I were not so covetous, I would be happier. = 如果我不那么贪婪，我会更快乐。
I wish I weren't so covetous of material possessions. = 我希望我不那么贪图物质享受。


被动语态:
His ambition was covetously desired by many. = 他的野心受到许多人的垂涎。
The position was covetously sought after by many applicants. = 许多申请者都垂涎这个职位。


疑问句:
Is he covetous of your success? = 他觊觎你的成功吗？
Are they covetous of our land? = 他们贪图我们的土地吗？


否定句:
He is not covetous of wealth. = 他不贪图财富。
She is not covetous of other people's belongings. = 她不贪图别人的东西。

词源:
The word "covetous" comes from the Old French word "coveiter," meaning "to desire eagerly."  This, in turn, derives from the Latin word "cupidus," meaning "desirous."
