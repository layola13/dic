dominoes:/ˈdɒmɪnəʊz/ | n. |多米诺骨牌 |Rectangular tiles, usually made of wood or plastic, with a line dividing them into two squares, each of which is marked with a number of spots.  Dominoes are used in various games where players match the numbers of spots on their tiles. |例句：We played dominoes for hours. 我们玩了几个小时的多米诺骨牌。

例句：The children knocked over the tower of dominoes. 孩子们把多米诺骨牌塔推倒了。


近义词： tiles (in the context of game pieces)


反义词：无


词性：名词
