interplacental: /ˌɪntərpləˈsɛntəl/ | adj. |胎盘间的，胎盘间隙的 | Relating to the space or area between placentas or between a placenta and another structure.  The term is primarily used in a medical or biological context, often referring to the transfer of substances between the mother and fetus. | Example: Interplacental hemorrhage is a serious complication of pregnancy. 胎盘间出血是妊娠期的一种严重并发症。|近义词: interplacentary (less common)|反义词: intraplacental (within the placenta) |形容词
