The journalist preaccused the company of environmental damage, prompting a swift denial.  记者预先指控该公司造成环境破坏，促使该公司迅速否认。|近义词：prejudge, anticipate accusation against,  forewarn of guilt |反义词：exonerate, vindicate, acquit
