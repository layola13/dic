melted:/ˈmɛltɪd/| adj.|融化的；消融的|The adjective "melted" describes something that has been changed from a solid to a liquid state by the application of heat.  It implies a process of softening and liquefying due to heat.|例句：The chocolate melted in the sun. 巧克力在阳光下融化了。The snow had melted overnight. 雪一夜之间融化了。|近义词：liquefied, fused, dissolved|反义词：frozen, solidified
