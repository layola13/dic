prepared:/prɪˈpɛərd/ | adj. | 准备好的；预备好的；有准备的 | Ready; arranged in advance.  |例句：He was well prepared for the exam. 他已为考试做好了充分的准备。

例句：The team is prepared to face any challenge.  团队已准备好迎接任何挑战。


近义词：ready, ready-made,  set, equipped


反义词：unprepared, unprepared for,  ill-prepared


词源:  From Old French *preparer, from Latin prōparāre "to prepare," from prō- "forward" + parāre "to prepare, get ready."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is prepared for the worst. = 他已为最坏的情况做好了准备。
She is always prepared to help others. = 她总是乐于助人。


一般过去时:
They were prepared to leave at a moment's notice. = 他们已准备好随时离开。
The students were well prepared for the presentation. = 学生们为演示做好了充分的准备。


现在完成时:
I have prepared everything for the party. = 我已经为派对准备好了所有东西。
She has prepared a delicious meal for us. = 她为我们准备了一顿美味的饭菜。


过去完成时:
We had prepared the documents before the meeting. = 我们在会议前已经准备好了文件。
He had prepared a speech, but he didn't get a chance to deliver it. = 他准备好了演讲，但没有机会发表。


将来时:
I will be prepared to answer any questions. = 我将准备好回答任何问题。
They will have prepared the room by the time the guests arrive. = 客人到达时，他们将已经准备好房间。


虚拟语气:
If I were better prepared, I would have done better on the test. = 如果我准备得更好，考试成绩会更好。
I wish I had been prepared for the unexpected event. = 我真希望我已经为突发事件做好了准备。


被动语态:
The food was prepared carefully. = 食物是精心准备的。
The meeting room was prepared in advance. = 会议室提前准备好了。


疑问句:
Are you prepared for the journey? = 你准备好旅行了吗？
Is the report prepared yet? = 报告准备好了吗？


否定句:
I wasn't prepared for such a difficult question. = 我没准备好回答这么难的问题。
She wasn't prepared to compromise. = 她不愿意妥协。


PHRASE->
be prepared to:  准备好做...

prepared remarks: 预先准备好的发言
preparedness:  准备状态
