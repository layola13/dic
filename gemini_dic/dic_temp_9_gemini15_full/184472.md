restaffs:/ˌriːˈstæf/| v.|重新配备员工|To provide a company or organization with a new staff.  This often happens after a period of layoffs or significant staff turnover. The process involves recruiting, hiring, and training new employees to fill the necessary roles.|例句：The company had to restaffe after a major restructuring. 公司在重大重组后不得不重新配备员工。|近义词：re-staff, restaffing|反义词：lay off, downsize.
