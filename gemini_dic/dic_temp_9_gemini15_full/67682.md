exuberance:/ɪɡˈzjuːbərəns/| n. |兴高采烈；热情洋溢；充沛；茂盛|The quality of being full of energy, excitement, and cheerfulness; overflowing abundance.  It often describes a feeling or display of great enthusiasm and energy.|例句：Her exuberance was infectious, and soon everyone was laughing and dancing.  她的热情洋溢，很快大家都笑了起来，跳起了舞。

例句：The exuberance of youth is often short-lived. 青春的活力往往是短暂的。


近义词：enthusiasm, zeal, ebullience, vivacity, buoyancy|反义词：apathy, lethargy, depression, gloom


词源：From Middle French *exubérance, from Latin exuberantia "abundance, overflow," from exuberare "to overflow," from ex- "out" + uber "udder, breast, fruitfulness."  The original sense referred to the overflowing abundance of milk from a cow's udder, later extending to other forms of abundance and overflowing energy.
