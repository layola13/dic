ruralite:/ˈrʊrəlˌaɪt/| n. |农村居民；乡下人 |A ruralite is a person who lives in a rural area.  Ruralites often have strong ties to the land and traditional ways of life. |例句：Many ruralites rely on farming or other agricultural pursuits for their livelihood. 许多农村居民依靠农业或其他农业活动为生。

The increasing availability of high-speed internet is slowly changing the lives of many ruralites.  越来越多的高速互联网正在慢慢改变许多农村居民的生活。|近义词：country dweller, villager, peasant (depending on context) |反义词：urbanite
