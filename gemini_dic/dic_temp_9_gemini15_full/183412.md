reproposing:/ˌriːprəˈpoʊzɪŋ/| v. |重新提出；再次提议 |The act of proposing something again after it has been previously rejected or considered.  It implies a renewed attempt to gain acceptance or approval. |例句：After the initial proposal was rejected, the committee is reproposing the budget with some modifications.  = 初步提案被否决后，委员会对预算进行了一些修改后再次提出。

例句：He kept reproposing the same idea, despite the team's clear lack of interest. = 尽管团队明显缺乏兴趣，他还是不断地提出同样的想法。


近义词：re-suggesting, resubmitting, proposing again, reiterating

反义词：withdrawing, rejecting, abandoning
