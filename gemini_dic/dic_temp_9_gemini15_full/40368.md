contracture:/ˈkɒntrækʧə(r)/, /ˈkɑːntrækʧər/| n.|挛缩，肌挛缩|A condition of shortening and hardening of muscles, tendons, or other tissues, often resulting in deformity and restricted movement.  It can be caused by various factors, including injury, disease, or lack of use.|例句：The stroke left him with a severe contracture in his left arm. 中风导致他的左臂严重挛缩。

例句：Physical therapy can help to alleviate contractures in patients with cerebral palsy. 物理治疗可以帮助缓解脑瘫患者的挛缩。


近义词：shortening, rigidity, fixation |反义词：relaxation, lengthening
