siliciferous:/ˌsɪlɪˈsɪfərəs/| adj.|含硅的，硅质的|Containing or consisting of silica.  The term refers to rocks, minerals, or other materials that are rich in silicon dioxide (SiO2).|例句：The geologist identified the siliciferous rock formation as indicative of a past volcanic environment. 地质学家将含硅岩石地层确定为过去火山环境的标志。

例句：Many diatoms have siliciferous shells, contributing to the formation of diatomaceous earth. 许多硅藻具有含硅的壳，有助于硅藻土的形成。


近义词：siliceous, silicious|反义词：silicon-free


词性:形容词
