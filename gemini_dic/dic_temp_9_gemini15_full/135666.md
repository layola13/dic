nonpregnant:/ˌnɒnˈprɛɡnənt/| adj. |未怀孕的|Not pregnant; not expecting a child.  The term describes a woman or female animal that is not currently carrying a fetus.|The doctor confirmed that she was nonpregnant.|近义词：unpregnant (less common)|反义词：pregnant


USAGE->
一般现在时:
She is nonpregnant. = 她没有怀孕。
The cat is nonpregnant. = 这只猫没有怀孕。


一般过去时:
She was nonpregnant at the time of the accident. = 事故发生时她没有怀孕。
The cow was nonpregnant last month. = 上个月那头母牛没有怀孕。


现在完成时:
She has been nonpregnant for several years. = 她好几年都没有怀孕了。
The mare has remained nonpregnant despite repeated attempts at breeding. = 尽管反复尝试繁殖，母马仍然没有怀孕。


过去完成时:
She had been nonpregnant for a year before trying again. = 她尝试再次怀孕前已经一年没有怀孕了。
The researchers had ensured the animals were nonpregnant before initiating the study. = 研究人员在开始研究之前已经确保动物没有怀孕。


将来时:
She will likely be nonpregnant for a while longer. = 她可能还要过一段时间才能怀孕。
The veterinarian predicts she will remain nonpregnant unless bred soon. = 兽医预测，除非很快繁殖，否则她将保持未怀孕状态。


虚拟语气:
If she were nonpregnant, she would consider adopting a child. = 如果她没有怀孕，她会考虑收养一个孩子。
I wish I had been nonpregnant when I was younger. = 我希望我年轻的时候没有怀孕。


被动语态:
(This adjective is rarely used in passive voice, as it describes a state rather than an action.)


疑问句:

否定句:

The dog is not nonpregnant; she's pregnant. = 这条狗没怀孕；她怀孕了。
