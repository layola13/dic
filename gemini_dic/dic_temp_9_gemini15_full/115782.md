mariolatry:/ˌmæriˈɒlətri/| n. |对圣母玛利亚的崇拜；圣母崇拜 |The excessive or idolatrous worship of the Virgin Mary.  Mariolatry is a term used to describe the practice of worshipping the Virgin Mary as if she were a goddess. This practice is considered heretical by some Christian denominations. |Example:  Some critics accuse certain Catholic traditions of bordering on mariolatry.  一些批评家指责某些天主教传统近乎于圣母崇拜。|近义词：hyperdulia (过度的敬礼),  veneration (崇敬) |反义词：iconoclasm (破除偶像崇拜)
