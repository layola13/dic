etymologies:/ˌɛtɪˈmɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 词源学；词源；语源 | The study of the origin of words and the way in which their meanings have changed throughout history.  It involves tracing words back to their earliest known forms and analyzing how their sounds and meanings have evolved. | Example:  The etymologies of many English words can be traced back to ancient Greek and Latin.  词源学中的许多英语单词可以追溯到古希腊语和拉丁语。|近义词：word origins, derivations | 反义词：N/A
