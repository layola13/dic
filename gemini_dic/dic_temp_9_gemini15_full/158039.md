plants:/plænts/| n.|植物；庄稼；工厂；设备|Plants are living organisms that produce their own food through photosynthesis. 植物是通过光合作用产生自身食物的生物体。|近义词：vegetation, flora |反义词：animals

一般现在时:
Plants grow towards the sunlight. = 植物向阳光生长。
Many plants need water to survive. = 许多植物需要水才能生存。

一般过去时:
The farmer planted many plants last spring. = 农民去年春天种了很多植物。
We visited a botanical garden where we saw many rare plants. = 我们参观了一个植物园，在那里我们看到了许多稀有植物。

现在完成时:
They have planted thousands of trees in the park. = 他们在公园里种了成千上万棵树。
