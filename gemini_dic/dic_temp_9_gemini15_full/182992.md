replenish:/rɪˈplɛnɪʃ/| vt.|补充，再装满|To fill something up again|The team replenished their water bottles after the race.  队员们赛后又装满了水壶。|The farmer replenished the soil with fertilizer. 农民用肥料补充土壤养分。|restock, refill, replenish|deplete, exhaust, empty|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We replenish the stock regularly. = 我们定期补充库存。
She replenishes her energy with a healthy meal. = 她用一顿健康餐补充能量。

一般过去时:
They replenished the supplies before the expedition. = 他们在探险前补充了物资。
He replenished his coffee cup after finishing the first one. = 他喝完第一杯咖啡后又续了一杯。

现在完成时:
We have replenished the water tank twice today. = 我们今天已经两次补充了水箱。
She has replenished her wardrobe with new clothes. = 她用新衣服补充了自己的衣橱。

过去完成时:
They had replenished the fuel before starting the long journey. = 他们在开始长途旅行之前已经补充了燃料。
She had replenished her savings account before buying the car. = 她在买车之前已经补充了储蓄账户的存款。

将来时:
We will replenish the inventory next week. = 我们下周将补充库存。
He will replenish his supplies after the camping trip. = 野营旅行后，他会补充他的物资。


虚拟语气:
If we replenished our stock now, we wouldn't have a shortage later. = 如果我们现在补充库存，以后就不会短缺了。
I wish I had replenished the oil before driving long distances. = 我真希望我在长途驾驶前加过油。


被动语态:
The water tank was replenished by the automated system. = 水箱由自动化系统补充。
The shelves were replenished with new books. = 货架上补充了新书。


疑问句:
Will you replenish the coffee pot? = 你会补充咖啡壶吗？
Have they replenished the supplies yet? = 他们已经补充物资了吗？


否定句:
They didn't replenish the fuel, so the car ran out of gas. = 他们没有补充燃料，所以汽车没油了。
We haven't replenished the printer ink yet. = 我们还没给打印机补充墨水。
