what:/wɒt/ /wʌt/| pron.|什么；多么；…怎么样|  The word "what" is an interrogative pronoun used to ask questions about things, events, or ideas. It can also function as an exclamation or to introduce a clause.  |例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|例句： What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！|近义词：which, how|反义词：nothing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? = 你喜欢吃什么？

一般过去时:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

现在完成时:
What have you been doing? = 你一直在做什么？
What has she accomplished? = 她取得了什么成就？

过去完成时:
What had you eaten before you felt sick? = 你在感到不舒服之前吃了什么？
What had they decided before the meeting started? = 会议开始之前他们决定了什么？

将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天打算做什么？
What will the weather be like? = 天气会怎么样？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我真希望知道他在想什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being built in the city center? = 市中心正在建造什么？

疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What color is your car? = 你的车是什么颜色？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
He didn't say what he wanted. = 他没有说他想要什么。


PHRASE->
what if = 如果...会怎么样
what for = 为什么
what about = ...怎么样
what's more = 而且；更重要的是
what's up = 怎么样/你好吗
