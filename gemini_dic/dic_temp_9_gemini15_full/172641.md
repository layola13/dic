pyrrhus:/ˈpɪrəs/| n. |皮洛士|A victory that is won at such a great cost that it is scarcely better than defeat.  A costly victory; a success that comes at too great a price.|例句：The general won the battle, but it was a Pyrrhic victory; his army was decimated.  将军赢得了战斗，但这却是一场代价惨重的胜利；他的军队被大量削弱了。|近义词：costly victory, hollow victory, bittersweet victory |反义词：decisive victory, easy victory |名词
