senescence:/səˈnɛsəns/| n. |衰老；老化；衰退 |The natural process of aging, characterized by a decline in physical and mental functions.  It encompasses a wide range of changes at the cellular, tissue, and organismal levels.  Senescence is a complex process influenced by genetic factors, lifestyle choices, and environmental exposures.|例句：The senescence of the leaves in autumn is a beautiful sight. 秋天树叶的衰老是一幅美丽的景象。
The senescence of the population is a major concern for many developed nations. 人口老龄化是许多发达国家的主要担忧。|近义词：aging, aging process, decay, deterioration|反义词：youth, rejuvenation
