relocked: /ˌriːˈlɒkt/ | v. |重新锁上，再次锁上 | To lock something again after it has been unlocked.  |例句：I relocked the door after hearing a noise outside. 我听到外面有声音后又把门锁上了。

例句：The police relocked the crime scene after the investigators left. 调查人员离开后，警方再次封锁了犯罪现场。


近义词：resecure, refasten, lock again


反义词：unlock, open
