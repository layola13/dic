semaeostomata:/ˌsiːmeɪoʊˈstoʊmətə/| n. | 海豆芽目 | A taxonomic order within the class Asteroidea (starfish) characterized by having a madreporite (a sieve plate) located on the oral (mouth) surface.  Members of this order typically have a relatively small, disc-shaped body and long, slender arms. They are often found in deep waters. |  The Semaeostomata are a fascinating group of starfish found in deep-sea environments. | 近义词：暂无精确近义词，可根据语境用Asteroidea (海星纲) 或相关的海星科属名代替 | 反义词： 暂无 |  名词
