ows:/aʊz/| n.| 绵羊|Ows is the plural of "ow," an archaic word for a sheep.|例句：A flock of ows grazed peacefully in the meadow.一群绵羊在草地上安静地吃草。|近义词：sheep, lambs|反义词：无


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The ows graze in the pasture. = 绵羊在牧场上吃草。
Many ows live on this farm. = 这个农场养了很多绵羊。


一般过去时:
The ows grazed peacefully yesterday. = 绵羊昨天安静地吃草。
The farmer counted the ows before nightfall. = 农夫在夜幕降临前清点绵羊。


现在完成时:
The ows have grazed all day. = 绵羊已经吃了一整天的草。
The farmer has sold many ows this year. = 今年农夫已经卖了很多绵羊。


过去完成时:
The ows had already grazed before the rain started. = 下雨前绵羊已经吃过草了。
The shepherd had counted the ows twice before he went to sleep. = 牧羊人在睡觉前数了两次羊。


将来时:
The ows will graze in the new field tomorrow. = 绵羊明天将在新牧场吃草。
The farmer will shear the ows next week. = 农夫下周将要剪羊毛。


虚拟语气:
If the ows had more grass, they would be healthier. = 如果绵羊有更多草吃，它们会更健康。
I wish the ows were not so skittish. = 我希望绵羊不要那么胆小。


被动语态:
The ows were sheared last week. = 绵羊上周被剪了毛。
The ows are being fed regularly. = 绵羊定期被喂食。


疑问句:
How many ows are there in the field? = 田里有多少只绵羊？
Are those ows yours? = 那些绵羊是你的吗？


否定句:
The ows were not grazing in the field. = 绵羊不在田里吃草。
There aren't many ows left on the farm. = 农场上剩下的绵羊不多了。


PHRASE->
a flock of ows = 一群绵羊
a herd of ows = 一群绵羊 (较少用，herd通常指牛群)

count the ows = 数绵羊
care for the ows = 饲养绵羊
