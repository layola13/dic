confessant:/kənˈfɛsənt/| n.|忏悔者；坦白者|A person who confesses, especially to a priest or in a legal context.  Someone who admits to a crime or wrongdoing.|例句：The confessant detailed the crime to the police.  忏悔者向警方详细描述了罪行。
例句：The priest listened patiently to the confessant's remorse. 牧师耐心地倾听了忏悔者的悔恨。|近义词：penitent, confessor|反义词：perpetrator (in the context of concealing wrongdoing)
