frimaire:/friːˈmɛər/| n. | 霜月 (法兰西共和历的第九个月) | Frimaire is the ninth month of the French Republican Calendar, running from November 21 to December 20. It is named after the French word for "frost" or "hoarfrost," reflecting the typical weather of that time of year.  | The French Revolutionary Calendar was in use from 1793 to 1806. During the period of Frimaire, the weather was usually cold and frosty. | 近义词: November (in Gregorian calendar) | 反义词:  None directly applicable, as it's a specific month name. | 名词
