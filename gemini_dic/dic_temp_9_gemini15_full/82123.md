groined:/ɡrɔɪnd/ | adj. | 拱形的；有拱肋的 | Having a groin or groins; arched.  A groined vault is a type of architecture where two or more barrel vaults intersect.  | 例句：The cathedral's groined ceiling was a masterpiece of engineering. 这座大教堂的拱形天花板是工程学的杰作。


The ceiling of the medieval hall was beautifully groined. 中世纪大厅的天花板是美丽的拱形。

近义词：arched, vaulted | 反义词：flat, plain | 词性：形容词

词源:
From Old French *groigne, from Old French groigne "snout, nose," from Vulgar Latin *grugnum, from Latin grunire "to grunt."  The architectural sense developed from the resemblance of the intersecting ribs of a groined vault to a pig's snout.
