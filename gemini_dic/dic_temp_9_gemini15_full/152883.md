pedantize:/ˈpɛd(ə)ntaɪz/| v.|卖弄学问；炫耀学识|To display learning or scholarship ostentatiously; to act in a pedantic manner.|例句：He pedantized about the correct pronunciation of the word. 他卖弄学识，纠正了这个词的正确发音。 He likes to pedantize during discussions. 他喜欢在讨论中卖弄学问。|近义词：show off, pontificate,  ostentate|反义词：be humble, be modest|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pedantizes about grammar rules. = 他卖弄语法规则的学问。
She pedantizes in every conversation. = 她每次谈话都卖弄学问。

一般过去时:
He pedantized about the historical context. = 他卖弄了历史背景的知识。
She pedantized during the lecture. = 她在讲座上卖弄学问。

现在完成时:
He has pedantized throughout his career. = 他在整个职业生涯中都一直卖弄学问。
She has pedantized on numerous occasions. = 她多次卖弄学问。

过去完成时:
He had pedantized for years before anyone noticed. = 多年以来，他一直卖弄学问，直到有人注意到。
She had pedantized so much that people avoided her. = 她卖弄学问太多，以至于人们都躲着她。

将来时:
He will pedantize at the next meeting. = 他将在下次会议上卖弄学问。
She will pedantize again if given the chance. = 如果有机会，她还会再次卖弄学问。

虚拟语气:
If he pedantized less, people would like him more. = 如果他不那么卖弄学问，人们会更喜欢他。
I wish she wouldn't pedantize so much. = 我希望她不要再那么卖弄学问了。

被动语态:
His knowledge was pedantized by him excessively. = 他的知识被他过度地卖弄了。
The subject was pedantized to death. = 这个话题被过度地卖弄学问而弄得枯燥乏味。

疑问句:
Does he often pedantize? = 他经常卖弄学问吗？
Why does she pedantize so much? = 她为什么那么卖弄学问？

否定句:
He doesn't pedantize unnecessarily. = 他不会不必要地卖弄学问。
She didn't pedantize during the presentation. = 她在演示过程中没有卖弄学问。


PHRASE->
pedantize about (something) = 卖弄...方面的学问
pedantize to (someone) = 向...卖弄学问
pedantize on (a topic) = 在...话题上卖弄学问
pedantize in (a manner) = 以...的方式卖弄学问
to pedantize unnecessarily = 不必要地卖弄学问


词源:
源自pedant (卖弄学问的人) + -ize (动词后缀)。Pedant本身源于意大利语pedante，最终来自希腊语paideutes (教师,训导者)。
