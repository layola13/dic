reconnaissances:/rɪˈkɒnɪs(ə)nsz/| n. |侦察；勘察；探测|The act of obtaining information about an enemy or area by observation or other means.  It involves sending out troops or agents to gather intelligence. |例句：The army conducted several reconnaissances before launching the attack.  军队在发动进攻之前进行了多次侦察。  |例句： Aerial reconnaissance revealed the enemy's troop movements.  空中侦察揭示了敌军的调动情况。|近义词：scouting, exploration, survey, investigation|反义词：retreat, withdrawal|名词
