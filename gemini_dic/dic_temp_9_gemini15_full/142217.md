ornithology:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɒlədʒi/ | n. |鸟类学 |The scientific study of birds, including their classification, anatomy, physiology, behavior, and distribution.  | Ornithology is a fascinating field that combines biology, ecology, and conservation. 鸟类学是一个结合生物学、生态学和保护的迷人领域。 | zoology, avian biology |  (无明确反义词)

Ornithologists study bird migration patterns. 鸟类学家研究鸟类的迁徙模式。
She observes birds in their natural habitat. 她观察鸟类在其自然栖息地。


一般过去时:
