cooperate:/koʊˈɒpəreɪt/| /koʊˈɑːpəreɪt/ | v. |合作；协同工作 |to work together with someone else to achieve something |The two companies cooperated on the project. 这两家公司在项目上进行了合作。|collaborate, work together |compete, oppose |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
We cooperate on many projects. = 我们在许多项目上合作。
They cooperate to achieve their goals. = 他们合作以实现目标。


一般过去时:
The team cooperated effectively last year. = 去年团队合作得很有效率。
We cooperated on the presentation. = 我们一起完成了演示文稿。


现在完成时:
We have cooperated with them for many years. = 我们与他们合作多年了。
They have cooperated successfully on several occasions. = 他们已经成功合作过几次了。


过去完成时:
They had cooperated on several projects before this one. = 在这个项目之前，他们已经合作过几个项目了。
We had cooperated fully with the investigation. = 我们已经充分配合了调查。


将来时:
We will cooperate fully with the authorities. = 我们将全力配合当局。
They will cooperate to find a solution. = 他们将合作寻找解决方案。


虚拟语气:
If they cooperated, the project would succeed. = 如果他们合作，项目就会成功。
I wish they would cooperate more effectively. = 我希望他们能更有效地合作。


被动语态:
The project was cooperated on by several teams. = 这个项目由几个团队合作完成。
The investigation was cooperated with fully by the suspect. = 嫌疑人充分配合了调查。


疑问句:
Will they cooperate with us? = 他们会与我们合作吗？
Do you cooperate well with your colleagues? = 你与同事合作得好吗？


否定句:
They refused to cooperate with the police. = 他们拒绝与警方合作。
He did not cooperate during the interrogation. = 他在审讯期间没有合作。


PHRASE->
