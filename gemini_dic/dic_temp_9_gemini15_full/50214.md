departs:/dɪˈpɑːrts/| v.|离开，出发，去世|To leave; to go away from a place; to die.  |例句：The train departs at 8:00 AM.  火车早上8点出发。

The old man departed peacefully in his sleep. 老人安详地睡梦中去世了。|近义词：leaves, goes, sets off, passes away|反义词：arrives, returns


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The bus departs every hour. = 公车每小时发车一次。
He departs for London tomorrow. = 他明天动身去伦敦。

一般过去时:
The plane departed on time. = 飞机准时起飞了。
She departed from her hometown years ago. = 她几年前离开了家乡。

现在完成时:
He has departed for a long journey. = 他已经出发去远行了。
The train has departed already. = 火车已经开走了。

过去完成时:
The ship had departed before we arrived at the port. = 我们到达港口前，船已经开走了。
They had departed for the mountains before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临前，他们已经出发去山里了。

将来时:
The flight will depart at 6 pm. = 航班将于下午6点起飞。
We will depart early in the morning. = 我们将在清晨出发。

虚拟语气:
If he departed now, he would miss the meeting. = 如果他现在离开，他会错过会议。
I wish he had departed earlier. = 我希望他早点离开。

被动语态:
The meeting was departed from by many attendees. = 许多与会者离开了会议。(被动语态在此处用法略显生硬，一般不用被动)
The decision was departed from in the end. = 最终没有采纳该决定。(depart from 表示偏离，在此处用被动)

疑问句:
When does the train depart? = 火车几点开？
Does the flight depart on time? = 航班准时起飞吗？

否定句:
The train does not depart until tomorrow. = 火车要到明天才开。
He did not depart for the city despite the invitation. = 尽管受到邀请，他也没有去那座城市。


PHRASE->
depart from: 偏离，违反
depart for: 动身前往
depart this life: 去世
depart this world: 去世
sudden depart: 骤然离世
