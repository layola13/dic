freestyle:/ˈfriːstaɪl/| n. adj. v.|自由泳；自由式；随意风格；自由发挥|A style of swimming or other activity where the participant is not restricted by prescribed movements or forms.  It can also refer to a musical style or an improvisational approach to performance.|例句：He won the freestyle swimming competition. 他赢得了自由泳比赛。

例句：She has a freestyle approach to painting. 她对绘画采取自由发挥的方式。


近义词：improvisation, ad-libbing, improvisation, freeform, informal


反义词：formal, structured, prescribed


词性: noun, adjective, verb
