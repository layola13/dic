glop's:/ɡlɑːps/ | n. | 胶状物；糊状物 | A sticky, shapeless mass; something thick and viscous.  |例句：The spilled paint was a glop of vibrant colors.  溢出的颜料是一团鲜艳的胶状物。|近义词：goo, slime, sludge, slop | 反义词：crisp, dry, solid
