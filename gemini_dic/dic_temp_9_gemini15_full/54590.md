dismantled:/dɪsˈmæntld/ | vt. |拆除；解散；使瓦解 | to take something apart; to disassemble; to break up or destroy the structure or organization of something. |例句：The old factory was dismantled and the land was sold.  旧工厂被拆除了，土地被卖掉了。  The team was dismantled after their poor performance.  这支球队由于表现不佳而被解散了。 |近义词：demolish, disassemble, break down, destroy |反义词：assemble, construct, build, erect |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They dismantle old buildings every week. = 他们每周都拆除旧建筑物。
The company dismantles its products at the end of their life cycle. = 公司在其产品生命周期结束时将其产品拆解。

一般过去时:
The workers dismantled the machine carefully. = 工人们仔细地拆卸了机器。
The government dismantled the outdated system. = 政府拆除了过时的系统。

现在完成时:
They have dismantled the old bridge. = 他们已经拆除了旧桥。
The protesters have dismantled the barriers. = 抗议者已经拆除了障碍物。

过去完成时:
They had dismantled the equipment before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经拆除了设备。
The army had dismantled the camp before dawn. = 军队在黎明前已经拆除了营地。

将来时:
They will dismantle the statue next month. = 他们下个月将拆除这座雕像。
The company will dismantle the old factory next year. = 公司明年将拆除旧工厂。

虚拟语气:
If they dismantled the building, they would have to find a new location. = 如果他们拆除了这座建筑物，他们将不得不寻找一个新的地点。
I wish they had dismantled the old car sooner. = 我希望他们早点拆除那辆旧车。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
They did not dismantle the machine. = 他们没有拆卸机器。
