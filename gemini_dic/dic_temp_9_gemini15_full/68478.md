familist:/ˈfæməlɪst/| n. |家庭至上主义者；重视家庭的人|A person who prioritizes family above other social or political considerations.  A familist might choose family obligations over career advancement or social activism.|例句：He's a familist; he always puts his family first, even if it means sacrificing his own ambitions. 他是个家庭至上主义者；他总是把家人放在第一位，即使这意味着牺牲自己的抱负。|近义词：family-oriented person, traditionalist (in the context of family values)|反义词：individualist, careerist|名词
