cert:/sɜːt/| n.|证书，证明；保证；确实；确定| A cert is an official document that shows that something is true or that someone has a particular skill or qualification.  It can also mean a guarantee or assurance. |例句：He showed me his driving cert. 他给我看了他的驾驶证。
She received a certificate of merit. 她获得了一张优秀证书。|近义词：certificate, diploma, license, guarantee, assurance|反义词：disproof, refutation


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The course provides a certificate. = 这门课程提供证书。
This document certifies the authenticity. = 此文件证明其真实性。


一般过去时:
The teacher certified his passing grade. = 老师证明了他的及格成绩。
They certified the accuracy of the report. = 他们证明了报告的准确性。


现在完成时:
He has certified the documents. = 他已经证明了这些文件。
She has received her certification. = 她已经收到了她的证书。


过去完成时:
They had certified the results before the announcement. = 他们在宣布结果之前已经认证了结果。
He had already certified the check. = 他已经认证了支票。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
If they certified the findings, we could proceed. = 如果他们证明了这些发现，我们就可以继续进行。
