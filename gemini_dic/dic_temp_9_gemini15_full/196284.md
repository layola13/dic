serenitude:/səˈrenɪti/ | n. |宁静；平静；安详 | A state of calmness, peacefulness, and tranquility; the absence of disturbance.  It often implies a feeling of deep peace and composure. |例句：The serene beauty of the mountain lake filled me with serenity.  山间湖泊的宁静之美使我感到宁静祥和。

例句：After a long and stressful day, she found serenity in meditation. 漫长而压力重重的一天之后，她在冥想中找到了平静。

近义词：calmness, peace, tranquility, quietude, composure

反义词：turbulence, chaos, turmoil, agitation, anxiety
