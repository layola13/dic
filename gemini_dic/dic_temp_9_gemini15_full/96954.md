infallible: /ɪnˈfæləbl/ | adj. | 无谬的；不会出错的；绝对可靠的 |  Free from error or fault; incapable of failing.  |例句：The system is not infallible, and errors can occur.  这个系统并非完美无缺，可能会出现错误。

例句：His judgment, while usually sound, is not infallible. 他的判断力虽然通常很可靠，但并非绝对不会出错。


近义词：unerring, faultless, perfect, impeccable | 反义词：fallible, erroneous, flawed, imperfect


词性：形容词

词源:
Infallible comes from the Old French word *infallible*, which itself derives from the Latin word *infallibilis*.  *In-* is a negative prefix meaning "not," *fallere* means "to deceive" or "to fail," and *-bilis* is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of." Therefore, *infallibilis* literally means "not capable of failing."
