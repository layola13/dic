on-guard:/ɒn ɡɑːd/| adj.|警惕的，戒备的|Being watchful and prepared for danger or trouble.|例句：The soldiers were on guard all night.  士兵们整夜都在警戒。|例句：She kept a watchful eye, always on guard against pickpockets. 她一直警惕地注视着周围，提防扒手。|近义词：alert, vigilant, watchful|反义词：negligent, careless, unaware


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The soldiers are on guard. = 士兵们正在警戒。
He is always on guard against his enemies. = 他总是提防着他的敌人。

一般过去时:
The guards were on guard last night. = 警卫们昨晚值勤。
She was on guard against his advances. = 她提防着他的示好。

现在完成时:
The police have been on guard since the incident. = 自从那件事发生以来，警察就一直处于戒备状态。
They have been on guard for hours. = 他们已经警戒了好几个小时。

过去完成时:
The soldiers had been on guard for days before the attack. = 在袭击发生之前，士兵们已经警戒了几天。
She had been on guard, sensing danger. = 她一直很警惕，感觉到了危险。

将来时:
The troops will be on guard throughout the night. = 部队将在整个晚上保持警戒。
We will be on guard against any attempts at deception. = 我们将警惕任何欺骗行为。

虚拟语气:
If I were on guard, I would have noticed the intruder. = 如果我当时警惕的话，就会注意到入侵者。
It would be wise to be on guard against such scams. = 警惕此类骗局是明智之举。

被动语态:
Guards were posted on guard. = 警卫被安排站岗。
The area is being kept under guard. = 该地区正在受到保护。


疑问句:
Are the guards on guard? = 警卫们在警戒吗？
Is he on guard against the threat? = 他在提防那个威胁吗？

否定句:
The soldiers were not on guard. = 士兵们没有警戒。
He was not on guard and was easily tricked. = 他没有警惕，很容易就被骗了。


PHRASE->
on guard against = 提防，警惕
keep on guard = 保持警惕
off guard = 不设防，猝不及防
go on guard = 开始警戒
stand on guard = 站岗
