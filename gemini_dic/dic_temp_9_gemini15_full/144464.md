outtalks:/ˌaʊtˈtɔːks/| vt.|比…更能说；说得比…多|To speak more than someone else; to surpass someone in a conversation or debate. |例句：He completely outtalks everyone else in the meeting. 他在会议上完全把其他人比下去了。|例句：She outtalks her brother by a mile. 她说话比她弟弟多得多。|近义词：outspeaks, surpasses, overshadows|反义词：is outtalked by, is silenced by


USAGE->

一般现在时:
He outtalks his colleagues in every meeting. = 他在每次会议上都比他的同事说得更多。
She outtalks her opponent during the debate. = 她在辩论中说得比对手多。


一般过去时:
He outtalked his opponent in the last debate. = 他在上次辩论中说得比对手多。
She outtalked everyone at the party. = 她在派对上比任何人都说得多。


现在完成时:
He has outtalked me on every occasion. = 他每次都说得比我多。
She has outtalked every other candidate. = 她比其他所有候选人都说得更多。


过去完成时:
He had outtalked her before the meeting even started. = 会议还没开始他就已经把她比下去了。
She had outtalked him on numerous occasions. = 她之前已经多次说得比他多。


将来时:
He will outtalk his opponent in the upcoming debate. = 他将在即将到来的辩论中说得比对手多。
She will outtalk him easily. = 她很容易就能说得比他多。


虚拟语气:
If he outtalked his boss, he might get fired. = 如果他比老板说得更多，他可能会被解雇。
If she had outtalked him, she would have won the argument. = 如果她比他说的更多，她就会赢得这场争论。


被动语态:
He was outtalked by his more experienced colleague. = 他被更有经验的同事说得哑口无言。
She was outtalked by the passionate speaker. = 她被热情洋溢的演讲者说得哑口无言。


疑问句:
Does he always outtalk everyone else? = 他总是比其他人说得更多吗？
Does she often outtalk her friends? = 她经常比她的朋友说得更多吗？


否定句:
He didn't outtalk her; she had the last word. = 他没有比她说得多；她说了最后一句话。
She didn't outtalk him this time; he was very persuasive. = 这次她没有比他说的多；他非常有说服力。



PHRASE->

outtalk someone into something  = 说服某人做某事
outtalk someone out of something = 说服某人不做某事
be outtalked by someone = 被某人说得哑口无言
outtalk the competition = 在竞争中以口才取胜
outtalk oneself =  说得太多，说错了话
