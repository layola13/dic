profeminist: /ˈproʊfɛmənɪst/ | adj. | 支持女权主义的；拥护女权的 |  A profeminist is a man or person who supports feminism and the rights and liberation of women, often without identifying as a feminist themselves.  They advocate for gender equality and challenge patriarchal systems. | Example: He's a profeminist who actively works to promote equal pay for women. 他是一位积极致力于为女性争取同工同酬的拥护女权者。  |近义词: feminist ally, supporter of women's rights | 反义词: misogynist, antifeminist |


词源:  The word "profeminist" is a combination of "pro-" (meaning "in favor of" or "supporting") and "feminist."  It emerged as a term to describe individuals who support feminist goals but may not fully identify with the feminist label or movement for various reasons, such as feeling the term doesn't encompass their specific views or experiences.
