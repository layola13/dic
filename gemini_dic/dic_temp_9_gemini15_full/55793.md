diverticulitis:/ˌdaɪvɜːrtɪkjuˈlaɪtɪs/| n. |憩室炎|Inflammation of a diverticulum, especially in the colon.  Diverticula are small pouches or sacs that can form in the lining of the digestive tract.  Diverticulitis occurs when one or more of these pouches become infected or inflamed. Symptoms can include abdominal pain, fever, nausea, and changes in bowel habits.|Example:  He experienced severe abdominal pain, diagnosed as diverticulitis.|近义词：diverticular disease|反义词：无
