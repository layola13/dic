cont:/kɒnt/| /kɑːnt/| v.|继续，延续|To continue; to carry on.|The meeting will cont. tomorrow.  会议将明天继续。|He cont. to work despite the difficulties. 他尽管遇到困难，仍然继续工作。|continue, proceed|stop, cease|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He continues to learn new things. 他继续学习新事物。
They continue their research. 他们继续他们的研究。


一般过去时:
She continued to play the piano. 她继续弹钢琴。
The rain continued throughout the night. 雨整夜不停地下着。


现在完成时:
He has continued to support us. 他一直支持我们。
They have continued their efforts. 他们一直努力。


过去完成时:
She had continued working even after she was tired. 她即使累了也继续工作。
They had continued their journey despite the storm. 他们尽管暴风雨依然继续他们的旅程。


将来时:
We will continue our journey tomorrow. 我们明天将继续我们的旅程。
They will continue to help others. 他们将继续帮助他人。


虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would continue my studies. 如果我有更多时间，我会继续我的学习。
I wish I could continue my trip. 我希望我能继续我的旅行。


被动语态:
The project was continued after a brief delay. 项目在短暂延迟后继续进行。
The meeting will be continued tomorrow morning. 会议将在明天上午继续。


疑问句:
Will you continue the project? 你会继续这个项目吗？
Did they continue their experiment? 他们继续他们的实验了吗？


否定句:
He did not continue the conversation. 他没有继续谈话。
They will not continue the work. 他们不会继续工作。



PHRASE->
cont. to do sth.:继续做某事
cont. with sth.:继续某事
cont. the discussion:继续讨论
cont. fighting:继续战斗
