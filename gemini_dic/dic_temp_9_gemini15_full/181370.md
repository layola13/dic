reinvigorates:/ˌriːɪnˈvɪɡəreɪts/| vt.|使恢复活力；使重新充满活力|To give new energy or strength to something or someone.|The vacation reinvigorated her spirit.  这次假期使她精神焕发。|The new manager reinvigorated the sales team. 新经理使销售团队重获活力。|energize, revitalize, refresh|debilitate, exhaust, weaken|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The fresh air reinvigorates me. = 新鲜的空气使我精神焕发。
Regular exercise reinvigorates the body. = 定期锻炼使身体恢复活力。

一般过去时:
