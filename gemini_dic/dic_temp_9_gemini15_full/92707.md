/ˈaɪslændz/ | n. |冰岛（复数）|The plural form of "Iceland," referring to multiple instances or aspects related to the country of Iceland.  It can refer to multiple islands geographically considered part of Iceland or multiple aspects of Icelandic culture, history, or geography.| Examples: The volcanic islands of Iceland are stunning.  (冰岛的火山岛屿令人惊叹。) The unique aspects of Iceland's history and culture are fascinating. (冰岛独特历史和文化方面令人着迷。) |近义词: Icelandic territories (冰岛领土) (when referring to geographical aspects)|反义词:  None |名词
