gulleting:/ˈɡʌlətɪŋ/| v.|吞咽；狼吞虎咽|The act of swallowing or gulping down food or drink quickly and greedily.  It often implies a lack of refinement or elegance in eating.|例句：He was gulleting down his food like a starved animal. 他像饿狼一样狼吞虎咽地吃着食物。|例句：The child was gulleting the ice cream, barely able to control their excitement.  孩子兴奋地狼吞虎咽着冰淇淋。|近义词：gorging, gobbling, devouring|反义词：sipping, nibbling
