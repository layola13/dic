icon:/ˈaɪkɒn/ /ˈaɪkɑːn/| n. |图标，偶像，标志 |A small picture or symbol on a computer screen that represents a program, file, or function.  An icon is also a person or thing that is a representative symbol of a particular idea, quality, or event. |例句：The recycle bin icon is usually a green arrow. 回收站图标通常是一个绿色箭头。

The singer became an icon for a generation.  这位歌手成为一代人的偶像。


近义词：symbol, emblem, image, idol |反义词：none
