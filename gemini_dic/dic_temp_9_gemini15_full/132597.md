nondilatability: /ˌnɒnˌdaɪləˈteɪbɪləti/ | n. |不可扩张性，不能扩张|The inability to dilate or expand.  This term is often used in medical contexts, referring to the inability of a blood vessel, pupil, or other structure to widen. |Example: The patient presented with nondilatability of the pupils, a symptom indicative of a serious neurological condition.  患者表现出瞳孔不能扩张，这是严重神经疾病的症状。|近义词：inexpansibility,  inelasticity |反义词：dilatability, expansibility
