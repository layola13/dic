profaners:/prəˈfeɪnərz/| n. |亵渎者，污蔑者|Those who profane something sacred;  people who use profane language or commit sacrilegious acts.  They show contempt or disrespect for something considered holy or sacred. |例句：The profaner defaced the ancient temple with graffiti.  亵渎者在古老的寺庙上涂鸦。

例句：The profaner's words were deeply offensive to religious believers. 亵渎者的言辞深深冒犯了信教者。


近义词：blasphemers, desecrators, defilers|反义词：reverers, worshippers, sanctifiers|名词
