quintain:/ˈkwɪnteɪn/| n. |五音步诗;五角靶;五角形目标|A quintain is a five-line stanza of verse, or a target consisting of a quintain (five-sided figure) that was used in a medieval game.  |例句：The poem was written in a quintain, with a consistent rhyme scheme. 这首诗是用五音步诗写的，押韵方案一致。

The knight tilted at the quintain with great skill and bravery.  骑士以高超的技巧和勇气向五角靶冲刺。|近义词：stanza (for the verse meaning), target |反义词：None specifically applicable.
