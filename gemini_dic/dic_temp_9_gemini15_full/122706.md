moguls:/ˈmoʊɡl̩z/| n.|大亨，巨头|Powerful and influential people, especially in business or a particular industry.  They are often wealthy and successful.|例句：The film industry is dominated by a few powerful moguls. 电影业由少数几个有权势的大亨所掌控。|例句：Media moguls often wield significant political influence. 媒体大亨通常拥有很大的政治影响力。|近义词：tycoons, magnates, leaders, giants|反义词：underlings, novices, beginners
