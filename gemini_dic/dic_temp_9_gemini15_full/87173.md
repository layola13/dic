herd:/hɜːrd/|n.|兽群；人群；牧群|A herd is a large group of animals of the same type that live and feed together.  It can also refer to a large group of people.|例句：A herd of elephants crossed the river.  一群大象过河了。
例句：A large herd of cattle grazed peacefully in the field.  一大群牛平静地吃草。|近义词：flock, drove, crowd|反义词：individual, single


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The shepherd herds his sheep every day. 牧羊人每天放羊。
The cattle herd peacefully in the field. 牛群在田野里平静地吃草。

一般过去时:
The farmer herded his cows to the barn. 农民把他的牛赶进牛棚。
The sheep herded together for protection. 羊群聚集在一起寻求保护。


现在完成时:
The rancher has herded thousands of cattle in his lifetime.  这位牧场主一生中已放牧了数千头牛。
The shepherd has herded his flock across the mountain. 牧羊人已将他的羊群赶过了山。


过去完成时:
The cowboys had herded the cattle before the storm hit. 牛仔们在暴风雨来临之前已经把牛赶好了。
The shepherd had herded the sheep to safety before the wolf appeared. 牧羊人在狼出现之前已将羊群赶到了安全的地方。


将来时:
The farmer will herd his sheep to the pasture tomorrow. 农民明天将把他的羊赶到牧场。
The cowboys will herd the cattle across the plains. 牛仔们将把牛群赶过平原。


虚拟语气:
If the shepherd herded the sheep more carefully, they wouldn't have been scattered. 如果牧羊人更仔细地放羊，它们就不会散开了。
If I had herded the cattle properly, they wouldn't have wandered off. 如果我当初把牛群赶好，它们就不会走丢了。


被动语态:
The sheep were herded into the pen. 羊群被赶进了羊圈。
The cattle are being herded across the river. 牛群正在被赶过河。


疑问句:
Are you herding the sheep today? 你今天放羊吗？
Who is herding the cattle? 谁在放牛？


否定句:
The farmer didn't herd the cows this morning. 农民今天早上没有放牛。
The shepherd couldn't herd the sheep because of the storm. 由于暴风雨，牧羊人无法放羊。
