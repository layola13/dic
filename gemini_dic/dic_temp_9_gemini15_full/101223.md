invitation:/ˌɪnvɪˈteɪʃən/| n. |邀请；请柬 |An invitation is a request to someone to go somewhere or to do something.  It can be a formal written request or an informal verbal one. |例句：I received an invitation to the wedding. 我收到了一份婚礼邀请函。
She extended an invitation to dinner. 她发出晚餐邀请。|近义词：request, summons |反义词：refusal
