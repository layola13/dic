drinkless:/ˈdrɪŋkləs/| adj.|不喝酒的；禁酒的|Not drinking alcohol; abstaining from alcohol.|例句：He leads a drinkless life. 他过着不喝酒的生活。|近义词：teetotal, abstinent, sober|反义词：alcoholic, drunken
