pottily:/ˈpɒtɪli/ | adv. | 琐碎地；微不足道地；少量地 |  The word "pottily" is a less common adverb that implies doing something in a small, insignificant, or trifling way. It suggests a lack of importance or impact.  The word carries a slightly negative connotation, implying that the action was done poorly or ineffectually.  |  He pottily attempted to fix the leak, using only a small piece of tape. 他用一小块胶带敷衍地尝试修补漏洞。  | 近义词：trivially, insignificantly, minimally | 反义词：significantly, importantly, substantially
