duplexed:/ˈdjuːplɛkst/| adj. | 双工的，双向的 | Having two channels or paths for transmitting information simultaneously in opposite directions.  This term is commonly used in telecommunications and networking to describe a communication system that can send and receive data at the same time. | Example: The duplexed communication system allowed for immediate responses to queries.  双工通信系统允许立即响应查询。  The new fiber optic cable supports duplexed transmission, increasing network speed. 新的光纤电缆支持双工传输，提高了网络速度。|近义词：two-way, bidirectional |反义词：simplex, unidirectional
