nonprofessorial: /ˌnɒnˌprɒfɛˈsɔːriəl/ | adj. | 非教授的；非学术的 | Not relating to or characteristic of a professor; not academic.  This adjective describes something or someone that lacks the qualities typically associated with professors, such as scholarly expertise, intellectual rigor, or a formal academic setting. |


例句：

The atmosphere of the meeting was distinctly nonprofessorial, filled with laughter and informal discussion.  会议气氛明显非学术性，充满了欢笑和非正式的讨论。

His approach to the problem was refreshingly nonprofessorial, focusing on practical solutions rather than theoretical debates. 他解决问题的方法令人耳目一新，是非学术性的，侧重于实际解决方案而非理论辩论。


近义词：unacademic, unprofessional, non-scholarly, lay, practical

反义词：professorial, academic, scholarly, intellectual


词性:形容词
