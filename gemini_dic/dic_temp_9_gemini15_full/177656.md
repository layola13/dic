recabling:/ˌriːˈkeɪblɪŋ/| n.|重新布线，重新接线|The act of replacing or reinstalling cables.  It often involves replacing old, damaged, or outdated cables with new ones to improve performance, reliability, or to accommodate new equipment.|例句：The recabling of the entire building took several weeks.  整个建筑物的重新布线花了几个星期。

例句：The audio system needed a complete recabling to eliminate the static. 音响系统需要完全重新布线以消除静电。
