cobwall: /ˈkɒb wɔːl/ | n. | 夯土墙 | A cob wall is a wall constructed from a mixture of clay, straw, and other organic materials, such as dung, and shaped by hand.  It is a traditional building technique used for centuries in various parts of the world.  Cob walls are known for their thermal mass, providing insulation and stability. | The old farmhouse had charming cob walls, covered in ivy.  古老的农舍有迷人的夯土墙，上面覆盖着常春藤。| wattle and daub, mud wall | brick wall, stone wall |  名词
