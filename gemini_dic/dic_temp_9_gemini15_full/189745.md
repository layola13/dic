salting:/ˈsɔːltɪŋ/ | v. | 腌制；加盐；贿赂 | The act of adding salt to something, typically food, to preserve it, enhance its flavor, or for other purposes.  It can also refer to the act of bribing someone. | 例句：She's salting the beef for tomorrow's dinner. = 她正在腌制牛肉准备明天的晚餐。

The chef salted the soup to taste. = 厨师加盐调味汤。

近义词：seasoning, preserving,  (in bribery context) bribing, greasing

反义词：desalting (removing salt)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They salt the meat every year. = 他们每年都腌肉。
She salts her food before tasting it. = 她吃东西之前都会先加盐。

一般过去时:
He salted the rim of the margarita glass. = 他在玛格丽塔酒杯边沿撒了盐。
We salted the vegetables before roasting them. = 我们在烤蔬菜之前先加了盐。

现在完成时:
They have salted the fish for several days. = 他们已经腌鱼好几天了。
She has salted the pasta water. = 她已经往意面水中加盐了。

过去完成时:
They had salted the pork before they smoked it. = 他们在熏肉之前已经腌制过了。
She had salted the bread before baking it. = 她烘烤面包之前已经加盐了。


将来时:
We will salt the eggplant before grilling it. = 我们将在烤茄子之前给它加盐。
They will salt the fish tomorrow morning. = 他们明天早上将腌制鱼。


虚拟语气:
If I salted the potatoes more, they would be tastier. = 如果我多加些盐在土豆上，它们会更美味。
I wish I had salted the chicken before cooking it. = 我真希望我在煮鸡肉之前先加过盐。


被动语态:
The fish is salted to preserve it. = 鱼被腌制是为了保存。
The meat is being salted in the cellar. = 肉正在地窖里腌制。


疑问句:
Do you salt your popcorn? = 你会在爆米花里加盐吗？
Have you ever salted your vegetables while cooking them? = 你在做菜的时候有没有往蔬菜里加过盐？


否定句:
I didn't salt the soup, it was already salty enough. = 我没有往汤里加盐，它已经够咸了。
They didn't salt the fries before serving them. = 他们上薯条之前没有加盐。


PHRASE->
salt away:  储蓄；保存
salt of the earth: 正直的人
salt the earth: 毁掉土地（使其无法耕种）
worth one's salt: 称职的；有价值的
take with a grain of salt:  将信将疑


词源:
The word "salting" derives from the Old English word "sealt," meaning salt.  The "-ing" suffix indicates the act of doing something.  The meaning and usage have evolved over time to include both culinary and figurative applications.
