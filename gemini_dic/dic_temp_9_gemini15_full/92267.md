hypervigilantly:/ˌhaɪpəˈvɪdʒɪləntli/| adv.|高度警惕地，过度警惕地|In a state of heightened alertness and excessive vigilance; often associated with anxiety or trauma.|例句：He scanned the room hypervigilantly, his eyes darting from person to person. 他高度警惕地扫视着房间，眼睛在人群中飞快地扫视着。  例句：She lived hypervigilantly after the attack, constantly checking locks and doors. 袭击之后，她生活得高度警惕，不断地检查门锁和门。|近义词：alertly, cautiously, watchfully|反义词：carelessly, nonchalantly, unconcernedly
