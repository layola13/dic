daubs:/dɔːbz/| n. vt.|涂抹；拙劣的绘画；少量|to cover (something) with a thick layer of paint or similar substance; a clumsy or careless painting; a small amount|例句：He daubed the wall with paint. 他用颜料涂抹墙壁。

The child's daub was surprisingly expressive.  孩子的涂鸦出人意料地富有表现力。

近义词：smear, smudge, slapdash, botch|反义词：refine, perfect, meticulous


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He daubs the canvas with vibrant colors. = 他用鲜艳的色彩涂抹画布。
She daubs butter on her toast. = 她往吐司上涂黄油。


一般过去时:
The artist daubed the landscape with bold strokes. = 艺术家用大胆的笔触涂抹风景。
He daubed the wound with antiseptic. = 他用消毒剂涂抹伤口。


现在完成时:
She has daubed the entire fence with paint. = 她已经用油漆涂抹了整个栅栏。
The children have daubed their artwork all over the kitchen table. = 孩子们把他们的绘画作品涂抹在整个厨房餐桌上。


过去完成时:
He had daubed the picture with several layers of paint. = 他已经在画上涂抹了好几层油漆。
They had daubed the graffiti over the mural. = 他们已经在壁画上涂抹了涂鸦。


将来时:

虚拟语气:

疑问句:
