chinwagging:/ˈtʃɪnwæɡɪŋ/| v.|闲聊，聊天 | Informal conversation; a chat.  |例句：We spent the afternoon chinwagging about old times. 我们一下午都在聊过去的事情。|近义词：chatting, gossiping, conversing |反义词：silence, quiet


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are chinwagging in the garden. = 他们在花园里聊天。
We chinwag every Friday afternoon. = 我们每个星期五下午都聊天。


一般过去时:
They chinwagged about their holidays. = 他们聊起了他们的假期。
We chinwagged for hours. = 我们聊了好几个小时。


现在完成时:
They have chinwagged about the project all morning. = 他们整个上午都在聊这个项目。
We have chinwagged with several potential clients. = 我们已经和几个潜在客户聊过了。


过去完成时:
They had chinwagged before the meeting started. = 他们在会议开始之前就聊过了。
We had chinwagged about the problem before seeking help. = 在寻求帮助之前，我们已经讨论过这个问题了。


将来时:
They will chinwag after dinner. = 他们晚饭后会聊天。
We will chinwag at the party. = 我们将在派对上聊天。


虚拟语气:
If they had more time, they would chinwag longer. = 如果他们有更多时间，他们会聊更久。
I wish we could chinwag more often. = 我希望我们能更经常地聊天。


被动语态:  (Chinwagging is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Are they chinwagging again? = 他们又在聊天了吗？
Were they chinwagging when you arrived? = 你到的时候他们在聊天吗？


否定句:
They didn't chinwag much at the conference. = 他们在会议上聊得不多。
We didn't chinwag about work. = 我们没有聊工作。


PHRASE->
chinwag about = 闲聊…
have a chinwag = 闲聊
chinwag with = 和…闲聊
a good chinwag = 一次愉快的聊天
a long chinwag = 一次长时间的聊天
