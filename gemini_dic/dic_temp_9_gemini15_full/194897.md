semi-finalist:/ˌsɛmiˈfaɪnəlɪst/| n. |半决赛选手 |A semi-finalist is a contestant who has reached the semi-final round of a competition.  They are one step away from the final round. |Example: She was a semi-finalist in the national spelling bee. 她 是全国拼字比赛的半决赛选手。  He is a semi-finalist for the prestigious award. 他是该著名奖项的半决赛选手。|近义词：competitor, contestant (in the semi-final round)|反义词：finalist, winner

They are semi-finalists in the debate tournament. 他们是辩论赛的半决赛选手。
