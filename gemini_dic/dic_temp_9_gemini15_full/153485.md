penology:/pɪˈnɒlədʒi/|/pɪˈnɑːlədʒi/| n. |刑罚学；监狱学|The study of punishment of crime and prison management.  It encompasses various aspects, from the philosophical justifications for punishment to the practical implementation of correctional systems. |例句：Penology is a complex field that considers the effectiveness and ethics of different punishment methods. 刑罚学是一个复杂的领域，它考虑不同惩罚方法的有效性和伦理问题。

例句：The professor’s research focuses on the application of penology in reducing recidivism. 教授的研究重点是将刑罚学应用于降低再犯率。


近义词：correctional science, prison science

反义词：无
