philistine:/ˈfɪləstiːn/| n. adj.|非文化人；粗俗的人；缺乏教养的人；腓力斯人 |A philistine is a person who is hostile or indifferent to culture and the arts, or who is considered unrefined or commonplace.  The term originates from the ancient Philistines, a people mentioned in the Bible who were often depicted as enemies of the Israelites and associated with lack of culture and refinement.  | He dismissed the art exhibition as "a load of philistine rubbish." 他认为艺术展览是一堆粗俗的垃圾。  |  barbarian, boor, vulgarian | cultured, refined, sophisticated | 名词，形容词
