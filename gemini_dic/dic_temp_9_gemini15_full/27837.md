canoness:/ˈkænənɛs/| n.|女教士，修道院女修女|A woman member of a religious order, especially one living in a community under a rule.  Canonesses often held a higher social status than nuns and sometimes engaged in scholarly pursuits or charitable work.|例句：She dedicated her life to the church as a canoness. 她作为女教士献身于教会。|例句：The canonesses lived a life of prayer and contemplation. 女教士们过着祈祷和沉思的生活。|近义词：nun, sister|反义词：laywoman
