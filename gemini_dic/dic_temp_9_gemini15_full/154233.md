periproctitis:/ˌpɛriprɒkˈtaɪtɪs/ | n. |直肠周围炎|Inflammation of the tissues surrounding the rectum.  The condition can be caused by a variety of factors, including infection (bacterial, viral, or fungal), injury, or diseases such as Crohn's disease. Symptoms may include pain, swelling, tenderness, and fever.
