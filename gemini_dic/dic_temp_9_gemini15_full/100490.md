intervened:/ˌɪntərˈviːnd/ | v. | 干预；介入；插手 | To become involved in a situation to try to stop or change it.  |例句：The police intervened to stop the fight. 警察介入制止了这场打斗。

例句：The United Nations intervened in the conflict to try to bring about peace.联合国介入冲突以试图促成和平。


近义词：interfere, mediate, arbitrate | 反义词：ignore, neglect, abstain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He intervenes in family disputes. = 他干预家庭纠纷。
She intervenes whenever she sees injustice. = 她一看到不公正的事情就介入。


一般过去时:
The government intervened to regulate the market. = 政府介入以规范市场。
He intervened on behalf of his friend. = 他为了朋友而介入。


现在完成时:
The army has intervened in the political crisis. = 军队已经介入政治危机。
She has intervened several times to help the poor. = 她已经多次介入以帮助穷人。


过去完成时:
The judge had intervened before the situation escalated. = 在情况升级之前，法官已经介入。
They had intervened numerous times before the final resolution. = 在最终解决之前，他们已经介入过很多次。


将来时:
The president will intervene if the negotiations fail. = 如果谈判失败，总统将介入。
We will intervene if necessary. = 必要时我们将介入。


虚拟语气:
If he had intervened earlier, the outcome might have been different. = 如果他早些介入，结果可能会有所不同。
I wish the police had intervened sooner. = 我希望警察早点介入。


被动语态:
The dispute was intervened by a neutral third party. = 纠纷由一个中立的第三方介入。
The situation was intervened in by the international community. = 国际社会介入了这种情况。


疑问句:
Did anyone intervene to help? = 有人介入帮忙了吗？
Will the government intervene in this matter? = 政府会介入此事吗？


否定句:
He didn't intervene in the argument. = 他没有介入争论。
They chose not to intervene in the conflict. = 他们选择不介入冲突。


PHRASE->
intervene in = 介入...
intervene to = 为了...而介入
intervene on behalf of = 代表...介入
military intervention = 军事干预
timely intervention = 及时干预
