naphtha:/ˈnæfθə/| n. | 汽油；石脑油 | A highly flammable, volatile, and often colorless liquid hydrocarbon mixture obtained by the distillation of petroleum or coal tar.  It's used as a solvent and fuel. | Example: The naphtha was used to clean the engine parts.  汽油被用来清洗发动机零件。|近义词：petroleum spirit, ligroin | 反义词：无
