flounces:/flaʊns/| n. v.|飘动；褶边；（轻率地）挥动； |A flounce is a sudden, dramatic movement, often expressing annoyance or impatience.  It can also refer to a ruffle or frill of fabric. |例句：She flounced out of the room in a huff. 她气冲冲地猛地离开了房间。 The dress had pretty flounces around the hem. 这件裙子的下摆有漂亮的褶边。|近义词：frill, ruffle, swish, prance|反义词：stillness, calmness|名词，动词
