cyanobacteria:/ˌsaɪˌænoʊbækˈtɪəriə/| n. |蓝细菌；蓝藻|Cyanobacteria are a phylum of bacteria that obtain their energy through photosynthesis. They are also known as blue-green algae, although they are not actually algae.|Example:  Cyanobacteria played a crucial role in the early Earth's atmosphere by producing oxygen. 蓝细菌通过产生氧气在早期地球的大气层中发挥了至关重要的作用。|近义词：blue-green algae|反义词：无
