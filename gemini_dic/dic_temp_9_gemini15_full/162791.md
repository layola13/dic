prebreathes:/ˌpriːˈbriːðz/| v.|预先呼吸；在呼吸前呼吸|To take a breath before speaking or singing, often to steady oneself or to prepare for a vocal performance.|例句：The singer prebreathed deeply before launching into the aria. 歌手在开始演唱咏叹调之前深吸了一口气。|例句：She prebreathed, gathering her courage before addressing the crowd.  她在向人群讲话之前先深吸了一口气，鼓起勇气。|近义词：inhale, breathe in|反义词：exhale, breathe out
