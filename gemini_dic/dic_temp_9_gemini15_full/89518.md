honking:/ˈhɑːŋkɪŋ/| n. adj. v.| 喇叭声；按喇叭；难听的；刺耳的 |The sound of a car horn;  the act of sounding a horn; unpleasant or jarring sound. |例句：The honking of cars filled the street.  汽车的喇叭声充满了街道。  The music was a bit honking. 这音乐有点刺耳。 The driver kept honking his horn. 司机不停地按喇叭。|近义词：tooting, blaring, screeching |反义词：quiet, silent


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The car honks loudly. = 汽车大声鸣笛。
He honks his horn at pedestrians. = 他朝行人按喇叭。


一般过去时:
The taxi honked its horn. = 出租车按响了喇叭。
She honked impatiently at the red light. = 她不耐烦地在红灯前按喇叭。


现在完成时:
The driver has honked several times. = 司机已经按了几次喇叭。
They have been honking their horns for hours. = 他们已经按喇叭好几个小时了。


过去完成时:
He had honked the horn before the accident. = 事故发生前他已经按过喇叭了。
The birds had been honking all night. = 鸟儿整夜都在鸣叫。


将来时:
I will honk my horn if necessary. = 必要时我会按喇叭。
He will honk the horn to warn the people. = 他会按喇叭警告人们。


虚拟语气:
If I honked my horn, would anyone hear me? = 如果我按喇叭，有人会听到吗？
I wish I hadn't honked my horn so loudly. = 我真希望我没有那么大声地按喇叭。


被动语态:
The horn was honked repeatedly. = 喇叭被反复按响。
The horn was being honked by the angry driver. = 喇叭正被愤怒的司机按响。


疑问句:
Why are you honking your horn? = 你为什么按喇叭？
Is anyone honking outside? = 外面有人按喇叭吗？


否定句:
He didn't honk his horn. = 他没有按喇叭。
I don't usually honk my horn at people. = 我通常不会朝人按喇叭。


PHRASE->
