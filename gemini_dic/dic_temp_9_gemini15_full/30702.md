certifier:/sɜːrtɪˈfaɪər/| n. |证明人；认证机构；认证者 |A person or organization that certifies something, confirming its authenticity, quality, or conformity to standards.  |例句：The certifier confirmed the authenticity of the diamond. 认证机构确认了钻石的真伪。|例句：The independent certifier gave the product a high rating.  独立的认证机构给予该产品很高的评价。|近义词：verifier, authenticator, validator |反义词：disqualifier


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The certifier checks the documents carefully. = 认证人员仔细检查文件。
He certifies the accuracy of the data. = 他证明数据的准确性。


一般过去时:
The certifier verified the signature on the document. = 认证人员核实了文件上的签名。
They certified the building as safe. = 他们证明该建筑物安全。


现在完成时:
The company has certified several products this year. = 该公司今年已认证了几种产品。
She has certified many documents as authentic. = 她已证明许多文件是真实的。


过去完成时:
The inspector had certified the machine before the accident. = 事故发生前，检查员已认证了机器。
They had certified the results before releasing the report. = 他们在发布报告之前已经认证了结果。


将来时:
The lab will certify the results next week. = 实验室下周将认证结果。
He will certify the accuracy of the information. = 他将证明信息的准确性。


虚拟语气:
If the certifier were here, he would confirm the findings. = 如果认证人员在这里，他会确认调查结果。
I wish the certifier had been more thorough. = 我希望认证人员更细致一些。


被动语态:
The documents were certified by a qualified expert. = 文件由合格专家认证。

疑问句:

否定句:
