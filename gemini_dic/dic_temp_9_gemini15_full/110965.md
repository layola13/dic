liquefying:/ˈlɪkwɪfaɪɪŋ/| v. |液化；使液化 |The process of turning something into a liquid.  It often refers to gases turning into liquids, but can also apply to solids. |例句：The gas is liquefying under high pressure.  气体在高压下正在液化。

例句：The company is working on a new process for liquefying natural gas. 公司正在研究一种新的天然气液化工艺。


近义词：liquefaction, melting (for solids)

反义词：solidifying, freezing

词源:
Comes from the verb "liquefy" + "-ing" (present participle).  "Liquefy" itself comes from the Latin "liquefacere,"  a combination of "liquefacere" (to make liquid) from "liquefactus" (made liquid) and "facere" (to make or do).
