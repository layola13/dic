prevenient:/prɪˈviːniənt/| adj.| 预先的；先行的；预防的 |  Coming before in time or order; preceding; anticipatory; preventative.  It often implies a preemptive action or influence. |例句：The prevenient grace of God prepares the soul for faith.  神的先恩预备灵魂接受信仰。

例句：His prevenient actions averted a potential disaster. 他先发制人的行动避免了一场潜在的灾难。


近义词：preceding, anticipatory, preemptive, preventative, prophylactic


反义词：subsequent, following, belated, reactive


词性：形容词
