recordation:/ˌrekərˈdeɪʃən/| n. |记录；录音；注册|The act of recording something, especially officially.  It refers to the process of making a permanent record of something, whether it's a legal document, a sound, or a video. |例句：The recordation of the deed ensured the transfer of property ownership.  记录契据确保了财产所有权的转移。

The official recordation of the birth certificate is crucial.  出生证明的正式登记至关重要。|近义词：recording, registration, documentation |反义词：destruction, erasure
