ironweed:/ˈaɪərnˌwid/| n. |铁线莲；紫菀属植物|A tall, coarse plant with purple flowers, often found in waste places.  The name refers to the plant's tough, wiry stems. |The ironweed grew tall and strong beside the abandoned farmhouse.|近义词：Vernonia,  (depending on specific species) thistle, weed|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with delicate flowers.
