parka:/ˈpɑːrkə/ /ˈpɑːrkɑː/| n. | 派克大衣，防寒外套 | A parka is a warm, hooded jacket, typically made of waterproof or water-resistant material, designed to protect the wearer from cold and wet weather.  It often has a fur-lined hood. | Example:  She wore a thick parka to protect herself from the blizzard. 她穿着一件厚厚的派克大衣来保护自己免受暴风雪的侵袭。

Example: I need a new parka for the winter. 我需要一件新的派克大衣过冬。

|近义词：anorak, down jacket, coat |反义词：None specifically. (Consider opposite of "warm" - light clothing)|名词|

词源:
The word "parka" originates from the Yupik word *parka*, meaning "clothing," specifically referring to a traditional Inuit hooded jacket.  The word entered English through contact with Inuit peoples in Alaska and Canada.
