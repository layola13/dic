complexes:/ˈkɒmplɛksɪz/| n.|复合体；情结；综合体 |A complex is a group of buildings or structures that are located close together and are used for a particular purpose.  It can also refer to a group of related ideas, feelings, or emotions that affect a person's behavior. |例句：The industrial complex sprawls across the valley.  这个工业综合体横跨山谷。|例句：He has a complex about his height. 他对自己的身高有自卑感。|近义词：compound, aggregate, system |反义词：simplicity, single, individual
