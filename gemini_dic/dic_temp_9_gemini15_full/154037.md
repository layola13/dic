perfumer:/pərˈfjuːmər/| n. |调香师，香水制造商 |A perfumer is a person who creates and develops fragrances for perfumes, colognes, and other scented products. They blend different aromatic ingredients to achieve a specific scent profile.  |The perfumer skillfully blended floral and woody notes to create a unique fragrance.  调香师巧妙地混合了花香和木香，创造出一种独特的香味。|fragrance creator, scent maker|
