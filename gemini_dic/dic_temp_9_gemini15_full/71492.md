flanders:/ˈflændərz/| n.|弗兰德斯（比利时北部地区）|Flanders is a region in northern Belgium, known for its rich history, culture, and beautiful landscapes. It is divided into five provinces: Antwerp, East Flanders, Flemish Brabant, Limburg, and West Flanders.  The region is predominantly Dutch-speaking.|例句：The Flemish people of Flanders are proud of their distinct culture. 弗兰德斯的佛兰芒人以其独特的文化而自豪。|近义词：Belgium (partially), Netherlands (culturally similar)|反义词：Wallonia (the French-speaking region of Belgium)|名词
