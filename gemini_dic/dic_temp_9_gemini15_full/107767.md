lapidescence:/læpɪˈdɛsəns/| n. |石化；变成石头|The process by which organic matter is converted into stone; petrifaction.  This geological process often preserves the original structure of the organism. |例句：The remarkable lapidescence of the ancient forest preserved the trees in exquisite detail.  古代森林的显著石化作用以精美的细节保存了树木。

The fossilization of a prehistoric creature resulted in its complete lapidescence.|史前生物的化石化导致其完全石化。


近义词：petrifaction, lithification|反义词：decomposition, decay|词性:名词
