redeposited:/ˌriːdɪˈpɒzɪtɪd/| v. | 再存款；重新存放；再次沉积 | To deposit something again, especially money into a bank account, or to cause something to be deposited again.  |例句：He redeposited the check after it was returned for insufficient funds. 他在支票因余额不足被退回后又存了进去。

例句：The geologist explained how the river redeposited the eroded sediment. 地质学家解释了河流如何重新沉积侵蚀的沉积物。


近义词：repay, reinvest, redeposit
