desist:/dɪˈzɪst/| v.|停止；放弃；终止|To cease or stop doing something.|The doctor advised him to desist from smoking.  医生建议他戒烟。|He desisted from his plan after realizing its flaws. 他在意识到计划的缺陷后放弃了计划。|continue, persist|begin, start, commence


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He desists from criticizing others. 他不再批评别人。
They desist from making noise. 他们停止制造噪音。

一般过去时:
She desisted from her studies for a while. 她暂时停止了学习。
He desisted from his attempt after many failures.  在多次失败后，他放弃了尝试。

现在完成时:
They have desisted from their harmful practices. 他们已经停止了有害的行为。
We have desisted from interfering in their affairs. 我们已经停止干涉他们的事务。

过去完成时:
He had desisted from drinking before he started his new job. 他在他开始新工作之前已经戒酒了。
She had desisted from complaining after receiving an apology. 在收到道歉后，她停止了抱怨。

将来时:
I will desist from further arguments. 我将停止进一步的争论。
They will desist from their actions if they are warned. 如果他们受到警告，他们将停止行动。

虚拟语气:
If only he would desist from his bad habits!  如果他能够戒掉坏习惯就好了！
I wish he would desist from interrupting me.  我希望他不要再打断我了。

被动语态:
Further action was desisted from due to unforeseen circumstances. 由于不可预见的情况，进一步的行动被停止了。
The project was desisted from due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目被终止了。

疑问句:
Will they desist from their demands? 他们会停止他们的要求吗？
Did he desist from his pursuit of knowledge? 他放弃了他对知识的追求了吗？

否定句:
He did not desist from his efforts despite the difficulties. 尽管困难重重，他并没有放弃努力。
They will not desist from their attempts to change the law. 他们不会放弃试图改变法律的尝试。


PHRASE->
desist from doing something = 停止做某事
desist from all activities = 停止一切活动
desist from further action = 停止进一步行动
desist from making trouble = 停止制造麻烦
desist under threat of punishment = 在惩罚的威胁下停止
