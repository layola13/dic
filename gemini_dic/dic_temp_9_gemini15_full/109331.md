lengthy:/ˈlɛŋθi/| adj.|冗长的，漫长的，费时的|  characterized by great length; long; extended.  It implies something that takes a considerable amount of time or space. |例句：The meeting was lengthy and unproductive.  会议冗长且没有效率。

The professor gave a lengthy lecture on the history of philosophy. 教授就哲学史做了一个冗长的演讲。


近义词：prolonged, protracted, extended, exhaustive, tedious, long-winded

反义词：brief, short, concise, succinct


词源:  From lengthy (14c.), from length + -y (suffix of adjectives).  The word "length" itself has roots in Old English "lengþ", meaning "length".
