cross-checking:/ˈkrɒsˌtʃekɪŋ/| n. |交叉核对，相互校验 |The act of verifying information or data by comparing it with other sources or data.  This ensures accuracy and reduces errors.  For example, you might cross-check your bank statement with your own records. |例句：We need to cross-check the figures to ensure accuracy. 我们需要交叉核对数据以确保准确性。

例句：The police cross-checked the witness's testimony with other evidence. 警方将证人的证词与其他证据进行了交叉核对。


近义词：verification, confirmation, validation, checking


反义词：neglecting, ignoring, overlooking


词性：名词
