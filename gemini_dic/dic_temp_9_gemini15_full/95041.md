inactivates:/ɪnˈæktɪveɪts/ | vt. |使失去活性；使无效；使不活动 | To make something inactive or ineffective.  |例句：The drug inactivates the virus.  这种药物使病毒失去活性。|例句：High temperatures inactivate many enzymes. 高温使许多酶失去活性。|近义词：deactivate, disable, neutralize |反义词：activate, energize
