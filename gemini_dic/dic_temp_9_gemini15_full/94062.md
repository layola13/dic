impasses:/ˈɪmpæsɪz/ | n. |僵局；死胡同；困境 | A situation in which no progress is possible, especially because of disagreement; a deadlock.  |例句：Negotiations reached an impasse when neither side would compromise.  谈判陷入僵局，因为双方都不肯妥协。

例句：The two countries are at an impasse over the territorial dispute.  这两个国家在领土争端上陷入僵局。

近义词：deadlock, stalemate, standstill, gridlock | 反义词：resolution, breakthrough, solution | 词性：名词
