eldar:/ˈɛldɑːr/| n. |艾尔达，精灵族（指托尔金作品中的种族）|Eldar is a name for the Elves in J.R.R. Tolkien's legendarium, particularly the High Elves of Beleriand.  They are renowned for their beauty, skill, and long lifespans.  The term distinguishes them from other Elves.  |Example: The Eldar of Gondolin were famed for their artistry and wisdom.  艾尔达冈多林族以其艺术和智慧而闻名。|近义词: Elf (a more general term)|反义词: Orc (in Tolkien's works), Mortal|名词
