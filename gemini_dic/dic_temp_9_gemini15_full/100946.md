inundations:/ˌɪnʌnˈdeɪʃənz/| n. |泛滥；洪水；大量涌入 |The overflowing of a large amount of water, especially over land which is usually dry.  It can also refer to an overwhelming abundance of something. |例句：The heavy rains caused widespread inundations in the low-lying areas. 大雨导致低洼地区大面积洪水泛滥。

例句：The city was overwhelmed by an inundation of tourists during the festival. 节日期间，大量的游客涌入城市，使城市不堪重负。|近义词：floods, deluges, overflows |反义词：droughts,deserts
