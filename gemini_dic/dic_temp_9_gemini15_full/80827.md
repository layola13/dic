gouaches:/ˈɡwɑːʃiz/| n.|水彩画；水彩颜料|Gouaches are opaque watercolors, meaning they are not transparent like traditional watercolors.  They are often used for creating vibrant and richly colored paintings.  The paint is typically applied in thick layers, allowing for a textured effect.|例句：The artist created a series of stunning gouaches depicting landscapes of the French countryside.  这位艺术家创作了一系列令人惊叹的水彩画，描绘了法国乡村的风景。


The gallery showcased several exquisite gouaches by a rising young artist. 画廊展出了几位冉冉升起的新星艺术家几幅精美绝伦的水彩画。|近义词：watercolors, paintings|反义词：oil paintings, acrylic paintings

词源:
The word "gouache" comes from the French word "gouache," which itself originates from the Italian word "guazzo," meaning "puddle" or "pool." This refers to the watery consistency of the paint.
