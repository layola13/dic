/ˈmjuːtɪniz/ | n. |哗变；叛乱；反抗 | The act of open rebellion against authority, especially by soldiers or sailors against their officers.  It often involves a refusal to obey orders or a seizure of control. |例句：The sailors staged a mutiny against the cruel captain. 水手们对残暴的船长发动了哗变。

例句：The soldiers' mutiny was quickly suppressed by the government.士兵的哗变很快就被政府镇压了。


近义词：rebellion, revolt, insurrection, uprising | 反义词：obedience, compliance, submission |  词性：名词
