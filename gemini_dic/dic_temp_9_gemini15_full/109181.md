legitimized:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmaɪzd/ | v. | 使合法化；使正当化 | To make something legal or acceptable.  | The government legitimized the new law by passing it through parliament.  政府通过议会通过了这项新法律，使其合法化。| justify, validate, authorize | invalidate, outlaw, prohibit | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government legitimizes new policies through public debate. = 政府通过公开辩论使新的政策合法化。
He legitimizes his actions by claiming they were necessary. = 他声称他的行为是必要的，以此来使它们正当化。


一般过去时:
The court legitimized the marriage after reviewing the documents. = 法院在审查文件后使婚姻合法化。
Her explanation legitimized her absence from the meeting. = 她的解释使她缺席会议的行为正当化。


现在完成时:
The recent election has legitimized the new president's power. = 最近的选举使新总统的权力合法化。
The success of the project has legitimized the company's approach. = 项目的成功使该公司的做法正当化。


过去完成时:
They had legitimized their claim to the land through years of possession. = 他们通过多年的占有使他们对这块土地的所有权合法化。
She had legitimized her position by publishing several influential papers. = 她通过发表几篇有影响力的论文使自己的地位正当化。


将来时:
The new law will legitimize the sale of cannabis. = 新法律将使大麻销售合法化。
His upcoming book will legitimize his theory on climate change. = 他即将出版的书将使他对气候变化的理论正当化。


虚拟语气:
If the treaty were legitimized, peace talks would begin. = 如果条约获得合法化，和平谈判就会开始。
If his actions were legitimized, he would not face any consequences. = 如果他的行为获得正当化，他就不会面临任何后果。


被动语态:
The decision was legitimized by the supreme court. = 最高法院使这一决定合法化。
His methods were legitimized by the scientific community. = 科学界使他的方法正当化。


疑问句:
Does this process legitimize the use of force? = 这个过程是否使武力的使用合法化？
How can we legitimize such unethical practices? = 我们如何使这种不道德的做法正当化？


否定句:
The court did not legitimize the contract due to irregularities. = 由于存在违规行为，法院没有使合同合法化。
His excuses did not legitimize his lateness. = 他的借口并没有使他的迟到正当化。
