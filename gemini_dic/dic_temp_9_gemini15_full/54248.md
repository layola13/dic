disgorges:/dɪsˈɡɔːrdʒɪz/| vt.|呕出；涌出；吐出；廉价抛售|To discharge (the contents of something) violently or suddenly.|The volcano disgorged a torrent of lava. 火山喷出熔岩洪流。The dam disgorged millions of gallons of water. 水坝泄洪数百万加仑的水。|vomit, eject, spew, belch|retain, keep, hold|动词
