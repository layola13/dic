cropper:/ˈkrɒpə(r)/| n. v.|收割者；破坏者；（使）突然倒下|A cropper is a person or thing that crops something, especially a person who harvests crops.  It can also refer to a sudden fall or failure. |例句：The farmer used a mechanical cropper to harvest his wheat.  这个农民用机械收割机收割小麦。  He came a cropper on the icy road. 他在结冰的路上摔了一跤。|近义词：harvester, reaper, faller|反义词：planter, sower
