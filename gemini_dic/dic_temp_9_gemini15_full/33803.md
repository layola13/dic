circumferentially:/ˌsɜːrkəmˌfɛrənʃəli/| adj. |周向地；圆周地| Relating to or in the direction of a circumference.  The term describes something that is happening or oriented around the circumference of a circle or cylinder. |例句：The pipe was reinforced circumferentially to withstand high pressure.  管道进行了周向加固以承受高压。

例句：The tree's growth rings are arranged circumferentially. 树木的年轮呈环状排列。


近义词：peripherally, circularly

反义词：radially, longitudinally


词源:
circum- (around) + ferre (to carry, bear) + -ential (relating to)  The word ultimately derives from the Latin word "circumferentia," meaning "carrying around," which gave rise to the English word "circumference".

USAGE->
There is no specific grammatical tense associated with the word "circumferentially" as it is an adverb.  It modifies verbs and describes how an action is performed.


例句1 (用于描述动作): The technician welded the seam circumferentially. 技师对接缝进行了周向焊接。
例句2 (用于描述方向): The crack extended circumferentially around the pipe. 裂纹沿着管道的周向延伸。

There are no common phrases specifically using "circumferentially."  Its usage is generally within technical descriptions of processes or spatial arrangements.
