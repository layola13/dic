capitalizers:/ˈkæpɪtəlaɪzərz/ | n. | 首字母大写者；使用大写字母的人或事物 | A person or thing that capitalizes, especially a program or function that converts lowercase letters to uppercase.  | Example: The automated capitalizers in the word processor corrected many of my typos.  自动更正程序修正了我许多打字错误。|近义词：uppercasers |反义词：lowercasers
