esox:/ˈiːsɒks/| n. |梭子鱼属|A genus of freshwater fish belonging to the family Esocidae, characterized by their elongated bodies, large mouths, and predatory nature.  They are known for their ambush hunting techniques.|例句：The northern pike is a well-known species of esox.  (北方狗鱼是梭子鱼属的一个著名物种。)  The esox genus includes several species adapted to various freshwater habitats.(梭子鱼属包括几种适应不同淡水栖息地的物种。)|近义词:pike, pickerel|反义词:None.
