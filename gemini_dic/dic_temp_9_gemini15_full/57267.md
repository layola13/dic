downplayed:/ˈdaʊnˌpleɪd/| v. |轻描淡写，贬低，不予重视 |To represent or treat (something) as less important than it really is. |例句：The government downplayed the seriousness of the situation. 政府轻描淡写了局势的严重性。

例句：They downplayed the risks involved in the project. 他们轻描淡写了该项目中的风险。


近义词：understate, minimize, gloss over, play down |反义词：exaggerate, overemphasize, highlight, stress
