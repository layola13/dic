lows:/loʊz/| n.|低点，低谷，低音|The lows of the economic cycle are always difficult.  经济周期的低谷总是很难熬。|近义词：depths, troughs, bottoms|反义词：highs, peaks, summits|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The temperature stays low. = 温度保持低温。
The price of oil is low. = 油价很低。


一般过去时:
The temperature was low yesterday. = 昨天的温度很低。
The stock prices were low last year. = 股票价格去年很低。


现在完成时:
The temperature has been low all week. = 整个星期温度都很低。
The unemployment rate has remained low. = 失业率一直保持低位。


过去完成时:
The temperature had been low for days before it snowed. = 下雪前几天温度一直很低。
The company's profits had been low for several quarters. = 公司的利润在好几个季度都很低。


将来时:
The temperature will be low tomorrow. = 明天的温度会很低。
The interest rates are expected to remain low. = 预计利率将保持低位。


虚拟语气:
I wish the price was lower. = 我希望价格能更低一些。
If the temperature were lower, we would go swimming. = 如果温度更低一些，我们就去游泳。


被动语态:
The volume is kept low. = 音量保持低。
The prices were kept low by government intervention. = 价格通过政府干预被保持在低位。


疑问句:
Is the volume low enough? = 音量够低吗？
Are the prices low enough for you? = 价格够低吗？


否定句:
The volume is not low enough. = 音量不够低。
The temperatures are not low this year. = 今年的温度并不低。


PHRASE->
low point = 低谷

low-key = 低调的

on the low = 秘密地
