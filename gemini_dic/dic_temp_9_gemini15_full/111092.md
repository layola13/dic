litanies:/ˈlɪtəniz/| n. |冗长的祈祷文；反复的抱怨|A litany is a series of prayers or supplications, often repetitive in nature. It can also refer to a prolonged or tedious account of complaints or criticisms.|例句：The priest recited the litany of the saints.  牧师诵念圣徒的祈祷文。

例句：She delivered a litany of complaints about the poor service. 她喋喋不休地抱怨服务差。

近义词：repetition, recital, chant, catalogue

反义词：brief prayer, concise statement
