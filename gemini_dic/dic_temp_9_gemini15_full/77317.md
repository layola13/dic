gathering:/ˈɡæðəriŋ/| n.|聚会，集会，聚集|An act or instance of gathering together; a meeting or assembly of people for a particular purpose.|例句：There will be a family gathering this weekend.  这个周末将有一场家庭聚会。|例句：The gathering of signatures took a long time. 收集签名花了很长时间。|近义词：meeting, assembly, congregation|反义词：dispersal, scattering


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer is gathering apples. = 农民正在采摘苹果。
She gathers wildflowers every spring. = 她每年春天都采摘野花。


一般过去时:
They gathered for a picnic in the park. = 他们在公园里聚餐。
We gathered around the campfire to tell stories. = 我们围坐在篝火旁讲故事。


现在完成时:
She has gathered all the necessary information. = 她已经收集了所有必要的信息。
We have gathered enough evidence to prove our case. = 我们已经收集了足够的证据来证明我们的案情。


过去完成时:
They had gathered all the supplies before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，他们已经收集好了所有物资。
Before the meeting, they had gathered in the lobby. = 会议开始前，他们聚集在大厅里。


将来时:
We will gather for a celebration next week. = 我们下周将举行庆祝活动。
The clouds will gather and it will rain soon. = 云彩将聚集，很快就会下雨。


虚拟语气:
If we gathered more data, we could reach a more accurate conclusion. = 如果我们收集更多数据，我们可以得出更准确的结论。
I wish we had gathered more evidence before the trial. = 我希望我们在审判前收集更多证据。


被动语态:
The evidence was gathered by the police. = 证据是由警方收集的。
The crops are gathered by hand. = 庄稼是手工收割的。


疑问句:
Are you gathering information for your research? = 你正在为你的研究收集信息吗？
What are they gathering in the field? = 他们在田里收集什么？


否定句:
They did not gather enough support for their proposal. = 他们没有为他们的提案获得足够的支持。
She didn't gather the courage to speak up. = 她没有鼓起勇气说话。


PHRASE->
a gathering storm = 即将到来的危机
a social gathering = 社交聚会
a family gathering = 家庭聚会
to gather momentum = 积聚力量
to gather speed = 加速
