scrapyard:/ˈskræpˌjɑːrd/| n. |废料场；拆车场 |A scrapyard is a place where old or unwanted metal, vehicles, and other materials are stored and often dismantled for parts or recycling.  |例句：The old car was taken to the scrapyard.  那辆旧车被送到了废料场。

例句：They found some usable parts in the scrapyard.  他们在废料场里找到了一些可用零件。

近义词：junkyard, salvage yard, scrap heap

反义词：factory, showroom

词性:名词
