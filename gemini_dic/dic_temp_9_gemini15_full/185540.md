revamper:/rɪˈvæmpər/ | n., v. |改造者；翻新者；改进者 |One who revamps something; someone or something that brings about a significant improvement or change.  |例句：The company hired a revamper to modernize its outdated website. 这家公司聘请了一位改造者来对其过时的网站进行现代化改造。

例句：He's a revamper of old houses, turning them into beautiful modern homes. 他是一位旧房改造者，把旧房子改造成美丽的现代住宅。


近义词：renovator, reformer, improver, updater | 反义词：deteriorator, destroyer


词性: 名词和动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He revamps old furniture. = 他翻新旧家具。
She revamps her marketing strategy every year. = 她每年都会修改她的营销策略。

一般过去时:
They revamped the engine. = 他们改造了引擎。
The designer revamped the logo. = 设计师重新设计了标识。

现在完成时:
We have revamped our website. = 我们已经改进了我们的网站。
She has revamped her wardrobe. = 她已经更新了她的衣橱。

过去完成时:
They had revamped the system before the launch. = 他们产品发布前已经改造了系统。
The company had revamped its image before the crisis. = 公司在危机之前已经重塑了形象。

将来时:
We will revamp the kitchen next month. = 我们下个月将改造厨房。
They will revamp their business plan. = 他们将修改他们的商业计划。

虚拟语气:
If they revamped the software, it would work better. = 如果他们修改软件，它会运行得更好。
I wish they had revamped the website earlier. = 我希望他们早点改进网站。

被动语态:
The car was revamped by a professional mechanic. = 这辆车由一位专业技师进行了翻新。
The curriculum will be revamped next year. = 教学大纲明年将进行修订。

疑问句:
Will they revamp the product? = 他们会改进产品吗？
Have they revamped the store's layout? = 他们已经改进商店的布局了吗？

否定句:
They didn't revamp the building. = 他们没有翻新那栋楼。
He hasn't revamped his approach yet. = 他还没改变他的方法。


PHRASE->
revamp a system =  改造系统

revamp an image =  重塑形象
