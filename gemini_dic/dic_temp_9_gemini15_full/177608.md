rebuildable:/ˌriːˈbɪldəbəl/| adj. | 可重建的，可修复的 | Capable of being rebuilt or reconstructed.  This adjective describes something that can be restored to its former state after being damaged or destroyed. | The old house, though dilapidated, was rebuildable with considerable effort.  这座旧房子虽然破旧不堪，但经过大量的努力是可以重建的。The bridge, though heavily damaged, is thankfully rebuildable.  桥梁虽然严重受损，但谢天谢地是可以重建的。| reconstructible, repairable | irreparable, unrebuildable | 形容词
