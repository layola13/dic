non-subscriber: /ˌnɑːn səbˈskraɪbər/ | n. | 非订户，非订阅者 | A person who does not subscribe to a service, publication, or organization.  | Example:  The non-subscriber was unable to access the premium content.  非订户无法访问高级内容。 |近义词:unsubscribed user, nonsubscriber, non-member |反义词:subscriber


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The non-subscriber cannot access the online library. = 非订户无法访问在线图书馆。
He is a non-subscriber to this magazine. = 他不是这份杂志的订户。


一般过去时:
She was a non-subscriber last year. = 她去年不是订户。
They were non-subscribers to the streaming service. = 他们过去不是该流媒体服务的订户。


现在完成时:
He has remained a non-subscriber for many years. = 他多年来一直没有订阅。
She has been a non-subscriber since the price increase. = 自从价格上涨以来，她就没有订阅了。


过去完成时:
Before the free trial ended, he was already a non-subscriber. = 在免费试用期结束之前，他已经不是订户了。
They had been non-subscribers for years before they decided to subscribe. = 在他们决定订阅之前，他们已经是非订户多年了。


将来时:
He will remain a non-subscriber until the price drops. = 在价格下降之前，他将仍然是非订户。
She will likely be a non-subscriber next year. = 她明年很可能仍然是非订户。


虚拟语气:
If he were a subscriber, he could access the exclusive content. = 如果他是订户，他就可以访问独家内容。
I wish I were a subscriber; I'd love to read that article. = 我希望我是订户；我很想读那篇文章。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
