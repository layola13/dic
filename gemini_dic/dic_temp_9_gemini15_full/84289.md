half-truth:/ˈhɑːftruːθ/| n. |半真半假的话，似是而非之词 |A statement that is partly true but also partly false, often intentionally misleading.  |Example:  He told me a half-truth about his past; while some parts were accurate, he omitted key details. 他对我说了关于他过去的一些半真半假的话；虽然有些部分是准确的，但他省略了关键细节。|近义词：partially true statement, white lie, misleading statement |反义词：whole truth, complete truth, lie
