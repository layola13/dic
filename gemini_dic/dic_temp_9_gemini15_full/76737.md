game-plan:/ˈɡeɪm plæn/| n.|比赛计划；策略；计划|A detailed plan or strategy for achieving a particular goal, especially in a competitive situation.  It often involves anticipating opponents' actions and devising counter-strategies.|例句：The team's game plan was to control the ball and tire out their opponents.  球队的比赛计划是控制球权，并让对手疲惫不堪。|例句：We need a solid game plan to launch this new product successfully.我们需要一个周密的计划来成功推出这款新产品。|近义词：strategy, scheme, tactic, blueprint|反义词：improvisation, winging it


USAGE->

Our team has a strong game plan for the upcoming match. = 我们团队为即将到来的比赛制定了强有力的计划。
What is your game plan for dealing with this crisis? = 你应对这场危机的计划是什么？
