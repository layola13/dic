predemocratic: /ˌpriːˌdɛməˈkrætɪk/ | adj. | 前民主的，民主之前的 |  Describing a period or system of government that existed before the establishment of democracy.  It often implies a lack of democratic principles, such as individual rights, free elections, and popular sovereignty. |  The predemocratic era was characterized by absolute monarchy and widespread social inequality.  前民主时代的特点是君主专制和普遍的社会不平等。 The historian focused on the predemocratic institutions of the region. 这位历史学家专注于该地区前民主时期的制度。|  autocratic, authoritarian, tyrannical | democratic | 形容词
