kiddos:/ˈkɪdoʊz/| n.|孩子们，孩子们（昵称）|An informal term for children.  It is a diminutive of "kid".|例句：The kiddos are all excited for the trip to the zoo. 孩子们都很兴奋要去动物园。

例句：Come on, kiddos, it's time for bed. 来吧，孩子们，该睡觉了。


近义词：children, kids, little ones, youngsters|反义词：adults, grown-ups|名词
