prognathous:/ˈprɒɡnəθəs/| adj.|前突的；凸嘴的 | Having a projecting jaw or chin.  The term often refers to a condition where the jaw protrudes beyond the upper teeth and/or the rest of the facial structure.  | 例句：The anthropologist noted the prognathous features of the skull. 人类学家注意到颅骨的前突特征。  The patient presented with a prognathous profile. 病人呈现出前突的面部轮廓。|近义词：protruding, projecting, jutting |反义词：orthognathous
