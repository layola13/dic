preeligible:/priːˈɛlɪdʒəbəl/ | adj. | 预先符合条件的，预先合格的 |  Eligible before a formal process of evaluation.  It implies a pre-qualification or preliminary screening has suggested eligibility, but final determination is pending.  | Example:  The preeligible applicants will be contacted for an interview. 预先符合条件的申请者将被联系进行面试。| 近义词：prequalified, provisionally eligible | 反义词：ineligible, disqualified
