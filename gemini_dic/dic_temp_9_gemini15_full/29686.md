catheterization:/ˌkæθədəraɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |导管插入术；导尿术|The process of inserting a catheter (a thin, flexible tube) into a body cavity, duct, or vessel.  This procedure is used for various medical purposes, such as draining fluids, injecting substances, or monitoring pressure.|Example: The patient required catheterization to relieve bladder distension.  病人需要导管插入术来缓解膀胱胀满。

The doctor performed a cardiac catheterization to assess the patient's heart condition. 医生进行了心脏导管插入术以评估病人的心脏状况。|近义词：cannulation|反义词：无
