contributors:/kənˈtrɪbjuːtərz/ | n. |贡献者，投稿者 |People who contribute something, such as money, time, or ideas, to a common purpose or project.  |The contributors to the charity drive exceeded their fundraising goal.  慈善募捐的捐款人超过了他们的筹款目标。|donors, givers, participants|receivers, detractors|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The contributors donate regularly. = 捐款人定期捐款。
Many contributors support this project. = 许多贡献者支持这个项目。


一般过去时:
The contributors helped build the school. = 贡献者们帮助建造了这所学校。
Several contributors submitted articles for the magazine. = 好几位投稿者为杂志提交了文章。


现在完成时:
The contributors have raised a significant amount of money. = 捐款人已经筹集了大量的资金。
The contributors have already submitted their work. = 投稿者们已经提交了他们的作品。


过去完成时:
The contributors had already pledged their support before the event. = 在活动开始之前，捐款人就已经承诺提供支持。
The contributors had completed their contributions by the deadline. = 投稿者们在截止日期前完成了他们的贡献。


将来时:
The contributors will be recognized at the ceremony. = 捐款人将在仪式上受到表彰。
The contributors will submit their work next week. = 投稿者们将在下周提交他们的作品。


虚拟语气:
If the contributors were more generous, we could achieve more. = 如果捐款人更慷慨一些，我们就能取得更大的成就。
I wish the contributors had submitted their work earlier. = 我希望投稿者们早点提交他们的作品。


被动语态:
The contributions were made by many generous contributors. = 许多慷慨的捐款人做出了贡献。
The articles were reviewed by the contributors before publication. = 文章在发表前由投稿者进行了审核。


疑问句:
Who are the main contributors to this project? = 这个项目的主要贡献者是谁？
How many contributors are there? = 有多少个投稿者？


否定句:
The contributors did not meet their target. = 捐款人没有达到他们的目标。
There were no anonymous contributors. = 没有匿名投稿者。
