hundredweight:/ˈhʌndrɪdweɪt/| n. |百磅重；担 |A hundredweight is a unit of weight equal to 100 pounds (45.36 kilograms) in the avoirdupois system.  It's often abbreviated as cwt.  The term is used primarily in the UK and some other Commonwealth countries.  |The farmer harvested a hundredweight of potatoes.  这位农民收获了一百磅土豆。

The shipment weighed over five hundredweight. 这批货重达五百磅以上。|近义词：quintal (in some regions)|反义词：None (It's a unit of measurement, not a concept with an opposite).
