词源:

The word "overtenaciousness" is formed by combining:

* **over-**: a prefix indicating "too much," "excessive," or "beyond."
* **tenacious**:  This comes from the Latin word *tenax*, meaning "holding fast," "firm," or "tough."  It entered English via Old French.
* **-ness**: a suffix forming abstract nouns, indicating a state, quality, or condition.


Therefore, "overtenaciousness" literally means the state or quality of being excessively tenacious.
