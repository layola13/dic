overdemanding: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪmændɪŋ/ | adj. | 过分要求的，要求过高的 |  Demanding too much; excessively or unreasonably exacting.  |  例句：The job was overdemanding, requiring long hours and intense focus. 这份工作要求过高，需要长时间工作和高度集中注意力。

例句：Her overdemanding boss made her feel constantly stressed. 她要求过高的老板让她感到持续的压力。


近义词：exacting, demanding, strenuous, oppressive, burdensome | 反义词：undemanding, easy, lenient, accommodating


词源：over- (prefix indicating excess) + demanding (adjective meaning making excessive demands)  前缀over-表示过度，demanding是形容词，意为“要求过高的”。  它源于动词demand，意思为“要求”。


USAGE->

The teacher is overdemanding of her students. = 老师对她的学生要求过高。
This project is overdemanding for the current team. = 这个项目对目前的团队来说要求过高。


一般过去时:
