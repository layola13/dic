pneumatic: /njuːˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 气动的；充气的 | Relating to or operated by air or gas under pressure.  Pneumatic systems use compressed air to power machinery or tools.  | 例句：The factory uses pneumatic drills to speed up production. 工厂使用气动钻来加快生产速度。 The pneumatic tyres on my bicycle provide a smooth ride. 我自行车的充气轮胎提供了平稳的骑行体验。| 近义词：air-powered, gas-powered | 反义词：hydraulic, manual

词源:
From Greek *pneumatikós ("of or pertaining to breath, wind, or spirit"), from pneûma ("breath, wind, spirit").  The modern English word reflects the use of compressed air in mechanical systems.
