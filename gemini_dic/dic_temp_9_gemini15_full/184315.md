respecifies:/ˌriːˈspɛsɪfaɪz/| vt.|重新指定；重新说明；重新规定|To specify again or differently; to restate the specifications of.|The company respecified the project's requirements after the initial feedback.|近义词：redefine, restate, revise|反义词：obfuscate, confuse


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He respecifies the design parameters regularly. = 他定期重新指定设计参数。
The engineer respecifies the materials needed for the project. = 工程师重新指定项目所需的材料。

一般过去时:
They respecified the contract terms last week. = 他们上周重新规定了合同条款。
She respecified her requirements after reviewing the initial proposal. = 她审查了初步提案后重新说明了她的要求。

The team has respecified the software's functionality. = 团队已经重新指定了软件的功能。

过去完成时:

The architect had respecified the building materials after discovering a flaw in the initial plan. = 建筑师在发现初始计划中的缺陷后，重新指定了建筑材料。
