shepherding:/ˈʃɛpərdɪŋ/| v. |牧羊；引导；照料|The act of guiding or leading; overseeing and caring for something or someone.  It often implies a protective and nurturing role.|例句：The shepherd was shepherding his flock across the mountain. 牧羊人正带领他的羊群穿过山脉。

例句：The teacher shepherded her students through the complex exam. 老师引导她的学生完成复杂的考试。


近义词：guiding, leading, directing, managing, overseeing |反义词：abandoning, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He shepherds his team through challenging projects. 他带领他的团队完成具有挑战性的项目。
She shepherds the children to school each morning. 她每天早上带领孩子们去上学。


一般过去时:
The experienced manager shepherded the new employees through their training. 经验丰富的经理引导新员工完成培训。
The captain shepherded his ship safely through the storm. 船长带领他的船安全地度过了风暴。


现在完成时:
She has shepherded numerous students to success. 她已经引导许多学生走向成功。
The organization has shepherded the project through several phases. 该组织已将项目引导完成了好几个阶段。


过去完成时:
He had shepherded the project to completion before the deadline. 他在截止日期前完成了项目。
They had shepherded the refugees to safety. 他们已将难民引导到安全地带。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
