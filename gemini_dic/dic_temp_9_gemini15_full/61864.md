emporia:/ɛmˈpɔːriə/| n.|商业中心；市场；商店；众多商店的集合|A place where goods are sold; a market; a shopping center.  Emporia often refers to a collection of shops or a large marketplace, often suggesting a bustling and diverse commercial area. |例句：The ancient city boasted a thriving emporium filled with exotic goods from across the known world.  这座古城拥有一个繁荣的商业中心，里面摆满了来自世界各地的奇珍异宝。|例句：The new shopping mall is a modern emporium, offering everything from clothing to electronics. 这个新的购物中心是一个现代化的商业中心，提供从服装到电子产品的一切商品。|近义词：marketplace, market, bazaar, mall, shopping center|反义词：deserted area, wilderness
