incorrigible:/ɪnˈkɒrɪdʒəbl/|/ɪnˈkɔːrɪdʒəbl/| adj. |不可救药的；屡教不改的 |Incorrigible means incapable of being corrected or reformed.  It describes someone whose bad habits or behavior are firmly established and unlikely to change.  |例句：His incorrigible optimism never wavered, even in the face of disaster. 他不可救药的乐观主义即使面对灾难也从未动摇。

例句：Despite repeated warnings, his incorrigible lateness continued to disrupt the team. 尽管反复警告，他屡教不改的迟到继续扰乱团队。


近义词：unamendable, irreformable, intractable, inveterate, persistent

反义词：reformable, correctable, amenable
