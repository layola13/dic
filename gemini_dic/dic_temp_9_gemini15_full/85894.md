headfirst:/ˈhɛdˌfɜːrst/ | adv. | 头朝下地；莽撞地，轻率地 |  Going in headfirst implies doing something without proper planning or consideration, often with a sense of reckless enthusiasm.  It can also literally mean diving headfirst into something. | 例句：He jumped headfirst into the swimming pool. = 他一头扎进了游泳池。

He rushed headfirst into the project without considering the risks. = 他不顾风险，一头扎进了那个项目。


近义词：recklessly, rashly, impulsively, precipitately | 反义词：cautiously, carefully, deliberately, cautiously


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He dives headfirst into every new challenge. = 他对每一个新的挑战都一头扎进去。
They jump headfirst into every opportunity. = 他们对每一个机会都欣然接受。


一般过去时:
She plunged headfirst into the crowd. = 她一头扎进了人群。
He threw himself headfirst into the work. = 他一头扎进了工作。


现在完成时:
I have always jumped headfirst into new experiences. = 我总是欣然接受新的体验。
They have thrown themselves headfirst into this project. = 他们已经全力以赴投入这个项目了。


过去完成时:
He had already jumped headfirst into the business before he fully understood the risks. = 在他完全了解风险之前，他已经一头扎进了这个生意。
She had thrown herself headfirst into the relationship without knowing him well. = 她没有充分了解他就一头扎进了这段感情。


将来时:
He will jump headfirst into his new job next week. = 他下周将全身心地投入他的新工作。
She will likely throw herself headfirst into the upcoming competition. = 她很可能会全力以赴参加即将到来的比赛。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I wouldn't jump headfirst into such a risky venture. = 如果我是你，我不会贸然投入如此冒险的企业。
I wish I hadn't jumped headfirst into that argument. = 我真希望我没有贸然卷入那场争论。


被动语态: (This adverb is rarely used in passive voice)  The project was approached headfirst. =  这个项目是被轻率地处理的。(This sounds unnatural, a better phrasing would be needed in most contexts.)
The task was undertaken headfirst and without planning. = 任务是被仓促而没有计划地执行的。


疑问句: (This adverb is rarely used in questions) Did he really jump headfirst into the fire? = 他真的跳进火里了吗？ (This sounds unnatural, better phrasing would be needed in most contexts)
Did they proceed headfirst with the plan, despite the warnings? = 尽管有警告，他们还是轻率地执行了计划吗？


否定句:
He didn't jump headfirst into the decision. = 他没有轻率地做出决定。
They didn't approach the problem headfirst. = 他们没有鲁莽地处理这个问题。
