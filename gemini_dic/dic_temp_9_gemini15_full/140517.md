oleanders:/ˈoʊliændərz/ | n. |夹竹桃属植物；夹竹桃 | Evergreen shrubs or small trees of the genus Nerium, having fragrant, usually pink or white flowers.  Oleanders are known for their beautiful blooms but are highly toxic. |例句：The oleanders in the garden were in full bloom. 花园里的夹竹桃盛开了。|例句：We admired the vibrant pink oleanders lining the walkway. 我们欣赏着沿人行道盛开的鲜艳粉红色的夹竹桃。|近义词：Nerium (botanical name) |反义词：无 |名词
