challengeable: /ˈtʃælɪndʒəbl/ | adj. | 可质疑的；可挑战的；易于挑战的 |  Open to challenge or dispute; capable of being challenged.  Something challengeable is not necessarily wrong, but it can be questioned or debated. |


例句：

His claims are highly challengeable and require further investigation. 他的说法极易受到质疑，需要进一步调查。

The referee's decision was challengeable, but ultimately upheld. 裁判的决定是可以质疑的，但最终还是维持了原判。


近义词：disputable, questionable, debatable, contestable


反义词：indisputable, undeniable, irrefutable, unquestionable


词源:

The word "challengeable" is derived from the verb "challenge," which ultimately comes from the Old French word "chalenger," meaning "to accuse," "to summon," or "to defy."  The suffix "-able" indicates that something is capable of being challenged or acted upon.
