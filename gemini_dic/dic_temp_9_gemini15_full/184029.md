residences:/ˈrezɪdənsɪz/| n. |住所；住宅；居住地 |Places where people live, often implying a more permanent or significant dwelling than simply a house or apartment.  It can refer to a single dwelling or a collection of dwellings.|例句：The university provides residences for its students. 大学为学生提供宿舍。|例句：Luxury residences are available in the upscale neighborhood. 高档住宅区有豪华住宅出售。|近义词：dwellings, homes, houses, apartments, accommodations |反义词： homelessness
