choppier:/ˈtʃɑːpiər/| adj.|波浪起伏的；动荡的；不稳定的；粗糙的 |More uneven or rough; less smooth.  It refers to something that is not smooth or regular, often with abrupt changes in direction or level.  |例句：The sea was choppier than we expected. 海比我们预期的要波涛汹涌。

The wind made the water choppier. 风使水更加波涛汹涌。


近义词：rough, uneven, turbulent, bumpy, jagged |反义词：smooth, calm, placid, even, serene|词性:形容词
