/ˌoʊvər.fəˈsɪlɪtiz/| n. |过度设施；设施过多|Excessive provision of facilities, often leading to inefficiency or waste.  This term often refers to a situation where there are more facilities than are necessary or can be effectively utilized, leading to potential problems like underutilization, increased maintenance costs, and reduced efficiency. |The new shopping mall suffers from overfacilities; many stores remain unoccupied, resulting in high vacancy rates.  这个新的购物中心设施过多；许多商店仍然空置，导致空置率很高。|underfacilities, sufficient facilities|underprovision of facilities
