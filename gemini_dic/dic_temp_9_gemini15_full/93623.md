imbitter:/ɪmˈbɪtər/| vt.|使痛苦；使怨恨；使不快|To make (someone) bitter or resentful.  To make (something) unpleasant or disagreeable.|例句：The constant criticism embittered him.  持续的批评使他变得痛苦。 The long wait embittered their anticipation. 漫长的等待使他们的期待变得不快。|近义词：sour, exasperate, gall|反义词：soothe, please, delight|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The constant noise embitters her. = 持续的噪音让她痛苦。
His failures embitter him. = 他的失败使他痛苦。

一般过去时:
The loss of his job embittered him. = 失去工作使他心怀怨恨。
The unfair treatment embittered her relationship with her boss. = 不公平的待遇使她与老板的关系恶化。

现在完成时:
Years of hardship have embittered her. = 多年的苦难使她变得辛酸。
His betrayal has embittered their friendship. = 他的背叛使他们的友谊破裂。

过去完成时:
He had been embittered by years of disappointment. = 他多年来一直因失望而痛苦。
She had been embittered by the injustice she had suffered. = 她因遭受的不公正待遇而变得痛苦。

将来时:
This situation will embitter him further. = 这种情况会使他更加痛苦。
Continued failure will embitter her spirit. = 持续的失败将会使她的精神变得痛苦。

虚拟语气:
If I were in his shoes, I would be embittered. = 如果我处在他的位置，我也会感到痛苦。
If she had known the truth, she would have been embittered. = 如果她知道真相，她本会感到痛苦。


被动语态:
He was embittered by the events of that day. = 他被那天发生的事情弄得很痛苦。
She was embittered by her experience. = 她被自己的经历弄得很痛苦。


疑问句:
Will this experience embitter him? = 这次经历会让他痛苦吗？
Has the situation embittered her relationship with her family? = 这种情况是否使她与家人的关系恶化？


否定句:
The victory did not embitter him. = 胜利并没有让他痛苦。
The experience did not embitter her. = 这段经历并没有让她痛苦。


PHRASE->
embitter one's life = 使某人的生活痛苦
embitter one's soul = 使某人心灵痛苦
embitter relations = 使关系恶化
embitter the taste = 使味道变苦
embitter the memory = 使回忆痛苦
