pawn:/pɔːn/| n. v.| 典当；棋子；爪牙；牺牲品 |

英文解析:
Noun: A pawn is a piece of the lowest value in the game of chess.  It can also refer to something or someone used as a tool to achieve a goal, often with little regard for their well-being.  In a financial context, a pawn is an item left as collateral for a loan.

Verb: To pawn something means to leave it as security for a loan.


例句:
Noun:
He moved his pawn two squares forward. 他把他的棋子向前移了两格。

The child was used as a pawn in the custody battle.  这个孩子在监护权争夺战中被当作棋子。


Verb:
I had to pawn my watch to pay the rent. 我不得不典当我的手表来付房租。

She pawned her jewelry to raise money for her trip. 她典当了她的珠宝为她的旅行筹集资金。


近义词:
Noun:  piece (chess),  tool, instrument, victim, sacrifice

Verb:  pledge, mortgage


反义词:
Noun:  king (chess),  hero, champion

Verb:  redeem


词性:
Noun, Verb
