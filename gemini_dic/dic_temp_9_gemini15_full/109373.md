lensless:/ˈlɛnzləs/| adj.|无透镜的|Lacking a lens or lenses;  often used to describe imaging systems that do not use traditional lenses to focus light.|例句：Lensless imaging is a rapidly developing field.  无透镜成像是一个快速发展的领域。|例句：The researchers developed a novel lensless camera. 研究人员开发了一种新型无透镜相机。|近义词：lenseless, achromatic (in the context of not using lenses to correct aberrations)|反义词：lensed
