dismounted:/ˌdɪsˈmaʊntɪd/ | v. | 下马；卸下；拆卸 | To get off a horse or other animal; to remove something from its mount or support. |例句：The knight dismounted gracefully from his steed.  骑士优雅地下马。

The soldiers dismounted their cannons before moving camp. 士兵们在搬营之前卸下了他们的火炮。


近义词：alighted, disembarked, descended | 反义词：mounted
