disqualifiable: /dɪsˈkwɑːlɪfaɪəbl/ | adj. | 可取消资格的，可取消参赛资格的 |  Capable of being disqualified; able to be rendered ineligible.  This adjective describes something that can be deemed ineligible or unfit for something, often due to a rule violation or failure to meet specific requirements. |


例句：

The athlete's actions were disqualifiable under the rules of the competition.  = 运动员的行为根据比赛规则是可以取消资格的。

His repeated breaches of contract made him disqualifiable for future projects. = 他反复违反合同，使他失去了未来项目的资格。


近义词：ineligible, invalid, unqualified

反义词：eligible, qualified, acceptable
