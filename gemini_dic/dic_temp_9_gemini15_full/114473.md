makuti:/məˈkuːti/| n. |茅草屋顶；茅草屋|A makuti is a roof made of woven palm leaves, commonly found in East Africa.  It can also refer to a hut or house with such a roof.|The villagers lived in houses with makuti roofs.|近义词：thatched roof|反义词：无
