out-greening:/ˌaʊtˈɡriːnɪŋ/| v.|超越绿化；胜过在环保方面|To surpass in the extent or quality of environmental initiatives or green practices.|The new building out-greened all the others in the complex with its innovative solar panels and rainwater harvesting system.  这座新建筑凭借其创新的太阳能电池板和雨水收集系统，在环保方面超越了大楼里的其他所有建筑。|outdo, surpass, exceed|pollute, damage, degrade|动词


USAGE->

一般现在时:
They out-green their competitors by investing in sustainable materials. = 他们通过投资可持续材料来超越竞争对手。
Our company out-greens its rivals in carbon emission reduction. = 我们公司在减少碳排放方面胜过竞争对手。


一般过去时:
Last year, the city out-greened its ambitious targets for tree planting. = 去年，这座城市在植树方面超越了其雄心勃勃的目标。
The project out-greened all expectations regarding environmental impact. = 该项目在环境影响方面超出了所有预期。


现在完成时:
This company has out-greened many of its competitors in the field of sustainable packaging. = 这家公司在可持续包装领域已经超越了许多竞争对手。
Our efforts have out-greened the initial goals set for environmental improvement. = 我们的努力已经超越了最初设定的环境改善目标。


过去完成时:
By 2020, they had out-greened their rivals in terms of renewable energy usage. = 到2020年，他们在可再生能源使用方面已经超越了竞争对手。
The company had out-greened its previous sustainability record by implementing new initiatives. = 通过实施新的举措，该公司已经超越了其之前的可持续性记录。


将来时:
We will out-green our competitors by developing cutting-edge green technology. = 我们将通过开发尖端绿色技术来超越竞争对手。
The city plans to out-green its current environmental standards in the coming years. = 该城市计划在未来几年超越其目前的环保标准。


虚拟语气:
If we invested more, we could out-green the industry leader. = 如果我们投资更多，我们就能超越行业领导者。
I wish we had out-greened our rivals in this important environmental campaign. = 我希望我们在这次重要的环保活动中能够超越竞争对手。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
