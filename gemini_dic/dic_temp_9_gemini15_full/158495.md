plenipotency:/ˌplɛnɪˈpoʊtənsi/| n. |全权；全权代理；权力 |The state or quality of being plenipotentiary; having full power or authority.  It refers to the full and absolute power to act on behalf of another, especially in diplomatic affairs.|例句：The ambassador possessed plenipotency to negotiate a peace treaty.  这位大使拥有谈判和平条约的全权。|例句：The plenipotency granted to the negotiator allowed for a swift resolution.  给予谈判者的全权使得问题迅速得到解决。|近义词：full power, authority, authorization, mandate |反义词：limited power, restriction, constraint |名词
