canvassing:/ˈkænvəsɪŋ/| v. |游说；拉选票；挨家挨户推销；彻底调查|To canvass means to actively seek support or votes, often by visiting people personally.  It can also refer to thoroughly examining or investigating something. |例句：The candidate is canvassing the neighborhood for votes.  这位候选人正在挨家挨户地拉选票。

例句：The committee canvassed the opinions of all members before making a decision. 委员会在做决定之前征求了所有成员的意见。

近义词：soliciting, campaigning, polling, surveying

反义词：ignoring, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are canvassing for donations. = 他们在四处募集捐款。
He canvasses the market for new products. = 他在市场上寻找新产品。


一般过去时:
She canvassed the neighborhood for support. = 她挨家挨户地争取支持。
We canvassed the options before choosing. = 我们在选择之前仔细权衡了各种选择。


现在完成时:
They have canvassed the entire town. = 他们已经走遍了整个城镇。
We have canvassed public opinion on the issue. = 我们已经调查了公众对这个问题的意见。


过去完成时:
She had canvassed the area before the election. = 她在选举前已经走访过那个地区。
They had canvassed all the evidence before reaching a conclusion. = 他们在得出结论之前已经调查了所有证据。


将来时:
He will be canvassing the voters next week. = 他下周将要挨家挨户地拜访选民。
We will canvass the possibilities before making a decision. = 我们将在做决定之前仔细考虑各种可能性。


虚拟语气:
If they canvassed more effectively, they would win the election. = 如果他们更有效地拉选票，他们就会赢得选举。
I wish they had canvassed the public opinion before implementing the policy. = 我希望他们在实施这项政策之前能够征求一下公众意见。


被动语态:
The neighborhood was canvassed by the volunteers. = 志愿者们走访了整个街区。
The issue was thoroughly canvassed by the committee. = 委员会对这个问题进行了彻底的调查。


疑问句:
Are they canvassing for a specific cause? = 他们在为某个特定的事业拉选票吗？
Have you canvassed all the available data? = 你已经调查了所有可用的数据了吗？


否定句:
They did not canvass the entire population. = 他们没有调查整个人口。
He will not canvass the neighborhood again. = 他不会再去挨家挨户地拜访了。


PHRASE->
canvass for votes = 拉选票
canvass support = 争取支持
canvass opinions = 征求意见
canvass the market = 调查市场
canvass the evidence = 调查证据


词源:
The word "canvassing" originates from the Old French word "canevas," meaning "canvas."  The original sense referred to the use of canvas as a surface for displaying or collecting signatures or votes.  Over time, the meaning evolved to encompass the broader act of soliciting support or opinions.
