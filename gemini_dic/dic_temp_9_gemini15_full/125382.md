multimodalities:/ˌmʌltiˌmoʊˈdælɪtiz/ | n. | 多模态性；多模式；多种方式 | The study of multimodalities examines how meaning is created and communicated through various semiotic modes, such as language, images, gestures, sounds, and spatial arrangements.  It goes beyond the traditional focus on language alone to encompass a richer understanding of communication. | Example: Researchers are exploring the multimodalities of online learning, analyzing how text, images, videos, and interactive elements contribute to student understanding.  研究人员正在探索在线学习的多模态性，分析文本、图像、视频和互动元素如何促进学生的理解。 | 近义词：multimodality, polymodality | 反义词：unimodality |  名词
