madcaps:/ˈmædˌkæps/ | n. | 狂妄自大的人；鲁莽的人；轻率的人 |  A madcap is a person who is wildly impulsive and reckless; someone who behaves in a rash and unpredictable way.  They often act without thinking of the consequences. | Example:  He was a madcap young man, always getting into trouble. 他是个鲁莽的年轻人，总是惹麻烦。  She's a madcap adventurer, always seeking thrills. 她是一位鲁莽的冒险家，总是寻求刺激。 | 近义词：daredevil, hothead, wild child, impetuous person | 反义词：cautious person, careful person, timid person
