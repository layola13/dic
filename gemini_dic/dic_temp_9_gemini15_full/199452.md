simultaneously: /ˌsɪmlˈteɪniəsli/ | adv. |同时地，同步地 | Happening or existing at the same time.  The adverb describes actions or events that occur at the same time. | 例句：The two events happened simultaneously. = 这两件事同时发生。

The lights went out simultaneously across the city. = 整个城市同时停电了。


近义词：concurrently, at the same time, together, concurrently, synchronously | 反义词：sequentially, successively, one after another


词源:  Comes from the Latin word "simul" (meaning "at the same time") + "taneous" (from the Latin "tanus," meaning "pertaining to").  The "-ly" suffix indicates an adverb.
