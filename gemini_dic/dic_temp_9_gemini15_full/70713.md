financing:/ˈfaɪnænsɪŋ/ | n. | 融资；资金筹措 | The process of providing or obtaining funds for a project or undertaking.  It involves various methods like loans, investments, and grants. | Example:  The company secured financing for its new factory through a bank loan.  这家公司通过银行贷款获得了新工厂的资金。 The startup is seeking financing from venture capitalists. 这家初创公司正在寻求风险投资家的融资。 |近义词：funding, capital raising, investment | 反义词：spending, disbursement


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company is financing the project through a bond issue. = 这家公司正在通过发行债券为项目融资。
He finances his education with a scholarship. = 他用奖学金资助他的教育。


一般过去时:
They financed the purchase of their new house with a mortgage. = 他们用抵押贷款支付了新房的购买费用。
The government financed the construction of the new bridge. = 政府资助了新桥的建设。


现在完成时:
We have financed several projects this year. = 今年我们已经为几个项目提供了资金。
They have financed their expansion through retained earnings. = 他们通过留存收益为扩张提供了资金。


过去完成时:
The company had financed the research before seeking approval. = 公司在寻求批准之前已经为研究提供了资金。
They had financed the project before the market crash. = 他们在市场崩盘之前已经为该项目提供了资金。


将来时:
We will finance the renovation next year. = 我们明年将为翻新工程提供资金。
The project will be financed by a grant from the foundation. = 该项目将由基金会的拨款资助。


虚拟语气:
If we had more financing, we could expand faster. = 如果我们有更多的资金，我们可以更快地扩张。
I wish we had secured financing earlier. = 我希望我们早点获得融资。


被动语态:
The project is being financed by the government. = 该项目正在由政府资助。
The acquisition was financed by private equity. = 这笔收购由私募股权融资。


疑问句:
How will the project be financed? = 项目将如何融资？
Where will the company find financing for this venture? = 这家公司将在哪里为这项投资找到资金？


否定句:
They did not secure financing for their new product launch. = 他们没有为新产品的发布获得资金。
The company was unable to find financing for its expansion plans. = 这家公司无法为其扩张计划找到资金。
