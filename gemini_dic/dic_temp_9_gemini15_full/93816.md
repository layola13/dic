immigrants:/ˈɪmɪɡrənts/| n. |移民 |People who have come to live permanently in a foreign country.|例句：The immigrants enriched the cultural landscape of the city.  移民丰富了这座城市的文化景观。|例句：Many immigrants face challenges integrating into a new society. 许多移民在融入新社会方面面临挑战。|近义词：migrants, newcomers, settlers|反义词：emigrants

USAGE->
一般现在时:
Many immigrants work hard to build new lives. = 许多移民努力建设新的生活。
Immigrants contribute significantly to the economy. = 移民对经济做出了重大贡献。


一般过去时:
My grandparents immigrated to this country a long time ago. = 我的祖父母很久以前就移民到这个国家了。
Many immigrants arrived seeking better opportunities. = 许多移民来到这里是为了寻求更好的机会。


现在完成时:
Millions of immigrants have settled in this region. = 数百万移民已定居在这个地区。
She has been an immigrant for five years. = 她已经移民五年了。


过去完成时:

The family had immigrated several times before finding a home. = 这个家庭在找到一个家之前已经移民了好几次。


将来时:
More immigrants will likely come to the country next year. = 明年可能会有更多移民来到这个国家。
He will immigrate to Canada next month. = 他下个月将移民到加拿大。


虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:
