earlier:/ˈɜːrlɪər/ | adv. | 更早，先前 |  Earlier, I mentioned the importance of planning.  更早些时候，我提到了计划的重要性。| 近义词：previously, before, formerly | 反义词：later


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He usually arrives earlier than me. 他通常比我到得早。
She starts work earlier than most people. 她比大多数人开始工作得早。

一般过去时:
I woke up earlier than usual this morning. 今天早上我比平时醒得早。
They arrived earlier than expected. 他们比预期的早到。

现在完成时:
We have finished earlier than we anticipated. 我们比预料的早完成了。
She has woken up earlier every day this week.  本周她每天都醒得比前一天早。

过去完成时:
He had arrived earlier than anyone else. 他比任何人都早到。
They had finished their work earlier that day. 他们那天早些时候就完成了工作。

将来时:
I will leave earlier tomorrow. 我明天会早点离开。
We'll start earlier to avoid the traffic. 我们会早点开始，以避免交通堵塞。

虚拟语气:
If I had left earlier, I wouldn't have missed the train. 如果我早点离开，我就不会错过火车了。
I wish I had arrived earlier. 我希望我早点到达。

被动语态:
The meeting was finished earlier than scheduled. 会议比预定的时间提前结束。
The work was completed earlier than expected. 工作比预期提前完成。

疑问句:
Did you arrive earlier than planned? 你比计划的早到吗？
Were they able to finish earlier today? 他们今天能早点完成吗？

否定句:
I didn't arrive earlier than usual. 我并没有比平时早到。
He didn't start earlier than he planned. 他并没有比他计划的早开始。
