kitesurfer:/ˈkaɪtsɜːrfər/| n. |风筝冲浪者|A person who kitesurfs.  Someone who participates in the sport of kitesurfing, using a kite to propel themselves across water on a board.|例句：He's an experienced kitesurfer, able to handle strong winds. 他是一位经验丰富、能够应对强风的滑翔伞运动员。 He is a professional kitesurfer who travels the world. 他是一位职业风筝冲浪运动员，周游世界。|近义词：kitesurfing enthusiast, windsurfer (related but different)|反义词:None
