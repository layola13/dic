rims:/rɪmz/ | n. | 轮辋，边缘 | The outer edge of a wheel; a circular border or edge of something.  |例句：The car's rims were scratched. 这辆车的轮辋被刮花了。


例句：The artist painted a beautiful rim around the picture.艺术家在画的周围画了一个美丽的边框。


近义词：flanges, borders, edges | 反义词：center, middle


词源:  The word "rim" comes from Old English *rim, meaning "border, edge."  It's related to words in other Germanic languages with similar meanings.
