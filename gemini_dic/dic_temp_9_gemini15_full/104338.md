juvenilely:/ˈdʒuːvənaɪli/ | adv. |幼稚地；孩子气地 |In a way that is characteristic of a young person; childishly.  The argument was settled juvenilely with a playground-style shoving match.| 近义词：childishly, immaturely, childishly,  infantilely | 反义词：maturely, adultly, sensibly


例句：

He behaved juvenilely, throwing a tantrum when he didn't get his way. 他表现得很幼稚，因为没有得到他想要的东西而大发脾气。

She reacted juvenilely to the criticism, bursting into tears. 她对批评的反应很幼稚，哭了起来。


词源：

源自于形容词juvenile，最终源自于拉丁语juvenis，意思是“年轻的”。
