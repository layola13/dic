neutralizing: /ˈnjuːtrəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 中和；使无效；抵消 |  To make something ineffective or harmless; to counteract the effect of something. |


例句：
The antidote neutralized the poison.  = 解毒剂中和了毒药。

The army neutralized the enemy's attack. = 军队抵消了敌人的进攻。


近义词：counteracting, canceling out, offsetting, nullifying


反义词：reinforcing, intensifying, exacerbating


词性：动词
