recurrences:/rɪˈkɜːrənsɪz/ | n. |复发；再现；重复出现 | The recurrence of the problem forced us to rethink our strategy.  问题的反复出现迫使我们重新考虑我们的策略。|近义词：repetition, reappearance, return |反义词：uniqueness, singularity


USAGE->

一般现在时:
The problem has recurrences every year. = 这个问题每年都会复发。
Recurrences of the disease are common. = 这种疾病的复发很常见。

一般过去时:
There were several recurrences of the earthquake. = 地震发生了好几次。
The doctor monitored for recurrences of the infection. = 医生监测感染的复发情况。

现在完成时:
There have been several recurrences of the symptoms. = 症状已经复发了好几次。
We have witnessed many recurrences of this historical event. = 我们目睹了这一历史事件的多次重演。

过去完成时:
Before the final diagnosis, there had been several recurrences of the pain. = 在最终诊断之前，疼痛已经复发了好几次。
By then, there had been no recurrences of the illness. = 到那时，疾病已经没有复发了。


将来时:
Doctors predict further recurrences of the disease. = 医生预测该疾病将进一步复发。
We expect recurrences of this pattern in the coming years. = 我们预计这种模式在未来几年还会出现。

虚拟语气:
If there were any recurrences, we would immediately begin treatment. = 如果有任何复发，我们将立即开始治疗。
I wish there were no recurrences of this tragic event. = 我希望不会再发生这样的悲剧。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:

The patient showed no recurrences of the infection. = 病人没有出现感染复发。
