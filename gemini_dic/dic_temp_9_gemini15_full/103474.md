jobbing:/ˈdʒɒbɪŋ/| n., v.| 零活工作；临时工作；找零活；从事临时工作 |  Doing odd jobs or temporary work; finding or undertaking such work. |例句：He makes a living by jobbing around the neighborhood. 他靠在附近做零活为生。| 近义词：odd job, casual work, temporary work | 反义词：permanent employment, full-time job | 名词，动词
