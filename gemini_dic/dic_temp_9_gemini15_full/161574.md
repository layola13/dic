potteries:/ˈpɒtəriːz/| n.|陶器；陶瓷制品；陶器厂；制陶业|Places where pottery is made or sold, or the pottery itself.  The word refers to both the factories and the products.|例句：The village is famous for its traditional potteries. 这个村庄以其传统的陶器而闻名。

例句：She collects antique potteries from all over the world. 她收集来自世界各地的古董陶器。


近义词：ceramics, earthenware, stoneware


反义词：无


词性：名词
