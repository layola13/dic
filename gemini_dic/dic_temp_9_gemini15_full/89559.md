honorless:/ˈɑːnərles/| adj.|不光彩的，不名誉的，无荣誉感的|Lacking honor; shameful or disgraceful.  It describes a person, action, or situation devoid of honor, integrity, or respect.|例句：His honorless actions led to his dismissal from the company. 他不光彩的行为导致他被公司解雇。

例句：The honorless treaty was quickly broken.  这份不光彩的条约很快就被撕毁了。|近义词：dishonorable, shameful, disgraceful, ignominious|反义词：honorable, respectable, glorious, reputable|词性：形容词

词源:
The word "honorless" is derived from the Old English word "honor," meaning "honor, respect, or dignity," combined with the suffix "-less," indicating the absence or lack of something.  The evolution of the word reflects the societal importance placed on honor throughout history.
