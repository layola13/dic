nephelodometer:/nɪˈfɛləˈdɒmɪtər/ | n. |浊度计，霞度计 |An instrument for measuring the density of clouds or the opacity of a liquid.  It measures the amount of light scattered by particles in a sample, which is related to the concentration of those particles. | Example: The nephelodometer was used to determine the turbidity of the water sample.  浊度计被用来测定水样的浊度。  The researchers used a nephelodometer to study cloud formation in the atmosphere. 研究人员使用浊度计来研究大气中的云层形成。| 近义词：turbidimeter, opacimeter | 反义词： (no direct antonym, as it measures a property rather than having an opposite) | 名词
