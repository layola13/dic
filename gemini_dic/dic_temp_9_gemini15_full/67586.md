extraterrestrial:/ˌɛkstrəˈtɛrɪstriəl/ | adj., n. | 地球外的；外星的；外星人 | Relating to or originating from outside the earth or its atmosphere.  An extraterrestrial being is a hypothetical life form from another planet. |例句：The movie depicts an encounter with extraterrestrial life.  这部电影描绘了与外星生命相遇的情景。

例句：Scientists are searching for evidence of extraterrestrial intelligence. 科学家们正在寻找地外智慧生命的证据。


近义词：alien, extraplanetary | 反义词：terrestrial


词性:  adj., n.

词源:
The word "extraterrestrial" combines the prefix "extra-" (meaning "outside" or "beyond") with "terrestrial" (meaning "of or relating to the earth").  Therefore, it literally means "outside the earth."  "Terrestrial" itself comes from the Latin word "terrestris,"  from "terra," meaning "earth."
