harvests:/ˈhɑːrvɪsts/ | n. | 收获；收成 | The process of gathering a crop from a field; the gathered crop itself.  It can also refer to the results or benefits gained from an effort. | Example: The farmer celebrated a bountiful harvest.  农民庆祝丰收。

The autumn harvest was plentiful this year. 今年秋收很丰盛。|近义词：crop, yield, gathering |反义词：famine, scarcity


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farmer harvests his wheat every year. = 农民每年收割他的小麦。
She harvests the benefits of her hard work. = 她收获着她努力工作的成果。


一般过去时:
They harvested the corn last week. = 他们上周收割了玉米。
He harvested a large crop of apples. = 他收获了大量的苹果。


现在完成时:
We have harvested all the rice. = 我们已经收割了所有的稻米。
The company has harvested significant profits this quarter. = 公司本季度获得了可观的利润。


过去完成时:
They had harvested the grapes before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前他们已经收割了葡萄。
The farmers had harvested the entire field by sunset. = 到日落时分，农民们已经收割了整个田地。


将来时:
We will harvest the soybeans next month. = 我们下个月将收割大豆。
They will harvest the benefits of their investment in the future. = 他们将来会收获投资的回报。


虚拟语气:
If we harvested earlier, we would have avoided the storm damage. = 如果我们早点收割，就能避免暴风雨造成的损失。
I wish we had harvested the wheat before the rain started. = 我真希望我们在下雨前就把小麦收割了。


被动语态:
The crops were harvested by machines. = 庄稼是由机器收割的。
The profits will be harvested by the shareholders. = 利润将由股东们获得。


疑问句:
When will you harvest your potatoes? = 你什么时候收割土豆？
Have they harvested the barley yet? = 他们收割大麦了吗？


否定句:
They didn't harvest the beans this year. = 他们今年没有收割豆子。
We haven't harvested the entire field yet. = 我们还没有收割整个田地。
