caviller:/kæˈvɪlə(r)/| v.|吹毛求疵者；挑剔者 |To find fault with; criticize; to raise trivial objections.|例句：He's always cavilling at minor details. 他总是对细枝末节吹毛求疵。|例句：Don't cavil over such small matters. 不要为这些小事挑剔。|近义词：criticize, carp, quibble |反义词：compliment, praise |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He cavils at everything. = 他对一切都吹毛求疵。
She constantly cavils about the food. = 她总是对食物吹毛求疵。

一般过去时:
He cavilled at the wording of the contract. = 他对合同措辞吹毛求疵。
They cavilled about the price. = 他们对价格吹毛求疵。

现在完成时:
He has cavilled at every suggestion I've made. = 他对我提出的每一个建议都吹毛求疵。
She has cavilled at my work for weeks. = 她已经对我的工作吹毛求疵了好几个星期了。

过去完成时:
He had cavilled at the plan before the meeting. = 他在会议前就对这个计划吹毛求疵了。
They had cavilled about the details for hours. = 他们已经为细节问题争论了好几个小时了。

将来时:
He will cavil at the new rules. = 他会对新规吹毛求疵。
They will cavil about the cost. = 他们会对成本吹毛求疵。

虚拟语气:
If he were here, he would cavil at everything. = 如果他在的话，他会对所有事情吹毛求疵。
I wish he wouldn't cavil so much. = 我希望他不那么吹毛求疵。

被动语态:
His work was cavilled at by the boss. = 他的作品受到了老板的批评。
The plan was cavilled at by several members of the committee. = 这个计划受到了委员会中几名成员的批评。

疑问句:
Does he often cavil at small things? = 他经常为小事吹毛求疵吗？
Why does she always cavil at my ideas? = 她为什么总是批评我的想法？

否定句:
He didn't cavil at the suggestion. = 他没有对这个建议吹毛求疵。
She didn't cavil at the extra work. = 她不抱怨额外的工作。


PHRASE->
