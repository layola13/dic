internationalizes:/ˌɪntərˌnæʃənəlaɪzɪz/| v. |国际化；使具有国际性|To give something an international character or scope; to make something relevant or applicable across national borders.|The company internationalizes its marketing strategy to reach a wider audience.  公司将其营销策略国际化以接触更广泛的受众。|globalize, globalise|localize, nationalize|动词
