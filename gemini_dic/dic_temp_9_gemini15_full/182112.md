reminiscing:/ˌrɛmɪˈnɪsɪŋ/| v.|回忆，缅怀|The act of recalling past experiences, events, or people, often with pleasure or fondness.  It often involves sharing these memories with others. |例句：We spent the evening reminiscing about our childhood adventures. 我们整个晚上都在回忆童年冒险经历。

例句：She sat by the fire, reminiscing about her time in Paris. 她坐在火炉旁，回忆她在巴黎的时光。


近义词：recalling, remembering, recollecting, musing, reflecting


反义词：forgetting, ignoring


词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reminisces about his college days. = 他回忆他的大学时光。
They often reminisce about their travels. = 他们经常回忆他们的旅行。


一般过去时:
She reminisced about her grandmother's stories. = 她回忆起她祖母的故事。
We reminisced for hours about old friends. = 我们花了几个小时回忆老朋友。


现在完成时:
I have reminisced about this trip many times. = 我已经多次回忆起这次旅行。
They have reminisced about their childhood together. = 他们已经一起回忆了他们的童年。


过去完成时:
He had reminisced about his youth before he died. = 他在去世前回忆了他的青春。
She had reminisced about the party before falling asleep. = 她睡着前回忆了派对。


将来时:
We will reminisce about this day in years to come. = 我们将在未来的几年里回忆这一天。
They will reminisce about their wedding anniversary. = 他们将回忆他们的结婚周年纪念日。


虚拟语气:
If I could reminisce, I would tell you about my adventures. = 如果我能回忆，我会告诉你我的冒险经历。
I wish I could reminisce with my grandfather again. = 我希望我能再次与我的祖父回忆往事。


被动语态:
His stories were reminisced about by many people. = 他的故事被许多人回忆。
The event was reminisced about fondly. = 这件事被人们 fondly 回忆。


疑问句:
Do you often reminisce about the past? = 你经常回忆过去吗？
Will you reminisce about your childhood at the reunion? = 你会在聚会上回忆你的童年吗？


否定句:
He doesn't like to reminisce about difficult times. = 他不喜欢回忆艰难的时光。
She didn't reminisce about her breakup. = 她没有回忆她的分手。


PHRASE->
reminisce about = 回忆…
reminisce over = 细细回忆…
fondly reminisce = 深情回忆
happily reminisce = 开心地回忆
bitterly reminisce = 苦涩地回忆
