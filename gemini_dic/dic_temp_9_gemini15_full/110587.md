limiter:/ˈlɪmɪtər/| n. |限制器，限幅器|A device or process that limits or restricts something, such as power, speed, or amplitude.|The audio limiter prevented the speakers from being damaged by excessively loud sounds.  音频限幅器防止扬声器被过大的声音损坏。|The speed limiter on the car kept us from going over the speed limit. 汽车上的速度限制器防止我们超速。|regulator, restrictor, controller|accelerator, amplifier|名词
