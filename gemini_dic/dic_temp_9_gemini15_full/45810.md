custom's:/ˈkʌstəmz/| n. |海关；风俗习惯；客户；顾客|Customs refers to the government department responsible for collecting taxes on imported goods and controlling the flow of goods and people across international borders.  It can also refer to the usual practices or ways of behaving in a particular society, group, or place.  Finally, it can be a shortened form of "customer's," referring to something belonging to a customer.|例句：I had to go through customs at the airport. 我必须在机场通过海关。

例句：The customs of that country are very different from ours. 那个国家的风俗习惯与我们的截然不同。
