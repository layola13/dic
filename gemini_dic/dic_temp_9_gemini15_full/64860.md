espousement: /ɪˈspaʊzmənt/ | n. | 拥护；支持；婚姻；结合 | The act of espousing or embracing a cause, belief, or person; marriage.  |例句：His espousal of radical views alienated some of his former supporters.  他的激进观点使他的一些前任支持者疏远了他。


例句：The couple's espousement was a joyous occasion. 这对夫妇的婚礼是一个快乐的场合。


近义词：support, advocacy, marriage, union | 反义词：rejection, opposition, divorce, separation | 词性: 名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government espouses policies of economic reform. = 政府支持经济改革政策。
He espouses a belief in the power of positive thinking. = 他拥护积极思考的力量。


一般过去时:
The church espoused the cause of the poor. = 教堂支持穷人的事业。
They espoused a new philosophy of education. = 他们采纳了一种新的教育理念。


现在完成时:
She has espoused many different causes throughout her life. = 她一生中支持过许多不同的事业。
He has espoused a new political party. = 他已经加入了一个新的政党。


过去完成时:
Before the war, they had espoused pacifism. = 在战争爆发前，他们信奉和平主义。
They had espoused a more traditional approach to management. = 他们曾采用过更传统的管理方法。


将来时:
She will espouse the principles of fairness and justice. = 她将支持公平与正义的原则。
He will espouse his ideas in a public forum. = 他将在公开场合阐述他的观点。


虚拟语气:
If he espoused my views, I would be very happy. = 如果他支持我的观点，我会非常高兴。
I wish he would espouse a more moderate approach. = 我希望他能采取更温和的策略。


被动语态:
His ideas were espoused by many people. = 他的想法得到了许多人的拥护。
The new policy was espoused by the majority of the board members. = 新政策得到了大多数董事会的成员的支持。


疑问句:
Does he espouse any particular political ideology? = 他支持任何特定的政治意识形态吗？
What causes does she espouse? = 她支持什么事业？


否定句:
He did not espouse the cause of social reform. = 他不支持社会改革事业。
They did not espouse the views of the opposition party. = 他们不支持反对党的观点。


PHRASE->
espouse a cause = 支持一项事业
espouse a belief = 拥护一种信仰
espouse a theory = 拥护一种理论
espouse a policy = 支持一项政策
espouse a principle = 拥护一项原则
