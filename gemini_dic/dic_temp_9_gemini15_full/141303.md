open-mouthedly:/ˈoʊpənˈmaʊθɪdli/| adv.|张着嘴地，目瞪口呆地|Describes an action performed with one's mouth open, often expressing surprise, shock, or awe.|例句：He stared open-mouthedly at the magician's trick. 他目瞪口呆地看着魔术师的戏法。|例句：She listened open-mouthedly to the incredible story. 她张着嘴听着这个不可思议的故事。|近义词：astonishedly, amazedly, dumbfoundedly|反义词：calmly, composedly|副词
