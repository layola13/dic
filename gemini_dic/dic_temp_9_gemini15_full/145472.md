overclassifying: /ˌoʊvərˈklæsɪfaɪɪŋ/ | v. |过度分类|The act of classifying something too broadly or into too many categories, often leading to inaccuracies or a loss of nuance.  It often implies a lack of precision or an oversimplification of complex data.|例句：Overclassifying these insects as simply "beetles" ignores the diversity within the order Coleoptera.  过度地将这些昆虫简单地分类为“甲虫”忽略了鞘翅目昆虫内部的多样性。

例句：The company's overclassifying of customer data resulted in inaccurate marketing campaigns. 公司对客户数据的过度分类导致了不准确的营销活动。


近义词：overcategorization, misclassification, oversimplification | 反义词：precise classification, nuanced categorization | 词性：动词
