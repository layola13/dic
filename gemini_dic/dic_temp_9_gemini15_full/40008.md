consulting: /kənˈsʌltɪŋ/ | n. |咨询；顾问服务 | The act of seeking expert advice or information.  It can also refer to the business of providing such advice. |例句：She's seeking consulting on her business strategy. 她正在寻求关于其商业战略的咨询。 |近义词：advising, advising, guidance, counsel |反义词：ignoring, neglecting


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He consults a doctor regularly. = 他定期去看医生。
The company consults with its clients on marketing plans. = 公司就营销计划与客户进行咨询。


一般过去时:
They consulted a lawyer about their legal problem. = 他们就法律问题咨询了一位律师。
I consulted the dictionary for the meaning of the word. = 我查字典以了解该词的意思。


现在完成时:
She has consulted several experts on this matter. = 她就此事咨询了几位专家。
We have consulted with the stakeholders multiple times. = 我们已经多次与利益相关者进行了协商。


过去完成时:
They had consulted with their team before making a decision. = 他们在做决定之前已经与团队进行了协商。
She had consulted a map before embarking on her journey. = 她出发前已经查阅了地图。


将来时:
I will consult my accountant about my taxes. = 我将咨询我的会计师关于我的税务问题。
They will consult an expert before starting the project. = 他们将在开始项目之前咨询专家。


虚拟语气:
If I needed advice, I would consult a professional. = 如果我需要建议，我会咨询专业人士。
I wish I had consulted a specialist sooner. = 我真希望我早点咨询专家。


被动语态:
The client was consulted before the final decision was made. = 在做出最终决定之前，已经咨询了客户。
The architect's design was widely consulted before implementation. = 在实施之前，建筑师的设计得到了广泛的咨询。


疑问句:
Will you consult an expert on this issue? = 你会就此问题咨询专家吗？
Did they consult anyone before making the changes? = 他们在做更改之前咨询过任何人吗？


否定句:
He didn't consult anyone before acting. = 他行动前没有咨询任何人。
They didn't consult the manual properly. = 他们没有正确查阅手册。


PHRASE->
management consulting = 管理咨询
business consulting = 商业咨询
financial consulting = 金融咨询
strategic consulting = 战略咨询
IT consulting = IT 咨询

词源:
The word "consulting" comes from the verb "consult," which originates from the Latin word "consulere," meaning "to deliberate, take counsel, or confer."  The suffix "-ing" transforms the verb into a gerund, functioning as a noun.
