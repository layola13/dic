lightering:/ˈlaɪtərɪŋ/| n. |轻载；卸货；减轻重量|The process of transferring cargo from a larger vessel to a smaller one, or vice versa, often for easier access to port or shallower waters.  It can also refer to the reduction of weight or load, such as on a ship or aircraft.|例句：The lightering of the cargo was necessary due to the shallow draft of the river.  货物需要轻载，因为河道水浅。

The lightering operation took several hours. 卸货作业花了几个小时。|近义词：unloading, transferring, reducing|反义词：loading, increasing
