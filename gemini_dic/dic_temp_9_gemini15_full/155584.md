philopolemic: /ˌfɪləʊpəˈliːmɪk/ | adj. |好争论的；好辩论的 |  Loving argument or controversy; fond of debate.  The word combines "philo-" (love) and "polemic" (controversy, debate). It describes someone who enjoys engaging in arguments and debates. |  He's quite philopolemic, always ready for a spirited discussion. 他相当好辩，总是准备好进行一场热烈的讨论。| 近义词：argumentative, disputatious, contentious | 反义词：conciliatory, peaceable, uncontroversial |
