mechanise: /ˈmekənaɪz/ | vt. | 机械化；使机械化 | To equip (a process, industry, etc.) with machinery; to make mechanical.  | 例句：The factory mechanised its production line to increase efficiency. 工厂将其生产线机械化以提高效率。
The company is planning to mechanise its accounting department. 公司计划将其会计部门机械化。|近义词：automate, motorize | 反义词：manualize, demechanize | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mechanise the process to save labour costs. = 他们使这个过程机械化以节省劳动力成本。
We mechanise our farming practices every year. = 我们每年都使我们的农业生产实践机械化。

一般过去时:
The factory mechanised its operations last year. = 工厂去年将其运营机械化了。
They mechanised the entire assembly line. = 他们将整个装配线机械化了。

现在完成时:
They have mechanised many aspects of their business. = 他们已经将其业务的许多方面机械化了。
The company has mechanised its data processing system. = 该公司已经将其数据处理系统机械化了。

过去完成时:
They had mechanised the factory before the new regulations were implemented. = 新规实施之前，他们已经将工厂机械化了。
The farm had mechanised its harvesting process by the time the season began. = 到季节开始时，该农场已经将其收割过程机械化了。

将来时:
We will mechanise the warehouse next year. = 我们明年将把仓库机械化。

被动语态:

疑问句:

词源:
The word "mechanise" comes from the French word "mécaniser," which itself is derived from the Greek word "mēkhanē" (μηχανή), meaning "machine" or "contrivance."  The suffix "-ise" is a common English suffix used to form verbs meaning "to make or become."
