endocrinology:/ˌɛn.dəʊ.krɪˈnɒl.ə.dʒi/ /ˌɛn.doʊ.krɪˈnɑː.lə.dʒi/| n. |内分泌学|The branch of physiology and medicine concerned with endocrine glands and hormones.  It studies the structure and function of these glands and the hormones they produce, as well as the effects of hormone imbalances on the body.|例句：She is studying endocrinology at a prestigious university. 她在一所著名的大学学习内分泌学。|例句：Advances in endocrinology have led to better treatments for many hormonal disorders. 内分泌学的进步导致了许多激素紊乱的更好治疗方法。|近义词：hormonology|反义词：N/A|名词
