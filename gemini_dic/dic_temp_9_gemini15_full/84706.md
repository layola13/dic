handicapped:/ˈhændiˌkæpt/| adj.|残疾的，有缺陷的|  A person with a physical or mental disability.  This word is considered outdated and potentially offensive by many.  It refers to someone who has a physical or mental impairment that limits their abilities.  More respectful and inclusive language is preferred. |The handicapped parking space is reserved for people with disabilities.|近义词：disabled, impaired|反义词：abled, unimpaired


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is handicapped by his poor eyesight. = 他因视力不好而行动不便。
The team is handicapped by the lack of funding. =  该团队因缺乏资金而处于劣势。


一般过去时:
She was handicapped by a childhood illness. = 她因儿时患病而留下残疾。
The project was handicapped by delays. =  该项目因延误而受阻。


现在完成时:
He has been handicapped by his injury for years. = 他多年来一直因受伤而行动不便。
The company has been handicapped by the economic downturn. =  该公司因经济低迷而受损。


过去完成时:
They had been handicapped by the lack of resources before the project started. =  在项目开始之前，他们就因缺乏资源而处于劣势。
She had been handicapped by her shyness throughout her life. = 她一生都因害羞而处于不利地位。


将来时:
He will be handicapped by his lack of experience in the new job. = 在新工作中，他将因缺乏经验而处于劣势。
The team will be handicapped by the absence of their star player. =  该团队将因其明星球员缺席而处于劣势。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't handicapped, he could participate in the marathon. = 如果他没有残疾，他就能参加马拉松比赛。
If the team hadn't been handicapped by injuries, they would have won the game. = 如果该团队没有因伤病而受损，他们就会赢得比赛。


被动语态:
The athletes were handicapped by the unfair rules. = 运动员们受到了不公平规则的限制。
The negotiations were handicapped by a lack of trust between the parties. =  谈判因双方缺乏信任而受阻。


疑问句:
Is he handicapped in any way? = 他有什么残疾吗？
Was the team handicapped by the weather conditions? =  该团队是否受到天气状况的影响？


否定句:
He is not handicapped by his age. = 他并没有因为年龄而受限。
The project wasn't handicapped by the budget cuts. = 该项目并未因预算削减而受阻。
