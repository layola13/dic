farms:/fɑːrmz/| n.|农场；农庄|A farm is an area of land used for growing crops or raising livestock.  It is often a place of rural life, involving agricultural practices and the raising of animals.|例句：We visited a dairy farm and saw many cows. 我们参观了一个奶牛场，看到了许多奶牛。|例句：My grandfather worked on a small farm his entire life. 我祖父一生都在一个小农场工作。|近义词：ranch, plantation, estate|反义词：city, urban area|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The farm produces a lot of wheat. = 这个农场生产很多小麦。
They farm this land every year. = 他们每年耕种这块地。

一般过去时:
He farmed that land for many years. = 他耕种那块地很多年了。
The farm yielded a poor harvest last year. = 这个农场去年收成很差。


现在完成时:
They have farmed this land for generations. = 他们世世代代耕种这块土地。
The farm has expanded significantly in recent years. = 近年来，这个农场规模已显著扩大。


过去完成时:
They had farmed the land before the drought hit. = 干旱来临之前，他们已经耕种了这片土地。
The farm had already been sold before we arrived. = 我们到达之前，农场已经被卖掉了。


将来时:
We will visit a strawberry farm next week. = 我们下周将参观一个草莓农场。
They will farm more organically in the future. = 他们将来会更注重有机耕作。


虚拟语气:
If I owned a farm, I would grow organic vegetables. = 如果我拥有一个农场，我会种植有机蔬菜。
I wish I had a farm in the countryside. = 我真希望在乡下有一个农场。


被动语态:
The land is farmed by local farmers. = 这块地由当地农民耕种。
The farm was damaged by the storm. = 农场被暴风雨破坏了。


疑问句:
Do you work on a farm? = 你在农场工作吗？
What kind of crops are farmed here? = 这里种植什么庄稼？


否定句:
He doesn't own a farm. = 他不拥有农场。
They didn't farm the land properly. = 他们没有正确地耕种土地。
