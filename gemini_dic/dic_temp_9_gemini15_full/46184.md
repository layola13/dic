cypress:/ˈsaɪprəs/| n. |柏树；柏木|A cypress is an evergreen coniferous tree belonging to the genus Cupressus.  They are known for their distinctive, often pyramidal shape and aromatic wood. They are often used in landscaping and have symbolic meanings in various cultures. |Example: The old cypress tree stood sentinel over the graveyard. 这棵古老的柏树守护着墓地。|近义词：cedar, juniper|反义词：无
