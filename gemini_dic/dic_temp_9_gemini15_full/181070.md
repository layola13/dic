reindeer: /ˈreɪndɪr/ | n. |驯鹿| A deer with large antlers, found in arctic and subarctic regions.  Reindeer are known for their ability to survive in extremely cold climates and their role in many indigenous cultures. They are also called caribou in North America. |Example:  The reindeer pulled Santa's sleigh across the snowy landscape.  驯鹿拉着圣诞老人的雪橇穿过雪景。

The reindeer herded together for warmth during the blizzard. 驯鹿在暴风雪中聚集在一起取暖。|近义词：caribou (in North America)|反义词：无|名词
