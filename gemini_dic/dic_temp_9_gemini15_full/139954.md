odontography:/ˌɒdənˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 牙科学；牙齿描绘术 | The scientific study of teeth, including their structure, development, and diseases.  It also refers to the process of recording or describing teeth, often through drawings or images. | Example:  The odontography of ancient civilizations reveals much about their diet and lifestyle.  古代文明的牙科学揭示了他们饮食和生活方式的许多信息。|近义词：dental science, dentistry |反义词：无
