pipe up:/paɪp ʌp/| v.|突然大声说话；插嘴|To speak suddenly and loudly, often to interrupt or to join a conversation.|例句：He suddenly piped up with a question. 他突然大声问了一个问题。


例句：Several people piped up in agreement.  几个人大声表示同意。


近义词：speak out, chime in, butt in


反义词：be silent, keep quiet


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He pipes up at every meeting. 他每次会议都插嘴。
They pipe up with complaints regularly. 他们经常大声抱怨。


一般过去时:
She piped up with a witty remark. 她突然插嘴说了一句俏皮话。
He piped up during the teacher's explanation. 他在老师讲解的时候插嘴。


现在完成时:
He's piped up several times already this morning. 他今天早上已经好几次插嘴了。
She's piped up with objections to the plan. 她已经多次提出反对该计划的意见。


过去完成时:
Before I could answer, he'd piped up with his own opinion.  在我开口之前，他已经插嘴表达了自己的意见。
They had piped up their concerns earlier in the meeting. 他们在会议较早的时候就大声表达了他们的担忧。


将来时:
He will pipe up when he disagrees. 他不同意的时候就会插嘴。
I think she'll pipe up with a suggestion soon. 我认为她很快就会插嘴提个建议。


虚拟语气:
If he piped up now, it would be very disruptive. 如果他现在插嘴，将会非常扰乱秩序。
I wish he hadn't piped up like that. 我真希望他不要那样插嘴。


被动语态: (Pipe up is rarely used in the passive voice.)


疑问句:
Did anyone pipe up with a solution? 有没有人大声提出解决方案？
Will anyone pipe up during the presentation?  在演示过程中会有人插嘴吗？


否定句:
He didn't pipe up during the entire meeting.  在整个会议期间，他都没有插嘴。
She didn't pipe up when they criticized her work. 当他们批评她的工作时，她没有反驳。


PHRASE->
pipe up and say  大声说出来
pipe up in protest 大声抗议
pipe up with an answer 大声回答
pipe up in support 大声支持
pipe up with a suggestion 大声建议
