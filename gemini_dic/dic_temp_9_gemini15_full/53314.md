disabler: /dɪsˈeɪblər/ | n. |使丧失能力者；使无效者；禁用程序| A person or thing that disables or renders something ineffective.  It can refer to a person who causes disability, a device that disables something, or a software program that disables other programs or functions. |例句：The virus acted as a disabler, shutting down the entire network.  病毒就像一个禁用程序，关闭了整个网络。  The bomb was a powerful disabler, rendering the enemy base unusable. 炸弹是一个强大的使丧失能力者，使敌方基地无法使用。|近义词：incapacitator, invalidator, deactivator |反义词：enabler
