mentioning: /ˈmɛnʃənɪŋ/ | v. |提及；说起；谈到 | The act of mentioning something; bringing something to someone's attention.  |例句：He mentioned going to the beach. 他提到了去海滩。|例句：She mentioned her new job in passing. 她顺便提到了她的新工作。|近义词：referring to, alluding to, remarking on |反义词：ignoring, overlooking


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are mentioning the project in the meeting. = 他们在会议上提到这个项目。
He mentions his trip to Paris frequently. = 他经常提到他去巴黎的旅行。

一般过去时:
She mentioned seeing him yesterday. = 她提到昨天见过他。
They mentioned the problem to their boss. = 他们向老板提到了这个问题。

现在完成时:
Have you mentioned the deadline to them? = 你已经向他们提到截止日期了吗？
He has mentioned this issue several times. = 他已经多次提到这个问题了。

过去完成时:
She had mentioned the possibility before the meeting. = 会议前她已经提到了这种可能性。
They had mentioned their concerns earlier. = 他们之前已经提到了他们的担忧。

将来时:
I will mention it to him tomorrow. = 我明天会向他提起这件事。
She will mention the new policy at the next meeting. = 她将在下次会议上提及这项新政策。

虚拟语气:
If I had mentioned it earlier, it might have been different. = 如果我早点提到它，情况可能会有所不同。
I wish I had mentioned my concerns. = 我希望我提到了我的担忧。

被动语态:
The issue was mentioned briefly in the report. = 报告中简要提到了这个问题。
The problem has been mentioned repeatedly. =这个问题已经被反复提及。

疑问句:
Did she mention anything about the party? = 她有提到关于派对的任何事情吗？
Has he mentioned when he'll be back? = 他有提到他什么时候回来吗？

否定句:
He didn't mention anything about his family. = 他没有提到任何关于他家人的事情。
She didn't mention the difficult parts of the job. = 她没有提到工作的难处。


PHRASE->
Worth mentioning = 值得一提
It's worth mentioning that...=值得一提的是...
Not to mention = 更不用说
Let me mention that...=让我提一下...
I must mention that...=我必须提到...
