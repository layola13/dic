collaterally:/kəˈlætərəli/ | adv. | 附带地；间接地；作为抵押 |  In a way that is secondary or incidental to the main purpose.  It refers to something that happens alongside the main event, but not as its primary focus. |例句：The company's losses were collaterally damaged by the economic downturn.  公司的损失是经济衰退的间接后果。

The new policy collaterally affected small businesses. 这项新政策间接影响了小型企业。


近义词：incidentally, indirectly, secondarily, concomitantly | 反义词：directly, primarily, principally | 词性:副词
