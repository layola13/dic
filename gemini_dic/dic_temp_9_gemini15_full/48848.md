deforested:/ˌdiːˈfɒrɪstɪd/|/ˈdiːfɔːrɛstɪd/| adj. |砍伐森林的；森林被砍伐的|Cleared of forests or trees.  The adjective describes an area that has undergone deforestation. |例句：The deforested land was barren and unproductive.  这片被砍伐的土地贫瘠且不生产。

The deforested area is now being replanted with native trees. 这片被砍伐的森林区域现在正在重新种植本地树木。


近义词：treeless, barren, denuded|反义词：forested, wooded

词源:
The word "deforested" is derived from the verb "deforest," which is a combination of the prefix "de-" (meaning "to remove" or "to deprive of") and the noun "forest."  The suffix "-ed" indicates the past participle form, often used as an adjective.
