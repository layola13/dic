reexplaining:/ˌriːˈɛkspleɪnɪŋ/ | v. |重新解释，再解释 |The act of explaining something again, often in more detail or a different way to ensure understanding.  It implies a previous explanation that was insufficient or unclear. |例句：After the initial explanation was poorly received, the professor felt compelled to reexplain the complex theory.  教授在最初的解释不受欢迎后，觉得有必要重新解释这个复杂的理论。

例句：The teacher reexplained the problem, using simpler terms this time. 老师用更简单的术语重新解释了这个问题。


近义词：reiterate, clarify, elucidate, expound upon | 反义词：misinterpret, obfuscate | 词性：动词
