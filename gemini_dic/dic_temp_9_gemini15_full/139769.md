octahedrite:/ˈɒkteɪhaɪdraɪt/| n. |八面体陨石|A type of stony-iron meteorite characterized by its octahedral structure, composed of kamacite and taenite intergrown in a pattern resembling intersecting planes.  Octahedrites are one of the three main classes of stony-iron meteorites, the others being pallasites and mesosiderites. They are named for their characteristic octahedral crystalline structure.|The meteorite found in the desert was identified as an octahedrite.|近义词:stony-iron meteorite|反义词:N/A
