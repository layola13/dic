glochidiate:/ɡləˈkɪdiˌeɪt/| adj.|具钩刺的；有芒刺的|Having small, barbed bristles or hooks, like those found on some plants (especially cacti).  The term refers to the presence of glochids, which are minute, barbed bristles that readily detach and cause irritation.|Example: The gardener wore gloves to avoid getting glochidiate cactus spines stuck in their skin.|近义词：barbed, spiny, prickly|反义词：smooth, glabrous
