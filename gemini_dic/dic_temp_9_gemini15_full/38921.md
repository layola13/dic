confessionist:/kənˈfɛʃənɪst/| n. |忏悔者；告解者；承认者|A person who confesses, especially one who makes a confession of faith or sins to a priest or other religious authority.  It can also refer to someone who openly admits to something.|例句：The confessionist poured out his heart to the priest.  忏悔者向牧师倾诉了他的心声。|例句：He was a staunch confessionist, never hiding his beliefs. 他是一位坚定的信仰者，从不隐藏他的信仰。|近义词：penitent, confessor, declarer |反义词：denier, concealer
