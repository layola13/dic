commuted:/kəˈmjuːt/| vt. n.|通勤；减刑；交换；兑换|to travel regularly between one's place of work and home|He commutes to work by train every day. 他每天乘火车上班通勤。|近义词：travel, commute,transport|反义词：stay, remain


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He commutes to the city every day. = 他每天往返于城市通勤。
They commute by bus. = 他们乘公共汽车通勤。


一般过去时:
She commuted to London for five years. = 她往返伦敦通勤了五年。
He commuted by car before the train strike. = 罢工前他开车通勤。


现在完成时:
I have commuted to this office for ten years. = 我已经往返这个办公室通勤十年了。
We have commuted daily since we moved. = 自从我们搬家以来，我们每天都通勤。


过去完成时:
He had commuted for years before he finally bought a house closer to work. = 在他最终买了一套离工作更近的房子之前，他已经通勤多年了。
She had commuted by bike before getting a car. = 在买车之前，她一直骑自行车通勤。


将来时:
I will commute by train next week. = 下周我将乘火车通勤。
They will commute to a new office next year. = 他们明年将通勤到一个新的办公室。


虚拟语气:
If I lived closer, I wouldn't have to commute so far. = 如果我住得近一点，我就不用通勤这么远了。
I wish I didn't have to commute every day. = 我希望我不必每天通勤。


被动语态:
The sentence was commuted to life imprisonment. = 判决被改为无期徒刑。
His prison term was commuted.  = 他的刑期被减轻了。


疑问句:
Do you commute to work? = 你上下班通勤吗？
How long have you been commuting? = 你通勤多久了？


否定句:
He doesn't commute anymore. = 他不再通勤了。
She didn't commute during the pandemic. = 大流行期间她没有通勤。


PHRASE->

commute one's sentence = 减刑

long commute = 长途通勤
