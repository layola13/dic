denationalising: /ˌdiːˌnæʃənəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 去国有化；取消国有化 | The process of transferring ownership or control of a nationalized industry or asset to private hands.  It involves removing government control and influence. | Example: The government is considering denationalising the railway system.  政府正在考虑将铁路系统私有化。 | 近义词：privatizing, de-nationalizing | 反义词：nationalizing, socializing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government is denationalising the postal service. = 政府正在将邮政服务私有化。
They are denationalising several state-owned enterprises. = 他们正在将几家国有企业私有化。


一般过去时:
The company was denationalised in the 1980s. = 这家公司在20世纪80年代被私有化了。
The government denationalised the airlines last year. = 政府去年将航空公司私有化了。


现在完成时:
The country has denationalised most of its industries. = 这个国家已经将其大部分产业私有化了。
They have denationalised the energy sector successfully. = 他们已经成功地将能源部门私有化了。


过去完成时:
Before the reforms, the industry had never been denationalised. = 在改革之前，这个行业从未被私有化过。
The government had already denationalised the banks by then. = 到那时，政府已经将银行私有化了。


将来时:
The government will denationalise the telecoms industry next year. = 政府明年将对电信行业进行私有化。
They will likely denationalise more state-owned assets in the future. = 他们未来可能会对更多国有资产进行私有化。


虚拟语气:
If they denationalised the railways, it might improve efficiency. = 如果他们将铁路私有化，可能会提高效率。
It would be better if the government had not denationalised the mining sector. = 如果政府没有将矿业部门私有化就好了。


被动语态:
The airline was denationalised by the previous government. = 这家航空公司被上届政府私有化了。
Many state-owned companies are being denationalised at present. = 目前许多国有公司正在被私有化。


疑问句:

否定句:
The government has not yet denationalised the water industry. = 政府尚未将水务行业私有化。
They did not denationalise the steel mills as planned. = 他们没有按照计划将钢铁厂私有化。
