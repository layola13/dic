mishna:/ˈmɪʃnə/| n. |米什纳；犹太教口传律法|The Mishna is the first major written compilation of the Oral Torah,  a collection of rabbinic interpretations and discussions of Jewish law. It covers a wide range of topics, including ritual practices, civil law, and ethical teachings. |The study of the Mishna is central to Jewish religious education and practice.  |近义词：Oral Torah, Talmud (partially) |反义词：None
