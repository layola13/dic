butane:/ˈbjuːteɪn/| n. |丁烷|A colorless, odorless, flammable gas that is used as a fuel. It is a hydrocarbon with the chemical formula C₄H₁₀.|例句：Butane is commonly used in lighters and camping stoves. 丁烷通常用于打火机和野营炉具。|例句：The gas leak was caused by a faulty butane canister. 燃气泄漏是由一个有故障的丁烷罐引起的。|近义词：propane,  LPG (liquefied petroleum gas)|反义词：无
