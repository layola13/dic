rehousing: /ˌriːˈhaʊzɪŋ/ | n. |  重新安置；住房的重新分配 | The act of providing new housing for people who have lost their homes, often due to demolition, disaster, or displacement.  It involves the process of finding suitable alternative accommodation and assisting with the relocation. |  例句：The council is undertaking a large-scale rehousing program for families affected by the flood.  市议会正在为受洪水影响的家庭进行大规模的重新安置项目。


例句：The government's rehousing initiative aims to provide affordable housing to low-income families. 政府的重新安置计划旨在为低收入家庭提供经济适用房。


近义词：resettlement, relocation, accommodation


反义词：homelessness, eviction


词性：名词
