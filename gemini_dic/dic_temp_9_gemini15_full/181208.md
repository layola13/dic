reinoculate:/ˌriːɪˈnɒkjʊleɪt/| vt.|再次接种，重新接种|To inoculate again; to introduce (a microorganism) again into a culture medium or a host.|The doctor decided to reinoculate the patient after the first vaccination proved ineffective.|近义词：revaccinate, inoculate again|反义词：deinoculate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reinoculate the plants every spring. = 他们每年春天都给植物重新接种。
The farmer reinoculates his fields with nitrogen-fixing bacteria. = 这位农民用固氮细菌再次接种他的田地。


一般过去时:

The doctor reinoculated the child after the initial dose failed to take. = 在初始剂量无效后，医生再次给孩子接种了疫苗。
