interlobar:/ˌɪntərˈloʊbɑːr/| adj.|叶间的，叶间部的|Situated between lobes.  This term is primarily used in anatomy and medicine, referring to structures or processes that occur between the lobes of an organ, most commonly the lungs or the brain.|例句：The patient presented with interlobar pleural effusion.  患者出现叶间胸腔积液。|例句：The study investigated interlobar septal thickening. 这项研究调查了叶间隔增厚。|近义词：interlobular (sometimes used interchangeably, but interlobar is more common for larger lobes)|反义词：intralobar (within a lobe)|形容词
