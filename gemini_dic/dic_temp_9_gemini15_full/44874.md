cry-baby:/ˈkraɪˌbeɪbi/| n. |爱哭鬼，爱哭的人 |A cry-baby is a person, especially a child, who cries a lot, often for trivial reasons.  This term is often used disparagingly. |例句：He's such a cry-baby; he cries every time he loses a game. 他真是个爱哭鬼，每次输了比赛就哭。|例句：Don't be such a cry-baby;  it's just a little scratch. 别这么爱哭，只是小擦伤而已。|近义词：whiner, complainer, milksop|反义词：tough, stoic, brave|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is a cry-baby. = 他是个爱哭鬼。
She is always a cry-baby. = 她总是爱哭。


一般过去时:
He was a cry-baby when he was young. = 他小时候是个爱哭鬼。
She was such a cry-baby yesterday. = 她昨天真是个爱哭鬼。


现在完成时:
He has been a cry-baby his whole life. = 他一辈子都是个爱哭鬼。
She has always been a cry-baby. = 她一直都是个爱哭鬼。


过去完成时:
He had been a cry-baby before he learned to control his emotions. = 在他学会控制情绪之前，他一直是个爱哭鬼。
She had always been a cry-baby until she started playing sports. = 直到她开始参加体育运动，她一直都是个爱哭鬼。


将来时:
He will still be a cry-baby if he doesn't change his attitude. = 如果他不改变态度，他仍然会是个爱哭鬼。
She will likely be a cry-baby for a while after the accident. = 事故发生后，她可能会哭一阵子。


虚拟语气:
If he weren't such a cry-baby, he'd be more popular. = 如果他不那么爱哭，他会更受欢迎。
I wish she weren't such a cry-baby. = 我希望她不要那么爱哭。


被动语态:  (Cry-baby is rarely used passively)
The child was considered a cry-baby by his classmates. = 这个孩子被他的同学们认为是个爱哭鬼。


疑问句:
Is he a cry-baby? = 他是爱哭鬼吗？
Was she a cry-baby as a child? = 她小时候是爱哭鬼吗？


否定句:
He is not a cry-baby. = 他不是爱哭鬼。
She wasn't a cry-baby, she was just sad. = 她不是爱哭鬼，她只是伤心了。


PHRASE->
big cry-baby:  爱哭鬼 (强调哭得多)
a real cry-baby:  十足的爱哭鬼 (强调程度)
such a cry-baby:  这么一个爱哭鬼 (强调程度)
that cry-baby:  那个爱哭鬼 (特指某人)
poor cry-baby: 可怜的爱哭鬼 (带同情意味)


词源:
The term "cry-baby" is a compound word formed from "cry" (verb meaning to weep) and "baby" (noun meaning a very young child).  It's a descriptive term reflecting the behavior of young children who frequently cry.  The term's usage likely emerged organically in the English language, with no single traceable origin point.
