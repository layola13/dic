irremissive:/ˌɪrɪˈmɪsɪv/| adj. |不可饶恕的，不宽恕的|Unforgiving; not remitting or pardoning.  It implies a refusal to forgive or pardon an offense, often suggesting a harsh or inflexible attitude. |例句：His irremissive anger prevented any reconciliation. 他不可饶恕的怒火阻碍了任何和解。|例句：The judge's irremissive sentence shocked everyone. 法官的严厉判决震惊了所有人。|近义词：unforgiving, implacable, inexorable, relentless |反义词：forgiving, merciful, lenient, clement
