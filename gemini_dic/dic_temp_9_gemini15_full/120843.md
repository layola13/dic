misbalances:/ˌmɪsˈbælənsɪz/| n. |不平衡；失衡；差距|A state of imbalance; a lack of balance or equilibrium.  It can refer to various aspects, including financial, environmental, or even emotional states.  The plural form indicates multiple instances of imbalance.|例句：The misbalances in the global economy are causing widespread concern. 全球经济的不平衡正在引起广泛关注。

例句：The company's financial misbalances led to its bankruptcy.  这家公司的财务失衡导致其破产。


近义词：imbalances, disequilibria, disparities, discrepancies


反义词：balances, equilibrium, harmony, stability


词性:名词
