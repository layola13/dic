/rɪˈpɔːrts/ | n. | 报告，报道 | A formal statement of facts or opinions; a news report.  | The police report detailed the events of the accident.  警察报告详细描述了事故经过。 | account, statement, article |  denial, refutation |  名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He reports the news every evening. = 他每天晚上报道新闻。
She reports directly to the CEO. = 她直接向CEO汇报工作。


一般过去时:
They reported the crime to the police. = 他们向警方报案。
He reported his findings in a detailed document. = 他在一个详细的文件中报告了他的发现。


现在完成时:
She has reported the incident to her supervisor. = 她已向她的主管报告了这一事件。
They have not yet reported their progress. = 他们尚未报告他们的进展。


过去完成时:
He had reported the problem before the meeting. = 他在会议之前已经报告了这个问题。
They had already reported the results when I called. = 我打电话时，他们已经报告了结果。


将来时:
She will report her findings next week. = 她下周将报告她的发现。
He will report to the board of directors tomorrow. = 他明天将向董事会汇报。


虚拟语气:
If he reported the truth, he would be in trouble. = 如果他报告真相，他会惹上麻烦。
I wish he had reported the accident sooner. = 我希望他早点报告事故。


被动语态:
The accident was reported to the authorities. = 事故已向当局报告。
The results will be reported in the next meeting. = 结果将在下次会议上报告。


疑问句:
What did he report to the meeting? = 他在会议上报告了什么？
Did she report the theft? = 她是否报案了？


否定句:
He did not report the incident. = 他没有报告这一事件。
She doesn't report to anyone but the CEO. = 她只向CEO汇报工作，除此之外没有人。


PHRASE->
report on =  就…做报告
report to = 向…汇报
report back = 汇报
file a report = 提交报告
make a report = 做一个报告
