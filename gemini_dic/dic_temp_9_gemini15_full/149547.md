pageboy:/ˈpeɪdʒbɔɪ/| n. |侍童，男童服务员；（妇女发型）盖过耳朵的短发|A pageboy is a young boy employed to run errands or assist in a place such as a hotel or a large house.  It also refers to a hairstyle for women. |例句：He worked as a pageboy at the Grand Hotel. 他在格兰德酒店当侍童。|例句：She got a pageboy haircut. 她剪了个盖过耳朵的短发。|近义词：errand boy, messenger (for the first definition); bob (for the second definition) |反义词：adult (for the first definition); long hair (for the second definition)

词源:
The word "pageboy" originates from the Old French word "page," meaning a boy servant or attendant.  The "-boy" suffix is a straightforward addition clarifying the gender.  The hairstyle's name derives from the association with the hairstyle often worn by pageboys historically.
