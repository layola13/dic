entomotomist:/ˌɛntəˈmɒtəmɪst/| n. |昆虫学家|A person who studies insects.  Entomologists research insect behavior, physiology, ecology, and classification. They may work in academic settings, museums, or government agencies. |Example: The entomotomist carefully examined the specimen under the microscope.  这位昆虫学家仔细地用显微镜检查标本。|近义词：entomologist|反义词：无
