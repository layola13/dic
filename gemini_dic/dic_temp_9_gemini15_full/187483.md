roller-skated:/ˈroʊlərˌskeɪtɪd/ | v. | 溜旱冰，滑旱冰 |  Past tense and past participle of "roller-skate".  It describes the action of having engaged in the activity of roller skating. |  例句：I roller-skated around the park yesterday. = 我昨天在公园里滑旱冰。

例句：They had roller-skated before the rain started. = 下雨前他们已经滑过旱冰了。

近义词：ice-skated (if on ice skates) | 反义词：None (this is a verb describing an action) | 词性: Verb (past tense and past participle)
