hosing: /ˈhoʊzɪŋ/ | n. | 水管喷射；供水系统；住房 | The act of spraying with a hose; a system of water pipes; housing. |例句：The gardener was hosing down the patio. 园丁正在用软管冲洗露台。  The city's inadequate hosing led to water shortages. 这个城市的供水系统不足导致缺水。


近义词：irrigation, watering, spraying, housing (in the sense of providing housing) | 反义词：None directly applicable


词源：源自古英语 *hōs, 指的是“长筒状物”，与现代英语 hose（软管）同源。  “housing” 的含义发展自“提供住所” 的概念，与 “hose” 的形状关联。
