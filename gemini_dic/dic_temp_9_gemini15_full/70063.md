feuar:/ˈfjuːər/| n.|长期地租佃土地的人；领有长期地租土地的人|A person who holds land under a feu.  A feuar holds land under a feudal tenure where they pay a fixed annual rent (feu-duty) to the superior (landowner).  This is a type of long-term leasehold that is distinct from freehold ownership.|例句：The feuars were responsible for maintaining the common areas.  长期地租土地的人负责维护公共区域。  The family has been feuar of this land for generations. 这个家族世世代代都是这块土地的长期地租佃户。|近义词：tenant, leaseholder|反义词：freeholder, owner|名词
