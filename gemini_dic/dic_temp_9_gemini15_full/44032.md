crimsoned:/ˈkrɪmzənd/| adj.|深红色的；绯红的|Turned red, especially in the face, typically as a result of embarrassment, anger, or exertion.|Her cheeks crimsoned with anger.  她的脸颊气得通红。|The sunset crimsoned the sky. 日落把天空染成了深红色。| 近义词：reddened, flushed, blushed| 反义词：blanched, paled
