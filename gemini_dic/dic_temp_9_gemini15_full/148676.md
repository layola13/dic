/ˌoʊvərˈtɪmɪdnɪsəz/| n. |过度胆怯；胆小过度|Excessive timidity; an extreme level of shyness or fearfulness.  This word is not commonly used and is considered somewhat awkward due to its length and the unusual combination of "over" and "timidnesses". It suggests a state where timidity is not just present, but excessively so.|The child's overtimidnesses prevented him from participating in class activities.  孩子的过度胆怯使他无法参加课堂活动。|extreme shyness, excessive fear, apprehension|boldness, courage, bravery|名词
