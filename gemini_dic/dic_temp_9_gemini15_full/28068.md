capering:/ˈkeɪpərɪŋ/| v.|嬉戏，跳跃，蹦跳|To move about in a playful or frisky manner; to leap or skip playfully.|例句：The children were capering about in the garden.孩子们在花园里蹦蹦跳跳。
The lambs were capering in the field. 小羊们在田野里欢快地跳跃。|近义词：frolicking, gamboling, skipping, leaping|反义词：stillness, quietude


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is capering around the room. = 他正在房间里蹦蹦跳跳。
The monkeys caper in the trees. = 猴子们在树上跳跃。


一般过去时:
They capered joyfully at the party. = 他们在派对上高兴地蹦跳。
The goat capered down the hill. = 山羊蹦蹦跳跳地跑下山。


现在完成时:
The children have capered all afternoon. = 孩子们整个下午都在蹦蹦跳跳。
The puppies have been capering since we got home. = 小狗们自从我们回家后就一直在蹦蹦跳跳。


过去完成时:
They had capered for hours before they finally tired. = 他们在最终感到疲倦之前已经蹦跳了好几个小时。
The clown had capered across the stage before the audience arrived. = 小丑在观众到来之前已经在舞台上蹦跳了一阵。


将来时:
The dancers will caper gracefully during the performance. = 舞蹈演员将在表演中优雅地跳跃。
The kids will caper with excitement when they see the presents. = 孩子们看到礼物时会兴奋地蹦跳。


虚拟语气:
If I were a young child again, I would caper all day long. = 如果我再年轻一点，我就会整天蹦蹦跳跳。
I wish I could caper as freely as those children. = 我希望我能像那些孩子一样自由地蹦跳。


被动语态:
(Capering is rarely used in the passive voice because it is an intransitive verb.)


疑问句:
Are the lambs capering in the field? = 小羊们在田野里蹦蹦跳跳吗？
Were the children capering when you arrived? = 你到达时孩子们在蹦蹦跳跳吗？


否定句:
The old man did not caper; he walked slowly. = 老人没有蹦跳；他慢慢地走着。
The injured bird could not caper in the air. = 受伤的鸟儿无法在空中飞舞。
