overwithholding: /ˌoʊvərwɪðˈhoʊldɪŋ/ | n. |过度预扣；过度扣缴|The act of withholding more tax than is legally required.  This often results in a tax refund at the end of the tax year.  |例句：Overwithholding can lead to a large tax refund, but it also means you are essentially lending the government your money interest-free.  过度预扣可能导致大量的退税，但这同时也意味着你实际上是在无息地借钱给政府。

例句：The company's policy of overwithholding resulted in many employees receiving substantial refunds. 公司过度预扣的政策导致许多员工收到巨额退款。


近义词：excess withholding, excessive tax deduction | 反义词：underwithholding
