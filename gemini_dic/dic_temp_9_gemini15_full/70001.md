fetes:/feɪts/| n.|节日，庆祝活动|A fete is a public event, typically held outdoors, that involves games, entertainment, and often food and drink stalls, designed to raise money for a charity or other cause.  It is often associated with a festive atmosphere and community spirit.|The village fete was a great success, raising over £1000 for the local school.|festival, celebration, fair|mourning, sorrow, sadness|名词
