outecho:/ˌaʊtˈɛkoʊ/| v.|回声；重复；模仿|To repeat or imitate (a sound or action), often in a mocking or exaggerated way.  The word is a combination of "out" and "echo," suggesting a more forceful or pronounced repetition.|例句：The comedian's act consisted largely of outechoing the mayor's speeches.  喜剧演员的表演主要内容是模仿市长的演讲。

例句：The protesters outechoed the government's pronouncements with their own slogans.  抗议者用自己的口号回应了政府的声明。


近义词：mimic, echo, repeat, parrot, mock


反义词：originate, innovate, create


词性:动词
