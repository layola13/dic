de-littering:/ˌdiːˈlɪtərɪŋ/ | v. | 清理垃圾，清除垃圾 | The act of removing litter from an area.  It implies a systematic or concerted effort to clean up. |  例句：The volunteers spent the morning de-littering the park. 志愿者们花了上午时间清理公园的垃圾。

例句：The council has launched a new initiative to de-litter the city streets. 市议会发起了一项新的倡议来清理市区街道的垃圾。


近义词：cleaning up, removing litter, litter removal, tidying up

反义词：littering, polluting


词性：动词
