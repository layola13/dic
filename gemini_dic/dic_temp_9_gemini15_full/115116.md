mangetouts:/ˈmæŋɡətuːz/| n.| 法式豆角 | French beans, typically slender and stringless, served whole.  They are often called "sugar snap peas" in the US. |例句：I added some mangetouts to the stir-fry. 我在炒菜里加了一些法式豆角。

例句：The mangetouts were perfectly cooked and tender. 法式豆角煮得恰到好处，很嫩。


近义词：French beans, sugar snap peas, string beans (though string beans can sometimes be stringy, unlike mangetouts)


反义词：无


词性：名词
