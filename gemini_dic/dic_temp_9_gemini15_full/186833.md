ringdove:/ˈrɪŋˌdʌv/| n. |环颈鸠，斑鸠|A type of dove characterized by a white ring around its neck. Ringdoves are known for their gentle cooing sounds.  |例句：The ringdove's soft cooing filled the air.  环颈鸠的轻柔咕咕声充满了空气。

例句：I saw a ringdove perched on the branch of an oak tree. 我看到一只环颈鸠栖息在橡树枝上。


近义词：wood pigeon (in some regions), turtledove


反义词：无


词性:名词
