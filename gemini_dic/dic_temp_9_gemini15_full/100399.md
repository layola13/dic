intersystematically:/ˌɪntərˈsɪstəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 系统间地，跨系统地 |  Acting or occurring between or among different systems.  This adverb highlights the interaction or connection between separate systems. | Example:  The data was analyzed intersystematically to identify correlations between different networks.  数据被系统间地分析以识别不同网络之间的关联。 | 近义词：cross-systematically, trans-systematically | 反义词：intra-systematically (within a single system)
