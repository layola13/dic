drop-anchor:/drɒpˈæŋkə(r)/|/ˈdrɑːpˌæŋkɚ/| vt.|抛锚；停泊|To lower an anchor into the water to secure a vessel in place.  This action typically signifies stopping or settling down.|例句：The captain ordered the crew to drop anchor in the sheltered bay. 船长命令船员在避风的港湾抛锚。

例句：We dropped anchor off the coast of the island and enjoyed a peaceful evening. 我们在岛屿海岸抛锚，享受了一个宁静的夜晚。


近义词：moor, anchor, secure


反义词：weigh anchor (起锚)


词性：动词
