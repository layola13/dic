regale:/rɪˈɡeɪl/| vt.|款待；使…快乐；|To entertain or amuse someone with stories, jokes, or other things.|例句：He regaled us with stories of his travels. 他给我们讲了他旅行的故事，让我们很开心。  He regaled the audience with anecdotes from his childhood. 他给观众讲了他童年时代的一些趣事，逗得大家哈哈大笑。|近义词：entertain, amuse, delight|反义词：bore, depress


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He regales his friends with funny stories every weekend. 他每个周末都给朋友们讲笑话取乐。
She regales her family with tales of her adventures abroad. 她给家人讲述她在国外的冒险故事。


一般过去时:
The comedian regaled the audience with witty jokes. 喜剧演员用俏皮话逗乐了观众。
He regaled his colleagues with stories of his recent vacation. 他给同事们讲了他最近假期的故事。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
