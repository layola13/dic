overcondense:/ˌoʊvər kənˈdɛns/| v.|过度浓缩，过度压缩|To condense excessively; to compress beyond a desirable or necessary point.  The process often leads to a loss of detail or clarity.|例句：The writer overcondensed the historical details, losing much of the nuance of the era.  作者过度浓缩了历史细节，丢失了那个时代的大部分细微之处。

例句：The company overcondensed its marketing message, making it confusing for consumers. 公司过度压缩了其营销信息，导致消费者感到困惑。


近义词：compress excessively, overpack, concentrate excessively|反义词：dilute, expand, rarefy


词性:动词
