/ˌsemiˈwɔːrfɛərz/ | n. |准战争状态，半战争状态 |A state of conflict that falls short of full-scale war, often characterized by proxy conflicts, skirmishes, or other forms of limited violence.  It can involve political maneuvering, economic sanctions, or cyber warfare, alongside traditional military actions on a smaller scale.  | Example: The escalating tensions between the two nations have pushed them into a state of semiwarfare.  =  两国之间不断升级的紧张局势已使它们陷入准战争状态。|近义词：limited conflict, proxy war, cold war, low-intensity conflict |反义词：peace, full-scale war
