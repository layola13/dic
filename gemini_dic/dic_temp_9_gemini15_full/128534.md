nexts:/neksts/| n.|下一次，接下来，接下去的事物 |The word "nexts" is not a standard English word. It's likely a misspelling of "next," which can be a noun, adjective, or adverb.  As a noun, it refers to something that comes immediately after something else; as an adjective, it means coming immediately after something else; as an adverb, it means after that.  |例句：What are your next steps? = 你接下来的步骤是什么？
The next chapter is more exciting. = 下一章更精彩。|近义词：following, subsequent|反义词：previous, prior


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is the next step? = 下一步是什么？
What comes next in the sequence? = 序列中接下来是什么？


一般过去时:
What was the next thing that happened? = 接下来发生了什么？
What did he do next? = 他接下来做了什么？


现在完成时:
What have you done next? = 你接下来做了什么？（非正式，通常用“What have you done?”）
What has been done next? = 接下来做了什么？（被动语态）


过去完成时:
What had happened next? = 接下来发生了什么？（不太常用，通常用过去时）
What had they done next? = 他们接下来做了什么？（不太常用，通常用过去时）


将来时:
What will you do next? = 你接下来会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？


虚拟语气:
If I knew what to do next, I would do it. = 如果我知道接下来该做什么，我会去做。
I wish I knew what would happen next. = 我希望我知道接下来会发生什么。


被动语态:
What will be done next? = 接下来会做什么？（被动语态）
What was decided next? = 接下来决定了什么？（被动语态）


疑问句:
What comes next? = 接下来是什么？
What next? = 接下来呢？（口语）


否定句:
I don't know what comes next. = 我不知道接下来是什么。
There's nothing next. = 没有接下来了。
