disfigure:/dɪsˈfɪɡər/| vt.|毁容；损害…的外观|to spoil the appearance of something or someone, especially the face, by cutting or damaging it|The accident severely disfigured his face.  事故严重毁了他的容貌。 The storm disfigured the landscape.  暴风雨破坏了景观。|mar, deform, deface|beautify, improve


USAGE->

The scar disfigures her face. = 这道疤痕毁了她的容貌。
He disfigures his artwork intentionally. = 他故意毁坏他的艺术作品。


一般过去时:

The vandal disfigured the statue. = 那个破坏分子毁坏了雕像。


现在完成时:
The accident has disfigured him for life. = 那次事故毁了他的一生。
Years of sun exposure have disfigured her skin. = 多年的日晒损害了她的皮肤。


过去完成时:
