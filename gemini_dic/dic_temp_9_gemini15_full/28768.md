carnivores:/ˈkɑːrnɪvɔːrz/|/ˈkɑːrnɪvɔːrz/| n. |食肉动物，肉食动物 |Animals that eat meat. Carnivores have specialized teeth and digestive systems adapted for consuming flesh.  They play a crucial role in maintaining the balance of ecosystems. |Example: Lions, tigers, and wolves are all carnivores.  狮子、老虎和狼都是食肉动物。
The study focused on the feeding habits of various carnivores in the African savanna. 这项研究的重点是非洲大草原上各种食肉动物的摄食习惯。| omnivores, predators |herbivores, plants| 名词
