issued:/ˈɪʃuːd/ | v. | 发布；发行；颁发；发出 | To put something into circulation; to give something out officially; to formally announce or declare something. | 例句：The government issued a statement denying the allegations. 政府发表声明否认这些指控。

例句：The bank issued me a new credit card. 银行给我发了一张新的信用卡。


近义词：published, released, distributed, delivered, announced | 反义词：received, withdrawn, recalled


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company issues new products every year. = 公司每年都会发行新产品。
He issues instructions clearly. = 他清晰地发布指令。


一般过去时:
The bank issued a loan to the small business. = 银行向这家小企业发放了贷款。
She issued a warning about the dangers of smoking. = 她警告吸烟的危害。


现在完成时:
The government has issued several new regulations this year. = 政府今年已经发布了几项新规章。
They have issued a press release about the incident. = 他们已经就此事发布了一份新闻稿。


过去完成时:
The judge had issued the warrant before the police arrived. = 在警察到达之前，法官已经签发了逮捕令。
The company had issued a recall notice before the problem was widespread. = 在问题广泛传播之前，公司已经发布了召回通知。


将来时:
The school will issue the report cards next week. = 学校下周将发放成绩单。
The authorities will issue a travel advisory if the situation deteriorates. = 如果情况恶化，当局将发布旅行建议。


虚拟语气:
If the court issued an injunction, the construction would stop. = 如果法院发出禁令，建筑工程就会停止。
I wish the bank had issued me a loan sooner. = 我希望银行早点给我贷款。


被动语态:
The new law was issued last month. = 新法律是上个月发布的。
The tickets were issued to the attendees. = 门票发给了与会者。


疑问句:
Has the company issued a statement yet? = 公司是否已经发表声明？
When will the university issue the acceptance letters? = 大学什么时候会发放录取通知书？


否定句:
The court did not issue a ruling on the case. = 法院没有对该案做出裁决。
The company has not yet issued an apology. = 公司尚未道歉。
