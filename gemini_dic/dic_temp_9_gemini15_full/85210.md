harken:/ˈhɑːrkn/| v.|倾听，留心|To listen attentively; to give heed.|例句：Harken to the wise words of your elders. 倾听你长辈的智慧之言。|近义词：listen, heed, attend|反义词：ignore, disregard


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He harkens to the advice of his mentor. = 他倾听他导师的建议。
They harken to the call of the wild. = 他们倾听大自然的召唤。


一般过去时:
She harkened to the old woman's story. = 她倾听了老妇人的故事。
The villagers harkened to the church bells. = 村民们倾听着教堂的钟声。


现在完成时:
I have harkened to your concerns. = 我已经倾听了你的担忧。
We have harkened to the warnings of the scientists. = 我们已经倾听了科学家的警告。


过去完成时:
He had harkened to the rumors before making a decision. = 他在做决定之前已经听取了谣言。
They had harkened to the prophecy for many years. = 他们多年来一直倾听着预言。


将来时:
She will harken to the advice of her doctor. = 她将倾听她医生的建议。
We will harken to the sounds of the ocean. = 我们将倾听大海的声音。


虚拟语气:
If I had harkened to my father's advice, I wouldn't be in this mess. = 如果我听了父亲的劝告，我就不会陷入这种困境。
I wish I had harkened to the warning signs. = 我真希望我当时注意到了警告信号。


被动语态:
His words were harkened to by the entire audience. = 他的话语得到了全体观众的倾听。
The advice was harkened to by the committee. = 委员会认真听取了建议。


疑问句:
Will you harken to my plea? = 你会倾听我的请求吗？
Did they harken to the instructions? = 他们听了指示吗？


否定句:
He did not harken to the warnings of the storm. = 他没有听到暴风雨的警告。
They did not harken to the pleas of the victims. = 他们没有理会受害者的求助。


PHRASE->
harken back to = 回顾
harken unto = 倾听
harken to the call of = 响应…的号召
to harken back = 追溯到
harken to reason = 听从理智
