congenial:/kənˈdʒiːniəl/ | adj. |意气相投的；合适的；令人愉快的 | Pleasant because of qualities that are suited to one's needs or nature; agreeable.  | 例句：They found a congenial atmosphere in the small mountain village.他们在小山村里找到了一个舒适宜人的氛围。

例句：The congenial couple enjoyed many shared hobbies. 这对志趣相投的夫妇有很多共同的爱好。


近义词：compatible, agreeable, pleasant, suitable, friendly

反义词：unsuitable, disagreeable, unpleasant, hostile, unfriendly

词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The weather is congenial today. = 今天天气宜人。
This job is congenial to my interests. = 这份工作符合我的兴趣。


一般过去时:
The atmosphere at the party was very congenial. = 派对的气氛非常融洽。

现在完成时:
I have found my new work environment to be very congenial. = 我发现我的新工作环境非常舒适。
They have always had a congenial relationship. = 他们一直关系融洽。


过去完成时:

将来时:

The new team members will hopefully create a congenial working environment. = 新团队成员有望创造一个融洽的工作环境。


虚拟语气:
If the weather were congenial, we would go for a walk. = 如果天气好的话，我们就去散步。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
