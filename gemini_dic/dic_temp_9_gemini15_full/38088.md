composting:/ˈkɒmˌpoʊstɪŋ/ | n. | 堆肥；堆肥法 | The process of decomposing organic matter, such as food scraps and yard waste, to create a nutrient-rich soil amendment.  | Example:  Composting kitchen scraps reduces landfill waste.  堆肥厨房垃圾减少了垃圾填埋场的垃圾。  We're starting a composting system in our backyard. 我们在后院开始了一个堆肥系统。|近义词：compost making, organic decomposition |反义词：landfilling


USAGE->

一般现在时:
We compost our vegetable scraps. = 我们把蔬菜残渣做成堆肥。
They compost leaves and grass clippings. = 他们把树叶和草屑做成堆肥。


一般过去时:
I composted the fallen leaves last autumn. = 我去年秋天把落叶堆肥了。
She composted the food waste regularly. = 她定期对食物垃圾进行堆肥。


现在完成时:
We have composted all our yard waste this year. = 今年我们已经把所有的园林垃圾都堆肥了。
They have been composting for five years. = 他们已经堆肥五年了。


过去完成时:
The compost had already decomposed before we added the new materials. = 在我们添加新材料之前，堆肥已经分解了。
They had composted all the waste before the inspection. = 他们在检查之前已经把所有的垃圾都堆肥了。


将来时:
We will compost the coffee grounds tomorrow. = 我们明天将把咖啡渣堆肥。
They will start composting next spring. = 他们将在明年春天开始堆肥。


虚拟语气:
If we composted more, we would have richer soil. = 如果我们堆肥更多，我们的土壤就会更肥沃。
I wish I had started composting sooner. = 我真希望我早点开始堆肥。


被动语态:
The leaves were composted in a large bin. = 树叶在一个大箱子里堆肥。

疑问句:

否定句:
