mechanize:/ˈmɛkənaɪz/| vt.| 使机械化；使自动化 | To equip (a process, industry, etc.) with machinery; to make automatic.  |例句：The factory has been mechanized to increase production. 工厂已经实现了机械化以提高产量。 |例句：They are trying to mechanize the entire process. 他们正试图使整个过程自动化。|近义词：automate, computerize |反义词：manualize, demechanize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They mechanize the assembly line. = 他们使装配线机械化。
We mechanize our farming practices. = 我们使我们的农业实践机械化。

一般过去时:
The company mechanized its production process last year. = 该公司去年将其生产流程机械化。
Ford mechanized the automobile industry. = 福特使汽车工业机械化。


现在完成时:
They have mechanized many of their operations. = 他们已经使他们的许多操作机械化。
Farmers have mechanized their harvesting techniques. = 农民们已经使他们的收割技术机械化。


过去完成时:
The factory had mechanized its processes before the new regulations were implemented. = 在新规章实施之前，工厂已经将其流程机械化。
They had mechanized the system long before I joined the company. = 我加入公司很久以前，他们就已经使该系统机械化了。


将来时:
We will mechanize the warehouse next year. = 我们明年将使仓库机械化。
They will mechanize their data entry system. = 他们将使他们的数据输入系统机械化。


虚拟语气:
If we mechanized the process, we would save a lot of time. = 如果我们使这个过程机械化，我们将节省大量时间。
It would be beneficial if they mechanized the entire workflow. = 如果他们使整个工作流程机械化，将是有益的。


被动语态:
The factory was mechanized in the 1960s. = 工厂在20世纪60年代实现了机械化。
The entire operation is being mechanized. = 整个操作正在机械化。


疑问句:
Will they mechanize the data collection process? = 他们会使数据收集过程机械化吗？
Has the company mechanized its accounting department? = 该公司是否已经使其会计部门机械化？


否定句:
They did not mechanize the production line. = 他们没有使生产线机械化。
We will not mechanize the process without further study. = 我们不会在没有进一步研究的情况下使该过程机械化。
