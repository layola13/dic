deficiently:/dɪˈfɪʃəntli/ | adv. | 不足地；缺陷地 | In a deficient manner; inadequately.  It describes something done or existing in a way that lacks something necessary or desirable.  |例句：The bridge was deficiently constructed, leading to its collapse. 这座桥梁建造不足，导致坍塌。

例句：The project was deficiently funded, resulting in delays.  这个项目资金不足，导致延误。


近义词：inadequately, insufficiently, imperfectly, poorly


反义词：adequately, sufficiently, perfectly, well


词源:  Derived from the adjective "deficient," which comes from the Latin word "deficiens," the present participle of "deficere," meaning "to fail," "to be wanting," or "to be lacking."  The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective into an adverb.
