humanizes:/ˈhjuːmənaɪzɪz/ | vt. |使人性化；赋予人性；使人道化 | To give human qualities or characteristics to something; to make something more humane or compassionate. | 例句：The novel humanizes the villain, making him a more sympathetic character. 这部小说使反派角色人性化，使他成为一个更令人同情的角色。  The new policy aims to humanize the prison system. 新政策旨在使监狱制度人性化。|近义词：personalize, personalize, empathize with |反义词：dehumanize, depersonalize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He humanizes his characters through detailed descriptions of their emotions. 他通过详细描述人物的情感来使他们人性化。
The company humanizes its brand through social media engagement. 这家公司通过社交媒体互动来使它的品牌人性化。

一般过去时:
The artist humanized the robot by giving it expressive eyes. 艺术家通过赋予机器人富有表情的眼睛来使它人性化。
The teacher humanized the subject matter by relating it to students' lives. 老师通过将主题与学生的生活联系起来，使之更易于理解。
