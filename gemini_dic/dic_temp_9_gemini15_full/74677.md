freebooty:/ˈfriːˌbuːti/| n.|掠夺品，战利品|Plunder, booty, spoils taken in war or by violence.  It often implies a lack of legitimacy or right to the acquisition.|例句：The pirates divided the freebooty amongst themselves. 海盗们瓜分了掠夺品。  The invading army amassed a significant freebooty. 入侵的军队积累了大量的战利品。|近义词：loot, plunder, spoils, booty|反义词：earnings (legitimate), property (legally owned)
