overtechnical:/ˌoʊvərˈtɛknɪkl/ | adj. |过于专业的；技术性过强的 | excessively technical; difficult to understand because it uses too much specialized language.  | The presentation was overtechnical and lost the audience.  这次演示过于专业，听众听得一头雾水。|  technical, specialized, jargon-filled | simple, clear, understandable | 形容词
