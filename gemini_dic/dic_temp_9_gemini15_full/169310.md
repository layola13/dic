promoing:/prəˈmoʊɪŋ/| v. | 推广；促销 | Promoting a product involves creating awareness and generating interest among potential customers. 推广产品包括提高知名度和激发潜在客户的兴趣。 | 近义词：advertising, marketing, publicizing | 反义词：suppressing, hindering


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They are promoting their new product online. = 他们在网上推广他们的新产品。
The company is promoting sustainable practices. = 公司正在推广可持续发展实践。


一般过去时:
We promoted the event through social media. = 我们通过社交媒体推广了这个活动。
The team promoted him to manager. = 团队提拔他为经理。


现在完成时:
They have been promoting this film for months. = 他们已经推广这部电影好几个月了。
She has promoted her business through networking. = 她通过人脉关系推广了自己的生意。


过去完成时:
They had already promoted the sale before the announcement. = 他们在公告之前就已经推广了促销活动。
The company had promoted several employees before the merger. = 在合并之前，公司已经提升了几名员工。


将来时:
We will promote the concert on radio. = 我们将在广播中宣传这场音乐会。
They will be promoting their new album next month. = 他们下个月将推广他们的新专辑。


虚拟语气:
If they promoted the product more aggressively, they would sell more. = 如果他们更积极地推广产品，他们就会卖出更多。
I wish they had promoted my work more. = 我希望他们能更多地推广我的作品。


被动语态:
The product was promoted through television commercials. = 该产品通过电视广告进行推广。
The event is being promoted by a well-known agency. = 该活动由一家知名机构进行推广。


疑问句:
How are they promoting their new app? = 他们如何推广他们的新应用程序？
What methods are you using to promote your business? = 你使用什么方法来推广你的业务？


否定句:
They are not promoting the product effectively. = 他们没有有效地推广产品。
The company did not promote him to senior manager. = 公司没有提拔他为高级经理。


PHRASE->
