liquefied:/ˈlɪkwɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 液化的；液态的 |  Made liquid; converted into a liquid state.  | Example: Liquefied natural gas is a cleaner-burning fuel. 液化天然气是一种燃烧更清洁的燃料。 | 近义词：melted, fused, dissolved | 反义词：solidified, frozen


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The gas liquefies under pressure. = 这种气体在压力下液化。
This substance liquefies at room temperature. = 这种物质在室温下液化。


一般过去时:
The scientists liquefied the oxygen. = 科学家们使氧气液化。
The heat liquefied the wax. = 热量使蜡熔化成液体。


现在完成时:
They have liquefied the air for industrial use. = 他们已经将空气液化用于工业用途。
Scientists have liquefied many gases. = 科学家们已经液化了许多气体。


过去完成时:
The experiment had liquefied the sample before the analysis. = 在分析之前，实验已经使样品液化。
The technicians had liquefied the gas before the transport. = 技术人员在运输前已经将气体液化。
