skitters:/ˈskɪtərz/| v.|轻快地移动，快速移动，（尤指轻微地）滑行 |to move lightly and quickly, especially by sliding slightly |The mouse skittered across the floor. 老鼠轻快地跑过地板。The children skittered on the ice. 孩子们在冰上滑行。|近义词：scurry, scamper, dart |反义词：lumber, trudge


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The mouse skitters across the floor. = 老鼠轻快地跑过地板。
The leaves skitter in the wind. = 叶子在风中飞舞。

一般过去时:
The ball skittered across the pavement. = 球在人行道上快速滚动。
He skittered to a stop. = 他迅速停了下来。


现在完成时:
The rain has skittered the leaves into a pile. = 雨水把树叶吹成一堆。
The car has skittered on the ice several times. = 车子在冰上已经滑了几次。


过去完成时:
The cat had skittered away before I could catch it. = 我还没来得及抓住它，猫就溜走了。
The bird had skittered into the bushes. = 鸟儿已经窜进了灌木丛。


将来时:
The ice will skitter under your feet. = 冰会从你脚下滑走。
The leaves will skitter down from the trees. = 叶子会从树上飘落下来。


虚拟语气:
If the ice were thinner, it would skitter more easily. = 如果冰更薄，它会更容易滑动。
I wish the children would not skitter so close to the edge. = 我希望孩子们不要跑到边缘那么近。


被动语态:
The small stones were skittered across the road by the wind. = 小石头被风吹过马路。
The papers were skittered across the desk by the sudden gust. = 突然一阵风把文件吹到了桌子上。


疑问句:
Did the ball skitter into the ditch? = 球是不是滚到沟里去了？
Does the mouse skitter across the floor every night? = 老鼠每天晚上都跑过地板吗？


否定句:
The car didn't skitter on the road. = 车子没有在路上滑行。
The boat didn't skitter across the water. = 船没有在水上滑行。


PHRASE->
skitter across:  滑过，掠过
skitter away: 迅速逃走
skitter down: 快速滑下
skitter off: 迅速离开
skitter to a halt: 迅速停下
