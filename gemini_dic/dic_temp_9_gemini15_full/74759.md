freewiller:/ˈfriːˌwɪlər/ | n. |自由意志者，相信自由意志的人 |A freewiller is someone who believes in free will, the ability to make choices that are not predetermined.  They believe individuals have genuine autonomy and are not merely puppets of fate or deterministic forces. |Example:  Many philosophers are freewillers, arguing against hard determinism.  许多哲学家都是自由意志论者，反对硬决定论。|近义词：libertarian (in the philosophical sense), autonomist |反义词：determinist, fatalist |
