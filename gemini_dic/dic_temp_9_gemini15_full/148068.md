oversatiety:/ˌoʊvərˈsætɪəti/| n. |过度饱食；厌腻；过量|The feeling of having eaten too much to the point of discomfort or disgust.  It implies a negative experience stemming from excessive consumption.  |例句：After the Thanksgiving feast, a feeling of oversatiety settled upon everyone.  感恩节盛宴之后，每个人都感到过度饱食。

例句：The constant stream of sugary treats led to oversatiety and a lack of appetite for anything else.  不断涌现的甜食导致了过度饱食，对其他任何东西都没有食欲。

近义词：satiation, surfeit, repletion, glut

反义词：hunger, appetite, starvation

词性: 名词
