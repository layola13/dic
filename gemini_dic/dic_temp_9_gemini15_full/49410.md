delivery:/dɪˈlɪvəri/ | n. |递送，交付；分娩；发表；履行 |The act of delivering something; the process of bringing something to its destination.  It can refer to physical goods, information, speeches, or even babies. |例句：The delivery of the package was delayed.包裹的递送被延误了。

The doctor assisted with the delivery of the baby.医生协助接生。|近义词： conveyance, shipment, childbirth, presentation |反义词：receipt, acceptance


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company delivers packages every day. = 公司每天送包裹。
She delivers newspapers to her neighborhood. = 她送报纸到她家附近的街区。


一般过去时:
He delivered a powerful speech. = 他发表了一篇有力的演讲。
They delivered the furniture yesterday. = 他们昨天送来了家具。


现在完成时:
We have delivered all the orders. = 我们已经送达了所有订单。
She has delivered twins. = 她生了一对双胞胎。


过去完成时:
They had delivered the goods before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期之前已经交付了货物。
She had delivered a baby just hours before. = 她几个小时前刚生了一个孩子。


将来时:
They will deliver the pizza in 30 minutes. = 他们将在30分钟内送来披萨。
The courier will deliver the documents tomorrow. = 快递员明天将送达文件。


虚拟语气:
If he delivered the speech better, he would have won the election. = 如果他演讲更好，他就会赢得选举。
I wish she had delivered her baby safely. = 我希望她能安全分娩。


被动语态:
The package was delivered this morning. = 包裹今天早上送到了。
The speech was well delivered. = 演讲很精彩。


疑问句:
When will the goods be delivered? = 货物什么时候送达？
How was the baby delivered? = 宝宝是怎么出生的？


否定句:
They didn't deliver the order on time. = 他们没有按时送达订单。
The company failed to deliver the promised results. = 公司未能实现承诺的结果。
