glomerulus:/ɡləˈmɛrjʊləs/ | n. |肾小球；小球；球状体 | A glomerulus is a network of capillaries (tiny blood vessels) within the kidney that filters blood to produce urine.  It's a crucial part of the nephron, the functional unit of the kidney. | Example: The glomerulus filters waste products from the blood.  肾小球过滤血液中的废物。 The damaged glomeruli led to kidney failure. 受损的肾小球导致肾衰竭。 |近义词：renal corpuscle, capillary tuft |反义词：N/A
