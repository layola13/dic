plaudits:/ˈplɔːdɪts/| n.| 赞扬；喝彩；夸奖 |  Expressions of praise or approval.  | The film received plaudits from critics and audiences alike. 这部电影受到了评论家和观众的一致好评。| praise, commendation, accolades, applause | criticism, condemnation, disapproval | 名词
