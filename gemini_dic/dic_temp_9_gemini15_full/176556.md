re-shortened:/ˌriːˈʃɔːtənd/| adj.|重新缩短的；再次缩减的|  A word formed by adding the prefix "re-" to "shortened," indicating that something has been shortened again or further reduced in length, duration, or size. It implies a previous shortening process. |例句：The already shortened report was re-shortened to fit the time constraints.  = 已经缩短的报告为了适应时间限制又被重新缩短了。


The document, re-shortened by the editor, was finally concise enough. =  编辑再次缩短的文件，最终足够简洁。|近义词：abbreviated, abridged, curtailed, condensed|反义词：lengthened, extended, prolonged|形容词
