disenthrall: /ˌdɪsɪnˈθrɔːl/ | vt. | 解放；使摆脱束缚 | To free from thrall; to liberate from bondage, slavery, or enchantment.  | 例句：The revolutionary movement aimed to disenthrall the people from the oppressive regime.  革命运动旨在使人民摆脱压迫政权的束缚。

The inspiring speech disenthralled the audience from their apathy.  鼓舞人心的演讲使听众摆脱了冷漠。| 近义词：liberate, free, release, emancipate | 反义词：enslave, bind, enthrall, imprison | 动词
