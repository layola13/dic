has:/hæz/ | v. | 有；拥有；已经；曾经 |  The third-person singular present tense of "have."  It indicates possession, completion of an action, or existence. | He has a new car. 他有一辆新车。|She has finished her work. 她已经完成了她的工作。 | have, possess | have not, lack | 动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He has a dog. = 他有一只狗。
She has many friends. = 她有很多朋友。

一般过去时:
This verb form does not exist in the simple past tense.  The simple past tense is "had".

现在完成时:
He has lived here for ten years. = 他在这里住了十年了。
She has eaten all the cake. = 她已经吃掉了所有的蛋糕。

过去完成时:
This verb form does not exist in the past perfect tense. The past perfect tense uses "had had".

将来时:
This verb form does not exist in the future tense. Future tense constructions use "will have".


虚拟语气:
If he has the time, he will help us. = 如果他有时间，他会帮助我们。
It is important that he has a good education. = 他受良好的教育很重要。


被动语态:
This verb form is not typically used in the passive voice. The passive voice would use "is/are/was/were had".


疑问句:
Has he finished his homework? = 他完成作业了吗？
Has she been to Paris? = 她去过巴黎吗？


否定句:
He has not (hasn't) seen the movie. = 他还没看过这部电影。
She has not (hasn't) gone to school today. = 她今天没去上学。



PHRASE->
has to = 必须
has been = 曾经是；一直是
has got = 有
that has = 那拥有
has become = 已经成为
