scrunches:/ˈskrʌntʃɪz/| v. n.|揉皱；发出摩擦声；挤压；|To crush or wrinkle something; to make a crunching sound; to squeeze together tightly.|例句：She scrunched up her nose in disgust. 她厌恶地皱起了鼻子。

例句：The leaves scrunched under his feet. 叶子在他脚下发出摩擦声。


近义词：crumple, crush, wrinkle, crunch|反义词：smooth, straighten, unfold|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scrunches his face when he's concentrating. = 他专注的时候会皱着脸。
She scrunches her nose when she smells something bad. = 她闻到难闻的气味时会皱鼻子。


一般过去时:
He scrunched the paper into a ball. = 他把纸揉成一团。
She scrunched her toes into the sand. = 她把脚趾头蜷缩进沙子里。


现在完成时:
I've scrunched up all the papers. = 我已经把所有的纸都揉皱了。
She has scrunched her hair into a bun. = 她已经把头发扎成一个发髻。


过去完成时:
He had scrunched the letter into his pocket. = 他已经把信塞进口袋里了。
She had scrunched her eyes shut in fear. = 她害怕地闭上了眼睛。


将来时:
I will scrunch the fabric to create texture. = 我将揉皱织物以创造纹理。
She will scrunch her hair before going out. = 她出门前会把头发揉乱。


虚拟语气:
If I scrunched the paper harder, it would be smaller. = 如果我把纸揉得更紧，它会更小。
I wish I hadn't scrunched my dress; now it's wrinkled. = 我真希望我没有把我的裙子揉皱；现在它皱了。


被动语态:
The fabric was scrunched to give it a textured look. = 布料被揉皱以使其具有纹理外观。
His face was scrunched up in pain. = 他的脸痛苦地皱了起来。


疑问句:
Did you scrunch the newspaper before throwing it away? = 你扔掉报纸之前揉皱了吗？
Will she scrunch her hair tonight? = 她今晚会把头发弄乱吗？


否定句:
He didn't scrunch the letter; he just folded it. = 他没有揉皱信，只是把它叠了起来。
She doesn't scrunch her hair often. = 她不经常揉乱头发。
