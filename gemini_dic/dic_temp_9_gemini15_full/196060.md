separateness: /ˈsɛpəreɪtnəs/ | n. | 分离；独立；个体性 | The state of being separate or distinct; individuality.  |例句：The separateness of the two cultures was striking.  两种文化的独立性非常显著。

例句：He valued his separateness from the crowd. 他珍视自己与人群的独立性。


近义词：isolation, independence, distinctiveness, individuality | 反义词：unity, togetherness, interdependence


词源:  Derived from the adjective "separate," ultimately from the Latin word "separatus," the past participle of "separare" (to separate).  The suffix "-ness" adds the quality or state of being.
