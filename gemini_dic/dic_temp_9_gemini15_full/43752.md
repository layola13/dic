词源:
Creepiest is the superlative form of "creepy," which ultimately derives from the verb "creep," meaning to move slowly and quietly. The sense of unease and slight fear associated with "creepy" likely developed from the association of slow, quiet movements with things that are potentially dangerous or sinister.
