coagulative: /koʊˈæɡjuleɪtɪv/ | adj. | 凝固的；使凝固的 | Relating to or promoting coagulation.  The process of changing from a liquid to a solid or semi-solid state. |

The coagulative properties of the blood helped stop the bleeding.  血液的凝固特性有助于止血。

The enzyme has a strong coagulative effect on milk.  这种酶对牛奶有很强的凝固作用。


近义词：clotting, thickening, solidifying

反义词：liquefying, thinning, dissolving


词性：形容词
