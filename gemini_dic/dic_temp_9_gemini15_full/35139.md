clough:/kluf/| n.|峡谷，山口，山隘|A steep, narrow gorge, especially one with a stream running through it.  A clough is a deep, narrow valley, often with steep sides and a stream running through it.  The term is primarily used in northern England and parts of Scotland.|例句：The hikers traversed the treacherous clough.  徒步旅行者穿越了险峻的峡谷。|例句：The stream cascaded down the rocky clough. 溪流从多岩石的峡谷中飞泻而下。|近义词：gorge, ravine, chasm, dell|反义词：plain, plateau, summit
