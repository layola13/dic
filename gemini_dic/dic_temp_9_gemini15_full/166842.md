pressin: /ˈprɛsɪn/ | adj. | 紧迫的，迫切的 | Pressing matters require immediate attention.  紧迫的事情需要立即处理。|近义词：urgent, crucial, vital | 反义词：unimportant, trivial |


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The deadline is pressing. = 截止日期迫在眉睫。
This problem is pressing. = 这个问题很紧迫。


一般过去时:
The situation was pressing. = 情况紧急。
The need for action was pressing. = 行动的必要性非常紧迫。


现在完成时:
The need has become increasingly pressing. = 需求变得越来越紧迫。
The problem has been pressing for weeks. = 这个问题已经持续几周，非常紧急。


过去完成时:
The matter had become pressing before the meeting. = 会议前，此事已变得十分紧急。
The urgency had been pressing on their minds. = 紧迫感一直压在他们心头。


将来时:
The situation will become more pressing. = 情况将会更加紧急。
The need for a solution will be pressing. = 对解决方案的需求将变得迫切。


虚拟语气:
If the situation were pressing, we would act immediately. = 如果情况紧急，我们会立即采取行动。
I wish the deadline weren't so pressing. = 我希望截止日期不要那么紧迫。


被动语态:
The issue is being pressed by the media. = 这个问题正受到媒体的关注（施压）。
The matter was pressed upon the committee. = 这件事被提交给委员会（被施压）。


疑问句:
Is the situation pressing? = 情况紧急吗？
Are there any pressing issues to discuss? = 有什么紧迫的问题需要讨论吗？


否定句:
The matter isn't pressing. = 这件事并不紧急。
The deadline isn't pressing at all. = 截止日期根本不紧迫。


PHRASE->
pressing need: 迫切的需求

pressing concern: 令人担忧的问题
pressing engagement: 重要的约会
pressing invitation: 紧急邀请
