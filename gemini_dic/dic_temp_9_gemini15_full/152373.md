pavan:/ˈpɑːvən/| n.|帕凡舞曲；庄严的舞蹈|A pavan is a slow, stately dance of Spanish or Italian origin, usually in 4/4 time, popular in the 16th and 17th centuries.  It's characterized by its solemn and dignified nature.|例句：The dancers gracefully executed a pavan to the accompaniment of lute music. 舞者优雅地伴着鲁特琴音乐跳着帕凡舞曲。 The composer included a pavan in his suite of dances.作曲家在他的舞曲组曲中加入了一首帕凡舞曲。|近义词：dance, ballet, processional|反义词：jig, quick step|名词
