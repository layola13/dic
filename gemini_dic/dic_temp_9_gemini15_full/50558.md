deputization: /ˌdɛpjʊtɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 代理；授权；委任 | The act of deputing or assigning someone to act on behalf of another; the process of giving someone authority to act as a deputy.  | Example: The deputization of the sheriff's officers to handle the emergency was crucial.  代理警长的警官处理紧急情况至关重要。 | 近义词: delegation, authorization, empowerment | 反义词: disempowerment, revocation
