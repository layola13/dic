flurriedly:/ˈflʌridli/ | adv. |慌慌张张地；匆忙地；手忙脚乱地 |In a hurried and confused manner.  It suggests a lack of organization and potentially some nervousness or anxiety. |例句：She flurriedly gathered her things and rushed out the door. 她慌慌张张地收拾好东西冲出门外。


例句：He flurriedly answered the phone, barely able to understand the caller. 他慌慌张张地接起电话，几乎听不懂对方在说什么。


近义词：hastily, hurriedly, rushedly, frantically


反义词：calmly, leisurely, deliberately, methodically


词性：副词
