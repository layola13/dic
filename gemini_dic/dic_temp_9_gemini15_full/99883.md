internalising: /ɪnˈtɜːrnəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 内化；吸收；使成为自己的一部分 | The act of accepting and integrating beliefs, values, or behaviours into one's own personality or worldview.  It implies a deep personal acceptance rather than mere intellectual understanding. | 例句：The children were internalising the values of their parents. 孩子们正在内化父母的价值观。


He is internalising the lessons from his mistakes. 他正在从自己的错误中吸取教训。


近义词：assimilate, absorb, integrate, incorporate | 反义词：reject, externalise, ignore


USAGE->
