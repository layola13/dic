samba:/ˈsæmbə/| n. v.|桑巴舞，桑巴音乐|A lively Brazilian dance of African origin, characterized by a fast rhythm and hip movements.|例句：She learned to dance the samba in Brazil.  她在巴西学会跳桑巴舞。|例句：The samba music filled the air. 桑巴音乐充满了空气。|近义词：rumba,  bossa nova|反义词：None
