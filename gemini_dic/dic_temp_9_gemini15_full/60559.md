elasmobranch:/ɪˈlæzməˌbræŋk/| n. |板鰓類動物；軟骨魚|A cartilaginous fish, such as a shark, ray, or skate, characterized by having a skeleton made of cartilage rather than bone, and possessing five to seven gill slits on each side of the head.  |Example: Sharks and rays are both elasmobranchs.  鲨鱼和鳐鱼都是板鳃类动物。|近义词：cartilaginous fish, chondrichthyan|反义词:bony fish, osteichthyan
