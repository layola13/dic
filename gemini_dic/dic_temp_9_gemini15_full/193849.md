self-director:/ˌsɛlf dəˈrɛktər/| n. | 自导者，自己当导演的人 |A self-director is someone who directs their own work or project, often in film, theatre, or other creative fields.  They are responsible for all aspects of the production, from conception to completion.  This term also applies to individuals who independently manage their own careers or professional development. | Example:  She's a self-director, handling everything from scriptwriting to post-production. 她是一个自导者，负责从编剧到后期制作的所有工作。  He's a self-director in his business, setting his own goals and managing his time effectively. 他在自己的事业中是自导者，设定自己的目标并有效地管理时间。|近义词：independent filmmaker, auteur, freelancer |反义词：employee, subordinate
