feigned:/feɪnd/| v. adj.| 假装的；伪装的 | To pretend to feel or have (something); simulate.  |例句：She feigned illness to avoid going to school. 她假装生病以避免去上学。|近义词：pretended, simulated, affected |反义词：genuine, real, authentic


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He feigns ignorance. = 他假装不知道。
She feigns interest in his stories. = 她假装对他的故事感兴趣。

一般过去时:
He feigned surprise at the news. = 他假装对这个消息感到惊讶。
She feigned sleep to avoid talking to him. = 她假装睡觉以避免和他说话。

现在完成时:
He has feigned illness for weeks. = 他已经假装生病好几个星期了。
She has feigned innocence throughout the trial. = 在整个审判过程中，她一直假装无辜。

过去完成时:
He had feigned ignorance before the police arrived. = 在警察到来之前，他假装不知道。
She had feigned indifference to his advances. = 她一直对他的追求表现得漠不关心。


将来时:
He will feign illness to avoid the meeting. = 他会假装生病以避免参加会议。
She will feign surprise when she receives the gift. = 当她收到礼物时，她会假装很惊讶。


虚拟语气:
If he feigned illness, he would have avoided the meeting. = 如果他假装生病，他就会避免参加会议。
I wish she hadn't feigned indifference. = 我希望她没有假装漠不关心。


被动语态:
Illness was feigned by him to escape work. = 他假装生病来逃避工作。(被动语态较少用)
His surprise was feigned, everyone knew it. = 他的惊讶是装出来的，大家都知道。(被动语态较少用)


疑问句:
Did he feign ignorance? = 他是假装不知道吗？
Was she feigning sleep? = 她是在假装睡觉吗？


否定句:
He didn't feign interest in the conversation. = 他对谈话没有假装感兴趣。
She didn't feign surprise at his proposal. = 她对他的求婚没有假装惊讶。
