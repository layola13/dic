hide:/haɪd/ | vt. n. | 隐藏；躲藏；兽皮 | To put or keep something out of sight; to conceal something.  |The children hid behind the tree. 孩子们躲在树后。|近义词：conceal, secrete, mask |反义词：show, reveal, expose |动词，名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hides his feelings well. 他把自己的感情隐藏得很好。
They hide the evidence from the police. 他们向警方隐瞒证据。

一般过去时:
She hid the letter in a drawer. 她把信藏在抽屉里。
The thief hid from the security cameras. 小偷躲避了安全摄像头。

现在完成时:
He has hidden his money in a safe place. 他把钱藏在一个安全的地方。
They have hidden the truth from us for years.  他们多年来一直对我们隐瞒真相。

过去完成时:
She had hidden the present before her son woke up.  在她儿子醒来之前，她已经藏好了礼物。
They had hidden the documents before the police arrived. 他们在警察到达之前已经藏起了文件。

将来时:
He will hide the truth from her. 他会向她隐瞒真相。
They will hide the evidence before the trial. 他们会在审判前隐藏证据。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would hide my feelings. 如果我是你，我会隐藏我的感情。
I wish I could hide from all my problems. 我希望我能逃避我所有的问题。

被动语态:
The truth was hidden from the public. 真相被隐瞒了公众。
The evidence was hidden in the basement. 证据被藏在地下室。

疑问句:
Where did he hide the money? 他把钱藏在哪里了？
Why did she hide the truth? 她为什么隐瞒真相？

否定句:
He didn't hide his disappointment. 他没有掩饰他的失望。
She didn't hide her anger. 她没有隐藏她的愤怒。
