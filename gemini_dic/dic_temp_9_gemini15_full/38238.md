computerises: /kəmˈpjuːtəraɪz/ | vt. | 使电脑化；用电脑控制 | To equip (a system, process, or organization) with computers or computer technology.  |  例句：The company computerised its accounting system last year.  公司去年将会计系统电脑化了。|例句：They plan to computerise the entire factory.他们计划将整个工厂电脑化。| 近义词：automate, digitize | 反义词：manualize, decomputerize


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company computerises its payroll system. = 公司用电脑处理工资系统。
They computerise their inventory management. = 他们用电脑管理库存。

一般过去时:
The factory computerised its production line. = 工厂将其生产线电脑化了。
We computerised our data storage system last month. = 我们上个月将数据存储系统电脑化了。

现在完成时:
The bank has computerised most of its operations. = 该银行已将其大部分业务电脑化。
They have computerised their customer service system. = 他们已经将客户服务系统电脑化了。

过去完成时:
They had computerised their billing system before the merger. = 在合并之前，他们已经将账单系统电脑化了。
The company had computerised its record keeping before the audit. = 公司在审计之前已经将记录保存电脑化了。

将来时:
We will computerise our marketing department next year. = 我们明年将把市场部电脑化。
The school will computerise its library system. = 学校将把图书馆系统电脑化。

虚拟语气:
If we computerised our operations, we would save a lot of time. = 如果我们把业务电脑化，我们将节省大量时间。
It would be better if they computerised the whole process. = 如果他们将整个过程电脑化会更好。

被动语态:
The system is being computerised by the IT department. = 系统正在由IT部门电脑化。
Our data will be computerised soon. = 我们的数据很快就会被电脑化。

疑问句:
Will the project be computerised on time? = 项目能否按时电脑化？
Has the company computerised its human resources management? = 公司是否已经将人力资源管理电脑化？

否定句:
The company did not computerise its operations last year. = 该公司去年没有将其业务电脑化。
They haven't yet computerised their accounting system. = 他们还没有将他们的会计系统电脑化。
