preparatory:/prɪˈpærətɔːri/|/ˈprɛpərətɔri/| adj. |预备的；准备的；预科的|Serving to prepare or make ready; preliminary.  It often refers to something that comes before a more important event or activity, or to education designed to prepare someone for further study.|例句：She took a preparatory course in French before going to Paris. 她去巴黎之前参加了法语预备课程。


例句：The preparatory work for the conference took several months. 会议的准备工作花了几个月的时间。


近义词： preliminary, introductory, antecedent, preparatory


反义词： final, concluding, ultimate


词性：形容词
