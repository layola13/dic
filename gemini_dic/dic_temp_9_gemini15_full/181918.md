remanding:/rɪˈmændɪŋ/| v.| 提交；还押；拘留；发回；指令；下令|  The act of sending someone back to custody or a lower court.  It implies a temporary return, usually for further proceedings or investigation.|


例句：

The judge remanded the defendant to await trial. = 法官将被告还押候审。

The case was remanded back to the lower court for further consideration. = 此案被发回下级法院进一步审理。


近义词：refer, return, send back, commit


反义词：release, free, discharge
