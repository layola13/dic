kingfishers:/ˈkɪŋˌfɪʃərz/| n. |翠鸟；翠鸟科|A kingfisher is a bird of the family Alcedinidae, characterized by their vibrant colors, large heads, long, sharp beaks, and short legs. They are known for their diving ability to catch fish.  |Example:  We saw a kingfisher diving for fish in the river. 我们看到一只翠鸟在河里捕鱼。  The kingfishers were perched on a branch, watching the water.  翠鸟栖息在树枝上，注视着水面。|近义词:  water king, belted kingfisher|反义词:  (no direct antonym)|名词
