kool:/kuːl/| adj.|酷的，凉爽的，冷静的 | Cool means stylish, fashionable, or impressive.  It can also mean calm, relaxed, or not easily upset.  |例句：That's a cool car! = 那辆车真酷！|例句：Stay cool under pressure. = 在压力下保持冷静。|近义词：awesome, stylish, trendy, calm, relaxed |反义词：uncool, unfashionable, hot-headed, agitated


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is cool under pressure. = 他在压力下很冷静。
This new phone is really cool. = 这款新手机真的很酷。

一般过去时:
She was cool and collected during the emergency. = 她在紧急情况下保持冷静和镇定。
The party was really cool. = 派对真的很酷。

现在完成时:
I have been feeling really cool since I had a shower. = 洗完澡后我一直感觉很凉爽。
They have become cool and sophisticated over the years. = 这些年来，他们变得很酷很老练。

过去完成时:
I had been cool and calm until the accident happened. = 直到事故发生前，我一直都很冷静。
The room had been cool before they turned on the heater. = 在他们打开暖气之前，房间很凉爽。

将来时:
The weather will be cool tomorrow. = 明天天气会凉爽。
I think she will be cool about the news. = 我认为她会对这个消息保持冷静。

虚拟语气:
If I were cool and confident, I would ask her out. = 如果我冷静自信，我会约她出去。
I wish I were cool like him. = 我希望我能像他一样酷。

被动语态:
The room is kept cool by the air conditioner. = 房间由空调保持凉爽。
The situation was kept cool by the police's quick response. = 警察的快速反应使局势保持冷静。

疑问句:
Is he cool with the plan? = 他同意这个计划吗？（是否冷静接受）
Is the water cool enough to drink? = 水够凉可以喝吗？

否定句:
He wasn't cool with the idea. = 他不同意这个想法。（不冷静接受）
The room isn't cool enough. = 房间不够凉爽。

PHRASE->
keep cool = 保持冷静

cool it = 冷静点儿
that's cool =  没关系；还不错
cool head = 冷静的头脑
