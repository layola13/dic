reprobatory: /ˈrɛprəbətɔːri/ | adj. | 反对的；谴责的；非难的 | expressing or showing disapproval; censorious.  |  例句：Her reprobatory glance silenced him instantly.  她的责备眼神立刻让他闭嘴了。

例句：The teacher gave him a reprobatory look for his disruptive behavior. 老师因为他扰乱课堂的行为给了他一个谴责的眼神。


近义词： disapproving, censorious, condemnatory, critical, disapproving, reproachful

反义词： approving, commendatory, praising


词源：  From Latin *reprobatorius, from reprobāre "to disapprove, reject," from re- "back, again" + probāre "to test, prove."  The ultimate root is the Proto-Indo-European *prewb- "to prove, test."
