preexamined:/ˌpriːɪɡˈzæmɪnd/| adj.| 预先审查的；预先检验的 |  Having been examined beforehand.  This adjective describes something that has undergone a prior examination or inspection. |例句：The preexamined documents were deemed acceptable.  预先审查的文件被认为是可以接受的。

The application was preexamined by the committee before being presented to the board. 委员会在向董事会提交申请之前对其进行了预先审查。|近义词：inspected, reviewed, vetted |反义词：unexamined, uninspected
