sanding:/ˈsændɪŋ/| v.|打磨，砂磨|The act of smoothing a surface using sandpaper or a similar abrasive material.  It is often used to prepare a surface for painting or finishing.|例句：I spent the afternoon sanding the table. = 我下午花了时间打磨桌子。

例句：The carpenter is sanding the wooden door before painting it. = 木匠在油漆木门之前正在打磨它。

近义词：abrading, smoothing, polishing|反义词：roughening

She sands the furniture before painting. = 她油漆家具之前会先打磨。
