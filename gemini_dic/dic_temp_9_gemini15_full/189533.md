salar:/ˈsælər/| n.|薪水，工资|Salary refers to fixed compensation paid regularly to an employee for their work.  It is typically paid on a monthly or annual basis.|My salary is paid directly into my bank account.我的工资直接打到我的银行账户。|wages, pay, remuneration, compensation|None|名词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He receives a high salary. = 他获得高薪。
She earns a modest salary. = 她挣的工资很一般。

一般过去时:
He received a bonus last year in addition to his salary. = 去年除了工资外，他还领到奖金。
She earned a lower salary at her previous job. = 她之前的工作工资比较低。

现在完成时:
He has received his salary for this month. = 他已经收到这个月的工资了。
She has earned a significant amount of money over the years through her salary and investments. = 多年来，她通过工资和投资赚了很多钱。

过去完成时:
He had received his salary before the rent was due. = 房租到期前，他已经领到工资了。
She had earned enough money to buy a car before she got promoted. = 升职之前，她已经攒够钱买车了。

将来时:
He will receive his first salary next month. = 他下个月将收到他的第一份工资。
She will earn a higher salary in her new position. = 她将在新职位上获得更高的薪水。

虚拟语气:
If I earned a higher salary, I would travel more. = 如果我薪水更高，我会多旅行。
I wish I earned a higher salary so that I could afford a bigger house. = 我希望我的薪水更高，这样我就能买得起更大的房子。

被动语态:
The salary is paid monthly. = 工资每月支付。
His salary was increased last year. = 他的工资去年增加了。

疑问句:
What is your annual salary? = 你的年薪是多少？
How much salary do you expect? = 你期望多少工资？

否定句:
He does not receive a high salary. = 他没有获得高薪。
She did not earn enough money to cover her expenses. = 她赚的钱不够支付她的开支。
