out-designing: /ˌaʊt dɪˈzaɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 超越设计，设计得更好 |  The act of surpassing or exceeding the design of something or someone else. It implies creating a superior design in terms of functionality, aesthetics, or innovation.  | 例句：Their new product completely out-designed the competition. 他们的新产品完全超越了竞争对手的设计。

Their innovative approach to software architecture out-designed all previous attempts at solving the problem. 他们创新的软件架构方法超越了所有先前解决该问题的尝试。


近义词：outshine, surpass, exceed, better, improve upon | 反义词：under-design, poorly design


词性:动词
