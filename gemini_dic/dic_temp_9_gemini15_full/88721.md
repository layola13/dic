hobbit:/ˈhɒbɪt/| n. |霍比特人 |A fictional race of small, hairy-footed humanoids in J.R.R. Tolkien's Middle-earth legendarium, known for their love of comfort, good food, and simple pleasures.  They are generally peaceful but possess surprising courage and resilience when necessary. |Example: Bilbo Baggins is a famous hobbit from the Shire. 比尔博·巴金斯是夏尔一个著名的霍比特人。|近义词：dwarf (in some contexts), halfling (alternative term used by Tolkien) |反义词：giant, ogre
