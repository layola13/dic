ecumenic:/ˌɛkjuˈmɛnɪk/| adj. |世界性的；普世的；大公的；具有普世教会性质的 | Relating to or promoting unity among different churches or religious groups.  The ecumenic movement seeks to foster cooperation and understanding between various Christian denominations. | 近义词：universal, worldwide, catholic (in the sense of universal) | 反义词：sectarian, divisive, parochial


例句：

The ecumenic council aimed to bridge the divides between different Christian churches.  =  普世会议旨在弥合不同基督教教会之间的分歧。

His ecumenic vision embraced people of all faiths. = 他具有普世视野，包容所有信仰的人。


词源：

From Greek *oikoumenē* ("the inhabited world," "the world"), from *oikein* ("to inhabit"). The term's religious usage developed from the early Christian concept of a unified, universal church.
