girdingly:/ˈɡɜːrdɪŋli/ | adv. | 戒备地；防备地；紧张地 |  In a way that shows preparation for something difficult or unpleasant; tensely.  It often implies a feeling of apprehension or anxiety. | 例句：He looked girdingly at the approaching storm. 他戒备地望着逼近的风暴。

例句：She spoke girdingly, as if bracing herself for an argument. 她紧张地说话，好像在为一场争论做准备。


近义词： apprehensively, anxiously, nervously, tensely, warily


反义词：calmly, confidently, serenely, nonchalantly


词源：源自动词gird，意为“束腰；准备；围绕”。  "-ingly" 是副词后缀，表示“以……的方式”。  因此，girdingly 指的是以准备或束腰的方式，暗示着一种紧张或戒备的状态。
