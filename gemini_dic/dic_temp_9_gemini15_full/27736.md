canings:/ˈkeɪnɪŋz/| n. |鞭打；体罚|The act of caning someone, or multiple instances of caning.  It refers to the punishment of whipping someone with a cane. |例句：The school's strict discipline included canings for minor infractions.  学校的严格纪律包括对轻微违规行为进行鞭打。|例句：The prisoner received several canings as punishment. 囚犯受到几次鞭打作为惩罚。|近义词：floggings, whippings, lashings |反义词：rewards, praise |名词
