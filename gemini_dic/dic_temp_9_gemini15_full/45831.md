customization:/ˌkʌstəmaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 定制，个性化 | The process of modifying a product or service to meet a particular customer's needs or preferences.  It involves tailoring something to be unique and specific to an individual or group. | Example: The company offers extensive customization options for its software. 这家公司为其软件提供了广泛的定制选项。

The car manufacturer provides customization options, allowing customers to choose their preferred color and interior. 汽车制造商提供定制选项，允许客户选择他们喜欢的颜色和内饰。|近义词：personalization, tailoring, adaptation |反义词：standardization, generalization |名词
