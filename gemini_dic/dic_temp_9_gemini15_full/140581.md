oligosepalous: /ˌɒlɪɡoʊˈsɛpələs/ | adj. | 少萼的 | Having few sepals; typically fewer than three.  This term is used primarily in botany to describe flowers with a reduced number of sepals.  A sepal is one of the modified leaves forming the calyx (outermost whorl) of a flower. | Example:  The *oligosepalous* flower of the plant was unusual, exhibiting only two sepals.  少萼的植物花朵很不寻常，只有两片萼片。 | 近义词: few-sepaled | 反义词: polysepalous
