hypothetically:/ˌhaɪpəˈθɛtɪkli/| adv. |假设地；理论上地|  In a hypothetical manner; assuming something for the sake of argument or discussion, even if it may not be true.  It expresses a condition contrary to fact or a supposition.| 例句：Hypothetically, if we won the lottery, we'd travel the world.  假设我们中了彩票，我们会周游世界。

例句：Hypothetically speaking, what would you do if you lost your job?  假设一下，如果你丢了工作你会怎么办？


近义词：theoretically, conjecturally, speculatively, supposably

反义词：factually, actually, really


USAGE->

We hypothetically explore different scenarios. = 我们假设性地探索不同的情景。
She hypothetically considers the possibilities. = 她假设性地考虑各种可能性。


一般过去时:

现在完成时:
We have hypothetically planned our next vacation. = 我们已经假设性地计划了我们的下一个假期。

词源:
