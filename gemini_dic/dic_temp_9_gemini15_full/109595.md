lethargy:/ˈlɛθɑːrdʒi/ | n. | 嗜睡；倦怠；无精打采 | A state of sluggishness, inactivity, or apathy.  It often involves a lack of energy and motivation. |  He felt a deep lethargy after the long journey. 他长途旅行后感到极度疲惫。  The hot weather induced a feeling of lethargy. 炎热的天气让人昏昏欲睡。| drowsiness, sluggishness, lassitude |  energy, vigor, enthusiasm | 名词
