proanarchy:/proʊˈænərki/| adj.| 亲无政府主义的；支持无政府主义的 |Proanarchy refers to a political philosophy or ideology that supports or advocates for anarchy, often with a positive or constructive connotation, emphasizing aspects like self-governance, voluntary cooperation, and decentralized social organization.  It contrasts with the more negative connotations often associated with the term "anarchy" itself. |Example:  The proanarchy movement focuses on building alternative social structures based on mutual aid and community empowerment. 亲无政府主义运动专注于建立基于互助和社区赋权的替代性社会结构。|近义词：anarcho-communist, anarcho-syndicalist |反义词：authoritarian, statist
