plentiful:/ˈplɛntɪfəl/| adj. |丰富的；充足的；大量的 | Abundant in supply; more than enough.  |例句：The harvest was plentiful this year. 今年的收成很丰盛。

例句：The garden is plentiful with flowers. 花园里鲜花盛开。


近义词：abundant, copious, ample, rich, bountiful | 反义词：scarce, meager, scanty, deficient


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The apples are plentiful this year. = 今年苹果很丰收。
There are plentiful resources available. = 有充足的资源可用。


一般过去时:
The land was plentiful with game. = 这片土地上猎物很多。
Food was plentiful during the harvest season. = 收获季节食物充足。


现在完成时:
Resources have been plentiful in recent years. = 近年来资源丰富。
We have had a plentiful supply of water. = 我们一直有充足的水供应。


过去完成时:
The cupboard had been plentiful before the guests arrived. = 客人到来之前橱柜里东西很充足。
They had a plentiful supply of food for the journey. = 他们为旅途准备了充足的食物。


将来时:
The harvest will be plentiful next year. = 明年的收成将会很丰盛。
We anticipate plentiful rainfall this season. = 我们预计本季降雨充沛。


虚拟语气:
If only resources were plentiful, we could solve the problem easily. = 如果资源充足，我们可以很容易地解决这个问题。
I wish the harvest were more plentiful. = 我希望收成更丰盛一些。


被动语态:
The land is plentifully supplied with water. = 这片土地得到了充足的水供应。(被动语态中，plentiful 通常不直接用被动形式)
The meeting was provided with plentiful refreshments. = 会议准备了充足的茶点。(被动语态中，plentiful 通常不直接用被动形式)


疑问句:
Are the resources plentiful enough for the project? = 这些资源足够项目使用吗？
Is the supply of food plentiful in this region? = 这个地区的食物供应充足吗？


否定句:
The harvest wasn't plentiful last year. = 去年收成不好。
The information available is not plentiful. = 可获得的信息并不充足。
