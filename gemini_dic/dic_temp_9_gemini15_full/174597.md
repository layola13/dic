quis: /kwɪs/ | pron. | 谁 |  Interrogative pronoun used to ask about the identity of a person or thing.  It is the nominative singular form of the Latin relative and interrogative pronoun *quis*. | 例句：Quis est? = Who is it?  谁是？


例句：Quis adest? = Who is present? 谁在场？


近义词：who, whom


反义词：N/A (no direct antonym, as it's an interrogative pronoun)


词性: pronoun
