motelier:/moʊtɛˈljɛr/| n. |汽车旅馆老板；汽车旅馆经营者|A motelier is the owner or manager of a motel.  They are responsible for the day-to-day operations of the establishment, including customer service, staff management, and maintenance.|Example: The motelier greeted us warmly and showed us to our room. 汽车旅馆老板热情地迎接我们并带我们去房间。|近义词：innkeeper, hotel manager|反义词：guest, traveler
