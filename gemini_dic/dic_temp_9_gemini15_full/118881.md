metastasizes:/məˈtæstəsaɪz/| v. |转移（癌细胞）|To spread to other sites in the body.|The cancer has metastasized to the lungs.|近义词：spread, disseminate|反义词：localize, remain localized|动词


USAGE->

The cancer metastasizes rapidly in some patients. = 癌症在一些患者体内迅速转移。
This type of tumor rarely metastasizes. = 这种类型的肿瘤很少转移。

一般过去时:
The tumor metastasized to the lymph nodes. = 肿瘤转移到了淋巴结。
