ensiform:/ˈɛnsɪfɔːrm/| adj. |剑形的，剑状的|Shaped like a sword; sword-shaped.  The term refers to a structure that resembles the shape of a sword blade, characterized by a long, slender, and pointed form. | Example: The ensiform cartilage of the sternum is also known as the xiphoid process.  剑状软骨是胸骨的剑突。|近义词：sword-shaped, xiphoid|反义词：rounded, blunt
