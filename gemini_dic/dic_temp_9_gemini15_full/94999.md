in-transit:/ˌɪnˈtrænzɪt/| adj.|在途的；运输中的|Being transported from one place to another; in the process of shipment or delivery.|例句：The package is in-transit and should arrive tomorrow.包裹正在运输中，预计明天到达。|例句：My order is currently in-transit, so I can’t track its exact location.我的订单目前正在运输中，所以我无法追踪它的确切位置。|近义词：en route, underway, on the way|反义词：arrived, delivered
