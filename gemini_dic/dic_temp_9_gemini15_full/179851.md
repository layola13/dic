reexplanation:/ˌriːˌɛkspləˈneɪʃən/ | n. |重新解释，再次解释 |A reexplanation is a second explanation of something that was previously explained, often to clarify or provide more detail. It aims to make the original explanation clearer or more understandable.  | Example:  Due to the confusion, the teacher provided a reexplanation of the complex mathematical concept.  由于存在混淆，老师对复杂的数学概念进行了重新解释。 |近义词：reiteration, clarification, elucidation |反义词：misunderstanding, obfuscation |名词
