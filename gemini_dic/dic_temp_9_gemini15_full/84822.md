hang:/hæŋ/ /hændʒ/| vt. vi. n.|悬挂；绞死；犹豫；拖延|to suspend or be suspended from a point; to execute by hanging; to delay or linger|The picture hangs on the wall.  这幅画挂在墙上。


He hanged himself in despair. 他绝望地自杀了。(注意：hang作“绞死”讲时，过去式为hanged，过去分词为hanged或hung)|近义词：suspend, dangle, delay|反义词：lift, raise, hurry


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He hangs the clothes on the line. 他把衣服挂在晾衣绳上。
The lamp hangs from the ceiling. 灯从天花板上垂下来。

一般过去时:
She hung the painting in the living room. 她把画挂在客厅里。
They hung the decorations for the party. 他们挂起了派对装饰。

现在完成时:
I have hung the laundry outside. 我把洗好的衣服晾到外面了。
He has hung the mirror in the hallway. 他已将镜子挂在走廊里。

过去完成时:
She had hung the curtains before the guests arrived. 客人到达之前，她已经挂好了窗帘。
They had hung the lights before the show began. 演出开始前，他们已经挂好了灯。

将来时:
We will hang the new tapestry tomorrow. 我们明天将挂上新的挂毯。
He will hang the artwork in his office. 他将在他的办公室里悬挂艺术品。

虚拟语气:
If I had more time, I would hang that picture. 如果我有更多时间，我会挂那幅画。
I wish I had hung the washing before the rain started. 我真希望雨开始之前我已经把衣服晾起来了。

被动语态:
The painting was hung in the gallery. 这幅画被挂在画廊里。
The clothes are hung out to dry. 衣服被晾在外面晒干。

疑问句:
Where should I hang this map? 我应该把这张地图挂在哪里？
How is the picture hung? 这幅画是怎么挂上去的？

否定句:
I didn't hang the picture because I didn't have a hammer.我没有挂画，因为我没有锤子。
They didn't hang the decorations until the last minute.他们直到最后一刻才挂装饰品。


PHRASE->
hang around:闲逛
hang on:坚持下去，稍等
hang up:挂断电话
hang out:出去玩
hang together:团结一致
