circumlocutionist: /ˌsɜːrkəmloʊˈkjuːʃənɪst/ | n. | 说话绕弯子的人；冗长乏味的人 | A person who habitually uses circumlocution; a person who speaks in a roundabout way.  They avoid direct and concise language, often using excessive words to express a simple idea. | The politician was a notorious circumlocutionist, known for his lengthy and evasive answers to simple questions.  这位政治家以说话绕弯子而臭名昭著，以对简单问题的冗长而含糊其辞的回答而闻名。|近义词：periphrast, wordy speaker, evader |反义词：conciseness, directness
