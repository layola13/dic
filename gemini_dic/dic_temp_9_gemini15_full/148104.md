overseasoning: /ˌoʊvərˈsiːzənɪŋ/ | n. | 过度调味；调味过多 | The act of adding too much seasoning to food, resulting in an overly strong or unpleasant flavor.  |例句：The overseasoning of the soup ruined its delicate flavor.  这汤调味过重，破坏了它原本细腻的味道。

例句：Her overseasoning of the stew made it inedible. 她把炖菜调味过重，导致它无法食用。


近义词：overspicing


反义词：underseasoning


词性：名词
