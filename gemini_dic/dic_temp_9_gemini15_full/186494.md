ricinolein:/ˌrɪsɪˈnoʊliːn/| n. |蓖麻油酸甘油酯|A triglyceride ester of glycerol and ricinoleic acid, the main constituent of castor oil.  It is a viscous, yellowish oil with a mild odor. |Example: Ricinolein is responsible for many of the properties of castor oil, such as its laxative effect. 蓖麻油酸甘油酯是蓖麻油许多特性的根源，例如其泻药作用。|近义词：castor oil (as a substance)|反义词：无
