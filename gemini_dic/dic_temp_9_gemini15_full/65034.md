etape:/ˈeɪtɑːp/|/ˈeɪtæp/| n. |阶段；路程的一段；驿站|A stage in a journey or process; a leg of a race.  It can also refer to a stopping place on a journey, especially in a relay race.|例句：The race was divided into several etapes.  这场比赛被分成几个阶段。|例句：The cyclists completed the challenging etape in record time.  自行车手以创纪录的时间完成了具有挑战性的路段。|近义词：stage, leg, phase, section|反义词：whole, entirety
