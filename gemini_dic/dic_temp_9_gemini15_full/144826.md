over-laboring: /ˌoʊvərˈleɪbəriŋ/ | v. | 过度劳累；过度工作 |  The act of working excessively or beyond reasonable limits, often leading to exhaustion and reduced efficiency.  It implies a negative connotation, suggesting that the effort is not productive or beneficial due to its excess. |


例句：

He's been over-laboring himself on the project, and it's starting to affect his health. = 他一直在过度工作这个项目，这开始影响他的健康了。

She admitted to over-laboring her essay, resulting in a lack of clarity and originality. = 她承认她在论文上过度劳累，导致缺乏清晰度和原创性。


近义词：overworking, overexerting, straining,  excessively working


反义词：resting, relaxing, idling, loafing


词性: 动词
