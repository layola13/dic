/ˌriːəˈfɪlieɪts/ | v. |重新加入；重新取得联系；使重新加入 | To become a member of an organization again, or to cause someone or something to do this.  | The company has reaffiliated with the trade association after a period of inactivity.  公司在一段时间不活跃后重新加入了行业协会。 |  rejoin, reunite |  disaffiliate, separate |动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reaffiliate with the organization every year. = 他们每年都重新加入该组织。
The school reaffiliates with the national association. = 这所学校重新加入全国协会。


一般过去时:
He reaffiliated with his old college fraternity. = 他重新加入了他以前的大学兄弟会。
The club reaffiliated with the governing body after a dispute. = 在发生争议后，俱乐部重新加入了管理机构。


现在完成时:
They have reaffiliated with the charity after a long break. = 他们在长期中断后重新加入了慈善机构。
The team has reaffiliated and is now competing again. = 该团队已重新加入，现在再次参加比赛。


过去完成时:
She had reaffiliated with the political party before the election. = 她在选举前已经重新加入了该政党。
The hospital had reaffiliated with the university medical center. = 医院已重新加入大学医疗中心。
