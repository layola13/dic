crossover:/ˈkrɒsˌoʊvər/ /ˈkrɒsˌəʊvə(r)/| n. v.|跨界，交叉，混合；越界|A crossover is something that combines elements of two or more different things.  It can refer to a product, an idea, or a person that bridges the gap between distinct categories or genres. As a verb, it means to move from one category or field to another.|例句：The new car is a crossover between an SUV and a sedan. 这款新车是SUV和轿车的混合车型。

The musician's style is a crossover between jazz and pop.这位音乐家的风格融合了爵士乐和流行乐。


近义词：hybrid, blend, combination, intermingling|反义词：separation, division, distinction|名词
