oafishly:/ˈoʊfɪʃli/| adj.|笨拙地；粗鲁地；愚蠢地 |In a clumsy or foolish manner;  showing a lack of grace or sensitivity.  |He oafishly tripped over the rug. 他笨拙地绊倒在地毯上。|She oafishly interrupted the speaker. 她粗鲁地打断了发言者。|近义词：clumsily, awkwardly, gauchely, ineptly |反义词：gracefully, elegantly, skillfully, subtly
