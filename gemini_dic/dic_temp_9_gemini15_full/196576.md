sestet:/ˈsɛstɛt/ | n. |六行诗;六行诗节|A sestet is the last six lines of a sonnet, or a six-line stanza in a poem.  It often marks a shift in tone or focus from the preceding octave (eight-line stanza).|The sestet of the sonnet beautifully resolves the tension built up in the octave.|近义词：six-line stanza|反义词：octave
