modelers:/ˈmɑːdəlɚz/ | n. | 建模者，模型制作人 | People who create models, often in fields like computer science, fashion design, or architecture. They use various techniques and tools to build representations of real-world objects, systems, or concepts.  | Example: The modelers created a detailed 3D model of the city for the new video game. 建模人员为新的电子游戏创建了城市的三维模型。  | 近义词: designers, architects, simulators | 反义词:  N/A
