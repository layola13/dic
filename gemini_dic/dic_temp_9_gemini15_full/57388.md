dozens:/ˈdʌzənz/| n.|几十；许多|A dozen is a group of twelve.  The word often implies an imprecise number, somewhat more than ten but less than a hundred.  It can be used with a plural noun.|例句：There were dozens of people at the concert. 音乐会上来了几十人。

例句：I bought dozens of apples at the market. 我在市场上买了许多苹果。

近义词：many, numerous, scores, a lot of
反义词：few, a handful

词性：名词
