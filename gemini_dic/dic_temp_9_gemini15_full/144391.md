outstride:/ˌaʊtˈstraɪd/| vt.|超过，胜过；超越|To walk with longer steps than (someone); to surpass or exceed (someone or something) in speed or extent.|例句：He outstripped all his rivals in the race. 他在比赛中超过了所有对手。|例句：The success of the film outstripped all expectations. 这部电影的成功超出了所有预期。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outpace, outrun|反义词：lag behind, fall short
