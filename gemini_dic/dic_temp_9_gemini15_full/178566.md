recorders:/rɪˈkɔːrdərz/| n.|录音机；记录者；（尤指）笛子|A device used for recording sound or a person or thing that records something.  Also, a type of woodwind instrument.|例句：We used a cassette recorder to capture the concert. 我们用盒式录音机录下了音乐会。

例句：The historian meticulously recorded the events of the war. 这名历史学家一丝不苟地记录了战争的事件。


近义词：recording device, documenters, chroniclers |反义词：N/A
