nestler:/ˈnɛslər/| n.| 筑巢者；尤指鸟类 | A nestler is an animal, usually a bird, that builds a nest.  |例句：The mother bird was a diligent nestler, carefully weaving twigs and leaves.  这只母鸟是个勤劳的筑巢者，仔细地编织着树枝和树叶。

例句：Many species of birds are excellent nestlers, creating intricate and well-camouflaged nests.  许多鸟类都是优秀的筑巢者，能建造复杂且伪装良好的巢穴。|近义词：bird builder, nest-maker |反义词：None specifically.  |词性 n.
