flowerful: /ˈflaʊərfl/ | adj. | 花一般的，充满花的，繁花似锦的 |  Descriptive; suggesting abundance of flowers. The word implies a profusion or beauty associated with flowers. It's not a common word and often feels slightly artificial or poetic. | 例句：The flowerful meadows stretched as far as the eye could see.  =  繁花似锦的草地一直延伸到视线尽头。

The garden was flowerful in spring. = 春天花园里鲜花盛开。


近义词：floral, flowery, blossoming, blooming | 反义词：barren, desolate, flowerless


词性：形容词
