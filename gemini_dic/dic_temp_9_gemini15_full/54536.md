dislike:/dɪsˈlaɪk/| vt. n.|不喜欢，厌恶|to feel or express a lack of liking for something or someone | I dislike the cold weather. 我不喜欢寒冷的天气。|近义词：abhor, detest, hate|反义词：like, love|动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
I dislike spicy food. = 我不喜欢辛辣的食物。
He dislikes early mornings. = 他不喜欢早起。

一般过去时:
She disliked the movie. = 她不喜欢那部电影。
They disliked his behavior. = 他们不喜欢他的行为。

现在完成时:
I have disliked him since we were children. = 从我们还是孩子的时候，我就讨厌他了。
She has disliked her job for years. = 她多年来一直不喜欢她的工作。

过去完成时:
I had disliked the teacher before the incident. = 在那件事发生之前，我就已经不喜欢那个老师了。
He had disliked her attitude for a long time. = 他很久以前就不喜欢她的态度了。


将来时:
I will dislike this job if I get it. = 如果我得到这份工作，我将会不喜欢它。
She will dislike the new rules. = 她会不喜欢新的规章制度。

虚拟语气:
I would dislike to see him suffer. = 我不想看到他受苦。
She wishes she didn't dislike her boss so much. = 她希望自己不要那么讨厌她的老板。

被动语态:
(Dislike is rarely used in the passive voice.  A more natural phrasing would be used.)  His behavior was disliked by many. = 他的行为不受许多人喜欢。
The new policy was disliked by the employees. = 新政策不受员工欢迎。

疑问句:
Do you dislike this color? = 你不喜欢这种颜色吗？
Does he dislike working overtime? = 他不喜欢加班吗？

否定句:
I don't dislike classical music. = 我不讨厌古典音乐。
He doesn't dislike her, he just doesn't understand her. = 他不喜欢她，他只是不理解她。


PHRASE->
dislike doing sth: 不喜欢做某事
have a dislike for sth/sb: 讨厌某事/某人
take a dislike to sth/sb: 开始讨厌某事/某人
show a dislike for sth/sb: 表露出对某事/某人的厌恶
strong dislike: 强烈的厌恶
