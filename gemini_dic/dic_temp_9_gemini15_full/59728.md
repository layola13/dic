eclogue:/ˈɛklɒɡ/| n. |田园诗；牧歌|A short poem, usually pastoral in setting, that typically features dialogue between shepherds.  It often deals with themes of love, nature, and simple rural life.  The form is associated with Theocritus and Virgil.|Example:  The poet's eclogue beautifully captured the idyllic serenity of the countryside. 这首诗人的田园诗完美地捕捉了乡村田园般的宁静。  The anthology included several eclogues celebrating the beauty of spring. 这部选集收录了几首赞美春天美丽的牧歌。|近义词：pastoral, idyll, bucolic|反义词：epic, tragedy|名词
