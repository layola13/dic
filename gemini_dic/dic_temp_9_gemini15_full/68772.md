farrowing:/ˈfærɔːɪŋ/ | n. | 母猪产仔；分娩（猪） | The act of a sow giving birth to piglets.  It encompasses the entire process, from the onset of labor to the delivery of the last piglet. | Example: The farmer carefully monitored the sow during farrowing to ensure a safe delivery.  = 农民在母猪分娩期间仔细观察，以确保安全分娩。  The farrowing rate was high this year, resulting in a large number of piglets. = 今年的母猪产仔率很高，产下了大量的仔猪。| 近义词：parturition, delivery (in pigs) | 反义词：N/A
