intercedes:/ˌɪntərˈsiːdz/| vt.|调解；说情；代祷|To intercede is to intervene on behalf of another person.  It often implies pleading or mediating to resolve a conflict or improve a situation.  The act of interceding usually involves a third party stepping in to help two other parties reconcile or reach an agreement.|例句：The priest interceded on behalf of the condemned man.  牧师为那个被判刑的人求情。


The teacher interceded in the argument between the two students. 老师调解了两个学生之间的争吵。

近义词：mediate, plead, advocate, intervene|反义词：ignore, neglect, abandon|动词
