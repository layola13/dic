proportionating: /prəˌpɔːrʃənɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 使成比例；使均衡 | To make something proportionate or adjust the proportions of something.  It involves adjusting the relative amounts or sizes of different parts to achieve a balanced or harmonious whole.  The word is often used in contexts where precise measurement and adjustment are required. | Example: The architect carefully proportionated the windows to the size of the rooms, ensuring a balanced aesthetic. 建筑师仔细地将窗户与房间大小成比例，以确保均衡的美感。 | 近义词: balancing, adjusting, regulating, scaling | 反义词: disproportionating, unbalancing
