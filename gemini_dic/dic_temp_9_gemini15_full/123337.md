monoicous:/məˈnɔɪkəs/| adj. |雌雄同株的；单性花的| Having both male and female reproductive organs in the same flower or plant.  Monoecious plants have separate male and female flowers on the same plant, unlike dioecious plants which have separate male and female plants.| Example: Corn is a monoicous plant, with separate male and female flowers on the same stalk. 玉米是雌雄同株的植物，同一茎上有分开的雄花和雌花。|近义词：hermaphrodite, androgynous|反义词：dioecious
