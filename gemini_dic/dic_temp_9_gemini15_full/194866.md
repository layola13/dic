semi-conductor:/ˈsɛmiˌkənˌdʌktər/ | n. | 半导体 | A substance whose electrical conductivity is intermediate between that of a conductor and an insulator, and is highly sensitive to temperature and the presence of impurities.  Semiconductors are used in many electronic devices, such as transistors and integrated circuits. | Example: Silicon is a common semiconductor used in computer chips.  硅是一种常用的用于计算机芯片的半导体。  |近义词:  None (it's a specific type of material) | 反义词: conductor, insulator |
