prostrative: /ˈprɒstrətɪv/ | adj. | 俯伏的；极度衰弱的；卑躬屈膝的 | Extremely humble and submissive; showing great respect.  Characterized by or showing physical or mental weakness or exhaustion. |

英文解析：The word "prostrative" describes someone or something that is lying flat, especially as a sign of submission or exhaustion.  It can refer to a physical posture or a state of extreme weakness or dejection.  The emphasis is on the utter humbleness and lack of strength, either physically or spiritually.

例句：

He lay in a prostrative position before the altar.  他俯伏在祭坛前。

The illness left him prostrative and weak.  这场疾病使他虚弱无力。


近义词：submissive, humble, prostrate, supplicant, abject, weak, debilitated, exhausted

反义词：proud, defiant, assertive, strong, vigorous, powerful
