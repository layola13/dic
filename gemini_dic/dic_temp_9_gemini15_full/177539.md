rebored:/ˌriːˈbɔːrd/| v.|重新镗孔，重新钻孔|To bore (a hole) again.  This usually implies enlarging an existing hole or creating a new one in a previously bored item.|例句：The mechanic had to rebore the engine cylinder to fix the damage. 机械师不得不重新镗孔发动机气缸以修复损坏。|例句：The old well needed to be re-bored to increase its water yield. 旧井需要重新钻孔以增加其出水量。|近义词：re-drill, enlarge|反义词：bore (initial drilling)
