propagandized:/ˌprɒpəˈɡændaɪzd/|/ˈprɑːpəɡændaɪzd/| v. |宣传；进行宣传 |To spread propaganda; to subject to propaganda.  The government propagandized its policies through state-controlled media.  政府通过国家控制的媒体宣传其政策。|近义词：advertised, promoted, indoctrinated |反义词：suppressed, silenced


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They propagandize their ideology through social media. = 他们通过社交媒体宣传他们的意识形态。
The company propagandizes its products with misleading advertisements. = 这家公司用误导性广告宣传其产品。


一般过去时:
The regime propagandized its successes to the public. = 该政权向公众宣传其成就。
The cult leader propagandized his followers into unquestioning obedience. = 邪教领袖通过宣传使他的追随者盲目服从。


现在完成时:
The media has propagandized the public into fearing immigrants. = 媒体已经通过宣传使公众害怕移民。
The government has propagandized its achievements for decades. = 政府几十年来一直在宣传其成就。


过去完成时:
They had propagandized the population for years before the revolution. = 他们在革命前多年来一直在向民众进行宣传。
The party had propagandized its ideology since its inception. = 该党自成立以来就一直在宣传其意识形态。
