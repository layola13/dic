enthrall:/ɪnˈθrɔːl/| vt.|使着迷；使入迷；迷住|To hold the attention of (someone) completely; fascinate.  |例句：The children were enthralled by the story. 孩子们被这个故事迷住了。|例句：The magician's performance enthralled the audience. 魔术师的表演迷住了观众。|近义词：captivate, fascinate, enchant, charm|反义词：bore, repel


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The music enthralls the audience. = 音乐使观众着迷。
The beautiful scenery enthralls her. = 美丽的风景使她着迷。

一般过去时:
The movie enthralled me. = 这部电影迷住了我。
The book enthralled her for hours. = 这本书让她着迷了好几个小时。

现在完成时:
The beauty of nature has enthralled me since childhood. = 自小到大，大自然的美丽就一直让我着迷。
His stories have enthralled generations of readers. = 他的故事迷住了几代读者。

过去完成时:
She had been enthralled by his charm before she even knew his name. = 在她甚至不知道他的名字之前，她就已被他的魅力所吸引。
I had been enthralled by the performance before the final act began. = 在最后一幕开始之前，我就已经被演出迷住了。

将来时:
I am sure this concert will enthrall you. = 我确信这场音乐会会让你着迷。
The new play is expected to enthrall audiences worldwide. = 这部新剧预计将迷住全世界的观众。

虚拟语气:
If the story were more engaging, it would enthrall the readers. = 如果故事更引人入胜，它就会迷住读者。
I wish the movie had enthralled me more. = 我希望这部电影能让我更着迷。

被动语态:
The audience was enthralled by the performance. = 观众被演出迷住了。
We were enthralled by the breathtaking view. = 我们被令人惊叹的景色迷住了。

疑问句:
Does the book enthrall you? = 这本书让你着迷吗？
Will the play enthrall the critics? = 这部戏会迷住评论家吗？

否定句:
The lecture did not enthrall the students. = 讲座并没有让学生着迷。
The movie failed to enthrall me. = 这部电影没能让我着迷。


PHRASE->

become enthralled = 开始着迷

utterly enthralled = 完全被迷住
completely enthralled = 完全被迷住


词源:
enthrall 源自古英语 *in-thrallen，其中 in- 是一个前缀，表示“in”或“into”，而 thrall 指的是“奴隶”或“束缚”。因此，enthrall 的字面意思是“使成为奴隶”或“完全控制”，引申为“迷住”、“使着迷”。  这个词的发展体现了语言的演变，从字面意义的“奴役”到比喻意义的“迷恋”。
