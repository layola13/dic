plummily:/ˈplʌməli/| adv.| 丰满地；丰腴地；圆润地 |In a plump or rounded manner.  It suggests a softness and fullness of form.|例句：She sat plummily in the armchair. 她丰满地坐在扶手椅上。

例句：The ripe plum fell plummily to the ground. 熟透的李子丰满地掉到了地上。


近义词：roundly, plumply, full-bodiedly


反义词：thinly, scrawny, angularly

词性: 副词
