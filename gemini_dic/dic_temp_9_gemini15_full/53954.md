discredence:/dɪsˈkredəns/| n. |不信任，怀疑 |The lack of belief or trust.  It suggests a loss of confidence or reputation. |例句：His controversial statements brought his leadership into discredence.  他的争议性言论使他的领导能力受到了质疑。|例句：The new evidence cast discredence on the defendant's alibi. 新证据使被告的不在场证明令人怀疑。|近义词：distrust, suspicion, doubt, disbelief |反义词：belief, trust, confidence, faith |名词
