canonry:/ˈkænənri/| n.|教士区；教士俸禄；教士职位 | A canonry is a position or benefice held by a canon in a cathedral or collegiate church, often involving specific duties and the right to receive income from the church's endowments.  It also refers to the residence or dwelling of a canon. | Example:  He inherited a lucrative canonry at Canterbury Cathedral. 他继承了坎特伯雷大教堂一份丰厚的教士俸禄。

The canonry provided him with a comfortable living. 教士职位为他提供了舒适的生活。|近义词：prebend, benefice |反义词：lay position (世俗职位)
