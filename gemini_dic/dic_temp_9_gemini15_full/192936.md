seborrhea: /ˌsɛbəˈriːə/ | n. | 油脂溢出；脂溢性皮炎 | A skin condition characterized by an excessive production of sebum (oil) by the sebaceous glands.  It can manifest as dandruff, cradle cap in infants, or other forms of scaling and inflammation. | Example:  She suffers from seborrheic dermatitis, a type of seborrhea. 她患有脂溢性皮炎，这是一种脂溢性皮炎。|近义词：dandruff (if referring to scalp), cradle cap (in infants) |反义词：dry skin
