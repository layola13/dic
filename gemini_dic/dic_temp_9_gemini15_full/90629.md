humanistically:/ˌhjuːməˈnɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 人道主义地；人本主义地 |  In a way that shows concern for human welfare and values.  It emphasizes human agency, reason, and ethics. | Example: The project was approached humanistically, prioritizing the needs of the community.  这个项目以人道主义的方式进行，优先考虑社区的需求。 | 近义词：compassionately, empathetically, ethically | 反义词：inhumanely, cruelly, callously
