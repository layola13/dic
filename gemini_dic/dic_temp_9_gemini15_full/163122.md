precolorations:/ˌpriːkʌləˈreɪʃənz/| n.|预着色；预先染色|The process of coloring something before it is used or processed further.  This often refers to pre-treating materials like fabrics or yarns before dyeing or printing. For example, in textile manufacturing, precolorations might involve applying a base color to create a more even final result.|例句：The fabric underwent precolorations to ensure a consistent dye uptake. = 这块布料经过预先染色以确保染料吸收均匀。|例句：Precolorations of the yarn improved the brightness of the finished product. = 预先染色纱线提高了成品的亮度。|近义词：pretreatment, pre-dyeing|反义词：post-coloring
