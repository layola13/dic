galoshes:/ɡəˈlɒʃɪz/| n.|胶鞋，套鞋|Over-shoes, typically made of rubber, worn over other shoes to protect them from water and mud.  They are often high-topped and waterproof.|例句：I wore my galoshes to keep my feet dry in the rain.  我穿上了我的胶鞋以保持我的脚在雨中干燥。

例句：She put on her galoshes before venturing out into the snow. 她穿上胶鞋才敢冒雪出门。


近义词：overshoes, rubbers|反义词：sandals, slippers
