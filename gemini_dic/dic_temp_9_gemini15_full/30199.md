celibacy:/ˈsɛləbəsi/| n. |独身；独身生活 |The state of abstaining from marriage and sexual relations.  Celibacy is often practiced for religious or personal reasons. |例句：He chose a life of celibacy after his wife passed away. 他妻子去世后选择了独身生活。|例句：Many priests take a vow of celibacy. 许多牧师都发誓保持独身。|近义词：abstinence, chastity |反义词：marriage, promiscuity |名词
