notebook:/ˈnoʊtbʊk/ | n. |笔记本，笔记簿 | A notebook is a small, bound book with blank pages for writing notes, drawing, or other purposes.  It can be used for various purposes, from taking notes in class to sketching ideas. | Example: I always carry a notebook to jot down my ideas. 我总是随身携带一个笔记本记下我的想法。 I need a new notebook for school. 我需要一本新的笔记本上学。|近义词：pad, journal, diary |反义词：N/A
