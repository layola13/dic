reembark:/ˌriːɪmˈbɑːrk/| vt.|重新登船；重新开始；再次从事|To embark again; to begin again; to resume an activity or undertaking.|例句：After a brief respite, they reembarked on their journey.  =短暂休息后，他们重新开始了旅程。

例句：He reembarked on his writing career after a long break. = 长期休息后，他重新开始了他的写作生涯。


近义词：restart, recommence, resume|反义词：disembark, abandon, cease|动词
