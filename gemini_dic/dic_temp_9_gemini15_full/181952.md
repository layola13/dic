remarriage:/ˌriːˈmæriɪdʒ/ | n. |再婚，续弦 | The act of marrying again after being divorced or widowed.  It implies a previous marriage that has ended.  | Example: Her remarriage to a younger man surprised everyone.  她的再婚对象是一个比她年轻的男人，这让所有人都很惊讶。  |近义词：second marriage,  |反义词：first marriage, singlehood |名词
