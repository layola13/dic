procompetition: /ˌproʊˌkɑːmpəˈtɪʃən/ | adj. | 促进竞争的；支持竞争的 | Promoting or supporting competition.  The term describes policies, actions, or behaviors that encourage competition in a market.  It's the opposite of anti-competitive. |

例句：

The government implemented procompetition policies to stimulate economic growth. = 政府实施了促进竞争的政策以刺激经济增长。

Procompetition measures are essential for a healthy and dynamic market. = 促进竞争的措施对于一个健康和充满活力的市场至关重要。


近义词：competitive, pro-competitive

反义词：anticompetitive, monopolistic


词性：形容词
