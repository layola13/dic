microbrewer:/ˈmaɪkroʊbruːər/ | n. |小型啤酒厂；精酿啤酒厂 | A microbrewery is a small brewery that produces beer on a smaller scale than large commercial breweries.  They often focus on unique and handcrafted beers. | Example:  The local microbrewery won an award for its innovative stout. 当地这家小型啤酒厂因其创新的世涛啤酒而获奖。|近义词：craft brewery, small brewery |反义词：macrobrewery, large-scale brewery
