reirrigate:/ˌriːˈɪrɪɡeɪt/| vt.|再次灌溉；重新灌溉|To irrigate again or anew.|The farmer had to reirrigate his fields after the recent dry spell.  农民最近经历了干旱期，不得不再次灌溉他的田地。
The gardener reirrigated the thirsty plants after a long hot day. 园丁在漫长炎热的一天之后再次灌溉了口渴的植物。|irrigate, water|drain, dry|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
They reirrigate the crops regularly. = 他们定期对农作物进行再次灌溉。
He reirrigates his lawn every week. = 他每周都给他的草坪重新浇水。

一般过去时:
The farmer reirrigated the parched land yesterday. = 农民昨天重新灌溉了干旱的土地。
She reirrigated her garden after the drought. = 干旱后，她重新灌溉了她的花园。

现在完成时:
They have reirrigated the fields twice this month. = 这个月他们已经两次重新灌溉了田地。
We have reirrigated the garden because of the heat wave. = 因为热浪，我们已经重新灌溉了花园。

过去完成时:
The plants had already been reirrigated before the inspection. = 在检查之前，植物已经被重新灌溉过了。
The farmer had reirrigated the crops before the storm hit. = 暴风雨来临之前，农民已经重新灌溉了农作物。

将来时:
We will reirrigate the orchard next week. = 我们下周将重新灌溉果园。
They will reirrigate the fields if the rain doesn't come soon. = 如果雨水很快不来，他们将重新灌溉田地。

虚拟语气:
If it didn't rain, we would have to reirrigate the lawn. = 如果不下雨，我们就必须重新灌溉草坪。
It would be better if they reirrigated the crops more frequently. = 如果他们更频繁地重新灌溉农作物会更好。
