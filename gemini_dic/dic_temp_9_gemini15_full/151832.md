passes:/ˈpæsɪz/ | n. v. | 通过；通行证；及格；度过 |  The act of moving past something or someone, or the state of being granted permission or approval.  |例句：The bill passed Congress. 这项法案通过了国会。

The runner easily passes his competitors.  这位跑步者轻松超越了他的竞争对手。


近义词：approves, succeeds, transcends, goes through, surpasses | 反义词：fails, rejects

词性：名词，动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He passes the ball to his teammate. 他把球传给他的队友。
She passes the test with flying colors. 她以优异的成绩通过了考试。

一般过去时:
The train passed through the tunnel. 火车穿过隧道。

过去完成时:
The deadline had passed before he submitted his work. 他提交作业之前截止日期已经过了。
They had passed the mountain before nightfall. 他们在夜幕降临之前翻过了山。

将来时:
