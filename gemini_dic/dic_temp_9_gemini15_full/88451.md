histology:/hɪˈstɒlədʒi/ | n. |组织学|The study of the microscopic anatomy of cells and tissues of plants and animals.  It involves examining thin sections of tissues under a microscope to identify different cell types and their arrangement. Histology is crucial in diagnosing diseases, as many diseases cause characteristic changes in tissue structure.|Example:  The pathologist performed a histology examination of the biopsy sample. 病理学家对活检样本进行了组织学检查。|近义词：tissue study, microscopic anatomy |反义词：macroscopic anatomy
