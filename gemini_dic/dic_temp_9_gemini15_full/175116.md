raffle:/ˈræfl/| n. v.|抽奖；彩票；进行抽奖|A raffle is a type of lottery where participants buy tickets for a chance to win a prize.  The winner is typically chosen randomly.  It can also refer to the act of organizing or conducting such a lottery.|例句：They're holding a raffle to raise money for the school. 他们正在举行抽奖活动为学校筹款。

The school held a raffle to raise funds. 学校举办了一场抽奖活动来筹集资金。|近义词：lottery, sweepstakes|反义词：None
