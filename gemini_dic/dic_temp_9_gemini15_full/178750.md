rector:/ˈrektər/| n.|教区牧师；校长；管理者|The rector of a church is the priest in charge of it.  A rector can also be the head of a college or school.  The term implies a position of authority and responsibility. |例句：The rector delivered a powerful sermon on Sunday. 教区牧师在周日发表了一篇充满力量的布道。

The school rector announced the new academic year. 学校校长宣布了新学年开始。|近义词：priest, pastor, headmaster, principal, president|反义词： parishioner, student
