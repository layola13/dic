munjeet:/ˈmʌndʒiːt/| n. |茜草(一种植物)|A climbing plant, *Rubia cordifolia*, whose roots yield a reddish-brown dye used in dyeing fabrics.  The dye is also known as munjeet dye.|The vibrant color of the sari was achieved using munjeet, a traditional Indian dye.|近义词: madder (though munjeet is a specific type of madder)|反义词: N/A
