extradites:/ˈekstrədaɪts/| vt.|引渡，遣送出境|To extradite someone means to officially hand them over to the authorities of another country to be tried for a crime.|The fugitive was extradited from Mexico to the United States to face charges of murder.|近义词：deport, expel|反义词：repatriate, welcome


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government extradites criminals to other countries. = 政府将罪犯引渡到其他国家。
They extradite suspects based on international treaties. = 他们根据国际条约引渡嫌疑人。

一般过去时:
The court ordered that he be extradited. = 法院下令将他引渡。
The country extradited the suspect to face trial. = 该国引渡了嫌疑人以接受审判。

现在完成时:
The US has extradited many fugitives this year. = 美国今年已引渡了许多逃犯。
Several countries have extradited individuals accused of terrorism. = 几个国家已经引渡了被指控犯有恐怖主义罪行的人。

过去完成时:
The suspect had been extradited before the trial. = 嫌疑人在审判前已被引渡。
They had already extradited him when the new evidence emerged. = 当新的证据出现时，他们已经将他引渡了。

将来时:
The government will extradite him as soon as possible. = 政府将尽快将他引渡。
They will extradite the accused to stand trial in their home country. = 他们将把被告引渡回国受审。

虚拟语气:
If they had sufficient evidence, they would extradite him. = 如果他们有足够的证据，他们就会将他引渡。
It would be better if the suspect were extradited immediately. = 如果嫌疑人立即被引渡会更好。

被动语态:
He was extradited to France to face charges. = 他被引渡到法国接受指控。
The criminal was extradited under a bilateral agreement. = 该罪犯根据双边协议被引渡。

疑问句:
Will he be extradited? = 他会被引渡吗？
Has the suspect been extradited yet? = 嫌疑人已经被引渡了吗？

否定句:
The government refused to extradite him. = 政府拒绝将他引渡。
They did not extradite the suspect due to lack of evidence. = 由于缺乏证据，他们没有引渡嫌疑人。


PHRASE->
to extradite someone to... = 将某人引渡到……
to be extradited from... = 从……被引渡
extradition treaty/agreement = 引渡条约/协议
request for extradition = 引渡请求
to fight extradition = 抵抗引渡
