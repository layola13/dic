reduced:/rɪˈduːst/| adj. | 减少的；缩减的；简化的 |  Reduced means made smaller or less in size, amount, or degree.  It can refer to something that has been lessened or diminished. |例句：The company reduced its workforce by 10%.  公司裁员10%。

The price of petrol has been reduced.  汽油价格已经降低了。


近义词：diminished, lessened, decreased, curtailed, abridged | 反义词：increased, enlarged, augmented, expanded | 词性：形容词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The government reduces taxes annually. = 政府每年都会减税。
The chef reduces the sauce until it thickens. = 厨师把酱汁浓缩到变稠。


一般过去时:
They reduced the speed limit after the accident. = 事故后他们降低了速度限制。
She reduced her spending to save money. = 她减少了开支以省钱。


现在完成时:
The company has reduced its carbon footprint significantly. = 公司已经显著减少了碳足迹。
We have reduced our reliance on fossil fuels. = 我们已经减少了对化石燃料的依赖。


过去完成时:
They had reduced the price before the sale started. = 他们在促销开始之前就降价了。
The team had reduced the workload before the deadline. = 团队在截止日期之前减少了工作量。


将来时:
The government will reduce taxes next year. = 政府明年将减税。
We will reduce our consumption of sugar. = 我们将减少糖的摄入量。


虚拟语气:
If they reduced the price, I would buy it. = 如果他们降价，我会买。
I wish they had reduced the workload earlier. = 我希望他们早点减少工作量。


被动语态:
The budget was reduced by 10%. = 预算减少了10%。
The noise was reduced by adding soundproofing. = 通过添加隔音材料降低了噪音。


疑问句:
Has the company reduced its emissions? = 公司是否减少了排放？
Have they reduced the number of employees? = 他们是否减少了员工人数？


否定句:
The company did not reduce its spending. = 公司没有减少开支。
They haven't reduced the workload yet. = 他们还没有减少工作量。
