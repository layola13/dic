self-therapy:/ˈsɛlf θɛrəpi/ | n. | 自我疗法 | The practice of using techniques to improve one's mental or emotional well-being without professional guidance.  It involves self-help strategies, such as mindfulness, journaling, and cognitive restructuring, to address personal challenges and promote healing. | Example:  She engaged in self-therapy techniques like meditation to manage her anxiety. 她通过冥想等自我疗法来控制焦虑。 |近义词: self-help, self-care, personal growth | 反义词: professional therapy, psychotherapy
