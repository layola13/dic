pasquin:/ˈpæskwɪn/| n. |小册子；讽刺文章；诽谤；|A pasquin is a satirical lampoon, usually posted publicly.  It's a short, witty, and often scurrilous writing intended to ridicule or criticize someone or something.  The term often refers to a written attack posted in a public place, similar to a modern-day internet meme or troll comment. |Example: The anonymous pasquin nailed to the town hall door caused quite a stir.  这贴在市政厅门口的匿名小册子引起了不小的骚动。|近义词：lampoon, satire, libel,  pamphlet|反义词：praise, eulogy, commendation|名词
