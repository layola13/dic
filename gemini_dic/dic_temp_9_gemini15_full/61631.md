emigrator:/ˈɛməɡreɪtər/ | n. | 移民者，移居国外者 | A person who emigrates from their native country to another.  | Example:  He is an emigrator from Ireland to Australia. 他是从爱尔兰移居澳大利亚的移民。 |近义词: immigrant (in the context of the destination country), expatriate |反义词: immigrant (in the context of the origin country), repatriate
