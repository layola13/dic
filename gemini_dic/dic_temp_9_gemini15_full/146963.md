overissue:/ˌoʊvərˈɪʃuː/| v.| 超额发行；过度发行 | To issue or produce more of something than is needed, allowed, or intended.  |例句：The bank overissued loans, leading to a financial crisis. 银行超额发放贷款，导致金融危机。
The company overissued shares, diluting the value of existing stock. 公司超额发行股票，稀释了现有股票的价值。|近义词：surplus, excess, oversupply |反义词：undersupply, shortage
