metrification:/ˌmɛtrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |度量衡制；公制化；韵律化|The process of converting something to the metric system or applying metric principles.  It can also refer to the process of giving something a rhythmic structure or pattern.|例句：The metrification of the country's measurement system was a gradual process.  国家的度量衡系统公制化是一个循序渐进的过程。|例句：The poet's careful metrification gave the poem a pleasing musicality.  诗人细致的韵律化使这首诗具有令人愉悦的音乐性。|近义词：standardization, systematization|反义词：de-standardization, randomization|名词
