depositions:/ˌdɛpəˈzɪʃənz/ | n. | 证词；沉淀物 | Written or oral statements made under oath, usually in legal proceedings.  Also refers to the process of settling or depositing. | 例句：The lawyer reviewed the depositions before the trial.  律师在审判前审查了证词。

The scientist observed the depositions of the minerals in the solution. 科学家观察了溶液中矿物的沉淀。| 近义词：testimony, statements, affidavits, sedimentation | 反义词： (for legal sense)  n/a; (for physical sense) dissolution, suspension
