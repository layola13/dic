ending:/ˈendɪŋ/ | n. |结局，结尾；终结；结果 | The ending of the movie was unexpected.  电影的结局出乎意料。| 近义词：conclusion, termination, finale | 反义词：beginning, start


The ending refers to the final part or conclusion of something, such as a story, a period of time, or a process. It can also refer to the result or outcome of an event or action.


例句:

The book had a happy ending. 这本书有一个快乐的结局。

The ending of the war brought relief to many. 战争的结束给许多人带来了解脱。
