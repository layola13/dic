rutty:/ˈrʌti/| adj.|坑坑洼洼的；破旧的；陈旧的 | Full of ruts; uneven and bumpy.  Characterized by being worn out or old. |例句：The rutty road made the journey uncomfortable.  = 坑坑洼洼的路使得旅程很不舒服。

The car's tires were damaged by the rutty terrain. =  坑洼的地形损坏了汽车的轮胎。


近义词：bumpy, rough, uneven, dilapidated, worn


反义词：smooth, level, even, new, pristine
