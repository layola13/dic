potterers:/ˈpɒtərərz/| n. |磨蹭的人；闲逛的人；笨手笨脚的人|People who potter, spending time on small tasks or activities in a leisurely way, often without much purpose. They are usually characterized by slowness and a lack of efficiency.  |The old man spent his days potterers in his garden.  这位老人整天在花园里闲逛。|dawdlers, idlers, dilettantes|workers, professionals|名词
