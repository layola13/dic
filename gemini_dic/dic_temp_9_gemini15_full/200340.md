skunkish:/ˈskʌŋkɪʃ/| adj.|臭的；令人厌恶的；卑鄙的；讨厌的 |Having the unpleasant qualities of a skunk; having a strong, unpleasant smell; mean or unpleasant.  |例句：The skunkish odor lingered in the air after the skunk had passed. 臭鼬走后，一股臭鼬的气味久久不愿散去。


The room had a slightly skunkish smell after the cat sprayed. 猫喷洒后房间里有一股轻微的臭味。

近义词：offensive, unpleasant, repulsive, nasty, vile, despicable,  malodorous

反义词：pleasant, agreeable, delightful, fragrant, sweet-smelling


词源:  Derived from "skunk," referring to the animal known for its foul-smelling spray.  The adjective adopts the animal's characteristic odor to describe something unpleasant or offensive.
