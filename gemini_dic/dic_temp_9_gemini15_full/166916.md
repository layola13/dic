prestiges:/ˈprɛstɪdʒ/| n.|声望，威望，声誉|High prestige is often associated with high social status and power.  高声望通常与较高的社会地位和权力相关。|例句：The company enjoys high prestige in the industry. 这家公司在业界享有很高的声誉。|近义词：reputation, renown, status|反义词：disrepute, disgrace, infamy|名词


USAGE->
