candler:/ˈkændlər/| n. |烛台制造者；烛台；（尤指）蜡烛制造商|A candler is a person who makes or sells candles, or a candlestick.  The term can also refer to a device used in candle making. |Example: The old candler's shop still smelled faintly of beeswax.  古老的烛台制造者的商店仍然隐隐约约地散发着蜂蜡的气味。|近义词：candlestick maker, chandler (更常用)|反义词：无

They made many candles last year. = 他们去年做了很多蜡烛。
