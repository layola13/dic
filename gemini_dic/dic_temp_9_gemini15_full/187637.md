rondo:/ˈrɒndoʊ/| n. |轮旋曲，回旋曲 |A rondo is a musical form in which a main theme (the "A" section) alternates with contrasting sections (the "B," "C," etc., sections).  The "A" section typically returns several times throughout the piece, providing a sense of unity and structure.  It's often characterized by its lively and repetitive nature. |Example:  The composer cleverly used the rondo form to create a sense of playful energy in his symphony.  作曲家巧妙地运用回旋曲式在他的交响乐中营造出一种嬉戏的活力。|近义词：roundelay (less common)|反义词：无
