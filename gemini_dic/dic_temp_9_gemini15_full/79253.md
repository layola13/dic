glasshouses:/ˈɡlæsˌhaʊzɪz/| n. |玻璃温室；玻璃房；易受批评的人或事 |A glasshouse is a building made of glass, used for growing plants.  The term also figuratively refers to a situation or person who is easily criticized because of their own flaws or vulnerabilities. |例句：He shouldn't throw stones when he lives in a glasshouse. 他自己也有缺点，却批评别人是不对的。|例句：The gardener carefully tended the plants in the glasshouse. 园丁仔细照料玻璃温室里的植物。|近义词：greenhouse, conservatory |反义词：open air,  unprotected structure
