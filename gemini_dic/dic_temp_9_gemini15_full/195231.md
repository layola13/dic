semiexhibitionists:/ˌsemiˌɛksɪbɪˈʃənɪsts/| n. |半炫耀者，半公开展示者|A semiexhibitionist is someone who displays a tendency towards exhibitionism but to a lesser degree or in a more subtle way than a full-blown exhibitionist. They might enjoy attention but not to the extent of seeking it out aggressively or behaving in overtly shocking ways.  They might subtly flaunt their achievements or possessions, or seek attention through indirect means. |The artist, though not a full-blown exhibitionist, possessed certain semiexhibitionistic tendencies, often subtly drawing attention to his work through carefully placed interviews and social media posts.|近义词：exhibitionist (to a lesser degree), attention-seeker|反义词：shy, withdrawn, recluse
