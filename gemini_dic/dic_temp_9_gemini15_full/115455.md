manubrium:/məˈnuːbrɪəm/| n. |胸骨柄，柄状突起|The manubrium is the uppermost part of the sternum, a flat bone located in the anterior (front) part of the chest. It articulates with the clavicles (collarbones) and the first two ribs.  |例句：The fracture involved the manubrium of the sternum. = 骨折涉及胸骨柄。

例句：The surgeon carefully examined the patient's fractured manubrium. = 外科医生仔细检查了患者骨折的胸骨柄。


近义词：handle (in some contexts), upper sternum


反义词：无


词源:  From Latin *manubrium, from manus ("hand") + -brium (suffix forming nouns of agency or instrumentality).  Literally meaning "handle" or "something held in the hand," it was applied anatomically due to its shape resembling a handle.
