refenced:/ˈrɛfənst/ | adj. | 参考的；引用的 |  Referring to or based on a specified source of information.  This word is likely a misspelling of "referenced".  | 例句：The article contained several referenced studies. 这篇文章包含了几项参考的研究。 | 近义词：cited, quoted, mentioned | 反义词：original, unreferenced |  adj.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The report is referenced in the bibliography. =这份报告在参考文献中被提及。
This data is referenced by multiple sources. = 这数据被多个来源引用。


一般过去时:
The professor referenced a classic text during his lecture. = 教授在讲座中引用了一部经典著作。
The article referenced several previous studies. = 这篇文章引用了几篇之前的研究。


现在完成时:
The researcher has referenced this article many times in his work. = 这位研究人员在他的作品中多次引用了这篇文章。
This theory has been referenced by countless scholars. = 这个理论已经被无数学者引用。


过去完成时:
They had referenced the original document before writing their paper. = 他们在写论文之前已经参考了原始文件。
The author had referenced that study in his earlier works. = 作者在其早期作品中已经引用过那项研究。


将来时:
We will reference this book in our upcoming presentation. = 我们将在即将到来的演示中参考这本书。
She will reference several experts in her speech. = 她将在她的演讲中引用几位专家。


虚拟语气:
If I had referenced the source correctly, I wouldn't have been accused of plagiarism. = 如果我正确地引用了来源，就不会被指控剽窃。
I wish I had referenced those studies more carefully. = 我希望我当时更仔细地参考了那些研究。


被动语态:
The study was referenced in several peer-reviewed journals. = 这项研究在几家同行评审期刊中被引用。
His work has been referenced extensively in the field. = 他的作品在该领域得到了广泛的引用。


疑问句:
Was the source properly referenced? = 来源是否被正确引用？
Have all the figures been referenced correctly? = 所有图表都已正确引用了吗？


否定句:
The student didn't reference any sources in his essay. = 学生在他的论文中没有引用任何来源。
The author failed to reference several key studies. = 作者未能引用几项关键研究。



PHRASE->
cross-referenced = 交叉引用的
properly referenced = 正确引用的
fully referenced = 完全引用的
poorly referenced = 引用不当的
self-referenced = 自引用的
