overaccumulates:/ˌoʊvərəˈkjuːmjʊleɪts/| v.|过度积累，积聚过多|To accumulate to an excessive degree; to gather or acquire more of something than is necessary or desirable.  The overuse of fertilizers can cause nutrients to overaccumulate in the soil.|例句：The company overaccumulated inventory, leading to significant losses. 公司库存积压过多，导致了巨大的损失。
The lake overaccumulates sediment due to erosion. 由于侵蚀，湖泊沉积物过多。|近义词：accumulate excessively, amass excessively, hoard|反义词：deplete, exhaust, diminish
