heat-resistant: /ˈhiːt rɪˈzɪstənt/ | adj. | 耐热的 | Able to withstand high temperatures without being damaged or destroyed.  This adjective describes materials or objects that can endure exposure to heat without significant alteration or failure. |  例句：This heat-resistant paint can withstand temperatures up to 500°C.  这种耐热涂料能承受高达500°C的温度。

例句：The oven's heat-resistant glass allows you to see the food while it's cooking. 烤箱的耐热玻璃让你可以在烹饪时看到食物。


近义词：heatproof, fireproof, thermostable | 反义词：heat-sensitive, heat-labile
