emblazoned:/ɪmˈbleɪznd/| vt.|装饰；显著地印在…上|To decorate (something) with a striking design; to display prominently.|The shield was emblazoned with the family crest.  盾牌上饰有家族纹章。|The company logo was emblazoned across the side of the building. 公司的标志醒目地印在建筑物的侧面。|adorned, decorated, inscribed|unadorned, plain|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He emblazons his car with stickers. 他用贴纸装饰他的车。
They emblazon their message on billboards. 他们在广告牌上醒目地展示他们的信息。

一般过去时:
She emblazoned her name on the trophy. 她在奖杯上刻上了她的名字。
The artist emblazoned the canvas with vibrant colors. 艺术家用鲜艳的色彩装饰了画布。

现在完成时:
They have emblazoned the city with festive decorations. 他们已经用节日装饰装饰了城市。
The team has emblazoned its logo on all its merchandise. 该团队已将其标志印在其所有商品上。

过去完成时:
He had emblazoned the banner before the parade began. 游行开始前，他已经装饰好了横幅。
She had emblazoned the cake with intricate designs before the party. 派对之前，她已经用复杂的图案装饰了蛋糕。

将来时:
They will emblazon the new building with solar panels. 他们将在新建筑上安装太阳能电池板（比喻性地，像装饰一样显著）。
We will emblazon the message across social media. 我们将在社交媒体上醒目地宣传这条信息。

虚拟语气:
If they emblazoned the message more clearly, more people would see it. 如果他们更清晰地展示信息，更多的人会看到它。
I wish they had emblazoned the warning signs more prominently. 我希望他们能更醒目地展示警告标志。

被动语态:
The banner was emblazoned with the company's slogan. 横幅上印着公司的标语。
The building is being emblazoned with a new mural. 建筑物正在被装饰上新的壁画。

疑问句:
Is the message clearly emblazoned on the poster? 海报上的信息是否清晰醒目？
How was the shield emblazoned? 盾牌是如何装饰的？

否定句:
He didn't emblazon his name on the document. 他没有在文件上署名（或醒目地展示）。
The artist did not emblazon the painting with excessive detail. 艺术家没有在画作上添加过多的细节。


PHRASE->
emblazoned with = 印有……；饰有……
emblazoned across = 横跨……醒目地展示
emblazoned on = 印在……上
emblazoned in = 用……装饰
emblazoned upon = 刻在……上
