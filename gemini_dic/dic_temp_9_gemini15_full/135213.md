/ˌnɒnˈoʊvəˌhedz/ | n. | 间接费用；非经常性开支 |  Nonoverheads refer to expenses that are not considered part of a company's regular operating costs.  These costs are often one-time or infrequent expenses. For example,  a major equipment purchase or a legal settlement could be considered nonoverheads. | The company's nonoverheads for the quarter were unexpectedly high due to the lawsuit.  这家公司的本季度间接费用由于诉讼而意外地很高。
The unexpected repairs added significantly to this year's nonoverheads.  意外的维修费用大大增加了今年的间接费用。| indirect costs, incidental expenses | regular operating expenses, direct costs |  名词
