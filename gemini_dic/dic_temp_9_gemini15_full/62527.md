endured:/ɪnˈdʊərd/| v.|忍受，忍耐，持续|To suffer something difficult, unpleasant, or painful for a long time; to withstand or survive a difficult experience.|例句：She endured years of abuse before finally escaping. 她忍受了多年的虐待才最终逃脱。|例句：The old house has endured for centuries. 这座老房子已经存在了几个世纪。|近义词：tolerated, suffered, sustained, withstood|反义词：avoided, escaped


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He endures the pain bravely. 他勇敢地忍受着痛苦。
She endures hardship with grace. 她优雅地忍受着苦难。


一般过去时:
They endured a long and difficult journey. 他们经历了一段漫长而艰难的旅程。
We endured the storm without much damage. 我们在风暴中没有遭受太大损失。


现在完成时:
She has endured much suffering in her life. 她一生经历了很多苦难。
He has endured countless setbacks to achieve his goal. 他为了实现目标忍受了无数的挫折。


过去完成时:
They had endured many hardships before reaching their destination. 他们到达目的地之前经历了许多艰难险阻。
She had endured years of loneliness before finding her true love. 在找到真爱之前，她忍受了多年的孤独。


将来时:
We will endure whatever challenges come our way. 我们将克服任何挑战。
They will endure the test of time. 他们将经受时间的考验。


虚拟语气:
If I had endured a little longer, I would have succeeded. 如果我再坚持一下，我就成功了。
I wish I had endured the pain instead of giving up. 我希望我当时忍受了痛苦而不是放弃。


被动语态:
The building endured the earthquake. 这座建筑经受住了地震的考验。(The building was endured by the earthquake is grammatically incorrect.  Passive voice is not naturally used with "endure" in this context.)
The hardship was endured by the whole family. 全家人共同忍受了这种艰难。(This is a less natural usage of passive voice with endure)


疑问句:
How long can you endure the pain? 你能忍受多久的痛苦？
Will they endure the pressure? 他们能承受住压力吗？


否定句:
He could not endure the heat. 他受不了热。
She would not endure such insults. 她不会忍受这样的侮辱。
