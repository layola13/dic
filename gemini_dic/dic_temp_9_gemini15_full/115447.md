manua:/ˈmænjuəl/ | n. |手册，指南，说明书 | A manual is a book that gives instructions or information on how to do something.  | Example: The car came with a detailed owner's manual. 这辆车附带一份详细的使用手册。 |近义词：handbook, guide, instruction booklet | 反义词：None


USAGE->

词源:
The word "manual" comes from the Latin word "manus," meaning "hand."  It originally referred to something done by hand, and later evolved to describe a book or guide that provides instructions or information.
