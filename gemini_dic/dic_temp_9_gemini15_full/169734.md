propulsion: /prəˈpʌlʃən/ | n. | 推进；推进力 | The forward movement of something, especially a vehicle or aircraft.  The act or process of propelling or driving something forward. | 例句：The rocket's propulsion system is crucial for its launch. 火箭的推进系统对其发射至关重要。

The ship relies on powerful propulsion to navigate through rough seas. 这艘船依靠强大的推进力在波涛汹涌的海面上航行。


近义词：thrust, drive, impetus, propulsion system

反义词：None directly opposite, but terms suggesting lack of movement could be considered contextual opposites, like inertia, stagnation.


词性：名词
