postgraduates: /ˈpoʊstˌɡrædʒuɪts/ | n. | 研究生 |  A postgraduate is a student who is studying for a degree after having already received a first degree (e.g., a bachelor's degree).  They are engaged in further education at a graduate level. | Example: Many postgraduates choose to specialize in a particular field of study.  许多研究生选择在一个特定的研究领域进行专业学习。  | 近义词：graduate students | 反义词： undergraduates
