northwest:/ˌnɔːrθˈwɛst/| adj., n.|西北，西北方向的|Northwest refers to the direction or region that is both north and west.  It can be used as an adjective to describe something's location or direction, or as a noun to refer to a region or area. |例句：The wind is blowing from the northwest.  西北风正在吹。

The plane flew northwest towards the mountains.  飞机向西北方向飞往山区。|近义词：northwesterly|反义词：southeast
