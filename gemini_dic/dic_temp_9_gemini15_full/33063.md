chowing:/tʃoʊɪŋ/ | n. | 咀嚼；嚼碎的食物 | The act of chewing food; food being chewed.  | Example: The dog was enjoying its chowing.  这只狗正在享受它的咀嚼。|近义词：chewing, mastication |反义词：swallowing


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is chowing down on a steak. = 他正在大口吃牛排。
She chows her food very carefully. = 她吃东西很仔细。


一般过去时:
The baby chowed on a teething biscuit. = 婴儿啃着磨牙饼干。
He chowed down the burger in minutes. = 他几分钟内就把汉堡吃掉了。


现在完成时:
The dog has chowed through the bone. = 这狗已经把骨头啃碎了。
She has chowed down three burgers already. = 她已经吃了三个汉堡了。


过去完成时:
He had chowed down a whole pizza before we arrived. = 在我们到达之前，他已经吃掉了一整个披萨。
They had chowed on some snacks earlier. = 他们早些时候吃了一些零食。


将来时:
I will chow down on some delicious pasta tonight. = 我今晚要吃美味的意大利面。
They will be chowing on popcorn during the movie. = 他们将在看电影时吃爆米花。


虚拟语气:
If I had time, I would chow down on a big juicy steak. = 如果我有时间，我会吃一块又大又多汁的牛排。
I wish I could chow down on that amazing dessert right now. = 我真希望我现在能吃掉那份美味的甜点。


被动语态:
The food was being chowed on by the hungry animals. = 饥饿的动物正在吃食物。
The steak was quickly chowed down. = 牛排很快就被吃掉了。


疑问句:
Is she chowing down on that sandwich? = 她正在吃那个三明治吗？
What are they chowing on? = 他们在吃什么？


否定句:
He wasn't chowing down because he wasn't hungry. = 他没吃东西，因为他没饿。
She didn't chow down on the whole pizza. = 她没有吃掉整个披萨。
