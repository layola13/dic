impugned:/ɪmˈpjuːnd/| vt.| 质疑；攻击；诽谤 | To challenge the truth, validity, or honesty of (a claim or statement); to attack or call into question the integrity or validity of someone or something. |例句：He impugned the witness's character, suggesting she was lying. 他质疑证人的品格，暗示她在说谎。

例句：The defendant's lawyer impugned the reliability of the evidence presented by the prosecution.  被告的律师质疑了检方提供的证据的可靠性。


近义词：challenge, question, dispute, assail, attack, discredit, denigrate |反义词：confirm, support, uphold, endorse, validate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He impugns the integrity of the organization. 他质疑该组织的诚信。
She impugns the accuracy of the report. 她质疑报告的准确性。


一般过去时:
They impugned the judge's impartiality. 他们质疑法官的公正性。
He impugned her motives. 他质疑她的动机。


现在完成时:
They have impugned the validity of the contract. 他们已经质疑了合同的效力。
She has repeatedly impugned his honesty. 她一再质疑他的诚实。


过去完成时:
The defense attorney had impugned the witness's credibility before the trial even began.  辩护律师甚至在审判开始之前就质疑了证人的可信度。
They had impugned the accuracy of the data several times. 他们已经多次质疑数据的准确性。


将来时:
He will impugn the findings of the investigation. 他将质疑调查的结果。
She will likely impugn the legitimacy of the election. 她很可能会质疑选举的合法性。


虚拟语气:
If he impugned the evidence, the case would collapse. 如果他质疑证据，案件就会崩溃。
I wish he hadn't impugned her reputation. 我希望他没有败坏她的名声。


被动语态:
His motives were impugned by the media. 他的动机受到了媒体的质疑。
The study's conclusions were impugned by several experts. 该研究的结论受到了几位专家的质疑。


疑问句:
Was his testimony impugned during the cross-examination?  在盘问期间他的证词被质疑了吗？
Will her qualifications be impugned by the committee?  她的资格会被委员会质疑吗？


否定句:
He did not impugn the integrity of the process. 他没有质疑流程的完整性。
She did not impugn his character. 她没有质疑他的品格。
