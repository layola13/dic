nonresponders:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈspɒndəz/ | n. |无应答者，无反应者 | Individuals or entities that do not respond to a stimulus, treatment, or survey.  They fail to show a reaction or provide a response. | Example: The researchers were concerned about the high number of nonresponders in their clinical trial.  研究人员对临床试验中大量的无应答者感到担忧。  The marketing team analyzed the reasons for the high rate of nonresponders to their email campaign. 市场团队分析了电子邮件活动中高无应答率的原因。|近义词：unresponsive individuals, nonreactors |反义词：responders, reactors
