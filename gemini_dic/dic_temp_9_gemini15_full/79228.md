glares:/ɡlerz/| v.|怒视，瞪眼|To stare angrily or fiercely.|She glared at him across the room. 她怒视着房间对面的他。|近义词：stare, scowl, frown|反义词：smile, beam


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He glares at the traffic. = 他怒视着交通。
She glares at me when I'm late. = 我迟到的时候她会怒视着我。

一般过去时:
He glared at the rude customer. = 他怒视着那个粗鲁的顾客。
She glared at her brother after the argument. = 争吵后，她怒视着她的弟弟。

现在完成时:
He has glared at me all morning. = 他整个上午都在怒视着我。
She has glared at that man several times. = 她已经几次怒视那个男人了。

过去完成时:
He had glared at her before she spoke. = 她说话前，他就已经怒视着她了。
She had glared at him throughout the entire meeting. = 在整个会议期间，她一直怒视着他。

将来时:
He will glare at anyone who interrupts him. = 他会怒视任何打断他的人。
She will glare at you if you don't listen. = 如果你不听，她会怒视你的。

虚拟语气:
If he glared at me one more time, I would leave. = 如果他再怒视我一次，我就走。
I wish he wouldn't glare at me like that. = 我希望他不要那样怒视着我。

被动语态:
He was glared at by the angry woman. = 他被那个愤怒的女人怒视着。
The child was glared at for misbehaving. = 那个孩子因为行为不端而被怒视着。

疑问句:
Why is he glaring at me? = 他为什么怒视着我？
Is she glaring at anyone? = 她正在怒视任何人吗？

否定句:
He didn't glare at her, he just looked at her. = 他没有怒视她，他只是看着她。
She didn't glare at the man; she simply ignored him. = 她没有怒视那个男人；她只是无视了他。


PHRASE->
glare at someone = 怒视某人
glare angrily = 愤怒地怒视
glare intensely = 剧烈地怒视
a death glare = 一种死亡般的凝视
glare with hatred = 充满仇恨地怒视
