emblazonments:/ɪmˈbleɪzənmənts/ | n. |装饰；纹章；显著标志 |The decorative or heraldic displays;  the act of emblazoning something.  It refers to the prominent and often ornate display of symbols, designs, or coats of arms, especially on a surface. |例句：The emblazonments on the knight's shield depicted his family crest.  骑士盾牌上的装饰显示了他的家族纹章。
The castle walls were covered in vibrant emblazonments. 城墙上布满了鲜艳的装饰。
