skinniest: /ˈskɪniɪst/ | adj. | 最瘦的，最细的 |  The superlative form of "skinny," describing the state of being extremely thin or slender. It emphasizes the highest degree of thinness among a group or comparison. |  例句：He's the skinniest person in the class. 他是班上最瘦的人。  She wore the skinniest jeans I've ever seen. 她穿着我见过的最细的牛仔裤。 | 近义词：thinnest, slimmest, leanest | 反义词：fattest, chubbiest, stoutest
