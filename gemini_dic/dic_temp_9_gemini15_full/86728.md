helms:/hɛlmz/ | n. |舵; 掌舵; 领导; 控制 | The ship's helmsman skillfully navigated the stormy seas.  这艘船的舵手熟练地驾驶着船只穿越暴风雨的海面。|近义词：rudder, steering wheel, leadership, control |反义词：follower, subordinate


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He helms the company with great success. = 他成功地领导着这家公司。
The captain helms the ship through the narrow channel. = 船长驾驶着船只通过狭窄的海峡。


一般过去时:
She helmed the project and delivered it on time. = 她负责这个项目并按时完成了。
The experienced sailor helmed the boat during the race. = 经验丰富的船员在比赛期间掌舵。


现在完成时:
He has helmed several successful startups. = 他领导过几家成功的创业公司。
She has helmed the department for five years. = 她已经领导这个部门五年了。


过去完成时:
Before the crisis, he had helmed the company for ten years. = 在危机之前，他领导这家公司十年了。
They had helmed the ship through many storms before reaching the port. = 在到达港口之前，他们已经驾驶着船只度过了许多风暴。


将来时:
She will helm the new initiative next year. = 她明年将领导这项新计划。
He will helm the organization through this challenging period. = 他将带领该组织度过这段充满挑战的时期。


虚拟语气:
If he helmed the project, it would be more efficient. = 如果他领导这个项目，效率会更高。
I wish she had helmed the team earlier. = 我希望她早点领导这个团队。


被动语态:
The company is helmed by a visionary leader. = 这家公司由一位有远见的领导者领导。
The ship was helmed by a seasoned captain. = 这艘船由一位经验丰富的船长掌舵。


疑问句:
Who helms the organization? = 谁领导这个组织？
Who helmed the ship during the emergency? = 紧急情况下谁掌舵？


否定句:
He didn't helm the project; someone else did. = 他没有领导这个项目；别人领导的。
She does not helm the department anymore. = 她不再领导这个部门了。
