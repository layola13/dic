cartoon:/kɑːrˈtuːn/| n. |卡通，漫画 |A cartoon is a drawing, especially a humorous one, made for a newspaper or magazine, or a short film using such drawings. Cartoons can be used for entertainment, satire, or political commentary.  |Example: I love watching cartoons on Saturday mornings. 我喜欢在星期六早上看卡通片。

Example: The newspaper published a cartoon satirizing the politician. 报纸刊登了一幅讽刺这位政治家的漫画。|近义词：comic strip, animation, caricature |反义词：photograph, realistic depiction |名词
