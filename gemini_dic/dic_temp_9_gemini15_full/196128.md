septentrial:/sepˈtɛntrɪəl/| adj.|北方的，北部的|Pertaining to the north; northern.  The word derives from the Latin word "septentriones," meaning "seven plough oxen," a constellation near the north pole.  This constellation was used by the ancient Romans to determine north.|例句：The septentrional winds brought cold weather. 北风带来了寒冷的天气。  The septentrional part of the country is known for its rugged mountains. 该国北部以其崎岖的山脉而闻名。|近义词：northern, northerly|反义词：meridional, southern
