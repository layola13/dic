exclusionist:/ɪkˈskluʒənɪst/| adj.|排斥主义的；排外的|An exclusionist is someone who believes in or practices exclusion, especially the exclusion of particular groups of people from a society or organization.  The term often carries a negative connotation, implying prejudice and discrimination.  It can refer to policies, practices, or ideologies that systematically disadvantage certain groups.|例句：The club's exclusionist policies prevented many qualified candidates from joining.  这个俱乐部的排外政策阻止了许多合格的候选人加入。

The government's exclusionist immigration laws were widely criticized. 政府的排外移民法受到了广泛批评。|近义词：bigoted, discriminatory, prejudiced, sectarian|反义词：inclusive, welcoming, tolerant
