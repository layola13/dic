quantifies:/ˈkwɒntɪfaɪz/ /ˈkwɑːntɪfaɪz/| vt.|量化；确定…的数量|To express or measure the quantity of something.|The study quantifies the impact of climate change on sea levels. 这项研究量化了气候变化对海平面的影响。|measures, assesses, determines|qualifies, describes|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The researcher quantifies the data using statistical methods. = 研究人员使用统计方法对数据进行量化。
This instrument quantifies the amount of pollutants in the air. = 此仪器可量化空气中的污染物数量。


一般过去时:
The scientist quantified the effects of the drug on the patients. = 科学家量化了药物对患者的影响。
They quantified the risk before making the investment. = 他们在投资前对风险进行了量化。


现在完成时:
We have quantified the losses incurred by the company. = 我们已经量化了公司蒙受的损失。
She has quantified her contribution to the project. = 她已经量化了她对项目的贡献。


过去完成时:
They had quantified the damage before submitting the insurance claim. = 他们在提交保险索赔之前已经量化了损失。
The team had quantified the variables before running the experiment. = 该团队在进行实验之前已经量化了变量。


将来时:

We will quantify the impact of the new policy on the economy. = 我们将量化新政策对经济的影响。


虚拟语气:
If we had more data, we could quantify the effect more accurately. = 如果我们有更多数据，我们就能更准确地量化效果。

被动语态:
