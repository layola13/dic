ethnicism:/ˈɛθnɪsɪzəm/| n. | 种族主义；种族中心主义 |The belief that one's own ethnic group is superior to others, often accompanied by prejudice and discrimination.  Ethnicism can manifest in various ways, from subtle biases to overt acts of violence.  |例句：Ethnicism played a significant role in the outbreak of the conflict. 种族主义在冲突爆发中扮演了重要角色。|近义词：racism, tribalism, ethnocentrism |反义词：multiculturalism, tolerance
