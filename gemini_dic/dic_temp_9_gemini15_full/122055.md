mistaking:/mɪˈsteɪkɪŋ/| v. | 误解；弄错；误认为 | The act of understanding or identifying something incorrectly.  It often implies a failure to recognize or understand the true nature of something. |例句：I was mistaking his kindness for weakness. 我错把他对我的好意当成了软弱。

例句：He was mistaking the shadow for a person. 他把影子误认为是人。


近义词：misunderstanding, misinterpreting, confusing, misjudging | 反义词：understanding, comprehending, recognizing


词性：现在分词 (verb)


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I am mistaking his intentions. = 我误解了他的意图。
He is always mistaking me for his brother. = 他总是把我误认为是他哥哥。


一般过去时:
I mistook his kindness for weakness. = 我错把他对我的好意当成了软弱。
She mistook the date of the meeting. = 她弄错了会议的日期。


现在完成时:
I have been mistaking him for someone else. = 我一直把他错认成别人。
They have mistaken my silence for agreement. = 他们错把我沉默当成了同意。


过去完成时:
I had mistaken the path, and got lost. = 我走错了路，迷路了。
She had mistaken the meaning of the word. = 她误解了这个词的意思。


将来时:
I will probably mistake the twins again. = 我可能会再次认错这对双胞胎。
He will mistake my good intentions for bad deeds. = 他会错把我的一片好心当成坏事。


虚拟语气:
If I hadn't mistaken the instructions, I wouldn't have failed the test. = 如果我没有误解说明，我就不会考试不及格。
I wish I hadn't mistaken his silence for consent. = 我真希望我没有把他的沉默误解为同意。


被动语态:
His words were mistaken for a threat. = 他的话被误解为威胁。
The painting was mistaken for a forgery. = 这幅画被误认为是赝品。


疑问句:
Am I mistaking the situation? = 我是不是误解了情况？
Were you mistaking his intentions? = 你是不是误解了他的意图？


否定句:
I wasn't mistaking the time. = 我没有弄错时间。
He didn't mistake the information. = 他没有弄错信息。



PHRASE->
be mistaken about: 对…弄错
mistake A for B: 把A误认为B
by mistake: 错误地，无意中
make a mistake: 犯错误
a mistake of fact: 事实性错误
