sculpts:/skʌlpt/| vt.|雕刻；塑造；雕塑|To shape or form (something) by carving, molding, or other means.  It can also refer to the creation of something in a figurative sense.|例句：The artist skillfully sculpts the clay into a beautiful bird. 艺术家巧妙地将粘土雕刻成一只美丽的鸟。

例句：Years of hardship have sculpted her face with deep lines.  多年的磨难在她脸上刻下了深深的皱纹。


近义词：carve, mold, shape, fashion, form|反义词：destroy, ruin, demolish|动词
