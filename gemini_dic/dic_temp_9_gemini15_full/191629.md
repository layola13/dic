schoolmate:/ˈskuːlmeɪt/ | n. | 同学 | A person who is a student at the same school as you.  | Example: I haven't seen my old schoolmates in years.  多年来我都没见过我的老同学们了。 |近义词： classmate, school friend | 反义词：  N/A


USAGE->
一般现在时:
My schoolmate helps me with my homework. = 我的同学帮我做作业。
He is a good schoolmate. = 他是一位好同学。

一般过去时:
My schoolmate played football with me. = 我的同学和我一起踢足球。
We were schoolmates in primary school. = 我们在小学是同学。


现在完成时:
I have known my schoolmate for five years. = 我认识我的同学五年了。
She has become a successful doctor since we were schoolmates. = 从我们还是同学以来，她已经成为一名成功的医生。


过去完成时:
I had known my schoolmate before we entered high school. = 在我们上高中之前我就认识我的同学了。
They had been schoolmates for many years before graduating. = 他们毕业前已经做了多年的同学。


将来时:
I will meet my schoolmate tomorrow. = 我明天将见到我的同学。
We will remain schoolmates until graduation. = 我们将一直是同学直到毕业。


虚拟语气:
If I were a schoolmate with him, I would have helped him. = 如果我和他是同学，我会帮助他的。
I wish I had been schoolmates with him during university. = 我真希望在大学期间和他成为同学。


被动语态:
He was chosen as the class representative by his schoolmates. = 他被他的同学们选为班长。
The project was completed with the help of his schoolmates. = 这个项目是在他同学的帮助下完成的。


疑问句:
Is he your schoolmate? = 他是你的同学吗？
Were you schoolmates with him? = 你和他曾经是同学吗？


否定句:
He is not my schoolmate. = 他不是我的同学。
They were not schoolmates in the same class. = 他们不是同一个班的同学。

PHRASE->
schoolmate reunion = 同学聚会
former schoolmate =  以前的同学
close schoolmate =  要好的同学
long-lost schoolmate = 失散多年的同学
a group of schoolmates =一群同学
