nonvocationally:/ˌnɒnvəʊˈkeɪʃənəli/ | adj. | 非职业地，业余地 | Not relating to or connected with one's vocation or profession.  It describes activities undertaken outside of one's regular job or career. | Example: He pursued his passion for painting nonvocationally, after work and on weekends. 他在工作之余和周末业余时间追求他对绘画的热情。|近义词：amateurishly, avocationally | 反义词：professionally, occupationally
