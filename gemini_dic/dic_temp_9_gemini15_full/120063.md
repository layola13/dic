millwright:/ˈmɪlˌraɪt/| n. |磨坊工匠，机械师|A millwright is a skilled tradesperson who designs, builds, installs, and maintains machinery in mills and factories.  They are often involved in the installation and repair of large-scale industrial equipment.  Millwrights possess a broad range of skills, including mechanical, electrical, and hydraulic expertise.|The millwright expertly repaired the damaged milling machine, ensuring its smooth operation.|近义词：mechanic, machinist, fitter|反义词：N/A
