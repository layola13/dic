photorealisms: /ˌfoʊtoʊˈriːəlɪzəm/ | n. | 超现实主义绘画；照片写实主义 | A style of painting that aims for an extremely realistic representation of a photograph's subject matter, often indistinguishable from a photograph itself.  It emphasizes technical skill and precise detail to create an illusion of reality. | The artist's photorealism was so convincing, many viewers mistook the painting for a photograph.  | 近义词：hyperrealism, realism | 反义词：abstract art, impressionism | 名词
