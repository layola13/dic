floscule:/ˈflɒskjuːl/| n.|小花；小穗；（诗）小诗|A small flower; a floret; (poetic) a short poem.  The term often refers to a small flower within a larger flower head, such as in a composite flower like a daisy.|Example:  Each tiny floscule contributed to the overall beauty of the sunflower.  每一朵小花都为向日葵的整体美感做出了贡献。|近义词：floret, floweret|反义词：None specifically, but could be contrasted with large flower or inflorescence.|名词
