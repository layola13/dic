lubricate:/ˈluːbrɪkeɪt/| vt. |润滑；使润滑|To apply a lubricant to (a machine or part) to reduce friction.  It also refers to making something smoother or easier. |例句：You should lubricate the hinges on the door to make them work smoothly. 你应该给门的铰链上油，让它们运转自如。

例句：Regularly lubricating your car's engine is essential for its longevity. 定期润滑汽车发动机对延长其使用寿命至关重要。


近义词：oil, grease, smooth, facilitate


反义词：dry, rough, hinder


词性：动词
