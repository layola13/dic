handwheel:/ˈhændˌwiːl/| n.|手轮，方向盘|A handwheel is a wheel that is turned by hand to operate a machine or control a mechanism.  It typically has a central hub and spokes or a solid disk.  Handwheels are used in a wide range of applications, from controlling the speed of a machine to adjusting the position of a valve.|例句：The operator turned the handwheel to adjust the focus of the microscope. 操作员转动手轮来调整显微镜的焦点。

The mechanic used the handwheel to steer the heavy machinery. 机械师使用手轮来操纵重型机械。


近义词：control wheel, steering wheel (in specific contexts)|反义词：None directly;  context-dependent opposites might relate to the action it controls (e.g., if controlling speed, "brake" might be considered a contextual opposite).|词性:Noun
