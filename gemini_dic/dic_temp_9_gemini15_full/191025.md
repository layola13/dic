scamper:/ˈskæmpər/| v.|轻快地跑；蹦蹦跳跳地跑|to run quickly and lightly, especially with short steps|The children scampered across the playground.孩子们在操场上蹦蹦跳跳地跑。The rabbit scampered into the bushes.兔子蹦蹦跳跳地跑进了灌木丛。|dash, scurry, hurry|walk slowly, stroll|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He scampers around the house every morning. 他每天早上都在房子周围蹦蹦跳跳。
The squirrels scamper up the trees. 松鼠快速地爬上树。

一般过去时:
The dog scampered after the ball. 狗追着球跑。
The children scampered home after school. 孩子们放学后跑回家。

现在完成时:
The puppy has scampered all over the garden. 小狗已经在花园里跑来跑去了。
She has scampered up the stairs. 她已经跑上楼梯了。

过去完成时:
The cat had scampered away before I could catch it.  在我抓住它之前，猫已经跑掉了。
They had scampered into the woods before the rain started.  在下雨开始之前，他们已经跑进树林里了。

将来时:
The mouse will scamper away if it sees a cat. 如果老鼠看到猫，它就会跑掉。
The kids will scamper down the hill when they see us. 孩子们看到我们就会从山上跑下来。

虚拟语气:
If I were a mouse, I would scamper away from the cat. 如果我是老鼠，我会躲开猫。
I wish I could scamper as fast as a cheetah. 我希望我能像猎豹一样快地跑。

被动语态: (被动语态不常用在此处)
The toy was scampered on by the playful dog.  (玩具被顽皮的狗乱跑踩踏了，此例句略显牵强)


疑问句:
Does your dog scamper around a lot? 你的狗经常乱跑吗？
Will the children scamper down the hill? 孩子们会从山上跑下来吗？

否定句:
The baby didn't scamper; he crawled. 婴儿没有跑，而是爬行。
The mice didn't scamper when the cat entered the room. 猫进入房间时，老鼠没有跑。
