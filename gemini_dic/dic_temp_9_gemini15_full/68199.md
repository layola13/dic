fair-off:/ferˈɔːf/| adj.|相当远,相当遥远|fairly far away or distant in time or space.  The expression suggests a significant, but not necessarily extreme, distance.|例句：The destination is fair off, we need to leave early. 目的地相当远，我们需要早点出发。
The success of the project is still fair off.  项目的成功还相当遥远。|近义词：distant, remote, far-off|反义词：nearby, close, proximate|
