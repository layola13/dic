requited:/rɪˈkwaɪtɪd/| adj.|  报答的；互换的；被报答的 | reciprocated; returned; repaid.  It refers to a feeling or action that is returned or reciprocated, especially in the context of love or affection. |例句：Their love was requited. 他们的爱情得到了回应。

Their kindness was requited with generosity. 他们的好意得到了慷慨的回报。


近义词：returned, reciprocated, repaid, answered


反义词：unrequited, unanswered, unreturned


词性：形容词
