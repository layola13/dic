plagiary:/ˈpleɪdʒəri/| n. |剽窃者；剽窃作品 | A plagiary is a person who plagiarizes; one who commits literary theft.  It can also refer to the act of plagiarism itself, though this usage is less common.  The term often carries a strong connotation of deception and dishonesty. |Example: He was exposed as a plagiary when his essay was found to be copied from another source. 他被揭露为剽窃者，因为他的论文被发现是从其他来源抄袭的。|近义词：thief, copier, imitator |反义词：originator, innovator |名词
