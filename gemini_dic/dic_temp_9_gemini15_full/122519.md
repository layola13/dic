mocks:/mɒks/|v.|嘲笑；模仿；取笑；使无效|To make fun of someone or something, often by imitating them. It can also refer to making something seem less important or worthless.|例句：The children mocked the teacher's accent. 孩子们嘲笑老师的口音。
例句：He mocked the seriousness of the situation by laughing. 他笑着嘲弄局势的严重性。|近义词：deride, ridicule, scoff, jeer|反义词：praise, commend, admire|动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He mocks his friends' bad jokes. = 他嘲笑朋友们糟糕的笑话。
They often mock the way he speaks. = 他们经常模仿他的说话方式。


一般过去时:
She mocked his attempt to sing. = 她嘲笑他唱歌的尝试。
The students mocked the principal's new hairstyle. = 学生们嘲笑校长的新发型。


现在完成时:

过去完成时:

She had mocked his ideas numerous times. = 她已经多次嘲笑他的想法了。


将来时:
They will mock him mercilessly. = 他们将会无情地嘲笑他。

虚拟语气:

被动语态:
