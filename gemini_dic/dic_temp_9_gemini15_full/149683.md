/pædʒɒk/| n. |小围场，牧场|A small enclosed field, especially one used for keeping horses or other livestock.  |Example: The farmer kept his prize-winning horses in a well-maintained paddock.|近义词：paddock, enclosure, field |反义词：vast expanse, open field.
