sang:/sæŋ/| v.|唱，歌唱|To produce musical sounds with the voice.|She sang a beautiful song. 她唱了一首美丽的歌。 He sang a lullaby to his baby. 他给他的宝宝唱了摇篮曲。|近义词：chant, hum, croon|反义词：whisper, speak


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I sing in the choir every Sunday. = 我每个星期天都在唱诗班唱歌。
The birds sing sweetly in the morning. = 鸟儿在早晨婉转地歌唱。

一般过去时:
She sang a beautiful song at the concert. = 她在音乐会上唱了一首美丽的歌。
He sang a song for his mother's birthday. = 他为他母亲的生日唱了一首歌。

现在完成时:
I have sung this song many times. = 我已经唱过这首歌很多次了。
She has sung professionally for over ten years. = 她专业唱歌已经超过十年了。

过去完成时:
He had sung before the audience many times before.  = 他以前多次在观众面前演唱过。
Before the performance, she had sung through the song several times. = 演出之前，她已经把这首歌排练了好几遍。

将来时:
I will sing at the wedding. = 我将在婚礼上唱歌。
The choir will sing a hymn during the ceremony. = 唱诗班将在仪式上唱赞美诗。

虚拟语气:
If I could sing, I would join the choir. = 如果我会唱歌，我就会加入唱诗班。
I wish I could sing like a professional singer. = 我希望我能像专业歌手一样唱歌。

被动语态:
The song was sung beautifully. = 这首歌唱得非常美妙。
The national anthem was sung with great pride. = 国歌唱得非常自豪。

疑问句:
Do you sing? = 你唱歌吗？
Can she sing opera? = 她会唱歌剧吗？

否定句:
I don't sing very well. = 我唱得不太好。
He didn't sing at the party. = 他在聚会上没有唱歌。


PHRASE->
sing along =  一起唱
sing out = 高声歌唱
sing the praises of = 赞扬
sing a different tune = 改变说法
sing someone's praises = 赞扬某人
