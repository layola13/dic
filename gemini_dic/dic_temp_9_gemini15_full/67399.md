external:/ɪkˈstɜːrnəl/| adj.| 外部的；外面的；外来的 | Existing or occurring outside a particular place, object, or group.  Something external is separate from something else and not part of its internal structure or essence. |例句：The external walls of the house need painting.  房屋的外墙需要粉刷。

例句：External pressure forced the company to change its policy. 外界压力迫使公司改变了政策。


近义词：outside, outward, exterior, extraneous

反义词：internal, inner, intrinsic

词性：形容词
