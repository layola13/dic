overtired: /ˌoʊvərˈtaɪrd/ | adj. | 非常疲惫的，过度疲劳的 | Extremely tired; exhausted.  | Example:  I'm overtired and need to go to bed. 我太累了，需要去睡觉。  I feel overtired after working all day without a break.  一天工作下来没休息，我感觉非常疲惫。|近义词：exhausted, fatigued, weary, drained |反义词：energetic, refreshed, invigorated
