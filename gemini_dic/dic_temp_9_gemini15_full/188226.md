rowport:/ˈroʊpɔːrt/ | n. | 排水口，船闸 | A rowport is an opening in the side of a ship or boat, typically used for rowing or draining water.  | Example: The rowport was clogged with seaweed, hindering the boat's drainage. 排水口被海藻堵住了，阻碍了船的排水。| 近义词：drain, opening, scuttle | 反义词：bulkhead, closure
